alyosha:/əˈljoʊʃə/ | n. | 阿廖沙 | A diminutive form of the Russian male given name Alexei, often used as a nickname.  It is a familiar and affectionate form. | Example: Alyosha was a kind and gentle soul. 阿廖沙是一个善良温柔的人。

Example:  In Dostoevsky's *The Brothers Karamazov*, Alyosha is the youngest brother. 在陀思妥耶夫斯基的《卡拉马佐夫兄弟》中，阿廖沙是最小的弟弟。 | 近义词:Alexei, Alex | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun
