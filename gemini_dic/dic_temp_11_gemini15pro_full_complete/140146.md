offc:/ˈɒfɪs/ (UK), /ˈɔːfɪs/ (US)|n.|办公室；办事处；职务；政府部门|A room or building where people work, usually sitting at desks.  A position of authority or responsibility, especially in government or a large organization.|例句：She works in an office downtown.  她在市中心的办公室工作。

例句：He holds the office of president.  他担任总统的职务。|近义词：bureau, workplace, department, agency|反义词:home, residence|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *office, from Latin officium "duty, service, function," from ops "power, help" + facere "to do."
