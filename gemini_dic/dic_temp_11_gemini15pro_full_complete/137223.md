nonsurface:/ˌnɒnˈsɜːrfɪs/
adjective
非表面的，非地表的
Not relating to, occurring on, or affecting a surface.
The nonsurface effects of the earthquake were widespread. 地震的非表面效应很广泛。
近义词: subsurface, underground, deep
反义词: surface, superficial
词性: adjective
