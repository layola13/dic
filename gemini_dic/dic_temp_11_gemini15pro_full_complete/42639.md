countershot:/ˈkaʊntərʃɒt/ (UK) /ˈkaʊntərʃɑːt/ (US)| n.|反击镜头；反驳；反证|A shot or argument made in response to another; a rebuttal.  A camera shot that balances or reverses a previous shot, often used in conversations.|The lawyer presented a powerful countershot to the prosecution's claim. 律师提出了强有力的反驳，反驳了控方的说法。

The director used a countershot to show the listener's reaction. 导演用了一个反拍镜头来显示听者的反应。|近义词：rebuttal, reply, response, counterargument|反义词:argument, claim, assertion|Noun
