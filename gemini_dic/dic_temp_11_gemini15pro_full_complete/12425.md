askant:/əˈskænt/ /əˈskɑːnt/| adj.|斜视的，怀疑的，不赞成的|Looking sideways or obliquely, often suggesting suspicion, disapproval, or contempt.  It can also describe something placed or positioned obliquely.|He looked askance at the dubious proposal. 他斜视着那份可疑的提案。 The picture hung askant on the wall. 画歪挂在墙上。|近义词：awry, askew, sideways, obliquely, disapprovingly|反义词:straight, level, approvingly|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *askaunt, from a- + skaun, from Old Norse *skeifr (“oblique, awry”).  Related to skew.
