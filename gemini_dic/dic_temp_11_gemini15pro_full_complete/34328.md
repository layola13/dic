clash:/klæʃ/| n. vi. vt.|冲突，撞击声，不协调|A loud, harsh, metallic sound, as of cymbals being struck together.  A conflict of opposing ideas or forces. To come into violent conflict.|The cymbals clashed loudly. 铙钹发出刺耳的撞击声。

The two armies clashed on the battlefield. 两军在战场上交锋。

Her bright pink dress clashed with her orange shoes. 她鲜艳的粉红色连衣裙和橙色的鞋子很不协调。|近义词：conflict, collide, disagree, disharmony|反义词：harmony, agreement, peace|词性: 名词, 动词
