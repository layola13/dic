attracts:/əˈtrækts/ | v. | 吸引，引起 |  draws attention, interest, or liking; causes someone or something to approach | The flower's bright color attracts bees.  这朵花鲜艳的颜色吸引了蜜蜂。 |  draws, allures, fascinates | repels, discourages | verb

Etymology: From Middle English *attracten, from Old French atraire, from Latin attrahere (“to draw to, attract”), from ad- (“to”) + trahere (“to draw, pull”).
