liveable:/ˈlɪvəbl/ (US), /ˈlɪvəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|宜居的；适于居住的|Fit or suitable to live in; habitable.  A place that is liveable is pleasant and easy to live in.| This city is becoming more and more liveable.  这个城市变得越来越宜居了。| The apartment is small but liveable. 这间公寓很小，但是可以住人。| synonyms: habitable, inhabitable, comfortable, agreeable, pleasant| antonyms: uninhabitable, unlivable, unpleasant| adjective
