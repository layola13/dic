beteem:/bɪˈstim/ | vt. |给予，授予；认为…合适|To consider (something) suitable; to grant; to bestow.  It also can mean "to allow, permit."  While the latter meaning is older, it is now archaic. |The king beteems his loyal knight a noble title. 国王授予他忠诚的骑士一个贵族头衔。

The gods beteem us with their blessings.  神明赐予我们祝福。|近义词：bestow, grant, confer, accord |反义词：withhold, deny, refuse |v.
