ecoterrorism: /ˈiːkoʊˈtɛrərɪzəm/ | n. |生态恐怖主义| The use of violence or the threat of violence, especially bombing, arson, and sabotage, in pursuit of environmentalist aims. | Ecoterrorism is a serious crime that can have devastating consequences. 生态恐怖主义是一种严重的犯罪，可能造成毁灭性的后果。|  environmental extremism, radical environmentalism | conservation, environmental protection | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
eco- + terrorist

eco-: from Greek oikos "house, dwelling place, family"

terrorist: from French terroriste, from Latin terror "great fear, dread," from terrere "to frighten."
