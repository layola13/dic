calcarate: /ˈkælkəreɪt/ | adj. | 钙化的，像骨刺的 | Resembling a spur; spur-shaped. | The calcarate sepals of the flower were a striking feature.  这朵花的钙化萼片是一个显著的特征。 | 近义词: spurred, calcified | 反义词: smooth, soft | 词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *calcāriātus, from calcārius (“of lime”), from calx (“lime, limestone”).
