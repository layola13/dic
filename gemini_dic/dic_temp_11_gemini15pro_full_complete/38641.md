concupiscible:/kənˈkjʊpɪsəb(ə)l/
adjective
容易动情的；贪求感官享受的
Characterized by concupiscence; lustful; desiring sensual pleasures.
The preacher warned against concupiscible desires, urging his flock to focus on spiritual matters.  牧师告诫人们要抵制贪求感官享受的欲望，敦促他的信徒专注于精神事务。
He was plagued by concupiscible thoughts that distracted him from his studies. 他被贪欲的念头所困扰，这使他无法专心学习。
近义词:lustful, carnal, desirous, sensual, libidinous
反义词:ascetic, chaste, platonic, spiritual, abstinent
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *concupiscibilis, from Latin concupiscere "to long for eagerly, covet," from com- "with" (see com-) + cupiscere "to desire."
