disallegiance:/ˌdɪsəˈliːdʒəns/
n.
不忠;背叛;变节
The act of refusing to support or obey a government or leader.
He was accused of disallegiance to the crown. 他被指控对王室不忠。
近义词:treachery, betrayal, disloyalty, perfidy, sedition
反义词:loyalty, allegiance, fidelity, devotion
词性:名词
