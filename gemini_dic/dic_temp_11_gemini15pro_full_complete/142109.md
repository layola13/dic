orienter:/ˈɔːriˌɛntər/ (US), /ɔːriˈɛntə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. |定向器，定向者；使适应，使熟悉|Someone or something that orients or causes orientation.  It can also mean to familiarize someone with new surroundings or circumstances. |The new employee orientation program helps orienters acclimate new hires to the company culture. 新员工入职培训计划帮助指导者使新员工适应公司文化。|近义词：guide, direct, familiarize, accustom, adjust, adapt|反义词：disorient, confuse|词性: noun, verb
Etymology: From the French word *orienter*,  from *orient* (east), ultimately from Latin *oriens* (rising).
