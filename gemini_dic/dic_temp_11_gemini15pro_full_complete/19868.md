bingeing:/ˈbɪndʒɪŋ/| n. v.|狂欢，放纵，无节制地做某事|The act of engaging in an activity, especially eating, drinking, or watching something, excessively and uncontrollably.|She admitted to bingeing on ice cream after the breakup. 她承认分手后狂吃冰淇淋。|He's been bingeing on that new TV show all weekend. 他整个周末都在狂看那部新电视剧。|近义词：overindulging, gorging, indulging, spreeing|反义词: abstaining, moderating|词性:Noun, Gerund/Present Participle of "binge"


Etymology:
From the noun "binge," which originated in the late 19th century, possibly from a dialectal word meaning "to soak."  The exact origin is uncertain.
