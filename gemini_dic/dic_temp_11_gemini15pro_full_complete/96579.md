inducer:/ɪnˈdjuːsər/ (US), /ɪnˈdʒuːsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|诱导物，诱导剂|Something that causes or brings about an effect, especially a physiological or biochemical one.  It acts as a trigger or catalyst for a particular process.|The lactose inducer stimulated the production of the enzyme.|近义词: agonist, stimulant, catalyst, trigger|反义词: inhibitor, repressor, blocker|noun
