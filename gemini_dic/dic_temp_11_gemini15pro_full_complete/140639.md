olympics: /əˈlɪmpɪks/ | n. | 奥林匹克运动会 |  a series of international sporting contests held every four years in a different country. The Olympics consist of the Summer Olympics and the Winter Olympics, which are held in alternating even-numbered years.  | The Olympics are held every four years. 奥林匹克运动会每四年举办一次。| The next Summer Olympics will be held in Paris. 下一届夏季奥运会将在巴黎举行。 | 近义词: Olympic Games | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

olympics: /əˈlɪmpɪks/ | adj. | 奥林匹克的 | relating to the Olympic Games. |  The Olympic flame is a symbol of the Games. 奥林匹克圣火是奥运会的象征。 | Olympic athletes train for years to compete. 奥林匹克运动员训练多年以参加比赛。 | 近义词: Olympian | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
