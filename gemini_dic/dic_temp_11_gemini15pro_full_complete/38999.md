confirmee:/ˌkɒnfɜːrˈmiː/ (US) /ˌkɑːnfɜːrˈmiː/| n. |被确认者；被批准者|A person who has received confirmation, especially in a religious context, or whose appointment or participation has been officially confirmed.|The bishop welcomed the confirmee into the church. 主教欢迎被确认者加入教会。|近义词:ratified person, approved candidate|反义词:nominee, applicant|词性:名词

词源: From the French word "confirmé," past participle of "confirmer," meaning "to confirm," ultimately derived from Latin "confirmare."
