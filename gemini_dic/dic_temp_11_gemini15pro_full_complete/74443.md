frangent:/ˈfreɪndʒənt/
adjective
易碎的，脆的；易破的
readily or easily broken; brittle
The frangent material shattered into a thousand pieces when it was dropped.  这种易碎的材料掉在地上就摔成碎片。

The archaeologist carefully handled the frangent pottery shards. 考古学家小心翼翼地处理易碎的陶器碎片。

近义词:brittle, fragile, breakable, delicate, frail
反义词:strong, tough, resilient, durable, robust
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *frangent-, present participle of frangere "to break," related to frangere "to break," from Proto-Indo-European *bʰreg- “to break.”
