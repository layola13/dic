joyfully:/ˈdʒɔɪfəli/ | adv. |快乐地，高兴地 | In a joyful manner; with joy or happiness. | She laughed joyfully at the good news. 她听到好消息高兴地笑了。|  They joyfully celebrated their victory. 他们高兴地庆祝他们的胜利。 | 近义词：happily, merrily, cheerfully, gladly | 反义词：sadly, sorrowfully, unhappily | 词性: 副词

词源: From joy + -fully.  "Joy" comes from Old French "joie" ultimately from Latin "gaudia," plural of "gaudium" meaning "joy, delight."  "-fully" is a suffix used to form adverbs of manner, meaning "in a specified manner."
