index-linking:/ˈɪndɛks lɪŋkɪŋ/ | n. | 指数挂钩 | The act of connecting a value, such as a payment or price, to a price index. |  Many pensions are index-linked to inflation so their value is not eroded over time. 许多养老金与通货膨胀指数挂钩，因此它们的价值不会随着时间的推移而被侵蚀。

Index-linking protects investors from the uncertainty of inflation. 指数化连接保护投资者免受通货膨胀的不确定性的影响。| synonyms: inflation-proofing, indexing | antonyms: fixed, unadjusted | noun
