dipody:/ˈdɪpədi/| n.|双足跳跃; 双足动物|A manner of locomotion involving leaps or springs on two feet.  A two-footed animal.|The kangaroo uses dipody to move quickly across the Australian outback. 袋鼠利用双足跳跃的方式快速穿越澳大利亚内陆。|synonyms: bipedal locomotion, bounding |antonyms: quadrupedal locomotion|noun

dipody:/ˈdɪpədi/| n.|双足跳跃的动物|An animal that moves by leaping or springing on two feet.|Jerboas are examples of dipody found in desert regions. 跳鼠是在沙漠地区发现的双足跳跃动物的例子。|synonyms: biped, two-footed animal |antonyms: quadruped|noun
