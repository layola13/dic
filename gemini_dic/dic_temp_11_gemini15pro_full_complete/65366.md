eumenides:/juːˈmɛnɪdiːz/
n.
复仇女神; 欧墨尼得斯
The Erinyes, also known as the Eumenides (meaning "the Kindly Ones"), were Greek goddesses of vengeance.
例句：The Eumenides pursued Orestes for the murder of his mother. 复仇女神追捕俄瑞斯忒斯，因为他谋杀了他的母亲。

例句：Aeschylus wrote a play about the Eumenides.  埃斯库罗斯写了一部关于复仇女神的戏剧。

近义词：Erinyes, Furies
反义词:  (No direct antonym for vengeful deities)
词性: noun (plural)

eumenide:/juːˈmɛnɪdiː/
n.
复仇女神; 欧墨尼得斯(单数)

例句：An eumenide pursued Orestes relentlessly.  一个复仇女神无情地追捕俄瑞斯忒斯。
