limb:/lɪm/| n.|肢体；树枝；分支|A limb is an arm or leg of a person or animal, or a large branch of a tree.|She broke a limb when she fell out of the tree. 她从树上摔下来摔断了一根肢体。|Synonym: branch, extremity, appendage|Antonym: torso, trunk|noun

limb:/lɪm/| n.|边缘|An edge or border of something, especially of the sun or moon.|The sun appeared over the eastern limb of the horizon. 太阳出现在地平线的东部边缘。|Synonym: edge, border, rim|Antonym: center, middle|noun
