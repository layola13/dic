hitchen:/ˈhɪtʃɪn/| n.|厨房; 炉灶|A hitchen is an old-fashioned word for a kitchen, especially a large one in a farmhouse or manor house. It often includes areas for cooking, baking, and other food preparation.|The old farmhouse had a large hitchen with a massive fireplace.|近义词：kitchen, galley|反义词: (No direct antonym)|名词
