alzheimer:/ˈɔːltshaɪmər/ /ˈæltshaɪmər/ | n. | 阿尔茨海默病；老年痴呆症 | A progressive mental deterioration that can occur in middle or old age, due to generalized degeneration of the brain. It is the most common cause of premature senility. |  My grandmother suffers from Alzheimer's disease. 我的祖母患有阿尔茨海默病。|  dementia, senility | None | noun
