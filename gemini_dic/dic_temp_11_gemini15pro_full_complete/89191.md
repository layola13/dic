homering:/ˈhoʊmərɪŋ/| n. |本垒打；全垒打|A hit in baseball that allows the batter to run around all four bases and score a run.|The batter hit a towering homering over the left-field fence.  击球手击出了一个高耸的本垒打，越过了左外野的围栏。|Synonym: homer, round-tripper|Antonym: out|noun
