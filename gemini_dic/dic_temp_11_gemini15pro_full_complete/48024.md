decisions:/dɪˈsɪʒənz/
n.
决定，抉择
The process of making choices, especially important ones.
We made some important decisions about the future of the company.  我们对公司的未来做出了一些重要的决定。
choices, resolutions, determinations
indecision, hesitation
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French decision, from Latin dēcisiō (“a cutting off, a decision”), from dēcīsus, past participle of dēcīdere (“to cut off, decide”), from de- (“off”) + caedere (“to cut”).
