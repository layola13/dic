overdriving:/ˌəʊvərˈdraɪvɪŋ/| n. |过度驱动；超速行驶；过度操劳|The act of driving a vehicle faster than is safe or legal, or working or using something too hard.|He was fined for overdriving his car. 他因超速驾驶而被罚款。|近义词：speeding, straining, overworking|反义词:underdriving, relaxing, coasting|词性:名词

overdrive:/ˌəʊvərˈdraɪv/| v. |过度驱动；超速行驶；过度操劳|To drive a vehicle faster than is safe or legal, or to work or use something too hard.|She overdrove her employees and they all quit. 她过度压榨员工，结果他们都辞职了.|近义词：speed, strain, overwork|反义词:underdrive, relax, coast|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + drive.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much," or "beyond." "Drive" comes from Old English *drīfan "to push, compel, urge," from Proto-Germanic *dreiban.
