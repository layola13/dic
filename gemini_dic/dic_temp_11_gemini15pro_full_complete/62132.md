encirclement: /ɪnˈsɜːrkəlmənt/ | /ɪnˈsɝːkəlmənt/ | n. |包围，环绕，围困|The encirclement of the city by enemy troops led to a long siege. 敌军对城市的包围导致了长时间的围困。|例句：The enemy completed the encirclement of the city. 敌人完成了对城市的包围。|例句：The general planned a strategy of encirclement to trap the enemy forces. 将军计划了一个包围战略来困住敌军。|近义词：surrounding, besiegement, enclosure |反义词: liberation, escape |词性: 名词

Etymology: From encircle + -ment. Encircle comes from French encercler, from en- "in" + cercle "circle."
