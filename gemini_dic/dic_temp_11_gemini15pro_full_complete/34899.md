cliques:/klɪks/ /kliːks/| n.|小圈子，派系|A small group of people, with shared interests or other features in common, who spend time together and do not readily allow others to join them.|例句：High school is full of cliques. 高中里有很多小圈子。

例句：It's hard to break into those cliques. 很难打入那些小圈子。|近义词：groups, circles, factions, gangs|反义词:inclusive groups, diverse groups|词性:noun
