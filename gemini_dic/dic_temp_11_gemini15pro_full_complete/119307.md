microatoll:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌætɒl/ | n. | 微环礁 | A microatoll is a small, low-lying island formed from coral, whose top is dead and flat due to past exposure to sea level. The living coral grows around the perimeter, often in a ring-shaped form, resembling a miniature atoll. | Microatolls provide valuable information about past sea levels. 微环礁提供了有关过去海平面的宝贵信息。| synonym: coral structure, reef | antonym: lagoon | noun

Etymology:
micro- (small) + atoll (ring-shaped coral reef)
