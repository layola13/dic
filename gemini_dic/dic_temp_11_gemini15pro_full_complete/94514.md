importune:/ˌɪmpɔːrˈtjuːn/ | v. | 恳求；纠缠；不断要求 | To ask (someone) for something or to do something in a way that is annoying because it is repeated many times. |  The beggar importuned passersby for money. 乞丐缠着路人要钱。

He importuned his father to let him go to the party. 他不断恳求父亲让他去参加聚会。 | synonyms: beseech, implore, entreat, pester, badger | antonyms: ignore, neglect, disregard | verb
