emphasize:/ˈɛmfəˌsaɪz/ (US) /ˈem.fə.saɪz/ (UK)| v. |强调，着重|To give particular importance or prominence to something. |The teacher emphasized the importance of doing homework. 老师强调了做作业的重要性。

The news report emphasized the severity of the storm.  新闻报道强调了风暴的严重性。

近义词：stress, highlight, underscore, accentuate|反义词: downplay, de-emphasize, minimize |词性: verb
