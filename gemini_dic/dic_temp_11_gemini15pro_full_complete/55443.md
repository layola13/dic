distincture:/dɪˈstɪŋktʃər/
noun
区别; 特征; 个性;
The state of being distinct or different.  A distinguishing characteristic or feature.
His voice has a distincture that makes him easily recognizable. 他的声音有一种独特的特征，使他很容易被认出来。
synonyms: distinction, difference, peculiarity, individuality
antonyms: similarity, sameness, uniformity
n.

etymology: From Late Latin *distinctura, from Latin distinctus (past participle of distinguere, "to distinguish").
