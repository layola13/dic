kauri:/ˈkaʊri/ (US), /ˈkɔːri/ (UK)| n.|贝壳杉，考里松|A large coniferous tree of the genus *Agathis*, native to New Zealand and other areas of the South Pacific, yielding valuable timber and resin.|The kauri tree is a giant of the New Zealand forest.  贝壳杉是新西兰森林的巨树。|The kauri forests were extensively logged in the 19th century. 19世纪，贝壳杉森林被大量砍伐。|近义词:Agathis|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific tree species)|词性:名词
