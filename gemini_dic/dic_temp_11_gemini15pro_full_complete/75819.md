fumifugist:/ˈfjuːmɪˌfjuːdʒɪst/ | n. |熏蒸师，烟熏消毒员 | A person who performs fumigation. | 例句：The fumifugist carefully sealed the building before releasing the fumigant.  熏蒸师在释放熏蒸剂之前仔细地密封了建筑物。

例句：The fumifugist ensured the area was properly ventilated after the treatment. 熏蒸师确保在处理后该区域通风良好。 | 近义词：exterminator, pest control operator | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin *fumus* (smoke) + *fugare* (to put to flight).  来自拉丁语 *fumus*（烟）+ *fugare*（驱散）。
