alumin:/əˈluːmɪn/ | n. |明矾|A white crystalline double sulfate of aluminum: the potassium double sulfate is called potash alum or potassium alum, KAl(SO4)2.12H2O, and the ammonium double sulfate is called ammonium alum, (NH4)Al(SO4)2.12H2O. They are used as mordants in dyeing, in medicine as astringents and styptics, and in purifying water.| Alum is used in pickling to keep pickles crisp. 明矾用于腌制泡菜以保持脆度。|Synonyms: alum, potassium alum |Antonyms: None | Noun
