aplanatism:/əˈplænəˌtɪzəm/
|n.|消球差，齐明性
|The state of being aplanatic; freedom from spherical aberration.|
|This lens boasts exceptional aplanatism, resulting in incredibly sharp images.| 这款镜头拥有出色的消球差性能，从而 menghasilkan gambar yang sangat tajam。
|近义词: stigmatism|
|反义词: spherical aberration|
|词性: noun|

aplanatic:/ˌeɪpləˈnætɪk/
|adj.|消球差的，齐明的
|Free from spherical aberration; corrected for spherical aberration.|
|An aplanatic lens system minimizes distortion and produces a clearer image.| 消球差透镜系统最大限度地减少失真并产生更清晰的图像。
|近义词: stigmatic|
|反义词: non-aplanatic,spherical|
|词性: adjective|
