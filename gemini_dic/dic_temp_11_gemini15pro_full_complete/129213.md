nobody:/ˈnoʊbədi/ (US), /ˈnəʊbɒdi/ (UK)|n.|无名小卒|A person who is not famous or important.|He was a nobody before he won the lottery. 他在赢得彩票之前是个无名小卒。|Synonyms: nonentity, unknown|Antonyms: somebody, celebrity|noun
