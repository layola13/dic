catastrophic:/ˌkætəˈstrɒfɪk/ (US) /ˌkætəˈstrɑːfɪk/ (UK)| adj.|灾难性的，毁灭性的|involving or causing sudden great damage or suffering|例句：The earthquake had catastrophic consequences for the region. 地震给该地区带来了灾难性的后果。

例句：The oil spill was a catastrophic event for the marine environment.  石油泄漏对海洋环境来说是一场灾难性事件。


近义词：disastrous, calamitous, devastating, ruinous|反义词: fortunate, beneficial, advantageous|词性:形容词
