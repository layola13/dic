biscuit-like:/ˈbɪskɪtˌlaɪk/| adj.|像饼干的；饼干状的|Resembling a biscuit in texture, shape, or taste.|The cake had a dry, biscuit-like texture.  这个蛋糕口感很干，像饼干一样。|近义词：crispy, crunchy, dry, crumbly|反义词：soft, moist, chewy|词性:形容词

词源:
biscuit (n.) + -like (suffix)

biscuit: from Old French bescuit "twice-baked," from bis "twice" + cuit "cooked," past participle of coire "to cook"

-like: Old English -lic, from gelic "like, similar, equal; proper, fitting," from Proto-Germanic *galikaz "having the same form," from PIE root *leik- "to be alike."
