dislodge:/dɪsˈlɒdʒ/ (UK), /dɪsˈlɑːdʒ/ (US)| v. |取出；移动；逐出|To remove something or someone, often with difficulty, from a fixed position.| The earthquake dislodged several boulders from the mountainside. 地震使几块巨石从山坡上滚落下来。|The rebels were dislodged from their stronghold. 叛军被从他们的要塞中赶了出来。|近义词：remove, displace, extricate, oust|反义词：lodge, embed, fix, secure|词性：动词

dislodged:/dɪsˈlɒdʒd/ (UK), /dɪsˈlɑːdʒd/ (US)| adj. |被移动的；被逐出的|Having been removed or forced from a previously fixed position. | The dislodged rock tumbled down the hill. 被移开的岩石滚下山坡。| Several families were dislodged by the flood. 好几户人家被洪水冲走了家园。|近义词：displaced, removed|反义词：fixed, embedded|词性：形容词
