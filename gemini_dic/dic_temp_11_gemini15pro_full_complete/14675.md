avuncularly:/əˈvʌŋkjələrli/
| adv. |像伯父（或叔父、舅父、姑父、姨父）般地；慈爱地|In a manner resembling or characteristic of an uncle, especially in showing kindliness or indulgence. |He smiled avuncularly at the young boy. 他慈爱地对小男孩笑了笑。|近义词：kindly, paternally, benevolently|反义词：sternly, harshly, coldly|词性:副词
词源: From avuncular + -ly. Avuncular comes from Latin avunculus (“maternal uncle”) + -ar.
