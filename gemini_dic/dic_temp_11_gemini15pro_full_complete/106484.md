kraal:/krɑːl/| n.|畜栏，围栏，村庄|An enclosure for livestock, especially cattle or sheep, typically made of thorn bushes.  A traditional African village of huts, typically enclosed by a fence.|The cattle were herded into the kraal for the night. 牛群被赶进畜栏过夜。|The Zulu kraal was a hub of activity.  祖鲁村庄是活动的中心。|Synonyms: enclosure, pen, compound, village, settlement|Antonyms: open field, wilderness|noun

kraal:/krɑːl/| vt.|把…关进畜栏|To enclose (livestock) in a kraal.|The farmer kraaled the sheep to protect them from predators.  农场主把羊关进畜栏以保护它们免受捕食者的伤害。|They kraaled the cattle at sunset. 他们在日落时把牛关进了畜栏。|Synonyms: pen, enclose, corral|Antonyms: release, free|verb
