missionaries:/ˈmɪʃəˌnɛriz/| n.|传教士|A person sent on a religious mission, especially one sent to promote Christianity in a foreign country.|例句：The missionaries traveled to remote villages to spread their faith. 传教士们前往偏远村庄传播他们的信仰。

例句：The missionaries worked tirelessly to convert the local population. 传教士们不知疲倦地工作以改变当地居民的信仰。|近义词：apostles, evangelists, preachers|反义词:None|Noun
