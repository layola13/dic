enginer:/ˈɛndʒɪnɪər/ | n. | 工程师；机师；火车司机 | A person who designs, builds, or maintains engines, machines, or public works. |  He is a talented enginer. 他是一位才华横溢的工程师。|  近义词: architect, designer, constructor, builder, mechanic | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French "engigneor" (designer of war engines), ultimately from Latin "ingeniare" (to devise).
