avalonia:/ˌævəˈloʊniə/ | n. | 阿瓦隆尼亚 |A legendary island paradise featured in Arthurian legend, often described as the place where King Arthur was taken to recover from his wounds after the Battle of Camlann. It is sometimes associated with Glastonbury in Somerset, England. |  The myths of Avalonia are shrouded in mystery. 阿瓦隆尼亚的神话笼罩在神秘之中。|  Synonyms:  paradise, utopia, Eden, Shangri-La | Antonyms:  dystopia, hell | Noun
