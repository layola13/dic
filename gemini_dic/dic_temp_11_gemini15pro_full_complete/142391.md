orthotomy:/ɔːrˈθɒtəmi/ | n. | 正交切割，直切 | The act or process of cutting at right angles. |  The surgeon performed an orthotomy to access the damaged tissue. 外科医生进行了直切以接近受损组织。| Example 2:  The orthotomy of the rock revealed a beautiful geode inside.  岩石的直切露出了里面美丽的晶洞。 | synonyms:  bisection, perpendicular cut | antonyms: oblique cut | noun
