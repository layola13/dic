counterinfluenced:/ˌkaʊntərˈɪnfluənst/
|v.| 抵消影响；对抗影响 |To exert influence against; to neutralize or offset the influence of something or someone.| The government's new policy counterinfluenced the negative effects of the recession. 政府的新政策抵消了经济衰退的负面影响。| The public health campaign counterinfluenced the tobacco industry's advertising. 公共卫生运动抵消了烟草业的广告宣传。| counteract, offset, neutralize | reinforce, strengthen |verb
