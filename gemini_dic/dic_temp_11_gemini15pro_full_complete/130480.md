nonabusively:/ˌnɒnəˈbjuːsɪvli/
adverb
非滥用地；不辱骂地
in a way that does not involve abuse or misuse.
She spoke to the child nonabusively, explaining the rules calmly and clearly. 她以非滥用的方式与孩子交谈，平静而清晰地解释规则。
The software was designed to be used nonabusively and responsibly. 该软件的设计旨在非滥用和负责任地使用。
近义词: respectfully, kindly, considerately, properly
反义词: abusively, harmfully, cruelly, offensively
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "opposite of"
abusive (adjective):  from Latin "abusus," past participle of "abuti"  meaning "to misuse"
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
