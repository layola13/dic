cannibalize:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪz/| v. |同类相食，拆用配件，损害（另一产品或公司）的销售|To take parts from (something) in order to repair or build something else. To reduce the sales of (one product) by introducing a similar product, especially one made by the same company. |The company cannibalized its older models to produce the new one.  这家公司拆用了旧型号的零件来生产新款。

The new product is cannibalizing sales of the existing product. 新产品正在蚕食现有产品的销售。|近义词：reuse, salvage, undermine, appropriate |反义词: construct, build, create |词性:动词

cannibalization:/ˌkænɪbəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ |n.| 同类相食，拆用配件，损害（另一产品或公司）的销售|The act of cannibalizing something. |The cannibalization of old equipment for spare parts is common practice.  拆用旧设备的零件是很常见的做法。

The introduction of the new model led to the cannibalization of sales of the older model.  新车型的推出导致了旧车型的销售额下降。|近义词：reuse, salvage, appropriation |反义词: construction, building |词性:名词
