jiffies:/ˈdʒɪfiz/| n.|片刻，一会儿，瞬间|A very short time; moments.|I'll be back in a jiffy. 我一会儿就回来。|Synonyms: moment, instant, second, trice|Antonyms: eternity, forever|noun

jiffies:/ˈdʒɪfiz/| n.|少量，少许（常用复数形式）|A small amount; a few.|I only need a few jiffies of your time. 我只需要占用你一点时间。|Synonyms: bit, little, tad, smidgen|Antonyms: lot, much, plenty|noun
