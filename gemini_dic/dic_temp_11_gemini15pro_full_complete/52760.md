dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/ | v. | 扩大，膨胀；详述 | To make or become wider, larger, or more open; to speak or write at length on a subject. | The pupils of her eyes dilated in the dark. 她的瞳孔在黑暗中扩大了。

The author dilated on the subject for several pages. 作者就这个主题详细阐述了好几页。 | 近义词：expand, enlarge, widen, amplify | 反义词：contract, constrict, shrink, compress | 词性: 动词

dilate:/ˈdaɪleɪt/ | adj. | 扩张的，膨胀的 (较少用作形容词，通常使用 dilated) | Widened, expanded. |  Her dilated pupils indicated surprise. 她放大的瞳孔表明她很惊讶。 | 近义词：expanded, enlarged, widened | 反义词：contracted, constricted, shrunk, compressed | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dilatare*, from Latin *dilatus*, past participle of *differre* "to carry apart, scatter, spread," from *dis-* "apart, asunder" + *ferre* "to carry."
