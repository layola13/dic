goos:/ɡuːs/ | n. |傻瓜，笨蛋|A silly or foolish person. |例句：He's such a goos, he fell for the oldest trick in the book. 他真是个傻瓜，上了最老套的当。

例句：Don't be a goos and believe everything you hear. 别傻了，不要听到什么都信。


|近义词：fool, idiot, nincompoop, dolt|反义词:genius, sage|词性:名词


词源:Likely a variant of "goose," used metaphorically to refer to a foolish person, similar to how "turkey" is used.  The use of animal names to denote foolishness is common in many languages.
