mohican:/moʊˈhiːkən/ | n. | 莫希干人；莫希干族人的；莫霍克族人的；莫希干式发型 | A member of a Native American people formerly inhabiting the Hudson River valley.  A hairstyle with a shaved head and a strip of upright hair along the middle of the scalp. |  Example 1: The Mohican tribe was known for its fierce warriors. 莫希干部落以其凶猛的战士而闻名。

mohican:/moʊˈhiːkən/ | adj. | 莫希干人的；莫希干族的 | Relating to the Mohican people or their language. | Example 1:  Mohican traditions are still practiced today. 莫希干人的传统至今仍在沿袭。

Example 2:  He studied the Mohican language. 他学习莫希干语。| 近义词: Mohawk | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
