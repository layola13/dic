hark-back:/ˈhɑːrk bæk/ (UK), /ˈhɑːrk bæk/ (US)| n. | 回顾；追溯；怀旧 |A hark-back is a return to something in the past.|  The design of the new car is a hark-back to the classic models of the 1960s. 这款新车的设计是对20世纪60年代经典车型的回顾。|  The politician's speech was a hark-back to the values of a previous era.  这位政治家的演讲是对过去时代价值观的回顾。| synonym: throwback, regression, return| antonym:  advancement, progression | noun

hark-back:/ˈhɑːrk bæk/ (UK), /ˈhɑːrk bæk/ (US)| v. | 回顾；追溯；怀旧 |To hark back to something means to return to or remember something in the past.| The film harks back to the golden age of Hollywood. 这部电影让人回想起好莱坞的黄金时代。|  He harks back to his childhood days whenever he visits his hometown.  每当他回到家乡，他都会回想起他的童年时光。| synonym: reminisce, recall, revisit| antonym: forget, ignore | verb
