loosest:/ˈluːsɪst/| adj.|最松的，最宽松的，最不严格的|Superlative form of loose: most loose.  Meaning not firmly or tightly fixed in place; detached or able to be detached.|Her hair was in the loosest of braids. 她的头发梳成了最松散的辫子。|Synonyms: slackest, baggiest, flabbiest|Antonyms: tightest, firmest, strictest|adjective
