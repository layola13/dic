dreamlands:/ˈdriːmlændz/
noun
梦境; 梦乡;  梦幻世界
A place or state of mind characterized by fantasy, reverie, or sleep.  Often used in the plural to suggest a series of fantastical or unreal experiences.
He often escapes into his dreamlands when life gets too stressful. 他在生活压力太大时经常逃到他的梦境中。
Synonyms: fantasy worlds, dream worlds, reveries
Antonyms: reality, wakefulness
