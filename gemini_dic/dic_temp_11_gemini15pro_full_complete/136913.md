nonsolidifying:/ˌnɒn.səˈlɪd.ɪ.faɪ.ɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːn.səˈlɪd.ə.faɪ.ɪŋ/ | adj. | 非固化的，不凝固的 | Not becoming solid; remaining liquid or gaseous. |  The nonsolidifying lubricant is ideal for high-temperature applications.  这种非固化润滑剂非常适合高温应用。

The chemical remains nonsolidifying even at low temperatures.  即使在低温下，这种化学物质仍然不会凝固。 | 近义词：noncoagulating, liquid, fluid | 反义词：solidifying, hardening, congealing | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + solidifying (present participle of solidify, from Latin solidus "solid" + -fy "to make")
