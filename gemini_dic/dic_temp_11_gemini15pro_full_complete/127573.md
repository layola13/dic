nectarial:/nɛkˈtɛəriəl/
adjective
蜜腺的；分泌花蜜的
Relating to, producing, or resembling nectar; nectary.
The nectarial glands of the flower attract bees.  这朵花蜜腺吸引蜜蜂。
nectary, nectariferous
non-nectarial
adjective
