columbiad:/kəˈlʌmbiæd/ | n. |哥伦比亚炮；一种大型加农炮 | A large-bore, smoothbore cannon, typically used in coastal defenses during the mid-19th century.  It was named after Christopher Columbus. |  The columbiad fired a shell that weighed over 200 pounds.  哥伦比亚炮发射的炮弹重达200多磅。|  The fort was armed with several columbiads.  这个堡垒配备了几门哥伦比亚炮。| synonyms: cannon, artillery piece | antonyms: N/A | noun
