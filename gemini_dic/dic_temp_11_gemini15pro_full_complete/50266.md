depersonalizes:/diːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使失去个性，使人格解体，使物化
to divest of personality or individuality; to make impersonal
The hospital gowns and sterile environment depersonalized the patients.  医院的病号服和无菌环境使病人失去了个性。
Modern medicine can sometimes depersonalize patients, treating them as a collection of symptoms rather than individuals. 现代医学有时会使病人失去个性，把他们当作一系列症状的集合，而不是一个个体。
synonyms: dehumanize, objectify, alienate
antonyms: personalize, individualize, humanize
词性:动词
