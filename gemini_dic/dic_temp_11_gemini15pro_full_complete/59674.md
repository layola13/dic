echiuroidea:/ˌɛkiʊˈrɔɪdiə/| n. |螠虫动物门|A phylum of marine worms, commonly known as spoon worms, characterized by their unsegmented bodies and a distinctive proboscis.|Echiuroidea are found in various marine environments. 螠虫动物门发现于各种海洋环境中。|近义词:spoon worms|反义词:None|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek *ēchī* (viper, adder) + *oura* (tail) + *-oidea* (suffix forming names of animal classes and orders). The name refers to the shape of some species, which resembles a viper's tail.
