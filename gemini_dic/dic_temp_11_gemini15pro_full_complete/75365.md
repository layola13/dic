froufrou:/ˈfruːfruː/ (US), /ˈfruːfruː/ (UK)| n. adj.|装饰华丽的，繁复的；充满褶皱的；沙沙作响的|Frills or ornamentation, typically on women's clothing; a rustling sound.|The dress had a lot of froufrou. 这条裙子有很多褶边。|She wore a froufrou pink dress. 她穿着一件装饰华丽的粉色连衣裙。|近义词：frills, flounces, ornamentation, ruffles, elaborate, fussy|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性: 名词, 形容词
Etymology: French, imitative of the sound of rustling silk.
