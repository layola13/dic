foreshow:/fɔːrˈʃoʊ/ (US), /fɔːˈʃəʊ/ (UK)| v.|预示，预兆|to show or indicate beforehand; portend|例句：The dark clouds foreshow a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。

例句：His nervousness foreshadowed his poor performance. 他的紧张预示着他糟糕的表现。

近义词：presage, portend, foretell, predict, augur|反义词: conceal, hide|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *foreshewen, from Old English forsċēawian (“to look at beforehand, foresee, provide for”), from for- (“before”) + sċēawian (“to look at, see, view”). Equivalent to fore- +‎ show.
