elocutionist:/ˌɛləˈkjuːʃənɪst/ | n. | 演说家；雄辩家；朗诵家 | A person who is skilled in the art of public speaking, especially one who delivers speeches with eloquent and expressive delivery. |  She was a renowned elocutionist, captivating audiences with her powerful voice and dramatic flair. 她是一位著名的演说家，用她洪亮的声音和戏剧化的技巧吸引着观众。

| 近义词: orator, speaker, rhetorician | 反义词: mumbler, stammerer | 词性: noun

elocution:/ˌɛləˈkjuːʃən/ | n. | 演说术；朗诵法；口才 | The art of clear and expressive speech, especially of distinct pronunciation and articulation. | Her elocution was impeccable, every word delivered with precision and clarity. 她的口才无可挑剔，每个字都清晰而准确。

| 近义词: diction, articulation, pronunciation | 反义词: inarticulacy | 词性: noun
