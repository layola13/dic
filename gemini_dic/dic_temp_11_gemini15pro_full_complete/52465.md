dieses:/ˈdiːzəs/ (German)| demonstrative pronoun, determiner|这，这个，这些|This word is a demonstrative pronoun or determiner in German, used to point to something nearby or previously mentioned. It inflects based on the gender and case of the noun it modifies.  It corresponds roughly to "this" or "these" in English.|Dieses Buch ist interessant. This book is interesting.|近义词：dieser, diese, dieses (different forms of the same word depending on gender and case)|反义词：jener, jene, jenes (that)|词性：pronoun, determiner

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Dieses Haus ist groß.  This house is big.

Dieses Auto fährt schnell. This car drives fast.


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Not applicable in German.  German doesn't have a direct equivalent to the present continuous tense. The simple present or other constructions are used to express actions happening now.


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Ich habe dieses Buch gelesen. I have read this book.

Er hat dieses Auto gekauft. He has bought this car.


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Not applicable in German. German doesn't have a direct equivalent to the present perfect continuous. Other constructions are used to convey a similar meaning.

虚拟语气:
Wenn ich dieses Buch hätte, würde ich es lesen. If I had this book, I would read it.

Wenn er dieses Auto hätte, würde er es fahren. If he had this car, he would drive it.


被动语态:
Dieses Buch wird gelesen. This book is being read.

疑问句:
Ist dieses Haus groß? Is this house big?

Gefällt dir dieses Auto? Do you like this car?


否定句:
Dieses Haus ist nicht groß. This house is not big.

Dieses Auto ist nicht schnell. This car is not fast.

注意事项:

The word "dieses" changes depending on the gender, number and case of the noun it refers to.

German doesn't have direct equivalents for all English tenses, particularly the continuous tenses.
