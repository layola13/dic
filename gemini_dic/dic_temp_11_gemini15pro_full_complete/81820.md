grew:/ɡruː/
verb
长大; 成长;  增加; 变得
past simple of grow. It means to increase in size, amount, or degree.
The city grew rapidly in the 19th century.  这个城市在19世纪迅速发展。
The problem grew worse over time.  问题随着时间的推移变得更糟。
近义词: develop, increase, expand, enlarge
反义词: shrink, decrease, diminish, contract
词性: verb
