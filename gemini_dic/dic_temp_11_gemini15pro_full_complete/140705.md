omnibuses:/ˈɒmnɪbʌsɪz/ (UK), /ˈɑːmnɪbʌsɪz/ (US)| n. |公共汽车（复数）; 汇编; |A volume containing several novels or other items previously published separately.  A bus or coach for public transportation. |The museum runs two omnibuses to the city centre every hour.  博物馆每小时有两班公共汽车开往市中心。|Synonyms: buses, coaches (for vehicles); anthologies, compilations (for books)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the vehicle sense. For the book sense, perhaps "individual publication")|Noun


omnibus:/ˈɒmnɪbʌs/ (UK), /ˈɑːmnɪbʌsɪz/ (US)| adj. |多项的; 综合的; |Including many things at once; comprehensive. |The omnibus spending bill included funding for several different programs.  综合支出法案包括为几个不同项目的拨款。|Synonyms: comprehensive, inclusive, all-encompassing|Antonyms: limited, specific, narrow|Adjective
