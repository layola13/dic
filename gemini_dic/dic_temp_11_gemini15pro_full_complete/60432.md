eiking: /ˈaɪkɪŋ/
noun
king, chieftain (especially in Viking times)
A leader or ruler, particularly during the Viking Age, often associated with seafaring, raiding, and exploration.
例句：The eiking led his warriors on a raid across the North Sea.  这位海盗王带领他的战士突袭了北海。

例句：Legends tell of a powerful eiking who discovered new lands. 传说有一位强大的海盗王发现了新的土地。

近义词：king, chieftain, leader, ruler, warrior
反义词：follower, subject
