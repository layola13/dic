hipness:/ˈhɪpnəs/| n.|时髦，赶时髦，熟悉潮流|The state or quality of being hip, fashionable, or aware of current trends.  It refers to a person's understanding and engagement with what is considered cool or trendy in music, fashion, culture, or other areas.|Her hipness was evident in her vintage clothing and knowledge of underground music. 她的时髦从她的复古服装和对地下音乐的了解中可见一斑。|Synonyms: trendiness, coolness, stylishness|Antonyms: uncoolness, squareness|noun
