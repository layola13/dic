nonstampable:/ˌnɒnˈstæmpəbl/
adjective
不可盖章的
Not capable of being stamped, or unsuitable for stamping.
This type of plastic bag is nonstampable due to its textured surface. 这种塑料袋由于表面有纹理而不能盖章。
The fabric is too delicate and therefore nonstampable.  这种织物太精致了，因此不能盖章。
近义词:unstampable
反义词:stampable
词性:adjective
