gadling:/ˈɡæd.lɪŋ/ | n. | 流浪汉；浪子；游手好闲的人 | A person who travels from place to place, especially one who does so without a settled home or regular work. | He was no more than a gadling, wandering from town to town. 他不过是个浪子，从一个镇子流浪到另一个镇子。| 近义词：vagabond, wanderer, rover, drifter | 反义词：homebody, settler | 词性: 名词

gadling:/ˈɡæd.lɪŋ/ | n. | 闲逛者；四处游荡的人 | A person who spends their time idly wandering or traveling. | The streets were filled with gadlings, enjoying the summer afternoon. 街上挤满了闲逛的人，享受着夏日的午后。| 近义词：loafer, idler, dawdler | 反义词：worker, busybody | 词性: 名词
