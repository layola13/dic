bodychecked: /ˈbɒdiˌtʃɛkt/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdiˌtʃɛkt/ (US)
verb
(冰球、曲棍球等)阻截，冲撞，身体拦截
To obstruct or impede (an opponent) in ice hockey, lacrosse, etc., with one's body.
The defenseman bodychecked the forward before he could get a shot off.  防守队员在对方前锋射门前用身体阻挡了他。
He was bodychecked into the boards. 他被撞到挡板上。
check, hit, tackle
--
verb
(非正式)严厉批评；谴责；训斥
To criticize or rebuke (someone) severely.
The coach bodychecked the player for his lack of effort. 教练严厉批评了该球员的消极比赛态度。
The boss bodychecked him for being late.  老板因为他迟到而训斥了他。
reprimand, scold, rebuke
