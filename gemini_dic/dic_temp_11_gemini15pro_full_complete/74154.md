n.

foundling:/ˈfaʊnd.lɪŋ/
n.
弃婴，被遗弃的孩子
A foundling is a baby or very young child who has been found abandoned and whose parents are unknown.
The nuns established an orphanage for foundlings. 修女们为弃婴建立了一个孤儿院。
近义词:orphan, deserted child
反义词: (无直接反义词，可用adopted child领养的孩子)
词性: 名词


词源:
From Middle English *fondling, *fundlyng, from Anglo-Norman fundling, present participle of fundre (“to pour out, shed”), from Latin fundere (“to pour, melt, cast”).  The original sense was “something poured out or cast away.”
