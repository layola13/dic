misapplication:/ˌmɪsæplɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
误用，滥用
the act of using something incorrectly or for the wrong purpose.
The misapplication of funds led to the company's bankruptcy.  资金的误用导致了公司的破产。

The misapplication of this medication can be dangerous.  这种药物的误用是很危险的。

近义词: misuse, abuse, perversion
反义词: proper use, correct application
词性: noun
