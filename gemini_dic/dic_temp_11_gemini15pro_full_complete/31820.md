checkwork:/ˈtʃɛkwɜːrk/
noun
格子图案；格子细工；方格花纹；格子布
work consisting of squares of different colors, like the pattern on a chessboard.
The tablecloth had a simple red and white checkwork pattern.  桌布是简单的红白格子图案。
近义词: checkered pattern, plaid, latticework
反义词: solid color, plain pattern
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "check" (meaning a pattern of squares) + "work" (meaning a product of labor or activity).  "Check" in this sense ultimately derives from Old French "eschec," meaning "chess" or "chessboard."
