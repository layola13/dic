anagogical:/ˌænəˈɡɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 寓意的，神秘的，奥秘的 | Relating to or revealing a higher or spiritual meaning, especially in literature or art. |  The anagogical interpretation of the text revealed a hidden message about salvation. 这篇文章的寓言解释揭示了一个关于救赎的隐藏信息。 |例句：The artist's work is rich in anagogical symbolism. 这位艺术家的作品充满了寓言象征意义。| 近义词：symbolic, allegorical, mystical, spiritual | 反义词：literal, concrete | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *anagogicus*, from Greek *anagōgikos* (“leading upward”), from *anagōgē* (“a leading upward”), from *ana* (“up”) + *agō* (“I lead”).
