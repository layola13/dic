antiputrescent:/ˌæntiˌpjuːtrɪˈsɛnt/| adj.|防腐的，抗腐败的|Preventing or arresting putrefaction or decay; antiseptic.|This antiputrescent cream can help preserve the food.  这种防腐霜有助于保存食物。|Synonyms: antiseptic, preservative, antibacterial, antimicrobial|Antonyms: putrescent, decaying, rotting|adjective
