awfullest:/ˈɔːfəlɪst/ | adj. | 极糟糕的，最可怕的 |  The superlative form of "awful," meaning extremely bad or unpleasant. While grammatically correct, it's considered nonstandard and "most awful" is generally preferred in formal writing.  |  That was the awfullest movie I've ever seen. 那是我看过的最糟糕的电影。 |  terrible, horrible, dreadful, atrocious | wonderful, excellent, fantastic | adjective
