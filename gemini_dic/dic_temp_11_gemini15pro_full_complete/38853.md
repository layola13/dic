conjecture:/kənˈdʒek.tʃər/ | n. vt.|推测，猜想；推测，猜想|An opinion or conclusion formed on the basis of incomplete information. |例句：It is pure conjecture that the manuscript is genuine.  这份手稿是真的纯粹是猜测。|近义词：guess, speculation, surmise, supposition|反义词：fact, certainty, knowledge|noun
conjecture:/kənˈdʒek.tʃər/ | vt.|推测，猜想|Form an opinion or conclusion about (something) on the basis of incomplete information.|例句：He conjectured that the king was already dead. 他推测国王已经死了。|近义词：guess, speculate, surmise, suppose|反义词：know, ascertain, prove|verb
