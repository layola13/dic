opaquely:/oʊˈpeɪkli/| adv.|不透明地；含糊地；隐晦地|in a way that is difficult to understand; not clearly expressed or easily understood|The report was written so opaquely that no one could understand it. 这份报告写得非常晦涩，没有人能理解。|The artist used opaquely colored glass to create the mosaic. 艺术家用不透明的彩色玻璃创作了这幅马赛克作品。|Synonyms: obscurely, vaguely, unclearly, ambiguously|Antonyms: clearly, transparently, lucidly|Adverb
