gismondine:/ˈɡɪzmənˌdaɪn/ | n. |吉斯蒙丁沸石|A zeolite mineral, a hydrated calcium aluminium silicate.  It occurs as white or colorless crystals. |Gismondine is often found in volcanic rocks. 吉斯蒙丁沸石常发现于火山岩中。| The gismondine crystals were small but perfectly formed. 吉斯蒙丁沸石晶体很小，但形态完美。 |近义词:zeolite, phillipsite, chabazite|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
