munerary:/ˈmjuːnərəri/ | adj. | 葬礼的，丧葬的 | Of or relating to a funeral or burial. |  The family held a small, munerary service for their beloved pet.  家人为他们心爱的宠物举行了一个小型葬礼。 |  funereal, sepulchral | celebratory, festive | adjective


Etymology:

From Latin *mūnerārius*, from *mūnus* ("gift, office, duty, service") + *-ārius* (suffix forming adjectives of appurtenance).  The sense development arose from the Roman custom of offering gifts to the dead.
