duplicitous:/djuːˈplɪsɪtəs/| adj.|欺骗的，奸诈的，两面派的|Deceitful, dishonest, and insincere, typically in a way that is meant to trick someone.|He played a duplicitous game, pretending to be her friend while secretly plotting against her. 他玩弄两面手法，假装是她的朋友，却暗中密谋反对她。|近义词：deceitful, dishonest, two-faced, cunning, crafty, wily, treacherous, insidious, mendacious|反义词：honest, sincere, truthful, straightforward, frank, candid, open, aboveboard|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *duplicitas* "doubleness," from *duplex* "twofold," from *duo* "two" + *plicō* "to fold."
