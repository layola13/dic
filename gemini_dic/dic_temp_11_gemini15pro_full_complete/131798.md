nonconscriptable:/ˌnɒnkənˈskrɪptəbl/ | adj. | 不可征召的，免服兵役的 | Not liable or eligible to be conscripted or drafted into military service. |  Example:  He was declared nonconscriptable due to a medical condition. 他由于健康状况被宣布免服兵役。

Example:  Many religious objectors are nonconscriptable. 许多出于宗教原因的拒服兵役者都可免服兵役。| 近义词：exempt, ineligible | 反义词：conscriptable, eligible | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + conscript (verb meaning "to enlist compulsorily") + -able (suffix forming adjectives)
