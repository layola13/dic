apposer:/əˈpəʊzər/
n.
放置者，并置者
Someone or something that places things side by side, especially for comparison.
The apposer placed the two paintings next to each other to highlight the contrast in styles.  放置者将两幅画并排放置，以突出风格上的对比。
近义词: juxtaposer, placer
反义词: separator, divider
词性: noun


appose:/əˈpəʊz/
vt.
并置，放置
To place side by side, especially for comparison or contrast.
The artist apposed the vibrant colors against a stark white background. 艺术家将鲜艳的色彩与纯白的背景并置。
近义词: juxtapose, place, position
反义词: separate, divide
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin appositus, past participle of apponere  "to put to, place near," from ad- "to" + ponere "to put, place."
