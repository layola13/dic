call-a-spade-a-spade:/ˌkɔːl ə ˈspeɪd ə ˈspeɪd/| idiom | 实话实说；直言不讳 | To speak frankly and directly about something, even if it is unpleasant or controversial.  | He's not very tactful; he always calls a spade a spade. 他不太圆滑，总是直言不讳。 | 近义词: speak frankly, be blunt, tell it like it is, not mince words | 反义词: beat around the bush, sugarcoat, mince words | idiom
