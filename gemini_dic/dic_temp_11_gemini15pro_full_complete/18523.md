benefaction: /ˌbɛnɪˈfækʃən/ | n. | 善行，捐赠 | An act of kindness or generosity, especially a charitable donation. |  Her benefactions to the university totalled over a million dollars. 她对这所大学的捐款总额超过一百万美元。 |  Example: His anonymous benefaction allowed the hospital to buy new equipment. 他的匿名捐赠使医院能够购买新设备。

Example: The library was built thanks to a generous benefaction from a local businessman.  由于当地一位商人的慷慨捐赠，这座图书馆得以建成。

| 近义词：donation, contribution, gift, endowment | 反义词：malefaction, detriment | 词性: 名词
