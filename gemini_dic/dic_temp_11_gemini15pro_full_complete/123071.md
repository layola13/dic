moneyball:/ˈmʌniˌbɔːl/
noun
金钱球，指棒球运动中基于数据分析的球队管理策略
A strategy in baseball, made popular by the Oakland A's, of using statistical analysis to evaluate and recruit players, focusing on undervalued skills rather than traditional measures.
The Oakland A's used moneyball to build a competitive team with a limited budget.  奥克兰运动家队利用金钱球策略以有限的预算打造了一支有竞争力的球队。

The book "Moneyball" tells the story of Billy Beane and his use of sabermetrics.  《点球成金》这本书讲述了比利·比恩和他使用赛伯计量学的故事。
近义词:sabermetrics (棒球统计分析)
反义词:traditional scouting (传统的球探方法)
词性:n.
