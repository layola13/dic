overlashing:/ˌoʊvərˈlæʃɪŋ/ | n. | 猛烈抨击，严厉斥责；过度反应 | A severe rebuke or denunciation; an excessive reaction. | The politician received an overlashing from the media after his controversial remarks.  这位政治家因其有争议的言论受到了媒体的猛烈抨击。|  reprimand, censure, criticism, attack | praise, commendation | noun

overlash:/ˌoʊvərˈlæʃ/ | v. | 猛烈抨击，严厉斥责；过度反应；(鞭子等)甩过头顶 | To rebuke or denounce severely; to react excessively; (of a whip, etc.) to swing back over the head. | The media overlashed the politician for his controversial remarks. 媒体严厉斥责了这位政治家发表的有争议的言论。 | reprimand, censure, criticize, attack | praise, commend | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From over + lash (v.).  The original sense referred to a whip lashing back over the head, then extended to the figurative sense of excessive reaction or criticism.  "Over" in this context means "excessively" or "too much."
