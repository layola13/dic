locomotivity:/ˌloʊkəmoʊˈtɪvɪti/| n. |移动能力，运动能力|The ability to move, especially to move from place to place.|The amoeba's locomotivity allows it to explore its environment.  变形虫的运动能力使其能够探索周围环境。|例句：The injury severely impaired his locomotivity.  这次受伤严重损害了他的行动能力。|近义词：mobility, movement, motion|反义词：immobility, stasis|词性: noun

locomotion:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊʃən/| n. |移动，运动|The act or power of moving from place to place.|Walking is a common form of locomotion.  步行是一种常见的运动方式。|例句：The invention of the wheel revolutionized locomotion.  车轮的发明彻底改变了运动方式。|近义词：movement, travel, transit|反义词：immobility, stasis|词性: noun
