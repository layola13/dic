clanism: /ˈklænɪzəm/
n.
宗族主义；家族主义；小集团主义
Excessive loyalty to one's own clan or group, often at the expense of others.  Favoring members of one's own clan or group, especially in appointments to positions.
Clanism can lead to corruption and inefficiency in government. 宗族主义会导致政府腐败和效率低下。

The company's hiring practices were criticized for promoting clanism. 该公司的招聘做法因 promoting clanism 而受到批评。

近义词: nepotism, cronyism, favoritism, sectarianism
反义词: impartiality, fairness, meritocracy
词性: noun
