estivate: /ˈɛstɪveɪt/ (US) /ˈiːstɪveɪt/ (UK)| v. | 夏眠；过夏 |To spend the summer in a state of dormancy or inactivity, similar to hibernation in winter.| During the hottest months, the desert tortoise estivates to avoid the extreme heat. 在最热的几个月里，沙漠龟会夏眠以避免酷暑。| 近义词：hibernate, sleep, lie dormant | 反义词：be active, awaken |词性：动词

estivation: /ˌɛstɪˈveɪʃən/ (US) /ˌiːstɪˈveɪʃən/ (UK)| n. | 夏眠；过夏 |The state of dormancy or inactivity during the summer.| Estivation is a survival strategy for many desert animals. 夏眠是许多沙漠动物的生存策略。| 近义词：hibernation, dormancy | 反义词：activity |词性：名词
