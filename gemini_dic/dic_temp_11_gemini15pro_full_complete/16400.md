barbule:/ˈbɑːrbjʊl/ (US), /ˈbɑːbjuːl/ (UK)| n. |小羽枝；羽支|A small, fine filament projecting from a barb of a feather, forming part of the interlocking structure that gives feathers their strength and shape.  It's like a tiny hook that helps hold the feather together.|The barbules of the down feather interlock to trap air and provide insulation. 绒羽的小羽枝互相交织以留住空气并提供隔热。|The intricate network of barbules gives the bird's wings their aerodynamic properties.  错综复杂的小羽枝网络赋予鸟翼空气动力学特性。|synonyms: filament, barb branch|antonyms: N/A|noun
