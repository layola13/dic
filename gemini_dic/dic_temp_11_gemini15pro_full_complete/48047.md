deckle:/ˈdɛkəl/| n.|毛边纸，毛边，纸槽|The rough, untrimmed edge of a sheet of paper.  A frame used in papermaking to form the edge of the sheet.|The artist tore the deckle edge off the watercolor paper.|Synonyms: rough edge, feathered edge, untrimmed edge|Antonyms: smooth edge, trimmed edge|noun
