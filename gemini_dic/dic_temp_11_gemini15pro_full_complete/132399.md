nondemonstrativeness:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɒnstrətɪvnəs/
noun
不流露感情；含蓄；内敛
Lack of outward display of emotion or affection.
He was known for his nondemonstrativeness, rarely showing affection even to his family. 他以不轻易表露情感而闻名，即使对家人也很少流露爱意。
Synonyms: reserved, restrained, undemonstrative, inhibited, reticent
Antonyms: demonstrative, affectionate, expressive, effusive, outgoing
词性: 名词
