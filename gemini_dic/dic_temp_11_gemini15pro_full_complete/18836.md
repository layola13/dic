besaile:/bɪˈseɪl/ | v. |包围，围攻；用帆装饰|To furnish or decorate with sails; to surround; to encompass. |The ship was besailed with colorful flags for the festival. 节日里，船上装饰着五彩缤纷的旗帜。|近义词：surround, encompass, besiege|反义词：expose, uncover|词性:动词

词源: From be- + sail.  The prefix "be-" adds intensity or completeness to the verb "sail."  Essentially, it implies being completely surrounded or enveloped, as if by sails.
