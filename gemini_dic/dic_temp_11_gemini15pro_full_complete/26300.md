butyrone:/ˈbjuːtɪˌroʊn/ | n. | 二丙酮;4-庚酮 |A colorless liquid ketone, C₇H₁₄O, having a peppermint-like odor. It is used as a solvent and in organic synthesis. |Butyrone is a solvent for nitrocellulose. 二丙酮是硝化纤维素的溶剂。|  Butyrone is also known as dipropyl ketone. 二丙酮也称为二丙基酮。| synonym: dipropyl ketone, 4-heptanone | antonym: N/A | noun
