brominate:/ˈbroʊməneɪt/| v. |溴化; 用溴处理|To treat or combine with bromine. |例句：The chemist decided to brominate the compound. 化学家决定将该化合物溴化。

例句：They brominated the alkene to form a vicinal dibromide. 他们将烯烃溴化，形成了邻位二溴化物。|近义词：halogenate|反义词: debrominate|词性:动词
