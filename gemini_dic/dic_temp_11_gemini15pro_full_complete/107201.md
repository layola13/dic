laguna:/ləˈɡuːnə/ /ləˈɡʊnə/| n. |泻湖；环礁湖|A shallow body of water, especially one separated from the sea by sandbars or coral reefs. |例句：We kayaked across the tranquil laguna. 我们乘皮划艇穿过平静的泻湖。

例句：The laguna was teeming with marine life.  泻湖里充满了海洋生物。


近义词：lagoon, pool, pond|反义词:ocean, sea|词性:noun
