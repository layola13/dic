clotty:/ˈklɒti/ (UK) /ˈklɑːti/ (US)| adj.|凝块的，结块的|Containing or resembling clots; thick and lumpy.|The paint was old and clotty, making it difficult to apply. 这油漆又旧又结块，很难涂抹。|例句：
The blood from the wound was thick and clotty.  伤口流出的血又浓又稠。

The gravy was too clotty and didn't pour well.  肉汁太稠了，倒不出来。


近义词：lumpy, thick, coagulated|反义词:smooth, thin, runny|词性:adjective

clot:/klɒt/ (UK) /klɑːt/ (US)| n. v.|凝块，血块；凝结，使凝结|A thick mass of coagulated liquid, especially blood; to form or cause to form clots.|A clot formed in his artery, blocking the blood flow. 他的动脉里形成了血块，阻塞了血液流动。|例句：
The doctor worked quickly to remove the blood clot. 医生迅速地清除了血块。

Adding lemon juice will help the milk clot.  加柠檬汁有助于牛奶凝结。


近义词：lump, clump, coagulation|反义词:dissolve, liquefy|词性:noun, verb
