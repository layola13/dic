nonaccommodation:/ˌnɒnəkɒməˈdeɪʃn/
n.
不适应，不调节，不妥协
Lack of adjustment or compromise; failure or refusal to accommodate.
The nonaccommodation of the company to the changing market led to its downfall.  这家公司未能适应变化的市场，导致了它的衰败。
The union's nonaccommodation on the key issue led to a strike. 工会在关键问题上不妥协导致了罢工。
intransigence, inflexibility, rigidity
accommodation, compromise, adjustment
noun
