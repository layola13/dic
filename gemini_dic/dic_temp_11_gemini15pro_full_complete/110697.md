linearities:/ˌlɪniˈærɪtiz/| n. |线性|The plural form of linearity, referring to multiple instances of being linear.  It describes systems or relationships that exhibit a straight-line progression or direct proportionality. |The engineer studied the linearities of the system's response to different inputs.  工程师研究了系统对不同输入的线性响应。|The graph demonstrated the linearities between voltage and current. 这张图表展示了电压和电流之间的线性关系。|synonyms: straightness, directness| antonyms: nonlinearity, curvature| noun
