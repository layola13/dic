non-original:/ˌnɒn əˈrɪdʒɪnəl/ | adj. | 非原创的；缺乏创意的 | Not new, fresh, or unique; derived from something else. |  This song sounds non-original; I think I've heard the melody somewhere before. 这首歌听起来不新颖；我觉得我在哪儿听过这旋律。

The plot of this movie is completely non-original and predictable.  这部电影的情节完全没有创意，而且可以预见。 | Synonyms: derivative, unoriginal, copied, clichéd, banal, stock, uninspired | Antonyms: original, innovative, creative, novel, fresh, unique, groundbreaking | adjective
