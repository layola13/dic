nonascendences:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndənsiz/
|n.| 非优势;非支配地位|A state of not having ascendancy or control; lack of dominance.  It signifies the absence of rising influence or power.|The nonascendences of the smaller nations in the global economy are a concern.| 近义词: subordination, subjection, dependence, inferiority| 反义词: dominance, ascendancy, supremacy, superiority|词性:名词
