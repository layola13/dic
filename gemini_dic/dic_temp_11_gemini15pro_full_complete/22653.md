borax:/ˈbɔːræks/ (US) /ˈbɔːræks/ (UK)| n.|硼砂; 粗硼砂|A white crystalline compound, hydrated sodium borate, used as a flux, in making glass, as a cleansing agent, and as a preservative.|Borax is often used in laundry detergents. 硼砂常用于洗衣粉中。|Synonyms: sodium borate, sodium tetraborate|Antonyms: None|noun

boraxes:/ˈbɔːræksɪz/ (US) /ˈbɔːræksɪz/ (UK)| n.|硼砂（borax的复数形式）|The plural form of borax, referring to multiple instances or types of this compound.|Different types of boraxes are used in various industrial processes. 不同种类的硼砂用于各种工业流程。|Synonyms: sodium borates, sodium tetraborates|Antonyms: None|noun
