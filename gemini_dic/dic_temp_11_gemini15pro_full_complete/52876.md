diminuendo:/dɪˌmɪnjuˈɛndoʊ/ /dɪˌmɪnjuˈɛndəʊ/ | n. adv. adj.| 渐弱，递减 |A gradual decrease in the volume of music or other sound. diminuendo:/dɪˌmɪnjuˈɛndoʊ/ /dɪˌmɪnjuˈɛndəʊ/ | adv.| 渐弱地，递减地 |In a gradually decreasing manner, especially in music. |The violinist played the passage diminuendo. 小提琴手渐弱地演奏了这段乐句。| synonym: decrescendo | antonym: crescendo | adverb
