asbo: /ˈæzbəʊ/ (UK)
noun
反社会行为令 (Anti-Social Behaviour Order)
An order issued by a court in the UK against a person who has been shown to have engaged in anti-social behaviour.
例句：He received an ASBO for repeatedly playing loud music at night.  他因反复在夜间大声播放音乐而收到了反社会行为令。

例句：The police issued an ASBO to the teenager who was vandalizing property.  警察向那个破坏财产的青少年发出了反社会行为令。

近义词：injunction, order, restriction
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider concepts like "commendation," "reward," or "approval")
词性: noun
