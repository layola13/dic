anticonscription:/ˌæntiˌkənˈskrɪpʃən/
|adj. n.|反征兵的，反征兵者|Opposed to conscription (compulsory enlistment for military service).|The anticonscription movement gained momentum during the Vietnam War.  反征兵运动在越南战争期间势头强劲。|
The young man, a staunch anticonscriptionist, refused to register for the draft.  这个年轻人是一个坚定的反征兵主义者，拒绝登记入伍。|
近义词：antidraft, pacifist|反义词：pro-conscription, militarist|词性: adjective, noun
