maladministration:/ˌmælədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/
|n.| 管理不善，治理不当|The maladministration of the company led to its bankruptcy. 该公司的管理不善导致其破产。|Example: The report revealed widespread maladministration within the government.  这份报告揭露了政府内部普遍存在的管理不善。|Synonyms: mismanagement, misconduct, misgovernment, corruption, incompetence|Antonyms: good governance, efficient administration|noun
