disincorporate:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/
verb
解散（公司）；使脱离法人资格
To deprive (a company, city, etc.) of corporate status; to dissolve or break up (a corporation).
The company was disincorporated after years of financial struggles.  这家公司在多年的财务困境后解散了。
The city voted to disincorporate and become a village. 该市投票决定解散并成为一个村庄。
近义词: dissolve, liquidate, break up
反义词: incorporate, establish, found
词性: verb


disincorporate:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/
adjective
已解散的；非公司组织的
Not incorporated; not formed into a legal corporation.
The formerly disincorporated community is now seeking to regain its city status.  这个以前解散的社区现在正在寻求恢复其城市地位。
Many disincorporated businesses operate as sole proprietorships or partnerships. 许多未成立公司的企业以独资企业或合伙企业的方式经营。
近义词: unincorporated, dissolved
反义词: incorporated, established
词性: adjective
