antithesis:/ænˈtɪθəsɪs/
|n.|对照，对立，对偶|a person or thing that is the direct opposite of someone or something else; a contrast or opposition between two things|例句：His carefree nature is the antithesis of his brother's seriousness. 他的无忧无虑的性格与他兄弟的严肃认真形成了鲜明的对比。|例句：Love is the antithesis of selfishness. 爱是自私的对立面。|近义词：opposite, contrast, reverse|反义词：similarity, resemblance|词性:名词

antithetical:/ˌæntɪˈθɛtɪk(ə)l/
|adj.|对立的，相反的|directly opposed or contrasted; mutually incompatible|例句：His views are antithetical to mine. 他的观点与我的截然相反。|例句：The idea of progress is antithetical to the concept of tradition.  进步的理念与传统的概念是对立的。|近义词：opposite, contrary, contrasting|反义词：similar, alike, compatible|词性:形容词
