coolants:/ˈkuːlənts/ | n. | 冷却剂；冷却液 | Substances used to reduce or absorb heat, often in engines or industrial processes.  |  The engine overheated because there weren't enough coolants.  发动机因为冷却液不足而过热。| synonyms: refrigerants, antifreeze | antonyms: heating agents | noun
