blithering:/ˈblɪðərɪŋ/| adj.|胡诌的，胡扯的，愚蠢的|Talking foolishly or senselessly.  Characterized by meaningless or foolish talk.|He was blithering on about something I couldn't understand. 他胡言乱语地说着一些我听不懂的事情。|近义词：babbling, rambling, nonsensical, foolish, idiotic|反义词：sensible, intelligent, coherent|adjective

blither:/ˈblɪðər/| v.|胡说，瞎扯|To talk foolishly or without making sense.|Stop blithering and tell me what happened! 别胡说了，告诉我发生了什么事！|近义词：babble, ramble, prate|反义词：articulate, speak clearly|verb
