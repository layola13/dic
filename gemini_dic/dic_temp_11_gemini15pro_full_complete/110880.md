lionizes:/ˈlaɪənaɪzɪz/
verb
崇敬；把…视为名人；将…捧为偶像
To treat (someone) as a celebrity.
The media lionizes athletes. 媒体把运动员捧为偶像。
Synonyms:  idolize, glorify, exalt, celebrate, admire, worship
Antonyms:  criticize, condemn, denigrate, disparage, vilify
词性:v.
