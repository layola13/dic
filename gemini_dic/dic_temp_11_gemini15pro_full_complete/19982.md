biogenist:/ˌbaɪoʊˈdʒɛnɪst/| n. |生物学家|A scientist who studies the origin, structure, development, function, and distribution of animals and plants.|例句：The biogenist specialized in marine biology.  这位生物学家专门研究海洋生物学。

例句：The biogenist is conducting research on genetic modification in plants.  这位生物学家正在进行植物基因改造的研究。|近义词：biologist, life scientist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: bio- (life) + -genist (one who produces or studies)
