feminism:/ˈfɛmɪnɪzəm/| n. |女权主义|The advocacy of women's rights on the ground of the equality of the sexes.|例句：She is a strong advocate for feminism. 她是一位坚定的女权主义倡导者。

例句：Feminism has made great strides in recent decades. 近几十年来，女权主义取得了巨大的进步。|近义词：womanism, gender equality|反义词: sexism, misogyny|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *féminisme*, from *féminin* (“feminine”) + *-isme* (“-ism”).
