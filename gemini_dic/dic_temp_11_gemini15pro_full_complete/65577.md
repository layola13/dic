evacuations:/ɪˌvækjuˈeɪʃənz/
n.
撤离，疏散
The act of removing people from a dangerous place.
例句：The evacuations were ordered because of the approaching hurricane.  由于飓风逼近，下令进行疏散。

例句：The city ordered evacuations ahead of the flood.  该市在洪水来临前下令疏散。

近义词：removals, withdrawals
反义词：occupations, entrances
词性：名词
