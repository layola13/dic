intramural: /ˌɪntrəˈmjʊrəl/ | adj. | 校内的，单位内的 | Existing or occurring within the bounds of an institution, especially a school or university. |  Our college has a very active intramural sports program. 我们学院有一个非常活跃的校内体育项目。| Example: The intramural basketball tournament starts next week. 校内篮球锦标赛下周开始。 | Synonyms: internal, domestic, inner | Antonyms: intercollegiate, interscholastic, external | Adjective
