centralest: /ˈsɛntrəlɪst/
adjective
最中心的
most central; situated at the very center.  This word is considered non-standard and rarely used.  It is grammatically incorrect to use the "-est" suffix with "central."
This building is supposedly the centralest point in the city. (Non-standard usage) 这个建筑据说是城市的最中心点。
He claimed his apartment was the centralest in the whole complex. (Non-standard usage) 他声称他的公寓是整个小区最中心的。
近义词: most central, innermost, middlemost
反义词: outermost, furthest, peripheral
词性: adjective

词源: From "central" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Central" derives from Late Latin "centralis," from "centrum" (center).
