homopolic:/ˌhɒməˈpɒlɪk/ /ˌhoʊmoʊˈpɑːlɪk/ | adj. | 同极的，单极的 |  Having or relating to a single pole or polarity.  Describes a situation where the poles or terminals of a circuit or device have the same polarity, like both positive or both negative. This often results in no current flow. | The homopolar motor is a simple DC motor with two magnetic poles.  这台单极电机是一个简单的带有两个磁极的直流电机。

The homopolar generator produces a direct current. 这台单极发电机产生直流电。 | 近义词: unipolar | 反义词: heteropolar, bipolar | 词性: adjective
