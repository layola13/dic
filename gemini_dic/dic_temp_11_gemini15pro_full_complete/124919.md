mudsill: /ˈmʌdsɪl/ | n. | 底梁，基础；基石；（比喻）社会底层 | A horizontal timber or beam laid on the ground to support the foundation of a building; (figuratively) the lowest or poorest class of people, especially seen as the essential foundation of society. | The house's mudsill had rotted, causing the whole structure to shift. 这座房子的底梁腐烂了，导致整个结构发生了偏移。

The politician referred to immigrants as the "mudsills" of society, a comment seen by many as offensive.  这位政客将移民称为社会的“底层”，这一评论被许多人视为冒犯。 | 近义词：foundation, base, bedrock, underpinning, cornerstone; (figuratively) working class, proletariat | 反义词: apex, peak, summit; (figuratively) elite, aristocracy | 词性: 名词
