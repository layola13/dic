coinquinate:/kɔɪŋˈkwɪneɪt/
|v.|污染，玷污|To make impure or unclean by contact or mixture; to contaminate or pollute.|Industrial waste coinquinates the river. 工业废料污染了河流。|近义词:contaminate, pollute, defile, taint|反义词:purify, cleanse, clean|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *coinquinatus, past participle of coinquinare "to defile, pollute," from Latin com- "together" + inquinare "to stain, pollute," from in- "in" + *quinere "to make dirty," related to Old Irish cuan "dirt, filth."
