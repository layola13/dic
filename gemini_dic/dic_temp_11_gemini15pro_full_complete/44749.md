crumblier:/ˈkrʌmbliər/| adj.|更易碎的，更酥脆的|More crumbly than something else; easily broken into small fragments.|The cake was crumblier than I expected.  这蛋糕比我想象的更酥脆。|The old bread was dry and crumblier.  这块老面包又干又碎。|近义词：friable, brittle, flaky|反义词:tough, firm, compact|词性:形容词
crumbliest:/ˈkrʌmbliɪst/| adj.|最易碎的，最酥脆的|Most crumbly; most easily broken into small fragments.|This is the crumbliest cheese I've ever tasted. 这是我尝过的最酥脆的奶酪。|Of all the biscuits, these are the crumbliest.  所有饼干中，这些最酥脆。|近义词：most friable, most brittle, most flaky|反义词:toughest, firmest, most compact|词性:形容词

词源:
From crumb + -ly + -er/-est.  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *krum-, meaning "crooked" or "bent."
