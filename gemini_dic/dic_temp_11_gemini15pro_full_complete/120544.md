miracle: /ˈmɪrəkəl/ (US) /ˈmɪrəkl/ (UK)| n. |奇迹，奇事|An event that appears inexplicable by the laws of nature and so is attributed to a supernatural or divine agency.|例句：The doctor said her recovery was a miracle. 医生说她的康复是一个奇迹。

例句：It's a miracle that no one was hurt in the accident.  事故中没有人受伤真是个奇迹。

近义词：wonder, marvel, phenomenon|反义词:disaster, curse|词性:名词
