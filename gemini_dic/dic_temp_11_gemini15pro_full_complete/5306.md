allottee:/əˌlɒˈtiː/ /əˌlɑːˈtiː/
n.
受让人，分配到的人
a person who is allotted something
The allottee is responsible for paying the annual maintenance fee. 受让人负责支付年度维护费。
近义词：recipient, beneficiary
反义词：allocator, grantor
词性：noun

Etymology:
From allot + -ee.  Allot comes from Old French aloter "to divide, share, apportion," from a- "to" + lot "share, portion."
