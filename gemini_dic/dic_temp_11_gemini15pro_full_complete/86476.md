Heidelberg: /ˈhaɪdl̩bɜːrɡ/ (British) /ˈhaɪdl̩bɜːrɡ/ (American)
|n.|海德堡|A city in southwestern Germany, situated on the Neckar River.|例句：Heidelberg is famous for its romantic atmosphere and historic castle. 海德堡以其浪漫的氛围和历史悠久的城堡而闻名。|例句: I studied at Heidelberg University for a semester. 我在海德堡大学学习了一个学期。|近义词: city, town|反义词: countryside, rural area|词性: noun


Etymology:
From German Heidelberg, from Middle High German Heidelberc, from Old High German Heidelberc, possibly from heidel (related to modern German Heide "heath") + berg "mountain".  Thus, "heath mountain".
