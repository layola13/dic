chrysotype: /ˈkrɪsəˌtaɪp/ | n. | 金印照片，金印法 | A photographic printing process that uses a solution of gold salts to create an image with a characteristic purple-brown tone. |  Example 1: The chrysotype, with its subtle warmth, was a popular choice for artistic photography in the late 19th century.  金印照片以其微妙的暖色调，是19世纪后期艺术摄影的流行选择。

Example 2:  He specialized in creating beautiful chrysotypes of landscapes and botanical subjects. 他擅长创作美丽的风景和植物主题的金印照片。 | 近义词: gold print | 反义词: (No direct antonym exists, could consider other printing processes like silver gelatin print) | 词性: noun
