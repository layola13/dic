brotherlinesses:/ˈbrʌðərlɪnɪsɪz/
n.
兄弟情谊，兄弟般的情谊
The quality or state of being brotherly; brotherly affection and unity.
Examples:
The brotherlinesses of the fraternity members were evident in their support for one another.  兄弟会成员之间的兄弟情谊体现在他们互相支持。

Their brotherlinesses extended beyond blood ties, encompassing a deep sense of camaraderie. 他们的兄弟情谊超越了血缘关系，包含了深厚的同志情谊。

Synonyms: fraternity, camaraderie, fellowship, brotherhood, amity
Antonyms: animosity, hostility, enmity, antagonism, discord
