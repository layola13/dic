anthophorous:/ænˈθɒfərəs/
adjective
产蜜的；多花的
Bearing or producing flowers; flowering.  Relating to plants that are attractive to bees and other pollinators because they produce nectar and pollen.
例句：The anthophorous plants in the garden attracted many bees. 花园里产蜜的植物吸引了许多蜜蜂。

例句：The botanist studied the anthophorous properties of various wildflowers.  植物学家研究了各种野花的产蜜特性。

近义词：flowering, nectariferous, melliferous
反义词：flowerless, sterile
词性：adjective
