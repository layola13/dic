nonaspirating:/ˌnɒnˈæspəreɪtɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈæspəreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非送气的
Not aspirated; pronounced without an audible puff of breath after the initial stop consonant.
The sounds /b/, /d/, and /ɡ/ are nonaspirating in English after /s/.  英语中/s/后的/b/、/d/和/ɡ/是非送气的。
The /p/ in "spin" is nonaspirating. “spin”中的/p/是非送气的。
近义词:unaspirated
反义词:aspirated
词性:adjective
