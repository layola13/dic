nonnihilism:/ˌnɒnˈnaɪɪlɪzəm/ | n. | 非虚无主义 | The belief that not everything is meaningless and without value; the rejection of nihilism.  It asserts that there are objective truths and values. |  Example 1: His philosophy embraced a form of nonnihilism, finding meaning in human connection.  他的哲学包含了一种非虚无主义的形式，在人与人之间的联系中找到了意义。

Example 2:  Her art is an expression of nonnihilism, celebrating the beauty and wonder of the natural world. 她的艺术是非虚无主义的一种表达，歌颂自然世界的美丽和奇妙。 | Near synonyms:  anti-nihilism,  optimism,  existentialism (depending on the context) | Antonym: nihilism | Noun
