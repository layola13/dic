overeruption:/ˌoʊvərɪˈrʌpʃən/
n.
过度喷发;过度萌出
An eruption that is excessive or beyond normal limits.  It specifically refers to a tooth erupting too far or a volcano erupting with unusual intensity.
The overeruption of the volcano caused widespread devastation.  火山的过度喷发造成了广泛的破坏。
The dentist noticed the overeruption of my lower incisors. 牙医注意到我的下门牙过度萌出。
近义词:  excessive eruption, hypereruption (for teeth)
反义词:  no direct antonym, possibly undereruption (for teeth)
