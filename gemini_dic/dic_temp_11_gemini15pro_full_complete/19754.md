billfish:/ˈbɪlfɪʃ/ | n. | 旗鱼；长嘴鱼 | A large, predatory, marine fish such as marlin, sailfish, or swordfish, characterized by a long, spear-like bill. |  The fisherman landed a huge billfish after a two-hour struggle.  这位渔夫经过两个小时的搏斗，钓到了一条巨大的旗鱼。

| Synonym: marlin, sailfish, swordfish, spearfish | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps small fish or bottom-dweller) | Noun
