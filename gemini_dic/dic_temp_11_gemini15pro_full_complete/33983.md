citable:/ˈsaɪtəbl/| adj.|可引用的，可被提及的|Suitable for being cited or quoted, especially as an authority or precedent.|Her research is based on citable sources. 她的研究基于可引用的资料。|Example: The judge requested citable legal precedents.  法官要求提供可引用的法律先例。|Synonyms: quotable, referable, mentionable|Antonyms: unquotable, unreliable|adjective
