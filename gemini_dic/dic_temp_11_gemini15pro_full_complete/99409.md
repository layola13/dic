interdistinguishing:/ˌɪntər dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪŋ/ | adj. |相互区分的，互相区别的 |  The act of distinguishing between things, highlighting their differences.  It implies a reciprocal process where each element's distinct characteristics are compared and contrasted with the others. |The artist's skill lies in interdistinguishing subtle shades of color.  这位艺术家的技巧在于区分细微的色调。 | The twins were so alike it was difficult interdistinguishing them. 这对双胞胎长得太像了，很难区分他们。| differentiating, discriminating | similar, alike | adjective
