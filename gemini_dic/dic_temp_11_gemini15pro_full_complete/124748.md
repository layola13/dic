mr:/ˈmɪstər/ (US), /ˈmɪstə(r)/ (UK)| n. |先生|A title of respect prefixed to a man's name or position.  Used as a form of address for men, regardless of marital status. |例句：Mr. Smith is our teacher. 史密斯先生是我们的老师。

例句：Please ask Mr. Jones to come in. 请让琼斯先生进来。|近义词：sir|反义词: Ms., Mrs., Miss|名词
