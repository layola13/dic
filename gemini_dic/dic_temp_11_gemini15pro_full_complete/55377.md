distemper:/dɪˈstɛmpər/| n. v.| 坏脾气，疾病，颜料 |A state of being bad-tempered and irritable.  An infectious viral disease, especially of canine animals.  A type of paint using glue or size as a medium.|
The child was in a distemper after missing his nap.  这个孩子因为没睡午觉而脾气暴躁。

The outbreak of distemper decimated the local fox population.  犬瘟热的爆发导致当地狐狸数量锐减。

The artist used distemper to create a mural on the wall.  艺术家用胶画颜料在墙上创作了一幅壁画。|近义词：illness, disease, ailment, bad mood, irritability |反义词: health, calmness, good humor|词性:名词, 动词

distemper:/dɪˈstɛmpər/| v.| 使不安，扰乱 |To disturb the composure of; upset. To mix (pigments) with size or glue to make distemper.|
The news distempered her calm.  这个消息扰乱了她的平静。

He distempered the pigments to create a vibrant paint. 他用胶水调和颜料，调制出一种鲜艳的涂料。|近义词：upset, disturb, agitate, ruffle |反义词: calm, soothe, pacify|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old French *destemprer*, from des- "away from" + temprer "to temper, mix, moderate".
