lordships:/ˈlɔːrdʃɪps/ | n. | 阁下（复数），领地（复数） | A respectful form of address used to refer to or address a lord or a group of lords, or to their authority or territory.  It can also refer to the territories or domains owned by a lord or lords. | Example 1: Their lordships will arrive shortly. 阁下们很快就会到达。

Example 2: The lordships were vast and prosperous. 这些领地广阔而繁荣。 | Synonym:  (for address) Your Grace, Your Honour; (for territory) domains, estates | Antonym: (for territory) common land | noun
