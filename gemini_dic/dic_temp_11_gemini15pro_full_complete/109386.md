lenticel:/ˈlɛntɪsɛl/| n.|皮孔；皮目|A small raised pore in the stem of a woody plant that allows gas exchange between the atmosphere and the internal tissues.|The lenticels on the cherry tree bark were clearly visible.|Synonyms: pore, stoma|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
