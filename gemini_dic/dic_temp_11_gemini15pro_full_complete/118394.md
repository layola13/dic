merocele: /ˈmɛrəˌsiːl/ /ˈmɛroʊˌsiːl/
noun
大腿疝；股疝
A protrusion of a portion of the abdominal viscera through the femoral ring.  A hernia in the upper thigh, just below the groin.
The patient presented with a painful merocele that required surgical repair.  病人出现了一个需要手术修复的疼痛性股疝。
近义词: femoral hernia
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific medical condition)
词性: n.
