hodograph:/ˈhɒdəɡrɑːf/ (US) /ˈhɑːdəɡræf/| n. |速端曲线；矢端图|A curve representing the variation of a vector quantity, typically velocity, by plotting the terminal points of vectors drawn from a fixed origin.|The hodograph of the wind speed showed a clear cyclonic pattern. 风速的速端曲线显示出明显的旋风模式。|Synonym: vector diagram|Antonym: N/A|noun

hodograph:/ˈhɒdəɡrɑːf/ (US) /ˈhɑːdəɡræf/| n. |矢端图|A diagram representing the successive velocities of a body.|The hodograph is useful in analyzing projectile motion. 矢端图在分析抛射体运动时很有用。|Synonym: velocity diagram|Antonym: N/A|noun
