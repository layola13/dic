nonengagement:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt/ |n.|不参与，不介入，脱离|Lack of engagement or involvement; the state of not being engaged or involved in something.|例句：The policy of nonengagement in foreign wars is popular.  不介入外国战争的政策很受欢迎。

例句：Her nonengagement in the project caused delays. 她对这个项目的不参与导致了延误。|近义词：inaction, aloofness, detachment, apathy|反义词：engagement, involvement, participation|词性: 名词
