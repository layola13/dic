isoperimetry:/ˌaɪsoʊpəˈrɪmətri/ | n. | 等周问题，等周定理 | The study of geometric figures with the same perimeter but different areas, or the same area but different perimeters, and the relationships between perimeter and area. |  The isoperimetric problem is to determine a plane figure of the largest possible area whose boundary has a specified length. 等周问题是确定边界长度一定的最大可能面积的平面图形。|  Isoperimetric inequalities relate the area of a set to its perimeter. 等周不等式将集合的面积与其周长联系起来。 | synonym: isoperimetrics | antonym: N/A | noun
