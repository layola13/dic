cauterizing:/ˈkɔːtəraɪzɪŋ/
v.
烧灼；烙
The act of burning tissue to stop bleeding or prevent infection.
The doctor is cauterizing the wound to stop the bleeding.  医生正在烧灼伤口以止血。
cauterize, sear, burn
heal, soothe
verb

cauterize:/ˈkɔːtəraɪz/
v.
烧灼；烙
To burn tissue with a hot iron, electric current, or caustic substance.
