incremation:/ˌɪnsɪˈmeɪʃən/ |n.|火葬，焚化|The act of burning a dead body to ashes. |His remains were dealt with by cremation. 他的遗体被火化了。|近义词：burning, incineration|反义词：burial, interment|词性:名词
Etymology: From Latin "incrematio," from "incremare" (to burn), from "in-" (in, on) + "cremare" (to burn).
