carceral:/ˈkɑːrsərəl/| adj.|监狱的，监禁的，与监禁有关的|Relating to, suggestive of, or characteristic of a prison or prisons.|The United States has a massive carceral system. 美国有一个庞大的监狱系统。|例句：The documentary explored the psychological impact of carceral environments. 这部纪录片探讨了监狱环境的心理影响。

例句：The report criticized the country's carceral state. 该报告批评了该国的监禁状态。|近义词：prison, penal, correctional|反义词：libertarian, free|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *carceralis*, from Latin carcer "prison".
