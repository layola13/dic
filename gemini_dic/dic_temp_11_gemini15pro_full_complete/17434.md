beanfest:/ˈbiːnfɛst/| n. |豆子盛宴，豆子节日|A celebration or festival centered around beans, often involving eating large quantities of beans or dishes made with beans. It can also refer to any situation where beans are plentiful or prominent. |例句：The annual chili beanfest is the town's biggest event.  年度辣椒豆子节是镇上最大的盛事。

例句：We had a beanfest at my house last night, trying out different bean recipes. 昨晚我们在我家举办了一场豆子盛宴，尝试了不同的豆子食谱。|近义词：bean feast, bean banquet|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "famine" if referring to the abundance aspect)|词性:noun
