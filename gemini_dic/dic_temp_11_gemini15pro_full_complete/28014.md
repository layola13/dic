capable-of:/ˈkeɪpəbəl ʌv/ | adj. | 有能力的，能够的 |  "Capable of" is an adjective phrase that describes someone or something as having the ability, skill, or potential to do something. It is followed by a noun or a gerund (verb ending in -ing). |  She is capable of great things. 她能成就大事。| synonyms: able, competent, qualified | antonyms: incapable, incompetent, unable | adjective phrase

注意事项:
"Capable of" 通常不用于被动语态。 虽然可以构成被动句式，但通常比较 awkward，建议使用其他表达方式。 例如，与其说 "The task is capable of being completed by him." 不如说 "He is capable of completing the task." 或 "The task can be completed by him."
