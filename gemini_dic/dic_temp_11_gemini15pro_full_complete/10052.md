apnea:/ˈæpniə/ (US) /ˈæpniə/ (UK)| n. |呼吸暂停|Temporary cessation of breathing, especially during sleep.|例句：Sleep apnea can be a serious health concern. 睡眠呼吸暂停可能是一个严重的健康问题。

例句：The diver experienced a brief apnea while underwater. 潜水员在水下经历了短暂的呼吸暂停。|近义词：asphyxia, respiratory arrest|反义词: eupnea|词性:名词
