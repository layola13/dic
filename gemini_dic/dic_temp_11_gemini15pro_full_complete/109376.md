lensman:/ˈlɛnzmən/| n.|摄影师；戴眼镜的人；摄影记者|A photographer, especially one who works for a newspaper or magazine; someone who wears glasses.|例句：The lensman captured a stunning sunset. 摄影师捕捉到了令人惊叹的日落。

例句：He's a renowned lensman in the fashion industry. 他是时尚界著名的摄影师。|近义词：photographer, cameraman, photojournalist|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "lens" + "man."  "Lens" comes from Latin "lens," meaning "lentil," due to the resemblance of a convex lens to the shape of a lentil.  "Man" comes from Old English "mann," meaning "human being, person."  The term gained popularity with the rise of photography in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
