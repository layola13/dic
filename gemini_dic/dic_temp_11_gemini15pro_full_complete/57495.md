dragees:/drɑˈʒeɪz/
noun
糖衣药丸；小糖果；糖衣杏仁
Small, colorful candies, often coated with sugar or chocolate.  These can also refer to pills coated in a similar way.
The wedding cake was decorated with silver dragees. 婚礼蛋糕上装饰着银色的糖衣杏仁。
synonyms:confections, sweets, bonbons, sugared almonds
antonyms:unsweetened, plain
