moderating:/ˈmɒdəreɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəreɪtɪŋ/ (US)| v. | 使缓和；主持；节制 | Making or becoming less extreme, intense, or violent; acting as a moderator in a discussion; exercising restraint or moderation. |例句：The chairman was moderating the debate. 主席主持了辩论。|近义词：tempering, mitigating, mediating, chairing |反义词：intensifying, aggravating, escalating|Verb

moderating:/ˈmɒdəreɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəreɪtɪŋ/ (US)| adj. | 缓和的；适度的 | Making or becoming less extreme, intense, or violent. |例句：The government is pursuing a moderating policy on interest rates. 政府正在奉行温和的利率政策。|近义词：temperate, mild, reasonable |反义词：extreme, intense, immoderate|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English moderaten, from Latin moderārī (“to restrain, regulate, govern”), from modus (“measure, manner”).
