hummable:/ˈhʌməbl/| adj.|可以哼唱的，易上口的|Easy to hum; catchy and memorable.|例句：The tune is very hummable. 这曲子很容易哼唱。|近义词：catchy, memorable, singable|反义词:unmemorable, forgettable|词性:形容词
词源:From hum + -able.  The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "fit to be," or "worthy of being."  Thus, "hummable" means "capable of being hummed."
