fretsome:/ˈfrɛtsəm/| adjective | 烦躁的，焦躁的 | Characterized by or expressing worry and agitation. | He had a fretsome night, tossing and turning, unable to sleep. 他度过了一个烦躁的夜晚，辗转反侧，无法入睡。|  She was fretsome about the upcoming exam. 她对即将到来的考试感到焦虑不安。 | 近义词: anxious, worried, agitated, restless | 反义词: calm, peaceful, tranquil | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *fretesum, from Old English *fretan (to fret) + -sum (forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fretaną (to gnaw, consume).
