gaggle:/ˈɡæɡl/| n.|一群（鹅）；咯咯叫声；嘈杂的人群|A flock of geese, especially when on the ground.  Also, an informal and often noisy or disorderly group of people.|例句：A gaggle of geese waddled across the road. 一群鹅摇摇摆摆地穿过马路。

例句：A gaggle of tourists crowded around the Mona Lisa. 一群游客围在蒙娜丽莎画像周围。|近义词：flock, group, crowd, throng|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑表示个体的词，例如 individual, single)|词性:noun
