eudipleura:/juːˈdɪplʊrə/ | n. | 尤迪普拉螺（一种海螺） | A genus of marine snails, specifically a thick-lipped rock snail.  It is characterized by its elaborate shell sculpture. |  Eudipleura Tampaensis is a common species found in Florida. 尤迪普拉螺坦帕亚种是一种常见于佛罗里达的物种。 |  Synonym:  Thick-lipped rocksnail (common name),  rock snail | Antonym:  N/A (It's a specific genus, not a concept with an opposite.) | noun
