bathometer:/bæˈθɒmɪtər/ | n. | 深水测深仪，测深计 | An instrument used for measuring the depth of water, especially in the sea. |  The oceanographer lowered the bathometer to measure the depth of the trench. 海洋学家放下测深计来测量海沟的深度。

The bathometer revealed the seabed was over 5,000 meters down. 测深计显示海底深度超过5000米。 | 近义词:  depth sounder, echo sounder, fathometer | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun
