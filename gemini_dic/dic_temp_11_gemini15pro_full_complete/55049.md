disquisitionary:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃənəri/ | adj. |  探究的；论述的；专题论文的 | Relating to or resembling a formal discourse or treatise, often lengthy and scholarly. |  His disquisitionary style made the lecture rather dry. 他的论述风格使讲座相当枯燥。|  Synonym:  argumentative, discursive, expository, analytical | Antonym:  concise, succinct, brief | Adjective

disquisition:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 专题论文；学术讨论；探究 | A formal and lengthy discourse or treatise on a particular subject, often scholarly in nature. | He delivered a lengthy disquisition on the history of philosophy. 他就哲学史发表了长篇大论。| Synonym: treatise, discourse, essay, dissertation, exposition | Antonym: summary, abstract, précis | Noun
