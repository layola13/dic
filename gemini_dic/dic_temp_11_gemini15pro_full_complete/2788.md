actually:/ˈæktʃuəli/ (US) /ˈæktʃʊəli/ (UK)| adv.| 事实上，实际上 |  Comes from Late Latin *actualis, from Latin actus "act, a doing, impulse, drive," past participle of agere "to set in motion, drive, lead, do, act."  It means in reality or in truth. |  I thought she was joking, but she was actually serious. 我以为她在开玩笑，但她实际上是认真的。 |  例句：He looks younger, but he's actually 50 years old. 他看起来更年轻，但他实际上已经50岁了。 | 近义词：really, truly, in fact, indeed | 反义词：apparently, seemingly | 词性: 副词
