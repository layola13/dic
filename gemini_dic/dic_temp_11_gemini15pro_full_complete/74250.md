foxhound:/ˈfɒksˌhaʊnd/ (UK) /ˈfɑːksˌhaʊnd/ (US)| n.|猎狐犬|A dog of a breed trained to hunt foxes, typically having a short dense coat, long ears, and strong legs.|The foxhound's keen sense of smell makes it a valuable asset in the hunt. 猎狐犬敏锐的嗅觉使它成为狩猎中的宝贵资产。|Synonyms: hound, hunting dog|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
