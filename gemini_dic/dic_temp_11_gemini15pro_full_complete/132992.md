nonenervating:/ˌnɒnɪˈnɜːveɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非使人衰弱的；不使人筋疲力尽的
Not causing weakness or fatigue.
The gentle yoga class was nonenervating and left me feeling refreshed. 这节轻松的瑜伽课并没有让我感到疲惫，反而让我精神焕发。
The cool breeze was nonenervating, a welcome change from the oppressive heat. 凉爽的微风令人精神振奋，与之前的闷热相比，真是令人欣喜的变化。
Synonyms: invigorating, refreshing, energizing, revitalizing
Antonyms: enervating, weakening, tiring, exhausting
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + enervating.  "Enervating" comes from the Latin "enervāre," meaning "to weaken," from "e-" (out) + "nervus" (sinew, nerve).  Therefore, "nonenervating" literally means "not weakening."
