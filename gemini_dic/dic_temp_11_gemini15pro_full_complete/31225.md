chansonnette:/ˌʃænsəˈnɛt/ | n. | 小歌；短歌；轻快的歌曲 | A short, light song, often of a sentimental or humorous nature. |  She sang a charming chansonnette about springtime in Paris. 她唱了一首关于巴黎春天的迷人小调。

The cabaret featured a series of witty chansonnettes.  这家卡巴莱歌舞表演的特色是一系列诙谐的小歌。| 近义词：ditty, song, tune | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps dirge or elegy for a contrasting mood) | 词性: noun
