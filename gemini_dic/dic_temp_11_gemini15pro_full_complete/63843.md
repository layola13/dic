epiglottiditis:/ˌɛpɪˌɡlɒˈtaɪtɪs/ /ˌɛpɪˌɡlɑːˈtaɪtɪs/
n.
会厌炎
Inflammation of the epiglottis, a flap of cartilage at the root of the tongue, which can cause difficulty in breathing.
The child was rushed to the hospital with epiglottiditis.  这孩子因会厌炎被紧急送往医院。
laryngitis, supraglottitis
