kickstarting:/ˌkɪkˈstɑːtɪŋ/
v.
启动，开始；促进，推动
The entrepreneur is kickstarting a new business venture.  这位企业家正在启动一项新的商业冒险。
They're kickstarting the economy with a series of new initiatives. 他们正在通过一系列新举措来刺激经济。
近义词: launching, initiating, beginning, boosting, jumpstarting
反义词: halting, stopping, ending

kickstart:/ˈkɪkstɑːt/
n.
启动，开始；促进，推动
The project needs a kickstart to get it going. 这个项目需要一个启动来使其进行下去。
The government provided a financial kickstart to the ailing industry. 政府为这个不景气的行业提供了财政支持。
近义词: boost, impetus, jumpstart
反义词: hindrance, obstacle
