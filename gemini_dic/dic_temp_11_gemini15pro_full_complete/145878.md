overdesirous: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈzaɪrəs/
adjective
过分渴望的；欲望过强的
Having or showing an excessive desire for something.
He was overdesirous of pleasing his boss. 他过分渴望取悦他的老板。

She is overdesirous of attention. 她过分渴望得到关注。

近义词: avaricious, covetous, greedy, rapacious
反义词: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic
词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "desirous" (adjective from the verb "desire" ultimately from Latin "desiderare" "to long for, wish for")
