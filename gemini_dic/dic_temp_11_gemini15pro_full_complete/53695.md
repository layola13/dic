disclaim:/dɪˈskleɪm/ | vt. |否认；放弃；拒绝承认|To deny any responsibility for or connection with.|例句：The company disclaimed all responsibility for the faulty product.  公司否认对缺陷产品负有任何责任。

例句：He disclaimed any knowledge of the conspiracy. 他否认对阴谋有任何了解。

|近义词：deny, renounce, repudiate|反义词：claim, admit, acknowledge|词性：动词
