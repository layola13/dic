breacher:/ˈbriːtʃər/| n.|破坏者，违反者，攻城锤|Someone who breaches something, such as a law, agreement, or fortification. |The data breach was perpetrated by a skilled breacher.  数据泄露是由一名熟练的破坏者实施的。

The breachers used explosives to enter the building.  攻城队用炸药进入了大楼。|近义词：violator, offender, trespasser, attacker|反义词:defender, protector|noun
