dhing:/ðiːŋ/
noun
东西; 事物
A thing is an object or an item.

例句：
I need to buy a few things at the store.
我需要在商店买些东西。

That's a strange-looking thing.
那是个看起来很奇怪的东西。

近义词：object, item, article, stuff
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a general term like "thing")
词性: noun
