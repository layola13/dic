bhisti:/ˈbiːsti/ | n. | 水夫；送水人 | A person in India who carries water, especially one employed to supply water to houses, gardens, etc. |  The bhisti filled the large earthenware pots with water from the well.  送水人从井里把水灌满了大陶罐。

The bhisti sprinkled water on the dusty road to cool it down.  送水人往尘土飞扬的路上洒水降温。 |Synonyms: water carrier, waterman | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "customer," "recipient") | noun
