deterrently:/dɪˈtɜːrəntlɪ/
adv.
威慑地，制止地
in a manner that discourages or prevents a particular action
The presence of police officers acted deterrently on potential troublemakers. 警察的出现对潜在的捣乱分子起到了威慑作用。
近义词: dissuasively, preventively
反义词: encouragingly, invitingly
词性: 副词

deter:/dɪˈtɜːr/
v.
威慑，阻止
to discourage (someone) from doing something, typically by instilling doubt or fear of the consequences.
The high security fence is designed to deter intruders. 高安全围栏旨在阻止入侵者。
近义词: discourage, dissuade, prevent
反义词: encourage, incite
词性: 动词

deterrent:/dɪˈtɜːrənt/
n.
威慑物，遏制物
a thing that discourages or is intended to discourage someone from doing something.
The death penalty is often seen as a deterrent to crime. 死刑通常被视为对犯罪的威慑。
近义词: discouragement, impediment, obstacle
反义词: encouragement, incentive
词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *dēterrere, from dē- + terrēre "to frighten".
