nonconcentric:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsɛntrɪk/
adjective
非同心的，不同心的
Not having a common center; not concentric.
The two circles are nonconcentric. 这两个圆非同心。
non-centred, eccentric, off-center
concentric, centred, coaxial
