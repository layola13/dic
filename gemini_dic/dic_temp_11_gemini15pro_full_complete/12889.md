assume:/əˈsjuːm/ (US), /əˈsjuːm/ (UK)| v. | 假定，假设；承担，担任；呈现，采取 | To suppose to be the case, without proof; to take or begin to have (power or responsibility); to begin to have a particular quality or appearance. |  I assumed he was coming, so I was surprised when he didn't show up. 我以为他要来，所以当他没有出现时我很惊讶。| synonyms: presume, suppose, posit, undertake, adopt | antonyms: disbelieve, doubt, relinquish | verb

assume:/əˈsjuːm/ (US), /əˈsjuːm/ (UK)| n. | 假定，假设 (rarely used as a noun) | An act of assuming; something that is assumed. | The assumption that he was guilty proved to be false.  他被认为有罪的假设被证明是错误的。（名词用法较罕见） | synonyms: presumption, supposition | antonyms: fact, reality | noun
