chronostratigraphic:/ˌkrɒnoʊˌstrætɪˈɡræfɪk/ /ˌkrɑːnoʊˌstrætɪˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
年代地层学的
Relating to chronostratigraphy, the branch of geology concerned with establishing the absolute ages of rock strata.
The chronostratigraphic chart shows the timeline of Earth's history. 年代地层图显示了地球的历史时间线。
The geologist presented new chronostratigraphic data for the region. 地质学家展示了该地区新的年代地层数据。
近义词: time-stratigraphic
反义词: None
词性: adjective

Etymology:
chrono- (from Greek khronos, "time") + stratigraphic (from stratum + -graphic)
