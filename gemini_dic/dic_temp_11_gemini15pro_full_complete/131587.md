noncompetency:/nɒnˈkɒmpɪtənsi/ /nɑːnˈkɑːmpɪtənsi/| n. |不胜任，不称职|Lack of ability or skill to do something successfully; incompetence.|例句：His noncompetency in managing finances led to the company's downfall. 他在财务管理上的不称职导致了公司的垮台。

例句：The board of directors expressed concerns about the CEO's noncompetency. 董事会对首席执行官的不称职表示担忧。|近义词：incompetence, inability, inadequacy, inefficiency|反义词：competence, ability, skill, proficiency|词性:名词
