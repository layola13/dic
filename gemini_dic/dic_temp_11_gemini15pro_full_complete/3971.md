agrotourist:/ˈæɡroʊˌtʊrɪst/| n.|农业旅游者；乡村旅游者|A person who engages in agrotourism, visiting working farms or other agricultural businesses for the purpose of enjoyment, education, or active involvement in the activities of the farm or business.|例句：The agrotourists enjoyed picking apples in the orchard. 农业旅游者喜欢在果园里摘苹果。

例句：Many agrotourists are interested in learning about sustainable farming practices. 许多农业旅游者对学习可持续农业实践感兴趣。|近义词：rural tourist, farm tourist, ecotourist|反义词:city tourist|词性:noun
