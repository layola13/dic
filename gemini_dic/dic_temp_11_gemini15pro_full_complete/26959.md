calciferous:/kælˈsɪfərəs/
adj.
含钙的；产钙的
Containing calcium carbonate; producing lime.
The calciferous glands of earthworms secrete calcium carbonate to neutralize humic acids. 蚯蚓的钙腺分泌碳酸钙来中和腐殖酸。
Synonyms: calcareous
Antonyms: non-calcareous
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *calcifer, from calx (lime) + -fer (bearing).
