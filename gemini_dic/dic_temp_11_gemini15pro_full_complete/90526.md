huerta:/ˈwer.tɑː/ (US), /ˈwɛr.tə/ (UK)| n.|菜园，果园，种植园|A cultivated piece of land, usually smaller than a farm, used for growing fruits, vegetables, or other crops.|My grandfather has a beautiful huerta where he grows tomatoes and peppers. 我的祖父有一个美丽的菜园，在那里他种植西红柿和辣椒。|The huerta provided fresh produce for the entire village. 这个菜园为整个村庄提供新鲜的农产品。|Synonyms: garden, orchard, plantation|Antonyms: wasteland, desert|noun
