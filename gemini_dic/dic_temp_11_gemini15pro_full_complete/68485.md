familyhoods:/ˈfæmɪliˌhʊdz/
n.
家庭关系；家庭状态
The state or quality of being a family; the collective body of families.  A relatively new term, gaining traction in sociological and anthropological discussions.
The researcher's study focused on the changing dynamics of familyhoods in modern society.  研究人员的研究重点是现代社会家庭关系的变化动态。
kinship, familial ties, family connections
(No clear antonym exists as it's a state of being, but concepts like estrangement or isolation could represent opposing ideas.)
