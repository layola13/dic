endive:/ˈɛndaɪv/ | n. | 菊苣，苦苣 | A leafy vegetable with a slightly bitter flavor, often used in salads. |  例句：I added some endive to the salad for a bit of bitterness. 我在沙拉里加了一些菊苣，增添一些苦味。
