deuterocanonical:/ˌdjuːtərəʊkəˈnɒnɪkəl/
adjective
次经的；次正典的
(of sacred writings) forming a second canon or a supplementary one.  Relating to or consisting of books and passages of the Bible not regarded as canonical by all churches, especially Protestants.
The Catholic Bible includes the deuterocanonical books. 天主教圣经包含次经。
Near Synonyms: apocryphal
Antonyms: canonical
词性: adjective
