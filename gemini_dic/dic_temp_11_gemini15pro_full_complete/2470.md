adjustment:/əˈdʒʌstmənt/ | n. | 调整，调节；校正；适应 |  A change or modification made to something in order to improve it or make it more suitable; the process of adapting or becoming used to something new. |  The mechanic made a few adjustments to the engine.  机械师对发动机做了一些调整。| synonyms: modification, alteration, adaptation, correction, change | antonyms: disarrangement, disorder, imbalance | noun

adjustment:/əˈdʒʌstmənt/ | n. | 调节；调整 |  The act of adjusting or the state of being adjusted. | She made a slight adjustment to the height of the chair. 她稍微调整了一下椅子的高度。 | synonyms: regulation, setting, tuning | antonyms: deregulation | noun
