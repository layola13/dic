nonvascular:/nɒnˈvæskjʊlə(r)/
adjective
无维管束的；非维管的
Lacking a vascular system; not containing vessels that conduct fluids.
Mosses are nonvascular plants.  苔藓是无维管植物。
Nonvascular plants rely on diffusion for water transport. 无维管植物依靠扩散来输送水分。
Synonyms: avascular
Antonyms: vascular
