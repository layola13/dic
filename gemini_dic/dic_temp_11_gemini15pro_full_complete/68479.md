familistery:/fəˈmɪlɪstəri/ |n.|家庭式住宅；和谐的大家庭|A large building or complex designed to house a cooperative community, especially one based on socialist principles.  It often includes shared living spaces, workshops, and recreational facilities. |The familistery provided housing, meals, and childcare for all its residents.|Synonyms: commune, cooperative, collective|Antonyms: individual household, single-family home|Noun

familistery:/fəˈmɪlɪstəri/|n.|和谐的大家庭|A group of people living together in a close-knit, cooperative community, resembling a large family. |The residents of the familistery formed a strong and supportive community.|Synonyms: community, brotherhood, sisterhood|Antonyms:  isolation, estrangement|Noun
