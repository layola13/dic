crescendi:/krəˈʃendi/ /krəˈʃɛndi/ | n. | 渐强，音量逐渐增大 |  The plural of crescendo. It refers to gradual increases in volume of a passage of music.  | The composer indicated several crescendi in the symphony's first movement.  作曲家在交响曲的第一乐章中标注了几个渐强。| synonyms:  augmentations,  swellings, increases | antonyms: diminuendos, decrescendos | noun
