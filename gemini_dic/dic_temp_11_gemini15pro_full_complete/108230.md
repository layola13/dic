laughably:/ˈlɑːfəbli/ /ˈlæfəbli/
adverb|可笑地，荒唐地|in a way that is so silly or ridiculous that it makes you laugh|His claims were laughably untrue. 他的说法可笑地不真实。|ludicrously, ridiculously, absurdly|seriously|词性: 副词

laughably:/ˈlɑːfəbli/ /ˈlæfəbli/
adjective|可笑的，荒唐的|(informal) used to describe something so silly or ridiculous that it makes you laugh. (It is most commonly used with negative connotations.) |The security measures were laughably inadequate.  安全措施简直可笑到令人难以置信。|ludicrous, ridiculous, absurd|serious|词性: 形容词
