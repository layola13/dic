lockjawed: /ˈlɒkˌdʒɔːd/ (British) /ˈlɑːkˌdʒɔːd/ (American)
| adj. | 牙关紧闭的；沉默寡言的 | Unable to speak because of tension or fear; having the jaws locked together, as in tetanus. |  He stood there, lockjawed and trembling, unable to utter a word. 他站在那里，牙关紧闭，浑身颤抖，一句话也说不出来。| synonyms: speechless, silent, mute, tight-lipped | antonyms: talkative, loquacious, garrulous | adjective
