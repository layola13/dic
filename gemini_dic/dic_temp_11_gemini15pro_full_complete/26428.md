byo:/ˈbaɪoʊ/
abbreviation|自带酒水 (Bring Your Own)|BYO is an abbreviation for "Bring Your Own," typically referring to alcoholic beverages.  It signifies that an establishment, such as a restaurant, allows patrons to bring their own drinks, often for a small corkage fee.|Many restaurants in this area are BYO.  这个地区的很多餐馆都允许自带酒水。|近义词:none|反义词:none|n.
