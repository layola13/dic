boatless: /ˈboʊtləs/ | adj. | 无船的，没有船的 | Without a boat; lacking boats. |  They were boatless and stranded on the island. 他们没有船，被困在了岛上。| The boatless fishermen had to find another way to make a living.  没有船的渔民不得不另谋生路。 | 近义词:  without a boat, lacking boats | 反义词:  having a boat, with a boat | 词性: 形容词

词源:  boat + -less. "boat" 源自古英语 "bāt," 指的是“船”。 "-less" 是一个古英语后缀，表示“没有”或“缺乏”。
