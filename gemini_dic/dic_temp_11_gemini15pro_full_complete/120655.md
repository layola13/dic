mis-started:/ˌmɪsˈstɑːrtɪd/
verb
启动失败，开始不顺利
failed to start correctly or successfully
The engine mis-started a few times before finally turning over.  发动机启动失败了几次，最后才发动起来。
The project mis-started due to a lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目一开始就不顺利。
Synonyms: faltered, stumbled, floundered
Antonyms: succeeded, thrived, flourished
词性: 动词
