continuation:/kənˌtɪnjuˈeɪʃn/ | n. | 续篇，续集 |The movie is a continuation of the TV series. 这部电影是电视剧的续集。| Example:  This book is a continuation of his previous work. 这本书是他之前作品的续篇。| Synonyms: sequel, follow-up | Antonyms:  prequel, beginning | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *continuationem,  from Latin continuatio, from continuatus, past participle of continuare "to join together," from continuus "continuous,"  from com- "together" + tenere "to hold."
