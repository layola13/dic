icosandrian:/ˌaɪkoʊˈsændriən/ | adj. | 二十雄蕊的；具二十雄蕊的 | Having 20 stamens, or approximately 20.  Derived from Greek *eikosi* "twenty" and *andros* "man," referring to the stamens. |  The icosandrian flower is characteristic of the rose family.  这种二十雄蕊的花是蔷薇科的特征。 |  polyandrous | - | adjective
