bloodlessly:/ˈblʌdləsli/
adverb
无血地；不流血地；不动用武力地
Without bloodshed; without violence or killing.
The coup was achieved bloodlessly. 这场政变兵不血刃地取得了成功。

bloodlessly:/ˈblʌdləsli/
adverb
冷酷无情地；残酷地
In a cold and ruthless manner.
He bloodlessly dismissed her from her job. 他冷酷无情地解雇了她。


近义词:peacefully, nonviolently, amicably
反义词:violently, bloodily


词性:adverb

词源: blood (n.) + -less (suffix) + -ly (suffix)
