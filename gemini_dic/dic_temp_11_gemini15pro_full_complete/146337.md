overfervent:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrvənt/| adj.| 过于热情的；狂热的| Excessively fervent or enthusiastic; too passionate.  Characterized by excessive zeal or enthusiasm.|  She delivered an overfervent speech that alienated some of the audience.  她发表了一篇过于热情的演讲，疏远了一些听众。|Synonyms:  passionate, fervid, ardent, fiery|Antonyms:  dispassionate, cool, lukewarm, halfhearted|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + fervent.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much".  "Fervent" comes from the Latin "fervere" meaning "to boil, glow, be hot".  Thus, "overfervent" literally means "overly boiling" or "excessively passionate".
