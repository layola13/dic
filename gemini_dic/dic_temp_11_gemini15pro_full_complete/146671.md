overhauling:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːlɪŋ/| n. |彻底检修，大修|A complete examination and repair of a machine, system, etc.|The car is due for an overhauling. 这辆车该大修了。|近义词：repairing, renovating, servicing, restoring|反义词:damaging, neglecting|noun

overhaul:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːl/| vt. |彻底检修，大修|To examine and repair a machine, system, etc. thoroughly|The mechanics overhauled the engine. 机修工彻底检修了发动机。|近义词：repair, renovate, service, restore|反义词:damage, neglect|verb
