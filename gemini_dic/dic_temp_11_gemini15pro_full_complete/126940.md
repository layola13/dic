narrated:/nəˈreɪtɪd/ /ˈnærˌeɪtɪd/
verb
讲述的，叙述的
told a story, gave an account of events
The documentary was narrated by David Attenborough.  这部纪录片由大卫·爱登堡解说。
The story is narrated from a child's perspective.  这个故事是从一个孩子的视角叙述的。
synonyms: recounted, related, told, described
antonyms: listened, silent, quiet
词性: v.
