lengthy:/ˈlɛŋθi/ | adj. |冗长的，漫长的|Extending for a considerable time or distance; prolonged.  Characterized by great length or duration, often to the point of being tedious or excessive. |The meeting was lengthy and unproductive. 会议冗长且没有成果。

The lengthy novel took me several weeks to finish.  这本冗长的小说花了我好几个星期才读完。|近义词：prolonged, protracted, extended, long, tedious|反义词：brief, concise, short, succinct|词性:形容词
词源: From length + -y.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *langaz ("long").
