elevenses:/ɪˈlevənzɪz/ | n. |上午茶; 早午餐; |A short break for a drink and light snack, typically taken at around eleven o'clock in the morning.|例句：We always have our elevenses at 11:00 a.m. 我们总是在上午11点享用上午茶。

例句：She prefers coffee and biscuits for her elevenses. 她喜欢咖啡和饼干作为上午茶。|近义词：morning tea, brunch, coffee break|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps dinner or supper as the opposite meal time) |词性:noun
