bookmarked: /ˈbʊkmɑːkt/ | adj. | 已加书签的，已标记的 |  Marked with a bookmark, indicating a place to return to in a book, document, or website.  Also used figuratively to describe something set aside for later attention. | I bookmarked several websites for my research project. 我为我的研究项目添加了几个网站的书签。 | Synonym: saved, flagged, marked | Antonym: unmarked, unflagged | adjective

bookmarked: /ˈbʊkmɑːkt/ | v. |  给...加书签，标记 | Past participle of "bookmark," the act of marking a place in a book or other document for later reference. Also used to indicate saving a website or other digital content for future access. | I've bookmarked this page so I can come back to it later. 我已经给这个页面加了书签，以便以后可以回来看。 | Synonym: saved, tagged | Antonym: unmarked, deleted | verb
