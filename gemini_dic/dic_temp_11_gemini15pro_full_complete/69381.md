feebler:/ˈfiːblər/| adj.|更虚弱的，更无力的|Comparative form of feeble: weaker in physical strength or influence.|Her voice was feebler than before. 她的声音比以前更虚弱了。|The government's position on the issue has grown feebler. 政府在这个问题上的立场越来越弱了。|近义词：weaker, frailer, less powerful|反义词：stronger, more powerful|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *febleer, comparative of *feble, from Old French feble, from Late Latin flebilis (“lamentable”), from Latin flēbilis, from flēō (“I weep, cry”).
