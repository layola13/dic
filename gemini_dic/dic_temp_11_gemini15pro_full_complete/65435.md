eupittone:/juː'pɪtoʊn/ | n. |优品酮; 尤比特酮| A ketone body, specifically acetoacetic acid. It is one of the three ketone bodies produced by the liver during periods of prolonged fasting or very low carbohydrate intake. | The levels of eupittone in the blood increase during ketosis.  血液中的优品酮水平在酮症期间会升高。| Synonyms: acetoacetic acid, diacetic acid | Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for a specific chemical compound) | noun
