over-plowed: /ˌōvərˈplaʊd/ | adj. | 过度耕作的；过度犁的 |  Excessively plowed, usually to the point of damaging the soil.  The land becomes depleted of nutrients and prone to erosion. | The over-plowed fields were barren and dusty.  过度耕作的田地荒芜而尘土飞扬。| over-cultivated, over-tilled | under-cultivated | adjective

over-plowed: /ˌōvərˈplaʊd/ | adj. | 比喻义：过度使用的；疲惫不堪的 | Figuratively used to describe something that has been excessively used or worked, resulting in exhaustion or depletion.  Similar to being "overworked" or "burnt out." |  The team felt over-plowed after the intense project.  团队在紧张的项目结束后感到筋疲力尽。| exhausted, depleted, overworked | rested, rejuvenated | adjective
