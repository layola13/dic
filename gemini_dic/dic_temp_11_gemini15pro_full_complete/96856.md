inexcusableness:/ˌɪnɪkˈskjuːzəbəlnəs/
n.
不可原谅; 不可辩解
The inexcusableness of his behavior shocked everyone.
他的行为不可原谅，震惊了所有人。
The sheer inexcusableness of her lateness angered her boss.
她迟到的不可原谅性激怒了她的老板。
inexcusable (adjective) + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns of quality)
近义词: unpardonableness, unforgivableness, unjustifiableness
反义词: excusability, forgivability, justifiability
词性: 名词
