mumpish:/ˈmʌmpɪʃ/| adj.|闷闷不乐的；愠怒的|Sullen and ill-tempered; sulky. |例句：He was mumpish and refused to talk. 他闷闷不乐，拒绝说话。

例句：She gave me a mumpish look when I asked her to help. 当我让她帮忙时，她给了我一个愠怒的眼神。|近义词：sulky, sullen, grumpy, morose, petulant|反义词：cheerful, happy, jovial, good-humored|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "mump," meaning "to sulk or be silent," of uncertain origin, possibly related to "momble" (to mumble) or from a Scandinavian source.
