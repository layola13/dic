mulligan:/ˈmʌlɪɡən/| n.|重打（高尔夫球或其他游戏的第一杆失误后允许的重打），重来，第二次机会|A mulligan is a second chance to perform an action, usually after the first attempt was unsuccessful, especially in golf.|He took a mulligan on the first hole after slicing his drive into the woods. 他在第一洞开球将球打进树林后，选择重打。|近义词：redo, retry, second chance|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
