outglooming:/ˌaʊtˈɡluːmɪŋ/
verb
使黯然失色，胜过…的忧郁
overshadowing gloomily; exceeding in gloom.
The dark clouds outglooming the setting sun.  乌云使落日黯然失色。
The poet's later works outgloomed his earlier, happier poems. 这位诗人后期的作品比他早期快乐的诗歌更显忧郁。
近义词:overshadowing, eclipsing, darkening
反义词:brightening, illuminating
词性:v.
