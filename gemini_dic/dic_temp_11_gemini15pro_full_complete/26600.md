cache:/kæʃ/ /kæʃ/| n. v.|缓存，隐藏处|A hidden store of things, or the place where they are kept.  A special part of a computer's memory that stores copies of frequently used information so that the computer can access it more quickly.|例句：The squirrels had a cache of nuts buried in the garden. 松鼠在花园里埋藏了一堆坚果。|近义词：hoard, store, stockpile|反义词: |n. v.

cache:/kæʃ/ /kæʃ/| v.|缓存|To store something in a hidden or secure place.|例句：The pirates cached their treasure on a deserted island. 海盗把他们的宝藏藏在一个荒岛上。|近义词：hide, conceal, stash|反义词: |v.
