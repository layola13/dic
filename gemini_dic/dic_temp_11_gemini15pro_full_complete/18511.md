benedict:/ˈbenɪdɪkt/| n.|本尼迪克特; 已婚男子; 新婚男子;  Benedictine liqueur|A Benedict is a newly married man, especially one who has long been a bachelor.  Benedict is also a male given name, and a surname. It is also the name of a liqueur. |例句：He was a confirmed bachelor until he met Susan, now he's a happy Benedict.  他一直是个坚定的单身汉，直到遇见了苏珊，现在他是个幸福的已婚男人。

例句：My uncle Benedict always makes the best cocktails. 我的本尼迪克特叔叔总是调制最好的鸡尾酒。|近义词：newlywed, bridegroom, husband|反义词:bachelor|词性:noun


benedict:/ˈbenɪdɪkt/| adj.|本笃会的|Relating to or characteristic of Saint Benedict or his monastic rule.|例句：Benedictine monks follow a strict routine of prayer and work. 本笃会修士遵循严格的祈祷和工作惯例。

例句：The Benedictine abbey is known for its beautiful architecture.  本笃会修道院以其美丽的建筑而闻名。|近义词：monastic|反义词:|词性:adjective
