miniaturization:/ˌmɪnɪətʃəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|小型化，微型化|The process of making something smaller.  The miniaturization of electronic components has revolutionized the computer industry.| Miniaturization has made portable devices possible.  小型化使便携式设备成为可能。|downsizing, reduction, shrinking|magnification, enlargement|noun
