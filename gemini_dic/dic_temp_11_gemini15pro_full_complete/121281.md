misfeature:/ˌmɪsˈfiːtʃər/ | n. | 缺陷，缺点，不当的功能 | A fault or defect in design or implementation. | This phone has a misfeature where the screen turns off randomly.  这款手机有一个缺陷，屏幕会随机关闭。|
The software is full of misfeatures that make it difficult to use. 这个软件充满了缺陷，使其难以使用。|Synonym: defect, flaw, bug, glitch, imperfection | Antonym: feature, advantage, strength | noun
