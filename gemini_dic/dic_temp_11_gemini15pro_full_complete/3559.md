agamogenesis:/ˌeɪɡæmoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ /ˌæɡæmoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
无性生殖，无配子生殖
Reproduction without the union of gametes.
Agamogenesis is a common method of reproduction in some invertebrates, such as sponges and starfish. 海绵和海星等一些无脊椎动物通常采用无性生殖的方式繁殖。
Certain plants also reproduce through agamogenesis, like the propagation of strawberries through runners.  某些植物也通过无性生殖进行繁殖，例如草莓通过匍匐茎进行繁殖。

近义词: asexual reproduction, apomixis
反义词: sexual reproduction
词性: noun
