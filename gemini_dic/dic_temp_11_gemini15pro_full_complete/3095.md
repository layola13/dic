例句：Amelia Earhart was a famous aeroplanist. 艾米莉亚·厄尔哈特是一位著名的飞行员。

|近义词：pilot, aviator, flier |反义词: passenger|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (relating to air) + planist (one who operates a plane).  Ultimately from Greek ἀήρ (aēr, “air”) + Latin planus (“flat, level”).
