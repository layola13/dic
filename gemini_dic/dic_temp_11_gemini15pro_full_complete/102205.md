italicize:/ɪˈtælɪsaɪz/ | vt. | 斜体化，用斜体印刷 | To print or write in italics; to emphasize. |  She italicized the foreign words in her essay. 她用斜体字标出了她文章中的外来词。

| Example: The title of the book was italicized on the cover. 书名在封面上是用斜体字印刷的。

| Synonyms: slant, oblique | Antonyms: regular, roman | Verb
