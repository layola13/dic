indevout:/ˌɪndɪˈvaʊt/
adjective
不虔诚的，不敬神的
Lacking reverence or piety; not devout.
He gave an indevout yawn during the sermon.  他在布道时不敬地打了个哈欠。
Synonyms: irreverent, impious, ungodly, profane
Antonyms: devout, pious, religious, reverent
词性: 形容词
