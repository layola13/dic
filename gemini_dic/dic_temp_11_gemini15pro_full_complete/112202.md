loop-stitching:/ˈluːp stɪtʃɪŋ/
n.
环形缝合，锁边缝合
A method of sewing that creates a series of loops, often used for decorative edges or to prevent fabric from fraying.
The seamstress used loop-stitching to finish the edge of the scarf.  女裁缝用环形缝合法缝好了围巾的边缘。

The blanket was finished with a decorative loop-stitching.  这条毯子用装饰性的环形缝法收尾。
近义词: blanket stitch, buttonhole stitch
反义词: (No direct antonym, but potentially: straight stitch, running stitch - as these are contrasting stitch types)
词性: noun
