overopinionatednesses:/ˌoʊvərəˈpɪnjəneɪtɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
过度自以为是；固执己见
The state of being excessively opinionated, often to the point of being dogmatic or close-minded.
His overopinionatednesses made it difficult to have a productive conversation. 他过度自以为是，使得很难进行富有成效的对话。
近义词：dogmatism, rigidity, inflexibility
反义词：open-mindedness, flexibility, receptiveness
词性: 名词

词源:
From "overopinionated" + "-ness".  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "opinionated" (adjective meaning "having very strong opinions"). "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).
