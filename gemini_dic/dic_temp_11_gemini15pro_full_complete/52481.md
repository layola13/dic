diethylamine:/ˌdaɪ.ɪˈθæl.ə.miːn/ /ˌdaɪ.ɛˈθæl.ə.ˌmin/| n.|二乙胺|A secondary amine with the chemical formula (C2H5)2NH. It is a colorless liquid with a strong, ammonia-like odor.  It is miscible with water and most organic solvents.|Diethylamine is commonly used as a corrosion inhibitor and in the production of certain pesticides.|近义词: N,N-Diethylamine|反义词: None|词性:noun

词源:  From *di-* + *ethyl* + *-amine*.  *Di-* indicates two ethyl groups attached to the nitrogen atom in the amine functional group.
