hydrogode:/ˈhaɪdrəɡoʊd/| n.|水翼船，水翼艇|A hydrofoil boat; a boat that is lifted clear of the water's surface by hydrofoils when travelling at speed.|例句：The hydrogode skimmed across the water. 水翼艇掠过水面。|例句：We took a hydrogode to the island. 我们乘坐水翼艇去了岛上。|近义词：hydrofoil, hydrofoil boat|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a conventional displacement hull boat)|词性:noun

词源:  From hydro- + -gode (probably from Greek hodós “way, road”).  "Hydro-" refers to water, and "-gode" likely relates to motion or travel.  The term "hydrofoil" is more commonly used, and "hydrogode" is a less frequent variant.
