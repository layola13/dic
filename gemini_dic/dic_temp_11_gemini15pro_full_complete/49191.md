delawares:/ˈdɛləwɛərz/
n.
特拉华州人；特拉华州的居民
People who live in or are from the state of Delaware.
The Delawares are known for their beautiful beaches. 特拉华州人以他们美丽的沙滩而闻名。
近义词:Delawareans
反义词:None
词性:名词(plural)

delawares:/ˈdɛləwɛərz/
adj.
特拉华州的
Relating to the state of Delaware.
We visited the Delaware beaches last summer. 我们去年夏天去了特拉华州的海滩。
近义词:Delawarean
反义词:None
词性:形容词(plural, attributive)


词源:
From "Delaware," originally the name of a Native American tribe, the Lenape.  The state of Delaware was named after Lord De La Warr, the first governor of the Virginia colony.
