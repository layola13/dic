ingeniousnesses:/ˌɪnˈdʒiːniəsnɪsɪz/
|n.|独创性；精巧；心灵手巧 (plural of ingeniousness)
|The quality of being ingenious; cleverness, inventiveness, resourcefulness.|
|The ingeniousnesses of the inventor were evident in his many creations.  这位发明家的独创性在他的众多发明中显而易见。|
|His ingeniousnesses in solving complex problems amazed everyone. 他解决复杂问题的巧妙方法使每个人都感到惊讶。|
|ingenuity, cleverness, inventiveness, resourcefulness, brilliance|
