mightless:/ˈmaɪtləs/| adj.|无力的，没有力量的|Lacking might or power; weak, powerless.|The mightless army was quickly defeated. 无力的军队很快就被打败了。|近义词：powerless, weak, impotent, feeble|反义词：powerful, strong, mighty|词性：形容词
词源：From Middle English *mihtles, from Old English *meahtlēas (“mightless, powerless, impossible, helpless”), equivalent to might +‎ -less.
