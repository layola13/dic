greeniest:/ˈɡriːnɪɪst/ | adj. | 最绿的；最环保的 |  Superlative form of "green," meaning having the most vibrant green color or being the most environmentally friendly. |  This is the greeniest valley I've ever seen. 这是我见过的最绿的山谷。 The company is striving to become the greeniest in the industry. 这家公司正努力成为业内最环保的公司。 | 近义词：most environmentally friendly, most eco-conscious | 反义词：brownest, least green, least environmentally friendly | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English grene, from Old English grēne, of Germanic origin. Related to Dutch groen and German grün. The suffix "-est" is the superlative ending.
