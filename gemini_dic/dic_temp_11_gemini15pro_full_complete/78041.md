genuflectory: /ˌdʒɛnjuˈflɛktəri/ | n. | 跪拜; 祈祷处 | A place for kneeling, especially one for prayer in a church. |  He paused at the genuflectory before entering the pew. 他在进入座位前在跪拜处停顿了一下。| The bride genuflected at the genuflectory before approaching the altar. 新娘走向祭坛前在祈祷处跪拜。| Synonym: prie-dieu, kneeler | Antonym: None | Noun
