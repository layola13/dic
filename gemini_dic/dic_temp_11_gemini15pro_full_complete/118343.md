meridionality:/ˌmɛrɪdiəˈnælɪti/ | n. | 经向性，南北方向性 | The state or quality of extending in a north-south direction; the condition of being determined by latitude or distance from the equator.  It often refers to the variations in climate, vegetation, and other geographical features along a north-south axis. | The meridionality of the mountain range influences the local climate.  这片山脉的经向性影响着当地的气候。 |  latitude, longitudinality (antonym) | longitudinality (antonym) | noun
