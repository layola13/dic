hibernation:/ˌhaɪbərˈneɪʃən/
n.
冬眠
A state of inactivity and metabolic depression in endotherms (warm-blooded animals) characterized by reduced body temperature, breathing rate, and heart rate.
Many animals, like bears and groundhogs, experience hibernation during the winter. 许多动物，如熊和土拨鼠，在冬天会冬眠。
hibernation is a survival strategy for animals in harsh winter conditions. 冬眠是动物在严酷的冬季条件下的一种生存策略。
dormancy, sleep, torpor
activity, wakefulness
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "hibernātiō" from "hibernāre" meaning "to spend the winter".  Ultimately from "hiems" meaning "winter".
