nondefensible:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɛnsɪbl/ | /ˌnɑːndɪˈfɛnsɪbl/
adj. | 不可辩护的，无法防守的 | Unable to be defended, justified, or excused; indefensible. | His actions were completely nondefensible. 他的行为完全不可辩护。 | The company's decision to pollute the river was nondefensible.  这家公司污染河流的决定是不可辩护的。 | 近义词: indefensible, unjustifiable, inexcusable | 反义词: defensible, justifiable, excusable | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
defensible (adjective): From Old French defensible, from defendre "to defend," ultimately from Latin defendere "to ward off, protect."
-ible (suffix): Meaning "capable of, worthy of, tending to," from Latin -ibilis.
