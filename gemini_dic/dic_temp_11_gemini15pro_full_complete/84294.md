half-utilized:/ˈhæfˈjuːtəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 半利用的，未充分利用的 |  Partially used; not used to its full potential. It describes something that is being used, but not as much or as effectively as it could be.  | The company's resources are half-utilized, leading to inefficiency.  公司资源利用率不足，导致效率低下。

The half-utilized office space was converted into a co-working area. 未充分利用的办公空间被改造成一个共享办公区域。 | Synonyms: underutilized, underemployed, partially used | Antonyms: fully utilized, maximized, optimized | adjective
