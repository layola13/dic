hotpot:/ˈhɑːtpɑːt/ (US) /ˈhɒtpɒt/ (UK)| n.|火锅，热锅菜|A metal cooking pot with a lid and handle, used for cooking food in liquid over direct heat.  A dish of meat and vegetables cooked in such a pot, usually at the table.|We had a delicious hotpot for dinner last night. 昨晚我们吃了一顿美味的火锅。|Synonyms: casserole, stew, fondue|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for the object, perhaps "cold dish")|noun


hotpot:/ˈhɑːtpɑːt/ (US) /ˈhɒtpɒt/ (UK)| n.|热点，麻烦|A difficult or dangerous situation.|The political situation in the region is a real hotpot.  该地区的政治局势是一个真正的热点。|Synonyms: trouble spot, flashpoint, danger zone|Antonyms: safe zone, peaceful area|noun
