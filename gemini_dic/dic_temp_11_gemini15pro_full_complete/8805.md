antienthusiast:/ˌæntiɪnˈθuːziæst/
n.|反热情者；缺乏热情的人|A person who is opposed to or lacks enthusiasm.|He's such an antienthusiast, he never seems excited about anything. 他真是个缺乏热情的人，似乎对什么都不感到兴奋。| 近义词: cynic, pessimist, skeptic|反义词: enthusiast, fanatic, zealot|词性:名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) : against, opposed to  (From Greek anti- "against, opposite of, instead of, in return, in place of")
enthusiast (noun): a person who is highly interested in a particular activity or subject. (From French enthousiaste, from Greek enthousiastes, from enthousiazein "be inspired or possessed by a god," from en- "in" + theos "god").
