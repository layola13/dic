emuscation: /ˌiːmʌsˈkeɪʃən/
noun
除藓；去苔
The process of removing moss or similar small plants from a surface.
The emuscation of the roof was necessary to prevent further damage.  屋顶除苔对于防止进一步的损坏是必要的。
The gardener undertook the careful emuscation of the old stone wall. 园丁对古老的石墙进行了仔细的除苔工作。
近义词: moss removal, cleaning
反义词: moss growth
