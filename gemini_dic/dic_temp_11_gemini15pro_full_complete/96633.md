indunas:/ɪnˈduːnɑːz/ | n. |酋长，首领，顾问|  A term, primarily used in Southern Africa, referring to chiefs, headmen, or advisors, often within a Zulu or Nguni context.  It's the plural form of "induna". | The indunas met with the king to discuss tribal matters. 酋长们与国王会面讨论部落事务。|  The king sought counsel from his wise indunas. 国王向他睿智的顾问们寻求建议。 |Synonyms: chiefs, headmen, leaders, councilors, advisors | Antonyms: followers, subordinates | noun
