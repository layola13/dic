awarer:/əˈwɛːrər/
adjective
更意识到的，更知道的
more conscious or knowledgeable of something.
She became awarer of the tension in the room. 她越来越意识到房间里的紧张气氛。
近义词:more conscious, more mindful, more cognizant
反义词:less aware, oblivious, unaware
词性:adjective

词源:
From Old English *geware, from Proto-Germanic *gawaraz, from Proto-Indo-European *wor- (to perceive, observe).  The comparative form "awarer" is formed by adding "-er" to the adjective "aware."
