isoclinic:/ˌaɪsəˈklɪnɪk/ | adj. | 等斜的，等倾的 | Having equal inclination; specifically applied to lines having the same inclination to the magnetic meridian, or to crystals whose axes have the same inclination. | The isoclinic lines on the map represent areas where the magnetic dip is the same.  地图上的等斜线表示磁倾角相同的区域。| The two surfaces are isoclinic. 这两个表面是等斜的。 | synonyms: equiangular, isogonal | antonyms: anisotropic | adjective

isoclinic:/ˌaɪsəˈklɪnɪk/ | n. | 等斜线，等倾线 | A line on a map connecting points of equal magnetic dip. | The isoclinics on this chart show the variation in magnetic dip.  这张图表上的等斜线显示了磁倾角的变化。| The isoclinics converge near the magnetic poles. 等斜线在磁极附近会聚。| synonyms: isogonic line, isodynamic line | antonyms: N/A | noun
