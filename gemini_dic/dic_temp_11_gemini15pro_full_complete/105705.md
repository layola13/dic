kinless:/ˈkɪnlɪs/
adjective
无亲无故的；没有亲属的
Without family or relatives; having no close relations.
He felt kinless and alone in the big city. 他在这个大城市感到无亲无故，孤苦伶仃。
Synonyms: friendless, isolated, lonely, solitary
Antonyms: related, kindred, familial
词性: 形容词
