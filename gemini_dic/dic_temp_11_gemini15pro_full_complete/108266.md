launderable:/ˈlɔːndərəbl/ /ˈlɑːndərəbl/| adj.|可洗的，耐洗的|Able to be washed, especially in a washing machine, without being damaged.|This dress is launderable, so you don't need to dry-clean it. 这件衣服可以水洗，所以你不需要干洗。|The sheets are launderable and can be put in the washing machine.  这些床单可以机洗。|近义词：washable, machine-washable|反义词:non-washable, dry-clean only|词性:形容词

词源:
From launder (v.) + -able.  "Launder" comes from the Old French "lavander," meaning "to wash."  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or suitability.
