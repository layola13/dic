hubs:/hʌbz/
n.
中心，枢纽；(轮)毂
A central point of activity or interest; a focal point. A center part of a wheel.
The airport is a major hub for international travel.  这个机场是国际旅行的主要枢纽。
The wheel hubs were covered in mud. 车轮毂上沾满了泥。
Synonyms: center, core, focus, nucleus
Antonyms: periphery, edge, margin
词性: 名词
