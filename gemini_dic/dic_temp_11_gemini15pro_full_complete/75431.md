fruiterer:/ˈfruːtərər/ (UK) /ˈfruːtərər/ (US)| n.|水果商；水果店老板|A person who sells fruit, especially one who owns a shop selling fruit.|例句：The fruiterer had a wide variety of apples on display. 水果商摆放着各种各样的苹果。

例句：I bought some mangoes from the local fruiterer. 我从当地的水果商那里买了一些芒果。


近义词：greengrocer, fruit seller, produce vendor|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
