blackberrylike:/ˈblækˌbɛriˌlaɪk/
adjective
像黑莓的
Resembling a blackberry in appearance, taste, or texture.  Having characteristics similar to a blackberry.
The berries were small and dark, blackberrylike in appearance. 这些浆果又小又黑，外观像黑莓。
The jam had a sweet, slightly tart, blackberrylike flavor.  这果酱有一种甜的，略带酸味的，像黑莓的味道。
近义词:  bramblelike, mulberrylike
反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym)
词性: adjective

blackberry:/ˈblækˌbɛri/
noun
黑莓
A dark purple aggregate fruit of any of various prickly shrubs of the genus Rubus.
I picked a basket of blackberries this morning. 我今天早上摘了一篮子黑莓。
Blackberries are rich in antioxidants. 黑莓富含抗氧化剂。
近义词: bramble, dewberry
反义词: N/A (No direct antonym)
词性: noun

词源:
"blackberrylike" is a compound adjective formed from "blackberry" and the suffix "-like."  "Blackberry" originates from Old English "blæcberie," meaning "black berry."  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
