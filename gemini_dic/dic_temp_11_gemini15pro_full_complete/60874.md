elegize: /ˈɛləˌdʒaɪz/
verb
写挽歌；哀悼；歌颂
to write or speak in praise of someone, often someone who has died; to lament
例句：The poet elegized his friend who had died in the war. 诗人写挽歌悼念在战争中牺牲的朋友。

例句：She elegized the passing of a simpler way of life. 她哀悼一种更简单的生活方式的消逝。

近义词：lament, mourn, eulogize, commemorate
反义词：celebrate, rejoice
词性: v.

词源: From Late Latin elegizare "to write an elegy," from Latin elegia "elegy" (see elegy).
