dynamically:/daɪˈnæmɪkli/ | adv. | 动态地，活力地 | In a way that relates to forces producing motion.  Characterized by constant change, activity, or progress. | The website content is dynamically generated based on user preferences.  网站内容根据用户偏好动态生成。

The system dynamically adjusts the temperature based on the environment. 系统根据环境动态调整温度。 | 近义词: actively, energetically, powerfully | 反义词: statically, passively, inertly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Late Latin *dynamicus, from Ancient Greek δυναμικός (dunamikós, “powerful”), from δύναμις (dúnamis, “power”).
