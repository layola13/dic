nonelects:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛkts/
n.
未当选者；非选民
Those who have not been elected to a position.  A person who does not or cannot vote.
The nonelects voiced their discontent with the election results. 未当选者表达了他们对选举结果的不满。
Many nonelects feel their voices are not heard in the political process. 许多非选民感到他们的声音在政治进程中没有被听到。
近义词:non-voters, defeated candidates
反义词:elects, voters, winners
词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "elect" (chosen).  From Latin "eligere" (to choose out).
