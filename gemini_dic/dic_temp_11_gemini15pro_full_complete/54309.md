dishabit:/dɪsˈhæbɪt/
verb
使不习惯；使失去习惯
To cause to lose a habit; to break of a custom.
The soldiers were dishabited by the long war. 长期的战争使士兵们失去了习惯。
近义词: break, unlearn, discontinue
反义词: habituate, accustom
词性: verb

dishabit:/dɪsˈhæbɪt/
verb
逐出；迫使迁离（尤指家园）
To drive out of a habitat; to dislodge.
Many families were dishabited by the floods. 许多家庭因洪水被迫迁离家园。
近义词: displace, evict, expel
反义词: settle, house
词性: verb
