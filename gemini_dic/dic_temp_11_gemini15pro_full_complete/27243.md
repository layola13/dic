caloriduct:/ˈkælərɪˌdʌkt/ | n. | 热导管，导热管 | A tube or pipe for conducting heat. |  Heat was transferred via a network of caloriducts embedded in the wall.  热量通过嵌入墙体内的热导管网络进行传递。 | Example: The caloriduct efficiently transferred heat from the solar panels to the water tank.  这个热导管有效地将热量从太阳能电池板传递到水箱。 | Synonyms: heat pipe, heat exchanger | Antonyms: insulator | noun
