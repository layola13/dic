cymiferous: /saɪˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
多果实的；结很多果实的
Bearing or producing fruit in abundance.
The cymiferous tree was laden with ripe apples. 这棵多果实的树上挂满了成熟的苹果。
fruitful, prolific, abundant
barren, sterile
