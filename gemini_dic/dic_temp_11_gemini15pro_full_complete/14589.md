avidiously:/əˈvɪdiəsli/ | adv. | 贪婪地；渴望地；热切地 | Showing great enthusiasm for or interest in something, typically something that is new or exciting.  Characterized by greed or intense desire. | She avidiously read every book she could get her hands on. 她如饥似渴地读着她能找到的每一本书。| He avidiously collected stamps. 他热切地收集邮票。 | 近义词：eagerly, greedily, enthusiastically, zealously | 反义词：apathetically, indifferently, halfheartedly | 词性: 副词
