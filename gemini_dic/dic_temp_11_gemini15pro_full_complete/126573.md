naff off:/ˈnæf ɒf/
verb
走开；滚蛋；别烦我
(British, informal, rude) Go away; stop bothering me.
例句：Naff off and leave me alone! 滚开，别烦我！

例句：He told the annoying salesman to naff off. 他让那个烦人的推销员滚蛋。

近义词：piss off, get lost, bugger off, sod off, clear off
反义词: come here, stay, welcome

词性: verb (不及物动词)
