outwork:/ˌaʊtˈwɜːrk/
verb
胜过；工作更努力
to work harder or more effectively than someone else
例句：He outworked everyone on the team. 他比队里任何人都更努力。
例句：She consistently outworks her colleagues and achieves better results. 她一直比同事工作更努力，取得了更好的成绩。

近义词：outperform, surpass, excel, beat
反义词：underperform, lag behind

词性：v.

outwork:/ˈaʊtwɜːrk/
noun
外围工事；外围作业
Defensive works outside a fortified place. Work done outside a factory or office.
例句：The outworks of the castle were heavily defended. 城堡的外围工事防守严密。
例句：The company outsourced some of its outwork to freelancers. 公司将一些外围工作外包给自由职业者。

近义词：rampart, bulwark, fortification, external work
反义词：interior, inside

词性：n.


词源:  From out- + work.  "Work outside" is from the early 15c. The sense of "surpass in working" is from 1875, originally American English.
