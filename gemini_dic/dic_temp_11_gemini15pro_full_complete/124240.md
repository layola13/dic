mossier:/ˈmɔːsiər/ (US), /ˈmɒsiə(r)/ (UK)| adj.| 苔藓的；长满苔藓的；古老的|Covered with moss; resembling moss; suggesting antiquity.|The old stone wall was mossier than I remembered. 那堵旧石墙比我记忆中的苔藓更多了。|近义词：mossy, overgrown, ancient, antique|反义词: bare, new, modern|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *mossi, from Old English *mosig, from moss + -ig. Cognate with Dutch mossig (“mossy”), German moosig (“mossy”).
