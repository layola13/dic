etiologist:/ˌiːtiˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |病原学家|A specialist in etiology; one who studies the causes or origins of diseases.| The etiologist traced the outbreak of the disease back to contaminated water.  这位病原学家将疾病的爆发追溯到受污染的水。|Synonyms:  pathologist,  causationist|Antonyms: None|Noun
