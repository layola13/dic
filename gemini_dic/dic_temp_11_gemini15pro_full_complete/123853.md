moralizer:/ˈmɔːrəlaɪzər/| n.|说教者，道德家|A person who preaches moral principles, often in a self-righteous or tedious way.  A person who lectures others on morality, often seen as excessively critical or judgmental. |He's such a moralizer; he always makes me feel guilty. 他真是个爱说教的人；他总是让我感到内疚。|Synonyms: preacher, sermonizer, didactic |Antonyms: hedonist, libertine| noun
