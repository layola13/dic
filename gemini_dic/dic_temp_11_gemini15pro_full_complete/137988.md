noob:/nuːb/ | n. |菜鸟，新手|A newbie; someone inexperienced or unskilled, especially in a particular activity or game. | He's a total noob at this game, he keeps dying. 他玩这个游戏完全是个菜鸟，总是死。|  She's such a noob when it comes to computers. 她在电脑方面真是个菜鸟。 | 近义词: newbie, beginner, rookie, amateur | 反义词: expert, veteran, pro, master | 词性: noun

词源: Shortening of newbie, which itself is derived from "new" + "-bie" (a suffix denoting a person new to something).
