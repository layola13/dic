briolette:/briːəˈlɛt/
n.
水滴形宝石; 梨形宝石
a pear-shaped gemstone, especially a diamond, cut with facets all around.
This briolette diamond pendant is truly stunning. 这款水滴形钻石吊坠真是令人惊艳。
synonyms: drop-cut, pear-shaped gem, pendeloque
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a shape. Could use terms like "round-cut," "square-cut" depending on context.)
