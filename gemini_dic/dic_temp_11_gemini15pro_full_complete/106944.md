laciest: /ˈleɪsiɪst/ | adj. | 最懒惰的 | Superlative form of lazy; most unwilling to work or use energy.  | He's the laziest person I know; he never lifts a finger. 他是我认识的最懒的人，他从不帮忙。| 近义词:  slothful, indolent, idle | 反义词:  hardworking, diligent, energetic | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *laci, from Old Norse *laki ("defect, failing"), from Proto-Germanic *lakō ("defect, failing").  Related to lack.
