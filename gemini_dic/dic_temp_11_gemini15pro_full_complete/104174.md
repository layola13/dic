junker:/ˈdʒʌŋkər/| n.|破烂货；破车；(尤指东普鲁士的)容克,垃圾佬|A person, especially a man, who collects or deals in discarded material.|The old junker was barely running.  这辆老爷破车几乎跑不动了。|Synonyms: clunker, wreck, beater|Antonyms:  new car, luxury car|noun

junker:/ˈdʒʌŋkər/| n.| (尤指东普鲁士的)容克(拥有大量土地的贵族)|A member of the Prussian landed aristocracy, especially one who supported the policies of Bismarck.|The Junkers held considerable political power in pre-war Germany.  容克在战前德国拥有相当大的政治权力。|Synonyms: aristocrat, nobleman, landholder|Antonyms: commoner, peasant|noun
