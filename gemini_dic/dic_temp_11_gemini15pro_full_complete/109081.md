legating: /ˈlɛɡeɪtɪŋ/
v.
委派；授权；遗赠
The act of entrusting or assigning a task, duty, or right to another; bequeathing something in a will.
He is legating his vast fortune to his only daughter. 他把他的巨额财产遗赠给了他唯一的女儿。
Synonyms: entrusting, assigning, delegating, bequeathing, committing, transferring
Antonyms: retaining, withholding, keeping, refusing
