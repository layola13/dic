nonanesthetized:/nɒnˌænɪsˈθɛtaɪzd/
adjective
未麻醉的
not anesthetized; not having received anesthesia.  Not numbed or made insensitive to pain.
The researchers conducted experiments on nonanesthetized animals.  研究人员对未麻醉的动物进行了实验。
The nonanesthetized patient felt a sharp pain during the procedure. 未麻醉的病人在手术过程中感到一阵剧痛。
Synonyms: awake, conscious, alert, sensitive
Antonyms: anesthetized, numb, unconscious, insensible
词性: adjective
