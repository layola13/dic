nonrecoil:/ˌnɒnˈrɪˈkɔɪl/ | adj. | 无后坐力的 | Lacking recoil; designed to minimize or eliminate recoil. |  A nonrecoil rifle is a powerful weapon with minimal kickback. 无后坐力步枪是一种强大的武器，后坐力极小。

The nonrecoil gun was developed for use in confined spaces.  无后坐力炮是为在密闭空间中使用而开发的。 | 近义词: recoilless | 反义词: recoiling | 词性: 形容词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + recoil (verb meaning "to spring back")
