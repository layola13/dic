assignors:/əˈsaɪnɔːrz/
n.
转让人，让与人
People or entities who transfer their rights or property to another party.
The assignors transferred all their rights in the patent to the company.  转让人将他们在专利中的所有权利转让给了公司。
The contract listed the assignors and their respective shares. 合同列出了转让人及其各自的股份。
synonyms: transferors, grantors
antonyms: assignees, grantees
noun
