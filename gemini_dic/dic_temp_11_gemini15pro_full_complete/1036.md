accelerations: /əkˌsɛləˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 加速；增加 |  Increases in the rate or speed of something. |  The company experienced rapid accelerations in growth during the first quarter.  公司在第一季度经历了快速的增长。| synonyms: increases, expansions, rises | antonyms: decreases, reductions, declines | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *accelerationem, from Latin acceleratus, past participle of accelerare "to hasten," from ad- "to" + celer "swift."
