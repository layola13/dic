nondiplomacies:/ˌnɒndɪˈpləʊməsiz/ | n. |缺乏外交手腕；非外交行为|The president's nondiplomacies have strained international relations. 总统缺乏外交手腕使国际关系紧张。|例句：His nondiplomacies caused a rift between the two countries. 他的非外交行为导致两国关系破裂。|近义词：tactlessness, bluntness, indiscretion|反义词：diplomacy, tact, discretion|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "diplomacy" (from French "diplomatie," ultimately from Greek "diploma," meaning "folded document").
