flourishes:/ˈflɜːrɪʃɪz/ (US), /ˈflʌrɪʃɪz/ (UK)| v. (third-person singular simple present flourishes, present participle flourishing, simple past flourished, past participle flourished) | 繁荣，兴旺；挥舞；炫耀 | to grow or develop successfully; to wave something around in a way that makes people notice it | The company flourished under his leadership.  这家公司在他的领导下蓬勃发展。 He flourished the sword before the battle. 他在战斗前挥舞着剑。 | synonyms: thrive, prosper, brandish, wave | antonyms: decline, wither, fail | verb
