coguardian:/koʊˈɡɑːrdiən/| n.|共同监护人|A person who shares the legal responsibility for the care and upbringing of a child with another person, typically a parent.|例句：Both parents agreed to appoint a coguardian for their children in case of their untimely deaths. 双方父母同意指定一名共同监护人为他们的孩子，以防他们过早死亡。

|例句：The coguardians worked together to make decisions about the child's education and healthcare. 共同监护人共同决定孩子的教育和医疗保健。|近义词：joint guardian, shared guardian|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
