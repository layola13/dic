cindery: /ˈsɪndəri/| adj.|灰烬的；含煤渣的；像煤渣的|Containing or resembling cinders; full of cinders.|The ground was cindery and barren after the fire. 火灾过后，地面全是煤渣，寸草不生。|Synonyms: ashen, ash-like, slaggy|Antonyms: fertile, lush|adjective

cindery: /ˈsɪndəri/| n.|煤渣状物；含煤渣之物|Something that resembles or contains cinders.|The cindery remains of the bonfire smoldered for days.  篝火的余烬闷烧了好几天。|Synonyms: ash, embers|Antonyms:  None|noun
