occlude:/əˈkluːd/| v. | 阻塞，闭塞 |To stop, close, or obstruct an opening, passage, or vessel.| The thrombus occluded the artery, cutting off blood flow. 血栓阻塞了动脉，切断了血流。|近义词：block, obstruct, close |反义词: open, clear| verb


occluded:/əˈkluːdɪd/| adj. | 闭塞的，阻塞的 |Closed, blocked, or obstructed.| The occluded artery caused severe pain. 闭塞的动脉引起了剧烈的疼痛。|近义词：blocked, obstructed, closed|反义词: open, clear| adjective



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin occludere "to shut up, close," from Latin ob "against, in the way" + claudere "to shut".
