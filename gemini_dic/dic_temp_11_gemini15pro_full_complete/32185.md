chiasmus:/kaɪˈæzməs/ | n. | 交叉配列法，交错配列法 | A rhetorical or literary figure in which words, grammatical constructions, or concepts are repeated in reverse order, in the same or a modified form.  | Example: "Ask not what your country can do for you; ask what you can do for your country."  肯尼迪的就职演说：“不要问你的国家能为你做什么，而要问你能为你的国家做什么。” | Example: "Poetry is the record of the best and happiest moments of the happiest and best minds."  诗歌是最佳头脑中最快乐时刻的记录，也是最快乐头脑中最棒时刻的记录。 |Synonym: antimetabole, inversion | Antonym: N/A | noun
