expirable:/ɪkˈspaɪərəbl/
adjective
可过期的，会到期的
Subject to expiry or capable of expiring; able to lose validity after a period of time.
The coupon is expirable on December 31st. 这优惠券在12月31日到期。
This milk is expirable in three days. 这牛奶三天后过期。
synonyms: perishable, short-lived, temporary
antonyms: permanent, everlasting, non-expiring
词性: adjective
