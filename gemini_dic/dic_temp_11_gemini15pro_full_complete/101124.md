investible:/ɪnˈvɛstəbl/| adj.|可投资的，适宜投资的|Suitable for investment; capable of being invested.|例句：The company is looking for investible assets in emerging markets.  该公司正在新兴市场寻找可投资资产。|近义词：bankable, financeable|反义词：uninvestable, risky|词性:形容词
Etymology: From invest + -able.  Invest comes from Latin investire, meaning "to clothe in," later "to endow with authority or office," then "to invest money."
