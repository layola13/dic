hagiographists:/ˌhæɡiˈɒɡrəfɪsts/ | n. |圣徒传记作者；吹捧者|A writer of hagiographies; a biographer who idealizes or idolizes the subject. | The hagiographists portrayed the king as a saintly figure. 圣徒传记作者将国王描绘成圣人。|  These hagiographists often exaggerate the virtues of their subjects. 这些吹捧者经常夸大他们对象的优点。|  biographers, eulogists | critics, debunkers | noun
