encoffin:/ɪnˈkɒfɪn/ /ɛnˈkɔːfɪn/
verb
入殓；装入棺材
to place (a corpse) in a coffin.
The undertaker encoffined the body. 殡仪员将尸体入殓。
They encoffined her remains and held a small, private funeral. 他们将她的遗体入殓，并举行了一个小型私人葬礼。
近义词:bury, inter
反义词:exhume, disinter
词性:v.
