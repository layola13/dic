downward:/ˈdaʊnwərd/ (US), /ˈdaʊnwəd/ (UK)| adj. adv.|向下地，下降地|Moving towards a lower place or level; decreasing in size, amount, or intensity.|例句：The stock market took a downward turn. 股票市场呈下降趋势。|近义词：descending, downwards|反义词：upward, ascending|词性:形容词/副词

downwards:/ˈdaʊnwədz/ (UK)| adv.|向下地，下降地|Towards a lower place or level.|例句：He looked downwards and saw the valley below. 他向下看，看到了下面的山谷。|近义词：downward, descending|反义词：upward, ascending|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dūnewearde (unattested), from dūne (“down”) + -weard (“-ward”). Cognate with Old Frisian dūneward (“downward”), Old Saxon dūniward (“downward”), Middle Dutch donwaerts (“downwards”), Old High German dūnewart (“downward”), German dünnwärts (“thinly scattered”).
