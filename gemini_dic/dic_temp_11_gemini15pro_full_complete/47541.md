deathling: /ˈdeθlɪŋ/
n.
亡灵；必死之人
A person or creature destined to die; a mortal.  A term often used in fantasy or literary contexts.
The deathling wandered the earth, searching for meaning.  亡灵徘徊在地球上，寻找意义。
The ancient prophecy spoke of a deathling who would save the world.  古老的预言提到一个将拯救世界的必死之人。
synonyms: mortal, human
antonyms: immortal, god, deity
词性: 名词
