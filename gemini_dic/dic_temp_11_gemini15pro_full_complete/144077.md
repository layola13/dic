outrunning:/ˌaʊtˈrʌnɪŋ/
verb
超越，跑得比…快
The act of running faster than someone or something else.
He was outrunning the police car. 他跑得比警车还快。

outrunning:/ˌaʊtˈrʌnɪŋ/
verb
超过，胜过
The act of exceeding or surpassing something.
Our sales figures are outrunning our projections. 我们的销售额超过了预期。

outrunning:/ˌaʊtˈrʌnɪŋ/
verb
逃脱，躲避
The act of escaping or evading something.
He was outrunning his problems. 他在逃避他的问题。


近义词: outstripping, exceeding, surpassing, escaping, evading
反义词: lagging behind, falling behind, succumbing to

词性:  present participle of outrun
