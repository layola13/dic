misplant:/ˌmɪsˈplɑːnt/ | v. n. | 误植，移植不当，移栽到不适宜的地方 | To plant in the wrong place or position, or in unsuitable soil.  |  The gardener misplanted the roses in the shade. 园丁把玫瑰误种在了阴凉处。 | synonyms: transplant incorrectly, wrongly position | antonyms: plant correctly, properly position | verb

misplant:/ˌmɪsˈplɑːnt/ | n. | 误植，移植错误 | A plant that has been planted in the wrong place or position. | The misplant withered quickly in the dry soil.  这株被误植的植物在干燥的土壤中很快就枯萎了。 | synonyms: wrongly positioned plant, incorrectly transplanted plant | antonyms: correctly positioned plant | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + plant (to put a plant into the ground so that it will grow).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English and indicates something done wrongly or badly. "Plant" comes from Old French "planter" and ultimately from Latin "plantare" (to set, to plant).
