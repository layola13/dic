judgmental: /ˌdʒʌdʒˈmentl/ | adj. | 判断的，爱评判的 | Having or displaying a tendency to make judgments (especially moral judgments) about others. |  She cast a judgmental eye over his scruffy clothes. 她用评判的眼光打量着他邋遢的衣服。

| Example: He made a judgmental remark about her lifestyle. 他对她的生活方式发表了评判性的言论。

| Synonyms: critical, censorious, disapproving, fault-finding | Antonyms: accepting, tolerant, open-minded, non-judgmental | Word type: Adjective
