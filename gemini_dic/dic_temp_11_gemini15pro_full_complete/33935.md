cirriform:/ˈsɪrɪfɔːrm/| adj.|卷云状的，纤维状的|Resembling a cirrus cloud; composed of or arranged in delicate filaments or fibers.|例句：The cirriform clouds drifted lazily across the sky. 卷云状的云朵懒洋洋地飘过天空。

例句：The bacteria formed cirriform colonies on the agar plate. 细菌在琼脂平板上形成了纤维状的菌落。|近义词：filamentous, fibrous, threadlike|反义词: cumuliform, stratiform |词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cirriformis*, from cirrus (“curl, wisp”) + -formis (“-shaped”).
