countermove:/ˈkaʊntərˌmuːv/| n.|反击，应对策略|An action taken in opposition to another action.|  The company launched a countermove against its competitor's aggressive advertising campaign.  公司针对竞争对手咄咄逼人的广告攻势发起反击。|synonyms: retaliation, riposte, counterattack| antonyms:  initiative, attack|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + move.  "Counter-" acts as a prefix meaning "against" or "in opposition to." "Move" refers to an action or strategy.
