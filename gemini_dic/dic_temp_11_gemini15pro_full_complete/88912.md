hold-in:/ˈhoʊldɪn/| adj.|抑制的，忍住的|Suppressed or restrained, especially referring to a bodily function, emotion, or feeling.|Her hold-in cough was noticeable to everyone in the room. 她忍住咳嗽的样子房间里每个人都注意到了。|Synonyms: suppressed, restrained|Antonyms: released, expressed|adjective
