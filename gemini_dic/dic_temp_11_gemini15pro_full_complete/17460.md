bearably:/ˈberəbli/| adv.|可以忍受地，尚可地|In a way that can be tolerated or endured; moderately.|The pain was bearably mild. 疼痛轻微，尚可忍受。|近义词：tolerably, acceptably, moderately|反义词：unbearably, intolerably|词性:副词
词源: From bearable + -ly.  "Bearable" comes from the verb "bear" (meaning "to endure") + the suffix "-able" (meaning "capable of being").  The verb "bear" has its roots in Old English "beran," related to the Old High German "beran" and the Gothic "bairan."
