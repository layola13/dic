corselet: /ˈkɔːrslɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɔːrslət/ (US)| n. |紧身胸衣；胸甲|A close-fitting laced bodice or underbodice, often reinforced with stays, worn by women to support or shape the figure; a piece of armor covering the torso.|The queen wore a corselet encrusted with jewels.|Synonyms: bodice, stays, bustier, basque|Antonyms: loose clothing, robe|noun
