bathmism:/ˈbæθmɪzəm/
n.
生长力;生长冲动;刺激生长
The inherent growth force or growth impulse, especially as postulated by some vitalists.
The bathmism of the tree caused it to grow tall and strong. 树木的生长力使它长得又高又壮。

Etymology: From Greek  βαθμός (bathmós, “step, degree”) + -ism.  The term was coined by the French physiologist Claude Bernard.
近义词: growth, development,  vital force,  nisus
反义词: atrophy, decline, degeneration, decay
词性: noun
