dancy:/ˈdænsi/| adj.| 舞蹈的，适合跳舞的；优美的，轻快的|Characterized by or suitable for dancing; graceful and lively.  Relating to or suggestive of dance. |Her dancy little tune was perfect for the party. 她轻快的小曲子非常适合派对。|近义词：rhythmic, graceful, lively, spirited|反义词:clumsy, awkward|词性:形容词

词源:  Likely derived from "dance" (n.) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Dance" itself comes from Old French "dancier."
