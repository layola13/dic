ostensibility:/ɑːˌstɛnsəˈbɪləti/
n.
表面性；貌似；虚饰
the appearance or pretense of being one thing when it is really something else
He maintained a posture of ostensibility while secretly plotting his revenge. 他表面上装作若无其事，暗地里却在密谋复仇。
近义词：pretense, semblance, appearance, show
反义词：reality, genuineness, authenticity
词性：名词

ostensible:/ɑːˈstɛnsəbl/
adj.
表面上的；貌似的；声称的
stated or appearing to be true, but not necessarily so
The ostensible reason for his visit was business, but he was really there to see his girlfriend. 他表面上的访问理由是商务，但实际上他是去看他的女朋友。
近义词：apparent, seeming, alleged, professed
反义词：real, actual, genuine, true
词性：形容词
