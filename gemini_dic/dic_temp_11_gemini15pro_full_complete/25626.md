bun:/bʌn/| n.|小圆面包；圆形发髻|A small, round, sweet cake, typically containing dried fruit.  A hairstyle in which the hair is drawn back into a tight coil at the back of the head.|例句：She ate a cinnamon bun for breakfast. 她早餐吃了一个肉桂卷。|例句：She wore her hair in a bun. 她把头发盘成一个发髻。|近义词：roll, coil (hair)|反义词: (No direct antonym for food. For hairstyle, perhaps "loose hair")|名词
