besmear: /bɪˈsmɪr/ | vt. | 涂抹；弄脏；诽谤 | To smear all over; to soil or stain. |  He besmeared his face with mud. 他把脸上涂满了泥巴。

The wall was besmeared with graffiti. 墙上被涂满了涂鸦。 | 近义词: smear, daub, smudge | 反义词: clean, cleanse | 词性: 动词


besmear: /bɪˈsmɪr/ | vt. | 诽谤，玷污（名誉）| To damage the reputation of someone by making false and malicious statements. | The politician's reputation was besmeared by scandalous rumours.  这位政治家的名誉被丑闻玷污了。

His opponents tried to besmear his character. 他的对手试图诽谤他的人格。 | 近义词: slander, defame, vilify | 反义词: praise, commend | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Old English *besmīeran, from be- + smīeran  "to smear."  The prefix "be-" intensifies the meaning of the verb "smear."
