bleater:/ˈbliːtər/| n.|爱抱怨的人，咩咩叫的动物 (尤指羊)|A person who complains constantly; a sheep or goat, especially one that bleats.|The bleater next door complained again about the noise.|隔壁那个爱抱怨的人又抱怨噪音了。|近义词：complainer, whiner, sheep, goat|反义词:praiser, silent person|noun

bleat:/bliːt/| v.  n.|发出咩咩叫声；抱怨；愚蠢地说|1.(v.) (of a sheep, goat, etc.) make a characteristic high-pitched cry; 2.(v.) complain feebly; 3.(n.) the cry of a sheep, goat, or calf; a feeble complaint.|1. The lambs bleated pitifully.|小羊羔可怜地咩咩叫。 2. He bleated about the unfairness of it all.|他抱怨这一切多么不公平。 3.
