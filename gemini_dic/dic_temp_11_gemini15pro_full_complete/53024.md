diocesan:/daɪˈɒsɪzən/ /daɪˈɑːsɪzən/
adjective|主教管区的；教区的|Relating to a diocese or bishop. |The diocesan synod meets annually.  教区主教会议每年举行一次。|The bishop addressed the diocesan council. 主教向教区议会发表了讲话。|近义词:episcopal|反义词:N/A
noun|主教管区的人；教区居民|A person who lives in a particular diocese. |He is a diocesan of the Chicago diocese. 他是芝加哥教区居民。|She is a diocesan known for her charitable works. 她是一位以慈善工作闻名的教区居民。|近义词:parishioner|反义词:N/A
词性: adjective, noun

词源:
From Late Latin *dioecesanus, from dioecesis (“diocese”), from Ancient Greek διοίκησις (dioíkēsis, “administration, province”).
