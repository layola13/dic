catchword:/ˈkætʃwɜːrd/ (US) /ˈkætʃwɜːd/ (UK)| n.|流行语；口号；醒目标语；(字典页左上角的)词目|A word or phrase that is used repeatedly and becomes associated with a particular person, group, or idea.|The president's catchword is "Make America Great Again".|近义词：slogan, motto, keyword, buzzword|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "obscure term")|noun
