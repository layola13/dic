cope-with:/koʊp wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 处理，应付 | to deal effectively with something difficult |  He copes with stress by exercising regularly. 他通过定期锻炼来应对压力。| 近义词：deal with, handle, manage | 反义词: succumb to, give in to | 词性: 动词短语

Etymology:
"Cope" comes from Old French "couper" (to strike, hit), later meaning "to encounter in battle."  "With" indicates accompaniment or opposition. The combined meaning evolved to dealing successfully with a difficult situation.
