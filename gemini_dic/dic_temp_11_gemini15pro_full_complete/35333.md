co-aration:/kəʊˌɛəˈreɪʃən/ |n.|共同耕作，联合耕作|The co-aration of the land allowed the small farmers to compete with larger farms.|例句：The co-aration agreement allowed the families to share the workload and increase their yields. 联合耕作协议允许这些家庭分担工作量并提高产量。|例句：Co-aration was essential for the survival of the small farming communities. 对于小型农业社区的生存来说，共同耕作至关重要。|近义词：cooperation, collaboration, joint effort|反义词：independent work, solo effort|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *coaratio, from Latin co- + aratio (“plowing”), from arare (“to plow”).
