confused:/kənˈfjuːzd/ | adj. | 迷茫的，困惑的 | Unable to think clearly; bewildered. | I was confused by the complicated instructions. 我被复杂的说明弄糊涂了。 |  She felt confused and disoriented after the long flight.  长途飞行后，她感到迷茫和不知所措。 | 近义词: bewildered, puzzled, perplexed, disoriented | 反义词: clear, certain, confident | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *confusen*, from Old French confus, past participle of confondre (“to confuse, confound”), from Latin confundere (“to pour together, mix, mingle, confuse”), from com- (“together”) + fundere (“to pour”).
