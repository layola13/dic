cachemia: /kæˈkiːmiə/
n.
恶病质，体液损耗
A condition of the body fluids, especially the blood, due to or resembling cachexia.  A general state of ill health involving emaciation and wasting.
Cachemia is a severe form of malnutrition that can occur in patients with chronic illnesses such as cancer. 恶病质是一种严重的营养不良，可发生于癌症等慢性疾病患者。
The patient's cachemia was so advanced that he was barely able to walk. 病人的恶病质非常严重，几乎走不动路了。
近义词: cachexia, emaciation, wasting, inanition
反义词: obesity, corpulence
词性: noun
