justifier:/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪər/| n.|辩护者，证明者，排版用的调整器|Someone or something that justifies something; a tool used in typesetting to adjust spacing between words and lines. |The lawyer acted as his justifier in court. 律师在法庭上充当了他的辩护人。| synonym: advocate, defender, vindicator | antonym: accuser, opponent, detractor | noun
justifier:/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪər/| n.|辩护，证明，正当理由|A fact, circumstance, or explanation that justifies something. |His justifier for being late was that his car broke down. 他迟到的理由是他的车抛锚了。| synonym: excuse, reason, explanation | antonym: accusation, condemnation, incrimination | noun
