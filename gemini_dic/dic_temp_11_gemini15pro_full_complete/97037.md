infectors:/ɪnˈfɛktərz/ | n. | 传染者，感染者 | People who transmit an infectious disease to others. | The infectors were quarantined to prevent further spread of the virus.  这些传染者被隔离以防止病毒进一步传播。 | Synonyms: transmitters, carriers, spreaders | Antonyms: immune individuals, resistant individuals | noun

infectors:/ɪnˈfɛktərz/ | n. | 传染者，感染者 | Agents or vectors that transmit an infectious disease. |  Mosquitoes can be infectors of malaria. 蚊子可以是疟疾的传染者。 | Synonyms: vectors, agents, transmitters | Antonyms: cures, remedies | noun
