/wɒt/ (UK) /hwɑːt/ (US)| pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么，多么 | used to ask for information about something or to ask about the reason for something | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| sort, kind, type | which | pronoun, determiner, adverb

/wʌt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US)| interjection | 什么（表示惊讶，不相信等） | used to express surprise or disbelief | What! You didn't go? 什么！你没去？| Really? | No way! | interjection


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living?  她靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? 她晚饭做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? 他整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? 在做决定之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you?  在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen if we don't act now? 如果我们现在不行动会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们下次会议将讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到今年年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will he have been studying by the time he graduates? 到他毕业时，他将已经学习什么多久了？
