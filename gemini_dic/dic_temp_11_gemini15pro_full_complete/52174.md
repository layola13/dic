diapophysis:/ˌdaɪəˈpɒfɪsɪs/ /ˌdaɪəˈpɑːfɪsɪs/
n.
骨干;骨干;骨干
The shaft of a long bone, between the epiphyses.
The diaphysis is the main or midsection (shaft) of a long bone. It is made up mostly of compact bone.
例句：The diaphysis of the femur is prone to fractures.  股骨骨干易发生骨折。

例句：A fracture in the diaphysis of the humerus can be quite painful. 肱骨骨干骨折会非常疼痛。

近义词：shaft
反义词:epiphyses
词性:noun
