lateritious:/ˌleɪtəˈrɪʃəs/
adjective
砖红色的，似砖的
Resembling brick; brick-red.
The lateritious soil was rich in iron oxide, giving it its distinctive hue.  砖红色的土壤富含氧化铁，赋予了它独特的色调。
The building was constructed of lateritious bricks. 这座建筑是用砖红色的砖建造的。
Synonyms: brick-red, reddish-brown, terra-cotta
Antonyms: pale, white, ashen
词性: adjective
