idiosyncrasy: /ˌɪdiəˈsɪŋkrəsi/ | n. | 特质，癖好，怪癖 | A mode of behavior or way of thought peculiar to an individual.  A distinctive or peculiar feature or characteristic of a place or thing. |  He has a few harmless idiosyncrasies, like always wearing a bow tie. 他有一些无伤大雅的怪癖，比如总是戴着领结。|  One of the idiosyncrasies of this printer is that you can't stop it once it has started. 这台打印机的一个怪癖是一旦启动就无法停止。 | synonym: peculiarity, quirk, eccentricity | antonym: normality, conformity | noun
