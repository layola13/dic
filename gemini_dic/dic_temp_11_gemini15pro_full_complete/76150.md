fuselike:/ˈfjuːzlaɪk/
adjective
像保险丝的；易熔的
Resembling a fuse; likely to melt or burn quickly.
The thin, fuselike wire glowed red hot.  细细的、像保险丝一样的电线发出炽热的光。
The artist used a fuselike material to create the sculpture.  艺术家用一种易熔的材料来创作雕塑。
Synonyms: fusible, meltable, flammable
Antonyms: nonflammable, fire-resistant
词性: 形容词
