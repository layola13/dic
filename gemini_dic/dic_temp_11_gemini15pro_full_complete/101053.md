inveil:/ɪnˈveɪl/
verb
掩盖，遮蔽
to cover something with a veil; to hide or disguise something
例句：The bride's face was inveiled until the ceremony.  新娘的脸在仪式前一直被遮盖着。

例句：The company inveiled its new product at a press conference.  该公司在新闻发布会上公布了其新产品。
近义词：conceal, hide, obscure, shroud, mask
反义词：reveal, uncover, expose
词性：v.
