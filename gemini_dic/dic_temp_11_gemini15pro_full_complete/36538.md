collaboratives:/kəˈlæbərətɪvz/
n.| 协作项目，合作成果； 合作式项目| Projects or ventures undertaken jointly by multiple parties.  The exhibition showcased several student collaboratives.  这个展览展示了几个学生的合作项目。|近义词: joint projects, partnerships, cooperative efforts|反义词: solo projects, individual efforts|词性:名词

collaborative:/kəˈlæbərətɪv/
adj.| 协作的，合作的| Involving joint effort and cooperation.  The team adopted a collaborative approach to problem-solving.  该团队采用了一种协作的方式来解决问题。|近义词: cooperative, joint, shared|反义词: individual, independent, solo|词性:形容词
