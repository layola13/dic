immeshed:/ɪˈmɛʃt/
adjective
缠住的，陷入的
involved in a complicated or unpleasant situation that it is difficult to escape from.
The whales were immeshed in the fishing nets. 鲸鱼被缠在渔网里。
近义词: entangled, trapped, involved
反义词: free, extricated, uninvolved
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From the verb "immesh," derived from the prefix "in-" (meaning "in," "into," "within") and the word "mesh" (referring to the openings in a net).  The word "mesh" ultimately comes from the Old English "mesc" or "max," meaning "net."
