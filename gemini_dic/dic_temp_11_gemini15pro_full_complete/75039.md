frigate:/ˈfrɪɡət/| n.|护卫舰|A warship that is smaller than a destroyer and larger than a corvette.|The frigate patrolled the coastline. 护卫舰在海岸线巡逻。|近义词：warship, destroyer (larger), corvette (smaller)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "civilian ship")|noun
