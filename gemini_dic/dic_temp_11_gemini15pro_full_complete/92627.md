iatrochemistry:/ˌaɪətroʊˈkɛmɪstri/
noun
医化学
A historical approach to medicine that sought to understand and treat disease in terms of chemical principles, popular in the 16th and 17th centuries.
Paracelsus was a key figure in the development of iatrochemistry.  帕拉塞尔苏斯是医化学发展的关键人物。
Iatrochemistry played a significant role in the transition from alchemy to modern chemistry. 医化学在从炼金术向现代化学的过渡中发挥了重要作用。
Synonyms:  chemiatry,  medical chemistry
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with traditional herbal medicine or other non-chemical approaches)
