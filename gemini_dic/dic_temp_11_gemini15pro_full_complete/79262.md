glassmaker:/ˈɡlæsˌmeɪkər/ | n. |玻璃制造者，玻璃工|A person who makes glass or glassware. | The glassmaker carefully shaped the molten glass into a delicate vase. 玻璃制造者小心翼翼地把熔化的玻璃塑造成一个精致的花瓶。| 近义词: glassblower, glass artisan | 反义词: None | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From glass + maker.  "glass" comes from Old English "glæs," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*glasam."  "maker" comes from Old English "-maca" (suffix forming agent nouns), from Proto-Germanic "*makōną" (to make).
