The term arose from the evolution of cloud computing.  Initially, cloud services primarily focused on Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), providing basic computing resources like servers and storage. The next logical step was to offer amore comprehensive platform, encompassing not only the infrastructure but also development tools and middleware, leading to the development of PaaS.
