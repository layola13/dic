aspartame: /ˈæspərteɪm/ | n. | 阿斯巴甜 | An artificial non-saccharide sweetener about 200 times as sweet as sucrose, and is commonly used as a sugar substitute in foods and beverages. |  Many diet sodas use aspartame as a sweetener. 很多无糖汽水使用阿斯巴甜作为甜味剂。

Aspartame is used as a table-top sweetener. 阿斯巴甜被用作餐桌甜味剂。

| 近义词: NutraSweet, Equal, sweetener | 反义词: sugar, sucrose | 词性: noun
