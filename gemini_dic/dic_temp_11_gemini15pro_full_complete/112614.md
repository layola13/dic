low-church:/ˌloʊˈtʃɜːrtʃ/| adj.|低教会派的，简朴的|Favoring a simple and unritualistic form of worship.  Emphasizing the preaching of the gospel over elaborate ceremonies.|例句：The new vicar’s low-church approach was welcomed by many in the congregation.  新牧师的低教会派方式受到了会众中许多人的欢迎。

例句：Her low-church wedding ceremony focused on the spiritual union rather than elaborate decorations. 她的简朴婚礼仪式注重精神上的结合，而不是精心装饰。|近义词：Evangelical, Protestant, simple, unpretentious|反义词：High-church, Anglo-Catholic, elaborate, ornate|词性:形容词
