nonhereditarily:/ˌnɒnheˈrɛdɪtərɪli/ | adv. | 非遗传地 | Not passed down through genes; acquired or developed during one's lifetime. | Example: The disease was acquired nonhereditarily, meaning it wasn't passed down from his parents.  这种疾病是非遗传获得的，意味着它不是从他父母那里遗传下来的。 | Example:  His musical talent developed nonhereditarily, fostered by years of dedicated practice. 他的音乐才能是非遗传性发展起来的，是多年来刻苦练习的结果。| Synonym: environmentally, acquiredly | Antonym: hereditarily, genetically | Word Type: Adverb
