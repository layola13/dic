cymule:/ˈsaɪmjuːl/ | n. | 幼苗，秧苗; particularly a young sprout of a plant raised from seed. | A cymule is a young plant, especially one grown from seed, that has just begun to develop its first true leaves. | The delicate cymules emerged from the soil after a week.  幼嫩的幼苗一周后破土而出。 | Example: The gardener carefully transplanted the tiny cymules into individual pots. 园丁小心翼翼地将小幼苗移植到单独的花盆里。| synonyms: seedling, sprout, plantlet | antonyms: mature plant, tree | noun
