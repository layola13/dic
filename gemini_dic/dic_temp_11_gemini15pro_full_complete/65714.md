eventilation:/ˌvɛntɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 通风，换气 |The process of providing fresh air to a room or building or of exhausting stale or polluted air. |The ventilation in this room is poor.  这个房间的通风很差。|Synonyms: airing, aeration, vent |Antonyms: suffocation, confinement | noun

eventilate:/ˈvɛntɪleɪt/ | vt. | 使通风，使换气；公开表达（感情或意见） |To allow fresh air to enter and circulate freely in (a room, building, etc.).  |We need to ventilate this room. 我们需要给这个房间通风。 He ventilated his grievances at the meeting. 他在会上表达了他的不满。 |Synonyms: air, aerate, air out |Antonyms: stifle, suffocate | verb
