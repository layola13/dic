cocounselling:/ˌkəʊˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/
n.
共同辅导；合作咨询
A form of peer counselling where two people take turns listening to and supporting each other, without giving advice or imposing solutions.
例句：Cocounselling can be a powerful tool for personal growth. 共同辅导可以成为个人成长的有力工具。

例句：They met weekly for cocounselling sessions. 他们每周见面进行共同辅导。
近义词：peer counselling, mutual support
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps) traditional therapy, professional counselling
词性:noun
