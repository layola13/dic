outmanipulating: /ˌaʊtməˈnɪpjuleɪtɪŋ/
verb
中文翻译：通过操纵胜过，比...更会操纵
英文解析：To surpass or defeat someone by being more skillful or cunning in manipulation.  This implies outsmarting someone through deceitful or clever tactics.
例句：He outmanipulated his opponents in the business deal, securing the best terms for himself. 他在商业交易中比对手更会操纵，从而为自己争取到了最好的条件。

例句：She was adept at outmanipulating her rivals, always staying one step ahead in the game of power. 她擅长通过操纵胜过对手，在权力游戏中总是领先一步。

近义词：outmaneuvering, outsmarting, outwitting
反义词：being manipulated, being outplayed
词性：Verb (present participle)
