industrializations:/ɪnˌdʌstriələˈzeɪʃənz/| n.|工业化（复数）|The process of transforming an agrarian society into an industrial one, through the development of manufacturing and other industries.|The rapid industrializations of several Asian countries have led to significant economic growth.  亚洲几个国家的快速工业化带来了显著的经济增长。|近义词：industrial development, modernization|反义词:deindustrialization, ruralization|词性:名词

Etymology: From industrialize + -ation.  Industrialize is from industrial + -ize. Industrial is from industry + -al. Industry comes from Old French industrie "activity, diligence," from Latin industria "diligence, activity," from industrius "diligent, active," from indo "in, within" + struere "to build."
