nonredemptive:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈdɛmp.tɪv/
adjective
没有挽回余地的；无益的；不能救赎的
Not serving to redeem or improve someone or something; offering no hope of improvement or relief.
The film offered a bleak and nonredemptive portrayal of inner-city life. 这部电影对市中心的生活进行了惨淡而无益的刻画。
近义词:hopeless, irredeemable, damning
反义词:redemptive, hopeful, positive
词性:adjective
