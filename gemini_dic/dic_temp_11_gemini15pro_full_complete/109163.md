legitimateness:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmɪtnəs/| n. |合法性；正统性|The quality or state of being legitimate; lawfulness. | The legitimacy of the election results was questioned. 选举结果的合法性受到了质疑。|synonyms: validity, lawfulness, legality, rightfulness|antonyms: illegality, illegitimacy, unlawfulness|noun

legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/| adj. |合法的；正当的；合理的|Conforming to the law or to rules. |He had a legitimate reason for being late. 他迟到是有正当理由的。|synonyms: legal, lawful, valid, justifiable|antonyms: illegal, unlawful, invalid, illegitimate|adjective
