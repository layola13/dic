PHRASE->
(Since '-itively' is a suffix, it doesn't form phrases on its own.  Here are some examples of words ending in '-itively' used in phrases)

Think intuitively = 直觉思考
Act decisively = 果断行动
Respond positively = 积极回应
Work collaboratively = 合作工作
Compete competitively = 竞争激烈


注意事项:

This suffix derives from the Latin "-ivus," which forms adjectives, and the English adverbial suffix "-ly."
