completed:/kəmˈpliːtɪd/ /kəmˈpliːtəd/ | adj. | 完成的，已完成的 |  finished, accomplished; having all parts or elements.  Derived from the verb "complete," meaning to finish or make whole. From the Latin "completus," past participle of "complere," meaning "to fill up." | I have completed my homework. 我已经完成了我的作业。 | finished, done, accomplished, fulfilled | incomplete, unfinished, pending, outstanding | adjective

completed:/kəmˈpliːtɪd/ /kəmˈpliːtəd/ | v. | 完成 | past tense and past participle of the verb "complete" |  He completed the race in record time. 他以创纪录的时间完成了比赛。| finish, conclude, fulfill, achieve | begin, start, commence, initiate | verb
