capitalised:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzd/| adj.| 大写的；资本化的 |  Converted to capital letters; having capital letters.  Having sufficient capital to operate. |  The first letter of every sentence should be capitalised.  每个句子的首字母都应该大写。 The business is adequately capitalised.  这家企业有充足的运营资本。 |  uppercase, upper-case | lowercase, lower-case | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb *capitalise*, from *capital* + *-ise*.
