hand-to-hand:/hæŋd tʊ hæŋd/ | adj. | 肉搏的，近身搏斗的 | involving fighting in which people use their hands or weapons held in their hands, rather than guns or bombs | The two knights engaged in hand-to-hand combat.  两个骑士进行了肉搏战。 | close-quarters, close combat | ranged | adjective

hand-to-hand:/hæŋd tʊ hæŋd/ | adv. | 肉搏地，近身地 | in a way that involves fighting in which people use their hands or weapons held in their hands, rather than guns or bombs |  They fought hand-to-hand for hours. 他们进行了数小时的肉搏战。| at close quarters, in close combat | remotely | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From hand + to + hand, literally meaning "hand touching hand," emphasizing the close proximity of combat.
