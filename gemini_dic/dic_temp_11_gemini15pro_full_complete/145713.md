overcovetousness:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌvɪtəsnəs/
n.
过度贪婪，贪得无厌
Excessive greed; an insatiable desire for more than one needs or deserves.
He was consumed by overcovetousness, always wanting more money and power.  他被过度贪婪所吞噬，总是想要更多的金钱和权力。
The sermon warned against the sin of overcovetousness.  布道警告人们要提防贪得无厌的罪恶。
近义词: greed, avarice, cupidity, rapacity
反义词: generosity, contentment, satisfaction
词性: 名词
