overestimates:/ˌoʊvərˈɛstɪmeɪts/| v.|高估，过高估计|To estimate something as being larger, greater, better, or more important than it really is.|He tends to overestimate his own abilities. 他往往高估自己的能力。|overvalues, exaggerates|underestimates|verb
overestimate:/ˌoʊvərˈɛstɪmeɪt/| n.|高估，过高的估计|An estimate that is too high or optimistic.|The government's overestimate of tax revenue caused a budget deficit. 政府对税收的过高估计导致了预算赤字。|overvaluation, exaggeration|underestimate|noun
