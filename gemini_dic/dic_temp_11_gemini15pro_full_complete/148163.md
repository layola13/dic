overserve:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrv/| vt.|过度服务；过量供应|To provide more service or goods than are needed or wanted; especially to serve too much alcohol to someone.|The bartender overserved the customer, who later caused a disturbance. 酒保给顾客提供的酒过量了，后来顾客引起了骚乱。|近义词：overindulge, ply|反义词：underserve, skimp|词性:动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "serve" (verb meaning "to provide service or goods")
