contributory:/kənˈtrɪbjəˌtɔːri/ /kənˈtrɪbjəˌtəri/
contributory: adj.| 贡献的，起作用的；捐助的 |Playing video games is contributory to his poor eyesight. 玩电子游戏导致他视力下降。|近义词: causative, instrumental, conducive |反义词: hindering, obstructive |词性:形容词
contributory:/kənˈtrɪbjəˌtɔːri/ /kənˈtrɪbjəˌtəri/
contributory: n.| 捐助者，贡献者|He was a major contributory to the charity. 他是这个慈善机构的主要捐助者。 |近义词: donor, contributor |反义词: recipient, beneficiary |词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *contributorius, from Latin contribuere "to contribute," from com- "together" + tribuere "to grant, bestow."
