jangler:/ˈdʒæŋɡlər/| n.|喋喋不休的人；发出刺耳声音的东西|A person who talks incessantly; something that makes a harsh, discordant sound.|The jangler on the train made it impossible to concentrate. 火车上喋喋不休的人让我无法集中注意力。|近义词：chatterbox, gossipmonger, windbag|反义词：quiet person, reticent person|noun

jangler:/ˈdʒæŋɡlər/| n.|发出叮当声的东西|Something that makes a jingling or clanging sound.|The jangler of keys in his pocket was annoying. 他口袋里钥匙的叮当声很烦人。|近义词：rattler, clinker|反义词: silent object|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *janglen*, frequentative of *jangle*, of uncertain origin. Related to Old French *jangler* (to chatter, prattle).
