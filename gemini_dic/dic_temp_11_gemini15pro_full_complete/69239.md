feasiblenesses:/ˌfiːzəˈbɪlətɪz/
n.
可行性，可行性方案
the state or degree of being easily or conveniently done
The feasiblenesses of the various proposals were carefully considered.  对各项提议的可行性进行了仔细的考虑。

近义词: possibilities, practicabilities
反义词: impossibilities, impracticalities
词性: noun (plural)


词源:  From feasible + -ness. Feasible comes from Old French faisable "doable," from faire "to do," from Latin facere "to make, do."
