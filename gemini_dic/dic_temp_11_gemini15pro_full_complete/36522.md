colitis:/kəˈlaɪtɪs/ /koʊˈlaɪtɪs/
n.
结肠炎
Inflammation of the colon, the longest part of the large intestine.
He suffers from ulcerative colitis. 他患有溃疡性结肠炎。

colitis:/kəˈlaɪtɪs/ /koʊˈlaɪtɪs/
noun
结肠炎
Inflammation of the colon.
She was diagnosed with colitis after experiencing abdominal pain and diarrhea. 她经历了腹痛和腹泻后被诊断出患有结肠炎。

colitis:/kəˈlaɪtɪs/ /koʊˈlaɪtɪs/
noun
结肠炎
A medical condition characterized by inflammation of the lining of the colon.
The doctor prescribed medication to treat his colitis. 医生开了药来治疗他的结肠炎。


近义词:inflammation of the colon, colon inflammation
反义词:None (Colitis is a medical condition, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
