effervesce:/ˌɛfərˈvɛs/| v. |冒泡，兴奋，活跃|To give off bubbles of gas; to be lively or enthusiastic.|The champagne effervesced in the glass. 香槟在杯子里冒泡泡。|近义词：fizz, bubble, sparkle, froth, foam, exhilarate|反义词:flat, still, dull|verb

effervescence:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsəns/| n. |冒泡，兴奋，活跃|The process of giving off bubbles of gas; liveliness or enthusiasm.|The effervescence of the crowd was contagious. 人群的兴奋情绪很有感染力。|近义词：fizz, sparkle, exuberance, ebullience|反义词:flatness, dullness|noun

effervescent:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsənt/| adj. |冒泡的，兴奋的，活跃的|Giving off bubbles of gas; lively or enthusiastic.|She has an effervescent personality. 她性格活泼。|近义词：bubbly, sparkling, lively, vivacious|反义词:flat, dull, lifeless|adjective
