hyphen's:/ˈhaɪfənz/ |n. |连字符; 分字符|A punctuation mark (-) used to join words or part of words, or to divide a word at the end of a line.|例句：Use a hyphen to join compound adjectives. 使用连字符连接复合形容词。|例句：The word "e-mail" contains a hyphen.  单词“电子邮件”包含一个连字符。|近义词: dash |反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
