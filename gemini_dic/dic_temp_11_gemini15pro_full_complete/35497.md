coadjust:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒʌst/
verb
共同调整，相互调节
to adjust or regulate (something) in relation to something else; to cause (two or more things) to be in proper relation to each other.
The gears need to be coadjusted for the machine to work smoothly.  这些齿轮需要相互调整才能使机器平稳运行。
The therapist helped the couple coadjust to their new roles as parents.  治疗师帮助这对夫妇适应他们作为父母的新角色。
coordinate, harmonize, adapt, reconcile
--
verb
词源：co- (together) + adjust (to put in order)
来源于拉丁语 ad- (to) + justus (right, just).  "Co-" 表示“共同，一起”。
