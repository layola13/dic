banshees:/ˈbænʃiz/ (US), /ˈbænʃiːz/ (UK)| n.|女妖，报丧女妖|A banshee is a female spirit in Irish mythology who heralds the death of a family member, usually by wailing or shrieking.|例句：Legend has it that the banshee's cries foretold the death of the chieftain.  传说女妖的哭声预示着酋长的死亡。

例句：In some stories, the banshee appears as a beautiful woman, while in others, she is a hag. 在一些故事中，女妖以美丽的女子形象出现，而在另一些故事中，她是一个老巫婆。|近义词：spirit, wraith, phantom|反义词: (No direct antonym for a supernatural being.  Could consider "living being" as a loose contrast.)|词性:noun
