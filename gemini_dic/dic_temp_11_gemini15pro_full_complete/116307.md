mastaba:/ˈmæstəbə/ /ˈmɑːstɑːbɑː/ | n. | 古埃及长方形平顶陵墓 | An ancient Egyptian mud-brick tomb with a rectangular base and sloping sides, resembling a truncated pyramid. | Example 1:  Archaeologists discovered a well-preserved mastaba containing the remains of a high-ranking official.  考古学家发现了一座保存完好的平顶墓，里面有一位高级官员的遗骸。

Example 2: The mastaba is considered a precursor to the later, more elaborate pyramids. 平顶墓被认为是后来更精致的金字塔的前身。 | Near Synonyms: tomb, sepulcher, burial chamber | Antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a type of tomb) | noun
