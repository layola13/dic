nonusurping:/ˌnɒnjuːˈzɜːpɪŋ/
adjective
非篡夺的，不僭越的
Not usurping; not seizing or holding by force or without right.
The nonusurping government was welcomed by the people.  这个不篡位的政府受到了人民的欢迎。
The king maintained a nonusurping stance regarding the church's authority. 国王在教会权威方面保持着不僭越的立场。
近义词: legitimate, rightful, lawful
反义词: usurping, seizing, appropriating
词性: adjective
