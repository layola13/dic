overempirical: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈpɪrɪkəl/ | adj. | 过于经验主义的；过度依赖经验的 | Relying too heavily on observation and experimentation, often at the expense of theory or deduction. | His approach to the problem was deemed overempirical, neglecting established theoretical frameworks. 他解决问题的方法被认为过于经验主义，忽略了既定的理论框架。 | The study's overempirical methodology drew criticism for its lack of conceptual grounding. 这项研究过度依赖经验的方法因缺乏概念基础而受到批评。 | 近义词: overly empirical, excessively empirical | 反义词: theoretical, deductive | 词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "empirical" (from Greek "empeiria" meaning "experience").
