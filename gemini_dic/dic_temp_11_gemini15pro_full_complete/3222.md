affectionless:/əˈfekʃənləs/
adjective
无情的，冷漠的，缺乏感情的
Lacking affection; unloving or uncaring.
She seemed cold and affectionless towards her own child. 她似乎对自己的孩子冷漠无情。

近义词:cold, uncaring, indifferent, apathetic, heartless, unfeeling, callous, insensitive
反义词:affectionate, loving, caring, warm, tender, compassionate, empathetic

词性:adjective
