imperturbability:/ˌɪmpərˌtɜːrbəˈbɪləti/
n.
沉着；冷静；泰然自若
The state of being calm and not easily upset or worried.
He faced the crisis with remarkable imperturbability.  他以非凡的沉着面对危机。

近义词: calmness, composure, serenity, tranquility, equanimity
反义词: anxiety, nervousness, agitation, distress, excitability
词性: noun

imperturbable:/ˌɪmpərˈtɜːrbəbəl/
adj.
沉着的；冷静的；泰然自若的
Unable to be upset or excited; calm.
She remained imperturbable throughout the crisis.  在整个危机期间，她保持镇静。

近义词: calm, composed, serene, tranquil, unflappable
反义词: anxious, nervous, agitated, distressed, excitable
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin imperturbabilis, from in- ‘not’ + perturbare ‘to disturb’ (from per- ‘through, thoroughly’ + turbare ‘to agitate’).
