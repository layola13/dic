crowdsources:/ˈkraʊdsɔːrsɪz/
verb
众包，群体外包
To obtain (information or input into a particular task or project) by enlisting the services of a number of people, either paid or unpaid, typically via the Internet.
The company crowdsources its product development. 这家公司将其产品开发众包。
近义词: crowdsource, outsource
反义词: insource


crowdsourced:/ˈkraʊdsɔːrst/
adjective
众包的
Obtained or produced using crowdsourcing.
The project used crowdsourced data. 这个项目使用了众包数据。
近义词: outsourced
反义词
