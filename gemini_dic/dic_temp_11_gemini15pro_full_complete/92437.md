hypoparathyroidism:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌpærəˈθaɪrɔɪdɪzəm/
|n.|甲状旁腺功能减退症|A condition in which the parathyroid glands do not produce enough parathyroid hormone, leading to low calcium levels in the blood.|She was diagnosed with hypoparathyroidism after experiencing muscle cramps and tingling.|近义词: None (this is a specific medical term)|反义词: hyperparathyroidism|词性: noun


hypoparathyroid:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌpærəˈθaɪrɔɪd/
|adj.|甲状旁腺功能减退的|Relating to or characterized by hypoparathyroidism.|Hypoparathyroid patients need calcium supplements.|近义词: None (this is a specific medical term)|反义词: hyperparathyroid|词性: adjective

词源:  "hypo-" (Greek: under, deficient) + "para-" (Greek: beside, near) + "thyroid" (Greek: shield-shaped, referring to the thyroid cartilage)
