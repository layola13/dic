broadcasting:/ˈbrɔːdkɑːstɪŋ/ | n. | 广播；播送 | the transmission of radio or television programs to a large audience | The broadcasting of the news reached millions of people.  新闻广播覆盖了数百万人。| synonyms: transmission, airing, diffusion | antonyms: receiving, suppression | noun

broadcasting:/ˈbrɔːdkɑːstɪŋ/ | adj. | 广播的；播送的 | relating to the transmission of radio or television programs | The broadcasting company is known for its unbiased news. 这家广播公司以其公正的新闻而闻名。| synonyms: transmitting, airing | antonyms: receiving | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "broadcast," which originally referred to scattering seeds widely in a field. The term was later adopted for the widespread dissemination of information via radio waves.
