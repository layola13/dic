inoperativeness:/ɪnˌɒpərətɪvnəs/ | n. |失效；不起作用；无效|The inoperativeness of the old law led to chaos. 旧法律失效导致了混乱。|The machine's inoperativeness was due to a faulty power supply.  这台机器的失效是由于电源故障造成的。|Synonyms: inefficacy, uselessness, invalidity, dysfunction, failure |Antonyms: effectiveness, functionality, validity, operation|noun


inoperative:/ɪnˈɒpərətɪv/ | adj. |无效的；不起作用的；不运行的|The contract is now inoperative due to a breach of terms. 由于违反条款，该合同现在无效。| The old heating system is inoperative. 旧的供暖系统不起作用了。|Synonyms: ineffective, useless, invalid, dysfunctional, defunct |Antonyms: effective, functional, valid, operational, working|adjective
