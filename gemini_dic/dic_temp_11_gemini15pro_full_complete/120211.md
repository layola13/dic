mineralogist:/ˌmɪnəˈrɑːlədʒɪst/
n.|矿物学家|A scientist who studies minerals, their composition, properties, occurrence, and uses.|The mineralogist identified the rock as quartz.  这位矿物学家将这块岩石鉴定为石英。|近义词:geologist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Modern Latin *mineralogicus*, from *mineralo-*, from Late Latin *minera* ("mine, ore") + *-logicus* ("-logical"), from Ancient Greek *-λογικος* (*-logikos*), from *λογος* (*logos*, "word, reason").
