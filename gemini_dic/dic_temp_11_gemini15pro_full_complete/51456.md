detonate:/ˈdɛtəneɪt/ | v. | 爆炸，引爆 | To cause (something, such as a bomb) to explode. | The terrorists detonated the bomb in a crowded market. 恐怖分子在拥挤的市场引爆了炸弹。 | explode, blow up | defuse, disarm | verb
detonated:/ˈdɛtəneɪtɪd/ | v. | （使）爆炸，（使）引爆（detonate的过去式和过去分词）|  Having been caused to explode. | The bomb detonated prematurely. 炸弹过早爆炸了。| exploded, blown up | defused, disarmed | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin detonare "to thunder down," from de- "down" + tonare "to thunder."
