iniquitousness:/ɪˈnɪkwɪtəsnəs/| n. |邪恶; 不义; 罪恶|The state of being iniquitous; wickedness, sinfulness.  It refers to a high degree of immorality or injustice.|The iniquitousness of the dictator's regime was evident in the widespread human rights abuses.  这个独裁者政权的邪恶体现在普遍存在的侵犯人权行为上。|近义词：wickedness, evil, sinfulness, depravity, immorality|反义词：righteousness, goodness, virtue, morality|词性:noun

iniquitous:/ɪˈnɪkwɪtəs/| adj. |邪恶的; 不义的; 罪恶的|Characterized by injustice or wickedness; sinful.|The iniquitous law discriminated against minorities. 这项邪恶的法律歧视少数族裔。|近义词：wicked, evil, sinful, immoral, unjust|反义词：righteous, good, virtuous, moral, just|词性:adjective
