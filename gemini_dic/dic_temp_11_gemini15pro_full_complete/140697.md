omizu:/oʊˈmiːzuː/ | n. | 水 | Japanese word for "water". It is borrowed from Chinese. | 例句：Could you please give me some omizu?  请给我一些水好吗？

例句：In Japan, omizu is often served with meals. 在日本，用餐时通常会提供水。| 近义词：water, mizu (Japanese) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for water, could consider concepts like "dehydration" or "arid") | 词性: noun
