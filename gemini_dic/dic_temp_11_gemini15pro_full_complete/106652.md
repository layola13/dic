kurta:/ˈkɜːrtə/ (UK), /ˈkʊrtə/ (US)| n.|库尔塔，一种及膝或及踝的长衫|A loose collarless shirt worn traditionally by men in South Asia.|He wore a white kurta and pajama pants. 他穿着一件白色库尔塔和睡裤。|Synonyms: tunic, shirt|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western-style shirt)|noun
