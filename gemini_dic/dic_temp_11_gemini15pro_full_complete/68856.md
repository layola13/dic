fashionistas:/ˌfæʃəˈnɪstəz/
n.
时尚达人，时尚人士
A person who is highly interested in fashion; a stylish and trendsetting person.
The fashionistas flocked to the new boutique to see the latest designs.  时尚达人们蜂拥至新开的精品店，想一睹最新的设计。
The fashionistas were all wearing the latest trends from Paris.  时尚达人们都穿着来自巴黎的最新潮流服饰。
近义词:trendsetters, style icons
反义词:
词性:noun
