kaolins: /ˈkeɪəlɪnz/ | n. | 高岭土;瓷土; 白陶土 | A fine, usually white clay formed by the weathering of aluminous minerals (especially feldspars). It is used in making porcelain and china, as a filler in paper and textiles, and in medicines and cosmetics. |  Kaolins are used extensively in the ceramics industry.  高岭土广泛用于陶瓷工业。

Kaolins are an important ingredient in many types of paper.  高岭土是许多类型纸张中的重要成分。 | 近义词: china clay, kaolin | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of clay) | 词性: noun
