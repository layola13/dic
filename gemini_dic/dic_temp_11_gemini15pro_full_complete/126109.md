mutative:/ˈmjuːtətɪv/| adj.|可变的，变化的|Relating to or causing mutation or change.  Having the power to change or mutate something.|The mutative power of radiation can cause cancer. 辐射的变异能力会导致癌症。|Synonyms: changeable, variable, mutable, alterable|Antonyms: immutable, unchangeable, fixed, stable|Adjective
