bridleless:/ˈbraɪdl̩ləs/| adj.|无辔头的，不用缰绳的|Without a bridle; uncontrolled, unrestrained.|The horse ran bridleless across the field. 那匹马没有辔头地跑过田野。|近义词：unbridled, unrestrained, uncontrolled|反义词：bridled, restrained, controlled|词性:形容词
Etymology: From bridle + -less.  Bridle comes from Old English *bridel, from Proto-Germanic *bridil-.
