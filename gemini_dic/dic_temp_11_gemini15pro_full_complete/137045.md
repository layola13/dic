nonstickier:/ˌnɑːnˈstɪkiər/ | adj. | 不那么粘的；更不粘的 | Less sticky; having a reduced tendency to adhere. | This new pan is nonstickier than my old one.  这个新锅比我的旧锅更不粘。 | The coating makes the surface nonstickier.  这种涂层使表面更不粘。 | 近义词：less adhesive, less sticky | 反义词：stickier, more adhesive | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix) + sticky + -er (comparative suffix). "Non-" negates the adjective "sticky," and "-er" forms the comparative degree. "Sticky" comes from Old English *sticig.
