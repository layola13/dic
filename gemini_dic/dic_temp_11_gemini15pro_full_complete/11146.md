archeologist:/ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒɪst/ (UK) /ˌɑːrkiˈɑːlədʒɪst/ (US)| n.|考古学家|A person who studies human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.|例句：The archeologist carefully unearthed a pottery shard. 考古学家小心翼翼地挖出一个陶器碎片。

|例句：The archeologist specialized in the Bronze Age. 这位考古学家专攻青铜器时代。|近义词：antiquarian, paleoanthropologist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
