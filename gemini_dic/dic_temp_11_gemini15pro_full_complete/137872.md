nonvirulently:/ˌnɒnˈvɪrʊləntli/
adverb
无毒地；非毒性地
in a nonvirulent manner; without virulence.  The bacteria was cultured nonvirulently in the lab.
例句：The strain of bacteria was modified to behave nonvirulently.  这种细菌的菌株被改造得无毒。

例句：The virus was engineered to replicate nonvirulently.  该病毒被设计成无毒复制。

近义词：harmlessly, innocuously, safely
反义词：virulently, toxically, harmfully
词性：adverb

词源：
From non- + virulent + -ly.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not". Virulent comes from Latin virulentus "poisonous," from virus "poison".  -ly is a suffix forming adverbs.
