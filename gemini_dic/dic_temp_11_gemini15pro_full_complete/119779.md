miglio:/ˈmiʎːo/ (Italian)| n.|英里，里程|A unit of distance equal to 1.609 kilometers (approximately 0.62 miles). It's derived from the Roman *mille passus*, meaning "thousand paces."|例句：Abbiamo percorso dieci miglia.  We traveled ten miles.


|近义词：chilometro (kilometer)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
