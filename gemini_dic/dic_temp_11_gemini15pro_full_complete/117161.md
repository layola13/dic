means:/miːnz/
n. (plural means)
方法，手段，工具
A method, course of action, or instrument by which something can be accomplished.
例句：
He achieved his goal by various means.
他通过各种方法实现了目标。

The ends justify the means.
只要目的正当，可以不择手段。

近义词：method, way, manner, approach, mode, process, procedure, technique, instrument, tool
反义词: block, hindrance, impediment, obstacle

v. (third-person singular simple present means, present participle meaning, simple past and past participle meant /ment/)
意思是，意味着
To have a particular meaning.
例句：
What does this word mean?
这个词是什么意思？

This means a lot to me.
这对我意义重大。

近义词：signify, indicate, denote, represent, express, convey, imply, suggest
反义词:


词性: 名词，动词
