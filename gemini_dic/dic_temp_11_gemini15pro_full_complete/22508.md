boomeranged:/ˈbuːməræŋd/ | v. | 回旋，产生事与愿违的结果 |  The criticism he made boomeranged on him. 他提出的批评反而落到了自己头上。 | Example:  His attempt to discredit his rival boomeranged and ended up hurting his own reputation. 他试图败坏对手名声的举动适得其反，最终损害了他自己的声誉。

Example: The prank they played on their teacher boomeranged when they ended up getting detention. 他们对老师玩的恶作剧反而让他们自己被留校了。 | Synonyms: backfire, rebound, recoil | Antonyms: succeed, prevail | Verb
