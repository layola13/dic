inflameable:/ɪnˈfleɪməbl/| adj.|易燃的，可燃的|Easily set on fire; flammable.  While technically correct, "inflammable" can be easily confused with "non-flammable," so "flammable" is the preferred term. |例句：The material is highly inflameable, so keep it away from open flames.  这种材料非常易燃，所以要远离明火。

例句：The inflameable liquid was stored in a special container.  易燃液体存放在一个特殊的容器里。|近义词：flammable, combustible|反义词:non-flammable, fire-resistant|词性:adjective
