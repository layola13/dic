all-or-nothing:/ˌɔːl ɔːr ˈnʌθɪŋ/| adj.|孤注一掷的；要么全赢要么全输的|Characterized by a situation where one either achieves complete success or total failure, with no intermediate outcome.|He made an all-or-nothing gamble on the stock market. 他在股市上孤注一掷。|近义词：do-or-die, high-stakes, desperate|反义词: cautious, moderate, measured|词性:adjective
all-or-nothing:/ˌɔːl ɔːr ˈnʌθɪŋ/| n.|孤注一掷的局面；要么全赢要么全输的局面|A situation where only complete success or total failure is possible.|It's an all-or-nothing situation; we either win the championship or we go home.  这是要么全赢要么全输的局面；我们要么赢得冠军，要么回家。|近义词：do-or-die situation, high-stakes gamble|反义词: compromise, middle ground|词性:noun
