non-realist:/ˌnɒnˈriːəlɪst/ | adj. | 非现实主义的；非写实主义的 | Not adhering to or representing realism, especially in art or literature. Characterized by a preference for the imaginative or fantastic. |  His paintings are known for their non-realist style, incorporating elements of fantasy and dreamlike imagery. 他的绘画以其非现实主义风格而闻名，融合了幻想和梦境般的意象。

The non-realist writer often explored themes of magic and mythology.  这位非现实主义作家经常探索魔法和神话的主题。 | 近义词: unrealistic, fantastical, surreal, idealistic | 反义词: realist, realistic, naturalistic | 词性: 形容词
