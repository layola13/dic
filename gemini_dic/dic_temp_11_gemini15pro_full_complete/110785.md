linigerous:/lɪˈnɪdʒərəs/
adjective
产亚麻的；亚麻似的
bearing or producing flax; resembling flax
The linigerous fields swayed gently in the breeze.  亚麻田在微风中轻轻摇曳。
Flax is a linigerous plant cultivated for its fiber. 亚麻是一种为了获取纤维而种植的产亚麻植物。
近义词:flaxen, linen
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *liniger, from linum "flax" + -ger "bearing, producing."
