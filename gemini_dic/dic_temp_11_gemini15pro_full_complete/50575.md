deracination:/diːˌræsɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
连根拔起；根除；流离失所
the process of being uprooted or displaced, either literally from one's native land or figuratively from one's customs, beliefs, or traditions.
The deracination he felt after leaving his homeland was profound.  离开家乡后，他感到深深的流离失所感。
displacement, uprooting, exile, alienation
establishment, rootedness, integration
noun
