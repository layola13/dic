interruptory:/ˌɪntəˈrʌptəri/ | adj. | 打断的，中断的 | Tending to or serving to interrupt; interrupting.  Breaking the continuity of something. |  His interruptory cough broke the silence. 他的打断性咳嗽打破了沉默。| 近义词: disruptive, interruptive, intrusive | 反义词: continuous, uninterrupted | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *interruptorius, from Latin interruptus, past participle of interrumpere "to break off, interrupt" (from inter- "between" + rumpere "to break").
