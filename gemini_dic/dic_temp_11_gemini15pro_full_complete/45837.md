customizes:/ˈkʌstəˌmaɪzɪz/
verb
定制，个性化
modifies something to make it better suited to a particular individual or purpose.
The company customizes its software to meet each client's specific needs.  这家公司根据每个客户的特定需求定制其软件。
personalizes, adapts, modifies, tailors, adjusts
standardizes, generalizes
词性: 动词
