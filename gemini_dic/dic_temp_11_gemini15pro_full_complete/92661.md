ibsenism:/ˈɪbsənɪzəm/ | n. | 伊卜生主义 | The literary style and philosophical outlook of Henrik Ibsen, particularly his focus on social problems and the psychological complexities of individuals.  |  Example: The play's critique of societal hypocrisy is a clear example of Ibsenism.  这出戏对社会虚伪的批判是伊卜生主义的一个明显例子。 | Example: Ibsenism influenced many subsequent playwrights and continues to resonate with modern audiences.  伊卜生主义影响了许多后来的剧作家，并继续与现代观众产生共鸣。 | Synonym: realism, naturalism, social commentary | Antonym: romanticism, escapism | noun
