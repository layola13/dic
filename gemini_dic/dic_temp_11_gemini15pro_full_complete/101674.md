irreligion:/ˌɪrɪˈlɪdʒən/
n.
lack of religion; absence of religious belief
The survey found a surprising amount of irreligion among young people. 这项调查发现在年轻人中存在着数量惊人的非宗教信仰。
近义词: atheism, nonbelief, secularism, godlessness
反义词: religion, piety, devoutness
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *irreligioun*, from Late Latin *irreligiō*, from Latin *in-*, not + *religiō*, religion.
