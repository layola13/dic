burn the candle at both ends:/bɜːrn ðə ˈkændl ət boʊθ ɛndz/
idiom
过度劳累，消耗精力
to work or do other things from early in the morning until late at night and so get very little rest
He's been burning the candle at both ends trying to finish this project. 他为了完成这个项目一直拼命工作，日夜操劳。
overwork, exhaust oneself
relax, rest
