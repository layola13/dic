giveaway:/ˈɡɪvəˌweɪ/ (US), /ˈɡɪvəˌweɪ/ (UK)| n. |赠品；泄露；暴露；不经意泄露的真相|Something that is given away free, especially for promotional purposes; something that reveals a secret or information unintentionally.|例句：The radio station was having a giveaway.  电台正在进行赠品活动。

例句：His nervous laugh was a dead giveaway. 他紧张的笑声完全暴露了他的秘密。|近义词：prize, gift, freebie, revelation, indicator|反义词：purchase, secret, concealment|词性:noun


giveaway:/ɡɪv əˈweɪ/ (US), /ɡɪv əˈweɪ/ (UK)| v. (phrasal verb - give something away)|泄露（秘密）；赠送；分发；放弃；暴露|To reveal something unintentionally; to give something to someone else for free.|例句：He gave away the ending of the movie. 他剧透了电影的结局。

例句：They're giving away free samples of ice cream. 他们正在免费分发冰淇淋样品。|近义词：reveal, divulge, betray, donate, distribute|反义词：conceal, hide, keep, purchase|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrasal verb "give away," composed of the verb "give" (Old English "giefan," from Proto-Germanic "*geban," related to Old Norse "gefa") and the adverb "away" (Old English "onweg," from "on" (on) and "weg" (way)).
