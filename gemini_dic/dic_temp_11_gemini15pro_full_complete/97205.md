inflammably:/ɪnˈflæməbli/ | adv. | 易燃地，可燃地 | Easily set on fire; readily ignited.  |  The material is inflammably and should be stored carefully.  这种材料易燃，应该小心存放。 | Synonyms: combustibly, ignitably | Antonyms: nonflammably, non-combustibly | Word Type: Adverb

Etymology:
From "inflammable" + "-ly". "Inflammable" comes from Late Latin "inflammabilis", from "inflammare" (to set on fire), from "in-" (in, into) + "flammare" (to flame), from "flamma" (flame).
