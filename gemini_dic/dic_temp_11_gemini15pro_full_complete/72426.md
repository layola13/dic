flugelman:/ˈfluːɡ(ə)lmən/| n.|领队；前导；指挥|A soldier who formerly acted as a guide or director of movements, especially in drill.  A leader, especially one who sets an example for others to follow.|The flugelman signaled the start of the march. 领队发出开始行军的信号。|Synonyms: leader, director, conductor, guide, pacesetter |Antonyms: follower, subordinate|noun
