eutychianism: /juːˈtɪkiənɪzəm/ | n. |  优提克斯主义; 一性论 |  A Christological heresy arising in the 5th century, asserting that the human nature of Christ was absorbed by his divine nature, so that Christ had only one nature after the Incarnation. |  Eutychianism was condemned at the Council of Chalcedon.  优提克斯主义在迦克墩会议上被谴责。|  Monophysitism |  Dyophysitism | noun

eutychian: /juːˈtɪkiən/ | adj. n. |  优提克斯派的；优提克斯派信徒 |  Relating to or supporting Eutychianism; a follower of Eutychianism. |  He was accused of holding Eutychian views. 他被指控持有优提克斯派的观点。|  Monophysite |  Dyophysite | adjective, noun
