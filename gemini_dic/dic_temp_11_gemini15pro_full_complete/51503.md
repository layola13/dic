detrain:/diːˈtreɪn/ | v. |下火车；（使）离开火车|To leave a train; to cause (passengers or goods) to leave a train.|The passengers detrained at the next station.  乘客们在下一站下车。| The soldiers were detrained near the front lines. 士兵们在靠近前线的地方下火车。|synonyms: disembark, alight|antonyms: entrain, board|verb

detrained:/diːˈtreɪn/ | adj. |（货物）已卸下火车的|Having left a train; unloaded from a train.|The detrained cargo was then transported by truck. 已卸下火车的货物随后用卡车运输。|The detrained soldiers quickly assembled. 下了火车的士兵们迅速集合。|synonyms: unloaded, offloaded|antonyms: loaded, onboard|adjective
