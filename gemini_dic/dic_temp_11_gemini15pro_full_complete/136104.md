nonrecurring:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈkɜːrɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːn.rɪˈkɜːrɪŋ/
adjective
非重复发生的，一次性的
not recurring; happening only once
The company offered a nonrecurring bonus to its employees. 公司向员工提供了一次性奖金。
synonyms: one-time, single, isolated, occasional
antonyms: recurring, repeated, regular, frequent
词性: 形容词
