fugally:/ˈfjuːɡəli/| adv.|俭朴地，节俭地|In a frugal manner; sparingly.  Characterized by or reflecting economy in the expenditure of resources.|She lived fugally, rarely indulging in luxuries. 她生活简朴，很少沉迷于奢侈品。|Near Synonyms: economically, thriftily, sparingly, modestly|Near Antonyms: extravagantly, lavishly, profusely, wastefully|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frugalis*, from *frugi* ("useful, thrifty, proper, temperate"), ultimately from *frux* ("fruit, profit, value").
