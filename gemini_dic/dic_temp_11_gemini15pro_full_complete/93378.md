illiberalism:/ɪˈlɪbərəlɪzəm/
n.
不自由主义；非自由主义
A political ideology that rejects liberal principles, such as individual rights, freedom of speech, and democracy.
The rise of illiberalism is a threat to democracy around the world.  世界各地不自由主义的兴起是对民主的威胁。
The government's illiberal policies have been met with widespread criticism.  政府的不自由政策遭到了广泛批评。
近义词：authoritarianism, despotism, tyranny
反义词：liberalism, democracy
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From il- (not) + liberal + -ism (doctrine).  源于il-（不）+ liberal + -ism（主义）
