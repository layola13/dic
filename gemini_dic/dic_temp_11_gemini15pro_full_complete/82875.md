gullwing:/ˈɡʌlˌwɪŋ/ (US), /ˈɡʌlˌwɪŋ/ (UK)| n. adj.|鸥翼；鸥翼式的|A gullwing door is a type of car door that is hinged at the roof rather than the side, so that it opens upwards and outwards, resembling the wings of a gull in flight. As an adjective, it describes something resembling or having such doors.|The classic Mercedes 300SL is famous for its gullwing doors. 这款经典的梅赛德斯300SL因其鸥翼门而闻名。|The DeLorean DMC-12 sports iconic gullwing doors. 德劳瑞恩DMC-12拥有标志性的鸥翼门。|近义词：butterfly doors, scissor doors|反义词: conventional doors|词性: noun, adjective
