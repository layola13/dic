expenditor:/ɪkˈspɛndɪtər/
n.|支出者；分配者；经费管理员|A person who authorizes or approves expenditures; someone who disburses or manages funds.|The expeditor reviewed and approved all invoices before payment.  支出者在付款前审查并批准了所有发票。|Synonyms: disburser, controller, administrator|Antonyms: saver, hoarder|noun
