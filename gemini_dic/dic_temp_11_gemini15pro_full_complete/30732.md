cervixes: /ˈsɜːrvɪsiːz/
noun
子宫颈（cervix 的复数）
The plural form of cervix, referring to the lower, narrow end of the uterus.
During childbirth, the cervixes of the women dilated. 分娩期间，这些妇女的子宫颈扩张了。
The doctor examined the patients' cervixes. 医生检查了病人的子宫颈。
近义词:  (for singular) cervix, neck of the womb
反义词:  None (anatomical term)
词性: noun (plural)
