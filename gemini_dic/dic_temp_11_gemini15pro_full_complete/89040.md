holoblast:/ˈhɒləˌblæst/ /ˈhoʊləˌblæst/ | n. |全裂卵,全裂型卵裂| An egg whose cleavage is holoblastic, meaning the entire egg divides into smaller cells, as opposed to meroblastic cleavage where only part of the egg divides. |The holoblast is characteristic of many mammals. 全裂卵是许多哺乳动物的特征。|  The sea urchin exhibits holoblastic cleavage during development. 海胆在发育过程中表现出全裂卵裂。|近义词:  complete cleavage|反义词: meroblast, partial cleavage|词性:noun
