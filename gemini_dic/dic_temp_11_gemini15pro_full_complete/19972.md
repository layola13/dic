biofeedbacks:/ˌbaɪoʊˈfiːdbæks/
|n.|生物反馈|The process of gaining greater awareness of many physiological functions primarily using instruments that provide information on the activity of those same systems, with a goal of being able to manipulate them at will.  通过使用主要提供这些系统活动信息的仪器，来获得对许多生理功能的更强意识的过程，目的是能够随意操纵它们。|She uses biofeedbacks to control her migraines. 她使用生物反馈来控制偏头痛。|synonyms: biofeedback (often used in singular form), physiological feedback|antonyms: None (lack of a widely accepted direct antonym)|noun
