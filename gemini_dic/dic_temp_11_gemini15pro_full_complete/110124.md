liddell:/ˈlɪdəl/| n.|利德尔|A surname, most famously associated with Henry George Liddell, a lexicographer known for "A Greek-English Lexicon," often referred to as "Liddell and Scott."  It's also the name of Alice Liddell, the girl who inspired Lewis Carroll's "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland."|Lewis Carroll wrote "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland" for Alice Liddell.|近义词: (For the lexicon)  LSJ (abbreviation), Greek-English dictionary|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun
