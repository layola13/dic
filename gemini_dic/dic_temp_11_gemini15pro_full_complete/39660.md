conservatism:/kənˈsɜːrvətɪzəm/| n.|保守主义；守旧性|A political or theological orientation advocating the preservation of the best in society and opposing radical changes.|例句：Conservatism is often associated with traditional values. 保守主义通常与传统价值观联系在一起。|例句：Her conservatism prevented her from embracing new ideas. 她的保守主义使她无法接受新思想。|近义词：traditionalism, orthodoxy|反义词：liberalism, radicalism|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *conservatisme*, from *conserver* "to preserve," from Latin *conservare*  (com- "together" + servare "to keep, guard").
