depriver:/dɪˈpraɪvər/| n. |剥夺者；使丧失者|Someone who deprives another person or thing of something. |The depriver of her inheritance left her penniless. 剥夺她继承权的人让她身无分文。|近义词：thief, robber, plunderer|反义词:provider, giver|词性:noun

deprive:/dɪˈpraɪv/| vt. |剥夺；使丧失|To take something away from someone or something; to prevent someone or something from having or using something.|The drought deprived the region of its crops.  干旱使该地区颗粒无收。|近义词：divest, strip, bereave|反义词:grant, provide, endow|词性:verb
