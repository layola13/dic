lowermost:/ˈloʊərˌmoʊst/ (US) /ˈləʊəməʊst/ (UK)| adj.|最低的，最下面的|situated at the lowest level|The lowermost branches of the tree were bare. 树最下面的枝条是光秃秃的。|The lowermost drawer of the cabinet contained old photographs. 橱柜最下面的抽屉里放着旧照片。|近义词：lowest, bottom, bottommost|反义词：highest, uppermost, topmost|词性:形容词
词源: From 'lower' + '-most'.  'Lower' comes from Old English 'lǣwer', comparative of 'lāg' (low). '-most' is a superlative suffix from Old English '-mest'.
