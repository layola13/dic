bungo:/ˈbʌŋɡoʊ/
n.
文学作品；（尤指）小说；夸张的文学语言；（尤指）夸张的文学语言
a literary work, especially a novel; exaggerated literary style; exaggerated literary style
例句：He is known for his dense and complex bungo. 他以其晦涩复杂的文学作品而闻名。
例句：Her latest bungo is a sprawling historical epic. 她最新的文学作品是一部庞大的历史史诗。
近义词：novel, book, literature, prose
反义词： (No direct antonym, could consider "simplicity" in terms of style)
词性：noun


Etymology:
From Japanese 文語 (bungo), from 文 (bun) meaning "writing, literature" and 語 (go) meaning "language, word."
