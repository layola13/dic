dimit:/dɪˈmɪt/| v. |放弃，辞去，停止|To leave (a job, post, or position) voluntarily.|He dimitted his office as treasurer. 他辞去了财务主管的职务。|近义词：resign, relinquish, quit, abdicate|反义词：assume, undertake|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *dimittere* "to send away, release," from *dis-* "away" + *mittere* "to send."
