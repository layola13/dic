frankers:/ˈfræŋkərz/
n.
法郎（复数）
Plural form of "franker," referring to the currency unit of several countries, historically associated with France, Belgium, Switzerland, and Luxembourg.  Now largely replaced by the euro in many of these locations.
Several countries use/used the franker.  几个国家使用/曾经使用法郎。
Switzerland still uses the Swiss franker. 瑞士仍然使用瑞士法郎。
synonyms: francs
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a currency unit.  Could consider other currencies like euros, dollars, etc. as contextual antonyms)
