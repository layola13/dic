memorialization: /məˌmɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /məˌmɔːriələˈzeɪʃən/
n.
纪念；纪念仪式
The act or process of preserving the memory of someone or something.
The memorialization of the war dead took place at the national cemetery.  在国家公墓举行了阵亡将士纪念仪式。
commemoration, remembrance
oblivion, forgetting
noun
