imperfectly:/ɪmˈpɜːrfɪktli/
adjective
不完美地，有缺陷地
not perfectly; having faults or defects.
The job was done imperfectly, and we had to redo it.  这项工作做得不完美，我们不得不重做。
The imperfectly formed crystal had several flaws.  这颗未完全成形的晶体有几个瑕疵。
Near Antonyms: perfectly, flawlessly, impeccably
Antonyms: perfectly
adverb
