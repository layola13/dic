cheapeners:/ˈtʃiːpənərz/
noun
降低价格的东西；使…便宜的人或物
Something that reduces the price of something else.
Supermarkets are using cheapeners to attract customers.  超市正在使用廉价商品来吸引顾客。
synonyms: discounts, reductions, bargains, sales, promotions
antonyms:  price hikes, increases, inflations
词性:n.
