nonfiler:/ˌnɑːnˈfaɪlər/ | /ˌnɒnˈfaɪlər/ | n. | 未申报者；不申报所得税者 | A person who fails to file a required report, especially an income tax return. | Example: The IRS is cracking down on nonfilers.  美国国税局正在严厉打击未申报者。| Example:  Many nonfilers are simply unaware of their obligations. 许多未申报者只是不知道他们的义务。 | Synonyms: delinquent, defaulter | Antonyms: filer, compliant | noun
