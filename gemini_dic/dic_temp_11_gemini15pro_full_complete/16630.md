barons:/ˈbærənz/
n.
男爵（baron的复数）
noblemen ranking below viscounts but above knights
The barons forced King John to sign the Magna Carta. 男爵们强迫约翰国王签署了《大宪章》。
noblemen, lords, peers
serfs, commoners
noun
