greenings:/ˈɡriːnɪŋz/
n.
绿化，青葱，绿色蔬菜
the process of becoming green or covered with green plants, or the new green parts of plants
The greenings of spring always make me feel hopeful. 春天的绿意总是让我感到充满希望。
Near synonyms: vegetation, verdure, foliage
Antonyms: browning, withering, decay
noun
