interchokes:/ˌɪntərˈtʃoʊks/ | n. |相互扼制;相互制约|A situation where two or more entities restrict or limit each other's actions or development. |The two countries' economies were in a state of interchoke, each dependent on the other but also wary of it. 这两个国家的经济处于相互制约的状态，彼此依赖却又相互提防。| Near synonyms: deadlock, stalemate, impasse, gridlock | Antonyms: cooperation, collaboration, synergy | noun
