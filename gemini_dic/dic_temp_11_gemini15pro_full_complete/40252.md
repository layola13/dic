continentalism:/ˌkɒntɪˈnɛntəlɪzəm/ |n.|大陆主义；大陆性|A political or economic policy that favors the interests of a continent or its inhabitants over those of other continents or nations.  The advocacy of policies or ideologies that prioritize the interests of a particular continent, often emphasizing self-sufficiency and regional cooperation.|例句：Continentalism has been a recurring theme in African politics.  大陆主义是非洲政治中反复出现的主题。

例句：The rise of continentalism can be seen as a reaction to globalization.  大陆主义的兴起可以被视为对全球化的反应。|近义词：regionalism, isolationism|反义词：internationalism, globalism|词性: noun
