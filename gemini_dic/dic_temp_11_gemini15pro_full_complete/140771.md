omophagic:/ˌɒməˈfædʒɪk/ | adj. | 食生肉的；吃生肉的 | Consuming or characterized by the eating of raw flesh. |  Ancient rituals sometimes involved omophagic practices. 古代仪式有时会涉及食生肉的习俗。

The lion's omophagic diet consists mainly of freshly killed prey.  狮子的食生肉饮食主要包括新鲜猎杀的猎物。 | 近义词: carnivorous, raw-meat-eating | 反义词: herbivorous, vegetarian | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ὠμοφάγος (ōmophagos)  'eating raw flesh', from  ὠμός (ōmos) 'raw' + -φάγος (-phagos) 'eating', from φαγεῖν (phagein)  'to eat'.
