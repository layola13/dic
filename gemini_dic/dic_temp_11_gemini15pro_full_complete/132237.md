nondeciduate:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪdʒuət/
adjective
非落叶的，常绿的
Not shedding leaves seasonally; evergreen.
The nondeciduous trees remained green throughout the winter. 这些常绿树整个冬天都保持绿色。
The nondeciduous nature of the forest provided year-round shelter for the animals.  这片森林的常绿特性为动物提供了全年的庇护所。
近义词: evergreen, persistent
反义词: deciduous
词性: adjective
