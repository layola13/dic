inaurate:/ɪˈnɔːɡjəreɪt/ /ɪˈnɑːɡjəreɪt/
verb
开创；创立； inaugurated；举行就职典礼；
To begin or introduce (something new, such as a policy or period of office) formally or ceremonially.
例句：The new mayor was inaugurated at City Hall. 新市长在市政厅宣誓就职。

例句：The president will be inaugurated in January. 总统将于一月份就职。

近义词：begin, commence, initiate, launch, start, introduce
反义词：conclude, end, terminate, finish
词性：v.
