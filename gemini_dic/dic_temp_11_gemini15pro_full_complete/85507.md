hastiest:/ˈheɪstiɪst/| adj.|最仓促的，最草率的|superlative adjective of hasty; done with excessive speed or without due consideration|He made the hastiest decision of his life. 他做出了人生中最草率的决定。|近义词：quickest, fastest, most hurried, most rushed|反义词：slowest, most deliberate, most thoughtful|词性:形容词

词源：From Middle English *hastif, from Old French hastif, from haste + -if.  Ultimately from Frankish *haifstiz.
