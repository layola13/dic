formative:/ˈfɔːrmətɪv/ | adj. n. |形成的，构成要素|Relating to formation or development; a word element, such as a prefix or suffix, that can be added to a base or root to form a new word.| The formative years of a child are crucial for their development.  一个孩子的形成时期对他们的发展至关重要。| synonyms: developmental, shaping, molding | antonyms: destructive, detrimental | adjective
