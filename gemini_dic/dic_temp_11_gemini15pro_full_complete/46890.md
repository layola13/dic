darius:/ˈdɛəriəs/| n.|大流士（男子名）|A male given name, of Persian origin, meaning "possessing goodness" or "kingly". It is derived from the Old Persian *Dārayavahuš*, meaning "he who holds firm the good".  It was the name of several Persian kings.|Darius was a great king of Persia.|近义词: (No direct synonyms for a proper noun.  Could consider other regal names like Cyrus, Xerxes)|反义词: (No antonyms for a proper noun)|词性:noun
