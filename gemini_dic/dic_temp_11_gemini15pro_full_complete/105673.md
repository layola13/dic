kingpin:/ˈkɪŋˌpɪn/| n.|主要人物；关键人物；大头目|A kingpin is the most important person in a group or organization, especially a criminal organization.  A kingpin is also a central pin or bolt upon which other parts turn, as in certain machinery or vehicles.|The kingpin of the drug cartel was finally arrested.  这个贩毒集团的头目最终被捕了。|Synonyms: leader, boss, chief, mastermind, linchpin|Antonyms: subordinate, underling|noun


kingpin:/ˈkɪŋˌpɪn/| adj.|最重要的；主要的|Most important; main.|The kingpin issue in the negotiation was the salary.  谈判中最关键的问题是薪水。|Synonyms: main, principal, central, chief, key|Antonyms: minor, secondary, peripheral|adjective
