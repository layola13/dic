monoecious: /məˈniːʃəs/ /məˈniːʃəs/
| adj. | 雌雄同株的；雌雄同体的 | Having both male and female reproductive organs in the same individual plant or animal. |  Corn is a monoecious plant, meaning it has separate male and female flowers on the same plant. 玉米是雌雄同株的植物，这意味着同一株植物上既有雄花也有雌花。

The earthworm is a monoecious animal. 蚯蚓是雌雄同体的动物。 | 近义词: hermaphroditic, bisexual | 反义词: dioecious | 词性: adjective
