nationally:/ˈnæʃənəli/
adverb
全国地，在全国范围内
across the whole nation; throughout a nation
The election was nationally televised.  选举在全国范围内电视转播。
The product is marketed nationally. 该产品在全国销售。
近义词:across the country, nationwide, throughout the nation
反义词:locally, regionally
词性:副词
