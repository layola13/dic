inlander:/ˈɪnləndər/| n.|内陆居民；内地人；本国人|A person who lives in the interior of a country or region, rather than on the coast.  Someone who is native to a country, as opposed to a foreigner. |例句：The inlander was unfamiliar with the customs of the coastal people.  这个内陆居民对沿海地区的习俗不熟悉。

例句：Many inlanders find the ocean fascinating. 许多内陆居民觉得海洋很迷人。|近义词：native, resident, citizen|反义词:foreigner, outsider, coastal dweller|词性:noun
