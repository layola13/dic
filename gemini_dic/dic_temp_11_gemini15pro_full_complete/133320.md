nonexplosives:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspləʊsɪvz/ | n. | 非爆炸物 | Materials or substances that do not explode, typically used in mining or construction where explosive materials are prohibited. |  These nonexplosives are safe for use in confined spaces. 这些非爆炸物可在密闭空间安全使用。|  Nonexplosive demolition agents were used to bring down the building. 使用非爆炸性拆除剂拆除了建筑物。| synonyms: inert materials, non-combustible materials | antonyms: explosives, combustibles | noun
