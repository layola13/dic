compendiously:/kəmˈpɛndiəsli/ | adv. | 简要地，简洁地 | In a brief and comprehensive manner; concisely. | He compendiously summarized the main points of the meeting. 他简要地总结了会议的要点。| The author compendiously described the complex history of the region in a single chapter.  作者用一章的篇幅简要地描述了该地区复杂的历史。 | 近义词: concisely, briefly, succinctly, summarily | 反义词: verbosely, lengthily, prolixly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *compendiosus, from Latin compendium (“abridgment, saving”), from com- (“together”) + pendere (“to weigh, pay”).  The original sense was "saving," as in saving time or words.
