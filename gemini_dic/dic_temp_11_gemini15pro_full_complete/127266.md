navarchy: /ˈneɪvɑːrki/
n.
naval supremacy; naval domination
海军霸权；制海权
The nation achieved navarchy after decades of investment in its fleet.  这个国家经过几十年对舰队的投资，实现了制海权。
Near synonyms:  naval dominance, maritime supremacy, sea power
Antonyms:  naval inferiority, maritime weakness
词性: noun

词源: From Greek *ναυαρχία (nauarchía), from ναύαρχος (naúarchos, “naval commander”), from ναῦς (naûs, “ship”) + ἄρχω (árkhō, “to rule, command”).
