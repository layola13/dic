archegonial: /ˌɑːrkɪˈɡoʊniəl/ | adj. | 藏卵器的；颈卵器的 | Relating to or resembling an archegonium, the female reproductive organ in mosses, ferns, and some other plants. | The archegonial neck is a channel through which the sperm swim to reach the egg. 藏卵器颈是精子游到卵细胞的通道。|  The development of the archegonial venter is crucial for successful fertilization. 藏卵器腹的发育对成功受精至关重要。| 近义词:  (No direct synonyms, but related terms include "female reproductive," "gametangial") | 反义词: antheridial (referring to the male reproductive organ) | 词性: adjective
