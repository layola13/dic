USAGE->
Simple Present:
What is his job? = 他的工作是什么？
What does she study at university? = 她在大学学习什么专业？

Present Continuous:
What are they doing in the park? = 他们在公园里做什么？
What is he cooking for dinner tonight? = 他今晚正在做什么晚餐？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? = 她做了什么值得获得这个奖项？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last weekend? = 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病之前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about before you arrived? = 你到达之前他们一直在争论什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen if we don't finish this project? = 如果我们不完成这个项目会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们将在会议上展示什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer? = 到明年夏天他们将建造什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将学习五年什么专业？
What will she have been working on for the past decade by 2030? = 到2030年，她在过去十年里将一直从事什么工作？


Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the issue? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

Negative Sentences:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you're saying. = 她不明白你在说什么。

Interrogative Sentences:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What are you thinking about? = 你在想什么？

Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。
