Navajos:/ˈnævəˌhoʊz/ /ˈnævəˌhəʊz/
n.
纳瓦霍人
A member of a Native American people of the southwestern United States.
The Navajos are known for their beautiful woven rugs and silver jewelry. 纳瓦霍人以其精美的编织地毯和银饰而闻名。

近义词:Navaho (singular), Diné (their own name)
词性:noun

注意事项:
Navajo can be used as a singular or plural noun.  When referring to the people collectively, "the Navajo" or "the Navajo people" is often used.  "Navajos" is also acceptable.  The preferred term for the people themselves is Diné.
