bowdlerize:/ˈboʊdləˌraɪz/| vt.|删节，净化，删除不当内容|To remove material that is considered improper or offensive from (a text or account), especially with the result that the text becomes weaker or less effective.|The publisher bowdlerized the novel before releasing it to a younger audience. 出版商在将小说发行给年轻读者之前删去了其中不当的内容。|近义词：censor, expurgate, sanitize|反义词：unabridged, unexpurgated|词性：动词

bowdlerized:/ˈboʊdləˌraɪzd/| adj.|被删节的，被净化的|Expurgated or censored to remove offensive or objectionable material.|The bowdlerized version of the play omitted several key scenes.  这个剧本的删节版省略了几个关键场景。|近义词：censored, expurgated, sanitized|反义词：unabridged, unexpurgated|词性：形容词


bowdlerization:/ˌboʊdlərəˈzeɪʃən/| n.|删节，净化|The act or result of removing material that is considered improper or offensive from a text or account.|The bowdlerization of the classic novel sparked controversy among literary scholars.  这部经典小说的删节在文学学者中引发了争议。|近义词：censorship, expurgation|反义词： / |词性：名词
