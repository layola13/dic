bismillah:/ˌbɪsmɪˈlæː/| interj.|奉真主之名|An Arabic phrase meaning "In the name of God (Allah)". It is commonly recited by Muslims before undertaking any significant action or task, as a way of seeking God's blessings and guidance.|Bismillah, let's begin the meeting. 奉真主之名，让我们开始会议吧。|Near synonyms: By the grace of God, God willing|Antonyms: None|Interjection
