bookishnesses:/ˈbʊkɪʃnəsɪz/
noun
书呆子气，学究气
The quality of being excessively devoted to reading and studying, often to the exclusion of social interaction and practical experience.
He displayed a certain bookishness that made him seem out of place at the party. 他表现出某种书呆子气，使他在聚会上显得格格不入。
近义词:pedantry, scholarlyness, intellectualism
反义词:worldliness, practicality
