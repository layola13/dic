digress:/daɪˈɡrɛs/ /dɪˈɡrɛs/| v.i.|离题，跑题|To deviate or wander away from the main topic or purpose in speaking or writing.|The speaker digressed from his prepared remarks to tell a personal anecdote. 演讲者偏离了他准备好的讲稿，讲了一个个人轶事。|例句：He digressed from the main point of the lecture. 他偏离了演讲的主题。|近义词：deviate, wander, ramble, stray|反义词：stay on topic, focus|词性:动词
