officinal:/əˈfɪsɪnəl/
adjective
药用的；药典记载的
Kept in a pharmacy specifically for compounding and dispensing medicines; used in or belonging to an apothecary's or druggist's shop or laboratory.
例句：Officinal herbs are used in traditional medicine. 药用草本植物用于传统医学。

例句：The officinal preparations are described in the pharmacopoeia. 药典中描述了药用制剂。

近义词：medicinal, therapeutic, pharmaceutical
反义词：non-medicinal

词性: adjective
