longboard:/ˈlɒŋbɔːrd/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋbɔːrd/ (US)| n.|长板|A type of skateboard that is longer than a typical skateboard, often used for cruising, downhill racing, and freestyle riding.|He rode his longboard down the hill. 他骑着长板滑下山。|近义词：skateboard, cruiser|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "shortboard" in surfing context)|noun

longboard:/ˈlɒŋbɔːrd/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋbɔːrd/ (US)| v.|进行长板运动|To ride a longboard.|She longboarded along the beach path. 她沿着海滩小路玩长板。|近义词：skateboard, cruise|反义词: (No direct antonym)|verb
