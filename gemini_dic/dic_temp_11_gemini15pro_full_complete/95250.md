incandescently:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsəntli/
adverb
炽热地；白热地；热情洋溢地
in a manner which emits light as a result of being heated; in a manner which shows strong emotion or passion
The sun beat down incandescently. 太阳炽热地照射着大地。
Her eyes shone incandescently with excitement. 她的眼睛因兴奋而闪闪发光。
Synonym: brightly, brilliantly, radiantly, passionately, fervently
Antonym: dimly, dully, coldly, apathetically
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From incandescent + -ly.  Incandescent comes from Late Latin incandescent-, incandescens, present participle of incandescere "to grow hot," from in- "into, in, on, upon" + candescere "to become white," from candere "to shine, glow white."
