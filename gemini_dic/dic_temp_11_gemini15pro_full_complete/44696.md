The crudeness of his language offended many people. 他的粗言秽语冒犯了很多人。

His early paintings show a certain crudeness of technique. 他早期的绘画显示出某种技术上的粗糙。|Synonyms:roughness, coarseness, vulgarity, rawness, simplicity, unsophistication|Antonyms:refinement, sophistication, elegance, polish, delicacy|noun
