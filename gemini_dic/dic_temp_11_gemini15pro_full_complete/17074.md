batikked:/bæˈtiːkt/| adj.|蜡染的；用蜡染法印染的|Decorated with a design made by waxing parts of the material before dyeing it.|The artist displayed several batikked scarves. 艺术家展示了几条蜡染围巾。|近义词：dyed, printed|反义词:plain, undyed|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From batik + -ed.  Batik itself comes from the Javanese word *batik*, from *amba* (to write) + *titik* (dot).
