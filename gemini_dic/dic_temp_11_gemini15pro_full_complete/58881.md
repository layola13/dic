durationless:/djʊˈreɪʃənləs/
adj.
无持续时间的，瞬间的
Having no duration; instantaneous.
The durationless flash of a camera's bulb. 相机灯泡瞬间的闪光。
近义词:instantaneous, momentary, fleeting, ephemeral
反义词:lasting, enduring, permanent, prolonged
词性:形容词


词源:
From duration + -less.  Duration comes from Late Middle English: from Old French, from late Latin duratio(n-), from durare ‘to last’, from durus ‘hard’. The suffix -less forms adjectives meaning 'without' or 'lacking'.
