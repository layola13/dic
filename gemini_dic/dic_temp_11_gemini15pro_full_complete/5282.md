allophonic:/ˌæləˈfɒnɪk/ (US) /ˌæləˈfɒnɪk/ (UK)| adj.|音位的，音位变体的|Relating to or being an allophone.  An allophone is one of a set of multiple possible spoken sounds (or phones) used to pronounce a single phoneme in a particular language.|The aspirated 'p' in 'pin' and the unaspirated 'p' in 'spin' are allophonic variations in English.|近义词：phonetic|反义词: phonemic|词性: adjective
