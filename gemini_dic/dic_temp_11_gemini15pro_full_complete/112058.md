longeval:/lɒnˈdʒiːvəl/ /lɑːnˈdʒiːvəl/| adj.|长寿的，长命的|Living a long time; of great age or duration.|例句：The tortoise is a longeval animal. 乌龟是一种长寿的动物。|例句：The longeval redwood trees towered over the forest. 长寿的红杉树高耸于森林之上。|近义词：long-lived, enduring, lasting|反义词：short-lived, ephemeral, transient|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *longaevus (“long-lived”), from longus (“long”) + aevum (“age, lifetime”).
