brumaire:/bruːˈmɛər/| n.|雾月, 法国共和历的第二个月|The coup of 18 Brumaire brought Napoleon to power.  雾月十八日政变使拿破仑掌权。|Synonym:  no direct synonym (as it's a specific month name), perhaps "autumn month" could be considered a loose synonym|Antonym:  no direct antonym|Noun

Etymology:
From French *Brumaire*, from *brume* ("mist, fog"), from Late Latin *bruma* ("winter solstice"), from Gaulish *bruma*.
