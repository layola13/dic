overprogrammed:/ˌoʊvərˈprəʊɡræmd/ | adj. | 过度编程的，过度安排的 | Having too many activities or tasks scheduled; excessively planned or structured. |  例句：The children were overprogrammed with extracurricular activities and had no time to just play. 孩子们的课外活动安排过多，没有时间玩耍。

例句：The conference was overprogrammed, leaving little room for spontaneous discussions.  会议安排得太满，几乎没有时间进行自发的讨论。 | 近义词：overscheduled, overloaded, overbooked | 反义词：under-scheduled, free | 词性：形容词
