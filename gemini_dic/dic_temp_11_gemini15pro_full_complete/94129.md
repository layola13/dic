impedance: /ɪmˈpiːdəns/ | n. |阻抗|The effective resistance of an electric circuit or component to alternating current, arising from the combined effects of resistance, inductance, and capacitance.| The impedance of the circuit is too high. 电路的阻抗太高了。|synonyms: resistance, reactance, obstruction | antonyms: conductance, admittance | noun

impedance: /ɪmˈpiːdəns/ | n. |障碍，妨碍|Something that makes it difficult for something to happen or move forward. |Bureaucracy is an impedance to progress. 官僚主义是进步的障碍。| synonyms: obstacle, hindrance, barrier | antonyms: aid, assistance, help | noun
