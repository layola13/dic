itchy:/ˈɪtʃi/| adj.|痒的，渴望的|Having or causing an uncomfortable feeling on the skin that makes you want to scratch; having a strong restless desire.|例句：My mosquito bites are so itchy. 我的蚊子包很痒。|近义词：irritated, scratchy, restless|反义词:comfortable, soothed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *gyccean (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *jukjaną (“to itch”), from Proto-Indo-European *yug- (“to yoke, join”).  Related to yoke. The figurative sense of "restless" arose in the 19th century.
