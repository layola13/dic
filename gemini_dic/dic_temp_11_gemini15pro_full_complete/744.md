absist:/æbˈsɪst/| v. |戒除，避开|To voluntarily refrain from something, typically a habit or practice.|He decided to absist from alcohol after the health scare. 他在健康受到惊吓后决定戒酒。|She abstained from voting in the election.  她在选举中弃权。|近义词：refrain, forbear, desist|反义词：indulge, partake|词性:动词
词源: From Latin abstinere "to hold back, abstain from," from abs- "away, from" + tenere "to hold."
