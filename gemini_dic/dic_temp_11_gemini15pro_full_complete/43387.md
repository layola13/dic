crank-out:/ˈkræŋkˈaʊt/
verb
大量生产；粗制滥造
to produce something quickly and in large amounts, especially something of low quality
The factory cranks out thousands of these plastic toys every day.  这家工厂每天都大量生产这种塑料玩具。
churn out, mass-produce
handcraft, meticulously create
verb
