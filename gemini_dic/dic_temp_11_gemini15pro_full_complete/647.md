abridgers:/əˈbrɪdʒərz/| n.|  缩写者；删节者| People who abridge texts, especially books, are called abridgers.  删减文本，尤其是书籍的人被称为缩写者。 |  The abridgers condensed the lengthy novel into a more manageable volume. 缩写者将冗长的小说精简成更易于管理的篇幅。 |The abridgers worked tirelessly to maintain the integrity of the original story while shortening it.  缩写者不知疲倦地工作，在缩短故事的同时保持了原著的完整性。 | synonyms: condensers, summarizers, editors | antonyms: expanders, elaborators | noun
