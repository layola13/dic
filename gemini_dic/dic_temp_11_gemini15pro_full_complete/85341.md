harpsichords:/ˈhɑːrpsɪkɔːrdz/
n.
大键琴（复数）
A keyboard instrument that produces sound by plucking strings with quills or plectrums.  It was widely used in Baroque music.
The museum has a collection of antique harpsichords.  博物馆收藏了一些古董大键琴。
近义词：pianoforte, clavichord
反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑现代乐器，例如 piano)
词性: noun
