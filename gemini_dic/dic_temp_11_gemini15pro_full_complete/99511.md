interfraternally:/ˌɪntərfrəˈtɜːrnəli/
adjective
兄弟会之间地，友爱地
in a brotherly or fraternal way between fraternities.
例句：The interfraternal council organized a campus-wide philanthropy event.  兄弟会间理事会组织了一场全校范围的慈善活动。

例句：Interfraternal relations were strained after the rivalry game.  对抗赛后，兄弟会之间的关系变得紧张。

近义词：amicably, fraternally, harmoniously
反义词：hostilely, antagonistically
词性：adjective
