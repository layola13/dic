laggingly:/ˈlæɡɪŋli/| adv.|滞后地，缓慢地|in a lagging manner; slowly and reluctantly; falling behind.  Indicating an action or process that is slow, delayed, or reluctant.|The economy recovered laggingly after the recession. 经济衰退后，经济复苏缓慢。|Synonyms: slowly, tardily, reluctantly, hesitantly|Antonyms: quickly, rapidly, promptly, eagerly|adverb

Etymology->
From lag (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Lag" comes from Old Norse "lakra" meaning "to go slowly."
