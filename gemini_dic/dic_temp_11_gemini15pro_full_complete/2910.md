advisably:/ədˈvaɪzəbli/
adverb
明智地；最好地；合乎情理地
In a way that is sensible and likely to achieve a good result.
It would be advisable to consult your doctor.  最好咨询一下你的医生。
She dressed advisably for the cold weather. 她穿得很暖和以应对寒冷的天气。
近义词: wisely, prudently, sensibly, judiciously
反义词: unwisely, foolishly, imprudently, rashly
词性: adverb

词源:
From advise + -able + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin ad- "to" + visere "to see."
