blading:/ˈbleɪdɪŋ/
n.|滑冰，溜冰，刀刃|The act of skating, typically on inline skates or ice skates.  It can also refer to the blades of a tool or machine.|I went blading in the park yesterday. 我昨天去公园滑冰了。|近义词：skating, rolling|反义词：(no direct opposite, possibly walking)|词性: noun

blading:/ˈbleɪdɪŋ/
n.|刀片，叶片|The blades of a tool or machine, such as a fan, propeller, or turbine.|The blading of the fan was damaged. 风扇的叶片损坏了。|近义词：blades, vanes|反义词: (no direct opposite)|词性: noun
