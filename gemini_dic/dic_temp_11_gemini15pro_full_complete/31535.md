charterer:/ˈtʃɑːrtərər/ | n. |承租人，包租人|A person or company that hires or leases something, especially a ship or aircraft, for their own use. | The charterer of the yacht planned a lavish cruise to the Caribbean. 包租游艇的人计划去加勒比海进行一次奢华的游轮之旅。|  The charterer requested a specific route for the cargo ship.  承租人要求货船走特定的航线。| synonyms: lessee, renter, hirer | antonyms: lessor, owner, landlord | noun

charterer:/ˈtʃɑːrtərər/ | n. |包租公司|A company that charters ships or aircraft. | The charterer specializes in transporting goods to remote islands. 这家包租公司专门向偏远岛屿运送货物。 | The charterer offers a variety of aircraft for business and leisure travel. 这家包租公司提供各种飞机用于商务和休闲旅行。 | synonyms: rental agency, leasing company | antonyms: owner, operator | noun
