endodermic:/ˌɛndəˈdɜːrmɪk/| adj.|内胚层的，内皮的|Relating to or derived from the endoderm, the innermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo.|The endodermic layer gives rise to the lining of the digestive tract.|Synonym: entodermal, hypoblastic|Antonym: ectodermic|adjective

endodermic:/ˌɛndəˈdɜːrmɪk/| n.|内胚层|The innermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo, giving rise to the lining of the digestive tract and associated structures.|The endodermic is crucial for the development of vital organs.|Synonym: entoderm, hypoblast|Antonym: ectoderm|noun
