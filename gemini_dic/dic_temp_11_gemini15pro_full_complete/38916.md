confessionalist:/kənˈfɛʃənəlɪst/
n.
忏悔者；告解者；认罪者；(尤指)宗派主义者
a person who confesses something, especially their sins; a person who emphasizes adherence to a particular religious confession or denomination.
The priest listened to the confessionalist's sins.  神父倾听了忏悔者的罪过。
The confessionalist political parties exacerbated the tensions in the region.  宗派主义政党加剧了该地区的紧张局势。
近义词：sectarian, denominationalist
反义词：nonsectarian, ecumenist
词性：名词

confessionalist:/kənˈfɛʃənəlɪst/
adj.
忏悔的；告解的；认罪的；(尤指)宗派主义的
relating to or characteristic of confession, especially of sins; relating to or based on adherence to a particular religious confession or denomination.
He joined a confessionalist Lutheran church. 他加入了一个宗派主义的信义宗教会。
The confessionalist nature of the conflict made it difficult to find a peaceful solution.  冲突的宗派性质使得难以找到和平的解决方案。
近义词：sectarian, denominational
反义词：nonsectarian, ecumenist
词性：形容词
