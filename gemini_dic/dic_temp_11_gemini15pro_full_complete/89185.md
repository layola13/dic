homeownerships:/ˌhoʊmˈoʊnərˌʃɪps/
n.
房屋所有权，房产拥有
The state of owning a home or homes.
Homeownerships in the area have increased significantly in recent years. 近年来该地区的房屋所有权显著增加。

Homeownerships declined during the recession. 经济衰退期间房屋所有权下降了。


近义词:homeownership (单数形式更常用), property ownership
反义词:renting, tenancy



词性:noun


词源:
From homeowner + -ship. Homeowner is from home + owner.  Ultimately from Old English *hām*  (home) and *āgan* (to own).
