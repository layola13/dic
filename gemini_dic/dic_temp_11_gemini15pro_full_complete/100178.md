interreign:/ˌɪntəˈreɪn/ | n. |空位期，过渡期，间歇期|A period of time between the end of one reign and the beginning of the next, or between the departure of one leader or incumbent and the arrival of their successor. |The interreign between the two monarchs was marked by political instability. 两位君主之间的过渡期以政治不稳定为标志。

The museum closed for renovations, leading to a brief interreign in its exhibitions.  博物馆因翻修而关闭，导致其展览出现短暂的间歇期。|近义词：interval, hiatus, gap, break |反义词：reign, incumbency, tenure |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French interregne, from Latin interregnum, from inter- ‘between’ + regnum ‘reign’.
