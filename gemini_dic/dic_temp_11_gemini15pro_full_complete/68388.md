fallowing:/ˈfæloʊɪŋ/
n.|休耕，休闲地|The farmer left the field fallowing for a season to improve the soil. 农民休耕这块田一个季度以改良土壤。|近义词：resting, uncultivated|反义词:cultivating, planting|词性:名词
fallowing:/ˈfæloʊɪŋ/
adj.|休耕的|The fallowing field was covered in weeds. 休耕的田地里长满了杂草。|近义词：resting, idle|反义词:cultivated, planted|词性:形容词

词源：From Middle English *falewinge, from Old English *fealwung, from fealgian (“to fallow”), from Proto-Germanic *falgōną (“to harrow, break up land”).
