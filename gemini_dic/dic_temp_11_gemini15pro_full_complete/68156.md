failings:/ˈfeɪlɪŋz/
n.
缺点，弱点，不足之处
shortcomings, flaws, or weaknesses in a person's character or behavior
His biggest failing was his inability to delegate. 他最大的缺点是不会委派工作。

近义词: shortcomings, weaknesses, flaws, defects, deficiencies
反义词: strengths, virtues, merits, advantages
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
