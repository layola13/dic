illiteracy:/ɪˈlɪtərəsi/
n.
文盲；缺乏教育；无知
the inability to read or write.
Illiteracy rates are still high in many developing countries. 许多发展中国家的文盲率仍然很高。
近义词:ignorance, unletteredness
反义词:literacy
词性:名词

illiterate:/ɪˈlɪtərət/
adj.
文盲的；不识字的；没受教育的
unable to read or write.
Many adults in this region are illiterate.  这个地区的许多成年人都是文盲。
近义词:unlettered, uneducated
反义词:literate
词性:形容词

illiterate:/ɪˈlɪtərət/
n.
文盲
a person who is unable to read or write.
The government is working to reduce the number of illiterates in the country. 政府正在努力减少该国文盲的数量。
近义词:unlettered person
反义词:literate person
词性:名词
