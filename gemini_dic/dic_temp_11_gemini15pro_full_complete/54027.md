discursivenesses:/dɪsˈkɜːsɪvnəsɪz/
noun
冗长；散漫；离题
the quality of being discursive; rambling tendency.
His discursivenesses made it difficult to follow his train of thought.  他说话冗长散漫，难以理解他的思路。
The professor's lecture was marked by long discursivenesses. 教授的讲座以冗长的离题而著称。
近义词:rambling, digression, prolixity, verbosity
反义词:conciseness, brevity, succinctness
词性:n.


Etymology: From discursive + -ness + -es (plural). Discursive comes from Late Latin *discursīvus, from Latin discursus, past participle of discurrere "to run to and fro, digress," from dis- "apart" + currere "to run."
