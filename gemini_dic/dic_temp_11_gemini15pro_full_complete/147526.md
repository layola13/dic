overpass:/ˈoʊvərˌpæs/ (US) /ˈəʊvəˌpɑːs/ (UK)| n.|天桥；立交桥；高架桥|A bridge, road, railway, or similar structure that crosses over another road, railway, or similar structure.|We drove over the overpass to get to the other side of the highway. 我们开车过天桥到达高速公路的另一边。|The train rumbled across the overpass high above the valley. 火车隆隆地驶过山谷上方的高架桥。|Synonyms: flyover, bridge, viaduct|Antonyms: underpass|noun

overpass:/ˈoʊvərˌpæs/ (US) /ˈəʊvəˌpɑːs/ (UK)| vt.|越过；超过；忽略|To cross over or pass above something.|The new highway overpasses the river. 新的高速公路跨过这条河。|He completely overpassed my suggestion in the meeting. 他在会议上完全忽略了我的建议。|Synonyms: bypass, ignore, disregard|Antonyms: acknowledge, address|verb
