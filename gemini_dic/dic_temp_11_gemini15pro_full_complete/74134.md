foundation's:/faʊnˈdeɪʃənz/
|n.|基金会的；地基的|The possessive form of "foundation," indicating belonging or origin.| The foundation's mission is to support education. 基金会的使命是支持教育。|Synonyms: base's, underpinning's, groundwork's|Antonyms:  (Not applicable for possessive forms)|Possessive noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *foundacioun*, from Old French *fondacion*, from Latin *fundātiō* (“a founding, foundation”), from *fundāre* (“to found”), from *fundus* (“bottom, base”).  The -'s is the possessive suffix.
