capitulate:/kəˈpɪtʃəleɪt/| vi.|投降，屈服|To cease resisting : surrender often after negotiation of terms.|例句：The enemy was forced to capitulate. 敌人被迫投降。|近义词：surrender, yield, submit|反义词:resist, defy|verb

capitulate:/kəˈpɪtʃəleɪt/| vi.|让步，停止争论|To give up resistance : acquiesce.|例句：She capitulated to their demands. 她屈服于他们的要求。|近义词：acquiesce, concede, give in|反义词:resist, oppose|verb
