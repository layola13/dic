dram:/dræm/| n.| 1.少量液体; 一小口 2.少量的烈酒 3.药剂量单位(等于1/8液体制盎司或3.7毫升) 4.少量; 一点儿 5.戏剧性; 扣人心弦|1. He took a dram of whisky. 他喝了一小口威士忌。 2. He poured himself a dram of Scotch. 他给自己倒了一点苏格兰威士忌。 3. The doctor prescribed a dram of cough syrup. 医生开了服一药剂量的咳嗽糖浆。 4. There's not a dram of truth in his story. 他的故事里没有一点儿真话。 5. The play was full of dram and excitement. 这部戏充满了戏剧性和刺激性。|近义词：drop, sip, bit, ounce, dash|反义词: lot, abundance|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *drame*, from Late Latin *dragma*, from Greek *δράγμα* (drágma, "handful, a small weight"), from *δράσσομαι* (drássomai, "to grasp"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰrebʰ-*, "to seize, grab."
