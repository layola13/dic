counterwork:/ˈkaʊntərwɜːrk/
n.
对抗，抵消；平衡力；对策
Action taken to oppose or offset another action; a force or influence that balances or offsets another; something that opposes or contrasts with something else.
The government's counterwork against the rebels was swift and decisive. 政府对叛军的反击迅速而果断。
The architect designed the building with a series of counterworks to stabilize it in high winds.  建筑师设计了一系列的平衡结构，以确保建筑物在大风中保持稳定。

近义词:counteraction, countermeasure, opposition, resistance
反义词:support, assistance, cooperation

词性:noun
