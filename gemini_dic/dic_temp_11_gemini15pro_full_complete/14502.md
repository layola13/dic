avenge:/əˈvɛndʒ/| vt.|报仇，复仇|To inflict punishment in return for an injury or wrong done to oneself or another.|He swore he would avenge his father's murder. 他发誓要为他父亲的谋杀报仇。|近义词：retaliate, revenge|反义词:forgive, pardon|动词

avenged:/əˈvɛndʒd/ |v. |已报仇的，已复仇的 (avenge的过去式和过去分词)|Having taken vengeance for a wrong; avenged.|He felt avenged when the culprit was finally caught. 当罪犯最终被抓住时，他感到报了仇。|近义词：retaliated, revenged|反义词:forgiven, pardoned|动词
