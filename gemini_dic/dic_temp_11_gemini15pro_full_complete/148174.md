overservilities:/ˌoʊvərˌsɜːrvɪˈlɪtiz/
|n.| 过分殷勤；奴颜婢膝 | Excessive subservience; an excessive willingness to serve or please others. | His overservilities made his colleagues uncomfortable. 他的过分殷勤让同事们感到不舒服。| The waiter's overservilities bordered on obsequiousness.  服务员过分的殷勤近乎谄媚。 |近义词：obsequiousness, servility, fawning |反义词：independence, assertiveness |词性:名词


词源:
From "over" (excessive) + "servility" (the condition of being a servant).  "Servility" comes from the Latin "servilis" (of a slave), from "servus" (slave).
