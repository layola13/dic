blood-relative:/ˈblʌd ˈrelətɪv/
n.
血亲，近亲
A person who is related to another by birth rather than by marriage.
My blood relatives are coming to visit for the holidays.  我的血亲假期要来拜访。
kin, kinsman, kinswoman, family member
in-law, spouse
noun
