idleness:/ˈaɪdl̩nɪs/ /ˈaɪdl̩nəs/ | n. |懒惰，闲散，无所事事 | The state of being idle; inactivity; lack of occupation. |  He lamented the idleness of his youth. 他对年轻时的懒散感到后悔。


She couldn't stand the idleness of a beach vacation. 她无法忍受海滩度假的无所事事。


近义词：inactivity, laziness, sloth, indolence | 反义词：activity, industry, diligence | 词性：名词
