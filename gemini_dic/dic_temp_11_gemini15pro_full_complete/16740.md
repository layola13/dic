bartered:/ˈbɑːrtərd/
verb
以物易物；交换
exchanged goods or services for other goods or services without using money.
The prisoners bartered cigarettes for food. 囚犯们用香烟换食物。
Synonyms: traded, exchanged, swapped
Antonyms: bought, purchased, sold
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *barten*, from Old French *barater* (“to cheat, deceive, barter”), of uncertain origin.
