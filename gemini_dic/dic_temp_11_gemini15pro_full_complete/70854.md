Finkelstein:/ˈfɪŋkəlstaɪn/| n.|芬克尔斯坦;  芬克尔斯坦反应|A surname of German and Ashkenazic Jewish origin. It is a habitational name, from various places called Finkelstein, meaning "raven's stone."  It is also associated with the Finkelstein reaction in organic chemistry.|例句：Dr. Finkelstein is a renowned chemist. 芬克尔斯坦博士是一位著名的化学家。

例句：The Finkelstein reaction is useful in organic synthesis. 芬克尔斯坦反应在有机合成中很有用。|近义词: (For the reaction) Halex reaction|反义词: None|词性:Noun
