counterdemonstrated:/ˌkaʊntərˈdɛmənˌstreɪtɪd/ | v. | 反示威，举行对抗性示威 | to demonstrate in opposition to another demonstration. | Example:  They counterdemonstrated against the proposed cuts to education funding. 他们在抗议削减教育经费的示威活动中举行了反示威。| Example:  A group of students counterdemonstrated in support of free speech. 一群学生举行反示威支持言论自由。 | Synonyms: protest, oppose, resist | Antonyms: support, endorse | verb
