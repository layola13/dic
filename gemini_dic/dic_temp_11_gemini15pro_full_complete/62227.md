encroach:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃ/ /ɛnˈkroʊtʃ/ |v. |侵入，侵占；蚕食，逐步侵蚀|to advance gradually beyond usual or acceptable limits|例句：The factory encroached on the villagers' farmland. 工厂侵占了村民的农田。

例句：The sea is encroaching on the land. 海水正在侵蚀陆地。


近义词：intrude, infringe, impinge, trespass|反义词：retreat, withdraw|词性：动词
