counterbrace:/ˈkaʊntərbreɪs/
noun
支撑物，对抗力，平衡力
A counterbrace is a brace or support applied in opposition to another, typically to provide stability or resist pressure.
The engineers installed a counterbrace to stabilize the bridge during the high winds.  工程师们安装了一个支撑物来稳定大桥以抵抗强风。
The counterbrace helped distribute the weight evenly across the structure.  支撑物帮助结构均匀地分布重量。
Synonyms: support, brace, strut, prop
Antonyms: strain, stress, pressure
