inebrious:/ɪnˈiːbriəs/ | adj. | 醉的；使人醉的 | intoxicated, drunk; tending to intoxicate |  The inebrious man stumbled out of the bar. 醉汉踉跄着走出酒吧。| 近义词: intoxicated, drunk, tipsy, inebriated | 反义词: sober, temperate | 词性: 形容词
词源: Late Latin inebriōsus, from Latin inēbriāre to intoxicate, from in- + ēbrius drunk
