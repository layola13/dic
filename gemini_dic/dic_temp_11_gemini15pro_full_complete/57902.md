driftpiece:/ˈdrɪftˌpiːs/| n. |浮木，漂流木|A piece of wood floating in the sea or washed ashore. |例句：The beach was littered with driftpieces. 海滩上到处都是漂流木。

例句：He found a large driftpiece and carved it into a beautiful sculpture. 他找到了一块大浮木，把它雕刻成了一座美丽的雕塑。|近义词：driftwood, flotsam, wreckage|反义词: None|词性:noun
