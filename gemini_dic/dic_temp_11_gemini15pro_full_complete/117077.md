meadowed:/ˈmedəʊd/ (UK), /ˈmedoud/ (US)| adj.|草地覆盖的，像草地的|Covered with meadows; resembling a meadow.|The valley was meadowed and lush. 山谷里草地覆盖，郁郁葱葱。|The meadowed hills were a beautiful sight. 草地覆盖的山丘是一道美丽的风景。|近义词：grassy, verdant|反义词: barren, arid|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From meadow + -ed.  "Meadow" comes from Old English *mǣdwe (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *mēdwō, from Proto-Indo-European *mē(i)- (“to mow, cut”).
