mohawk: /ˈmoʊhɔːk/ (US) /ˈməʊhɔːk/ (UK)| n. adj.|莫霍克族人，莫霍克语，莫霍克发型|A member of a North American Indian people formerly living in the Mohawk River valley of eastern New York state.  Also refers to their language and a hairstyle with a shaved head except for a central strip of upright hair. |Example: He sported a bright red mohawk. 他留着一个亮红色的莫霍克发型。

|Example:  The Mohawk tribe was known for its fierce warriors. 莫霍克部落以其骁勇善战的武士而闻名。|近义词: Iroquois (for the tribe), hairstyle (for the haircut)|反义词: None|词性: noun, adjective

mohawk: /ˈmoʊhɔːk/ (US) /ˈməʊhɔːk/ (UK)| n.| 莫霍克河|A river in central New York, flowing southeast into the Hudson River.|Example: The Mohawk River is an important waterway. 莫霍克河是一条重要的水道。|Example: They canoed down the Mohawk River. 他们乘独木舟顺莫霍克河而下。|近义词: None|反义词: None|词性: noun
