kinematic:/ˌkɪnəˈmætɪk/ /ˌkaɪnəˈmætɪk/| adj. |运动学的；运动的|Relating to motion considered without reference to its cause, i.e. to forces or mass.|The kinematic viscosity of a fluid is its dynamic viscosity divided by its density.|近义词：kinetic, motional|反义词:static, motionless|词性:形容词
kinematics:/ˌkɪnəˈmætɪks/ /ˌkaɪnəˈmætɪks/| n. |运动学|The branch of mechanics concerned with the motion of objects without reference to the forces which cause the motion.|Robotics involves complex kinematics to control the movement of robotic arms.|近义词：dynamics (although technically different)|反义词:statics|词性:名词
