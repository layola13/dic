curtain-raiser:/ˈkɜːrtnˌreɪzər/ (US) /ˈkɜːtnˌreɪzə/ (UK)| n. |开场戏；序幕；预演|An event or performance that precedes a more important one.|The short play served as a curtain-raiser for the main performance. 这出短剧作为主要表演的序幕。|近义词：prelude, opener, warm-up, introduction|反义词: afterpiece, finale|词性: 名词

Etymology:  From "curtain" (referring to the theater curtain) + "raiser" (one who raises).  The image is of a short performance given before the main curtain is raised for the principal event.
