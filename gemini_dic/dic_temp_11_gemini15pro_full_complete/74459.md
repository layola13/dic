franked:/fræŋkt/
adjective
已付邮资的；免费邮寄的
Officially stamped for free postage.  Having had the postage paid, especially by an official mark.
The letter was franked. 这封信已付邮资。
Synonyms: post-free, prepaid
Antonyms: unfranked, unpaid
词性: adjective

franked:/fræŋkt/
verb (past participle of *frank*)
坦率地表达；盖销邮票；免费邮寄
Express (something) freely and frankly. Mark (a letter or other postal item) with an official mark to indicate that postage has been paid or does not need to be paid.
The senator's mail is franked. 参议员的邮件是免费邮寄的。
Synonyms: stamp, authorize, endorse
Antonyms: censor, suppress
词性: verb (past participle)
