leapfrogs:/ˈliːpfrɒɡz/ (British), /ˈliːpfrɔːɡz/ (American)| v. (leapfrogged, leapfrogging)|跳蛙式超越，跃进，突飞猛进|To surpass or advance by leaps and bounds, often by skipping over intervening stages or competitors.  The company leapfrogged its competitors by investing heavily in new technology.|近义词：outstrip, surpass, overtake, bypass|反义词：lag behind, fall behind|词性：动词

leapfrogs:/ˈliːpfrɒɡz/ (British), /ˈliːpfrɔːɡz/ (American)| n. |跳房子游戏 (通常指复数形式)|A game in which players vault over each other's backs.|The children were playing leapfrogs in the park.|近义词：hopscotch (though not exactly the same)|反义词：N/A|词性：名词
