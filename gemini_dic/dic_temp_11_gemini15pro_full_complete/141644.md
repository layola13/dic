optimism's:/ˈɒptɪmɪzəm/| n.|乐观主义（的）|It is the possessive form of "optimism," referring to something belonging to or associated with optimism.  It signifies the quality of being hopeful and confident about the future.|例句：Optimism's power should never be underestimated.  乐观主义的力量永远不应该被低估。|近义词：hopefulness, positivity|反义词:pessimism, negativity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French optimisme, from Latin optimum (best). Coined by Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz.
