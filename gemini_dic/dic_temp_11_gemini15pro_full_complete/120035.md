millionths:/ˈmɪljənθs/| n.|百万分之一|Representing one part in a million; a very small fraction.  It is the plural form of "millionth".|The error was only a few millionths of a second.  误差只有百万分之几秒。|Near synonyms: tiny fractions, minute amounts, negligible amounts|Antonym: wholes, large quantities|noun
