convexity:/kənˈvɛksɪti/ /kɑːnˈvɛksɪti/| n. |凸性，凸度|The quality of being convex, having an outline or surface curved like the exterior of a circle or sphere.|The lens has a slight convexity.  这个透镜略微凸起。|synonyms: curvature, bulge, roundness|antonyms: concavity, hollowness|noun

convexity:/kənˈvɛksɪti/ /kɑːnˈvɛksɪti/| n. |（债券价格与收益率关系的）凸性|In finance, a measure of the curvature in the relationship between bond prices and bond yields.|The bond's convexity makes it attractive to investors.  该债券的凸性使其对投资者具有吸引力。|synonyms: bond convexity, price sensitivity|antonyms: N/A|noun
