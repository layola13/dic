chaunt:/tʃɑːnt/ /tʃænt/| n. v. |吟诵；单调的重复|A repeated rhythmic phrase, typically one shouted or sung in unison by a crowd.|The crowd chaunted the team's name.  人群反复吟诵着队伍的名字。|Synonyms: chant, chorus, sing, intone|Antonyms: whisper, murmur|noun
chaunt:/tʃɑːnt/ /tʃænt/| n. v. |单调的说话|To speak in a monotonous and repetitive tone.|He chaunted the rules over and over. 他一遍又一遍地单调地重复着规则。|Synonyms: drone, repeat, reiterate|Antonyms: vary, diversify|verb
