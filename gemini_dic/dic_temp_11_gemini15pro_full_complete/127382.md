neanderthaloid:/niːˈændərθəlɔɪd/ | n. adj.| 尼安德特人样的；尼安德特人|A person resembling or likened to a Neanderthal, especially in being perceived as crude, unintelligent, or reactionary.  A Neanderthal-like hominid. |例句：He acted like a neanderthaloid, grunting and refusing to use a fork. 他表现得像个尼安德特人，发出咕哝声，拒绝使用叉子。

|例句：Some people dismiss new technology as unnecessary, but these neanderthaloids are often left behind.  有些人认为新技术是不必要的，但这些顽固不化的人常常会被时代抛弃。|近义词：caveman, primitive, ape, brute |反义词: sophisticated, civilized, modern, advanced |词性：名词，形容词

neanderthaloids:/niːˈændərθəlɔɪdz/ | n. | 尼安德特人样的人；尼安德特人|Plural form of neanderthaloid, referring to multiple individuals resembling or likened to Neanderthals. | 例句：The neanderthaloids in the bar started a brawl. 酒吧里那些粗鲁的家伙开始了一场斗殴。

| 例句：He considered the committee members a bunch of neanderthaloids, resistant to any progressive ideas. 他认为委员会成员是一群尼安德特人，抵制任何进步的思想。 | 近义词：cavemen, primitives, apes, brutes | 反义词: sophisticates, civilized people, modern people | 词性：名词
