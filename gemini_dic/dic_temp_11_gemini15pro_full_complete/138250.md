nosy-parkers:/ˈnəʊzi ˈpɑːkəz/| n.|爱管闲事的人，好事之徒|People who are excessively interested in the affairs of others.  They are often considered intrusive and gossipy.|例句：The nosy-parkers across the street are always watching what we do. 街对面的那些爱管闲事的人总是盯着我们的一举一动。

例句：Don't be such a nosy-parker! It's none of your business. 别这么爱管闲事！这不关你的事。|近义词：busybody, gossip, meddler|反义词:mind their own business|词性:noun
