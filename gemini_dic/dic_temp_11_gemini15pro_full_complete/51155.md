desport:/dɪˈspɔːrt/
verb
娱乐；玩耍；嬉戏
to enjoy oneself; to play or frolic
They desported themselves in the park all afternoon. 他们在公园里玩了一下午。
近义词: amuse, entertain, divert, frolic, play, revel, sport
反义词: toil, labor, work
词性：v.


desport:/dɪˈspɔːrt/
noun
娱乐；玩耍；嬉戏
pastime; amusement; recreation
Their desport was interrupted by the rain.  他们的娱乐活动被雨打断了。
近义词: amusement, entertainment, diversion, pastime, recreation, sport
反义词: work, labor, toil
词性：n.
