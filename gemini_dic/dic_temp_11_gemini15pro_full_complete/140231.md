officiously:/əˈfɪʃəsli/
adjective
爱管闲事地；多管闲事地
in a way that is too eager to tell people what to do or to give them advice
He officiously corrected her grammar. 他多管闲事地纠正她的语法。
officious, meddlesome, interfering
reserved, discreet
adjective
