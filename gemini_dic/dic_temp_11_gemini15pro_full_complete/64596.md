erudition:/ˌɛrʊˈdɪʃən/ | n. | 博学，学识 | deep and extensive learning; scholarship |  He displayed impressive erudition in his lecture on ancient history. 他在古代史讲座上展现了令人印象深刻的博学。 | erudition: profound scholarship, extensive knowledge | ignorance, illiteracy | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *eruditio, from Latin erudire (“to educate, instruct”), from e- (“out, from”) + rudis (“rough, untrained”).
