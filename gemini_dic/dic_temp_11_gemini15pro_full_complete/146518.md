overgentle:/ˈoʊvərˈdʒɛntəl/| adj.|过分温柔的，过于温和的|Excessively gentle; too mild or lenient.|He was overgentle with the unruly children, and they took advantage of him. 他对那些不守规矩的孩子们过于温柔，他们就利用了他。|The overgentle breeze barely stirred the leaves.  过于温和的微风几乎没有吹动树叶。|近义词：too gentle, excessively mild, lenient, indulgent|反义词：harsh, strict, severe, firm|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From 'over-' (meaning "excessively") + 'gentle'.  'Over-' is from Old English 'ofer', and 'gentle' is from Old French 'gentil'.
