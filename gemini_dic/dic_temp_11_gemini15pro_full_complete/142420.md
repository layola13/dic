oryxes:/ˈɔːrɪksɪz/
n.
oryxes（羚羊的复数形式）
Large, heavily built antelopes with long, straight or slightly curved horns.
The oryx lowered its horns as the lion approached.  羚羊在狮子靠近时 lowered its horns。
近义词：antelopes
反义词：（无直接反义词，可考虑 predator 捕食者）
词性：noun
