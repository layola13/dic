outflees:/ˌaʊtˈfliːz/
verb
逃得比…快; 比…逃得更快
Runs away faster than.
The gazelle outflees the lion.  瞪羚比狮子跑得快。
近义词: outruns, escapes, evades
反义词:  is caught by, succumbs to
词性: verb
