disarrangement:/ˌdɪsəˈreɪndʒmənt/ | n. |扰乱，混乱，杂乱 |The disarrangement of the furniture made the room look messy. 家具的杂乱无章使房间看起来很乱。|  The disarrangement of her plans caused her considerable stress. 她计划的被打乱给她带来了相当大的压力。 | 近义词: disorder, chaos, mess, confusion | 反义词: order, arrangement, organization | 词性: 名词

词源: dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "reversal") + arrangement (from Old French "arengement," meaning "putting in order")
