carbonated:/ˈkɑːrbəneɪtɪd/ /ˈkɑːrbənˌeɪtɪd/
adjective
碳酸化的；充碳酸气的
Containing carbon dioxide gas, which makes it bubbly.
例句：I prefer carbonated water to still water. 我喜欢碳酸水胜过静水。

例句：Carbonated drinks can be bad for your teeth. 碳酸饮料可能对你的牙齿不好。

近义词：fizzy, sparkling
反义词：flat, still
词性: adjective
