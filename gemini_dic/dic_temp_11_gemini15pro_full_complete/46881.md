daring:/ˈdɛərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdeərɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|大胆，勇敢；冒险|Adventurous courage; willingness to take risks.|例句：Her daring impressed everyone. 她的勇气给每个人留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：boldness, audacity, bravery, courage|反义词：timidity, cowardice, caution|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *darynge, present participle of daren (“to dare”), from Old English *dearr (“dare”).
