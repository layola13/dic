chameck:/tʃəˈmɛk/| n.|敲诈；骗局；诡计|A trick or swindle; a fraud.|He tried to pull a chameck on me, but I saw through his scheme. 他试图骗我，但我看穿了他的诡计。|近义词：scam, fraud, hoax, trick, swindle, deception, ruse, artifice, wile, dodge|反义词：honesty, integrity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Yiddish  *chamek*, from Hebrew *ḥōméq* (חוֹמֶק), meaning "evasive, elusive".
