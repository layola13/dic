leeward:/ˈliːwərd/ | adj. n.|下风的，背风的；下风处|Situated on or toward the side sheltered from the wind; downwind.|The leeward side of the island is warmer and drier.  岛屿的背风面更加温暖干燥。|The ship drifted leeward. 船漂向下风处。|近义词：downwind, sheltered|反义词：windward|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *leward, from Old English *hlēward, from hlēo (“shelter, lee”) + -ward (“toward”). Cognate with Dutch lijwaarts (“leeward”), German leewärts (“leeward”).
