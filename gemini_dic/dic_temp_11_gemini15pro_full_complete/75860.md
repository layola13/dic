functionalisms:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəlɪzəmz/
n.
功能主义（理论）
Theories that explain something based on its function or role.
Functionalisms in sociology emphasize the interconnectedness of social institutions. 社会学中的功能主义强调社会制度的相互联系。
Anthropological functionalisms examine the purpose of cultural practices. 人类学功能主义考察文化习俗的用途。
近义词: theories, doctrines, principles
反义词: dysfunctions
