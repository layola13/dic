atomistic:/ˌætəˈmɪstɪk/ | adj. | 原子论的；原子式的；个人主义的 |  Relating to or consisting of separate, independent elements or individuals.  Characterized by a focus on individual parts rather than the whole. | The political philosophy promotes an atomistic view of society, where individual rights are paramount.  这种政治哲学提倡一种原子式的社会观，其中个人权利至高无上。|  individualistic, fragmented, particularistic, discrete | holistic, collective, unified | adjective
