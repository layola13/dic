achilles: /əˈkɪliːz/ | n. | 阿基里斯；阿喀琉斯 |  A Greek hero of the Trojan War, the central character of Homer's *Iliad*. He was invulnerable except for his heel. |  Achilles was a great warrior. 阿基里斯是一位伟大的战士。 |  Synonyms: hero, warrior | Antonyms: coward, weakling | noun

achilles tendon: /əˈkɪliːz ˈtendən/ | n. | 跟腱 | The strong tendon joining the muscles in the calf of the leg to the heel bone. |  He ruptured his Achilles tendon playing basketball. 他打篮球时跟腱断裂了。 | Synonyms: heel cord | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun
