docents:/ˈdoʊsənts/| n.|讲解员（复数）|Plural form of docent, referring to multiple people who act as guides in a museum, art gallery, or zoo.|The docents were well-versed in the museum's collection.|近义词：guides, instructors, teachers|反义词: students, pupils|noun
