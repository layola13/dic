grunch:/ɡrʌntʃ/ | n. |抱怨；牢骚；不满 | A complaint or grumble. | He let out a grunch about the long hours. 他抱怨工作时间太长。 | 近义词: complaint, grumble, grievance, moan | 反义词: praise, compliment | 词性:名词

词源:  Possibly a blend of "grunt" and "crunch," mimicking the sound of a complaint. 也许是“grunt”（咕哝）和“crunch”（嘎吱作响）的混合，模仿抱怨的声音。
