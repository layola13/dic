hastinesses:/ˈheɪstɪnɪsɪz/
|n.|仓促，草率，轻率|The state of being hasty; undue speed; rashness.  A tendency to act too quickly without sufficient thought or consideration.|His hastinesses in making decisions often led to mistakes. 他做决定时的草率经常导致错误。|近义词：rashness, precipitateness, impetuosity, hurry, hustle|反义词：carefulness, deliberation, caution, patience|词性:名词
