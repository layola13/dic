greed:/ɡriːd/| n.|贪婪，贪心|Intense and selfish desire for something, especially wealth, power, or food.|His greed knows no bounds. 他的贪婪没有限度。|近义词：avarice, covetousness, rapacity|反义词：generosity, altruism|词性: 名词
Etymology: From Old English *grǣdiġ, from grǣdig "greedy," from Proto-Germanic *grēdigaz, from *grōd- "to grow."  Relatives include Dutch gretig "eager" and German gierig "greedy, eager."
