bauble:/ˈbɔːbl/ (US) /ˈbɔːbəl/ (UK)| n. | 小玩意儿；廉价而俗丽的饰品 | A small, showy trinket or ornament, often cheap or worthless. |  She wore a necklace of brightly coloured baubles. 她戴着一条色彩鲜艳的小玩意儿项链。

The Christmas tree was decorated with tinsel and baubles. 圣诞树上装饰着金银丝和一些小饰品。 | 近义词：trinket, ornament, knick-knack, gewgaw, trifle | 反义词: treasure, heirloom | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *baube*, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to *babel*.  The word originally referred to a child's toy, and later came to mean any showy but cheap ornament.
