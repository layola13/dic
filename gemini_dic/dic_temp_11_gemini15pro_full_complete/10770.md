aqualung: /ˈækwəlʌŋ/ | n. | 水肺；水中呼吸器 | An apparatus containing compressed air, worn by divers for breathing underwater. |  He strapped on his aqualung and plunged into the deep blue sea. 他背上水肺，跳入深蓝的大海。

|  The aqualung allowed her to explore the coral reef for hours.  水肺让她能够探索珊瑚礁数小时。| 近义词: scuba, diving apparatus | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "snorkel") | 词性: noun
