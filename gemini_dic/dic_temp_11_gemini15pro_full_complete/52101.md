diametrically:/ˌdaɪəˈmɛtrɪkli/ | adv. | 完全地，截然地 | completely opposed; at opposite extremes |  The two candidates hold diametrically opposed views on the issue.  两位候选人对这个问题持完全相反的观点。| synonyms: absolutely, completely, totally, utterly, entirely | antonyms: partially, slightly, somewhat | adverb

diametrically:/ˌdaɪəˈmɛtrɪkli/ | adv. |  正好相反地 |  in direct opposition; at opposite poles |  Their interpretations of the poem were diametrically opposed. 他们对这首诗的理解截然相反。 | synonyms: oppositely, contrarily, inversely | antonyms: similarly, likewise | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diametralis, from Latin diametros (“diameter”), from Ancient Greek διάμετρος (diámetros, “diameter”), from διά (diá, “through, across”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
