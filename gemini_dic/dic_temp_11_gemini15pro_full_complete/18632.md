ETYMOLOGY->
From Karl Benz (1844-1929), German engineer and automobile pioneer, founder of Benz & Cie. which later became part of Daimler-Benz and then Daimler AG.  The chemical prefix "benz-" comes from "benzo-", ultimately from the Arabic "lubān jāwī" meaning "Javan frankincense" from which benzoic acid was derived.
