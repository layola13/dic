holmiums:/ˈhoʊlmiəmz/ | n. |钬 |A chemical element (symbol Ho) with atomic number 67, belonging to the lanthanide series. It is a silvery-white metal that is relatively soft and malleable. | Holmiums are used in some specialized lasers and magnets. 钬用于一些特殊的激光器和磁铁。| synonym:  lanthanide, rare earth element | antonym: N/A | noun
