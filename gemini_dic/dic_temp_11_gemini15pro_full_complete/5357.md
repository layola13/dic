allurers:/əˈlʊrərz/| n. |诱惑者；引诱者|People who attract others by seeming glamorous or exciting.  Those who tempt or entice.|例句：The city's allurers promised wealth and fame. 这座城市的诱惑者们承诺财富和名望。

例句：The allurers of the gambling world often lead people to ruin. 赌博世界的诱惑者常常导致人们走向毁灭。|近义词：tempters, enticers, seducers, charmers|反义词：deterrents|词性: 名词(plural)
