capital-intensive:/ˈkæpɪtlɪnˈtɛnsɪv/
adjective
资本密集型的
Requiring a large amount of capital investment relative to labor input.  Industries like manufacturing and oil exploration are often capital-intensive.
The steel industry is extremely capital-intensive. 钢铁行业是极其资本密集型的。
Building a new semiconductor factory is a capital-intensive undertaking. 建造一个新的半导体工厂是一项资本密集型的事业。

近义词:capital-heavy, high-capital
反义词:labor-intensive
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "capital" (meaning accumulated wealth used for investment) + "intensive" (meaning characterized by a high degree of concentration or effort).
