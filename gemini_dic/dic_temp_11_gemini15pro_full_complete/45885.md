cutest:/ˈkjuːtɪst/| adj.|最可爱的|Superlative form of cute: most cute.  Used to describe something or someone as being extremely charming, delightful, and appealing, often in a way that evokes feelings of tenderness or affection.|That puppy is the cutest thing I've ever seen! 那只小狗是我见过最可爱的东西！|The baby's smile is just the cutest. 宝宝的笑容真是太可爱了。|adorable, charming, delightful, sweet, lovely|ugly, unpleasant, hideous|adjective
