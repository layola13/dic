immixable:/ɪmˈɪksəbl/
adjective
不可混合的
Not mixable; incapable of being mixed.
Oil and water are immiscible liquids. 油和水是不可混合的液体。
The two chemicals proved immixable, remaining separate layers even after vigorous shaking. 这两种化学物质被证明是不可混合的，即使剧烈摇晃后仍然保持分离层。
近义词: immiscible, unmixable, non-miscible
反义词: miscible, mixable
词性: adjective

immixable:/ɪmˈɪksəbl/
词源: From in- (not) + miscible (capable of being mixed), from Latin miscere (to mix).
词性: adjective
