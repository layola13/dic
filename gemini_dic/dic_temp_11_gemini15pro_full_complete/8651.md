anticonservationist:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪst/
|n.|反自然保护主义者；反对环境保护者|Someone who opposes environmental conservation efforts.|The anticonservationist lobby argued against the new national park proposal. 反对环境保护的游说团体反对新的国家公园提案。|
|近义词: environmental opponent, anti-environmentalist|反义词: conservationist, environmentalist|词性: 名词|
