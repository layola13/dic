filtration's:/fɪlˈtreɪʃənz/
noun
过滤，滤清
The process of separating solids from liquids or gases by passing the mixture through a filter.
The filtration's efficiency depends on the size of the pores in the filter.  这种过滤的效率取决于过滤器中孔隙的大小。
filtration systems are crucial for clean drinking water. 过滤系统对于清洁饮用水至关重要。
filtering, purification, screening
contamination, pollution
n.
