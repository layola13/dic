highlife:/ˈhaɪlaɪf/| n.| 高级生活；上流社会的生活；西非流行音乐的一种|A style of popular dance music featuring jazzy horns and guitars, originating in Ghana.|They went to the club to listen to some highlife. 他们去俱乐部听一些西非流行音乐。|Synonyms: luxury, opulence, extravagance|Antonyms: poverty, hardship, destitution|noun
