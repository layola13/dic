locomotive:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊtɪv/ | n. adj.| 火车头；机车；运动的 |A powered rail vehicle used for pulling trains.  Relating to locomotion; capable of movement.| The old steam locomotive was beautifully restored.  这台古老的蒸汽机车被精美地修复了。 |The locomotive power of the engine is impressive.  这台发动机的动力令人印象深刻。| synonyms: engine, train, motor | antonyms: stationary, immobile | noun, adjective
