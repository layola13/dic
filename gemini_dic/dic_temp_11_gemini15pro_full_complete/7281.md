angrier:/ˈæŋɡriər/| adj.|更生气的，更愤怒的|Comparative form of "angry".  Indicates a greater degree of anger than "angry". |She was angrier than I expected. 她比我预期的要生气。|He became angrier with each passing minute.  随着每一分钟的过去，他变得越来越生气。|近义词：madder, more furious, more irritated|反义词：calmer, happier, more peaceful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *angrier, *angrere, comparative of angry, from Old Norse *angrari, comparative of angr (“grief, sorrow, anguish, anger”).
