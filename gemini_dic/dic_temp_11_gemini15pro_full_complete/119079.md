metreless:/ˈmiːtərləs/
adjective
无韵律的，没有格律的
Lacking metrical structure or rhythm; not in verse form.
His metreless poems were often criticized for their lack of form.  他无韵律的诗歌经常因缺乏形式而受到批评。
The writer experimented with metreless prose poetry.  这位作家尝试了无韵律的散文诗。
近义词:prosaic, unmetrical, arrhythmic
反义词:metrical, rhythmic, poetic
词性:adjective
