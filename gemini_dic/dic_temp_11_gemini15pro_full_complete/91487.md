hymenopter:/ˌhaɪmɪˈnɒptər/ (US) /ˌhaɪmɪˈnɒptə(r)/ (UK)| n. |膜翅目昆虫|An insect of the order Hymenoptera, including bees, wasps, ants, and sawflies, characterized by two pairs of membranous wings.|例句：The hymenopter stung the unsuspecting hiker.  膜翅目昆虫蛰了毫无戒心的徒步旅行者。

例句：Bees are a well-known type of hymenopter. 蜜蜂是一种著名的膜翅目昆虫。|近义词：insect|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


hymenopteran:/ˌhaɪmɪˈnɒptərən/ (US) /ˌhaɪmɪˈnɒptərən/ (UK)| adj. |膜翅目的|Relating to or belonging to the order Hymenoptera.|例句：The hymenopteran wings shimmered in the sunlight. 膜翅目昆虫的翅膀在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：The scientist specialized in hymenopteran behavior.  这位科学家专门研究膜翅目昆虫的行为。|近义词：insectan |反义词: N/A|词性:adjective


词源: From Greek *hymēn* "membrane" + *pterón* "wing."
