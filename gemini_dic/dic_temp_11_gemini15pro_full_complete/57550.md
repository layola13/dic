draki:/ˈdrɑːki/| n.|龙形风筝; (Drakis的复数)|A draki is a Fijian kite, often large and colourful, traditionally flown during Easter.|例句：The draki soared high above the beach. 龙形风筝在沙滩上空翱翔。

例句：Children were flying drakis in the park. 孩子们在公园里放龙形风筝。|近义词：kite|反义词:None|词性:noun
