determinable:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnəbl/
adjective
可确定的，可决定的
capable of being determined, ascertained, or established.
The exact date of the meeting is determinable. 会议的确切日期是可以确定的。
The cause of the fire was quickly determinable. 火灾的原因很快就能确定。
近义词: ascertainable, definable, calculable
反义词: indeterminable, uncertain, vague
词性: adjective
