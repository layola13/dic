dissuade:/dɪˈsweɪd/
verb
劝阻，阻止
To persuade someone not to do something.
He tried to dissuade her from leaving. 他试图劝她不要离开。
dissuade somebody from doing something
近义词:deter, discourage, advise against
反义词:persuade, encourage, urge
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dissuadere, from dis- "apart, away" + suadere "to advise, urge."
