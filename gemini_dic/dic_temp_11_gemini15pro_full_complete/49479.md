delusion:/dɪˈluːʒən/ | n. | 妄想，错觉 | A fixed false belief that is not based in reality. | He suffers from delusions of grandeur, believing he is a king. 他患有夸大妄想症，认为自己是个国王。 |  She had delusions that she was being followed. 她有被人跟踪的妄想。 | illusion, hallucination, misconception | reality, truth | noun

delusional:/dɪˈluːʒənl/ | adj. | 妄想的，错觉的 | Characterized by or holding delusions. | He became delusional after the accident. 事故发生后，他开始出现妄想。| The patient's delusional thinking made it difficult to treat him. 病人妄想的思维方式使治疗变得困难。|  hallucinatory, psychotic | rational, sane | adjective
