circumscriptively:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskrɪptɪvli/
adverb
限制地，限定地
in a way that limits or restricts something; narrowly
He spoke circumscriptively about the incident, avoiding any details that might incriminate him. 他对这起事件的描述很有限，避免提及任何可能让他入罪的细节。
Synonyms: narrowly, restrictively, limitedly, confinedly
Antonyms: broadly, widely, extensively
词性: 副词
