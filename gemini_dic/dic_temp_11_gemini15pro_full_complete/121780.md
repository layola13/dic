misrecital:/ˌmɪsrɪˈsaɪtl/
n.
错误的叙述；误述
an incorrect or inaccurate account of something, especially something said or written
The lawyer pointed out the misrecital of the facts in the witness's testimony.  律师指出了证人证词中对事实的误述。

近义词: misstatement, misrepresentation, falsification, distortion
反义词: accurate account, correct statement, truthful representation

词性: noun

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning wrong or bad) + "recital" (from Old French "reciter" and Latin "recitare," meaning to read aloud or repeat)
