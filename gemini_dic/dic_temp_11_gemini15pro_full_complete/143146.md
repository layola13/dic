outcept:/aʊtˈsɛpt/ | prep. conj.| 除...之外 | Except. Primarily a dialectal or nonstandard variant of "except." | Outcept for the rain, we had a great time.  除了下雨之外，我们玩得很开心。 | 近义词: except, barring, but, save, excluding | 反义词: including | 词性: 介词, 连词


Etymology:
From Middle English *outtake, from out- + take.  Influenced by except.
