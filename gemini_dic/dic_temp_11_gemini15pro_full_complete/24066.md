brenningly:/ˈbrɛnɪŋli/| adv.|燃烧地，炽热地|In a burning manner; intensely; fervently.  Describes something done with great passion or intensity.|She stared at him brenningly, her eyes filled with unspoken desire. 她燃烧般地盯着他，眼里充满了无法言喻的渴望。|Example: He argued brenningly for his beliefs. 他热情地为自己的信仰辩护。|Synonyms: intensely, passionately, fervently, ardently, fiercely|Antonyms: apathetically, indifferently, coldly|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From burning + -ly. Burning from burn, from Old English *bærnan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch branden and German brennen.
