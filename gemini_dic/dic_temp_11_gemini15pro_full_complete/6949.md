andalusite:/ˈændəlʊsaɪt/
noun
红柱石
A reddish-brown or green mineral, aluminum silicate, Al2SiO5, that occurs in metamorphic rocks.
Andalusite is often used in the manufacture of high-temperature ceramics. 红柱石常用于制造高温陶瓷。
Synonyms:chiastolite, silicate
Antonyms:N/A
