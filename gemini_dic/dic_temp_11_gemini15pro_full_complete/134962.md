nonnationalizations:/ˌnɒnˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/  (US: /ˌnɑːnˌnæʃənələˈzeɪʃənz/)
| n. | 非国有化 | The act or process of reversing nationalization, returning businesses or industries from state ownership to private control. |  The government pursued a policy of nonnationalizations, selling off state-owned enterprises. 政府奉行非国有化政策，出售国有企业。 | denationalization, privatization | nationalization | noun
