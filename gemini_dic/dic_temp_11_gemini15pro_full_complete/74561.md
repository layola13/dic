The company was accused of fraudulency in its advertising practices. 这家公司被指控在其广告行为中存在欺诈。|近义词：fraud, deceit, dishonesty, deception, chicanery|反义词：honesty, integrity, truthfulness|词性: 名词


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French fraude + -ency.
