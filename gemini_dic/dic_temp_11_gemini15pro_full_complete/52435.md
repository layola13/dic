didynamia: /ˌdaɪdɪˈneɪmiə/ /ˌdɪdɪˈneɪmiə/
| n. |二强雄蕊；双雄蕊|A class of plants in the Linnaean system having two long and two short stamens. |Didynamia is a term used in botany to classify plants with two long and two short stamens.|Linnaeus's classification system included the class Didynamia. 林奈的分类系统包括二强雄蕊纲。|Didynamia is a key characteristic of the mint family. 二强雄蕊是薄荷科植物的一个关键特征。|tetradynamous| - |noun
