all-hands:/ˌɔːlˈhændz/ | adj. |全体人员的，全体动手的 | Involving all the people who work in a particular place or at a particular job. | The CEO called an all-hands meeting to discuss the company's future. 首席执行官召集全体员工大会讨论公司的未来。| The company held an all-hands meeting to announce the merger. 公司召开全体员工大会宣布合并。 | Synonyms: company-wide, general, involving everyone | Antonyms: select, exclusive, limited | adjective
