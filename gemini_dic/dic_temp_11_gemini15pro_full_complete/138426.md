nourish:/ˈnɜːrɪʃ/ /ˈnʌrɪʃ/| vt.|滋养，养育；培养，助长|To provide with the food or other substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition.|The soil in this area nourishes a wide variety of plants.  这个地区的土壤滋养了各种各样的植物。|近义词：feed, sustain, nurture|反义词：starve, deprive|verb

nourishment:/ˈnɜːrɪʃmənt/ /ˈnʌrɪʃmənt/| n.|营养，滋养；养料|Food or other substances that provide the body with the nutrients it needs for growth and health.|Young children need plenty of nourishment to grow strong and healthy.  幼儿需要大量的营养才能健康成长。|近义词：food, sustenance, nutrition|反义词：deprivation, starvation|noun
