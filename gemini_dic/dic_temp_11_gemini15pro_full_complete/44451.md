cross-street: /ˈkrɔːs striːt/ (American) /ˈkrɒs striːt/ (British)
n.|十字街; 交叉路; 交叉街道|A street that intersects another street.  The street that crosses another specified street.|例句：The cross-street is Main Street. 十字街是主街。|例句：The restaurant is located at the cross-street of Elm and Oak. 这家餐厅位于榆树街和橡树街的交叉口。|近义词：intersection, crossroad|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词
