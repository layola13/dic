expulsion:/ɪkˈspʌlʃən/
n.|驱逐，排出，开除|The expulsion of the diplomat caused an international incident.  外交官的驱逐引发了一场国际事件。|The expulsion of air from the lungs is a vital part of respiration.  空气从肺部的排出是呼吸的重要组成部分。|近义词：removal, ejection, dismissal, discharge|反义词：admission, acceptance, inclusion|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin expulsiōn- (stem of expulsiō), from Latin expulsus, past participle of expellere ‘drive out’, from ex- ‘out’ + pellere ‘to push, drive’.
