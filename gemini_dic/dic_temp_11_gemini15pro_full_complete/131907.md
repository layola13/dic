noncontextualized:/ˌnɑːnkənˈtekstʃuəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 非语境化的，脱离上下文的 |  Not placed within or related to a particular context; lacking context.  | The study presented noncontextualized vocabulary lists, making it difficult for students to truly grasp the meaning of the words. 这项研究提供了非语境化的词汇列表，让学生难以真正掌握单词的含义。

The example sentence he gave was noncontextualized and therefore difficult to understand. 他给的例句脱离了上下文，因此很难理解。| 近义词: decontextualized, abstract, isolated | 反义词: contextualized, situated, embedded | 词性: adjective
