copycatted:/ˈkɒpiˌkætɪd/ | v. | 模仿;抄袭; |  Meaning: having been imitated or copied, especially in a slavish or unoriginal way.  Derived from the noun "copycat" and the past participle suffix "-ed". | He copycatted my project and handed it in as his own.  他抄袭了我的项目，并把它当作自己的交上去了。 |  Synonyms: imitated, emulated, duplicated, cloned, mirrored,  | Antonyms: original, innovative, unique, novel, groundbreaking | Verb
