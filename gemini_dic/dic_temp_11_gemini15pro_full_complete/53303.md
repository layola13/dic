discontentedly:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪdli/
| adv. |不满地，不高兴地|In a manner expressing dissatisfaction or lack of contentment.  Showing unhappiness or displeasure.|He sighed discontentedly and shuffled his feet. 他不满地叹了口气，拖着脚走。|Synonym: unhappily, grumblingly, sullenly, resentfully|Antonym: contentedly, happily, cheerfully|adverb

discontent:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt/
| n. |不满，不满足|Lack of contentment; dissatisfaction.|There was widespread discontent among the workers. 工人们普遍不满。|Synonym: dissatisfaction, unhappiness, disaffection|Antonym: contentment, satisfaction, happiness|noun

discontented:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪd/
| adj. |不满的，不满足的|Not content; dissatisfied.|She was discontented with her job and wanted a change. 她对自己的工作不满意，想要换工作。|Synonym: dissatisfied, unhappy, restless|Antonym: content, satisfied, happy|adjective
