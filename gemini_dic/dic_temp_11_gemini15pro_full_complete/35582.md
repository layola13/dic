coanchored:/koʊˈæŋkərd/
adjective
共同锚定的，共同定位的
Two genes are said to be coanchored if they are located close together on the same chromosome and tend to be inherited together.  如果两个基因位于同一条染色体上并且往往一起遗传，则称它们为共同锚定的。
The study identified several coanchored genes associated with the disease. 这项研究确定了几个与该疾病相关的共同锚定的基因。
Near synonyms: linked, associated, coupled
Antonyms: unlinked, independent, separate
词性: adjective
