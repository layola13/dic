oversoak: /ˌoʊvərˈsoʊk/
verb
过度浸泡，浸泡过久
to soak something for too long a time
I oversoaked the beans and they turned to mush. 我把豆子泡得太久了，它们都变成糊状了。
The dress was oversoaked and the color ran.  这件衣服浸泡时间过长，颜色都褪掉了。

近义词: soak, drench, saturate
反义词: dehydrate, dry
词性: verb
