nontenurial:/ˌnɒnˈtɛnjʊəriəl/ | adj. | 非保有土地的，非土地所有制的 |  Relating to or denoting a system of land ownership in which the owner does not have full rights over the land, especially the right to sell it. Derived from "non-" (not) + "tenurial" (relating to the holding of land). |  The community lived under a nontenurial system, meaning they could farm the land but not sell it.  这个社区生活在一个非土地所有制体系下，这意味着他们可以耕种土地，但不能出售。 |  nonfreehold | freehold | adjective
