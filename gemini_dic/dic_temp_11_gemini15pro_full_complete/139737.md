ochre /ˈoʊkər/ (US) /ˈəʊkə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.|赭石，赭色|A natural earthy pigment containing hydrated iron oxide, ranging in colour from light yellow to deep orange or brown.  As an adjective, it describes something having this colour.|例句：The walls were painted a deep ochre. 墙壁被漆成了深赭色。|近义词：yellow, orange, brown, sienna, umber|反义词:blue, green, violet|词性:名词, 形容词

ochrier /ˈoʊkriər/ (US) /ˈəʊkriə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更赭的，赭色更深的|More ochre in color; having a deeper or more intense ochre hue.|例句：The sunset was an ochrier shade than usual.  日落比平常的颜色更偏赭色。|近义词：darker ochre, deeper ochre, more orange-brown|反义词:paler, lighter, less ochre|词性:形容词
