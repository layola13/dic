mandor:/ˈmændɔːr/ (US), /ˈmændɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|工头，领班|A mandor is a foreman or overseer, particularly in Southeast Asia, especially in Indonesia and Malaysia.  They supervise a group of workers, often in agriculture or construction.|The mandor instructed the workers to start harvesting the rice. 工头指示工人开始收割稻谷。|Synonym: foreman, supervisor, overseer|Antonym: worker, laborer|Noun
