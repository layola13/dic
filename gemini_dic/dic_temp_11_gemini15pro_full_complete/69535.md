fellfare:/ˈfɛlfɛər/ | n. | 田鸫 | A large thrush (Turdus pilaris) that breeds in northern Eurasia and winters farther south, often in flocks.  It has a grey head, back, and rump, a reddish-brown breast, and black-tipped wings and tail. |  例句：A large flock of fellfares descended on the berry bushes. 一大群田鸫飞落到浆果丛上。

例句：The fellfare's distinctive call echoed through the woods. 田鸫独特的叫声在树林中回荡。 | 近义词：thrush, fieldfare | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
