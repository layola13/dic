disintegrator:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪtər/| n.|分解器，粉碎机；崩解剂|A machine that breaks down or disintegrates something into small particles.  A substance that causes something to disintegrate.|The scientist used a disintegrator to pulverize the rock sample. 科学家用分解器将岩石样品粉碎。|Synonyms: pulverizer, grinder, crusher, atomizer|Antonyms: integrator, assembler, unifier|noun

disintegrate:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪt/| v.|分解，瓦解，碎裂|To break up into small parts or fragments; to decay or decompose.|The old documents had disintegrated over time.  旧文件随着时间的推移已经碎裂了。|Synonyms: crumble, decompose, decay, fall apart|Antonyms: integrate, combine, unite|verb
