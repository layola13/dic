needled:/ˈniːdld/| v. |用针刺；(比喻) 刺激，激怒；不断地烦扰|He needled her about her weight. 他不断地取笑她的体重。|近义词：goaded, provoked, irritated, annoyed, pestered|反义词: soothed, calmed, placated|词性:动词

Etymology->
From needle + -ed.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *nēdlō ("needle").
