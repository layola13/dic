epigrammatic:/ˌepɪɡrəˈmætɪk/
adjective
警句的；格言式的；简洁精辟的
Concise and witty, often paradoxical or satirical.  Characterized by the use of epigrams.
His writing style is epigrammatic and memorable. 他的写作风格简洁精辟，令人难忘。

Her epigrammatic comments were always on point. 她简洁精辟的评论总是切中要害。

近义词: pithy, concise, succinct, terse, aphoristic
反义词: prolix, verbose, rambling, lengthy, diffuse
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *epigramma* "inscription," literally "on writing," from *epi* "on, upon" + *gramma* "letter, piece of writing."
