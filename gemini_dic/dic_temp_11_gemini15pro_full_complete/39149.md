confutant:/kənˈfjutənt/| n. |驳斥者，反驳者|Someone who confutes or opposes another in argument.|He was a skilled confutant, dismantling his opponent's arguments with ease.|近义词：opponent, adversary, contradictor|反义词:proponent, supporter|词性:noun
Etymology:  From Late Latin *confūtāns*, present participle of confūtāre (“to confute”), from con- (“together, with”) + futāre (“to check, blame, disprove”), probably from an unattested noun *fūtis* (“chatter, silliness”).
