dollarise:/ˈdɒləraɪz/ (British) /ˈdɑːləraɪz/ (American)| v. |美元化，以美元计价|To convert a country's currency or economy to the US dollar.  The process of aligning a country's currency or economy with the US dollar.|The government decided to dollarise the economy. 政府决定将经济美元化。| The country dollarised its economy in 2001. 这个国家在2001年实现了经济美元化。|Synonyms: monetize, denominate in dollars|Antonyms: demonetarize, de-dollarise|verb
