coproduces:/ˌkoʊprəˈdjuːsɪz/
verb
共同制作；联合出品
To produce something together with another or other people.
The film was coproduced by France and Italy. 这部电影是由法国和意大利联合出品的。
synonyms: co-create, collaborate on, jointly produce
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "produce alone" or "solely produce")
词性: v.
