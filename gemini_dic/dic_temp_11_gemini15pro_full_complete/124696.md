mow-down:/ˈmoʊ daʊn/| v. |击倒，摧毁，杀死|To kill or injure a large number of people or things suddenly and violently, especially with gunfire or a vehicle. |The gunman mowed down shoppers in the mall. 枪手在商场里扫射了购物者。

The car mowed down several pedestrians. 汽车撞倒了几个行人。|近义词：slaughter, massacre, decimate|反义词: protect, save|词性:动词
