bandit:/ˈbændɪt/| n.|强盗，土匪|A robber, especially one belonging to a gang and typically operating in an isolated or lawless area.  A person who behaves dishonestly or unfairly in order to get money.|The bandits held up the stagecoach.|近义词：robber, outlaw, highwayman, brigand, desperado|反义词:lawman, police officer|noun
