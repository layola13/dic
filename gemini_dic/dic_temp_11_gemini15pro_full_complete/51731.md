devoicing:/diːˈvɔɪsɪŋ/
noun
浊音清化；去嗓音化
The process of changing a voiced sound to a voiceless one, typically in a particular phonetic context.
In German, final obstruents undergo devoicing. 在德语中，词尾的阻塞音会发生浊音清化。
近义词: unvoicing
反义词: voicing
词性: n.

Etymology: de- + voicing.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse," and "voicing" refers to the vibration of the vocal cords during speech.
