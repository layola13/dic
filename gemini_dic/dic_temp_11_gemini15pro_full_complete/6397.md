amphimacer:/æmˈfɪməsər/ | n. | 长短长格 | A metrical foot in poetry consisting of three syllables: a long or stressed syllable followed by a short or unstressed syllable, and then another long or stressed syllable. |  Classical Greek and Latin poetry utilized the amphimacer. 古典希腊语和拉丁语诗歌使用了长短长格。 | 近义词: cretic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a metrical foot) | 词性: noun
