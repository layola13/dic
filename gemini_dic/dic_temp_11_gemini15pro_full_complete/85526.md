hatbands:/ˈhætˌbændz/
n.
帽带；帽箍
A band of material encircling the crown of a hat.
The cowboy adjusted his hatbands. 牛仔调整了一下他的帽带。
近义词: hat ribbon, hat strap
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
