deflated:/dɪˈfleɪtɪd/
adjective
泄气的；灰心的；瘪了的
Reduced in size or importance; emptied of air or gas.
The deflated balloon lay on the floor.  瘪了的气球躺在地板上。
The team was deflated after their loss. 球队输球后士气低落。
Synonyms: discouraged, disheartened, depressed, flat, collapsed
Antonyms: inflated, encouraged, elated, buoyant
词性: adjective


deflate:/dɪˈfleɪt/
verb
放气；使泄气；使灰心
To release air or gas from; to reduce in size or importance.
He deflated the tires before storing the car. 他在存放汽车之前把轮胎放了气。
The criticism deflated her ego. 批评打击了她的自信心。
Synonyms: flatten, collapse, puncture, discourage
Antonyms: inflate, encourage, boost
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deflāre*, from *de-* (down, away from) + *flāre* (to blow).
