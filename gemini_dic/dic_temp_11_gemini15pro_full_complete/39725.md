consimility:/ˌkɒnsɪˈmɪlɪti/ | n. |相似性，类似 |Resemblance; similarity.  A state of being similar; likeness. |The consimility between the twins was striking.  这对双胞胎之间的相似之处非常惊人。| 近义词：similarity, resemblance, likeness, similitude, analogy | 反义词：dissimilarity, difference, unlikeness | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *consimilitās, from Latin consimilis (“very similar”), from con- (“together”) + similis (“like”).
