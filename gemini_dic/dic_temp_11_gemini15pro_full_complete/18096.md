behaviouristic:/bɪˈheɪvjərɪstɪk/
adjective
行为主义的
Relating to behaviourism, a school of psychology that focuses on observable behaviours rather than internal mental states.
Behaviouristic psychology emphasizes the role of environmental factors in shaping behaviour.  行为主义心理学强调环境因素在塑造行为中的作用。
His approach to teaching was strictly behaviouristic, focusing on rewards and punishments. 他的教学方法严格遵循行为主义，注重奖励和惩罚。
近义词:behavioral
反义词:cognitive, mentalistic
词性:adjective
