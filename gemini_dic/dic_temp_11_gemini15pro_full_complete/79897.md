gnattiest:/ˈnætiɪst/
adjective
最狡猾的；最精明的
most shrewd or astute, especially in a tricky or underhanded way.
That gnattiest lawyer got his client off on a technicality. 那位最狡猾的律师利用技术细节使他的客户脱罪。
synonyms: shrewdest, sharpest, craftiest, wiliest, slickest, trickiest
antonyms: naive, gullible, ingenuous
词性: 形容词
