elementoid:/ˌɛlɪˈmɛntɔɪd/
noun
类元素; 似元素之物
Resembling or having the properties of an element; element-like.  Something that resembles an element, but isn't a true chemical element.
Scientists discovered a new elementoid with unique properties. 科学家发现了一种具有独特属性的新的类元素。
analogue, substitute
element, component
n.
