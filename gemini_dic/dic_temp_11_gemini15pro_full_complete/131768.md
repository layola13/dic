nonconjugates:/nɒnˈkɒndʒʊɡeɪts/| n.| 非共轭物|Substances, especially in chemistry or biochemistry, that do not undergo conjugation (a chemical reaction where two molecules combine).  This usually refers to molecules that don't change their form readily in certain reactions. |The experiment focused on isolating the nonconjugates from the conjugated proteins.|近义词:nonconjugated, unreacted, unchanged (depending on context)|反义词:conjugates, reacted, changed (depending on context)|noun
