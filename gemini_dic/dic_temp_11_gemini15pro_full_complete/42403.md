counterclaimant:/ˈkaʊntərˌkleɪmənt/ | n. | 反诉人，反请求人 | A person who makes a counterclaim (a claim made in opposition to another claim in a legal action). |  The counterclaimant argued that the original plaintiff was actually at fault. 反诉人辩称最初的原告实际上有过错。

The counterclaimant filed a response to the original lawsuit. 反诉人对最初的诉讼提交了答辩。 | Synonym: counterplaintiff | Antonym: claimant, plaintiff | noun
