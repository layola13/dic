adjudicator:/əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtər/| n.|裁决者，评判员，裁判员|A person who makes a formal judgment or decision about a problem or disputed matter.|The adjudicators awarded the first prize to the young violinist.|Synonyms: judge, arbiter, referee, umpire, examiner, assessor|Antonyms: defendant, plaintiff|noun

adjudicator:/əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtər/| n.|裁定者，判决者|Someone who officially decides who is right in a disagreement or competition.|The adjudicators listened carefully to both sides of the argument.|Synonyms: judge, arbiter, referee, umpire, examiner, assessor|Antonyms: defendant, plaintiff|noun
