divulgences:/daɪˈvʌldʒənsɪz/
|n.|泄露，透露，公开|The act of revealing private or sensitive information.|The recent divulgences about the company's financial troubles have caused its stock price to plummet. 最近该公司财务困境的披露导致其股价暴跌。|近义词：disclosures, revelations, leaks|反义词：concealments, suppressions|词性:名词

divulgence:/daɪˈvʌldʒəns/
|n.|泄露，透露，公开|The act of revealing private or sensitive information.|The divulgence of the secret documents caused a scandal. 秘密文件的泄露引发了一场丑闻。|近义词：disclosure, revelation, leak|反义词：concealment, suppression|词性:名词
