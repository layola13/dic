backlit:/ˌbækˈlɪt/ | adj. | 背光的；逆光的 | Illuminated from behind, creating a silhouette or highlighting the subject. | The backlit photo made her hair look like a halo. 这张逆光照片使她的头发看起来像个光环。

The photographer used a backlit setting to create a dramatic effect. 摄影师使用逆光设置来营造戏剧效果。 | 近义词: illuminated, highlighted,  | 反义词: frontlit, shadowless | 词性: adjective
