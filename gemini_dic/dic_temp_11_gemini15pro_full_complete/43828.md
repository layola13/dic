creolizing:/ˈkriːəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
克里奥尔化；混合化
The process by which a pidgin language becomes a creole language, a mother tongue.  The process of cultural mixing and adaptation, especially in language.
例句：The creolizing of languages in the Caribbean resulted in a rich tapestry of dialects. 加勒比地区语言的克里奥尔化形成了丰富的方言体系。

例句：The music of New Orleans is a great example of creolizing, blending European, African, and Caribbean influences. 新奥尔良的音乐是克里奥尔化的一个很好的例子，它融合了欧洲、非洲和加勒比海的影响。
近义词：blending, mixing, hybridizing, merging
反义词：purifying, separating, isolating
词性 v.
