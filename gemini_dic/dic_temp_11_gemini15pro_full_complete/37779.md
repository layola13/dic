compatriot:/kəmˈpeɪtriət/ /kəmˈpæt.ri.ət/ | n. |同胞，同国人| A person from the same country. |例句：She felt a strong sense of camaraderie with her compatriots abroad.  她在国外与同胞们有一种强烈的友情。

例句：He was proud to represent his compatriots at the international competition. 他很自豪能在国际比赛中代表他的同胞。|近义词：countryman, fellow citizen|反义词：foreigner, alien|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *compatriote*, from Late Latin *compatriōta*, from Latin *com-* (together) + *patriōta* (fellow countryman), from *patria* (native land, fatherland).  Ultimately from Latin *pater* (father).
