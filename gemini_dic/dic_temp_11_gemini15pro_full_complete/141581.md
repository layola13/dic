oppressingly:/əˈpresivli/ | adv. | 压抑地；沉重地；令人窒息地 | In a way that is oppressive; so as to cause someone to feel worried and uncomfortable | The atmosphere in the room was oppressingly silent. 房间里的气氛压抑得令人窒息。| 近义词: heavily, stiflingly, suffocatingly, overwhelmingly | 反义词: lightly, airily, refreshingly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From oppress + -ing + -ly.  Oppress comes from Old French *opresser, from Latin oppressare, frequentative of opprimere "to press against, crush," from ob "against" (see ob-) + premere "to press" (see press (v.1)).
