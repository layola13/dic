denies:/dɪˈnaɪz/
verb
否认，否定
states that something said, reported, or believed to be true is false.
He denies all responsibility for the accident. 他否认对这起事故负有任何责任。
近义词: contradicts, refutes, disavows, repudiates
反义词: admits, confesses, acknowledges, confirms
词性: verb

denies:/dɪˈnaɪz/
Etymology: From Old French *denier, from Latin dēnegāre (“to deny, refuse”), from dē- (“away, from”) + negāre (“to say no”).
词源：源自古法语 *denier，来自拉丁语 dēnegāre（“否认，拒绝”），由 dē-（“远离，从”）+ negāre（“说不”）组成。
