antibacklash:/ˈæntiˈbæklæʃ/| adjective, noun |防反冲的，防回程的；反齿隙机构|A device or mechanism designed to eliminate or reduce backlash. Also used as an adjective to describe such devices.  A backlash is a clearance or lost motion in a mechanism caused by gaps between parts.|The steering wheel had an antibacklash mechanism to improve precision.  方向盘有一个防回冲机构以提高精度。|Synonyms: anti-backlash, non-backlash |Antonyms: backlash |adjective, noun
