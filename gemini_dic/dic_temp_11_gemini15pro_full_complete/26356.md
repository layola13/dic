by-all-manner-of-means:/baɪ ɔːl ˈmænər əv miːnz/
| idiom |一定，务必，当然可以|Certainly; definitely; by all means.  It emphasizes willingness and enthusiasm in agreeing to a request. |"Can I borrow your car?" "By all manner of means!" “我能借你的车吗？” “当然可以！”|Synonyms: absolutely, certainly, definitely, of course, without a doubt|Antonyms: absolutely not, certainly not, definitely not, no way, under no circumstances
| adverbial phrase
