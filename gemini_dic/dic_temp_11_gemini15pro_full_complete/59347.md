earls: /ɜːrlz/
n.
伯爵（复数）
A British nobleman ranking above a viscount and below a marquess.
The earls gathered for the royal ceremony.  伯爵们聚集在一起参加皇家仪式。
近义词: noblemen, peers
反义词: commoners
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *eorl, from Proto-Germanic *erlaz, meaning "man, warrior, nobleman."  Cognate with Old Norse jarl.  Originally referring to a military leader or chieftain, the title eventually became hereditary and denoted a rank of nobility.
