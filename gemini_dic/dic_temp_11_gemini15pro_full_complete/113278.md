luteic:/luːˈtiːɪk/
adjective
黄体的；促黄体素的
Relating to the corpus luteum, a hormone-secreting structure that forms in an ovary after an egg has been released.
The luteic phase of the menstrual cycle is the time between ovulation and the start of the next period. 月经周期的黄体期是从排卵到下一次月经开始的时间。
The drug stimulates luteic function. 这种药物能刺激黄体功能。
Synonyms: luteal
Antonyms: follicular (referring to the follicular phase of the menstrual cycle)
词性: 形容词
