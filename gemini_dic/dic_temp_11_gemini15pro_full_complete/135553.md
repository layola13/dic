nonpolarities:/ˌnɑːnpəˈlærɪtiz/ | n. | 非极性 | The lack of polarity; the state of not having distinct poles or oppositely charged ends. This term often refers to molecules or substances that do not have a dipole moment. | Nonpolarities in the molecular structure contribute to the substance's inability to dissolve in water. 分子结构中的非极性导致该物质不溶于水。|  nonpolarity, apolarity | polarity | noun
