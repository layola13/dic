machineability:/məˌʃiːnəˈbɪləti/| n.|可加工性，机械加工性|The ease with which a material can be machined. This encompasses factors like how easily it can be cut, drilled, shaped, and finished without excessive tool wear or damage to the workpiece.|The machineability of aluminum is generally high. 铝的可加工性通常很高。|Synonyms: workability, manufacturability|Antonyms: intractability, unworkability|noun
