goddess: /ˈɡɒdɪs/ (British) /ˈɡɑːdəs/ (American)| n. |女神|A female deity, a woman revered for her beauty and charm, or a woman of very high standing.|例句：Aphrodite was the Greek goddess of love and beauty.  阿芙罗狄蒂是希腊的爱与美女神。

例句：She was worshipped as a goddess. 她被奉为女神。

近义词：deity, divinity, icon|反义词: mortal|词性: noun
