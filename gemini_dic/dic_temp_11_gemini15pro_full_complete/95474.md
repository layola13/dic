incitingly:/ɪnˈsaɪtɪŋli/
adverb
煽动地，刺激地
in a manner that stirs up or provokes (violent or unlawful behavior)
Her incitingly passionate speech roused the crowd to action. 她那煽动人心的演讲激起了群众的行动。
Synonyms: provocatively, inflammatory, agitatingly
Antonyms: calmingly, soothingly, appeasingly
词性: 副词
