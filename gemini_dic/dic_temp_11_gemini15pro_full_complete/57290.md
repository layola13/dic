downshifting:/ˈdaʊnˌʃɪftɪŋ/
n.
减速，放慢生活节奏；降档
The act of simplifying one's lifestyle, often by working less or moving to a less demanding job or location, in order to reduce stress and increase personal fulfillment.
Many people are choosing downshifting and moving to the countryside. 很多人选择放慢生活节奏，搬到乡下。
synonyms: simplifying, scaling back, decelerating
antonyms: upshifting, accelerating, intensifying
noun

downshift:/ˈdaʊnˌʃɪft/
v.
降低档位；减速，放慢生活节奏
To change to a lower gear in a vehicle; to simplify one's lifestyle.
He downshifted as he approached the corner. 他接近拐角时降低了档位。
She downshifted her career to spend more time with her family. 她放慢了职业发展的脚步，以便有更多时间陪伴家人。
synonyms: decelerate, slow down, simplify
antonyms: accelerate, speed up, intensify
verb
