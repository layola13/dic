linings:/ˈlaɪnɪŋz/
n.
衬里；内衬；里子
A layer of material covering the inner surface of something.
The coat had a silk lining.  这件外套有丝绸衬里。
The brake linings need to be replaced.  刹车片需要更换。
lining:/ˈlaɪnɪŋ/
n.
内层; 衬里; 覆盖物
The material used to cover the inside surface of something.
The linings of my shoes are worn out. 我的鞋衬磨损了。
lining:/ˈlaɪnɪŋ/
n.
(胃、肠等的)黏膜
A mucous membrane, especially of the stomach or intestines.
The lining of the stomach protects it from the acid. 胃黏膜保护胃部免受酸的侵蚀。
lining
n.
(云的)银边
A bright outline, especially of a cloud.
The dark clouds had silver linings.  乌云镶着银边。


近义词:covering, inner layer, facing, inside, interior
反义词:exterior, outside

词性: 名词 (Noun)
