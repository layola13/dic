dynamites:/ˈdaɪnəˌmaɪts/
n.|炸药（复数）|A high explosive consisting of nitroglycerin mixed with an absorbent material and typically molded into sticks.|The miners used dynamites to blast through the rock. 矿工们用炸药炸穿岩石。|explosives, bombs|None|Noun
