fixer-upper:/ˈfɪksər ˈʌpər/| n.|待修缮的房屋；需要修理的东西|A house or other property in need of repairs, often bought at a low price with the intention of renovating it.|例句：They bought a fixer-upper in the countryside and spent months renovating it. 他们在乡下买了一栋需要修缮的房子，花了几个月时间翻新。

例句：That old car is a bit of a fixer-upper, but it has potential. 那辆旧车需要一些修理，但它还是有潜力的。|近义词：handyman's special, renovation project, doer-upper|反义词:turnkey, move-in ready|词性:名词
