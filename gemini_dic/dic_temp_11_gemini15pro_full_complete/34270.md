claque: /klæk/ | n. |  喝彩；拍手喝彩；雇佣的喝彩者；捧场者 | A group of people hired to applaud a performer or performance; an act of applauding. | The opera singer paid for a claque to ensure a positive reception.  这位歌剧演唱家花钱雇人喝彩，以确保获得积极的反响。| synonyms: cheering section, fan club, applause | antonyms: booing, hissing, disapproval | noun

claque: /klæk/ | n. |  (法语)捧场；捧场者；雇佣的喝彩者 |  (from French) A group of people hired to applaud or show approval at a performance or public event. | The politician's claque erupted in cheers as he took the stage.  这位政治家的捧场者在他上台时爆发出欢呼声。| synonyms:  applauders, hired admirers | antonyms: critics, detractors | noun
