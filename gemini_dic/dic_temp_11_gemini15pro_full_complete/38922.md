confessionless:/kənˈfɛʃənˌlɪs/
adjective
无忏悔的；未坦白的；未供认的
Without confession; not involving or marked by confession.
He lived a confessionless life, never admitting to his wrongdoings. 他过着无忏悔的生活，从未承认自己的错误行为。
The police were frustrated by the confessionless suspect.  警方对这个未坦白的嫌疑人感到沮丧。
近义词:unrepentant, unapologetic, remorseless
反义词:repentant, apologetic, remorseful
词性:adjective
