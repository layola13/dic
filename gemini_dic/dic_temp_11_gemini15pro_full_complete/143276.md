outdodging:/ˌaʊtˈdɒdʒɪŋ/
verb
巧妙地躲避，比...更胜一筹
surpassing in dodging; evading more skillfully than.
The spy was a master of outdodging his pursuers.  这个间谍是个躲避追捕的大师。
He outdodged the other players and scored the winning goal. 他比其他球员躲避得更巧妙，并打进了制胜一球。
Synonyms: outmaneuvering, outwitting, circumventing
Antonyms: being caught, being outmaneuvered
词性: 动词
