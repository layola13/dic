accompanable:/əˈkʌmpəniəbl/
adj.
可陪伴的，宜作伴的
Suitable or enjoyable to be with as a companion.
She's a very accompanable person, always cheerful and engaging. 她是一个非常宜于作伴的人，总是兴高采烈，引人入胜。
近义词: companionable, sociable, gregarious
反义词: unsociable, aloof, solitary
词性: 形容词
词源: From accompany + -able.  "Accompany" comes from Old French "acompignier," ultimately from Latin "ad" (to) + "companio" (companion).
