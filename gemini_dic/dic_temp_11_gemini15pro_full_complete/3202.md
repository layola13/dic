affected: /əˈfɛktɪd/ | adj. | 做作的，假装的；受影响的 | Influenced or touched by an external factor.  Artificial, pretentious, or designed to impress. |  例句：She spoke in an affected British accent. 她说话带着一种做作的英国腔调。

例句：The affected area was quarantined. 受影响的区域被隔离了。

近义词：artificial, pretentious, unnatural, influenced, impacted | 反义词：natural, genuine, unaffected, untouched | 词性：形容词

affect: /əˈfɛkt/ | v. | 影响；假装；感动 | To have an influence on; to make a difference to. To pretend to have or feel (something). | 例句：The new law will affect many people. 新法律将影响很多人。

例句：He affected an air of indifference. 他装出一副漠不关心的样子。


近义词：influence, impact, touch, move, feign, pretend | 反义词：leave untouched, be unaffected by | 词性：动词
