amylogenesis:/ˌæmɪloʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun
淀粉生成,淀粉合成
The process by which starch is formed in plants.
例句：Amylogenesis is crucial for energy storage in plants.  淀粉生成对植物的能量储存至关重要。

例句：Researchers are studying amylogenesis to improve crop yields.  研究人员正在研究淀粉生成以提高作物产量。
近义词：starch biosynthesis, starch formation
反义词：amylolysis (starch breakdown)
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From amyl- + -genesis.  "amyl-" comes from Latin "amylum" (starch), which itself comes from Greek "ἄμυλον" (amylon), meaning "starch," "fine flour." "-genesis" comes from Greek "γένεσις" (genesis), meaning "origin," "creation," "generation."
