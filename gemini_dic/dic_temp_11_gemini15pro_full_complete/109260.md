lemmas:/ˈlɛməz/| n. |词元；词目；引理|A lemma is the canonical form, dictionary form, or citation form of a set of words.  In English, for example, run, runs, ran and running are forms of the same lexeme, with run as the lemma. |The linguist explained the concept of lemmas to the class.|近义词：headword, entry|反义词: None|词性: Noun
