gastrophrenic:/ˌɡæstroʊˈfrɛnɪk/
adjective
胃膈的；胃膈神经的
Relating to the stomach and the diaphragm.
The gastrophrenic ligament connects the stomach to the diaphragm. 胃膈韧带连接胃和横膈膜。
The gastrophrenic artery supplies blood to both the stomach and the diaphragm.  胃膈动脉为胃和横膈膜供血。

The gastrophrenic nerve.
Irritation of the gastrophrenic nerve can cause hiccups. 胃膈神经受到刺激会导致打嗝。
