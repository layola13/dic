misalleges:/ˌmɪsəˈlɛdʒɪz/ | v. |错误地断言；诬称； falsely alleges; asserts incorrectly | The lawsuit misalleges that the company knowingly sold defective products. 该诉讼错误地断言该公司明知故犯地销售了缺陷产品。| 近义词：misrepresents, misstates, distorts, falsifies | 反义词：states correctly, represents accurately, verifies, confirms | 词性: 动词

词源: mis- (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + allege (verb meaning "to assert or claim something without proof")
