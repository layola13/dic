labyrinthian: /ˌlæbəˈrɪnθiən/ | adj. | 迷宫般的，复杂的，曲折的 | Resembling a labyrinth; intricate and confusing. |  The movie's plot was so labyrinthian that I couldn't follow it.  这部电影的情节错综复杂，我完全看不懂。

The building's labyrinthian corridors made it easy to get lost.  这栋楼的走廊像迷宫一样，很容易迷路。 | 近义词: intricate, complex, convoluted, tortuous | 反义词: simple, straightforward, clear | 词性: 形容词

labyrinthine: /ˌlæbəˈrɪnθaɪn/ | adj. | 迷宫般的，复杂的，曲折的 |  Resembling a labyrinth; intricate and confusing. (This is a less common variant spelling.) |  He navigated the labyrinthine bureaucracy with surprising ease.  他以惊人的轻松驾驭了迷宫般的官僚机构。
