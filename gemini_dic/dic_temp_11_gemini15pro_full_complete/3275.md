affirmer:/əˈfɜːrmər/
n.
确认者;断言者;肯定者
Someone who affirms something; one who asserts positively.
The judge asked if there was an affirmer in the courtroom. 法官问法庭上是否有确认者。
近义词:asserter, declarer, supporter, advocate
反义词:denier, objector, opponent
词性:名词
