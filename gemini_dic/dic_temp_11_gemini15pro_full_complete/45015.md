csn:/siː ɛs ɛn/ | abbreviation | Could, Should, Not (Can, Should, Not - less common) | CSN is an abbreviation, often used in informal writing or online communication, that stands for "could, should, not" or less commonly "can, should, not". It implies a dilemma or internal debate about a course of action. | Example:  CSN go to the party tonight.  Lots of work to do. (Could/Should/Not go to the party tonight.
