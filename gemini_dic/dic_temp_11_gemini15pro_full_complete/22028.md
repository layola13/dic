bok choy: /ˈbɑːk ˌtʃɔɪ/ (US) /ˌbɒk ˈtʃɔɪ/ (UK)| n.|白菜|A leafy green vegetable with white stalks, widely used in Chinese cuisine.|例句：I added bok choy to my stir-fry. 我在我的炒菜里加了白菜。

例句：Bok choy is a good source of vitamins. 白菜是维生素的良好来源。

近义词：Chinese cabbage, pak choi|反义词: (No direct antonym for a vegetable)|词性:noun
