bequests:/bɪˈkwɛsts/
n.
遗产；遗赠物
Something left to someone in a will.
例句：The museum received several valuable bequests from a wealthy art collector.  博物馆收到了一位富有的艺术收藏家几笔价值不菲的遗产。

例句：His will included bequests to his children and several charities.  他的遗嘱包括给他子女和几个慈善机构的遗产。

近义词：legacy, inheritance, endowment
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
