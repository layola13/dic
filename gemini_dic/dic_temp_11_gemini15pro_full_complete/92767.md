ichthyosaur:/ˈɪkθiəˌsɔːr/ | n. | 鱼龙 | An extinct marine reptile resembling a dolphin, with a long pointed head, four flippers, and a vertical tail fin. |  Example: The museum displayed a complete ichthyosaur fossil.  博物馆展出了一具完整的鱼龙化石。 | Example:  Ichthyosaurs were dominant predators in the Mesozoic seas. 鱼龙是中生代海洋中的主要掠食者。| Synonym:  fish lizard | Antonym: N/A | noun
