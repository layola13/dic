excitatory:/ɪkˈsaɪtətɔːri/ | adj. |兴奋的；刺激的| Increasing or causing excitement or stimulation. |The excitatory neurotransmitter glutamate plays a crucial role in learning and memory. 兴奋性神经递质谷氨酸在学习和记忆中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: stimulating, arousing, invigorating |Antonyms: inhibitory, depressing, suppressing |adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *excitātōrius, from Latin excitāre (“to excite”). Surface analysis: excit(e) + -atory.
