bustle:/ˈbʌsəl/ (US), /ˈbʌsl/ (UK)| n. v.|喧闹，熙攘；裙撑；忙碌|A framework worn under the back of a woman's skirt to puff it out; energetic and noisy activity.|
The market was full of bustle and activity.  市场里熙熙攘攘，一片繁忙。
She bustled about the kitchen, preparing dinner. 她忙着在厨房里准备晚餐。|
近义词：rush, hurry, flurry, commotion, fuss, stir, ado, hustle, bustle (n.), hasten, hurry, dash, rush (v.)|
反义词：calm, quiet, stillness, peace, tranquility (n.), dawdle, linger, procrastinate (v.)|词性: 名词，动词


bustles:/ˈbʌsəlz/ (US), /ˈbʌslz/ (UK)| v. (bustle 的第三人称单数形式)|喧闹，熙攘；裙撑；忙碌|Third-person singular simple present indicative form of bustle.|
