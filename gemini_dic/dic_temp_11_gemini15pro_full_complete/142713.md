otiosely:/ˈoʊʃiəsli/ | adv. | 无用地，多余地，懒散地 | In a useless or superfluous manner; lazily.  Characterized by inaction or lack of effort. | He otiosely flipped through the channels, unable to find anything interesting. 他懒散地翻着频道，找不到任何有趣的节目。 |  The committee members otiosely debated the issue for hours, reaching no conclusion. 委员会成员们无用地就这个问题辩论了几个小时，没有得出任何结论。 | 近义词: unnecessarily, pointlessly, idly, lazily | 反义词: usefully, productively, actively, diligently | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Latin *otiosus* "idle, at leisure," from *otium* "leisure, ease."
