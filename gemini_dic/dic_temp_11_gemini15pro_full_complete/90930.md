hurdy-gurdy:/ˈhɜːrdi ˈɡɜːrdi/ | n. | 手摇风琴；街头手摇琴 | A stringed musical instrument, played by turning a crank that rubs against the strings. The melody is played on keys, and one or more drone strings provide a continuous accompaniment. | The hurdy-gurdy player entertained the crowd in the town square. 手摇风琴演奏者在城镇广场上娱乐人群。 | The street musician's hurdy-gurdy filled the air with a lively tune. 街头音乐家的手摇风琴在空中演奏着欢快的曲调。 | synonym: barrel organ, wheel fiddle | antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific instrument) | noun
