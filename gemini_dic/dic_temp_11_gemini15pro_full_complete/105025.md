kenyon:/ˈkɛnjən/ | n. |峡谷；深谷； Kenyon College(肯扬学院) 的简称|A deep gorge, typically one with steep rocky walls and a stream or river flowing through it.  A Kenyon is often carved by a river over a long period of time. It can also refer to Kenyon College, a highly selective liberal arts college in Gambier, Ohio.|例句：The hikers marveled at the beauty of the kenyon.  徒步旅行者惊叹于峡谷的美丽。

例句：He graduated from Kenyon. 他毕业于肯扬学院。|近义词：canyon, gorge, ravine, valley|反义词:plain, plateau|词性:noun
