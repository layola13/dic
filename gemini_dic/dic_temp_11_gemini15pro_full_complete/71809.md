词源:
From Old English *flēos, *flys (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *fleusą (“fleece”). Cognate with Old Frisian flūs (“fleece”), Old Saxon fliūs, Middle Dutch vlies, Dutch vlies (“fleece”), Old High German fliuz, German Vlies (“fleece”), Old Norse flís (“splinter, chip”), Gothic 𐍆𐌻𐌰𐌿𐍃 (flaus, “wool”). Related to flense.
