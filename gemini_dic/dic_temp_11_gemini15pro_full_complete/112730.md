lozenger:/ˈlɑːzɪndʒər/ /ˈlɒzɪndʒə(r)/| n.|菱形, 斜方形, 含片, 糖锭|A small, flavored tablet that is dissolved in the mouth to relieve a cough or sore throat.|She sucked on a lozenger to soothe her throat. 她含着一块含片来舒缓喉咙疼痛。|近义词：tablet, pastille, diamond (shape)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

lozenger:/ˈlɑːzɪndʒər/ /ˈlɒzɪndʒə(r)/| adj.(heraldry) |(纹章学)菱形的|Shaped like a diamond (rhombus), especially in heraldry.|The lozenger shield bore the family crest. 菱形盾牌上有家族的徽章。|近义词：diamond-shaped, rhombic|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
