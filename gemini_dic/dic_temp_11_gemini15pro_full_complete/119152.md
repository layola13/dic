mewl:/mjuːl/
verb
(猫) 喵喵叫；(婴儿) 哭；呜咽
to cry weakly, like a kitten or baby
The kitten mewled softly. 小猫轻轻地喵喵叫。


mewl:/mjuːl/
noun
喵喵叫；呜咽
a weak cry, like that of a kitten or baby
The faint mewl of a newborn baby. 新生儿微弱的哭声。


mewl:/mjuːl/
近义词: whimper, cry, whine
反义词: roar, shout
