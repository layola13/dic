bibulosity:/ˌbaɪbjuˈlɒsɪti/ /ˌbaɪbjuˈlɑːsɪti/
n.
过度饮酒；嗜酒；酒瘾
Excessive fondness for or indulgence in alcoholic beverages.
He was known for his bibulosity and often frequented the local taverns. 他因嗜酒而闻名，经常光顾当地的酒馆。

His increasing bibulosity worried his family. 他日益增长的酗酒问题令家人担忧。

近义词: alcoholism, drunkenness, inebriety
反义词: sobriety, temperance, abstinence
词性: noun

词源: From Latin *bibulus* "given to drinking," from bibere "to drink."
