exterminates:/ɪkˈstɜːmɪneɪts/
v.
消灭，根除
completely destroys something, especially something harmful, such as a pest or a disease.
The pest control company exterminates termites.  害虫防治公司消灭白蚁。
The government is trying to exterminate the illegal drug trade. 政府正在努力根除非法毒品交易。
annihilates, eradicates, destroys
preserve, protect
verb
