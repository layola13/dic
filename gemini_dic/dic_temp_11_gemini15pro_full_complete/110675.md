lindon: /ˈlɪndən/ | n. |椴树；林登（男子名）| A type of tree with heart-shaped leaves and fragrant yellowish-white flowers, also used as a given name. | The lindon tree in our backyard provides lovely shade in the summer. 我们后院的椴树在夏天提供了可爱的阴凉。|  Linden wood is soft and easily carved. 椴木质地柔软，易于雕刻。|Synonym: lime tree (UK), basswood (US) | Antonym: N/A | Noun
