cumbo:/ˈkʌmboʊ/
n.
山谷，盆地；大量，堆积
A deep valley or hollow; a large quantity or pile.
The hikers descended into the cumbo, seeking shelter from the storm.  徒步旅行者们为了躲避暴风雨而下到盆地里。
A cumbo of paperwork awaited her on her desk.  她桌上堆积如山的文书工作正等着她。
近义词:valley, basin, hollow, heap, pile
反义词:peak, summit, mountain
词性:名词

词源：
Cumbo is likely a variant of "combe" or "coombe," which comes from Old English "cumb," meaning "valley" or "hollow." It's also related to the Welsh "cwm."
