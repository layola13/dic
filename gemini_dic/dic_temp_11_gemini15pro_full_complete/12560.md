aspire:/əˈspaɪər/ | v. |渴望，立志，追求 |to have a strong desire to achieve something high or great | She aspired to a career in medicine. 她渴望从事医学事业。| I aspire to be a writer. 我渴望成为一名作家。 |  近义词: desire, yearn, aim, strive | 反义词:  resign, give up | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French aspirer, from Latin aspirare (“to breathe upon, to breathe on, to blow gently, to pant after”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + spirare (“to breathe”).
