kazillion:/kəˈzɪljən/
| n. |无数，极多；兆，京|An indefinitely large number.  A humorous or hyperbolic term for a very large, unspecified number.|例句：There are a kazillion things I need to do today. 我今天有无数的事情要做。

例句：He has a kazillion dollars. 他有巨额的钱。|近义词：zillion, gazillion, jillion, bajillion, trillion|反义词:few, little|词性:名词
