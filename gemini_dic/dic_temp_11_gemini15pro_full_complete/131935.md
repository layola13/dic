noncontract:/ˌnɑːnˈkɑːntrækt/
adjective
非合同的，非契约的
Not involving or covered by a contract; not bound by contract.
Employees in noncontract positions can be dismissed more easily.  非合同制员工更容易被解雇。
The work was done on a noncontract basis. 这项工作是在非合同的基础上完成的。
近义词:uncontracted, informal
反义词:contract, contracted
词性:adjective
