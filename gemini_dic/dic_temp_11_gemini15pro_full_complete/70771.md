finest:/ˈfaɪnɪst/| adj.|最好的，最精美的|of high quality; excellent|例句：This is the finest silk I've ever seen. 这是我见过最好的丝绸。|例句：They serve the finest wines in the restaurant.  这家餐厅供应最好的葡萄酒。|近义词：best, excellent, superb, top-quality|反义词：worst, poorest, lowest-quality|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *finest, superlative of *fin (“fine”), from Old French fin, from Latin fīnis (“end, limit, boundary”). More at fine.
