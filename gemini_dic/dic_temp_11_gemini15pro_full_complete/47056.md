/deɪt/ |/deɪt/| n. v. |日期；约会；枣；过时|A particular day of the month or year.  An appointment to meet someone socially. |
例句：The date on the letter is June 10th. 信上的日期是6月10日。

例句：I have a date with him tonight. 今晚我和他有个约会。

近义词：appointment, rendezvous, engagement (for date as in appointment); day (for date as in a day of the month) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun, verb


/deɪt/ |/deɪt/| v. |注明日期；确定年代；显示…的年龄；（和…）约会|To mark with a date. To determine the age of something. To go on dates with someone. |
例句：Please date and sign this document. 请在这份文件上签名并注明日期。

例句：The archaeologist dated the artifact to the 1st century BC. 考古学家确定这件文物属于公元前1世纪。

近义词：timestamp, chronologize, go out with |反义词: N/A |词性: verb
