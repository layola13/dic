confederation:/kənˌfedəˈreɪʃn/| n.|联盟，邦联，同盟|An organization that consists of a number of parties or groups united in an alliance or league.|Example 1: The United States was originally a confederation of 13 states. 美国最初是由13个州组成的邦联。

Example 2:  The trade confederation negotiated a new contract for its members.  该贸易联盟为其成员谈判了一份新合同。|Synonym: alliance, league, union, federation|Antonym: division, separation, disunity|noun
