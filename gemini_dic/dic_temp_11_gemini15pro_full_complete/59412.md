earthenwares:/ˈɜːrθənˌwɛərz/
n.
陶器，土器
Dishes and other articles made of baked clay.
The museum has a fine collection of earthenwares from the 18th century.  这家博物馆收藏了18世纪的精美陶器。
近义词：pottery, ceramics, crockery
反义词：(无明确反义词，可考虑 metalware 金属器皿)
词性：名词
