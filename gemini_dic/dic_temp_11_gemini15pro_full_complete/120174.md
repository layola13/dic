minder:/ˈmaɪndər/| n.|照顾者，保姆，提醒物|A person who looks after someone (such as a child or sick person) or something.|例句：She hired a minder for her children after school.  她雇了一个人放学后照顾她的孩子。

例句：Set a reminder on your phone as a minder for the appointment. 把你的手机设个提醒，提醒你预约。|近义词：caretaker, guardian, babysitter, reminder, prompt|反义词:neglecter|noun
