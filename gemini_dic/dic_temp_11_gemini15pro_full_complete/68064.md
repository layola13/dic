fad:/fæd/| n. |一时流行的狂热；时尚；风尚|A short-lived enthusiasm for something, especially one that is shared by many people.|例句：The latest fad among teenagers is collecting vintage clothing. 青少年中最新的流行趋势是收集复古服装。

例句：He dismissed it as a passing fad. 他认为这只是一时的风尚，不屑一顾。|近义词：craze, trend, fashion, vogue|反义词：classic, standard, tradition|词性:名词
