kerchief:/ˈkɜːrtʃɪf/ (US), /ˈkɜːtʃiːf/ (UK)| n. |头巾，方巾，围巾|A square piece of cloth used as a covering for the head or neck.|She tied a kerchief around her head to keep her hair out of her face. 她用一条头巾包住头，不让头发掉到脸上。|Synonyms: scarf, bandana, head covering|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider) uncovered, bareheaded|noun
