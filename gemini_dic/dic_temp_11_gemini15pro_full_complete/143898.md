outpowers:/ˌaʊtˈpaʊərz/
verb
胜过，压倒，超过
to be stronger than and defeat someone or something
例句：The larger army easily outpowered the smaller rebel force.  更大的军队轻易地击败了较小的叛军部队。

例句：The new software outpowers the older version in terms of speed and efficiency.  新软件在速度和效率方面都优于旧版本。
近义词：overpower, overcome, defeat, conquer, surpass, exceed
反义词：be outpowered by, lose to, succumb to
词性：v.
