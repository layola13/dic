continually:/kənˈtɪnjuəli/| adv.|不断地，频繁地|Happening frequently or repeatedly over a period of time.|The phone continually rang, interrupting our conversation. 电话不断地响，打断了我们的谈话。|近义词：constantly, frequently, repeatedly, incessantly|反义词：intermittently, occasionally, rarely|词性: 副词
词源: From Late Latin *continualis*, from Latin continuus (“continuous”).
