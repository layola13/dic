machined:/məˈʃiːnd/| adj.|机加工的，用机器制造的|Made or shaped by a machine.  Created by mechanical means, not by hand. |The part was precisely machined from a solid block of aluminum.  这个零件是用一块实心铝块精密加工而成。|例句：The machined surface was perfectly smooth.  加工后的表面非常光滑。|近义词：manufactured, fabricated, engineered|反义词:handmade, handcrafted|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "machine" + "-ed" (past participle suffix), ultimately from Latin "machina".
