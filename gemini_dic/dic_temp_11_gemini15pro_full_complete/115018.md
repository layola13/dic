manderil:/ˈmændrɪl/ | n. | 山魈 | A large, brightly colored baboon (Mandrillus sphinx) of western Africa, the male of which has a red and blue face and rump. |  The male mandrill is one of the most colorful mammals in the world. 雄性山魈是世界上颜色最鲜艳的哺乳动物之一。 |  The mandrill's bright colors serve as a warning to predators. 山魈鲜艳的颜色是对捕食者的警告。| synonyms: baboon, primate | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific animal) | noun
