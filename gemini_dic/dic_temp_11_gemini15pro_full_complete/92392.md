hypodactylum:/ˌhaɪpəʊˈdæktɪləm/ | n. |下指畸形；趾（指）数减少|A congenital malformation characterized by fewer than the normal number of fingers or toes. |Example 1:  The infant was born with hypodactylum, missing two fingers on his left hand.  这个婴儿出生时患有下指畸形，左手少了两个手指。

Example 2: Hypodactylum can range in severity, from missing a single digit to missing all fingers and toes. 下指畸形的严重程度不一，从缺失一个指头到缺失所有手指和脚趾都有可能。 |近义词:oligodactyly|反义词:polydactyly|词性:noun
