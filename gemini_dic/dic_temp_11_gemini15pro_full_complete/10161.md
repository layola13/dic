aponeurotomy:/əˌpɒnjuˈrɒtəmi/ | n. |腱膜切开术 |Surgical incision of an aponeurosis.  It involves cutting through an aponeurosis to relieve tension or pressure, often used to treat conditions like Dupuytren's contracture.|  Aponeurotomy was performed to release the contracted tissue in his hand.  腱膜切开术是为了释放他手中收缩的组织。 | Synonyms: fasciotomy | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but suturing of the aponeurosis would be the opposite procedure) | noun
