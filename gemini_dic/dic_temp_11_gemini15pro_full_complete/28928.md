carrington:/ˈkærɪŋtən/| n. |卡林顿|A surname, derived from a place name in Lincolnshire, England, meaning "Keri's farm/settlement".  It is also associated with the powerful Carrington family depicted in the television series "Dynasty".|Richard Carrington was a prominent astronomer. 理查德·卡林顿是一位杰出的天文学家。|The Carrington Event was a powerful geomagnetic storm in 1859. 卡林顿事件是1859年的一次强大的地磁风暴。|近义词: (As a surname, no direct synonyms)  Possibly other surnames of similar origin.|反义词: (Not applicable for proper nouns)|词性:Noun
