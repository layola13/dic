bowser:/ˈbaʊzər/ (US), /ˈbaʊzə/ (UK)| n.|喷嘴；注油枪；油罐车；（电子游戏中的）库巴|A device that pumps liquid, especially gasoline, into a tank.  Also, a character in the Mario video game franchise.|

例句：The bowser at the gas station was out of order.  加油站的注油枪坏了。

例句：Bowser kidnapped Princess Peach again!  库巴又绑架了桃花公主！

近义词：pump, tanker (for vehicle), nozzle|反义词:|词性:noun


bowser:/ˈbaʊzər/ (US), /ˈbaʊzə/ (UK)| n.|饮水器；水槽|A large container holding water for animals to drink.|

例句：The farmer filled the bowser with fresh water for the cows.  农民给奶牛的饮水器装满了清水。

例句：The dog lapped water from the bowser.  狗从水槽里舔水喝。

词源：Bowser's name in Japanese, Koopa, comes from the Korean name for kimchi, "gukbap."  This indirectly led to his English name, Bowser, which is a type of kimchi.  The meaning shifted over time to refer to devices that dispense liquids.
