noncoalescent:/ˌnɒnˌkəʊəˈlɛsənt/ | adj. | 非聚结的，不合并的 | Not coalescing; remaining separate or distinct. | The noncoalescent droplets of oil formed a slick on the water. 油的非聚结液滴在水上形成了一层浮油。 |  The noncoalescent nature of the particles made the mixture unstable. 颗粒的不合并性质使混合物不稳定。 | 近义词: separate, distinct, unmerged, discrete | 反义词: coalescent, merged, united, combined | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix) : not
coalescent (from Latin coalescere "to grow together")
