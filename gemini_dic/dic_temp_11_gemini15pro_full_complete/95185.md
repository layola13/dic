inaugurate: /ɪˈnɔːɡjəreɪt/ | v. | 就职；开幕；开始 |  formally begin or introduce (a system, policy, or period). | The mayor inaugurated the new park. 市长为新公园揭幕。| They inaugurated a new era of economic reform. 他们开创了经济改革的新纪元。| 近义词:  commence, initiate, launch, debut | 反义词: conclude, terminate, end | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin inaugurare "to take omens from birds," from in- "in" + augurare "to predict," from augur "soothsayer." Originally, the word referred to Roman religious ceremonies where the auspices (signs from birds) were taken before important events like the inauguration of a magistrate.
