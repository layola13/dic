cremaillere:/krɛmɑːjɛər/ | n. |齿条，齿轨; 棘轮; (山地)攀登者用的小铁钩 |A toothed rack or bar, especially one engaging with a pinion to convert rotary motion to linear motion or vice versa; a ratchet; a small iron hook used by mountain climbers. |The cog railway uses a cremaillere system to ascend the steep mountain. 齿轨铁路使用齿轨系统爬上陡峭的山坡。|Synonyms: rack, cogwheel, ratchet |Antonyms: pinion (in a functional sense, not a direct opposite)| noun
