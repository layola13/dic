ambidextrousness:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəsnəs/| n.| 双手灵巧，左右开弓|The ambidextrousness of the surgeon allowed him to operate with either hand.  这位外科医生的双手灵巧使他可以用任何一只手进行手术。|例句：Her ambidextrousness is a great asset in her chosen sport of tennis. 她的双手灵巧是她所选择的网球运动的一大优势。|近义词：versatility, adaptability, two-handedness|反义词:one-handedness, clumsiness|noun

ambidextrous:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs/| adj.| 双手灵巧的，左右开弓的|An ambidextrous person can use both hands equally well. 一个双手灵巧的人可以平等地使用两只手。|例句：He was an ambidextrous pitcher, equally skilled with both arms. 他是一个双手灵巧的投手，两只手臂都同样熟练。|近义词：versatile, adaptable, two-handed|反义词:one-handed, clumsy|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ambidexter "having two right hands" (figuratively, "skillful," literally "right-handed on both sides"), from ambi- "both" + dexter "right, right hand."
