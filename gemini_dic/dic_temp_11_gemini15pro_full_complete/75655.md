fuka:/ˈfuːkə/| n.|深谷，裂缝|A deep narrow valley or ravine.|例句：The hikers descended into the fuka.  徒步旅行者们下到了深谷里。

例句：The fuka was teeming with wildlife. 深谷里充满了野生动物。|近义词：gorge, ravine, canyon, chasm|反义词:ridge, peak, summit|词性:名词

Etymology:  From Japanese 深(fuka), meaning "deep."
