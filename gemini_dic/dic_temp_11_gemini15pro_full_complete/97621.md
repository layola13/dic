inharmoniousnesses:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmoʊniəsnəsɪz/
| n. |不和谐，不协调|The state or quality of being inharmonious; lack of harmony; discord.|The inharmoniousnesses of the different musical styles made the concert jarring. 不同音乐风格的不协调让这场音乐会很不和谐。|近义词：discords, dissonances, clashes, conflicts|反义词：harmonies, concord, agreement|词性:名词

Etymology:
From inharmonious + -ness.  "Inharmonious" itself comes from "in-" (not) + "harmonious" (harmonious, from Latin "harmonia").  The "-ness" suffix transforms the adjective into a noun denoting a state or quality.
