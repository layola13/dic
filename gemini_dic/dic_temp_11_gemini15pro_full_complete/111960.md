lombard:/ˈlɒmbɑːd/ (UK), /ˈlɑːmbɑːrd/ (US)| n. adj.|  伦巴第人；伦巴第语；(尤指高利贷) 贷款；典当业| A native or inhabitant of Lombardy. Also, the language of Lombardy.  Historically, a Lombard could also refer to a banker or moneylender, often associated with high interest rates.| He spoke Lombard with his family. 他和家人说伦巴第语。|Synonyms: moneylender, pawnbroker (as a noun relating to finance)|Antonyms: borrower, debtor (as a noun relating to finance)|noun, adjective

lombard:/ˈlɒmbɑːd/ (UK), /ˈlɑːmbɑːrd/ (US)| v.| 放款；以…作抵押借款| To lend money, often at high interest rates, taking something as security.| He lombarded his watch to pay for the trip. 他典当了他的手表来支付旅行费用。|Synonyms: pawn, hock|Antonyms: redeem, repay|verb
