midfields:/ˈmɪdˌfiːldz/
n.
中场，中场区
The midfield is the area of a playing field between the two teams' defensive and attacking areas.
The coach told the midfielders to control the midfield. 教练告诉中场球员要控制中场。
center field, midfield area
defense, sidelines
noun

midfield:/ˈmɪdˌfiːld/
adj.
中场的
Relating to or located in the midfield of a playing field.
He is a midfield player. 他是一名中场球员。
central
peripheral, wing
adjective

midfield:/ˈmɪdˌfiːld/
adv.
在中场
In or into the midfield.  (Less common usage)
The ball was passed midfield. 球被传到了中场。
centrally
peripherally
adverb
