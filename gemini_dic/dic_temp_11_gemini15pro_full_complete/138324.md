noters:/ˈnəʊtərz/
n.
记录者，记笔记的人
People who take notes, especially in a meeting or lecture.
The noters diligently recorded every word of the presentation.  记录员们勤奋地记录了演讲的每一个字。
scribes, recorders
forgetters
noun
