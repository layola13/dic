nonreciprocal:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈsɪp.rə.kəl/ /ˌnɑːn.rɪˈsɪp.rə.kəl/
adjective
非互易的；单向的；不相互的
Not reciprocal; not interchangeable; one-way.
A nonreciprocal agreement is one in which one party receives benefits without offering benefits in return.  一个非互惠协议是指一方获得利益而不提供回报的协议。
The two countries have a nonreciprocal trade agreement. 这两个国家有一个非互惠贸易协定。
近义词:unidirectional, one-sided, asymmetric
反义词:reciprocal, mutual, two-sided
词性:adjective
