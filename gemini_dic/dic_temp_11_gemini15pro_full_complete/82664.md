guessed:/ɡest/ | v. | 猜测，估计 | Guessed is the past tense and past participle of the verb "guess." It means to form an opinion or give an answer about something when you do not know much or anything about it. |  I guessed her age correctly. 我正确地猜出了她的年龄。

I guessed the answer to the riddle. 我猜出了谜底。 | synonyms: surmised, conjectured, speculated, estimated | antonyms: knew, ascertained, calculated | verb
