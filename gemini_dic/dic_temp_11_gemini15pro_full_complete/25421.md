bulked:/bʌlkt/| v. |增大，使更大；显得庞大|The bodybuilder bulked up for the competition.  健美运动员为了比赛而增大了肌肉。|近义词：enlarge, expand, swell, grow|反义词: shrink, reduce, decrease|verb

bulked:/bʌlkt/| adj. |庞大的，巨大的|He looked bulked up in his new winter coat. 他穿着新的冬衣显得十分臃肿。|近义词：large, massive, huge|反义词: small, tiny, slight|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bulken*, from Old Norse *bolgna* ("to swell"), from Proto-Germanic *bulgnaną* ("to swell"). Cognate with Dutch *bulken* ("to bulk, bulge"), German *bolgen* ("to bulge, billow").  The meaning "to increase in size or volume" arose from the earlier sense of swelling.
