globby:/ˈɡlɑːbi/| adj.|粘糊糊的，胶状的|Covered in a thick, sticky substance; viscous.|The oatmeal was left on the stove too long and became globby.|近义词：gooey, sticky, slimy, viscous|反义词:dry, firm, solid|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Likely a derivative of "glob," meaning a lump or rounded mass, with the suffix "-y" added to form an adjective indicating a characteristic of being like or covered in globs.  "Glob" itself is of uncertain origin, possibly imitative.
