mariolater:/ˌmæriˈɒlətər/ | n. | 圣母崇拜者; 奉圣母玛利亚为神的人 | A person who venerates the Virgin Mary excessively or to the point of idolatry. |  He was accused of being a mariolater because of his extreme devotion to Mary. 他因对玛利亚的极度虔诚而被指责为圣母崇拜者。| synonym: Mariologist, devotee of Mary | antonym: iconoclast, critic of Marian devotion | noun
