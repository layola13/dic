geranium:/dʒəˈreɪniəm/ | n. | 天竺葵，老鹳草 | A flowering plant with red, pink, or white flowers, often grown in gardens.  |  例句：The geraniums on the balcony are in full bloom. 阳台上的天竺葵盛开了。

例句：She has a beautiful collection of scented geraniums. 她收集了很多香味宜人的天竺葵。 | 近义词：pelargonium | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *geranium, from Ancient Greek γερανιον (geranion, “crane's-bill”), diminutive of γέρανος (géranos, “crane”), referring to the shape of the fruit which resembles a crane's bill.
