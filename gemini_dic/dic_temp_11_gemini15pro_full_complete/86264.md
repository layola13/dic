heavenward:/ˈhɛvənwərd/| adj. adv.|向天的，朝天的|Directed or moving towards heaven; ascending.|The balloon drifted heavenward. 气球飘向天空。|近义词：skyward, upwards, Godward|反义词：earthward, downward|词性：形容词/副词
Etymology: From Old English *heofonweard, from heofon "heaven" + -weard "-ward".
