nonconsequent:/nɒnˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ /nɑːnˈkɑːnsɪkwent/
adjective
不相关的，不合逻辑的
not following logically as a consequence; illogical.
His argument was filled with nonconsequent statements, jumping from one topic to another without any clear connection. 他的论证充满了不相关的陈述，在没有明确联系的情况下从一个主题跳到另一个主题。
The jury dismissed his testimony as nonconsequent and irrelevant to the case. 陪审团认为他的证词不相关，与案件无关，予以驳回。
irrelevant, illogical, unrelated, fallacious, inconsistent
consequent, logical, relevant, consistent, reasoned
