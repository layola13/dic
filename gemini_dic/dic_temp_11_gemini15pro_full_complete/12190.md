as-large-as-life:/æz ˌlɑːrdʒ əz ˈlaɪf/
|adj.|
|栩栩如生的；和真人一样大小的；引人注目的；夸大的|
|Appearing to be very real or dramatic;  exactly like the real person or thing; attracting a lot of attention.|
|例句1：The statue of the hero was as large as life in the town square.  英雄的雕像在城镇广场上栩栩如生。

例句2：She burst into the room, as large as life, and demanded to see the manager. 她冲进房间，气势汹汹，要求见经理。|
|近义词：lifelike, realistic, vivid, flamboyant, theatrical|
|反义词：inconspicuous, unassuming, modest, subdued|
|词性：形容词|
