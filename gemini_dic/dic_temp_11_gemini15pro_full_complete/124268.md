mote's:/moʊts/| n.|微粒，尘埃，瑕疵|A tiny piece of dust, dirt, or other foreign matter.  It's the archaic plural form of "mote."|例句：And why beholdest thou the mote that is in thy brother's eye, but considerest not the beam that is in thine own eye? 你为什么看见你弟兄眼中有刺，却不想自己眼中有梁木呢？|近义词：speck, particle, dust|反义词:beam|词性:名词
