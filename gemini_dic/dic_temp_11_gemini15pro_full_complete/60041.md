educational:/ˌedʒuˈkeɪʃənl/ | adj. | 教育的；有教育意义的 | Providing knowledge or relating to education. |
例句：The museum offers a range of educational programs for children.  博物馆为孩子们提供了一系列教育项目。

例句：This documentary is both entertaining and educational. 这部纪录片既有娱乐性又有教育意义。

近义词：instructive, informative, enlightening | 反义词：uneducational, unenlightening | 词性: adjective
