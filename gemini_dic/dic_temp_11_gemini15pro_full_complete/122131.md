mistranslation:/ˌmɪstrænsˈleɪʃn/| n. |误译，翻译错误|An incorrect or faulty translation from one language to another. |例句：The mistranslation caused a diplomatic incident. 误译引发了一场外交事件。

例句：There were several mistranslations in the subtitles of the movie.  这部电影的字幕有几处误译。

近义词：misinterpretation, wrong translation, inaccurate translation|反义词: accurate translation, correct translation|词性:noun
