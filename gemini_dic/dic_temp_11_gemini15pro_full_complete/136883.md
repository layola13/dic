nonsobrieties: /nɒn.səˈbraɪ.ə.tiz/
noun
非清醒状态;  放纵;  不节制 (plural of nonsobriety)
Lack of sobriety; states of being not sober; instances of intemperance or indulgence.
The holiday season is often marked by nonsobrieties of all kinds.  假日季节通常以各种各样的放纵为标志。
His youthful nonsobrieties eventually caught up with him. 他年轻时的不节制最终让他自食其果。

Near synonyms: excesses, indulgences, intemperances
Antonyms: sobriety, abstinence, moderation
词性: noun (plural)


Etymology:
"Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "sobriety" (from Latin "sobrietas," meaning "soberness").
