interlibel: /ˌɪntərˈlaɪbəl/
n.
相互诽谤; 交互诽谤
An exchange of defamatory statements between two parties.  It implies a back-and-forth of libelous accusations.
The escalating dispute resulted in an interlibel between the two rival companies. 这场不断升级的争端导致了两家竞争公司之间的相互诽谤。
The political candidates engaged in a bitter interlibel, accusing each other of corruption and misconduct.  两位政治候选人进行了激烈的相互诽谤，指责对方腐败和行为不端。
近义词: mutual defamation, cross-libel, counter-libel
反义词:  praise, commendation
