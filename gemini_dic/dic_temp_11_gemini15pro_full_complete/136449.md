nonreverses:/ˌnɒnriˈvɜːsɪz/
| n. |非反转录|Things or sequences that do not undergo reverse transcription.  Specifically referring to sequences in genomes that are not derived from retrotransposons or other retroelements.|Nonreverses comprise a significant portion of the human genome. 非反转录序列构成了人类基因组的重要组成部分。|Near synonyms: non-retroelements, non-retroviral sequences|Antonyms: reverses, retroelements|noun
