anthropogeography:/ˌænθrəpəʊdʒiˈɒɡrəfi/ (UK) /ˌænθrəpoʊdʒiˈɑːɡrəfi/ (US)| n.|人文地理学|The study of the relationship between human societies and their environment. It explores how humans influence the environment and how the environment influences human societies.|例句：Anthropogeography helps us understand the distribution of cultures across the globe. 人文地理学帮助我们理解全球文化分布。

例句：She's specializing in anthropogeography, focusing on the impact of climate change on migration patterns. 她专攻人文地理学，重点研究气候变化对迁徙模式的影响。

近义词：human geography, cultural geography|反义词: physical geography|词性:noun
