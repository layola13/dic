cringingnesses:/ˈkrɪndʒɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
畏缩， cringe 的复数形式
the state of being excessively humble or servile.
His cringingnesses made him appear weak. 他的畏缩让他显得软弱。

近义词:servility, obsequiousness, submissiveness, fawning, groveling
反义词:boldness, confidence, assertiveness
词性:noun (plural)


cringe:/krɪndʒ/
v.
畏缩；感到尴尬不安
recoil; shrink back
I cringed at the thought of having to sing in front of everyone.  我一想到要在大家面前唱歌就感到尴尬不安。

近义词: recoil, flinch, wince, shrink
反义词: boast, strut
词性:verb
