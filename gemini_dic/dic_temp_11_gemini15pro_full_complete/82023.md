grippingness:/ˈɡrɪpɪŋnɪs/| n.|吸引力；扣人心弦；引人入胜|The grippingness of the story kept me reading until dawn.  故事的吸引力让我一直读到天亮。|近义词：fascination, captivation, enthrallment|反义词：dullness, tediousness, boredom|词性:名词

Etymology: From "gripping" (adjective) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition). "Gripping" comes from the verb "grip," ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gripanan.
