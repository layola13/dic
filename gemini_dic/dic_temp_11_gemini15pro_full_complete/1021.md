accedes:/əkˈsiːdz/| v. |同意，应允，加入| to agree to a request, proposal, or course of action; to become a party to a treaty or agreement; (of a monarch) to formally assume a position of authority |例句：The company president acceded to the workers' demands for higher wages. 公司总裁同意了工人们提高工资的要求。

例句：She acceded to the throne after the death of her father. 她父亲去世后，她继承了王位。|近义词：agrees, consents, complies, joins |反义词：refuses, declines, rejects, resists|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *accedere, from Latin accedere (“to approach, go to, arrive at, come near to, be added to, enter upon, undertake, agree to”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + cedere (“to go, yield”).
