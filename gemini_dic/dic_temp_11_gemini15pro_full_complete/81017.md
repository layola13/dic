gradualistic: /ˌɡrædʒuəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 渐进的，渐变的 | Proceeding by gradual steps or degrees. Characterized by gradualism.  | The museum's exhibit charted the gradualistic evolution of the species over millions of years.  博物馆的展览描绘了该物种数百万年来渐进的进化过程。 | The company adopted a gradualistic approach to implementing the new policy. 公司采取渐进式的方法来实施新政策。 | 近义词: evolutionary, progressive, incremental, step-by-step | 反义词: abrupt, sudden, rapid, revolutionary | 词性: 形容词

gradualism: /ˈɡrædʒuəlɪzəm/ | n. | 渐进主义，渐变论 | A theory or principle advocating gradual rather than sudden or revolutionary change.  | Gradualism is a key concept in Darwin's theory of evolution. 渐进主义是达尔文进化论中的一个关键概念。 | The government's approach to economic reform was based on gradualism. 政府的经济改革方法是基于渐进主义的。 | 近义词:  evolutionism, progressionism | 反义词: revolution, catastrophism | 词性: 名词
