cynoidea:/saɪˈnɔɪdiə/ | n. | 犬型亚目 |  A suborder of carnivoran mammals that includes dogs, wolves, foxes, jackals, and related species.  It is characterized by a relatively long snout, non-retractile claws, and specialized teeth for a carnivorous diet. |  Cynoidea includes a diverse range of canids, from the small fennec fox to the large gray wolf. 犬型亚目包括从小型耳廓狐到大型灰狼的各种犬科动物。|  The evolutionary history of Cynoidea can be traced back millions of years. 犬型亚目的进化史可以追溯到数百万年前。| 近义词: Canidae, canids | 反义词:  None (There is no direct antonym for a taxonomic group.) | 词性: noun
