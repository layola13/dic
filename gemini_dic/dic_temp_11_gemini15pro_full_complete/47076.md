datively:/ˈdeɪtɪvli/

与格地，间接宾语地
in a dative manner; as an indirect object.
The pronoun "him" is used datively in the sentence "She gave him the book." 她把书给了他。（him 在句中作间接宾语）

The preposition "to" often marks a dative construction. 介词“to”通常标志着与格结构。
