neutralization:/ˌnjuːtrələˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 中和; 平衡; 抵消|The neutralization of the acid with an alkali produced salt and water. 酸与碱的中和产生了盐和水。|The painting uses color neutralization to create a sense of calm. 这幅画运用色彩中和来营造一种平静的感觉。| synonyms: counteraction, cancellation, balancing| antonyms: intensification, strengthening | noun

neutralize:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪz/ |v.| 中和; 抵消; 使无效|The antibodies neutralize the toxins released by the bacteria. 抗体中和了细菌释放的毒素。|The government's efforts to neutralize the opposition were unsuccessful. 政府压制反对派势力的努力没有成功。| synonyms: counteract, nullify, offset | antonyms: intensify, strengthen | verb

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *neutralisen, from late Latin neutralizāre, from neuter "neither".
