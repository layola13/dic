fly-off-the-handle:/ˌflaɪ ɒf ðə ˈhændl/| idiom|勃然大怒，突然发脾气|To suddenly lose your temper and become angry.|例句：He's got a terrible temper and is likely to fly off the handle at any moment. 他脾气很暴躁，随时都可能大发雷霆。

例句：When I told her the news, she completely flew off the handle. 当我告诉她这个消息时，她勃然大怒。|近义词：lose one's temper, blow up, go ballistic, hit the roof|反义词：stay calm, keep one's cool|phrase
