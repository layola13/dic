chouan:/ʃuːˈɑːn/ | n. |舒昂党人；保皇党游击队员|A member of a counterrevolutionary and royalist insurgent group in western France during the French Revolution, especially in the region of Brittany, Maine, and Anjou. | The chouans harassed the Republican troops in the countryside.  舒昂党人在乡下骚扰共和军。|  The government sent troops to suppress the chouan rebellion. 政府派兵镇压舒昂党的叛乱。|Synonyms:  royalist, insurgent, rebel, counterrevolutionary | Antonyms: republican, revolutionary | Noun
