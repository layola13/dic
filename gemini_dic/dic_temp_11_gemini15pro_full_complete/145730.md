overcritically: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪkli/
adverb|过于批判地，吹毛求疵地|In an excessively critical manner, finding fault with even minor details.|He overcritically examined every aspect of her work, leaving her feeling demoralized.| 近义词: hypercritically, excessively critically, too critically | 反义词: uncritically, leniently, charitably |词性: 副词


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "critical" (adjective meaning "inclined to find fault or judge severely") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
