jaspilite:/ˈdʒæspəlaɪt/ | n. |碧玉岩，碧玉铁质岩|A dense, fine-grained, chemical sedimentary rock containing hematite, magnetite, or other iron oxides interlayered with silica, usually chert.  It is typically banded red, brown, or black. |The jaspilite formations in the region are visually stunning. 该地区的碧玉岩构造在视觉上令人惊叹。| The geologist identified the sample as a type of jaspilite. 地质学家将样本鉴定为一种碧玉岩。 |Synonyms: iron formation, banded iron formation (BIF)| Antonyms: None (lack of a true opposite for a specific rock type) | noun
