body-popping:/ˈbɒdi pɒpɪŋ/ | n. | 机械舞 | A style of street dance, also called popping, that involves quickly contracting and relaxing muscles to cause a jerking motion in the body. |  Example 1: He's amazing at body-popping; his moves are so precise. 他跳机械舞棒极了，动作非常精准。

Example 2:  Body-popping originated in California in the 1970s. 机械舞起源于20世纪70年代的加利福尼亚。 | 近义词: popping, electric boogaloo | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a dance style) | 词性: noun
