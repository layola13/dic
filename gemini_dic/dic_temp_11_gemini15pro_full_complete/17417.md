beamless:/ˈbiːmlɪs/| adj.|无梁的，无光束的|Lacking beams, especially of light; dark.  Without visible rays of light.|The cave was beamless and eerily silent.  这个洞穴没有光线，静得可怕。|Synonyms: dark, unlit, rayless, lightless|Antonyms: bright, radiant, luminous|adjective
