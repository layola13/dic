ejaculatory:/ɪˈdʒækjʊlətɔːri/ /ɪˈdʒækjələˌtɔri/
noun
射精管
A duct that discharges semen.
The ejaculatory ducts open into the urethra. 射精管通入尿道。
Blockage of the ejaculatory duct can cause infertility. 射精管阻塞会导致不育。
近义词:  (for duct)  vas deferens, seminal duct
反义词:  N/A (for noun form)
词性: noun
