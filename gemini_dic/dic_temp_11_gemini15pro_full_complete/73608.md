forewarn:/fɔːrˈwɔːrn/
verb
预先警告，事先通知
to warn someone of a danger or problem that is likely to happen in the future
The weather forecast forewarned us of the blizzard. 天气预报预先警告了我们暴风雪的到来。
The guidebook forewarned tourists about the dangers of walking alone at night. 旅行指南预先警告游客夜间独自步行的危险。
synonyms: caution, alert, prewarn, advise, tip off
antonyms: surprise, ambush, catch off guard
词性: 动词
