cravingly:/ˈkreɪvɪŋli/
adverb
渴望地；贪婪地
In a manner expressing strong desire or longing.

He drank the water cravingly after his long run. 长跑后，他贪婪地喝着水。
Synonyms:longingly, yearningly, avidly, greedily
Antonyms:indifferently, apathetically
词性: 副词
