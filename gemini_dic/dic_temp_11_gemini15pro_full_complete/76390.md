gaff's:/ɡæfs/ | n. | gaff的复数形式，鱼叉，鱼钩，（尤指举办派对、招待客人的）住处，家，老窝 | Gaff's plural form, referring to a large hook with a handle for landing large fish, or informally to a person's home, especially where parties or entertainments are held.  | 例句：He kept his fishing gaffs in the shed. 他把他的鱼叉放在棚子里。

例句：We're having a party at my gaff tonight. 今晚我们在我家开派对。 | 近义词：home, house, place, pad, digs (informal), residence | 反义词： N/A | 词性：名词

gaff's:/ɡæfs/ | n. | gaff 的所有格形式，鱼叉的，家的 | Possessive form of gaff, belonging to or associated with a gaff (fish hook or home). | 例句：The gaff's handle was worn smooth from years of use.  鱼叉的把手因为多年的使用而磨光滑了。

例句：The party at my gaff's going to be epic! 在我家举办的派对将会非常盛大！ | 近义词：(Depending on the meaning of gaff) hook's, home's, house's | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词
