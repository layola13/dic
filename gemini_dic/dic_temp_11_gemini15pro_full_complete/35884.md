cocobolo:/ˈkoʊkəˌboʊloʊ/ /ˌkɒkəˈbəʊləʊ/
n.
cocobolo wood;  可可波罗木
A tropical American tree (Dalbergia retusa) yielding a valuable dark, variegated, fragrant cabinet wood.  一种产于美洲热带的树木（黄檀属），出产一种珍贵的深色、杂色、芳香的家具木材。
The guitar was made of beautiful cocobolo. 这把吉他是用漂亮的可可波罗木制成的。
synonyms: rosewood, granadillo
antonyms: N/A
noun

cocobolas:/ˌkoʊkəˈboʊləz/ /ˌkɒkəˈbəʊləz/
n. (plural)
cocobolo woods; 可可波罗木（复数）
Refers to multiple pieces or sources of cocobolo wood. 指多块或多种来源的可可波罗木。
He used various cocobolas to create the inlay. 他用各种可可波罗木来制作镶嵌物。
synonyms: rosewoods, granadillos
antonyms: N/A
