instanter:/ɪnˈstæntər/| adv.|立即，马上|immediately; at once|例句：The judge ordered him to leave the courtroom instanter. 法官命令他立即离开法庭。|近义词：immediately, forthwith, promptly, directly|反义词:later, eventually|词性:副词
Etymology: From Latin *instanter*, from *instans* ("present, urgent"), present participle of *instare* ("to stand near, press upon, urge").
