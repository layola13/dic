feringee: /fəˈrɪŋɡi/ | n. | 外国人（尤指欧洲人）；异教徒 | A foreigner, especially a European, often used in South Asia and parts of East Africa.  It can sometimes have negative connotations. |  Example: The feringees were surprised by the local customs.  外国的游客对当地的风俗感到惊讶。

Example:  In some older texts, "feringee" is used to refer to any non-Muslim.  在一些古老的文献中，“feringee”指的是任何非穆斯林。 | 近义词: foreigner, outsider, alien | 反义词: native, local | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Portuguese *ferengi, from Arabic faranjī (فرنجي), ultimately from the Franks.
