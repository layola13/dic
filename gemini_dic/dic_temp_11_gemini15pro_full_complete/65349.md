eulogising:/ˈjʊlədʒaɪzɪŋ/
verb
赞颂，颂扬
Praising someone or something highly, typically in a formal speech or writing.
He was eulogising the virtues of the Roman Empire. 他在赞颂罗马帝国的伟大之处。
近义词: praising, extolling, lauding, commending
反义词: criticizing, condemning, denouncing
词性: verb (present participle)

eulogise:/ˈjʊlədʒaɪz/
verb
赞颂，颂扬
To praise someone or something highly, typically in a formal speech or writing.
The speaker eulogised the late president. 演讲者赞颂了已故总统。
近义词: praise, extol, laud, commend
反义词: criticize, condemn, denounce
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek eulogia "praise," from eu- "well" + -logia "speaking," related to legein "to speak."
