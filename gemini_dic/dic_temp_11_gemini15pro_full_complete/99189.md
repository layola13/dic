intercivic:/ˌɪntərˈsɪvɪk/
adjective
城市间的；城际的
Relating to or involving different cities, especially within the same country or region.
Intercivic cooperation is essential for regional development. 城市间的合作对区域发展至关重要。
The intercivic bus service connects several major cities. 城际巴士服务连接几个主要城市。
近义词: intercity, intermunicipal
反义词: intracity, local
词性: adjective
