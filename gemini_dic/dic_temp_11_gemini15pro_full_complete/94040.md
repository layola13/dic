impart:/ɪmˈpɑːrt/ | v. |传授，告知；给予，赋予 | To communicate information, knowledge, or a quality to someone or something. |  She imparted her wisdom to her students. 她把自己的智慧传授给学生们。

The spice imparts a warm, earthy flavor to the dish.  这种香料给菜肴增添了温暖的泥土气息。| synonyms: communicate, convey, transmit, disclose, reveal, divulge | antonyms: withhold, conceal, suppress | verb
