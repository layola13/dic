noninsistence:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈsɪstəns/
noun
非坚持；不坚持；不强求
Lack of insistence; refraining from demanding or pressing.
Her noninsistence on the matter surprised everyone. 她对这件事不坚持的态度让每个人都感到惊讶。
non-insistence
synonyms: acquiescence, acceptance, compliance, resignation, yielding, forbearance, tolerance, leniency, indulgence, permissiveness
antonyms: insistence, persistence, perseverance, tenacity, firmness, resolve, determination, rigidity, inflexibility, dogmatism
n.
