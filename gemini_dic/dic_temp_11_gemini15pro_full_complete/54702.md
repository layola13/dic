disorientate: /dɪsˈɔːriənˌteɪt/ (UK), /dɪsˈɔːriənˌteɪt/ (US)
|v.|使迷失方向，使迷惑|To cause (someone) to lose their sense of direction or their bearings; to confuse.|The dense fog disorientated the hikers. 浓雾使徒步旅行者迷失了方向。|Synonyms: confuse, bewilder, perplex, confound|Antonyms: orient, guide, direct|verb
disorient: /dɪsˈɔːriənt/ (UK), /dɪsˈɔːriənt/ (US)
|v.|使迷失方向，使迷惑|To cause (someone) to lose their sense of direction or their bearings; to confuse.|The sudden change in light disoriented him. 突然的光线变化使他迷失了方向。|Synonyms: confuse, bewilder, perplex, confound|Antonyms: orient, guide, direct|verb

注意事项:  "disorientate" 和 "disorient" 在含义上没有区别，"disorientate" 更常见于英式英语，而 "disorient" 更常见于美式英语.
