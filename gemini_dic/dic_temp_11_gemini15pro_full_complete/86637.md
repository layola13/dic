hella:/ˈhɛlə/| adv.|非常，极其，很|Used to emphasize something, similar to "very" or "really".  Originates from Northern California slang, potentially a shortening of "hell of a".|例句：That party was hella fun. 那个派对非常有趣。

|例句：I'm hella tired after that workout.  那次锻炼后我累坏了。
|近义词：very, really, extremely, totally|反义词:slightly, a little|词性：副词
