distillable:/dɪˈstɪləbl/
adjective
可蒸馏的
Capable of being distilled; able to be purified or separated by distillation.
Ethanol is a distillable liquid. 乙醇是一种可蒸馏的液体。
The essential oils are distillable from the plant.  这些精油可以从植物中蒸馏出来。
近义词:purifiable, refinable
反义词:non-distillable, undistillable
词性:adjective
词源:From distil + -able.  Distil comes from the Latin word "distillare," meaning "to drip down."
