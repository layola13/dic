decarbonate:/diːˈkɑːrbəneɪt/
verb
脱碳
To remove carbon or carbon dioxide from something.
The government is committed to decarbonizing the economy by 2050. 政府致力于在2050年前实现经济脱碳。
The company is investing in technologies to decarbonize its operations. 该公司正在投资技术以使其运营脱碳。
decarbonise (British English)
carbonate (opposite for the process of adding carbon)
v.
