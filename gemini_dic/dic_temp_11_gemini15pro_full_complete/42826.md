courier:/ˈkʊriər/ (US), /ˈkʌriə(r)/ (UK)| n. |信使，快递员，导游|A person or company that delivers messages, packages, or documents.|The courier delivered the package this morning. 快递员今天早上送来了包裹。|Synonyms: messenger, delivery person, runner|Antonyms: recipient, receiver|noun

courier:/ˈkʊriər/ (US), /ˈkʌriə(r)/ (UK)| v. |递送，发送|To send something by courier.|I couriered the documents to the head office. 我把文件快递到了总部。|Synonyms: dispatch, send, ship|Antonyms: receive, collect|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *courier* (Modern French *courrier*), from *courir* "to run," from Latin *currere* "to run."
