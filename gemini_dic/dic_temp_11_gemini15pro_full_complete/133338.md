nonextendible:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɛndɪbl/
adj.
不可扩展的，不可延长的
Not capable of being extended or lengthened.
This contract is for a nonextendible period of one year.  这份合同为期一年，不可延期。
The warranty covers the product for a nonextendible period of 90 days.  该产品的保修期为90天，不可延期。
近义词:fixed, unchangeable, inflexible
反义词:extendable, expandable, flexible
词性:形容词

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "extend" (from Latin "extendere" - to stretch out) + suffix "-ible" (capable of being)
