inapathy:/ˌɪnˈæpəθi/
n.
冷漠，无动于衷
Lack of feeling, emotion, interest, or concern.
He showed complete inapathy towards the suffering of others.  他对别人的痛苦表现出完全的冷漠。
inapathy towards the political process 对政治进程的冷漠
近义词：indifference, apathy, unconcern, listlessness
反义词：interest, concern, enthusiasm, passion
词性: 名词

词源:  From in- (not) +‎ apathy.  由in-（不）+ apathy（冷漠）构成。
