newly-wed: /ˈnjuːli wɛd/ (US), /ˈnjuːli wed/ (UK)
newly-wed: adj. n.
newly-wed: 新婚的；新婚夫妇
newly-wed:  Referring to someone who has recently been married.  Formed by combining "newly" (recently) and "wed" (married).
newly-wed: The newly-wed couple looked blissfully happy.  这对新婚夫妇看起来幸福极了。
newly-wed: The hotel offered a special discount for newly-weds. 这家酒店为新婚夫妇提供特别折扣。
newly-wed: 近义词: just married, recently married
newly-wed: 反义词: long-married, divorced
newly-wed: 词性: 形容词, 名词 (通常用复数 newly-weds)
