de-polishes:/diːˈpɒlɪʃɪz/
verb
去除光泽，使失去光泽
Removes the polish from something.
The cleaner de-polishes the floor, leaving it matte.  清洁剂去除了地板的光泽，使其变得哑光。
dulls, takes the shine off
polishes, shines, buffs
verb

de-polishes:/diːˈpɒlɪʃɪz/
noun
去光剂，消光剂
A substance or process that removes polish.
Use this de-polish to remove the wax from your car. 使用这种去光剂去除你车上的蜡。
dulling agent, matting agent
polish, wax, sealant
noun
