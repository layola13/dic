infixation:/ɪnˌfɪkˈseɪʃən/| n.|词缀插入，中缀|The process of inserting an affix, or a morpheme, inside a word stem.  This is less common than prefixation or suffixation.|Example:  "Absogoddamlutely" is an example of infixation, with "goddamn" inserted into "absolutely".  绝对地变成了“绝对他妈地”，这就是一个词缀插入的例子，在“绝对地”中间插入了“他妈的”。

Example: In some languages, infixation is a common grammatical process. 在某些语言中，词缀插入是一种常见的语法过程。|近义词:tmesis (although not perfectly synonymous)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider) prefixation, suffixation|词性:noun
