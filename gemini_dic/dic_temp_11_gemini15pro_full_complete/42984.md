covetiveness:/ˈkʌvɪtɪsnəs/| n.|贪婪，贪欲|The state of being covetous; an excessive or inordinate desire to possess something, typically something belonging to someone else.|His covetiveness for power led to his downfall. 他对权力的贪婪导致了他的垮台。|近义词：greed, avarice, rapacity, cupidity|反义词：generosity, altruism|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *coveitousnesse, from Old French coveitise, from coveitos (“covetous”), from Latin cupidus (“desirous”), from cupere (“to desire”).
