impale:/ɪmˈpeɪl/| vt.|刺穿，钉住|To pierce with a sharp stake or point.|The knight was impaled by the enemy's lance. 骑士被敌人的长矛刺穿了。|The butterfly was impaled on a pin. 蝴蝶被钉在针上了。|近义词：pierce, transfix, spear|反义词:extricate, remove|verb

impaled:/ɪmˈpeɪld/| adj.|被刺穿的，被钉住的|Pierced with a sharp stake or point.|The impaled butterfly was part of the scientist's collection.  被钉住的蝴蝶是科学家收藏的一部分。|The impaled knight lay dead on the battlefield. 被刺穿的骑士死在战场上。|近义词：pierced, transfixed, speared|反义词:freed, released|adjective
