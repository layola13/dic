mouthiness:/ˈmaʊθɪnəs/
n.
爱顶嘴；多嘴；言语无礼
The tendency to talk back or be impudent.
Her mouthiness got her into trouble with the teacher. 她多嘴的毛病让她和老师的关系很紧张。
impudence, impertinence, sassiness
respectfulness, politeness
