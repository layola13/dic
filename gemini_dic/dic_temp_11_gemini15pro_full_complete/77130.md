gasbags:/ˈɡæsˌbæɡz/
noun
气囊；空话连篇的人；吹牛大王
A person who talks at length but says little of substance; a pompous, boastful person. Also, literally, a bag containing gas.
The politician was dismissed as a gasbag by his opponents.  这个政客被他的对手斥为夸夸其谈之徒。
The hot air balloon rose majestically, its gasbags filled to capacity.  热气球雄伟地升了起来，它的气囊充满了气。
synonyms: windbag, blowhard, braggart, loudmouth
antonyms: quiet person, listener, introvert
