eclectic:/ɪˈklɛktɪk/ | adj. | 折衷的；兼取各家的；博采众长的 | selecting from or made up of a variety of sources or styles |  She has an eclectic taste in music, enjoying everything from classical to hip-hop. 她对音乐的品味很广泛，从古典到嘻哈都喜欢。 | synonyms: diverse, varied, broad, wide-ranging | antonyms: narrow, limited, restricted | adjective

eclectics:/ɪˈklɛktɪks/ | n. | 折衷主义者；博采众家者 |  people who follow an eclectic method or approach | The conference attracted eclectics from various fields of study.  这次会议吸引了来自各个研究领域的博采众长者。 | synonyms: generalists | antonyms: specialists | noun
