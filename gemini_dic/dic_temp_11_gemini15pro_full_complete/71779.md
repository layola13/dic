fleck:/flek/
n.|斑点，小片，微粒|a small mark or spot, typically one of a different colour from its surroundings.|例句：There were flecks of paint on the floor. 地板上有一些油漆斑点。|近义词：speck, spot, dot, particle|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 large patch, expanse)|词性:名词

fleck:/flek/
vt.|使…有斑点，点缀|to mark with flecks.|例句：The bird's breast was flecked with brown. 这只鸟的胸部有棕色斑点。|近义词：speckle, dot, stipple|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 clear, cleanse)|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *flekke, from Old Norse *flekkr (“spot, stain”)
