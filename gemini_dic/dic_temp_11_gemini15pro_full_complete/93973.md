impacable:/ɪmˈpækəbəl/ | adj. | 无可指责的，无懈可击的 | Not capable of being affected or disturbed; incapable of sin or wrongdoing. |  His reputation is impacable. 他的声誉无可指责。 | 近义词: impeccable, flawless, faultless, unblemished, irreproachable | 反义词: flawed, imperfect, blemished, culpable | 词性: 形容词

impacable:/ɪmˈpækəbəl/ | adj. | 无情的，铁石心肠的 |  Not capable of being moved to pity or compassion. (Rare usage) |  The impacable judge showed no mercy.  铁面无私的法官没有表现出任何怜悯。(较少使用) | 近义词: ruthless, merciless, pitiless, unfeeling | 反义词: compassionate, merciful, sympathetic | 词性: 形容词
