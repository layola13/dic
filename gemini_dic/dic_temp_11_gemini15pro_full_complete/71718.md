flavoursome:/ˈfleɪvəsəm/| adj.|美味的，可口的|Having a pleasant and distinct flavour; tasty.| The chef prepared a flavoursome dish with fresh herbs. 厨师用新鲜香草做了一道美味的菜。|近义词：tasty, delicious, savoury, palatable|反义词：bland, tasteless, insipid|词性:形容词
词源: From flavour + -some.  Flavour ultimately derives from Old French *flaur, from Latin *flātor "smell, fragrance," from flāre "to blow."
