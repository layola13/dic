bmis:/ˈbmiːz/ (plural of bmi) | n. |身体质量指数（复数）| Body Mass Indexes, the plural form of BMI (Body Mass Index).  It's a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women. | The doctor tracked the patients' bmis over several years to study the effects of the new diet. 医生追踪了几年病人的身体质量指数，以研究新饮食的效果。| 近义词: (for singular BMI)  body mass index values | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
