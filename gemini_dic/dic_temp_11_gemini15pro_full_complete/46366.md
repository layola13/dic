dadaist:/ˈdɑːdəɪst/| n. adj.|达达主义者；达达主义的|A follower of Dadaism, an early 20th-century avant-garde artistic and literary movement that rejected logic and reason in favor of absurdity and irrationality.|The dadaist artist created a collage of random images and words.|Synonyms: absurdist, nihilist, iconoclast|Antonyms: traditionalist, classicist|Noun, Adjective

dadaism:/ˈdɑːdəɪzəm/| n.|达达主义|An early 20th-century avant-garde artistic and literary movement that rejected logic and reason in favor of absurdity and irrationality.|Dadaism emerged as a reaction to the horrors of World War I.|Synonyms: Dada, absurdism|Antonyms: traditionalism, classicism|Noun
