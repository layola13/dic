macrodactyly: /ˌmækroʊˈdæktɪli/ | n. | 巨指（趾）畸形 | A congenital condition characterized by abnormally large fingers or toes. | Example 1: Macrodactyly can affect one or more digits. 巨指畸形可以影响一个或多个手指或脚趾。

Example 2:  Surgery is sometimes necessary to correct macrodactyly. 有时需要手术来矫正巨指畸形。 | Synonyms: macrodactyl, megadigit | Antonyms: brachydactyly | noun



词源: macro- (large) + -dactyl (finger, toe) from Greek *makros* (large) and *daktylos* (finger, toe).
