outwear: /ˈaʊtwɛər/ (US), /ˈaʊtweə(r)/ (UK)| n. |外衣，外套|Clothing designed to be worn outdoors, especially over other clothes, for protection from the elements.| She bought a new outwear for the winter. 她买了一件新的冬季外套。| Examples:  He layered his outwear to stay warm in the cold. 他把外套叠穿以在寒冷的天气里保暖。  She chose a brightly colored outwear for better visibility in the fog.  她选择了一件颜色鲜艳的外套以便在雾中更容易被看到。 |Synonyms: outerwear, coat, jacket, overgarment|Antonyms: underwear, undergarment|Noun
