frondous:/ˈfrɒndəs/ (US) /ˈfrɒndəs/ (UK)| adj.|多叶的；枝繁叶茂的|Characterized by abundance of leaves or fronds; leafy.|例句：The frondous ferns created a lush, green carpet on the forest floor.  茂盛的蕨类植物在森林地面上形成了 lush, green carpet。

例句：The tree was frondous and provided ample shade on a hot summer day. 这棵树枝繁叶茂，在炎热的夏日提供了充足的阴凉。|近义词：leafy, luxuriant, lush|反义词：bare, leafless|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frondōsus*, from frōns (“leaf, foliage”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives indicating abundance or fullness).
