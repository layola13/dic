monothelism:/məˈnɒθəlɪzəm/
n.
一志论
The doctrine that Christ had only one will.
Monothelism was condemned as heresy. 一志论被谴责为异端邪说。
近义词:monothelitism
反义词:dyothelitism
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μόνος (mónos, “alone, single, only”) + θέλημα (thélēma, “will”).
