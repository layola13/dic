gymnoblastea:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈblæstiə/ | n. | 裸芽螅纲 | An order of hydrozoans characterized by having the hydroid stage dominant and lacking a protective covering (theca) around the polyps.  |  Example: Gymnoblastea includes hydroids like the common Cordylophora. 裸芽螅纲包括像常见的Cordylophora这样的水螅。 | Example:  Scientists are studying the reproductive cycles of various Gymnoblastea species. 科学家正在研究各种裸芽螅纲物种的繁殖周期。| Synonym: Athecata | Antonym: Calyptoblastea | Noun
