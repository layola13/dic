nonsolutions:/ˌnɒn.səˈluː.ʃənz/
n.|无解，无效的方案|Actions that do not solve a problem or address an issue effectively.  These are approaches or attempts that fail to provide a resolution or desired outcome.|The committee proposed several nonsolutions that only exacerbated the problem.|近义词:ineffective measures, failed attempts, dead ends|反义词:solutions, remedies, answers|词性:名词

Etymology:
non-:  Latin prefix meaning "not"
solution: from Latin *solutio*, from solvere "to loosen, dissolve, solve"
