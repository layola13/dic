dongle: /ˈdɒŋɡəl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːŋɡəl/ (US) | n. | 电子狗，加密狗 | A small piece of hardware that connects to a computer or other device, usually to unlock or enable specific software or functionality. | Example: I need a dongle to use this software. 我需要一个加密狗才能使用这个软件。

Example:  She lost the dongle for her internet access. 她弄丢了上网用的加密狗。

Synonym: adapter, hardware key, security key

Antonym: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "built-in" or "integrated" as opposed to needing an external device)

Word type: Noun
