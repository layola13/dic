brusquely:/ˈbrʌskli/ | adv. | 唐突地，粗鲁地 | In a blunt, abrupt, and often rude manner. | "She answered him brusquely, clearly annoyed by the interruption." 她唐突地回答了他，显然是被打扰了。|例句：He brusquely told her to leave. 他粗鲁地让她离开。| 近义词：abruptly, curtly, sharply, roughly | 反义词：politely, gently, kindly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From the French word "brusque," meaning "lively, fierce, rough," ultimately from the Italian word "brusco," meaning "sour, tart."
