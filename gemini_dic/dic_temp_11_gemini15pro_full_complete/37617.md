communional:/kəˈmjuːnjənəl/
adj.
共享的，共有的，共同的
Relating to or involving a group of people; shared by a group.
例句：
The communal kitchen was a hub of activity during mealtimes.  公共厨房在用餐时间是活动的中心。

The villagers shared a communal sense of loss after the fire.  火灾过后，村民们有一种共同的失落感。

近义词：shared, collective, joint, common
反义词：private, individual, personal
词性: adjective


communional:/kəˈmjuːnjənəl/
adj.
圣餐的
Relating to Holy Communion.
例句：
The communal wine was blessed by the priest. 圣餐酒由神父祝圣。

They gathered for a communal service. 他们聚集在一起参加圣餐仪式。
近义词：eucharistic, sacramental
反义词：secular
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *communionalis, from Latin communis "common".  源自晚期拉丁语 *communionalis，来自拉丁语 communis “共同的”。
