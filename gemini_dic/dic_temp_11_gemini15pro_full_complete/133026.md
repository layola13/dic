nonentreatingly:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtriːtɪŋli/ | adv. | 无威胁地；非治疗性地 |  In a way that does not pose a threat or is not intended as a treatment. | He approached the frightened animal nonentreatingly, with slow, gentle movements.  他缓慢而轻柔地靠近那只受惊的动物，没有表现出任何威胁。| The doctor explained the procedure nonentreatingly, emphasizing that it was purely diagnostic. 医生以非治疗性的方式解释了这个程序，强调它纯粹是为了诊断。 | synonym: harmlessly, innocuously | antonym: threateningly, aggressively | 词性: 副词
