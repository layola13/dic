nuance:/ˈnjuːɑːns/ (US), /ˈnjuːɒns/ (UK)| n.|细微差别；微妙之处|a subtle difference in or shade of meaning, expression, or sound.|The poem captured the nuances of the changing seasons. 这首诗捕捉到了四季变化的微妙之处。|synonyms: subtlety, shade, distinction, refinement|antonyms:  blatancy, obviousness|noun

nuance:/ˈnjuːɑːns/ (US), /ˈnjuːɒns/ (UK)| vt.|使微妙化；使具有细微差别|to give nuances to.|He nuanced his performance with subtle gestures. 他用细微的手势使他的表演更加微妙。|synonyms: refine, vary, modulate|antonyms: simplify, coarsen|verb
