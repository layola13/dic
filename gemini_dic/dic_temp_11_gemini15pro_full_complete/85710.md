havoc:/ˈhævək/
noun
中文翻译：大破坏，浩劫
英文解析：widespread destruction
例句：The earthquake caused havoc and destruction. 地震造成了严重的破坏。

例句：The virus wreaked havoc on the computer system. 病毒对计算机系统造成了严重破坏。

近义词：devastation, ruin, destruction, mayhem
反义词：order, peace, calm
词性：noun

词源: From the Anglo-Norman *havoc, borrowed from the Old French cri de haro ("cry of haro"), a cry for help or justice.  The word originally referred to the right to pillage granted to soldiers, and later evolved to mean general devastation.
