nonostentatious:/ˌnɒnɒstənˈteɪʃəs/ |adj.| 不炫耀的；不铺张的；不招摇的 | Not ostentatious; characterized by reserve or lack of display; modest. | She lived a nonostentatious life, preferring simple clothes and quiet evenings at home. 她过着简朴的生活，喜欢简单的衣服和安静的夜晚在家。| He gave a nonostentatious but generous donation to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐了一笔不张扬但慷慨的捐款。 |  Synonyms: modest, unpretentious, understated, simple, plain | Antonyms: ostentatious, showy, flamboyant, pretentious, extravagant | Adjective
