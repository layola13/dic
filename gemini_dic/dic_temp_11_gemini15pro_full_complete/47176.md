day: /deɪ/
n.|一天; 白天; 时期; 节日|A period of twenty-four hours, especially from twelve o'clock midnight to twelve o'clock midnight the following night.  The part of a twenty-four-hour period which is not night; daylight hours.|例句：It's been a long day. 今天是漫长的一天。

例句：We spent the day at the beach. 我们在海滩上度过了一天。|近义词：date, daytime|反义词:night|词性:名词
