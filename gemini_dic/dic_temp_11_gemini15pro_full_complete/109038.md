legal's:/ˈliːɡlz/| n.|法律事务；法律部门；律师事务所|Something that belongs to or is related to the legal profession or a legal entity.  It's the possessive form of "legal," though using "legal's" is often grammatically awkward and generally avoided.  It's better to rephrase using "of the legal department/team/profession".|The legal's decision is final. (Awkward - better: The legal department's decision is final.)|近义词: law, jurisprudence |反义词: illegal, unlawful|词性:Noun (possessive)
