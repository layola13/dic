multipresent:/ˌmʌltiˈprɛzənt/| adj.|同时出现在多处的，无所不在的|Being or appearing to be in more than one place at the same time.|The CEO seemed multipresent, attending meetings in multiple cities via video conference on the same day. 首席执行官似乎无处不在，同一天通过视频会议参加了多个城市的会议。|近义词：ubiquitous, omnipresent|反义词：localized, confined|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From multi- (meaning "many") + present (meaning "being in a specified place").  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many". "Present" comes from Latin "praesent-" meaning "being at hand, being before one, existing."
