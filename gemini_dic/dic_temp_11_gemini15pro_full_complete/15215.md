The mountains formed a dramatic backdrop to the scene.
群山构成了这幅景象的壮丽背景。


The political backdrop to the negotiations was complex.
谈判的政治背景很复杂。


近义词：background, setting, scenery | 反义词: foreground | 词性: 名词
