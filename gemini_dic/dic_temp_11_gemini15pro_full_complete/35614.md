coastguard:/ˈkoʊstɡɑːrd/ /ˈkəʊstɡɑːd/| n.| 海岸警卫队；海岸警备队|An organization responsible for maritime safety, security, and search and rescue near a nation's coasts.  一个负责国家海岸附近海上安全、保安和搜救的组织。|The coastguard rescued the stranded sailors. 海岸警卫队救起了搁浅的船员。|近义词：coast guard, lifeboat service|反义词: None|Noun
