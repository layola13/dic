denay: /dɪˈneɪ/
verb
否认，拒绝
to say that something is not true
例句：He denayed all knowledge of the crime. 他否认对这起犯罪有任何了解。

例句：She denayed having seen him before. 她否认以前见过他。

近义词：deny, contradict, refute, reject
反义词：admit, accept, acknowledge, confirm
词性：v.
