anodized:/ˈænəˌdaɪzd/| adj.| 阳极氧化的，经过阳极氧化处理的 |Coated with a protective oxide layer by an electrolytic process in which the metal forms the anode.  | The anodized aluminum frame is both lightweight and corrosion-resistant.  阳极氧化的铝框架既轻便又耐腐蚀。 | synonyms: oxidized, coated | antonyms: uncoated, untreated | adjective

anodize:/ˈænəˌdaɪz/| vt.| 阳极氧化 |To coat (a metal, especially aluminum) with a protective oxide layer by an electrolytic process in which the metal forms the anode. | The company anodizes aluminum parts to increase their durability.  这家公司对铝部件进行阳极氧化处理以提高其耐用性。 | synonyms: oxidize, coat | antonyms: strip, uncoat | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From anode + -ize. Anode from Greek _anodos_ "way up," from _ana_ "up" + _hodos_ "way."
