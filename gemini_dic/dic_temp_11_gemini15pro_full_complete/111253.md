litigating: /ˈlɪtɪɡeɪtɪŋ/
verb
进行诉讼;打官司
present participle of litigate:  taking a claim or dispute to a court of law.
The company is litigating a patent infringement case.  该公司正在就专利侵权案件进行诉讼。
They are litigating against the government. 他们正在起诉政府。
Synonyms: suing, contesting, prosecuting
Antonyms: mediating, arbitrating, settling, compromising
verb (present participle)
