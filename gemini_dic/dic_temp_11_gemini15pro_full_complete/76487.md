galago:/ɡəˈleɪɡoʊ/ (US), /ɡəˈlɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 婴猴，丛猴|A small, nocturnal primate with large eyes and ears, native to Africa.|The galago leaped from branch to branch with incredible agility. 婴猴以惊人的敏捷性从一根树枝跳到另一根树枝。|近义词：bushbaby|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From Bantu languages, possibly from Yao *galago*.
