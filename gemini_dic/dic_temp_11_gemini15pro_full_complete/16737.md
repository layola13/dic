bartends:/bɑːrˈtɛndz/
verb
调酒;担任酒吧招待
works as a bartender
例句：He bartends at a local pub.  他在当地一家酒吧担任酒保。

例句：She bartends part-time to earn extra money. 她兼职做酒保赚外快。

近义词：mixes drinks, serves drinks, works behind the bar
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider)  abstains, avoids alcohol, is a teetotaler
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From bar + tend (to attend to).  "Bar" refers to the counter where drinks are served.  "Tend" means to take care of or serve.
