gourmand:/ˈɡʊərmɑːnd/ (US), /ˈɡɔːmənd/ (UK)| n.| 美食家；贪吃的人|A person who enjoys good food and drink in large quantities; a connoisseur of food. | He's a gourmand who loves to try new restaurants. 他是一位喜欢尝试新餐厅的美食家。 | Synonyms: epicure, gastronome, foodie, gourmet | Antonyms: ascetic, abstemious | noun

gourmet:/ˈɡʊərmā/ (US), /ˈɡɔːmeɪ/ (UK)| n. adj.| 美食家；精美的|A connoisseur of food and drink; (adj.) of high quality and expensive. |He is a true gourmet, appreciating the subtleties of fine cuisine. 他是一位真正的美食家，欣赏精致菜肴的微妙之处。  As an adjective:  They serve gourmet meals at the restaurant. 这家餐厅提供美味佳肴。| Synonyms: epicure, connoisseur, gastronome (n.);  delicious, exquisite, fine (adj.) | Antonyms: glutton (n., sometimes); plain, simple, basic (adj.) | noun, adjective
