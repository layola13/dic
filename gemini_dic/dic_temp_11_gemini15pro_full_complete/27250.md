calorific: /ˌkæləˈrɪfɪk/ | adj. | 产热的；热量的；高热量的 | Relating to the amount of heat produced when something burns. |  This chocolate bar is very calorific. 这块巧克力棒的热量很高。| Synonyms: high-calorie, energy-rich, heat-producing | Antonyms: low-calorie, diet, light | adjective
