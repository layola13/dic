lava: /ˈlɑːvə/ (UK) /ˈlɑːvə/ (US)| n. |熔岩，火山岩|Molten rock that erupts from a volcano or fissure, or solid rock resulting from cooling of this. |The lava flowed down the mountainside. 熔岩顺着山坡流了下来。

The volcano erupted, spewing lava and ash into the sky. 火山爆发，喷出熔岩和火山灰。|近义词：magma, molten rock|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps solidified rock) | noun
