illogicalities:/ɪˌlɒdʒɪˈkælɪtiz/
noun
不合逻辑之处；不合逻辑； illogicality的复数形式
The illogicalities of the legal system are frustrating.  法律制度的不合逻辑之处令人沮丧。
synonyms: absurdities, fallacies, inconsistencies, irrationalities
antonyms: logic, reason, rationality, sense
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

illogicality:/ɪˌlɒdʒɪˈkælɪti/
noun
不合逻辑；不合逻辑之处
There's an illogicality in your argument.  你的论点中有一个不合逻辑之处。
synonyms: absurdity, fallacy, inconsistency, irrationality
antonyms: logic, reason, rationality, sense
词性: 名词 (singular noun)

illogical:/ɪˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
不合逻辑的；不合理的
It's illogical to assume that because something is expensive, it must be good quality.  认为昂贵的东西一定质量好是不合逻辑的。
synonyms: absurd, fallacious, inconsistent, irrational, unreasonable
antonyms: logical, rational, reasonable, sensible
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From illogic + -al + -ity (suffix forming abstract nouns).  Illogic comes from in- (not) + logic.
