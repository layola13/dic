irre:/ɪˈrɛv/ (stress on the second syllable) | prefix | 不可逆的，不能取消的 | not, opposite of; reversible meaning able to be reversed.  Irre- indicates the impossibility of reversing something. |  The damage to the ecosystem is irreversible.  生态系统的破坏是不可逆转的。| Near synonyms: un-, non-, in- | Opposite: re- (implying reversal) | prefix
