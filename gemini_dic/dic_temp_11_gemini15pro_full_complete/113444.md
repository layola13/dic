lynchian:/ˈlɪntʃən/| adj.|林奇式的，怪诞的，超现实的，暴力凶残的|Characteristic of the surreal, violent, dreamlike, and often darkly humorous world depicted in the films of David Lynch.|例句：The film had a distinctly Lynchian atmosphere, full of unsettling imagery and unexplained events. 这部电影有一种明显的林奇式氛围，充满了令人不安的图像和无法解释的事件。

例句：The deserted town had a strange, Lynchian quality that made me feel uneasy.  这个荒凉的小镇有一种奇怪的，林奇式的特质，让我感到不安。|近义词：surreal, bizarre, grotesque, eerie, unsettling|反义词：normal, ordinary, mundane|词性:adjective
