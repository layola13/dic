abrase:/əˈbreɪz/ /əˈbreɪs/ | v. |擦伤，磨损；|To rub or wear away by friction; to scrape or wear off.|The rocks abraded the ship's hull. 岩石磨损了船体。|近义词：scrape, chafe, erode, wear down|反义词:build up, augment|verb
词源: From Latin *abrasus*, past participle of *abradere* "to scrape off," from *ab* "off, away from" + *radere* "to scrape, shave."
