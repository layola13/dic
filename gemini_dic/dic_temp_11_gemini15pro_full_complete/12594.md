assagai:/ˈæsəɡaɪ/ /ˈæsəɡɑːɪ/| n. |投枪，短矛|A slender, iron-tipped spear, usually thrown by hand, used chiefly in southern Africa.|The Zulu warriors advanced, brandishing their assagais.  祖鲁族战士挥舞着他们的投枪前进。|近义词：spear, javelin|反义词:shield, armor|词性:noun

Etymology: From the Zulu word *isi-agayi*
