folk-rockers:/ˈfoʊkˌrɑːkərz/
n.
民谣摇滚歌手；民谣摇滚乐队
Musicians or bands who play folk-rock music, a genre that blends elements of folk music and rock music.
The folk-rockers played a set of their greatest hits.  民谣摇滚歌手演奏了他们的热门歌曲。
The festival featured several popular folk-rockers.  这个节日有几位受欢迎的民谣摇滚歌手。
近义词：folk musicians, acoustic rock artists
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider genres far removed like death metal bands)
词性: Noun


folk:/foʊk/
n., adj.
人们；民间的
People in general; relating to the traditional customs of a community.
Folk music is often passed down through generations. 民间音乐通常代代相传。
Many folk tales involve magical creatures. 许多民间故事都涉及神奇的生物。
近义词：people, traditional, ethnic
反义词: modern, contemporary
词性: Noun, Adjective

rock:/rɑːk/
n., v.
岩石；摇滚乐；摇晃
A large mass of stone; a type of popular music; to move back and forth.
The climber scaled the steep rock face. 登山者攀登了陡峭的岩壁。
Rock music emerged in the mid-20th century. 摇滚乐出现在20世纪中期。
The baby's cradle rocked gently. 婴儿的摇篮轻轻地摇晃着。
近义词：stone, boulder, sway
反义词: pop (music genre contrast), still
词性: Noun, Verb

-er:/ər/

近义词: -or, -ist (sometimes)
反义词:  N/A (suffix, no direct antonym)
