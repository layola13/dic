arcos:/ˈɑːrkoʊz/ (US), /ˈɑːkɒz/ (UK)| n. | 反余弦 | The inverse cosine function, often written as arccos(x) or cos⁻¹(x), gives the angle whose cosine is x. | The arccos of 0.5 is 60 degrees.  反余弦0.5是60度。

The arccos function is crucial in trigonometry. 反余弦函数在三角学中至关重要。 | 近义词: inverse cosine | 反义词: cosine | 词性: noun
