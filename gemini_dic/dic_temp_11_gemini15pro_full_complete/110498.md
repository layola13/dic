limation:/lɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 升华; (心理)升华作用 | The process of turning a solid directly into a gas without going through a liquid state.  The channeling of unacceptable impulses into socially acceptable activities. |  Example 1: The dry ice underwent sublimation, disappearing into a cloud of vapor. 干冰经历了升华，消失成一团蒸汽。

Example 2:  He found sublimation in his artwork, transforming his anger into beautiful paintings. 他在艺术创作中找到了升华，将他的愤怒转化为美丽的图画。 | synonym: vaporization, refinement | antonym: condensation, deposition | noun
