allot:/əˈlɒt/ (UK) /əˈlɑːt/ (US)| vt.|分配，拨给|to give something, especially a share of something available, for a particular purpose|例句：They allotted everyone a separate desk. 他们给每个人都分配了一张单独的桌子。

例句：The government has allotted £10 million for the project. 政府已经为该项目拨款1000万英镑。

近义词：allocate, assign, apportion, distribute|反义词: withhold, retain|词性:动词
