depository:/dɪˈpɒzɪtəri/ /dɪˈpɑːzɪtɔːri/
n.
存放处，储藏处，保管处；仓库；受托者
A place where something is deposited for safekeeping or storage.  A person or organization entrusted with something for safekeeping.
The bank serves as a depository for the company's funds.  这家银行充当公司资金的存放处。
The museum is a depository of local history.  这家博物馆是当地历史的宝库。
storehouse, repository, warehouse
n/a
noun
