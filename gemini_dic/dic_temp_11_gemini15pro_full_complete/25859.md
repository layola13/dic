burka:/ˈbɜːrkə/ (UK), /ˈbɜːrkɑː/ (US)|n.|罩袍，波卡|A long, loose garment covering the whole body from head to feet, worn in public by many Muslim women.|She wore a burka that concealed her entire face. 她穿着一件遮住整个脸的罩袍。|The burka provided protection from the sun and sand.  罩袍提供了防晒和防沙的保护。|近义词：niqab, chador, hijab|反义词：(None -  Lack of a specific antonym.  One might consider Western clothing, but this isn't a direct opposite.)|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Urdu بُرقع (burqaʿ), from Persian بُرقع (burqaʿ), from Arabic بُرْقُع (burquʿ, “face veil”).
