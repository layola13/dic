divertimento:/ˌdɪvɜːrtɪˈmɛntoʊ/ /ˌdaɪvɜːrtɪˈmɛntoʊ/
| n. |嬉游曲，娱乐性乐曲|A light and entertaining composition, typically for chamber ensemble, often in several movements.|Mozart composed several divertimentos for various instrumental combinations. 莫扎特创作了几首不同乐器组合的嬉游曲。|
|近义词：serenade, cassation, divertimento|反义词:dirge, lament|名词|
