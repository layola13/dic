awfully:/ˈɔːf(ə)li/ /ˈɑːfəli/| adv.|非常地，极其地，可怕地|Used to emphasize the degree of something, often something unpleasant or negative. It can also be used (less commonly now) to mean 'very'.|The traffic was awfully heavy this morning. 今早交通非常拥堵。|近义词：very, extremely, terribly, dreadfully|反义词: slightly, a little|词性:副词
