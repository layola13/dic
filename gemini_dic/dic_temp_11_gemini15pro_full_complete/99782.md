intermembranous:/ˌɪntərˈmɛmbrənəs/| adj.|膜内的，膜间的|Located or occurring between membranes.  Formed within a membrane, not from cartilage.|例句：Intramembranous ossification is the process by which flat bones of the skull are formed. 膜内成骨是颅骨扁平骨形成的过程。

例句：The intermembranous space is crucial for cellular function. 膜间隙对细胞功能至关重要。|近义词：interlaminar, intrameningeal|反义词: extramembranous (this is a less common antonym and sometimes context-specific)|词性: adjective
