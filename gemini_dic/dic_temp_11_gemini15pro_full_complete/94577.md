impoverishing: /ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 使贫困的；使枯竭的 | Making something weaker or worse in quality, especially by reducing its richness or effectiveness. | The war has had an impoverishing effect on the country. 战争使这个国家变得贫困。| synonyms: depleting, debilitating, exhausting, weakening | antonyms: enriching, enhancing, strengthening | adjective

impoverish: /ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃ/ | vt. | 使贫穷；使枯竭 | To make someone very poor. To make something weaker or worse in quality. |  Years of drought have impoverished the soil. 多年的干旱使土壤贫瘠。| synonyms: bankrupt, ruin, deplete, exhaust | antonyms: enrich, enhance, strengthen | verb
