defogs:/diːˈfɒɡz/ | v. | 除雾，使清晰 | Clears fog or condensation from a surface, often a windshield or window. | The car's defroster defogs the windshield quickly. 汽车的除霜器很快地清除挡风玻璃上的雾气。| The pilot defogged the cockpit window before takeoff. 飞行员在起飞前清除驾驶舱窗户上的雾气。 | synonyms: clears, demists, dehumidifies | antonyms: fogs, mists, obscures | verb
