mayapan:/ˌmɑːjəˈpɑːn/
n.
玛雅潘
A large pre-Columbian Maya city and important archaeological site located in the Yucatán Peninsula of Mexico.
The ruins of Mayapan offer a glimpse into the late Maya period.  玛雅潘遗址让我们得以一窥玛雅晚期的情况。
Mayapan was a significant political and economic center. 玛雅潘曾是一个重要的政治和经济中心。
近义词:Chichen Itza, Uxmal (other Maya cities)
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
