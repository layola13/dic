devoid-of:/dɪˈvɔɪd əv/
adjective
缺乏的，没有的
entirely lacking or free from
The landscape was devoid of life.  这片土地上毫无生气。
Synonyms: lacking, empty, barren, bereft, destitute
Antonyms: full, replete, abundant
词性: 形容词
