outgo:/ˈaʊtɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈaʊtɡəʊ/ (UK)
n.|支出，开销|An outgo is an amount of money that you spend regularly.  支出是指你定期花费的金额。
|例句：The monthly outgoings on the house are considerable. 这房子每月的开销相当大。

|例句：Careful budgeting helps to control outgoings. 精打细算有助于控制开支。
|近义词：expenditure, expense, cost, outlay|反义词：income, revenue
词性:名词
