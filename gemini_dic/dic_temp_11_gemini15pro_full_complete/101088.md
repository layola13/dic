inveracity:/ˌɪnvəˈræsɪti/
n.
lack of truthfulness; untruthfulness
The politician's inveracity was well known, but his charisma kept him in office.  这位政客的不诚实是众所周知的，但他的个人魅力使他得以保住职位。
His inveracity cost him his job and his reputation. 他的不诚实让他丢了工作和名声。
dishonesty, falsehood, mendacity, untruthfulness
truthfulness, honesty, veracity
noun
