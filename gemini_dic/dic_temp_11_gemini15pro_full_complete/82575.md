guardant:/ˈɡɑːrdənt/ | adj. | 警惕的，守护的; (纹章学) 左顾的 |  (Heraldry) In a position of vigilance, facing the observer, typically with the head turned to the left. Also used more generally to mean watchful or vigilant. | The lion rampant guardant on the shield symbolized the king's vigilance. 盾牌上左顾的跃狮象征着国王的警惕。 |  vigilant, watchful, alert | unaware, inattentive | adjective

guardant:/ˈɡɑːrdənt/ | n. | 守卫者 | A guardian or protector. (This usage is less common than the adjective form.) |  He stood as a guardant at the gate. 他像个守卫一样站在门口。 | guardian, protector, watchman | assailant, attacker | noun
