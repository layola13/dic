morning-room:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ ruːm/ /ˈmɔːrnɪŋ rʊm/
n.
早晨起居室，早餐厅
A room, often with large windows, used as a living room or breakfast room, especially in the morning.
She enjoyed reading in the bright morning-room. 她喜欢在明亮的早晨起居室里阅读。
The family often gathered in the morning-room for breakfast and to plan their day.  这家人经常在早晨起居室里吃早餐并计划他们的一天。
synonyms: living room, sitting room, breakfast room, sunroom
antonyms: bedroom, dining room
