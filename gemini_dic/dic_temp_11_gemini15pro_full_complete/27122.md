calipers:/ˈkælɪpərz/| n. |卡尺，测径器|A measuring instrument with two adjustable jaws used to measure the thickness or diameter of an object.|The engineer used calipers to measure the diameter of the pipe. 工程师用卡尺测量了管子的直径。|Synonyms: gauge, micrometer|Antonyms: None|Noun

caliper:/ˈkælɪpər/| n. |卡钳，制动钳|A device, typically used in vehicles, that applies pressure to a disc brake to slow or stop a wheel.|The car's calipers needed replacing after years of wear and tear. 汽车的卡钳在多年的磨损后需要更换。|Synonyms: brake caliper|Antonyms: None|Noun
