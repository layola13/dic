entoglossal:/ɛnˈtɒɡlɒsəl/
adjective
内舌的；舌内的
Located within the tongue; specifically (of certain insect mouthparts) attached within the preoral cavity and capable of only limited protrusion.
The fly uses its entoglossal mouthparts to lap up liquids.  苍蝇用它内舌的口器舔舐液体。
Entoglossal mouthparts are characteristic of certain insect groups. 内舌口器是某些昆虫群的特征。
Synonyms:  intra-lingual (less common)
Antonyms:  exserted, protrusible
词性: adjective
