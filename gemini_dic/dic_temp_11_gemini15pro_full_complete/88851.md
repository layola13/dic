hogwash:/ˈhɒɡwɒʃ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːɡwɑːʃ/ (US)| n. |废话；胡说八道；空洞的言辞|Nonsense; empty talk.  It's often used to dismiss something as untrue or unimportant.|例句：That's a load of hogwash!  那是一派胡言！

例句：He dismissed her claims as complete hogwash. 他认为她的说法完全是胡说八道。

近义词：nonsense, balderdash, rubbish, bunk, claptrap, twaddle|反义词:truth, fact, reality|词性:名词
