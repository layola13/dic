mimicking:/ˈmɪmɪkɪŋ/ | v. | 模仿，效仿 | Imitating someone or something's behavior, speech, or appearance. | She was mimicking her teacher's accent. 她正在模仿她老师的口音。 | He was mimicking the bird's song. 他在模仿鸟的叫声。| 近义词：imitating, copying, aping, emulating | 反义词：originating, innovating | 词性: 动词 (present participle of mimic)

mimic:/ˈmɪmɪk/ | n. v. adj. | 模仿者，模仿的 |  n. A person who copies the behavior or speech of others. v. To imitate someone or something's behavior, speech, or appearance. adj. Imitative. |  n. He's a talented mimic and can impersonate anyone. 他是一个有才华的模仿者，可以模仿任何人。 v.  Parrots can mimic human speech. 鹦鹉可以模仿人类的讲话。 adj. The snake's mimicry of a vine is remarkable. 这条蛇对藤蔓的模仿非常出色。|近义词：imitator, impersonator (n.); imitate, copy (v.); imitative (adj.)| 反义词：original (n., adj.)|词性: 名词, 动词, 形容词

词源: From Greek *mimos* "mime, imitator, actor"
