fluate:/ˈfluːeɪt/ | n. | 氟化物；氟酸盐 | A chemical compound containing fluorine.  A salt of hydrofluoric acid. | Sodium fluate is used in insecticides. 氟化钠用于杀虫剂。| 近义词: fluoride | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

fluate:/ˈfluːeɪt/ | v. | 氟化；用氟处理 | To treat or combine with fluorine or a fluorine compound. | The water supply is fluated to prevent tooth decay. 供水经过氟化处理以防止蛀牙。| 近义词: fluoridate, fluoridize | 反义词: defluorinate | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fluatus, past participle of *fluare (“to flow”), from fluō (“to flow”). Related to fluent and fluid.  The chemical sense arose from the use of fluorspar (calcium fluoride) as a flux.
