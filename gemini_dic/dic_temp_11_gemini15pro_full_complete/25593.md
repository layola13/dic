bummery:/ˈbʌməri/| n.|令人失望的事；扫兴的事|Something that is unpleasant, disappointing, or depressing. |例句：Losing the game in the final seconds was a real bummery. 在最后几秒钟输掉比赛真是太令人失望了。

例句：It's a bummery that the concert was cancelled. 音乐会被取消真是太扫兴了。|近义词：disappointment, setback, letdown, drag, downer|反义词:joy, blessing, boon|词性:名词
