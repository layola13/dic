mucinogen:/mjuːˈsɪnədʒən/ | n. | 粘蛋白原 | A substance that stimulates the formation of mucin. |  Goblet cells secrete mucinogen which hydrates to form mucus. 杯状细胞分泌粘蛋白原，水合后形成粘液。| synonym:  mucus precursor | antonym: None | noun
