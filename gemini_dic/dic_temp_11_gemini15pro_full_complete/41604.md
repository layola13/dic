corpuscularian:/kɔːrˈpʌskjʊˈlɛəriən/
| adj. n.|微粒的，微粒说的；微粒说信奉者|Relating to or based on the theory that all matter is composed of minute particles.  A believer in the theory that all matter is composed of minute particles.| Newton was a corpuscularian in his approach to optics. 牛顿在光学研究中采用微粒说。|近义词：atomic, particulate|反义词: wave-like|adjective
