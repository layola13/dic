detests:/dɪˈtɛsts/| v.|憎恶，厌恶|To feel intense dislike or hatred for something or someone.|She detests hypocrisy. 她厌恶虚伪。|近义词：abhors, loathes, hates|反义词：loves, adores, likes|verb

词源: From Latin detestari "to curse while calling a deity to witness; to execrate, abominate," from de- "down, away" + testari "to bear witness, testify" (from testis "witness").
