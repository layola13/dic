bowdlerism:/ˈboʊdlərɪzəm/| n.|删节；净化；(为迎合道德标准而对书籍、剧本等的) 删改；|An instance of expurgating a text, usually by omitting or modifying supposedly offensive words or passages.|The play was full of bowdlerisms, removing all the original author's swear words and sexual references.  这出戏充满了删改，删除了原作者所有的脏话和性暗示。|censorship, expurgation|inclusion, unexpurgated|noun

bowdlerize:/ˈboʊdləˌraɪz/| v.|删节；净化；(为迎合道德标准而对书籍、剧本等进行) 删改|To expurgate (a text) by omitting or modifying parts considered offensive.|The editor bowdlerized the novel before publishing it, removing all references to sex and violence. 编辑在出版小说之前对其进行了删改，删除了所有关于性和暴力的内容。|censor, expurgate|include, retain|verb
