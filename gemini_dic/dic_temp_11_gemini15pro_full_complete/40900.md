cool-headed:/ˌkuːlˈhedɪd/ | adj. | 冷静的；头脑冷静的 | Having the ability to stay calm and think clearly in difficult situations. |  例句：She remained cool-headed in the emergency and saved the day.  她在紧急情况下保持冷静，挽救了局面。 | 近义词：calm, composed, level-headed, unflappable | 反义词：hot-headed, excitable, panicky | 词性: 形容词

cool-headed:/ˌkuːlˈhedɪd/ | adj. | 冷静的；头脑冷静的 |  A cool-headed person is not easily upset and can think clearly even when under pressure. |例句：The pilot's cool-headed response averted a disaster.  飞行员冷静的反应避免了一场灾难。 | 近义词：collected, self-possessed | 反义词：rash, impulsive | 词性: 形容词
