incontinently:/ˌɪnkɒnˈtɪnəntli/ /ˌɪnkɑːnˈtɪnəntli/
adverb
立即地，马上；失禁地
immediately, at once; uncontrollably
She began to laugh incontinently. 她忍不住大笑起来。
He departed incontinently for the airport. 他立即动身前往机场。
近义词:immediately, forthwith, instantly, promptly, directly, straightway
反义词:later, subsequently
词性:adverb

incontinent:/ˌɪnkɒnˈtɪnənt/ /ˌɪnkɑːnˈtɪnənt/
adjective
失禁的；不能自制的
unable to control urination or defecation; lacking self-restraint
He became doubly incontinent after the stroke.  中风后，他大小便都失禁了。
She was incontinent with rage. 她怒不可遏。
近义词:uncontrolled, unrestrained, immoderate
反义词:continent, controlled, restrained
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *incontinenter, from Latin incontinens ("immoderate, unrestrained") + -ly/-ter.  The Latin word is from in- ("not") + continens ("holding together, restraining"), present participle of continēre ("to hold together, contain, restrain").
