hobblebush:/ˈhɒblˌbʊʃ/ (US) /ˈhɔːblˌbʊʃ/ (UK)| n. |美洲荚蒾，仙女木|A North American shrub (Viburnum lantanoides syn. Viburnum alnifolium) with large, rounded leaves and clusters of white flowers, followed by red berries that turn black.  The stems are sometimes used for making baskets.|We found a hobblebush laden with ripe berries on our hike. 我们在徒步旅行中发现了一株挂满成熟浆果的美洲荚蒾。|Synonym: witch-hobble, moosewood|Antonym: N/A|Noun
