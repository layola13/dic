execration:/ˌɛksɪˈkreɪʃ(ə)n/
n.
诅咒，咒骂，憎恶
The act of cursing or denouncing someone or something; a curse; a feeling of intense loathing or abhorrence.
His execration of the corrupt officials was heard by everyone. 他对腐败官员的咒骂每个人都听到了。
The dictator's name was met with universal execration.  独裁者的名字遭到了普遍的咒骂。
近义词：curse, damnation, anathema, abomination, abhorrence, detestation
反义词：blessing, benediction, adoration, praise
词性：名词
