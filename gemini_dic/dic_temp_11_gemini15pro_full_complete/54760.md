dispatchers:/dɪˈspætʃərz/
n.
调度员，派遣员
People who send out vehicles, employees, or equipment to designated locations or to perform specific tasks.
The dispatchers coordinated the emergency response to the accident.  调度员协调了对事故的紧急救援。
The taxi dispatchers were overwhelmed with calls during the rush hour.  出租车调度员在高峰时段接到了大量的电话。
近义词： coordinators, controllers, routers
反义词： receivers
