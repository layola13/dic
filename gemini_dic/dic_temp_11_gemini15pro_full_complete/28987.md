cartels:/kɑːrˈtɛlz/
n.
卡特尔，同业联盟，垄断联盟
An association of manufacturers or suppliers with the purpose of maintaining prices at a high level and restricting competition.
The drug cartels control much of the cocaine trade.  这些贩毒集团控制了大部分可卡因交易。

The oil cartel OPEC decided to reduce production. 石油卡特尔欧佩克决定减产。


synonyms:syndicate, combine, monopoly, trust, consortium, coalition
antonyms:competition, free market

词性: noun
