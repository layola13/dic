fonding:/ˈfɒndɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːndɪŋ/ (US)| n.|喜爱；溺爱；爱抚|The act of caressing or showing affection, especially to a child.|She showered her baby with fonding. 她对她的宝宝充满了爱抚。|Near synonyms: caressing, cuddling, petting|Antonyms: neglect, abuse|noun

fonding:/ˈfɒndɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːndɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|（仅用于构成复合词）喜爱的|Used in compound words to indicate fondness or affection for something.|He's a car-fonding enthusiast. 他是一个喜爱汽车的狂热者。 (Note: This usage is less common and often considered informal.)|Near synonyms: loving, doting (in compounds)|Antonyms:  disliking, averse (in compounds)|adjective
