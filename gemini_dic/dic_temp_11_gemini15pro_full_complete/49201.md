delaying:/dɪˈleɪɪŋ/
verb
推迟，延迟，延期
postponing or deferring something
例句：Delaying the decision will only make things worse.  推迟决定只会使事情变得更糟。

例句：He's delaying his retirement until next year. 他把退休推迟到明年。
近义词：postponing, deferring, stalling, procrastinating
反义词：expediting, hastening, accelerating
词性: present participle of "delay"


delay:/dɪˈleɪ/
noun
延误，延迟，耽搁
a period of time by which something is late or postponed
例句：There was a long delay at the airport. 机场延误了很长时间。

例句：The project suffered several delays due to bad weather.  由于恶劣天气，该项目遭遇了几次延误。
近义词：postponement, deferment, hold-up
反义词：advancement, expedition
词性: noun


delay:/dɪˈleɪ/
verb
推迟，延迟，耽搁
to make something happen at a later time than originally planned or expected
例句：The flight was delayed due to fog.  由于大雾，航班延误了。

例句：Don't delay, act now! 不要拖延，现在就行动！
近义词：postpone, defer, stall, procrastinate
反义词：expedite, hasten, accelerate
词性: verb
