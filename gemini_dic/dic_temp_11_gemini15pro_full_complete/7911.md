antenniform:/ænˈtɛnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
触角状的，像触角的
Resembling an antenna in form or function; shaped like an antenna.
The antenniform appendages of the insect helped it to sense its surroundings.  这种昆虫的触角状附肢帮助它感知周围的环境。
The robot had two antenniform sensors protruding from its head.  这个机器人的头部伸出两个触角状的传感器。
近义词:antenna-like, antenna-shaped
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., spherical, flat)
词性:adjective
