overslide:/ˌoʊvərˈslaɪd/ | v. |  滑过；越过；忽略 | to slide or pass over; to skip or omit | The car overslid the icy patch on the road. 汽车滑过了路上的冰块。| synonyms: bypass, overlook, skip, ignore | antonyms: address, confront, acknowledge | verb

overslide:/ˌoʊvərˈslaɪd/ | n. | 滑坡;覆盖层 | an overlying geological stratum or the act of oversliding| The overslided rocks formed a new layer on the mountain. 滑坡的岩石在山上形成了新的覆盖层。| synonyms: landslide, slip, covering, overlay| antonyms: base, foundation, underpinning | noun
