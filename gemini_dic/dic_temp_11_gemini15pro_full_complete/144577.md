outwastes:/ˌaʊtˈweɪsts/ | v. | 超过…的浪费；在浪费方面胜过 | To surpass in wasting; to exceed in extravagance or profusion. | He outwastes his brother by spending double his income. 他花的钱是他兄弟的两倍，在浪费方面超过了他兄弟。| The company outwastes its competitors by throwing away usable materials. 该公司丢弃可用材料，比竞争对手更浪费。 |近义词：outspend, squander | 反义词：conserve, save |词性：动词
