charismatic:/ˌkærɪzˈmætɪk/| adj.|有魅力的；有感召力的|Exercising a compelling charm which inspires devotion in others.|例句：He was a charismatic leader who inspired his followers. 他是一位富有魅力的领导者，激励着他的追随者。

例句：She has a charismatic personality that draws people to her. 她具有 charismatic 的个性，吸引人们靠近她。|近义词：charming, magnetic, captivating, alluring|反义词：uninspiring, dull, boring|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "khárisma" meaning "gift of grace,"  derived from "kharizesthai" meaning "to show favour."  Later adopted into Late Latin as "charisma," referring to a divinely conferred power or talent. The modern English adjective "charismatic" emerged in the late 19th century.
