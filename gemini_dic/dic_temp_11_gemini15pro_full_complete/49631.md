demigrate:/ˈdɛmaɪɡreɪt/
verb
部分迁移，降级迁移
To migrate (data or a system) from a higher version or platform to a lower one.
We decided to demigrate our servers to a less expensive operating system. 我们决定将我们的服务器降级迁移到成本更低的操作系统。
The company demigrated its customer database from the cloud back to a local server.  公司将其客户数据库从云端迁移回本地服务器。
近义词: downgrade, revert
反义词: upgrade, migrate
词性: v.
词源: From de- (down) + migrate.
