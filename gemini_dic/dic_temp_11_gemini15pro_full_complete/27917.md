canters:/ˈkæntərz/ (US), /ˈkæntəz/ (UK)| n. v.| 慢跑，轻快地跑；(马)慢跑|A canter is a smooth three-beat gait of a horse, faster than a trot but slower than a gallop.  As a verb, it means to ride or cause a horse to canter.|The horse cantered across the field.  这匹马轻快地跑过田野。|近义词：trot, lope|反义词:gallop, sprint|词性:名词, 动词
