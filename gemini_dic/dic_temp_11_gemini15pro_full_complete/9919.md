aperient:/əˈpɪəriənt/| adj. n.|通便的；泻药|A substance that causes bowel movements.  Having a mild laxative effect.|例句：The doctor prescribed an aperient to relieve her constipation. 医生给她开了泻药来缓解便秘。

例句：Prunes are a natural aperient. 西梅是一种天然的泻药。|近义词：laxative, purgative, cathartic|反义词：constipating, astringent|词性：adjective, noun
Etymology: From Late Middle English *aperient*, from Old French *aperiant*, present participle of *aperir* "to open," from Latin *aperīre* "to open."
