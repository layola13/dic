jutty:/ˈdʒʌti/| n. v.|突出物，伸出；突出，伸出|A structure that juts out, such as a pier or a projecting part of a building.|The jutting cliffs offered a dramatic view of the ocean. 悬崖峭壁提供了壮观的海洋景色。|jut out, project, protrude|recess, indent|noun, verb
jutty:/ˈdʒʌti/| v.|突出，伸出|To extend outward or project beyond a surrounding line or surface.|The balcony jutted out over the street. 阳台伸出到街道上方。|project, protrude, stick out|recede, retract|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *jutte, probably from Old French *jute, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old French jeter "to throw," from Latin jactare, frequentative of jacere "to throw." The sense evolution might be from "something thrown out" to "something projecting."
