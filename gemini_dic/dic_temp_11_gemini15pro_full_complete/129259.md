nodded-off: /ˈnɒdɪd ɒf/ (UK) /ˈnɑːdɪd ɔːf/ (US)| verb phrase | 打瞌睡，不知不觉睡着 | To fall asleep briefly, usually unintentionally. | Example: I nodded off during the movie. 我在看电影的时候打瞌睡了。

Example:  He nodded off in his chair after a long day at work.  他在工作了一整天后，在椅子上打瞌睡了。

近义词: dozed off, drifted off, dropped off | 反义词: woke up, became alert | 词性: verb phrase
