melanochroi:/ˌmɛləˈnɒkrɔɪ/ | n. | 黑色素沉着的人；深色皮肤的人 |  A term used in physical anthropology to describe people with dark skin and dark hair, typically associated with populations from regions with high levels of solar radiation. | Example:  Melanochroi are often found in tropical regions. 黑色素沉着的人通常在热带地区被发现。 | Example:  Early anthropologists classified many African populations as melanochroi. 早期的人类学家将许多非洲人口归类为黑色素沉着的人。 | 近义词: dark-skinned people, brunets | 反义词: xanthochroi (浅色皮肤的人), leukochroi (白皮肤的人) | 词性: noun
