accent:/ˈæksent/ (US), /ˈæksənt/ (UK)| n. vt.|口音，腔调；重音；强调；重读|A distinctive way of pronouncing a language, especially one associated with a particular country, area, or social class.  To pronounce with a particular accent; to emphasize something.|She speaks with a strong southern accent. 她说话带有浓重的南方口音。|synonyms: pronunciation, intonation, inflection, emphasis, stress|antonyms: monotone|noun, verb
