despatched:/dɪˈspætʃt/ | v. |派遣，发送，迅速处理| to send someone or something somewhere for a particular purpose| The package was despatched by courier. 包裹由快递员派送。| synonyms: send, dispatch, forward, transmit | antonyms: receive, hold, retain | verb

despatched:/dɪˈspætʃt/ | v. |杀死，处决 (旧用法) | to kill someone | The traitor was swiftly despatched. 叛徒被迅速处决了。| synonyms: kill, execute, eliminate | antonyms: revive, resuscitate | verb

despatched:/dɪˈspætʃt/ | adj. |已发送的，已处理的 | sent or dealt with quickly and efficiently | The despatched documents arrived safely. 已发送的文件安全到达。 | synonyms: sent, delivered, completed | antonyms: pending, unfinished | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *despechier*, from *des-* (expressing removal) + *pechier* "to impede," from Latin *impedicare* "to entangle, shackle."  The spelling with "-tch" became common in the 17th century.
