cheapish:/ˈtʃiːpɪʃ/| adj.|稍微便宜的，有点便宜的|Relatively cheap; inexpensive.  It implies that something is cheap but perhaps not the cheapest possible option.  It also can suggest that something is cheap in a negative way, implying low quality. |That restaurant is cheapish, but the food isn't great. 那家餐馆有点便宜，但是食物不太好。|近义词：inexpensive, moderately priced, reasonable|反义词:expensive, pricey, costly|词性:形容词
词源:cheap + -ish (suffix indicating somewhat or slightly)
