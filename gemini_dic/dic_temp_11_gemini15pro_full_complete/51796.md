devout:/dɪˈvaʊt/ | adj. | 虔诚的，投入的 | Having or showing deep religious feeling or commitment.  |  He was a devout Catholic. 他是一个虔诚的天主教徒。 | religious, pious, dedicated, committed | irreligious, impious, uncommitted | adjective
