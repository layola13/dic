confines:/ˈkɒnfaɪnz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnfaɪnz/ (US)| n. |界限；范围；限制|The physical boundaries or limits of a place or area; the state of being restricted or imprisoned.|She felt stifled by the confines of small-town life. 她觉得小镇生活的局限令人窒息。|Synonyms: boundaries, limits, restrictions, constraints|Antonyms: expanse, freedom, liberty|noun


confine:/kənˈfaɪn/ (UK) /kənˈfaɪn/ (US)| vt. |限制；使局限；禁闭|To restrict something or someone within certain limits; to keep someone or something in a particular place, especially by force.|The illness confined him to bed. 疾病把他限制在床上。|Synonyms: restrict, limit, restrain, imprison|Antonyms: liberate, free, release|verb
