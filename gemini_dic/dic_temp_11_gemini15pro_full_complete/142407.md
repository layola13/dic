Orville: /ˈɔːrvɪl/ (US), /ˈɔːvɪl/ (UK)| proper n.|奥维尔|A male given name, likely of French origin, meaning "gold town". It's also associated with Orville Wright, one of the Wright brothers, known for inventing and building the world's first successful airplane.|My uncle's name is Orville. 我叔叔的名字叫奥维尔。|Orville Wright was a pioneer in aviation. 奥维尔·莱特是航空领域的先驱。|近义词: (As a name, no direct synonyms, but similar-sounding names exist: Orvel, Orval)|反义词: N/A|词性: proper noun
