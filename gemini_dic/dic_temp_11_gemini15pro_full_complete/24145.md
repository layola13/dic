bribeabilities:/ˌbraɪbəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可贿赂性；受贿可能性|The ease with which someone or something can be bribed.  It refers to the susceptibility to corruption through bribery.|The bribeabilities of government officials are a serious concern. 政府官员的可贿赂性是一个严重的问题。|Synonym: corruptibility, venality|Antonym: incorruptibility, integrity|Noun
