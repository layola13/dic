juneteenth:/ˈdʒuːnˌtiːnθ/ (US) /dʒuːnˈtiːnθ/ (UK)| n.|六月节 (解放日)|A holiday celebrated on June 19 in the United States to commemorate the emancipation of enslaved African Americans.|Juneteenth is a time for reflection and celebration. 六月节是反思和庆祝的时刻。|Juneteenth is now a federal holiday. 现在，六月节是联邦假日。|Synonyms: Emancipation Day, Freedom Day, Jubilee Day|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but could be contrasted with days of oppression or slavery)|noun
