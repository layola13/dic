fell:/fɛl/
verb
砍伐，打倒，击倒
to cut down a tree
例句：They felled several trees to make way for the new road.  他们砍伐了几棵树，为新路腾出空间。

例句：The boxer felled his opponent with a single punch.  拳击手一拳就把对手击倒了。
近义词：cut down, knock down, chop down
反义词：plant, raise
词性: verb

fell:/fɛl/
adjective
凶猛的，残酷的
fierce, cruel, or sinister
例句：A fell beast emerged from the shadows.  一头凶猛的野兽从阴影中出现。

例句：She had a fell look in her eyes.  她的眼神凶狠。
近义词：fierce, cruel, savage
反义词：gentle, kind
词性: adjective

fell:/fɛl/
noun
兽皮, (尤指)羊皮；高沼地；山
animal skin, especially sheepskin; a hill or high moor
例句：The shepherd sold the fell of the sheep.  牧羊人卖掉了羊皮。

例句：The hikers crossed the fell in the pouring rain.  徒步旅行者在大雨中穿过了高沼地。
近义词：hide, skin, moor, hill
反义词：(For moor/hill) valley, lowland
词性: noun
