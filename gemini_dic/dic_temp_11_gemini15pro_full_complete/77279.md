gastrula:/ˈɡæstrʊlə/| n. |原肠胚|An early multicellular embryo, composed of two or more germinal layers of cells from which the various organs later derive.  The stage succeeding the blastula. |The gastrula stage is crucial in embryonic development. 原肠胚阶段在胚胎发育中至关重要。

The formation of the gastrula involves complex cell movements. 原肠胚的形成涉及复杂的细胞运动。|近义词: embryo 胚胎|反义词: None (gastrula represents a specific developmental stage)|词性: noun
