antitheism:/ˈæntiˌθiːɪzəm/ /ˈæntɪˌθiɪzəm/
n.
反对神论；反神论；无神论
Active opposition to theism; the belief that there is no god.
Antitheism goes beyond simple atheism in that it involves active opposition to theistic beliefs. 反神论不仅仅是简单的无神论，它还包括积极反对有神论信仰。
Antitheism is sometimes confused with atheism. 反神论有时会被与无神论混淆。
近义词：militant atheism, strong atheism
反义词:theism, deism
词性:noun
