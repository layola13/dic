bottle:/ˈbɒtl/ (British), /ˈbɑːtl/ (American)| n. v.|瓶子；一瓶（的量）；装瓶|A container, typically made of glass or plastic, with a narrow neck, used for storing liquids.  To put (a liquid) into a bottle.|例句：Please pass me the bottle of water. 请递给我那瓶水。|近义词：container, flask, jar, jug|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用“tap” 水龙头，表示非瓶装)|noun, verb
