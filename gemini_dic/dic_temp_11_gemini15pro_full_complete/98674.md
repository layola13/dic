insulation:/ˌɪnsəˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 隔热；绝缘；隔离；隔音材料 | Material used to prevent heat, sound, or electricity from passing through. | The house has good insulation, so it stays warm in winter. 这房子隔热性能好，所以冬天很暖和。| synonyms: padding, lagging, isolation | antonyms: conduction, connection | noun

insulations:/ˌɪnsəˈleɪʃnz/ | n. | 隔热材料（insulation 的复数形式）| Multiple types or layers of materials used to prevent heat, sound, or electricity from passing through. |  The builder used several different insulations in the walls and roof.  建筑工人使用了多种不同的隔热材料用于墙壁和屋顶。| synonyms: paddings, laggings | antonyms: conductors, connections | noun
