liberalises:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使自由化，放宽限制
To make something less strict or controlled, often relating to laws, regulations, or social attitudes.
The government has liberalized trade regulations. 政府已经放宽了贸易管制。
liberalize, loosen, relax, deregulate
restrict, regulate, control, tighten
v.
