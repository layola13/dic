ganglionate:/ˈɡæŋɡliəˌneɪt/| adj.|神经节的； having ganglia|The octopus has a well-developed, ganglionate nervous system. 章鱼拥有一个高度发达的神经节神经系统。|近义词:gangliated, ganglionic|反义词: aganglionic|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
