hamulose:/ˈhæmjʊloʊs/| adj.|钩状的，有小钩的|Having small hooks or hook-like projections.|The hamulose scales of the butterfly wing create iridescence. 蝴蝶翅膀上的钩状鳞片产生虹彩。|近义词：hooked, barbed, uncinate|反义词:smooth, straight|词性:形容词

词源:  From Latin *hamulus (diminutive of hamus "hook") + -ose (forming adjectives meaning "full of, abounding in").
