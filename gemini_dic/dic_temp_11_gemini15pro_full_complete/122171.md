mistunes:/ˌmɪsˈtjuːnz/ | v. | 使失调，调错 | to tune or adjust improperly; to put out of tune. | The piano tuner mistuned several of the keys. 钢琴调音师把几个键调错了。| synonyms: untunes, detunes | antonyms: tunes, harmonizes | verb
mistunes:/ˌmɪsˈtjuːnz/ | n. | 失调，不和谐的声音 | The state of being out of tune; a discordant sound. | The mistunes in the orchestra were distracting. 管弦乐队的失调令人分心。| synonyms: disharmony, discordance | antonyms: harmony, concord | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + tune (to adjust to a musical pitch).
