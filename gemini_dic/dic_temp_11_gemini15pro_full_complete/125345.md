multilinear:/ˌmʌltiˈlɪniər/| adj.|多线性的|Relating to a function that is linear in each of its arguments separately.  A function f(x₁, x₂, ..., xₙ) is multilinear if it is linear in each variable xᵢ when all other variables are held constant.|The determinant of a matrix is a multilinear function of its rows (or columns). 行列式的值是其行（或列）的多线性函数。

The dot product is a multilinear function.  点积是一个多线性函数。|近义词:N/A (Highly specific mathematical term)|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
