neediest:/ˈniːdiɪst/| adj.|最贫困的，最需要的|Those who are the neediest in our society deserve our help. 我们社会中最贫困的人应该得到我们的帮助。|例句：The charity focuses on helping the neediest families in the city.  这个慈善机构致力于帮助城市中最贫困的家庭。

例句：The neediest students often qualify for financial aid. 最贫困的学生通常有资格获得经济援助。

近义词：poorest, most deprived, most disadvantaged|反义词：richest, wealthiest, most affluent|词性:形容词

词源: From "needy" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Needy" comes from Old English "nēadig," meaning "compulsory, necessary," later evolving to mean "in need, poor."
