domino:/ˈdɒmɪnəʊ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːmɪnoʊ/ (US)| n. |多米诺骨牌；多米诺骨牌效应|A small rectangular block used in the game of dominoes, typically made of ivory, bone, wood, or plastic and marked with spots representing numbers. Also refers to a chain reaction caused by one event setting off a series of similar events.|  I like playing dominoes with my grandfather. 我喜欢和我祖父玩多米诺骨牌。|  The collapse of one bank triggered a domino effect throughout the financial system. 一家银行的倒闭引发了整个金融体系的多米诺骨牌效应。|Synonyms: tile, piece, block|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly "stability," "stasis" in the context of the domino effect)|noun
