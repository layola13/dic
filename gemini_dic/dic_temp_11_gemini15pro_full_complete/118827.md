metantimonic:/ˌmɛtəænˈtɪmənɪk/
adjective
偏锑酸的
Relating to or containing antimony in its +5 oxidation state.  Specifically, refers to compounds containing the Sb(V) anion, often as the antimonate(V) ion, SbO₄³⁻.
Potassium metantimonate is used in some analytical tests.  偏锑酸钾用于某些分析测试。
Metantimonic acid is a strong oxidizing agent. 偏锑酸是一种强氧化剂。
Synonyms: antimonate(V),  pentavalent antimony
Antonyms: antimonous,  trivalent antimony
词性: adjective
