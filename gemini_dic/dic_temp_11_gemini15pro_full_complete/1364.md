accumulator:/əˈkjuːmjəleɪtər/ | n. | 蓄电池；累加器；积聚者 |A device or person that accumulates something.  A rechargeable battery that stores energy. In programming, a variable that accumulates a sum or total. | The car's accumulator was dead, so we couldn't start it. 汽车的蓄电池没电了，所以我们无法启动它。

The data is fed into an accumulator that keeps a running total. 数据被输入到一个累加器中，该累加器会保持一个运行总数。 | synonyms: battery, collector, gatherer, reservoir | antonyms: disperser, distributor | noun
