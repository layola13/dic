embellishing:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃɪŋ/
verb
修饰；润色；装饰
making something more attractive by adding decorative details or features.
She embellished the story with a few imaginative details. 她用一些想象的细节润色了故事。
enhancing, adorning, decorating
simplifying, minimizing
verb

embellishment:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃmənt/
noun
装饰；修饰；润色
a decorative detail or feature added to something to make it more attractive.
The embellishments on the wedding cake were exquisite. 婚礼蛋糕上的装饰精美绝伦。
decoration, ornamentation, adornment
simplification, minimalism
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *embellir, from en- (“in, on”) + bel- (“beautiful”) + -ir (infinitive suffix). Ultimately from Latin bellus (“beautiful, handsome, charming”).
