definable:/dɪˈfaɪnəbl/
adj.|可定义的；可下定义的；可确定的|Able to be clearly described, explained, or determined.|The boundaries of the property are easily definable.  这处房产的边界很容易确定。|The concept of beauty is not easily definable. 美的概念不容易定义。|近义词:determinable, specifiable, describable|反义词:indefinable, vague, ambiguous|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from define + -able.
