harpsichord:/ˈhɑːrpsɪkɔːrd/ (US) /ˈhɑːpsɪkɔːd/ (UK)| n.|大键琴|A keyboard instrument that looks like a small grand piano. When a key is pressed, a plectrum plucks the strings, producing a distinctive sound.|Bach composed many pieces for the harpsichord. 巴赫为大键琴创作了许多作品。|近义词：clavichord, virginal|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern piano)|noun
