headscarf: /ˈhɛdskɑːrf/ (US), /ˈhɛdskɑːf/ (UK)| n. |头巾，头巾|A piece of cloth worn over the head, often for religious or cultural reasons.|She wore a colorful headscarf to protect her hair from the sun. 她戴着一条色彩鲜艳的头巾，以保护她的头发免受阳光照射。|近义词：hijab, scarf, head covering|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用uncovered hair 裸露的头发)|词性: noun
