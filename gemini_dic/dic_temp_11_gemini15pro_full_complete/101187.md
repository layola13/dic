invincibility:/ɪnˌvɪnsəˈbɪləti/| n.|无敌; 不可战胜|The state of being impossible to defeat or overcome.|His army had a reputation for invincibility. 他的军队以不可战胜而闻名。|The team had an air of invincibility about them. 这支队伍展现出一种无敌的气势。|近义词：impregnable, invulnerable, unconquerable|反义词：vulnerability, weakness|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French invincibilité, from Latin invincibilitas, from invincibilis (see invincible).
