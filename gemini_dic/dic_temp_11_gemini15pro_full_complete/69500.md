felineness:/ˈfiːlaɪnˌnɛs/
n.
猫性，猫科动物的特性
The quality of being feline; resembling or suggestive of a cat.
Her movements had a certain felineness, a graceful, predatory quality. 她的动作有一种猫的特性，优雅而略带掠夺性。
Synonyms: catlikeness, feline quality, stealth, grace, agility
Antonyms: clumsiness, awkwardness, gaucheness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From feline + -ness. Feline from French félin, from Latin felīnus, from fēlis "cat."
