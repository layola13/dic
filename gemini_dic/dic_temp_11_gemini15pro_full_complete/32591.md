chiromantical:/ˌkaɪrəˈmæntɪkl/
adjective
中文翻译：手相术的；手相学的
英文解析：Relating to or based on chiromancy (palmistry).
例句：She gave a chiromantical reading at the carnival. 她在嘉年华上进行了一次手相解读。

例句：He dismissed chiromantical predictions as mere superstition. 他认为手相预测只是迷信，不予理会。

近义词：palmistic
反义词：N/A
词性：adjective


chiromancy:/ˈkaɪrəˌmænsi/
noun
中文翻译：手相术；手相学
英文解析：The art or practice of supposedly telling fortunes by examining the lines and other features of the hand.
例句：Chiromancy has been practiced for centuries in many cultures. 手相术在许多文化中已经流传了几个世纪。

例句：She learned chiromancy from her grandmother. 她跟祖母学习手相术。

近义词：palmistry
反义词：N/A
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *chiromantia, from Greek kheir ("hand") + manteia ("divination").
