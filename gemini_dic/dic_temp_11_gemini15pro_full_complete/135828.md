nonprophetic:/ˌnɑːnprəˈfɛtɪk/ | adj. | 非预言性的，非先知的 | Not prophetic; not foretelling the future.  |  The economist's analysis was nonprophetic, failing to predict the recession.  这位经济学家的分析并非预言性的，未能预测到经济衰退。 | Synonyms: nonpredictive, unprophetic | Antonyms: prophetic, predictive | adjective

nonprophetic:/ˌnɑːnprəˈfɛtɪk/ | adj. | 不具预言性质的 | Lacking the quality of prophecy; not inspired by divine influence. | His nonprophetic words were simply based on observation. 他不具预言性质的话仅仅基于观察。| Synonyms: mundane, ordinary | Antonyms: oracular, inspired | adjective
