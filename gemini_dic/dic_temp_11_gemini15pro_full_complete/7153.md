angelling:/ˈeɪndʒəlɪŋ/
n.
垂钓鳟鱼;假扮成钓鳟鱼的人
The act of fishing for trout.  Often implies a deceptive or artful approach.
He was angelling in the river when he saw a deer. 他在河边钓鳟鱼时看到了一只鹿。
fishing, angling
hunting

v.
钓鱼（尤指钓鳟鱼）
To fish, especially for trout, typically with a rod and line.
He was angelling for compliments. 他在拐弯抹角地想得到赞美。
fish, angle
hunt

词性:  n. & v.
