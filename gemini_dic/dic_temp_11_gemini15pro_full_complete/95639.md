incompatible:/ˌɪnkəmˈpætəbl/ | adj. | 不兼容的，不相容的，合不来的 | Not able to exist or work together successfully. |  The software is incompatible with my operating system.  这个软件与我的操作系统不兼容。| synonyms: conflicting, clashing, discordant, antagonistic, irreconcilable | antonyms: compatible, consistent, harmonious | adjective

incompatible:/ˌɪnkəmˈpætəbl/ | adj. |  无法共存的，格格不入的 |  Two people with incompatible personalities.  两个性格不合的人。 | synonyms:  mismatched, unsuited, ill-suited | antonyms:  suited, compatible, well-matched | adjective

词源:
From Late Latin incompatibilis, from in- ‘not’ + compatibilis ‘sympathetic’, from compati ‘suffer with’.
