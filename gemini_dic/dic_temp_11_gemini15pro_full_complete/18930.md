bespeaks:/bɪˈspiːks/| vt.|表明；显示；预示|suggests; is evidence of; indicates|Her elegant attire bespeaks good taste. 她的优雅着装显示出她的好品味。|近义词：indicates, suggests, implies, signifies, attests to, betokens|反义词：belies, contradicts, masks|词性：动词

bespeaks:/bɪˈspiːks/| vt.|预订；约定|orders or reserves something in advance|He bespoke a new suit for the wedding. 他为婚礼预订了一套新西装。|近义词：orders, reserves, books|反义词：cancels|词性：动词
