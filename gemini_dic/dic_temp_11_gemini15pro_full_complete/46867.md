dareful:/ˈdɛərfʊl/
adjective
大胆的，勇敢的
Showing a willingness to take risks; adventurous and bold.
She made a dareful leap across the chasm. 她大胆地跳过了峡谷。
近义词:bold, audacious, courageous, daring, adventurous, intrepid
反义词:timid, cautious, cowardly, fearful, apprehensive
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From dare + -ful.  Essentially meaning "full of dare."
