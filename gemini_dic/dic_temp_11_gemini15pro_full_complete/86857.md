hemidactyl:/ˌhɛmɪˈdæktɪl/| n. |壁虎科动物；半趾动物|A gecko; any lizard of the family Gekkonidae, characterized by adhesive toe pads.|The hemidactyl clung to the ceiling with ease. 壁虎轻松地 clung在了天花板上。|Synonyms: gecko, lizard |Antonyms: N/A|noun

hemidactylous:/ˌhɛmɪˈdæktɪləs/| adj. |半趾的|Having the toes partly webbed.|Some species of frogs are hemidactylous. 一些种类的青蛙是半趾的。|Synonyms: partially webbed |Antonyms: fully webbed|adjective
