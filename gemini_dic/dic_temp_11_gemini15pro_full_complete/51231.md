destroyers:/dɪˈstrɔɪərz/
n.|驱逐舰；破坏者|A warship designed for high speed and maneuverability, typically equipped with guided missiles, torpedoes, and guns. Also, a person or thing that destroys something.|The destroyers patrolled the coast. 驱逐舰在海岸巡逻。|近义词:warships, demolishers, wreckers|反义词:creators, builders|词性:名词


词源:
From the verb "destroy" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Destroy" comes from Old French "destruire," from Latin "destruere,"  meaning "to unbuild, pull down, demolish."
