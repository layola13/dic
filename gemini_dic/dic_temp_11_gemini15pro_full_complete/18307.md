bellboys: /ˈbelbɔɪz/ (US), /ˈbelbɔɪz/ (UK)| n. |行李员，门童|A hotel employee who performs services for guests, such as carrying luggage, delivering messages, and running errands.|The bellboys quickly whisked our luggage up to our room. 行李员迅速地把我们的行李送到房间。|The bellboy hailed a cab for us. 门童为我们叫了一辆出租车。|Synonyms: porter, page|Antonyms: N/A|noun
