all-hot-and-bothered:/ɔːl hɒt ænd ˈbɒðəd/| adj.| 激动不安的；烦躁的；恼火的；焦虑的 | Feeling agitated, anxious, or upset.  This idiom often implies a state of flustered excitement, especially due to romantic or sexual interest. |  She was all hot and bothered after her run in the heat.  她在大热天跑步后感到激动不安。|  flustered, agitated, anxious, upset, worked up | calm, relaxed, composed | adjective
