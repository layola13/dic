investigated: /ɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪtɪd/
verb
调查过，研究过
past tense and past participle of investigate: having carefully examined a situation or problem in order to discover the truth
The police investigated the cause of the accident. 警察调查了事故的原因。
synonyms: examined, explored, probed, scrutinized, inspected
antonyms: ignored, neglected, overlooked
