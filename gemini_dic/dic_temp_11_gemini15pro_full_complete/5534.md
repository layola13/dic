alphabets:/ˈælfəbɛts/ | n. | 字母表 | A set of letters or symbols in a fixed order, used to represent the basic sounds of a language.  From Late Middle English alphabet, from Old French alphabet, or via Latin from Greek *alphabētos, from alpha + bēta, the first two letters of the Greek alphabet. | The English alphabet has 26 letters. 英文有26个字母。 | synonyms: letters, characters | antonyms: N/A | noun
