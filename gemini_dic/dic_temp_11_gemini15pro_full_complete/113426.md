lymphogenic:/lɪmfəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
淋巴生成的；由淋巴产生的
Produced in or carried by lymph; relating to the production of lymph.
The lymphogenic spread of cancer cells is a serious concern. 癌细胞的淋巴扩散是一个严重的问题。
Lymphogenic metastasis is a common route for cancer spread. 淋巴转移是癌症扩散的常见途径。
近义词:lymphatic
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "hematogenous" (blood-borne))
词性:adjective
