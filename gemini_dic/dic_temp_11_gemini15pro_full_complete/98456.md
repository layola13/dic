inspire:/ɪnˈspaɪər/(US) /ɪnˈspaɪə(r)/(UK)| vt.|激勵；鼓舞；啟發|to fill (someone) with the urge or ability to do or feel something, especially to do something creative|例句：The teacher inspired her students to work hard.  老师激励她的学生努力学习。

例句：His bravery inspired us all. 他的勇气鼓舞了我们所有人。|近义词：motivate, encourage, stimulate|反义词：discourage, dispirit|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: enspire, from Old French: inspirer, from Latin: inspirare "breathe or blow into," figuratively "infuse, inspire," from in- "in" + spirare "to breathe."
