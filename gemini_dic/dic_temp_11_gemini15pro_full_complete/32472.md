chinatowns:/ˈtʃaɪnəˌtaʊnz/
n.
唐人街（复数）
A district of any non-Chinese city in which the population is predominantly of Chinese origin.
Many Chinatowns celebrate Chinese New Year with parades and lion dances. 许多唐人街都会举行游行和舞狮来庆祝中国新年。
Chinatowns offer a taste of Chinese culture and cuisine.  唐人街提供中国文化和美食的体验。
synonyms: Chinese quarters, Chinese districts
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider areas with different ethnic majorities, e.g., Little Italy)
noun
