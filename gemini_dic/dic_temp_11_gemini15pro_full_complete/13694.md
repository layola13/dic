attribution:/ˌætrɪˈbjuːʃən/ | n. | 归因，归属，属性 | the act of saying or believing that something is the result or work of a particular person or thing | The painting's attribution to Rembrandt is now questioned by several experts. 这幅画被认为是伦勃朗的作品，但现在一些专家对此提出了质疑。 |  ascription, credit | denial, repudiation | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *attributio, from Latin attributus, past participle of attribuere (“to assign, bestow”), from ad (“to”) + tribuere (“to allot, bestow”).
