administratress: /ədˈmɪnɪˌstreɪtrɪs/
n.
女管理员，女遗产管理人
a woman who administers an estate or business.
例句：The administratress of the estate handled all the legal matters.  遗产的女管理人处理了所有法律事务。

例句：She was appointed administratress of the company after her father's death. 她父亲去世后，她被任命为公司的女管理人。

近义词：administrator, manager, executive, director
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "subordinate" or "employee")
词性: noun
