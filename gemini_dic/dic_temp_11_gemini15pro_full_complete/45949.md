cves:/siː viː iːz/ | n. | Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures (常见漏洞和暴露) |  A system that provides a reference-method for publicly known information-security vulnerabilities and exposures.  It's maintained by MITRE. |  The latest cves show a critical vulnerability in a popular software library. 最新的cves显示一个流行的软件库中存在严重漏洞。 | synonyms: vulnerabilities, weaknesses, flaws, exposures | antonyms: strengths, securities, protections | abbreviation
