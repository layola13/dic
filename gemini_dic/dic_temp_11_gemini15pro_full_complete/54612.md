dismaying:/dɪsˈmeɪɪŋ/| adj.|令人沮丧的，令人失望的|Causing dismay; disheartening.  Feeling discouraged or let down.|The news of the factory closing was dismaying to the workers.|近义词： disheartening, discouraging, upsetting, distressing|反义词： encouraging, heartening, cheering|词性: adjective
dismay:/dɪsˈmeɪ/| n. v. |沮丧，惊慌；使沮丧，使惊慌|Consternation and distress, typically that caused by something unexpected.|The sudden drop in sales filled the CEO with dismay.  The coach was dismayed by the team's poor performance. |近义词： consternation, alarm, anxiety|反义词：encouragement, comfort, relief|词性: noun, verb
