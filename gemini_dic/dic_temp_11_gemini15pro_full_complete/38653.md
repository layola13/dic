concurring:/kənˈkɜːrɪŋ/| adj.| 同意的，一致的，同时发生的|Happening at the same time; agreeing with something; expressing agreement.|例句：The judge issued a concurring opinion. 法官发表了同意意见。|近义词：agreeing, coincident, concurrent, simultaneous|反义词：dissenting, disagreeing, opposing|adjective

concurring:/kənˈkɜːrɪŋ/| n.| 同意，一致|Agreement or the expression of agreement.|例句：Her concurring vote secured the victory. 她的赞成票确保了胜利。|近义词：agreement, concurrence, accord|反义词：disagreement, dissent, opposition|noun
