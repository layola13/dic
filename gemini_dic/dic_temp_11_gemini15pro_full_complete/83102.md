gust:/ɡʌst/| n.|一阵强风；突然一阵（雨、雪、烟等）；（感情的）爆发，迸发|A sudden, strong rush of wind, rain, snow, smoke, etc., or a sudden outburst of feeling. |例句：A gust of wind slammed the door shut. 一阵强风把门砰地关上了。

例句：She felt a sudden gust of anger. 她感到一阵怒火涌上心头。

近义词：blast, squall, rush, surge, outburst|反义词:calm, lull|词性:名词

gust:/ɡʌst/| vi.|刮一阵强风|To blow in gusts. |例句：The wind gusted fiercely all night. 整夜狂风大作。

例句：The wind was gusting to 50 miles per hour.  风速阵风达到每小时50英里。

近义词：blow, blast, squall|反义词:calm|词性:动词
