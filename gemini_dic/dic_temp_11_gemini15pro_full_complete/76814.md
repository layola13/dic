gamosepalous:/ˌɡæmoʊˈsɛpələs/| adj.|合萼的，萼片联合的|Having the sepals united, at least at the base, forming a tube or cup.|The hibiscus flower is gamosepalous, with its sepals fused together.  芙蓉花是合萼的，萼片融合在一起。|Example 2: Gamosepalous calyces are a common characteristic in the Lamiaceae family.  合萼的花萼是唇形科植物的共同特征。|synonym: sympetalous, connate|antonym: polysepalous, chorisepalous|adjective
