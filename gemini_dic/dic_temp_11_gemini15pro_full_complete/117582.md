megalomaniac: /ˌmɛɡələˈmeɪniæk/ | n. adj. | 狂妄自大的人；夸大妄想症患者 | A person who has an obsession with their own power and importance, often to an unrealistic or delusional degree. |  He was a megalomaniac who believed he could control the world. 他是一个夸大妄想狂，认为自己可以控制世界。|Synonyms: egomaniac, narcissist, braggart | Antonyms: humble person, modest person | noun

megalomaniac: /ˌmɛɡələˈmeɪniæk/ | adj. | 夸大妄想的；狂妄自大的 | Exhibiting or characterized by delusions of grandeur or an obsession with power. | His megalomaniac plans involved building a giant statue of himself. 他那狂妄自大的计划包括为自己建造一座巨大的雕像。| Synonyms: narcissistic, egotistical, self-important | Antonyms: humble, modest | adjective
