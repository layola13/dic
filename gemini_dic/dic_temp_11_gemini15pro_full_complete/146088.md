overeducations:/ˌoʊvərˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənz/
noun
过度教育
The state of having more formal education than is required for a particular job or than is beneficial to an individual's well-being.  The perceived mismatch between education levels and job opportunities.
Many graduates are facing overeducation in today's job market.  许多毕业生在当今的就业市场上面临着过度教育的困境。
Overeducation can lead to job dissatisfaction. 过度教育会导致工作不满。
