constrainer:/kənˈstreɪnər/| n. |限制者，约束者|Something that constrains or restricts.  Something that limits or controls what can happen or what someone can do. |The primary constrainer on the project was the limited budget.  这个项目的主要限制因素是有限的预算。|Synonyms: restriction, limitation, constraint, curb, check, restraint|Antonyms:  enabler, facilitator, catalyst|noun

constrains:/kənˈstreɪnz/| v. |限制，约束|Severely restrict the scope, extent, or activity of.|Financial constraints have forced the company to cut back on research. 资金限制迫使公司削减研究经费。|Synonyms: restrict, limit, curb, check, restrain|Antonyms: enable, facilitate, encourage|verb
