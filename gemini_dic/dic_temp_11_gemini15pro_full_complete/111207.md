lithophagous:/lɪˈθɒfəɡəs/| adj.|食石的，吞石的|Feeding on rock or stones.  Specifically, (of an animal) swallowing stones or grit, either to aid digestion or to acquire minerals. |例句：Certain birds are lithophagous, ingesting small stones to help grind food in their gizzards.  某些鸟类是食石的，它们会吞下小石头来帮助磨碎嗉囊里的食物。

例句：The lithophagous habits of some dinosaurs are evidenced by the gastroliths found fossilized alongside their skeletons. 一些恐龙的食石习性可以通过与其骨骼一起发现的胃石化石来证明。|近义词：rock-eating, stone-eating|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *líthos* (stone) + *-phagos* (eating), from *phageîn* (to eat).
