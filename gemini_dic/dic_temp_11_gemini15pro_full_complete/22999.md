bouge:/buʒ/
noun|动词|move, budge, stir|To move or cause to move slightly.  It is primarily used in Canadian French.|The child bouged a little in his sleep.|近义词：shift, wiggle, fidget|反义词:still, freeze|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bouger, from Vulgar Latin *bullire "to bubble, boil," from Latin bullire "to boil, bubble up."  The semantic shift likely occurred through the intermediate sense of "to move agitatedly like boiling water."
