interprets:/ɪnˈtɜːrprɪts/
verb
解释，诠释，理解
explains or understands the meaning of something
The lawyer interprets the law for his client. 律师为他的客户解释法律。
Synonyms: explains, understands, construes, elucidates, deciphers
Antonyms: misunderstands, misinterprets, distorts, confuses
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *interpreter (Modern French interpréter), from Latin interpretārī (“to explain, expound, interpret, translate”), from interpres (“broker, negotiator, translator, interpreter, expounder”).
