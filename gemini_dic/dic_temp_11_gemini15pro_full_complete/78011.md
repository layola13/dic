gentleship:/ˈdʒɛntəlʃɪp/
n.
gentle birth; gentility; courtesy; kindness; the quality of being gentle
the condition or rank of a gentlewoman or gentleman.  A courteous or polite form of address (often archaic or humorous).
The gentleship of his manners impressed everyone. 他的举止文雅给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
He addressed her as "your gentleship" with a playful bow. 他开玩笑地鞠了一躬，称呼她为“夫人”。
近义词: gentility, courtesy, politeness, kindness, nobility
反义词: rudeness, coarseness, vulgarity, unkindness
词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *gentleschipe*, equivalent to *gentle* + *-ship*. Cognate with Scots *gentrice* (“gentility, courtesy”).
