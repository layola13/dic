domineeringness:/ˌdɒmɪˈnɪərɪŋnəs/ | n. | 专横跋扈，盛气凌人 | The quality of being domineering; an overbearing manner.  | Her domineeringness made it difficult for her colleagues to contribute to projects.  她的专横跋扈使得她的同事很难对项目做出贡献。 |  例句：His domineeringness alienated his friends. 他的盛气凌人疏远了他的朋友。| 近义词：arrogance, imperiousness, bossiness | 反义词：humility, modesty, submissiveness | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From domineering + -ness.  Domineering comes from the verb 'dominate' which ultimately derives from the Latin word "dominus" meaning "lord" or "master."
