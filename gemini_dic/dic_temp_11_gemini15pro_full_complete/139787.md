词源：From Spanish *octava ron* ‘one-eighth part’, from Latin *octavus* ‘eighth’.  Originally referred to a person of one-eighth black ancestry.  Later applied to the confection, perhaps because the brown colour of the coconut resembled the skin tone of such a person.
