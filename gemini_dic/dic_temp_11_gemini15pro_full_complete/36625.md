collectivised:/kəˈlɛktɪvaɪzd/| adj.|集体化的，集体经营的|Brought under collective ownership or control, especially by the state.|Example: The farms were collectivised after the revolution. 革命后，农场被集体化了。|Example:  Collectivised agriculture was a key feature of the communist regime. 集体化农业是共产主义政权的一个主要特征。|Synonym: communal, shared, centralized|Antonym: private, individual, decentralized|adjective

collectivise:/kəˈlɛktɪvaɪz/| v.|集体化|To bring under collective ownership or control, especially by the state.|Example: The government collectivised the land. 政府将土地集体化。|Example: The Soviet Union collectivised its agriculture in the 1930s.  苏联在20世纪30年代将其农业集体化。|Synonym: nationalize, communalize, socialize|Antonym: privatize, individualize|verb
