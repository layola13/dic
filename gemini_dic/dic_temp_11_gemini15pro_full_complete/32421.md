chilostomata: /kaɪləˈstɒmətə/ /kaɪləˈstoʊmətə/
| n. | 唇口纲 | An order of Bryozoa characterized by a calcareous, box-like zooid with a hinged operculum covering the orifice. | The chilostomata are the most diverse order of bryozoans. 唇口纲是苔藓动物中最具多样性的一个目。|  Bryozoans of the order Cheilostomata are common in fouling communities.  唇口目苔藓动物在污损群落中很常见。 | 近义词: None (It's a specific taxonomic group)| 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek χεῖλος (kheîlos, “lip”) + στόμα (stóma, “mouth”).
