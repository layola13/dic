color-blind:/ˈkʌlər blaɪnd/ | adj. | 色盲的 | Unable to distinguish certain colors, wholly or partly. |  Example 1: He is color-blind and can't tell red from green. 他是色盲，分不清红色和绿色。

Example 2:  The test revealed that she was color-blind in one eye. 测试显示她一只眼睛色盲。 | Near synonyms: color deficient, color weak | Antonym:  No direct antonym, but one could say "having normal color vision" | adjective


color-blindness:/ˈkʌlər ˌblaɪndnəs/ | n. | 色盲 | The inability to distinguish certain colors, wholly or partly. | Example 1: Color-blindness affects approximately 8% of men and 0.5% of women. 色盲影响大约8%的男性和0.5%的女性。

Example 2:  He inherited color-blindness from his father. 他的色盲是遗传自他父亲的。 | Near synonyms: color deficiency, color vision deficiency | Antonym: normal color vision | noun
