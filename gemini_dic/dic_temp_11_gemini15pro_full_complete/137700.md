nonusurious:/ˌnɒnjuːˈʒʊəriəs/
adjective
非高利贷的
Not charging excessive or exorbitant interest; not usurious.
The loan agreement was carefully drafted to ensure it was nonusurious and complied with all applicable regulations. 这份贷款协议经过精心起草，以确保它是非高利贷的，并符合所有适用法规。
The bank offers nonusurious loans to small businesses. 这家银行向小企业提供非高利贷的贷款。
Synonyms: fair, reasonable, legitimate, legal
Antonyms: usurious, exorbitant, excessive
词性: adjective
