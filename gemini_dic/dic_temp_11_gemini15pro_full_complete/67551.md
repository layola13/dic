extraneously:/ɛkˈstreɪniəsli/
adverb
外在地；无关地；非必要地
Coming from outside; not forming an essential or vital part; irrelevant.
The lawyer argued that the witness's testimony was extraneous to the case and should be disregarded. 律师辩称证人的证词与案件无关，应不予考虑。
extraneous information  无关信息
synonym: externally, outwardly, irrelevantly, unnecessarily
antonym: internally, inherently, relevantly, necessarily
词性: 副词
