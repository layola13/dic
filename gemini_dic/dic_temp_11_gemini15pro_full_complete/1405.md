accusingly:/əˈkjuːzɪŋli/
adverb
指责地，控诉地
in a way that suggests that you think someone has done something wrong
"You stole my pen!" she said accusingly. “你偷了我的笔！”她指责地说。
近义词: reproachfully, critically, blamingly
反义词: approvingly, supportively
词性: adverb

词源: From the verb "accuse" (from Old French *acus(er), from Latin accusare "to call to account"), plus the adverbial suffix "-ingly".
