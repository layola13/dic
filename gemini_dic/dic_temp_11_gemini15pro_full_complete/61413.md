embody:/ɪmˈbɒdi/ (UK) /ɪmˈbɑːdi/ (US)| v.|体现，包含，代表，使具体化|to express or represent (something) in a tangible or visible form|The dancer's movements embodied the spirit of freedom.  舞者的动作体现了自由的精神。

The constitution embodies the ideals of democracy. 宪法体现了民主的理想。|近义词：represent, personify, incorporate, exemplify|反义词：exclude, omit|词性:动词
