anti-consumer:/ˌænti kənˈsuːmər/
adjective
反消费主义的
Opposed to or hostile towards consumerism, the preoccupation of society with the acquisition of consumer goods.
Many anti-consumer activists believe that excessive consumption is harmful to the environment.  许多反消费主义者认为过度消费对环境有害。
The documentary presented an anti-consumer message, encouraging viewers to simplify their lives. 这部纪录片传达了反消费主义的信息，鼓励观众简化生活。
近义词:  anti-materialistic, non-materialistic
反义词:  consumerist, materialistic
词性: adjective
