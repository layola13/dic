belleek:/bəˈliːk/
n.
贝里克瓷器; 一种产自爱尔兰贝里克镇的瓷器
A type of fine Parian china produced in Belleek, Ireland.
This antique shop specializes in belleek and other Irish pottery. 这家古董店专营贝里克瓷器和其他爱尔兰陶器。
近义词: Parian china, porcelain
反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑粗陶 earthenware)
词性: 名词

词源:  Named after the village of Belleek, County Fermanagh, Ireland, where the pottery is produced.
