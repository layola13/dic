bombable:/ˈbɒməbəl/| adj.|可轰炸的；易受轰炸的|Susceptible to being bombed; suitable as a target for bombs.|The bridge was considered a bombable target.  这座桥被认为是一个可轰炸的目标。|Synonyms: vulnerable, exposed, susceptible|Antonyms: invulnerable, protected, safe|adjective


Etymology->

From bomb + -able.  Ultimately from the Greek word *bombos* meaning a deep, hollow sound.
