fendered:/ˈfɛndəd/| adj.|有挡泥板的，有防护装置的|Provided or equipped with fenders, which are protective guards or bumpers, often found on boats, cars, or other vehicles to prevent damage from impact.|The fendered tugboat nudged the larger ship into the dock.  有挡泥板的拖船将较大的船推入码头。|近义词：protected, guarded, shielded|反义词:unprotected, exposed|词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "fender" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Fender" itself comes from the verb "defend," tracing back to Old French "defendre" and ultimately to Latin "defendere."
