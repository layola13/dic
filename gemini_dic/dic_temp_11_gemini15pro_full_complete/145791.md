overdebilitated:/ˈoʊvər dəˈbɪlɪˌteɪtɪd/
adjective
过度衰弱的，极度虚弱的
Excessively weakened or enfeebled, beyond simply being debilitated.  It implies a severe state of weakness.
The overdebilitated patient was unable to sit up in bed without assistance.  过度衰弱的病人无法在没有帮助的情况下坐起来。

The marathon runner, overdebilitated by the extreme heat, collapsed just before the finish line.  由于极度高温，过度虚弱的马拉松选手在终点线前倒下了。


近义词: extremely weak, severely weakened, enfeebled, exhausted, frail
反义词: strong, healthy, robust, vigorous, energetic
词性: adjective
