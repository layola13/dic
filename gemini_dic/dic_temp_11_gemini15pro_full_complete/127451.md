nebule:/ˈnɛbjuːl/ (US), /ˈnɛbjʊl/ (UK)| n. |星云; 云状物|A faint, cloudlike mass of gas and dust in interstellar space.|The Horsehead Nebula is a famous example. 马头星云是一个著名的例子。|synonyms: cloud, haze, mist|antonyms: clarity, clearness|noun

nebular:/ˈnɛbjʊlə(r)/ (UK), /ˈnɛbjələr/ (US)| adj. |星云的; 模糊的|Relating to or resembling a nebula; indistinct or vague.|The nebular hypothesis explains the formation of the solar system. 星云假说解释了太阳系的形成。|synonyms: hazy, misty, cloudy, vague|antonyms: clear, distinct, sharp|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nebula*, meaning "mist, fog, cloud, vapor," related to *nubes* "cloud."
