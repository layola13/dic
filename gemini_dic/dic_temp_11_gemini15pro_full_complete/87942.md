higgle:/ˈhɪɡəl/| v.|讨价还价，争论价格|To bargain persistently, especially over the price of something.|例句：They spent hours haggling over the price of the car. 他们在汽车价格上讨价还价了几个小时。|例句：The vendor and the customer haggled over the price of the fruit.  小贩和顾客就水果的价格讨价还价。|近义词：bargain, barter, negotiate, dicker|反义词:agree, concede|词性:动词
