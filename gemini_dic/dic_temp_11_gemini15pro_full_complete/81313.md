grapeless:/ˈɡreɪplɪs/| adj.|无葡萄的，没有葡萄的|Lacking grapes; without grapes.  Derived from "grape" + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).|"The grapeless vine looked barren."  这棵没有葡萄的藤蔓看起来很荒芜。|Synonyms: vineless, barren (when referring to vines)|Antonyms: grape-bearing, fruitful (when referring to vines)|adjective
