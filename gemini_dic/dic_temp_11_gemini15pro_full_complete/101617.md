irreconciliation:/ɪˌrɛkənˌsɪliˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 不可调和，无法和解，不一致 |The irreconciliation between the two families lasted for generations.  两个家族之间的不和持续了几代人。 | Example:  The irreconciliation between the two parties made a peaceful solution impossible. 双方之间的不可调和使得和平解决成为不可能。 | Synonyms:  discord, conflict, antagonism, hostility | Antonyms:  reconciliation, agreement, harmony, accord | noun
