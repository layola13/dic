out-glooms: /ˌaʊtˈɡluːmz/
verb
使更显阴郁；使黯然失色
to make something seem gloomy or dark by comparison
The storm clouds out-gloomed the already gray landscape. 暴风云使原本灰暗的景色更加阴郁。
eclipse, overshadow, darken
brighten, lighten
词性：动词

词源：
From out- + gloom.  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed," and "gloom" refers to darkness or a depressed mood. Therefore, "out-gloom" literally means "to surpass in gloominess."
