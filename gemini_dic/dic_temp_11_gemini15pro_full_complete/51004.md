plural form of desistence.
例句：The multiple desistences from the various factions complicated the peace process. 各个派系的多次停战使和平进程复杂化。

词性：名词

词源：From the verb "desist," which comes from the Old French "desister" and ultimately from the Latin "desistere" (to stand off, leave off).
