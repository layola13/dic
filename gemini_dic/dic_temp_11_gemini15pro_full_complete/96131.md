indemnify:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪ/| vt.|赔偿，保障|To compensate for loss or damage; to protect against future loss or damage.|The insurance company will indemnify you for the damage to your car. 保险公司将赔偿你的车辆损失。|近义词：compensate, reimburse, protect|反义词: penalize, charge|verb

indemnity:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪti/| n.|赔偿，保障|Protection against loss or damage; security against legal responsibility for one's actions.|The company agreed to pay an indemnity to the victims of the accident. 公司同意向事故受害者支付赔偿金。|近义词：compensation, restitution, security|反义词: penalty, forfeiture|noun
