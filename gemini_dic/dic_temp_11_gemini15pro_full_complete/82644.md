gueparde:/ɡəˈpɑːrd/| n.|猎豹|A cheetah, a large spotted cat known for its speed. It is native to Africa and parts of Iran.  Derived from the Italian word "gattopardo," meaning "cat-leopard," which in turn comes from the Latin "cattus pardus." |例句：The gueparde is the fastest land animal. 猎豹是陆地上跑得最快的动物。

例句：The gueparde stalked its prey through the tall grass. 猎豹在高高的草丛中追踪猎物。|近义词：cheetah|反义词:|词性:noun
