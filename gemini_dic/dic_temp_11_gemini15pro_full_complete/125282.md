multifid:/ˈmʌltɪfɪd/| adj. n.|多裂的，多裂肌|A muscle with multiple divisions or attachments.|The multifid muscles support the spine. 多裂肌支撑着脊柱。|近义词:multipartite, multidivided|反义词:undivided, whole|词性:形容词/名词

Etymology: From Latin *multifidus*, from *multus* ("many") + *fidus* ("divided," past participle of *findere* "to cleave, split").
