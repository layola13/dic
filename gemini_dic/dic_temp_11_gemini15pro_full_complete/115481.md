manumotor:/ˌmænjʊˈmoʊtər/ | adj. | 手动的，手控的 | Operated by hand; not automatic. |  The manumotor valve required manual adjustment.  手动阀需要手动调节。|  The potter skillfully used a manumotor wheel to shape the clay.  陶工熟练地使用手动轮来塑造粘土。 | 近义词: manual, hand-operated | 反义词: automatic, motorized | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *manumotus*, from *manus* (hand) + *motus* (moved), past participle of *movēre* (to move).
