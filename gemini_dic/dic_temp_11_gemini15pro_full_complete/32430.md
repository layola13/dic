chime:/tʃaɪm/| n. vi. vt.|钟声，和谐，插话|A sound made by a bell or a set of bells.  To make a ringing sound, typically a clear, high-pitched sound. To be in agreement or harmony.|The church bells chimed at noon. 教堂的钟在中午敲响了。|Synonyms: ring, peal, toll, resonate|Antonyms: clang, clash|noun, verb

chime:/tʃaɪm/| n. vi. vt.|插话，附和，符合|To interrupt a conversation briefly to add a comment, often in agreement. To be in agreement or harmony.|She chimed in with her opinion. 她插话发表了她的意见。|Synonyms: interject, interrupt, concur, agree|Antonyms: disagree, object|noun, verb
