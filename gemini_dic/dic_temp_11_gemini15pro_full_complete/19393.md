It is produced by the reaction of carbon dioxide with water and a base, or by the neutralization of carbonic acid with a base.|The body uses bicarbonate to regulate blood pH. 身体使用碳酸氢盐来调节血液的pH值。|synonyms: hydrogen carbonate|antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + carbonate (from carbon, ultimately from Latin carbo "coal").  The "bi-" prefix refers to the presence of one hydrogen ion in the anion (HCO3-), compared to the two in carbonic acid (H2CO3).
