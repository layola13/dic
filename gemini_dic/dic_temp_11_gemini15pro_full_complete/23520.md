brain-drain:/ˈbreɪn dreɪn/
noun
人才流失
The emigration of highly trained or educated people to a country where they can enjoy better pay or living conditions.
Many developing countries suffer from a brain drain, as their brightest minds move to wealthier nations. 许多发展中国家都遭受人才流失，因为他们最优秀的人才迁移到更富裕的国家。
近义词:human capital flight, talent migration, loss of skilled workers
反义词:talent gain, repatriation of talent


brain-drains:/ˈbreɪn dreɪnz/
noun
人才流失(复数)
The plural form of brain drain, referring to multiple instances of this phenomenon.
The conference addressed the brain drains affecting several African nations.  这次会议讨论了影响几个非洲国家的人才流失问题。
近义词:human capital flights, talent migrations, losses of skilled workers
反义词:talent gains, repatriations of talent
