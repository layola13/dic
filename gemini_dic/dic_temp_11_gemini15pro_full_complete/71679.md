flaunt:/flɔːnt/ | v. |炫耀，卖弄，招摇 | To display (something) ostentatiously, especially in order to provoke envy or admiration or to show defiance. | She flaunted her new diamond ring. 她炫耀着她的新钻戒。 |  He flaunted his wealth by driving expensive cars. 他开着豪车炫耀自己的财富。 | 近义词：show off, boast, parade, display | 反义词：hide, conceal, downplay | 词性：动词

词源:
From Middle English *flaunten (“to display ostentatiously”), of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse flana (“to stare, gape”).
