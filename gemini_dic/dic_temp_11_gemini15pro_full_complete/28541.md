carding:/ˈkɑːrdɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːrdɪŋ/| n. |梳理，刷毛，起毛，非法盗用信用卡 |The process of combing fibers to prepare them for spinning.  Also, the fraudulent practice of using stolen credit card information.|The wool needs carding before it can be spun into yarn. 羊毛在纺成纱线之前需要梳理。/  Police are cracking down on carding operations. 警方正在打击盗用信用卡的行动。|近义词：combing, brushing, skimming (for fraudulent credit card use)|反义词: None (for the textile sense) / legitimate use (for the fraud sense) |词性: Noun
