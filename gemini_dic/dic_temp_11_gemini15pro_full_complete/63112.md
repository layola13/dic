ensigncy:/ˈɛnsənsi/ | n. | 旗帜;标志 | A flag or banner, especially one used as a symbol of office or authority. (This meaning is archaic.) |  The king's ensigncy was carried at the head of the procession. 国王的旗帜在队伍的最前面。| 近义词: banner, flag, standard | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French enseigne, based on Latin insignia ‘distinguishing marks’.
