equinia:/ɪˈkwɪnɪə/ /ɪˈkwaɪnɪə/ | n. | 马鼻疽; 腐鼻病 | A contagious bacterial disease of horses, donkeys, and mules, characterized by inflammation of the mucous membranes, especially of the respiratory tract, and by the formation of nodules and ulcers in the skin, lungs, and other organs. It is transmissible to humans. | The vet diagnosed the horse with equinia. 兽医诊断这匹马得了马鼻疽。|  The outbreak of equinia led to the quarantine of the entire stable. 马鼻疽的爆发导致整个马厩被隔离。| 近义词:glanders | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific disease) | 词性: noun
