bairam: /ˈbaɪˌrɑːm/ /ˈbaɪrəm/ | n. | 节日，(尤指穆斯林的)拜兰节 | A Muslim festival, especially Eid al-Fitr or Eid al-Adha. |  Example 1: They celebrated Bairam with feasting and prayer. 他们以盛宴和祈祷庆祝拜兰节。

Example 2:  The family gathered for a special meal during Bairam.  家人在拜兰节期间聚在一起吃了一顿特别的饭。| Synonym: festival, feast, celebration, holiday | Antonym: workday | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Turkish *bayram*, from Ottoman Turkish *بايرام* (*bayrām*), from Persian *بایرام* (*bayrām*). Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly from Middle Turkic *bajram/* (“holiday, festival”).
