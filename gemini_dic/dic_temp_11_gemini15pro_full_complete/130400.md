non-visceral:/ˌnɒnˈvɪsərəl/
adjective
非本能的; 非内脏的; 理性的
Not relating to deep inward feelings; not instinctive or emotional.  Relating to the intellect rather than to emotions.
例句：
His reaction to the news was surprisingly non-visceral, more thoughtful than emotional.  他对这个消息的反应出奇地理性，更多的是思考而不是情绪化。

She took a non-visceral approach to the problem, carefully analyzing the data before making a decision. 她对这个问题采取了一种理性的方法，在做出决定之前仔细分析了数据。


近义词：
cerebral, intellectual, rational, logical, unemotional, detached, objective
反义词：
visceral, instinctive, emotional, intuitive, gut, passionate


词性
adjective
