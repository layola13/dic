encystation:/ɛnˌsɪˈsteɪʃən/
n.
包囊形成，成囊
The process by which a parasite forms a cyst or protective wall around itself.
The encystation of the amoeba allowed it to survive harsh conditions.  阿米巴的包囊形成使其能够在恶劣条件下生存。

近义词: cyst formation
反义词: excystation
