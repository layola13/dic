galleylike:/ˈɡæliˌlaɪk/
adjective
像船舱的；狭长拥挤的
Resembling a galley, especially in being long, narrow, and cramped.
The apartment was galleylike, with a tiny kitchen crammed into one end.  这公寓像船舱一样，狭长拥挤，一个小厨房挤在房间的一头。
The corridor was dark and galleylike. 走廊又黑又像船舱一样狭窄。
近义词: cramped, narrow, confined, constricted
反义词: spacious, roomy, wide

词性: adjective
