heteroplastic:/ˌhɛtərəˈplæstɪk/
adjective
异质成形的，异种移植的
Formed from tissue not normal to the part, or from tissue of another individual.
Example: Heteroplastic bone grafting involves using bone tissue from a donor.  异种骨移植涉及使用来自供体的骨组织。
Example:  The surgeon performed a heteroplastic skin graft.  外科医生进行了异种皮肤移植。
近义词: allogeneic, xenogeneic
反义词: autoplastic, isoplastic
词性: adjective
