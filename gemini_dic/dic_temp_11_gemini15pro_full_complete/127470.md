necessary:/ˈnesəseri/ (US) /ˈnesəs(ə)ri/ (UK)| adj.|必要的，必需的|Something that is necessary is needed in order for something else to happen. |例句：A driver's license is necessary to drive a car. 驾照是开车所必需的。

例句：Food and water are necessary for survival. 食物和水是生存所必需的。|近义词：essential, required, vital, indispensable, crucial|反义词：unnecessary, optional, nonessential|词性:形容词
