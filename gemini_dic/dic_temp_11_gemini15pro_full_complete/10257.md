apotheosizing:/əˈpɑːθiəˌsaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|神化；将…尊奉为神；理想化|Elevating something to the status of a god; glorifying or idealizing to an extreme degree.|The biographer was criticized for apotheosizing the president, ignoring his flaws and failures.|近义词:glorifying, exalting, deifying, idolizing|反义词:vilifying, denigrating, condemning|词性:动词(present participle)
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *apothéose*, from Late Latin *apotheosis*, from Greek *apotheōsis*, from *apo* (from) + *theos* (god) + *-osis* (suffix forming nouns of action or process).
