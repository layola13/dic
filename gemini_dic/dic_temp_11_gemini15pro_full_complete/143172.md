outclerk: /ˈaʊtklɜːrk/ (British English) /ˈaʊtklɜːrk/ (American English)
n.|外勤办事员；户外办事员|A clerk who works outside of an office, often dealing with customers or performing tasks in the field.|The outclerk delivered the packages to the customers. 外勤办事员把包裹送给了顾客们。|例句：The outclerk was responsible for checking inventory in the warehouse.  外勤办事员负责检查仓库里的库存。|近义词：field clerk, outside clerk|反义词：office clerk, inside clerk|词性: 名词

词源:  "out-" (meaning outside) + "clerk" (meaning a worker who keeps records or accounts)
