outtell:/ˌaʊtˈtɛl/
verb
胜过；讲述更精彩的故事；比...讲得更详细
to surpass in telling stories; to tell something more fully or effectively than another
He outtold all the other storytellers with his captivating tales of adventure. 他以其引人入胜的冒险故事胜过所有其他讲故事的人。
The witness outtold the defendant's version of events.  证人比被告讲述的事件版本更详细。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词:  be outdone, fall short
词性: verb
