non-musical:/ˌnɑːnˈmjuːzɪkəl/
adjective
非音乐的；不懂音乐的
Not related to or skilled in music.
She has a non-musical ear and can't tell the difference between a violin and a viola. 她不懂音乐，分辨不出小提琴和中提琴的区别。
Synonyms: unmusical, non-melodious, tone-deaf
Antonyms: musical, melodious, tuneful
词性: adjective
