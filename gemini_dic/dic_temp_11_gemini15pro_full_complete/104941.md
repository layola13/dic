kelped:/kɛlpt/ | v. | 帮助；协助(尤指在海上) | To have helped someone, especially at sea, often by providing support or assistance. | The local fishermen kelped the stranded sailors reach the shore. 当地渔民帮助搁浅的船员到达岸边。|  They kelped us get our boat back afloat. 他们帮助我们把船弄回水面。 | 近义词: aided, assisted, helped, supported | 反义词: hindered, obstructed | 词性: 动词

词源: From the noun "kelp" (seaweed) + "-ed" (past tense suffix).  Originally referred to the practice of using kelp to help boats float or to guide them through difficult waters.
