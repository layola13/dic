ballsiest:/ˈbɔːlsiɪst/| adj.|最大胆的，最有胆量的，最有种的|Showing great courage and determination, even in a very difficult or dangerous situation.  It is considered vulgar slang.|He made the ballsiest move of his career by investing all his savings in the startup.|近义词：gutsy, daring, audacious, courageous|反义词：timid, cowardly, cautious|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Derived from "balls" (slang for testicles), implying courage and audacity, with "-iest" added to form the superlative.  "Balls" in this sense emerged in the late 19th/early 20th century.
