nonmetaphorically: /ˌnɑːnˌmɛtəˈfɔːrɪkli/ | adv. | 非比喻地，字面上地 | In a literal sense; not figuratively or metaphorically.  It means exactly what it says, without any hidden or symbolic meaning. |  He said he was dying, nonmetaphorically, from boredom. 他说他快要无聊死了，这不是比喻，是真的。

He explained the situation nonmetaphorically, sticking to the bare facts. 他非比喻地解释了情况，坚持只讲事实。 | literally, directly | figuratively, metaphorically | adverb
