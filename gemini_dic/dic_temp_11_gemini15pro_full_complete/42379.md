counterbidding:/ˈkaʊntərˌbɪdɪŋ/
noun
还价；反投标；竞争性出价
the act of offering a higher price than someone else in order to buy something, or the act of making a higher bid than someone who has already bid in an auction.
Example: There was a fierce round of counterbidding before the painting was finally sold.  在画作最终售出之前，经历了一轮激烈的竞价。

Example: The company engaged in aggressive counterbidding to acquire its rival.  该公司进行了激烈的反竞标以收购其竞争对手。
synonym: outbidding, competitive bidding, raising the bid
antonym: accepting a bid, conceding
n.
