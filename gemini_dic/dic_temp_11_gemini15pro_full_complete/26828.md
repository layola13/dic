cagot:/kæˈɡoʊ/ /ˈkæɡɒt/| n.|贱民，被社会排斥的人|A member of a despised and outcast group, especially in southern France and northern Spain.|In medieval times, cagots were forced to live in separate communities and were often denied basic rights. 在中世纪，贱民被迫生活在独立的社区，并且经常被剥夺基本权利。|outcast, pariah, untouchable|aristocrat, nobleman|noun
