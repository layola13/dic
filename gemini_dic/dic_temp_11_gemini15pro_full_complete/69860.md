ferromagnets:/ˌferəʊˈmæɡnɪts/ | n. | 铁磁体 |A material (such as iron) that exhibits ferromagnetism.  A ferromagnet is a material that can be permanently magnetized by exposure to a magnetic field. |Iron and nickel are common ferromagnets. 铁和镍是常见的铁磁体。|  Lodestone is a naturally occurring ferromagnet. 天然磁石是一种天然存在的铁磁体。 | synonyms: magnetic material, magnetic substance | antonyms: diamagnetic material, paramagnetic material | noun
