out-voter: /ˈaʊt ˌvoʊtər/
noun
在外地投票者;不在户籍地投票的选民
A person who votes in a place other than their usual place of residence, often due to temporary relocation for work or study.
Many college students are out-voters during election season. 许多大学生在选举期间都是在外地投票者。
Out-voters need to register in advance to vote absentee.  在外地投票者需要提前登记才能缺席投票。
Synonyms: absentee voter, non-resident voter
Antonyms: resident voter
n.
