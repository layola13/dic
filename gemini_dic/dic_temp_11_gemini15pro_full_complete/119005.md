methodic:/məˈθɒdɪk/ (US) /məˈθɑːdɪk/ (UK)| adj.|有条理的，有条不紊的，井井有条的|Done according to a systematic or established procedure; orderly.|She is a methodic worker, always following a set routine. 她是一个有条理的工作者，总是遵循一套固定的程序。|The detective conducted a methodic search of the crime scene.  侦探对犯罪现场进行了有条不紊的搜索。|近义词：systematic, organized, orderly, structured|反义词：disorganized, haphazard, chaotic, random|词性:形容词

词源:
From French *méthodique*, from Late Latin *methodicus*, from Greek *μεθοδικός (methodikos)*, from *μέθοδος (methodos)* meaning "way, pursuit, following after."
