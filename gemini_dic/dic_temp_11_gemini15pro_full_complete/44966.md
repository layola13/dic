crystallizes:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪz/
verb
结晶；明确；具体化
(of a substance) form or cause to form crystals.  Give a definite or concrete form to (an idea, plan, or feeling).
The salt crystallizes as the water evaporates. 盐随着水的蒸发而结晶。
His vague ideas crystallized into a concrete plan. 他模糊的想法具体化成了一个具体的计划。
近义词: solidify, harden, materialize, concretize
反义词: dissolve, melt, liquefy
词性: verb
