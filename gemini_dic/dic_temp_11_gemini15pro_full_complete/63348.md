entoil:/ɪnˈtɔɪl/
verb
使陷入困境，使纠缠
to involve someone in a difficult or complicated situation that they cannot easily escape from
The expedition was soon entoiled in the dense jungle. 探险队很快就陷入了茂密的丛林中。
近义词:entangle, ensnare, involve, implicate
反义词:extricate, free, liberate
词性:v.

Etymology:
From en- (meaning "in" or "into") + toil (meaning "hard work" or "a net").  The original meaning was "to catch in a net," later evolving to the more figurative sense of being caught in a difficult situation.
