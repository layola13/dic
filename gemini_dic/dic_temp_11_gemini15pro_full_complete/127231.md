nauseant:/ˈnɔːziənt/ /ˈnɔːʃənt/
| adj.|令人作呕的； nauseating |Causing nausea; nauseating. |The smell of the rotting fish was nauseant. 腐烂的鱼的气味令人作呕。|近义词：nauseating, sickening, disgusting, revolting|反义词:pleasant, agreeable|词性:形容词
nauseant:/ˈnɔːziənt/ /ˈnɔːʃənt/
| n.|催吐剂|An agent that induces nausea; an emetic.|The doctor prescribed a nauseant to help the patient expel the poison. 医生开了催吐剂帮助病人排出毒素。|近义词：emetic, vomitive|反义词:anti-emetic|词性:名词
