compensative:/kəmˈpɛnsətɪv/
adjective
补偿的，赔偿的
serving to compensate; making up for a loss or deficiency.
例句：The company offered a compensative payment for the delay. 公司为延误提供了补偿金。
例句：She received compensative damages for the injury she sustained. 她因受伤获得了赔偿金。
近义词：compensatory, remedial, restorative
反义词：punitive, detrimental
词性: adjective

compensatory:/kəmˈpɛnsətɔːri/
adjective
补偿的，赔偿的
reducing or offsetting the unpleasant or unwelcome effects of something.
例句：He received compensatory damages for his injuries.  他因受伤获得了赔偿金。
例句：The government offered compensatory payments to farmers affected by the drought. 政府向受干旱影响的农民提供补偿款。
近义词：compensative, remedial, restorative
反义词：punitive, detrimental
词性: adjective

词源:  Late 16th century from late Latin *compensativus, from compensat- ‘weighed together’, from the verb compensare (see compensate).
