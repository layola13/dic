apteran:/ˈeɪptərən/ (US), /ˈæptərən/ (UK)| adj. n.|无翅的；无翅昆虫|An apteran insect or animal is one that has no wings.  Apteran can also be used as a noun to refer to an apterous insect.|例句：The apteran stick insect blended in perfectly with the branch.  这只无翅竹节虫与树枝完美地融合在一起。

例句：Apterans are often found in leaf litter and soil. 无翅昆虫常在落叶层和土壤中发现。|近义词：wingless, flightless|反义词：winged, alate|词性: adjective, noun


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek *ἄπτερος (ápteros, “wingless”), from ἀ- (a-, “without”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”).
