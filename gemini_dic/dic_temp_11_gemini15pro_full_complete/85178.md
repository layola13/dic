harei:/ˈhɑːreɪ/ (US), /ˈheəri/ (UK)| n.|野兔|A fast-running, long-eared mammal resembling a large rabbit.|The harei darted across the field. 野兔飞快地穿过田野。|Synonyms: rabbit, jackrabbit, leveret|Antonyms: predator|Noun
