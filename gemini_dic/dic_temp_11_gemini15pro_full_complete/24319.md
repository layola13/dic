brilliance:/ˈbrɪljəns/| n.|光辉；光彩；卓越；杰出|The brilliance of the diamond dazzled her. 钻石的光辉使她眼花缭乱。|近义词：radiance, splendor, luminosity, genius, excellence|反义词：dullness, mediocrity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle French *brilliant*, from Italian *brillante*, present participle of *brillare* "to shine," from Vulgar Latin *berillare*, of uncertain origin.
