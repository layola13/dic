nondeformities: /ˌnɒndiˈfɔːrmɪtiz/ | n. | 无畸形，无变形 | Absence of deformities; the state of being free from malformations or distortions. |  The surgeon noted the nondeformities of the patient's limbs after the successful surgery. 手术成功后，外科医生注意到病人四肢没有畸形。|  The skeletal remains showed no signs of nondeformities, indicating a healthy individual. 骨骼遗骸没有显示出畸形的迹象，表明这是一个健康的个体。 | synonyms: normality, soundness,  integrity | antonyms: deformities, malformations, distortions | noun

词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
deformity (noun): From Late Latin deformitas, from Latin deformis "misshapen," from de- "away from" + forma "form, shape."
-ities (suffix):  Plural form of the suffix "-ity," used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
