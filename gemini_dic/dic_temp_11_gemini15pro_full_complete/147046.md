overlayed:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪd/ | adj. | 覆盖的，重叠的 | Covered or superimposed with something else.  It describes something placed on top of something else, often partially obscuring what's underneath. | The image was overlayed with text. 图片上覆盖着文字。 | synonyms: superimposed, overlaid, covered, layered | antonyms: uncovered, exposed, bare | adjective

overlayed:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪd/ | v. | 覆盖，重叠 (past participle of overlay) | To cover or superimpose something with something else. | The artist overlayed the painting with a layer of varnish. 艺术家在画上覆盖了一层清漆。 | synonyms: superimpose, cover, layer | antonyms: uncover, expose, remove | verb
