directorships:/dəˈrɛktərˌʃɪps/ |n.|董事职位；董事身份|The position of being a director of a company or organization.  A person may hold multiple directorships.|He holds several directorships on the boards of major corporations. 他在几家大公司的董事会担任董事职务。|Near Synonyms: board memberships, board seats, directorship, chairmanship (when applicable)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) unemployment, dismissal|noun
