filterers:/ˈfɪltərərz/ | n. | 过滤者；筛选者 | People or things that filter something.  Those who remove impurities or unwanted elements from a substance or information. | The filterers removed all the impurities from the water. 过滤者从水中去除了所有杂质。| The search engine's filterers block inappropriate content. 搜索引擎的过滤器屏蔽了不适当的内容。 | synonyms: strainers, sifters, purifiers, screeners | antonyms: polluters, contaminators | noun

filterers:/ˈfɪltərərz/ | n. | 过滤装置 | Devices that filter something. Mechanisms designed to remove impurities or unwanted elements from a substance. | The coffee filterers were clogged. 咖啡过滤装置堵塞了。| Air filterers help to improve air quality. 空气过滤装置有助于改善空气质量。 | synonyms: filters, strainers, sieves | antonyms: N/A | noun
