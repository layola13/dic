agreement:/əˈɡriːmənt/| n.|协议；同意；一致|A negotiated and typically legally binding arrangement between parties as to a course of action.|We finally reached an agreement on the price. 我们最终就价格达成了一致。|近义词：accord, contract, deal, pact, settlement, understanding|反义词：disagreement, conflict, dispute|noun
