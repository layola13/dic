brucine:/ˈbruːsiːn/ | n. |番木鳖碱；马钱子碱|A very poisonous alkaloid, C₂₃H₂₆N₂O₄, closely related to strychnine and occurring with it in the seeds of *nux vomica*.  It is used medicinally as a stimulant to the central nervous system. |Brucine is less toxic than strychnine. 番木鳖碱的毒性比士的宁小。|strychnine,  vomicine|  (no common antonym exists for a specific chemical compound)|noun
