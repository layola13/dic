collidine:/ˈkɒlɪdiːn/ /ˈkɑːlɪdiːn/| n. |可力丁；甲基吡啶|A colorless, flammable liquid, C₈H₁₁N, any of several isomeric methylpyridines, obtained from coal tar and bone oil, used in organic synthesis.|Collidine is used as a solvent in organic synthesis. 可力丁在有机合成中用作溶剂。|synonyms: methylpyridine|antonyms: N/A|noun
