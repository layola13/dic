bodyguard: n.
bodyguard: 保镖
bodyguard: a person employed to protect someone from attack or harm.
bodyguard: The celebrity travelled with a team of bodyguards.  这位名人带着一队保镖出行。

bodyguard: The president's bodyguards were always close at hand. 总统的保镖总是近在咫尺。
