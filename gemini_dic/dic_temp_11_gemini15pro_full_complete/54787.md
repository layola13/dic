dispensation:/ˌdɪspenˈseɪʃn/ | n. |分配; 分发; 免除; 特许; (尤指宗教的) 特许,豁免; 天意,神的旨意| an act of dispensing; something that is distributed or given out; exemption from a rule or usual requirement | The teacher gave me a dispensation to leave class early. 老师特许我提前下课。 | 近义词: exemption, immunity, release, exception, distribution, allocation | 反义词: requirement, obligation, restriction | 词性: noun

dispense:/dɪˈspens/ | v. | 分配; 分发; 免除; 配(药); | to distribute or give out; to administer; to exempt from a rule or obligation | The machine dispenses snacks and drinks. 这台机器分发零食和饮料。| 近义词: distribute, allocate, administer, provide, supply | 反义词: withhold, retain, collect, gather | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English dispensacioun, from Old French dispensacion, from Latin dispensātiō (“management, administration”), from dispensō (“weigh out, distribute”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + pendō (“weigh, pay”).
