babylonish:/ˌbæbɪˈloʊnɪʃ/ | adj. | 巴比伦的；混乱的，嘈杂的 | Resembling or characteristic of the ancient city of Babylon, especially its language or confusing, noisy character.  |  The market was a babylonish cacophony of different languages. 市场里充斥着各种语言，嘈杂得像巴比伦一样。 | Synonyms: chaotic, confusing, disorderly, tumultuous, noisy | Antonyms: orderly, organized, quiet, peaceful | adjective

babylonish:/ˌbæbɪˈloʊnɪʃ/ | n. | 巴比伦语；混乱的言语 | The language of ancient Babylon or a confused and unintelligible jargon. |  He spoke a strange babylonish that nobody could understand. 他说了一种奇怪的巴比伦语，没人能听懂。 | Synonyms: jargon, gibberish, cant | Antonyms: clarity, lucidity | noun
