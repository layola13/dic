ormolu:/ˈɔːrməluː/
n.
镀金青铜;金铜合金
An alloy of copper, zinc, and tin, gilded or made to resemble gilding.  A decorative object made of ormolu.
The ormolu clock on the mantelpiece glittered in the firelight. 壁炉架上的镀金青铜钟在火光下闪闪发光。
近义词:gilt bronze, gilded bronze
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain bronze" or "unadorned bronze")
词性:noun
