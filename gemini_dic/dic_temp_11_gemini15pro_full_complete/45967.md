cyanaurate:/ˌsaɪəˈnɔːreɪt/
noun
氰金酸盐
A salt of cyanoauric acid, containing the [Au(CN)4]- anion.
Potassium cyanaurate is used in gold plating. 氰金酸钾用于镀金。
近义词: gold cyanide complex

词性:n.
