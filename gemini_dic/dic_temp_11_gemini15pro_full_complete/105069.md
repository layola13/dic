kerbsides:/ˈkɜːbsaɪdz/ (UK) /ˈkɜːrbˌsaɪdz/ (US)| n.|路边，街沿|The area at the side of a road, usually paved and raised slightly above the level of the road itself.  Often used in the context of waste collection or parking.|The council collects garden waste from the kerbsides every fortnight.  市政委员会每两周从路边收集一次园艺垃圾。|Synonyms: curbside (US), roadside, pavement edge|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "road centre")|Noun


kerbside:/ˈkɜːbsaɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɜːrbˌsaɪd/ (US)| adj.|路边的|Relating to or located at the kerb/curb.|Kerbside recycling collections are becoming increasingly common. 路边回收越来越普遍。|Synonyms: curbside (US), roadside|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|Adjective
