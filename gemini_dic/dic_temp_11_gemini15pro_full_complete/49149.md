dejectednesses:/dɪˈdʒɛktɪdnɪsɪz/| n. |沮丧，灰心丧气|The state of being dejected; low spirits.  A collection or accumulation of instances of dejection.  This word is rarely used in modern English, and "dejection" is preferred.|His repeated dejectednesses worried his friends. 他反复的沮丧让他的朋友们很担心。|Near synonyms: dejection, despondency, discouragement, downheartedness|Antonyms: cheerfulness, elation, happiness|Noun


dejected:/dɪˈdʒɛktɪd/| adj. |沮丧的，灰心的|Sad and dispirited.|He looked dejected after failing the exam.  考试不及格后，他看起来很沮丧。|Near synonyms: downcast, depressed, disheartened|Antonyms: cheerful, elated, happy|Adjective
