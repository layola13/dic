funkiness:/ˈfʌŋkɪnəs/
n.
古怪;时髦;特殊
The state or quality of being funky.  A distinctive unconventional and stylish quality.
Her apartment had a certain funkiness that I really liked. 她的公寓有一种我非常喜欢的独特古怪的风格。
Synonyms: unconventionality, eccentricity, quirkiness, distinctiveness, individuality, originality, coolness, hipness, stylishness
Antonyms: conventionality, normality, ordinariness, dullness, blandness
