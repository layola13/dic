apanthropy:/ˌæpænˈθrəpi/
n.
厌世；遁世；不与人来往
Apathy towards human beings; dislike of human society.
He retreated to the mountains in apanthropy, seeking solitude away from the world. 他因厌世而隐居山林，寻求远离尘世的孤独。
近义词: misanthropy, reclusiveness
反义词: philanthropy, sociability
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Greek "apanthrōpos" (ἀπάνθρωπος), meaning "avoiding mankind," from "a-" (ἀ-) "without, not" + "anthrōpos" (ἄνθρωπος) "man, human being."
