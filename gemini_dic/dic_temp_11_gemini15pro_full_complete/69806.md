ferociously:/fəˈroʊʃəsli/
adverb
凶猛地，残暴地
in a ferocious manner; savagely.
The lion attacked the zebra ferociously. 狮子凶猛地攻击了斑马。
Synonyms: savagely, fiercely, violently, brutally, ruthlessly
Antonyms: gently, mildly, kindly, tenderly
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ferōciōsus, from Latin ferōx (“fierce, wild, savage”) + -iōsus.
