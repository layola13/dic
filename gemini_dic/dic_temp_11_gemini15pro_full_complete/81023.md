gradual:/ˈɡrædʒuəl/| adj.|逐渐的，逐步的|Happening, changing, or done slowly and by degrees; not sudden or abrupt.|The country experienced a gradual shift towards democracy. 这个国家经历了逐步向民主的转变。|The snow accumulated gradually, inch by inch. 雪逐渐累积，一寸一寸地.|Synonyms: progressive, steady, incremental, piecemeal|Antonyms: abrupt, sudden, rapid, immediate|adjective
