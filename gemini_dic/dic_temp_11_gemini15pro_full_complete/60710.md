electro-hydraulic:/ɪˌlektroʊ haɪˈdrɔːlɪk/ | adj. | 电液的；电动液压的 | Operated by both electricity and hydraulic fluid.  A system that uses both electrical and hydraulic components to achieve greater control and power. |  The aircraft uses an electro-hydraulic flight control system. 这架飞机使用电液飞行控制系统。| Synonyms: electrohydraulic, electropneumatic | Antonyms: manual, mechanical | adjective
