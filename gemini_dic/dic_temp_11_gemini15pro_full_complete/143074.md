outbranch:/ˌaʊtˈbræntʃ/
verb
分支；长出枝条
To spread out or divide into branches; to extend or develop in a branching manner.
The main road outbranches into several smaller lanes.  主干道分支成几条小巷。
The company outbranched into new markets. 公司拓展到新的市场。
近义词: branch out, diverge, ramify, spread out
反义词: converge, unite, consolidate
词性: v.

outbranch:/ˌaʊtˈbræntʃ/
noun
分支；分部
A branch or subdivision extending outward.
The local library is an outbranch of the city's main library system.  这家地方图书馆是市主要图书馆系统的一个分支。
The company opened a new outbranch in California. 公司在加州开设了一个新的分部。
近义词: branch, offshoot, subsidiary
反义词: main body, headquarters, center
词性: n.
