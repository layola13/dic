PHRASE->
None (This is a suffix, not a standalone word, and doesn't have common phrases.)


注意事项:
This suffix is always unstressed.  It's pronounced with a schwa /ə/ before the /z/ sound.

This suffix generally follows verbs ending in -ize, though there can be exceptions.
