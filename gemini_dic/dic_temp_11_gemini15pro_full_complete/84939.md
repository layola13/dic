Hanukkah: /ˈhɑːnəkə/ (US), /ˈxænəkə/ (UK)| n.|光明节|A Jewish festival commemorating the rededication of the Second Temple in Jerusalem at the time of the Maccabean Revolt against the Seleucid Empire. It is celebrated for eight nights and days, starting on the 25th day of Kislev according to the Hebrew calendar, which may occur at any time from late November to late December in the Gregorian calendar.|例句：We celebrate Hanukkah by lighting candles on the menorah each night. 我们每晚点燃烛台上的蜡烛来庆祝光明节。

例句：My family gets together for a big meal on the first night of Hanukkah. 我的家人在光明节的第一天晚上聚在一起吃大餐。


近义词：Festival of Lights (光明节的另一个名称)|反义词: None (没有确切的反义词)|词性: noun
