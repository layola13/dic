changelessness:/ˈtʃeɪndʒləsnəs/| n. |不变性，永恒性|The state of being unchanging or constant.|The changelessness of the mountains gives a sense of peace.  群山的永恒给人一种宁静的感觉。|近义词：immutability, permanence, constancy|反义词：mutability, variability, inconstancy|词性:名词

Etymology:
From change + -less + -ness.  "Change" comes from Old French "changier" (to change). "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking." "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
