belay:/bɪˈleɪ/| v. |停止；固定；阻止；确认|To secure a climber with a rope; to stop or cease.|The climber belayed his partner from the top of the cliff. 登山者从悬崖顶上用绳子固定他的同伴。|近义词：secure, fasten, stop, cease|反义词:release, continue, start|词性:动词

belayed:/bɪˈleɪd/ | adj. |停止的；固定的；已确认的|Secured with a rope; stopped or ceased.|The belayed climber waited for his partner to ascend.  已被固定的登山者等待他的同伴上来。|近义词：secured, fastened, stopped|反义词:released, continuing|词性:形容词
