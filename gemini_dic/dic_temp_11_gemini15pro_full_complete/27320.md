calypso:/kəˈlɪpsoʊ/ (US), /kəˈlɪpsəʊ/ (UK)| n. |卡里普索歌曲；卡里普索舞|A type of West Indian (Caribbean) music in a syncopated rhythm, typically with improvised lyrics on topical or satirical subjects.  It is also a type of dance performed to this music.|
例句：The band played a lively calypso that got everyone dancing. 乐队演奏了一首欢快的卡里普索歌曲，让每个人都跳起舞来。

例句：She learned to dance the calypso during her trip to Trinidad. 在去特立尼达的旅行中，她学会了跳卡里普索舞。

近义词：reggae, soca, mento|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
