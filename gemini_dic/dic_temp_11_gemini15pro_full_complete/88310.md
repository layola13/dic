hipper:/ˈhɪpər/| adj.|更时髦的，更懂行的|More fashionable or aware of the latest trends, especially in music, fashion, and culture.  Derived from "hip" with the comparative suffix "-er".|She's much hipper than her older sister. 她比她姐姐更时髦。|Synonyms: trendier, cooler, more stylish|Antonyms: unfashionable, outdated, square|adjective

hip:/hɪp/| adj. n.|时髦的，熟悉内情的；臀部|Fashionable, aware of the latest trends; the area around the top of the leg.|He's a really hip guy. 他是个很时髦的家伙。|Synonyms: trendy, cool, stylish|Antonyms: unfashionable, outdated, square|adjective, noun
