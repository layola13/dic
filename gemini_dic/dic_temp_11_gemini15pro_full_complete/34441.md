clavated:/ˈkleɪveɪtɪd/| adj.|棒状的，棍棒形的|Shaped like a club; thicker at the apex than at the base.|The fungus had a distinctive clavated fruiting body.  这种真菌有一个独特的棒状子实体。|The plant's clavated stems were covered in tiny hairs. 这种植物的棒状茎上覆盖着细小的绒毛。|近义词：club-shaped, clubbed|反义词:tapering, pointed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *clavatus*, past participle of *clavare* "to make into a club," from *clava* "club."
