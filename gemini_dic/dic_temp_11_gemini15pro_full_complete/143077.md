outbranching: /ˌaʊtˈbrɑːntʃɪŋ/ | n. | 分支；分叉；衍生 | The process of producing branches or offshoots; something that branches out. | The outbranching of the company into new markets has been successful. 公司向新市场的扩张很成功。 | 近义词: branching, ramification, forking | 反义词: convergence, merging | 词性: 名词

词源:  From out- + branching, from branch + -ing.  Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *branca, of uncertain origin.
