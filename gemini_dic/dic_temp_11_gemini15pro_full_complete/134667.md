nonmanufacture:/ˌnɒnˌmænjuˈfæktʃər/ /ˌnɑːnˌmænjuˈfæktʃər/
noun
非制造；非生产
the absence or cessation of manufacturing or production.
The nonmanufacture of certain goods led to shortages. 某些商品的停产导致了短缺。
近义词: nonproduction
反义词: manufacture, production
词性: noun

nonmanufacture:/ˌnɒnˌmænjuˈfæktʃər/ /ˌnɑːnˌmænjuˈfæktʃər/
verb
不制造；不生产
to not manufacture or produce something.
The company decided to nonmanufacture the product due to low demand. 由于需求低迷，公司决定停止生产该产品。
近义词: discontinue production
反义词: manufacture, produce
词性: verb
