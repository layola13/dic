frankalmoigne:/ˈfræŋkˌælmɔɪn/| n.|  自由捐赠地; 免役地|Land held by a religious house free from secular service.|The abbey held the land in frankalmoigne.|近义词:mortmain|反义词:fee simple|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Anglo-Norman *franc almoigne, from Old French franc almoigne (“free alms”), from franc (“free”) + almoigne (“alms”).
