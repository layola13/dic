mactra:/ˈmæktʃrə/| n. |海螂|A genus of marine bivalve mollusks, commonly known as trough shells or duck clams, belonging to the family Mactridae.  They are characterized by their thin, triangular shells and their ability to burrow rapidly in sand.|The beach was littered with empty mactra shells.|近义词：trough shell, duck clam, clam|反义词: None|词性:noun
词源: From Modern Latin *Mactra*, from Ancient Greek μάκτρα (máctra, “kneading trough”), from μάσσω (mássō, “I knead”).  The name refers to the shape of the shell.
