audible:/ˈɔːdəbl/ (US) /ˈɔːdɪbl/ (UK)| adj.|听得见的，可听见的|Able to be heard.|The music was barely audible over the noise of the traffic. 音乐声在交通噪音中几乎听不见。|例句：Her voice was barely audible above the din. 她的声音在喧闹声中几乎听不见。|近义词：hearable, perceptible, discernible|反义词：inaudible, imperceptible, faint|词性：形容词

audibly:/ˈɔːdəbli/ (US) /ˈɔːdɪbli/ (UK)| adv.|听得见地|In a way that can be heard.|She sighed audibly. 她清晰地叹了口气。|例句：He groaned audibly when he saw the bill. 当他看到账单时，他发出了一声听得见的呻吟。|近义词：clearly, distinctly, perceptibly|反义词：inaudibility, imperceptibly, faintly|词性：副词
