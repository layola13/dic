bonnier:/ˈbɒniər/| adj.|更好的，更美好的|Comparative form of "bonny" (meaning attractive, beautiful, healthy-looking, especially of children or women).  It describes something more pleasant, agreeable, or favorable than something else being compared.|The weather is bonnier today than it was yesterday. 今天的天气比昨天更好。|Synonyms: better, finer, prettier, more attractive|Antonyms: worse, uglier, less attractive|adjective
