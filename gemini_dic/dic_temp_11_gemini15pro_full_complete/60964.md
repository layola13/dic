selfishness:/ˈsɛlfɪʃnəs/
n.
自私，自私自利
The state or quality of being selfish; exclusive regard to one's own interest or happiness.
Her selfishness made it difficult for her to maintain friendships. 她的自私让她很难维持友谊。
egoism, self-centeredness, greed
altruism, selflessness
noun
