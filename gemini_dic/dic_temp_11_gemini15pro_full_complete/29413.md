catacrotic: /kætəˈkrɒtɪk/
adjective
回降的；降支的
Relating to the descending part of a pulse wave, especially a secondary downward stroke in the descending limb of a sphygmographic tracing.  This secondary downstroke is considered abnormal and may indicate certain cardiovascular conditions.
The catacrotic pulse was a sign of the underlying heart condition.  回降脉搏是潜在心脏病的征兆。
The doctor noted the catacrotic notch on the patient's pulse tracing. 医生注意到病人脉搏描记图上的降支切迹。
近义词:  descending
反义词:  anacrotic
词性: adjective
