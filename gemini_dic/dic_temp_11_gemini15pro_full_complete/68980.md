fatiguability:/fəˌtiːɡəˈbɪləti/| n.|易疲劳性，疲劳度|The state of being easily fatigued; susceptibility to fatigue.  This can be a symptom of various medical conditions.|  Her fatiguability made it difficult for her to hold a full-time job. 她的易疲劳性使她难以从事全职工作。|Synonyms:  tirelessness, weariness, exhaustion|Antonyms:  energy, vigor, resilience|noun

fatigue:/fəˈtiːɡ/| n. v.|疲劳，疲乏；使疲劳|n. Extreme tiredness, exhaustion. v. To cause someone to feel tired or exhausted. |  The long journey fatigued the travelers.  漫长的旅程使旅行者感到疲劳。  She was suffering from fatigue. 她感到疲劳。|Synonyms: tiredness, exhaustion, weariness|Antonyms: energy, vigor, freshness|noun, verb

fatigable:/fəˈtiːɡəbl/ | adj.|易疲劳的|Easily fatigued; susceptible to fatigue. |  He was fatigable and needed frequent rests. 他很容易疲劳，需要经常休息。|Synonyms:  tired, weary, exhausted|Antonyms: energetic, vigorous, tireless|adjective
