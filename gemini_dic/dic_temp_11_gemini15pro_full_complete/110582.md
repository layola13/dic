| adv. |有限地，在有限的程度上|In a limited way; to a limited extent. |His knowledge of the subject is limitedly applicable to real-world problems. 他对这个主题的了解仅在有限的程度上适用于实际问题。| The medication is only limitedly effective.  这种药物的疗效有限。 |近义词：restrictedly, narrowly, slightly, marginally|反义词: extensively, broadly, widely|词性: 副词

limitedly: /ˈlɪmɪtɪdli/
|词源:  From "limited" + "-ly." "Limited" comes from the Latin "limitare," meaning "to bound, to limit," from "limes," meaning "boundary, limit." The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives. |
|中文翻译: 有限地，在有限的程度上|
|英文解析: In a limited way; to a limited extent. Used to describe something that is restricted in scope, degree, or application.|
|例句: The drug is only limitedly effective in treating the disease. 这种药物在治疗这种疾病方面疗效有限。

|例句:  Her experience in this field is limitedly relevant.  她在该领域的经验有限的相关性。|
|近义词: restrictedly, narrowly, slightly, marginally|
|反义词: extensively, broadly, widely|
|词性: adverb (adv.)|
