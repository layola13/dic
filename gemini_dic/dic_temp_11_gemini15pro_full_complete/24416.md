brisker:/ˈbrɪskər/| adj.|更轻快的，更活泼的，更 brisk 的|Comparative form of brisk: more active, lively, or brisk.|The wind grew brisker as the day wore on.  风随着时间的推移越来越大了。|The conversation became brisker after the initial awkwardness.  寒暄过后，谈话变得更加热烈了。|近义词：livelier, quicker, faster, speedier|反义词：slower, calmer, quieter|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *brisk, from Middle French *brisque (“nimble, lively”), from Italian *brisco, possibly of Germanic origin.
