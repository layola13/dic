divertissement:/ˌdiːvɜːrtɪsˈmɑːn/ (US), /ˌdiːvɜːtiːsˈmɒ̃/ (UK)| n.|娱乐, 消遣, 插曲|A divertissement is a short piece of entertainment that is performed during a longer performance, such as a play or opera. It is often light and amusing, and it may not be directly related to the main story.|The ballet provided a delightful divertissement between acts of the opera.| synonym: entertainment, amusement, diversion, recreation | antonym: boredom, monotony | noun
