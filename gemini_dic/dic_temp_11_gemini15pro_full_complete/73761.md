formability:/ˌfɔːməˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可成形性，成形性 | The formability of a metal determines how easily it can be shaped into different forms without cracking or tearing.  金属的可成形性决定了它在不开裂或撕裂的情况下可以多么容易地成形为不同的形状。 |  The sheet metal's formability was excellent, allowing for complex curves.  这种金属薄板的可成形性非常好，可以弯曲成复杂的曲线。 | synonyms: malleability, plasticity, ductility | antonyms: rigidity, stiffness, inflexibility | noun
