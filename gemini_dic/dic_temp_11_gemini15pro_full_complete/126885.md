narc:/nɑːrk/| n. | 告密者，毒品管制警察 | A police officer who specializes in narcotics.  A person who informs on someone to the police or other authority. |例句：The narc busted the dealer. 缉毒警察逮捕了毒贩。

|近义词：informer, snitch, rat |反义词:  None (lack of a direct antonym for a person who informs)|词性: noun


narc:/nɑːrk/| v. | 告发，密报 | To inform on someone to the police or other authority. |例句：He narced on his roommate for dealing drugs. 他向警方告发他的室友贩毒。

|近义词：inform, snitch, rat |反义词:  None (lack of a direct antonym for the act of informing)|词性: verb
