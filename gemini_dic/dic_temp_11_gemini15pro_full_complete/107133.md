ladyishnesses:/ˈleɪdiɪʃnəsɪz/
n.
女子气，女人腔调; 装腔作势

ladyish:/ˈleɪdiɪʃ/
adj.
女子气的; 装腔作势的
Resembling or characteristic of a lady in an excessive or affected way, often used derogatorily.
He found her rather ladyish and overly concerned with appearances. 他觉得她很女人气，过分注重外表。
近义词: effeminate, affected, pretentious, mincing
反义词: masculine, natural, unaffected
词性: 形容词


词源:

From lady + -ish (suffix forming adjectives meaning "somewhat like") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  The word "lady" itself comes from Old English "hlǣfdīge" meaning "loaf-kneader," showing its original connection to domestic roles.
