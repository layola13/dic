foryete:/fərˈjɛtən/ |v. (archaic)|忘记|To lose the remembrance of; cease to remember. |He foryete his promise. 他忘记了他的承诺。|She hath foryete me. 她已经忘记我了。| 近义词: forget, overlook |反义词: remember, recall |词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *forgeten, from Old English forġietan (“to forget”), from Proto-Germanic *fargeitaną (“to forget”), from Proto-Indo-European *gʰed- (“to leave behind”). Cognate with Scots foryet (“to forget”), West Frisian ferjitte (“to forget”), Dutch vergeten (“to forget”), German vergessen (“to forget”), Danish glemme (“to forget”).
