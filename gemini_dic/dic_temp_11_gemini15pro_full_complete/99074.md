interavailability:/ˌɪntərəˌveɪləˈbɪləti/ | n. | 互用性，互操作性 | The interavailability of systems is crucial for seamless data exchange.  The ability of different systems or equipment to be used together or to share information. |  例句：The interavailability of the two networks allowed for seamless communication. 两个网络的互通性实现了无缝通信。

例句：Ensuring interavailability between different software platforms is a key challenge for developers. 确保不同软件平台之间的互操作性是开发人员面临的关键挑战。 | 近义词：interoperability, compatibility, interchangeability | 反义词：incompatibility, isolation | 词性：名词

词源: inter- (between) + availability (the quality of being able to be used or obtained)
