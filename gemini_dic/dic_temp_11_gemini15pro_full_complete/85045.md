harbourer:/ˈhɑːrbərər/ (UK) /ˈhɑːrbərər/ (US)| n.|窝藏者，庇护者|Someone who shelters or hides someone or something, especially someone who is hiding from the authorities.|The police were searching for the harbourer of the escaped convict. 警方正在搜捕逃犯的窝藏者。|近义词：protector, shelterer, concealer|反义词: betrayer, informant|noun
