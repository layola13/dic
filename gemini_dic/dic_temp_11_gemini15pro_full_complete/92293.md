hyphening:/ˈhaɪfənɪŋ/
n.
加连字符，用连字符连接
The act of joining words or parts of words with a hyphen.
Hyphening is often used to create compound adjectives. 用连字符连接常用于构成复合形容词。
Near synonyms: hyphenation, joining, connecting
Antonyms: separation, disconnecting
