dialogged:/ˈdaɪəlɒɡd/ |v.|用对话表达；以对话方式进行|They dialogged about their differences for hours. 他们就彼此的分歧对话了几个小时。|例句：The play dialogged the tensions between tradition and modernity.  这出戏以对话的形式展现了传统与现代之间的紧张关系。

例句：The two sides dialogged for weeks before reaching an agreement.  双方对话了数周才达成协议。|近义词：discussed, conversed, talked|反义词：argued, fought, disputed|词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From "dialogue" + "-ed."  "Dialogue" comes from the Old French "dialoge," which in turn comes from the Latin "dialogus" and ultimately from the Greek "dialogos," meaning "conversation."  The suffix "-ed" forms the past participle of verbs, often indicating a completed action.
