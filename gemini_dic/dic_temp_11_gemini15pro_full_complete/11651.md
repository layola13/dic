armil:/ˈɑːrmɪl/ | n. |  环圈; 臂环; (尤指古代的)手镯; 腕甲 | An armil, or armilla, is a bracelet or armband, especially one worn in ancient times. |  The queen wore a golden armil encrusted with jewels. 女王戴着一个镶嵌珠宝的金色臂环。 | synonyms: bracelet, armband, bangle | antonyms: N/A | noun

armil:/ˈɑːrmɪl/ | n. | 天球仪; (尤指古代的)浑仪 | An armil, also spelled armilla, can also refer to an astronomical instrument, especially an ancient one, consisting of rings representing the principal celestial circles. |  Ptolemy's armil was a complex device used to track the movements of the stars. 托勒密的天球仪是一个用来追踪恒星运动的复杂装置。 | synonyms: astrolabe, sphere, celestial globe | antonyms: N/A | noun
