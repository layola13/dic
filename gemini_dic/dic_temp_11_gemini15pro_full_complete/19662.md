bilateral:/baɪˈlætərəl/| adj.|双边的，两侧的|Involving two sides, parties, or countries.|例句：The two countries signed a bilateral trade agreement. 这两个国家签署了一项双边贸易协定。|例句：Bilateral talks between the two leaders are scheduled for next week.  两位领导人之间的双边会谈定于下周举行。|近义词：mutual, reciprocal, two-sided|反义词：unilateral, multilateral|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bilateralis*, from *bi-* (two) + *lateralis* (of the side, from *latus* (side)).
