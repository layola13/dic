internes:/ɪnˈtɜːnz/ | n. | 实习生（复数）| Interns are students or recent graduates who work temporarily for a company or organization to gain practical experience. |  Example 1: The internes were given a variety of tasks to help them learn about the business. 实习生们被分配了各种各样的任务，以帮助他们了解业务。

Example 2:  The hospital has a program for medical internes. 这家医院有一个针对实习医生的项目。 | Synonyms: trainees, apprentices | Antonyms: professionals, experts | Noun
