absorbs:/əbˈzɔːrbz/ | v. | 吸收;理解;承受;使…全神贯注 | The sponge absorbs water. 海绵吸水。| synonyms: soak up, take in, imbibe, assimilate | antonyms: release, emit, excrete | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *absorbēre, present active infinitive of absorbeō (“I swallow down, suck in, engulf”), from Latin ab (“from, away from”) + sorbeō (“I suck in, swallow, absorb”).
