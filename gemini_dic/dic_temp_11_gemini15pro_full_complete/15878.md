ballistite:/ˈbælɪstaɪt/| n. |巴里斯太火药，无烟火药|A smokeless propellant made from two high explosives, nitrocellulose and nitroglycerin.|Ballistite was one of the first smokeless powders, widely adopted for military use.|近义词：cordite, smokeless powder|反义词:black powder|noun
