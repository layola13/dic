flocky:/ˈflɒki/
adjective
绒毛状的，似羊群的
Covered with or resembling small tufts of wool or flock.
The material was flocky and soft to the touch.  这种材料绒毛状的，摸起来很柔软。
The toy was covered in bright, flocky fur.  这个玩具覆盖着鲜艳的绒毛。
synonyms: fluffy, downy, velvety, fleecy
antonyms: smooth, bald, bare
词性: adjective
