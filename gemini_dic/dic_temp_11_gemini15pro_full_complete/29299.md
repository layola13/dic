castaway:/ˈkæstəweɪ/| n. adj.|被抛弃的人，漂流者；被抛弃的，遭难的|A person who has been shipwrecked and stranded on a deserted island or coast.  Something that has been discarded or abandoned.|例句：The castaway survived for months on coconuts and rainwater.  那个漂流者靠椰子雨水生存了几个月。|近义词：marooned, stranded, shipwrecked, derelict|反义词:rescued, saved|词性:名词, 形容词

castaway:/kæst əˈweɪ/| v.|抛弃，丢弃|To throw away or discard something.|例句：The old toys were castaway in the attic.  旧玩具被丢弃在阁楼里。|近义词：discard, abandon, jettison|反义词:keep, retain|词性:动词
