intermaxilla: /ˌɪntərˈmæksɪlə/ | n. | 前颌骨，间颌骨 |  A small bone, or pair of bones, between the maxillae of most vertebrates, forming part of the upper jaw or supporting the upper beak.  In humans, it represents the portion of the maxilla bearing the incisor teeth. | The intermaxilla plays a crucial role in the development of the face. 前颌骨在面部发育中起着至关重要的作用。| The cleft lip and palate can result from a failure of the intermaxilla to fuse properly. 唇裂和腭裂可能是由于前颌骨未能正确融合造成的。 | synonyms: premaxilla, incisive bone | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific bone) | noun
