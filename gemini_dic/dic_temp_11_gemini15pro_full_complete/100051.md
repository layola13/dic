interpleader:/ˌɪntərˈpliːdər/ | n. |共同诉讼；互诉；介入诉讼|A legal proceeding in which a third party who holds property that is subject to conflicting claims by two or more other parties asks a court to determine the rightful owner. |Example 1: The insurance company filed an interpleader action to determine which of the two claimants was entitled to the proceeds of the life insurance policy.  保险公司提起共同诉讼，以确定两个索赔人中哪一个有权获得人寿保险的收益。

Example 2:  Faced with competing claims for the inherited estate, the executor initiated interpleader proceedings. 面对遗产的竞争性索赔，遗嘱执行人启动了共同诉讼程序。|Synonyms: intervention, interposition |Antonyms: N/A (Interpleader is a legal procedure and doesn't have a direct opposite.)|noun
