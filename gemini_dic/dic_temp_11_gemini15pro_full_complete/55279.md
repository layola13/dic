dissite: /dɪˈsaɪt/
n.
碎屑岩
Clastic rock, especially a rock formed from the mechanical disintegration of other rocks.
Dissite is a type of sedimentary rock formed from the fragments of pre-existing rocks.  碎屑岩是一种由先前存在的岩石碎片形成的沉积岩。
The geologist identified the sample as dissite. 地质学家将样本鉴定为碎屑岩。
近义词: clastic rock, sedimentary rock
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider igneous or metamorphic rock)
