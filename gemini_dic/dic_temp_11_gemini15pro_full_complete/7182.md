angiocarpous: /ˌændʒiəˈkɑːrpəs/ | adj. | 被果皮包裹的 | Having the fruit enclosed within a covering formed from the receptacle or floral envelope.  | Example: The fig is an example of an angiocarpous fruit. 无花果是被果皮包裹的果实的一个例子。

Example:  Angiocarpous fungi develop their spores within an enclosed structure. 被果皮包裹的真菌在封闭结构内发育孢子。 | 近义词: indehiscent | 反义词: gymnocarpous | 词性: adjective
