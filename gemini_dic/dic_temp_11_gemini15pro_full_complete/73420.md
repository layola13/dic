forelegs:/ˈfɔːrleɡz/
noun
前腿
The front legs of an animal, especially a four-legged mammal.
The dog stood on its hind legs, its forelegs waving in the air. 狗用后腿站立，前腿在空中挥舞。
front legs, forelimbs
