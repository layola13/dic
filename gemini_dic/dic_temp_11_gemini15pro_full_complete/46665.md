damourite:/ˈdæməˌraɪt/| n. |绢云母|A fine-grained, compact variety of muscovite, usually occurring as a scaly or massive aggregate.  It is often found in metamorphic rocks and is sometimes referred to as hydromuscovite due to its higher water content.|The damourite in the rock sample gave it a silky sheen.|近义词：muscovite, sericite|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *damour, named after Damour, the locality in the French Pyrenees where it was first described. Ultimately from a personal name.
