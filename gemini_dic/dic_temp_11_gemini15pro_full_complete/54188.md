disestablishes:/ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃɪz/
|v.| 废除，取消，使失去地位 |To deprive (a church, for example) of its status as an officially established institution.  To end the established status of (something) : to cause (something) to no longer have official status. |The government disestablished the church in 1972. 政府在1972年取消了教会的官方地位。|Synonyms: abolish, annul, invalidate, revoke|Antonyms: establish, found, institute|Verb
