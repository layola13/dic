dependence:/dɪˈpɛndəns/ | n. | 依赖，依靠；瘾 | reliance on someone or something else for support, help, etc.; the state of being addicted to something |  He has a dependence on alcohol. 他酗酒。| synonyms: reliance, addiction, habit | antonyms: independence, self-reliance | noun

dependence:/dɪˈpɛndəns/ | n. | 依赖性；附属性 | the state of being controlled or determined by something else | The economy's dependence on oil imports is a concern. 经济对石油进口的依赖令人担忧。| synonyms:  reliance, subjection, subordination | antonyms: autonomy, freedom | noun
