bevy:/ˈbevi/
noun
一群；一批
a large group of people or things of a particular kind
例句：A bevy of beauties surrounded the rock star. 一群美女围绕着摇滚明星。

例句：He was surrounded by a bevy of advisors. 他被一群顾问包围着。

近义词：group, flock, collection, assembly, gathering, company
反义词：few, handful

词性:n.
