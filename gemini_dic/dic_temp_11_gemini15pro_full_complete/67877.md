fabricators:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪtərz/
n.
制造者，装配工，捏造者
a person or company that makes something, especially using machinery
The fabricators delivered the steel beams for the new bridge.  这些制造商交付了新桥的钢梁。

The fabricators were skilled in welding and metalwork.  这些装配工精通焊接和金属加工。

He accused the newspaper of fabricating the story. 他指责这家报纸捏造了这个故事。

manufacturer, producer, constructor, maker, inventor, assembler
liar, falsifier, perjurer

noun
