biasedly: /ˈbaɪəsɪdli/
| adv. | 有偏见地；偏颇地 | In a way that shows bias or prejudice; unfairly. | The referee judged the game biasedly, favoring the home team. 裁判偏袒主队，不公平地判罚比赛。|
The article was written biasedly, presenting only one side of the story. 这篇文章写得有偏见，只呈现了故事的一面。|
近义词: unfairly, prejudicially, partially | 反义词: fairly, impartially, objectively | 词性: 副词
