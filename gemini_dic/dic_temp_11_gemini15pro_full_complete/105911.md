kiva:/ˈkiːvə/ (US), /ˈkiːvɑː/ (UK)| n.|基瓦贷款，一种小额贷款|Kiva is a non-profit organization that allows people to lend money via the Internet to low-income entrepreneurs and students in over 80 countries.|I made a small loan through Kiva to a woman starting a bakery in Peru.|近义词：microloan, peer-to-peer lending|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
