nonstick: /ˌnɑːnˈstɪk/ | adj. |不粘的，防粘的 | Covered with a special coating that prevents food from sticking. |  I always use a nonstick pan for cooking eggs. 我总是用不粘锅煎鸡蛋。

The nonstick surface makes cleaning up a breeze. 不粘表面使清洁变得轻而易举。


近义词: nonsticky, non-stick | 反义词: sticky | 词性: adjective
