hyperfemininity:/ˌhaɪpərfɛmɪˈnɪnɪti/ | n. | 超女性气质，极端女性化 |  An exaggerated adherence to stereotypically feminine gender roles, often involving a focus on physical appearance, submissiveness, and nurturing behavior.  It emphasizes traits traditionally associated with women, often to an excessive degree. |  Her hyperfemininity, characterized by frilly dresses and a delicate demeanor, seemed out of place in the boardroom. 她极端的女性化，穿着褶边连衣裙，举止娇弱，在董事会议室里显得格格不入。|  The advertisement played on notions of hyperfemininity, portraying women solely as domestic goddesses. 这则广告利用了极端女性化的概念，将女性描绘成纯粹的家庭女神。 | synonyms: ultra-femininity, exaggerated femininity | antonyms: masculinity, androgyny | noun
