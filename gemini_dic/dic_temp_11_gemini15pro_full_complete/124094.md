morseled:/ˈmɔːrsəld/ | v. | 切碎；分成小块 | To divide into small pieces; to serve in small portions. | She morseled the bread for the birds. 她把面包掰碎喂鸟。 | The chef morseled the vegetables for the garnish.  厨师把蔬菜切碎用作装饰。| 近义词: fragment, crumble, dice, chop | 反义词: combine, join, unite | 词性: 动词

词源:  From the noun "morsel" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Morsel" comes from Old French "morcel" (small piece, bit), diminutive of "mors" (bite), from Latin "morsus" (a bite), past participle of "mordere" (to bite).
