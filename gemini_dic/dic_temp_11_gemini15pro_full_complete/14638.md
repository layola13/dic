avoirdupois:/ˌævərdəˈpɔɪz/
|n.| 常衡制; 常衡 |A system of weights based on a pound of 16 ounces, used for measuring most goods other than precious metals, gems, and medicines. |例句：The grocer sells his produce by avoirdupois weight.  杂货商按常衡出售他的农产品。

例句：Most goods are sold by avoirdupois weight rather than troy weight. 大多数商品按常衡而不是金衡出售。|近义词：weight, system of weights|反义词: troy (weight)|词性: noun
