natty:/ˈnæti/| adj.|整洁的，时髦的|Stylish and neat in dress or appearance; dapper. |He looked very natty in his new suit. 他穿着新西装看起来很时髦。|近义词：smart, stylish, dapper, chic, fashionable|反义词：scruffy, unkempt, sloppy, messy|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Early 18th century (in the sense ‘nimble, dexterous’): perhaps a variant of neat. The current sense dates from the mid 19th century.
