lighte:/ˈlaɪtə/|v.|点燃，照亮 (古英语，已过时)|The knight lighte the torch. 骑士点燃了火把。|近义词：ignite, kindle, illuminate|反义词:extinguish, darken|动词

词源: From Middle English *lighten, from Old English *līhtan, from Proto-Germanic *liuhtjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *lewk- (“light”). Cognate with Scots licht (“to light”), West Frisian ljochtsje (“to light, to shine”), Dutch lichten (“to lift, to raise, to lighten”), German leuchten (“to shine, to gleam”), Danish lyse (“to shine”), Swedish lysa (“to shine”). Related to light (n.).
