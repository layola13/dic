oversleep: /ˌoʊvərˈsliːp/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈsliːp/ (UK)
verb
睡过头，睡得太久
To sleep longer than intended.
I overslept this morning and missed my bus. 我今天早上睡过头了，错过了我的公交车。
He overslept and was late for work. 他睡过头了，上班迟到了。
近义词：sleep in, sleep late
反义词：wake up early
词性：verb
