例句：I saw a bodyworker to help with my back pain. 我去看了一位身体工作者来治疗我的背痛。|近义词：massage therapist, physical therapist, healer, practitioner|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) office worker, sedentary worker|词性:noun
