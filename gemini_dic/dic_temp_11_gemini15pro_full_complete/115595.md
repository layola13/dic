marbled:/ˈmɑːr.bəld/ /ˈmɑːr.bl̩d/ | adj. | 大理石纹的，斑驳的 | Having streaks or patterns resembling marble. |  The baker created a marbled cake with swirls of vanilla and chocolate. 面包师制作了一个带有香草和巧克力漩涡的大理石纹蛋糕。

The countertop was made of a beautiful marbled stone.  台面是由一块漂亮的大理石纹石头制成的。

| 近义词：mottled, streaked, veined | 反义词：plain, solid, uniform | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From marble + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin *marmor.
