cagily:/ˈkeɪdʒɪli/| adv.|谨慎地，狡猾地|In a cautious and secretive manner, often with the intention to deceive or manipulate.|He cagily avoided answering the question directly. 他小心翼翼地避免直接回答这个问题。|近义词：slyly, craftily, cunningly, surreptitiously, stealthily|反义词：openly, frankly, honestly|词性:副词

词源:
From "cagy" + "-ly". "Cagy" is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "cage," suggesting a guarded or restrained quality.
