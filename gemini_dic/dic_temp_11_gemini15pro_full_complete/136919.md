Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
soluble (adjective):  From Late Latin solubilis, from Latin solvere "to loosen, dissolve,"  related to solutus "loosened, dissolved."
-ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
