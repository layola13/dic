autumnal:/ɔːˈtʌmnəl/ | adj. | 秋天的；秋季的；像秋天的 | Of or characteristic of autumn; produced or gathered in autumn. | The autumnal leaves fell gently to the ground. 秋天的树叶轻轻地落在地上。| synonyms: fall, autumn, harvest | antonyms: spring, summer, vernal | adjective

autumnal:/ɔːˈtʌmnəl/ | adj. | 秋季的；成熟的；衰老的 |  Relating to or suggestive of the season of autumn, especially with reference to the colours and atmosphere associated with it; mature; declining. |  He felt an autumnal melancholy. 他感到一种秋季的忧郁。 | synonyms:  mature, declining, aging | antonyms: youthful, young, burgeoning | adjective
