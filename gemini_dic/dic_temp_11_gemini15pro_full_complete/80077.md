goar:/ɡɔːr/ | n. | 凝块，乳皮 | A thick film or clot, especially on the surface of a liquid; a curdled substance.  This word is primarily used in dialect, particularly in Scotland and Ireland. | The goar on the top of the milk was thick. 牛奶上面的乳皮很厚。 | 近义词: film, scum, crust, curd | 反义词: liquid | 词性: 名词
