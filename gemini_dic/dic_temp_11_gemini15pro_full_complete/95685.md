inconcoct:/ˌɪnkənˈkɒkt/ |v.| 编造，捏造，虚构 |To devise, invent, or fabricate something, especially a story or plan, often with deceitful intent. |He concocted a story about being robbed. 他编造了一个关于被抢劫的故事。|She concocted a plan to get her revenge. 她策划了一个复仇计划。|近义词: fabricate, invent, devise, contrive, make up|反义词: discover, find, uncover|词性:动词
