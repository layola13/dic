nonexcitableness:/ˌnɑːnɪkˌsaɪtəbəlnəs/
noun
无兴奋性；不易兴奋；不兴奋
The state or quality of being nonexcitable; inability to be stimulated or excited.
The nonexcitableness of the nerve cells after the prolonged stimulation was concerning.  长时间刺激后神经细胞的无兴奋性令人担忧。
The drug induced a state of nonexcitableness in the patient.  这种药物使病人处于一种无兴奋状态。

近义词:inertness, inactivity, impassivity, apathy
反义词:excitability, responsiveness, sensitivity

词性:noun
词源:non- (not) + excitable + -ness (state of being)
