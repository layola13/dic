interannually:/ˌɪntərˈænjuəli/
adv.
逐年地，每年地
between years; from one year to the next.
The company reviews its performance interannually to track long-term growth.  该公司每年都会回顾其业绩以追踪长期增长。
The data is collected and analyzed interannually.  数据是逐年收集和分析的。
近义词: yearly, annually
反义词: N/A
词性: 副词
