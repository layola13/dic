nonprovident:/nɒnˈprɒvɪdənt/ | adj. | 无远见的；挥霍的；不节俭的 | Lacking foresight; not providing for the future; wasteful; improvident. | He was nonprovident with his inheritance and quickly spent it all. 他挥霍了他的遗产，很快就花光了所有的钱。|  He made a nonprovident decision to invest in a failing business. 他做了一个不明智的决定，投资于一家即将倒闭的企业。 | 近义词: improvident, wasteful, spendthrift, prodigal | 反义词: provident, thrifty, frugal, economical | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *non providens*, from non "not" + providens "foreseeing, providing," present participle of providēre "to foresee, provide."
