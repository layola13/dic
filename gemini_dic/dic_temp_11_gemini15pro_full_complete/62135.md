encircles:/ɪnˈsɜːrk(ə)lz/
verb
包围，环绕
to surround or form a circle around something.
The enemy troops encircled the city. 敌军包围了这座城市。

The fence encircles the entire property. 篱笆环绕着整个房产。

surround, enclose, encompass
free, release
