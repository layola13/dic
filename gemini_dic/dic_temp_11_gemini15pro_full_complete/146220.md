overexplaining:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspleɪnɪŋ/
|v.|过度解释；解释得过于详细|The act of explaining something too thoroughly, often to the point of being tedious or condescending.|She kept overexplaining the joke, and it stopped being funny. 她一直过度解释那个笑话，结果一点也不好笑了。|Synonyms: elaborating excessively, belaboring, dwelling on|Antonyms: summarizing, simplifying|Verb
