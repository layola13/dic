disintegrative:/dɪsˈɪntɪˌɡreɪtɪv/
adjective
分裂的，瓦解的
tending to cause disintegration; causing something to break up into small parts or components.
The disintegrative forces within the empire eventually led to its collapse.  帝国内部的分裂力量最终导致了它的崩溃。
The report highlighted the disintegrative effects of long-term unemployment on families. 报告强调了长期失业对家庭的瓦解性影响。

近义词: disruptive, destructive, divisive
反义词: unifying, cohesive, constructive
词性: adjective


词源:
From disintegrate + -ive.  Disintegrate comes from dis- (expressing negation or reversal) + integrate (from Latin integrāre, meaning "to make whole").
