nondeducible:/ˌnɒndɪˈdjuːsɪbl/ | /ˌnɑːndɪˈduːsəbl/
adjective | 不可扣除的；不能推断的
Not able to be deducted, especially from taxable income; not able to be inferred. |  This expense is nondeducible from your income tax. 这项费用不能从你的所得税中扣除。 |
例句：The contribution to the political party was deemed nondeducible.  对政党的捐款被认为是不可扣除的。

例句：The conclusion was nondeducible from the evidence presented. 从提出的证据来看，这个结论是无法推断出来的。


近义词：non-deductible, unexpendable, noninferable, noninferrable
反义词：deductible, inferable, inferrable
词性: adjective
