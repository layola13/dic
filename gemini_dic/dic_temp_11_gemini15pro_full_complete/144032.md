outrhymes:/ˌaʊtˈraɪmz/
verb
以更完美的韵律胜过；压倒韵律
to surpass in rhyming; to outdo in the art or practice of rhyming.
Shakespeare outrhymed all his contemporaries.  莎士比亚的韵律超过了他所有的同时代人。
Synonyms: surpass, excel, outdo, outstrip, transcend
Antonyms:  lose to, be inferior to
词性: 动词
