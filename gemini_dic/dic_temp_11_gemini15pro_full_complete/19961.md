bioengineer:/ˈbaɪoʊˌɛndʒɪˈnɪr/| n.  |生物工程师|A person who combines biological science with engineering principles to design and create systems, devices, and products for a variety of applications, including healthcare, agriculture, and environmental remediation.| Bioengineers are working on developing new artificial organs. 生物工程师正在致力于开发新型人造器官。|Synonyms: biomedical engineer, genetic engineer|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

bioengineer:/ˌbaɪoʊˌɛndʒɪˈnɪr/| vt.  |进行生物工程|To design or create using the principles of bioengineering.|They bioengineered a new type of bacteria to clean up oil spills. 他们利用生物工程技术设计了一种新型细菌来清理石油泄漏。|Synonyms: genetically modify, bio-engineer|Antonyms: N/A|Verb
