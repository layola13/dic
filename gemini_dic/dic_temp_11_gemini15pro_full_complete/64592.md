erudite:/ˈɛrʊdaɪt/| adj.|博学的，有学问的|Having or showing great knowledge or learning.|例句：The professor was known for his erudite lectures.  这位教授以其博学的讲座而闻名。

例句：She impressed everyone with her erudite conversation. 她用渊博的学识给每个人留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：learned, scholarly, knowledgeable, well-read, educated|反义词：ignorant, uneducated, unlearned, illiterate|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *ērudītus*, past participle of *ērudīre* (“to educate, train, instruct”), from *ex* (“out”) + *rudis* (“rough, untrained”).
