breathinesses: /ˈbrɛθinəsɪz/
noun
气喘吁吁；呼吸声的沙哑
the audible escape of breath between vocal cords, or the quality of a voice that results from this.
Her voice had a slight breathiness that added to her mystique. 她的声音略带沙哑，更增添了她的神秘感。
synonyms: huskiness, raspiness, whisper, murmur
antonyms: clarity, resonance, fullness
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From breathy + -ness.  Breathy comes from breath + -y.  Breath from Old English bræth.
