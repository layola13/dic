corporateness:/ˈkɔːrpərətnəs/ /ˈkɔːrpərɪtnəs/
n.
公司性质;法人资格;集体性
The quality of being a corporation; the state of being incorporate.  The quality of being united or combined into one body.
The corporateness of the organization ensured its legal standing.  这个组织的公司性质确保了它的法律地位。
The corporateness of the team was evident in their synchronized performance.  团队的集体性在他们同步的表演中显而易见。
近义词: collectivity, unity, incorporation
反义词: individuality, division, separation
词性: noun
