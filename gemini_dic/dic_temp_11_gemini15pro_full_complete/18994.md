bestowers:/bɪˈstoʊərz/
n.
授予者；给予者
Those who confer a gift, honor, or right.
The bestowers of the Nobel Prize are highly respected. 诺贝尔奖的授予者备受尊敬。

The queen was the bestower of honors at the ceremony. 女王是典礼上的荣誉授予者。
donors, givers, grantors
recipients, takers

词性: 名词 (Noun)
