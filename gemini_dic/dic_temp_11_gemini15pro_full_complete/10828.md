aquitanian:/ˌækwɪˈteɪniən/ | adj. n.|阿基坦的；阿基坦人|Relating to or characteristic of Aquitaine, a historical region in southwestern France.  A native or inhabitant of Aquitaine.|例句：The Aquitanian wines are renowned for their quality. 阿基坦葡萄酒以其品质而闻名。

|例句：He is an Aquitanian by birth. 他出生于阿基坦。|近义词：Gascon (sometimes), Southwestern French|反义词: None (too specific)|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Latin *Aquitanicus*, from Aquitania (the name of the region), ultimately of unknown origin, possibly from Basque or a pre-Indo-European language.
