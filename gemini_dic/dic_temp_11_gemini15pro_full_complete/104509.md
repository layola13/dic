kaleidoscopical:/kəˌlaɪdəˈskɒpɪkəl/
adjective
万花筒般的；千变万化的；瞬息万变的
Constantly changing or varying, like the patterns in a kaleidoscope.
The city offers a kaleidoscopical array of cultural experiences.  这座城市提供了千变万化的文化体验。
Her mood was kaleidoscopical, shifting from joy to sadness in an instant. 她的情绪变化莫测，瞬间从喜悦转为悲伤。

近义词:ever-changing, multifaceted, varied, diverse, protean
反义词:static, unchanging, monotonous
词性:adjective

词源:
From kaleidoscope + -ical.  Kaleidoscope comes from Greek kalos "beautiful" + eidos "form" + skopein "to view."
