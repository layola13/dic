hawking:/ˈhɔːkɪŋ/| n.|叫卖；兜售|The act of selling goods by calling out to passersby in the street.|He makes a living by hawking souvenirs to tourists. 他靠向游客兜售纪念品为生。|近义词：peddling, vending|反义词:buying, purchasing|词性:noun
hawking:/ˈhɔːkɪŋ/| adj.|叫卖的；兜售的|Relating to or engaged in the act of hawking.|The hawking of goods is a common sight in many cities. 在许多城市，兜售商品是很常见的景象。|近义词：peddling, vending|反义词:buying, purchasing|词性:adjective
