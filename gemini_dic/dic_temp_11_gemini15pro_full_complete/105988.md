knackish: /ˈnækɪʃ/ | adj. | 巧妙的，灵巧的，有诀窍的 | Clever and skillful, especially in a tricky way; having a knack. | He has a knackish way of solving puzzles. 他有一种巧妙的解谜方法。 |  The old carpenter had a knackish approach to woodworking, effortlessly creating intricate designs.  老木匠有一种巧妙的木工方法，毫不费力地创造出复杂的图案。 | 近义词: adept, skillful, dexterous, ingenious, clever, artful | 反义词: clumsy, awkward, inept, unskilled | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From knack + -ish.  "Knack" likely comes from  Middle Scots *knax, probably of imitative origin.
