holohemihedral:/ˌhɒləʊˌhɛmiˈhiːdrəl/ |adj.|全半面象的|Crystallizing in a form exhibiting only half the planes or faces required by the full symmetry of the system.|Examples of holohemihedral crystals are rare in nature.  全半面象晶体在自然界中很少见。|The mineral pyrite sometimes exhibits holohemihedral forms. 黄铁矿有时呈现全半面象的形式。|Near synonyms: hemihedral, merohedral|Antonym: holohedral|Adjective

Etymology->
From *holo-* (whole) + *hemi-* (half) + *-hedral* (relating to the faces of a crystal)
