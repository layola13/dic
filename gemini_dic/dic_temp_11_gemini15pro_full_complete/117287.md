mechanurgy:/məˈkænɜːrdʒi/ | n. |机械法术，机械魔法|The study or practice of constructing and using mechanical devices, often in a fantastical or magical context, to achieve supernatural effects.  It combines elements of mechanics, magic, and sometimes alchemy.|Example 1:  The dwarves of the mountain were renowned for their mechanurgy, crafting intricate clockwork automatons. 山里的矮人以其机械法术而闻名，他们能制造复杂的钟表自动机。

Example 2: She studied ancient texts on mechanurgy, hoping to unlock the secrets of animating inanimate objects. 她研究古代关于机械法术的文献，希望能解开使无生命物体动起来的秘密。|Synonym:  magitek, technomagic, clockwork magic |Antonym:  natural magic, sorcery |Noun
