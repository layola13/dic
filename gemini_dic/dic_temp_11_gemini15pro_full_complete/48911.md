defriend:/diːˈfrɛnd/
verb
取消好友；和…绝交
To remove someone as a friend, especially on a social networking website.
I defriended him on Facebook after he posted those offensive comments. 我在他发布那些攻击性评论后，在脸书上取消了他的好友关系。
近义词: unfriend, block
反义词: befriend, add (as a friend)
词性: verb

defriend:/diːˈfrɛnd/
词源:  de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + friend (noun)  The prefix "de-" is attached to the noun "friend" to create a verb meaning to reverse the action of befriending. This formation is relatively recent, coinciding with the rise of social media.
中文翻译: 取消好友；和…绝交
英文解析: To remove someone from a list of friends or contacts, especially on a social networking website.  It implies a deliberate act of severing a social connection.
例句:
She defriended him after their argument.  她和他们争吵后取消了他的好友关系。

He defriended several people who spread misinformation online.  他在网上取消了几个散播虚假信息的人的好友关系。


近义词: unfriend, block, delete
反义词: befriend, add, follow

词性: verb
