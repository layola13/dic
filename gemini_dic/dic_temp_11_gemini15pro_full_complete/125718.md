municipals:/mjuːˈnɪsɪpəlz/
| n. |市政债券|Debt securities issued by state and local governments, generally to finance public projects such as roads, schools, and sewers.  These bonds are often tax-exempt.|Investors are often drawn to municipals because of their tax advantages.  投资者经常被市政债券的税收优势所吸引。|municipal bonds, munis| corporates, government bonds
|noun
