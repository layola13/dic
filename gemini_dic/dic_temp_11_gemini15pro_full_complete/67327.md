extemporization:/ɛkˌstɛmpəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ɪkˌstɛmpərɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
即兴创作；即席演奏；即兴表演
The act of composing, performing, or producing something without preparation; improvisation.
He gave a brilliant extemporization on the current political situation. 他就当前的政治局势发表了一场精彩的即兴演讲。
Synonyms: improvisation, ad-libbing, off-the-cuff performance
Antonyms: preparation, rehearsal, premeditation
noun


extemporize:/ɛkˈstɛmpəraɪz/ /ɪkˈstɛmpəraɪz/
v.
即兴创作；即席演奏；即兴表演
To compose, perform, or produce something without preparation; improvise.
She had to extemporize a speech when the scheduled speaker failed to appear.  当预定的发言人没有出现时，她不得不即兴发表演讲。
Synonyms: improvise, ad-lib, wing it
Antonyms: prepare, rehearse, plan
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin extemporizatio, from Latin extemporizare "to do something offhand," from ex tempore "at the moment," from ex "out of" + tempore "time."
