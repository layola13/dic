cenotaph: /ˈsenəˌtæf/ /ˈsenəˌtɑːf/ | n. |纪念碑，衣冠冢|A monument erected to the memory of a person or persons buried elsewhere.|The town erected a cenotaph in memory of the soldiers lost at war. 该镇为在战争中牺牲的士兵建造了一座纪念碑。|
近义词: monument, memorial, shrine |
反义词: grave, tomb |
词性: noun


Etymology:
From French cénotaphe, from Ancient Greek κενοτάφιον (kenotáphion), from κενός (kenós, “empty”) + τάφος (táphos, “tomb”). Literally "empty tomb".
