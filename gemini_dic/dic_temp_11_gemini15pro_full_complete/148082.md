overscented: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛntɪd/ | adj. | 香味过浓的；香味太重的 | Having too much scent; excessively perfumed. | The room was overscented with cheap air freshener. 房间里充满了廉价空气清新剂的浓烈气味。 |  The overscented lobby gave me a headache.  香味过浓的大厅让我头疼。 | 近义词: overpowering, cloying | 反义词: unscented, subtle | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "scent" (noun meaning "a distinctive smell") + "-ed" (suffix forming an adjective)
