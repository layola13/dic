beslabber:/bɪˈslæbər/
verb
涂抹口水；弄得满是口水
To cover with saliva or slobber.
The baby beslabbered his bib.  婴儿把口水弄得围嘴上都是。
The dog beslabbered the visitor with enthusiastic greetings.  狗热情地问候着来访者，弄得他满身口水。
