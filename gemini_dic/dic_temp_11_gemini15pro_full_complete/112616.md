low-class:/ˈloʊ klæs/ (US) /ˈləʊ klɑːs/ (UK)| adj.|低级的，下等的，粗俗的|Of a low social class; vulgar or inferior.|He made some low-class jokes that offended everyone. 他讲了一些低级笑话，冒犯了所有人。|Synonyms: inferior, cheap, vulgar, common, plebeian|Antonyms: high-class, upper-class, refined, sophisticated|adjective

low-class:/ˈloʊ klæs/ (US) /ˈləʊ klɑːs/ (UK)| n.|下层阶级|The lower social class; the proletariat.|She grew up in a low-class neighborhood. 她在一个下层阶级的社区长大。|Synonyms: proletariat, working class, underclass|Antonyms: upper class, aristocracy, elite|noun
