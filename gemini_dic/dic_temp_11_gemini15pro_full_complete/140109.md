off-piste: /ˌɒfˈpiːst/ (UK), /ˌɔːfˈpiːst/ (US) | adj. adv.| 野雪的，非雪道的；在非雪道上滑雪 | Skiing or snowboarding on areas of a mountain that are not marked out for safe skiing. |  Example: He enjoys the challenge of off-piste skiing. 他喜欢野雪滑雪的挑战。

off-piste: /ˌɒfˈpiːst/ (UK), /ˌɔːfˈpiːst/ (US) | adj. adv.| 野雪的，非雪道的；在非雪道上滑雪 | Unconventional, unorthodox. | Example:  Her off-piste approach to business has been remarkably successful.  她非传统的商业方法取得了显著的成功。


off-piste: /ˌɒfˈpiːst/ (UK), /ˌɔːfˈpiːst/ (US) | n.| 野雪区 | The area outside of marked ski runs. | Example: The off-piste was covered in fresh powder. 野雪区覆盖着新鲜的粉雪。


近义词: backcountry, ungroomed, unmarked

反义词: on-piste, groomed, marked

词性: adjective, adverb, noun


词源:  Borrowed from French "hors piste," literally "outside the track."  "Hors" means "outside" and "piste" means "track" or "trail."
