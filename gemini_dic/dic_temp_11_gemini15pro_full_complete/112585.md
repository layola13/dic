Lovesick puppy:  A lovesick puppy describes someone who is excessively lovesick, often to a comical degree. 指那些过度相思病的人，通常带有一种滑稽的意味。
Hopelessly lovesick: This phrase emphasizes the intensity of the lovesickness.  强调相思病的强烈程度。
Lovesick fool: This phrase is sometimes used to describe someone whose lovesickness is seen as foolish or naive.  有时用来形容那些相思病被认为是愚蠢或天真的人。
Lovesick blues: This refers to the feeling of sadness and melancholy associated with being lovesick. 指与相思病相关的悲伤和忧郁的感觉。
Cured of lovesickness: This refers to the state of no longer being lovesick. 指不再相思病的状态。
