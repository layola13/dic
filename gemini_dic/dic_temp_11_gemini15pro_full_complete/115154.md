manhead:/ˈmænˌhɛd/ | n. | 人头，顽固的人，鱼漂 |A float for fishing, shaped like a person's head. Also, can refer to a stubborn or obstinate person (informal, often derogatory). |  The old fisherman used a manhead as a float. 老渔夫用一个人头形状的鱼漂。|  He's such a manhead; he never listens to anyone. 他真是个顽固的人，从来不听别人的。 | synonyms: float, stubborn person, hardhead | antonyms: follower, agreeable person | noun
