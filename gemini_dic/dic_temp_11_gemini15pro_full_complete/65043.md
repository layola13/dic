etched:/ɛtʃt/
adjective
蚀刻的；铭记的
permanently fixed in the memory
例句：The image of her face was etched in his mind. 她的脸庞深深地印在他的脑海里。
例句：The landscape is etched with the marks of erosion.  这片地貌被侵蚀的痕迹所刻画。
近义词：engraved, carved, imprinted, fixed
反义词：forgotten, erased
词性：adjective

etched:/ɛtʃt/
verb
蚀刻；铭记
to cut a design or words into a hard surface, especially glass or metal; (of an experience or image) to be permanently fixed in the memory
例句：The glass was etched with a delicate floral pattern. 玻璃上蚀刻着精致的花卉图案。
例句：The events of that day were etched forever in her memory. 那一天的事件永远铭刻在她的记忆中。
近义词：engrave, carve, imprint, fix
反义词：erase, forget
词性：verb
