nondualities:/ˌnɒn.djuːˈæl.ɪ.tiz/ | n. |非二元性；非对偶性| The state or quality of not being composed of two separate or opposing elements.  The recognition of the interconnectedness and unity of all things, often associated with spiritual or philosophical concepts. |  Buddhist philosophy emphasizes the nondualities of self and other. 佛教哲学强调自我和他人的非二元性。

The concept of nondualities challenges our usual way of thinking about opposites. 非二元性的概念挑战了我们通常对对立面的思考方式。| 近义词：oneness, unity, interconnectedness, wholeness | 反义词：duality, separation, dichotomy | 词性: 名词
