intellectualize:/ɪn.təˈlɛk.tʃu.ə.laɪz/ | n. | 理智化；知识化 | The act or process of intellectualizing. |  Her intellectualization of the situation made it difficult for her to connect with others who were grieving. 她对情况的理性化使她难以与其他悲伤的人产生联系。| rationalization, overthinking, analysis | feeling, empathy | noun
