herringboning: /ˈherɪŋˌboʊnɪŋ/
n.
人字形，人字形图案，人字形砌砖法
A pattern or arrangement resembling the bones of a herring, often used in fabric, brickwork, or skiing.
The brick pathway was laid in a herringbone pattern.  这条砖砌的小路采用了人字形图案。
Skiers often use the herringbone technique to climb uphill.  滑雪者经常使用人字形技术爬坡。
Synonyms: zigzag, chevron
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "straight" or "linear" in some contexts)
