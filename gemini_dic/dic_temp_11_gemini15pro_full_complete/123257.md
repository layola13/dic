monodelphic:/ˌmɒnəˈdɛlfɪk/
adjective
单孔的；单子宫的
Having a single uterus, as in most placental mammals.  Relating to or characteristic of Monodelphia (a former subclass of mammals).
Monodelphic mammals nourish their young through a placenta. 单孔类哺乳动物通过胎盘滋养幼崽。
The human reproductive system is monodelphic. 人类的生殖系统是单子宫的。
近义词:  eutherian
反义词:  didelphic (having two uteri)
词性: adjective
