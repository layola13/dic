go's:/ɡoʊz/ | n. | 行为，行动（通常指奇怪或不寻常的行为）; 事情; 职责;  | something that someone does or a situation that someone is responsible for, especially when unusual or strange  |  That's John's go - always interrupting. 那是约翰的作风 - 总是打断别人。|近义词：actions, doings, business, affair | 反义词: inaction | 词性:名词

go's:/ɡoʊz/ |  v. (go的第三人称单数形式) | 去；进行；进展; 离开; |  to move or travel from one place to another | He goes to work by bus. 他乘公共汽车去上班。| 近义词: travel, depart, leave | 反义词: come, arrive, stay | 词性:动词
