ceilidh:/ˈkeɪli/ (UK) /ˈkeɪli/ (US)| n. |凯利舞会；爱尔兰或苏格兰的传统社交聚会|A ceilidh is a traditional Scottish or Irish social gathering. In its most basic form, it simply means a social visit. In contemporary usage, it usually involves playing Gaelic folk music and dancing, either at a house party or a larger concert venue.|We went to a ceilidh last night and had a great time. 我们昨晚去参加了一个凯利舞会，玩得很开心。|The village hall hosted a ceilidh to raise money for the local school. 村礼堂举办了一场凯利舞会，为当地学校筹款。|Synonyms: party, gathering, social, dance|Antonyms: isolation, solitude|Noun
