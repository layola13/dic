kickless:/ˈkɪklɪs/| adj.|无抵抗的，温顺的，没有后坐力的|Lacking resistance or spirit; submissive; (of a firearm) having no recoil.|The kickless rifle was surprisingly easy to control. 这把无后坐力步枪出奇地容易控制。|近义词:docile, submissive, unresisting, recoil-less|反义词:resistant, rebellious, recoiling|词性:形容词

词源: "kick" (踢) + "-less" (无...的)  The suffix "-less" forms adjectives meaning "without" or "lacking" the quality expressed by the base word.  Therefore, "kickless" literally means "without a kick."  In the context of firearms, this refers to the absence of recoil.  In a more general sense, it describes something lacking resistance or spirit.
