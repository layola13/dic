conquers:/ˈkɒŋkəz/ (UK), /ˈkɑːŋkərz/ (US)| v.|征服，克服，战胜|overcomes and gains control of (a place or people) by military force.  Defeats or overcomes (something).|Alexander the Great conquered many lands. 亚历山大大帝征服了许多土地。 She conquered her fear of public speaking. 她克服了对公开演讲的恐惧。|近义词：defeats, overcomes, vanquishes, subdues|反义词：loses, surrenders, fails, yields|词性:动词
