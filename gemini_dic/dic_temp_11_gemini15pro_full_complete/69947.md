fesswise:/ˈfɛswaɪz/| adv.| 横着，水平地，正面向前地| In heraldry, a charge placed horizontally, or fesswise, is laid across the shield as a fess. 在纹章学中，横向放置或正面朝前的图案被像横条一样放置在盾牌上。|The lion was depicted fesswise on the shield. 狮子被描绘成横卧在盾牌上。|horizontally, across|vertically|adverb
Etymology->
From fess +‎ -wise.  Fess comes from Old French fes, fais "bundle, burden," perhaps from Latin fascia "band, bandage." The "-wise" suffix means "in the manner of".
