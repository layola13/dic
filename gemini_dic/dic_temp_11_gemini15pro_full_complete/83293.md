gynandrian:/ɡɪˈnændriən/ | adj. n. |雌雄同体的，雌雄同体的人或动物| Having both male and female reproductive organs.  A person or animal having both male and female sex organs.|例句：Gynandrian flowers have both stamens and pistils. 雌雄同体的花朵同时拥有雄蕊和雌蕊。|例句：The gynandrian butterfly exhibited characteristics of both sexes. 这只雌雄同体的蝴蝶展现了两种性别的特征。|近义词：hermaphrodite, androgynous |反义词: unisexual|词性:形容词, 名词
Etymology->
From Greek *gynandrikos*, from *gyne*  "woman" + *aner* (genitive *andros*) "man."
