fear:/fɪr/ (US); /fɪə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.| 恐惧，害怕 | An unpleasant emotion caused by the perception of danger, pain, or harm.|  She has a fear of spiders. 她很怕蜘蛛。| He feared for his life. 他害怕失去生命。 | synonyms: terror, fright, dread, alarm, panic, anxiety, apprehension | antonyms: courage, bravery, boldness, confidence | noun
fear:/fɪr/ (US); /fɪə(r)/ (UK)| v.| 害怕，恐惧 | To be afraid of; to expect or anticipate with anxiety.|  I fear that I may be late. 我担心我会迟到。 | Do you fear death? 你害怕死亡吗? | synonyms: dread, be afraid of, worry about, be anxious about | antonyms:  be brave, be bold, be unafraid | verb
