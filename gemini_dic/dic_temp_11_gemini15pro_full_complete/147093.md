overlies:/ˌoʊvərˈlaɪz/
verb
覆盖；叠加；位于…之上
extends over and covers part of; be located above and partly cover
The granite overlies a bed of sandstone. 花岗岩覆盖在一层砂岩之上。
overlaps, covers, blankets
