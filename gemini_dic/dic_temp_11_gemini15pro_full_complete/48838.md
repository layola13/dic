defoliator:/diːˈfoʊliˌeɪtər/| n.|落叶剂；食叶昆虫|Something that causes leaves to fall off, especially a chemical spray or an insect.|The defoliator stripped the trees bare in a matter of days. 落叶剂在几天内就把树上的叶子剥光了。|近义词：defoliant, leaf stripper|反义词: fertilizer| noun

defoliate:/diːˈfoʊliˌeɪt/| v.|使落叶；除去…的叶子|To cause the leaves of a plant to fall off, especially prematurely.|The caterpillars defoliated the entire tree. 毛毛虫把整棵树的叶子都吃光了。|近义词：strip, denude|反义词:fertilize|verb
