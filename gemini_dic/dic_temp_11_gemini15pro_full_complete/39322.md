conium:/ˈkəʊniəm/ | n. | 毒芹；芹毒碱 | A highly poisonous herbaceous plant of the parsley family, native to Europe and North Africa. |  Socrates was executed by drinking a potion containing conium. 苏格拉底被赐饮含有毒芹的毒药处死。

Conium is a dangerous plant and should never be ingested. 毒芹是一种危险的植物，绝对不能摄入。 | 近义词: hemlock, poison hemlock | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可以考虑解毒剂 antidote) | 词性: noun
