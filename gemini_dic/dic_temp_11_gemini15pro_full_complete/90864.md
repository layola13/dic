hungrily:/ˈhʌŋɡrɪli/| adv.|饥饿地；渴望地|In a hungry manner; showing a strong desire for something.  It describes the way someone does something, driven by hunger or a strong desire.|The children ate hungrily after their long journey. 孩子们长途旅行后狼吞虎咽地吃了起来。|She hungrily devoured the book, eager to find out the ending. 她如饥似渴地读着这本书，急于知道结局。|eagerly, ravenously, greedily|apathetically, indifferently|adverb
