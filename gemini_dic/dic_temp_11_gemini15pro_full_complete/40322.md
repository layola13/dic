contourne:/kɒnˈtʊəneɪ/ /kɑːnˈtʊərn/ | v. | 绕行，迂回 | To bypass, go around, especially a difficulty or obstacle. | The hikers contoured the flooded area by following a higher trail.  徒步旅行者沿着更高的路径绕过了洪水区。|Synonyms: bypass, circumvent, skirt | Antonyms: confront, face, meet | Word Type: Verb
