disbursers:/dɪsˈbɜːrsərz/
noun
支付员，付款人，财务人员
People who distribute funds or payments.
The company's disbursers processed the employee bonuses last week.  公司财务人员上周处理了员工奖金。
Synonyms: payers, distributors, allocators
Antonyms: recipients, collectors
n.
