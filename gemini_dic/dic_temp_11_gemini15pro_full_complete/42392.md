countercampaign:/ˈkaʊntərˌkæmˈpeɪn/| n.|反宣传，对抗运动|A campaign designed to counteract another campaign.|The company launched a countercampaign to address the negative publicity.  这家公司发起了一场反宣传活动，以应对负面宣传。|Example: The politician's countercampaign effectively neutralized his opponent's attacks. 这位政治家的反宣传活动有效地抵消了对手的攻击。|Synonyms: counteroffensive, counterattack, rebuttal|Antonyms: campaign, promotion|noun

countercampaign:/ˈkaʊntərˌkæmˈpeɪn/| v.|开展反宣传活动，进行对抗运动|To conduct a campaign in opposition to another.|They countercampaigned against the proposed new law. 他们开展了反对拟议新法的活动。|Example:  The group countercampaigned vigorously against the misinformation being spread.  该组织积极开展反宣传活动，以对抗正在传播的错误信息。|Synonyms: oppose, resist, counterattack|Antonyms: support, promote|verb
