crevicing: /ˈkrɛvɪsɪŋ/
n.|裂缝填塞，填塞裂缝|The act of filling crevices, especially in rock climbing.  在攀岩中尤指填塞裂缝的行为。|例句：Crevicing is an important technique in traditional climbing.  裂缝填塞是传统攀岩中的一项重要技术。|例句：He spent hours crevicing the route before attempting the climb. 他花了几个小时填塞路线上的裂缝，然后才尝试攀登。|近义词：jamming, wedging|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps removing protection)|词性: noun

crevice: /ˈkrɛvɪs/
n.|裂缝，缝隙|A narrow opening or crack, especially in rock or wall.  尤指岩石或墙壁中的狭窄开口或裂缝。|例句：The tiny crevice provided shelter for the insect.  这小小的裂缝为昆虫提供了庇护。|例句：Water seeped through the crevice in the dam. 水从大坝的裂缝中渗出。|近义词：crack, fissure, gap, chink|反义词: closure, seal|词性: noun
