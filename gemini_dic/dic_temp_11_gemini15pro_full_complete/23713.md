brassicaceous:/ˌbræsɪˈkeɪʃəs/| adj.|十字花科的|Relating to or belonging to the plant family Brassicaceae (also known as Cruciferae), which includes cabbages, broccoli, mustard, and other vegetables.|Kale is a brassicaceous vegetable packed with nutrients. 羽衣甘蓝是一种富含营养的十字花科蔬菜。|近义词：cruciferous|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a taxonomic classification)|词性: adjective
