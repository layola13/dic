meatier:/ˈmiːtiər/| adj.|更多肉的；更丰满的；更有实质性的|Having more meat; more substantial or robust.|例句：The stew was meatier than I expected.  这道炖菜比我预料的更丰盛。|例句：He prefers a meatier novel with more complex characters. 他更喜欢人物更复杂、更有实质性的小说。|近义词：fleshier, more substantial, richer|反义词：leaner, thinner, less substantial|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From meat + -ier (comparative suffix).  The suffix "-ier" is used to form the comparative degree of adjectives, indicating a greater degree of the quality expressed by the adjective.  "Meat" comes from Old English "mete," meaning "food."
