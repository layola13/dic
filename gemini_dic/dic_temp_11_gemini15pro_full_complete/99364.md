interdependent:/ˌɪntər.dɪˈpen.dənt/
adjective
相互依存的，互相依赖的
(of two or more people or things) needing each other in order to exist or be successful.
例句：The two companies are interdependent.  这两家公司相互依存。

例句：Countries with interdependent economies  经济相互依存的国家


近义词：interrelated, interconnected, reliant, dependent
反义词：independent, autonomous
词性：adjective
