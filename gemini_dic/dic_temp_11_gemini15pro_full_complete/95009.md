in-your-face:/ˌɪn jər ˈfeɪs/| adj.|咄咄逼人的；直截了当的；不顾他人感受的|Characterized by direct and forceful confrontation, often considered aggressive or provocative.  It describes something that is bold, assertive, and possibly even rude or offensive because it is not subtle or tactful.|Her in-your-face attitude made her unpopular with her colleagues. 她咄咄逼人的态度让她不受同事欢迎。|He delivered an in-your-face critique of the company's policies. 他对公司政策进行了直言不讳的批评。|近义词:aggressive, assertive, bold, confrontational, provocative|反义词:subtle, tactful, reserved, understated|词性:adjective
