disally:/dɪˈsælaɪ/ | v. | 使脱离同盟关系，使失去同盟| To deprive of alliance or an ally; to alienate an ally. |  The king's harsh policies disallied him from several powerful dukes. 国王的严苛政策使他失去了几位强大的公爵的支持。| The incident disallied the two countries.  这一事件使两国脱离了同盟关系。 | 近义词: alienate, estrange, isolate | 反义词: ally, unite | 词性: 动词

词源:  From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + ally (to unite or form a connection between).
