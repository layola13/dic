noncredences:/ˌnɒnˈkriːdənsɪz/
n.
不信；怀疑；不相信
Lack of belief; disbelief.
His noncredence in the supernatural was well known. 他对超自然现象的怀疑是众所周知的。
Near Synonyms: disbelief, skepticism, doubt, incredulity
Antonyms: belief, faith, credence, trust

noncredence:/ˌnɒnˈkriːdənsɪz/
Etymology:  "non-" (not) + "credence" (belief).  "Credence" comes from Old French "creance" meaning "belief, trust," ultimately from Latin "credere" (to believe).
