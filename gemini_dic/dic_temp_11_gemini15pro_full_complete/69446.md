fehmgericht:/ˈfeɪmɡəˌrɪxt/| n.|秘密法庭，私刑|A medieval German court that operated in secret and often employed torture to extract confessions.  It was characterized by its lack of due process and often resulted in arbitrary punishments.|The fehmgericht was notorious for its brutal methods. 秘密法庭因其残忍的手段而臭名昭著。|The accused had no right to legal representation in the fehmgericht. 被告在秘密法庭上没有获得法律代理的权利。|Synonyms: kangaroo court, vigilante court|Antonyms: court of law, due process|noun
