a-teams:/ˈeɪ tiːmz/| n.| A-Team, 特A级团队|A highly skilled and effective group of people, typically assembled for a specific mission or project.  Originally referring to the fictional team in the 1980s television series "The A-Team."|The company assembled an a-team of engineers to develop the new product. 公司组建了一支由工程师组成的特A级团队来开发新产品。|Synonyms: elite team, crack team, task force|Antonyms: incompetent team, disorganized group|noun
