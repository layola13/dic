disapprove:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːv/ | v. | 不赞成，反对 | To have or express an unfavorable opinion of. |  He disapproved of her choice of friends. 他不赞成她对朋友的选择。| 近义词: object to, condemn, deprecate | 反义词: approve, endorse, sanction | 词性: 动词

disapproved:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvd/ | adj. | 不被赞成的，不被认可的 | Not receiving approval; rejected. |  The disapproved project was shelved indefinitely.  这个不被认可的项目被无限期搁置了。 | 近义词: rejected, condemned, censured | 反义词: approved, accepted, endorsed | 词性: 形容词
