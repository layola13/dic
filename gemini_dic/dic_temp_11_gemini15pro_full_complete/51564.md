deuteronomist:/ˌdjuːtərˈɒnəmɪst/ | n. |申命记学者；申命记作者|A scholar or theologian specializing in the study of Deuteronomy, the fifth book of the Bible.  It can also refer to the hypothetical author or authors of Deuteronomy.|The deuteronomist emphasized the importance of covenant loyalty.|Synonym: biblical scholar, theologian|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps secular scholar)|noun


deuteronomistic:/ˌdjuːtərəˈnɒmɪstɪk/ | adj. |申命记的；与申命记相关的|Relating to or characteristic of Deuteronomy or its author(s).|The deuteronomistic history comprises the books of Joshua, Judges, Samuel, and Kings.|Synonym: Deuteronomic|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|adjective
