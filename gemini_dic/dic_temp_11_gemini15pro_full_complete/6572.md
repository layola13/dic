anabaptistry:/ˌænəˈbæptɪstri/ | n. | 再洗礼池，再洗礼堂 | A building or part of a church used for the rite of baptism by immersion, especially by Anabaptists.  | The church's anabaptistry was a simple, yet beautiful structure. 教堂的再洗礼池是一个简朴而美丽的建筑。 |  The anabaptistry was filled with water for the ceremony. 再洗礼池里注满了水，准备进行仪式。| synonym: baptistery | antonym: None | noun
