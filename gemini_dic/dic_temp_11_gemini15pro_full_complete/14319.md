automation: /ˌɔːtəˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 自动化 | The use of automatic equipment in a system of manufacturing or other production process. |  The factory increased production by introducing automation. 工厂通过引进自动化提高了产量。

The automation of the assembly line resulted in job losses.  流水线的自动化导致了失业。 | 近义词: mechanization, computerization | 反义词: manual operation | 词性: 名词


词源:  From the Greek "automatos" (self-acting) + "-ion" (forming nouns of action or process).
