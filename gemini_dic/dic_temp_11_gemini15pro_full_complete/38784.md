conductances:/kənˈdʌktənsɪz/
n.|电导；电导率|The ability of a material or substance to conduct electricity.  It is the reciprocal of resistance.|The conductances of the wires were carefully measured.  这些导线的电导被仔细测量。|conductivity, admittance|resistance|noun
