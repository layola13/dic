apagogical:/ˌæpəˈɡɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 反证法的，间接论证的 | Relating to or involving a method of proof that establishes the validity of a proposition by showing the impossibility of its contradictory. | The lawyer used an apagogical argument to demonstrate his client's innocence. 律师用反证法证明了他的当事人的清白。 | synonyms: indirect, reductio ad absurdum | antonyms: direct, deductive | adjective
