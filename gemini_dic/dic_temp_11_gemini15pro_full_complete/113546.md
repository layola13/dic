macadam:/məˈkædəm/ | n. | 碎石路；用碎石铺成的路 | A road surface made of small broken stones. | The old macadam road was bumpy and uneven.  旧的碎石路坑坑洼洼，很不平坦。 | synonyms:  gravel road, crushed stone road, stone road| antonyms: paved road, asphalt road | noun

macadamize:/məˈkædəˌmaɪz/ | v. | 用碎石铺路 | To pave with macadam. | They macadamized the road leading to the town. 他们用碎石铺设了通往城镇的道路。| synonyms: pave, surface, metal | antonyms: unpave, dig up | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From John Loudon McAdam (1756–1836), a Scottish engineer who invented this road-building method.
