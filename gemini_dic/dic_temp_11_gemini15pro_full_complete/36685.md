colligates:/ˈkɒlɪɡeɪts/
| v. |共现，搭配，组合|occurs with another word or phrase in a statistically significant way|Certain words colligate with each other, like "heavy rain" or "strong wind".  某些词语会一起出现，例如“大雨”或“强风”。|例句：The words "strong" and "coffee" frequently colligate. “浓”和“咖啡”这两个词经常搭配出现。

例句：Researchers analyze large corpora to identify words that colligate. 研究人员分析大型语料库以识别搭配出现的词语。|近义词：co-occur, combine, associate|反义词:separate, diverge|词性:动词
