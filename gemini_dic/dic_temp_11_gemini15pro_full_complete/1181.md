acclimatising:/əˈklaɪmətaɪzɪŋ/
verb
适应环境；驯化；服水土
The process of becoming accustomed to a new climate or environment.
It took him a while to acclimatise to the heat. 他花了一段时间才适应炎热的天气。
acclimating, adapting, adjusting
inadapting
