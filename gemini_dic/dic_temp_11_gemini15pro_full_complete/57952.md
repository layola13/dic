drip-feeding:/ˈdrɪp fiːdɪŋ/
n.|点滴喂养；少量多次地提供；|The government has been accused of drip-feeding information to the public. 政府被指责向公众一点点地透露信息。|例句：The information was drip-fed to the press over several weeks.  这些信息在几周内被陆续透露给媒体。

例句：They drip-fed the market with new products to maintain interest.  他们少量多次地向市场推出新产品以保持人们的兴趣。|近义词：rationing, doling out, meting out|反义词：releasing, divulging, dumping|词性:名词

drip-feed:/ˈdrɪp fiːd/
v.|点滴喂养；少量多次地提供；|The evidence was drip-fed to the jury.  证据被一点一点地提供给陪审团。|例句：The company drip-feeds its employees with information.  该公司一点点地给员工提供信息。

例句：They are drip-feeding funds into the project to avoid overspending. 他们为了避免超支，正逐步地把资金投入到项目中。|近义词：ration, dole out, mete out|反义词：release, divulge, dump|词性:动词

词源:  "Drip" (滴下) + "feed" (喂养).  The term originates from the medical practice of administering fluids intravenously drop by drop.  该词源于医疗实践中逐滴静脉输液。
