clawback:/ˈklɔːbæk/| n.| 收回；追回；取消（已给的钱或好处）|The government proposed a clawback of excessive executive pay. 政府提议收回过高的行政人员薪酬。|例句：The company instituted a clawback policy for bonuses paid to employees who later engaged in misconduct.  该公司制定了一项政策，追回后来有不当行为的员工的奖金。

例句：There was public outcry over the clawback of social security benefits. 公众对社会保障福利的取消感到强烈不满。|近义词：recoup, recovery, retrieval, restitution|反义词:grant, award|词性:名词
