complexification:/kəmˌplɛksɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |复杂化，复杂性增加|The process of making something more complex; an increase in complexity.|The complexification of the software made it difficult to use. 软件的复杂化使得它难以使用。|例句：The complexification of the legal system has made it harder for ordinary people to understand.  法律体系的复杂化使得普通人更难理解。

例句：The complexification of the plot made the novel more engaging.  情节的复杂化使小说更引人入胜。

|近义词：complication, elaboration, sophistication|反义词：simplification, streamlining|词性: 名词
