crowns:/kraʊnz/ | n. | 王冠；花冠；顶点；牙冠；货币 | A symbolic headwear worn by monarchs.  The top part of something, like a tree or a tooth. crowns:/kraʊnz/ | v. | 加冕；表彰；居…之顶；圆满完成 | To officially place a crown on the head of a monarch. To recognize someone's achievements. To be situated on the top of something. To complete something successfully.  |  They crowned him king. 他们为他加冕为国王。 | synonyms: enthrone, honor, reward, cap, top | antonyms: dethrone, disgrace | verb
