imperativism:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪvɪzəm/
n.
命令主义，指令主义
The doctrine that moral obligations are commands.
This philosopher's work is rooted in imperativism.  这位哲学家的作品根植于命令主义。
近义词:authoritarianism, absolutism, dictatorialism
反义词:libertarianism, anarchism
词性:noun

Etymology: From imperative + -ism.  来自 imperative（命令）+ -ism（主义）
