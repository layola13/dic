durene: /ˈdjʊəriːn/ | n. | 杜烯; 1,2,4,5-四甲苯 |  A hydrocarbon, C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>2</sub>(CH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>4</sub>, isomeric with cymene, occurring in coal tar. It is a colorless crystalline solid with a camphor-like odor. | Durene is used in the production of certain types of plastics. 杜烯用于生产某些类型的塑料。| Durene is an important intermediate in the synthesis of pyromellitic dianhydride. 杜烯是均苯四甲酸二酐合成中的重要中间体。| synonyms: 1,2,4,5-tetramethylbenzene | antonyms: N/A | noun
