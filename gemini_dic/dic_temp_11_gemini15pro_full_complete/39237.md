congratulates: /kənˈɡrætʃəleɪts/ (US) /kənˈɡrætʃʊleɪts/ (UK)
|v.|祝贺|expresses pleasure to someone at that person's success or good fortune. |He congratulated her on her promotion. 他祝贺她升职。|近义词：felicitates, commends|反义词：condoles, commiserates|Verb (Third-person singular simple present)
