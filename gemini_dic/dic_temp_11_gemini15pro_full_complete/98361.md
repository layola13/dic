insisters:/ɪnˈsɪstərz/
|n.|坚持者，坚决要求者|People who insist on something; those who persistently demand or maintain something.|The insisters finally got their way and the meeting was rescheduled. 坚持者们最终如愿以偿，会议改期了。|近义词：demanders, persisters, assertors|反义词：acquiescers, compromisers|词性: Noun, plural
