interdenominationally:/ˌɪntərdiːˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənəli/
| adv. |跨教派的，不分教派的|Involving or relating to different Christian denominations.|The conference was held interdenominationally, welcoming members from various churches.  这次会议是跨教派举行的，欢迎来自各教会的成员。|例句：The charity event was organized interdenominationally, bringing together people of different faiths.  这个慈善活动是跨教派组织的，汇集了不同信仰的人们。

例句：They worked interdenominationally to address the issue of poverty in the community. 他们跨教派地合作以解决社区的贫困问题。
|近义词：ecumenically, nonsectarian|反义词：sectarian, denominational|词性:副词
