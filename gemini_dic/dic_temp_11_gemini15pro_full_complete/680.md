abscind:/æbˈsɪnd/ | v. | 切除，割掉；正式脱离，退出（组织、团体等） | To cut off; to formally separate or withdraw (from an organization, group, etc.) | The surgeon abscinded the diseased tissue.  外科医生切除了病变组织。

The member abscinded from the political party.  该成员脱离了政党。 | 近义词：sever, detach, separate, remove, withdraw, resign | 反义词：attach, join, unite | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *abscindere*, from *ab-* (“away from”) + *scindere* (“to cut, split, cleave, divide”).
