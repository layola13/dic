foy:/fɔɪ/
n.|盛宴，庆祝|a feast or celebration; a large or elaborate meal.|The king held a grand foy for his victorious army. 国王为他的胜利之师举行了一场盛大的宴会。|近义词：feast, banquet, celebration|反义词：fast, abstinence|词性: 名词


Etymology: From Middle English *foie, *feye, from Anglo-Norman feie, feste, from Old French feste “feast, festival,” from Vulgar Latin *festa, from Latin festum.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰes- (“religious rite, ceremony”).
