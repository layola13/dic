indeprivable:/ˌɪndɪˈpraɪvəbl/
adjective
不可剥夺的
Not able to be taken away or denied.
Everyone has the indeprivable right to education.  每个人都有不可剥夺的受教育权。
The declaration asserted people's indeprivable right to liberty.  该宣言申明了人民不可剥夺的自由权利。

近义词:inalienable, unalienable, absolute, inviolable
反义词:deprivable, forfeitable, revocable
词性:adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + deprivable (able to be deprived), from deprive (to take away from), from Latin privare (to bereave, rob), from privus (separate, individual).  The prefix "in-" negates the meaning of "deprivable."
