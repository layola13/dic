arm:/ɑːrm/ (UK) /ɑːrm/ (US)| n. v. |手臂；武器；装备；(复数)纹章|A limb connecting the hand and wrist to the shoulder; a branch or division of a company or organization; weapons and ammunition; to supply or equip with weapons.|

例句：
She broke her arm in the accident.
她在事故中摔断了胳膊。

He held the baby in his arms.
他把婴儿抱在怀里。

The country is arming itself for war.
这个国家正在备战。

The company is an arm of the government.
这家公司是政府的一个部门。


近义词：limb, appendage, weapon, equip, outfit
反义词:disarm
词性:名词, 动词
