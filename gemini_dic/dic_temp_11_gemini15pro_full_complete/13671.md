attractions:/əˈtrækʃənz/| n.| 吸引力，吸引人的事物；景点；引力|Something that attracts or is intended to attract people by appealing to their senses or emotions. Places of interest, especially to tourists.|例句：The city has many tourist attractions. 这个城市有很多旅游景点。|近义词：allurements, appeals, enticements|反义词：repulsions, deterrents|Noun
