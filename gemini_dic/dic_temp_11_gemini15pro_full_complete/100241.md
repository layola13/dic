interruptable:/ˌɪntərˈrʌptəbəl/ | adj. | 可中断的，可打断的 | Able to be interrupted; not continuous.  | The program is interruptable, allowing users to pause and resume tasks.  这个程序是可中断的，允许用户暂停和恢复任务。 | The speaker delivered an interruptable presentation, welcoming questions throughout. 演讲者发表了一个可中断的演讲，全程欢迎提问。 | 近义词: discontinuable, susceptible to interruption | 反义词: uninterruptible, continuous | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From interrupt + -able.  "Interrupt" comes from the Latin "interruptus," past participle of "interrumpere"  meaning "to break off" or "to break apart."  The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being" or "worthy of being."
