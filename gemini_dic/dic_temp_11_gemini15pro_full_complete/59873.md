ecumenism:/ɪˈkjuːmənɪzəm/ | n. | 基督教合一；普世教会主义 | The principle or aim of promoting unity among the world's Christian churches. |  The Pope appealed for greater ecumenism between the different branches of Christianity.  教皇呼吁基督教不同派别之间加强团结。| synonyms:  Christian unity, interfaith dialogue, religious harmony | antonyms: sectarianism, division, schism | noun

ecumenical:/ˌɛkjuːˈmɛnɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 普世的；基督教合一的 | Representing a number of different Christian churches. |  The ecumenical council brought together leaders from many denominations.  普世教会会议汇集了来自许多教派的领袖。| synonyms: universal, worldwide, global, general, catholic | antonyms: sectarian, parochial, local, limited | adjective
