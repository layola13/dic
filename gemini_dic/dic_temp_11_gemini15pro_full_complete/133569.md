nonflagrances:/ˌnɒnˈfleɪɡrənsɪz/ | n. | 非现行犯状态；非公然犯罪状态 | The absence of a flagrant offense or crime being committed openly.  It refers to a situation where a crime is not being committed at the time of arrest. |  Police cannot arrest someone for a misdemeanor based on nonflagrances unless they have a warrant. 警方不能仅仅因为非现行犯罪而逮捕一个人，除非他们有逮捕令。

The suspect was apprehended hours after the robbery, a case of nonflagrances.  嫌疑人在抢劫发生几小时后被逮捕，这是一个非现行犯罪的案例。 | 近义词: non-observance, absence of flagrancy | 反义词: flagrancy, in flagrante delicto | 词性: 名词
