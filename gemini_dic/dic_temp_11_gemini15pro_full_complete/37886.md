complainers:/kəmˈpleɪnərz/ | n. | 抱怨者，爱抱怨的人 | People who express dissatisfaction or annoyance frequently or habitually. | The manager was tired of dealing with the complainers. 经理厌倦了应付那些抱怨者。|  The restaurant received a lot of negative reviews from complainers. 这家餐厅收到了很多来自抱怨者的负面评价。 | 近义词：whingers, gripers, moaners | 反义词：praisers, commenders | 词性: noun
