glistering:/ˈɡlɪstərɪŋ/ /ˈɡlɪsərɪŋ/| adj.|闪闪发光的，闪耀的|Covered with or reflecting a soft, tremulous light.|The glistening snow covered the mountains. 闪闪发光的雪覆盖了群山。|近义词：sparkling, shimmering, glittering, gleaming|反义词:dull, matte|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *glisteren, from Old English *glistian, from Proto-Germanic *glistaną, from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰleys- (“to shine”).  Related to glitter.
