enlarge:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒ/ /ɛnˈlɑːrdʒ/ | v. |扩大，放大，增大|To make something bigger or more extensive.|They are planning to enlarge their house by adding an extension. 他们计划扩建房屋，增加一个延伸部分。|例句：The company enlarged its market share.  公司扩大了市场份额。|近义词：expand, increase, augment, amplify|反义词:reduce, shrink, diminish, decrease|词性:动词

enlarge:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒ/ /ɛnˈlɑːrdʒ/ |  adj. |放大的 (主要用于摄影)|Made larger, especially in photography.|He showed us an enlarged photograph of the insect. 他给我们看了昆虫的放大照片。|例句：This is an enlarged copy of the original document. 这是原件的放大副本。|近义词：magnified, expanded|反义词:reduced, miniature|词性:形容词
