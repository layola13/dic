not-least:/ˌnɒt ˈliːst/ | adv. | 尤其是；特别是 |  Used to emphasize a particular item in a list of things. It implies that all the items in the list are important, but this one is particularly significant. |  The location was chosen for several reasons, not least its proximity to the airport.  这个地点的选择有几个原因，尤其是它靠近机场。 | Synonyms: especially, particularly, notably, in particular | Antonyms: least of all, insignificantly | adverb

not-least:/ˌnɒt ˈliːst/ | adj. |  重要的；显著的 (less common usage) | Sometimes used attributively to mean 'significant' or 'important'. |  He played a not-least role in the company's success. 他在公司的成功中扮演了重要的角色。 | Synonyms: significant, important, substantial | Antonyms: insignificant, unimportant, trivial | adjective
