de-Americanizes: /diːəˈmɛrɪkəˌnaɪzɪz/
verb
去美国化；使失去美国特色
To cause something or someone to lose American characteristics or influences.
The new government is trying to de-Americanize the country's education system.  新政府正试图去除国家教育体系中的美国特色。
The immigrant gradually de-Americanized his accent over time.  随着时间的推移，这位移民逐渐淡化了他口音中的美国味。
近义词: denationalize, westernize (depending on context)
反义词: Americanize
词性: verb
