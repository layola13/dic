neutrinoless:/ˌnjuːtrɪˈnəʊləs/| adj.|无中微子的|Characterized by the absence of neutrinos.  Relating to a nuclear process that does not involve neutrinos.|Neutrinoless double beta decay is a theorized radioactive decay process. 无中微子双β衰变是一种理论上的放射性衰变过程。|近义词: neutrino-free|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "neutrino-rich")|词性: adjective
