crural:/ˈkruːrəl/| adj.|腿的，小腿的|Relating to the leg, especially the thigh or shank.  Specifically referring to the area between the knee and ankle (or the equivalent part in an animal).|The crural muscles are essential for walking and running. 小腿肌肉对于行走和跑步至关重要。|Synonyms: leg, shank, shin|Antonyms: brachial (relating to the arm)|adjective
crural:/ˈkruːrəl/| n.|腿部|The leg, especially the shank.|Injuries to the crural area can be debilitating. 腿部的损伤可能会使人衰弱。|Synonyms: leg, shank, shin|Antonyms: arm|noun
