microzooespore:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈzoʊəˌspɔːr/
n.
微型游动孢子
A small, motile zoospore.  It is typically smaller than a regular zoospore.
Microzooespores are released into the water to find a suitable substrate. 微型游动孢子被释放到水中以寻找合适的基质。
The microzooespores swam vigorously towards the light source. 微型游动孢子朝着光源奋力游动。

近义词: zoospore, planospore
反义词: (No specific antonym, perhaps aplanospore - a non-motile spore)
词性: Noun
