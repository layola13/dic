ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *cosinage*, from Old French *cousinage*, from *cousin* (“cousin”), from Latin *consobrinus*.  The original meaning referred to falsely claiming kinship to gain an inheritance.  The meaning broadened to encompass any kind of fraudulent deception.
