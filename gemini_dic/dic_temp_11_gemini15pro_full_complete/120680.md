misadjust:/ˌmɪsəˈdʒʌst/
verb
调整不当；校准错误
To adjust wrongly or improperly.
The mechanic misadjusted the carburetor, causing the engine to stall.  机修工错误地调整了化油器，导致发动机熄火。
misaligned, maladjusted, misarranged
adjust, regulate, tune
