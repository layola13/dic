disquiet:/dɪsˈkwaɪət/ /dɪsˈkwaɪɪt/ | n. v. |不安，焦虑；使…不安 |A feeling of anxiety or worry.|  I felt a deep disquiet about the future. 我对未来感到深深的不安。| 近义词: unease, anxiety, worry, apprehension | 反义词: calm, peace, tranquility | 词性: 名词

disquiet:/dɪsˈkwaɪət/ /dɪsˈkwaɪɪt/ | n. v. | 使不安，使焦虑 |To make someone feel worried or anxious.| The news disquieted her. 这消息让她感到不安。| 近义词: disturb, worry, upset, bother | 反义词: soothe, calm, reassure | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *desquiet, from des- (expressing negation) + quiet (quiet).
