forbearance:/fɔːrˈbɛərəns/| n. |克制；忍耐；宽容；（债权人对债务人）延期偿还|Patient self-control; restraint and tolerance.|例句：He showed great forbearance in his dealings with them. 他在与他们打交道时表现出了极大的克制。

例句：The bank granted him forbearance on his loan. 银行同意他延期偿还贷款。|近义词：patience, tolerance, restraint, leniency, indulgence|反义词：impatience, intolerance, harshness, severity|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *forberinge, from Old English forbering, equivalent to forbear +‎ -ing. Cognate with Dutch verbering (“forbearance”).
