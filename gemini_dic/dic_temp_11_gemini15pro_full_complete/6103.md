americans:/əˈmerɪkənz/
n.
美国人
People who are citizens of the United States of America.
Many Americans enjoy watching baseball. 许多美国人喜欢看棒球。
近义词: US citizens, people from the US
反义词: foreigners, non-Americans
词性: noun


Etymology: From America + -an (suffix forming adjectives and nouns denoting origin or association) + -s (plural suffix).  Ultimately derived from Americus, the Latinized version of Amerigo Vespucci's first name, the Italian explorer.
