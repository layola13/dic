anemosis:/ˌænɪˈmoʊsɪs/| n. |风散，风媒传播|The spreading or dissemination of anything (e.g., seeds, pollen, disease) by the wind. | Dandelion seeds rely on anemosis for dispersal. 蒲公英种子依靠风传播。 The anemosis of fungal spores can contribute to the spread of plant diseases. 真菌孢子的风传播会导致植物病害的蔓延。|近义词：wind dispersal, aeolian dispersal|反义词: N/A |noun
