gherkins:/ˈɡɜːrkɪnz/ /ˈɡɝːkɪnz/| n. |小黄瓜，腌黄瓜|A small variety of cucumber, often pickled.  A small, prickly cucumber pickled in vinegar or brine.| I love gherkins on my sandwiches. 我喜欢在三明治里放腌黄瓜。|Synonyms: cornichons, pickles|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fresh cucumbers")|Noun
