notarize: /ˈnoʊtəˌraɪz/ | vt. | 公证，证明 | To certify or attest to (a document, signature, etc.) officially, usually by a notary public. | Example: I need to have this document notarized before I submit it. 我需要在提交这份文件之前进行公证。

Example:  She had her signature notarized at the bank. 她在银行公证了她的签名。


|近义词：certify, authenticate, validate|反义词: invalidate |词性: verb|
