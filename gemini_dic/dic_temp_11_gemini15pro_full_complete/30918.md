chained:/ˈtʃeɪnd/| adj.|被链条拴住的，束缚的，受约束的|Bound with a chain or chains; figuratively, restricted or constrained.|The dog was chained to the fence. 这条狗被链条拴在篱笆上。|The prisoners were chained together. 囚犯们被链条锁在一起。|近义词：bound, fettered, shackled, restricted|反义词：free, liberated, unchained|词性:形容词

词源：From Old French *chaëné (Modern French chaîné), past participle of chaëner “to chain,” from Vulgar Latin *catenare, from Latin catena “chain.”
