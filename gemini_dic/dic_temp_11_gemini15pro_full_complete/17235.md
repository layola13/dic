bawling:/ˈbɔːlɪŋ/
verb
大声哭喊；放声痛哭
Crying loudly and unrestrainedly.
The baby was bawling its eyes out.  婴儿哭得很大声。
近义词: crying, sobbing, weeping, wailing
反义词: laughing, giggling, chuckling
词性: verb (present participle)

bawl:/bɔːl/
verb
大声叫喊；放声痛哭
To shout or cry loudly.
The sergeant bawled at the recruits. 军士对新兵大吼大叫。
近义词: shout, yell, scream, roar, cry
反义词: whisper, murmur
词性: verb


Etymology:
From Middle English *baullen, *bawlen, of uncertain origin, perhaps imitative. Cognate with Dutch bellen (“to bark, bellow, low”), German bellen (“to bark”).
