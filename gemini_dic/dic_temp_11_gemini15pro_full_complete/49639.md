demilitarised:/diːˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd/
adjective
非军事化的，解除武装的
An area from which military forces, weapons, or fortifications have been removed.
例句：The demilitarised zone between the two countries was heavily patrolled.  两国之间的非军事区戒备森严。

例句：They agreed to demilitarise the border region. 他们同意将边境地区非军事化。
近义词：neutralized, unarmed
反义词：militarised, armed
词性：adjective


demilitarized:/diːˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd/
verb
使非军事化，解除武装
To remove military forces, weapons, or fortifications from.
例句：The treaty required both sides to demilitarize the disputed territory.  条约要求双方将争议领土非军事化。

例句：The government decided to demilitarize the island. 政府决定将该岛非军事化。
近义词：neutralize, disarm
反义词：militarize, arm
词性：verb


词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + military + -ised/-ized (suffix forming adjectives and verbs)
