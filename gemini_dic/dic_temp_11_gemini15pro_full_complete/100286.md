interseminate:/ˌɪntərˈsɛmɪneɪt/
verb
散播；使混杂；互相传播
To disseminate among; to spread throughout something else; intermix.
The gardener interseminated wildflower seeds throughout the formal garden. 园丁把野花的种子散播在整个正式花园里。
intermix, intermingle, scatter, disperse, disseminate, spread
