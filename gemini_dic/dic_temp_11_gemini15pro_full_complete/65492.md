europeanizing:/ˌjʊərəˈpiːənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
欧洲化
The act of making something more European in character or style.
Many countries in Eastern Europe were europeanizing their economies after the fall of the Soviet Union.  东欧许多国家在苏联解体后都在使其经济欧洲化。
The new government is europeanizing the legal system. 新政府正在使法律制度欧洲化。
近义词: westernizing, modernizing
反义词: traditionalizing, localizing
词性: verb
