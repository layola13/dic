cropped:/krɒpt/ (British) /krɑːpt/ (American)| adj.|裁剪的，修剪的，短的|Cut short or shortened, especially of hair or clothing; (of an image) having had the edges removed.|例句：She wore a cropped jacket and jeans. 她穿着一件短夹克和牛仔裤。|近义词：trimmed, cut, shortened, clipped|反义词：long, overgrown, full-length|词性:形容词

cropped:/krɒpt/ (British) /krɑːpt/ (American)| v.|（尤指头发或衣物）剪短，裁短；（图像）剪裁|past participle of crop|例句：The photo was cropped to remove the background. 这张照片被裁剪以去除背景。|近义词：trim, cut, shorten, clip|反义词：extend, lengthen|词性:动词
