forethoughts:/ˈfɔːrθɔːts/| n. |预先考虑；深谋远虑；预见|Careful planning or consideration in advance; prudent foresight.|She had the forethought to bring an umbrella. 她很有先见之明地带了一把伞。|近义词：foresight, prudence, anticipation, precaution, planning|反义词：recklessness, carelessness, improvidence, imprudence|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *forethoughtes, equivalent to fore- +‎ thought.
