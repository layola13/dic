decivilized:/diːˈsɪvɪlaɪzd/
adj.
不文明的，野蛮的
Lacking the qualities of civilization; barbarous.
The decivilized behavior of the rioters shocked the nation.  暴徒不文明的行为震惊了全国。
近义词: barbaric, savage, uncivilized, primitive
反义词: civilized, cultured, refined
词性: 形容词

decivilize:/diːˈsɪvɪlaɪz/
vt.
使不文明，使野蛮
To cause to lose the qualities of civilization; to barbarize.
War can decivilize even the most cultured societies. 战争即使是最有文化的社会也能变得野蛮。
近义词: barbarize, brutalize
反义词: civilize, refine
词性: 动词

词源:  de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + civilized.  "de-"（前缀，意思是“移除”或“反转”）+ civilized（文明的）。
