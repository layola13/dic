dodecaphonic:/ˌdoʊˌdekəˈfɑːnɪk/
adjective
十二音的
Relating to or based on a system of musical composition in which the twelve notes of the chromatic scale are treated as of equal importance and arranged in series.
Schoenberg developed the dodecaphonic, or twelve-tone, system of composition.  勋伯格发展了十二音作曲体系。
Synonym: twelve-tone, serial
Antonym: tonal, diatonic
词性: adjective
