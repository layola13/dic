agitpropists:/ˈædʒɪtprɒpɪsts/
n.
宣传鼓动者
People who spread political propaganda, especially for communist or socialist causes.
The agitpropists distributed leaflets urging workers to join the strike.  宣传鼓动者散发传单，敦促工人参加罢工。
Near Synonyms:  propagandists, activists, campaigners
Near Antonyms:  pacifists, moderates
noun
