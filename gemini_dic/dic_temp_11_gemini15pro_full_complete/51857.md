dextro:/ˈdɛkstrəʊ/
prefix|右旋的，向右的|Relating to or designating a chemical compound that rotates the plane of polarized light to the right, or clockwise.|Dextro-rotatory substances are often labeled with a (+).  右旋物质通常用(+)标记。|Dextrose is a dextro-rotatory form of glucose. 葡萄糖是右旋形式的葡萄糖。|dextrorotatory, right-handed|levo-, laevo-|prefix
