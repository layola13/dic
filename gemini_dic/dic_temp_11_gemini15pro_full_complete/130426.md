nonability:/ˌnɒnəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 无能，无力 | Lack of ability; inability. |  He admitted his nonability to perform the task. 他承认自己没有能力完成这项任务。|  The team's nonability to score cost them the game. 球队的无力得分让他们输掉了比赛。| 近义词: inability, incapacity, incompetence | 反义词: ability, capability, competence | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + ability (n.)  From Latin habilitas, from habilis "apt, fit," from habere "to have, hold."
