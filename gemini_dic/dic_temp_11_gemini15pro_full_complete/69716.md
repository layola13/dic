fentanyl:/ˈfɛntənɪl/ | n. | 芬太尼 | A synthetic opioid analgesic similar to morphine but much more potent, used medically to treat severe pain, especially after surgery, and illegally as a recreational drug. | The doctor prescribed fentanyl for the patient's post-operative pain. 医生给病人开了芬太尼来治疗术后疼痛。

Fentanyl is a powerful opioid that can be deadly in high doses.  芬太尼是一种强效阿片类药物，高剂量服用可能是致命的。 | 近义词: morphine, sufentanil, alfentanil | 反义词: naloxone (antagonist) | 词性: noun
