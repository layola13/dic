disenthrone:/dɪsɪnˈθroʊn/
verb
废黜；罢免
To remove from a throne; depose from regal power.  To remove from a position of authority or dominance.
The rebels sought to disenthrone the king.  叛军试图废黜国王。
The new technology could disenthrone the current market leader.  这项新技术可能会取代目前的市场领导者。
Synonyms: depose, dethrone, oust, unseat
Antonyms: enthrone, crown, install
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + enthrone.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "reversal or removal," and "enthrone" means "to place on a throne." Thus, "disenthrone" literally means "to remove from a throne."
