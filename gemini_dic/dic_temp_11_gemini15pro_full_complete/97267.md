inflowing:/ˈɪnfloʊɪŋ/| adj.|流入的，注入的|Flowing inward or toward a central point.  The inflow of investment capital stimulated the economy.|例句：The inflowing river provided fresh water for the town. 流入的河流为小镇提供了淡水。

例句：The country experienced a large inflowing of immigrants.  这个国家经历了大量的移民涌入。|近义词：incoming, entering, inward|反义词:outflowing, exiting|词性:形容词

词源: "in-" (prefix meaning "in, into, within") + "flowing" (present participle of "flow").  From Old English "in" and "flowan."
