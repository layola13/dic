eclaircise: /eɪˈklɛərsaɪz/ | v. | 阐明，解释 | To make clear or lucid; to explain. |  She eclaircised the complex theory with simple examples. 她用简单的例子阐明了这个复杂的理论。

The professor eclaircised the poem's meaning to the students. 教授向学生们解释了这首诗的含义。


近义词: clarify, elucidate, explicate, illuminate | 反义词: obfuscate, confuse, obscure | 词性: 动词

词源:  From French *éclaircir*, from *é-*, out + *clair*, clear.  The "-cise" ending is likely influenced by words like "exercise" and "exorcise".
