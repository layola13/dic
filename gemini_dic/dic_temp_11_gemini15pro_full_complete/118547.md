meson:/ˈmeːzɒn/ (UK) /ˈmiːzɒn/ (US)| n.|介子|A subatomic particle with a mass greater than that of an electron and less than that of a proton, and which is either positively or negatively charged or neutral.|Physicists have identified many types of mesons. 物理学家已经识别出许多类型的介子。|近义词:hadron (all mesons are hadrons) |反义词:lepton, baryon|noun
