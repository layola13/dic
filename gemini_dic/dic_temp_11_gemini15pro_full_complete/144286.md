outspeed:/ˌaʊtˈspiːd/
verb
超过…的速度; 比…更快
to move faster than someone or something else
例句：The cheetah outsped the gazelle. 猎豹的速度超过了瞪羚。
例句：Their new boat easily outspeeds all the others in the race. 他们的新船在比赛中轻松地超过了所有其他船只。
近义词：outrun, outpace, surpass, exceed
反义词：lag behind, trail
词性：v.
