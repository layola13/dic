evasively:/ɪˈveɪsɪvli/| adv.|逃避地，推诿地，含糊其辞地|In a manner that is not direct or clear, tending to avoid commitment or revealing information.|He answered the question evasively, avoiding eye contact.|近义词：ambiguously, indirectly, vaguely|反义词：directly, frankly, openly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From evade (v.) + -ive (adj.) + -ly (adv.)
Evade comes from the Latin evadere "to escape," from ex- "out" + vadere "to go."
