doorplane:/ˈdɔːrpleɪn/| n.|登机门桥;廊桥|A movable bridge-like structure used to allow passengers to board and disembark an aircraft.  It connects the aircraft door to the airport terminal.|例句：The passengers walked across the doorplane to board the flight.  乘客们走过廊桥登机。

例句：The doorplane was retracted after all the passengers had disembarked. 所有乘客下机后，廊桥被撤回了。|近义词：jet bridge, jetway, air bridge, passenger boarding bridge|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
