mandarin:/ˈmændərɪn/ (US), /ˈmændərɪn/ (UK)| n. adj.| 普通话；柑橘；中国官话；满清官吏 |A high public official of imperial China.  A small citrus fruit similar to a tangerine. The standard literary and official form of Chinese based on the Beijing dialect.| The Emperor addressed his mandarins. 皇帝向他的官员们讲话。|  She peeled a mandarin. 她剥了一个柑橘。 He speaks Mandarin fluently. 他说一口流利的普通话。 | 近义词:official (n.), tangerine (n.), Putonghua (n.)| 反义词:citizen (n., for official)|noun, adjective
