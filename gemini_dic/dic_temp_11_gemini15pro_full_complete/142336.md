orthognathism: /ˌɔːrθəɡˈnæθɪzəm/ | n. | 正颌；颌骨正常 | The condition of having straight jaws, where the teeth meet normally.  It is the opposite of prognathism (protruding jaw) and retrognathism (receding jaw). |  Example 1: Her orthognathism gave her a balanced facial profile. 她的正颌使她的面部轮廓均衡。

Example 2:  The surgeon specializes in correcting deviations from orthognathism.  这位外科医生专门矫正颌骨异常。 | 近义词: normal occlusion | 反义词: prognathism, retrognathism | 词性: noun
