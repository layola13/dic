nonpsychically:/ˌnɒnˈsaɪkɪkli/
adv.
非心灵地，非超自然地
Not involving psychic phenomena or extrasensory perception.
例句：The message was delivered nonpsychically, through a letter. 这条消息是通过信件传递的，而非心灵感应。

例句：He claimed to have solved the crime nonpsychically, through careful deduction. 他声称是通过细致的推理，而不是超能力，破了这个案子。

近义词：materially, physically, empirically
反义词：psychically, supernaturally
词性：副词
