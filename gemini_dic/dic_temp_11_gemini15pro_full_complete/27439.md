cammock:/ˈkæmək/| n. |金雀花，染料木|A spiny shrub (Genista tinctoria) of the pea family, with clusters of yellow flowers and slender branches, formerly used as a source of yellow dye.|例句：The cammock bloomed brightly on the hillside. 山坡上的金雀花开得很鲜艳。

例句：The weaver used cammock to create a vibrant yellow dye for the wool.  织工用金雀花为羊毛制作鲜艳的黄色染料。|近义词：dyer's greenweed, whin|反义词:|名词

词源:  From Middle English *cammok, from Old English cammoc.  Related to Dutch kamille and German Kamille (“chamomile”).  Despite the similarity in name to chamomile, they are not closely related botanically.
