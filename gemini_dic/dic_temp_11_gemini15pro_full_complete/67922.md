facelessnesses:/ˈfeɪsləsnɪsɪz/
n.

The facelessnesses of the bureaucracy made it difficult to get anyone to take responsibility. 官僚机构的匿名性使得很难找到任何承担责任的人。
anonymity, impersonality
individuality, personality
