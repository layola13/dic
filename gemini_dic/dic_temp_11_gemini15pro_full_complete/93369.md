illegitimating:/ɪˌlɪˈdʒɪtɪmeɪtɪŋ/
|v.|使不合法；剥夺合法地位；宣布…为私生子|Depriving of legitimacy or legal status; declaring illegitimate, especially declaring a child to be born out of wedlock.|The king's actions were seen as illegitimating the previous ruler's claim to the throne. 国王的行动被视为剥夺了前统治者对王位的合法继承权。|
|The new law illegitimated all previously recognized customary marriages. 新法律宣布所有先前认可的习俗婚姻无效。|
|Synonyms: invalidating, nullifying, delegitimizing|
|Antonyms: legitimizing, validating, authorizing|
|Verb|
