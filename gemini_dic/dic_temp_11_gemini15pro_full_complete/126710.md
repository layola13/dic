nameplates:/ˈneɪmˌpleɪts/| n. |铭牌，名牌|A small plate bearing a person's name, especially on a door or desk. |The nameplates on the doors were tarnished with age. 门上的铭牌因年代久远而失去光泽。|synonyms: name tags, labels, plaques|antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly anonymity) |noun

nameplates:/ˈneɪmˌpleɪts/| n. |铭牌，名牌|A plate attached to something, giving its name, details, or other information. |The nameplate on the machine indicated it was manufactured in Germany. 机器上的铭牌表明它是在德国制造的。|synonyms: data plates, information plates, identification tags|antonyms: (No direct antonym) |noun
