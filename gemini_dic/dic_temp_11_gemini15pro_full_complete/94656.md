impression:/ɪmˈprɛʃən/| n.|印象；感想；印记；压痕|An impression is an idea, feeling, or opinion about something or someone, especially one formed without conscious thought or on the basis of little evidence. |The candidate made a good impression on the voters.  这位候选人给选民留下了好印象。

The trip left a lasting impression on me.  这次旅行给我留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：feeling, notion, idea, belief, opinion, view, perception, sense|反义词：reality|词性:名词

impression:/ɪmˈprɛʃən/| n.|模仿；滑稽模仿|An impression is an amusing imitation of a famous person. |He did a hilarious impression of the president. 他对总统进行了滑稽的模仿。

She can do a really good impression of Cher. 她能惟妙惟肖地模仿雪儿。|近义词：imitation, impersonation, mimicry|反义词：--|词性:名词
