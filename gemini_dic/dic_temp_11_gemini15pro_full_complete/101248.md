invocational:/ˌɪnvəˈkeɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 祈求的，祈祷的；专业的，职业的 | Relating to invocation or prayer;  pertaining to a vocation or occupation. |  The invocational prayer opened the ceremony.  祷告词开启了仪式。|  The school offers invocational training in several trades. 学校提供几种行业的职业培训。 | 近义词: prayerful, vocational, occupational | 反义词: secular, non-vocational | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *invocationalis, from Latin invocatio (“a calling upon, invocation”), from invocare (“to call upon”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + vocare (“to call”).
