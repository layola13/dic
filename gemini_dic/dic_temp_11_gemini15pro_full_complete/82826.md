gula:/ˈɡuːlə/| n.| 糖; 食糖; 甜的东西|A crystalline or powdered sweet substance, especially sucrose, obtained from various plants, used to sweeten food and drink.|The recipe calls for two cups of gula.|近义词：sugar, sucrose, sweet, sweetener|反义词:none|词性:名词

Etymology: From Malay *gula*, from Sanskrit गुड (guḍa, “raw sugar, jaggery”).
