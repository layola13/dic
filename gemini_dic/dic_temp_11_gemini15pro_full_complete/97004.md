infarct: /ˈɪnfɑːrkt/ (US) /ˈɪnfɑːkt/ (UK)| n.|梗塞，梗死|An area of tissue death caused by obstruction of the blood supply to the area.|The patient suffered a myocardial infarct. 患者患有心肌梗死。|Synonyms: infarction, blockage, occlusion|Antonyms: perfusion, flow|Noun


infarct: /ɪnˈfɑːrkt/ (US) /ɪnˈfɑːkt/ (UK)| vt.|使梗塞|To cause an infarct in (an organ or tissue).|The blood clot infarcted the lung tissue. 血凝块使肺组织梗塞。|Synonyms: obstruct, occlude, block|Antonyms: perfuse, irrigate|Verb
