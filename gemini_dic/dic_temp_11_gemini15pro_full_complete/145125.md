Etymology:

"Over-" (prefix): From Old English "ofer," meaning "above," "across," "beyond."
"Argumentative": From Late Latin "argumentativus," from Latin "argumentum" (argument).
"-ness" (suffix): Forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
