overdiscourages:/ˌoʊvər dɪsˈkɜːrɪdʒɪz/
verb
过度 discourage（使气馁）
To discourage to an excessive degree; to cause someone to lose enthusiasm or confidence completely.
The constant criticism from her boss overdiscouraged her, and she eventually quit her job. 她老板不断的批评让她彻底失去了信心，最终她辞掉了工作。
Synonyms: demoralize, dispirit, crush
Antonyms: encourage, inspire, motivate
词性: 动词
