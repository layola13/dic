athermous:/ˈeɪθərməs/| adj.|不透热的，绝热的|Not allowing heat to pass through; impervious to heat.  Relating to a process or reaction occurring without gain or loss of heat.|The athermous container kept the liquid nitrogen cold for hours. 这个不透热的容器使液氮保持了数小时的低温。|近义词：adiabatic, heat-insulating|反义词:diathermic, heat-conducting|词性:adjective
词源: From Greek *a-* "not" + *thermē* "heat"
