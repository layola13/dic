agiotage:/ˈædʒiəˌtɑːʒ/ /ˌædʒiəˈtɑːʒ/
noun
兴奋，激动；投机狂热
A state of excitement or agitation; speculation, especially in stocks.
The agiotage surrounding the new cryptocurrency was palpable.  围绕这种新加密货币的投机狂热是显而易见的。
Near synonyms: excitement, frenzy, furor, hype, mania
Antonyms: calmness, tranquility, apathy
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *agiotage*, from *agioter*, "to agitate," from Italian *aggio*, "exchange premium," from Late Latin *agium*. Ultimately from Latin *addere*, "to add."
