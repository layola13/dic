life's:/laɪfs/ |  n. (possessive form) | 人生的，生命的 |  The possessive form of "life," indicating belonging or association with life.  It's a contraction of "life is" or "life has." |  Life's too short to worry about small things.  人生苦短，不要为小事烦恼。 | synonym: lifetime's | antonym: death's | possessive pronoun


life's:/laɪfs/ | v. (contraction) | 是;有 | Contraction of "life is" or "life has". | Life's been good to me.  生活一直对我很好。 | synonym: is, has | antonym: isn't, hasn't | contraction
