antipredator:/ˌæntiˈprɛdətər/ | adj. | 反捕食者的，反掠夺者的 | Relating to or denoting behavior by prey animals that serves to protect them from predators. |  The gazelle's speed is a crucial antipredator adaptation.  瞪羚的速度是一种关键的反捕食者适应。|  The bright coloration of the poison dart frog serves as an antipredator signal.  毒镖蛙鲜艳的颜色是一种反捕食者信号。| synonyms: defensive, protective | antonyms: predatory, aggressive | adjective
