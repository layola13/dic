bologna:/bəˈloʊni/ (US), /bəˈlɒɡnə/ (UK)| n.|博洛尼亚香肠，大腊肠|A large seasoned sausage of beef and pork, often smoked.|例句：I made a sandwich with bologna. 我做了一个博洛尼亚香肠三明治。

例句：He prefers bologna to ham. 他喜欢博洛尼亚香肠胜过火腿。|近义词：salami, sausage, luncheon meat|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fresh meat" as a contrast)|词性:noun
