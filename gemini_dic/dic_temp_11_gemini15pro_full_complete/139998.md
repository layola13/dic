odysseus:/əˈdɪsiəs, oʊˈdɪsiəs/| n. |奥德修斯| Odysseus is the protagonist of Homer's epic poem, the Odyssey. He is known for his cunning, intelligence, and ten-year journey home after the Trojan War. | Odysseus is a legendary hero. 奥德修斯是一位传奇英雄。

Odysseus's journey was fraught with peril. 奥德修斯的旅程充满了危险。 | 近义词: hero, protagonist, traveler | 反义词: antagonist, villain | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin _Ulixsēs_, from Ancient Greek _Ὀδυσσεύς (Odusseús)_. The Latin form _Ulixes_ gives rise to the alternative English spelling "Ulysses". The etymology of the Greek name is uncertain, with various theories connecting it to words meaning "to hate", "to be wrathful", or "to suffer pain".
