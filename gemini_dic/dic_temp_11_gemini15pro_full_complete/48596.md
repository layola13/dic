defecatory:/ˈdɛfɪkətɔːri/ /ˈdiːfɪkətɔːri/
adjective
排泄的；有关排泄的
Relating to or concerned with defecation (the discharge of feces from the body).
The defecatory reflex is triggered by the presence of stool in the rectum.  排泄反射是由直肠内粪便的存在引发的。
The patient's defecatory habits were carefully monitored.  病人的排泄习惯受到了仔细的监测。
synonyms:  excretory, eliminatory
antonyms:  retentive
