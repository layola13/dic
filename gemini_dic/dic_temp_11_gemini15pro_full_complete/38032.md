componentize:/kəmˈpoʊnənˌtaɪz/
|v.|组件化，模块化|To design or build something using components, making it modular and reusable.|We need to componentize our software to make it more flexible and maintainable. 我们需要将我们的软件组件化，使其更灵活和易于维护。|modularize, decompose|monolithize|verb
componentization:/kəmˌpoʊnənˌtaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|组件化，模块化|The process of componentizing something.|The componentization of the system allowed for easier updates and upgrades. 系统的组件化使得更新和升级更容易。|modularization, decomposition|monolithization|noun
