inviolate:/ɪnˈvaɪəleɪt/ | adj. |神圣的，不可侵犯的，未受亵渎的| Free from violation, injury, desecration, or profanation; undisturbed, pure. |  This ancient burial ground should remain inviolate.  这片古老的墓地应该保持神圣不可侵犯。 |  sacred, hallowed, sacrosanct, untouchable | violated, profaned, desecrated | adjective
Etymology: From Latin inviolātus (“unhurt, unharmed, inviolate”), past participle of inviolō (“I leave unharmed, do not violate”), from in- (“not”) + violō (“I violate, injure, outrage”).
