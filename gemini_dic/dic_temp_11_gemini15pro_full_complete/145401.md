overcautiously:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəsli/| adv.|过于谨慎地|In an excessively cautious manner; too carefully.|He approached the dog overcautiously, afraid it might bite. 他非常小心地靠近那只狗，害怕它会咬人。|近义词：carefully, cautiously, gingerly, prudently|反义词：recklessly, carelessly, rashly|词性: 副词
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + cautiously (adverb form of cautious, from Latin *cautiosus*, from cautus "wary," past participle of cavere "to beware")
