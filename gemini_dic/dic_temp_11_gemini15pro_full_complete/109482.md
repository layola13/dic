leprosy: /ˈlɛprəsi/ (US) /ˈlɛprəsi/ (UK)| n. |麻风病；麻风；受排斥的状态|A chronic infectious disease that mainly affects the skin, mucous membranes, and nerves, causing discoloration and lumps on the skin and, in severe cases, disfigurement and deformities.|例句：Leprosy is a curable disease. 麻风病是一种可治愈的疾病。

例句：He was ostracized from the community because of his leprosy. 他因为患麻风病而被社区排斥。|近义词：Hansen's disease|反义词: health|词性:noun
