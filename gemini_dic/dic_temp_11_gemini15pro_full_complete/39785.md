consonantism:/ˈkɑːnsənənˌtɪzəm/| n.|辅音音系；辅音系统|The study of the consonants of a particular language or variety of a language is called consonantism.  对特定语言或语言变体的辅音的研究被称为辅音音系。|The consonantism of this dialect is characterized by its complex consonant clusters.  这种方言的辅音音系以其复杂的辅音丛为特征。|近义词:consonant system|反义词:vocalism|词性:noun
