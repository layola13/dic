fills:/fɪlz/ | v. | 填充，装满，弥补，担任（职位）| to make or become full; to occupy a space, time, or role |  He filled the glass with water. 他把杯子装满了水。 | synonyms: occupy, stuff, pack, replenish | antonyms: empty, drain, vacate | verb

fills:/fɪlz/ | n. | (fill的复数) 充满，饱足，足够 | the action of filling something; an amount that fills something | The restaurant offered free refills on drinks. 这家餐厅提供免费续杯饮料。| synonyms: abundance, plenty, sufficiency | antonyms: lack, scarcity, deficiency | noun
