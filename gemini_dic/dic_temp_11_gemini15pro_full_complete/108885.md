lectured:/ˈlektʃərd/
verb
讲课，作讲座；训斥
delivered a lecture; scolded someone
The professor lectured the students on the history of art. 教授给学生们讲授艺术史。
He lectured me about the dangers of smoking. 他教训我吸烟的危害。
teach, instruct, address, preach
scold, reprimand, rebuke, admonish
v.
