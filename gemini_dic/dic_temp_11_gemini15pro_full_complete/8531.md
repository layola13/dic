anticentralism:/ˌæntiˈsɛntrəlɪzəm/ | n. | 反中央集权主义; 地方分权主义 | Opposition to the concentration of power in a central authority, advocating for greater autonomy or power at local or regional levels. |  The growing anticentralism in the region led to calls for greater local control over resources. 该地区日益增长的反中央集权主义导致人们呼吁加强地方对资源的控制。

The party's platform embraced anticentralism, promising to devolve power to regional governments. 该党的纲领拥护反中央集权主义，承诺将权力下放给地方政府。 | 近义词：decentralization, regionalism, localism | 反义词：centralism, centralization | 词性: noun
