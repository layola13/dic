luckiest:/ˈlʌkiɪst/ | adj. | 最幸运的 |  Superlative form of "lucky," meaning having or bringing the most good fortune. |  She was the luckiest person I knew, always winning raffles. 她是我认识的最幸运的人，总是赢得抽奖。 |  Most fortunate, blessed | Unluckiest, least fortunate | adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *lukkiest, superlative of lukky (“lucky”), from  lucky  +‎  -est.
