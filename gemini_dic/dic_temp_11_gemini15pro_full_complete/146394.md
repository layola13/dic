overfluencies:/ˌoʊvərˈfluːənsiz/| n.|口语不流利，言语重复或停顿|Extra speech events, such as fillers, repetitions, and revisions, that do not add propositional information to an utterance.  These can occur in spontaneous speech and are often considered normal disfluencies unless excessive. |Her overfluencies made it difficult to follow her train of thought. 她的口语不流利让人很难跟上她的思路。|Synonyms: disfluencies, nonfluencies, hesitations, fillers|Antonyms: fluency, eloquence|noun
