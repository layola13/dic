descriers:/dɪˈskraɪərz/
| n. |描述者；发现者|People who describe something or discover something, especially by careful observation.|The descriers of the new land painted vivid pictures with their words. 这些新大陆的描述者用他们的语言描绘了生动的画面。|近义词：observers, detectors, finders, narrators|反义词：concealers|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "descry," from Old French "descrier" meaning "to proclaim, announce, describe," from Late Latin "describere" meaning "to write down, copy, describe."
