ischiopodite:/ɪskiˈɒpədaɪt/| n. |坐节；坐肢基节|The ischiopodite is the sixth segment of a crustacean's leg, attaching the meropodite to the body.|The crab’s ischiopodite was injured in the fight. 这只螃蟹的坐节在打斗中受伤了。|Synonym:  basis, coxa (in some contexts)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, potentially the distal segments like dactyl or propodus depending on context)|noun
