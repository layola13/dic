enthrall:/ɪnˈθrɔːl/ | vt. | 迷住，吸引 | to captivate or charm completely; to hold spellbound | The magician's performance enthralled the audience. 魔术师的表演迷住了观众。|  The storyteller enthralled the children with his tales of adventure.  讲故事的人用他的冒险故事吸引了孩子们。 | synonyms: captivate, fascinate, mesmerize, charm, enchant | antonyms: bore, repel, disgust | verb

enthralling:/ɪnˈθrɔːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 迷人的，吸引人的 | captivating; fascinating | The enthralling performance left the audience speechless.  引人入胜的表演让观众哑口无言。|  We watched an enthralling documentary about the Amazon rainforest. 我们观看了一部关于亚马逊雨林的引人入胜的纪录片。 | synonyms: captivating, fascinating, mesmerizing, charming, enchanting | antonyms: boring, tedious, dull | adjective
