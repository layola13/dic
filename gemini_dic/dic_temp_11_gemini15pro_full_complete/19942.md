biodegradations:/ˌbaɪoʊˌdɛɡrəˈdeɪʃənz/
|n.|生物降解（过程）|The process by which organic substances are broken down into simpler substances by the action of living organisms, such as bacteria, fungi, or insects.|
|例句：Biodegradations of pollutants are crucial for environmental protection. 污染物的生物降解对环境保护至关重要。|
|例句：The company is researching new methods for biodegradations of plastic. 该公司正在研究塑料生物降解的新方法。|
|近义词：decomposition, breakdown, decay|
|反义词：synthesis, formation|
|词性：名词|
