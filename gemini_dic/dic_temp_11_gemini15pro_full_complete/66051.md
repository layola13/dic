exasperatedness:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪtɪdnəs/ | n. |恼怒，极度愤怒|The state of being exasperated; extreme annoyance or irritation.|Her exasperatedness was evident in the tight clench of her jaw. 她的恼怒从她紧咬的下巴可以明显看出。|The constant interruptions drove him to a state of exasperatedness.  不断的打断让他恼怒不已。|近义词：frustration, annoyance, irritation, vexation|反义词：calmness, patience, composure|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From exasperated + -ness.  Exasperated comes from the Latin exasperatus, past participle of exasperare "to roughen, irritate," from ex- "out" + asper "rough."
