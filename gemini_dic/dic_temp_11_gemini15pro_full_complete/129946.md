non-Malthusian: /ˌnɒn mælˈθjuːziən/ | adj. | 非马尔萨斯的 | Relating to or characteristic of a view that does not accept the Malthusian theory that population tends to increase faster than the means of subsistence. |  Many economists today hold non-Malthusian views on population growth. 今天的许多经济学家对人口增长持有非马尔萨斯主义的观点。|  The government adopted a non-Malthusian approach to family planning. 政府采取了非马尔萨斯主义的计划生育方法。 | synonyms:  neo-Malthusian (ironically), cornucopian | antonyms: Malthusian | adjective
