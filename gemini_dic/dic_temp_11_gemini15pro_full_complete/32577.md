chirographic:/ˌkaɪrəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
手写的
Of or relating to handwriting; written by hand.
Her chirographic skills were admired by all. 她的书法技巧受到所有人的钦佩。
The manuscript was a beautiful example of chirographic art. 这份手稿是书法艺术的美丽典范。
近义词:handwritten, manuscript
反义词:typed, printed
词性:adjective

chirography:/kaɪˈrɒɡrəfi/
noun
书法
Handwriting; penmanship.
His chirography was elegant and precise. 他的书法优雅而精准。
The chirography in the old letters was difficult to decipher. 旧信中的书法难以辨认。
近义词:handwriting, penmanship, calligraphy
反义词:typing, printing
词性:noun
