defaulted: /dɪˈfɔːltɪd/ | adj. | 违约的，拖欠的 | having failed to fulfill an obligation, especially to repay a loan or appear in a law court | The defaulted loan caused significant problems for the bank. 这笔违约贷款给银行造成了重大问题。 | synonyms: overdue, unpaid, delinquent | antonyms: current, paid, settled | adjective
