delimiting:/dɪˈlɪmɪtɪŋ/
adjective|限定的，界定的|Marking or defining the limits or boundaries of something.|The surveyor was tasked with delimiting the property lines. 测量员的任务是划定地产界线。|Synonyms: bounding, defining, outlining, circumscribing|Antonyms: expanding, extending, broadening|词性:形容词

delimit:/dɪˈlɪmɪt/
verb|限定，界定|To fix or mark the limits or boundaries of.|The countries delimited their shared border in the treaty.  这些国家在条约中划定了共同边界。|Synonyms: define, demarcate, bound, circumscribe|Antonyms: expand, extend, broaden|词性:动词
