enharmonical: /ɛnhɑːrˈmɒnɪkəl/ /ɛnhɑːrˈmɑːnɪkəl/
| adj. | 和声的，等音的 |  In music, relating to or denoting a note, interval, or scale that is equivalent in pitch to another but written differently.  Specifically, of or relating to enharmonic modulation. | The enharmonic equivalent of C sharp is D flat.  升C的等音是降D。| synonyms: equivalent, identical, same, uniform | antonyms: different, dissimilar, unequivalent | adjective
