indiscretionary:/ˌɪndɪˈskreʃəneri/
adjective
中文翻译：无须判断的；无需斟酌的；自主决定的
英文解析：Not requiring or subject to discretion; allowing independent judgment or action.  Often used in a financial context to describe funds or spending that is not subject to specific limitations or controls.
例句：The CEO has indiscretionary power over the company's budget. 首席执行官对公司预算拥有自主决定权。

例句：He was given an indiscretionary fund to use for emergencies. 他获得了一笔无需斟酌的紧急资金。

近义词：autonomous, independent, unrestricted, discretionary (sometimes, depending on context)
反义词：restricted, limited, controlled
词性：adjective
