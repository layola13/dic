cashability:/ˌkæʃəˈbɪləti/
n.
可兑现性；变现能力
The ease with which an asset can be converted into cash.
The cashability of this bond is very high.  这种债券的变现能力很高。
liquidity, convertibility
illiquidity, inconvertibility
noun
