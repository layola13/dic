jailbird:/ˈdʒeɪlbɜːrd/
n.
囚犯，惯犯
A person who has spent a lot of time in prison.
例句：He's a known jailbird with a long rap sheet.  他是一个臭名昭著的惯犯，犯罪记录很长。

例句：The jailbird was released after serving his sentence.  这个囚犯服刑期满后被释放了。

近义词：convict, inmate, prisoner
反义词:law-abiding citizen


ETYMOLOGY->
jail (n.) + bird (n.)
"jail" from Old French "jaiole," meaning "cage."
"bird" from Old English "brid."
The combination creates the image of someone caged like a bird.
