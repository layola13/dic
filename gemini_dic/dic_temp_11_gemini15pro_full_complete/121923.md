misshapenly:/ˌmɪsˈʃeɪpənli/
adv. |畸形地；不成形地|In a misshapen manner; deformedly.  Being twisted or bent out of the normal or natural shape. |His fingers were misshapenly curled due to arthritis. 他的手指由于关节炎而畸形地卷曲着。|The tree grew misshapenly, its branches twisted in every direction. 这棵树长得畸形，树枝向四面八方扭曲。|Synonym: deformedly, grotesquely, contortedly|Antonym: symmetrically, perfectly, regularly
词性: 副词

词源:
From misshapen + -ly. Misshapen itself comes from the Old English *mis- + shapen (past participle of shape).
