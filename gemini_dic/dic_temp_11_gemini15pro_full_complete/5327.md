allows:/əˈlaʊz/
v.
允许，准许
gives permission for something to happen
The law allows people to defend themselves. 法律允许人们自卫。

近义词:permits, enables, authorizes
反义词:forbids, prohibits, prevents
词性:动词(verb)

PHRASE->
allow for  考虑到；为…留出余地
Allow for extra time in case of traffic delays.  预留额外的时间以防交通延误。

allow of  容许；容许有...的可能
The rules allow of no exceptions.  规则不容许有任何例外。

not allow that  不承认；不认为
I do not allow that he is innocent. 我不认为他是无辜的。


as the law allows  在法律允许的范围内

allow oneself to be swayed  让自己被左右
