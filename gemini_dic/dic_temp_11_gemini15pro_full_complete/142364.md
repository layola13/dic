orthophonic:/ˌɔːrθəˈfɒnɪk/| adj.|正音的，矫正发音的|Relating to or designed to improve speech sounds; producing or reproducing sounds with high fidelity and a wide range of frequencies.|The orthophonic recordings of the 1920s offered improved sound quality.  20世纪20年代的正音唱片提供了改进的音质。|近义词：acoustic, phonetic|反义词：distorted, muffled|词性:adjective

orthophony:/ˌɔːrˈθɒfəni/| n.|正音法，矫正发音法|The art or science of correct pronunciation; the production of clear and accurate speech sounds.|Orthophony is important for singers and public speakers. 正音法对于歌手和公众演讲者来说很重要。|近义词：elocution, pronunciation|反义词：dysphonia|词性:noun
