asterophyllite:/ˈæstərəˌfɪlaɪt/
n.
星叶石
A hydrous potassium iron silicate mineral, typically brown in color with a submetallic luster, often found in nepheline syenites.
Asterophyllite is a relatively rare mineral. 星叶石是一种相对稀有的矿物。
Asterophyllite crystals can exhibit a distinctive star-like pattern. 星叶石晶体可以展现出独特的星状图案。
Synonyms: none (highly specific mineral name)
Antonyms: N/A (not applicable for mineral names)
