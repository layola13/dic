brutalizing:/ˈbruːtəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb|使残暴；使丧失人性；虐待|The war brutalized many young soldiers. 战争使许多年轻士兵变得残暴。|例句：The constant violence brutalized the entire community.  持续的暴力使整个社区丧失了人性。

例句：The prison system can brutalize even the most hardened criminals.  监狱系统甚至会使最顽固的罪犯变得更加残暴。|近义词：dehumanizing, savaging, battering|反义词：humanizing, civilizing|词性:v.


brutalized:/ˈbruːtəlaɪzd/
adjective|残暴的；丧失人性的；受虐待的|He was brutalized by his experiences in the war.  他在战争中的经历使他变得残暴。|例句：The brutalized population lived in constant fear. 受虐待的人民生活在持续的恐惧之中。

例句：She felt brutalized by the constant criticism. 她觉得不断的批评让她丧失了人性。|近义词：dehumanized, battered, traumatized|反义词：civilized, humane|词性:adj.


词源: From *brutal* (残忍的) + *-ize* (使...化) + *-ing* (现在分词/动名词后缀).  Ultimately from Latin *brutus* (dull, irrational, beastly).
