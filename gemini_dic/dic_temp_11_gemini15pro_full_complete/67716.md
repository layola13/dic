exuperation:/ɛksjʊpəˈreɪʃən/ |n.|超越，胜过，优越|The exuperation of her performance over the others was evident. 她的表演明显优于其他人。|例句：His exuperation of all difficulties was remarkable. 他克服所有困难的精神令人瞩目。

例句：The team's exuperation of their rivals led them to the championship.  团队超越了他们的对手，最终赢得了冠军。|近义词：surpassing, exceeding, transcendence, triumph|反义词： inferiority, subordination, failure|词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *exuperatio*, from the verb *exuperare* "to surpass," from *ex-* "out, beyond" + *superare* "to overcome, surpass."
