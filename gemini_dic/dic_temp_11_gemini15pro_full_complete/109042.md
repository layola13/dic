legal-sized:/ˈliːɡl saɪzd/
adjective
法律尺寸的，标准尺寸的
Of the size required or permitted by law, typically referring to paper.  Relates to the dimensions of paper commonly accepted for legal documents, typically 8.5 x 14 inches in the US.
I need some legal-sized paper for this contract.  我需要一些法律尺寸的纸来打印这份合同。
The lawyer requested all documents to be printed on legal-sized paper. 律师要求所有文件都打印在法律尺寸的纸上。
近义词:standard-sized
反义词:oversized, undersized
词性:adjective
