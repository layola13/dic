compensates:/ˈkɒmpenseɪts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpenseɪts/ (US)| v. |补偿，弥补|to give something to someone in return for something they have lost or suffered|The company compensates its employees for overtime work. 公司补偿员工加班费。|Synonyms: offset, counterbalance, make up for, atone for|Antonyms: penalize, forfeit|verb

compensate:/ˈkɒmpenseɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpenseɪt/ (US)| v. |补偿，弥补|to provide something good to balance or reduce the bad effects of something|Her enthusiasm compensates for her lack of experience. 她的热情弥补了经验的不足。|Synonyms: redress, remedy, rectify|Antonyms: worsen, aggravate|verb
