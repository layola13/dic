ectomorph:/ˈɛktəmɔːrf/| n.|瘦长型体质者|A person with a lean and delicate body build characterized by long limbs and a slight build.  This body type is often associated with difficulty gaining weight or muscle mass. |He's a classic ectomorph – tall, thin, and struggles to put on weight.|近义词:skinny, slender, lean, lanky|反义词:endomorph, mesomorph|noun
