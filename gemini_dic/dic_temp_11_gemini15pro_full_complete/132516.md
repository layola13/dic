nondeterminants:/ˌnɒndɪˈtɜːmɪnənts/ | n. | 非限定词 | Words that do not function as determiners, such as adjectives, adverbs, and nouns.  They do not specify or limit the noun they modify in the same way determiners do. | Example: "Beautiful flowers" - "beautiful" is a nondeterminant; it describes the flowers but doesn't specify which ones.  美丽的鲜花 | Synonym: non-determiners, modifiers | Antonym: determiners | noun


nondeterminant:/ˌnɒndɪˈtɜːmɪnənt/ | adj. | 非限定的 | Not serving to determine or fix something; not decisive.  Often used in computer science and logic to describe systems or processes that can have multiple possible outcomes or paths. | Example: "A nondeterminant algorithm can produce different results on each run." 一个非确定性算法每次运行可能会产生不同的结果。| Synonym: indeterminate, unspecified, unfixed | Antonym: determinant, definite, fixed | adjective
