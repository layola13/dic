custombuilt:/ˈkʌstəm bɪlt/ | adj. | 定制的，按客户要求制造的 |  Designed and built to a customer's specifications, rather than being mass-produced. |  We had a custombuilt bookcase made to fit the alcove.  我们定制了一个书柜来放壁龛。 | bespoke, tailor-made | mass-produced | adjective

Etymology:
"custom" (from Old French "costume," meaning "habit, practice") + "built" (past participle of "build," from Old English "byldan," meaning "to construct")
