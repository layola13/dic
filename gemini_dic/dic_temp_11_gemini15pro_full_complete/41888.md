cosier:/ˈkoʊziər (US), ˈkəʊziər (UK)| adj.|更舒适的，更惬意的|Comparative form of cosy/cozy: more cosy/cozy.  Meaning warm, comfortable, and inviting, often suggesting a feeling of intimacy and relaxation.|The room was made cosier by the soft lighting and warm fire. 房间里的柔和灯光和温暖的火炉使它更加舒适。|Synonyms: snug, comfortable, warm, inviting, homely|Antonyms: uncomfortable, cold, uninviting, bleak|adjective


cosy:/ˈkoʊzi (US), ˈkəʊzi (UK)| adj.|舒适的，惬意的|Giving a feeling of comfort, warmth, and relaxation.|It was a cosy little cottage with a fireplace. 这是一间舒适的小屋，带有一个壁炉。|Synonyms: snug, comfortable, warm, inviting, homely|Antonyms: uncomfortable, cold, uninviting, bleak|adjective
