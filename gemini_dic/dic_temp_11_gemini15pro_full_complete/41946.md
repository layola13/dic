cosmopolitical:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən/ | /ˌkɑːzməˈpɑːlətən/ | adj. | 世界性的，全球公民的，四海为家的 | Belonging to all the world; not limited to just one part of the world.  Free from local, provincial, or national ideas, prejudices, or attachments. |  She has a very cosmopolitan outlook on life. 她有着非常世界性的生活观。|  例句：Living in New York City gives you a cosmopolitan experience.  住在纽约市会给你一种世界性的体验。|  近义词：international, global, worldly, universal | 反义词：provincial, parochial, insular | 词性: adjective

cosmopolite:/ˈkɒzməpəlaɪt/ | /ˈkɑːzməpəlaɪt/ | n. | 世界公民，四海为家的人 | A person who is free from local, provincial, or national prejudices or attachments; a citizen of the world. | He considered himself a cosmopolite and felt at home anywhere in the world. 他认为自己是一个世界公民，在世界任何地方都感到宾至如归。 |  例句：The hotel attracts cosmopolites from all over the world. 这家酒店吸引了来自世界各地的世界公民。| 近义词：internationalist, global citizen | 反义词：local, provincial | 词性: noun

词源:
From Greek *kosmopolítēs* "citizen of the world," from *kósmos* "world, universe" + *polítēs* "citizen."
