capo:/ˈkɑːpoʊ/ (US), /ˈkæpoʊ/ (UK)| n.|变调夹; 头目; (黑手党)头目|A device used on stringed instruments, especially guitars and banjos, to shorten the effective length of the strings, thus raising the pitch.|He used a capo on the third fret to play the song in a different key. 他在第三品使用了变调夹，以便用不同的调来演奏这首歌。|近义词：clamp|反义词: N/A|noun
