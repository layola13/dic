Maastricht: /ˈmɑːstrɪxt/ (UK), /ˈmɑːstrɪxt/ (US) | n. | 马斯特里赫特 |  A city in the Netherlands, the capital of the province of Limburg. It is known for its historical architecture, including the St. Servatius Basilica and the Helpoort city gate, and for the signing of the Maastricht Treaty in 1992, which led to the creation of the European Union. | Maastricht is a beautiful city with a rich history. 马斯特里赫特是一个拥有丰富历史的美丽城市。|  Limburg, city, capital | None | proper noun
