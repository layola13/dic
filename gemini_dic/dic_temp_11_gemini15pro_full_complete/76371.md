gadman:/ˈɡædˌmæn/ | n. | 矿工；煤矿工人 | A man who works in a gad (a pointed iron bar used for breaking up rock or coal). |  He was a gadman, working long hours in the dangerous coal mine. 他是一个矿工，在危险的煤矿里长时间工作。|  miner, collier |  surface worker (arguably) | noun

Etymology: From "gad" (a pointed iron bar) + "man" (a male person).  "Gad" likely comes from Old Norse "gaddr" (spike, nail).
