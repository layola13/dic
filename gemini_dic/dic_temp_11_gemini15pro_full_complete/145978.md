overdocument:/ˌoʊvərˈdɑːkjəmənt/ | vt. | 过度记录；提供过多文件 | To provide excessive documentation or evidence, often to the point of redundancy. | The lawyer overdocumented the case, submitting hundreds of pages of unnecessary exhibits. 律师过度记录了这个案子，提交了数百页不必要的证据。|  He overdocumented his travel expenses, providing receipts for every single purchase. 他过度记录了他的旅行费用，提供了每一笔购买的收据。 |近义词: overprove, overdo | 反义词: underdocument |词性:动词

overdocument:/ˌoʊvərˈdɑːkjəmənt/ | n. | 过度记录；过多文件 | Excessive documentation or evidence, often redundant. | The committee was overwhelmed by the overdocumentation submitted for the proposal. 委员会对提交的提案的过度记录感到不知所措。 | The overdocumentation made it difficult to find the key information. 过多的文件使得难以找到关键信息。 | 近义词: overkill, redundancy | 反义词: insufficient documentation | 词性:名词
