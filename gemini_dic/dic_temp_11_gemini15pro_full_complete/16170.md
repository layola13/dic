bankers:/ˈbæŋkərz/| n.|银行家|A person who owns or manages a bank or group of banks.  A person employed by a bank, especially in a senior role. | The bankers met to discuss the financial crisis. 银行家们开会讨论金融危机。|The bankers approved the loan application. 银行家们批准了贷款申请。|近义词：financier, investor, lender|反义词：borrower, debtor|Noun
