demission:/dɪˈmɪʃən/| n.|辞职，退位，辞去|The minister announced his demission from the cabinet. 部长宣布辞去内阁职务。|resignation, abdication|appointment, accession|noun
Etymology: From Late Middle English *demission*, from Late Latin *demissionem*, accusative singular of *demissio* (“lowering, a letting down, sinking, abatement, diminution”), from Latin *dēmissus*, past participle of *dēmittō* (“I send down, lower, let down, let fall, drop, dismiss, discharge, resign”), from *dē* (“down, away”) + *mittō* (“I send”).
