blastula:/ˈblæstʃʊlə/ (US), /ˈblæstjʊlə/ (UK)| n. |囊胚|An early stage of embryonic development in animals, consisting of a hollow sphere of cells surrounding a fluid-filled cavity called the blastocoel.|The blastula stage follows the morula stage and precedes the gastrula stage. 囊胚阶段在桑椹胚阶段之后，原肠胚阶段之前。|Synonyms: blastosphere|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

blastula:/ˈblæstʃʊlə/ (US), /ˈblæstjʊlə/ (UK)| n. |囊胚|A hollow sphere of cells, formed during an early stage of embryonic development in animals.|The blastula is formed by cleavage of the fertilized ovum and contains a fluid-filled cavity called the blastocoel.  囊胚由受精卵分裂形成，包含一个充满液体的腔，称为囊胚腔。|Synonyms: blastocyst (specifically in mammals)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
