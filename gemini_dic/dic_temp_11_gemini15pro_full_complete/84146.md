half-nylon:/ˌhæf ˈnaɪlɒn/ | adj. | 半尼龙的 | Made of a blend of nylon and another material, typically cotton or another natural fiber.  A fabric composed partly of nylon. |  This jacket is half-nylon, half-cotton, so it's both durable and comfortable. 这件夹克是半尼龙半棉的，所以既耐用又舒适。| The tent is made of a half-nylon material. 这个帐篷是用一种半尼龙材料做的。|  part-nylon, semi-nylon | full-nylon, all-nylon | adjective
