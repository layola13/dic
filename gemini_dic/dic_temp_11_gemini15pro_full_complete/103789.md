一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Les jours passent vite. = The days pass quickly.
Il travaille tous les jours. = He works every day.


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
(Not applicable to "jours" as it is a noun and doesn't typically take continuous tenses.  Focus on describing actions happening during the days)
Il est en train de travailler pendant ces jours. = He is currently working during these days.
Elle profite de ses jours de vacances. = She's enjoying her vacation days.


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
J'ai travaillé pendant plusieurs jours. = I have worked for several days.
Il a passé trois jours à Londres. = He has spent three days in London.


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
(Not typically used with "jours" - focus on activities occurring over a period of days)
Il a travaillé dur ces derniers jours. = He has been working hard these last few days.
Elle a étudié sans relâche pendant des jours. = She has been studying tirelessly for days.


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Les jours étaient longs et chauds. = The days were long and hot.
Il passa deux jours à la campagne. = He spent two days in the countryside.


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
(Focus on what was happening during the days)
Pendant ces jours-là, il travaillait à la ferme. = During those days, he was working on the farm.
Elle lisait un livre pendant ces jours pluvieux. = She was reading a book during those rainy days.


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Il avait passé plusieurs jours à Rome avant de venir à Paris. = He had spent several days in Rome before coming to Paris.
Elle avait travaillé pendant des jours avant de terminer son projet. = She had worked for days before finishing her project.



过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

(Focus on activities happening leading up to a point in the past)

Il avait travaillé dur pendant des jours avant la présentation. = He had been working hard for days before the presentation.

Elle avait étudié pendant des jours avant l'examen. = She had been studying for days before the exam.




一般将来时 (Simple Future):

Les jours seront plus longs en été. = The days will be longer in summer.


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
(Describe actions that will be ongoing during future days)

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
(Not commonly used with "jours")
