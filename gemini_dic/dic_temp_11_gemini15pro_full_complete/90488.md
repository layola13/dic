huchen:/ˈhuːkən/| n. |哲罗鲑|A large salmonid fish (Hucho hucho) native to the Danube River basin in Europe. It is one of the largest salmonids in the world and is considered a prized game fish.|The huchen is a powerful predator, feeding on other fish.|近义词：Danube salmon, hucho|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From German Huchen, from Middle High German hūchen, from Old High German *huohho. Ultimately of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Czech hlavatka (“chub”), from Slavic *glava (“head”).
