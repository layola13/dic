density:/ˈdɛnsɪti/ | n. |密度; 稠密; 浓度| The degree of compactness of a substance. | The density of the population in urban areas is much higher than in rural areas. 城市地区的人口密度比农村地区高得多。|  The forest has a high density of trees. 这片森林的树木密度很高。 |  thickness, compactness | sparseness, thinness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *densitas, from Latin densus (“dense, thick”) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).
