camouflaged:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒd/
|adj.|伪装的，被掩饰的|Disguised or concealed by camouflage.  Having the appearance of something else.|The soldiers were camouflaged in green and brown uniforms. 士兵们穿着绿棕相间的制服进行伪装。|近义词：disguised, concealed, hidden|反义词：exposed, visible, revealed|词性:形容词
camouflage: /ˈkæməˌflɑːʒ/
|n. vt.|伪装，掩饰|The disguising of military personnel, equipment, and installations by painting or covering them to make them blend in with their surroundings; the act of concealing the identity of something.|The soldiers used camouflage to blend in with the forest. 士兵们用伪装与森林融为一体。|近义词：disguise, concealment, cover|反义词：exposure, revelation|词性:名词,动词
