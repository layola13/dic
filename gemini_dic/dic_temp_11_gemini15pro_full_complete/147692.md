overpraise: /ˌoʊvərˈpreɪz/ | vt. | 过度赞扬；过分夸奖 | To praise excessively or beyond what is deserved. | He overpraised his son's mediocre performance in the school play. 他过度赞扬了他儿子在学校戏剧中的平庸表现。

The media overpraised the new phone, making it seem much better than it actually was. 媒体过分夸奖了这款新手机，使其看起来比实际好得多。

近义词: exaggerate, laud, overrate | 反义词: criticize, understate, deprecate | 词性: verb (动词)
