nonembryonal:/ˌnɒnˈɛmbriənəl/ | adj. | 非胚胎的 | Not pertaining to, derived from, or characteristic of an embryo.  Relating to tissues or structures that are not part of the embryo proper. |  Nonembryonal carcinoma is a type of germ cell tumor. 非胚胎癌是一种生殖细胞肿瘤。|  The nonembryonal parts of the placenta provide nourishment to the developing fetus. 胎盘的非胚胎部分为发育中的胎儿提供营养。 | extraembryonic | embryonic | adjective
