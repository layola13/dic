immunotherapy:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊˈθɛrəpi/| n.|免疫疗法|Treatment that uses certain parts of a person's immune system to fight diseases such as cancer.|例句：Immunotherapy has shown promising results in treating some types of cancer. 免疫疗法在治疗某些类型的癌症方面已显示出良好的效果。|例句：He's undergoing immunotherapy for his lymphoma. 他正在接受淋巴瘤的免疫疗法。|近义词：biological therapy, biotherapy|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
immuno- (from Latin immunis "exempt, free, safe") + -therapy (from Greek therapeia "healing, medical treatment")
