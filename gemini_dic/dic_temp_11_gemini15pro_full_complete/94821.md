impunity:/ɪmˈpjuːnɪti/
n.
豁免，免责
freedom from punishment or from the unpleasant consequences of something
The dictator ruled with impunity, his crimes going unpunished.  独裁者肆无忌惮地统治着，他的罪行没有受到惩罚。

He continued to flout the law with apparent impunity. 他继续公然藐视法律而显然未受到惩罚。


近义词:exemption, immunity, freedom from punishment
反义词:liability, accountability, responsibility
词性:noun
