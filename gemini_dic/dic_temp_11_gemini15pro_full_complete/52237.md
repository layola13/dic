diazeuctic:/ˌdaɪəˈzuːktɪk/
adjective
分离的，不相连的
separating, disjunctive; denoting a compound in which the constituent elements are felt to be independent of each other, or a style marked by such compounds.
Diazeuctic compounds retain a clear sense of the individual words within them, unlike synthetic compounds. For example, "bittersweet" is synthetic, while "blue-green" is diazeuctic.
近义词: disjunctive, distinct, separate
反义词: synthetic, combined, unified
词性: adjective
