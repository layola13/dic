non-rhotic:/ˌnɒnˈrɒtɪk/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈroʊtɪk/ (US)| adj.|非卷舌音的|Relating to or denoting a variety of English (such as British Received Pronunciation or Boston English) in which r is not pronounced after vowels.|Her accent is non-rhotic, so she doesn't pronounce the 'r' in 'car'.|她的口音是非卷舌音的，所以她在“car”这个词中不发“r”音。|non-r-pronouncing|rhotic|adjective
