boy-o:/ˈbɔɪ oʊ/
interjection
小子；伙计；（表示惊讶、赞叹等）
An exclamation of surprise, delight, or emphasis, often used to address a male.  It's a more informal and sometimes condescending term.  Primarily used in Hiberno-English (Irish English).
例句：Boy-o, that was a close call!  小子，那真是千钧一发！
例句：Boy-o, you've grown!  伙计，你长大了！
近义词：man, fellow, chap, lad
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's an interjection)
词性: interjection
