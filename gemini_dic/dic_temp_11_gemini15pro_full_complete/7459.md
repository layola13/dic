An order of crustaceans that includes woodlice and pill bugs.
These small crustaceans belong to the order Isopoda. 这些小型甲壳类动物属于等足目。

Isopods, like those in the order Anisopoda, play important roles in the ecosystem. 像等足目中的那些等足类动物一样，在生态系统中扮演着重要的角色。
