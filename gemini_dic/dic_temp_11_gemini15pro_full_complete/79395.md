gliff:/ɡlɪf/
noun|雕文，象形文字|A carved or inscribed symbol, especially one used in ancient writing systems.|Example: The archaeologists discovered ancient gliffs on the cave walls. 考古学家在洞穴墙壁上发现了古代雕文。
Example:  The gliffs on the tombstone told a story of a great warrior. 墓碑上的雕文讲述了一位伟大战士的故事。
|synonym:  symbol, character, inscription, hieroglyph|antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider "plain surface" or "blank space")
词性: 名词

词源:
Likely a variant of "glyph," which comes from the French "glyphe" and ultimately from the Greek "gluphē," meaning "carving."
