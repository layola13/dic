nonwriter:/ˈnɒnˌraɪtər/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˌraɪtər/| n.|非作家，不写作的人|A person who does not write, especially as a profession.|He's a nonwriter, he prefers to express himself through painting. 他不是作家，他更喜欢通过绘画来表达自己。|non-author, non-scribe|writer, author|noun
Etymology->
From non- + writer.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without," originating from Latin. "Writer" comes from the Old English "writere," meaning "one who writes."
