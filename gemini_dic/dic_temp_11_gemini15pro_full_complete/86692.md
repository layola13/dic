helloes:/həˈləʊz/ /hɛˈloʊz/ | n. | 你好（复数）, 问候语（复数）| A plural form of hello, used to greet two or more people.  While less common than "hello," it maintains the same core meaning. | Helloes, everyone, welcome to the meeting. 各位好，欢迎来到会议。| Helloes, ladies and gentlemen, thank you for coming. 女士们先生们，你们好，感谢你们的到来。| 近义词：greetings, hi, hey | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a greeting) | noun

注意事项:
Helloes is a less common plural form of hello.  While grammatically correct, it is less frequently used than simply saying "hello" to multiple people.  In modern English, "hello everyone" or "hello all" are more common.
