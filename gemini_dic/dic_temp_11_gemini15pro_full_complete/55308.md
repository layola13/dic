dissolvablenesses:/dɪˈzɒlvəblənəsɪz/
n.
可溶性，溶解性
The quality or state of being dissolvable; the capacity to be dissolved.
The dissolvablenesses of the various compounds were tested in the lab.  这些化合物的溶解性在实验室里进行了测试。
Near synonyms: solubility, dissolvability
Antonyms: insolubility, indissolubility
noun
