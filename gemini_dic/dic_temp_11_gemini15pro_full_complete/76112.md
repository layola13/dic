furthering:/ˈfɜːrðərɪŋ/
verb
推进，促进
the act of promoting or advancing something.
例句：He dedicated his life to furthering the cause of education. 他毕生致力于推进教育事业。

例句：She's furthering her studies by taking online courses. 她通过上网课来继续深造。

近义词：advancing, promoting, developing, enhancing, progressing
反义词：hindering, obstructing, impeding, delaying, halting
词性：v.
