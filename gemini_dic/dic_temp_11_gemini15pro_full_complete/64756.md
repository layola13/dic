escrow:/ˈɛskrəʊ/ (UK) /ˈɛskroʊ/ (US)| n.| 第三方托管，暂管;  |Something of value, such as a deed, money, or piece of property, held by a disinterested third party (called an escrow agent, escrow holder, or escrowee) until the completion of a specified condition or event.  |The buyer's deposit is held in escrow until the sale is completed.  买方的定金由第三方保管，直到交易完成。| synonyms: bond, deposit, guarantee | antonyms: debt, liability | noun

escrow:/ˈɛskrəʊ/ (UK) /ˈɛskroʊ/ (US)| vt.|  托管，暂管 |To place (something) in escrow.  |The funds were escrowed until the deal was finalized.  资金被托管直到交易最终完成。|synonyms: hold, deposit, safeguard | antonyms: release, distribute | verb
