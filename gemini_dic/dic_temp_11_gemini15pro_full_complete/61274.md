emasculative: /ɪˈmæskjʊˌleɪtɪv/
adjective
使人丧失男子气概的；阉割的
Depriving a man of his male role or identity; making someone feel less of a man.
He found the constant criticism from his boss emasculative. 他觉得老板不断的批评使他丧失了男子气概。
His new job made him feel emasculative, as he had no real authority. 他的新工作让他感觉很没有男子气概，因为他没有任何实权。
emasculating, unmanly, weakening
empowering, strengthening
