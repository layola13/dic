ascetically:/əˈsɛtɪkli/
adverb|禁欲地，苦行地|Characterized by or suggesting the practice of severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons.|He lived ascetically in a small cabin in the woods. 他在森林里的小木屋里过着苦行僧般的生活。|
近义词: austerely, rigorously, simply, abstemiously
反义词: luxuriously, indulgently, hedonistically
词性: adverb

ascetic:/əˈsɛtɪk/
adjective|禁欲的，苦行的|Characterized by or suggesting the practice of severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons.|The monk led an ascetic life, dedicating himself to prayer and fasting.  这位僧侣过着苦行僧的生活，致力于祈祷和禁食。|
近义词: austere, spartan, abstemious
反义词: luxurious, indulgent, hedonistic
词性: adjective

ascetic:/əˈsɛtɪk/
noun|禁欲者，苦行者|A person who practices severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons.|The ascetic lived a life of poverty and solitude.  这位苦行者过着贫困和孤独的生活。|
近义词: hermit, recluse, monk
反义词: hedonist, sybarite
词性: noun


词源:  From the Greek *askētikos* "laborious, athletic," from *askēsis* "exercise, training," later "ascetic practice," from *askein* "to exercise, train."
