cerebel:/ˈsɛrəbɛl/| n. |小脑|The cerebellum is a part of the brain located at the back of the head, responsible for coordinating voluntary movements, balance, and posture. |Damage to the cerebellum can result in impaired coordination and balance. 小脑损伤会导致协调性和平衡性受损。|Synonyms: hindbrain (part of)|Antonyms: None|Noun
