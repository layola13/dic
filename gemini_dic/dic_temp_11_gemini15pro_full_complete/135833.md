nonproportional:/ˌnɒnprəˈpɔːʃənəl/ | adj. | 非比例的，不成比例的 | Not proportional; not increasing or decreasing in size, amount, or degree at the same rate. |  Example: The pay raise was nonproportional to the amount of work done.  加薪与工作量不成比例。| Synonyms: disproportionate, unequal, unbalanced | Antonyms: proportional, proportionate, commensurate | Adjective
