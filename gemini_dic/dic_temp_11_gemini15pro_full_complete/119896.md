militarization:/ˌmɪlɪtərəˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|军事化|The process of organizing something or someone for military purposes.  The act of equipping with military forces and defenses.|例句：The militarization of the police force has been a controversial issue. 警察部队的军事化一直是一个有争议的问题。|近义词：armament, buildup|反义词：demobilization, disarmament|词性: 名词

词源: From military + -ize + -ation.  Ultimately from Latin *militaris* "of a soldier."
