ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *epitomizare, from Ancient Greek ἐπιτομίζειν (epitomízein, “to abridge, summarize”), from ἐπιτέμνειν (epitémnein, “to cut short”), from ἐπί (epí, “on, upon”) + τέμνειν (témnein, “to cut”).
