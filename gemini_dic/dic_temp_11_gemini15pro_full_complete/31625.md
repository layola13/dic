chatbot:/ˈtʃætˌbɑːt/ (US) /ˈtʃætˌbɒt/ (UK)| n. |聊天机器人|A computer program designed to simulate conversation with human users, especially over the Internet.|例句：The chatbot answered my questions instantly. 聊天机器人立即回答了我的问题。|例句：Many companies are using chatbots for customer service. 许多公司正在使用聊天机器人来进行客户服务。|近义词：virtual assistant, conversational AI, automated assistant|反义词:human, live chat|词性:名词

Etymology:  A blend of "chat" and "robot". "Chat" comes from the late 19th century, shortened from "chatter," meaning to talk rapidly or idly. "Robot" comes from the Czech word "robota," meaning "forced labor," coined by Karel Čapek in his 1920 play R.U.R.
