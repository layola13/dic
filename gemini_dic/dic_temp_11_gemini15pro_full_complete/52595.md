diagenesis:/ˌdaɪəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.|成岩作用|The physical and chemical changes occurring during the conversion of sediment to sedimentary rock.|Diagenesis transforms loose sediment into hard rock. 成岩作用将松散的沉积物转化为坚硬的岩石。|lithification, cementation|metamorphism|noun

diagenesis:/ˌdaɪəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.|成岩作用|The process by which sediments are lithified into sedimentary rocks, involving compaction, cementation, recrystallization, and other chemical reactions.|The diagenesis of the sand resulted in sandstone. 沙子的成岩作用形成了砂岩。|compaction, consolidation|metamorphism|noun
