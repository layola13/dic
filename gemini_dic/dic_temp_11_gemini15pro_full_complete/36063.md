coedited:/ˌkoʊˈɛdɪtɪd/
verb
共同编辑
having been edited by two or more people.
This book was coedited by two prominent scholars in the field. 这本书是由该领域的两位杰出学者共同编辑的。
co-authored, jointly edited
solely edited
