endocrines: /ˈɛndəkrɪnz/ | n. | 内分泌物；激素 | Substances (such as hormones) secreted by endocrine glands.  These substances are released directly into the bloodstream and regulate various bodily functions. |  The doctor checked the patient's endocrines to understand the hormonal imbalance. 医生检查了病人的内分泌状况以了解激素失衡的原因。|  The endocrines play a vital role in growth and development. 内分泌物在生长发育中起着至关重要的作用。 | synonyms: hormones,  secretions | antonyms: exocrines | noun
