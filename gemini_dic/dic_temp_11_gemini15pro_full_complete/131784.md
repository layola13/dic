nonconnotative:/ˌnɒnkənˈɒtətɪv/ | /ˌnɑːnkɑːnˈoʊtətɪv/ | adj. | 非内涵的；无隐义的 |  Not suggestive of any additional meaning or emotional overtones beyond the literal, explicit meaning.  | The word "table" is nonconnotative; it simply refers to a piece of furniture. “桌子”这个词是非内涵的；它仅仅指一件家具。 | Synonyms: denotative, literal, objective | Antonyms: connotative, suggestive, evocative | Word type: Adjective
