outshut:/ˌaʊtˈʃʌt/
Verb
关在外面，排除在外，隔绝
To shut out; to exclude; to bar from a place or from privileges.
The heavy snowstorm had outshut them from the world. 暴风雪将他们与世隔绝。
exclude, bar, shut out
admit, include, let in
