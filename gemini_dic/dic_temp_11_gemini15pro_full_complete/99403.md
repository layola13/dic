interdisciplinary:/ˌɪntərˈdɪsəplɪnəri/
adjective
跨学科的，跨领域的
involving two or more academic, scientific, or artistic disciplines.
例句：The university offers an interdisciplinary program in environmental studies. 这所大学提供一个环境研究的跨学科项目。

例句：Interdisciplinary research is becoming increasingly important in addressing complex societal challenges.  跨学科研究在应对复杂的社会挑战方面变得越来越重要。

近义词：multidisciplinary, cross-disciplinary
反义词：specialized, unidisciplinary
词性: adjective
