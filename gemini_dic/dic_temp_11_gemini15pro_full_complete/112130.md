英文解析: An informal British term for a toilet or lavatory.  Derived from the French phrase "garde à l'eau" (watch out for the water), which was shouted as wastewater was thrown from windows. Over time, the phrase was shortened and Anglicized.

例句：I'm just popping to the loo. 我就去一下洗手间。
