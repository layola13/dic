absolutory:/ˌæbsəˈluːtəri/
adjective
赦免的；免罪的
Serving to absolve; containing or implying absolution.
The jury delivered an absolutory verdict. 陪审团作出了无罪判决。
近义词:  exonerative, acquitting, freeing, clearing
反义词:  incriminating, condemning
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *absolutorius, from Latin absolūtus, past participle of absolvere (“to absolve”).  Surface analysis is ab- + solūtus (“loosened, released”) + -ōrius.
