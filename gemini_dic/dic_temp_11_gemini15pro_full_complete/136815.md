nonshrinkable:/ˌnɒnˈʃrɪŋkəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈʃrɪŋkəbl/ (UK)| adj.|不可缩水的，防缩的|Not able to shrink or be shrunk; resistant to shrinking.  Made of a material that will not shrink when washed or heated.|This shirt is nonshrinkable, so you can wash it in hot water. 这件衬衫是不可缩水的，所以你可以用热水洗。|The wool is specially treated to be nonshrinkable.  这种羊毛经过特殊处理，可以防缩。|近义词：preshrunk, washable, non-contracting|反义词: shrinkable|词性:形容词
