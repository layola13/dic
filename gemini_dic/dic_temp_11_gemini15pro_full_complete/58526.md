duff:/dʌf/
n.|屁股；残次品；发酵的面团；(尤指徒步旅行或野餐时携带的)食物|Something useless or of inferior quality; In baking, the mixture of flour, etc., that forms the basis of bread, cakes, etc.; Food, supplies. |The movie was a complete duff. 这部电影完全是部烂片。|Synonyms: rubbish, trash, garbage, junk, duds|Antonyms: treasure, gem, masterpiece|Noun

duff:/dʌf/
adj.|无用的；残次的|Useless or of inferior quality.|The engine was duff. 引擎坏了。|Synonyms: faulty, defective, broken, useless|Antonyms: functional, working, useful|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Perhaps related to Low German duff "dough," or to Dutch doof "deaf, dull, stale."
