imperishability:/ˌɪmpɛrɪʃəˈbɪləti/| n. |不朽，永恒，不灭|The quality of being imperishable; inability to decay or be destroyed.|His poems speak of the imperishability of love. 他的诗歌歌颂了爱情的永恒。|近义词：immortality, permanence, eternity, indestructibility|反义词：mortality, perishability, transience|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *imperishabilitas, from imperishabilis (“imperishable”), from in- (“not”) + perire (“to perish”) + -abilis (“-able”).
