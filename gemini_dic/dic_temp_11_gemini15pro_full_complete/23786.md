brawniest:/ˈbrɔːnɪɪst/
adjective
最强壮的；最结实的
Physically the strongest, most muscular, or most powerful.
例句：He was the brawniest man in the village. 他是村子里最强壮的男人。

例句：She had brawny arms from years of working on the farm. 多年在农场工作使她拥有强壮的胳膊。

近义词：strongest, most muscular, most powerful, burliest, sturdiest
反义词：weakest, frailest, most feeble

词性: adjective


brawny:/ˈbrɔːni/
adjective
强壮的；结实的
Physically strong, muscular, and powerful.
例句：He was a brawny blacksmith with powerful arms. 他是一个强壮的铁匠，有着强壮的胳膊。

例句：The brawny lumberjack easily lifted the heavy log. 强壮的伐木工人轻松地举起了沉重的原木。

近义词：strong, muscular, powerful, burly, sturdy
反义词：weak, frail, feeble

词性: adjective


brawn:/brɔːn/
noun
强壮的肌肉；体力
Physical strength and muscular power.
词源：From Old French *braon (“fleshy part of the arm”), from Germanic *braχwan (“arm”). Cognate with Dutch brawn.
例句：He relied on brawn rather than brains to get the job done. 他依靠体力而不是智力来完成这项工作。

例句：She admired the brawn of the weightlifters. 她钦佩举重运动员的强壮体格。

近义词：strength, muscle, power, might
反义词：weakness, frailty, feebleness

词性: noun
