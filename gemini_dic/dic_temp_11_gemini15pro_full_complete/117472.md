medivacing:/ˈmɛdɪvækɪŋ/ | v. |  医疗后送，用直升机或飞机运送伤员或病人 |  The soldier was medivaced out of the combat zone.  这名士兵被用直升机从战区后送出去。| Example: They were medivacing the injured climber off the mountain.  他们正在用直升机将受伤的登山者从山上运送下来。 | Synonyms: evacuating, airlifting | Antonyms: N/A | Verb

medivac:/ˈmɛdɪvæk/ | n. | 医疗后送，医疗后送飞机或直升机 | The medivac arrived quickly and transported the patient to the hospital. 医疗后送飞机迅速抵达，并将病人送往医院。| Example:  The medivac landed on the hospital roof. 医疗后送直升机降落在医院屋顶上。 | Synonyms: air ambulance, medical transport | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
