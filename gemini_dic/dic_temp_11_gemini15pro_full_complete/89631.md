hookable:/ˈhʊkəbl/
adjective
可钩住的；易上瘾的；引人入胜的
Able to be hooked or fastened with a hook; easily addicted; captivating.
The fish were hookable that day. 那天鱼很容易上钩。
The plot of the novel was so hookable that I couldn't put it down. 这部小说的情节引人入胜，我爱不释手。
Synonyms: catching, addictive, engaging, gripping
Antonyms: boring, tedious, uninteresting
词性: adjective
