navigable:/ˈnævɪɡəbl/| adj.|可航行的，可通航的|Suitable for sailing or traveling on water.|The river is navigable for small boats. 这条河适合小船航行。|近义词：passable, traversable|反义词：impassable, unnavigable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin navigabilis, from Latin navigare "to sail," from navis "ship" + agere "to drive, lead, do."
