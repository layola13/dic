engulfing:/ɪnˈɡʌlfɪŋ/ | adj. | 吞没的，席卷的 |  completely surrounding or covering something, especially in a threatening way | The flames engulfed the building.  火焰吞没了建筑物。| synonyms: enveloping, immersing, overwhelming | antonyms: releasing, freeing, exposing | word type: adjective

engulf:/ɪnˈɡʌlf/ | vt. | 吞没，席卷 | to surround and cover something completely | The tsunami engulfed the coastal villages. 海啸吞没了沿海村庄。| synonyms:  submerge, overwhelm, consume | antonyms:  release, free, expose | word type: verb
