miasma: /maɪˈæzmə/ /miˈæzmə/ | n. | 瘴气，臭气，不良气氛 | A heavy, vaporous, or unhealthy atmosphere. An oppressive or unpleasant atmosphere. | The miasma of despair settled over the town after the factory closed. 工厂关闭后，绝望的气氛笼罩着整个城镇。

The swamp gave off a miasma of decaying vegetation. 沼泽散发出一股腐烂植物的臭气。 | 近义词: stench, stink, reek, fetor, odor, aroma (negative connotation) | 反义词: fragrance, perfume, scent (positive connotation) | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Late Latin miasma, from Greek miasma "stain, pollution," from miainein "to stain," from root *mei- "to change, go, move."
