hippie:/ˈhɪpi/| adj.|嬉皮的，嬉皮士风格的|Characteristic of a hippie or the hippie movement.|They lived a hippie lifestyle. 他们过着嬉皮士般的生活方式。|synonyms: bohemian, unconventional, counter-cultural|antonyms: conventional, traditional, conformist|adjective
