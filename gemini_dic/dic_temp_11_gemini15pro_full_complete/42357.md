counterargues:/ˈkaʊntərˌɑːrɡjuz/
|v.|反驳，提出相反的论点|presents an opposing argument; argues against something|
He counterargues that the evidence is not conclusive. 他反驳说证据不确凿。
She skillfully counterargued his points, leaving him speechless. 她巧妙地反驳了他的观点，让他哑口无言。
|近义词：rebuts, disputes, challenges, contradicts|
|反义词：supports, agrees, confirms|
|词性：动词|
