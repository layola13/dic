antiradiant:/ˌæntiˈreɪdiənt/| adj.|抗辐射的|Preventing or reducing the harmful effects of radiation.  It can describe materials, clothing, or other measures designed to protect against radiation.|例句：They wore antiradiant suits while working in the contaminated area. 他们在污染区域工作时穿着防辐射服。

例句：The new phone case boasts antiradiant properties.  这款新的手机壳号称具有防辐射特性。|近义词：radiation-proof, radioprotective|反义词:radioactive|词性:形容词
