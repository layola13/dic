liable:/ˈlaɪəbl/| adj.|有责任的，有义务的；易于…的，倾向于…的|Subject to or likely to incur something undesirable, such as legal obligation, penalty, burden, or misfortune. Also, susceptible or prone to something.|例句：He is liable for his wife's debts. 他对妻子的债务负有责任。|近义词：responsible, accountable, prone, susceptible|反义词：exempt, immune|词性:形容词

liable:/ˈlaɪəbl/| adj.|有责任的，有义务的；易于…的，倾向于…的|Likely to do or to be something.|例句：The area is liable to flooding. 这个地区容易发生洪水。|近义词：likely, apt, inclined|反义词：unlikely|词性:形容词
