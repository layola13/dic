heterozygote: /ˌhɛtərəˈzaɪɡoʊt/ (US) /ˌhɛtərəˈzaɪɡɒt/ (UK)| n. | 杂合子 | An organism that has two different alleles for a particular gene. |  Example:  The heterozygote for eye color might have one allele for brown eyes and one for blue eyes.  杂合子的眼睛颜色可能有一个棕色眼睛的等位基因和一个蓝色眼睛的等位基因。

Example: In a simple dominant/recessive scenario, the heterozygote will express the dominant phenotype.  在一个简单的显性/隐性情况下，杂合子将表达显性表型。 | 近义词: hybrid | 反义词: homozygote | 词性: noun

heterozygotes: /ˌhɛtərəˈzaɪɡoʊts/ (US) /ˌhɛtərəˈzaɪɡɒts/ (UK) | n. | 杂合子（复数） | Plural form of heterozygote. Refers to multiple organisms that have two different alleles for a particular gene. | Example: The study focused on the offspring of heterozygotes. 这项研究的重点是杂合子的后代。

Example: Heterozygotes for the sickle cell trait have some resistance to malaria. 镰状细胞性状的杂合子对疟疾有一定的抵抗力。| 近义词: hybrids | 反义词: homozygotes | 词性: noun
