gyratory:/ˈdʒaɪrəˌtɔːri/ (US), /ˈdʒaɪrətri/ (UK)| n.|环形交叉路口，环岛|A traffic circle or roundabout.|例句：The new gyratory has improved traffic flow significantly. 新的环形交叉路口显著改善了交通流量。

例句：Take the second exit off the gyratory. 在环岛的第二个出口出去。|近义词：roundabout, traffic circle, rotary|反义词：intersection, crossroads|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gyratorius, from gyrat-, past participle stem of gyrare "to turn, whirl," from Greek gyros "circle, ring."
