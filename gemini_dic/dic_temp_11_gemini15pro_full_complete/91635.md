hyperbole:/haɪˈpɜːrbəli/ (US), /haɪˈpɜːbəli/ (UK)| n.|夸张; 夸张法|An extravagant statement or figure of speech not intended to be taken literally, used to create emphasis or humorous effect.|例句：He used hyperbole to describe his fishing trip, claiming he caught a fish as big as a boat. 他用夸张的手法描述他的钓鱼之旅，声称他钓到一条像船一样大的鱼。

例句：Her claim to be the best singer in the world was pure hyperbole. 她自称是世界上最好的歌手，纯属夸张。|近义词：exaggeration, overstatement, embellishment|反义词：understatement, litotes|词性:noun
