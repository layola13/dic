fallout:/ˈfɔːlaʊt/| n.|放射性尘埃，附带后果，余波|An unpleasant consequence, especially a negative reaction to some event or action.|The political fallout of the scandal was considerable. 这起丑闻造成的政治后果相当严重。|近义词：aftermath, repercussions, consequences|反义词:benefit, advantage|词性:名词
fallouts:/ˈfɔːlaʊts/| n.|放射性尘埃（复数），附带后果（复数），余波（复数）|The plural form of "fallout," referring to multiple instances of unpleasant consequences, especially negative reactions to some event or action.|The economic fallouts of the war were devastating. 战争带来的经济后果是毁灭性的。|近义词：aftermaths, repercussions, consequences|反义词:benefits, advantages|词性:名词
