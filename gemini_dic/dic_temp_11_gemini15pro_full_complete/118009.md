memoriter:/ˌmɛməˈraɪtər/| adv. adj.| 凭记忆地；熟记地 | From Latin "memoriter" (from memory). Meaning "by heart," "by rote." Refers to reciting something from memory, without referring to a text. | She recited the poem memoriter. 她凭记忆背诵了这首诗。| 近义词: by heart, by rote, from memory | 反义词: extempore, impromptu | 词性: adverb, adjective
