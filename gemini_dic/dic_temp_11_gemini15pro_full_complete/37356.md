commends:/kəˈmɛndz/
verb
推荐；称赞；表扬；委托；托付
praises; entrusts
The teacher commended the student for their hard work. 老师表扬了这个学生的努力学习。
He commended his soul to God. 他将灵魂托付给上帝。
praise, applaud, laud, compliment
criticize, condemn, censure
