jellygraph:/ˈdʒɛliɡræf/ | n. | 果冻图; 凝胶图谱 | A visual representation, often a graph or chart, showing the results of gel electrophoresis, a laboratory technique used to separate mixtures of DNA, RNA, or proteins according to molecular size. | Example 1: The jellygraph clearly showed the different protein bands.  果冻图清楚地显示了不同的蛋白条带。

Example 2:  Researchers analyzed the jellygraph to identify the specific DNA fragments. 研究人员分析了凝胶图谱以鉴定特定的 DNA 片段。| 近义词:  electrophoretogram, gel image | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
