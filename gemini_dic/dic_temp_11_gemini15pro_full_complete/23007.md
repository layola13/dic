boughten:/ˈbɔːtən/
adjective
买来的；商店买来的
Purchased from a store or commercially made, rather than homemade.
She served us store-bought cookies, not homemade ones, which was a bit disappointing. 她给我们的是商店买的饼干，而不是自制的，这有点令人失望。
The boughten bread didn't taste as good as the bread she usually baked.  买来的面包不如她平时烤的面包好吃。
近义词:store-bought, purchased, commercial
反义词:homemade, home-baked, hand-crafted
词性:adjective

词源:
From "buy" + "-en" (a suffix forming past participles, often with a sense of 'made of' or 'provided with').  While "bought" is the standard past participle of "buy," "boughten" retains a specialized meaning.
