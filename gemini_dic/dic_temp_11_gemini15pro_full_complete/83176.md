gutturalize: /ˈɡʌtərəlaɪz/ (US) /ˈɡʌtərəlaɪz/ (UK)| v. | 发喉音；使带喉音 | To pronounce a sound in the throat; to modify a speech sound by giving it a guttural quality. |  He gutturalized the "r" in the word. 他把单词中的“r”发成了喉音。

The singer gutturalized her vocals for dramatic effect.  这位歌手用喉音演唱，以达到戏剧性的效果。 | 近义词: rasp, growl, croak | 反义词: None | 词性: verb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *gutturalis "of the throat," from Latin guttur "throat."
