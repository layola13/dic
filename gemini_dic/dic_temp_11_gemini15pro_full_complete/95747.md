inconsisting:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪstɪŋ/
adjective
不一致的，不协调的
not staying the same throughout; containing elements that disagree with each other.
Her statements were inconsistent with the evidence. 她的陈述与证据不符。
incompatible, contradictory, conflicting, discrepant
consistent, constant, uniform, compatible
adjective
