concluders:/kənˈkluːdərz/| n.|结论者；结束者；总结者|People who bring something to a close, especially an argument, discussion, or meeting.  Those who finalize something or draw the final conclusions.|The concluders of the peace treaty ensured all parties were satisfied. 和平条约的缔结者确保各方都满意。|synonyms: finalizers, settlers, finishers|antonyms: initiators, beginners, starters|noun
