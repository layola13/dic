mulewort:/ˈmjuːlwɜːrt/ /ˈmjuːlˌwɝːt/| n.| 母菊, 菊蒿|A European composite plant ( _Tanacetum parthenium_ syn. _Chrysanthemum parthenium_ ) having finely divided aromatic leaves and buttonlike white-rayed flower heads formerly used medicinally.  It is also known as feverfew.|The herbalist recommended mulewort for her migraines.|近义词：feverfew, featherfew, midsummer daisy|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
