husbandom:/ˈhʌzbəndəm/ | n. | 男神；理想的丈夫；（女性粉丝对男性偶像的称呼） | A term used primarily by female fans to refer to a male fictional character, celebrity, or other figure whom they greatly admire and often fantasize about marrying.  It reflects a strong emotional connection and idealization. |  例句：She considers Tom Hiddleston her husbandom. 她把汤姆·希德勒斯顿视为她的男神。

例句：My husbandom is Levi from Attack on Titan. 我心目中的男神是《进击的巨人》中的利威尔。| 近义词：crush, idol, bias (in K-pop fandom) | 反义词: N/A (lacks a direct antonym) | 词性: noun
