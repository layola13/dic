barreled:/ˈbærəld/ | adj. | 桶装的 |  shaped like a barrel; stored in a barrel |  We bought barreled pickles. 我们买了桶装泡菜。| 近义词：cask, keg | 反义词： (no clear opposite) | 词性：形容词
