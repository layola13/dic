disgustedly:/dɪsˈɡʌstɪdli/
adverb
厌恶地，憎恶地
in a way that shows strong dislike or disapproval
She turned away disgustedly. 她厌恶地转过身去。
Synonyms: repulsively, loathsomely, nauseatingly, sickeningly, distastefully
Antonyms: approvingly, admiringly, appreciatively
词性: 副词
