outcries:/ˈaʊtkraɪz/| n.|强烈抗议，大声疾呼|a strong expression of public disapproval or anger|例句：There were public outcries against the new tax.  公众强烈抗议这项新税。

例句：The government faced outcries over its handling of the crisis. 政府因其处理危机的方式面临强烈抗议。|近义词：protests, objections, complaints, clamor, uproar|反义词:praise, acclaim, approval|noun
