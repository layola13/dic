cracking:/ˈkrækɪŋ/| adj. v. n.| 极好的，很棒的；破裂，爆裂；裂缝，爆裂声|A cracking sound is a sudden, sharp sound, like something breaking.  Cracking can also be used informally to describe something as excellent.|例句：That's a cracking idea! 那真是个好主意！ The ice made a cracking noise. 冰发出破裂的声音。|近义词：excellent, superb, wonderful, breaking, splitting, snapping|反义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, mending, joining|词性:形容词，动词，名词
Etymology: From the verb "crack" (Middle English craken), of imitative origin.
