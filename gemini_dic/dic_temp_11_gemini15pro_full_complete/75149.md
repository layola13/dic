fritillaria: /ˌfrɪtɪˈleəriə/ | n. | 贝母 | A bulbous plant of the lily family, with drooping, bell-shaped flowers, often checkered in various colors. |  The fritillaria imperialis, also known as the crown imperial, is a striking plant in the spring garden. 贝母，又名皇冠贝母，是春季花园里引人注目的植物。


Fritillaria meleagris, or snake's head fritillary, has distinctive checkered petals.  花格贝母的花瓣有独特的格子图案。 | 近义词:  lily (general term for similar plants) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fritillus "dice-box," referring to the checkered pattern of some species. 源于拉丁语 *fritillus “骰子盒”，指的是某些物种的格子图案。
