canyoning:/ˈkænjənɪŋ/ /kænˈjɒnɪŋ/
n.
峡谷漂流；溪降
The descent of canyons using various techniques such as rappelling, swimming, and jumping.
We went canyoning in the Alps last summer.  去年夏天我们去阿尔卑斯山玩了溪降。
近义词：canyoneering, rappelling, gorge walking
反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly hiking, mountaineering if considered as contrasting activities)
词性：名词


canyonings:/ˈkænjənɪŋz/ /kænˈjɒnɪŋz/
n.
峡谷漂流运动（canyoning 的复数形式）
Multiple instances of the activity of descending canyons.
They offer guided canyonings for all skill levels. 他们提供各种技能水平的峡谷漂流指导。
近义词：canyoneerings, rappellings
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性：名词


词源：From canyon + -ing, influenced by French *canyoning.
