gladstone:/ˈɡlædstən/| n.|格拉德斯顿包，格莱斯顿|A type of traveling bag, typically made of stiff leather, with a hinged frame opening into two equal compartments.|He packed his clothes in his trusty gladstone.|近义词：suitcase, travel bag, portmanteau|反义词:None|名词

gladstone:/ˈɡlædstən/| adj.|格莱斯顿式的|Relating to or characteristic of William Ewart Gladstone, a British statesman who served as Prime Minister four times during the Victorian era.  (Less common usage).|His gladstone liberalism championed free trade.|近义词:liberal, progressive (in the 19th-century sense)|反义词:conservative, reactionary (in the 19th-century sense)|形容词
