outblushing:/ˌaʊtˈblʌʃɪŋ/
|v.|胜过脸红；比…更害羞|Surpassing someone in blushing; being more shy or embarrassed than another.|She was outblushing even the shyest girl in the room. 她甚至比房间里最害羞的女孩还要脸红。|Near synonyms: outshining, exceeding, surpassing|Antonyms: paling, fading|Verb

outblushing:/ˌaʊtˈblʌʃɪŋ/
|adj.|非常害羞的；脸红得厉害的|Exceptionally shy or blushing intensely.|Her outblushing nature made it difficult for her to speak in public. 她非常害羞的性格使她在公共场合难以讲话。|Near synonyms: bashful, timid, shy|Antonyms: bold, confident, outgoing|Adjective
