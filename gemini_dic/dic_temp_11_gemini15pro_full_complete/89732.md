hoping:/ˈhoʊpɪŋ/| v.|希望，盼望|Present participle of "hope". Expresses the act of desiring something to happen or be true.|I'm hoping for good weather tomorrow. 我希望明天天气好。|Synonyms: wishing, expecting, anticipating|Antonyms: despairing, despondent|verb
