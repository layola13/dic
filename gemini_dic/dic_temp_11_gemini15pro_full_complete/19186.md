bewitchedness:/bɪˈwɪtʃɪdnəs/| n. |着迷，迷恋；被施魔法的状态|The state of being bewitched; fascination or enchantment.|She was struck by the bewitchedness in his eyes. 她被他眼中深深的迷恋所震撼。|近义词：enchantment, fascination, captivation, spellbound|反义词:disenchantment, indifference|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bewitched + -ness.  Bewitched comes from the Old English be- + wiccian "to practice witchcraft."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
