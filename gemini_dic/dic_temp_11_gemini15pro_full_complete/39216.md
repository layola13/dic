conglomerated:/kənˈɡlɒməreɪtɪd/ (US) /kənˈɡlɑːməreɪtɪd/ (UK)| adj.|聚集的，凝聚的|Formed into a mass or coherent whole.|例句：The artist used conglomerated materials like shells, stones, and glass in their sculpture. 艺术家在他们的雕塑中使用了贝壳、石头和玻璃等聚集的材料。

例句：Over time, the dust particles had conglomerated into larger clumps. 随着时间的推移，灰尘颗粒凝聚成了更大的团块。|近义词：aggregated, amassed, clustered, combined|反义词：dispersed, scattered, separated|词性:形容词

conglomerate:/kənˈɡlɒmərət/ (US) /kənˈɡlɑːmərət/ (UK)| n.|企业集团，混合物|A large corporation formed by the merging of separate and diverse firms; a mass formed by the union of diverse things.|例句：The conglomerate owns businesses in various industries, from food production to telecommunications. 这个企业集团拥有从食品生产到电信等各个行业的业务。

例句：The rock was a conglomerate of different minerals, fused together over millions of years.  这块岩石是由不同的矿物混合而成的，经过数百万年融合在一起。|近义词：corporation, combine, mixture, composite|反义词：component, element|词性:名词

conglomerate:/kənˈɡlɒməreɪt/ (US) /kənˈɡlɑːməreɪt/ (UK)| v.|聚集，凝聚|To gather into a rounded mass or ball; to form a conglomerate.|例句：The small stones conglomerated at the bottom of the stream. 小石头聚集在溪底。

例句：Over time, the individual businesses conglomerated into a powerful multinational corporation. 随着时间的推移，各个企业合并成一个强大的跨国公司。|近义词：aggregate, amass, cluster, combine|反义词：disperse, scatter, separate|词性:动词
