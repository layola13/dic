anteriority:/ˌænˌtɪəriˈɔːrəti/
n.
先前，居先，优先权
the state of being or happening before something else
The anteriority of the evidence is crucial to the case.  证据的优先性对案件至关重要。

近义词:precedence, priority, preeminence
反义词:posteriority, subsequence

词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *anterioritas, from Latin anterior "former, prior."
