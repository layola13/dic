lethalnesses:/ˈliːθəlnəsəz/
n.
致命性，致死率
The plural form of lethality, referring to multiple instances or degrees of being deadly or fatal.
The scientist compared the lethalnesses of different strains of the virus.  科学家比较了不同病毒株的致命性。
The report detailed the various lethalnesses of the different poisons. 报告详细说明了不同毒药的各种致命性。
近义词: fatalities, mortalities, deadliness
反义词: harmlessness, safety, innocuousness
词性: 名词
