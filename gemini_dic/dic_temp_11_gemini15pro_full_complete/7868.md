antecedental:/ˌæntɪsiˈdɛntl/| adj.|先行的，在先的，先前的|Going before; preceding, especially in time or order. antecedent:/ˌæntɪˈsiːd(ə)nt/| n.|先行词，前情，祖先|Something that existed before or logically precedes another.
