industriousnesses:/ɪnˌdʌstriəsnɪsɪz/
noun
勤奋，勤勉
The state of being industrious; diligence.
Her industriousnesses led to her great success in business. 她勤奋刻苦使她在事业上取得了巨大成功。
近义词: diligence, assiduity, industry
反义词: laziness, indolence, sloth
词性: noun


Etymology:
From industrious + -ness.  Industrious comes from the Late Latin *industriōsus, from Latin industria "diligence" + -ōsus "-ous."
