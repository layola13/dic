authorizing:/ˈɔːθəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
授权;批准
present participle of authorize, which means to give official permission for something to happen.
The manager is authorizing the purchase of new equipment. 经理正在授权购买新设备。
The committee is authorizing the release of the funds. 委员会正在授权发放资金。
Synonyms: permitting, sanctioning, endorsing, approving
Antonyms: forbidding, prohibiting, denying, refusing
verb (present participle)
