gypsiferous:/dʒɪpˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
含石膏的，石膏质的
Containing gypsum; consisting of gypsum.
The soil in this region is gypsiferous.  这个地区的土壤含石膏。
The walls of the cave were gypsiferous.  洞穴的墙壁是石膏质的。
近义词:gypseous
反义词: (No direct antonym, could be something like gypsum-free or non-gypseous depending on context)
词性:adjective
