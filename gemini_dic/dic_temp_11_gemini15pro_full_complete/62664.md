engages:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪz/
verb
吸引，占用(时间，注意力等)；雇佣；与…建立密切关系；答应，保证；与…交战
This book engages my interest. 这本书引起了我的兴趣。
engage in conversation  参与谈话
近义词:absorb, employ, hire, promise, involve, occupy, captivate, enlist, fight
反义词:bore, dismiss, fire, ignore, release, detach, disengage
词性:v.
