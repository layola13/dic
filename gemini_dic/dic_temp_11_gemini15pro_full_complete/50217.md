depascent:/dɪˈpæsənt/
adjective
放牧的；吃草的
feeding on growing grass; grazing.
例句：The depascent herds moved slowly across the plains.  吃草的牧群缓缓地穿过平原。

例句：Depascent sheep dotted the hillside. 吃草的羊群点缀着山坡。

近义词：grazing, pasturing, browsing
反义词：predatory, carnivorous
词性：adjective
词源：From Latin *depascentem, present participle of depascere "to feed down, graze down," from de- "down" + pascere "to feed, pasture."
