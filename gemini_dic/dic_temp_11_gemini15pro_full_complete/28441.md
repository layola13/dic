carboyed:/ˈkɑːrˌbɔɪd/
verb
用大玻璃瓶装
transported in a carboy (a large glass bottle encased in wicker or other protective material).
The wine was carboyed from the vineyard to the winery.  葡萄酒被用大玻璃瓶从葡萄园运到酿酒厂。
The chemicals were carefully carboyed for safe transport. 这些化学品被小心地装入大玻璃瓶以便安全运输。
近义词: bottled, decanted
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "poured out", "dispensed loosely")
词性: verb (past tense)
