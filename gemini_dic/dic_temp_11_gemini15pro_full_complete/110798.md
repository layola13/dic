linkboy:/ˈlɪŋkˌbɔɪ/| n.|火把男孩，执灯引路者|A boy who carried a torch to light the way at night, especially in the streets before streetlights were common.|The linkboy guided the carriage through the darkened streets. 火把男孩引导马车穿过黑暗的街道。|Near synonyms: torchbearer, lamplighter|Antonym: None (The concept is of presence of light, the opposite would be absence of light, not a specific person)|noun
