hecticness:/ˈhɛktɪknəs/
| n. |忙碌；狂乱；紧张|The state of being hectic; full of activity, rush, or confusion.|The hecticness of modern life can be overwhelming. 现代生活的忙碌可能会让人难以承受。|The hecticness of her schedule left her little time for relaxation. 她繁忙的日程让她几乎没有时间放松。|近义词：busyness, frenzy, hustle, bustle|反义词：calm, peace, tranquility|词性:名词

词源: From hectic + -ness.  Hectic itself comes from the Greek word *hektikós*, meaning "habitual," referring originally to a type of fever.
