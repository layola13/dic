bedspread: /ˈbedˌspred/ (US), /ˈbedspred/ (UK)| n. |床罩，床单|A decorative cover for a bed, often reaching to the floor.| She bought a new bedspread with a floral pattern. 她买了一条新的花卉图案的床罩。| The hotel bedspread was thick and luxurious. 酒店的床罩又厚又豪华。|Synonyms: bed cover, coverlet, quilt, comforter|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sheet" as a minimal covering)|Noun
