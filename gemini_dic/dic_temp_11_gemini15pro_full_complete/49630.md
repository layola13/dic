demigorge:/ˌdɛmiˈɡɔːrdʒ/
verb
涌出；倾泻；吐出
to discharge violently; spew forth.  To empty or pour out contents.
The volcano demigorged molten lava. 火山喷涌出熔岩。
The truck demigorged a stream of refugees. 卡车涌出一群难民。
近义词:spew, vomit, discharge, eject, emit
反义词:absorb, ingest, contain
词性:v.


demigorge:/ˌdɛmiˈɡɔːrdʒ/
noun
(罕见)峡谷；深谷
a deep gorge or ravine.
The river carved a demigorge through the mountains.  河流在群山中刻出了一条深谷。(这个用法比较罕见)
近义词:gorge, ravine, canyon, chasm
反义词:peak, summit, plateau
