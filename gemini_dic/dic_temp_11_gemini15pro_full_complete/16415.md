bardiglio:/bɑːrˈdiːljoʊ/

A type of gray marble, typically with dark gray or bluish veining, quarried in Italy.
This elegant bathroom features flooring and countertops made of bardiglio marble.  这间优雅的浴室采用巴迪格里奥大理石地板和台面。
The bardiglio fireplace added a touch of classic sophistication to the living room. 巴迪格里奥壁炉为客厅增添了一丝经典的精致感。
近义词: marble, limestone
反义词: (No direct antonym, could be contrasted with other stone types like granite)
词性: noun
