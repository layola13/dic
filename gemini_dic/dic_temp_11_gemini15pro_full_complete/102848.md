jasper:/ˈdʒæspər/ | n. | 碧玉; 碧玉制品; |An opaque, impure variety of quartz, usually red, yellow, brown or green in color. | The artisan crafted a beautiful pendant from a piece of jasper. 工匠用一块碧玉制作了一个漂亮的吊坠。|  My grandmother gave me a jasper brooch. 我的祖母给了我一个碧玉胸针。 | synonyms: chalcedony, agate, chert | antonyms: diamond, ruby (in terms of preciousness and transparency) | noun
