noncontinuity:/ˌnɒnkɒntɪˈnjuːɪti/
noun
不连续性；中断
Lack of continuity or cohesion.
The frequent power outages resulted in noncontinuity of service. 频繁的停电导致服务中断。
近义词:discontinuity, interruption, break
反义词:continuity, connectedness
词性:n.
