duty-frees: /ˈdjuːti friːz/
noun
免税商品；免税店
Goods or a shop selling goods that are exempt from the payment of certain taxes, especially in an airport or on a ship or aircraft.
We bought some perfume at the duty-frees. 我们在免税店买了一些香水。
Synonyms: duty-free shop, tax-free shop
Antonyms: taxable goods, taxed goods
n.


duty-free: /ˈdjuːti friː/
adjective
免税的
Exempt from the payment of certain taxes.
Duty-free cigarettes are cheaper. 免税香烟更便宜。
Synonyms: tax-free, tax-exempt
Antonyms: taxable, taxed
adj.
