at your mercy:/æt jɔːr ˈmɜːrsi/ | idiom | 任你摆布；在你掌握之中 | Being completely in the power of someone and dependent on them for safety or kindness. |  The hostages were at the terrorists' mercy. 人质任由恐怖分子摆布。 | Synonyms: powerless, vulnerable, defenseless, exposed, helpless | Antonyms: in control, powerful, dominant | idiom
at your mercy:/æt jɔːr ˈmɜːrsi/ | idiom | 受制于你；任凭你处置 | In a situation where someone has complete control over you and can treat you as they wish. | The defeated army was at the mercy of the conquering general. 败军之将受制于战胜的将军。 | Synonyms: under someone's thumb, at someone's disposal | Antonyms: in command, in charge | idiom
