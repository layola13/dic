halmas:/ˈhɑːlməs/ | n. | 圣尼古拉斯节前夕 | Halmas is the eve of St. Nicholas Day, particularly in parts of Belgium and the Netherlands. |  In the Netherlands, children eagerly await halmas, hoping St. Nicholas will bring them gifts. 荷兰的孩子们热切地期盼着圣尼古拉斯节前夕，希望圣尼古拉斯会给他们带来礼物。| 近义词: St. Nicholas Eve | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Dutch *halm-avond* or *halm-messe*, meaning "straw evening" or "straw mass," possibly referring to a custom involving straw.  源自荷兰语 *halm-avond* 或 *halm-messe*，意思是“稻草之夜”或“稻草弥撒”，可能指的是一种与稻草有关的习俗。
