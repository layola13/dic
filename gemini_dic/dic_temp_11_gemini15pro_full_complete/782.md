absorbents:/əbˈzɔːrbənts/
| n. |吸收剂；吸收物|Substances that can absorb liquids or gases.|The spill was cleaned up using special absorbents.  这些溢出物用特殊的吸收剂清理干净了。|近义词：absorbers, sponges,  |反义词: impermeable materials|noun


absorbant:/əbˈzɔːrbənt/
| adj. |吸收性的；能吸收的|Having the ability to absorb liquids or gases.|Absorbant paper towels are essential in the kitchen.  吸水性强的纸巾在厨房里是必不可少的。|近义词：porous, permeable |反义词: impermeable, nonabsorbent|adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *absorbēns, present participle of absorbēre (“to swallow down, engulf”), from Latin ab (“from, away from”) + sorbēre (“to suck in, absorb”).
