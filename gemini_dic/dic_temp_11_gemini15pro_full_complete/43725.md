creed:/kriːd/
noun
信条，教义，原则
A set of beliefs or aims which guide someone's actions.
He stated his creed: "Work hard, play hard, and be honest." 他陈述了他的信条：“努力工作，尽情玩乐，诚实待人。”
belief, dogma, doctrine
disbelief, doubt
n.
