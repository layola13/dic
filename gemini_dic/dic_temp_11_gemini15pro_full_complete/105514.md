kilojoules:/ˈkɪləˌdʒuːlz/ /ˈkɪloʊˌdʒuːlz/| n. |千焦耳|A unit of energy equal to 1000 joules, used to measure energy in food or the amount of work done. |The label on the cereal bar said it contained 200 kilojoules.  麦片棒的标签上写着它含有200千焦耳。|Synonym: kJ (abbreviation), thousands of joules|Antonym: N/A|noun
