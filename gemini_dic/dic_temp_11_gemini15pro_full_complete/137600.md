nontrier:/ˌnɒnˈtraɪər/ | n. | 不努力的人，不尝试的人 | Someone who doesn't try or make an effort.  | He's a real nontrier; he never puts any effort into his work. 他真是个不努力的人；他从来不在工作上付出任何努力。 | Example:  She labeled him a nontrier after he gave up on the project so easily.  她因为他如此轻易地放弃了这个项目，就给他贴上了不努力的标签。 | Synonyms: slacker, loafer, idler | Antonyms: trier, hard worker, striver | Noun
