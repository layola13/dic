exchanger:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒər/ | n. | 交换器；交换者；兑换商 | A person, device, or organization that exchanges something. |  The heat exchanger transfers heat from one fluid to another.  热交换器将热量从一种流体传递到另一种流体。| synonyms: trader, dealer, broker, converter | antonyms: hoarder, collector | noun


exchanger:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒər/ | n. | 交换生 | A student who goes to a different country to study for a period of time. | She was an exchanger student in France for a year. 她作为交换生在法国待了一年。 | synonyms: exchange student | antonyms:  domestic student, local student | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *eschangier (modern French échanger), from Late Latin excambiare "to exchange," from ex- "out" + cambiare "to change, barter."
