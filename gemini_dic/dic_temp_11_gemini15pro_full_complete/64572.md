eruption:/ɪˈrʌpʃən/ | n. | 爆发，喷发 | A sudden outbreak of something, typically something unwelcome or noisy.  A volcanic eruption. | The eruption of the volcano caused widespread devastation. 火山爆发造成了大范围的破坏。| synonyms: outburst, explosion, burst, discharge | antonyms: suppression, containment | noun
