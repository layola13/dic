counterspell:/ˈkaʊntərˌspel/| n. v.|反制咒语，对抗魔法；施反制咒语|A spell that negates the effect of another spell.|The wizard cast a counterspell to nullify the dragon's fire breath.  巫师施放了一个反制咒语来抵消龙的火焰吐息。|Synonyms: neutralize, negate, annul, counter, oppose|Antonyms: enhance, amplify, strengthen, empower|noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + spell.  "Counter-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite," and "spell" refers to a magical incantation.
