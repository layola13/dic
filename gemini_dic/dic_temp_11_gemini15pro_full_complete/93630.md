imboil:/ɪmˈbɔɪl/ | v. | 浸透，使充满 | To saturate or permeate something, especially with a liquid; to steep or soak.  It can also mean to cause a feeling to become very strong. | The fabric was imboiled with dye. 布料被染料浸透了。 | 近义词: saturate, permeate, imbue, infuse, steep, soak | 反义词: drain, dry | 词性: 动词

词源: From im- (in, into) + boil (to bubble or effervesce).  While it appears to be related to boiling, the meaning has shifted to focus on immersion and saturation rather than the application of heat.
