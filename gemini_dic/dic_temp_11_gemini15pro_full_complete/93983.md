impactors:/ɪmˈpæktərz/| n.|撞击器，冲击器|Objects that collide with another object, often at high speed, causing an impact.|例句：The lunar impactors helped scientists understand the moon's composition. 月球撞击器帮助科学家了解月球的构成。

例句：Meteorite impactors can create craters on planetary surfaces. 陨石撞击器可以在行星表面形成陨石坑。|近义词：colliders, strikers|反义词: N/A|Noun
