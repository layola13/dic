immolated:/ˈɪməleɪtɪd/
verb
献祭的，牺牲的
killed as a sacrificial victim, especially by fire; destroyed by fire.
例句：The ancient Aztecs immolated prisoners of war to their gods. 古代阿兹特克人将战俘献祭给他们的神。

例句：His reputation was immolated on the altar of public opinion. 他的名誉被公众舆论的祭坛所吞噬。
近义词：sacrificed, offered, killed, destroyed
反义词：saved, rescued, preserved
词性：v.

verb

反义词：save, rescue, preserve
词性：v.
