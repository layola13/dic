niggardous:/ˈnɪɡərdəs/| adj.|吝啬的，小气的|stingy, miserly; unwilling to spend or give; meager.  This word is now rarely used because it sounds similar to a racial slur, despite having a different etymology.|He was niggardous with his praise, offering only a grudging nod. 他吝于赞美，只是勉强地点点头。|The niggardous funding for the project hampered its progress. 对该项目吝啬的资助阻碍了它的进展。|近义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious, frugal|反义词：generous, lavish, prodigal|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *nigardous*, from Old North French *nigard*, from *nig*, "miser."  Despite the phonetic similarity, this word has no etymological connection to the offensive racial slur.
