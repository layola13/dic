nonsaccharines:/ˌnɑːnˈsækərɪnz/ /ˌnɒnˈsækərɪnz/| n. |非糖精;|Substances that are not saccharines, typically artificial sweeteners.|Many people switch to nonsaccharines to reduce their sugar intake. 许多人改用非糖精来减少糖的摄入量。|The bakery offers a variety of desserts made with nonsaccharines. 这家面包店提供各种用非糖精制作的甜点。|近义词：artificial sweeteners, sugar substitutes|反义词:saccharines, sugars|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From non- + saccharine.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without."  "Saccharine" comes from the Latin "saccharum," meaning "sugar," ultimately from the Greek "σάκχαρον (sakcharon)."
