iframes:/ˈaɪfreɪmz/| n.|内嵌框架，子框架|An HTML element that allows you to embed another HTML document within the current one.|The website uses iframes to display content from other sources.  该网站使用内嵌框架来显示来自其他来源的内容。|近义词：embedded frame, subframe|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "standalone page")|noun
