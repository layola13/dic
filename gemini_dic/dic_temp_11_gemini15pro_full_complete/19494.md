bidentate:/baɪˈdɛnteɪt/
adjective
二齿的；双齿的
Having two teeth or tooth-like projections, especially used in chemistry to describe a ligand that can bond to a central metal atom through two donor atoms.
Ethylenediamine is a bidentate ligand. 乙二胺是一个二齿配体。
近义词: chelating (when referring to ligands)
反义词: monodentate
词性: adjective

bidentate:/baɪˈdɛnteɪt/
noun
二齿配体
A chemical compound or ion that can bond to a central metal atom through two donor atoms.
The bidentate formed a stable complex with the metal ion.  这个二齿配体与金属离子形成了稳定的络合物。
近义词: chelating agent
反义词: monodentate ligand
词性: noun
