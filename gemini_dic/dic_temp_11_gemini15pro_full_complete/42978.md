coveted: /ˈkʌvɪtɪd/ | adj. | 渴望的，梦寐以求的 | Highly desired or wished for. |  This antique vase is a coveted item among collectors.  这个古董花瓶是收藏家们梦寐以求的物品。

The coveted Nobel Prize is awarded annually.  令人垂涎的诺贝尔奖每年颁发一次。 | 近义词: desired, sought-after, desirable, in-demand | 反义词: unwanted, undesirable, disregarded | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "covet," which comes from Old French "coveiter," meaning "to long for, desire eagerly," derived from Latin "cupiditare," frequentative of "cupere" meaning "to desire."
