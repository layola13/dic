impeller:/ɪmˈpɛlər/| n.|叶轮;推进器|A rotating component of a centrifugal pump, compressor, or other machine, that imparts motion to a fluid by throwing it outwards from the center.|The impeller of the pump was damaged and needed to be replaced. 水泵的叶轮损坏了，需要更换。|近义词：rotor, blade, vane|反义词:|noun
