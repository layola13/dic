designed: /dɪˈzaɪnd/ | adj. | 设计的，计划的 |  Past participle of the verb "design," meaning intentionally created or planned. | This building was designed by a famous architect. 这栋建筑是由一位著名的建筑师设计的。 | The program is designed to help students learn English. 这个项目旨在帮助学生学习英语。 | 近义词：planned, intended, created, conceived, formulated | 反义词：unplanned, accidental, random | 词性:  形容词

词源: From Old French "designer" from Latin "designare"  meaning "to mark out, designate."

PHRASE->
designed for: 专为...而设计  The software is designed for beginners.  这个软件是为初学者设计的。
designed to: 旨在...  The program is designed to help the unemployed. 这个项目旨在帮助失业者。
designed by: 由...设计  The dress was designed by a famous fashion designer. 这件衣服是由一位著名的时装设计师设计的。
poorly designed: 设计糟糕  The website is poorly designed and difficult to navigate.  这个网站设计得很糟糕，很难浏览。
well-designed: 设计良好 The experiment was well-designed and produced reliable results.  这个实验设计得很好，产生了可靠的结果。
