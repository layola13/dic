irresistibilities:/ɪˌrɛzɪstɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
不可抗拒性，无法抵挡的魅力
The quality of being impossible to resist; the power to compel interest or attraction.
Her laughter has an irresistibility that draws everyone in. 她的笑声有一种无法抗拒的魅力，吸引着每个人。
Synonyms: allure, magnetism, charm, appeal
Antonyms: repulsion, aversion, distaste
Noun
