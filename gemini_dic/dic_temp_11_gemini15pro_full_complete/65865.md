evokers:/ɪˈvoʊkərz/| n.|唤起者；激发者|People or things that evoke a particular feeling, memory, or image.  Those who call forth or summon something.|The evokers of ancient magic were revered in their society.  古代魔法的唤起者在他们的社会中受到尊敬。|synonyms: conjurers, summoners, elicitors|antonyms: suppressors, inhibitors|noun
