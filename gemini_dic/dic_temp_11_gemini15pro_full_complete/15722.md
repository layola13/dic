balanoid:/ˈbælənɔɪd/| adj. n.|橡子形的，橡子状的；Balanus属的动物|Resembling an acorn; belonging to the genus Balanus (a type of barnacle).|Balanoid barnacles are common on rocky shores.  橡子藤壶在岩石海岸很常见。
The balanoid shape of the fruit made it easy to identify. 这种水果的橡子形状使它很容易辨认。|近义词：acorn-shaped, gland-shaped|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun


Etymology: From Greek *βάλανος (bálanos) "acorn" +‎ -oid.
