noncommunicatively:/ˌnɑːnkəˈmjuːnɪkətɪvli/
adverb
不交流地，不沟通地
in a way that does not involve communicating with other people.
He sat noncommunicatively in the corner, refusing to engage in conversation.  他默默地坐在角落里，拒绝参与谈话。
She stared noncommunicatively out the window, lost in her thoughts. 她茫然地盯着窗外，陷入了沉思。
Synonyms: silently, taciturnly, unresponsively
Antonyms: communicatively, expressively, talkatively
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From non- + communicative + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "communicative" relates to the act of communicating, and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.  词源：由non- + communicative + -ly组成。“Non-”是一个前缀，意思是“不”，“communicative”与交流的行为有关，“-ly”是一个构成副词的后缀。
