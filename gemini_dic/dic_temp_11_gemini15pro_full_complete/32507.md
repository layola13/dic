chinoiserie:/ˌʃiːnˈwɑːzəri/
n.
中国风，中国式装饰品
Decorative objects in a pseudo-Chinese style.
The room was decorated with elaborate chinoiseries, including lacquered screens and porcelain vases.  这个房间装饰着精致的中国风饰品，包括漆屏风和瓷花瓶。

chinoiserie:/ˌʃiːnˈwɑːzəri/
n.
(对中国事物的)模仿，(尤指艺术和建筑方面的)中国风
Imitation or evocation of Chinese motifs and techniques in Western art, furniture, and architecture, especially in the 18th century.
The architect incorporated chinoiserie elements, such as a pagoda-style roof, into the design of the garden pavilion.  建筑师在花园凉亭的设计中融入了中国风的元素，例如宝塔式的屋顶。

近义词:orientalia, exoticism
