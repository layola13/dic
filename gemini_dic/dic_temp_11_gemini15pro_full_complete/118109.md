mennonite:/ˈmɛnənaɪt/
n.
门诺派教徒；门诺派信徒
A member of a Protestant sect originating in the 16th century from the Anabaptist movement. Mennonites are known for their pacifism, simple living, and emphasis on community.
Example: The Mennonite community welcomed the refugees with open arms.  门诺派社区张开双臂欢迎难民。

Example:  Many Mennonites are farmers and live a simple life. 许多门诺派教徒是农民，过着简朴的生活。


近义词:Anabaptist, Amish (related but distinct)
