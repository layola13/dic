homophonous: /hɒˈmɒfənəs/ (UK) /həˈmɑːfənəs/ (US) | adj. | 同音的 | Having the same pronunciation as another word but a different meaning, origin, or spelling. |  The words "to," "too," and "two" are homophonous.  “to”、“too”和“two”是同音词。 | Examples:  "There," "their," and "they're" are homophonous. “there”、“their”和“they're”是同音词。 | Synonyms: homophonic, like-sounding | Antonyms: heterophonous | word type: adjective
