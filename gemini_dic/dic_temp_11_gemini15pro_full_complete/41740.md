corrosivenesses:/kəˈroʊsɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|腐蚀性；侵蚀性；腐蚀度|The quality of being corrosive; the degree to which something is corrosive.|The corrosivenesses of certain acids can damage various materials. 某些酸的腐蚀性会损坏各种材料。|近义词：causticity, acidity, corrosivity|反义词：inertness, harmlessness|词性:名词
