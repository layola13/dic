by-inches:/baɪ ˈɪntʃɪz/
|adv.| 缓慢地，逐渐地|Gradually, little by little, in small increments. |The project is proceeding by inches.  这个项目进展缓慢。|The snow melted by inches. 雪慢慢地融化了。|近义词: gradually, slowly, piecemeal, bit by bit|反义词: rapidly, quickly, suddenly, all at once|词性: 副词

Etymology (词源):
From the phrase "by inches," literally meaning "by increments of inches," emphasizing a slow and measured progress.  The use of "inch" to represent a small amount dates back to Old English "ynce."
