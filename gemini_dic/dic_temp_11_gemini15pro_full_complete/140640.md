olympus: /əˈlɪmpəs/ | n. | 奥林匹斯山；众神之山；仙境 | Mount Olympus, the highest mountain in Greece, in Greek mythology the home of the gods; a place or state of supreme happiness or bliss. |  He felt as though he'd reached Olympus when he won the gold medal.  他赢得金牌时感觉自己仿佛登上了奥林匹斯山。 | 近义词：paradise, heaven, utopia | 反义词: hell, underworld | 词性: 名词


Etymology: From Latin *Olympus*, from Ancient Greek Ὄλυμπος (*Ólympos*). Ultimately of Pre-Greek origin.
