filiality:/fɪˈliːælɪti/ /fɪˈliːælɪti/
n.
孝顺，孝敬
the virtue of respect and obedience to one's parents.
例句：Filiality is a core value in many cultures.  孝顺是许多文化中的核心价值观。

例句：She showed great filiality by caring for her aging parents. 她通过照顾年迈的父母表现出了极大的孝顺。

近义词：obedience, respect, devotion, piety
反义词：disrespect, disobedience, irreverence
词性: noun
