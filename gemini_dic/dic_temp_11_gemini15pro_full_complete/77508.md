gazingly:/ˈɡeɪzɪŋli/| adv.|凝视着地，目不转睛地|In a manner of gazing; intently.  She looked at him gazingly, lost in admiration. |例句：She gazed gazingly at the sunset. 她凝视着日落。

He listened gazingly to her every word. 他目不转睛地听着她说的每一个字。|近义词：intently, fixedly, steadfastly|反义词：briefly, fleetingly|词性: adverb

词源: From the verb "gaze" + the adverbial suffix "-ly".  "Gaze" comes from Middle English gasen, of uncertain origin.
