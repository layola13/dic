nonexponential:/ˌnɒnˌɛkspəˈnɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 非指数的 | Not increasing or decreasing at an exponential rate.  Characterized by a growth or decay rate that does not follow an exponential function. | The growth of the plant was nonexponential, showing a linear trend instead.  这种植物的生长是非指数型的，呈现出线性趋势。| The cooling of the object followed a nonexponential curve. 物体的冷却遵循一条非指数曲线。 | linear, gradual | exponential | adjective
