coney:/ˈkoʊni/ (US), /ˈkʌni/ (UK)| n.|兔子，野兔；(尤指)穴兔； Coney Island科尼岛|A coney is a small rabbit, especially a European rabbit.  A coney can also refer to Coney Island, a famous amusement park and beach in New York City.|例句：The coney darted into its burrow. 兔子冲进了它的洞穴。

例句：They spent the day at Coney Island. 他们在科尼岛度过了一天。|近义词：rabbit, hare|反义词: None|词性: noun
