interhybridizing:/ˌɪntərˈhaɪbrɪˌdaɪzɪŋ/
| adj. | 杂交的，互相交配的 |  (of species or varieties) able to interbreed or hybridize with each other |  These interhybridizing species of birds create a complex taxonomic challenge. 这些能够互相交配的鸟类物种给分类学带来了复杂的挑战。 |  interbreeding, crossbreeding | N/A | adjective
interhybridize:/ˌɪntərˈhaɪbrɪˌdaɪz/
| v. | 互相交配，杂交 | to hybridize or crossbreed with each other | Two closely related plant species can interhybridize to produce fertile offspring.  两个亲缘关系密切的植物物种可以互相交配，产生可育后代。| interbreed, crossbreed | N/A | verb
