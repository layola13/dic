outshouted:/ˌaʊtˈʃaʊtɪd/
verb
大声喊过，盖过…的喊叫声
The protestors were outshouted by the police using megaphones. 抗议者的喊声被使用扩音器的警察盖过了。
outcry, yell over, drown out
whisper, murmur
v.
