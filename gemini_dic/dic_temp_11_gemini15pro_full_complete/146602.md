overgrievous: /ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvəs/
adjective
过度悲伤的; 过度沉痛的
excessively grievous; causing too much grief or sorrow.  This word emphasizes an excessive or unwarranted level of grief or sorrow.
例句：His reaction to the news seemed overgrievous, considering the circumstances.  他对这个消息的反应似乎过于悲伤，考虑到当时的情况。

例句：She found the movie's portrayal of loss overgrievous and melodramatic. 她觉得这部电影对失去的刻画过于悲伤，而且戏剧化。
近义词：excessive, immoderate, undue, exorbitant
反义词：moderate, reasonable, slight, mild
词性: adjective
