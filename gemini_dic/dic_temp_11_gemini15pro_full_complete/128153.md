nettings:/ˈnɛtɪŋz/
n.
网状物，网，网状结构
A piece of material made of threads of rope, wire, etc., woven together with spaces in between.
The fishermen mended their nettings. 渔民们修补了他们的渔网。
近义词:mesh, network, net
反义词:solid
词性:名词
