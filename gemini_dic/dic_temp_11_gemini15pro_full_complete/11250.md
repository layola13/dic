archpresbytery: /ˌɑːrtʃˈprɛzbɪtəri/ | n. | 总铎区；总铎的职位或管辖范围 | A major territorial division of a diocese, similar to a deanery, overseen by an archpriest. |  The archpresbytery held a meeting to discuss the needs of the local parishes. 总铎区召开会议讨论当地教区的需求。

The archpresbytery encompasses a large geographical area. 这个总铎区涵盖了很大的地理区域。


| 近义词: deanery | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
