ETYMOLOGY->
From French *lexicographie*, from Greek *lexikographikos*  "pertaining to dictionary-writing," from *lexikon* "dictionary" + *-graphikos* "related to writing."  *Lexikon* is from *lexis* "word," from *legein* "to speak."
