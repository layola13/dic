northerns:/ˈnɔːrðərnz/
noun
北方人；北部地区的人；（尤指）美国北部各州的人
People who live in the north, especially the northern states of the US.
Many northerners have moved to Florida for the warm weather. 许多北方人为了温暖的天气搬到了佛罗里达。
synonyms: northeners
antonyms: southerners
n.

northerns:/ˈnɔːrðərnz/
noun
北风；（尤指）强劲的北风
A strong northerly wind, especially a cold one.
The sudden northerns brought a dramatic drop in temperature. 突如其来的北风使气温骤降。
synonyms: north wind, northerly gale
antonyms: southern wind
n.
