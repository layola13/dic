excambium:/ɛksˈkæmbiəm/
n.
形成层，交换层
(botany) A layer of actively dividing cells between the xylem and phloem tissues of plants that is responsible for the secondary growth of stems and roots.
The excambium produces new xylem and phloem cells, increasing the girth of the plant. 形成层产生新的木质部和韧皮部细胞，增加植物的周长。
The botanist studied the excambium of the tree to determine its age. 植物学家研究了树的形成层以确定它的年龄。
近义词:cambium
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific tissue)
词性:noun
