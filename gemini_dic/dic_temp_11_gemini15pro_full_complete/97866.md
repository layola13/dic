inly:/ˈɪnli/
adverb
内在地；完全地；仅仅；唯有
within; entirely; solely; only
He looked at her inly. 他专注地看着她。
Synonyms: inwardly, internally, solely, only, exclusively
Antonyms: outwardly, externally, partly
