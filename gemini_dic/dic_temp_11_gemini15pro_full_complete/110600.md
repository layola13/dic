limn:/lɪm/| v. |描绘，绘画；清晰地描述|to describe something or someone clearly and in detail|例句：The artist limned the dancer's movements with delicate strokes. 艺术家用细腻的笔触描绘了舞者的动作。

例句：The writer limned the character's personality with vivid prose. 作家用生动的散文清晰地描述了人物的性格。

|近义词：depict, portray, describe, paint, sketch|反义词：obscure, confuse|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *limnen*, from Old French *luminer* (“to illuminate, brighten; to paint, color”), from Latin *illuminare*, from *in-* + *lūmināre* (“to light up, illuminate”), from *lūmen* (“light”).  Doublet of *illuminate*.
