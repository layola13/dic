evict:/ɪˈvɪkt/ | v. |驱逐，逐出|To force someone to leave a property, especially a rented one, by a legal process.|The landlord evicted the tenants for not paying rent. 房东因租户未付房租而将其驱逐。|例句：The family was evicted from their apartment after failing to pay rent for three months.  这个家庭因三个月未付房租而被赶出公寓。

例句：She was evicted for breaching the terms of her lease. 她因违反租约条款而被驱逐。


近义词：expel, oust, remove|反义词:admit, welcome|词性:动词
