dollied:/ˈdɒlid/ (UK) /ˈdɑːlid/ (US)| v. |用移动摄影车拍摄；(使)移动；|To move (something, especially a camera) on a dolly.  It also means to move something smoothly on wheels or rollers. |The camera dollied in for a close-up of the actor's face. 摄影机用移动车推近拍摄演员的面部特写。|近义词：moved, wheeled, rolled|反义词：stationary, fixed|词性：动词

Etymology: From "dolly" (a low platform on wheels) + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Dolly" itself likely originates from the proper name "Dorothy" or "Doll," used as a pet name.
