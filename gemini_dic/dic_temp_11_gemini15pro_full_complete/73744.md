forleave 并非标准英语单词。更常见且正确的表达是 "leave of absence" 或简称为 "leave".  "Forleave" 可能是某些地区或特定语境下的非正式用法，或者拼写错误。 以下解析基于 "leave" 一词。


leave: /liːv/ | n. | 离开；许可；休假 |  The act of departing or going away; permission to be absent from duty or work. | He took a leave of absence from work. 他休假了。 She asked for leave to visit her sick mother. 她请假去看望生病的母亲。| 近义词：departure, permission, absence, furlough | 反义词: arrival, presence, work, duty | 词性: 名词

词源:  Middle English leve, from Old English lēaf, permission, from Germanic *laubiz; akin to Old High German louba leave
