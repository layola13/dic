churchmanly:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃmənli/
adjective
像牧师的；有教会人士风范的；虔诚的
Resembling or characteristic of a churchman; befitting a clergyman; devout.
He had a churchmanly air about him, always dignified and composed. 他身上有一种牧师的气质，总是庄重而沉着。
The old professor spoke in a churchmanly tone, full of wisdom and piety. 老教授以一种牧师般的语气讲话，充满了智慧和虔诚。
近义词:pious, devout, religious, reverent
反义词:irreverent, impious, secular, worldly
词性:adjective

churchman:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃmən/
noun
教会人士；牧师；神职人员
A member of the clergy; a clergyman.
Etymology: From Old English *ciricman, from cirice (church) + man. The suffix "-ly" transforms the noun into an adjective.
He was a respected churchman in the community. 他是社区里一位受人尊敬的教会人士。
Many churchmen attended the conference. 许多神职人员参加了会议。
近义词:clergyman, cleric, minister, parson, priest
反义词:layman, nonbeliever
词性:noun
