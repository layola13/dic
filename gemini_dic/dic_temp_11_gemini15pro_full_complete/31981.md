chemoreceptors:/ˌkiːmərɪˈsɛptərz/| n. |化学感受器|Sensory receptors that respond to chemical stimuli, such as changes in the concentration of specific molecules or ions.|The chemoreceptors in the nose and mouth allow us to perceive tastes and smells. 鼻子和嘴巴里的化学感受器使我们能够感知味道和气味。|近义词:sensory receptors, chemical sensors|反义词: None|Noun
