decentralise:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪz/| v. |分散，去中心化|To distribute the administrative powers or functions of (a centralized authority) among several local authorities.|The company decided to decentralise its operations and give more power to regional offices.  该公司决定将其业务分散，并赋予区域办事处更多权力。|Synonyms: distribute, disperse, devolve|Antonyms: centralise, consolidate|verb

decentralize:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪz/| v. |分散，去中心化|To distribute the administrative powers or functions of (a centralized authority) among several local authorities.|Many companies are now decentralizing their operations. 许多公司现在正在分散运营。|Synonyms: distribute, disperse, devolve|Antonyms: centralise, consolidate|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + centralize, from central + -ize.  Ultimately from Latin centrum "center."
