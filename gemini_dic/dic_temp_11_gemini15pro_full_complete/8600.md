anticline:/ˈæntiklaɪn/| n. |背斜|A fold in rock layers that is convex upwards. The oldest rock layers are in the core of the fold, and the youngest are on the outside.|例句：The anticline trapped oil and gas, making it a prime drilling location.  背斜圈闭了石油和天然气，使其成为主要的钻探地点。

例句：Geologists studied the anticline to understand the region's tectonic history. 地质学家研究背斜以了解该地区的构造历史。|近义词：upfold, arch|反义词: syncline|词性: noun
