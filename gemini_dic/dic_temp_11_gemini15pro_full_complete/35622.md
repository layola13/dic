coat-tails:/ˈkoʊtˌteɪlz/
noun
衣尾，裙裾；(比喻)借助他人权势
the back flaps of a long coat; figuratively, the power or influence of a popular or successful person
He rode into office on the coat-tails of the president. 他凭借总统的权势当选。
influence, power, patronage
