looming:/ˈluːmɪŋ/| adj.|逼近的，即将发生的；（通常指不好的事情）令人担忧的|appearing large and threatening; about to happen, especially in a way that is worrying or frightening|Example: The looming deadline added to the pressure. 逼近的截止日期增加了压力。

Example:  Dark clouds loomed on the horizon.  乌云在地平线上逼近。|近义词：imminent, impending, approaching, menacing, threatening|反义词：distant, receding, past, unlikely|词性:形容词
