castanets:/ˌkæstəˈnɛts/| n.|响板|A percussion instrument consisting of two small concave pieces of wood or ivory, held in the hand and clicked together. |The dancer clicked her castanets rhythmically. 舞蹈演员有节奏地敲击响板。|Synonyms: clappers, finger cymbals|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


castanets:/ˌkæstəˈnɛts/| n.|栗子|Technically, the word "castanets" can also refer to the chestnut tree or its fruit, though this usage is rare. |The castanets in the orchard were ripe for picking. 果园里的栗子成熟了，可以采摘了。|Synonyms: chestnuts|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
