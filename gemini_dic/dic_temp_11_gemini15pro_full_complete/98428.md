inspectingly:/ɪnˈspɛktɪŋli/
adverb
检查地，审视地
in a way that involves looking closely and critically at something or someone.
He looked at her inspectingly, trying to gauge her reaction. 他审视地看着她，试图揣摩她的反应。
近义词: scrutinizingly, critically, carefully, closely
反义词: cursorily, perfunctorily
词性: 副词
