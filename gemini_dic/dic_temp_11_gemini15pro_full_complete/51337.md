detectibility:/dɪˌtɛktəˈbɪləti/
n.|可检测性，可探测性|The quality of being able to be detected or discovered.  The ease with which something can be detected. |The detectibility of the signal is low due to the surrounding noise. 由于周围的噪音，信号的可探测性很低。|discoverability, detectability, perceptibility, observability|undetectability, imperceptibility|noun
