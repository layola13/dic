barothermograph:/ˌbærəˈθɜːrməɡræf/ | n. | 气压温度记录器 | An instrument that records both atmospheric pressure and temperature. | The barothermograph showed a significant drop in pressure before the storm. 气压温度记录器显示风暴前气压显著下降。 |  barograph, thermograph |  N/A | noun

Etymology:
baro- (from Greek báros "weight, pressure") + thermo- (from Greek thérme "heat") + -graph (from Greek gráphō "write")
