angioplasty: /ˈændʒiəˌplæsti/ | n. | 血管成形术 | A medical procedure in which a balloon is used to open a blocked artery. | Example 1: The doctor recommended angioplasty to widen the narrowed artery. 医生建议进行血管成形术来扩张狭窄的动脉。

Example 2:  Angioplasty can help restore blood flow to the heart. 血管成形术可以帮助恢复心脏的血液流动。 | 近义词: balloon angioplasty, PTCA (percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From *angio- + -plasty. *angio- comes from Greek *angeîon*, meaning "vessel". *-plasty* comes from Greek *plastós*, meaning "molded" or "formed".
