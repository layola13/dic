copastor:/ˈkoʊpæstər/| n.|共同牧师，联合牧师|A copastor is a pastor who shares the responsibilities of leading a church with another pastor.|Pastor John and Pastor Mary are copastor of this church. 约翰牧师和玛丽牧师是这个教会的共同牧师。|Synonyms: co-pastor, associate pastor|Antonyms: sole pastor|noun
