antiliterately:/ˌæntiˈlɪtərətli/
adv. |反 literacy 地；与 literacy 相反地；|In a manner opposed to literacy or the promotion of literacy.  以反对 literacy 或推广 literacy 的方式。|He behaved antiliterately, refusing to read the instructions. 他表现得反 literacy，拒绝阅读说明。|近义词:illiterately, unliterately|反义词:literately|词性:副词
