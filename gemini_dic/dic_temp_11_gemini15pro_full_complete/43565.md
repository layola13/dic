crazy-quilts:/ˈkreɪzi kwɪlts/ | n. | 百衲被；拼布被 | A type of quilt made from various shapes and sizes of fabric pieces, often with elaborate embroidery and embellishments. | My grandmother made a beautiful crazy-quilt with scraps of fabric from old dresses. 我的祖母用旧衣服的碎布做了一条漂亮的百衲被。 | patchwork, quilt | blanket | noun

crazy-quilt:/ˈkreɪzi kwɪlt/ | adj. | 杂乱无章的；拼凑的 | Resembling a crazy quilt in its variety and lack of order. | The city's architecture is a crazy-quilt of styles, from Victorian to modern. 这座城市的建筑风格杂乱无章，从维多利亚时代到现代都有。|  patchwork, jumbled, heterogeneous | uniform, homogeneous | adjective
