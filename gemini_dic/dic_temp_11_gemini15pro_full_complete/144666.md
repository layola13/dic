outwringing:/ˌaʊtˈrɪŋɪŋ/
|n.| 彻底挤出；拧干 |The outwringing of a wet cloth.  湿布的拧干。| English explanation: The act of wringing something out completely, especially to remove excess water. | Example: The outwringing of the laundry left it almost dry.  拧干的衣物几乎快干了。|Synonym: wringing, squeezing| Antonym: soaking, saturating|noun
