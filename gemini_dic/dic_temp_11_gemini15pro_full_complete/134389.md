nonintoxicants:/nɒnɪnˈtɒksɪkənts/
noun
无酒精饮料; 非麻醉剂; 非醉人剂
Substances that do not cause intoxication.
People who abstain from alcohol often choose nonintoxicants at social gatherings.  人们戒酒后，在社交聚会上通常会选择不含酒精的饮料。
The store specializes in nonintoxicants like herbal teas and sparkling juices. 这家商店专门经营不含酒精的饮料，例如凉茶和气泡果汁。
synonyms: soft drinks, non-alcoholic beverages, temperance drinks
antonyms: intoxicants, alcoholic beverages
n.
