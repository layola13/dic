ETYMOLOGY->
From over + powerful + -ness.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, indicating an excessive degree of powerfulness.  "Powerful" comes from the Old French "poer" (power) + "-ful" (full of).  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
