contractionary:/kənˈtræktʃəneri/ | adj. | 收缩的；紧缩的 | Tending to cause a reduction in economic activity, typically through restrictive monetary or fiscal policy. | The government implemented contractionary policies to combat inflation. 政府实施紧缩政策以对抗通货膨胀。 |  The central bank's contractionary monetary policy led to higher interest rates. 中央银行的紧缩货币政策导致利率上升。 | synonyms: restrictive, deflationary, tightening | antonyms: expansionary, inflationary, loosening | adjective
