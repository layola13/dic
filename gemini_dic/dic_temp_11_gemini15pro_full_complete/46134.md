cyme:/saɪm/ | n. |聚伞花序 |A flower cluster in which the central or terminal flower opens first, followed by the lateral flowers.  The main stem ends in a flower, and the other flowers grow from lateral stems below it.| The elderberry bush has a flat-topped cyme. 接骨木灌木有一个平顶的聚伞花序。| synonym: inflorescence, flower cluster | antonym: N/A | noun
