approximately: /əˈprɑːksɪmətli/ | adv. | 大约，近似地 | close to a particular number or time although not exactly that number or time | The flight takes approximately three hours.  这趟航班大约需要三个小时。| approximately 2,000 people attended the concert. 大约2000人参加了音乐会。 | nearly, roughly, about, around | exactly, precisely | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *approximatus, past participle of approximare (“to approach, draw near”), from Latin ad (“to”) + proximus (“nearest”).
