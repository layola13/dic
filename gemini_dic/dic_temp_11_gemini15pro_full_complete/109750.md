leviable:/ˈlɛviəbl/| adj.|可征收的，可课税的|Something that is leviable can legally be taxed or charged.  It refers to something upon which a levy can be imposed.|The government announced that a new tax would be leviable on imported luxury goods. 政府宣布将对进口奢侈品征收新税。|Synonyms: taxable, chargeable, assessable|Antonyms: exempt, non-taxable|adjective
