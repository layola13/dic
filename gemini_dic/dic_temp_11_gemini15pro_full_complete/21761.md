boatman:/ˈboʊtmən/ (US), /ˈbəʊtmən/ (UK)| n.|船夫；划船的人|A person who operates or works on a boat, especially one who transports passengers or goods across a river or lake. |The boatman ferried us across the river. 船夫把我们渡过了河。

The boatman skillfully navigated the narrow canal. 船夫熟练地驾驶着小船穿过狭窄的运河。

|近义词：ferryman, oarsman, waterman|反义词:passenger|noun
