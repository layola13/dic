depressurizer:/diːˈprɛʃəraɪzər/ | n. | 减压器；卸压装置 | A device used to reduce pressure, typically in a sealed system.  |  The depressurizer on the spaceship malfunctioned, causing a rapid loss of cabin pressure.  宇宙飞船上的减压器发生故障，导致舱内压力迅速下降。 | synonyms: pressure reducer, pressure relief valve, pressure regulator | antonyms: pressurizer | noun
