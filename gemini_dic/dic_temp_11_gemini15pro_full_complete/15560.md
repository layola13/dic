bagpipes:/ˈbæɡˌpaɪps/| n. |风笛|A wind instrument consisting of a reed melody pipe and several drones enclosed in an airtight bag supplied from the player's lungs.|The sound of the bagpipes echoed through the valley. 风笛的声音在山谷中回荡。|Synonyms: pipes|Antonyms: None|Noun
