mulch:/mʌltʃ/| n. v.|覆盖物，护根物；用覆盖物覆盖|A protective covering, such as decaying leaves, bark, or compost, spread around or over plants.|She spread mulch around the roses to protect them from the winter cold. 她把覆盖物铺在玫瑰周围以保护它们免受冬天的寒冷。|近义词: covering, compost, straw|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 bare ground 裸露的地面)|词性: 名词, 动词
Etymology: Middle English *molsh*, probably from Old English *mol*, dust
