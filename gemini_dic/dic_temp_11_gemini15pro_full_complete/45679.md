curtesy:/ˈkɜːtəsi/ /ˈkɜːrtisi/ | n. | 礼貌；谦恭；(尤指女子行的)屈膝礼 |A polite action or remark, especially one required by convention.  A gesture of respect made by women, consisting of a slight lowering of the body with a bending of the knees.|  She greeted them with a curtesy. 她行屈膝礼向他们问好。 | 近义词: courtesy, politeness, civility | 反义词: rudeness, impoliteness, discourtesy | noun

curtsy:/ˈkɜːtəsi/ /ˈkɜːrtisi/ | v. | 行屈膝礼 | To make a curtsy. | She curtsied to the queen. 她向女王行屈膝礼。 | 近义词: bow | 反义词: (No direct antonym for the action itself) | verb
