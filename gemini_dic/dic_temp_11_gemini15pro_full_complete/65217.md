etiological:/ˌiːtiəˈlɒdʒɪkl/
adjective
病因学的
Relating to the causes or origins of diseases.
The etiological factors of lung cancer are complex and include smoking, genetic predisposition, and environmental exposures. 肺癌的病因很复杂，包括吸烟、遗传易感性和环境暴露。
The study focused on the etiological role of certain viruses in the development of autoimmune diseases. 这项研究的重点是某些病毒在自身免疫性疾病发展中的病因作用。
Synonyms: causal, causative
Antonyms: resultant, consequential
词性: adjective
