lurer:/ˈlʊrər/| n.| 诱惑者；骗子；诱饵 |Someone who tempts or tricks others into something, often for their own gain. | He was a lurer, promising riches but delivering nothing. 他是个骗子，承诺财富却什么也没兑现。| synonyms: deceiver, trickster, con artist, swindler, temptress | antonyms: honest person, dupe, victim | noun

lure:/lʊr/ | n. vt.| 诱惑；诱饵；吸引 |Something that tempts or attracts, often deceptively.  (v.) To tempt or attract with the promise of pleasure or gain.| The lure of easy money drew him into a life of crime.  轻松赚钱的诱惑使他走上了犯罪的道路. (v.) They lured him into a trap. 他们把他引诱到陷阱里。| synonyms: attraction, bait, enticement, temptation, allurement (n.); entice, tempt, attract, seduce (v.) | antonyms: deterrent, repellent (n.); repel, deter (v.) | noun, verb
