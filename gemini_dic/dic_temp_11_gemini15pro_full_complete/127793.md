neighbourhood:/ˈneɪbərhʊd/ (US) /ˈneɪbəhʊd/ (UK)| n.|街区，社区，附近|A district or area with distinctive characteristics, or the people living there. |例句：I grew up in a friendly neighbourhood. 我在一个友好的社区长大。

例句：There's a good Italian restaurant in the neighbourhood. 这附近有一家不错的意大利餐厅。

近义词：community, area, vicinity, district, locale|反义词:remoteness, isolation|词性:noun


neighbourhood:/ˈneɪbərhʊd/ (US) /ˈneɪbəhʊd/ (UK)| n.|邻近，附近|The state of being near or next to something or someone. |例句：The house is in the neighbourhood of the park. 这房子在公园附近。

例句：The cost of the repair will be in the neighbourhood of $500. 维修费用大概在500美元左右。

近义词：proximity, vicinity, nearness|反义词:distance, remoteness|词性:noun
