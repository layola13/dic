capitule:/ˈkæpɪtʃʊl/| n.|小头状花序; (植物) 头状花序|A capitule is a compact cluster of sessile flowers, often surrounded by bracts, characteristic of the Asteraceae (daisy) family.|The sunflower's large, showy head is actually a capitule composed of many tiny flowers.|近义词：head, inflorescence|反义词: (没有明确的反义词)|noun
