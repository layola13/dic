bacchantic: /ˌbækəˈnæntɪk/ | adj. | 狂欢的；酒神节似的；放荡的 | Characterized by or resembling the revelry associated with the worship of Bacchus, the Roman god of wine; frenzied, orgiastic, riotously drunken. | The bacchantic revelry continued until dawn. 狂欢的盛宴一直持续到黎明。| The music pulsed with a bacchantic energy. 音乐随着狂欢的能量跳动。 | Synonyms: orgiastic, Dionysian, riotous, debauched, dissipated | Antonyms: restrained, sober, moderate, ascetic | adjective

bacchanal: /ˈbækənl/ | n. | 狂欢；酒神节 | A wild, drunken revelry; an orgy. | The annual bacchanal attracted thousands of revelers. 年度狂欢节吸引了成千上万的狂欢者。 |  The party quickly devolved into a bacchanal. 派对很快变成了一场狂欢。 | Synonyms: orgy, revelry, carousal, saturnalia | Antonyms:  prayer meeting, temperance meeting | noun
