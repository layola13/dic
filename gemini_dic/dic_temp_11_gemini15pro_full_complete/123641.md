montro:/ˈmɒnstroʊ/ (US) /ˈmɒnstrəʊ/ (UK)| n.|怪物，妖怪|A large, frightening imaginary creature.|例句：The children were scared by the montro in the movie. 孩子们被电影里的怪物吓坏了。|例句：Legends tell of a terrible montro that lived in the forest. 传说森林里住着一个可怕的怪物。|近义词：monster, beast, creature|反义词:hero, savior|词性:名词

词源:
From Italian *montro*, from Latin *monstrum* (“divine omen, portent, monster”), from *monēre* (“to warn, advise, remind”).
