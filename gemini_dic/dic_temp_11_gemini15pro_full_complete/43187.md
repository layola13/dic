crabbedly:/ˈkræbɪdli/
adverb
暴躁地，乖戾地
in a bad-tempered, irritable, or peevish manner.
He answered crabbedly, clearly annoyed by the interruption. 他暴躁地回答，显然被打扰得很生气。
近义词：grumpily, irritably, peevishly, sullenly
反义词：amiably, cheerfully, pleasantly, good-naturedly
词性：副词

crabbed:/ˈkræbɪd/
adjective
暴躁的，乖戾的
bad-tempered, irritable, or peevish.
He had a crabbed and unpleasant disposition. 他性情乖戾，令人不快。
近义词：grumpy, irritable, peevish, sullen
反义词：amiable, cheerful, pleasant, good-natured
词性：形容词

crab:/kræb/
noun
螃蟹
a crustacean with a broad, flat shell, five pairs of legs, and a single pair of pincers.
We caught crabs in the rock pools. 我们在岩石池里捉螃蟹。
近义词：(no close synonyms for the animal itself), crustacean
反义词: N/A
词性：名词

词源:
From Middle English *crabbede*, from Old English *crabba* (crab) + *-ed* (suffix forming adjectives).  The sense evolved from the crab's sideways movement and its tendency to pinch, to a general sense of ill-temper and difficulty.
