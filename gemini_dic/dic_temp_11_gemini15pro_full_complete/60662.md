electoral:/ɪˈlɛktərəl/| adj.|选举的，有关选举的|Relating to an election or electors.|例句：The electoral college is a system used to elect the president of the United States. 选举团是用来选举美国总统的制度。

例句：The candidate focused on electoral reform during his campaign. 候选人在其竞选活动中重点关注选举改革。|近义词：voting, election, ballot|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *electoral, from Old French electoral, from Latin ēlectorālis (“of or belonging to an elector”), from ēlector (“elector”).  The Latin word ēlector comes from the verb ēligere (“to choose out, elect”).
