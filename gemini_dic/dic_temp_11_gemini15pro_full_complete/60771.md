electrolyzation:/ɪˌlɛktrɒˈlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|电解，电解作用|The electrolyzation of water produces hydrogen and oxygen. 水的电解产生氢气和氧气。|Example: The electrolyzation of brine produces chlorine gas.  盐水的电解产生氯气。|Synonym: electrolysis|Antonym: N/A|noun

electrolyse:/ɪˈlɛktrəlaɪz/ |v.|电解|We electrolyse water to produce hydrogen. 我们电解水来产生氢气。|Example:  The company electrolyses seawater to obtain various minerals.  这家公司电解海水以获取各种矿物质。|Synonym: decompose by electrolysis|Antonym: N/A|verb
