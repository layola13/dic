cinch:/sɪntʃ/ | n. vt.| 容易的事，必然发生的事；稳操胜券；（非正式）确定的事；束带；用带子捆扎 |Something sure or easy; a certainty.  To fasten or tighten with a cinch. |  Winning the championship was a cinch for the team. 赢得冠军对这支球队来说是轻而易举的事。 |synonyms: breeze, pushover, snap, shoo-in, certainty | antonyms: challenge, difficulty, long shot | noun

cinch:/sɪntʃ/ | vt.| 用带子捆扎；确定；| To make certain of something. | He cinched the deal with a handshake. 他以握手的方式确定了这笔交易。| synonyms: secure, clinch, guarantee | antonyms: lose, jeopardize | verb
