etherealization:/ɪˌθɪəriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |  etherealization; 使空灵化; 精神化; |The process of making something ethereal; the act of becoming ethereal. The process of making something delicate, airy, or unearthly.  A state of being refined or spiritualized.|Her etherealization was complete, she seemed to float above the ground. 她完全空灵化了，似乎漂浮在地面上。|The artist's etherealization of the landscape transformed it into a dreamscape. 艺术家将风景空灵化，将其变成了梦境。|近义词：spiritualization, refinement, sublimation|反义词：materialization, concretization|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ethereal + -ization.  Ethereal comes from Late Latin aethereus "of the upper air," from Greek aither "upper air; bright, pure sky; the fifth element" (see ether). The suffix -ization forms nouns of action or process.
