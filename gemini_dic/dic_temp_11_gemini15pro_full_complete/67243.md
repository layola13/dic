expulsionist:/ɪkˈspʌlʃənɪst/
n.|驱逐论者，主张驱逐者|A person who advocates or supports the expulsion of a group of people from a place. |The expulsionist rhetoric fueled the growing anti-immigrant sentiment. 驱逐论者的言论加剧了日益增长的反移民情绪。|近义词：nativist, xenophobe|反义词：inclusionist, integrationist|词性: 名词

expulsionist:/ɪkˈspʌlʃənɪst/
adj.|主张驱逐的，支持驱逐的|Advocating or supporting the expulsion of a group of people from a place.|The expulsionist policies of the government led to widespread protests. 政府的驱逐政策引发了广泛的抗议。|近义词：exclusionary|反义词：inclusive|词性: 形容词
