benightedness:/bɪˈnaɪtɪdnəs/
n.
愚昧，无知；蒙昧
the state of being intellectually or morally ignorant; unenlightenment.
His benightedness led him to believe that the Earth was flat. 他的愚昧让他相信地球是平的。
Near synonyms: ignorance, unenlightenment, backwardness, primitiveness
Antonyms: enlightenment, knowledge, wisdom, sophistication
noun

benighted:/bɪˈnaɪtɪd/
adj.
愚昧的，无知的；蒙昧的
in a state of intellectual or moral darkness; unenlightened.
The benighted villagers were suspicious of outsiders. 愚昧的村民对外来者抱有戒心。
Near synonyms: ignorant, unenlightened, backward, primitive
Antonyms: enlightened, knowledgeable, wise, sophisticated
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + night + -ed + -ness.  Originally meant overtaken by night, then figuratively overtaken by intellectual or moral darkness.
