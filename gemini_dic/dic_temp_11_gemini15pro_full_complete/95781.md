incontinent:/ɪnˈkɒntɪnənt/ (UK), /ˌɪnˈkɑːntɪnənt/ (US)| n.|失禁者|A person who is unable to control their bladder or bowels.|例句：The nursing home specializes in the care of incontinents. 这家养老院专门护理失禁者。|近义词：N/A|反义词:N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incontinentem, accusative singular masculine of incontinens, from Latin in- (“not”) + continens (“holding together, restraining”), present participle of continēre (“to hold together, contain, restrain”).
