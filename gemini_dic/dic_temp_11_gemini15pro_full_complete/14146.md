authority: /əˈθɔːrəti/ (US) /ɔːˈθɒrɪti/ (UK)| n.| 权威，权力，当局，官方，专家 | power to give orders or make decisions; the right to act in a specified way, delegated from one person or organization to another; official permission; a person or organization having political or administrative power and control; a person with specialized knowledge |  The local authority is responsible for waste collection. 当地政府负责垃圾收集。| synonyms: power, jurisdiction, command, control, dominion, rule, sway, influence, dominance, supremacy, mastery, expert, specialist | antonyms:  subordination, weakness, impotence | noun

authority: /əˈθɔːrəti/ (US) /ɔːˈθɒrɪti/ (UK)| n.| 根据，理由 | grounds; justification; a quotation or citation from an authoritative source | He spoke with authority on the subject. 他就这个主题权威地发言。| synonyms: justification, reason, grounds, basis, warrant | antonyms:  unfoundedness | noun
