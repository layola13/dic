empiristic:/ɪmˈpɪrɪsɪstɪk/ | adj. |经验主义的；经验论的 | Based on, concerned with, or verifiable by observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic. |例句：His approach to the problem was empiristic, relying on data and observation rather than abstract theories. 他解决这个问题的方法是经验主义的，依靠数据和观察而不是抽象的理论。

例句：Empiristic philosophy emphasizes the role of experience in acquiring knowledge. 经验主义哲学强调经验在获取知识中的作用。|近义词：empirical, experiential, observational |反义词：theoretical, speculative, a priori |词性:形容词
