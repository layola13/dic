nondeceptiveness:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɛptɪvnəs/
n.|真实性，不欺骗性|The quality of being honest and not misleading; truthfulness.|The company prides itself on the nondeceptiveness of its advertising. 这家公司以其广告的真实性为荣。|Synonyms: honesty, truthfulness, veracity, integrity, candor, frankness|Antonyms: deceptiveness, dishonesty, deceit, fraud, duplicity|noun
