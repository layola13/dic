incubiture: /ˈɪŋkjuːbɪtʃər/ (alternative pronunciation: /ˈɪŋkjʊbeɪtər/)
noun
孵化器; 培养器; 细菌培养器
A piece of equipment used to keep eggs or premature babies warm until they are ready to hatch or develop further; an environment that encourages the growth or development of something.
The hospital's neonatal unit has several state-of-the-art incubators for premature babies.  这家医院的新生儿病房有几个最先进的早产儿保育箱。
Start-up companies often benefit from the resources and support provided by business incubators. 创业公司通常受益于企业孵化器提供的资源和支持。
Synonyms: incubator, hatcher, breeder, nurturer
Antonyms: destroyer, inhibitor
