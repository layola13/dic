frab:/fræb/| v. n.|欺骗，骗取；抢夺，攫取；|To deceive or cheat someone, often in a petty or opportunistic way. Also, to grab or snatch something roughly. |He frabbed the last piece of cake before anyone else could get it. 他在别人拿到之前抢走了最后一块蛋糕。|Synonyms: cheat, swindle, con, snatch, grab|Antonyms: give, offer, share|verb

frab:/fræb/| n.|骗子；抢夺者；|A person who frabs or cheats; a snatch or grab.|He's a frab, always looking for an angle. 他是个骗子，总是想方设法占便宜。|Synonyms: cheat, swindler, con artist, thief|Antonyms: philanthropist, donor|noun
