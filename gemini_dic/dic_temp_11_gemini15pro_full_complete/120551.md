miraculousness:/ˌmɪˈrækjələsnəs/| n. |奇迹般；神奇；不可思议|The state or quality of being miraculous; the character of a miracle.|The miraculousness of her recovery astonished the doctors. 她康复的神奇程度让医生们感到惊讶。|近义词：wonder, marvel, magic, supernaturalness|反义词：ordinariness, commonness|词性:名词

词源: From miraculous + -ness.  "miraculous" comes from Late Latin "miraculosus", from Latin "miraculum" (miracle) + "-osus" (forming adjectives). "-ness" is an Old English suffix forming nouns of state or quality.
