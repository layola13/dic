intersecant:/ˌɪntərˈsɛkənt/| n. adj.|交割线，相交的|A line that intersects another; intersecting.|The two intersecants formed a right angle. 两条交割线形成了一个直角。|近义词：intersecting line, transversal|反义词:parallel line|词性:名词, 形容词
Etymology: From Latin *intersectantem*, present participle of *intersecare* "to cut across, divide," from *inter* "between" + *secare* "to cut."
