homogenization:/həˌmɒdʒɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /həˌmɑːdʒənɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |均质化，同质化|The process of making something uniform or consistent throughout.  The homogenization of milk prevents cream from separating.|例句：The homogenization of the milk makes it smoother. 牛奶的均质化使它更顺滑。

例句：Cultural homogenization can lead to the loss of unique traditions. 文化同质化会导致独特传统的丧失。|近义词：uniformity, standardization, blending |反义词:heterogenization, diversification| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *homogénéisation*, from *homogénéiser* (“to homogenize”), from *homogène* (“homogeneous”), from Ancient Greek *ὁμογενής (homogenḗs)*, from *ὁμός (homós, “same”) + *γένος (génos, “kind, race, stock”)*.
