mediumly:/ˈmiːdiəmli/| adv.|中等地，适度地|Moderately; to a medium degree.  It describes something that is neither very much nor very little, but somewhere in between.|The steak was cooked mediumly, just how I like it. 牛排烤得中等熟，正合我意。|近义词：moderately, fairly, somewhat|反义词: extremely, excessively|词性:副词
词源: From 'medium' + '-ly'. 'Medium' comes from Latin 'medius' meaning "middle".
