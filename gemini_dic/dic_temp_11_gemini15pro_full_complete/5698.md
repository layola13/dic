aluminize: /əˈluːmənaɪz/ | v. | 镀铝；用铝覆盖 | To coat or cover with aluminum. |  The telescope mirror was aluminized to improve its reflectivity.  望远镜的镜面镀了铝以提高其反射率。

The car parts were aluminized for corrosion resistance.  汽车零件镀铝以防腐蚀。| 近义词: coat, cover, plate | 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly strip, remove) | 词性: verb

Etymology: From aluminum + -ize, a suffix forming verbs meaning "to cause to be or resemble," "to subject to the process of," "to treat with," "to become like," from French -iser, from Latin -izare, from Greek -izein.
