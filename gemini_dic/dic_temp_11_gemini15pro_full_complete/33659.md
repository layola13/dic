cinephilia:/ˌsɪnəˈfɪliə/ | n. |热爱电影，影迷癖 |A great love of films; passion for cinema. | She has a true cinephilia, spending hours watching and discussing films. 她是一位真正的影迷，花费大量时间观看和讨论电影。| Example 2: His cinephilia led him to start a film club. 他对电影的热爱使他创办了一个电影俱乐部。 | 近义词:film enthusiasm, movie love | 反义词: film apathy | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *cinéphile*, from *ciné-* (short for *cinématographe*, "cinematograph") + *-phile* (from Greek *philos*, "loving").
