congou:/ˈkɒŋɡəʊ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡuː/ (US)| n. |工夫茶，红茶的一种 |A black tea, typically a strong, full-bodied variety from China.  It refers to a particular method of processing black tea, rather than a specific type of tea leaf.|  Example:  She brewed a pot of strong congou tea. 她泡了一壶浓工夫茶。| Example: He prefers congou to other black teas. 比起其他红茶，他更喜欢工夫茶。 |Synonym: black tea, Bohea |Antonym:  N/A (No direct antonym for a type of tea) |noun
