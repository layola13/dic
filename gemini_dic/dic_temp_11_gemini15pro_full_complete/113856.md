madelines:/ˈmædəlɪnz/ | n. | 玛德琳蛋糕 |  Small, shell-shaped sponge cakes, often flavored with lemon zest and made with ground almonds or nuts. They are associated with the French writer Marcel Proust and his novel "Remembrance of Things Past". |  例句：The aroma of freshly baked madelines filled the kitchen.  新鲜出炉的玛德琳蛋糕的香味弥漫了整个厨房。

例句：She served tea and madelines in the afternoon.  下午她端上了茶和玛德琳蛋糕。 | 近义词：petit fours, tea cakes | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "savory pastries") | 词性: noun
