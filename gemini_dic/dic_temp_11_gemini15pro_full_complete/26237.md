buttermilks:/ˈbʌtərˌmɪlks/ | n. |酪乳，白脱牛奶|A fermented dairy drink, traditionally the liquid remaining after churning butter from cream; now usually made by culturing pasteurized skim or low-fat milk with lactic acid bacteria. | I used buttermilk in the pancake recipe. 我在煎饼食谱中用了酪乳。|  Synonyms: cultured milk, sour milk | Antonyms: cream, whole milk | noun
