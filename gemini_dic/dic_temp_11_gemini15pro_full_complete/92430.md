hyponatraemic:/ˌhaɪpəʊnəˈtriːmɪk/ | adj. | 低钠血症的 | Having a lower than normal concentration of sodium in the blood. |  The patient presented with hyponatraemic symptoms, including nausea and confusion. 该患者表现出低钠血症症状，包括恶心和混乱。

The marathon runner became hyponatraemic after drinking too much water. 马拉松运动员喝太多水后出现低钠血症。| 近义词: hyponatremic (American spelling) | 反义词: hypernatraemic (high blood sodium) | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
hypo- (Greek: under, below) + natr- (Latin: natrium, sodium) + -emic (Greek: pertaining to blood)
