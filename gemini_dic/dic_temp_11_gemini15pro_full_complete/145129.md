overarrange:/ˌoʊvərəˈreɪndʒ/| v. |过度安排，安排过满|To arrange or plan something excessively, often to the point of being unnecessarily complicated or burdensome.|例句：I think we've overarranged the children's schedule; they have no time to just play. 我认为我们把孩子们的日程安排得太满了；他们没有时间玩耍。

例句：The conference was overarranged, with too many speakers and not enough time for discussion.  这次会议安排得过满，演讲者太多，讨论时间不够。|近义词：overorganize, overschedule, overplan|反义词: underarrange, underplan|词性:动词
