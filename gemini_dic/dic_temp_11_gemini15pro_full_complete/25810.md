burgess:/ˈbɜːrdʒɪs/| n.|市民，公民（尤指市镇的）|A burgess is a citizen of a borough or town, especially one with full rights.|The burgesses of the town voted for a new mayor. 该镇的市民投票选举了一位新市长。|近义词：citizen, townsman, inhabitant|反义词:alien, foreigner|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *burgeis*, from *burc* "town," from Late Latin *burgus* "fort, fortified town," of Germanic origin.
