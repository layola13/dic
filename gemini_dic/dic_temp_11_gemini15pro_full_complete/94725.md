impropriety:/ˌɪmprəˈpraɪəti/ | n. | 不适当；不得体；不恰当 | the quality or state of being improper; an improper act or remark | Her impropriety cost her the job. 她的不当行为让她丢了工作。| synonyms: indecorum, indecency, unseemliness | antonyms: propriety, decency, seemliness | noun

impropriety:/ˌɪmprəˈpraɪəti/ | n. |失当行为；不检点行为| An act or remark that is not proper or appropriate. |  The politician's improprieties led to his resignation. 这位政客的不当行为导致他辞职。 | synonyms: misconduct, wrongdoing, misbehavior | antonyms: rectitude, righteousness, good conduct | noun
