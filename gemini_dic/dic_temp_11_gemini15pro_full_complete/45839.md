customly:/ˈkʌstəmli/ | adv. | 通常地，习惯地 |  In a customary manner; habitually.  It describes actions performed according to custom or habit. | He customly arrives early for meetings. 他通常早到开会。|  She customly takes a walk after dinner. 她习惯晚饭后散步。 | 近义词: usually, habitually, typically, ordinarily, commonly, generally | 反义词: unusually, rarely, seldom, infrequently | 词性: 副词

词源: From "custom" + "-ly."  "Custom" comes from Old French "costume," ultimately from Latin "consuetudo," meaning "habit," "usage."
