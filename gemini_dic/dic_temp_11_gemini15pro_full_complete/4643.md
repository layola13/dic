ale's:/eɪlz/
noun
啤酒; 麦芽酒 (尤指淡色啤酒); (尤指节日或特殊场合饮用的) 啤酒，麦芽酒
Belonging to or associated with ale. Specifically referring to a type of beer, traditionally brewed without hops.  It can also be used possessively to indicate something that belongs to or is related to ale.
The ale's flavor was surprisingly robust.  这麦芽酒的味道出奇地浓郁。
synonyms: beer, brew, malt liquor
antonyms: wine, spirits
