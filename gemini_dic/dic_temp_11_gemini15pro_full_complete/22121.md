bolt-on:/ˈboʊlt ˌɑːn/ /ˈbəʊlt ɒn/| adj. n.|附加的，螺栓固定的；附加装置|Something that is bolt-on can be easily added to something else.  It is often used to describe accessories or features that are not essential to the main product.|The car has several bolt-on extras, like a spoiler and alloy wheels. 这辆车有几个附加装置，比如扰流板和合金轮毂。|近义词：add-on, supplementary, auxiliary|反义词：integrated, built-in|词性: adjective, noun

bolt-on:/ˈboʊlt ˌɑːn/ /ˈbəʊlt ɒn/| n.|附加装置|A bolt-on is a device or feature that can be easily attached or added to something else.|The company offers a range of bolt-on accessories for its computers. 这家公司为其电脑提供一系列附加配件。|近义词：add-on, attachment, accessory|反义词：integrated component|词性: noun

词源:  "Bolt" comes from Old English "bolt," meaning a short, thick arrow.  "On" is also from Old English "on," meaning, well, "on."  The compound word "bolt-on" refers to something easily attached, like bolting something on.
