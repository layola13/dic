carucage:/ˈkærəkɪdʒ/| n. |车役税，耕地税|A medieval English tax levied on each ploughland or carucate.|The carucage was a significant source of revenue for the crown in the 12th and 13th centuries.  车役税是12世纪和13世纪王室的重要收入来源。| The king imposed a heavy carucage on the landowners. 国王向土地所有者征收了沉重的车役税。|Synonyms: tax, levy, duty|Antonyms: exemption, rebate|noun
