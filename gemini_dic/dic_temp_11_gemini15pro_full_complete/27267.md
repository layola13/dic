calque:/ˈkælk/ /kɑːlk/ | n. |仿造词，译借词|A word or phrase borrowed from another language by literal translation.|The English term "skyscraper" is a calque of the German word "Wolkenkratzer".|近义词：loanword, borrowing|反义词:neologism (although not a perfect opposite, it represents a different way of creating words)|noun
