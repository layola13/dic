hacked:/hækt/| v. |被黑客入侵的；被非法侵入的|gained unauthorized access to data in a system or computer; illegally accessed and modified (a computer program)|例句：My email account was hacked. 我的电子邮件账户被黑客入侵了。

例句：Someone hacked into the company's database and stole sensitive information. 有人入侵了公司的数据库，窃取了敏感信息。|近义词：compromised, breached, cracked|反义词：secured, protected|词性:v. (past participle of hack)

hacked:/hækt/| adj. |被黑客入侵的；被非法侵入的|illegally accessed and modified (a computer program); (informal) clumsy or inept|例句：The hacked website was taken down for repairs. 被黑客入侵的网站被关闭维修了。

例句：He tried a hacked-together solution, but it didn't work. 他尝试了一个凑合的解决方案，但它不起作用。|近义词：compromised, breached, cracked, jerry-rigged (informal)|反义词：secured, protected, polished|词性:adj.

Etymology: From the verb "hack," which originally meant "to cut or chop roughly." In the context of computing, it evolved to mean gaining unauthorized access through skill and persistence.

hacked account: an account that has been compromised by unauthorized access. 例句：My hacked account was used to send spam emails. 我的被盗账户被用来发送垃圾邮件。

hacking tools: software or hardware used for hacking. 例句：Hacking tools are readily available online, unfortunately. 不幸的是，黑客工具很容易在网上找到。
