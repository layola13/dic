gazundered:/ˌɡæzˈʌndəd/
verb
压价成交的；被压价的
Reduced (the offer price of a property) at the last minute, typically just before contracts are exchanged.
The seller was gazundered just before completion.  卖方在即将完成交易前被压价了。
近义词: undercut, lowball
反义词: inflate, overbid
词性: verb (past participle)
