flummox:/ˈflʌməks/| v. | 使困惑，使不知所措 | to confuse someone so much that they do not know what to do or say | The complicated instructions completely flummoxed me. 复杂的指令让我完全不知所措。|Synonyms: bewilder, perplex, confound, baffle | Antonyms: clarify, enlighten, explain| verb


flummox:/ˈflʌməks/| n. | 困惑，慌乱 (较少用) | a state of confusion or bewilderment | His sudden question threw me into a flummox. 他的突然提问让我不知所措。| Synonyms: confusion, bewilderment, perplexity | Antonyms: clarity, understanding| noun
