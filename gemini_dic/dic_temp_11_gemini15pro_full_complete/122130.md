mistranslating: /ˌmɪs.trænsˈleɪ.tɪŋ/ | v. | 误译，翻译错误 |  The act of translating incorrectly, conveying the wrong meaning from one language to another. | He was fired for mistranslating a crucial document. 他因为误译了一份重要文件而被解雇了。 |  Misinterpreting the original text led to mistranslating the entire passage. 对原文的误解导致了整段的误译。 | misinterpreting, misconstruing | correctly translating, accurately translating | verb
