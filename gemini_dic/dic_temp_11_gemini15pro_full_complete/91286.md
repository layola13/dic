hydrokinetic:/ˌhaɪdrəʊkɪˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|水力的，水动力的|Relating to the energy of moving water, especially its use in generating power.|Hydrodynamic and hydrokinetic technologies offer sustainable ways to generate electricity. 水力和水动力技术提供了可持续的发电方式。|近义词:hydrodynamic, water-powered|反义词:static, stationary|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From hydro- (water) + -kinetic (relating to motion).  "Hydro-" comes from the Greek "hydōr" (water), and "-kinetic" comes from the Greek "kinētikos" (moving).
