jiggish:/ˈdʒɪɡɪʃ/ | adj. | 轻佻的，不正庄的 | Characterized by or suggestive of lively, often frivolous, activity; playfully or inappropriately suggestive of sexual activity. |  Her jiggish behavior at the funeral was highly inappropriate. 她轻佻的举止在葬礼上非常不合适。|  She gave him a jiggish wink. 她轻佻地对他眨了眨眼。 | 近义词: flirtatious, frivolous, playful, frisky | 反义词: serious, solemn, demure | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From jig (a lively dance) + -ish (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from a probably imitative Old French word *giguer (to dance).
