outfables:/ˈaʊtˌfeɪblz/
noun
寓言故事，虚构的故事
fictional stories, often with a moral or lesson, that go beyond the usual or traditional fables.  These stories often incorporate elements of fantasy, magic, or science fiction.
The author's collection of outfables explored themes of artificial intelligence and human consciousness. 作者的寓言故事集探讨了人工智能和人类意识的主题。
Synonyms:  myths, legends, fantasies, tall tales
Antonyms:  facts, truths, realities
