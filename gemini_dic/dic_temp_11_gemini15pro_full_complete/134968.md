nonnatives:/nɒnˈneɪtɪvz/
noun
非本地人；非土著人
People who are not native to a particular place.
Many nonnatives struggle with the local dialect. 许多非本地人难以理解当地方言。
The nonnatives brought new customs to the island. 非本地人给岛上带来了新的习俗。
Synonyms: foreigners, immigrants, aliens, outsiders
Antonyms: natives, locals, indigenous people
