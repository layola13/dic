comfiness:/ˈkʌmfinəs/
noun
舒适；惬意
The state of being comfortable and cozy.
例句：I love the comfiness of my new sweater. 我喜欢我的新毛衣的舒适感。

例句：The room had a sense of comfiness that made me want to stay all day. 房间里有一种舒适感，让我想要待一整天。

近义词：comfort, coziness, snugness
反义词：discomfort, unease


ETYMOLOGY->
From comfy (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality). Comfy is a shortened form of comfortable.  Ultimately from Latin commodus "suitable, convenient, proper".
