despoiling:/dɪˈspɔɪlɪŋ/
verb
掠夺，破坏，蹂躏
The act of plundering or ravaging; depriving of something valuable by force or violence.
The invaders were accused of despoiling the country of its natural resources.  侵略者被指控掠夺该国的自然资源。
plundering, pillaging, ravaging
preserving, protecting
verb
