inductive:/ɪnˈdʌktɪv/| adj.|归纳的，感应的|Based on, or using a method of, reasoning in which a general conclusion is drawn from a set of particular facts, observations, or data; of, relating to, or produced by electric or magnetic induction.|例句：The inductive approach to research involves gathering data and then formulating a theory. 归纳研究方法包括收集数据，然后形成理论。|近义词：empirical, experimental|反义词：deductive, theoretical|词性:形容词
