indoaniline:/ˌɪndoʊˈænɪlɪn/ | n. | 吲哚苯胺 |A class of dyes characterized by their intense blue color, derived from indophenol. ETYMOLOGY->
From *indo-* (indicating indole) + *aniline*.  The term reflects the chemical structure of these dyes, which are derived from both indole and aniline compounds.
