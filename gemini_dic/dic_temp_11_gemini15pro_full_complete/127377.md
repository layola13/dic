neanderthal:/niˈændərˌθɔːl/, /niˈændərˌtɑːl/ | n. adj. |尼安德特人；穴居人；粗鲁的人 | An extinct species of human that was widely distributed in ice-age Europe between c. 120,000–35,000 years ago, with a receding forehead and prominent brow ridges. The term ‘Neanderthal’ can also be used informally as an adjective to describe someone as unintelligent or uncivilized. |  例句：Neanderthals lived in Europe during the Ice Age. 尼安德特人生活在冰河时代的欧洲。| 近义词：caveman, primitive man | 反义词: modern human | 词性: noun, adjective

neander:/niˈændər/ | n. | 尼安德特人 (Neanderthal的缩写) | Shortened form of Neanderthal. | 例句：Neanders were skilled hunters. 尼安德特人是熟练的猎人。| 近义词：Neanderthal, caveman | 反义词: modern human | 词性: noun
