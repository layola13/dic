nighters: /ˈnaɪtərz/
noun
夜猫子;熬夜的人;通宵活动
People who stay up late at night, especially to work or study.
例句：The library was full of nighters during exam week.  考试周图书馆里挤满了熬夜学习的人。

例句：The nighters were fueled by coffee and pizza.  熬夜的人靠咖啡和披萨补充能量。

近义词：night owls, all-nighters
反义词: early birds
