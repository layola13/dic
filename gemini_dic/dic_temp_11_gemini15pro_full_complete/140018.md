oedipally:/ˌiːdɪˈpælɪ/ /ˌɛdɪˈpælɪ/
adjective
俄狄浦斯式的；恋母情结的
Relating to or characteristic of the Oedipus complex; exhibiting or motivated by an Oedipus complex.  (Oedipus complex: in psychoanalytic theory, a desire for sexual involvement with the parent of the opposite sex and a concomitant sense of rivalry with the parent of the same sex; a crucial stage in the normal developmental process.)
His relationship with his mother was intensely, almost oedipally, close. 他和他母亲的关系非常亲密，几乎到了俄狄浦斯情结的地步。
He has an oedipal attachment to his mother. 他对他的母亲有着俄狄浦斯式的依恋。
近义词: incestuous
反义词: none
词性: adjective
词源: From Oedipus + -al + -ly.  Oedipus was a figure in Greek mythology who unknowingly killed his father and married his mother.
