bigamy: /ˈbɪɡəmi/ | n. | 重婚罪，双重婚姻 | The act of marrying one person while legally married to another. |  He was charged with bigamy after his first wife discovered his second marriage. 他在第一任妻子发现他的第二次婚姻后被指控重婚。

| Example: Bigamy is a serious crime in most countries. 在大多数国家，重婚是一项严重的罪行。| Synonym:  polygamy (more general, referring to multiple marriages) | Antonym: monogamy | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bigamie (unattested), from Late Latin bigamia, from Latin bis (“twice”) + Greek γάμος (gámos, “marriage”).
