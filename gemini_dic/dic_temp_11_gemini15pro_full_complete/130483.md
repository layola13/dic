nonacademic:/ˌnɒnækəˈdɛmɪk/ /ˌnɑːnækəˈdɛmɪk/
adjective
非学术的；非学院的
Not related to school or scholarly pursuits.
His interests were primarily nonacademic. 他的兴趣主要是非学术性的。
non-scholarly, non-educational
academic, scholarly
