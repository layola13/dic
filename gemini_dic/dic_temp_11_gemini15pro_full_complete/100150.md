interradiating:/ˌɪntərˈreɪdieɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |相互照射的，互相辐射的 | emitting rays of light that appear to intersect or blend; radiating mutually. | The interradiating beams of the searchlights created a dazzling spectacle in the night sky. 探照灯相互照射的光束在夜空中营造出令人眼花缭乱的景象。|  The interradiating colours of the sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues.  相互辐射的日落色彩将天空染上了鲜艳的色彩。| 近义词：intermingling, intersecting, blending | 反义词：separate, distinct | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From inter- (prefix meaning "between," "among," "mutually") + radiate (verb meaning "to emit rays," "to spread out from a center") + -ing (present participle suffix).
