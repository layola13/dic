outsmarting:/ˌaʊtˈsmɑːrtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
智胜; 瞒过
outwitting someone by being more clever or cunning than they are.
He outsmarted his opponents by anticipating their next move. 他预料到对手的下一步行动，从而智胜了他们。
近义词: outwitting, outmaneuvering, tricking
反义词: being outsmarted, being fooled, being tricked
词性: v.
