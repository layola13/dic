cusping:/ˈkʌspɪŋ/| adj. n.|处于交界点，即将发生的， cusp的现在分词|being on the cusp of something; transitional. cusping:/ˈkʌspɪŋ/| n.|尖端，顶点|The point or apex of something, especially a tooth or a wave.|例句：The cusping of the wave was perfect for surfing.  海浪的尖端非常适合冲浪。|近义词：apex, point, peak|反义词:base, bottom|词性:名词
