overreflective:/ˌoʊvərɪˈflektɪv/
adjective
过度反思的，过度反省的
Given to or characterized by excessive reflection or introspection.
He became overreflective and withdrawn after the breakup.  他分手后变得过度反思和孤僻。
The overreflective nature of her personality often led to paralysis by analysis. 她过度反思的性格常常导致她分析瘫痪。
近义词:introspective, contemplative, ruminative
反义词:impulsive, unthinking, rash
词性:adjective
