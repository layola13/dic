heteroclitic:/ˌhɛtərəˈklɪtɪk/ | adj. |  不规则的，变格不规则的 |  (of a noun or other part of speech) irregularly inflected.  A word that deviates from the normal pattern of inflection in its grammatical class. | The word "oxen" is a heteroclitic plural of "ox."  牛的复数形式“oxen”是一个不规则变格的例子。|  irregular, anomalous | regular, normal | adjective
