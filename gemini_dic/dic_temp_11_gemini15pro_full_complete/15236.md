backfire:/ˌbækˈfaɪər/ | vi. n.|适得其反，逆火| (of a plan or action) have an unintended and unwelcome result that is the opposite of what was intended. /An explosion of fuel in the exhaust of an internal combustion engine, especially one caused by faulty ignition.|  His plan to get a promotion backfired when his boss found out he'd been taking credit for other people's work. 他为了升职的计划适得其反，因为老板发现他一直在窃取别人的功劳。|  The car backfired loudly as it went down the street. 汽车在街上行驶时发出很大的逆火声。| 近义词:rebound, boomerang, misfire| 反义词:succeed, work, function|词性:动词,名词
