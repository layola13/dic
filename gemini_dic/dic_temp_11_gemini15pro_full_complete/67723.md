exuviable:/ˌɛksˈjuːviəbəl/
adj.
可蜕皮的; 可脱落的
able to be shed or cast off, especially referring to an outer covering like skin, feathers, or an exoskeleton.
The snake's skin is exuviable.  蛇的皮肤是可以蜕皮的。
The cicada's exoskeleton is exuviable. 蝉的外骨骼是可以脱落的。
Synonyms:  shedable, moltable, deciduous
Antonyms:  permanent, persistent, non-deciduous
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exuviabilis*, from exuviae ("things stripped off, slough") + -abilis ("capable of being").
