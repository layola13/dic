kulan:/ˈkuːlɑːn/| n.|库兰，亚洲野驴|A wild ass (Equus hemionus kulan) native to Central Asia.|The kulan is an endangered species.|近义词：onager, Asiatic wild ass|反义词: (No direct antonym for an animal species. Could consider "domesticated donkey" as a contrasting term, but not a true antonym.)|词性:noun
Etymology: From Kazakh or Kyrgyz  *qulan*, meaning "wild ass," ultimately from Turkic *qulan*.
