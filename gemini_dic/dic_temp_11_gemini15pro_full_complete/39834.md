conspire:/kənˈspaɪər/ | v. | 密谋，共谋；协力 | to secretly plan with someone else to do something illegal or harmful |  The rebels conspired to overthrow the government. 反叛者密谋推翻政府。| synonyms: plot, scheme, intrigue | antonyms:  cooperate (in a positive sense), dissent | verb


conspire:/kənˈspaɪər/ | v. | 凑巧促成；共同导致 | to act or happen together in a way that produces a particular result |  Events conspired to bring about his downfall.  种种事件凑到一起导致了他的垮台。| synonyms: combine, contribute, converge | antonyms: clash, conflict, diverge | verb
