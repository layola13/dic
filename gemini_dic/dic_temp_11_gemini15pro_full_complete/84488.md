haltingness:/ˈhɔːltɪŋnɪs/| n.|犹豫, 迟疑, 断断续续|The haltingness of his speech suggested he was nervous.  他讲话吞吞吐吐，表明他很紧张。|例句：The haltingness of her steps revealed her uncertainty. 她迟疑的脚步显示出她的犹豫不决。|近义词：hesitancy, hesitation, uncertainty, indecision|反义词:fluency, decisiveness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From halting + -ness.  Halting itself comes from the verb "halt," meaning to stop or pause.  The suffix "-ness" turns the adjective "halting" into a noun, indicating the state or quality of being halting.
