gibbeting:/ˈdʒɪbɪtɪŋ/
n.
公开示众，绞刑示众
The public display of a criminal's corpse on a gibbet.
The gibbeting of the murderer served as a warning to others.  谋杀犯被示众以儆效尤。
exposure, display
concealment
noun
