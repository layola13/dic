contemptibleness:/kənˈtɛmptəblənəs/
n.
卑鄙；可鄙；下贱
The contemptibleness of his behavior shocked everyone.
他的行为的卑鄙震惊了所有人。
His contemptibleness was evident in his treatment of the poor.
他对穷人的态度显露出了他的卑鄙。
近义词:despicableness, vileness, baseness
反义词:nobility, worthiness, respectability
词性:noun

contempt:/kənˈtɛmpt/
n.
轻蔑；蔑视
She looked at him with contempt.
她轻蔑地看着他。
He felt nothing but contempt for the politicians.
他对政客们只有蔑视。
近义词:scorn, disdain, disrespect
反义词:respect, admiration, esteem
词性:noun

contemptible:/kənˈtɛmptəbl/
adj.
可鄙的；卑劣的；令人轻视的
His behavior was contemptible.
他的行为令人不齿。
It was a contemptible act of betrayal.
这是一个卑鄙的背叛行为。
近义词:despicable, vile, base
反义词:noble, worthy, respectable
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *contemptibilis, from Latin contemptus (past participle of contemnere “to despise, scorn”), from com- + temnere “to despise.”
