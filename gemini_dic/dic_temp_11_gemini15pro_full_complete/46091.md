cyclostomous:/ˌsaɪkləˈstoʊməs/| adj.|圆口的，環口的|Having a circular mouth or mouth opening; belonging to or resembling the Cyclostomata (a group of jawless fish including lampreys and hagfish).|Lampreys are cyclostomous fish. 七鳃鳗是圆口鱼。|近义词:circular-mouthed|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider gnathostomatous (jawed))|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κύκλος (kúklos, “circle, ring”) + στόμα (stóma, “mouth”)
