hirtellous:/ˌhɜːrˈtɛləs/ | adj. | 具细毛的；微糙的 | Covered with short, stiff hairs; slightly rough. | The leaves of the plant are hirtellous, giving them a slightly rough texture. 这种植物的叶子有细毛，使它们的质地略显粗糙。 | Example 2: The caterpillar was hirtellous, covered in tiny bristles. 这条毛毛虫全身长满了细小的刚毛，毛茸茸的。 | Synonyms: pubescent, hairy, bristly | Antonyms: glabrous, smooth | adjective
