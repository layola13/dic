intense:/ɪnˈtɛns/| adj.|强烈的，剧烈的，紧张的|Existing in a high degree; forceful or extreme.|The competition was intense. 竞争非常激烈。|近义词：extreme, severe, strong, powerful|反义词：mild, gentle, weak|词性:形容词
Etymology: From late Middle English, from Old French *intens (French intense), from Latin intensus, past participle of intendere ‘to stretch out, strain’, from in- ‘towards’ + tendere ‘to stretch’.
