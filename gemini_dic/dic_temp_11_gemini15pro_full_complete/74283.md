fracas:/ˈfreɪkəs/ (US), /ˈfrækɑː/ (UK)| n.|争吵，喧闹，吵闹的场面|A noisy disturbance or quarrel.|例句：A fracas broke out in the bar after the football game. 足球比赛结束后，酒吧里爆发了一场争吵。

例句：The political fracas dominated the headlines for weeks.  这场政治风波占据了数周的头条新闻。|近义词：brawl, melee, uproar, disturbance, quarrel, fight, altercation|反义词：peace, calm, tranquility|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fracas* "crash, din, uproar," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Italian *fracasso* "crash, uproar," perhaps imitative.
