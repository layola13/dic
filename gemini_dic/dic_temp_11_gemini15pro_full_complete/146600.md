overgrieves: /ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvz/
verb
过度悲伤，过分悲伤
To grieve excessively or to an undue degree.  To feel excessive sorrow.
She overgrieved the loss of her pet hamster, refusing to leave her room for days. 她因失去宠物仓鼠而过度悲伤，好几天不愿离开房间。
He overgrieved his grandfather's death, neglecting his work and isolating himself. 他过度悲伤于祖父的去世，忽略了工作，把自己孤立起来。
Synonym: mourn excessively, sorrow excessively
Antonym: celebrate, rejoice
词性: 动词
