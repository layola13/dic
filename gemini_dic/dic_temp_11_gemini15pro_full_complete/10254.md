apotheosize: /əˈpɒθiəˌsaɪz/ /əˈpɑːθiəˌsaɪz/ | vt. | 神化；将…尊奉为神；高度赞扬 | To elevate to the rank of a god; to glorify; to idealize. |  The Romans apotheosized their emperors after death.  罗马人将他们的皇帝死后神化。| Synonyms: deify, exalt, glorify, idolize | Antonyms: condemn, criticize, denigrate | verb
