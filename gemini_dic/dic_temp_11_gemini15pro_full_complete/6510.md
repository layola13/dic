amusee:/əˌmjuːˈzeɪ/ /əˌmyuːˈzeɪ/
n.
被招待者；受款待者
A person who is amused or entertained.  Someone who is a guest at an amusement or entertainment.
The amusee at the magic show was thoroughly impressed.  魔术表演的观众印象非常深刻。
guest, visitor, spectator, attendee
host
noun
