exoterical:/ˌeksəˈtɛrɪkəl/
adjective
外界的；公开的；通俗的
intended for or likely to be understood by the general public; readily comprehensible
例句：The exoterical doctrines of the Pythagoreans were those which they were willing to divulge to the world at large. 毕达哥拉斯学派的外传教义是他们愿意向全世界公开的教义。

例句：His exoterical teachings were simple moral lessons, while his esoteric teachings were reserved for his inner circle. 他的公开教义是简单的道德课程，而他的秘传教义则只传授给他的核心圈子。
近义词：public, popular, accessible, understandable, common, conventional, orthodox
反义词：esoteric, arcane, hidden, secret, occult, private
词性：adjective
