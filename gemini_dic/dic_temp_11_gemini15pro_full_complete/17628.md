beboppers:/ˈbiːbɒpəz/ (US) /ˈbiːbɒpəz/ (UK)| n. |比波普爵士乐爱好者；比波普爵士乐演奏者|A devotee or performer of bebop jazz, a style of jazz characterized by fast tempos, complex harmonies, and virtuosic improvisation.|  Bebop was considered controversial when it emerged in the 1940s. The beboppers challenged conventional jazz styles. 比波普爵士乐在 20 世纪 40 年代出现时被认为是有争议的。比波普爵士乐爱好者挑战了传统的爵士乐风格。|synonyms: jazz musicians, bebop artists, modern jazz players|antonyms: traditional jazz musicians, Dixieland players|noun
