hypermnesic:/ˌhaɪpərˈmnesɪk/ | adj. | 超忆症的；记忆力超群的 | Having an unusually good memory; relating to or characterized by hypermnesia. |  She has a hypermnesic ability to recall details from her childhood. 她拥有超忆症的能力，能回忆起童年的细节。 |  She demonstrated a hypermnesic recall of events. 她展现了对事件的超强记忆力。 | 近义词: eidetic, retentive | 反义词: amnesic, forgetful | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Greek hyper- "over, beyond" + mnēsis "memory."
