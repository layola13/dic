minglingly:/ˈmɪŋɡlɪŋli/
adverb
混合地，交融地
in a mingling manner; so as to mingle or mix.
The scents of flowers and freshly baked bread mingled minglingly in the air.  花香和新鲜出炉的面包的香味混合地飘散在空气中。
The two rivers flow minglingly towards the sea. 两条河流交融地汇入大海。

近义词:intermixedly, blendedly, combinedly
反义词:separately, distinctly, individually
词性:adverb
