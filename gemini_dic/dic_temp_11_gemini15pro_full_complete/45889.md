cuteys:/ˈkjuːtiz/| n.|小可爱们，可爱的人们|A term of endearment, often used for a group of adorable people or things, especially children or animals.  It can also be used in a slightly patronizing or flirtatious way.|Look at those cuteys playing in the park! 瞧那些在公园里玩耍的小可爱们！|近义词：sweeties, darlings, cuties|反义词:brats, monsters|noun
