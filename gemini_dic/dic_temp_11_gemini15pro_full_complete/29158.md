caseworkers:/ˈkeɪswɜːrkərz/
n.
个案工作者；社会工作者
A person employed by a social welfare agency or other organization to provide social services to individuals, families, or groups.  They manage the cases of their clients, providing support and resources.
The caseworkers helped the family find stable housing. 个案工作者帮助这个家庭找到了稳定的住所。

The experienced caseworker understood the complexities of the situation.  经验丰富的个案工作者了解情况的复杂性。

近义词:social worker, counselor, advocate
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "opponent" in a legal context)
词性:noun
