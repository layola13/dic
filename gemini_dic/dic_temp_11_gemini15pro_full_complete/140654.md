omber:/ˈɒmbrə(r)/, /ˈɑːmbər/ | adj. n. | 渐变的，阴影色的；渐变色 |A color that shades gradually from light to dark.  A dyeing technique that creates a gradual transition of color. |  Her hair was dyed in shades of ombre, from blonde to brown. 她的头发染成了从金色到棕色的渐变色。 | synonyms: graduated, shaded, dip-dyed | antonyms: uniform, solid, monochromatic | adjective

omber:/ˈɒmbrə(r)/, /ˈɑːmbər/ | n. | 渐变色 | The style or effect of ombre coloring. | The artist used an ombre effect in the painting. 艺术家在这幅画中使用了渐变效果。| synonyms: gradation, shading | antonyms: uniformity, solidity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ombré*, past participle of *ombrer* "to shade," from *ombre* "shade, shadow," from Latin *umbra*.
