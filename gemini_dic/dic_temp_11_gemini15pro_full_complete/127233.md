nausea:/ˈnɔːziə/ (US) /ˈnɔːsiə/ (UK)| n. |恶心，作呕|A feeling of sickness in the stomach, often accompanied by the urge to vomit.|例句：The smell of the hospital made her feel nausea. 医院的气味让她感到恶心。|例句：He experienced nausea after the roller coaster ride. 他坐过山车后感到恶心。|近义词：sickness, queasiness, vomiting (when severe)|反义词: wellness, comfort|词性:名词

nauseas: This is not a standard English word.  The singular "nausea" is typically used, even when referring to multiple instances or types of nausea.  If you need to emphasize distinct episodes, you could say "bouts of nausea" or "waves of nausea".
