eventless:/ɪˈventləs/
adjective
平淡无奇的；缺乏刺激的；平静的
Lacking significant events; uneventful.
It was an eventless journey, nothing exciting happened. 这是一趟平淡无奇的旅程，什么激动人心的事情都没发生。
The meeting was surprisingly eventless. 会议出奇地平静。
Synonyms: uneventful, quiet, dull, boring, monotonous, humdrum
Antonyms: eventful, exciting, thrilling, dramatic
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From event + -less.  The suffix "-less" means "without" or "lacking."  Therefore, "eventless" literally means "without events."
