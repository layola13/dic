forsaker:/fərˈseɪkər/| n.|抛弃者，遗弃者|One who forsakes or abandons someone or something. |He was a forsaker of his family, leaving them with nothing. 他抛弃了自己的家庭，让他们一无所有。|近义词：deserter, abandoner, renouncer|反义词：protector, guardian, supporter|词性:noun
forsake:/fərˈseɪk/| vt.|放弃，抛弃，摒弃|To abandon or leave someone or something, especially someone or something that one has a duty to protect or look after.  To give up or renounce something. |She forsook her career to raise her children. 她为了抚养孩子而放弃了事业。 He forsook his bad habits and started a new life. 他摒弃了坏习惯，开始了新生活。|近义词：abandon, desert, renounce|反义词：keep, maintain, cherish|词性:verb

词源：
From Old English *forsacan "oppose, renounce, deny, refuse," from for- "away" + sacan "strive, contend, dispute."  The original sense was "to contend against, oppose."  Later, it evolved to mean "to give up contesting for, renounce, abandon."
