overdignified: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪɡnɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 过分庄重的，过于严肃的 | Excessively dignified; pompous or pretentious in manner.  Appearing more dignified than is appropriate or natural. | He spoke in an overdignified manner, making him seem rather pompous.  他说话的语气过分庄重，让他显得有些自命不凡。| 近义词: pompous, pretentious, stuffy, formal, stiff, aloof | 反义词: informal, casual, relaxed, natural, humble, unpretentious | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "dignified" (from Latin "dignitas" meaning "worth, dignity")
