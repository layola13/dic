donating:/ˈdoʊneɪtɪŋ/ /dəʊˈneɪtɪŋ/
|v.|捐赠，捐献|The act of giving something, especially money, to a charity or other good cause.|He is donating blood. 他在献血。|近义词：contributing, gifting|反义词：receiving, taking|词性: Verb, present participle of donate
donate:/ˈdoʊneɪt/ /dəʊˈneɪt/
|v.|捐赠，捐献|To give something, especially money, to a charity or other good cause.|She donated $100 to the Red Cross. 她向红十字会捐赠了100美元。|近义词：contribute, gift|反义词：receive, take|词性: Verb

词源: From Latin *dōnāre*, present active infinitive of *dōnō* (“I give, present”), from *dōnum* (“gift”).
