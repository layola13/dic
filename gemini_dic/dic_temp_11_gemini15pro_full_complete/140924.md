oncrna:/ˈɒŋkˌɑːrˌɛnˈeɪ/ /ˈɑːŋkˌɑːrˌɛnˈeɪ/ | n. |长链非编码RNA |A long non-coding RNA (lncRNA) is a type of RNA molecule that is longer than 200 nucleotides and does not code for a protein.  These molecules play diverse roles in cellular processes, including gene regulation, chromatin modification, and signaling pathways.  They are often implicated in various diseases, including cancer. | Long non-coding RNAs (lncRNAs) have emerged as critical regulators of gene expression in diverse biological processes. | 近义词: lncRNA | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider protein-coding RNA) | 词性: noun
