advertises:/ˈædvərtaɪzɪz/
verb
做广告；宣传；公布
to make something known generally or in public, especially in order to sell it.
The company advertises its products on television. 这家公司在电视上宣传它的产品。

advertises:/ˈædvərtaɪzɪz/
verb
(of a quality or fact) make (something) known; indicate.
His gruff manner advertised a basic shyness. 他粗鲁的举止表明他本质上很害羞。


Synonym: promote, publicize, market, announce
Antonym: conceal, hide, suppress
词性: 动词

PHRASE->
advertise for something  为某物做广告（通常指招聘）
They are advertising for a new secretary. 他们正在招聘一个新秘书。
advertise on television/radio/the internet  在电视/广播/互联网上做广告
The company advertises heavily on television. 这家公司在电视上大做广告。
heavily advertised  被大肆宣传的
The new movie is heavily advertised. 这部新电影被大肆宣传。
false advertising  虚假广告
The company was accused of false advertising. 这家公司被指控虚假广告。
to advertise something as something  把某物宣传成某物
They advertised the product as a revolutionary breakthrough. 他们把这个产品宣传成一项革命性的突破。
