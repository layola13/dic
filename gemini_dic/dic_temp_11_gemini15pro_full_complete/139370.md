obsecrate: /ˈɒbsɪkreɪt/ (UK) /ˈɑːbsɪkreɪt/ (US)
verb
恳求，哀求，央求
To beg or beseech someone earnestly or desperately.
He obsecrated her to forgive him. 他恳求她原谅他。
The villagers obsecrated the gods for rain. 村民们恳求神明降雨。

synonyms: implore, beseech, entreat, supplicate, importune
antonyms: demand, command, order
词性: 动词
