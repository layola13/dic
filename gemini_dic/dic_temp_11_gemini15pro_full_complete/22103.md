bolshevik:/ˈbɒlʃəvɪk/ (UK), /ˈboʊlʃəvɪk/ (US)| n. adj.| 布尔什维克，激进的 |A member of the majority faction of the Russian Social Democratic Party, which seized power in the October Revolution of 1917.  Also used more broadly to refer to a revolutionary socialist.|  Lenin was a Bolshevik. 列宁是布尔什维克。|Synonyms: communist, revolutionary, radical, extremist|Antonyms: moderate, conservative|noun, adjective

bolshevik:/ˈbɒlʃəvɪk/ (UK), /ˈboʊlʃəvɪk/ (US)| adj.| 激进的 |Having or showing qualities of a Bolshevik, especially in being revolutionary or radical.| He had a bolshevik attitude towards social reform. 他对社会改革持有激进的态度。|Synonyms:  revolutionary, radical, militant, extremist|Antonyms: moderate, conservative, reactionary|adjective
