elisor:/ɪˈlaɪzər/ | n. | 选派官员，特别验尸官 | An officer appointed by a court to perform a function that a sheriff or coroner is unable or disqualified to perform. | The judge appointed an elisor to summon the jury because the sheriff was a relative of the defendant. 法官指派了一名选派官员去召集陪审团，因为治安官是被告的亲戚。 | Synonyms: deputy, appointee, commissioner | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
