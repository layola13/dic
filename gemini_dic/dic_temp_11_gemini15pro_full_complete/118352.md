merism:/ˈmɛrɪzəm/ /ˈmiːrɪzəm/| n.|部分列举法，对偶法，举隅法|A figure of speech by which a whole is referred to by naming a pair of contrasting parts.|例句：Using "lock, stock, and barrel" to mean "everything" is a merism.  用“枪机、枪托和枪管”来表示“一切”是一种举隅法。

例句： "From head to toe" is a merism for the whole body. “从头到脚”是对全身的举隅法。|近义词：synecdoche, metonymy|反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym for a figure of speech)|词性:noun
