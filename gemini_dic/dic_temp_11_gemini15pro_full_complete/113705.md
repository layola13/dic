macrencephalous:/ˌmækrənˈsɛfələs/| adj.|巨脑的，巨头的|Having an abnormally large brain.  Characterized by abnormal largeness of the head, usually associated with mental retardation.|The study examined the skulls of several macrencephalous individuals. 这项研究检查了几个巨头个体的头骨。|Synonyms: megacephalic, macrocephalic|Antonyms: microcephalic|adjective

macrocephaly:/ˌmækroʊˈsɛfəli/| n.|巨头畸形|A condition in which the head is abnormally large.  This can be due to a variety of causes, including genetic disorders and hydrocephalus.|Macrocephaly can be a symptom of a more serious underlying condition. 巨头畸形可能是更严重的潜在疾病的症状。|Synonyms: megacephaly, macrencephaly|Antonyms: microcephaly|noun
