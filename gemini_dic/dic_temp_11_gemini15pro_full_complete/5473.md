alomancy: /ˈæləˌmænsi/ | n. | 金属操控术 | A fictional magic system in Brandon Sanderson's *Mistborn* series where users burn ingested metals to gain supernatural abilities. |  Vin burned pewter, enhancing her strength and speed.  文烧掉了锡，增强了她的力量和速度。

Kelsier used his allomancy to leap across the rooftops.  凯西尔运用他的金属操控术跳过屋顶。 | 近义词: magic, sorcery, thaumaturgy | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "mundanity," "normality") | 词性: noun
