enquiry: /ɪnˈkwaɪəri/ (UK) /ˈɪŋkwəri/ (US) | n. | 询问，查询 | An act of asking for information. |  I made an enquiry about train times. 我询问了火车时刻表。

I've had several enquiries about the job vacancy. 我收到了好几份关于这个职位空缺的咨询。

近义词: inquiry, question, query | 反义词: answer, reply, response | 词性: noun
