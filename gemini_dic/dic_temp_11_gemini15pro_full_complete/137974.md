nonylenic:/ˌnɒnɪˈliːnɪk/ | adj. |壬烯的；九碳烯酸的| Relating to or derived from nonylene, a hydrocarbon with nine carbon atoms.  It specifically refers to an unsaturated fatty acid with nine carbon atoms. | Nonylenic acid is found in some plant oils. 壬烯酸存在于一些植物油中。|  Nonylenic derivatives are used in the production of certain plastics. 壬烯衍生物用于生产某些塑料。 | 近义词:  (depending on context) unsaturated, fatty acid | 反义词: (depending on context) saturated | 词性: adjective
