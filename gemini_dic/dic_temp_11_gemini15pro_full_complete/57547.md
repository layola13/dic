drake:/dreɪk/| n.|公鸭；男性；(尤指著名的)男人；龙|A male duck.  A man, especially a famous one. Also occasionally, a dragon.|例句：The drake preened his feathers. 公鸭梳理它的羽毛。

例句：Drake, the rapper, released a new album.  说唱歌手德雷克发行了一张新专辑。|近义词：male duck, man, fellow|反义词：duck, hen, female|词性:noun

词源:From Old English *draca (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *drakaz. Cognate with Old Norse dreki (“dragon, drake”), Old Saxon draco (“dragon”), Old High German trahho (“dragon”), German Drache (“dragon, kite”), Dutch draak (“dragon, kite”), Gothic 𐌳𐍂𐌰𐌺𐍉 (drakō, “serpent, dragon”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰerḱ- (“to see”).
