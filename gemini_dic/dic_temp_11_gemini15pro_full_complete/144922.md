over-theatrical: /ˌoʊvər θiˈætrɪkəl/ | adj. | 过于戏剧化的，夸张的 | Excessively theatrical; exaggerated or melodramatic in style or behavior. Characterized by exaggerated emotions or gestures, often to an unnatural or affected degree.  Derived from "over" (excessive) + "theatrical" (relating to the theater).  Implies artificiality and a lack of genuineness. | Her reaction was over-theatrical, as if she were performing for an audience. 她的反应过于戏剧化，好像她在为观众表演。

Her over-theatrical sobs seemed insincere. 她夸张的哭泣声显得不真诚。


近义词: histrionic, melodramatic, exaggerated, affected, dramatic, stagy
反义词: understated, subtle, natural, genuine, restrained
词性: adjective
