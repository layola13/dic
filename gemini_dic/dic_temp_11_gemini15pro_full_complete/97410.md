infringer:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒər/| n.|侵权者，违反者|Someone who encroaches upon or violates the rights of another, especially in regard to patents, copyrights, or trademarks.|The company sued the infringer for patent violation.  这家公司起诉了侵犯其专利的侵权者。|The music label pursued legal action against several online infringers.  这家音乐厂牌对几名网络侵权者提起了法律诉讼。|近义词：violator, transgressor, offender|反义词: observer, conformist|词性:noun
