orphanet:/ˈɔːrfənɛt/| n.|孤儿网，罕见病信息网站|A portal for information on rare diseases and orphan drugs.|例句：Orphanet provides a comprehensive database of rare diseases. 孤儿网提供了一个罕见的疾病综合数据库。

例句：Researchers often use Orphanet to find information about specific rare diseases. 研究人员经常使用孤儿网查找特定罕见疾病的信息。|近义词：rare disease database, genetic disease database|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology:  A portmanteau of "orphan" and "internet".  The term "orphan" in this context refers to orphan diseases, which are conditions so rare that they receive little attention or funding for research.
