ninetieth:/ˈnaɪntiɪθ/ | n. | 九十分之一 | One of ninety equal parts of a whole. | He inherited a ninetieth share of the estate. 他继承了这笔遗产的九十分之一。| Synonym: one-ninetieth | Antonym: whole/all | fraction
