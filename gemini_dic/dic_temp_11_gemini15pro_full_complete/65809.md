evidently:/ˈevɪdəntli/| adv.|显然地，明显地|Clearly; obviously; it appears that.|例句：Evidently, she had been crying. 显然，她一直在哭。|近义词：apparently, obviously, clearly, seemingly, visibly|反义词：unclearly, doubtfully, vaguely|词性:副词
词源: Late 14c., from Old French *evidentment (Modern French évidemment), from evident.
