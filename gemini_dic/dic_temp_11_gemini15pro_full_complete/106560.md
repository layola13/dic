kruller:/ˈkrʊlər/| n.|油炸圈饼，麻花(一种荷兰式油炸圈饼，常扭成螺旋状)|A kruller is a kind of twisted doughnut, popular in Dutch cuisine, that is deep-fried and often coated in sugar.|例句：I love the crispy texture of a freshly made kruller. 我喜欢新鲜出炉的油炸圈饼酥脆的口感。

例句：The bakery is famous for its delicious krullers. 这家面包店以其美味的油炸圈饼而闻名。

近义词：doughnut, twist, fritter|反义词:|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *kruller*, ultimately from *krullen* "to curl, to twist".
