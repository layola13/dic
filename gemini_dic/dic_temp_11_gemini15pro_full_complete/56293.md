dogginess:/ˈdɔːɡinəs/
n.
犬性；像狗的特性；卑劣
The quality of being like a dog, especially in behavior; meanness or vileness.
He showed his dogginess by snapping at the waiter. 他对服务员咆哮，显露出他的卑劣。
Synonyms: meanness, vileness, baseness, churlishness, sullenness
Antonyms: kindness, generosity, nobility
