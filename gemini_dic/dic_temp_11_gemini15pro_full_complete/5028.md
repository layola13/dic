PHRASE->
(While 'all-in-all' doesn't form many set phrases, it's often used in summative statements.)

1. All in all, it was a good day. 总而言之，这是美好的一天。
2. All in all, a worthwhile experience.  总而言之，一次值得的经历。
3. Considering all in all, the decision was sound. 考虑到所有因素，这个决定是合理的。 (Not a set phrase, but demonstrates usage in context.)
4. Taking everything all in all, it was a success. 从各方面来看，这都是一次成功。 (Similar to the above.)
5. All in all, I'm satisfied with the outcome. 总而言之，我对结果感到满意。
