dorsiventral:/dɔːrsɪˈventrəl/
adjective
背腹的；上下不同的
Having differentiated dorsal and ventral surfaces.  Having an upper surface and a lower surface that are structurally different.
The leaves of many plants are dorsiventral, meaning they have distinct upper and lower surfaces. 许多植物的叶子是背腹的，这意味着它们有明显的上下表面。

The dorsiventral flattening of the body is a characteristic feature of flounders.  身体的背腹扁平化是比目鱼的一个特征。

synonym: bifacial
antonym: radial, cylindrical
adjective
