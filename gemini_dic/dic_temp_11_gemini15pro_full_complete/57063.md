doubtful:/ˈdaʊt.fʊl/ (US), /ˈdaʊt.fəl/ (UK)| adj.|怀疑的；可疑的；不确定的| Feeling uncertain about something; not sure that something is true or will happen.  |例句：I'm doubtful about his ability to finish the job. 我怀疑他完成这项工作的能力。

例句：The future of the project is doubtful.  这个项目的未来是不确定的。

近义词：uncertain, questionable, dubious, skeptical|反义词: certain, sure, confident|词性:形容词
