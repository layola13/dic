ambitionist:/æmˈbɪʃənɪst/
n.|野心家，抱负极大的人|A person who is excessively ambitious, often at the expense of others.  This word carries a negative connotation, suggesting ruthless pursuit of power or success.| He was known as a ruthless ambitionist, willing to step on anyone to get to the top. 他被称为无情的野心家，为了爬到顶峰不惜踩着任何人往上爬。|近义词：careerist, climber, go-getter, social climber|反义词:slacker, underachiever|词性:名词

Etymology: From ambition + -ist.  "Ambition" comes from the Latin "ambitio," meaning "a going around," referring to candidates canvassing for votes in ancient Rome. This evolved to mean a strong desire for advancement or achievement. The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who practices or is characterized by something.
