intergrapple:/ˌɪntərˈɡræpəl/ | v. |互相扭打，互相格斗|To grapple with one another; to engage in a close fight or struggle.|The two wrestlers intergrappled fiercely in the ring. 两个摔跤手在擂台上激烈地扭打在一起。| The warring factions intergrappled for control of the city. 交战各派为争夺城市控制权而互相格斗。|近义词：grapple, wrestle, fight, contend|反义词：disengage, separate, yield|词性:动词

Etymology:
From inter- + grapple.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally," from Latin inter "between, among, in the midst of, during." "Grapple" comes from the Old French word "graper" meaning "to seize, grasp."
