noncommodities:/ˌnɑːnkəˈmɑːdətiz/ | n. | 非商品，非大宗商品 | Goods or services that are not easily standardized or traded on a commodity exchange, often involving specialized knowledge, skills, or customization. |  例句：Education and healthcare are considered noncommodities. 教育和医疗保健被认为是非商品。


例句：Consulting services are typically classified as noncommodities. 咨询服务通常被归类为非商品。

近义词：services, specialties | 反义词：commodities | 词性：名词

词源：non- (prefix meaning "not") + commodity (from Old French "commodité," meaning "advantage, convenience, opportunity," ultimately from Latin "commoditas")
