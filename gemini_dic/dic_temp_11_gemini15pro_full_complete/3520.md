aftest:/ˈɑːftɪst/ | adj. | 后面的，最后面的 | Situated behind or towards the rear; hindmost. | The aftest cabin on the ship offered the best views. 船上最后面的舱室视野最佳。|  The aftest car in the train was the quietest. 火车最后一节车厢最安静。 | 近义词: rearmost, hindmost, last | 反义词: foremost, frontmost | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "aft" (meaning towards the stern of a ship) + "-est" (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Old English "æftan" (behind).
