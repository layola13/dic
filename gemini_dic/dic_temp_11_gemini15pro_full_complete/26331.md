buzz-saws: n.
buzz-saws: 嗡嗡作响的圆锯; 喧闹声
buzz-saws:  A circular saw that makes a loud buzzing sound.  It can also refer to a loud, harsh, or irritating noise.
buzz-saws: The buzz-saws in the lumber mill were deafening.  伐木厂里的圆锯声震耳欲聋。
buzz-saws: The constant buzz-saws of traffic made it hard to concentrate.  持续不断的交通喧闹声让人难以集中注意力。
buzz-saws: circular saws, power saws
