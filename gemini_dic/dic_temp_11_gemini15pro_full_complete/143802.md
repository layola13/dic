outorganizes:/ˌaʊtˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzɪz/
verb
胜过…的组织能力; 组织能力比…强
to organize something more effectively than someone or something else
例句：The company outorganized its competitors and quickly gained market share.  这家公司在组织方面胜过竞争对手，迅速获得了市场份额。

例句：She outorganized the entire team and single-handedly planned the successful event. 她组织能力比整个团队都强，独自一人策划了这次成功的活动。
近义词：outmaneuver, surpass, outperform
反义词：be outorganized, be surpassed
词性：verb
