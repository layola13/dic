husten:/ˈhuːstən/ (German)| v. |咳嗽|To cough.  The act of expelling air from the lungs with a sudden sharp sound.|例句：Ich muss husten. 我必须咳嗽。|例句：Er hustet die ganze Nacht. 他整夜咳嗽。|近义词：cough|反义词: (None -  Coughing doesn't have a direct opposite)|verb

虚拟语气:

Ich wünschte, ich würde nicht so viel husten. = I wish I wouldn't cough so much. 我希望我没有咳嗽得这么厉害。

被动语态 (Not applicable to intransitive verbs like "husten")


疑问句:
Hustest du? = Are you coughing? 你在咳嗽吗？
Warum hustet er so viel? = Why is he coughing so much? 他为什么咳嗽得这么厉害？

否定句:
Ich huste nicht. = I am not coughing. 我没有咳嗽。
Er hustet nicht mehr. = He is not coughing anymore. 他不再咳嗽了。


PHRASE-> (Phrases are less common with single verbs like "husten"  and are more contextual.  These examples demonstrate usage):
stark husten = to cough heavily/severely 剧烈咳嗽
trocken husten = to have a dry cough 干咳
sich vor Husten krümmen = to double over with coughing 咳嗽得弯下腰
wegen des Hustens = because of the coughing 因为咳嗽
einen Hustenanfall haben = to have a coughing fit 咳嗽发作
