inadvisabilities:/ɪnˌədˌvaɪzəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.|不明智之举；不妥当的行为|The inadvisabilities of travelling alone in a dangerous area were obvious.  独自前往危险地区的不明智之处显而易见。|English Explanation: Plural form of "inadvisability," referring to actions or decisions that are unwise or imprudent.|例句：He pointed out the numerous inadvisabilities of the proposed plan. 他指出了这项提议计划的诸多不妥之处。

例句：Despite the inadvisabilities, she insisted on going ahead with her plan. 尽管有诸多不妥，她仍坚持继续她的计划。|近义词：imprudences, follies, indiscretions|反义词: wisdoms,  prudences|词性:名词
