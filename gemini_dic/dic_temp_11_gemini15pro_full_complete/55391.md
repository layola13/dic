distension:/dɪˈstɛnʃən/ | n. |膨胀，扩张| The act of swelling or expanding.| Abdominal distension can be a sign of serious illness. 腹部膨胀可能是严重疾病的征兆。| synonyms: swelling, inflation, bloating, expansion | antonyms: contraction, shrinking, deflation | noun
