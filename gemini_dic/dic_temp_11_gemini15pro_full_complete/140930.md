ondoyant:/ˈɒndəˌwɑːnt; ɒ̃dɥaˈjɑ̃/ | adj. | 波动的，起伏的，闪光的 | Undulating, shimmering, having a wavelike motion. Etymology: From French *ondoyant*, present participle of *ondoier* "to undulate, wave," from *onde* "wave," from Latin *unda* "wave."
