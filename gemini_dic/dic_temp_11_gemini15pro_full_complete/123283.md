monogamian: /məˈnɒɡəmɪən/ /məˈnɑːɡəmiən/
noun|一夫一妻制者；单配偶制动物|A person who practices or advocates monogamy; an animal that mates with only one partner during a breeding season or for life.|Swans are often cited as examples of monogamians in the animal kingdom. 天鹅经常被认为是动物界一夫一妻制的例子。|近义词: monogamist|反义词: polygamist|词性:n.

monogamian: /məˈnɒɡəmɪən/ /məˈnɑːɡəmiən/
adjective|一夫一妻的；单配偶制的|Practicing or advocating monogamy; relating to monogamy.|They have a monogamian relationship. 他们有着一夫一妻的关系。|近义词: monogamous|反义词: polygamous|词性:adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *μόνος (mónos, “alone, single”) + γάμος (gámos, “marriage”).
