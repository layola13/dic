categorize:/ˈkætəɡəˌraɪz/ /ˈkætəɡərʌɪz/| v. |分类，归类|To divide things into groups according to their type.|例句：The librarian categorized the books by genre. 图书管理员按体裁对书籍进行了分类。|例句：It's difficult to categorize this particular artist's work. 很难对这位艺术家的作品进行归类。|近义词：classify, group, sort, arrange, pigeonhole|反义词: randomize, disorganize|词性:动词
