clodpated:/ˌklɑːpˈpeɪtɪd/| adj.|愚蠢的，笨的|A clodpated person is stupid or foolish.|He made a clodpated decision to invest all his money in that failing company. 他愚蠢地决定把所有钱都投资在那家倒闭的公司。|例句：She made a rather clodpated remark about the weather. 她对天气做了一个相当愚蠢的评论。|近义词：stupid, foolish, silly, dim-witted, obtuse|反义词：intelligent, smart, bright, clever|词性：形容词

词源:  From "clod" (lump of earth) + "pate" (head), literally meaning "having a head like a lump of earth."  Implies a dull or unintelligent mind.
