bedrizzle:/bɪˈdrɪzəl/ |v. |细雨蒙蒙，沾湿 |To sprinkle or cover lightly with drops of liquid, as if with a fine drizzle. | The flowers were bedrizzled with dew. 花上沾满了露水。|  The cook bedrizzled the cake with icing. 厨师在蛋糕上淋了一层糖霜。 |近义词：drizzle, sprinkle, spray |反义词：drench, soak |词性: 动词

词源:  "be-" (prefix meaning "to make, cover with") + "drizzle" (noun meaning "light rain")
