overgratified:/ˌoʊvərˈɡrætɪfaɪd/| adj.| 过度满足的，过分高兴的|  Excessively gratified; having had one's desires or appetites indulged to an excessive degree.  This often implies a state of being spoiled or jaded, where further pleasure or satisfaction is difficult to achieve.| He felt overgratified after winning the lottery, to the point where nothing else seemed exciting anymore. 他在赢得彩票后感到过度满足，以至于其他任何事情似乎都不再令人兴奋了。|  satiated, indulged, spoiled|  unsatisfied, unfulfilled| adjective
