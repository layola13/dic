crypto-calvinist: /ˌkrɪptoʊˈkæl.vɪ.nɪst/ | n. | 秘密加尔文主义者 | A person who outwardly conforms to the doctrines and practices of a non-Calvinistic church, but secretly holds Calvinistic beliefs.  |  He was accused of being a crypto-calvinist, outwardly Lutheran but secretly subscribing to Calvin's predestination theology. 他被指控为秘密加尔文主义者，表面上是路德教徒，但暗中赞同加尔文的预定论神学。 | Synonyms:  dissembler,  hypocrite | Antonyms:  openly Calvinist,  professed Calvinist | noun

crypto-calvinist: /ˌkrɪptoʊˈkæl.vɪ.nɪst/ | adj. | 秘密加尔文主义的 | Of or relating to crypto-calvinism. | The crypto-calvinist movement caused significant turmoil within the Lutheran church. 秘密加尔文主义运动在路德教会内部引起了巨大的骚动。 | Synonyms:  clandestine, concealed | Antonyms:  overt,  public | adjective
