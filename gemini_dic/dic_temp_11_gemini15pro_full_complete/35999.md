codexes:/ˈkoʊˌdɛkˌsiz/
n.
法典；手抄本；药典
An ancient manuscript text in book form.  A collection of rules or regulations.
This library houses several ancient codexes.  这个图书馆收藏了几部古代法典。
The codexes contained detailed descriptions of medicinal herbs. 这些药典包含了药草的详细描述。
synonyms: manuscripts, volumes, compilations, books, compendiums
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object like a codex.  Could consider "fragments" or "individual pages" as opposites of a collected book form.)
词性: noun
