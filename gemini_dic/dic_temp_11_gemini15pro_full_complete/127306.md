navvying:/ˈnævɪɪŋ/ | n. | 挖土方；土木工程；苦力活 | The act or process of digging or excavating, especially for the construction of canals, railroads, or other large earthworks.  It can also refer to the hard manual labor involved in such work. |  The navvying for the new railway line was arduous and dangerous.  新铁路的挖土工作艰苦而危险。| synonyms: excavation, earthmoving, digging, construction | antonyms:  filling, backfilling | noun

navvy:/ˈnævi/ | n. | 挖土工人；苦力 | A laborer employed in excavating, especially for the construction of canals, railroads, or other large earthworks. | The navvies worked tirelessly to complete the canal.  挖土工人们不知疲倦地工作以完成运河的修建。| synonyms: laborer, workman, construction worker | antonyms:  supervisor, manager, foreman | noun
