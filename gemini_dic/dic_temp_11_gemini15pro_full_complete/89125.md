homesteaded: /ˈhəʊmstedɪd/ | v. | 取得宅地；在宅地上居住 | to acquire or occupy a homestead |  He homesteaded 160 acres in Oklahoma. 他在俄克拉荷马州获得了160英亩的宅基地。| settled, occupied | abandoned | verb
home-steaded: /ˈhəʊmˌstedɪd/ | adj. | 已取得宅地的 | having acquired or occupied a homestead |  He lived in a homesteaded cabin. 他住在一个已取得宅地的木屋里。| settled, occupied | abandoned | adjective


Etymology: From home + stead.  "Stead" originally meant "place" or "position".
