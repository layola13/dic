canary:/kəˈneəri/ | n. |金丝雀；淡黄色；女歌手|A small, yellow songbird originally from the Canary Islands, often kept as a pet. Also, a bright yellow color, or a female singer. |例句：My canary sings beautifully every morning. 我的金丝雀每天早上都唱得很动听。

例句：She wore a dress the color of a canary. 她穿了一件金丝雀颜色的连衣裙。|近义词：songbird, finch (for the bird); yellow (for the color); vocalist, singer (for the singer) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


canary:/kəˈneəri/ | adj. |淡黄色的|Having a bright yellow color. |例句：The walls were painted a canary yellow. 墙壁被漆成了淡黄色。

例句：She bought a canary yellow dress for the summer party. 她为夏季派对买了一件淡黄色的连衣裙。|近义词：yellow, bright yellow, lemon yellow |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From Spanish *canario*, "of the Canary Islands," from Latin *canaria*, "of dogs," from *canis*, "dog." The islands were named for the large dogs found there, not the birds. The birds were later named after the islands.
