apace:/əˈpeɪs/| adv.|快速地，迅速地|At a fast pace; swiftly.  It indicates that something is happening quickly or at a rapid rate.|Construction on the new highway is proceeding apace. 新高速公路的建设正在快速进行。|近义词：swiftly, rapidly, quickly, speedily, expeditiously|反义词：slowly, gradually, leisurely|词性:副词

Etymology: From Middle English *a pas*, from *a* (meaning "on, in, at") + *pas* (meaning "pace, step").  Ultimately from Latin *passus* ("step").
