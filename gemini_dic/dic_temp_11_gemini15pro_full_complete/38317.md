concealments:/kənˈsiːlmənts/
n.
隐藏；隐蔽；隐瞒；隐蔽处
the act of hiding something, or the fact that it is hidden
The spy used several concealments to avoid detection.  这个间谍用了一些隐藏方法来躲避侦查。

The documents were discovered in a clever concealment behind a loose brick. 这些文件在一个松动砖块后面的巧妙的隐蔽处被发现。

Near synonyms: hiding, cover, camouflage, disguise, secrecy

Antonyms: revelation, exposure, disclosure
