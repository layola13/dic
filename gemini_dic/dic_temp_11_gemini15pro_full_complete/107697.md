lanolin: /ˈlænəlɪn/ | n. | 羊毛脂，羊毛蜡 | A fatty substance secreted by the sebaceous glands of sheep, used in ointments and cosmetics. |  Lanolin is a common ingredient in moisturizing creams. 羊毛脂是保湿霜中的常见成分。

Lanolin is also used in some lip balms to prevent chapping. 羊毛脂也用于一些润唇膏中以防止嘴唇干裂。


| 近义词: wool fat, wool wax | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
