out-of-character:/ˌaʊt əv ˈkærɪktər/ | adj. | 不符合性格的，不寻常的 | Not typical of someone's usual behavior; unusual or unexpected. |  He's usually very shy, so that outburst was completely out-of-character. 他通常很害羞，所以那次爆发完全不符合他的性格。| atypical, unusual, uncharacteristic, aberrant | typical, characteristic, usual, normal | adjective

out-of-character:/ˌaʊt əv ˈkærɪktər/ | adj. | 格格不入的 | Not fitting in with a particular group or situation. | Her flamboyant dress was out-of-character for the somber occasion.  她华丽的连衣裙与这庄严的场合格格不入。 | incongruous, inappropriate, unsuitable | appropriate, suitable, fitting | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"out-" (Old English *ūt, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *ūt) meaning "out, outside, away" + "of" (Old English of, from Proto-Germanic *af) meaning "from, away from" + "character" (Middle English caracter, from Old French caractere, ultimately from Latin character, from Greek kharaktēr, meaning "a distinguishing mark, brand")
