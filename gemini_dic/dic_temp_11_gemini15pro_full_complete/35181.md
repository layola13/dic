cloys:/klɔɪz/| v.|使腻烦，使过饱；使厌倦|to cause someone to feel that they have had too much of something enjoyable, so that they no longer enjoy it| The sweetness of the dessert soon cloys. 这道甜点的甜味很快就让人腻了。|近义词：satiate, surfeit, glut|反义词: stimulate, entice|词性:动词

cloys:/klɔɪz/| v.|(古语)阻塞，堵塞 (obsolete)|to block or obstruct something|The narrow passage was cloyed with debris.  狭窄的通道被碎石堵塞了。（古语用法，现代英语很少使用）|近义词：obstruct, block, clog|反义词: clear, open|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *cloien, *cloyen, from Anglo-Norman *cloer, *cloyer (“to nail, to spike, to stop up, to clog”), ultimately from Latin claudere (“to close, to shut”).
