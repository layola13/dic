outcurse:/ˌaʊtˈkɜːrs/
n.
诅咒，咒骂
An act of cursing someone or something.
He uttered a furious outcurse against his enemies. 他愤怒地诅咒他的敌人。
近义词：curse, malediction, imprecation, anathema
反义词：blessing, benediction


词源:
From out- + curse.  "Out-" intensifies the meaning of "curse."
