frolicsome: /ˈfrɒlɪksəm/ (US) /ˈfrɒlɪksəm/ (UK)| adj. |嬉戏的，爱闹着玩的 | Full of gaiety; playful; frisky. Characterized by lively and playful activity. | The frolicsome puppies chased each other around the garden. 嬉戏的小狗在花园里互相追逐。|  The children were frolicsome in the park, running and laughing. 孩子们在公园里嬉闹着，跑来跑去，笑着。 | 近义词: playful, frisky, lively, sportive, merry, jolly | 反义词: serious, solemn, subdued, quiet, still | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From frolic + -some.  Frolic itself comes from Middle Dutch vrolyc "joyful," related to Old High German frōlich (German fröhlich).
