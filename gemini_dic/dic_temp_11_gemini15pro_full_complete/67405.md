externalistic:/ˌɛkstɜːrnəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
外在的，外部主义的
Relating to or characterized by externalism, a philosophical theory that emphasizes the role of external factors in shaping mental states and determining meaning.
Externalistic factors, such as social structures and cultural norms, play a significant role in individual behavior. 外在因素，例如社会结构和文化规范，对个人行为起着重要作用。
The externalistic theory of meaning holds that the meaning of a word is determined by its relation to the world. 外部主义的意义理论认为，一个词的意义是由它与世界的关系决定的。
近义词: external, extrinsic, outward
反义词: internal, intrinsic, inward
词性: adjective
