fateful:/ˈfeɪtfəl/| adj.|决定性的；重大的；致命的；预言性的|Having an important and often negative effect on the future.|例句：a fateful decision 一个重大的决定

例句：The fateful day arrived when she had to make her final decision.  她不得不做出最终决定的那重要的一天到来了。

近义词：critical, decisive, momentous, significant, portentous|反义词:insignificant, trivial|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from fate + -ful.
