mollycoddled:/ˈmɑːliˌkɑːdld/ | adj. | 娇生惯养的，溺爱的 | Pampered and overprotected, treated with excessive indulgence. | He was a mollycoddled young man, unable to cope with the harsh realities of life. 他是一个娇生惯养的年轻人，无法应对生活中残酷的现实。| The mollycoddled child threw a tantrum when he didn't get his way.  这个娇生惯养的孩子在没有得到满足的时候大发脾气。| 近义词: pampered, coddled, spoiled, overprotected | 反义词: independent, self-reliant, hardened | 词性: 形容词

mollycoddle:/ˈmɑːliˌkɑːdl/ | v. | 娇惯，溺爱 | To treat someone with excessive indulgence and overprotection. |  She mollycoddles her dog, letting it sleep in her bed and feeding it gourmet food. 她溺爱她的狗，让它睡在她的床上，并喂它美味的食物。| Don't mollycoddle him; he needs to learn to stand on his own two feet.  不要溺爱他；他需要学会自立。| 近义词: pamper, coddle, spoil, overprotect | 反义词: neglect, ignore, discipline | 词性: 动词
