calluses:/ˈkæləsəz/ (US), /ˈkæləsɪz/ (UK)| n. |老茧；胼胝；硬结|A thickened and hardened part of the skin or soft tissue, especially in an area that has been subjected to friction.|Example 1:  The guitarist had calluses on his fingertips. 吉他手的手指上有老茧。

Example 2: Years of hard work had given him calluses on his palms. 多年的辛苦工作使他的手掌上长满了老茧。|近义词：corn, hardening, thickening|反义词: softness, tenderness|词性: Noun
