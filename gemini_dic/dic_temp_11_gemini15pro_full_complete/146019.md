overdrainages:/ˌoʊvərˈdreɪnɪdʒɪz/
n.
过度排水，排水过度
Excessive drainage, the removal of too much water from an area.
The overdrainages of the peat bogs led to their degradation. 泥炭沼泽的过度排水导致了它们的退化。
over-irrigation, waterlogging
under-drainage, inadequate drainage
noun
