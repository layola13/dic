heeding:/ˈhiːdɪŋ/| n. |注意，留心|The act of paying attention to something; taking notice of. |例句：Heeding the warnings, they evacuated the area. 注意到警告后，他们撤离了该地区。

例句：She wasn't heeding my advice. 她没听我的劝告。

近义词：attention, notice, regard, mindfulness, observing, listening, alertness|反义词:ignoring, neglecting, disregarding, inattention|词性:noun (gerund or verbal noun)
