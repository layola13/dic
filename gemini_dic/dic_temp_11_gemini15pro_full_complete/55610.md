disadvantageous:/ˌdɪsədvænˈteɪdʒəs/
adjective
不利于；不利的；有害的
Presenting or creating unfavorable circumstances; detrimental.
It's disadvantageous to be short if you want to play basketball. 如果你想打篮球，个子矮是不利的。
The current economic climate is disadvantageous to small businesses.  当前的经济环境对小型企业不利。

近义词: unfavorable, detrimental, adverse, harmful, prejudicial
反义词: advantageous, beneficial, favorable, helpful

词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *disadvantageosus, from dis- "not, opposite of" + Late Latin *advantageosus "advantageous," from Latin abante "in front" + agent-, present participle stem of agere "to drive, lead, do, act."
