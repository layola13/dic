microbenthos: /ˌmaɪkrəʊˈbɛnθɒs/ (UK) /ˌmaɪkroʊˈbɛnθɑːs/ (US)| n. |微型底栖生物|Benthic organisms, especially those too small to be caught by a bottom sampler with a mesh size of 1 mm.  These organisms live in or on the sediments of a body of water.|The study focused on the microbenthos of the intertidal zone. 这项研究集中在潮间带的微型底栖生物。|Synonyms: microfauna (sometimes), meiobenthos (partially overlapping) |Antonyms: macrobenthos| noun
