cygnets:/ˈsɪɡnɪts/| n. |小天鹅（复数）|Young swans, typically those not yet fully fledged.|例句：The cygnets were swimming close to their mother. 小天鹅们紧紧地游在母亲身边。

例句：We saw a family of cygnets on the lake. 我们在湖上看到了一家小天鹅。|近义词：baby swans|反义词:adult swans|词性:noun
Etymology: From Middle English *signet, diminutive of signe (“swan”), from Old English cygnet, diminutive of cygn (“swan”), from Proto-Germanic *swangi- (“to swing, sway”), referring to the swan's graceful movements.  Cognate with German Schwan (“swan”). The spelling with cy- was influenced by French cygne.
