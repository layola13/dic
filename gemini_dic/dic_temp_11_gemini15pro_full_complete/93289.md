ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ilke, from Old English *ilca, *ylca (“same”), from Proto-Germanic *ijalīkaz (“same”). Cognate with Scots ilk (“same”), West Frisian ilike (“same”), Dutch gelijke (“equal”), German gleich (“equal”). More at each.
