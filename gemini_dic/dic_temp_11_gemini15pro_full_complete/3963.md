agrostographical:/əˌɡrɒstəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
农业地理学的
Relating to the geographical distribution of plants, especially agricultural crops.  Describing or relating to agrostography, the branch of biogeography dealing with the geographical distribution of grasses.
The agrostographical study revealed the optimal regions for cultivating specific types of rice. 这项农业地理学研究揭示了种植特定类型水稻的最佳区域。
Agrostographical maps are essential tools for agricultural planning. 农业地理图是农业规划的重要工具。
Near Synonyms: phytogeographical, biogeographical
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider terms like "non-geographical," "unlocalized")
