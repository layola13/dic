export:/ɪkˈspɔːrt/ (US) /ɪkˈspɔːt/ (UK)| vt.|出口，输出|The company exports coffee to Europe. 这家公司向欧洲出口咖啡。|近义词:sell abroad, ship overseas|反义词:import|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *exporten*, from Latin *exportāre* (“to carry out, carry away”), from *ex-* (“out of, from”) + *portāre* (“to carry”).
