bedrabble:/bɪˈdræbəl/
adjective
邋遢的，泥泞的，湿透的
wet and dirty, or covered in mud
The children came home bedraggled and bedrabbled after playing in the rain. 孩子们在雨中玩耍后，回家时浑身湿透，泥泞不堪。
近义词:muddy, soiled, drenched, draggled
反义词:clean, tidy, immaculate
词性:adjective

bedraggle:/bɪˈdræɡəl/
verb
使…湿透，拖脏，弄乱
make (something) wet and limp; cause (someone) to appear untidy
Her hair was bedraggled by the rain. 她的头发被雨淋湿了。
近义词:drench, soak, draggle
反义词:dry, preen
词性:verb
