dissected:/daɪˈsektɪd/ /dɪˈsektɪd/
verb, adjective
解剖的，仔细分析的
carefully examined or analyzed.  If something is dissected, it is cut apart in order to study its internal structure.  It can also mean to analyze something in great detail.
The biology students dissected a frog. 生物系的学生解剖了一只青蛙。
The lawyer dissected the witness's testimony. 律师仔细分析了证人的证词。
近义词: analyzed, examined, scrutinized, investigated
反义词: synthesized, assembled, constructed
词性: past participle of dissect
词源: From Latin dissectus, past participle of dissecare "to cut apart," from dis- "apart" + secare "to cut."
