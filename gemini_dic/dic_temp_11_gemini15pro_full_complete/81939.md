grimacingly:/ˈɡrɪməsɪŋli/ | adv. |  做鬼脸地，痛苦地 | In a way that involves grimacing; with a grimace.  Showing pain or discomfort in the face. | He grimacingly accepted the bitter medicine. 他痛苦地咽下了苦药。| 近义词：wincingly, painfully, reluctantly | 反义词：smilingly, happily, readily | 词性: 副词

词源: From grimace + -ing + -ly.  "Grimace" comes from the Old French "grimace" meaning "distorted face."
