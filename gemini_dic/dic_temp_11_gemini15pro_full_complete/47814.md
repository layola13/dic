decarbonizing:/diːˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
脱碳
The process of reducing or eliminating carbon dioxide emissions.
Many countries are decarbonizing their economies to combat climate change. 许多国家正在对其经济进行脱碳以应对气候变化。
decarbonise, reducing emissions, going green
carbonizing, increasing emissions
