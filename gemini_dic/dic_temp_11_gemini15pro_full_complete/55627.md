dit:/dɪt/
noun
小点; 点; 短音; 少量
a short high-pitched sound made by electronic equipment
例句：The dit and dah of Morse code. 摩尔斯电码的点和划。
例句：He sent a dit to his friend using Morse code. 他用摩尔斯电码给他的朋友发了一个点。
近义词：dot, speck, point
反义词：dash (in Morse code)
词性：n.


dit:/dɪt/
verb
轻敲; 轻拍
to make a short high-pitched sound
例句：The machine ditted regularly. 机器发出有规律的滴答声。
例句：She ditted her fingers on the table impatiently. 她不耐烦地用手指轻敲桌子。
近义词：tap, pat
反义词：N/A
词性：v.


Etymology:  Likely imitative of a short, sharp sound.
