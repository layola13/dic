ostentatiously: /ˌɒstɛnˈteɪʃəsli/ /ˌɑːstɛnˈteɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 炫耀地，招摇地 |  Characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to impress or attract notice.  In a manner meant to attract attention, especially in a way that shows wealth or importance. | He ostentatiously displayed his expensive watch. 他炫耀地展示了他昂贵的手表。|  She ostentatiously flicked her hair back. 她炫耀地甩了甩头发。 | synonym: showily, flamboyantly, pretentiously, conspicuously | antonym: modestly, humbly, discreetly, subtly | word type: adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ostentatiosus, from Latin ostentatio (“showing, display”), from ostentare (“to display”), frequentative of ostendere (“to show, exhibit”).
