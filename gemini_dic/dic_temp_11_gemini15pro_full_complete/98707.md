insupposable:/ˌɪnsəˈpoʊzəbl/
adjective
不能假设的，不可想象的
Not capable of being supposed or imagined; inconceivable.
Her sudden disappearance was insupposable; no one could have predicted it. 她突然失踪是不可想象的；没有人能够预料到。
The idea of him betraying us is insupposable. 他背叛我们的想法是不可想象的。
Synonyms: inconceivable, unimaginable, unthinkable, improbable
Antonyms: conceivable, imaginable, thinkable, probable
词性: 形容词
