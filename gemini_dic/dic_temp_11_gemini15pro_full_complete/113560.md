macaronic:/ˌmækəˈrɒnɪk/ | adj. |滑稽混合的，用多种语言混杂写成的|Characterized by a mixture of languages, especially Latin mixed with vernacular languages in a jocular or burlesque way. | The poet entertained the crowd with a macaronic verse, mixing Latin and Italian phrases for comic effect.  诗人用一首滑稽混合的诗歌娱乐了观众，将拉丁语和意大利语短语混合在一起，产生了喜剧效果。| synonyms: burlesque, jumbled, mixed, multilingual | antonyms: homogenous, monolingual, pure, unmixed | adjective

macaronic:/ˌmækəˈrɒnɪk/ | n. |滑稽混合诗，用多种语言混杂写成的作品| A composition, especially verse, containing a mixture of languages, typically Latin with the vernacular, often for humorous effect. |  His latest work is a macaronic, blending English and French in a playful manner. 他的最新作品是一部滑稽混合作品，以一种俏皮的方式融合了英语和法语。| synonyms: medley, potpourri, hodgepodge | antonyms:  monograph, treatise | noun
