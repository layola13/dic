nonpictorial:/ˌnɒnpɪkˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. | 非图画的，非形象的 | Not consisting of or represented by pictures; not pictorial.  | The report was nonpictorial, consisting only of text and tables.  这份报告是非图画的，只包含文字和表格。 | The design was simple and nonpictorial, relying on geometric shapes.  设计简洁明了，非图像化，依靠几何形状。 | 近义词：abstract, nonrepresentational, textual | 反义词：pictorial, graphic, visual | 词性: adjective

Etymology: non- + pictorial. "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not." "Pictorial" comes from the Latin "pictorius," meaning "pertaining to painting."
