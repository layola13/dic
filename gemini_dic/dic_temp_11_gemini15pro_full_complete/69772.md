fermata:/fərˈmɑːtə/ /fərˈmeɪtə/
| n. |延音，延长记号；休止|A pause of unspecified length on a note or rest in music, indicated by a symbol over the note or rest.|The conductor added a fermata to the final chord, allowing the music to resonate. 指挥在最后一个和弦上加了一个延音，让音乐回响。|近义词：pause, hold, rest|反义词: continuation|词性:名词
