flanconade:/ˌflæŋkəˈneɪd/ | n. |侧翼攻击，包抄；侧翼炮火|A military maneuver or attack directed at the flank of an enemy formation.|The general ordered a flanconade to break the enemy lines. 将军下令侧翼攻击以突破敌人的防线。|Synonyms: flank attack, outflanking maneuver |Antonyms: frontal assault| noun
