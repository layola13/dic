dethroner:/dɪˈθrəʊnər/ | n. | 废黜者，罢黜者 | Someone who dethrones another, especially a monarch. | The dethroner of the former king was never officially identified.  废黜前国王的人从未被正式确认。| synonyms: deposer, ouster, remover | antonyms: entroner, installer, inaugurator | noun

dethrone:/dɪˈθrəʊn/ | vt. | 废黜，罢免（尤指君主）| To remove from a throne or position of power or authority. | The military coup dethroned the president. 军事政变废黜了总统。| synonyms: depose, oust, unseat | antonyms: enthrone, install, inaugurate | verb
