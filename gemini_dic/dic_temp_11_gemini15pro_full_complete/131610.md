noncompliance:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈplaɪəns/ | /ˌnɒnkəmˈplaɪəns/ | n. | 不服从，不顺从；违反；未遵守 | Failure or refusal to comply with a rule, regulation, or request. | The company was fined for noncompliance with environmental regulations.  该公司因未遵守环境法规而被罚款。|  The student's noncompliance with school rules resulted in suspension.  这名学生因不遵守校规而被停学。| 近义词：disobedience, violation, infringement, breach | 反义词：compliance, obedience, conformity | 词性: 名词

noncompliant:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈplaɪənt/ | /ˌnɒnkəmˈplaɪənt/ | adj. | 不服从的，不顺从的；违反的；未遵守的 | Failing or refusing to comply with a rule, regulation, or request. | The noncompliant factory was shut down by the authorities.  这家违规工厂被当局关闭。|  The doctor warned the noncompliant patient about the risks of ignoring his advice.  医生警告了不听从医嘱的病人忽视建议的风险。| 近义词：disobedient, rebellious, defiant, unruly | 反义词：compliant, obedient, conforming | 词性: 形容词
