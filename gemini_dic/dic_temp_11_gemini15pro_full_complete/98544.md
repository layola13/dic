instillator:/ɪnˈstɪleɪtər/ | n. |灌输者；谆谆教诲者|A person who instills something, such as a quality, idea, or feeling, into another person. |例句：The teacher was a true instillator of moral values in his students.  这位老师是他学生们真正的道德价值观的灌输者。

例句：She acted as an instillator of confidence in her team. 她充当了团队的信心灌输者。


近义词：inculcator, teacher, mentor, instructor |反义词:corruptor, subverter| 词性: noun
