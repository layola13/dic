hammocklike: /ˈhæməkˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像吊床一样的 | Resembling a hammock; suspended or swinging like a hammock. | The curtains draped hammocklike across the window. 窗帘像吊床一样挂在窗户上。 |  The material hung hammocklike between two trees.  这种材料像吊床一样悬挂在两棵树之间。 | 近义词: suspended, hanging, swinging | 反义词: taut, rigid | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From "hammock" + "-like". "Hammock" comes from the Spanish "hamaca", originally from the Taino (Arawakan) word "hamaka".  "-like" is an Old English suffix meaning "having the form or characteristics of".
