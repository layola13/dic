outpursues:/ˌaʊtpərˈsuːz/ | v. |  超越，胜过，超过 | To pursue something more intensely or successfully than someone or something else. |  The new company outpursued its competitors in the race for market share. 这家新公司在市场份额的竞争中胜过了它的竞争对手。 |  Example: She outpursued all the other runners and won the marathon. 她超越了所有其他选手，赢得了马拉松比赛。| Synonyms: surpass, outstrip, outdo, excel, exceed | Antonyms: lag behind, fall behind | Verb
