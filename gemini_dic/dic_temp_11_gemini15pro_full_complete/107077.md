laddery:/ˈlædəri/
adjective
梯状的；梯子似的
Resembling or consisting of a ladder or ladders.
The rock face was laddery, with narrow ledges and crevices.  岩石表面呈梯状，带有狭窄的岩架和裂缝。
The children climbed the laddery scaffolding. 孩子们爬上了梯子状的脚手架。
Near synonyms: ladder-like, stepped, tiered
Antonyms: smooth, unbroken, continuous
词性: adjective
