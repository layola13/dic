burgherships: /ˈbɜːrɡərʃɪps/
|n.|市民身份，公民身份；市民权|The status of being a burgher, or citizen, especially of a town or city. This includes the rights, privileges, and duties associated with such membership.|Many immigrants sought burgherships in their new countries to gain full legal rights. 许多移民在新国家寻求公民身份以获得完整的合法权利。|
|Synonyms: citizenship, freedom of the city|
|Antonyms: statelessness, alien status|
