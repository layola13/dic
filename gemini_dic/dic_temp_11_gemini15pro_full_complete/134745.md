nonmedullated:/ˌnɒnˈmɛd.jʊ.leɪ.tɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɛd.jə.leɪ.tɪd/| adj.|无髓的，无髓鞘的|Not covered with or containing a myelin sheath.  This usually refers to nerve fibers. |Nonmedullated nerve fibers transmit signals more slowly than myelinated ones. 无髓神经纤维比有髓神经纤维传递信号慢。|近义词:unmyelinated|反义词:myelinated|词性:形容词
