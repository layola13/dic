moseying:/ˈmoʊziɪŋ/ (US), /ˈməʊziɪŋ/ (UK)| v. | 漫步，闲逛 |  Moving in a slow, relaxed way, often without a particular destination. | He was moseying along the beach, enjoying the sunset. 他沿着海滩漫步，欣赏日落。|  She moseyed into the cafe and ordered a latte. 她慢悠悠地走进咖啡馆，点了一杯拿铁。 | synonyms: strolling, ambling, wandering, meandering | antonyms: rushing, hurrying, sprinting | verb
