chintz:/tʃɪnts/| n.|印花棉布，光滑的印花棉布|A glazed printed cotton fabric, often with a floral pattern.|The curtains were made of a cheerful chintz.|近义词: calico, glazed cotton|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 plain fabric, unpatterned fabric)|词性: noun
Etymology: From Hindi chī̃ṭ, from Sanskrit chitra “bright, variegated.”
