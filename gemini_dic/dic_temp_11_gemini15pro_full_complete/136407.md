nonretaliation:/ˌnɒn.rɪ.tæ.liˈeɪ.ʃən/ /ˌnɑːn.rɪ.tæ.liˈeɪ.ʃən/
n.
nonretaliation
不报复，不打击
The policy of nonretaliation was crucial for maintaining peace. 不报复政策对于维持和平至关重要。
The agreement included a clause guaranteeing nonretaliation against whistleblowers.  该协议包含一项条款，保证不会对举报人进行报复。
近义词:forgiveness, pardon, leniency, forbearance
反义词:retaliation, revenge, reprisal
词性:名词

nonretaliations:/ˌnɒn.rɪ.tæ.liˈeɪ.ʃənz/ /ˌnɑːn.rɪ.tæ.liˈeɪ.ʃənz/
n. (plural)
nonretaliations (usually used in the singular)
不报复行为（通常使用单数形式）
The treaty called for nonretaliations in the event of an accidental border crossing.  该条约呼吁在发生意外越境事件时不采取报复行动。
Discussions about nonretaliations were central to the peace talks.  关于不报复的讨论是和平谈判的核心。
近义词:amnesties, pardons
反义词:retaliations, revenges, reprisals
词性:名词


词源:
From non- + retaliation.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "the opposite of."  "Retaliation" comes from the Late Latin word "retaliatio,"  meaning "a paying back in kind," from "retaliare"  meaning "to repay in kind."
