discontinues:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuːz/
verb
停止；中断；终止
To stop doing something; to interrupt or end something.
The company discontinues the production of this model.  这家公司停止了这个型号的生产。
The train service will be discontinued from next month. 火车服务将从下个月起停止。
synonyms: cease, stop, terminate, abandon, halt
antonyms: continue, resume, maintain, persist
词性: 动词


discontinued:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuːd/
adjective
停止的；中断的；终止的
Having been stopped or interrupted; no longer in production or use.
This is a discontinued product.  这是一个停产的产品。
The discontinued railway line is now a popular walking path.  这条废弃的铁路线现在是一条很受欢迎的步行道。
synonyms: stopped, ceased, terminated, abandoned, obsolete
antonyms: continued, current, ongoing, active
词性: 形容词

discontinuation:/ˌdɪskɒntɪnjuˈeɪʃn/
noun
停止；中断；终止
The act of stopping or interrupting something; the state of being stopped or interrupted.
The discontinuation of the service caused much inconvenience.  这项服务的停止造成了很多不便。
The discontinuation of funding led to the project's failure. 资金的中断导致了项目的失败。
synonyms: cessation, termination, interruption, abandonment
antonyms: continuation, resumption, persistence
词性: 名词

词源:
From the Late Latin discontinuatus, past participle of discontinuare "to break the continuity of," from dis- "apart" + continuare "to continue."
