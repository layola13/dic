groundwork:/ˈɡraʊndwɜːrk/| n.|基础工作，准备工作|Preliminary or basic work that is done in preparation for something else. |例句：The team laid the groundwork for the project.  团队为该项目奠定了基础工作。

例句：Without proper groundwork, the building will collapse. 没有适当的基础工作，建筑物将会倒塌。|近义词：foundation, base, basis, preparation, footing |反义词:superstructure, conclusion|noun
