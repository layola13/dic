individuator:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒueɪtər/
n.
个体化者，赋予个体特征者
One that individuates; that which bestows individuality.  Something that distinguishes a particular person or thing from others of the same kind.
The artist's unique style serves as an individuator, setting their work apart.  艺术家的独特风格使其作品与众不同，成为一个赋予个体特征的因素。
synonyms: differentiator, distinguisher, identifier
antonyms: generalizer, homogenizer
noun
