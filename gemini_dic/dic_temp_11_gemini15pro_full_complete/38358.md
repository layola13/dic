concentrative:/ˈkɒnsəntrətɪv/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəntreɪtɪv/ (US)| adj.|专注的，专心的|Characterized by concentration; tending to concentrate.|She has a concentrative mind, perfect for detailed work. 她心思专注，非常适合做细致的工作。|近义词: focused, attentive, absorbed|反义词: distracted, inattentive, unfocused|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin concentrativus, from concentrare "to concentrate" + -ivus.  The English word "concentrative" emerged in the mid-19th century.
