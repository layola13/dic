interleave:/ˌɪntərˈliːv/| v. |插入；交错；散布|To arrange things so that they are interspersed with each other.|She interleaved the pages of the two books. 她把两本书的书页交错排列。|Synonyms: intersperse, interlace, intertwine|Antonyms: separate, divide|verb

interleaved:/ˌɪntərˈliːvd/| adj. |交错的；插入的|Arranged so that things are interspersed with each other.|The book contained interleaved blank pages for notes. 这本书里插入了空白页以便做笔记。|Synonyms: interspersed, interlaced, intertwined|Antonyms: separated, divided|adjective
