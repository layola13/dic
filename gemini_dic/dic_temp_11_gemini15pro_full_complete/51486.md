detractation:/ˌdiːtrækˈteɪʃən/
noun
诋毁；贬损；诽谤
The act of taking away a part, especially a desirable part; lessening the value or reputation of a person or thing by malicious speech.
The politician's speech was filled with detraction of his opponent's character. 这位政治家的演讲充满了对他对手人格的诋毁。
synonyms: defamation, slander, libel, disparagement, calumny, denigration
antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim, applause, eulogy
n.
