overconcentrated:/ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪtɪd/
adjective
过度集中的；过于浓缩的
excessively concentrated; containing too much of a particular substance in relation to other substances
The coffee was overconcentrated and tasted bitter. 咖啡太浓了，尝起来很苦。
The overconcentrated syrup crystallized in the bottle. 过于浓缩的糖浆在瓶子里结晶了。
近义词:saturated, dense, thick
反义词:dilute, weak, thin
词性:adjective

overconcentrate:/ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪt/
verb
过度集中；过于浓缩
to concentrate excessively; to make something too strong by removing too much water
Be careful not to overconcentrate the mixture. 小心不要把混合物弄得太浓缩。
The company overconcentrated its resources on a single product. 这家公司把资源过度集中在单一产品上。
近义词:oversaturate
反义词:dilute
词性:verb


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively," "too much") + "concentrated" (past participle of "concentrate," from Latin "concentrare" - to bring to a center, to combine)
