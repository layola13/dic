nonobjectification:/ˌnɒnˌɒbdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 非对象化，非物化 |  The act of treating a person as a human being with agency and inherent worth, rather than as a mere object to be used or controlled.  It emphasizes recognizing someone's subjectivity, feelings, and rights. |  The feminist movement promotes the nonobjectification of women in media.  女权运动提倡媒体中女性的非物化。

The company's new policy encourages the nonobjectification of employees and values their individual contributions. 公司的新政策鼓励员工的非物化，并重视他们的个人贡献。| 近义词: humanization, personification, respect, subjectivity | 反义词: objectification, dehumanization, commodification | 词性: 名词


词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + objectification (from object + -ification)
