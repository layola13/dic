It is often used to describe a power that is believed to control what happens in the future.|例句：It seems kismet that we met again after all these years.  多年后我们再次相遇，看来是天意。|例句：She accepted her kismet with quiet dignity. 她平静而庄重地接受了自己的命运。|近义词：fate, destiny, fortune, lot, doom|反义词：free will, choice|词性:名词
