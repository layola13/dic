aberystwyth:/ˌæbəˈrɪstwɪθ/| n.|阿伯里斯特威斯|Aberystwyth is a historic market town and seaside resort town in Ceredigion, Wales, often referred to as the cultural capital of Wales. It is located at the confluence of the rivers Ystwyth and Rheidol.|例句：Aberystwyth is a popular tourist destination. 阿伯里斯特威斯是一个受欢迎的旅游目的地。
