词源:  From Hebrew קַבָּלָה (qabālā, “received tradition, reception”), from קִבֵּל (qibbēl, “to receive, accept”). Ultimately from Akkadian *kabālu (“to receive”).


注意事项：Kabbalas is a less common plural form of Kabbalah.  Kabbalah itself is often used as both singular and plural.
