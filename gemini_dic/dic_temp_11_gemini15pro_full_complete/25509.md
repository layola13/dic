bullionist:/ˈbʊliənɪst/| n.|金块主义者；金本位制拥护者|A person who advocates a monetary system based on gold or silver bullion, especially one in which currency is directly convertible to gold or silver.|The prominent bullionist argued for a return to the gold standard.|Synonyms: goldbug, hard-money advocate|Antonyms: fiat money supporter|noun
