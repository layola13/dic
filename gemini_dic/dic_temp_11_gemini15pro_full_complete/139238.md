obligable:/ˈɒblɪɡəbl/ /ˈɑːblɪɡəbl/
adjective
有义务的；应尽义务的
Bound by duty or obligation; obliged.  Subject to or accountable for fulfilling an obligation.
He felt obligable to help his family. 他觉得有义务帮助他的家人。
obligated, bound, compelled, required
exempt, free, unobligated
词性: 形容词
