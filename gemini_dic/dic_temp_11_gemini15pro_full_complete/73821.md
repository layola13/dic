formful:/ˈfɔːrmfʊl/
adjective
富有形式的；形状优美的
Having a definite or pleasing form; shapely.
例句：The sculptor created a formful statue of a dancer. 雕塑家创作了一个优美的舞者雕像。

例句：The poem was formful, with a clear structure and rhyme scheme. 这首诗歌形式优美，结构清晰，押韵规则。

近义词：shapely, well-formed, graceful
反义词：formless, shapeless, amorphous
词性: adjective
