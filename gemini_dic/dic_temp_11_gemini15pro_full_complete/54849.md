displacing:/dɪsˈpleɪsɪŋ/
verb
取代，置换
the act of moving something from its place or position.
The rising floodwaters displaced thousands of people.  上涨的洪水使数千人流离失所。
近义词: replacing, removing, supplanting, ousting
反义词: placing, positioning, installing, establishing
词性: verb (present participle)
词源: From displace + -ing, from dis- + place. Ultimately from Latin dis- + platea (“broad street, courtyard”).
