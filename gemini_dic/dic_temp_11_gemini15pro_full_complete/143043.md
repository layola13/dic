outbluffing: /ˌaʊtˈblʌfɪŋ/
verb
以虚张声势胜过; 比…更会虚张声势
outdoing someone in bluffing; bluffing more successfully than another.
He won the poker game by outbluffing his opponents. 他通过比对手更会虚张声势赢得了扑克游戏。
近义词: outsmarting, outmaneuvering
反义词: yielding, surrendering
词性: v.


词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "bluff" (verb meaning "to deceive someone by pretending to have more strength or confidence than one actually has") + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
