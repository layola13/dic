coactivity:/koʊækˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 共同活动，协同作用 |The coactivity of several muscles is necessary for complex movements.  多个肌肉的协同作用对于完成复杂运动是必要的。| The report highlighted the coactivity of different government agencies in addressing the crisis. 报告强调了不同政府机构在应对危机中的协同作用。 | 近义词：collaboration, cooperation, synergy | 反义词：inaction, opposition | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From co- (together) + activity.  "Co-" comes from the Latin prefix "com-" (with, together). "Activity" comes from the Late Latin "activitas," from the Latin "actus" (act, deed).
