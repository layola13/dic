mylodon:/ˈmaɪlɵdɒn/ (US) /ˈmaɪlədɒn/ (UK)| n. |大地懒|An extinct genus of ground sloth that lived in South America during the Pleistocene epoch.  It was a large, herbivorous mammal.|例句：The Mylodon was a massive creature, much larger than any living sloth.  大地懒是一种巨大的生物，比任何现存的树懒都要大得多。

例句：Fossil evidence suggests that Mylodon may have survived into the early Holocene. 化石证据表明，大地懒可能存活到了全新世早期。|近义词：ground sloth, Glossotherium|反义词: N/A (extinct animal) |词性:noun
