antipasto:/ˌæntiˈpɑːstoʊ/ (US), /ˌæntiˈpæstoʊ/ (UK)
n.
开胃菜，前菜
A small dish of food served before the main part of a meal.
例句：We started our meal with a delicious antipasto of cured meats and olives. 我们以美味的腌肉和橄榄开胃菜开始了用餐。

例句：The restaurant offers a wide variety of antipasti, including bruschetta and marinated artichokes. 这家餐厅提供各种各样的开胃菜，包括意式烤面包和腌朝鲜蓟。

近义词：appetizer, starter, hors d'oeuvre
反义词:dessert, afters
词性:noun
