matrilinearly:/ˌmæt.rɪˈlɪn.i.ə.li/
adv.
母系地
in a matrilinear way; through the female line; according to a system in which descent and inheritance are traced through the female line.
The Naxi people of southwest China traditionally inherit property matrilinearly.  纳西族传统上是母系社会，财产由女性继承。
近义词: through the female line
反义词: patrilinearly
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From matrilineal + -ly.  Matrilineal from  matri- "mother" + lineal "of or in a line."
