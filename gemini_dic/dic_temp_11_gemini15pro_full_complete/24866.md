brows: /braʊz/
| n. |眉毛; 额; |The part of the face above the eyes covered with short hairs. |She raised her brows in surprise. 她惊讶地挑起了眉毛。|Synonyms: eyebrows, forehead |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical feature) |noun


brows: /braʊz/
| v. |浏览; 吃草; |To look through or glance at casually; to graze. |He browsed the shelves of the bookstore. 他浏览了书店的书架。  The deer were browsing in the meadow. 鹿在草地上吃草。|Synonyms: scan, skim, graze, nibble |Antonyms: scrutinize, pore over, devour |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *brū*, eyebrow, related to Dutch *brauw* and German *Braue*.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brūnō*.
