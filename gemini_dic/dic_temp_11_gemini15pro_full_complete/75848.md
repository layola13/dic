funambulation:/fjuːˌnæmbjʊˈleɪʃn/
n.|走钢丝；走绳索|The act of walking on a tightrope or slack rope. |He impressed the crowd with his skillful funambulation. 他精湛的走钢丝表演给观众留下了深刻的印象。|Synonyms: tightrope walking, ropewalking|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "walking on the ground")|Noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *funambulus "rope-walker," from Latin funis "rope" + ambulare "to walk."
