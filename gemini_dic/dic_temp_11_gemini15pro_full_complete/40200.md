contestable:/kənˈtɛstəbl/ /kənˈtɛstəbəl/
adjective
可争辩的，可质疑的
Open to challenge or dispute; that can be contested.
The election result is contestable.  选举结果是可以质疑的。
The judge's decision was contestable. 法官的判决是可争辩的。
近义词: disputable, debatable, challengeable
反义词: indisputable, undeniable, unquestionable
词性: adjective

contested:/kənˈtɛstɪd/ /kənˈtɛstɪd/
adjective
有争议的，竞争激烈的
Disputed or contended for; competitive.
The contested territory remains a source of conflict. 有争议的领土仍然是冲突的根源。
It was a closely contested election. 这是一场竞争激烈的选举。
近义词: disputed, challenged
反义词: undisputed, unchallenged, agreed upon
词性: adjective

contest:/ˈkɒntɛst/ /ˈkɑːntɛst/
noun
比赛，竞赛，争夺
A competition or struggle for supremacy or prize.
She entered a beauty contest. 她参加了一场选美比赛。
There was a contest for the leadership of the party. 党内为争夺领导权展开了竞争。
近义词: competition, match, game, tournament
反义词: agreement, harmony
词性: noun

contest:/kənˈtɛst/ /kənˈtɛst/
verb
竞争，争夺，质疑
Engage in competition to attain or control something; oppose (an action, decision, or theory) as mistaken or wrong.
He contested the election result. 他对选举结果提出了质疑。
Three candidates are contesting the presidency. 三名候选人正在角逐总统职位。
近义词: challenge, dispute, oppose, compete
反义词: concede, accept, surrender
词性: verb
