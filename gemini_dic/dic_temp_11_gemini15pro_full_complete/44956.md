cryptozoological:/ˌkrɪptoʊˌzuːəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
| adj. | 神秘动物学的 | Relating to the search for and study of animals whose existence or survival is disputed or unsubstantiated, such as the Loch Ness monster and the Yeti. |  Cryptozoological expeditions often venture into remote and unexplored territories. 神秘动物学考察经常冒险进入偏远和未经探索的地区。 |  The museum has an exhibit on cryptozoological creatures.  这家博物馆有一个关于神秘动物的展览。 | 近义词: paranormal, mythical | 反义词: scientific, proven | 词性: adjective

词源:  From crypto- (meaning "hidden" or "secret") + zoological (relating to the study of animals). The term was coined by Bernard Heuvelmans in the mid-20th century.
