modulars:/ˈmɒdjʊləz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdʒələrz/ (US)| n.| 模块; 模块化组件; 预制组件|Units designed for easy assembly and disassembly, often used in construction or manufacturing.  These units can be combined in various ways to create different structures or systems.|  The company specializes in building modulars for schools and hospitals.  这家公司专门为学校和医院建造模块化建筑。| prefabricated units, components, sections| monolithic, integrated| noun
