ethological:/ˌiːθəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ /ˌiːθəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/
adjective
动物行为学的
Relating to the study of animal behavior in natural conditions.
Ethological studies have shown that some animals exhibit altruistic behavior.  动物行为学研究表明，一些动物表现出利他行为。
The researchers used an ethological approach to understand the chimpanzees' social interactions. 研究人员使用动物行为学方法来理解黑猩猩的社会互动。
近义词: behavioral, instinctual
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "learned" in some contexts)
词性: adjective
