ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abacus*, from Ancient Greek ἄβαξ (ábax, “counting board, side-board”), perhaps from a Semitic source such as Hebrew אבק (ʾavaq, “dust”), referring to sand or dust used as a writing surface for early counting boards.
