de-powering:/diːˈpaʊə.rɪŋ/
verb
使失去权力；削弱；剥夺权力
The process of taking away power or authority from someone or something.
The new regulations are aimed at de-powering the unions. 新法规旨在削弱工会的力量。
Synonyms: weakening, disempowering, undermining, crippling
Antonyms: empowering, strengthening, bolstering
verb

de-powering:/diːˈpaʊə.rɪŋ/
adjective
使人失去权力的；削弱的
Having the effect of taking away power or authority.
The de-powering effects of the new law were felt immediately.  新法律的削弱效力立即显现出来。
Synonyms: weakening, disempowering
Antonyms: empowering, strengthening
adjective
