con:/kɒn/ (British) /kɑːn/ (American)| n. |反对票；反对论；骗局；欺诈|An argument or evidence against something; a swindle or trick.|The cons of living in a big city are the noise and pollution.  大城市生活的缺点是噪音和污染。|近义词: disadvantage, drawback, swindle, fraud|反义词: pro, advantage, benefit|词性:名词

con:/kɒn/ (British) /kɑːn/ (American)| vt. |仔细研读；精读；哄骗|To study carefully; to persuade someone to do something by deception.|He conned the old lady out of her life savings. 他骗走了老太太的毕生积蓄。|近义词: study, peruse, deceive, trick|反义词: - |词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "contra" (against).  The noun meaning "swindle" arose from the idea of tricking someone into agreeing with a false proposition.
