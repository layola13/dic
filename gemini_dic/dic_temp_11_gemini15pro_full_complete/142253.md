orphanage: /ˈɔːrfənɪdʒ/ (UK), /ˈɔːrfənɪdʒ/ (US)
n.
孤儿院
A residential institution for the care and education of orphans.
The children were raised in an orphanage after their parents died.  孩子们在父母去世后被抚养在孤儿院里。

近义词: children's home, orphan asylum
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用family home 家庭)
词性: noun
