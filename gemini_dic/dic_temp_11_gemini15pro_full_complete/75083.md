frill:/frɪl/| n. v.|褶边，装饰；装腔作势；(动词)给…加褶边|A decorative edging of gathered or pleated material, typically on clothing; something unnecessary or superfluous, added for ornamentation or show; to decorate with a frill.|例句：The dress had a delicate frill around the neckline. 这件连衣裙领口有一圈精致的褶边。|近义词：ruffle, flounce, furbelow, ornament, decoration|反义词：plainness, simplicity|词性:noun, verb

frill:/frɪl/| v. |给…加褶边；装腔作势|To provide or decorate with a frill; to add unnecessary ornamentation or affectation.|例句：She frilled the edges of the tablecloth. 她在桌布的边缘加了褶边。|近义词：decorate, adorn, embellish|反义词：simplify, strip|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *frillen, likely of imitative origin, related to Middle Dutch *vrille ("curl").
