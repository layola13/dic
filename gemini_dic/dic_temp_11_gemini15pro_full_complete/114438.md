词源:
From Middle English *maken, from Old English macian (“to make, form, build, create, do, perform, compose”), from Proto-Germanic *makōną (“to make, fit”), from Proto-Indo-European *mag- (“to knead, fit”). Cognate with West Frisian meitsje (“to make”), Dutch maken (“to make”), German machen (“to make, do”), Danish mage (“to be able, have time to”), Swedish må (“may”), Icelandic mega (“may, can”).
