consorting:/kənˈsɔːtɪŋ/
consort: v. |交往，结交；陪伴|To spend time with someone, especially someone disapproved of.|He was seen consorting with known criminals. 他被看到与已知的罪犯交往。|Synonyms: associating, fraternizing, mingling|Antonyms: avoiding, shunning|verb

consorting: n. |交往，结交|The act of spending time with someone, especially someone disapproved of.|Her consorting with the enemy led to her arrest. 她与敌人交往导致她被捕。|Synonyms: association, fraternization|Antonyms: avoidance, shunning|noun
