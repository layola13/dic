daybreak:/ˈdeɪbreɪk/| n. |黎明，破晓|The moment of first light in the morning. |例句：We woke at daybreak to watch the sunrise. 我们在黎明醒来观看日出。

例句：Daybreak found them miles away from the city. 破晓时分，他们发现自己离城市已经很远了。|近义词：dawn, sunrise, first light|反义词:sunset, dusk, nightfall|词性:名词
