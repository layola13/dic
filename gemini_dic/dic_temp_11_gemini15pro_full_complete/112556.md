lovelies:/ˈlʌvliːz/
noun
美人們；可愛的東西們
A group of lovely people or things, often used as a term of endearment.
"Send my love to all the lovelies."  把我的愛送給所有美人。
synonyms: beauties, dears, sweethearts
antonyms: uglies, monsters
