ointment:/ˈɔɪntmənt/| n. |药膏；软膏；油膏|A smooth, thick substance, usually containing medicine, that is put on the skin to treat injuries or skin problems.|
She applied some ointment to the burn. 她往烧伤处涂了些药膏。

The doctor prescribed an ointment for his eczema. 医生给他开了治疗湿疹的药膏。|近义词：cream, salve, balm|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "irritant" in some contexts)|词性:noun
