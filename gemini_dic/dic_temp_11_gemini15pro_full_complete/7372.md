animalcular:/ˌænɪˈmælkjələr/
adjective
微小的；由微生物组成的
Relating to or consisting of animalcules; microscopic.
The scientist peered through his microscope at the animalcular life teeming in the drop of pond water. 科学家透过显微镜观察一滴池塘水中丰富的微生物。
近义词: microscopic, tiny, minute
反义词: macroscopic, huge, giant
词性: adjective

animalcule:/ˌænɪˈmælkjuːl/
noun
微生物；微动物
A microscopic animal.
He observed the animalcules swimming in the sample under his microscope. 他在显微镜下观察样本中游动的微生物。
近义词: microorganism, microbe
反义词: macroorganism
词性: noun

词源: From French animalcule, diminutive of animal.  Ultimately from Latin animal, meaning "living being,"  from anima "breath, soul."
