brittling:/ˈbrɪtlɪŋ/| n. |脆化，变脆|The brittling of the metal made it unusable. 金属的脆化使其无法使用。|例句：The brittling of the plastic caused it to crack. 塑料的脆化导致它破裂。

The extreme cold caused a brittling effect on the rubber seals. 极度寒冷导致橡胶密封件变脆。|近义词：weakening, embrittlement|反义词: strengthening, toughening|词性:noun (gerund or verbal noun)

brittling:/ˈbrɪtlɪŋ/| adj. |使变脆的|The brittling effect of the cold was evident. 寒冷的脆化效应是显而易见的。|例句：The brittling cold cracked the pavement. 寒冷的天气使路面开裂。

The brittling agent caused the material to shatter.  脆化剂导致材料破碎。|近义词：weakening, deteriorating|反义词: strengthening, reinforcing|词性:adjective (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
