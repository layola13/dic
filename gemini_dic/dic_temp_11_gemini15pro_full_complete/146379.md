overfloridness:/ˌoʊvərˈflɔːrɪdnɪs/
|n.| 过分华丽；过分修饰|The overfloridness of his prose made it difficult to read.|他文章的过分华丽使得它难以阅读。|近义词：ornateness, flamboyance, extravagance|反义词：simplicity, plainness, conciseness|词性: 名词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "florid" (adjective meaning "having a red or flushed complexion; elaborately or excessively ornamented") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
