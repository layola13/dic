orograph: /ˈɔːrəɡræf/ | n. | 山脉图；地形起伏图 | A diagram or model representing the relief features of a land surface. |  Example 1: The orograph showed the steep slopes of the mountain range.  山脉图显示了山脉的陡坡。

Example 2:  The geologist used an orograph to explain the formation of the valley. 地质学家用地形起伏图来解释山谷的形成。 | Synonym: relief map, topographic map | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "flat map" in a loose sense) | noun
