asseveratory:/əˈsɛvəˌrətɔːri/ /əˈsɛvəˌreɪtɔːri/
adjective
断言的，肯定的
affirming or asserting positively; declaratory
He made an asseveratory statement about his innocence. 他断言自己是无辜的。
近义词: affirmative, assertive, positive, emphatic
反义词: hesitant, uncertain, equivocal, ambiguous
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *asseveratorius, from Latin asseverare "to assert earnestly," from ad- "to" + severus "serious."
