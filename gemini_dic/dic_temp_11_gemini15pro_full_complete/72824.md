foliar:/ˈfoʊliər/| adj.|叶的；叶状的|Relating to or consisting of leaves. |Foliar sprays are absorbed through the leaves of a plant. 叶面喷雾剂通过植物的叶子被吸收。|Synonyms: leafy |Antonyms: root, stem |adjective

foliar:/ˈfoʊliər/| n.|叶面肥料|A fertilizer that is applied to the leaves of plants. |The farmer used a foliar feed to supplement the soil nutrients. 农民使用叶面肥料来补充土壤养分。|Synonyms: leaf fertilizer |Antonyms: soil fertilizer |noun
