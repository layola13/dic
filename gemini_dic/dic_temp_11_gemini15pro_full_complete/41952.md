cosmosphere:/ˈkɒzməsfɪə(r)/ | n. | 宇宙球；天象仪；宇宙；世界 | A spherical theater or building in which images of the planets, stars, and other celestial bodies are projected onto a domed ceiling, giving a realistic simulation of the night sky. It can also refer to the celestial sphere itself or, more broadly, the universe or the world. | Example 1: The students visited the cosmosphere to learn about the constellations. 学生们参观了宇宙球，学习了星座知识。

Example 2:  The vastness of the cosmosphere continues to amaze scientists. 宇宙的浩瀚持续令科学家们惊叹。| Synonym: planetarium, universe, world, cosmos | Antonym:  none (due to its encompassing nature) | noun
