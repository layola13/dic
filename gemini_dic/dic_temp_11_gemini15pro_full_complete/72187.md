floppies:/ˈflɒpiz/| n. |软盘|Flexible magnetic disks, typically 3.5 or 5.25 inches in size, used for storing data, especially in older computer systems.  Now largely obsolete.|I found a box of old floppies in the attic. 我在阁楼里发现了一箱旧软盘。|近义词：diskettes|反义词:hard drives, USB drives|Noun


floppy:/ˈflɒpi/| adj. |松软的，耷拉的|Soft and flexible; tending to droop or hang loosely.|The puppy's ears were long and floppy. 小狗的耳朵又长又耷拉。|近义词：limp, droopy|反义词:stiff, rigid|Adjective
