bim:/bɪm/
noun|Building Information Modeling|建筑信息模型|A digital representation of physical and functional characteristics of a facility.|The architect used BIM to design the new hospital.|synonyms: digital model, virtual design, 3D model|antonyms: blueprint, drawing, 2D model|n.
