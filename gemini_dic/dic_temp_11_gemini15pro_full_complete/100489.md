intervene:/ˌɪntərˈviːn/| v. |干预，介入，插手，调停，阻挠|To come between things, persons, or events; especially to come between disputing people or groups and try to settle their differences.|The teacher intervened in the fight between the two students. 老师介入到两个学生之间的打架中。|synonyms: interfere, meddle, intercede, mediate|antonyms: ignore, abstain, withdraw|verb

intervene:/ˌɪntərˈviːn/| v. |发生，出现（指不好的事情）|To occur or happen between two periods of time or events.|Several years intervened between their first and second meetings. 他们的第一次和第二次会面之间隔了好几年。|synonyms: occur, happen, transpire|antonyms: N/A|verb
