corvetto:/kɔːrˈvettoʊ/ /kɔːrˈvɛtoʊ/
n.
腾跃; 跳跃; 弯曲; (马的)腾空跳跃
a leap or curvet (as of a horse)
The horse performed a beautiful corvetto. 这匹马做了一个漂亮的腾跃。

近义词:leap, bound, jump, curvet, capriole
反义词:descent, fall

词性:noun


词源:
From Italian *corvetta*, diminutive of *corvo* (“raven”), from Latin *corvus*.  The original sense was a hop or jump like that of a raven.
