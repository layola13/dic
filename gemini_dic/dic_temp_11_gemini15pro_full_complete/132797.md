nondyspeptical: /ˌnɒndɪsˈpɛptɪkəl/
adjective
无消化不良的；促进消化的
Not suffering from or causing dyspepsia; promoting good digestion.
Example:  The herbal tea is said to have nondyspeptical properties.  据说这种凉茶具有促进消化的功效。

Example:  He enjoyed a nondyspeptical meal after his long journey.  长途旅行后，他享用了一顿不会引起消化不良的饭菜。

Synonym:  digestive, eupeptic
Antonym:  dyspeptic, indigestible
Word type: adjective
