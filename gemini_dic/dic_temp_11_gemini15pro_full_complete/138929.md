nutritiousness:/nʊˈtrɪʃəsnəs/| n.|营养价值；营养程度|The nutritiousness of a food is determined by its vitamin and mineral content.  一种食物的营养价值取决于它的维生素和矿物质含量。|例句：The nutritiousness of breast milk is unparalleled. 母乳的营养价值是无可比拟的。

The dietitian emphasized the nutritiousness of whole grains. 营养师强调了全谷物的营养价值。|近义词：nutritional value, nourishment|反义词: unhealthiness, poor nutrition|词性:noun
