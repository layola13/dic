hendecagonal:/ˌhɛndɛˈkæɡənəl/| adj.|十一邊形的；十一角形的|Having eleven sides and eleven angles.  Relating to a hendecagon.|例句：A hendecagonal prism has eleven sides. 一个十一棱柱有十一面。

例句：The building was designed with a hendecagonal base. 这座建筑设计为十一边形的底座。|近义词：11-sided, eleven-angled|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps circular or non-polygonal)|词性:adjective
