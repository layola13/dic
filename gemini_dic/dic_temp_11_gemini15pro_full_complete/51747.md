devolvements: /ˌdiːˈvɒlvmənts/ | n. | 移交，下放，转让（权力、责任等） | The act of devolving something, especially power or responsibility, to a lower level. |  The government planned a series of devolvements to regional assemblies. 政府计划了一系列向地方议会下放权力的行动。

The devolvements of responsibilities to local communities empowered them to make their own decisions. 将责任下放给地方社区，使他们能够自己做决定。 | 近义词: delegations, transfers, assignments | 反义词: centralizations, consolidations | 词性: 名词

词源: From the verb "devolve," which comes from the Old French "devolvere" and ultimately from the Latin "devolvere" (to roll down, transfer).
