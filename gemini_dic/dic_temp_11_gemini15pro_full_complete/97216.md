inflatedness:/ɪnˈfleɪtɪdnɪs/
n.
膨胀；夸大；通货膨胀
The state of being inflated or exaggerated.
The inflatedness of the housing market is a concern for many economists. 房地产市场的膨胀让很多经济学家担忧。
The inflatedness of his ego was apparent in his boastful speech. 他自夸的演讲中，自我膨胀显而易见。
近义词:inflation, exaggeration, distension, bombast
反义词:deflation, understatement, modesty, humility
词性:noun

词源: From inflate + -ed + -ness.  "Inflate" comes from Latin inflāre, meaning "to blow into, puff up."
