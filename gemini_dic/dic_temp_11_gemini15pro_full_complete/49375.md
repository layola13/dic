delirant:/dɪˈlɪərənt/| adj.| 谵妄的，神志不清的|Experiencing delirium; raving.  Characterized by or resulting from delirium.|The patient was delirant from the high fever.|近义词：raving, frenzied, unhinged|反义词：lucid, rational, coherent|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin dēlīrāns, present participle of dēlīrāre (“to be out of one's furrow, to rave”), from dē- (“away from, out of”) + līra (“furrow”).
