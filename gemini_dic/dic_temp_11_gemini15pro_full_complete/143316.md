outdwelling:/ˌaʊtˈdwɛlɪŋ/
n.|外住; 外居; 居住在外面|The act of dwelling or living outside, especially outside of a city or town.  A dwelling located outside of a city or town.|Many people are choosing outdwelling as a way to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. 许多人选择住在城外，以此来躲避都市生活的喧嚣。|synonyms: suburban living, rural living, exurban living|antonyms: city dwelling, urban living|noun
