ananthous:/əˈnænθəs/
adjective
无花的，不开花的
Lacking flowers; flowerless.
The fern is an ananthous plant. 蕨类植物是一种无花植物。
Synonyms: flowerless, abloom
Antonyms: flowering, blooming
Etymology: From Greek *ἀνανθής (ananthēs, “flowerless”) from ἀν- (an-, “not”) + ἄνθος (anthos, “flower”)
word class: adjective
