PHRASE->
try to get the jump on = 试图抢占…的先机
get the jump on someone/something = 抢占某人/某事的先机
get a jump on =  抢先一步
get the jump on the competition =  抢占竞争先机
have/get the jump on sb = 比某人抢先一步

词源：  "Get the jump on" originated in the American West, likely referring to literally jumping on someone before they could react, gaining a significant advantage, especially in a fight or ambush. Over time, the meaning broadened to include any situation where someone gains an advantage by acting first.
