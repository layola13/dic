antibromic:/ˌæntiˈbroʊmɪk/
adjective
抗臭味的；防臭的
Preventing or counteracting foul odors; deodorizing.
Antibromic agents are used in some deodorants.  抗臭剂用在一些除臭剂中。
The company patented an antibromic compound for use in athletic wear.  这家公司为一种用于运动服的抗臭化合物申请了专利。
synonyms: deodorizing, deodorant, anti-odor, anti-malodor
antonyms: odoriferous, smelly, foul-smelling
词性: adjective
