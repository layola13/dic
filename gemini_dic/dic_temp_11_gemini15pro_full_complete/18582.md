benighted:/bɪˈnaɪtɪd/
adjective
愚昧的，落后的
in a state of moral or intellectual darkness; unenlightened.
例句：The benighted population was susceptible to the demagogue's propaganda.  这愚昧无知的人群很容易受到煽动家的宣传。

例句：Missionaries sought to bring education to the benighted regions of the world.  传教士试图将教育带到世界上落后的地区。
近义词：ignorant, unenlightened, backward, uncivilized, primitive
反义词：enlightened, educated, civilized, advanced, sophisticated
词性: adjective
