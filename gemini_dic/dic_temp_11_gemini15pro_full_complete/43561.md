ETYMOLOGY->
From crazy + -ness.  Crazy from 1570s, perhaps from craze (n.) "crack, flaw" (1560s), of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Norwegian dialect word krasa "to tear apart." The -ness suffix forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
