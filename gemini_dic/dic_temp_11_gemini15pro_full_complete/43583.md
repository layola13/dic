cream:/kriːm/| n. adj. v.|奶油，乳脂；精华；面霜；奶油色的|A thick, white or pale yellow fatty liquid made from milk, used in cooking or eaten as a dessert.  Also, the best part of something, or a pale yellow color.|She added cream to her coffee. 她往咖啡里加了奶油。|近义词：butter, lotion, best, elite|反义词:skim milk, worst, dregs|noun

cream:/kriːm/| adj.|奶油色的，乳白色的|Having the pale yellow color of cream.|She wore a cream dress to the wedding. 她穿了一件奶油色的连衣裙去参加婚礼。|近义词：off-white, ivory, beige|反义词:black, dark|adjective

cream:/kriːm/| v.|把…搅成糊状；提取精华|To beat a food until it is smooth and thick, or to remove the best part of something.|Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.  把黄油和糖一起搅打至蓬松。|近义词：blend, whisk, extract|反义词:separate, dilute|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cresme, from Late Latin chrisma (“ointment, unguent”), from Ancient Greek χρῖσμα (khrîsma, “anointing, unguent”), from χρίω (khríō, “I anoint”). Doublet of chrism.
