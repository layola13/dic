almsgiver: /ˈɑːmzˌɡɪvər/ | n. |施舍者，布施者 | A person who gives alms, money or goods to the poor and needy. | The almsgiver offered the beggar a warm meal.  施舍者给乞丐提供了一顿热饭。| Synonyms: donor, philanthropist, benefactor | Antonyms: recipient, taker, miser | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ælmessyfan, from ælmesse ("alms") + -yfan ("giver").  The word "alms" itself comes from Old English ælmesse, from Late Latin eleemosyna, from Greek ἐλεημοσύνη (eleēmosynē, “pity, alms”), from ἐλεήμων (eleḗmōn, “merciful, compassionate”).
