confiscation:/ˌkɒnfɪˈskeɪʃn/ (US: /ˌkɑːnfɪˈskeɪʃn/)| n.|没收，充公|The act of taking something away from someone, especially as a punishment or by an authority.|The government ordered the confiscation of all illegal weapons. 政府下令没收所有非法武器。|近义词：seizure, appropriation, forfeiture|反义词: return, restitution|noun
