manipulate:/məˈnɪpjʊleɪt/ /məˈnɪpjəleɪt/
verb
操纵，控制，影响
to control or influence someone or something, often in a skillful or devious way.
He manipulated the stock market for his own profit. 他操纵股市以牟取私利。
control, influence, exploit, maneuver
leave alone, neglect, ignore
词性: 动词
