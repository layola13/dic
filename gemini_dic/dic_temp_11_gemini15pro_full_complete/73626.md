foreworn:/fɔːrˈwɔːrn/
adjective
筋疲力尽的，疲惫不堪的
Exhausted or worn out, especially from overwork or worry.
He looked foreworn and weary after his long journey. 他长途跋涉后看起来疲惫不堪。
The troops were foreworn after months of fighting.  部队经过几个月的战斗后疲惫不堪。
近义词: exhausted, weary, tired, fatigued, spent
反义词: energetic, refreshed, invigorated
词性: adjective

foreworn:/fɔːrˈwɔːrn/
adjective
(因过度使用而)破旧的
Worn out or dilapidated from excessive use.
Her foreworn coat offered little protection from the cold. 她那件破旧的外套几乎抵挡不住寒冷。
The furniture was foreworn and shabby. 家具破旧不堪。
近义词: worn-out, dilapidated, shabby, threadbare
反义词: new, pristine, immaculate
词性: adjective
