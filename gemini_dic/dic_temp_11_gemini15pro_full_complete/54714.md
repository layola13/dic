disowning:/dɪsˈoʊnɪŋ/
| n. |否认，断绝关系|The act of refusing to acknowledge or accept something or someone as one's own.|His disowning of his family shocked everyone. 他与家人断绝关系震惊了所有人。|近义词：repudiation, renunciation, rejection|反义词：acceptance, acknowledgment, embracing|noun

disown:/dɪsˈoʊn/
| vt. |否认，断绝关系|To refuse to acknowledge or accept something or someone as one's own.|He disowned his son after he was convicted of a crime.  在他儿子被判有罪后，他与儿子断绝了关系。|近义词：reject, renounce, repudiate|反义词：accept, acknowledge, embrace|verb
