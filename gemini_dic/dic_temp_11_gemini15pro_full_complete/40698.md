conversionist:/kənˈvɜːrʒənɪst/ | n. |皈依者；改变信仰者；改变主意者 |A person who has been converted to a different religion, belief, or opinion. |  He was a conversionist to Buddhism. 他是一位皈依佛教的人。| synonyms: convert, proselyte, neophyte | antonyms: apostate, heretic | noun

conversionist:/kənˈvɜːrʒənɪst/ | n. |改造者，改建者 |A person who converts something, especially a building, for a different purpose. | The conversionist transformed the old factory into loft apartments.  这位改造者将旧工厂改建成了阁楼公寓。 | synonyms: remodeler, renovator,  transformer | antonyms:  -- | noun
