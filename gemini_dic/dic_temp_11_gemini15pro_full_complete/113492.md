lysimeter:/laɪˈsɪmɪtər/
n.|渗漏计，蒸渗计|A device used to measure the amount of water that percolates through soil or other materials, especially for studying evapotranspiration and drainage.  It typically consists of a container filled with soil and vegetation, designed to allow drainage and measurement of the water that passes through.|The researchers used a lysimeter to study the effects of different irrigation methods on water use by crops. 研究人员使用渗漏计来研究不同灌溉方法对作物用水的影响。|近义词: evapotranspirometer|反义词: None|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *lysimètre*, from Ancient Greek λύσις (lúsis, “dissolution, loosening”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
