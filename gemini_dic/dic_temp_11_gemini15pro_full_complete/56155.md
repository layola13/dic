doddart:/ˈdɒdɑːt/ | n. | 笨蛋；傻瓜 | A foolish or eccentric person. | He's such a doddart, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个笨蛋，又把钥匙锁在车里了。 | 近义词：fool, idiot, nitwit, simpleton | 反义词: genius, sage | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a variant of "dodderer," meaning a person who dodders or walks unsteadily, implying senility and thus foolishness.  The added "art" might be a playful or intensifying suffix.
