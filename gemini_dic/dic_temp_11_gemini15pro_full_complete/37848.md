competitive:/kəmˈpɛtɪtɪv/| adj.|竞争的，有竞争力的|Inclined, desiring, or suited to compete; characterized by competition.  Relating to or characterized by rivalry.|例句：The company operates in a highly competitive market.  这家公司在一个竞争激烈的市场中运营。|近义词：cutthroat, aggressive, ambitious, rivalrous|反义词:noncompetitive, cooperative, unambitious|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From late Latin *competitivus, from Latin competere "to strive in common," from com- "together" + petere "to strive for, seek."
