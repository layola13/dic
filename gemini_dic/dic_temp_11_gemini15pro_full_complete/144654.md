outwrenches:/ˌaʊtˈrɛntʃɪz/
verb
胜过；超过；以扳手拧开
to surpass or exceed in wrenching; to unscrew or loosen with a wrench.
He outwrenched his opponent in the arm-wrestling match. 他在扳手腕比赛中胜过对手。
The mechanic outwrenched the bolt with a large wrench.  机械师用一个大扳手拧开了螺栓。
近义词:outdo, surpass, excel, defeat, conquer, overcome, outstrip, outmaneuver, outsmart
反义词:lose to, be defeated by, succumb to
词性:v.
