cementatory:/sɪˈmɛntətɔːri/ /sɪˈmɛntətɔːri/
adjective
粘合的；胶结的
Relating to or of the nature of cement; having the properties of binding or uniting.
The cementatory substance in concrete is crucial for its strength.  混凝土中的胶凝物质对其强度至关重要。
The protein acts as a cementatory agent, binding the cells together.  这种蛋白质充当胶结剂，将细胞结合在一起。
近义词: adhesive, binding, cohesive, unifying
反义词: separating, dividing, disconnecting
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *caementātōrius, from Latin caementum (“quarry stone, rubble”).
