classify: /ˈklæsɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈklæsɪfaɪ/ (UK)| vt.| 分类，归类 | To arrange or organize things into categories or classes based on shared characteristics or qualities.|  Scientists classify animals into different groups. 科学家将动物分为不同的类别。

He helped to classify the books in the library. 他帮助图书馆对书籍进行分类。

|近义词：categorize, sort, group, order, arrange|反义词:  mix, jumble, confuse, disorder|词性: verb (动词)
