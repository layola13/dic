nonevocative:/ˌnɒnˈvɒkətɪv/ | adj. | 非呼格的 |  Not used as a term of address; not vocative.  It describes words or phrases that are not used to directly address someone or something. | The word "table" is nonevocative, whereas "John!" is vocative. | 近义词: non-addressing | 反义词: vocative | 词性: adjective
