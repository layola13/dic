luring:/ˈlʊrɪŋ/| adj.|诱惑的，吸引人的|Tempting and attractive; enticing.|The bakery window displayed a luring array of pastries.  面包店的橱窗里摆放着一系列诱人的糕点。|The restaurant's luring aroma drew in customers from the street. 餐厅诱人的香味吸引了街上的顾客。|近义词:enticing, tempting, attractive, seductive, alluring|反义词:repulsive, repellent, off-putting, unappealing|词性:形容词
词源:From the verb "lure," which comes from Old French "luire" meaning "to shine, attract birds with a decoy."
