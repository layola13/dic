intensifier:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪər/| n.|增强器，加剧者|A word, especially an adverb or adjective, that has little semantic content of its own but that serves to intensify the meaning of the word or phrase it modifies.|The word "very" is an intensifier in the sentence "I'm very happy."|近义词：amplifier, booster, enhancer|反义词:mitigator, softener|词性:noun
