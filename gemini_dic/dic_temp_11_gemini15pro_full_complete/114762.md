maltas:/ˈmɔːltəs/| n. | 麦芽 | Malt used in brewing or distilling, often from barley.  It refers to grain, typically barley, that has been germinated and then dried. This process converts starches into sugars, essential for fermentation in the production of beer, whisky, and other alcoholic beverages.  Sometimes also used in non-alcoholic drinks and food.| The brewery uses only the finest maltas. 这家酿酒厂只使用最好的麦芽。 | 近义词：malt (singular form, often used interchangeably), barley (raw material) | 反义词: N/A | noun
