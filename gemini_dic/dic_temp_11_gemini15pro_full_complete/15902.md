ballow:/ˈbæləʊ/| n. |捆，包，尤指棉花包|A large bale or bundle, especially of cotton.|The warehouse was filled with ballows of cotton. 仓库里堆满了棉花包。|近义词：bale, bundle, pack|反义词: None|Noun

Etymology:
From Middle French *balle*, from Old French *bale*, from Frankish *balla*.  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *ballō* meaning "ball, round object." This suggests the original meaning referred to a rounded bundle.
