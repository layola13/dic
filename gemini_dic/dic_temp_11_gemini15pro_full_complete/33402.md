churchiest:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃiɪst/
adjective
最像教堂的；最虔诚的
Most resembling a church; most devout or pious.
例句：Of all the villages, this one felt the churchiest. 在所有村庄中，这个感觉最像教堂。

例句：He was the churchiest man I'd ever met, always quoting scripture. 他是我见过的最虔诚的人，总是引用圣经。
近义词：devout, pious, religious
反义词：irreverent, impious, secular
词性: adjective
