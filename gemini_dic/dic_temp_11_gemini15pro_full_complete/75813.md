fumid:/ˈfjuːmɪd/ | adj. | 烟熏的，烟雾弥漫的 | Filled with or exposed to fumes; smoky. | The air in the factory was thick and fumid. 工厂里的空气又浓又呛。 | The fumid atmosphere made it difficult to breathe. 烟雾弥漫的空气使人难以呼吸。 | 近义词: smoky, hazy, foggy | 反义词: clear, airy | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *fumidus, from fumus (“smoke”).
