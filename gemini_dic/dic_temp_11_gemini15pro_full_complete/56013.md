do-it-yourselfer: /ˌduːɪtjərˈsɛlfər/
| n. | 自己动手做的人；DIY爱好者 | A person who prefers to do home repairs, improvements, or other tasks themselves rather than hiring professionals. | My neighbor is a real do-it-yourselfer; he built his entire deck himself. 我的邻居是个真正的DIY爱好者；他亲手建造了整个露台。| Synonyms: handyman, hobbyist, tinkerer | Antonyms: professional, contractor | Noun



Etymology:  A compound formed from "do it yourself" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Do it yourself" emerged in the mid-20th century, reflecting the growing trend of home improvement and self-reliance.
