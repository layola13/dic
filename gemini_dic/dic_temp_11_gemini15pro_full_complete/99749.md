intermeasures:/ˌɪntərˈmeʒərz/
noun
中间措施；中间的步骤；过渡措施
Actions or steps taken between major phases or stages of a process or project.
The intermeasures taken by the government stabilized the economy during the transition. 政府采取的中间措施在过渡时期稳定了经济。
synonyms: interim measures, intermediate steps, transitional actions
antonyms: final measures, concluding steps
词性: 名词(n.)
