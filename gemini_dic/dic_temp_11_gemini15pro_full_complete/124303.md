motherers:/ˈmʌðərərz/
n.
抚育者; 过分溺爱孩子的人
People who mother others, often to an excessive degree.
She's one of those motherers who fusses over everyone. 她就是那种对每个人都过分关心的人。

近义词: nurturers, overprotective parents
反义词: neglectful parents
词性: 名词 (plural)


词源:
From the verb "to mother" (v.) + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns). The verb "to mother" comes from the Old English "mōdor" (mother).
