biosignatures:/ˌbaɪoʊˈsɪɡnətʃərz/
n.
生物标记，生物签名
Any substance, pattern, or phenomenon in a planetary environment that can be interpreted as evidence of past or present life.
Scientists are searching for biosignatures on Mars. 科学家们正在火星上寻找生物标记。

The rover is equipped with instruments to detect biosignatures. 探测车配备了探测生物标记的仪器。

近义词: biomarkers, indicators of life
反义词: abiotic factors, non-living indicators
