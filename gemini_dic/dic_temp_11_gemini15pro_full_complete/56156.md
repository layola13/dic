dodded:/ˈdɒdɪd/ (UK) /ˈdɑːdɪd/ (US)| v.| 打盹，点头，瞌睡|To briefly fall asleep, often unintentionally, characterized by a drooping of the head.|The old man dodded off during the long sermon. 老人听着冗长的布道打起了瞌睡。|
The student dodded in class, tired from studying all night.  这个学生上课打瞌睡，因为学习了一整夜很累。|近义词：dozed, nodded, napped|反义词:stayed awake, remained alert|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dodden, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse *dodda (to toddle).
