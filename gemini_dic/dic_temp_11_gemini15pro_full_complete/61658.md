emissory:/ɪˈmɪsəri/ | adj. | 发射的；放射的 | Having the power or function of emitting; emitting. |  The emissivity of a material is its ability to emit infrared energy.  这种材料的辐射率指的是它发射红外能量的能力。 | synonyms: radiant, emitting, radiative | antonyms: absorbent, non-radiative | adjective

emissory:/ɪˈmɪsəri/ | n. | 发射体；放射体 | Something that emits, especially radiation. | The sun is a powerful emissivity. 太阳是一个强大的发射体。 | synonyms: emitter, radiator | antonyms: absorber | noun
