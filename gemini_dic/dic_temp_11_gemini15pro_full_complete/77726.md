gemmulation:/ˌdʒɛmjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 芽生，芽殖，出芽繁殖 | The process of reproduction by budding, where a new organism develops from an outgrowth or bud due to cell division at one particular site. |  The gemmulation of sponges is a common form of asexual reproduction. 海绵的出芽生殖是一种常见的无性繁殖形式。

The hydra reproduces through gemmulation. 水螅通过出芽繁殖。 | 近义词：budding, sprouting | 反义词：fission | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "gemmula" (small bud) + "-ation" (forming nouns of action or process).  Ultimately from Latin "gemma" (bud, gem).
