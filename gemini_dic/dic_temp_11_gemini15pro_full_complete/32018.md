chequered:/ˈtʃekərd/ (US) /ˈtʃekəd/ (UK)| adj.|格子花纹的；多变的，盛衰无常的|marked with alternating squares of different colors; marked by periods of good and bad fortune|例句：He wore a checkered shirt. 他穿了一件格子衬衫。|例句：She has had a checkered career, with periods of both success and failure. 她的职业生涯起起伏伏，既有成功也有失败。|近义词：varied, mixed, uneven|反义词：uniform, consistent, unvaried|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From the Old French word "eschequier" meaning "chessboard," ultimately from the Latin "scaccarium" referring to a chessboard or counting board. The pattern resembling a chessboard led to its association with variety and change.
