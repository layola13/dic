australopithecus: /ˌɔːstreɪloʊˈpɪθɪkəs/ /ˌɒstrəlɒˈpɪθɪkəs/ | n. |南方古猿|An extinct hominid genus that existed in Africa between four and two million years ago, considered an ancestor of humans. |  Australopithecus afarensis is one of the best-known species of early hominins. 南方古猿阿法种是早期人类中最著名的物种之一。| synonym: hominid,  ape-man | antonym: modern human | noun
