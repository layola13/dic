corrosively:/kəˈroʊsɪvli/
adv.
腐蚀地，侵蚀地
in a corrosive manner; in a way that gradually destroys or weakens something.
The acid rain corrosively affected the ancient statues. 酸雨腐蚀了古老的雕像。
Synonyms: destructively, harmfully, damagingly
Antonyms: constructively, beneficially, helpfully
词性: 副词
