nontheological:/ˌnɒnˌθiːəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非神学的；非宗教的
Relating to subjects other than, or not connected with, theology.
Example:  He offered a nontheological explanation for the phenomenon. 他对这一现象提出了非神学解释。

Example:  The university offers a variety of nontheological courses. 这所大学提供各种非神学课程。

Synonym: secular, lay, worldly
Antonym: theological, religious, divine
词性: 形容词
