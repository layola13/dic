contravener:/ˈkɒntrəˌviːnə(r)/ (US) /ˈkɑːntrəˌviːnər/| n. |违反者，违规者|A person who contravenes a law, treaty, or other rule.|The company was fined for being a contravener of environmental regulations.  这家公司因违反环境法规而被罚款。|Synonyms: violator, offender, transgressor, infringer|Antonyms: conformist, observer, adherent|noun

contravene:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːn/ (US) /ˌkɑːntrəˈviːn/| v. |违反，违背|To go or act contrary to; violate; break (a law, treaty, or other rule).|Parking in this zone contravenes city ordinances. 在此区域停车违反城市条例。|Synonyms: violate, infringe, breach, disobey|Antonyms: obey, comply with, observe, uphold|verb
