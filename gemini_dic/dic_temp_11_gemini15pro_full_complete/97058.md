inferencing:/ˈɪnfərənsɪŋ/
n.
推理；推断
The process of drawing a conclusion from evidence.
From the data collected, the team performed statistical inferencing to understand customer behavior.
从收集到的数据中，团队进行了统计推断以了解客户行为。
deduction, reasoning, conclusion
induction, guessing
noun
