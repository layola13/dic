driftweed:/ˈdrɪftˌwiːd/| n.|漂流的杂草，漂浮的海藻|Detached seaweed carried by water currents or tides.|例句：The beach was covered in driftweed after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上覆盖着漂流的杂草。

例句：Driftweed can provide a habitat for small marine creatures. 漂浮的海藻可以为小型海洋生物提供栖息地。|近义词：seaweed, kelp, wrack|反义词: (无明确的反义词)|词性:名词
