admissible:/ədˈmɪsəbl/ /ədˈmɪsɪbl/ | adj. |可接受的；可采纳的；可容许的|Allowable or acceptable, especially in a court of law.|例句：The judge ruled the evidence admissible. 法官裁定证据可受理。

例句：His excuse was barely admissible. 他的借口勉强说得过去。

近义词：acceptable, allowable, permissible, valid, legitimate|反义词：inadmissible, unacceptable, invalid, prohibited|词性:形容词
