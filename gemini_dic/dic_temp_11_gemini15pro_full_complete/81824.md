grewsomest:/ˈɡruːsəməst/
adjective
最可怕的，最令人毛骨悚然的
Superlative form of gruesome: most gruesome.  Evoking horror and disgust.
The police described the murder scene as the grewsomest they had ever encountered. 警方形容谋杀现场是他们见过的最可怕的。
The movie contained the grewsomest special effects I've ever seen. 这部电影包含了我见过的最可怕的特效。
近义词:horrific, ghastly, macabre, grisly
反义词:pleasant, delightful, agreeable
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From gruesome + -est.  Gruesome from Middle English grue, grueing ("to shudder, to feel horror"), from Old English gryre ("terror, horror").
