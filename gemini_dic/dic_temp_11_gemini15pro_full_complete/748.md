absolute:/ˈæbsəluːt/ /ˈæbsəˌl(j)uːt/| n.|绝对；绝对事物（指不受任何限制的事物）|Something that is absolute; something viewed or existing independently and not in relation to other things. |Moral absolutes are difficult to define.  道德的绝对性很难定义。|近义词：entirety, totality|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English absolut, from Late Latin absolūtus, past participle of absolvere (“to absolve, acquit; set free, detach; complete, finish”), from ab (“from, away from”) + solvere (“to loosen, untie, release, dissolve”).
