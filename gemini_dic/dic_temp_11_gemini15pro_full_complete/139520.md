obtainal: /əbˈteɪnəl/ | adj. | 可获得的，可得到的 |  Relating to something that can be obtained or acquired. This word is not standard English and is rarely used. It appears to be formed by adding the suffix "-al" to "obtain," but this formation is not common. |  (Since "obtainal" is not a standard word, constructing natural examples is difficult.  Here are some illustrative examples that might be found in contexts where the word is coined): The obtainal resources were limited. 可获得的资源是有限的。 The information was obtainal through the library.  这些信息可以通过图书馆获得。 | 近义词: obtainable, available, accessible | 反义词: unobtainable, unavailable, inaccessible | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Likely derived from the verb "obtain" (from Old French "obtenir," from Latin "obtinere" - to hold, possess) with the suffix "-al" added to form an adjective. However, this formation is not standard English.
