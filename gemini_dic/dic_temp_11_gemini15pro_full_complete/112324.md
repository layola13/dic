lorel:/ˈlɔːrəl/ /ˈlɒrəl/| n.|月桂树，月桂冠，荣誉，胜利|A laurel is an evergreen tree or shrub with smooth, shiny leaves.  A laurel wreath was a symbol of victory in ancient Greece and Rome, and laurels are still sometimes used to represent success or honor. |The poet was awarded the laurels for his outstanding work. 这位诗人因其杰出的作品而被授予桂冠。|Synonyms:  accolade, award, honor, triumph, victory|Antonyms: defeat, failure, loss|noun
