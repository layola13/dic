culling:/ˈkʌlɪŋ/| n. |剔除，选择；挑选；宰杀|The annual seal cull is controversial.  每年的海豹捕杀活动备受争议。|近义词：selection, choosing, picking, slaughter|反义词:preservation, conservation|noun
culling:/ˈkʌlɪŋ/| v. |剔除，选择；挑选；宰杀|(cull something from something)  He culled the best passages from the poet's works. 他从这位诗人的作品中挑选出最好的段落。|近义词：select, choose, pick, slaughter|反义词:preserve, conserve|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old French "cuillir" (to gather, collect), from Latin "colligere" (to gather together), from "com-" (together) + "legere" (to gather).  The sense of "selecting and killing" arose from the practice of gathering the weakest animals from a herd for slaughter.
