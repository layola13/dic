chevron-weave:/ˈʃɛvrən wiːv/
n.
人字形编织；人字形图案
A method of weaving or a pattern resembling a series of chevrons.
The upholstery featured a striking chevron-weave pattern.  座椅装饰以醒目的人字形编织图案为特色。
The rug was made with a tight chevron-weave.  地毯采用紧密的人字形编织法制成。
近义词:herringbone weave, zig-zag weave
反义词: (No direct antonym for a weaving pattern)
词性: noun
