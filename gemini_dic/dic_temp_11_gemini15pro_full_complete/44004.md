criminalizing:/ˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使…成为犯罪；宣布…为非法
The act of making something illegal.
例句：They are criminalizing homelessness. 他们正在把无家可归定为犯罪。

例句：The government is considering criminalizing certain types of online speech. 政府正在考虑将某些类型的网络言论定为犯罪。

近义词：proscribing, outlawing, banning
反义词：legalizing, decriminalizing, permitting
词性: v. (动词 -ing 形式，用作现在分词或动名词)
