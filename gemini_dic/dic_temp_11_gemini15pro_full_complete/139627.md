occipitoaxial:/ɒkˌsɪpɪtəʊˈæksiəl/
adjective
枕轴的
Relating to the occiput (back of the head) and the axis (second cervical vertebra).
Occipitoaxial instability is a serious condition. 枕轴不稳是一种严重的疾病。
近义词:atlanto-occipital  (although not strictly synonymous, as it refers to the atlas and occiput)
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

occiput: /ˈɒksɪpʊt/
noun
枕骨
The back part of the head or skull.
The occiput is the bone at the back of the skull. 枕骨是位于颅骨后部的骨头。
近义词: back of the head
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

axial: /ˈæksiəl/
adjective
轴的，轴向的
Relating to or situated around an axis.
The axial skeleton includes the skull, vertebral column, and rib cage.  轴向骨骼包括颅骨、脊柱和肋骨。
近义词: central, longitudinal
反义词: peripheral, radial
词性: adjective
