multipaths:/ˈmʌltɪpæθs/ | n. | 多路径 | Multiple paths that a signal can take to reach a destination, often causing interference or distortion. | The multipaths in the building caused the Wi-Fi signal to be weak and unreliable.  大楼里的多路径导致Wi-Fi信号微弱且不可靠。| synonyms: multiple routes, alternative paths | antonyms: single path, direct route | noun

multipath:/ˈmʌltɪpæθ/ | adj. | 多路径的 | Relating to or characterized by multiple paths. |  Multipath propagation can affect the accuracy of GPS signals. 多径传播会影响GPS信号的精度。 | synonyms: multi-route, multi-channel | antonyms: single-path, direct | adjective
