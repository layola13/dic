jovian:/ˈdʒoʊviən/ | adj. | 木星的；像木星的；雄伟的，庄严的 | Relating to Jupiter, the largest planet in our solar system; also, majestic or impressive like Jupiter. |例句：The jovian atmosphere is composed mostly of hydrogen and helium. 木星的大气主要由氢和氦组成。

jovian:/ˈdʒoʊviən/ | n. |  (假设的)木星人 | A hypothetical inhabitant of Jupiter.  | 例句：In science fiction, Jovians are often depicted as gas giants. 在科幻小说中，木星人通常被描绘成气态巨行星。

例句：Some scientists speculate about the possibility of microbial life on one of the Jovian moons. 一些科学家推测在木星的某颗卫星上可能存在微生物。 | 近义词：Jupiterian | 反义词：N/A (no direct opposite for a hypothetical being) | 词性:名词
