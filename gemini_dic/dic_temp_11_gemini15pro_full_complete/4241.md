airmen:/ˈɛəmən/ | n. |飞行员；空勤人员|A member of an air force or aircrew, especially one who is not an officer.|The airmen were preparing for their next mission. 飞行员们正在为他们的下一次任务做准备。|Synonyms: pilot, aviator, flier |Antonyms: civilian, ground crew | noun

Etymology: From air + man.  The word "air" traces back to Old French "air" and ultimately from Latin "aer," meaning "air." "Man" comes from Old English "mann," meaning "human being, person."
