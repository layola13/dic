negligee:/ˈnɛɡlɪʒeɪ/ /ˈnɛɡləˌʒeɪ/
noun
女子睡衣；家居服；轻薄的晨衣
A woman's light, loose dressing gown or robe, typically made of sheer or delicate fabric and worn in the bedroom or at home.
She slipped into a silk negligee. 她穿上一件丝绸睡衣。
dressing gown, robe, nightgown, peignoir
daywear, outerwear
