deformities:/dɪˈfɔːrmɪtiz/| n.|畸形，缺陷|A deformity is a significant difference in the shape or structure of a part of the body compared to what is considered normal.  It can be present at birth (congenital) or acquired later in life.|The child was born with severe deformities of the limbs.|近义词：malformations, abnormalities, imperfections, defects|反义词:perfections, normalities|noun
