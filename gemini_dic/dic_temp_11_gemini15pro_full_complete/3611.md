adjective

aged is the past participle of the verb 'age', meaning to grow old. 'ageds' is not a standard English word.  The plural of "aged" as an adjective is still "aged," not "ageds". Use "aged" to describe a group of older people.

Many aged people require assistance with daily tasks. 许多老年人需要帮助完成日常工作。
近义词: elderly, old, senior
反义词: young, youthful
词性: adjective (This is not typically used in the plural form "ageds")
