nonfashionableness:/ˌnɒnˈfæʃənəbəlnəs/
n.
不时尚，不时髦
lack of fashion; being out of style; not conforming to the current trends.
Her nonfashionableness was evident in her outdated clothes. 她的不时尚体现在她过时的衣服上。

The nonfashionableness of his attire made him stand out.  他衣着的过时让他显得格格不入。

近义词:unstylishness, outdatedness, dowdiness
反义词:fashionableness, stylishness, trendiness
词性:noun
