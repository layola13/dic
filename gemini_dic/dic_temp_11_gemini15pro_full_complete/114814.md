mambo:/ˈmæmboʊ/ (US), /ˈmæmbəʊ/ (UK)| n. vi.|曼波舞; 曼波音乐|A Latin American dance of Cuban origin, characterized by strong rhythms and exaggerated hip movements.  Also refers to the music that accompanies this dance.|例句：They danced the mambo all night long. 他们整晚都在跳曼波舞。

例句：The band played a lively mambo. 乐队演奏了一曲活泼的曼波舞曲。|近义词：rumba, salsa, cha-cha-cha|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific dance)|词性:noun, verb
