disappointers:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntərz/
n.|令人失望的人或事物|People or things that cause disappointment.
|The new phone turned out to be one of the biggest disappointers of the year.  这款新手机成了年度最令人失望的产品之一。
|The team, after a string of losses, were seen as disappointers by their fans.  这支队伍在连败之后被球迷视为令人失望的对象。
|synonyms: letdowns, failures, flops|
|antonyms: delights, successes, triumphs|
|noun|


disappoint:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪnt/
v.|使失望|To fail to fulfill the hopes or expectations of.
|I hate to disappoint you, but I can't make it to the party. 我很不想让你失望，但我不能去参加派对了。
|The movie disappointed me; it wasn't as good as the reviews said. 这部电影让我失望；它不像评论说的那么好。
|synonyms: disillusion, frustrate, let down|
|antonyms: please, delight, satisfy|
|verb|
