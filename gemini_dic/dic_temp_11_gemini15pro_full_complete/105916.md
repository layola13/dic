kiwi:/ˈkiːwiː/ /ˈkiwi/ | n. |几维鸟，猕猴桃|A flightless bird native to New Zealand, also refers to the fruit of the Chinese gooseberry.|例句：The kiwi is a national symbol of New Zealand. 几维鸟是新西兰的国家象征。

例句：I love eating kiwi fruit for breakfast. 我喜欢早餐吃猕猴桃。

近义词：(for the fruit) Chinese gooseberry|反义词: None|词性:noun


kiwi:/ˈkiːwiː/ /ˈkiwi/ | adj. |新西兰的|Relating to New Zealand.|例句：Kiwi culture is unique and diverse. 新西兰文化独特而多元。

例句：She speaks with a kiwi accent. 她说话带有新西兰口音。

近义词：New Zealand|反义词: None|词性:adjective
