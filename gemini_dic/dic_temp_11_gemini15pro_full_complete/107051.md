lactucin: /lækˈtjuːsɪn/ | n. | 乳苣素，乳苣苦素 | A bitter crystalline substance found in the juice of wild lettuce (Lactuca virosa).  It acts as a mild sedative and analgesic. |  Lactucin is being studied for its potential medicinal properties. 乳苣素正在被研究其潜在的药用价值。| Lactucin is found in several species of Lactuca. 乳苣素存在于几种莴苣属植物中。 | synonyms: lactucarium, bitter principle | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a chemical compound) | noun
