helpless:/ˈhɛlplɪs/| adj.|无助的，无能的|Unable to manage by oneself; needing the aid of others.|The earthquake victims were utterly helpless, trapped beneath the rubble.|近义词：powerless, dependent, vulnerable|反义词:capable, competent, independent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *helplies, from help + -less.
