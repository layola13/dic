draftback:/ˈdræftˌbæk/ |n.|退税；回扣；返还的关税；通风不良|A partial refund of import duties on goods that are subsequently exported.|The company received a draftback on the exported goods.  该公司收到了出口商品的退税。|synonyms: refund, rebate, remission, drawback|antonyms: surcharge, extra charge|noun


draftback:/ˈdræftˌbæk/ |n.|逆流；回流；反吹|A reverse flow of air, gas, or smoke.|The fireplace experienced a draftback, filling the room with smoke. 壁炉出现了逆流，房间里充满了烟雾。|synonyms: backdraft, backflow, reflux|antonyms: outflow, draft|noun
