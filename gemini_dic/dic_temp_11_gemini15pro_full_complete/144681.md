outyelps:/ˌaʊtˈjɛlps/
verb
大声喊叫，盖过…的喊叫声
to yelp louder than someone or something else
The excited fans outyelped the referee's whistle.  激动的粉丝们的喊叫声盖过了裁判的哨声。
近义词: outshout, outscream
反义词: whisper, murmur
词性: verb

词源:
From out- + yelp.  由out- + yelp组成.  "Out-" prefix indicates exceeding or surpassing in an action. "Yelp" means to utter a sharp, quick cry, especially of pain or alarm.
