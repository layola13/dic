close-run:/ˌkloʊs ˈrʌn/ | adj. | 势均力敌的；难分胜负的；旗鼓相当的 | A close-run contest or competition is one in which the competitors are very close and it is difficult to predict who will win. | The election was close-run, with the winner declared by a mere handful of votes. 选举势均力敌，获胜者仅以几票之差险胜。

The marathon was a close-run race, with the top three runners finishing within seconds of each other. 马拉松比赛难分胜负，前三名选手的成绩仅相差几秒钟。 | 近义词: close, tight, neck and neck | 反义词: one-sided, decisive, landslide | 词性: adjective
