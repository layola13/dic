atomic:/əˈtɒmɪk/ (UK) /əˈtɑːmɪk/ (US)| adj.|原子的；原子能的；极微小的|Relating to atoms or nuclear energy; extremely small.|

例句：
The atomic bomb caused widespread destruction. 原子弹造成了大范围的破坏。

The atomic structure of a diamond is very complex.  钻石的原子结构非常复杂。


近义词：nuclear, infinitesimal, tiny, minute|
反义词:huge, massive, enormous|
词性:adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *atomique*, from Latin *atomus* ("atom"), from Ancient Greek *ἄτομος (átomos, “indivisible”)*, from *ἀ- (a-, “not”) + τέμνω (témnō, “I cut”)*.
