opposi:/əˈpɒzi/ | n. | opposition |  The act of opposing or resisting. The state of being in conflict or competition. | The political party in opposition is criticizing the government's new policy. 反对党正在批评政府的新政策。| synonyms: resistance, antagonism, defiance | antonyms: support, agreement, approval | noun

oppose:/əˈpoʊz/ | vt. | 反对，对抗 | To be against or resist something.  | Many people oppose the construction of the new highway. 许多人反对修建这条新高速公路。| synonyms: resist, combat, object to | antonyms: support, advocate, endorse | verb
