ingestions:/ɪnˈdʒɛstʃənz/
n.
摄取；吸收；消化
the process of taking food, drink, or another substance into the body by swallowing or absorbing it.
The patient's symptoms suggest a possible accidental ingestion of poison.  这位病人的症状表明可能是意外摄入了毒药。
The excessive ingestion of alcohol can lead to serious health problems.  过量摄入酒精会导致严重的健康问题。
consumption, intake, absorption
excretion, expulsion
noun
