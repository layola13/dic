monomineralic:/ˌmɒnəʊmɪˈnɛrəlɪk/ |adj.|单矿物的，由单一矿物组成的|Consisting of a single mineral.  A monomineralic rock is composed of only one mineral species.|This rock is monomineralic, composed entirely of quartz.|近义词:unimineralic|反义词:polymineralic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
mono- (prefix): from Greek monos "single, alone"
mineral: from Medieval Latin minerale "something mined," from minera "mine"
-ic (suffix): forming adjectives, from French or Late Latin -icus, from Greek -ikos.
