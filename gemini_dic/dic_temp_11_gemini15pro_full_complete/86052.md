hean: /hiːn/
adjective
卑鄙的，卑劣的; 平均的; 吝啬的; 
mean; base; contemptible; niggardly; average.
He's a hean man. 他是个卑鄙的人。
近义词: mean, base, vile, contemptible, despicable, low, abject, sordid, ignoble, degraded, shameful, dishonorable, unworthy, disreputable, ignominious, scurvy, low-down, cheap, nasty, dirty, filthy, foul, odious, obnoxious, offensive, repulsive, repugnant, abhorrent, detestable, execrable, abominable
反义词: noble, generous
词性: adjective
