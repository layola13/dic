namelessnesses:/ˈneɪmlɪsnəsɪz/
n.
无名；匿名；默默无闻
The state or quality of being nameless; anonymity; obscurity.
The namelessnesses of the victims added to the tragedy. 受害者的无名无姓使得这场悲剧更加令人痛心。
Near Synonyms: anonymity, obscurity, incognito
Antonyms: fame, renown, celebrity
