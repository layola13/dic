awkward:/ˈɔːkwərd/ (US), /ˈɔːkwəd/ (UK)| adj.|尴尬的，笨拙的，棘手的|Lacking grace or skill in movement; difficult to handle or manage; causing embarrassment or discomfort.|例句：He felt awkward dancing with such a tall woman. 他和这么高的女人跳舞感觉很尴尬。|近义词：clumsy, ungainly, embarrassing|反义词:graceful, skillful, convenient|词性:形容词
