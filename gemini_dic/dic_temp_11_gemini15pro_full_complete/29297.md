castanet:/ˌkæstəˈnɛt/ | n. |响板；卡斯塔尼特|A small concave piece of wood, ivory, or plastic, held in pairs in the hand and clicked together rhythmically, typically as an accompaniment to Spanish dancing.|The dancer clicked her castanets with precision.|Synonyms: clappers, finger cymbals|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument)|Noun
