dispelling:/dɪˈspɛlɪŋ/
verb
驱散，消除
the act of making something go away, especially a belief, feeling, or idea
The article aims at dispelling some of the myths surrounding mental illness. 这篇文章旨在消除一些围绕精神疾病的误解。
synonyms: eliminating, removing, banishing, erasing
antonyms: creating, fostering, encouraging, promoting
