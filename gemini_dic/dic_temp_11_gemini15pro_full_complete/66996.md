expiate:/ˈekspiˌeɪt/| v. |赎罪，弥补|To make amends for; to atone for.  To avert the wrath of a deity by rites of sacrifice or purification.|He expiated his crime by serving a long prison sentence. 他服了很长的刑期来赎他的罪。|近义词：atone, redeem, redress|反义词:commit, sin|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin expiare "to atone for, purify," from ex- "out" + piare "to appease, purify," related to pius "dutiful, devout."
