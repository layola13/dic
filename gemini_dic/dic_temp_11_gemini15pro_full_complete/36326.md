cohosts:/ˈkoʊhoʊsts/ (US) /ˈkəʊhəʊsts/ (UK)| n.|共同主持人，联合主持人|A person who hosts something, such as a show or event, with another person or people.|例句：The cohosts of the podcast have great chemistry. 播客的联合主持人配合默契。

例句：The cohosts welcomed the audience to the awards ceremony.  两位共同主持人在颁奖典礼上欢迎观众。|近义词：joint hosts, presenters|反义词:guest, audience|词性:noun
