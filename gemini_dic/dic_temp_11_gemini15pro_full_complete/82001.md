griot:/ˈɡriːoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡriːɒt/ (UK)| n.| 歌颂者，吟游诗人|A West African storyteller, singer, musician, and oral historian who perpetuates the oral traditions and history of a family or community.|The griot told stories of ancient kings and heroes. 歌颂者讲述了古代国王和英雄的故事。|近义词：bard, storyteller, minstrel, narrator|反义词: listener, audience|noun
