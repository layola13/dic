microrecord:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌrɛkɔːrd/ |n.|微型记录，缩微记录|A microrecord is a photographic reproduction of a document, drawing, or other image, greatly reduced in size for ease of storage and retrieval. |Libraries often use microrecords to store newspapers and old documents. 图书馆经常使用缩微胶卷存储报纸和旧文件。|synonyms: microfilm, microfiche|antonyms: macrorecord (not commonly used), enlargement|noun
