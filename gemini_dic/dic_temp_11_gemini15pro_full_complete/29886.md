caustic:/ˈkɔːstɪk/ (US), /ˈkɔːstɪk/ (UK)| adj. n.|腐蚀性的；刻薄的；焦散线|
(adj.) Able to burn or corrode organic tissue by chemical action; sarcastic in a scathing and bitter way.
(n.) A caustic substance; a curve or envelope of light rays refracted or reflected by a curved surface.

这种化学物质腐蚀性很强，应小心处理。


近义词：(adj.) corrosive, abrasive, mordant, acrimonious,  sarcastic, biting, vitriolic
