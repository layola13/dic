aberrance: /ˈæbərəns/ (US), /əˈbɛrəns/ (UK)| n. |异常，偏差，反常行为|A departure from what is normal, usual, or expected, typically an unwelcome one.|例句：His sudden outburst of anger was an aberrance from his normally calm demeanor. 他突然爆发的愤怒与他平常冷静的举止截然不同。

例句：Genetic aberrances can lead to serious health problems. 基因异常会导致严重的健康问题。

近义词：anomaly, deviation, abnormality, irregularity, eccentricity|反义词：normality, regularity, standard|词性：名词
