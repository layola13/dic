cosigning:/ˌkoʊˈsaɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 共同签署，联署 | The act of signing a loan or other legal document along with another person, often to guarantee that the other person will fulfill the terms of the agreement.  This makes the cosigner legally responsible for the debt if the primary borrower defaults. |  My father is cosigning on my student loan. 我父亲共同签署了我的学生贷款。 | Synonyms: guaranteeing, vouching, endorsing | Antonyms: refusing, declining | noun
