along: /əˈlɒŋ/ (UK) /əˈlɔːŋ/ (US) | adv. prep.|沿着，顺着；向前；一起；进展|Moving in a constant direction on (or beside) a more or less horizontal surface or line. | We walked along the beach. 我们沿着沙滩散步。 |alongside, down, forward| against, back|adverb, preposition

along: /əˈlɒŋ/ (UK) /əˈlɔːŋ/ (US) | adv.| 一起；进展；进行| Together; accompanying someone.  Making progress or advancing. |Come along, we're going to be late. 快点，我们要迟到了。| together, with| alone, behind |adverb


Etymology: From Old English *andlang "along," from and- "against, opposite" + lang "long." The original sense was "along the length of."
