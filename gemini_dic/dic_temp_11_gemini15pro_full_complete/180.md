abawed:/əˈbɔːd/| adj.|充满敬畏的，惊叹的| Filled with awe; deeply impressed and reverential. |例句：She stood abawed by the sheer size of the cathedral. 她被大教堂的宏伟规模所震撼。

例句：The audience was abawed by the magician's incredible performance.  观众对魔术师精彩的表演惊叹不已。

近义词：awestruck, amazed, astonished, wonderstruck|反义词: unimpressed, indifferent, apathetic|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *abawede, from a- (intensifier) + bawede (past participle of bawen, "to bow").  The sense evolved from physical bowing in reverence to a feeling of profound respect and wonder.
