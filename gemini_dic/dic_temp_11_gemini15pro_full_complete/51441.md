dethrone:/diˈθroʊn/| vt.| 废黜；取代；罢免|To remove a king or queen from power; to remove someone from a position of authority or dominance.|The rebels dethroned the king and established a republic.  叛军废黜了国王，建立了共和国。

The new technology is dethroning traditional methods in many industries. 新技术正在许多行业取代传统方法。|Synonyms: oust, depose, unseat, displace, supplant|Antonyms: crown, enthrone, install, instate|verb
