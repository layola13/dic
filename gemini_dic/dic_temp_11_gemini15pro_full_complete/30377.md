centesimation:/ˌsɛntɪˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 百分之一抽样，百分之一处决，百分之一税 | The act of selecting by lot and punishing every hundredth man.  The Romans sometimes decimated mutinous legions, but centesimation was much rarer. |  Centesimation was a brutal form of military punishment. 百分之一处决是一种残酷的军事惩罚。|  The historical accounts of centesimation are often exaggerated.  关于百分之一处决的历史记载经常被夸大。 |  decimation, sampling |  None | noun
