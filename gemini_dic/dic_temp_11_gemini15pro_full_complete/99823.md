intermingle:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡl/ | v. |混合，交织|to mix together or combine | The flavors intermingled beautifully in the dish.  这道菜的味道完美地融合在一起。|近义词：mix, blend, merge, combine |反义词: separate, divide, disentangle |词性:动词
intermingled:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡld/ | adj. |混合的，交织的|mixed together or combined | The intermingled scents of flowers and herbs filled the air.  花草混合的香味弥漫在空气中。|近义词：mixed, blended, merged, combined |反义词: separated, divided, disentangled |词性:形容词

词源：From inter- + mingle, from Old English mengan "to mix."
