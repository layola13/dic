disciple:/dɪˈsaɪpəl/| n.|信徒，弟子，门徒|A follower or student of a teacher, philosopher, or religious leader.  A person who believes in and follows the teachings of another.|例句：Buddha had many disciples. 佛陀有很多弟子。

例句：He became a disciple of the famous philosopher. 他成为了那位著名哲学家的弟子。|近义词：follower, adherent, student, pupil|反义词：leader, teacher, mentor|词性:noun
