hyperconfident:/ˈhaɪpərˈkɒnfɪdənt/| adj.|过于自信的，极度自信的|Excessively confident; having excessive confidence in oneself or one's abilities.  Characterized by an inflated sense of one's own capabilities, often to the point of arrogance.|He was hyperconfident, bordering on arrogant, about his chances of winning the election. 他对赢得选举的机会过于自信，近乎傲慢。|Synonyms: overconfident, cocky, arrogant, presumptuous, self-assured, egotistical|Antonyms: diffident, insecure, hesitant, timid, self-doubting|adjective
