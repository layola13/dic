gauzily: /ˈɡɔːzɪli/ | adv. | 薄纱地，朦胧地 | In a manner resembling gauze; thinly and delicately.  Covered or draped with gauze. | The dancer moved gauzily across the stage.  舞者轻盈地飘过舞台。| 近义词: thinly, delicately, sheerly, transparently | 反义词: thickly, opaquely, densely | 词性: 副词

词源: From gauze + -ly. Gauze comes from Middle French gaze, perhaps from Gaza, a city in Palestine.
