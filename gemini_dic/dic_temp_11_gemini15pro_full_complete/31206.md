Chang's. | We went to Changs for dinner last night. 我们昨晚去长氏中餐馆吃晚饭了。| Synonym: P.F. Etymology:
Derived from the name of Paul Fleming Chang, the founder of P.F. Chang's China Bistro.  It's a proper noun used colloquially as a generic term.
