lodes:/loʊdz/| n. |矿脉；矿床；富矿带|A lode is a vein of metal ore in the earth.|The miners discovered a rich lode of gold. 矿工们发现了一条丰富的金矿脉。|Synonyms: vein, deposit, seam|Antonyms: barrenness, scarcity|noun
