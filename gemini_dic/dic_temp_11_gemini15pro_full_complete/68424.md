falsifiers:/ˈfɔːlsɪfaɪərz/
n.
造假者；伪造者；窜改者
People who falsify something; those who create forgeries or misrepresent the truth.
The art experts quickly identified the painting as a forgery and began searching for the falsifiers.  艺术专家很快认定这幅画是赝品，并开始寻找造假者。
The falsifiers were eventually caught and brought to justice. 这些造假者最终被抓获并绳之以法。
synonyms:  forgers, liars, deceivers, fabricators, perjurers
antonyms: authenticators, verifiers, truth-tellers
noun
