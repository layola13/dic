caesarian:/sɪˈzɛəriən/ /sɪˈzærɪən/| adj. n.|剖腹产的；剖腹产|Relating to or involving a Caesarian section (a surgical operation for delivering a baby by cutting through the wall of the abdomen and uterus).  A Caesarian section; a Caesarian birth. |She had a caesarian section with her first child. 她第一个孩子是剖腹产的。|The baby was delivered by caesarian.  这个婴儿是剖腹产出生的。|Synonyms: C-section, Cesarean|Antonyms: vaginal birth|adjective, noun
