adjudges:/əˈdʒʌdʒɪz/
verb
判决，裁定，宣布
Officially declares someone to be something, usually after careful consideration.
The court adjudges the defendant guilty. 法庭宣判被告有罪。
synonyms: decrees, rules, decides, proclaims
antonyms:  absolves, acquits, exonerates
词性:动词
