housewives:/ˈhaʊswɪvz/ (US), /ˈhaʊswaɪvz/ (UK)| n. |家庭主妇|A married woman whose main occupation is caring for her family, especially her children and home. |例句：Many housewives feel unfulfilled and want to return to work. 许多家庭主妇感到没有成就感，想重返工作岗位。

例句：The television program was aimed at housewives.  这个电视节目是针对家庭主妇的。

近义词：homemaker, stay-at-home mom/mum |反义词: working woman, career woman |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hūswīf (attested as oblique cases hūswīfan, hūswīfa), from hūs "house" + wīf "woman, wife".
