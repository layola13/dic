archetypal:/ˌɑːrkɪˈtaɪpəl/ | adj. | 原型的，典型的 |  Relating to or denoting an archetype; very typical of a certain kind of person or thing. |  Example:  The archetypal hero's journey involves trials and tribulations.  典型的英雄之旅包含着考验和磨难。

Example:  Hamlet is the archetypal tragic hero.  哈姆雷特是典型的悲剧英雄。| 近义词: classic, exemplary, prototypical, quintessential | 反义词: atypical, unusual, unconventional | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *arkhetupos* "original pattern," from *arkhe* "beginning" + *tupos* "type."
