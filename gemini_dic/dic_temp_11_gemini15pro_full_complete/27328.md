camaieu:/kæˈmaɪ.uː/
noun
浮雕,单色画,同色调的绘画
a painting or carving done in different shades of a single color; a piece of jewelry in which the design is formed by layers of different shades of the same color
The artist specialized in camaieus, creating stunning portraits using only shades of grey.  这位艺术家擅长单色画，只用灰色调就创作出令人惊叹的肖像。
Cameo, monochrome, relief
Polychrome
