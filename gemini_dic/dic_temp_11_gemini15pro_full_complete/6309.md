amortization:/ˌæmərtaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /əˌmɔːtɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 摊销，分期偿还 |The process of gradually reducing a debt or cost over a period of time by making regular payments.|  The company is following a policy of rapid amortization of its capital equipment. 该公司正在奉行快速摊销其资本设备的政策。

amortization:/ˌæmərtaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /əˌmɔːtɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 摊销，分期偿还 |The reduction of the value of an asset over a period of time.|  The amortization of the loan is spread over ten years.  这笔贷款的摊销期为十年。

amortization:/ˌæmərtaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /əˌmɔːtɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 摊销，分期偿还 |The gradual writing off of an intangible asset over a period of time.| The amortization of goodwill is a complex accounting issue. 商誉的摊销是一个复杂的会计问题。


近义词: depreciation, write-off, expensing
反义词: appreciation
词性: noun
