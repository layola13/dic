enlivened:/ɪnˈlaɪvənd/
verb | 使生动；使活泼；使有生气 |  To make something more lively, interesting, or exciting. | The music enlivened the party.  音乐使聚会气氛活跃起来。| 近义词: animated, invigorated, stimulated | 反义词: subdued, dull, lifeless |词性: 动词

enlivened:/ɪnˈlaɪvənd/
adjective | 生动的；活泼的；有生气的 | Made more lively, interesting, or exciting. | The storyteller's enlivened descriptions captivated the children.  讲故事人生动的描述吸引了孩子们。| 近义词: lively, vibrant, animated | 反义词: dull, lifeless, boring |词性: 形容词
