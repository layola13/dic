loneliest:/ˈloʊnliɪst/ | adj. | 最孤独的 | Superlative form of lonely: most lonely.  Characterized by feeling isolated and alone. |  She felt loneliest at night when everyone else seemed to have someone. 她晚上感觉最孤独，那时其他人似乎都有人陪伴。|  The loneliest house on the street was at the very end. 街上最孤单的房子就在最尽头。 |  most desolate, forlorn, isolated | happiest, most sociable | adjective

lionliest:  This word is not standard English.  It appears to be a misspelling of "loneliest."  Please refer to the entry for "loneliest" above.
