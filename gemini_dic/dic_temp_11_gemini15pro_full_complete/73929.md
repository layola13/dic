fortalice:/ˈfɔːrtəlɪs/| n.|小堡垒，要塞|A small fort or fortress.  A fortified place of refuge or defense.|The fortalice offered protection from bandits.|近义词:fort, fortress, stronghold, bastion|反义词:vulnerability, weakness|词性:noun
Etymology: From Old French *fortelesce*, from *fort* (strong) + *-lesce* (suffix denoting a place). Ultimately from Latin *fortis* (strong).
