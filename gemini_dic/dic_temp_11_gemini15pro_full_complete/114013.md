magmatic:/mæɡˈmætɪk/
adjective
岩浆的；岩浆产生的
Relating to, or derived from magma.
The geologist studied the magmatic rocks for clues about the volcano's history.  地质学家研究了岩浆岩，以寻找关于火山历史的线索。
The valley was formed by magmatic activity millions of years ago.  这个山谷是数百万年前由岩浆活动形成的。
igneous, volcanic
plutonic, sedimentary
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *magmaticus, from Latin magma (“dregs, sediment”), from Ancient Greek μάγμα (mágma, “kneaded mass, salve”), from μάσσω (mássō, “I knead”).
