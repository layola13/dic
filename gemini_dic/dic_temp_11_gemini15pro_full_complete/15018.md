babion:/ˈbeɪbiən/| n. |狒狒|A baboon is a type of large monkey found in Africa and parts of Asia. They are known for their long snouts and colorful bottoms.|The baboon snatched the banana from the tourist. 狒狒从游客手中抢走了香蕉。|Synonyms: monkey, primate|Antonyms: None|Noun

babion:/ˈbeɪbiən/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋 (Informal, archaic, derogatory)|A foolish or simple person. This usage is now outdated and considered offensive.|He called him a babion for believing such a ridiculous story.  他称他为傻瓜，因为他相信了这样一个荒谬的故事。|Synonyms: fool, idiot, dolt|Antonyms: genius, intellectual|Noun
