eugenically:/juːˈdʒɛnɪkli/
adverb
优生学地，优生地
In a way that relates to or promotes eugenics, the science of improving a population by controlled breeding to increase the occurrence of desirable heritable characteristics.
Forced sterilizations were once performed eugenically in some countries.  一些国家曾经以优生为目的进行强制绝育。
The idea of eugenically selecting embryos is controversial.  优生学选择胚胎的想法是有争议的。
近义词:selectively
反义词:randomly
词性:adverb
