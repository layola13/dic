ladettes:/ləˈdɛts/
noun
女汉子，假小子; 行为举止像男孩子的年轻女性
Young women who behave in a boisterous or crude manner traditionally associated with young men, such as drinking excessive amounts of alcohol, swearing, and engaging in laddish banter.
例句：The ladette culture of the 1990s embraced drinking pints and football.  90年代的“女汉子”文化崇尚豪饮啤酒和热爱足球。

例句：She was known as a bit of a ladette in college, always up for a party.  在大学里，她有点像个假小子，总是乐于参加派对。
近义词：tomboy, hoyden
反义词：lady, gentlewoman
词性:n.
