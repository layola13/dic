illustrations:/ˌɪləˈstreɪʃənz/| n.| 插图，图解，例证 |Visual examples used to explain or decorate something. |The book contains numerous illustrations. 这本书包含大量的插图。| synonyms: pictures, images, diagrams, figures, examples | antonyms:  (No direct antonym. Consider "text," "words," or "blank") | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *illustrationem,  from Latin illustrare "to light up, brighten, illuminate, make clear," figuratively "to make famous, renowned,"  from assimilated form of in- "in"  + lustrare "to brighten," from lustrum "purification offering, expiatory sacrifice, a period of five years."  The notion is of "lighting up" a subject, making it clear.
