nonsignatories:/ˌnɒnˈsɪɡnətərɪz/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɪɡnətɔːriz/
noun
未签署者；非签字国；未签字人
Those who have not signed a particular treaty or agreement.
Many countries remain nonsignatories to the treaty. 许多国家仍然没有签署该条约。
The nonsignatories were excluded from the negotiations. 未签署者被排除在谈判之外。
synonyms: non-members, outsiders
antonyms: signatories, members
n.
