distinctively:/dɪˈstɪŋktɪvli/
adjective
独特地，明显地
in a way that is readily distinguishable from others; characteristically.
Her voice was distinctively high-pitched. 她的声音明显很高亢。
The region is distinctively marked by its red soil. 该地区以其红土而闻名。
characteristically, uniquely, markedly, noticeably
typically, commonly, ordinarily
adverb
