filmmaking:/ˈfɪlmmeɪkɪŋ/
n.
电影制作; 电影摄制
The process of making a film, from initial idea to finished product.
She studied filmmaking in college. 她大学里学习电影制作。

近义词:cinematography, moviemaking, film production
反义词:N/A
词性:名词
