irrationally:/ɪˈræʃənəli/
adverb
不理性地，无理性地
In a manner not in accordance with reason or logic.
He acted irrationally, driven by fear and anger. 他被恐惧和愤怒驱使，行为很不理性。
She irrationally blamed herself for the accident. 她不理智地将事故归咎于自己。

近义词:unreasonably, illogically, absurdly, foolishly
反义词:rationally, logically, sensibly, reasonably
词性:adverb
