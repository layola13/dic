nonindictment:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈdaɪtmənt/ | n. |不起诉，免予起诉|A formal decision by a grand jury not to bring charges against someone accused of a crime. | The grand jury's nonindictment was a major victory for the defense. 大陪审团的不起诉决定对被告来说是一场重大胜利。

The nonindictment came as a surprise to many.  不起诉令许多人感到意外。

|近义词：acquittal, exoneration, dismissal|反义词：indictment, prosecution|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + indictment.  "Indictment" comes from Anglo-French enditement, enditer "to accuse," ultimately from Latin indictus, past participle of indicere "to proclaim, point out."
