desserts:/dɪˈzɜːrts/| n.|甜点|Sweet courses served at the end of a meal.|We had chocolate cake and ice cream for dessert. 我们吃了巧克力蛋糕和冰淇淋作为甜点。|近义词：sweets, puddings, afters|反义词:appetizers, starters|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desservir, meaning "to clear the table," from des- "un-, reverse of" + servir "to serve." Originally referred to the course served after the table was cleared of the main dishes.
