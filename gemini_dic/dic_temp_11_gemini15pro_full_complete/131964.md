noncontumaciously:/ˌnɑːnkɑːnˌtuːˈmeɪʃəsli/
adverb
不服从地; 顺从地
Without defiance or disobedience; obediently.
He noncontumaciously accepted the court's decision. 他顺从地接受了法院的判决。
Synonym: obediently, submissively, respectfully
Antonym: defiantly, rebelliously, contumaciously
词性: 副词
