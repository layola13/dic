bunchberry:/ˈbʌntʃˌbɛri/| n.|Bunchberry, 四照花|A low, creeping plant (Cornus canadensis) of the dogwood family, bearing small greenish flowers surrounded by four white bracts and producing a cluster of bright red berries.|We found a patch of bunchberries blooming on the forest floor. 我们在森林地面上发现了一片盛开的四照花。|Synonyms: dwarf cornel, crackerberry, creeping dogwood|Antonyms: N/A|noun
