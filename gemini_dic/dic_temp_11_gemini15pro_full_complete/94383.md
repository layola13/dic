impleadable:/ɪmˈpliːdəbəl/ | adj. | 可被控告的；可被传唤的 |  Able to be sued or brought before a court of law as a defendant.  This term is primarily used in legal contexts. | The company is impleadable for breach of contract.  该公司可因违反合同而被起诉。


The CEO was found to be impleadable in the lawsuit. 首席执行官被认定可以作为被告参与诉讼。| 近义词: suable, actionable | 反义词: immune, exempt | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *impleden, from Anglo-French *impleder, from Latin implacitare (“to plead”), from in- (“in”) + placitare (“to please, appease, plead”), from placitum (“decision, decree”), from placere (“to please”).
