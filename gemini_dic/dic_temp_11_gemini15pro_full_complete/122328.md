mixabilities:/ˌmɪksəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可混合性，互溶性 | The mixabilities of different paint colors are important for artists. 不同颜料的互溶性对艺术家来说很重要。| This study investigates the mixabilities of various biofuels. 这项研究调查了各种生物燃料的互溶性。| 近义词：compatibility, miscibility | 反义词：immiscibility, incompatibility | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
mixable (adjective) + -ity (suffix forming abstract nouns)
From mix (verb) + -able (suffix forming adjectives)
Ultimately from Old English *miscian, of Germanic origin.
