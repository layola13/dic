leaguing: /ˈliːɡɪŋ/
verb
结盟，联合
The act of joining in a league or alliance.
Several nations were leaguing together against the common enemy.  几个国家联合起来对抗共同的敌人。
Synonyms:  allying, uniting, combining, joining forces, collaborating, confederating
Antonyms:  separating, dividing, splitting, disbanding
词性: 动词

The process of forming a league or alliance.
The leaguing of these two powerful companies created a monopoly. 这两家强大的公司联合起来，造成了垄断。
