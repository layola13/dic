milliosmole:/ˌmɪliˈɒzməʊl/ (UK) /ˌmɪliˈɑːzmoʊl/ (US)| n.| 毫渗摩尔 |A unit of osmotic concentration equal to one thousandth of an osmole. It represents the concentration of a solution in terms of the number of particles that contribute to its osmotic pressure. One milliosmole is equivalent to one-thousandth of an osmole.|  The patient's urine milliosmoles were elevated, indicating dehydration. 病人的尿液毫渗摩尔升高，表明脱水。| synonyms: mOsm, milliosmol | antonyms: N/A | noun
