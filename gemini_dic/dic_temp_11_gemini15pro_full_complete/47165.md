dawdle:/ˈdɔːdl/ (US), /ˈdɔːd(ə)l/ (UK)| v. |闲逛，游荡，磨蹭|To waste time; to spend time idly; to move slowly and lackadaisically.|例句：Stop dawdling and get ready for school! 别磨蹭了，准备上学！|例句：He dawdled along the riverbank, enjoying the scenery. 他沿着河岸漫步，欣赏着风景。|近义词：linger, loiter, procrastinate, delay|反义词：hurry, hasten, rush|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From perhaps a dialectal variant of *daddle*, frequentative of *dad*, to walk unsteadily.
