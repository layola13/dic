noiselessly:/ˈnɔɪzləsli/ | adv. | 无声地，静静地 | Without any noise; silently. |  She noiselessly closed the door behind her. 她静静地关上了身后的门。| The cat noiselessly padded across the carpet. 猫无声地走过地毯。| 近义词：silently, quietly, soundlessly | 反义词：noisily, loudly | 词性：副词 | 词源：noise (n.) + -less (suffix) + -ly (suffix).  From "noise" meaning "unwanted sound", with the suffix "-less" indicating absence and "-ly" creating an adverb.
