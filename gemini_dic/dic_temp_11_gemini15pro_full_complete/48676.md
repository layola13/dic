deferment:/dɪˈfɜːrmənt/ | n. |延期，推迟| the act of delaying something that is due now until a later time. |The bank offered loan deferment to students facing financial hardship. 银行为面临经济困难的学生提供了贷款延期。|postponement, delay| advancement, acceleration | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From defer + -ment.  "defer" comes from the Old French *deferrer*, from the Latin *differre* "to set apart, postpone," from *dis-* "apart" + *ferre* "to carry, bear."
