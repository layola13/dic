noninclusive:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkluːsɪv/ | adj. | 不包容的，不全面的 | Not including all members of a group or category; exclusive. |  This price is noninclusive of tax. 这个价格不含税。 |  The study group is noninclusive and only admits students with high GPAs.  这个学习小组不具有包容性，只录取GPA高的学生。 | exclusive, restrictive, limited | inclusive, comprehensive, all-encompassing | adjective
