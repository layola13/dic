overgratefulness: /ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪtfʊlnəs/ | n. | 过分感激，过度感恩 |  The state of being excessively or overly grateful.  It can sometimes be perceived as insincere or awkward. | Her overgratefulness made him uncomfortable. 她的过度感激让他感到不舒服。| 近义词: excessive gratitude, profuse thanks | 反义词: ingratitude, ungratefulness | 词性: 名词

overgrateful: /ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪtfʊl/ | adj. | 过分感激的，过度感恩的 | Excessively or overly grateful. |  She was overgrateful for his small act of kindness. 她对他小小的善举表示过分感激。| 近义词: excessively grateful, unduly thankful | 反义词: ungrateful, unappreciative | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "grateful" (from Late Latin "gratus," meaning "pleasing, thankful").
