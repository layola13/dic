help oneself to:/hɛlp wʌnˈsɛlf tuː/
verb phrase
自行取用；随意享用
To serve oneself (something), especially without asking permission.
He helped himself to another slice of cake. 他又拿了一块蛋糕。
synonyms: take, grab, appropriate
antonyms: offer, share, give
verb phrase

help oneself to:/hɛlp wʌnˈsɛlf tuː/
verb phrase
擅自拿取；未经允许取用
To take something without permission.
She helped herself to the money in the cash register. 她擅自拿走了收银机里的钱。
synonyms: steal, pilfer, purloin
antonyms: return, replace, relinquish
