gianter:/ˈdʒaɪəntər/| n.|巨人; 巨物; 庞然大物;|A person or thing that is extraordinarily large or powerful.|The new skyscraper is a gianter among the other buildings. 这座新的摩天大楼在其他建筑物中鹤立鸡群。|近义词：giant, behemoth, colossus|反义词:dwarf, midget|词性:名词
词源：From giant + -er.  Giant comes from the Old French "geant," ultimately from the Greek "gigas."  The suffix "-er" forms the comparative degree of adjectives and also denotes someone or something associated with a particular quality or characteristic.
