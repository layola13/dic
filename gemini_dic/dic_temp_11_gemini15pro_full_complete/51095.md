desperadoism:/ˌdɛspəˈrɑːdoʊɪzəm/
n.
亡命之徒行径;不顾一切的行为
The reckless behavior or actions characteristic of a desperado; a desperate course of action.
His desperadoism led him to rob a bank. 他的亡命之徒行径导致他抢劫了银行。
recklessness, desperation, audacity, foolhardiness
caution, prudence, restraint
noun
