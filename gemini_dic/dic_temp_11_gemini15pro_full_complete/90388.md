however:/haʊˈɛvər/ | adv. conj.| 然而，但是，无论如何 | Used to introduce a statement that contrasts with or seems to contradict something that has been said previously. | He was feeling ill; however, he went to work. 他感觉不舒服；然而，他还是去上班了。 |  The book is expensive; however, it's worth it. 这本书很贵；然而，它值得。 | Near synonyms: nevertheless, nonetheless, yet, still, though | Near antonyms: therefore, consequently | adverb, conjunction

Etymology: From Middle English *hou ever*, from Old English *hū æfre* ("however, howsoever"). *hū* means "how" and *æfre* is related to "ever."
