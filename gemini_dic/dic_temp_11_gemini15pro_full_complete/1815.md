acritical:/eɪˈkrɪtɪkəl/
adj.
不加评论的，不带批评的；缺乏判断力的；无批判性的
Not critical; lacking in discrimination or judgment.
Her acritical acceptance of the story worried me. 她对这个故事不加批判的接受让我担心。
近义词:uncritical, noncritical, undiscriminating, unjudging
反义词:critical, discerning, judgmental
词性:形容词

acritical:/əˈkrɪtɪkl/
adj.
不重要的，无关紧要的
Unimportant; irrelevant.
This information is acritical to the case.  这条信息与案件无关紧要。
近义词:unimportant, irrelevant, insignificant, trifling
反义词:critical, crucial, essential, important
词性:形容词


词源:a- (not) + critical (critical comes from the Greek word "kritikos" meaning "able to judge")
