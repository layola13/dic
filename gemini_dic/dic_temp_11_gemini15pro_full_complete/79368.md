glew:/ɡluː/
| v. | 粘贴; 用胶水粘合 | To join or fasten with glue. |  I glewed the pieces of wood together. 我把木块粘在一起。

I glewed the broken vase back together. 我把破花瓶粘回去了。

| 近义词: glued, stuck, adhered, cemented, fastened | 反义词: separate, detach, unglue | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *glewen, from Old French gluer, from Late Latin glūtāre, from Latin glūten (“glue”).
