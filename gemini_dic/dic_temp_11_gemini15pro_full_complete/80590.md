googled: /ˈɡuːɡəld/ (US) /ˈɡuːɡld/ (UK)| v. |用谷歌搜索|Searched for information using the Google search engine.| I googled the restaurant to find its address. 我用谷歌搜索了这家餐厅，找到了它的地址。|looked up, searched, researched| - |verb
Etymology: Derived from the proper noun "Google," the name of the search engine,  converted into a verb by adding the past tense suffix "-ed."
