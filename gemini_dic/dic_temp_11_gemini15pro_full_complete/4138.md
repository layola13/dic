air-mail:/ˈeərˌmeɪl/| n. adj. |航空邮件|Mail that is transported by airplane.|例句：I sent the letter by air-mail. 我通过航空邮件寄了这封信。|例句：Please send this package via air-mail. 请用航空邮件寄送这个包裹。|近义词：airmail,  air post|反义词: surface mail|词性:名词, 形容词

air-mail:/ˈeərˌmeɪl/| v. |用航空邮件寄送|To send something by airmail.|例句：I air-mailed the documents to the head office. 我用航空邮件把文件寄到了总部。|例句：She air-mailed a birthday card to her friend in Australia. 她用航空邮寄了一张生日贺卡给在澳大利亚的朋友。|近义词：send by air, dispatch by air|反义词: send by surface mail|词性:动词

Etymology:
From air + mail.  "Air" referring to the medium of transport and "mail" referring to postal matter.
