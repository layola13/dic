berets:/bəˈreɪz/ /ˈbɛreɪz/
n.
贝雷帽；贝雷帽（beret 的复数）
A soft, round, flat-crowned cap, usually made of felt or wool.
The soldiers wore berets as part of their uniform. 士兵们戴着贝雷帽作为他们制服的一部分。

beret:/bəˈreɪ/ /ˈbɛreɪ/
n.
贝雷帽
A soft, round, flat-crowned cap, usually made of felt or wool.
She wore a bright red beret. 她戴着一顶鲜红色的贝雷帽。
近义词：cap, hat
反义词：(无直接反义词，可以考虑helmet头盔，表示不同类型的帽子)
词性：名词
