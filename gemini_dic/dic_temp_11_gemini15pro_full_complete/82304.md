groupware:/ˈɡruːpwɛːr/| n.|群件；协同软件|Software designed to facilitate collective working by a group of people.|Our company uses groupware to improve collaboration on projects. 我们公司使用群件来改进项目协作。|近义词：collaborative software, groupwork software|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "standalone software")|词性:noun

Etymology:  "group" + "software", a blend word coined in the late 1980s.  "Group" comes from Old French "groupe"  and ultimately from Italian "gruppo" (knot, bunch). "Software" is a combination of "soft" (yielding readily to touch) and "ware" (articles of merchandise).
