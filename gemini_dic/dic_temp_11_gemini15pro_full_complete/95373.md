incest:/ˈɪnsɛst/ | n. | 乱伦；近亲相奸 | Sexual intercourse between people so closely related that they are forbidden by law to marry. | Incest is a taboo in almost every culture. 乱伦在几乎所有文化中都是禁忌。| Examples:  She was a victim of incest. 她是乱伦的受害者。 The book dealt with the difficult subject of incest. 这本书讨论了乱伦这个棘手的主题。| 近义词: inbreeding | 反义词: exogamy | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin incestuosus, from Latin incestus (“unclean, unchaste”), from in- (“not”) + castus (“pure, chaste”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱeh₂s- (“pure, holy”).
