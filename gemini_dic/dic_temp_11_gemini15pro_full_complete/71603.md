flat-packing:/ˈflætˌpækɪŋ/ | n./adj. | 平装家具；平装的 | Furniture that is sold unassembled and packed flat in a box for easy transport and storage; also used as an adjective to describe this type of furniture. | I bought some flat-packing furniture for my new apartment. 我为我的新公寓买了一些平装家具。| Synonyms:  knock-down, self-assembly, ready-to-assemble (RTA) | Antonyms: assembled, pre-assembled | Noun/Adjective

flat-pack:/ˈflætˌpæk/ | v. | 平装；把…装成扁平包装 | To pack (something) flat, especially furniture for transportation. |  We flat-packed the wardrobe before moving house. 我们在搬家前把衣柜拆卸平装了。| Synonyms:  disassemble, knock down | Antonyms: assemble, put together | Verb
