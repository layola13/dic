actionless:/ˈækʃənlɪs/
adjective
无行动的；不活跃的；静止的
Lacking action; inactive; inert.
The play was criticized for its actionless plot and dull characters.  这出戏因其缺乏动作的情节和枯燥的人物而受到批评。
The hero, surprisingly, remained actionless throughout the climax.  令人惊讶的是，这位英雄在整个高潮部分都保持静止。
近义词:inactive, inert, passive, stagnant, static
反义词:active, dynamic, energetic, vigorous
词性:adjective
