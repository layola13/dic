haemoplastic:/ˌhiːməˈplæstɪk/| adj.|血浆的，造血的|Relating to blood plasma or the formation of blood cells.  It describes something that helps in the formation of blood or is related to it.| Haemoplastic medication can help patients with certain blood disorders. 血浆药物可以帮助患有某些血液疾病的患者。| synonyms:  hematopoietic,  hemopoietic| antonyms:  anti-hemopoietic| adjective
