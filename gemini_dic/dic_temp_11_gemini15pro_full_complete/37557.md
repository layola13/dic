commorance:/ˈkɒmərəns/ /kɑːmərəns/| n.|居住；逗留；暂住|A period of residence; a stay.|He took up commorance in a small village. 他在一个小村庄里居住了一段时间。|近义词：residence, stay, sojourn, abode|反义词: departure, egress|noun


commorant:/ˈkɒmərənt/ /ˈkɑːmərənt/| adj.|居住的；逗留的|Residing; staying.|He is commorant in London. 他居住在伦敦。|近义词：resident, dwelling, inhabiting|反义词: nonresident, transient|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin commorantia, from commorans, present participle of commorari "to stay, abide, sojourn," from com- "together" + morari "to delay, linger, stay," from mora "delay."
