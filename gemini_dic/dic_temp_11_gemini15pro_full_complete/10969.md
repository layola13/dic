arbitrary: /ˈɑːrbɪtrɛri/ (US), /ˈɑːbɪtrəri/ (UK)| adj.|任意的；武断的；专横的|Based on random choice or personal whim, rather than any reason or system.|The choice of restaurant was arbitrary, as no one had a preference.  餐厅的选择是任意的，因为没有人有偏好。|  He made an arbitrary decision to close the factory. 他武断地决定关闭工厂。 | synonyms: random, capricious, whimsical, despotic, dictatorial | antonyms: reasoned, logical, rational, objective, justifiable | adjective
