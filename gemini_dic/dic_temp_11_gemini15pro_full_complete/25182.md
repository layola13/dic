budded:/ˈbʌdɪd/| v. adj.|发芽，萌芽；开始形成|A past tense and past participle of "bud".  Meaning to begin to grow or develop; to form buds.  Can also be used as an adjective to describe something that has budded.|The trees have budded early this year. 今年树木发芽很早。|Near synonyms: sprouted, germinated, developed, commenced|Near antonyms: withered, died, shriveled|verb, adjective

budded:/ˈbʌdɪd/| adj.|已发芽的，已萌芽的|Used as an adjective to describe something that has already formed buds.|The budded branches promised a bountiful harvest.  已经发芽的树枝预示着丰收。|Near synonyms: burgeoning, nascent, incipient|Near antonyms: barren, sterile, dead|adjective
