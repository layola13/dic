flatulent:/ˈflætʃʊlənt/ (US), /ˈflætjʊlənt/ (UK)| adj.|胀气的；爱吹牛的；空洞的|Affected by or producing gas in the digestive system; pompous and empty. |例句：He felt flatulent after eating too many beans. 他吃了太多豆子后感到胀气。

例句：His flatulent speech impressed no one. 他夸夸其谈的演讲没有打动任何人。|近义词：gassy, bloated, pompous, pretentious|反义词:deflated, humble, modest|词性:adjective

flatus:/ˈfleɪtəs/ (US), /ˈfleɪtəs/ (UK)| n.|肠胃胀气；屁|Gas generated in or expelled from the digestive tract. |例句：The flatus was quite audible.  这个屁的声音很大。

例句：He excused himself for the involuntary flatus. 他为不由自主地放屁道歉。|近义词：gas, wind|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
