anti-induction:/ˌænti-ɪnˈdʌkʃən/ | adj. | 反感应的，抗感应的 |  Relating to or designed to prevent or reduce induction, especially electromagnetic induction.  |  The anti-induction cable reduced the interference. 反感应电缆减少了干扰。 |  non-inductive | inductive | adjective

anti-induction:/ˌænti-ɪnˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 反感应 | The act of preventing or reducing induction. | The anti-induction measures were successful. 反感应措施很成功。| counterinduction | induction | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + induction.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite of," derived from Greek. "Induction" comes from the Latin "inducere," meaning "to lead into."
