cruises:/ˈkruːzɪz/ | n. | 巡航，游弋；海上航游 |A journey on a large ship for pleasure, typically calling at several ports.| We went on a Caribbean cruise last summer. 我们去年夏天去了加勒比海巡游。 |synonyms: voyages, sailings, tours, expeditions | antonyms: flights, stays, stops | noun

cruise:/kruːz/ | v. | 巡航，游弋；漫游 |To travel at a moderate speed, especially for pleasure or in search of something. | The police car cruised the streets. 警车在街上巡逻。 | synonyms: sail, voyage, roam, wander | antonyms: stop, stay, remain | verb
