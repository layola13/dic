houserooms:/ˈhaʊsruːmz/| n. |房间（通常指房屋内的所有房间）| The houserooms were spacious and well-lit.  房屋的房间宽敞明亮。|  rooms, apartments | building, exterior | noun
Etymology:  house (from Old English "hūs") + rooms (from Old English "rūm," meaning "space").  The word combines the meaning of a dwelling and the individual spaces within it.
