barati:/bəˈrɑːti/| n.|朋友，同志，伙伴（通常用于南亚地区）|A friend or comrade, often used in South Asia. It comes from Hindi/Urdu.  It can also refer to a member of a barat (wedding procession).|Example: He is my barati from childhood. 他是我从小到大的朋友。|Example: The baratis danced joyfully in the wedding procession. 婚礼队伍中的伙伴们欢快地跳着舞。|Synonym: friend, companion, pal, comrade, mate|Antonym: enemy, foe|Noun
