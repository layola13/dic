decoying:/dɪˈkɔɪɪŋ/
verb
诱捕，诱骗
The act of luring someone or something into a trap or dangerous situation.
The hunters were decoying ducks with realistic-looking models. 猎人用逼真的模型诱捕鸭子。
luring, enticing, entrapping
repelling, deterring
v.
