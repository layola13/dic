over-report:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈpɔːrt/ | v. | 过度报告；夸大报告；多报 | To report something in excess of the actual amount or degree; to exaggerate in reporting. | The company was accused of over-reporting its profits.  该公司被指控夸大其利润。|  The witness tended to over-report the speed of the car. 目击者倾向于夸大汽车的速度。| 近义词: exaggerate, overstate, inflate | 反义词: under-report, downplay, minimize | verb

over-report:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈpɔːrt/ | n. | 过度报告；夸大报告 | An instance of reporting something in excess of the actual amount or degree. | The investigation revealed an over-report of expenses. 调查揭露了费用的多报。| There was an over-report of donations to the charity. 慈善机构的捐款被多报了。| 近义词: exaggeration, overstatement | 反义词: under-report, understatement | noun
