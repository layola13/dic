monogastric:/ˌmɒnəˈɡæstrɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈɡæstrɪk/| adj. | 单胃的 | Having a single-chambered stomach (as opposed to a ruminant stomach with multiple compartments). |  Pigs are monogastric animals. 猪是单胃动物。| synonym: single-stomached | antonym: polygastric, ruminant | adjective
monogastric:/ˌmɒnəˈɡæstrɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈɡæstrɪk/| n. | 单胃动物 | An animal with a single-chambered stomach. | The study focused on the digestive system of monogastrics. 这项研究集中于单胃动物的消化系统。| synonym: non-ruminant | antonym: ruminant | noun
