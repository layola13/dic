词性:n.
中文翻译:凝聚力; 精神; 活力
英文解析:A suffix used to form nouns from verbs, often with the sense of action or process, or result of an action.  While "ning" itself isn't a standalone word in standard English, it appears as a productive suffix in words like "evening," "learning," and "running."  It can also be encountered in some dialects or informal contexts as a standalone word, meaning something similar to "vitality" or "energy."
