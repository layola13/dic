flingdust: /ˈflɪŋˌdʌst/
noun
飞尘，尘土，被扬起的尘土
Fine particles of dust or dirt flung up into the air, especially by a moving vehicle or person.
The jeep kicked up flingdust as it sped down the dirt road. 吉普车沿着土路疾驰，卷起了飞扬的尘土。
近义词: dust, dirt, grime, grit
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly "cleanliness")
