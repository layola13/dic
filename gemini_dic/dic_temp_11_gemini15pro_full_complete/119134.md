metrosideros:/ˌmɛtrəˈsaɪdərɒs/ /ˌmɛtroʊˈsaɪdərɒs/ | n. | 铁心木属；波户木 |A genus of trees and shrubs in the myrtle family (Myrtaceae), native to New Zealand, Australia, and other Pacific islands, characterized by showy flowers with numerous stamens.  Many species are cultivated as ornamentals for their striking red, pink, or orange flowers. | The pōhutukawa (Metrosideros excelsa) is a well-known metrosideros species.  波户树是一种著名的铁心木属植物。 | synonyms: ironwood tree, rātā, pōhutukawa | antonyms: N/A | noun
