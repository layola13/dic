advowson: /ədˈvaʊzən/
n.
Patronage; the right to present a nominee to a vacant ecclesiastical benefice.
The right to appoint a clergyman to a church living.
The advowson of the local church belonged to the manor lord.  当地教会的委任权属于庄园领主。
近义词: patronage, right of presentation
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, as it's a specific legal term)
词性: noun
