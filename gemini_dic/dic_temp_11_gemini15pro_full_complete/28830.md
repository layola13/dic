carpal: /ˈkɑːrpl/ (US), /ˈkɑːpəl/ (UK) | adj. n. | 腕骨的；腕骨 | Relating to the bones of the wrist (carpus).  A bone of the carpus. |  Example: Carpal tunnel syndrome is a common problem. 腕管综合征是一个常见的问题。 | Example:  The eight carpal bones form the wrist joint. 八块腕骨构成了腕关节。| 近义词: wrist (adj.) | 反义词: (No direct antonym for an anatomical term) | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *carpālis, from Latin carpus (“wrist”), from Greek καρπός (karpós, “wrist”).
