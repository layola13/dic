imagesetter:/ˈɪmɪdʒˌsɛtər/| n.|照排机，图像输出机|A machine that produces high-quality text and images on photographic paper or film, typically used in printing.|The imagesetter produced crisp, clear text for the book cover.|Synonyms: typesetter, output device|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hand compositor" for older technology)|noun
