baffle:/ˈbæfl/ (US), /ˈbæfəl/ (UK)| v. n.|使困惑，阻碍，挡板|To confuse or frustrate someone completely; to prevent something, especially sound or light, from passing through.|The complex instructions completely baffled me. 复杂的指令让我完全摸不着头脑。|Synonyms: confuse, perplex, bewilder, frustrate, foil|Antonyms: clarify, enlighten, explain, aid, assist|verb

baffle:/ˈbæfl/ (US), /ˈbæfəl/ (UK)| n.|挡板，隔板|A device used to deflect or regulate the flow of something, such as air or liquid.|The engine's baffle reduced the noise significantly.  发动机的挡板显著降低了噪音。|Synonyms: deflector, screen, barrier|Antonyms: opening, passage|noun
