innominate:/ɪˈnɒmɪnət/ (UK), /ɪˈnɑːmɪnət/ (US)| adj.|无名的；未命名的；髋骨的|Not having a name; anonymous.  Specifically referring to the hip bone (os coxae) in anatomical contexts.|The innominate artery supplies blood to the arm and head.  无名动脉为手臂和头部供血。|Synonyms: nameless, unnamed, anonymous (general);  os coxae, hip bone (anatomical)|Antonyms: named, identified|Adjective


innominate:/ɪˈnɒmɪnət/ (UK), /ɪˈnɑːmɪnət/ (US)| n.|髋骨|The hip bone (os coxae).|The fracture involved the innominate bone.  骨折涉及髋骨。|Synonyms: os coxae, hip bone|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *innominatus, from Latin in- "not" + nominatus "named," past participle of nominare "to name," from nomen "name."
