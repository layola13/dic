nonmilitant:/ˌnɒnˈmɪlɪtənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɪlɪtənt/
adjective
非军事的；非好战的；不激进的
Not militant; not inclined to engage in warfare or combat; not aggressive or combative.
The group adopted a nonmilitant approach to achieving its goals.  这个团体采取了一种非军事的方式来实现其目标。
The protestors held a nonmilitant demonstration outside the government building. 抗议者在政府大楼外举行了非军事示威。
近义词:peaceful, pacifist, dovish, nonaggressive
反义词:militant, aggressive, combative, hawkish
词性:adjective

nonmilitant:/ˌnɒnˈmɪlɪtənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɪlɪtənt/
noun
非军事人员；非好战分子
A person who is not militant; a person who advocates peaceful or non-aggressive methods.
The nonmilitants in the group advocated for negotiation.  该组织中的非军事人员主张谈判。
Many nonmilitants participated in the peace march. 许多非军事人员参加了和平游行。
近义词:pacifist, dove
反义词:militant, warmonger, hawk
词性:noun
