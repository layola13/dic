amiably:/ˈeɪmiəbli/| adv.|和蔼地；亲切地|In a friendly and pleasant manner.|She smiled amiably at him. 她和蔼地对他笑了笑。|Kindly, agreeably, genially, affably, pleasantly|Unpleasantly, rudely, curtly, nastily|adverb
ETYMOLOGY->
From French amiable + -ly, from Latin amicabilis "friendly," from amicus "friend."
