nonpuerile:/ˌnɒnpjuːəˈraɪl/
adjective
非幼稚的，成熟的
Not childish or puerile; mature.
Her nonpuerile demeanor impressed the interviewers.  她的成熟举止给面试官留下了深刻的印象。
He handled the delicate situation with nonpuerile grace. 他以成熟的优雅处理了微妙的局面。


synonym
mature, sophisticated, grown-up, adult, serious
antonym
childish, puerile, immature, infantile, juvenile
Etymology
From non- + puerile (from Latin puerilis, meaning "boyish").  由non- + puerile（来自拉丁语puerilis，意思是“男孩气的”）组成。
