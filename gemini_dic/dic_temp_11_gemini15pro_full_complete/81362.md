grappa:/ˈɡræpə/| n.|格拉巴酒|An Italian brandy made from grape pomace (the skins, seeds, and stems left after grapes have been pressed for wine).|例句：I enjoyed a glass of grappa after dinner. 我晚饭后享用了一杯格拉巴酒。

|例句：The grappa had a strong, fruity aroma.  这格拉巴酒有浓郁的水果香气。

|近义词：marc, brandy|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
