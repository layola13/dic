entodermic:/ˌɛntəˈdɜːrmɪk/ | adj. | 内胚层的，内皮的 | Relating to or derived from the endoderm, the innermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo. | The entodermic layer develops into the lining of the digestive tract and other internal organs. 内胚层发育成消化道和其他内部器官的内膜。|  The scientist studied the entodermic development of the frog embryo.  科学家研究了青蛙胚胎的内胚层发育。 | synonyms: endodermal, hypoblastic | antonyms: ectodermic | adjective
