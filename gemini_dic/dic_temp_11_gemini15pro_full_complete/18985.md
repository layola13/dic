bestir:/bɪˈstɜːr/
verb
奋起；激励；活跃起来
To rouse oneself to action; exert oneself.
He finally bestirred himself to go to work. 他终于振作精神去上班了。
近义词: rouse, stir, activate, energize
反义词: rest, relax, be idle, be still
词性: verb
