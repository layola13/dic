audience /ˈɔːdiəns/ (US) /ˈɔːdiəns/ (UK)
n.|观众；听众；读者；觐见|A group of people who have gathered to watch or listen to something (a play, concert, somebody speaking, etc.) or are targeted by a book, film, etc.|The audience applauded wildly at the end of the concert. 音乐会结束时，观众热烈鼓掌。|spectators, listeners, viewers|private meeting|noun
