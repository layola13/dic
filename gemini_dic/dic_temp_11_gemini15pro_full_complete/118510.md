mesoblastic:/ˌmezəˈblæstɪk/
adjective
中胚层的；由中胚层形成的
Relating to or derived from the mesoblast, the middle layer of the primary germ layers of an embryo.
The mesoblastic layer gives rise to the muscles, bones, and circulatory system.  中胚层会发育形成肌肉、骨骼和循环系统。
The scientist studied the mesoblastic development of the chick embryo.  科学家研究了鸡胚胎的中胚层发育。
近义词: mesodermal
反义词:  ectoblastic, endoblastic
词性: adjective
