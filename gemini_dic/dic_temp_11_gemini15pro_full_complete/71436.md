flak:/flæk/ | n. | 高射炮火，猛烈批评 | Anti-aircraft fire, strong criticism. | The fighter jet was hit by heavy flak. 这架战斗机遭到猛烈的高射炮火袭击。| synonyms: criticism, fire, barrage, attack | antonyms: praise, acclaim, approval | noun

flak:/flæk/ | n. | 猛烈批评 | Strong criticism. | He took a lot of flak for his controversial decision.  他因其有争议的决定受到了很多批评。| synonyms: criticism, disapproval, condemnation, censure | antonyms: praise, acclaim, approval, commendation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Fliegerabwehrkanone* (anti-aircraft gun), shortened to *Flak*.
