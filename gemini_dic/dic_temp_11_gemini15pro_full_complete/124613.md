mousing:/ˈmaʊsɪŋ/ /ˈmaʊzɪŋ/| n. |用鼠标操作；捕鼠；像老鼠一样偷偷摸摸地行动|The act of using a computer mouse; the act of hunting mice; moving like a mouse, quietly and stealthily.| He spent hours mousing around on the internet. 他花了几个小时在网上浏览。|近义词：clicking, browsing, hunting (mice)|反义词: (For hunting mice) releasing|noun


mousing:/ˈmaʊsɪŋ/ /ˈmaʊzɪŋ/| adj. |捕鼠的；像老鼠一样的|Relating to the act of hunting mice; resembling a mouse in appearance or behavior.|She has mousing habits, always sneaking around. 她有像老鼠一样的习惯，总是偷偷摸摸的。|近义词：mouselike, furtive|反义词: bold, open|adjective
