eruptives:/ɪˈrʌptɪvz/ | n. | 喷出物；火山喷发物 |  Substances ejected from a volcano, such as lava, ash, and rocks.  | The eruptives from the volcano covered the surrounding area in ash. 火山喷出的物质覆盖了周围地区的火山灰。 |  ejecta, emissions, discharges | deposits, intrusions | noun

eruptive:/ɪˈrʌptɪv/ | adj. | 喷发的；爆发的 |  Relating to or characterized by eruption. | The eruptive phase of the volcano lasted for several weeks.  火山的喷发阶段持续了几个星期。 | explosive, volcanic | dormant, quiescent | adjective
