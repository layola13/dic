disinheritance:/ˌdɪsɪnˈherɪtəns/
n.|剥夺继承权，取消继承资格|The act of preventing someone from inheriting property.|His disinheritance left him with nothing but the clothes on his back. 他被剥夺了继承权，身无分文。|近义词：exclusion, deprivation|反义词: inheritance, bequeathal|词性:名词

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + inheritance (from inherit, from Old French enheriter, from Latin inhereditare).
