corny:/ˈkɔːrni/ (US), /ˈkɔːni/ (UK)| adj.|老套的，陈腐的，庸俗的|Sentimental or mawkishly emotional, often in a way that is predictable and not very sophisticated.|That joke was so corny, I almost groaned. 那個笑話太老套了，我差點呻吟出來。

His pick-up lines were incredibly corny. 他的搭訕詞簡直老掉牙了。|近义词：banal, clichéd, hackneyed, trite, cheesy|反义词：original, fresh, innovative|词性：形容词
