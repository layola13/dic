excambie:/ɛksˈkæmbi/ | v. | exchange, barter | To give something and receive something else in return; to trade.  |  He excambied his old car for a new bicycle. 他用他的旧车换了一辆新自行车。|  例句：The two countries excambied prisoners of war.  这两个国家交换了战俘。 | 近义词：exchange, barter, swap, trade | 反义词: retain, keep


词性: verb (动词)

词源: From Old French *escambier, from Late Latin excambiare, from Latin ex- + cambiare "to change," from cambire "to barter."
