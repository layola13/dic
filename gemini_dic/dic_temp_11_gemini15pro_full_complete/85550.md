hatchetmen:/ˈhætʃɪtmən/| n. |打手；暴徒；受雇杀人或恐吓的凶手|A person hired to commit acts of violence or intimidation, especially murder. |The dictator used hatchetmen to silence his political opponents. 独裁者利用打手压制他的政敌。|The company hired hatchetmen to break up the union. 公司雇佣了打手来破坏工会。|synonyms: thug, goon, enforcer, assassin, hitman|antonyms: protector, guardian, defender|noun
