away:/əˈweɪ/
adv.|离开；在远处；消失|at a distance in space or time |The children are away at school. 孩子们在外地上学。
away:/əˈweɪ/
adj.|不在的；遥远的|not present; at a distance|He's away on business. 他出差了。

Etymology: From Old English *aweg (unattested), from *on weg “on the way,” from on “on” + weg “way.”  Cognate with Old Frisian awai, Old Saxon aweg, Middle Dutch awech, Dutch weg.

Synonyms: absent, gone, off, distant, remote
Antonyms: present, here, near, close

词性: adverb, adjective
