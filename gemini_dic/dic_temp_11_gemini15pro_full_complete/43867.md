cresol:/ˈkriːsɒl/ (US) /ˈkriːzɒl/ (UK)| n.|甲酚；煤酚；煤馏油酸|A thick, oily liquid obtained from coal tar or petroleum, consisting of a mixture of three isomeric phenols.  It is used as a disinfectant and antiseptic.|Cresol is an effective disinfectant against bacteria and fungi.|例句：Cresol was commonly used as a disinfectant in hospitals.  甲酚以前常在医院用作消毒剂。

例句：The strong smell of cresol filled the laboratory.  实验室里充满了浓烈的甲酚气味。|近义词：disinfectant, antiseptic|反义词: None|词性:noun
