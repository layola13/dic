ensemble:/ɒnˈsɒmb(ə)l/ |/ɑːnˈsɑːmb(ə)l/| n.|全体，整体；剧团，乐团；全体演员；组合，集成|A group of items viewed as a whole rather than individually.  A group of musicians, actors, or dancers who perform together. | The museum showcased an ensemble of antique furniture.  博物馆展出了一套古董家具。| synonyms: group, collection, troupe, company, aggregation, combination | antonyms: individual, separate, single | noun

ensemble:/ɒnˈsɒmb(ə)l/ |/ɑːnˈsɑːmb(ə)l/| adj.|全体的，整体的；配套的|Comprising a group of complementary items. | She wore an ensemble of a floral dress and matching hat. 她穿了一套碎花连衣裙和配套的帽子。| synonyms: matching, coordinated, complementary | antonyms: mismatched, clashing | adjective
