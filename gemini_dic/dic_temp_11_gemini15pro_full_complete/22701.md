boredly:/ˈbɔːrdlɪ/ | adv. | 无聊地，沉闷地 | In a bored manner; showing or experiencing boredom. | She listened to the lecture boredly, doodling in her notebook. 她无聊地听着讲座，在笔记本上涂鸦。|  He sighed boredly and flipped through the channels. 他无聊地叹了口气，不停地切换频道。 | 近义词：listlessly, apathetically, dully | 反义词：enthusiastically, eagerly, attentively | 词性: 副词

词源:  From the adjective "bored" + the adverbial suffix "-ly." "Bored" comes from the verb "bore," which originates from the Old English word "borian," meaning "to pierce, perforate." The sense of "to weary by tediousness" developed later.
