foxtrotting:/ˈfɒkstrɒtɪŋ/ /ˈfɑːkstrɑːtɪŋ/
verb
狐步舞,跳狐步舞
the action of dancing the foxtrot.
They were foxtrotting across the dance floor. 他们正在舞池中跳狐步舞。
近义词:dancing, waltzing
反义词:(无直接反义词，可考虑standing still, sitting)
词性:v.


foxtrot:/ˈfɒkstrɒt/ /ˈfɑːkstrɑːtɪŋ/
noun
狐步舞
a ballroom dance performed to syncopated music.
The foxtrot is a graceful dance. 狐步舞是一种优雅的舞蹈。
近义词:waltz, tango
反义词:(无直接反义词)
词性:n.
