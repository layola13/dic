kanji:/ˈkɑːndʒiː/
n.
汉字
Japanese characters; the system of Japanese writing using Chinese characters.
例句：Kanji is difficult to learn.  汉字很难学。

例句：She is studying kanji. 她正在学习汉字。

近义词：Chinese character
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps  hiragana or katakana as alternative Japanese writing systems)
词性: noun
