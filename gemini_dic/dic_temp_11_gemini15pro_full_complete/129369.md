nomad:/ˈnoʊmæd/ (US), /ˈnɒmæd/ (UK)| n.|游牧民；流浪者|A member of a people who have no fixed residence but move from place to place usually seasonally and within a well-defined territory.|The nomads of the Sahara move their herds to new pastures each season.|近义词：wanderer, migrant, rover, itinerant|反义词:settler, resident|noun

nomad:/ˈnoʊmæd/ (US), /ˈnɒmæd/ (UK)| adj.|游牧的；流浪的|Relating to or characteristic of nomads.|They lived a nomadic life, travelling from town to town.|近义词：wandering, roaming, migratory, itinerant|反义词:settled, sedentary|adjective
