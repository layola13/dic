hodman:/ˈhɒdmən/ (UK) /ˈhɑːdmən/ (US)| n. | 苦力, 杂工, 砌砖工助手 | A hodman is a laborer who carries bricks, mortar, and other materials for bricklayers. | The hodman struggled under the weight of the bricks.  这个杂工在砖块的重压下挣扎。|  The hodman quickly mixed the mortar.  这个砌砖工助手迅速地搅拌灰泥。 |Synonyms: laborer, builder's helper, bricklayer's assistant | Antonyms: supervisor, foreman | noun
