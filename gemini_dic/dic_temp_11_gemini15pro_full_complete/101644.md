irrefutabilities:/ˌɪrɪˈfjuːtəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 不可辩驳性；无可争辩性 | The state or quality of being irrefutable; undeniable truths or facts. | The irrefutabilities of the evidence led to a swift conviction.  证据的不可辩驳性导致了迅速定罪。 | Synonyms:  indisputability, incontestability, undeniable nature | Antonyms: disputability, contestability, questionability | noun

irrefutable: /ˌɪrɪˈfjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可辩驳的；无可争辩的 | Impossible to refute; undeniable. |  His argument was based on irrefutable evidence. 他的论点基于无可辩驳的证据。 | Synonyms: undeniable, incontestable, indisputable, unarguable | Antonyms: refutable, disputable, questionable, debatable | adjective

refute: /rɪˈfjuːt/ | vt. | 反驳；驳斥 | Prove (a statement or theory) to be wrong or false; disprove. | The lawyer refuted the allegations against her client. 律师驳斥了对她委托人的指控。 | Synonyms: disprove, debunk, invalidate, contradict | Antonyms: prove, confirm, corroborate, substantiate | verb
