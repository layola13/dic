absence: /ˈæbsəns/ (US) /ˈæbsəns/ (UK)| n.|缺席，缺乏，没有|The absence of evidence is not evidence of absence. 缺乏证据并不等于没有证据。|例句：His absence from the meeting was noticed by everyone.  他缺席会议，每个人都注意到了。

例句：The long absence of rain caused a severe drought. 长时间的缺雨导致了严重的干旱。|近义词：lack, nonexistence, want|反义词：presence, existence|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English absence, from Old French absence, from Latin absentia (“absence”), from absēns (“absent”), from ab- (“away from”) + ēns, present participle of esse (“to be”).
