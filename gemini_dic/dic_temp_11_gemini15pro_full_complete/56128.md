docudrama:/ˈdɒkjuːˌdrɑːmə/ (UK) /ˈdɑːkjuːˌdrɑːmə/ (US)| n.|纪实剧，文献片|A television programme or film which dramatizes real events.|例句：The docudrama about the Titanic disaster was very moving. 这部关于泰坦尼克号灾难的纪实剧非常感人。

例句：They are making a docudrama about the life of Nelson Mandela. 他们正在制作一部关于纳尔逊·曼德拉生平的纪实剧。

近义词：documentary, dramatized documentary|反义词:fiction|词性:noun
