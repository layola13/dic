naffed:/næft/
adjective
恼火的，生气的
annoyed, irritated, or disgruntled.
例句：He was naffed when his team lost the match.  他很生气他的球队输了比赛。

例句：She got totally naffed with him for being late. 她因为他迟到而对他非常恼火。


近义词：annoyed, irritated, vexed, disgruntled, miffed, peeved
反义词：pleased, happy, delighted, content
词性：adjective
词源：Originating in British slang, likely a euphemism derived from a more vulgar term.  The exact etymology is uncertain.
