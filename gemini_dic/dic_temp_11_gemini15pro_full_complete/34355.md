classic: /ˈklæsɪk/ | adj. n. | 经典的；典型的；一流的；经典作品；名著 | Of the first or highest quality, class, or rank; serving as a standard, model, or guide. classic: /ˈklæsɪk/ | n. | 经典作品；名著；杰作 | A work of enduring excellence, especially in art or literature. |  "Pride and Prejudice" is a classic. 《傲慢与偏见》是一部经典之作。 | synonyms: masterpiece, standard, archetype | antonyms: mediocrity, failure | noun
