heptagons:/ˈhɛptəɡɒnz/ (US) /ˈhɛptəɡən/ (UK)| n. |七边形|A plane figure with seven straight sides and seven angles. |例句：Regular heptagons have seven equal sides and seven equal angles. 正七边形有七条相等的边和七个相等的角。

|例句：The building was designed with heptagons incorporated into its structure. 这座建筑的设计中融入了七边形结构。|近义词：septagon|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
