out-of-the-chute:/ˌaʊt əv ðə ˈʃuːt/
adjective
中文翻译：刚开始的；起步阶段的；一开始就…的
英文解析：Immediately from the beginning; at the very start, especially with energy and speed, like a horse racing out of the starting gate.
例句：The new product was out-of-the-chute successful, selling a million units in the first month.  这款新产品一上市就获得了成功，第一个月就售出了一百万台。

例句：He was out-of-the-chute aggressive in the negotiations, making it clear he wouldn't compromise. 他在谈判一开始就咄咄逼人，明确表示他不会妥协。

近义词：fast-starting, initial, immediate, right off the bat
反义词：slow-starting, gradual, delayed
词性：adjective
