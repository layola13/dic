fountainlike:/ˈfaʊntənlaɪk/| adj.|像喷泉一样的，喷泉似的|Resembling a fountain in appearance or movement.  The water shot up in a fountainlike spray.  水像喷泉一样喷射出来。|近义词：springlike, jetting, spraying|反义词:dripping, trickling|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From "fountain" + "-like". "Fountain" comes from Old French "fontaine," ultimately from Latin "fons" (source). "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
