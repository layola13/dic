blizzarded:/ˈblɪzərdɪd/| adj.|被暴风雪袭击的；被大量事物压垮的|Overwhelmed, inundated, as by a blizzard.  Covered or affected by a blizzard.|例句：The city was blizzarded for three days straight. 这个城市连续三天被暴风雪袭击。

例句：I was blizzarded with emails after the announcement.  公告发布后，我的邮箱被邮件淹没了。|近义词：overwhelmed, inundated, swamped|反义词:calm, peaceful|词性:形容词

blizzard:/ˈblɪzərd/| n. vi.|暴风雪；大量；使应接不暇|A severe snowstorm characterized by strong sustained winds of at least 56 km/h (35 mph) and lasting for a prolonged period—typically three hours or more. A large quantity of something. To overwhelm with a large quantity of something.|例句：The blizzard made travel impossible. 暴风雪使得出行变得不可能。

例句：A blizzard of paperwork landed on his desk.  一大堆文件落在他的桌子上。|近义词：snowstorm, deluge, barrage|反义词:calm, peace|词性:名词,动词


词源: From blizzard (n.), from uncertain origin, perhaps related to German blitz "lightning."  The verbal sense "to overwhelm" is a figurative extension of the literal sense.
