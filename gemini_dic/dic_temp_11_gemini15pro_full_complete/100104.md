interpours:/ˌɪntərˈpɔːrz/ | v. |插入，插话，介入|To insert something between other things or to interrupt a conversation. |例句：He interpoursed his opinion into the discussion. 他把自己的意见插进了讨论中。

例句：She interpoursed a question during the lecture. 她在讲座中插了一个问题。

|近义词：interject, insert, introduce, interpolate|反义词:remove, extract, withdraw|词性:动词
