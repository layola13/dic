hypercompetitive:/ˌhaɪpər kəmˈpɛtɪtɪv/
adjective
过度竞争的，极度竞争的
Excessively competitive; intensely competitive, often to an unhealthy or destructive degree.
The tech industry is known for its hypercompetitive environment. 科技行业以其极度竞争的环境而闻名。
In this hypercompetitive market, businesses must constantly innovate to survive.  在这个竞争极其激烈的市场中，企业必须不断创新才能生存。
近义词: cutthroat, fiercely competitive, ruthless, aggressive
反义词: cooperative, collaborative, laid-back
