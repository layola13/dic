carburized:/ˈkɑːrbjʊˌraɪzd/ /ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪzd/ | adj. | 渗碳的，碳化的 | Treated or combined with carbon.  The process of increasing the carbon content of the surface of steel by exposing it to a carbon-rich atmosphere at elevated temperatures. This increases the surface hardness and wear resistance. | The gears were carburized to improve their durability. 这些齿轮经过渗碳处理以提高其耐用性。| synonyms: carbonized, cemented | antonyms: decarburized | adjective

carburize:/ˈkɑːrbjʊˌraɪz/ /ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪz/ | v. | 渗碳，使碳化 | To treat or combine with carbon. To increase the carbon content of the surface of a metal, especially steel, by exposing it to a carbon-rich atmosphere at high temperature. |  The manufacturer carburizes the steel to harden it. 制造商对钢进行渗碳处理以使其硬化。 | synonyms: carbonize, cement | antonyms: decarburize | verb
