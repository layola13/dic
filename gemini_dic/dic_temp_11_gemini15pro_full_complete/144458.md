outta:/ˈaʊtə/| prep. informal|从...出来，离开，出自，缺乏|A nonstandard pronunciation of "out of".  Used to indicate movement away from a place, origin, or condition.|例句：Get outta here!  离开这里！|例句：I'm outta money. 我没钱了。|近义词：out of|反义词:into|词性:介词 (preposition) - informal contraction
