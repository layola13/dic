amount:/əˈmaʊnt/| n. vi.|数量，金额；总计，合计|A quantity of something, typically the total of a thing or things in number, size, value, or extent. |The total amount of money spent was $1000.  花费的总金额为1000美元。|近义词：quantity, sum, total|反义词:lack, deficit|noun
amount:/əˈmaʊnt/| vi.|总计，等于|To add up to; to be equivalent to. |His silence amounted to an admission of guilt. 他的沉默等于承认了罪行。|近义词：equal, total, add up to|反义词:differ, decrease|verb
