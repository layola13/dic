nondisturbance:/ˌnɒndɪˈstɜːbəns/ | n. | 不干扰，不打扰; 平静，安宁 |  The state of not being disturbed or interfered with.  | The lease included a nondisturbance clause to protect the tenant.  租约中包含一项不干扰条款，以保护租户。 | synonyms: peace, tranquility, quiet, calm | antonyms: disturbance, interruption, interference | noun


nondisturbance:/ˌnɒndɪˈstɜːbəns/ | n. | 不受干扰，平安无事;  (尤指租户权益的)非干扰(权) |  A legal agreement that existing contracts, such as a lease, will be honored in the event of a change of ownership. | The lender required a nondisturbance agreement from the landlord.  贷款方要求房东提供一份非干扰协议。| synonyms: immunity, protection, security | antonyms: vulnerability, exposure, risk | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + disturbance.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Disturbance" comes from the Old French word "destourber," meaning "to disturb" or "to interrupt."
