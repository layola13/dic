acrylamides: /əˈkrɪləˌmaɪdz/ | n. | 丙烯酰胺（复数） | A group of organic compounds with the chemical formula CH2=CHCONH2 and its derivatives, often used in the production of polyacrylamide. | Acrylamides are found in some cooked foods.  丙烯酰胺存在于一些烹制食品中。|  Acrylamide levels in fried potatoes can be high.  炸薯条中的丙烯酰胺含量可能很高。 | synonyms: propenamides | antonyms: None | noun
