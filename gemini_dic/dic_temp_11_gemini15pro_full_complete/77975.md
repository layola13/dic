gentianose:/ˌdʒɛnʃiˈənoʊs/| n.|龙胆三糖|A trisaccharide composed of two glucose units and one fructose unit, found in the roots of gentians and some other plants.|Gentianose is a reserve carbohydrate in some plant species. 龙胆三糖是一些植物物种中的储备碳水化合物。| Gentianose can be hydrolyzed to glucose and fructose. 龙胆三糖可以水解成葡萄糖和果糖。|Synonyms: trisaccharide|Antonyms: monosaccharide|noun
