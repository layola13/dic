blitzkrieged:/ˈblɪtskriːɡd/
v. |闪电战；快速突击；|To attack suddenly and overwhelmingly, like a blitzkrieg.  像闪电战一样突然而势不可挡地攻击。|The small country was blitzkrieged by its larger neighbor.  这个小国被其强大的邻国闪电战了。|overran, overwhelmed, stormed, routed|defended, protected, resisted|verb


blitzkrieg:/ˈblɪtskriːɡ/
n. |闪电战；|A sudden and overwhelming military attack.  突然而势不可挡的军事攻击。|The blitzkrieg took the enemy completely by surprise.  闪电战完全出乎敌人的意料。| onslaught, assault, offensive|defense, retreat|noun
