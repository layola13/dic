necrotic:/nəˈkrɒtɪk/| adj.|坏死的，腐烂的|Relating to or descriptive of necrosis (the death of most or all of the cells in an organ or tissue due to disease, injury, or failure of the blood supply).|The surgeon removed the necrotic tissue.|近义词：dead, gangrenous, mortified|反义词：living, healthy|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
