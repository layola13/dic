boneyard:/ˈboʊnjɑːrd/| n.|飞机坟场；骨场；墓地|A place where old aircraft, ships, or other vehicles are stored or scrapped.  A place where many people have died, especially in a battle or disaster. |The Mojave Desert is a famous boneyard for retired airplanes. 莫哈韦沙漠是著名的退役飞机存放地。|synonyms: graveyard, cemetery, junkyard, scrapyard|antonyms:  - (no direct antonym, perhaps "new car lot" for vehicle context)|noun


boneyard:/ˈboʊnjɑːrd/| adj.|瘦骨嶙峋的；荒凉的|Resembling a boneyard; barren or desolate.|The landscape was boneyard dry after the drought.  干旱过后，土地变得荒凉干燥。|synonyms: skeletal, gaunt, barren, desolate|antonyms: lush, fertile, vibrant|adjective
