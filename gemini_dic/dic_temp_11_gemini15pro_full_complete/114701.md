mallam:/ˈmæləm/| n. |先生，老师，学者 (主要用于西非，尤指对穆斯林宗教领袖或老师的尊称)|A title of respect, especially in parts of West Africa, for a Muslim religious teacher or scholar. |例句：The mallam led the prayers at the mosque.  这位先生在清真寺带领祈祷。

例句：We sought advice from the local mallam. 我们向当地的学者寻求建议。

|近义词：teacher, scholar, cleric, mister|反义词: (无明确的反义词)|noun
