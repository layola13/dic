illogically:/ɪˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb|不合逻辑地，无理地|In a way that does not follow the rules of logic; unreasonably.|He acted illogically, making decisions based on emotion rather than reason. 他行为不合逻辑，决策基于情感而不是理性。|unreasonably, irrationally, absurdly, senselessly|logically, rationally, reasonably
词性: 副词

illogically:/ɪˈlɑːdʒɪkli/
adverb|不合逻辑地，无理地|In a way that does not follow the rules of logic; unreasonably.|She illogically concluded that because the sky was blue, it must be sunny. 她不合逻辑地断定，因为天空是蓝色的，所以一定是晴天。|unreasonably, irrationally, absurdly, senselessly|logically, rationally, reasonably
