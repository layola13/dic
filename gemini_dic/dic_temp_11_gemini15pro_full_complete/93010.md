idiosyncratic:/ˌɪdiəsɪŋˈkrætɪk/| adj.|特殊的，特有的，怪异的|Relating to idiosyncrasy; peculiar or individual.|He has an idiosyncratic way of tying his shoelaces. 他系鞋带的方式很特别。|Synonyms: peculiar, quirky, eccentric, distinctive, unique|Antonyms: typical, common, ordinary, usual, conventional|adjective

idiosyncrasy:/ˌɪdiəˈsɪŋkrəsi/| n.|特性，怪癖，特质|A mode of behavior or way of thought peculiar to an individual.|One of his idiosyncrasies is that he always wears a hat indoors.  他其中一个怪癖就是在室内也总是戴着帽子。|Synonyms: peculiarity, quirk, eccentricity, mannerism|Antonyms: normality, conventionality|noun
