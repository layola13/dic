danishry: /ˈdeɪnɪʃri/ | n. | 丹麦糕点；丹麦式 | Danish pastries, or simply "Danish," are a type of sweet pastry made of laminated dough created by incorporating 27 layers of butter.  They often contain fillings such as fruit, cheese, or custard. | I love to have a Danish with my coffee in the morning. 我早上喜欢喝咖啡配丹麦糕点。 | Synonyms: Viennoiserie, pastry | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "savory bread" or "unsweetened pastry") | Noun

danishry: /ˈdeɪnɪʃri/ | adj. | 丹麦的，丹麦风格的(较少用) |  Relating to or characteristic of Denmark or its people.
