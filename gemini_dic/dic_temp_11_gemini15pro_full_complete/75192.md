frizzle:/ˈfrɪzəl/ | v. |  卷曲，使卷曲；油炸，煎(发出滋滋声) | To form (hair) into tight curls; to fry something until it is crisp and curled at the edges. | The heat frizzled my hair.  高温使我的头发卷曲了。| synonyms: curl, crisp, sear, sizzle | antonyms: straighten, flatten | verb

frizzle:/ˈfrɪzəl/ | n. |  卷发；油炸声 | The act of frizzing or the state of being frizzed; a sizzling sound. |  The frizzle of the bacon woke me up. 培根的油炸声把我吵醒了。| synonyms: curl, sizzle | antonyms: - | noun
