decontaminate:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪt/ | v. |净化，清除污染，消毒|To remove or neutralize contaminants, making something safe to handle or use.|例句：The workers decontaminated the area after the chemical spill. 工人们在化学品泄漏后对该区域进行了净化。

例句：They had to decontaminate the lab before conducting the next experiment.  在进行下一个实验之前，他们必须对实验室进行消毒。|近义词：cleanse, purify, sanitize, sterilize|反义词：contaminate, pollute|词性：动词

decontamination:/ˌdiːkənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |净化，清除污染，消毒|The process of removing or neutralizing contaminants.|例句：The decontamination of the site took several weeks.  该地点的净化工作花费了数周时间。

例句：Thorough decontamination is essential to prevent the spread of infection.  彻底的消毒对于防止感染的传播至关重要。|近义词：cleansing, purification, sanitation, sterilization|反义词：contamination, pollution|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (meaning "remove, reverse") + contaminate (from Latin contaminatus, past participle of contaminare "to pollute").
