divergency:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒənsi/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒənsi/
|n.|分歧，差异，背离|A drawing apart or separation.|The divergency of their opinions led to a heated debate. 他们的意见分歧导致了激烈的辩论。|Synonyms: difference, disagreement, deviation, disparity|Antonyms: agreement, convergence, similarity, uniformity|noun

divergent:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒənt/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒənt/
|adj.|分歧的，不同的，相异的|Tending to be different or develop in different directions.|The two species followed divergent evolutionary paths. 这两个物种沿着不同的进化路径发展。|Synonyms: different, varying, dissimilar, disparate|Antonyms: similar, convergent, uniform, alike|adjective
