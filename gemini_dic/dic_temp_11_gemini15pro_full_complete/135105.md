nonobsessive:/ˌnɒnəbˈsɛsɪv/ /ˌnɑːnəbˈsɛsɪv/| adj.| 非强迫性的，非执着的|  Not characterized by obsession; not excessively preoccupied with something. | She has a nonobsessive interest in healthy eating. 她对健康饮食有着非执着的兴趣。|non-obsessed, casual, relaxed|obsessive, compulsive, fixated|adjective
Etymology-> From non- + obsessive.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "obsessive" derives from the Latin "obsessus," meaning "besieged" or "blockaded."
