comprisal:/kəmˈpraɪzəl/| n.|报复；报复行为|an act of retaliation; something unpleasant done to someone because of something unpleasant that they have done|The bombing was in reprisal for the assassination of their leader. 这次爆炸是对他们领导人遇刺的报复。|Synonyms: retaliation, revenge, retribution, vengeance|Antonyms: forgiveness, pardon|Noun
