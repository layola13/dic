circumduce:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈd(j)uːs/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈduːs/
verb
环绕移动；旋转；画圆
to draw (a line, etc.) around something; to trace the outline of; to make a circular movement
The surgeon circumduced the patient's arm to test their range of motion.  外科医生旋转病人的手臂以测试他们的活动范围。
The compass point circumduced a perfect circle.  圆规的尖端画了一个完美的圆。

synonym: rotate, revolve, circle, gyrate
antonym: fix, stay, remain
词性: 动词(verb)
