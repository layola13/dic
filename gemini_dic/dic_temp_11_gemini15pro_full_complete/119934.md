milkness: /ˈmɪlk.nəs/
n.
乳状, 乳白, 乳白色; 似牛奶
The state or quality of being milky or resembling milk.
The fog had a strange milkness to it. 这雾带着一种奇特的乳白色。
近义词: milkiness, lactescence
反义词: clarity, clearness
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From milk + -ness.  "Milk" comes from Old English *meolc (with various spellings), from Proto-Germanic *melk-, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *melg- ("to milk; milk"). The suffix "-ness" is a native English suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
