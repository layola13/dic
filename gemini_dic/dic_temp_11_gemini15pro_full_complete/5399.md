Etymology:
From Middle French *almain*, *allemain*, from Old French *alemant*, from Frankish *Alaman*, from Proto-Germanic *Alamanniz* (plural).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *alja* (“all”) + *mann* (“man”).  Cognate with Old High German *alaman*, German *Alemanne* (“Alemanni”).
