foxly:/ˈfɒksli/| adj.|狡猾的，像狐狸一样的|Resembling or characteristic of a fox, especially in cunning or slyness.  This word evokes the image of a fox's cleverness and craftiness, often with a negative connotation.|He gave her a foxly grin. 他对她狡黠一笑。|近义词：cunning, sly, crafty, wily, artful, devious|反义词：honest, forthright, ingenuous, naive|词性:形容词
