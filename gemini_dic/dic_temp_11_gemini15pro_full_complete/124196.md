moselle:/moʊˈzɛl/| n.|摩泽尔葡萄酒，摩泽尔河|A light, dry white wine produced in the Moselle valley, a region of Germany along the Moselle River. Also refers to the river itself.|例句：We enjoyed a bottle of crisp Moselle with our dinner. 我们晚餐喝了一瓶清爽的摩泽尔葡萄酒。

例句：The Moselle River flows through Germany, France, and Luxembourg. 摩泽尔河流经德国、法国和卢森堡。|近义词：Riesling (often used for Moselle wines), white wine|反义词:red wine|词性:noun
