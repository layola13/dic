multiphotography:/ˌmʌlti fəˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |多重摄影，多次曝光摄影|The process or technique of producing multiple exposures on a single photographic image. This can be achieved through various methods, including taking multiple pictures on the same frame of film or digitally combining separate images.| He experimented with multiphotography to create surreal and dreamlike images. 他尝试用多重曝光摄影来创作超现实和梦幻般的图像。| synonyms: multiple exposure, superposition, composite photography | antonyms: single exposure | noun
