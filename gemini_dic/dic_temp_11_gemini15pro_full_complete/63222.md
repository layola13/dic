enteritis:/ˌentəˈraɪtɪs/| n. |肠炎|Inflammation of the intestine, especially the small intestine, usually causing diarrhea.|The doctor diagnosed him with enteritis after he experienced severe abdominal pain and diarrhea. 医生在他经历了严重的腹痛和腹泻后，诊断他患有肠炎。|Synonyms: inflammation of the bowels, colitis (when affecting the colon)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a disease condition, but one might consider "intestinal health")|noun
