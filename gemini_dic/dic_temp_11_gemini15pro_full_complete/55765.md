diversified:/dɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪd/ /daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪd/| adj.|多样化的，多元化的|Made up of different kinds of people or things.  Investing in a diversified portfolio is a way to manage risk.|She has a diversified investment portfolio. 她有一个多元化的投资组合。|He believes a diversified workforce leads to greater innovation. 他认为多元化的员工队伍能带来更大的创新。|synonyms: varied, assorted, heterogeneous, multifaceted|antonyms: homogeneous, uniform, similar, concentrated|adjective
