bamboozle:/bæmˈbuːzl/
verb
欺骗，迷惑
to trick or deceive someone, often in a playful or mischievous way
The salesman bamboozled him into buying a used car.  推销员把他骗买了辆二手车。
近义词:trick, deceive, hoodwink, dupe, swindle
反义词: enlighten, inform, clarify
词性:v.

bamboozled:/bæmˈbuːzld/
adjective
被欺骗的，被迷惑的
Confused and tricked
I was completely bamboozled by his complicated explanation. 我被他复杂的解释完全搞糊涂了。
近义词: tricked, deceived, confused, bewildered
反义词: clear-headed, informed
词性:adj.


词源:
Origin uncertain, possibly from a dialectal word meaning "to bewilder, confuse."  起源不确定，可能来自一个意为“使迷惑、困惑”的方言词。
