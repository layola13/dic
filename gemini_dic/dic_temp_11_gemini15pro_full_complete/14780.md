awning:/ˈɔːnɪŋ/| n. |遮阳篷，雨篷|A canvas or other material stretched over a framework and attached to a building to provide shade or shelter from rain.|We sat under the awning of the cafe, watching the people go by. 我们坐在咖啡馆的遮阳篷下，看着人们走来走去。|The brightly striped awning shielded the shop window from the strong sunlight.  色彩鲜艳的条纹遮阳篷保护商店橱窗免受强烈阳光照射。|synonyms: canopy, sunshade, marquee|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "exposure" in a contextual sense)|noun
