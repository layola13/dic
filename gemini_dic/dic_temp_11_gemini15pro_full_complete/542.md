aboriginal: /ˌæbəˈrɪdʒənl/ /ˌæbəˈrɪdʒɪnl/ | adj. n. |土著的；土著居民|inhabiting or existing in a land from the earliest times or from before the arrival of colonists; indigenous.  A person, animal, or plant that has been in a country or region from earliest times.|例句：The aboriginal people of Australia have a rich cultural heritage. 澳大利亚的土著居民拥有丰富的文化遗产。|近义词：indigenous, native, original|反义词：non-native, foreign|词性: adjective, noun
