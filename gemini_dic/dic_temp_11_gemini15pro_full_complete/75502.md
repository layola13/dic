fryable:/ˈfraɪəbl/| adj.|可油炸的，适合油炸的|Suitable for frying.  Something that can be cooked in hot oil.|例句：These potatoes are fryable. 这些土豆可以油炸。|近义词：cookable, edible|反义词:uncookable|词性:形容词
词源: From fry + -able.  "Fry" comes from Old French "frire".
