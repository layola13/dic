iatromathematical: /ˌaɪˌætrəˌmæθəˈmætɪkəl/ | adj. | 医药数学的，医用数学的 |  Relating to iatromathematics, a historical approach to medicine that incorporated mathematical principles, especially from the fields of geometry and astronomy.  It attempted to describe the human body and its functions, as well as diagnoses and treatments, in mathematical terms. |  The physician's iatromathematical theories were considered quite radical at the time. 这位医生的医药数学理论在当时被认为相当激进。 | Near synonyms:  quantitative medicine, mathematical medicine  | Antonyms: qualitative medicine, holistic medicine | adjective
