impermissibilities:/ɪmˌpɜːrmɪsəˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
不可允许的事物；不允许的行为；违禁行为
The state or quality of being impermissible; something that is not permissible.
The report detailed the various impermissibilities uncovered during the audit.  这份报告详细列出了在审计过程中发现的各种违规行为。
The company has strict rules regarding impermissibilities in the workplace. 公司对工作场所的违规行为有严格的规定。
Synonyms: illegalities, improprieties, infractions, violations, offenses
Antonyms: permissibilities, allowances, authorizations
