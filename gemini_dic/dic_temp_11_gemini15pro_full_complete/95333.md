incensed:/ɪnˈsenst/| adj.|激怒的，愤怒的|Very angry; enraged.  Originating from the Latin "incendere" meaning "to set on fire," it implies a burning anger.|例句：She was incensed by the unfair treatment. 她对不公平的待遇感到愤怒。

例句：He was incensed at the suggestion that he was lying. 他对关于他说谎的暗示感到愤怒。|近义词：enraged, furious, outraged, indignant, infuriated|反义词：calm, peaceful, pleased, content|词性:形容词
