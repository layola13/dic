inundant:/ˈɪnʌndənt/ | adj. | 溢出的，泛滥的；过多的，过剩的 | Overflowing; abundant to excess.  Characterized by an overwhelming abundance or excess. | The library was inundated with requests for the new bestseller.  图书馆被新畅销书的请求淹没了。 | Synonyms: overflowing, awash, flooded, saturated, overwhelmed | Antonyms: scarce, deficient, lacking, meager, sparse | adjective

inundate:/ˈɪnʌndeɪt/ | v. | 淹没；泛滥；使充满 |  (1) To flood; overwhelm with water. (2) To overwhelm with things or people to be dealt with. | The town was inundated after the hurricane. 飓风过后，小镇被淹没了。  The customer service department was inundated with complaints. 客服部门收到了大量的投诉。 | Synonyms: flood, deluge, swamp, overwhelm, submerge | Antonyms: drain, dry, empty | verb
