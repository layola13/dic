geat:/ɡeɪt/ | n. | 门, 入口, 大门, (闸门式的)通道 |A hinged barrier that swings open and shut, usually made of wood, metal, or other rigid material. Used to close an opening in a wall, fence, or other barrier. |  例句：The geat creaked open slowly. 大门缓缓地嘎吱作响。
