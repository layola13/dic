attesters:/əˈtestərz/| n. |证明人，证人，鉴定人|People who attest to the truth or validity of something.  Those who provide evidence or confirmation.|例句：The attesters signed the document to confirm its authenticity. 证明人签署了文件以确认其真实性。

例句：Several attesters corroborated the victim's account of the incident. 几位证人证实了受害者对事件的描述。|近义词：witnesses, verifiers, certifiers, corroborators|反义词：deniers, challengers|noun
