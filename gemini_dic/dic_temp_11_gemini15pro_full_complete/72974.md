foolery:/ˈfuːləri/ /ˈfuːlri/| n. |愚蠢的行为；傻事；玩笑；胡闹| Foolish behavior or speech; silly actions or jokes. |  His antics were just harmless foolery. 他的滑稽动作只是无伤大雅的玩笑。 | synonyms: nonsense, silliness, tomfoolery, absurdity, buffoonery | antonyms: seriousness, wisdom, sense, prudence | noun


Etymology->
From Middle English *folerye*, from Old French *folerie*, from *fol* (“fool”) + *-erie* (suffix forming abstract nouns). Ultimately from Latin *follis* (“bellows, windbag”).
