antitraditionalists:/ˌæntitrəˈdɪʃənəlɪsts/
|n.|反传统主义者|People who oppose or reject traditional beliefs, customs, or practices.  Those who challenge established norms and advocate for change.|The antitraditionalists questioned the relevance of age-old customs in modern society. 反传统主义者质疑古老习俗在现代社会中的相关性。|
|synonyms: iconoclasts, reformers, radicals, revolutionaries, nonconformists|
|antonyms: traditionalists, conservatives, conformists|
|noun|

antitraditionalist:/ˌæntitrəˈdɪʃənəlɪst/
|adj.|反传统的|Opposing or rejecting traditional beliefs, customs, or practices. |They took an antitraditionalist stance on the issue of marriage. 他们在婚姻问题上采取了反传统的立场。|
|synonyms: iconoclastic, reformist, radical, revolutionary, nonconformist|
|antonyms: traditional, conservative, conformist|
|adjective|
