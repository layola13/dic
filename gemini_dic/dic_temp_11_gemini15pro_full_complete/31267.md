chapattis:/tʃəˈpɑːtiːz/
noun
印度薄饼（chapati的复数形式）
Thin, flat unleavened bread cooked on a griddle, originating from the Indian subcontinent.
We had chapattis with our curry. 我们咖喱配着印度薄饼吃。
Chapattis are a staple food in many parts of India. 印度薄饼是印度许多地区的主食。
Synonyms: roti, flatbread
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a food item)
