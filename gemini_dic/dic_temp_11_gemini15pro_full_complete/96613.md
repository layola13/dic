indulgence:/ɪnˈdʌldʒəns/ | n. | 放纵；沉溺；嗜好；宽容；恩惠 | the act of allowing yourself or another person to have something enjoyable, especially more than is good for you |  She treated herself to a weekend of indulgence. 她给自己一个周末的放纵。| synonyms: gratification, pleasure, luxury, pampering, excess | antonyms: abstinence, self-denial, restraint, moderation | noun

indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ | v. | 放纵；沉溺；满足（欲望）；听任；迁就 | allow oneself or another person to enjoy the pleasure of. |  I indulged in a long hot bath. 我尽情享受了一个长时间的热水澡。| synonyms:  gratify, satisfy, fulfill, cater to, pander to | antonyms: resist, deny, abstain, refrain | verb
