facsimileing: /fækˈsɪmɪliɪŋ/
verb
传真；复制
the act of sending a facsimile or making a copy of something.
例句：He was facsimileing the document to the head office. 他正在把文件传真到总部。

例句：Facsimileing old photographs can help preserve them. 复制老照片有助于保存它们。

近义词：copying, duplicating, reproducing, transmitting
反义词：N/A
词性：v.


facsimile: /fækˈsɪmɪli/
noun
传真；复制品
an exact copy of something.
例句：The museum displayed a facsimile of the Declaration of Independence. 博物馆展出了《独立宣言》的复制品。

例句：She sent a facsimile of the contract to her lawyer. 她把合同的传真件发给了她的律师。

近义词：copy, duplicate, replica, reproduction
反义词：original
词性：n.
