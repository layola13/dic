intraracial:/ˌɪntrəˈreɪʃəl/ |adj.|同种族内的，种族内部的|Occurring within a single race or ethnic group.  Relating to members of the same race. |例句：Intraracial marriage is more common than interracial marriage. 同种族婚姻比跨种族婚姻更普遍。

例句：Intraracial tensions can sometimes be as complex as interracial conflicts.  种族内部的紧张关系有时会像种族间的冲突一样复杂。|近义词：within a race, same-race|反义词：interracial|词性:adjective
