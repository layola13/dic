fine-tune:/ˌfaɪnˈtjuːn/| vt. |微调，精细调整|To make small adjustments to something in order to improve its performance or effectiveness.|例句：The engineer fine-tuned the engine to improve fuel efficiency. 工程师微调了发动机以提高燃油效率。|近义词：adjust, refine, tweak|反义词:worsen, damage|verb
fine-tune:/ˌfaɪnˈtjuːn/| n. |微调，精细调整|A small adjustment made to something to improve its performance or effectiveness.|例句：The car's suspension needed a fine-tune. 汽车的悬架需要微调。|近义词：adjustment, refinement, tweak|反义词:deterioration|noun
