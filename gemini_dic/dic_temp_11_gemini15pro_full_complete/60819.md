electroplaters:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌpleɪtərz/| n. |电镀工；电镀工人|People who plate metal objects with a thin layer of another metal using electrolysis.  They work in industries like automotive, jewelry, and electronics. |The electroplaters carefully coated the car parts with chrome. 电镀工小心地给汽车零件镀上铬。| Electroplaters ensure a uniform and durable finish on various metal products. 电镀工确保各种金属产品表面均匀耐用。| 近义词:platers, metal finishers| 反义词: N/A |Noun
