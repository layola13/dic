bodaciously:/boʊˈdeɪʃəsli/
adverb|放肆地，大胆地，惊人地|In a bodacious manner; remarkably; extraordinarily.|She bodaciously asked for a raise after only a week on the job. 她才工作一周就大胆地要求加薪。|Synonyms: audaciously, brazenly, daringly, outrageously|Antonyms: timidly, shyly, cautiously|词性:副词

bodacious:/boʊˈdeɪʃəs/
adjective|放肆的，大胆的，惊人的；极好的，很棒的|Remarkable; extraordinary; excellent; admirable.|She wore a bodacious dress to the party. 她穿了一件惊艳的礼服去参加派对。|Synonyms: excellent, outstanding, remarkable, awesome, amazing|Antonyms: terrible, awful, dreadful, unremarkable|词性:形容词
