lazen:/ˈleɪzən/ | v. | 偷懒，怠惰 | To act lazily; to relax and avoid work or exertion. | He lazened around the house all day. 他整天在家无所事事。|  I lazened by the pool, soaking up the sun. 我在泳池边懒洋洋地晒太阳。| 近义词: lounge, loaf, idle, relax | 反义词: work, toil, strive | 词性: 动词

词源:  From the adjective "lazy" + the verb-forming suffix "-en."  "Lazy" comes from Middle English *lazi, from Old English *lǣs, meaning "slow, sluggish."
