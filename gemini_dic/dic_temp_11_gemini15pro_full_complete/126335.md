myositic:/ˌmaɪəˈsaɪtɪk/ /ˌmaɪoʊˈsaɪtɪk/| adj.|肌炎的，与肌炎有关的|Relating to or affected by myositis (inflammation of muscle tissue).|例句：She experienced myositic pain in her legs. 她腿部经历了肌炎的疼痛。

例句：Myositic symptoms can include muscle weakness and pain. 肌炎的症状可能包括肌肉无力和疼痛。|近义词：inflammatory (inflammatory muscle disease)|反义词: healthy, normal (in the context of muscle tissue) |词性：形容词
