cryptosporidia:/ˌkrɪptoʊspɔːˈrɪdiə/| n. |隐孢子虫（复数）|A parasitic protozoan of the genus Cryptosporidium that can cause diarrhea.  A microscopic parasite that causes the diarrheal disease cryptosporidiosis. |例句：Cryptosporidia are a common cause of waterborne illness. 隐孢子虫是水传播疾病的常见原因。

例句：The water was contaminated with cryptosporidia. 水被隐孢子虫污染了。|近义词：parasite, protozoan|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a biological organism)|Noun
