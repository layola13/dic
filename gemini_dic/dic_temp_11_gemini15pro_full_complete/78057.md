geocaching:/ˈdʒiːoʊˌkæʃɪŋ/ /ˈdʒiːəʊˌkæʃɪŋ/
n.|寻宝游戏；地理藏宝|A recreational activity in which participants use a Global Positioning System (GPS) receiver or mobile device and other navigational techniques to hide and seek containers, called "geocaches" or "caches", at specific locations marked by coordinates all over the world.|We went geocaching in the woods last weekend.  我们上周末去树林里玩寻宝游戏了。|treasure hunting, letterboxing| |noun
