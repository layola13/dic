abaft:/əˈbæft/| adv. prep.|在船尾, 向船尾|Behind, toward the stern of a ship or aircraft. |The galley is located abaft the engine room. 厨房位于机舱后部。|The sailor went abaft to check the rudder. 那名水手到船尾去检查舵。|近义词：aster, behind, rearward|反义词:forward, ahead|词性:副词, 介词
abaft:|/əˈbæft/| prep.|在...的后面|In or toward the rear or stern of a ship or aircraft.|We secured the life raft abaft the main mast. 我们把救生筏固定在主桅杆的后面。|The cabins are located abaft the bridge. 船舱位于舰桥后面。|近义词：behind, astern of|反义词:forward of, before|词性:介词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æftan, from *æf + -tan (a suffix indicating location). Cognate with Old Frisian *eftan, Dutch achter, German hinten. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *after.
