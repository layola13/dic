incontentation:/ˌɪnkɒntɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 不满足，不满意 | Lack of contentment; dissatisfaction. |  His incontentation with the current political climate was palpable. 他对当前政治气候的不满是显而易见的。| Example:  She expressed her incontentation with the service at the restaurant.  她对餐厅的服务表示不满。 | Synonyms: discontent, dissatisfaction, disquiet, unease | Antonyms: contentment, satisfaction, pleasure, happiness | Noun
