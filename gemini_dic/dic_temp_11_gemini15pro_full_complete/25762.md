burbot:/ˈbɜːrbɒt/ (UK), /ˈbɜːrbɑːt/ (US)| n. | 淡水鳕鱼；江鳕 |A freshwater fish of the cod family, found in northern Europe and Asia, and in North America. |例句：The burbot is the only member of the cod family that lives exclusively in freshwater. 江鳕是鳕鱼家族中唯一完全生活在淡水中的成员。

例句：Burbot are nocturnal and feed primarily on other fish. 江鳕是夜行动物，主要以其他鱼类为食。|近义词：eelpout, freshwater cod, ling |反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific fish species) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *barbotte, from Late Latin *barbottus, alteration of Latin barba "beard," referring to the single barbel on the chin of the fish.
