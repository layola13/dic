nonadmissibilities:/ˌnɒnədˌmɪsəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 不可受理性，不许可性 | The state or quality of being inadmissible; instances of being inadmissible.  This often refers to reasons why someone or something might be excluded, rejected, or not allowed. |  Due to several nonadmissibilities, his application for a visa was rejected. 由于一些不可受理的原因，他的签证申请被拒绝了。 | His past criminal record was one of the nonadmissibilities that prevented him from entering the country. 他的犯罪记录是阻止他入境的不许可原因之一。 |  ineligibilities, disqualifications | admissibilities, qualifications | noun
