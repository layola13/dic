averruncate: /ˌævəˈrʌŋkeɪt/
verb
阻止；防止；避免(灾祸等)；消除
to avert, ward off, or prevent (something evil or harmful); to remove or destroy completely
The shaman performed a ritual to averruncate the evil spirits from the village.  萨满举行仪式以驱除村庄里的邪灵。
The mayor promised to averruncate the threat of flooding. 市长承诺要消除洪水的威胁。
近义词: prevent, avert, obviate, preclude, forestall, ward off, exorcise
反义词: cause, incite, provoke, encourage, facilitate
词性: verb

Etymology: From Latin *averruncāre*, from *ab-* (“away, from”) + *erruncāre* (“to weed out”), from *ē-* (variant of *ex-*) (“out”) + *runcāre* (“to weed”), from *runcō* (“a weeding-hook”).
