hellful:/ˈhɛlfl/| adj.|地狱般的，充满痛苦的|Resembling or characteristic of hell; extremely unpleasant or difficult.|The traffic jam was hellful, taking three hours to travel just ten miles. 交通堵塞简直是地狱般的，开了三个小时才走了十英里。|Synonyms: infernal, agonizing, torturous, harrowing|Antonyms: heavenly, blissful, pleasant, delightful|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "hell" + "-ful."  "Hell" comes from Old English "hel,"  meaning "the underworld, abode of the dead." "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
