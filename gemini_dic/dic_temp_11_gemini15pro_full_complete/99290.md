intercoordinating:/ˌɪntərkoʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
相互协调
The act of coordinating different elements or parts with each other.  This implies a reciprocal relationship where the elements influence and adjust to one another.
The different departments are intercoordinating their efforts to improve efficiency. 不同部门正在相互协调，努力提高效率。
Intercoordinating the work of different teams can be challenging.  协调不同团队的工作可能具有挑战性。
近义词: collaborating, cooperating, integrating, synchronizing
反义词: conflicting, clashing, diverging
