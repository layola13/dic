lovesicknesses:/ˈlʌvˌsɪknəsɪz/
n.
相思病，单恋的痛苦
The state of being lovesick; a yearning for a romantic partner.
She suffered from lovesicknesses after her boyfriend moved away.  她男朋友搬走后，她饱受相思之苦。
lovesickness, heartache, pining
wellness, indifference
