marl:/mɑːrl/ | n. | 泥灰岩；泥灰土 | A crumbly mixture of clay and calcium carbonate, used especially as a fertilizer. |  The farmer spread marl on his fields. 农民在地里撒了泥灰。 | synonyms: loam, clay, silt | antonyms: sand, gravel | noun

marl:/mɑːrl/ | v. | 用泥灰土施肥 | To fertilize with marl. |  He marled the fields every spring. 他每年春天都用泥灰土给田地施肥。 | synonyms: fertilize, enrich, amend | antonyms: deplete, impoverish | verb
