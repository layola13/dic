lithology:/lɪˈθɒlədʒi/ /lɪˈθɑːlədʒi/
noun
岩性；岩相学
The study of the general character of rocks, including their origin, age, composition, and structure.  It also refers to the description of these characteristics, especially in a particular area.
The lithology of the area is primarily sandstone. 该地区的岩性主要是砂岩。
Synonyms: petrology, rock type, stone composition
Antonyms: None (Lithology describes a characteristic, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
