dorian:/ˈdɔːriən/ | adj. n.| 多里安式的；多里安人 |Relating to or denoting a style of ancient Greek architecture, one of the three classical orders, characterized by fluted columns with simple capitals and no base. Also, a member of the Dorian people, one of the four major groups of ancient Greece. |The temple was built in the Dorian style. 这座寺庙是以多利安风格建造的。|  He was a Dorian by birth. 他是多里安人。|近义词:Spartan (for the people), Doric (for the style) |反义词:Ionian, Corinthian (architectural styles)|词性:adjective, noun


词源: From Latin *Dōrius*, from Ancient Greek *Δώριος* (*Dṓrios*).
