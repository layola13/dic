champerty: /ˈtʃæmpərti/ | n. | 包揽诉讼，帮诉 | The illegal act of helping someone with a lawsuit in return for part of any money they win. | Example 1: The lawyer was accused of champerty for funding his client's lawsuit in exchange for a percentage of the settlement. 律师因资助其客户的诉讼以换取一定比例的和解金而被指控犯有包揽诉讼罪。

Example 2: Champerty is considered an offense against justice because it can encourage frivolous lawsuits. 包揽诉讼被认为是对司法的一种犯罪，因为它会鼓励无意义的诉讼。| 近义词: maintenance, barratry | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider lawful legal representation) | 词性: noun
