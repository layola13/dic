nothingist:/ˈnʌθɪŋɪst/ | n. |虚无主义者；认为生命没有意义的人|A person who believes that life is meaningless and that there is no objective morality or inherent value in anything. | He called himself a nothingist, rejecting all established values and beliefs. 他自称为虚无主义者，拒绝所有既定的价值观和信仰。|Example:  The nothingist felt no attachment to material possessions.  这个虚无主义者对物质财富没有任何依恋。|Synonyms: nihilist, pessimist, cynic |Antonyms: optimist, idealist |noun
