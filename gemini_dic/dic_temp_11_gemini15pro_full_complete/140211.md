Etymology->
From *official* + *-ese*, a suffix used to form nouns denoting the language or style characteristic of a particular group or place (like *Chinese*, *Japanese*). *Official* comes from Old French *official*, from Late Latin *officialis*, from Latin *officium* "duty, service, office."
