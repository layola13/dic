ire's:/ˈaɪərz/
noun
愤怒；怒火
A strong feeling of anger and displeasure.
Her face was red with ire. 她的脸因愤怒而涨得通红。
Synonyms: anger, rage, fury, wrath
Antonyms: calmness, peace, tranquility
词性: 名词
