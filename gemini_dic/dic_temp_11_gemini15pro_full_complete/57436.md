drachma:/ˈdrækmə/ | n. | 德拉克马（希腊货币单位） | A drachma was a unit of currency used in ancient and modern Greece. |  He paid ten drachma for the loaf of bread. 他花了十德拉克马买了一条面包。|  The museum displayed several ancient drachma coins.  博物馆展出了几枚古德拉克马硬币。 | synonym: currency, money | antonym: (no direct antonym for a unit of currency) | noun

drachmal:/ˈdrækməl/ | adj. | 德拉克马的，与德拉克马有关的 | Relating to or denominated in drachmas. | The drachmal debt was substantial. 德拉克马债务数额巨大。 |  The government introduced new drachmal regulations. 政府出台了新的德拉克马法规。| synonym: monetary, financial (in a broad sense) | antonym: (no direct antonym) | adjective
