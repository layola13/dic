maestros:/ˈmaɪstroʊz/ (US), /ˈmaɪstrəʊz/ (UK)| n. |大师; 巨匠; 名家|A maestro is a master of a particular skill, especially in the arts, such as conducting, painting, or music.|The maestro led the orchestra with passion and precision.  这位大师以激情和精准的技艺指挥着乐队。|The culinary maestro created a dish that was both beautiful and delicious.  这位烹饪大师创作了一道既美观又美味的菜肴。| synonyms: expert, virtuoso, genius, master, prodigy| antonyms: amateur, novice, beginner| noun
