observe:/əbˈzɜːrv/ (US) /əbˈzɜːv/ (UK)| v. |观察；遵守；庆祝；评论|To watch carefully, to follow a rule or custom, to celebrate a holiday or ritual, to make a remark.|例句：Scientists observed the behavior of the animals. 科学家观察了动物的行为。|近义词：watch, monitor, comply with, celebrate, comment|反义词：ignore, disregard, violate, neglect|verb

observe:/əbˈzɜːrv/ (US) /əbˈzɜːv/ (UK)| n. |观察；遵守；庆祝；评论 (较少用作名词)|An act of observing.  (Less common usage)|例句：The astronomer made a careful observe of the comet. 天文学家对彗星进行了仔细的观察. (用法较不常见)|近义词：observation, observance|反义词：neglect, ignorance|noun
