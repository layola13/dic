belles-lettres:/ˌbɛl ˈlɛtrə/ | n. | 纯文学；文学作品 |  Literature regarded as a fine art, especially as having a purely aesthetic function.  Refers to literary works valued for their artistic merit and style rather than their informative or practical content. |  He devoted his life to the study of belles-lettres. 他一生致力于纯文学研究。 | synonyms: literature,  literary works, writings,  letters,  fiction, creative writing | antonyms: technical writing, scientific literature | noun
