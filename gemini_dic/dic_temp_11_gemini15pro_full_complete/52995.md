dinner-dance: /ˈdɪnər dɑːns/ | n. | 晚宴舞会；餐舞会 | A formal social occasion that combines dinner with dancing. |  We're going to a dinner-dance on Saturday. 我们周六要去参加一个晚宴舞会。| The company held its annual dinner-dance at a luxury hotel. 公司在一家豪华酒店举行了年度晚宴舞会。 | 近义词: ball, formal, party | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
dinner (from Old French disner, meaning "break one's fast") + dance (from Old French dancier)
