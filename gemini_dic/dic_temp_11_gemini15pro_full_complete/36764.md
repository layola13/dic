collude:/kəˈluːd/
verb
串通，共谋，勾结
To conspire or plot secretly, especially for an illegal or deceitful purpose.
The company's executives colluded to fix prices. 公司高管串通操纵价格。
Several officials were accused of colluding with the enemy. 几名官员被指控与敌人勾结。
Synonyms: conspire, plot, scheme, intrigue
Antonyms: cooperate (in a legal and ethical way), oppose, resist
词性: 动词
