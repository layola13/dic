jingoism:/ˈdʒɪŋɡoʊɪzəm/
n.
侵略主义；沙文主义；强硬外交政策
Extreme patriotism, especially in the form of aggressive or warlike foreign policy.
The government was accused of jingoism after it declared war on its smaller neighbor. 政府在向其较小的邻国宣战后被指责为侵略主义。
jingoism:/ˈdʒɪŋɡoʊɪzəm/
n.
侵略主义；沙文主义；强硬外交政策
The rising tide of jingoism in the country worried many international observers.  该国日益高涨的沙文主义让许多国际观察员感到担忧。
近义词:chauvinism, nationalism, warmongering
反义词:pacifism, internationalism
词性:noun

jingoisms:/ˈdʒɪŋɡoʊɪzəmz/
n.
侵略主义；沙文主义；强硬外交政策（jingoism的复数形式）
Various acts or expressions of extreme patriotism, especially in the form of aggressive or warlike foreign policy.
His speeches were filled with jingoisms, calling for the nation to assert its dominance. 他的演讲充满了沙文主义，呼吁国家维护其统治地位。
jingoisms:/ˈdʒɪŋɡoʊɪzəmz/
n.
侵略主义；沙文主义；强硬外交政策（jingoism的复数形式）
The newspaper was criticized for publishing a series of jingoisms that inflamed tensions with neighboring countries.  这家报纸因发表一系列煽动与邻国紧张关系的沙文主义言论而受到批评。
近义词:chauvinisms, nationalisms
反义词: (No direct plural antonym, consider using "acts of pacifism" or similar)
词性:noun


词源: Late 19th century: from the refrain 'by Jingo' in a popular music-hall song of 1878.
