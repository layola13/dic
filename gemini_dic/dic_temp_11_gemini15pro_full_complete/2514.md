administrative:/ədˈmɪnɪstreɪtɪv/
adjective
管理的；行政的
relating to the management of a company, organization, or institution.
例句：He has an administrative role in the company. 他在公司担任管理职务。

例句：She has excellent administrative skills. 她拥有优秀的管理技能。

近义词：managerial, executive, organizational, bureaucratic
反义词：non-administrative, operational

词性: adjective
