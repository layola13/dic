blindfish:/ˈblaɪndˌfɪʃ/| n. |盲鱼|A fish that lacks eyes and pigment, typically living in caves or underground waters.  It has adapted to its dark environment and relies on other senses like touch and smell to navigate and find food.|例句：The blindfish navigated the dark cave with ease. 盲鱼轻松地在黑暗的洞穴中穿行。

例句：Blindfish are fascinating examples of adaptation to extreme environments. 盲鱼是适应极端环境的迷人例子。|近义词：cavefish|反义词:sighted fish|词性:noun
