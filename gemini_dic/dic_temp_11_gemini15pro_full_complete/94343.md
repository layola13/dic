impingent:/ɪmˈpɪndʒənt/
adjective
迫切的；紧急的；即将发生的；撞击的
Having an effect or impact, especially a negative one; about to happen.
例句：The impingent deadline loomed over them.  迫在眉睫的截止日期笼罩着他们。

例句：The impingent sound of the siren startled everyone.  警笛刺耳的声音吓了所有人一跳。

近义词：urgent, pressing, imminent, forthcoming
反义词：distant, remote, unimportant, insignificant
词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin impingent- ‘striking, pushing against’, from the verb impingere, from in- ‘into’ + pangere ‘to fix’.
