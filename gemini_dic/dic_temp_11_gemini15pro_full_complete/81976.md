grindery: /ˈɡraɪndəri/ (US), /ˈɡraɪndəri/ (UK)| n. | 磨坊，研磨工场; 磨具; 研磨材料; |A place where grinding is done or materials for grinding are made or sold.  A workshop where edged tools or other objects are sharpened or ground. | The old grindery was powered by a water wheel. 这家老磨坊由水车提供动力。|  The knife maker sourced his grindery from a specialist supplier. 刀匠从专业供应商那里采购研磨材料。 | synonyms: mill, workshop, factory | antonyms:  (No direct antonym.  Could consider "assembly plant" as a contrasting concept if referring to the creation rather than the reduction of materials) | noun
