ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *yiven, from Old English *ġiefan, ġefan (“to give”), from Proto-Germanic *gebaną (“to give”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰebʰ- (“to give, take”). Cognate with Scots gien (“given”), West Frisian jaan (“to give”), Dutch geven (“to give”), German geben (“to give”), Danish give (“to give”), Swedish ge (“to give”), Icelandic gefa (“to give”).
