overdrawn: /ˌoʊvərˈdrɔːn/ (American) /ˌəʊvəˈdrɔːn/ (British)| adj.|透支的；(喻)疲惫不堪的|Having taken more money out of a bank account than the account contains; (figuratively) exhausted, depleted.|例句：My account is overdrawn. 我的账户透支了。|近义词：overspent, in the red, depleted, exhausted|反义词：in credit, solvent, affluent, energized|词性:形容词

overdrawn: /ˌoʊvərˈdrɔːn/ (American) /ˌəʊvəˈdrɔːn/ (British)| n.|透支|The amount of money that has been taken out of a bank account in excess of the balance.|例句：The overdrawn amounted to $200. 透支金额为200美元。|近义词：deficit, shortfall|反义词：surplus, credit|词性:名词
