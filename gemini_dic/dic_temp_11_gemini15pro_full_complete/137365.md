nontelegraphic:/ˌnɒnˌtɛlɪˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
非电报式的
Not resembling or characteristic of a telegram; not terse or abbreviated.  Expressed in a full and natural way, rather than in short phrases.
The letter was long and nontelegraphic, providing a detailed account of his journey. 这封信很长，不像电报，详细地描述了他的旅程。
Her writing style is fluid and nontelegraphic, making it easy to read. 她的写作风格流畅自然，不像电报，很容易读。
Synonyms: verbose, prolix, lengthy, detailed, expansive
Antonyms: telegraphic, terse, concise, succinct, brief
词性: 形容词
