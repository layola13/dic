epigraphics:/ˌepɪˈɡræfɪks/
n.
题铭学; 碑铭学
The study of inscriptions, especially as evidence of language and history.
The professor of epigraphics lectured on ancient Roman inscriptions.  题铭学教授讲授了古罗马的铭文。
近义词:inscription, paleography
反义词: (None, as it's a specialized field of study)
词性:noun

epigraphic:/ˌepɪˈɡræfɪk/
adj.
题铭的; 碑铭的
Relating to epigraphics or inscriptions.
The museum houses a collection of epigraphic stones from the Roman era.  博物馆收藏了一批罗马时代的题铭石。
近义词:inscribed, engraved
反义词: (None, it describes a characteristic)
词性:adjective
