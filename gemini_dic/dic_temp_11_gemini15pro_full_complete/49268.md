deliber:/dɪˈlɪbər/| v. |仔细考虑，商议|To think about or discuss something very carefully, especially before making a decision.|The jury deliberated for two days before reaching a verdict. 陪审团商议了两天，才作出裁决。|近义词：consider, ponder, contemplate, discuss|反义词：rush, ignore|动词

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərət/| adj. |故意的，深思熟虑的|Done or said on purpose rather than by accident.|The attack was a deliberate attempt to destabilize the government. 这次袭击是蓄意破坏政府稳定的企图。|近义词：intentional, planned, conscious|反义词：accidental, unintentional, impulsive|形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin  dēlīberāre (“to weigh in one's mind, consider, deliberate”),  from  dē-  (“from, down from, out of”) +  lībra  (“scales, balance”).
