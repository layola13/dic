ecbasis:/ˈɛkbəsɪs/
noun
外出；出神；昏厥
A state of being out of oneself; a trance.
She fell into an ecbasis after the shock. 她受到震惊后昏厥了。
近义词: trance, stupor, coma
反义词: consciousness, alertness
词性: n.


Etymology: From Greek ekbasis "a going out," from ekbainein "to go out," from ek "out" + bainein "to go."
