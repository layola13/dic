nonsalutary:/nɒnˈsæljʊtəri/
adjective
无益的，不健康的
Not conducive to health or well-being; harmful.
The nonsalutary effects of smoking are well documented. 吸烟的有害影响已被充分证明。
synonyms: unhealthy, harmful, detrimental, injurious, deleterious, noxious, baneful, pernicious
antonyms: salutary, beneficial, healthy, wholesome
词性: 形容词
