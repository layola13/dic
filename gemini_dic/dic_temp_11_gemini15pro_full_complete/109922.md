libellist: /ˈlaɪbələst/ | n. |诽谤者；造谣者| A person who publishes or writes a libel.  A libel is a published false statement that is damaging to a person's reputation. | He was accused of being a libellist after the article was published. 他在文章发表后被指控为诽谤者。|  The libellist spread rumors about his political rival. 这个造谣者散布关于他政治对手的谣言。 | synonym: slanderer, defamer, calumniator | antonym:  apologist, defender | noun
