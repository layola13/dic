imperviousness:/ɪmˈpɜːrviəsnəs/| n. |不透水性；无动于衷；不受影响|the state of not allowing fluid to pass through.  Also, not affected or hurt by; not allowing something to enter or pass through. |The imperviousness of the rock made it ideal for building a dam. 这块岩石的不透水性使其成为建造水坝的理想材料。|  His imperviousness to criticism was astonishing. 他对批评的无动于衷令人惊讶。 |synonyms: impermeability, impenetrability, resistance, insensitivity, indifference |antonyms: permeability, penetrability, sensitivity, vulnerability| noun
