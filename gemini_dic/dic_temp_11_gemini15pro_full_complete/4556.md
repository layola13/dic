alchemister:/ˈælkəmɪstər/
n.
炼金术士
An alchemist, especially in archaic usage.  A person who practices alchemy.
The alchemister claimed he could turn lead into gold.  这位炼金术士声称他可以把铅变成金子。
近义词: alchemist, sorcerer, magician
反义词: scientist
词性: 名词
