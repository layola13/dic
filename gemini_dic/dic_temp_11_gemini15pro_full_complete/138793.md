numismatists:/ˌnjuːmɪzˈmætɪsts/
n.
钱币收藏家；钱币学家
A person who studies or collects coins, medals, paper money, etc.
My grandfather was a keen numismatist and had a vast collection of ancient Roman coins. 我的祖父是一位热衷的钱币收藏家，收藏了大量的古罗马硬币。
The numismatists authenticated the rare coin as genuine. 钱币学家鉴定这枚稀有硬币为真品。
synonyms: coin collector, numismatologist
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who disregards or destroys currency)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *numismate*, from Latin *numisma* (“coin”), from Ancient Greek *νόμισμα* (*nómisma*, “current coin, usage, custom, law”), from *νομίζω* (*nomízō*, “I hold as customary, use customarily, practice, observe a custom, hold by custom, hold as valid, deem, think, consider, believe, suppose, imagine”).
