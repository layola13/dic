neanderthal: /niˈændərˌθɔːl/ (US), /niˈændərtɑːl/ (UK)| n. adj.|尼安德特人；穴居人；粗鲁的人|An extinct species of human that lived in Europe and parts of western and central Asia.  Figuratively, it refers to someone who is perceived as primitive, unintelligent, or brutish. |例句：Neanderthals lived in Europe during the Ice Age. 尼安德特人生活在冰河时代的欧洲。

例句：He called him a Neanderthal for his outdated views. 他因为他的过时观点称他为尼安德特人。|近义词：caveman, primitive, brute|反义词：modern human, sophisticated person|词性: 名词，形容词
