overcast: /ˌoʊvərˈkæst/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈkɑːst/ (UK)| adj.|阴天的；多云的|Covered with clouds; dull. |例句：The sky was overcast, threatening rain. 天空阴沉，似有雨意。|例句：It was a cold, overcast day. 那是一个寒冷阴沉的日子。|近义词：cloudy, dull, gray|反义词：sunny, clear, bright|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *overcasten, from over- + casten (“to cast”).  Originally meant "overthrown, defeated". The meteorological sense developed from the notion of the sky being "covered over" with clouds.
