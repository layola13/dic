glasslike:/ˈɡlɑːslaɪk/ | adj. | 像玻璃的；光滑的；透明的 | Resembling glass in smoothness, transparency, or brittleness. | The lake was glasslike, reflecting the surrounding mountains.  湖面像玻璃一样，倒映着周围的山峦。| Synonyms: glassy, vitreous, transparent, smooth, polished | Antonyms: opaque, rough, dull | adjective

glasslike:/ˈɡlɑːslaɪk/ | adj. | 像玻璃的；光滑的；透明的 |  Having the smooth, shiny, or transparent qualities of glass. |  Her skin had a glasslike quality, almost translucent. 她的皮肤像玻璃一样，几乎是半透明的。 | Synonyms: glassy, vitreous, crystal-like, pellucid | Antonyms: opaque, rough, dull | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From glass + -like.  Glass comes from Old English *glæs, from Proto-Germanic *glasam.  -like is a suffix meaning "resembling," from Old English -līc.
