half-radically:/ˌhæf ˈrædɪkli/ | adv. | 半彻底地，不完全彻底地 |  Partially or incompletely radical;  embracing some radical principles or methods but not all.  It describes an approach that adopts some aspects of radical change while retaining some traditional elements. | The government implemented the reforms half-radically, addressing some key issues but leaving others untouched. 政府半彻底地实施了改革，解决了一些关键问题，但其他问题仍未得到解决。|  partially, incompletely, moderately | thoroughly, completely, fully | adverb
