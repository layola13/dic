indeterminable:/ˌɪndɪˈtɜːrmɪnəbl/ | adj. | 不可确定的，无法断定的 | Not able to be determined or established; impossible to discover or ascertain. | The age of the artifact is indeterminable. 这件文物的年代无法确定。|  The cause of the fire remains indeterminable. 火灾的原因仍然无法确定。|近义词: unascertainable, indefinable, unknown, unclear | 反义词: determinable, ascertainable, knowable, clear | 词性: 形容词
