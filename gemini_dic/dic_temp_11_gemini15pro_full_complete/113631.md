machiguenga:/ˌmɑːtʃiˈɡɛŋɡə/
| n. |马基延加人|A member of an indigenous people of southeastern Peru.  They are known for their traditional hunter-gatherer lifestyle and unique cultural practices.|The machiguenga maintain a close relationship with the rainforest. 马基延加人与雨林保持着密切的关系。|Synonyms: Matsigenka (alternative spelling)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

| adj. |马基延加人的|Relating to the Machiguenga people or their language. |Machiguenga art often depicts animals and spirits of the rainforest. 马基延加艺术经常描绘雨林中的动物和神灵。|Synonyms: Matsigenkan (alternative spelling)|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
