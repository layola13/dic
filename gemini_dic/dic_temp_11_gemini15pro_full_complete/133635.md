nonforfeiting:/ˌnɑːnfɔːrˈfɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不可丧失的，(保险)不失效的 |  Guaranteeing the continuation of benefits or rights even if payments lapse or certain conditions are not met.  Typically used in the context of insurance or pension plans. | Example: He had a nonforfeiting life insurance policy, so even if he missed a payment, he wouldn't lose all his accumulated value. 他有一份不可丧失的寿险保单，即使他错过付款，也不会失去所有累计价值。

Example: Nonforfeiting clauses protect the policyholder's investment. 不丧失条款保护投保人的投资。|  Synonyms: inalienable, unlosable, guaranteed, vested | Antonyms: forfeitable, revocable, conditional | word property: adjective
