allantoid: /əˈlæntɔɪd/ | n. adj. | 尿囊，尿囊的 |  A vascular fetal membrane of reptiles, birds, and mammals that develops as a pouch or sac from the hindgut and functions as part of the embryo's circulatory system, in respiration, and/or in storing excretory products.  In placental mammals, the allantois contributes to the formation of the umbilical cord and placenta. |  The allantoid plays a crucial role in the development of the embryo. 尿囊在胚胎发育中起着至关重要的作用。 The allantoid artery carries oxygenated blood to the fetus. 尿囊动脉将含氧血液输送到胎儿。| 近义词:  chorion (绒毛膜, in the context of embryonic membranes), fetal membrane (胎膜) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun, adjective

allantoic: /ˌælənˈtɔɪɪk/ | adj. | 尿囊的 | Relating to or denoting the allantois. | The allantoic fluid provides cushioning for the developing embryo. 尿囊液为发育中的胚胎提供缓冲。 Allantoic cysts can sometimes develop during pregnancy. 怀孕期间有时会出现尿囊囊肿。| 近义词: None | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
