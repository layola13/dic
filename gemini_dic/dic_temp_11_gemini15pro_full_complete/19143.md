bevies:/ˈbɛviz/| n. |一群，一队（尤指女性或鸟类）|A large group, especially of women or birds.  It suggests a lively and often attractive group. |例句：A bevy of beauties graced the ballroom. 一群美女为舞厅增添了光彩。|例句：A bevy of quail burst from the undergrowth. 一群鹌鹑从灌木丛中飞出。|近义词：group, flock, company, collection, gathering|反义词:few, individual|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "bevee," meaning a drink, especially one shared by a group of hunters.  The sense shifted from a drinking group to the group itself, and later specifically to a group of women or birds.
