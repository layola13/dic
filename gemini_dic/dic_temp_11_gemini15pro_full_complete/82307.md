grouse:/ɡraʊs/| v. n.|抱怨，牢骚；松鸡|To complain or grumble about something.|例句：He's always grousing about the weather. 他总是抱怨天气。|近义词：complain, grumble, moan, whine|反义词：praise, commend|词性:动词


grouse:/ɡraʊs/| n.|松鸡|A type of game bird found in upland areas.|例句：We saw a grouse in the woods. 我们在树林里看到了一只松鸡。|近义词：ptarmigan, prairie chicken|反义词:|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *grouse, of unknown origin.  Possibly imitative of the bird's call.
