nooklike:/ˈnʊklaɪk/
adjective
类似角落的; 隐蔽的
Resembling or suggestive of a nook; secluded or cozy.
The attic room, with its sloping ceiling and small window, felt nooklike and inviting. 阁楼房间，有着倾斜的天花板和小窗户，感觉像个角落一样，很吸引人。
The cafe had a nooklike area with comfortable armchairs, perfect for reading. 这家咖啡馆有一个类似角落的区域，配有舒适的扶手椅，非常适合阅读。
Synonyms: cozy, secluded, snug, intimate, sheltered
Antonyms: exposed, open, spacious, public
词性: 形容词
