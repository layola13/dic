knappy:/ˈnæpi/| adj.|尿布的；起绒的；蓬松的；有小疙瘩的|Covered with or resembling small nubs or knots, like a diaper's texture, or having a fluffy, raised texture.| The baby's knappy was soaked. 婴儿的尿布湿透了. | The blanket had a soft, knappy texture.  这毯子质地柔软蓬松。| 近义词: fluffy, nubby, diapered|反义词: smooth, flat|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *knoppy, from Old English *cnoppa (“knob, button”), from Proto-Germanic *knuppaz (“knob, button”). Cognate with Scots knappy (“knobby, lumpy”), German Knopf (“button, knob”). The sense of "diapered" arose from the nubby texture of some diapers.
