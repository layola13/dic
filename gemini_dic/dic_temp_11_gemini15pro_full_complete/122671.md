modulating:/ˈmɒdjʊleɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdʒəleɪtɪŋ/ (US)| v. | 调制；调节；调整；变调 |  Varying the strength, tone, or pitch of one's voice. Changing some aspect of a wave or signal, typically to encode information. |  Example: The singer was modulating her voice to express a range of emotions. 歌手正在调整她的声音来表达一系列的情感。 | Synonyms: adjusting, regulating, varying, adapting | Antonyms: fixing, maintaining, stabilizing | Verb

modulating:/ˈmɒdjʊleɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdʒəleɪtɪŋ/ (US)| adj. | 调制的；调节的 |  Used to describe something that modulates or is modulated. | Example: The modulating signal controlled the amplitude of the carrier wave. 调制信号控制着载波的振幅。 | Synonyms: adjusting, regulating, varying, adapting | Antonyms: fixed, constant, unchanging | Adjective
