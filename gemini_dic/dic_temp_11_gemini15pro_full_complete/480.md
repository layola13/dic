abnodate:/ˈæbnəˌdeɪt/| v. |放弃；取消；正式宣布无效|To formally renounce or relinquish a right, claim, or power; to abolish by authoritative decree.| The king abnodated his claim to the throne. 国王放弃了王位继承权。|近义词：renounce, relinquish, abdicate, resign, revoke, repeal, annul, nullify, cancel, abolish|反义词：affirm, assert, maintain, uphold, ratify, confirm|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *abnodare, from Latin ab- + nodare “to tie in a knot,” from nodus “knot.”  The sense development likely involved the idea of untying a legal knot or obligation.
