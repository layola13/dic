outwanderers:/ˌaʊtˈwɑːndərərz/
noun
流浪者；漂泊者；离家远游的人
People who wander far from home or their usual surroundings.
The outwanderers sought new lands beyond the horizon.  流浪者在地平线之外寻找新的土地。
The group of outwanderers finally settled in a fertile valley.  这群漂泊者最终定居在一个肥沃的山谷里。
synonyms: nomads, wanderers, travelers, rovers, explorers
antonyms: settlers, residents, stay-at-homes
n.
