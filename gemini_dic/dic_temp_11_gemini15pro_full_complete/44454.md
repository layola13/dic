cross-the-line:/ˌkrɒs ðə ˈlaɪn/ | v. | 越界；过分 | To go beyond the acceptable limit of behavior or morality. | He crossed the line when he started insulting his colleagues. 他开始侮辱同事时就过分了。|  The comedian crossed the line with several jokes that were offensive to the audience.  这位喜剧演员的几个笑话冒犯了观众，太过火了。 | 近义词：overstep, go too far, exceed the limit | 反义词: behave appropriately, stay within bounds | 词性: 动词
