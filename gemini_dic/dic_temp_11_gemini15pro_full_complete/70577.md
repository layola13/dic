fillibeg:/ˈfɪlɪˌbeɡ/| n. |短裙；菲力贝格裙（苏格兰高地男子传统服饰的一部分）|A kilt, especially a kilt worn without trews (trousers).|The Scotsman proudly wore his fillibeg at the Highland games. 苏格兰人自豪地在高地运动会上穿着他的菲力贝格裙。|近义词：kilt, plaid|反义词:trousers, pants|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Scottish Gaelic *feileadh beag*  "little kilt," from *feileadh* "kilt, covering, cloth" + *beag* "little."  Originally referring to the smaller, single piece of cloth worn as a kilt, as opposed to the *feileadh mòr* ("big kilt" or belted plaid) which was a larger piece of cloth also used as a cloak.
