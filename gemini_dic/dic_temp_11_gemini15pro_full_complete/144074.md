outrow:/ˈaʊtroʊ/
verb
胜过（划船）
to row faster than
例句：The Cambridge crew outrowed Oxford by three lengths.  剑桥队以三个船身的优势胜过牛津队。

例句：They easily outrowed their competitors in the final race. 他们在决赛中轻松地胜过了对手。

近义词：outpace, surpass, beat, defeat
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：v.


outrow:/ˈaʊtroʊ/
noun (rare)
胜过（划船）的行为或实例
the act or instance of rowing faster than
例句：The outrow by the Cambridge team was decisive.  剑桥队的胜出是决定性的。

例句：Their outrow secured them the championship title. 他们的胜出确保了他们的冠军头衔。

近义词：victory, triumph
反义词：defeat, loss
