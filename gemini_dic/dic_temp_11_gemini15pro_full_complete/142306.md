orthodontically:/ˌɔːrθəˈdɑːntɪkli/ | adv. | 正畸地，牙齿矫正地 | In a way that relates to orthodontics, the branch of dentistry concerned with the correction of irregularities of the teeth. | Her teeth were orthodontically corrected when she was a teenager. 她十几岁时就做了牙齿矫正。| 近义词: dentally | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From orthodontics + -ally.  Orthodontics comes from the Greek *orthos* (straight, correct) + *odous* (tooth).
