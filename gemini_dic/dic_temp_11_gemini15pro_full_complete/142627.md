osteotomy: /ˌɒstiˈɒtəmi/ (US) /ˌɒstiˈɒtəmi/ (UK)| n.| 骨切开术|The surgical cutting of a bone.  It's often done to correct a deformity or to help a bone heal properly.|The doctor performed an osteotomy to realign the patient's fractured femur. 医生进行了骨切开术以重新调整病人骨折的股骨。|Synonyms: bone cutting,  bone section|Antonyms: bone fusion, bone grafting|noun
