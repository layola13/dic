magnesia:/mæɡˈniːʒə/ /mæɡˈniːziə/
n.
氧化镁; 轻质氧化镁; 重质氧化镁; 镁乳
A white, slightly alkaline, earthy substance, magnesium oxide, MgO, used in medicine as an antacid and laxative, and in industry in refractories, cements, insulation, and fertilizers.
例句：Magnesia is often used as an antacid. 氧化镁常被用作抗酸剂。

例句：Magnesia is a key ingredient in some types of cement.  氧化镁是某些类型水泥的关键成分。

近义词：magnesium oxide, periclase (矿物学)
反义词:  (无直接反义词，可根据具体语境寻找化学性质相反的物质)
词性: noun


magnesias:/mæɡˈniːʒəz/ /mæɡˈniːziəz/
n.
氧化镁（复数）; 轻质氧化镁（复数）; 重质氧化镁（复数）; 镁乳（复数）
Plural form of magnesia.  Refers to multiple types or instances of magnesium oxide.
例句：The different magnesias used in this experiment had varying densities.  这个实验中使用的不同种类的氧化镁密度各不相同。

例句：These magnesias are all suitable for industrial applications. 这些氧化镁都适用于工业用途。

近义词：magnesium oxides
反义词: (无直接反义词，可根据具体语境寻找化学性质相反的物质)

词源: From Modern Latin *magnesia alba (“white magnesia”), from Latin Magnesia (ad Sipylum), a region in ancient Lydia (modern Turkey) where magnesium carbonate was found.
