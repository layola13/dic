bordel: /bɔʁ.dɛl/ (French pronunciation)| n. |妓院; 乱糟糟的地方|A brothel; a place of disorder or chaos.|例句：The police raided the bordel and arrested several people. 警察突袭了妓院，逮捕了几个人。

例句：His apartment was a complete bordel; clothes were strewn everywhere. 他的公寓乱七八糟，衣服到处都是。|近义词：brothel, whorehouse (for the primary meaning); mess, shambles (for the secondary meaning)|反义词:order, tidiness|词性:noun

词源: From Old French *bordel, from Old Italian *bordello "small hut," diminutive of borda "hut, plank," of Germanic origin.
