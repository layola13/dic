fixednesses:/ˈfɪksɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
固定性；僵化；刻板
The state of being fixed or unchanging; inflexibility.
His fixedness on the issue prevented any compromise. 他对这个问题的固执阻碍了任何妥协。
近义词: rigidity, inflexibility, stubbornness, obstinacy
反义词: flexibility, adaptability, open-mindedness
词性: Noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From fixedness, from fixed + -ness.  "Fixed" comes from the Old French "fixe," from Latin "fixus," past participle of "figere" (to fix, fasten).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
