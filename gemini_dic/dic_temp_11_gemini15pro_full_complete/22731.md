born-again:/ˌbɔːrn əˈɡɛn/ | adj. |重生的，获得新生的| Having experienced a spiritual rebirth or renewal, often referring to a renewed commitment to Christianity. | He is a born-again Christian. 他是一位重生基督徒。| synonyms: renewed, converted | antonyms: unconverted, unregenerate | adjective
