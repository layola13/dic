inter-chains:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪnz/| adj.|链间的，跨链的|Existing or occurring between different blockchains.  Referring to interaction, communication, or transfer of value between separate and independent blockchain networks.|例句：Inter-chains technology allows for seamless transfer of assets between different blockchains.  跨链技术允许在不同区块链之间无缝转移资产。

例句：The inter-chains communication protocol enables these blockchains to work together.  跨链通信协议使这些区块链能够协同工作。|近义词：cross-chain|反义词:intra-chain|词性:形容词
