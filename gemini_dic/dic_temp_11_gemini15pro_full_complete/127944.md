neophyte:/ˈniːəˌfaɪt/ | n. | 新手，初学者；新皈依者 | A person who is new to a subject, skill, or belief. |  She's a neophyte in the world of finance. 她是一位金融界的新手。

The neophyte painter was eager to learn from the master. 这位新手画家渴望向大师学习。


| 近义词: beginner, novice, tyro, rookie, newcomer | 反义词: expert, veteran, professional, master | 词性: noun
