This word is often considered informal or dialectal. | He strutted around with a cockyolly air, boasting about his latest achievement.  他趾高气扬地走来走去，吹嘘着他最近的成就。| Near Synonyms: arrogant, conceited, cocky, presumptuous, swaggering | Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming | Adjective
