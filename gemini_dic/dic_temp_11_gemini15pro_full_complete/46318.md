dabbing:/ˈdæbɪŋ/ | n. | 轻拍，轻涂; (舞蹈动作)点舞 | The act of applying something lightly, or a dance move involving dropping the head with one arm extended out and the other bent inwards. | She applied the ointment with gentle dabbing motions. 她用轻拍的动作涂抹药膏。

He celebrated his touchdown with some enthusiastic dabbing. 他用充满激情的点舞庆祝他的触地得分。 | 近义词：patting, tapping, blotting (for application);  hitting the dab (for dance) | 反义词: smearing, rubbing (for application) | 词性: noun


dabbing:/ˈdæbɪŋ/ | v. | 轻拍，轻涂 | Present participle of "to dab". |  She was dabbing her eyes with a tissue. 她正在用纸巾轻拍她的眼睛。 | 近义词：patting, tapping, blotting | 反义词: smearing, rubbing | 词性: verb (present participle)


词源: From the verb "dab," which likely originated as an imitative word suggesting a light, quick touch.
