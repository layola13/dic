desertrix: /dɪˈzɜːtrɪks/
n.
female deserter; a woman who deserts
A woman who leaves her post or duty without permission.
She was branded a desertrix when she abandoned her military post.  她放弃了她的军事岗位，被打上了逃兵的烙印。
The queen, fearing for her life, became a desertrix and fled the castle.  女王担心自己的生命安全，成了逃兵，逃离了城堡。
Synonyms: runaway, fugitive, absconder
Antonyms: loyalist, adherent, stalwart
noun
