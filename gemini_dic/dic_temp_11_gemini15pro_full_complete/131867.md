nonconsumption:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. | 不消费，非消费 |  The act of not consuming, especially as a conscious choice.  It can refer to abstaining from purchasing or using goods and services, often for ethical, environmental, or economic reasons. |  Example 1:  Nonconsumption is becoming a popular trend among millennials concerned about overconsumption and its impact on the planet. 千禧一代越来越关注过度消费及其对地球的影响，不消费正在成为一种流行趋势。

Example 2: Her nonconsumption of animal products is part of her vegan lifestyle. 她不食用动物产品是她纯素生活方式的一部分。 | Near Synonyms: abstention, avoidance, boycotting | Near Antonyms: consumption, purchasing, using | noun
