cosily:/ˈkəʊzɪli/ (UK) /ˈkoʊzɪli/ (US)| adv.|舒适地，惬意地|In a cosy manner; snugly and comfortably.|We sat cosily by the fire. 我们舒适地坐在火炉旁。|The cat curled up cosily in its basket.  猫舒服地蜷缩在篮子里。|近义词：snugly, comfortably, warmly|反义词：uncomfortably, coldly|词性:副词

词源: From cosy + -ly.  Cosy (meaning comfortable and warm) has roots in Scottish and Northern English dialects. Ultimately related to Middle Low German kūse (meaning "choice, dainty").
