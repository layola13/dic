nongraphitic:/ˌnɒnɡrəˈfɪtɪk/| adj.|非石墨的|Not having the properties of graphite; not consisting of or resembling graphite.  This term is typically used in materials science to describe carbon materials that lack the layered, crystalline structure of graphite.|The electrode material was a nongraphitic form of carbon.|近义词：amorphous (when referring to carbon), noncrystalline|反义词：graphitic|词性:形容词
