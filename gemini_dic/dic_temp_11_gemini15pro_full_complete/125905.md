musculosity:/ˌmʌskjʊˈlɒsɪti/ | n. |肌肉发达，强壮 |The state or quality of being muscular. | He admired the musculosity of the weightlifter. 他很羡慕举重运动员发达的肌肉。 |  His musculosity was evident beneath his tight shirt.  他紧身衬衫下的肌肉很明显。| synonyms: muscularity, brawn, strength | antonyms: weakness, frailty | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *musculositas, from Latin musculosus (“muscular”) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  musculosus is derived from musculus (“muscle”) (ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mūs (“mouse”) because of the resemblance of a flexed bicep to a mouse).
