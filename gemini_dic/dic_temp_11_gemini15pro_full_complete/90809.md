humps:/hʌmps/
n.
隆起，驼峰，堆
rounded raised parts of a surface; a camel's hump
The camel has two humps. 这只骆驼有两个驼峰。

近义词:bumps, lumps, mounds, ridges
反义词:dips, hollows, depressions
词性:名词


hump:/hʌmp/
v.
(使)隆起，弓起背部；费力搬运
to raise into a hump; to carry something heavy with difficulty
He humped the sack of potatoes onto his back. 他把一袋土豆扛到背上。

近义词:carry, heave, lug, lift
反义词:lower, drop
词性:动词
