overcompensatingly: /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːmpənˌseɪtɪŋli/
adverb
过度补偿地；过分补偿地
in a way that compensates excessively for something perceived as a deficiency or inadequacy.
He smiled overcompensatingly, trying to hide his nervousness. 他过度补偿地笑了笑，试图掩饰他的紧张。
She dressed overcompensatingly elegantly for the casual party.  她在休闲派对上穿着过分优雅，像是过度补偿。

近义词: excessively, overly, too much
反义词: insufficiently, inadequately
词性: adverb

词源:  From overcompensate + -ing + -ly.  Overcompensate comes from over- + compensate. Compensate comes from Latin compensare "to weigh together, counterbalance," from com- "together" + pensare "to weigh."
