holstein:/ˈhoʊlˌstaɪn/, /ˈhɒlˌstaɪn/ | n. | 荷斯坦牛; 荷斯坦奶牛 |A breed of large dairy cattle, black and white in colour, originating in the Netherlands, known for high milk production.  |例句：The farmer milked his Holsteins twice a day. 农民每天给他的荷斯坦奶牛挤两次奶。

|例句：Holsteins are prized for their milk yield. 荷斯坦奶牛因其产奶量高而受到重视。|近义词：Friesian (especially in British English), dairy cow |反义词: bull (male counterpart, not a direct opposite in terms of breed) |词性: noun
