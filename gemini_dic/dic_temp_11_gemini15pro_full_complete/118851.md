metaphrastical:/ˌmɛtəˈfræstɪk(ə)l/
| adj.|逐字翻译的；直译的|Characterized by literal translation, especially word-for-word translation, sometimes at the expense of idiom.|Her translation of the poem was rather metaphrastical, capturing the literal meaning but losing some of the original's poetic nuances.|近义词：literal, verbatim, word-for-word|反义词：idiomatic, free, paraphrastic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *metaphrasticus*, from Greek *metaphrastikos*, from *metaphrazein* "to translate literally," from *meta-* "over, across" + *phrazein* "to tell, declare."
