noninfallible:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfælɪbl/ | adj. | 非绝对可靠的，易犯错的 | Not incapable of making mistakes; not perfect or infallible. |  The doctor admitted that his diagnosis was noninfallible and further tests were needed. 医生承认他的诊断并非绝对可靠，需要进一步检查。|  Humans are noninfallible and prone to errors. 人类并非绝对可靠，容易犯错。 |近义词: fallible, imperfect, errant |反义词: infallible, perfect, unerring |词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
infallible (adjective): From Late Latin infallibilis, from in- "not" + fallere "to deceive, err."
