irresolutions:/ɪˌrezəˈluːʃənz/| n. |优柔寡断，犹豫不决|The state of being unable to decide; indecision.|His irresolutions about the future of the company caused anxiety among the employees.  他对公司未来的犹豫不决引起了员工们的焦虑。|近义词：indecision, hesitation, vacillation|反义词:determination, resolve, decisiveness|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *irresolutio, from Latin in- "not" + resolutus (past participle of resolvere "to loosen, dissolve, explain, decide").
