diglossic:/daɪˈɡlɑːsɪk/ /daɪˈɡlɒsɪk/
adjective
双语的，双言现象的
Characterized by diglossia, a situation in which two distinct varieties of a language are used in a community, each with its own social function.
Example: The diglossic situation in this country involves the use of both a formal written language and a more colloquial spoken dialect.  这个国家的双语现象涉及使用正式书面语和更口语化的方言。

Example:  Researchers are studying the complex diglossic relationship between the standard language and various regional dialects. 研究人员正在研究标准语言和各种区域方言之间复杂的双语关系。

近义词:bilingual, bidialectal
反义词:monolingual
词性:adjective
