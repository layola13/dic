keymaps:/ˈkiːmæps/| n. |键位映射，快捷键映射|A set of keystrokes that are programmed to perform a specific action.  It allows users to customize how their keyboard interacts with software applications.|I customized my keymaps in Vim to improve my coding efficiency. 我在Vim中自定义了我的键位映射以提高我的编码效率。|synonyms: keyboard shortcuts, hotkeys, key bindings|antonyms: N/A|noun
