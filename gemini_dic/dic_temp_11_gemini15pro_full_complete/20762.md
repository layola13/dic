blandishment:/ˈblændɪʃmənt/ | n. | 奉承；甜言蜜语；哄骗 | something that is said or done to coax or flatter someone into doing something |  He used blandishments to persuade her to join him. 他用甜言蜜语劝说她加入他。| synonyms: flattery, cajolery, coaxing, sweet talk, allurement | antonyms: criticism, reprimand, condemnation | noun
