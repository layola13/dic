ethicalism:/ˈeθɪkəlɪzəm/
n.|伦理主义，道德主义|The belief that ethical considerations should be paramount in decision-making.  相信道德考量应该在决策中至关重要。|Ethicalism is a core principle of our organization. 伦理主义是我们组织的核心原则。|近义词：moralism, principlism|反义词：immoralism, nihilism|词性:noun

Etymology:
From ethical + -ism.  来自 ethical + -ism。
