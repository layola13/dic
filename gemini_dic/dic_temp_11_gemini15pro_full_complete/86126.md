heartened:/ˈhɑːrtənd/| adj.|感到鼓舞的，振奋人心的|Made to feel more cheerful or hopeful.|The team was heartened by the coach's encouraging words. 球队受到了教练鼓舞人心的话语的鼓舞。|近义词：encouraged, cheered, uplifted, inspired|反义词：discouraged, disheartened, depressed|词性:形容词
词源: From hearten + -ed, from heart + -en. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hartōną.
