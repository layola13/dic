amphiscians: /æmˈfɪʃiənz/
| n. | 两极圈之间的居民；两边都有影子的人 | People living between the tropics, where the sun is directly overhead at noon twice a year, causing shadows to fall north and south at different times. |  The amphiscians observed the sun's path carefully to mark the changing seasons. 两极圈之间的居民仔细观察太阳的路径来标记季节的变化。 |  tropic-dwellers |  None | Noun


amphiscian: /æmˈfɪʃiən/
| adj. | 两极圈之间的；两边都有影子的 |  Relating to or inhabiting the tropics, where shadows are cast both north and south at different times of the year. | The amphiscian region experiences intense solar radiation.  两极圈之间的区域经历强烈的太阳辐射。 | tropical | polar | Adjective
