in a bad light:/ɪn ə bæd laɪt/|phrase|处于不利地位；显得不好|To present or show someone or something in a way that makes them appear unfavorable or negative. |The media presented the candidate in a bad light, focusing only on his past mistakes. 媒体对这位候选人的报道很不利，只关注他过去的错误。

The incident cast the company in a bad light.  这起事件使公司形象受损。|近义词:unfavorably, negatively, poorly|反义词:favorably, positively, well|phrase
