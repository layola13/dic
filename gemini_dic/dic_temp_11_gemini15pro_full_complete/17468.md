beardie:/ˈbɪədi/| n.|大胡子，留胡子的人，长胡子的宠物（尤指蜥蜴）|A person with a beard, especially a long one. Also, a pet, especially a lizard, with beard-like appendages. |例句：He's a real beardie, with a beard down to his chest. 他是个真正的大胡子，胡须一直垂到胸口。

例句：My beardie (lizard) loves basking under the lamp. 我的长胡子蜥蜴喜欢在灯下晒太阳。|近义词：bearded dragon (specifically for the lizard), beardy bloke|反义词: clean-shaven person|词性: noun
