jointing:/ˈdʒɔɪntɪŋ/
n.
连接，接合，接缝
The process of joining two or more pieces of material together.
The jointing of the pipes was faulty and caused a leak. 管道的连接处有问题，导致漏水。

jointing:/ˈdʒɔɪntɪŋ/
n.
(尤指肉的)切割，切分
The act of cutting meat into joints.
The butcher demonstrated the correct jointing of a chicken. 屠夫演示了正确的鸡肉切割方法。


Etymology->
From joint + -ing.  Ultimately from Latin *jungere "to join."

Synonyms->
connection, junction, coupling, articulation, seam (for n. 1)
carving, butchering (for n. 2)

Antonyms->
separation, disconnection, division (for n. 1)
