costliest:/ˈkɔːstliɪst/
adjective
最昂贵的
most expensive; having the highest price or cost
例句：The costliest item in the store was a diamond necklace.  商店里最昂贵的商品是一条钻石项链。

例句：Buying a house is often the costliest purchase a person makes. 买房子通常是一个人最昂贵的购买。

近义词：most expensive, priciest, dearest
反义词：cheapest, least expensive, most affordable
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From "costly" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Costly" comes from Middle English "costly," from "cost" (price, expense) + "-ly" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Cost" ultimately derives from Latin "constare" (to stand firm, cost).
