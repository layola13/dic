oddest:/ˈɒdɪst/ (UK) /ˈɑːdɪst/ (US)| adj.|最奇怪的，最古怪的|Superlative form of "odd."  It means most strange, unusual, or peculiar.|That's the oddest thing I've ever heard. 那是听过的最奇怪的事情。|The museum houses the oddest collection of artifacts.  这家博物馆收藏了最奇怪的文物。|Synonyms: strangest, weirdest, most peculiar, most unusual|Antonyms: most normal, most common, most usual|adjective
