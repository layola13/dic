footplate:/ˈfʊtplāt/| n.|踏板，脚踏板，（机车）司机台|A flat plate or platform for the feet to rest on, especially one on a locomotive for the driver and fireman.|The driver stood on the footplate, watching the track ahead. 司机站在司机台上，注视着前方的轨道。|Synonyms: platform, pedal, tread, footrest|Antonyms: N/A|noun

footplate:/ˈfʊtplāt/| n.|（机车）司机室|The compartment in a locomotive for the driver and fireman.|Access to the footplate is restricted to authorized personnel only. 只有授权人员才能进入司机室。|Synonyms: cab, driver's compartment|Antonyms: N/A|noun
