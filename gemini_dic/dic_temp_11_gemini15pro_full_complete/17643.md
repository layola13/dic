becharm:/bɪˈtʃɑːrm/
verb
使…陶醉；迷住；吸引
To attract and please someone greatly.
She was becharmed by his elegant manners and witty conversation. 她被他优雅的举止和诙谐的谈吐迷住了。
The prince was becharmed by Cinderella's beauty and kindness. 王子被灰姑娘的美貌和善良所吸引。
synonyms: enchant, captivate, fascinate, charm, allure
antonyms: repel, disgust, offend
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + charm.  "Charm" comes from Old French "charme" (magical incantation, song, verse), from Latin "carmen" (song, verse, incantation).
