deciduous: /dɪˈsɪdʒuəs/ /dɪˈsɪdʒəwəs/
adjective
落叶的；脱落的
Shedding foliage at the end of the growing season.  Trees that lose their leaves every year are called deciduous trees.
例句：
Deciduous trees lose their leaves in the fall.  落叶树木在秋天落叶。

The gardener planted a row of deciduous shrubs. 园丁种了一排落叶灌木。

近义词：
leaf-shedding, caducous
反义词：
evergreen, persistent

词性:
adjective
