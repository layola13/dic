overhandle:/ˌoʊvərˈhændl/| v. |过度处理，过分干预|To manage or control something too much, often to the point of causing problems.|例句：The manager overhandled the situation, causing more stress for the team. 经理对情况过度处理，给团队带来了更多压力。

例句：He overhandled the delicate antique, and it broke. 他过度摆弄那件精致的古董，结果把它弄坏了。|近义词：micromanage, interfere, meddle|反义词: neglect, ignore |词性:动词
