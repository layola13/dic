lactation:/lækˈteɪʃ(ə)n/
n.
哺乳；泌乳；哺乳期
the process of producing milk
例句：The hormone prolactin stimulates lactation.  催乳素刺激乳汁分泌。

例句：Problems with lactation can be distressing for new mothers.  哺乳问题会让新妈妈感到苦恼。

近义词：breastfeeding, nursing
反义词：weaning
词性：名词
