hymenean:/ˌhaɪmɪˈniːən/
adjective|婚姻的；婚礼的；新婚的|Of or relating to marriage or weddings; nuptial.|例句：The hymenean altar was decorated with white lilies. 婚礼祭坛装饰着白色的百合花。|例句：Hymenean bliss filled the air as the couple exchanged vows.  当这对夫妇交换誓言时，空气中充满了婚姻的幸福。|近义词：nuptial, marital, wedding, matrimonial|反义词：single, unmarried|词性: adjective
hymenean:/ˌhaɪmɪˈniːən/
noun|婚礼歌曲；结婚进行曲|A wedding song or march; a marriage ceremony.|例句：The organist played a traditional hymenean as the bride walked down the aisle. 当新娘走过红毯时，风琴手演奏了一首传统的婚礼进行曲。|例句：The ancient hymenean echoed through the halls of the castle. 古老的婚礼歌曲在城堡的大厅里回荡。|近义词：wedding song, nuptial song, bridal march|反义词：dirge, lament|词性: noun
