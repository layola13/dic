allied: /əˈlaɪd/ | adj. | 联合的，同盟的 | Joined by or relating to a treaty or agreement. |  The allied forces fought together in the war. 联合部队在战争中并肩作战。| Example: The allied nations worked together to defeat their common enemy.  同盟国共同努力打败了他们的共同敌人。 | Synonyms: united, joined, federated, confederated | Antonyms: enemy, opposed, hostile | adjective

allied: /əˈlaɪd/ | n. | 同盟者，盟友 | A person, group, or nation united with another for a common purpose. |  Several small countries were allies of the powerful nation.  几个小国是大国的盟友。| Example:  During World War II, the United States and Great Britain were allies. 在第二次世界大战期间，美国和英国是盟友。| Synonyms: partner, friend, associate, confederate | Antonyms: enemy, opponent, foe | noun
