nonresponsibilities:/ˌnɑːnrɪˌspɑːnsəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 无责任; 免责事项; 不承担的责任 |  Lack of responsibilities or things for which one is not responsible.  This word describes the state or condition of not having responsibilities. It is often used in legal or formal contexts to delineate what someone is *not* accountable for. |  The contract clearly outlines the company's nonresponsibilities in case of natural disasters.  该公司合同明确规定了在自然灾害发生时该公司不承担的责任。


The lease agreement specified the landlord's nonresponsibilities regarding pest control. 租约中明确规定了房东在虫害控制方面不承担的责任。 | 近义词: exemptions, exclusions, immunities | 反义词: responsibilities, duties, obligations | 词性: 名词
