followable:/ˈfɒləʊəbəl/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊəbəl/ (US)| adj.|可理解的，易懂的；可遵循的|Easy or possible to be followed or understood.  It describes something that is clear, logical, and not difficult to comprehend or comply with.|The instructions were clear and followable, even for a beginner.  说明书清晰易懂，即使是初学者也能理解。|Synonyms: comprehensible, understandable, accessible, clear, lucid|Antonyms: incomprehensible, confusing, obscure, unclear, convoluted|Word type: adjective

followable:/ˈfɒləʊəbəl/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊəbəl/ (US)| adj.|可追踪的，可跟踪的|Able to be tracked or followed.|His online activity is easily followable due to his frequent posts.  由于他频繁发帖，他的在线活动很容易追踪。|Synonyms: traceable, trackable|Antonyms: untraceable, untrackable|Word type: adjective
