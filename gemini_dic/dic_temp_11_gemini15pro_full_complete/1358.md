accumulating: /əˈkjuːmjəleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. v. | 积聚的，累积的 | Gradually gathering or increasing in number or quantity.  |  The snow is accumulating on the roof. 雪正在屋顶上积聚。| synonyms: gathering, amassing, collecting, increasing, piling up | antonyms: decreasing, diminishing, dwindling, lessening, reducing | Verb/Adjective

Etymology: From Latin *accumulātus*, past participle of accumulāre (“to heap up”), from ad (“to, toward”) + cumulāre (“to heap”), from cumulus (“heap”).
