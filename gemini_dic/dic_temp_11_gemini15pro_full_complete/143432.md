outfloating:/ˌaʊtˈfloʊtɪŋ/
adjective
外浮的，漂浮在外的
Floating on the surface and moving outward.  Something that is outfloating is buoyant and drifting away.
The outfloating debris from the shipwreck posed a hazard to navigation.  失事船只漂浮在外的残骸对航行造成了危险。
近义词：drifting, floating
反义词：submerged, sinking
词性：adjective

outfloating:/ˌaʊtˈfloʊtɪŋ/
verb
外浮，漂浮在外
To float on the surface and move outward.
The logs were outfloating towards the open sea.  原木漂浮到外海去了.
近义词：drift, float
反义词：sink, submerge
词性：verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'out-' (prefix indicating outward movement) + 'floating' (present participle of 'float,' meaning to rest or move on the surface of a liquid).  The word combines the idea of outward motion with the state of being afloat.
