crewmanship:/ˈkruːmənʃɪp/
n.
航海技术，船员技能
The skill and efficiency of a ship's crew.
The yacht's crewmanship was exemplary, navigating the treacherous waters with ease.  这艘游艇的船员技术堪称典范，轻松地航行在危险的水域。
近义词：seamanship, seafaring
反义词：(No direct antonym, but possibly incompetence, ineptitude)
