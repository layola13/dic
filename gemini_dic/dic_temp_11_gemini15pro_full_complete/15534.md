ETYMOLOGY->
From Yiddish *בייגל (*beygl), from Polish *bajgiel, from Middle High German *böugel (“ring, bracelet”), diminutive of bouc (“ring”). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *baugaz (“ring, something bent”). Cognate with German Bügel (“stirrup, bow”).
