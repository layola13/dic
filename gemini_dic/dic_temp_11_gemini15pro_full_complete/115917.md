maror:/mɑːˈrɔːr/
n.
bitter herbs
A bitter herb, especially horseradish, eaten at the Passover Seder.
The maror symbolizes the bitterness of slavery in Egypt.
The Seder plate included charoset, maror, and a roasted shank bone.
近义词:bitter herbs
反义词:sweet herbs
词性:noun
