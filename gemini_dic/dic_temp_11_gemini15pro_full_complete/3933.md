agritourism:/ˈæɡriˌtʊəˌrɪzəm/
n.|农业旅游|A form of tourism in which visitors stay on farms or in agricultural areas, participating in farm activities or experiencing rural life.|We went agritourism last summer and stayed at a beautiful vineyard.  我们去年夏天去了一个美丽的葡萄园进行农业旅游。|Example 2: Agritourism is becoming increasingly popular as people seek authentic travel experiences. 随着人们寻求真实的旅行体验，农业旅游越来越受欢迎。|Synonyms: farm tourism, rural tourism|Antonyms: urban tourism|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *agri-* (combining form of *agriculture*) + *tourism*.
