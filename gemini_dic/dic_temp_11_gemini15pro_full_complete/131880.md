noncontaminative:/ˌnɒnkənˈtæmɪnətɪv/ | adj. | 无污染的，不污染的 | Not contaminating; free from contamination.  Not introducing or containing substances that pollute or make something impure. |  The new manufacturing process is noncontaminative, ensuring the purity of the final product. 新的制造工艺是无污染的，确保了最终产品的纯度。|  The laboratory uses noncontaminative cleaning agents to maintain a sterile environment.  实验室使用无污染的清洁剂来维持无菌环境。| Synonyms: clean, pure, sterile, unpolluted, uncontaminated | Antonyms: contaminating, polluting, unclean, impure, contaminated | adjective
