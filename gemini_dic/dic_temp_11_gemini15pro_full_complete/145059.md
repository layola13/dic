overambitiously: /ˌoʊvəræmˈbɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 过于雄心勃勃地，野心过大地 |  In a manner that exceeds reasonable or realistic ambition; excessively ambitious. | He overambitiously promised to finish the project in a week, a deadline he clearly couldn't meet. 他过于雄心勃勃地承诺在一周内完成这个项目，这个期限他显然无法完成。| Synonym: excessively ambitiously, impractically ambitiously, unrealistically ambitiously | Antonym: modestly, realistically, cautiously | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "ambitious" (from French ambitieux, from Latin ambitiosus "going around canvassing for votes," from ambire "go around") + "-ly" (Old English -lice, from Proto-Germanic *-liko).
