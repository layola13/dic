non-viable:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈvaɪəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|不可行的，无法生存的|Not capable of working, succeeding, or developing sufficiently to be practical or sustainable.|The project was deemed non-viable due to the lack of funding.  这个项目由于缺乏资金被认为是不可行的。|Synonyms: impractical, infeasible, unsustainable, unworkable|Antonyms: viable, feasible, practical, sustainable|adjective

non-viable:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈvaɪəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|无法存活的|Not capable of living or developing normally.|The premature infant was considered non-viable.  这个早产儿被认为是无法存活的。|Synonyms:  inviable|Antonyms: viable|adjective
