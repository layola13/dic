botch:/bɒtʃ/ /batʃ/ | v. n. | 笨拙地做；拙活；补缀；失败 | To spoil something by doing it badly.  A piece of clumsy or spoiled work. |  He botched the repair job so badly that the car broke down again. 他把修理工作搞砸了，结果车子又坏了。| synonyms: bungle, mess up, ruin, spoil | antonyms: fix, repair, perfect, improve | verb

botch:/bɒtʃ/ /batʃ/ | n. | 拙活；失败 | A piece of clumsy or spoiled work; a muddle; a mess. | The whole project was a botch from start to finish.  整个项目从头到尾都是一团糟。| synonyms: mess, bungle, failure, fiasco | antonyms: success, triumph, masterpiece | noun
