overhonestly:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːnɪstli/ | adv. | 过于诚实地，过于坦率地 |  Being excessively or unnecessarily honest, often to the point of being blunt or tactless.  |  He overhonestly told her she looked terrible in that dress. 他过于坦率地告诉她，她穿那条裙子看起来很糟糕。 | 近义词：too honestly, excessively honestly, bluntly | 反义词：dishonestly, deceptively | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "honestly" (from "honest," from Old French "honeste," ultimately from Latin "honestus," meaning "honorable").
