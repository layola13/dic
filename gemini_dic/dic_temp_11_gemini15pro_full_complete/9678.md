antiterrorists:/ˌæntiˈtɛrərɪsts/| n.|反恐人员，反恐分子|People who fight against terrorism.  Those who oppose terrorism, either through active measures or ideological resistance.|The antiterrorists worked tirelessly to prevent the attack. 反恐人员不知疲倦地工作以阻止袭击。|The government deployed antiterrorists to the region. 政府向该地区部署了反恐人员。|Synonyms: counterterrorists, anti-terrorism operatives|Antonyms: terrorists|Noun
