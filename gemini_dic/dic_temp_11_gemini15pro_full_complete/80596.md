googly:/ˈɡuːɡli/ /ˈɡɑːɡli/| n.| 曲球（板球运动中的一种投球方式）|A type of delivery in cricket, bowled by a leg-spinner, but delivered with an off-spinner's action, so that the ball spins in the opposite direction to what the batsman would expect.|The batsman was completely bamboozled by the googly.  这个击球手完全被曲球迷惑了。|Synonyms: wrong 'un, bosie|Antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a specific type of bowling delivery)|noun

googly:/ˈɡuːɡli/ /ˈɡɑːɡli/| adj.| 令人惊讶的，出乎意料的（主要用于澳大利亚英语）|Surprising or unexpected (chiefly Australian English).|He gave me a googly look. 他给了我一个惊讶的眼神。|Synonyms: surprising, unexpected, astonishing|Antonyms: expected, predictable|adjective
