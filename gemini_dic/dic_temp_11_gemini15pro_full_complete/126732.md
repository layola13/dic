nanai:/ˈnɑːnaɪ/ | interj. | 再见；拜拜 (非正式，主要用于儿童) |  A childish way of saying goodbye, similar to "bye-bye".  It's often used in a playful or affectionate way. |  "Nanai, see you tomorrow!" 再见，明天见！ | Synonym: bye-bye, see you, so long | Antonym: hello, hi | Interjection


nanai:/ˈnɑːnaɪ/ | n. |  (非正式) 琐碎的事物；无价值的东西 | Trivial things; things of little or no value. (Informal) | "All that glitters is not gold; sometimes it's just nanai."  所有闪光的不都是金子；有时只是一些无价值的东西。 | Synonym:  trifles, trinkets, knick-knacks | Antonym: valuables, treasures | Noun
