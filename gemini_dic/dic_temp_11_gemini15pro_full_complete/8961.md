antihydropic:/ˌæntihaɪˈdrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 抗水肿的；利尿的 |  Relating to or used to reduce or prevent dropsy (edema, an abnormal accumulation of fluid in the body). | The doctor prescribed antihydropic medication to alleviate the swelling in her legs. 医生开了抗水肿药来缓解她腿部的肿胀。|  diuretic, anti-edema | hydropic | adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἀντί (antí, “against”) + ὕδωρ (húdōr, “water”) +‎ -ic.
