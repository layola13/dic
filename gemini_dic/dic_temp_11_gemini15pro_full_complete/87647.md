hexachlorophane:/ˌhɛksəˌklɔːrəˈfeɪn/| n. |六氯酚|An antibacterial and antifungal agent, C13H6Cl6O2, formerly widely used in soaps, deodorants, and other products but now restricted because of potential neurotoxicity.|Hexachlorophane was once a common ingredient in surgical scrubs. 六氯酚曾经是外科手术擦洗液中的常见成分。|Synonyms: G-11, hexachlorophene|Antonyms: None|noun

hexachlorophane:/ˌhɛksəˌklɔːrəˈfeɪn/| adj. |含六氯酚的|Containing or treated with hexachlorophane.|Hexachlorophane soap was once popular. 含六氯酚的肥皂曾经很流行。|Synonyms: None|Antonyms: None|adjective
