concepts:/ˈkɑːnsepts/ | n. | 概念，观念 |  A general notion or idea; an abstract understanding of something.  Derived from Latin *conceptum*, the past participle of *concipere* "to take in, conceive, become pregnant," from *com-* "together" + *capere* "to take." |  The concept of freedom is difficult to define. 自由的概念很难定义。| synonyms: idea, notion, thought, theory | antonyms:  misconception, fallacy | noun

concepts:/ˈkɑːnsepts/ | n. | 设想，构想 | A plan or intention; something conceived in the mind. |  He had a brilliant concept for a new product. 他对新产品有一个绝妙的设想。 | synonyms: plan, design, scheme, idea | antonyms:  reality, actuality | noun
