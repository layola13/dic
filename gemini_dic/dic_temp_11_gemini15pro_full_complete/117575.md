megalithic:/ˌmɛɡəˈlɪθɪk/| adj.| 巨石的，巨石建造的| Relating to or denoting prehistoric monuments made of large stones.|  Stonehenge is a megalithic structure. 巨石阵是一个巨石建筑。| The village has a megalithic tomb dating back to the Bronze Age.  这个村庄有一座青铜时代的巨石墓。| 近义词: massive, monumental | 反义词: small, tiny | 词性: 形容词 | 词源: mega (large) + lith (stone)
