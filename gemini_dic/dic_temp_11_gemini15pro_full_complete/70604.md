Derived from the Hindi word "filmī," ultimately from the English "film."|She had a very filmi reaction to the news, fainting dramatically. 她对这个消息的反应非常戏剧化，夸张地晕倒了。|The set design was quite filmi, with exaggerated colors and props.  布景设计很像电影，色彩和道具都过于夸张。|近义词：cinematic, theatrical, dramatic, affected, artificial|反义词：realistic, genuine, natural|词性: adjective
