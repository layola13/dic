friedel-crafts:/ˈfriːdəl kræfts/ | adj. | 傅-克反应的 | Relating to or denoting a type of organic reaction involving an electrophilic aromatic substitution that is catalyzed by a Lewis acid, such as aluminum chloride. | Friedel-Crafts alkylation is a common method for attaching alkyl groups to aromatic rings. 傅-克烷基化是将烷基连接到芳香环上的常用方法。

Friedel-Crafts acylation introduces an acyl group to an aromatic ring. 傅-克酰基化将酰基引入芳香环。 | 近义词: electrophilic aromatic substitution | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific reaction type) | 词性: adjective
