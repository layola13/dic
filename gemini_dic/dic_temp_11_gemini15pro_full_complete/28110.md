capitalize:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪz/ | v. | 大写；利用；使资本化 | To write or print (a letter of the alphabet) in capital form; to convert into capital; to use to one's advantage. |  He capitalized the first letter of each word in the title. 他把标题中每个单词的首字母都大写了。 | synonyms: uppercase, fund, finance | antonyms: lowercase | verb

capitalize:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪz on/ | v. | 利用；从…中获利 | To take advantage of something; to profit from something. |  She capitalized on her opponent's mistake and won the match. 她利用对手的失误赢得了比赛。 | synonyms: exploit, leverage, utilize | antonyms: neglect, ignore | verb
