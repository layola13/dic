casper:/ˈkæspər/| n.|卡斯珀，鬼马小精灵|A friendly ghost, especially in children's stories.  It's often used as a name for a ghost in general, especially a harmless one.| Casper the Friendly Ghost is a beloved cartoon character. 鬼马小精灵卡斯珀是一个备受喜爱的卡通人物。|Example: My little brother is convinced our house has a casper. 我弟弟确信我们家有一个鬼马小精灵。|Synonyms: ghost, phantom, apparition, specter|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a friendly ghost.  One could use "demon" or "evil spirit" for an opposite concept.)|noun
