adept:/əˈdɛpt/ | adj. n. | 熟练的；内行的；专家；能手 | Very skilled or proficient at something. | She is adept at playing the piano. 她很擅长弹钢琴。 |  He's adept at finding cheap flights. 他很擅长找到便宜的航班。 | synonyms: skilled, proficient, expert, masterful, accomplished | antonyms: unskilled, inept, clumsy, awkward | adjective

adept:/ˈædept/ | n. | 老手；熟练的人 | A person who is skilled or proficient at something. | He is an adept in the art of negotiation. 他是谈判高手。 | She's an adept at using social media for marketing. 她是利用社交媒体进行营销的专家。| synonyms: expert, proficient, master, specialist, authority | antonyms: novice, beginner, amateur | noun
