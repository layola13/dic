nondetermination:/ˌnɒndɪˌtɜːmɪˈneɪʃn/ |n.| 非决定性; 不确定性 |The theory of quantum mechanics introduces nondetermination into the physical world.|量子力学理论将非决定性引入物理世界。| synonyms: indeterminacy, uncertainty, unpredictability | antonyms: determinism, certainty, predictability | noun

nondeterministic:/ˌnɒndɪˌtɜːrmɪˈnɪstɪk/ |adj.| 非确定性的 |A nondeterministic algorithm may give different results on different runs, even with the same input.|即使输入相同，非确定性算法在不同的运行中也可能给出不同的结果。| synonyms: indeterministic, uncertain, unpredictable | antonyms: deterministic, certain, predictable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + determination.  "Determination" comes from Late Middle English, from Old French *determinacion (French détermination),  from Latin dēterminātiō (“a bounding, limiting, determining”), from dēterminātus, past participle of dētermināre (“to bound, limit, determine”), from dē (“from, down from, away from”) + termināre (“to limit, bound, set bounds to”), from terminus (“boundary, limit, end”).
