colemanite:/ˈkoʊlməˌnaɪt/| n. |硬硼钙石|A hydrous calcium borate mineral, Ca₂B₆O₁₁·5H₂O, occurring as white or colorless crystals and used as a source of borax.|Colemanite is an important source of boron. 硬硼钙石是重要的硼来源。|Synonym: borate, priceite|Antonym: N/A (lacks a direct opposite)|noun
