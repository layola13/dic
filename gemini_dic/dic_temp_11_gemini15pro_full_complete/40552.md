controverters:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːtəz/ | n. | 争论者，辩论者 |  People who engage in controversies or disputes; those who argue against something. | The controverters debated the issue for hours. 争论者就这个问题辩论了几个小时。| synonyms: disputants, arguers, debaters, challengers | antonyms: proponents, supporters, advocates | noun

controversy:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːsi/ | n. | 争论，争议 |  Disagreement, typically when prolonged, public, and heated. | The new policy sparked controversy among the staff. 新政策在员工中引发了争议。| synonyms: dispute, disagreement, debate, contention | antonyms: agreement, harmony, concord | noun

controvert:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːt/ | v. | 反驳，争论 | To argue against something; to oppose or deny the truth of something. |  He controverted the claims made by the prosecution. 他反驳了控方的说法。 | synonyms: dispute, challenge, oppose, deny, rebut | antonyms: confirm, support, uphold, endorse | verb
