lichenoid:/laɪˈkiːnɔɪd/
adjective
苔藓样的；类似苔藓的
Resembling a lichen in appearance, especially having flat, purplish, shiny bumps.  Characterized by or resembling lichen planus, a chronic inflammatory skin disease.
The patient presented with lichenoid eruptions on her arms and legs. 病人的手臂和腿上出现了苔藓样皮疹。
近义词:  lichenous,  papular
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, consider antonyms for the symptoms it describes, like "smooth" if referring to texture)
词性: adjective


lichenoid:/laɪˈkiːnɔɪd/
noun
苔藓样病变
A skin lesion resembling lichen planus.
The dermatologist biopsied the lichenoid to confirm the diagnosis. 皮肤科医生对苔藓样病变进行了活检以确认诊断。
近义词: lichen planus-like lesion
反义词: (No direct antonym exists)
词性: noun
