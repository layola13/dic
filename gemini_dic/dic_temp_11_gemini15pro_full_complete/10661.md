approve:/əˈpruːv/| v.| 批准，认可；赞成，同意|To officially agree to or accept something.|The board approved the merger. 董事会批准了合并。|近义词：accept, endorse, sanction, ratify|反义词：reject, disapprove, oppose|verb

approve:/əˈpruːv/| v.| 满意,喜欢|To have a favorable opinion of.|I don't approve of smoking. 我不赞成吸烟。|近义词：like, favor|反义词：dislike, disfavor|verb
