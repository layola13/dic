anthropic: /ænˈθrɒpɪk/ (UK) /ænˈθrɑːpɪk/ (US)| adj.|人类的，与人类相关的|Relating to human beings or their presence in the universe.  Influenced by human activity.|例句：The anthropic principle suggests that the universe must be compatible with the existence of observers. 人择原理表明宇宙必须与观察者的存在相容。

例句：Anthropic climate change is causing significant shifts in global weather patterns.  人为气候变化正在引起全球天气模式的重大变化。|近义词：human, anthropocentric|反义词: non-anthropic, natural|词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *ánthrōpos* (human being) + *-ikos* (suffix forming adjectives).
