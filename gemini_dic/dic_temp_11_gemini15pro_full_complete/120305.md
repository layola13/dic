minicities:/ˈmɪniˌsɪtiz/
n.
微型城市；小型城市；袖珍城市
Miniature versions of cities, often built for experimental or educational purposes, or as self-contained communities.  They can also refer to smaller cities that offer many of the amenities of larger urban areas.
Many architects are experimenting with building minicities that are sustainable and self-sufficient. 许多建筑师正在尝试建造可持续发展且自给自足的微型城市。

Minicities are popping up all over the country, offering residents a more manageable and community-focused lifestyle. 小型城市在全国各地涌现，为居民提供更易于管理且以社区为中心的生活方式。

近义词:small cities, microcities, compact cities
反义词:megacities, metropolises
词性:名词
