deistically:/ˌdeɪˈɪstɪkəli/
adv.
按照自然神论的观点
in a deistic manner; according to the belief in deism.
Deistically, the universe was created by a God who then ceased to intervene in its workings.  按照自然神论的观点，宇宙是由上帝创造的，然后上帝就不再干预它的运作了。
近义词: naturalistically
反义词: theistically
