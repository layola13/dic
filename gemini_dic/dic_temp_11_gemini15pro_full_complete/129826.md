non-hedonistic:/ˌnɒnˌhiːdənˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非享乐主义的 | Not pertaining to, characterized by, or devoted to the pursuit of pleasure; not hedonistic. |  Example:  She adopted a non-hedonistic lifestyle, focusing on spiritual growth rather than material possessions. 她过着一种非享乐主义的生活方式，专注于精神成长而不是物质财富。

Example:  The monks lived a non-hedonistic existence, dedicated to prayer and contemplation.  僧侣们过着非享乐主义的生活，致力于祈祷和沉思。 | 近义词: ascetic, self-denying, abstemious | 反义词: hedonistic, pleasure-seeking, indulgent | 词性: adjective
