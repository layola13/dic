conferrals:/kənˈfɜːrəlz/
noun
会议; 商讨; 授予; (尤指官方的)协商
Formal meetings or discussions, especially those held by people in authority.
The president held a series of conferrals with his advisors. 总统与他的顾问们举行了一系列的会议。
consultations, meetings, discussions
arguments, disagreements
n.
