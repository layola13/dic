advantaging:/ədˈvæntɪdʒɪŋ/
|v.|使…处于有利地位；有利于|Giving one player an extra turn is advantaging them.|例句1：The new policy is advantaging large corporations. 新政策对大公司有利。

例句2：This change will be advantaging those who already have wealth. 这一变化将有利于那些已经拥有财富的人。|近义词：benefiting, favoring, aiding, assisting|反义词：disadvantaging, hindering, harming|Verb (present participle)
