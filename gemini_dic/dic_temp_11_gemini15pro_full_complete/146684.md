overhear:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪr/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈhɪə(r)/ (UK)| v.|无意中听到，偶然听到|to hear (something) without the speaker's intention or knowledge| I overheard them talking about the surprise party. 我无意中听到他们在谈论惊喜派对。|

例句：
She overheard a conversation between her boss and a client.
她无意中听到老板和客户之间的谈话。

I overheard some juicy gossip while waiting in line.
我在排队时无意中听到了一些有趣的八卦。


近义词：eavesdrop, listen in|
反义词: ignore|
词性: verb
