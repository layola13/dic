delightedness:/dɪˈlaɪtɪdnəs/
n.
高兴, 快乐
The state of being delighted; great pleasure.
She expressed her delightedness at the news of her friend's engagement. 她对朋友订婚的消息表示高兴。
近义词: delight, joy, happiness, pleasure, elation
反义词: unhappiness, sorrow, sadness, displeasure
词性: 名词

词源:  From delighted + -ness.  "Delighted" comes from the verb "delight," which traces back through Middle English *deliten and Old French deleiter to the Latin delectare "to allure, charm, delight," from de- "from" + lactare "to entice, allure." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
