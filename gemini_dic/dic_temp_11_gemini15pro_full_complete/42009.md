costermonger:/ˈkɒstəˌmʌŋɡə(r)/ | n. |水果小贩；街头叫卖的小贩|A person who sells fruit and vegetables, typically from a stall or barrow in the street. |例句：The costermonger's barrow was laden with fresh produce. 水果小贩的手推车装满了新鲜的农产品。

例句：The costermonger called out his wares in a booming voice. 水果小贩大声吆喝着他的货物。|近义词：greengrocer, vendor, hawker, peddler|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 buyer, purchaser)|词性:noun
