megilph:/ˈmɛɡɪlf/ | n. | 噪音，喧闹，混乱 | A loud, confused noise; a clamor; a hubbub. | The megilph in the marketplace made it difficult to hear my friend.  市场里的喧闹声让我很难听到朋友的声音。| 近义词：din, racket, uproar, commotion, babel | 反义词：silence, quiet, stillness | 词性：名词

Etymology:  Likely a variant or alteration of "mingle," influenced by words like "gulp" and perhaps "bellow," suggesting a mixture of sounds.  Its origin is uncertain and it is now considered archaic or obsolete.
