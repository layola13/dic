nonmartialness:/ˌnɒnˈmɑːrʃəl.nəs/
|n.| 非军事性；非尚武性 |The country's nonmartialness was reflected in its low military spending.  这个国家的非军事性体现在其低军事开支上。| The era was marked by a spirit of nonmartialness and pacifism.  那个时代以非军事性和和平主义的精神为标志。| 近义词: pacifism, peacefulness | 反义词: bellicosity, militarism | 词性: noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
martial (adjective): from Latin "martialis" (of Mars, the god of war)
-ness (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
