dinotherium:/ˌdaɪnəˈθɪəriəm/| n.|恐兽，地兽|An extinct mammal of the elephant kind, of great size, characterized by having two large tusks projecting downwards from the lower jaw.|The dinotherium, with its downward-curving tusks, was a unique creature of the Miocene epoch.  恐兽向下弯曲的长牙是中新世时期一种独特的生物。|Dinotherium fossils have been found in Europe, Asia, and Africa. 在欧洲、亚洲和非洲都发现了恐兽化石。|近义词：mastodon, gomphothere|反义词:|noun
