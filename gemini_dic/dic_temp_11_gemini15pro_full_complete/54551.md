dislocate:/ˈdɪsləkeɪt/ (US) /dɪsˈləʊkeɪt/ (UK)| v. |使脱臼；使错位；扰乱|To force a bone out of its normal position in a joint; to disrupt the normal arrangement or order of something. |例句：He dislocated his shoulder playing rugby. 他在打橄榄球时肩膀脱臼了。

例句：The earthquake dislocated the train tracks. 地震使火车轨道错位了。|近义词：displace, disturb, disrupt|反义词：locate, place, order|词性：动词
