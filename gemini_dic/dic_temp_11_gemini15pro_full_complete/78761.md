gilden:/ˈɡɪldən/ | adj. | 镀金的，金色的 | Covered with gold or a gold-colored substance; having a golden color. |  The gilden frame of the painting added to its grandeur. 这幅画的镀金框增添了它的庄严。| Synonyms: gold-plated, gilt, aureate | Antonyms: dull, tarnished | adjective

gilden:/ˈɡɪldən/ | v. | 镀金 | To cover with gold or a gold-colored substance. | They gilded the dome of the cathedral. 他们给大教堂的穹顶镀金。| Synonyms: gold-plate, gilt | Antonyms: strip, tarnish | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *gilden, gildenen, from Old English gyldan (“to gild”), from Proto-Germanic *guldijaną (“to gild”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰel- (“yellow, green, bright”). Cognate with Dutch vergulden (“to gild”), German vergolden (“to gild”), Swedish förgylla (“to gild”).
