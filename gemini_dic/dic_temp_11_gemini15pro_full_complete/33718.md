circensian:/sɜːrˈsɛnsiən/
adjective
古罗马竞技场的；竞技场的
of or relating to the Circus Maximus in ancient Rome, especially to the games and spectacles performed there.
The circensian games were a popular form of entertainment in ancient Rome. 古罗马竞技比赛是古代罗马一种流行的娱乐形式。
The emperor enjoyed the circensian spectacles. 皇帝喜欢观看竞技场的表演。
近义词:arena, gladiatorial, Roman
反义词:none
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *circensis*, from *circus* (“circle, ring, circus”).
