bidirectionality:/ˌbaɪˌdɪˌrɛkʃəˈnælɪti/ | n. |双向性，双向性|The property of operating or functioning in two directions.|The new software allows for bidirectionality of data transfer, meaning information can be sent and received simultaneously.  这种新软件允许数据双向传输，这意味着可以同时发送和接收信息。|reciprocity, two-way communication|unidirectionality, one-way|noun
