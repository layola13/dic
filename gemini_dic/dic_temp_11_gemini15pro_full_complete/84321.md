half-yearly:/ˌhɑːfˈjɪrli/ /ˌhæfˈjɪrli/
adjective
半年一次的；每半年的
Occurring or payable twice a year.
The company publishes half-yearly financial reports.  这家公司每半年发布一次财务报告。
The school has half-yearly exams. 学校每半年举行一次考试。
synonym:biannual, semiannual
antonym:annual, quarterly
词性:adjective


half-yearly:/ˌhɑːfˈjɪrli/ /ˌhæfˈjɪrli/
adverb
每半年一次地
Twice a year.
The interest is paid half-yearly. 利息每半年支付一次。
Dividends are distributed half-yearly. 股息每半年分配一次。
synonym:biannually, semiannually
antonym:annually, quarterly
词性:adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" + "yearly".  "Half" comes from Old English "healf," and "yearly" from Old English "ġēarlic."
