hatchel:/ˈhætʃəl/ | n. v. |梳麻机，梳理（亚麻、黄麻等）|A tool with long metal teeth used for combing flax, hemp, or jute. |例句：The farmer used a hatchel to prepare the flax for spinning. 农民用梳麻机准备亚麻以便纺纱。

例句：She hatcheled the wool before spinning it into yarn. 她先梳理羊毛，然后再纺成纱线。|近义词：comb, card |反义词: N/A |词性: 名词, 动词
