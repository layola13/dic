naturelike:/ˈneɪtʃərˌlaɪk/
adjective
自然的，像自然的
Resembling nature; natural.
The garden had a naturelike appearance, with winding paths and wildflowers.  这个花园有一种自然的外观，蜿蜒的小路和野花。
The artificial waterfall looked surprisingly naturelike. 人造瀑布看起来出奇地自然。
近义词:natural, naturalistic, lifelike
反义词:artificial, unnatural, synthetic
词性:adjective
