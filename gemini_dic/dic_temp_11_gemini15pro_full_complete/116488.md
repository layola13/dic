materializers:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzərz/
n.
物质化者；实现者；具体化者
People or things that cause something to appear in physical form or to become a reality.
The materializers of this grand project deserve recognition.  这个宏伟项目的实现者值得认可。
New technologies are the materializers of our dreams. 新技术使我们的梦想得以实现。
Synonyms: realizers, actualizers, concretizers
Antonyms: destroyers, obstructors
noun
