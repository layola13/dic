confinedly:/kənˈfaɪnɪdli/| adv.|狭窄地，有限地，局促地|In a limited or restricted way; narrowly.|She smiled confinedly, as if afraid to show too much emotion. 她腼腆地笑了笑，好像害怕表露太多情感。|Synonyms: narrowly, restrictedly, limitedly|Antonyms: broadly, widely, expansively|词性: 副词

词源:
From confine + -ed + -ly.  "Confine" comes from Old French "confine" (border, limit), ultimately from Latin "confinis" (bordering, having a common boundary).
