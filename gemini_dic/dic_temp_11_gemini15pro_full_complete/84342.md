halfoval:/ˌhæfˈoʊvəl/| adj. n.|半椭圆形的，半椭圆形|A shape resembling one half of an oval.|The window had a halfoval shape.  窗户是半椭圆形的。|semi-oval, half-elliptical|round, circular|adjective, noun
Etymology: "half" + "oval". "Half" from Old English *healfe, from Proto-Germanic *halbaz. "Oval" from Late Latin ovalis, from Latin ovum "egg".
