laypeople: /ˈleɪˌpiːpəl/ (US), /ˈleɪˌpiːpl/ (UK)| n. | 外行，非专业人士 | People who are not trained or experienced in a particular subject or activity. |  The scientific jargon was difficult for laypeople to understand.  这个科学术语对非专业人士来说很难理解。 | Example: The doctor explained the diagnosis in terms that laypeople could understand. 医生用外行能理解的术语解释了诊断结果。| Synonyms: nonprofessionals, amateurs, non-experts | Antonyms: professionals, experts, specialists | noun
