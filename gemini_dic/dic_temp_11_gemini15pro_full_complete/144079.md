outrush:/ˈaʊtrʌʃ/| n.|涌出，冲出|A sudden rapid outward flow or movement.|There was a sudden outrush of water from the broken dam.  大坝决堤，水流汹涌而出。|近义词：outburst, eruption, surge|反义词：influx, inflow|词性: 名词

Etymology: From out- + rush.  "out-" 表示向外，"rush" 表示冲，涌。
