addicted:/əˈdɪktɪd/| adj.|上瘾的，沉迷的|Physically and mentally dependent on a particular substance, activity, or person.| He's addicted to video games. 他沉迷于电子游戏。|近义词：dependent, hooked, obsessed|反义词: indifferent, averse|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin addictus, past participle of addicere "to assign, devote, give over," from ad- "to" + dicere "to say, pronounce." Originally meaning "bound over by decree (to a creditor or master)," the sense of "given over to a habit" is first recorded in 1909.
