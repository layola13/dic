disinhabited:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhæbɪtɪd/
adjective
无人居住的；空置的
Not inhabited; deserted.
The explorers discovered a disinhabited island. 探险家们发现了一个无人居住的岛屿。
The old house stood disinhabited for years. 这栋老房子空置了多年。
近义词: deserted, uninhabited, unoccupied, vacant
反义词: inhabited, populated, occupied
