gymnocarpous:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈkɑːrpəs/
adjective
裸子植物的；裸果的
Having naked seeds, as the conifers.  The ovules and seeds are not enclosed in an ovary.
The pine tree is a gymnocarpous plant. 松树是一种裸子植物。
Gymnocarpous plants bear their seeds exposed, unlike angiosperms. 裸子植物的种子是裸露的，不像被子植物。
synonym:achlamydeous
antonym:angiospermous
