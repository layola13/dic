ashling: /ˈæʃlɪŋ/ | n. |  艾什琳（女子名）; 小鸟（尤指鸣禽）| Aisling is an Irish female given name, meaning "dream" or "vision". It can also refer to a small bird, especially a songbird, in a poetic or literary context. |  Her name is Ashling. 她的名字是艾什琳。| The ashling sang sweetly in the morning. 小鸟在清晨甜美地歌唱。 | 近义词: songbird, bird | 反义词: (无明显的反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源: From Irish *aisling*, from Old Irish *aislinge*.
