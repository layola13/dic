miscounting:/ˌmɪsˈkaʊntɪŋ/
n.|错误计数，计数错误|The act of counting incorrectly. |There was a miscounting of the votes, and the election had to be held again. 票数统计错误，选举不得不重新举行。|miscalculation, error|correct counting|noun

miscount:/ˌmɪsˈkaʊnt/
v.|数错，算错|To count something incorrectly. |I miscounted the number of people attending the party. 我数错了参加聚会的人数。|miscalculate, underestimate, overestimate|count correctly, calculate correctly|verb
