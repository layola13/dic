buddy-up:/ˈbʌdi ʌp/ | phrasal verb |结成伙伴；成为朋友 |To pair up with someone, often for a specific purpose or activity. |例句：The teacher asked the students to buddy up for the project. 老师让学生们结成小组完成这个项目。

例句：They decided to buddy up and travel across Europe together. 他们决定结伴一起游遍欧洲。|近义词：team up, pair up, partner up |反义词：split up, separate |词性：动词短语

buddy:/ˈbʌdi/ | n. | 朋友；伙伴；老兄 | A close friend or companion. | 例句：My buddy and I went to the movies last night. 我和我的朋友昨晚去看电影了。

例句：He's a good buddy of mine. 他是我的好朋友。 | 近义词：friend, pal, mate | 反义词：enemy, foe |词性：名词
