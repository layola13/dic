insanableness:/ɪnˈseɪnəbəlnəs/
n.
疯狂；精神错乱
The state of being insane; insanity.
His insanableness was evident in his wild ramblings and erratic behavior. 他的疯狂从他狂野的漫谈和古怪的行为中可见一斑。
insanity, madness, lunacy, dementia, psychosis
sanity, lucidity, rationality, reason, soundness of mind
