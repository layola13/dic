outcheat:/ˌaʊtˈtʃiːt/
verb
胜过；以欺骗手段战胜
To defeat or surpass someone by cheating.
He outcheated his opponent in the poker game. 他在扑克游戏中以欺骗手段战胜了对手。
近义词: outwit, outmaneuver, defraud, swindle
反义词: be outwitted, be cheated


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + cheat.  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed," while "cheat" refers to acting dishonestly or unfairly to gain an advantage.
