cocksure:/ˌkɒkˈʃʊə(r)/ /ˌkɑːkˈʃʊr/| adj.|过于自信的；自以为是的|Certain of one's own ability and refusing to accept advice or criticism; arrogant.|例句：He was a cocksure young man who thought he knew everything. 他是一个自以为是的年轻人，认为自己什么都知道。

例句：Her cocksure attitude annoyed many of her colleagues. 她自以为是的态度惹恼了许多同事。

|近义词：arrogant, overconfident, presumptuous, self-assured (negative connotation), confident (positive connotation)|反义词：humble, modest, diffident, insecure|词性:形容词
