lackadaisicalnesses:/ˌlækəˈdeɪzɪkəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 懒散，懈怠 | The state of being lackadaisical; lack of enthusiasm and determination. |  His lackadaisicalnesses in his studies resulted in poor grades.  他学习上的懒散导致成绩很差。 | 近义词：lethargy, indolence, apathy, listlessness | 反义词：diligence, enthusiasm, energy, zeal | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From lackadaisical + -ness.  Lackadaisical itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from a dialectal pronunciation of "lack a day" or "alack the day," expressing weariness or regret.
