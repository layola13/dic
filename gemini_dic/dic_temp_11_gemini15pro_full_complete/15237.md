backfiring:/ˌbækˈfaɪərɪŋ/| n. vi.|适得其反，事与愿违| (of a plan or action) having the opposite result to what was intended.| The company's new marketing campaign backfired and lost them customers. 公司的新营销活动适得其反，反而失去了客户。| The prank backfired and he ended up getting in trouble. 这个恶作剧适得其反，他最终陷入了麻烦。|synonyms: rebounding, recoiling, boomeranging| antonyms: succeeding, working, thriving|v.
backfire:/ˌbækˈfaɪər/| vi.|适得其反，事与愿违| (of a plan or action) have an unintended and unwelcome result that is the opposite of what was intended.| Her attempt to help backfired and made the situation worse. 她试图帮忙，结果适得其反，使情况变得更糟。| His plan to get a promotion backfired when his boss found out about his scheme. 他升职的计划在他老板发现他的阴谋后适得其反。|synonyms: rebound, recoil, boomerang| antonyms: succeed, work, thrive|v.
