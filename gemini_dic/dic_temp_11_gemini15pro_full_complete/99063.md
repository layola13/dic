interassuring:/ˌɪntərəˈʃʊrɪŋ/
adjective
相互保证的；使人安心的
Providing or intended to provide mutual reassurance.
The two friends exchanged interassuring glances before the big presentation.  两个朋友在大报告之前交换了令人安心的眼神。
近义词: reassuring, comforting, supportive
反义词: unsettling, disturbing, alarming
词性: adjective


Etymology:
"inter-" (prefix): between, among, mutually. From Latin "inter" (between, among).
"assure" (verb): to remove doubt or anxiety from someone's mind. From Old French "assurer" (to make certain, guarantee), ultimately from Latin "assecurare" (to make safe).
"-ing" (suffix): forms present participles and gerunds, often used to create adjectives.
