misbegotten:/ˌmɪsbɪˈɡɒtn/ /ˌmɪsbɪˈɡɑːtn/
adjective
不幸的；私生的；计划不周的
ill-conceived; poorly planned; illegitimate
例句：The misbegotten policy caused widespread chaos.  这项计划不周的政策导致了广泛的混乱。

例句：The film was a misbegotten attempt at a comedy.  这部电影是一次不幸的喜剧尝试。
近义词：ill-fated, disastrous, unfortunate, illegitimate
反义词：well-conceived, fortunate, legitimate
词性: adjective
