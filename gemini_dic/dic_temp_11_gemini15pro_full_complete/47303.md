de-integrating:/diːˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪŋ/
verb
反向整合，分解，去整合
The process of separating previously combined parts or elements of a system or organization.
The company is de-integrating its operations to focus on core businesses.  该公司正在分解其业务，以专注于核心业务。
The software allows for de-integrating large datasets into smaller, manageable chunks.  该软件允许将大型数据集分解成更小、更易于管理的数据块。
近义词: disintegrating, separating, dismantling, decoupling
反义词: integrating, combining, unifying, consolidating
词性: verb (present participle)


de-integrated:/diːˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪd/
adjective
已分解的，已去整合的
Having been separated into previously combined parts.
The formerly integrated company is now a collection of de-integrated businesses.  这家以前整合的公司现在是一系列已分解的企业的集合。
The de-integrated system proved to be more efficient in the long run.  事实证明，分解后的系统从长远来看效率更高。
近义词: separated, disintegrated, dismantled, decoupled
反义词: integrated, combined, unified, consolidated
词性: adjective


词源:
From de- + integrating, the present participle of integrate, from Latin integratus, past participle of integrare "to make whole," from integer "whole."
