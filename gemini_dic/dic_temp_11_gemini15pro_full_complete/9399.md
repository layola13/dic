antiquities:/ænˈtɪkwɪtiz/
n.
古物；古董；古代文物
Objects, buildings, or works of art from the ancient past.
例句：The museum houses a vast collection of Roman antiquities.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的罗马文物。

例句：She has a passion for collecting Egyptian antiquities. 她热爱收藏埃及文物。

近义词：relics, artifacts, ancient objects
反义词：modern artifacts, contemporary objects
词性：名词(plural)

词源：From Old French "antiquité" and directly from Latin "antiquitas," meaning "old age, antiquity."  Ultimately from "antiquus," meaning "old, ancient."
