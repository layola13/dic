hypotenuse's:/haɪˈpɒtənjuːzɪz/ |n.|斜边的；直角三角形斜边的|The hypotenuse's length is calculated using the Pythagorean theorem.  直角三角形的斜边长度是用勾股定理计算的。|The square of the hypotenuse's length is equal to the sum of the squares of the other two sides. 斜边长度的平方等于其他两条边长度的平方和。|近义词: (none -  it's a specific mathematical term) |反义词: (none) |词性: noun (possessive form)

Etymology: From Late Latin *hypotenusa, from Ancient Greek ὑποτείνουσα (hupoteínousa, “subtending”), present participle feminine of ὑποτείνω (hupoteínō, “I subtend, stretch under”), from ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + τείνω (teínō, “I stretch, extend”).
