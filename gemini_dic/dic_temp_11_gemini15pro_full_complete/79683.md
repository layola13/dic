glow's:/ɡloʊz/
n.|光辉;光晕|The glow's of the setting sun was beautiful. 落日的光辉很美。| English Explanation:  The possessive form of "glow," indicating something belongs to or is associated with a glow.  For example, "the glow's warmth" refers to the warmth emanating from a glow.|例句：The fire's glow illuminated the room. 火光照亮了房间。|近义词：radiance, luminescence, shimmer|反义词：dullness, darkness|词性:名词
