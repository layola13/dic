kla:/klɑː/ /klæ/ | determiner, pronoun | which; what | Used to ask questions about one or more people or things from a limited number. Similar to "which" but less formal. | Example: Kla color is your car?  你的车是什么颜色？ | Example: Kla book are you reading? 你在读哪本书？| Synonym: which | Antonym: none (not applicable for this word type) | word type: determiner, pronoun
