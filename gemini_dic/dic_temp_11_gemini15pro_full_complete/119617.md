middens:/ˈmɪdnz/| n. |垃圾堆；废物堆；贝冢|A refuse heap; a dunghill. Specifically, a mound or deposit containing shells, animal bones, and other refuse that indicates the site of a prehistoric settlement.|Archaeologists excavated the middens and found evidence of early human habitation. 考古学家挖掘了这些贝冢，发现了早期人类居住的证据。|Synonyms: dump, heap, mound, refuse pile|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "clean area", "pristine site")|noun

middens:/ˈmɪdnz/| n. |贝冢（尤指史前人类居住地）|A kitchen midden, also known as a shell midden, is a prehistoric refuse heap, or mound, consisting chiefly of the shells of edible mollusks intermingled with animal bones, shards of pottery, implements, and other refuse. |The middens provided valuable insights into the diet and lifestyle of the ancient coastal communities.  这些贝冢为了解古代沿海社区的饮食和生活方式提供了宝贵的见解。|Synonyms: shell midden, kitchen midden, refuse heap|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "clean area", "pristine site")|noun
