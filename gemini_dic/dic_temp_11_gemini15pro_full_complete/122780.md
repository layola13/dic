molarities:/məˈlærɪtiz/| n. |摩尔浓度，摩尔浓度值|The concentration of a solution expressed in moles of solute per liter of solution.  A measure of the number of moles of solute in one liter of solution.|The molarities of the solutions were carefully measured before the experiment. 实验前仔细测量了溶液的摩尔浓度。|近义词：concentration, molar concentration|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps dilution)|词性:noun
