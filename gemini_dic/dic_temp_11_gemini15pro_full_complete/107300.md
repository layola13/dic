lamarckian:/lɑːˈmɑːrkiən/
adjective
拉马克的；拉马克学说的
Relating to the theory of evolution developed by Jean-Baptiste Lamarck, which proposes that acquired characteristics during an organism's lifetime can be inherited by its offspring.
His theory of evolution is considered Lamarckian. 他的进化论被认为是拉马克学说的。

Lamarckian:/lɑːˈmɑːrkiən/
noun
拉马克主义者；信奉拉马克学说的人
A person who believes in or follows the principles of Lamarckism.
Many scientists of his time were Lamarckians. 他那个时代的许多科学家都是拉马克主义者。


近义词:neo-Lamarckian
反义词:Darwinian, Mendelian
词性:adjective, noun
