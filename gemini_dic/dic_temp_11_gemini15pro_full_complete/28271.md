captivating:/ˈkæptɪveɪtɪŋ/| adj.|迷人的，有魅力的|Having the power to attract and hold the interest; fascinating.|Her captivating smile made everyone feel at ease. 她迷人的微笑让每个人都感到轻松自在。|近义词:enthralling, fascinating, charming, alluring, mesmerizing|反义词:repulsive, dull, boring, tedious, uninteresting|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin captivare "to take captive," from captus, past participle of capere "to take, seize."
