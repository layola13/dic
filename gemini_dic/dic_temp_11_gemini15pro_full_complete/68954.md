fathering:/ˈfɑːðərɪŋ/ | n. | 父亲的身份；养育子女的行为 | The act of being a father; the process of raising children. |  He took pride in his fathering of his two sons. 他以身为两个儿子的父亲而自豪。

fathering:/ˈfɑːðərɪŋ/ | v. |  成为...的父亲；生育；养育 | To become the father of; to beget; to raise (a child). | He fathered three children. 他育有三个孩子。


fathering:/ˈfɑːðərɪŋ/ | adj.|  父系的 | Relating to or characteristic of a father. |  His fathering instincts kicked in when he saw the lost child. 当他看到走失的孩子时，他的父性本能被激发了。

近义词: parenting, upbringing, raising, nurturing, siring
反义词: mothering (specifically referring to the mother's role), neglecting

词性: noun, verb, adjective (less common)


词源: From the noun "father" + the suffix "-ing" which forms nouns of action or process, and also present participles of verbs.  Ultimately from Old English "fæder," of Germanic origin.
