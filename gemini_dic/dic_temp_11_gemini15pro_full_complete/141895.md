ordeal:/ɔːrˈdiːl/| n.|考验；磨难；痛苦的经历|A severe test or experience that is very unpleasant or difficult.|例句：The hostages went through a terrifying ordeal. 人质们经历了一场可怕的磨难。

例句：Climbing Mount Everest was an incredible ordeal. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一次难以置信的考验。|近义词：trial, tribulation, test, hardship, suffering|反义词：comfort, ease, pleasure|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ordǣl, from or- "out, away" + dǣl "portion, share," literally "that which is dealt out."  Originally referred to a judgment by divine intervention, such as surviving a physical test.
