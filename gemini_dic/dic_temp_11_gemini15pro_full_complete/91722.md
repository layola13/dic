hypercyanotic:/ˌhaɪpərˌsaɪəˈnɑːtɪk/ | adj. |紫绀过度的，高度紫绀的 |  Marked by or causing extreme cyanosis (a bluish discoloration of the skin resulting from poor circulation or inadequate oxygenation of the blood).  Relates to a severe decrease in oxygen levels in the blood, leading to a bluish discoloration of the skin and mucous membranes. | The baby was hypercyanotic at birth due to a heart defect.  这个婴儿出生时由于心脏缺陷而出现紫绀过度。 | The patient experienced a hypercyanotic spell.  病人经历了一次严重的紫绀发作。 |  cyanotic, blue, dusky |  pink, flushed, ruddy | adjective
