discontinuers:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuərz/
|n.|停止使用者，中断者|People who cease using a product, service, or medication are called discontinuers.  停止使用某种产品、服务或药物的人被称为停止使用者。|The study followed a cohort of patients, including discontinuers and completers. 这项研究跟踪了一组患者，包括停止治疗者和完成治疗者。|近义词：cessators, abandoners|反义词：continuers, users|词性: 名词

discontinuer:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuər/
|n.|停止使用者，中断者|A person who discontinues something.  一个停止做某事的人。|Many discontinuers of the drug reported experiencing withdrawal symptoms. 许多停止服用该药的人报告说经历了戒断症状。|近义词：cessator, abandoner|反义词：continuer, user|词性: 名词

词源:  From discontinue + -er.  来自 discontinue + -er。
