interpervasive: /ˌɪntərˈpɜːrveɪsɪv/
interpervasive| adj.|互相渗透的，遍布的|Mutually pervasive; pervading each other; extending throughout one another.|The interpervasive nature of the internet and social media has made global communication instantaneous.|近义词：pervasive, widespread, ubiquitous, omnipresent|反义词：localized, limited, contained|词性:形容词
词源: "inter-" (between, among) + "pervasive" (spreading widely throughout an area or a group of people).  "Pervasive" comes from Latin "pervadere" (to go through, pervade), from "per-" (through) + "vadere" (to go).
