habilimented:/həˈbɪlɪmɛntɪd/
adjective
穿着衣服的，装扮的
dressed, clothed, especially in a particular or elaborate way.
The king was habilimented in a magnificent purple robe. 国王身穿华丽的紫色长袍。
近义词:attired, clad, arrayed, garbed
反义词:unclothed, naked, undressed
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From French *habilementer*, from *habile* "able, suitable," from Latin *habilis* "easily handled, manageable, apt, skillful," from *habere* "to hold, have."  The sense evolution is from "making fit or suitable" to "dressing, clothing."
