desegregate:/diːˈseɡrɪɡeɪt/
verb
取消种族隔离
To eliminate racial segregation in; integrate racially.
The government finally decided to desegregate schools in the South. 政府最终决定取消南方学校的种族隔离。
The army was desegregated in 1948.  军队在1948年取消了种族隔离。

integrate, mix
segregate, discriminate
