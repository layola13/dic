ethnologist: /ɛθˈnɒlədʒɪst/ (British) /ɛθˈnɑːlədʒɪst/ (American)
n.
民族学家，人种学家
A scholar who studies the characteristics of various peoples and their relationships to each other.
Ethnologists often conduct fieldwork to study different cultures. 民族学家经常进行田野调查来研究不同的文化。
The ethnologist spent years living with the tribe to understand their customs.  这位民族学家花了数年时间与该部落生活在一起，以了解他们的习俗。
anthropologist, ethnographer
sociologist
