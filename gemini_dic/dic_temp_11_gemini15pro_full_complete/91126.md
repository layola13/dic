hybrid:/ˈhaɪbrɪd/| n. adj.|混合物，杂交种，混合动力车|Something that is made by combining two different elements; a mixture.|例句：The car is a hybrid, powered by both petrol and electricity. 这辆车是混合动力车，由汽油和电力驱动。

例句：Scientists have created a new hybrid of wheat. 科学家们创造了一种新的小麦杂交品种。|近义词：mixture, blend, combination, crossbreed|反义词：purebred, unmixed|词性: 名词, 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *hybrida 'offspring of a tame sow and wild boar,' of unknown origin.
