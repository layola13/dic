missing-links:/ˈmɪsɪŋ lɪŋks/ | n. | 缺失的环节，未知的联系 |  Something which is needed to complete a chain or sequence, especially in a process of development or evolution.  It often refers to a hypothetical transitional form in evolution. | The discovery of the feathered dinosaur provided a missing link between dinosaurs and birds.  发现这种有羽毛的恐龙为恐龙和鸟类之间的联系提供了缺失的环节。 | synonyms: missing piece, gap, void | antonyms: connection, link, bridge | noun
