hypervigilance:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɪdʒɪləns/| n.|过度警觉，高度警惕|The state of being highly or abnormally alert to potential danger or threats.|After the robbery, she experienced a period of hypervigilance, constantly scanning her surroundings.|近义词: watchfulness, alertness, vigilance, caution|反义词: obliviousness, inattention, carelessness|词性:noun

Etymology:
From hyper- + vigilance. Hyper- comes from Greek *hupér (over, above). Vigilance comes from Latin *vigilantia (watchfulness).
