cpg:/siː piː ʒiː/ | n. | consumer packaged goods | consumer packaged goods (CPG) are items used daily by average consumers that require routine replacement or replenishment, such as food, beverages, clothes, tobacco, makeup, and household products.  | Example: The CPG industry is highly competitive.  消费品行业竞争非常激烈。| Example:  Marketing strategies for CPG brands often focus on building brand loyalty. 消费品品牌的营销策略通常侧重于建立品牌忠诚度。 | Synonyms: FMCG (fast-moving consumer goods), consumer goods | Antonyms: durable goods | noun
