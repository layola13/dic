inconsiderableness:/ɪnkənˌsɪdərəb(ə)lnəs/
|n.|微不足道；不重要；无足轻重|The inconsiderableness of the amount made it hardly worth mentioning.  数额微不足道，几乎不值得一提。|Synonyms: insignificance, triviality, unimportance|Antonyms: importance, significance, consequence|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + considerable (worthy of consideration) + -ness (forming nouns expressing a state or quality). Ultimately from Latin considerare (to consider).
