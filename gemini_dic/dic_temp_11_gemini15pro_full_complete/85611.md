haughtily:/ˈhɔːtɪli/ | adv. |傲慢地，高傲地 | In a haughty manner; arrogantly. Showing an excessive amount of pride and disdain for others. | She tossed her head haughtily and walked away. 她傲慢地昂起头，走开了。| He haughtily dismissed their concerns. 他傲慢地驳回了他们的担忧。| 近义词: arrogantly, disdainfully, superciliously, proudly | 反义词: humbly, modestly, respectfully | 词性: 副词

haughty: /ˈhɔːti/ | adj. |傲慢的，高傲的 | Arrogantly superior and disdainful. Having or showing an attitude of superiority and contempt for people or things perceived to be inferior. |  The haughty waiter sneered at our simple clothes.  傲慢的侍者嘲笑我们简朴的衣着。|  She gave him a haughty stare. 她傲慢地瞪了他一眼。| 近义词: arrogant, disdainful, supercilious, proud | 反义词: humble, modest, respectful | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Old French *hautif, *haltif, from haut “high,” from Latin altus.
