cissoid:/ˈsɪsɔɪd/| n. |蔓叶线；蔓叶曲线|A plane curve resembling a leaf of ivy.  It is the locus of a point on a line from a fixed point on a circle to a variable point on the circle, where the distance from the variable point to a tangent to the circle at the fixed point equals the distance from the fixed point to the variable point.|The cissoid of Diocles is a cubic curve.|近义词:curve|反义词:|noun
