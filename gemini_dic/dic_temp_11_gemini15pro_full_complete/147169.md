overloyalty:/ˌoʊvərˈlɔɪəlti/ | n. | 过度忠诚，过分忠诚 | Excessive loyalty, often to a fault; loyalty that is detrimental to oneself or others. | His overloyalty to the company led him to make some questionable decisions. 他对公司的过度忠诚导致他做出了一些 questionable 的决定。| 近义词: excessive devotion, blind loyalty, undue allegiance | 反义词: disloyalty, treachery, betrayal | 词性: 名词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "loyalty" (from Old French "loialte," meaning "fealty")
