intune:/ɪnˈtjuːn/| adj.|协调的，和谐的; 理解的，领悟的|In harmony with; in sympathy with.  Understanding or sharing the feelings of another.|例句：She felt completely intune with his thoughts. 她感觉与他的想法完全一致。

例句：The design of the building is perfectly in tune with its surroundings. 这栋建筑的设计与其周围环境完美协调。|近义词：harmonious, sympathetic, attuned, resonant|反义词：discordant, dissonant, out of tune, unsympathetic|词性:形容词
