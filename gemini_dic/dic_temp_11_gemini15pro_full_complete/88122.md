hijra:/ˈhɪdʒrə/ | n. | 海吉拉，印度跨性别群体 | A term used in South Asia – particularly in India, Pakistan and Bangladesh – to refer to transgender people, the transgender community, and an identity. It is also used to refer to eunuchs, intersex people, and transgender women. | Example:  The hijra community has a long and rich history. 海吉拉社群有着悠久而丰富的历史。

Example:  Some hijras perform at weddings and births.  一些海吉拉会在婚礼和出生庆典上表演。| Synonym:  transgender, transsexual (sometimes, though not always accurate or preferred)  | Antonym:  cisgender (though not a direct opposite, it represents the other end of the gender spectrum) | noun
