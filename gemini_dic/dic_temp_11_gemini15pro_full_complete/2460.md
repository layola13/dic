adjustabilities:/əˌdʒʌstəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
可调节性，可调整性
The quality or state of being adjustable; the capacity for adjustment.
This chair offers a wide range of adjustabilities to ensure maximum comfort. 这把椅子提供了广泛的可调节性，以确保最大的舒适度。
The adjustabilities of the new software make it very user-friendly.  新软件的可调整性使其非常用户友好。
synonyms: flexibility, adaptability, versatility
antonyms: inflexibility, rigidity, fixity
词性: 名词
