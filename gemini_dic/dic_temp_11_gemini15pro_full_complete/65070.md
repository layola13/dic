eternity:/ɪˈtɜːrnəti/ /ɪˈtɝːnəti/| n.|永恒，永久，无穷|A state of infinite time; time without beginning or end. |例句：The concept of eternity is difficult to grasp.  永恒的概念很难理解。

例句：He promised her an eternity of love. 他承诺给她永恒的爱。|近义词：infinity, perpetuity, forever|反义词：temporality, brevity, finiteness|词性: noun
