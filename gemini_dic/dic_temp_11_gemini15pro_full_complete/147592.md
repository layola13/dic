overplan:/ˈoʊvərplæn/| v. |过度计划；计划过多|To plan in excessive detail or to make more plans than are necessary.|例句：They overplanned their vacation and ended up feeling stressed instead of relaxed. 他们过度计划了他们的假期，结果感到压力而不是放松。

例句：We overplanned the project, and now we have too many contingencies. 我们过度计划了这个项目，现在我们有太多的应急方案了。|近义词：overorganize, overschedule|反义词: underplan, improvise|词性:动词
