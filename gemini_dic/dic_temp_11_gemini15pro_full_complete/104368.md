ka-ching:/kəˈtʃɪŋ/
|interjection, noun|
|钱币声，赚钱的声音；（表示）赚钱了|
|Imitates the sound of a cash register, suggesting that money has been made or received.|
|例句：Ka-ching! Another sale!  钱币声！又一笔生意！|
|例句：Every time I sell a piece of art, I hear that sweet ka-ching! 我每卖出一件艺术品，就能听到那悦耳的赚钱声！|
|近义词：cha-ching, ker-ching|
|反义词：(None - represents a sound/action, not a concept with an opposite)|
|词性: interjection, noun|


ka-ching:/kəˈtʃɪŋ/
|noun|
|赚钱；进账|
|The sound of a cash register, representing the act of making money.|
|例句：With this new product, we're expecting a lot of ka-ching. 有了这款新产品，我们预计会赚很多钱。|
|例句：The ka-ching of the register was music to his ears. 收银机的钱币声对他来说就像音乐一样悦耳。|
|近义词：profit, income, earnings|
|反义词：loss, expense|
|词性: noun|
