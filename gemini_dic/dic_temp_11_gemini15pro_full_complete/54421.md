disinfect:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛkt/| vt.|消毒，杀菌|To cleanse of infection, usually by chemical means; sterilize.|例句：The hospital staff disinfected the operating room before the surgery. 医院工作人员在手术前对手术室进行了消毒。|近义词：sterilize, sanitize, cleanse|反义词:infect, contaminate|verb
disinfection:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛkʃən/| n.|消毒，杀菌|The process of cleansing something, especially with a chemical, in order to destroy bacteria.|例句：Regular disinfection of surfaces can help prevent the spread of illness.  定期对表面进行消毒有助于防止疾病传播。|近义词：sterilization, sanitization|反义词:infection, contamination|noun
