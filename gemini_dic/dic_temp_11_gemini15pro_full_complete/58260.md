drumming:/ˈdrʌmɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdrʌmɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v.|击鼓，鼓声；敲击声|The continuous, rhythmic beating of drums.  Also, the act of playing drums or tapping rhythmically.|例句：The drumming of the rain on the roof was soothing. 雨点打在屋顶上的鼓声令人舒缓。|近义词：beating, tapping, rapping|反义词:silence, quiet|词性:名词, 动名词

drumming:/ˈdrʌmɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdrʌmɪŋ/ (UK)| v.|击鼓，敲击|To play the drums or to tap rhythmically.|例句：He was drumming his fingers nervously on the table. 他紧张地用手指敲着桌子。|近义词：beat, tap, rap|反义词:stop, cease|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "drum" + "-ing" suffix. "Drum" comes from Middle Dutch "dromme" and Middle Low German "dromme."
