marxist: /ˈmɑːksɪst/ (UK) /ˈmɑːrksist/ (US)| adj. n.|马克思主义的；马克思主义者|Relating to the political and economic theories of Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels, later developed by their followers.|例句：Marxist philosophy has had a profound impact on the 20th century. 马克思主义哲学对20世纪产生了深远的影响。

例句：He is a self-proclaimed Marxist. 他自称是马克思主义者。|近义词：communist, socialist, leftist|反义词：capitalist, conservative, right-wing|词性: adjective, noun
词源：From the name of Karl Marx (1818–83), German political philosopher + -ist.
