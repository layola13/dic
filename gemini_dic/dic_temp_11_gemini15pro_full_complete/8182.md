anti-democratical:/ˌæntiˌdeməˈkrætɪkl/ | adj. | 反民主的，非民主的 | Opposed to or violating the principles of democracy.  |  The king's actions were seen as anti-democratical by the people.  国王的行为被人民视为反民主的。 |  The government implemented anti-democratical policies. 政府实施了反民主的政策。 | synonyms: undemocratic, authoritarian, totalitarian, despotic, tyrannical | antonyms: democratic, egalitarian, liberal, republican | adjective


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
democratical (adjective): relating to or supporting democracy (from Greek dēmokratia)
