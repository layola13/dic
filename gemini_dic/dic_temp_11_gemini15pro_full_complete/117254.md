mechanicalization:/ˌmekənɪzeɪʃən/ |n.|机械化|The process of using machinery to do work that was previously done by hand or with animal power.  The introduction of machinery, especially on a large scale, to replace human or animal labor.|The mechanicalization of agriculture has greatly increased farm output. 农业机械化极大地提高了农业产量。|mechanisation, automation|manual labor|noun
