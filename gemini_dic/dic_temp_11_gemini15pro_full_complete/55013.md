dispute:/dɪˈspjuːt/ (US), /dɪˈspjuːt/ (UK)| n. vt. |争议，争论；辩驳，反驳|A disagreement, argument, or debate.|The two countries are in a dispute over territory. 这两个国家在领土问题上存在争议。|近义词：argument, debate, quarrel, controversy, contention|反义词：agreement, harmony, concord, accord|词性: noun, verb

dispute:/dɪˈspjuːt/ (US), /dɪˈspjuːt/ (UK)| vt. | 争议，争论；辩驳，反驳|To argue against; to question the truth or validity of.|He disputed the judge's decision. 他对法官的判决提出了异议。|近义词：challenge, contest, oppose, question|反义词：accept, concede, admit|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desputer* (Modern French *disputer*), from Latin *disputare* "to discuss, examine, argue, discourse," from *dis-* "apart, in different directions" + *putare* "to think, reckon, believe, suppose."
