gadgetries /ˈɡædʒɪtriz/
noun
小玩意儿；小配件；精巧的小机械装置

He was fascinated by all sorts of gadgetries, especially those related to computers. 他对各种小玩意儿都很着迷，尤其是与电脑相关的。
synonyms: gadgets, devices, appliances, gizmos
antonyms: necessities, essentials
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
