overbravely:/ˌoʊvərˈbreɪvli/ | adv. | 过于勇敢地，鲁莽地 |  In a manner that is excessively brave, often to the point of recklessness or foolishness. | He overbravely charged into the enemy lines, disregarding the danger. 他过于勇敢地冲入敌阵，全然不顾危险。 | The knight overbravely challenged the dragon, a decision he soon regretted.  这位骑士过于勇敢地挑战了巨龙，这个决定他很快就后悔了。| 近义词: recklessly, foolishly, rashly, imprudently | 反义词: cautiously, prudently, wisely, carefully | 词性: 副词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "brave" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
