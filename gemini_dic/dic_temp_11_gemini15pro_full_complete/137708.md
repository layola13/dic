nonutilities:/ˌnɒnjuːˈtɪlətiz/
noun
非公用事业公司
Companies that are not involved in providing essential services like electricity, gas, or water.
Many nonutilities are investing in renewable energy sources. 许多非公用事业公司正在投资可再生能源。
