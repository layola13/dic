blemish:/ˈblɛmɪʃ/| n. vt.|瑕疵，缺点；玷污|A mark or flaw that spoils the appearance of something.|The vase had a small blemish on its side.  这个花瓶侧面有个小瑕疵。|blemish:/ˈblɛmɪʃ/| vt.|玷污，损害|To spoil the appearance or quality of something.|The scandal blemished his reputation.  这桩丑闻玷污了他的名誉。|近义词：defect, flaw, imperfection, fault, mark, spot, stain|反义词：perfection, virtue, asset|词性: 名词, 动词

词源: From Old French *blesmir, from blemir "to make pale, fade, discolor," probably of Germanic origin.
