cavetto:/kəˈvɛtoʊ/ /kæˈvɛtoʊ/
noun
凹槽，空心线脚
A concave moulding, especially one in the quadrant of a circle.
The architect included a cavetto moulding in the design of the window frame.  建筑师在窗框的设计中加入了一个凹槽线脚。
synonyms:concavity, hollow, groove, channel
antonyms:convexity, bulge, projection
n.
