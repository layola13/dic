besmirch:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃ/
verb
玷污，污损；败坏（名声）
To damage the reputation of (someone or something) in the opinion of others.
His reputation was besmirched by the false accusations. 他的名誉被诬告玷污了。
synonyms: sully, tarnish, smear, stain, soil, blacken, defile
antonyms: cleanse, purify, honor, exalt
词性: 动词
