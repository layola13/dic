flimflammer:/ˈflɪmˌflæmər/| n. |骗子；江湖郎中|A person who uses tricks and deception to cheat others, especially by selling worthless goods or services. |He was a flimflammer who sold fake cures for all sorts of ailments. 他是一个江湖骗子，兜售各种疾病的假药。|Synonyms: con artist, swindler, fraudster, charlatan|Antonyms: honest person, reputable dealer|noun

flimflam:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/| n. |欺骗；花言巧语|Nonsense intended to deceive. |His explanation was just a bunch of flimflam. 他的解释只是一堆花言巧语。|Synonyms: trickery, deception, fraud, humbug|Antonyms: honesty, truthfulness|noun

flimflam:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/| v. |欺骗；哄骗|To deceive or trick someone, especially by using elaborate or misleading language. |They flimflammed the old lady out of her life savings. 他们骗走了老太太的毕生积蓄。|Synonyms: swindle, cheat, defraud, hoodwink|Antonyms: be honest with, deal fairly with|verb
