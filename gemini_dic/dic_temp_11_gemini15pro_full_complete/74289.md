fractal-dimension:/ˈfræktəl daɪˈmɛnʃən/ | n. | 分形维数 | A statistical quantity that gives an indication of how completely a fractal appears to fill space, as one zooms down to finer and finer scales.  It is a measure of the complexity of a fractal's shape. | The coastline of Britain has a fractal dimension of approximately 1.25.  英国海岸线的维度大约是 1.25。 | Synonyms: fractal dimension, Hausdorff dimension | Antonyms: topological dimension | noun

fractal-dimensions:/ˈfræktəl daɪˈmɛnʃənz/ | n. | 分形维数（复数）|  The plural form of fractal dimension, referring to multiple instances or types of this measurement. | Many natural objects, like clouds and trees, can be characterized by their fractal dimensions. 许多自然物体，如云和树木，可以用它们的分形维数来描述。| Synonyms: fractal dimensions, Hausdorff dimensions | Antonyms: topological dimensions | noun
