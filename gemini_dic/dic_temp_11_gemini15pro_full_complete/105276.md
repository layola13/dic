khronos:/ˈkrɒnɒs/| n.|时间, 时序|From Ancient Greek χρόνος (khrónos, “time”).  Refers to the sequential passage of time, often in a personified or mythological context.|Chronos, the personification of time, was often depicted as an old man with a long beard.  时间之神克洛诺斯通常被描绘成一个留着长胡子的老人。|synonyms: time, duration, period|antonyms: timelessness, eternity|noun
