expeditive:/ɪkˈspiːdiətɪv/ /ɛkˈspiːdiətɪv/
adjective
权宜之计的；方便的；迅速的
(of a process or action) done with speed and efficiency, but not always showing care or thought; suitable or convenient for a particular purpose, though possibly improper or immoral.
例句：Taking an expeditive route, they bypassed the traffic jam.  他们抄近路避开了交通堵塞。

例句：The manager made an expeditive decision to fire the employee, without a proper investigation. 经理没有进行适当的调查，就迅速决定解雇这名员工。

近义词：efficient, quick, swift, rapid, prompt, summary
反义词：slow, inefficient, deliberate, thorough, meticulous
词性: adjective
