cineration:/ˌsɪnəˈreɪʃən/
|n.|焚烧；烧成灰烬|The process of reducing something to ashes by burning. |Medical waste undergoes incineration to prevent the spread of disease.  医疗废物经过焚烧以防止疾病传播。| cremation, burning, combustion |extinction, preservation| noun

cinerate:/ˈsɪnəreɪt/
|vt.|焚烧；烧成灰烬|To reduce something to ashes by burning.|The city incinerates its garbage.  这座城市焚烧垃圾。|burn, cremate, combust|extinguish, preserve|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cineratio, from Latin cineratus (“reduced to ashes”), past participle of cinerare (“to reduce to ashes”), from cinis (“ashes”).
