brighten:/ˈbraɪtn/| v. |使明亮，使快乐，使振作；变亮，变快乐|To make or become lighter or more cheerful.|The morning sun brightened the room. 晨曦照亮了房间。|近义词：lighten, cheer, enliven|反义词: darken, dim, sadden|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old English *beorhtnian, from beorht "bright".
