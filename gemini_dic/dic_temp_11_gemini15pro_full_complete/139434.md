obsidian:/əbˈsɪdiən/| n.|黑曜石|A hard, dark, glassy volcanic rock formed by the rapid solidification of lava without crystallization.|Example 1:  The ancient Aztecs used obsidian to make sharp tools and weapons. 古代阿兹特克人用黑曜石制作锋利的工具和武器。

Example 2:  She wore a necklace with a polished obsidian pendant. 她戴着一条带有抛光黑曜石吊坠的项链。|近义词：volcanic glass|反义词: None|词性:noun
