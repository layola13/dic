cryptocrystalline:/ˌkrɪptoʊˈkrɪstəlɪn/ | adj. |隐晶质的；微晶的| Composed of crystals too small to be seen with an ordinary microscope.  Made up of minute crystals, not visible with an ordinary light microscope.|  The chert exhibited a cryptocrystalline texture. 这块燧石呈现出隐晶质的结构。|  microcrystalline, fine-grained |  macrocrystalline, coarsely crystalline | adjective
