fished:/fɪʃt/
verb
钓到鱼;捕到鱼
past tense and past participle of fish.  The act of catching fish, or the result of having caught fish.
He fished for trout in the river. 他在河里钓鳟鱼。
He has fished in these waters for many years. 他在这些水域捕鱼多年了。
Synonyms: angled, trawled
Antonyms: released, freed
词性: 动词
