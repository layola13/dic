blacklegging:/ˈblækˌlɛɡɪŋ/| n. |破坏罢工；欺骗；（体育）违规参赛|The act of working during a strike, especially by a non-union member, or of undermining a strike in some other way. Also, acting dishonestly or deceitfully, or competing illegally in sports.|例句：The union accused the company of hiring blacklegs to replace the striking workers. 工会指责公司雇用破坏罢工者来取代罢工工人。

例句：He was accused of blacklegging by playing in a tournament he wasn't eligible for. 他被指控违规参加了他没有资格参加的锦标赛。|近义词：scabbing, strikebreaking, cheating, double-dealing, deception|反义词：supporting, cooperating, honesty, integrity|词性：名词

词源：源自19世纪煤矿工人对矿工罢工的蔑称。 "blackleg" 原指矿工腿上沾染的煤尘，后来被用来指代那些在罢工期间继续工作的矿工，暗示他们是“肮脏的”或“不忠诚的”。
