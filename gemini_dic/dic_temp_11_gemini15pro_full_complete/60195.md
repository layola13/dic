effigial: /ɪˈfɪdʒiəl/
adjective
肖像的；雕像的；模拟的
Representing or symbolizing a person or thing, especially in the form of a sculptured figure.
The effigial monument commemorated the fallen soldiers. 这座雕像纪念碑是为了纪念阵亡的士兵。
synonyms: representative, symbolic, figurative, emblematic
antonyms: literal, actual, real
词性: 形容词
