chapelling:/ˈtʃæpəlɪŋ/
n.
小教堂婚礼；在小教堂举行婚礼
The act of getting married in a chapel.
They had a simple chapelling ceremony with close family and friends. 他们与亲朋好友举行了一个简单的小教堂婚礼。
近义词: chapel wedding, chapel service
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider large church wedding, secular wedding)
