inedibility:/ˌɪnɛdəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可食性；不能食用 |The inedibility of certain mushrooms can be fatal. | Certain types of mushrooms are known for their inedibility and should be avoided at all costs. | 近义词：non-edibility, uneatableness | 反义词：edibility | 词性：名词

词源：
From in- (not) + edible (eatable) + -ity (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Ultimately from Latin *edibilis (eatable) from edere (to eat).
