agora's:/ˈæɡərəz/
n.
阿戈拉的；集市的；广场的
Belonging to or associated with an agora, an open space used for assemblies and markets in ancient Greece.
The agora's bustling atmosphere was captivating.  集市熙熙攘攘的气氛令人着迷。
近义词:marketplace's, forum's
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) private's, secluded's
词性:Possessive noun (Noun with 's indicating possession)
