antiradars:/ˌæntiˈreɪdɑːrz/
n.
反雷达装置，反雷达措施
Devices or measures designed to counter the detection capabilities of radar systems.
The plane was equipped with antiradars to evade enemy detection.  这架飞机配备了反雷达装置以躲避敌人的侦察。
synonyms: radar countermeasures, electronic countermeasures (ECM)
antonyms: radar detectors
