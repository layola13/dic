nieces:/ˈniːsiz/ | n. |侄女；外甥女|A daughter of one's brother or sister, or of one's brother-in-law or sister-in-law.| My nieces are coming to visit me next week. 我的侄女们下周要来看我。 |Synonyms:  (no single perfect synonym, depends on context - could include relatives, family members, girls) |Antonyms: (no direct antonym) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *niece, *nece, from Latin neptis (“granddaughter, niece”), from PIE *népōts (“grandson, nephew”). Cognate with nephew. Doublet of neptis.
