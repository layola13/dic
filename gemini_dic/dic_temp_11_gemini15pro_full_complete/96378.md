indiscovery:/ˌɪndɪˈskʌvəri/
noun
未发现的状态; 未被发现
the state of not being discovered or known
例句：The tomb remained in indiscovery for centuries. 这座坟墓几个世纪以来一直未被发现。

例句：Many species of insects are still in indiscovery in the Amazon rainforest. 亚马逊雨林中还有许多昆虫种类未被发现。
近义词：obscurity, unknown, hidden, concealed
反义词：discovery, known, found, revealed
词性:n.
