coulis:/ˈkuːli/ /kuˈliː/| n.| 库利，酱汁，果泥|A thin, often strained, sauce made from puréed fruits or vegetables, typically served as a garnish or accompaniment to desserts or savory dishes.|The chef drizzled raspberry coulis over the cheesecake.  厨师在芝士蛋糕上淋了覆盆子酱。|The mango coulis added a vibrant touch to the panna cotta.芒果酱为意式奶冻增添了鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：sauce, purée, compote|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "thick paste")|noun
