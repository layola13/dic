cutouts:/ˈkʌtˌaʊts/| n.|剪纸；镂空图案；删节；(电影的)剪辑片段|Shapes or figures cut out of paper or other material; omitted sections; excised portions (as of a film).|The children made cutouts of snowflakes for their winter art project. 孩子们为他们的冬季艺术项目制作了雪花的剪纸。|The director decided to use cutouts from other films to create a montage effect. 导演决定使用其他电影的剪辑片段来营造蒙太奇效果。|近义词：shapes, figures, clippings, excerpts|反义词:inclusions|词性:noun
