gouache:/ɡuˈɑːʃ/ /ɡwɑːʃ/| n.|水粉画，水粉颜料|A method of painting using opaque pigments ground in water and thickened with a glue-like substance.|She prefers to work with gouache because of its vibrant colors. 她比较喜欢用水粉颜料作画，因为它颜色鲜艳。|近义词:watercolor, tempera, poster paint|反义词:acrylic, oil paint|名词
