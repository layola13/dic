buchu:/ˈbuːtʃuː/ | n. |布枯，香叶天竺葵|An aromatic South African shrub (Agathosma betulina or A. crenulata of the family Rutaceae) whose leaves are used medicinally. |Example: Buchu is traditionally used as a urinary antiseptic. 布枯传统上被用作泌尿防腐剂。|Example:  He made a tea with buchu leaves. 他用布枯叶泡了茶。|Synonym:  diosma| Antonym: N/A | noun
