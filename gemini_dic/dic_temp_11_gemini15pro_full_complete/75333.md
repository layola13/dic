frostfish:/ˈfrɒstfɪʃ/ (US) /ˈfrɒstfɪʃ/ (UK)| n.|冰鱼，霜鱼|A long, slender, scaleless fish found in shallow coastal waters, especially in New Zealand and Australia.  It is known for its translucent, gelatinous flesh.|The frostfish is a delicacy in some cultures. 霜鱼在某些文化中被认为是美味佳肴。|Synonyms: icefish, whitebait, noodlefish|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could consider fish with scales/firm flesh)|noun
