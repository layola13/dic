houseling:/ˈhaʊzəlɪŋ/
n.
圣餐;临终圣餐; (尤指临终前的)宗教仪式
the Christian sacrament of Holy Communion, especially when given to someone who is dying.
例句：He received houseling before he died. 他在去世前领受了圣餐。

例句：The priest administered houseling to the dying man.  神父给垂死的人举行临终圣礼。


近义词：communion, Eucharist, sacrament
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑亵渎：desecration, profanation)
词性: noun
