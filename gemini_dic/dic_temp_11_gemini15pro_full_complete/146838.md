overinclining:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈklaɪnɪŋ/
adjective
过度倾斜的
Having a tendency to incline or lean too much; excessively inclined.
The tower was overinclining dangerously after the earthquake. 地震后，塔楼危险地过度倾斜。
The overinclining tree threatened to fall on the house.  过度倾斜的树木有倒在房子上的危险。
近义词:leaning, tilting, sloping
反义词: upright, vertical
词性:adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "incline" (verb meaning "to lean or slope") + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
