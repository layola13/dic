beat-the-drum:/biːt ðə drʌm/| v.|大力宣传，摇旗呐喊|To actively promote or publicize something, often enthusiastically.|例句：The company is beating the drum for its new product.  该公司正在大力宣传其新产品。

例句：Politicians are beating the drum for education reform. 政客们正在大声疾呼教育改革。

近义词：promote, advocate, champion, publicize|反义词：suppress, conceal, downplay|词性：动词短语


beat:/biːt/| v.|敲打，打败|To strike repeatedly, to defeat.|例句：He beat the drum rhythmically. 他有节奏地敲鼓。

例句：Our team beat theirs in the final match.  我们的队伍在决赛中击败了他们的队伍。


the:/ðə, ðiː/| art.|定冠词|Definite article, used to refer to specific nouns.|例句：The drum is a percussion instrument.  鼓是一种打击乐器。

例句：The sun is shining brightly today. 今天阳光明媚。


drum:/drʌm/| n.|鼓|A percussion instrument, typically cylindrical, with a membrane stretched over one or both ends.|例句：He plays the drum in a band. 他在一个乐队里打鼓。

例句：The sound of the drums echoed through the valley. 鼓声在山谷中回荡。

词源：This idiom originates from the military practice of using drums to signal troops, announce events, and stir up enthusiasm. Over time, it evolved to mean promoting or advocating something vigorously.
