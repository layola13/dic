bur's:/bɜːrz/
n.
burrs, 毛刺，刺果，针尾草
A rough, prickly seed case or flower head that clings to clothing or animal fur.  Also, a rotary cutting tool.
例句：The bur's stuck to my socks.  毛刺粘在我的袜子上了。

例句：The dentist used a bur to remove the decay. 牙医用钻头去除龋齿。
近义词：prickles, thorns, barbs, burrs
反义词:smoothness

词性: noun (plural: burs or burrs)

词源: From Middle English *burre*, from Old Norse *burr*.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *burdą*, meaning "thorn, prickle."
