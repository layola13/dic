misassessment:/ˌmɪsəˈsɛsmənt/
n.
错误的评估；误判
An incorrect or faulty assessment or evaluation of something or someone.
The misassessment of the market led to significant financial losses. 对市场的错误评估导致了重大的经济损失。
misjudgment, misevaluation, wrong assessment, inaccurate assessment
correct assessment, accurate assessment, proper evaluation
noun
