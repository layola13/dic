ecologic:/ˌiːkəˈlɒdʒɪk/ /ˌiːkəˈlɑːdʒɪk/ | adj. | 生态的；生态学的 | Relating to ecology or the interrelationship of organisms and their environment. |  The ecologic balance of the forest was disturbed by the introduction of a new species.  森林的生态平衡因引进一个新物种而遭到破坏。|  近义词: ecological, environmental | 反义词: none | 词性: adjective

ecologic:/ˌiːkəˈlɒdʒɪk/ /ˌiːkəˈlɑːdʒɪk/ | n. | 生态学; 生态 | The study of the relationships between organisms and their environment. | Rarely used as a noun.  See "ecology" for noun usage. | 近义词: ecology, bionomics | 反义词: none | 词性: noun (rare)


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *écologique*, from Greek *oikos* ("house, dwelling") + *-logos* ("study").
