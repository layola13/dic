closets: /ˈklɒzɪts/ (British) /ˈklɑːzɪts/ (American) | n. | 壁橱，衣橱 | A small, enclosed space, often used for storage, especially for clothes. |  I hung my coat in the closet. 我把我的外套挂在壁橱里。| synonyms: wardrobe, cupboard, cabinet | antonyms: open space, exterior | noun

closets: /ˈklɒzɪts/ (British) /ˈklɑːzɪts/ (American) | adj. | 秘密的，不公开的 |  Hidden from view or knowledge; private. (This meaning is less common than the noun form.) |  He kept his political views closeted. 他对自己的政治观点秘而不宣。 | synonyms: secret, hidden, concealed | antonyms: open, public, revealed | adjective
