nonscarcity:/nɑːnˈskɛrsɪti/
n.
非稀缺性
The state or quality of not being scarce; abundance.
Due to technological advancements, information has reached a state of nonscarcity. 由于技术的进步，信息已经达到了非稀缺性的状态。
近义词:abundance, plentifulness, profusion
反义词:scarcity, lack, dearth
词性:名词

non-/nɑːn/
prefix
不，非
Meaning "not," freely used as an English formative, especially of adjectives and nouns.
Non-essential services were suspended during the lockdown.  非必要服务在封锁期间暂停。
近义词:not, un-
反义词:pro-,
词性:前缀

scarcity:/ˈskɛrsɪti/
n.
稀缺性；不足；缺乏
The state of being scarce or in short supply; shortage.
The scarcity of water in the desert made survival difficult. 沙漠中水的稀缺使得生存变得困难。
近义词:lack, shortage, dearth
反义词:abundance, plenty, surplus
词性:名词
