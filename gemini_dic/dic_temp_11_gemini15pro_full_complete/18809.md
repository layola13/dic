狂暴，狂怒状态(单数)
A state of violent or destructive rage or frenzy.
His berserkness frightened everyone. 他的狂暴吓坏了所有人。
近义词：frenzy, rage, fury, madness
反义词：calmness, tranquility, peacefulness
词性：名词



词源: From Old Norse *berserksr, meaning "bear-shirt" or "bear-skin," referring to fierce warriors who wore bear skins in battle.  The word entered English in the 19th century.
