analogic:/ˌænəˈlɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 相似的，类比的 | Based on analogy; expressing or implying analogy. |  This analogic reasoning helps us understand complex systems. 这种类比推理帮助我们理解复杂系统。|  The teacher used an analogic example to explain the concept. 老师用一个类比的例子来解释这个概念。 | similar, analogous, comparable, parallel | dissimilar, different, unlike | adjective

analogical:/ˌænəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 相似的，类比的 | Based on analogy; expressing or implying analogy. | This analogical argument is based on the similarities between the two cases. 这个类比论证是基于两个案例之间的相似性。 | The lawyer presented an analogical case to support his argument. 律师提出了一个类似的案例来支持他的论点。| similar, analogous, comparable, parallel | dissimilar, different, unlike | adjective


词源: Late 16th century: from French analogique, or via late Latin from Greek analogikos, from analogia ‘proportion’ (see analogy).
