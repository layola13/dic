junkiest:/ˈdʒʌŋkiɪst/| adj.|最破烂的；质量最差的；最劣质的|Superlative form of junky: most junky.  It refers to something of extremely poor quality, often old, broken, or useless.|That's the junkiest car I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最破的车。|The junkiest part of town was filled with abandoned buildings. 城里最破败的地方到处都是废弃的建筑。|Near synonyms:  ramshackle, dilapidated, broken-down, beat-up, shabby, trashy|Near antonyms:  pristine, immaculate, high-quality, top-of-the-line|adjective
