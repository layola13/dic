houthis:/ˈhuːθiːz/
noun
胡塞武装;也门胡塞运动
Members of a predominantly Zaidi Shia Muslim group called Ansar Allah (Partisans of God), also known as the Houthi movement.  They are based in northern Yemen and have been involved in conflicts with the Yemeni government and other groups.
The Houthis seized control of Sana'a, Yemen's capital. 胡塞武装夺取了也门首都萨那的控制权。
The Houthis launched a missile attack. 胡塞武装发动了导弹袭击。
synonyms: Ansar Allah, Houthi rebels, Houthi movement
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps opposing forces in the Yemeni conflict, such as the Saudi-led coalition)
