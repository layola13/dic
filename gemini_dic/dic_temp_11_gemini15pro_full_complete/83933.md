half-civilly:/ˌhɑːfˈsɪvɪlli/
| adv. |半礼貌地，勉强客气地|Partly civilly; with a mixture of civility and rudeness.  Showing some politeness, but not completely. |He responded half-civilly, muttering a grudging "thank you." 他勉强客气地回应，咕哝着说了声“谢谢”。|例句：She gave a half-civilly nod and turned away. 她勉强客气地点了点头，然后转身离开了。|近义词：curtly, perfunctorily, grudgingly|反义词：politely, courteously, graciously|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
"half-" (Old English healf) + "civilly" (from "civil" - Latin civilis, meaning "relating to citizens," from civis "citizen")
