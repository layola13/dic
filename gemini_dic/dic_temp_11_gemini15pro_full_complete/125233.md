multicultures:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərz/| n. |多元文化|The existence of multiple cultures within a society.|例句：Many universities strive to create vibrant multicultures on their campuses. 许多大学致力于在校园内创造充满活力的多元文化。

例句：The city is known for its thriving multicultures, attracting people from all over the world.  这座城市以其繁荣的多元文化而闻名，吸引了来自世界各地的人们。

|近义词：cultural diversity, multiculturalism|反义词: monoculture|词性:名词
