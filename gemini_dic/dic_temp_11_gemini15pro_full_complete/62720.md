{engl} = what
/wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) |pron. det. adv. int.|什么，多么，|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|Synonyms: which, how much, |Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, depends on context)|pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? = 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done since she arrived? = 她自从到达以来做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie started? = 电影开始前他们吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 到今天结束时你将完成什么？
What will they have achieved by next year? = 到明年他们将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习什么五年了？
What will she have been working on by the time she retires? = 到她退休时，她将一直从事什么工作？
