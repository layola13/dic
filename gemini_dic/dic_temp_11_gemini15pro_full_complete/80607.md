goon:/ɡuːn/| n.| 暴徒; 愚笨的人; 受雇打手; (尤指受雇扰乱罢工的)打手| a stupid, foolish, or awkward person; a thug or hired muscle, especially one hired to disrupt strikes. |例句：Those goons were hired to intimidate the strikers. 那些暴徒是被雇来恐吓罢工者的。|近义词：thug, hoodlum, ruffian, lout, idiot, fool|反义词:hero, genius|词性:noun

goon:/ɡuːn/| v.| (尤指愚蠢地)继续做，持续| to continue; to keep on doing something, especially in a stupid or annoying way|例句：He just gooned on about his problems. 他只是不停地唠叨他的问题。|近义词：continue, persist, carry on|反义词:stop, cease|词性:verb
