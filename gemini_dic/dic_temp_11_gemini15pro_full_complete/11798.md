arrangeable:/əˈreɪndʒəbl/
adjective
可安排的，可整理的
Capable of being arranged or put in order; adaptable.
The flowers are arrangeable in many different ways. 这些花可以以许多不同的方式排列。
近义词: adaptable, manageable, flexible, adjustable
反义词: unmanageable, inflexible, fixed, rigid
词性: adjective

arrangeable:/əˈreɪndʒəbl/
adjective
可安排的，可筹备的
Capable of being planned or organized.
The wedding date is arrangeable. 婚礼日期可以安排。
近义词: plannable, organizable, schedulable
反义词: unplannable, non-negotiable


词源:  From arrange + -able.  "Arrange" ultimately comes from the Old French "arengier," meaning "to put in a rank or row."
