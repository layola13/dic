foreword:/ˈfɔːrwɜːrd/| n.|前言，序言|An introductory note or essay, as in a book, typically not by the author.|The book has a foreword by the author's former teacher.|近义词：preface, introduction, prolog|反义词:afterword, epilogue|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *foreword*, from Old English *foreword* ("preface, introduction"), from *fore* ("before") + *word* ("word, speech"). Equivalent to *fore- +‎ word*.
