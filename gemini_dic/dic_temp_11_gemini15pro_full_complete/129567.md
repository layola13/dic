non-challenging:/ˌnɒnˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective
不具挑战性的；容易的
Not demanding or stimulating; easy.
The hike was non-challenging and suitable for beginners.  这次徒步旅行没有什么挑战性，适合初学者。
The course was non-challenging, even for the younger students. 这门课程即使对年纪小的学生来说也很容易。
Synonyms: easy, simple, undemanding, straightforward, effortless
Antonyms: challenging, difficult, demanding, strenuous, arduous
詞性: adjective
