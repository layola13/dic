commonsense:/ˈkɒmənˌsɛns/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmənˌsɛns/ (US)| n. |常识；情理|Good judgment in everyday matters; sound practical judgment.|She showed a lot of common sense in handling the situation. 她处理这种情况时表现出了很多常识。|近义词：common knowledge, practical wisdom, good sense, levelheadedness|反义词：foolishness, stupidity, absurdity|noun

commonsensical:/ˌkɒmənˈsɛnskɪkəl/ (UK), /ˌkɑːmənˈsɛnsɪkəl/ (US)| adj. |符合常识的；明智的|Exhibiting or characterized by good sense.|It seems commonsensical to wear a helmet when riding a bike. 骑自行车时戴头盔似乎是常识。|近义词：sensible, practical, wise, prudent|反义词：foolish, silly, impractical, unwise|adjective
