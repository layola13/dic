crystal-like:/ˈkrɪstəlˌlaɪk/
adj.
像水晶的，晶莹剔透的
Resembling crystal in appearance or properties; clear and sparkling.
The lake had crystal-like water.  湖水清澈透明，像水晶一样。
Her eyes were crystal-like. 她的眼睛晶莹剔透。
近义词: crystalline, glassy, transparent, clear, limpid
反义词: opaque, cloudy, murky, dull
