dharnas:/ˈdɑːrnəz/ (US), /ˈdɑːnəz/ (UK)| n. | 静坐示威，静坐抗议 | A form of non-violent protest in which participants sit and refuse to move until their demands are met. Derived from the Hindi word "धारना" (dharnā), meaning "to sit."  It originated in India and is often used in South Asia. | Example 1: The farmers organized dharnas to protest the new agricultural laws. 农民们组织静坐示威抗议新的农业法。

Example 2: The dharnas continued for weeks, disrupting traffic and daily life. 静坐示威持续了数周，扰乱了交通和日常生活。 | 近义词: sit-in, protest, demonstration | 反义词:  compliance, obedience | 词性: 名词
