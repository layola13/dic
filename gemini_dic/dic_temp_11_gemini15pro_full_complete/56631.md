donkey:/ˈdɒŋki/ (UK) /ˈdɑːŋki/ (US)| n.|驴；傻瓜；顽固的人|A domesticated hoofed mammal of the horse family, typically smaller than a horse and with longer ears and a braying call.  Figuratively, it can also refer to a stubborn or foolish person.|The farmer used a donkey to carry his goods. 农民用驴来驮运货物。|

He's such a donkey, he never listens to advice. 他真是个傻瓜，从来不听劝。|近义词：ass, mule, fool, idiot|反义词:genius, scholar|noun
