一般现在时 (Simple Present):
不适用 been

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
不适用 been

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have been busy all day. 我一整天都很忙。
She has been to Japan twice. 她去过日本两次。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
They have been studying English for three years. 他们学习英语已经三年了。
We have been waiting for the bus for an hour. 我们等公交车已经等了一个小时了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
不适用 been

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
不适用 been


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had been to London before he moved to New York. 在他搬到纽约之前，他去过伦敦。
She had been working at the company for five years before she quit.  她在辞职之前，在那家公司工作了五年。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been playing the piano for an hour when the phone rang. 电话响的时候，我一直在弹钢琴已经一个小时了。
They had been arguing for hours before they finally reached a compromise. 在最终达成妥协之前，他们已经争论了好几个小时。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
不适用 been

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
不适用 been

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have been living here for ten years. 到明年，我将已经在这里住了十年了。
By the time you arrive, she will have been working for six hours. 到你到达的时候，她将已经工作了六个小时。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By the end of the year, we will have been traveling for three months. 到年底，我们将已经旅行三个月了。
By next summer, he will have been studying French for five years. 到明年夏天，他将已经学习法语五年了。

虚拟语气:
If I had been there, I would have helped. 如果我当时在那里，我就会帮忙了。
I wish I had been more careful. 我希望我当时更小心一些。


被动语态:
The house has been painted blue. 房子已经被漆成蓝色了。
The cake had been eaten before I arrived.  在我到达之前，蛋糕已经被吃掉了。


疑问句:
Have you been to the museum? 你去过博物馆吗？
Where have you been all day? 你一整天都去哪儿了？


否定句:
I haven't been feeling well lately. 我最近感觉不太好。
She hadn't been to the beach before. 她以前没去过海滩。
