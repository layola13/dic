angelicize:/ænˈdʒɛlɪsaɪz/
verb
使英国化;赋予英国风格;使像英国人;给...起英国名字;净化;使圣洁;理想化
To make English or more English in form or character; to give an English form or appearance to.
Many immigrants anglicized their names to fit in better. 许多移民将他们的名字英国化，以便更好地融入社会。
The spelling of many foreign words has been anglicized over time. 许多外来词的拼写随着时间的推移已被英国化。

synonyms: anglify,  Englishize
antonyms:  localize,  nationalize
