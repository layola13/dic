disrobes:/dɪsˈroʊbz/
v.|脱去…的衣服，使…脱衣|to remove clothes from someone or something; to undress|The king disrobed in his private chamber.|近义词：undress, strip, divest|反义词：dress, clothe, robe
词性: 动词


disrobes:/dɪsˈroʊbz/
n.|脱衣，脱光衣服的行为|the act of removing one's clothes or another's clothes|The disrobes of the priest before the ceremony was a solemn ritual.|近义词：undressing, stripping|反义词：dressing, clothing, robing
词性: 名词 (较少使用)
