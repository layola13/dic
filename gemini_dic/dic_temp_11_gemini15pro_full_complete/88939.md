holdout:/ˈhoʊldaʊt/| n.|拒不妥协者；抵抗者；坚持者|a person, group, or thing that refuses to agree, surrender, or participate with others.|The last holdouts finally surrendered after a long siege.  最后的抵抗者在长期围困后最终投降了。|synonyms: resister, dissenter, objector, nonconformist, diehard|antonyms: conformist, cooperator, collaborator|noun

holdout:/ˈhoʊldaʊt/| n.|坚持；抵抗；拒不合作|the act of refusing to agree, surrender, or participate.|The holdout against the new development lasted for months.  对新开发项目的抵制持续了数月。|synonyms: resistance, opposition, defiance|antonyms: cooperation, compliance, agreement|noun
