individualizations:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuəlɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |个体化，个性化|The process of making something specific to an individual or individuals; the act of adapting something to meet the particular needs or characteristics of different people.|例句：Individualizations of treatment plans are essential for effective patient care.  针对个体患者的治疗方案对于有效的病人护理至关重要。

|例句：The company offers individualizations of its products to meet diverse customer preferences.  公司提供产品的个性化定制以满足不同的客户偏好。|近义词：personalizations, customizations, adaptations|反义词：standardization, uniformity|词性:noun
