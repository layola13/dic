corruptive:/kəˈrʌptɪv/
adjective
腐败的；堕落的；贪污的
tending to corrupt; having the power to corrupt or make morally depraved.
例句：The corruptive influence of power can be difficult to resist. 权力带来的腐败影响可能难以抗拒。
例句：He was accused of corruptive practices while in office. 他被指控在任期间有不法行为。
近义词：corrupting, demoralizing, debasing, pernicious, harmful
反义词：uplifting, wholesome, beneficial, constructive
词性: adjective
