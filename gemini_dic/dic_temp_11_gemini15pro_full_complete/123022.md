monastics:/məˈnæstɪks/
n.|修道士，僧侣，修道生活|People who live a monastic life dedicate themselves to religious pursuits.  过着修道生活的人致力于宗教追求。|例句：The monastics lived a life of prayer and contemplation. 修道士们过着祈祷和沉思的生活。|近义词：monks, nuns, religious, clergy|反义词:laity, secular|Noun


monastic:/məˈnæstɪk/
adj.|修道的，僧侣的，隐居的|Related to or characteristic of monks or nuns or their way of life. 与僧侣或修女或他们的生活方式有关或具有其特征。|例句：The monastic community provided a sanctuary from the outside world. 修道院提供了一个远离外界的避难所。|近义词：cloistered, ascetic, contemplative, secluded|反义词:worldly, secular|Adjective
