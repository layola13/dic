bahaism: /ˈbɑːhaɪɪzəm/ /ˈbeɪhaɪɪzəm/
n.
巴哈伊教
A monotheistic religion emphasizing the spiritual unity of all humankind.  It teaches the essential worth of all religions, and the unity and equality of all people.
Bahaism promotes world peace and understanding.  巴哈伊教提倡世界和平与理解。
近义词: Baha'i Faith
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially other religions depending on context)
词性: noun


baha'i: /bəˈhɑːiː/ /bəˈheɪiː/
n. adj.
巴哈伊信徒；巴哈伊的
A follower of Bahaism; relating to Bahaism.
He is a Baha'i. 他是一位巴哈伊信徒。
The Baha'i temple is beautiful. 巴哈伊寺庙很漂亮。
近义词: Bahaist (less common)
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun, adjective
