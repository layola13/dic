fielder: /ˈfiːldər/ (US) /ˈfiːldə(r)/ (UK)| n. |守场员，外野手|A player in a sport, especially baseball or cricket, who is positioned in the field to catch or stop the ball. |The fielder caught the fly ball.  外野手接住了飞球。|The center fielder made a diving catch. 中外野手飞身接球。|Synonyms: outfielder, infielder, player |Antonyms: batter, pitcher |noun
