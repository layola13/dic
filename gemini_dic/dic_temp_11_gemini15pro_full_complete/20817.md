blast-it:/ˈblæst ɪt/ | int. | 该死！；见鬼！；真糟糕！| An exclamation of annoyance or frustration.  Expresses anger or impatience. Derived from "blast," meaning to explode or curse, intensified with "it" for emphasis.| Blast it! I forgot my keys again!  该死！我又忘了带钥匙了！|Blast it! The train is delayed. 真糟糕！火车晚点了。|Synonyms: damn it, darn it, shoot,  |Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for exclamations of this nature) |interjection
