melaniline: /məˈlænɪlɪn/ | n. |  苯胺紫；苯胺红 | A synthetic purple dye, also known as mauveine, historically significant as the first synthetic organic dye.  It's derived from aniline. |  Melaniline was accidentally discovered while trying to synthesize quinine. 苯胺紫是在试图合成奎宁时偶然发现的。|  The discovery of melaniline revolutionized the dye industry. 苯胺紫的发现彻底改变了染料工业。 | Synonyms: mauveine, aniline purple, Perkin's mauve | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
