declassification:/diːˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |解密，解除机密|The declassification of the documents revealed a cover-up. 这些文件的解密揭露了一桩掩盖事件。|例句：The declassification of the files after fifty years revealed some surprising information.  五十年后这些文件的解密揭露了一些令人惊讶的信息。

例句：The government announced the declassification of several top-secret documents. 政府宣布解密几份绝密文件。

近义词：disclosure, release|反义词:classification, secrecy|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + classification.  The "de-" prefix comes from Latin, while "classification" ultimately derives from Latin "classis" (class) and "-ficare" (to make).
