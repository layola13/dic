illuminators:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪtərz/
n.
照明器；照明者；阐明者
A person or thing that illuminates something.  Something that provides light.  Someone who makes something clearer or easier to understand.
The illuminators of the manuscript used gold leaf to create a dazzling effect.  手稿的照明者使用金箔来制造耀眼的效果。
The streetlights are the primary illuminators of the city at night.  路灯是夜晚城市的主要照明器。
The professor's lectures were illuminating, making complex topics easy to grasp.  教授的讲座很有启发性，使复杂的主题易于理解。
synonyms: lights, lamps, clarifiers, expositors
antonyms: obscurers, dimmers, confounders
noun
