canaliculus:/ˌkænəˈlɪkjʊləs/| n.|小管，细管|A small channel or duct, especially within the body, such as those in bone or cartilage.|例句：The canaliculi in bone allow for the passage of nutrients and waste products. 骨中的小管允许营养物质和废物的通过。

例句：Tears drain through the lacrimal canaliculi into the lacrimal sac.  眼泪通过泪小管流入泪囊。|近义词：duct, channel, tubule|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

canaliculi:/ˌkænəˈlɪkjʊlaɪ/| n.|小管，细管（复数）|Plural form of canaliculus, referring to multiple small channels or ducts.|例句：The dentist cleaned the dentinal canaliculi. 牙医清洁了牙本质小管。

例句： Blockage of the canaliculi can lead to various health issues. 小管阻塞会导致各种健康问题。|近义词：ducts, channels, tubules|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
