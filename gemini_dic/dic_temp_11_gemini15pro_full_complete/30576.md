ceratosaurus:/ˌsɛrətoʊˈsɔːrəs/| n. |角鼻龙|A large predatory dinosaur of the late Jurassic period, characterized by a horn on its nose and two smaller horns above its eyes.  It belongs to the Theropoda clade.|The Ceratosaurus was a fearsome predator. 角鼻龙是一个可怕的掠食者。|The museum displayed a complete Ceratosaurus skeleton.  博物馆展出了一具完整的角鼻龙骨架。|近义词:dinosaur, theropod|反义词:None (It's a specific type of dinosaur, so it doesn't have a direct opposite)|词性:noun
