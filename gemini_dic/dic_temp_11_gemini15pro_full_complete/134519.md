noun
无性欲，性冷淡
lack of sexual desire; absence of libidinousness.
She experienced a period of nonlibidinousness after the traumatic event.  她在那次创伤事件后经历了一段时间的性冷淡。

Antonyms: libidinousness, lustfulness, lasciviousness
