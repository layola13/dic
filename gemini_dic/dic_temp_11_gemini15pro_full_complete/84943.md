hanun: /ˈhɑːnʊn/ | n. | 哈努语 |  Hanunó'o script, also formerly Hanunoo, is an indigenous Indic script used by the Hanunó'o Mangyan people of southern Mindoro in the Philippines. | The Hanunó'o script is traditionally written on bamboo using a knife. | synonyms:  Hanunó'o script | antonyms: N/A | noun
