lairdocracy: /ˈlɛərdɒkrəsi/ | n. | 领主统治；地主政治 | A system of government or rule by landed aristocracy or lairds (especially in Scotland). |  The historian argued that the 18th-century Highlands were marked by a rigid lairdocracy. 这位历史学家认为，18世纪的高地地区的特点是严格的地主政治。

The clan system and lairdocracy often led to conflicts over land and resources.  氏族制度和地主政治经常导致土地和资源冲突。 | Near synonyms: aristocracy, landed gentry, oligarchy | Antonyms: democracy, republic | Noun
