capitative:/ˈkæpɪteɪtɪv/
adjective
按人头收费的；人均的
relating to or involving a fixed payment per person
例句：The hospital receives a capitative payment for each patient enrolled in the health plan.  这家医院对参加健康计划的每位病人收取固定费用。

例句：Capitative healthcare systems incentivize providers to focus on preventative care. 按人头收费的医疗保健系统激励医疗服务提供者专注于预防性护理。

近义词：per capita, per head
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider fee-for-service)
词性: adjective
