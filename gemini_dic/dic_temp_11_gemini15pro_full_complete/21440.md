blowhole:/ˈbloʊˌhoʊl/| n.|喷水孔；通风孔|A nostril in the top of the head of a whale, dolphin, or other cetacean, through which air is exhaled.|We saw a whale spout water from its blowhole. 我们看到一条鲸鱼从它的喷水孔喷出水来。|Synonyms: nostril, vent, spiracle|Antonyms: N/A|noun

blowhole:/ˈbloʊˌhoʊl/| n.|（岩石的）气孔，通风孔|A hole in the roof of a coastal cave, through which sea water and spray are forced by the action of waves.|The waves crashed against the rocks, sending spray high into the air through the blowhole. 海浪拍打着岩石，水花从气孔中喷涌而出。|Synonyms: vent, orifice, opening|Antonyms: N/A|noun
