magnitude:/ˈmæɡnɪtjuːd/
n.
大小；量级；震级；重要性；重要；巨大
Greatness of size, extent, or importance.

例句：
They don't seem to grasp the magnitude of the problem.
他们似乎没有意识到这个问题的严重性。

The earthquake had a magnitude of 7.2 on the Richter scale.
这次地震的里氏震级为7.2级。


近义词：size, extent, importance, significance, greatness, immensity, vastness
反义词：insignificance, smallness, unimportance

词性：名词
