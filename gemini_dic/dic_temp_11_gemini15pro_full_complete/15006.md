babelize:/ˈbeɪbəlaɪz/| v. |使言语混乱，使(语言)变得像婴儿的呓语，使变成巴别塔式的混乱|To confuse or bewilder with a multitude of voices or sounds; to cause a confusion of languages, like that at the Tower of Babel.|The construction site was babelized with the shouts of workers in different languages.  建筑工地上的工人们说着不同的语言，一片混乱。|The sudden influx of tourists babelized the small, quiet town. 突然涌入的游客让这个安静的小镇变得喧闹混乱。|近义词：confuse, bewilder, confound|反义词：clarify, simplify|词性:动词
