nondetachment:/ˌnɒndɪˈtætʃmənt/ | n. |  无执着；不执着；超脱 |  The state of not being emotionally attached to something or someone.  The ability to remain objective and not be influenced by personal feelings or desires. |  He practiced nondetachment to worldly possessions. 他对世俗 possessions practiced nondetachment. |  detachment, objectivity, impartiality, neutrality | attachment, dependence,  partiality, bias | noun
