chromophotograph:/ˈkroʊməˌfoʊtəˌɡræf/| n.|彩色照片|A photograph in color.|例句：The museum displayed a chromophotograph of a Victorian street scene. 博物馆展出了一张维多利亚时代街景的彩色照片。

例句：Early chromophotographs were often less vibrant than modern color photographs. 早期的彩色照片通常不如现代彩色照片鲜艳。|近义词：color photograph, colour photograph|反义词:monochrome photograph|词性:noun
