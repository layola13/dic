dividingly:/dɪˈvaɪdɪŋli/
adverb
分裂地，造成不和地
in a way that causes disagreement or hostility between people.
The issue of immigration has divided the country dividingly. 移民问题分裂了这个国家，造成了严重的不和。
The politician's rhetoric dividingly split public opinion.  这位政客的言辞分裂了公众舆论，造成了分歧。
近义词: controversially, disruptively, factiously
反义词: unifyingly, harmoniously
词性: adverb

词源: From divide + -ing + -ly.  "Divide" comes from the Latin "dividēre," meaning "to force apart."
