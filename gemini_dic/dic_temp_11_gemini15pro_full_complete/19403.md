bicentenary:/ˌbaɪsɛnˈtɛnəri/ | n. adj.| 二百周年纪念; 二百年的 |A bicentenary is the two-hundredth anniversary of a significant event. | The city is celebrating its bicentenary this year.  这个城市今年正在庆祝其二百周年纪念。 |近义词：bicentennial |反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially centenary/centennial) |词性: noun, adjective

bicentenary:/ˌbaɪsɛnˈtɛnəri/ | adj.| 二百年的 |  Relating to a period of 200 years. | The bicentenary celebrations lasted for a week. 为期一周的二百周年庆典。 | 近义词：bicentennial |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + centenary (a period of 100 years). Ultimately from Latin centum (hundred) and annus (year).
