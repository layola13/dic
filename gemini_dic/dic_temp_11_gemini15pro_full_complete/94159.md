impenetrability:/ɪmˌpɛnɪtrəˈbɪlɪti/
n.|不可渗透性；难以理解|The impenetrability of the jungle made it difficult to explore.  这片丛林的不可渗透性使得它难以被探索。|The impenetrability of his argument made it hard to follow. 他的论证难以理解，很难跟得上。|impermeability, opacity, impassability|penetrability, accessibility, understandability|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impenetrabilitas, from impenetrabilis (“impenetrable”) + -itas (“-ity”).  Impenetrabilis is from in- (“not”) + penetrabilis (“penetrable”), from penetrare (“to penetrate”), from penitus (“within”).
