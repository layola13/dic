normie:/ˈnɔːrmi/| n.|普通人，常人；缺乏独特兴趣或个性的人|A normie is a person who is perceived as conventional or boring, especially in terms of their interests, tastes, and lifestyle. They are often seen as following mainstream trends and lacking unique or individual characteristics.|例句：He felt like a normie at the comic convention, surrounded by people in elaborate costumes. 他觉得自己在漫展上像个普通人，周围都是穿着精致服装的人。

例句：She's such a normie; she only listens to pop music and watches reality TV. 她真是个普通人；她只听流行音乐，看真人秀。|近义词：basic, conventional, mainstream|反义词：hipster, nonconformist, alternative|词性:名词
