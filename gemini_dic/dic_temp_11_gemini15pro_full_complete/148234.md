oversimplified:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪd/| adj.|过度简化的|Simplified to such an extent that a distorted impression is given.|例句：The explanation of the complex political situation was oversimplified.  对复杂政治局势的解释过于简化了。

例句：His oversimplified view of history ignores many important factors. 他对历史过于简化的看法忽略了许多重要因素。|近义词：reductive, simplistic, shallow|反义词: nuanced, complex, sophisticated|词性:形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "simplified" (past participle of "simplify," from "simple" + "-fy").  Ultimately from Latin "simplex" (single, simple).
