abstractively:/æbˈstræktɪvli/
adv.
抽象地，理论上
Thinking or considering something in a theoretical way, without reference to concrete instances.
She thought about the problem abstractively, ignoring the specific details. 她抽象地思考这个问题，忽略了具体细节。

She spoke abstractively about love and loss. 她抽象地谈论了爱与失去。

近义词:theoretically, conceptually, ideally, generally
反义词:concretely, specifically, practically, empirically
词性:副词
