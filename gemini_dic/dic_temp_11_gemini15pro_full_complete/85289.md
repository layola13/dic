harmonized:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪzd/ | /ˈhɑːrməˌnaɪzd/ | adj. | 和谐的，协调的 | brought into agreement or made compatible | The international regulations have been harmonized to facilitate trade.  国际规则已协调一致，以促进贸易。| The different musical styles were harmonized in the composition.  不同的音乐风格在这首作品中得到了和谐统一。 |  synonyms: coordinated, unified, consistent, reconciled, integrated | antonyms: discordant, conflicting, dissonant, incompatible | adjective

harmonize:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪz/ | /ˈhɑːrməˌnaɪz/ | v. | 使和谐，协调 | to bring into agreement or make compatible |  The government is trying to harmonize its policies with those of its neighbors. 政府正在努力使其政策与邻国协调一致。 | We need to harmonize our efforts to achieve our goals. 我们需要协调我们的努力来实现我们的目标。 | synonyms: coordinate, unify, reconcile, integrate, blend, synthesize | antonyms: clash, conflict, disagree, contrast | verb
