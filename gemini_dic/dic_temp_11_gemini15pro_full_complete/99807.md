intermigrate:/ˌɪntərˈmaɪɡreɪt/ | v. |互相迁移，不同群体之间互相迁移 | To migrate between different groups or areas, often within a larger region. | Example 1: The two tribes intermigrated, sharing resources and cultural practices.  两个部落互相迁移，共享资源和文化习俗。

Example 2:  Bird populations often intermigrate between different habitats depending on the season.  鸟类种群经常根据季节在不同的栖息地之间迁移。 | 近义词:  migrate, move, relocate | 反义词:  stay, remain | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + migrate.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "migrate" means "to move from one place to another."
