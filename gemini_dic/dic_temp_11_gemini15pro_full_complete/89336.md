homographic:/ˌhɒməˈɡræfɪk/ |adj.|同形异义的|Having the same spelling as another word but a different meaning and origin.|The words "lead" (the metal) and "lead" (to guide) are homographic.|近义词:homonymous|反义词:heterographic|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *homo-* (same) + *-graphos* (written)
