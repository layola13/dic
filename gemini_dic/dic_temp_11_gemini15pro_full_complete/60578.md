elated:/ɪˈleɪtɪd/
adjective
兴高采烈的；喜气洋洋的
Feeling or expressing great happiness or triumph.
She was elated at the news of her success. 她听到自己成功的消息欣喜若狂。
近义词：joyful, jubilant, ecstatic, thrilled
反义词：depressed, dejected, downcast
词性:adjective


elated:/ɪˈleɪtɪd/
Etymology:  From Latin elatus, past participle of efferre (“to carry out, bring forth”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + ferre (“to bear, carry”).  The sense evolution is from "lifted up" to "spiritually uplifted" to "joyful."
中文翻译：兴高采烈的；喜气洋洋的
英文解析：Feeling or expressing great happiness or triumph.
例句：
He was elated by his team's victory. 他为团队的胜利而兴高采烈。
The children were elated to go on a field trip. 孩子们对要去实地考察感到兴高采烈。
近义词： overjoyed, thrilled, ecstatic, jubilant
反义词： despondent, dejected, depressed, miserable
词性: adjective
