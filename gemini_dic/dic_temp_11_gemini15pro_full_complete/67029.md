explainer:/ɪkˈspleɪnər/| n.| 解释者，说明者，讲解员| someone or something that explains something | The museum hired an explainer to guide visitors through the exhibit.  博物馆聘请了一名讲解员引导游客参观展览。| synonyms: commentator, analyst, interpreter | antonyms: obfuscator, confuser | noun


explainer:/ɪkˈspleɪnər/| n.| 解释性视频（或文章）| a video or piece of writing that explains a complex issue in a simple way | The news website published an explainer on the new tax laws.  新闻网站发布了一篇关于新税法的解释性文章。| synonyms:  exposition, elucidation, summary | antonyms: complication, mystification | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From explain + -er.  Explain comes from the Late Middle English *explanen*, from Old French *esplaner*, from Latin *explanare* "to make level, explain," from *ex- "out" + *planare "to make plain."
