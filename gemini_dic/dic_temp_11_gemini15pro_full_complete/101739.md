irresistibly:/ɪˌrɪˈzɪstəbli/ | adv. | 不可抗拒地 |  In a way that is so strong, attractive, or appealing that you cannot prevent yourself from being affected. | The warm, sandy beach was irresistibly inviting.  温暖的沙滩令人无法抗拒。 | Synonyms: overwhelmingly, compellingly, powerfully, unavoidably | Antonyms: resistibly, weakly | word type: adverb


irresistible:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbl/ | adj. | 不可抗拒的 | Too attractive and tempting to be resisted. |  The chocolate cake was irresistible. 巧克力蛋糕太诱人了，无法抗拒。| Synonyms: tempting, alluring, enticing, captivating | Antonyms: resistible, unappealing, unattractive | word type: adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *irresistibilis, from in- "not" + resistere "to resist"  + -ibilis "-ible".
