acetabuliferous:/ˌæsɪˌtæbjʊˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
具髋臼的；有髋臼的
Bearing acetabula (the cup-shaped sockets of the hip joints).
例句：The acetabuliferous dinosaur left distinctive footprints.  这种有髋臼的恐龙留下了独特的足迹。

例句：Acetabuliferous structures are crucial for the movement of many animals. 髋臼结构对于许多动物的运动至关重要。
