eozoic:/ˌiːəˈzoʊɪk/
adjective
始生代的
Relating to or denoting the Eozoic era, the earliest era of the Precambrian eon.
The eozoic rocks contain few fossils. 始生代的岩石几乎不含化石。
近义词:Precambrian
反义词:Cenozoic
词性:adjective
