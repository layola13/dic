overconcentrating: /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度集中；过于专注 | Focusing too intensely on one thing to the detriment of others. |  He was so overconcentrating on the game that he didn't hear the phone ring. 他过于专注于游戏，以至于没有听到电话响。| 近义词: fixating, obsessing | 反义词: relaxing, neglecting | 词性: 动词 (present participle)

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "concentrating" (present participle of "concentrate"), from Latin "concentrare" (to bring to a center), from "com-" (with, together) + "centrum" (center).
