middle-ranking:/ˈmɪdl ˈræŋkɪŋ/
adjective
中等职位的；中级的
Holding a position or status that is neither very high nor very low within a hierarchy.
He's a middle-ranking officer in the army. 他是军队中一名中级军官。
Synonyms: mid-level, intermediate
Antonyms: senior, junior, top-ranking, low-ranking
词性: 形容词
