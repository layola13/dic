anti-populism:/ˌæntiˈpɒpjʊlɪzəm/ /ˌæntiˈpɑːpjəlɪzəm/
| n. | 反民粹主义 | Opposition to populism; a political stance that rejects populist rhetoric and policies. | The rise of anti-populism is a reaction against the simplistic solutions offered by populist leaders. 反民粹主义的兴起是对民粹主义领导人提出的简单化解决方案的一种反弹。| near synonyms: anti-demagoguery | antonyms: populism | noun
