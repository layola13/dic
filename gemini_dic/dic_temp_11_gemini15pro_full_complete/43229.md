crackers:/ˈkrækərz/ (US), /ˈkrækəz/ (UK)| n.|薄脆饼干；鞭炮|Thin, crisp biscuits; firecrackers|例句：I like to eat cheese and crackers. 我喜欢吃奶酪和饼干。

例句：The children were excited to light the firecrackers. 孩子们对点燃鞭炮感到兴奋。|近义词：biscuits, snacks (for food); firecrackers, fireworks (for explosives)|反义词:(No direct antonym, depends on context)|词性:名词

词源：From Middle Dutch *kraken, frequentative of kraken  "to crack, break," from Proto-Germanic *krakōną "to make a sharp, sudden noise."

注意事项: cracker作为“鞭炮”的用法主要在美国英语中常见。在英国英语中，firecracker 更为常用。
