faradism:/ˈfærədɪzəm/ | n. |法拉第疗法；感应电疗法 |A form of electrotherapy in which faradic current is used to stimulate muscles and nerves.|Doctors used faradism to treat her muscle atrophy. 医生用法拉第疗法治疗她的肌肉萎缩。|Synonym: electrotherapy, galvanism|Antonym: None|noun

faradic:/fəˈrædɪk/ | adj. |法拉第的；感应电流的|Relating to or induced by faradic current.|Faradic stimulation can help improve muscle strength. 法拉第刺激可以帮助提高肌肉力量。|Synonym: galvanic|Antonym: None|adjective
