mutilated: /ˈmjuːtɪleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 残缺的，毁损的 |  seriously damaged, especially by cutting or tearing off a part |  The mutilated body of the victim was found in the woods. 受害者残缺的尸体在树林里被发现。| synonyms: maimed, disfigured, deformed, injured, damaged | antonyms: intact, whole, undamaged, uninjured | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mutilatus, past participle of mutilare (“to maim, cut off”), from mutilus (“maimed, mutilated”).
