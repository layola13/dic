disproof:/dɪsˈpruːf/
n.|反驳；反证；证伪|The scientist offered a disproof of the theory.  这位科学家提出了该理论的反证。|
例句：The lawyer presented evidence in disproof of the charges. 律师提出了证据以反驳指控。
例句：His experiment resulted in the disproof of the long-held belief. 他的实验导致了长期以来人们坚持的信念被证伪。
近义词：refutation, rebuttal, contradiction|
反义词：proof, confirmation, verification|
词性：名词

disprove:/ˌdɪsˈpruːv/
v.|反驳；证明…是虚假的|The latest evidence disproves his theory. 最新证据反驳了他的理论。|
例句：The scientist set out to disprove the common misconception. 科学家开始着手反驳普遍存在的误解。
例句：The new data disproved the initial hypothesis. 新数据证明了最初的假设是错误的。
近义词：refute, rebut, contradict|
反义词：prove, confirm, verify|
词性：动词


词源：
Late Middle English: from dis- ‘reversal’ + prove.
