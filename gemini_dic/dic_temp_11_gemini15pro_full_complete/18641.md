Example 2:  Be careful when handling benzine, as it's highly flammable.  处理汽油时要小心，因为它非常易燃。| Synonym: gasoline, petrol | Antonym:  N/A (benzine refers to a specific substance, it doesn't have a direct opposite) | noun
