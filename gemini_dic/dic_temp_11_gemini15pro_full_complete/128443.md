news:/njuːz/ /nuːz/ | n. |新闻，消息|reports of recent events, especially as broadcast on television or radio or published in newspapers|例句：I heard some interesting news today. 我今天听到了一些有趣的新闻。

例句：The news of his death shocked everyone. 他去世的消息震惊了所有人。

近义词：information, report, tidings, story|反义词: silence, rumor, speculation |词性: noun
