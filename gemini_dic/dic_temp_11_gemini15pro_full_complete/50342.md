deploys:/dɪˈplɔɪz/ | v. | 部署; 调动; 有效地利用| to position troops or equipment strategically; to put something into effective action | The company deploys its resources effectively.  这家公司有效地利用其资源。| Example: The general deployed his troops along the border. 将军沿着边界部署了他的部队。

Example:  The company deployed new software to improve efficiency. 公司部署了新软件以提高效率。 | Synonyms:  place, position, station, arrange, utilize, employ | Antonyms: withdraw, withhold, retract | verb

deploys:/dɪˈplɔɪz/ | n. | 部署 |  the act of deploying something, especially troops or equipment. | The rapid deployment of the troops prevented further casualties. 部队的快速部署防止了进一步的伤亡。| Example: The deployment of the new system went smoothly. 新系统的部署进展顺利。| Synonyms: placement, positioning, stationing | Antonyms: withdrawal, retraction | noun
