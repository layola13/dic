heteroplasmy:/ˌhɛtərəˈplæzmi/ | n. |异质性 |The presence of more than one type of organellar genome (mitochondrial DNA or plastid DNA) within a cell or individual.  It's a key concept in mitochondrial genetics and can have significant implications for disease expression. |  The heteroplasmy of mitochondrial DNA can influence the severity of mitochondrial diseases. 线粒体DNA的异质性会影响线粒体疾病的严重程度。

The degree of heteroplasmy varied widely among the patients. 患者的异质性程度差异很大。 | 近义词:  heterogeneity, variation | 反义词: homoplasmy | 词性: noun

heteroplasmic:/ˌhɛtərəˈplæzmɪk/ | adj. |异质性的 |Exhibiting heteroplasmy; having more than one organellar genome type within a cell or individual. | The heteroplasmic nature of mitochondrial DNA makes it challenging to predict disease inheritance. 线粒体DNA的异质性使得预测疾病遗传变得困难。

The analysis revealed a heteroplasmic mutation in the mitochondrial genome.  分析揭示了线粒体基因组中的异质性突变。 |近义词: heterogeneous, varied | 反义词: homoplasmic | 词性: adjective
