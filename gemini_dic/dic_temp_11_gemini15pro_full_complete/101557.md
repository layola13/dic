ironmongery:/ˈaɪənmʌŋɡəri/
n.
五金器具；金属制品
Ironmongery refers to metal goods, especially those used in building and household repairs, such as tools, locks, hinges, nails, etc.
例句：The old ironmongery shop sold everything from nails to garden tools.  这家老五金店出售从钉子到园艺工具的所有东西。

例句：He inherited his father's ironmongery business. 他继承了父亲的五金生意。

近义词：hardware, metalware
反义词: (No direct antonym, but possibly "software" in a metaphorical sense)
词性: noun
