gratin:/ˈɡrætɪn, ɡræˈtæ̃/ /ˈɡrætn/| n. |奶油烤菜; 脆皮烤菜;  焗烤食品 (尤指土豆)|A dish with a browned or crusted top, often using breadcrumbs and cheese.  A gratin is a culinary technique in which an ingredient is topped with a browned crust, often using breadcrumbs, grated cheese, eggs or butter. |例句：The potato gratin was perfectly cooked, with a crispy top and creamy interior. 土豆焗烤做得非常完美，上面酥脆，里面绵软。

例句：She prepared a delicious vegetable gratin for the dinner party. 她为晚宴准备了一道美味的蔬菜焗烤。|近义词：au gratin, casserole |反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially "stew" as a contrasting cooking method)|词性:noun
