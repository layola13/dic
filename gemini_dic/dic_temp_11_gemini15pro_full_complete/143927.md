outpunched:/ˌaʊtˈpʌnʃt/
verb
以出拳击败, 打出更多拳胜过
defeated by punching more effectively; surpassed in punching
The smaller boxer outpunched his larger opponent, surprising everyone.  较小的拳击手以出拳击败了他的大个子对手，令所有人惊讶。
outboxed, outfought
--- (No clear antonym, possibly "was outpunched by")
词性:动词
