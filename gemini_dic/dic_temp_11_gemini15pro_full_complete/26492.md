cabalist:/ˈkæbəlɪst/| n.|卡巴拉学者，神秘主义者|A person who studies or practices Kabbalah, a system of Jewish mystical interpretation.|He was known as a learned cabalist, delving into the secrets of the universe.|近义词：mystic, occultist|反义词:skeptic, rationalist|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cabalista*, from Hebrew *qabbalah* "received doctrine, tradition," from *qibbel* "to receive."
