dwelling:/ˈdwɛlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdwɛlɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|住所，住宅|A place of residence; a house or home.|例句：They found a simple dwelling in the forest. 他们在森林里找到了一个简陋的住所。

例句：The cave served as a dwelling for the early humans.  这个洞穴是早期人类的住所。

近义词：residence, abode, house, home, domicile|反义词: wilderness, outdoors|词性: noun
