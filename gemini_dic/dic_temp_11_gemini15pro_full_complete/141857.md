orchestrators:/ˈɔːrkɪstreɪtərz/
n.
策划者，组织者，协调者
A person who organizes or coordinates something, especially something complex.
The orchestrators of the merger were highly praised for their skill and efficiency.  这次合并的组织者因其娴熟的技巧和高效率而备受赞誉。
The event was a triumph thanks to the meticulous work of the orchestrators.  由于组织者的细致工作，这次活动取得了圆满成功。
Synonyms: organizers, coordinators, planners, arrangers, directors
Antonyms: participants, followers, subordinates
