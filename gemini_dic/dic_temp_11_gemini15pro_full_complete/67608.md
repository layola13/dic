extravascular:/ˌekstrəˈvæskjʊlər/
adjective
血管外的；脉管外的
Located or occurring outside of a blood vessel.
The extravascular fluid helps maintain tissue hydration.  血管外液体有助于维持组织的水合作用。
Extravascular blood clotting can lead to serious complications. 血管外凝血会导致严重的并发症。
Synonyms: extravasal
Antonyms: intravascular
词性: 形容词
