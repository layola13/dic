deemphasizers:/diːˈɛmfəˌsaɪzərz/ | n. |弱化器；减弱者；不强调者|Something that deemphasizes or reduces the emphasis on something else.  It can refer to a person, thing, or action that diminishes the importance or prominence of something. |The artist used darker colors as deemphasizers to draw attention to the brighter focal point of the painting. 艺术家使用较暗的颜色作为弱化器，以将注意力吸引到画作中更明亮的焦点。| The new policy includes deemphasizers meant to reduce the importance of standardized testing.  这项新政策包含一些弱化措施，旨在降低标准化测试的重要性。 | synonyms: downplayers, minimizers, softeners | antonyms:  emphasizers,  highlighters, intensifiers | noun
