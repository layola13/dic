overcautioned:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃənd/| adj.|过于谨慎的，过分小心的|Excessively cautious; too careful or wary.|He was overcautioned in his investments, missing out on several lucrative opportunities. 他在投资方面过于谨慎，错过了几个赚钱的机会。|例句：She was overcautioned about crossing the street, looking both ways multiple times. 她过马路时非常小心，反复看了好几遍。|近义词：overly cautious, excessively careful, timid, apprehensive|反义词：reckless, careless, impulsive, daring|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "cautioned" (past participle of "caution," from Old French caution, from Latin cautio "a taking heed," from cautus "wary," past participle of cavere "to beware, take heed").
