illuminee:/ɪˌlʊːmɪˈniː/
n.
被启迪者；受教者
a person who has been enlightened or instructed, especially in spiritual matters.
The illuminee felt a profound sense of connection to the universe.  这位受教者感到与宇宙之间深刻的联系。
The guru gathered his illuminees for a private session.  上师召集他的信徒们进行一次私人会议。
近义词: disciple, follower, student, learner
反义词: teacher, instructor, guru, mentor
词性: noun


Etymology:
From French *illuminé*, past participle of *illuminer* "to illuminate," from Latin *illuminare* "to light up, enlighten," from *in-* "in" + *luminare* "to light," from *lumen* "light."
