overcharge:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒ/ /ˌəʊvəˈtʃɑːdʒ/
verb|过高收费，过度充电|To charge someone more than the fair or usual price; to fill a battery with more electricity than is good for it.|The taxi driver overcharged us.|近义词：overprice, gouge, inflate|反义词: undercharge, discount|v.

overcharge:/ˈoʊvərˌtʃɑːrdʒ/ /ˈəʊvəˌtʃɑːdʒ/
noun|过高的收费，过度充电|The act of charging too much or of charging a battery for too long.|We complained about the overcharge on our bill.|近义词：surcharge, excess charge|反义词: undercharge, discount|n.
