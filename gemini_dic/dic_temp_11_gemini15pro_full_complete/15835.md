ballads:/ˈbælədz/ | n. | 民谣，歌谣 | A narrative poem, often of folk origin and intended to be sung, characterized by simple language and dramatic action. |  The singer performed several traditional ballads. 歌手演唱了几首传统民谣。| synonyms: song, ditty, lay, chantey | antonyms: epic, novel | noun


ballad:/ˈbæləd/ | n. | 民谣，歌谣 | A slow, romantic popular song. | The radio played a slow ballad. 收音机播放了一首舒缓的民谣。| synonyms: song, love song, slow jam | antonyms: up-tempo song, dance music | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "balade" (dancing song), from Old Provençal "balada", from "balar" (to dance). Ultimately from Late Latin "ballare".
