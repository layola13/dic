bugwort:/ˈbʌɡwɜːrt/| n. |臭蒿; 艾菊|A European plant (Artemisia vulgaris) of the daisy family, with small yellowish flowers and a bitter aromatic smell. It was formerly used medicinally.|The scent of bugwort hung heavy in the air. 空气中弥漫着浓重的臭蒿味。|Synonym: mugwort, felon herb|Antonym: N/A|noun

bugwort:/ˈbʌɡwɜːrt/| n. |北美艾菊|A North American composite plant (Artemisia Absinthium) having silvery leaves and numerous small yellowish flower heads and formerly used medicinally.|The settlers used bugwort to treat various ailments. 移民们用北美艾菊来治疗各种疾病。|Synonym:  wormwood|Antonym: N/A|noun
