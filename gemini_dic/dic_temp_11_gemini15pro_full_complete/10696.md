apr:/eɪ piː ɑːr/ (US) /eɪ piː ɑːr/ (UK)| n. abbreviation|年度百分比率（Annual Percentage Rate）|Annual Percentage Rate, the annual rate charged for borrowing or earned through an investment, expressed as a single percentage number that represents the actual yearly cost of funds over the term of a loan. This includes any fees or additional costs associated with the transaction.|The APR on my credit card is 19.9%. 我的信用卡的年利率是19.9%。|Synonym: interest rate, cost of credit|Antonym: N/A|abbreviation
