biohermal: /ˌbaɪoʊˈhɜːrməl/
adjective
生物礁的; 由生物礁形成的
Relating to a bioherm, a mound-like structure built up of the remains of sessile organisms, such as corals or shellfish.
The biohermal limestone was formed by the accumulation of coral skeletons.  生物礁灰岩是由珊瑚骨骼堆积形成的。
The geologist studied the biohermal structures in the ancient reef.  地质学家研究了古代珊瑚礁中的生物礁结构。
近义词: reefal, organic
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider terms like "inorganic" or "abiotic" for contrasting concepts)
词性: adjective
