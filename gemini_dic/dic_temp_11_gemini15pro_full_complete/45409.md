cupolas:/ˈkjuːpələz/ (US), /ˈkjuːpələs/ (UK)| n. |穹顶，圆屋顶；小塔|A small dome, especially a small dome on a roof or turret.| The church has a beautiful copper cupola. 教堂有一个美丽的铜质穹顶。| The cupola atop the courthouse provides a panoramic view of the city. 法院顶部的小塔提供了城市的全景。 | synonyms: dome, turret, rotunda | antonyms: base, foundation | noun

cupolas:/ˈkjuːpələz/ (US), /ˈkjuːpələs/ (UK)| n. |(冶金炉的)穹顶|A rounded vault forming or resembling a dome, especially one forming the roof of a furnace.| The cupola of the blast furnace allows gases to escape. 高炉的穹顶允许气体逸出。|  The molten metal was poured from the cupola. 熔融的金属从穹顶倒出。| synonyms: dome, vault, roof | antonyms: base, floor | noun
