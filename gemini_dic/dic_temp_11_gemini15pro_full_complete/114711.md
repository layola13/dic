malleate:/ˈmælieɪt/ /ˈmeɪlieɪt/
|v.|锤打；延展；锻造|To hammer or beat (metal) into a shape; to shape or form (something) by hammering or beating; to extend or spread thinly. |The blacksmith malleated the iron into a horseshoe.| 近义词: hammer, forge, shape, mold, work, beat,  flatten, extend, spread| 反义词: compress, condense, shrink|词性: 动词

malleable:/ˈmæliəbl/ /ˈmeɪliəbl/
|adj.|可锻造的；可塑的；易受影响的|Capable of being hammered or beaten into a shape without breaking or cracking; adaptable or easily influenced.|Gold is a highly malleable metal. |近义词: pliable, ductile, adaptable, flexible, impressionable| 反义词: rigid, inflexible, unyielding, brittle|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *malleāre* "to hammer," from *malleus* "hammer."
