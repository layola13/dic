hurdy-gurdists:/ˈhɜːrdi ˈɡɜːrdɪsts/ | n. | 手摇风琴演奏者 | A person who plays the hurdy-gurdy, a stringed instrument cranked by a wheel rubbed against the strings. | The hurdy-gurdists entertained the crowd with their lively music.  手摇风琴演奏者用他们活泼的音乐娱乐了人群。 |  musicians, instrumentalists |  - | noun
词源: From hurdy-gurdy + -ist.  The word "hurdy-gurdy" is of uncertain origin, possibly imitative of the sound of the instrument.
