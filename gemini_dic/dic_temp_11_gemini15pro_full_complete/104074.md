jumbomania:/ˌdʒʌmboʊˈmeɪniə/
n.
狂热的收集癖，尤指对大型或不寻常物品的收集癖
An excessive desire to collect things, especially large or unusual items.
He has a jumbomania for vintage cars, filling his garage with them. 他对老爷车有一种狂热的收集癖，把他的车库都塞满了。
collecting mania, obsession with collecting
-
noun
