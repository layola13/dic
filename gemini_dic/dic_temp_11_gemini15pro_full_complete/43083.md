cowriters:/ˈkoʊˌraɪtərz/| n. |合著者，共同执笔者|People who write something together, such as a book, play, or screenplay.|Example 1: The cowriters of the hit musical met in college. 这部热门音乐剧的合著者们在大学相遇。

Example 2:  The novel has two cowriters, a historian and a novelist. 这部小说有两个合著者，一位历史学家和一位小说家。|Synonyms: collaborators, joint authors|Antonyms:  sole author|noun
