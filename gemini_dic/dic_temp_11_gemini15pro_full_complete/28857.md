carpet-bomb:/ˈkɑːrpɪt bɒm/ /ˈkɑːrpɪt bɑːm/| v. |地毯式轰炸|To bomb an area heavily and indiscriminately.|The air force carpet-bombed the city.|近义词：bombard, blitz|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用protect, defend)|词性: 动词

carpet-bombs:/ˈkɑːrpɪt bɒmz/ /ˈkɑːrpɪt bɑːmz/| n. |地毯式轰炸|The act of carpet-bombing.|The carpet-bombs destroyed everything.|近义词：bombardment, blitzkrieg|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用protection, defense)|词性: 名词


词源:  "carpet" + "bomb".  "Carpet" refers to the way the bombs cover an area like a carpet.  The term originated during World War II.
