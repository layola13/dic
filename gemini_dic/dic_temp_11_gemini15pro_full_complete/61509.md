embroude:/ɪmˈbraʊd/ /ɛmˈbruːd/ | v. | 刺绣，装饰，润色（文字） | To decorate with needlework; to embellish, especially with ornate details or fictitious additions. | She embrouded the tablecloth with a delicate floral pattern. 她在一块桌布上绣上了精致的花卉图案。

The author embrouded his travelogue with fanciful tales of adventure.  作者用天马行空的冒险故事润色了他的游记。 | 近义词：embroider, adorn, embellish, ornament | 反义词：simplify, strip, plain | 词性：动词

词源：From Middle English *embrowden, from Old French embroder, from en- (“in, on”) + broder (“to embroider”), from a Germanic source akin to Old English bord (“border, edge”).
