appenage:/ˈæpɪnɪdʒ/
n.
附属物，附带物
Something added or attached to something larger or more important.
The Duchy of Cornwall is an appanage of the Prince of Wales. 康沃尔公爵领地是威尔士亲王的附属地。

近义词:adjunct, accessory, appendage, attachment, supplement
反义词:core, essence, necessity

词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French apanage, from apaner "to provide with bread," from Latin ad- "to" + panis "bread." Originally referred to lands and revenues granted to younger sons of royalty.
