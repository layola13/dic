institutionalizing: /ˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|制度化；使…机构化|The process of establishing something as a convention or norm within an organization or culture.  Making something a permanent or ingrained part of a system.|The government is institutionalizing new regulations for environmental protection. 政府正在将新的环境保护法规制度化。|institutionalise, systematize, normalize|deinstitutionalize, personalize|verb

institutionalizing: /ˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/
|adj.|制度化的|Describes something that is becoming established as a regular or common practice within a system.|The institutionalizing of racism is a serious societal problem. 种族主义的制度化是一个严重的社会问题。|systematic, established, ingrained|informal, ad hoc|adjective
