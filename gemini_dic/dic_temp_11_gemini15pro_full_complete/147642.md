overpolished:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːlɪʃt/| adj.|过度抛光的；过于修饰的；矫揉造作的|Having been polished too much; excessively refined or elegant, often to the point of artificiality.|Her overpolished presentation seemed insincere. 她的演讲过于雕琢，显得不真诚。|例句：His shoes were so overpolished they looked like mirrors. 他的鞋子擦得锃亮，就像镜子一样。

例句：The overpolished prose felt artificial and lacked genuine emotion.  过于修饰的散文感觉很做作，缺乏真情实感。

近义词：overrefined, pretentious, affected, artificial|反义词:natural, genuine, unpolished, rough|词性:adjective
