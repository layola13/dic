canzonet:/ˌkænzəˈnɛt/ | n. |小曲，短歌 |A short song, especially a light, lyrical one. |She sang a sweet canzonet. 她唱了一首甜美的小曲。| The canzonet was a popular form in the Renaissance.  这首小曲是文艺复兴时期的一种流行形式。 |近义词：song, ditty, aria |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "epic" for a contrasting long form) |词性:noun

词源:
From Italian *canzonetta*, diminutive of *canzone* ("song"), from Vulgar Latin *cantio* , from Latin *cantus* ("song"), from *cano* (“to sing”).
