avoirdupois:/ˌævərdəˈpɔɪz/ | n. | 常衡制；体重 | A system of weights based on a pound of 16 ounces, used for all goods except precious metals and medicines. |  Her avoirdupois increased after the holidays.  假期过后，她的体重增加了。 | Synonym: weight, mass | Antonym: lightweight | noun

avoirdupois:/ˌævərdəˈpɔɪz/ | adj. |  常衡的 | Relating to the avoirdupois system of weights. |  The avoirdupois pound is commonly used in the United States. 常衡磅在美国普遍使用。| Synonym: weight-based | Antonym: troy (when referring to weight systems) | adjective
