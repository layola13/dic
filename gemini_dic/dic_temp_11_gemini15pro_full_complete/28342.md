carangoid:/kəˈræŋɡɔɪd/
| adj. n.|鲹科的，鲹科鱼|Relating to or resembling the carangids, a family of marine fishes including jacks, pompanos, and scads.  Also, a fish of the Carangidae family.|例句：The carangoid fish darted through the coral reef.  这种鲹科鱼飞快地穿过珊瑚礁。|例句：The fisherman caught a large carangoid. 渔民捕到了一条大鲹鱼。|近义词：jack, trevally, scad, pompano|反义词: (No specific antonym, perhaps "non-carangoid")|词性：adjective, noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From  Carangidae (family name) + -oid (suffix meaning "resembling"). Carangidae comes from the French *carangue*, from Portuguese *carango*, ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly from a Tupi language of Brazil.
