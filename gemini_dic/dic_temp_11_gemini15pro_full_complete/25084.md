bubblier:/ˈbʌbliər/ | adj. |更 bubbly 的，更起泡的，更活泼的 |More bubbly; containing more bubbles; more effervescent and enthusiastic. | The champagne felt bubblier than usual.  这香槟感觉比平时更起泡。| The hostess was even bubblier than last time we saw her. 女主人比我们上次见到她时更加活泼。| 近义词：fizzier, livelier, more effervescent | 反义词：flatter, duller | 词性: adjective

Etymology->
From *bubble* + *-ier* (comparative suffix).  *Bubble* ultimately comes from imitative origins, reflecting the sound of bubbles.
