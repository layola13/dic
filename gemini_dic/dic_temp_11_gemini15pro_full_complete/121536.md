mismarked:/ˌmɪsˈmɑːrkt/ | adj. | 标错的，标记错误的 | Incorrectly marked or labeled. | The product was mismarked and sold at the wrong price.  该产品被标错了价格，以错误的价格出售了。| synonyms: mislabeled, wrongly marked | antonyms: correctly marked, accurately labeled | adjective

mismarked:/ˌmɪsˈmɑːrkt/ | v. | 标错，标记错误 | To mark or label incorrectly. |  The cashier mismarked the item, causing confusion at checkout. 收银员把商品标错了，在结账时造成了混乱。| synonyms: mislabel, wrongly mark | antonyms: correctly mark, accurately label | verb
