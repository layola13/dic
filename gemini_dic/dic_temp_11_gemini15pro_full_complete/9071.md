antimilitarist:/ˌæntiˈmɪlɪtərɪst/| n. adj.|反军国主义者；反军国主义的|A person who opposes militarism; opposed to militarism. |例句：The antimilitarist protested the war.  反军国主义者抗议战争。

例句：She held strong antimilitarist views. 她持有强烈的反军国主义观点。|近义词：pacifist, dove, anti-war|反义词：militarist, hawk, pro-war|词性:名词, 形容词

Etymology: anti- (against) + militarist (one who advocates militarism).  "Militarist" comes from Latin "militaris" (of soldiers), from "miles" (soldier).
