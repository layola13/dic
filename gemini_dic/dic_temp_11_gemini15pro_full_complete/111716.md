locomotor:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊtər/ | adj. | 运动的；移动的；位移的 | relating to movement or the ability to move |  The locomotor system allows animals to move from one place to another.  运动系统使动物能够从一个地方移动到另一个地方。 |  例句：
The child's locomotor skills are developing rapidly.  这个孩子的运动技能发展迅速。

The injury affected his locomotor functions.  受伤影响了他的运动功能。| 近义词：mobile, motile | 反义词：immobile, stationary | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *locomōtōrius (“of or pertaining to moving from place to place”), from locus (“place”) + mōtōrius (“of or pertaining to moving”), from moveō (“I move”).
