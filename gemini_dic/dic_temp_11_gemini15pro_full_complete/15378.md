backup's:/ˈbækʌps/| n.|备份的; 备份副本|A backup's purpose is to restore data in case of a system failure. 备份的目的是在系统故障时恢复数据。|例句：Always ensure your backup's integrity. 始终确保备份的完整性。

backup's:/ˈbækʌps/| n.|属于备份的; 与备份相关的|The backup's storage location should be secure. 备份的存储位置应该是安全的。|例句：The backup's schedule is every Sunday at midnight.  备份的计划是每周日午夜。|近义词: copy, reserve, spare |反义词: original |词性: 名词 (所有格)


词源:  From "backup" + possessive suffix "'s". "Backup" itself originates from the verb phrase "back up," meaning to support or reinforce, later evolving to mean creating a copy of data for security.
