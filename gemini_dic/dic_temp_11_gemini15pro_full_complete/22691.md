bore-down:/bɔːr daʊn/
verb
向下钻孔；施加压力；俯冲；(尤指分娩时)用力
to press down hard on something
The eagle bore down on its prey. 老鹰向它的猎物俯冲下去。
She bore down with all her might. 她使出浑身的力气。

近义词:press, push, bear down, descend, swoop
反义词:ascend, rise

词性:verb (动词)

词源: From "bore" (to drill a hole) + "down" (adverb indicating direction).  The sense of pressing down developed metaphorically from the action of a drill.
