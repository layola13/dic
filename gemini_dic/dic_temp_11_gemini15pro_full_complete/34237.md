clannishnesses:/ˈklænɪʃnəsəz/
noun
排他性，抱团性，小团体主义
The tendency to be clannish; exclusivity.  The state or quality of being clannish.
Their clannishnesses made it difficult for outsiders to integrate into the community.  他们的排他性使得外来者很难融入这个社区。
clannishness, insularity, exclusivity
inclusiveness, openness
