hero-worshipers:/ˈhɪəroʊ ˌwɜːrʃɪpərz/ (US) /ˈhɪərəʊ ˌwɜːʃɪpəz/ (UK)
n.
崇拜英雄者；英雄崇拜者
People who admire and idolize heroes, often excessively.  They may emulate their heroes' behavior, style, and values.
The band's concerts are always packed with hero-worshipers.  这支乐队的演唱会总是挤满了崇拜英雄的人。
The young boy was one of the athlete's most devoted hero-worshipers.  这个小男孩是这位运动员最忠实的崇拜者之一。
synonyms: admirers, devotees, fans, idolizers, followers
antonyms: critics, detractors, skeptics
noun
