adversed:/ədˈvɜːrst/ | adj. | 不利的，逆境的，反对的 |  Unfavorable or harmful; opposing or hostile.  Characterized by misfortune or difficulty. | The company faced adversed market conditions.  这家公司面临不利的市场环境。| 近义词: unfavorable, hostile, opposing, contrary, antagonistic | 反义词: favorable, advantageous, propitious, beneficial, supportive | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *adversus* "turned toward, facing, opposite, hostile," past participle of *advertere* "to turn toward," from *ad-* "to" + *vertere* "to turn."
