navigates:/ˈnævɪɡeɪts/| v.|航行，导航，指引，引导|to find a direction across, along, or over an area of water or land, often by using a map or other equipment; to move or travel through a place, often one that is difficult to move through|The captain skillfully navigates the ship through the narrow channel. 船长熟练地驾驶船只穿过狭窄的海峡。|steer, pilot, direct, guide|founder, lose one's way|verb
Etymology: From Latin *navigare "to sail, sail over, swim," from navis "ship" + agere "to drive, lead, act."
