journeyings:/ˈdʒɜːrniɪŋz/
n.
旅行，旅程
acts of traveling; trips
His journeyings took him all over the world. 他的旅行带他走遍了全世界。

近义词: travels, trips, voyages, expeditions
反义词: stay, inactivity, idleness


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *journeiinge, *jorneiynge, from Anglo-Norman jurnee, jurneie (“day's work, day's travel”), from Old French jornee (“day, day's travel, day's work”), from Late Latin diurnum (“day, day's pay”), from Latin dies (“day”).  The -ing suffix transforms the noun into a gerund, indicating the action or process of journeying.
