actinomycosis:/ˌæktɪnoʊmaɪˈkoʊsɪs/| n.|放线菌病|A chronic bacterial disease caused by various filamentous, branching bacteria of the genus *Actinomyces*. It is characterized by the formation of abscesses and granulomas that may drain through sinus tracts.|例句：Actinomycosis is a rare but serious infection. 放线菌病是一种罕见但严重的感染。

例句：The doctor diagnosed the patient with cervicofacial actinomycosis. 医生诊断该病人患有颈面部放线菌病。|近义词:nocardiosis (诺卡菌病, 类似的细菌感染)|反义词:None (没有明确的反义词，因为它是指一种特定疾病)|词性:noun
