insulite: /ˈɪnsəˌlaɪt/ | vt. | 侮辱，辱骂 | To speak to or treat with disrespect, insolence, or contempt. | He insulited her by questioning her intelligence. 他通过质疑她的智力来侮辱她。|  He felt insulited when they didn't invite him to the party. 当他们没有邀请他参加聚会时，他感到受到了侮辱。 | 近义词: offend, affront, insult, slight | 反义词: compliment, praise, honor | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Late Latin *insultare, frequentative of Latin insultare "to jump or leap at or upon, insult, assail," from in- "on, upon" + saltare "to leap, dance" (from PIE root *sel- "to jump").
