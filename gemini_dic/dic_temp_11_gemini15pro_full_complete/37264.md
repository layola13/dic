comini:/ˈkɒmɪni/ /ˈkoʊmɪni/ | n. |科米尼; 小茴香（尤指孜然）| A spice made from the dried fruit of a plant related to parsley, used in Middle Eastern and Indian cooking. It has a strong, warm, slightly bitter flavor.|  Example: I added comini to the lamb stew for an authentic flavor. 我在炖羊肉里加了小茴香，以获得正宗的风味。

Example: The aroma of comini filled the air. 小茴香的香味弥漫在空气中。| 近义词：cumin, caraway | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
