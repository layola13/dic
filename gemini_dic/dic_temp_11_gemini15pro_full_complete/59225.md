dystrophies: /ˈdɪstrəfiz/ | n. | 营养不良; 萎缩; 退化; 变性 |  A group of disorders, often inherited, characterized by progressive weakness and degeneration of muscle fibers. |  Muscular dystrophies are a group of genetic diseases that cause progressive weakness and loss of muscle mass. 肌肉萎缩症是一组导致进行性无力和肌肉质量丧失的遗传性疾病。 |  Many different dystrophies affect various muscle groups. 许多不同的营养不良会影响不同的肌肉群。 | synonyms: degenerations, atrophies | antonyms: growths, developments | noun
