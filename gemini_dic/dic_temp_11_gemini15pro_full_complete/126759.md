nanites:/ˈneɪnaɪts/| n. |纳米机器人，纳米机械|Microscopic robots or machines, typically depicted in science fiction, that can manipulate matter at the atomic or molecular level.  They are often portrayed as self-replicating and capable of performing complex tasks, including medical procedures and manufacturing.|The nanites repaired the damaged tissue in seconds. 纳米机器人几秒钟内就修复了受损的组织。|Synonyms: nanobots, nanomachines|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "macromachines")|Noun
