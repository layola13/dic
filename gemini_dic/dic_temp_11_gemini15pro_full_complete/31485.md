charmedly:/ˈtʃɑːrmɪdli/| adv.|迷人地，有魅力地|In a charming manner; attractively.  It suggests being captivated or delighted by someone or something's charm.|She smiled charmedly at him. 她迷人地对他笑了笑。|近义词：charmingly, attractively, captivatingly, delightfully, enchantingly|反义词：unattractively, displeasingly, repellently|词性:副词
词源: From "charmed" (past participle of "charm") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Charm" ultimately comes from the Latin "carmen" meaning "song, incantation."
