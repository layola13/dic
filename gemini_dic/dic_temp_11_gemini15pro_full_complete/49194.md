delawyers:/dɪˈlɔɪərz/
n.
延迟律师；拖延诉讼的律师
Lawyers who specialize in delaying legal proceedings, often using various tactics to prolong a case.
The delayers managed to postpone the trial for another six months.  拖延诉讼的律师设法将审判推迟了六个月。
近义词:procrastinators, stallers
反义词:expediters
词性:名词

词源:
delay (v.) + -er (suffix forming agent nouns) + -s (plural suffix)
From "delay" (meaning to postpone or cause to be late) + "-er" (suffix denoting a person who performs an action) + "-s" (plural marker).
