archaean:/ɑːrˈkiːən/ | adj. n.|太古的，太古代的；太古代地层|Relating to or denoting the earliest eon in the history of Earth, following the Hadean eon and preceding the Proterozoic eon, from about 4 to 2.5 billion years ago.  Also, a rock or fossil from this eon.|The archaean rocks are the oldest known.|近义词：Precambrian, primordial|反义词:modern, recent|词性:adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *arkhaios* (ancient) + *-an* (suffix forming adjectives and nouns).
