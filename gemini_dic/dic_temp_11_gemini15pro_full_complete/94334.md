impiety:/ɪmˈpaɪəti/ | n. | 不敬，不虔诚 | Lack of piety or reverence, especially for God or religion; wickedness. |  His impiety shocked the devout villagers.  他的不敬震惊了虔诚的村民们。|  profanity, irreverence, ungodliness | piety, reverence, devotion | noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *impietas, from Latin impius (“impious”) + -tas. Equivalent to im- + piety.
