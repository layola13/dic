dietetic /ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪk/
adjective
饮食的；营养学的
Relating to diet or nutrition.
Example: Dietetic advice can help manage chronic diseases. 营养学建议可以帮助控制慢性疾病。
Example:  She is studying dietetic science at university.  她在大学学习营养学。
Synonym: nutritional, dietary
Antonym: unhealthy, unwholesome
词性: adjective


dietitian /ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/
noun
营养师；饮食学家
A person who is an expert on diet and nutrition.
Example: The dietitian recommended a balanced meal plan. 营养师推荐了一个均衡的膳食计划。
Example:  Consult a registered dietitian for personalized dietary advice.  咨询注册营养师以获得个性化的饮食建议。
Synonym: nutritionist
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unqualified advisor")
词性: noun


dietetics /ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪks/
noun
营养学
The science of applying the principles of nutrition to the feeding of individuals or groups.
Example:  She majored in dietetics in college. 她在大学主修营养学。
Example:  Dietetics plays a crucial role in public health. 营养学在公共卫生中起着至关重要的作用。
Synonym:  nutrition science, nutritional science
Antonym: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


词源:  From Greek diaita "manner of living," later "diet," from diaitasthai "lead one's life."
