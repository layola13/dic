geopolitically:/ˌdʒiːoʊpəˈlɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 地缘政治上地 | In a way that relates to the study of the effects of geography (both human and physical) on international politics and international relations. | The situation is geopolitically complex.  局势在地缘政治上很复杂。| The region is geopolitically significant. 该地区在地缘政治上很重要。 | 近义词: strategically, globally | 反义词: domestically, locally | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "geopolitics" + "-ally".  "Geopolitics" comes from the German *Geopolitik*, coined by Rudolf Kjellén, from *geo-* (earth) + *Politik* (politics).
