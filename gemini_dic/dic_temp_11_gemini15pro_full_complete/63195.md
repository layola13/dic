entasia:/ɛnˈteɪʒə/ /ɛnˈteɪziə/
noun
内生菌，内生真菌
A fungus living within the tissues of a plant, typically causing no obvious symptoms of disease.
Endophytic fungi live inside plant tissues without causing any apparent harm to the host plant. 内生真菌生活在植物组织内部，不会对宿主植物造成任何明显的伤害。

The researchers isolated several entasia strains from the leaves of the coffee plant. 研究人员从咖啡植物的叶片中分离出几种内生真菌菌株。

近义词: endophyte
反义词: pathogen
词性: n.
