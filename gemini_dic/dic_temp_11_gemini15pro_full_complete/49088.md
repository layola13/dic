deictically:/daɪˈktɪkli/ | adv. | 指示地 | In a way that points to or refers to something within the context of the utterance or text.  It relies on the context for its meaning. | "This" is a deictic word because its meaning depends on what is being pointed to.  “这”是一个指示词，因为它的含义取决于所指的对象。 |  近义词: indexical, demonstratively | 反义词:  None (lack of a direct opposite) | 词性: adverb

Etymology:  From Ancient Greek *deiktikós* (pointing, showing) from *deíknunai* (to show).
