apologias:/əˌpɒləˈdʒiːəz/
n.
formal defenses or justifications, especially of a belief, idea, or action
a formal written defense of one's opinions or conduct.
She offered no apologies for her outburst. 她对自己的爆发没有表示歉意。
His book was an apologia for capitalism. 他的书是对资本主义的辩护。
synonyms: defense, justification, explanation, vindication, excuse, plea, argument
antonyms:  accusation, condemnation, indictment, criticism
noun
