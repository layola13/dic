invisible:/ɪnˈvɪzəbl/| adj.| 看不见的；无形的；不显眼的| not perceptible to the eye |  The bacteria are invisible without a microscope.  没有显微镜，这些细菌是看不见的。|近义词: unseen, imperceptible, hidden, concealed|反义词: visible, apparent, obvious|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *invisibil*, from Late Latin *invisibilis*, from Latin *in-* (not) + *visibilis* (visible), from *vidēre* (to see).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *weyd-*.
