globulous:/ˈɡlɒbjʊləs/ (US) /ˈɡlɑːbjələs/ (UK)| adj.|球状的；球形的；圆滚滚的|Resembling a globe or sphere; round.  Something globulous is shaped like a ball or globe. It often implies a sense of fullness or roundness.|The planet appeared as a small, globulous object in the telescope.  这颗行星在望远镜里看起来像一个小的球状物体。

The child clutched a globulous balloon in his hand.  孩子手里抓着一个圆滚滚的气球。|近义词：spherical, round, globular, orbicular|反义词：flat, planar|词性：形容词
