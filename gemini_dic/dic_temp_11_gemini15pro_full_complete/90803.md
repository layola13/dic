humphed:/hʌmft/ | v. | 哼了一声; 咕哝; 发出不满的声音 | To make a sound expressing annoyance or disapproval, typically a short, deep sound made in the throat. | He humphed and turned away, clearly displeased. 他哼了一声，转过身去，显然很不高兴。

She humphed when I suggested we go to the opera. 当我建议我们去看歌剧时，她哼了一声。 | 近义词：grunted, groaned, muttered, grumbled | 反义词: agreed, approved, praised | 词性: 动词

Etymology:  Likely imitative of the sound itself.
