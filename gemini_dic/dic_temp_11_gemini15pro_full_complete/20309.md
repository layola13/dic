biserrate:/baɪˈsɛreɪt/
adjective
双锯齿状的
Having teeth that are themselves serrated, like the teeth of a double saw.
The leaf margins were distinctly biserrate, a key characteristic for identification.  叶缘明显呈双锯齿状，这是鉴定的一个关键特征。
The fossil showed a biserrate cutting edge, suggesting it was used for sawing wood. 这块化石显示出双锯齿状的切割边缘，表明它曾被用来锯木头。
synonyms: doubly serrate, double-toothed
antonyms: smooth, entire
