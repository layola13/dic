non-sequence:/nɒnˈsiːkwəns/ | n. | 不相干的事物；不合逻辑的推论 | A statement that does not logically follow from the previous statement or argument. |  His argument was full of non sequiturs, jumping from one unrelated point to another. 他的论点充满了不合逻辑的推论，从一个不相关的点跳到另一个不相关的点。

| Synonyms: illogicality, fallacy, irrelevance | Antonyms:  logical sequence, coherence, relevance | noun
