huskies:/ˈhʌskiz/| n.|哈士奇犬，沙哑的声音|A breed of sled dog known for its endurance and thick coat.  Also refers to the plural of "husky" which can describe a hoarse voice.| The huskies howled at the moon. 这些哈士奇对着月亮嚎叫。|近义词：sled dogs, (for voice) hoarse, raspy|反义词: (for voice) clear, smooth|noun

huskies:/ˈhʌskiz/| adj.|沙哑的(仅用于描述声音)|Having a hoarse or rough voice.|His voice was huskies from shouting. 他的声音因为喊叫而沙哑。|近义词：hoarse, raspy, gravelly|反义词: clear, smooth|adjective
