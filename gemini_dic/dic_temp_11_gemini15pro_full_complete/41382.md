cormophyta:/ˌkɔːrməˈfaɪtə/ | n. | 蕨类植物；维管植物 |  A plant that has a stem, roots, and leaves, and that reproduces by spores or seeds.  A vascular plant.|  Example: Ferns, conifers, and flowering plants are all cormophyta. 蕨类植物、针叶树和开花植物都是蕨类植物。 | Synonym: tracheophyte, vascular plant | Antonym: thallophyte | noun
