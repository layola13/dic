good-time:/ˈɡʊd taɪm/ | adj. |  快乐的；寻欢作乐的；及时行乐的 | Enjoying or devoted to pleasure and entertainment, especially of a frivolous kind. | He's a real good-time guy, always up for a party. 他是个真正的乐天派，总是乐于参加聚会。 |  She had a good-time girl reputation. 她有个爱玩的名声。 | 近义词：fun-loving, pleasure-seeking, convivial | 反义词：serious, responsible, sober | 词性:形容词

词源:
From the phrase "good time," meaning a pleasant or enjoyable experience, attested from the mid-19th century.  The hyphenated form as an adjective emerged later.
