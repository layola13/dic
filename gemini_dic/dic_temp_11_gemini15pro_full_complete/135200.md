nonornamental:/ˌnɒnˌɔːrnəˈmentl/
adjective
非装饰性的，不加修饰的
Not decorative or ornamental; plain.
The building's facade was nonornamental, emphasizing function over aesthetics.  这栋建筑的外观不加修饰，强调功能而非美观。
The chairs were simple and nonornamental, perfect for the minimalist office.  这些椅子简单朴素，非常适合极简主义的办公室。
近义词:plain, simple, unadorned, undecorated
反义词:ornate, decorative, embellished, ornamental
词性:adjective
