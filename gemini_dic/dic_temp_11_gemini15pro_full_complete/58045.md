drolling:/ˈdrɒlɪŋ/ | adj. | 滑稽的，有趣的，古怪的 | Amusing in an odd or whimsical way. | He told a drolling story about his childhood. 他讲了一个关于他童年的滑稽故事。|  The comedian's drolling delivery had the audience in stitches.  这位喜剧演员滑稽的表达方式让观众笑得前仰后合。 | 近义词: comical, humorous, whimsical, witty, funny | 反义词: serious, solemn, somber | 词性:形容词

词源:  From the verb "droll," meaning "to jest" or "to make fun," which comes from the Middle French "droler."
