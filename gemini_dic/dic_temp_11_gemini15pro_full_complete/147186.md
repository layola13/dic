overluxurious: /ˌoʊvərluɡˈʒʊəriəs/ | adj. | 过于奢华的，极尽奢华的 | Excessively luxurious; characterized by extreme luxury and extravagance. |  The overluxurious hotel offered solid gold bathtubs and diamond-encrusted faucets. 这家过于奢华的酒店提供纯金浴缸和镶钻的水龙头。

The billionaire's overluxurious yacht was the size of a small cruise ship.  这位亿万富翁的超级豪华游艇有一艘小型游轮那么大。 | 近义词：lavish, opulent, extravagant | 反义词：austere, simple, modest | 词性: 形容词

**Etymology:**

* **over-**:  Prefix meaning "excessive," "too much." From Old English *ofer-*.
* **luxurious**: From Latin *luxuria* "abundance, profusion, luxury," from *luxus* "excess, extravagance."
