epiphora:/ɪˈpɪfərə/ | n. | 结语；反复；（修辞）首语重复法 | The repetition of a word or phrase at the end of successive clauses, phrases, or sentences.  This is a figure of speech. |  "I have a dream... I have a dream..." is an example of epiphora.  “我有一个梦想……我有一个梦想……”是首语重复法的一个例子。 | synonyms: epistrophe, repetition | antonyms: anaphora | noun
