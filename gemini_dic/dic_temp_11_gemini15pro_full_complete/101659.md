irregulous: /ɪˈrɛɡjʊləs/
adjective
不规则的，无规律的
Not regular; lacking regularity or uniformity; abnormal.
The artist's irregulous brushstrokes gave the painting a unique texture.  这位艺术家不规则的笔触赋予了这幅画独特的质感。
His heartbeat was irregulous, causing concern for his doctor. 他的心跳不规律，让他的医生很担心。
近义词: irregular, abnormal, anomalous, erratic
反义词: regular, normal, uniform, symmetrical
词性: adjective

词源:  A blend of *irregular* and *regulous*, likely coined humorously or to emphasize a deviation from regularity. *Regulous* itself is not a standard English word and is likely a back-formation from *regular*.
