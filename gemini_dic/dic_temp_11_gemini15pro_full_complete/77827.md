generativeness:/ˌdʒɛnərəˈtɪvnəs/
n.| 生成性，创造性|The quality or capacity of something to generate or produce something else, especially new ideas or forms.|例句：The generativeness of the human mind is remarkable. 人类思维的创造力是非凡的。|近义词：creativity, productivity, fertility, fecundity|反义词：sterility, barrenness|词性:名词
Etymology: From generative + -ness.  Generative from generate + -ive. Generate from Latin *generāre, frequentative of generāre "to beget, produce," from genus "race, stock, kind."
