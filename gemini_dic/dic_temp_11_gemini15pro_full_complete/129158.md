no-nonsense:/ˌnoʊˈnɑːnsəns/
adjective
严肃的；务实的；直截了当的
Practical and serious, and only interested in doing things that are effective or achieving results;  not tolerating or engaging in frivolous behavior.
She has a no-nonsense approach to management. 她采用务实的管理方法。
He's a no-nonsense kind of guy. 他是个很务实的人。
近义词: practical, pragmatic, businesslike, serious, down-to-earth, straightforward
反义词: frivolous, impractical, unrealistic, silly, foolish
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From "no" + "nonsense" (meaning foolish or frivolous behavior or talk).  The term arose in the mid-19th century, gaining popularity in the 20th century.
