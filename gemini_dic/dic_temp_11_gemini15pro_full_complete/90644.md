humanizers:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzərz/
n.
使人性化的人或事物; 人性化因素
People or things that make something more humane or relatable.
The book includes several anecdotes that serve as humanizers, making the historical figures seem more relatable.  这本书包含几个轶事，作为人性化的因素，使历史人物显得更贴近生活。
humanizing factors, human elements
dehumanizers
noun
