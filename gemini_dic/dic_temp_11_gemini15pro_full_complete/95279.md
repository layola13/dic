incapacity:/ˌɪnkəˈpæsəti/ | n. |无能力；无力；不胜任；丧失行为能力|Lack of ability or power to do something; being unable to do something; inability to function legally or mentally. |例句：His incapacity to make decisions led to the project's failure. 他无力做决定导致了项目的失败。

|例句：The judge ruled that due to her mental incapacity, she was not responsible for her actions.  法官裁定，由于她精神上无行为能力，她不对自己的行为负责。
|近义词：inability, incompetence, disability, powerlessness|反义词：ability, capability, competence, fitness|词性：名词

incapacity:/ˌɪnkəˈpæsəti/ | n. |无资格|lack of legal qualification or fitness|例句：His incapacity to hold public office was due to a previous conviction. 他无资格担任公职是因为他之前的犯罪记录。

|例句：Her incapacity to sign the contract rendered it invalid. 她没有资格签署合同，导致合同无效。|近义词：ineligibility, disqualification|反义词：eligibility, qualification|词性：名词
