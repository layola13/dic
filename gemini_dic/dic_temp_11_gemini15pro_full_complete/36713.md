collision:/kəˈlɪʒən/| n.|碰撞，冲突，抵触|An instance of one moving object or person striking violently against another.|The two cars were involved in a head-on collision.  这两辆汽车发生了正面碰撞。|近义词：crash, impact, smash|反义词:avoidance, separation|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin "collisio" "a striking together," from Latin "collidere" "to strike together," from "com-" "together" + "laedere" "to strike, hurt."
