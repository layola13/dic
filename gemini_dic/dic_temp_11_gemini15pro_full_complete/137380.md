nontemporarinesses:/nɒnˌtɛmpəˈrɛrɪnəsɪz/
|n.|非暂时性;永久性|The state or quality of not being temporary; permanence.|The lawyer argued for the nontemporarinesses of the client's residency status.|近义词: permanence, perpetuity|反义词: temporariness, transience|词性: Noun


Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
temporary (adjective): lasting for only a limited period of time
-ness (suffix): forming nouns denoting a state, condition, or quality
-es (suffix): plural form
