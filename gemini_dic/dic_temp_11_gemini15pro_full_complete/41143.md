copublishers:/ˌkoʊˈpʌblɪʃərz/
noun
共同出版商
Two or more publishers who collaborate to produce and distribute a publication.
Example: The book was copublished by two university presses. 这本书由两家大学出版社共同出版。
Example:  The small independent publisher sought copublishers in Europe to expand its reach.  这家小型独立出版社在欧洲寻找共同出版商以扩大其影响力。
synonyms: joint publishers, co-publishers
antonyms: sole publisher
