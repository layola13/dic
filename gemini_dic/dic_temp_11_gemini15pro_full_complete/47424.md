deadnesses:/ˈdɛdnɪsɪz/
noun
麻木；迟钝；沉寂
lack of vitality or sensation; dullness
The deadness of the economy is worrying. 经济的沉寂令人担忧。
Near synonyms: apathy, dullness, lifelessness, numbness, torpor
Antonyms:  liveliness, vibrancy, animation, energy
