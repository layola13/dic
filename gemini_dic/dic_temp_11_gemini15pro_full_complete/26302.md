butyryl:/ˈbjuːtɪrɪl/ |adj.|丁酰的;丁酰基|A univalent acyl radical derived from butyric acid.  It's a component in organic chemistry related to butyric acid. |Butyryl chloride is a highly reactive compound. 丁酰氯是一种高活性化合物。| Butyryl-CoA is an important intermediate in metabolism. 丁酰辅酶A是代谢中的重要中间体。 | 近义词:butanoyl (more systematic name) | 反义词:N/A | 词性: Adjective (primarily), can also function as a combining form (prefix)


ETYMOLOGY->
From butyric (related to butter) + -yl (suffix denoting a radical).  Ultimately from Latin *butyrum* (butter).
