fractionate:/ˈfrækʃəneɪt/ | v. | 分馏，分级，分步提取 | To separate something into different parts or fractions. | The chemist fractionated the crude oil into its various components. 化学家将原油分馏成各种成分。

The process fractionates the mixture into several different substances. 这个过程将混合物分离成几种不同的物质。

| 近义词：separate, divide, split, refine, distill | 反义词：combine, mix, blend | 词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fractionatus*, past participle of *fractionare* "to break," from Latin *fractio* "a breaking," from *frangere* "to break."
