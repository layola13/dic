attirement:/əˈtaɪərmənt/

n.|装备|Equipment|The knight's attire included a shining sword and sturdy shield. 骑士的装备包括一把闪亮的剑和一面坚固的盾牌。|Synonyms: equipment, gear, paraphernalia|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
