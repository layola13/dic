connutritious:/kəˌnjuːˈtrɪʃəs/
adjective
共同营养的; 促进共同生长的
Growing together and providing nourishment to each other.
例句：The connutritious relationship between the two plants allowed them both to thrive. 这两种植物间的营养共生关系使它们都能茁壮成长。

例句：Scientists are studying the connutritious properties of certain fungi and their host plants. 科学家正在研究某些真菌及其寄主植物的营养共生特性。

近义词：symbiotic, mutualistic
反义词：parasitic, antagonistic
词性：adjective

词源：
From con- (together) + nutritious.  "Con-" comes from Latin "cum" meaning "with, together." "Nutritious" comes from Latin "nutrire" meaning "to nourish."
