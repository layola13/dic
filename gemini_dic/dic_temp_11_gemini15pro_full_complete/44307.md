croon:/kruːn/
verb
低吟浅唱，哼唱，柔声说
to sing or speak in a soft, gentle voice
例句：He crooned a lullaby to his baby daughter. 他低声哼唱摇篮曲哄他的宝贝女儿。

例句：The singer crooned romantic songs in a smoky voice. 歌手用低沉沙哑的声音哼唱着浪漫的歌曲。

近义词：hum, sing, murmur, warble
反义词：shout, yell, scream
词性：v.

croon:/kruːn/
noun
低吟浅唱，哼唱
a soft, gentle singing or speaking voice
例句：The mother's gentle croon soothed the crying baby. 母亲温柔的哼唱声安抚了哭闹的婴儿。

例句：The lovers walked along the beach, listening to the croon of the waves. 恋人们沿着海滩散步，听着海浪的低吟。

近义词：hum, song, murmur
反义词：shout, yell, scream
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *kronen, of imitative origin.
