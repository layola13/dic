chegoe:/ˈtʃiːɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈtʃiːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|沙蚤，吉哥蚤|A tropical flea (Tunga penetrans) that burrows into the skin, especially of the feet, causing inflammation and ulceration.|The chegoe burrowed into his foot, causing a painful lesion. 沙蚤钻进了他的脚，引起疼痛的病灶。|Synonyms: jigger, chigger, sand flea|Antonyms: None|Noun

chegoe:/ˈtʃiːɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈtʃiːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|沙蚤病|Infestation with chigoes/chegoes.|He contracted chegoe while walking barefoot on the beach. 他在沙滩上赤脚行走时感染了沙蚤病。|Synonyms: jigger infestation, chigger infestation|Antonyms: None|Noun
