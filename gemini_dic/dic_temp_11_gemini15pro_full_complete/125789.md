muricated:/ˈmjʊrɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.|有刺的，粗糙的|Covered with short, sharp points or prickles.|The muricated surface of the seashell made it difficult to hold.|近义词：spiny, prickly, thorny, barbed|反义词:smooth, soft|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *mūrīcātus*, past participle of *mūrīcāre* (“to make like a murex”), from *mūrex* (“murex, a kind of shellfish”).  The murex shell is known for its spiky, rough exterior.
