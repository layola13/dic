adopted: /əˈdɑːptɪd/ | adj. | 被收养的；被采用的 |  Legally taken by another person as one's own child or taken up and used. |  Example 1: They have two adopted children and one biological child. 他们有两个领养的孩子和一个亲生孩子。

Example 2:  The company adopted a new marketing strategy. 这家公司采用了一种新的营销策略。 | Synonyms: fostered, accepted, chosen, embraced | Antonyms: biological, natural, rejected, abandoned | Word Type: Adjective

adopted: /əˈdɑːptɪd/ | v. |  收养；采用；采取 | To take another person's child legally as one's own or to start to use a particular method or to show support for an idea. | Example 1:  They adopted a baby girl from China. 他们从中国收养了一个女婴。

Example 2: The school adopted a new approach to teaching languages. 学校采用了一种新的语言教学方法。 | Synonyms: foster, choose, embrace, endorse | Antonyms: reject, abandon, discard | Word Type: Verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adoptāre*, from ad- ‘to’ + optāre ‘choose’.
