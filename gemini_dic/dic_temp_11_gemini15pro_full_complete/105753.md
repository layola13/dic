király: /ˈkirɑːj/ (Hungarian pronunciation)| adj. n.| king, royal, regal; excellent, great|  "Király" is a Hungarian word that primarily means "king".  It can also be used as an adjective to describe something royal or regal.  In informal contexts, it can also mean "excellent" or "great".| He was a true király, beloved by his people.
