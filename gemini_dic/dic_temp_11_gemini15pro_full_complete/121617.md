misotheism:/ˌmɪsəˈθiːɪzəm/ | n. |仇神论；憎恨神|Hatred of God or gods.  A stronger sentiment than atheism, which is simply the lack of belief in God or gods. |例句：Misotheism, unlike atheism, doesn't simply deny the existence of God, it expresses hatred towards him.  仇神论不同于无神论，它不仅仅是否认神的存在，而是表达对神的憎恨。

例句：His writings seemed to express a form of misotheism, railing against a cruel and uncaring deity. 他的作品似乎表达了一种仇神论的形式，抨击一个残酷无情的的神。|近义词：theophobia (对神的恐惧), antitheism (反神论) |反义词: theophily (对神的爱), theism (有神论) |词性: noun
