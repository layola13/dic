gleamingly:/ˈɡliːmɪŋli/| adv.|闪亮地，闪烁地|In a gleaming manner; with a gleam or gleams.  Shining brightly; radiating light.|例句：The polished silver gleamed brightly. 磨光的银子闪闪发亮。|近义词：brightly, radiantly, sparklingly, lustrously|反义词:dully, dimly|词性:副词

词源:
gleamingly 源于 gleam，gleam 源于古英语 *glæm，意为“shine, flash.”  -ingly 是一个副词后缀，表示“以...方式”。

用法说明:
Gleamingly 是一个副词，用来描述某物闪耀或发光的方式。它通常用来修饰动词，表示动作以闪耀的方式进行。

例句：
The sun gleamed brightly in the sky.
太阳在天空中闪耀着光芒。

Her eyes gleamed with excitement.
她的眼睛闪耀着兴奋的光芒。

The newly waxed car gleamed gleamingly in the sunlight.
新打过蜡的汽车在阳光下闪闪发光。

The sword gleamed gleamingly in the warrior's hand.
战士手中的剑闪闪发光。


近义词:
brightly, shiningly, radiantly, sparklingly, lustrously

反义词:
dully, dimly, faintly
