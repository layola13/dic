amianthiform:/æmiˈænθɪfɔːrm/
adjective
石棉状的，像石棉的
Resembling amianthus; having a fine, fibrous, flexible structure.
The mineral had an amianthiform appearance, with its thin, flexible fibers. 这种矿物具有石棉状外观，纤维细而柔韧。
The rock displayed amianthiform qualities, making it easily mistaken for asbestos. 这块岩石呈现出石棉状的性质，很容易被误认为是石棉。
近义词:asbestiform, fibrous
反义词:compact, solid
词性:adjective
词源:From Greek *ἀμίαντος (amiantos, “undefiled, pure; asbestos”) +‎ -iform.
