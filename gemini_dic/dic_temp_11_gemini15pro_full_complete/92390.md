hypocrystalline:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈkrɪstəlaɪn/
adj.
半晶质的，部分结晶的
Partly crystalline; composed of crystals of more than one mineral, some of which are very small or indistinct.
The rock sample was determined to be hypocrystalline, containing both visible crystals and a fine-grained matrix. 这块岩石样本被确定为半晶质的，包含可见晶体和细粒基质。
hypocrystalline basalt exhibits a texture with some larger crystals embedded in a finer-grained groundmass. 半晶质玄武岩呈现出一种质地，其中一些较大的晶体嵌入在更细粒的基质中。
近义词:hemicrystalline
反义词:holocrystalline
词性:adjective
