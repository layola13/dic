lias:/ˈlaɪæs/ /ˈliːæs/| n.|岩层，地层（尤指早侏罗世地层）|A bluish-grey limestone, shale, or marl, occurring in western Europe, belonging to the Lower Jurassic period and containing ammonites and other fossils.  早侏罗世的地层，蓝灰色的石灰岩、页岩或泥灰岩，产于西欧，含有菊石和其他化石。|The dinosaur fossils were found in the Lias.  这些恐龙化石是在早侏罗世地层中发现的。|Synonyms: stratum, layer, formation|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific geological formation)|noun
