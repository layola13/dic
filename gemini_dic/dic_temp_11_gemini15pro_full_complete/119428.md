micrograph: /ˈmaɪkrəɡræf/ | n. | 显微照片，显微图 | A photograph taken through a microscope. |  This micrograph shows the structure of a cell. 这张显微照片显示了细胞的结构。

| The micrograph revealed the presence of bacteria. 这张显微照片揭示了细菌的存在。 | 近义词: photomicrograph | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From micro- + -graph.  "micro-" comes from Greek "mikros" meaning "small". "-graph" comes from Greek "graphein" meaning "to write" or "to draw".
