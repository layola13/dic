humorously:/ˈhjuːmərəsli/ | adv. | 幽默地；滑稽地 | In a way that is funny or amusing. | She humorously recounted the story of her disastrous first date. 她幽默地讲述了她灾难性的第一次约会的故事。 | He humorously imitated his boss's mannerisms. 他滑稽地模仿了他老板的举止。 | 近义词: comically, wittily, amusingly, jokingly | 反义词: seriously, solemnly, gravely | 词性: 副词

词源:  From humor + -ous + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin _humor_ "fluid," later "temperament, mood."
