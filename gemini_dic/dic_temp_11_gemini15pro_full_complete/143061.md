outbought:/ˌaʊtˈbɔːt/
verb
买过，胜过
To buy more than someone else, especially a competitor.
Berkshire Hathaway outbought its competitors in the stock market last quarter.  伯克希尔·哈撒韦公司在上个季度的股票市场上买过了它的竞争对手。
outbid, outspend
underspend
v.
