coved:/koʊvd/| adj.|穹顶状的，凹形的，像海湾的|A coved ceiling is one that curves inward where it meets the walls, creating a rounded, concave appearance.  穹顶形的天花板是指在天花板与墙壁相接处向内弯曲，形成圆形凹面的外观。|The dining room had a beautifully coved ceiling. 餐厅有一个漂亮的穹顶天花板。|近义词：arched, vaulted, concave|反义词:convex|词性:形容词
coved:/koʊvd/| n.|小海湾，凹处|A cove is a small sheltered bay.  海湾是一个小的隐蔽的海湾。|We anchored the boat in the cove. 我们把船停泊在海湾里。|近义词：bay, inlet, creek|反义词:headland, promontory|词性:名词
