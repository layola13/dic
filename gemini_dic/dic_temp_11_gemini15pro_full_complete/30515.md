ceos:/ˌsiːiːˈoʊz/ | n. | 首席执行官们 (复数) | CEOs is the plural form of CEO, which stands for Chief Executive Officer.  The CEO is the highest-ranking executive in a company, responsible for making major corporate decisions, managing the overall operations and resources of a company, and acting as the main point of communication between the board of directors and corporate operations. | The CEOs of several major tech companies met to discuss industry trends. 几家大型科技公司的首席执行官们开会讨论行业趋势。| CEOs are often highly visible and represent the face of their companies. 首席执行官们通常非常引人注目，代表着他们公司的形象。 | synonyms: chief executives, heads, leaders | antonyms: subordinates, employees | noun
