kick-start:/ˈkɪkˌstɑːrt/
verb
启动；重新启动；促进；使…迅速开始
To give a boost or impetus to (something) so that it can begin or develop more quickly.
The government is trying to kick-start the economy. 政府正试图刺激经济。
jump-start, boost, revitalize
hinder, obstruct
词性:动词

kick-start:/ˈkɪkˌstɑːrt/
noun
启动；促进；刺激
An act or instance of kick-starting something; a boost or impetus.
The tax cuts are intended to provide a kick-start to the economy. 减税旨在刺激经济。
boost, impetus, stimulus
hindrance, obstacle
词性:名词
