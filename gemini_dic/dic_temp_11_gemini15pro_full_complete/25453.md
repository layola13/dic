bulldogging:/ˈbʊldɒɡɪŋ/ | n. | 摔牛；(非正式)顽强的，坚韧不拔的 |The act of wrestling a steer to the ground by grabbing its horns and twisting its neck.  Informally, it can also refer to a tenacious and persistent approach to a task. | He's good at bulldogging. 他很擅长摔牛。|  He showed his bulldogging spirit by working late every night to finish the project. 他每天晚上工作到很晚完成这个项目，展现了他顽强不拔的精神。 | synonyms: wrestling, grappling, tenacity, persistence | antonyms:  yielding, surrender, weakness | noun

bulldogging:/ˈbʊldɒɡɪŋ/ | adj. | 顽强的，坚韧不拔的 |  Characterized by tenacity and persistence.  | He has a bulldogging attitude towards problem-solving. 他解决问题有股韧劲。 | synonyms: tenacious, persistent, resolute, determined | antonyms: yielding, irresolute, wavering | adjective
