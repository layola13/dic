outslandered: /aʊtˈslændərd/
Verb
诽谤，诋毁
Having been falsely and maliciously spoken of.  Damaged in reputation.
例句：He felt he had been outslandered by the newspaper article. 他觉得他被报纸文章诽谤了。

例句：She was outslandered by her rivals who spread false rumors about her. 她的竞争对手散布关于她的谣言，诽谤了她。


近义词：defamed, vilified, slandered, calumniated, libeled
反义词：praised, lauded, commended, extolled
词性：verb (past participle)

词源:
From out- + slander, from Old French esclandre (see scandal).
