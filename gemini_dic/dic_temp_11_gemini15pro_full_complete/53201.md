diptychous:/ˈdɪptɪkəs/
adjective
双联的；双折的；成对的
Having two leaves or folds; consisting of two corresponding parts or panels.
The diptychous structure of the seed pod allowed it to split open easily.  这种双瓣结构的豆荚很容易裂开。
The artist presented a diptychous painting, showcasing two distinct yet related scenes.  艺术家展示了一幅双联画，展示了两个截然不同却又相关的场景。
近义词: bipartite, twofold, double
反义词: single, undivided
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *diptychos* (two-folded), from *di-* (two) + *ptychē* (fold).
