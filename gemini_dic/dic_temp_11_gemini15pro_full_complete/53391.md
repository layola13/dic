disagrees:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːz/
verb
不同意；有分歧
differs in opinion; does not agree
He disagrees with his parents about his career choice. 他和他的父母在择业问题上意见不一致。
近义词:differs, clashes, conflicts, objects
反义词:agrees, concurs, assents
词性:verb

disagree:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/
verb
不同意；有分歧
differs in opinion; does not agree
The two experts disagreed on the best course of action.  两位专家对最佳行动方案意见不一。
近义词:differ, clash, conflict, object
反义词:agree, concur, assent
词性:verb
