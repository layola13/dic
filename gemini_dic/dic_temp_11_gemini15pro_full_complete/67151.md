exposers:/ɪkˈspəʊzəz/ | n. | 揭发者，揭露者，曝光者 |  A person or thing that exposes something, especially something discreditable.  | The exposers of the scandal were hailed as heroes. 丑闻的揭露者被誉为英雄。 | synonyms: whistleblower, informer, revealer, uncoverer | antonyms: concealer, hider, suppressor | noun

expose:/ɪkˈspəʊz/ | v. | 揭露，揭发，曝光 | To make something visible or known, typically something discreditable. | The documentary exposed the corruption within the company.  这部纪录片揭露了公司内部的腐败。| synonyms: reveal, uncover, divulge, unveil | antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress | verb
