gasserian: /ɡəˈsɪəriən/
adjective
加塞尔神经节的；三叉神经节的
Relating to the gasserian ganglion (also known as the trigeminal ganglion).
The gasserian ganglion is a sensory ganglion of the trigeminal nerve (cranial nerve V).  It contains the cell bodies of the sensory neurons that supply the face, mouth, and nasal cavity.
