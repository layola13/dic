back-off:/ˌbæk ˈɒf/ (UK) /ˌbæk ˈɔːf/ (US)| phrasal verb |后退，放弃，停止干预|To move backwards away from something or someone; to stop being involved in a situation, especially in order to allow other people to deal with it.|The police told the protesters to back off. 警察告诉抗议者退后。

The company backed off from its original proposal. 公司放弃了最初的提案。|近义词：retreat, withdraw, desist|反义词：advance, approach, interfere|词性：动词短语


back-off:/ˈbækˌɒf/ (UK) /ˈbækˌɔːf/ (US)| n. |后退，放弃|The act of moving backwards or withdrawing from a situation.|The union's back-off from its demands surprised management. 工会放弃其要求令管理层感到惊讶。|近义词：retreat, withdrawal|反义词：advance, approach|词性：名词
