overfilm: /ˈoʊvərˌfɪlm/ | n. v. | 薄膜，覆盖层；（用薄膜）覆盖 | A thin film or coating; to cover with a thin film. |

例句：
A thin overfilm of oil coated the water.
一层薄薄的油膜覆盖在水面上。


The jeweler overfilmed the silver with a protective lacquer.
珠宝商在银器上涂了一层保护漆。


近义词：coating, film, layer | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "uncover," "expose") | 词性: noun, verb |


overfilm: /ˌoʊvərˈfɪlm/ | v. | 拍摄附加镜头；在…上加拍（影片）| To film additional footage over previously filmed material. |

例句：
They had to overfilm several scenes due to technical difficulties.
由于技术问题，他们不得不重拍几个场景。


The director decided to overfilm the ending to make it more dramatic.
导演决定重新拍摄结局，使其更具戏剧性。


近义词：reshoot, refilm | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: verb |


词源: "over-" (prefix indicating covering or exceeding) + "film" (noun referring to a thin layer or a motion picture).
