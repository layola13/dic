brashly:/ˈbræʃli/ | adv. | 轻率地，鲁莽地，傲慢地 | In a self-assertive and sometimes noisy or disrespectful way. | He brashly announced his intention to run for president. 他傲慢地宣布他打算竞选总统。| 近义词: impetuously, recklessly, boldly, rashly | 反义词: cautiously, carefully, timidly | 词性: 副词
词源: From brash + -ly.  Brash itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to a dialectal word meaning "attack," "rush," or "shatter."
