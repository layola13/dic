matelote: /ˌmætəˈloʊt/ (US), /ˌmætəˈlɒt/ (UK)| n.| 马特洛克鱼汤（一种法国红酒炖鱼，通常用淡水鱼）| A French stew of fish, usually freshwater fish, cooked in red wine.| We had a delicious matelote for dinner last night. 昨晚我们晚餐吃了一顿美味的马特洛克鱼汤。|近义词: fish stew, bourguignonne (when made with beef)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "grilled fish")|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *matelote*, from *matelot* "sailor," from Dutch *mattenoot* "sailor, shipmate." The dish was traditionally prepared by sailors.
