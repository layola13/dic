benefactress:/ˈbɛnɪfæktərɪs/| n.|女施主，女恩人|A woman who gives money or other help to a person or cause.|例句：The local library was built thanks to a generous benefactress.  这家当地图书馆的建成多亏了一位慷慨的女施主。

例句：She became the college's greatest benefactress, donating millions of dollars over the years. 多年来，她向这所大学捐赠了数百万美元，成为了其最大的女捐助者。|近义词：patron, donor, philanthropist|反义词:malefactor|noun
