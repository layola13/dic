cylindricalness:/ˌsɪlɪnˈdrɪkəlnəs/
n.
圆柱形，圆柱状
The state or quality of being cylindrical.
The cylindricalness of the pipe made it easy to roll.  这根管子的圆柱形状使它很容易滚动。
The architect admired the cylindricalness of the columns.  建筑师很欣赏这些柱子的圆柱形状。
近义词: cylindricality
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shapes like "sphericality," "cuboidness")
