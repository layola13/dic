as-of:/ˌæz ˈʌv/ | prep. |截至；到...为止 |Indicates a specific date or time at which something is true or valid. |The price list is valid as of July 1st.  价格表自7月1日起生效。| Synonyms: from, effective, starting | Antonyms: after, beyond | preposition

as-of:/ˌæz ˈʌv/ | adj. |截至...的 |Relating to a specific date or time.  |The as-of date for the report is December 31st.  这份报告的截止日期是12月31日。| Synonyms: current, effective | Antonyms: past, outdated | adjective
