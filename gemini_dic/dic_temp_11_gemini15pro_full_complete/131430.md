nonclosely:/nɒnˈkloʊsli/ | adv. | 不紧密地，不密切地 | Not in a close manner; loosely or distantly. | The houses were nonclosely spaced, each with a large garden.  这些房子间隔不紧密，每个房子都有一个大花园。| The topics were nonclosely related, making the presentation disjointed.  这些主题联系不紧密，使得演示文稿不连贯。 | 近义词: loosely, distantly, sparsely | 反义词: closely, tightly, densely | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "closely" (adverbial form of "close")
