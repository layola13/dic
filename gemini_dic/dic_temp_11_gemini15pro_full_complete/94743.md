improvident:/ɪmˈprɒvɪdənt/ (US) /ɪmˈprɑːvɪdənt/| adj.|浪费的；无远见的；不节俭的|Not having or showing foresight; spendthrift or thoughtless. Failing to provide for future needs.|He was improvident and quickly spent all his inheritance. 他挥霍无度，很快就花光了所有的遗产。|Synonyms: wasteful, extravagant, reckless, imprudent|Antonyms: thrifty, frugal, provident, cautious|Adjective
