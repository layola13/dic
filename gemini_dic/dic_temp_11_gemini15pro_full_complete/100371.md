interstratified:/ˌɪntərˈstrætɪfaɪd/
adjective
间层状的，互层的
Formed, arranged, or deposited in layers with others of a different character; interbedded.  A rock consisting of alternating layers of different materials is said to be interstratified.
The cliffs are composed of interstratified sandstone and shale. 这些悬崖由交替层状的砂岩和页岩组成。
The soil profile revealed interstratified layers of clay and sand. 土壤剖面显示出粘土和沙子的互层。
layered, stratified, laminated
homogeneous, uniform
adjective
