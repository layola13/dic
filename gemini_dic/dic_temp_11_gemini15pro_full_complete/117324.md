meddles:/ˈmɛdəlz/| v. |干涉，干预，管闲事|To interfere in something that is not one's concern.|She always meddles in other people's affairs. 她总是干涉别人的事情.|近义词：interfere, intervene, intrude|反义词:ignore, abstain|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *medler, from Vulgar Latin *misculāre, frequentative of Latin misceō (“I mix”).
