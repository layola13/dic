outscooped:/ˌaʊtˈskuːpt/ |v. |抢先报道；捷足先登 |To publish a news story before a competitor; to get a scoop on. |The rival newspaper outscooped us on the mayor's resignation. 对手报纸抢先报道了市长辞职的消息。| synonyms: outmaneuvered, preempted, forestalled | antonyms:  scooped, beaten | verb

outscooped:/ˌaʊtˈskuːpt/ |adj. |被抢先报道的| Having been outmaneuvered in reporting a news story. |The newspaper felt outscooped and embarrassed. 这家报纸感到被抢先报道了，很尴尬。| synonyms: preempted, beaten | antonyms: first, leading | adjective
