czaristic:/zɑːrˈɪstɪk/
adjective
沙皇的；沙皇制度的；专横的
Of or relating to a czar; characteristic of a czar.  Also, autocratic or domineering.
The czaristic regime was overthrown in 1917.  沙皇政权在1917年被推翻了。
The CEO's czaristic management style alienated many employees.  首席执行官专横的管理风格疏远了许多员工。
Synonyms: autocratic, dictatorial, tyrannical, despotic, imperial
Antonyms: democratic, egalitarian
词性: 形容词
