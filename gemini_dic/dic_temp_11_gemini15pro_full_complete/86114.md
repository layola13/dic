heartbrokenly:/ˈhɑːrtˌbroʊkənli/| adv.| 心碎地，悲伤地|In a way that shows that someone is extremely sad because their relationship with someone has ended.|She wept heartbrokenly when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后心碎地哭了起来。|近义词：sorrowfully, sadly, despairingly, mournfully|反义词：joyfully, happily|词性:副词
词源: From heartbroken + -ly. Heartbroken is from heart + broken.
