cupolaed:/ˈkjuːpələd/| adj.|圆顶形的，有圆屋顶的|Having a cupola or dome.  Resembling a cupola or dome.|The cupolaed roof of the church was a prominent feature on the skyline. 教堂的圆顶屋顶是天际线上一个显著的特征。|domed, vaulted|flat|adjective

Etymology: From cupola + -ed.  "Cupola" comes from the Italian word "cupola," meaning "small cask" or "dome," ultimately from the Late Latin "cupula," diminutive of "cupa," meaning "tub" or "cask."
