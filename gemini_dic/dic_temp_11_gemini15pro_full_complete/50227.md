depend-from:/dɪˈpɛnd frɒm/ | phrasal verb | 从...垂下，依赖于... |  This usage is archaic and rarely used. It means to hang down from or to be supported by. |  The chandelier depended from the ceiling. 枝形吊灯从天花板上垂下来。 | 近义词: hang from, dangle from, be suspended from | 反义词: support, uphold | 词性: 动词短语
