overrichness:/ˈoʊvərˈrɪtʃnəs/| n.|过度丰富，过剩|An overrichness of nutrients in the soil can be detrimental to some plants. 土壤中养分过度丰富会对某些植物有害。|例句：The overrichness of the dessert made it too sweet for my taste. 这道甜点太丰富了，对我来说太甜了。

例句：The overrichness of detail in the painting made it difficult to appreciate the overall composition.  这幅画细节过于丰富，难以欣赏整体构图。


近义词：abundance, excess, surplus, plethora|反义词:scarcity, lack, deficiency, dearth|词性:noun
