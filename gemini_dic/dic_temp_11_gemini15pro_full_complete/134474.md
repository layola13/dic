nonknowledgeable:/ˌnɑːnˈnɑːlɪdʒəbl/
adjective
无知的，缺乏知识的
Lacking knowledge or information about a particular subject or general knowledge.
She was nonknowledgeable about the history of art. 她对艺术史一无所知。

The new intern was nonknowledgeable about the company's procedures.  新来的实习生对公司的程序不了解。


Synonym: ignorant, uninformed, uneducated, unlearned
Antonym: knowledgeable, informed, educated, learned, erudite
词性: 形容词
