caducibranchiate:/kəˌduːsɪˈbræŋkiət/
adjective
蜕膜鳃的，幼态持续的
Having persistent larval gills; relating to or denoting an amphibian that retains its larval gills throughout life, such as the axolotl.
The caducibranchiate axolotl is a fascinating creature.  墨西哥钝口螈是一种迷人的生物。
The museum's exhibit showcased several caducibranchiate species.  博物馆的展览展示了几种幼态持续的两栖动物。
近义词: neotenous, perennibranchiate
反义词: metamorphosed
