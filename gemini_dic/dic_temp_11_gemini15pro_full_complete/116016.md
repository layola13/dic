marshmallow:/ˈmɑːrʃˌmæloʊ/ (US), /ˈmɑːʃˌmæləʊ/ (UK)| n.|棉花糖; 药物蜀葵根; 柔弱的人|A soft, spongy confection made from sugar, gelatin, and often corn syrup.  Also, the root of the marshmallow plant, formerly used medicinally.|例句：I roasted marshmallows over the campfire. 我在篝火上烤棉花糖。

例句：She has a marshmallow heart. 她心肠很软。|近义词：confection, sweet, treat|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a confection)|词性: Noun
