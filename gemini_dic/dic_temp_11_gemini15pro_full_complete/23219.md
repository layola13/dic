bowhead:/ˈboʊˌhɛd/| n.|弓头鲸|A baleen whale (Balaena mysticetus) of Arctic seas, having a large head and a deeply arched mouth.|例句：The bowhead whale is the only baleen whale that lives year-round in Arctic waters. 弓头鲸是唯一一种全年生活在北极水域的须鲸。

例句：Bowhead whales can live for over 200 years. 弓头鲸可以活200多年。|近义词：whale, right whale|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bow + head, referring to the highly arched shape of the upper jaw.
