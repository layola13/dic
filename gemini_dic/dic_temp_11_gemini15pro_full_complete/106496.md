krameric: /ˈkreɪmərɪk/ | adj. | 克莱默的；与克莱默有关的 | Relating to or characteristic of Kramer, a fictional character in the American television sitcom *Seinfeld*. |  He had a very Krameric entrance, bursting through the door without knocking. 他以一种非常克莱默式的姿态进门，没有敲门就冲了进来。|  eccentric, quirky, unusual | conventional, ordinary | adjective

Etymology:
Derived from the surname of Cosmo Kramer, a fictional character in the television series *Seinfeld*. The name "Kramer" itself has German origins.
