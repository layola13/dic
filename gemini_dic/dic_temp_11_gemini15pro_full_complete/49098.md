deiformity:/ˌdiːɪˈfɔːrmɪti/ | n. |神性，神的样子|The artist attempted to capture the deiformity of the goddess in his sculpture.  艺术家试图在他的雕塑中捕捉女神的神性。| English Explanation: The state or quality of having a godlike form or nature; resemblance to a god or goddess. | Example: The ancient Egyptians believed in the pharaoh's deiformity. 古代埃及人相信法老的神性。| Synonym: divinity, godhood, godlikeness | Antonym: mortality, humanness | Noun
