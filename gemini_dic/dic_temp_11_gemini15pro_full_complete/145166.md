overassuredly:/ˌoʊvərəˈʃʊrədli/
adverb
过于自信地；过分自信地
in an overassured manner; too confidently
He overassuredly stated he would win the race, despite having never run a marathon before. 他过分自信地声称他会赢得比赛，尽管他以前从未跑过马拉松。
She overassuredly presented her project, ignoring the valid concerns of the panel. 她过于自信地展示了她的项目，忽略了专家组的合理担忧。
近义词:cockily, arrogantly, presumptuously
反义词:hesitantly, timidly, modestly
词性:adverb

词源:
From over- + assured + -ly.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Assured" comes from the Old French "assurer," meaning "to make safe or secure," ultimately derived from the Latin "ad-" (to) + "securus" (secure).  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
