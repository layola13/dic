love-in:/ˈlʌvɪn/| n.|爱之集会；和平集会|A gathering or demonstration, especially in the 1960s, expressing love, peace, and togetherness, often involving music, poetry, and other forms of artistic expression.|例句：The love-in attracted thousands of people advocating for peace. 爱之集会吸引了成千上万倡导和平的人们。

例句：They organized a love-in to protest against the war. 他们组织了一场爱之集会来抗议战争。|近义词：gathering, festival, celebration, demonstration|反义词：protest, riot, demonstration (if the demonstration is against something)|词性:名词
