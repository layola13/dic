amniota:/ˈæmniˌoʊtə/ | n. |羊膜动物|An amniote is a vertebrate animal whose embryo develops inside an amniotic sac. This sac contains amniotic fluid, which cushions and protects the developing embryo.|Birds, reptiles, and mammals are all amniotes. 鸟类、爬行动物和哺乳动物都是羊膜动物。|synonyms:  amniotic vertebrate|antonyms: anamniote|noun
