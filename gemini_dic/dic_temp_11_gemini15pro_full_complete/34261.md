clapping:/ˈklæpɪŋ/| n.|鼓掌，拍手|the sound of two flat surfaces being hit together repeatedly, especially the hands|The audience erupted in clapping at the end of the performance.  演出结束时，观众爆发出热烈的掌声。|近义词：applause, ovation|反义词:booing|noun

clapping:/ˈklæpɪŋ/| adj.|热烈的，精彩的|used to describe something worthy of applause|It was a clapping performance! 这是一场精彩的演出！|近义词：excellent, splendid|反义词:terrible|adjective
