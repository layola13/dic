deritualize:/diːˈrɪtʃuəlaɪz/
verb
使非宗教仪式化；使世俗化；去除仪式
To remove the ritualistic elements from something; to make something less formal or ceremonial.
The wedding ceremony was deritualized, focusing more on personal vows than traditional practices. 婚礼仪式被简化了，更注重个人誓言而不是传统习俗。
The company deritualized its annual performance review process, making it more conversational and less formal. 公司简化了年度绩效考核流程，使其更具对话性和更不正式。
secularize, informalize, modernize
ritualize, formalize, traditionalize
v.
