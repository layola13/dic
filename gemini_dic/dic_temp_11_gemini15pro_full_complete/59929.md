edgeless:/ˈɛdʒləs/
adj.
无边的，无限的，没有棱角的
Lacking a sharp edge; blunt. Also, boundless or infinite.
The artist created an edgeless painting, blurring the lines between reality and imagination. 艺术家创作了一幅无边界的画作，模糊了现实与想象之间的界限。
The vast, edgeless desert stretched before them. 广袤无垠的沙漠展现在他们面前。
近义词: boundless, infinite, limitless, unbounded, continuous
反义词: edged, sharp, bounded, finite, limited
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From edge + -less.  "Edge" comes from Old English *ecg, from Proto-Germanic *agjō.  "-less" comes from Old English -lēas, meaning "without," from Proto-Germanic *-lausaz.
