flooder's:/ˈflʌdərz/
noun
淹没者(的),泛滥者(的),充满者(的)
Possessive form of "flooder," referring to something belonging to or associated with a person or thing that floods.  It indicates ownership or origin.
The flooder's boat was swept away in the current. 淹没者的船被水流冲走了。
Synonyms: inundator's, overwhelmer's
Antonyms: drainer's, emptier's
