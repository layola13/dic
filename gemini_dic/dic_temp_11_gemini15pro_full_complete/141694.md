opuscle: /ˈɒpəskəl/ (BrE) /ˈɑːpəskəl/ (AmE) | n. | 小体，微粒 | A minute particle or granule, especially one in an organism. |  Red blood cells, or erythrocytes, are small, round opuscles that contain hemoglobin. 红细胞，或称红血球，是含有血红蛋白的小而圆的微粒。

|  Sensory opuscles are microscopic structures in the skin that detect touch. 感官小体是皮肤中检测触觉的微观结构。

| 近义词: corpuscle, particle, granule | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin *opūsculum, diminutive of opus (“work”).
