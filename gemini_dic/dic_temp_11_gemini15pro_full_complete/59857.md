ectropion:/ɛkˈtroʊpiən/
n.
外翻;睑外翻
the eversion of an edge or margin, as of an eyelid.
Ectropion is a condition in which the lower eyelid turns outwards. 下眼睑外翻是一种下眼睑向外翻转的疾病。
The ophthalmologist diagnosed her with ectropion. 眼科医生诊断她患有睑外翻。
近义词:eversion
反义词:entropion
词性:noun
