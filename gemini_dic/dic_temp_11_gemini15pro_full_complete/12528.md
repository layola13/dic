asphyctic: /əsˈfɪktɪk/ | adj. | 窒息的；导致窒息的 | Relating to or causing asphyxia; suffocating. | The victim died from asphyctic fumes. 受害者死于窒息性烟雾。 |  The asphyctic smoke filled the room. 窒息性的烟雾充满了房间。 | 近义词: suffocating, choking, smothering | 反义词: reviving, resuscitating | 词性: adjective

asphyxia: /æsˈfɪksiə/ | n. | 窒息 | A condition arising when the body is deprived of oxygen, causing unconsciousness or death; suffocation. | The autopsy revealed that the cause of death was asphyxia. 尸检显示死因是窒息。 | He died of asphyxia due to drowning. 他因溺水而窒息死亡。 | 近义词: suffocation, choking, smothering | 反义词: respiration, ventilation | 词性: noun
