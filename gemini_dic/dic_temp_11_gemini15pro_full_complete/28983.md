carte:/kɑːrt/| n.|菜单，菜肴，纸牌，地图|A list of dishes available at a restaurant; a playing card; a map. |例句：The restaurant's carte offered a wide variety of dishes. 这家餐厅的菜单提供各种各样的菜肴。

例句：He played the queen of hearts carte. 他出了一张红心Q。|近义词：menu, dish, card, map|反义词: None|词性:noun


carte:/kɑːrt/| n.|点菜，点菜的权利|The freedom or ability to choose what one wants to eat. |例句：We were given carte blanche to order whatever we wanted.  我们可以随意点任何我们想吃的东西。

例句：With carte, you can customize your meal. 有了点菜的权利，你可以定制你的餐点。|近义词：choice, option, selection|反义词:restriction|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "carte," from Latin "charta," meaning "paper, papyrus."  The word evolved to encompass various meanings related to things written or printed on paper, such as menus, playing cards, and maps.
