commercialistic:/kəˈmɜːrʃəlˈɪstɪk/
adjective
商业化的，唯利是图的
Characterized by or emphasizing the principles of commerce; profit-oriented.
The museum has become increasingly commercialistic in recent years, focusing more on gift shop sales than educational exhibits.  近年来，这家博物馆变得越来越商业化，更加注重礼品店的销售，而不是教育展览。
synonyms: mercenary, materialistic, profiteering
antonyms: altruistic, philanthropic, non-profit
词性: adjective


commercialistic:/kəˈmɜːrʃəlˈɪstɪk/
adjective
商业主义的
Exhibiting or stressing commercialism.
His approach to art is very commercialistic; he only paints what he thinks will sell. 他对待艺术的态度非常商业化；他只画他认为能卖出去的东西。
synonyms: mercantile, money-minded, businesslike
antonyms: artistic, idealistic, non-commercial
词性: adjective
