hardliner:/ˈhɑːrdˌlaɪnər/ | n. |强硬派；强硬路线者| someone who has very fixed beliefs and who is unwilling to change them, especially in politics| The hardliners in the party refused to compromise.  党内的强硬派拒绝妥协。| synonyms: extremist, zealot, diehard,  | antonyms: moderate, conciliator, compromiser | noun

hardliner:/ˈhɑːrdˌlaɪnər/ | adj. |强硬的；强硬路线的|  unwilling to compromise, especially in politics | The government took a hardline stance on the issue. 政府在这个问题上采取了强硬立场。 | synonyms: uncompromising, inflexible, rigid, unyielding | antonyms: flexible, moderate, compromising | adjective
