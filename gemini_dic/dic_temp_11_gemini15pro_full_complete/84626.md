hand-and-glove:/ˌhænd ən ˈɡlʌv/| adj.|密切合作的，关系亲密的|Working hand-and-glove, the two detectives quickly solved the case.  两位侦探密切合作，迅速破案。|English Explanation:  Very closely, as if fitting together perfectly; in close cooperation or association.  The phrase suggests a tight, seamless partnership, like a hand fitting perfectly into a glove.|Example: The politician and the businessman worked hand-and-glove to push the new legislation through.  这位政治家和商人密切合作，推动新立法获得通过。|Synonyms: closely, intimately, collaboratively|Antonyms: independently, separately|Adjective
