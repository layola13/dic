insinuators:/ɪnˈsɪnjuˌeɪtərz/
n.
暗示者；旁敲侧击的人；含沙射影的人
People who insinuate; those who subtly introduce an idea or feeling into the mind of another, often with negative connotations.
The insinuators spread rumors about the candidate's personal life. 那些含沙射影的人散布关于候选人私生活的谣言。
近义词:  gossipmongers, rumourmongers, backbiters, slanderers
反义词:  frank speakers, forthright speakers
