eggman:/ˈɛɡmæn/ | n. | 蛋人；（尤指）讨厌鬼，坏蛋 | A term sometimes used as a derogatory nickname, especially for a person considered foolish or unpleasant.  It was popularized as the name of the villain in the Sonic the Hedgehog video game series. |  He called his rival an eggman, implying he was foolish. 他称他的对手为蛋人，暗示他很愚蠢。 | The mischievous child was labeled an eggman by the frustrated neighbors.  这个淘气的孩子被沮丧的邻居们称为讨厌鬼。 | synonyms: jerk, villain, bad guy, rascal | antonyms: hero, good guy | noun
