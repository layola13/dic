dagon:/ˈdeɪɡɒn/ (US) /ˈdeɪɡən/ (UK)| n. |达贡|A Philistine god, often represented as half-man, half-fish.  A Semitic deity, worshipped especially in Ashdod, Beth Dagon, and Gaza.  Often depicted as a merman-like figure.|The archaeologists unearthed a statue of Dagon in the ruins of the ancient temple. 考古学家在古庙遗址中挖掘出一尊达贡雕像。|The fishermen prayed to Dagon for a bountiful catch. 渔民们向达贡祈祷能捕获丰收的渔获。|近义词: deity, god, idol|反义词: None|Noun
