nonequivocating:/ˌnɒnɪˈkwɪvəkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
明确的，毫不含糊的
Expressing oneself clearly and directly, without ambiguity or hesitation.
Her nonequivocating stance on the issue earned her the respect of her colleagues. 她对这个问题毫不含糊的立场赢得了同事们的尊重。
The CEO gave a nonequivocating denial of the rumors. 首席执行官明确否认了这些谣言。

近义词:unequivocal, unambiguous, clear, direct, forthright, explicit
反义词:equivocal, ambiguous, vague, unclear, indirect, evasive
词性:adjective


词源:
From non- + equivocating (present participle of equivocate).  "Equivocate" comes from Late Latin aequivocatus, past participle of aequivocare "to use words of double meaning," from Latin aequivocus "having a double meaning," from aequi- "equal" + vocare "to call."
