flycatchers:/ˈflaɪˌkætʃərz/ | n. | 捕蝇鸟 | A family of passerine birds that typically feed on insects they catch in flight. |  Many different species of flycatchers can be found in North America.  许多不同种类的捕蝇鸟可以在北美找到。| synonyms:  insectivores, flysnappers | antonyms: (No direct antonym for a type of bird) | noun

flycatcher:/ˈflaɪˌkætʃər/ | n. | 捕蝇草 | Any of various plants, especially of the genus Dionaea, that trap and digest insects. | The Venus flytrap is a popular example of a flycatcher plant.  捕蝇草是捕蝇植物的一个流行例子。| synonyms:  carnivorous plant | antonyms: (No direct antonym for a type of plant) | noun
