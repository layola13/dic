bighting:/ˈbaɪtɪŋ/| adj. n.| 刺骨的，凛冽的；咬；钳住|Sharp and piercing; the act of biting or gripping something firmly.|The bighting wind chilled us to the bone. 刺骨的寒风让我们感到彻骨的寒冷。|The bighting of the trap ensured the animal couldn't escape. 陷阱的咬合确保了动物无法逃脱。|近义词：nipping, piercing, sharp, biting (as a noun: grip, hold)|反义词:gentle, mild, warm (for adj.); release (for noun)|adjective, noun

bight:/baɪt/| n.|绳圈，弯曲；海湾|A loop or bend in a rope; a curve or recess in a coastline.|Make a bight in the rope and secure it to the post. 在绳子上打个圈，然后把它固定在柱子上。|The ship anchored in the bight of the bay. 船停泊在海湾的弯曲处。|近义词：loop, bend, curve, bay, cove|反义词:straight|noun
