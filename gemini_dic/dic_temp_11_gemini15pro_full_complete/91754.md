hyperemesis:/ˌhaɪpərˈemɪsɪs/| n.|妊娠剧吐|severe nausea and vomiting during pregnancy, much more extreme than typical morning sickness|例句：Hyperemesis gravidarum can be a serious complication of pregnancy. 妊娠剧吐可能是怀孕的严重并发症。

例句：She suffered from hyperemesis throughout her first trimester.  她在妊娠的头三个月一直遭受妊娠剧吐的折磨。|近义词：severe vomiting, excessive nausea and vomiting|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider mild nausea)|词性:noun
