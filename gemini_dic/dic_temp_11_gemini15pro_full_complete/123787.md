mootnesses:/ˈmuːtnəsɪz/
n.
无实际意义；不重要；悬而未决
The abstract mootness of the debate made it uninteresting. 这场辩论的抽象性和无实际意义使其变得枯燥乏味。
The mootness of the issue after the election rendered further discussion unnecessary. 选举后，这个问题变得毫无意义，无需进一步讨论。
irrelevance, inconsequence, immateriality
relevance, significance, importance
noun
