fasters:/ˈfæstərz/| n.|禁食者；斋戒者；断食者|People who abstain from food for a period of time, often for religious or health reasons.|Many fasters find that their mental clarity improves after a few days without food.  许多禁食者发现，几天不吃东西后，他们的思维清晰度会提高。|近义词： abstainers, non-eaters |反义词: gluttons, overeaters| noun
