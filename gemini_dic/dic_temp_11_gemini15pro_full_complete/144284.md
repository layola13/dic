outspeak:/ˌaʊtˈspiːk/
verb
胜过…的口才; 比…说得更坦率
to speak louder, longer, or more effectively than someone else
She outspoke her opponent in the debate. 她在辩论中胜过了她的对手。

outspeak:/ˌaʊtˈspiːk/
verb
坦率直言; 大胆说出
to speak frankly and boldly
He outspoke against the corruption in the government. 他坦率地批评了政府的腐败行为。

近义词: outtalk, surpass, exceed
反义词: be quiet, be silent
词性: verb
