imbracery: /ɪmˈbreɪsəri/ | n. | (建筑)窗饰, 花饰窗格;  |Ornamental stonework, especially of an elaborate or openwork design, typically seen in Gothic windows.  It refers to the decorative tracery that supports stained glass in a Gothic window. | The cathedral's imbracery was incredibly detailed, featuring intricate patterns of leaves and flowers.  这座大教堂的窗饰非常细致，以错综复杂的叶子和花卉图案为特色。|  The delicate imbracery filtered the sunlight, creating a kaleidoscope of colors on the floor. 精致的窗饰过滤了阳光，在地板上创造出万花筒般的色彩。| Synonyms: tracery, fretwork, latticework | Antonyms: plainness, simplicity | Noun
