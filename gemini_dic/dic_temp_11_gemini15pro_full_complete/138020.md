noonshun: /ˈnuːnʃʌn/ | n. | 午休；午睡 | A rest or sleep taken at midday. | Example: I usually take a noonshun after lunch. 我通常午饭后午休一会儿。

Example:  She enjoys a quiet noonshun in her hammock. 她喜欢在吊床上安静地午睡。


近义词: nap, siesta, midday rest | 反义词: work, activity | 词性: noun

词源:  A blend of "noon" and "shun" (probably influenced by words like "luncheon").  "Shun" in this context likely carries the sense of "taking shelter" or "avoiding the heat of the day."
