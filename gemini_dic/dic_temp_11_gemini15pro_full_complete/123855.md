moralizes:/ˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪz/
verb
说教；教训
To make moral judgments, especially in a self-righteous or tedious way.
He's always moralizing about the dangers of drinking. 他总是教训人喝酒的危害。
Synonyms: preaches, sermonizes, lectures
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider:  encourages, condones, approves)
词性: 动词

moralize: /ˈmɔːrəlaɪz/
