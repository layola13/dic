oenological:/ˌiːnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 葡萄酒学的；酿酒学的 | Relating to the science of winemaking; of or pertaining to oenology. |  The oenological properties of the grapes were carefully analyzed.  葡萄的酿酒学特性经过了仔细的分析。

The winemaker employed several oenological techniques to enhance the flavor. 酿酒师采用了几种酿酒技术来提升葡萄酒的风味。 | 近义词: vinicultural, viticultural | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From French *œnologique*, from Greek *οἶνος* (oinos, "wine") + *-λογία* (-logia, "-logy").
