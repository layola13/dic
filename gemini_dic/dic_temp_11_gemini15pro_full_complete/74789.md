freight:/freɪt/| n. vt.|货物，运费；运送|Goods carried by a vehicle, ship, aircraft, or train; the charge for transporting something by freight; to transport goods commercially.|The company freighted the goods by rail.  公司通过铁路运输货物。|近义词：cargo, shipment, goods, merchandise, haul, transport|反义词: N/A|词性:noun, verb

词源: From Middle English *fraight, *freight, from Middle French *fraite, *freyte, from Old French fret (“freight, ship's cargo, hire of a ship”), from Frankish *fraht (“earnings, wages, profit”), from Proto-Germanic *fra- (“forward, away”) + *ēhti (“property, possession”). Cognate with Dutch vracht (“freight”), German Fracht (“freight”), Swedish frakt (“freight”).
