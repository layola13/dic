mostahiba:/mʊsˈtæhɪbə/ | adj. |  推荐的，可取的，受欢迎的 |  Recommended, desirable, or pleasing; often used in Islamic contexts to refer to actions that are encouraged but not obligatory. |  Giving charity is a mostahiba act in Islam.  在伊斯兰教中，施舍是一种值得称赞的行为。 |  desirable, commendable, preferable, advisable |  undesirable, objectionable, reprehensible | adjective

Etymology: From Arabic مستحب (mustaḥabb), verbal noun of استحب (istaḥabba) "to consider something good, desirable," from the root ح ب ب (ḥ-b-b) related to love and preference.
