briefest:/ˈbriːfɪst/| adj.|最简短的|superlative adjective of brief; shortest in duration, length, or conciseness. |The chairman's remarks were the briefest on record. 主席的讲话是有史以来最简短的。|The briefest explanation is often the best. 最简短的解释通常是最好的。|近义词：shortest, most concise, succinctest|反义词:longest, most verbose, prolixest|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Middle English *brevest, *briefest, from Old French *bref, from Latin brevis (“short”).|词源:源自中古英语 *brevest、*briefest，来自古法语 *bref，来自拉丁语 brevis（“短的”）。
