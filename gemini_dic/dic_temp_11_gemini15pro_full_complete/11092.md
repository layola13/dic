archaizes:/ˈɑːrkeɪaɪzɪz/
verb
模仿古体; archaize的第三人称单数
To give a deliberately archaic quality to.  To imitate or reproduce archaic styles or features.
The author archaizes his language to create a sense of historical authenticity.  作者模仿古体来营造一种历史的真实感。
Synonyms:  antique,  outdate,  retrogress
Antonyms:  modernize,  update
词性: 动词
