bussed:/bʌst/ | v. |用公共汽车运送；亲吻|To transport by bus; to kiss.|The children were bussed to school. 孩子们被用校车送到学校。|
The tourists were bussed from the airport to the hotel. 游客们被公共汽车从机场送到酒店。|Synonyms: transported, conveyed, kissed |Antonyms: walked, drove (for transport), |verb (past tense of *bus*)

bussed:/bʌst/ | v. |清理餐桌|To clear tables in a restaurant, usually collecting dirty dishes.|He bussed tables to earn extra money during the summer. 他在夏天清理餐桌来赚外快。|She bussed tables at the cafe after school. 她放学后在咖啡馆清理餐桌。|Synonyms: clear, clean |Antonyms: serve, set |verb (past tense of *bus*)


ETYMOLOGY->
From *bus* (n.), shortened from *omnibus*. The meaning "to kiss" is American slang, likely originating from the image of pressing lips together like the doors of a bus.
