bros:/broʊz/
|n.|兄弟们；伙计们|Informal plural of "bro," short for "brother." Used to address a group of male friends or acquaintances.|例句：Hey bros, what are you up to tonight? 嘿，兄弟们，今晚你们打算做什么？

例句：The bros went to the game together.  这帮兄弟们一起去看比赛了。|近义词：guys, dudes, friends, mates|反义词:|n.
