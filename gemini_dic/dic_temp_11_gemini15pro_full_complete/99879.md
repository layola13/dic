*Note:*  "internal's" itself is not a standard English word.  It's formed by adding the possessive suffix "'s" to the adjective "internal." While grammatically possible, it's unusual and often better replaced with a phrase like "the internals of..." or "the internal parts of...". The analysis above breaks down the components of this non-standard formation.
