monoceros: /məˈnɒsərəs/ (UK) /məˈnɑːsərəs/ (US) | n. | 独角兽 | A mythical animal typically represented as a horse with a single straight horn projecting from its forehead. |  Example: The unicorn, or monoceros, is a symbol of purity and grace. 独角兽，或者说monoceros，是纯洁和优雅的象征。

Example:  In medieval bestiaries, the monoceros was often described as a fierce creature. 在中世纪的动物寓言集中，独角兽通常被描述为一种凶猛的生物。

| 近义词: unicorn | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a mythical creature) | 词性: noun
