endangerment:/ɪnˈdeɪndʒərmənt/ | n. | 危害，危及；濒危 |The act of endangering or the state of being endangered.  The act of putting something or someone at risk or in danger. |  Pollution poses a serious endangerment to the local wildlife. 污染对当地野生动物构成严重威胁。 | synonyms: jeopardy, peril, risk, threat | antonyms: safety, security, protection | noun
