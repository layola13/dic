ichthyol: /ɪkˈθiːɒl/ | n. | 鱼石脂，鱼石醇 | A thick, bituminous liquid with a characteristic odor, obtained by the dry distillation of bituminous schists containing fossil fish remains. It contains sulfur and is used as an antiseptic and in some skin ointments. |  She applied ichthyol ointment to the boil. 她把鱼石脂软膏涂在疖子上。 |  The vet prescribed ichthyol for the horse's hoof infection. 兽医开了鱼石脂来治疗马蹄感染。 | synonyms: ammonium bituminosulfonate | antonyms: None | noun
