griddle:/ˈɡrɪdəl/| n.|煎锅，烤盘|A flat metal surface used for cooking, typically heated from below.|She cooked pancakes on the griddle.  她在煎锅上煎薄饼。|近义词：pan, skillet, frying pan|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps oven as it uses enclosed heat)|noun
