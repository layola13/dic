anteriorities:/ˌænˌtɪəriˈɔːrətiz/
n.
优先事项；优先权；先前发生的事情
things that existed or happened before other things, especially things that are considered to be more important
The museum prioritizes the display of antiquities, giving anteriorities to artifacts from the region.  这家博物馆优先展示古物，优先考虑来自该地区的文物。
The legal system needs to consider the anteriorities of existing claims. 法律体系需要考虑现有索赔的优先权。
synonyms: precedents, priorities, antecedence
antonyms: posterities, subsequents
noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *anterioritas*, from Latin anterior "former, prior."
