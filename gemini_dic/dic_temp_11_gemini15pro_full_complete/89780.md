horicon:/ˈhɔːrɪkɑːn/| n.|霍里康湖; 霍里康沼泽|A shallow lake in southeastern Wisconsin, important as a wildlife refuge.  It is a remnant of glacial Lake Oshkosh.|Horicon Marsh is a haven for migratory birds. 霍里康沼泽是候鸟的天堂。|Synonyms: marsh, wetland, swamp|Antonyms: desert, upland|noun
