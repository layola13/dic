centuriate:/sɛnˈtʊəriət/ /sɛnˈtjʊəriət/ | n. | 百人队队员; 百夫长 | A member of a centuria, a military unit of the Roman army originally comprising one hundred men. |  The centuriate was a seasoned veteran, hardened by years of campaigning.  这位百人队队员是一位经验丰富的老兵，经受了多年战役的磨练。| synonym: soldier, legionary | antonym: civilian | noun

centuriate:/sɛnˈtʊəriət/ /sɛnˈtjʊəriət/ | adj. | 百人队的; 由百人队组成的 | Divided into centuries or relating to a centuria. | The centuriate assembly was an important political body in ancient Rome. 百人队会议是古罗马重要的政治机构。 | synonym: century-based | antonym: N/A | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *centuriātus*, past participle of *centuriāre* (“to divide into hundreds”), from *centuria* (“a company of one hundred men”), from *centum* (“hundred”).
