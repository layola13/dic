damnation:/dæmˈneɪʃn/
n.
诅咒，咒骂；毁灭
Condemnation to eternal punishment in hell; the state of being condemned to eternal punishment in hell.
He feared eternal damnation. 他害怕永远的诅咒。
近义词: condemnation, perdition, doom
反义词: salvation, redemption
词性: 名词

damnation:/dæmˈneɪʃn/
n.
毁灭
The act of damning or the state of being damned.
The project was a damnation from the start. 这个项目从一开始就注定要失败。
近义词: ruin, destruction
反义词: success, prosperity
词性: 名词
