cherubical:/ˌtʃɛrʊˈbɪkəl/ /ˌtʃɛrəˈbɪkəl/ | adj. | 天使般的，天真无邪的 | Resembling or suggesting a cherub, especially in innocence or beauty.  Having a sweet, innocent face or expression, like that of a cherub, a type of angel often depicted as a beautiful child. |  The baby had a cherubical face, with rosy cheeks and large, innocent eyes.  婴儿有一张天使般的脸，有着玫瑰色的脸颊和天真无邪的大眼睛。

The painting depicted a cherubical figure playing a harp. 这幅画描绘了一个天使般的形象在弹奏竖琴。 | 近义词：angelic, innocent, sweet, pure | 反义词：devilish, wicked, impish | 词性: 形容词
