overinsured: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈʃʊrd/ | adj. | 过度保险的 | Having insurance coverage exceeding the actual value of the insured item or person. |  The house was so overinsured that it would have been more profitable to burn it down than to sell it. 这房子保险过高，烧掉比卖掉更划算。| Near Synonyms: excessively insured, overprotected | Antonyms: underinsured, uninsured | adjective
