finnan:/ˈfɪnən/ | n. | 芬南熏鱼 | A haddock cured with salt and usually smoked.  Specifically, a haddock smoked over a peat fire. |  We had finnan haddie for breakfast. 我们早餐吃了芬南熏黑线鳕。 | 近义词: smoked haddock, haddock | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fresh haddock") | 词性: noun

词源:
From Scottish Gaelic *fionnach*, from *fionn* (“white, fair”).  The term originally applied to the white, or fair, haddock, as distinguished from the smoked fish.  The term then transferred to the smoked haddock itself.
