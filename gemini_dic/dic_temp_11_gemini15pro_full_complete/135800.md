nonprolifically:/ˌnɒnprəˈlɪfɪkli/ | adv. | 非多产地，不旺盛地 |  In a way that is not prolific; not producing much fruit, offspring, or work. | The artist worked nonprolifically during his later years, producing only a handful of paintings.  这位艺术家晚年创作不多，只画了几幅画。| 近义词: sparsely, infrequently, rarely | 反义词: prolifically, abundantly, copiously | 词性: 副词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + prolific + -ally (adverbial suffix)
