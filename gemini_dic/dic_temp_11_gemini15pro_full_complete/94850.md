ims:/ɪmz/ | abbreviation | 信息管理系统(Information Management Systems) |  IMS refers to Information Management Systems, a type of software application or suite of applications that facilitate the creation, storage, organization, retrieval, and sharing of information within an organization. | Example: The company invested in a new IMS to improve data accessibility. 公司投资了一个新的信息管理系统来提高数据可访问性。 | Synonym: data management system, information system | Antonym: N/A | noun
