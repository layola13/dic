bulkier:/ˈbʌlkiər/ | adj. | 更笨重的，更庞大的 |Comparative form of bulky: more bulky.  Having greater bulk or volume. | The new phone is bulkier than my old one.  这款新手机比我的旧手机更笨重。| The package was bulkier than I expected.  这个包裹比我预期的要大。 | 近义词: larger, heavier, more massive | 反义词: smaller, lighter, less massive | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From bulk + -y + -er.  Bulk ultimately comes from Old Norse *bulki, meaning "cargo, heap."
