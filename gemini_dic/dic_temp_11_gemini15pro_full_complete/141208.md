oopak: /ˈuːpæk/ | n. | 包装，包装材料 |  A type of expanded polystyrene (EPS) foam commonly used for protective packaging. It is lightweight, shock-absorbent, and thermally insulating. | The delicate electronics were shipped in oopak to prevent damage during transit.  这些精密电子产品用oopak包装运输，以防止运输过程中损坏。| synonyms: packing, packaging, wrapping, cushioning, padding | antonyms: unpacking | noun
