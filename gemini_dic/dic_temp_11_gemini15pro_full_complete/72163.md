floored:/flɔːrd/| adj.| 惊呆的，不知所措的；被彻底击败的；铺了地板的 |Overwhelmed, astonished, or defeated; covered with flooring. |例句：I was absolutely floored by the news. 我被这个消息惊呆了。

例句：He was floored by his opponent in the first round. 他在第一轮就被对手彻底击败了。|近义词：astonished, amazed, stunned, overwhelmed, defeated, beaten|反义词：calm, composed, victorious, unimpressed|词性:形容词

floored:/flɔːrd/| v.|  铺地板；使惊呆，使不知所措 | To cover with flooring; to overwhelm, astonish, or defeat. |例句：We've just floored the entire upstairs. 我们刚刚把整个楼上都铺上了地板。

例句：The unexpected news completely floored me.  这突如其来的消息让我完全不知所措。|近义词：cover, install, overwhelm, astonish, defeat|反义词： uncover, expose, compose, encourage|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "floor," meaning "to cover with a floor." Figurative meaning of "overwhelm" or "defeat" arose in the late 19th century. 源自动词“floor”，意思是“铺地板”。“压倒”或“击败”的比喻意义出现在19世纪后期。
