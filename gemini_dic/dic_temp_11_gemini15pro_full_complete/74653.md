free-standing:/ˌfriːˈstændɪŋ/| adj.|独立式的；自立的；单独的|Not attached to anything else and therefore able to support itself; self-supporting.|例句：There's a free-standing lamp in the corner. 角落里有一盏落地灯。|例句：The sculpture is free-standing, not attached to any wall.  这件雕塑是独立式的，没有依附在任何墙上。|近义词：independent, self-supporting, detached|反义词：attached, connected, dependent|词性:形容词

free-standing:/ˌfriːˈstændɪŋ/| n.|独立式建筑；独立式装置|Something that is free-standing, especially a piece of furniture or a building.|例句：The cottage is a free-standing building. 这间小屋是一栋独立式建筑。|例句：They sell free-standings for cookers and other kitchen appliances. 他们出售炉灶和其他厨房用具的独立式装置。|近义词：independent structure, separate unit|反义词：attached structure, connected unit|词性:名词
