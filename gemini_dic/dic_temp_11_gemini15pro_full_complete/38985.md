confirmability:/kənˌfɜːrməˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可证实性，可确认性 | The quality of being able to be confirmed or verified; the ability to be proven or supported by evidence. |  The researchers emphasized the importance of confirmability in their study to ensure the validity of the results. 研究人员强调了研究中可证实性的重要性，以确保结果的有效性。|  verifiability, testability, corroboration | refutability, deniability | noun
