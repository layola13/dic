overidentifies:/ˌoʊvər aɪˈdɛntɪˌfaɪz/
|v.|过度认同，过分认同|To associate oneself too closely with someone or something else, often to the point of losing one's own separate identity.  It also refers to a statistical situation where there are more independent pieces of information than are needed to identify a parameter.| He overidentifies with his favorite sports team, to the point where their losses feel like personal failures. 他过度认同他最喜欢的运动队，以至于他们的失利感觉就像是他个人的失败。|近义词: idealizes, romanticizes, empathizes excessively|反义词: disassociates, distances, differentiates|词性:动词

overidentification:/ˌoʊvər aɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
|n.|过度认同，过分认同|The act or process of overidentifying.|Her overidentification with the fictional character led her to dress and act like her. 她对虚构人物的过度认同导致她穿衣打扮和行为都像她。|近义词: idealization, romanticization, excessive empathy|反义词: disassociation, distancing, differentiation|词性:名词
