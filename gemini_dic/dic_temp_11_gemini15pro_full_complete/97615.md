inharmonic:/ɪnhɑːrˈmɑːnɪk/
adjective
不和谐的，非谐的
lacking harmony; discordant.  Not consisting of simple multiples of a fundamental frequency.
The inharmonic ringing of the bells was unsettling.  钟声的不和谐的响声令人不安。
inharmonic overtones created a strange timbre.  不和谐的泛音创造了一种奇怪的音色。
near synonyms: discordant, dissonant, jarring, clashing
antonyms: harmonic, consonant, melodious, euphonious
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + harmonic.  "In-"  is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not, opposite of, without."  "Harmonic" comes from Late Latin harmonicus,  from Greek harmonikos "musical," from harmonia "harmony, concord of sounds."
