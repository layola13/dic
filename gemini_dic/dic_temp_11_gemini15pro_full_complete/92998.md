idiomuscular:/ˌɪdioʊˈmʌskjʊlər/
adjective
肌内注射的；肌肉本身的
Relating to or involving muscular contractions produced by stimuli applied directly to the muscle tissue, rather than through a nerve.
Stimulation of a denervated muscle can still elicit a contraction, known as an idiomuscular contraction. 对失神经支配的肌肉进行刺激仍然可以引起收缩，称为肌内收缩。
The physician tested for idiomuscular response by tapping the muscle directly. 医生通过直接敲击肌肉来测试肌内反应。

近义词: intramuscular
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "neurogenic" for muscle contractions originating from nerve impulses)
词性: adjective
