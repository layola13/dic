crotchetiness:/ˈkrɒtʃɪtɪnəs/ | n. |古怪; 乖僻; |The state of being crotchety; irritability and eccentricity, especially in old age. |His crotchetiness made him difficult to work with. 他的古怪脾气让他很难共事。|Synonyms: irritability, grumpiness, cantankerousness, irascibility, testiness, peevishness |Antonyms: affability, amiability, geniality |Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From crotchety + -ness.  Crotchety itself likely comes from  crotchet (a musical note), perhaps with the idea of a quick, unpredictable change in mood.
