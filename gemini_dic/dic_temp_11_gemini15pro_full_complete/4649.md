alecithal:/ˌeɪliːˈsaɪθəl/
adjective
无黄的，不含卵黄的
Lacking yolk; said of eggs, especially mammalian eggs.
Mammalian eggs are typically alecithal, containing very little yolk. 哺乳动物的卵通常是无卵黄的，只含有很少的卵黄。
The alecithal nature of the human egg reflects its dependence on the mother for nutrients. 人类卵子的无卵黄性质反映了它对母体营养的依赖。
近义词:oligolecithal (containing very little yolk)
反义词:polylecithal (containing much yolk), macrolecithal (having a large yolk)
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀ- (a-, 'not') + λέκιθος (lékithos, 'yolk') + -al (adjective suffix).
