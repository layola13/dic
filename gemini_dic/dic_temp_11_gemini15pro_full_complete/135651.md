nonpredicatively:/ˌnɒnprɪˈdɪkətɪvli/ | adv. | 非断言地，非谓语地 |  In a way that does not function as a predicate, or in which something is not defined in terms of itself. |  The concept of "set" is defined nonpredicatively in axiomatic set theory to avoid Russell's paradox.  集合的概念在公理化集合论中被非断言地定义，以避免罗素悖论。 |  non-predicatively | predicatively | adverb
