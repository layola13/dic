blandest:/ˈblændɪst/| adj.|最温和的；最平淡的；最乏味的|Lacking strong flavor; mild; tasteless.  Also, lacking in interest or excitement.|The soup was the blandest I've ever tasted. 这汤是我尝过最淡的。|近义词：mildest, dullest, insipidest|反义词：spiciest, most flavorful, most exciting|词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *blaund, from Old French *bland, from Latin blandus (“charming, alluring, flattering, enticing, coaxing, gentle, mild”).
