ETYMOLOGY->
From 'hail' + 'storm'.  'Hail' comes from Old English "hagol," related to Old Norse "hagl" and German "Hagel," all meaning "hail." 'Storm' comes from Old English "storm," meaning "violent weather," related to Old Norse "stormr" and Dutch "storm."
