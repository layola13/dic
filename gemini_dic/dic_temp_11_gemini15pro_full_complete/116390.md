mastodynia:/ˌmæstəˈdɪniə/ /ˌmæsˈtɒdɪnɪə/| n. |乳房痛，乳房胀痛|Pain or tenderness in the breast.|例句：She experienced mastodynia during her menstrual cycle. 她月经周期期间经历了乳房胀痛。

例句：Mastodynia can be a symptom of various conditions, including hormonal changes. 乳房痛可能是多种情况的症状，包括荷尔蒙变化。|近义词：breast pain, mammalgia|反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑 analgesia (无痛))|词性: noun
