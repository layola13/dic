microculture:/ˈmaɪkroʊkʌltʃər/| n. |微观文化;亚文化群|A microculture is a group of people who share similar values, beliefs, and customs within a larger society, often based on shared interests, activities, or experiences.  It's a smaller culture within a larger one.|Example: The online gaming community forms a distinct microculture with its own language and customs.  线上游戏社群形成了独特的微观文化，拥有自己的语言和习俗。

Example:  The company's engineering department has its own microculture, distinct from the marketing or sales teams. 公司的工程部门有其自身的微观文化，与市场营销或销售团队不同。|近义词：subculture, miniculture|反义词: macroculture, mainstream culture|词性: noun
