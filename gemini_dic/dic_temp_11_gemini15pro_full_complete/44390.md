cross-fertilizable:/ˌkrɒs ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzəbl/ (US) /ˌkrɒs ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可异花受精的，可交叉受精的|Capable of being fertilized by the pollen of another plant or variety.|例句：These two varieties of apple are cross-fertilizable. 这两个品种的苹果可以互相授粉。|近义词：interfertile, cross-pollinating|反义词：self-fertilizing, self-pollinating|词性: 形容词
Etymology: cross- + fertilizable.  "Cross-"  indicates interaction between different entities, while "fertilizable" refers to the capacity to be fertilized.  The term specifically relates to the biological process of fertilization involving distinct individuals or varieties.
