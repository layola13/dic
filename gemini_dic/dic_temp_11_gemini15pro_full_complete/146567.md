overgraduated:/ˌoʊvərˈɡrædʒueɪtɪd/
adjective
过度毕业的；学历过高的
Having more education than is necessary or appropriate for a particular job or career.
He felt overgraduated for the entry-level position.  他觉得自己的学历对于这个入门级职位来说太高了。
Many recent graduates are overgraduated and underemployed. 许多应届毕业生学历过高，就业不足。
近义词: overqualified, overeducated
反义词: underqualified, undereducated
词性: adjective
