aureole:/ˈɔːriˌoʊl (US), ˈɔːriəl (UK)| n.|光环，晕轮，神像之光环|A circle of light or color around something, especially a religious figure's head, or any encircling ring of light or glory.|The setting sun cast an aureole around the mountains. 落日给群山披上了一层光环。|近义词：halo, nimbus, corona, glory, radiance|反义词: darkness, shadow|noun
