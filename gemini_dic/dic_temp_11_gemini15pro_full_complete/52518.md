differentiator:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃiˌeɪtər/ | n. |区别者，区分者，微分器|Something that distinguishes something from something else; a distinguishing characteristic or feature. |The main differentiator between the two products is the price. 这两种产品的主要区别在于价格。|Synonyms: distinction, characteristic, feature, mark, peculiarity, quality, trait |Antonyms: similarity, commonality | noun

differentiate:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃiˌeɪt/ | v. |区分，区别，辨别|Recognize or ascertain what makes (someone or something) different.|He is unable to differentiate between right and wrong. 他无法分辨是非。|Synonyms: distinguish, discriminate, separate, discern |Antonyms: confuse, conflate | verb
