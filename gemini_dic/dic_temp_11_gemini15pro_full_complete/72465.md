flunk-out:/ˈflʌŋk aʊt/
verb
退学；因成绩不及格而被学校除名
To fail a course or exam, resulting in dismissal from school or college.
He flunked out of college after his first year. 他第一年后就因成绩不及格被大学开除了。
fail, drop out, be dismissed
graduate, pass, succeed
v.
