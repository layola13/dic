imagers:/ˈɪmɪdʒərz/
n.
成像器；影像生成器；图像传感器
People who create or produce images, especially using technical equipment.  Those who work with imaging technology to produce visual representations.
Modern astronomical imagers can capture stunning details of distant galaxies. 现代天文成像器可以捕捉到遥远星系的惊人细节。
Synonyms:  imaging specialists, image creators, visualizers
Antonyms:  viewers, observers
noun
