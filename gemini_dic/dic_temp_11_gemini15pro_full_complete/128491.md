newspapery:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpəri/ | adj. | 像报纸的；报纸上的；充满报纸内容的 |  Resembling or characteristic of a newspaper, often in a negative or trivial way, suggesting superficiality or sensationalism. | The writing style was rather newspapery and lacked depth. 这篇文章的写作风格很像报纸，缺乏深度。|The story felt very newspapery, full of gossip and speculation.  这个故事感觉很像报纸上的内容，充满了流言蜚语和猜测。 | journalistic, tabloid | literary, in-depth | adjective
