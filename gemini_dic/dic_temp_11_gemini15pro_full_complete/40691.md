converser's:/kənˈvɜːrsərz/
n.|谈话者的，交谈者的| Belonging to or associated with a person who engages in conversation.  It is the possessive form of "converser". |The converser's tone was calm and reassuring.  谈话者的语气平静而令人安心。|近义词:speaker's, discussant's, interlocutor's|反义词:listener's, audience's|词性:noun, possessive


converser:/kənˈvɜːrsər/
n.|交谈者，谈话者|A person who engages in conversation. |He was a skilled converser, able to put anyone at ease. 他是一个熟练的谈话者，能够让任何人都感到轻松自在。|近义词:speaker, discussant, interlocutor|反义词:listener, audience|词性:noun


词源:
From Late Middle English *conversour, from Anglo-Norman conversour, Old French converseor, from Late Latin conversātor, agent noun from conversārī (“to associate with, keep company with, live with, dwell with, converse, cohabit”), from Latin conversus, perfect passive participle of convertō (“I turn around, I turn about, I turn back, I return, I change, I transform, I convert, I alter”), from con- (“together, with”) + vertō (“I turn”).
