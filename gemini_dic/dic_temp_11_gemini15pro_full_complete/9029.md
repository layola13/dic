antimarket:/ˌæntiˈmɑːrkɪt/ | adj. | 反市场的，反对市场的 | Opposed to or hostile towards the free market or market-based principles. |  Many anti-market regulations were put in place during the crisis. 危机期间，许多反市场规章制度得以实施。| The protesters held anti-market sentiments, believing corporations held too much power. 抗议者抱有反市场情绪，认为企业权力过大。 | Synonyms: interventionist, protectionist, anti-capitalist | Antonyms: pro-market, free-market, laissez-faire | adjective
