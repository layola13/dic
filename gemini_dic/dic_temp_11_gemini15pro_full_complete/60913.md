elephantiac:/ˌɛlɪfænˈtaɪək/| adj.|象皮病的；巨大的；浮肿的|Affected with, resembling, or characteristic of elephantiasis, a condition characterized by the gross enlargement of a limb or body part.  Also used figuratively to describe something excessively large or swollen.|The man's elephantiac leg made it difficult for him to walk.  这个男人的象腿使他行走困难。
The bureaucracy had become elephantiac and unwieldy. 这个官僚机构变得臃肿而笨拙。|近义词：swollen, enlarged, massive, huge, immense|反义词：small, tiny, miniature|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *elephantiasis* "disease causing resemblance to an elephant," from *elephas* "elephant."
