nonradiant:/ˌnɒnˈreɪdiənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈreɪdiənt/| adj.|非辐射的，不发光的|Not emitting radiation, especially not emitting light or heat.|The nonradiant side of the moon is extremely cold. 月球不发光的一面极其寒冷。|Synonyms: nonluminescent, dark, dull, unlit|Antonyms: radiant, luminous, bright, shining|adjective

nonradiant:/ˌnɒnˈreɪdiənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈreɪdiənt/| n.|非辐射体|A body that does not emit radiation.|The nonradiant object absorbed all the light.  这个非辐射体吸收了所有的光。|Synonyms: non-emitter|Antonyms: radiator, emitter|noun
