cordal:/ˈkɔːrdl/| adj.|衷心的，诚挚的|Relating to the heart; heartfelt; sincere. Showing warm and sincere friendliness.|例句：She sent a cordal message of support. 她发了一条表示支持的诚挚信息。|近义词：heartfelt, sincere, warm, earnest|反义词：insincere, cold, distant|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *cordālis, from Latin cord-, cor heart.
