afresh:/əˈfreʃ/
adverb
重新；再度
again, from the beginning; anew
例句：She started her life afresh in a new country. 她在一个新的国家重新开始了她的生活。

例句：After the fire, they had to rebuild their business afresh. 火灾之后，他们不得不重新建立他们的生意。

近义词：anew, again, newly
反义词：old, stale
词性：副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From a- + fresh.  The prefix "a-" in this context means "in a particular state or manner," so "afresh" literally means "in a fresh manner."
