assessors:/əˈsesərz/| n.|评估员，估价员，评审员|A person who officially estimates the value of something.  Someone who judges or decides the amount, value, quality, or importance of something.|The assessors determined the value of the property for tax purposes. 评估员确定了该房产的税收价值。|The assessors judged the entries in the art competition. 评审员评判了艺术比赛的参赛作品。|Synonyms: evaluator, appraiser, judge, examiner|Antonyms: N/A|noun
