outsail:/ˌaʊtˈseɪl/
verb
超过…的航行速度；比…航行得更好
to sail faster or better than another ship or sailor
The smaller yacht easily outsail the bigger one in the strong wind.  这艘较小的游艇在大风中轻松地超过了那艘较大的游艇。
synonyms: outstrip, surpass, beat, defeat
antonyms: lag behind, trail
