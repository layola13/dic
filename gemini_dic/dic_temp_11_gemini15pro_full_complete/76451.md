gainsayers:/ˌɡeɪnˈseɪərz/ | n. | 反对者，否认者 | People who deny or contradict a statement or idea. | The project faced opposition from a vocal group of gainsayers.  这个项目遭到了一群直言不讳的反对者的反对。 | Synonyms: opponents, dissenters, contradictors, challengers | Antonyms: supporters, proponents, advocates | noun

gainsay:/ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ/ | v. | 反驳，否认 | To deny or contradict (something). |  She couldn't gainsay the evidence against her. 她无法反驳对她不利的证据。 | Synonyms: deny, dispute, contradict, challenge | Antonyms: confirm, acknowledge, admit, concede | verb
