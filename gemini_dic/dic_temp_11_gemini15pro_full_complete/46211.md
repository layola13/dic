cyrus:/ˈsaɪrəs/ | n. |居鲁士；塞勒斯|A male given name, from Persian, meaning "sun."  It is the name of several Persian kings, most notably Cyrus the Great. | Cyrus the Great founded the Achaemenid Empire. 居鲁士大帝建立了阿契美尼德帝国。| Cyrus was a popular name in the 19th century. 赛勒斯在19世纪是一个很受欢迎的名字。 | 近义词: (As a name, no direct synonyms, but similar historical figures might include Darius, Xerxes) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun
