indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/| v.|放纵；沉溺；满足；纵容|To allow oneself or another person to have or do something enjoyable, especially something that is considered unhealthy or bad.|She indulged in a long hot bath. 她泡了个长时间的热水澡。|Synonyms: pamper, spoil, gratify, treat, accommodate, cater to|Antonyms: deny, deprive, abstain, refrain|verb

indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/| n.|沉溺；嗜好|An act of indulging; something indulged in.|Chocolate is my only indulgence. 巧克力是我唯一的嗜好。|Synonyms: luxury, treat, extravagance, pleasure, gratification|Antonyms: abstinence, deprivation, self-denial|noun
