chicano:/tʃɪˈkɑːnoʊ/ (US), /tʃɪˈkɑːnəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.| 墨西哥裔美国人；奇卡诺文化|A person of Mexican descent living in the United States, especially one who is culturally or politically active.  Often used to assert a distinct cultural identity.|Example: Many Chicano artists use their work to express their experiences with discrimination. 许多奇卡诺艺术家用他们的作品来表达他们遭受歧视的经历。

Example: Chicano literature often explores themes of identity and belonging. 奇卡诺文学经常探讨身份认同和归属感的主题。|近义词：Mexican American, Latino, Hispanic|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered contextually as Anglo-American in certain discussions)|词性: Noun, Adjective
