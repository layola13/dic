belfries: /ˈbɛlfriz/ | n. | 钟楼，钟塔 | A tower, often attached to a church or other building, that houses bells. |  The bells in the belfry rang out across the town. 钟楼里的钟声响彻全城。 | The church belfry offered a panoramic view of the countryside. 教堂的钟楼可以欣赏到乡村的全景。 | synonyms: campanile, steeple, tower | antonyms: crypt, basement | noun
