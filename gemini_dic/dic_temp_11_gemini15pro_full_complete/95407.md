incurable:/ɪnˈkjʊrəbl/ /ɪnˈkjʊərəbəl/ | adj. | 不可治愈的 | Not capable of being cured. |  His cancer was diagnosed as incurable. 他的癌症被诊断为无法治愈。|  interminable, irremediable, hopeless | curable, treatable | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *incurable*, from Old French *incurable*, from Latin *incurabilis*, from *in-* (not) + *curabilis* (curable), from *cura* (care, cure).
