leakiness:/ˈliːkɪnəs/
n.|泄漏；渗漏；不严密|The leakiness of the roof caused damage to the ceiling. 屋顶的渗漏导致天花板受损。|近义词：permeability, porousness|反义词:tightness, impermeability|词性:名词
Etymology: From leaky + -ness.  Leaky traces back to Old English *lēac "leaky," from Proto-Germanic *lekan "to drip, leak."
