anticeremonious:/ˌæntisɛrɪˈmoʊniəs/| adj.| 反礼仪的，不拘礼节的，随便的|Opposed to or lacking ceremony; informal.  Characterized by a deliberate avoidance of ceremony or formality, often to a degree that seems rude or disrespectful.| He gave an anticeremonious wave and left the party. 他随意地挥了挥手，离开了派对。|Synonyms: informal, casual, unceremonious, unpretentious, natural, easygoing, relaxed|Antonyms: formal, ceremonious, ritualistic, conventional, traditional|词性: 形容词
