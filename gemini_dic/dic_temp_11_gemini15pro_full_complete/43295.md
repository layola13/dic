ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crafti*, from craft +‎ -y.  Craft originally meant "strength, skill," from Old English *cræft* (compare Dutch *kracht*, German *Kraft* "strength").  The meaning shifted towards "skill, art" and later acquired connotations of cunning and deceit.
