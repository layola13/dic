fiddlier:/ˈfɪdlər/ | adj. | 更复杂的，更棘手的 | More intricate or difficult to deal with; requiring more skill or dexterity. |  This puzzle is much fiddlier than the last one. 这个谜题比上一个更棘手。|  The new software is fiddlier to use.  这款新软件使用起来更复杂。 | 近义词: trickier, more complex, more complicated, more difficult | 反义词: simpler, easier, straightforward | 词性: 形容词

词源: fiddly (adj.) + -er (comparative suffix).  "Fiddly" itself comes from "fiddle" (v.), referring to the delicate and intricate movements required to play a violin.
