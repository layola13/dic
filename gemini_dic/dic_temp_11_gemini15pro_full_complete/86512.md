heirship:/ˈɛərʃɪp/
n.
继承权；继承资格
The state or condition of being an heir; the right of inheriting.
She claimed heirship to the estate. 她声称拥有这片地产的继承权。

近义词:inheritance, succession
反义词:disinheritance

词性:noun

词源: From Middle English *eirshipe, equivalent to heir +‎ -ship.
