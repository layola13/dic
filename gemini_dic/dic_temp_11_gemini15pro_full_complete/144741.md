over-arousal:/ˈoʊvər əˈraʊzəl/| adj.|过度兴奋的|Characterized by a state of heightened physiological and emotional activity.|The over-aroused puppy barked incessantly. 过度兴奋的小狗不停地吠叫。|Synonyms: overstimulated, hyperaroused|Antonyms: calm, tranquil|adjective
