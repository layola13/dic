anapaest:/ˈænəpiːst/ | n. |抑抑扬格，短短长格|A metrical foot consisting of two short or unstressed syllables followed by one long or stressed syllable. |例句：The word "understand" is an anapaest.  “understand”这个单词是一个抑抑扬格。

例句：The poem was written in anapaestic meter. 这首诗是用抑抑扬格韵律写成的。|近义词：tribrach (in some contexts) |反义词: dactyl |词性:noun
