offlet:/ˈɒflɪt/ | n. |次品，残次品，劣等货，瑕疵品| A product that is substandard or has imperfections, often sold at a reduced price. | The factory sold its offlets at a discount. 这家工厂打折出售它的次品。|  synonyms: reject, substandard, defective, flawed, imperfect, second, irregular | antonyms: perfect, flawless, premium, high-quality | noun

offlet:/ˈɒflɪt/ | n. |副产品，下等产品| A byproduct or inferior product resulting from a manufacturing process. | Leather offlets are used to make glue. 皮革下脚料用来制造胶水。 | synonyms: byproduct, waste product, residue, leftovers | antonyms: main product, primary product | noun
