building:/ˈbɪldɪŋ/
|n.|建筑物；房屋；楼宇|A structure built for permanent use, such as a house, office, or factory.|
例句：The building is very old.  这栋楼很旧。

例句：They are planning to build a new building. 他们计划建造一座新楼。


|近义词：structure, edifice, construction|
|反义词：ruin, wreckage|
|词性：名词|


词源: From Middle English *bilding, from Old English *bylding, from Proto-Germanic *bildingą, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰel- (“to swell, bloom”). Cognate with German Bildung (“formation, education”).  The original sense was "act of building," later "something built."
