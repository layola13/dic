overfertilize: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrtəlaɪz/
verb
过度施肥
To apply too much fertilizer to (soil or plants).
The farmer overfertilized his crops, resulting in stunted growth.  农民过度施肥，导致作物生长发育不良。
He overfertilized his lawn, and now it's burnt. 他给草坪施肥过量，现在草坪都烧焦了。
近义词: overfeed
反义词: underfertilize
词性: verb


overfertilize: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrtəlaɪz/
noun
过度施肥(的状态或行为)
The act of applying too much fertilizer.
Overfertilization can lead to environmental problems. 过度施肥会导致环境问题。
The overfertilization of the field caused runoff into the nearby stream. 田地过度施肥导致肥料流入附近的溪流。
近义词: overfeeding
反义词: underfertilization
词性: noun
