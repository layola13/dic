gribble:/ˈɡrɪbəl/| n. |蛀木水虱; 船蛆|A small, marine crustacean that bores into wood, causing damage to submerged structures.|例句：The gribble is a tiny crustacean that can cause significant damage to wooden boats and piers. 蛀木水虱是一种微小的甲壳类动物，会对木船和码头造成重大损害。

例句：Gribble infestations can weaken wooden structures over time.  蛀木水虱的侵扰会随着时间的推移削弱木结构。|近义词：woodworm, shipworm|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *griblen, frequentative of *griben (“to dig, grub”), from Old Norse gryfja (“to dig, burrow”). Cognate with Norwegian dialectal gribla (“to gnaw, nibble”).
