boycott:/ˈbɔɪkɒt/ (UK) /ˈbɔɪkɑːt/ (US)| n. vt.|抵制，联合抵制|An act of refusing to buy, use, or deal with a product, service, or company as a form of protest.|The consumers boycotted the company's products due to their unethical labor practices. 由于该公司不道德的劳动行为，消费者抵制其产品。|近义词：ban, embargo, blacklist, ostracize|反义词:patronize, support|名词, 动词
