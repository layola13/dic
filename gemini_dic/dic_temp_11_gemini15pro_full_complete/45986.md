cyanosis:/ˌsaɪəˈnoʊsɪs/| n. |发绀，紫绀|A bluish discoloration of the skin and mucous membranes resulting from inadequate oxygenation of the blood.|The patient presented with cyanosis of the lips and fingertips. 病人的嘴唇和指尖出现发绀。|Near synonyms: blueness, lividity|Antonyms:  healthy complexion, normal coloration|noun
