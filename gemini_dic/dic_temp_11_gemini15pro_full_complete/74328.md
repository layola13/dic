fragilely:/ˈfrædʒɪlli/ | adv. | 脆弱地；易碎地 | In a fragile or delicate manner; easily broken or damaged. | The old woman held the antique vase fragilely, afraid it would shatter. 老妇人小心翼翼地拿着古董花瓶，生怕它会碎。| 近义词：delicately, gingerly, precariously | 反义词：strongly, robustly, firmly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "fragile" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Fragile" comes from the Latin "fragilis," meaning "breakable," from "frangere," meaning "to break."
