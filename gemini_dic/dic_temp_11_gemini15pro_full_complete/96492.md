indivision:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪʒən/
n.
不可分割, 整体, 团结
the state of being undivided; unity
The painting emphasizes the indivision of the family. 这幅画强调了家庭的团结。
近义词：unity, wholeness, integrity
反义词：division, separation, fragmentation
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French indivision, from medieval Latin indivisio(n-), from in- ‘not’ + divisio ‘division’.
