mastodonsaurus:/ˌmæstəˌdɒnəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |乳齿螈|An extinct genus of temnospondyl amphibian from the Middle Triassic.  It was a large, aquatic predator with a massive, flat head and sharp teeth.|The Mastodonsaurus was a fearsome predator of the Triassic period. 乳齿螈是三叠纪时期可怕的掠食者。|Near synonyms: temnospondyl, amphibian|Antonyms: N/A|noun
