cloggily:/ˈklɑːɡɪli/ | adv. | 笨拙地，缓慢地 | In a clogged or obstructed manner; slowly and heavily, as if impeded. | The old man walked cloggily through the mud. 老人笨拙地走过泥泞。|  The traffic moved cloggily through the city center.  交通在市中心缓慢移动。 | 近义词：heavily, laboriously, ponderously, sluggishly | 反义词：quickly, easily, smoothly, swiftly | 词性: 副词

词源: From clog + -ily.  Clog itself comes from Middle English *cloggen, of uncertain origin.
