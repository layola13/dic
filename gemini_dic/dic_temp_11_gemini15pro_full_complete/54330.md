dishearteningly:/dɪsˈhɑːrtnɪŋli/
adverb
令人沮丧地
in a way that causes someone to lose hope or enthusiasm
The team played dishearteningly in the final, losing by a large margin.  这支队伍在决赛中表现令人沮丧，惨败。
The news was dishearteningly familiar. 这消息熟悉得令人沮丧。
Synonyms: depressingly, disappointingly, discouragingly
Antonyms: encouragingly, hearteningly, inspiringly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From disheartening + -ly.  Disheartening comes from dis- (expressing negation) + hearten (to give courage or confidence to).  The verb hearten comes from heart, referring to the emotional center.
