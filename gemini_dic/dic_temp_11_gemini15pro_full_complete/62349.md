endermic:/ɛnˈdɜːrmɪk/ | adj. |  经皮的；皮内的 |  Relating to or denoting the application of a medicine through the skin, typically by rubbing. |  Endermic administration of drugs was once common but is now rarely used. 经皮给药曾经很普遍，但现在很少使用。 | Synonyms: transdermal, percutaneous | Antonyms: oral, intravenous | adjective


endermic:/ɛnˈdɜːrmɪk/ | n. | 经皮给药（法）|  The application of a medicine through the skin. | The doctor prescribed an endermic ointment for the rash. 医生开了皮内用药膏治疗皮疹。 | Synonyms: transdermal application, percutaneous administration | Antonyms: oral administration, intravenous injection | noun
