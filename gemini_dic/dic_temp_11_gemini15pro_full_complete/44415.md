cross-my-heart:/ˌkrɒs maɪ ˈhɑːt/ /ˌkrɑːs maɪ ˈhɑːrt/| idiom |我保证，我发誓，千真万确 |A gesture made by drawing an X across one's chest, typically used to emphasize the truthfulness of a statement.|Example: I cross my heart, I didn't break the vase. 我发誓，我没有打破花瓶。|近义词：I swear, I promise, truly, honestly |反义词:I lie, I deceive |词性:idiom

词源: This idiom likely originates from the Christian practice of making the sign of the cross, which symbolizes faith and truthfulness.  Over time, a simplified version of this gesture, drawing an X across the chest, became associated with making a solemn promise or emphasizing the truth of a statement.
