entomolite:/ˈɛntəmɒˌlaɪt/| n. |昆虫化石，节肢动物化石|A fossil of an arthropod, especially an insect.|Example 1: The entomolite revealed a new species of ancient beetle.  昆虫化石揭示了一种新的古代甲虫。

Example 2: The museum's collection boasts a wide array of entomolite specimens.  博物馆的收藏品中拥有大量的昆虫化石标本。|近义词：fossil, arthropod fossil|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "living insect/arthropod")|词性:noun
