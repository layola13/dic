ETYMOLOGY->
From French, literally "camp assistant."  "Aide" comes from the Old French "aide" (help, assistance), from Latin "adjutare" (to help). "De" is a French preposition meaning "of" or "from." "Camp" comes from Latin "campus" (field, plain).
