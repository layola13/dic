bidarka:/baɪˈdɑːrkə/
noun
单人皮划艇，一种爱斯基摩人的小船
A small, skin-covered kayak used by Eskimos, typically for one person.
例句：The hunter skillfully maneuvered his bidarka through the icy waters. 猎人熟练地驾驶着他的单人皮划艇穿过冰冷的水域。

例句：Bidarkas were traditionally made from seal skins stretched over a wooden frame.  传统上，单人皮划艇是用海豹皮绷在木架上制成的。

近义词：kayak, canoe
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "ship" as a larger contrasting vessel)
词性:n.
