anti-oxygenating:/ˌæntiˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 抗氧化剂 | A substance that prevents or slows down oxidation. | The company is developing a new anti-oxygenating for use in food preservation. 该公司正在开发一种新的抗氧化剂，用于食品保鲜。 | antioxidant, anti-oxidative | oxidant, pro-oxidant | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): from Greek *anti- meaning "against," "opposite of"
oxygen (noun): from French oxygène, coined by Antoine Lavoisier from Greek oxys "sharp, acid" + -genes "producing"
-ate (suffix): forms verbs meaning "to act on or treat with"
-ing (suffix): forms present participles and adjectives
