kerargyrite:/kəˈrɑːrdʒɪˌraɪt/
Noun
角银矿
A mineral, silver chloride (AgCl), that is an important ore of silver.
Kerargyrite is a common secondary mineral found in the oxidized zone of silver deposits. 角银矿是一种常见的次生矿物，发现于银矿床的氧化带中。
Crystals of kerargyrite are typically cubic or octahedral. 角银矿晶体通常为立方体或八面体。
Synonym: chlorargyrite, horn silver
Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific mineral.  Could consider antonyms related to its properties, like "native silver" which is not a compound)
