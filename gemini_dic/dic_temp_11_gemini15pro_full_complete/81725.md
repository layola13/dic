greenockite:/ˈɡriːnəˌkaɪt/ | n. | 绿镉矿 | A rare yellow mineral consisting of cadmium sulfide. It is the principal ore of cadmium. | Greenockite is a secondary mineral formed by weathering of other cadmium-bearing minerals.  绿镉矿是一种由其他含镉矿物风化形成的次生矿物。

Greenockite coatings can sometimes be found on sphalerite. 绿镉矿的涂层有时可以在闪锌矿上找到。 | 近义词: cadmium sulfide (化学成分) | 反义词:  None (没有确切的反义词，因为它是一种具体的矿物) | 词性: noun
