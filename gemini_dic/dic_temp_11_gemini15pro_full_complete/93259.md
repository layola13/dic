ilang-ilang:/ˈiːlæŋˈiːlæŋ/ | n. | 伊兰伊兰, 香水树 |  A tropical tree (Cananga odorata) with fragrant greenish-yellow flowers, native to Southeast Asia, from which an essential oil is extracted. |  The perfume she wore had a distinct ilang-ilang scent. 她用的香水有一种明显的伊兰伊兰香味。|  Synonym: cananga, ylang-ylang | Antonym: N/A | Noun

ilang-ilang:/ˈiːlæŋˈiːlæŋ/ | adj. | 伊兰伊兰的, 香水树的 | Made from or scented with the flowers of the ilang-ilang tree. | She bought an ilang-ilang scented candle. 她买了一支伊兰伊兰香味的蜡烛。 | Synonym: cananga, ylang-ylang | Antonym: N/A | Adjective
