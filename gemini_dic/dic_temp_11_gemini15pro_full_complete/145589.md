overconcerned: /ˌoʊvər.kənˈsɜrnd/ | adj. | 过分担忧的，过分关心的 | Excessively worried or anxious about something or someone. | She was overconcerned about her son's health. 她过分担心儿子的健康。|  He's overconcerned with what people think of him. 他过分在意别人对他的看法。 | 近义词: anxious, apprehensive, worried, solicitous | 反义词: unconcerned, indifferent, apathetic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "concerned" (adjective meaning "worried, anxious").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "concerned," indicating an excessive or unwarranted level of worry.
