bubale:/ˈbuːbɑːleɪ/ /ˈbjuːbɑːleɪ/
n.
狷羚，布巴尔狷羚
A large African antelope with twisted horns.
The bubale is a type of antelope found in grassy savannas.  这种狷羚是在长满草的大草原上发现的一种羚羊。
Bubales are often preyed upon by lions.  狷羚经常被狮子捕食。
近义词:hartebeest, antelope
反义词:predator
词性:noun
