beg-the-question:/ˌbeɡ ðə ˈkwɛstʃən/| v.|以待决之题作为论据；预先假设；回避问题|To assume the truth of the conclusion of an argument in one of the premises; to assume as true something that needs to be proved.|Circular reasoning often begs the question. 循环论证常常以待决之题作为论据。

He begged the question when he said the drug was safe because it was FDA approved. 他说这种药是安全的，因为它获得了FDA的批准，这就预先假设了结论。|近义词：assume, presuppose, take for granted|反义词：prove, demonstrate|词性:动词
