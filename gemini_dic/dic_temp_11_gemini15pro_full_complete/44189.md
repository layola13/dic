croakiest:/ˈkroʊkiɪst/| adj.|最嘶哑的|Superlative form of croaky: most croaky.  Describes a voice that is extremely hoarse, rough, and low, often due to illness or overuse.|例句：The singer had the croakiest voice I've ever heard.  这位歌手的声音是我听过最嘶哑的。

例句：After yelling at the game all day, my voice was croakiest than ever. 在比赛中喊了一整天后，我的声音比以往任何时候都更嘶哑。|近义词：hoarsest, raspiest, gruffest|反义词：clearest, smoothest, mellowest|词性:形容词
