nontransparence:/ˌnɒntrænˈspærəns/ | n. |不透明性，不透明度|Lack of transparency; the quality of being nontransparent.  The state or condition of not being transparent, either literally or figuratively. This can refer to physical objects that do not allow light to pass through or to processes, decisions, or information that are not open and accessible.|The nontransparence of the frosted glass provided privacy. 磨砂玻璃的不透明性提供了隐私。|The nontransparence of the government's decision-making process led to public distrust. 政府决策过程的不透明导致了公众的不信任。| opacity, opaqueness | transparency, clarity | noun
