knightlike:/ˈnaɪtˌlaɪk/
adjective
骑士般的；侠义的；具有骑士精神的
Resembling or characteristic of a knight, especially in bravery, courtesy, or chivalry.
He displayed knightlike courage in the face of danger. 他在危险面前表现出骑士般的勇气。

knightlike:/ˈnaɪtˌlaɪk/
adjective
骑士般的；侠义的；具有骑士精神的
Having the qualities of a knight, such as honor, courtesy, and bravery.
His knightlike behavior impressed everyone at the tournament. 他骑士般的行为给锦标赛上的每个人都留下了深刻的印象。


近义词:chivalrous, gallant, courteous, honorable
反义词:cowardly, dishonorable, rude, ungallant
词性:adjective


词源：
From Middle English *knightlich, equivalent to knight +‎ -like.
