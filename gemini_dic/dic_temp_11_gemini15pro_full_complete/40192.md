conterminal:/ˌkoʊˈtɜːrmɪnl/| adj.|共终的，同终边的|Having the same terminal side or endpoint, especially of angles in trigonometry.  Angles that share the same initial side and terminal side.|Example 1: The angles 30° and 390° are coterminal. 30度角和390度角是共终角。

Example 2:  -120° and 240° are coterminal angles. -120度角和240度角是共终角。|近义词:equivalent (in certain contexts)|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
