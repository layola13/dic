cherub:/ˈtʃɛrəb/ (US), /ˈtʃɛrʊb/ (UK)| n.|小天使，胖娃娃|A beautiful or innocent-looking child, often depicted as a winged angel. An angel of the second order.|例句：The painting depicted a cherub playing a harp. 这幅画描绘了一个小天使在弹奏竖琴。

例句：Her nephew is a little cherub with rosy cheeks. 她的侄子是一个有着红润脸颊的小天使。|近义词：angel, seraph|反义词:demon, devil|Noun


cherubim:/ˈtʃɛrəbɪm/ (US), /ˈtʃɛrʊbɪm/ (UK)| n.|小天使（复数）|The plural form of cherub.  Angels of the second order.|例句：Cherubim are often depicted guarding sacred places. 小天使经常被描绘成守护圣地。

例句：The artist painted a choir of cherubim singing praises.  艺术家画了一群唱赞美诗的小天使。|近义词：angels, seraphs|反义词:demons, devils|Noun
