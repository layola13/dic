commodity:/kəˈmɒdəti/ (UK) /kəˈmɑːdəti/ (US)| n. |商品，货物，日用品|A raw material or primary agricultural product that can be bought and sold, such as copper or coffee.|例句：Oil is a valuable commodity. 石油是一种珍贵的商品。|近义词：product, merchandise, goods, article|反义词: service, liability|noun

commodity:/kəˈmɒdəti/ (UK) /kəˈmɑːdəti/ (US)| n. |有用之物，有价值的品质|A useful or valuable thing, quality, or person.|例句：Time is a precious commodity. 时间是一种宝贵的商品。|近义词：asset, advantage|反义词: disadvantage, drawback|noun
