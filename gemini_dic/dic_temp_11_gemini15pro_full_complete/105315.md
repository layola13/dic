kibitzing:/ˈkɪbɪtsɪŋ/| n.|闲聊，指手画脚|The kibitzing from the sidelines was distracting the players.  场外观众的闲聊让选手们分心。|Kibitzing is generally discouraged in serious chess matches. 在正式的象棋比赛中，通常不鼓励闲聊。|近义词：chatting, gossiping, meddling, interfering|反义词: silence, ignoring|noun

kibitz:/ˈkɪbɪts/| v.|闲聊，指手画脚|He was kibitzing with his friends about the game. 他和朋友们闲聊着比赛。|Don't kibitz while I'm trying to concentrate. 我正努力集中注意力，别来打扰我。|近义词：chat, gossip, meddle, interfere|反义词: ignore|verb
