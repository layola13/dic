deviate:/ˈdiːvieɪt/ (US), /ˈdiːviˌeɪt/ (UK)| v. | 偏离，背离 | To depart from an established course, standard, or principle. | The hikers deviated from the trail to explore a cave.  徒步旅行者偏离了小径去探索一个洞穴。

The rocket deviated from its course and exploded. 火箭偏离了轨道并爆炸了。| 近义词：stray, diverge, digress, veer | 反义词：conform, adhere, comply | 词性：动词

deviate:/ˈdiːvieɪt/ (US), /ˈdiːviˌeɪt/ (UK)| n. | 偏离者，背离者| Someone or something that departs from a norm.  (This usage is less common.) |  He was considered a deviate from the community's strict moral code. 他被认为是社区严格道德规范的偏离者。| 近义词：nonconformist, maverick | 反义词：conformist | 词性：名词
