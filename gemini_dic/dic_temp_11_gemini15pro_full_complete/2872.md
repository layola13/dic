adversifolious: /ˌædvɜːrsiˈfəʊliəs/
adjective
背叶的；对生叶的
Having leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs, but with each pair at right angles to the pairs above and below it.
The adversifolious arrangement of the leaves minimizes self-shading and maximizes light capture.  这种背叶的叶子排列方式最大限度地减少了自遮蔽，并最大限度地增加了光线捕获。
The botanist identified the plant as adversifolious due to its unique leaf arrangement.  植物学家根据其独特的叶片排列方式将该植物鉴定为背叶植物。
近义词: decussate, opposite
反义词: alternate, spiral, whorled
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *adversus* ("opposite, turned towards") + *folium* ("leaf").
