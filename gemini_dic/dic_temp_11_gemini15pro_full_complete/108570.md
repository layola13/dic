what:/wɒt/(UK) /wɑːt/(US) |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information specifying something.|What color is your car? 你的车是什么颜色的？|近义词: which |反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym) | pronoun, determiner, adverb


USAGE->

USAGE->
Simple Present:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done with the money? = 他用这笔钱做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她时她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? = 明天的会议上他们会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
