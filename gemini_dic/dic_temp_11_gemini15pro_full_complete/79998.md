go kaput:/ɡoʊ kəˈpʊt/| v. | 损坏，报废，垮掉 | To break down or cease to function; to fail completely. |例句：My old car finally went kaput. 我的旧车终于报废了。

例句：The business went kaput after the recession. 经济衰退后，这家公司倒闭了。|近义词：break down, fail, die, conk out, give out, collapse |反义词: work, function, succeed, thrive |词性:动词

go kaput:/ɡoʊ kəˈpʊt/| adj.(informal) | 坏了的，报废的 | Broken, ruined, or useless. |例句：The engine is kaput. 引擎坏了。

例句：The project is kaput. 这个项目失败了。|近义词：broken, ruined, useless, defunct |反义词: working, functional, useful |词性:形容词
