overmodernizing: /ˌoʊvərˈmɒdərnɪˌzaɪzɪŋ/ /ˌoʊvərˈmɑːdərˌnaɪzɪŋ/
Verb
过度现代化
The act of modernizing something to an excessive or unnecessary degree.
Many critics argue that the city's historic district is being overmodernized, losing its original charm. 许多评论家认为，这座城市的历史街区正在被过度现代化，失去了原有的魅力。
overmodernize (verb)
overmodernization (noun)
overmodernized (adjective)
