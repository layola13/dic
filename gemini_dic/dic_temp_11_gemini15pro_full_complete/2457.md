adjuring:/əˈdʒʊərɪŋ/
verb
恳求；告诫；命令
Form of the verb adjure: present participle
He adjured them to tell the truth. 他恳求他们说出真相。
近义词： implore, beseech, entreat, urge, exhort, command
反义词： allow, permit
词性：v.
