decorousness:/ˈdekərəsnəs/ /dɪˈkɔːrəsnəs/
noun
端庄；合宜；礼貌
The quality of being decorous; propriety.  Characterized by dignified propriety in conduct, manners, appearance, or character.
Her decorousness and polite demeanor made her a favorite among the staff.  她的端庄和礼貌的举止使她成为员工中最受欢迎的人。
Near Synonyms: propriety, decency, seemliness, modesty, dignity, respectability
Near Antonyms: impropriety, indecency, indecorum, immodesty, vulgarity
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From decorous + -ness.  Decorous from French décorer (to decorate) + -ous. Ultimately from Latin decorus (fitting, proper).
