denormalizations:/diːˌnɔːrmələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 反规范化，非规范化 | The process of increasing redundancy in a database by adding redundant data or grouping data to improve read performance, at the expense of write performance and data integrity.  | Example: Denormalizations can improve query speed, but they can also make updates more complex. 反规范化可以提高查询速度，但也可能使更新更加复杂。 | Example: The team decided to implement denormalizations to address performance issues. 团队决定实施反规范化来解决性能问题。 | Synonyms: redundancy, data duplication | Antonyms: normalization, data integrity | Noun
