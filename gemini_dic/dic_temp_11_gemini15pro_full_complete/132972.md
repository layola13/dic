nonenactments:/ˌnɒnɪˈnæktmənts/
n.
未制定；未颁布；未实施
The lack of clear guidelines resulted in numerous nonenactments of proposed policies.  缺乏明确的指导方针导致许多拟议的政策未得到实施。
The museum showcased historical nonenactments, highlighting laws that were never passed.  博物馆展示了历史上的未颁布法案，重点介绍了从未通过的法律。
Synonyms: omissions, failures, neglect, inaction
Antonyms: enactments, implementations, adoptions
