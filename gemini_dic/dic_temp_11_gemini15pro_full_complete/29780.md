cattleless: /ˈkæt(ə)llɪs/
adjective
无牛的，没有牲畜的
Without cattle; lacking cattle.
The cattleless farmer had to find a new way to make a living.  那个没有牲畜的农民不得不寻找新的谋生方式。
The once prosperous ranch now stood cattleless and deserted.  曾经繁荣的牧场现在空无一牛，一片荒凉。
近义词: livestockless
反义词: cattle-rich, cattle-owning
词性: adjective
