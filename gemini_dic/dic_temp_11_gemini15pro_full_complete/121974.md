missive:/ˈmɪsɪv/| n.|信，公文，官方函件|A written message, especially a formal or official letter.|She received a missive from the president of the company. 她收到了公司总裁的来信。|Example: The ambassador delivered the missive to the foreign minister. 大使将公文递交给了外交部长。|Synonyms: letter, communication, message, dispatch, note|Antonyms: conversation, speech|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *missif* (recorded only from the 15th c.) or directly from Medieval Latin *missīvus*, from Latin *missus*, past participle of *mittō* ("I send, dispatch, let go").
