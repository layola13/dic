disciplinant:/ˈdɪsɪplɪnənt/| n.|判别式|In mathematics, the discriminant of a polynomial is a quantity that depends on the coefficients and allows deducing some properties of the roots without computing them. |The discriminant of the quadratic equation is b²-4ac. 二次方程的判别式是b²-4ac。|Synonym: determinant |Antonym: N/A| Noun
