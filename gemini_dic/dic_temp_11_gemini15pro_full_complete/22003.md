boilerplate:/ˈbɔɪlərpleɪt/ (US) /ˈbɔɪləpleɪt/ (UK)| n. adj.|样板文件，公式化文件；（引申）陈词滥调，老生常谈| Standardized or formulaic text, often used as a starting point for documents or websites.  It can also refer to clichés or predictable statements. |The lawyer quickly skimmed the boilerplate sections of the contract. 律师快速浏览了合同中的样板条款。|Synonyms: template, formula, cliché, stereotype, stock phrase |Antonyms: original, innovative, unique| noun

boilerplate:/ˈbɔɪlərpleɪt/ (US) /ˈbɔɪləpleɪt/ (UK)| adj.|样板的，公式化的|Standardized or formulaic. |The politician delivered a boilerplate speech about the economy.  这位政治家发表了一篇关于经济的公式化演讲。|Synonyms: formulaic, standardized, clichéd, stock |Antonyms: original, innovative, unique| adjective
