interassure:/ˌɪntərəˈʃʊr/ | v. |互相保证；互相担保|To give mutual assurance or guarantee.|The two parties interassured each other that the agreement would be honored.|近义词:reassure, guarantee, promise|反义词:betray, break a promise|词性:动词

词源:  "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + "assure" (from Old French "assurer" meaning "to make certain, guarantee").
