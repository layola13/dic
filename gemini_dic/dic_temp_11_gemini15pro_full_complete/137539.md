nontranscribing: /ˌnɑːnˈtrænˈskraɪbɪŋ/ | adj. | 非转录的；非转录基因的 | Not transcribing; relating to a gene that is not transcribed. |  Nontranscribing DNA segments make up a large portion of the human genome.  非转录DNA片段构成了人类基因组的很大一部分。 | synonyms: silent, inactive, dormant | antonyms: transcribing, active, expressed | adjective

Etymology:
From non- + transcribing, the present participle of transcribe, from Latin transcribere "to copy," from trans- "across" + scribere "to write."
