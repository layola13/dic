moralising:/ˈmɒrəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
说教的，道德化的
The act of expressing judgments about what is right and wrong, especially in a way that is intended to influence other people's behaviour.
He gave a moralising lecture on the dangers of drinking. 他做了一场关于酗酒危害的说教。

近义词:preachy, didactic, sententious, sanctimonious
反义词: amoral, non-judgmental
词性: adjective


moralising:/ˈmɒrəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪŋ/
noun
说教
The act or practice of moralizing.
His constant moralising was beginning to annoy his friends. 他不断的 moralising 开始惹恼他的朋友了。
近义词:preaching, sermonizing
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
