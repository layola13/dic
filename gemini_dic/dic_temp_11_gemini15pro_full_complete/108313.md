lauric:/ˈlɔːrɪk/ | adj. |月桂酸的；十二烷酸的|  Relating to or derived from lauric acid, a saturated fatty acid found in many vegetable fats and oils.| Lauric acid is a common ingredient in soaps and shampoos. 月桂酸是肥皂和洗发水中常见的成分。| 近义词:dodecanoic (technical name)| 反义词: None| 词性: 形容词
