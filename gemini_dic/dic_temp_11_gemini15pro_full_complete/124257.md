mostra:/ˈmɔːstrə/ | n. | 展览; 陈列; 显示 | An exhibition or display, especially of works of art or other items of interest. |  Example 1: The museum is hosting a mostra of Renaissance paintings.  博物馆正在举办文艺复兴时期绘画展。

Example 2:  Did you see the mostra of local crafts at the town hall? 你看过市政厅举办的当地工艺品展吗？ | Synonyms: exhibition, show, display, exposition | Antonyms: concealment, hiding | noun
