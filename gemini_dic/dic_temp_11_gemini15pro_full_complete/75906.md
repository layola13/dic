fundraising:/ˈfʌndˌreɪzɪŋ/
noun
筹款；募捐；资金筹集
The act of collecting or producing money for a particular purpose, especially for a charity.
The school's fundraising efforts were very successful. 学校的筹款活动非常成功。
They held a fundraising gala for the new hospital. 他们为新医院举办了一场筹款晚会。
synonyms: collection, solicitation,募捐,筹资
antonyms: spending, disbursement,支出,分配
n.
