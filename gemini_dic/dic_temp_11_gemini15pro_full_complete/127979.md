neotropical:/ˌniːəˈtrɒpɪkəl/
adjective
新热带区的
Relating to or denoting the biogeographic region comprising Central and South America, the Mexican lowlands, the Caribbean islands, and southern Florida.
The neotropical rainforest is home to a vast array of species.  新热带雨林是大量物种的家园。
The Amazon is a neotropical river. 亚马逊河是一条新热带河流。
Synonym:South American, Central American, Caribbean
Antonym:Nearctic, Palearctic
词性: adjective
