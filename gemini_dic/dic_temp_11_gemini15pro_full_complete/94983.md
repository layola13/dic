in-the-event-of:/ɪn ði ɪˈvɛnt əv/| prepositional phrase|如果；万一；倘若|In case something happens; should something occur.  This phrase introduces a contingency plan or action to be taken under specific circumstances.|例句：In the event of fire, please use the stairs.  万一发生火灾，请走楼梯。|近义词：in case of, if, should, in the event that|反义词: (No direct antonym, but can be contrasted with "regardless of")|词性: prepositional phrase
