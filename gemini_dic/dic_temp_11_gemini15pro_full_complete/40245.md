continence:/ˈkɒntɪnəns/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntɪnəns/ (US)| n.|节制；自制力；克制力；禁欲|The state of exercising self-restraint, especially with regard to desires or impulses;  the ability to control movements of the bowels and bladder.|He struggled to maintain his continence in the face of temptation. 他在诱惑面前努力保持克制。|Synonyms: self-control, restraint, self-discipline, abstinence, moderation|Antonyms: incontinence, intemperance, immoderation|noun

continence:/ˈkɒntɪnəns/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntɪnəns/ (US)| n.|（大小便）自制，节制|The ability to control movements of the bowels and bladder.|The doctor inquired about her bowel continence after the surgery. 医生询问了她术后排便的控制情况。|Synonyms: bladder control, bowel control|Antonyms: incontinence|noun
