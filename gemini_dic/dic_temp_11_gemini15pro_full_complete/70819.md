finical:/fɪˈnɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 过分讲究的，挑剔的，吹毛求疵的 | Excessively particular or fastidious, especially about details of dress, food, etc.; difficult to please. | She's so finical about her food that she drives everyone crazy. 她对食物太挑剔了，把大家都逼疯了。 | He was finical about his appearance, always adjusting his tie and smoothing his hair.  他非常在意自己的外表，总是整理领带和抚平头发。 | 近义词: fussy, particular, fastidious, picky, choosy | 反义词: easygoing, undemanding, tolerant | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From fine (adj.) + -ical, influenced by fine (n.) "a sum of money exacted as a penalty."  The original sense was "subject to fines," hence "punctilious, precise," which evolved to "overly particular."
