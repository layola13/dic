mlang:/ˈɛmˌlæŋ/ | n. | 机器语言 | Machine Language, a low-level programming language consisting of binary or hexadecimal instructions that can be directly executed by a computer.  | Example: The programmer wrote the program in mlang for optimal performance. 程序员用机器语言编写程序以获得最佳性能。 | Example:  Mlang is difficult for humans to read and write directly. 机器语言对人类来说很难直接读写。 | Synonym: machine code, assembly language (loosely) | Antonym: high-level language | noun
