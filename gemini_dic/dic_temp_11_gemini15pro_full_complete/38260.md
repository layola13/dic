comradeship: /ˈkɒmreɪdʃɪp/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmrɑːdʃɪp/ (US)| n.|同志情谊；友情；伙伴关系|The feeling of friendship and loyalty that exists between comrades. |The soldiers developed a strong sense of comradeship during the war.  战争期间，士兵们建立了深厚的同志情谊。|近义词：friendship, fellowship, camaraderie, companionship|反义词：enmity, hostility|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From comrade +‎ -ship.  Comrade comes from the Spanish word "camarada," meaning roommate or cabin mate, ultimately derived from the Latin "camera" meaning chamber or room.
