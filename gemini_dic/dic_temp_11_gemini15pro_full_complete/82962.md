gunged:/ɡʌndʒd/
adjective
糊满污垢的；塞满黏糊糊东西的
covered or filled with thick, sticky, unpleasant matter.
The sink was gunged up with hair and soap. 水槽里塞满了头发和肥皂，糊成一团。
近义词: clogged, blocked, filthy, dirty
反义词: clean, clear

词性: adjective
