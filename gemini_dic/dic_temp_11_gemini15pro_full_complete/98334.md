insinuate:/ɪnˈsɪnjuˌeɪt/ /ɪnˈsɪnjueɪt/| v. |暗示；巧妙地引入；使潜入；使迂回地潜入|To suggest or hint something indirectly, especially something unpleasant or disapproving.|She insinuated that he was lying, but she didn't accuse him directly. 她暗示他在撒谎，但她没有直接指责他。|He insinuated himself into her affections. 他巧妙地赢得了她的爱。|Synonyms: imply, suggest, intimate, hint|Antonyms: declare, state, assert|verb
