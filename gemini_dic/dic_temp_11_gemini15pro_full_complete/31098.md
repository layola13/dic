championing: /ˈtʃæmpiənɪŋ/
verb
支持；拥护；捍卫
actively supporting or defending the cause of someone or something.
She has spent her career championing the rights of the disabled. 她毕生致力于维护残疾人的权利。
近义词: advocating, supporting, promoting, defending, upholding
反义词: opposing, attacking, criticizing, denouncing
词性: verb (动名词形式)

champion: /ˈtʃæmpiən/
noun
冠军；优胜者；捍卫者
a person, animal, or plant that has defeated all others in a competition.
He is the world champion in the 100 meters. 他是100米赛跑的世界冠军。
近义词: winner, victor, advocate, supporter, defender
反义词: loser, opponent, critic, attacker
词性: noun

champion: /ˈtʃæmpiən/
verb
支持；拥护；捍卫
actively support or defend the cause of.
He championed the cause of free speech. 他捍卫言论自由事业。
近义词: advocate, support, promote, defend, uphold
反义词: oppose, attack, criticize, denounce
词性: verb

词源: From Old French *champion, from Late Latin campio, "gladiator, fighter in the campus (field)," from Latin campus "field."
