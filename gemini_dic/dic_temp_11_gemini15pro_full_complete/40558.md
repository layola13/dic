controverts:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːts/ (UK), /ˈkɑːntrəvɜːrts/ (US)| v. | 反驳，驳斥，争论 | To argue against something; to deny the truth of something; to dispute or contest. |  The scientist controverts the widely held belief that the earth is flat.  这位科学家反驳了地球是平的这一普遍的观点。

|  His latest book controverts the claims made by previous historians. 他最新的著作反驳了先前历史学家的说法。


| 近义词: disputes, challenges, contests, denies, refutes, rebuts | 反义词: confirms, supports, corroborates, affirms, accepts, concedes | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin controversia, from controversus "turned against," from contra "against" + versus, past participle of vertere "to turn."
