hydracid:/ˈhaɪdræsɪd/
noun
氢酸
An acid that does not contain oxygen, but contains hydrogen.  A binary compound of hydrogen with a more electronegative element.
Hydrochloric acid (HCl) is a common hydracids used in laboratories. 氯化氢（HCl）是一种实验室常用的氢酸。
Hydrogen sulfide (H2S) is a hydracids with a characteristic rotten egg smell. 硫化氢（H₂S）是一种具有典型臭鸡蛋气味的氢酸。
近义词: binary acid, non-oxygen acid
反义词: oxyacid
