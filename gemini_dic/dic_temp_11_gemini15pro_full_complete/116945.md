mazelike:/ˈmeɪzˌlaɪk/
adjective
迷宫似的，像迷宫的
Resembling a maze; intricate and confusing.
The old city's streets were mazelike, and we got lost several times.  这座老城的街道像迷宫一样，我们迷路了好几次。
The building's corridors were mazelike and difficult to navigate.  这栋楼的走廊像迷宫一样，很难找到方向。
synonyms: labyrinthine, winding, twisting, confusing, intricate
antonyms: straightforward, simple, clear, direct
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From maze + -like.  Maze comes from Old French *maser "to bewilder," from Vulgar Latin *matiare, of unknown origin.
