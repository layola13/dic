novice:/ˈnɒvɪs/ (UK) /ˈnɑːvɪs/ (US)| n.| 新手，初学者 |A person new to or inexperienced in a field or activity. |例句：She's a novice at playing the piano. 她弹钢琴是个新手。

例句：He was still a novice in the world of finance. 他在金融界还是个新手。

近义词：beginner, learner, apprentice, trainee, tyro | 反义词：expert, professional, master, veteran | 词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *novice* (Modern French *novice*), from Latin *novicius* "new, fresh, young; newly arrived, newly enrolled," from *novus* "new."
