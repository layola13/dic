nongenetic:/ˌnɒn.dʒəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
非遗传的
Not relating to or caused by genes; acquired rather than inherited.
例句：Environmental factors can cause nongenetic changes in an organism. 环境因素会导致生物体的非遗传改变。

例句：Nongenetic factors, such as diet and lifestyle, play a significant role in determining an individual's health. 非遗传因素，如饮食和生活方式，在决定个人健康方面起着重要作用。

近义词：acquired, environmental, external
反义词：genetic, hereditary, inherited
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non "not".
genetic (adjective): From Greek genetikos "generative, pertaining to origin", from genesis "origin, creation, generation".
