high-powered:/ˌhaɪˈpaʊərd/| adj.|高性能的；强有力的；有权势的|Having a lot of power or influence; very effective or efficient.  Origin:  'high' (meaning large or great) + 'powered' (past participle of 'power,' meaning to supply with power).|例句：He's a high-powered executive in a multinational corporation. 他是一家跨国公司的高管。

例句：She drives a high-powered sports car. 她开着一辆高性能跑车。|近义词：influential, potent, strong, effective|反义词：weak, powerless, ineffective|词性: adjective
