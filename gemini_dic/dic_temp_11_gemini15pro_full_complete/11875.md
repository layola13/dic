arriviste:/ˌærɪˈvɪst/ /əˈriːvɪst/| n.|暴发户，野心家|An ambitious and unscrupulous person who has recently acquired wealth or power.|He's an arriviste who climbed the corporate ladder using questionable tactics. 他是个野心勃勃、不择手段的暴发户，用 questionable 的手段爬上了公司的阶梯。|Synonyms: parvenu, social climber, upstart, nouveau riche|Antonyms: established member, old money, aristocrat|Noun
