ceremonialists:/ˌserɪˈmoʊniəlɪsts/
n.
仪式专家；司仪
Experts in ceremonies; those who officiate at ceremonies.
The ceremonialists prepared the altar for the ancient ritual.  仪式专家为古代仪式准备了祭坛。
The ceremonialists ensured the coronation proceeded flawlessly.  司仪们确保加冕典礼顺利进行。
synonyms:officiants, masters of ceremonies, ritualists
antonyms:participants, attendees, guests
noun
