dissent:/dɪˈsɛnt/ | n. vi.| 异议，不同意 |Disagreement with the majority opinion.|  Two justices dissented from the majority opinion. 两位法官对多数意见表示异议。

dissent:/dɪˈsɛnt/ | vi.| 持异议，不同意 |To disagree with the majority opinion.| Several members dissented from the party line. 几名成员对党的路线表示异议。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *dissentir*, from Latin dissentire ("to disagree, differ in sentiment"), from dis- ("apart, away") + sentire ("to feel, think").

SYNONYMS->
disagreement, objection, opposition, protest, discord, variance

ANTONYMS->
agreement, assent, concurrence, harmony, accord, consent
