fucktard:/ˈfʌktɑːrd/
noun
笨蛋;蠢货;傻瓜
A highly offensive term for a stupid or foolish person.
That fucktard crashed his car into a lamppost. 那个笨蛋把他的车撞到了灯柱上。
Synonyms: idiot, moron, imbecile, asshole, jerk
Antonyms: genius, intellectual, smart, bright
词性: 名词 (noun)

词源:
A combination of "fuck" (vulgar slang, expressing anger, frustration, or contempt) and "retard" (offensive term for someone with intellectual disabilities).  The term is considered highly offensive and derogatory due to its use of both a vulgarity and a slur against people with disabilities.
