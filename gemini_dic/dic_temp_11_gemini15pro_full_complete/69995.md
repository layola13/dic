fetches:/ˈfɛtʃɪz/ /ˈfɛtʃəz/ | n. | 取得；拿来；诡计；手腕；(狗)衔回猎物 |  The act of going to get and bringing something back. Also, a trick, a ruse, or a whim.  In the context of dogs, it refers to their instinct to retrieve objects. | The dog fetches the ball. 狗把球捡回来了。 | synonyms: retrieves, obtains, collects, gathers, tricks, ruses, whims, fancies | antonyms: abandons, leaves, ignores, discards | noun

fetch:/fɛtʃ/ /fɛtʃ/ | v. | (去)拿来；售得；(指价格)达到 | To go to a place and bring something back. Also, to achieve a particular price when sold. | Could you fetch me my glasses from the other room? 你能从另一个房间帮我把眼镜拿来吗？| synonyms: retrieve, obtain, collect, gather, bring, carry | antonyms: leave, abandon, discard | verb
