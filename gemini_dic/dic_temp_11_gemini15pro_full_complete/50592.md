deranged:/dɪˈreɪndʒd/
adjective
精神错乱的，疯狂的
mad; insane.  Driven to madness.  Seriously mentally disturbed.
例句：The deranged man screamed at passersby.  那个疯子对着路人尖叫。
例句：He had a deranged look in his eyes. 他的眼神疯狂。
近义词：insane, mad, lunatic, demented, unhinged
反义词：sane, rational, lucid
词性：adjective

derange:/dɪˈreɪndʒ/
verb
使精神错乱，扰乱
to disturb the condition, action, or function of; to make insane
例句：Grief deranged his mind.  悲伤使他精神错乱。
例句：The loud noise deranged the meeting. 巨大的噪音扰乱了会议。
近义词：upset, disorder, disarrange, unsettle, disturb
反义词：organize, order, arrange, calm
词性：verb
