demerger:/diːˈmɜːrdʒər/
n.
公司分拆，业务剥离
The separation of a company into two or more independent companies.
The demerger of the company resulted in two separate entities.  这家公司的分拆导致了两个独立的实体。
The demerger created a new, smaller company focused on software development. 分拆创建了一个专注于软件开发的新的、规模较小的公司。
synonyms: divestiture, spin-off, separation
antonyms: merger, acquisition, consolidation
noun
