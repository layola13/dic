marshall:/ˈmɑːrʃəl/ (US) /ˈmɑːʃl/ (UK)| v.|安排；整理；集结|To gather together and arrange in order.|例句：He marshalled his arguments for the debate.  他整理了辩论的论点。|近义词：organize, arrange, assemble|反义词:disorganize, scatter|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French mareschal, marescal, from Frankish *marhskalk "horse servant," from *marha- "horse" + *skalk- "servant."
