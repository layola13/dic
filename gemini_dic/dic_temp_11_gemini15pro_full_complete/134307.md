noninsertion:/ˌnɒnɪnˈsɜːʃən/ | n. | 非插入，不插入 | The noninsertion of the gene resulted in a different phenotype.  该基因的非插入导致了不同的表型。 |  The policy emphasizes the noninsertion of foreign objects into the machinery. 该政策强调不要将异物插入机器中。 | 近义词: omission, exclusion, absence | 反义词: insertion, inclusion | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From non- + insertion.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without," and "insertion" comes from the Latin "insertio,"  meaning "the act of putting in."
