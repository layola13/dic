destigmatize:/diːˈstɪɡmətaɪz/
verb
去除污名，消除耻辱
To remove the stigma or negative associations from something.
例句：
Mental health advocates are working to destigmatize mental illness.
心理健康倡导者正在努力消除精神疾病的污名。

例句：
The campaign aims to destigmatize HIV and AIDS.
该运动旨在消除对艾滋病毒和艾滋病的污名。

近义词：normalize, legitimize, decriminalize
反义词：stigmatize, condemn, ostracize
词性:v.


词源:
From de- + stigmatize, from stigma + -ize.  "Stigma" comes from Greek στίγμα (stigma) meaning "mark, brand, tattoo."
