megacephalic:/ˌmɛɡəsɛˈfælɪk/| adj.|巨头的，头大的|Having an abnormally large head.  A megacephalic individual may experience various health challenges due to the increased size of their head.|The doctor diagnosed the child as megacephalic.|Synonyms: macrocephalic|Antonyms: microcephalic|adjective

megacephaly:/ˌmɛɡəˈsɛfəli/| n.|巨头畸形|The condition of having an abnormally large head.|Megacephaly can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetic mutations and hydrocephalus.|Synonyms: macrocephaly|Antonyms: microcephaly|noun
