lay-to-rest:/ˌleɪ tə ˈrest/
verb phrase
安葬；平息；消除
to bury a dead person; to put an end to something, especially a worry or fear
They laid him to rest in the family plot. 他们把他安葬在家族墓地里。

The government has finally laid the rumors to rest. 政府终于平息了谣言。

近义词: bury, inter, entomb, put to rest, dispel, allay
反义词: exhume, resurrect, revive

词性: verb phrase

词源:  From the literal act of laying a body to rest in a grave.  "Lay" meaning to put or place, and "rest" referring to the final resting place.
