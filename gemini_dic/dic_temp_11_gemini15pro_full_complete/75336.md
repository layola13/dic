frostiest:/ˈfrɔːstiɪst/| adj.|最冷的，最冷淡的|Superlative form of frosty; characterized by the most intense cold or the most unfriendly manner.  The frostiest reception I've ever received.  这是我所受到过的最冷淡的接待。|例句：It was the frostiest winter in decades.  这是几十年来最冷的冬天。

例句：Her frostiest glare silenced the entire room. 她最冰冷的一 glare 让整个房间鸦雀无声。|近义词：coldest, iciest, chilliest, most frigid, most unfriendly, most hostile|反义词：warmest, friendliest, most cordial|词性:形容词
