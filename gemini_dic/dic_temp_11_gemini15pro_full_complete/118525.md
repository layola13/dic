mesodermal:/ˌmezəˈdɜːrməl/ |adj.| 中胚层的；由中胚层发育而成的 |Relating to or derived from the mesoderm (the middle layer of the three primary germ layers of an embryo).|The mesodermal layer gives rise to muscles and connective tissues. 中胚层会发育形成肌肉和结缔组织。|  The heart is a mesodermal organ. 心脏是一个中胚层器官。|Synonyms:  mesoblastic | Antonyms:  ectodermal, endodermal|adjective
