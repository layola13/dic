bark-up-the-wrong-tree:/bɑːrk ˌʌp ðə ˌrɔːŋ ˈtriː/| idiom |找错目标；追错方向|To pursue a mistaken or misguided line of thought or course of action.|The police are barking up the wrong tree if they think I stole the money.  警察如果认为是我偷了钱，那他们就找错人了。|例句：You're barking up the wrong tree if you think he'll lend you money. 如果你认为他会借钱给你，那你就错了。

|近义词：on the wrong track, off the mark, miss the point|反义词：on the right track, hit the nail on the head|idiom
