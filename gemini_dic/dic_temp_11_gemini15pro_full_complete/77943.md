genomics:/dʒɪˈnoʊmɪks/
n.
基因组学
The study of genomes, including their structure, function, evolution, and mapping.
Genomics has revolutionized our understanding of human diseases. 基因组学彻底改变了我们对人类疾病的理解。
Genomics plays a crucial role in personalized medicine. 基因组学在个性化医疗中起着至关重要的作用。
