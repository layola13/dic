nominatable:/ˈnɒmɪneɪtəbl/
adjective
可提名的
able to be nominated.
例句：He is a nominatable candidate for the award. 他是该奖项的候选人。

例句：The film is nominatable for an Oscar. 这部电影可以被提名奥斯卡奖。

近义词：eligible, qualified
反义词：ineligible, unqualified
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From nominate + -able.  Nominate comes from the Latin "nominatus," the past participle of "nominare" meaning "to name."  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin meaning "capable of being."
