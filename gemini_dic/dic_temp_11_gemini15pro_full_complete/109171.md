legitimator:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmeɪtər/
n.
合法化者；赋予合法性的人（或事物）
One who makes something legitimate; one who legitimizes.
The United Nations acted as a legitimator for the new government. 联合国充当了新政府的合法化者。
legitimizer, validator, authorizer
illegitimizer, invalidator
