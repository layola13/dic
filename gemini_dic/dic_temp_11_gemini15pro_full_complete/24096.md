breviature:/briːviətʃər/ /brɛviətʃɚ/| n.|简写；缩写；摘要|A shortened form of a longer text; an abbreviation. |The breviature of the document summarized the key points.  这份文件的摘要总结了要点。|Synonym: abbreviation, abstract, summary, synopsis|Antonym: expansion, elaboration|noun

breviature:/briːviətʃər/ /brɛviətʃɚ/| n.|（天主教）日课经|A book containing the daily prayers and hymns of the Roman Catholic Church. |The priest read from the breviature during morning prayers.  神父在晨祷时读日课经。|Synonym:  divine office, liturgy|Antonym: None|noun
