feverishly:/ˈfiːvərɪʃli/ /ˈfivərɪʃli/
adverb
狂热地；兴奋地；发烧似地
In a way that shows a lot of nervous excitement or activity because you want to do something very much or because you are worried about something.

She worked feverishly to finish the project on time.  她拼命工作以按时完成项目。

He feverishly searched for his lost keys. 他狂热地寻找丢失的钥匙。

近义词: excitedly, frantically, intensely, passionately
反义词: calmly, apathetically, listlessly

词性: adverb
