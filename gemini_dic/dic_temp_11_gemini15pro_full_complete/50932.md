desiderata:/dɪˌzɪdəˈrɑːtə/ /dɪˌzɪdəˈreɪtə/
desiderata|n.|必需品；愿望；迫切需要的东西|Things wanted or needed; the necessities.|The poem "Desiderata" lists some of the desiderata for a peaceful life. 这首诗《渴望》列出了一些平静生活所必需的东西。|
desiderata|近义词：necessities, requirements, essentials, wants, desires|反义词：nonessentials, inessentials|词性：名词


desideratum:/dɪˌzɪdəˈreɪtəm/ /dɪˌzɪdəˈrɑːtəm/
desideratum|n.|必需品；愿望；迫切需要的东西|Something wanted or needed.|A reliable car is a desideratum for a traveling salesman.  对于一名旅行推销员来说，一辆可靠的汽车是必需品。|
desideratum|近义词：necessity, requirement, essential, want, desire|反义词：nonessential, inessential|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēsīderātum*, neuter past participle of *dēsīderāre* "to long for, desire," from *de-* "from" + *sīderāre* "to long for a star," from *sīdus* "star, constellation."
