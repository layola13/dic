hippodrome:/ˈhɪpədrəʊm/ (US) /ˈhɪpədrəʊm/ (UK)| n. |竞技场，跑马场|An oval stadium for chariot and horse races in ancient Greece and Rome. A modern structure for equestrian performances or other spectacles.|The emperor attended the races at the hippodrome.|近义词: arena, stadium, racecourse, track|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) theater, concert hall|词性: Noun
