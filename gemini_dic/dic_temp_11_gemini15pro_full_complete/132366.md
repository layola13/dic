nondelicateness:/ˌnɒnˈdɛlɪkətnəs/
noun
不雅;粗俗;粗鲁
lack of delicacy; indelicacy.  The nondelicateness of his remark shocked everyone present.
His nondelicateness embarrassed everyone.  他粗俗的言论让每个人都很尴尬。
近义词:indelicacy, coarseness, rudeness, vulgarity, crudeness
反义词:delicacy, refinement, politeness, tact, sensitivity
