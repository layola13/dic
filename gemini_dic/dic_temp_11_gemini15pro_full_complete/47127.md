例句：The title of Dauphin fell out of use after the French Revolution. 法国大革命后，多芬的头衔就不再使用了。|近义词：crown prince, heir apparent|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *daufin, from the personal name Dauphin, originally a nickname meaning "dolphin," from Latin delphinus.  The lords of Viennois, whose coat of arms featured dolphins, were known as the Dauphins, and the title passed to the French crown when the last Dauphin of Viennois ceded his lands to King Philip VI in 1349.
