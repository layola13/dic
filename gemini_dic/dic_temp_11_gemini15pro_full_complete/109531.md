less /les/ | adj. adv. | 更少的，较少的；更小地，较小地 | smaller in size, amount, degree, or importance |  I have less money than him. 我比他的钱少。 | Near synonyms: fewer, smaller, minor, reduced | Antonyms: more, greater, larger, increased | 词性: 形容词，副词

less /les/ | pron. | 更少（的） | a smaller amount of something | I eat less now than I used to. 我现在吃得比以前少了。 | Near synonyms: fewer | Antonyms: more | 词性: 代词

I am eating less these days. 我这几天吃得比较少。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
Do you eat less meat than before? 你现在比以前少吃肉吗？

否定句:

词源：From Old English *lēssa (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *laisiz (“less”), from Proto-Indo-European *leys- (“to loosen, slacken”). Cognate with Old Frisian lesse, lessa (“less”), Old Saxon les, Old High German las (“weak, infirm, small”), Gothic 𐌻𐌰𐌹𐍃𐍃 (laiss, “less”). Related to loose.
