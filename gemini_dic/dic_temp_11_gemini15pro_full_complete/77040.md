garlands:/ˈɡɑːrləndz/ | n. | 花环，花圈，彩灯串 | A wreath of flowers and leaves, worn on the head or hung as a decoration. |  The bride wore a garland of white roses. 新娘戴着白色玫瑰花环。

The hall was decorated with garlands of flowers. 大厅装饰着花环。 | 近义词: wreath, festoon, chaplet | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

garland:/ˈɡɑːrlənd/ | vt. | 用花环装饰 | To decorate with a garland. | They garlanded the winning horse. 他们用花环装饰了获胜的马匹。
