gastromyth: /ˈɡæstrəʊmɪθ/ | n. | 饮食谬误，关于食物的错误观念 | A gastromyth is a widespread but untrue story about food. |  Example: It's a gastromyth that eating carrots improves your eyesight at night.  吃胡萝卜能改善夜视力是一个饮食谬误。

Example: Another common gastromyth is that MSG is inherently unhealthy.  另一个常见的饮食谬误是味精本质上不健康。 | Synonyms: food myth, culinary myth, food fallacy | Antonyms: nutritional fact, dietary truth | noun
