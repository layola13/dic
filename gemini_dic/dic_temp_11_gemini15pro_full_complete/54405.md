disincarceration:/ˌdɪsɪnˌkɑːrsəˈreɪʃən/
n.
释放，去监禁化
The act of releasing someone from prison, or the policy of reducing the number of people in prison.
The report advocated for policies of decarceration and disincarceration to address the overcrowded prisons.  这份报告主张采取减少监禁和去监禁化的政策来解决监狱人满为患的问题。
The disincarceration of non-violent offenders is a growing trend.  释放非暴力犯罪者是一个日益增长的趋势。
Synonyms: release, liberation, freeing, emancipation
Antonyms: imprisonment, incarceration, confinement
