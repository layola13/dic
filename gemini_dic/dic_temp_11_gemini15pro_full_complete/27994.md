canyada:/kænjəˈɑːdə/ | n. |峡谷，溪谷 | A deep gorge, typically one with a river flowing through it. |  We hiked through the stunning canyada. 我们徒步穿越了令人惊叹的峡谷。|  The canyada echoed with the sound of rushing water. 峡谷回荡着奔腾的水声。|近义词：canyon, gorge, ravine | 反义词: plain, plateau | 词性: 名词

词源: From Spanish *cañada*, meaning "glen, dale, or ravine," derived from Latin *canna*, meaning "reed" or "cane," referring to the vegetation often found in such places.
