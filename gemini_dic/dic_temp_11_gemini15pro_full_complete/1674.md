acnode:/ˈæknoʊd/| n. |孤立点，尖点|A point on a curve where two branches meet tangentially without crossing.  An isolated point not upon a curve, but whose coordinates satisfy the equation of the curve. |The graph of the equation y^2 = x^3 has an acnode at the origin.|近义词: cusp, singular point|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "smooth point" in some contexts)|noun
