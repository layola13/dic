elasipoda:/ɪˌlæˈsɪpədə/
| n. |海参纲|A class of echinoderms, commonly known as sea cucumbers, characterized by their soft, elongated bodies and reduced or absent tube feet. They are mostly benthic, living on the seafloor.|The deep-sea elasipoda have fascinating adaptations to their environment. 深海海参对其环境有着惊人的适应能力。|近义词:holothurian (though this refers to the whole class Holothuroidea, of which Elasipoda is a part)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek *elasipous*  ("flat-footed"), from *elasys* ("soft") + *pous* ("foot").
