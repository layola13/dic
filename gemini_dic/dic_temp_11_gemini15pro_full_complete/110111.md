licks:/lɪks/
n.|舔; 少量; 打; 鞭打|The cat gave its paw a few licks. 猫舔了几下爪子。|近义词: laps, hits, beats|反义词: none (in the sense of "a small amount")|noun

licks:/lɪks/
v.|(舔) 击败; 征服; 轻易地打败 (informal)|The flames licked at the dry wood. 火苗舔着干木。|近义词: defeat, conquer, overcome|反义词: lose to, be defeated by|verb
