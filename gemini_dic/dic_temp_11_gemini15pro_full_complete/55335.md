dissuasory:/dɪˈswɔːsəri/ /dɪˈswer.sɔːri/
adjective
劝阻的，阻止的，威慑的
tending or intended to dissuade; deterring.
The high price of the tickets was dissuasory to many fans.  高昂的票价让许多粉丝望而却步。
The government imposed dissuasory taxes on cigarettes. 政府对香烟征收高额的劝阻性税收。
近义词: deterrent, discouraging, preventative
反义词: encouraging, promoting, inciting
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin dissuasorius, from dissuadere "to advise against," from dis- "apart, away" + suadere "to urge, persuade."
