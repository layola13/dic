nursehound:/ˈnɜːrshaʊnd/ | n. |铰口鲨，猫鲨|A type of shark (Scyliorhinus stellaris) found in the eastern Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea, known for its distinctive patterned skin and relatively small size.  It is also known as the large-spotted dogfish, greater spotted dogfish, or bull huss. |例句：The nursehound is a common sight for divers in these waters.  铰口鲨在这些水域是潜水员常见的景象。

例句：Nursehounds are bottom-dwelling sharks that feed on crustaceans and small fish. 铰口鲨是底栖鲨鱼，以甲壳类动物和小鱼为食。|近义词：dogfish, huss, shark|反义词: N/A (No direct opposite for a specific animal)|词性: noun
