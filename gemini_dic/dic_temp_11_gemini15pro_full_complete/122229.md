misworshiper:/ˌmɪsˈwɜːrʃɪpər/ | n. | 误拜者，错误崇拜者 | Someone who worships falsely or directs worship to an unworthy object. |  The misworshiper knelt before the idol, unaware of its false power. 误拜者跪在偶像面前，没有意识到它的虚假力量。

The leader was denounced as a misworshiper for leading his followers astray.  这位领导人因将追随者引入歧途而被谴责为误拜者。 | 近义词: idolater, heretic | 反义词: devotee, believer | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From mis- + worshiper.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "badly," or "wrongly." "Worshiper" comes from the Old English "worþscip," meaning "honor, respect, reverence," combined with "-er," a suffix denoting an agent or one who performs an action.
