narcissistic:/nɑːr.sɪˈsɪs.tɪk/ /nɑːr.səˈsɪs.tɪk/| adj.|自恋的，自我陶醉的|Having or showing an excessive interest in or admiration of oneself and one's physical appearance.|例句：He has a narcissistic personality. 他有自恋型人格。|例句：She posted a narcissistic selfie on Instagram. 她在Instagram上发布了一张自恋的自拍照。|近义词：self-centered, egotistical, vain, conceited|反义词：humble, modest, selfless|词性:形容词

narcissist:/ˈnɑːr.sɪ.sɪst/ /ˈnɑːr.sə.sɪst/| n.|自恋者|A person who has an excessive interest in or admiration of themselves.|例句：He's a complete narcissist – he only cares about himself. 他是个十足的自恋狂——他只关心自己。|例句：Many people consider her a narcissist because of her constant boasting. 许多人认为她是一个自恋者，因为她不断地吹嘘自己。|近义词：egotist, egomaniac|反义词：altruist|词性:名词

narcissism:/ˈnɑːr.sɪ.sɪ.zəm/ /ˈnɑːr.sə.sɪ.zəm/| n.|自恋|Excessive interest in or admiration of oneself and one's physical appearance.|例句：Narcissism can be a destructive force in relationships. 自恋在人际关系中可能是一种破坏性的力量。|例句：Her narcissism prevents her from seeing her own flaws. 她的自恋使她看不到自己的缺点。|近义词：vanity, self-love, egotism|反义词：humility, selflessness|词性:名词


词源: From Narcissus, a character in Greek mythology who fell in love with his own reflection.
