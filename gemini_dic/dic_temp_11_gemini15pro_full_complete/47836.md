decayable:/dɪˈkeɪəbl/
adjective
可腐烂的；易腐败的
Subject to decay; likely to decay.
例句：Decayable organic matter is broken down by bacteria and fungi.  可腐烂的有机物会被细菌和真菌分解。

例句：Food waste is decayable and should be composted. 食物残渣是可腐烂的，应该堆肥。

近义词：perishable, decomposable, biodegradable, putrescible
反义词：imperishable, non-biodegradable, indestructible
词性: adjective
