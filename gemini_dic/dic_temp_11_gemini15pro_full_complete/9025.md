antiman:/ˈæntiˌmæn/| n.|反物质人；反男人|A hypothetical subatomic particle with the same mass as an ordinary particle but with opposite charge and magnetic properties. It is also used figuratively to describe someone who opposes traditional masculine roles or behavior.|例句：In the realm of science fiction, antiman often plays a crucial role. 在科幻小说领域，反物质人经常扮演着至关重要的角色。

例句：He was considered an antiman because he challenged the societal norms of masculinity.  他被认为是一个反传统男性的人，因为他挑战了社会对男性气质的规范。|近义词：counterpart (in physics), nonconformist|反义词：man, conformist|词性: noun
