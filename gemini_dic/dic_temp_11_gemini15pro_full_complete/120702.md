misagree:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/ (US), /ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/ (UK)| vi.|不同意，意见不合|To have a different opinion from someone else; to not be in accord.|例句：I disagree with his assessment of the situation. 我不同意他对情况的评估。|近义词：differ, dissent, object|反义词:agree, concur, consent|verb

misagree:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/ (US), /ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/ (UK)| vi.|不一致，不符|To be unlike or dissimilar; to clash.|例句：The two accounts of the accident disagree.  关于事故的两种说法不一致。|近义词：conflict, clash, vary|反义词:match, correspond, coincide|verb
