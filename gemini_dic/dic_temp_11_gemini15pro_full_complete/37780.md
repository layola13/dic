compatriotic: /kəmˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪk/ /kəmˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪk/
adjective
同胞的；爱国的
Sharing the same country of origin; patriotic.
例句：Compatriotic fervor swept the nation during the Olympics. 奥运会期间，爱国热情席卷全国。

例句：He felt a strong compatriotic bond with the other soldiers. 他与其他士兵感到了一种强烈的同胞情谊。


近义词：fellow countryman, national
反义词：foreign, alien
词性: adjective
