leopardesses: /ˈlɛpədɪsɪz/
n.
雌豹, 母豹
Female leopards.
The leopardesses stalked their prey through the tall grass.  雌豹在高高的草丛中跟踪猎物。
A coalition of leopardesses defended their territory fiercely.  一群雌豹凶猛地保卫着它们的领地。
近义词: female leopards
反义词: male leopards (雄豹)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lepartes*, from Old French *leopart*, *liopart*, from Latin *leopardus*, from Ancient Greek *λέοπαρδος (léopardos)* (leopard), from *λέων (léōn)* (lion) + *πάρδος (párdos)* (panther, leopard).
