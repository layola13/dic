habeas:/ˈheɪbiəs/| n. |人身保护令|A writ requiring a person under arrest to be brought before a judge or into court, especially to secure the person's release unless lawful grounds are shown for their detention.|The lawyer filed a writ of habeas corpus to challenge the legality of his client's detention. 律师提交了人身保护令状，对当事人被拘留的合法性提出质疑。|Synonyms: writ, legal order, court order|Antonyms: detention, imprisonment|noun
