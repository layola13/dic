myalgias:/maɪˈældʒəz/ | n. | 肌肉疼痛 | Muscle pain.  It's characterized by aching, soreness, and stiffness in the muscles. | I woke up with terrible myalgias after my workout.  我锻炼后醒来时肌肉疼痛得厉害。|  She experienced myalgias as a side effect of the medication. 她服药后出现肌肉疼痛的副作用。 | 近义词: muscle aches, muscle pains | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "muscle comfort," "pain relief") | 词性: Noun (plural)

Etymology:  From the Greek "myo-" (muscle) + "-algia" (pain).
