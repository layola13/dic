overfluentness:/ˌoʊvərˈfluːəntnəs/
n.
过度流利，口若悬河
Excessive fluency of speech, often to the point of incoherence or inappropriateness.
His overfluentness made it difficult to follow his train of thought.  他太流利了，让人很难跟上他的思路。
近义词: volubility, loquacity, garrulity
反义词: hesitancy, reticence
词性: noun

Etymology:
From over- + fluent + -ness.  "Over-" indicates excess, "fluent" refers to smooth and easy flow of speech, and "-ness" forms a noun denoting a state or quality.  由"over-"（过度）+ "fluent"（流利）+ "-ness"（名词后缀表示状态或性质）构成。
