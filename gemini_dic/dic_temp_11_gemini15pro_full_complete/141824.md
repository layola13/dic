obituary: /əˈbɪtʃuˌeri/ | n. |讣告，讣闻|A notice of a death, especially in a newspaper, typically with a brief biography of the deceased person.|例句：The newspaper published his obituary the day after he died. 报纸在他去世的第二天刊登了他的讣告。

例句：She wrote a moving obituary for her grandmother. 她为祖母写了一篇感人的讣告。

近义词：death notice, necrology|反义词: None |词性: Noun
