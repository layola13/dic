commissar:/ˌkɒmɪˈsɑːr/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmɪsɑːr/ (US)| n.|政委，委员|An official of the Communist Party, especially in the former Soviet Union, who oversaw the political indoctrination and loyalty of members of the armed forces or other organizations.|The commissar ensured that the soldiers followed the party line. 政委确保士兵们遵循党的路线。|Synonyms: official, commissioner, delegate, representative|Antonyms: civilian, non-party member|noun
