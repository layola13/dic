autogenesis:/ˌɔːtəˈdʒɛnəsɪs/
n.
自生，自然发生
The theory of autogenesis proposes that life arose from non-living matter.  自生理论认为生命起源于非生命物质。
The idea of autogenesis has been largely discredited by modern science.  自生说在很大程度上已被现代科学所否定。
Autogenesis is the creation of life from non-living matter.  自生是指从非生命物质中创造生命。
例句：The concept of autogenesis was once widely accepted.  自生的概念曾经被广泛接受。

例句：Scientists have long debated the possibility of autogenesis.  科学家们长期以来一直在争论自生的可能性。
近义词：abiogenesis, spontaneous generation
反义词：biogenesis
词性：noun
