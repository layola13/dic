escalators:/ˈeskəleɪtərz/ (US), /ˈeskəleɪtəz/ (UK)
n.
自动扶梯，电动扶梯
A moving staircase that carries people between different levels of a building or structure.
The shoppers rode the escalators to the second floor. 顾客们乘坐自动扶梯上了二楼。
近义词:moving staircase, stairs, elevator, lift
反义词:stairs, ramp
词性:名词(plural)
