exitless:/ˈɛksɪtlɪs/
adjective
没有出口的；无出路的
Having no exit; offering no means of escape.
The mouse found itself trapped in an exitless maze. 老鼠发现自己被困在一个没有出口的迷宫里。
近义词:trapped, inescapable, closed, blocked
反义词:open, accessible, free
