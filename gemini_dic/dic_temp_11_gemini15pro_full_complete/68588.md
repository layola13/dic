fans:/fænz/
n.
粉丝；狂热爱好者；扇子
A person who has a strong interest in or admiration for a particular person or thing.
例句：The band has millions of fans around the world.  这个乐队在世界各地有数百万粉丝。

例句：He's a big fan of science fiction movies. 他是科幻电影的忠实粉丝。

近义词：admirers, followers, enthusiasts, supporters
反义词：critics, detractors
词性: 名词

fans:/fænz/
n.
扇子
A device for creating a current of air by movement of a surface or surfaces.
例句：She used a fan to cool herself down. 她用扇子给自己降温。

例句：The fans in the room were whirring loudly. 房间里的风扇呼呼作响。
近义词：blowers, ventilators
反义词: heater
词性: 名词


词源:
From Old French *van (modern French van) "winnowing fan, basket for winnowing grain," from Latin vannus "winnowing fan, shovel." The sense of "enthusiastic admirer" is first recorded in 1889, originally in sports (baseball).
