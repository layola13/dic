agreeability:/əˌɡriːəˈbɪləti/
n.|同意，一致；和蔼可亲；令人愉快|the quality of being pleasant or pleasing|Her agreeability made her popular.|近义词：pleasantness, affability, amiability|反义词：disagreeableness, hostility, unpleasantness|词性:名词
Etymology: From agreeable + -ity. Agreeable from French agréable, from agréer (to please, accept), from à (to) + gré (pleasure, liking), from Latin gratus (pleasing).
