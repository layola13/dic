galling:/ˈɡɔːlɪŋ/| adj.|令人恼火的，使人愤怒的|Causing annoyance or resentment; irritating.|例句：It was galling to have to apologize to him.  不得不向他道歉真是令人恼火。|近义词：annoying, irritating, exasperating, vexing|反义词：soothing, pleasing, comforting|词性:形容词
词源: From gall (v.) "make sore by rubbing" (early 14c.), from Old French galer "to rub, chafe" (12c.), from a Germanic source related to Old English gealla "gall, sore," from Proto-Germanic *gallon (source also of Old Norse gall "sore," Middle Dutch galle "itching"), from PIE root *ghel- (2) "to shine; yellow, green," also "gallbladder, gall."  The original sense is of chafing, irritating.  The figurative sense of "vex, irritate" is recorded by late 14c.
