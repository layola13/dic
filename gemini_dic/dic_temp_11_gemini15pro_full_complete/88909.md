hold-forth:/hoʊld ˈfɔːrθ/| vi.|滔滔不绝地讲；发表长篇大论|to speak lengthily and formally, often in a pompous or tedious way|例句：He held forth for over an hour on the subject of tax reform. 他就税制改革滔滔不绝地讲了一个多小时。|例句：She held forth at great length about the benefits of vegetarianism. 她详细地阐述了素食主义的好处。|近义词：expatiate, declaim, preach, sermonize|反义词：be quiet, listen|词性：动词短语
