nonadjudicative:/ˌnɑːnəˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtɪv/
adjective
非裁决性的
Not involving the judgment of a case; not pertaining to the function of a judge or court in making a formal decision about a dispute.  Relating to procedures or actions that are advisory, investigative, or conciliatory, rather than leading to a binding decision.
The ombudsman's role is nonadjudicative; they investigate complaints but don't have the power to issue rulings. 申诉专员的角色是非裁决性的；他们调查投诉，但无权发布裁决。
The mediator facilitated a nonadjudicative process to help the parties reach a mutually acceptable agreement. 调解员促进了非裁决性程序，以帮助各方达成双方都能接受的协议。
近义词: non-judicial, advisory, conciliatory, investigative
反义词: adjudicative, judicial, binding
词性: adjective
词源:  From "non-" (not) + "adjudicative" (relating to adjudication).  "Adjudicate" comes from Latin "adjudicare" (to award, to judge), from "ad-" (to) + "judicare" (to judge).
