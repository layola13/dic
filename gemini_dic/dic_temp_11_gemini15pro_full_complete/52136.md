diapedesis:/ˌdaɪəpɪˈdiːsɪs/ /ˌdaɪəpiːˈdiːsɪs/
noun
渗出；血细胞渗出
The passage of blood cells through the intact walls of the capillaries, typically accompanying inflammation.  白细胞穿过完整的毛细血管壁，通常伴有炎症。
During an inflammatory response, white blood cells undergo diapedesis to reach the site of injury. 在炎症反应期间，白细胞会发生渗出以到达损伤部位。
Leukocytes migrate from the bloodstream to the tissues by diapedesis. 白细胞通过渗出从血液迁移到组织。
synonyms: extravasation, transmigration
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for this biological process)
n.
