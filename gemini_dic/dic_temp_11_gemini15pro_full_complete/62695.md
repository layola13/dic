engine:/ˈɛndʒɪn/ (US), /ˈɛndʒɪn/ (UK)| n.|引擎，发动机; 机车，火车头; 工具，手段|A machine with moving parts that converts power into motion.  Something that is the driving force behind a particular process or activity.|The car's engine stalled. 汽车的引擎熄火了。|Synonyms: motor, machine, mechanism, apparatus, device|Antonyms:  (Engine doesn't have a direct antonym.  One might consider "brake" or "impediment" in certain contexts.)|noun
