arriving:/əˈraɪvɪŋ/| adj. v.|到达的，来临的|Present participle of "arrive."  Indicates the act of coming to a particular place.|例句：We saw the arriving passengers disembark the plane. 我们看到到达的乘客下了飞机。|近义词：coming, approaching, reaching|反义词:departing, leaving|词性:动词现在分词/形容词
Etymology: From Old French *arriver, from Latin arripare "to come to shore," from ad- "to" + ripa "shore, bank."
