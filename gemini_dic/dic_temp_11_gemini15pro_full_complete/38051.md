composed:/kəmˈpoʊzd/ (American) /kəmˈpəʊzd/ (British)| adj.|沉着的；镇定的；平静的；由…组成（构成）的|calm, tranquil;  made up of|例句：He appeared composed despite the chaos surrounding him. 尽管周围一片混乱，他看起来依然很镇定。|近义词：calm, serene, tranquil, collected, self-possessed|反义词：agitated, nervous, distraught, anxious|词性:形容词

composed:/kəmˈpoʊzd/ (American) /kəmˈpəʊzd/ (British)| v. (past participle of compose) |组成；构成；创作（乐曲、诗歌等）；使平静|create, constitute, make up, calm|例句：The committee is composed of ten members.  委员会由十名成员组成。|近义词：create, write, form, constitute|反义词：destroy, dismantle, discompose|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old French *compos*, past participle of *composer*, from Latin *componere* "to put together," from *com-* "together" + *ponere* "to put, place."
