barren:/ˈbærən/ | adj. | 贫瘠的；不育的；无结果的；空洞的 | Too much rain can make land barren.  过多的雨水会使土地贫瘠。| unproductive, sterile, infertile, desolate, arid | fertile, fruitful, productive | adjective

barren:/ˈbærən/ | n. | 荒地 |  The travelers crossed a vast, barren.  旅行者穿越了一片广阔的荒地。| wasteland, desert | fertile land, oasis | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bareyn*, from Old French *baraigne*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Vulgar Latin *barranus* (“muddy, miry”), from Gaulish.
