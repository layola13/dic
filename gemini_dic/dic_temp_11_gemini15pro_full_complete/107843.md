lardass:/ˈlɑːrdæs/| n.|肥猪; 胖子; 懒汉|A derogatory term for an obese person, often implying laziness and greed.|例句：He called his overweight neighbor a lardass.  他骂他超重的邻居是肥猪。

例句：Don't be a lardass, get up and exercise!  别做个懒胖子，起来锻炼身体！|近义词：fatso, slob, tub of lard|反义词:thin person, slim person|词性:名词

词源:  Compound of lard (noun) + ass (noun).  Lard refers to animal fat, implying excessive body fat. Ass adds a derogatory and insulting connotation.
