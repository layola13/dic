discussions:/dɪˈskʌʃənz/| n.|讨论；商讨；谈论|A conversation or debate about a particular topic.|We had several discussions about the project before we started. 我们在开始之前对这个项目进行了多次讨论。|
近义词:talks, conversations, debates, deliberations|反义词:arguments, disagreements|词性:名词|

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French discussion, from late Latin discussio(n-), from the verb discutere ‘examine’.|
