disremember:/ˌdɪsrɪˈmɛmbər/
verb
忘记，记不住
To fail to remember; forget.
I disremember what he told me. 我不记得他告诉过我什么。
近义词: forget, misremember
反义词: remember, recall
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + remember.

USAGE->
