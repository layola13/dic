moble:/ˈmoʊbaɪl/ (possibly intended as "mobile") | adj. | 流动的，移动的 | Able to move or be moved freely or easily.  | Example:  A mobile library brings books to people in remote areas.  移动图书馆将书籍带给偏远地区的人们。

Example:  Mobile phones have revolutionized communication.  手机彻底改变了通讯方式。 | 近义词: movable, portable, transportable | 反义词: immobile, fixed, stationary | 词性: adjective
