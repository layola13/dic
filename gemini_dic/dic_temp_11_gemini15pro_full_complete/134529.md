nonlicentious:/ˌnɒnlaɪˈsɛnʃəs/ | /ˌnɑːnlaɪˈsɛnʃəs/ | adj. | 不放荡的；有节制的；庄重的 | Not licentious; restrained, especially in sexual behavior; decent. |  She maintained a nonlicentious lifestyle, avoiding excessive drinking and promiscuity. 她保持着一种有节制的生活方式，避免酗酒和滥交。|  His nonlicentious behavior earned him the respect of his colleagues. 他有节制的行为赢得了同事们的尊重。| 近义词：chaste, pure, virtuous, decent, modest, restrained | 反义词：licentious, promiscuous, dissolute, debauched, immoral | 词性：形容词

词源：
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non. 表示“不”，来自拉丁语 non。
licentious (adjective): From Latin licentiosus "unrestrained," from licentia "freedom, license."  来自拉丁语 licentiosus “不受约束的”，来自 licentia “自由，许可”。
