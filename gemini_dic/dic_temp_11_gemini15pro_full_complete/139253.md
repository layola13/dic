obliged:/əˈblaɪdʒd/
adjective
感激的; 必须的; 负有义务的
Feeling or showing gratitude.  Compelled by circumstances or legally or morally bound to do something.
例句：I'm much obliged to you for your help. 我非常感谢你的帮助。

例句：We are obliged to obey the law. 我们有义务遵守法律。

近义词：grateful, thankful, indebted, compelled, required, obligated
反义词：ungrateful, unthankful, exempt

obliged:/əˈblaɪdʒd/
verb (past tense and past participle of oblige)
迫使; 施恩惠于
Compel someone to do something. Do a service or favour for someone.
例句：The law obliges parents to send their children to school. 法律规定父母必须送孩子上学。

例句：He obliged us with a song. 他为我们唱了一首歌。

近义词：compel, force, require, coerce, favor, accommodate
反义词：exempt, excuse


词性:  既可以是形容词，也可以是动词的过去式/过去分词

词源: From Old French *obliged, past participle of obliger, from Latin obligāre (“to bind, obligate”), from ob- (“towards, against”) + ligāre (“to bind”).
