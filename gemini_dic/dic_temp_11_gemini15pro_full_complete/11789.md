arraign:/əˈreɪn/ | v. |传讯；控告；责难 |to call or bring (someone) before a court to answer to an indictment |The suspect was arraigned on charges of murder. 嫌疑犯因谋杀罪被传讯。|近义词：indict, accuse, charge, prosecute |反义词：exonerate, acquit | verb

arraign:/əˈreɪn/ | v. |责难；谴责 |to criticize severely or angrily |He was arraigned by his colleagues for his unprofessional conduct. 他因不专业的行为受到同事的谴责。|近义词：denounce, criticize, condemn, censure |反义词：praise, commend | verb
