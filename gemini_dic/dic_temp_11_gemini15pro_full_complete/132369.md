nondelinquents:/ˌnɒndɪˈlɪŋkwənts/ | n. | 非违规者；未犯罪者 |  People who have not committed any crime or offense, especially juveniles.  This term is often used in contrast to "delinquents". |  The study compared the academic performance of delinquents and nondelinquents. 这项研究比较了违规者和非违规者的学习成绩。|  The program aims to keep nondelinquents from engaging in criminal activities.  该计划旨在防止非违规者参与犯罪活动。 | Synonyms: law-abiding citizens, conformists | Antonyms: delinquents, offenders, criminals | noun
