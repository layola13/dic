obstetricians:/ˌɒbstəˈtrɪʃənz/ | n. | 产科医生 | A doctor who specializes in pregnancy, childbirth, and the postpartum period. |  例句：The obstetricians monitored the pregnant woman's health throughout her pregnancy. 产科医生在整个怀孕期间监测孕妇的健康。

例句：Many obstetricians recommend breastfeeding for the first six months of a baby's life. 许多产科医生建议婴儿出生后前六个月进行母乳喂养。

| 近义词：gynecologist (妇科医生),  pregnancy doctor (孕期医生) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
