noneminent:/nɒnˈemɪnənt/ | adj. | 不杰出的，不出名的 | Not eminent; not distinguished or prominent. |  The noneminent scientist worked diligently in his lab.  这位不出名的科学家在他的实验室里勤奋地工作。|  The conference was attended by many noneminent but dedicated researchers.  许多不出名的但很敬业的研究人员参加了这次会议。 | 近义词: obscure, undistinguished, unknown, inconspicuous | 反义词: eminent, distinguished, prominent, famous, renowned | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From non- + eminent.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Eminent" comes from the Latin "ēminēns," present participle of "ēminēre" meaning "to stand out," from "ex-" (out) + "minēre" (to project).
