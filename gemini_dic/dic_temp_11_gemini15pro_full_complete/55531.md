distributing:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪŋ/
verb
分发，分配
the act of spreading something, or the way in which something is spread
The company is responsible for distributing goods to retailers.  这家公司负责将货物分发给零售商。
synonyms: dispensing, allocating, sharing, apportioning
antonyms: collecting, gathering, hoarding
verb

distributing:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪŋ/
adjective
分布的，分配的
spreading or supplying something
The distributing agent plays a key role in the supply chain. 分销代理在供应链中扮演着关键角色。
synonyms: dispersive, scattering, spreading
antonyms: concentrating, centralizing
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "distribuere" (to distribute), from "dis-" (apart, asunder) + "tribuere" (to allot, assign).
