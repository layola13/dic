overrunner:/ˌōvərˈrʌnər/| n.|超额完成者；溢出物；蔓延物；超支者|A person or thing that overruns or exceeds a limit, especially in terms of time or budget.  Something that spreads or spills over its intended boundaries.|The project had several overrunners who needed to be reassigned. 这个项目有几个超额完成者需要重新分配。|近义词：exceed, surpass, overflow|反义词:underrunner, laggard|noun


overrun:/ˌōvərˈrʌn/| v.|泛滥；超过；蹂躏；蔓延|To spread over or occupy excessively; to exceed a limit, especially of time or cost.| Weeds had overrun the garden. 杂草已经蔓延到整个花园。|近义词：overwhelm, infest, invade|反义词:underperform|verb


overrun:/ˈōvərˌrʌn/| adj.|泛滥的；蔓延的；超出限度的|Spread over or occupied excessively; exceeding a limit, especially of time or cost.|The project was overrun and over budget. 这个项目超时且超预算。|近义词：overwhelmed, infested, invaded|反义词:under budget|adjective
