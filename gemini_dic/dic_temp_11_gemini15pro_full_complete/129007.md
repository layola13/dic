nirvanas:/nɪrˈvɑːnəz/ /nɪrˈvænəz/| n. |涅槃，极乐世界；天堂；解脱|A state of perfect happiness and peace; a state of freedom from suffering and the cycle of birth and death, especially in Buddhism and Hinduism. Also used informally to refer to a state of bliss or complete relaxation.|He found his nirvana in playing the guitar. 他在弹吉他中找到了他的极乐世界。|近义词：paradise, heaven, bliss, ecstasy|反义词：hell, suffering, torment|词性:noun
