alfione: /ælfiˈoʊn/ | n. | 阿尔菲奥内; 阿尔菲奥尼（一种意大利红葡萄酒） |  A red Italian wine grape variety, primarily grown in the Veneto region of Italy, known for producing wines with a deep ruby color, aromas of red fruit, and a slightly bitter finish.  | This alfione has a pleasant, slightly bitter almond aftertaste.  这种阿尔菲奥内葡萄酒有令人愉悦的、略带苦味的杏仁回味。|Synonyms:  Veneto red, Italian red | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
