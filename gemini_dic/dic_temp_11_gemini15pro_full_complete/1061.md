accentuated: /əkˈsɛntʃueɪtɪd/
adjective
强调的;突出的
made more noticeable or prominent
Her dress accentuated her tiny waist. 她的裙子突出了她纤细的腰肢。
accentuated, highlighted, emphasized
downplayed, minimized
词性:形容词

accentuate: /əkˈsɛntʃueɪt/
verb
强调;突出
to make something more noticeable
The rising inflation rate only accentuates the need for further economic reform.  不断上升的通货膨胀率只会更加突出进一步经济改革的必要性。
emphasize, highlight, stress
downplay, de-emphasize
词性:动词

词源:  From Late Latin *accentuatus, past participle of accentuare, from Latin accentus (“accent, tone”)
