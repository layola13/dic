finalize:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪz/| v. | 完成；最终确定；定案 |To put the final touches on something; to complete the last part of a plan/project/deal, etc. |例句：They finalized the deal yesterday. 他们昨天完成了交易。

例句：We need to finalize the arrangements for the wedding. 我们需要最终确定婚礼的安排。|近义词：complete, conclude, finish, settle |反义词: begin, start, commence |词性:动词
