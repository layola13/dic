anhistous:/ænˈhɪstəs/
adjective
没有历史的；无历史记载的
Lacking a history or historical record.  Not recorded in history; historically unrecorded or undocumented.  Relating to something that has no documented history.
The small, anhistous tribe had no written records and relied solely on oral traditions. 这个小而没有历史记载的部落没有书面记录，完全依赖口头传统。

Synonyms: undocumented, unrecorded, historyless
Antonyms: historical, documented, recorded
词性: adjective
