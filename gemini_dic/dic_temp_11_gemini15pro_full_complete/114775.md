maltier:/ˈmɔːltiər/ | adj. | 更麦芽味的，麦芽味更浓的 | Having a stronger flavor of malt.  More resembling malt in flavor, aroma, or color. | This beer is maltier than the one I had last week.  这啤酒比我上周喝的麦芽味更浓。| The new recipe uses a maltier type of barley. 新配方使用了一种麦芽味更浓的大麦。 |近义词: richer, more flavorful |反义词: less malty, blander |词性: 形容词
