betters:/ˈbetərz/| n.| 较优者，较佳者；上司；前辈|Someone or something that is superior to another.  "This new model betters its predecessor in several ways."  这个新模型在几个方面都优于它的前一代。|近义词：superiors, improves upon, surpasses|反义词: inferiors,worsens|词性:名词

betters:/ˈbetərz/| v.|胜过；改善；提高（better 的第三人称单数形式）|To improve or surpass something or someone. "He betters his performance every year." 他每年都提高自己的表现。|近义词：improves, enhances, surpasses|反义词: worsens, diminishes|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *betteren*, from Old English *betera*, comparative of *gōd* (“good”). Cognate with Dutch *beter*, German *besser*, Gothic *batiza*.
