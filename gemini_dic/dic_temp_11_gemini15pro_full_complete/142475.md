osiered:/ˈoʊʒərd/ | adj. | 柳条制的；用柳条编成的 | Made of or lined with osiers (willow twigs). | The boat was moored alongside an osiered bank. 小船停泊在一个柳条编织的河岸边。 | The family lived in a small, osiered cottage. 这家人住在一个用柳条编织的小屋里。 | 近义词: wicker, woven | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "solid" or "unwoven" depending on context) | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old English *oserian, from os "osier" + -erian (suffix forming verbs).  The word "osier" itself comes from Proto-Germanic *ozwijō, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wey- (to bend, plait).
