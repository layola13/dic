maxims:/ˈmæksɪmz/
n.
格言；箴言；座右铭
A short, pithy statement expressing a general truth or rule of conduct.
His maxims about life are quite inspiring. 他的人生格言非常鼓舞人心。
synonyms: adage, proverb, saying, aphorism, precept
antonyms: nonsense, fallacy
