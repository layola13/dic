outjuggle: /ˌaʊtˈdʒʌɡəl/
verb
胜过；以更熟练的技巧胜过
to surpass someone in juggling, especially metaphorically, by displaying greater skill or dexterity in manipulating a situation.
He outjuggled his opponents in the negotiations and secured a favorable deal. 他在谈判中胜过对手，达成了有利的协议。
outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
--- (no clear antonym)
词性: 动词
