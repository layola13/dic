jemidar:/ˈdʒɛmɪdɑːr/
noun
印度次级军官，下士
A native officer in the Indian army, holding a rank equivalent to that of a lieutenant.
The jemidar led his men bravely into battle.  这位下士勇敢地带领他的士兵投入战斗。

The jemidar gave the order to advance.  下士下令前进。

近义词:lieutenant, subedar
反义词:(No direct antonym. Consider using terms like "private," "recruit," or "enlisted man" to represent lower ranks.)
词性:n.
