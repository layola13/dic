kelps:/kelps/ | n. | 海带；大型褐藻 | Large brown seaweeds (order Laminariales) that live in cold coastal waters. |
Kelps form dense underwater forests that provide habitat for a variety of marine life. 海带形成了茂密的海底森林，为各种海洋生物提供了栖息地。

Kelps are harvested for various uses, including food and fertilizer. 海带被收获用于各种用途，包括食物和肥料。

| 近义词: seaweed, algae | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: noun |

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *kelp*, from Old Norse *kjǫlp* (“bulge, lump”), from Proto-Germanic *kilpą (“protuberance, something round and prominent”).
