anti-inflationary:/ˌænti-ɪnˈfleɪʃəneri/
adjective
反通货膨胀的，抑制通货膨胀的
Tending to curb or reduce inflation.
The government implemented anti-inflationary policies to stabilize the economy. 政府实施了反通货膨胀政策以稳定经济。
The central bank raised interest rates as an anti-inflationary measure.  中央银行提高利率作为一项反通货膨胀措施。

近义词:deflationary, disinflationary
反义词:inflationary
词性:adjective


词源:
anti- (prefix) : against, opposed to  来自希腊语 anti-，意为“反对，相反”。
inflationary (adjective): relating to or causing inflation. 来自名词 inflation 加后缀 -ary 构成。
inflation:  a general increase in prices and fall in the purchasing value of money. 来自拉丁语 inflatio，意为“膨胀，肿胀”。
