cyphered:/ˈsaɪfərd/ | v. (past tense) | 加密，用密码写 |  To convert (a message) into cipher; encode. |  He cyphered the important document before sending it.  他在发送重要文件之前将其加密。 | encrypt, code, encode | decipher, decode | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *cifre*, from Arabic *sifr*, meaning "zero," later used for any numeral, and then for codes generally.  The word evolved to "cypher" in English, and "cyphered" is its past participle form used as an adjective.
