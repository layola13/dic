baboonery:/ˌbæˈbuːnəri/ |n.|愚蠢的行为；胡闹；粗鲁的行为|Extreme silliness or foolishness; boisterous or rude behavior.  It often implies mischievousness and a lack of civilized manners, like the perceived behavior of baboons.|The fraternity's baboonery resulted in several noise complaints from the neighbors.  兄弟会的胡闹行为招致邻居的几起噪音投诉。|Synonyms:  folly, tomfoolery, antics, shenanigans, buffoonery|Antonyms:  seriousness, solemnity, decorum, propriety|Noun
