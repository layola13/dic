critically:/ˈkrɪtɪkli/| adv.|批判地；危急地；严重地|In a way that expresses disapproval or finds fault; in a way that expresses judgment on the merits and faults of something; in a serious or dangerous condition.|She looked at him critically. 她批判性地看了他一眼。| The patient is critically ill. 病人病危。|近义词：analytically, seriously, gravely, disapprovingly|反义词：uncritically, approvingly, positively|词性:副词

词源: From Late Latin *criticus, from Ancient Greek κριτικός (kritikós, “able to discern, judge”), from κρίνω (krínō, “I separate, distinguish, judge”).
