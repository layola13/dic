arrastre:/əˈræstri/ | n. | 拖运；拖运费；（尤指矿石的）地面运输 | The act of hauling or dragging, especially ore, on the ground.  Specifically, a method of moving ore cars, usually by means of an endless chain or cable. Also, the charge for such hauling. | The arrastre moved the ore down the mountain.  矿石通过地面运输运下山。| 近义词：haulage, conveyance, transport | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

arrastre:/əˈræstri/ | n. | （尤指西班牙语国家中）牛马拖曳的打谷机 | A crude thrashing floor where grain is trodden out by mules or horses, especially in Spanish-speaking countries. | The farmers used an arrastre to thresh the wheat. 农民们用牛马拖曳的打谷机打麦子。| 近义词：thresher, threshing floor | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *arrastre*, from *arrastrar* "to drag," from Vulgar Latin *arrastrare*, from Latin *trahere* "to pull, draw."
