lorn:/lɔːrn/| adj.|孤独的，凄凉的，被遗弃的|Feeling abandoned and lonely.  Characterized by sadness and isolation.|The lorn figure on the hilltop evoked a sense of melancholy.  山顶上孤独的身影唤起了一种忧郁的感觉。|Synonyms: desolate, forlorn, forsaken, lonely, abandoned|Antonyms: cheerful, happy, accompanied, social, comforted|adjective
