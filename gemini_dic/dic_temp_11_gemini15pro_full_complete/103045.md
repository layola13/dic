jellyfish: /ˈdʒɛliˌfɪʃ/ (US), /ˈdʒɛliˌfɪʃ/ (UK)| n. |水母|A free-swimming marine animal with a gelatinous bell-shaped body and tentacles that can inject venom.|  I saw a jellyfish while swimming in the ocean. 我在海里游泳时看到了一只水母。|Synonyms: medusa, sea jelly|Antonyms: None|Noun

jellyfish: /ˈdʒɛliˌfɪʃ/ (US), /ˈdʒɛliˌfɪʃ/ (UK)| adj. (informal, rare) |软弱的，优柔寡断的|Lacking firmness or backbone; indecisive. | He acted jellyfish when confronted by the bully.  当面对恶霸时，他表现得软弱无能。|Synonyms: spineless, weak, indecisive|Antonyms: resolute, decisive, strong|Adjective
