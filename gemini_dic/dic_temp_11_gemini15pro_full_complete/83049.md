gurgled:/ˈɡɜːrɡld/ (US) /ˈɡɜːɡld/ (UK)| v.|汩汩地流; 发出汩汩声|A sound like water flowing quickly through a narrow space, or the sound someone makes when liquid is blocking their throat.|The baby gurgled happily after being fed.  婴儿喂饱后高兴地咯咯叫。|Synonyms: bubbled, rippled, flowed|Antonyms: trickled, dripped, stagnated|verb

gurgling:/ˈɡɜːrɡlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɡɜːrɡlɪŋ/ (UK)| n. | 汩汩声; 咯咯声|The sound of gurgling.|The gurgling of the stream was soothing. 小溪的潺潺流水声令人舒缓。|Synonyms: bubbling, rippling|Antonyms: dripping, splashing|noun
