frangipani:/ˌfrændʒɪˈpɑːni/ /ˌfrændʒɪˈpæni/| n.|鸡蛋花，缅栀子|A tropical American tree or shrub with clusters of fragrant white, yellow, or pink flowers.  It is widely cultivated as an ornamental in warm regions.|例句：The scent of frangipani filled the air. 空气中弥漫着鸡蛋花的香味。

例句：She wore a frangipani blossom in her hair. 她头发上戴着一朵鸡蛋花。|近义词：plumeria|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
