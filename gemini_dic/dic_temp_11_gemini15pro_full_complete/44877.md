cryal: /ˈkraɪəl/
adjective
冰的；冰状的；冰冻的
Relating to or consisting of ice; icy; frozen.
The cryal cave shimmered with blue light.  冰洞闪烁着蓝光。
The cryal landscape stretched for miles. 冰冻的景观绵延数英里。
近义词: icy, glacial, frozen
反义词: warm, thawed, molten
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek "kryos" meaning "icy cold," "frost," or "ice."
