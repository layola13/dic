nightlessly:/ˈnaɪtləsli/
adv.
无眠地，不间断地
Without nighttime or sleep; continuously.
She worked nightlessly to finish the project. 她不眠不休地工作以完成这个项目。
He coughed nightlessly throughout the night. 他整夜不停地咳嗽。
近义词:incessantly, continuously, constantly
反义词:intermittently, occasionally, sporadically
词性:副词

词源:
From "night" + "-less" (suffix indicating absence) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Night" comes from Old English "niht," from Proto-Germanic "*nahts," from Proto-Indo-European "*nokts-."  "-less" comes from Old English "-lēas." "-ly" comes from Old English "-lice."
