conduit:/ˈkɒndʊɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnduɪt/ (US)| n.|管道，导管；渠道，引水道；(尤指)水管，沟渠；传递途径|A channel for conveying water or other fluids.|The electrician ran the wires through a metal conduit. 电工把电线穿过金属管道。|Synonyms: channel, pipe, tube, duct, passage|Antonyms: barrier, obstruction|noun

conduit:/ˈkɒndʊɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnduɪt/ (US)| n.|媒介，手段；渠道|A means by which something is transmitted.|The newspaper served as a conduit for political propaganda. 这家报纸成了政治宣传的工具。|Synonyms: medium, channel, vehicle, instrument|Antonyms: block, hindrance|noun
