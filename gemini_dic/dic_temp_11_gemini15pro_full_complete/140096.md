off-key:/ˌɒfˈkiː/ (UK), /ˌɔːfˈkiː/ (US)| adj.|跑调的，不和谐的，不合时宜的|Not in tune; not in harmony musically. Also used figuratively to describe something inappropriate or discordant.|Her singing was a little off-key this evening. 她今晚的歌声有点跑调。|Synonyms: out of tune, discordant, inappropriate, unsuitable, jarring|Antonyms: in tune, harmonious, appropriate, suitable, melodious|adjective

off-key:/ˌɒfˈkiː/ (UK), /ˌɔːfˈkiː/ (US)| adv.|跑调地，不和谐地|In a manner that is not in tune or harmony.|He sang the national anthem rather off-key. 他唱国歌有点跑调。|Synonyms: out of tune, discordantly|Antonyms: in tune, harmoniously|adverb
