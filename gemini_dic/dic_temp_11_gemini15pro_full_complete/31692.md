chautauqua: /ˌʃɔːˈtɔːkwə/ | adj. | 萧陶夸式的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a chautauqua. | The chautauqua spirit of intellectual curiosity permeated the town. 求知的萧陶夸精神弥漫着整个城镇。| synonym: educational, enlightening, cultural | antonym: ignorant, unenlightened | adjective
