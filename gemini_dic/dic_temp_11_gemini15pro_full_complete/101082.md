inventory:/ˈɪnvənˌtɔːri/ (US) /ˈɪnvənˌtri/ (UK)| n. |存货，库存，清单|A complete list of items such as property, goods in stock, or the contents of a building.|The store took an inventory of all its merchandise.  这家商店清点了所有商品。|近义词：stock, supply, store|反义词: shortage, deficit|noun

inventory:/ˈɪnvənˌtɔːri/ (US) /ˈɪnvənˌtri/ (UK)| vt. |编制清单，盘点|To make a complete list of.|They inventoried the museum's collection of antique toys. 他们为博物馆的古董玩具收藏编制了清单。|近义词：catalog, list, register|反义词: - |verb
