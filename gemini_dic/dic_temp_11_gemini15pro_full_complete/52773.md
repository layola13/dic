dilators:/ˈdaɪˌleɪtərz/ | n. |扩张器，扩张肌|A medical instrument used to stretch or enlarge an opening or passage in the body.|The surgeon used dilators to open the narrowed artery.  外科医生使用扩张器来扩张狭窄的动脉。|Synonyms: expanders, stretchers|Antonyms: constrictors, compressors|noun


dilators:/daɪˈleɪtəz/ | n. |（肌肉）扩张肌|Muscles that cause dilation, such as the iris dilator muscle.|The iris dilators control the size of the pupil. 虹膜扩张肌控制瞳孔的大小。|Synonyms: expanding muscles|Antonyms: constricting muscles|noun
