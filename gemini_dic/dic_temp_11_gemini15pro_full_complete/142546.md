ostensible:/ɒˈstensɪbl/ /ɑːˈstensəbl/| adj.|表面的，假装的| Appearing or claiming to be one thing when it is really something else.| He gave an ostensible reason for his absence, but we all knew he was lying. 他给出了一个表面上的缺席理由，但我们都知道他在撒谎。|  She had no ostensible motive for the crime. 她没有明显的犯罪动机。| synonyms: apparent, seeming, outward, professed | antonyms: real, genuine, actual, true | word type: adjective
