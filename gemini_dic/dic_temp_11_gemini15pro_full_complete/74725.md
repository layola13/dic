freer:/ˈfriːər/| adj.|更自由的, 更无拘无束的|comparative form of free, meaning less restricted or constrained.|Birds feel freer in the open sky. 鸟儿在开阔的天空中感觉更自由。|近义词：more liberated, less restricted|反义词:more restricted, less liberated|词性:形容词

freer:/ˈfriːər/| n.|释放者，解放者 (古语, 罕用)|One who frees or liberates, especially from slavery or captivity.  This usage is archaic.|The freer opened the cages and let the birds fly. 解放者打开笼子，让鸟儿飞翔。|近义词：liberator, emancipator|反义词:captor, enslaver|词性:名词
