backlash:/ˈbæklæʃ/| n. vi.|强烈反应；反冲；后冲；强烈反对|A strong negative reaction by a large number of people, especially to a social or political change.| The government is facing a backlash over its new education policy. 政府正面临对其新教育政策的强烈反对。|synonyms: reaction, resistance, opposition, counteraction, recoil|antonyms: support, approval|noun

backlash:/ˈbæklæʃ/| vi.|发生强烈反应；产生反冲|To react strongly and negatively.| The proposed changes backlashed on the government. 提议的改革引起了对政府的强烈反对。|synonyms: react, recoil, rebound|antonyms: support, approve|verb
