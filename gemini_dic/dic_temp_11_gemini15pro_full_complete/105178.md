key-in-on:/kiː ɪn ɑːn (American) / /kiː ɪn ɒn (British)| phrasal verb | 集中注意力于；对准；关注 | To focus one's attention on something or someone; to zero in on. | The detective keyed in on the suspect's nervous behavior.  侦探注意到嫌疑人紧张的行为。| 近义词：focus on, zero in on, hone in on, concentrate on | 反义词：ignore, neglect | 词性：动词短语

key-in-on:/kiː ɪn ɑːn (American) / /kiː ɪn ɒn (British)| phrasal verb | 输入；键入 | To enter data into a computer or other electronic device. | She keyed in the password to access her account. 她输入密码以访问她的帐户。| 近义词：input, enter, type | 反义词：output, retrieve | 词性：动词短语
