ashram:/ˈɑːʃrɑːm/ /ˈæʃrɑːm/
n.
修行处，静修院
(especially in South Asia) a secluded building or community devoted to religious instruction or meditation.
He spent several months meditating at an ashram in India. 他在印度的一个修行院里冥想了几个月。
hermitage, monastery, retreat, sanctuary
palace, city
noun
