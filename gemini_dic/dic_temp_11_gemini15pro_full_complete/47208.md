daylong:/ˈdeɪlɒŋ/ /ˈdeɪlɔːŋ/
adjective
持续一整天的；全天的
Lasting for a whole day.
We went on a daylong hike in the mountains. 我们在山上进行了一整天的徒步旅行。
The conference was a daylong event.  这次会议是一整天的活动。
近义词:all-day
反义词:short, brief
词性:adjective
