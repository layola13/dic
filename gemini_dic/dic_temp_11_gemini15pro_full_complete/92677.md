ice-lolly:/ˈaɪs ˌlɒli/ (UK) /ˈaɪs ˌlɑːli/ (US)| n.|冰棍，冰棒|A frozen confection made of flavored water or fruit juice, usually on a stick.|例句：The children enjoyed their ice-lollies on a hot summer's day. 孩子们在大热天里享用了他们的冰棍。

例句：I bought an ice-lolly from the ice cream van. 我从冰淇淋车里买了一根冰棒。

近义词：popsicle (US), ice pop (US), freezer pop (US)|反义词:|词性:noun
