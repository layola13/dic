interlocution:/ˌɪntərloʊˈkjuːʃən/ |n.|对话，交谈，谈话|An instance of speaking or conversing with someone; a conversation or dialogue. |"The interlocution between the two leaders was tense but productive." 两位领导人之间的对话气氛紧张但富有成效。|Synonyms: conversation, dialogue, discourse, exchange, colloquy|Antonyms: silence, monologue|noun

interlocutory:/ˌɪntərˈlɑːkjətɔːri/ |adj.|对话的，中间的，非最终的|Of, relating to, or consisting of dialogue;  in law, not finally decisive.|"The judge issued an interlocutory injunction to prevent the company from selling the property until the lawsuit was resolved." 法官发布了中间禁令，阻止该公司在诉讼解决之前出售该房产。|Synonyms: conversational, interim, provisional|Antonyms: final, conclusive, definitive|adjective
