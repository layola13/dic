interpervasiveness:/ˌɪntərˌpɜːrˈveɪsɪvnəs/
|n.|普遍性；渗透性；弥漫性|The quality of being present and pervasive throughout. The interpervasiveness of technology in modern life is undeniable.|The interpervasiveness of mobile technology has transformed the way we communicate. 移动技术的普遍性已经改变了我们沟通的方式。|ubiquity, pervasiveness, prevalence, omnipresence|rarity, scarcity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between, among, together) + pervade (to spread or be present throughout) + -ness (forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Pervade comes from Latin pervadere (to go through, pervade), from per- (through) + vadere (to go).
