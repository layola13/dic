interlaminated:/ˌɪntərˈlæmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
相互层压的，交层粘合的
Composed of layers of different materials bonded together.
The desk is made of interlaminated wood and metal. 这张桌子由交叠粘合的木材和金属制成。

The material is strong and lightweight because it is interlaminated. 这种材料既结实又轻便，因为它采用了交叠粘合的结构。

近义词: layered, laminated, stratified
反义词: homogeneous, uniform
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From inter- + laminated, past participle of laminate.  "Inter-" signifies "between" or "among," and "laminate" means to create by layering.
