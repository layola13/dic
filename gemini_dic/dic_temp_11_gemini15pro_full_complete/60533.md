elaiodic:/ˌɛlaɪˈɒdɪk/
adjective
油的；脂肪酸的
Containing or relating to oleic acid, a fatty acid found in olive oil and other vegetable and animal fats.
Elaiodic acid is a trans isomer of oleic acid.  油酸是油酸的反式异构体。
Olive oil is rich in elaiodic acid precursors. 橄榄油富含油酸前体。
近义词:oleic, fatty
反义词:lean, non-fatty
词性:adjective
