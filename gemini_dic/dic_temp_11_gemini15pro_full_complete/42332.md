counter-tide: /ˈkaʊntərˌtaɪd/ | n. |逆潮; 反潮流; 对抗性趋势| A tide that flows in the opposite direction to the main current.  A contrary tendency or movement.| The counter-tide of public opinion began to turn against the government's policies. 公众舆论的逆潮开始转向反对政府的政策。| synonyms: backlash, reaction, resistance | antonyms: trend, current, vogue | noun


counter-tide: /ˈkaʊntərˌtaɪd/ | adj. |逆潮的; 反潮流的| Going against the prevailing trend. |  His counter-tide views on education made him unpopular with the school board. 他反潮流的教育观点使他在学校董事会不受欢迎。| synonyms: opposing, contrary, dissenting | antonyms: supporting, conforming, mainstream | adjective
