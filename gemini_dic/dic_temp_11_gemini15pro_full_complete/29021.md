cartography:/kɑːrˈtɒɡrəfi/ (US) /kɑːrˈtɑːɡrəfi/ (UK)| n. |制图学，地图绘制|The science or art of making maps.  It involves the representation of geographic features on a two-dimensional surface.|例句：Modern cartography relies heavily on computer software. 现代制图学非常依赖计算机软件。|近义词：mapmaking, charting|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *cartographie*, from Greek *khártēs* (map) + *-graphia* (writing).
