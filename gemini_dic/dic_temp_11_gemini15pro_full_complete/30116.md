cedary:/ˈsiːdəri/| adj.|雪松的；雪松似的；有雪松香味的|Of or relating to cedar trees; having the fragrance of cedar.|The air was cedary and fresh. 空气中弥漫着雪松的清新香味。|近义词：cedar-scented, cedar-like|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider words like "piney" or "floral" if contrasting scents) |词性: adjective

词源: From cedar + -y. Ultimately from the Latin cedrus.
