ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dasen*, from Old Norse *dasa* (“to become exhausted, be dazed”), from Proto-Germanic *dasaną* (“to be tired, stunned”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰes₂-* (“to disappear, perish”).  The meaning shifted from "to daze, stun" to "to impress greatly" in the 16th century.
