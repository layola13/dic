ambulance: /ˈæmbjələns/ (US) /ˈæmbjʊləns/ (UK)| n. |救护车|A vehicle equipped to transport injured or sick people to a hospital.|The ambulance arrived quickly after the accident. 救护车在事故发生后迅速赶到。|An ambulance rushed the injured man to the hospital. 救护车把受伤的男子迅速送往医院。|近义词：emergency vehicle, paramedic unit|反义词: None|词性: Noun|词源: From French *ambulance* (“mobile hospital”), from *ambulant* (“walking; movable”), from Latin *ambulāns*, present participle of *ambulāre* (“to walk”).
