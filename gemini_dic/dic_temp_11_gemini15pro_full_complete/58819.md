duotype:/ˈdjuːəˌtaɪp/ | n. |双色版；双色印刷品|A process for making a two-color print from a single negative, or a print made by this process. | Example: This old photograph is a beautiful duotype, with sepia and blue tones. 这张老照片是一张漂亮的双色照片，带有棕褐色和蓝色调。

Example: Duotypes were popular in the early 20th century for their artistic effect. 双色印刷在20世纪初因其艺术效果而流行。| 近义词: two-color print | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) monochrome print | 词性: noun
