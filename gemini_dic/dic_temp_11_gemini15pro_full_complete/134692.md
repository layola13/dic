nonmasculine:/ˌnɒnˈmæskjʊlɪn/
adjective
非男性的；非阳性的
Not having qualities traditionally associated with men; feminine or neuter.
例句：They offer a range of nonmasculine clothing options.  他们提供一系列非男性化的服装选择。

例句：The protagonist rejects traditional nonmasculine roles. 主人公拒绝传统的非男性角色。

近义词：feminine, effeminate, unmanly
反义词：masculine
词性：adjective
