folklores:/ˈfoʊklɔːrz/ (US) /ˈfəʊklɔːz/ (UK)| n. |民间传说，民俗|Traditional beliefs, customs, and stories of a community, passed through the generations.|Example:  The book is a collection of local folklores. 这本书是当地民间传说的合集。|Synonym:  traditions, myths, legends, lore|Antonym:  facts, reality|noun
