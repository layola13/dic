homotaxial:/ˌhoʊmoʊˈtæksiəl/| adj.|同轴的，顺层的|Having the same taxonomic relationship; of the same axes.  Relating to or exhibiting homotaxis.|The homotaxial deposits suggest the regions experienced similar geological events.|Synonyms: conformable, concordant|Antonyms: unconformable, discordant|adjective
