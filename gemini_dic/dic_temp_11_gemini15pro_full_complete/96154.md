indentured:/ɪnˈdɛntʃərd/| adj.|契约的；受契约约束的；|Bound by a contract, typically one for labor over a period of time, often in exchange for passage to a new country or release from debt.|Example:  Indentured servants worked for years to pay off their passage to America.  契约佣工工作多年来偿还他们到美国的旅费。

Example:  The indentured laborers had few rights and were subject to harsh treatment.  受契约约束的劳工几乎没有权利，并受到严酷的对待。|近义词：bonded, contracted, apprenticed|反义词：free, liberated|词性:形容词
