ati:/ˈeɪtiː/
noun
ATI (abbreviation)
ATI can stand for several things, depending on the context.  Commonly it refers to "Advanced Micro Devices' Technologies, Inc.", formerly ATI Technologies Inc., a major semiconductor company specializing in graphics processing units. It can also stand for "Assessment Technologies Institute," a company providing nursing assessments.
例句：My computer has an ATI graphics card. 我的电脑有一块ATI显卡。

例句：She is studying for the ATI TEAS exam. 她正在为ATI TEAS考试学习。

近义词：(depending on context) AMD, Nvidia (for graphics cards), HESI (for nursing assessments)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun (abbreviation)
