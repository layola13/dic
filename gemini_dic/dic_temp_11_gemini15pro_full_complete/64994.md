estrange:/ɪˈstreɪndʒ/ | vt. | 使疏远；使不和；离间 | To cause someone to be no longer close or friendly to someone else. |  His behavior estranged him from his family. 他的行为使他与家人疏远了。

The argument estranged the two friends. 这次争吵使这两个朋友疏远了。


近义词: alienate, separate, distance | 反义词: reconcile, reunite | 词性: 动词
