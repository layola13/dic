bridge-builder:/ˈbrɪdʒ ˌbɪldər/| n.|桥梁建造者；搭桥人；促成和解的人|A person or organization that builds bridges, either literally or figuratively (creating connections between people or groups).|例句：He is a renowned bridge-builder in the community, helping to resolve conflicts between different groups. 他是社区里著名的桥梁建造者，帮助解决不同群体之间的冲突。

例句：The organization acts as a bridge-builder between businesses and local communities.  该组织充当企业和当地社区之间的桥梁。|近义词：mediator, conciliator, negotiator, unifier|反义词:divider,  separatist|词性: Noun
