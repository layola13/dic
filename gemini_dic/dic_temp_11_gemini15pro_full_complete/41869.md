coscripted:/kəˈskrɪptɪd/
adjective
被征召的，被强制入伍的
forced to serve in the military
例句：Many young men were conscripted into the army during the war. 战争期间，许多年轻人被征召入伍。

例句：He felt conscripted into a role he didn't want. 他感觉自己被迫扮演了一个他不想要的角色。

近义词：drafted, enlisted, compelled, forced
反义词：volunteer, voluntary


词性: adjective

词源: From Latin conscribere "to enroll, enlist," from com- "together" + scribere "to write."  The original sense referred to enrolling or listing names, which evolved into the meaning of compulsory enrollment in military service.
