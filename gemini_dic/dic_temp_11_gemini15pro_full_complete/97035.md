infectivities:/ɪnˌfɛkˈtɪvɪtiz/
noun
传染性，感染力
The plural form of infectivity, referring to the ability of a pathogen to establish an infection.
Researchers are studying the infectivities of different strains of the virus. 研究人员正在研究不同病毒株的传染性。
infectivity, contagiousness, transmissibility
non-infectivity, resistance
n.
