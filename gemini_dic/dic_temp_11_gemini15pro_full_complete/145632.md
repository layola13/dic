overconstructs:/ˌoʊvərˈkənˌstrʌkts/ | v. | 过度解读，过度阐释 | To analyze or interpret something in an excessively complex or elaborate way, often reading in meanings that are not intended. |  She tends to overconstruct simple remarks, finding hidden meanings where none exist. 她倾向于过度解读简单的评论，在没有隐藏含义的地方寻找隐藏含义。

The critic overconstructs the poem, attributing symbolic significance to every line.  这位评论家过度解读了这首诗，赋予每一行象征意义。| 近义词：overanalyze, overinterpret, misread | 反义词：underinterpret, simplify | 词性: 动词
