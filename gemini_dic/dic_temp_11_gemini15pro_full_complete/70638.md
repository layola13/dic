filter:/ˈfɪltər/ (US) /ˈfɪltə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.| 过滤器，滤镜；过滤，筛选|A device or material that removes unwanted elements from a liquid or gas; to remove unwanted elements from a liquid or gas. |例句：The coffee filter separates the grounds from the brewed coffee. 咖啡滤纸将咖啡渣与煮好的咖啡分离。

例句：I filtered the water to remove impurities. 我过滤了水以去除杂质。|近义词：sieve, strainer, screen, purify, refine|反义词:pollute, contaminate|noun, verb

filter:/ˈfɪltər/ (US) /ˈfɪltə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 滤镜|A software feature that modifies an image or sound. |例句：She used a filter to make the photo look vintage. 她用滤镜让照片看起来复古。
