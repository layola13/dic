misnaming:/ˌmɪsˈneɪmɪŋ/
n.
错误命名；叫错名字
the act of giving something the wrong name
He apologized for the misnaming of the product. 他为产品的错误命名道歉了。

近义词: misnomer, mislabeling, incorrect naming
反义词: correct naming, accurate labeling

词性: noun

词源:  From mis- + naming, from name + -ing.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "wrong" or "bad." "Naming" comes from the verb "to name," which has roots in Old English "nama" and Proto-Germanic "*namō."
