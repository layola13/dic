chapeless:/ˈtʃæpəllɪs/
adjective
无礼拜堂的；没有小教堂的
Lacking a chapel.  Not having a private chapel (especially said of a large house or institution).
The chapeless manor felt strangely secular. 这座没有礼拜堂的庄园感觉异常世俗。
The newly built school was surprisingly chapeless.  新建的学校竟然没有礼拜堂，令人惊讶。
近义词: without a chapel
反义词: with a chapel
词性: adjective
