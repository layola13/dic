gabardine:/ˈɡæbərdiːn/ /ˈɡæbərˌdaɪn/
noun
华达呢；轧别丁；一种斜纹防水布料
A tightly woven, durable fabric with a slight twill, often used for raincoats and other outerwear.
例句：He wore a gabardine raincoat. 他穿着一件华达呢雨衣。

例句：The trousers were made of a hard-wearing gabardine.  这条裤子是用耐磨的华达呢做的。

近义词：twill, serge, drill
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "delicate fabric")
词性: n.
