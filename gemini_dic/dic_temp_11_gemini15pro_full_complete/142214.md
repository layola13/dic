ornithologist:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 鸟类学家 | A scientist who studies birds. |  例句：The ornithologist spent years studying the migration patterns of eagles.  这位鸟类学家花了数年时间研究老鹰的迁徙模式。

例句：She became an ornithologist because of her lifelong fascination with birds. 她因为对鸟类终生的迷恋而成为了一名鸟类学家。 | 近义词：birdwatcher, birder | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὄρνις (órnis, “bird”) +‎ -λόγος (-lógos, “one who speaks (about), student of”).
