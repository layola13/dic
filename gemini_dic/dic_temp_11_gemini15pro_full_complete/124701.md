ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *māwian, from Proto-Germanic *māwjana, from Proto-Indo-European *mēh₂-.  Cognate with German mähen (“to mow”), Dutch maaien (“to mow”).  The -er suffix denotes an agent or instrument that performs the action of the verb.
