motioner:/ˈmoʊʃənər/| n.|提议者；动议者|A person who makes a formal proposal or motion, especially in a meeting or legislative assembly.  A motioner suggests or proposes something for consideration or action.|The motioner argued passionately for the new policy.  提议者热情地为新政策辩护。|近义词：proposer, sponsor, mover|反义词:opponent, objector|词性:noun
词源: From the verb "motion" + "-er" (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "motio," meaning "a moving."
