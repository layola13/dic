elaborate:/ɪˈlæbəreɪt/ (v.) /ɪˈlæbərət/ (adj.)| v. 详细阐述； adj. 精心制作的；复杂的|v. To explain or describe something in detail. adj.  Intricate and rich in detail.|v.  Could you elaborate on that point? 你能详细说明那一点吗？ adj.  She wore an elaborate gown to the ball. 她穿着一件精致的礼服去参加舞会。|v.  expound, explain, detail adj. complex, intricate, detailed|v.  simplify, condense adj. simple, basic|verb, adjective


elaborate:/ɪˈlæbəreɪt/ (v.) /ɪˈlæbərət/ (adj.)| n.|详尽的细节；精心制作的东西 (This meaning is less common and usually derived from the verb form)|n. The architect presented his elaborate for the new building.  建筑师展示了他对新建筑的详尽细节。|n. detail, intricacy |n. summary, outline|noun (less common)
