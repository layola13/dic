emptier:/ˈemptiər (US), ˈemptiə(r) (UK)| adj.|更空的, 更空虚的| comparative form of empty: containing nothing or less than something else |The cookie jar was emptier than I expected.  饼干罐比我预料的要空。|近义词：more vacant, more barren, more desolate|反义词:fuller, more replete|词性:形容词

emptier:/ˈemptiər (US), ˈemptiə(r) (UK)| n.|清空者，使…空虚的人或物|One who or that which empties something.|The drought was the great emptier of the reservoir. 干旱是水库最大的清空者。|近义词：remover, clearer, drainer|反义词:filler, replenisher|词性:名词
