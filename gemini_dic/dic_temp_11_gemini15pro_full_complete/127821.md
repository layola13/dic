nematoblast:/ˈnɛmətəˌblæst/ /ˈniːmətəˌblɑːst/ | n. | 刺丝囊，刺细胞 | A specialized cell in the tentacles of certain coelenterates, such as jellyfish and sea anemones, containing a barbed or venomous thread that can be projected in self-defense or to capture prey. |  Jellyfish use nematoblasts to sting and paralyze their prey. 水母用刺丝囊刺伤并麻痹它们的猎物。|  The nematoblasts on the tentacles caused a painful burning sensation. 触须上的刺丝囊引起了一种痛苦的灼烧感。 | synonyms: cnidoblast, cnidocyte | antonyms: N/A | noun
