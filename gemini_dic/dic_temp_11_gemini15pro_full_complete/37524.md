commonable:/ˈkɒmənəbl/ /ˈkɑːmənəbl/
adjective
可共用的，可共享的，(土地)可共用的
Suitable for or allowing common use or sharing; held in common.
The village green is commonable land, available for all residents to enjoy.  村庄绿地是可共用的土地，所有居民都可以享用。
The park's benches are commonable, providing rest for anyone who needs it. 公园的长椅是可共用的，为任何需要的人提供休息的地方。
近义词:sharable, shareable, joint, public
反义词:private, exclusive


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *comounable*, from *comoune* (“common”) + *-able*. Equivalent to *common* + *-able*.
