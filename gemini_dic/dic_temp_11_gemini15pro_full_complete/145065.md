overamplifying:/ˌoʊvərˈæmplɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
过度放大; 过分夸大
To amplify something excessively, beyond what is accurate or appropriate.
The media is overamplifying the severity of the flu season. 媒体夸大了流感季节的严重性。
The politician was accused of overamplifying his achievements.  这位政治家被指责夸大了他的成就。
Synonyms: exaggerating, overstating, inflating, magnifying
Antonyms: downplaying, minimizing, understating
verb
