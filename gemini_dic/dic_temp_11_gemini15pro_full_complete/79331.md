glebe:/ɡliːb/| n.|耕地，田地；教堂所属土地|A glebe is a plot of land owned or formerly owned by a parish church.  教堂所属土地是指由教区教堂拥有或以前拥有的土地。|The vicarage stood on a small glebe overlooking the village.  牧师住宅坐落在俯瞰村庄的一小块教堂所属土地上。|synonyms: land, field, farm, ground, acreage|antonyms: city, urban area|noun
