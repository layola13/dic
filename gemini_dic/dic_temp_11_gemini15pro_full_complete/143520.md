outgreening:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnɪŋ/
verb
超越绿化; 在环保方面胜过
The act of surpassing someone or something in terms of environmental consciousness or action.
Costa Rica is outgreening many developed nations with its ambitious reforestation projects. 哥斯达黎加凭借其雄心勃勃的重新造林项目，在环保方面超越了许多发达国家。

The company is investing heavily in renewable energy to outgreen its competitors.  这家公司正在大力投资可再生能源，以在环保方面胜过其竞争对手。

近义词: outperforming, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: lagging behind, falling short
词性: v.
