overcloseness:/ˌoʊvərˈkloʊsnəs/| n.| 过度亲密，过分亲近|A state of excessive emotional or physical intimacy, often leading to discomfort or a feeling of being smothered.  It can occur in various relationships, including familial, romantic, and platonic. |Their overcloseness made him feel suffocated.  他们的过度亲密让他感到窒息。|The overcloseness of the family was stifling to the teenager. 这个家庭的过度亲密让这个青少年感到压抑。|closeness, intimacy, nearness|distance, detachment, aloofness|noun
