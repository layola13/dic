contortion:/kənˈtɔːrʃən/ /kənˈtɔːrʃn/
n.
扭曲，扭歪；弯曲
a twisted or bent condition; the act of twisting or deforming something out of its natural shape
The contortions of the dancer's body were mesmerizing.  舞者身体的扭曲令人着迷。

contortions:/kənˈtɔːrʃənz/ /kənˈtɔːrʃnz/
n. (plural)
（尤指面部的）怪相，扭曲
（especially of the face）a strange, twisted expression
His face was in contortions of pain. 他的脸因痛苦而扭曲。


近义词:twist, bend, deformation, warp, grimace, distortion
反义词:straightening, smoothness
词性:noun
