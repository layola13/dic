apposable:/əˈpəʊzəbl/ | adj. | 可相对的，可对生的 | Able to be placed opposite something else, especially a thumb that can be brought into contact with the other fingers. |  Humans have apposable thumbs, which allows them to grasp objects. 人类有可相对的拇指，这使得他们能够抓住物体。 |  opposable thumb allows us to manipulate tools. 可相对的拇指使我们能够操作工具。|  prehensile, opposable thumb | non-apposable | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *appōnibilis, from Latin appōnere (to put to, apply), from ad- (to) + pōnere (to put, place).
