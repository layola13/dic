latest:/ˈleɪtɪst/| adj.|最新的，最近的|The latest news from the front is encouraging.  来自前线的最新消息令人鼓舞。|例句：This is the latest fashion. 这是最新的时尚。|近义词：newest, recent, current|反义词：oldest, earliest|词性:形容词

*Note:* "lastest" is often misspelled.  The correct spelling is "latest."  It is the superlative form of "late" when referring to time.  There is no word "lastest" in standard English.
