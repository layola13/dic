basify:/ˈbeɪsɪfaɪ/| vt.|碱化；使…成碱性|To make (a solution or substance) more basic or alkaline by increasing the concentration of hydroxide ions.|Sodium hydroxide is often used to basify solutions. 氢氧化钠常用来碱化溶液。|The chemist basified the solution by adding ammonia. 化学家通过添加氨来碱化溶液。|alkalize, neutralize (partially)|acidify|verb
