debauchee:/ˌdɛbɔːˈʃiː/ /ˌdɛbɔːˈtʃiː/
n.
放荡者；酒色之徒
a person given to excessive indulgence in sensual pleasures.
He was known as a debauchee and a gambler. 他以放荡和赌博而闻名。

近义词:libertine, profligate, rake, roué, reprobate
反义词:ascetic, puritan
词性:noun
