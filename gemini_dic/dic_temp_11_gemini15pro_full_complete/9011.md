antiloquy: /ænˈtɪləkwi/ | n. | 对语；对白；(尤指)唱和诗 | A response, often in the form of a poem, to another poem or speech. |  The poet wrote an antiloquy to his friend's elegy. 这位诗人写了一首对唱诗来回应他朋友的挽歌。| synonym: response, reply, rejoinder | antonym: monologue, soliloquy | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *antiloquium, from anti- "against, opposite" + loqui "to speak."
