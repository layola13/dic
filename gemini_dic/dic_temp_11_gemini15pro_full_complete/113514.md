mad:/mæd/
| adj.|疯狂的，发疯的；狂热的；着迷的；非常恼火的|Driven to insanity; insane. Extremely enthusiastic; infatuated. Very annoyed; angry.|He was mad with grief. 他悲伤得发狂了。|Synonyms: insane, crazy, lunatic, demented, deranged, frantic, furious, enraged, infuriated|Antonyms: sane, rational, calm, composed|adjective


mad:/mæd/
| adj.|极好的，很棒的（非正式）|Excellent; wonderful (informal).|That's a mad outfit! 那套衣服真棒！|Synonyms: awesome, fantastic, brilliant, excellent|Antonyms: terrible, awful, dreadful|adjective
