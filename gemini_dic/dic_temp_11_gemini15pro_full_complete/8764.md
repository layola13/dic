antidote:/ˈæntidəʊt/ (UK) /ˈæntidoʊt/ (US)| n. |解毒剂；对抗手段|A medicine taken or given to counteract a particular poison.|
The hospital gave him an antidote to the snake venom. 医院给他服用了蛇毒的解毒剂。

The best antidote to a bad mood is a good laugh.  好心情的最佳良药是一次开怀大笑。


近义词:remedy, cure, countermeasure|
反义词:poison, toxin, venom|
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *antidotum, from Greek antidoton "given against," from anti "against" + didonai "to give."
