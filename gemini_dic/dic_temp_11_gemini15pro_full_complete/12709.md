assertor:/əˈsɜːrtər/ | n. |断言者，主张者|A person who asserts something; someone who makes a firm statement or claim. |例句：He was a strong assertor of his rights. 他坚决维护自己的权利。

例句：The assertor of this theory has yet to provide conclusive evidence.  这个理论的倡导者还没有提供确凿的证据。

近义词：proponent, advocate, claimant, maintainer |反义词：denier, opponent |词性:noun
