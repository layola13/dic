geordies:/ˈdʒɔːdiːz/
noun
乔迪人（纽卡斯尔及其周边地区的人）
People from Newcastle upon Tyne and the surrounding areas in Northeast England, often recognized for their distinctive accent and dialect.
例句：The Geordies are known for their friendly nature.  乔迪人以友善的性格而闻名。

例句：Many Geordies are passionate supporters of Newcastle United Football Club. 许多乔迪人都是纽卡斯尔联队的热情支持者。
近义词：Newcastle natives, northeasterners (more general)
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it refers to a specific group of people)
词性:n.
