nonconducivenesses:/ˌnɑːnkənduːsɪvnəsəz/
n.
不传导性；不利条件
The state or quality of not being conducive; unfavorableness.
The nonconducivenesses of the environment hindered the growth of the plants.  这种环境的不利条件阻碍了植物的生长。
non-conduciveness, unfavorableness
conduciveness, favorableness
