non-citizen:/ˌnɒnˈsɪtɪzən/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɪtɪzn/ | n. |非公民；外国人|A person who is not a citizen of a particular country. | Example:  Non-citizens are not allowed to vote in national elections. 非公民不允许在全国选举中投票。

Example: The non-citizen was denied entry into the country.  这位非公民被拒绝入境。| 近义词:foreigner, alien, immigrant|反义词:citizen, national|词性:noun
