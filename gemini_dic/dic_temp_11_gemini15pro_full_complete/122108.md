mistless:/ˈmɪstləs/| adj.|无雾的，晴朗的|Free from mist; clear.|The mistless morning promised a beautiful day. 晴朗的早晨预示着美好的一天。|近义词：clear, bright, sunny, cloudless|反义词:misty, foggy, hazy|词性:形容词
词源:mist + -less.  "mist" comes from Old English *mist, of Germanic origin. "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking," from Old English -lēas.
