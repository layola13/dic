mullet:/ˈmʌlɪt/ (US), /ˈmʌlɪt/ (UK)| n.|胭脂鱼；鲻鱼；乌鱼；(尤指男子留的)后短前长式发型|A hairstyle, typically for men, in which the hair is short at the front and sides, but long in the back.  A fish found in coastal waters, having a silvery body and esteemed as food.|He sported a mullet, popular in the 80s. 他留着80年代流行的鲻鱼头。|The fishermen caught a large mullet in their net. 渔民们用网捕到了一条大鲻鱼。|近义词：(hairstyle) shag, (fish) gray mullet|反义词: (No direct antonym for the fish or hairstyle)|词性:noun


mullet:/ˈmʌlɪt/ (US), /ˈmʌlɪt/ (UK)| n.|愚蠢的人；笨蛋 (Informal, dated)|A foolish or stupid person.|Don't be such a mullet! 别这么傻！|(This usage is less common and considered dated.)|近义词: idiot, fool, dolt|反义词: genius, smart person|词性:noun
