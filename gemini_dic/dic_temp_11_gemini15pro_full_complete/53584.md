disburden:/dɪsˈbɜːrdən/
verb
卸下负担，解除负担
to relieve (someone) of a burden or responsibility
The company was disburdened of its debt.  这家公司卸下了债务。
He disburdened himself of his worries by talking to a friend. 他向朋友倾诉，从而解除了自己的忧虑。
近义词: relieve, unburden, unload, discharge
反义词: burden, load, encumber
词性: verb

disburden:/dɪsˈbɜːrdən/
noun
卸下负担，解除负担（较少用作名词）
the act of disburdening
The disburden of his responsibilities felt like a great relief.  卸下他的责任让他感到如释重负。（名词用法相对少见）
近义词: relief, unloading
反义词: burdening, loading
词性: noun
