PHRASE->
bung something up: 堵塞某物 (e.g., bung up the toilet)
get bunged up: 被堵塞 (e.g., My nose gets bunged up when I have a cold.)
bung it in:  (informal) 放进去，塞进去 (e.g., Just bung it in the back of the car.)
bung something on: (informal, chiefly British)  匆忙穿上 (e.g., I bunged on a coat and rushed out.)
bung a quid in: (informal, chiefly British)  捐出一英镑 (e.g., Bung a quid in for the charity collection.)
