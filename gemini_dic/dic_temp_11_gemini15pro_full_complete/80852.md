gourd:/ɡɔːrd/ /ɡʊərd/| n. |葫芦；瓠瓜|A fruit with a hard shell and many seeds, some types of which are used as containers or for decoration. |例句：The farmer grew a giant gourd in his garden. 农民在他的花园里种了一个巨大的葫芦。

例句：She used dried gourds to make birdhouses. 她用晒干的葫芦做鸟窝。|近义词：squash, calabash|反义词: (无明确反义词)|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *gourde*, from Latin cucurbita, meaning "gourd."
