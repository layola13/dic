derisively:/dɪˈraɪsɪvli/
| adv. |嘲笑地，嘲弄地 |in a manner expressing contempt or ridicule. |He laughed derisively at her attempts to sing. 他嘲笑她唱歌的尝试。|近义词：mockingly, scornfully, contemptuously |反义词：respectfully, admiringly |词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *dērīsīvus, from Latin dērīdeō (“I mock, ridicule”), from dē (“down, away from”) + rīdeō (“I laugh”).
