fiacre: /ˈfiːækrə(r)/ (US) /ˈfɪækrə(r)/ (UK)| n. |出租马车；旧式出租汽车|A horse-drawn carriage for hire; an old-fashioned taxi.|例句：The fiacre clip-clopped down the cobbled street. 出租马车在鹅卵石街道上发出嘚嘚的响声。

例句：She hailed a fiacre to take her to the opera house. 她叫了一辆出租马车去歌剧院。|近义词：cab, taxi, carriage, hackney|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From French *fiacre*, from the Hôtel Saint Fiacre in Paris, where the first carriages for hire were available. The hotel was named after Saint Fiacre, the patron saint of gardeners.
