carnivore: /ˈkɑːrnɪvɔːr/ (US) /ˈkɑːnɪvɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.| 食肉动物; 食肉植物 |An animal or plant that eats meat. |Lions are carnivores. 狮子是食肉动物。|Synonyms: predator, meat-eater|Antonyms: herbivore, omnivore|noun

carnivorous: /kɑːrˈnɪvərəs/ (US) /kɑːˈnɪvərəs/ (UK)| adj.| 食肉的 |(of an animal) feeding on other animals.|(of a plant) able to trap and digest small animals, especially insects. |Venus flytraps are carnivorous plants. 捕蝇草是食肉植物。|Synonyms: predatory, meat-eating|Antonyms: herbivorous, omnivorous|adjective
