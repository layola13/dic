aeroponic:/ˌeərəˈpɒnɪk/ /ˌɛrəˈpɑːnɪk/| adj.|气栽的，无土栽培的|Relating to or involving growing plants in an air or mist environment without soil or an aggregate medium.|例句：The aeroponic system allows for faster growth and higher yields.  气栽系统可以使植物生长更快，产量更高。

例句：Aeroponic gardening is becoming increasingly popular in urban areas. 气栽园艺在城市地区越来越受欢迎。|近义词：hydroponic, soilless|反义词：geoponic, soil-based|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (air) + -ponic (from Greek ponos "labor," related to peine "hunger, toil," thus literally "working in the air").
