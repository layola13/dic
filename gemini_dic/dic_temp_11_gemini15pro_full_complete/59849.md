ectopy:/ˈɛktəpi/| n.|异位，异常位置|An abnormal position or displacement of an organ or body part.|例句：Cardiac ectopy can cause irregular heartbeats. 心脏异位会导致心律不齐。

例句：The doctor discovered an ectopy of the thyroid gland. 医生发现了甲状腺异位。|近义词：displacement, malposition|反义词: orthotopy|词性:noun
