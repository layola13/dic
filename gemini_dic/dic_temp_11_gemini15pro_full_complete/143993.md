outrates:/ˌaʊtˈreɪts/
verb
超过，胜过
To surpass in rating or ranking; to be superior to.
Tesla's Model S outrates many other electric vehicles in terms of range. 特斯拉的Model S在续航里程方面超过了许多其他电动汽车。
The new restaurant quickly outrated its competitors in online reviews. 这家新餐馆的在线评论很快就超过了竞争对手。
近义词: surpasses, exceeds, outperforms, overtakes
反义词: lags behind, trails, underperforms
词性: v.
