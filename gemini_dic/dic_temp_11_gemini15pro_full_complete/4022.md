ahold:/əˈhəʊld/ (US) /əˈhoʊld/| idiom|抓住，掌握；控制|To get ahold of something means to grasp or take hold of it physically or figuratively. It can also mean to gain control or influence over something or someone.|I finally got ahold of the rope and pulled myself up. 我终于抓住了绳子，把自己拉了上来。

The company is trying to get ahold of the market. 这家公司正试图控制市场。|近义词：grasp, grip, seize, obtain, control|反义词: release, lose, forfeit|词性: idiom
