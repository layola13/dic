friarly:/ˈfraɪərli/
adjective
修士般的；像修士的；适合修士的
Resembling or characteristic of a friar; suitable for a friar.
He had a friarly appearance, with his long robe and sandals. 他有着修士般的外表，穿着长袍和凉鞋。
The quiet, contemplative atmosphere felt distinctly friarly.  安静沉思的氛围让人感到非常像修士的。
近义词: monastic, clerical, religious
反义词: secular, worldly
词性: adjective

词源:
From friar + -ly.  Friar comes from Old French frere, from Latin frater "brother."
