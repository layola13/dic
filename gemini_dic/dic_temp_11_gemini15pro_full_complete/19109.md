betting:/ˈbetɪŋ/| n.|投注，打赌|The act of gambling money on an event or outcome.|He lost a lot of money on horse betting. 他在赛马赌博中损失了很多钱。|近义词：gambling, wagering|反义词:abstaining|noun


betting:/ˈbetɪŋ/| adj.|与打赌有关的|Relating to or used for betting.|The betting shop was crowded on Saturday.  周六投注站人满为患。|近义词：gambling|反义词:N/A|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "bet," which comes from the Old English "betan" meaning "to improve, amend, make better."  The sense of "to stake something on an outcome" developed later.
