consciences:/ˈkɒnʃənsɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnʃənsɪz/ (US)| n. |良心，良知|A person's moral sense of right and wrong, viewed as acting as a guide to one's behaviour.|She had a guilty conscience about lying to her parents. 她对向父母撒谎感到内疚。|Synonyms: morals, scruples, ethics|Antonyms: immorality, unscrupulousness|Noun
