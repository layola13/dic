edit:/ˈɛdɪt/ (US), /ˈɛdɪt/ (UK)| v. n.|编辑，校订；编辑版本|To prepare written material for publication by correcting, revising, or adapting.|She edits manuscripts for a publishing house. 她为一家出版社编辑稿件。|synonyms: revise, correct, amend, alter, modify, adapt, adjust, change, improve, refine, polish, prepare, process, redact|antonyms:  distort, misrepresent, falsify|verb

edit:/ˈɛdɪt/ (US), /ˈɛdɪt/ (UK)| n.|编辑版本；剪辑|A version of something, such as a film or text, that has been edited.|The director's cut of the film is a much longer edit. 导演剪辑版的电影要长得多。|synonyms: version, edition, revision, redaction|antonyms: original, draft|noun
