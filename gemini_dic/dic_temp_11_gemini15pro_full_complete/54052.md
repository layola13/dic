disdainfully:/dɪsˈdeɪnfəli/
adverb
轻蔑地，不屑地
showing contempt or lack of respect
She disdainfully rejected his offer of help. 她轻蔑地拒绝了他的帮助。
近义词：contemptuously, scornfully, haughtily
反义词：respectfully, admiringly
词性：副词
词源：From disdain + -ful + -ly. Disdain comes from Old French desdeigner, from des- "away from" + deigner "to deign."
