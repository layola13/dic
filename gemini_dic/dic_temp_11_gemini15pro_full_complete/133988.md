nonhistrionic:/ˌnɒnhɪstrɪˈɒnɪk/
adjective
不矫揉造作的；平静的；不戏剧化的
Not overly dramatic or emotional; calm and restrained.
例句：Her nonhistrionic response to the crisis calmed everyone down. 她对危机的平静反应让大家都冷静了下来。

例句：He delivered the bad news in a nonhistrionic manner. 他以一种平静的方式传达了这个坏消息。

近义词：calm, composed, restrained, unemotional, understated, low-key
反义词：histrionic, dramatic, theatrical, emotional, exaggerated, over-the-top
词性: adjective
