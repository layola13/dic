lignification:/ˌlɪɡnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.|木质化，木质素形成|The process by which plant cell walls become rigid and woody due to the deposition of lignin.|The lignification of the cell walls provides structural support to the plant.|近义词:wood formation, hardening|反义词:delignification|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Latin lignum ("wood") + -ficātiō, from the verb facere ("to make, do").
