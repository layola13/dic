anorthoscope:/ˈænɔːrθəˌskoʊp/
n.|变形镜，歪像镜|An optical instrument that distorts images, which then appear normal when viewed in a cylindrical mirror.|The anorthoscope created a bizarre, distorted image that resolved into a recognizable picture when spun.|Synonym: distorting mirror|Antonym: normal mirror|noun
