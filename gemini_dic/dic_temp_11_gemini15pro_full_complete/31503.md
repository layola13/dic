charnel: /ˈtʃɑːrnl/ | adj. n. | 阴森的，尸体的；停尸房，藏尸所 | relating to or suggestive of death and decay; a building or vault in which corpses or bones are piled |  The charnel house held the remains of plague victims.  停尸房里存放着瘟疫死者的遗体。 |  The air had a charnel smell. 空气中弥漫着尸体腐烂的气味。 | 近义词：ghastly, gruesome, macabre, sepulchral | 反义词: pleasant, agreeable | 词性: adjective, noun

词源：From Old North French *charnel, from Late Latin carnāle “charnel house,” neuter of carnālis “of the flesh, fleshy,” from Latin caro, carn- “flesh.”
