finback:/ˈfɪnbæk/| n. | 鳍背鲸，长须鲸 |A baleen whale (Balaenoptera physalus) having a dark gray back, a white belly, and a small dorsal fin located far back on the body. It is second in size only to the blue whale. | 例句：The finback whale is the second largest animal on Earth. 长须鲸是地球上第二大的动物。

例句：Finbacks are known for their long, slender bodies. 长须鲸以其修长苗条的身体而闻名。|近义词：rorqual, fin whale |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
