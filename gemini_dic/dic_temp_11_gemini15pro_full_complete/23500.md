brahmoism:/ˈbrɑːmoʊɪzəm/
n.
Brahmoism;  梵天主义
A monotheistic religion founded in India in the 19th century by Ram Mohan Roy, emphasizing social reform and the unity of God.
Raja Ram Mohan Roy is considered the father of Brahmoism. 拉贾·拉姆·莫汉·罗伊被认为是梵天主义之父。
Brahmo Samaj is the central organization of Brahmoism. 梵社是梵天主义的核心组织。
synonym:Brahmo Samaj
antonym:polytheism
noun
