lombardeer:/ˌlɒmbɑːˈdɪə(r)/ /ˌlɑːmbɑːrˈdɪr/| n.| 贷款人；典当商；放高利贷者|A person who lends money at high rates of interest, especially one who accepts personal goods as security.|He took his watch to the lombardeer as a last resort. 他把他的手表拿到当铺作为最后一搏。|Synonyms: pawnbroker, moneylender, usurer|Antonyms: borrower, debtor|noun
