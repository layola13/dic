bobo:/ˈboʊboʊ/| n. |纨绔子弟，富家子弟，上流社会人士 (通常指对政治漠不关心的)|A person, typically of the upper middle class, who is perceived as having liberal, politically correct views, especially on social issues, and as being out of touch with working-class people.|例句：The bobos in the city are always talking about organic food and sustainable living. 城市里的富家子弟总是在谈论有机食品和可持续生活。|例句：He's a typical bobo, driving a hybrid car and shopping at Whole Foods. 他是一个典型的富家子弟，开着混合动力汽车，在全食超市购物。|近义词：bourgeois, yuppie, hipster|反义词:proletarian, working class|词性:名词

bobo:/ˈboʊboʊ/| adj. |纨绔子弟的，富家子弟的，上流社会的|Characteristic of or relating to a bobo.|例句：The restaurant had a very bobo atmosphere. 这家餐厅有一种很上流社会的气氛。|例句：She gave him a bobo look. 她给了他一个纨绔子弟般的眼神。|近义词：bourgeois, yuppie, hipster|反义词:proletarian, working class|词性:形容词
