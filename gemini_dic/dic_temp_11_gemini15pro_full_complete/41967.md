cossack: /ˈkɒsæk/ (UK) /ˈkɑːsæk/ (US)| n.|哥萨克，哥萨克人|A member of a people of southern Russia and Ukraine, noted for their horsemanship and military skill.|例句：The Cossacks were famed for their fierce independence. 哥萨克人以其强烈的独立性而闻名。

例句：A group of Cossacks rode across the steppe. 一群哥萨克人骑马穿过大草原。


近义词：horseman, warrior|反义词: civilian|词性: noun
