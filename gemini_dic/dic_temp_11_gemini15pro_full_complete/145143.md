overassertion:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrʃən/ | n. | 过分自信; 过于断言;| The act of asserting something too strongly or too often.  It can be seen as aggressive or arrogant. |  His overassertion of his authority alienated his colleagues. 他过分强调自己的权威，疏远了他的同事。|  overconfidence, assertiveness, dogmatism |  humility, modesty, diffidence | noun
overassert:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrt/ | v. | 过于自信地断言; 过分主张; |To assert something too strongly or too often. | He overasserted his opinions in the meeting. 他在会议上过分坚持自己的意见。 | assert aggressively, overstate, exaggerate | understate, downplay, be modest | verb
