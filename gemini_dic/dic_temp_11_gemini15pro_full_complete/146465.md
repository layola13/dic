overfruitfully:/ˌoʊvərˈfruːtfəli/ | adv. | 过于丰盛地，过度富有成果地 |  Producing or yielding fruit in excessive abundance.  It implies an abundance that is perhaps unnecessary or even detrimental. | The orchard overfruitfully produced apples, resulting in many rotting on the ground.  果园苹果产量过高，导致许多苹果烂在地上。 | The vine overfruitfully produced grapes, making them smaller and less sweet than usual. 葡萄藤过度结果，使葡萄比平时更小更不甜。 | 近义词: excessively, abundantly, prolifically | 反义词: sparsely, meagerly | 词性: 副词
