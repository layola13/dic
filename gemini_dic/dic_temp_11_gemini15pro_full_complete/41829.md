corvage:/ˈkɔːrvɪdʒ/| n.|徭役；劳役；无偿劳动（尤指中世纪农民为领主提供的）|A feudal obligation, usually labor, owed by a tenant to a lord. |Example 1: The peasants were burdened by heavy corvage, working long hours in the lord's fields. 农民们背负着沉重的徭役，在领主的田地里长时间劳作。

Example 2: The corvage system was a significant aspect of the feudal economy. 徭役制度是封建经济的一个重要方面。|Synonyms: labor service, forced labor, servitude, villein service |Antonyms: freedom, liberty, paid work| noun
