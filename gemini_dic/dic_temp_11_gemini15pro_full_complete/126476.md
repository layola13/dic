mythologists:/mɪˈθɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |神话学家；神话研究者|A person who studies or is an expert in mythology. |例句：The mythologists have offered various interpretations of this ancient myth.  神话学家们对这个古老的神话提出了各种各样的解释。

例句：Many mythologists believe that myths reflect the values and beliefs of a culture. 许多神话学家认为，神话反映了一种文化的价值观和信仰。|近义词：mythographer, folklorist|反义词: N/A| Noun
