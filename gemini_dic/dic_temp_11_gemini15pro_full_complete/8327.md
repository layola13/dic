anti-personnel:/ˌænti pɜːsəˈnɛl/ | adj. | 反人员的，杀伤人员的 | Designed for use against enemy personnel, rather than against equipment or installations. |例句：Anti-personnel landmines are a major hazard in many war-torn countries.  反人员地雷在许多饱受战争蹂躏的国家是一个主要危险。

例句：The army used anti-personnel weapons in the attack.  军队在袭击中使用了杀伤人员的武器。| 近义词：anti-infantry | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

anti-personnel mine:/ˌænti pɜːsəˈnɛl maɪn/ | n. | 反人员地雷 | A type of landmine designed to be exploded by the presence, proximity, or contact of a person. | 例句：Anti-personnel mines indiscriminately kill and maim civilians. 反人员地雷不分青红皂白地杀害和致残平民。

例句：The use of anti-personnel mines is banned by international treaty.  国际条约禁止使用反人员地雷。| 近义词：landmine | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源:
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti, meaning "against" or "opposite".
personnel: from French personnel, meaning "personal".
