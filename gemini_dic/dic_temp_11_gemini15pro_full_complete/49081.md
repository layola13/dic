dehyphening:/diːˈhaɪfənɪŋ/
n.|去连字符化|The process of removing hyphens from a word or phrase. |Example: The dehyphenening of "e-mail" to "email" has become common.  “电子邮件”从“e-mail”到“email”的去连字符化已经变得很普遍。|Synonyms: unhyphenating, consolidation |Antonyms: hyphenating, joining|noun
