noncorporations:/ˌnɑːnkɔːrpəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |非法人团体，非公司组织| Entities that are not incorporated as corporations; they can include partnerships, sole proprietorships, and limited liability companies (LLCs). | Example: Many small businesses operate as noncorporations to avoid the complexities of corporate governance. 许多小企业以非法人形式运营，以避免公司治理的复杂性。

Example:  Noncorporations are often subject to different tax regulations than corporations. 非法人团体通常比公司遵守不同的税收规定。| 近义词: unincorporated businesses, unincorporated associations | 反义词: corporations | 词性: noun


词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + corporation (from Latin "corporatio," meaning "body, corporation")
