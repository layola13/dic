halos:/ˈheɪloʊz/ (US) /ˈheɪləʊz/ (UK)| n.|光环，晕轮，神像之光圈|A ring of light appearing to surround the sun or moon, or any luminous body.|例句：The sun appeared with a halo around it. 太阳周围出现了一个光环。|例句：The painting depicts an angel with a halo. 这幅画描绘了一个头顶光环的天使。|近义词：aura, nimbus, ring, corona|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
