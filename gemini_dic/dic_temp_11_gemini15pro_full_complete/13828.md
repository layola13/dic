auditorially:/ˌɔːdɪˈtɔːriəli/| adv.|听觉上地，通过听觉|Relating to the sense of hearing or perceived through hearing. |She processes information auditorially. 她通过听觉处理信息。|She learned the song auditorially, by listening to it repeatedly. 她通过反复听来学习这首歌，也就是通过听觉学习。|近义词：acoustically, by ear|反义词: visually|词性: 副词
