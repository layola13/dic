hastefully:/ˈheɪstfəli/ | adv. | 仓促地，急促地 | Marked by excessive speed and a lack of due consideration. | She hastefully packed her bags, forgetting several important items. 她仓促地收拾行李，忘记了几件重要的东西。 | speedily, swiftly, precipitately | slowly, thoughtfully | adverb
