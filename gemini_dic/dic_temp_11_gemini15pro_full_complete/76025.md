furial:/ˈfjʊəriəl/| adj.|狂怒的，暴怒的|Characterized by or expressing fury; raging.  Filled with or marked by extreme anger.|His furial outburst shocked everyone in the room. 他暴怒的爆发让房间里的每个人都感到震惊。|近义词：furious, enraged, wrathful, indignant, irate|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *furialis, from Latin furia (“fury, rage”) + -alis.
