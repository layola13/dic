interruptive:/ˌɪntəˈrʌptɪv/| adj.|中断的，干扰的|Causing or tending to cause interruption.|例句：Interruptive noises from the construction site made it hard to concentrate. 建筑工地的嘈杂声使人难以集中注意力。|例句：Her interruptive behavior during the meeting was very unprofessional.  她在会议期间打断别人的行为很不专业。|近义词：disruptive, intrusive, obstructive|反义词：continuous, smooth, uninterrupted|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *interruptīvus, from Latin interruptus (past participle of interrumpere ‘to interrupt’).
