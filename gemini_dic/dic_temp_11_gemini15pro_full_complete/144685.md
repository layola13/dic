outzany:/ˌaʊtˈzeɪni/
verb
超越；胜过；比...更滑稽
To surpass or outdo someone in zaniness or eccentricity.
He outzanied all the other comedians with his bizarre routine. 他以其古怪的表演超越了所有其他喜剧演员。
She tried to outzany him with an even more ridiculous costume. 她试图用一件更滑稽的服装胜过他。
近义词: outdo, surpass, eclipse
反义词:  be outdone, be surpassed
