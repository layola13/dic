aggrievedly:/əˈɡriːvdli/
adverb
委屈地，愤愤不平地
in a way that shows that you feel unfairly treated
例句：
"It's not fair," she said aggrievedly.  “这不公平，”她委屈地说。

He shook his head aggrievedly. 他愤愤不平地摇了摇头。

近义词：resentfully, indignantly, bitterly
反义词：cheerfully, happily, contentedly
词性：副词
