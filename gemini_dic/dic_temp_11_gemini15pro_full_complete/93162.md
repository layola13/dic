igniter:/ɪɡˈnaɪtər/ /ɪɡˈnɑɪtər/ | n. |点火器，点火装置|A device used to ignite a fuel mixture, such as in an engine or a gas appliance.|The igniter on my gas stove isn't working. 我的煤气灶上的点火器坏了。|Synonyms: lighter, starter, fire starter|Antonyms: extinguisher|noun

igniter:/ɪɡˈnaɪtər/ /ɪɡˈnɑɪtər/ | n. |导火线，点燃物|Something that initiates or fuels a process or event.|The controversial speech acted as an igniter for the protests.  这番有争议的演讲成了抗议活动的导火索。|Synonyms: catalyst, spark, trigger|Antonyms: deterrent, suppressant|noun
