interproduced:/ˌɪntərprəˈdjuːst/
|adj.|互相产生的，互相制造的|Produced or made by each other; mutually generative.|例句：The interproduced effects of the two chemicals created a new compound. 这两种化学物质互相产生的作用形成了一种新的化合物。|例句：The interproduced anxieties of the team members affected their performance. 团队成员之间互相产生的焦虑影响了他们的表现。|近义词：interrelated, interdependent, interactive|反义词：independent, separate|词性:形容词

词源:
"inter-" (prefix) + "produced" (past participle of "produce")
"inter-" comes from Latin, meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally."
"produce" comes from Latin "producere," meaning "to lead forth, bring out, prolong, extend, produce."
