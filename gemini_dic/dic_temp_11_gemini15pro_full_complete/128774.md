night:/naɪt/| n.|夜晚，夜间|The night is dark and full of terrors. 夜晚黑暗而充满恐怖。|例句：I prefer working at night. 我更喜欢在晚上工作。|例句：The stars shone brightly in the night sky. 星星在夜空中闪耀。|近义词：nighttime, darkness|反义词:day, daytime|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *night, *nyght, from Old English *niht* (West Saxon, Kentish), *næht, neht* (Anglian), from Proto-Germanic *nahts*, from Proto-Indo-European *nokʷts-. Cognate with Scots nicht (“night”), West Frisian nacht (“night”), Dutch nacht (“night”), German Nacht (“night”), Danish nat (“night”), Swedish natt (“night”), Icelandic nótt (“night”), Latin nox (“night”), Ancient Greek νύξ (núx, “night”), Sanskrit नक्त (nakta, “night”).
