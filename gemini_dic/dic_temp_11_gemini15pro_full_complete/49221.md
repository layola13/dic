delegate:/ˈdɛlɪɡeɪt/ /ˈdɛlɪɡət/ | n. vt.|代表，委托|A person chosen or elected to represent others, especially at a conference or meeting.  |例句：The delegate from France addressed the assembly. 法国代表向大会致辞。

delegate:/ˈdɛlɪɡeɪt/ /ˈdɛlɪɡət/ | n. vt.|代表，委托|To entrust (a task or responsibility) to another person, typically one who is less senior than oneself. |例句：He delegated the task to his assistant. 他把这项任务委托给了他的助手。

delegate:/ˈdɛlɪɡeɪt/ /ˈdɛlɪɡət/ | n.|代表|近义词：representative, envoy, commissioner, agent, deputy |反义词: constituent
