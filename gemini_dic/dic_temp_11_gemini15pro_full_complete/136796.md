nonseverance:/ˌnɒnˈsɛvərəns/ | n. | 不可分割，非分离，连续性 | The nonseverance of the contract means that it remains in effect despite the change in ownership.  合同的不可分割性意味着尽管所有权发生变化，合同仍然有效。 | The judge emphasized the nonseverance of the two charges, meaning they must be tried together. 法官强调了两项指控的不可分割性，这意味着它们必须一起审理。 | 近义词：inseparability, indivisibility, unity, continuity | 反义词：severance, separation, division, discontinuity | 词性: 名词
