overcriticalnesses:/ˌōvərˈkrɪtɪkəlnəsɪz/
|n.|过度批评，吹毛求疵|The state of being excessively critical; a tendency to find fault with everything.|Her overcriticalnesses made it difficult for anyone to work with her. 她过分的挑剔使得很难有人和她共事。|近义词：hypercriticism, faultfinding, captiousness|反义词：tolerance, leniency, acceptance|词性:名词

词源:
From "over-" (excessive) + "critical" (inclined to find fault) + "-ness" (forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Ultimately from Greek "kritikos" (able to discern).
