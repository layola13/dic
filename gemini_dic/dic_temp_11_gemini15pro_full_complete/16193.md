banksters:/ˈbæŋkstərz/
noun
银行歹徒; 金融大盗
A derogatory term for bankers, especially those perceived as greedy, corrupt, or irresponsible.  It blends "banker" with "gangster."
The banksters were blamed for the financial crisis. 这些银行歹徒被指责引发了金融危机。
synonyms:  financiers, loan sharks, robber barons
antonyms: philanthropists, benefactors
n.
