beseechers:/bɪˈsiːtʃərz/
n.
恳求者；祈求者
People making earnest requests or pleas.
The beseechers knelt before the king, begging for mercy. 跪在地上的恳求者乞求国王的仁慈。
supplicants, petitioners, pleaders
demanders, objectors

词性: 名词 (Noun)
