filenames:/ˈfaɪlˌneɪmz/| n.|文件名|A filename is the name given to a computer file. It is used to identify and locate the file within a file system.|例句：Please send me the filenames of the documents you want me to review. 请把你要我审查的文件的文件名发给我。

例句：The filenames are too long and complicated. 这些文件名太长太复杂了。|近义词：filepaths|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "file contents")|词性:noun
