curioso:/kuˈrjoʊsoʊ/ (US), /kjʊəˈrɪəʊsəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|好奇的，求知的；古怪的，奇特的|Inquisitive; eager to learn or know; strange; unusual.|He was a curioso, always collecting strange and unusual objects. 他是个好奇的人，总是收集一些奇奇怪怪的东西。|The shop sold curioso items like shrunken heads and two-headed snakes. 这家商店出售稀奇古怪的物品，例如缩小的头颅和双头蛇。|近义词：inquisitive, curious, peculiar, odd, strange, bizarre|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, ordinary, usual|词性:形容词

词源: From Spanish *curioso*, from Latin *cūrĭōsus* ("careful, diligent, inquisitive"), from *cūra* ("care, concern").
