brochantite:/broʊˈʃænˌtaɪt/
n.
绿铜矾
A basic copper sulfate mineral, Cu₄SO₄(OH)₆, occurring in emerald-green crystals.
Brochantite is a secondary mineral that forms in oxidized copper deposits. 绿铜矾是一种次生矿物，形成于氧化的铜矿床中。
Brochantite crystals were found in the mine. 在矿山中发现了绿铜矾晶体。

近义词: antlerite, posnjakite (These are other copper sulfate minerals with different hydration levels)
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific mineral)
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after the French mineralogist and geologist André Brochant de Villiers (1772-1840).
