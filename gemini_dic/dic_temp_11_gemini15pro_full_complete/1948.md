actionability:/ˌækʃənəˈbɪləti/| n.|可操作性，可行动性|The quality of being actionable; the capacity to be acted upon or put into effect.|The report highlighted the actionability of the recommendations, meaning they could be easily implemented.  这份报告强调了这些建议的可操作性，这意味着它们可以很容易地实施。|例句：The actionability of the marketing plan depends on having sufficient resources. 这份营销计划的可行性取决于是否有足够的资源。|近义词：feasibility, practicality, viability|反义词：inactionability, impracticality|noun
