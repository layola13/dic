advan:/ədˈvæn/ | prefix | 前进，提前，向前 |  A prefix meaning "forward, in advance, towards the front". Derived from Latin "abante" (before, in front).  It's often used to create words related to progress or improvement. |  The company seeks to **advan**ce its position in the market. (这家公司试图提升其市场地位。) | 近义词: pre-, pro-, fore- | 反义词: retro-, re- | 词性: prefix
