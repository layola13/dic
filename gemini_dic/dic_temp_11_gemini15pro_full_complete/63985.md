epistemology: /ɪˌpɪstɪˈmɒlədʒi/ (UK) /ɪˌpɪstəˈmɑːlədʒi/ (US) | n. | 认识论 | The study or a theory of the nature and grounds of knowledge especially with reference to its limits and validity. |  Epistemology asks questions like: "What is knowledge?" and "How do we know what we know?" | 近义词: theory of knowledge, gnoseology | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps ignorance or nescience in a loose sense) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *epistēmologia*, from Ancient Greek ἐπιστήμη (epistḗmē, “knowledge”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).
