deviled:/ˈdɛvəld/| adj.|加辣的，加魔鬼酱的，极坏的，恶毒的|Highly seasoned, as with hot spices or mustard.  Figuratively, mischievous or wicked.|例句：Deviled eggs are a popular picnic food.  魔鬼蛋是受欢迎的野餐食物。

deviled:/ˈdɛvəld/| n.|加辣的菜肴|A dish prepared with hot seasoning.|例句：This deviled crab is quite tasty. 这道加辣蟹很美味。

例句：We had deviled chicken for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了加辣鸡肉。|近义词：spicy dish|反义词:bland dish|词性:名词
