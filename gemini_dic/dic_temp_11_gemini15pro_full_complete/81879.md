grieved:/ɡriːvd/
adjective
悲痛的，伤心的
Feeling or expressing great sadness, especially due to loss.
She was deeply grieved by the death of her father. 她为父亲的去世深感悲痛。
近义词：sorrowful, heartbroken, mournful, devastated
反义词：joyful, happy, elated


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *greven*, from Old French *grever* "to burden, annoy, harm, injure," from Late Latin *gravare*, from Latin *gravis* "heavy."  The sense shifted from "burdened" to "saddened" over time.
