chirruppers:/ˈtʃɪrəpərz/
|n.|唧唧喳喳叫的人或动物（通常指鸟类）|People or animals, typically birds, that chirp repeatedly.|The chirruppers in the trees woke me up early this morning. 树上的鸟儿唧唧喳喳地叫，把我一大早就吵醒了。|Synonyms: singers, warblers|Antonyms: silent creatures|noun
chirruppers:/ˈtʃɪrəpərz/
|n.|爱闲聊的人|People who chatter or gossip.|The chirruppers in the office were always gossiping. 办公室里那些爱闲聊的人总是在八卦。|Synonyms: chatterboxes, gossips|Antonyms: quiet people|noun
