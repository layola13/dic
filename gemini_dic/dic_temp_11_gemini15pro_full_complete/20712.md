blah:/blɑː/ | interj. | 废话，胡说，瞎扯 |  Meaningless chatter; nonsense.  Used to express disinterest or contempt. | Example: He was talking a lot of blah about the economy. 他一直在胡扯些关于经济的废话。 | Synonym: nonsense, rubbish, gibberish, drivel | Antonym: sense, meaning, substance | interjection


blah:/blɑː/ | n. | 废话，胡扯，瞎扯 |  Meaningless chatter; nonsense. | Example: All that blah about the new policy is confusing. 所有关于新政策的废话都令人困惑。 | Synonym: nonsense, rubbish, gibberish, drivel | Antonym: sense, meaning, substance | noun


Etymology:  Probably imitative of idle chatter.  First recorded in the early 20th century.
