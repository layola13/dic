nonexecutive:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv/ | n. adj. | 非执行官；非执行的 | A nonexecutive director is a member of a company's board of directors who is not part of the executive team.  A non-executive role is one that does not involve day-to-day management. |  The company appointed three new nonexecutives to the board.  公司任命了三位新的非执行董事进入董事会。 | The chairman holds a nonexecutive position. 主席担任非执行职位。 | Synonyms: independent director, external director, non-management  | Antonyms: executive, manager | noun, adjective
