ebracteate:/iːˈbræktiɪt/
adjective
无苞片的
lacking bracts; without bracts.  A bract is a modified leaf associated with a flower or inflorescence.
The ebracteate stem of the plant made it easy to identify.  这种植物无苞片的茎使其易于识别。
近义词:bractless
反义词:bracteate, bracteolate
词性:adjective
