gyre:/dʒaɪər/ | n. | 旋转; 螺旋; 环流; 漩涡 | A circular or spiral motion or form; a vortex. | The ocean currents move in gyres. 洋流以环流的形式运动。|  The birds circled in a slow gyre above the carcass. 鸟儿在尸体上方缓慢地盘旋。 | synonym: spiral, whirl, vortex, eddy | antonym: straight line | noun

gyre:/dʒaɪər/ | v. |  旋转; 环绕; 盘旋 | To move in a circle or spiral; to whirl. | The leaves gyred in the wind. 树叶在风中旋转。 | The smoke gyred upwards from the chimney. 烟雾从烟囱向上盘旋。 | synonym: whirl, spin, rotate, revolve | antonym: still, stationary | verb
