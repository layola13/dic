obsessingly:/əbˈsesɪŋli/ | adv. | 着魔地，痴迷地 |  In a way that shows one is obsessed; persistently and to an excessive degree. | He stared obsessively at the photograph. 他痴迷地盯着那张照片。|  She checked her phone obsessively for messages. 她不停地查看手机上的信息。| 近义词: compulsively, fixatedly, preoccupiedly | 反义词: casually, indifferently | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From obsess + -ing + -ly.  "Obsess" comes from the Latin *obsidēre*  meaning "to besiege," from *ob-* ("against, towards") + *sidēre* ("to sit").  The original meaning related to being besieged by evil spirits.
