habitable:/ˈhæbɪtəbl/| adj.|适宜居住的，可居住的|Suitable for living in; offering the basic necessities for life.|例句：The planet Mars is not currently habitable for humans. 火星目前不适合人类居住。

例句：Scientists are searching for habitable planets in other solar systems. 科学家们正在其他星系中寻找宜居行星。|近义词：livable, inhabitable, residential|反义词：uninhabitable, unlivable|词性：形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *habitabilis*, from Latin habitāre (“to inhabit, to dwell”), from habitus, perfect passive participle of habēre (“to have, to hold”).
