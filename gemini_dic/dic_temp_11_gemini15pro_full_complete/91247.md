hydrodynamometer:/ˌhaɪdrəʊˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtər/ | n. | 水力测功计，水力测力计 | An instrument used for measuring the power output of a hydraulic machine, such as a turbine or pump.  It measures the force exerted by a stream of water. | Example: The engineers used a hydrodynamometer to assess the efficiency of the new hydroelectric dam. 工程师们使用水力测功计来评估新水电站大坝的效率。

Example: A hydrodynamometer was employed to calibrate the pump's performance.  一个水力测功计被用来校准泵的性能。| 近义词: dynamometer, power meter | 反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a measuring instrument) | 词性: noun
