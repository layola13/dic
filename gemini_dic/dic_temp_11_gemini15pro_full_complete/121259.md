misevaluate:/ˌmɪsɪˈvæljuˌeɪt/
verb
错误评估，低估，高估
To assess or judge the value or worth of something incorrectly.
She misevaluated the situation and made a poor decision. 她错误地评估了情况，做出了糟糕的决定。
The company misevaluated the market potential of the new product. 公司错误地评估了新产品的市场潜力。
misjudge, underestimate, overestimate
correctly evaluate, accurately assess
