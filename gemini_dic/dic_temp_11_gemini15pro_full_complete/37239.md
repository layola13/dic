comfortful: /ˈkʌmfərtfəl/ | adj. | 舒适的，令人感到安慰的 | Providing or experiencing physical comfort. While not strictly incorrect, this word is considered non-standard and "comfortable" is generally preferred. |  He tried to make his guests feel as comfortful as possible. 他努力让客人感到尽可能舒适。 |  She found the old armchair comfortful and relaxing. 她觉得那张旧扶手椅舒适又放松。 | 近义词: comfortable, cozy, snug | 反义词: uncomfortable, uneasy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "comfort" + "-ful".  "Comfort" comes from Old French "confort", ultimately from Latin "confortare"  meaning "to strengthen much".
