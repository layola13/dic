biodegradable:/ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdəbl/| adj.|可生物降解的|Capable of being decomposed by bacteria or other living organisms.|例句：Biodegradable packaging helps reduce environmental pollution. 可生物降解的包装有助于减少环境污染。

例句：This plastic bag is biodegradable, so it won't harm the environment.  这个塑料袋是可生物降解的，所以不会对环境造成危害。


近义词：compostable, decomposable|反义词：non-biodegradable, persistent|词性：adjective

词源：bio- (life) + degradable (able to be broken down)
