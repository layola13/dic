occult:/əˈkʌlt/
|adj. n. v.|神秘的; 隐秘的; 玄奥的;  神秘的事物; 掩星|Supernatural, mystical, or magical beliefs, practices, or phenomena.   To hide or conceal from view.|The occult practices of the cult were kept secret.  该邪教的神秘活动是保密的。  The moon occults the sun during a solar eclipse. 在日食期间，月亮遮蔽了太阳。 |近义词：(adj.) hidden, secret, esoteric; (v.) conceal, obscure|反义词：(adj.) obvious, overt, apparent; (v.) reveal, expose|词性:形容词, 名词, 动词

词源:  From Latin occultus (“hidden, secret”), past participle of occulere (“to cover over, conceal”), from ob (“over, against”) + calere (“to hide”).
