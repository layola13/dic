clear-sightedly:/ˌklɪərˈsaɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 明确地；洞察力强地；目光敏锐地 | In a way that shows a clear understanding of a situation and good judgment about what to do next. | She clear-sightedly assessed the situation and made a wise decision. 她明确地评估了情况，并做出了明智的决定。 |  perspicaciously, astutely, wisely, judiciously | shortsightedly, blindly, foolishly | adverb

Etymology:
From "clear-sighted" + "-ly."  "Clear" comes from Old French "cler" (bright, shining).  "Sighted" comes from Old English "gesīhþ" (seeing, sight).  "-ly" is a common suffix forming adverbs.
