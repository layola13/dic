mushy:/ˈmʌʃi/ (US), /ˈmʌʃi/ (UK)| adj.|糊状的，软烂的；感伤的，多情的|Soft and pulpy; excessively sentimental.|The overripe bananas were mushy.|近义词：soft, pulpy, sentimental, slushy|反义词：firm, hard, unsentimental|词性:形容词

Etymology: From mush + -y.  "Mush" originally referred to boiled cornmeal, known for its soft texture.  The figurative sense of "overly sentimental" developed later.
