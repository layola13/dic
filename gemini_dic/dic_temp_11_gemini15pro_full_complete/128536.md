nexuses:/ˈnɛksəsɪz/ /ˈnɛksəsiz/
n.|连接;枢纽;中心;关系|A nexus is a connection or series of connections linking two or more things.|The airport is a nexus for international travel.  这个机场是国际旅行的枢纽。|connections, links, centers, hubs|peripheries, outskirts
n.|一组事物之间的关系;（尤指）一系列事件之间的联系|A nexus is also a means of connection between things or a connected group or series.|There is a nexus between poverty and crime. 贫困与犯罪之间存在联系。|link, connection, relationship, correlation|division, separation
