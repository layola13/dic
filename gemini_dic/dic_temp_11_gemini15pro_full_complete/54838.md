displace:/dɪsˈpleɪs/ /dɪˈspleɪs/| vt.|取代，移置，撤职|to force something or someone out of its usual or original position|The rising floodwaters displaced thousands of people.  上涨的洪水使数千人流离失所。|synonyms: move, relocate, remove, supplant, oust|antonyms: place, settle, establish|verb
