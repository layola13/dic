overruler:/ˌoʊvərˈruːlər/
n.
统治者; 支配者; 裁决者
One who overrules or exercises dominant power or influence.
The overruler of the small kingdom was known for his cruelty.  这个小王国的统治者以残暴著称。
Synonyms: ruler, governor, dictator, autocrat
Antonyms: subject, citizen, subordinate
Noun

overrule:/ˌoʊvərˈruːl/
vt.
否决; 驳回; 宣布无效; 统治; 支配; 控制
To reject or disallow the decision or argument of (a person); to prevail over.
The judge overruled the objection. 法官驳回了异议。
The king overruled his advisors and declared war. 国王否决了顾问们的意见，宣布开战。
Synonyms: reject, disallow, override, invalidate, dominate, control
Antonyms: accept, allow, uphold, validate, submit
Verb
