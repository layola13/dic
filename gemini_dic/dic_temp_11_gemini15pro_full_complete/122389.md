mm:/ˈmɪlɪmiːtər/ /ˈmɪləˌmiːtər/| n. |毫米|A unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter.|The screw was only a few millimeters long. 这颗螺丝只有几毫米长。|近义词:  millimetre (British spelling)|反义词: kilometer, meter|noun

mm:/m(ə)m/| int. |嗯|Used to express agreement, satisfaction, or pleasure.|Mm, this cake is delicious. 嗯，这蛋糕真好吃。|近义词:  yeah, yes, uh-huh|反义词: no|interjection
