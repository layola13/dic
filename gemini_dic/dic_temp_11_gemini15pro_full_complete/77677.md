gelsemic: /dʒɛlˈsiːmɪk/ | adj. | 钩吻属的，与钩吻属植物有关的 | Relating to or derived from plants of the genus *Gelsemium*, especially the Carolina jasmine (*Gelsemium sempervirens*), or containing gelsemine. |  Gelsemic acid is a component of *Gelsemium sempervirens*. 钩吻酸是钩吻的成分。 |  Gelsemic compounds are known for their neurotoxic properties.  钩吻化合物以其神经毒性而闻名。 | Near Synonyms: Gelsemine-containing | Antonyms: None | adjective
