anglo-vernacular:/ˈæŋɡləʊ vərˈnækjʊlər/
adjective
英语本土的
Relating to or denoting a hybrid language or style combining elements of English and a local vernacular.
The anglo-vernacular schools of colonial India played a crucial role in disseminating Western education.  殖民时期印度的英语本土学校在传播西方教育方面发挥了至关重要的作用。

anglo-vernacular:/ˈæŋɡləʊ vərˈnækjʊlər/
noun
英语本土化语言或风格
A hybrid language or style combining elements of English and a local vernacular.
The use of anglo-vernacular was common in administrative settings during the British Raj. 在英属印度时期，英语本土化语言在行政环境中很常见。

近义词:hybrid, mixed, macaronic
反义词:pure, unmixed
