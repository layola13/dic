jambee: /dʒæmˈbiː/
noun
詹比舞; 一种牙买加的民间舞蹈
A lively Jamaican folk dance, characterized by jumping movements and often performed to the accompaniment of drums and other percussion instruments.
Example: The performers danced the jambee with infectious energy.  表演者们以极富感染力的活力跳着詹比舞。
Example:  The festival featured traditional jambee and other cultural performances.  这个节日以传统的詹比舞和其他文化表演为特色。
Synonym: dance, jig
Antonym: stillness, rest
词性: 名词
