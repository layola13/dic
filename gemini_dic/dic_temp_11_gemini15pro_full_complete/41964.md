cosponsorship:/koʊˈspɑːnsərʃɪp/
n.
共同赞助，联合赞助
the act of sponsoring something together with another person or organization.
The conference benefited from the cosponsorship of several major corporations.  这次会议得益于几家大公司的联合赞助。
The bill passed with bipartisan cosponsorship.  该法案在两党的共同支持下获得通过。
joint sponsorship, shared sponsorship
sole sponsorship
noun
