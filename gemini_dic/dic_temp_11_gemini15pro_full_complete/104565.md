kanawa:/kəˈnɑːwə/| n.|独木舟（尤指毛利人的）;渡船|A canoe, especially one used by the Maori people of New Zealand; a ferry.|例句：The kanawa glided silently across the lake. 独木舟静静地滑过湖面。

例句：They crossed the river in a kanawa. 他们乘独木舟过河。


近义词：canoe, waka, outrigger|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
