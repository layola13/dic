intercalibrations:/ɪntərˌkælɪˈbreɪʃənz/| n. |校准，相互校准，比对|The process of comparing and adjusting measurement instruments or procedures to ensure consistency and accuracy between different laboratories or systems.  |例句：Intercalibrations are essential for ensuring the reliability of scientific data across different research groups.  相互校准对于确保不同研究小组之间科学数据的可靠性至关重要。

例句：Regular intercalibrations of the equipment are necessary to maintain quality control.  定期对设备进行相互校准对于维持质量控制是必要的。|近义词：comparisons, standardizations, calibrations, adjustments|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly) discrepancies, inconsistencies|词性:noun
