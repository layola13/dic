fly-drive:/ˈflaɪˌdraɪv/
noun
自驾游； fly-drive holiday/tour/package
A prearranged holiday where you fly to a destination and then hire a car.
We booked a fly-drive holiday to Florida. 我们预订了去佛罗里达的自驾游。
近义词: self-drive tour, road trip
反义词: guided tour, package tour
词性: n.

fly-drive:/ˈflaɪˌdraɪv/
adjective
自驾游的
Relating to a holiday where you fly to a destination and then hire a car.
We chose a fly-drive package because we wanted to explore at our own pace. 我们选择了一个自驾游套餐，因为我们想按照自己的节奏去探索。
近义词: self-drive
反义词: guided
词性: adj.
