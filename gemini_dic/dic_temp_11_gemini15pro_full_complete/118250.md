merchandise:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪs/ | n. | 商品，货物 | Goods that are bought and sold. | The store offers a wide variety of merchandise. 这家商店提供各种各样的商品。 |  The company's merchandise is very popular. 这家公司的商品很受欢迎。 | synonyms: goods, wares, commodities, products | antonyms: services | noun

merchandise:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪs/ | vt. | 推销，销售 | To promote or sell goods. | The company is aggressively merchandising its new product line.  这家公司正在积极推销其新的产品线。| They merchandised the toys with a popular cartoon character. 他们用一个受欢迎的卡通人物来推销玩具。 | synonyms: market, sell, promote, retail | antonyms: buy, purchase | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *marchandise "trade, goods," from marchand "merchant," from Late Latin mercatans "merchant," present participle of mercāri "to trade," from merx "merchandise."
