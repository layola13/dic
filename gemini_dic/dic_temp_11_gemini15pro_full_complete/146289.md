overfastidiousnesses:/ˌōvərˌfæˈstɪdiəsnəsəz/
| n. | 过分挑剔, 过分讲究| Excessive fastidiousness; the state of being too critical or demanding, especially about minor details.|  Her overfastidiousnesses about cleanliness made it difficult for anyone to live with her. 她对清洁的过分挑剔使得任何人都难以和她一起生活。 | 近义词: fussiness, meticulousness, pickiness | 反义词: carelessness, negligence, sloppiness | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessive) + "fastidious" (very attentive to and concerned about accuracy and detail) + "-ness" (forming nouns expressing state or quality) + "-es" (plural suffix). "Fastidious" comes from Latin "fastidiosus," meaning "disgusting, squeamish, disdainful," ultimately from "fastus" (disdain, arrogance) + "-diosus" (suffix forming adjectives).
