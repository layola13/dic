gumshoes:/ˈɡʌmʃuːz/
noun
侦探，私人侦探
A detective, especially a private investigator.
例句：The gumshoe followed the suspect for days.  侦探跟踪了嫌疑人好几天。

例句：She hired a gumshoe to investigate her husband's disappearance. 她雇了一个私家侦探来调查她丈夫的失踪。

近义词：detective, investigator, private eye, sleuth
反义词: (无直接的反义词)
词性: noun


gumshoe:/ˈɡʌmʃuːz/
adjective
侦探的，私家侦探的
Relating to or characteristic of a detective, especially a private investigator.
例句：He used gumshoe tactics to uncover the truth. 他用了侦探手段来揭露真相。

例句：The gumshoe novel was full of suspense. 这本侦探小说充满了悬念。

近义词：detective, investigative
反义词: (无直接的反义词)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the rubber soles of shoes worn by detectives, which allowed them to move quietly.  来自于侦探穿的橡胶底鞋，这种鞋可以让他们悄无声息地行动。
