epileptiform:/ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪfɔːrm/ | adj. |癫痫样的，癫痫形的|Resembling epilepsy or its manifestations, particularly the characteristic seizure patterns observed on an electroencephalogram (EEG).  Characterized by abnormal electrical activity in the brain, similar to that seen in epilepsy.| The patient's EEG showed epileptiform activity, suggesting a predisposition to seizures.  该病人的脑电图显示癫痫样放电，提示有癫痫发作的倾向。 |  Synonym: epileptoid | Antonym: normal (in the context of brain activity) | Adjective
