cheaper:/ˈtʃiːpər/| adj.|更便宜的|Comparative form of cheap.  Used to describe something that costs less than something else.|This store is cheaper than the one across the street. 这家商店比街对面的那家便宜。|
近义词：less expensive, more affordable, lower-priced|反义词：more expensive, pricier, costlier|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *chepere, comparative of chep (“cheap”), from Old English cēap (“bargain, purchase, sale, market, price, low price”), from Proto-Germanic *kaupą (“bargain, purchase, business transaction, market”).
