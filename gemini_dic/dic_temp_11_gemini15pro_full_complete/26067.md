bushmeat: /ˈbʊʃmiːt/
noun
野味
The meat of wild animals, especially those hunted in Africa and Asia.
例句：The trade in bushmeat is a serious threat to many endangered species. 野味贸易是对许多濒危物种的严重威胁。

例句：Bushmeat is a major source of protein for some rural communities. 野味是一些农村社区的主要蛋白质来源。

近义词：game, wild game
反义词: farmed meat, domesticated meat
词性: n.
