chud:/tʃʌd/| n.|笨蛋，乡巴佬，(尤指)右翼极端分子|A derogatory slang term for a person considered to be unsophisticated, unintelligent, or socially inept, often with right-wing extremist connotations.  It gained popularity online as a pejorative used against those perceived as holding reactionary or bigoted views.|He called them a bunch of chuds for their outdated opinions. 他因为他们过时的观点称他们是一群笨蛋。|近义词：yokel, hick, redneck, bigot|反义词:cosmopolitan, sophisticated|Noun

chud:/tʃʌd/| n.|食人族（Cannibalistic Humanoid Underground Dweller）|This meaning originated from the 1984 horror film "C.H.U.D." (Cannibalistic Humanoid Underground Dwellers) and is less commonly used in everyday language. It refers to fictional monstrous creatures living underground.|The movie "C.H.U.D." is about cannibalistic humanoids living beneath the city. 电影“C.H.U.D.”讲述的是生活在地下的食人族。|近义词:monster, creature, mutant|反义词:human|Noun
