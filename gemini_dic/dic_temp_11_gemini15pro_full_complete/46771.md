dangerless:/ˈdeɪndʒərlɪs/
adjective
无危险的；安全的
Free from danger; safe.
The path seemed dangerless, so we continued our hike. 这条路看起来很安全，所以我们继续徒步旅行。
Synonyms: safe, secure, harmless, innocuous
Antonyms: dangerous, perilous, hazardous, risky
词性: 形容词

词源:
From danger + -less.  Danger comes from Old French dangier, from Late Latin *dominiarium "power, control," from Latin dominus "lord, master." The suffix -less is an Old English suffix meaning "without."
