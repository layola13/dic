interminate:/ɪnˈtɜːrmɪnət/ | adj. | 无限的，无终结的，没完没了的 | Having or seeming to have no end; endless. | The movie felt interminable.  这部电影让人感觉没完没了。|  The wait seemed interminable.  等待似乎无休无止。 | 近义词: endless, ceaseless, infinite, boundless | 反义词: finite, limited, temporary, brief | 词性: 形容词 | 词源: From Late Latin interminatus, from in- ‘not’ + terminatus ‘limited’.
