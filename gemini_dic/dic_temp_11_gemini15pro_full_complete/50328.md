deplorement:/dɪˈplɔːrmənt/
n.
悲叹；痛惜；哀悼
an expression of disapproval and regret
The president expressed his deplorement of the violence. 总统对暴力事件表示痛惜。

近义词: lamentation, mourning, grieving, sorrow, regret
反义词: rejoicing, celebration, joy

词性: 名词 (noun)

词源: From French *déploiement*, from Old French *deplorer* "to bewail, lament," from Latin *dēplōrāre* "to weep for, lament, bewail," from *dē-* "completely" + *plōrāre* "to weep, cry out."
