outfoxes:/ˌaʊtˈfɒksɪz/ /ˌaʊtˈfɑːksɪz/
verb
以智取胜，胜过
outwit, surpass in cunning
The young chess player outfoxed his opponent with a clever move.  这位年轻的棋手以一个巧妙的棋步智胜了对手。
He outfoxed the police by changing his appearance. 他通过改变自己的外貌智胜了警方。
synonyms: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver, circumvent
antonyms: be outwitted, be outsmarted

词性: verb
