hilock:/ˈhɪlək/|n.|小山丘；小土墩|A small hill or mound.|We climbed the hilock to get a better view of the valley. 我们爬上小山丘，以便更好地欣赏山谷的景色。|The children were rolling down the grassy hillock. 孩子们在草坡上滚下来。|近义词：knoll, mound, hill|反义词:valley, depression|词性:名词
Etymology->
From Middle English *hilloke*, a diminutive of *hil*, from Old English *hyll* (“hill”), from Proto-Germanic *hulniz* (“hill, mound”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kelH-* (“to bend, curve”). Cognate with Scots *hilloc*, *hillock* (“small hill, mound”), West Frisian *hilke* (“small hill”), Dutch *heuvel* (“hill, hillock”), German *Hügel* (“hill”), Danish *høj* (“hill, height”).
