logining: /ˈlɒɡɪnɪŋ/ | Not a standard English word |  （非标准英语词汇，建议使用logging in）| This is not a standard English word. The correct form is "logging in". This refers to the process of accessing a computer system or online account by entering a username and password. |  Incorrect: I am logining to my account. Correct: I am logging in to my account. 我正在登录我的帐户。 | 近义词: signing in, accessing | 反义词: logging out, signing out |  Not a standard word, therefore no standard part of speech.
