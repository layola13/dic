hyperdeliciousness:/ˌhaɪpər dɪˈlɪʃəsnəs/
|n.|超级美味，极度美味|An extreme level of deliciousness, beyond simply being delicious.  This word emphasizes the extraordinary and intense pleasure derived from the taste of something.| The hyperdeliciousness of the chocolate cake made me want to eat the whole thing.|Synonyms: exquisite, scrumptious, delectable, divine|Antonyms: disgusting, unappetizing, revolting|Noun
