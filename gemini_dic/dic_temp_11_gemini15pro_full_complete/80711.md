goriness:/ˈɡɔːrɪnɪs/
n.
血腥; 恐怖;
The state or quality of being gory; explicit violence and bloodshed.
The goriness of the movie made me sick to my stomach. 这部电影的血腥程度让我反胃。
近义词：bloodiness, gruesomeness, carnage
反义词：pleasantness, agreeableness
