campaigning:/kæmˈpeɪnɪŋ/
noun
竞选活动； campaigning
the activity of trying to persuade people to vote for a particular person or political party.
例句：The campaigning for the election was intense. 选举的竞选活动非常激烈。
例句：She's been campaigning for equal rights for years. 她多年来一直为争取平等权利而奔走呼号。
近义词：canvassing, electioneering
反义词：inaction
