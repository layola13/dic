envyingly:/ˈɛnviɪŋli/
adverb
羡慕地，嫉妒地
in a way that shows you wish you had what someone else has
She looked envyingly at her friend's new car. 她羡慕地看着朋友的新车。
近义词: jealously, covetously
反义词: admiringly, approvingly
词性: adverb

词源: From envy (n.) + -ing + -ly.  The word "envy" comes from the Old French "envie," ultimately from the Latin "invidia," meaning "envy, jealousy, ill-will."
