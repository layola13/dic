epoophoron:/ˌɛpoʊˈɒfərɒn/ | n. | 附睾 | A rudimentary organ representing the remains of the Wolffian duct, situated in the mesosalpinx between the ovary and the Fallopian tube. It consists of a number of small tubules converging toward a longitudinal duct. | The epoophoron can sometimes form cysts. | 近义词: parovarium, organ of Rosenmüller | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an anatomical structure) | noun
