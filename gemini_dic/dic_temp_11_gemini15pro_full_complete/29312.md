castered:/ˈkæstərd/| adj.|装有脚轮的，有轮子的|Fitted with casters (small wheels) for easy movement. |The hospital bed was castered, allowing nurses to move it easily.  医院的病床装有脚轮，护士可以轻松移动。|The piano was castered for ease of movement.  为了便于移动，钢琴装有脚轮。|近义词：wheeled, mobile, rolling|反义词：fixed, stationary|词性:形容词

词源:
From "caster" + "-ed."  "Caster" itself originates from the verb "cast," meaning to throw or project.  In this context, "cast" refers to the swiveling action of the wheel.
