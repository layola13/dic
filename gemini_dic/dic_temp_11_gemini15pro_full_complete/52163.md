diaphragm: /ˈdaɪəfræm/ (US) /ˈdaɪəfræm/ (UK)| n.|横膈膜；光圈；隔膜；振动膜|A dome-shaped, muscular partition separating the thorax from the abdomen in mammals. diaphragm: /ˈdaɪəfræm/ (US) /ˈdaɪəfræm/ (UK)| n.|横膈膜；光圈；隔膜；振动膜|A thin opaque structure with a central aperture, used to regulate the amount of light passing through a lens, as in a camera or microscope.|Adjusting the diaphragm on the camera will alter the depth of field. 调整相机的光圈会改变景深。|synonyms: aperture, iris|antonyms: none|noun
