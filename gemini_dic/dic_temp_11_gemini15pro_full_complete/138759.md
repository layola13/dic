numerator:/ˈnjuːməˌreɪtər/| n.|分子|The top number in a fraction. It represents how many parts of the whole are being considered.|例句：The numerator of the fraction 3/4 is 3.  分数 3/4 的分子是 3。

例句：In the fraction 5/8, 5 is the numerator. 在分数 5/8 中，5 是分子。|近义词：top number (in a fraction)|反义词:denominator|词性:noun
