consternation:/ˌkɒn.stəˈneɪ.ʃən/ /ˌkɑːn.stərˈneɪ.ʃən/| n. |惊愕，惊恐，丧胆| a feeling of great surprise or worry|  The news of the sudden death caused widespread consternation.  这突如其来的死讯引起了广泛的惊愕。|近义词：dismay, alarm, shock, panic|反义词:calm, composure, tranquility|词性:名词
词源: from Latin consternatio(n-), from consternare ‘throw into confusion’, from con- ‘together’ + sternere ‘spread, lay flat’.
