machineabilities:/məˌʃiːnəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|机械加工性，可加工性|The ease with which a material can be machined; the relative speed with which metal can be cut.  指一种材料容易被加工的程度；金属可以被切割的相对速度。|The machineabilities of different alloys vary considerably. 不同合金的机械加工性差异很大。|Synonyms: workability, machinability|Antonyms: unworkability, intractability|noun
