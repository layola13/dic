antispiritualistic:/ˌæntiˌspɪrɪtʃuəˈlɪstɪk/
adj.
反唯灵论的，反对唯灵论的
opposed to spiritualism; not believing in the existence of spirits or in communication with the dead.
His antispiritualistic views were unpopular among his friends, who were interested in séances. 他反对唯灵论的观点在他对降神会感兴趣的朋友中并不受欢迎。
近义词:materialistic, skeptical, unbelieving
反义词:spiritualistic, mystical, occult
词性:形容词
