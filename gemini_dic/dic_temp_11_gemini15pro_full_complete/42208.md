councilor:/ˈkaʊnsələr/ (US), /ˈkaʊnsələ(r)/ (UK)| n.| 市议员，顾问|A member of a council, especially a city council.  A person who gives advice.|例句：The councilor voted against the proposed development.  这位市议员投票反对拟议的开发项目。

例句：She sought the advice of a financial councilor. 她向一位财务顾问寻求建议。|近义词：councilman, councilwoman, advisor, consultant|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
