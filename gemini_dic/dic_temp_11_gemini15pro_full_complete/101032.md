invected:/ɪnˈvɛktɪd/
adjective
呈V形的，尖底锯齿状的
(heraldry) Having a serrated edge with the points curving inward.  Used to describe an ordinary in heraldry.
The shield was invected azure.  盾牌呈蓝色尖底锯齿状。
近义词: serrated, indented, notched
反义词: smooth, straight, plain
词性: adjective

inveigh:/ɪnˈveɪ/
verb
猛烈抨击，痛骂
To speak or write bitterly or vehemently against something or someone.
He inveighed against the injustice of the system. 他猛烈抨击该制度的不公。
近义词: denounce, condemn, rail against, fulminate against
反义词: praise, commend, laud
词性: verb
