antistimulant:/ˌæntɪˈstɪmjʊlənt/
|n. adj.|抗兴奋剂，抑制兴奋的|A substance that reduces stimulation or excitement.|The doctor prescribed an antistimulant to help him sleep. 医生给他开了抗兴奋剂以帮助他睡眠。|The medication had an antistimulant effect. 这种药物具有抗兴奋作用。|近义词:depressant, sedative, tranquilizer|反义词:stimulant, excitant|词性:名词, 形容词
词源:anti- (against) + stimulant (something that stimulates)
