limitableness:/ˈlɪmɪtəblənəs/
n.
可限制性，局限性
The limitableness of human knowledge is a constant reminder of the vastness of the unknown.  人类知识的局限性不断提醒着我们未知的浩瀚。
The limitableness of resources necessitates careful planning. 资源的有限性需要仔细的规划。
近义词: finiteness, boundedness, restrictability
反义词: infiniteness, unboundedness, limitlessness
词性: noun

limitable:/ˈlɪmɪtəbl/
adj.
可限制的，有限的
Human lifespan is limitable. 人类的寿命是有限的。
The resources available for the project are limitable.  这个项目可用的资源是有限的。
近义词: finite, bounded, restricted
反义词: infinite, unbounded, limitless
词性: adjective

词源:
From limitable + -ness.  Limitable is derived from limit + -able.  Limit ultimately comes from the Latin word "limes," meaning "boundary."
