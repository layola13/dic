ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hwæt, neuter singular nominative and accusative of hwā (“who”), from Proto-Germanic *hwat, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷód. Cognate with Scots what (“what”), West Frisian wat (“what”), Dutch wat (“what”), German was (“what”), Danish hvad (“what”), Swedish vad (“what”), Icelandic hvað (“what”), Gothic 𐍈𐌰𐍀 (hwap, “what”).
