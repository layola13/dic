overpollinates:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːləˌneɪts/
verb
过度授粉
To pollinate excessively, often resulting in reduced fruit or seed set.  The act of transferring too much pollen to a flower, which can sometimes have negative consequences for the plant's reproductive success.
The farmer overpollinated the apple orchard, resulting in smaller than usual apples.  农民对苹果园过度授粉，导致苹果比平常小。
Bees overpollinated the squash blossoms, causing them to wither. 蜜蜂过度授粉了南瓜花，导致它们枯萎。
