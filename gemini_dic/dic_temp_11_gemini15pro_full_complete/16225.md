banqueteer:/ˌbæŋkwɪˈtɪr/
n.
宴会参加者；赴宴者
A person who attends a banquet.
例句：The banqueteers were dressed in their finest attire. 赴宴者们都穿着他们最好的服装。

例句：The banqueteers feasted on a lavish spread of food and drink.  赴宴者们尽情享用丰盛的食物和饮料。

近义词：guest, attendee, reveler
反义词：host
词性：noun
