gulleted:/ˈɡʌlɪtɪd/
adjective
有食道的，大吞大咽的，狼吞虎咽的
Having a gullet (esophagus). Figuratively, capable of swallowing large amounts or swallowing greedily.
The gulleted frog quickly consumed the unsuspecting insect.  这只大嘴蛙迅速吞下了毫无戒心的昆虫。
The competitive eater gulleted down hotdogs one after another.  这位大胃王选手狼吞虎咽地吃下一个又一个热狗。
近义词: gluttonous, voracious
反义词: abstemious
词性: adjective
