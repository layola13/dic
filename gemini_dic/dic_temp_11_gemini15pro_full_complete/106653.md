kurtas:/ˈkʊərtəz/ /ˈkɜːrtəz/| n.|库尔塔，一种及膝或过膝的长袖衬衫|A kurta is a long, loose-fitting shirt worn traditionally in South Asia.|He wore a white kurta for the ceremony. 他在典礼上穿了一件白色库尔塔。|近义词：tunic, shirt|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "trousers/pants" as a contrasting garment)|noun
