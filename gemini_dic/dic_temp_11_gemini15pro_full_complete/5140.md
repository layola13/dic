allele:/əˈliːl/| n.|等位基因|One of two or more alternative forms of a gene that arise by mutation and are found at the same place on a chromosome.|Example 1:  The child inherited one allele for brown eyes from the mother and one allele for blue eyes from the father.  这个孩子从母亲那里遗传了一个棕色眼睛的等位基因，从父亲那里遗传了一个蓝色眼睛的等位基因。

Example 2:  Different alleles can produce variations in traits like eye color or blood type.  不同的等位基因可以导致性状的变异，例如眼睛颜色或血型。|近义词：gene variant|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
