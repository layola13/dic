kilobits:/ˈkɪləˌbɪts/| n. |千比特|A unit of digital information or computer storage capacity equal to one thousand bits.|The file size is 80 kilobits.  文件大小是80千比特。|近义词：kbps (kilobits per second)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps megabits or gigabits for larger units)|noun
