musclebound: /ˈmʌsəlˌbaʊnd/ | adj. | 肌肉发达的，肌肉僵硬的 | Having large, well-developed muscles that sometimes restrict movement.  A person who is musclebound may have difficulty with flexibility and agility. | He was so musclebound that he had trouble touching his toes. 他肌肉太发达了，以至于很难碰到脚趾。|  The bodybuilder was so musclebound he could barely bend his arms.  这个健美运动员肌肉太发达了，几乎弯不了胳膊。 | 近义词: brawny, muscular, strapping | 反义词: weak, flabby | 词性: 形容词
