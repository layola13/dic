detestably:/dɪˈtɛstəbli/| adv.|可恨地，讨厌地，令人厌恶地|in a detestable manner; hatefully.  Characterized by or deserving strong dislike.|Her behavior was detestably rude. 她的行为令人厌恶地粗鲁。|Synonyms: abhorrently, abominably, disgustingly, hatefully, loathsomely, offensively, repugnantly, repellently, revoltingly|Antonyms: admirably, delightfully, pleasantly, wonderfully|Adverb
