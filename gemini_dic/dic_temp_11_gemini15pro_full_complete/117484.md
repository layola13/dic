medley:/ˈmedli/ | n. |混合物，混杂物，集成曲，杂录 | A mixture of different things, especially a musical piece made up of parts of other pieces. | The concert ended with a medley of popular songs.  音乐会以一首流行歌曲的集成曲结束。 | Synonyms: mixture, assortment, blend, potpourri, miscellany | Antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity | noun

medley:/ˈmedli/ | n. |混合泳 | A swimming race which comprises four different strokes. |  She won the gold medal in the 400-meter individual medley. 她赢得了400米个人混合泳的金牌。 | Synonyms:  mixed race (in swimming) | Antonyms: single stroke race | noun
