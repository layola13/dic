defraud:/dɪˈfrɔːd/| vt.|欺诈，蒙骗|To deprive someone of something by dishonest means.|She defrauded him of his inheritance. 她骗取了他的遗产。|近义词：trick, dupe, con|反义词：protect, safeguard|verb
