fluo:/ˈfluːoʊ/ (US), /ˈfluːəʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.|荧光的，荧光灯；荧光剂|A fluorescent color or light; a fluorescent lamp or substance.  Relating to fluorescence.|例句：The fluo paint glowed brightly under the black light.  荧光涂料在黑光下发出明亮的光。|近义词：fluorescent, luminous, bright, glowing|反义词：dull, dark, dim|词性: adjective, noun


Etymology:  Shortened from "fluorescent."  Ultimately from Latin "fluere" (to flow).
