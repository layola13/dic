impasse:/ˈɪmpæs/ (US), /ˈɪmpɑːs/ (UK)| n.|僵局，死胡同|A situation in which no progress is possible, especially because of disagreement; a deadlock.|The negotiations reached an impasse, and neither side was willing to compromise. 谈判陷入了僵局，双方都不愿妥协。|synonyms: deadlock, stalemate, standstill, gridlock|antonyms: breakthrough, agreement, solution|noun
