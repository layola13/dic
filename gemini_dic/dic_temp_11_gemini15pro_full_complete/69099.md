fauvism:/ˈfɔːvɪzəm/
n.
野兽派
An early 20th century movement in painting characterized by violent colors and bold distortions.
Fauvism, led by Matisse, revolutionized the use of color in modern art.  野兽派，由马蒂斯领导，彻底改变了现代艺术中色彩的运用。
Fauvism was a short-lived but influential movement. 野兽派是一个短暂但有影响力的运动。
Synonym:Expressionism,
Antonym:Realism
