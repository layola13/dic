bestirring:/bɪˈstɜːrɪŋ/
verb
激励；唤醒；使奋起
To rouse to action or exertion; stir up; agitate.
He finally bestirred himself to go to work. 他终于鼓起劲儿去上班。
近义词：rouse, awaken, energize, stimulate, activate
反义词：calm, soothe, pacify, quiet
词性：v.

Etymology:
From be- + stir.  "Be-" is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "around," "about," "on all sides," "thoroughly," and often intensifying the meaning of the verb it's attached to.  "Stir" comes from Old English *styrian, meaning "to move, agitate."
