dispassionately:/dɪsˈpæʃənɪtli/
adverb
冷静地；不动感情地
without emotion; in a calm and reasonable way
The judge listened dispassionately to both sides of the argument. 法官冷静地听取了双方的辩论。
She dispassionately assessed the situation. 她冷静地评估了局势。
近义词: calmly, objectively, impartially, coolly
反义词: emotionally, passionately, subjectively, heatedly
词性: adverb

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + passionate + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *passio*, suffering, from pati, to suffer.
