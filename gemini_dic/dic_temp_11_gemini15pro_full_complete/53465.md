disarmaments:/dɪsˈɑːrməmənts/| n.|裁军，解除武装（disarmament 的复数形式）|The disarmaments talks were a crucial step towards peace. 裁军谈判是迈向和平的关键一步.|近义词：arms reductions, demilitarization|反义词:armament, militarization|名词

词源：
From disarm + -ment.  Disarm comes from the Old French *desarmer*, from des- (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + armer (to arm), ultimately from Latin arma (arms, weapons).
