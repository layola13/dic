nutshell:/ˈnʌtˌʃɛl/ |n.| 坚果壳; 简洁的概要; 核心; 要点|The explanation was complex, but the presenter summarized it in a nutshell.  讲解很复杂，但演讲者将其概括为了要点。|Example: He explained the complex theory in a nutshell. 他简明扼要地解释了这个复杂的理论。|Synonyms: gist, summary, essence, core, crux|Antonyms: elaboration, detail, expansion|noun

nutshells:/ˈnʌtˌʃɛlz/ |n.| 坚果壳（复数）; 简洁的概要（复数）; 核心（复数）; 要点（复数）|The explanations were complex, but the presenter summarized them in a few nutshells.  讲解很复杂，但演讲者将其概括为了几个要点。|Example:  She presented the main arguments in a few nutshells. 她用几句话概括了主要论点。|Synonyms: gists, summaries, essences, cores, cruxes|Antonyms: elaborations, details, expansions|noun
