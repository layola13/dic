nonmonistically:/ˌnɒnˌmɒnɪˈstɪkəli/
adv.
非一元论地
in a way that is not monistic; in a way that rejects monism
The philosopher argued nonmonistically, emphasizing the duality of mind and body. 这位哲学家以非一元论的方式进行论证，强调了身心二元性。
Near Synonyms: pluralistically, dualistically
Antonyms: monistically
词性: 副词
