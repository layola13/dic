mgogo:/məˈɡoʊɡoʊ/ | n. | 祖母; 外祖母 (Especially in East Africa) | A term of endearment and respect for a grandmother or elderly female relative, primarily used in East Africa, particularly in Swahili and related Bantu languages. | Example: The children ran to greet their Mgogo when she arrived. 孩子们跑去迎接他们祖母的到来。| Example: Mgogo told us stories of her youth. 祖母给我们讲了她年轻时的故事。| Synonym: Grandmother, Nana, Grandma | Antonym: Grandson, Granddaughter | Noun
