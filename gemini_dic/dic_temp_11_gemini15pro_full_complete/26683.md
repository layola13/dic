cadaverously:/kəˈdævərəsli/| adv.|形容死人般地，惨白地，憔悴地|Resembling a corpse; pale, gaunt, and thin, as if from illness or weariness.  Suggesting the appearance or characteristics of a corpse.|He smiled cadaverously, his skin stretched tight over his cheekbones. 他惨白地笑了笑，皮肤紧紧地绷在颧骨上。|Synonyms: ghastly, deathly, pallid, haggard, gaunt, wan|Antonyms: healthy, robust, ruddy, vibrant|词性:副词
