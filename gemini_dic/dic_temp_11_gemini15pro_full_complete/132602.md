nondimensioned:/ˌnɒndaɪˈmɛnʃənd/ | adj. | 无量纲的 |  Having no physical dimensions assigned to it; expressed as a pure number or ratio.  It means the quantity is independent of any particular unit of measurement. | The Reynolds number is a nondimensioned quantity used in fluid mechanics. 雷诺数是流体力学中使用的无量纲量。 |  The ratio of wingspan to chord length is a nondimensioned parameter. 翼展与弦长之比是一个无量纲参数。| dimensionless, unitless | dimensioned | adjective
