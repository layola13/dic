irreprovable:/ˌɪrɪˈpruːvəbl/
adjective
无可指责的，无懈可击的
blameless; beyond reproach
His conduct was irreprovable. 他的行为无可指责。
近义词: blameless, impeccable, unimpeachable, faultless
反义词: culpable, reproachable, blameworthy
词性: adjective

irreprovable:/ˌɪrɪˈpruːvəbl/
词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French irreprovable, from reprover ‘reprove’.
