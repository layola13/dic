commonhold:/ˈkɒmənˌhəʊld/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmənˌhoʊld/ (US)| n.|共有产权|A system of property ownership where residents of a multi-unit building collectively own the freehold of the building and the land it stands on, and each resident owns a leasehold of their individual unit.|Example 1: The residents decided to convert their building to commonhold to have more control over its management. 共有产权的居民决定将他们的建筑改为共有产权，以便更好地控制其管理。

Example 2:  Commonhold offers an alternative to traditional leasehold ownership. 共有产权提供了一种传统租赁所有权的替代方案。|近义词:freehold (in some aspects), strata title (Australia), condominium (US)|反义词:leasehold|词性:名词
