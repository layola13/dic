itinerant:/aɪˈtɪnərənt/| adj. n.|巡回的，流动的；巡回者，流动人员|Traveling from place to place, especially to work; a person who travels from place to place, especially to work|例句：Itinerant preachers spread their message across the country.  巡回传教士在全国各地传播他们的信息。

|例句：The itinerant worker moved from farm to farm, harvesting crops. 流动的工人从一个农场搬到另一个农场，收割庄稼。|近义词：nomadic, wandering, traveling, roaming, peripatetic|反义词：settled, stationary, resident, fixed|词性:形容词, 名词

itinerants:/aɪˈtɪnərənts/| n.|巡回者，流动人员|People who travel from place to place, especially to work|例句：The itinerants set up camp by the river. 流动人员在河边扎营。


|例句：Many itinerants found work during the harvest season. 许多流动人员在收获季节找到了工作。|近义词：nomads, wanderers, travelers, roamers|反义词：residents, settlers|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *itinerantem (nominative itinerans), present participle of itinerari "to journey," from Latin iter "journey, road."
