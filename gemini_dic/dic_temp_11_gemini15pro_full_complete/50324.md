deplore:/dɪˈplɔːr/ /dɪˈplɔr/| vt. | 哀叹，强烈反对，谴责 | to feel or express strong disapproval of (something) | The president deplored the violence and called for calm. 总统谴责了暴力行为，并呼吁保持冷静。|  近义词：lament, bewail, condemn, criticize | 反义词：applaud, praise, commend | 词性: 动词

deplore:/dɪˈplɔːr/ /dɪˈplɔr/| vt. | 惋惜，深感遗憾 | to feel or express deep regret or sadness about (something) |  We deplore the loss of life in the recent earthquake. 我们对最近地震造成的生命损失深感遗憾。| 近义词：mourn, grieve, regret | 反义词：celebrate, rejoice | 词性: 动词
