freeways:/ˈfriːweɪz/
noun
高速公路
A broad highway designed for high-speed traffic, typically with multiple lanes and limited access.
We took the freeway to Los Angeles. 我们走高速公路去洛杉矶。
highways, expressways
roads, streets
