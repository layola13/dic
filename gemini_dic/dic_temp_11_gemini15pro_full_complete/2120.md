adaptationally:/ˌædæpˈteɪʃənəli/
adv.
适应地，顺应地
in a way that adapts or adjusts to changing circumstances.
例句：The company responded adaptationally to the changing market.  这家公司对不断变化的市场做出了适应性反应。

例句：Organisms evolve adaptationally to their environments.  生物会适应环境而进化。
近义词：flexibly, adjustably, responsively
反义词：rigidly, inflexibly, unadaptably
词性：副词
