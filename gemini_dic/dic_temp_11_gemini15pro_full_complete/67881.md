fabrile:/ˈfeɪbraɪl/ (US), /ˈfiːbraɪl/ (UK)| adj.|发热的，伴有发烧的|Characterized by or showing the symptoms of a fever.|例句：The patient presented with febrile convulsions. 病人出现高热惊厥。

例句：A febrile illness swept through the school. 一种高热疾病席卷了整个学校。

近义词：feverish, pyrexic|反义词:afebrile, apyretic|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *febrilis, from Latin febris (“fever”) + -ilis (adjective suffix).
