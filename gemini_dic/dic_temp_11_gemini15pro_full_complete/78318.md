gerontologies: /ˌdʒɛrənˈtɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 老年学 | The scientific study of old age, the process of aging, and the particular problems of old people. |  Many universities now offer degrees in gerontologies. 许多大学现在都提供老年学学位。|  Gerontology helps us understand the aging process. 老年学帮助我们理解衰老过程。| synonyms:  geriatrics, age studies | antonyms:  pediatrics, youth studies | noun
