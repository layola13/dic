denunciative:/dɪˈnʌnsiˌeɪtɪv/
adjective
谴责的，告发的
Expressing strong disapproval; condemning.
The senator delivered a denunciative speech against the proposed law.  参议员发表了谴责拟议法律的演讲。
His denunciative tone made it clear he disapproved of their actions. 他谴责的语气表明他不赞成他们的行为。

近义词:condemnatory, critical, accusatory, incriminatory
反义词:laudatory, complimentary, approving
词性:adjective
