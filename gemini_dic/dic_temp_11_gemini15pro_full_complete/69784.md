ferment:/fərˈment/ (US), /fəˈment/ (UK)| n. v.|发酵，酵素；使发酵，煽动|A process in which a substance breaks down into simpler substances, often producing gas; to cause or undergo fermentation; to incite or stir up (trouble or disorder).|The wine is fermenting in the cellar. 葡萄酒在地窖里发酵。  The unrest is fermenting rebellion among the people.  动荡正在民众中煽动叛乱。|近义词: brew, fester, incite, agitate|反义词: suppress, quell|n. v.

ferments:/fərˈments/ (US), /fəˈments/ (UK)| n. v. (plural)|发酵，酵素；使发酵，煽动|Plural form of "ferment."
