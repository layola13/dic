misrepresentation:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪzɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 歪曲；虚假陈述；误传 | the act of giving a false or misleading account of the nature of something. |  He accused the company of misrepresentation of the facts. 他指责公司歪曲事实。| synonyms: distortion, falsification, misstatement | antonyms: truth, accuracy, honesty | noun

misrepresent:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛnt/ | vt. | 歪曲；不如实地叙述；误传 | give a false or misleading account of the nature of something. |  The advertisement misrepresented the qualities of the product.  这则广告歪曲了产品质量。 | synonyms: distort, falsify, misstate | antonyms: portray accurately, represent truthfully | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From mis- + represent.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "incorrect." "Represent" comes from the Latin "repraesentare," meaning "to present again," "to exhibit," or "to portray."
