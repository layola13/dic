chessy:/ˈtʃiːzi/| adj.|俗气的，庸俗的；劣质的；不真诚的|Something that is cheesy is cheap, tacky, and obviously of poor quality or taste, often overly sentimental or dramatic.  It can also refer to something predictable or clichéd.|That romantic comedy was so cheesy, with predictable plot twists and over-the-top acting. 那部浪漫喜剧太俗套了，情节曲折都能猜到，演技也浮夸。|Synonyms: corny, tacky, clichéd, sentimental, mawkish|Antonyms: sophisticated, tasteful, refined, genuine|adjective
