grovet:/ˈɡroʊvɪt/| n.|小树林，小树丛|A small wood, orchard, or group of trees. |例句：We walked through the shady grovet. 我们穿过阴凉的小树林。

例句：The children played hide-and-seek in the apple grovet. 孩子们在苹果园里玩捉迷藏。


近义词：copse, thicket, wood, grove|反义词:field, plain|词性:名词
