malacozoic:/ˌmæləˈzoʊɪk/| adj.| 软体动物的；软体动物时代的 |Relating to or denoting the Mollusca, a large phylum of invertebrate animals (including snails, clams, and squids) with a soft unsegmented body usually enclosed in a shell.  Also refers to the Malacozoic Era, a hypothetical era proposed by some geologists to represent the time period dominated by mollusks. |The malacozoic fossils found in these rocks date back millions of years.  这些岩石中发现的软体动物化石可以追溯到数百万年前。| The museum has an extensive collection of malacozoic shells.  博物馆收藏了大量的软体动物贝壳。 |近义词：molluscan, molluscoid|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "vertebrate" in a broad sense) |词性: adjective
