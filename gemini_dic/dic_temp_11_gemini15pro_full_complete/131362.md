nonchronological:/ˌnɒnˌkrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˌkrɑːnəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非按时间顺序的
Not arranged in the order in which events happened.
The film uses a nonchronological narrative, jumping between different periods of the character's life. 这部电影采用非时间顺序的叙事方式，在角色人生的不同时期之间跳跃。
The events are presented in a nonchronological order, making the story a bit confusing.  这些事件的呈现方式并非按时间顺序排列，使得故事有点令人困惑。
近义词: anachronic, nonlinear
反义词: chronological, sequential
词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
chrono- (combining form): from Greek khronos "time"
-logical (suffix): from Greek -logikos "-ical," from logos "word, reason"
