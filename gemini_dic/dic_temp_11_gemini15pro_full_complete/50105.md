dentilingual:/ˌdɛntiˈlɪŋɡwəl/
adjective
齿舌的；与齿舌有关的
Relating to the radula, a toothed, ribbon-like structure used by mollusks for feeding.
Snails use their dentilingual apparatus to scrape algae off rocks.  蜗牛用它们的齿舌器官从岩石上刮下藻类。
The dentilingual ribbon is covered in tiny teeth. 齿舌带覆盖着微小的牙齿。
近义词: radular
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "smooth" or "jawed" in contrasting feeding mechanisms)
词性: adjective
