diffracted:/dɪˈfræktɪd/ | adj. | 衍射的，绕射的 |  Caused to spread out from a straight path, typically of waves of light or sound. | The diffracted light created a rainbow pattern. 衍射的光形成了彩虹图案。| synonyms: scattered, dispersed, spread, deviated | antonyms: focused, concentrated | adjective

diffract:/dɪˈfrækt/ | v. | （使）衍射，绕射| Cause (something) to change direction by interfering with its passage. | The prism diffracted the light beam. 棱镜使光束发生衍射。 | synonyms: deviate, deflect, bend, scatter | antonyms: focus, converge | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *diffractus*, past participle of *diffringere*, from *dis- "apart" + frangere "to break."
