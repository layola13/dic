hawai'is:/həˈwaɪiz/
n.
夏威夷群岛;夏威夷州
The Hawaiian Islands, or the state of Hawaii, located in the central Pacific Ocean.
We spent our honeymoon in the Hawai'is.  我们在夏威夷群岛度过了蜜月。
近义词:Hawaiian Islands
反义词: (无)
词性:名词


Etymology:
From Hawaiian *Hawaiʻi, from Proto-Polynesian *Sawaiki, meaning "homeland". The apostrophe represents a glottal stop, a consonant sound in the Hawaiian language.  The plural form is formed by adding "s".
