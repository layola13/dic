Example 2:  We saw a field full of kingcups on our walk. 我们散步时看到一片田野开满了金凤花。| Synonym: marsh marigold, buttercup | Antonym: N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *kinges cuppes*, from Old English *cyne-cuppe*, literally "king's cup," referring to the shape of the flower.
