checkroom: /ˈtʃɛkˌruːm/ (US), /ˈtʃekˌrʊm/ (UK) | n. |衣帽间；行李寄存处|A room where coats, bags, and other belongings can be left temporarily, typically in a public building such as a theatre, restaurant, or museum.|

例句：
Please leave your coat in the checkroom.
请把你的外套放在衣帽间。

例句：
The hotel has a checkroom for guests' luggage.
酒店有供客人存放行李的行李寄存处。

近义词：cloakroom, baggage room, left luggage office|
反义词: N/A |
词性: noun
