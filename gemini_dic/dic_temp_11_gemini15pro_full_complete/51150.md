despondingly:/dɪˈspɒndɪŋli/ /dɪˈspɑːndɪŋli/
adverb
沮丧地；失望地
In a way that shows you are very unhappy and without hope.

She shook her head despondingly. 她沮丧地摇了摇头。
He sat down heavily and stared despondingly out the window. 他重重地坐了下来，沮丧地盯着窗外。

近义词: despairingly, dejectedly, hopelessly, gloomily
反义词: cheerfully, hopefully, optimistically
词性: adverb
