overmeriting:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛrɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 过分应得的，奖励过度的 | Having more merit than is warranted or deserved; excessively rewarding.  | He felt the praise he received was overmeriting, considering his minimal contribution to the project. 他觉得鉴于他对这个项目的贡献微乎其微，他得到的赞扬有些过分。 | 近义词: undeserved, excessive, unwarranted | 反义词: deserved, merited, earned | 词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "meriting" (present participle of "merit," meaning "to deserve").  由表示“过度”的前缀 "over-" 和 "merit"（意为“应得”）的现在分词 "meriting" 组成。
