nebula:/ˈneb.jʊ.lə/ /ˈneb.jə.lə/ | n. |星云|A large cloud of gas and dust in space, spread out over a vast area, which is the birthplace of stars. |The Orion Nebula is a spectacular sight through a telescope.  猎户座星云透过望远镜看起来非常壮观。|The Horsehead Nebula is a dark nebula silhouetted against a brighter nebula. 马头星云是一个在更明亮的星云背景下剪影出的暗星云。| synonyms: cloud, galaxy (loosely) | antonyms: void, emptiness | noun
