amortizement:/əˌmɔːrtɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|摊销，分期偿还|The amortization of the loan will be spread over ten years.  这笔贷款将在十年内摊销。|Etymology: From Old French *amortissement, from amortir "to deaden, extinguish," from a- "to" + mort "dead."|例句：The company uses a straight-line method of amortization for its assets. 该公司对其资产采用直线摊销法。|近义词：depreciation, write-off|反义词: appreciation|词性:noun

amortization:/əˌmɔːrtɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|摊销，分期偿还|The amortization schedule shows the breakdown of principal and interest payments.  摊销计划表显示了本金和利息的支付细目。|Etymology: From Old French *amortissement, from amortir "to deaden, extinguish," from a- "to" + mort "dead."|例句：The amortization of the debt allowed the company to improve its cash flow.  债务的摊销使公司能够改善其现金流。|近义词：depreciation, write-off|反义词: appreciation|词性:noun
