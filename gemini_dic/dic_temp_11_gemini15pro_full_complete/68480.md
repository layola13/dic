familistic:/fæmɪˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
家庭的；家族主义的
Oriented or focused on the family or family values.  Emphasizing the importance of family relationships.
例句：They have a very familistic culture, where family ties are highly valued. 他们有一个非常家庭化的文化，家庭关系非常受重视。

例句：Her familistic values influenced her decision to stay close to home for college. 她的家庭价值观影响了她选择在家附近上大学的决定。

近义词：family-oriented, family-centered, close-knit
反义词：individualistic
词性: adjective
