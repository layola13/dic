colliquable:/kəˈlɪkwəbl/
adjective
可液化的，可熔化的
Capable of being liquefied or melted.
The scientist experimented with various colliquable substances.  这位科学家用各种可液化的物质做实验。
Molten lava is a colliquable rock. 熔岩是可液化的岩石。
Synonyms: fusible, meltable, liquefiable
Antonyms: infusible, non-meltable, solid, unmeltable
词性: adjective
