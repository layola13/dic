verb
胜过狩猎; 在狩猎中胜过
To have hunted more successfully than someone else.
例句：He outhunted his rivals and brought home the biggest deer. 他在狩猎中胜过他的对手，带回了最大的鹿。

例句：She consistently outhunted the other members of the expedition, securing vital food for the group.  她不断地在狩猎中胜过探险队的其他成员，为团队确保了重要的食物。


近义词：outdo, surpass, excel
反义词：be outdone, be surpassed
词性：动词
