brachiosaurus:/ˌbrækiəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |腕龙|A large dinosaur with a very long neck and forelegs, and a shorter tail and hindlegs.  It was a herbivore that lived during the Late Jurassic period.|The museum's centerpiece was a complete brachiosaurus skeleton.  博物馆的中心展品是一具完整的腕龙骨架。|  The brachiosaurus stretched its long neck to reach the leaves high in the trees. 腕龙伸长脖子去够树上的叶子。|近义词：sauropod|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

brachiosauruses:/ˌbrækiəˈsɔːrəsaɪz/| n. |腕龙（复数）|Plural form of brachiosaurus, referring to more than one of these dinosaurs.|Brachiosauruses were among the largest animals to ever walk the Earth. 腕龙是有史以来在地球上行走过的最大的动物之一。| Many brachiosauruses roamed the prehistoric plains. 许多腕龙在史前的平原上漫游。|近义词：sauropods|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
