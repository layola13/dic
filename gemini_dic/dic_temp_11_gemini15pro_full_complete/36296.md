coherent:/koʊˈhɪrənt/ /kəʊˈhɪərənt/| adj.|连贯的，一致的，条理清楚的|Sticking together; logical and consistent.|Her speech was coherent and easy to follow. 她的演讲条理清晰，易于理解。|Synonyms: logical, consistent, rational, reasoned, lucid, clear, intelligible, comprehensible|Antonyms: incoherent, illogical, rambling, disjointed|adjective

coherent:/koʊˈhɪrənt/ /kəʊˈhɪərənt/| adj.|团结的，凝聚的|(of a group) united and working together effectively.|They formed a coherent group dedicated to fighting for equality.  他们组成了一个团结一致的团体，致力于争取平等。|Synonyms: united, cohesive, solid, unified|Antonyms: divided, fragmented, disunited|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cohaerent-*, present active participle of *cohaerēre*  "to stick together," from *co-* "together" + *haerēre* "to stick."
