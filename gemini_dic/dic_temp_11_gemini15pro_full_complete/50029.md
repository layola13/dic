denominators:/dɪˈnɑːməˌneɪtərz/| n.|分母|The denominator of a fraction is the number below the line, which indicates how many parts the whole is divided into. 分数的分母是线下的数字，表示整体被分成多少部分。|3/4 -  4 is the denominator. 3/4 - 4是分母。| The common denominator for 1/2 and 2/3 is 6. 1/2和2/3的公分母是6。|近义词:divisor|反义词:numerator|词性:noun
