outdweller:/ˈaʊtˌdwɛlər/
noun
居住在城外的人；郊区居民
A person who dwells outside of a city or main residential area; a suburbanite.
Many outdwellers commute to the city for work. 许多居住在城外的人都到城里上班。
synonyms: suburbanite, commuter, exurbanite
antonyms: city dweller, urbanite
n.
