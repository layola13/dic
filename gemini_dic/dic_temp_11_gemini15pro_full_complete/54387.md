disillusionist: /ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənɪst/ | n. | 幻灭者；使人醒悟者 | someone who destroys someone else's beliefs in something good or worthwhile | The disillusionist exposed the corrupt practices of the company.  这位幻灭者揭露了公司的腐败行为。| synonyms: cynic, pessimist, skeptic | antonyms: idealist, optimist, dreamer | noun

disillusion: /ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒən/ | vt. | 使幻想破灭；使清醒 | to free from illusion or misconception | The harsh realities of war disillusioned many young soldiers. 战争的残酷现实使许多年轻士兵的幻想破灭。 | synonyms: disenchant, undeceive | antonyms: enchant, delude | verb
