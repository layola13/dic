halidom:/ˈhælɪdəm/| n.|圣物，圣地，神圣的东西|Something holy or sacred; a sanctuary.  It can also be used as an oath, similar to "by my faith".|例句：The king swore by the halidom of the crown. 国王以王冠的神圣起誓。

例句：The ancient halidom was a place of pilgrimage for centuries.  这座古老的圣地几个世纪以来一直是朝圣之地。|近义词：relic, shrine, sanctuary|反义词:profane object, unholy place|词性:noun
