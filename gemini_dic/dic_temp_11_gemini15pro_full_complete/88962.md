holidam:/ˈhɒlɪdəm/| n.|假期, 节日 (古语, 现已弃用)|A period of rest and recreation, especially one spent away from home.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. |例句：In olden days, the holidam was a time for feasting. 在古时候，假期是用来宴饮的。

例句：The holidam was declared by the king. 国王宣布了假期。|近义词：holiday, vacation, festival|反义词: workday|词性: 名词
