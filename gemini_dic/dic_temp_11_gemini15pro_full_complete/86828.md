hematomas:/hiːməˈtəʊməz/ (British) /ˌhiːməˈtoʊməz/ (American)| n. |血肿（复数）|Plural form of hematoma, referring to multiple localized swellings filled with blood.|After the accident, she had multiple hematomas on her legs and arms.|bruises, contusions| |noun|
