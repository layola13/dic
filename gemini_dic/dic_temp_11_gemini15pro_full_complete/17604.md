beautiless:/ˈbjuːtɪlɪs/
adjective
没有美丽的；缺乏魅力的；不美的
lacking beauty; unattractive
The beautiless concrete building loomed over the street.  这栋毫无美感的混凝土建筑矗立在街道上。
The furniture was functional but beautiless.  这些家具很实用，但毫无美感。
近义词:ugly, unattractive, plain, homely
反义词:beautiful, attractive, lovely, pretty
词性:adjective
