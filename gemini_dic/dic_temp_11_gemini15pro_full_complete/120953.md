mischannel:/ˌmɪsˈtʃæn(ə)l/
verb
误导；错误地引导
to direct something into a wrong channel or course; to misdirect.
The government mischanneled funds intended for education. 政府将用于教育的资金误用了。
misdirect, misuse, misapply
guide correctly, use properly
v.
