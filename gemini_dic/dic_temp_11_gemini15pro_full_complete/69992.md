fetched-up:/fɛtʃt ˈʌp/ | v. | 到达，结束，停止 | To arrive at a place, especially unexpectedly or after a long journey; to bring to a halt or standstill. | The ship fetched up on a deserted island after the storm.  这艘船在暴风雨后在一个荒岛上停了下来。 | arrive, end up, wind up | depart, leave | verb

fetched-up:/fɛtʃt ˈʌp/ | adj. |  (informal)  精疲力竭的，憔悴的 | Exhausted, worn out, looking unwell. | After walking for miles, she looked utterly fetched-up.  走了几英里后，她看起来精疲力竭。 | exhausted, worn out, haggard | refreshed, energetic | adjective
