appetizing:/ˈæpɪtaɪzɪŋ/| adj.|开胃的；诱人的；刺激食欲的|Making food look appetizing is an important part of cooking.  使食物看起来开胃是烹饪的重要组成部分。|例句：The aroma from the kitchen was very appetizing. 厨房飘来的香味很诱人。|近义词：tempting, alluring, enticing, mouthwatering|反义词：unappetizing, uninviting, off-putting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb *appetize*,  from French *appétissant* "appetising," present participle of *appétir* "to whet the appetite," from *ap-* (variant of *ad-*) "to" + Late Latin *appetītus* "natural desire," from Latin *appetere* "to strive for, desire," from *ad-* "to" + *petere* "to seek, go to, attack."
