canonizes:/ˈkænənaɪzɪz/
verb
正式宣布（死者）为圣徒；推崇，高度赞扬
officially declares (a dead person) a saint; regards as being above criticism or beyond question
The Church canonized him in the 15th century. 教会在15世纪将他册封为圣徒。
The critics have canonized her first novel.  评论家们对她的小说推崇备至。
synonyms: sanctify, deify, exalt, glorify
antonyms: condemn, denounce, vilify
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin canonizare "to put into the canon of the saints," from Latin canon (see canon).
