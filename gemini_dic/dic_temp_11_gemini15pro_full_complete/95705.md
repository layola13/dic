incongealable:/ˌɪnkənˈdʒiːləbl/
adjective
不能冷冻的，不会凝固的
Not capable of being congealed or solidified by cold.
Certain oils are incongealable even at very low temperatures.  某些油即使在非常低的温度下也不会凝固。
The substance proved to be incongealable, frustrating the scientists' attempts to solidify it.  这种物质被证明是不能凝固的，这让科学家们试图使其凝固的努力受挫。
近义词: non-freezing, unfreezable
反义词: freezable, congelable
词性: adjective
词源:  From in- (not) + congelable (capable of being congealed), from congeal (to solidify), ultimately from Latin congelare (to freeze together).
