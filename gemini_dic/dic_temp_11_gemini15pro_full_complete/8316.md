anti-papist:/ˌæntiˈpeɪpɪst/ | n. | 反天主教者 | A person who is opposed to the Catholic Church. | The anti-papist circulated pamphlets denouncing the Pope.  这个反天主教者散发小册子谴责教皇。 | Synonyms: anti-Catholic | Antonyms: Catholic, papist | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + papist.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to."  "Papist" is a derogatory term for a Catholic, derived from "papacy," referring to the office and authority of the Pope.
