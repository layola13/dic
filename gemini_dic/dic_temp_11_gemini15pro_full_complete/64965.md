estiferous:/esˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
炎热的，夏季的
Relating to summer or characterized by summer weather; hot.
The estiferous weather made it difficult to work outdoors. 炎热的天气使得户外工作变得困难。
The dog days of August are truly estiferous. 八月的三伏天真是炎热。
近义词: summery, hot, sweltering, torrid
反义词: wintry, cold, frigid, freezing
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin aestivus "of summer," from aestus "heat," related to aestas "summer."  The "-ferous" suffix comes from Latin -fer "bearing, producing," from ferre "to bear, carry."  Thus, estiferous literally means "heat-bearing" or "summer-bearing."
