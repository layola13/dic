highhats:/ˈhaɪˌhæts/| n.|镲片（尤指爵士鼓中的一对）|A pair of small cymbals mounted on a stand, played with a foot pedal, especially in a drum kit. |例句：The drummer's highhats kept a steady rhythm throughout the song.  鼓手的镲片在整首歌中保持着稳定的节奏。

例句：He accented the beat with a quick tap on the highhats. 他用快速敲击镲片的方式来强调节拍。|近义词：cymbals|反义词: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument)|词性:noun
