abashed:/əˈbæʃt/| adj.|尴尬的，羞愧的|Feeling or caused to feel uneasy and self-conscious.|She was abashed by his unexpected praise. 她被他突如其来的赞扬弄得不好意思。|近义词：embarrassed, ashamed, mortified, humiliated|反义词：unembarrassed, confident, composed|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *esbahir "to astonish," from Vulgar Latin *exbadare "to gape at," from Latin ex- "out" + badare "to gape."  The meaning shifted from "astonished" to "disconcerted" and then to "ashamed."
