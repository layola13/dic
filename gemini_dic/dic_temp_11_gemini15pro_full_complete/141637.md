optimates:/ˈɒptɪˌmeɪts/ /ˈɑːptɪˌmeɪts/
noun
贵族，最优者，元老院中保守派
The aristocratic or conservative faction in the Roman Senate.  They typically supported the traditional political institutions and the interests of the established elite.
The optimates resisted the reforms proposed by the populares. 贵族们反对平民派提出的改革。
Cicero was generally aligned with the optimates. 西塞罗通常与贵族派结盟。
synonyms: aristocrats, nobles, conservatives
antonyms: populares, reformers, radicals
