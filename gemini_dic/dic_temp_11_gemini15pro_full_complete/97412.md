infringes:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒɪz/
verb
侵犯，违反
act so as to limit or undermine (something); encroach on.
His use of the image infringes copyright. 他对该图片的使用侵犯了版权。
synonyms: contravene, violate, breach, break, transgress, disobey, defy, flout
antonyms: comply with, obey, uphold, observe
词性: 动词
