majapahit:/ˌmɑːdʒəˈpɑːhɪt/| n.|满者伯夷|An ancient Javanese Hindu-Buddhist kingdom that flourished from the late 13th century to the early 16th century. It is considered one of the greatest and most powerful empires in the history of Indonesia and Southeast Asia.|The Majapahit Empire controlled much of what is now Indonesia. 满者伯夷帝国控制了现在印尼的大部分地区。

Majapahit art and architecture are still admired today. 满者伯夷的艺术和建筑至今仍受人赞赏。|近义词：empire (帝国), kingdom (王国)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
