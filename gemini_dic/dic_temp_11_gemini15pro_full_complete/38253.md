comrade:/ˈkɒm.ræd/ (UK), /ˈkɑːm.ræd/ (US)| n.|同志，伙伴|A person who shares one's activities or is a fellow member of an organization, especially a political one.|例句：He greeted his comrades with a warm embrace. 他热情地拥抱了他的同志们。

例句：The comrades fought side by side in the war.  这些同志们在战争中并肩作战。


近义词：companion, friend, colleague, associate, partner|反义词：enemy, opponent, foe|词性:noun
