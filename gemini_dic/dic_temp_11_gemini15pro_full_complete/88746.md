hockday:/ˈhɒkdeɪ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːkdeɪ/ (US)| n. |欢庆日，租地人向地主缴纳租金的日子，旧时一种勒索钱财的节日 |A day of festivity or tribute, historically a day on which tenants paid rent to their landlords, or a day associated with a now obsolete practice of extorting money. |The hockday festivities were a highlight of the village year.  村庄一年中最重要的时刻就是欢庆日庆祝活动。

The lord of the manor collected rents on hockday.  庄园领主在欢庆日收租。|近义词：holiday, festival, celebration |反义词: workday |Noun
