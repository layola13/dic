incommunicado:/ˌɪnkəˌmjuːnɪˈkɑːdoʊ/ (US) /ˌɪnkəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪdoʊ/ (UK)| adj. adv.|无法通讯的，隔离的|Not able to communicate with others; in isolation, especially when deliberately prevented from doing so.|The hostages were held incommunicado for several weeks. 人质被隔离了好几个星期，无法与外界联系。|例句：He was held incommunicado by the police. 他被警方隔离审讯，无法与外界联系。|例句：She went incommunicado for a week while on vacation. 她度假时一个星期都与外界失联了。|近义词：isolated, secluded, inaccessible, unapproachable|反义词：communicative, accessible, reachable|词性:形容词/副词

词源:
From Spanish *incomunicado*, from Latin *in-* (not) + *communicatus* (shared, communicated), past participle of *communicare* (to share, communicate).
