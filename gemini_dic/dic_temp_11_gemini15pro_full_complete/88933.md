holding:/ˈhoʊldɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhəʊldɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |持有物，股份；财产；支持；保留|Something that is held or owned, especially land or shares in a company.  |例句：The company's holdings include several valuable properties.  这家公司的资产包括几处很有价值的房产。|近义词：possession, asset, property|反义词:debt, liability|词性:noun

holding:/ˈhoʊldɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhəʊldɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |保持的；固定的|Keeping something in place. |例句：The holding pattern of the airplane circled the airport. 飞机以盘旋的航线在机场上空盘旋。|近义词：supporting, retaining|反义词:releasing|词性:adjective
