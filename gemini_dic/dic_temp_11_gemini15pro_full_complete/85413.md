harvestable:/ˈhɑːrvɪstəbl/ /ˈhɑːrvəstəbəl/| adj.|可收获的，可收割的|Ready to be harvested; mature enough to be gathered or collected. |The apples are harvestable in October. 苹果在十月份可以收获。|近义词：ripe, mature, ready|反义词:immature, green, unripe|词性:形容词
词源: From "harvest" + "-able". "Harvest" comes from Old English "hærfest" meaning "autumn".
