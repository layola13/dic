cover-ups:/ˈkʌvərˌʌps/ | n. | 掩盖；隐瞒；遮掩物 | an attempt to prevent something illegal or embarrassing from becoming known |  The company tried a cover-up of the safety violations.  这家公司试图掩盖违反安全规定的行为。 | synonyms: concealment, disguise, whitewash | antonyms: revelation, exposure | noun

cover-ups:/ˈkʌvərˌʌps/ | n. | 遮盖物；掩盖物 | something that is used to cover something else | She wore a cover-up over her swimsuit.  她在泳衣外面穿了一件罩衫。 | synonyms: veil, cloak, screen | antonyms: unveiling, uncovering | noun
