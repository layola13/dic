deputy:/ˈdepjʊti/ (US), /ˈdɛpjʊti/ (UK)| n. |代理人，副手，代表|A person appointed to act for or in place of another.|The sheriff's deputy arrested the suspect.  治安官的副手逮捕了嫌疑人。|Synonyms: assistant, substitute, representative, agent|Antonyms: principal, chief, leader|noun
