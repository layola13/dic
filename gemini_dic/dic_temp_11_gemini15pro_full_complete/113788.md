macroural:/ˈmækroʊərəl/| adj.|长尾的；大尾的|Having a long or large tail.  Relating to or characteristic of macrourans (long-tailed decapod crustaceans, especially lobsters, shrimps, and prawns).|例句：Macroural crustaceans are an important part of the marine ecosystem. 长尾甲壳类动物是海洋生态系统的重要组成部分。

例句：The macroural morphology of the shrimp allows it to swim efficiently.  虾的长尾形态使其能够有效地游泳。|近义词：long-tailed|反义词: short-tailed|词性:adjective
词源: From Greek *makrós* "long" + *oura* "tail"
