inbreedings:/ˈɪnˌbriːdɪŋz/
n.
近亲繁殖，同系交配
the act of breeding from closely related people or animals, especially over many generations.
Excessive inbreedings can lead to genetic defects. 过度的近亲繁殖会导致遗传缺陷。

近义词:inbreeding, interbreeding
反义词:outbreeding, crossbreeding
词性:名词(复数)

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- + breeding, from breed (v.).  The noun is first attested 1866.
