chaudron:/ˈʃoʊdrən/ (US), /ˈʃɔːdrən/ (UK)| n. |大锅; 大汽锅|A large metal pot or kettle, typically cauldron-shaped, used for cooking over an open fire.|The witches brewed a potion in their cauldron. 女巫们在大锅里煮药水。|例句：

The soup simmered in the large chaudron.
汤在大锅里煨着。

The chef stirred the stew in the chaudron with a long ladle.
厨师用长勺在大锅里搅拌炖菜。

|近义词：cauldron, kettle, pot|反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical object.  Perhaps "plate" or "bowl" if contrasting serving vessels.)|词性: noun
