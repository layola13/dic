anfractuose:/ænˈfræktʃuəs/ | adj. | 蜿蜒的，曲折的；错综复杂的 |  Characterized by winding and intricate passages or paths.  Having many twists and turns. | The anfractuose canyons made navigation difficult. 蜿蜒的峡谷使得航行变得困难。 |  sinuous, tortuous, convoluted, labyrinthine | straight, direct | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *anfractuosus, from Latin anfractus (“a bending, a curve, a circuit”), from anfrangere, from ambi- (“around”) + frangere (“to break”).
