outhammered:/ˌaʊtˈhæmərd/
verb
以锤击胜过; 锤击得比...更响亮
outperform or surpass someone in hammering or something involving forceful repetitive action.
He outhammered his opponent in the blacksmithing competition. 他在铁匠比赛中以锤击胜过了他的对手。
The rhythmic drumming outhammered the sound of the rain.  有节奏的鼓声盖过了雨声。
synonyms: outdo, surpass, outperform, excel, beat, defeat, triumph over, conquer
antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性:动词
