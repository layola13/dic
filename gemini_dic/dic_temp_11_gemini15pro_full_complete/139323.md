oboes:/ˈoʊboʊz/ (US), /ˈəʊbəʊz/ (UK)| n. |双簧管（复数）|A double-reed woodwind instrument with a conical bore and a distinctive, somewhat nasal timbre. It is a staple of the orchestra and is also used in chamber music and solo performances.|The oboes played a beautiful melody. 双簧管演奏了一段优美的旋律。|Synonyms: (none for the instrument itself - could list other woodwinds like 'clarinets', 'flutes')|Antonyms: (N/A - no direct opposite for a musical instrument)|Noun
