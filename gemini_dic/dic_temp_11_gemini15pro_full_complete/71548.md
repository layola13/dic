flash's:/flæʃɪz/ | n. | 闪光；闪现；一瞬间 | flash's is the possessive form of "flash," meaning something belonging to or associated with a flash.  | The flash's brightness blinded me momentarily. 那道闪光的光亮让我一时失明了。 | synonyms: glimmer's, flicker's | antonyms: darkness's, gloom's | noun
