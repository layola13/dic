PHRASE->
approach to (something)  ...的方法/途径  Her approach to the problem was unique. 她解决这个问题的方法很独特。
be approaching  即将来临  Winter is approaching. 冬天快到了。
make an approach  进行接洽  He made an approach to the company for a job. 他与该公司接洽，想找一份工作。
approach with caution  谨慎处理  Approach the situation with caution.  谨慎处理这种情况。
soft approach  软性方法  A soft approach is often more effective. 软性方法通常更有效。
