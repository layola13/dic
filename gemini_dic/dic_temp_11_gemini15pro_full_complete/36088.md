coello:/koˈeʎo/ (Spanish pronunciation)| n.|兔子; (informal) clever person; |A rabbit; (informally, especially in Spain) a clever or skillful person. |例句：Vi un coello en el jardín. 我在花园里看到一只兔子。

例句：Ese chico es un coello para las matemáticas.  那孩子数学很厉害。|近义词：conejo (rabbit), listo (clever), hábil (skillful)|反义词:torpe (clumsy), tonto (stupid)|词性:noun

注意事项:

* "Coello" is primarily used in Spain.  In other Spanish-speaking countries, "conejo" is the standard word for rabbit.
* The informal meaning of "coello" as a clever or skillful person is not universally understood across all Spanish-speaking regions.  It's more common in Spain.
*  The pronunciation provided is a general Castilian Spanish pronunciation.  Variations may exist in other Spanish dialects.
