makaron:/ˌmækəˈrɒn/ | n. | 杏仁蛋白饼 | A small, round cookie made with egg whites, sugar, and ground almonds or coconut, often sandwiched together with buttercream, ganache, or jam. |  Example:  The bakery is famous for its colorful and delicious makarons. 这家面包店以其色彩缤纷、美味的杏仁蛋白饼而闻名。

Example: She brought a box of assorted makarons as a gift. 她带了一盒什锦杏仁蛋白饼作为礼物。 | 近义词: macaroon | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
