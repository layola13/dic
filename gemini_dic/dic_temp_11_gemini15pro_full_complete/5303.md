allots:/əˈlɒts/ (UK), /əˈlɑːts/ (US)| v. |分配，拨给| To distribute or apportion something between or among people or things. | The government allots funds for education. 政府拨款用于教育。| Example: The teacher allots each student 15 minutes to present their project. 老师给每个学生分配15分钟来展示他们的项目。| Synonyms: assign, allocate, distribute, apportion | Antonyms: withhold, keep, retain | verb

allot:/əˈlɒt/ (UK), /əˈlɑːts/ (US)| n. |分配额，份额 (通常指土地)| A portion or share of something allocated to someone or something.  | He received a generous allot of land. 他分到了一大块土地。| Synonyms: share, portion, allowance, quota | Antonyms: whole, entirety| noun
