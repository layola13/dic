heuristic:/hjʊˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 启发式的；探索式的 |  Enabling a person to discover or learn something for themselves. | Example:  He employed a heuristic approach to solving the problem. 他采用了一种启发式的方法来解决这个问题。
Example: Heuristic methods are often used in computer science. 启发式方法常用于计算机科学。 | 近义词: exploratory, investigative, trial-and-error | 反义词: algorithmic, systematic | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *heuriskein* "to find, discover."
