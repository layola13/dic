lichenographical:/ˌlaɪkənəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
地衣学的
Relating to the study of lichens.
Lichens are composite organisms that arise from algae or cyanobacteria living among filaments of multiple fungi species in a mutualistic relationship.  The combined lichen has properties different from those of its component organisms.
例句：The lichenographical society held its annual meeting last month. 地衣学会上个月举行了年会。

例句：His lichenographical research focuses on the symbiotic relationships between fungi and algae. 他的地衣学研究重点是真菌和藻类之间的共生关系。
