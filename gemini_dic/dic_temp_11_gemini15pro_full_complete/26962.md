calcification:/ˌkælsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |钙化（单数）|The process of hardening by depositing calcium salts, or the resulting hardened tissue or deposit.|The calcification of the artery can lead to serious health problems. 动脉钙化会导致严重的健康问题。|Synonyms: hardening, ossification, mineralization|Antonyms: softening, demineralization|noun
