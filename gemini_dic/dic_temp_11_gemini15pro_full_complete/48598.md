defected:/dɪˈfɛktɪd/
adjective
已叛逃的，有缺陷的，有瑕疵的
Having abandoned a cause or duty; deserted. Having a flaw or imperfection.
Several party members defected to the opposition. 一些党员叛逃到了反对党。
This product is defected and needs to be returned. 这个产品有缺陷，需要退货。
近义词:rebellious, flawed, imperfect, faulty
反义词:loyal, perfect, flawless
词性:adjective

defected:/dɪˈfɛktɪd/
verb (past participle of defect)
叛逃，脱离
Past participle of the verb "defect," meaning to abandon a cause or duty.
He had defected from the army. 他已经从军队叛逃了。
The software engineer defected to a competing company.  这个软件工程师跳槽到了一家竞争公司。
近义词:deserted, abandoned, apostatized
反义词:joined, supported
词性:verb (past participle)


词源:
From Latin defectus, past participle of deficere "to fail, desert, abandon," from de- "down, away" + facere "to do, make."
