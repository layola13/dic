expositionary:/ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃəneri/
adjective
说明性的，解释性的
serving to expound or set forth; explanatory
The professor delivered an expositionary lecture on the history of philosophy.  教授做了一个关于哲学史的解释性讲座。
近义词:explanatory, elucidative, illustrative, interpretive
反义词:unclear, confusing, ambiguous
词性:adjective
