despairers:/dɪˈspeərərz/
n.
绝望者，感到绝望的人
People who have lost all hope.
The despairers sat huddled together, waiting for the inevitable. 绝望者们挤在一起坐着，等待着不可避免的事情发生。
The refugees, many of them despairers, had lost everything. 许多难民都陷入了绝望，他们失去了一切。
近义词:pessimists, defeatists
反义词:optimists, hopers
词性:名词

despair:/dɪˈspeər/
n.
绝望；令人绝望的人或事
The complete loss or absence of hope.
A feeling of profound sadness and hopelessness.
He sank into despair after losing his job.  他失业后陷入了绝望。
The news filled them with despair. 这个消息使他们充满了绝望。
近义词:hopelessness, despondency
反义词:hope, optimism
词性:名词

despair:/dɪˈspeər/
v.
绝望；丧失信心
Lose or be without hope.
She despaired of ever finding a job. 她对找到工作感到绝望。
He despaired at the thought of failing the exam. 他一想到考试不及格就感到绝望。
近义词:lose hope, give up
反义词:hope, be optimistic
词性:动词

词源：
From Old French *desperer, from Latin dēspērāre (“to be without hope, despair”), from dē- (“without”) + spērāre (“to hope”).
