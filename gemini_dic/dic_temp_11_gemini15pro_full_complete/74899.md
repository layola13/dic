freshmanship: /ˈfrɛʃmənˌʃɪp/
n.
大学一年级生的身份；大学一年级新生期间
The state or period of being a freshman.
He struggled with homesickness during his freshman year.  他在大学一年级时与思乡作斗争。
近义词: freshman year, first year
反义词: senior year, final year
词性: 名词

Etymology:  From freshman + -ship.  Freshman itself comes from fresh (new) + man.  The suffix "-ship" denotes a state or condition of being.
