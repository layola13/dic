extinct:/ɪkˈstɪŋkt/ | adj. | 灭绝的，绝种的；熄灭的；不再活跃的 | No longer existing or active; having died out. | The dodo is an extinct bird. 渡渡鸟是一种已灭绝的鸟类。| The volcano is now extinct. 这座火山现在已经熄灭了。 | 近义词: vanished, defunct, died out, obsolete | 反义词: extant, existing, living, surviving | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *extinctus*, past participle of *extinguere* "to extinguish, quench, put out, destroy," from *ex* "out" + *stinguere* "to quench."
