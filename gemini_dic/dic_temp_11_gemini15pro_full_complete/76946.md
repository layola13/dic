gappiest:/ˈɡæpiɪst/| adj.|最快乐的，最兴高采烈的|Superlative form of "gappy" (informal): feeling or showing great happiness and excitement.  It expresses a higher degree of happiness than "happy" or even "very happy."|例句：She had the gappiest grin I'd ever seen. 她露出了我见过的最灿烂的笑容。

例句：The children were gappiest on Christmas morning. 孩子们在圣诞节早上是最快乐的。

|近义词：jolliest, merriest, most cheerful, most joyful|反义词：saddest, most miserable, most unhappy|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "gappy" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Gappy" likely originates from "gap" (Old Norse "gap" - opening), possibly referring to a wide smile revealing gaps between teeth, thus expressing joy.
