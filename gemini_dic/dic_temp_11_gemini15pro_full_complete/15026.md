baboo:/bəˈbuː/
n.
奉承者，马屁精
A person who behaves obsequiously, especially to someone in authority, typically in order to gain an advantage.
He's a real baboo to the boss, always agreeing with everything he says. 他对老板真是个马屁精，总是同意他所说的一切。
sycophant, flatterer, yes-man
critic, opponent

词性: noun
