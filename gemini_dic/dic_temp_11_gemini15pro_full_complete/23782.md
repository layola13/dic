brawn:/brɔːn/
noun
体力；肌肉；强壮
Physical strength; muscular power; sturdiness.
He's got plenty of brawn, but not much brain. 他很有力气，但是脑子不太灵光。
近义词: strength, muscle, might, power
反义词: weakness, frailty, feebleness
词性: n.

Etymology: From Old French *braon, from a Germanic source, probably Frankish *brago "shoulder, upper arm." Related to Old English brǣgn "brain."  The connection between "muscle" and "brain" may be due to the idea of the head as the strongest part of the body.
