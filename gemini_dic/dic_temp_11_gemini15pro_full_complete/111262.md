litigiously:/lɪˈtɪdʒəsli/
| adv. |好诉讼地；爱打官司地|in a litigious manner; inclined to engage in lawsuits. |例句：He is known for behaving litigiously, always ready to take legal action. 他以好诉讼的行为而闻名，总是准备采取法律行动。

例句：She responded litigiously to the slightest provocation. 她对最轻微的挑衅都反应激烈，动辄诉诸法律。|近义词：contentiously, argumentatively, quarrelsomely |反义词: amicably, peaceably |词性:副词
