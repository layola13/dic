ominous: /ˈɑːmɪnəs/ (US), /ˈɒmɪnəs/ (UK)| adj.|不祥的，预兆的|Giving the impression that something bad or unpleasant is going to happen; threatening; inauspicious.|例句：The ominous clouds suggested a storm was coming.  不祥的乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。

例句：There was an ominous silence before the explosion. 爆炸前是一片不祥的寂静。

近义词：threatening, menacing, foreboding, sinister, portentous|反义词:auspicious, promising, propitious|词性:adjective
