measuredness:/ˈmeʒərdnɪs/

ETYMOLOGY->
From *measured* + *-ness*.  *Measured* comes from the verb *measure*, from Old French *mesurer*, from Latin *mēnsūra* ("measure"). The suffix *-ness* forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
