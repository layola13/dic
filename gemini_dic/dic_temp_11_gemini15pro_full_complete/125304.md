multiglazed:/ˌmʌltiˈɡleɪzd/
adjective
多层玻璃的
Having multiple layers of glass, typically for insulation or soundproofing.
The multiglazed windows kept the house warm in the winter and cool in the summer. 多层玻璃窗在冬天保暖，夏天凉爽。
double-glazed, triple-glazed
single-glazed
adjective
