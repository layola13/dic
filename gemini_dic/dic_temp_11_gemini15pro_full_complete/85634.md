词源：From Old French *hanche (Modern French hanche), from Frankish *hanka, from Proto-Germanic *hankō. Cognate with Dutch hank.


注意事项：haunches 通常指动物或人的后腿及臀部区域，而 haunch 则指单侧的臀部或后腿。
