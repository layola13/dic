bullfights:/ˈbʊlˌfɑɪts/ | n. | 斗牛 | A public spectacle, especially in Spain, Portugal, and Latin America, in which a bullfighter on foot, usually with a cape and sword, baits and kills a bull. | Many animal rights activists protest against bullfights. 许多动物权利活动家抗议斗牛。|  The bullfights in Pamplona are famous worldwide. 潘普洛纳的斗牛闻名世界。| 近义词: corrida | 反义词:  None (No direct antonym for the spectacle itself. One might consider "animal welfare event" as a contrasting concept.) | 词性: noun
