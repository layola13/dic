muskox:/ˈmʌskɒks/ (US) /ˈmʌskɒks/ (UK)| n. |麝牛|A large, shaggy-haired Arctic mammal (Ovibos moschatus) with a musky odor, inhabiting Greenland and the Canadian Arctic.  It has short legs, a long, thick coat, and curved horns.|Muskoxen are well adapted to the harsh Arctic climate. 麝牛非常适应严酷的北极气候。|Near Synonyms: ovibos|Antonyms: None|Noun


muskoxen:/ˈmʌskɒksən/ (US) /ˈmʌskɒksən/ (UK)| n. |麝牛（复数）|Plural of muskox.|A herd of muskoxen protected themselves from wolves by forming a defensive circle. 一群麝牛围成防御圈保护自己免受狼的攻击。|Near Synonyms: oviboses|Antonyms: None|Noun
