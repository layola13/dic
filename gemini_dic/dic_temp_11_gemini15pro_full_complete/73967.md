fortifying:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
增强体力的;使人振奋的;坚固的
making something stronger, especially in order to protect it.  Also, making someone feel stronger physically or mentally.
The news was fortifying. 这消息令人振奋。
Synonyms: strengthening, invigorating, enriching, bolstering
Antonyms: weakening, debilitating, undermining
词性: adjective
