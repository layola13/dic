enduros:/ɪnˈdjʊəroʊz/ /ɛnˈdjʊəroʊz/
n.
耐力赛，耐力越野赛
A long-distance race, typically for motorcycles or mountain bikes, over rough terrain.
He competed in several enduros last year.  他去年参加了几场耐力赛。
近义词:  rally, race, competition
反义词: sprint
词性: 名词

enduro:/ɪnˈdjʊəroʊ/ /ɛnˈdjʊəroʊ/
n.
耐力赛，耐力越野赛
A long-distance race, typically for motorcycles or mountain bikes, over rough terrain.
The enduro was a grueling test of both rider and machine. 这场耐力赛是对车手和赛车的严峻考验。
近义词: rally, race, competition
反义词: sprint
词性: 名词


词源:  Shortened from *endurance run*. Ultimately from French *endurer* "to endure," from Latin *indurare* "to harden, make hard."
