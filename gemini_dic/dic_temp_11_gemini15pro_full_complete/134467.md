nonjuridically:/ˌnɒnˌdʒʊrɪˈdɪkli/
adverb
非司法地；在法律之外
Outside of legal proceedings or considerations; not legally binding.
The agreement was made nonjuridically, with a handshake.  这个协议是非司法地达成的，只是握了手而已。
The issue was settled nonjuridically between the two parties.  这个问题是由双方在法律之外解决的。
Synonym: extrajudicially, informally
Antonym: judicially, legally
词性: 副词
