legworks: /ˈleɡwɜːrks/
n.
腿部动作；腿功；（尤指舞蹈中的）腿部技巧
Footwork, especially in dance or other physical activities requiring skillful use of the legs.
The dancer's legworks were incredibly intricate and captivating.  这位舞者的腿功复杂精妙，引人入胜。
Her legworks in the tango were particularly impressive.  她在探戈中的腿部动作尤其令人印象深刻。
近义词: footwork, steps
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider stillness, inactivity)
词性: noun


词源:  A compound word formed from "leg" and "works," where "works" refers to the movements or actions performed.  "Leg" comes from Old Norse "leggr." "Work" comes from Old English "worc."
