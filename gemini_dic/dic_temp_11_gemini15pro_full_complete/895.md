abuna:/ɑːˈbuːnə/ | n. |  阿布纳（埃塞俄比亚正教会主教的头衔）|  A title given to the head of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church.  It is derived from the Ge'ez word meaning "our father". |  The Abuna traditionally resided in Gondar.  阿布纳传统上居住在贡德尔。

The current Abuna is Mathias.  现任阿布纳是马蒂亚斯。 | Synonym: patriarch, metropolitan | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps subordinate clergy) | noun
