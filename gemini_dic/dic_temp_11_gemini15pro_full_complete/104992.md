kenneled:/ˈkɛnəld/ |v. |被关进狗舍；被禁闭|placed or kept in a kennel; confined|The dogs were kenneled during the storm. 狗在暴风雨期间被关在狗窝里。|The prisoners were virtually kenneled in their tiny cells. 囚犯们实际上被关在他们的小牢房里。|近义词：confined, caged, penned|反义词：released, freed|词性:动词

词源: From kennel + -ed, the past participle suffix.  The noun "kennel" comes from Old Northern French *kenil, from Latin canile "dog house," from canis "dog."
