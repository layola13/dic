nonemergencies:/ˌnɒnɪˈmɜːdʒənsiz/ | n. | 非紧急情况 | Situations that do not require immediate or urgent attention.  |  The hospital asks that people with nonemergencies visit their doctor instead of the emergency room. 医院请求非紧急情况的病人去看他们的医生，而不是去急诊室。 |  Non-emergency calls can be made to the local police station. 非紧急情况可以打电话给当地警察局。 | synonyms: non-urgencies, routine matters | antonyms: emergencies, crises | noun
