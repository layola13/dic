orthogon:/ˈɔːrθəɡɒn/ (UK) /ˈɔːrθəɡɑːn/ (US)| n.|正交向量，正交函数|A line or plane that is at right angles to another; a function that is orthogonal to another. |The two vectors are orthogon to each other. 这两个向量互相正交。|Synonyms: perpendicular, normal |Antonyms: parallel, oblique |noun

orthogonal:/ɔːˈθɒɡənəl/ (UK) /ɔːrˈθɑːɡənəl/ (US)| adj.|正交的，直角的|Relating to or involving right angles; statistically independent.|The x and y axes are orthogonal. x轴和y轴是正交的。|Synonyms: perpendicular, normal, unrelated, independent |Antonyms: parallel, oblique, related, dependent |adjective
