immature:/ˌɪməˈtʃʊr/ /ˌɪməˈtʊr/| adj.|不成熟的，幼稚的|Not fully developed or grown; behaving in a childish way.|例句：He's a bit immature for his age. 他年纪不小了，但有点幼稚。|近义词：childish, juvenile, undeveloped|反义词：mature, grown-up, adult|词性:形容词
immaturity:/ˌɪməˈtʃʊrəti/ /ˌɪməˈtʊrəti/| n.|不成熟，幼稚|The state of not being fully developed or grown; childish behavior.|例句：His immaturity is starting to annoy me. 他的幼稚开始让我恼火了。|近义词：childishness, juvenility|反义词：maturity, adulthood|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *immaturus, from in- ‘not’ + maturus ‘ripe.’
