implying:/ɪmˈplaɪɪŋ/
verb
暗示，意味着
suggesting something indirectly rather than stating it directly
"Are you implying I'm fat?" she asked.  “你在暗示我胖吗？”她问。
synonyms: suggesting, hinting, intimating
antonyms: stating, declaring, specifying
词性: 动词 (present participle of "imply")
