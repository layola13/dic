overcleverness: /ˌoʊvərˈklɛvərnəs/
noun
过度聪明；自作聪明
The state of being too clever, often resulting in counterproductive or overly complicated solutions.  Being excessively clever, often to the point of being detrimental or annoying.
His overcleverness led him to devise a plan so intricate that it ultimately failed.  他过度聪明，导致他设计了一个如此复杂的计划，最终失败了。
Her overcleverness made her unpopular with her colleagues. 她自作聪明，使得她在同事中不受欢迎。
synonyms:  cunning,  craftiness,  deviousness,  guile,  slyness,  artfulness,  contrivance,  scheming,  outfoxing,  outsmarting
antonyms:  simplicity,  straightforwardness,  artlessness,  naiveté,  ingenuity,  guilelessness
n.
