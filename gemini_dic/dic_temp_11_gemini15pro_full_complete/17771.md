bedlamite:/ˈbedləˌmaɪt/| n.|疯子，精神病人|A person who is insane or wildly chaotic; a lunatic.|The streets were filled with bedlamites during the riot. 骚乱期间，街上到处都是疯子。|近义词：lunatic, madman, maniac, insane person|反义词：sane person|词性:noun

bedlamite:/ˈbedləˌmaɪt/| adj.|疯狂的，混乱的|Characterized by wild disorder and chaos.|The party descended into a bedlamite scene of drunken revelry. 派对沦为一场疯狂的醉酒狂欢。|近义词：chaotic, frantic, frenzied|反义词：calm, orderly, peaceful|词性:adjective

Etymology: From Bedlam, the popular name for the Hospital of St. Mary of Bethlehem in London, a former lunatic asylum.
