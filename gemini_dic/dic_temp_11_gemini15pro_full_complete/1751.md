acquired:/əˈkwaɪərd/ | adj. | 已获得的，已习得的 | Obtained, learned, or developed through experience, practice, or effort.  |  He has acquired a good knowledge of French. 他法语学得很好。 | Synonyms: obtained, gained, learned, developed | Antonyms: lost, forfeited, innate, inherent | adjective

acquired:/əˈkwaɪərd/ | vt. |  获得，学到 |  To gain or obtain something, typically by one's own efforts or actions. | She acquired a taste for fine wine during her travels. 她在旅途中培养了对美酒的品味。| Synonyms: obtain, gain, earn, achieve, attain | Antonyms: lose, forfeit, surrender | verb
