naturopaths:/ˈneɪtʃəroʊpæθs/| n. |自然疗法医师；自然疗法医生|A naturopath is a practitioner of naturopathy, a system of alternative medicine based on the theory that diseases can be successfully treated or prevented without the use of drugs, by techniques such as diet, exercise, and massage.|I consulted a naturopath for my chronic headaches. 我咨询了一位自然疗法医生治疗我的慢性头痛。|Synonyms: naturopathic physician, naturopathic doctor, holistic health practitioner|Antonyms: conventional doctor, allopathic physician|Noun
