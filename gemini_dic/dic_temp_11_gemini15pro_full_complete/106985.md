laconic: /ləˈkɒnɪk/ (UK) /ləˈkɑːnɪk/ (US)| adj. | 简洁的；言简意赅的 | Using few words; expressing much in few words; concise.  |  She gave a laconic shrug. 她耸了耸肩，简短地表示了一下。 | Synonyms: brief, concise, terse, succinct, pithy | Antonyms: verbose, prolix, loquacious, garrulous | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *Lakōnikos*, "of Lakonia,"  referring to the Spartans, inhabitants of Lakonia, who were known for their brief and pithy speech.
