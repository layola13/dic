hindenburg: /ˈhɪndənbɜːrɡ/ | n. | 興登堡 |  Refers to Paul von Hindenburg, a German field marshal and statesman, or the LZ 129 Hindenburg, a German passenger airship that caught fire and was destroyed in 1937. | The Hindenburg disaster marked the end of the airship era. 興登堡空難標誌著飛艇時代的終結。| synonym: Zeppelin (for the airship) | antonym: N/A | noun

hindenburg: /ˈhɪndənbɜːrɡ/ | adj. | 興登堡式的 | Relating to or characteristic of Paul von Hindenburg or the Hindenburg disaster, often implying a sense of impending doom or disaster.  | The project had a Hindenburg-like feel to it, with everyone sensing its imminent failure. 這個項目有一種興登堡式的感覺，每個人都感覺到它即將失敗。| synonym: disastrous, ill-fated | antonym: auspicious, fortunate | adjective
