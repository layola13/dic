danubian:/ˌdænˈjuːbiən/ | adj. n.| 多瑙河的；多瑙河流域的；多瑙河流域居民 |Relating to or characteristic of the Danube River or the region through which it flows; a native or inhabitant of the Danube region. |The danubian countries have a rich history. 多瑙河流域国家有着悠久的历史。| The danubian waltz is a popular dance form. 多瑙河华尔兹是一种流行的舞蹈形式。 | 近义词:Danube River, Central European (in some contexts) | 反义词: None | 词性: 形容词, 名词


Etymology:
From Latin *Danubius* (the river Danube), from earlier *Danuvius*, likely of Celtic origin.
