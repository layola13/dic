continuum:/kənˈtɪnjuːəm/| n. |连续统一体，连续统，闭联集|a continuous sequence in which adjacent elements are not perceptibly different from each other, although the extremes are quite distinct|The spectrum of visible light is a continuum.|近义词：spectrum, range, scale, progression, sequence, series, succession, chain, stream, flow|反义词：discontinuity, break, interruption|noun
