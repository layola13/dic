apiarist:/ˈeɪpiərɪst/ | n. |养蜂人；养蜂家|A person who keeps bees, especially for commercial production of honey.|例句：The apiarist carefully inspected each hive for signs of disease. 养蜂人仔细检查每个蜂箱，寻找疾病的迹象。

例句：My grandfather was an apiarist and sold honey locally. 我的祖父是一位养蜂人，在当地出售蜂蜜。|近义词：beekeeper, |反义词:| noun
