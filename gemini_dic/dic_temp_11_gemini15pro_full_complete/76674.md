galvanizing:/ˈɡælvənaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 镀锌；激励；刺激 | The process of coating iron or steel with a protective layer of zinc. |  The bridge underwent galvanizing to prevent rust.  这座桥进行了镀锌以防止生锈。| synonyms: coating, plating, energizing, stimulating | antonyms:  weakening, discouraging, depressing | noun

galvanize:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/ | vt. | 镀锌；激励；刺激 | To coat (iron or steel) with a protective layer of zinc. To shock or excite someone, typically into taking action. | The news galvanized the public into action. 这消息激励了公众采取行动。| synonyms: coat, plate, energize, stimulate | antonyms: weaken, discourage, depress | verb

galvanized:/ˈɡælvənaɪzd/ | adj. | 镀锌的；受到激励的 | Coated with zinc; stimulated into action. | The galvanized steel is resistant to rust. 镀锌钢耐腐蚀。| synonyms: coated, plated, energized, stimulated | antonyms: uncoated, unprotected, unmotivated | adjective
