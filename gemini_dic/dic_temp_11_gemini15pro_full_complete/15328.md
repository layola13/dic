backslide:/ˈbækslaɪd/| vi.|倒退，故态复萌|To revert to a worse state, especially after a period of improvement.  To relapse into bad habits or error.|After months of progress, he backslid into his old ways. 几个月取得进步后，他又回到了老样子。|近义词：relapse, regress, revert, retrogress|反义词:progress, advance, improve|verb

backsliding:/ˈbækslaɪdɪŋ/| n.|倒退，故态复萌|The act of backsliding; a relapse into bad habits or error.|His backsliding into drug use worried his family. 他再次吸毒让家人很担心。|近义词：relapse, regression, reversion, retrogression|反义词:progress, advancement, improvement|noun
