illustriousnesses:/ɪˌlʌstriəsnəsiz/
n.
杰出，卓越，辉煌
the state or quality of being illustrious; eminence; renown.
The illustriousnesses of the Nobel Prize winners were celebrated worldwide. 诺贝尔奖获得者的杰出成就举世闻名。
近义词：eminence, distinction, renown, fame, greatness
反义词：obscurity, insignificance, mediocrity
词性：名词

词源: From illustrious + -ness (suffix forming nouns of quality or state) + -es (plural suffix)  Ultimately from Latin illustris "bright, clear, luminous," figuratively "renowned, famous, excellent," from in- "in, on" + lustrare "make bright" (from PIE root *leuk- "light, brightness").
