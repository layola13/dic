integumentary:/ɪnˌtɛɡjʊˈmɛntəri/
adjective
外皮的；覆盖体被的
Relating to or forming a natural outer covering, such as skin, hair, or a shell.
The integumentary system protects the body from the external environment.  外皮系统保护身体免受外部环境的侵害。

The skin is the largest organ of the integumentary system. 皮肤是外皮系统中最大的器官。

synonym: dermal, cutaneous
antonym: internal, visceral
