flameproofs:/ˈfleɪmpruːfs/
noun
防火材料，阻燃剂
Materials used to make something flameproof.
The curtains were treated with flameproofs.  这些窗帘经过了防火处理。
近义词:fire retardants, flame retardants
反义词:flammable materials
词性:n.


flameproof:/ˈfleɪmpruːf/
adjective
防火的，阻燃的
Resistant to catching fire.
These overalls are flameproof.  这些工作服是防火的。
近义词:fire-resistant, fireproof
反义词:flammable, inflammable
词性:adj.


flameproof:/ˈfleɪmpruːf/
verb
使…防火，使…阻燃
To make something flameproof.
They flameproofed the children's pyjamas. 他们给孩子们的睡衣做了防火处理。
近义词:fireproof, fire-retard
反义词:ignite
词性:v.
