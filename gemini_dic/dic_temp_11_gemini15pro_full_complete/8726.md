antidandruff:/ˌæntiˈdændrəf/| adj.|去头皮屑的，防头皮屑的|Designed to prevent or treat dandruff.  A product that helps eliminate or reduce dandruff flakes on the scalp.|例句：I use antidandruff shampoo. 我使用去屑洗发水。|近义词：anti-dandruff, dandruff-fighting|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'dandruff-causing' but not commonly used) |词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + dandruff.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Dandruff" comes from  the Old English "tænder" (tender)  + "drif" (to drive, expel).
