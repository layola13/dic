mameluco:/ˌmɑːməˈluːkoʊ/ (US), /ˌmæməˈluːkəʊ/ (UK)| n.|连体衣，工作服，婴儿服|A one-piece garment, typically for infants or young children, or a loose-fitting overall worn as work clothing.|例句：The baby looks adorable in his little mameluco.  婴儿穿着他的小连体衣看起来很可爱。

例句：The mechanics wore mamelucos to protect their clothes. 机械工穿着工作服来保护他们的衣服。|近义词：jumpsuit, coverall, onesie, romper|反义词: separates (指分开的上衣和裤子)|词性:noun
