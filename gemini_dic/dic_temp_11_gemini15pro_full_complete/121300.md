misfit: /ˈmɪsfɪt/ | n. | 不适应环境的人，不称职的人 | A person who is not suited to their social environment or who is regarded as eccentric or unconventional. | He was a social misfit in high school. 他在高中时是一个不合群的人。| eccentric, nonconformist, outcast | conformist, insider | noun
