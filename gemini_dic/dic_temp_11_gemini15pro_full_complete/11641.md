armet: /ˈɑːrmɪt/ | n. | 头盔，盔甲 | A type of helmet that enclosed the entire head, popular in the late Middle Ages and Renaissance.  It was often fitted with a visor that could be raised or lowered. | The knight wore a gleaming armet into battle.  骑士戴着闪亮的盔甲头盔上战场。| synonyms: helmet, helm, casque | antonyms: None | noun
