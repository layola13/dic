despising:/dɪˈspaɪzɪŋ/
Verb (present participle)
轻视，鄙视
Feeling contempt or a deep repugnance for.
He spent his life despising those who had wronged him. 他一生都在鄙视那些冤枉他的人。
Near synonyms:  loathing, abhorring, detesting, hating, scorn
Antonyms:  admiring, respecting, revering
verb
