cephalate:/ˈsɛfəleɪt/
adjective
有头的；头状的
Having a head or head-like structure.
Cephalate mollusks are a diverse group.  有头软体动物是一个多样化的群体。
Many insect larvae are cephalate. 许多昆虫幼虫是有头的。
synonym: headed
antonym: headless
part of speech: adjective


cephalate:/ˈsɛfəleɪt/
noun
有头动物
An animal possessing a head.
The cephalate moved slowly across the ocean floor.  这种有头动物在海底缓慢移动。
Cephalates are more complex than organisms without a distinct head.  有头动物比没有明显头部的生物更复杂。
synonym: none (more specific terms like "vertebrate" might be used depending on the context)
antonym: none
part of speech: noun

Etymology:
From Greek *kephalē*  (head) + -ate.
