inharmoniousness:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmoʊniəsnəs/ | n. |不和谐，不协调|Lack of harmony; discordance.  The state or quality of being inharmonious.  It refers to a lack of agreement or compatibility, resulting in a sense of conflict or dissonance. |The inharmoniousness of their voices made the song unpleasant to listen to. 他们的声音不和谐，使得歌曲听起来很不舒服。|近义词：discord, disharmony, dissonance, conflict, incongruity|反义词：harmony, concord, agreement, consonance, compatibility|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + harmonious + -ness (noun suffix).  "Harmonious" comes from Late Latin harmoniosus, from Greek harmonikos, from harmonia "joint, agreement, concord of sounds,".
