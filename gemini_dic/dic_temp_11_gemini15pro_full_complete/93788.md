immensurate:/ɪˈmenʃərət/ /ɪˈmenʃʊrət/| adj.|不能相比的；不相称的；不相应的|Not proportionate; of unequal measure; not matching; too large or too small in comparison with something else.|His salary is incommensurate with his experience. 他的薪水与他的经验不相称。|The task is incommensurate with her abilities. 这项任务与她的能力不符。|近义词：disproportionate, inadequate, unequal, incompatible|反义词：commensurate, proportionate, adequate, suitable|词性:形容词
