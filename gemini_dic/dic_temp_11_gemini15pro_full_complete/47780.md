词源:
From French décanter, from dé- (expressing removal) + cant (edge, side), from Latin cantus. The original meaning was to pour off gently, as if tilting a container only to its edge.
来自法语 décanter，由 dé-（表示移除）+ cant（边缘，侧面）组成，源自拉丁语 cantus。最初的意思是轻轻地倒出，就像只将容器倾斜到边缘一样。
