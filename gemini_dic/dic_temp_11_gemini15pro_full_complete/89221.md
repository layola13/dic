homeworking:/ˈhoʊmwɜːrkɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhəʊmwɜːkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|在家工作的|Relating to the practice of working from home.|She has a homeworking arrangement with her employer. 她和她的雇主达成了在家工作的协议。|近义词：telecommuting, remote|反义词：office-based|词性:形容词
