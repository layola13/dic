drinkless:/ˈdrɪŋkləs/
adjective
不喝酒的;滴酒不沾的
Not drinking alcohol; abstaining from alcohol.
He's been drinkless for five years. 他已经五年滴酒不沾了。
近义词:teetotal, abstinent, sober
反义词:drinking, alcoholic
词性:adjective
