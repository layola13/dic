lactimide:/lækˈtaɪmaɪd/ | n. | 乳交酯 | A cyclic diester of lactic acid.  It is the monomer used to produce polylactic acid (PLA), a biodegradable plastic. |  Lactide is a key intermediate in the production of PLA. 乳交酯是生产聚乳酸的关键中间体。

Lactide can exist as different stereoisomers. 乳交酯可以以不同的立体异构体存在。 | 近义词: (As a monomer for PLA) dilactide | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
