affiliated: /əˈfɪlieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 附属的，隶属的 |  Connected with or controlled by a larger organization. | Our local club is affiliated with a national organization. 我们的地方俱乐部隶属于一个国家组织。|  The college is affiliated with the university.  这所学院隶属于这所大学。 | associated, connected, related | independent, separate | adjective

词源:
From Latin *affiliatus*, past participle of *affiliare* "to adopt as a son," from *ad-* "to" + *filius* "son."
