deprecators:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪtərz/
n.
反对者，弃用者
Those who express disapproval of something or someone, or advocate for something to be discontinued.
Many deprecators of the old system welcomed the new software. 许多旧系统的反对者都欢迎新软件。
The deprecators of the proposed law organized a protest.  提议法律的反对者组织了一场抗议。
critics, objectors, dissenters
supporters, proponents, advocates
noun


deprecate:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪt/
v.
反对，弃用，轻视
To express disapproval of; to plead against; to represent as of small value.
The teacher deprecated the use of cell phones in class. 老师反对在课堂上使用手机。
We deprecate violence of any kind. 我们反对任何形式的暴力。
criticize, condemn, denounce
approve, praise, commend
verb
