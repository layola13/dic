fabrication:/ˌfæbrɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 制造；捏造；伪造物 | The act of constructing something, or something constructed; a lie or falsehood. |  The report was a complete fabrication. 这份报告完全是捏造的。| synonyms: construction, manufacture, invention, falsehood, lie | antonyms: truth, reality, fact | noun

fabricate:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪt/ | v. | 制造；捏造；伪造 | To construct or manufacture something; to invent a lie or falsehood. | He fabricated a story about being robbed. 他编造了一个关于自己被抢劫的故事。| synonyms: construct, manufacture, invent, forge, falsify | antonyms: dismantle, destroy, expose, uncover | verb
