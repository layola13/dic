overloosely:/ˌoʊvərˈluːsli/
adverb
过于松散地; 过于宽松地
in a way that is too loose
The bandage was overloosely applied and soon came undone.  绷带缠得太松了，很快就散开了。
loosely, slackly
tightly, firmly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + loosely.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much".  "Loosely" derives from the Old English word "lēas," meaning "free from restraint."
