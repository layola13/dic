extrapolating:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪtɪŋ/
verb
推断；外推
Estimating or concluding something by assuming that existing trends will continue or a current method will remain applicable.
Scientists are extrapolating from the current birth rate to predict the population in 2050. 科学家们根据目前的出生率来预测2050年的人口数量。
Synonyms: infer, deduce, project, conjecture
Antonyms: interpolate


extrapolate:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪt/
verb
推断；外推
To infer or estimate by extending or projecting known information.
The company extrapolated the sales figures for the next quarter based on the current trends.  公司根据目前的趋势推断下个季度的销售数据。
Synonyms: infer, deduce, project, conjecture
Antonyms: interpolate
