industrious:/ɪnˈdʌstriəs/| adj.|勤奋的，勤勉的|Characterized by hard work and perseverance.|例句：She is an industrious student who always completes her assignments on time. 她是一位勤奋的学生，总是按时完成作业。

例句：The industrious ants worked tirelessly to build their hill. 勤劳的蚂蚁不知疲倦地建造它们的蚁丘。


近义词：hardworking, diligent, assiduous, sedulous|反义词:lazy, idle, indolent, slothful|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *industriōsus, from Latin industria (“diligence”) + -ōsus.
