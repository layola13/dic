miniconglomerate: /ˌmɪni kənˈɡlɒmərət/ (UK) /ˌmɪni kənˈɡlɑːmərət/ (US) | n. | 小型企业集团，微型企业集团 | A small conglomerate, a company that is diversified into a number of different, often unrelated businesses, but on a smaller scale than a large conglomerate. |  Example 1: The entrepreneur built a miniconglomerate through a series of shrewd acquisitions.  这位企业家通过一系列精明的收购建立了一个小型企业集团。

Example 2:  The miniconglomerate faced challenges managing its diverse portfolio of businesses.  这个小型企业集团在其多元化的业务组合管理方面面临挑战。 | Near synonyms: small conglomerate, diversified company, holding company | Antonym: specialized business | Noun
