interstimulating:/ˌɪntərˈstɪmjʊleɪtɪŋ/
adjective
互相激励的；互相刺激的
Causing mutual stimulation or excitement.  Engaging or interesting to each other.
The conversation was interstimulating, with both parties bouncing ideas off each other.  这场对话是互相激励的，双方都互相交流想法。
The interstimulating workshop encouraged participants to share their experiences and learn from one another.  这个互相激励的研讨会鼓励参与者分享经验并互相学习。
近义词: mutually stimulating, mutually inspiring, synergistic
反义词: monotonous, boring, dull
词性: adjective

词源:
"inter-" (prefix): between, among, mutually. From Latin "inter"
"stimulating" (present participle of "stimulate"): encourage or arouse interest or enthusiasm in. From Latin "stimulare" "to prick, urge on"
