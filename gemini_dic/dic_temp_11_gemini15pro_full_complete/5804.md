amaryllis:/ˌæməˈrɪlɪs/| n. |孤挺花；朱顶红|Amaryllis refers to a genus of flowering bulbs, most commonly referring to the species *Hippeastrum*, known for their large, showy trumpet-shaped flowers.|The amaryllis is a popular winter-blooming plant.  朱顶红是受欢迎的冬季开花植物。|The amaryllis bulbs I planted are starting to sprout. 我种的孤挺花球茎开始发芽了。|近义词：hippeastrum|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *amaryssion*, from Greek *ἀμαρύσσω* (amarýssō, “to sparkle, glitter”)
