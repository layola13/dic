misdeclare:/ˌmɪsdɪˈkleər/
verb
错误地申报；虚报
to declare something incorrectly, especially on official documents.
The company misdeclared its profits to the tax authorities.  该公司向税务机关虚报了利润。
The goods were misdeclared as "toys" to avoid paying higher import duties. 这些货物被错误地申报为“玩具”以避免支付更高的进口税。
misstate, falsify
declare correctly, state accurately
v.
