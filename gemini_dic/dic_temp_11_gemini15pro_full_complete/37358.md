commensalism:/kəˈmɛnsəlɪzəm/| n. |共生，共栖|A symbiotic relationship between two organisms of different species in which one derives some benefit while the other is neither harmed nor benefited.|Barnacles growing on whales is an example of commensalism. 藤壶生长在鲸鱼身上是共生的一个例子。|近义词：symbiosis, mutualism (sometimes)|反义词:parasitism, amensalism|noun
