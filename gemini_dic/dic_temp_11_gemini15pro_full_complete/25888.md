burlier: /ˈbɜːrliər/ (US), /ˈbɜːlɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更强壮的，更结实的|Comparative form of burly: more burly.  Meaning stronger and more heavily built.|He was burlier than his brother, with broader shoulders and thicker arms. 他比他哥哥更强壮，肩膀更宽，胳膊更粗。| The burlier of the two men lifted the heavy crate with ease.  两个男人中较强壮的那位轻松地举起了沉重的板条箱。|近义词：stronger, sturdier, huskier, beefier|反义词：weaker, slighter, thinner, frailer|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *borlich, from Old English *burlic (“noble, excellent, stately”), equivalent to burly +‎ -er. Cognate with Scots burlie (“stately, large”), German bürlich (“noble, excellent, stately”).
