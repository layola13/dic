cyanided: /ˈsaɪənaɪdɪd/ | adj. | 氰化的，用氰化物处理过的 | Treated with cyanide. | The cyanided tailings were stored in a pond. 氰化尾矿被储存在一个池塘里。 | The gold ore was cyanided to extract the gold. 金矿石用氰化物处理以提取黄金。 | 近义词: cyanide-treated | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "untreated" or "purified") | 词性: adjective

词源:  From cyanide + -ed.  Cyanide itself comes from cyan (blue), referring to Prussian blue, a pigment from which cyanide can be obtained.
