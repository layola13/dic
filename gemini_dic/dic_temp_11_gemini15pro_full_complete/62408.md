endogenicity:/ˌɛndoʊdʒəˈnɪsɪti/ |n.|内生性|The quality of being generated within a system or organism.|例句：The endogenicity of the hormone allows for precise regulation of its effects.  这种激素的内生性使其作用能够得到精确的调节。

例句：The study explored the endogenicity of the growth factor in the tumor cells.  这项研究探讨了肿瘤细胞中生长因子的内生性。|近义词：inherence, innateness, intrinsicness|反义词：exogenicity, external origin|词性:noun
