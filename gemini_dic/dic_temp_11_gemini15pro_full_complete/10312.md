appeacher:/əˈpiːtʃər/| n.|弹劾者，检举人|A person who impeaches someone, especially a public official.|例句：The appeachers presented their case against the judge. 弹劾者陈述了他们对法官的指控。

例句：The appeachers sought to remove the president from office. 弹劾者试图罢免总统的职务。|近义词：accuser, prosecutor, complainant|反义词：defender, advocate|词性:noun
