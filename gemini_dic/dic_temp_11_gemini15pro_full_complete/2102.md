adamical:/əˈdæmɪkəl/
adj.
亚当的; 人类始祖的; 天真的; 纯洁的;
Of or relating to Adam, the first man according to the Bible; characteristic of the innocence and simplicity attributed to Adam.
例句：The painting depicted an adamical scene of paradise. 这幅画描绘了天堂般纯洁的景象。

例句：His adamical nature made him easily trusting of others. 他天真的本性使他很容易相信别人。

近义词：pristine, innocent, pure, uncorrupted
反义词：corrupt, sinful, worldly, sophisticated
词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Adam + -ical.  Adam, from Hebrew אָדָם (ādām, “man; mankind”).  -ical, suffix forming adjectives from nouns, from Latin -icalis, from Greek -ικος (-ikos).
