ideation:/ˌaɪdiˈeɪʃən/
n.
构思; 创意; 观念形成
The process of forming ideas or concepts.
The marketing team held a brainstorming session for ideation on the new product.  市场营销团队就新产品进行了头脑风暴以形成创意。
The ideation phase of the project is crucial for its success.  项目的构思阶段对其成功至关重要。

近义词:conception, innovation, brainstorming, generation of ideas
反义词:lack of ideas, stagnation

词性: noun
