outsatisfied: /ˌaʊtˈsætɪsfaɪd/
Verb
超过…的满意度; 比…更满意
To satisfy more than someone or something else.
He outsatisfied his customers with exceptional service. 他以卓越的服务让顾客比预期更满意。
The new restaurant outsatisfied the competition with its innovative menu. 新餐厅以其创新的菜单比竞争对手更能满足顾客。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词: disappoint, underperform
词性: verb
