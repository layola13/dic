intelligibilities: /ɪnˌtɛlɪdʒɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
可理解性；清晰度
the quality of being intelligible; understandability.
The intelligibilities of the recordings varied depending on the background noise.  这些录音的可理解度因背景噪音而异。
