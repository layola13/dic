embosses:/ɪmˈbɒsɪz/ /ɪmˈbɑːsɪz/
verb
凸印，压印
to decorate (a surface) with raised ornament
例句：The company embosses its logo on all its stationery.  该公司在其所有文具上都压印了公司标志。

例句：The leather cover of the book was embossed with gold lettering. 书的皮革封面用金色字体压印。
近义词：raise, stamp, mold, carve
反义词：flatten, smooth
词性：v.


emboss:/ɪmˈbɒs/ /ɪmˈbɑːs/
noun
凸印，浮雕
a raised design or piece of ornament produced by embossing
例句：The emboss on the coin was worn smooth.  硬币上的浮雕图案被磨平了。

例句：The invitation had a delicate emboss of flowers.  请柬上有精美的花卉浮雕。
近义词：relief, ornamentation
反义词：indentation
词性：n.
