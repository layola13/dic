amphipod:/ˈæmfɪpɒd/ (US) /ˈæmfɪpɑːd/| n. |片脚类动物|A small crustacean with a laterally compressed body, typically found in marine and freshwater environments.  They are characterized by their lack of a carapace and their different shaped legs, some for swimming and some for walking.|例句：The amphipod scuttled across the seabed. 片脚类动物在海底快速移动。

例句：Amphipods are an important part of the marine food chain.  片脚类动物是海洋食物链的重要组成部分。|近义词：crustacean, scud|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek *amphípodos*, from *amphí* "on both sides" + *poús* "foot".
