cataleptic:/ˌkætəˈlɛptɪk/| adj.| 僵直性昏厥的，紧张性精神分裂症的|Relating to or resembling catalepsy, a condition characterized by muscular rigidity and a trance-like state of consciousness.|The patient remained in a cataleptic state for several hours. 病人保持僵直状态几个小时。|Synonyms: rigid, stiff, immobile, trance-like|Antonyms: flexible, mobile, responsive|adjective

catalepsy:/ˈkætəˌlɛpsi/| n.| 僵直性昏厥，紧张性精神分裂症|A medical condition characterized by a trance or seizure with a loss of sensation and consciousness accompanied by rigidity of the body.|Catalepsy can be a symptom of certain neurological disorders.  僵直性昏厥可能是某些神经系统疾病的症状。|Synonyms: rigidity, stupor, trance|Antonyms: flexibility, responsiveness|noun
