graffiti: /ɡrəˈfiːti/ (US), /ɡræˈfiːti/ (UK)| n. |涂鸦，graffiti art 涂鸦艺术|Writings or drawings scribbled, scratched, or sprayed illicitly on a wall or other surface in a public place.|The walls were covered in graffiti.|Synonyms: street art, mural, tagging, scribbling|Antonyms: clean wall, blank canvas|Noun
