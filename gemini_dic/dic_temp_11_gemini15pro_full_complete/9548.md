antiseptic:/ˌæntiˈsɛptɪk/ | n. |防腐剂，抗菌剂 |A substance that prevents the growth of disease-causing microorganisms.| The nurse used an antiseptic to clean the patient's skin. 护士用防腐剂清洁病人的皮肤。|Synonyms: disinfectant, germicidal, antimicrobial | Antonyms: contaminant, pollutant | noun
