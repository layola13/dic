donatist:/ˈdɒnətɪst/ /ˈdoʊnətɪst/| n. |多纳图斯派信徒；多纳图斯主义者 |A follower of Donatus, a 4th-century bishop of Carthage who believed that the sacraments were invalid if administered by clergy who had lapsed during persecution. |The Donatists refused to acknowledge the authority of the Church in Rome. 多纳图斯派拒绝承认罗马教会的权威。| 近义词：schismatic, heretic | 反义词：orthodox | 词性：noun

donatist:/ˈdɒnətɪst/ /ˈdoʊnətɪst/| adj. |多纳图斯派的；多纳图斯主义的|Relating to Donatism, a schismatic Christian sect in North Africa in the 4th-5th centuries. |The Donatist controversy weakened the Church in North Africa. 多纳图斯派的争议削弱了北非的教会。| 近义词：schismatic, heretical | 反义词：orthodox | 词性：adjective
