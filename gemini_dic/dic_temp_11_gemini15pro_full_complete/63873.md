epileptogenous:/ɪˌpɪlɛpˈtɒdʒɪnəs/| adj.|致痫的，诱发癫痫的|Causing or tending to cause epilepsy.  Derived from "epilepto-" (relating to epilepsy) + "-genous" (producing or causing).|Certain drugs can be epileptogenous in susceptible individuals.  某些药物在易感人群中可能致痫。| Stimulating certain areas of the brain can be epileptogenous.  刺激大脑的某些区域可能会诱发癫痫。| epileptogenic, convulsant | anticonvulsant | adjective
