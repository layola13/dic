investigation:/ɪnˌvɛstɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |调查，研究|The police launched a full investigation into the robbery. 警方对这起抢劫案展开了全面调查。|例句：The investigation into the cause of the fire is ongoing. 对火灾原因的调查正在进行中。  The team conducted a thorough investigation of the market. 该团队对市场进行了彻底的调查。|近义词：inquiry, probe, examination, research, study|反义词:cover-up, concealment|词性:noun

词源:
From Late Latin investigatio, from investigare "to track, trace out," from in- "into" + vestigare "to track, trace," from vestigium "footprint, trace."
