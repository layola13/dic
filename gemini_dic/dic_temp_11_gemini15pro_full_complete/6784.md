anaphoric:/ˌænəˈfɒrɪk/ | adj. | 指代的，回指的 |  Relating to or of the nature of anaphora, especially in grammar; referring back to something that has already been mentioned. | The pronoun "he" is anaphoric in the sentence "John said he was tired."  约翰说他累了。（这里的“他”指代前面提到的“约翰”） | synonyms: referential, backward-referring | antonyms: cataphoric, forward-referring | adjective
