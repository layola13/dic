ameliorants:/əˈmiːliərənts/| n.|改良剂，改进剂|Substances added to soil to improve its physical properties, such as water retention, aeration, and drainage. |The farmer used soil ameliorants to improve the texture and fertility of his land.  这位农民使用土壤改良剂来改善他土地的质地和肥力。|Synonyms: conditioners, additives, enhancers|Antonyms: pollutants, contaminants|noun
