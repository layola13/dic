diorama:/ˌdaɪəˈrɑːmə/ /ˌdaɪəˈræmə/| n.|立体模型；透视画|A three-dimensional miniature or life-size scene, often enclosed in a glass showcase, typically showing historical events, nature scenes, or fictional settings.|The museum's diorama of the Battle of Gettysburg was incredibly detailed.  博物馆关于葛底斯堡战役的立体模型非常精细。|The children created a diorama of a rainforest for their school project. 孩子们为了学校的项目制作了一个热带雨林的立体模型。|近义词:model, exhibit, display|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
