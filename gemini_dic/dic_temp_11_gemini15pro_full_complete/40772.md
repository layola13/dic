convinces:/kənˈvɪnsɪz/
verb
说服，使确信
persuades someone to do something or believe something.
The salesman convinced me to buy the car.  售货员说服我买了那辆车。
synonyms: persuades, satisfies, assures
antonyms: dissuades,  dissatisfies, doubts
词性: 动词
