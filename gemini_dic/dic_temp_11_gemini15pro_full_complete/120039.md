millipede:/ˈmɪlɪpiːd/ | n. |千足虫，马陆|A small creature with a long, thin body and many pairs of legs, belonging to the class Diplopoda. |例句：I saw a millipede crawling on the forest floor. 我看到一只千足虫在森林地面上爬行。

例句：Millipedes are important decomposers in the ecosystem. 千足虫是生态系统中重要的分解者。

近义词：diplopod |反义词: None|词性: noun
