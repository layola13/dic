individuative:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuːətɪv/
adj.
赋予个性的, 区别个体的, 个体化的
Giving individual character; distinguishing individuals.  Serving to mark out or emphasize individuality.
例句：Individuative education fosters unique talents. 个体化教育培养独特才能。

例句：The artist's individuative style made her work instantly recognizable.  这位艺术家的个性化风格使她的作品一眼就能认出来。
近义词：distinctive, individualizing, characteristic, peculiar
反义词：generic, general, common, uniform
词性: adjective
