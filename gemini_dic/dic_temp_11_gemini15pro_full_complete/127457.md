nebulous:/ˈnɛbjʊləs/ | adj. | 模糊的，朦胧的；星云的 | (of a concept or idea) unclear, vague, or ill-defined.  |  The plan was rather nebulous and lacked specific details.  这个计划相当模糊，缺乏具体的细节。 |  The nebulous gas cloud gradually coalesced into a star. 模糊的气体云逐渐聚集成一颗恒星。 | 近义词: vague, hazy, unclear, indistinct, amorphous, obscure | 反义词: clear, distinct, precise, definite, concrete, specific | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *nebulosus*, from nebula (“mist, fog, cloud, vapor”), from Proto-Indo-European *nébʰōl- (“cloud”).
