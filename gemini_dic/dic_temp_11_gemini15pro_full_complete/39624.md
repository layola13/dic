consents:/kənˈsɛnts/| n. |同意，赞同；准许，许可|Agreement or permission to do something.|The project can proceed only with the consents of all stakeholders.  该项目只有得到所有利益相关者的同意才能进行。|近义词：agreements, approvals, permissions|反义词:disagreements, objections, refusals|noun
consent:/kənˈsɛnt/| v. |同意，赞同；准许，许可|To give permission for something to happen.|He consented to the proposal. 他同意了这个提议。|近义词：agree, approve, permit|反义词:disagree, object, refuse|verb
