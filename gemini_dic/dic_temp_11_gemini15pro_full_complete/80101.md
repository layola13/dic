gobble-up:/ˈɡɑːbl ʌp/ (US), /ˈɡɒbl ʌp/ (UK)| phrasal verb | 吞并；狼吞虎咽；贪婪地抓住|To consume or acquire something quickly and completely, often in a greedy or enthusiastic manner.  It can refer to eating food quickly, but also to acquiring things like land, companies, or information.|The company gobbled up several smaller competitors last year.  这家公司去年吞并了几家较小的竞争对手。|Synonyms: devour, consume, engulf, absorb, monopolize|Antonyms: relinquish, release, distribute, share|verb phrase
