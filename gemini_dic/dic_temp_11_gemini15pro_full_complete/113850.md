madelaines:/ˌmædəˈleɪnz/ | n. |玛德琳蛋糕 |Small, shell-shaped sponge cakes, often flavored with lemon and almond.  These cakes are known for their distinctive hump on one side. They are of French origin. |例句：
The madelaines were perfectly golden brown and smelled of lemon. 玛德琳蛋糕烤成了完美的金棕色，散发着柠檬的香味。

I dipped the madeleine in my tea and savored the delicate flavor. 我把玛德琳蛋糕浸在茶里，品尝着它细腻的味道。
|近义词：cookies, cakes, pastries |反义词: None (No direct opposite for a specific type of pastry) |词性:noun
