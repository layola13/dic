antilithic:/ˌæntiˈlɪθɪk/| adj. n.|抗结石的，溶解结石的；抗结石药|Preventing or dissolving calculi (stones, usually in the kidneys or gallbladder).|Example: The doctor prescribed an antilithic medication to help dissolve the kidney stones. 医生开了抗结石药来帮助溶解肾结石。

Example:  Antilithic herbs are often used in traditional medicine. 抗结石药草常用于传统医学中。|近义词：litholytic, calculus-dissolving|反义词:lithogenic|词性:adjective, noun
