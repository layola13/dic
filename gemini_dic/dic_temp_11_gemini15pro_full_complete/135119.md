nonocculting:/ˌnɑːnəˈkʌltɪŋ/
adjective
非掩星的
Not causing an occultation; not blocking light or other radiation.
The nonocculting design of the coronagraph allows for continuous observation of the sun's corona. 日冕仪的非掩星设计允许对太阳日冕进行连续观测。
The satellite employs a nonocculting sensor to monitor solar activity. 这颗卫星使用非掩星传感器来监测太阳活动。
近义词:unobscured, transparent
反义词:occulting, blocking
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + occulting, the present participle of occult (“to hide, conceal, cover over”).  Ultimately from Latin *occulere, present active infinitive of occulō (“I cover over, conceal”), from ob (“over, against”) + the root of celō (“I hide, conceal”).
