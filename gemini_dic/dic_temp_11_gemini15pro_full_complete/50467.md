deprehensible:/ˌdɛprɪˈhɛnsɪbl/
adjective
应受谴责的；可非难的
deserving of censure or condemnation.
His conduct was thoroughly reprehensible. 他的行为完全应受谴责。
近义词: blameworthy, culpable, censurable, reproachable
反义词: commendable, laudable, praiseworthy
词性: adjective

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin deprehensibilis, from deprehendere ‘seize, discover’ (see deprehend).
