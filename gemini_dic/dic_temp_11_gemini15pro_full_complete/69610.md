femicides:/ˈfɛmɪsaɪdz/| n. |杀害女性; 对女性的谋杀|The increasing rate of femicides is a serious global issue.  杀害女性的比率不断上升是一个严重的全球性问题。|Femicides are often linked to domestic violence.  杀害女性通常与家庭暴力有关。|近义词： murders of women, female homicides |反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider concepts like "protection of women", "women's safety") |词性:noun

Etymology:
From  femin-  +‎ -cide.  femin- comes from Latin femina (“woman”). -cide comes from Latin -cida (“killer, slayer”), from caedere (“to cut, kill”).
