fripperies:/ˈfrɪpəriz/| n. | 华而不实的东西；小玩意儿；廉价而俗丽的装饰品 | Showy or unnecessary ornaments; trifling things; cheap and gaudy decorations. |  She spent a fortune on fripperies for her apartment. 她花了一大笔钱为她的公寓添置了一些华而不实的小玩意儿。

The room was cluttered with fripperies. 房间里堆满了廉价俗丽的装饰品。

| 近义词：trifles, gewgaws, baubles, trinkets | 反义词：essentials, necessities | 词性：名词


词源:

From French *friperie* "old clothes, rags," from *friper* "to rub, wear to rags," of unknown origin.  The sense shifted from "old clothes" to "cheap finery."
