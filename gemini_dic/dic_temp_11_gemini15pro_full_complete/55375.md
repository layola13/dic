distasting:/dɪsˈteɪstɪŋ/
adjective
令人厌恶的，讨厌的
Causing dislike or disgust; offensive.
The idea of eating insects is distasting to many people.  很多人觉得吃昆虫很恶心。
He found the smell of cigarettes distasting. 他觉得香烟的味道令人作呕。
近义词:repulsive, repugnant, offensive, unpalatable, disagreeable
反义词:appealing, pleasant, attractive, agreeable
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + taste + -ing.  Dis- 表示否定， taste 味道， -ing 形容词后缀。
