hygienics:/hɑɪˈdʒɛnɪks/
n.
卫生学；保健法
The science of health and its preservation.
例句：She studied food hygienics in college. 她在大学学习食品卫生学。

例句：Proper hygienics are essential in preventing the spread of disease.  适当的卫生措施对于预防疾病传播至关重要。

近义词：hygiene, sanitation
反义词：unhygienic conditions, filth
词性：名词
