banu: /ˈbɑːnuː/
noun
巴努人，巴努部落
A member of a pastoral nomadic people inhabiting southeastern Iran, western Pakistan, and southern Afghanistan.
The banu migrated seasonally with their flocks. 巴努人随着他们的羊群季节性迁徙。
synonyms:  nomadic tribe, pastoralist group
antonyms: settled community
