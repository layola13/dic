ahriman:/ˈɑːrimən/| n.|阿里曼|In Zoroastrianism, Ahriman is the destructive spirit, the source of all evil in the universe, and the eternal opponent of Ahura Mazda (Ormazd), the good spirit.|Ahriman is often depicted as a monstrous figure in Zoroastrian art.  阿里曼在琐罗亚斯德教艺术中常被描绘成一个可怕的人物。

Ahriman's influence can be seen in the natural disasters and human suffering of the world.  阿里曼的影响可以在自然灾害和人类苦难中看到。|近义词:devil, Satan, Angra Mainyu|反义词:Ahura Mazda, Ormazd|词性:noun
