air-dry:/ˈer draɪ/
verb|风干，晾干|To dry something by exposing it to the air, rather than using artificial heat.|I air-dried the laundry on the clothesline. 我把衣服晾在晾衣绳上风干。|近义词:dry naturally, line-dry|反义词:machine-dry, tumble-dry|v.

air-dry:/ˈer draɪ/
adjective|风干的，晾干的|Dried by exposure to the air.|The air-dried flowers retained their color.  风干的花朵保留了它们的色彩。|近义词:naturally dried, line-dried|反义词:machine-dried, tumble-dried|adj.
