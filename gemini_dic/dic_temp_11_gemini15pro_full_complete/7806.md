antacid:/ˈæntˌæsɪd/ /ænˈtæsɪd/| n. adj.|抗酸剂，抗酸的|A substance that neutralizes stomach acidity.|She took an antacid to relieve her heartburn. 她服用了抗酸剂来缓解胃灼热。|Synonyms: antacid agent, antiacid, alkali, base|Antonyms: acid|noun

antacid:/ˈæntˌæsɪd/ /ænˈtæsɪd/| adj.|抗酸的|Having the ability to neutralize acids.|Antacid tablets are commonly used to treat indigestion. 抗酸片常用于治疗消化不良。|Synonyms: alkaline, basic|Antonyms: acidic|adjective
