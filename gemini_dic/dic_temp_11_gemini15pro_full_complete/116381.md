mastitis: /mæˈstaɪtɪs/ | n. |乳腺炎|Inflammation of the mammary gland, typically due to bacterial infection, often occurring during breastfeeding.  |例句：The cow developed mastitis after giving birth. 这头奶牛产犊后患上了乳腺炎。

例句：Mastitis can be a painful condition for breastfeeding mothers. 乳腺炎对于哺乳期母亲来说可能是一种痛苦的疾病。|近义词：mammitis|反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease condition) |词性:noun
