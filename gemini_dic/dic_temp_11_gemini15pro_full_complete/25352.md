ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *buildan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *bildaną (“to build”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰel- (“to swell, thrive”). Cognate with Old Saxon bildian, Old Frisian bilda, Middle Dutch bilden, Old High German buildan, German bilden (“to form, shape, educate”), Old Norse bylja (“to swell, billow”).
