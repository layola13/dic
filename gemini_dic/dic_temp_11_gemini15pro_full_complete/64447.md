erigible:/ˈɛrɪdʒɪbl/| adj.|合格的，有资格的|Suitable; qualified; capable of being erected.|The candidate is eligible for the position.  这位候选人有资格担任该职位。|He is eligible to vote. 他有资格投票。|近义词：qualified, suitable, fit, appropriate|反义词：ineligible, unqualified, unsuitable|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *erigibilis, from erigere "to erect, raise, build," from ex- "out" + regere "to direct, guide, rule."  The original sense was "able to be erected," later extended to mean "able to be raised to an office or position" and then generalized to "qualified."
