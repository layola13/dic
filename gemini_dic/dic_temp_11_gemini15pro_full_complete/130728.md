nonalluvial:/ˌnɒnəˈluːviəl/ | adj. | 非冲积的 | Not deposited or made by flowing water; not alluvial. | The nonalluvial soils of the region are less fertile.  该地区的非冲积土壤肥力较低。| Synonyms: non-fluvial, upland | Antonyms: alluvial, fluvial | adjective

nonalluvials:/ˌnɒnəˈluːviəlz/ | n. | 非冲积物 | Material or land that is not deposited by rivers or streams; not alluvium. | The nonalluvials in this area are mainly composed of volcanic rock. 这个地区的非冲积物主要由火山岩组成。 | Synonyms: non-fluvial deposits | Antonyms: alluvium, alluvial deposits | noun
