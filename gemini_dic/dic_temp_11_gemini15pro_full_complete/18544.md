beneficiary:/ˌbenɪˈfɪʃiəri/ /ˌbenəˈfɪʃieri/ | n. | 受益人；受惠者 | a person who derives advantage from something, especially a trust, will, or insurance policy |  She was the sole beneficiary of her father's will. 她是她父亲遗嘱的唯一受益人。

| 近义词: recipient, heir, legatee | 反义词: benefactor, donor | 词性: noun


beneficiary:/ˌbenɪˈfɪʃiəri/ /ˌbenəˈfɪʃieri/ | adj. |  受益的；受惠的 (less common usage) | receiving or entitled to receive something good, such as money or property | The beneficiary countries received substantial aid. 受益国获得了大量援助。

| 近义词: favored, privileged | 反义词: disadvantaged | 词性: adjective
