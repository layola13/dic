depot:/ˈdiːpoʊ/ (US), /ˈdɛpəʊ/ (UK)| n. |仓库；贮藏所；火车站；公共汽车站|A building for the storage of large quantities of goods; a railroad or bus station.|The army set up a supply depot near the front lines. 军队在前线附近设立了一个补给站。|Synonyms: warehouse, storehouse, repository, terminal, station|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "distribution center" in some contexts)|noun

depot:/ˈdiːpoʊ/ (US), /ˈdɛpəʊ/ (UK)| n. |车库；车辆段|A place where buses, trains, or other vehicles are housed and maintained.|The city's bus depot is located on the outskirts. 市公共汽车车库位于郊区。|Synonyms: garage, barn, shed (for smaller vehicles), yard|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms)|noun
