amyloplastic:/ˌæmɪloʊˈplæstɪk/| adj.|淀粉塑性的，淀粉可塑的|Relating to or exhibiting amyloplasty, the process of starch formation in plants.|Amyloplastic grains are visible under the microscope. 在显镜下可以看到淀粉塑性颗粒。|近义词:starch-forming|反义词:None|词性:形容词

amyloplastic:/ˌæmɪloʊˈplæstɪk/| n.|淀粉体|A plastid that stores starch.|Amyloplasts are abundant in potato tubers. 马铃薯块茎中淀粉体含量丰富。|近义词:leucoplast, statolith|反义词:None|词性:名词
