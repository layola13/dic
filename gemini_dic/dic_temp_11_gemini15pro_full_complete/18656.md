beo:/beɪoʊ/ | n. | 喧闹; 骚动; 吵闹; 喧嚣 | A state of noisy activity and confusion. | The beo of the marketplace made it hard to hear. 市场上的喧闹声让人很难听到。| 近义词：din, uproar, commotion, racket, hubbub | 反义词：quiet, silence, stillness, peace | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old English *bēow, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bewōną (“to beat, strike”). Cognate with Old Norse beygja (“to bend, bow”), Old English būgan (“to bend, bow”). The sense development is likely from the idea of striking or beating creating noise.
