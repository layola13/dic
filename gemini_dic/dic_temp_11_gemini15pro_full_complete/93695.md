imitation:/ˌɪmɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 模仿；仿制品；赝品 | The painting was a cheap imitation of a Monet. 这幅画是莫奈作品的廉价仿制品。 | Example: Her imitation of the teacher was hilarious. 她模仿老师的样子滑稽极了。 | Synonyms: copy, replica, mimicry | Antonyms: original, genuine | noun

imitate:/ˈɪmɪteɪt/ | v. | 模仿；仿效 | Children often imitate the behavior of adults. 孩子们经常模仿成年人的行为。 | Example: He tried to imitate his father's signature. 他试图模仿他父亲的签名。 | Synonyms: copy, mimic, emulate | Antonyms: originate, innovate | verb
