miscount:/ˌmɪsˈkaʊnt/ |v. n. |数错；误算；错误计数；错误的计数|To count something incorrectly, resulting in a wrong total. | I miscounted the number of people attending the party and didn't have enough chairs. 我数错了参加派对的人数，椅子不够。| The cashier miscounted my change and gave me too much money. 收银员数错了我的零钱，多给了我钱。 |近义词: miscalculate, underestimate, overestimate |反义词: count correctly, calculate accurately| verb
miscount:/ˌmɪsˈkaʊnt/ |n.|错误的计数| An incorrect count or tally. |There was a miscount in the election results, so they had to recount the ballots. 选举结果出现了误算，所以他们不得不重新计票。 | Due to a miscount, the inventory showed more items than were actually in stock. 由于计数错误，库存显示的物品数量比实际库存多。|近义词: error, inaccuracy, mistake|反义词: accurate count, correct tally| noun
