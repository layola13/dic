blight:/blaɪt/
n.|枯萎病；荒芜；祸害|A disease that causes plants to wither and die. Something that spoils or damages something.|例句：The potato blight caused widespread famine. 马铃薯枯萎病造成了大范围的饥荒。

例句：Urban blight affects many inner cities.  城市衰败影响了许多市中心。
近义词：disease, affliction, curse, plague, scourge
反义词：blessing, boon
词性:名词

blight:/blaɪt/
vt.|损害；摧残；使…枯萎|To cause something to wither and die; to spoil or damage something.|例句：The frost blighted the crops. 霜冻使农作物枯萎了。

例句：His career was blighted by scandal. 他的事业被丑闻毁了。
近义词：ruin, spoil, destroy, damage, impair
反义词：improve, enhance
词性:动词


词源: From Middle English *bliȝten, from Old English *blīġetan (“to make pale, bleach, blight”), from Proto-Germanic *blīhwijaną (“to become pale, lose color”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleyǵʰ- (“pale, weak, fearful”). Cognate with Dutch bleken (“to bleach”).
