myristone: /ˈmɪrɪstoʊn/ | n. | 肉豆蔻酮; 十四烷酮 | A white crystalline ketone found in nutmeg butter and other vegetable fats. It is used in perfumery and organic synthesis. | Myristone is a common ingredient in some perfumes. 肉豆蔻酮是一些香水中常见的成分。| Myristone can be synthesized in a laboratory setting. 肉豆蔻酮可以在实验室环境下合成。 |  Synonyms: tetradecanone | Antonyms: N/A | noun
