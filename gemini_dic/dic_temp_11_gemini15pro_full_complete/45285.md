culture:/ˈkʌltʃər/ (US) /ˈkʌltʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|文化，教养，种植，培养|The customs, arts, social institutions, and achievements of a particular nation, people, or other social group.|例句：Chinese culture is rich and diverse. 中国文化丰富多彩。

例句：The company promotes a culture of innovation.  这家公司提倡创新文化。|近义词：civilization, society, heritage|反义词：barbarism, ignorance|noun

culture:/ˈkʌltʃər/ (US) /ˈkʌltʃə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|培养，培植|To grow (something, typically bacteria or tissue cells) in a scientifically controlled environment.|例句：We cultured the bacteria in the lab. 我们在实验室里培养细菌。

例句：They are culturing cells to study the effects of the new drug. 他们正在培养细胞来研究新药的效果。|近义词：grow, cultivate, nurture|反义词：destroy, kill|verb
