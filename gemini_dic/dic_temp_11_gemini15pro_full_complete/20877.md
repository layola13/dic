blaze-forth:/bleɪz fɔːrθ/
verb
爆发，迸发，突然出现
Suddenly shine brightly or powerfully; to burst out suddenly and strongly.
例句：
His anger blazed forth. 他的怒火爆发了。

例句：
Inspiration blazed forth in her mind. 灵感在她脑海中迸发。

近义词：burst forth, erupt, flare up, flash
反义词：die down, fade, dwindle
词性：verb (不及物动词)
