gratifiable:/ˈɡrætɪfaɪəbl/
adjective
可满足的；令人愉悦的
capable of being gratified or satisfied.
Her desire for knowledge was easily gratifiable. 她对知识的渴望很容易得到满足。
近义词:satisfiable, pleasing, fulfilling
反义词:unsatisfiable, displeasing
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *gratificabilis, from Latin gratificari (“to gratify”) + -abilis.
