estimative:/ˈɛstɪmətɪv/| adj.|估计的，推测的|Relating to or based on estimation; approximate.|例句：The estimative cost of the project is one million dollars.  该项目的估计成本是一百万美元。|例句：His estimative powers were highly developed. 他的估算能力非常强。|近义词：approximate, conjectural, speculative|反义词：precise, exact|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin aestimativus, from Latin aestimat-, past participle stem of aestimare "to value, appraise,"  from aes "bronze, money" (see aes-) + -timare frequentative of timere "to fear."  The notion is of "setting a price on."
