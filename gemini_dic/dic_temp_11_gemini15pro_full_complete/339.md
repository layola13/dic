abhorrent:/əˈbɔːrənt/ /æbˈhɔːrənt/
adjective
令人憎恶的；可恶的
Inspiring disgust and loathing; repugnant.
His abhorrent behavior shocked everyone present. 他令人憎恶的行为震惊了所有在场的人。

The idea of harming animals is abhorrent to me.  伤害动物的想法让我深恶痛绝。

synonym: detestable, loathsome, repugnant, repulsive, hateful, odious, abominable, execrable
antonym: admirable, delightful, pleasant, appealing, lovable

词性: 形容词
