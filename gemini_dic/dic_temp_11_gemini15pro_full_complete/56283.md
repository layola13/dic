doggerelize:/ˈdɒɡərəlaɪz/ (US) /ˈdɒɡərəlaɪz/ (UK)| v. |打油诗化；使粗俗化；使变得低俗|To write or rewrite something in the style of doggerel, or to make something trivial or commonplace. |He doggerelzied the epic poem, turning it into a humorous rhyme.| 近义词: trivialize, vulgarize, debase| 反义词: elevate, ennoble, refine|词性:动词

doggerelizer:/ˈdɒɡərəlaɪzər/ (US) /ˈdɒɡərəlaɪzər/ (UK)| n. |打油诗作者；使低俗化的人|A person who writes doggerel or makes something trivial or commonplace. |The doggerelzer reduced the complex theory to a simple jingle.| 近义词: rhymester, versifier| 反义词: scholar, intellectual|词性:名词

doggerelization:/ˌdɒɡərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (US) /ˌdɒɡərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |打油诗化；粗俗化；低俗化|The act of writing something in the style of doggerel, or the process of making something trivial or commonplace. |The doggerelzation of the classic play angered many theatre enthusiasts.| 近义词: trivialization, vulgarization, degradation| 反义词: elevation, refinement, improvement|词性:名词
