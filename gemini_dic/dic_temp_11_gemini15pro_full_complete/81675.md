greenbacker:/ˈɡriːnˌbækər/| n.|绿背纸币支持者；(尤指美国南北战争时期)主张发行没有黄金储备的纸币的人|A person, especially during and after the American Civil War, who advocated the continued use of paper money not backed by gold (greenbacks).|例句：The Greenbackers believed that increasing the money supply would help farmers and other debtors. 绿背纸币支持者认为增加货币供应量将有助于农民和其他债务人。

例句：The Greenback Party gained some support in the late 19th century. 绿背党在19世纪后期获得了一些支持。|近义词：inflationist 通货膨胀主义者|反义词：goldbug 金本位制拥护者|词性：名词
