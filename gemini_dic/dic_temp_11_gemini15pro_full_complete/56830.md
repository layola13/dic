dormouse:/ˈdɔːrmaʊs/ (UK), /ˈdɔːrˌmaʊs/ (US)| n.|睡鼠|A small rodent, typically having a bushy tail and, in some species, the ability to hibernate.|The dormouse curled up in a ball for its winter hibernation. 睡鼠蜷成一团准备冬眠。|Synonyms: rodent, mouse|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for an animal)|Noun

dormice:/ˈdɔːrmaɪs/ (UK), /ˈdɔːrˌmaɪs/ (US)| n.|睡鼠（复数）|Plural form of dormouse, referring to more than one dormouse.|The dormice were active during the night, searching for food. 睡鼠在夜间活动，寻找食物。|Synonyms: rodents, mice|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for an animal)|Noun
