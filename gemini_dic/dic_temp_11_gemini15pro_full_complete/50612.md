deregulating: /diːˈreɡjʊleɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
解除管制，撤销管制
The act of removing regulations or controls from an industry or sector.
The government is deregulating the telecommunications industry. 政府正在解除对电信行业的管制。
The airline industry was deregulated in the 1970s.  航空业在20世纪70年代被解除管制。
近义词: liberalizing, freeing
反义词: regulating, controlling
词性: verb (present participle)


deregulate: /diːˈreɡjʊleɪt/
