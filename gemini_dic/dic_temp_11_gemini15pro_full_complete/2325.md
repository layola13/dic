adherable:/ədˈhɪərəbl/| adj.|可粘附的，可粘着的|Capable of adhering; sticky.  Able to stick fast to a surface.|The label is adherable to most surfaces.  这个标签可以粘在大多数表面上。|近义词：sticky, adhesive, clingy|反义词：non-adhesive, non-sticky|词性:形容词
词源: From adhere + -able. Adhere comes from Latin adhaerere, from ad- "to" + haerere "to stick."
