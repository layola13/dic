nosy:/ˈnoʊzi/ (US) /ˈnəʊzi/ (UK)| adj.|爱管闲事的，好打听的|Inclined to inquire too curiously into other people's affairs.|She's so nosy, always trying to find out everyone's business. 她太爱管闲事了，总是想打听每个人的事。|Synonyms: inquisitive, curious, prying, interfering|Antonyms: respectful, discreet, unintrusive|adjective

nosiness:/ˈnoʊzinəs/ (US) /ˈnəʊzinɪs/ (UK)| n.|爱管闲事，好打听|The state of being nosy.|Her nosiness got her into trouble. 她的爱管闲事给她惹了麻烦。|Synonyms: inquisitiveness, curiosity, prying|Antonyms: discretion, reserve|noun
