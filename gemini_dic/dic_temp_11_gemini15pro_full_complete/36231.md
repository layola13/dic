cognisor:/ˈkɒɡnɪzər/ /ˈkɑːɡnɪˌzɔːr/| n.|认识者；知情者；审理者|Someone who knows or is aware of something; a person who acknowledges or recognizes something; a judge or other legal official who hears and determines a case. |The cognisor of the facts was the only witness.|近义词：percipient, observer, witness, judge, adjudicator|反义词:ignoramus,  unaware person|noun
