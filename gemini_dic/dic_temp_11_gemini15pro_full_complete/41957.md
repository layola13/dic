cosovereign:/ˈkoʊˈsɑːvrɪn/
adjective
共同主权的; 联合主权的
Sharing sovereignty; exercising sovereignty jointly.
The two tribes acted as cosovereign nations, each with its own distinct responsibilities. 这两个部落作为共同主权的国家，各自承担着不同的责任。
Near Synonyms: joint sovereign, shared sovereign
Antonyms: subordinate, dependent
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
co- (prefix): together, jointly (from Latin "com-" meaning "with, together")
sovereign (noun/adjective): a supreme ruler, possessing supreme or ultimate power (from Old French "soverain," ultimately from Latin "superanus" meaning "supreme")
