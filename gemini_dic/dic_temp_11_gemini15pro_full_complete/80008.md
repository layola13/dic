go-out-of-style:/ɡoʊ aʊt əv staɪl/ | Verb Phrase | 过时，不再流行 | To become unfashionable or no longer in current use. |  Her dress went out of style years ago. 她的裙子几年前就过时了。

Bell bottoms have gone out of style again. 喇叭裤又过时了。


近义词: become unfashionable, become outdated, fall out of fashion | 反义词: be in style, be fashionable, be trendy | 词性: Verb Phrase

词源:  "Go out" meaning to cease to be in a particular state and "style" referring to fashion or trend. The phrase literally means to move from being in style to not being in style.
