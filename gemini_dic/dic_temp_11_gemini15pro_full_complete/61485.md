The good news embrightened her day.  这个好消息让她的一天都充满了希望。 | 近义词: brighten, illuminate, cheer, encourage | 反义词: darken, dim, depress, discourage | 词性: 动词

词源:  em- (prefix meaning "to cause to be") + bright (adjective)
