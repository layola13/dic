ambs:/æmz/ | n. |羊羔（复数）| Lambs are young sheep. | The lambs were frolicking in the field. 羊羔在田里嬉戏。 | 近义词: sheep (泛指绵羊),  ewe lambs (母羊羔), ram lambs (公羊羔) | 反义词: None (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 mature sheep 成年羊) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *lambes, *lombes, plural of *lamb, *lombe, from Old English *lambru, plural of lamb. Cognate with Scots lambs, lammys (plural of lamb), West Frisian leaemmer, lemmer (lamb), Dutch lammeren (plural of lam), German Lämmer (plural of Lamm), Old Norse lömb (plural of lamb).
