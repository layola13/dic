jollying:/ˈdʒɒliɪŋ/| v.|哄骗；开玩笑；劝慰；鼓励|To try to make someone feel happier or more cheerful, especially when they are sad or worried; to joke with someone in a friendly way.|He was trying to jolly her along a bit because she seemed so miserable. 他试图让她高兴起来，因为她看起来很痛苦。|近义词：encouraging, cheering, teasing, kidding|反义词：discouraging, depressing|词性:动名词 (gerund or present participle)
词源:From jolly (adjective) + -ing (suffix forming present participles and gerunds).  Ultimately from Old French joli meaning "pretty, pleasant".
