nonsensitively:/ˌnɒnˈsɛnsɪtɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɛnsətɪvli/
adverb|不敏感地，缺乏同情心地|in a way that shows a lack of awareness or consideration for the feelings of other people|He nonsensitively joked about her weight. 他不敏感地拿她的体重开玩笑。|insensitively, thoughtlessly, unfeelingly|sensitively, considerately, thoughtfully|词性: 副词

nonsensitive:/ˌnɒnˈsɛnsɪtɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɛnsətɪv/
adjective|不敏感的，缺乏同情心的|not aware of or showing concern for the feelings of other people|He made a nonsensitive remark about her appearance. 他对她的外表发表了不敏感的评论。|insensitive, unfeeling, inconsiderate|sensitive, considerate, thoughtful|词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + sensitive (from Latin *sensitīvus, from sentire "to feel").  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "nonsensitive" into the adverb "nonsensitively".
