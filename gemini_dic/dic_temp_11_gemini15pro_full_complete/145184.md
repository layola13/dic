overaward:/ˌoʊvərəˈwɔːrd/
verb
过度奖励，过分奖励
to give someone more of an award (such as money) than they should receive
The scholarship committee realized they had overawarded financial aid and had to ask some students to return a portion. 奖学金委员会意识到他们过度奖励了经济援助，不得不要求一些学生退还一部分。
verb
过分授予
to give someone too much of something (like a prize, honor, or benefit)
Some critics argue that the Nobel Prize is often overawarded to individuals whose contributions are not truly groundbreaking. 一些评论家认为，诺贝尔奖经常被过度授予那些贡献并非真正具有开创性的人。

近义词: overcompensate, overpay
反义词: underpay, undercompensate
词性: verb
