gimlet:/ˈɡɪmlɪt/| n.|手摇钻，锥子，鸡尾酒|A small hand tool with a pointed, spiral blade for boring holes, especially in wood. Also, a cocktail made with gin or vodka and lime juice.|He used a gimlet to make a hole for the screw. 他用锥子钻了个孔来上螺丝。|Synonyms: awl, auger, drill, borer|Antonyms: none|noun

gimlet:/ˈɡɪmlɪt/| adj.|锐利的，尖锐的|Sharp, piercing, or penetrating, as a gimlet's point; keen.|He had a gimlet gaze that seemed to bore right through me. 他锐利的目光似乎要把我洞穿。|Synonyms: piercing, sharp, keen, penetrating|Antonyms: dull, blunt|adjective
