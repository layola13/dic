indagation:/ˌɪndəˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |调查，探究|A systematic investigation or examination; a careful search for information or truth. |The detective's indagation led him to the culprit.  侦探的调查把他引向了罪犯。

The scientist conducted a thorough indagation into the properties of the new material.  科学家对新材料的特性进行了彻底的探究。|近义词：investigation, inquiry, examination, probe, research|反义词:ignorance, disregard|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin indagationem, accusative of indagatio (“a searching into, investigation”), from indagare (“to trace out, track down, investigate”), from in- (“in, into”) + dagare (“to prick, stab”).
