nestorian:/nɛˈstɔːriən/| adj. n.|聂斯托利派的，聂斯托利派信徒|Relating to Nestorius, a 5th-century patriarch of Constantinople who was condemned as a heretic for his doctrines concerning the nature of Christ.  A follower of Nestorius.|例句：The Nestorian church spread eastward through Persia and into China. 聂斯托利派教会向东传播，经波斯传入中国。|近义词：heretical, dissident|反义词：orthodox|词性：adjective, noun

nestorian:/nɛˈstɔːriən/| n.|聂斯托利派信徒|A follower of Nestorius.|例句：He was identified as a Nestorian. 他被认定为聂斯托利派信徒。|近义词：heretic, dissenter|反义词：orthodox believer|词性：noun
