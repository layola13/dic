endermatic:/ˌɛndərˈmætɪk/
adjective
经皮的；皮内给药的
Relating to the application of a medicine through the skin, especially by rubbing.  The medicine is absorbed through the skin.
The doctor prescribed an endermatic cream for the patient's rash. 医生给病人开了经皮使用的药膏来治疗皮疹。
The endermatic application of the medication provided localized pain relief.  药物的经皮给药提供了局部止痛效果。
近义词: transdermal, percutaneous
反义词: oral, intravenous
词性: adjective
词源:  From Greek *ἐν (en, “in”) + *δέρμα (derma, “skin”) + *-ικός (-ikos, adjectival suffix).
