interoceanic:/ˌɪntərˌoʊʃiˈænɪk/ | adj. | 海洋间的，连接两个大洋的 | Situated between two oceans; connecting two oceans. |  The Panama Canal is an interoceanic waterway. 巴拿马运河是一条连接两个大洋的水道。

The interoceanic highway connects the Atlantic and Pacific coasts. 这条横贯大陆的公路连接大西洋和太平洋海岸。| 近义词: transoceanic | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From inter- (between) + oceanic (relating to the ocean).  "Inter-" comes from Latin, meaning "between, among, in the midst of." "Oceanic" comes from Late Latin oceanicus, from Latin oceanus "ocean," from Greek ōkeanos.
