bractless:/ˈbræktləs/
adj.
无苞片的
lacking bracts; having no bracts. Bracts are modified leaves associated with flowers or inflorescences.
The bractless flowers of this species are distinctive.  这种植物的无苞片花朵是其显著特征。
The botanist identified the plant by its bractless inflorescence. 植物学家通过其无苞片的花序识别了这种植物。
近义词:ebracteate
反义词:bracteate, bracted
词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bract + -less.  Bract comes from the Latin word "bractea" meaning thin plate of metal, gold leaf, which was later applied to the leaf-like structures associated with flowers. The suffix "-less" means without.
