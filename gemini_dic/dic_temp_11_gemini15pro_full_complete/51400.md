determiner:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnər/ | determiner | 限定词 | A word (such as *a*, *the*, *some*, *any*, *my*, *your*, *his*, etc.) that comes before a noun to show how the noun is being used. It limits or "determines" the noun's meaning. | Example: *The* cat sat on *my* lap.  这只猫坐在我的腿上。 | 近义词: (No direct synonyms, as "determiner" is a grammatical category. Words within categories, like articles, possessive pronouns, demonstratives, can have synonyms.) | 反义词: (No antonyms exist for the grammatical term "determiner") | 词性: determiner (限定词)
