aryanism:/ˈɛəriənɪzəm/ | n. | 雅利安主义 | A racist ideology that holds that Aryans are a superior race.  It was a central tenet of Nazi ideology. | Hitler's regime promoted Aryanism and persecuted those deemed non-Aryan. 希特勒政权宣扬雅利安主义并迫害那些被认为是非雅利安人的人。| Near Synonyms: racism, white supremacy | Antonyms: racial equality, egalitarianism | noun
