jinglers: /ˈdʒɪŋɡlərz/
n.
作响的东西；创作广告歌曲的人；叮当作响的饰物
People who compose or perform jingles, short catchy tunes used in advertising. Also refers to small metal objects that make a jingling sound.
The company hired a team of jinglers to create a memorable tune for their new product. 这家公司聘请了一组广告歌曲创作者为他们的新产品创作一首令人难忘的曲子。
The child loved playing with the jinglers on her crib.  孩子喜欢玩婴儿床上叮当作响的小玩意儿。
近义词: composers, musicians, songwriters (for people); bells, chimes, trinkets (for objects)
反义词:  (No direct antonym, depends on the specific meaning)
