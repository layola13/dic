calabarine:/ˈkæləbəriːn/| adj.|卡拉巴毒素的；产于卡拉巴尔的|Relating to or from Calabar, a port city in southeastern Nigeria, or to the Calabar bean, which contains the poisonous alkaloid physostigmine.|The calabarine plant is known for its toxic properties. 这种卡拉巴植物以其毒性而闻名。|Synonyms: Eserine, physostigmine|Antonyms: None|adjective

calabarine:/ˈkæləbəriːn/| n.|卡拉巴豆；毒扁豆|The seed of the Calabar bean, containing physostigmine, a poisonous alkaloid.|Calabarine was historically used in trials by ordeal. 卡拉巴豆在历史上曾用于神判。|Synonyms: ordeal bean, chop nut|Antonyms: None|noun
