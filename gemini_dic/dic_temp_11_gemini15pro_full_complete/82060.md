grits:/ɡrɪts/| n.|粗燕麦粉，玉米粥|Coarsely ground hulled corn that is boiled and eaten, especially in the southern US.|例句：Grits are a popular breakfast food in the South.  粗燕麦粉是南方很受欢迎的早餐食物。

例句：She made cheese grits for breakfast. 她早餐做了奶酪粗燕麦粉。|近义词：porridge, polenta|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "refined grains")|词性:noun
