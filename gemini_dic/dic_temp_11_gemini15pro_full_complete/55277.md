dissipativity:/ˌdɪsɪˈpeɪtɪvɪti/
n.
消散性，耗散性
The property of a system that tends to dissipate energy, often converting it into heat.  A system's ability to disperse or scatter energy, typically as heat.
The dissipativity of the material allows it to absorb shocks effectively.  这种材料的耗散性能使其能够有效地吸收冲击。
The researchers studied the dissipativity of the system to understand energy loss. 研究人员研究了系统的耗散性，以了解能量损失。
dissipation, damping
conservation, accumulation
noun
