noncomprehensively:/ˌnɒnkɒmprɪˈhensɪvli/ | adv. | 不全面地，不完整地 |  Not including everything; incompletely.  Not fully grasping or understanding something. | The topic was explained noncomprehensively, leaving out crucial details.  这个主题讲解得不全面，遗漏了关键细节。 | The student noncomprehensively understood the complex theorem.  这个学生没有完全理解这个复杂的定理。 | 近义词: incompletely, partially, inadequately | 反义词: comprehensively, thoroughly, completely | 词性: 副词

Etymology: non- + comprehensively.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "comprehensively" is an adverb derived from "comprehensive," which comes from the Latin "comprehendere," meaning "to grasp mentally."
