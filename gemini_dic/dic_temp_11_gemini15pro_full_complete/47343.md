de-sludges:/diːˈslʌdʒɪz/
verb
除污泥；清除污泥
Removes sludge or sediment from something.
The wastewater treatment plant de-sludges the tanks regularly. 污水处理厂定期清除污泥。
The crew de-sludged the holding tank of the ship. 船员清理了船舶的储油舱。
synonyms: clean, clear, purge, remove
antonyms: contaminate, pollute
v.
