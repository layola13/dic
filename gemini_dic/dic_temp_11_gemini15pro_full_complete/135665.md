nonpreferentially:/ˌnɒnˌprɛfəˈrɛnʃəli/ | adv. | 非优先地，无差别地 |  Without preference or favoritism; in a way that does not show any bias or discrimination. | The vaccine was distributed nonpreferentially to all citizens. 疫苗被无差别地分发给所有公民。|  The aid was given nonpreferentially to all affected areas. 援助无差别地提供给了所有受灾地区。 | synonym: impartially, equally, uniformly | antonym: preferentially, selectively, discriminatorily | 词性: 副词
