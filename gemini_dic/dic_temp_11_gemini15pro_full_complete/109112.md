legging: /ˈlɛɡɪŋ/ | n. | 紧身裤，绑腿 | A close-fitting garment covering the leg, typically made of stretchy fabric and worn by women or girls. |  She wore a pair of black leggings and a long sweater. 她穿了一条黑色紧身裤和一件长毛衣。

legging: /ˈlɛɡɪŋ/ | n. | (尤指旧时的) 绑腿，裹腿 | A covering for the leg, from the ankle to the knee, worn especially in former times. |  Soldiers used to wear leather leggings as part of their uniform.  士兵们过去穿皮绑腿作为制服的一部分。


近义词: tights, hose, jeggings
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) loose trousers, baggy pants
