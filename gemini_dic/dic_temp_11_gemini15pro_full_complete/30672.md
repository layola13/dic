cerote:/seˈɾote/| n. |耳垢；(墨西哥俚语，冒犯性)白痴，笨蛋|A highly offensive word used in Mexico to insult someone, similar to "idiot" or "moron" in English. It literally refers to earwax.|Example: ¡No seas cerote! (Don't be an idiot!)|近义词：(Neutral) tonto, idiota, imbécil (Offensive) pendejo, estúpido|反义词: inteligente, listo|词性: Noun (primarily), can be used adjectivally.

注意事项:

*This word is extremely offensive and should be avoided in polite conversation.  Its usage is restricted to very informal settings among close friends, and even then, caution is advised. Using it with strangers or in formal situations can be highly disrespectful and lead to conflict. Consider the context and relationship before using this word.*

*While "cerote" literally translates to earwax, its idiomatic meaning as an insult is far more prevalent and impactful.*

*Regional variations in usage and intensity of the insult may exist within Mexico.*
