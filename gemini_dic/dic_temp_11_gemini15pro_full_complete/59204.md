dysphoria:/dɪsˈfɔːriə/
|n.|烦躁不安，焦虑，不适|A state of unease or generalized dissatisfaction with life.|She experienced gender dysphoria from a young age. 她从小就经历性别焦虑。|近义词：unease, malaise, anxiety, discomfort|反义词：euphoria, well-being, comfort|词性:名词
Etymology: From Greek δυσ- (dys-) "bad, difficult" + φέρειν (pherein) "to bear".
