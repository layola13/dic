ephah:/ˈiːfə/ | n. | 伊法 (古代的容量单位) | An ancient Hebrew unit of dry measure, equal to about a bushel.  | Example: The recipe called for an ephah of flour.  食谱需要一伊法的面粉。 | Example:  They harvested several ephahs of barley. 他们收获了几伊法的大麦。| Synonym: bushel, measure | Antonym: N/A | noun

ephah:/ˈiːfə/ | n. | 伊法 (比喻大量的) | A large quantity (figurative usage, often in religious contexts). | Example: An ephah of blessings was bestowed upon them.  大量的祝福赐予了他们。 | Example:  He poured out an ephah of anger. 他倾泻出大量的愤怒。| Synonym: abundance, profusion | Antonym: scarcity, dearth | noun
