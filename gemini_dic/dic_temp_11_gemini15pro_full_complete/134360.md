noninterdependent:/ˌnɒnɪntərˈdɪpɛndənt/
adjective
非相互依赖的
Not dependent on each other; independent.  Existing or functioning independently of each other.  Not affecting or influenced by each other.
The two departments operate in a noninterdependent manner. 这两个部门以非相互依赖的方式运作。
Individual farmers made noninterdependent decisions about their crops. 个体农民独立地做出关于他们作物的决定。
Synonyms: independent, autonomous, separate, unconnected
Antonyms: interdependent, dependent, reliant, connected
词性: adjective
