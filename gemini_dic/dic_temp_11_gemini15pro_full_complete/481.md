abnodation: /ˌæbnəˈdeɪʃən/
n.
放弃；弃权；辞职
The act of giving up a right, claim, or office.
He announced his abnodation of the throne. 他宣布放弃王位。
近义词：resignation, renunciation, abandonment, relinquishment
反义词：assumption, acceptance
词性：名词
