ganoidal:/ɡəˈnɔɪdəl/| adj.| 楯鳞的，硬鳞质的|Covered with or resembling ganoid scales; having bright, enamel-like surface.|The ganoidal scales of the gar are thick and bony.|近义词：bony, scaled, enamelled|反义词:soft, smooth|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ganoïde*, from Greek *ganos* "brightness, sheen" + *-oid* "like, resembling."
