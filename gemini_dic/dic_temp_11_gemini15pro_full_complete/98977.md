inter-quarter:/ˌɪntərˈkwɔːrtər/ | adj. | 季度间的，跨季度的 | Occurring between quarters of a year. | Example: The inter-quarter growth rate was surprisingly high. 季度间的增长率出奇的高。| Example: Inter-quarter fluctuations in sales are common in this industry.  这个行业的季度间销售额波动很常见。 | 近义词: between quarters | 反义词: intra-quarter | 词性: 形容词


Etymology:
"inter-" (prefix): from Latin "inter" meaning "between, among"
"quarter": from Old French "quartier" ultimately from Latin "quartarius" meaning "fourth part"
