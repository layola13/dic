martyred:/ˈmɑːrtərd/ | adj. | 殉难的；折磨的；受苦的 | Having been killed because of one's beliefs, especially religious or political beliefs; tormented; suffering |  He was martyred for his faith. 他因其信仰而殉难。| synonyms: sacrificed, immolated, slain, persecuted | antonyms: survivor, spared | adjective

martyred:/ˈmɑːrtərd/ | v. |  使殉难；折磨 | kill (someone) because of their beliefs.  | They martyred him for refusing to renounce his faith. 他们因为他拒绝放弃信仰而将他处死。| synonyms:  execute, kill, slaughter, sacrifice | antonyms: save, rescue | verb
