overdescriptivenesses:/ˌoʊvərˌdɪˈskrɪptɪvnəsɪz/
n.
过度描述
The state or quality of being excessively descriptive; the use of too many details in a description.
Her overdescriptivenesses made the story tedious. 她过多的描述使得故事冗长乏味。
Her prose is marred by overdescriptivenesses. 她的散文败笔在于过多的描述。

近义词:verbosity, prolixity, wordiness
反义词:conciseness, brevity, terseness
词性:noun
