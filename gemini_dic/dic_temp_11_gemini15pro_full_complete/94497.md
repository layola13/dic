importing:/ɪmˈpɔːtɪŋ/ | gerund, n. | 进口，输入 | The act of bringing goods or services into a country from abroad for sale. |  例句：Importing luxury cars is a lucrative business. 进口豪车是一项利润丰厚的业务。

例句：The importing of skilled workers can help fill labor shortages.  引进技术工人可以帮助填补劳动力短缺。

近义词： bringing in, introduction, shipment | 反义词：exporting | 词性：动名词，名词
