cressiest:/ˈkrɛsiɪst/
adjective
最卷曲的，最弯曲的
having the most curves or bends; most curly.
Her hair was the cressiest I'd ever seen, a mass of tight ringlets. 她有着我见过最卷曲的头发，一团团紧紧的卷发。
The plant had the cressiest leaves, spiraling inwards like a fern.  这种植物有着最卷曲的叶子，像蕨类植物一样向内盘旋。
近义词:curliest, waviest, most convoluted
反义词:straightest, flattest
词性:adjective
词源: From "cress," referring to the curly leaves of watercress, + "-y" (adjective forming suffix) + "-est" (superlative suffix).
