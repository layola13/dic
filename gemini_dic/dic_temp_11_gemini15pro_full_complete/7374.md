animalculine: /ˌænɪˈmælkjʊlɪn/ | adj. | 微小的，动物性的，像小动物的 |  Resembling a small animal; tiny; microscopic. |  He peered through the microscope at the animalculine life teeming in the drop of pond water. 他透过显微镜观察池水中大量繁殖的微生物。 | 近义词: microscopic, minuscule, tiny, infinitesimal | 反义词: gigantic, huge, enormous | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From French *animalcule*, diminutive of *animal*, from Latin *animal*, neuter of *animalis* "having breath, living, animate," from *anima* "breath, soul."  The "-ine" suffix adds the sense of "of or pertaining to."
