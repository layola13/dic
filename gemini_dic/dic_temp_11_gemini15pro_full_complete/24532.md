broadest:/ˈbrɔːdɪst/| adj.|最广泛的，最宽阔的|superlative form of broad; having the greatest extent from side to side; widest.|The river is broadest at its mouth. 这条河在入海口最宽。|近义词：widest, most extensive, most comprehensive|反义词：narrowest, smallest, least extensive|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *brodest, from Old English brādōst (“broadest”), superlative form of brād (“broad”). Cognate with Old Frisian brēdst (“broadest”), Dutch breedst (“broadest”), German breitest (“broadest”), Old Norse breiðastr (“broadest”).|
