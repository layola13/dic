chaetopod:/ˈkiːtəˌpɒd/ (US) /ˈkiːtəʊˌpɒd/ (UK)| n. |环节动物|A segmented worm, such as an earthworm or a marine bristle worm, belonging to the phylum Annelida.  Characterized by a body divided into repeating segments, each with bristles called chaetae.|Earthworms are common chaetopods found in soil. 蚯蚓是土壤中常见的环节动物。|The marine chaetopod displays iridescent colors.  这种海洋环节动物展现出彩虹般的颜色。|近义词：annelid|反义词:|noun
