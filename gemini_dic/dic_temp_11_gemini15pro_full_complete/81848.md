grid:/ɡrɪd/| n.|网格，格子，输电网|A network of lines that cross each other to form a series of squares or rectangles.|The city is laid out in a grid pattern. 这座城市以网格状布局。|synonyms: network, lattice, mesh|antonyms: disarray, chaos|noun

grid:/ɡrɪd/| v.|准备，做好准备(常与up连用)，使形成网格|To prepare oneself for something difficult or challenging.|He gritted his teeth and gridded himself for the pain. 他咬紧牙关，准备好迎接痛苦。|synonyms: prepare, brace, ready|antonyms:  disarm, unprepared|verb
