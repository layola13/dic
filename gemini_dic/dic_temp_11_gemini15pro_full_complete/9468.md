antirepublicans:/ˌænti rɪˈpʌblɪkənz/
noun
反共和主义者；反对共和政体的人
Those who oppose republicanism or a republic are called antirepublicans.
The antirepublicans staged a protest against the newly formed government. 反共和主义者举行了抗议新成立的政府的示威活动。
The historian specialized in the writings of antirepublicans during the French Revolution.  这位历史学家专门研究法国大革命时期反共和主义者的著作。
近义词: monarchists, royalists, anti-democrats
反义词: republicans, democrats
词性: n.
