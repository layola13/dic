giving-up:/ˈɡɪvɪŋ ʌp/| n.|放弃，投降|The act of surrendering or ceasing to try.|After numerous attempts, his giving-up was inevitable. 经过多次尝试，他的放弃是不可避免的。|synonyms: surrender, resignation, abandonment|antonyms: perseverance, persistence|noun

giving-up:/ˈɡɪvɪŋ ʌp/| adj.| (Informal, hyphenated when prenominal)  | 放弃的，认输的 | Describing the act of surrendering or ceasing to try. | He had a giving-up look on his face. 他脸上有一种放弃的表情。 | synonyms: defeated, resigned | antonyms: determined, persistent | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From the phrasal verb "give up," composed of the verb "give" (Old English *giefan, from Proto-Germanic *geban) and the adverb/preposition "up" (Old English up, uppe, from Proto-Germanic *upp).
