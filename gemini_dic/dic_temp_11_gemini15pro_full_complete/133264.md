nonexhibitionism:/ˌnɒnˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 非暴露癖，不爱出风头 |  The state or quality of not being exhibitionistic; a lack of desire to show off or attract attention to oneself. |  Her nonexhibitionism made her a calming presence in the otherwise flamboyant group.  她不爱出风头的性格使她在其他浮夸的群体中显得格外平静。 | 近义词: modesty, humility, reserve, discretion | 反义词: exhibitionism, ostentation, flamboyance | 词性: 名词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + exhibitionism (from "exhibition" + "-ism")
