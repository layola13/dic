nonspaciousness:/nɒnˈspeɪʃəsnəs/
n.|非宽敞性；狭窄；缺乏空间|The nonspaciousness of the apartment made it feel cramped. 公寓的狭窄使它感觉很局促。|近义词:crampedness, confinement, smallness|反义词:spaciousness, roominess, vastness|词性:名词
Etymology: non- + spacious + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "spacious" means having ample space, and "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives.
