epitheted:/ˈɛpɪθɛtɪd/
adj.|带有修饰语的，加有绰号的|Characterized by the use of epithets; having a descriptive name or nickname added.|The king, epitheted "the Great," ruled wisely.|近义词:labelled, designated, nicknamed, called|反义词:unnamed, undesignated|词性:形容词

词源:
From the past participle of *epithet*, from French *épithète*, from Latin *epitheton*, from Greek *epítheton*  "something added," from neuter of *epíthetos* "added, attributed," from *epitithenai* "to put upon, add," from *epi* "upon" + *tithenai* "to put."
