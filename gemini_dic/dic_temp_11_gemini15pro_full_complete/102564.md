jaggednesses:/ˈdʒæɡɪdnəsɪz/
n.
参差不齐；锯齿状；粗糙
the state of being jagged or having many sharp points.
The jaggednesses of the rocks made climbing difficult.  岩石的参差不齐使得攀爬变得困难。
近义词: unevenness, roughness, irregularity
反义词: smoothness, evenness
词性: 名词

jagged:/ˈdʒæɡɪd/
adj.
锯齿状的；参差不齐的；不规则的
having a rough or sharp edge; irregular or uneven.
The climber carefully navigated the jagged peaks.  攀登者小心翼翼地绕过锯齿状的山峰。
近义词: rough, uneven, serrated, notched
反义词: smooth, even
词性: 形容词

-ness:/nɪs/
suffix
名词后缀，表示状态或性质
A suffix that forms nouns expressing state or quality.
Happiness is a state of mind. 幸福是一种心态。
近义词: -ity, -hood, -ship
反义词: 无
词性: 后缀

词源:
From jagged + -ness.  来自形容词 jagged 加名词后缀 -ness。  Jagged itself comes from the probably Scandinavian word "jag" meaning a sharp projection.  Jagged 本身可能来自斯堪的纳维亚语单词 "jag"，意思是尖锐的突起。
