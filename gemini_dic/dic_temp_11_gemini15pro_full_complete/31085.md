champer:/ˈtʃeɪmbər/| n.|房间，室；腔，膛；弹膛；议院|A private room, especially a bedroom; An enclosed space or cavity; The part of a gun that holds the cartridge or shell before it is fired; A legislative or judicial body.|例句：The queen retired to her champer. 女王退到了她的房间。|例句：The heart has four champers. 心脏有四个腔室。|近义词：room, chamber, compartment, cavity|反义词:hall, corridor|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French *chambre, from Latin camera "vault, arched roof," from Greek kamara "anything with an arched cover."
