incorrigibility:/ɪnˌkɒrɪdʒəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可救药， incorrigibleness |The incorrigibility of his behavior led to his expulsion from school.  他的行为不可救药，导致他被学校开除。|Synonyms: hopelessness, depravity, inveteracy,  incurableness | Antonyms: corrigibility,  reformability, amendability | noun

incorrigible: /ɪnˈkɒrɪdʒəbl/ | adj. | 不可救药的， incorrigible |He's an incorrigible liar. 他是个无可救药的骗子。|Synonyms: hopeless, incurable, irredeemable, inveterate | Antonyms: corrigible, reformable,  amendable | adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *incorrigibilis, from in- "not" + corrigibilis "corrigible," from corrigere "to correct."
