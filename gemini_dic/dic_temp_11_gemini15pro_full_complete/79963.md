go-ape:/ɡəʊˈeɪp/ | idiom | 发怒；勃然大怒；变得狂野 | To become extremely angry or excited. | He went ape when he found out his car had been stolen. 他发现他的车被偷了，气疯了。 | synonyms: go ballistic, go bananas, lose it, fly off the handle | antonyms: calm down, stay calm | idiom

go-ape:/ɡəʊˈeɪp/ | idiom | 狂热；着迷 | To become extremely enthusiastic or passionate about something. | She's gone ape over that new band. 她对那个新乐队非常着迷。 | synonyms: go crazy for, be wild about, be passionate about | antonyms: be indifferent to, be apathetic about | idiom
