eversion:/ɪˈvɜːrʒən/
n.
外翻，翻转
the act of turning something inside out or outwards.
The eversion of the eyelid can be caused by various medical conditions. 眼睑外翻可能是由各种疾病引起的。

The surgeon performed an eversion of the stomach lining.  外科医生进行了胃粘膜外翻术。

近义词: turning out, turning inside out, extrusion
反义词: inversion, inturning, invagination
词性: noun
