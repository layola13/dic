词源：
"Jerrycan" is a portmanteau word, combining "jerry" (a slang term for Germans during World War II) and "can".  The design of the jerrycan originated in Germany in the 1930s for military use, and its robust and efficient design was quickly adopted by Allied forces.
"Jerrycan"是一个混合词，由"Jerry"（二战期间对德国人的俚语）和"can"（罐子）组合而成。油桶的设计起源于20世纪30年代的德国，用于军事用途，其坚固高效的设计很快被盟军采用。
