flans:/flænz/
n.
果馅饼，蛋奶糕，果冻
An open pastry or sponge case containing a sweet or savoury filling.
例句：She baked a delicious apple flan for dessert. 她烤了一个美味的苹果馅饼作为甜点。

例句：The flan wobbled precariously as he carried it to the table.  他把果馅饼端到桌子上时，它摇摇晃晃的。

近义词：tart, quiche, pie
反义词: (No direct antonym for a food item)
词性: noun


flans:/flænz/
v.
（用果馅饼）招待，供应（flan的第三人称单数）
Serves or provides with flans. (Third person singular present tense of "to flan")
例句：He flans the guests with a selection of homemade pastries.  他用各种自制糕点招待客人。

例句：The restaurant flans its patrons with a complimentary dessert. 这家餐厅为顾客提供免费甜点。

近义词：serves, provides, offers
反义词: deprives, withholds
词性: verb
