noncontracts:/ˌnɑːnˈkɑːntrækts/
n.
非合同; 非契约
Agreements or arrangements that are not legally binding contracts.
The noncontracts between the two parties allowed for flexibility in their dealings.  双方之间的非契约关系使得他们的交易更具灵活性。
These noncontracts are based on trust and mutual understanding. 这些非契约是基于信任和相互理解的。
non-binding agreements, informal agreements
