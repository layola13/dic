dravidian:/drəˈvɪdiən/| adj. n.| 德拉维的；德拉维人；德拉维语 |Relating to or denoting a family of languages spoken in southern India and Sri Lanka, or the peoples who speak them.|  The Dravidian languages are spoken by over 200 million people. 德拉维语系有超过两亿的使用者。|近义词:South Indian (for people)|反义词:Indo-Aryan|词性:形容词, 名词
