ambidextrously:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəsli/
adv.
灵巧地；熟练地
Skillfully using both hands.
She can write ambidextrously, equally well with either hand. 她能用双手熟练地写字，两只手写得一样好。
近义词: skillfully, adeptly, proficiently
反义词: clumsily, awkwardly
词性: 副词

ambidextrous:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs/
adj.
双手灵巧的；左右开弓的
Able to use both hands equally well.
He's an ambidextrous tennis player, able to hit powerful shots with either hand. 他是一位双手灵巧的网球运动员，可以用任何一只手打出强有力的击球。
近义词: versatile, adaptable
反义词: one-handed
词性: 形容词


词源:  From Latin *ambidexter "having two right hands," from ambi- "both" + dexter "right-handed, skillful."
