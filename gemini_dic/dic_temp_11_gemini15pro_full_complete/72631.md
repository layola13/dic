flyless:/ˈflaɪləs/| adj.|无飞行能力的，不能飞的|Unable to fly; lacking the ability or power of flight.|The kiwi is a flyless bird. 几维鸟是一种不会飞的鸟。|The dodo was a large, flyless bird. 渡渡鸟是一种大型的不会飞的鸟。|近义词：flightless, wingless|反义词：winged, volant|词性:形容词

词源:
From fly + -less.  The suffix "-less" is of Old English origin and means "without" or "lacking."  Therefore, "flyless" literally means "without the ability to fly."
