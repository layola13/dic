musclier:/ˈmʌskliər/ (US), /ˈmʌskjʊlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更强壮的，肌肉更发达的|Comparative adjective of muscular, meaning having well-developed muscles. It indicates a greater degree of muscularity than another person or thing being compared.|He looks musclier than he did last year. 他看起来比去年更强壮了。|The bodybuilder was musclier than the average person.  这个健美运动员比一般人肌肉更发达。|近义词：stronger, more muscular, brawnier, huskier, beefier|反义词：weaker, less muscular, flabbier|词性: adjective
词源: From French *musculeux*, from Latin *musculus* ("little mouse," later "muscle").
