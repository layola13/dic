germline:/ˈdʒɜːrmlaɪn/
noun
生殖细胞系，种系
The sequence of DNA that can be passed to offspring.  It includes the genetic material in germ cells (egg and sperm).
Mutations in the germline can be inherited by offspring.  生殖细胞系的突变可以被后代遗传。
synonyms: germ plasm, germplasm
antonyms: somatic
