deliverables:/dɪˈlɪvərəblz/
n.
可交付成果；交付物
Something that must be produced or achieved as a result of a process or project.
The project manager listed the key deliverables for the next quarter.  项目经理列出了下一季度的主要交付成果。
The first deliverable is due next week. 第一个交付成果将于下周到期。
