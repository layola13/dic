basseting:/ˈbæsɪtɪŋ/
n.
垂耳；低矮；矮脚化
The act of breeding or developing animals, especially dogs, to have short legs and a long body.
Basseting is a common practice among breeders of certain dog breeds.  矮脚化是某些犬种繁育者中的常见做法。
The basseting of the dachshund gives it its characteristic long body and short legs.  腊肠犬的矮脚化赋予了它特有的长身体和短腿。
近义词:dwarfing
反义词:elongating
词性:noun
