dezincify:/diːˈzɪŋkɪfaɪ/
verb
除锌
To remove zinc from something, especially from brass.
The process is used to dezincify the brass.  这个过程用于去除黄铜中的锌。
The acidic solution will dezincify the alloy. 酸性溶液会使合金除锌。
Synonyms: extract zinc, remove zinc
Antonyms: zincify, galvanize
词性:动词
