biosphere:/ˈbaɪoʊsfɪr/ | n. | 生物圈 | The regions of the surface and atmosphere of the Earth or another planet occupied by living organisms. |  The biosphere is a vital part of the Earth's systems. 生物圈是地球系统的重要组成部分。

The impact of pollution on the biosphere is devastating. 污染对生物圈的影响是毁灭性的。| 近义词: ecosphere | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From bio- + sphere.  "Bio" comes from the Greek "bios" meaning "life," and "sphere" comes from the Greek "sphaira" meaning "ball" or "globe."
