ethology:/iːˈθɒlədʒi (UK), iˈθɑːlədʒi (US)/| n.|动物行为学|The scientific and objective study of animal behaviour, usually with a focus on behaviour under natural conditions, and viewing behaviour as an evolutionarily adaptive trait.|例句：Researchers in ethology have made significant contributions to our understanding of animal communication. 动物行为学研究人员对我们理解动物交流做出了重大贡献。

例句：The ethology of primates offers insights into the evolution of social behavior. 灵长类动物的行为学为了解社会行为的进化提供了见解。|近义词：animal behavior, zoology|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "anthropology" as a contrasting field)|词性:noun
