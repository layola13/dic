melanism:/ˈmɛlənɪzəm/ | n. | 黑色素沉着；黑化 |The development of dark-colored pigment in the skin, feathers, or hair of animals, resulting in an unusually dark appearance. |  Melanism in leopards is a common phenomenon in dense forests.  豹子的黑化现象在茂密的森林中很常见。

Melanism is an adaptation that can help animals camouflage in their environment.  黑化是一种适应性特征，可以帮助动物在环境中伪装。| 近义词: hyperpigmentation | 反义词: albinism, leucism | 词性: noun
