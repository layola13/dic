nonsuccessive:/ˌnɒnsəkˈsesɪv/ | adj. | 非连续的，不连续的 | Not following one after the other without interruption; not consecutive. | The meetings were nonsuccessive, occurring sporadically throughout the year. 这些会议不是连续的，而是全年零星地举行。| The athlete suffered nonsuccessive injuries, making it difficult to train consistently.  这位运动员遭受了不连续的伤病，这使得他很难持续训练。 | 近义词: nonconsecutive, intermittent, sporadic | 反义词: consecutive, successive | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (not) + successive (following one after the other)  来自拉丁语 success-, 意思是 "跟随"。
