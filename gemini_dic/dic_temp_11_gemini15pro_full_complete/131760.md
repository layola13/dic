nonconjecturable:/ˌnɒnkənˈdʒɛktʃərəbl/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈdʒɛktʃərəbl/
adjective
不可推测的；无法猜想的
Not able to be conjectured or guessed.
The true motives behind the crime remain nonconjecturable.  这起犯罪背后的真正动机仍然无法推测。
His intentions were completely nonconjecturable. 他的意图完全无法猜测。
近义词:inscrutable, unfathomable, imponderable, indecipherable
反义词:obvious, evident, clear, apparent
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + conjecturable, from Late Latin conjecturabilis, from Latin conjectura ("conjecture") + -able.
