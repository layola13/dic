indulger:/ɪnˈdʌldʒər/| n.|放纵者；沉溺者；纵容者|A person who indulges in something, especially something considered pleasurable or wrong.  Someone who yields to their desires or allows themselves to enjoy something to excess.|He was an indulger of fine wines and rich food. 他是个沉迷于美酒佳肴的人。|Synonyms: hedonist, epicurean, sybarite, bon vivant|Antonyms: ascetic, abstainer|noun
