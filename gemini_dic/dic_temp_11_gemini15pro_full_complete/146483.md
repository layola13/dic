overfurnishing: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrnɪʃɪŋ/ | n. | 过度装修；家具过多 | The act of furnishing a room or house with too much furniture, resulting in a cluttered or cramped appearance. |  The overfurnishing of the small apartment made it feel even smaller.  这套小公寓的家具太多，让它感觉更小了。 |  clutter, excess furniture | sparseness, minimalism | noun

overfurnish: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrnɪʃ/ | vt. | 过度装修；布置过多家具 | To furnish a room or house with too much furniture. | They overfurnished their living room, leaving little space to move around. 他们把客厅里的家具放得太多，几乎没有活动的空间了。 | overcrowd, clutter | underfurnish | verb
