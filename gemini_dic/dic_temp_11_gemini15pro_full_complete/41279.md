cordialize: /ˈkɔːrdʒəlaɪz/ /ˈkɔːrdiəlaɪz/ | v. |使融洽；使和睦；使友好；热诚地对待 |To make cordial; to make friendly or heartfelt.  | We must cordialize our relations with neighboring countries. 我们必须与邻国建立友好关系。| The two families cordialized their differences after years of feuding.  这两个家庭在多年的不和之后和解了。 | 近义词: reconcile, befriend, appease | 反义词: alienate, estrange | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From cordial + -ize.  "Cordial" comes from the Late Latin "cordialis,"  meaning "of the heart," from Latin "cor," meaning "heart."  The suffix "-ize" forms verbs meaning "to make, render, or become."
