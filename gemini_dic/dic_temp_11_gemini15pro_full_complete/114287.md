mainmast:/ˈmeɪnˌmɑːst/ | n. | 主桅 |The principal mast of a sailing vessel.  The tallest mast on a ship, usually located amidships. | The mainmast snapped in the storm.  主桅在暴风雨中折断了。| The ship's mainmast towered above the harbor.  船的主桅高耸于海港之上。 | 近义词: main, principal mast | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可以考虑 mizzenmast, foremast 指其他桅杆) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *maynmast*, from Old English *mægenmæst* (“mainmast”), equivalent to *mægen* (“main, strength, power”) + *mæst* (“mast”). Cognate with Middle Low German *mēnmast* (“mainmast”), Dutch *meezenmast* (“mainmast”).
