decumbent: /dɪˈkʌmbənt/
| adj. | 躺着的；斜倚的；俯卧的 | lying down; reclining | The photograph showed a decumbent figure resting beneath a tree. 照片显示一个躺卧的身影在树下休息。| Synonyms: recumbent, reclining, prostrate, supine | Antonyms: upright, erect, standing, vertical | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *decumbentem*, present participle of *decumbere* "to lie down," from *de-* "down" + *-cumbere* "to lie."
