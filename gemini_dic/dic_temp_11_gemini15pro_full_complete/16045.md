bandelet:/ˈbændəlɪt/ | n. | 小带，细带；（纹章学）横带 | A small band or ribbon; in heraldry, a diminutive of the bend. | The knight's armor was adorned with a bandelet bearing his family crest.  骑士的盔甲上装饰着一条带有家族徽章的细带。| Synonym: ribbon, strip, strap | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "wide band") | Noun
