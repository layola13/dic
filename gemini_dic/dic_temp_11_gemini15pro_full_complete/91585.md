hyperacutely:/ˌhaɪpərəˈkjutli/| adv.|极度急性地，非常迅速地|Characterized by extreme acuteness or suddenness.  Referring to a process or condition that develops very rapidly and intensely, often with a severe or critical nature.|The patient hyperacutely reacted to the medication. 病人对药物的反应非常迅速剧烈。|Near synonyms: extremely acutely, very rapidly, intensely acutely|Near antonyms: gradually, slowly, mildly|词性:副词
