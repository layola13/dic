grasslike:/ˈɡræslaɪk/ | adj. | 像草的；草似的 | Resembling grass, especially in long, narrow leaves. | The plant has grasslike leaves. 这种植物有像草一样的叶子。| The sedge had long, grasslike leaves. 这种莎草有长长的、像草一样的叶子。| 近义词: grassy, reedy | 反义词: treelike, shrubby | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From grass + -like.  "Grass" comes from Old English "græs," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*grasan-" (to grow, sprout).  "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling," from Old English "-līc."
