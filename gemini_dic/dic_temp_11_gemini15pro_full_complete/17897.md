beeper:/ˈbiːpər/| n.|寻呼机，传呼机，蜂鸣器|A small electronic device that makes a beeping sound, often used for paging or signaling.  A beeper can also refer to a device that emits a regular beeping sound, like a timer or alarm.|My doctor is on call and carries a beeper. 我的医生随时待命，随身带着一个寻呼机。

The oven timer's beeper went off, signaling that the cookies were ready. 烤箱计时器的蜂鸣器响了，提示饼干已经烤好了。|近义词：pager, buzzer, alarm|反义词:|noun
