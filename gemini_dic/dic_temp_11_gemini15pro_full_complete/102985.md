jeep:/dʒiːp/| n.|吉普车；小型越野车|A small, sturdy motor vehicle with four-wheel drive, especially one used by the military.|例句：He drove his jeep across the rough terrain. 他开着他的吉普车穿越了崎岖的地形。

例句：The army uses jeeps for reconnaissance missions. 军队使用吉普车进行侦察任务。

近义词：four-wheel drive, SUV, off-road vehicle|反义词:car, sedan|词性:noun
