discourteousnesses:/dɪsˈkɜːrtɪəsnəsɪz/
n.
无礼，粗鲁行为(复数)
The plural form of discourteousness, referring to multiple instances of rude or impolite behavior.
His discourteousnesses to the staff were unacceptable. 他对员工的无礼行为是不可接受的。
The numerous discourteousnesses during the meeting disrupted the proceedings. 会议期间的多次无礼行为扰乱了会议进程。
近义词:rudeness, impoliteness, incivility
反义词:courtesy, politeness, civility
词性:名词

Etymology:
From discourteous + -ness.  Discourteous itself comes from the Old French descourtois, meaning "rude" or "impolite."  The prefix dis- indicates negation, and courteous derives ultimately from the Latin "cohors," meaning "court."
