obloquy:/ˈɒbləkwɪ/ /ˈɑːbləkwɪ/| n. | 辱骂；诽谤；恶名 | Strong public criticism or verbal abuse. |  He endured years of obloquy after the scandal. 他在丑闻之后忍受了多年的诽谤。 |  Example: The disgraced politician lived the rest of his days in obloquy.  这位声名狼藉的政客在余生中都生活在骂名之中。|Synonyms:  disgrace, dishonor, shame, ignominy, opprobrium, vituperation, slander, calumny | Antonyms: praise, acclaim, honor, respect | noun
