chondrite:/ˈkɑːndraɪt/| n.|球粒陨石|A stony meteorite containing small spherical bodies called chondrules.|Scientists studied the chondrite to learn more about the early solar system. 科学家们研究球粒陨石以了解更多关于早期太阳系的信息。|Meteorite, stony meteorite|achondrite|noun
Etymology->
From Ancient Greek χόνδρος (khóndros, “grain, groats”) + -ite.
