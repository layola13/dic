contoured:/ˈkɒntʊəd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntʊrd/ (US)| adj.|轮廓分明的，有轮廓的|Having an outline or surface that curves in and out.|She wore a contoured dress that accentuated her figure. 她穿着一件凸显身材的紧身连衣裙。|近义词：shaped, sculpted, molded|反义词：flat, shapeless|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French "contourné," past participle of "contourner" (to go around), from "con-" (with) + "tourner" (to turn). Ultimately from Latin "tornus" (lathe).
