capitally:/ˈkæpɪtəli/| adv.|极好地，出色地，要命地，死|A performance done capitally. 表演得非常出色。|近义词：excellently, superbly, wonderfully, admirably, first-rate|反义词：poorly, badly, terribly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *capitaliter, from Latin capitalis (“of the head, chief, principal”), from caput (“head”).
