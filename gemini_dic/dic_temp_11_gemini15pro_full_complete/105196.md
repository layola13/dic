keyed-in:/kiːdˈɪn/| adj.|输入的，键入的|Typed in using a keyboard.  Data is keyed-in by the operator.|The data was keyed-in incorrectly, leading to errors in the report.  数据输入错误，导致报告出现错误。|input, entered, typed|handwritten, analog|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->From "key" (referring to keyboard keys) + "in" (indicating the action of inputting).  Relatively modern term, arising with the widespread use of computers and keyboards.
