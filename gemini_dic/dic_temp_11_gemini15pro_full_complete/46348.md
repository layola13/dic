dactylic:/dækˈtɪlɪk/| adj.|抑扬格的，长短短格的|Of or relating to a dactyl. A dactyl is a metrical foot consisting of one stressed syllable followed by two unstressed syllables.|The poem was written in dactylic hexameter.|Near synonyms:  anapestic, rhythmic|Antonym: iambic|Adjective
