hyloist:/ˈhaɪlɔɪɪst/
n.
物质生命论者，泛心论者
Someone who believes in hyloism (the doctrine that matter is the only reality and that mind or spirit is merely a manifestation of matter).
Hyloists believe that consciousness arises from physical processes in the brain. 物质生命论者认为意识源于大脑的物理过程。
The philosopher argued against hyloism, proposing instead a dualist view of mind and body.  这位哲学家反对物质生命论，转而提出身心二元论的观点。
materialist, physicalist
idealist, dualist
noun
