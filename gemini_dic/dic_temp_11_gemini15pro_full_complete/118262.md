merchandising:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 商品推销，商品销售；销售规划 | the activity of promoting the sale of goods, especially by their presentation in retail outlets. |  The store's visual merchandising is very appealing.  这家商店的视觉营销非常吸引人。 | synonyms: marketing, promotion, salesmanship | antonyms:  procurement, purchasing | noun

merchandizings: This is not a standard English word. The singular form, "merchandising," is typically used and doesn't have a plural form in this context.  If referring to multiple merchandising strategies, one would say "merchandising strategies" or "merchandising efforts."
