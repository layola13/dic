inurbane:/ˌɪnɜːrˈbeɪn/
adjective
粗鲁的，不文雅的
Lacking refinement or good manners; rude and uncouth.
例句：His inurbane manner made him unpopular at social gatherings.  他粗鲁的举止让他在社交聚会上不受欢迎。

例句：She found his inurbane remarks offensive. 她觉得他粗鲁的言论令人反感。

近义词：rude, impolite, uncivil, discourteous, boorish, churlish
反义词：polite, courteous, civil, refined, urbane, sophisticated
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *inurbanus*, from in- 'not' + urbanus 'of the city, urbane'.
