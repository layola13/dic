frowner:/ˈfraʊnər/| n. |皱眉者，不悦者|A person who frowns; someone who has a frowning expression. |He's a real frowner, always seems to be disapproving of something. 他真是个爱皱眉的人，似乎总是对某些事情不满意。|近义词：grouch, complainer, pessimist|反义词: smiler, optimist|词性: noun
