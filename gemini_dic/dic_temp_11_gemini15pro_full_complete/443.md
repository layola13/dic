ablegate:/ˈæblɪɡeɪt/
noun
特使；代表；使节
A person who is sent by another person or organization to represent them, especially at a conference or meeting.
The Pope sent an ablegate to the conference. 教皇派了一名特使参加会议。
Synonyms: envoy, delegate, representative, emissary, commissioner, legate, ambassador, agent
Antonyms: receiver, recipient

Etymology:
From Late Latin *ablegatus, from Latin ablegare (“to send away, dispatch (on a mission), delegate”), from ab- + legare (“to send as an ambassador, delegate, bequeath”), from legatus (“ambassador, envoy, legate”).
