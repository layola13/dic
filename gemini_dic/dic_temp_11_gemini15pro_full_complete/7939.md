anterooms:/ˈæntiˌruːmz/ /ˈæntiˌrʊmz/| n. |前厅，接待室|A small room leading into a larger one.|例句：Guests waited in the anteroom before being admitted to the main hall. 客人们在被允许进入大厅之前在前厅等候。

例句：The anteroom served as a waiting area for patients. 前厅用作病人的等候区。|近义词：lobby, waiting room, foyer, reception area|反义词: main hall, principal room|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "anti-chambre," from "anti-" (before) + "chambre" (room).  Ultimately from Latin "antecamera" (ante- + camera).
