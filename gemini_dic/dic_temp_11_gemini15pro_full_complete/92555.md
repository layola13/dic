hysteresis:/ˌhɪs.təˈriː.sɪs/ /ˌhɪs.təˈriː.səs/| n.|滞后现象；迟滞现象|The hysteresis of the material makes it suitable for use in magnetic storage devices. 这种材料的磁滞现象使其适用于磁存储设备。|例句：The hysteresis of the thermostat caused the temperature to fluctuate.  恒温器的滞后现象导致温度波动。

例句：The economic hysteresis caused long-term unemployment after the recession. 经济滞后现象在经济衰退后造成了长期失业。|近义词：lag, delay, retardation|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


Etymology->
From Ancient Greek ὑστέρησις (hustérēsis), from ὑστερέω (hustéreō, “I am behind, I come too late”), from ὕστερος (hústeros, “later”).
