cheliform:/ˈkiːlɪfɔːrm/| adj.|螯形的，钳形的|Shaped like the claw of a crab or other crustacean.  Resembling a chela; pincer-shaped.|The cheliform appendages of the crab were powerful and adept at crushing shells.|近义词：chelate, forcipate|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "smooth" or "unsegmented" in context)|词性: adjective
