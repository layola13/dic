destitute:/ˈdɛstɪˌtjut/ /ˈdɛstɪˌtuːt/| adj. n.|贫困的，赤贫的；缺乏的，没有的|Without the basic necessities of life; lacking something needed or desirable.|例句：The earthquake left many people destitute. 地震使许多人一贫如洗。|近义词：impoverished, indigent, penniless, needy, deprived|反义词：wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous|词性:形容词

destitutes:/ˈdɛstɪˌtuts/ /ˈdɛstɪˌtuːts/| n.|穷人，贫民|People who are destitute.|例句：The charity provides food and shelter for destitutes.  这个慈善机构为穷人提供食物和住所。|近义词：the poor, the needy, the impoverished|反义词：the rich, the wealthy, the affluent|词性:名词
