double-books:/ˈdʌbəl bʊks/
verb
重复预订；双重预订
to make two or more reservations for the same person or thing at the same time
I accidentally double-booked myself for two meetings on Friday. 我不小心在星期五安排了两个会议。
The restaurant was double-booked and had to turn people away.  这家餐厅被重复预订了，不得不把一些客人拒之门外。
overbook, reserve twice
