appalling:/əˈpɔːlɪŋ/
adjective
令人震惊的；骇人听闻的
causing shock or dismay; horrific.
The conditions in the refugee camp were appalling.  难民营的条件骇人听闻。
The weather has been appalling all week. 这一周的天气一直非常糟糕。
近义词:horrific, terrible, dreadful, awful, shocking, ghastly
反义词:wonderful, excellent, marvelous, delightful
词性:adjective

词源:
From Old French *apaler "to make pale," from a- "to" + palir "to grow pale," from Latin pallere "to be pale."  The sense shifted from "making pale with fear" to "filling with horror."
