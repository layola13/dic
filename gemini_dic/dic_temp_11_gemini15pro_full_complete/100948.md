inundators:/ˈɪnʌndeɪtərz/
n.
淹没者；泛滥者；使充满的人（或物）
People who or things that inundate something or someone.
The inundators of the market with cheap goods drove many local businesses to bankruptcy.  市场上充斥着廉价商品的倾销者导致许多当地企业破产。
flooders, overwhelmers
drainers, relievers
noun
