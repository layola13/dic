ectosarc:/ˈektəʊsɑːrk/| n. |外质|The outer, non-granular, hyaline layer of cytoplasm in amoeboid cells.|The amoeba extends its ectosarc to form pseudopods. 变形虫伸出它的外质形成伪足。|Synonyms: ectoplasm|Antonyms: endosarc|noun
ectosarc:/ˈektəʊsɑːrk/| n. |外胚层|The outer layer of the cytoplasm of a cell.|The ectosarc is responsible for cell movement and protection. 外胚层负责细胞运动和保护。|Synonyms: ectoplasm|Antonyms: endoderm|noun
