half-rises:/ˈhɑːf raɪzɪz/
noun
半起身；半起立
A movement where you lift your upper body partially, but not fully, from a lying or sitting position.
The patient did a series of half-rises to strengthen his abdominal muscles. 病人做了一系列的半起身来增强他的腹部肌肉。
partial rises, mini sit-ups
full sit-ups, lying flat
n.
