moggan:/ˈmɒɡən/ (UK) /ˈmɑːɡən/ (US)| n.|无舵雪橇，平底雪橇|A sled without runners, used for sliding downhill over snow and ice.  A simple sled, usually made of wood or plastic, with a flat bottom and curved front, used for sliding downhill over snow. It is steered by shifting weight or dragging feet.|例句：We rode a moggan down the snowy hill. 我们坐着无舵雪橇滑下山坡。

例句：The children were having fun with their moggans on the snowy slope. 孩子们在雪坡上玩他们的平底雪橇玩得很开心。|近义词：sled, toboggan, luge|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
