implicate:/ˈɪmplɪkeɪt/ | v. | 牵连；暗示；表明 | To show that someone or something is involved in something bad or criminal; to suggest that something is true without saying it directly. | The evidence implicates him in the robbery. 证据表明他参与了抢劫。 | synonyms: incriminate, involve, entangle | antonyms: absolve, exonerate | verb

implicate:/ˈɪmplɪkeɪt/ | v. |  意味着；必然包含 | To have as a necessary consequence or effect. |  His decision implicates a change in policy. 他的决定意味着政策上的改变。 | synonyms: entail, imply | antonyms: exclude | verb
