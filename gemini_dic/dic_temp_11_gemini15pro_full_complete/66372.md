exclusive: /ɪkˈskluːsɪv/ (US), /ɪkˈskluːsɪv/ (UK)| adj. n.|独有的，排外的；独家新闻|Intended or available for only a particular person or group; not shared.| This room is for the exclusive use of guests.  这个房间是专供客人使用的。|近义词：sole, private, restricted|反义词: inclusive, shared, general|词性:形容词

exclusive: /ɪkˈskluːsɪv/ (US), /ɪkˈskluːsɪv/ (UK)| n.|独家新闻，独家经营的项目|An item or story published or broadcast by only one source.|The newspaper ran an exclusive on the mayor's resignation. 这家报纸独家报道了市长辞职的消息。|近义词：scoop|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin exclusivus, from excludere ‘to shut out’.
