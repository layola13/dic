overfeasted: /ˌoʊvərˈfiːstɪd/ | adj. | 吃过量的，暴饮暴食的 | Having eaten too much; excessively indulged in food. |  After the Thanksgiving dinner, I felt completely overfeasted. 感恩节晚餐后，我感觉吃得太多了。|  I was so overfeasted I could barely move. 我吃得太多了，几乎动不了了。| 近义词: overindulged, stuffed, glutted | 反义词: hungry, starved | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessively) + "feast" (a large meal, especially a celebratory one).  "Feast" ultimately comes from the Old French "feste" and Latin "festa," meaning "holiday, feast day."
