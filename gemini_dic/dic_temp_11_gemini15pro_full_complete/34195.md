clamper:/ˈklæmpər/| n.|钳子；夹子；夹紧装置；(电子)钳位电路|A device that holds something tightly in place, or an electronic circuit that limits the voltage of a signal.|The carpenter used a clamper to hold the two pieces of wood together. 木匠用夹子把两块木头夹在一起。|The circuit uses a clamper to prevent voltage spikes. 电路使用钳位电路来防止电压尖峰。| synonyms: clamp, fastener, grip, holder | antonyms: releaser |noun


clamper:/ˈklæmpər/| v.|夹紧，固定|To fasten or fix something firmly in place.|He clamped the papers together with a bulldog clip. 他用夹子把文件夹在一起。|She clamped her hand over her mouth to stifle a scream. 她用手捂住嘴以抑制尖叫。|synonyms: clamp, fasten, secure, fix |antonyms: release, loosen, unfasten |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "clamp" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Clamp" comes from Middle Low German or Middle Dutch "klampe," meaning "a lump, mass, or clamp."
