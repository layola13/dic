designful:/dɪˈzaɪnfʊl/
adj.
富有设计的，设计精巧的
Showing or displaying good design; well-designed.
Her apartment was designful, with carefully chosen furniture and art. 她的公寓设计精美，家具和艺术品都经过精心挑选。
近义词: well-designed, stylish, artistic, creative
反义词: plain, simple, uninspired, unimaginative
词性: 形容词

词源: design + -ful.  "Design" comes from the Old French "desseigner" (to mark out, designate, contrive), ultimately from Latin "designare" (to mark out).  The suffix "-ful" means "full of" or "characterized by".
