ambidexterity:/ˌæmbɪdɛkˈstɛrɪti/| n. |灵巧，双手都善用，双撇子|The ability to use both hands equally well.  It can also refer to being skillful in two different areas or activities.|She demonstrated ambidexterity by writing equally well with both hands. 她展示了她的灵巧，两只手都能写得很好。|He showed political ambidexterity by appealing to both liberal and conservative voters. 他通过同时吸引自由派和保守派选民，展现了政治上的左右逢源。| 近义词:versatility, adaptability, two-handedness|反义词:clumsiness, awkwardness, one-handedness|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *ambidexteritas, from Latin ambidexter "having two right hands; skillful; double-dealing," from ambi- "both" + dexter "right-handed; skillful."
