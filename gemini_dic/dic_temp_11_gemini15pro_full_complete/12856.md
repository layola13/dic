assonances: /ˈæsənənsɪz/ | n. |  类音, 谐音,  |  The repetition of vowel sounds in nearby words. It is used as a literary device to create internal rhyming within phrases or sentences. |  "The rain in Spain falls mainly on the plain" is a famous example of assonance. 西班牙的雨主要下在平原上，这是一个著名的类音例子。 |  rhyme, consonance, alliteration | dissonance | noun
