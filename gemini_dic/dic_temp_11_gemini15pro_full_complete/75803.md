fumed:/fjuːmd/
adjective
冒烟的；熏制的；愤怒的
filled with or smelling of smoke or fumes; also, enraged
例句：He slammed the door and stormed out, fumed. 他砰地一声关上门，怒气冲冲地走了出去。

例句：The fumed oak cabinets gave the kitchen a rustic feel. 熏制橡木橱柜给厨房增添了乡村气息。

近义词：angered, enraged, infuriated, smoky, smoke-filled
反义词：calm, pleased, tranquil, clear, ventilated
词性：adjective


fume:/fjuːm/
noun
烟；气体；愤怒
gas, smoke, or vapor, especially if irritating or offensive; also, a state of anger
例句：The fumes from the factory choked the nearby residents. 工厂排放的烟雾呛得附近居民喘不过气来。

例句：She was in a fume after her flight was delayed. 她的航班延误后，她非常生气。

近义词：smoke, gas, vapor, anger, rage
反义词：calm, tranquility
词性：noun


fume:/fjuːm/
verb
冒烟；熏制；发怒
emit gas, smoke, or vapor; also, to be in a state of anger
例句：The volcano fumed for days after the initial eruption. 火山爆发后的几天里一直冒着烟。

例句：He fumed about the unfair treatment he received. 他对受到的不公平待遇感到愤怒。

近义词：smoke, smolder, rage, seethe
反义词：calm down, relax
词性：verb
