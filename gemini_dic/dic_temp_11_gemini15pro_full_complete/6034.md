amenable:/əˈmiːnəbl/ /əˈmenəbl/| adj.|顺从的，服从的；易受影响的；经得起检验的|Open to suggestion; willing to be persuaded or controlled; capable of being tested or examined.|The children are generally amenable to discipline. 孩子们通常遵守纪律。|Synonyms: agreeable, compliant, receptive, responsive, susceptible|Antonyms: intractable, uncooperative, resistant, unresponsive, unwilling|adjective

amenable:/əˈmiːnəbl/ /əˈmenəbl/| adj.|经得起检验的|Capable of being tested or examined.|The theory is amenable to experimental verification.  这个理论经得起实验验证。|Synonyms: testable, verifiable|Antonyms: untestable, unverifiable|adjective
