词源:
From probably Low German *huken, *hurken (“to squat, sit”), from Middle Low German hūken (“to squat, sit”). Related to Dutch hurken (“to squat”), German hocken (“to squat, crouch”).  可能来自低地德语 *huken, *hurken（“蹲下，坐下”），来自中古低地德语 hūken（“蹲下，坐下”）。 与荷兰语 hurken（“蹲下”）、德语 hocken（“蹲下、蜷缩”）相关。
