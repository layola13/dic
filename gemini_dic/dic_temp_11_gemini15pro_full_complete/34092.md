n.
文明状态；文明行为；有教养
The state or quality of being civilized; refined or cultivated behavior.
The museum showcased the civilizednesses of ancient Greece.  这家博物馆展示了古希腊的文明。
Her civilizednesses at the dinner table impressed everyone. 她在餐桌上的文明举止给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
近义词: civility, refinement, culture, politeness, courtesy
反义词: barbarity, savagery, rudeness, incivility


词性: 名词 (Plural Noun)
词源:  From civilized + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin *civilis (civil) + -ized (suffix forming adjectives) + -ness.
