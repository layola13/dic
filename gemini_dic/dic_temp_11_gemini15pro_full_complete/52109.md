diamonded:/ˈdaɪəməndɪd/
adj.
镶嵌钻石的; 钻石状的
Adorned or inlaid with diamonds; shaped like a diamond.
Her diamonded tiara sparkled under the lights. 她镶钻的冕状头饰在灯光下闪闪发光。
The window was composed of diamonded panes. 窗户是由菱形玻璃组成的。
近义词: diamond-encrusted, diamond-studded
反义词: plain, unembellished
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From diamond + -ed (suffix forming adjectives).  The word "diamond" itself ultimately comes from the Greek "adámas," meaning "invincible" or "unbreakable," via Old French "diamant."
