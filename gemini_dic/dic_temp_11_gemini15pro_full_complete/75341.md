frosting-on-the-cake:/ˈfrɒstɪŋ ɒn ðə keɪk/ (US) /ˈfrɒstɪŋ ɒn ðə keɪk/ (UK)| n.|锦上添花之物;额外的好处|Something that makes a good situation even better.  It refers to an added bonus or extra benefit that enhances an already positive outcome.|例句：Winning the game was great, but getting the MVP award was the frosting on the cake. 赢得比赛很棒，但获得MVP奖是锦上添花。

例句：Getting a promotion was wonderful, and the pay raise was the frosting on the cake.  升职很棒，加薪更是锦上添花。|近义词：bonus, extra, perk, cherry on top|反义词:disappointment, drawback|词性:noun
