multiserially:/ˌmʌltiˈsɪəriəli/
adverb
多列地，多排地
occurring in multiple series or rows.  Arranged in many rows.
The segments of the millipede are multiserially arranged.  这千足虫的体节是多列排列的。
The muscle fibers are attached multiserially to the tendon. 肌肉纤维多列地附着在肌腱上。
近义词:  polyseriate
反义词: uniseriate, uniserial
词性: adverb
