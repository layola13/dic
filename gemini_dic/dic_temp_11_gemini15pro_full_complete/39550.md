conscienceless:/ˈkɒnʃənsləs/ (US) /ˈkɑːnʃənsləs/| adj.|无良的，没良心的|Lacking a conscience; without moral scruples.| He showed a conscienceless disregard for the safety of others. 他对他人安全的漠视毫无良心。|近义词：unscrupulous, amoral, ruthless, heartless|反义词：conscientious, scrupulous, moral|词性:形容词

conscience:/ˈkɒnʃəns/ (US) /ˈkɑːnʃəns/| n.|良心，道德心|An inner feeling or voice viewed as acting as a guide to the rightness or wrongness of one's behavior.| My conscience wouldn't let me lie. 我的良心不允许我说谎。|近义词：morals, scruples|反义词：N/A|词性:名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French conscience, from Latin conscientia, from conscient- ‘knowing with’, from the verb conscire, from con- ‘together’ + scire ‘know’.
