dowerless:/ˈdaʊərlɪs/
adjective
无嫁妆的；没有天赋的
Without a dower (the property a woman brings to her husband at marriage); lacking natural gifts or advantages.
She was a dowerless but ambitious young woman. 她是一位没有嫁妆但雄心勃勃的年轻女子。
synonyms: portionless, unendowed, impoverished, indigent
antonyms: wealthy, rich, endowed, affluent
词性: adjective
