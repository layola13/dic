globalizations:/ˌɡloʊbələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |全球化（複數）| The process by which businesses or other organizations develop influence or start operating on an international scale, whereby national and regional economies, societies, and cultures have become integrated through a global network of trade, communication, immigration and transportation. | Example 1: The globalizations of markets have led to increased competition. 全球化的市场导致竞争加剧。

Example 2:  The protests highlighted concerns about the negative impacts of globalizations. 抗议活动突出了人们对全球化负面影响的担忧。 | Synonym: internationalization, globalization (singular), mundialization | Antonym: localization, regionalization | noun
