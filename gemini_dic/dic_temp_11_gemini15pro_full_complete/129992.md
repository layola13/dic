non-necessity:/ˌnɒn nəˈsesəti/ | n. | 非必需品；不必要；非必要性 | The report highlighted the non-necessity of the proposed new building.  这份报告强调了拟建新楼的不必要性。|  Luxury items are, by definition, non-necessities. 奢侈品，顾名思义，就是非必需品。 | 近义词: inessentiality, nonessential, luxury | 反义词: necessity, essential, requirement | 词性: noun

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + necessity (from Latin *necessitas*, "compulsion, need")
