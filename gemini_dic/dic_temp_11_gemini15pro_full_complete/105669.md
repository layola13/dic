kingmaking:/ˈkɪŋmeɪkɪŋ/
n.|造王者，拥立国王|The act of influencing the choice of a ruler, especially a king.  This can involve political maneuvering, financial support, or even military force. |The union leader's endorsement was a crucial act of kingmaking in the mayoral election.| 近义词: power broking, political maneuvering|反义词: rebellion, deposition|词性:名词
