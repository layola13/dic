nonfeeblest:/nɒnˈfiːblɪst/
adjective
最不虚弱的; 最强壮的
Not the feeblest; strongest or most robust.  "Non-" negates "feeblest," creating a superlative meaning.
例句：He was the nonfeeblest of the group, easily lifting the heavy box. 他是小组里最强壮的，轻松地举起了沉重的箱子。

例句：The nonfeeblest argument for change came from the community most affected.  最有力的改革论据来自受影响最大的社区。
近义词：strongest, most robust, most powerful, sturdiest
反义词：feeblest, weakest, frailest
词性: adjective


词源:
"Non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "feeble" (from Old French "feble," meaning "weak") + "-est" (superlative suffix).
