bumkins:/ˈbʌmkɪnz/| n. |乡巴佬，土包子|An unsophisticated or socially awkward person from the countryside. |例句：The city slickers laughed at the bumkins who came in for the market.  城里那些油嘴滑舌的人嘲笑那些进城赶集的乡巴佬。

例句：He felt like a bumkin when he visited the sophisticated art gallery. 当他参观那家高雅的艺术画廊时，他觉得自己像个土包子。|近义词：yokel, rustic, hick, country bumpkin|反义词:sophisticate, cosmopolitan|词性:noun
