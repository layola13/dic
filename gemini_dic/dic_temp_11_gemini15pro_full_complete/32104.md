chestfuls:/ˈtʃɛstfʊlz/
noun
满满的几箱；几胸膛
A quantity that would fill several chests.
He carried chestfuls of gold coins from the dragon's lair. 他从龙穴里搬出了几箱满满的金币。
Near synonyms:  loads, heaps, piles
Opposite: handful
词性:名词
