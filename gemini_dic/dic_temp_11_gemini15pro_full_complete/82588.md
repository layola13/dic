guardhouse:/ˈɡɑːrdhaʊs/ | n. | 警卫室；岗亭 | A small building for a guard or guards, typically at the entrance to a military base, prison, or other secure area. | The guardhouse was located at the main gate of the base. 警卫室位于基地的正门。

The prisoner was taken to the guardhouse. 囚犯被带到了警卫室。 | 近义词：gatehouse, guardroom, security post | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
