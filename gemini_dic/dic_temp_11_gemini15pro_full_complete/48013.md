decipherments:/dɪˈsaɪfərmənts/| n. |解密，破译|The act of deciphering something, or the results of this act.  The text is full of obscure literary allusions and private jokes, resisting easy decipherment. 这篇文章充满了晦涩的文学典故和私人笑话，难以轻易解读。|例句：The decipherments of the ancient scrolls revealed a lost civilization.  古代卷轴的破译揭示了一个失落的文明。

例句：Recent decipherments of Mayan hieroglyphs have shed new light on their culture.  最近对玛雅象形文字的破译为他们的文化提供了新的线索。|近义词：interpretations, translations, solutions, decodings|反义词:encryptions, codifications|词性:名词
