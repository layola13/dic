absorptivity:/əbˌsɔːpˈtɪvɪti/
noun
吸收率；吸收性
The absorptivity of a material is its ability to absorb radiation.
一种材料的吸收率是其吸收辐射的能力。
The absorptivity of the black surface is very high.
黑色表面的吸收率非常高。

absorption, absorbance
reflectivity, transmittance
n.
