mummying: /ˈmʌmiɪŋ/ | n. | 木乃伊化；使成木乃伊；防腐处理 | The process of preserving a body by embalming and wrapping it in cloth, as practiced in ancient Egypt. | The mummying process involved removing the internal organs and wrapping the body in linen bandages.  木乃伊化过程包括移除内脏器官，并用亚麻绷带包裹尸体。 |  embalming, preservation | decay, decomposition | noun

mummying: /ˈmʌmiɪŋ/ | v. |  将…制成木乃伊；使…干燥如木乃伊 | To make (a body) into a mummy; to preserve (something) by drying. |  The ancient Egyptians mummified their pharaohs. 古埃及人把他们的法老制成木乃伊。 |  embalm, preserve | exhume, disinter | verb
