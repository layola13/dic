devolutionaries:/ˌdiːvəˈluːʃənɛriz/
n.
权力下放主义者；地方分权主义者
People who support the transfer of power from a central government to regional or local governments.
The devolutionaries argued for greater local control over education. 权力下放主义者主张地方对教育有更大的控制权。
Devolutionaries believe that decisions should be made closer to the people they affect. 地方分权主义者认为，决策应该更贴近受影响的人民。
Synonyms: decentralists, regionalists, autonomists
Antonyms: centralists, unitarians
