co-inhabitant:/koʊɪnˈhæbɪtənt/| n.|同居者，共同居住者|A person or animal that lives in the same dwelling as another.|例句：My co-inhabitant is a very tidy person. 我的同居者是一个非常爱干净的人。

例句：The squirrels and birds are co-inhabitants of the forest. 松鼠和鸟类是森林的共同居住者。

|近义词：roommate, housemate, flatmate, cohabitant |反义词:stranger, outsider|词性:noun
