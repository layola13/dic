extirpatory:/ˈɛkstərpeɪtɔːri/
adjective
根除的，灭绝的
tending or serving to extirpate; destroying completely
The extirpatory surgery successfully removed the cancerous tumor.  根除性手术成功地切除了癌性肿瘤。
The government launched an extirpatory campaign against the invasive species. 政府发起了一场根除入侵物种的运动。
近义词:eradication, destructive, eliminative
反义词:preservative, constructive
词性:adjective

extirpate:/ˈɛkstərpeɪt/
verb
根除，灭绝
to destroy completely; wipe out
The team worked tirelessly to extirpate the disease from the region.  该团队不知疲倦地努力将该疾病从该地区根除。
They tried to extirpate the weeds from the garden. 他们试图清除花园里的杂草。
近义词:eradicate, eliminate, abolish
反义词:preserve, conserve, propagate
词性:verb
