dioristic:/ˌdaɪəˈrɪstɪk/
adjective
区分的，界限的
Relating to or marking a boundary or limit; distinctive.  Serving to distinguish or define.
This dioristic feature helps to separate this species from others.  这个区分性的特征有助于区分该物种与其他物种。
The river forms a dioristic boundary between the two countries. 这条河形成了两国之间的界限。
近义词: distinctive, characteristic, differentiating, delimiting, defining
反义词: unifying, blending, merging, combining
词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek *διορίζειν (di-orizein, “to define, delimit”), from *ὅρος (horos, “boundary, limit”).
