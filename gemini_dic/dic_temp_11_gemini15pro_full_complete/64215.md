equilibration:/ˌiːkwɪlɪˈbreɪʃən/ /ˌɛkwɪlɪˈbreɪʃən/
n.
平衡，均衡
The process of achieving equilibrium or balance.  The act of bringing something into a state of balance or equilibrium.
After some initial turbulence, the market reached a state of equilibration.  在最初的动荡之后，市场达到了平衡状态。
The equilibration of supply and demand is a fundamental concept in economics. 供求均衡是经济学中的一个基本概念。
balance, stabilization
imbalance, disequilibrium
noun
