online: /ˌɒnˈlaɪn/ /ˌɑːnˈlaɪn/ | adj. adv. | 在线，联网 | Connected to a computer network or accessible via a computer network. |  例句：The application form is available online.  申请表可以在线获取。| 近义词：connected, networked | 反义词：offline | 词性：形容词，副词

online: /ˌɒnˈlaɪn/ /ˌɑːnˈlaɪn/ | adv. | 在线地 |  In a way that involves or uses the internet. | 例句：They sell their products online. 他们在网上销售他们的产品。| 近义词：digitally, virtually | 反义词：offline | 词性：副词
