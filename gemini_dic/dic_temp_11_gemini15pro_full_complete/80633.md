goosewing:/ˈɡuːswɪŋ/ | n. |鹅翼；斜桁；三角帆；(复数)鹅毛笔|A sail set on a boom extending forward from the mast at an angle of about 45 degrees; also, the boom itself. Also, a quill pen made from a goose feather.|The small boat hoisted its goosewing and sailed out of the harbor. 小船升起三角帆，驶出了港口。|Synonyms: spinnaker, jib, lateen|Antonyms: (For the sail) mainsail |noun

goosewing:/ˈɡuːswɪŋ/ | adj. |鹅翼形的|Shaped like a goose's wing; angled or sloping.|The goosewing roofline gave the house a distinctive appearance. 鹅翼形的屋顶使这栋房子外观独特。|Synonyms: angled, sloping, oblique|Antonyms: straight, horizontal |adjective
