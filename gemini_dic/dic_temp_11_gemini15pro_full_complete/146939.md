overinterested:/ˌoʊvərˈɪntrəstɪd/ | adj. | 过分感兴趣的，过分热心的 | Excessively or unduly interested; showing too much interest, often in a way that is intrusive or inappropriate. |  He was a little overinterested in her personal life. 他对她私生活有点过分感兴趣了。| nosy, intrusive, meddlesome | indifferent, apathetic | adjective

Etymology->

From over- + interested.  "Over-" acts as a prefix indicating "excessively" or "too much". "Interested" derives from the past participle of the verb "interest," which ultimately comes from the Latin "interesse" meaning "to be of importance, to concern."
