muscales:/mʌsˈkeɪliːz/
adjective
肌肉的；强健的
Relating to or involving the muscles; having well-developed muscles.
Muscular strength is important for athletes.  运动员的肌肉力量很重要。
The boxer had a muscular physique.  这位拳击手身材强健。
Synonym: strong, brawny, sinewy, robust
Antonym: weak, feeble, frail
词性: adjective
