despicable:/dɪˈspɪkəbl/ /dɛˈspɪkəbl/| adj.|可鄙的，卑劣的|Deserving hatred and contempt.|His conduct was despicable. 他的行为卑劣。|近义词：contemptible, vile, base, mean, low|反义词：admirable, commendable, praiseworthy|词性:形容词
词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin despicabilis, from despicari ‘look down on’, from de- ‘down’ + specere ‘to look’.
