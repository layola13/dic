antiradiating:/ˌæntiˈreɪdieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
防辐射的
Designed to prevent the emission or propagation of radiation, especially electromagnetic radiation.
The scientist developed an antiradiating suit for workers at the nuclear power plant. 科学家为核电站的工人研制了一种防辐射服。
近义词:radioprotective, radiation-proof, radiation-shielding
反义词:radiating
词性:adjective
