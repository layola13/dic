backcourts:/ˈbækˌkɔːrts/| n.|后场|The area of a basketball, volleyball, or other court farthest from the net or basket.  It is also used metaphorically to refer to behind-the-scenes political maneuvering.|The team's backcourt was particularly strong this season. 这个赛季球队的后场尤其强大。|Synonyms: back court, rear court|Antonyms: frontcourt|Noun
backcourt:/ˈbækˌkɔːrt/| n.|后场球员（通常指篮球或排球）|Two players who make up the backcourt of a team in sports like basketball, typically the guards.|Their backcourt dominated the game. 他们的后场球员主导了比赛。|Synonyms: guards (in basketball)|Antonyms: frontcourt players|Noun
