globalize:/ˈɡloʊbəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 全球化；使全球化 | To make something worldwide in scope or application. | McDonald's has globalized its menu, offering some local dishes in different countries. 麦当劳已经将其菜单全球化，在不同国家提供一些当地菜肴。

| Example: The company decided to globalize its operations to reach new markets.  这家公司决定将其业务全球化，以开拓新市场。 | Synonym: internationalize, universalize, broaden, expand | Antonym: localize, regionalize | verb

globalization:/ˌɡloʊbələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 全球化 | The process by which businesses or other organizations develop international influence or start operating on an international scale. | Globalization has led to increased interconnectedness between countries. 全球化导致国家之间相互联系的加深。

| Example: The globalization of the fashion industry has made trends spread rapidly across the world.  时尚产业的全球化使得潮流在世界范围内迅速传播。 | Synonym: internationalization, universalization | Antonym: localization, regionalization | noun
