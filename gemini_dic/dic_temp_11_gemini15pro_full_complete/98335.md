insinuate: /ɪnˈsɪnjuːeɪt/ | v. | 暗示；含蓄地表达；巧妙地取得…的信任(或好感) | To suggest or hint something indirectly, often something unpleasant or disapproving.  To introduce (oneself or one's ideas) gradually or in a subtle, indirect, or artful way. | He insinuated that I was lying. 他暗示我在撒谎。

She insinuated herself into his confidence. 她巧妙地取得了他的信任。| 近义词: imply, suggest, intimate, hint | 反义词: declare, state, assert | verb
