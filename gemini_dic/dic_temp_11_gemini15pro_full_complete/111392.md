lividly:/ˈlɪvɪdli/| adv.|愤怒地，铁青地|In a furious, enraged manner; with a bluish or grayish discoloration of the skin (especially from rage).|Her face was lividly pale with shock. 她吓得脸色铁青。|Synonyms: furiously, angrily, wrathfully, irately|Antonyms: calmly, peacefully, serenely|adverb

livid:/ˈlɪvɪd/| adj.|铁青的，愤怒的|Furiously angry; discolored, as from a bruise; black and blue; grayish blue.|He was livid when he discovered the damage to his car.  他发现他的车被损坏了，勃然大怒。|Synonyms: enraged, furious, infuriated|Antonyms: calm, pleased, happy|adjective
