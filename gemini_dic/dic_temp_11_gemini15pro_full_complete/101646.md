irrefutable:/ˌɪrɪˈfjuːtəbl/ /ˌɪrɪˈfjuːdəbl/
adjective
无可辩驳的；不容置疑的
Impossible to deny or disprove.

The evidence against him was irrefutable.
针对他的证据是无可辩驳的。

Her logic was irrefutable, leaving no room for argument.
她的逻辑无懈可击，没有争论的余地。

Synonyms: indisputable, undeniable, incontrovertible, unassailable
Antonyms: disputable, questionable, refutable, debatable
词性: adjective
