hydromellonic:/ˌhaɪdrəməˈlɒnɪk/ | adj. |蜂蜜酒的；与蜂蜜酒有关的 | Relating to or characteristic of hydromels (honey wine). | This hydromellonic beverage is surprisingly refreshing.  这种蜂蜜酒令人惊讶地清爽。| 近义词: mead, honey-wine | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From hydro- (water) + melli- (honey) + -onic (adjectival suffix).  Ultimately from Greek *ὕδωρ (hýdōr, “water”) and μέλι (méli, “honey”).
