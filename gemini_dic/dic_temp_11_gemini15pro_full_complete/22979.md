botulinum:/ˌbɒtʃʊˈlɪnəm/ /ˌbɑːtʃʊˈlɪnəm/ | n. | 肉毒杆菌；肉毒毒素 | A bacterium producing botulin, a highly poisonous neurotoxin.  A neurotoxin produced by the bacterium Clostridium botulinum. | Example: Botulinum toxin is used in small doses to treat muscle spasms. 肉毒杆菌毒素少量用于治疗肌肉痉挛。 | Synonym: botulin, botulinus toxin | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific toxin) | noun

botulins: /ˈbɒtʃʊlɪnz/ /ˈbɑːtʃʊlɪnz/ | n. | 肉毒杆菌毒素（复数） |  Plural form of botulinum, referring to the toxins produced by the bacterium Clostridium botulinum.  These toxins are categorized into types A through G. | Example:  Different botulins have varying effects on the nervous system. 不同的肉毒杆菌毒素对神经系统有不同的影响。| Synonym: botulinum toxins | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific toxin) | noun
