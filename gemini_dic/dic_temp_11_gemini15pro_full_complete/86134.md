heartfulness:/ˈhɑːrtfəlnəs/| n.|真诚；善意；慈悲；全心全意|A quality of being full of heart; sincerity, kindness, compassion. It also refers to a system of Raja Yoga meditation.|She approached the situation with heartfulness and understanding. 她以真诚和理解的态度处理这种情况。|近义词：sincerity, kindness, compassion, benevolence, wholeheartedness|反义词：cruelty, insincerity, malice, apathy|词性:名词
