hearkeners:/ˈhɑːrkənərz/| n. |倾听者；听众|People who listen attentively.  Those who pay heed to what is said.|The hearkeners gathered around the storyteller, captivated by his tales. 倾听者聚集在讲故事的人周围，被他讲述的故事迷住了。|Synonyms: listeners, audience, followers|Antonyms: speakers, talkers|Noun
