improbable:/ɪmˈprɒbəbl/ (US) /ɪmˈprɑːbəbl/| adj.|不可能的，未必发生的|Not likely to be true or to happen; unlikely.|It is improbable that she will win the lottery. 她不太可能中彩票。|近义词：unlikely, doubtful, dubious|反义词：probable, likely, possible|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin improbabilis, from in- ‘not’ + probabilis ‘provable, likely’.
