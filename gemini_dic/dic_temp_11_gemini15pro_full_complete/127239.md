nauseously: /ˈnɔːʃəsli/ /ˈnɔːziəsli/ | adv. | 恶心想吐地，令人作呕地 |  In a way that causes nausea; sickeningly. | The room smelled nauseously of stale beer. 房间里散发着令人作呕的啤酒馊味。| The fish tasted nauseously fishy. 这条鱼尝起来腥味十足，令人恶心。 | 近义词：sickeningly, disgustingly | 反义词：pleasantly, appealingly | 词性：副词

nauseous: /ˈnɔːʃəs/ /ˈnɔːziəs/ | adj. | 恶心想吐的，令人作呕的 | Affected with nausea; inclined to vomit.  |  I felt nauseous after the long car ride.  长途汽车旅行后，我感到恶心。 | The smell of the garbage made me nauseous. 垃圾的气味让我感到恶心。 | 近义词：sick, queasy | 反义词：well, healthy | 词性：形容词


词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin nauseosus, from Latin nausea (see nausea).
