intendiment:/ɪnˈtɛndɪmənt/| n. |意图，含义，理解|An intendiment is the intended meaning or purpose of something, especially in law.  It can also refer to the understanding or interpretation of something.|The judge considered the intendiment of the law before making a ruling. 法官在作出裁决前考虑了法律的含义。

The intendiment of the contract was clear to both parties. 合同的意图对双方都很明确。|近义词：intention, meaning, purpose, import, significance|反义词：misinterpretation, misunderstanding|词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *entendement* "understanding, meaning," from *entendre* "to understand," from Latin *intendere* "to stretch out, turn one's attention to," from *in-* "toward" + *tendere* "to stretch."
