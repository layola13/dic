half-sung:/ˌhɑːf ˈsʌŋ/ | adj. | 半唱的；未唱完的 | Sung only partially; not sung to completion. | The lullaby was half-sung, interrupted by the baby's cry. 摇篮曲只唱了一半，就被婴儿的哭声打断了。 |  The melody was half-sung, trailing off into silence.  旋律只唱了一半，逐渐消失在寂静中。| Near synonyms: partially sung, incompletely sung | Antonyms: fully sung, completely sung | adjective


half-sung:/ˌhɑːf ˈsʌŋ/ | n. | 未唱完的歌曲 | A song that is not sung to completion. | The half-sung melodies echoed in her mind. 未唱完的旋律在她脑海中回荡。|  The half-sungs of her childhood still bring comfort. 她童年时未唱完的歌曲仍然带来安慰。 | Near synonyms: unfinished song, incomplete song | Antonyms: finished song, complete song | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"half-" (Old English healf) + "sung" (past participle of "sing," Old English singan).  The combination means "sung only partially."
