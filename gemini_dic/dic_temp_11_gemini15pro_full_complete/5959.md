ambrosially:/æmˈbroʊʒəli/ /æmˈbroʊziəli/ | adv. |美味地；香甜地；芳香地 |  In a way that suggests the food of the Greek gods, incredibly delicious and fragrant. | The roast chicken was cooked ambrosially, filling the house with a heavenly aroma. 烤鸡做得香气扑鼻，满屋子都是诱人的香味。|  The nectarines tasted ambrosially sweet and juicy.  油桃尝起来甜美多汁，美味极了。 | 近义词: divinely, deliciously, heavenly, delectably | 反义词: unpleasantly, distastefully, horribly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *ambrosialiter, from ambrosia +‎ -aliter. Ultimately from Ancient Greek ἀμβροσία (ambrosía, “food of the gods, immortality”).
