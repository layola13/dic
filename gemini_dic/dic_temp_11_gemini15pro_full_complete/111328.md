live-it-up: /ˌlɪv ɪt ˈʌp/
verb phrase
尽情享受；狂欢；过奢华的生活
to enjoy oneself by doing exciting and enjoyable things, especially spending a lot of money
They decided to live it up and go to a fancy restaurant. 他们决定好好享受一番，去了一家高档餐厅。
He's been living it up in Las Vegas since he won the lottery. 他中了彩票后一直在拉斯维加斯过着奢华的生活。
近义词: celebrate, indulge, splurge, party
反义词: scrimp, save, economize
词性: verb phrase
