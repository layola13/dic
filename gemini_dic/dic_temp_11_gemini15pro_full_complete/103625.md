joisted:/ˈdʒɔɪstɪd/
adjective
装有托梁的；由托梁支撑的
supported by joists.
The ceiling is joisted with heavy timbers. 天花板由厚重的木梁支撑。
The floor is joisted with steel beams.  地板由钢梁支撑。
近义词: supported, braced
反义词: unsupported


词性: adjective

词源: From the noun "joist" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives from nouns).  "Joist" comes from Old French "giste" meaning a "beam, rafter" ultimately from Latin "iacēre" meaning "to lie".
