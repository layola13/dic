admirers:/ədˈmaɪrərz/ | n. | 钦佩者，仰慕者 | People who admire someone or something; fans. |  She has many admirers because of her talent. 她因才华横溢而拥有众多仰慕者。

The band has gained thousands of admirers since their first album. 自从发行第一张专辑以来，这支乐队已经赢得了成千上万的粉丝。 | 近义词：fans, devotees, followers | 反义词：critics, detractors | 词性: 名词

词源: From the verb "admire" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting persons performing an action).  "Admire" comes from Old French "admirer"  (to wonder at, marvel at), from Latin "admirari" (to wonder at), from "ad-" (to) + "mirari" (to wonder).
