glenlike:/ˈɡlɛnˌlaɪk/
adjective
像峡谷的；峡谷似的
Resembling a glen; having the characteristics of a glen.
The house was situated in a glenlike valley. 这座房子坐落在一个峡谷般的山谷里。
The narrow, glenlike passage led to a hidden cave.  狭窄的、像峡谷一样的通道通向一个隐藏的洞穴。
近义词:canyonlike, valleylike, ravinelike
反义词:mountainous, hilly, flat
词性:adjective
词源: glen (n.) + -like (suffix)
