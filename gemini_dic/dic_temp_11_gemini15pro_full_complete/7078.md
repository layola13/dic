anergy:/ˈænərdʒi/| n.| 无力，无反应；免疫耐受|Absence of energy; lack of reaction to a specific antigen or allergen to which the body would normally react.|The patient exhibited anergy to the skin test, suggesting a weakened immune system.  病人对皮肤测试表现出无反应，表明免疫系统 weakened.|近义词：inactivity, lethargy, lassitude, apathy, unresponsiveness|反义词：energy, vigor, reactivity, responsiveness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Greek *ἀν- (an-) "without" + *ἔργον (ergon) "work".
