interpetalary: /ˌɪntəˈpɛtəˌlɛri/
adjective
花瓣间的，位于花瓣之间的
Situated between petals.  Specifically, of stamens, inserted between the petals, not on the receptacle or hypanthium as usual.
例句：The interpetalary stamens are a distinguishing feature of this flower.  这种花瓣间的雄蕊是这朵花的显著特征。

例句：The botanist noted the interpetalary placement of the nectaries.  植物学家注意到蜜腺位于花瓣之间。

近义词: interpetalous
反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider) epipetalous (on the petals), receptacular (on the receptacle)
词性: adjective
