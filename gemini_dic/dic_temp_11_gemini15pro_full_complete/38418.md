concertante:/ˌkɒntʃəˈtɑːntɪ/ | adj. n. |协奏的，协奏曲|A musical composition for two or more solo instruments and orchestra, or a performance of such a work.  It emphasizes the solo instruments more than a concerto grosso, but less than a solo concerto. | Mozart wrote a Sinfonia Concertante for Violin and Viola. 莫扎特写了一首小提琴和中提琴的协奏交响曲。| Near synonyms: concerto, concertino | Near antonym: symphony | adjective, noun

concertante:/ˌkɒntʃərˈtænti/ | adj. n. |协奏的，协奏曲|In a concerto style, featuring prominent solo instruments.  Can also refer to the composition itself. | The concertante style highlights the virtuosity of the individual performers. 协奏风格突出了个别演奏者的技巧。|Near synonyms: concerto, concertino | Near antonym: symphony | adjective, noun
