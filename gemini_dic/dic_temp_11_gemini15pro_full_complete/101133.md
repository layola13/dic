investigational:/ɪnˌvɛstɪˈɡeɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 研究性的，调查性的 |  Relating to or involved in investigation, especially scientific or medical research. |  The drug is still in the investigational stage.  这种药物仍处于研究阶段。 | Synonyms: experimental, research, exploratory | Antonyms: proven, established, confirmed | adjective

investigational:/ɪnˌvɛstɪˈɡeɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 实验性的 | Being tested or studied, not yet proven or approved for general use. |  The hospital is conducting an investigational trial of a new cancer drug. 这家医院正在进行一种新的抗癌药物的试验。 | Synonyms: trial, pilot, test | Antonyms: standard, conventional, approved | adjective
