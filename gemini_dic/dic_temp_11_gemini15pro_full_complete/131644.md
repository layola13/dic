noncompulsive:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpʌlsɪv/ /ˌnɑːnkəmˈpʌlsɪv/| adj.|非强迫性的，非强制性的|Not caused by or relating to an uncontrollable urge to perform an act, especially one which is harmful to oneself.|She adopted a noncompulsive approach to eating. 她采取了非强迫性的饮食方式。|近义词：non-obsessive, voluntary, optional|反义词：compulsive, obsessive, mandatory|词性:形容词
