cartway:/ˈkɑːrtweɪ/ /ˈkɑːrtˌweɪ/
n.
车道；(尤指)私人道路
A private road or way, especially one maintained by its users.
The farmer granted his neighbor a cartway across his land. 农民允许他的邻居穿过他的土地的车道。

cartway:/ˈkɑːrtweɪ/ /ˈkɑːrtˌweɪ/
n.
(尤指)供车辆行驶的道路
A road or way, especially one for wheeled vehicles.
The old cartway was too narrow for modern traffic.  这条旧车道对于现代交通来说太窄了。

cartway:/ˈkɑːrtweɪ/ /ˈkɑːrtˌweɪ/
n.
(历史)(尤指)运货马车使用的道路
(historical) A way or road, especially one used by carts.
The cartway was deeply rutted from centuries of use. 这条车道由于几个世纪的使用而变得坑坑洼洼。
