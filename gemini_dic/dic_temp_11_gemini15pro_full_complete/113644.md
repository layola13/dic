machine-gunning:/məˈʃiːnˌɡʌnɪŋ/ | n. | 机枪扫射；猛烈抨击 | The act of firing a machine gun, or figuratively, a rapid and intense barrage of something, like criticism or questions. | The soldiers resorted to machine-gunning the enemy position. 士兵们开始用机枪扫射敌人的阵地。|  The witness was machine-gunned with questions by the reporters.  证人被记者连珠炮似地提问。 | synonyms: strafing, peppering, bombarding | antonyms: protecting, shielding | noun

machine-gunning:/məˈʃiːnˌɡʌnɪŋ/ | v. | 用机枪扫射；猛烈抨击 | To fire a machine gun at; to subject to a rapid and intense barrage of something, like criticism or questions. | They machine-gunned the car as it sped away.  他们用机枪扫射那辆疾驰而去的汽车。 | The speaker was machine-gunned with heckles from the audience. 演讲者遭到观众一连串的质问。| synonyms: strafe, pepper, bombard | antonyms: protect, shield | verb
