lanceolate:/ˈlænsɪəleɪt/| adj.|披针形的，矛尖形的|Shaped like a lance head; tapering to a point at each end, especially at the apex.|例句：The willow tree has lanceolate leaves. 柳树的叶子是披针形的。

例句：The petals of the flower were lanceolate in shape. 花的花瓣呈披针形。|近义词：lance-shaped, spear-shaped, tapering|反义词:ovate, cordate|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lanceolatus*, from lanceola ("small lance"), diminutive of lancea ("lance").
