nonreceptivities:/ˌnɒnrisɛpˈtɪvɪtiz/
n.
无接受性；不接受；不感受性
Lack of receptiveness or the inability to receive or be receptive to something, often referring to ideas, suggestions, or influences.
The government's nonreceptivities to new ideas hindered progress. 政府对新思想的不接受阻碍了进步。
Near Synonyms: unreceptiveness, resistance, closed-mindedness, unwillingness
Antonyms: receptiveness, openness, willingness, acceptance
noun


nonreceptive:/ˌnɒnrisˈɛptɪv/
adj.
不接受的；无感受性的；不敏感的
Not receptive; unwilling or unable to receive or be influenced by something.
He was nonreceptive to our suggestions. 他对我们的建议不接受。
Near Synonyms: unreceptive, resistant, closed-minded, unsympathetic
Antonyms: receptive, open, willing, sympathetic
adjective
