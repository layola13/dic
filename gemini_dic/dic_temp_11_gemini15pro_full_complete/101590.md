irradiating:/ɪˈreɪdieɪtɪŋ/
verb, adjective|照射，辐射，(使)容光焕发|emitting or reflecting light; exposing to radiation|The sun was irradiating the beach. 太阳照射着海滩。|近义词：illuminating, shining, radiating|反义词:darkening, obscuring|v.
irradiating:/ɪˈreɪdieɪtɪŋ/
verb, adjective|照射的，辐射的，容光焕发的|exposed to radiation|The irradiating light from the explosion blinded everyone. 爆炸发出的强光使每个人都失明了。|近义词：illuminated, radiant|反义词:dull, dim|adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin irradiatus, past participle of irradiare (“to shine upon”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + radiare (“to shine, gleam, beam”), from radius (“ray, spoke of a wheel”).
