array:/əˈreɪ/| n.|数组，阵列，排列，一系列|An ordered series or arrangement of things, often in rows or columns.  A large number or wide range of things.|例句：The telescope picked up a vast array of signals from space.  望远镜接收到了来自太空的大量信号。

例句：There was a dazzling array of fabrics to choose from. 有各种各样令人眼花缭乱的布料可供选择。|近义词：collection, series, range, assemblage, group, set|反义词:disorder, chaos|词性:noun
