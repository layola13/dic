forfend:/fɔːrˈfɛnd/
verb
阻止，防止，避免
to prevent something bad from happening
例句：God forfend that such a thing should happen.  上帝保佑这种事不要发生。

例句：May fate forfend!  但愿命运阻止！

近义词：prevent, avert, preclude, obviate
反义词：cause, facilitate, encourage
词性：v.
