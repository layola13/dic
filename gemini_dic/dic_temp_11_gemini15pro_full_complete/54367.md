dishpan:/ˈdɪʃˌpæn/| n.|洗碗盆；洗涤槽|A pan, usually plastic, used for washing dishes.|She filled the dishpan with hot, soapy water. 她把洗碗盆里装满了热的肥皂水。|Example:  I soaked the dirty dishes in the dishpan. 我把脏盘子泡在洗碗盆里。|Synonyms: washing-up bowl, sink, basin|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "draining board" as the opposite function)|Noun
