half-flatteringly:/ˌhæfˈflætərɪŋli/
| adv. |半奉承地，略带奉承地|Partly or slightly flattering, suggesting a compliment but not entirely sincere. |She smiled half-flatteringly at him, unsure if she genuinely admired his work or just wanted something from him. 她半奉承地对他笑了笑，不确定她是真的欣赏他的作品还是只是想从他那里得到些什么。|例句2：He half-flatteringly referred to her as the "office genius," a title she knew he didn't entirely mean. 他半奉承地称她为“办公室天才”，她知道他并非完全是这个意思。|近义词：fawningly, insincerely, subtly complimentary|反义词：critically, frankly, honestly|词性:副词

Etymology:
half- (Old English healf) + flatteringly (from flatter + -ing + -ly)
