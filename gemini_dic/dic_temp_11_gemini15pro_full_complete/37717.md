comparativeness:/kəmˈpærətɪvnəs/| n. |比较性，可比性|The comparativeness of the two products is striking. 这两种产品的可比性很强。|例句：The study explored the comparativeness of different learning methods.  这项研究探讨了不同学习方法的比较性。

例句：The comparativeness of their situations made it easier to understand their reactions.  他们处境的相似性使人们更容易理解他们的反应。|近义词：similarity, comparability, resemblance|反义词：dissimilarity, difference, incomparability|词性:noun
Etymology: From comparative + -ness.  "Comparative" comes from Late Latin *comparativus, from Latin comparare "to compare," from com- "together" + parare "to make ready, prepare."  "-ness" is an Old English suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state, quality, or condition.
