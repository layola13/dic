jingoes:/ˈdʒɪŋɡoʊz/
|n.|侵略主义者；沙文主义者；极端爱国者|A person who professes patriotism loudly and aggressively, especially one who favors a belligerent foreign policy.|The jingoes were calling for war.|Synonyms: chauvinist, nationalist, warmonger, hawk|Antonyms: pacifist, dove|noun


jingoism:/ˈdʒɪŋɡoʊɪzəm/
|n.|侵略主义；沙文主义；极端爱国主义|Extreme patriotism, especially in the form of aggressive or warlike foreign policy.|Jingoism was rampant in the years leading up to the war.|Synonyms: chauvinism, nationalism, warmongering, hawkishness|Antonyms: pacifism, dovishness|noun
