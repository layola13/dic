fractured:/ˈfræktʃərd/| adj.|破碎的，断裂的；不和的，分裂的|Broken or cracked. Also, describes a situation or group that is divided and not working together.|The earthquake left the buildings fractured and unstable. 地震使建筑物断裂且不稳定。|synonyms:broken, cracked, split, splintered, fragmented, ruptured, disunited, divided, factious|antonyms:whole, intact, united, solid|adjective

fractured:/ˈfræktʃərd/| v.|（使）断裂，（使）折断|(cause something to) break or crack.|The bone fractured in the fall.  这块骨头在摔倒时断了。|synonyms:break, crack, split, splinter, shatter, rupture|antonyms:mend, repair, heal|verb
