cloth-eared:/ˈklɔːθˌɪərd/ /ˈklɑːθˌɪrd/| adj.|充耳不闻的，对音乐无欣赏力的|Unable to appreciate or distinguish musical sounds; tone-deaf.  Having no ear for music; insensitive to music or its nuances.|He's so cloth-eared, he can't tell the difference between a symphony and a pop song. 他对音乐如此充耳不闻，以至于无法区分交响乐和流行歌曲。|Synonyms: tone-deaf, unmusical, insensitive|Antonyms: musical, discerning, sensitive|adjective
