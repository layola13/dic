aspergillosis:/æsˌpɜːrˌdʒɪˈloʊsɪs/
|n.|曲霉病|A disease caused by infection with Aspergillus fungi, typically affecting the respiratory system.|Pulmonary aspergillosis is a serious condition that can lead to respiratory failure. 肺曲霉病是一种严重的疾病，可导致呼吸衰竭。|
|近义词:fungal infection|反义词:|名词


词源:  From Modern Latin *aspergillo- + -sis, from Aspergillus, the genus of fungi that causes the disease. Aspergillus is derived from Latin aspergillum "holy water sprinkler," because the shape of the fungus resembles the sprinkler.
