overluxuriant: /ˌoʊvərˌlʌɡˈʒʊəriənt/
adjective
过度繁茂的；过于奢华的
Characterized by excessive luxuriance or growth; excessively ornate or elaborate.
The overluxuriant vegetation in the rainforest made it difficult to navigate.  雨林里过度繁茂的植被使得通行变得困难。
The mansion's overluxuriant decor bordered on gaudy.  这座豪宅过于奢华的装饰近乎俗丽。
近义词: rampant, profuse, exuberant, lavish
反义词: sparse, meager, restrained, simple
词性: adjective

词源:
From over- + luxuriant.  Over- 表示“过度”，luxuriant 源自拉丁语 luxurians，意为“繁茂的”。
