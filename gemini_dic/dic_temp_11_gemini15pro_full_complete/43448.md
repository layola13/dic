crare: /kreɪr/
v.|用小驳船运送|To transport by crare. |They crared the timber down the river. 他们用平底船将木材沿着河向下游运送。|Synonym: transport, convey, ship|Antonym: receive, unload|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crar(e), from Old Norse *krar, *karr.  Cognate with Swedish *kårra “cart, barrow”. Ultimately related to Proto-Germanic *karraz “cart, carriage”.
