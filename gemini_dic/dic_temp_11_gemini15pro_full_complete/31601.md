chastenment:/ˈtʃeɪs(ə)nmənt/| n. |惩罚；磨炼；教训|The experience was a chastenment for him, making him more humble.  这段经历对他来说是一次磨炼，使他更加谦逊。|The team suffered a chastenment at the hands of their rivals. 这支队伍遭到了对手的痛击。|近义词：punishment, discipline, correction, castigation|反义词:praise, reward, commendation|词性:noun

词源:
From Middle English *chastisment, borrowed from Old French chastissement, from chastier (“to chastise”), from Latin castigō (“I correct, punish”).
