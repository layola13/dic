nondefinitive:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɪnətɪv/
adjective
非最终的，非决定性的，非限定的
Not providing a final or conclusive answer, result, or solution.
The test results were nondefinitive, so further investigation is needed.  测试结果并非最终结果，因此需要进一步调查。
The doctor gave a nondefinitive diagnosis, pending further tests. 医生给出了一个非最终诊断，等待进一步检查。
near synonyms: inconclusive, provisional, tentative, preliminary
antonyms: definitive, conclusive, final, decisive
词性: adjective
