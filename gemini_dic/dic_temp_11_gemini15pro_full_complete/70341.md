fieldwork: /ˈfiːldwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈfiːldwɜːk/ (UK)| n.| 实地考察；野外工作|Practical work conducted by a researcher in the natural environment, rather than in a laboratory or office.|例句：The anthropologist spent years doing fieldwork in the Amazon rainforest.  这位人类学家在亚马逊雨林进行了多年的实地考察。

例句：Students will be required to complete a fieldwork project as part of the course. 学生需要完成一个实地考察项目作为课程的一部分。|近义词：research, investigation, study|反义词: lab work, office work|词性:noun
