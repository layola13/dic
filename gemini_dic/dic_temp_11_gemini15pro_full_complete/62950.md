enneandrian:/ˌɛniˈændriən/
adjective
九雄蕊的；九数的
Having nine stamens; arranged in nines.
The enneandrian flower has nine stamens.  这朵九雄蕊的花有九个雄蕊。
The botanist identified the plant as enneandrian due to its nine stamens.  植物学家根据其九个雄蕊将该植物鉴定为九雄蕊植物。
近义词: nine-stamened
反义词: (No specific antonym, would depend on a specific number of stamens other than nine)
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek "ennea-" (nine) + "andros" (man, stamen)
