frankenfood:/ˈfræŋkənˌfuːd/
n.
基因改造食品,转基因食品
Genetically modified food, especially food perceived as unnatural or created through processes seen as potentially dangerous or ethically questionable.
Frankenfoods are a controversial topic, with some people concerned about their potential health and environmental impacts.  基因改造食品是一个备受争议的话题，一些人担心其对健康和环境的潜在影响。
Many consumers are wary of frankenfoods and prefer to buy organic produce. 许多消费者对转基因食品持谨慎态度，更喜欢购买有机产品。
synonyms: genetically modified food, GM food, genetically engineered food
antonyms: organic food, natural food
noun
