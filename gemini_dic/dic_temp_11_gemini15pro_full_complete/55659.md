ditolyl:/daɪˈtɒlaɪl/ /daɪˈtɑːlɪl/| n. |二甲苯基，甲苯基|A hydrocarbon radical, (CH3C6H4)2, formed by removing a hydrogen atom from a molecule of toluene; any of several isomeric crystalline hydrocarbons, C14H14, obtained by the action of sodium on bromotoluene.  Two tolyl groups joined together.|Ditolyl is used in the production of certain types of plastics. 二甲苯基用于生产某些类型的塑料。|Synonyms: bitolyl |Antonyms: None|Noun

ditolyl:/daɪˈtɒlaɪl/ /daɪˈtɑːlɪl/| adj. |二甲苯基的|Containing two tolyl groups.  |Ditolyl methane is a chemical compound. 二甲苯基甲烷是一种化合物。|Synonyms: bitolyl |Antonyms: None|Adjective
