dozers: /ˈdoʊzərz/ | n. | 推土机（复数）|  A large, powerful tractor with a broad metal blade at the front for moving earth, rocks, etc.| The dozers cleared the land for the new construction project. 推土机清理了新建筑项目的土地。|  bulldozers, earthmovers | excavators | noun


dozers: /ˈdoʊzərz/ | n. | 昏昏欲睡的人（复数，非正式）|  People who are sleepy or sluggish.|  After a long day of work, the commuters looked like a bunch of dozers on the train.  一天辛苦工作后，通勤者在火车上看起来像一群昏昏欲睡的人。| sleepyheads, sluggards | energetic people | noun

词源: dozer 是 doze (打瞌睡) 的派生词，加上 -er 后缀构成名词，表示“进行打瞌睡的人或物”。推土机被称为 dozer 是因为它缓慢而有力的动作类似于打瞌睡的人。最初 doze 来自古英语 *dūsian.
