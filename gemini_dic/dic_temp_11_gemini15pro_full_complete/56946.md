dotties:/ˈdɑːtiz/ /ˈdɒtiz/ | n. | 傻瓜，糊涂虫；（尤指老年人的）轻度痴呆 |A person, especially an elderly one, who is slightly senile or confused. | She called him an old dotty. 她称他为老糊涂。| 近义词:  foolish, silly, daft, senile, confused | 反义词:  wise, intelligent, astute | 词性: noun


dotties:/ˈdɑːtiz/ /ˈdɒtiz/ | n. |  (dotty的复数形式)  对某事物着迷的人；…迷 | People who are enthusiastically devoted to something. |  He's one of those computer dotties. 他是那些电脑迷之一。 | 近义词: fanatics, enthusiasts, devotees | 反义词:  apathetics, skeptics | 词性: noun
