non-committal:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɪt(ə)l/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kəˈmɪtəl/| adj.|不表态的，不承担义务的，含糊的|Not expressing or revealing commitment to a definite opinion or course of action.|例句：He gave a non-committal answer when asked about his future plans.  当被问到未来的计划时，他给出了一个不置可否的答案。|近义词：evasive, ambiguous, guarded, reserved|反义词：committed, definite, decisive|词性:形容词

non-committal:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɪt(ə)l/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kəˈmɪtəl/| n.|不表态，不承担义务|The state of being non-committal.|例句：Her non-committal made it difficult to know what she really thought. 她的不表态让人很难知道她真正的想法。|近义词：evasion, ambiguity|反义词：commitment, decisiveness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + committal.  "Committal" meaning the act of committing or pledging oneself.  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning of "committal".
