footpaths:/ˈfʊtpɑːθs/ /ˈfʊtpæθs/| n. |人行道；小路|A path for people to walk on, especially along the side of a road or field. |例句：We strolled along the footpaths. 我们沿着人行道漫步。

例句：The footpaths were muddy after the rain. 雨后人行道很泥泞。

近义词：sidewalks, pathways, trails, walks|反义词:roadway, carriageway|词性:名词
