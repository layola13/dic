adapts:/əˈdæpts/
verb
适应，改编
adjusts to new conditions or uses.
The chameleon adapts its skin color to its surroundings. 变色龙的肤色会随着周围环境而变化。
近义词:adjusts, modifies, conforms, accommodates
反义词:resists, remains, stagnates
词性:v.


Etymology:
From Middle English *adapten, from Late Latin adaptāre (“to fit, to adjust”), from Latin ad (“to”) + aptāre (“to fit”).
