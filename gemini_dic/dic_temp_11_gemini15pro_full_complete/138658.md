nudibrachiate:/ˌnjuːdɪˈbrækiət/ /ˌnuːdɪˈbreɪkiət/
adjective|裸鳃的，裸鳃亚目的|Having gills not enclosed in a cavity but arranged in tufts, plumes, or leaflike processes along the back or sides, as in certain gastropods.|Nudibrachiate sea slugs are known for their vibrant colors. 裸鳃海蛞蝓以其鲜艳的颜色而闻名。|Synonyms:  naked-gilled|Antonyms: tectibranch (covered-gilled)|词性: adjective

nudibrachiate:/ˌnjuːdɪˈbrækiət/ /ˌnuːdɪˈbreɪkiət/
noun|裸鳃亚目动物|A gastropod mollusk of the order Nudibranchia, having external gills.|The nudibrachiate's bright colors warned predators of its toxicity.  这只裸鳃亚目动物鲜艳的颜色警告捕食者它有毒。|Synonyms: sea slug, nudibranch|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly shelled gastropods)|词性: noun
