interjectionalize:/ˌɪntərdʒɛkʃənəlaɪz/| v. |感叹词化；使成为感叹词|To make or treat something as an interjection; to express something with the force of an interjection.|He interjectionalized the phrase "Good heavens!" with such emphasis that everyone jumped. 他用如此强调的语气感叹“我的天哪！”，以至于每个人都跳了起来。|Near synonyms: exclaim, ejaculate| Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "articulate calmly")|verb
