dunnesses: /ˈdʌnɪsɪz/
n.
愚蠢；迟钝
plural of dunness: the quality of being slow to understand; obtuseness.
The dunnesses of the students frustrated the teacher.  学生的迟钝让老师很沮丧。
Near Synonyms: stupidities, obtusenesses, doltishnesses, idiocies
Antonyms: intelligences, clevernesses, shrewdnesses
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
