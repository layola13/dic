dematerializing:/diːməˈtɪəriəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb, present participle
非物质化，消失
The process of ceasing to exist in physical form; converting assets into cash.
The magician made the rabbit dematerializing before our eyes.  魔术师让兔子在我们眼前消失了。
The documents are being dematerialized and stored electronically.  文件正在被非物质化并以电子方式存储。
近义词: disappearing, vanishing, fading, dissolving
反义词: materializing, appearing, forming
