digesters:/daɪˈdʒɛstərz/| n.|消化器，消化装置|A digester is a container or apparatus used for digestion, especially of organic matter, as by bacteria. |The anaerobic digester converts organic waste into biogas.  厌氧消化器将有机废物转化为沼气。

The farm uses digesters to break down manure.  农场使用消化器分解粪便。|近义词：processor, reactor, vessel|反义词:|noun
