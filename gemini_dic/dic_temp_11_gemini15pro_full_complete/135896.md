nonprudently:/nɑːnˈpruːdəntli/
adverb
不谨慎地，轻率地
Not in a prudent or careful manner; unwisely.
He invested his money nonprudently and lost a great deal. 他不谨慎地投资，损失惨重。
She nonprudently revealed confidential information. 她轻率地泄露了机密信息。
Synonym: unwisely, imprudently, rashly, carelessly, foolishly
Antonym: prudently, wisely, carefully, cautiously, judiciously
词性: 副词
