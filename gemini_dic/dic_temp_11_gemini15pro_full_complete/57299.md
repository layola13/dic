downslide:/ˈdaʊnslaɪd/| vi.|下滑，衰退|To decline or deteriorate gradually.|The company's profits have downslided in recent years.  这家公司的利润近年来一直在下滑。|近义词：decline, deteriorate, worsen|反义词:improve, recover, rise|verb
