highwaymen:/ˈhaɪweɪmən/| n. |拦路强盗，公路劫匪|A robber who attacks travellers on a highway.|例句：Highwaymen were a common threat in the 18th century. 18世纪，拦路强盗是常见的威胁。

例句：The highwaymen demanded their money and valuables. 拦路强盗索要他们的钱财和贵重物品。|近义词：bandit, robber, outlaw, brigand|反义词:lawman, police officer|词性:noun
