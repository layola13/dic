lemur:/ˈliːmə(r)/ | n. |狐猴 |A small, arboreal primate with a pointed snout, large eyes, and a long tail, native to Madagascar.  |  Example: The lemur leaped gracefully from branch to branch. 狐猴优雅地从一根树枝跳到另一根树枝。| Example:  We saw several different species of lemurs at the zoo. 我们在动物园里看到了几种不同的狐猴。 | Synonym: primate, monkey (loosely) | Antonym: N/A | noun
