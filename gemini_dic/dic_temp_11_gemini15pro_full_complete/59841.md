ectopic: /ɪkˈtɒpɪk/ (British) /ɛkˈtɑːpɪk/ (American)| adj.|异位的，异常部位的|Occurring in an abnormal position or place.  Relating to or denoting pregnancy in which the fetus develops outside the uterus, typically in a fallopian tube.|例句：The doctor diagnosed an ectopic pregnancy. 医生诊断出宫外孕。|例句：Ectopic heartbeats can be a sign of a serious heart condition.  异位心跳可能是严重心脏疾病的征兆。|近义词：aberrant, displaced, abnormal|反义词：normal, typical|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *ektopos* "out of place," from *ek* "out" + *topos* "place."
