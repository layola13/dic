outgalloping:/ˌaʊtˈɡæləpɪŋ/
verb
超越疾驰，跑得比...快
Running faster than someone or something else while galloping.
The young horse was outgalloping the older, more experienced one.  这匹年轻的马比那匹年长、更有经验的马跑得更快。
The champion outgalloped his rivals to the finish line.  冠军超越了他的对手，率先冲过了终点线。
近义词: outrunning, outpacing
反义词: lagging behind, trailing
词性: verb (present participle)

outgalloping:/ˌaʊtˈɡæləpɪŋ/
