motionlessly:/ˈmoʊʃənləsli/| adv.|一动不动地，静止地|Without any movement; in a still manner.  The statue stood motionlessly in the garden. 雕像一动不动地立在花园里。|近义词: still, static, immobile, stationary|反义词: moving, active, dynamically|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From motion + -less + -ly.  "Motion" comes from Old French "mocien" and ultimately from Latin "motio," meaning "a moving."  "-less" is a suffix indicating absence, and "-ly" forms adverbs.
