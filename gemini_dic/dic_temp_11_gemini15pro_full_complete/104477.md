kaizar:/ˈkaɪzər/| n.|皇帝，凯撒|A title of the emperors of the Holy Roman Empire and Austria, and of the German emperors.|例句：The kaizar ruled over a vast empire. 凯撒统治着一个庞大的帝国。

例句：He was referred to as the kaizar of the industry. 他被称为行业的凯撒。|近义词：emperor, ruler, monarch|反义词:subject, citizen|词性:noun
词源:From German *Kaiser*, from Latin *Caesar*. Ultimately from a cognates with the Roman family name *Caesar*.
