indecorous:/ɪnˈdɛkərəs/ | adj. | 不合礼仪的，不得体的 | Not proper or suitable for the circumstances; lacking good taste or manners. |  Her indecorous laughter during the funeral was shocking.  她在葬礼上不合时宜的笑声令人震惊。| Synonyms: improper, unseemly, inappropriate, unbecoming | Antonyms: decorous, proper, appropriate, seemly | adjective

indecorous:/ɪnˈdɛkərəs/ | adj. | 不雅的，失礼的 |  Behaving in a way that is not considered polite or socially acceptable. | His indecorous joke offended many of the guests. 他不得体的笑话冒犯了许多客人。 | Synonyms: impolite, rude, disrespectful, uncouth | Antonyms: polite, courteous, respectful, refined | adjective
