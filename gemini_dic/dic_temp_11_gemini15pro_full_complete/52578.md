diffusive:/dɪˈfjuːsɪv/| adj.|扩散的，弥漫的|spreading widely or thinly; tending to diffuse.|The room was filled with the diffusive scent of lilies. 房间里充满了百合花的弥漫香味。|Synonyms: spreading, pervasive, dispersed, widespread|Antonyms: concentrated, localized, focused|adjective

diffusive:/dɪˈfjuːsɪv/| adj.|冗长的，散漫的|(of speech or writing) lengthy and verbose; lacking conciseness.|His diffusive writing style made it difficult to follow his argument.  他冗长的写作风格让人很难理解他的论点。|Synonyms: rambling, verbose, prolix, long-winded|Antonyms: concise, succinct, brief, terse|adjective
