depredationist:/ˌdɛprɪˈdeɪʃənɪst/
n.
掠夺者；破坏者
Someone who causes widespread destruction or damage.
The depredationist raided the village, stealing livestock and burning homes.  掠夺者袭击了村庄，偷走了牲畜，烧毁了房屋。
Synonyms:  marauder, raider, plunderer, pillager, vandal, destroyer
Antonyms: protector, defender, guardian, conservator
noun


depredation:/ˌdɛprɪˈdeɪʃən/

Synonyms:  plundering, pillaging, looting, ravaging, destruction
