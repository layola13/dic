angelos:/ˈændʒəˌlɒs/ | n. | 天使; 使者 |A spiritual being believed to act as an attendant, agent, or messenger of God, conventionally represented in human form with wings and a long robe. | The angelos appeared to Mary and told her she would bear a son. 天使向玛丽显现并告诉她她将生一个儿子。| Synonyms: seraph, cherub, heavenly messenger | Antonyms: demon, devil | Noun
