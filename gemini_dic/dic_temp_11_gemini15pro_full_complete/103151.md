jerrymandering:/ˈdʒɛriˌmændərɪŋ/ | n. | 不公正的选区划分；杰利蝾螈 | The practice of manipulating the boundaries of an electoral constituency so as to favor one party or class. |  Republicans were accused of jerrymandering the district to ensure their candidate's victory. 共和党人被指控为了确保他们的候选人获胜而不公正地划分选区。|  gerrymandering, redistricting | fairness, impartiality | noun
