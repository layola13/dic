nigglings:/ˈnɪɡlɪŋz/
| n. |琐事；烦扰；(持续不断的)轻微的疼痛或不适|Small complaints or worries; persistent slight pains or discomforts.|The nigglings of an old injury kept him awake at night.  旧伤的隐隐作痛让他夜不能寐。|近义词：complaints, worries, aches, pains, irritations|反义词:comforts, relief|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the verb "niggle," which originated in the late 16th century.  Likely related to Low German "niggeln" (to fidget, trifle).
