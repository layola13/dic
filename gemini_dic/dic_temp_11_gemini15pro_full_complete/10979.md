arbitrations:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪʃənz/
n.
仲裁；调停；公断
The process by which a dispute is settled by a neutral third party or parties, rather than by a court of law.
The two companies agreed to settle their dispute through binding arbitration.  两家公司同意通过具有约束力的仲裁来解决他们的纠纷。

The union and management have agreed to submit the issue to arbitration. 工会和管理层同意将此问题提交仲裁。

近义词:mediations, adjudications, settlements
反义词:litigations, lawsuits, disputes
词性:noun (plural)

词源:
From Late Middle English: from Old French arbitracion, from Latin arbitratio(n-), from arbitrari ‘give judgment’ (see arbitrate).
