boatings:/ˈboʊtɪŋz/ | n. | 划船，泛舟 | The act of traveling in a boat, especially for pleasure. |  We enjoyed several pleasant boatings on the lake during our vacation. 我们在假期期间在湖上享受了几次愉快的划船。| 近义词: sailing, rowing, paddling | 反义词: hiking, driving | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From "boating," which is the present participle of the verb "boat" (meaning to travel by boat), plus the suffix "-s" to indicate plural instances or activities.  The verb "boat" ultimately comes from the Old English word "bāt" meaning "boat."
