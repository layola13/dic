chivy:/ˈtʃɪvi/| v. |催促；使...着急；责备；|To repeatedly urge or nag someone to do something.|例句：He's always chivying me to get things done faster. 他总是催促我快点把事情做完。

例句：She chivied the children into putting their toys away. 她催促孩子们把玩具收起来。|近义词：badger, harass, pester, nag, goad|反义词：encourage, support|词性：动词

chivy:/ˈtʃɪvi/| n. |轻微的责备；催促|A mild reprimand or urging.|例句：He gave me a chivy about being late. 他因为我迟到而责备了我几句。

例句：She got a chivy from her boss about the unfinished report. 她因为未完成的报告受到了老板的责备。|近义词：reprimand, rebuke, scolding|反义词：praise, commendation|词性：名词
