florilege:/ˈflɔːrɪˌlɛʒ/ /ˈflɔːrəˌlɛʒ/ | n. |选集，文选，摘录；花束，花环 | An anthology or collection of choice literary extracts; a bouquet or garland of flowers. | The professor compiled a florilege of medieval poetry. 教授编纂了一本中世纪诗歌选集。 |  The bride carried a beautiful florilege of white roses.  新娘拿着一个美丽的白色玫瑰花环。 | synonyms: anthology, compilation, collection, bouquet, garland, nosegay | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider "omission," "exclusion," or "dispersal" for contrasting concepts)| noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *florilège*, from Latin *florilegium*, from *flos* (flower) + *legere* (to gather).
