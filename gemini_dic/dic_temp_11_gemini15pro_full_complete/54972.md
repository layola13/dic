disproportional: /ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənəl/ | adj. | 不成比例的，不相称的 | Not having the correct or appropriate relationship between parts; too large or too small in relation to something else. |  The punishment was disproportional to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不成比例。| disproportionate, unequal, unbalanced | proportional, commensurate, balanced | adjective

disproportionally: /ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənəli/ | adv. | 不成比例地，不相称地 |  To a degree that is too large or too small in comparison with something else. |  Minority groups are disproportionally affected by unemployment. 少数群体受失业的影响尤为严重。| unevenly, unequally | proportionally, equally | adverb

词源:
From dis- + proportional, from Latin *proportionālis, from prōportiō "proportion, analogy, symmetry."
