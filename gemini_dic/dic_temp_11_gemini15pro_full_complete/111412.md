lixivial:/lɪkˈsɪvɪəl/| adj.| 淋溶的；淋滤的；可溶解的|Pertaining to, produced by, or containing lye; obtained by leaching; of or relating to the process of leaching.  Relating to or characterized by lixiviation (the process of extracting a soluble substance from a solid by washing or percolation).|例句：The lixivial process is used to extract gold from ore. 淋滤法用于从矿石中提取金。

例句：Lixivial soils are often deficient in nutrients. 淋溶土通常缺乏营养。|近义词：leachable, soluble|反义词：insoluble|词性:形容词
