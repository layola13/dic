aerothermodynamic:/ˌɛroʊˌθɜːrmoʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/
adjective
气动热力学的
Relating to the study of the effects of heat and motion on objects moving through the air.  It combines the principles of aerodynamics and thermodynamics.
The aerothermodynamic properties of the spacecraft during re-entry were carefully analyzed.  航天器重返大气层期间的气动热力学特性经过了仔细分析。
Near Antonyms: isothermal
adjective

aerothermodynamics:/ˌɛroʊˌθɜːrmoʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/
noun
气动热力学
The study of the effects of heat and motion on objects moving through the air.
Aerothermodynamics plays a crucial role in hypersonic flight. 气动热力学在高超音速飞行中起着至关重要的作用。
Near Synonyms: hypersonic aerodynamics


ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (relating to air) + thermo- (relating to heat) + dynamics (relating to forces and motion).
