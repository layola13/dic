ghostishly:/ˈɡoʊstɪʃli/ | adv. | 鬼魅般地，幽灵似地 | In a manner resembling a ghost; eerily or spookily. | The old house stood on the hill, ghostishly silhouetted against the moon.  那栋老房子矗立在山上，在月光下呈现出鬼魅般的轮廓。|  例句：A ghostishly pale figure emerged from the fog. 一个鬼魅般苍白的身影从雾中出现。 | 近义词：eerily, spookily, hauntingly | 反义词： cheerfully, brightly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:  From ghost + -ish + -ly.  "Ghost" comes from Old English "gast" meaning "spirit, soul."  "-ish" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "somewhat like" or "having the quality of." "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives. 词源：来自 ghost + -ish + -ly。“Ghost”来自古英语“gast”，意思是“精神、灵魂”。“-ish”是一个构成形容词的后缀，意思是“有点像”或“具有……的性质”。“-ly”是一个由形容词构成副词的后缀。
