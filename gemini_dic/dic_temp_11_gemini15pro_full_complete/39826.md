conspiration:/kənˌspɪˈreɪʃn/
n.
阴谋，共谋，密谋
a secret plan by a group to do something unlawful or harmful.
They were accused of conspiracy to murder. 他们被指控密谋谋杀。

The two men were arrested for conspiracy to rob a bank.  这两人因密谋抢劫银行而被捕。


conspiracy:/kənˈspɪrəsi/
n.
阴谋，共谋，密谋
a secret plan by a group to do something unlawful or harmful.
There is a conspiracy of silence surrounding the affair.  围绕这件事有一种保持沉默的阴谋。

The conspiracy to overthrow the government was foiled. 推翻政府的阴谋被挫败了。


近义词:plot, scheme, intrigue
反义词:fairness, honesty
