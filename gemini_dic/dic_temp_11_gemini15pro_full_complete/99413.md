interdivisionally:/ˌɪntərˌdɪˈvɪʒənəli/
|adv.|部门间地，跨部门地|Between or among divisions of a company or organization.|例句：The company encourages interdivisional communication to foster collaboration.  公司鼓励跨部门沟通以促进合作。|例句：Interdivisional rivalry can sometimes hinder overall productivity. 部门间的竞争有时会阻碍整体生产力。|近义词：across divisions, between departments|反义词：intradivisionally, within a department|词性:副词
词源: inter- (between) + division + -al (adjective suffix) + -ly (adverb suffix)
