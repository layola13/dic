eclegm: /ˈɛklɛm/ | n. | 咳出物，痰 |  Matter expectorated from the throat or lungs; sputum. | He coughed up some eclegm. 他咳出了一些痰。|  The doctor examined the eclegm under a microscope. 医生在显微镜下检查了痰。 | 近义词: sputum, phlegm | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *eclegma, from Greek ἐκλείχω (ekleikhō, “I lick up”).
