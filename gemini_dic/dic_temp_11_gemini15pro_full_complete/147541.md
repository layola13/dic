overpayments:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪmənts/| n.|多付，超额付款|The company discovered several overpayments and refunded the customers.  公司发现了几笔多付的款项，并把钱退给了顾客。| Overpayments can be caused by clerical errors. 多付可能是由于文书错误造成的。|synonyms: excess payments, surplus payments|antonyms: underpayments, short payments|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + payment. "Over-" is a prefix indicating "excessive" or "too much".  "Payment" comes from the Old French "paiement" meaning "the act of paying".
