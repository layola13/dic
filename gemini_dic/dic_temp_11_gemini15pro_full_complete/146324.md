overfeminize: /ˌoʊvərˈfɛmɪnaɪz/ | v. | 过分女性化，过度女性化 | To make something or someone excessively feminine, often to the point of stereotype or caricature. |  Some critics argue that the media overfeminizes young girls. 一些评论家认为媒体过度女性化了年轻女孩。

The portrayal of women in this movie is overfeminized and unrealistic.  这部电影中对女性的刻画过于女性化，不现实。 | 近义词: emasculate (in the sense of depriving of masculine qualities) | 反义词: masculinize | 词性: 动词
