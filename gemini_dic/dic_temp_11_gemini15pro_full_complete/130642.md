nonadmitteds:/ˌnɑːnəˈmɪtɪdz/ | n. | 未录取者; 未被接纳者 | People who have not been admitted (to a school, program, hospital, etc.).  This is a plural noun formed by adding "-s" to "nonadmitted." | The nonadmitteds were disappointed but determined to apply again next year. 未录取的学生们很失望，但决心明年再次申请。 |  The university sent letters to all the nonadmitteds explaining the decision.  大学向所有未被录取的学生发送了信件，解释了这一决定。| synonyms:  rejects, declined applicants | antonyms:  admitteds, accepted applicants | noun
