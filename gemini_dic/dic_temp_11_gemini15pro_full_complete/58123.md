dropsical:/ˈdrɒpsɪkəl/ | adj. | 水肿的，水肿性的 |  Affected with, or characteristic of, dropsy; edematous; hydropic.  Swollen with excessive fluid. |  His dropsical legs made it difficult for him to walk. 他的水肿的腿使他行走困难。| The patient's dropsical condition worsened overnight. 病人的水肿情况一夜之间恶化了。 | synonyms: edematous, hydropic, swollen | antonyms: slim, thin, slender | adjective
