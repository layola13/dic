don /dɒn/ (British), /dɑːn/ (American)| v. |穿上，戴上| To put on (an item of clothing). | He donned his coat and hat before going outside. 他出门前穿上了外套，戴上了帽子。| She donned her new dress for the party. 她穿上她的新裙子去参加派对。|近义词: put on, wear|反义词: doff, take off|词性:动词

don /dɒn/ (British), /dɑːn/ (American)| n. |大学导师，先生| A university tutor (especially at Oxford or Cambridge), or a Spanish nobleman or gentleman. | He was a respected don at Oxford. 他是牛津大学一位受人尊敬的导师。| The Don was known for his generosity. 这位先生以慷慨大方而闻名。|近义词: tutor, professor, sir|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
