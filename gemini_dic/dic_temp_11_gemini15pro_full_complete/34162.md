clamberer:/ˈklæmərər/| n. |攀登者；攀缘植物|A person or plant that clambers.|The clamberer reached the top of the cliff. 攀登者到达了悬崖顶端。|The ivy is a vigorous clamberer. 常春藤是一种生命力旺盛的攀缘植物。|近义词：climber, scaler, creeper (for plants)|反义词: N/A|名词

词源: From the verb "clamber" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Clamber" originates from the Middle English word "clambren," likely of imitative origin.
