chronometer:/krəˈnɒmɪtər/ | n. |精密计时器；航海天文钟 |An instrument for measuring time accurately, especially one used for determining longitude at sea. | The captain checked the chronometer to ensure they were on schedule. 船长查看了精密计时器，以确保他们按计划进行。| The scientist used a chronometer to time the experiment precisely.  科学家使用精密计时器精确地计时实验。| synonym: timekeeper, timer, stopwatch | antonym:  None (lack of a direct opposite for a precision instrument) | noun
