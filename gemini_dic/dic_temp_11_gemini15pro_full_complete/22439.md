bookful:/ˈbʊkfʊl/| adj.|满书的；饱学的；书呆子气的|Filled with books; well-read; studious.  Having read many books.|He has a bookful mind, always quoting authors and referencing literary works. 他满腹经纶，总是引用作家和提及文学作品。|Synonyms: well-read, erudite, learned, scholarly|Antonyms: ignorant, unlearned, illiterate|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "book" + "-ful" (suffix indicating "full of").
