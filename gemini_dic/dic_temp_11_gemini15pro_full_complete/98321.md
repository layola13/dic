insignificantly:/ˌɪnsɪɡˈnɪfɪkəntli/ | adv. |无关紧要地，微不足道地 |  In a way that is not important or noticeable; to a very small degree. | The price difference was insignificantly small. 价格差异微不足道。|  Synonym: negligibly, trivially, slightly | Antonym: significantly, substantially, importantly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + significant (important) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The word "significant" comes from the Latin "significans," present participle of "significare" (to signify), from "signum" (sign) + "-ficare" (to make).
