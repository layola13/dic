downpouring:/ˌdaʊnˈpɔːrɪŋ/| adj.|倾盆大雨的，瓢泼大雨的|A heavy fall of rain.|The rain was downpouring, making it difficult to see the road.  雨倾盆而下，难以看清道路。|近义词：torrential, pouring, bucketing (down)|反义词:drizzling, sprinkling|词性:形容词

downpour:/ˈdaʊnˌpɔːr/| n.|倾盆大雨|A sudden and heavy fall of rain.|The unexpected downpour caught us unprepared.  突如其来的倾盆大雨让我们措手不及。|近义词：deluge, torrent|反义词:drizzle, sprinkle|词性:名词

词源: "downpour" comes from the combination of "down" and "pour".  "Down" indicates the direction of the rain, and "pour" signifies a heavy flow. "Downpouring" is the present participle form of the verb "downpour," used as an adjective.  "downpour" 源于 "down" 和 "pour" 的组合。 "Down" 指雨的方向，"pour" 指大量的流动。"Downpouring" 是动词 "downpour" 的现在分词形式，用作形容词。
