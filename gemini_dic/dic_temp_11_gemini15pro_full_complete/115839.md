markets:/ˈmɑːrkɪts/ (US), /ˈmɑːkɪts/ (UK)| n.|市场;股市;行情;销路|A regular gathering of people for the purchase and sale of provisions, livestock, and other commodities. Also refers to the demand for a particular product or service.|The local farmers' markets are always bustling on Saturdays. 当地的农贸市场每逢星期六总是熙熙攘攘。|synonyms: marketplaces, bazaars, exchanges, outlets|antonyms: monopolies|noun
