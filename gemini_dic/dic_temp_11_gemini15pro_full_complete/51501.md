detracts:/dɪˈtrækts/ | v. | 减损，贬低 | third-person singular present tense form of "detract" |  His constant complaining detracts from the overall team morale. 他不断的抱怨降低了整个团队的士气。 | synonyms: diminishes, reduces, lessens, belittles, depreciates | antonyms: enhances, augments, increases, improves, boosts | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dētrahere*, from *dē* ("from, away") + *trahere* ("to draw, pull").
