irreverend:/ɪˈrɛvərənd/ | adj. |不尊敬的，不虔诚的|Showing a lack of respect for people or things that are generally taken seriously.|He made some irreverent remarks about the royal family. 他对皇室成员发表了一些不敬的言论。|近义词：disrespectful, impious, profane, blasphemous|反义词：reverent, respectful, pious|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin irreverendus, from in- "not" + reverendus "reverend."
