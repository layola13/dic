assize:/əˈsaɪz/| n.|巡回审判；(旧时)法令；(尤指面包、啤酒的)法定尺寸、重量|A session of a court held periodically in specified locations by judges travelling on a particular circuit.|The assizes were held in the town hall. 巡回审判在市政厅举行。|近义词：court, trial, hearing|反义词: N/A|名词

assize:/əˈsaɪz/| v.|规定(尺寸、重量等)|To fix the size, weight, or price of (something, especially bread or ale) by official regulation.|The government assized the price of bread. 政府规定了面包的价格。|近义词：regulate, fix, standardize|反义词: deregulate|动词
