lacing: /ˈleɪsɪŋ/ | n. |系带，鞋带；系带的动作；（食物或饮料中的）少量添加物 |The act of fastening or tightening something with laces, or a lace used for this purpose. Can also refer to a small amount of something added to food or drink. |She was adjusting the lacing of her boots. 她正在调整靴子的系带。 |近义词：fastening, binding, tie, cord, thread, dash, touch, hint |反义词：unlacing, loosening |noun

lacing: /ˈleɪsɪŋ/ | v. |用带子系紧；穿带子；给…添加（少量酒精或药物等）|To fasten or tighten something with laces; to thread a lace through something; to add a small amount of a substance to food or drink. |He laced his shoes tightly. 他把鞋带系得很紧。 |近义词：tie, fasten, bind, thread, add, spike |反义词：unlace, loosen, remove |verb
