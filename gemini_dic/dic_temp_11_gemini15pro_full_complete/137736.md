英文解析: In a way that is not valid; illegally or without justification.  It describes an action or process carried out in a manner that is not officially or legally acceptable, or that lacks logical or factual soundness.

由于缺少签名，合同无效执行。

He argued nonvalidly, basing his claims on misinformation.
他的论证无效，因为他的说法基于错误的信息。

近义词: invalidly, wrongly, incorrectly, illegitimately, unsoundly
