adumbrates:/ædˈʌmbreɪts/ | v. | 预示，暗示，略述 | to give a slight indication of something to come; to outline or sketch broadly | The clouds adumbrate a coming storm.  乌云预示着即将到来的暴风雨。|  foreshadows, intimates, suggests | reveals, clarifies | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adumbrare* "to sketch, represent faintly," from *ad-* "to" + *umbrare* "to cast a shadow," from *umbra* "shadow."
