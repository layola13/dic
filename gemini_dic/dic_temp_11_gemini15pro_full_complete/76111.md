furtherest:/ˈfɜːrðɪst/ | adj. | 最远的；进一步的 |  Superlative form of far: most distant in space or time; most advanced.  Considered nonstandard by some, with furthest preferred. |  He lives in the furtherest house down the lane. 他住在巷子尽头最远的那所房子里。|  He took the argument to its furtherest extreme. 他把这场争论推向了最极端的地步。 | 近义词: furthest, farthest | 反义词: nearest, closest | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *ferthest, from Old English fierrest, fyrrest (“furthest, most distant, last”), superlative form of feor (“far”). Cognate with West Frisian fierst, fierrest (“furthest”), Dutch verst (“furthest”), German fürst (“prince”). The meaning "most advanced" is recorded from the 14th century.
