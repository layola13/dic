overphilosophizing:/ˌoʊvərˌfɪlˈɒsəˌfaɪzɪŋ/| n.|过度哲学化；过度思辨|The act of thinking too deeply or too much about philosophical questions, often to the point of inaction or confusion.|He was prone to overphilosophizing, which often prevented him from making decisions. 他容易过度思辨，这常常让他无法做出决定。|overthinking, ruminating|underthinking, practicality|noun
overphilosophize:/ˌoʊvərˌfɪlˈɒsəˌfaɪz/| v.|过度哲学化；过度思辨|To think too deeply or too much about philosophical questions, often to the point of inaction or confusion.|She tends to overphilosophize simple problems. 她倾向于对简单的问题过度思辨。|overthink, ruminate|underthink, simplify|verb
