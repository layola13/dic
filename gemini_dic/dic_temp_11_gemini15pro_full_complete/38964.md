configure:/kənˈfɪɡər/ (US), /kənˈfɪɡə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|配置，设定|To arrange or organize something, especially computer hardware or software, so that it works in a particular way. |例句：The network is configured to accept incoming mail.  该网络被配置为接受传入邮件。

例句：You can configure the software to suit your needs. 你可以根据自己的需要配置软件。|近义词：set up, customize, adjust, adapt|反义词: disarrange, disorder|verb

configure:/kənˈfɪɡər/ (US), /kənˈfɪɡə(r)/ (UK)| n.|配置，布局|The arrangement of parts or elements in a particular form, especially of computer hardware or software.|例句：The system allows for a variety of configurations. 该系统允许多种配置。

例句：The software's configuration is user-friendly. 该软件的配置是用户友好的。|近义词：arrangement, layout, setup, design|反义词: N/A|noun
