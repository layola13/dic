nonchalky:/ˌnɑːnˈtʃɔːki/
adjective
非白垩的，不似粉笔的
Not chalky; not resembling chalk in texture or appearance.
The paint dried to a smooth, nonchalky finish.  这油漆干后表面光滑，没有粉状感。
The tablets were nonchalky and easy to swallow.  这些药片不似粉笔，很容易吞咽。
Near Antonyms: chalky
Antonyms: powdery, friable
