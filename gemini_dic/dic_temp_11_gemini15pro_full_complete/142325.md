orthodromics:/ˌɔːrθəˈdrɒmɪks/ | n. | 大圆航线；球面航行学 |  The study of orthodromes, which are great circle routes, the shortest distance between two points on the surface of a sphere. |  Orthodromics is essential for long-distance navigation, especially in aviation and shipping. 球面航行学对于长途航行至关重要，尤其是在航空和航运领域。 | 近义词: great-circle navigation | 反义词: rhumb-line navigation | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *orthos* (straight) + *dromos* (running, course).
