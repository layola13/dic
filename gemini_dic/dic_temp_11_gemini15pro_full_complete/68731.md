farmed:/fɑːrmd/ | v. |耕种的；养殖的 | Cultivated or used for agriculture; raised or grown for food.  | The fish are farmed in large ponds. 这些鱼是在大型池塘里养殖的。 |  The family farmed the land for generations.  这个家庭几代人都在这块土地上耕作。| 近义词: cultivated, grown, raised | 反义词: wild, uncultivated | 词性: 动词(过去式和过去分词)

词源: From the verb "farm," which comes from the Old French word "ferme" meaning "rent, lease," ultimately from the Latin "firma" meaning "fixed payment."  The "-ed" suffix indicates the past participle form, used to create passive voice constructions or as an adjective.
