overregiment:/ˌoʊvərˈrɛdʒɪmənt/| n.|过度管制，过度组织|Excessive and overly strict regimentation or organization, often stifling creativity and individuality.|The overregimentation of the school stifled the students' creativity.|近义词: overorganization, micromanagement, excessive control|反义词: deregulation, freedom, autonomy|词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "regiment" (from French "régiment," ultimately from Latin "regimen" meaning "rule," "government").  The word suggests an excess of regimentation.
