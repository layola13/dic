flawier:/ˈflɔː.i.ər/ | adj. | 更有瑕疵的，缺点更多的 | Having more flaws; more imperfect. | The second vase was flawier than the first, with several noticeable chips. 第二个花瓶比第一个更有瑕疵，有几个明显的缺口。|  The diamond, though larger, was considerably flawier.  这颗钻石虽然更大，但瑕疵也更多。 | 近义词: more imperfect, more defective, less perfect | 反义词: more perfect, flawless, impeccable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From flaw + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Old Norse *flaga (“flake, fragment”).
