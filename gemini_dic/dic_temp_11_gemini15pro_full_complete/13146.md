asymmetry:/eɪˈsɪmətri/ /əˈsɪmətri/| n. |不对称，不对称性|Lack of equality or equivalence between parts or aspects of something; lack of symmetry.|The artist deliberately used asymmetry to create a sense of unease. 艺术家故意使用不对称来营造一种不安的感觉。|The building's asymmetry makes it stand out. 这座建筑的不对称性使它格外引人注目。|近义词：imbalance, disproportion, unevenness|反义词：symmetry, balance, evenness|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin asymmetria, from Ancient Greek ἀσυμμετρία (asymmetría, “incommensurability, lack of proportion”), from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + συμμετρία (symmetría, “symmetry”).
