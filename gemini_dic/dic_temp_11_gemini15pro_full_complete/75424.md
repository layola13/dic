fruit:/fruːt/
n.|水果|The sweet and fleshy product of a tree or other plant that contains seed and can be eaten as food.|Apples, bananas, and oranges are all types of fruit. 苹果、香蕉和橙子都是水果种类。|近义词:produce, harvest|反义词:vegetable|词性:名词

fruit:/fruːt/
v.|结果实；产生成果|To bear or produce fruit.|The apple tree fruited heavily this year. 今年苹果树结果实累累。|近义词:yield, produce|反义词:barren, unproductive|词性:动词
