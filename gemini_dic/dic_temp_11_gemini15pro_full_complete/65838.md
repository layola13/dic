eviscerate: /ɪˈvɪsərˌeɪt/ /ɪˈvɪsəˌreɪt/
verb
取出内脏；除去主要部分
to remove the inner organs of a body; to deprive something of its essential part
The surgeon eviscerated the fish before grilling it.  外科医生在烤鱼之前先把鱼的内脏取出来了。
The budget cuts have eviscerated the city's social programs. 预算削减掏空了该市的社会项目。
近义词: disembowel, gut, remove the entrails of
反义词: fill, stuff, replenish
词性: verb

eviscerate: /ɪˈvɪsərˌeɪt/ /ɪˈvɪsəˌreɪt/
verb
严厉批评；痛斥
to criticize severely and devastatingly
The critic eviscerated the author's new novel.  评论家严厉批评了作者的新小说。
His argument was completely eviscerated by the opposing lawyer. 他的论点被对方律师彻底驳倒了。
近义词: excoriate, denounce, condemn
反义词: praise, commend, laud
词性: verb


词源: From Latin *evisceratus*, past participle of *eviscerare* "to disembowel," from *ex-* "out" + *viscera* "internal organs."
