disconsolatory:/dɪskənˈsɑːlətɔːri/ /dɪskənˈsɒlətri/
adjective
令人沮丧的；无法安慰的；郁闷的
Causing or expressing dejection; cheerless.  Offering no consolation; incapable of being consoled.
例句：His disconsolatory words only deepened her sadness. 他令人沮丧的话语只会加深她的悲伤。

例句：She gave him a disconsolatory look. 她给了他一个郁闷的眼神。

近义词：dejecting, disheartening, depressing, dispiriting
反义词：cheering, encouraging, comforting, heartening
词性: adjective
