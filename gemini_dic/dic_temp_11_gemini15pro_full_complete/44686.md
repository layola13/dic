crucigerous:/kruːˈsɪdʒərəs/| adj.|十字形的，带有十字架的|Bearing a cross, typically as a heraldic symbol.  Referring to plants of the Cruciferae family (now Brassicaceae), having flowers with four petals in the shape of a cross.|The shield was crucigerous, bearing a golden cross on a field of blue.|近义词：cruciform, cross-bearing, cross-shaped|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "plain," "unadorned," depending on context)|词性: adjective
