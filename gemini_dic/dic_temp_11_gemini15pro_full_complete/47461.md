dealbation:/diːælˈbeɪʃən/
noun
洗白;漂白;冲淡;淡化
The dealbation of the coffee made it taste weak. 咖啡被冲淡后味道变淡了。
The politician attempted a dealbation of his image after the scandal.  这位政治家在丑闻之后试图洗白自己的形象。
近义词:whitening, bleaching, dilution, weakening
反义词:darkening, strengthening, intensification
词性:n.

Etymology: From Latin "dealbare" (to whiten), from "de-" (from, down, away) + "albare" (to whiten).
