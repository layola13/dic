liliaceous:/ˌlɪliˈeɪʃəs/
adjective
百合科的；像百合的
of or relating to the lily family (Liliaceae)
Lilies, tulips, and daffodils are all liliaceous plants. 百合、郁金香和水仙都是百合科植物。
The liliaceous flowers filled the garden with fragrance.  百合科的花朵使花园充满芬芳。
synonyms: lily-like, lilied
antonyms: non-liliaceous
