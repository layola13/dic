disproportionate: /ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːʃənət/ | adj. | 不成比例的，不相称的 | Too large or too small in comparison with something else; not having the correct relationship in size, amount, importance etc. | The punishment was disproportionate to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不成比例。|  The country's great influence in the world is disproportionate to its relatively small size.  该国在世界上的巨大影响力与其相对较小的规模不成比例。 | 近义词: excessive, inordinate, unbalanced, unequal | 反义词: proportionate, balanced, commensurate | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From dis- + proportionate, from Late Latin *proportionatus, from Latin proportio "comparative relation, analogy," from pro "for" + portio "share, portion."
