flamed:/fleɪmd/ | v. adj.|燃烧的，发炎的，激怒的|Past tense and past participle of flame.  Set something on fire, become suddenly bright, or react angrily and passionately. |The logs flamed brightly in the fireplace.  原木在壁炉里熊熊燃烧。|近义词：blazed, burned, ignited, glowed, enraged, infuriated|反义词: extinguished, calmed, soothed|词性:动词(过去式和过去分词), 形容词

flamed:/fleɪmd/ | adj.|火焰状的，(网络用语)被攻击的|Covered in flames or flame-like markings; (internet slang) having been subjected to harsh criticism or personal attacks. |She wore a flamed dress to the party. 她穿着一件火焰状的连衣裙去参加聚会。  He got flamed on the forum for his controversial opinion. 他因为有争议的观点在论坛上被攻击了。|近义词：fiery, blazing, criticized, attacked|反义词: unadorned, praised|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *flaumen, *flamben (“to flame”), from Old French flamber (“to blaze”), from Late Latin flammare (“to set on fire”), from Latin flamma (“flame”).
