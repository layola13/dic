barney:/ˈbɑːrni/| n.|争吵，喧闹，哄骗|A loud, boisterous argument or fight; a hoax or deception.|例句：They got into a real barney over who would pay the bill. 他们为了谁付账吵了一架。

例句：The whole story turned out to be a barney.  整个故事原来是个骗局。|近义词：quarrel, row, brawl, hoax, scam|反义词：agreement, harmony, truth|noun
