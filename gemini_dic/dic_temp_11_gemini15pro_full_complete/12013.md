arthropodal:/ɑːrˈθrɒpədəl/
adjective
节肢动物的
relating to or characteristic of arthropods.
例句：Arthropodal characteristics include an exoskeleton and jointed appendages. 节肢动物的特征包括外骨骼和分节的附肢。

例句：Scientists discovered a new arthropodal species in the rainforest. 科学家在雨林中发现了一种新的节肢动物物种。
近义词：invertebrate, arthropod
反义词: vertebrate
词性: adjective


arthropod:/ˈɑːrθrəpɒd/
noun
节肢动物
an invertebrate animal of the large phylum Arthropoda, such as an insect, spider, or crustacean.
例句：Arthropods are the most numerous group of animals on Earth. 节肢动物是地球上数量最多的动物群体。

例句：The lobster is a marine arthropod. 龙虾是一种海洋节肢动物。
近义词：invertebrate
反义词: vertebrate
词性: noun

词源: From Greek *ἄρθρον (arthron, “joint”) + *πούς (pous, “foot”).
