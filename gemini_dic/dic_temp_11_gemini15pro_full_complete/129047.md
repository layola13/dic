nitride:/ˈnaɪtraɪd/| n. |氮化物|A compound of nitrogen with a more electropositive element.|Titanium nitride is often used as a coating for cutting tools. 氮化钛常被用作切削工具的涂层。|近义词: compound |反义词:  (No direct antonym exists for a chemical compound like nitride) |词性: noun

Etymology:
From nitr(o)-  + -ide.  "Nitro-" ultimately derives from Greek *nítron, "natron," referring to naturally occurring sodium carbonate. The "-ide" suffix denotes a binary compound.
