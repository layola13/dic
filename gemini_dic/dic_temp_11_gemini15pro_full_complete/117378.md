mediators:/ˈmiːdieɪtərz/| n.|调解员，调停人|A person who attempts to make people involved in a conflict come to an agreement; a go-between.|The mediators worked tirelessly to find a peaceful resolution to the dispute. 调解员不知疲倦地努力寻找和平解决争端的办法。|
mediators:/ˈmiːdieɪtərz/| n.|介质，媒介|A substance or object that allows something else to happen or exist.|The enzyme acts as a mediator in the chemical reaction. 这种酶在化学反应中充当介质。|
mediators:/ˈmiːdieɪtərz/| n.|中介物|Something that transmits or carries something else.|Cytokines are mediators of inflammation. 细胞因子是炎症的介质。|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *mediatour*, from Old French *mediateur*, from Latin *mediator*, from *mediare* ("to be in the middle"), from *medius* ("middle").

近义词: negotiator, arbitrator, conciliator, intermediary, go-between
反义词: antagonist, opponent, adversary
词性: noun (plural)
