editorialization: /ˌedɪˌtɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. |  评论化，社论化 | The act of injecting personal opinions or bias into a supposedly objective piece of writing or reporting. | The documentary's heavy editorialization undermined its credibility. 这部纪录片浓厚的评论色彩损害了它的可信度。|  Near synonyms: commentary, opinionating,  bias, slant, spin | Near antonyms: objectivity, neutrality, impartiality | noun
