dicalcic:/daɪˈkælsɪk/
adjective
双钙的；含二钙的
Containing two calcium atoms or ions per molecule.
Dicalcium phosphate is a common food additive.  磷酸二钙是一种常见的食品添加剂。
Synonyms:  calcium-containing, calcium-rich (more general)
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) calcium-deficient, calcium-poor
POS: adjective
