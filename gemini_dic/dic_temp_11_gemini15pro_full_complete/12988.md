astonied:/əˈstɒnɪd/ (UK) /əˈstɑːnɪd/ (US)| adj. | 震惊的，惊愕的 | Filled with sudden and overwhelming surprise or wonder; astounded. |例句：He was astonied by the news of his brother's death.  他被他哥哥的死讯惊呆了。

例句：She stood astonied at the sight of the burning building.  看到燃烧的建筑物，她惊呆了。

近义词：astounded, amazed, surprised, shocked, stunned, dumbfounded | 反义词：unfazed, unsurprised, calm, composed | 词性：形容词
