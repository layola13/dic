imagines:/ɪˈmædʒɪnz/
v. |想象，设想|
He imagines himself a great writer.  他把自己想象成一个伟大的作家。
She imagines that she can fly. 她想象自己能飞。
Etymology: From Middle English *imaginen, from Old French imaginer, from Latin imaginārī (“to imagine, conceive, picture to oneself, form an image of”), present active infinitive of imaginor (“I form a picture to myself”), from imāgō (“image, likeness, form, appearance”) + -or.
Synonym: envisions, conceives, visualizes, pictures, fantasizes
Antonym: disbelieves, doubts, ignores, forgets
词性:动词 (Verb)
