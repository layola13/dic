conventioneering:/kənˌvɛnʃəˈnɪərɪŋ/
n.
会议组织；参加会议；会议旅游
the business or activity of organizing conventions
例句：The city's conventioneering business is booming. 这座城市的会议组织业务正在蓬勃发展。

例句：Conventioneering can be a lucrative industry for cities. 会议旅游对城市来说可能是一个利润丰厚的产业。

近义词：conference organizing, meeting planning, MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions)
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) leisure, vacationing
