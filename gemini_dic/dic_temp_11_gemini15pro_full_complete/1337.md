accrue: /əˈkruː/ | v. | 积累，增加 | to increase over a period of time | Interest accrues on the account daily.  利息每天都在账户上累积。|Synonyms: accumulate, collect, gather | Antonyms: decrease, diminish, dwindle | verb

accrue: /əˈkruː/ | v. |  (权利、利益等)自然增长，产生 | to come as a natural increase or advantage |  Benefits accrue to society from free education. 免费教育使社会受益。 | Synonyms: arise, result, proceed | Antonyms: forfeit, lose | verb
