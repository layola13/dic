henpecked:/ˈhenpekt/| adj.|怕老婆的，妻管严的|Continually nagged and dominated by one's wife.  A man who is constantly criticized and controlled by his wife.|例句：He was a henpecked husband, always obeying his wife's every command. 他是个妻管严，总是听从妻子的每一个命令。

例句：John's friends joked that he was henpecked, as his wife made all the decisions.  约翰的朋友们开玩笑说他是妻管严，因为他妻子做所有的决定。|近义词：browbeaten, bullied, dominated|反义词:dominant, assertive|词性:形容词
词源:From hen + peck, referring to a hen pecking at a rooster.  由hen（母鸡）+ peck（啄）组成，指的是母鸡啄公鸡。
