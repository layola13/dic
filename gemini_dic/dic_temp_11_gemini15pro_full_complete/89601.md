hoodoo:/ˈhuːduː/| n.|厄运；不祥之人或物；骗局；巫毒教|A hoodoo is something that is believed to bring bad luck.  It can also refer to a person or thing that causes trouble or difficulty, a form of folk magic or conjuring, or a tall, thin spire of rock.|That broken mirror proved to be a real hoodoo — everything went wrong after I dropped it. 那面破镜子真是个厄运之兆——自从我把它摔碎后，一切都变得不顺利了。|近义词：jinx, curse, bad luck, misfortune, hex|反义词:good luck, charm, blessing|noun


hoodoo:/ˈhuːduː/| vt.|用巫毒魔法施咒于；给…带来厄运|To hoodoo someone or something is to cast a spell on them or bring them bad luck.|She believed her rival had hoodooed her business. 她认为她的对手用巫毒魔法诅咒了她的生意。|近义词：curse, hex, jinx|反义词:bless|verb
