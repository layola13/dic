embryon:/ˈɛmbriən/ | n. |胚胎；胚芽；萌芽|An organism in the early stages of development, especially before it has reached a distinctively recognizable form. |例句：The embryon is developing rapidly.  胚胎正在迅速发育。

例句：The scientist studied the chicken embryon. 科学家研究了鸡的胚胎。|近义词：fetus, germ, bud|反义词:adult|词性:noun
