nondeodorants:/ˌnɒndiˈəʊdərənts/ |n.| 非除臭剂；不含除臭剂的产品|Products that do not contain deodorants, often marketed as natural alternatives.| Many people are switching to nondeodorants due to concerns about chemicals in conventional antiperspirants. 许多人因为担心传统止汗剂中的化学物质而改用非除臭剂。 |Near synonyms: deodorant-free products, unscented products|Antonyms: deodorants, antiperspirants|Noun

nondeodorant:/ˌnɒndiˈəʊdərənt/ |adj.| 非除臭的；不含除臭剂的|Not containing deodorant.| She prefers nondeodorant soap. 她更喜欢不含除臭剂的肥皂。|Near synonyms: deodorant-free, unscented|Antonyms: deodorant, antiperspirant|Adjective
