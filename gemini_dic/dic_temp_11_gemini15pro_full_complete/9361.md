antipsoric:/ˌæntiˈsɔːrɪk/ | adj. n. |抗疥疮的；抗疥疮药|Relating to or used against the itch; a remedy for the itch, specifically for scabies.  Derived from the Greek words "anti" (against) and "psora" (itch).| Example: The doctor prescribed an antipsoric cream for the patient's scabies. 医生给病人开了抗疥疮的药膏治疗疥疮。|Synonym:  antiscabietic|Antonym:  -|adjective, noun
