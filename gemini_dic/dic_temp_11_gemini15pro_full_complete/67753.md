eyebrow:/ˈaɪˌbraʊ/ (US), /ˈaɪˌbraʊ/ (UK)| n.|眉毛|The strip of hair growing above each eye.|She raised an eyebrow in surprise. 她惊讶地挑起了眉毛。

|近义词:brow|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "eyelid" as a contrasting feature)|词性:noun
