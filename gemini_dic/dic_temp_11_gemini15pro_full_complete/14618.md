avocation:/ˌævəˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 业余爱好，副业 |  An activity pursued for pleasure; a hobby.  Something one does in addition to a principal occupation, especially for pleasure. |  Photography is his avocation. 摄影是他的业余爱好。|  He works as a lawyer, but his avocation is painting. 他是一位律师，但他的副业是绘画。 |Synonyms: hobby, pastime, leisure pursuit | Antonyms: vocation, profession, job | noun
