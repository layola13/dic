nonapplicableness:/ˌnɒnəˈplɪkəbəlnəs/ | n. | 不适用性；不相关性 | The nonapplicableness of the rule in this situation is clear.  该规则在此情况下的不适用性显而易见。 |  inapplicability, irrelevance | applicability, relevance | noun

Etymology:
From non- + applicable + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Applicable" comes from the Late Latin "applicabilis", meaning "that can be applied". "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives.
