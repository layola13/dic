longhouses:/ˈlɔːŋhaʊzɪz/
n.
长屋（复数）
A traditional dwelling of some Native American peoples, especially the Iroquois, typically a long, bark-covered wooden structure in which several families lived.
The Iroquois longhouses were often over a hundred feet long.  易洛魁人的长屋通常超过一百英尺长。
Longhouses provided shelter for many families. 长屋为许多家庭提供了住所。
近义词：dwellings, residences, communal houses
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用single-family homes 单户住宅)
词性: 名词
