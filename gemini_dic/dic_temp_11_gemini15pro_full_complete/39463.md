conniption:/kəˈnɪpʃən/| n. |情绪失控；大发脾气|A sudden fit of rage or hysterics. |She threw a conniption when she found out her flight was cancelled. 她得知航班取消后大发雷霆。|

Example:|He had a conniption when he realized he'd lost his wallet. 他意识到钱包丢了之后勃然大怒。|
|近义词：fit, tantrum, outburst, hysteria|
|反义词：calm, composure|
|词性：名词|

词源:
The origin of "conniption" is uncertain, but it is thought to be related to the word "corruption" or possibly a shortening of "corruption of the humors," referring to an imbalance in the body thought to cause illness or fits.  "Conniption"的起源不确定，但据认为与“corruption”一词有关，或者可能是“corruption of the humors”的缩写，指的是体内失衡，被认为会导致疾病或抽搐。
