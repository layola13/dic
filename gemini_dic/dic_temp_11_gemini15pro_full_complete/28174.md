capote:/kəˈpoʊt/ /kæˈpəʊt/| n.|斗篷，披风；卡波特（人名）|A long, loose cloak, reaching to the ankles or below.  A cloak reaching to the knees, worn especially by men in the 18th century.  Also the surname of the American author Truman Capote. |She wore a heavy capote to protect herself from the cold. 她穿着一件厚重的斗篷来御寒。

He looked quite dashing in his velvet capote. 他穿着天鹅绒披风，看起来十分潇洒。|近义词：cloak, cape, mantle|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly light clothing)|词性:noun
