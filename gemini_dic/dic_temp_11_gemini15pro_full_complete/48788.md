deflection:/dɪˈflɛkʃən/ | n. |偏移;偏离|The politician deflected the question about the scandal.  这位政治家避开了关于丑闻的问题。|例句：His deflection of criticism was masterful. 他对批评的转移非常高明。|近义词：evasion, avoidance|反义词:confrontation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dēflectiōn-, dēflectiō, from Latin dēflectere (to bend down, turn aside), from dē- (down, away) + flectere (to bend).
