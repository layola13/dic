confesses:/kənˈfɛsɪz/
| v. |承认，坦白|admits something, usually something wrong or embarrassing.|He confessed to stealing the money. 他承认偷了钱。|
|近义词：admits, acknowledges, owns up|
|反义词：denies, conceals|
|词性:动词(第三人称单数)|

confess:/kənˈfɛs/
| v. |承认，坦白|admit something, usually something wrong or embarrassing.|I confess I don't know the answer. 我承认我不知道答案。|
|近义词：admit, acknowledge, own up|
|反义词：deny, conceal|
|词性:动词|


词源: From Old French confesser, from Latin confiteri "to acknowledge, confess, admit," from com- "together" (see com-) + fateri "to acknowledge, confess."
