immechanical:/ɪmɪˈkænɪkəl/
adjective
非机械的; 不擅机械的; 没有机械技能的;
Not mechanically inclined; lacking mechanical aptitude or skill.
His approach to the problem was remarkably immechanical. 他解决这个问题的方法非常不机械化。
Near Antonyms: mechanical, skilled, adept, proficient
Antonyms: mechanical
