linoleate:/ˈlɪnəʊliɪt/ /ˈlɪnoʊliˌeɪt/ | n. |亚油酸盐；亚油酸酯| A salt or ester of linoleic acid. |  Linoleate is an essential fatty acid important for cell membrane health. 亚油酸盐是细胞膜健康的重要必需脂肪酸。|  Dietary sources of linoleate include vegetable oils like safflower and sunflower oil. 亚油酸盐的膳食来源包括红花油和葵花籽油等植物油。 |  Synonyms: linoleic acid salt, linoleic acid ester | Antonyms: None | noun
