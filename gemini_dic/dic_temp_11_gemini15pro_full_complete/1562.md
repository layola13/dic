achillean:/əkɪliːən/| adj.|阿基里斯的；像阿基里斯的；勇敢的，刀枪不入的（除脚踵外）|Relating to or resembling Achilles, especially in being invulnerable except in a particular spot.|His achillean strength was legendary. 他阿基里斯般的强大是传奇的。|Synonyms: heroic, valiant, invulnerable, godlike|Antonyms: weak, vulnerable, cowardly|adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *Achilleus*, from Ancient Greek Ἀχιλλεύς (Akhilleús).
