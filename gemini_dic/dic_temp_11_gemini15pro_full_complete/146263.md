overfacilely:/ˌəʊvərˈfeɪsɪlli/| adv.|过于轻易地，过于肤浅地|Done or produced too easily or readily; superficial.|He overfacilely dismissed the concerns of the protesters. 他过于轻易地驳回了抗议者的担忧。|The author overfacilely concluded that the war was inevitable. 作者过于草率地得出战争不可避免的结论。|近义词：superficially, glibly, readily, easily|反义词：thoroughly, painstakingly, carefully|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessive) + "facile" (easy) + "-ly" (adverb suffix). "Facile" comes from the French word "facile," which originates from the Latin "facilis," meaning "easy."
