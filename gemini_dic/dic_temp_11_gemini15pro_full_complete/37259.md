coming:/ˈkʌmɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkʌmɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |即将到来的，接下来的| arriving or approaching soon |The coming week will be busy. 接下来的这周将会很忙。|例句：The coming storm is expected to be severe. 即将到来的暴风雨预计会很严重。|近义词：approaching, forthcoming, imminent, impending|反义词：past, gone, bygone|词性：形容词
coming:/ˈkʌmɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkʌmɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |到来，来临| the act of arriving |The coming of spring brings new life. 春天的到来带来了新的生命。|例句：The coming of the internet changed the world. 互联网的到来改变了世界。|近义词：arrival, advent|反义词：departure, going|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cumende (present participle of cuman "to come"), from Proto-Germanic *kwemaną, from Proto-Indo-European *gwem- "to come, go."
