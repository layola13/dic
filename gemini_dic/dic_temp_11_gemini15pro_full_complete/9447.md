antireductionism:/ˌænti rɪˈdʌkʃənɪzəm/
|n.|反还原论|The philosophy that not all phenomena can be explained by reducing them to simpler or more fundamental components.  It asserts that some complex systems have emergent properties that cannot be predicted or understood solely by analyzing their constituent parts.|Biological systems are a common target of antireductionist arguments.  生物系统是反还原论论证的常见目标。|holism, organicism|reductionism|noun
