boustrophedonic:/ˌbuːstrəˈfɛdənɪk/
adjective
回旋形的，来回书写的
alternating in direction, as the writing or plowing of certain ancient cultures; written from right to left and from left to right in alternate lines.
The ancient Greeks sometimes used a boustrophedonic writing style. 古希腊人有时使用来回书写的书写方式。
boustrophedon, serpentine, meandering, sinuous
unidirectional, straightforward
adjective
