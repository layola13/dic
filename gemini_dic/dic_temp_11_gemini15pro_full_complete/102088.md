isoprene: /ˈaɪsəˌpriːn/ /ˈaɪsoʊˌpriːn/ | n. | 异戊二烯 | A branched-chain unsaturated hydrocarbon, C5H8, obtained from petroleum and used in the manufacture of synthetic rubber. | Isoprene is a key building block for many natural products, including rubber. 异戊二烯是许多天然产物（包括橡胶）的关键组成部分。| Isoprene units are linked together to form long chains in rubber molecules. 异戊二烯单元连接在一起形成橡胶分子中的长链。 | synonyms: 2-methyl-1,3-butadiene | antonyms: N/A | noun
