bypassed:/ˌbaɪˈpæst/ /ˌbaɪˈpæːst/| v. |绕过，避开，忽略|To go around or avoid something, often an obstacle or difficulty. |The surgeon bypassed the blocked artery.  外科医生绕过了阻塞的动脉。

The new highway bypassed the town, reducing traffic congestion. 新建的高速公路绕过了城镇，减少了交通拥堵。|近义词：circumvent, avoid, detour, sidestep|反义词：confront, face, meet|词性:动词

bypassed:/ˌbaɪˈpæst/ /ˌbaɪˈpæːst/| adj. |被绕过的，被忽略的|Something that has been bypassed or ignored. |The bypassed town suffered economically. 被绕过的城镇经济遭受了损失。

The bypassed security measures proved ineffective. 被忽略的安全措施被证明是无效的。|近义词：ignored, neglected, overlooked|反义词：included, considered|词性:形容词
