demi-bastion:/ˈdɛmiˌbæstʃən/| n.|半堡, 半棱堡|A projecting part of a fortification, similar to a bastion but smaller, usually covering only half the curtain wall.|The fort featured two demi-bastions at each corner, offering overlapping fields of fire.|Synonyms: half-bastion|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "full bastion")|Noun


demi-bastion:/ˈdɛmiˌbæstʃən/| n.|半堡, 半棱堡|A projection from a fortified wall, half the size of a full bastion.|The demi-bastion provided limited but crucial defense for the gatehouse.|Synonyms: half-bastion|Antonyms:  bastion|Noun
