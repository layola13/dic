nondistinguishable:/ˌnɒndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/ | adj. | 不可区分的，无法分辨的 | Not able to be distinguished or differentiated. | The twins are so alike they are virtually nondistinguishable. 这对双胞胎长得太像了，几乎无法区分。 |  The two shades of blue are almost nondistinguishable. 这两种蓝色几乎无法区分。 | 近义词: indistinguishable, identical, alike, similar | 反义词: distinguishable, different, distinct | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
distinguishable (adjective): From distinguish (verb), ultimately from Latin distinguere ("to separate by pricking," "to mark differently"), from dis- ("apart") + stinguere ("to prick," "to extinguish").
