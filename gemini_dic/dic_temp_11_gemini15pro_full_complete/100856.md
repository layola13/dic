introspectively:/ˌɪntrəˈspɛktɪvli/
ad.|内省地，反省地|in a way that involves looking inward at one's own thoughts and feelings|She examined the situation introspectively, trying to understand her role in the conflict. 她内省地审视了情况，试图理解自己在冲突中的作用。|例句：He sat quietly, staring introspectively at the fire. 他静静地坐着，内省地凝视着火。|近义词：reflectively, thoughtfully, pensively|反义词：externally, superficially|词性: 副词
