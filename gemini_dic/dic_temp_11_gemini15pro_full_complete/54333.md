disheartens:/dɪsˈhɑːrtn/
verb
使沮丧，使灰心
To cause (someone) to lose determination or confidence.
The team was disheartened by the loss. 球队因失利而感到沮丧。
discourage, demoralize, deject
encourage, hearten
词性: 动词
