lambasted:/læmˈbeɪstɪd/
verb
严厉批评，痛斥
criticize (someone or something) harshly.
The coach lambasted the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉批评了球队的糟糕表现。
The film was lambasted by critics. 这部电影受到了评论家的猛烈抨击。

近义词:criticize, censure, condemn, denounce, castigate, excoriate, berate
反义词:praise, commend, laud, extol
词性:verb
