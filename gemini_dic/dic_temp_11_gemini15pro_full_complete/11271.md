arcograph:/ˈɑːrkəɡræf/ | n. | 弧线规，弧线图绘制仪 | An instrument for drawing arcs of large radius, especially for drawing full-size arches in architectural work. |  This arcograph allows for precise drawing of large arches.  这个弧线规可以精确地绘制大拱门。

| Example: The architect used an arcograph to draft the plans for the cathedral's dome. 建筑师使用弧线规来绘制大教堂穹顶的平面图。 | Synonym:  curvograph, arc-drawing instrument | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps ruler for drawing straight lines) | Noun
