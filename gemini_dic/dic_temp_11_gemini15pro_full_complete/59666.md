echinodermata:/ɪˌkaɪnəˈdɜːrmətə/ | n. | 棘皮动物门 |A phylum of marine invertebrates characterized by a hard, spiny covering or skin and a radial symmetry.  They include starfish, sea urchins, and sea cucumbers. | Starfish are a well-known example of echinodermata. 海星是棘皮动物门的一个著名例子。|  Echinoderms exhibit radial symmetry. 棘皮动物呈现出辐射对称性。 | 近义词: invertebrates (in some contexts) | 反义词: vertebrate | 词性: Noun
