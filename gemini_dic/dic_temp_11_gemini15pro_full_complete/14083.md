austrine:/ˈɔːstraɪn/| adj.|南方的，南半球的|Relating to the south or the southern hemisphere.|例句：Austrine species are adapted to warmer climates. 南方物种适应温暖的气候。|例句：Many austrine birds migrate north for the winter. 许多南方鸟类冬季向北迁徙。|近义词：southern, meridional|反义词：northern, boreal|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *australis*, meaning "southern," ultimately from *auster*, meaning "south wind."
