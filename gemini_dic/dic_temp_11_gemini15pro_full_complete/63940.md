epipubis:/ˈɛpɪˌpjuːbɪs/
n.
耻骨前骨，前耻骨
A pair of bones projecting forward from the pelvic girdle in some mammals, such as marsupials.
Marsupials have epipubic bones that support their pouch.  有袋动物有支撑育儿袋的耻骨前骨。
Epipubic bones are a characteristic feature of marsupials. 耻骨前骨是有袋类动物的一个特征。
synonym:prepubic bone
antonym: (No direct antonym, as it's an anatomical feature)
noun
