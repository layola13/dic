ericaceous:/ˌɛrɪˈkeɪʃəs/
adj.
石南科的；属于杜鹃花科的
Relating to or belonging to the heath family (Ericaceae), which includes heathers, rhododendrons, and azaleas.
Ericaceous plants thrive in acidic soil.  石南科植物在酸性土壤中茁壮成长。
The garden features a stunning collection of ericaceous shrubs.  这个花园以令人惊叹的石南科灌木收藏为特色。
Synonyms: heathlike
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could use terms like non-ericaceous)
词性: adjective
