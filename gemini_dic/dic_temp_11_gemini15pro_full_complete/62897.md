enlightenment:/ɪnˈlaɪtnmənt/ /ɛnˈlaɪtnmənt/| n.|启蒙；启迪；教化；开明|the state of being enlightened or the act of enlightening someone|The Enlightenment was a period of great intellectual and cultural ferment. 启蒙运动是一个伟大的知识和文化动荡的时期。|synonyms: awakening, education, illumination, insight, understanding|antonyms: ignorance, darkness, confusion|noun

enlighten:/ɪnˈlaɪtn/ /ɛnˈlaɪtn/| vt.|启发；启蒙；教导|to give someone greater knowledge and understanding about a subject or situation|Could you enlighten me on this matter? 你能给我解释一下这件事吗？|synonyms: educate, inform, teach, instruct|antonyms: confuse, mislead, deceive|verb
