litigators:/ˈlɪtɪɡeɪtərz/
n.|诉讼当事人，诉讼律师|A person or party involved in a lawsuit.  Specifically, it often refers to the lawyers involved in a case.|The litigators spent hours preparing for the trial. 诉讼律师们花了几个小时准备庭审。|Synonyms: litigants, lawyers, attorneys, counsel,  parties|Antonyms: mediator, arbitrator|noun

litigator:/ˈlɪtɪɡeɪtər/
n.|诉讼当事人，诉讼律师|Someone who participates in a lawsuit.|Both litigators presented their arguments to the judge. 双方诉讼当事人都向法官提出了他们的论点。|Synonyms: plaintiff, defendant, lawyer, attorney|Antonyms: mediator, arbitrator|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *litigātor*, from *litigāre* "to go to law," from *lis* "lawsuit" + *agere* "to drive, lead, act, do."
