in full swing:/ɪn fʊl swɪŋ/
adjective
全面展开；正如火如荼进行中
At its height of activity; fully operational.
The party was in full swing by the time we arrived. 我们到达时，派对正进行得如火如荼。
The project is in full swing and we're making good progress.  这个项目正在全力进行中，我们进展顺利。
近义词: underway, in progress, at its peak, going strong
反义词: stalled, halted, at a standstill, inactive
词性: adjective

词源:  The phrase originates from the imagery of a swing moving back and forth, reaching its highest point and fullest motion.  "In full swing" therefore describes something operating at its maximum capacity or intensity.
