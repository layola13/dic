misfitting:/ˌmɪsˈfɪtɪŋ/| adj. n.|不合身的，不合适的；格格不入的人|Failing to fit or harmonize; not properly adjusted or adapted.  A person who is not suited to their social environment or circumstances.|例句：The misfitting shoes gave her blisters. 这双不合脚的鞋让她起了水泡。|近义词：ill-fitting, unsuitable, inappropriate, incongruous, maladjusted|反义词：fitting, suitable, appropriate, harmonious, well-adjusted|词性:形容词

misfitting:/ˌmɪsˈfɪtɪŋ/| n.|格格不入的人；不称职的人|A person who is poorly adapted to a situation or environment.|例句：He felt like a misfitting in the corporate world. 他觉得自己在企业界格格不入。|近义词：outcast, nonconformist, maverick|反义词：conformist|词性:名词
