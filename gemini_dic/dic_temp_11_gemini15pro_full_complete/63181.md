entame:/ɒnˈtɑːm/ /ɛnˈtɑːm/ | n. |开始，开场，序言，开局|An introductory action, remark, or passage, especially in a book, play, piece of music, or game. | The entame to the symphony was surprisingly upbeat. 交响乐的开场出人意料地欢快。|  The author's entame captivated the reader from the first page. 作者的开场白从第一页就吸引了读者。 | 近义词：beginning, start, opening, introduction, prelude, preface | 反义词：conclusion, end, finale | 词性: noun

词源:
From French _entame_, from _entamer_ "to begin," from _en-_ "in" + _tamer_ "to cut," from Latin _domare_ "to tame."
