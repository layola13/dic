anti-egoist:/ˌæntiˈiːɡəʊɪst/ | n. | 反利己主义者；非利己主义者 | A person who opposes egoism or acts in a way that is not self-centered. |  He is an anti-egoist, always putting others' needs before his own. 他是一个反利己主义者，总是把别人的需求放在自己的前面。|  She acted as an anti-egoist by donating her prize money to charity. 她把奖金捐给了慈善机构，表现得像个反利己主义者。 | 近义词: altruist, humanitarian | 反义词: egoist, narcissist | 词性: 名词
