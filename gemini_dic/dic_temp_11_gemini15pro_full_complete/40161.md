contender:/kənˈtendər/ | n. |竞争者，角逐者|A person or group competing with others to achieve something.|例句：He is a strong contender for the championship. 他是冠军的有力竞争者。

例句：The two companies are major contenders in the computer market.  这两家公司是计算机市场的主要竞争者。|近义词：rival, competitor, candidate, applicant|反义词：non-contender, also-ran|词性:名词
