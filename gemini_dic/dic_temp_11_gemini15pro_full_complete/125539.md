multispecies:/ˌmʌltiˈspiːʃiːz/
adjective
多物种的
Involving or relating to multiple species.
This multispecies probiotic is designed to support gut health in a variety of animals.  这种多物种益生菌旨在支持各种动物的肠道健康。
The researchers studied the multispecies interactions in the coral reef ecosystem. 研究人员研究了珊瑚礁生态系统中的多物种相互作用。
近义词:polyspecific
反义词:monospecific
词性:adjective

Etymology:
"multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much") + "species" (from Latin "species" meaning "kind, sort").
