antipharmic: /ˌæntiˈfɑːrmɪk/ | adj. | 抗药的，解毒的 |  Counteracting the effects of poison or drugs; antidotal.  Relating to an antipharm, a medicine that counteracts the effects of another medicine.  |  The doctor administered an antipharmic agent to counteract the poison. 医生使用了抗药剂来中和毒素。 | Synonyms: antidotal, counteracting,  neutralizing | Antonyms:  toxic, poisonous | adjective

antipharmic: /ˌæntiˈfɑːrmɪk/ | n. | 解毒剂 | A medicine that counteracts the effects of another medicine or poison. | The herbalist provided an antipharmic to ease the patient's suffering.  草药师提供了一种解毒剂来减轻病人的痛苦。 | Synonyms: antidote, remedy | Antonyms: poison, toxin | noun
