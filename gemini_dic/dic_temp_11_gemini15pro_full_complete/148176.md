overserving:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvɪŋ/ | v. | 过度服务；提供过多的服务 | Providing more service than is necessary or desired; often in the context of providing too much food or drink. |  The restaurant was known for overserving its customers, leading to a lot of wasted food. 这家餐馆以过度招待顾客而闻名，导致大量食物浪费。

The waiter kept overserving us wine, even though we said we'd had enough. 服务员不停地给我们倒酒，即使我们说已经够了。 | 近义词: overdoing, overindulging | 反义词: underserving, neglecting | 词性: 动词 (verb)
