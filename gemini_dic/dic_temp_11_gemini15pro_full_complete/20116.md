biotype:/ˈbaɪ.əʊ.taɪp/ | n. | 生物型，生物类型 | A group of organisms having the same genotype.  A group of organisms sharing a specific combination of characteristics. |  The researchers identified several distinct biotypes within the bacterial population. 研究人员在细菌种群中确定了几种不同的生物型。| synonyms: genotype, phenotype, strain, variety | antonyms: N/A | noun

biotypes:/ˈbaɪ.əʊ.taɪps/ | n. | 生物型，生物类型（复数）| Plural form of biotype, referring to multiple groups of organisms with the same or similar genotypes. | The different biotypes exhibited varying degrees of resistance to the antibiotic. 不同的生物型对这种抗生素表现出不同程度的耐药性。 | synonyms: genotypes, phenotypes, strains, varieties | antonyms: N/A | noun
