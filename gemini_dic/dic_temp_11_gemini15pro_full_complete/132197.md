nondeadly:/ˌnɑːnˈdedli/
adjective
非致命的
Not designed or intended to cause death.
The police used nondeadly force to subdue the suspect. 警察使用非致命武力制服了嫌疑人。
Near Synonyms: nonlethal, harmless, safe, innocuous
Antonyms: lethal, deadly, fatal
词性: 形容词
