noncommercial:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɜːʃəl/ (US) /ˌnɒn kəˈmɜːʃəl/ (UK)| adj. |非商业的，公益性的|Not intended to make a profit.|The noncommercial radio station plays a variety of music.|Synonyms: nonprofit, charitable|Antonyms: commercial, for-profit|adjective
