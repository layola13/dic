disclass:/dɪsˈklæs/| v.|取消…的资格，剥夺…的等级|To deprive of class or rank; to degrade.|He was disclassed for cheating in the exam. 他因考试作弊被取消了资格。|近义词：degrade, demote, relegate|反义词：promote, upgrade|词性:动词
Etymology: dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + class (noun meaning "a group sharing the same economic, social, or cultural status")
