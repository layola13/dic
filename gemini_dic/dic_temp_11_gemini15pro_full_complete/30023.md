cayman:/ˈkeɪmən/| n.|开曼群岛人，开曼群岛鳄鱼|A large aquatic reptile of the Americas similar to the alligator, with a more heavily armored belly and feet not fully webbed. Also refers to a person from the Cayman Islands.|例句：The cayman lurked in the murky water.  开曼鳄潜伏在浑浊的水中。

例句：He met a Caymanian on his trip to the Caribbean. 他在去加勒比海的旅途中遇到了一位开曼群岛人。|近义词：alligator, crocodile|反义词:|noun
