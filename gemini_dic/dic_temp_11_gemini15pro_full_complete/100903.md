intuitive:/ɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/ /ɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/| adj.|直觉的；凭直觉的|Understood without the need for conscious reasoning; based on instinctive feeling.|She had an intuitive understanding of the problem. 她对这个问题有一种直观的理解。|近义词：instinctive, innate, natural|反义词： reasoned, analytical, logical|词性:形容词

intuitive:/ɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/ /ɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/| n.|直觉者|A person who relies on intuition.|He's an intuitive and often makes decisions based on gut feelings. 他是一个凭直觉行事的人，经常根据直觉做决定。|近义词：instinctual person|反义词：logical person|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intuitivus, from intueri "to look at, contemplate," from in- "into" + tueri "to look at, watch, guard."
