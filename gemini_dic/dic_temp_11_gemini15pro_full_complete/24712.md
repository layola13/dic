brontosaurus:/ˌbrɒntəˈsɔːrəs/ (US) /ˌbrɒntəˈsɔːrəs/ (UK)| n. |雷龙|A huge herbivorous dinosaur of the late Jurassic period, now officially called Apatosaurus.
