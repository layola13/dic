creamiest:/ˈkriːmiɪst/| adj.|最乳脂状的，最柔滑的，最细腻的|Superlative form of creamy: having a smooth, rich texture like cream.|The creamiest ice cream I've ever tasted was in Italy. 我尝过的最柔滑的冰淇淋是在意大利。|近义词：smoothest, richest, silkiest|反义词:runny, watery, thin|词性:形容词
Etymology: From cream + -y + -est.  "Cream" comes from Old French "cresme," ultimately from Latin "chrisma" (ointment).
