instrumentally:/ˌɪnstrəˈmɛntəli/ | adv. | 工具地；起到作用地；乐器地 |  In a way that relates to or involves instruments.  Pertaining to, or performed on, a musical instrument; as, instrumental music. Serving as a means or agency; conducive to some end; helpful; as, instrumental in effecting a reconciliation. | He was instrumental in bringing about the peace talks. 他在促成和谈中起了重要作用。| Synonyms: helpfully, effectively, usefully, conducively,  | Antonyms: hinderingly, obstructively, detrimentally | adverb
