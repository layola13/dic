chia:/ˈtʃiːə/| n.|奇亚籽|A tiny, oval-shaped, black or white seed of the chia plant (Salvia hispanica), native to Mexico and Guatemala, valued for its nutritional content and ability to absorb liquid, forming a gel-like consistency.|I added chia seeds to my smoothie for extra fiber. 我在冰昔里加了奇亚籽以增加纤维。|Synonyms: salba, chia seed|Antonyms: None|Noun

chia:/ˈtʃiːə/| n.|奇亚（植物）|A plant of the mint family (Salvia hispanica), native to Mexico and Guatemala, cultivated for its edible seeds.|Chia plants are drought-tolerant. 奇亚植物耐旱。|Synonyms: Salvia hispanica|Antonyms: None|Noun
