iodal:/ˈaɪədəl/| adj.|碘醛的，含碘醛基的|Containing or relating to the iodoform group.  Pertaining to, derived from, or containing iodine.|例句：Iodal compounds are used in some disinfectants. 碘醛化合物用在一些消毒剂中。

例句：The iodal reaction produced a characteristic yellow precipitate. 碘醛反应产生了一种特有的黄色沉淀。|近义词：iodoform (in some contexts)|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From *iod(o)-* (combining form meaning "iodine") + *-al* (adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Greek *ioeidēs* (violet-colored) referring to the color of iodine vapor.
