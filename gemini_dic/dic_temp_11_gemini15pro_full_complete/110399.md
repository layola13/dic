lii:/liː/ |n.|里(长度单位，中国)|A traditional Chinese unit of length, approximately half a kilometer.|例句：The village is about two lii away.  这个村子大约两里路远。

例句：He walked ten lii that day. 他那天走了十里路。|近义词：Chinese mile|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) |词性:noun
