cutler:/ˈkʌtlər/| n.|刀匠，餐具商；刀剪制造者|A cutler makes and sells knives and other cutting tools.  刀匠制造和销售刀具和其他切割工具。|例句：The cutler sharpened the blade to a razor's edge.  刀匠把刀刃磨得像剃刀一样锋利。

例句：He apprenticed himself to a cutler to learn the trade. 他拜师学艺，想成为一名刀匠。

近义词：knife-maker, toolmaker|反义词:|词性:noun
