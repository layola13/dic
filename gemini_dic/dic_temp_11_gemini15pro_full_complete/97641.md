inheritances:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtənsɪz/
n.
遗产，继承物
Something that is received from someone who has died.
She received a substantial inheritance from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里继承了一大笔遗产。

近义词:legacy, bequest, heritage
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 debt 债务)
词性:名词(plural)

inheritance:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtəns/
n.
遗产，继承物
Something that is received from someone who has died.
He spent his inheritance on a new car. 他把遗产花在新车上买了一辆新车。
近义词:legacy, bequest, heritage
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 debt 债务)
词性:名词(singular)
