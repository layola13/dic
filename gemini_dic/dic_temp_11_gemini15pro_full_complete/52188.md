diarrhoea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (UK) /ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (US)| n. |腹泻|Frequent and watery bowel movements, a common symptom of gastrointestinal upset.|例句：He suffered from diarrhoea after eating contaminated food. 他吃了被污染的食物后腹泻。

diarrhea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (US) /ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (UK)| n. |腹泻|Frequent and watery bowel movements, a common symptom of gastrointestinal upset.|例句：She had diarrhea and couldn't leave the house. 她腹泻了，不能出门。

例句：The baby's diarrhea was a cause for concern. 婴儿的腹泻令人担忧。|近义词：the runs, loose bowels, the trots|反义词: constipation|词性: noun


Etymology: From Late Latin *diarrhoia, from Ancient Greek διάρροια (diárrhoia, “flowing through”), from διά (diá, “through”) + ῥέω (rhéō, “to flow”).
