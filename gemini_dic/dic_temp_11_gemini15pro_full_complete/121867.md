misreward:/ˌmɪsrɪˈwɔːrd/
verb
错误地奖励；给予不当的奖励
To reward inappropriately or give a reward that is not deserved.
The company misrewarded its employees by giving bonuses to those who hadn't met their targets.  这家公司错误地奖励了员工，给那些没有达到目标的人发了奖金。
The algorithm misrewarded clickbait articles, leading to a decline in quality content. 该算法错误地奖励了标题党文章，导致高质量内容的减少。

近义词: misprize, undervalue, underreward
反义词: reward, overreward

词性: verb
