diasporic:/ˌdaɪəˈspɒrɪk/ | adj. | 散居的；离散的 | Relating to or denoting a diaspora or the people in it. |  Example: The diasporic communities maintain strong ties to their homeland.  这些散居社群与他们的祖国保持着紧密的联系。

Example:  Diasporic literature often explores themes of displacement and belonging.  散居文学常常探讨流离失所和归属感的主题。 | 近义词:  expatriate, dispersed, scattered, exiled | 反义词:  native, indigenous | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *diaspora* "scattering," from *diaspeirein* "to scatter," from *dia-* "across, through" + *speirein* "to sow, scatter."
