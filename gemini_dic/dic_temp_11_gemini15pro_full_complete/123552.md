monseigneur:/mɔ̃sɛɲœʀ (Fr)/ | n. |大人，阁下，殿下|A French title of honor, equivalent to "My Lord," used to address or refer to a person of high rank, especially a prince, bishop, or cardinal.| Monseigneur, your carriage awaits. 阁下，您的马车已备好。|Near synonyms: Your Excellency, Your Lordship, Your Grace| Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a title of respect) | Noun
