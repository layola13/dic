assalamualaikum:/ˌæsəlæmuˈɑːleɪkʊm/| int.|愿平安与你同在|An Arabic greeting meaning "Peace be upon you".  It is a common greeting among Muslims.|Example: Assalamualaikum, brother. 愿平安与你同在，兄弟。|Example:  When entering a Muslim home, it's customary to say "Assalamualaikum".  当你进入穆斯林家庭时，习惯上要说“愿平安与你同在”。|近义词:Peace be upon you|反义词:None|词性:Interjection

PHRASE->
This word is generally not used as part of a phrase.  It stands alone as a greeting.  It's typically responded to with "Wa alaikum assalam" (and peace be upon you).  This is the most common "phrase" associated with Assalamualaikum.  It is more of a call and response.
