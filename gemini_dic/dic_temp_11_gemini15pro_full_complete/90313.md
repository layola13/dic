housewifed:/ˈhaʊswɪfaɪd/
verb|使成为家庭主妇；像家庭主妇一样管理|To make someone into a housewife or to manage something in a way similar to how a housewife would. |She felt housewifed after having children, her career taking a backseat. 生了孩子后，她感觉自己变成了家庭主妇，事业退居二线。| He housewifed the budget, carefully tracking every penny. 他像家庭主妇一样管理预算，仔细记录每一分钱。|domesticated, home-bound|independent, career-driven|v.
