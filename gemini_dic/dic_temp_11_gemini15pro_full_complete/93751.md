immaturity:/ˌɪməˈtʃʊrəti/ | n. | 不成熟，幼稚 | The state of not being fully developed or grown; lack of experience or wisdom. |  His immaturity was evident in his impulsive decisions. 他的不成熟体现在他冲动的决定上。| Synonyms: childishness, puerility, adolescence, greenness | Antonyms: maturity, adulthood, sophistication, ripeness | noun

immature:/ˌɪməˈtʃʊər/ | adj. | 不成熟的，幼稚的 | Not fully developed or grown; lacking experience or wisdom. |  He made some immature remarks that offended several people. 他说了一些不成熟的话，冒犯了几个人。| Synonyms: childish, puerile, adolescent, green | Antonyms: mature, adult, sophisticated, ripe | adjective
