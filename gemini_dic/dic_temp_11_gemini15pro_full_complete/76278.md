fyrdung:/ˈfɪrˌdʊŋ/| n.|军区，四分之一，(尤指中世纪挪威的) 国民军四分之一 |A fyrdung was a division of the medieval Norwegian leidang, the system of conscription and naval defense.  Fyrdungs were responsible for providing ships and men for the defense of the kingdom.| In medieval Norway, each fyrdung was responsible for supplying a quarter of the ships and men needed for the leidang.| 近义词:levy, contingent, quarter| 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "whole" in the sense of the entire army)|词性:noun
