orion:/ɔːˈraɪən/ | n. |猎户座|A constellation (a group of stars that form a recognizable pattern) located on the celestial equator and visible throughout the world. It is one of the most conspicuous and recognizable constellations in the night sky. |Orion is easily identifiable by its three bright stars that form Orion's Belt.| 近义词:Hunter (poetic)| 反义词: None| Noun
