nonrumination:/ˌnɒnˌruːmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 非反刍,  不反复思考| The practice of not dwelling on negative thoughts or feelings.  It involves intentionally shifting your attention away from distressing experiences and focusing on the present moment. |  Practicing nonrumination can improve mental well-being.  练习非反刍可以改善心理健康。|Synonyms: mindfulness, acceptance, letting go | Antonyms: rumination, brooding, dwelling | noun
