nongenetically:/ˌnɒn.dʒəˈnetɪ.kli/ | adv. | 非基因地，非遗传地 | Not related to or determined by genes; through environmental or other non-genetic factors.  |  Example: The behavior was nongenetically determined, influenced by upbringing and environment.  这种行为不是由基因决定的，而是受成长环境的影响。 | Example:  Nongenetically modified crops are becoming increasingly popular. 非转基因作物越来越受欢迎。 | Synonyms: environmentally, acquired, learned | Antonyms: genetically, hereditarily |  adverb
