elderish:/ˈɛldərɪʃ/| adj.|稍老的，上了年纪的|Somewhat old; elderly.|例句：He had an elderish appearance, with graying hair and wrinkles around his eyes. 他看起来有点老了，头发灰白，眼角布满皱纹。

例句：She has an elderish aunt who lives in the countryside. 她有一个上了年纪的姑姑住在乡下。|近义词：elderly, aging, older|反义词：young, youthful|词性:形容词

词源: elder (年长的) + -ish (表示有点…的)
