bonneted:/ˈbɒnɪtɪd/ /ˈbɑːnɪtɪd/
adjective
戴帽的；装有引擎罩的
wearing a bonnet; having a bonnet or hood
The bonneted woman walked briskly through the market. 戴着帽子的女人轻快地走过市场。
The car has a newly designed bonneted engine. 这辆车有一个新设计的引擎罩。
synonyms: hooded, covered
antonyms: bareheaded, uncovered
词性: adjective
