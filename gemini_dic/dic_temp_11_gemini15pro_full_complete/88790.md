hoe's:/hoʊz/| n.|锄头；妓女（俚语，冒犯性极强）|A garden tool with a flat blade fixed at an angle to a long handle, used for weeding and breaking up the surface of the ground; A derogatory slang term for a prostitute or a promiscuous woman. |例句：The farmer used his hoe's to till the soil. 农民用他的锄头耕地。|近义词：(for tool) spade, mattock; (for slang) slut, whore (both highly offensive)|反义词:(no direct antonym for the tool) |词性:名词

hoe's:/hoʊz/| n.|（某人）的锄头|The possessive form of "hoe," indicating ownership.  Primarily refers to the garden tool. Using this to refer to a person (e.g., "that hoe's car") is extremely offensive.|例句：This is my father's hoe's. 这是我父亲的锄头。|近义词: (no direct synonym, depends on context) |反义词: (no antonym) |词性:名词
