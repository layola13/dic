overparked:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːrkt/
adjective
过度停车的
Parked in excess of the available parking spaces.  Characterized by excessive parking, often leading to congestion and inconvenience.
The street was overparked, making it difficult to find a spot. 这条街停满了车，很难找到一个停车位。
The shopping center was overparked during the holiday season. 假日期间，购物中心停满了车。
近义词: congested, overcrowded
反义词: underutilized, empty
词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "parked" (past participle of "park")
