linoleumed:/lɪˈnoʊliəmd/
adjective
铺了油毡的
Covered with linoleum.
The linoleumed floor was easy to clean.  铺了油毡的地板很容易清洁。
近义词: linoleum-covered
反义词: (No direct opposite, consider "bare", "uncovered", "carpeted")
词性: adjective

词源:  From "linoleum" + "-ed".  "Linoleum" comes from Latin "linum" (flax) + "oleum" (oil).
