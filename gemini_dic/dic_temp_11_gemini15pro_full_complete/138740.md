numbly:/ˈnʌmli/| adv.|麻木地；呆滞地|In a numb way; without feeling or responsiveness.  Feeling physically numb, as if one's senses have been dulled or deadened, often due to shock, grief, or extreme cold.|She numbly accepted the news of his death. 她麻木地接受了他去世的消息。|She stared numbly at the wall, unable to process what she'd just heard. 她呆滞地盯着墙，无法理解她刚刚听到的内容。|近义词：insensibly, dully, blankly|反义词：keenly, sharply, acutely|词性:副词
