asexually:/ˌeɪˈsɛkʃʊəli/
| adv. |无性地|Without sexual reproduction; not involving the union of male and female gametes.|Some plants reproduce asexually. 一些植物进行无性繁殖。|
|近义词：vegetatively, nonsexually|
|反义词：sexually|
|词性：副词|

asexual:/ˌeɪˈsɛkʃʊəl/
| adj. |无性的|Relating to, characterized by, or reproducing without sexual reproduction.|Asexual reproduction is common in single-celled organisms. 无性繁殖在单细胞生物中很常见。|
|近义词：nonsexual, vegetative|
|反义词：sexual|
|词性：形容词|
