orthid:/ˈɔːrθɪd/
n.
正形贝类，正形贝，腕足动物的一种
An extinct articulate brachiopod of the order Orthida, common as fossils from the Ordovician to the Permian periods.
Orthids are a group of extinct brachiopods. 正形贝是一组已灭绝的腕足动物。
Many orthid fossils were found in the Ordovician rocks. 在奥陶纪岩石中发现了许多正形贝化石。
近义词:brachiopod
词性:n. (noun)
