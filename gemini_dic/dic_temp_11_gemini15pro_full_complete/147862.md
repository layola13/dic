overrationalization:/ˌoʊvərˌræʃənələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度理性化；过度合理化 | The act of applying rational thought to an excessive degree, often to justify actions or beliefs that are based on emotional or irrational motivations.  It involves creating elaborate and often spurious explanations to avoid confronting uncomfortable truths or feelings. |  He engaged in overrationalization to justify his impulsive purchase of a new car. 他过度理性化来为其冲动购买新车的行为辩护。

His explanation for his lateness sounded like overrationalization. 他对自己迟到的解释听起来像是过度合理化。 | 近义词: overthinking, intellectualization, rationalization | 反义词: intuition, emotional response | 词性: noun
