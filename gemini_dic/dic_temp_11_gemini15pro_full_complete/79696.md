glowstick:/ˈɡloʊstɪk/| n.|荧光棒|A sealed plastic tube containing chemicals that produce a glowing light when the tube is bent and shaken, often used at parties or concerts.|例句：The children waved their glowsticks enthusiastically during the concert. 孩子们在音乐会上热情地挥舞着荧光棒。

例句：Glowsticks provided a beautiful, ethereal light for the evening festivities. 荧光棒为晚上的庆祝活动提供了美丽而空灵的光线。

近义词：chemiluminescent stick, light stick, glow stick|反义词:|词性:noun
