kilters:/ˈkɪltərz/
noun
苏格兰短裙; (尤指)苏格兰男裙
A traditional Scottish garment resembling a knee-length skirt.
The men all wore kilts to the Highland games.  男人们都穿着苏格兰短裙参加高地运动会。
kilt, pleated skirt
trousers, pants
n.

out of kilter:/ˌaʊt əv ˈkɪltər/
adjective, adverb
失常的; 出故障的; 不协调的
Not in normal working order; malfunctioning; disorganized.
The clock is out of kilter and needs to be repaired. 时钟坏了，需要修理。
malfunctioning, broken, disordered, awry
working, functional, organized, in order
adj., adv.
