canister:/ˈkænɪstər/ (US), /ˈkænɪstə(r)/ (UK)| n.|小罐，筒，容器|A metal container, typically cylindrical and airtight, used for storing dry goods, chemicals, or film.|例句：The tea leaves are stored in a canister. 茶叶存放在一个小罐里。

例句：He kept his coffee beans in an airtight canister. 他把咖啡豆放在一个密封的罐子里。|近义词：container, receptacle, box, tin, jar|反义词: None|词性:noun
