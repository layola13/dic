grizzle:/ˈɡrɪzəl/| vi. vt. n.| (vi.)发牢骚，抱怨；(vt.)使烦恼，使苦恼；(n.)灰白色,细雨，毛毛雨 |(vi.) To complain in a whining or fretful way. (vt.) To cause to feel annoyed or discontented. (n.)  A grayish white color. A fine drizzle or mist.|(vi.) The child grizzled all the way home because he didn't get a toy.  孩子一路回家都在抱怨，因为他没有得到玩具。

(vt.) The constant noise grizzled her.  持续的噪音让她很烦躁。

(n.) The sky was a grizzle of clouds. 天空是一片灰蒙蒙的云。

(n.)  A cold grizzle fell from the sky.  天空下起了冰冷的毛毛雨。|近义词：(vi. & vt.) complain, grumble, whine, fret; (n.) gray, drizzle, mist|反义词: (vi. & vt.)  praise, compliment, rejoice; (n.)  black, bright, sunshine|词性: verb (vi. & vt.), noun
