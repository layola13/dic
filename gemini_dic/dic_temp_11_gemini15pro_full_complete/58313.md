dryadic:/draɪˈædɪk/ | adj. | 树仙的，与树仙有关的；树木的，与树木有关的 | Of or relating to dryads (tree nymphs in Greek mythology); of or relating to trees or woods. |  The dryadic figure seemed to melt into the bark of the ancient oak.  树仙的身影似乎融入了古老橡树的树皮中。 |  The poet's dryadic verses evoked the spirit of the forest.  诗人关于树仙的诗句唤起了森林的精神。 | 近义词: sylvan, arboreal, woody | 反义词: urban, treeless | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From Greek *druas* "tree" + -ic.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dóru* "tree, wood".
