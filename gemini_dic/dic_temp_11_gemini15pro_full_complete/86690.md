hello's:/həˈləʊz/ /həˈloʊz/ |n.| 你好（用于表示某人或某物属于某人）|It represents "hello" plus the possessive 's, and is a casual, often informal way of saying something belongs to someone greeted with "hello." This usage is not standard English and generally considered incorrect. | Hello's your name?
