intermutation:/ˌɪntərˌmjuːˈteɪʃən/
n.
相互转化，相互变化
The intermutation of elements is a fundamental concept in alchemy.  元素的相互转化是炼金术中的一个基本概念。
The poem explored the intermutation of love and loss. 这首诗探讨了爱与失去的相互转化。
Synonyms: transformation, transmutation, conversion, metamorphosis
Antonyms: stagnation, fixity, immutability
noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *intermutatio, from Latin inter- + mutare "to change".
