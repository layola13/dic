misspeaking:/mɪsˈspiːkɪŋ/
n.|失言，说错话|The act or an instance of speaking incorrectly or unintentionally conveying a wrong impression.|I apologize for my misspeaking; I didn't mean to offend anyone.|Synonyms: slip of the tongue, verbal blunder, gaffe|Antonyms: articulation, clarity, eloquence
词性:名词
