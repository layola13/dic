inter-pollinated:/ˌɪntərˈpɑːləneɪtɪd/ |adj.|异花授粉的|Pollinated by pollen from a different plant of the same species, or from a plant of a different but closely related species.|例句：The inter-pollinated flowers produced a new variety. 这些异花授粉的花朵培育出了一个新品种。|例句：The scientist inter-pollinated two species of orchids. 科学家对两种兰花进行了异花授粉。|近义词：cross-pollinated, hybridized|反义词：self-pollinated|词性：形容词

词源：
"inter-" (prefix): from Latin, meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally."
"pollinate" (verb): from Latin "pollen" (fine flour), referring to the powdery substance produced by flowers.
