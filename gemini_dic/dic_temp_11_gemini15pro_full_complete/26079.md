bushwhacker:/ˈbʊʃˌwækər/| n.| 游击队员；丛林强盗；伏击者 | A person who uses guerrilla warfare, especially from forested or rural areas, often engaging in ambushes and raids. |  Example 1: The bushwhackers ambushed the patrol from the dense undergrowth.  游击队员从茂密的灌木丛中伏击了巡逻队。

Example 2: During the Civil War, bushwhackers were a constant threat to both Union and Confederate forces. 在内战期间，游击队员对联邦和同盟军都是持续的威胁。 | 近义词: guerrilla, partisan, irregular | 反义词: regular soldier, conventional force | 词性: noun
