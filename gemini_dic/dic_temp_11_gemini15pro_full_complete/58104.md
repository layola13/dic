dropless:/ˈdrɒplɪs/| adj.|无滴的，不滴落的|Preventing dripping; designed not to drip.  This word is formed by combining "drop" with the suffix "-less," which signifies "without" or "lacking."  It describes something that doesn't drip or allows for dispensing without dripping.|The dropless bottle design is a real improvement.  这个无滴瓶设计是一个真正的进步。|The dropless faucet saved us from constantly wiping up puddles. 这个不滴水的水龙头使我们不必再不断地擦水坑了。|leakproof, non-drip, dripless|leaky, dripping|adjective
