gollum:/ˈɡɒləm/ (UK), /ˈɡoʊləm/ (US)| n.|咕噜|A fictional character from J.R.R. Tolkien's fantasy novels *The Hobbit* and *The Lord of the Rings*. He is a creature corrupted by the One Ring, obsessed with its recovery, and speaks in a distinctive, raspy voice.|Gollum hissed and whispered, "My precious." 咕噜嘶嘶低语道：“我的宝贝。”|Synonyms: wretch, creature (in a negative sense)|Antonyms: hero, savior|Noun
