nonmoderates:/nɑːnˈmɑːdərɪts/ | n. |非温和派；非中间派| People who hold extreme political views, either very conservative or very liberal, and who are unwilling to compromise. | The nonmoderates in both parties refused to support the bipartisan bill.  两党中的非温和派人士拒绝支持这项两党合作的法案。|Synonyms: extremists, radicals, hardliners | Antonyms: moderates, centrists | Noun

nonmoderate:/nɑːnˈmɑːdərɪt/ | adj. |非温和的；非中间派的| Not moderate; extreme. | The senator's nonmoderate stance on the issue alienated many voters.  参议员在这个问题上的非温和立场疏远了许多选民。|Synonyms: extreme, radical, immoderate | Antonyms: moderate, centrist | Adjective
