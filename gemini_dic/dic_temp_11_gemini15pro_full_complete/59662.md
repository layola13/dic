echinite:/ˈɛkɪnaɪt/ | n. |海胆化石，海胆纲动物|A fossil sea urchin, or a member of the Echinoidea class.|例句：The museum displayed a collection of echinites from the Jurassic period.  博物馆展出了一批侏罗纪时期的海胆化石。

例句：He found an echinite embedded in the cliff face. 他在悬崖壁上发现了一块海胆化石。|近义词：sea urchin fossil, echinoid|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
