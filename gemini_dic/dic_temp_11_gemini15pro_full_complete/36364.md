coincidental:/kəʊˌɪnsɪˈdentl/ /koʊˌɪnsɪˈdɛntl/| adj.|巧合的，偶然的|Happening by chance; not planned or intended.|例句：It was purely coincidental that we both chose the same restaurant. 我们俩选择同一家餐馆纯属巧合。|例句：It's coincidental that you should be staying in the same hotel. 你竟然住在同一家酒店，真是巧合。|近义词：accidental, fortuitous, unplanned, chance|反义词：planned, intentional, deliberate|词性:形容词

coincidence:/kəʊˈɪnsɪdəns/ /koʊˈɪnsɪdəns/| n.|巧合，偶然事件|The occurrence of events that happen at the same time by accident but seem to have some connection.|例句：It was a remarkable coincidence that we met again after so many years.  这么多年后我们再次相遇真是一个非凡的巧合。|例句：By coincidence, I met my old friend at the airport.  很巧，我在机场遇到了我的老朋友。|近义词：accident, chance, fluke|反义词：intention, plan|词性:名词
