nondedicated:/ˌnɒnˈdedɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
非专用的，未指定的
Not specifically allocated or assigned for a particular purpose.
The printer is nondedicated and can be used by anyone in the office. 这台打印机是非专用的，办公室里任何人都可以使用。
The company uses a nondedicated server for testing purposes.  这家公司使用一个非专用服务器进行测试。
synonyms: general-purpose, shared, common, universal
antonyms: dedicated, exclusive, specific, assigned
词性: 形容词
