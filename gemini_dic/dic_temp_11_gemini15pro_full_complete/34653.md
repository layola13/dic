clerestoried: /ˈklɪərstɔːrid/ | adj. | 有天窗墙的，有高侧窗的 | Having a clerestory or clerestories. A clerestory is a high section of wall that contains windows above eye level. These windows admit light to the center of a room. | The architect designed a clerestoried living room to maximize natural light.  建筑师设计了一个带天窗墙的客厅，以最大限度地利用自然光。|  The old church boasted a magnificent clerestoried nave. 这座古老的教堂拥有宏伟的天窗墙中殿。 | Synonyms: high-windowed, light-filled | Antonyms: windowless, dark | adjective
