lancelet:/ˈlænslət/| n. |文昌鱼|A small, translucent, fish-like marine animal that is a member of the chordate subphylum Cephalochordata.  It is considered to be a primitive chordate and is studied for insights into the evolution of vertebrates.|Lancelets are important for studying the origins of vertebrates. 文昌鱼对于研究脊椎动物的起源很重要。|The lancelet burrows in the sand. 文昌鱼在沙子里挖洞。|synonyms: amphioxus|antonyms: vertebrate|noun
