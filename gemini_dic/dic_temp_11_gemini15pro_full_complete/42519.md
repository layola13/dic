counterirritation:/ˌkaʊntərɪrɪˈteɪʃən/| n.|对症刺激，反刺激疗法|The application of a mustard plaster is a form of counterirritation.  敷芥末膏是一种反刺激疗法。| Counterirritation involves creating a minor irritation to relieve a more serious one. 反刺激疗法包括制造轻微的刺激来缓解更严重的刺激。|近义词: counter-stimulus, revulsion, derivation |反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "palliative care" which focuses on relieving symptoms without necessarily addressing the underlying cause) | 词性:noun
