nereis:/ˈnɪəriːs/| n. |沙蚕；海虫|A marine worm, typically found in shallow waters, belonging to the class Polychaeta.  It is often used as bait for fishing. |例句：Nereis are commonly found burrowing in the sand. 沙蚕通常在沙子里挖洞。

例句：The fisherman used nereis as bait to catch fish.  渔夫用沙蚕作诱饵捕鱼。|近义词：ragworm, clam worm, sandworm|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin "Nereis,"  ultimately from Greek Νηρηΐς (Nērēïs), one of the fifty Nereids, sea nymphs in Greek mythology.  The name refers to their graceful movements in the water.
