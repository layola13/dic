customising:/ˈkʌstəˌmaɪzɪŋ/
verb
定制，个性化
The process of modifying something to meet individual needs or preferences.
She is customising her phone with a new case and wallpaper. 她正在用新的手机壳和壁纸定制她的手机。
The company specialises in customising software for businesses. 这家公司专门为企业定制软件。
近义词:personalizing, tailoring, adapting, modifying
反义词:standardizing, generalizing
词性:v.
