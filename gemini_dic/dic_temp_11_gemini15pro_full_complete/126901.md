narcoses:/nɑːrˈkoʊsiːz/
n.
麻醉状态;昏迷
a state of stupor, unconsciousness, or insensibility
The patient was in a deep narcosis after the surgery.  病人手术后处于深度麻醉状态。

narcosis:/nɑːrˈkoʊsɪs/
n.
麻醉;昏迷

近义词:anesthesia, stupor, coma
反义词:consciousness, alertness
词性:noun
