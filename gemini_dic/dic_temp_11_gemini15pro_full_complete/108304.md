laurels: /ˈlɔːrəlz/ | n. | 桂冠；荣誉；成就 |  A crown of laurel leaves awarded as a prize in a contest or as a symbol of victory or distinction.  It can also refer to honor and recognition more generally. |  He rested on his laurels after winning the championship. 他在赢得冠军后就躺在功劳簿上睡大觉了。| synonyms: honors, awards, accolades, achievements, triumphs | antonyms: failures, defeats | noun


laurel: /ˈlɔːrəl/ | n. | 月桂树；桂冠 | A type of evergreen tree or shrub, or its leaves. Also refers to a wreath of laurel awarded as a mark of honor. | The laurel wreath is a symbol of victory. 桂冠是胜利的象征。| synonyms: bay tree, bay laurel | antonyms: N/A | noun
