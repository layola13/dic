acrylics: /əˈkrɪlɪks/
n.
丙烯酸树脂；丙烯酸颜料
A type of fast-drying synthetic paint made from acrylic resin.
例句：She prefers working with acrylics because they clean up easily with water. 她喜欢用丙烯颜料作画，因为它们很容易用水清洗。

例句：The artist used vibrant acrylics to create a stunning mural.  这位艺术家用鲜艳的丙烯颜料创作了一幅令人惊叹的壁画。
