bear with:/ber wɪð/ | v. | 忍受；容忍；耐心等待 | To be patient with someone or something, especially when they are annoying or difficult. | Example 1: Please bear with me while I find the document. 请耐心等我一下，我正在找文件。

Example 2: I know this process is slow, but please bear with us. 我知道这个过程很慢，但请耐心等待我们。 | Synonyms: tolerate, endure, put up with | Antonyms: lose patience with, refuse to tolerate | verb


bare with:  This is often a misspelling of "bear with". While not a standard phrase, some people might use "bare with" intending to mean "be open and honest with," or "be vulnerable with."  However, this usage is not widely accepted and is generally considered incorrect.
