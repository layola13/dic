block:/blɒk/ (British) /blɑːk/ (American)| n. v. adj.|块; 街区; 阻塞; 阻挡|A solid piece of hard material, typically with flat surfaces; an area of a city enclosed by streets; obstruct (something) by placing an obstacle in the way.|

例句：
The child was playing with wooden blocks.
孩子正在玩积木。

The museum is just two blocks down the street.
博物馆就在这条街往下走两个街区。

近义词：chunk, lump, piece, slab (noun); barricade, obstruct, hinder (verb); solid, dense (adjective)
反义词:unblock, clear (verb); open, permeable (adjective)
词性:noun, verb, adjective


block:/blɒk/ (British) /blɑːk/ (American)| v.|阻塞; 阻挡|To obstruct (something) by placing an obstacle in the way.|

例句：
The road was blocked by a fallen tree.
道路被一棵倒下的树挡住了。

He blocked the punch with his arm.
他用手臂挡住了那一拳。

近义词：barricade, obstruct, hinder
反义词:unblock, clear
词性:verb


block:/blɒk/ (British) /blɑːk/ (American)| adj.|块状的; 成批的|Formed of or resembling a solid mass; relating to or consisting of a large quantity of things dealt with as a unit.|
