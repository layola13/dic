observably:/əbˈzɜːrvəbli/| adv.|可观察地，明显地|In a way that can be observed or noticed.  It describes something that is apparent or evident enough to be seen or perceived.|The situation had observably improved.  情况明显好转了。|Synonym: noticeably, perceptibly, visibly, evidently, clearly, manifestly|Antonym: imperceptibly, subtly, invisibly|词性:副词
