half-worsted:/ˌhɑːf ˈwɜːstɪd/ | adj. | 半精纺的 | Made from yarn that is less tightly twisted than worsted yarn, resulting in a slightly fuzzier and softer fabric. | The half-worsted fabric is perfect for a comfortable, everyday sweater. 这件半精纺毛衣非常适合日常穿着，舒适度很高。| 近义词: semi-worsted | 反义词: worsted | 词性: 形容词
