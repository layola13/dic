lady's:/ˈleɪdiz/| n.|女士的；贵妇的|Something that belongs to a lady; a possessive form of lady.|例句：This is the lady's handbag. 这是这位女士的手提包。|近义词：woman's, female's|反义词：gentleman's, man's|词性:名词所有格

词源: From Middle English *ladies*, *ladyes*, from Old English *hlǣfdigan* (“lady”), genitive singular and nominative/accusative plural of *hlǣfdīġe*.
