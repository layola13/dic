morassy:/məˈræsɪ/ /məˈræsi/| adj.|沼泽的；泥泞的；像沼泽的|Resembling a marsh or swamp; boggy. Characterized by a soft, wet consistency.|The ground was morassy after the heavy rain. 雨后地面泥泞不堪。|近义词：marshy, swampy, boggy, miry|反义词：dry, arid, firm, solid|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French *marasse* "marsh," from Frankish *marisk* "marsh," from Proto-Germanic *mariskaz*.  Related to English "marsh".
