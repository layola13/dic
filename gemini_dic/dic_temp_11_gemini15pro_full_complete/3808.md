agnosic:/æɡˈnɑːstɪk/ | adj. n. | 不可知论的，不可知论者 | Relating to or characteristic of an agnostic;  a person who believes that nothing is known or can be known of the existence or nature of God or of anything beyond material phenomena; a person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God. |  例句：He has an agnostic view of the world. 他对世界持有不可知论的观点。| 例句：She declared herself an agnostic. 她宣称自己是一个不可知论者。 | 近义词：skeptical, doubting, unbeliever | 反义词：believer, devout, religious | 词性: adjective, noun | 词源: From Greek 'agnostos' (unknown) + '-ic'
