harborless:/ˈhɑːrbərlɪs/| adj.|无港口的，没有避难处的|Lacking a harbor or haven; offering no shelter or refuge.  |The harborless coast was exposed to the full fury of the storm.  无港口的海岸完全暴露在风暴的狂怒之下。|例句：The ship was caught in a storm off the harborless coast. 这艘船在无港口的海岸附近遭遇了暴风雨。|近义词：unsheltered, exposed, unprotected|反义词：sheltered, protected, safe|词性:形容词
词源: harbor (港口) + -less (无，没有)
