crossbred:/ˈkrɒsbrɛd/ (British) /ˈkrɔːsbred/ (American)| adj. n.|杂交的，杂种的|Bred by crossing different breeds or varieties.|例句：The farmer raises crossbred cattle for their hardiness.  这位农民饲养杂交牛，因为它们很强壮。

例句：Crossbred dogs are often healthier than purebreds. 杂交狗通常比纯种狗更健康。|近义词：hybrid, mixed-breed|反义词：purebred, pedigree|词性：形容词/名词


crossbreed:/ˈkrɒsbriːd/ (British) /ˈkrɔːsbrid/ (American)| v.|杂交，使杂交|To produce (an animal or plant) by crossbreeding.|例句：They crossbred different varieties of corn to develop a disease-resistant strain.  他们将不同品种的玉米杂交，以培育出抗病品种。

例句：The scientists successfully crossbred the two species of flowers. 科学家们成功地将两种花进行了杂交。|近义词：hybridize, interbreed|反义词：inbreed|词性：动词
