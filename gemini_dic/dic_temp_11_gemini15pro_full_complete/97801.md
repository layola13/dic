injuring: /ˈɪndʒərɪŋ/
verb
伤害，损害
present participle of injure
The accident resulted in injuring several passengers.  这场事故导致几名乘客受伤。
近义词: harming, damaging, hurting, wounding
反义词: healing, repairing, mending
词性: Verb (Present Participle)

Etymology:
From Middle English injuren, from Anglo-Norman enjurer, from Old French injurier (“to insult, offend, injure”), from Latin injūriāre (“to act unjustly towards, wrong, injure”), from injūria (“wrong, injustice, injury”), from in- (“not”) + jūs (“right, law”) + -ia.
