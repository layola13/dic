dentiloquy:/ˌdɛntɪˈlɒkwi/| n.|腹语术|The art of speaking in such a way that the voice appears to come from a source other than the speaker, typically a dummy.|The ventriloquist amazed the audience with his dentiloquy, making his dummy seem to have a personality of its own.|Synonyms: ventriloquism|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly clear speech, audible speech)|noun
