mirth:/mɜːrθ/| n.|欢笑，高兴，欢乐|Amusement, especially as expressed in laughter.|例句：Her eyes sparkled with mirth. 她的眼睛里闪烁着喜悦的光芒。

例句：The party was filled with mirth and laughter. 聚会充满了欢声笑语。

近义词：merriment, gaiety, glee, jollity, hilarity|反义词: sadness, sorrow, gloom, melancholy|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mirthe*, from Old English *myrgþ* (“joy, mirth, gladness, pleasure, delight”), from Proto-Germanic *murgiþō* (“joy”), from Proto-Indo-European *merǵ-* (“to rub, press, stroke”). Cognate with Old Frisian *merth* (“joy”), Old Saxon *murdi* (“joy”), Old High German *murdi* (“joy”), Gothic *𐌼𐌰𐍂𐌶𐌾𐌰* (marzja, “joy”). Related also to Old English *mearh* (“horse”). More at mare.
