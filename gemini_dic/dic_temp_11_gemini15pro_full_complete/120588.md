mirror-images:/ˈmɪrər ˌɪmɪdʒɪz/ |n.|镜像; 镜中之像|An exact copy or representation of something, as if reflected in a mirror.  Two figures that are mirror images of each other are identical in form but reversed in orientation.|The two buildings were almost mirror images of each other.|近义词: reflections, duplicates, counterparts|反义词: opposites, inversions|词性: 名词

mirror-image:/ˈmɪrər ˌɪmɪdʒ/ |adj.|镜像的; 镜面反射的|Relating to or being a mirror image; reversed in orientation.|The photograph showed a mirror-image version of the text.|近义词: reflected, reversed, inverted|反义词: original, unreversed|词性: 形容词
