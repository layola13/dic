abstentionism:/æbstɛnʃənɪzəm/
n.
弃权主义，不投票主义
The deliberate act of refraining from voting or participating in elections or other political processes.
High levels of abstentionism marked the recent elections.  最近的选举弃权率很高。
synonyms: nonparticipation, boycott, withdrawal
antonyms: participation, involvement, engagement
noun

abstention:/æbˈstɛnʃən/
n.
弃权，节制
An act of refraining from something, such as voting or drinking alcohol.
She practiced abstention from alcohol for health reasons. 她为了健康而戒酒。
synonyms: refraining, abstinence, avoidance
antonyms: indulgence, participation, involvement
noun

abstain:/æbˈsteɪn/
v.
弃权，戒除
To restrain oneself from doing or indulging in something.
He decided to abstain from voting in the election. 他决定在选举中弃权。
synonyms: refrain, desist, forbear
antonyms: indulge, partake, participate
verb
