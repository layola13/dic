over-enthusiastically:/ˌoʊvərɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪkli/
adverb
过度热情地
with excessive enthusiasm; in a way that shows too much excitement or eagerness.
She clapped over-enthusiastically, startling the people around her. 她过度热情地鼓掌，吓到了周围的人。
The salesman greeted me over-enthusiastically, which made me suspicious.  销售员过度热情地和我打招呼，这让我起了疑心。

近义词:excessively, overly, too eagerly
反义词:apathetically, indifferently, half-heartedly
词性:adverb

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "enthusiastic" (adjective from Greek "enthousiasmos" meaning "divine inspiration or possession") + "-ally" (adverbial suffix)
