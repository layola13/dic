hypostatic:/ˌhaɪpəˈstætɪk/| adj.| 本体论的；位格的；实质的|Relating to hypostasis, the underlying reality or substance of something, especially in Christian theology, referring to the three persons of the Trinity as distinct but sharing the same divine nature.  |例句：The hypostatic union of Christ's divine and human natures is a central tenet of Christian belief. 基督神性和人性的位格合一是基督教信仰的核心原则。

例句：The doctrine of the hypostatic union is complex and has been debated for centuries. 位格合一论是复杂的，并且已经争论了几个世纪。|近义词：essential, substantial, ontological|反义词: insubstantial, unreal|词性:adjective
