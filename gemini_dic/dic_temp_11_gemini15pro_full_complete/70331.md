fielders:/ˈfiːldərz/| n. |外野手；守备员|A fielder is a player in sports like baseball, cricket, and softball who is positioned in the field to catch or stop the ball hit by the batter. |The fielders were quick to react to the line drive. 外野手对击出的平直球反应迅速。|Near Synonyms:  outfielder, infielder, defender |Antonyms: batter, hitter|Noun

fielders:/ˈfiːldərz/| n. |守备员；外野队员|Someone who fields, especially a member of a fielding side in cricket or baseball.|The cricket team had excellent fielders who rarely missed a catch. 这支板球队拥有优秀的守备员，他们很少漏接球。|Near Synonyms:  outfielder, infielder, defender, player |Antonyms: batter, hitter, pitcher|Noun
