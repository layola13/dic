hool:/huːl/ | n. | 喧闹，狂欢，吵闹 | A noisy, boisterous celebration or activity; a spree. |  The team went on a hool after winning the championship. 球队赢得冠军后尽情狂欢。| synonyms: revelry, spree, celebration, carousal, binge | antonyms: quiet, peace, calm | noun

Etymology->
From Scottish, probably related to  "hull," meaning "husk" or "shell," suggesting the idea of casting off restraint.
