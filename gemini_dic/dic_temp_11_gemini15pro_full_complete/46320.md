dabbled:/ˈdæbəld/| v. | 浅尝；涉猎；玩水 | To casually engage in an activity without serious commitment or deep involvement. | He dabbled in painting, but never considered himself a serious artist.  他涉猎过绘画，但从未把自己当成一个真正的艺术家。|  dabble in, dabble with | Synonyms: tinker, toy, dip into, experiment with | Antonyms: specialize in, master, immerse oneself in | Verb

dabble:/ˈdæbəld/| adj. | 浅尝辄止的 |  Engaged in superficially or intermittently. |  His dabble knowledge of coding wasn't enough to land him the job. 他浅尝辄止的编程知识不足以让他获得这份工作。| dabble knowledge, dabble attempt | Synonyms: superficial, cursory, dilettante | Antonyms: profound, deep, thorough | Adjective
