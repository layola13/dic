fluencies:/ˈfluːənsiz/| n.|流利度，流畅度|The smooth, effortless, and rapid quality of speech or writing, often indicating proficiency or mastery of a language or skill.  It can also refer to the ability to express oneself easily and articulately.|Her fluencies in French and Spanish are impressive. 她法语和西班牙语的流利程度令人印象深刻。|近义词：articulacy, eloquence, smoothness, facility|反义词:hesitancy, disfluency, stammering|noun
