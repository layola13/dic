crystal:/ˈkrɪstl/ | n. adj.| 水晶; 晶体; 清澈透明的 |A clear, transparent mineral or glass substance, often having a regular symmetrical shape.  A piece of this substance, typically cut into a specific shape. Clear and transparent like crystal. |  The vase is made of crystal.  这个花瓶是水晶做的。 The stream had crystal clear water.  小溪里的水清澈透明。| 近义词: glass, quartz, diamond, clear, transparent | 反义词: opaque, cloudy, murky | 词性: 名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French cristal, from Latin crystallus, from Ancient Greek κρύσταλλος (krustallos, “ice, rock-crystal”), from κρύος (kruos, “icy cold, frost”).  The original meaning referred to quartz that was thought to be permanently frozen ice.
