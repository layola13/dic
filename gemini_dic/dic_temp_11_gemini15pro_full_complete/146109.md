overembellishes:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈbɛlɪʃɪz/
verb
过度装饰；过分修饰
To add excessive or unnecessary embellishments to something; to decorate or adorn something to an excessive degree.
The author overembellishes the story with unnecessary details. 作者在故事中添加了不必要的细节，使其过于修饰。
Synonyms: overdecorate, overadorn, overdo, exaggerate, elaborate excessively
Antonyms: simplify, understate, minimize, downplay
词性: 动词

词源:
From over- + embellish.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much".  "Embellish" comes from Old French "embellir," meaning "to make beautiful," ultimately from Latin "bellus," meaning "beautiful."
