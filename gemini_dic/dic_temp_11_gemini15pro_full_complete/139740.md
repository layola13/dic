ochroleucous:/ˌɒkrəˈluːkəs/| adj.|淡黄白色的，米黄色的|Of a pale yellowish-white color; creamy or buff.|例句：The ochroleucous walls of the ancient city reflected the desert sun. 古城的米黄色墙壁反射着沙漠的阳光。

例句：The bird's ochroleucous plumage provided excellent camouflage in the dry grasslands.  这只鸟米黄色的羽毛在干燥的草原上提供了极好的伪装。|近义词：cream, buff, ivory, yellowish-white|反义词:dark, bright, colorful|词性:adjective
词源:From Greek ōkhrós (pale yellow) + leukós (white).
