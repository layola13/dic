noncrystalline:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɪstəlɪn/ /ˌnɒnˈkrɪstəlɪn/| adj.|非晶的，非结晶的|Not having a crystalline structure; amorphous.|例句：Glass is a noncrystalline solid.  玻璃是一种非晶固体。|近义词：amorphous, glassy|反义词：crystalline|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + crystalline.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "crystalline" refers to a substance having a regular, repeating arrangement of atoms or molecules.
