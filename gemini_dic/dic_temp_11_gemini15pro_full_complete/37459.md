commission:/kəˈmɪʃən/ (US), /kəˈmɪʃn/ (UK)| n. vt.|佣金，委托，委任|An amount of money paid to someone for selling goods or services.  The act of officially asking someone to write, make, or create something or to do a task.|例句：She earns a commission on every sale she makes. 她每销售一件商品就赚取佣金。|近义词：fee, payment, charge, delegation, mandate|反义词: omission|noun

commission:/kəˈmɪʃən/ (US), /kəˈmɪʃn/ (UK)| vt.| 委任，委托|To officially ask someone to write, make, or create something or to do a task.|例句：The artist was commissioned to paint a mural.  这位艺术家被委任绘制一幅壁画。|近义词：authorize, appoint, engage, hire|反义词: dismiss, fire|verb
