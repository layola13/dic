arlock: /ˈɑːrlɒk/ | n. | 气锁, 空气闭锁 | An airlock is a device which permits the passage of people or materials between environments with different pressures. |  例句：The astronauts used the airlock to enter the space station.  宇航员使用气闸进入空间站。

例句：The airlock prevented the pressure from equalizing too quickly.  气闸防止压力过快均衡。 | 近义词：air valve, pressure chamber | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
