censurer:/ˈsɛnʃərər/| n.|批评者，责难者|A person who expresses strong disapproval; someone who condemns or criticizes.|The politician faced a barrage of criticism from censurers after the scandal broke.  这位政客在丑闻曝光后，面临着批评者的猛烈抨击。|Synonyms: critic, detractor, faultfinder, reprover|Antonyms: supporter, admirer, advocate|Noun

censurer:/ˈsɛnʃərər/| n.|审查员|An official who examines books, plays, news reports, etc., to remove anything considered offensive or harmful.|The censurer removed several scenes from the film before its release. 审查员在电影上映前删除了几个场景。|Synonyms: censor, examiner, reviewer|Antonyms:  promoter, distributor|Noun
