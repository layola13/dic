coinquination:/kɔɪŋkwɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
污染；玷污
The act of polluting or making something impure.
The coinquination of the river by industrial waste is a serious problem. 工业废料对河流的污染是一个严重的问题。
pollution, contamination, defilement
purification, cleansing
noun
