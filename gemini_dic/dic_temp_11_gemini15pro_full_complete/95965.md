inculpably:/ɪnˈkʌlpəbli/
adj.
无过失地，无辜地
without blame; innocently.
He acted inculpably in the matter. 他在这件事上是无辜的。

近义词:blamelessly, irreproachably, faultlessly
反义词:culpably, guiltily

词性:adjective (形容词)

词源:
From Late Latin *inculpabilis, from in- "not" + culpabilis "blameable," from culpare "to blame."
