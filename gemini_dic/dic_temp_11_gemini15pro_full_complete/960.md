academism:/əˈkædɪˌsɪzəm/ | n. |学院派；学院主义；拘泥形式|A style of painting or other art that follows traditional rules and methods rather than expressing an artist's original ideas.  A doctrine or practice based on the principles of a particular academy or school of thought, often characterized by a rigid adherence to rules and conventions.|The painting was criticized for its excessive academism. 这幅画因其过度的学院主义而受到批评。|Synonyms: conventionalism, formalism, traditionalism|Antonyms: innovation, originality, unconventionality|noun
