melatonin: /ˌmeləˈtəʊnɪn/ (UK) /ˌmeləˈtoʊnɪn/ (US) | n. |褪黑素，褪黑激素|A hormone secreted by the pineal gland that regulates sleep cycles. It is often used as a supplement to treat insomnia and jet lag. |  I take melatonin to help me sleep. 我服用褪黑素来帮助睡眠。

Melatonin levels naturally decrease with age.  随着年龄的增长，褪黑素水平自然下降。

|近义词: sleep aid, soporific |反义词:  (No direct antonym, but potentially stimulants like caffeine) | 词性: noun
