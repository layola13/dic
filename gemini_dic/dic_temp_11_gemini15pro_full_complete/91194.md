hydraulicity:/ˌhaɪdrɔːˈlɪsɪti/ | n. | 水力性，液压性 | The property of a substance relating to its behavior under hydraulic conditions, specifically its ability to transmit or be affected by hydraulic pressure.  This can refer to the ease with which a fluid flows through a system, the force it exerts, or its resistance to flow. |  The hydraulicity of the soil affects the dam's stability. 土壤的水力性影响大坝的稳定性。| synonyms: hydraulic conductivity, permeability | antonyms: impermeability | noun
