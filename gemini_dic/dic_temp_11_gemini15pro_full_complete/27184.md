callings:/ˈkɔːlɪŋz/
n.
职业，使命，天职
a strong urge toward a particular way of life or career; a vocation
She felt a calling to become a nurse. 她感到自己有当护士的天职。

例句：
He found his calling in teaching. 他在教学中找到了自己的使命。
She has a calling to help others. 她有一种帮助他人的使命感。

近义词：vocation, profession, occupation, career, pursuit
反义词：aversion, disinclination

词性: 名词 (plural)
