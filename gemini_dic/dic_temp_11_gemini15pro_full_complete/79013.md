githeri:/ɡɪˈθɛri/ | n. | 玉米豆子炖菜（肯尼亚传统菜肴）| A Kenyan traditional dish consisting of maize and beans boiled together. |  We had githeri for dinner last night. 昨晚我们晚饭吃了玉米豆子炖菜。|  Example: Githeri is a staple food in many Kenyan households. 玉米豆子炖菜是许多肯尼亚家庭的主食。 | Synonyms:  maize and beans stew,  bean and corn stew | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
