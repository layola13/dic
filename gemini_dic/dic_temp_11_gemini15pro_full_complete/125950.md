musicalness:/ˈmjuːzɪkəlnəs/| n. |音乐性；旋律优美|The quality of being musical or melodious.  The musicalness of her voice captivated the audience.|例句：The musicalness of the melody made it instantly memorable. 这段旋律的音乐性让它令人难忘。

例句：His guitar playing lacked musicalness. 他的吉他演奏缺乏音乐性。|近义词：melody, melodiousness, harmony, euphony|反义词：discordance, dissonance, cacophony|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From musical + -ness.  "Musical" ultimately derives from Latin "mūsicus" (musical), from "mūsa" (muse).  The suffix "-ness" is a native English suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
