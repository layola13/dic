hemastatics:/ˌhiːməˈstætɪks/
noun
止血药，止血法
agents or methods used to stop bleeding
The surgeon used several hemostatics during the operation to control the bleeding.  外科医生在手术过程中使用了多种止血剂来控制出血。
synonyms: coagulants, styptics
antonyms: anticoagulants


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek haima (blood) + statikos (causing to stand still)
