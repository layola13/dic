dryest:/ˈdraɪɪst/| adj.|最干燥的|Superlative form of dry; having the least moisture.|The Atacama Desert is one of the dryest places on Earth. 阿塔卡马沙漠是地球上最干燥的地方之一。|The dryest month of the year is usually July.  一年中最干燥的月份通常是七月。|近义词：arid, parched, dehydrated|反义词：wettest, dampest, moistest|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *driegste, *druyeste, from Old English drīgeste (superlative of drīge (“dry”)), from Proto-Germanic *draugiztaz.
