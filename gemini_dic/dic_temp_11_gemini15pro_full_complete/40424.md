contraries:/ˈkɒntrəriz/ | n. |相反的事物；对立面|Things that are contraries are in opposition to each other. |例句：Hot and cold are contraries.  冷和热是相反的。|近义词：opposites, antitheses |反义词:similarities, likenesses | noun

contrary:/ˈkɒntrəri/ | adj. |相反的；对立的|If something is contrary to something else, it is the opposite of it. |例句：The results were contrary to expectations. 结果与预期相反。|近义词：opposite, conflicting |反义词:similar, consistent | adjective

contrary:/ˈkɒntrəri/ | n. |相反的事物；对立面|A contrary is something that is the opposite of something else. |例句：On the contrary, I think it's a great idea.  相反，我认为这是一个好主意。|近义词：opposite, reverse |反义词:same, identical | noun
