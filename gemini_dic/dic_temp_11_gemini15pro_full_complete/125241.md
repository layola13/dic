multidentate:/ˌmʌltiˈdɛnteɪt/| adj.|多齿的，多配位的|Having many teeth or tooth-like projections, or, in chemistry, having multiple binding sites.|例句：EDTA is a multidentate ligand. EDTA是一种多齿配体。|例句：The multidentate grip of the tool ensured a secure hold.  这个工具的多齿状夹持确保了牢固的握持。|近义词：polydentate, chelating (in chemistry)|反义词：monodentate, bidentate|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From multi- (many) + dentate (toothed), from Latin *dentatus*, past participle of *dentare* "to provide with teeth," from dens (tooth).
