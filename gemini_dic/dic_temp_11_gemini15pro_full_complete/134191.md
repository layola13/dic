noninclusivenesses:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈkluːsɪvnɪsɪz/
noun
非包容性；排斥性
The state or quality of not being inclusive; exclusion.  The plural form of noninclusiveness.
The report highlighted the noninclusivenesses of the current system, noting how it marginalized certain groups.  这份报告强调了当前制度的排斥性，指出它是如何边缘化某些群体的。
Synonyms: exclusions, prejudices, biases, discriminations
Antonyms: inclusiveness, inclusivity, acceptance, integration
词性: 名词
