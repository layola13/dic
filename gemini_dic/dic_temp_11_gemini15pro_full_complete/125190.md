multicellular:/ˌmʌltiˈsɛljʊlər/ |adj.|多细胞的|Composed of multiple cells, as opposed to unicellular.|例句：Humans are multicellular organisms. 人类是多细胞生物。

例句：Multicellular organisms are more complex than unicellular organisms. 多细胞生物比单细胞生物更复杂。|近义词：pluricellular|反义词：unicellular|词性: adjective

Etymology->
From multi- + cellular. Multi- comes from Latin multus "much, many". Cellular comes from Latin cellula "small room, cell," diminutive of cella "storeroom, chamber."
