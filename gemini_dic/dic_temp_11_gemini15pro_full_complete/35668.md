cobbing:/ˈkɒbɪŋ/| n.| 玉米棒；去雄；粗糙的修整|Cobbing is the process of removing the male flowers from maize plants to prevent self-pollination. It can also refer to a rough or rudimentary form of trimming or shaping.|The farmer was cobbing the maize plants in the field.|近义词：topping, trimming, pruning|反义词:planting, growing|词性:noun

cobbing:/ˈkɒbɪŋ/| v.| 去雄；粗糙地修整|Cobbing refers to the act of removing the male flowers from a maize plant. It can also mean to roughly trim or shape something.|He was cobbing the corn to improve its yield. |近义词：topping, trimming, pruning|反义词:planting, growing|词性:verb
