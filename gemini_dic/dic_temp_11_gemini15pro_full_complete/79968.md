go-bad:/ɡoʊ bæd/ | v. | 变坏，腐败，变质 | To deteriorate in quality, especially of food or drink; to become unpleasant or undesirable. | The milk went bad after being left out of the fridge. 牛奶放在冰箱外面后变坏了。| synonyms: spoil, decay, decompose, rot | antonyms: improve, freshen, ameliorate | verb

go-bad:/ɡoʊ bæd/ | v. | (of a situation or relationship) deteriorate; become unpleasant or difficult. | Things went bad between them after the argument. 争吵之后，他们的关系变坏了。 | synonyms: deteriorate, worsen, decline | antonyms: improve, recover | verb
