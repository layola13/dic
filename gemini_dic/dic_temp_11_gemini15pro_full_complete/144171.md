outshown:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊn/
verb
胜过，比...更出色
surpassed in performance or appearance, especially unexpectedly.
Her latest novel outshone all her previous works. 她的最新小说胜过她所有以前的作品。
outshine, surpass, eclipse, outdo, outstrip
be outshone by, be overshadowed by, be inferior to
