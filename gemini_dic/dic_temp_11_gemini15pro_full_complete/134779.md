nonmetamorphosis:/ˌnɒnˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəsɪs/ | n. |无变态, 非变态|The state or quality of not undergoing metamorphosis; absence of metamorphosis.  It refers to development in some animals where the young hatch from eggs resembling miniature adults and do not undergo a larval stage. |Silverfish exhibit nonmetamorphosis, hatching from eggs as tiny, wingless versions of the adults.| 近义词: ametabolism | 反义词: metamorphosis | 词性: noun
