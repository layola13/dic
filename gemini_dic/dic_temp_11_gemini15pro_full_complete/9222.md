antipapacy:/ˌæntiˈpeɪpəsi/
n.
反对教皇制; 反对教皇权威
Opposition to the authority of the pope, especially the temporal power of the Roman Catholic pope.
The rise of antipapacy sentiment led to the Reformation.  反对教皇情绪的兴起导致了宗教改革。
The historian wrote extensively about the history of antipapacy in Europe.  这位历史学家详细地撰写了欧洲反对教皇制的历史。
Synonyms: anti-Catholicism, anti-clericalism
Antonyms: papism, ultramontanism
noun
