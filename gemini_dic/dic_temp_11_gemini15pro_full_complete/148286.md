oversocial:/ˌoʊvərˈsoʊʃəl/ | adj. | 过度社交的；社交过度的 | Excessively sociable; engaging in social interaction to an excessive degree. |  She seemed oversocial, always needing to be surrounded by people. 她似乎过度社交，总是需要被人群包围。|  He's so oversocial that he often neglects his own needs to please others. 他太喜欢社交了，以至于经常为了取悦别人而忽略自己的需求。 | 近义词: gregarious, outgoing, extroverted | 反义词: antisocial, withdrawn, introverted | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "social" (adjective relating to society or interaction with others).  The prefix "over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, across." "Social" comes from Late Latin "socialis," from "socius" meaning "companion, ally."
