nathusii:/nəˈθuziːaɪ/ | adj. | 纳氏的，指代生物亚种 |  Relating to or denoting a subspecies, typically used in taxonomy to specify a particular subspecies. Named after Hermann Nathusius (1809-1878), a German zoologist and agriculturalist. | Pipistrellus nathusii is a bat species. 纳氏伏翼是一种蝙蝠。 | 近义词: (Often no direct synonym, depends on the species)  | 反义词: (No antonym) | 词性: adjective
