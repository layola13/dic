blowtorch:/ˈbloʊˌtɔːrtʃ/ (US) /ˈbləʊˌtɔːtʃ/ (UK)| n. |喷灯，喷火器|A portable device that produces a very hot flame by burning a mixture of fuel and air or oxygen, used for tasks such as welding, brazing, and soldering.|He used a blowtorch to melt the ice on the pipes.|synonyms: torch, flamethrower|antonyms: extinguisher|noun


blowtorches:/ˈbloʊˌtɔːrtʃɪz/ (US) /ˈbləʊˌtɔːtʃɪz/ (UK)| n. |喷灯（复数）|Plural form of blowtorch, referring to multiple portable devices producing a very hot flame.|The workers used blowtorches to cut through the metal.|synonyms: torches, flamethrowers|antonyms: extinguishers|noun
