inauguration: /ɪˌnɔːɡjəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 就职典礼；开幕式；落成典礼 |  A formal ceremony to mark the beginning of something, such as the term of office of a president or the opening of a new building. | The inauguration of the new president was a grand affair. 新总统的就职典礼是一场盛大的活动。 | synonyms: commencement, initiation, opening, dedication | antonyms: conclusion, termination | noun

inaugurate: /ɪˈnɔːɡjəreɪt/ | vt. | 为…举行就职典礼；开创；开始 | To formally begin or introduce something new. | The mayor inaugurated the new park with a ribbon-cutting ceremony. 市长以剪彩仪式为新公园揭幕。 | synonyms: commence, initiate, launch, begin | antonyms: conclude, terminate | verb
