ecotourists: /ˈiːkəʊˌtʊərɪsts/ /ˈiːkoʊˌtʊrɪsts/ | n. | 生态游客 | Tourists who travel to natural areas, often to support conservation efforts and observe wildlife, while minimizing their environmental impact. |  Ecotourists often visit national parks and reserves. 生态游客经常参观国家公园和保护区。 | Ecotourists enjoy observing wildlife in their natural habitat. 生态游客喜欢观察野生动物在其自然栖息地。 | synonyms: nature tourists, responsible tourists, green tourists | antonyms: mass tourists, irresponsible tourists | noun
