carveries: /ˈkɑːrvəriːz/
noun
雕刻品；雕刻艺术；(尤指餐馆提供的)切好的烤肉
places where meat is carved, especially in restaurants
The hotel is famous for its Sunday carveries. 这家酒店的周日烤肉很有名。
synonyms: carvings, sculptures, buffet, smorgasbord
antonyms: N/A
n.


carvery: /ˈkɑːrvəri/
noun
(尤指餐馆提供的)切好的烤肉, 雕刻品；雕刻艺术
a place in a restaurant where cooked meat is cut into slices for you
We went to the carvery for Sunday lunch. 我们去自助餐厅吃了周日午餐。
synonyms: buffet, smorgasbord, cafeteria
antonyms: N/A
n.
