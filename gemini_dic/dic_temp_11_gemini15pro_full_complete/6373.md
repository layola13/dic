amphicarpous:/æmfɪˈkɑːrpəs/
adj.
两型果实的，具有地上和地下两种果实的
Producing fruit of two kinds, especially aerial and subterranean fruit, differing in form.
The amphicarpous plant produced both above-ground and below-ground seeds. 这种两型果实的植物既能在地上结籽，也能在地下结籽。
Near Synonyms: geocarpic, hypogeous
Antonyms: epigeous
词性: adjective
