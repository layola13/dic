magnitudes:/ˈmæɡnɪtjuːdz/| n. |大小；量级；震级；重要性|a great size or extent of something.|例句：They didn't seem to appreciate the magnitude of the problem. 他们似乎没有意识到这个问题的重要性。|例句：The earthquake registered 7.2 on the Richter scale of magnitude. 这次地震的震级为里氏7.2级。|近义词：size, extent, scale, importance, significance|反义词：insignificance, unimportance|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Latin *magnitūdō*, from *magnus* ("great") + *-itūdō* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).
