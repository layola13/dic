defectiveness:/dɪˈfɛktɪvnəs/| n.|缺陷；不完美；缺点|The defectiveness of the product led to numerous customer complaints.  该产品的缺陷导致众多客户投诉。|例句：The defectiveness of the bridge caused it to collapse. 桥梁的缺陷导致其倒塌。|近义词：faultiness, imperfection, flaw, deficiency|反义词：perfection, soundness, completeness|词性:名词
词源: From Late Middle English *defectyfnes, from Anglo-Norman and Old French defectif, from Latin dēfectivus (“incomplete, defective”), from dēficiō (“I fail, am wanting, revolt”), from dē (“down, away”) + faciō (“I do, make”).
