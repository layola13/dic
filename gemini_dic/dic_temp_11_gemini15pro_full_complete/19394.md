bicarbs:/ˈbaɪˌkɑːrbs/ | n. |碳酸氢盐；小苏打| Bicarbs are a type of salt, specifically bicarbonates, often used in baking or as an antacid.  碳酸氢盐是一种盐，特别是碳酸氢盐，通常用于烘焙或作为抗酸剂。 | The recipe calls for two teaspoons of bicarbs.  这个食谱需要两茶匙小苏打。| synonyms: baking soda, sodium bicarbonate | antonyms:  acids | noun
