frenzied:/ˈfrɛn.zid/ | adj. |疯狂的，狂乱的|Characterized by wild and uncontrolled activity or emotion. |The shoppers were frenzied in their pursuit of bargains. 购物者疯狂地追求便宜货。|Synonyms: frantic, wild, furious, hectic, frenetic |Antonyms: calm, peaceful, tranquil, composed, serene| adjective


frenzy:/ˈfrɛn.zi/ | n. | 狂乱，疯狂，狂热|A state of wild excitement or uncontrolled activity. |The media frenzy surrounding the celebrity wedding was overwhelming. 围绕名人婚礼的媒体狂潮势不可挡。|Synonyms: madness, hysteria, turmoil, chaos, furor |Antonyms: calmness, peace, tranquility, order, composure| noun
