hatboxes:/ˈhætˌbɑːksɪz/
n.
帽盒（复数）
Boxes designed to hold hats, often made of cardboard, leather, or other sturdy materials.
She carefully packed her hats in their respective hatboxes. 她小心翼翼地把她的帽子装进各自的帽盒里。
The attic was full of old hatboxes filled with memories. 阁楼里满是装着回忆的旧帽盒。
synonyms: hat cases, containers for hats
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) open shelves, hooks
