overengineered:/ˌoʊvərˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrd/| adj.|过度设计的，过分工程化的|Designed with more complexity than is necessary or desirable, often resulting in a product that is more expensive, harder to use, or less reliable than it needs to be.|This software is overengineered; it has so many features that nobody uses.|Synonyms: overcomplicated, overdesigned, overbuilt|Antonyms: simple, streamlined, basic|Adjective
