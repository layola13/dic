daylit:/ˈdeɪlɪt/| adj.|日光照亮的，白天可见的|Lit by daylight; visible in daylight. |The daylit room was bright and airy. 日光照亮的房间明亮通风。|近义词：sunlit, light, bright|反义词:dark, dim, night|词性:形容词

词源: From day + lit, the past participle of light.  "Day" comes from Old English "dæg," and "light" from Old English "lēoht."
