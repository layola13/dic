micromanaged:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒd/
|adj.|被微观管理的；被事无巨细地管理的|Controlled or managed with excessive attention to minor details.|The employees felt micromanaged and demotivated. 员工们感到被微观管理，失去了动力。|Synonyms: overmanaged, overcontrolled|Antonyms: empowered, autonomous|adjective

micromanage:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒ/
|v.|微观管理；事无巨细地管理|To control or manage with excessive attention to minor details.|The new boss tends to micromanage, which frustrates the team. 新老板倾向于微观管理，这使团队感到沮丧。|Synonyms: overmanage, overcontrol|Antonyms: empower, delegate|verb

micromanagement:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒmənt/
|n.|微观管理；事无巨细地管理|The practice of controlling or managing with excessive attention to minor details.|The company suffered from excessive micromanagement.  这家公司深受过度微观管理之害。|Synonyms: overmanagement, overcontrol|Antonyms: empowerment, autonomy|noun
