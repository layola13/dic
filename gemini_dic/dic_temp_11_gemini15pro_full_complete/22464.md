bookmaker:/ˈbʊkmˌeɪkər/ (US), /ˈbʊkmeɪkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|博彩公司，庄家|A person or organization that accepts and pays out bets on horse races, sporting events, etc.|例句：The bookmaker is offering odds of 2 to 1 on that horse.  那个博彩公司在那匹马身上提供的赔率是2比1。

例句：I placed a bet with the bookmaker on the outcome of the football match. 我在博彩公司那里就足球比赛的结果下注了。

近义词：turf accountant, oddsmaker|反义词: bettor, gambler|词性:noun
