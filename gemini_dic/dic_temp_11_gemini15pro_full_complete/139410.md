observers:/əbˈzɜːrvərz/| n.|观察者，观察员|A person who watches or notices something.|例句：The observers noted the bird's unusual behavior. 观察员注意到了这只鸟不同寻常的行为。

例句：United Nations observers monitored the elections. 联合国观察员监督了选举。|近义词：onlookers, viewers, witnesses, spectators, monitors|反义词:participants, actors|Noun
