atrocity:/əˈtrɒsəti/ (US) /əˈtrɑːsəti/ (UK)| n.|暴行，残暴行为|An act of extreme cruelty and violence, typically one involving physical injury or death.|The atrocities committed during the war shocked the world. 战争期间犯下的暴行震惊了世界。|近义词：barbarity, cruelty, savagery, wickedness, inhumanity|反义词：kindness, compassion, humanity|词性:名词

atrocities:/əˈtrɒsətiz/ (US) /əˈtrɑːsətiz/ (UK)| n. (plural)|暴行（复数）|Plural form of atrocity, referring to multiple acts of extreme cruelty and violence.|The museum documented the atrocities of the regime.  博物馆记录了该政权的暴行。|近义词：barbarities, cruelties, savageries|反义词：acts of kindness, acts of compassion|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *atrocitās*, from Latin *atrōx* "fierce, cruel."
