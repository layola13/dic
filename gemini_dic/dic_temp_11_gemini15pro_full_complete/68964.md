fathers-in-law: /ˈfɑːðərz ɪn lɔː/ (US), /ˈfɑːðəz ɪn lɔː/ (UK)| n. |岳父；公公（复数）|The plural form of "father-in-law," referring to more than one father of one's spouse.  It indicates a relationship by marriage, not blood.|My fathers-in-law are both retired teachers. 我的岳父和公公都是退休教师。|Near synonyms:  in-laws (general term)|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps biological father)|Noun
