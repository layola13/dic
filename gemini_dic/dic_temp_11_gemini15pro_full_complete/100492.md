intervenes:/ˌɪntərˈviːnz/
verb
干预，介入，插手，调停
comes between two things, people, or events and affects what happens
The central bank intervened in the currency markets today to try to stabilize the exchange rate. 中央银行今天对货币市场进行了干预，试图稳定汇率。
intercede, mediate, interfere
stand by, ignore
v.
