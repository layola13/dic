existentialisms:/ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəlɪzəmz/
n.
存在主义（的各种形式）
The philosophical theory or approach that emphasizes the existence of the individual person as a free and responsible agent determining their own development through acts of the will.  It is often associated with themes of absurdity, anxiety, freedom, and responsibility.
Many 20th-century artists and writers explored themes of existentialism in their work.  许多20世纪的艺术家和作家在他们的作品中探索了存在主义的主题。
近义词:philosophies of existence
反义词:essentialism, determinism
词性:noun
