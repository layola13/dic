lighten:/ˈlaɪtn/ (US), /ˈlaɪtən/ (UK)| v. |变亮，变轻，使愉快，减轻|To make or become lighter in weight, color, or mood; to reduce a burden or problem. |例句：The sky began to lighten as dawn approached. 随着黎明的临近，天空开始变亮。|近义词：brighten, illuminate, alleviate, ease|反义词：darken, weigh down, burden|词性:动词

lighten:/ˈlaɪtn/ (US), /ˈlaɪtən/ (UK)| adj. |轻的|Less heavy than usual or expected. |例句：This new laptop is much lighter than my old one. 这台新笔记本电脑比我的旧电脑轻得多。|近义词：light, weightless|反义词：heavy|词性:形容词 (rare, usually used in compounds like "lighten up")


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *līhtan, from Proto-Germanic *līhtjaną (“to make light, illuminate”), from Proto-Indo-European *leuk- (“light, brightness”).
