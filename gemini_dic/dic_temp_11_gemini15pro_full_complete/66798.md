expanded:/ɪkˈspændɪd/ | adj. | 扩展的，扩大的，展开的 | Having been made larger or more extensive. |  The company expanded its operations into new markets. 公司将其业务扩展到新的市场。 |
expanded:/ɪkˈspændɪd/ | v. | 扩展，扩大，展开（expand 的过去式和过去分词）| To increase in size, scope, or extent. | They expanded the house by adding a new wing. 他们通过增加新的侧翼扩大了房子。|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *expandere*, from *ex-* "out" + *pandere* "to spread."

SYNONYMS->
enlarged, widened, broadened, increased, stretched, developed, amplified

ANTONYMS->
contracted, shrunk, reduced, diminished, compressed, condensed

VERB FORM->
expand (present), expands (third person singular present), expanding (present participle), expanded (past tense and past participle)
