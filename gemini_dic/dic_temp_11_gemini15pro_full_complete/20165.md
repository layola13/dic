bipont:/ˈbaɪpɒnt/ | adj. n. | 双点字体的；双点铅字 |  A typeface or font size of approximately 4.5 points, roughly half the size of minion. Originating from the printing house of Christophe Plantin in Antwerp, and later used by his sons-in-law, the Moretus family. The name refers to the two points that made up the size of the type. | This book was printed in bipont, making it incredibly difficult to read. 这本书是用双点字体印刷的，非常难读。|  The printer used bipont type for the footnotes. 印刷商用双点字体印刷脚注。 | Near synonyms: diamond, brillant | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific size; generally, larger font sizes) | adjective, noun
