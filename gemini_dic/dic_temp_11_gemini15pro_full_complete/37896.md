complaisantly: /kəmˈpleɪzəntli/ | adv. | 顺从地；殷勤地；讨好地 | In a way that shows a willingness to please; obligingly.  |  He complaisantly agreed to her request. 他殷勤地同意了她的请求。 | She complaisantly offered to drive him home. 她讨好地提出开车送他回家。 | 近义词: obligingly, agreeably, submissively, deferentially | 反义词: stubbornly, defiantly, rebelliously | 词性: 副词

complaisant: /kəmˈpleɪzənt/ | adj. | 顺从的；殷勤的；讨好的 | Willing to please others; obliging. | He had a complaisant nature, always eager to help. 他性格随和，总是乐于助人。 |  The hotel staff were unfailingly complaisant. 酒店员工总是殷勤周到。 | 近义词: obliging, agreeable, amenable, accommodating | 反义词: uncooperative, unhelpful, disagreeable | 词性: 形容词

词源: From French *complaisant*, from present participle of *complaire* "to please," from Latin *complacere* "to be very pleasing," from *com-* "with" + *placere* "to please."
