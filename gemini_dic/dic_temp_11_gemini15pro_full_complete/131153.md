nonbookishness:/ˌnɒnˈbʊkɪʃnəs/
n.|非书卷气，不爱读书|The quality of not being bookish; a lack of interest in reading or academic pursuits. |His nonbookishness was surprising, given his family's academic background. 考虑到他的家庭学术背景，他不爱读书令人惊讶。|Synonyms: unscholarliness, philistinism, worldliness |Antonyms: bookishness, scholarly, studious|noun
