bound-in:/ˌbaʊndˈɪn/
adjective
装订的，固定式的
Bound together as part of a book.  Integrated, incorporated.
This dictionary has several bound-in maps. 这本词典有几张装订好的地图。
The notebook came with bound-in dividers. 这个笔记本带有固定的分隔页。
synonym: built-in, integrated, incorporated
antonym: loose-leaf, detachable
词性: 形容词
