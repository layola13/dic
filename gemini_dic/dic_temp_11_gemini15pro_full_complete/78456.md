getter:/ˈɡɛtər/| n.|获取器，读取器|A method that retrieves the value of a member variable.  A getter is often paired with a setter, which allows the value of the member variable to be changed.|The `getName()` method is a getter that returns the name of the object.|近义词:accessor, retriever|反义词:setter|词性:noun
