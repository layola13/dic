kitling:/ˈkɪtlɪŋ/| n.|小猫，幼猫|A young kitten, usually one that is still dependent on its mother.  A kitling is a very young kitten.|The kitling was so small it could fit in the palm of my hand. 这只小猫太小了，可以放在我的手掌里。|The mother cat carefully groomed her kitlings. 母猫细心地给它的小猫们梳理毛发。|Synonyms: kitten, kitty, catling|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly adult cat)|noun
