lithotypic:/ˌlɪθəˈtɪpɪk/ | adj. | 岩石类型的，岩石类型的 | Relating to or characteristic of a rock type; descriptive of the physical characteristics of a rock. | The lithotypic description of the sample included color, texture, and grain size.  该样本的岩石类型描述包括颜色、质地和粒度。| synonyms: petrographic, petrologic | antonyms: None | adjective

lithotypic:/ˌlɪθəˈtɪpɪk/ | adj. | 岩石类型的，岩石类型的 |  Descriptive of the macroscopic characteristics of a rock unit, especially as relating to its origin and composition. | The geologist noted the lithotypic variations within the stratigraphic sequence. 地质学家注意到地层层序中岩石类型的变化。 | synonyms: rock-type, petrological | antonyms: None | adjective
