nonabsolutenesses:/ˌnɒnˌæbsəˈluːtnəsɪz/
|n.|非绝对性；非完全性|The state or quality of not being absolute; lack of absoluteness.  The concept of nonabsolutenesses acknowledges the inherent limitations and contextual dependencies of knowledge and truth.|The philosopher explored the nonabsolutenesses of moral principles in a complex world. 哲学家在一个复杂的世界中探索了道德原则的非绝对性。|近义词:relativity, contingency, uncertainty|反义词:absoluteness, certainty, definiteness|词性:noun


Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
absolute (adjective): complete; total; not limited in any way
-ness (suffix): forms nouns expressing state or quality
