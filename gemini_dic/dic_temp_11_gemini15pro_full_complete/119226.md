miascite:/ˈmaɪəsaɪt/| n. |镁霞正长岩|A plutonic rock composed essentially of orthoclase and biotite, with smaller amounts of hornblende, pyroxene, and olivine.|The miascite formed deep within the Earth's crust. 镁霞正长岩在地壳深处形成。|Synonym:  syenite (a more general term)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific rock type)|noun
