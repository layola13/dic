balkanization:/ˌbɔːlkənɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|巴尔干化|The division of a multinational state into smaller ethnically homogeneous entities, often accompanied by conflict.|The balkanization of the region has led to increased tensions. 该地区的巴尔干化导致紧张局势加剧。|fragmentation, division, disintegration|unification, integration|noun

balkanize:/ˈbɔːlkənaɪz/ |vt.|使巴尔干化|To divide (a region or body) into smaller mutually hostile states or groups.|Politicians fear the country will balkanize along ethnic lines.  政客们担心这个国家会沿着种族界限巴尔干化。|divide, fragment, partition|unite, integrate|verb

balkanized:/ˈbɔːlkənaɪzd/ |adj.|巴尔干化的|Divided into smaller, often hostile, units.|The formerly unified nation became a balkanized collection of warring states.  这个曾经统一的国家变成了一个巴尔干化的交战国集合体。|fragmented, divided, disintegrated|unified, integrated|adjective
