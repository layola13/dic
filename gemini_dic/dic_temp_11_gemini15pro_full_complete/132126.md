ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): from Latin *non-, meaning "not"
crystallized (adjective): from crystal + -ize (verb suffix) + -ed (adjective suffix). "Crystal" ultimately derives from Greek "krystallos," meaning "ice," later applied to quartz and other transparent minerals.
