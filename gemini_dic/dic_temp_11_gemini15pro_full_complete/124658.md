mov:/mʌv/| v. |移动; 搬家;  感动;  提议;  采取行动;  进展;  出售;  排便|To change from one place or position to another; to cause someone to have strong feelings, such as sadness, sympathy, or admiration; to make a formal suggestion about something at a meeting so that it can be discussed and voted on. |例句：He moved the chair closer to the fire. 他把椅子挪近火炉。

|近义词：shift, transfer, relocate, stir, touch, propose, proceed, sell, defecate|反义词:stay, remain, fix, freeze|词性:v.
