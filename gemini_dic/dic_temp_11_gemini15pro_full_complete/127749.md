negotious: /nɪˈɡoʊʃəs/ | adj. | 爱谈判的；精于谈判的；善于讨价还价的 | Skilled in negotiation; shrewd in bargaining. | He's a negotious businessman, always looking for the best deal. 他是一位精明的商人，总是寻求最好的交易。|  She was a negotious shopper, never paying full price for anything. 她是一个很会讨价还价的顾客，从不付全价买任何东西。 |近义词： shrewd, astute, canny, bargaining | 反义词： naive, gullible, unbusinesslike | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From Latin *negotiosus*, from negotium (“business, trade, occupation”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of," "prone to," "characterized by").  The word "negotiate" itself comes from the same Latin root.
