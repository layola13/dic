breakages:/ˈbreɪkɪdʒɪz/
n.
破损，损坏；损耗
the act of breaking something, or the things that are broken
例句：The shop suffered heavy breakages during the riot.  这家商店在骚乱中损失惨重。

例句：We have to account for breakages in the inventory. 我们必须把库存中的损耗计算在内。

近义词：damage, loss, breakage, fractures, cracks
反义词：repairs, mending, fixing


词性: 名词(plural noun)


词源: From the verb "break" (Old English *brecan) + suffix "-age" (Old French -age, from Latin -aticum).
