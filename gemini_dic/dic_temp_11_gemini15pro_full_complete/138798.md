nummular:/ˈnʌmjʊlər/| adj.|钱币状的，圆形的|Resembling a coin in shape; discoid.  Relating to or consisting of money.|The doctor noted the nummular eczema on the patient's arm. 医生注意到病人手臂上的钱币状湿疹。|近义词：discoid, circular, round|反义词：irregular, shapeless|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin "nummulus" (small coin), diminutive of "nummus" (coin).
