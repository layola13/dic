flak's:/flæks/ | n. |高射炮的; 防空炮火的 (通常指猛烈批评或反对)| possessive form of flak, referring to something belonging to or associated with flak (anti-aircraft fire, or by extension, strong criticism). | The candidate faced flak's barrage of questions.  这位候选人面临一连串的质问。|近义词: criticism, opposition, attack |反义词: praise, support |名词

词源: flak comes from the German word *Fliegerabwehrkanone* meaning "aircraft defense cannon."  The possessive form 'flak's' simply adds the possessive marker 's'.
