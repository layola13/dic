bannock:/ˈbænək/ | n. |烤饼，燕麦饼|A type of flat quick bread or any large, round article baked or cooked from grain.  Traditionally made from oats or barley flour, bannocks are cooked on a griddle or baked in an oven.|My grandmother used to make delicious bannocks over an open fire. 我的祖母过去常常在篝火上做美味的烤饼。| The restaurant served bannock with butter and jam. 这家餐厅供应加黄油和果酱的烤饼。 |近义词：flatbread, scone, oatcake |反义词: None|词性:noun
