antigravities:/ˌæntiˈɡrævɪtiz/
n.
反重力；反重力装置；抵消重力的力
Forces that counteract gravity.  A hypothetical force that opposes gravity.
例句：Scientists are exploring the possibilities of antigravities. 科学家们正在探索反重力的可能性。

例句：The spaceship utilized antigravities to lift off.  宇宙飞船利用反重力装置起飞。


近义词：levitation, zero gravity
反义词：gravity
词性：名词(plural)
