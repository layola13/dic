aeroyacht:/ˈɛəroʊjɑːt/ | n. | 空中游艇，豪华飞艇 | A large, luxurious airship or dirigible, often privately owned, designed for comfortable long-distance travel.  | Example: The billionaire's aeroyacht offered breathtaking views of the Alps.  亿万富翁的空中游艇提供了阿尔卑斯山脉的壮丽景色。

| Example:  Aeroyachts are becoming increasingly popular amongst the ultra-wealthy. 空中游艇在超级富豪中越来越受欢迎。| 近义词: airship, dirigible, blimp | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "aero-" (from Greek "aēr," meaning "air") + "yacht" (from Dutch "jacht," a light, fast sailing vessel).
