assumings:/əˈsuːmɪŋz/ | n. | 假设，臆断 |  Something taken for granted or supposed to be true; an assumption.  It is the plural form of "assuming" which, when used as a noun, represents the act of assuming. |  Her assumings about his wealth proved to be false. 她的关于他财富的假设被证明是错误的。 | 近义词: assumptions, presumptions, suppositions | 反义词: facts, certainties | noun

词源: From the verb "assume" which comes from Late Latin "assumere"  (to take to oneself, adopt, usurp), from "ad-" (to) + "sumere" (to take).
