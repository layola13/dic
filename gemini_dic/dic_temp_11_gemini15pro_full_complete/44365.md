cross-chex:/ˈkrɔːs tʃɛks/ | n. |交叉检查，反复核对| A method of verifying information by checking it against multiple sources or using different methods. | Example: We performed cross-chex on the data to ensure its accuracy. 我们对数据进行了交叉检查，以确保其准确性。| Example:  Cross-chex is important in accounting to prevent errors. 交叉检查在会计中很重要，可以防止错误。|Synonym: double-check, verify, confirm | Antonym: overlook, neglect | Noun
