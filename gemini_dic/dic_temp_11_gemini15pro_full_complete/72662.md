foaled:/foʊld/
verb
产驹；生小马
gave birth to a foal (of a horse)
The mare foaled last night. 母马昨晚产驹了。
近义词:  delivered, birthed (for animals in general)
反义词: None (The opposite action would be related to conception, not birth.)
词性: verb
