mattedness:/ˈmætɪdnəs/
n.
缠结；纠结；杂乱
The state of being matted, tangled, or clumped together.
The mattedness of the dog's fur showed it hadn't been groomed in weeks.  狗毛的杂乱表明它已经好几个星期没有梳理过了。
tangledness, clumpiness, unkemptness
neatness, orderliness
noun
