downy:/ˈdaʊni/| adj.| 绒毛的，柔软的|Covered with soft, fine hair or feathers; like down. |例句：The baby bird has downy feathers. 小鸟长着绒毛。

例句：The blanket is soft and downy.  毯子柔软蓬松。|近义词：fluffy, soft, feathery|反义词:coarse, rough|词性:adjective

downy:/ˈdaʊni/| n.|绒毛,鸭绒|Soft, fine feathers or hair. |例句：The downy from the duck is used to fill pillows. 鸭绒用来填充枕头。

例句：The chick was covered in soft, white downy. 小鸡身上覆盖着柔软的白色绒毛。|近义词：fluff, down|反义词: - |词性:noun
