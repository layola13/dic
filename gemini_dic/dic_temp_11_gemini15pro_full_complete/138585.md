nub:/nʌb/| n.|要点，核心，关键；小块，瘤|a small lump or protuberance; the central point or heart of a matter; a small piece|The nub of the problem is lack of funding. 问题的关键是缺乏资金。|Synonyms: gist, core, kernel, heart, crux, point, essence|Antonyms: periphery, edge, exterior|noun

nub:/nʌb/| v.|去除…的突出部分；击中|to remove the nub of something; to strike|He nubbed the cigar before lighting it. 他在点燃雪茄之前先剪掉了一小块。|Synonyms: trim, clip, pare|Antonyms: extend, lengthen|verb
