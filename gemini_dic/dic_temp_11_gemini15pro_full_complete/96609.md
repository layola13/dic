indue:/ɪnˈdjuː/ /ɪnˈduː/| vt.|赋予，授予，使充满|To provide with something; endow, especially with a quality or power.|The king was indued with wisdom and courage. 国王被赋予了智慧和勇气。|Synonyms: invest, endow, imbue, furnish, provide|Antonyms: deprive, divest, strip|Verb

indue:/ɪnˈdjuː/ /ɪnˈduː/| vt.|穿上，披上(尤指正式服装)|To put on (especially ceremonial robes).|The priest indued his vestments before the service.  神父在仪式前穿上了法衣。|Synonyms: don, assume, put on|Antonyms: doff, remove, take off|Verb
