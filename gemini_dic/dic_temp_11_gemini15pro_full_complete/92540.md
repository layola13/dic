hyracoid: /ˈhaɪrəkɔɪd/ | n. adj. |蹄兔；蹄兔类的|An animal of the order Hyracoidea, which comprises the hyraxes.  These small, thickset mammals are herbivorous and resemble rodents but are more closely related to elephants and manatees.|Hyraxes are the closest living relatives to elephants. 蹄兔是大象现存关系最近的亲戚。| The hyracoid skull shows similarities to that of elephants. 蹄兔类的头骨显示出与大象头骨的相似之处。 |近义词:  dassie, rock rabbit |反义词:  N/A |词性: noun, adjective

hyracoid: /ˌhaɪrəˈkɔɪdiən/ | adj. |蹄兔类的|Relating to or belonging to the order Hyracoidea.|The hyracoidian digestive system is specialized for processing tough plant matter. 蹄兔类的消化系统专门用于处理坚韧的植物。|Hyracoidian species are found in Africa and the Middle East.  蹄兔类物种分布在非洲和中东。|近义词: N/A |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective
