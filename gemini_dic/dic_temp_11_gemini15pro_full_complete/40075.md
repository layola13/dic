contagious:/kənˈteɪdʒəs/| adj.|传染的，有感染力的| Spreading by contact, capable of being transmitted from one person to another.|  The flu is highly contagious. 流感传染性很强。|  infectious, communicable, catching | non-contagious, resistant | adjective

contagious:/kənˈteɪdʒəs/| adj.|蔓延的，有感染力的（指情绪、想法等）| Spreading quickly from one person to another.|  His enthusiasm was contagious. 他的热情很有感染力。| infectious, spreading, catching |  uninspiring, demotivating | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contagiosus, from Latin contingere "to touch," from com- "together" + tangere "to touch."
