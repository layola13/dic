antisag:/ˈæntiˌsæɡ/| adj. n.|防下垂的，抗下垂|Preventing or resisting sagging.  It describes something designed to counteract the effects of gravity, preventing drooping or sagging.  This term is often used in engineering and manufacturing contexts.|The mattress features antisag technology for long-lasting support.  这张床垫采用抗下垂技术，提供持久支撑。|The bridge's antisag cables ensure its structural integrity. 这座桥的抗下垂缆索确保了其结构完整性。|近义词：supportive, firm, reinforced|反义词:sagging, drooping, weak|词性:adjective, noun
