conine:/ˈkoʊnaɪn/ /ˈkɒnaɪn/ | n. | 毒芹碱 | A poisonous alkaloid, C8H17N, extracted from the hemlock plant (Conium maculatum), a colorless, oily liquid with a mousy odor. It is a neurotoxin that causes paralysis. |  Socrates was executed by drinking a potion containing conine. 苏格拉底被赐饮含有毒芹碱的毒药而死。| Conium maculatum contains the alkaloid conine, a neurotoxin that causes respiratory paralysis.  毒芹含有毒芹碱，一种会导致呼吸麻痹的神经毒素。 |  nicotine, poison hemlock alkaloid | antidote | noun
