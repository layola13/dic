contrafagotto:/ˌkɒntrəfəˈɡɒtəʊ/ (UK), /ˌkɑːntrəfəˈɡɑːtoʊ/ (US)| n. |低音巴松管，倍低音巴松管|A double bassoon pitched an octave lower than the normal bassoon.|The contrafagotto added a deep, resonant tone to the orchestra's performance. 低音巴松管为乐队的演奏增添了深沉而洪亮的音色。|Synonym: double bassoon, sub-contrabassoon|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps piccolo as a contrasting high-pitched instrument)|Noun
