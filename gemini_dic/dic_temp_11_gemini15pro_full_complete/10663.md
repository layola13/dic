approved:/əˈpruːvd/ | v. | 批准，认可 | To officially agree to or accept something. | The board approved the new project. 董事会批准了这个新项目。 | synonyms:  authorize, sanction, ratify, endorse | antonyms: disapprove, reject, deny | verb

officially approved: 正式批准  The drug was officially approved by the FDA.  该药物已获得 FDA 正式批准。
approved for: 批准用于  The medication is approved for the treatment of headaches. 该药物被批准用于治疗头痛。
