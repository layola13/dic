clambering:/ˈklæmbərɪŋ/
verb
攀登，爬
climbing awkwardly, using both hands and feet
例句：The children were clambering over the rocks. 孩子们在攀爬岩石。

例句：They clambered up the steep hill. 他们攀登上了陡峭的山坡。

近义词：climbing, scrambling, scaling
反义词：descending, going down
词性：verb, present participle of clamber

clamber:/ˈklæmbər/
verb
攀登，爬
to climb with difficulty, using your hands and feet
例句：We clambered over the rocks to reach the beach. 我们翻过岩石到达了海滩。

例句：The hiker clambered up the mountainside.  徒步旅行者攀登上了山坡。

近义词：climb, scramble, scale
反义词：descend, go down
词性：verb
