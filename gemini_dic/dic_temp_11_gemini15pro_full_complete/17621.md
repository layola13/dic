bebleed: /biːˈbliːd/
verb
(使)过度出血；(使)大量流血
to lose a large amount of blood; to cause someone or something to lose a large amount of blood
The soldier was bebled to death on the battlefield.  这个士兵在战场上流血过多而死。
The doctor bebled the patient to reduce the swelling. 医生给病人放血以消肿。
近义词: bleed profusely, hemorrhage
反义词: clot, coagulate
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + bleed.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "completely."  "Bleed" comes from Old English *blēdan.
