doubleclicked:/ˈdʌbəlklɪkt/
verb
双击
Clicked a mouse button twice in rapid succession.
I doubleclicked the icon to open the application. 我双击图标打开了应用程序。
He doubleclicked the file to launch the program. 他双击文件启动了程序。
近义词:double-click
反义词:single click
词性:v.
