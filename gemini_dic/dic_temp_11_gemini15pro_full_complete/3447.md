afrit:/ˈæfrɪt/| n.|恶魔，妖怪|A powerful evil jinn or demon in Arabian and Muslim mythology.  An afrit is often depicted as a fiery, winged creature capable of great destruction.|The storyteller described a terrifying afrit emerging from the lamp.|Synonyms: demon, jinn, genie, evil spirit|Antonyms: angel, deity|noun
