arias:/ˈɑːriəz/ /ˈeəriəz/
noun
咏叹调；独唱曲
A long accompanied song for a solo voice, typically one in an opera or oratorio.
The soprano’s aria was the highlight of the opera.  女高音的咏叹调是歌剧的亮点。
近义词：solo, song, air
反义词: chorus, ensemble
词性:n.
