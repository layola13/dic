irresistibleness:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbəlnəs/
n.
不可抗拒性；无法抵挡的魅力
The quality of being so attractive or compelling that one cannot resist.
Her irresistibleness captivated everyone in the room. 她的魅力征服了房间里的每个人。
The irresistibleness of the chocolate cake made me break my diet. 巧克力蛋糕的诱惑让我放弃了节食。
Synonyms: allure, appeal, charm, magnetism, fascination
Antonyms: repulsiveness, unattractiveness, unpleasantness
Noun


irresistible:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbl/
adj.
不可抗拒的；无法抵挡的
Too attractive or compelling to be resisted.
He had an irresistible urge to laugh. 他有一种无法抗拒的笑的冲动。
She found his offer irresistible. 她觉得他的提议无法拒绝。
Synonyms: tempting, alluring, enticing, captivating, compelling
Antonyms: resistible, unattractive, unappealing, repellent
Adjective


resist:/rɪˈzɪst/
v.
抵抗；抵制；反抗
Withstand the action or effect of.
He couldn't resist the temptation to eat the cake. 他无法抵挡吃蛋糕的诱惑。
They resisted the government's new policies. 他们抵制政府的新政策。
Synonyms: oppose, withstand, defy, combat, fight
Antonyms: surrender, yield, submit, succumb
Verb



ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + resistere (to resist) + -ible (capable of being) + -ness (state of being).  Ultimately from Latin.
