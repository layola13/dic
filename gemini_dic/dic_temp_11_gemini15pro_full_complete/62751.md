engorged:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒd/
adjective
充血的，肿胀的
(of a part of the body) swollen with blood or other fluid.
The tick was engorged with blood after feeding on the dog.  这只蜱虫吸了狗的血后肿胀起来。
Her breasts felt engorged and tender. 她的乳房感觉肿胀和 tenderness。
近义词: swollen, distended, inflated, congested
反义词: shrunken, deflated, empty


词性: adjective

词源: From French engorger, from en- ‘in’ + gorge ‘throat.’
