beavering:/ˈbiːvərɪŋ/| adj. | 忙碌的，勤奋的 |  Engaged in activity; working hard, like a beaver. |  She was beavering away at her desk all day. 她整天都在办公桌前忙个不停。 |  He's been beavering away at that project for weeks. 他几个星期以来一直在忙那个项目。|  industrious, diligent, busy, active |  lazy, idle | adjective

词源:
From the noun "beaver" + "-ing," referring to the industrious nature of beavers. 源自名词“beaver”+“-ing”，指的是海狸的勤劳天性。
