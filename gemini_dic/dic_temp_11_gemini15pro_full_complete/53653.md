dischargers:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒərz/
| n. | 排放者；卸货者；放电器 | People or things that discharge something (e.g., pollutants, cargo, electricity). | The factory was identified as one of the main dischargers of pollutants into the river.  这家工厂被确定为河流主要污染物排放者之一。| dischargers are responsible for proper waste disposal. 排放者有责任妥善处理废物。 | synonyms: emitters, releasers, exporters | antonyms: absorbers, retainers, importers | noun

| v. | 排放；卸货；放电；解雇；履行义务  | To release something (e.g., pollutants, cargo, electricity); to allow someone to leave (e.g., a hospital, the military, a job); to perform a duty or obligation. | The company discharges waste into the river.  这家公司向河里排放废物。 | The hospital discharged the patient yesterday. 医院昨天让病人出院了。 | synonyms: release, emit, expel, dismiss, fire | antonyms: absorb, retain, admit, hire | verb
