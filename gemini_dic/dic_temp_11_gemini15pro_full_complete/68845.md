fashionable:/ˈfæʃnəbl/ (US) /ˈfæʃənəbl/ (UK)| adj.|流行的；时髦的|Characteristic of, or conforming to, the current fashion. |Her dress is very fashionable. 她的裙子很时髦。|The fashionable shops are all on this street. 这些时髦的商店都在这条街上。|近义词：stylish, trendy, chic, in vogue|反义词：unfashionable, outdated, old-fashioned|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old French *facionable*, from facion (“fashion, shape, form, appearance, manner, conduct”), from Latin factiō (“a making, a doing, a creating, a performing; a deed, act, achievement; a way of acting, manner of doing; a faction, party, company”), from faciō (“I make, do”).
