copyright: /ˈkɑːpiˌraɪt/ /ˈkɒpiˌraɪt/| n. |版权，著作权|The exclusive legal right, given to an originator or an assignee to print, publish, perform, film, or record literary, artistic, or musical material, and to authorize others to do the same.|The book is protected by copyright. 这本书受版权保护。| synonym: intellectual property, author's right | antonym: plagiarism, infringement | noun

copyright: /ˈkɑːpiˌraɪt/ /ˈkɒpiˌraɪt/| vt. | 保护版权，获得版权|To secure a copyright on a work.|He copyrighted his new song. 他为他的新歌注册了版权。| synonym: register, protect | antonym: pirate, steal | verb
