fraternizes:/ˈfrætərnaɪzɪz/
verb
亲善; 友好交往; 交好; (尤指与敌方或地位较低者)称兄道弟
To associate or form a friendship with someone, especially when one is of higher status or rank, or with an enemy.
The soldiers were warned not to fraternize with the locals.  士兵们被警告不要与当地人 fraternize。
He was accused of fraternizing with the enemy. 他被指控与敌人 fraternize。
synonyms: associate, socialize, mingle, consort
antonyms: shun, avoid, ostracize
词性: 动词(verb)
