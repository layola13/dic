innardses:/ˈɪnərdzɪz/ | n. | 内脏，内部器官 |  The internal organs, especially the intestines, of a person or animal.  Refers to the contents within something, often used informally or humorously.  This word is considered a non-standard plural form of "innards." "Innards" itself is already plural. |  He examined the innardses of the clock. 他检查了钟表的内部结构。(This usage, while attested, is considered non-standard. Using "innards" would be grammatically preferable.) |  insides, guts, viscera | exterior, outside | noun
