dormant:/ˈdɔːrmənt/ (US) /ˈdɔːmənt/ (UK)| adj.|休眠的，静止的，潜伏的|In a state of rest or inactivity; temporarily inactive, as if asleep.  Not in use but capable of becoming active.|例句：The volcano has been dormant for centuries. 这座火山已经休眠了几个世纪了。|例句：The seeds remain dormant in the ground until spring. 种子在地下休眠直到春天。|近义词：inactive, latent, quiescent, sleeping|反义词：active, awake, lively|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *dormant (Modern French dormant), present participle of dormir "to sleep," from Latin dormire "to sleep."
