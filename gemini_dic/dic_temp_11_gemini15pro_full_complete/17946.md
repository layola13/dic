befallen:/bɪˈfɔːlən/
verb
降临于；发生于
(especially of something bad) happen to someone.
A great misfortune has befallen him.  他不幸遭遇了一场大灾难。
近义词: happen, occur, take place
反义词: averted, prevented
词性: verb
