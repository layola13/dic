fructoses:/ˈfrʌktoʊsiz/ /ˈfrʌktoʊsiːz/| n. |果糖（复数）|A simple sugar found in honey and fruit.  A monosaccharide with the same chemical formula as glucose but with a different structure.|Fructoses are found in many fruits.  许多水果中都含有果糖。|Synonyms:  fruit sugar, levulose|Antonyms: None|Noun
