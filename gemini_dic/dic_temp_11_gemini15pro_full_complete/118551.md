mesonotum:/ˈmiːzəʊˌnəʊtəm/, /ˈmɛsəˌnoʊtəm/ | n. |中胸背板|The dorsal (upper) part of the middle segment of the thorax of an insect.|The mesonotum of this beetle is particularly shiny.  这只甲虫的中胸背板特别有光泽。|Synonym: scutum (in some contexts)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, potentially the ventral part - metasternum) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μέσος (mésos, “middle”) + νῶτον (nôton, “back”).
