extollments:/ɪkˈstəʊlmənts/ | n. | 赞扬，称赞 |  Acts of praising something or someone highly.  It refers to the expressions of praise themselves, rather than the act of praising. | The king received numerous extollments on his bravery after the battle. 国王在战后因其英勇获得了无数的赞扬。| Near Synonyms: praises, accolades, encomiums, laudations | Near Antonyms: criticisms, condemnations, denunciations | noun
