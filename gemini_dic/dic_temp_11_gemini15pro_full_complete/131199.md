nonbureaucratic: /ˌnɒnˌbjʊərəˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 非官僚主义的 | Not characterized by excessive reliance on procedures, paperwork, and formal rules; not bureaucratic.  Characterized by flexibility and efficiency. |  The company has a nonbureaucratic structure, allowing for quick decision-making.  这家公司拥有非官僚主义的结构，可以快速决策。 | Synonyms: informal, flexible, streamlined, efficient, uncomplicated | Antonyms: bureaucratic, formal, rigid, inflexible, complex | adjective
