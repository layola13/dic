overpromotes: /ˌoʊvərprəˈmoʊts/
verb
过度晋升，提拔过快
To promote (someone) to a position for which they are not qualified or experienced enough.
The company overpromoted him to a management position, and he struggled to handle the responsibilities.  这家公司过度提拔他到管理职位，他难以胜任。
He was overpromoted and quickly found himself in over his head. 他被提拔过快，很快就发现自己力不从心。
近义词: overadvance, elevate prematurely
反义词: demote, downgrade
词性: verb


overpromote: /ˌoʊvərprəˈmoʊt/
noun (rarely used)
过度晋升
The act of promoting someone excessively or beyond their capabilities.
His rapid rise through the ranks was seen as an overpromote by many of his colleagues. 他的迅速晋升被许多同事视为过度提拔。
Overpromote is a common issue in rapidly expanding companies. 在快速扩张的公司里，过度晋升是一个常见问题。
近义词: overadvancement, premature elevation
反义词: demotion, downgrade
词性: noun


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "promote" (verb meaning "to raise to a higher position")
