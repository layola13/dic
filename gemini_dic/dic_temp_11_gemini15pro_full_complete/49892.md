demurrable:/dɪˈmɜːrəbl/
adjective
可异议的，可反对的
Open to objection; that may be demurred to.  Specifically relating to a pleading in law, signifying that it is defective in substance and open to attack.
例句：The lawyer argued that the plaintiff's claim was demurrable due to insufficient evidence.  律师辩称，原告的主张由于证据不足而可以提出异议。

例句：The contract contained a demurrable clause that allowed one party to unilaterally terminate the agreement.  合同中包含一项可反对的条款，允许一方单方面终止协议。
近义词：objectionable, disputable, challengeable
反义词：unobjectionable, indisputable, unchallengeable
词性: adjective
词源: From the verb "demur" (from Old French *demorer, from Latin demorari "to linger, delay"), + "-able" (suffix indicating capability or worthiness).
