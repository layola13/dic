hyperlogicality:/ˌhaɪpərˌlɒdʒɪˈkælɪti/ | n. | 过度逻辑性，极端逻辑性 | The state or quality of being excessively logical, often to the point of impracticality or ignoring emotional considerations. | His hyperlogicality prevented him from understanding the nuances of human relationships.  他的过度逻辑性使他无法理解人际关系的细微之处。 | 近义词: over-rationality, excessive logic | 反义词: irrationality, emotionalism | 词性: noun

词源:  From hyper- (meaning "excessive, beyond") + logicality (from logical + -ity).
