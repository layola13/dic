chronograph:/ˈkrɒnəɡrɑːf/
n.|计时码表，计时器|A watch or clock with a built-in stopwatch function.|He used his chronograph to time his laps around the track. 他用他的计时码表来记录他在跑道上的单圈时间。|近义词:stopwatch, timer|反义词:|名词
