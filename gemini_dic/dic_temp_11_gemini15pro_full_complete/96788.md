ineradicably:/ˌɪnɪˈrædɪkəbli/| adv.|根深蒂固地，无法根除地|Not capable of being eradicated; permanently.  It describes something so deeply ingrained or established that it cannot be removed or destroyed.|His name is ineradicably etched in the history books. 他的名字已深深地铭刻在史册上。|The experience left an ineradicably sad memory. 那次经历留下了永远无法抹去的悲伤记忆。|近义词:permanently, indelibly, ingrained|反义词:temporarily, superficially, erasable|词性:副词
