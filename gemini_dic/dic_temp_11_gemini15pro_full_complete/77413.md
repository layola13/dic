gaveling:/ˈɡævəlɪŋ/ | n. | 敲槌; 敲槌声; (会议主席用槌)宣布结束 |The gaveling signaled the end of the session. | 例句：The judge’s gaveling brought the courtroom to order. 法官敲响法槌，使法庭恢复秩序。

例句：The constant gaveling from the construction site next door was incredibly disruptive. 隔壁建筑工地不断的敲击声让人烦躁不安。 | 近义词：hammering, banging, rapping | 反义词: silence, quiet | 词性: 名词

词源:  Derived from the noun "gavel," which refers to a small mallet used by a judge or presiding officer. "Gavel" ultimately comes from the Old Norse word "gafl," meaning "gable."
