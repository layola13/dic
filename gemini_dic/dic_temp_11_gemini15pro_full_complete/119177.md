mezuzoth:/məˈzuːzɒθ/ /məˈzuːzoʊθ/
|n.|门柱圣卷盒（复数）|A parchment inscribed with religious texts and attached in a case to the doorpost of a Jewish house as a sign of faith.|The mezuzoth on the doorposts reminded them of their covenant with God.|近义词:phylacteries (in a broader sense, as both are reminders of religious observance)|反义词:None (lack of a mezuzah might symbolize a lack of faith, but there isn't a single direct antonym)|noun
