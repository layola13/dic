outboxes:/ˈaʊtbɒksɪz/
n.
发件箱；已发送邮件文件夹
A folder on a computer or other device that stores copies of email messages that have been sent.
I checked my outboxes to make sure the email had been sent successfully. 我检查了我的发件箱，以确保邮件已成功发送。
He regularly clears his outboxes to free up space. 他定期清理发件箱以释放空间。
Synonyms: sent folder, sent items, sent mail
Antonyms: inbox, received mail
noun
