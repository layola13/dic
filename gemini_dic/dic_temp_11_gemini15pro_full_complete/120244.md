minglers:/ˈmɪŋɡlər/| n. |交际者，善于交际的人|Someone who mingles, especially at a social event. |She's a real mingler; she can talk to anyone.| 近义词:socializer, mixer, networker | 反义词:loner, introvert | 词性:noun

Etymology: From the verb "mingle" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Mingle" comes from Old English "mengen" (to mix).
