boasts:/boʊsts/ (US) /bəʊsts/ (UK)| v. n.| 拥有；吹嘘；自夸 | To possess something to be proud of; to speak with excessive pride and self-satisfaction about one's achievements, possessions, or abilities. | The city boasts a beautiful harbor. 这座城市拥有一个美丽的港口。| synonyms: brags, flaunts, prides itself on, possesses | antonyms: downplays, minimizes, belittles, hides | verb, noun


boast:/boʊsts/ (US) /bəʊsts/ (UK)| n.| 吹嘘；夸口；自夸的话 | An act of boasting; a proud statement, often exaggerated. | His boast about winning the lottery turned out to be true. 他吹嘘自己中了彩票，结果是真的。| synonyms: brag, vaunt, pretension | antonyms: modesty, humility, understatement | noun


Etymology: From Middle English *bosten*, from Old French *boster*, perhaps of Germanic origin.
