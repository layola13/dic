fixated:/ˈfɪkseɪtɪd/| adj.|固执的，着迷的，念念不忘的|Obsessively focused on something or someone.|He became fixated on the idea of climbing Mount Everest. 他一心想着要攀登珠穆朗玛峰。|近义词：obsessed, preoccupied, captivated|反义词: indifferent, uninterested|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "fixate," which comes from the Latin "fixus," the past participle of "figere," meaning "to fix" or "to fasten."
