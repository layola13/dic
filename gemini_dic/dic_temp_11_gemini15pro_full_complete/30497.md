centumvir: /sɛntʌmˈvɜː(r)/ | n. |百人法庭成员；百人委员会成员 | A member of a body of one hundred men in ancient Rome, especially one of a legal body. |  The centumvirs were responsible for judging civil cases. 百人法庭负责审理民事案件。|  The case was brought before the centumvirs for judgment.  该案件被提交给百人法庭进行审判。 | Synonyms: juror, judge, magistrate | Antonyms: defendant, plaintiff | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *centumvir*, from *centum* ("hundred") + *vir* ("man").
