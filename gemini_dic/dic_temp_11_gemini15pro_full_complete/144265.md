outsonneted:/ˌaʊtˈsɑːnɪtɪd/
verb
胜过；在十四行诗创作方面超过
surpassed in sonnet writing; outdone in a contest of sonnets.
Shakespeare outsonneted all his contemporaries.  莎士比亚的十四行诗超过了他所有的同时期的诗人。
outshine, surpass, excel, outdo
be inferior to, fall short of
词性：动词
