chorusing: /ˈkɔːrəsɪŋ/ | n. | 合唱；齐声；齐鸣 | The act of singing or speaking in chorus; simultaneous utterance by a number of people or animals. | The chorusing of the frogs filled the night air. 青蛙的合唱声充满了夜空。 |  近义词:  singing, chanting, harmonizing | 反义词: soloing, silence | noun

chorusing: /ˈkɔːrəsɪŋ/ | adj. | 合唱的；齐声的 |  Singing or speaking in chorus.  |  The chorusing voices of the children were beautiful. 孩子们齐声的歌声很美妙。 |  近义词:  unison, concerted, harmonious | 反义词: solo, individual | adjective
