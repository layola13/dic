conventionalism:/kənˈvɛnʃənəlɪzəm/
|n.|因循主义，传统主义，约定俗成|The adherence to conventional beliefs or practices.  A view that ethical principles are simply the reflection of social conventions.|Her art challenges conventionalism and explores new forms of expression. 她的艺术挑战传统主义，探索新的表达形式。|近义词:conformity, traditionalism, orthodoxy|反义词:unconventionality, originality, innovation|词性:名词

Etymology:
Late 19th century: from conventional + -ism.
