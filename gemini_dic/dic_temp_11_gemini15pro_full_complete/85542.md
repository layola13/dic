hatchery:/ˈhætʃəri/ | n. | 孵化场；孵化器 |A place where eggs are hatched, especially fish or poultry.  A place for breeding and raising young animals, especially fish. |例句：The salmon hatchery releases thousands of young fish into the river each year.  每年鲑鱼孵化场都会向河里放生数千条幼鱼。

例句：They raise chickens at a large hatchery.  他们在一家大型孵化场养鸡。|近义词：nursery, breeding ground |反义词: None | 词性: noun
