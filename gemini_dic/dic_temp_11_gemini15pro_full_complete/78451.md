geth:/ɡɛθ/ | n. | 以太坊客户端 | A command-line interface for running a full Ethereum node implemented in Go.  It is the most common way to interact with the Ethereum network. | Example:  I used geth to deploy my smart contract to the Ethereum blockchain. 我使用geth将我的智能合约部署到以太坊区块链。 | Example: Syncing the blockchain with geth can take a long time. 使用geth同步区块链可能需要很长时间。 | 近义词: Ethereum client, node software | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
