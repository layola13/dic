make-an-end-of:/meɪk ən ɛnd əv/ | phrasal verb |结束，终止，消灭|To finish something completely, often in a decisive or destructive way.  It implies a deliberate and final action. |例句：The king vowed to make an end of the rebellion. 国王发誓要结束叛乱。

例句：They decided to make an end of their partnership. 他们决定结束合作关系。|近义词：finish, terminate, abolish, destroy, eliminate, exterminate|反义词:begin, start, commence, initiate, establish|词性:动词短语
