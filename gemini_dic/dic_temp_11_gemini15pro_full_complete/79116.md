ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gladen, from Old Norse *glǫðna (“to glow, to gleam”), from Proto-Germanic *glōaną (“to glow”). Related to Old English glæd (“bright, shining”).  The sense of "pleased" likely developed from the association of brightness with happiness.
