anacardiaceous:/ˌænəˌkɑːrdiˈeɪʃəs/ | adj. |漆树科的；漆树科植物的|Belonging to or relating to the Anacardiaceae family (cashew family), which includes trees, shrubs, and woody vines known for producing resins, oils, and sometimes edible fruits.|The mango and cashew are anacardiaceous fruits. 芒果和腰果都是漆树科的水果。|Synonyms: cashew family, Anacardiaceae|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

anacardiaceous:/ˌænəˌkɑːrdiˈeɪʃəs/ | n. |漆树科植物|A plant belonging to the Anacardiaceae family.|Poison ivy is a common anacardiaceous plant. 毒葛是一种常见的漆树科植物。|Synonyms: cashew family plant, Anacardiaceae plant|Antonyms: N/A|noun
