canceration: /kænsəˈreɪʃən/
n.
癌变；溃疡；恶化
The process of becoming cancerous; the formation of a cancer.  Also, a cancerous growth or ulcer.
The doctor observed the canceration of the tissue. 医生观察到组织的癌变。
Synonyms: carcinogenesis, malignancy, cancerous growth
Antonyms: remission, healing, recovery
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *canceratio, from Latin cancer (“crab, cancer”) + -atio.
