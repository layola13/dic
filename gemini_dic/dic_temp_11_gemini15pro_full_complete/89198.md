homeshoring:/ˈhoʊmʃɔːrɪŋ/ /ˈhəʊmʃɔːrɪŋ/ | n. | 家庭办公；在家工作 | The practice of hiring people to work from their homes, often using the internet and other communication technologies. | Many companies have adopted homeshoring to reduce office costs and offer employees greater flexibility.  许多公司已经采用家庭办公来降低办公成本，并为员工提供更大的灵活性。| 近义词: telecommuting, remote work | 反义词: office work, onsite work | 词性: noun

词源: "home" + "shoring" (analogous to offshoring), coined in the early 2000s.
