malcontent:/ˌmælˈkənˈtent/ | n. | 不满分子 | A person who is dissatisfied and rebellious. | The group of malcontents plotted to overthrow the government.  这群不满分子密谋推翻政府。 | Synonyms: dissident, rebel, insurgent | Antonyms: conformist, loyalist | noun
