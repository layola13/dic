naughtier:/ˈnɔːtiər/ | adj. | 更淘气的，更顽皮的 |Comparative form of naughty: more naughty.  Used to describe someone who behaves badly or disobediently, to a greater degree than another. |例句：My younger brother is naughtier than me. 我的弟弟比我更淘气。|例句：The puppy was naughtier today, chewing on everything in sight.  这只小狗今天更淘气了，把所有看到的东西都咬了一遍。|近义词：mischievous, disobedient, unruly, worse |反义词：well-behaved, obedient, good |词性:形容词


词源: From Old English *nāwiht (meaning "worthless," "wicked")
