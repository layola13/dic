blastoidea:/blæˈstɔɪdiə/
n.
海蕾纲，海蕾类
An extinct class of stemmed echinoderms related to the crinoids and cystoids, common in the Paleozoic era. They had a bud-shaped body enclosed in plates.
Blastoids were abundant in the Mississippian period.  海蕾类在密西西比纪时期数量众多。
