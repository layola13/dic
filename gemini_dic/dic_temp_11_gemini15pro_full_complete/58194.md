droyle:/drɔɪl/| n.| 权利，特权；附属物 |A right, privilege, or appurtenance.  Specifically, a feudal right or due. |The king granted him the droyle to collect taxes in the province. 国王授予他在该省征税的特权。|近义词：right, privilege, prerogative, appurtenance |反义词:duty, obligation |词性:noun

Etymology: From Old French *droil, *dreit, from Latin *directum, neuter past participle of dirigere "to direct, guide, put straight," from dis- "apart" + regere "to direct, guide, rule."  The same Latin root gives us the English words "direct" and "regent."
