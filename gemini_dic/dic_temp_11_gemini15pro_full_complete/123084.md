moneywort:/ˈmʌniwɜːrt/ |n.|金钱草，过路黄|A creeping plant with rounded, fleshy leaves and small yellow flowers, often used as ground cover. It's also known as creeping Jenny or Lysimachia nummularia.|例句：The moneywort cascaded down the rocks, creating a beautiful waterfall of green. 金钱草像瀑布般从岩石上倾泻而下，形成一道美丽的绿色瀑布。

例句：Moneywort is a popular choice for hanging baskets due to its trailing habit. 由于其蔓生的习性，金钱草是吊篮的热门选择。|近义词：creeping Jenny, creeping Charlie, Lysimachia nummularia|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
