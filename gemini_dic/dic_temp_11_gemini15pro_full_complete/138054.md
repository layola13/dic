norie:/ˈnɔːri/| n.|海苔, 紫菜|A type of edible seaweed, often used in Japanese cuisine, particularly for making sushi rolls.|例句：The sushi chef skillfully wrapped the rice and fish in a sheet of norie. 寿司师傅熟练地用一张海苔包住了米饭和鱼。

例句：Norie is a good source of iodine and other nutrients. 海苔是碘和其他营养物质的良好来源。|近义词：seaweed, laver|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a type of seaweed)|词性:noun
