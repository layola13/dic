lionhood:/ˈlaɪənˌhʊd/
noun
雄狮的身份; 雄狮的特性
The state or quality of being a lion; lion-like nature or character.
He carried himself with a certain lionhood, a majestic air of authority.  他带着某种雄狮般的气质，一种威严的权威感。
The film explored the themes of courage and lionhood.  这部电影探讨了勇气和雄狮特性的主题。
Synonyms: majesty, bravery, courage, valor, boldness, fearlessness
Antonyms: cowardice, timidity, fearfulness
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From lion + -hood (suffix denoting state, condition, or quality).  Lion comes from Old French "lion" and ultimately from Latin "leo".  "-hood" is a native English suffix.
