cursiveness:/ˈkɝːsɪvnəs/| n.|草书，草写，流畅|The cursiveness of her handwriting made it difficult to read. 她的笔迹过于草，很难辨认。|例句：He admired the cursiveness of the signature. 他很欣赏签名流畅的草体。

例句：She practiced her cursiveness until her handwriting flowed smoothly. 她练习草书直到她的笔迹流畅为止。|近义词：fluency, smoothness|反义词:clumsiness, stiffness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cursive + -ness.  Cursive comes from Medieval Latin *cursīvus, from Latin cursus (“running, course”), past participle of currere (“to run”).
