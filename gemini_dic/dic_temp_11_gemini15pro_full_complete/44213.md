crochet:/ˈkroʊʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈkrəʊʃeɪ/ (UK)| n. v. |钩针编织；钩针织物；钩针|A method of creating fabric from yarn or thread using a crochet hook.  As a verb, it means to create fabric using this method.  As a noun, it can refer to the method itself, the fabric created, or the hook used.|She crocheted a beautiful blanket for her granddaughter. 她为孙女钩织了一条漂亮的毯子。|近义词:knit, tat|反义词:unravel|词性:noun, verb
