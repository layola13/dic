defaceable:/dɪˈfeɪsəbl/
adjective
可污损的；可毁坏的
Susceptible to being defaced; capable of being marred or disfigured.
The monument was made of a soft stone, making it easily defaceable.  这座纪念碑是用一种软石头做的，很容易被污损。
Vandals targeted the easily defaceable wall with graffiti.  破坏者用涂鸦瞄准了容易被污损的墙壁。
近义词:vulnerable, damageable, marrable
反义词:indestructible, impervious, resistant
词性:adjective
词源:From "deface" (from Old French "desfacer," meaning "to disfigure") + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").
