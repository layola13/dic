jaunt:/dʒɔːnt/ | n. vi. | 短途旅行；远足 | A short pleasure trip; to make a short journey for pleasure. | We took a jaunt to the beach. 我们到海滩去玩了一趟。 | Synonyms: trip, excursion, outing, expedition, journey, tour, trek | Antonyms: stay, remain | noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
