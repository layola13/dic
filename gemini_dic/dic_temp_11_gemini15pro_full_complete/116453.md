matchsticks:/ˈmætʃˌstɪks/ | n. | 火柴棍 | Small sticks of wood or cardboard tipped with a flammable substance that ignites when struck against a rough surface. |  He lit the candle with a matchstick.  他用一根火柴点燃了蜡烛。 | synonyms: matches, spunks (informal) | antonyms:  N/A | noun

matchstick:/ˈmætʃˌstɪk/ | adj. | 火柴棍似的;细长的 | Resembling a matchstick in shape, thin and long. | Her legs were matchstick thin. 她的腿像火柴棍一样细。 | synonyms: spindly, thin, slender | antonyms: thick, stout | adjective
