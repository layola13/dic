bakemeat:/ˈbeɪkmiːt/
n.
烤肉; 糕饼; 食品; 供烘焙的肉
Meat for baking; baked food.  Historically, it referred specifically to meat pies or pastries filled with meat.
例句：The aroma of bakemeat filled the medieval kitchen. 中世纪厨房里充满了烤肉的香味。

例句：She prepared a variety of bakemeats for the feast. 她为宴会准备了各种各样的糕点。
近义词：pastry, baked goods, pie
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "raw ingredients")
词性: noun
