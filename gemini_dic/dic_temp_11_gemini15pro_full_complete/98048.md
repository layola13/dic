inpaint:/ɪnˈpeɪnt/| v. | 修补，修复（图像）| To restore or reconstruct lost or damaged parts of an image or artwork, often using digital tools.  | Example: The software can inpaint the scratches on the old photo.  这个软件可以修复老照片上的划痕。

Example:  He inpainted the missing parts of the fresco. 他修复了壁画缺失的部分。| 近义词: restore, retouch, reconstruct, repair | 反义词: damage, corrupt |词性:动词
