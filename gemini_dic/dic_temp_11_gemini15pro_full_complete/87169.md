herculean:/ˌhɜːrˈkjuːliən/ /ˌhɝːˈkjuːliən/| adj.|艰巨的，费力的|Characterized by great strength or requiring great strength or effort, like the mythical hero Hercules.|例句：Cleaning the Augean stables was a herculean task.  清洗奥革阿斯的牛圈是一项艰巨的任务。

例句：She made a herculean effort to finish the project on time. 她付出了巨大的努力才按时完成了项目。|近义词：arduous, laborious, strenuous, demanding, formidable|反义词：easy, simple, effortless|词性:adjective
