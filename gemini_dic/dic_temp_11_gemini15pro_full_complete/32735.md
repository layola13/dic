chloroleucite:/ˌklɔːroˈluːsaɪt/ | n. | 绿白榴石 | A rare mineral consisting of potassium and lead chloride.  It is a colorless or white isometric mineral.| Chloroleucite was discovered in volcanic fumaroles. 绿白榴石是在火山喷气孔中发现的。| Chloroleucite crystals are usually small and difficult to identify. 绿白榴石晶体通常很小，难以识别。 | Synonym:  None (very specific mineral) | Antonym: N/A | noun
