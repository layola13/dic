archive: /ˈɑːrkaɪv/ (US) /ˈɑːkaɪv/ (UK)| n. v. adj.|档案馆，档案文件；存档，归档|A place where historical documents or records are kept.|例句：The museum's archive contains thousands of photographs.  博物馆的档案馆藏有数千张照片。|近义词：record, annals, chronicle, collection|反义词: current, contemporary|noun

archive: /ˈɑːrkaɪv/ (US) /ˈɑːkaɪv/ (UK)| v. |存档，归档|To store historical documents or records.|例句：The documents were archived for future reference.  这些文件被存档以备将来参考。|近义词：store, file, save|反义词: delete, erase|verb

archive: /ˈɑːrkaɪv/ (US) /ˈɑːkaɪv/ (UK)| adj. |档案的|Relating to or contained in archives.|例句：The archive footage showed the president's first inauguration.  这段档案录像展示了总统的第一次就职典礼。|近义词：historical, recorded, documented|反义词: current, contemporary|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "archif" (noun), "archiver" (verb), from Latin "archivum," from Greek "arkheion" meaning "public building, government house," from "arkhē" meaning "government."
