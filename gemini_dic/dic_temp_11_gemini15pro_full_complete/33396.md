churches:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃɪz/
n.
教堂（church的复数形式）
Buildings used for public Christian worship.
Many churches have beautiful stained-glass windows. 许多教堂都有美丽的彩色玻璃窗。
The bells of the churches rang out across the city. 教堂的钟声响彻整个城市。

近义词:chapels, cathedrals
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与宗教场所相对的词语，例如：secular buildings 世俗建筑)
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cirice (unattested), from Proto-West Germanic *kirika, from Proto-Germanic *kirkō, borrowed from Late Latin *chēricha, a variant of Latin cyriaca, from Ancient Greek κυριακή (kyriakḗ, “of the Lord”), from κύριος (kýrios, “lord, master”). Cognate with West Frisian tsjerke, Dutch kerk, German Kirche, Scots kirk.
