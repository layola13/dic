createuser:/kriˈeɪt ˈjuːzər/ |n.| 创建用户 | The command 'createuser' is used to add new users to a database.  命令 'createuser' 用于向数据库添加新用户。 | 近义词: adduser, newuser | 反义词: deleteuser, removeuser | 词性: noun (often used as a command)

Etymology:  A compound word formed from "create" (from Latin *creāre*, meaning "to produce, make") and "user" (from "use," ultimately from Latin *ūsus*).
