nuncio:/ˈnʌnʃioʊ/ (US), /ˈnʌnsɪəʊ/ (UK)| n. |使节；教廷大使|An ambassador of the pope sent to a foreign government. |The papal nuncio delivered a message from the Vatican. 教廷大使传达了梵蒂冈的讯息。|Synonyms: legate, envoy, ambassador|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "rebel" or "dissident" in a specific context) |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *nuntius "messenger," from Latin nuntius "messenger, announcer, news," related to novus "new."
