monoplane:/ˈmɒnəpleɪn/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəpleɪn/| n. |单翼飞机|An airplane with only one set of wings, in contrast to a biplane or triplane.|The Wright brothers' first successful aircraft was a monoplane.|Near synonyms: aircraft, airplane, plane|Antonyms: biplane, triplane|noun
