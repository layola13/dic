constricting:/kənˈstrɪktɪŋ/
adjective
收缩的，紧缩的，狭窄的
Making something narrower or tighter; limiting or restricting something.
The constricting bandage was cutting off circulation to his fingers.  收缩的绷带切断了他的手指的血液循环。

The constricting rules stifled creativity. 这些限制性的规则扼杀了创造力。

synonym: tightening, squeezing, compressing, limiting, restricting
antonym: expanding, widening, loosening, relaxing, enlarging
词性: adjective
