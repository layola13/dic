natron:/ˈneɪtrɒn/ (US), /ˈneɪtrən/ (UK)| n.|天然碱，泡碱|A naturally occurring mixture of hydrous sodium carbonate, used in ancient times for various purposes, including mummification and glassmaking. |Ancient Egyptians used natron in the mummification process. 古埃及人用泡碱来制作木乃伊。|Synonyms: soda ash, alkali|Antonyms: acid|noun
