boofhead:/ˈbuːfhed/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A derogatory term for a stupid or foolish person.  It implies a lack of intelligence or common sense.|Example 1: "Don't be such a boofhead, of course the Earth isn't flat!"  别这么傻，地球当然不是平的！

Example 2: "He drove his car into a ditch. What a boofhead!" 他把车开进了沟里。真是个笨蛋！|Synonym: idiot, moron, fool, nitwit, dunce|Antonym: genius, intellect, mastermind|noun
