causativities:/ˌkɔːzəˈtɪvɪtiz/
n.
因果关系；因果性；使役性
The study of causativities in linguistics explores how different grammatical constructions express cause and effect.  语言学中对因果关系的研究探索了不同的语法结构如何表达因果关系。
The philosopher delved into the causativities of human behavior. 这位哲学家深入研究了人类行为的因果关系。
Synonyms: causality, causation, causes
Antonyms: effects, consequences (These are not strict antonyms, but represent the opposite end of the cause-and-effect relationship)
noun
