docketed:/ˈdɑːkɪtɪd/| adj.| 已归档的，已登记的 | Officially recorded and filed, typically a legal document. | The case was docketed and scheduled for a hearing next month.  这个案子已登记在案，并安排在下个月举行听证会。| Synonyms: filed, registered, recorded, logged | Antonyms: unfiled, unregistered, pending | adjective

docket:/ˈdɑːkɪt/| n. vt.|  docket (n.) a list of cases to be heard by a court; a summary of a document. docket (vt.) to enter (a case or suit) onto a docket; to make a brief summary of. | docket (n.) The judge reviewed the docket before the court session. 法官在开庭前审阅了案件清单。 docket (vt.) The clerk docketed the new lawsuit. 书记员将新的诉讼登记在案。 | Synonyms: (n.) agenda, calendar, schedule, list; (vt.) register, record, file | Antonyms: (n.) none; (vt.) remove, expunge | noun, verb
