carwashes:/ˈkɑːrˌwɒʃɪz/ /ˈkɑːrˌwɑːʃɪz/
|n.|洗车场；洗车服务|A place or business that washes cars, often automatically.|例句：I took my car to the carwash. 我把我的车开到洗车场去了。

例句：There are several carwashes in town. 城里有好几家洗车场。
|近义词：car wash (singular), auto wash|反义词: (None -  no direct opposite for a place of service)
|词性:名词
