go-fer:/ˈɡoʊfər/| n.|杂工，跑腿的|A person whose job involves running errands, especially fetching things.|例句：He started as a go-fer in the mailroom but quickly worked his way up. 他从邮件室的跑腿小弟开始做起，但很快就升职了。

例句：She was tired of being treated like a go-fer and decided to quit. 她厌倦了被当作跑腿的，决定辞职。|近义词：errand boy/girl, runner, lackey|反义词:boss, manager|词性:noun
