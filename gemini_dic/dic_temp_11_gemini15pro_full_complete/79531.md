globulimeter:/ˌɡlɒbjuˈlɪmɪtər/| n. |血球计，红细胞计数器|An instrument used to measure the number of red blood cells in a given volume of blood.|The doctor used a globulimeter to check the patient's red blood cell count. 医生用血球计检查了病人的红细胞计数。|Synonym: hemocytometer|Antonym: N/A|noun
Etymology-> From Latin *globulus* (small sphere) + -meter (measuring instrument).
