despoilments:/dɪˈspɔɪlmənts/
noun
掠夺，破坏，赃物
The act of despoiling; the state of being despoiled; robbery; pillage.
The museum was filled with the despoilments of ancient tombs.  博物馆里摆满了从古墓中掠夺来的物品。
Synonyms:  pillaging, plundering, looting, ravaging, ransacking
Antonyms:  preservation, restoration, protection
