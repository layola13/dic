linguistic:/lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk/ | adj. |语言的，语言学的| relating to language or linguistics |例句：Linguistic skills are essential for effective communication. 语言技能对于有效沟通至关重要。|例句：The study explored the linguistic development of children. 这项研究探讨了儿童的语言发展。|近义词：grammatical, lexical, phonetic, phonological |反义词:nonlinguistic |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French linguistique, from Latin lingua (“tongue, language”) + -istique (from Greek -istikos).
