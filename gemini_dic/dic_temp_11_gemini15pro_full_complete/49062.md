dehumanize:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪz/ |v. |使丧失人性, 使失去人道; 非人化|To deprive of human qualities, personality, or spirit; to treat as if less than human. |例句：The propaganda campaign sought to dehumanize the enemy.  宣传运动试图把敌人非人化。

例句：War can dehumanize even the most compassionate people. 战争即使是最富有同情心的人也会变得麻木不仁。

|近义词：degrade, demean, brutalize, objectify|反义词：humanize, personalize, dignify|词性：动词
