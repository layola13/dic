lauriferous:/ˌlɔːrɪˈfɜːrəs/
adjective
戴桂冠的；荣誉的；带来荣誉的
Bearing or producing laurels; crowned with laurel.  Also used figuratively to mean honored or worthy of honor.
The lauriferous poet accepted the award with grace.  这位戴桂冠的诗人优雅地接受了奖项。
synonyms: laurelled, crowned, honored, triumphant, glorious
antonyms: disgraced, dishonored, shamed, inglorious
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *laurifer* (laurel-bearing), from *laurus* (laurel) + *-fer* (bearing, producing).  The *-fer* element is related to the verb *ferre* (to bear, carry).
