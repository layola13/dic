gathered: /ˈɡæðərd/ | v. adj. |聚集，收集; 收拢的，有褶皱的 | to bring together into one place or group; to collect.  As an adjective, it describes something that has been pulled together, often creating folds or gathers. |  We gathered around the campfire to sing songs. 我们围着篝火唱歌。 | Synonyms: assembled, collected, amassed, accumulated, pleated, ruffled | Antonyms: dispersed, scattered, spread, unfolded, smooth | verb, adjective


gathered: /ˈɡæðərd/ | adj. |聚集的，收拢的| brought or come together; assembled | The gathered crowd waited for the concert to begin.  聚集的人群等待着音乐会的开始。 | Synonyms: assembled, collected, congregated | Antonyms: dispersed, scattered | adjective
