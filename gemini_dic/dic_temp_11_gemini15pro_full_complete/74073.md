fossilizable:/ˈfɒsɪlaɪzəbl/ /ˈfɑːsəlaɪzəbl/
adjective
可化石的;  易变成化石的
Capable of being fossilized; readily converted into a fossil.
The hard parts of the animal are fossilizable, but the soft tissues are not.  动物的坚硬部分可以变成化石，但软组织不行。
fossilized, petrified
degradable,decayable
词性:形容词
