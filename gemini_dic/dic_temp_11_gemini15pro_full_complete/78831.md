gimmickless:/ˈɡɪmɪklɪs/
adjective
没有噱头的，朴实无华的
Lacking gimmicks; straightforward and without any tricks or devices designed to attract attention.
The new marketing campaign is refreshingly gimmickless, focusing on the product's quality.  这个新的营销活动令人耳目一新，它没有噱头，专注于产品质量。
The restaurant's gimmickless approach to dining – simple food, good service – has made it a local favorite. 这家餐厅朴实无华的用餐方式——简单的食物，优质的服务——使它成为当地人的最爱。
Synonyms: unpretentious, straightforward, simple, honest, authentic, genuine
Antonyms: gimmicky, contrived, flashy, ostentatious
词性: 形容词
