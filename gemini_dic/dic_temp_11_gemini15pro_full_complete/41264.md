cord:/kɔːrd/ (UK) /kɔːrd/ (US)| n. |绳索；线；电线；灯芯绒|A length of thick string or rope composed of several strands twisted together.  A unit of measurement for cut firewood.|
The climbers were roped together by a strong cord.  登山者用一根结实的绳索系在一起。

The lamp had a frayed cord.  这盏灯的电线磨损了。

We ordered a cord of wood for the fireplace. 我们订购了一捆木柴用于壁炉。


|近义词：rope, string, cable, wire, twine|反义词: None|词性:noun|

cord:/kɔːrd/ (UK) /kɔːrd/ (US)| v. |用绳子捆绑|To tie or bind with cord.|
He corded the newspapers for recycling.  他用绳子把报纸捆起来以便回收。
|近义词：tie, bind, fasten|反义词: untie, unbind|词性:verb|
