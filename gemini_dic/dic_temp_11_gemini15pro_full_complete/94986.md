in-the-hole:/ɪn ðə hoʊl/| adj.|亏损的，负债的|Being in a position of owing money or having a negative balance.  In a losing position or state of disadvantage.|Example: After a series of bad investments, he found himself deeply in the hole.  在几次糟糕的投资之后，他发现自己深陷亏损。
Example: The team was in the hole early in the game, but they managed to come back and win.  这支队伍在比赛初期处于劣势，但他们设法扭转局面并赢得了比赛。|近义词：indebted, in debt, behind, losing, underwater|反义词:in the black, ahead, winning, profitable|词性:形容词
