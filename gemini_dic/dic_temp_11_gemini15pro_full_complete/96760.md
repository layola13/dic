ineptitude:/ɪˈneptɪtjuːd/
n.
无能；笨拙；不称职
lack of skill or ability to do something successfully
His ineptitude as a leader is obvious. 他作为领导的无能显而易见。
ineptness, incompetence, inability, awkwardness
skill, ability, competence, aptitude
noun
