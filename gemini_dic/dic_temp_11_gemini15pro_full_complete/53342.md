disadvantaged:/ˌdɪsədˈvæntɪdʒd/| adj.|处于不利地位的，弱势的|lacking the normal or necessary advantages in life, such as money, education, or health|例句：Disadvantaged children often face significant challenges in education. 弱势儿童在教育方面经常面临重大挑战。|近义词：underprivileged, deprived, impoverished|反义词:privileged, advantaged|词性:形容词
词源:dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + advantage + -ed (suffix forming adjectives)
