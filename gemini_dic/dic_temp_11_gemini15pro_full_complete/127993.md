nepheline:/ˈnɛfəˌlaɪn, ˈnɛfəˌlin/
n.
霞石; 烧碱正长石
A silica-poor igneous rock-forming mineral that occurs as colorless, white, yellowish, grayish, or reddish crystals.
Nepheline syenite is a plutonic rock. 霞石正长岩是一种深成岩。
Nepheline is a feldspathoid mineral. 霞石是一种似长石矿物。
Synonym: nephelite
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral, could use a contrasting mineral like quartz)
