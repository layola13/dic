inhabit:/ɪnˈhæbɪt/ | v. |居住于，栖息于|to live in a place|例句：Many different species inhabit the rainforest. 许多不同的物种栖息在雨林中。

例句：Polar bears inhabit the Arctic regions. 北极熊栖息在北极地区。

近义词：live in, reside in, occupy, dwell in, populate |反义词: vacate, desert, abandon |词性：动词

inhabitate:/ɪnˈhæbɪteɪt/ | v. |居住于，栖息于 (较不常用，inhabit 更常见)|to live in a place (less common than inhabit)|例句：Various tribes inhabitate the remote valleys.  不同的部落居住在偏远的峡谷里。

例句：Rare birds inhabitate the island sanctuary. 稀有鸟类栖息在这个岛屿保护区。

近义词：inhabit, live in, reside in, occupy, dwell in, populate|反义词: vacate, desert, abandon |词性：动词


词源: Late Middle English: from Latin inhabitat- ‘dwelled in’, from the verb inhabitare, from in- ‘in’ + habitare ‘dwell’.
