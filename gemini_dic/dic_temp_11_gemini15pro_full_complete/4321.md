ajar:/əˈdʒɑːr/ | adv. | 微开地，半开地 | In a slightly open state. | The window was standing ajar. 窗户半开着。| synonyms: partly open, slightly open | antonyms: closed, shut, wide open | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *on char*, from *on* (on) + *char* (turn, hinge).  The original sense was "on the turn," meaning "turning on the hinges," thus "slightly open."
