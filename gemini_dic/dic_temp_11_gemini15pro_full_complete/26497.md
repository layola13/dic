caballed:/kəˈbɔːld/
verb
密谋，秘密策划
conspired together secretly, especially for political purposes.
The ministers caballed against the prime minister. 部长们密谋反对首相。
Synonyms: conspired, plotted, intrigued, schemed
Antonyms: cooperated, collaborated
词性: 动词

caballed:/kəˈbɔːld/
adjective
(用作表语)密谋的
having caballed; resulting from a cabal.
The caballed plan failed miserably.  这个密谋的计划彻底失败了。
Synonyms:  conspiratorial, clandestine, covert
Antonyms: open, transparent
词性: 形容词
