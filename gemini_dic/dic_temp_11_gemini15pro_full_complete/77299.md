gatekeeping:/ˈɡeɪtkiːpɪŋ/
n.
把关；看门；守门；信息控制
The act of controlling access to something, often information or opportunities.
Journalists accused the government of gatekeeping information about the pandemic.  记者指控政府控制疫情信息。
The prestigious university's admissions process is known for its strict gatekeeping. 这所名牌大学的录取过程以其严格的把关而闻名。
control, filtering, screening, censorship
openness, transparency, access
noun
