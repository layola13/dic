choicest:/ˈtʃɔɪsɪst/
adjective
精选的，最好的
of very fine quality; excellent.
例句：She always buys the choicest cuts of meat. 她总是买最好的肉块。

例句：The hotel offers its guests the choicest accommodations. 这家酒店为客人提供最好的住宿。

近义词：best, finest, select, prime, top-quality, superior, excellent, first-rate, premium
反义词：worst, poorest, inferior, low-quality

词性: adjective
