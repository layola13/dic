camellias:/kəˈmiːliəz/
|n.|山茶花；茶花|A flowering evergreen shrub or small tree of the genus Camellia, native to Asia, having glossy leaves and showy, waxy flowers.|The camellias in her garden were a riot of color. 她花园里的山茶花五彩缤纷。|synonyms: tea plant, tea tree|antonyms: None|noun
