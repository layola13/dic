offerors:/ˈɔːfərərz/
n.
提供者，要约人
People or organizations who make an offer, especially in a business or legal context.
The offerors submitted their proposals by the deadline.  提供者在截止日期前提交了他们的提案。
Three offerors competed for the contract. 三个要约人竞争这份合同。
synonyms: bidders, tenderers, proposers
antonyms: offerees, recipients
noun
