metaphysics: /ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪks/ | n. |形而上学 |The branch of philosophy that deals with the first principles of things, including abstract concepts such as being, knowing, substance, cause, identity, time, and space. |例句：He studied metaphysics in college. 他在大学学习形而上学。|近义词：philosophy, ontology, cosmology |反义词: physics, empiricism | noun

metaphysical: /ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪkəl/ | adj. |形而上的 |Of or relating to metaphysics. |例句：The question of God's existence is a metaphysical one.  上帝存在的问题是一个形而上学的问题。|近义词：abstract, philosophical, speculative |反义词: physical, empirical, concrete | adjective
