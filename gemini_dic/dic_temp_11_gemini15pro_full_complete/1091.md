acceptive:/əkˈsɛptɪv/| adj.|可接受的，易于接受的|Willing to accept something or someone.  Relatively tolerant of or open to new ideas or change.|She was surprisingly acceptive of the new regulations.|近义词：tolerant, receptive, open-minded, agreeable|反义词: intolerant, resistant, closed-minded|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin acceptivus, from Latin accept- ‘taken’, from the verb accipere.
