befriends:/bɪˈfrɛndz/
verb
与...交朋友；友好对待
to act as a friend to (someone), especially when they are in need of help or support
She befriended the new student. 她和新来的学生交了朋友。
近义词: befriends, pals around with, makes friends with
反义词: antagonizes, alienates, shuns
词性: verb

befriend:/bɪˈfrɛnd/
verb
与...交朋友；友好对待
to act as a friend to (someone), especially when they are in need of help or support
He befriended me when I first arrived in the city and knew no one. 我刚到这个城市，一个人也不认识的时候，他帮了我很多。
近义词: supports, assists, champions
反义词:  opposes, hinders, obstructs
词性: verb
