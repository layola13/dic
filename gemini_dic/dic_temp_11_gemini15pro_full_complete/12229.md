asbestos:/æsˈbɛstəs/ /əsˈbɛstɒs/
n.
石棉
a fibrous mineral that was formerly used for fireproofing and insulation, but its use has been greatly reduced because of the serious health risks posed by inhalation of its fibres.
The old building was full of asbestos. 这栋老建筑里到处都是石棉。

近义词: amphibole, serpentine (这些是石棉的矿物类型，并非完全同义)
反义词:  无明确的反义词

词性: noun

词源: From Latin *asbestinus, from Ancient Greek ἄσβεστος (ásbestos, “inextinguishable, unquenchable”), from ἀ- (a-, “un-”) +‎ σβέννυμι (sbénnumi, “I quench, extinguish”).
