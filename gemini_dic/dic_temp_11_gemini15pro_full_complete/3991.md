agx:/eɪdʒiːˈɛks/ | n. |  Advanced Graphics eXtension | Advanced Graphics eXtension (AGX) is a brand name used by Apple for its GPUs designed for mobile devices, primarily iPads and iPhones. It signifies Apple's custom design and focus on performance and efficiency for graphics-intensive tasks like gaming, video editing, and augmented reality applications.  | Apple's latest A16 Bionic chip features a powerful 5-core AGX GPU.  苹果最新的 A16 仿生芯片配备了强大的 5 核 AGX GPU。 |  GPU, graphics processor, graphics card | integrated graphics | noun
