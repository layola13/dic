mushin:/muːˈʃɪn/| n.| 無心;  空靈;  平常心|A state of mind free from thoughts and emotions, allowing one to react spontaneously and without hesitation. This concept is often associated with Zen Buddhism and martial arts.|He achieved mushin during the kendo match, allowing him to react instinctively to his opponent's attacks. 他在劍道比賽中達到了無心的境界，讓他能本能地應對對手的攻擊。|Synonyms:  tranquility, composure, equanimity|Antonyms: anxiety, distraction, agitation|Noun
