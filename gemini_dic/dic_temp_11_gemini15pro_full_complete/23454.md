braes:/breɪz/
n.
斜坡，山坡
slopes or hillsides, especially the sides of a steep river valley or ravine.
The braes were covered in purple heather.  山坡上覆盖着紫色的石南花。
Near synonyms: slopes, hillsides, banks
Antonyms: plains, valleys
noun

brae:/breɪ/ (singular form)
n.
斜坡，山坡
a slope or hillside, especially the side of a steep river valley or ravine.
The sheep grazed peacefully on the brae. 羊群在山坡上安静地吃草。
Near synonyms: slope, hillside, bank
Antonyms: plain, valley
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Scottish Gaelic *bràigh*, from Old Irish *brí*, from Proto-Celtic *brigā* (hill, mountain).  Cognate with Welsh *bre*.
