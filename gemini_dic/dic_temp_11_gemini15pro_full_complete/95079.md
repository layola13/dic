inaffectation:/ɪnˌæfɛkˈteɪʃən/
n.
自然；不做作；真挚
the quality of being natural and not trying to seem better or more important than you are
She spoke with complete inaffectation. 她说话非常自然不做作。

近义词:naturalness, genuineness, sincerity, artlessness, simplicity, unpretentiousness
反义词:affectation, pretentiousness, artificiality
