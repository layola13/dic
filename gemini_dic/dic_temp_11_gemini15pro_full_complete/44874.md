cry-baby: /ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi/ (US) /ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi/ (UK)| n.|爱哭鬼，爱抱怨的人|A person, especially a child, who cries easily or complains frequently, often for trivial reasons. |例句：He's such a cry-baby, always whining about something. 他真是个爱哭鬼，总是抱怨个不停。

例句：Stop being such a cry-baby and deal with it! 别再像个爱哭鬼一样了，去面对它!

近义词：whiner, complainer, moaner|反义词: stoic, tough, resilient|词性: noun


cry-baby:  /ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi/ (US) /ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi/ (UK)| adj. |爱哭的，爱抱怨的 (less common as an adjective)|Characterized by crying easily or complaining frequently. |例句：He has a cry-baby attitude. 他有一种爱抱怨的态度。

例句：She gave him a cry-baby look. 她给了他一个委屈巴巴的眼神。


近义词：whiny, complaining|反义词: stoic, tough|词性: adjective
