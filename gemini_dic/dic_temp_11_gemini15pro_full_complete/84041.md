half-feminine:/ˌhæf ˈfɛmɪnɪn/ | adj. | 半女性化的；带有女性气质的 | Partially or imperfectly feminine; having some characteristics traditionally associated with femininity. | He had a half-feminine way of walking. 他走路的样子有点女性化。|  She cultivated a half-feminine air of mystery. 她培养了一种略带女性气质的神秘感。 | 近义词: effeminate, womanish | 反义词: masculine, manly | 词性: 形容词


Etymology:
half- (Old English healf) + feminine (from Old French feminin, ultimately from Latin femina "woman").
