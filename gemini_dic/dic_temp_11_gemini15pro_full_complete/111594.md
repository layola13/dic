lopsided: /ˌlɒpˈsaɪdɪd/ (UK) /ˌlɑːpˈsaɪdɪd/ (US)| adj.| 不平衡的，倾向一方的|Uneven or disproportionate in size, weight, or shape; heavier, larger, or more developed on one side than the other.|The painting hung lopsided on the wall. 这幅画歪歪斜斜地挂在墙上。|synonyms: asymmetrical, uneven, unbalanced, off-center, tilted, askew|antonyms: balanced, symmetrical, even, uniform|adjective

lopsided: /ˌlɒpˈsaɪdɪd/ (UK) /ˌlɑːpˈsaɪdɪd/ (US)| adj.| (比赛)一边倒的|Heavily weighted in favor of one side; one-sided (especially of a contest).|The election result was a lopsided victory for the incumbent. 选举结果是对现任者一边倒的胜利。|synonyms: one-sided, unequal, disproportionate|antonyms: even, close, balanced|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From lop + sided.  Lop meaning to hang down limply, and sided referring to having sides. Thus, lopsided suggests something hanging unevenly on its sides.
