fruitive:/ˈfruːɪtɪv/
adjective
结果实的; 多产的; 享受的;  与果实有关的
Relating to, consisting of, or producing fruit; fruitful. Also, enjoying the fruits or results of actions, especially pleasurable ones.
The fruitive vineyards yielded a bountiful harvest.  多产的葡萄园收获了丰收的葡萄。
He lived a fruitive life, enjoying the rewards of his hard work. 他过着富有成果的生活，享受着辛勤工作的回报。
synonyms:fruitful, productive, prolific, rewarding, enjoyable
antonyms:barren, unproductive, sterile, unfruitful, unrewarding
词性: adjective
