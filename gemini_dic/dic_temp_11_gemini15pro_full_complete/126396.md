myrtaceous:/ˌmɜːrˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 桃金娘科的；桃金娘属的 | Belonging to or resembling the myrtle family (Myrtaceae), which includes eucalyptus, guava, and cloves. |  The eucalyptus tree is a myrtaceous plant. 桉树是桃金娘科植物。| Synonyms: myrtle-like | Antonyms: N/A | adjective

myrtaceous:/ˌmɜːrˈteɪʃəs/ | n. | 桃金娘科植物 | A plant belonging to the myrtle family (Myrtaceae). |  Many myrtaceous shrubs are grown for their fragrant flowers. 许多桃金娘科灌木因其芬芳的花朵而被种植。| Synonyms: myrtle | Antonyms: N/A | noun
