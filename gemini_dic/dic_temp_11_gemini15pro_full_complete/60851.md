electrotyping:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌtaɪpɪŋ/
n.
电铸术，电版印刷
The process of making a metal copy of something by using electricity to deposit metal onto a mold.
Electrotyping was a common method for producing printing plates in the 19th century.  电铸术是19世纪制作印刷版的常用方法。
galvanoplasty, electroforming
---
electrotype:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌtaɪp/
n.
电版
A metal copy of something made by electrotyping.
The museum displayed an electrotype of a famous ancient coin.  博物馆展出了一枚著名古钱币的电版。
replica, copy, duplicate
---
electrotype:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌtaɪp/
vt.
用电版印刷，电铸
To make an electrotype of something.
The printer electrotyped the page to create a printing plate. 印刷工人用电版印刷页面来制作印刷版。
duplicate, reproduce, copy
