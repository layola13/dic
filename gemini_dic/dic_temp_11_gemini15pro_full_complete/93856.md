immobilizer: /ɪˈmōbəˌlaɪzər/ | n. |  防盗器，固定器 | An electronic device fitted to a motor vehicle that prevents the engine from being started without the correct key.  A device that prevents something from moving. |  Example: The car thief couldn't start the engine because of the immobilizer. 汽车窃贼因为防盗器而无法启动引擎。

Example:  The doctor used an immobilizer to keep the patient's broken leg from moving. 医生用固定器来防止病人断腿移动。| Synonym:  anti-theft device, security system, stabilizer, brace, splint | Antonym:  activator,  starter | noun
