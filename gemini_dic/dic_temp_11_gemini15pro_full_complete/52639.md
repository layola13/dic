digital:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəl/| n.|数字式仪表，数字；手指|A finger or toe. A numerical readout or display. |例句：The digital clock displayed the time clearly.  数字时钟清晰地显示时间。|近义词：digit, numeral, number|反义词：-|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *digitalis "pertaining to a finger," from Latin digitus "finger, toe." The modern computing sense emerged in the 1940s.
