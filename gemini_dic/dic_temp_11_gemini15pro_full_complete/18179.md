belace:/bɪˈleɪs/| vt.|用花边装饰，用带子系紧|To adorn or trim with lace; to fasten or tie with a lace or laces.|She belaced her dress with delicate white lace. 她用精致的白色蕾丝装饰了她的连衣裙。|近义词：trim, adorn, decorate, fasten, tie|反义词：untie, loosen|词性：动词

Etymology: From be- + lace, from Old French *lacier, from las "snare, net," from Latin laqueus "noose, snare."
