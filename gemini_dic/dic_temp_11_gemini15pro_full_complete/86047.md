healthsome:/ˈhɛlθsəm/| adj.|有益健康的，促进健康的|Promoting or conducive to good health.  Relating to good health, wholesome.|例句：A healthsome diet is important for a long life.  有益健康的饮食对长寿很重要。|近义词：healthy, salubrious, beneficial|反义词：unhealthy, unwholesome|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *helsam, from Old English hālsum (“wholesome, healthy, sound”), from Proto-Germanic *hailaz (“whole, healthy”). Cognate with West Frisian hâlsum (“wholesome”), Dutch heilzaam (“wholesome, salutary”), German heilsam (“wholesome, salutary”).  The modern spelling arose by association with health.
