antithetically:/ˌæntɪˈθɛtɪk(ə)li/
adverb
对立地；相反地
in a way that shows or involves antithesis; in opposite ways

antithetically:/ˌæntɪˈθɛtɪk(ə)li/
adverb
中文翻译：对立地，相反地
英文解析：in a way that shows or involves antithesis; in opposite ways.  Meaning in a way that expresses contrast or opposition.
例句：The two candidates presented their views antithetically.  两位候选人对立地提出了他们的观点。

例句：He argued his point antithetically, highlighting the flaws in the opposing argument. 他以对立的方式论证了他的观点，突出了对方论点中的缺陷。
近义词：oppositely, contrarily, conversely
反义词：similarly, likewise
词性：adverb
