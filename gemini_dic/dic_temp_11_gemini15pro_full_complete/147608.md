overplease:/ˌoʊvərˈpliːz/
verb
过度取悦；过分讨好
To please someone excessively, often to the point of being insincere or obsequious.
He overpleased his boss by constantly agreeing with him, even when he was wrong. 他总是同意老板的意见，即使老板错了也一样，以此过度讨好老板。
She tried to overplease her guests by offering them too much food and attention. 她试图通过提供过多的食物和关注来过度取悦她的客人。
近义词: fawn, flatter, indulge
反义词: neglect, ignore
词性: verb
