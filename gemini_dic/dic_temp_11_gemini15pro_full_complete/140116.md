off-roaders:/ˌɒfˈrəʊdəz/ (UK) /ˌɔːfˈroʊdərz/ (US)
n.
越野车驾驶者；越野爱好者
People who drive vehicles designed for off-road driving, or enthusiasts of off-roading.
Many off-roaders enjoy exploring remote areas. 许多越野爱好者喜欢探索偏远地区。
Off-roaders gathered for the annual Jamboree. 越野爱好者聚集在一起参加年度大会.
Synonyms:  four-wheelers, mudders, adventurers
Antonyms: city drivers, commuters
noun
