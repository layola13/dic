animatingly:/ˈænɪmeɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 生动地，活泼地 | In a way that shows excitement and enthusiasm, bringing something to life. | She described the scene animatingly, her gestures bringing the story to life. 她绘声绘色地描述了那个场景，她的手势使故事栩栩如生。|  She spoke animatingly about her trip to Italy. 她兴致勃勃地讲述了她的意大利之旅。 | 近义词: vividly, enthusiastically, spiritedly, lively | 反义词: dully, lifelessly, monotonously | 词性: 副词

词源: From animate + -ing + -ly.  Animate comes from Latin animatus, past participle of animare "to give breath to, quicken," from anima "breath, life, soul."
