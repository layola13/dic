amphorae:/æmˈfɔːriː/| n. |双耳细颈瓶，坛子|A tall, ancient Greek or Roman jar with two handles and a narrow neck, used for storing oil or wine. |The archaeologists unearthed several amphorae filled with ancient olive oil. 考古学家出土了几个装满古代橄榄油的双耳细颈瓶。|近义词：jar, vase, urn|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern container")|Noun
