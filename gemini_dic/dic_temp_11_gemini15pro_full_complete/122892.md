mollycoddlers:/ˈmɒliˌkɒdləz (UK), ˈmɑːliˌkɑːdlərz (US)| n. |娇生惯养的人，溺爱孩子的人|A person who pampers or overprotects someone, often a child.|He accused the school of producing a generation of mollycoddlers, unable to cope with the real world. 他指责学校培养了一代娇生惯养的人，无法应对现实世界。| Mollycoddlers are often criticized for not letting children develop independence. 娇惯孩子的人经常被批评不让孩子培养独立性。 |Synonyms: cosset, pamperer, overprotector |Antonyms: disciplinarian, strict parent| noun
