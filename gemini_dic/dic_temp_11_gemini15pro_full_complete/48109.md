decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/ | v.  n. |下降; 衰退; 谢绝;婉拒 |To decrease in quantity, strength, or importance; to politely refuse something. | The company's profits declined by 10% last year.  该公司去年的利润下降了10%。 | synonyms: decrease, fall, refuse, reject | antonyms: increase, rise, accept, agree | verb

decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/ | n. |下降; 衰退; 谢绝; 衰退期 | A gradual and continuous loss of strength, importance, or quality.  | The decline in sales caused concern among the management.  销售额的下降引起了管理层的担忧。 | synonyms: downturn, decrease, slump | antonyms: increase, rise, growth | noun
