messengers:/ˈmesɪndʒərz/
n.|信使，使者|People who carry messages or information. |The king sent messengers to all parts of the kingdom. 国王派遣信使到王国各地。|
messengers:/ˈmesɪndʒərz/
n.|送信人，通讯员|Those who deliver communications. |The messengers delivered the telegrams promptly. 通讯员迅速地送达了电报。|
messengers:/ˈmesɪndʒərz/
n.|先驱，预兆|Something that foreshadows or announces something else. |Dark clouds are often messengers of a coming storm. 乌云通常是暴风雨来临的预兆。|


Etymology:
From Old French messagier, from message (message), from Latin missus (sent). Ultimately from the Latin verb mittere (to send).
