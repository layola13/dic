"παρά" (para) means "beside" or "alongside," and "ἀλλήλων" (allēlōn) means "one another." Thus, "παράλληλος" literally means"beside one another," referring to lines that run alongside each other without intersecting. The suffix "-ist" is added to form a noun denoting a person who advocates or practices something.
