outgnawn:/ˌaʊtˈnɔːn/
verb
啃穿；咬坏；超过（通过啃咬）
having gnawed something more extensively or effectively than another. The beaver had outgnawn its rival and felled the largest tree.
The hungry mouse outgnawn the wooden barrier.  饥饿的老鼠啃穿了木栅栏。
The persistent woodworm outgnawn the antique furniture. 坚持不懈的蛀虫啃坏了古董家具。
Synonyms: outbite, erode, corrode
Antonyms: repair, restore
词性: 动词
