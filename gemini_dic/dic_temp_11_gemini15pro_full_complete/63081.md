enschedule:/ɛnˈskɛdʒuːl/
verb
安排；预定；列入计划
to place something in a schedule; to plan or schedule something
The meeting is enscheduled for next Tuesday.  会议安排在下周二举行。
The surgery is enscheduled for 9 a.m. tomorrow. 手术安排在明天上午9点。
近义词: schedule, plan, arrange
反义词: cancel, postpone
词性: verb

词源: en- (prefix meaning "to put into" or "to cause to be") + schedule
