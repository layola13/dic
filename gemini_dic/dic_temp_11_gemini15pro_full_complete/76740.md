game-plans:/ˈɡeɪm plænz/
noun
比赛计划，作战方案
A detailed plan or strategy for achieving success in a particular game, contest, or situation.
The coach refused to discuss his game-plans for the upcoming match. 教练拒绝讨论即将到来的比赛的计划。
The company's game-plan for increasing market share involved aggressive advertising. 该公司增加市场份额的计划包括积极的广告宣传。
synonyms: strategies, tactics, plans, schemes, blueprints
antonyms: improvisation, spontaneity
n.
