dermophyte:/ˈdɜːrməˌfaɪt/| n. |皮肤癣菌|A fungus that causes skin diseases, especially ringworm.|Example 1: The dermatologist identified the infection as being caused by a dermophyte. 皮肤科医生确定这种感染是由皮肤癣菌引起的。

Example 2:  Dermophytes thrive in warm, moist environments. 皮肤癣菌在温暖潮湿的环境中 thrive。|Synonym:  ringworm fungus, tinea fungus|Antonym: N/A|Noun
