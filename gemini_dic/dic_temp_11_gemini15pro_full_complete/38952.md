confiding:/kənˈfaɪdɪŋ/ | adj. | 坦白的，信任的；易于信任的 |  Showing trust by imparting secrets or private matters; inspiring trust. | She had a confiding nature, always ready to share her thoughts and feelings. 她性格坦诚，总是乐于分享自己的想法和感受。 | Example: He gave her a confiding smile. 他给了她一个充满信任的微笑。| Synonym: trusting, open, frank, candid, intimate | Antonym: secretive, suspicious, wary, guarded | adjective
