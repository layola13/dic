leeches:/ˈliːtʃɪz/
n.
水蛭;吸血鬼;榨取他人钱财者
Aquatic or terrestrial annelid worms with suckers at both ends. Some species suck blood.  Figuratively, a person who extorts money from others.
Leeches were once commonly used in medicine. 水蛭曾经普遍用于医疗。
They're financial leeches, draining the company's resources. 他们是金融吸血鬼，耗尽了公司的资源。
parasites, bloodsuckers, sponges
benefactors, donors
noun
