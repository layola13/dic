addressograph: /əˈdrɛsəˌɡræf/ | vt. | 用地址印刷机印刷 | To print (something) using an addressograph. | They addressographed the envelopes for the fundraiser.  他们用地址印刷机印刷了募捐活动的信封。| synonyms: print addresses, label | antonyms: handwrite, write | verb
