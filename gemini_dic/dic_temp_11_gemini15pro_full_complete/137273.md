nonsympathizer:/ˌnɒnˈsɪmpəθaɪzər/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɪmpəθaɪzər/| n. |非同情者；不支持者|A person who does not feel or express sympathy or support for a particular person, group, cause, or belief.|例句：He was considered a nonsympathizer by the striking workers.  他被罢工工人视为不支持者。

例句：Many nonsympathizers criticized the group's tactics. 许多不支持者批评了该组织的策略。|近义词：opponent, antagonist, dissenter, objector|反义词：supporter, sympathizer, advocate, ally|词性：名词
