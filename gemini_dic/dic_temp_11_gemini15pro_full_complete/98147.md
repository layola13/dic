insatiable:/ɪnˈseɪʃəbəl/ /ɪnˈseɪʃiəbəl/
adjective
无法满足的；贪得无厌的
(of a desire or need) impossible to satisfy.
She had an insatiable curiosity about everything around her. 她对周围的一切都有着无法满足的好奇心。
insatiable:/ɪnˈseɪʃəbəl/ /ɪnˈseɪʃiəbəl/
adjective
贪婪的；不知足的
always wanting more of something; not able to be satisfied.
His insatiable appetite for power led to his downfall. 他对权力贪得无厌的欲望导致了他的垮台。

近义词: unquenchable, ravenous, voracious, greedy
反义词: satisfied, content, moderate
词性: adjective
