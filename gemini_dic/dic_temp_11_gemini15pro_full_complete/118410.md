merrimake: /ˈmeriˌmeɪk/ (US), /ˈmɛriˌmeɪk/ (UK) | n. | 欢宴，狂欢，嬉戏 | A noisy, lively celebration or party, often characterized by merrymaking and revelry. | They held a merrimake to celebrate the harvest. 他们举办了一场欢宴来庆祝丰收。|  The wedding was a grand merrimake with music, dancing, and feasting. 婚礼是一场盛大的狂欢，有音乐、舞蹈和盛宴。| 近义词: revelry, festivity, celebration, carousal | 反义词: mourning, grief, solemnity | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *merimaken*, from meri (merry) + maken (to make).  The word literally means "to make merry."
