gabblers:/ˈɡæblərz/
n.
饶舌者，喋喋不休的人
People who talk excessively, especially about unimportant things.
The gabblers in the office make it hard to concentrate. 办公室里那些喋喋不休的人让人很难集中注意力。
chatterboxes, gossips
quiet, reserved people
noun
