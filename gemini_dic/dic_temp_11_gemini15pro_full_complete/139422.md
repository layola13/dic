obsessional:/əbˈseʃənl/| adj. n.|痴迷的，强迫性的；强迫症患者|Relating to or characterized by obsession; persistently preoccupied with a single idea or activity.  Also, a person who suffers from an obsession.|He had an obsessional fear of germs. 他有对细菌的强迫性恐惧。|近义词：compulsive, obsessive, fixated|反义词：casual, indifferent|词性：形容词, 名词

obsessional:/əbˈseʃənl/| n.|强迫症患者|A person who suffers from an obsession.|The obsessional washed his hands fifty times a day.  这个强迫症患者每天洗手五十次。|近义词：compulsive person|反义词：None|词性：名词
