brainiacs:/ˈbreɪniæks/| n.|天才，智多星|Highly intelligent people, often used informally or playfully.|Those brainiacs in the lab are working on a new invention. 实验室里的那些天才们正在研发一项新发明。|近义词：geniuses, intellectuals, whizzes|反义词：dunces, idiots|noun

词源:
From "brain" + "-iac," a suffix forming nouns meaning "one affected by" or "one obsessed with."
