awestruck:/ˈɔːstrʌk/| adj.|充满敬畏的，感到惊叹的|Filled with awe; deeply impressed and reverential.|例句：The children were awestruck by the magician's performance. 孩子们对魔术师的表演充满了敬畏。|近义词：amazed, astonished, wonderstruck|反义词：unimpressed, indifferent|词性:形容词

词源: From awe + struck, past participle of strike.  The sense is of being struck or filled with awe.
