high-rises:/ˈhaɪˌraɪzɪz/
n.
高层建筑；高楼大厦
Tall buildings with many floors; skyscrapers.
Many high-rises have sprung up in the city center.  许多高层建筑在市中心拔地而起。
The city skyline is dominated by gleaming high-rises. 城市天际线被闪闪发光的高楼大厦所占据。
skyscrapers, towers, multi-story buildings
low-rise buildings, bungalows
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "high" + "rise" (noun, meaning an upward slope or increase), used attributively (high rise) before becoming a noun itself (high-rise).
