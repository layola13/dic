exerting:/ɪɡˈzɜːtɪŋ/
|v.|施加，运用，发挥|applying or using (strength, ability, influence, etc.) actively and to the full.|She was exerting all her strength to open the door. 她正使出浑身力气开门。|近义词: applying, using, employing, wielding|反义词: relaxing, withholding|Verb
