fang:/fæŋ/| n.|犬齿，尖牙，毒牙|a long, pointed tooth, especially a canine tooth of a dog or wolf, or a similar tooth of a snake or other animal. |The wolf bared its fangs. 狼露出了它的尖牙。|Synonyms: tooth, canine, tusk|Antonyms: gum|noun

fang:/fæŋ/| n.|根，支柱|a root or one of the parts that a root divides into. |The plant's fangs gripped the soil tightly. 植物的根紧紧地抓住土壤。|Synonyms: root, radix|Antonyms: branch, stem|noun
