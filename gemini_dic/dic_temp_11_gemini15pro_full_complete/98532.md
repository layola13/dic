instigates:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪts/
verb
煽动，唆使，激起
To urge on; stir up; foment; provoke
例句：The government accused the opposition party of instigating the riots. 政府指责反对党煽动骚乱。
例句：He was accused of instigating violence. 他被指控煽动暴力。
近义词：incite, provoke, abet, foment, stimulate, encourage
反义词：deter, discourage, prevent, suppress
词性：v.

instigate:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/
verb
煽动，唆使，激起
To urge on; stir up; foment; provoke
例句：Rumors instigated widespread panic. 谣言引发了广泛的恐慌。
例句：She instigated a campaign to raise awareness about the issue. 她发起了一场提高人们对这个问题认识的运动。
近义词：initiate, launch, commence
反义词：halt, stop, cease
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin instigatus, past participle of instigare “to incite, urge on,” from in- “in” + stigare “to prick, goad.”
