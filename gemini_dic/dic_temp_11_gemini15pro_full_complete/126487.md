例句：Mytiloid bivalves are common in intertidal zones.  贻贝超科的双壳类动物在潮间带很常见。|近义词：mussel-like, mytiliform|反义词: (No direct antonym, could use terms like "un-mussel-like")|词性: adjective, noun
