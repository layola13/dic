chattily:/ˈtʃætɪli/| adv.|健谈地，饶舌地|In a chatty and informal manner.  以健谈和非正式的方式。|She spoke chattily about her day. 她絮絮叨叨地谈论着她的一天。|Synonyms: talkatively, loquaciously, garrulously|Antonyms: taciturnly, quietly, reservedly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From chat + -ily.  来源于 chat (聊天) + -ily (副词后缀，表示方式)。
