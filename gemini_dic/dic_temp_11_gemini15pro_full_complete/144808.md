over-hardened:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdnd/
adjective
过度硬化的
Made too hard, especially by excessive heat treatment.  The metal became brittle and prone to cracking because it was over-hardened.
The blade was over-hardened and snapped under pressure. 刀刃过度硬化，在压力下断裂了。
The steel was over-hardened, making it brittle. 钢铁过度硬化，使其变脆。
近义词: over-tempered, excessively hardened
反义词: soft, malleable, ductile
词性: adjective


over-hardened:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdnd/
verb (past participle)
过度硬化（动词的过去分词形式）
Having been made too hard. The over-hardened steel was unsuitable for the intended purpose.
The over-hardened components fractured easily. 过度硬化的部件很容易断裂。
These over-hardened cookies are impossible to eat! 这些过度硬化的饼干根本没法吃！
近义词: over-tempered
反义词: softened
词性: verb (past participle)


词源:
"Over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "harden" (verb meaning to make hard)
