interpollinated:/ˌɪntərˈpɑːləneɪtɪd/
verb
相互授粉的，异花授粉的
pollinated by pollen from a different plant of the same species or from a different species.
The apple trees were interpollinated with crabapple pollen. 这些苹果树与海棠花粉异花授粉。
cross-pollinated, hybridized
self-pollinated
v.
