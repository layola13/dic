alphabetize: /ˈælfəbəˌtaɪz/ | vt. | 按字母顺序排列 | To arrange in alphabetical order. |  Example: Please alphabetize these files by the clients' last names.  请按照客户的姓氏按字母顺序排列这些文件。

Example:  I alphabetized my CD collection. 我把我的CD收藏按字母顺序排列好了。

近义词: sort, order, arrange
反义词: disorder, disarrange
词性: verb

词源: From alpha +‎ -betize, based on alphabetic.
