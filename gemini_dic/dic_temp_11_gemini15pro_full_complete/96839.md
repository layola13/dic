inevitable:/ɪnˈevɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可避免的，必然发生的 | Certain to happen; unavoidable. |例句：Death is inevitable. 死亡是不可避免的。|例句：The accident was the inevitable consequence of carelessness.  这起事故是粗心大意的必然结果。| 近义词：unavoidable, certain, inescapable | 反义词：avoidable, preventable |词性:形容词

inevitable:/ɪnˈevɪtəbl/ | n. | 不可避免的事 | Something that is inevitable. | 例句：The inevitable happened and they lost the game. 不可避免的事情发生了，他们输掉了比赛。 | 例句：She accepted the inevitable with grace. 她优雅地接受了不可避免的事情。|近义词：certainty, necessity | 反义词: improbability |词性:名词
