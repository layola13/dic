munificences:/ˌmjuːnɪˈfɪsənsiz/
|n.|慷慨赠予；捐赠；施舍|The king was known for his munificences to the arts. 国王以其对艺术的慷慨捐赠而闻名。|例句：His munificences to the university were well known. 他对这所大学的慷慨捐赠是众所周知的。

例句：The museum was built thanks to the munificences of several wealthy donors.  这座博物馆的建成多亏了几位富有捐赠者的慷慨解囊。
|近义词：generosity, liberality, bounty, philanthropy|反义词：stinginess, miserliness, parsimony|词性:名词(plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *munificence*, from Latin *munificentia* ("bounty, liberality"), from *munificus* ("bountiful, liberal, generous") + *-entia* (suffix forming abstract nouns).  *Munificus* is from *munus* ("gift, service, duty") + *-ficus* (suffix meaning "making" or "doing").
