journeywork: /ˈdʒɜːrniwɜːrk/ | n. | 旅行工作；（尤指）计日工作；按件计酬的工作 | Work done by the day or piece, especially by an artisan traveling from place to place. |  The carpenter did journeywork for several months before settling down in one town. 木匠在定居之前做了几个月的旅行工作。 |  He earned a meager living doing journeywork. 他靠做计日工作勉强维持生计。 | Synonyms: piecework, day labor, itinerant work | Antonyms: permanent employment, salaried work | noun
