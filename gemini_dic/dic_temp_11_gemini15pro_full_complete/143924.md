outpromised:/ˌaʊtˈprɒmɪst/
verb
做出比…更多的承诺;在承诺方面胜过
To make more or greater promises than someone else.
The candidate outpromised his opponent, pledging lower taxes and better healthcare. 这位候选人在承诺方面胜过他的对手，承诺降低税收和改善医疗保健。
Synonyms: outbid, surpass, exceed
Antonyms: underpromise, underbid
词性: 动词
