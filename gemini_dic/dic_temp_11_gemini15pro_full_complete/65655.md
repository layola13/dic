evaporator:/ɪˈvæpəreɪtər/| n. |蒸发器；蒸发皿|A device used to transform a liquid into a vapor. |例句：The evaporator in the refrigerator cools the air.  冰箱里的蒸发器冷却空气。

例句：The chemist used an evaporator to concentrate the solution. 化学家用蒸发皿浓缩溶液。|近义词：vaporizer, volatilizer|反义词:condenser|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From evaporate + -or.  Ultimately from Latin *exvaporatus, from ex- (out) + vapor (steam, vapor).
