grateful: /ˈɡreɪtfəl/ (US) /ˈɡreɪtfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|感激的，感谢的|Feeling or showing an appreciation of kindness; thankful.|

例句：
I am grateful for your help.
我很感激你的帮助。

I'm eternally grateful to you for your kindness.
我永远感激你的好意。


近义词：thankful, appreciative, indebted|
反义词：ungrateful, unappreciative|
词性：adjective|


词源:
From Middle English *grateful*, from Old French *grateful*, from Late Latin *grāt(us)* (pleasing, agreeable, thankful) + *-ful*.  The Latin *grātus* is related to *grātia* (grace, favor, thanks).
