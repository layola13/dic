注意事项:

“Deutsches” technically functions as an adjective, but in English, "German" is more commonly used as both the adjective and the noun.  While “Deutsches” is grammatically correct in German when used attributively (before a noun),  it sounds unusual in English. Using "German" is almost always preferred.
