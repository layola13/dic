nonhubristic:/ˌnɒnhjuːˈbrɪstɪk/| adj.|不傲慢的，谦逊的|Not characterized by hubris; humble, modest.  Lacking excessive pride or arrogance.|例句：Her nonhubristic acceptance of the award was refreshing. 她谦逊地接受了奖项，令人耳目一新。|近义词：humble, modest, unassuming, unpretentious|反义词：hubristic, arrogant, haughty, conceited|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non* meaning "not"
hubristic: from Greek *ὕβρις* (hybris) meaning "excessive pride, arrogance" + -istic (suffix) forming adjectives.
