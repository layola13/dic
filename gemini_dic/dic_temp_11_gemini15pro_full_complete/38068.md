composite:/ˈkɒmpəzɪt/ (UK) /kəmˈpɑːzɪt/ (US)| adj. n.|复合的，合成的；复合材料|Made up of various parts or elements.|例句：The aircraft is made of composite materials. 这架飞机是由复合材料制成的。|近义词：compound, combined, complex|反义词：simple, basic|词性:形容词,名词

composite:/ˈkɒmpəzɪt/ (UK) /kəmˈpɑːzɪt/ (US)| adj. n.|混合的，综合的；合成物|A thing made up of several parts or elements.|例句：A composite photograph was created from multiple images. 这张合成照片是由多张图片合成的。|近义词：mixture, blend, amalgamation|反义词：element, component|词性:形容词,名词
