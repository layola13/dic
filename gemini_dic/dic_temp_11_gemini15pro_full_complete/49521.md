demandingly:/dɪˈmændɪŋli/
adverb|苛刻地；要求高地|In a way that shows that you expect a lot of attention or effort from other people.|She looked at him demandingly. 她苛求地望着他。|近义词：exactingly, rigorously, stringently|反义词： leniently, easily, tolerantly|词性: 副词
Etymology: From "demanding" + "-ly". "Demanding" comes from the verb "demand," which originates from Old French "demander" (to ask, request), ultimately from Latin "demandare" (to entrust, commit).
