hemlock:/ˈhɛmlɒk/ (UK) /ˈhɛmlɑːk/ (US)| n. v.|铁杉；毒芹；毒杀|A poisonous plant or a poisonous drink made from it. Also used as a verb meaning to poison with hemlock.|例句：Socrates was hemlocked for corrupting the youth of Athens. 苏格拉底因腐蚀雅典青年而被毒芹毒死。

例句：The ancient Greeks used hemlock to execute criminals. 古希腊人用毒芹处决罪犯。|近义词：poison, toxin|反义词:antidote, remedy|词性:名词, 动词

词源: From Old English hymlice, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *humalaz.
