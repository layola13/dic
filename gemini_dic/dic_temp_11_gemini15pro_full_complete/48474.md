deep-rooted:/ˌdiːpˈruːtɪd/ /ˌdiːpˈruːtəd/
adjective
根深蒂固的；由来已久的
firmly established and difficult to change
例句：The problem of poverty in this region is deep-rooted.  这个地区贫困问题根深蒂固。

例句：They had a deep-rooted suspicion of all foreigners.  他们对所有外国人都怀有根深蒂固的怀疑。

近义词：ingrained, entrenched, established, fixed, firm, deep-seated
反义词：superficial, shallow
词性：adjective
