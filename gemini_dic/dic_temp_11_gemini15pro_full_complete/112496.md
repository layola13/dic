louring:/ˈlaʊərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlaʊərɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.| 阴沉的，怒容满面的 | appearing dark and threatening, especially of the sky; looking angry and sullen | The louring sky threatened rain. 阴沉的天空预示着要下雨。| synonyms: dark, overcast, gloomy, threatening, sullen, scowling | antonyms: bright, sunny, cheerful, clear | adjective

louring:/ˈlaʊərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlaʊərɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 阴沉，怒容 | the state of being dark and threatening; a sullen or angry look  | The louring of his brow indicated his displeasure. 他紧皱的眉头表明了他的不悦。| synonyms: darkness, gloom, sullenness | antonyms: brightness, cheerfulness | noun
