alexic:/əˈlɛksɪk/| adj.|失语的，与词汇有关的|Relating to words or vocabulary; suffering from alexia (inability to read).|例句：The patient exhibited alexic symptoms after the stroke. 中风后，病人表现出失语症状。

例句：Alexic aphasia affects a person's ability to understand written words. 失语性失语症会影响一个人理解书面文字的能力。|近义词：lexical, verbal|反义词：nonverbal, nonlexical|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *lexis* (word, speech) + *-ikos* (suffix forming adjectives).
