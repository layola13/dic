magnesia:/mæɡˈniːʒə/ |n.|氧化镁；镁土；轻质氧化镁|A white, tasteless powder, magnesium oxide, MgO, used as an antacid and laxative.  It is also used in industry as a refractory material.|Milk of magnesia is often used to treat indigestion.|Synonyms: magnesium oxide, periclase|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a chemical compound.  One might consider substances with opposite properties, such as acids if considering its basic nature in reactions.)|noun
