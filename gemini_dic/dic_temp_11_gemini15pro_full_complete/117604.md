megaphone:/ˈmɛɡəfoʊn/ (US) /ˈmɛɡəfəʊn/ (UK)| n. |扩音器；喇叭|A cone-shaped device used to amplify the voice or other sounds.|The coach used a megaphone to address the team across the field. 教练用扩音器在球场上向队员讲话。|Synonyms: loudspeaker, amplifier, bullhorn|Antonyms: microphone (in terms of function, not opposite meaning)|noun
