disinfectant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktənt/ | n. |消毒剂|A chemical liquid that destroys bacteria.|例句：We used a strong disinfectant to clean the bathroom. 我们用强力消毒剂清洁了浴室。|例句：The disinfectant killed most of the germs on the surface.  这种消毒剂杀死了表面上的大部分细菌。|近义词：antiseptic, germicide, sanitizer|反义词: contaminant|词性:noun

disinfectants:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktənts/ | n. |消毒剂（复数）|Plural form of disinfectant, referring to multiple types or instances of disinfectants.|例句：Various disinfectants are available for different purposes.  各种消毒剂可用于不同目的。|例句：The hospital uses several different disinfectants to prevent the spread of infection.  医院使用几种不同的消毒剂来防止感染传播。|近义词：antiseptics, germicides, sanitizers|反义词: contaminants|词性:noun
