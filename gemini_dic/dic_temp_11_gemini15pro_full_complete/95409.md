incidence:/ˈɪnsɪdəns/| n.|发生率，影响，入射|The incidence of skin cancer is increasing.  皮肤癌的发病率正在上升。|Synonyms: occurrence, frequency, prevalence|Antonyms: rarity, infrequency|noun

incidence:/ˈɪnsɪdəns/| n.|影响范围，涉及面|The tax cuts will have a wide incidence. 减税的影响范围很广。|Synonyms: impact, effect, influence|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incidentia, from incidere "to fall upon, befall, happen," from in- "in, on, upon" + cadere "to fall."
