Manchu:/ˈmænˌtʃuː/| adj. n.|满族的，满洲人；满语|Relating to Manchuria, its people, or their language.  A member of a people originating in Manchuria who formed the last imperial dynasty of China (Qing dynasty).|例句：The Manchu emperors ruled China for centuries. 满清皇帝统治了中国几个世纪。
