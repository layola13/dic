aswing:/əˈswɪŋ/
adj.
摇摆的，摆动的
Moving back and forth or from side to side.
The pendulum was aswing. 钟摆正在摆动。
近义词: swinging, swaying, oscillating
反义词: still, motionless
词性:形容词
