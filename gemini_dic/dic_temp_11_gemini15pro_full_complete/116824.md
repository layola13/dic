maxillary:/ˈmæksɪləri/ (US), /mækˈsɪləri/ (UK)| adj. n.| 上颌的，上颌骨 |Relating to the jaw or jawbone, especially the upper jaw.  Pertaining to the maxilla.  Sometimes used as a noun to refer to the maxillary bone.| The maxillary sinus is located above the upper teeth. 上颌窦位于上牙上方。|The dentist specialized in maxillary surgery.  这位牙医专攻上颌外科手术。| 近义词: jawbone (n.), upper jaw (n.)| 反义词: mandibular|词性: adjective, noun

maxilla: /ˈmæksɪlə/ (US), /mækˈsɪlə/ (UK) | n. | 上颌骨 |The upper jawbone in vertebrates, usually bearing teeth.|  The maxilla forms part of the palate and nose. 上颌骨构成上颚和鼻子的一部分。|A fracture of the maxilla can be a serious injury. 上颌骨骨折可能是严重的损伤。| 近义词: jawbone (n.), upper jaw (n.)| 反义词: mandible|词性: noun
