overjoyfulness:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪfəlnəs/ | n. | 极度喜悦，狂喜 |The state of being extremely joyful; ecstatic happiness. | Her overjoyfulness at the news was infectious. 她听到这个消息欣喜若狂，很有感染力。|  The overjoyfulness he felt upon seeing his newborn daughter was indescribable.  看到刚出生的女儿，他感受到的喜悦难以言喻。 | 近义词：ecstasy, rapture, bliss, elation | 反义词：despair, misery, sorrow, sadness | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From over- + joyfulness, from joyful + -ness.  Ultimately from Old French joie.
