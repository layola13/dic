massagists:/məˈsɑːʒɪsts/
n.
按摩师，推拿师
A person who gives massages professionally.
例句：The masseuse expertly worked out the knots in my back. 按摩师熟练地解开了我背部的结节。

例句：Many athletes employ personal masseuses to help with muscle recovery. 许多运动员雇用私人按摩师来帮助肌肉恢复。

近义词：masseuse (female), masseur (male), therapist
反义词：(No direct antonym, but perhaps "patient" or "client" in the context of the massage interaction)
词性: Noun (plural)

massage:/məˈsɑːʒ/
n. vt.
按摩，推拿
The rubbing and kneading of muscles and joints of the body with the hands, especially to relieve tension or pain.
例句：I booked a massage at the spa. 我在水疗中心预订了按摩。

例句：She gave him a back massage. 她给他按摩了背部。

近义词：rubdown, manipulation
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性: Noun, Verb
