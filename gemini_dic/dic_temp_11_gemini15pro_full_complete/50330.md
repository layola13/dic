deplorers:/dɪˈplɔːrərz/ | n. | 哀叹者；悲叹者；痛惜者 | People who deplore something; those who express strong disapproval or regret. | The deplorers of the current political climate gathered to protest.  当前政治气候的哀叹者聚集起来抗议。| 近义词: mourners, lamenters, critics | 反义词:  approvers, supporters, celebrants | noun

deplorers:/dɪˈplɔːrərz/ | n. | 哀悼者；悲悼者 | People who mourn or grieve for something lost or someone deceased. | The deplorers of the fallen hero wept openly.  这位倒下的英雄的哀悼者公开哭泣。 | 近义词: mourners, grievers | 反义词: celebrants, revelers | noun
