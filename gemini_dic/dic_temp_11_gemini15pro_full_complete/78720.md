gigerium: /ɡɪˈdʒɪəriəm/
n.
gigerium (plural gigeria)
吉格利亚，吉格尔风格的艺术作品或设计
A style of biomechanical artwork and design, notably seen in the work of H.R. Giger.  It is characterized by a fusion of organic and mechanical elements, often with dark, surreal, and erotic undertones.
Gigerium is a term used to describe the distinct artistic style of H.R. Giger, characterized by its biomechanical and often unsettling imagery.
