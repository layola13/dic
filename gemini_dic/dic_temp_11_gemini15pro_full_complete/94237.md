imperilling: /ɪmˈpɛrɪlɪŋ/ /ɪmˈpɛrəlɪŋ/
verb
使处于危险之中; 危及
putting someone or something in danger of being harmed or damaged
The war is imperilling the lives of thousands of people. 这场战争危及着数千人的生命。

imperilling: /ɪmˈpɛrɪlɪŋ/ /ɪmˈpɛrəlɪŋ/
近义词: endangering, jeopardizing, risking, threatening
反义词: protecting, safeguarding, securing
词性: present participle of imperil
