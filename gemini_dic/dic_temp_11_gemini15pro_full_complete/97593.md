inhabited:/ɪnˈhæbɪtɪd/ | adj. |有人居住的；栖息的|Lived in; occupied by people or animals. |例句：The island is inhabited by a small community of fishermen.  这个岛上居住着一个小渔民社区。

例句：The dense forest is inhabited by various species of birds. 这片茂密的森林里栖息着各种鸟类。

|近义词：occupied, populated, settled|反义词：uninhabited, deserted, vacant|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Latin *inhabitatus, past participle of inhabitare (see inhabit).
