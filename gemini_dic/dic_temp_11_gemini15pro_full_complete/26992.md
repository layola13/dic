calculabilities:/ˌkælkjʊləˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可计算性；可推算性|The quality or state of being calculable; things that are capable of being calculated or estimated.  It refers to the ability to determine something through calculation or logical reasoning.|The calculabilities of the project's success are high, given the current market trends.  该项目的成功率很高，考虑到目前的市场趋势。|Synonym: predictability, estimability, computability|Antonym: unpredictability, uncertainty|noun
