hydrochloric:/ˌhaɪdrəˈklɔːrɪk/| adj.|盐酸的，氯化的|relating to or containing hydrochloric acid; consisting of hydrogen and chlorine|Hydrochloric acid is a strong acid. 盐酸是一种强酸。|近义词:muriatic (archaic)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the adjective. One might consider "alkaline" as an antonym in a chemical context, referring to a basic substance.)|词性:adjective
Etymology: hydro- + chlor- + -ic.  From hydro- "water" + chlor- "greenish-yellow," referring to chlorine, + -ic.
