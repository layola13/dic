boarders:/ˈbɔːrdərz/
n.
寄宿生，登船者，登机者
People who live and receive meals at a school, college, or other institution.  Also, those who board a ship or aircraft.
The school has accommodations for 50 boarders. 这所学校可以容纳50名寄宿生。
The boarders were asked to assemble in the dining hall. 寄宿生被要求到餐厅集合。

近义词:residents, lodgers, inmates (in some contexts), passengers
反义词:day students, commuters, locals


boarders:/ˈbɔːrdərz/
n.
边界，边境
The edge or boundary of something, often a country or region. (Less common usage)
The two countries share a long, mountainous boarders. 这两个国家共享漫长而多山边界。（不太常用）
They patrolled the boarders of their property. 他们巡逻了他们房产的边界。（不太常用）


近义词:borders, boundaries, limits, edges, frontiers
反义词:center, interior, middle
