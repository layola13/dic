arbalester: /ˈɑːrbəlɛstər/ | n. |弩手，十字弓射手 | A person who operates or shoots an arbalest (a type of crossbow). |  The arbalester took aim and fired a bolt at the target. 弩手瞄准目标，射出一支弩箭。

The king's arbalesters formed a crucial part of his army. 国王的弩手组成了他军队的重要组成部分。


| 近义词: crossbowman, archer | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-combatant") | 词性: noun
