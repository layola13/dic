aperitif-wines:/əˌpɛrɪˈtiːf waɪnz/| n.|开胃酒|Wines typically served before a meal to stimulate the appetite. These are often dry, light-bodied, and lower in alcohol content. They can include white wines, sparkling wines, or fortified wines like vermouth.|We enjoyed a glass of aperitif-wine before dinner. 我们在晚餐前享用了一杯开胃酒。|Synonyms: aperitifs, pre-dinner drinks|Antonyms: digestifs, after-dinner drinks|Noun
