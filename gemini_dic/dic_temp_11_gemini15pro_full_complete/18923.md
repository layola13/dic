bespattered:/bɪˈspætərd/
adjective
溅满的，斑驳的
covered with spots or splashes of something
The front of his shirt was bespattered with mud. 他的衬衫前面溅满了泥。
synonyms: splashed, splattered, spotted, speckled, stained
antonyms: clean, spotless, immaculate
词性: adjective

bespattered:/bɪˈspætərd/
verb
溅污，玷污
to cover with spots or splashes of something
The car was bespattered with mud after driving through the puddles. 开车穿过水坑后，汽车溅满了泥。
synonyms: splash, splatter, spot, speckle, stain
antonyms: clean, cleanse
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + spatter.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "all over," "thoroughly," or "to affect all over."  "Spatter" comes from the Middle Dutch "spatten" meaning "to splash."
