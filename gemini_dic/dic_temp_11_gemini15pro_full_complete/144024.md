outreigning:/ˌaʊtˈreɪnɪŋ/
verb
超越统治；比…统治时间更长
ruling longer than; exceeding in reign
King Louis XIV outreigned all other French monarchs. 路易十四的统治时间超过了所有其他法国君主。
近义词: outlast, surpass
反义词: be surpassed, be outlasted
词性: verb (动词)
