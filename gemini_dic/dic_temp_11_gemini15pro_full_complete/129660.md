non-deductible:/ˌnɒn dɪˈdʌktɪbl/ (US) /ˌnɒn dɪˈdʌktəbl/ (UK)| adj.|不可扣除的|Not allowed to be subtracted from gross income when calculating taxable income.|例句：The expenses for the trip were non-deductible.  这次旅行的费用是不可扣除的。|例句：Non-deductible expenses must be paid from after-tax income. 不可扣除的费用必须用税后收入支付。|近义词：non-allowable, unexpendable|反义词: deductible|词性:形容词

non-deductible:/ˌnɒn dɪˈdʌktɪbl/ (US) /ˌnɒn dɪˈdʌktəbl/ (UK)| n.|不可扣除项目|An expense that cannot be subtracted from gross income when calculating taxable income.|例句：Entertainment expenses are often considered a non-deductible.  招待费通常被认为是不可扣除的项目。|例句：The company listed several non-deductibles on their tax form.  公司在税表上列出了几项不可扣除的项目。|近义词：non-allowable expense|反义词: deduction|词性:名词
