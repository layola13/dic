amplexicaul:/ˌæmpləˈksikɔːl/
amplexicaul|adj.|抱茎的，茎抱的|Clasping the stem; said of a leaf or bract whose base lobes extend completely around the stem.|The plant's amplexicaul leaves gave it a distinctive appearance. 这种植物的抱茎叶使其具有独特的外观。|amplective, clasping|free|adjective
amplexicaul:/ˌæmpləˈksikɔːl/
amplexicaul|adj.|抱茎的，茎抱的| (of a leaf or bract) having lobes, auricles, or a sheathing base that clasps the stem. |The amplexicaul leaves of the plant were a key identifying feature.  这种植物的抱茎叶是关键的识别特征。|stem-clasping, embracing|free, separate|adjective
