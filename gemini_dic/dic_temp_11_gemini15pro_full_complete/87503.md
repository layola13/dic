heterochrony:/ˌhɛtərˈɒkrəni/ | n. | 异时性，异时发生 | An evolutionary change in the rate or timing of developmental events.  It can result in changes in the size, shape, or characteristics of an organism compared to its ancestors. |  Example 1: Heterochrony in human evolution is thought to have contributed to our large brain size. 人类进化中的异时性被认为促成了我们的大脑尺寸。

Example 2: The different beak shapes of Darwin's finches are partly due to heterochrony during development. 达尔文雀的不同喙形部分是由于发育过程中的异时性造成的。 | 近义词: allometry,  heterokairos | 反义词: isochrony | 词性: noun
