The police investigated the case of a nonconsenting individual being subjected to medical procedures. 警方调查了一起未经同意的人被进行医疗程序的案件。


近义词: unwilling, involuntary, forced, coerced | 反义词: consenting, willing, voluntary | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "consenting" (present participle of "consent," from Latin "consentire" - "to feel together," from "com-" (with, together) + "sentire" (to feel)).
