centralising:/ˈsɛntrəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
集中化，中央集权化
The act of bringing something under the control of a central authority.
The government is centralising control of education. 政府正在集中控制教育。
The company is centralising its operations in London.  这家公司正在将其业务集中在伦敦。
centralize, consolidate, unify
decentralize, distribute, disperse
v.
