handspring:/ˈhændsprɪŋ/| n.|手翻，翻筋斗|An acrobatic movement in which a person launches themselves forward and over, landing first on their hands and then on their feet.|She performed a perfect handspring on the mat. 她在垫子上做了一个完美的手翻。|近义词：flip, somersault, cartwheel|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "fall")|Noun
