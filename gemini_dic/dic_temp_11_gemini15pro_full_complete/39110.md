confronters:/kənˈfrʌntərz/
noun
对抗者，面对者
Those who confront someone or something, typically to challenge or address an issue.
The confronters demanded answers from the CEO about the company's environmental record.  对抗者要求首席执行官回答有关公司环境记录的问题。
The confronters staged a protest outside the government building.  对抗者在政府大楼外举行了抗议。
challengers, opponents, dissenters
supporters, allies, conformists
n.
