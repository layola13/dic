erythrism:/ˈɛrɪθrɪzəm/ | n. | 赤化 | An unusual reddish pigmentation of fur, feathers, hair, skin, eggs, or flowers.  A genetic mutation causes overproduction of reddish pigment and/or underproduction of darker pigments. |  The fox exhibited erythrism, with a striking reddish coat. 这只狐狸表现出赤化现象，有着醒目的红色皮毛。

The botanist noted erythrism in the normally yellow flowers. 植物学家注意到通常是黄色的花朵出现了赤化现象。 | 近义词: rufism | 反义词: albinism, melanism | 词性: noun

From Greek *erythrós* (red) + *-ismos* (forming nouns of condition).
