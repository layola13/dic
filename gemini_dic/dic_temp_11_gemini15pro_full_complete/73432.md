forelook:/fɔːrˈlʊk/ | v. | 预见，预测 | To foresee or anticipate; to look forward. |  He forelooked the coming storm and brought an umbrella. 他预见了即将到来的暴风雨，带了一把伞。

He forelooked the difficulties of the project. 他预见了项目的困难。 | 近义词: foresee, anticipate, predict, forecast | 反义词: disregard, ignore |词性: 动词
