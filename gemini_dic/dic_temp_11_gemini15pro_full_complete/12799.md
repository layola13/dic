assistances:/əˈsɪstənsɪz/
|n.|援助，帮助|The company offers financial assistance to students.  这家公司为学生提供经济援助。|
|例句：The teacher provided assistance to the struggling student. 老师给学习困难的学生提供了帮助。|
|例句：They received emergency assistance after the earthquake. 地震后他们得到了紧急援助。|
|近义词：help, support, aid, backing|
|反义词：hindrance, obstruction, opposition|
|词性：名词|

assistances (plural of assistance) comes from the Old French "assistance," derived from Late Latin "assistentia," based on the Latin verb "assistere" meaning "to stand by, support, or help."
