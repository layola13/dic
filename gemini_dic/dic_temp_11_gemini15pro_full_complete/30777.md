cestodean:/sesˈtoʊdiən/
adjective|绦虫的；绦虫纲的|Relating to or denoting the class Cestoda of parasitic flatworms, comprising the tapeworms.|Tapeworms are cestodean parasites that can infect humans and animals.|Synonym: tapeworm, cestode|Antonym: None|词性: adjective

cestodean:/sesˈtoʊdiən/
noun|绦虫；绦虫纲动物|A parasitic flatworm of the class Cestoda, typically having a long ribbon-like body with many segments.|The veterinarian identified the parasite as a cestodean.|Synonym: tapeworm, cestode|Antonym: None|词性: noun
