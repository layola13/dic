goodie:/ˈɡʊdi/| n.|好东西，糖果，赠品|A small item, often a sweet or toy, given as a gift or treat.|The children were given goodie bags at the end of the party. 孩子们在派对结束时得到了装满糖果的礼品袋。|Synonyms: treat, gift, present|Antonyms: baddie, undesirable|Noun
