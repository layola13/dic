mimographer:/ˈmɪmiəˌɡræfər/ /ˈmɪmiəˌɡrɑːfər/ | n. |油印机操作员；复印员|A person who operates a mimeograph machine, a duplicating machine that produces copies from a stencil. |例句：The mimographer ran off 50 copies of the newsletter. 油印机操作员印了50份通讯稿。

例句：The mimographer was responsible for distributing the copies to all the staff. 油印机操作员负责把复印件分发给所有员工。|近义词：duplicator operator, copyist|反义词: None (The role is becoming obsolete so direct antonyms are rare)|词性: Noun
