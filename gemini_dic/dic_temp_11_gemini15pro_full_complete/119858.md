mileposts:/ˈmaɪlˌpoʊsts/| n. |里程碑，里程标|A milepost is a marker, typically a post, placed at intervals along a road or railway to indicate distance. It can also metaphorically represent a significant stage or event in a journey, project, or life. |We passed several mileposts on our drive across the country. 我们在驾车穿越全国的过程中经过了几个里程碑。|The publication of her first novel was a major milepost in her career. 她第一部小说的出版是她职业生涯中的一个重要里程碑。|synonyms: milestone, landmark, marker, signpost|antonyms: N/A|noun
