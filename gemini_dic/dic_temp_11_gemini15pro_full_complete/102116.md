isotonicities:/ˌaɪsoʊtəˈnɪsətiz/ | n. | 等渗性；等张性 | The plural form of isotonicity, referring to the state of having the same osmotic pressure.  This term describes solutions having the same concentration of solutes as another solution, specifically relating to the effect on cells or tissues. |  The isotonicities of the different solutions were carefully measured before the experiment.  不同溶液的等渗性在实验前被仔细测量。| synonyms: osmotic balances,  osmotic equilibrium | antonyms: anisotonicities, hypertonicities, hypotonicities | noun
