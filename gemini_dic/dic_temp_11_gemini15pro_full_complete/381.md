abing:/ˈɑːbɪŋ/ | n. | 阿饼; 一种菲律宾烤薄饼 | A type of Filipino flatbread, often grilled and served with various toppings. | Example: I enjoyed a delicious abing with cheese and garlic. 我享用了一份美味的加奶酪和大蒜的阿饼。 | Example: Street vendors sell abing filled with sweet fillings for dessert. 街头小贩出售加甜馅的阿饼作为甜点。 | 近义词: flatbread, pancake | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps thick bread) | 词性: noun

词源: From Tagalog *abing*
