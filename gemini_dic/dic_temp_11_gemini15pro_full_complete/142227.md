ornithotomist:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒtəmɪst/ | n. | 鸟类解剖学家 | A person who dissects birds; one who studies the anatomy of birds. | The ornithotomist carefully examined the wing structure of the sparrow. 鸟类解剖学家仔细检查了麻雀的翅膀结构。|Synonym: bird anatomist | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bird conservationist" in a philosophical sense) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
