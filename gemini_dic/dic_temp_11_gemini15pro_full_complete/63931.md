epipodiale: /ˌɛpɪˈpəʊdiˌeɪl/ | n. | 附肢骨; 远端肢骨 |  A bone in the distal part of a tetrapod limb, derived from elements of the tarsus, carpus, or their digits.  Relating to or designating the epipodium, the distal segment of a vertebrate limb, comprising the metacarpus or metatarsus and the phalanges. | The epipodiale of the horse has evolved for swift running. 马的附肢骨已经进化成适合快速奔跑的形态。 | The bird's epipodiale allows for grasping and perching. 鸟类的附肢骨使其能够抓握和栖息。 |  近义词: distal limb bone | 反义词: proximal limb bone | 词性: noun
