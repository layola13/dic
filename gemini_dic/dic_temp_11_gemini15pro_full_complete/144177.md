outshrill:/ˌaʊtˈʃrɪl/
verb
胜过…的喊叫声；盖过…的喊叫声
to shout more loudly than someone or something else
例句：The protestors outshrilled the police sirens. 抗议者的喊叫声盖过了警笛声。

例句：She outshrilled her rivals at the rally. 她在集会上用喊叫声压倒了她的对手。

近义词：outcry, overshout, drown out
反义词：be drowned out, be outshouted
词性：v.
