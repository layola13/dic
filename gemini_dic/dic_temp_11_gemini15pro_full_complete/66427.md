exegesist:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒiːsɪst/| n. |注释者，圣经注释家|A scholar who carefully studies and explains texts, especially religious ones.  A person skilled in exegesis.|The exegesist offered a compelling interpretation of the ancient scripture.  这位注释家对古代经文提出了令人信服的解释。|近义词：interpreter, commentator, analyst, expounder|反义词:misinterpreter, distorter|noun
