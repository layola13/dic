imbenching:/ɪmˈbɛntʃɪŋ/
|n.| 就职; 入职;  就任; |The act of placing someone in a position of authority, especially a judge.  It can also refer to the official seating of a judge in court.|His imbenching as a Supreme Court justice was a momentous occasion. 他就任最高法院法官是一个重要的时刻。|近义词: inauguration, investiture, installation, seating|反义词: removal, dismissal|词性: noun
