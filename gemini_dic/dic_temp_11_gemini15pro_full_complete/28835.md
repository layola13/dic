Carpathian: /kɑːrˈpeɪθiən/ | adj. n.|  喀尔巴阡山脉的；喀尔巴阡山脉 | Relating to the Carpathian Mountains, a mountain range in central and eastern Europe.  Also, can refer to the mountains themselves. | The Carpathian Mountains are home to a diverse range of wildlife. 喀尔巴阡山脉是各种野生动物的家园。| The Carpathian region is known for its stunning scenery. 喀尔巴阡地区以其迷人的景色而闻名。 | synonym: mountainous, Transylvanian (for the region) | antonym: flat, lowland | adjective, noun

Etymology: From German *Karpathen, from Latin *Carpates, likely derived from the name of a Dacian tribe, possibly meaning "rocky" or "cliff."
