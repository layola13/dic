akene:/eɪˈkiːn/| n.|瘦果；单粒果|A small, dry, one-seeded fruit that does not open to release the seed.  A type of simple dry fruit that develops from a single carpel and does not split open at maturity. It is typical of the buttercup family (Ranunculaceae).|The dandelion produces achenes that are dispersed by the wind.|近义词:samara, cypsela|反义词:capsule, follicle|noun
