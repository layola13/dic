nicknaming:/ˈnɪkˌneɪmɪŋ/
n.
给…起绰号; 绰号命名
the act of giving someone a nickname.
My friends and I have a habit of nicknaming each other.  我和我的朋友们习惯于互相起绰号。
He enjoys nicknaming his colleagues based on their quirks. 他喜欢根据同事们的怪癖给他们起绰号。
近义词:  name-calling, labeling
反义词:  (无直接反义词, 可考虑 using full names, formal address)
