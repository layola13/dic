misproduce:/ˌmɪsprəˈdjuːs/ /ˌmɪsprəˈduːs/
verb
生产不良，生产错误，制造出低劣产品
To produce badly, incorrectly, or in a way that results in an inferior product.
The factory misproduced a batch of cars with faulty brakes. 这家工厂生产了一批刹车有缺陷的汽车。
The play was misproduced and poorly received by critics.  这部戏剧制作得很差，受到了评论家的差评。

近义词: mismanufacture, bungle, mishandle
反义词: produce, manufacture, create
词性: verb
