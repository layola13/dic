adverse:/ˈædvɜːrs/ (US), /ˈædvɜːs/ (UK)| adj.|不利的，有害的，相反的|Preventing success or development; harmful; unfavorable.|例句：Adverse weather conditions forced the cancellation of the flight.  恶劣的天气条件迫使航班取消。|例句：He had an adverse reaction to the medication. 他对药物有不良反应。|近义词：unfavorable, detrimental, harmful, negative, hostile|反义词：favorable, beneficial, advantageous, positive|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *adversus* "turned towards, opposite, unfavorable," past participle of *advertere* "to turn toward," from *ad-* "to" + *vertere* "to turn."
