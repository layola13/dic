dispatch:/dɪˈspætʃ/ (US), /dɪˈspætʃ/ (UK)| n. v.|派遣，发送；急件，快信；新闻报道，消息；处理，解决|He dispatched the letter immediately. 他立即寄出了信。|近义词：send, transmit, forward, deal with, handle, kill|反义词: receive, hold, retain|词性:noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French despescher "hasten, expedite," from des- "out, away" + pescher "impede, hinder."  The sense evolution appears to be from "remove an impediment" to "expedite."
