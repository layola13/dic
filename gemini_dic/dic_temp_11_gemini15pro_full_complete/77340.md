gaucho:/ˈɡaʊtʃoʊ/ | n. |  南美牛仔；牧童 | A skilled horseman, adept with the lasso, bolas, and knife, typically of mixed Spanish and Indian descent, who herded cattle in the pampas of South America.  |  The gaucho skillfully herded the cattle across the pampas.  南美牛仔熟练地将牛群赶过潘帕斯草原。

The gaucho's life was one of freedom and adventure. 南美牛仔的生活充满了自由和冒险。| 近义词：cowboy, herdsman, horseman | 反义词: city-dweller | 词性: noun
