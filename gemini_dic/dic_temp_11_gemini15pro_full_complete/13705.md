attrition:/əˈtrɪʃən/ | n. | 消耗；磨损；损耗；人员自然缩减 |  The gradual reduction in the number of employees through retirement, resignation, or other reasons, rather than through layoffs.  |  The company is trying to avoid layoffs and is relying on attrition to reduce staff.  这家公司正试图避免裁员，依靠自然减员来减少员工。| synonyms: erosion, weakening, depletion, decline | antonyms: growth, increase, strengthening, augmentation | noun

attrition:/əˈtrɪʃən/ | n. |  摩擦；磨擦 | The process of wearing something down by rubbing it. | The attrition of the stone tools made them less effective. 石器的磨损使得它们效率降低。 | synonyms: abrasion, friction, chafing, grinding | antonyms:  polishing, smoothing | noun
