cotransfers:/koʊˈtrænsfɜːrz/
|n.|共同转移; 协同转移|The cotransfers of genes and plasmids are common in bacteria. 基因和质粒的共同转移在细菌中很常见。|例句：The study investigated the cotransfers of antibiotic resistance genes.  这项研究调查了抗生素抗性基因的共同转移。

例句：Researchers observed cotransfers of genetic material between different bacterial species. 研究人员观察到不同细菌种类之间遗传物质的共同转移。|近义词：joint transfers, simultaneous transfers|反义词: independent transfers|词性: 名词 (plural noun)
