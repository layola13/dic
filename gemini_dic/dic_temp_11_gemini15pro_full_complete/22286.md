bong:/bɒŋ/ (UK), /bɑːŋ/ (US)| n. v.|水烟筒; 钟声;发出钟声|A water pipe used for smoking cannabis or other drugs; a deep resonant sound, like that of a large bell; to make a deep resonant sound.|

例句：
The bong made a deep resonant sound. 水烟筒发出低沉的共鸣声。

He took a long drag from the bong. 他深深地吸了一口大麻烟。


近义词：(for n.) pipe, water pipe, hookah; (for v.) chime, toll, clang
反义词: (for v.) silence
词性: noun, verb


bong:/bɒŋ/ (UK), /bɑːŋ/ (US)| v.|发出钟声|To make a deep resonant sound, like that of a large bell.|

例句：

The bell bonged loudly, announcing the start of the service. 钟声响亮地响起，宣布仪式开始。


近义词：chime, toll, clang
