kinder:/ˈkɪndər/ (US), /ˈkaɪndər/ (UK)| adj.|更友善的；更亲切的；更和蔼的|Of a sympathetic or helpful nature; showing or motivated by sympathy, understanding, and generosity.|She's always been very kind to me. 她一直对我非常友善。|近义词：nicer, gentler, friendlier|反义词：crueler, meaner, harsher|词性:形容词

kinder:/ˈkɪndər/ (US), /ˈkaɪndər/ (UK)| n.|幼儿园（尤用于德国、奥地利和瑞士）|A preschool for children.|Her daughter attends a local kinder. 她的女儿在当地一家幼儿园上学。|近义词：preschool, nursery school, kindergarten|反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可用 elementary school 表示小学来对比)|词性:名词
