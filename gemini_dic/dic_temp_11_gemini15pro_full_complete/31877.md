cheerless:/ˈtʃɪərlɪs/| adj.|阴郁的，无欢笑的，沉闷的|Lacking cheer; gloomy; depressing.|The cheerless weather reflected my mood.  阴沉的天气反映了我的心情。|The room was cheerless and empty. 房间里空荡荡的，毫无生气。|近义词：gloomy, bleak, dreary, dismal, somber|反义词：cheerful, bright, happy, joyful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From cheer + -less.  Cheer comes from Old French chiere "face, countenance," from Late Latin cara.
