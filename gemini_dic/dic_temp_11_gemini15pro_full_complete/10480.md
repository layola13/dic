n. |涂抹器，敷贴器|A device used to apply something, such as paint, medicine, or cosmetics.|She used a cotton applicator to apply the antiseptic. 她用棉签涂抹了消毒剂。|
近义词:tools, instruments, devices
反义词:|None|
词性:Noun


applicators:/ˈæplɪˌkeɪtərz/
n. |施用者，应用者|Someone who applies something.|The applicators of the new technology will benefit greatly. 新技术的应用者将受益匪浅。|
近义词:users, employers, implementers
反义词:|None|
词性:Noun


词源: From the verb "apply," which comes from the Late Latin "applicare," meaning "to fold or attach to."  The "-ator" suffix forms nouns denoting an agent or instrument.
