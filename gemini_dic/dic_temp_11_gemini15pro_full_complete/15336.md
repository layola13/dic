backspin:/ˈbækˌspɪn/| n.|回旋，倒旋|A backward rotation of a ball.  The ball spins in the opposite direction to its direction of travel.|The golfer put backspin on the ball to stop it quickly on the green.  高尔夫球手给球加了回旋，使其在果岭上迅速停止。|近义词：underspin|反义词：topspin|词性:noun
