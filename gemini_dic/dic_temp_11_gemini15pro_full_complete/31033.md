chamber:/ˈtʃeɪmbər/ (US), /ˈtʃeɪmbə/ (UK)| n.|房间，室；会议厅；腔，膛|A room, especially a bedroom or a private room used for a special purpose. Also, a hall used for formal meetings of a legislative or other official body, or a closed space in a machine or body.|

例句：
The members met in the council chamber. 
议员们在会议厅会面。

The heart has four chambers.
心脏有四个腔室。

近义词：room, hall, compartment, cavity
反义词:exterior, outside

词性:noun


chamber:/ˈtʃeɪmbər/ (US), /ˈtʃeɪmbə/ (UK)| v.|把…关进房间；装填（弹药）|To place or enclose in a chamber.|

例句：
He chambered a round in the rifle.
他把子弹装进了步枪。

The prisoner was chambered in a small cell.
囚犯被关在一个小牢房里。

近义词：confine, enclose
反义词:release, liberate

词性:verb
