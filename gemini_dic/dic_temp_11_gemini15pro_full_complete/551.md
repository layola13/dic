abort:/əˈbɔːrt/ (US) /əˈbɔːt/ (UK)| v. n.|中止，夭折，流产|To stop a process or activity before it is finished; to end a pregnancy deliberately.|例句：The mission had to be aborted due to bad weather.  由于天气恶劣，任务不得不中止。|近义词：cancel, terminate, halt, stop, miscarry|反义词:continue, proceed, complete|词性:动词

abort:/əˈbɔːrt/ (US) /əˈbɔːt/ (UK)| n.|中止，失败|The act of aborting something.|例句：The launch was an abort. 发射失败了。|近义词：failure, termination|反义词:success, completion|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abortus*, past participle of *aborīrī* "to miscarry, pass away, disappear," from *ab* "away, from" + *orīrī* "to arise, originate, be born."
