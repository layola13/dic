inserters:/ɪnˈsɜːrtərz/ | n. | 插入器，插入装置 |A device or mechanism used for inserting something, such as a component into a machine or a letter into an envelope. | The factory uses automatic inserters to place components on circuit boards. 工厂使用自动插入器将元件放置在电路板上。

The inserters quickly folded and stuffed the leaflets into envelopes.  插入器快速地将传单折叠并塞入信封中。 | synonyms:  insertion device,  insertion tool,  feeding mechanism | antonyms: extractor, remover | noun
