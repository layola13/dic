imbalances:/ɪmˈbælənsɪz/ | n. |不平衡，失衡|lack of balance; a state in which opposing forces or influences are not equal|The economic imbalances between the two countries led to trade tensions. 这两个国家之间的经济失衡导致了贸易紧张。|近义词：disparity, inequality, disproportion, asymmetry|反义词：balance, equilibrium, stability|noun
