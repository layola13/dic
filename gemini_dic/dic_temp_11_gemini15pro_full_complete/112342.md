lori:/ˈlɔːri/ (US), /ˈlɔːri/ (UK)| n.|吸蜜鹦鹉, 鹦鹉的一种|A kind of parrot, often brightly coloured, found in Australia, eastern Indonesia, Papua New Guinea, and some Pacific islands, characterized by its specialized brush-tipped tongue for feeding on nectar and pollen.|The lori is a beautiful bird with vibrant plumage.|近义词：parrot, parakeet|反义词:|名词
