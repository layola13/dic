destitution:/ˌdɛstɪˈtjuːʃən/
n.|贫困；穷困潦倒|The earthquake left thousands of people in utter destitution. 地震使成千上万的人陷入极度贫困。|Synonyms: poverty, impoverishment, indigence, penury|Antonyms: wealth, affluence, prosperity|noun

destitution:/ˌdɛstɪˈtuːʃən/
n.|赤贫；穷途末路|Many families were reduced to destitution during the war. 战争期间，许多家庭陷入赤贫。|Synonyms: impoverishment, pauperism, beggary|Antonyms:  opulence, richness, comfort|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *destitutionem, from Latin destitutus (“abandoned, forsaken”), past participle of destituere (“to abandon, forsake”), from de- (“down, away from”) + statuere (“to set, place, establish”).
