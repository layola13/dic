blueprints:/ˈbluːprɪnts/ | n. | 蓝图，设计图；方案 | a detailed plan or drawing that shows how something is to be made or built; a plan for achieving something |  The architects presented the blueprints for the new stadium. 建筑师们展示了新体育场的蓝图。| synonyms: plan, design, scheme, draft | antonyms: improvisation,  extemporization | noun
