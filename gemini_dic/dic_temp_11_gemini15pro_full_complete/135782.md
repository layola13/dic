nonprogressive:/ˌnɒnprəˈɡrɛsɪv/ | adj. | 非进行性的，非持续性的 | Not able to be used in the progressive (continuous) tenses.  Describing verbs that do not usually have a continuous form. |  The verb "know" is nonprogressive.  "知道"这个动词是非进行性的。

| 近义词: stative | 反义词: progressive, dynamic | 词性: adjective


nonprogressive:/ˌnɑːnprəˈɡrɛsɪv/ | adj. | 非进行性的，非持续性的 | Not able to be used in the progressive (continuous) tenses.  Describing verbs that do not usually have a continuous form. |  I own a car. (Not *I am owning a car.*) 我拥有一辆车。（不能说“我正在拥有一辆车”）

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I understand the concept. 我理解这个概念。
He likes spicy food. 他喜欢辛辣的食物。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):  Not typically used with nonprogressive verbs.  非进行性动词通常不用于现在进行时。

I have known him for years. 我认识他很多年了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):  Not typically used with nonprogressive verbs. 非进行性动词通常不用于现在完成进行时。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous): Not typically used with nonprogressive verbs. 非进行性动词通常不用于过去完成进行时。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):

PHRASE->

what about = ...怎么样 (This works with nonprogressive verbs.  e.g., What about his opinion? 他的意见怎么样？)
what if = 如果...会怎么样 (This can be used with nonprogressive verbs in hypothetical situations. e.g., What if she disagrees? 如果她不同意怎么办？)
no matter what = 无论如何 (Can be used with nonprogressive verbs. e.g.,  No matter what, I believe her. 无论如何，我相信她。)
what's more = 而且；更重要的是 (Can introduce a clause with a nonprogressive verb. e.g.,  He's intelligent, and what's more, he understands the complexities of the situation. 他很聪明，而且，他了解情况的复杂性。)
so what = 那又怎样 (Can be a response to a statement with a nonprogressive verb. e.g., "He doesn't like it." "So what?" “他不喜欢。”“那又怎样？”)
