obligingly:/əˈblaɪdʒɪŋli/
adverb|殷勤地，乐于助人地|In a way that shows a willingness to do favors or help others.
|He obligingly carried her suitcase up the stairs.|他殷勤地帮她把行李箱搬上楼。
|She obligingly offered to drive me to the airport.|她乐于助人地提出开车送我去机场。
|kindly, helpfully, graciously|reluctantly, unwillingly|adverb
