haughty:/ˈhɔːti/ | adj. | 傲慢的，高傲的 | Arrogantly superior and disdainful.  Having or showing arrogant superiority to and disdain of those one views as unworthy. |  She gave him a haughty look. 她傲慢地看了他一眼。|  例句：He dismissed their objections with a haughty wave of his hand. 他傲慢地挥了挥手，驳回了他们的反对意见。| 近义词: arrogant, disdainful, supercilious, snobbish | 反义词: humble, modest, unassuming | 词性:形容词
