overhastens:/ˌoʊvərˈheɪsənz/| v. |加速；催促；过分催促|To hasten excessively; to cause something to happen too quickly.|His ambition overhastened his downfall. 他的野心加速了他的垮台。|近义词：rush, precipitate, accelerate|反义词:delay, hinder, decelerate|词性:动词
Etymology: From "over-" (excessive) + "hasten" (to hurry).  "Hasten" comes from Middle English "hasten," from Old Norse "hasta."
