nonattenders:/ˌnɑːnəˈtɛndərz/
n.
未出席者；缺席者
People who do not attend a particular event or meeting.
Many nonattenders cited illness as the reason for their absence. 许多未出席者以生病为由缺席。
absentees, no-shows
attendees, participants
