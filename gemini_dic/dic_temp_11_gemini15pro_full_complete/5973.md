ambulation:/ˌæmbjuˈleɪʃn/| n. |行走，移动|The act of walking or moving about.|The therapist encouraged early ambulation after the injury.  治疗师鼓励伤后尽早下地活动。|walking, movement, locomotion|immobility, confinement|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ambulat-, past participle stem of ambulare "to walk," from ambire "to go around," from ambi- "around" + ire "to go."
