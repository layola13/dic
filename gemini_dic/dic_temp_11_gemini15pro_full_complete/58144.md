dross:/drɒs/| n. |浮渣，糟粕，废物|Waste matter; refuse.|The smelting process separates the metal from the dross.|近义词：scum, refuse, waste, impurities|反义词:essence, valuables|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *dros, from Old English *drōs (“dregs, lees”), from Proto-Germanic *drōsą (“dregs, filth”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrewh₁- (“to become muddy, turbid”). Cognate with Dutch droes (“dregs, grounds, sediment”), German Drusen (“dregs, grounds”), Icelandic dras (“dregs, refuse”).
