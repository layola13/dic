browdyng: /ˈbraʊdɪŋ/
n.
绣花，刺绣
Embroidery, the art or handicraft of decorating fabric or other materials with needlework.
She spent hours browdyng a beautiful tapestry. 她花了几个小时绣了一幅美丽的挂毯。
近义词: embroidery, needlework, stitching
反义词: None


词性: noun (名词)
