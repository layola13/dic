bollywood: /ˈbɑːliˌwʊd/ | n. |宝莱坞，印度孟买的电影业|A Hindi-language film industry based in Mumbai, India, known for its musicals, melodramas, and action films.  It is a portmanteau of Bombay (the former name of Mumbai) and Hollywood. |India's Bollywood produces hundreds of films every year. 印度的宝莱坞每年制作数百部电影。|The new Bollywood romance is a box-office hit.  这部新的宝莱坞爱情片是票房热门。|近义词: Indian cinema, Hindi cinema |反义词: None |词性:noun
