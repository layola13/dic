aurantiaceous:/ɔːˌrænˈteɪʃəs/
adjective
橙色的，橘红色的
Of the color orange, typically the shade resembling that of the fruit of an orange tree.
The setting sun painted the sky in aurantiaceous hues.  落日将天空染成了橙色。
The aurantiaceous butterfly flitted among the flowers.  橙色的蝴蝶在花丛中飞舞。
近义词:orange, tangerine, reddish-orange
反义词:azure, cerulean, indigo
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *aurantiāceus, from aurantium (“orange (fruit)”).
