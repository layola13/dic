FAQ: /ˌɛf eɪ ˈkjuː/ | n. | 常见问题；常见问题解答 | Frequently Asked Question.  A question that is asked often.  It is less common to use FAQ in the singular. |  One FAQ we receive is about our return policy. 我们收到的一个常见问题是关于我们的退货政策。| synonyms: common question, frequent question, recurring question | antonyms: N/A | noun


Etymology:  FAQ is an initialism, meaning it is formed from the first letter of each word in the phrase "Frequently Asked Questions." It has become so common that it is often written in lowercase (faqs) and is sometimes even used as a singular noun (FAQ).
