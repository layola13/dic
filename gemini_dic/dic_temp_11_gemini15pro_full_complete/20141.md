bipeltate:/baɪˈpɛlteɪt/ | adj. | 双盾形的；两面有盾状附属物的 | Having two shield-shaped structures or attachments, often referring to the shape of a leaf or other plant part where the petiole attaches to the blade, not at the margin, but towards the centre. |  Example: The water lily has bipeltate leaves. 睡莲有双盾形的叶子。
Example: Some species of nasturtium exhibit bipeltate foliage.  某些种类的旱金莲展示出双盾形的叶子。 | Near synonyms: peltate, shield-shaped | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps non-peltate or lacking a petiole) | word type: adjective
