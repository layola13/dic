needs-must:/ˌniːdz ˈmʌst/ | adv. | 必需；不得不 | Indicates compulsion or inevitability, often due to circumstances.  Implies a reluctant acceptance of necessity. | Example: Needs-must I go, though I'd rather stay. 虽然我宁愿留下，但我不得不走。| Example:  The car broke down, so needs-must we walk. 车坏了，所以我们不得不步行。| Synonym: necessarily, inevitably, perforce | Antonym: optionally, unnecessarily | adverb

needs-must:/ˌniːdz ˈmʌst/ | n. | 必然性；不可避免性 | The state or condition of being necessary or unavoidable. | Example: The needs-must of the situation forced her hand.  形势的必然性迫使她采取行动。| Example:  There's a certain needs-must about paying taxes.  缴税有一定的必然性。 | Synonym: necessity, inevitability, compulsion | Antonym: option, choice | noun
