nondecisiveness:/ˌnɒndɪˈsaɪsɪvnəs/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈsaɪsɪvnəs/
n.
优柔寡断，犹豫不决
Lack of decisiveness; inability to make decisions.
His nondecisiveness cost him the election. 他的优柔寡断让他输掉了选举。
近义词：indecision, hesitancy, vacillation
反义词：decisiveness, determination, resolution
词性：名词
