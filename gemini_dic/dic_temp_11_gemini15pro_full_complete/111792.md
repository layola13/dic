lofting:/ˈlɒftɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈlɔːftɪŋ/ (US)| n. |阁楼搭建;  高尔夫球高飞球; 放样|The act or process of building a loft in a house or other building;  In golf, a shot that sends the ball high in the air;  A technique used in design and manufacturing to create a three-dimensional object from a series of two-dimensional cross-sections.|例句：They finished lofting the bedroom to create more space. 他们完成了卧室的阁楼搭建以创造更多空间。

例句：His lofting shot landed perfectly on the green. 他的高飞球完美地落在了果岭上。


近义词：garreting (for building a loft), pitching (for golf)|反义词: None|词性:noun


lofting:/ˈlɒftɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈlɔːftɪŋ/ (US)| v. |搭建阁楼; 打高飞球; 放样|To build a loft; To hit a golf ball high in the air; To create a three-dimensional object from a series of two-dimensional cross-sections.|例句：They are lofting the attic to make an extra bedroom.  他们正在搭建阁楼以建造一个额外的卧室。

例句：He lofted the ball over the trees and onto the fairway. 他把球高高地打过了树，落在了球道上。

近义词：constructing, erecting (for building); chipping, pitching (for golf)|反义词: None|词性:verb
