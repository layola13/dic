acanthopterygious: /əˌkænθɒpˈtɛrɪdʒɪəs/
adjective
棘鳍鱼类的；属于棘鳍目鱼类的
Relating to or belonging to the Acanthopterygii, a large group of bony fishes characterized by having spiny rays in their fins.
例句：The perch is an acanthopterygious fish.  河鲈是一种棘鳍鱼。

例句：Acanthopterygious fishes are the dominant group in most marine environments. 棘鳍鱼类是大多数海洋环境中的优势群体。

近义词：spiny-finned
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps soft-finned could be considered in a loose sense)
词性: adjective
