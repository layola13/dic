embase:/ˈembɛɪs/ |n.|数据库，文献库|A database containing biomedical and pharmacological abstracts and citations, primarily from European journals.  It's used by researchers, healthcare professionals, and others to find relevant literature in these fields.| Researchers use Embase to find articles related to their studies. 研究人员使用Embase查找与他们研究相关的文章。|Synonyms: database, bibliographic database, MEDLINE (partially), Scopus (partially)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

embase:/əmˈbeɪs/ |v. (rare, archaic, often used with "in")|放置于底座,嵌入|To set or fix firmly in a surrounding mass; to embed.| The jewel was embased in gold. 这颗宝石被嵌入黄金中。|Synonyms: embed, encase, implant, insert|Antonyms: extract, remove, unearth|Verb
