edgily:/ˈɛdʒɪli/
adjective
紧张不安地；焦躁地
In a nervous or irritable way.
She tapped her foot edgily, waiting for the news. 她紧张不安地敲着脚，等待着消息。
He laughed edgily, clearly uncomfortable with the situation.  他不安地笑了笑，显然对这种情况感到不舒服。
Synonym: nervously, anxiously, irritably, tensely
Antonym: calmly, serenely, peacefully
词性: adjective
