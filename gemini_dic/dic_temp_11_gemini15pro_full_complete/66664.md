exobiology:/ˌɛksoʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 外空生物学；宇宙生物学 | The study of life beyond Earth, encompassing the search for extraterrestrial life and the effects of extraterrestrial environments on living organisms. | Exobiology research focuses on the possibility of life on Mars. 外空生物学研究的重点是火星上存在生命的可能性。 |  Exobiology explores the potential for life on other planets. 外空生物学探索其他行星上存在生命的可能性。 |  astrobiology, bioastronomy |  - | noun
