DVD-ROM: /ˌdiː viː ˈdiː rɒm/ (British) /ˌdiː viː ˈdiː rɑːm/ (American) | n. | 数字多功能光盘只读存储器 |  A type of optical disc that can store large amounts of data, typically used for software, movies, and other multimedia files.  The data can be read but not written or erased. | I installed the software from the DVD-ROM. 我从DVD-ROM安装了软件。 | The movie came on a DVD-ROM. 这部电影装在一个DVD-ROM里。 | 近义词: CD-ROM, Blu-ray Disc | 反义词: DVD-RAM, DVD-RW, DVD+RW | 词性: noun
