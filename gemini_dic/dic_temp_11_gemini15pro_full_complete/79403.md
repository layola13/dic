glimmery:/ˈɡlɪməri/| adj.|微光的，闪烁的|Emitting a faint, unsteady light; shimmering. |The lake had a glimmery surface in the moonlight. 月光下，湖面波光粼粼。|The glimmery lights of the city twinkled in the distance. 远处城市的灯光闪烁。|近义词：shimmering, sparkling, twinkling, glistening|反义词：dull, dark, dim|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From glimmer + -y.  "glimmer" comes from Middle English *glimeren, frequentative of glimen "to shine," of uncertain origin.
