antiobesity:/ˌæntiːoʊˈbiːsəti/
adjective
抗肥胖的
Relating to or intended to prevent or reduce obesity.
The doctor recommended an antiobesity drug to the patient. 医生向病人推荐了一种抗肥胖药物。
New research is focusing on antiobesity strategies that involve lifestyle changes. 新的研究集中在涉及生活方式改变的抗肥胖策略上。
近义词: weight-loss, slimming
反义词: obesogenic


词性: adjective

词源: anti- (against) + obesity (the state of being obese)
