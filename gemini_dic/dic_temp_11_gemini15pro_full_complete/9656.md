antisymmetries:/ˌæntiˈsɪmətriz/| n. | 反对称性，反对称 |  The absence or opposite of symmetry; the property of being antisymmetric.  A relation is antisymmetric if whenever (a, b) and (b, a) are both in the relation, then a = b. | The mathematical concept of antisymmetries is crucial in understanding certain physical phenomena.  数学上的反对称性概念对于理解某些物理现象至关重要。|Near synonyms: asymmetry, nonsymmetry | Antonyms: symmetry | noun
