The inconvertibleness of the currency caused problems for international trade.  这种货币的不可兑换性给国际贸易造成了问题。

The inconvertibleness of his beliefs made it difficult to reason with him. 他的信念不可动摇，很难和他讲道理。

The inconvertibleness of the local currency made it hard for tourists to spend money.  当地货币的不可兑换性使得游客很难花钱。


近义词：immutability, unchangeableness, inflexibility
反义词：convertibility, changeability, flexibility
词性: noun
