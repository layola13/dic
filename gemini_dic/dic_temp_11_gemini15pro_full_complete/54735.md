disparaging: /dɪˈspærɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
贬低的，轻蔑的
expressing a negative opinion of someone or something, suggesting that they are of little value or importance
He made some disparaging remarks about her appearance. 他对她的外貌做了一些贬低的评论。
近义词: derogatory, belittling, denigrating, slighting, deprecating, contemptuous
反义词: complimentary, flattering, praising, laudatory
词性: adjective
