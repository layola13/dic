anti-polishes:/ˌæntiˈpɒlɪʃɪz/
noun
去光剂，磨光剂的反义词;  指使表面粗糙或暗淡的物质或行为
Substances or actions that roughen or dull a surface, the opposite of polishes.
The artist used anti-polishes to create a matte texture on the sculpture.  这位艺术家使用去光剂在雕塑上创造了一种哑光质感。
The weathering process acted as an anti-polish on the stones. 风化过程就像石头上的去光剂一样。
synonyms: abrasives, dulling agents, matting agents
antonyms: polishes, glosses, shines
n.
