inlayer:/ˈɪnˌleɪər/
n.
内层;夹层
A layer situated between or within other layers.
The protective inlayer of the clothing kept the wearer warm.  这件衣服的保护内层使穿着者保持温暖。
inner layer, lining, interlayer
outer layer, surface
noun
