bucolic: /bjuːˈkɒlɪk/ /buːˈkɑːlɪk/
adjective
田园的；乡村的；牧歌的
Relating to the pleasant aspects of the countryside and country life.
例句：The painting depicts a bucolic scene of shepherds tending their flocks in a lush green meadow. 这幅画描绘了牧羊人在郁郁葱葱的草地上放牧的田园风光。

例句：They longed for the bucolic simplicity of their childhood summers in the countryside. 他们渴望回到童年夏天在乡下那种田园般的简朴生活。

近义词：pastoral, rustic, idyllic, rural
反义词：urban, metropolitan, industrial
词性：adjective
