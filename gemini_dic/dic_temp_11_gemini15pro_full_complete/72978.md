foolhardihood:/ˈfuːlhɑːrdɪhʊd/| n.|鲁莽；蛮勇|Reckless boldness; foolish daring; a rash or reckless act.  It implies a disregard for danger or consequences.|He showed foolhardihood in climbing the mountain without any equipment. 他在没有装备的情况下攀登这座山，表现出十足的鲁莽。|近义词：recklessness, rashness, temerity, audacity|反义词：caution, prudence, circumspection|词性:noun
