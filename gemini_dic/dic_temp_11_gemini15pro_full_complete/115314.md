mannerliness:/ˈmænərlɪnɪs/| n.|礼貌；彬彬有礼|Politeness and good manners; courteous behavior.|Her mannerliness and grace impressed everyone at the dinner party. 她的礼貌和优雅给晚宴上的每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|Example: His mannerliness made him a popular guest. 他的彬彬有礼使他成为受欢迎的客人。|Synonyms: civility, courtesy, politeness, good manners|Antonyms: rudeness, impoliteness, incivility, discourtesy|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mannerly + -ness.  Mannerly from manner + -ly. Manner from Old French maniere, from Latin manuarius "of or belonging to the hand," from manus "hand."
