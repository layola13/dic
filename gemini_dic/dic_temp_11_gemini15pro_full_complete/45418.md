cupreous:/ˈkjuːpriəs/| adj.|含铜的；铜色的；像铜的|Containing or resembling copper, especially in color.|The mineral displayed a cupreous sheen.  这种矿物呈现出铜的光泽。|The solution turned a cupreous hue. 溶液变成了铜色。|Synonyms: coppery, copper-colored, brassy|Antonyms:  silvery, golden|adjective
