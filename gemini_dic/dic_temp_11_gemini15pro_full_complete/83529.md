hadit:/ˈhædɪt/| v. |拥有过；曾经有|Had is the past tense and past participle of "have." It indicates possession or ownership in the past. It is also used as an auxiliary verb to form the past perfect tense. |例句：I had a dog when I was a child. 我小时候有一条狗。


He had already left when I arrived. 当我到达时，他已经离开了。|近义词：owned, possessed, experienced|反义词：lacked|词性:verb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Do you have a car? = 你有车吗？
They have a meeting every Monday. = 他们每周一都开会。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am having a good time. = 我玩得很开心。
She is having lunch with her friends. = 她正在和朋友们吃午饭。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have had this car for five years. = 我拥有这辆车五年了。
They have had many successes. = 他们取得了很多成功。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been having trouble sleeping lately. = 我最近一直有睡眠问题。
He has been having a lot of fun. = 他一直玩得很开心。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I had a bicycle when I was young. = 我小时候有一辆自行车。
She had a headache yesterday. = 她昨天头疼。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was having dinner when you called. = 你打电话的时候我正在吃晚饭。
They were having a party last night. = 他们昨晚在聚会。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already had breakfast before you arrived. = 你来之前我已经吃过早餐了。
She had finished her work before she left. = 她离开之前已经完成了工作。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been having nightmares for weeks before I saw a doctor. = 我看医生前几周一直做噩梦。
They had been having discussions for months before they reached an agreement. = 他们在达成协议之前已经讨论了好几个月。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will have a new phone next week. = 我下周将会有一个新手机。
They will have a meeting tomorrow. = 他们明天将要开会。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be having dinner with my family tonight. = 今晚我将和我的家人一起吃晚饭。
She will be having a party next weekend. = 她下周末将要举行一个聚会。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have had this car for ten years. = 到明年，我将拥有这辆车十年了。
By the end of the month, they will have completed the project. = 到月底，他们将完成这个项目。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been living here for five years. = 到明年，我将已经在这里住了五年了。 (注意：这里用living更合适, 因为“拥有居住”这个表达不自然)
By the time she graduates, she will have been studying English for six years. = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习英语六年了。

虚拟语气:
If I had had more time, I would have gone to the party. = 如果我有更多的时间，我就会去参加聚会了。
I wish I had had the chance to talk to him. = 我希望我有机会和他谈谈。

被动语态: (Had is not typically used in the passive voice in this sense.  The passive voice is more often used with the verb "have" in the sense of "experience" something. )
The party was had by everyone. (This is grammatically correct, but sounds unnatural.)  大家开了个派对。(更自然的表达是：Everyone had a party.)
The argument was had in private. (This is grammatically correct, but sounds unnatural.)  争论是私下进行的。(更自然的表达是：They had the argument in private.)

疑问句:
Did you have a good time? = 你玩得开心吗？
Had she left before you arrived? = 你来之前她已经离开了吗？

否定句:
I did not have any money. = 我没有钱。
He had not seen her before. = 他以前没见过她。
