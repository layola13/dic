filler:/ˈfɪlər/| n.|填充物，填料；（电影、广播或电视节目中的）填充节目；（香烟中的）填充料|Something used to fill a space, time, or container; an unimportant or insubstantial item used to fill space or time.|The magazine was full of filler, with very little actual news. 这本杂志充满了填充内容，几乎没有真正的新闻。|Synonyms: padding, stuffing, stopgap, placeholder|Antonyms: essence, core, substance|noun


filler:/ˈfɪlər/| adj.|填充的|Used to fill something.|Filler material is used to level uneven surfaces. 填充材料用于找平不平的表面。|Synonyms: padding, stuffing|Antonyms: essential, core|adjective
