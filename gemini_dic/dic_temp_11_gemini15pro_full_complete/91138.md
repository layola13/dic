hybridizations:/ˌhaɪbrɪdəˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 杂交，混合 | The process of forming a hybrid, often referring to the mixing of different varieties of plants or animals, or the combining of different elements or concepts. |  Hybridizations between different species can sometimes produce sterile offspring. 不同物种之间的杂交有时会产生不育的后代。 | synonyms: crossings, interbreedings, blends, combinations | antonyms: separations, isolations | noun
