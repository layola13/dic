hustle:/ˈhʌsl/ (US), /ˈhʌsl/ (UK)| v. n.| 催促, 赶紧, 奔忙, 努力工作, 兜售, 招揽生意|To move or act quickly and energetically; to sell or promote something aggressively.|He hustled to finish the project before the deadline. 他赶紧完成这个项目，争取在截止日期前完成。|近义词：hurry, rush, hasten, strive, peddle|反义词： dawdle, linger, procrastinate|词性: Verb, Noun

词源:  From the Dutch word *husselen* meaning "to shake, toss," likely related to the Middle Dutch *hutselen*. The sense of "to hurry" developed in the 19th century, and the sense of "to swindle" emerged around the same time.
