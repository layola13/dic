emblement:/ɪmˈblɛmənt/| n.|收获物；耕作物；土地收益|Annual crops produced by cultivation legally belonging to the tenant with the implied right to harvest them even after the tenancy has ended.|The farmer was granted the emblements of his fields even after he lost his lease.|近义词：crop, harvest, yield, produce|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用fallow - 休耕的土地)|noun
