outfeeding:/ˈaʊtˌfiːdɪŋ/
n.
外饲,补充饲喂，外部供给
The process of providing supplementary feed to animals outside of their main enclosure or feeding area.
The farmer practiced outfeeding to supplement the cows' grazing with hay during the winter.  农民在冬天实行外饲，用干草补充奶牛的放牧。
Outfeeding helped the piglets transition to solid food. 外饲帮助小猪过渡到固体食物。
supplementary feeding, external feeding
confined feeding
noun
