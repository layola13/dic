hedonistically:/ˌhiːdənˈɪstɪkli/ /ˌhedənˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. |享乐地，享乐主义地 | In a way that is characterized by pleasure-seeking; devoted to the pursuit of pleasure and self-gratification. | He lived hedonistically, indulging in every pleasure imaginable. 他过着享乐主义的生活，沉溺于一切可以想象的快乐。|  She spent her vacation hedonistically, lounging on the beach and sipping cocktails. 她享乐主义地度过了假期，躺在沙滩上啜饮鸡尾酒。 | synonym: sensually, luxuriously, self-indulgently | antonym: ascetically, abstemiously |词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek word "hēdonē" (ἡδονή), meaning "pleasure," + "-istic" (forming adjectives meaning "relating to") + "-ally" (forming adverbs from adjectives).
