accepting:/əkˈseptɪŋ/
adjective|
接受的，认可的；包容的|
Willing to receive or give approval to something offered or proposed.  Characterized by a tolerant or receptive attitude. |
She has a very accepting attitude towards people from different backgrounds. 她对来自不同背景的人持非常包容的态度。

He was accepting of the offer. 他接受了这个提议。

近义词:receptive, tolerant, open-minded, welcoming
反义词:rejecting, intolerant, closed-minded, unwelcoming
词性:adjective


Etymology: From the Latin "acceptāre" (to receive favorably), which is derived from "ad" (to) + "capere" (to take).  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb "accept" into a present participle, which can function as an adjective.
