fraiche:/freɪʃ/| adj.|新鲜的；清新的；(尤指食品)未经冷冻或腌制的；(人)精神饱满的；(脸色)红润的|Fresh, especially of food : not frozen or preserved.  Also used to describe someone as looking healthy and full of energy, or a complexion that is rosy.|The salad was made with crisp, fraiche vegetables. 这份沙拉是用新鲜爽脆的蔬菜做的。|She looked fraiche and youthful. 她看起来清新而年轻。|近义词:fresh, new, recent, invigorated|反义词:stale, wilted, tired, jaded|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *frais*, ultimately from Latin *friscus* meaning "fresh."
