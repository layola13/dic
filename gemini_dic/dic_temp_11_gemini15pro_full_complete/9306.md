antiplatelet:/ˌæntiˈpleɪtlɪt/| adj.|抗血小板的|Preventing or inhibiting the aggregation of platelets (blood cells involved in clotting).|Aspirin is an antiplatelet drug. 阿司匹林是一种抗血小板药物。|Antiplatelet therapy is crucial after a stroke. 中风后抗血小板治疗至关重要。|Synonyms: antithrombotic, anticoagulant (partially)|Antonyms: prothrombotic, coagulant|adjective
