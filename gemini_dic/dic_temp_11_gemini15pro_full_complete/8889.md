antifundamentalist:/ˌæntiˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪst/
adjective
反原教旨主义的
Opposed to or rejecting religious fundamentalism.
The author's antifundamentalist views are evident throughout his work.  作者的反原教旨主义观点在他的整个作品中都很明显。
The conference attracted a diverse group of antifundamentalist scholars.  这次会议吸引了各种各样的反原教旨主义学者。
近义词:liberal, progressive, secular
反义词:fundamentalist, orthodox, conservative
词性:adjective


antifundamentalist:/ˌæntiˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪst/
noun
反原教旨主义者
A person who opposes religious fundamentalism.
He is a vocal antifundamentalist and frequently criticizes religious extremism. 他是一个直言不讳的反原教旨主义者，经常批评宗教极端主义。
Many antifundamentalists advocate for the separation of church and state. 许多反原教旨主义者主张政教分离。
近义词:secularist, freethinker, nonconformist
反义词:fundamentalist, traditionalist
词性:noun


词源:
"anti-" (prefix meaning "against, opposed to") + "fundamentalist" (from "fundamentalism,"  referring to a strict adherence to basic principles of a religion or ideology)
