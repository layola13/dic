anyhow:/ˈɛniˌhaʊ/| adv.|无论如何；不管怎样；总之|Meaning: in any way, manner, or order; regardless. anyhow:/ˈɛniˌhaʊ/| adv.|随便地；马虎地；杂乱无章地|Meaning: in a careless or haphazard manner.|Example: He threw his clothes on the floor anyhow. 他把衣服随便扔在地板上。|Synonym: carelessly, haphazardly, messily|Antonym: carefully, neatly, methodically|词性: 副词
