grimy:/ˈɡraɪmi/| adj.|肮脏的，污秽的|Covered with or characterized by grime; dirty.|The grimy walls of the alley were covered in graffiti. 小巷肮脏的墙壁上满是涂鸦。|近义词：dirty, filthy, soiled, squalid|反义词：clean, spotless, pristine|词性:形容词
词源：From Old English *grīmig, from grīm (“grime”).
