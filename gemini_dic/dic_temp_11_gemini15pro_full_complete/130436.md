nonabrogable:/ˌnɒnəˈbrəʊɡəbəl/
adjective
不可废除的，不可取消的
Not able to be repealed or abolished.
The constitution guarantees certain nonabrogable rights, such as freedom of speech. 宪法保障某些不可废除的权利，例如言论自由。
The treaty established a nonabrogable alliance between the two nations. 该条约在两国之间建立了不可废除的联盟。
Synonyms:inalienable, inviolable, unassailable
Antonyms:revocable, repealable, abrogable
词性:adjective
