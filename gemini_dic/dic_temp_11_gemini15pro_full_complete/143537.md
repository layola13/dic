outgunning: /ˌaʊtˈɡʌnɪŋ/
verb
胜过；以火力压制
defeating someone by having more or better weapons
The rebels were clearly outgunned by the government troops. 叛军显然在火力上被政府军压制。
The smaller company was outgunned by its larger rival in the advertising campaign. 这家小公司在广告宣传活动中被其更大的竞争对手击败了。
近义词: surpassing, overwhelming, dominating, outpowering
反义词: being outmatched, being overpowered, being defeated
词性: v. (gerund or present participle)
