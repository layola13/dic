herbalism:/ˈɜːrbəlɪzəm/ /ˈhɜːrbəlɪzəm/
noun
草药学，草药医术
The study or practice of using plants, especially herbs, for medicinal purposes.
She studied herbalism and learned how to make natural remedies. 她学习了草药学，学会了如何制作天然药物。
Herbalism has been practiced for centuries in many cultures.  草药学在许多文化中已经实践了几个世纪。
近义词: herbology, phytotherapy
反义词: allopathy, conventional medicine
词性: noun
