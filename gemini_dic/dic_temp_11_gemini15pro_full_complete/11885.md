arrogate:/ˈærəɡeɪt/ | v. | 僭取；擅取；霸占 | To claim or take something without justification | He arrogated to himself the right to decide what everyone should do. 他擅自决定每个人应该做什么。| The government arrogated the power to imprison people without trial. 政府僭取了未经审判即可监禁人民的权力。 |  近义词：usurp, seize, appropriate, assume | 反义词：relinquish, renounce, abdicate | 词性: verb

词源:  From Latin *arrogatus*, past participle of *arrogare* (“to claim for oneself”), from *ad* (“to, toward”) + *rogare* (“to ask”).
