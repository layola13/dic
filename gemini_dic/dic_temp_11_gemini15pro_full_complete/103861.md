一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner?  她晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done to help? 他做了什么来帮忙？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading all afternoon? 她整个下午都在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? 你离开之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么长时间？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们将在下次会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have accomplished by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer? 到明年夏天他们将建成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习了什么？



虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你的。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What time is the movie? 电影是什么时候？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该做什么。
She didn't say what she wanted. 她没说她想要什么。
