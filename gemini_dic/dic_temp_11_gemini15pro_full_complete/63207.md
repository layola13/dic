attentive:/əˈtɛntɪv/| adj.|专心的；注意的；殷勤的|Paying close attention to something or someone; watchful; solicitous. |She was an attentive student, always eager to learn. 她是一位专心的学生，总是渴望学习。|The waiter was very attentive to our needs. 服务员对我们的需求非常殷勤。|近义词：alert, observant, mindful, considerate|反义词：inattentive, distracted, negligent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *attentīvus, from Latin attentus (“attentive”), past participle of attendere (“to attend to”).
