livery's:/ˈlɪv(ə)riz/| n.|制服；（尤指旧时仆役或官员的）特殊制服，号衣；（公司或行业的）官方颜色；（尤指出租的）车辆；（尤指马匹的）饲养；肝脏（尤指动物的）；|The company's livery consists of a dark blue suit and a red tie.  这家公司的制服是深蓝色西装和红色领带。|近义词：uniform, attire, costume|反义词: civilian clothes|词性:名词

词源:
From Old French *liveison, from livrer "to deliver, hand over," from Latin liberare "to set free," from liber "free." Originally referred to the delivery of provisions or clothing, especially to servants or retainers.
