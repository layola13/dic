hyperstimulating:/ˌhaɪpərˈstɪmjuleɪtɪŋ/
adjective
过度刺激的，超刺激的
Causing excessive stimulation.  Increasing the activity of (something) beyond normal limits.
The loud music and flashing lights were hyperstimulating for the baby.  喧闹的音乐和闪烁的灯光对婴儿来说过度刺激了。
The caffeine was hyperstimulating, making it difficult for her to sleep. 咖啡因过度刺激了她，使她难以入睡。
近义词:overstimulating, excessively stimulating
反义词:unstimulating, calming, soothing
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (prefix meaning 'over, excessive') + stimulating (present participle of stimulate, from Latin stimulāre 'to goad, urge,' from stimulus 'goad, prick').
