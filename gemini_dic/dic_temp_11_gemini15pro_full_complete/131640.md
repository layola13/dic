noncompressible:/ˌnɒnkəmˈprɛsəbl/ | adj. | 不可压缩的 | Not able to be compressed; resisting compression. |  The liquid inside the container is noncompressible.  容器内的液体是不可压缩的。|  incompressible, non-condensable | compressible | adjective


Etymology->
From non- + compressible.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "compressible" comes from the Latin "compressus," the past participle of "comprimere," meaning "to press together."
