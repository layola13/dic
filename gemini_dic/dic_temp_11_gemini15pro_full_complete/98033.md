inorganically:/ˌɪnɔːrˈɡænɪkli/
adv.
无机地，无生命地
Not involving or derived from living matter; not organic.
Plants take in nutrients from the soil, both organically and inorganically.  植物从土壤中吸收营养，包括有机和无机的。
Near synonyms: non-organically, abiotically
Antonyms: organically, biotically
词性:副词
