interadaptation:/ˌɪntərˌædæpˈteɪʃən/ | n. |相互适应|The reciprocal adaptation of two or more species or populations to each other.|例句：The interadaptation of the bee and the flower is a classic example of coevolution. 蜜蜂和花朵的相互适应是共同进化的典型例子。|例句: The interadaptation between the host and the parasite is a complex biological process.  宿主和寄生虫之间的相互适应是一个复杂的生物过程。|近义词：coadaptation, coevolution|反义词: maladaptation|词性: noun

词源:  From inter- + adaptation. "Inter-" signifies "between" or "among." "Adaptation" comes from Late Latin adaptātiōn-, from adaptāre "to adjust, fit," from ad- "to" + aptāre "to fit."
