headmen:/ˈhedmən/| n. |酋长，头人|A leader or chief, especially of a tribe or village. |The headmen of the various tribes met to discuss the treaty. 各个部落的头人聚在一起讨论条约。|Synonyms: chief, leader, chieftain, elder |Antonyms: follower, subordinate| noun

headmen:/ˈhedmən/| n. |头目，领导人|(often used in a historical context) The leader of a group of workers, especially in a mine or on a ship. |The headmen oversaw the loading of the cargo.  头目监督货物的装载。|Synonyms: foreman, overseer, supervisor, boss |Antonyms: worker, subordinate| noun
