geriatricians: /ˌdʒɛriəˈtrɪʃənz/ | n. |老年病学家，老年医学专家| A physician who specializes in the care of older adults, including the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of age-related diseases and conditions. | My grandmother sees a geriatrician for her health concerns. 我祖母去看老年病医生解决她的健康问题。|Geriatricians focus on the unique health needs of elderly patients. 老年病医生专注于老年患者独特的健康需求。|  Synonyms: gerontologists, elderly care physicians | Antonyms: pediatricians | noun
