amphigoric:/ˌæmfɪˈɡɔːrɪk/ | adj. n. | 模棱两可的，谜一般的；谜语，难解之谜 |  Of or relating to an amphigoric; obscure or nonsensical in meaning, like a riddle.  | The professor's lecture was so amphigoric that the students left feeling more confused than enlightened. 教授的讲座如此含糊不清，以至于学生们离开时感到更加困惑而不是豁然开朗。 |  The poem was an amphigoric, full of strange imagery and obscure allusions. 这首诗是一个谜，充满了奇特的意象和晦涩的典故。 | 近义词: enigmatic, obscure, puzzling, ambiguous, riddle | 反义词: clear, lucid, straightforward | 词性: adjective, noun

词源: From French *amphigouri*, from Italian *anfìguri*, a nonsense verse form, of uncertain origin, perhaps ultimately from Greek *amphi* “around, about” + *gyros* “circle, ring.”
