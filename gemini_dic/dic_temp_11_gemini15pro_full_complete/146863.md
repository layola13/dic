overindustrializes:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度工业化
To industrialize excessively, beyond the point of sustainability or economic benefit.
The small island nation overindustrialized, polluting its waters and depleting its natural resources.  这个小岛国过度工业化，污染了水域，耗尽了自然资源。
