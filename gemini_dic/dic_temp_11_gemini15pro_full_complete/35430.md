co-owning:/koʊˈoʊnɪŋ/
| adj. |共同拥有的|The act of owning something together with one or more other people.|例句：They are co-owning a vacation home in the mountains. 他们在山上共同拥有一栋度假屋。|近义词：jointly owning, shared ownership|反义词:sole ownership, individual ownership|词性:形容词
co-owning:/koʊˈoʊnɪŋ/
| n. |共同所有|The state of owning something together with one or more other people.|例句：Co-owning a business can be challenging. 共同拥有一家企业可能充满挑战。|近义词：joint ownership, shared ownership|反义词:sole ownership, individual ownership|词性:名词
