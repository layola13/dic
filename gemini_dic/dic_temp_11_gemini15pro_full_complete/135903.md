例句：Nonpsychics often find it difficult to understand the experiences of those who claim to have psychic abilities. 非通灵者通常很难理解那些声称拥有通灵能力的人的经历。

近义词：ordinary people, average people, regular people
反义词：psychics, mediums, clairvoyants
