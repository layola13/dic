furioso:/ˌfjʊəriˈoʊsoʊ/ /ˌfjʊərɪˈəʊsəʊ/
adjective
狂暴的；激烈的；暴怒的
Characterized by fury; furious.
The maestro directed the orchestra with furioso passion. 指挥家以狂暴的热情指挥乐队。
The furioso storm raged through the night.  狂暴的风暴整夜肆虐。
近义词: furious, raging, violent, vehement
反义词: calm, peaceful, gentle, tranquil
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Italian *furioso*, from Latin *furiōsus* (“full of fury, raging, mad”), from *furia* (“fury, rage”) + *-ōsus* (suffix forming adjectives meaning “full of, prone to”).
