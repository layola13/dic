groupie: /ˈɡruːpi/ (US), /ˈɡruːpi/ (UK)| n. |追星族，歌迷，(尤指)乐队迷|A person, especially a young woman, who regularly follows a pop group or other celebrity around, especially from town to town, or attends many of their public appearances, expressing admiration for them.|She was a devoted groupie, following the band on every tour. 她是一位忠实的歌迷，每次巡演都跟着乐队。|Synonyms: fan, admirer, devotee|Antonyms: critic, detractor|noun

groupie: /ˈɡruːpi/ (US), /ˈɡruːpi/ (UK)| n. |乐队迷|A person who follows a band or other music group around, especially on tour.|He's been a groupie since he was a teenager. 他从青少年时期就一直是乐队迷。|Synonyms: fan, follower, admirer|Antonyms: critic, hater|noun
