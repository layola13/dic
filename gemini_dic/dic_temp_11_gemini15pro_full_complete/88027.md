highball:/ˈhaɪbɔːl/| n.|高球酒（一种由烈酒加苏打水或姜汁汽水等非酒精饮料混合而成的鸡尾酒）; 高球杯|A mixed alcoholic drink made with a spirit and a mixer such as soda water or ginger ale, served in a tall glass.  A tall glass used for such drinks.|例句：He ordered a highball at the bar. 他在酒吧点了一杯高球酒。

例句：She poured the whisky into a highball glass. 她把威士忌倒入高球杯中。|近义词：cocktail, mixed drink|反义词:neat(纯的，不加任何东西的酒)|noun
