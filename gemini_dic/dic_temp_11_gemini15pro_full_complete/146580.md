overgratitude: /ˌoʊvərˈɡrætɪtjuːd/
| n. | 过分的感激，过度的感恩 | Excessive gratitude; being overly thankful. | She felt a sense of overgratitude for his small act of kindness. 她对他的小小善举感到过分的感激。 | 近义词: excessive gratitude, profuse thanks | 反义词: ingratitude, ungratefulness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From 'over-' (prefix meaning "excessive") + 'gratitude' (from Latin 'gratitudo', from 'gratus' meaning "pleasing, thankful").
