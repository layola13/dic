megastardom: /ˈmɛɡəˌstɑːrdəm/ | n. | 巨星地位；超级明星身份 | The state of being extremely famous and successful as a performer, especially in the entertainment industry.  |  She achieved megastardom after her latest film. 她最新上映的电影让她获得了巨星的地位。 |  Fame, celebrity, superstardom | Obscurity, anonymity | noun

Etymology: mega- + stardom
