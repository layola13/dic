impriming:/ɪmˈprɪntɪŋ/ | n. | 印记，铭刻，早熟学习 | The process by which a young animal comes to recognize another animal, person, or thing as a parent or other object of habitual trust. | The ducklings showed strong imprinting on the farmer. 小鸭子对农民表现出强烈的印记。| Synonyms:  impression, stamping,  conditioning | Antonyms: forgetting, erasure | Noun
