exsiliency:/ɪɡˈzɪliənsi/ /ɛkˈsɪliənsi/
noun
弹性，韧性，快速恢复的能力
The ability to recover quickly from setbacks or difficulties.
The exsiliency of the rubber band allowed it to snap back into shape.  这根橡皮筋的弹性使它能够迅速恢复原状。
The country demonstrated remarkable exsiliency in the face of economic hardship.  面对经济困难，这个国家展现了非凡的韧性。
resilience, elasticity, springiness, buoyancy
rigidity, inflexibility, stiffness, fragility
