meek:/miːk/| adj.|温顺的，谦恭的，顺从的|Quiet, gentle, and easily imposed on; submissive.|She spoke in a meek voice. 她用柔和的声音说话。|近义词：gentle, mild, submissive, humble, unassuming|反义词：aggressive, assertive, bold, arrogant, proud|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *mjúkr, from Proto-Germanic *mjukiz, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)meyg- (“soft”). Cognate with Swedish mjuk (“soft”), Norwegian mjuk (“soft”), Danish mjuk (“soft”), Icelandic mjúkur (“soft”). Related to Middle English meke, from Old English mēce.
