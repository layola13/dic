noninhabitabilities:/ˌnɒnɪnˌhæbɪtəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.| 不可居住性；不适于居住 |The noninhabitabilities of the planet Mars pose significant challenges for future human colonization. 火星的不可居住性给未来人类的殖民化带来了巨大的挑战。| The report detailed the noninhabitabilities of the area following the chemical spill. 报告详细说明了化学品泄漏后该地区的不适于居住。| 近义词: uninhabitability, unsuitability for living | 反义词: habitability, suitability for living | 词性: 名词

noninhabitable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhæbɪtəbl/
|adj.| 不可居住的；不适于居住的 |The extreme temperatures make the desert noninhabitable. 极端的温度使沙漠不适于居住。| The damaged building was declared noninhabitable after the fire.  火灾后，受损的建筑被宣布为不适于居住。| 近义词: uninhabitable, unlivable | 反义词: habitable, livable | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From non- + inhabitable + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or condition). Inhabitable comes from Latin inhabitabilis, from inhabitare "to dwell in," from in- "in" + habitare "to dwell."
