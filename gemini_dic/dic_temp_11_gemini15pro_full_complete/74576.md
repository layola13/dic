fraxin:/ˈfræksɪn/| n. |梣木碱; 白蜡树属|A fluorescent glucoside C16H18O7 extracted from the bark of the ash tree and used as an indicator and in laser dyes.  It is related to aesculin.|The fraxin found in the bark has medicinal properties. | 近义词: aesculin (similar compound), coumarin (related compound) | 反义词: None| noun
