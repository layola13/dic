overelliptically:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlɪptɪkəli/
adv.|过于省略地|In an excessively elliptical manner; with too much omitted.  Using ellipsis to an excessive degree, making the meaning obscure or difficult to understand.|She spoke overelliptically, leaving out crucial details that made her story hard to follow. 她说话过于省略，漏掉了关键的细节，使她的故事难以理解。|Example 2: The contract was written overelliptically, leading to confusion and disputes. 合同写得过于简略，导致了混淆和争议。|近义词：tersely, concisely, succinctly, compactly|反义词: verbosely, prolixly, lengthily|词性:副词
