disparage: /dɪˈspærɪdʒ/ | v. | 贬低，轻视，诋毁 | To speak about someone or something in a way that shows you do not respect or value them. | He disparaged his competitor's products. 他贬低了他的竞争对手的产品。| Synonyms: belittle, denigrate, deprecate, discredit | Antonyms: praise, commend, extol | verb

disparage: /dɪˈspærɪdʒ/ | v. | 贬低，轻视，诋毁 | To represent as being of little worth. |  The article disparaged her achievements. 这篇文章贬低了她的成就。 | Synonyms: underestimate, undervalue, minimize | Antonyms: overestimate, exaggerate | verb
