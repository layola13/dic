counterintuitive:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/ | adj. | 反直觉的 | Contrary to intuition or common sense; not readily understandable. | Example: It may seem counterintuitive, but sometimes increasing prices can actually boost sales. 这似乎反直觉，但有时提高价格实际上可以促进销售。

Example: The findings of the study were counterintuitive. 这项研究的结果出乎意料。| 近义词: illogical, paradoxical, unexpected | 反义词: intuitive, logical, obvious | 词性: adjective
