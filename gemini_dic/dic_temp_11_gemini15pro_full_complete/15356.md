backstreets:/ˈbækˌstrits/| n.|后街，偏僻小巷|Narrow, less-traveled streets, often associated with poverty or crime.  They are typically away from the main thoroughfares of a city or town.|We got lost in the backstreets of Rome. 我们在罗马的小巷里迷路了。|近义词：alleys, side streets, lanes|反义词:main streets, avenues|noun

backstreets:/ˈbækˌstrits/| n.|秘密活动，非正式渠道|The world of informal or illicit activities, often hidden from public view.|He made his fortune in the backstreets of the financial market. 他在金融市场的秘密交易中发了财。|近义词：underworld, black market|反义词:legitimate business, open market|noun
