ancienter:/ˈeɪnʃəntər/ | adj. | 更古老的 |  A comparative form of "ancient," meaning older or of a more remote period in history.  While grammatically correct, it is considered archaic and rarely used in modern English. "More ancient" is generally preferred. |  "He spoke of ancienter times, of days long past."  他谈到了更古老的时代，谈到了过去很久的日子。 | 近义词: older, more ancient | 反义词: newer, more recent | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *ancienner, *auncienner, comparative adjective from ancien (“ancient”).  Ultimately from Latin antiquus.
