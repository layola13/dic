lance:/læns/ | n. v. | 长矛，鱼叉；刺，戳 | A long weapon with a wooden shaft and a pointed metal head, used for thrusting or throwing.  To pierce with a lance or something pointed. |  The knight lowered his lance and charged. 骑士放低长矛冲锋陷阵。| Synonyms: spear, javelin, pike | Antonyms: shield, armor | noun

lance:/læns/ | n. v. | 刺破，切开（脓肿等） | To pierce or cut open (an abscess, boil, etc.) with a lancet or other sharp instrument. | The doctor lanced the boil to drain the pus. 医生刺破脓疮以排出脓液。| Synonyms: pierce, puncture, incise | Antonyms: suture, close | verb
