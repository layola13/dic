monothalamous:/ˌmɒnəˈθæləməs/| adj.|单室的；单心皮的|Having a single chamber or compartment, especially of an ovary (in botany) or a shell (in zoology).|例句：The monothalamous ovary is characteristic of certain plant families.  这种单心皮的子房是某些植物科的特征。

例句：The foraminiferan had a monothalamous shell.  这种有孔虫有一个单室的壳。|近义词：unicameral, unilocular|反义词：multilocular, polythalamous|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *mónos* ("single, alone") + *thálamos* ("chamber, room")
