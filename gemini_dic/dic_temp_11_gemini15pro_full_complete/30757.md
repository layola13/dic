cess:/ses/ | n. |税; 税款; 评估; |A tax, assessment, or levy. |The government imposed a new cess on imported goods. 政府对进口商品征收了新的税。|Synonyms: tax, levy, duty, assessment|Antonyms: exemption, rebate|noun

cess:/ses/ | n. |停止; 中止; |A ceasing or stopping.  (Archaic) |The rain showed no sign of cess. 雨没有停止的迹象。|Synonyms: cessation, stop, halt, pause|Antonyms: continuation, commencement|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cesse* "cessation, ceasing, rest," from Latin *cessare* "to yield, give way, cease, stop," frequentative of *cedere* "to go, yield, withdraw."
