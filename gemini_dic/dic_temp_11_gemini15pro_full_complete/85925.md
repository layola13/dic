headline:/ˈhedlaɪn/ | n. | 标题；头条新闻 | A heading at the top of an article or page in a newspaper or magazine. | The headline read "Local Man Wins Lottery".  当地男子赢得彩票。 | synonym: title, caption | antonym: body, text | noun

headline:/ˈhedlaɪn/ | v. |  以...作为头条；担任主角 | To feature as the main performer or attraction. | The band headlined the festival.  这支乐队是音乐节的主角。 | synonym: star, feature | antonym: support, accompany | verb
