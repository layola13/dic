arborists:/ˈɑːrbərɪsts/
n.
树艺师，树木栽培专家
A specialist in the cultivation and care of trees and shrubs.
例句：The arborists carefully pruned the branches of the old oak tree.  树艺师仔细地修剪了老橡树的枝条。

例句：The city hired certified arborists to maintain the trees in the park.  这座城市聘请了经过认证的树艺师来维护公园里的树木。

近义词：tree surgeon, tree specialist
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *arborarius, from arbor "tree."
