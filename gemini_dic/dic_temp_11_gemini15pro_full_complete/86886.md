hemisect:/ˈhɛmɪˌsɛkt/| v.| 二等分，对半切 | To divide (something) into two equal parts.| The surgeon hemisected the brain for further examination.  外科医生将大脑对半切开以进行进一步检查。|例句：The line hemisects the angle. 这条线将角二等分。|近义词：bisect, divide in half|反义词: combine, join|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin *hēmisēctus*, from *hēmī-* (half) + *sēctus* (cut, divided).
