hennery:/ˈhenəri/| n.|鸡舍，养鸡场|A place for raising hens.  A building or enclosure where chickens are kept.|The farmer expanded his hennery to accommodate more chickens.  农民扩建了他的鸡舍以容纳更多的鸡。|chicken coop, poultry house|roost|noun
Etymology: From Middle English *henneri, from hen + -ery. Ultimately from Old English *henn (hen) and -ery (a place for).
