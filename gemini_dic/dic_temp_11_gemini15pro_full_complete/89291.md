homoeomerical:/ˌhɒmiːəˈmɛrɪkəl/
adjective
同质的，由相似部分组成的
composed of similar parts; homogeneous
例句：Anaximander believed that the universe was homoeomerical, made of a boundless, undifferentiated substance called apeiron.  阿那克西曼德认为宇宙是同质的，由一种叫做“apeiron”的无限、未分化的物质构成。

例句：The philosopher argued that the homoeomerical nature of the substance allowed for constant change and transformation. 这位哲学家认为，这种物质的同质性允许不断的变化和转化。
近义词：homogeneous, uniform, similar
反义词：heterogeneous, diverse, dissimilar
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὁμοιομερής (homoiomerḗs, “having similar parts”), from ὁμοῖος (homoîos, “similar”) + μέρος (méros, “part”).
