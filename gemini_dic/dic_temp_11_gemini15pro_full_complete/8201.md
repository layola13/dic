anti-educational:/ˌæntiˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənəl/| adj.|反教育的，不利于教育的|Opposing or hindering education; not promoting or conducive to learning.|Some critics consider reality television to be anti-educational, arguing that it promotes superficiality and discourages critical thinking.|例句：Some people believe that excessive video game playing is anti-educational.  有些人认为过度玩电子游戏不利于教育。

例句：The government's new policy was criticized for being anti-educational and limiting access to knowledge.  政府的新政策因其反教育性和限制获取知识而受到批评。|近义词：uneducational, counter-educational, harmful to education|反义词：educational, pro-educational, conducive to learning|词性:adjective
