annualised: /ˈænjuəlaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈænjuəˌlaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|年度化的，按年计算的|Calculated or expressed as an annual rate.  It is used to compare rates that cover different periods of time.|The annualised return on the investment was 12%. 这项投资的年化回报率为12%。|Synonyms: annualized (US spelling), yearly, per annum|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

annualised: /ˈænjuəlaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈænjuəˌlaɪzd/ (US)| v.|按年计算|To calculate or express something as an annual rate.|The company annualised its profits for the last quarter. 公司将上一季度的利润按年计算。|Synonyms: annualize (US spelling)|Antonyms: N/A|verb
