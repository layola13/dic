canes:/keɪnz/| n.|拐杖；（尤指用作惩罚的）棍棒；细长的茎；藤条|A stick used to help someone to walk, especially an old person.  Also, a thin stem of a plant, especially a raspberry or blackberry; a long, thin piece of wood used for beating someone.|The old man leaned heavily on his canes. 老人沉重地倚在他的拐杖上。

The headmaster still uses canes for discipline. 校长仍然用藤条进行惩戒。

Sugar canes grow tall in tropical climates. 甘蔗在热带气候下长得高。|近义词：sticks, staffs, rods, poles, stems|反义词: (For walking canes) none; (For beating canes) reward, praise|词性:noun
