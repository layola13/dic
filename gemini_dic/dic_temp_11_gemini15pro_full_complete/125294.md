multifront:/ˈmʌltiˌfrʌnt/
adjective
多方面的，多线的
Having several distinct aspects or occurring on several fronts.
The company faced a multifront war against its competitors.  这家公司面临着与竞争对手的多线战争。
The country's economic problems were multifront, involving trade imbalances, unemployment, and inflation. 这个国家的经济问题是多方面的，包括贸易不平衡、失业和通货膨胀。
synonyms: multifaceted, many-sided, multifarious
antonyms: single-front, unified, singular
