coadjuting:/koʊˈædʒuːtɪŋ/
adjective
辅助的，协助的
acting as a coadjutor; assisting or helping.
The coadjuting bishop assisted the elderly bishop in his duties.  辅助主教协助年迈的主教履行职责。
近义词: assisting, helping, aiding, supporting
反义词: hindering, obstructing, impeding
词性: adjective

coadjutor:/koʊˈædʒətər/
noun
助手，副手，助理主教
a person who helps another, especially a bishop appointed to assist a diocesan bishop.
The bishop appointed a coadjutor to help manage the large diocese. 主教任命了一名助理主教来协助管理这个大的教区。
近义词: assistant, helper, aide, deputy
反义词: opponent, adversary
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *coadiutor, from Latin coadiuvāre (“to help, assist”), from co- (“together”) + adiuvāre (“to help”).
