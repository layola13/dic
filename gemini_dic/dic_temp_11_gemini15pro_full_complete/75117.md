frisette:/frɪˈzɛt/ | n. |  小卷发，鬈发 | A tight curl or ringlet of hair.  | She had a frisette of dark curls framing her face. 她浓密的黑色鬈发环绕着脸庞。| synonym: ringlet, curl, frizz | antonym: straight hair | noun

frisette:/frɪˈzɛt/ | n. |  (尤指羊毛或马海毛做的)毛圈花式织物 |  A looped pile fabric, especially of wool or mohair. | The coat was made of a soft, warm frisette. 这件外套是用柔软温暖的毛圈织物制成的。| synonym: looped pile fabric, terry cloth (similar but not exact) | antonym: flat fabric, smooth fabric | noun
