envelop:/ɪnˈveləp/ | vt. | 包裹，覆盖 |  To wrap up, cover, or surround completely. |  She enveloped the baby in a soft blanket. 她用柔软的毯子把婴儿裹了起来。|  Darkness enveloped the city. 黑暗笼罩着城市。| 近义词：cover, surround, enclose, shroud | 反义词：expose, uncover, bare | 词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *enveloper (“to wrap, cover”), from en- (“in”) + voloper (“to wrap”).  The modern French form is envelopper.
