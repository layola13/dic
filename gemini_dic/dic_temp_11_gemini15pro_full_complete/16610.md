barometry:/bəˈrɒmɪtri/ | n. | 气压测定法；气压测量学 | The science of measuring atmospheric pressure, especially for the purpose of forecasting weather. |  Barometry plays a crucial role in weather forecasting.  气压测定法在天气预报中起着至关重要的作用。 |  synonyms:  hypsometry (for altitude measurement using barometric pressure) | antonyms: N/A | noun

barometric:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 气压的；气压计的 | Relating to barometry or a barometer. | The barometric pressure dropped significantly before the storm.  暴风雨来临前，气压显著下降。| synonyms: atmospheric, pressure-related | antonyms: N/A | adjective
