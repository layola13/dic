operable:/ˈɒpərəbl/ (UK) /ˈɑːpərəbl/ (US)| adj.|可操作的，可使用的，可动手术的|Suitable to be subjected to a surgical operation.|The tumor is operable.  肿瘤可以手术。|
近义词:functional, workable, usable, in working order, running|
反义词:inoperable, nonfunctional, broken, unusable|
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *operabilis*, from Latin operārī (“to work”), from opera (“work”).  See operate.
