Medicaid: /ˈmedɪkeɪd/

词性: n.

中文翻译: 医疗补助 (指美国政府为低收入人群提供的医疗保险)

英文解析: A U.S. government program financed by federal, state, and local funds, that provides health care for low-income people.

例句：
Medicaid helps millions of Americans access essential healthcare services.
医疗补助帮助数百万美国人获得必要的医疗保健服务。

Many low-income families rely on Medicaid for their healthcare needs.
许多低收入家庭依靠医疗补助来满足他们的医疗保健需求。

近义词: welfare, public assistance

反义词:  (Medicaid 没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 private insurance 私人保险)

词源:  A blend of "medical" and "aid".
