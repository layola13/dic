dauntlessness:/ˈdɔːntləsnəs/| n.|无畏，勇敢，大胆|The firefighter showed incredible dauntlessness in rushing into the burning building.|消防员冲进燃烧的建筑物表现出令人难以置信的无畏。|近义词：courage, bravery, fearlessness, intrepidity|反义词：fearfulness, timidity, cowardice|词性:名词

Etymology: From *dauntless* + *-ness*. *Dauntless* comes from the Middle English *daunten*, from Old French *danter*, meaning "to subdue, tame," ultimately from Latin *domitare*, "to tame."  The suffix *-ness* forms nouns expressing a state or quality.
