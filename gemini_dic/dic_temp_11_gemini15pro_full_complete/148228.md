oversimpleness:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪsnəs/ | n. | 过度简化；过于简单 | The oversimpleness of the explanation made it misleading.  这种解释过于简单，反而造成了误导。 |  The report suffered from oversimpleness and failed to address the complexities of the issue.  这份报告过于简化，未能解决问题的复杂性。|  simplicity, naiveté | complexity, sophistication | noun

oversimplify:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪ/ | v. | 过度简化；过于简单化 |  It's easy to oversimplify complex issues.  复杂的议题很容易被过度简化。 | Don't oversimplify the problem; there are many factors to consider.  不要把问题过于简单化；有很多因素需要考虑。 | simplify, condense | complicate, elaborate | verb

oversimplified:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 过于简化的 | The oversimplified explanation left many questions unanswered. 过于简化的解释留下了许多未解答的问题。 |  The textbook offered an oversimplified view of the historical period.  这本教科书对这段历史时期进行了过于简化的描述。 | simplistic, reductive | nuanced, complex | adjective
