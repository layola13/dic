ascertain:/ˌæsərˈteɪn/| v. |确定，查明|To find something out for certain; make sure of.|例句：The police are trying to ascertain the facts of the case. 警方正试图查明案件的事实。|例句：We need to ascertain whether the information is reliable. 我们需要确定信息是否可靠。|近义词：determine, discover, establish, verify, confirm|反义词：guess, speculate, assume|词性:动词

词源:
From Old French *acertener "to inform, to make certain," from a- "to" (see ad-) + certain "certain."  The spelling with sc- is a 16c. modification.
