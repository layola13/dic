ardours:/ˈɑːrdərz/
n.
热情，激情，热心
great enthusiasm or passion
She pursued her artistic ardours with unwavering dedication. 她以不懈的奉献追求着她的艺术热情。

ardours:/ˈɑːrdərz/
n.
辛劳，艰苦
strenuous and difficult tasks or experiences
The ardours of the journey tested their endurance. 旅途的艰辛考验了他们的耐力。


Etymology: From Middle English *ardures, from Old French ardour, from Latin ardor (“burning, heat, zeal, passion”), from ardēre (“to burn”).

Synonyms: passion, zeal, fervor, enthusiasm, eagerness, dedication, commitment, diligence, industry, perseverance, toils, labors, hardships, difficulties, struggles

Antonyms: apathy, indifference, lethargy, laziness, idleness, ease, comfort
