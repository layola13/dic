fracturing:/ˈfræktʃərɪŋ/ | n. | 断裂；破碎；压裂 | The process of breaking or cracking something, especially rock or bone.  |  Hydraulic fracturing is used to extract natural gas from shale rock. 水力压裂用于从页岩中提取天然气。 | synonyms: breaking, cracking, splitting, rupturing | antonyms: mending, repairing, joining, fusing | noun

fracturing:/ˈfræktʃərɪŋ/ | adj. |  (通常不单独使用) 断裂的; 破碎的 |  Describing something that causes fractures. This is less common than the noun form. | The fracturing pressure of the rock was immense. 岩石的断裂压力巨大。 | synonyms: breaking, cracking, splitting, rupturing | antonyms: mending, repairing, joining, fusing | adjective
