above-water:/əˈbʌv ˈwɔːtər/
adjective
水面上的；露出水面的；没有负债的；经济上可行的
being above the surface of the water; not submerged;  financially sound; solvent.
The boat stayed above-water despite the rough seas.  尽管海面波涛汹涌，船仍然浮在水面上。
The company managed to stay above-water during the recession.  该公司在经济衰退期间设法保持了盈利。
近义词: afloat, solvent, viable
反义词: submerged, underwater, bankrupt, insolvent
词性: adjective
