anachronism:/əˈnækrənɪzəm/ | n. |时代错误，年代误植，不合时代的人或物 |Something that is out of place in time; a thing belonging or appropriate to a period other than that in which it exists, especially a thing that is conspicuously old-fashioned.| The play was set in ancient Rome, but the inclusion of a wristwatch was a glaring anachronism. 这部戏剧的背景设定在古罗马，但其中出现的手表是一个明显的时代错误。| Example:  The knight riding a motorcycle in the movie was a deliberate anachronism intended for comic effect.  这部电影里骑摩托车的骑士是一个为了喜剧效果而故意设计的时代错误。

Example: Using a quill pen in a modern office is an anachronism. 在现代办公室使用羽毛笔是一个时代错误。 | Synonyms:  misplacement,  inappropriateness,  incongruity,  solecism | Antonyms:  chronological accuracy, contemporaneity | noun
