fierceness:/ˈfɪərsnəs/| n.|凶猛，激烈，强烈|The fierceness of the storm surprised everyone.  这场风暴的猛烈程度让每个人都感到惊讶。|例句：The lion attacked its prey with unmatched fierceness. 狮子以无比的凶猛攻击它的猎物。  The fierceness of the competition was evident in the athletes' faces.  比赛的激烈程度在运动员的脸上显而易见。|近义词：ferocity, intensity, vehemence, savagery|反义词：gentleness, mildness, meekness|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *fiersnes, from Old French feresce, from fierce + -ness. Ultimately from Latin ferus (“wild, savage”).
