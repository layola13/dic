microstate:/ˈmaɪkroʊsteɪt/ | n. |微观状态; 微小国家|A specific microscopic configuration of a thermodynamic system, characterized by the specific properties of each particle in the system. In political science, it refers to a very small sovereign state.|

例句：
The microstate of Monaco is known for its glamour and casinos. 摩纳哥这个微型国家以其魅力和赌场而闻名。

Each microstate of the gas has a specific energy associated with it. 气体的每个微观状态都有一个特定的能量与之相关。


近义词：ministate (for political context)  微型国家
反义词：macrostate (in physics)  宏观状态; megastate, large country (in political science)  大国
词性：noun
