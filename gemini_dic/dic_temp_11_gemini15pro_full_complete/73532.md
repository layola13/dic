foresightedness:/fɔːrˈsaɪtɪdnəs/| n.|远见，深谋远虑|The company's success was due to the foresightedness of its founder.|由于其创始人的远见卓识，公司取得了成功。|近义词：prudence, foresight, vision, prescience|反义词：shortsightedness, myopia|词性:名词

Etymology:
From fore- + sighted + -ness. Fore- is from Old English fore- "before, in front, earlier." Sighted is from sight + -ed. Sight is from Old English siht "power of seeing." -ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
