ephemerae:/ɪˈfɛmərəˌiː/
|n.|蜉蝣；短暂的事物；昙花一现的事物|Plural of ephemera, meaning short-lived things, especially insects.  These could be mayflies, or things like tickets or pamphlets from an event that are only relevant for a short time.|The ephemerae of youth are often forgotten in old age. 青春期的短暂事物在老年时常被遗忘。|Synonyms: transience, ephemerality, short-lived things|Antonyms: permanence, eternity|noun

ephemera:/ɪˈfɛmərə/
|n.|蜉蝣；短暂的事物；昙花一现的事物|Things that exist or are used or enjoyed for only a short time.|Old concert tickets and brochures are just ephemera. 旧的音乐会门票和小册子只是些昙花一现的东西。|Synonyms: transience, ephemerality, short-lived things|Antonyms: permanence, eternity|noun
