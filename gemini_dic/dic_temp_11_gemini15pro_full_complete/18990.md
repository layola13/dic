bestowal:/bɪˈstoʊəl/
noun
授予，赠与
the act of giving something, especially something valuable or important
The bestowal of the Nobel Prize is a great honor.  诺贝尔奖的授予是一项极高的荣誉。

The queen honored him with the bestowal of a knighthood. 女王授予他骑士爵位以示嘉奖。

近义词:conferment, awarding, presentation, granting
反义词:denial, refusal, revocation, withdrawal
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bestowale*, from bestow + -al.  Ultimately from Old English *bestandan* (to stand by, assist).
