enthronize: /ɪnˈθroʊnaɪz/ | vt. | 使登基；使 enthrone；任命；推崇；崇拜 | To place (someone) on a throne, especially as a monarch; to invest with sovereign power. enthronization: /ɪnˌθroʊnɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 登基； enthronement；就任；推崇 | The act or ceremony of enthroning someone, especially as a monarch. | The enthronization of the new king was a grand spectacle. 新国王的登基典礼是一场盛大的场面。  The enthronization of this new technology as the industry standard has been swift. 这项新技术迅速成为行业标准。 | 近义词: coronation, inauguration, investiture | 反义词: deposition, dethronement | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + throne + -ize.  "Throne" comes from the Old French "trone" and ultimately from the Greek "thronos" meaning "seat, chair."  The suffix "-ize" forms verbs meaning "to make or become."
