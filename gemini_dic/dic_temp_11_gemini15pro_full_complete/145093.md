overapplications:/ˌoʊvərˌæplɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|过度申请；过量施用|The overapplications of fertilizer can harm the environment. 过量施用肥料会危害环境。|例句：There were so many overapplications for the internship that they had to extend the deadline for reviewing resumes.  实习的申请太多了，他们不得不延长审核简历的截止日期。

例句：Overapplications of pesticides can lead to resistant strains of insects. 过量使用杀虫剂会导致害虫产生抗药性。|近义词：excess applications, superfluous applications|反义词: underapplications, insufficient applications|词性:名词
