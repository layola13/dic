indecorously: /ˌɪndɪˈkɔːrəsli/ | adv. | 不雅地，不得体地 | In a manner lacking propriety and good taste; unseemly. | She behaved indecorously at the funeral, laughing loudly and making inappropriate jokes.  她在葬礼上的举止很不雅观，大声笑着，还开着不合时宜的玩笑。| 近义词: improperly, unseemly, inappropriately, immodestly | 反义词: decorously, properly, appropriately, respectfully | 词性: 副词

indecorous: /ˌɪndɪˈkɔːrəs/ | adj. | 不雅的，不得体的 | Not in keeping with good taste and propriety; unseemly. | His indecorous behavior embarrassed everyone present. 他不雅的举止让在场的所有人都感到尴尬。| 近义词: improper, unseemly, inappropriate, immodest | 反义词: decorous, proper, appropriate, respectful | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin indecorus "unseemly," from in- "not" + decorus "seemly, fitting, proper."
