outcrop:/ˈaʊtkrɒp/ (UK) /ˈaʊtkrɑːp/ (US)| n. vi.|露出地面的岩层；露出；出现|A part of a rock formation that appears above the surface of the surrounding land.  The appearance of something at the surface.|例句：The quarry face revealed a large outcrop of granite. 采石场表面露出了一大块花岗岩。|近义词：emergence, appearance, showing, exposure|反义词:concealment, covering|词性:名词/动词

outcrop:/ˈaʊtkrɒp/ (UK) /ˈaʊtkrɑːp/ (US)| vi.|露出；出现|To appear at the surface.|例句：Seams of coal outcropped along the river bank. 煤层沿着河岸露出。|近义词：emerge, appear, surface|反义词:disappear, vanish|词性:动词
