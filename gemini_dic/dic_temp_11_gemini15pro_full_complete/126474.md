mythologies:/mɪˈθɒlədʒiz/
n.
神话；神话学；神话集
A collection of myths, especially one belonging to a particular religious or cultural tradition.
The book explores the mythologies of ancient Greece.  这本书探讨了古希腊的神话。
Many mythologies feature a creator god. 许多神话中都有一个造物主神。
synonyms:myths, legends, folklore, lore, traditions, tales, stories
antonyms:facts, realities, truths
noun
