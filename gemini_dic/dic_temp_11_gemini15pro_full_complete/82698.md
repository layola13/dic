guffer:/ˈɡʌfər/| n.|古怪老头；爱吹牛的老头；(尤指)古怪的老矿工|An elderly man, especially one who is eccentric or talks a lot about his experiences, particularly a miner.|The old guffer sat by the fire, regaling anyone who would listen with tales of his mining days.|近义词：codger, old-timer, geezer|反义词:youngster, youth|noun
