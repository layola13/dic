knightly:/ˈnaɪtli/| adj.|骑士般的；勇敢的；高贵的|Behaving in a way that is typical of a knight; brave, honorable, and courteous.  Having or showing the qualities idealized as those of a knight; chivalrous.|He behaved in a knightly fashion, opening doors for her and offering his coat. 他表现得很有骑士风度，为她开门，并把外套给她穿。|近义词：chivalrous, gallant, courteous, honorable, brave|反义词：cowardly, dishonorable, rude, ungallant|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *knightli, equivalent to knight +‎ -ly.  Ultimately from Old English *cniht (“boy, servant”).
