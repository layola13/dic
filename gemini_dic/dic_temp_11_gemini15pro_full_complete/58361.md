dual-controlled:/ˌduːəl kənˈtroʊld/ | adj. | 双重控制的，由两人共同控制的 | Designed or operated so that two people must act together to control it. | The driving school car was dual-controlled, allowing the instructor to take over if necessary.  驾校的汽车是双重控制的，允许教练在必要时接管。|  The nuclear weapons are dual-controlled, requiring two separate launch codes. 核武器是双重控制的，需要两个独立的发射码。 | synonyms: jointly controlled, shared control | antonyms: single-controlled, independently controlled | adjective

dual-control:/ˌduːəl kənˈtroʊl/ | n. | 双重控制 | A system requiring two people to operate something. | The dual-control system ensures safety during critical operations. 双重控制系统确保了关键操作期间的安全。 | The plane's dual-control allowed both pilots to fly. 飞机的双重控制允许两名飞行员驾驶。 | synonyms: joint control, shared control | antonyms: single control, independent control | noun
