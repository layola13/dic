freehold:/ˈfriːhoʊld/ (US) /ˈfriːhəʊld/ (UK)| n. |永久产权；终身保有权|An estate in land, of indefinite duration, that a person owns and can pass on to heirs. It is the highest form of land ownership.|She purchased the freehold of the property last year. 她去年购买了该房产的永久产权。|Synonyms: fee simple, outright ownership|Antonyms: leasehold, tenancy|noun

freehold:/ˈfriːhoʊld/ (US) /ˈfriːhəʊld/ (UK)| adj. |永久产权的；终身保有权的|Relating to or denoting an estate held in fee simple.|They live in a freehold house. 他们住在一栋拥有永久产权的房子里。|Synonyms: fee simple, owned outright|Antonyms: leasehold, rented|adjective
