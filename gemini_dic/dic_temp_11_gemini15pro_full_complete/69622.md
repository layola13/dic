femininity:/ˌfemɪˈnɪnəti/ | n. | 女性气质；女人味；柔媚 |The quality or nature of being feminine; womanliness.| She exuded an air of femininity. 她散发着女性魅力。 |  synonyms: womanliness, femaleness, ladyhood | antonyms: masculinity, maleness | noun

femininity:/ˌfemɪˈnɪnəti/ | n. | 女性；妇女 | The fact or condition of being female. (This usage is less common) |  The femininity of the group was striking.  这个群体女性居多。（这个用法不太常见）| synonyms: womanhood, womankind | antonyms: manhood, mankind | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *féminité*, from Latin fēminīnitās ("womanhood"), from fēminīnus ("female, feminine"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʷḗn ("woman").
