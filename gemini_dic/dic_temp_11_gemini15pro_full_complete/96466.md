individualization:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|个性化，个体化|The process of making something suitable for particular people or things, or the process of becoming suitable for particular people or things.|例句：Individualization of instruction is crucial for effective learning.  个性化教学对于有效学习至关重要。

例句：The company offers individualization of its products to meet specific customer needs.  该公司提供产品个性化服务以满足特定客户的需求。|近义词：personalization, customization, tailoring|反义词：standardization, uniformity|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From individual + -ization.  "Individual" comes from Late Latin individuālis, meaning "indivisible," from Latin individuus, also meaning "indivisible."  "-ization" is a suffix forming nouns of action or process.
