beanos:/ˈbiːnəʊz/ (British English)| n.|盛宴；聚会；欢乐时光|A festive occasion, especially a social gathering with food.  A jolly time.  Derived from rhyming slang "beano" (a comic) for "piano", then extended to general merriment and feasts, likely influenced by the image of a jovial sing-along around a piano.  Became popular in the early 20th century.|We're having a real beanos for New Year's Eve.  除夕夜我们将举行一场真正的盛宴。|The kids had a beanos at the birthday party. 孩子们在生日派对上玩得很开心。|synonyms: party, celebration, feast, bash, shindig|antonyms: wake, funeral|noun
