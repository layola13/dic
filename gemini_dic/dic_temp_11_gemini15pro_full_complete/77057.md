garnetiferous:/ˌɡɑːrˈnɪtɪfərəs/
adjective
含柘榴石的；产柘榴石的
Containing garnet; yielding garnet.
The geologist identified a garnetiferous schist in the mountain range.  地质学家在山脉中发现了一种含柘榴石的片岩。
The mine is known for its garnetiferous deposits.  该矿以其柘榴石矿床而闻名。
Synonyms: garnet-bearing, garnet-rich
Antonyms: barren (of garnet)
词性: adjective
