dux:/dʌks/
n.
leader, top student
A leader; the leading or most distinguished person or thing of a kind or group. Especially the top student in a class at a school.
The dux of the school gave a brilliant valedictorian speech.  学校的尖子生发表了精彩的告别演说。
synonyms: leader, head, chief, top student, valedictorian
antonyms: follower, last, bottom
