nonspeculations:/ˌnɒnspekjʊˈleɪʃənz/
noun
非推测; 非投机; 不做猜测
The report focused on facts and nonspeculations.
该报告侧重于事实而非推测。
This is based on evidence, not nonspeculations.
这是基于证据，而不是推测。
synonyms: facts, certainties, realities
antonyms: speculations, conjectures, guesses
n.


Etymology:
From non- + speculation.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Speculation" comes from the Latin word "speculatio," meaning "observation, contemplation."
