napiform:/ˈneɪpɪfɔːrm/
adjective
芜菁状的；萝卜状的
Having the shape of a turnip; broader at the base and tapering toward the tip.
The radish has a napiform root.  这个萝卜有一个芜菁状的根。
近义词:turnip-shaped, turbinate
反义词:(No specific antonym, possibly "elongated," "linear")
词性: adjective

napiform:/ˈneɪpɪfɔːrm/
词源: From Latin *napiformis, from napus (“turnip”) + -formis (“-shaped”).
用法: Used to describe roots, tubers, or other plant parts that are shaped like a turnip.  It's a technical term primarily used in botanical or horticultural contexts.
