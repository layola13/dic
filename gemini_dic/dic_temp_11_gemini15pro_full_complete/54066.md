diseasedly:/dɪˈziːzɪdli/ | adv. | 病态地，不健康地 | In a diseased manner; exhibiting symptoms of disease; unhealthily.  | He coughed diseasedly, clutching his chest. 他病态地咳嗽着，捂着胸口。|  She spoke in a low, diseasedly whisper. 她低声地，病怏怏地说着。 | 近义词: sickly, morbidly, unhealthily | 反义词: healthily, robustly | 词性: 副词

词源:

From *diseased* + *-ly*. *Diseased* comes from the Old French *desaisi*  "dispossessed, deprived of," from *des-* (expressing removal) + *saisir*  "to seize." The meaning shifted to "afflicted with disease" in Middle English.
