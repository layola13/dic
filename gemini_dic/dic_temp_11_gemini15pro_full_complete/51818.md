dewed:/djuːd/ | adj. | 露水般的，被露水打湿的 | Covered with dew; dewy. | The dewed grass sparkled in the early morning sunlight. 清晨的阳光下，沾满露水的草地闪闪发光。| The spider web, dewed with moisture, glistened in the dim light.  沾满湿气的蜘蛛网在昏暗的光线下闪闪发光。 | 近义词: dewy, moist, damp | 反义词: dry, arid | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From dew + -ed.  The suffix "-ed" is used to form adjectives from nouns, indicating "having" or "characterized by" the quality of the noun. "Dew" comes from Old English *dēaw, from Proto-Germanic *dawwaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dheus-  "to flow."
