nolt:/nɒlt/ | n. | 无赖; 恶棍; 流氓 | A rogue or villain. | He was known as a nolt and a scoundrel in his youth. 他年轻时被称为无赖和恶棍。 | 近义词: rogue, scoundrel, villain, rascal, miscreant | 反义词: hero, saint | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a variant of "knolt," itself possibly derived from Middle Dutch "knolle" meaning "lump" or "knob," perhaps referring to a rough or uncouth person. The exact etymology is uncertain.
