gordian:/ˈɡɔːrdiən/ | adj. | 戈尔迪之结的; 棘手的; 难解的 |Extremely intricate; difficult to solve.  Relating to the Gordian Knot, a legendary knot tied by Gordius, king of Phrygia, which was said to be impossible to untie.  Alexander the Great cut the knot with his sword, thus "solving" the problem. |  The negotiators faced a gordian knot of conflicting interests. 谈判代表们面临着错综复杂的利益冲突。| Synonyms: intricate, complex, involved, tangled, knotty | Antonyms: simple, straightforward, easy | adjective
