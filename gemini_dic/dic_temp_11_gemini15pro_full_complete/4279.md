airward:/ˈɛərwərd/| adj. adv.|向上的，朝天空的；向上地，朝天空地|Directed or moving toward the sky; upward.|The bird soared airward. 鸟儿向空中翱翔。|近义词：upward, skyward|反义词:downward, earthward|词性:形容词/副词

Etymology:
From air + -ward, a suffix indicating direction.  The suffix "-ward" comes from Old English "-weard," meaning "toward," related to the word "weardan" (to guard, watch over).  Thus, "airward" literally means "toward the air."
