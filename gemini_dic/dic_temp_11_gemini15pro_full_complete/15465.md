bade:/beɪd/ /bæd/ | v. | 命令；吩咐；告诫；邀请；道别 | past tense of *bid* (meaning "command" or "request"). It can also be the past tense of *bid* (meaning "to offer a price").  | Example 1: She bade him farewell. 她向他告别。

Example 2: He bade me sit down. 他让我坐下。| 近义词: commanded, ordered, instructed, invited, farewell | 反义词: obeyed, followed, complied | 词性: 动词
