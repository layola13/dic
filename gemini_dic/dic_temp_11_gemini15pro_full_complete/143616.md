outjest:/aʊtˈdʒɛst/
verb
胜过…的玩笑; 比…更会开玩笑
To surpass someone in jesting or joking; to be more witty or humorous than someone else.
He tried to outjest the comedian, but failed miserably. 他试图比那个喜剧演员更会开玩笑，但惨败了。
Synonyms: outwit, surpass, excel
Antonyms: be outdone, lose to
词性: 动词
