fecundation:/ˌfiːkənˈdeɪʃn/ | n. | 受精；施肥 | The act or process of fertilizing or being fertilized; impregnation. |  The fecundation of the ovum by the sperm initiates the development of a new organism.  卵子受精于精子，开始了新生物体的发育。| synonyms: fertilization, impregnation, conception | antonyms: sterility, infertility | noun

fecundation:/ˌfiːkənˈdeɪʃn/ | n. | 肥沃；多产 | The act of making something fertile or productive. | The farmer practiced yearly fecundation of the fields to ensure a good harvest. 农民每年都对田地进行施肥，以确保丰收。 | synonyms: enrichment, fertilization | antonyms: depletion, impoverishment | noun
