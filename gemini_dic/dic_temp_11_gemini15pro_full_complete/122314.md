mittyesque: /mɪtiˈɛsk/
adjective
米蒂式的; 爱幻想的; 脱离现实的
Having characteristics of Walter Mitty, the fictional character in James Thurber's short story "The Secret Life of Walter Mitty", who escapes from mundane reality through daydreams of heroism and adventure.
He has a mittyesque fantasy life, where he's a famous surgeon, a daring pilot, and a world-renowned author.
他有一个米蒂式的幻想生活，在那里他是一位著名的外科医生，一位勇敢的飞行员和一位世界知名的作家。

His mittyesque tendencies led him to embellish even the most mundane stories.
他爱幻想的倾向导致他即使是最平凡的故事也加以润色。


近义词: fanciful, dreamy, imaginative, unrealistic, quixotic
反义词: realistic, pragmatic, down-to-earth
词性: adjective


词源:  Derived from the name "Walter Mitty," the protagonist of James Thurber's short story "The Secret Life of Walter Mitty" (1939).  The suffix "-esque" means "in the style of" or "resembling."
