crustlike:/ˈkrʌstlaɪk/
adjective
像外壳的；硬皮状的
Resembling a crust; having a hard or brittle surface.
The bread had a crustlike exterior.  这面包的外面像一层硬壳。
The dried mud formed a crustlike layer on the ground. 干泥在地面上形成了一层硬皮。
近义词:crusty, shell-like, hardened
反义词:soft, pliable, flexible
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From crust + -like.  "Crust" comes from the Old Norse "krusta" meaning "crust, shell, rind." "-like" is a suffix used to form adjectives meaning "resembling, similar to."
