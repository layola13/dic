interdependence:/ˌɪntərˈdɪˈpendəns/ |n.|相互依存，互相依赖|The complex interdependence of the global economy means no country can remain isolated. 全球经济复杂的相互依存关系意味着没有哪个国家可以孤立存在。|例句：The interdependence of species within an ecosystem is essential for its survival. 生态系统内物种的相互依存对其生存至关重要。

例句：The project highlighted the interdependence of the different departments within the company. 该项目突出了公司内部不同部门之间的相互依存关系。|近义词：interrelation, interconnection, interaction, dependency|反义词：independence, autonomy|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + dependence.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally." "Dependence" comes from the Old French "dependence" and ultimately from the Latin "dependere," meaning "to hang down" or "to rely on."
