nonrevisions:/ˌnɒnriˈvɪʒənz/
noun
未修订版本；无修订
absence of revisions; documents or texts that have not been revised or altered.
The archive contained numerous nonrevisions of the manuscript, showing the author's initial thoughts.  档案馆里保存着许多未修订的手稿版本，展现了作者最初的想法。
The lawyer requested all nonrevisions of the contract to ensure accuracy.  律师要求提供合同的所有未修订版本，以确保准确性。
synonyms: original versions, first drafts, unaltered copies
antonyms: revisions, amended versions, updated copies
n.
