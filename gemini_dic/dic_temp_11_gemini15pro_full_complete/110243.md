lifequake:/ˈlaɪfˌkweɪk/| n.|生活大地震，人生巨变|A significant disruption or upheaval in one's personal life, often causing a radical shift in perspective or lifestyle.|例句：The loss of her job was a lifequake that forced her to re-evaluate her priorities. 失去工作对她来说是一场生活大地震，迫使她重新评估自己的优先事项。

例句：Having a baby can be a joyful but also a significant lifequake, changing everything from sleep schedules to long-term plans. 生孩子可以是快乐的，但也是一场重大的人生巨变，从睡眠时间表到长期计划，一切都发生了改变。

近义词：upheaval, crisis, turning point, epiphany, transformation|反义词:stability, normalcy, routine|词性:noun
