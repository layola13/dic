curtailment:/kərˈteɪlmənt/
|n.|缩减；削减；限制|The curtailment of civil liberties is a serious issue. 对公民自由的限制是一个严重的问题。|The government announced the curtailment of spending on public projects. 政府宣布削减公共项目的开支。|reduction, decrease, cutback|expansion, increase|noun
curtailed:/kərˈteɪld/
|v. (past tense)|缩减；削减；限制|The company curtailed its operations due to the economic downturn. 由于经济低迷，公司缩减了业务。|Budget cuts have curtailed the library's hours. 预算削减缩短了图书馆的开放时间。|reduced, decreased, shortened|expanded, increased|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *curtailler, frequentative of curtal "to dock, cut short," from curte "short," from Latin curtus "short."
