demolitionist:/ˌdɛməˈlɪʃənɪst/| n. |爆破专家；拆除专家|A person skilled in the use of explosives to demolish buildings or other structures.|The demolitionist carefully placed the charges to bring down the old factory. 爆破专家小心地放置炸药，以拆除旧工厂。|Synonyms: dynamiter, blaster|Antonyms: builder, constructor|noun
