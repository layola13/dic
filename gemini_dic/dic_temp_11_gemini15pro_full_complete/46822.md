Danube:/ˈdænjuːb/ (US), /ˈdænjʊb/ (UK)| n. |多瑙河|A major river in Central and Eastern Europe, flowing from Germany through Austria, Slovakia, Hungary, Croatia, Serbia, Bulgaria, Romania, Moldova, and Ukraine to the Black Sea.|The Danube River is the second longest river in Europe. 多瑙河是欧洲第二长河。|The Danube Delta is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 多瑙河三角洲是联合国教科文组织世界遗产。|近义词:river (泛指河流)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *Danuby*, from Old French *Danubi*, from Latin *Danubius*, *Danuvius*, ultimately from Celtic *Dānuvius*.
