disbursement:/dɪsˈbɜːrsmənt/ | n. |支付，支出，拨款|The act of paying out money, especially from a fund or public treasury.|The company made a large disbursement to its shareholders. 公司向其股东支付了大笔款项。|近义词：payment, expenditure, outlay|反义词:receipt, income|noun

disburse:/dɪsˈbɜːrs/ | vt. |支付，支出，拨款|To pay out (money), especially from a fund or public treasury.|The government disbursed funds for the new highway project. 政府为新的高速公路项目拨款。|近义词：pay, expend, outlay|反义词:receive, collect|verb
