hymnody:/ˈhɪmnədi/ | n. | 赞美诗; 赞美诗集; 赞美诗学 | Hymns are an important part of Christian hymnody. 赞美诗是基督教赞美诗的重要组成部分。| The church's hymnody is rich and varied. 教堂的赞美诗丰富多彩。| 近义词: hymns, psalmody | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hymnōdia, from Ancient Greek ὑμνῳδία (humnōidía, “singing of hymns”), from ὕμνος (humnos, “hymn”) + ᾠδή (ōidḗ, “song, ode”).
