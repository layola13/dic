misfunctioning:/ˌmɪsˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ/
adjective
故障的；失灵的
not working or operating correctly.
The malfunctioning equipment caused a delay in production.  失灵的设备导致生产延误。
The technician quickly diagnosed the malfunctioning component. 技术员迅速诊断出故障部件。
近义词: faulty, defective, broken, failing, inoperative
反义词: working, functioning, operational

词性: adjective
词源:  "mis-" (meaning "badly" or "wrongly") + "function" + "-ing" (present participle suffix).  The word "function" comes from the Latin "functio," meaning "performance."
