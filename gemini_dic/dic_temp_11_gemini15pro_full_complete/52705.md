digressers:/daɪˈɡrɛsərz/
| n. |离题者；跑题者|People who digress; those who deviate from the main subject in conversation or writing.  Those who stray from the main point.|The professor had to repeatedly remind the digressers in the class to stay on topic. 教授不得不反复提醒课堂上跑题的学生回到主题。|近义词：wanderers, ramblers, strays|反义词:focusers,  attenders|Noun


digress:/daɪˈɡrɛs/
| v. |跑题；离题|To deviate or wander away from the main subject or topic in speaking or writing.|He digressed from his prepared remarks to tell a personal anecdote. 他偏离了他准备好的讲话稿，讲了一个个人轶事。|近义词：deviate, stray, wander, ramble|反义词:focus, concentrate|Verb
