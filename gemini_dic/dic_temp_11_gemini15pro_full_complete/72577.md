fluxible:/ˈflʌksɪbəl/
adjective
易变的，可变的，灵活的
Readily or frequently changing; adaptable or variable.
例句：The global economy is fluxible and requires businesses to be adaptable. 全球经济瞬息万变，需要企业灵活应变。

例句：Her plans were fluxible, changing according to the weather. 她的计划是灵活的，会根据天气变化而改变。

近义词：changeable, adaptable, flexible, variable, fluid, mutable, versatile
反义词：inflexible, rigid, fixed, unchangeable, constant, stable
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin fluxibilis (“flowing”), from Latin fluxus (“a flowing”), past participle of fluere (“to flow”).
