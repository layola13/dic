overliberality:/ˌoʊvərˌlɪbəˈrælɪti/
n.
过度慷慨，过分大方
Excessive generosity; being too liberal or giving.
His overliberality with company funds led to his dismissal. 他对公司资金的过度慷慨导致了他的解雇。
Synonyms: extravagance, prodigality, lavishness
Antonyms: stinginess, parsimony, miserliness
