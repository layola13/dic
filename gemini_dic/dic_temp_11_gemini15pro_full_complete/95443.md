incircumspection:/ˌɪnsərˈkʌmspɛkʃən/ | n. | 缺乏深思熟虑；轻率；鲁莽 | Lack of caution and good judgment; rashness. | His incircumspection led to a costly mistake. 他的轻率导致了一个代价高昂的错误。|  She demonstrated incircumspection by sharing confidential information. 她因泄露机密信息而表现出轻率。 | 近义词：rashness, imprudence, recklessness, carelessness, thoughtlessness | 反义词：caution, prudence, circumspection, foresight, deliberation | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *incircumspectus (“not circumspect”), from Latin in- (“not”) + circumspectus (“circumspect”).
