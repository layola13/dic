mainstream:/ˈmeɪnstriːm/
n.|主流，主流思想|The prevailing current of thought, influence, or activity.|Hollywood movies are generally considered mainstream entertainment. 好莱坞电影通常被认为是主流娱乐。|近义词：conventional, orthodox, popular|反义词:alternative, unconventional, fringe|名词
mainstream:/ˈmeɪnstriːm/
adj.|主流的|Belonging to the prevailing current of thought, influence, or activity.|Mainstream media often avoids controversial topics. 主流媒体经常避免有争议的话题。|近义词：conventional, orthodox, established|反义词:alternative, unconventional, fringe|形容词
mainstream:/ˌmeɪnˈstriːm/
vt.|使...成为主流，纳入主流|To bring something into the mainstream.|The company is trying to mainstream its new product.  这家公司正试图将其新产品纳入主流。|近义词：popularize, normalize|反义词:marginalize|动词
