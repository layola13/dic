outwasting:/ˌaʊtˈweɪstɪŋ/
verb
过度浪费; 过度消耗
Exceeding in wasting or squandering.  Using up or spending something to a greater extent.
He was outwasting his inheritance on frivolous purchases. 他把遗产浪费在轻浮的购物上。
The company was outwasting its resources on a failing project.  这家公司在一个失败的项目上过度消耗资源。
近义词:squandering, dissipating, prodigal
反义词:saving, conserving, hoarding
词性:v.
