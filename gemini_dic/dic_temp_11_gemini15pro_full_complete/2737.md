adulatory:/ˈædʒʊləˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 奉承的，谄媚的 | Excessively praising or admiring.  Characterized by or given to adulation. |  The adulatory reviews of the movie surprised even the director.  这部电影的阿谀奉承的评论甚至让导演都感到惊讶。| synonyms: flattering, fawning, obsequious, sycophantic | antonyms: critical, disparaging, derogatory | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adulatorius*, from Latin adulat-, stem of adulari (“to fawn upon”), equivalent to ad- (“to”) + ŭlŭlāre (“to howl, wail”).
