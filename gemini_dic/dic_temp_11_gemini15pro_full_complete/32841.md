cholera: /ˈkɑːlərə/ (US) /ˈkɒlərə/ (UK)| n. |霍乱|An acute intestinal infection caused by ingestion of contaminated water or food, characterized by severe diarrhea, vomiting, and dehydration.|例句：Cholera is a serious public health concern in many developing countries. 霍乱是许多发展中国家的一个严重的公共卫生问题。

例句：The outbreak of cholera spread rapidly through the refugee camp. 霍乱疫情在难民营迅速蔓延。
