brinksmanship: /ˈbrɪŋkmənʃɪp/ | n. | 边缘政策 | The art or practice of pursuing a dangerous policy to the limits of safety before stopping, typically in politics. | The two countries engaged in a dangerous game of brinksmanship. 这两个国家进行了一场危险的边缘政策游戏。|  The Cuban Missile Crisis was a classic example of brinksmanship. 古巴导弹危机是边缘政策的典型例子。 | synonyms:  gambling, risk-taking, daredevilry, audacity | antonyms: caution, prudence, diplomacy, compromise | noun
