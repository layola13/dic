atoning:/əˈtoʊnɪŋ/
adjective
赎罪的，补偿的
making amends for something bad one has done.
例句：He was atoning for his past sins. 他正在为自己过去的罪孽赎罪。

例句：She spent the rest of her life atoning for her mistake.  她用余生来弥补她的错误。


近义词：expiating, redeeming, compensating
反义词：sinning, offending, wronging
词性: adjective
