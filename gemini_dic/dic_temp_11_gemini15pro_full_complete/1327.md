accrete:/əˈkriːt/| v.|增长，累积，逐渐增加|To grow or increase gradually by the addition of new layers or particles.|例句：The snow accreted on the roof overnight. 屋顶上的雪一夜之间积了起来。|例句：Over millions of years, dust accreted to form planets. 数百万年来，灰尘积聚形成了行星。|近义词：accumulate, gather, amass, collect|反义词:erode, diminish, decrease|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin accrescere "to grow larger," from ad- "to" + crescere "to grow."
