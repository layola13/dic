overrigorous:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪɡərəs/ | adj. | 过于严格的，过于严谨的 | Excessively rigorous; too strict or precise.  Characterized by excessive rigor, demanding an unnecessary degree of precision or exactness. |  The grading system was considered overrigorous by many students.  许多学生认为评分系统过于严格。

The overrigorous application of the rules stifled creativity. 过于严格地应用规则扼杀了创造力。 | 近义词: strict, stringent, rigid, inflexible, Draconian | 反义词: lenient, lax, flexible, permissive | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "rigorous" (from Latin rigor "stiffness, hardness," from rigēre "to be stiff")
