counterbalancer:/ˌkaʊntərˈbælənsər/| n.|平衡器，平衡重，抗衡因素|A counterbalancer is something that offsets or balances the effect of something else. |The heavy base acts as a counterbalancer, preventing the lamp from tipping over.  沉重的底座起到平衡器的作用，防止灯倾倒。|Synonyms: counterpoise, equalizer, offset|Antonyms: catalyst, instigator|noun

counterbalance:/ˌkaʊntərˈbæləns/| n. vt.|平衡，抵消；平衡物，抗衡力|As a noun, it refers to something that offsets or balances the effect of something else. As a verb, it means to offset or balance the effect of something. |The tightrope walker used a long pole as a counterbalance.  走钢丝的人用一根长杆来保持平衡。|Synonyms: compensate for, neutralize, counteract|Antonyms: aggravate, exacerbate|noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + balance.  Counter- means "against" or "opposite," and balance refers to a state of equilibrium.
