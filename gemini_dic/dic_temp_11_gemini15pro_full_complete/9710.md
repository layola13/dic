antitrochanter:/ˌæntiˈtroʊkæntər/ | n. |  对转子，反转子 | A process on the ilium of the pelvis of birds and some reptiles that articulates with the trochanter of the femur. It provides additional support and stability to the hip joint. | The antitrochanter of the bird's pelvis helps it to maintain balance while walking.  鸟类骨盆的对转子有助于它在行走时保持平衡。 | Near synonyms:  iliac process,  pelvic process | Antonyms: N/A (anatomical term, lacks a direct opposite) | noun
