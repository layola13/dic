macrolinguistics:/ˌmækroʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks/ | n. |宏观语言学|The study of language in relation to social, cultural, and behavioral factors, rather than the internal structure of language itself.  It often encompasses sociolinguistics, psycholinguistics, anthropological linguistics, and dialectology. | Macrolinguistics examines how social factors influence language change. 宏观语言学研究社会因素如何影响语言变化。| 近义词:  sociolinguistics, language and society | 反义词: microlinguistics | 词性: noun
