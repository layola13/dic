greener:/ˈɡriːnər/| adj.|更绿的; 更环保的|Comparative form of "green."  It means having more of the qualities associated with the color green, such as being covered with vegetation or being environmentally friendly.|例句：The grass is greener on the other side.  这山望着那山高。

例句：We need to find greener ways to generate electricity. 我们需要找到更环保的发电方式。|近义词：more environmentally friendly, more sustainable|反义词：less green, browner, less sustainable|词性:形容词
