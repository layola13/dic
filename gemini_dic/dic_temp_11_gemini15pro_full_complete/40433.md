contrary:/ˈkɒntrəri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntrɛri/ (US)| adj. n. adv.|相反的，对立的；相反的事物；反面|Opposite in nature, direction, or meaning.|例句：He acted contrary to my advice. 他违背了我的建议。|近义词：opposite, opposing, conflicting, adverse, reverse|反义词：similar, alike, consistent, agreeable, supporting|词性:形容词

contrary:/ˈkɒntrəri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntrɛri/ (US)| n.|相反，相反的事物|Something that is the opposite or reverse of something else.|例句：The contrary of love is not hate, but indifference.  爱的反面不是恨，而是冷漠。|近义词：opposite, reverse, antithesis|反义词：same, identical|词性:名词

contrary:/ˈkɒntrəri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntrɛri/ (US)| adv.|相反地，反之|In an opposite or contrasting manner.|例句：Contrary to popular belief, he is actually quite shy. 与普遍的看法相反，他其实很害羞。|近义词：conversely, oppositely|反义词：similarly, likewise|词性:副词
