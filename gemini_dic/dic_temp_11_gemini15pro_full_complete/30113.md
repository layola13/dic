cedared:/ˈsiːdərd/ | adj. |雪松覆盖的；雪松木制的|Covered with cedar trees; made of cedar wood.|例句：The house had a pleasant, cedared scent. 这房子有一种令人愉悦的雪松香味。|例句：They relaxed in the shade of the cedared hillside. 他们在雪松覆盖的山坡的树荫下放松。|近义词：cedar-covered, cedar-wood |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 treeless, bare) |词性:形容词

词源: From cedar + -ed (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin cedrus, from Greek kedros.
