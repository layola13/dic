oratorio:/ˌɔːrəˈtɔːrioʊ/ (US), /ˌɒrəˈtɔːriəʊ/ (UK)| n. |清唱剧|A large musical composition for orchestra, choir, and soloists, usually on a religious theme, performed without costumes, scenery, or action.| Handel's "Messiah" is a famous oratorio.  亨德尔的《弥赛亚》是一部著名的清唱剧。| synonym: cantata, sacred music | antonym: opera | noun
