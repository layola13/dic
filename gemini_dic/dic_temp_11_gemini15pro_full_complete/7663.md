anode:/ˈænəʊd/ (UK) /ˈænˌoʊd/ (US)| n.|阳极，正极|The positive electrode in an electrolytic cell, toward which the negatively charged ions (anions) migrate.|Electrons flow from the anode to the cathode. 电子从阳极流向阴极。|The anode corroded quickly in the experiment. 在实验中，阳极腐蚀得很快。|Synonyms: positive electrode, positive pole|Antonyms: cathode, negative electrode|noun
