almightiship:/ɔːlˈmaɪtinʃɪp/ | n. | 全能;万能;上帝的权威 | The state or quality of being almighty; omnipotence. |  The painting depicts the almightiship of God. 这幅画描绘了上帝的万能。 | synonyms: omnipotence, supremacy, sovereignty | antonyms: powerlessness, weakness, impotence | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From almighty + -ship.  Almighty comes from Old English *eallmihtig (all-mighty), from eall (all) + mihtig (mighty).  The suffix -ship denotes state or condition.
