alterity:/ˌɔːlˈterɪti/ | n. |差异性，他者性 | The state of being other or different; otherness.  The quality or state of being radically unlike or opposite to something else. |  Her work explores the concept of alterity and the challenges of intercultural understanding. 她的作品探讨了差异性的概念以及跨文化理解的挑战。| synonyms: otherness, difference, distinctness, diversity, unlikeness, foreignness, strangeness | antonyms: sameness, similarity, identity, likeness, uniformity | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *alteritas*, from Latin alter ("other").
