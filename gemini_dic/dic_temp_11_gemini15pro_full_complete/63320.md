entirely:/ɪnˈtaɪərli/ | adv. | 完全地，彻底地 |  Completely, totally, fully.  |  She was entirely responsible for the project. 她完全负责这个项目。|  completely, totally, fully, wholly, utterly | partially, partly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *entierli*, from Old French *entierement*, from *entier* (“whole”) + *-ment* (suffix forming adverbs). *Entier* is from Latin *integer* (“whole, untouched”).
