on-the-bias:/ˌɒn ðə ˈbaɪəs/ | adj. | 有偏见的；不公平的 | Operating or functioning with inherent bias; exhibiting systematic distortion or deviation from a neutral or objective standard, especially in data or algorithms. |  The algorithm was found to be on-the-bias, favoring certain demographics over others. 该算法被发现存在偏见，偏向某些人口群体。 |  The on-the-bias reporting unfairly portrayed the candidate. 有偏见的报道不公平地描绘了这位候选人。 | Synonyms: biased, prejudiced, unfair, partial, discriminatory | Antonyms: unbiased, impartial, fair, objective, neutral | adjective
