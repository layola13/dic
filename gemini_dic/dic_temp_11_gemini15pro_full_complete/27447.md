camouflageable:/ˌkæməˈflɑːʒəbl/
adjective
可伪装的，可隐藏的
able to be camouflaged.
The chameleon is highly camouflageable, blending seamlessly into its surroundings. 变色龙非常善于伪装，可以完美地融入周围环境。
Camouflageable clothing is essential for soldiers in combat.  对作战中的士兵来说，可伪装的衣服是必不可少的。
近义词:disguisable, concealable
反义词:conspicuous, noticeable
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From camouflage + -able.  Camouflage itself comes from the French camoufler "to disguise," a slang term from the early 20th century.
