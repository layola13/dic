bitchin:/ˈbɪtʃɪn/ /ˈbɪtʃən/
| adj. |极好的，很棒的；令人不愉快的，糟糕的 (俚语) |Exceptionally good, excellent; unpleasant, bad (slang).  It has two opposing meanings depending on context and tone. |That concert was bitchin'! 那场音乐会真是太棒了！|近义词：awesome, excellent, cool, amazing (positive); awful, terrible, lousy (negative) |反义词：awful, terrible, lousy (positive); awesome, excellent, cool (negative)|形容词

bitchin':/ˈbɪtʃɪn/ /ˈbɪtʃən/
