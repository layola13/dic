ambassadorship:/æmˈbæsədərʃɪp/| n.|大使的职位；大使身份|The position or office of an ambassador.  The period of time during which someone is an ambassador.|She was offered the ambassadorship to France. 她被任命为驻法国大使。|Synonyms: embassy, legation, diplomatic mission|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider "exile," "banishment")|Noun
