alexandrines:/ˌæˌlɪɡˈzændraɪnz/ | n. | 十二音步诗; 亚历山大体诗 |A line of verse having six iambic feet (12 syllables).|  Example: The poem was written entirely in alexandrines. 这首诗完全是用十二音步诗写的。 | Example:  He preferred the stately rhythm of alexandrines. 他更喜欢十二音步诗庄重的节奏。 | Synonym: iambic hexameter | Antonym: N/A | Noun
