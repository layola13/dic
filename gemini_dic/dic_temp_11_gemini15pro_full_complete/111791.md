loftinesses:/ˈlɔːftɪnɪsɪz/
n.
崇高，高尚；高傲，傲慢
The state or quality of being lofty; elevation, especially in a moral or spiritual sense; haughty demeanor.
The loftinesses of his ideals inspired many. 他的崇高理想激励了许多人。
近义词：haughtiness, arrogance, pride, nobility, grandeur
反义词：humility, modesty, lowliness
词性: noun (plural)

loftiness:/ˈlɔːftɪnɪs/
n.
崇高，高尚；高傲，傲慢
The state or quality of being lofty; elevation, especially in a moral or spiritual sense; haughty demeanor.
His loftiness made him difficult to approach. 他的高傲使他难以接近。
近义词：haughtiness, arrogance, pride, nobility, grandeur
反义词：humility, modesty, lowliness
词性: noun

lofty:/ˈlɔːfti/
adj.
高的；崇高的；高傲的
Of imposing height; elevated in character or ideals; haughty.
He had lofty ambitions. 他有崇高的抱负。
近义词：high, tall, elevated, noble, haughty
反义词：low, humble, modest
词性: adjective
