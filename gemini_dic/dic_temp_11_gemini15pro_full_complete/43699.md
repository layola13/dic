credited-with:/ˈkrɛdɪtɪd wɪð/
verb phrase
被认为是…的功臣；被认为是…的原因
believed to be the reason for something;  officially recognized as having achieved something
Marie Curie is credited with the discovery of radium. 玛丽·居里被认为是镭的发现者。
The invention of the telephone is credited to Alexander Graham Bell. 电话的发明被认为是亚历山大·格雷厄姆·贝尔的功劳。
Synonyms: attributed to, ascribed to
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "denied credit for," "disputed attribution")
词性: 动词短语
