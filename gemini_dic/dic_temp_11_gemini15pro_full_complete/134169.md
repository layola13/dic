nonincandescently:/ˌnɒnɪnkænˈdɛsəntli/
adverb|非白炽地，不发白炽光地|Not incandescently; without emitting light as a result of being heated.|The stars shone nonincandescently, reflecting the light of a distant sun.|近义词:dully, dimly|反义词:brightly, incandescently|词性:副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non "not".
incandescent (adjective): From Latin incandescens, present participle of incandescere "to glow, to become white-hot," from in- "into, in, on, upon" + candescere "to become white, to glow white," from candere "to shine, to be white," related to candidus "white, bright, pure, sincere."
-ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
