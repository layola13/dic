ministers:/ˈmɪnɪstərz/
n.
部长；大臣；牧师
A head of a government department. A member of the clergy, especially Protestant churches.
The Prime Minister and his ministers discussed the matter. 首相和他的部长们讨论了这件事。
The minister delivered a moving sermon. 牧师发表了一篇感人的布道。
synonyms:secretary, clergyman, pastor, preacher, vicar
antonyms:congregant, parishioner
noun
