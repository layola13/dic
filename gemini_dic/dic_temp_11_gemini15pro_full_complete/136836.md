nonsilver:/nɑːnˈsɪlvər/
adjective
非银的；非银质的
Not made of silver; containing no silver.
The nonsilver coins are made of copper and nickel. 这些非银币是由铜和镍制成的。
Near Synonyms: base metal, non-argentic
Antonyms: silver, argent
词性: 形容词
