encrustment:/ɪnˈkrʌstmənt/
n.|结壳，外壳，硬壳|A hard coating or deposit on a surface.|The pipe was clogged with an encrustment of minerals.|近义词：coating, deposit, crust, scale|反义词: erosion, wearing away
词性: 名词


encrust:/ɪnˈkrʌst/
vt.|使结壳，包上硬壳|To cover or coat with a hard crust.|Barnacles encrusted the hull of the ship.|近义词：coat, cover, overlay|反义词: strip, uncover
词性: 动词
