manumit:/ˌmænjuˈmɪt/ | vt. | 解放；释放（奴隶） | To release from slavery or servitude; to emancipate. |  The plantation owner manumitted all of his slaves in his will.  种植园主在他的遗嘱中解放了他所有的奴隶。

The law allowed slave owners to manumit their slaves under certain conditions. 该法律允许奴隶主在某些条件下解放他们的奴隶。 | 近义词：liberate, free, emancipate, release | 反义词：enslave, imprison, confine | 词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *manūmittere* "to release from one's hand (manus), to free a slave," from *manus* "hand" + *mittere* "to send, let go."
