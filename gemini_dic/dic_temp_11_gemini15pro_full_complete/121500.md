mislearn:/ˌmɪsˈlɜːrn/ | v. | 学错；误学 | To learn something incorrectly or acquire false information. |  He mislearned the formula and got the wrong answer. 他学错了公式，所以得到了错误的答案。

She mislearned the capital of France as Rome. 她把法国的首都误学成了罗马。| 近义词: learn incorrectly, misunderstand, misconceive | 反义词: learn correctly, understand | 词性:动词
