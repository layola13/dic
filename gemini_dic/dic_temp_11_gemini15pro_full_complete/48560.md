defamingly:/dɪˈfeɪmɪŋli/ | adv. | 诽谤地，中伤地 | in a way that damages the reputation of someone or something | He spoke defamingly about his former employer. 他诽谤地谈论他的前任雇主。| 近义词: slanderously, libelously, maliciously | 反义词: respectfully, flatteringly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "defame" + suffix "-ingly."  "Defame" comes from Old French "diffamer," from Latin "diffamare" (to spread abroad an evil report), from "dis-" (apart, asunder) + "fama" (reputation, fame).
