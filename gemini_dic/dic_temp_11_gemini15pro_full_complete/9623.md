antistatism:/ˌæntiˈstætɪzəm/
|n.|反国家主义，反政府主义|Opposition to state intervention or control, especially in economic and social affairs.|The growing antistatism among the population led to protests against government regulations.  民众日益增长的反国家主义情绪导致了反对政府监管的抗议活动。|Synonyms: libertarianism, anarchism|Antonyms: statism, authoritarianism|noun


antistatisms:/ˌæntiˈstætɪzəmz/
|n.|反国家主义，反政府主义（复数）|Multiple instances or forms of opposition to state intervention or control, especially in economic and social affairs.|The author's book explored the various antistatisms that emerged during the 20th century. 作者的书探讨了20世纪出现的各种反国家主义形式。|Synonyms: libertarianisms, anarchisms|Antonyms: statisms, authoritarianisms|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (Greek): against, opposed to
statism (from state): the principle or policy of concentrating extensive economic, political, and related controls in the state
