consequent:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnsɪkwent/ (US)| adj. n.|结果的，随之发生的；结果，推论|resulting from something that has happened previously; a thing that follows another.|例句：The earthquake and consequent tsunami caused widespread devastation. 地震和随之而来的海啸造成了大范围的破坏。|近义词：resulting, ensuing, subsequent, following|反义词：prior, preceding, antecedent|词性:形容词

consequent:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnsɪkwent/ (US)| n.|结果，推论|a thing that follows another.|例句：One consequent of global warming is rising sea levels. 全球变暖的一个后果是海平面上升。|近义词：result, outcome, effect|反义词：cause, origin|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin consequent- ‘following closely’, from the verb consequi, from con- ‘together with’ + sequi ‘to follow’.
