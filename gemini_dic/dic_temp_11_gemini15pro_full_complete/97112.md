infidel:/ˈɪnfɪdəl/ | n. |异教徒，不信教者|A person who does not believe in a particular religion, especially Christianity or Islam. |例句：The crusaders fought against the infidels. 十字军与异教徒作战。

例句：In the past, people of different faiths were often labeled as infidels. 过去，不同信仰的人经常被贴上异教徒的标签。|近义词：nonbeliever, unbeliever, heretic, pagan|反义词：believer, faithful|词性：名词

infidel:/ˈɪnfɪdəl/ | adj. |不信教的，异端的|Not believing in a particular religion, especially Christianity or Islam. |例句：Infidel beliefs were considered dangerous and subversive. 异端信仰被认为是危险和颠覆性的。

例句：The king declared war on the infidel nations. 国王向不信教的国家宣战。|近义词：unbelieving, heretical, pagan|反义词：believing, faithful|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *infidele*, from Latin infidelis “unfaithful, unbelieving,” from in- “not” + fidelis “faithful” (from fides “faith”).
