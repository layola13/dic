forcefully:/ˈfɔːrsfəli/ (US), /ˈfɔːsfʊli/ (UK)| adv.|强有力地，强制地|In a strong and assertive manner; using force or coercion.|He argued his point forcefully. 他强有力地阐述了他的观点。|She forcefully pushed the door open. 她用力地把门推开。|近义词：powerfully, strongly, vigorously, emphatically, compellingly|反义词：weakly, gently, mildly, passively|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "force" (from Old French "force," from Latin "fortis" meaning "strong") + "-ful" (suffix indicating "full of" or "characterized by") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
