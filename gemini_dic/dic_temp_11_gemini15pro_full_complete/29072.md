carvel-built: /ˈkɑːrvəlˌbɪlt/ | adj. | 卡拉维尔帆船建造的；（尤指船）坚固的，精巧建造的 |  Built like a carvel, a type of ship known for its sturdy construction. Refers to a method of boatbuilding where planks are laid edge-to-edge and caulked.  Can also describe something built with similar sturdiness and craftsmanship. | The carvel-built yacht was admired for its sleek lines and robust hull. 这艘卡拉维尔帆船建造的游艇因其流畅的线条和坚固的船体而备受赞赏。 | Near synonyms:  sturdily built, robustly constructed, well-built, solidly built | Antonym: flimsy, poorly built | adjective
