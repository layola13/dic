folk-rock: /ˈfəʊk rɒk/ (UK) /ˈfoʊk rɑːk/ (US)| n.|民俗摇滚|A genre of popular music that combines elements of folk music and rock music.|Bob Dylan's early work is considered a cornerstone of folk-rock. 鲍勃·迪伦的早期作品被认为是民谣摇滚的基石。

The Byrds popularized folk-rock in the mid-1960s.  伯兹乐队在20世纪60年代中期推广了民谣摇滚。|近义词: folk-pop, roots rock|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps genres like heavy metal or electronic music)|词性: noun
