mayday:/ˈmeɪdeɪ/| n. int.|求救信号；五月一日|A distress signal (especially used in radio communication). Also refers to May 1st, especially as a traditional spring festival or as an international labor day.|例句：The ship sent out a mayday call.  这艘船发出了求救信号。|近义词：SOS, distress call, emergency call|反义词: (No direct antonym for a distress call)|词性: noun, interjection

mayday:/ˈmeɪdeɪ/| n.|五月一日|May 1st, particularly celebrated as International Workers' Day or Labour Day in many countries. Also a traditional spring festival.|例句：Mayday celebrations are held around the world. 全世界都在庆祝五一劳动节。|近义词：International Workers' Day, Labour Day, May Day|反义词: (No direct antonym for a date)|词性: noun
