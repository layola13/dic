colorblind:/ˈkʌlərblaɪnd/| adj.|色盲的|Unable to distinguish certain colors, wholly or partly.|例句：He's colorblind and can't tell the difference between red and green. 他是色盲，分不清红色和绿色。

例句：My uncle is colorblind, so he has trouble matching his clothes. 我叔叔是色盲，所以他搭配衣服有困难。|近义词：color-deficient|反义词：N/A|词性:形容词
Etymology: color + blind.  "unable to distinguish colors," 1881, from color (n.) + blind (adj.).  Earlier (1855) as a noun, "a person who is colorblind."
