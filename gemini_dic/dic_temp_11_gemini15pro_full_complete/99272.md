interconnected:/ˌɪntər.kəˈnɛktɪd/
adjective
互相连接的；互相联系的
mutually joined or related.
The global economy is now so interconnected that a crisis in one country can quickly spread to others. 全球经济现在联系如此紧密，以至于一个国家的危机可以迅速蔓延到其他国家。
近义词：interrelated, linked, networked, intertwined
反义词：separate, detached, isolated, independent
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From inter- + connect + -ed.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," derived from Latin. "Connect" comes from the Latin "connectere," meaning "to bind together."  "-ed" is a suffix used to form the past participle of verbs, and in this case creates an adjective.
