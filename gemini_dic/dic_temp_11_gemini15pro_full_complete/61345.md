embeds:/ɪmˈbedz/ | n. | (通常指计算机)嵌入 | A representation of something (such as a video or map) that is included in a webpage, email, or other electronic message. | I added some video embeds to the blog post. 我在博客文章中添加了一些视频嵌入。| insertion, integration | removal, extraction | noun
