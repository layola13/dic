miscopies:/ˌmɪsˈkɒpiz/ | n. | 错误副本；抄写错误 | An act or instance of copying something incorrectly. | The document was full of miscopies and had to be redone.  这份文件充满了抄写错误，不得不重做。| synonyms: errors, mistakes, inaccuracies | antonyms: accurate copies, correct transcriptions | noun
miscopy:/ˌmɪsˈkɒpi/ | v. | 错抄; 抄写错误 | To make an error in copying something. | He miscopied the phone number and couldn't reach his friend. 他抄错了电话号码，联系不上他的朋友。| synonyms: transcribe incorrectly, reproduce wrongly | antonyms: copy correctly, transcribe accurately | verb
