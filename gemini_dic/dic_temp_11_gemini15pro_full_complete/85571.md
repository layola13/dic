haters:/ˈheɪtərz/ (US), /ˈheɪtəz/ (UK)| n.| 憎恨者，仇恨者，讨厌者|A person who greatly dislikes a specified person or thing.|例句：The singer ignored her haters and focused on her fans.  这位歌手无视她的黑粉，专注于她的粉丝们。

例句：He's attracted a lot of haters with his controversial opinions. 他因其有争议的观点招致了许多仇恨者。|近义词：critics, detractors, adversaries, enemies|反义词：fans, supporters, admirers|词性:名词
