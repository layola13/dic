lustiest:/ˈlʌstiɪst/| adj.|最健壮的；最精力充沛的；最热情的；最渴望的|Having or showing great vigor, energy, enthusiasm, or desire.|例句：He gave the lushest performance of his career. 他奉献了他职业生涯中最精彩的演出。|例句：The garden boasts the lushest foliage I've ever seen.  这个花园拥有我见过的最茂盛的叶子。|近义词：vigorous, robust, hearty, enthusiastic, passionate, eager|反义词：weak, feeble, frail, apathetic, indifferent|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *lusti, from Old English *lustī, *lystig (“pleasant, beautiful, joyful, merry, desirable, vigorous, lustful”), from Proto-Germanic *lustijaz (“desirable, pleasant”). Related to lust.
