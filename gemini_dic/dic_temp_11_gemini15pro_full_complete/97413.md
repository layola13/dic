infringing:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective
侵权的
acting so as to violate a law or right, especially patent, copyright, or trademark.
The company was accused of infringing on a competitor's patent.  该公司被指控侵犯了竞争对手的专利。
synonyms: violating, breaching, contravening, breaking, transgressing, disobeying, defying
antonyms: complying with, obeying, respecting, upholding, honoring
词性: adjective

infringe:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒ/
verb
侵犯
act so as to limit or undermine (something); encroach on.
They infringed on the homeowner’s privacy by taking photos through the window. 他们通过窗户拍照，侵犯了房主的隐私。
synonyms: encroach on, impinge on, intrude on, trespass on, invade, violate, breach, contravene
antonyms: comply with, obey, respect, uphold, honor
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French infringir, from Latin infringere "to break, shatter, weaken, violate, invalidate," from in- "in, into, on, upon" + frangere "to break."
