dozedness: /ˈdoʊzɪdnəs/ /ˈdəʊzɪdnəs/
noun
昏昏欲睡，困倦
The state of being sleepy or drowsy.
例句：
The long drive induced a state of dozedness. 长途驾驶使人昏昏欲睡。

He struggled to overcome his dozedness during the meeting.  他在会议中努力克服困意。


近义词：sleepiness, drowsiness, somnolence
反义词：wakefulness, alertness, vigilance
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From doze (v.) + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  Doze ultimately comes from Old English *dūsian, of uncertain origin.
