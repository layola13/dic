homogenize:/həˈmɒdʒənaɪz/, /həˈmɑːdʒənaɪz/ | vt. | 使均匀，使均质化 | To make something uniform or consistent throughout. To break down the particles in (a substance such as milk or paint) by forcing it through small openings. | The milk is homogenized to prevent the cream from separating. 牛奶经过均质化处理以防止奶油分离。

The city council tried to homogenize the different neighborhoods. 市议会试图使不同的街区同质化。 | 近义词：blend, mix, standardize, uniform | 反义词：separate, diversify, vary | 动词

homogenization:/həˌmɒdʒənaɪˈzeɪʃən/, /həˌmɑːdʒənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 均质化，同质化 | The process of making something uniform or consistent throughout. | The homogenization of culture can lead to a loss of unique traditions. 文化的同质化会导致独特传统的丧失。

The homogenization of milk prevents cream from separating. 牛奶的均质化可以防止奶油分离。| 近义词：standardization, uniformity | 反义词：diversification, variation | 名词
