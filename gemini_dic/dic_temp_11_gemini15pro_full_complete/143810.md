outpainting:/ˈaʊtˌpeɪntɪŋ/
n.
外绘，扩展绘画
The process of extending an existing image by generating new image content beyond its original boundaries.
The AI uses outpainting to create a wider landscape around the original photo.  AI使用外绘技术在原始照片周围创建更广阔的景观。
extrapolation, extension, expansion
inpainting, cropping, reduction
noun
