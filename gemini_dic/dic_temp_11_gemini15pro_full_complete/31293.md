chapiter:/ˈtʃæptər/ | n. |章，回，篇|A main division of a book, treatise, or the like.  A period or stage in a history or in a person's life. |例句：The first chapter of the book introduces the main characters.  这本书的第一章介绍了主要人物。

例句：He's starting a new chapter in his life. 他正在开启人生的新篇章。|近义词：section, part, division |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


Etymology:  From Old French "chapitre," from Late Latin "capitulum" (little head), diminutive of Latin "caput" (head).  Originally referring to the headings of chapters in books, which were often more prominent than the body text.
