argonautic:/ˌɑːrɡəˈnɔːtɪk/
adjective
阿尔戈英雄的；冒险的
Relating to the Argonauts, the heroes who sailed with Jason in search of the Golden Fleece; adventurous.
Jason's argonautic expedition was fraught with peril.  杰森的阿尔戈英雄探险充满了危险。
The team embarked on an argonautic quest for the lost treasure.  团队开始了寻找失落宝藏的冒险之旅。
近义词:adventurous, daring, heroic, epic
反义词:cowardly, timid, cautious
