intercollegiate:/ˌɪntərˌkəˈliːdʒɪət/
adjective
大学间的；学院间的
existing or occurring between different colleges
例句：The intercollegiate athletics competition drew large crowds.  大学间的体育比赛吸引了大批观众。

例句：She participated in intercollegiate debate tournaments. 她参加了校际辩论比赛。

近义词：interscholastic, interuniversity
反义词：intramural

词性：adjective
