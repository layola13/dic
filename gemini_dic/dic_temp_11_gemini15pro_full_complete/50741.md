derricks:/ˈderɪks/| n.|井架，起重机|A derrick is a kind of crane with a movable pivoted arm used for lifting heavy objects.  井架是一种带有可移动枢轴臂的起重机，用于吊起重物。|The derricks were used to hoist the heavy sections of the bridge into place. 井架被用来把桥梁的沉重部分吊装到位。|The derricks on the oil rig were constantly in motion. 石油钻井平台上的井架不停地运转着。|近义词：crane, hoist|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "support" in some contexts) |词性:noun
