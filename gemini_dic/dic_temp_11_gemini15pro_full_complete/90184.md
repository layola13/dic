hotspot:/ˈhɒtspɒt/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtspɑːt/ (US)| n.|热点，热门地点；危险地区；无线网络覆盖区域|A place of significant activity, danger, or controversy; a location offering public wireless internet access.|例句：This cafe is a popular hotspot for students.  这家咖啡馆是学生们的热门聚集地。

例句：The border region remains a hotspot for political tension.  边境地区仍然是政治紧张局势的热点地区。|近义词：center, hub, focal point, access point|反义词:backwater, quiet zone|词性:noun
