markups: /ˈmɑːrkʌps/ (US) /ˈmɑːkʌps/ (UK)| n.| 涨价，加价，成本加成 |An amount added to the cost price of goods to cover overhead and profit. |The store has a 50% markup on all its clothes. 这家商店所有衣服的加价幅度都是50%。|synonyms: increase, rise, boost|antonyms: markdown, discount, reduction|noun


markups: /ˈmɑːrkʌps/ (US) /ˈmɑːkʌps/ (UK)| n.| (文件的)标记，注释 |Notes or other marks made on a document. |The editor's markups on the manuscript were extensive. 编辑在手稿上的标记很广泛。|synonyms: annotations, notes, comments|antonyms: deletions, erasures|noun
