idolatrical:/ˌaɪdəˈlætrɪk(ə)l/| adj.|偶像崇拜的；盲目崇拜的|Relating to or characterized by idolatry; excessively devoted or admiring.|例句：Their idolatrical practices were condemned by the religious authorities. 他们的偶像崇拜行为受到了宗教当局的谴责。

例句：She displayed an almost idolatrical reverence for her mentor. 她对她的导师表现出近乎盲目崇拜的尊敬。|近义词：idolatrous, worshipping, adoring, revering, devoted|反义词：irreverent, impious, skeptical|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *idolatricus, from Greek eidōlatrikos "given to idolatry," from eidōlatrēs "idolater" (see idolater).
