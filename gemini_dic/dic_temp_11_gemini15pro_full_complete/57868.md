drg:/drʌɡ/ | n. | 诊断相关组 | Diagnosis-Related Group (A system to classify hospital cases into groups expected to require similar hospital resources).  |  The hospital uses DRGs to determine how much to charge for a patient's stay.  这家医院使用诊断相关组来确定病人住院的费用。 | synonyms: classification, category | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly "individual case") | noun

drg:/drʌɡ/ | abbreviation | 诊断相关组 | Diagnosis-Related Group |  The DRG for this patient's surgery is 470.  这位病人的手术诊断相关组是470。 | synonyms: DRG code, case mix group | antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | abbreviation
