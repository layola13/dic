bombardment:/bɒmˈbɑːdmənt/ (UK), /bɑːmˈbɑːrdmənt/ (US)| n.|轰炸，炮击，攻击|A continuous attack with bombs, shells, or other missiles.  A barrage of questions or criticisms.|The city was under heavy bombardment. 这座城市遭受猛烈轰炸。|Synonyms: shelling, barrage, cannonade, onslaught, assault|Antonyms: defense, protection|noun

bombard:/bɒmˈbɑːd/ (UK), /bɑːmˈbɑːrd/ (US)| v.|轰炸，炮击，攻击|Attack (a place or person) continuously with bombs, shells, or other missiles.  Barrage (someone) with questions, criticisms, or information.|The troops bombarded the enemy positions.  部队炮击了敌人的阵地。|Synonyms: shell, barrage, cannonade, attack, assail|Antonyms: defend, protect|verb
