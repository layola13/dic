melainotype:/məˈlaɪnəˌtaɪp/| n. |黑色照片，铁板照片|A photograph made by a process in which a collodion negative on a blackened metal or glass plate produces a positive image.|Example: The photographer specialized in creating beautiful melainotypes of Civil War soldiers.  这位摄影师专门创作南北战争时期士兵的精美铁板照片。

Example:  Melainotypes were popular in the mid-19th century due to their sharp detail and durability.  铁板照片因其清晰的细节和耐用性在19世纪中期很受欢迎。|近义词：ferrotype, tintype|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "digital photograph" as a modern contrast)|词性: noun
