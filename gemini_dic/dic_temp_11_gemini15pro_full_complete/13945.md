auntre:/ˈɑːntri/ | n. | 洞穴，岩洞 | A natural underground cavity, typically one with an opening to the surface. |  He discovered a hidden auntre in the mountains. 他在山中发现了一个隐蔽的洞穴。| synonyms: cave, cavern, grotto | antonyms: surface, exterior | noun

Etymology: From Old French *antre, from Latin antrum, from Greek ἄντρον (ántron), "cave, grotto".
