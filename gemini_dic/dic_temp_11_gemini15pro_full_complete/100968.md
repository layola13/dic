inutility:/ˌɪnjuːˈtɪləti/
n.
无用, 无效, 无益
lack of utility or usefulness; uselessness.
The inutility of their efforts was apparent to everyone. 他们的努力是徒劳的，这一点每个人都看得出来。

近义词: uselessness, futility, inefficacy, pointlessness
反义词: utility, usefulness, effectiveness, value
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *inutilité*, from *inutile* (“useless”), from Latin *inutilis*, from *in-* (“not”) + *utilis* (“useful”).
