meliboean:/ˌmɛlɪˈbiːən/| adj.|田园牧歌式的，乡村的|Relating to or characteristic of a shepherd's life; pastoral; rustic; idyllic.|例句：The painting depicted a meliboean scene of shepherds tending their flocks in a sun-drenched meadow. 这幅画描绘了一个田园牧歌般的场景：牧羊人在阳光普照的草地上放牧羊群。

例句：Her poetry evokes a meliboean world of peace and tranquility. 她的诗歌唤起了一个和平宁静的田园世界。|近义词：pastoral, bucolic, idyllic, rustic|反义词：urban, metropolitan, sophisticated|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->

From Latin *Meliboeus*, a shepherd in Virgil's *Eclogues*.  The name itself likely comes from Greek *μελίβοιος (meliboios)* meaning "caring for oxen" or "herding cattle."
