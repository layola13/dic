inextinct:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɪŋkt/
adjective
未灭绝的，现存的
not extinct; still in existence
例句：The coelacanth, once thought extinct, is now known to be inextinct.  腔棘鱼，曾经被认为已经灭绝，现在已知仍然存在。

例句：Surprisingly, several species of birds previously classified as extinct are now considered inextinct.  令人惊讶的是，一些以前被列为灭绝的鸟类现在被认为仍然存在。
近义词：existent, extant, surviving, living
反义词：extinct, vanished, lost
