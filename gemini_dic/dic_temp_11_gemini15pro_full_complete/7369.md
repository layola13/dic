animadvert:/ˌænɪmædˈvɜːrt/
|v.|批评；非难；评论|To make (usually critical) remarks about something or someone.|The teacher animadverted on the student's poor performance in the exam. 老师批评了学生在考试中糟糕的表现。|Synonyms: criticize, censure, denounce, comment, remark|Antonyms: praise, commend, laud|verb

animadvert:/ˌænɪmædˈvɜːrt/
|v.|评论|To comment on something.|The critic animadverted on the film's unusual plot.  评论家评论了这部电影不同寻常的情节。|Synonyms: comment, remark, observe|Antonyms: ignore, overlook|verb
