gutterling:/ˈɡʌtərlɪŋ/| n. |街头顽童; 流浪儿; 小流氓|A young street urchin, often mischievous or delinquent.  A child who lives or plays in the streets.|The gutterlings were throwing rocks at the passing cars. 这些街头顽童向过往的车辆投掷石块.|Synonyms: urchin, waif, ragamuffin, street arab, scamp|Antonyms: privileged child, well-behaved child|Noun
