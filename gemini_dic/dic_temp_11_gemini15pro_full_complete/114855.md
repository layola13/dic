mammilliform:/ˈmæmɪlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
乳头状的，乳头形的
Resembling a nipple or teat in shape; shaped like a breast or nipple.
The cactus has mammilliform tubercles.  这种仙人掌有乳头状的瘤块。

近义词: nipple-shaped, teat-shaped, papilliform
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., flat, concave)
词性: adjective
