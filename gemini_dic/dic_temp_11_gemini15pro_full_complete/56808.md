doricism: /ˈdɔːrɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 古朴风格；多利安式；多利安方言 |  A style of classical architecture characterized by simplicity and strength, originating in the Dorian order of ancient Greece.  Also refers to the dialect spoken by the Dorians. | The temple's doricism conveyed a sense of strength and austerity. 这座寺庙的多利安式风格传达出一种力量感和朴素感。 |  simplicity, austerity, classicism | ornateness, elaborateness | noun
