long-row-to-hoe:/lɒŋ ˈrəʊ tə həʊ/ (UK) /lɔːŋ ˈroʊ tə hoʊ/ (US)| idiom |漫长的路要走；艰巨的任务|A long and arduous task or undertaking that requires sustained effort and perseverance.  It often implies a difficult or challenging journey or process.|例句：Getting this project finished is going to be a long row to hoe. 完成这个项目将是一项艰巨的任务。

例句：Building a successful business is a long row to hoe, but it's worth the effort.  创建一个成功的企业是一条漫长的路，但值得付出努力。|近义词：arduous task, uphill battle, difficult journey, challenging process |反义词:easy task, simple job, quick win| idiom
