disordinately:/dɪsˈɔːrdɪnətli/ | adv. | 过度地，无秩序地，杂乱无章地 | Excessively or without order; to an excessive degree. Characterized by disorder or irregularity. | He spends a disproportionately large amount of his income on entertainment. 他把收入中不成比例的大部分花在了娱乐上。 | The books were disordinately piled on the desk. 书杂乱无章地堆在桌子上。 | 近义词: excessively, immoderately, unduly, inordinately | 反义词: moderately, reasonably, orderly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation or reversal) + ordinate (arranged in order) + -ly (forming adverbs). Ultimately from Latin ordinatus, past participle of ordinare "to set in order," from ordo "order, rank."
