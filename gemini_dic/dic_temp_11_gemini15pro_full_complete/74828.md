frenetically:/frəˈnɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 狂热地，疯狂地 | In a frenetic and frantic manner; wildly excited or active. |  She worked frenetically to finish the project before the deadline. 她疯狂地工作，以便在截止日期前完成项目。| Synonyms: frantically, maniacally, wildly, furiously | Antonyms: calmly, peacefully, quietly | word type: adverb

frenetically:/frəˈnɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 狂热地，疯狂地 | Characterized by frenzied activity; fast and energetic in a rather wild and uncontrolled way. | He paced frenetically around the room, waiting for the news. 他在房间里焦躁地踱来踱去，等待着消息。 | Synonyms: hysterically, agitatedly, hectically | Antonyms: leisurely, slowly, deliberately | word type: adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *frénétique* (frenzy), from Late Latin *phreneticus*, from Greek *phrenitikos*, from *phrenitis* (inflammation of the brain), from *phrēn* (mind).
