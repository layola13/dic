commemoration:/kəˌmeməˈreɪʃn/
n.
纪念；庆祝
An act of remembering or marking a significant event.
The ceremony was a commemoration of the 50th anniversary of the war.  这个仪式是为了纪念战争50周年。
remembrance, celebration, observance
forgetting, disregard
noun

commemorative:/kəˈmemərətɪv/
adj.
纪念的；庆祝的
Serving to commemorate.
A commemorative stamp was issued to mark the occasion.  为纪念这一事件发行了一枚纪念邮票。
remembrance, celebratory
forgettable
adjective

commemorate:/kəˈmeməreɪt/
vt.
纪念；庆祝
To recall and show respect for (someone or something).
The statue commemorates the soldiers who died in the war.  这座雕像是为了纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵。
celebrate, memorialize, honor
ignore, forget
verb
