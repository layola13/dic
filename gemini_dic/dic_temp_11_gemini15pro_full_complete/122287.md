mitosis:/maɪˈtoʊsɪs/ /mɪˈtəʊsɪs/
n.
有丝分裂
A type of cell division that results in two daughter cells each having the same number and kind of chromosomes as the parent nucleus, typical of ordinary tissue growth.
例句：Mitosis is essential for growth and repair in multicellular organisms.  有丝分裂对于多细胞生物的生长和修复至关重要。

例句：The process of mitosis ensures that each new cell receives a complete set of chromosomes. 有丝分裂的过程确保每个新细胞都获得一套完整的染色体。

近义词：cell division, karyokinesis
反义词：meiosis
词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μίτος (mítos, “thread”) +‎ -osis.
