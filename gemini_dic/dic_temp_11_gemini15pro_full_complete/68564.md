fangleness:/ˈfæŋɡəlnəs/
noun
新奇；新奇的事物
The state or quality of being newfangled; novelty.
The fangledness of the new gadget wore off quickly. 这个新奇小玩意儿的新鲜劲儿很快就过去了。
近义词: novelty, newness, innovation
反义词: oldness, staleness, antiquity
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From newfangled + -ness.  "Newfangled" dates back to the late 16th century. "New" is from Old English "neowe," and "fangled" likely comes from "fangle," meaning "a silly invention," of uncertain origin.
