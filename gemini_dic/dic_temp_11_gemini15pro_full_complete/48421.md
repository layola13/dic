deducibility:/ˌdɪˌduːsəˈbɪləti/
n.
可推导性，可演绎性
The quality or state of being deducible; the capability of being deduced or inferred.
The deducibility of the theorem from the axioms is clear.  该定理从公理的可推导性是显而易见的。
deducibility, derivability, inferability
n/a
noun
