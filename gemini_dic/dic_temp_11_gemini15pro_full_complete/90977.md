hurrying:/ˈhɜːriɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhʌriɪŋ/ (UK)| v. adj.|匆忙，赶忙|present participle of hurry (verb): moving or acting with haste; rushing.|She was hurrying to catch the train. 她正匆忙赶火车。|近义词：rushing, hastening, speeding|反义词：dawdling, lingering|verb, adjective
