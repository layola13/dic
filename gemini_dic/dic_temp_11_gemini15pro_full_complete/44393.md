cross-fertilizes:/krɒsˈfɜːtɪlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使异花授粉；相互影响，相互促进 | To fertilize a plant with the pollen from another plant, typically of a different variety. Also, to cause (ideas, influences, etc.) to be exchanged or combined so as to produce something new and better. |  Modern dance has cross-fertilized with other dance forms. 现代舞已经与其他舞蹈形式相互影响。 | synonyms: hybridize, interbreed, crossbreed, mingle, fuse, blend, merge, combine, integrate, amalgamate | antonyms: isolate, separate, segregate | verb
