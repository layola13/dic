encoding:/ɪnˈkoʊdɪŋ/ | n. | 编码，编码方式 | The process of converting information from one form to another.  This often involves representing characters or symbols using a specific set of rules. |  Example 1: The encoding of the file is UTF-8.  该文件的编码是UTF-8。

Example 2:  Different countries use different encodings for their characters. 不同的国家使用不同的字符编码。 | synonym: code, cipher, representation | antonym: decoding, deciphering | noun

encodings:/ɪnˈkoʊdɪŋz/ | n. | 编码（复数） | Plural form of "encoding," referring to multiple ways of converting information. | Example 1: The website supports multiple encodings.  该网站支持多种编码。

Example 2:  We need to check the encodings of these files before processing them. 我们需要在处理这些文件之前检查它们的编码。 | synonym: codes, ciphers, representations | antonym: decodings, decipherings | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "encode" + "-ing."  "Encode" comes from French "encoder," from "en-" (in) + "coder" (to code), ultimately from Latin "caudex" (tree trunk, book). The "-ing" suffix turns the verb into a noun, representing the act or process of encoding.
