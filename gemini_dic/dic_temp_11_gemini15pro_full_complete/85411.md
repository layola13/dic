harvestability:/ˌhɑːrvɪstəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可收获性；可采性 | The ease and efficiency with which a crop can be harvested. |  The harvestability of the wheat crop was reduced by the heavy rains. 小麦的可收获性因暴雨而降低。 |  reaping potential, yield potential |  unharvestability (not a standard word, but conveys the opposite meaning) | noun
