knickknackery:/ˈnɪknækəri/
n.
小摆设，小玩意儿
A collection of small, inexpensive ornaments or trinkets.
Her shelves were filled with knickknackery she'd collected over the years. 她架子上摆满了她多年来收集的小玩意儿。
Synonyms: trinkets, ornaments, baubles, gewgaws
Antonyms: valuables, necessities
