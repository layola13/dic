introvert: /ˈɪntrəvɜːt/ (US) /ˈɪntrəvɜːt/ (UK)| n. adj.|内向的人；内向的|A person who tends to be preoccupied with his or her own thoughts and feelings rather than with external things.  A shy, reticent person.|She's a bit of an introvert and prefers to spend time alone.|近义词: shy, reserved, withdrawn, reticent|反义词: extrovert, outgoing, gregarious|词性: noun, adjective
Etymology:  From Latin intro- "inwardly" + vertere "to turn".
