arapaima: /ˌærəˈpaɪmə/ | n. | 巨骨舌鱼 | A very large South American freshwater fish with bony scales. It is one of the largest freshwater fish in the world. |  The arapaima is a prized game fish. 巨骨舌鱼是一种珍贵的游钓鱼。 | synonyms: pirarucu | antonyms: None | noun

Etymology: From the Tupi language, from *arapaima.
