doctrinairisms:/ˌdɒktrɪˈnɛərɪzəmz/
|n.|教条主义；空洞的理论|A rigid adherence to doctrines or theories, especially without regard to practical considerations.|His speech was full of doctrinaireisms, lacking any real connection to the problems at hand. 他的演讲充满了教条主义，与实际问题脱节。|近义词: dogmas, rigidities, orthodoxies|反义词: pragmatism, flexibility, open-mindedness|词性:Noun (plural)
Etymology: From French *doctrinaire*, from *doctrine* (doctrine) + *-aire* (forming agent nouns). Ultimately from Latin *doctrina* (teaching).
