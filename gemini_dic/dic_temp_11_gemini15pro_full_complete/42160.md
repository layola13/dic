couchettes:/kuːˈʃets/ | n. |卧铺（通常指火车上的）|A sleeping compartment on a train or ship, usually with bunks. |  I booked a couchette on the overnight train to Rome. 我在开往罗马的夜车上订了一个卧铺。

|  The couchettes were surprisingly comfortable. 卧铺出乎意料地舒适。|近义词：berth, bunk |反义词:seat |Noun
