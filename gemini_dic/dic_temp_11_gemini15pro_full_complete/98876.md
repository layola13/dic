intendeds:/ɪnˈtɛndɪdz/ | n. | 意图，目的；打算（intend 的复数形式）| The intendeds of the new policy are to reduce pollution and improve public health. 新政策的目的是减少污染和改善公众健康. | 近义词: aims, goals, objectives, purposes | 反义词: unintended consequences| noun


Etymology: From Middle English *entenden*, from Old French *entendre* "to intend, understand," from Latin *intendere* "to stretch out, turn one's attention to," from *in-* "toward" + *tendere* "to stretch."  The '-s' ending marks it as a plural noun.
