overfaintly: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪntli/ | adv. | 过于微弱地，极其微弱地 |  In a manner that is too faint; excessively faintly. 词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "faintly" (adverb form of "faint").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "faintly."
