dispels:/dɪˈspɛlz/
verb
驱散，消除
to make something go away, especially a feeling, idea, or belief
例句：The rising sun dispelled the mist.  初升的太阳驱散了薄雾。

例句：Her explanation dispelled all our doubts. 她的解释消除了我们所有的疑虑。
近义词：banish, eliminate, disperse, dissipate, scatter
反义词：gather, collect, accumulate
词性：v.

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *dispellen, from Anglo-Norman dispeller, from Latin dispellere (“to drive away, scatter, disperse”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + pellere (“to drive, push”).
