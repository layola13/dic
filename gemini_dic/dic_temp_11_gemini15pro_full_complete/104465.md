kaiserdom:/ˈkaɪzərdɔːm/ (UK), /ˈkaɪzərdɑːm/ (US)| n.|大教堂；皇帝的大教堂|A large, important cathedral, especially one in Germany or Austria. It literally translates to "emperor's dome" or "imperial cathedral".|例句：The Kölner Dom is a magnificent kaiserdom.  科隆大教堂是一座宏伟的皇帝大教堂。

例句：Aachen Cathedral, or the Aachener Kaiserdom, is Charlemagne's burial place.  亚琛大教堂，或亚琛皇帝大教堂，是查理曼大帝的埋葬地。|近义词：cathedral, minster|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
