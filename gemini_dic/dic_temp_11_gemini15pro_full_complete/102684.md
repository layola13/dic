jamin:/ˈdʒæmɪn/| n.| 保证金，担保，抵押品 |A sum of money deposited or pledged to secure the release of a person from custody or to ensure their appearance in court; something pledged as security for the fulfillment of an undertaking.| He posted jamin to get out of jail. 他缴纳了保证金才得以出狱。 |Synonyms: bail, security, bond, guarantee, pledge, collateral| Antonyms: debt, liability| noun
