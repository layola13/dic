doubtlessly:/ˈdaʊtləsli/| adv.|无疑地，肯定地|Without any doubt; certainly.|He will doubtlessly succeed. 他肯定会成功。|近义词：certainly, undoubtedly, definitely, assuredly|反义词：doubtfully, questionably|词性:副词
Etymology: From doubt + -less + -ly.  "Doubt" comes from Old French "douter" (to doubt), ultimately from Latin "dubitare".
