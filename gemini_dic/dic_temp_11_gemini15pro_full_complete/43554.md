crazedly:/ˈkreɪzɪdli/| adv.|疯狂地，狂暴地，狂热地|In a crazed manner; insanely.|He laughed crazedly, his eyes wide with fear. 他疯狂地大笑，眼睛里充满了恐惧。|She crazedly searched for her lost keys. 她疯狂地寻找她丢失的钥匙。|近义词：insanely, madly, frantically, wildly|反义词：calmly, sanely, rationally|词性: 副词

词源: From craze + -ed + -ly. Craze from Old Norse *krasa "to shatter".
