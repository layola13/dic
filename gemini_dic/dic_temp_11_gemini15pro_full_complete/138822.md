nuncupate:/nʌŋˈkʌpeɪt/| v. |指定；口头宣告；口述遗嘱|To declare or name orally; to make a will orally.|He nuncupated his last wishes to his family gathered around his bedside. 他向聚集在他床边的家人口述了他的遗愿。|近义词：declare, pronounce, nominate, designate|反义词: imply, insinuate|词性: verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nuncupatus*, past participle of *nuncupare* "to name, call, declare solemnly," from *nomen* "name" + *capere* "to take."
