diatribe: /ˈdaɪəˌtraɪb/ | n. | 抨击；檄文；长篇的攻击性演说 | A forceful and bitter verbal attack against someone or something. | The politician launched a diatribe against his opponent.  这位政治家对他的对手发起了猛烈的抨击。

The article was a long diatribe against the evils of modern society. 这篇文章是对现代社会弊病的长篇抨击。| synonyms: tirade, harangue, rant, denunciation, fulmination | antonyms: praise, encomium, eulogy, panegyric | noun
