heterogeneousness:/ˌhetərəʊˈdʒiːniəsnəs/ /ˌhetərəˈdʒiːniəsnəs/ | n. |异质性，多样性|The state or quality of being heterogeneous; consisting of dissimilar or diverse ingredients or constituents.|The heterogeneousness of the student population enriches the learning environment. 学生群体的多样性丰富了学习环境。|Synonyms: diversity, variety, heterogeneity, dissimilarity, multifariousness|Antonyms: homogeneity, uniformity, similarity, sameness|noun

heterogeneous:/ˌhetərəˈdʒiːniəs/ /ˌhetərəˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. |异质的，由不同种类组成的|Diverse in character or content.|The city is a heterogeneous mix of cultures and nationalities. 这座城市是文化和民族的异质混合体。|Synonyms: diverse, varied, mixed, assorted, miscellaneous|Antonyms: homogeneous, uniform, similar, alike|adjective
