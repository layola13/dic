landlessness:/ˈlændləsnəs/
n.
无地; 土地缺乏; 无土地状态
the state of having no land or not owning any land.
Landlessness is a major problem in many developing countries.  许多发展中国家都存在严重的土地缺乏问题。
The landlessness of the peasants led to widespread unrest. 农民没有土地导致了广泛的社会动荡。
近义词:lack of land, land poverty, propertylessness
反义词:land ownership, landholding
