hfc:/ˈeɪtʃˌɛfˈsiː/ | n. | 氢氟碳化合物 (Hydrofluorocarbon) | A hydrofluorocarbon (HFC) is a man-made organic compound that contains fluorine and hydrogen atoms, and are the most common type of organofluorine compounds. | HFCs are potent greenhouse gases commonly used in refrigeration and air conditioning. 氢氟碳化合物是强效温室气体，常用于制冷和空调。 |  HFCs are being phased out due to their high global warming potential. 由于全球变暖潜能值高，氢氟碳化合物正在被逐步淘汰。| synonyms: refrigerant, coolant | antonyms: natural refrigerant | noun
