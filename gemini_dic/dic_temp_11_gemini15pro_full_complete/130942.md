nonassertive:/ˌnɒnəˈsɜːtɪv/ /ˌnɑːnəˈsɜːrtɪv/| adj.|不自信的；不果断的；顺从的|Not assertive; disposed to avoid conflict.  Characterized by a lack of confidence and a reluctance to express one's own opinions or needs.|She was too nonassertive to complain about the bad service at the restaurant. 她太不自信了，不敢抱怨餐厅糟糕的服务。|Synonyms: timid, submissive, passive, unassertive, retiring, diffident, meek, self-effacing|Antonyms: assertive, aggressive, confident, bold, forceful|adjective
