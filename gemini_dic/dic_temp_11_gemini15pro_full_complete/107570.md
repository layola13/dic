landlubber:/ˈlændˌlʌbər/ | n. | 旱鸭子；不习惯航海的人 | A person unfamiliar with the sea and ships; someone who is awkward or clumsy on a ship. |  The old sailor laughed at the landlubber's attempts to tie a knot. 老水手嘲笑旱鸭子试图打结的样子。

landlubber:/ˈlændˌlʌbər/ | n. | 内地人;不靠海居住的人 | A person who lives inland and is unfamiliar with coastal life. | He was a landlubber who had never seen the ocean before. 他是一个内地人，以前从未见过大海。


|近义词：lubber, landsman |反义词: sailor, seaman | 词性: noun
