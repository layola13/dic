ductile:/ˈdʌktaɪl/ | adj. |  延展的；柔软的；易受影响的 | capable of being drawn out into wire or threads; easily molded or shaped; easily influenced or persuaded |  Copper is a ductile metal, easily shaped into wires. 铜是一种延展性金属，很容易塑造成电线。 | Synonyms: pliable, flexible, malleable, yielding | Antonyms: rigid, inflexible, stubborn | Word type: Adjective
