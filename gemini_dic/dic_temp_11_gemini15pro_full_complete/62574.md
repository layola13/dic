enervate: /ˈɛnərveɪt/ (US) /ˈɛnəveɪt/ (UK)| vt. |使衰弱；使无力|to reduce the mental or physical vigor of; weaken|The long illness enervated him.  这长期的疾病使他身体虚弱。|近义词：weaken, debilitate, sap, exhaust|反义词：invigorate, strengthen, energize|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ēnervātus*, past participle of ēnervāre (“to remove the sinews of, weaken”), from ē- (variant of ex-, “out of, from”) + nervus (“sinew, tendon, nerve”).
