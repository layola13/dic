anodon:/ˈænədɒn/ (US) /əˈnəʊdɒn/ (UK)| n.|无齿蚌，河蚌|A freshwater mussel, especially of the genus Anodonta, characterized by the absence of hinge teeth.|The anodon burrows into the mud at the bottom of the river. 无齿蚌钻入河底的泥土里。|Synonyms: mussel, clam|Antonyms: None|Noun
