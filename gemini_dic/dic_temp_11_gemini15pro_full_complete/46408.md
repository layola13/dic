daffy:/ˈdæfi/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻的，古怪的|Silly or foolish, especially in a lighthearted way; eccentric.|He had a daffy idea about selling his car and buying a unicycle.|近义词：silly, foolish, goofy, wacky, zany|反义词：sensible, serious, rational|词性:形容词
词源:  Likely a variant of "daft," from Middle English "dafte," meaning "mildly insane or foolish."  Ultimately of uncertain origin.
