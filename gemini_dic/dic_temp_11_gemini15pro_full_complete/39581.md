conscription:/kənˈskrɪpʃən/| n. |征兵,强制征募|The act of forcing people to join the military service. |The government introduced conscription during the war. 政府在战争期间实行了征兵制度。|  Conscription was necessary to bolster the army's dwindling ranks.  为了加强军队日益减少的队伍，征兵是必要的。 | synonyms: draft, enlistment, levy | antonyms: volunteering, voluntary enlistment | noun
