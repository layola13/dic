collaborator:/kəˈlæbəreɪtər/| n.|合作者，协作者|A person who works jointly on an activity or project.|例句：The scientists were collaborators on the research project.  这些科学家是该研究项目的合作者。

例句：She is a frequent collaborator with other musicians. 她经常与其他音乐家合作。|近义词：partner, associate, colleague, confederate|反义词：opponent, adversary, competitor|词性:noun
