embezzlement:/ɪmˈbezlmənt/| n.| 贪污，挪用公款 |The act of dishonestly appropriating or secreting assets, usually financial, by one or more individuals to whom such assets have been entrusted.| He was charged with embezzlement of company funds. 他被指控挪用公司资金。|Synonyms: misappropriation, fraud, theft, defalcation|Antonyms: honesty, integrity|noun
