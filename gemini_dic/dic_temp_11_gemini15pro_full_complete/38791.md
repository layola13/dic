conduction:/kənˈdʌkʃən/
n.|传导，输送|The conduction of heat through metal is very efficient. 金属的导热性非常高。|Examples: Heat conduction is an important factor in building design.  热传导是建筑设计中的一个重要因素。 The conduction of electricity through copper wires is essential for powering our homes. 通过铜线输送电力对于我们家庭用电至关重要。|Synonyms: transmission, conveyance, transfer|Antonyms: insulation, resistance|noun
