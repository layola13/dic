lamping:/ˈlæmpɪŋ/
noun
n.
照射；用灯照射（尤指狩猎）; (非正式) 猛烈攻击；痛打
The act of hunting at night using a powerful lamp.  Also, attacking someone violently.
They went lamping for rabbits. 他们晚上用强光灯去猎兔子。
He gave the burglar a good lamping. 他狠狠地揍了那个窃贼一顿。
近义词：hunting, shining, bashing, thrashing
反义词：None (for hunting sense); protecting, defending (for attacking sense)
词性: 名词


lamping:/ˈlæmpɪŋ/
verb
v.
用灯照射（尤指狩猎）; (非正式) 猛烈攻击；痛打
To hunt at night using a powerful lamp. Also, to attack someone violently.
They were lamping deer in the fields. 他们在田野里用灯照射鹿。
He lamped the guy who insulted his girlfriend.  他痛打了那个侮辱他女朋友的家伙。
近义词：hunting, shining, bashing, thrashing
反义词：None (for hunting sense); protecting, defending (for attacking sense)
词性: 动词


词源:  Derived from the noun "lamp" + the suffix "-ing," indicating the action or process of using a lamp.
