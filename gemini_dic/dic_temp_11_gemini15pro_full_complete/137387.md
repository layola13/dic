nontenability:/ˌnɒntɛnəˈbɪləti/ |n.| 不可维持性，站不住脚|The nontenability of his position became increasingly clear. 他的立场越来越站不住脚。|Synonyms: untenability, unmaintainability, indefensibility|Antonyms: tenability, defensibility, maintainability|noun

nontenable:/ˌnɒnˈtɛnəbl/ |adj.| （论点、立场等）站不住脚的，难以维持的|His argument was clearly nontenable. 他的论点显然站不住脚。|Synonyms: untenable, indefensible, unsustainable|Antonyms: tenable, defensible, sustainable|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + tenable (capable of being held or defended)  + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  "Tenable" comes from Old French "tenable," from Latin "tenere" (to hold).
