brass:/bræs/| n. adj.|黄铜，铜管乐器，厚脸皮|A yellow alloy of copper and zinc. Also, wind instruments made of brass.  Informally, it can refer to boldness or impudence.|The trumpets and trombones make up the brass section of the orchestra.  (铜管乐器) He had the brass to ask for a raise after only a week on the job. (厚脸皮) |近义词:nerve, audacity, bronze (for the metal)|反义词:shyness, timidity|词性:noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bræs, from Proto-Germanic *brasa-, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old High German brasa "glow, embers."
