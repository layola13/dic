bravoed:/brɑːˈvoʊd/
verb
喝彩；称赞
Past tense of "bravo," meaning to shout "bravo" as an expression of approval or appreciation.
The audience bravoed the performers after their stunning performance. 观众在演员们精彩的表演后向他们喝彩。
The crowd bravoed the soloist's impressive high notes.  人群为独唱演员令人印象深刻的高音喝彩。
acclaim, applaud, cheer
criticize, boo
