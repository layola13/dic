nondegenerate:/ˌnɒndɪˈdʒɛnərət/ | adj. | 非退化的，非简并的 | Not degenerate; having a full set of distinct characteristics or properties.  Specifically in mathematics and physics, referring to a case where a certain quantity or system does not reduce to a simpler or less varied form. |  The matrix is nondegenerate, so it has an inverse.  这个矩阵是非退化的，所以它有逆矩阵。 | The system exhibits nondegenerate energy levels. 该系统展现出非简并的能级。 |  nonsingular, distinct | degenerate | adjective
