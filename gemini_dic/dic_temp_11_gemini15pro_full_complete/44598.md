crowbar:/ˈkroʊˌbɑːr/ (US) /ˈkrəʊˌbɑː(r)/ (UK)| n.|撬棍; 铁棍|A metal bar with a curved end, used as a lever for forcing things open or apart.|He used a crowbar to pry open the crate. 他用撬棍撬开了板条箱。|The firefighters used a crowbar to force open the door of the burning building. 消防队员用撬棍强行打开了着火建筑的门。|Synonyms: pry bar, jimmy, lever|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

crowbars:/ˈkroʊˌbɑːrz/ (US) /ˈkrəʊˌbɑːz/ (UK)| n.|撬棍（复数）|Plural form of crowbar, referring to more than one crowbar.|He keeps several crowbars in his truck for emergencies. 他的卡车里放着好几根撬棍以备不时之需。|They used two crowbars to lift the heavy rock. 他们用了两根撬棍来抬起那块沉重的石头。|Synonyms: pry bars, jimmies, levers|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
