backcountry:/ˈbækˌkʌntri/ | n. |偏远地区，穷乡僻壤|Remote, undeveloped rural areas, often mountainous or forested, far from cities and towns.|例句：They enjoy hiking in the backcountry. 他们喜欢在偏远地区徒步旅行。

backcountry:/ˈbækˌkʌntri/ | adj. |偏远的，穷乡僻壤的|Relating to or characteristic of remote, undeveloped rural areas.|例句：We stayed in a backcountry cabin for a week. 我们在一个偏远的乡村小屋里待了一个星期。


Etymology:  From "back" + "country". "Back" in this sense means remote or situated away from the main settlements.


Synonyms: wilderness, outback, bush, hinterland

Antonyms: urban, city, metropolitan, developed, populated
