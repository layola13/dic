misgiving:/ˌmɪsˈɡɪvɪŋ/
n.
疑虑，担忧，不安
a feeling of doubt or apprehension about the outcome or consequences of something.
I had serious misgivings about his ability to do the job. 我对他的工作能力有严重的疑虑。

We had misgivings about the trip. 我们对这次旅行感到担忧。


近义词:doubt, apprehension, worry, concern, qualm, reservation
反义词:confidence, assurance, certainty
词性:名词
