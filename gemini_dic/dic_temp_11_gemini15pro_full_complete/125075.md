A system of government or social organization dominated by religious leaders, specifically Muslim clerics. | The rise of mullahism in the region has led to stricter social controls.  该地区毛拉主义的兴起导致了更严格的社会控制。|  Synonyms: clericalism, theocracy | Antonyms: secularism, laity | noun
