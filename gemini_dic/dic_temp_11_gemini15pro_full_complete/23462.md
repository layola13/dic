braggartism:/ˈbræɡərˌtɪzəm/ | n. | 吹牛；自夸；自吹自擂 | The excessive act of boasting or bragging.  A tendency to boast about achievements or possessions. | He was known for his braggartism, constantly talking about his supposed accomplishments. 他以自吹自擂而闻名，不断谈论他所谓的成就。| He displayed unbearable braggartism after winning the lottery. 他中了彩票后，表现出令人难以忍受的自夸。 | synonyms: boasting, bragging, vaunting, swaggering, bombast, pretension | antonyms: modesty, humility, humbleness | noun
