|n.| 本地性，原生性（复数） |The nativenesses of the plants were carefully documented. 这些植物的原生性被仔细地记录了下来。|
|英文解析: Plural form of "nativeness," referring to the quality or state of being native or indigenous to a particular place.  It often refers to multiple instances or aspects of this quality.|
|例句：The study compared the nativenesses of different bird species in the region. 这项研究比较了该地区不同鸟类的原生性。|
|例句：The nativenesses of the various ingredients contributed to the unique flavor of the dish. 各种成分的原生性造就了这道菜独特的风味。|
|近义词：indigeneity, autochthony |
|反义词：foreignness, alienness |
|词性：noun|


词源:
From "native" + "-ness" + "-es"
"native" comes from Old French "natif" and Latin "nativus," meaning "born."
"-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
"-es" is a plural suffix.
