exsiccate:/ˈɛksɪkeɪt/ |v. |使干燥，脱水，使枯竭|To remove the moisture from something; to dry up. |The botanist exsiccated the plant specimens for preservation.  植物学家将植物标本干燥以保存。|The hot desert sun exsiccated the land. 炎热的沙漠阳光使土地干涸。|近义词:dehydrate, desiccate, dry|反义词:hydrate, moisten|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin exsiccātus, past participle of exsiccāre (“to dry up”), from ex (“out”) + siccāre (“to dry”).
