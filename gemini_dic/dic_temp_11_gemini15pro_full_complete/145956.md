overdistantly:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪstəntli/ | adv. | 过于疏远地，冷漠地 | in a manner that is excessively distant or aloof |  He spoke to her overdistantly, making her feel unwelcome. 他和她说话过于疏远，让她感到不受欢迎。| Example:  She treated him overdistantly after the argument. 争吵之后，她对他非常冷淡。| Synonym: coldly, aloofly, remotely, reservedly | Antonym: warmly, intimately, closely | 词性: 副词
