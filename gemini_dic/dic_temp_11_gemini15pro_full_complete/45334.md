cumshaw:/ˈkʌmʃɔː/| n.|小费，赏钱，赠品，红包|A tip, gratuity, or present, especially one given to a servant or other employee. Originally Pidgin English, from the Amoy dialect of Chinese.|例句：He gave the waiter a generous cumshaw. 他给了服务员丰厚的小费。

例句：She received a cumshaw for her excellent service. 她因出色的服务收到了赏钱。

近义词：tip, gratuity, gift, bonus, perk|反义词:penalty, fine|词性:名词
