downloads:/ˈdaʊnloʊdz/ (US) /ˈdaʊnləʊdz/ (UK)| n.|下载; 下载内容; 下载次数|The website has millions of downloads every day.  该网站每天有数百万次的下载量。|  The downloads are available in various formats. 这些下载内容有各种格式。|近义词：transfers, uploads|反义词: uploads|词性:名词

Etymology:  "download" comes from "down" + "load".  "Down" indicates the direction of transfer (from a larger system to a smaller one), and "load" refers to transferring data or programs. "Downloads" is simply the plural form.
