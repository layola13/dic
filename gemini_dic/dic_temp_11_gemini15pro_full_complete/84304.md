half-white:/ˌhɑːf ˈwaɪt/
adjective
半白人血统的；混血白人

例句：He is half-white and half-Asian. 他是欧亚混血儿。

例句：The half-white community faces unique challenges. 混血白人社群面临着独特的挑战。

近义词：mixed-race, biracial
反义词：full-blooded, pure-blooded
词性：adjective
