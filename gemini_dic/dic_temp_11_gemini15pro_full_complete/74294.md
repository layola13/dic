fractionalism:/ˈfrækʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
派系主义;分裂主义;部分主义
The state of being split into factions or fractions.  A tendency to form factions or break into factions.
The fractionalism of the party threatens its future. 该党的派系主义威胁着它的未来。
近义词: factionalism, division, schism, fragmentation, disunity
反义词: unity, solidarity, cohesion, integration
词性: noun

fractionalism:/ˈfrækʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
极小化;微型化
The act of making something smaller or less substantial.
The fractionalism of modern technology allows for smaller and more powerful devices. 现代技术的微型化使得更小更强大的设备成为可能。
近义词: miniaturization, reduction, downsizing
反义词: enlargement, expansion, augmentation
词性: noun
