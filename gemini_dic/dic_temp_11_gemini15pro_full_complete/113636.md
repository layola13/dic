machinate:/ˈmækɪneɪt/| v. |密谋，策划，(尤指)阴谋策划|to make secret plans, especially for something that is illegal or harmful|The rebels were machinating to overthrow the government. 叛乱分子正密谋推翻政府。|Synonyms: plot, scheme, conspire, intrigue|Antonyms:  cooperate, assist, support|verb

machination:/ˌmækɪˈneɪʃn/| n. |阴谋，诡计|(often machinations) a secret and complicated plan, especially one for getting power or control|The machinations of the villain were finally uncovered. 恶棍的阴谋最终被揭露了。|Synonyms: plot, scheme, conspiracy, intrigue|Antonyms: honesty, openness, transparency|noun
