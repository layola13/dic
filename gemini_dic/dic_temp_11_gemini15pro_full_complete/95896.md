incredibly:/ɪnˈkrɛdəbli/| adv.|非常地，难以置信地|Meaning "to an incredible degree," from incredible + -ly.  It intensifies the adjective it modifies, indicating a high degree of the quality described.|The scenery was incredibly beautiful.  风景美得令人难以置信。|近义词：extremely, remarkably, unbelievably, astonishingly|反义词:credibly, believably|词性:副词
