geordi:/ˈdʒɔːrdi/| n.|乔迪（对泰恩赛德地区人的称呼，尤指纽卡斯尔人）;  (尤指纽卡斯尔人戴的)扁平帽|A Geordie is a person from Tyneside, especially Newcastle, in northeast England.  Geordie can also refer to the dialect spoken there. |He's a Geordie, so he has a strong accent. 他是纽卡斯尔人，所以口音很重。|近义词：Newcastle resident, Northumbrian|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Southerner as a broad contrast)|词性:noun
