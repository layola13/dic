hero-worship: /ˈhɪəroʊ wɜːrʃɪp/ /ˈhɪərəʊ wɜːʃɪp/
noun
英雄崇拜；偶像崇拜
Excessive admiration for someone, often treating them as a perfect being.
She had a teenage hero-worship of the rock star. 她十几岁时曾对这位摇滚明星有过英雄般的崇拜。
He engaged in hero-worship of his older brother. 他对他哥哥进行英雄崇拜。
admiration, idolization, veneration
criticism, disapproval
n.
