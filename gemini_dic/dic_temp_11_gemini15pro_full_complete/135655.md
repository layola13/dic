nonpreferability:/ˌnɑːnˌprɛfərəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可取性，非优选性 | The nonpreferability of this option stems from its high cost and limited benefits.  This option's nonpreferability is due to its complexity and potential risks. | 例句：The nonpreferability of this route was due to heavy traffic.  这条路线的不可取性是由于交通拥堵。 The report highlighted the nonpreferability of using fossil fuels. 报告强调了使用化石燃料的不可取性。 | 近义词：undesirability, disadvantage, inferiority | 反义词：preferability, desirability, advantage | 词性: 名词

Etymology: non- + preferability.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "preferability" comes from "preferable" (meaning "more desirable or suitable"), which in turn derives from the Latin "praeferre" ("to carry in front," hence "to prefer").
