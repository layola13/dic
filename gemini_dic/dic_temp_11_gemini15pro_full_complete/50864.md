desensitize:/ˌdiːˈsɛnsɪtʌɪz/ /ˌdiːˈsɛnsəˌtaɪz/ |v.|使脱敏，使不再敏感|To make someone less sensitive to something, especially something unpleasant or shocking, by repeatedly exposing them to it.|The film desensitized viewers to violence. 这部电影使观众对暴力不再敏感。|近义词:numb, dull, inure|反义词:sensitize, sharpen|verb

desensitization:/ˌdiːˌsɛnsɪtʌɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌdiːˌsɛnsətɪˌzeɪʃən/|n.|脱敏，减敏|The process of making someone less sensitive to something.|Systematic desensitization is a common treatment for phobias. 系统脱敏是治疗恐惧症的常见方法。|近义词:immunization, habituation|反义词:sensitization|noun
