out-shaping:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪpɪŋ/
|v.|胜过；在…方面超过|Surpassing or exceeding in shaping or forming something; outperforming in design or creation.|The new architect is out-shaping his competitors with innovative and sustainable building designs.  这位新建筑师以创新和可持续的建筑设计胜过他的竞争对手。|例句：Her pottery skills are out-shaping everyone else in the class. 她的陶艺技巧在班上超过了所有人。|近义词：surpassing, excelling, outdoing, outperforming|反义词：underperforming, lagging behind|词性:动词

Etymology:
"out-" (prefix):  from Old English "ūt", meaning "out, outside, outward, from within to the outside"
"-shaping" (verb): from "shape" (v.), from Old English "scapan, scieppan" meaning "to create, form, destine."
