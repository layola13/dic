achromaticity:/ˌeɪkrəʊməˈtɪsɪti/ |n.| 消色差，无色性|The achromaticity of the lens is crucial for clear images.  镜头的消色差对于清晰的成像是至关重要的。|Example: The telescope boasts excellent achromaticity, allowing for sharp, color-free views of celestial objects. 这架望远镜拥有出色的消色差，可以清晰无色地观察天体。 |Synonym: colorlessness, neutrality|Antonym: chromaticity, colorfulness|Noun

achromatic:/ˌeɪkrəˈmætɪk/ |adj.| 消色的，无色的|An achromatic lens corrects for chromatic aberration. 消色差透镜可以校正色差。|Example:  The artist preferred achromatic paintings, focusing on shades of grey and black.  这位艺术家偏爱无色的绘画，专注于灰色和黑色的阴影。|Synonym: colorless, neutral|Antonym: chromatic, colorful|Adjective
