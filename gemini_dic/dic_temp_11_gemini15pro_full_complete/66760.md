exosmosis:/ˌɛksɒzˈməʊsɪs/ /ˌɛɡzɑːzˈmoʊsɪs/
noun
外渗；外渗透
The outward passage of water from a cell or vessel into the surrounding medium due to higher solute concentration outside the cell or vessel.
The exosmosis of water from the plant cells caused them to shrink. 植物细胞的水分外渗导致它们收缩。
synonyms: outward osmosis, outward diffusion
antonyms: endosmosis
