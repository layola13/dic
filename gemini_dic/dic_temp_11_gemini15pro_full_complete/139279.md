obliterable:/əˈblɪtərəbl/
adjective
可消除的，可抹去的
able to be obliterated; erasable.
The pencil marks are obliterable. 铅笔痕迹是可以擦掉的。
近义词:erasable, deletable, removable
反义词:indelible, permanent, ineradicable
词性:adjective

obliterate:/əˈblɪtəreɪt/
verb
抹去，消除；摧毁
destroy utterly; wipe out.
The bombing raid obliterated the entire city.  轰炸袭击摧毁了整个城市。
近义词:destroy, erase, annihilate
反义词:create, build, preserve
词性:verb
