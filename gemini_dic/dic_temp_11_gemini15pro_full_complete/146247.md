overextracted:/ˌōvərɪkˈstræktɪd/ | adj. | 过萃的，过度提取的 |  (Of coffee or tea) brewed for too long, resulting in a bitter flavor.  | The coffee was overextracted and tasted burnt. 咖啡过萃了，尝起来有股焦味。 | overbrewed, oversteeped | underextracted, weak | adjective

overextracted:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstræktɪd/ | adj. | 过萃的，过度提取的 | (Of flavors or other substances) removed excessively from a source material. | The vanilla extract was overextracted, making the dessert too strong. 香草精提取过度，使甜点味道太浓了。| concentrated, strong | mild, weak | adjective
