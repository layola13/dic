nonrespondents:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈspɑːn.dənts/ |n.|未答复者，无应答者|People who do not answer a survey, questionnaire, or request for information.|例句：The researchers attempted to contact the nonrespondents to understand the reasons for their lack of participation. 研究人员试图联系未答复者，以了解他们不参与的原因。

例句：The high percentage of nonrespondents made it difficult to draw firm conclusions from the survey. 高比例的未答复者使得很难从调查中得出确切的结论。|近义词：non-participants, defaulters|反义词:respondents, participants|词性:名词
