enhance:/ɪnˈhæns/ /ɛnˈhæns/| vt.|提高，增强，增进|To increase or improve the quality, value, or attractiveness of something.|例句：The new software enhances the quality of the images. 新软件提高了图像的质量。|例句：Regular exercise can enhance your physical and mental well-being.  规律的运动可以增强你的身心健康。|近义词：improve, boost, strengthen, augment, upgrade|反义词：diminish, decrease, reduce, weaken, impair|词性：动词
