brown: /braʊn/ | n. | 棕色；褐色 | a color produced by mixing red, yellow, and black | She wore a dress of deepest brown. 她穿着一件深棕色的连衣裙。 | 近义词: tan, auburn, chestnut | 反义词: white, black | 词性: noun

brown: /braʊn/ | v. | 变成棕色；变成褐色 | to become brown, typically by cooking or burning | The bread browned nicely in the oven. 面包在烤箱里烤成了漂亮的棕色。| 近义词: tan, roast | 反义词: bleach, whiten | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *brūn, from Proto-Germanic *brūnaz, of uncertain origin.
