kumar:/ˈkuːmɑːr/ (sometimes /ˈkʊmɑːr/) | n. | 男孩，儿子，王子，未婚男子 (尤其指印度教徒) | A title used in India, especially by Hindus, for a boy or unmarried man, meaning "son" or "prince". It's derived from Sanskrit. | Example: The young kumar was known for his bravery.  这位年轻的库马尔以他的勇敢而闻名。| Synonym: boy, son, lad, youth, prince (in Indian context) | Antonym:  girl, daughter (loosely), man, elder | Noun
