faithlessnesses:/ˈfeɪθləsnɪsɪz/
noun
不忠诚，背信弃义（复数）
The state of being faithless; lack of faith or loyalty; treachery.
Her repeated faithlessnesses eventually led to the end of their marriage. 她反复的不忠最终导致了他们婚姻的结束。
Synonyms: betrayals, infidelities, treacheries, disloyalties
Antonyms: loyalties, fidelities, faithfulness
