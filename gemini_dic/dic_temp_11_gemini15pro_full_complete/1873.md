acrosporous:/əˈkrɒspərəs/
adj.
顶生的，顶孢的
Having spores situated at the apex or tip.
The acrosporous moss grew on the top of the rock.  顶生的苔藓生长在岩石的顶部。
Acrosporous mosses form upright, unbranched shoots. 顶孢藓类形成直立的、不分枝的芽。
近义词: terminal, apical
反义词: basal
词性: adjective
