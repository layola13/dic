hanaper:/ˈhænəpər/| n.|钱袋，金库，财政部|A basket, usually of wickerwork, used especially in England for documents of state, especially those relating to the Chancery.  Historically, fees paid to the Chancery were kept in such a basket, hence the extended meaning of treasury or exchequer.|The hanaper held the Great Seal and other important documents.|Synonyms: treasury, exchequer, coffer|Antonyms: debt, deficit|noun
