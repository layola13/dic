jiggliest:/ˈdʒɪɡliɪst/| adj.|最摇晃的，最抖动的|Superlative form of "jiggly," meaning most wobbly or shaky.|例句：That's the jiggliest jelly I've ever seen! 那是我见过最摇晃的果冻！

例句：The bridge was so jiggliest that I felt scared to cross it. 这座桥摇晃得厉害，我不敢过。

近义词：wobbliest, shakiest, trembliest|反义词:firmest, steadiest|词性:形容词

jiggly:/ˈdʒɪɡli/| adj.|摇晃的，抖动的|Meaning wobbly or shaky, often used to describe something soft and yielding.|例句：The jelly was so jiggly that it wobbled when I touched it. 果冻太摇晃了，我一碰它就晃动。

例句：Her jiggly arms made her self-conscious. 她摇晃的手臂让她感到难为情。

近义词：wobbly, shaky, trembly|反义词:firm, steady|词性:形容词


词源: From "jiggle," a frequentative form of "jig," meaning to dance or move quickly up and down or back and forth.  The "-y" suffix turns it into an adjective, and "-est" creates the superlative form.
