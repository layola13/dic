Knoxvillites:/ˈnɒksvɪlaɪts/
noun
诺克斯维尔人，诺克斯维尔市民

Many Knoxvillites enjoy the city's vibrant music scene. 许多诺克斯维尔市民喜欢这个城市充满活力的音乐景象。
Knoxvillites are known for their Southern hospitality.  诺克斯维尔人以其南方的好客而闻名。
synonyms: residents of Knoxville, citizens of Knoxville
antonyms: non-residents of Knoxville
