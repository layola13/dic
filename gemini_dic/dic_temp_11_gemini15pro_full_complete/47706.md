debuts:/ˈdeɪbjuːz/ (US), /ˈdeɪbjuːz/ (UK)
n.
首次亮相；初次登台；首演
First public appearances; initial presentations or performances.
The actress made her theatrical debut in a small off-Broadway production.  这位女演员在外百老汇的一部小型作品中首次登台亮相。

The band's debut album went platinum.  这支乐队的首张专辑获得了白金销量。

近义词: premieres, beginnings, introductions
反义词: finales, endings, conclusions
词性: 名词 (Noun)
