overexciting:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ/| adj.|过度兴奋的，过于刺激的|Causing excessive excitement; too stimulating.|The overexciting movie kept the children awake all night. 过于刺激的电影让孩子们整夜都睡不着。|近义词：thrilling, stimulating, exhilarating|反义词：boring, dull, tedious|词性:形容词
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + exciting (present participle of excite, from Latin excitare "to rouse, stir up")
