coaxes:/ˈkoʊksɪz/ (US), /ˈkəʊksɪz/ (UK)| v.|哄劝；劝诱|To persuade someone gently and patiently to do something.|例句：She coaxed the cat down from the tree. 她哄着猫从树上下来。

例句：He coaxed a smile from the shy child. 他哄得那个害羞的孩子笑了。

近义词：persuade, cajole, wheedle, entice|反义词：dissuade, discourage|词性：动词
