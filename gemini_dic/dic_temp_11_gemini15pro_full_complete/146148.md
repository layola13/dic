overengineer:/ˌəʊvərˌɛndʒɪˈnɪə(r)/
verb|过度工程化，过度设计|To design or build something to be more complex or elaborate than necessary.|They overengineered the software, making it difficult to use. 他们过度设计了这个软件，导致它很难使用。|overdesign, overcomplicate|underengineer, simplify|v.
overengineer:/ˌoʊvərˌɛndʒɪˈnɪər/
noun|过度工程化的产品/系统|Something that has been overengineered.|The bridge was an overengineer, capable of withstanding far more weight than it would ever carry. 这座桥过度工程化了，能够承受比它实际承载的重量多得多的重量。|overdesign, overcomplication|simplification, minimalism|n.
