italianesque:/ɪˌtæliəˈnɛsk/
adjective
意大利风格的，类似意大利的
Suggesting Italian characteristics; reminiscent of Italy, especially in a romanticized or picturesque way.
The restaurant had an Italianesque atmosphere, with checkered tablecloths and opera music playing softly.  这家餐厅充满了意大利风情，格子桌布和柔和的歌剧音乐。
Her villa in Tuscany had an Italianesque charm. 她在托斯卡纳的别墅具有意大利式的魅力。
近义词:Italianate, Italian-style
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective
