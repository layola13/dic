outpoisons:/ˌaʊtˈpɔɪzənz/
verb
以毒胜过；毒害更甚
to poison more effectively or to a greater degree than someone or something else.
The Borgias outpoisoned their rivals in Renaissance Italy.  博尔吉亚家族在文艺复兴时期的意大利毒害了他们的竞争对手。
The new pesticide outpoisons the older formulas. 新型杀虫剂比旧配方毒性更大。
surpass, outdo, eclipse, overshadow
(No direct antonym, consider "be less poisonous than" or "succumb to poison")
