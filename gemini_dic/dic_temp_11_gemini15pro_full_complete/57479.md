draftsmen: /ˈdræftsmən/ (US), /ˈdrɑːftsmən/ (UK)| n. | 绘图员，制图员 | A person who draws plans and sketches, especially of buildings or machinery. | The draftsmen worked diligently on the blueprints for the new skyscraper. 绘图员们在新的摩天大楼的蓝图上辛勤工作。 | Synonyms: draughtsman (UK), drafter, designer, illustrator | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

draftsman: /ˈdræftsmən/ (US), /ˈdrɑːftsmən/ (UK)| n. | 起草者 | A person who writes a document, especially one that is official or legal.  | The draftsman of the constitution was a renowned legal scholar. 宪法的起草者是一位著名的法律学者。 | Synonyms: writer, author, composer | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From draft + -s- + -man.  "draft" originates from the Old Norse "draught" meaning "to draw, pull".
