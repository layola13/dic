mistrust:/mɪsˈtrʌst/
n.|不信任，怀疑|Lack of trust or confidence.|He looked at me with mistrust. 他带着不信任的目光看着我。|近义词：suspicion, doubt, distrust|反义词：trust, confidence, faith|名词
mistrust:/mɪsˈtrʌst/
vt.|不信任，怀疑|To regard with suspicion or doubt.|I mistrust his motives. 我怀疑他的动机。|近义词：doubt, suspect, distrust|反义词：trust, believe, confide|动词

词源:
From Middle English *mistrusten, from mis- + trusten (“to trust”).  Equivalent to mis- +‎ trust.
