nonfilamentous:/ˌnɒnˌfɪləˈmentəs/ | adj. | 非丝状的 | Not filamentous; lacking filaments.  A nonfilamentous bacterium is one that does not grow in the form of a thread. |  Certain bacteria are nonfilamentous, existing as single cells or clusters.  某些细菌是非丝状的，以单细胞或簇状存在。 | Non-filamentous fungi like yeasts reproduce by budding.  像酵母这样的非丝状真菌通过出芽繁殖。 | unicellular, single-celled | filamentous | adjective
