biosecurity:/ˌbaɪoʊsɪˈkjʊrɪti/ | n. |生物安全|  The set of precautions taken to prevent the spread of infectious diseases, especially in livestock or crops, and to protect the environment from harmful biological agents.|  例句：Biosecurity measures are crucial to prevent the spread of avian flu.  生物安全措施对于预防禽流感的传播至关重要。

例句：The farm has strict biosecurity protocols in place.  这个农场有严格的生物安全协议。| 近义词：biosafety, biodefense | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps vulnerability) | 词性: noun
