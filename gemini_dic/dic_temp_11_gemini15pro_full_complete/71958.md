flightily:/ˈflaɪtɪli/| adv.|轻浮地，轻率地，反复无常地|In a frivolous and capricious manner; irresponsibly.|She flitted flightily from one topic to another. 她轻浮地从一个话题跳到另一个话题。|例句：He dismissed the serious matter rather flightily. 他相当轻率地驳回了这个严肃的问题。

例句：She dressed rather flightily for a funeral. 她穿得相当轻浮，不适合参加葬礼。|近义词：frivolously, capriciously, erratically, giddily|反义词：seriously, responsibly, thoughtfully, sensibly|词性:副词
