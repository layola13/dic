methionine:/məˈθaɪəniːn/
n.
蛋氨酸; 甲硫氨酸
An essential amino acid that is important in many metabolic functions. It is found in most protein-containing food, especially eggs, fish, and meat.  It contains sulfur and is important for normal growth and metabolism.
Methionine is an essential amino acid. 蛋氨酸是一种必需氨基酸。
Methionine plays a crucial role in protein synthesis. 蛋氨酸在蛋白质合成中起着至关重要的作用。
