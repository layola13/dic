alkalinization:/ˌælkələnaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |碱化；碱化作用|The process of making something alkaline, or increasing its alkalinity.  This can refer to soil, water, or even the body. |The alkalinization of the soil is necessary for some plants to thrive. 土壤的碱化对于某些植物的生长是必要的。

The doctor recommended alkalinization therapy to balance her pH levels. 医生建议她进行碱化疗法以平衡她的pH值。|近义词:neutralization, basification |反义词:acidification| noun

alkalinize: /ˈælkəlaɪnaɪz/ | v. |使碱化|To make something alkaline, or increase its alkalinity. |Adding lime to the soil will alkalinize it.  在土壤中添加石灰会使其碱化。

They alkalinize the water to reduce its acidity.  他们使水碱化以降低其酸度。|近义词:neutralize, basify |反义词:acidify| verb


词源: From alkali + -ize + -ation.  "Alkali" ultimately comes from Arabic "al-qalīy," meaning "ashes of the saltwort."
