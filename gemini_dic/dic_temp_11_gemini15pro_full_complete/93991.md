impairing:/ɪmˈpɛərɪŋ/
adjective
损害的，削弱的
Weakening or damaging something, especially a faculty or function.
Excessive alcohol consumption is impairing to one's health. 过量饮酒有害健康。
Synonyms: damaging, harming, weakening, hindering, deteriorating, undermining
Antonyms: improving, enhancing, strengthening, benefiting
词性: 形容词
