humanly:/ˈhjuːmənli/| adv.|人性地，以人力所能及的方式|Within a humanly possible timeframe. 在人力所能及的时间范围内。|近义词：naturally, possibly|反义词:supernaturally, inhumanly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "human" + "-ly".  "Human" comes from the Latin word "humanus," meaning "of man, human, humane, kind, gentle, polite, cultivated, refined." The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives.
