arcubus:/ˈɑːrkəbʌs/ | n. | 反余弦函数 | The inverse cosine function, denoted as arccos(x) or cos⁻¹(x), which gives the angle whose cosine is x. | Example: The arcubus of 0.5 is 60 degrees.  反余弦函数0.5的结果是60度。

Example:  In trigonometry, the arcubus is an important function. 在三角学中，反余弦函数是一个重要的函数。| 近义词: inverse cosine, cos⁻¹(x) | 反义词: cosine (cos(x)) | 词性: noun
