bountyhood:/ˈbaʊntiˌhʊd/
n.
慷慨, 丰饶的状态; 赏金猎人职业
The state or quality of being bountiful; the profession of a bounty hunter.
The land enjoyed a period of bountyhood, with plentiful harvests and prosperous trade. 这片土地经历了一段丰饶的时期，收获丰足，贸易繁荣。
He embraced bountyhood, tracking down criminals for reward. 他接受了赏金猎人的职业，追捕罪犯以获取赏金。
近义词: abundance, plentifulness, bounty, generosity
反义词: scarcity, dearth, lack, poverty
词性: 名词
