overpromising:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːmɪsɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度承诺的；答应过头的 | Making promises that are unlikely to be fulfilled. | The company is notorious for overpromising and underdelivering.  这家公司因承诺过多而交付不足而臭名昭著。 |  He overpromised on the project deadline and now he's struggling to meet it. 他对项目截止日期做出了过高的承诺，现在他正在努力赶上。| 近义词：overcommitting, exaggerating | 反义词：underpromising, realistic | 词性：形容词
