imputers:/ɪmˈpjuːtərz/| n. |插补器，估算器|A person or thing that imputes (attributes something, especially a fault or crime).  Specifically in statistics, it refers to a process or algorithm used to fill in missing values in a dataset.|The imputers filled the missing data using the mean of the observed values. 插补器使用观察值的平均值填充缺失的数据。|Synonyms: estimators, fillers, predictors|Antonyms: N/A (The concept of an antonym doesn't apply directly to this technical term.)|Noun
