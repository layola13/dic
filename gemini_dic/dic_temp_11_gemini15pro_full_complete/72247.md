floriform:/ˈflɔːrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
花形的；花状的
Having the shape of a flower.
The artist created a floriform sculpture out of metal.  这位艺术家用金属创作了一个花形的雕塑。
The bacteria formed a floriform colony.  细菌形成了一个花状的菌落。
近义词:flower-shaped, flowerlike
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shapeless, amorphous)
词性: adjective
