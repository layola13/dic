begod:/bɪˈɡɒd/ /biˈɡɑːd/ | int. |我的天哪；真的；（表示惊讶、恼怒等）| An exclamation of surprise, annoyance, etc. | Begod, I've never seen anything like it! 我的天哪，我从未见过这样的事！| 近义词：by God, my goodness, good heavens | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an interjection) | 词性:感叹词

例句：
Begod, it's cold today!
我的天哪，今天真冷！

Begod, I forgot my keys!
我的天哪，我忘记带钥匙了！


词源:
From the phrase "by God," a mild oath or exclamation.  Over time, it contracted to "begod."

用法说明:
"Begod" is an interjection primarily used in Hiberno-English (Irish English). It expresses surprise, astonishment, annoyance, or emphasis.  It's considered somewhat archaic and informal, more common in older generations or in rural areas.  In other dialects of English, similar interjections like "by God," "my goodness," or "good heavens" would be more common.
