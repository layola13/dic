coastlines:/ˈkoʊstlaɪnz/| n. |海岸线|The boundary between land and sea. The outline of a coast, especially as shown on a map.|例句：The rugged coastlines of Maine are breathtaking.  缅因州崎岖的海岸线令人叹为观止。

例句：Rising sea levels threaten many of the world's coastlines.  海平面上升威胁着世界上的许多海岸线。|近义词：shores, coasts, seaside|反义词:inland|noun
