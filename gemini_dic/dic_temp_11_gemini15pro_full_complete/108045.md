latchet: /ˈlætʃɪt/ (US), /ˈlætʃɪt/ (UK)| n. |鞋带；系带；门闩 |A strap or thong used as a fastener for a shoe or sandal. Also, a latch, especially one for a door or gate.|例句：The latchet of her sandal broke. 她的凉鞋带断了。|例句：He lifted the latchet and opened the gate. 他 поднял защелку и открыл ворота. |近义词：strap, thong, fastener, latch |反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "buckle" as an alternative fastening method) |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *læccet, from læccan ("to seize, grasp"). Cognate with Dutch lachet ("lace, string"), German Lasche ("flap, strap").
