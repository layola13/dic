half-contentedly:/ˌhæf kənˈtɛntɪdli/
| adv. | 半心半意地；不太满意地 |  In a way that suggests a moderate degree of contentment, but also some dissatisfaction or reservation.  | She smiled half-contentedly, clearly still troubled by the news. 她不太满意地笑了笑，显然还在为这个消息感到困扰。| Near Synonyms: half-heartedly, lukewarmly, apathetically | Antonyms: enthusiastically, whole-heartedly, eagerly | 词性: 副词 (Adverb)
