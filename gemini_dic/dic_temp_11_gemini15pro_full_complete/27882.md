cant's:/kænts/ | n. |  虚伪的行为；伪善的言辞；黑话；行话 | Insincere talk; hypocritical statements; jargon; the language of a particular group. |  "The politician's speech was full of cant's about serving the people." 这位政客的演讲充满了关于为人民服务的虚伪言辞。 | 近义词: hypocrisy, pretense, jargon, slang | 反义词: sincerity, honesty, genuineness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the Old North French *cant, from chanter "to sing," from Latin cantare "to sing."  Originally referred to the whining tone of beggars and then to insincere religious pronouncements.  Later broadened to include any kind of hypocritical or insincere talk.
