filarial:/fɪˈleəriəl/ | adj. | 丝虫的；由丝虫引起的 | Relating to or caused by filariae (parasitic worms). | Example: Filarial infections are common in tropical countries. 丝虫病感染在热带国家很常见。

Example:  The doctor diagnosed a filarial worm infection. 医生诊断出丝虫感染。 | 近义词:  helminthic, parasitic | 反义词:  nonparasitic | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *fīlāria (“thread”), diminutive of *fīlum (“thread”).
