erased:/ɪˈreɪst/| adj.|被擦除的，被抹去的|Removed, obliterated, or deleted, as if by rubbing or wiping.|The teacher erased the writing on the board.  老师擦掉了黑板上的字。|近义词：deleted, removed, obliterated, expunged|反义词:written, recorded, preserved|词性:形容词
erased:/ɪˈreɪst/| v.|擦除，抹去|To remove (writing, recorded material, etc.) from a surface, as by rubbing or wiping.|He erased the mistake from his paper. 他从他的论文中擦去了错误。|近义词：delete, remove, obliterate, expunge|反义词:write, record, preserve|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *ērasus, past participle of ēradere “to scrape out,” from ex- “out” + radere “to scrape.”
