cloud's: /klaʊdz/
noun
云的；云朵的
Possessive form of "cloud," indicating ownership or association.  It refers to something that belongs to or is related to a cloud.
The cloud's shape was constantly changing. 云的形状不断变化。
The airplane flew through the cloud's edge. 飞机飞过了云的边缘。
Synonyms: cloud (in possessive context)
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a possessive form)
