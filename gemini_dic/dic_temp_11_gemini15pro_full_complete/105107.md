kerosenes:/ˈkɛrəsiːnz/
noun
煤油（ kerosene 的复数）
a flammable hydrocarbon oil used as fuel in lamps and heaters.
Kerosenes are used in jet engines.  煤油用于喷气发动机。
synonyms:  paraffin, lamp oil, coal oil
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a fuel.  One could consider alternative fuels like gasoline, diesel, propane as functional antonyms.)
词性: noun
