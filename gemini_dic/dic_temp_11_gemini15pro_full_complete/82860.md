gullet:/ˈɡʌlɪt/| n. |食道，咽喉；沟渠，水槽|The food goes down the gullet into the stomach. 食物通过食道进入胃里。|例句：

The pill stuck in his gullet.
药丸卡在他的食道里。

A small fish bone got stuck in my gullet.
一根小鱼刺卡在我的喉咙里。

|近义词：esophagus, throat|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

gullet:/ˈɡʌlɪt/| n. | （尤指狭窄的）水道，沟渠| Rainwater flowed down the gullet beside the road. 雨水沿着路边的沟渠流淌。 | 例句：

The gullet carried water away from the fields.
这条沟渠把水从田地里引走。

Debris clogged the gullet, causing flooding.
碎屑堵塞了水道，造成了洪水。


|近义词：channel, ditch, gutter|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
