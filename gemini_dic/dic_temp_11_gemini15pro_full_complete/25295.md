bugbears:/ˈbʌɡˌbɛərz/ (UK), /ˈbʌɡˌberz/ (US)| n.|妖怪; 讨厌的东西; 令人生畏的事物|A thing that causes obsessive fear or worry.  Something that is a source of annoyance or frustration.|例句：
His main bugbear is people who talk loudly on their phones in public.
他最讨厌的是那些在公共场合大声打电话的人。
