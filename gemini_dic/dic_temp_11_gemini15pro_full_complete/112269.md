lophobranch:/ˈlɒfəbræŋk/ /ˈloʊfəbræŋk/| n. |海龙科鱼类|A fish of the order Lophobranchii, such as a seahorse or pipefish, having a tubelike snout and an upright posture.|Seahorses and pipefish are examples of lophobranchs. 海马和尖嘴鱼是海龙科鱼类的例子。|近义词：syngnathid|反义词: None|词性: noun

lophobranchii:/ˌlɒfəˈbræŋkiˌaɪ/ /ˌloʊfəˈbræŋkiˌaɪ/| n. |海龙目|An order of bony fish characterized by a tube-like snout and an upright posture, including seahorses, pipefish, and seadragons.|The lophobranchii order includes some of the most fascinating fish in the ocean. 海龙目包括一些海洋中最迷人的鱼类。|近义词：Syngnathiformes|反义词: None|词性: noun
