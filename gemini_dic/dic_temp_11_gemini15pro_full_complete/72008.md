flintlike:/ˈflɪntˌlaɪk/| adj.|像燧石的，坚硬的，无情的|Resembling flint, especially in hardness or coldness of character.  Having a hard, unyielding nature; inflexible.|Her flintlike gaze intimidated him. 她的眼神像燧石般坚硬，令他害怕。|近义词：hard, inflexible, unyielding, stony, impassive|反义词：soft, yielding, compassionate, sympathetic|词性:形容词
词源: From flint + ‎-like.
