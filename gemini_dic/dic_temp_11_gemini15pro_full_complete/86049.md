healthy:/ˈhelθi/ (US), /ˈhelθi/ (UK)| adj.|健康的，健全的|In good health; free from disease.  Characterized by soundness or well-being.|例句：She has a healthy lifestyle. 她有一个健康的生活方式。|例句：He looks healthy and strong. 他看起来健康强壮。|近义词：well, fit, robust, strong, wholesome|反义词：unhealthy, sick, ill, diseased|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *helthli, from Old English *hǣlþlīċ, equivalent to health +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots hail, healthy (“healthy”), West Frisian healich (“holy”), Dutch heilzaam (“wholesome, salutary”), German heilsam (“wholesome, salutary”).
