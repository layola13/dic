electroplaque:/ɪˌlektrəˈplæk/ | n. | 电板 | A flat, enlarged cell found in electric organs of certain fish, such as electric eels and rays, that generates an electric discharge. | Electric eels have thousands of electroplaques that can produce a powerful electric shock. 电鳗拥有数千个电板，可以产生强大的电击。|  electrogenic cell, electrocyte | None | noun
