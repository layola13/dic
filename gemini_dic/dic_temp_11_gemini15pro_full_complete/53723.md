discoast:/dɪsˈkoʊst/
n.
disco-themed coast or coastal area; a place by the coast known for its disco culture or events.
A stretch of coastline known for its disco-themed parties and nightlife.
We spent the summer discoasting along the Italian Riviera, dancing the nights away at beach clubs.
近义词:  party coast, club coast
反义词:  tranquil coast, serene coastline
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"Disco" (from discothèque, a place where people dance to recorded music, particularly popular in the 1970s) + "coast" (the part of the land near the sea).  The term is a neologism, likely coined informally to describe a coastal area associated with disco music and culture.  It's not a standard dictionary entry.
