decapitate:/dɪˈkæpɪteɪt/
verb
斩首，砍头
to cut off the head of
The king was decapitated after the rebellion. 国王在叛乱后被斩首了。
近义词:behead, guillotine
反义词:N/A
词性:verb


Etymology:
From Late Latin decapitatus, past participle of decapitare  "to behead," from Latin de- "off" + caput "head."
