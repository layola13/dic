ionosphere:/aɪˈɑːnəˌsfɪr/ /aɪˈɒnəˌsfɪə(r)/| n. |电离层|The region of the earth's upper atmosphere where a high concentration of ions and free electrons exists, causing the reflection of radio waves.|Radio waves bounce off the ionosphere. 无线电波会从电离层反射回来。|近义词: upper atmosphere, thermosphere|反义词: 无|词性: 名词
