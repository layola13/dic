nonarchitectonic:/ˌnɒnˌɑːrkɪtɛkˈtɒnɪk/
adj.
非建筑的；非结构的；非构造的
Not pertaining to architecture or architectural design; lacking organization or structure.
The artist's work was described as nonarchitectonic, a chaotic yet compelling blend of colors and shapes. 艺术家的作品被描述为非建筑的，一种混乱却又引人注目的颜色和形状的混合。
non-structural, amorphous, unorganized, formless
architectonic, structural, organized, formed
adjective
