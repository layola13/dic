cloisterer:/ˈklɔɪstərər/ (US), /ˈklɔɪstərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |隐居者，修道士|A person who lives in a cloister, especially a monk or nun.|He lived a solitary life as a cloisterer, dedicating his time to prayer and contemplation. 他过着隐居的生活，像个修道士一样，把时间都花在祈祷和沉思上。|近义词：recluse, hermit, monk, nun|反义词：extrovert, socialite|词性: 名词

词源:  From cloister + -er.  "Cloister" comes from Old French cloistre, from Latin claustrum "lock, bolt, barrier, enclosed place," from claudere "to close."
