emblazon: /ɪmˈbleɪzən/ | vt. |装饰；纹饰；宣扬；颂扬 | To adorn or decorate richly, especially with heraldic devices; to proclaim or celebrate publicly. |  The knight's shield was emblazoned with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上饰有金色的狮子。

The company's logo was emblazoned on all of their products. 公司的标志装饰在他们所有的产品上。| 近义词: adorn, decorate, ornament, celebrate, extol | 反义词: obscure, conceal, hide | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *emblazoner, from en- "in, on" + blason "coat of arms." Ultimately from Frankish *blaso "trumpet, horn" (source also of Old High German blasan "to blow"). The original sense was "to put a coat of arms on a shield."
