dewani:/deɪˈvɑːni/ | n. | 民事（尤指婚姻和家庭事务）;  世俗的 |  Relating to civil as opposed to religious or military affairs, especially in law relating to marriage and family matters; secular. |  The couple had a dewani marriage ceremony. 这对夫妇举行了民事婚礼。| synonyms: civil, secular, non-religious | antonyms: religious, sacred, ecclesiastical | noun

Etymology: From Hindi दीवानी (dīvānī), from Persian دیوانی (dīvānī), ultimately from Arabic ديوان (dīwān, “collection of poems; council; tribunal”).
