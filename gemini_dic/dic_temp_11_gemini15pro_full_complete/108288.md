laurate:/ˈlɔːrɪt, ˈlɒr-/| n.|月桂酸酯; 月桂酸盐|A salt or ester of lauric acid.|Sodium laurate is a common ingredient in soap. 月桂酸钠是肥皂中的常见成分。|Synonyms: dodecanoate|Antonyms: None|noun

laurate:/ˈlɔːrɪt, ˈlɒr-/| adj.|十二烷酸的；月桂酸的|Derived from or containing lauric acid.|Laurate esters are used in cosmetics. 月桂酸酯用于化妆品。|Synonyms: dodecanoic|Antonyms: None|adjective
