antidote:/ˈæntidoʊt/| n. |解毒剂；对抗手段|A medicine taken or given to counteract a particular poison. Something that counteracts or neutralizes an unpleasant feeling or situation.|例句：1. There is no known antidote for this poison.  这种毒药没有已知的解毒剂。

2. Laughter is a good antidote to stress.  笑是缓解压力的良药。|近义词：remedy, cure, countermeasure|反义词:poison, toxin|noun
