emulousness:/ˈɛmjʊləsnəs/
n.
竞争心，好胜心，渴望 emulate others
A feeling of wanting to do as well as or better than others.
Her emulousness drove her to surpass all her classmates.  她的好胜心驱使她超越所有同学。
近义词: competitiveness, ambitiousness, rivalry
反义词: apathy, indifference, contentment
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin aemulus "rivaling," from Latin aemulari "to rival," from aemus "rivaling, emulating, imitative," of uncertain origin. The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, signifying a state or quality.
