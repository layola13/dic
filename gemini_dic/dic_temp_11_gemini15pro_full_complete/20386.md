bitangent:/baɪˈtændʒənt/| adj. n.|双切线的，双切线|A bitangent is a line that is tangent to a curve at two distinct points. |The bitangent of the ellipse intersects the curve at two points.  这条椭圆的双切线与曲线相交于两点。|The bitangent line touched the curve at two distinct locations. 这条双切线在两个不同的位置与曲线相切。|Synonyms: double tangent|Antonyms: N/A|adjective, noun
