anisotropous:/ˌænɪˈsɒtrəpəs/, /ˌænˌaɪsəˈtrɑːpəs/ | adj. | 各向异性的 | Exhibiting properties with different values when measured along axes in different directions. | Wood is an anisotropous material, exhibiting different strength and stiffness properties along its grain compared to across the grain.  木材是一种各向异性材料，沿纹理方向的强度和刚度与横纹方向不同。| anisotropic | isotropic | adjective

anisotropy:/ˌænɪˈsɒtrəpi/, /ˌænˌaɪˈsɑːtrəpi/ | n. | 各向异性 | The property of being directionally dependent, as opposed to isotropy. | The anisotropy of the crystal affects its optical properties. 晶体的各向异性会影响其光学特性。 | directional dependence, inhomogeneity | isotropy, homogeneity | noun
