book-smart:/ˈbʊk smɑːrt/
adjective
书呆子气的，只会纸上谈兵的
Having a lot of academic knowledge learned from books, but lacking practical experience or social skills.
He's book-smart but lacks common sense. 他书读得很好，但缺乏常识。
近义词:academic, studious, learned, erudite
反义词:street-smart, practical, savvy
词性:adjective


book-smart:/ˈbʊk smɑːrt/
noun
书呆子
A person who is very intelligent and knowledgeable, but lacks practical experience or social skills.
He's a book-smart, but he doesn't know how to change a tire. 他是个书呆子，但他不知道怎么换轮胎。
近义词:nerd, geek, egghead
反义词:street-smart, practical, savvy
词性:noun
