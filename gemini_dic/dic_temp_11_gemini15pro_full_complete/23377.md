brachiolaria:/brækiəˈlɑːriə/
n.
腕幼体
A free-swimming larval stage in many starfishes, characterized by three long, prehensile, ciliated arms.
The brachiolaria larva of the starfish attaches itself to a substrate before metamorphosing into a juvenile starfish.
近义词: larva
反义词: adult (in the context of life cycle stages)
词性: noun


brachiolaria:/brækiəˈlɑːriə/
n.
腕幼体
A free-swimming larval stage in many starfishes, equipped with three long arms used for attachment and locomotion.
During its development, the starfish transforms from a brachiolaria larva into its adult form.
近义词:  bipinnaria (another starfish larval stage)
反义词:  adult (in the context of life cycle stages)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
