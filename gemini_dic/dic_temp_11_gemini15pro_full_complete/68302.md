falconries: /ˈfɔːlkənriz/
n.
驯鹰术; 饲养猎鹰的地方
The sport of hunting with trained hawks; places where hawks are kept and trained.
The king enjoyed the ancient art of falconries. 国王喜欢古老的驯鹰术。
近义词: hawking, falconry
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "animal cruelty" in a specific context)
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *fauconnerie*  (modern French *fauconnerie*), derived from *faucon* "falcon," from Late Latin *falco*, ultimately of Germanic origin.
