by-rote:/baɪˈroʊt/
adverb|死记硬背地，机械地|From memory, without understanding. Learned or done by repeating something many times rather than by understanding it.|She learned her lines by rote. 她死记硬背台词。|近义词：mechanically, mindlessly|反义词： thoughtfully, understandingly|词性: adverb
Etymology: From Middle English *bi rote*, from *bi* ("by") + *rote* ("rote").  The word "rote" itself comes from Old French *rote* ("hurdy-gurdy," hence "mechanical repetition"), ultimately from Late Latin *rota* ("wheel").
