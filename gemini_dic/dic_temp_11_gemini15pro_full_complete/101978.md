isochasmic:/ˌaɪsəˈkæzmɪk/
adjective
等染色体数的; 同配的
Having the same number of chromosomes.  Relating to or characterized by isochasmy.
例句：Isochasmic species have similar chromosome numbers. 等染色体数的物种具有相似的染色体数。

例句：The isochasmic lines in the hybrid showed stable inheritance.  杂种中等染色体数的品系显示出稳定的遗传性。

近义词：homoploid (同倍体)
反义词：heteroploid (异倍体), anisoploid (非整倍体)
词性: adjective
