activism: /ˈæktɪvɪzəm/ | n. |行动主义; 积极主义|The practice of vigorous action or involvement as a means of achieving political or other goals, especially in support of or opposition to one side of a controversial issue.|  Example:  Student activism played a key role in the civil rights movement.  学生行动主义在民权运动中扮演了关键角色。

Example:  Her activism for environmental protection is admirable. 她对环境保护的积极行动令人钦佩。| 近义词: advocacy, campaigning, militancy | 反义词: apathy, indifference, inaction | 词性: noun

Etymology: From active + -ism.  Late 19th century.
