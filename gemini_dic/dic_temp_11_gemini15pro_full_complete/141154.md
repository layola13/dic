ooelogy: /ˌoʊɪˈlɒdʒi/
n.|卵子学|The study of eggs, particularly bird eggs.| Ornithologists often specialize in ooelogy, meticulously cataloging and studying different bird eggs. 鸟类学家通常专攻卵子学，仔细地对不同的鸟蛋进行分类和研究。|近义词:ovology|反义词: None|词性: noun


Etymology:  From Greek  ᾠόν (ōión, “egg”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
