afforded:/əˈfɔːrdɪd/
v.|提供; 给予; 负担得起|provide or supply (an opportunity or facility). be able to spare or give up. have enough money to pay for.|The rooftop afforded a panoramic view of the city. 屋顶提供了城市的全景。 I can't afford a new car. 我买不起新车。|近义词：provide, supply, give, offer, furnish, yield; pay for, manage, bear the cost of|反义词：refuse, deny, withhold; be unable to afford|词性：动词
