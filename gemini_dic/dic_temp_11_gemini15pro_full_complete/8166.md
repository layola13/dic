anti-culture:/ˈæntiˌkʌltʃər/
noun
反文化
A movement or lifestyle that rejects or opposes the prevailing social norms and values.
The punk rock movement was a significant expression of anti-culture in the 1970s.  20世纪70年代的朋克摇滚运动是反文化的重要表现形式。
The anti-culture movement questioned traditional societal norms.  反文化运动质疑传统的社会规范。
synonyms: counterculture, nonconformity
antonyms: mainstream culture, dominant culture
