besmirching:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃɪŋ/
verb
玷污，损害(名誉)
The scandal besmirched the reputation of the entire company.  这桩丑闻玷污了整个公司的名誉。
例句：The politician's reputation was besmirched by the allegations of corruption. 这位政治家的声誉因腐败指控而受损。

例句：His attempt to besmirch his opponent's character backfired. 他试图诋毁对手的性格，结果适得其反。

近义词：sullying, tarnishing, defaming, smearing, discrediting
反义词：honoring, praising, extolling, lauding
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *besmirchen, from be- + smirchen (“to besmirch”), from Old English smiercian (“to smear, anoint, pollute”), from Proto-Germanic *smerkōną (“to smear”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)mer- (“to rub, smear”). Cognate with Dutch smeren (“to smear, lubricate”), German schmieren (“to smear, lubricate”), Icelandic smyrja (“to smear, anoint”).
