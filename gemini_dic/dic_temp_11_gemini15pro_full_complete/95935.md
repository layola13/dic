incubate:/ˈɪŋkjubeɪt/ /ˈɪn.kjə.beɪt/| v. |孵化，培养，酝酿|to promote the growth or development of : foster|The scientists incubated the bacteria in a warm environment. 科学家们在温暖的环境中培养细菌。|Synonyms: foster, nurture, cultivate, develop, hatch|Antonyms: neglect, stifle, suppress|verb

incubated:/ˈɪŋkjubeɪted/ /ˈɪn.kjə.beɪ.tɪd/ | adj. |孵化的，培养的|maintained under conditions favorable for hatching or development|The project was incubated within the university's research lab.  这个项目是在大学的研究实验室里孵化出来的。|Synonyms: fostered, nurtured, cultivated, developed|Antonyms: neglected, stifled, suppressed|adjective
