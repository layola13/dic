emergentnesses:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒəntnɪsɪz/ | n. | 出现；浮现；紧急情况（复数） | The state or quality of being emergent; the process of emerging.  The fact of something emerging. Instances of things emerging. | The emergentnesses of new technologies are changing the world rapidly. 新技术的出现正在迅速改变世界。The emergentnesses of several crises required immediate attention. 几起危机的出现需要立即关注。| 近义词：emergences, appearances, manifestations | 反义词：disappearances, vanishings | 词性: 名词

词源:  From emergent + -ness. Emergent from Latin emergere "to rise out, come forth," from ex- "out" + mergere "to dip, plunge."
