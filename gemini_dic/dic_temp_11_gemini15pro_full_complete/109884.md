liable:/ˈlaɪəbl/
adjective
有责任的，有义务的；易于…的，倾向于…的
Subject to or likely to incur (something undesirable).
The company is liable for any damage caused by its products.  这家公司对其产品造成的任何损害负有责任。
He is liable to get angry if you contradict him.  如果你反驳他，他很容易生气。
synonyms: responsible, accountable, prone, susceptible
antonyms: immune, exempt, resistant
词性: 形容词


liablest:/ˈlaɪəb(ə)lɪst/
adjective
最有可能的，最易于…的 （liable的最高级）
Most subject to or likely to incur (something undesirable).
Of all the candidates, she is the liablest to make a mistake under pressure. 在所有候选人中，她是最有可能在压力下犯错的。
Which type of tree is liablest to be struck by lightning? 哪种树最容易被闪电击中？
synonyms: most susceptible, most prone
antonyms: least susceptible, least prone
词性: 形容词
