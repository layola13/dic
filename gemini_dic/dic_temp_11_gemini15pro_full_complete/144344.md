outstart:/ˌaʊtˈstɑːrt/
verb
超越，胜过，超过
To surpass or excel, especially in speed or quickness.
He outstarted his rivals in the race and won easily. 他在比赛中超越了对手，轻松获胜。
outstrip, outdo, surpass, excel
lag behind, fall behind
v.
