arbalest: /ˈɑːrbəlɪst/ | n. | 弩，十字弓 | A powerful medieval crossbow.  A weapon designed to shoot projectiles, typically bolts or quarrels, with greater force and range than a regular bow. It typically required a mechanical device to cock. | The knight raised his arbalest and took aim. 骑士举起弩，瞄准目标。 | The arbalestier fired a bolt at the castle gate.  弩手向城堡大门射出一支弩箭。 |  近义词: crossbow, ballista | 反义词: bow, sling | noun
