forcers:/ˈfɔːrsərz/| n.|强迫者；强制因素；推动者|People or things that cause something to happen, especially quickly or in a particular direction. |Greenhouse gases are major climate forcers. 温室气体是主要的气候驱动力。|Synonyms: drivers, agents, catalysts |Antonyms: inhibitors, deterrents|noun
