immemorially:/ˌɪməˈmɔːriəli/
adv.
自古以来，从无法追忆的时代起
Since time immemorial; for a very long time.
The river has flowed immemorially towards the sea. 这条河自古以来就流向大海。
近义词:anciently, timelessly, always
反义词:recently, lately
词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *immemoriālis (“immemorial”), from in- (“not”) + memoriālis (“of memory”), from memoria (“memory”).  Equivalent to immemorial +‎ -ly.
