abhors:/əˈbɔːrz/ /æbˈhɔːrz/
verb
憎恶，厌恶
regard with disgust and hatred.
She abhors the way people leave their trash on the beach. 她厌恶人们把垃圾留在沙滩上的行为。
He abhors violence in any form.  他憎恨任何形式的暴力。
loathe, detest, despise, abominate
admire, love, cherish, relish
词性:动词
