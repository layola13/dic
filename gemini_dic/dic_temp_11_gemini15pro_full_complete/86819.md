hematoid:/ˈhiːmətɔɪd/ | adj. | 血样的；似血的 | Resembling blood; bloody. | The wound oozed a hematoid fluid. 伤口渗出一种血样的液体。|  The hematoid stain on the carpet was alarming. 地毯上的血迹令人担忧。 | 近义词: bloodlike, sanguineous, bloody | 反义词: bloodless | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *haimat-* (blood) + *-oid* (resembling).
