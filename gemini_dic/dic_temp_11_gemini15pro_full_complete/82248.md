groundbreaking: /ˈɡraʊndˌbreɪkɪŋ/
| adj. | 开创性的；突破性的 | innovative; pioneering; introducing new ideas or methods. | The scientist made a groundbreaking discovery that changed the way we understand the universe. 这位科学家做出了一个开创性的发现，改变了我们对宇宙的理解。| 近义词: innovative, pioneering, revolutionary | 反义词: conventional, traditional, unoriginal | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "groundbreaking" (ceremony of breaking ground for a new construction) + "-ing" (suffix forming a noun from a verb).  源于“groundbreaking”（新建筑破土动工仪式）+“-ing”（从动词构成名词的后缀）。
