mispassion:/ˌmɪsˈpæʃən/
n.
误解的热情；错爱的激情
A strong feeling or enthusiasm directed towards something or someone mistakenly or inappropriately.
His mispassion for gambling led him to ruin. 他对赌博的错爱导致他走向毁灭。
近义词: infatuation, obsession
反义词: apathy, indifference
词性: 名词
