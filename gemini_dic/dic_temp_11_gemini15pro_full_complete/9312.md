antipode:/ˈæntɪˌpoʊd/ (US), /ænˈtɪpəd/ (UK)| n. |对跖点；正相反的事物；截然相反的人|A place on the earth diametrically opposite to another.  Something that is the direct opposite of something else. A person who is the complete opposite of another person.|例句：New Zealand is the antipode of Spain. 新西兰是西班牙的对跖点。


例句：His lifestyle is the antipode of mine. 他的生活方式与我的截然相反。


近义词：opposite, contrary, reverse|反义词:same, similar|词性:noun
