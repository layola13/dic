demisting:/diːˈmɪstɪŋ/
n.|除雾，去雾|The process of removing mist or condensation from a surface, such as a car windshield or bathroom mirror.|The demisting system in my car is very effective. 我的车里的除雾系统非常有效。|近义词：defogging, clearing|反义词：fogging, misting|noun
demist:/diːˈmɪst/
v.|除雾，去除雾气|To remove mist or condensation from a surface.|I had to demist the windshield before I could drive. 我开车前必须先清除挡风玻璃上的雾气。|近义词：defog, clear|反义词：fog, mist|verb
