chemo-therapist:/ˌkiːmoʊˈθɛrəpɪst/ | n. | 化疗师，化学治疗师 | A medical professional specializing in the administration of chemotherapy to treat cancer. |  The chemo-therapist explained the potential side effects of the treatment. 化疗师解释了治疗的潜在副作用。 | The chemo-therapist monitored the patient's blood counts throughout the course of chemotherapy. 化疗师在整个化疗过程中监测病人的血细胞计数。 |  oncologist | None | Noun

Etymology:
chemo- (from Greek *khēmeia* meaning "alchemy") + therapist (from Greek *therapeutēs* meaning "attendant, caretaker")
