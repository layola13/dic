gaiting:/ˈɡeɪtɪŋ/
n.
步态，步法
the way a person or animal walks
例句：The horse's gaiting was smooth and rhythmic. 这匹马的步态流畅而有节奏。

例句：The therapist analyzed the patient's gaiting to identify the source of their pain.  治疗师分析了病人的步态以确定他们疼痛的来源。

近义词：walk, stride, gait
反义词：(No direct antonym, but consider "immobility," "stillness")
词性：名词
