on-field:/ˈɒn fiːld/ (UK) /ˈɑːn fiːld/ (US)| adj.|场上，比赛场上的|Relating to or taking place on the field of play, as opposed to off the field.|例句：His on-field performance was impressive. 他在比赛场上的表现令人印象深刻。|例句：The coach discussed the on-field strategy with the team. 教练与团队讨论了场上的策略。|近义词：in-game, in-play|反义词：off-field, off-court|词性:形容词

on-field:/ˈɒn fiːld/ (UK) /ˈɑːn fiːld/ (US)| n.|比赛场地|The playing field itself.|例句：The fans rushed onto the on-field after the game. 比赛结束后，球迷们冲进了比赛场地。|例句：The on-field was muddy after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，比赛场地泥泞不堪。|近义词：field, pitch, ground|反义词：stands, bleachers|词性:名词
