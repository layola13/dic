haemadromometry:/ˌhiːmədrɒˈmɒmɪtri/ | n. |血流速度测定法| The measurement of the velocity of blood flow. | Haemadromometry is crucial for understanding cardiovascular health. 血流速度测定法对于理解心血管健康至关重要。|  The doctor used haemadromometry to assess the patient's circulation. 医生使用血流速度测定法来评估病人的血液循环。 | 近义词: blood flow measurement,  hemodynamics | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *haima* (blood) + *dromos* (running, course) + *-metria* (measurement).
