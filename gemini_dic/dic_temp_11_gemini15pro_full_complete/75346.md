frostweed:/ˈfrɔːstwiːd/| n. |霜草，冰草|A plant, *Verbesina virginica* or *Helianthemum canadense*, that exudes ice crystals from its stem in late autumn or early winter. |Example 1:  The frostweed was covered in delicate ice formations. 霜草上覆盖着精致的冰晶。

Example 2:  I saw frostweed growing along the edge of the forest. 我看到森林边缘生长着霜草。|近义词：ice plant, iceweed |反义词: N/A | noun
