koumiss:/kuːˈmɪs/ | n. | 马奶酒 | A fermented drink made from mare's milk. |  Example 1:  The nomads offered us koumiss, a slightly sour but refreshing drink.  游牧民族给我们提供了马奶酒，一种略带酸味但令人耳目一新的饮料。

Example 2:  Koumiss is a traditional beverage in Central Asia. 马奶酒是中亚地区的传统饮料。 | 近义词:  airag (specifically Mongolian koumiss) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Russian кумыс (kumys), from Turkic *kımız. Ultimately from Proto-Turkic *kɨmɨz.
