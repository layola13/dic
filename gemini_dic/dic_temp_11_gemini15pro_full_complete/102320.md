ivory-towerish:/ˈaɪvəri ˈtaʊərɪʃ/| adj.|象牙塔式的，脱离实际的|Resembling or suggesting an ivory tower; impractical, out of touch with reality.  Characteristic of those who live or exist in an "ivory tower,"  a metaphorical place, away from the practicalities and concerns of everyday life.|His ivory-towerish theories have no bearing on the real world. 他的象牙塔式理论与现实世界无关。

The professor's ivory-towerish approach to economics made it difficult for students to connect the concepts to real-life situations.  这位教授象牙塔式的经济学方法使学生难以将这些概念与现实生活联系起来。|近义词：unrealistic, impractical, academic, theoretical, esoteric, secluded, cloistered |反义词：practical, realistic, down-to-earth, pragmatic|词性: 形容词
