haemocytolysis:/ˌhiːməsaɪˈtɒlɪsɪs/ /ˌhiməsaɪˈtɑːləsɪs/
noun
溶血
The destruction or lysis of red blood cells.
Example 1: The venom of certain snakes causes rapid haemocytolysis.  某些蛇的毒液会导致快速溶血。

Example 2:  The lab test showed evidence of haemocytolysis in the patient's blood sample. 实验室检测显示病人的血液样本中有溶血的迹象。
近义词: hemolysis
反义词:  N/A (There isn't a direct antonym for the destruction of red blood cells.)
词性: n.
