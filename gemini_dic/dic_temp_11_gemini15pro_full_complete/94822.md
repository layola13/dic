impute:/ɪmˈpjuːt/ | vt. | 归罪于，归咎于；嫁祸于 | To attribute or ascribe (something dishonest or undesirable) to a person. | The police impute the accident to the driver's negligence. 警方将事故归咎于司机的疏忽。| attribute, ascribe, assign |  exonerate, absolve | verb
