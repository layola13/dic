explicative:/ˈɛksplɪkətɪv/| adj.|解释的，说明的|Serving to explain or interpret something; making clear the meaning of something.|The teacher provided explicative notes on the difficult poem.|近义词：explanatory, interpretive, illustrative|反义词：implicit, ambiguous|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *explicativus*, from Latin explicare (“to unfold, explain”), from ex- (“out”) + plicare (“to fold”).
