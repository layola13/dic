enlightener:/ɪnˈlaɪtnər/| n. |启蒙者；教化者；开导者|A person who helps someone understand something better or gives them new knowledge or insight.| Voltaire was a great enlightener of the 18th century.  伏尔泰是18世纪伟大的启蒙者。|
The teacher acted as an enlightener, guiding her students towards a deeper understanding of the subject.  老师扮演着启蒙者的角色，引导学生更深入地理解这门学科。|近义词：educator, teacher, instructor, mentor, guide|反义词：deceiver, misleader, obscurer|词性:noun
