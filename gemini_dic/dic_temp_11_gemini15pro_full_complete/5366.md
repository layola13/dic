allusive:/əˈluːsɪv/
adjective
暗示的，引经据典的
Suggesting something indirectly; hinting at.
Her poems are richly allusive and draw on a wide range of literary sources. 她的诗歌充满了典故，借鉴了大量的文学资源。

The novel's allusive style made it challenging to understand. 这部小说的暗示性风格使它难以理解。

synonym: suggestive, indirect, hinting, evocative, reminiscent
antonym: explicit, direct, clear, straightforward
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin allusivus, from alludere ‘to play with’ (in the sense ‘hint at’), from ad- ‘to’ + ludere ‘to play’.
词性: 形容词
