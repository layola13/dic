chastises:/ˈtʃæstaɪzɪz/| v. |责备，惩罚，斥责|to rebuke or reprimand severely|例句：The teacher chastises the student for being late. 老师斥责学生迟到。

例句：She was chastised for her carelessness. 她因粗心而受到责备。

近义词：reprimand, rebuke, scold, admonish, censure|反义词: praise, commend, laud|词性:动词
