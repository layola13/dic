decapitator:/dɪˈkæpɪˌteɪtər/
n.
斩首者，砍头者
One who decapitates; an executioner.
The decapitator remained masked throughout the execution.  刽子手在整个行刑过程中都戴着面具。
The film depicted a gruesome scene involving a decapitator and his victim.  这部电影描绘了一个可怕的场景，涉及一个刽子手和他的受害者。

近义词:executioner, headsman, killer
反义词:protector, savior

词性:noun

词源: From the Late Latin decapitator, from decapitare "to behead," from de- "off" + caput "head."
