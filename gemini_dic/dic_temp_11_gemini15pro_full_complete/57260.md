downlying:/ˈdaʊnˌlaɪɪŋ/| adj.|低洼的；下层的；底层的|Situated at a lower level; underlying.|The downlying areas were flooded after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，低洼地区都被淹没了。|The downlying bedrock is primarily granite. 下层的基岩主要是花岗岩。|近义词：low-lying, lower, underlying, basal|反义词：upland, elevated, higher|词性:形容词

词源:  "down" (向下) + "lying" (躺着的，位于的) -  The word is a compound formed from the adverb "down" and the present participle of the verb "lie."  It literally describes something that lies down or at a lower level.
