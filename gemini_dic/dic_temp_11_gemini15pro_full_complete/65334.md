euhemerist:/juːˈhiːmərɪst/
n.|神话历史化主义者；理性解释神话者|A euhemerist interprets myths as traditional accounts of historical persons and events. |Many classical scholars adopted a euhemeristic approach to mythology.|Synonyms: rationalist, literalist, historicist|Antonyms: mythologist, fabulist|noun
