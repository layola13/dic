compositeness:/kəmˈpɑːzɪtnəs/| n. |合成性，复合性|The state or quality of being composite; the result of being made up of distinct parts or elements.|The compositeness of the material makes it strong and flexible. 这种材料的复合性使它既坚固又灵活。|近义词：complexity, compoundness|反义词：simplicity, homogeneity|词性: noun
Etymology: Late 16th century: from composite + -ness.
