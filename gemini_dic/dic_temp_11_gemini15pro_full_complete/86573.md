helicoptering:/ˈhɛlɪkɒptərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhɛlɪkɑːptərɪŋ/ (US)| n. v. (present participle)|盘旋，直升机式育儿，过分关注|The act of hovering or behaving like a helicopter, especially in parenting, characterized by excessive involvement and control.  Derived from "helicopter" + "-ing."|例句：The helicoptering parent hovered around her child, constantly intervening.  这个直升机式的家长在孩子身边徘徊，不断干预。

例句：Helicoptering can stifle a child's independence. 直升机式育儿会扼杀孩子的独立性。|近义词：hovering, overparenting, meddling|反义词:neglectful, inattentive|词性:Noun (Gerund), Verb (present participle)
