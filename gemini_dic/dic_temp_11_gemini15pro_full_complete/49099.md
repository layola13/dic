deify:/ˈdiːɪfaɪ/ /ˈdeɪɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 神化；奉若神明 | To treat or regard someone or something as a god or a divine being. |  The Romans deified their emperors. 罗马人神化他们的皇帝。

The fans practically deified the young pop star. 歌迷们简直把这位年轻的流行歌星奉若神明。

近义词：idolize, worship, exalt, glorify
反义词：condemn, vilify, disparage
词性：动词 (verb)

词源：From Late Latin deificare "to make a god of," from Latin deus "god" + -ficare, combining form of facere "to make."
