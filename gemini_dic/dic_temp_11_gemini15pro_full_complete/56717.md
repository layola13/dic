doop:/duːp/|n.|傻瓜，易受骗的人|A gullible or easily fooled person.|He was such a doop to believe that story. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然相信了那个故事。|Synonyms: fool, sucker, dupe, chump|Antonyms: skeptic, cynic|noun
