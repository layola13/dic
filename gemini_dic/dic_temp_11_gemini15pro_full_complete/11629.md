ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *armature*, from Latin *armātūra* ("arms, armor, equipment"), from *armō* ("I arm, equip for war"), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂er-/*h₃er- ("to fit together, to join").
