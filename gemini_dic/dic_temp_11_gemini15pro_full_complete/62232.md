encroaching: /ɪnˈkroʊtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 侵占的，逐渐逼近的 |  Gradually intruding on or taking over something, especially territory or rights.  | The encroaching desert was slowly swallowing the farmland.  沙漠逐渐蚕食着农田。 | synonyms: intruding, invading, advancing, trespassing | antonyms: receding, retreating, withdrawing | adjective

encroach: /ɪnˈkroʊtʃ/ | v. | 侵占，侵犯 | Intrude on (a person's territory or a thing considered to be a right). |  The new shopping mall encroached on the local wildlife habitat. 新建的购物中心侵占了当地野生动物的栖息地。 | synonyms: intrude, infringe, impinge, trespass | antonyms: respect, uphold, observe | verb
