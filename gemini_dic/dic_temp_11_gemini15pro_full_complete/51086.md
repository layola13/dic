despecification:/diːˌspɛsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
取消规格；去规格化；解除规范
The process of removing or reducing the detailed specifications of a product or system, often to allow for greater flexibility or to simplify production.
The despecification of the product allowed it to be sold at a lower price.  该产品的去规格化使其能够以更低的价格出售。
The company undertook a despecification process to streamline manufacturing. 公司进行了去规格化流程以简化制造。
近义词: simplification, generalization, standardization
反义词: specification, detailing, elaboration
词性: noun
