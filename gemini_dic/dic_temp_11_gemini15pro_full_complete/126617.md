词源：
From Old English *nægl, from Proto-Germanic *naglaz (compare West Frisian neil, Dutch nagel, German Nagel, Danish negl, Swedish nagel).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(h₁)nogʰ- (“nail, claw”).
