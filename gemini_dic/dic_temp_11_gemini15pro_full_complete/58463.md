ducting:/ˈdʌktɪŋ/
n.
管道系统，管路，导管
A system of ducts for ventilation, air conditioning, etc.
The air conditioning ducting needs to be cleaned.  空调管道需要清洗。

ducting:/ˈdʌktɪŋ/
n.
(雷达)波导，大气波导
A layer of air in the atmosphere that can guide radio waves.
Radar ducting can extend the range of radar detection. 雷达波导可以扩展雷达探测范围。


Etymology:
From duct + -ing.  "Duct" comes from the Latin word "ductus," meaning "leading."

Synonyms:
channel, conduit, pipe, tube

Antonyms:
(No direct antonyms, but consider concepts like "open air," "unconfined space" as contrasting ideas)

Word Type:
Noun
