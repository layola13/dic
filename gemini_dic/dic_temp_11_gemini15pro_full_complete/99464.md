interfaith:/ˌɪntərˈfeɪθ/| adj.|不同信仰的；跨宗教的|Relating to or involving people of different religious faiths.|例句：The interfaith dialogue promoted understanding and respect between different religious communities.  跨宗教对话促进了不同宗教社群之间的理解和尊重。

例句：The interfaith service brought together people from various religious backgrounds to pray for peace.  跨宗教的仪式将来自不同宗教背景的人们聚集在一起祈祷和平。|近义词：interreligious, ecumenical|反义词：sectarian, denominational|词性:形容词

Etymology:
inter- + faith
inter-: from Latin "inter" meaning "between, among"
faith: from Old French "feid"  meaning "trust, belief, confidence; pledge, promise"
