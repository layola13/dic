looked-over:/lʊktˈoʊvər/ | adj. | 检查过的；浏览过的；忽视的 | examined, inspected; browsed; disregarded |  The accountant looked over the financial statements. 会计师检查了财务报表。|  She looked over the menu quickly. 她快速浏览了菜单。 | synonyms: examined, inspected, reviewed, scanned, perused, neglected, ignored | antonyms: unexamined, uninspected, overlooked (in the sense of missed), noticed | adjective


looked-over:/lʊktˈoʊvər/ | v. | 检查；浏览；忽视 | to examine, inspect; to browse; to disregard | The doctor looked over the patient. 医生检查了病人。 | He looked over the job applicants' resumes. 他浏览了求职者的简历。 | synonyms: examine, inspect, review, scan, peruse, neglect, ignore | antonyms: overlook (in the sense of miss), notice | verb

词源:  "look" (Old English *lōcian "to see, look at") + "over" (Old English ofer "above, across, beyond")
