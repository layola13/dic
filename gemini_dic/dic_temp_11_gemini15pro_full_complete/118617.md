messengership:/ˈmɛsɪndʒərʃɪp/| n.|信使的职位；使者的身份|The state or office of a messenger.  The act of being a messenger. |He held the messengership for the king. 他担任国王的信使。|近义词：mission, duty, assignment|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From messenger + -ship.  "Messenger" comes from the Old French "messagier" derived from the Late Latin "missaticarius" (one who performs a missio, a sending or mission). "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting state, condition, or office.
