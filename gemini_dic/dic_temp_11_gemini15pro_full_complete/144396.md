outstriking:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪkɪŋ/
verb
超过打击; 胜过; 打击超过
striking out further or more effectively than another or others.
The boxer was outstriking his opponent in the early rounds.  这位拳击手在早期回合中打击次数超过了他的对手。
The company is outstriking its competitors in the development of new technologies.  该公司在新技术开发方面胜过其竞争对手。
近义词: surpassing, exceeding, outperforming, outdoing
反义词: underperforming, lagging behind
词性: Verb
