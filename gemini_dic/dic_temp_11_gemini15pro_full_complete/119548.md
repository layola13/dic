microspectroscope:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈspɛktroʊskoʊp/ | n. | 显微分光镜 | A microscope combined with a spectroscope, used to observe the absorption or emission spectra of very small objects. | Scientists used a microspectroscope to analyze the pigment in a single grain of pollen. 科学家用显微分光镜分析了一粒花粉中的色素。 |  Synonyms: microspectrophotometer | Antonyms: N/A | noun
