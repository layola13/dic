hails:/heɪlz/
verb
下冰雹；倾泻；热情赞扬；招呼；来自
Comes from Old English *hagōl, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch hagel and German Hagel.
It hails a lot in this region during spring.  在这个地区春天经常下冰雹。
Critics hailed the movie as a masterpiece.  评论家们盛赞这部电影是杰作。
近义词: pour, shower, acclaim, greet
反义词:  (for hail as precipitation) shine, (for hail as greeting) ignore


hails:/heɪlz/
noun
冰雹；一阵
Comes from Old English *hagōl, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch hagel and German Hagel.
The hailstones were as big as golf balls.  冰雹像高尔夫球一样大。
A hail of bullets struck the vehicle. 一阵弹雨袭击了车辆。
近义词: barrage, volley
反义词:  sunshine
