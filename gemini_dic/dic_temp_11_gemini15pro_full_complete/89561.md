honour:/ˈɒnə(r) (UK), ˈɑːnər (US)/
n.|荣誉，光荣|High moral standards.|She received an honour for her bravery. 她因勇敢而获得荣誉。
honour:/ˈɒnə(r) (UK), ˈɑːnər (US)/
v.|尊敬，给予荣誉|To regard with great respect.|They honoured him with a knighthood. 他们授予他爵位以示敬意。
honour:/ˈɒnə(r) (UK), ˈɑːnər (US)/
n.|荣幸，光荣的事情|A privilege or pleasure.|It was an honour to meet her. 见到她很荣幸。
