nonaluminum:/ˌnɑːnˌəˈluːmɪnəm/
adjective
非铝的；非铝制的
Not made of aluminum; other than aluminum.
This pan is made of a nonaluminum material.  这个锅是用非铝材料制成的。
The bridge is built with nonaluminum alloys. 这座桥是用非铝合金建造的。
近义词:non-aluminium, non-aluminous
反义词:aluminum, aluminium, aluminous
词性:adjective
