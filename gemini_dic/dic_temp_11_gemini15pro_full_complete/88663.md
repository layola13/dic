hoarse:/hɔːrs/ /hɔrs/| adj.|嘶哑的，沙哑的|Having a rough or grating voice, often because of a sore throat or overuse.|例句：Her voice was hoarse from shouting. 她的声音因为喊叫而嘶哑了。

例句：He woke up with a hoarse cough. 他醒来时咳嗽得很厉害，声音嘶哑。

近义词：raspy, husky, gruff, gravelly|反义词：clear, smooth, melodious|词性：形容词
