outpolled:/ˌaʊtˈpəʊld/
verb
以更多的票数胜过，在投票中击败
To receive more votes than (another candidate or party) in an election.
The incumbent mayor was outpolled by a newcomer.  现任市长在选举中被一位新人击败。
The socialist candidate outpolled his rival by a wide margin.  社会主义候选人以很大的优势击败了他的对手。
近义词: defeat, beat, surpass, outvote
反义词: lose to
