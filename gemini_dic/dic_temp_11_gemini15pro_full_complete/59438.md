earthshaking:/ˈɜːrθʃeɪkɪŋ/| adj.|惊天动地的；极其重大的|Having a profound effect; revolutionary.|The invention of the internet was an earthshaking event. 互联网的发明是一个惊天动地的事件。|近义词：groundbreaking, momentous, revolutionary, transformative|反义词:insignificant, trivial|词性:形容词

词源:  From "earth" + "shaking," literally meaning causing the earth to shake, figuratively meaning having a profound impact.
