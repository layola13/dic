lithophane:/ˈlɪθəfeɪn/ |n.|透光瓷板，瓷浮雕|A translucent porcelain plaque, thin enough to transmit light, with a design in intaglio that appears as a cameo when backlit.|The lithophane cast a warm glow when the candle was placed behind it. 透光瓷板在蜡烛放在后面时发出温暖的光芒。|Near Synonyms: cameo, bas-relief, intaglio|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "opaque plaque")|Noun


lithophane:/ˈlɪθəfeɪn/ |n.|透光浮雕|A translucent relief, usually made of porcelain, that appears as a cameo when backlit.|The artist skillfully carved the lithophane, creating a detailed portrait.  艺术家巧妙地雕刻了透光浮雕，创作了一幅精细的肖像。|Near Synonyms: cameo, bas-relief, intaglio|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "opaque relief")|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *lithophanie*, from Ancient Greek *λίθος* (líthos, “stone”) + *φαίνειν* (phaínein, “to show, to make appear”).
