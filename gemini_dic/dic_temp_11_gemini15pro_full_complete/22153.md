bombard:/bɒmˈbɑːd/ (UK), /bɑːmˈbɑːrd/ (US)| v. |轰炸，炮击；大量提问；向…连续提出问题|To attack a place with continuous gunfire or bombs; to direct a continuous flow of questions or requests at someone.|The enemy bombarded the city for days. 敌人连续几天轰炸了这座城市。|近义词：shell, attack, assail, pepper, batter|反义词: defend, protect|词性:动词

bombardment:/bɒmˈbɑːdmənt/ (UK), /bɑːmˈbɑːrdmənt/ (US)| n. |轰炸，炮击；大量提问|A continuous attack with bombs or other missiles; a continuous flow of questions, criticisms, or requests.|The city was under constant bombardment. 这座城市持续遭受轰炸。|近义词：barrage, shelling, assault|反义词: defense, protection|词性:名词
