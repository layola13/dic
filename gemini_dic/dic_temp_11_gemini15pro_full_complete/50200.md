depaint:/diːˈpeɪnt/ | v. | 去除绘画，去除图像，去涂料 | To remove paint or a painted image from a surface, often digitally.  To reverse the process of painting. | The software can depaint the background of an image, leaving only the subject.  该软件可以去除图像的背景，只留下主体。 | remove, erase, delete | paint, add | verb

depaint:/diːˈpeɪnt/ | n. | 去除绘画（的动作或过程） | The act or process of removing paint or a painted image, especially digitally. | The depaint process took several hours due to the complexity of the image. 去除绘画的过程由于图像的复杂性而花费了几个小时。| removal, erasure, deletion | painting, addition | noun
