obstructionary:/əbˈstrʌkʃəneri/
adjective
妨碍的，阻碍的
Relating to or characterized by obstruction; hindering progress or flow.
The obstructionary tactics of the opposition party prevented the bill from passing. 反对党的阻挠策略阻止了该法案的通过。
The obstructionary behavior of the child caused a delay in the family's departure.  孩子的阻挠行为导致了家人出发时间的延误。

近义词: obstructive, hindering, impeding, blocking
反义词: helpful, facilitating, aiding, assisting
词性: adjective


词源:
From obstruct + -ion + -ary.  Obstruct comes from Latin obstructus, past participle of obstruere "to build in front of, block up," from ob- "against" + struere "to pile, build."
