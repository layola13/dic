collect:/kəˈlekt/ | v. n. adj.|收集，聚集，领取；收藏品；募捐|To gather or assemble things, often of a particular type. Also, to pick something up.  As a noun, it refers to a short prayer, or a collection of items. As an adjective (less common), it refers to payment being collected at the point of delivery.|例句：She collects stamps as a hobby. 她以集邮为爱好。|近义词：gather, assemble, accumulate, amass|反义词:disperse, scatter|词性:verb
