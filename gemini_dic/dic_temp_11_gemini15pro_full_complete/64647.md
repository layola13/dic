erythrolein: /ɪˌrɪθroʊˈliːɪn/ | n. | 赤藓糖醛；赤藓醛 | An aldehyde, C₅H₈O₄, obtained by the oxidation of erythrite.  A colorless to yellowish syrup, soluble in water, alcohol, and ether. | Erythrolein can be derived from erythritol through oxidation. 赤藓糖醛可以通过氧化得到赤藓糖醛。|  Erythrolein is a useful intermediate in organic synthesis. 赤藓糖醛是有机合成中的有用中间体。 | synonyms: none commonly used | antonyms: none | noun
