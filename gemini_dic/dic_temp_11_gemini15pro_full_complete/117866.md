melodie:/ˈmɛlədi/ | n. | 旋律，曲调 | A sequence of single notes that is musically satisfying. |  She hummed a sweet melodie. 她哼唱了一支甜美的曲调。| synonym: tune, air, theme | antonym: dissonance, cacophony | noun

melodie:/ˈmɛlədi/ | n. |歌曲，乐曲 | A piece of music, especially a song. | The melodie brought tears to her eyes. 这首曲子让她热泪盈眶。 | synonym: song, tune, piece | antonym: silence, noise | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mélodie*, from Late Latin *melodia*, from Greek *melodía*  "singing, chanting, a choral song," from *melos* "song, strain" + *oide* "song, ode."
