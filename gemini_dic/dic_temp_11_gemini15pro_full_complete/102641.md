jalopy:/dʒəˈlɒpi/ /dʒəˈlɑːpi/| n.|破旧的汽车，老爷车|An old, dilapidated car.  A jalopy is typically unreliable and in poor condition.|例句：He drove up in his rusty old jalopy. 他开着他那辆锈迹斑斑的破车来了。

例句：The jalopy coughed and sputtered before finally starting.  这辆老爷车咳了几声，喷了几下，最后才发动起来。|近义词：clunker, beater, banger (British)|反义词：luxury car, sports car|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
The origin of "jalopy" is uncertain, but some theories suggest it might be related to the Mexican city of Jalapa, known for its junkyards. Another theory connects it to the term "jalap," a purgative drug, implying the car is a source of trouble.
