gruel:/ˈɡruːəl/| n. |稀粥，麦片粥|A thin liquid food of oatmeal or other meal boiled in milk or water.|The prisoners were given nothing but gruel to eat. 囚犯们只得到稀粥吃。|Synonyms: porridge, broth, soup|Antonyms: feast, banquet|noun

gruel:/ˈɡruːəl/| v. |使精疲力竭，使极度疲劳 (通常用于被动语态)|To exhaust or wear out completely.|He was completely grueled after the marathon. 马拉松赛后他精疲力竭。|Synonyms: exhaust, enervate, fatigue|Antonyms: energize, invigorate|verb
