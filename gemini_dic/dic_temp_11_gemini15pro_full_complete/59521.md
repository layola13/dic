easy-peasy:/ˌiːziˈpiːzi/ | adj. | 非常容易，轻而易举 |  A child's term, reduplicating easy, emphasizing the simplicity of a task. |  "Tying your shoes is easy-peasy."  系鞋带很容易。 |  Synonyms: simple, effortless, child's play, a piece of cake | Antonyms: difficult, hard, challenging | adjective
