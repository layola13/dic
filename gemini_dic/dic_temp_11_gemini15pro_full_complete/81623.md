grecism: /ˈɡriːsɪzəm/ | n. | 希腊语用法; 希腊风格; 希腊习俗 | A word or expression borrowed from Greek.  A feature of language or style, or a custom, that is derived from Greek. |  The use of "hubris" is a common grecism in English literature.  在英语文学中，“傲慢”的使用是一种常见的希腊语用法。| Synonyms: Hellenism, Greekism | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider Romanism/Latinism for stylistic contrasts) | noun
