noncriminal:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɪmɪnl/
adjective
非犯罪的；不构成犯罪的
Not of a criminal nature; not involving or relating to crime.
Parking violations are considered noncriminal offenses. 停车违规被认为是非犯罪行为。
The investigation focused on noncriminal aspects of the case. 调查集中在本案的非犯罪方面。
Synonyms: lawful, legal, legitimate, licit
Antonyms: criminal, illegal, unlawful, illicit
词性: 形容词
