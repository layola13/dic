miscomputed:/ˌmɪskəmˈpjuːtɪd/
verb
错误计算的，计算错误的
calculated incorrectly.
The budget was miscomputed, resulting in a significant shortfall.  预算计算错误，导致了严重的资金短缺。
The trajectory of the rocket was miscomputed, causing it to miss its target. 火箭的弹道计算错误，导致它错过了目标。
miscalculated, misjudged, underestimated, overestimated
correctly calculated, accurately computed
