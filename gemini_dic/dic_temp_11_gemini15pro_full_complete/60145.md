effectuate:/ɪˈfɛktʃueɪt/
verb
使生效；实现；完成
To bring about; to cause to happen; to carry out; to put into effect.
The CEO worked to effectuate the merger of the two companies. 首席执行官致力于实现两家公司的合并。

The new policy was effectuated on January 1st.  新政策于1月1日生效。

近义词: achieve, accomplish, execute, implement, realize
反义词: prevent, hinder, obstruct
词性: verb
词源: From Late Latin effectuatus, past participle of effectuare "to make, perform," from Latin effectus (see effect) + -are (infinitive suffix).
