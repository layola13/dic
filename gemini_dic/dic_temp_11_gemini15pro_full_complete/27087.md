calice:/ˈkælɪs/| n.|圣餐杯; 花萼|A cup used to hold the wine in the Christian Eucharist.  The sepals of a flower, typically forming a whorl that encloses the petals and forms a protective layer around a flower in bud.|The priest raised the calice.  神父举起了圣餐杯。 The calice of the rose was a deep green. 玫瑰的花萼是深绿色的。|近义词：chalice (for the Eucharist sense), sepal (for the botanical sense)|反义词: N/A|noun
