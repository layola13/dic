overlasciviousness: /ˌoʊvər ləˈsɪviəsnəs/
n.
过度淫荡，极度好色
Excessive lustfulness; extreme lasciviousness.
His overlasciviousness made everyone around him uncomfortable. 他的过度好色让周围的人都感到不舒服。
近义词: lechery, lustfulness, salaciousness
反义词: chastity, modesty, purity
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From over- + lasciviousness.  "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "excessive, too much."  "Lasciviousness" comes from Late Latin *lasciviosus, from Latin lascivus "wanton, playful, sportive," of uncertain origin.
