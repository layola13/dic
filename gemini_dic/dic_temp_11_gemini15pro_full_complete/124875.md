mudbaths:/ˈmʌdbɑːðz/
n.
泥浴
A bath in mud, often for therapeutic or cosmetic purposes.
She enjoyed the mudbaths at the spa. 她很享受温泉浴场的泥浴。
近义词：mud therapy, mud treatment
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "shower," "bath" in regular water)
