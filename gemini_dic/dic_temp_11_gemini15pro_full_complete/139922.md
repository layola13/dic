odinism:/ˈoʊdɪnɪzəm/| n. |奥丁主义，北欧异教|A modern religious movement based on the pre-Christian beliefs and practices of the Norse and Germanic peoples, focusing on the worship of Norse gods such as Odin, Thor, and Freyja.|Odinism is growing in popularity in some parts of Europe. 奥丁主义在欧洲某些地区越来越受欢迎。|近义词：Norse paganism, Heathenry, Asatru|反义词:Christianity, Islam, Judaism|词性:noun
