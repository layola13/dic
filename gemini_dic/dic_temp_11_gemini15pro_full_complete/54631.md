dismisser:/dɪsˈmɪsər/
n.
解雇者；不予考虑者；轻视者
Someone who dismisses something or someone.
The dismisser of the complaint failed to properly investigate the issue.  该投诉的驳回者未能妥善调查此事。
The dismisser of new ideas stifled innovation within the company.  轻视新想法的人扼杀了公司内部的创新。
synonyms: rejecter, denier, scornful
antonyms: acceptor, believer, supporter
noun
