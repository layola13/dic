defectors:/dɪˈfɛktərz/| n.|叛逃者，变节者|People who abandon their country, cause, or party in favor of an opposing one.  Those who defect are often motivated by ideological, political, or personal reasons.|Many defectors from the former Soviet Union sought asylum in the United States.  许多前苏联的叛逃者在美国寻求政治庇护。|synonyms: traitors, deserters, renegades, turncoats|antonyms: loyalists, patriots|noun
