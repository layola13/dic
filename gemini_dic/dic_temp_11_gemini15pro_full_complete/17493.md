bearishnesses:/ˈbɛərɪʃnəsɪz/
noun
看跌；熊市气氛
The general bearishness in the market made investors hesitant.
例句：The prevailing bearishness in the stock market has led many investors to sell their holdings.  股市普遍的看跌情绪导致许多投资者抛售了他们的持股。

例句：Despite the company's strong performance, the analyst's report contributed to the bearishness surrounding the stock. 尽管公司业绩强劲，但分析师的报告加剧了围绕该股票的看跌情绪。
近义词：pessimism, negativity, gloom
反义词：optimism, bullishness, positivity
词性：n.
