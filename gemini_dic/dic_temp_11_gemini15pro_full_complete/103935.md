judgment:/ˈdʒʌdʒmənt/ (US) /ˈdʒʌdʒmənt/ (UK)| n.|判断；判断力；判决；意见；看法|The ability to make considered decisions or come to sensible conclusions.  A misfortune or calamity viewed as a divine punishment.|例句：He showed good judgment in choosing his staff. 他在选择员工方面展现了良好的判断力。

例句：The judgment of the court was in her favor. 法院的判决对她有利。

近义词：decision, verdict, opinion, assessment, evaluation, discernment, conclusion, ruling, sentence|反义词：indecision, misconception|词性：名词

etymology->
From Old French "jugement", from "jugier" (to judge), from Latin "iudicare".
