overabstemiously: /ˌoʊvəræbˈstiːmiəsli/ | adv. | 过度节制地，过分克制地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive abstinence, especially from food or alcohol.  It implies an extreme level of self-denial, often to an unhealthy or detrimental degree. | He lived overabstemiously, barely eating enough to sustain himself. 他生活过分节制，几乎没吃足够维持生命的食物。|  The monk lived overabstemiously, fasting for days at a time.  这位僧侣生活极其简朴，一次禁食数天。 | 近义词: austerely, sparingly, moderately | 反义词: gluttonously, excessively, immoderately | 词性: 副词

词源:
From "over-" (excessively) + "abstemious" (moderate, especially in eating and drinking) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Abstemious" comes from Latin "abstemius," from "abs-" (from, away from) + "temetum" (strong drink).
