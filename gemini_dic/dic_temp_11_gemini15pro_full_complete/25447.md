bulldog:/ˈbʊldɔːɡ/ (US), /ˈbʊldɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|斗牛犬；顽固的人|A breed of dog with a short, wrinkled face and a strong, muscular body.  Also used to describe a person who is tenacious and determined.|例句：The bulldog fiercely defended its territory. 斗牛犬凶猛地保卫着它的地盘。

例句：He's a real bulldog when it comes to negotiations.  在谈判方面，他是个真正顽固的人。|近义词：tenacious person, determined person|反义词:coward, quitter|词性:noun
