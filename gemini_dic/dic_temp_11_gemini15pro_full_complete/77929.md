genericness:/dʒəˈnɛrɪknəs/ | n. | 普遍性；一般性；通俗性 | The quality or state of being generic; lack of distinguishing features or qualities. | The genericness of the plot made the movie predictable.  这部电影的情节老套，很容易预测。| Synonyms: generality, commonality, universality | Antonyms: specificity, uniqueness, distinctiveness | noun
