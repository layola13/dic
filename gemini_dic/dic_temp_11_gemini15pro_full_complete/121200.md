misdrive: /ˌmɪsˈdraɪv/
verb
错误地驾驶；打偏（球）
To drive (a vehicle) incorrectly or to hit (a ball) off-center, causing it to go in an unintended direction.
He misdrove his car into a ditch. 他把车开进了沟里。
The golfer misdrove the ball into the woods.  高尔夫球手把球打歪到了树林里。
近义词: mishit, misplay
反义词:  drive well, hit accurately
词性: verb


misdrive: /ˌmɪsˈdraɪv/
noun
失误的击球；错误的驾驶
An instance of driving incorrectly or hitting a ball off-center.
His misdrive cost him the game. 他的失误让他输掉了比赛。
The misdrive sent the car careening across the road. 错误的驾驶使汽车在路上横冲直撞。
近义词: mishit, error
反义词: perfect drive, accurate hit
词性: noun
