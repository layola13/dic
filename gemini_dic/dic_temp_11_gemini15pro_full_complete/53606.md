discant:/ˈdɪskænt/ /dɪˈskænt/| n. v.|高音部，童声高音；以高音部演唱|A descant is a high-pitched melody sung above the main tune.|The choir sang a beautiful descant during the hymn.|近义词：treble, soprano|反义词:bass|词性:n.

discant:/ˈdɪskænt/ /dɪˈskænt/| v.|以高音部演唱，详述|To descant is to sing or speak elaborately or at length.|He descanted on the virtues of a vegetarian diet.|近义词：elaborate, expound|反义词:summarize|词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French deschant, from Late Latin discantus, from dis- + cantus ("song").  Ultimately from Latin canere ("to sing").
