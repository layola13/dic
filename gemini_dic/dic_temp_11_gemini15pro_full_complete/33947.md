cirrocumulus:/ˌsɪroʊˈkjuːmjələs/ | n. |卷积云|A type of cloud forming a thin white layer of small ripples. It is a high-altitude cloud, usually formed of ice crystals.|例句：The cirrocumulus clouds looked like fish scales in the sky. 卷积云看起来像天空中的鱼鳞。

例句：The appearance of cirrocumulus clouds sometimes signals an approaching weather front. 卷积云的出现有时预示着天气锋面的到来。|近义词： mackerel sky (非正式)|反义词:  None (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑与低空云如层云stratus对比)|词性: Noun
