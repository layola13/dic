nonelliptic:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɪptɪk/
adjective
非椭圆的
Not having the shape of an ellipse; not elliptical.
The comet follows a nonelliptic path. 这颗彗星沿着一条非椭圆形的轨迹运行。
non-circular, hyperbolic, parabolic
elliptical, oval
