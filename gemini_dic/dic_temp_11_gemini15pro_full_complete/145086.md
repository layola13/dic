overanxious:/ˌoʊvərˈæŋkʃəs/| adj.|过度焦虑的|Excessively anxious; unduly apprehensive.|She was overanxious about the upcoming exam. 她对即将到来的考试过度焦虑。|近义词：apprehensive, worried, nervous, stressed, agitated|反义词：calm, relaxed, tranquil, serene|词性:形容词
Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "anxious" (from Latin anxius "solicitous, uneasy, troubled in mind," from angere "to strangle, torment").
