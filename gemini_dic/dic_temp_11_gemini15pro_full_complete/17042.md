bathetic:/bəˈθɛtɪk/| adj.|陈腐的，老套的，矫揉造作的|Characterized by bathos; excessively sentimental or emotional, to the point of being trite or insincere.|Her bathetic plea for forgiveness left the jury unmoved. 她矫揉造作的求饶请求没有打动陪审团。|近义词：mawkish, sentimental, trite, clichéd, hackneyed|反义词：genuine, sincere, original, fresh|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Greek *bathos* "depth," also "height, sublimity," then "anticlimax, sudden descent from the sublime to the ridiculous."
