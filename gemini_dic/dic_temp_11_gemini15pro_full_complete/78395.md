get at:/ɡet æt/ | phrasal verb | 达到；了解；意指；批评；攻击 | To reach or gain access to something; to discover or understand something; to imply or suggest something; to criticize or attack someone repeatedly. |

例句：
The files are locked away, and I can't get at them.
这些文件被锁起来了，我拿不到。

I'm not sure what she's getting at.
我不确定她指的是什么。


近义词：reach, access, understand, imply, criticize, attack | 反义词：hide, conceal, misunderstand, praise, defend | 词性：动词短语
