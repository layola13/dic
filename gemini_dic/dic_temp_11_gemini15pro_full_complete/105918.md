kiwi:/ˈkiːwiː/ | n. |猕猴桃；几维鸟；新西兰人|A flightless bird native to New Zealand, with hair-like feathers, short strong legs, and a long slender bill. Also, the edible berry of a woody vine native to China, having a fuzzy brown skin and slightly acidic green flesh.  Also, a person from New Zealand. |例句：I had a kiwi for breakfast this morning. 我今天早上吃了一个猕猴桃。

|例句：Kiwis are known for their friendly nature.  新西兰人以其友好的天性而闻名。|近义词：(for the fruit) kiwifruit, Chinese gooseberry; (for the bird) apteryx; (for the person) New Zealander |反义词: None|词性:Noun
