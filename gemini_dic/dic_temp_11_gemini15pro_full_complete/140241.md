offloader:/ˈɔːfˌləʊdər/ | n. |卸货机，卸载器，卸载程序|A person or device that removes a load from something, especially a vehicle or ship.  A program that transfers data from one system to another to reduce the load on the original system.|The dockworkers used a specialized offloader to remove the cargo from the ship. 码头工人使用专门的卸货机从船上卸下货物。

The system administrator used an offloader program to transfer some of the data to a backup server. 系统管理员使用卸载程序将一些数据传输到备份服务器。|Synonyms: unloader, discharger, remover|Antonyms: loader, charger|Noun


offload:/ˌɔːfˈləʊd/ | v. |卸载，卸下，减轻…的负担|To remove a load from something, especially a vehicle or ship. To transfer work or responsibility to someone else.|The truck driver offloaded the pallets of goods at the warehouse. 卡车司机在仓库卸下了货盘。

She offloaded some of her work to her assistant to meet the deadline. 为了赶上截止日期，她把一些工作交给了她的助手。|Synonyms: unload, discharge, transfer, delegate|Antonyms: load, charge|Verb
