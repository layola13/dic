biohazards:/ˈbaɪoʊˌhæzərdz/
noun
生物危害
A biohazard is a biological substance that poses a threat to the health of living organisms, primarily humans. This can include medical waste, samples of microorganisms, viruses or toxins.
The hospital has strict procedures for handling biohazards.  这家医院对处理生物危害有严格的程序。
Biohazard warning signs were posted around the contaminated area.  生物危害警告标志被张贴在污染区域周围。
synonyms: biological hazards, biorisks, contagion, infection
antonyms: safety, security
