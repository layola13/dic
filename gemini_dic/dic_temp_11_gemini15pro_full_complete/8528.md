anticensoriousness:/ˌæntiˌsɛnsɔːriəsnɪs/
|n.| 不爱挑剔；宽容 |The anticensoriousness of the teacher created a relaxed classroom atmosphere.  老师不爱挑剔的性格营造了轻松的课堂氛围。|  A spirit of anticensoriousness prevailed in the group.  这个团体中弥漫着宽容的精神。| tolerance, forbearance, leniency | censoriousness, criticalness, fault-finding | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + censorious (fault-finding) + -ness (forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Censorious ultimately derives from Latin *censor*, a Roman official who conducted the census and supervised public morals.
