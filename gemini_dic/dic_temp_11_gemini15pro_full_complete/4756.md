alfileria:/ˌælfɪˈlɪəriə/ | n. | 针状雀麦，红针茅 | A common annual grass native to California, with reddish stems and needle-like leaves.  It is used as forage for livestock and is also valued for erosion control. | The hills were covered with alfileria after the winter rains. 冬雨过后，山上覆盖着针状雀麦。| Synonyms: filaree, red-stem filaree, pin grass | Antonyms: N/A | noun

alfileria:/ˌælfɪˈlɪəriə/ | n. |  红针茅，一种牧草| A type of grass used as fodder, particularly in arid regions. |  Alfileria provides valuable grazing for cattle in dry climates. 红针茅为干燥气候下的牛提供宝贵的牧草。| Synonyms: redstem filaree, pinclover | Antonyms: N/A | noun
