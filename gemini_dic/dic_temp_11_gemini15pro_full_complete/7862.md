antebellum:/ˌæntiˈbɛləm/| adj.|战前的，尤指美国南北战争前的|Existing before a war, especially the American Civil War. |The antebellum South was known for its large plantations and reliance on slave labor.  美国南北战争前的南方以其大型种植园和对奴隶劳动的依赖而闻名。|例句：The antebellum period in the United States was marked by rapid economic growth. 美国的战前时期以经济快速增长为标志。

例句：Antebellum architecture is characterized by large columns and grand entrances. 战前建筑的特点是大柱子和宏伟的入口。|近义词：prewar|反义词：postwar|词性：adjective
