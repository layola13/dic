ignoble:/ɪɡˈnoʊbəl/| adj.|卑鄙的，不光彩的|Not honorable in character or purpose; base.|He committed an ignoble act of betrayal. 他做出了卑鄙的背叛行为。|Synonyms: base, mean, low, despicable, dishonorable, shameful, unworthy, sordid|Antonyms: noble, honorable, worthy, virtuous|adjective

ignoble:/ɪnˈnəʊb(ə)l/| adj.|卑鄙的，不光彩的|Not honorable in character or purpose; of humble origin or social status.|She felt it was ignoble to betray her friend's trust for personal gain. 她觉得为了个人利益背叛朋友的信任是卑鄙的。|Synonyms: base, mean, low, despicable, dishonorable, shameful, unworthy, sordid|Antonyms: noble, honorable, worthy, virtuous|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ignobel*, from Latin *ignobilis* "not noble," from *in-* "not" + *nobilis* "noble, famous, well-known"
