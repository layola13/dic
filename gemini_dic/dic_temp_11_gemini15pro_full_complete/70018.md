fetishly:/ˈfetiʃli/ | adv. | 恋物地；迷恋地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive or irrational devotion to a particular object or activity, like a fetish.  Derived from the noun "fetish." | She gazed fetishly at the antique doll. 她迷恋地注视着那个古董娃娃。 | 近义词: obsessively, fixatedly | 反义词: indifferently, apathetically | 词性:副词

词源:
From French *fétiche*, from Portuguese *feitiço* "artificial charm, amulet, sorcery," originally "artificial," from Latin *facticius* "artificial," from *facere* "to make, do."
