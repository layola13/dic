enure:/ɪˈnjʊər/ (US) /ɪˈnjʊə(r)/ (UK)| v. |生效；使习惯；赋予权利| To take effect; to accustom or habituate; to give a right to someone by law or custom. | The new law will enure to the benefit of the citizens. 这项新法律将生效，使公民受益。

The contract enures to the benefit of both parties. 这份合同对双方都有效。| 近义词: benefit, serve, avail, profit | 反义词: harm, injure |词性:动词
