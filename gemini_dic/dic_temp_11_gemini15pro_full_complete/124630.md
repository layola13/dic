mouth:/maʊθ/ (US) /maʊθ/ (UK)| n. v. |嘴，口;  开口，河口; 说，发出（声音）|The opening in the face used for eating, speaking, and breathing. Also, the opening of a river, cave, or container. As a verb, it means to form words silently with the lips or to declaim pompously.|

例句：
She opened her mouth to speak. 她张开嘴说话。
The river flows into the sea at its mouth. 这条河流在河口流入大海。

近义词：opening, orifice, aperture (n.); utter, pronounce, articulate (v.)|
反义词: none (no single direct antonym)|
词性: noun, verb
