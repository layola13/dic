gang: /ɡæŋ/ | n. |帮派，团伙，一群人|A group of people who associate together for social or criminal purposes. |例句：The gang was known for its violent crimes.  这个帮派以暴力犯罪而闻名。

例句：A gang of teenagers were hanging out at the mall. 一群青少年在商场闲逛。|近义词：crew, band, group, clique, mob|反义词:individual, loner|词性:noun


gang: /ɡæŋ/ | v. | 组成一伙；合伙攻击|To form a group or to attack someone as a group.|例句：The workers ganged together to demand better pay. 工人们联合起来要求更高的工资。

例句：They ganged up on him and stole his wallet. 他们合伙攻击他并偷走了他的钱包。|近义词：unite, combine, ally,  attack, assault|反义词:separate, disperse, defend|词性:verb



ETYMOLOGY->

From Old Norse *gangr, meaning "a going, a walk, a way," related to Old English gang "a going, a course, a journey."  The sense of "a group of people going together" developed later.
