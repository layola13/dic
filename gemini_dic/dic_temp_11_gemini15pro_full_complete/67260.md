expurgator:/ˈekspərˌɡeɪtər/ | n. |  净化者；修订者；删节者 |  A person who expurgates something, especially a book or other written material, by removing parts considered offensive or unsuitable. | The expurgator removed all references to sex and violence from the novel.  净化者从小说中删除了所有关于性和暴力的内容。 | 近义词: censor, editor, reviser | 反义词:  none | noun

expurgate: /ˈekspərˌɡeɪt/ | v. | 删除（书籍、电影等中被认为是冒犯性的部分）；净化 | To remove parts of a book, film, etc. that are considered to be offensive or unsuitable. |  They expurgated the book before publishing it in schools. 他们在学校出版这本书之前删除了其中的一些内容。 | 近义词: censor, bowdlerize, redact | 反义词: include, insert | verb
