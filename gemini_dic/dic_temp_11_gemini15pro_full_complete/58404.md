dubitable:/ˈdjuːbɪtəbl/ /ˈduːbɪtəbl/ | adj. | 可疑的；不确定的 | Open to doubt or question; uncertain. |  The witness's statement was considered dubitable due to his previous dishonesty.  证人由于先前的不诚实，他的陈述被认为是可疑的。

The dubitable nature of the evidence made it difficult for the jury to reach a verdict.  证据的可疑性质使陪审团难以作出裁决。 | 近义词：doubtful, questionable, uncertain, suspect | 反义词：indubitable, unquestionable, certain, sure | 词性: adjective

dubious:/ˈdjuːbiəs/ /ˈduːbiəs/ | adj. | 半信半疑的，可疑的；不确定的，靠不住的 | Hesitant or doubtful; not to be relied upon; suspect.  | I was dubious about his promises. 我对他的承诺持怀疑态度。

The meat looked a bit dubious, so I didn't eat it. 这肉看起来有点可疑，所以我没吃。 | 近义词：doubtful, questionable, uncertain, suspect | 反义词：certain, sure, reliable | 词性: adjective
