fatigue:/fəˈtiːɡ/ (US), /fəˈtiːɡ/ (UK)| n. v.|疲劳，疲乏；使疲劳|Extreme fatigue can lead to serious health problems. 过度疲劳会导致严重的健康问题。|例句：The journey fatigued the travelers. 旅途使旅行者疲惫不堪。|近义词：tiredness, exhaustion, weariness|反义词:energy, vigor|n.
fatigue:/fəˈtiːɡ/ (US), /fəˈtiːɡ/ (UK)| v.|使疲劳，使疲乏|The long hours fatigued him. 长时间的工作使他疲惫不堪。|例句：She was fatigued by the constant demands of her job. 她被工作中不断的需求弄得筋疲力尽。|近义词：tire, exhaust, wear out|反义词:energize, invigorate|v.
