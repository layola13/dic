co-worker:/ˈkoʊˌwɜːrkər/ (US) /ˈkəʊˌwɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |同事，合作者|A person who works with another person, especially in the same office or department.|例句：My co-worker helped me finish the project. 我的同事帮我完成了这个项目。

例句：I went to lunch with my co-workers. 我和我的同事们一起去吃午饭了。


近义词：colleague, associate, peer|反义词: boss, superior, manager |词性: noun
