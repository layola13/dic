meteorite:/ˈmiːtiəraɪt/| n. |陨石|A stony or metallic mass of matter that has fallen to the earth from outer space; a fallen meteoroid.|例句：The scientists studied the meteorite to learn more about the early solar system. 科学家们研究陨石以了解更多关于早期太阳系的信息。

例句：The impact of the meteorite created a large crater. 陨石的撞击形成了一个大陨石坑。|近义词：meteoroid, bolide|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *meteōros* "high in the air," from *meta* "between, with, after" + *aeirein* "to raise, lift."
