muzzler:/ˈmʌzlər/| n.|口罩；枪口套；止吠器|A device, typically made of straps or wire, used to prevent an animal, especially a dog, from biting or barking.  Something that prevents or restricts speech or expression.|The dog wore a muzzler to prevent it from biting.  这只狗戴着口罩以防它咬人。|Synonyms: gag, silencer, restraint|Antonyms: amplifier, megaphone|noun


muzzle:/ˈmʌzl/| n. vt.|动物的口鼻部；枪口；止吠器；用口鼻摩擦；强制沉默|The projecting part of the face of an animal, including the nose and mouth; the open end of the barrel of a firearm;  to put a muzzle on (an animal); to prevent (a person or group) from expressing their opinions freely.|The dog licked its muzzle. 狗舔了舔它的鼻子。  The government tried to muzzle the press. 政府试图压制新闻界。|Synonyms: snout, nose,  silence, suppress|Antonyms:  unmuzzle, free, liberate|noun, verb
