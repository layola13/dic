It implies a weaving or interspersing of mentions rather than a single, isolated reference. |  He kept intermentioning his new car during the meeting, which was quite distracting. 他在会议期间不断穿插提及他的新车，这很让人分心。|  She skillfully intermentioning details about the suspect throughout her testimony.  她在证词中巧妙地穿插提到了嫌疑人的细节。| synonyms: interspersed, interwoven, interspersing | antonyms: omitting, excluding | adjective
