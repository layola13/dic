agree:/əˈɡriː/ | vi. vt. | 同意，赞成；一致；适合 | To have the same opinion or feeling as someone else; to say that you will do something that someone else wants or suggests |  I agree with you. 我同意你的看法。 | synonyms: concur, consent, assent | antonyms: disagree, dissent, object | verb

agree:/əˈɡriː/ | vi. vt. | (气候，食物) 相宜，适宜| To be suitable or healthy for someone | The climate here agrees with me. 这里的气候很适合我。 | synonyms: suit, be good for | antonyms: disagree with, be bad for | verb
