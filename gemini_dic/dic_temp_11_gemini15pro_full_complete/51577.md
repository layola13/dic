deutoplastic:/ˌdjuːtəˈplæstɪk/
adjective
继发性可塑的，后生可塑的
Relating to or influenced by environmental factors rather than hereditary ones; acquired.  Pertaining to adaptability in response to environmental influences.
The researchers studied the deutoplastic changes in the brain caused by prolonged exposure to stress. 研究人员研究了长期暴露于压力下导致的大脑继发性可塑性变化。

近义词: acquired, adaptive, environmental, phenotypic
反义词: hereditary, congenital, genetic, genotypic
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *deúteros* "second" + *plastikós* "able to be molded."
