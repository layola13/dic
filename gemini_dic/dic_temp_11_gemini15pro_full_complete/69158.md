fawned:/fɔːnd/| v. | 奉承；讨好；巴结 | To exhibit affection or attempt to please, like a dog wagging its tail.  To seek favor or attention by flattery and obsequious behavior. | The courtiers fawned over the new king, hoping to gain his favor. 朝臣们对新国王阿谀奉承，希望获得他的青睐。

The dog fawned on its owner, wagging its tail excitedly.  狗对它的主人摇着尾巴表示亲昵。 | 近义词: flatter, grovel, kowtow, toady | 反义词: disdain, scorn, ignore |词性:动词
