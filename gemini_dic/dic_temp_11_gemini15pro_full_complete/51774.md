devotionality:/ˌdiːvəʊʃəˈnælɪti/ | n. | 虔诚，献身；灵修 |  The quality of being devoted; piety; religious worship or observance. |  She impressed everyone with her deep devotionality to her faith. 她对信仰的虔诚给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。| synonyms: piety, devoutness, religiousness, godliness | antonyms: impiety, irreverence, ungodliness | noun


devotionalities:/ˌdiːvəʊʃəˈnælɪtiz/ | n. | 灵修材料（常用复数）| Religious materials designed for personal reflection and prayer, such as books, pamphlets, or online resources. | She reads daily devotionalities to strengthen her faith. 她每天阅读灵修材料来增强她的信仰。 | synonyms: devotional readings, meditations, reflections | antonyms: secular literature | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *devociounalite*, from Old French *devotionalité*, from Late Latin *devotionalitas*, from Latin *devotionalis* ("devoted"), from *devotio* ("dedication, devotion"), from *devovere* ("to vow, dedicate"), from *de-* ("down, from") + *vovere* ("to vow").
