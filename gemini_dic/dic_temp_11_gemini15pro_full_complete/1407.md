accustomable:/əˈkʌstəməbl/
adjective
可习惯的；易适应的
Capable of being accustomed; easily habituated.
She is accustomable to the hot weather. 她很容易适应炎热的天气。
acclimatable, adaptable, used to
unaccustomed, inadaptable
