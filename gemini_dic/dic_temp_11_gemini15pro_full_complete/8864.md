antifeudalism:/ˌæntiˈfjuːdəlɪzəm/
n.
反封建主义
Opposition to or the fight against the feudal system.
The rise of antifeudalism led to significant social and political changes. 反封建主义的兴起导致了重大的社会和政治变革。
The French Revolution was a powerful expression of antifeudalism. 法国大革命是反封建主义的有力表现。
近义词:antifeudal
反义词:feudalism, feudal
词性:名词
