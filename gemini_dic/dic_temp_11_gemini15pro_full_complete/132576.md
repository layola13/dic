nondieting's:/ˌnɑːnˈdaɪətɪŋz/ |n.| 非节食的（状态、行为或原则）|  Relating to or characteristic of an approach that rejects dieting.  It usually refers to the principles or practices of intuitive eating or a healthy relationship with food without focusing on restrictive diets.|  Nondieting's popularity is growing as people realize the harm of restrictive diets.  非节食的理念越来越受欢迎，因为人们意识到了限制性饮食的危害。 | synonyms:  intuitive eating, mindful eating, healthy eating habits | antonyms: dieting, restrictive eating, calorie counting | noun
