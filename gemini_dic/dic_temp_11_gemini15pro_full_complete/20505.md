trivial:/ˈtrɪviəl/| adj.|琐碎的，不重要的|Of little value or importance.  Relating to matters of little value or importance.|例句：It's a trivial matter, don't worry about it. 这只是一件小事，别担心。|近义词：unimportant, insignificant, minor, petty|反义词:important, significant, crucial, essential|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *trivialis*, "belonging to the crossroads," from *trivium* "a place where three roads meet," from *tri-* "three" + *via* "road."  Originally referred to something commonplace and found everywhere (like at a crossroads), hence losing its significance.
