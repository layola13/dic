legacy:/ˈlɛɡəsi/| n.|遗产，遗赠，遗留问题|Something (such as property or money) that is received from someone who has died; something that happened in the past or that comes from someone in the past|He left his children a legacy of love and respect. 他给孩子们留下了爱与尊重的遗产。|Synonyms: inheritance, bequest, heritage|Antonyms: debt, burden|noun

legacy:/ˈlɛɡəsi/| adj.|(computing) denoting software or hardware that has been superseded but is difficult to replace because of its wide use|outdated but still in use|The company is still using legacy software. 该公司仍在使用旧版软件。|Synonyms: outdated, obsolete|Antonyms: modern, current|adjective
