bondman:/ˈbɒndmən/ (UK) /ˈbɑːndmən/ (US)| n.|奴隶，农奴，契约佣工|A man who is a slave or serf, or who is bound to service without wages.|例句：In feudal times, the bondman was tied to the land. 在封建时代，农奴被束缚在土地上。

|例句：The bondman worked tirelessly for his lord. 这个农奴为他领主不知疲倦地工作。|近义词：slave, serf, villein|反义词：freeman, lord|词性：名词

bondman:/ˈbɒndmən/ (UK) /ˈbɑːndmən/ (US)| n.|保人，担保人|A person who acts as surety for another.|例句：He acted as bondman for his friend's loan. 他作为朋友贷款的担保人。

|例句：The bondman was liable for the debt if the borrower defaulted. 如果借款人违约，担保人将承担债务。|近义词：surety, guarantor|反义词：debtor, borrower|词性：名词
