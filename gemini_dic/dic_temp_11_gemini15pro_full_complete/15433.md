bactrian:/ˈbæktriən/
adjective|双峰骆驼的；(尤指)中亚细亚的|Relating to Bactria, an ancient region in Central Asia. Also, relating to the two-humped camel. |The Bactrian camel is well adapted to the harsh desert climate. 双峰骆驼非常适应恶劣的沙漠气候。|近义词: two-humped|反义词: dromedary (referring to camels)|词性:adjective

bactrian:/ˈbæktriən/
noun|双峰驼|A two-humped camel. |Bactrians are known for their ability to survive in extreme temperatures. 双峰驼以其在极端温度下生存的能力而闻名。|近义词: two-humped camel|反义词: dromedary|词性:noun
