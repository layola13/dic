disavow:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊ/| vt.|否认，否定，拒绝承认|to deny responsibility for or connection with; to refuse to acknowledge or accept|The government disavowed any involvement in the scandal. 政府否认与此丑闻有任何牵连。|近义词：deny, repudiate, reject, renounce|反义词：acknowledge, admit, accept, avow|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *disavowen*, from Old French *desavouer*, from *des-* (expressing negation) + *avouer* (“to avow, confess”), from Latin *advocare* (“to call to witness, summon”).
