incrustation:/ɪnˌkrʌˈsteɪʃən/ | n. |结壳； incrustations（复数）|A hard coating or deposit of something, such as minerals or scale. |The ancient vase was covered in incrustations of salt from years spent submerged in the ocean.  这件古老的花瓶上覆盖着多年浸泡在海水中形成的盐壳。|近义词: deposit, coating, crust, scale, accumulation |反义词: erosion, dissolving |词性: 名词

incrustation:/ɪnˌkrʌˈsteɪʃən/ | n. |装饰；镶嵌 |Ornamentation or decoration, especially with jewels or precious metals. |The golden crown was covered in elaborate incrustations of diamonds and rubies.  金色的王冠上镶嵌着精美的钻石和红宝石。|近义词: adornment, embellishment, decoration |反义词: plainness, simplicity |词性: 名词
