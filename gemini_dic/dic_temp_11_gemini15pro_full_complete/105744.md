kippering:/ˈkɪpərɪŋ/
n.
熏制鲱鱼, 熏鱼, 熏制过程
the process of preserving fish, especially herring, by salting and smoking them.
例句：The kippering process gives the fish a unique smoky flavour.  熏制过程赋予鱼独特的烟熏味。

例句：The smell of kippering filled the air. 空气中弥漫着熏鱼的味道。
近义词：smoking, curing
反义词：(None -  no direct opposite for a preservation process)
词性: noun
