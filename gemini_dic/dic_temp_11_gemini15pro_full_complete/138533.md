nows: /naʊz/
pronoun| nows (nonstandard) |现在，目前| Nows is a nonstandard or dialectal form of "now," primarily used in informal speech. It often emphasizes the present moment, or conveys a sense of immediacy.  It's generally considered grammatically incorrect in formal writing. | "He's working on it right nows." 他现在正在做这件事。 | 近义词: now, currently, presently | 反义词: then, later | 词性: pronoun (nonstandard, often used as an adverb)

注意事项:
"nows" is nonstandard and should be avoided in formal contexts. While some dictionaries may list it as a dialectal variant, it's generally considered informal or even incorrect.  Using "now" is always the safer and more widely accepted option.
