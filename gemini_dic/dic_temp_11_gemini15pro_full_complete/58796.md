duodecahedron:/ˌdʊəˌdekəˈhiːdrən/ |n.|十二面体|A solid figure with twelve plane faces, especially one whose faces are regular pentagons. |A dodecahedron is a complex shape to visualize.  一个十二面体是一个难以想象的复杂形状。| The ancient Greeks studied the properties of the dodecahedron. 古希腊人研究了十二面体的性质。|polyhedron, solid|sphere |noun
