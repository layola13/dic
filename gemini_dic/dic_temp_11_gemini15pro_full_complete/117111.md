mealworm:/ˈmiːlwɜːrm/ | n. | 黄粉虫 | A small, yellowish-brown beetle larva, often used as food for pets such as reptiles and birds, and increasingly for human consumption. |  Mealworms are a good source of protein for reptiles. 黄粉虫是爬行动物良好的蛋白质来源。

Mealworms are now being considered as a sustainable food source for humans. 黄粉虫现在被认为是人类可持续的食物来源。 | 近义词: larva, grub | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider predator like bird or reptile) | 词性: noun
