might:/maɪt/ |aux. v. |可能，也许；可以；应该| expressing possibility, permission, or suggestion|例句：He might be at home. 他可能在家。 |近义词：may, could, perhaps, possibly |反义词：cannot, must not|词性:助动词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He might come tomorrow. 他明天可能会来。
She might know the answer. 她可能知道答案。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He might be working late tonight.  他今晚可能工作到很晚。
They might be traveling right now. 他们现在可能正在旅行。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He might have finished the project. 他可能已经完成了这个项目。
She might have gone to the store. 她可能已经去商店了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He might have been working on this all day. 他可能一整天都在忙这个。
They might have been traveling for weeks. 他们可能已经旅行了好几个星期了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He might have gone to the library yesterday. 他昨天可能去了图书馆。
She might have seen the movie last week. 她上周可能看了那部电影。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He might have been sleeping when I called. 我打电话的时候他可能正在睡觉。
She might have been cooking dinner at that time. 她当时可能正在做晚饭。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He might have already left before I arrived. 他可能在我到达之前就已经离开了。
She might have finished the book before the weekend. 她可能在周末之前就已经读完了那本书。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He might have been working on that project for months before he quit. 他辞职前可能已经做了几个月的那个项目。
She might have been studying English for years before she moved to the US.  她搬到美国之前可能已经学习了多年的英语。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He might go to the party next weekend. 他下周末可能会去参加聚会。
She might visit her family next month. 她下个月可能会去看望她的家人。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
He might be working on the project next week. 他下周可能正在做这个项目。
She might be traveling in Europe next summer. 她明年夏天可能正在欧洲旅行。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
He might have finished the project by next year. 他到明年可能已经完成了这个项目。
She might have graduated by then. 她到那时可能已经毕业了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, he might have been working here for five years. 到明年，他可能已经在这里工作五年了。
By the time she finishes, she might have been studying for a decade. 到她完成的时候，她可能已经学习了十年。


虚拟语气:
I might go to the party if I have time. 如果我有时间，我可能会去参加聚会。
She might have helped if you had asked. 如果你问了，她可能会帮忙。


被动语态 (少用):
The problem might be solved soon. 这个问题可能很快就会得到解决。
The decision might have been made already.  这个决定可能已经做出了。

疑问句:
Might he be at home? 他可能在家吗？
Might she know the answer? 她可能知道答案吗？

否定句:
He might not come tomorrow. 他明天可能不会来。
She might not know the answer. 她可能不知道答案。
