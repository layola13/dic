arranged: /əˈreɪndʒd/ | adj. | 安排好的，整理好的 |  Put in a certain order; planned or organized. | The flowers were beautifully arranged.  这些花被安排得很漂亮。| 近义词: organized, planned, ordered, sorted | 反义词: disorganized, messy, chaotic, jumbled | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From the French "arranger," from "à rang," meaning "in a row" or "in order." Ultimately from Frankish "*hring," meaning "circle" or "ring."
