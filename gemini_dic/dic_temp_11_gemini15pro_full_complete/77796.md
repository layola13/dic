generalizable:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzəbl/ /ˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzəbl/| adj.|可概括的，可推广的|Able to be made more general; applicable to a wider range of situations or circumstances.|The results of this study are generalizable to other populations. 这项研究的结果可以推广到其他人群。|近义词：applicable, universal, transferable|反义词：specific, particular, unique|词性:形容词

generalizable:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzəbl/ /ˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzəbl/| adj.|普遍适用的|Widely applicable; relevant or valid in a variety of contexts.|The findings are not generalizable to the entire country. 这些发现并不适用于整个国家。|近义词：broad, widespread, general|反义词：limited, restricted, narrow|词性:形容词
