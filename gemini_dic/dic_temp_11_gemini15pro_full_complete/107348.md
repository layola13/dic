lamebrained:/ˌleɪmˈbreɪnd/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻的|Stupid or foolish; lacking intelligence or good judgment.|例句：That lamebrained idea is never going to work. 那个愚蠢的想法永远不会奏效。

例句：He made a lamebrained decision to quit his job without another lined up. 他做了一个愚蠢的决定，在没有找到下一份工作的情况下就辞掉了现在的工作。|近义词：stupid, foolish, silly, idiotic, asinine, witless|反义词：intelligent, smart, bright, clever, wise|词性：形容词
