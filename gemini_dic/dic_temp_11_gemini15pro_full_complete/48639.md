defending:/dɪˈfɛndɪŋ/| v. adj.| 防守，保卫；辩护；卫冕| Present participle of "defend."  The act of protecting something or someone from attack or criticism. | The lawyer is defending his client in court.  律师正在法庭上为他的客户辩护。| 近义词：protecting, safeguarding, guarding, shielding, advocating for| 反义词:attacking, assaulting, prosecuting| 词性:动词现在分词，形容词

defending:/dɪˈfɛndɪŋ/| adj.| 防御的，保卫的|  An adjective describing something that is designed for or engaged in defense. | The defending champion played well. 卫冕冠军表现出色。| 近义词：protective, safeguarding| 反义词:offensive, attacking| 词性:形容词
