bullfist:/ˈbʊlfɪst/ | n. | 拳頭；暴力；恐嚇 | A fist, especially a large, strong one, often associated with aggression or intimidation. | He threatened them with his bullfist. 他用拳头威胁他们。| 近义词: fist, clenched fist | 反义词: open hand | 词性: 名词

词源:  Compound of "bull" (referring to strength and size, as in "bull-necked") and "fist."
