bilaterals:/ˌbaɪˈlætərəlz/
n.
双边关系，双边协议
agreements or discussions held between two groups (usually countries)
例句：The two countries signed several bilaterals. 这两个国家签署了几项双边协议。

例句：The president engaged in a series of bilaterals with Asian leaders. 总统与亚洲领导人进行了一系列双边会谈。

近义词：agreements, pacts, treaties
反义词：multilateral
