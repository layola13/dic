mutations:/mjuːˈteɪʃənz/| n.|突变，变异|A change that occurs in our DNA sequence, either due to mistakes when the DNA is copied or as the result of environmental factors such as UV light and cigarette smoke.|Exposure to radiation can cause mutations in DNA. 暴露于辐射会导致DNA突变。|synonyms: alterations, variations, modifications|antonyms: invariability, stability|noun
