amend:/əˈmend/ | v. | 修改，修订，改进 | to make changes to something, especially a law or a document, in order to improve it | The government has promised to amend the law. 政府已承诺修订法律。|  Congress voted to amend the Constitution. 国会投票修改宪法。 | synonyms: revise, alter, modify, correct, improve, emend | antonyms: worsen, damage, impair | verb
