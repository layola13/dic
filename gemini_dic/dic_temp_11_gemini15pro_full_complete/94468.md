imponderable:/ɪmˈpɒndərəbl/ (British) /ɪmˈpɑːndərəbl/ (American)| adj. n.|难以估量的，无法衡量的；未知因素|Something that is imponderable is impossible to estimate or assess with any accuracy.|The imponderables of the situation made it difficult to plan ahead.  这个情况的不可估量因素使得难以提前计划。|The future is full of imponderables. 未来充满了未知数。|Synonyms:  unforeseeable, unpredictable, incalculable, uncertain, unknown|Antonyms:  predictable, calculable, certain, known|adjective, noun
