depauperize:/dɪˈpɔːpəraɪz/
verb
使贫穷；使枯竭；使 impoverish
To make poor; to deprive of natural richness; to exhaust the resources or strength of.
Excessive logging has depauperized the soil. 过度伐木导致土壤贫瘠。
The region was depauperized by the war.  这个地区因战争而变得贫困。
Synonyms: impoverish, deplete, exhaust, drain
Antonyms: enrich, enhance, augment
