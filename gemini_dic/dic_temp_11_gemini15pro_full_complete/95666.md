incomputable:/ˌɪnkəmˈpjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可计算的 | Not able to be calculated or computed, especially by a computer.  | Example: Some mathematical problems are incomputable, meaning no algorithm can solve them in a finite amount of time.  一些数学问题是不可计算的，这意味着没有任何算法可以在有限的时间内解决它们。

Example: The complexity of the universe makes some phenomena practically incomputable. 宇宙的复杂性使得某些现象实际上是不可计算的。 | 近义词: incalculable, unsolvable, noncomputable | 反义词: calculable, computable, solvable | 词性: adjective
