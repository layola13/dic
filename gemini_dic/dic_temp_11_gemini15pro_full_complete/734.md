absinthial:/ˌæbsɪnˈθiəl/| adj.|苦艾酒的，像苦艾酒的|Relating to or resembling absinthe, especially in taste or effect; characterized by a bitter, licorice-like flavor.  Evoking the intoxicating or hallucinatory effects traditionally associated with absinthe.|The cocktail had an absinthial bitterness. 这款鸡尾酒有一种苦艾酒般的苦味。|Synonyms: bitter, licorice-flavored, intoxicating|Antonyms: sweet, bland|adjective
