oversaturation:/ˌoʊvərˌsætʃəˈreɪʃən/ |n.| 过饱和，过度饱和|The oversaturation of the market with cheap goods led to a drop in prices.  廉价商品市场过度饱和导致价格下跌。|例句：The oversaturation of the market led to a decline in profits. 市场过度饱和导致利润下降。|近义词：glut, surplus, excess|反义词：undersupply, scarcity, shortage|词性：名词

oversaturated:/ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ |adj.| 过饱和的，过度饱和的|The market is oversaturated with similar products. 市场充斥着同类产品。|例句：The oversaturated job market makes it difficult for new graduates to find work.  过度饱和的就业市场使得新毕业生找工作很困难。|近义词：glutted, saturated, overflowing|反义词：undersupplied, scarce|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + saturation.  "Saturation" comes from Late Latin "saturatio," from Latin "saturare" meaning "to fill full, sate."
