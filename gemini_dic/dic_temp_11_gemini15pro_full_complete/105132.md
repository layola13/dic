kestrel:/ˈkestrəl/ | n. |红隼|A small falcon with a reddish-brown back and tail.  A bird of prey of the falcon family.|例句：A kestrel hovered over the field, searching for mice.  一只红隼在田野上空盘旋，寻找老鼠。|例句：The kestrel plummeted from the sky and snatched a vole. 红隼从天而降，抓住了一只田鼠。|近义词：falcon, hawk|反义词:prey (though not a direct opposite, it represents the other side of the predator-prey relationship)|词性:noun
