lichenic:/laɪˈkɛnɪk/
adjective
地衣的；似地衣的
Relating to, resembling, or characteristic of lichens.
Lichens are a complex life form that is a symbiotic partnership of two separate organisms, a fungus and an alga.
The lichenic growth on the rocks added to the stark beauty of the landscape. 岩石上的地衣生长为荒凉的景观增添了美感。
The biologist specialized in lichenic flora of the high alpine regions.  这位生物学家专门研究高山地区的地衣植物群。
近义词: mossy (长满苔藓的), fungal (真菌的)
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, consider phrases like "lichen-free" or describing the opposite surface texture/appearance)
词性: adjective
