hypercapnia:/ˌhaɪpərˈkæpniə/| n.|高碳酸血症|A condition of abnormally high levels of carbon dioxide in the blood.|The patient's hypercapnia was treated with mechanical ventilation. 病人的高碳酸血症用机械通气治疗。|Untreated hypercapnia can lead to serious complications. 未经治疗的高碳酸血症会导致严重的并发症。|近义词:hypercarbia|反义词:hypocapnia|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (Greek: 'over, above') + capn- (Greek: 'smoke, carbon dioxide') + -ic (suffix forming adjectives).  The term literally means "excessive carbon dioxide."
