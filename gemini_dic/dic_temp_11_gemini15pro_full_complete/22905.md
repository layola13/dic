bother:/ˈbɒðə(r)/ (UK), /ˈbɑːðər/ (US)| v.  n. adj.|打扰，烦扰；麻烦；操心；费心|To cause somebody trouble or slight worry, or to make the effort to do something.|例句：I don't want to bother you with my problems. 我不想用我的问题来打扰你。|近义词：disturb, annoy, trouble, pester, inconvenience|反义词：soothe, comfort, please|词性:动词

bother:/ˈbɒðə(r)/ (UK), /ˈbɑːðər/ (US)| n.|麻烦；不便；令人讨厌的人或事|Something that causes trouble or worry.|例句：It's a bother having to go to the bank.  去银行真麻烦。|近义词：trouble, nuisance, inconvenience, hassle|反义词：convenience, ease|词性:名词

bother:/ˈbɒðə(r)/ (UK), /ˈbɑːðər/ (US)| adj.|（用于否定句或疑问句）值得的，重要的|Worth the time, money, or effort.|例句：Don't bother getting dressed up - it's just a family meal. 不用特意打扮——只是家常便饭。|近义词：important, significant, worthwhile|反义词：unimportant, insignificant, trivial|词性:形容词
