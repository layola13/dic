anosmia:/ænˈɒzmiə/ /ɑːnˈɑːzmiə/
n.
失嗅症;嗅觉丧失
Loss of the sense of smell, either total or partial.
She suffered from anosmia after contracting COVID-19. 她感染新冠后患上了失嗅症。
The anosmia made it impossible for him to enjoy his favorite foods. 失嗅症让他无法享受他最喜欢的食物。

近义词:
hyposmia (嗅觉减退)
olfactory dysfunction (嗅觉功能障碍)

反义词:
normosmia (正常嗅觉)
hyperosmia (嗅觉过敏)

词性:
noun (名词)

词源:
From Ancient Greek ἀν- (an-, “without”) + ὀσμή (osmḗ, “smell”).
