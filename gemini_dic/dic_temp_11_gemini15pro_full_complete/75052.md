frightenable:/ˈfraɪtənəbl/| adj.|易受惊的，胆怯的|Able to be frightened; easily frightened.|The small child was frightenable and hid behind his mother.  这个小孩很容易受惊吓，躲在他妈妈身后。|例句：The frightenable horse reared up at the sudden noise.  这匹胆小的马因突如其来的噪音而尥蹶子。|近义词：timid, fearful, skittish, nervous, apprehensive|反义词：brave, courageous, fearless, bold|词性:形容词

词源:  From *frighten* + *-able*. *Frighten* comes from the Old English *fryhtan*, meaning "to frighten."  *-able* is a suffix of Latin origin, meaning "capable of being."
