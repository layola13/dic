contractile:/kənˈtræktəl/ | adj. | 可收缩的，能收缩的 | Capable of contracting or shortening. | Smooth muscle is contractile tissue. 平滑肌是可收缩的组织。 | The heart is a contractile organ. 心脏是一个可收缩的器官。 |  synonyms: shrinkable, contractible | antonyms: inextensible, noncontractile | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contractilis, from Latin contractus (past participle of contrahere "to draw together, contract"), from com- "together" + trahere "to draw, pull".
