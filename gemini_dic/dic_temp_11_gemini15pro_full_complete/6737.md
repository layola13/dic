analysing: /ˈænəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 分析，解析 |  The process of examining something methodically and in detail in order to explain and interpret it. |  She is analysing the data from the experiment. 她正在分析实验数据。| synonyms: examining, investigating, studying | antonyms: synthesizing, combining, ignoring | verb

analysing: /ˈænəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 分析的，解析的 | Relating to or involving analysis. |  She has an analysing mind. 她具有分析的头脑。 | synonyms: analytical, critical, investigative | antonyms: synthetic, uncritical, superficial | adjective


Etymology: From the verb "analyse," which comes from the French "analyser," derived from the Greek "analyein," meaning "to loosen up, dissolve."
