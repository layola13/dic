americanization:/əˌmɛrɪkənaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|美国化|The process of making something or someone more American in character or culture.  The influence of American culture and business on other countries.|The americanization of European cuisine is evident in the popularity of fast food chains.| 近义词: westernization, assimilation| 反义词: localization, indigenization|Noun
