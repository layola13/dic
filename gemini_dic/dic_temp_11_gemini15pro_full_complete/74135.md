foundational:/faʊnˈdeɪʃənl/
adjective
基本的；基础的；根本的
Serving as or forming a base; fundamental.
例句：A foundational principle of justice is equality before the law.  法律面前人人平等是正义的一项基本原则。

例句：These courses are foundational to your understanding of the subject. 这些课程是你理解这门学科的基础。

近义词：basic, fundamental, essential, primary, underlying
反义词：superficial, secondary, peripheral
词性: adjective
