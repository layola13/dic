inimitable:/ɪˈnɪmɪtəbl/
adjective
独一无二的; 无法模仿的; 独特的
so good or unusual as to be impossible to copy; unique.
Her inimitable style. 她独特的风格。
近义词:unique, matchless, unparalleled, peerless, exceptional, singular, distinctive, individual, idiosyncratic, characteristic, special, remarkable, extraordinary, outstanding, superb, perfect, consummate, supreme, ideal, exemplary, faultless, flawless, impeccable
反义词:common, ordinary, imitable
词性:形容词
