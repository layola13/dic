animatic:/ˌænɪˈmætɪk/
noun
动画样片，故事板动画
A preliminary version of a film, produced by shooting drawings or photographs of the storyboards and adding a soundtrack.
The director showed the animatic to the producers to get their approval. 导演向制片人展示了动画样片以获得他们的批准。
The animatic helped the team visualize the pacing of the film.  动画样片帮助团队将电影的节奏形象化。
synonyms: storyboard, rough cut, preview
antonyms: final cut, finished film
n.
