limited's:/ˈlɪmɪtɪdz/
noun
有限公司的 (所有格)
Possessive form of "limited," a shortened form of "limited company," indicating ownership or association with a private company with limited liability.  A limited company is a type of business structure where the company's liability is separate from its owners. This means that the owners' personal assets are protected in case the company incurs debts or faces lawsuits.
例句：
ABC Limited's profits have increased this year.  ABC有限公司今年的利润有所增长。

The company filed for bankruptcy, and limited's assets were liquidated. 该公司申请破产，有限公司的资产被清算。

近义词: (In some contexts, depending on the specific meaning) company's, corporation's, firm's
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a possessive form)
词性: Possessive noun (formed from an adjective)
