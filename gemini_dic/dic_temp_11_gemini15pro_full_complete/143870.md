outpointing:/ˌaʊtˈpɔɪntɪŋ/
verb
以点数胜过, 超过
defeating someone by scoring more points than them in a competition, especially one involving judges.
The boxer outpointed his opponent in a close match.  拳击手在一场势均力敌的比赛中以点数战胜了对手。
近义词: outscore, defeat, beat, surpass
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: v.


outpoint:/ˌaʊtˈpɔɪnt/
noun
得分超过
The act of scoring more points than an opponent.
The outpoint secured the victory for the team.  得分超过确保了队伍的胜利。
近义词: advantage, lead, edge
反义词: deficit, disadvantage
词性: n.
