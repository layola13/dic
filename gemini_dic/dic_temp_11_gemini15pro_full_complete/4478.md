albescent:/ælˈbɛsənt/
adjective
变白的，发白的
Becoming white; whitish.
The albescent leaves of the plant shimmered in the moonlight.  这种植物发白的叶子在月光下闪闪发光。
近义词: whitish, paling, bleaching
反义词: darkening, blackening
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *albescentem, present participle of albescere (“to grow white, whiten”), from albus (“white”).
