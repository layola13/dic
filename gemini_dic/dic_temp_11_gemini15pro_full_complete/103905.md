juddered:/ˈdʒʌdərd/ | v. | 震动，颤抖，颠簸 | To shake violently and rapidly; vibrate with large, jerky movements. | The old bus juddered to a halt.  旧公共汽车颠簸着停了下来。| The train juddered as it went over the points. 火车经过岔道时剧烈震动了一下。 | synonyms: shake, vibrate, tremble, shudder, quiver | antonyms: steady, stabilize, smooth | verb
