aggrieve: /əˈɡriːv/ | vt. | 使委屈；使悲痛；侵害 | To cause someone to feel resentment or sorrow, typically as a result of unfair treatment.  |  The decision to close the library aggrieved many residents.  这个关闭图书馆的决定使许多居民感到委屈。 | Synonyms: offend, distress, upset, wrong | Antonyms: please, comfort, soothe | verb

aggrieve: /əˈɡriːv/ | adj. | （古）受委屈的；感到痛苦的 | Feeling resentment or sorrow, typically as a result of unfair treatment. (Archaic) |  He felt deeply aggrieved by the accusations. 他对这些指控感到非常委屈。 | Synonyms: distressed, upset, offended | Antonyms: pleased, contented, satisfied | adjective
