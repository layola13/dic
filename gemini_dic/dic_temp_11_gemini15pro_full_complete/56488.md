dome:/doʊm/| n.|穹顶，圆顶，苍穹|A rounded vault forming the roof of a building or structure, typically a church, or other large building.|The dome of St. Paul's Cathedral is a famous landmark. 圣保罗大教堂的穹顶是一个著名的地标。|Synonyms: cupola, vault|Antonyms: base, foundation|noun

dome:/dəʊm/| v.|加圆屋顶，形成穹顶|To cover with a dome.|They domed the stadium to protect it from the weather. 他们在体育场上加了个圆顶以防风雨。|Synonyms: cover, cap|Antonyms: uncover, expose|verb
