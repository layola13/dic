Example: Atazavir can be effective in reducing hallucinations and delusions. 阿塔齐尔可以有效减少幻觉和妄想。| 近义词: antipsychotic, neuroleptic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider)  propsychotic | 词性: noun
