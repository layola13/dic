concamerate:/ˌkɒnkæməˈreɪt/| adj. v.|拱形的，穹顶状的；使成穹顶状|Having the form of a vault or chamber; vaulted, arched. To provide with or construct in the form of a vault or arch.|The ceiling was concamerated, giving the room a grand and spacious feel.  天花板呈拱形，使房间感觉宏伟宽敞。| The architect concamerated the roof of the new museum.  建筑师将新博物馆的屋顶设计成拱形。|近义词：arched, vaulted, domed|反义词:flat, level|词性:adjective, verb

Etymology: From Latin *concameratus*, past participle of *concamerare* "to vault, arch," from *com-* "together" + *camera* "vault, arched roof, chamber."
