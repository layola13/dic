outpeer:/ˌaʊtˈpɪər/
verb
胜过，超过
to surpass someone in ability, skill, or achievement
例句：She easily outpeered her rivals in the competition. 她在比赛中轻松胜过对手。
例句：The new software outpeers all existing programs in terms of speed and efficiency.  这款新软件在速度和效率方面超过所有现有程序。

近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, outperform
反义词：lag behind, fall short of
词性：v
