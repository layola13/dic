conchitic:/kɒnˈkɪtɪk/
adjective
贝壳状的，由贝壳组成的
Composed of shells or shell fragments.
The geologist discovered a conchitic limestone formation.  地质学家发现了一个贝壳状的石灰岩地层。
She found conchitic fragments scattered along the beach. 她发现贝壳碎片散落在海滩上。
近义词: shelly
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-conchitic")
词性: adjective
