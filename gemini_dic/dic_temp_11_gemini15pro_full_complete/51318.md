detaining:/dɪˈteɪnɪŋ/
verb, present participle
拘留，扣留
keeping someone in official custody, typically for questioning about a crime or in politically sensitive situations.
The police are detaining a suspect in connection with the robbery. 警方拘留了一名与抢劫案有关的嫌疑人。
Immigration officials were detaining the refugees. 移民官员扣留了难民。
Synonyms: holding, keeping, confining, imprisoning, arresting
Antonyms: releasing, freeing, liberating
