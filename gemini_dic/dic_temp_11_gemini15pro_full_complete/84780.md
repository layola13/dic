handsel: /ˈhændsəl/ | n. vt. |  预兆；好运；开张礼物；以…作为开端 | A gift given for good luck or as an omen of good things to come, especially at the beginning of a new year or when starting a new venture; to give such a gift. |  She gave him a new wallet as a handsel for his new job. 她送给他一个新钱包作为他新工作的开张礼物。| synonyms: omen, token, gift, earnest, augury | antonyms: curse, jinx | noun
handsel: /ˈhændsəl/ | vt. |  以…作为开端；用…举行开幕典礼 | To inaugurate or begin something with a gift or token; to use something for the first time. | The shop was handselled with a special ceremony. 这家商店以一个特别的仪式开张。| synonyms: inaugurate, launch, begin, commence, initiate | antonyms: conclude, finish, end, terminate | verb
