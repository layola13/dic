chagrinning:/ʃəˈɡrɪnɪŋ/ /ʃəˈɡrɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 懊恼的，失望的 | Feeling or expressing displeasure or disappointment at having failed or been humiliated. | He gave a chagrinning smile when he realized his mistake. 他意识到自己的错误时，露出了懊恼的笑容。| 近义词：disappointed, vexed, mortified, annoyed | 反义词：pleased, delighted, happy | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From French *chagrin*, from Old North French *chagreiner* "to grieve, sadden," of unknown origin.  Possibly related to Old French *chigre* "sadness, grief".
