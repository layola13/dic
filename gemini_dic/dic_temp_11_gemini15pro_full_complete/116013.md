marshlander:/ˈmɑːrʃləndər/ | n. | 沼泽地居民；生长在沼泽地带的动植物 | A person or animal that lives in a marshland. |  The marshlanders are known for their unique way of life, adapted to the swampy environment. 沼泽地居民以其独特的生活方式而闻名，他们适应了沼泽环境。

|  Many rare birds are marshlanders, thriving in the wetland habitats. 许多珍稀鸟类是沼泽地居民，在湿地栖息地繁衍生息。 | 近义词: swamp dweller, wetland inhabitant | 反义词: highlander, mountaineer | 词性: noun
