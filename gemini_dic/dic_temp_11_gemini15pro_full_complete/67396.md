exterminatory:/ɪkˈstɜːmɪnətɔːri/
adjective
灭绝的，毁灭性的
serving to exterminate; tending to exterminate.
The government launched an exterminatory campaign against the rebels. 政府对叛军发动了灭绝性的打击。

The exterminatory rhetoric employed by the dictator further fueled the conflict. 独裁者使用的灭绝性言辞进一步加剧了冲突。

近义词:destructive, eradicative, ruinous
反义词:preservative, constructive, protective
词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin exterminātōrius, from extermināt- (past participle stem of extermināre ‘drive out, banish, destroy’), from ex- ‘out of, from’ + terminus ‘boundary, limit’.
