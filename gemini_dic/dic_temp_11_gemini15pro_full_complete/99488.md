interfiltrate:/ˌɪntərˈfɪltreɪt/ | v. | 渗透，浸润 | To filter or permeate into or through something, especially a tissue or substance. | The rainwater interfiltrated the soil, nourishing the plants. 雨水渗透到土壤中，滋养了植物。 |  The cancerous cells had interfiltrated the surrounding tissue. 癌细胞已经渗透到周围的组织中。 | 近义词: permeate, infiltrate, percolate | 反义词: exfiltrate | 词性: 动词
