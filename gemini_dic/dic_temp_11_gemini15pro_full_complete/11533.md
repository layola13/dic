ariosophy:/ˌɛəriˈɒsəfi/ | n. |神智学;奥义智学|Ariosophy (from the Greek ἀρειότης areiotēs "excellence" and σοφία sophia "wisdom") is an esoteric racialist form of occultism, developed by Austrian mystic Guido von List, and further developed by his followers Lanz von Liebenfels and Jörg Lanz von Liebenfels. It primarily focuses on the alleged superiority of the Aryan race. |  His beliefs were rooted in Ariosophy, a mystical and racist ideology. 他的信仰植根于神智学，一种神秘主义和种族主义意识形态。| 近义词: Theosophy, occultism | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "rationalism" or "materialism" as opposing philosophies) | 词性: noun
