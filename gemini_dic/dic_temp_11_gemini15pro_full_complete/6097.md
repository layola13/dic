americanizing:/əˈmɛrɪkəˌnaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使美国化
The process of making something or someone more American in character or style.
Many immigrants are americanizing their names to fit in better.  许多移民正在使他们的名字美国化，以便更好地融入。
Fast food chains are americanizing the diets of people around the world.  快餐连锁店正在使世界各地人们的饮食美国化。
americanise, westernize
localize, nationalize
