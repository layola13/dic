creeky:/ˈkriːki/| adj.|吱吱作响的，嘎吱嘎吱的|Making a harsh, high-pitched sound, typically of something wooden.|The stairs were old and creeky. 楼梯又旧又嘎吱作响。|近义词：squeaky, creaking|反义词:silent, quiet|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *creken*, frequentative of *creken* (“to crack, make a loud noise”), of imitative origin.
