hesitating:/ˈhezɪteɪtɪŋ/| adj.|犹豫的，迟疑的|showing reluctance or delay in acting or speaking|例句：He stood there, hesitating before entering the room. 他站在那儿，犹豫着要不要进房间。|近义词：uncertain, hesitant, wavering, indecisive|反义词：decisive, resolute, confident|词性:形容词
hesitate:/ˈhezɪteɪt/| v.|犹豫，迟疑|to pause before saying or doing something, especially because you are nervous or not certain|例句：She hesitated before answering the question.  她在回答问题之前犹豫了一下。|近义词：pause, waver, falter|反义词：decide, resolve, proceed|词性:动词
