bookishly:/ˈbʊkɪʃli/| adv.|书呆子气地；学究式地|In a manner characteristic of a bookish person; in a studious or pedantic way.|He smiled bookishly, pushing his glasses up his nose. 他书呆子气地笑了笑，把眼镜往上推了推。|近义词：studiously, pedantically, academically|反义词：practically, pragmatically|词性:副词

Etymology: From "bookish" + "-ly".  "Bookish" comes from "book" (from Old English "bōc") + "-ish" (a suffix forming adjectives from nouns, meaning "of the nature of").
