capoeira:/kæpuˈeɪrə/| n.|卡波埃拉|A Brazilian martial art that combines elements of dance, acrobatics, and music.|例句：Capoeira is known for its fluid and expressive movements. 卡波埃拉以其流畅而富有表现力的动作而闻名。

例句：They were practicing capoeira on the beach. 他们在沙滩上练习卡波埃拉。|近义词：martial art, fighting style|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "passivity")|词性:noun
