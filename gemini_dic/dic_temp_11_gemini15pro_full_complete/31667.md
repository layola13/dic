chattiness:/ˈtʃætɪnɪs/
n.
健谈，饶舌，话多
The quality of being chatty; talkativeness.
Her chattiness made it difficult to get any work done. 她太健谈了，很难完成任何工作。
loquacity, garrulity, talkativeness
reticence, taciturnity
