obtenebration:/ˌɒbtɛnɪˈbreɪʃən/ /ˌɑːbtɛnɪˈbreɪʃən/| n. |昏暗，黑暗，朦胧|The act of darkening or obscuring; the state of being darkened or obscured.  A darkening or obscuring of the sight or understanding.|A thick fog caused the obtenebration of the city skyline.  浓雾使城市天际线变得朦胧。|近义词：darkening, obscuring, dimming, shadowing|反义词: illumination, brightening, clarification|词性:noun
词源:From Latin obtenebrāre (“to darken, obscure”), from ob- (“against, toward, over”) + tenebrae (“darkness, shadows”).
