altheine:/ˈælθiːɪn/| n.|茶碱,咖啡碱|A purine alkaloid that occurs naturally in tea and coffee. It is a stimulant of the central nervous system.|例句：Altheine is the same compound as caffeine. 茶碱与咖啡碱是同一种化合物。

例句：The altheine in the tea kept me awake. 茶里的茶碱让我保持清醒。|近义词：caffeine, theine|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
