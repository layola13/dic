creationism: /kriˈeɪʃənɪzəm/
n.
创造论，神创论
The belief that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution.
Many people find it difficult to reconcile science and creationism.  很多人觉得很难调和科学和神创论。
Creationism is still taught in some schools. 在一些学校里仍然教授神创论。
近义词: intelligent design
反义词: evolution, Darwinism
词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *creātiōnismus, from Latin creātiō (“creation”) + -ismus.  Ultimately from Latin creō (“create”).
