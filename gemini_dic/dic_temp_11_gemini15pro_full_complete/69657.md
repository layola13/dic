fenceful:/ˈfɛnsfʊl/
adjective
有栅栏的；被栅栏围着的
Enclosed or surrounded by fences; having many fences.
The property was fenceful, providing privacy and security. 这处房产有很多栅栏，提供了隐私和安全保障。
The countryside was fenceful, with fields divided by numerous barriers. 乡村到处都是栅栏，田野被众多的栅栏隔开。
近义词: fenced, enclosed
反义词: open, unenclosed
词性: adjective
词源: "fence" + "-ful"  "fence" comes from Old French "fense," ultimately from Latin "defensa" (defense). The suffix "-ful" forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
