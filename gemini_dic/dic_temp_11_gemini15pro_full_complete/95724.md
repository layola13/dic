inconsequentiality:/ɪnˌkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃiˈælɪti/ | n. | 無關緊要；不重要；無足輕重 |The state or quality of being inconsequential; lack of importance or significance. |The inconsequentiality of the matter was apparent to everyone. 这件事的无关紧要性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。|Synonyms: insignificance, triviality, unimportance, pettiness |Antonyms: significance, importance, consequence, relevance| noun

inconsequential:/ɪnˌkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 無關緊要的；不重要的；無足輕重的 |Not important or significant. |He dismissed the issue as inconsequential. 他认为这个问题无关紧要而不予理会。|Synonyms: insignificant, trivial, unimportant, negligible, petty |Antonyms: significant, important, consequential, relevant| adjective
