outplotting:/ˌaʊtˈplɒtɪŋ/
v.
智胜，比…更精明地策划，胜过…的计谋
To plan more cleverly than; to outmaneuver by superior planning.
The general outplotted his enemy, leading them into a trap. 将军智胜了他的敌人，将他们引入了陷阱。
outmaneuver, outwit, outsmart
