byword:/ˈbaɪwɜːrd/ | n. | 谚语，格言；代名词，象征 | A byword is a word or phrase that is associated with a particular person or thing, often representing a characteristic quality or idea. | "Honesty" is a byword for Abraham Lincoln.  诚实是亚伯拉罕·林肯的代名词。| synonym: proverb, motto, catchphrase, slogan, epithet | antonym:  anonymity, obscurity | noun


byword:/ˈbaɪwɜːrd/ | n. | 典型；绰号 | A byword is something that is widely known as a typical or striking example of a particular quality or characteristic, often something bad. |  His name has become a byword for corruption. 他的名字已经成了腐败的代名词。| synonym:  epitome, embodiment, archetype | antonym: counter-example, exception | noun
