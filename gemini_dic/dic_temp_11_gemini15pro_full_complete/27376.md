came-by:/keɪm baɪ/ | phrasal verb |经过；路过| To visit someone briefly or incidentally. | I came by your house yesterday, but you weren't home. 我昨天路过你家，但你不在。| synonyms: visit, drop by, stop by | antonyms: avoid, bypass | verb phrase
