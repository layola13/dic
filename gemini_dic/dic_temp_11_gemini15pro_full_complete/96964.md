infancies: /ˈɪnfənsiz/
n.
婴儿期（复数）
the early stages of growth or development of something.
例句：The company is still in its infancy.  这家公司仍处于初期阶段。

例句：During the infancy of the internet, few people realized its potential. 在互联网的初期，很少有人意识到它的潜力。

近义词：beginnings, early stages, nascent stages
反义词：maturity, late stages, end
词性：名词
