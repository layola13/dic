ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cynique* and Latin *cynicus*, from Greek *kynikos* "like a dog," from *kyōn* "dog."  The Cynics were a school of Greek philosophers who advocated a simple life and rejected conventional social norms. Their name may have derived from their dog-like disregard for social conventions.
