decorums:/dɪˈkɔːrəmz/
|n.|礼仪；礼节；得体；合宜|Propriety and good taste in conduct or appearance.  The customs and rules of polite behavior.  Plural of decorum.|The ambassador observed the decorums of the court. 大使遵守了宫廷礼仪。|Near Synonyms: etiquette, proprieties, protocol, civilities, formalities, niceties, amenities, courtesies, manners,mores, conventions, customs, usages, practices. |Antonyms: impropriety, indecorum, rudeness, bad manners, disrespect, impoliteness, uncouthness.|noun
