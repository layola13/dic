conclusible:/kənˈkluːzɪbl/
adjective
可推断的，可下结论的
Capable of being concluded or inferred; deducible.
The evidence presented was conclusible, leading the jury to a guilty verdict.  呈堂证供是可以得出结论的，导致陪审团作出了有罪判决。
The detective found the case conclusible based on the forensic evidence.  侦探根据法医证据发现此案可以得出结论。
近义词:deducible, inferable, demonstrable
反义词:inconclusive, ambiguous, uncertain
词性:adjective
