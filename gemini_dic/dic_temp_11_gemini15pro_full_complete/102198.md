italica:/ɪˈtælɪkə/ | adj. n. | 斜体的；意大利体；斜体字 |  Relating to a typeface with characters that slant to the right, used especially for emphasis or distinction.  Also, can refer to the typeface itself.  Derived from "Italic," referring to the region of Italy. | The title of the book was printed in *italica*.  这本书的标题是用斜体印刷的。 |  The word "emphasis" was written in *italica* to highlight its importance.  单词“emphasis”用斜体书写以突出其重要性。 | Synonyms: italic, oblique | Antonyms: roman, upright | Adjective, Noun
