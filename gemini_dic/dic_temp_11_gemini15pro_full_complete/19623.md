bigwigs:/ˈbɪɡwɪɡz/
noun
大人物，重要人物
Important or influential people.
The bigwigs at the company decided to merge with their competitor. 公司的大人物们决定与竞争对手合并。
The museum gala was attended by bigwigs from the art world.  博物馆的庆典晚会吸引了艺术界的大人物们出席。
synonyms: VIPs, dignitaries, high-ups, top brass, movers and shakers
antonyms:  nobodies, underlings, small fry
n.
