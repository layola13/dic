kelpfish:/ˈkɛlpfɪʃ/| n. |海藻鱼|A fish that lives among kelp, often camouflaged to resemble it.  These fish are typically small and elongated, and many species are found in the kelp forests of the Pacific Ocean.|例句：The kelpfish blended perfectly with the swaying kelp.  海藻鱼完美地融入了摇曳的海藻中。

例句：Divers spotted several different species of kelpfish hiding amongst the kelp fronds.  潜水员发现了几种不同的海藻鱼藏在海藻叶中。|近义词：seaweed fish|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
