computerist: /kəmˈpjuːtərɪst/
n.|计算机专家；电脑迷|A person who is skilled in or enthusiastic about using computers. |He's a real computerist, always building new systems. 他是一位真正的计算机专家，总是组装新的系统。|近义词: programmer, software engineer, computer expert, techie, geek|反义词: computer illiterate, technophobe|词性: 名词

Etymology:
computer + -ist.  The suffix "-ist" is used to form nouns that refer to a person who practices or is skilled in a particular art, science, or activity.
