imbrown:/ɪmˈbraʊn/ | v. | 使变成褐色，晒黑 | To make brown, especially by exposure to the sun. | The sun imbrowned his face. 太阳晒黑了他的脸。| The leaves imbrowned in autumn. 秋天树叶变成了褐色。| 近义词: brown, tan, darken | 反义词: bleach, lighten | 词性: 动词

Etymology: im- (prefix meaning "in" or "into") + brown (adjective referring to the color). The prefix "im-" becomes "in-" before "b".
