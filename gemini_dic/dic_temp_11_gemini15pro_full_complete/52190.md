diarrhoeal:/ˌdaɪəˈriːəl/ /ˌdaɪəˈriːəl/ | adj. | 腹泻的 | Relating to or characterized by diarrhoea. |  The doctor diagnosed a diarrhoeal illness. 医生诊断出一种腹泻性疾病。

The diarrhoeal outbreak affected many children in the village. 腹泻的爆发影响了村里许多孩子。


diarrhea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ /ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ | n. | 腹泻 | An intestinal disorder characterized by frequent and watery bowel movements. |  The main symptom of the infection was diarrhea.  这种感染的主要症状是腹泻。

He suffered from severe diarrhea after eating contaminated food. 他吃了受污染的食物后患上了严重的腹泻。


Etymology: From Late Latin *diarrhoicus, from Greek diarrhoia "a flowing through," from diarrhein "to flow through," from dia- "through" + rhein "to flow."


Synonym:  loose, watery, runny (bowels)

Antonym: constipated
