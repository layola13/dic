noneclipsed:/ˌnɒnɪˈklɪpst/ | adj. | 非蚀的，未被遮蔽的 | Not eclipsed; fully visible.  Referring to a celestial body that is not obscured by another. | The noneclipsed portion of the moon was brightly shining.  未被遮蔽的部分月亮明亮地照耀着。| The noneclipsed sun shone brightly in the sky. 未被遮蔽的太阳在天空中闪耀。| synonyms: uneclipsed, visible, uncovered, exposed | antonyms: eclipsed, obscured, hidden, covered | adjective
