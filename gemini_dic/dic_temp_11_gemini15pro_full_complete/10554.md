appreciable:/əˈpriːʃəbl/| adj.|可观的，明显的，值得重视的|Large or important enough to be noticed.|There has been an appreciable drop in the crime rate. 犯罪率显著下降。|近义词：noticeable, significant, considerable, substantial|反义词：insignificant, negligible, imperceptible|词性:形容词
词源: Late 16th century: from late Latin appretiabilis ‘valuable’, from appretiare ‘to set a price to’, from ad- ‘to’ + pretium ‘price’.
