manganic:/mæŋˈɡænɪk/
adjective
锰的；含锰的；三价锰的
Containing or relating to manganese, especially with valence 3.
Manganese(III) oxide is a manganic compound.  氧化锰(III)是一种锰化合物。
Manganic salts are typically unstable. 锰盐通常不稳定。
Near Synonyms:  manganese,  manganese(III)
Antonyms:  manganous
word type: adjective
