hypomorphic:/ˌhaɪpəˈmɔːrfɪk/
adjective
亚形的，形态减弱的
Having a reduced or partial function compared to the wild-type allele.  A hypomorphic mutation results in a partial loss of gene function.
The researchers identified a hypomorphic allele of the gene responsible for eye development.  研究人员发现了负责眼睛发育的基因的一个亚形等位基因。
The hypomorphic mutation resulted in a milder form of the disease.  亚形突变导致了较轻微的疾病形式。
Near Synonyms: leaky, weakly expressive
Antonym: hypermorphic, neomorphic
