childcare: /ˈtʃaɪldˌker/ (US), /ˈtʃaɪldkeər/ (UK)| n.|儿童保育，托儿服务|The provision of care for children, especially by a crèche, nursery, or childminder while parents are working or otherwise unavailable.|例句：Finding affordable childcare is a major challenge for many working parents. 许多工作的父母发现找到负担得起的托儿服务是一项重大挑战。
