impatience:/ɪmˈpeɪʃəns/| n. |不耐烦，急躁|The state of being unable to wait patiently or tolerate delay; restlessness or irritability.|Her impatience was evident in her constant fidgeting. 她的烦躁不安从她不停地坐立不安中显现出来。|He tapped his foot impatiently, waiting for the bus. 他不耐烦地敲着脚，等着公共汽车。|Synonyms: restlessness, irritability, eagerness, anxiety|Antonyms: patience, tolerance, composure|noun
