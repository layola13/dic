multipaned:/ˌmʌltiˈpeɪnd/
adjective
多窗格的
Having multiple panes of glass.  A multipaned window is divided into sections by bars or strips between the panes of glass.
The multipaned windows offered a glimpse of the garden. 多窗格的窗户可以瞥见花园。
The old house had charming multipaned windows. 这座老房子有着迷人的多窗格窗户。
Synonyms: multi-paned, multi-light
Antonyms: single-paned
词性: adjective


Etymology:
"multi-" (prefix meaning "many") + "paned" (from "pane," meaning a single sheet of glass).  The word "pane" comes from the Old French "pan" meaning "piece of cloth, side," ultimately from Latin "pannus" meaning "cloth, rag."
