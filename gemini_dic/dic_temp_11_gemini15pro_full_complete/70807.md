finger:/ˈfɪŋɡər/ (US) /ˈfɪŋɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. |手指；指状物；少量；指针|Part of the hand, excluding the thumb, used for touching, feeling, and manipulating objects. Also, anything resembling a finger in shape or function. Can also refer to a small amount or a pointer.|例句：She pointed her finger at him. 她用手指着他。|例句：He added a finger of whiskey to his coffee. 他在咖啡里加了一点威士忌。|近义词：digit, pointer|反义词:|noun
