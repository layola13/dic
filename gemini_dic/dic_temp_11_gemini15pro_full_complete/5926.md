ambitiously: /æmˈbɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 野心勃勃地，雄心壮志地 |  In a manner that shows a strong desire and determination to succeed. | She ambitiously pursued her goal of becoming a CEO. 她雄心勃勃地追求着成为首席执行官的目标。 |  He worked ambitiously to finish the project ahead of schedule. 他雄心勃勃地工作，以期提前完成项目。 | 近义词: eagerly, zealously, aspiringly | 反义词: lazily, apathetically, halfheartedly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *ambiciouseli, from Old French ambicïeus, from Latin ambitiōsus (“going around, canvassing for office”), from ambītiō (“going around, a circuit, ambition”), from ambīre (“to go around”), from ambi- (“around”) + īre (“to go”).
