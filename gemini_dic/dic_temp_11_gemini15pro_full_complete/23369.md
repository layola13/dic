brach:/brɑːk/ /brætʃ/| n. |猎犬的一种；(尤指)短腿猎犬|A type of hound, especially one with short legs.|The hunter followed the brach through the woods. 猎人跟着短腿猎犬穿过树林。|Synonyms: hound, beagle, basset hound|Antonyms: None|noun

brach:/brɑːk/ /brætʃ/| adj. |(诗歌用语)分支的；岔开的|(poetic) Branched; forked.|The brach of the river led to a hidden waterfall. 河的支流通向一个隐藏的瀑布。|Synonyms: branched, forked, divided|Antonyms: straight, unbranched|adjective
