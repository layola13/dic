digitize:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪz/| n. |数字化，数位化 (这个词性相对少见)|The process of converting analog information or data to digital form.|The digitize of historical records is important for preservation.  历史记录的数字化对于保存至关重要。|近义词：conversion, encoding, computerization|反义词: N/A|noun
