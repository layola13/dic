depravedly:/dɪˈpreɪvɪdli/
adverb
堕落地；邪恶地；腐败地
In a morally corrupt or wicked manner.
He grinned depravedly. 他邪恶地咧嘴一笑。
The dictator ruled depravedly, enriching himself while his people starved.  独裁者腐败地统治着，让自己富裕起来，而他的人民却在挨饿。

近义词:wickedly, sinfully, perversely, corruptly, immorally
反义词:righteously, virtuously, morally
