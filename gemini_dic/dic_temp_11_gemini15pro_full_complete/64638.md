erythrochroism:/ɪˌrɪθrəˈkroʊɪzəm/
n.
红变色，红变色现象
The property possessed by some crystals of exhibiting a different color according to the direction of the transmitted light.  Some minerals exhibit erythrochroism.
Some minerals, like ruby, exhibit erythrochroism.  一些矿物，比如红宝石，会呈现红变色现象。

dichroism, pleochroism
(No commonly used antonym)
noun
