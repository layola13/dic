overdirected:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈrɛktɪd/
adjective
过度指导的，指导过多的
given too much direction or guidance, often resulting in a lack of independence or creativity.
The play felt overdirected, with every actor's movement precisely controlled.  这出戏感觉指导过度，每个演员的动作都被精确控制。
The child's life was so overdirected that she had no room to develop her own interests.  这个孩子的生活被过度指导，以至于她没有空间发展自己的兴趣。
synonyms: overmanaged, overcontrolled, micromanaged
antonyms: underdirected, unguided, independent
词性: 形容词
