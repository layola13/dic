jailbroken:/ˈdʒeɪlˌbroʊkən/
adjective
越狱的
(of a smartphone or other electronic device) modified to remove restrictions imposed by the manufacturer or operator, especially to allow the installation of unauthorized software.
My jailbroken iPhone lets me customize it in ways I couldn't before. 我的越狱iPhone让我可以以前所未有的方式进行自定义。
He downloaded a pirated app onto his jailbroken phone. 他把一个盗版应用下载到了他的越狱手机上。
近义词:unlocked, rooted (for Android)
反义词:stock, unmodified, restricted
词性:adjective
