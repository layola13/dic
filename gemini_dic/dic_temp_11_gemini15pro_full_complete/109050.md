legalises:/ˈliːɡəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使合法化；将…合法化
To make something legal; to authorize something by law.
The government has legalised the use of cannabis for medicinal purposes. 政府已将药用大麻合法化。
legalizes (美式拼写)
authorize, permit, legitimize
prohibit, ban, outlaw
v.
