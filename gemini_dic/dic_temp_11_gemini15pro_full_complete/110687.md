lineages:/ˈlɪniɪdʒɪz/
n.
血统，世系；谱系
A sequence of biological entities, such as genes, cells, or people, connected by ancestry.
The royal family’s lineage can be traced back centuries.  这个皇室家族的世系可以追溯到几个世纪以前。

lineages:/ˈlɪniɪdʒɪz/
n.
血统，世系；谱系
The line of descendants of a particular ancestor; ancestry.
They come from a long lineage of farmers. 他们来自一个农民世家。

lineages:/ˈlɪniɪdʒɪz/
n.
血统，世系；谱系
A series of organisms connected by reproduction from parent to offspring; a lineage.
The study traced the lineage of the virus. 这项研究追溯了该病毒的谱系。
