manacle: /ˈmænək(ə)l/ | n. | 手铐，镣铐 | A metal band, chain, or shackle for fastening someone's hands or ankles. | The police placed manacles on the suspect. 警察给嫌疑人戴上了手铐。 | 近义词: handcuff, shackle, fetter | 反义词:  (none direct - consider "key" as it unlocks) | 词性: Noun
