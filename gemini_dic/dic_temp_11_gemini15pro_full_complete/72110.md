flockly: /ˈflɒkli/ (British) /ˈflɑːkli/ (American) | adv. | 成群地，群集地 | In a flock-like manner; resembling a flock.  Describes actions or appearances suggestive of a flock of birds or animals.  This word is not commonly used and may be considered archaic or dialectal. |  The sheep moved flockly across the field. 羊群成群结队地穿过田野。| The starlings flew flockly towards the trees.  椋鸟成群地飞向树林。 |  Near synonyms: gregariously, collectively | Near antonyms: individually, separately | adverb
