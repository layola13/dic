chastest:/ˈtʃeɪstɪst/| adj.|最贞洁的，最纯洁的|Superlative form of chaste: most chaste.  Free from obscenity; decent. Not having experienced sexual intercourse; virginal.|She led a chastest life, dedicated to her faith. 她过着最贞洁的生活，致力于她的信仰。|The novel portrays the chastest love story I've ever read. 这部小说描绘了我读过的最纯洁的爱情故事。|近义词：purest, virtuous, immaculate, innocent|反义词：lewd, impure, promiscuous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *chasteste*, superlative of *chaste*, from Old French *chaste* (modern French *chaste*), from Latin *castus* (“morally pure, undefiled, chaste”).
