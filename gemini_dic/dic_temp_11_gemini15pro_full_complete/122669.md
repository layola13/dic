modulated:/ˈmɒdjʊleɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdʒəleɪtɪd/ (US)| adj.|调制的，调整的，变调的|adjusted or regulated by or as if by a modulator; having the amplitude, frequency, or phase of a carrier wave varied in accordance with another signal |The modulated signal was clear and strong. 调制后的信号清晰而强劲。|近义词：adjusted, regulated, tempered, varied|反义词：unmodulated, constant, steady|词性:形容词

modulate:/ˈmɒdjʊleɪt/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdʒəleɪt/ (US)| v.|调制，调整，变调|to exert a modifying or controlling influence on |He modulated his voice to a whisper. 他把声音调低到耳语。|近义词：adjust, regulate, temper, vary|反义词：maintain, fix|词性:动词


词源：From Latin modulatus, past participle of modulari (“to regulate, measure”), from modulus (“measure”).
