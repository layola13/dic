interradiates:/ˌɪntərˈreɪdieɪts/ | n. | 中间物，中间体 | Substances formed during a chemical reaction that are not the final products but are converted into the final products in subsequent steps. |  The interradiates in this reaction are highly unstable. 这个反应中的中间体非常不稳定。| synonyms: intermediates, intermediary products | antonyms: final products, end products | noun

interradiate:/ˌɪntərˈreɪdieɪt/ | adj. | 中间的，居间的 | Located between radii or rays. | The interradiate plates of a sea urchin are located between the ambulacral areas. 海胆的间辐板位于步带区之间。 | synonyms: intermediate, interstitial | antonyms: terminal, peripheral | adjective

interradiate:/ˌɪntərˈreɪdieɪt/ | v. | 放射，发光 | To emit rays or light. (Rarely used) | The crystal interradiates a soft glow.  这颗水晶发出柔和的光芒。| synonyms: radiate, emit | antonyms: absorb, extinguish | verb
