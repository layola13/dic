furfuraceous:/ˌfɜːrfəˈreɪʃəs/ | adj. | 麸皮状的；糠秕状的；像头皮屑的 | Covered with or resembling scales or scurf.  Characterized by bran-like or scaly particles. |  The furfuraceous rash on his scalp was diagnosed as seborrheic dermatitis.  他的头皮上的麸皮状皮疹被诊断为脂溢性皮炎。 | Synonyms: scaly, scurfy, dandruffy | Antonyms: smooth, clear | adjective
