meditate:/ˈmedɪteɪt/| v. | 冥想，沉思，思考 |To focus one's mind for a period of time, in silence or with the aid of chanting, for religious or spiritual purposes or as a method of relaxation.|  I meditate for 20 minutes every morning. 我每天早上冥想20分钟。

|She meditated on the meaning of life. 她沉思着生命的意义。

|近义词：contemplate, ponder, reflect, muse |反义词: rush, hurry |词性: 动词
