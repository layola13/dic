outglooms:/ˌaʊtˈɡluːmz/
verb
使黯然失色；使相形见绌
to be darker or more depressing than something else; to surpass in gloominess.
The storm clouds outgloomed the already gray landscape.  暴风云使原本灰暗的景色更加黯淡。
His recent failures outgloomed his previous successes. 他最近的失败使他之前的成功黯然失色。
近义词:overshadow, eclipse, darken
反义词:brighten, cheer, illuminate
词性:v.
