cauterize:/ˈkɔːtəraɪz/| v. | 烧灼；烙 | to burn (tissue) with a hot iron, electric current, or a caustic substance, especially to stop bleeding or prevent infection | The doctor cauterized the wound to stop the bleeding. 医生烧灼伤口以止血。

The surgeon cauterized the blood vessel.  外科医生烧灼了血管。


| synonym: burn, sear, scorch | antonym: heal, soothe | verb |


cautery:/ˈkɔːtəri/| n. | 烧灼；烙术；烙器 | an instrument or a caustic substance used for cauterizing | The vet used a cautery to remove the growth. 兽医用烙器去掉了赘生物。

The doctor used cautery to stop the bleeding. 医生用烧灼术止血。


| synonym: branding iron, electrocautery | antonym:  N/A (no direct antonym for a medical instrument) | noun |
