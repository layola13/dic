authoritativeness:/əˌθɔːrɪˈteɪtɪvnəs/| n.|权威性，威信，可靠性|The authoritativeness of the study was questioned due to its small sample size.  这项研究的权威性因其样本量小而受到质疑。|例句：The authoritativeness of his voice commanded respect. 他那充满权威的声音令人敬畏。|近义词：authority, credibility, reliability, influence, power|反义词：uncertainty, doubt, weakness, unreliability, invalidity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From authoritative + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin auctoritas "official power, influence, command, dignity," from auctor "author, founder, promoter"
