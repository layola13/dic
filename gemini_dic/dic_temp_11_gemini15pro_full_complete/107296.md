lamaists:/ˈlɑːməɪsts/
noun
喇嘛教徒
Followers of Lamaism, a term sometimes used (often considered outdated or inaccurate) to refer to Tibetan Buddhism.
Many Western scholars in the 19th and early 20th centuries referred to Tibetan Buddhists as Lamaists.  许多西方学者在19和20世纪早期将藏传佛教徒称为喇嘛教徒。
The term "Lamaist" is now generally avoided as it can be seen as reductive and misleading.  现在通常避免使用“喇嘛教徒”一词，因为它可能被视为简化和误导。
synonyms: Tibetan Buddhists
antonyms:  None (It's a religious designation, not a concept with a direct opposite.)
