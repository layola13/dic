air-droppable:/ˈɛərˌdrɒpəbəl/ (US), /ˈeəˌdrɒpəbəl/ (UK)
adjective
可空投的
Suitable for being dropped from an aircraft, especially by parachute.
例句：The supplies were packaged in air-droppable containers.  这些物资被打包成可空投的集装箱。

例句：The humanitarian aid was air-droppable to the remote village. 人道主义援助被空投到偏远的村庄。

近义词：parachutable
反义词: non-air-droppable
