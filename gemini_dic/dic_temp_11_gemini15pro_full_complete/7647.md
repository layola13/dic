annulose:/ˈæn.jə.ləs/ | adj. | 环状的；有环纹的 | Having rings or ringlike segments; marked with rings. | The earthworm's body is annulose. 蚯蚓的身体是环状的。 | The millipede has an annulose body.  千足虫的身体有环纹。 | 近义词: ringed, segmented, annular | 反义词: smooth, unsegmented | 词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *annulosus, from annulus (“ring”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives indicating fullness or abundance).
