integrability:/ˌɪntɪɡrəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可积性，可整合性 | The quality of being integrable; capable of being integrated.  It often refers to the ability of a function to be integrated, or the ability of different systems or components to work together harmoniously. | The integrability of this function is questionable. 这个函数的可积性值得怀疑。| 近义词: coherence, compatibility, interoperability | 反义词: incompatibility, disunity | noun
