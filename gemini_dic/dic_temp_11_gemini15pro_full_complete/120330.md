minihospitals:/ˈmɪniˌhɒspɪtəlz/ (US) /ˈmɪniˌhɒspɪtlz/ (UK)| n. |小型医院，社区医院|Small-scale hospitals, often specializing in specific treatments or serving a local community, and typically having fewer beds than a traditional hospital.  They often focus on outpatient care and less complex procedures.|Many minihospitals are popping up in suburban areas to provide convenient access to healthcare. 许多小型医院正在郊区涌现，以提供便捷的医疗服务。|Synonyms: community hospitals, clinics, medical centers|Antonyms: large hospitals, teaching hospitals|noun
