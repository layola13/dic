nontypical:/ˌnɑːnˈtɪpɪkəl/| adj.|非典型的，不典型的|Not representative of a type, group, or class.  Deviating from a norm. |例句：This patient's symptoms are nontypical for the flu.  这个病人的症状不是典型的流感症状。|例句：The architecture of the building is nontypical for this region.  这栋建筑的结构在这个地区并不典型。|近义词：atypical, unusual, abnormal, irregular|反义词：typical, usual, normal, standard|词性:形容词
词源:non- (prefix meaning "not") + typical (from Late Latin typicus, from Greek typikos "of a type," from typos "impression, figure, type")
