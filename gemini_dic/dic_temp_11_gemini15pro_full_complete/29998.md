cavity:/ˈkævəti/| n.|空腔；洞；蛀牙|An empty space within a solid object, or a hole in a tooth caused by decay.|The dentist discovered a small cavity in my tooth. 牙医在我的牙齿上发现了一个小蛀牙。|synonyms: hole, hollow, gap, pit, crater|antonyms: fullness, solid, protrusion|noun

cavity:/ˈkævəti/| n.|（感情上的）空虚|A feeling of emptiness or lack of fulfillment.|He felt a deep cavity in his life after his wife left.  妻子离开后，他感到生活中一片空虚。|synonyms: emptiness, void, vacuum|antonyms: fullness, completeness|noun
