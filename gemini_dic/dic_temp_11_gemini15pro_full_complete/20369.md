bistort:/ˈbɪstɔːrt/ | n. | 拳参 | A perennial herb (genus Bistorta) of the buckwheat family, having small pink or white flowers in dense spikes and a thick, twisted rootstock. |  The bistort's twisted root was traditionally used in herbal medicine.  拳参扭曲的根传统上用于草药。| synonyms: snakeweed, knotweed | antonyms: N/A | noun

bistort:/ˈbɪstɔːrt/ | n. | 拳参 | Any of several plants of the genus Bistorta, especially B. officinalis, having spikes of pink flowers and astringent roots formerly used medicinally. |  He identified the plant as a type of bistort. 他将这种植物鉴定为一种拳参。| synonyms: snakeweed, knotweed | antonyms: N/A | noun
