bonanza:/bəˈnænzə/| n.|繁荣，兴盛；好运；大量|A sudden increase in wealth, good fortune, or something that is desirable.|The discovery of gold in California was a bonanza for many people. 加州黄金的发现对许多人来说是一场盛宴。|Synonyms: windfall, godsend, boom, prosperity|Antonyms: misfortune, hardship, bust, recession|noun
