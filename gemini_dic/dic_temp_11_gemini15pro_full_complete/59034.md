dwarfism: /ˈdwɔːrfɪzəm/ (US) /ˈdwɔːfɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |侏儒症；矮小；|A medical condition characterized by abnormally short height caused by genetic or hormonal factors, resulting in an adult height significantly below average.|  Achondroplasia is the most common type of dwarfism.  软骨发育不全是最常见的侏儒症类型。|Synonyms: short stature, nanism|Antonyms: gigantism|Noun
