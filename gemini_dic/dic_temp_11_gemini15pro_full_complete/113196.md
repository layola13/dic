luringly:/ˈlʊərɪŋli/ | adv. | 诱惑地，诱人地 | In a way that tempts or attracts someone to do something, often something wrong or unwise. | The aroma of freshly baked bread wafted luringly from the bakery.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味从面包店飘来，诱人极了。|  The salesman described the car's features luringly.  销售员诱人地描述了这辆车的特点。| enticingly, temptingly, seductively | repulsively, offputtingly | adverb

词源:
From lure (verb) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverb suffix).  "Lure" comes from Old French "luire" meaning "to shine," later evolving to mean "to attract" like a shining object.
