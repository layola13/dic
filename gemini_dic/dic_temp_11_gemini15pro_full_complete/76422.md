gaian:/ˈɡaɪən/ /ˈɡeɪən/
adjective|盖亚的；盖亚学说的；与盖亚假说相关的|Relating to or characteristic of the Gaia hypothesis, which postulates that the Earth functions as a self-regulating system.|The gaian view suggests that the Earth's systems work together to maintain life. 地球盖亚的观点认为地球的系统共同作用以维持生命。|近义词:ecological, holistic|反义词:reductionist|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Gaia (Greek goddess of Earth) + -an (suffix forming adjectives). 来自盖亚（希腊大地女神）+ -an（构成形容词的后缀）。
