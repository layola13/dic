crisp:/krɪsp/| adj. n.|脆的，凉爽的，干脆利落的；薯片|Firm, dry, and brittle; (of the air or weather) cool, fresh, and invigorating; briskly decisive and matter-of-fact.|The apples were crisp and juicy. 这些苹果又脆又多汁。|近义词：brittle, crunchy, fresh, brisk|反义词：soggy, soft, stale, sluggish|词性:形容词

crisp:/krɪsp/| n.|薯片|A thin slice of potato fried until crisp.|I like eating crisps while watching movies. 我喜欢一边看电影一边吃薯片。|近义词：chips, potato chips|反义词:|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old English *crisp, from Proto-Germanic *kruspaz, from Proto-Indo-European *kreus- “to freeze, form a crust.”
