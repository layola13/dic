countercountermeasures:/ˈkaʊntərˈkaʊntərmɛʒərz/| n. |反反制措施，应对反制措施的措施|Measures taken to counteract an adversary's countermeasures.  These are actions designed to defeat attempts to interfere with one's original measures.|The general outlined the countercountermeasures they had developed in case their initial strategy was compromised. 将军概述了他们为防止最初战略受损而制定的反反制措施。

The government implemented countercountermeasures to address the enemy's attempts to disrupt their cyber defenses. 政府实施了反反制措施，以应对敌人破坏其网络防御的企图。|Synonyms: counter-countermeasures, counteractions to countermeasures|Antonyms:  (Difficult to define direct antonyms, perhaps) vulnerability, weakness|noun
