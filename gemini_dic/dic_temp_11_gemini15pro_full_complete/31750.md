cheetah: /ˈtʃiːtə/ | n. | 猎豹 | A large, spotted cat of Africa and parts of Asia, known as the fastest land animal. It is characterized by its slender body, long legs, and distinctive tear-like markings on its face. | The cheetah chased the gazelle at incredible speed.  猎豹以惊人的速度追逐瞪羚。 |  Synonyms: hunting leopard | Antonyms: prey | Noun
