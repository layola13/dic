hispanicized:/hɪˈspænɪsaɪzd/
adjective
西班牙化的，受西班牙文化影响的
Having adopted or been influenced by Hispanic language or culture.
Many words in Filipino languages are hispanicized due to the Spanish colonial period.  许多菲律宾语言中的单词由于西班牙殖民时期而西班牙化了。
The city has a distinctly hispanicized feel. 这座城市有一种明显的西班牙化感觉。
synonyms: Hispanified, Latinized
antonyms: Anglicized
词性: adjective
