feldspar:/ˈfɛldˌspɑːr/ /ˈfɛlˌspɑːr/ | n. | 长石 | An abundant rock-forming mineral typically occurring as colorless or pale-colored crystals and consisting of aluminosilicates of potassium, sodium, and calcium. | Feldspar is a common component of many igneous rocks. 长石是许多火成岩的常见成分。|  Feldspar is used in the production of ceramics. 长石用于陶瓷生产。 | synonyms: albite, anorthite, orthoclase | antonyms: N/A | noun

feldspathic:/fɛldˈspæθɪk/ | adj. | 长石的 | Of, relating to, or containing feldspar. |  The feldspathic nature of the rock made it ideal for carving.  这块岩石的长石性质使其非常适合雕刻。 | The geologist identified the feldspathic composition of the sample. 地质学家确定了样品的长石成分。| synonyms: N/A | antonyms: N/A | adjective
