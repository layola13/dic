cutlases: /ˈkʌtləsɪz/
noun
弯刀（cutlass的复数）
plural of cutlass; short, broad, slightly curved swords.
The pirates brandished their cutlases, ready to board the ship. 海盗们挥舞着弯刀，准备登船。
The museum displayed a collection of antique cutlases. 博物馆展出了一批古董弯刀。
synonyms: sabers, scimitars, machetes
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a weapon, could consider "peace offering")
