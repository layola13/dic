accrediting: /əˈkrɛdɪtɪŋ/
accrediting |v. | 认证；授权；委派；相信 | Giving official authorization or approval to an institution or program, especially one in education.  Also, to believe something to be true or to attribute something to someone. | The agency is responsible for accrediting universities. 该机构负责认证大学。 | synonyms: certifying, licensing, endorsing, authorizing, validating, recognizing, commissioning, believing, attributing | antonyms: disqualifying, revoking, disapproving, rejecting, discrediting | Verb

accrediting: /əˈkrɛdɪtɪŋ/
accrediting |adj. | 认证的；授权的 | Having the power or authority to officially approve or recognize something, especially relating to education. |  The accrediting body renewed the school's certification. 认证机构更新了学校的认证。| synonyms: certifying, licensing, authorizing | antonyms: disqualifying, revoking | Adjective
