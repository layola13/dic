botany: /ˈbɒtəni/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtəni/ (US) | n. | 植物学 | The scientific study of plants, including their physiology, structure, genetics, ecology, distribution, classification, and economic importance. |  Botany is a fascinating field of study. 植物学是一个引人入胜的研究领域。| synonyms: plant science, phytology | antonyms: zoology | noun
