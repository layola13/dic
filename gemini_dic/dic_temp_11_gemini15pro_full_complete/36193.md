cog:/kɒɡ/ (UK) /kɑːɡ/ (US)| n.|齿轮; 嵌齿; 轮齿; (尤指组织、机器的)小齿轮;  (大组织或机构中的)小人物，无足轻重的人|A tooth on the rim of a wheel or gear. Also, a subordinate person in a large organization.|The small cogs in the machine kept everything running smoothly.  机器里的小齿轮使一切运转顺畅。|Synonyms: gear, wheel, tooth, component|Antonyms: whole, entirety|noun

cog:/kɒɡ/ (UK) /kɑːɡ/ (US)| v.|给…装齿轮; 骗; 作弊|(often followed by `up') manipulate in a fraudulent manner; to fix or load (dice) for cheating|He cogged the machine to make it run faster. 他给机器装上齿轮，使它运转得更快。|Synonyms: rig, fix, manipulate|Antonyms: break, disable|verb
