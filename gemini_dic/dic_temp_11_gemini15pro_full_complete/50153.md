denunciatory:/dɪˈnʌnsiəˌtɔːri/
adjective
谴责的，告发的
Expressing strong disapproval; condemning.
The politician delivered a denunciatory speech against his opponent's policies.  这位政治家发表了谴责其对手政策的演讲。
The article was denunciatory of the government's handling of the crisis. 这篇文章谴责了政府对危机的处理。
synonym: accusatory, critical, condemnatory, incriminatory
antonym: complimentary, laudatory, approving, supportive
词性: adjective
