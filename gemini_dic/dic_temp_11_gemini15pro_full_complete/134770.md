nonmercurial:/ˌnɒn.mɜːrˈkjʊəriəl/
adjective
非水银的; 不易激动的，稳定的
Not mercurial; not readily changing or fluctuating; stable.
Her nonmercurial temperament made her a reliable and steady leader. 她沉稳的性格使她成为一个可靠而坚定的领导者。
Synonyms: stable, steady, calm, even-tempered, phlegmatic, imperturbable, composed
Antonyms: mercurial, volatile, erratic, fickle, capricious
词性: adjective
