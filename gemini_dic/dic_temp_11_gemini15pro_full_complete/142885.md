out-of-the-box:/ˌaʊt əv ðə ˈbɑːks/ | adj. | 开箱即用的，现成的，非传统的，创新的 | Ready to use immediately without any further assembly or configuration; unconventional, creative. | This software is out-of-the-box and very user-friendly.  这个软件开箱即用，非常用户友好。| The company is known for its out-of-the-box marketing campaigns. 这家公司以其不落俗套的营销活动而闻名。 | 近义词: ready-made, off-the-shelf, innovative, creative | 反义词: conventional, traditional, custom-made | 词性: adjective

out-of-the-box:/ˌaʊt əv ðə ˈbɑːks/ | adv. | 以一种开箱即用的方式; 创新地 | In a way that is ready to use immediately or that is unconventional and creative. | They were thinking out-of-the-box to solve the complex problem. 他们正在跳出固有思维模式来解决这个复杂的问题。| She approached the project out-of-the-box, leading to some innovative solutions. 她以创新的方式处理这个项目，最终找到了一些新颖的解决方案。| 近义词: creatively, innovatively, unconventionally | 反义词: conventionally, traditionally | 词性: adverb
