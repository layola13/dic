londonism:/ˈlʌndənɪzəm/
noun
伦敦特色；伦敦腔；伦敦人的习性
A characteristic or peculiarity of London or its inhabitants.

Londonism can be seen in the city's unique blend of architecture and culture.
在伦敦独特的建筑和文化融合中，可以看到伦敦特色。

The Cockney accent is a well-known londonism.
伦敦腔是一种著名的伦敦特色。

Her londonism was evident in her love of theatre and her quick wit.
她对戏剧的热爱和敏捷的才思体现了她的伦敦人习性。


Synonyms: London characteristic, London peculiarity, London way, London style
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but could consider "provincialism" or characteristics of other cities)
词性: 名词
