curcumin:/ˈkɜːrkjʊmɪn/ | n. | 姜黄素 | A bright yellow chemical produced by some plants, notably turmeric. It is responsible for the yellow color of turmeric and is used as a food coloring and in some medicines. |  Curcumin has shown promise in treating inflammation. 姜黄素在治疗炎症方面显示出希望。| synonyms: diferuloylmethane | antonyms: N/A | noun
