heterozygoted: /ˌhɛtərəˈzaɪɡəʊtɪd/ (British) /ˌhɛtərəˈzaɪɡoʊtɪd/ (American)| adj.|杂合的|Having two different alleles of a particular gene or genes.|The plant is heterozygoted for flower color.|近义词: hybrid, heterozygous|反义词: homozygous, homozygoted|词性:形容词

词源:  From hetero- (different) + zygote (fertilized egg) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Greek *heteros* (other, different) and *zygotos* (yoked, paired).
