nonalliances:/ˌnɒnəˈlaɪənsɪz/
n.
非联盟,不结盟
The state of not being in alliance; absence of alliance or cooperation.
Many countries chose nonalliances during the Cold War. 许多国家在冷战期间选择不结盟。
The policy of nonalliances helped them avoid direct conflict. 不结盟政策帮助他们避免了直接冲突。
neutrality, nonalignment, independence
alliance, coalition, partnership
