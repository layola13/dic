fly-pasts:/ˈflaɪ pɑːsts/ /ˈflaɪ pæsts/ | n. | 低空飞行表演，掠过飞行 | A ceremonial flight of aircraft, typically low over a crowd or location. |  The air show featured impressive fly-pasts by the latest fighter jets.  航展上最新的战斗机进行了令人印象深刻的低空飞行表演。

The Queen's birthday celebrations included a fly-past by the Royal Air Force. 女王的生日庆典包括皇家空军的低空飞行表演。 | 近义词：flyover, flyby | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:  Compound of "fly" (verb) and "past" (adverb/preposition).  "Fly" traces back to Old English *flēogan, related to Old High German fliogan and Gothic fleugan. "Past" comes from Old French passé, past participle of passer "to pass," from Late Latin passare.
