nonpalatals:/nɒnˈpælətəlz/
| adj. | 非腭音的 | Sounds that are not produced with the tongue near or touching the hard palate.  These sounds are articulated elsewhere in the mouth, such as the lips, teeth, or back of the tongue. | The sounds /p/, /b/, /t/, /d/, /k/, and /g/ are examples of nonpalatal consonants in English. | 近义词: non-palatal | 反义词: palatal | 词性: adjective
