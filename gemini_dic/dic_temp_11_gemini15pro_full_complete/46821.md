danu:/ˈdɑːnuː/
n.
Danu, Dana
(Irish mythology) a primordial goddess associated with rivers and water; mother of the Tuatha Dé Danann
Danu is a key figure in Irish mythology. 达努是爱尔兰神话中的关键人物。
Synonym: Dana
Antonym: N/A (Deities typically lack direct antonyms)
Etymology: From Old Irish *Danu
