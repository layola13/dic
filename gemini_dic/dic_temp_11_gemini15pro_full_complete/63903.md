epipetalous: /ˌɛpɪˈpɛtələs/
adjective
花瓣上的；附生于花瓣上的
(of stamens) attached to the petals.
例句：The epipetalous stamens are a key feature of this flower.  这种花的主要特征是其附生于花瓣上的雄蕊。

例句：Epipetalous flowers are common in the primrose family.  报春花科植物中花瓣上着生的花很常见。


近义词: petaliferous (bearing petals), gamopetalous (having united petals - sometimes confused, but refers to petal fusion, not stamen attachment)
反义词:  No direct antonym, but "free" or "separate" could describe stamens not attached to petals.
词性: adjective
