cupric:/ˈkjuːprɪk/
adjective
铜的；含铜的
Containing or relating to copper, especially in its +2 oxidation state.
Cupric sulfate is a common chemical compound. 硫酸铜是一种常见的化合物。
近义词: copper(II)
反义词: cuprous
词性: adjective
