clamming-up:/ˈklæmɪŋ ʌp/
|phrasal verb|
|闭嘴，沉默，不愿说话|
|Becoming silent suddenly, refusing to speak.|
|He clammed up as soon as the police arrived. 他警察一来就闭口不言了。|
|She clammed up when I asked her about her past. 当我问起她的过去时，她就不说话了。|
|shut up, fall silent, refuse to talk|
|open up, speak freely, be talkative|
|verb phrase|
