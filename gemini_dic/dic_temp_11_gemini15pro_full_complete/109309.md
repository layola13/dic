lending:/ˈlɛndɪŋ/| n.|贷款，借贷|The act of giving something to someone with the expectation that it will be returned.  The bank specializes in lending to small businesses.|例句：The lending of money is a risky business.  借钱是一项有风险的业务。

例句：The library has a strict policy against the lending of rare books. 图书馆有严格的政策，禁止外借珍本图书。|近义词：loan, financing, credit|反义词:borrowing, repayment|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *lendyng, from Old English *lēnung, verbal noun from lǣnan (“to lend”), from Proto-Germanic *lainjaną (“to lend”).
