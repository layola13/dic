PHRASE->
cut back on = 减少，削减 (e.g., cut back on spending 减少开支)
cut back to = 减少到 (e.g., cut back to a skeleton staff 减少到最基本的员工)
cut back from = 从...减少 (e.g., cut back from 10 employees to 5 从10名员工减少到5名)
cut back sharply = 大幅削减 (e.g., cut back sharply on production 大幅削减产量)
cut back drastically =  大幅削减 (e.g., cut back drastically on expenses 大幅削减开支)
