cockeyednesses:/ˌkɒkˈaɪdnɪsɪz/
noun
斜视，歪眼，古怪；偏见
The state of being cockeyed, or having a squinting eye; astigmatism; an odd or eccentric quality.
His cockeyednesses made him look untrustworthy. 他的斜视让他看起来不值得信任。
synonyms: astigmatism, squint, eccentricity, peculiarity, oddity, strangeness, quirkiness
antonyms: normalcy, conventionality, straightness
n.


cockeyed:/ˌkɒkˈaɪd/
adjective
斜视的，歪眼的；古怪的，偏见的
Having a squinting eye; astigmatic; odd or eccentric.

synonyms: astigmatic, squinting, eccentric, peculiar, odd, strange, quirky
antonyms: normal, conventional, straight
