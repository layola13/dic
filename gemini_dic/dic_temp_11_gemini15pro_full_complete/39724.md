consimilitude:/ˌkɒnsɪˈmɪlɪtjuːd/ /ˌkɑːnsɪˈmɪlɪtuːd/| n. |相似,相似性,类似| likeness, resemblance; similarity in nature or appearance. | The consimilitude between the two paintings was striking.  这两幅画的相似之处令人吃惊。|近义词：similarity, resemblance, likeness, analogy, similitude|反义词：difference, dissimilarity, disparity, unlikeness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *consimilitudo, from Latin consimilis (“very similar”), from com- (“together, very”) + similis (“like”).
