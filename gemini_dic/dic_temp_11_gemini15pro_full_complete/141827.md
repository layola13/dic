orbless:/ˈɔːrblɪs/| adj.|无球的，无天体的|Lacking orbs; without orbs.  This refers to a celestial body or something spherical.  It can also be used metaphorically to describe something lacking a central focus or guiding principle.|The orbless sky seemed strangely empty after the eclipse.|近义词：vacant, empty, void|反义词:spherical, globular|词性:形容词
