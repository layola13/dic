alois:/ˈɑːlɔɪs/ /əˈlɔɪs/| n. |阿洛伊斯（男子名）|A male given name, of German origin, a variant of Aloysius.  It is derived from the Old High German names Chlodowig and Ludwig, composed of the elements *hlūd ("famous, loud") and *wīg ("battle, fight").| Alois was a kind and gentle man. 阿洛伊斯是一个善良温柔的人。| Alois was known for his artistic talent. 阿洛伊斯以他的艺术天赋而闻名。|近义词: Aloysius, Louis |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
