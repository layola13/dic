hardboard:/ˈhɑːrdbɔːrd/| n. |硬纸板，纤维板|A type of board made from compressed wood fibers.  It's dense, strong, and inexpensive.|The back of the cupboard is made of hardboard.  橱柜的背面是用硬纸板做的.|近义词：fiberboard, pressedboard|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps plywood for a different but similar material)|noun
