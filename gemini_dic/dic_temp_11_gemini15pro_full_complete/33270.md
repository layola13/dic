chrysoberyl: /ˈkrɪsəˌberəl/ | n. |金绿宝石|A yellowish-green or brownish-green gemstone variety of beryllium aluminate. It is known for its hardness and chatoyancy (cat's eye effect) in some varieties.  |例句：The chrysoberyl cat's eye ring sparkled in the light. 金绿猫眼戒指在灯光下闪闪发光。

例句：Chrysoberyl is a valuable and durable gemstone. 金绿宝石是一种珍贵且耐用的宝石。 |近义词：gem, gemstone, jewel |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chrysobéril*, from Ancient Greek χρυσόβηρυλλος (khrusóbērullos, “golden beryl”), from χρυσός (khrusós, “gold”) + βήρυλλος (bḗrullos, “beryl”).
