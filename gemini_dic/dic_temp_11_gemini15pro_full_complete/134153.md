nonimpressionistic:/ˌnɒnɪmˈprɛʃənɪstɪk/
adjective
非印象派的
Not relating to or characteristic of impressionism, especially in art or literature.
Her nonimpressionistic style focused on realistic portrayal of the subject. 她的非印象派风格专注于对主题的现实描绘。
Near Antonyms: impressionistic
Antonyms: impressionist
