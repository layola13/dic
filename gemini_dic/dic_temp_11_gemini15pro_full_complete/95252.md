incanescent:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsənt/
adjective
白炽的；发出白热光的；辉煌的；热情洋溢的
emitting light as a result of being heated; shining brightly; passionate and eloquent.
The incandescent bulb lit up the room. 白炽灯照亮了房间。
The speaker's incandescent delivery captivated the audience. 演讲者热情洋溢的演讲吸引了观众。
Synonyms:  glowing, luminous, radiant, brilliant, fervent
Antonyms:  dull, dark, dim, extinguished, apathetic
词性: adjective
