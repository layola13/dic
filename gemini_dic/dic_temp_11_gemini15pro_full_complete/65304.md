eudemonistic:/ˌjuːdɪˈmɒnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 享乐主义的，快乐主义的 |  Relating to or based on the ethical theory that happiness or well-being is the chief good. |  He lived a eudemonistic life, pursuing pleasure and avoiding pain.  他过着享乐主义的生活，追求快乐，避免痛苦。 | Synonyms: hedonistic, Epicurean | Antonyms: ascetic, self-denying | Adjective

eudemonism:/ˌjuːdɪˈmɒnɪzəm/ | n. | 享乐主义，快乐主义 | The ethical theory that happiness or well-being is the chief good. | Eudemonism is a philosophy that emphasizes the importance of happiness. 快乐主义是一种强调幸福重要性的哲学。 | Synonyms: hedonism, Epicureanism | Antonyms: asceticism, self-denial | Noun
