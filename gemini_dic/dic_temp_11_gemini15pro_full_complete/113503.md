What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? = 她晚饭做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been studying at university? = 他在大学里一直在学什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last weekend? = 你上周末做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was he thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间他在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病之前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been working on before she changed jobs? =  她换工作之前一直在做什么？


Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer? = 到明年夏天他们将建成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
