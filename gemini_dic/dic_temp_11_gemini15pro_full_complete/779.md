absorbable:/əbˈzɔːrbəbl/ /əbˈsɔːr-/
adjective
可吸收的;能吸收的
Capable of being absorbed.
Sutures used in surgery must be absorbable.  外科手术中使用的缝合线必须是可吸收的。

absorbable:/əbˈzɔːrbəbl/ /əbˈsɔːr-/
adjective
易理解的;易消化的
Easy to understand or assimilate.
The information presented in the lecture was easily absorbable.  讲座中提供的信息很容易理解。

近义词:digestible, assimilable, permeable
反义词:nonabsorbable, indigestible, impermeable
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From absorb + -able.  Ultimately from Latin absorbere.
