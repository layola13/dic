descender:/dɪˈsendər/| n.|下伸部（字母）|The part of a letter that extends below the baseline, as in the letters g, j, p, q, and y.  |The descender of the letter 'y' can sometimes be difficult to write neatly. 字母“y”的下伸部有时难以写得整齐。|近义词:tail (informal)|反义词:ascender|词性:noun
