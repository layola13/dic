adorablenesses:/əˈdɔːrəblənəsɪz/
n.
可爱之处；令人喜爱之处
The quality of being adorable; extreme cuteness or lovableness.
Her puppy's adorablenesses were endless, from its tiny paws to its wagging tail.
它的小狗从它的小爪子到摇尾巴都可爱极了。
synonyms: charms, sweetness, loveliness, cuteness
antonyms: unpleasantness, ugliness, repulsiveness
noun
