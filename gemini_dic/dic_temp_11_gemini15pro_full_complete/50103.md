dentilave:/ˈdɛntɪˌleɪv/
n.
齿状装饰，齿状突起
A series of small, tooth-like projections forming an ornamental molding or edge.
The dentilave along the roofline added a touch of elegance. 屋顶边缘的齿状装饰增添了一丝优雅。
近义词:toothing, dentil
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "smooth" in a descriptive context)
词性:noun
