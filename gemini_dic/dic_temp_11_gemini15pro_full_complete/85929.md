headliner:/ˈhedˌlaɪnər/| n.|主要演员；领衔主演；头条新闻；|A performer who receives top billing, as at a concert or nightclub.  A major news story.|The Rolling Stones were the headliners at the festival. 滚石乐队是这个音乐节的领衔主演。|Synonyms: star, lead, principal, main attraction|Antonyms: supporting act, opening act, minor act|noun
