adeem:/əˈdiːm/| adj.|被收回的，被没收的；被赎回的|Taken away as a forfeit or penalty; taken back; revoked, as a grant or privilege.  This word is often used in legal contexts.|The king adeemed the lands from the traitor. 国王从叛徒手中收回了土地。|Synonyms: revoked, withdrawn, rescinded, canceled, annulled|Antonyms: granted, bestowed, awarded|adjective

adeem:/əˈdiːm/| v.|收回，撤销（遗产中的特定遗赠）|To revoke or take away (a legacy or bequest) by satisfying it during one's lifetime, usually by giving an equivalent gift.  Primarily used in legal contexts related to wills and estates.|The father adeemed the car from his son's inheritance by gifting him an equivalent amount of money before his death.  父亲在去世前给了儿子一笔等值的钱，从而从儿子的遗产中收回了汽车。|Synonyms: revoke, withdraw, cancel, annul, rescind|Antonyms: grant, bestow, award, bequeath|verb
