claver:/ˈklævər/ (US), /ˈkleɪvə(r)/ (UK)| vi. n.|闲聊，空谈；胡扯；闲谈，饶舌|to talk idly or boastfully; to chat or gossip. |例句：They spent the afternoon clavering about nothing in particular. 他们在下午闲聊了一些无关紧要的事情。|近义词：chat, chatter, gossip, prattle|反义词: be silent, be quiet|verb
