non-Darwinians:/ˌnɒn dɑːˈwɪniənz/ | n. | 非达尔文主义者 |  People who do not accept the theory of evolution by natural selection as proposed by Charles Darwin.  Those who hold alternative theories of evolution or creationism. |  The non-Darwinians at the conference presented various alternative theories.  会议上的非达尔文主义者提出了各种替代理论。 |  creationists, anti-evolutionists | evolutionists, Darwinians | noun
