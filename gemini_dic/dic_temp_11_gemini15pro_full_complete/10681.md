approximating:/əˈprɒksɪmeɪtɪŋ/
verb
接近，近似
coming close to a particular number, amount, or condition, but not exactly the same
The total cost is approximating $1 million.  总成本接近100万美元。
近义词: approaching, nearing, estimating
反义词: diverging, differing
词性: present participle of approximate

approximate:/əˈprɒksɪmeɪt/
adjective
大概的，近似的
almost correct or accurate, but not completely so
The approximate cost of the project is $1 million.  该项目的大概成本是100万美元。
近义词: rough, estimated, close
反义词: exact, precise
词性: adjective

approximate:/əˈprɒksɪmeɪt/
verb
接近，近似
come close or be similar to something in quality, nature, or quantity
His description approximated the truth. 他的描述接近事实。
近义词: approach, near, resemble
反义词: differ, diverge
词性: verb

词源: From Late Latin approximatus, past participle of approximare "to approach," from Latin ad- "to" + proximare "to draw near," from proximus "nearest."
