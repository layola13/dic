accustomednesses:/əˈkʌstəmdnɪsɪz/
n.
习惯性，适应性
The state of being accustomed to something; habitualness.
His accustomednesses to the city life made it hard for him to adapt to the countryside. 他对城市生活的习惯让他很难适应乡村生活。

近义词: habits, practices, routines, customs
反义词: unfamiliarity, strangeness, newness
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
