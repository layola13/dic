narwhales:/ˈnɑːrˌweɪlz/ (US), /ˈnɑːˌwɔːlz/ (UK)| n.|独角鲸|A medium-sized toothed whale that possesses a large "tusk" from a protruding canine tooth, mostly found in Arctic waters.|Narwhales are found in the Arctic Ocean. 独角鲸生活在北冰洋。| Narwhales can dive to incredible depths. 独角鲸可以潜到令人难以置信的深度。|Synonyms: unicorn whale, narwal|Antonyms: N/A|noun
