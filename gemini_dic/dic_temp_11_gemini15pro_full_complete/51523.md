detritus:/dɪˈtraɪtəs/
n.
废物，碎屑，残渣
waste or debris of any kind.
The detritus of a hurricane littered the beach.  飓风的残骸散落在海滩上。

detrituses:/dɪˈtraɪtəsiːz/
n.
废物，碎屑，残渣（detritus 的复数形式，较少使用）
waste or debris of any kind (plural form, less common).
The street cleaners swept up the detrituses left by the parade.  清洁工清扫了游行队伍留下的垃圾。

近义词: debris, waste, remains, rubble, wreckage
反义词: cleanliness, purity
