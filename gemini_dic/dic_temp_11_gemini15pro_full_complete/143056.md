outblusters:/ˌaʊtˈblʌstərz/ | n. |  大声斥责者；胜过咆哮的人 |  Those who outdo others in blustering; those who shout louder than others.  This word is often used figuratively to describe someone who overpowers others with their loud and aggressive speech. | The debate became a contest of who could outbluster the other. 这场辩论变成了一场看谁能比对方更大声叫嚣的比赛。  | synonyms: ranters, shouters, bullies | antonyms:  listeners, negotiators, mediators | noun

outbluster:/ˌaʊtˈblʌstər/ | v. |  胜过咆哮；大声斥责 |  To shout louder than; to intimidate or overwhelm with noisy, aggressive speech. |  He tried to outbluster his opponent, but it didn't work. 他试图用咆哮压倒对手，但没有奏效。 | synonyms:  outshout, drown out, cow | antonyms:  yield, submit, concede | verb
