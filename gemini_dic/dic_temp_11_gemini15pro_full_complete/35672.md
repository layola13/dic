cobblers:/ˈkɒbləz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːblərz/ (US)| n.|补鞋匠; 鞋匠; 胡说; 果馅饼|A person who mends shoes. Also used to express disbelief or disagreement, similar to "nonsense."  A dish of fruit baked with a thick, biscuit-like topping.|例句：The cobblers in the town were known for their quality work. 镇上的鞋匠以其高质量的工作而闻名。

例句： "Cobblers!" he exclaimed, "That's not true at all!" “胡说！”他喊道，“那根本不是真的！”

例句： My grandmother makes the best apple cobblers. 我的祖母做的苹果馅饼最好吃。

近义词：shoemaker, shoe-mender (for the person); nonsense, rubbish, balderdash (for the expression of disbelief); crumble, crisp (for the dish) |反义词:(No direct antonym for the person or the dish)  Truth, fact (for the expression of disbelief)|词性:名词


cobblers:/ˈkɒbləz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːblərz/ (US)| int.|胡说八道！|Used to express disbelief or disagreement.|例句："He said he could fly." "Cobblers!" “他说他会飞。” “胡说八道！”

例句："I won the lottery!" "Cobblers! You're always joking." “我中了彩票！” “胡说！你总是开玩笑。”

近义词：nonsense, rubbish, balderdash|反义词:Truth, fact|词性:感叹词
