appointers:/əˈpɔɪntərz/| n. |任命者；指定人|People who have the power or authority to appoint someone to a position or office.  Those who select or designate individuals for roles or tasks.|The appointers chose a new CEO. 任命者选择了一位新的首席执行官。|Synonyms: nominators, selectors, designators|Antonyms:  discharge, dismiss (verbs related to removing someone from a position)|noun
