matweed:/ˈmætˌwiːd/| n. |野麦草，垫状草|A type of coarse grass that grows in dense mats, typically found in coastal areas or sandy soils. It's often used for erosion control.|The matweed held the sand dunes together.  野麦草把沙丘固定在一起。|近义词：beachgrass, sandgrass, dune grass|反义词: N/A|noun
