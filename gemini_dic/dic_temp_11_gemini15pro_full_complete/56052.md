docentship: /ˈdoʊsənʃɪp/ | n. |  讲解员的职位；讲解员的工作 | The position or work of a docent, especially in a museum or other cultural institution.  | She began her docentship at the museum after retiring from teaching.  她退休后在博物馆开始了讲解员的工作。| 近义词:  guideship,  | 反义词: N/A | 词性:  noun

Etymology: From docent + -ship. Docent comes from the present participle of the Latin verb docēre, meaning "to teach."
