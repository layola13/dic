coridine:/ˈkɔːrɪdiːn/| n. |柯林定; 2,4,6-三甲基吡啶|A colorless liquid alkaloid, C₈H₁₁N, derived from coal tar and bone oil and used as a solvent and in organic synthesis. It is also known as 2,4,6-collidine.|Coridine is a useful solvent in organic chemistry. 柯林定是有机化学中一种有用的溶剂。|Coridine was used in the synthesis of a new pharmaceutical compound. 柯林定被用于合成一种新的药物化合物。|近义词:collidine, trimethylpyridine|反义词:None|词性:noun
