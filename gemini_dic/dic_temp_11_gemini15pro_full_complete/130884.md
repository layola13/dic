nonaromatically:/ˌnɒnærəˈmætɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnærəˈmætɪkli/
| adv. | 非芳香地，不具有芳香性地 |  Not exhibiting the properties of an aromatic compound, especially not having the characteristic stability and reactivity of an aromatic ring system. | The compound reacted nonaromatically under these conditions. 该化合物在这些条件下非芳香地反应. | nonaromatic, non-aromatic  | aromatically | adverb
