birdlike:/ˈbɜːrdlaɪk/| adj.|像鸟的；鸟一样的|Resembling a bird in appearance, behavior, or sound.|例句：Her birdlike movements were graceful and captivating. 她鸟一样的动作优雅迷人。

例句：He had a birdlike way of tilting his head when he listened. 他倾听时像鸟一样歪着头。|近义词：avian, birdish|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using phrases like "un-birdlike," "mammalian," or describing specific contrasting traits)|词性: adjective
