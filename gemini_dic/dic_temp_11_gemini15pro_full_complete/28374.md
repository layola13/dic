carbanil:/ˈkɑːrbənɪl/ |n.| 苯氨基甲酰基 |A univalent radical derived from carbamic acid by loss of a hydroxyl group, –CONHPh. |The carbanil group can be introduced to alcohols to form urethanes.| 近义词: phenylcarbamoyl | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From carb(amic) + anil(ine) + -yl.
