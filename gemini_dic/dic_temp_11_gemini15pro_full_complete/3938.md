agro-ecological:/ˌæɡroʊˌiːkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
农业生态的
Relating to the study of ecological processes applied to agricultural production systems.  It seeks to optimize the interactions between plants, animals, humans and the environment while addressing the social and economic aspects of food systems.
The farm is transitioning to agro-ecological practices.  这个农场正在向农业生态实践转型。
The agro-ecological approach considers the farm as an ecosystem. 农业生态方法将农场视为一个生态系统。
近义词:sustainable agriculture, eco-farming, biodynamic
反义词:conventional agriculture, industrial agriculture
词性:adjective

agro-ecological:/ˌæɡroʊˌiːkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
noun
农业生态学
The study of ecological processes applied to agricultural production systems.
Agro-ecological principles are key to sustainable food production. 农业生态学原则是可持续粮食生产的关键。
The course covers the basics of agro-ecological farming. 这门课程涵盖了农业生态耕作的基础知识。
近义词:sustainable agriculture, eco-farming, biodynamic
反义词:conventional agriculture, industrial agriculture
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
agro- (from Greek agrós, "field") + ecological (from ecology, from Greek oikos, "house" + -logy, "study of")
