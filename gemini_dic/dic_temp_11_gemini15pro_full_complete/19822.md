bimetallist:/baɪˈmɛtəlɪst/ | n. |金银复本位主义者；支持金银复本位制的人|A person who advocates bimetallism (a monetary system in which the government defines the value of its currency in terms of both gold and silver).|The bimetallists argued that a currency backed by both gold and silver would be more stable than one based on just gold.|Synonyms: silverite (often used derisively), double standard advocate |Antonyms: gold standard advocate, monometallist| noun

bimetallism:/baɪˈmɛtəlɪzəm/ | n. |金银复本位制|A monetary system in which the government defines the value of its currency in terms of both gold and silver. |During the late 19th century, bimetallism was a major political issue in the United States.|Synonyms: double standard |Antonyms: gold standard, monometallism| noun
