mclennan:/məˈklɛnən/ | n. | 麦克伦南（姓氏） | A Scottish surname, derived from the Gaelic Mac Gille Fhinnein, meaning "son of the fair-haired servant".  It's associated with a prominent Canadian anthropologist, John Ferguson McLennan. | John McLennan wrote several influential books on kinship and social evolution.  约翰·麦克伦南写了几本关于亲属关系和社会进化的有影响力的书籍。| 近义词: (无，因为是姓氏) | 反义词: (无，因为是姓氏) | 词性: 名词
