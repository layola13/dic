noneconomies:/nɒnɪˈkɒnəmiz/ | n. | 不经济;规模不经济; |  A situation or condition where larger scale operations do not lead to lower costs per unit, or where there are disadvantages associated with increasing size or scale. | The company experienced noneconomies of scale after merging with its competitor, leading to higher production costs. 这家公司在与竞争对手合并后出现了规模不经济的现象，导致生产成本上升。

The sprawling nature of the empire led to noneconomies in administration and communication. 帝国的扩张导致了行政和沟通上的不经济。 | 近义词: diseconomies of scale | 反义词: economies of scale | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "economies" (plural of "economy," from Old French "economie" and ultimately from Greek "oikonomia," meaning "household management").
