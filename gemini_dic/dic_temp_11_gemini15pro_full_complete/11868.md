arrived:/əˈraɪvd/ | v. |到达，抵达|reached a destination|例句：We arrived at the station just in time. 我们刚好及时到达了车站。

例句：The package arrived yesterday. 包裹昨天到了。

近义词：reached, appeared, came, showed up|反义词：departed, left|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *arriver (“to come to shore, to land, to arrive”), from Vulgar Latin *arripare, from Latin ad- (“to”) + ripa (“bank, shore”).

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He arrives early every day. 他每天都到得很早。
The train arrives at 8:00 am. 火车早上8点到达。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
They are arriving soon. 他们很快就到了。
The guests are arriving now. 客人们现在正在到达。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The plane has arrived safely. 飞机已安全抵达。
She has arrived at a decision. 她已经做出了决定。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
We have been arriving late all week. 我们整周都迟到。 (不常见用法，通常用现在完成时)
They have been arriving in small groups all day.  他们一整天都一小群一小群地陆续到达。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
We arrived late last night. 我们昨晚很晚才到。
He arrived at the party at 9 pm. 他晚上9点到达派对。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was cooking dinner when they arrived. 他们到达时我正在做饭。
She was reading a book when the visitor arrived.  客人到来时，她正在看书。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
They had already arrived when I got there. 我到那里时他们已经到了。
The rain had stopped before we arrived. 我们到达之前雨已经停了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
We had been waiting for hours when the bus finally arrived.  我们等了好几个小时，公共汽车终于来了。
She had been working on the project for weeks when the deadline arrived.  当截止日期到来时，她已经在这个项目上工作了好几周了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
They will arrive tomorrow. 他们明天到达。
The train will arrive at 10:00 am. 火车上午10点到达。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be arriving at the airport at 3 pm. 我下午3点会到达机场。
He will be arriving later tonight. 他今晚晚些时候会到。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, they will have arrived in London.  下周之前，他们将抵达伦敦。
By the time you read this, I will have arrived home.  当你读到这封信的时候，我应该已经到家了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, she will have been living here for two years since she arrived. 到明年，自从她到达以来，她将已经在这里住了两年了。（更常见用法）
By the time the guests arrive, we will have been cooking for hours.  到客人到达的时候，我们将已经做了几个小时的饭了。


PHRASE->
have arrived = 已经到达
just arrived = 刚刚到达
newly arrived = 新到达的
safe arrival = 安全到达
arrive at a decision/conclusion = 做出决定/得出结论
