baff:/bæf/| v.| 使…困惑；使…为难；阻碍；挫败|To perplex or bewilder; to frustrate or check.|The complex instructions completely baffed me.|近义词：confuse, bewilder, puzzle, perplex, stump, foil, thwart|反义词：clarify, enlighten, help, aid|词性:动词
词源:Origin uncertain; perhaps related to Low German baffen 'to bark'.
