outpaces:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪsɪz/
verb
超过，胜过
to go faster, do better, or develop more quickly than someone or something else.
The company's sales growth outpaced its competitors. 该公司的销售增长超过了竞争对手。
China's economic growth has outpaced that of many developed countries. 中国的经济增长超过了许多发达国家。
近义词: surpasses, exceeds, overtakes, outruns
反义词: lags behind, trails, falls behind
词性: verb

词源:  "out-" (meaning "beyond, surpassing") + "pace" (from Old French "pas," ultimately from Latin "passus" - a step)
