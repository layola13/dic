antiministerial:/ˌæntiˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəl/
adjective
反政府的；反对部长的
Opposed to the policies of a particular government or minister.
The crowd was chanting antiministerial slogans.  人群高呼反政府口号。
Synonyms: dissident, rebellious, oppositional, subversive, insurgent
Antonyms: pro-government, ministerial, loyalist
词性: 形容词
