gigabit:/ˈɡɪɡəbɪt/ | n. | 千兆位；吉比特 | A unit of information equal to one thousand million (10⁹) bits or 1000 megabits.  It is often used to measure data transfer rates. |  The internet connection offers speeds of up to one gigabit per second.  该互联网连接提供高达每秒1千兆位的速度。

The file is several gigabits in size.  这个文件有几个G的大小。| 近义词: Gbit | 反义词: kilobit | 词性: noun
