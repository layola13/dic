gabler:/ˈɡeɪblər/| n.| 健谈者，爱说话的人|A person who talks a lot, especially about unimportant things.|He's such a gabler, he can talk for hours about nothing. 他真是个健谈的人，可以滔滔不绝地谈论几个小时的废话。|近义词：chatterbox, talker, conversationalist|反义词：quiet person, introvert|词性: 名词

词源：From German *Gabler*, from *Gabel* "fork," referring to someone who talks incessantly, as if using a fork to shovel words into their mouth. 源自德语 *Gabler*，来自 *Gabel* “叉子”，指的是不停说话的人，好像用叉子把话塞进嘴里。
