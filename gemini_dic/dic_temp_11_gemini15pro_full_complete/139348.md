obscurantism:/əbˈskjʊərənˌtɪzəm/
|n.|蒙昧主义，愚民政策|The deliberate suppression or distortion of knowledge and information.|The regime used obscurantism to control the population, limiting access to education and independent media.  该政权利用愚民政策来控制民众，限制他们接受教育和接触独立媒体。|obscurity, ignorance, suppression of knowledge|enlightenment, clarity, education
|noun|

obscurantist:/əbˈskjʊərənˌtɪst/
|adj.|蒙昧主义的；|Relating to or characterized by obscurantism.|The group's obscurantist beliefs hindered scientific progress.  该团体的蒙昧主义信仰阻碍了科学进步。|backward, reactionary|progressive, enlightened
|adjective|
