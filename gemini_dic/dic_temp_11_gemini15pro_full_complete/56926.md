doth:/dʌθ/
verb
做，进行，产生
Archaic third-person singular present form of do.  It is now used primarily in poetic or religious contexts.
She doth protest too much, methinks. 我看她抗议得太多了。
近义词: does, performs, executes, acts
反义词: refrains from, avoids, neglects
词性: verb (archaic)

词源:
From Middle English *doþ*, from Old English *dēþ*, third-person singular present indicative of *dōn* (“to do”). Cognate with West Frisian *docht*, Dutch *doet*, German *tut*.
