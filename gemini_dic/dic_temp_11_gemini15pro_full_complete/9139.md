antinational:/ˌæntiˈnæʃənəl/
adjective
反国家的，危害国家的
Opposing one's own nation or its interests.
His antinational activities led to his arrest. 他的反国家活动导致他被捕。
近义词: unpatriotic, treasonous, subversive, seditious
反义词: patriotic, nationalistic, loyal
词性: adjective
