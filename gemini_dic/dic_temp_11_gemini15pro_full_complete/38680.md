condemn: /kənˈdɛm/ | vt. |谴责，指责；宣判；判刑；迫使…接受困境 | to express strong disapproval of; to pronounce guilty; to sentence to a punishment; to force into an undesirable state | The judge condemned the thief to five years in prison. 法官判处小偷五年徒刑。| synonyms: denounce, censure, convict, sentence | antonyms: praise, commend, acquit, exonerate | verb

condemn: /kənˈdɛm/ | vt. |宣布…不适于使用；宣布…为危楼 | to officially declare (something, especially a building) to be unfit for use | The building was condemned after the fire. 这栋楼在火灾后被宣布为危楼。| synonyms: declare unfit, proscribe | antonyms: approve, authorize | verb
