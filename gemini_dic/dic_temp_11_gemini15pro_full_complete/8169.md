anti-cynical:/ˌæntiˈsɪnɪkl/| adj.|反愤世嫉俗的；非愤世嫉俗的|Opposed to or rejecting cynicism; characterized by hopefulness and a belief in the goodness of human nature.  Not exhibiting the qualities of a cynic, such as pessimism, distrust, and scorn.|Her anti-cynical view of the world was refreshing. 她对世界不愤世嫉俗的看法令人耳目一新。| The charity worker maintained an anti-cynical attitude despite witnessing so much suffering.  尽管目睹了如此多的苦难，慈善工作者仍然保持着不愤世嫉俗的态度。| 近义词:optimistic, hopeful, positive, trusting|反义词:cynical, pessimistic, distrustful, scornful|词性:adjective

anti-cynical:/ˌæntiˈsɪnɪkl/| n.|反愤世嫉俗者；非愤世嫉俗者 (rarely used as a noun)|A person who opposes or rejects cynicism. |While not a common usage, one could say she was an anti-cynical in a world full of pessimists. 虽然不常用，但可以说在一个充满悲观主义者的世界里，她是一个反愤世嫉俗的人。|He considered himself an anti-cynical, always looking for the good in people. 他认为自己是一个反愤世嫉俗的人，总是寻找人们身上的优点。|近义词:optimist, idealist|反义词:cynic, pessimist|词性:noun (rare)
