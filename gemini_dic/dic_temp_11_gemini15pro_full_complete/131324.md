nonchangeable:/ˌnɒnˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/ |adj.|不可改变的，不变的|Not able to be changed; fixed.|The rules of the game are nonchangeable.  比赛规则是不可改变的.|近义词：immutable, unalterable, fixed, permanent, constant|反义词：changeable, variable, mutable, alterable, inconstant|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- + changeable.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "changeable" comes from the Old French "changier" meaning "to change."
