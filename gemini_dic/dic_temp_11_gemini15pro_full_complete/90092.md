hostler:/ˈhɒstlə(r) (UK), ˈhɑːstlər (US)| n.|马夫；旅店老板；（大学的）宿舍管理员|A person who takes care of horses, especially at an inn or stable. Also, a person who manages a hostel or dormitory.|The hostler led the horses to the stable. 马夫把马牵到马厩里。|The university hostler was very strict about quiet hours. 大学宿舍管理员对安静时间的要求非常严格。|Synonyms: stableman, groom, innkeeper, manager|Antonyms: guest, lodger, boarder|noun

hostler:/ˈhɒstlə(r) (UK), ˈhɑːstlər (US)| n.|骗子；敲诈者|A person who deceives or swindles someone, especially to obtain money.|The hostler cheated the tourists out of their money. 骗子骗走了游客的钱。|Be careful of hostlers in crowded tourist areas. 在拥挤的旅游区要小心骗子。|Synonyms: swindler, con artist, cheat, fraudster|Antonyms: honest person, philanthropist|noun
