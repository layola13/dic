modulo:/ˈmɒdjʊləʊ/ (UK), /ˈmɑːdʒʊloʊ/ (US)| adj. prep. n.|模; 模数; 以...为模;|A mathematical operation that finds the remainder after division of one number by another (called the modulus of the operation).|

例句：
15 modulo 4 is 3.
15 模 4 等于 3。

We can express this as 7 = 2 (modulo 5).
我们可以将其表示为 7 = 2 (以 5 为模)。


近义词：mod, remainder|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, preposition, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin _modulus_ ("small measure," "unit of measure"), diminutive of _modus_ ("measure," "manner," "fashion," "method").
