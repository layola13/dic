berryless:/ˈberɪləs/| adj.|无果实的，不结果实的|Lacking berries; without berries.|The berryless shrub was just ornamental. 这棵无果实的灌木只是用来装饰的。|Example: The unusually cold spring resulted in berryless bushes this year. 今年异常寒冷的春天导致灌木不结果实。|Synonyms: barren, fruitless, infertile|Antonyms: fruitful, fertile, berry-laden|adjective
