outlearn:/ˌaʊtˈlɜːrn/
verb
超越学习，学习速度超过
To surpass in learning; to learn faster or more effectively than.
The young apprentice quickly outlearned his master.  年轻的学徒很快就在学习上超过了他的师傅。
The new AI system is outlearning human experts in certain fields. 新的AI系统在某些领域的学习速度超过了人类专家。
surpass, excel, outstrip
lag behind, fall behind
v.
