nontolerableness:/nɒnˈtɒlərəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
不可容忍性；无法忍受
The state or quality of being intolerable; inability to be tolerated.
The nontolerableness of the working conditions led to a strike.  工作条件的不可容忍性导致了罢工。
近义词:  intolerability, unacceptability, insufferability
反义词: tolerability, acceptability
词性: noun

Etymology:
From non- + tolerable + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "tolerable" comes from the Latin "tolerabilis," meaning "able to be borne," and "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives.
