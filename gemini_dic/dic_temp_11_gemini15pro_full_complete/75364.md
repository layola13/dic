frothy:/ˈfrɒθi/ (UK), /ˈfrɔːθi/ (US)| adj.|泡沫的，起泡的；（饮料）有泡沫的；轻浮的，肤浅的|Covered with or resembling froth; full of or producing froth; (of a liquid) foamy; frivolous and superficial.|

例句：
The cappuccino was topped with a thick layer of frothy milk.
卡布奇诺咖啡上面覆盖着一层厚厚的奶泡。

The beer had a nice frothy head.
这杯啤酒有一层漂亮的泡沫。

Her writing style is rather frothy and lacks depth.
她的写作风格相当肤浅，缺乏深度。

The politician delivered a frothy speech full of empty promises.
这位政客发表了一场充满空洞承诺的浮夸演讲。


近义词：foamy, bubbly, airy, frivolous, superficial|
反义词：flat, still, serious, profound|
词性: adjective
