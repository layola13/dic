fat-cat:/ˈfætˌkæt/| n. |有钱有势的人；贪婪的资本家|A wealthy and powerful person, especially one seen as greedy or exploitative. |The article criticized the fat cats of Wall Street. 这篇文章批评了华尔街的那些贪婪的资本家。|Synonyms: tycoon, plutocrat, bigwig|Antonyms: pauper, have-not|Noun

fat-cat:/ˈfætˌkæt/| adj. |（尤指生意人）养尊处优的，饱食无忧的|(especially of businessmen) wealthy and privileged| They led a fat-cat lifestyle, with luxury cars and expensive holidays. 他们过着养尊处优的生活，拥有豪车和奢华的假期。|Synonyms: privileged, pampered, well-heeled|Antonyms: impoverished, struggling|Adjective
