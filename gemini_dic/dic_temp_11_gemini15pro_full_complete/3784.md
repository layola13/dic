aglimmer:/əˈɡlɪmər/| adj.| 微光的，闪烁的| Gleaming faintly; glimmering. | The city lights were aglimmer in the distance. 城市的灯光在远处闪烁。|近义词：glimmering, shimmering, sparkling, twinkling|反义词:dull, dark|词性:形容词
Etymology: From a- + glimmer.  "A-" acts as an intensifier.  Glimmer itself comes from Middle English *glimeren, frequentative of glimen "to shine," probably of imitative origin.
