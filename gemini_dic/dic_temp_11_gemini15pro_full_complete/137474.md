nontolerant:/nɒnˈtɒlərənt/ /nɑːnˈtɑːlərənt/| adj.|不宽容的，没有耐性的|Not tolerant; unwilling or unable to tolerate or endure.  Lacking tolerance or respect for beliefs or practices differing from or conflicting with one's own.|She was nontolerant of any views that differed from her own. 她不能容忍任何与她不同的观点。|近义词：intolerant, bigoted, prejudiced|反义词: tolerant, accepting, open-minded|词性:形容词
