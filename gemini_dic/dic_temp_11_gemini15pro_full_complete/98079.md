inquiring:/ɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋ/| adj.|好奇的，探究的，爱打听的|Showing an interest in learning new things; questioning.|例句：She gave him an inquiring look. 她好奇地看了他一眼。|近义词：curious, inquisitive, questioning, probing|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, uninterested|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *enquiring, present participle of enqueren, from Anglo-Norman enquerre, from Old French enquerre (“to inquire”), from Latin inquirere (“to seek into, search for, investigate”), from in- (“in, into”) + quaerere (“to seek, ask”).
