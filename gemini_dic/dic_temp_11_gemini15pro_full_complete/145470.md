overclassifies:/ˌoʊvərˈklæsɪfaɪz/ | v. | 过度分类，分类过细 | To classify something too narrowly or with too many distinctions. | The library overclassifies its books, making it difficult to find what you're looking for. 图书馆对书籍的分类过于细致，很难找到你要找的。| The government overclassifies documents, keeping too much information from the public. 政府对文件过度保密，对公众隐瞒了太多信息。 | overcategorize, oversystematize | underclassify | verb
