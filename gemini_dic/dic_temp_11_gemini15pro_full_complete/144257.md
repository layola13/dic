outsnores:/ˌaʊtˈsnɔːrz/
verb
比...更大声地打鼾
snores louder than someone else.
He outsnores his wife every night. 他每晚都比他妻子打鼾声音大。
近义词:snores louder than
反义词:snores softer than
词性:v.

Etymology:
"out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "snore" (verb meaning to breathe noisily during sleep)
