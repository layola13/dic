overinvesting: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛstɪŋ/
|n.|过度投资|The act of investing too much money or resources in something, often to the point of diminishing returns or negative consequences.|The company's overinvesting in new technology led to financial difficulties.  公司对新技术的过度投资导致了财务困难。|overspending, excessive investment|underinvesting|noun
overinvest: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛst/
|v.|过度投资|To invest too much money or resources in something.|They overinvested in the stock market and lost a lot of money. 他们在股市上过度投资，损失了很多钱。|overspend, invest excessively|underinvest|verb
