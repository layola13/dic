bourgeoisification:/ˌbʊərʒwɑːzɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 中产阶级化；资产阶级化 | The process by which a neighborhood or area becomes more middle-class, often involving an influx of wealthier residents, increased property values, and changes in the character of the area. |  Example 1: The bourgeoisification of the neighborhood has led to the opening of several upscale boutiques and restaurants. 这个社区的中产阶级化导致了几家高档精品店和餐馆的开业。

Example 2:  The rapid bourgeoisification of the city center displaced many long-term residents. 市中心快速的资产阶级化进程迫使许多长期居民搬迁。 | 近义词: gentrification,  upscaling | 反义词: proletarianization, impoverishment | 词性: noun
