dialectologically:/ˌdaɪəˌlɛktəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ /ˌdaɪəˌlɛktəˈlɑːdʒɪkli/
adv.
方言学地
in a way that relates to the study of dialects
例句：The pronunciation of this word varies dialectologically.  这个词的发音因方言而异。
例句：The study examined the issue dialectologically, comparing regional variations. 这项研究从方言学的角度考察了这个问题，比较了地区差异。
近义词：regionally, geographically
反义词: universally, globally
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From dialect + -o- + -logy + -ical + -ly.  Dialect from Late Latin dialectus, from Ancient Greek διάλεκτος (diálektos, “conversation, dialect”), from διαλέγομαι (dialégomai, “I converse”).
