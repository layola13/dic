decene:/ˈdiːsiːn/ /ˈdɛsiːn/ | n. |癸烯|An alkene hydrocarbon with the formula C10H20. There are many isomers of decene, depending on the location of the double bond.|Decene is used in the production of detergents. 癸烯用于生产洗涤剂。|近义词: alkene, olefin |反义词: alkane |词性: noun
