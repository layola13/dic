balotade:/ˌbæləˈtɑːd/ /ˌbæləˈteɪd/
noun
腾跃舞步（芭蕾舞中的一种跳跃动作）
A jumping step in ballet in which the dancer leaps into the air with one leg extended forward and the other bent backward, switching the positions of the legs in midair before landing.
The ballerina performed a graceful balotade, her legs moving with precision.  芭蕾舞演员表演了一个优雅的腾跃舞步，她的腿移动得非常精准。
The balotade is a challenging step that requires strength and control. 腾跃舞步是一个具有挑战性的舞步，需要力量和控制力。
近义词:  cabriole, sissone (similar ballet jumps)
反义词:  N/A (no direct opposite for a specific ballet step)
词性: n.


Etymology:
From French *balotade*, from Italian *ballottata*, from *ballottare* "to toss about," frequentative of *ballare* "to dance," from Late Latin *ballare*. Ultimately from Greek *ballízein* "to dance."
