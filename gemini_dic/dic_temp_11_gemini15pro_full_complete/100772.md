intreasure:/ɪnˈtrɛʒə(r)/
verb
珍藏；铭记；高度重视
To cherish and hold something dear, as if it were a treasure.
She intreasured the memories of her childhood. 她珍藏着童年的记忆。
近义词:cherish, treasure, value, prize
反义词:disregard, neglect, ignore
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- + treasure.  "In-" acts as an intensifier, similar to "en-" in words like "enthrone."  "Treasure" comes from Old French "tresor," ultimately from Latin "thesaurus" meaning "treasure, storehouse."
