jaspachate:/ˈdʒæspəkeɪt/| n. |碧玉玛瑙|A variety of agate that resembles jasper, typically opaque and multicolored.|The artisan crafted a beautiful pendant from a piece of jaspachate.|近义词: agate, jasper |反义词: None |词性: noun

Etymology:
From jasper + agate.  Jasper itself comes from Old French jaspre, ultimately from Latin iaspidem, accusative of iaspis, from Greek ἴασπις (iaspis). Agate comes from Latin achates, from Greek ἀχάτης (akhátēs), the name of a river in Sicily where it was first found.
