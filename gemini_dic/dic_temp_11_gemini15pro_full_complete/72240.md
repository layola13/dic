floridities:/flɔːˈɪdɪtiz/
| n. |华丽的词藻；过分修饰的语言|Excessively ornate or flowery language; an instance of this.|Her prose is marked by floridities that some find charming and others find tedious. 她文章的特点是辞藻华丽，有些人觉得很有趣，有些人觉得很乏味。|近义词：ornateness, embellishments, flourishes, extravagance|反义词：simplicity, plainness, conciseness, austerity|词性:noun

floridity:/flɔːˈɪdɪti/
| n. |华丽；绚丽|The quality or state of being florid or flowery.|The floridity of his writing style made it difficult to understand. 他华丽的写作风格使文章难以理解。|近义词：ornateness, embellishment, flourish, extravagance|反义词：simplicity, plainness, conciseness, austerity|词性:noun

florid:/ˈflɒrɪd/ (British) /ˈflɔːrɪd/ (American)
| adj. |华丽的；绚丽的；红润的|Having a red or flushed complexion; elaborately or excessively ornamented.|He gave a florid speech, full of elaborate metaphors and grand pronouncements. 他发表了一篇华丽的演讲，充满了精美的隐喻和宏伟的宣言。|近义词：ornate, flowery, flamboyant, embellished, ruddy, flushed|反义词：simple, plain, austere, pale|词性:adjective
