irretrievable:/ˌɪrɪˈtriːvəbl/
adjective
无法挽回的；不能复原的
not able to be retrieved or put right.
The data loss was irretrievable. 数据丢失是无法挽回的。
He made an irretrievable mistake. 他犯了一个无法挽回的错误。
近义词:irreversible, irrecoverable, lost, ruined
反义词:retrievable, recoverable, reversible
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + retrievable, from retrieve (to get or bring back), from re- (back) + Old French trover (to find).
