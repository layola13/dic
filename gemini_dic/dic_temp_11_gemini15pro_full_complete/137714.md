nonvacancies:/ˌnɒnvˈeɪkənsiz/ | n. |空缺；职位空缺(的反义，即指没有空缺) |  The state of having no vacancies; the absence of available positions or openings. | Due to the recent hiring freeze, there are currently numerous nonvacancies within the company. 由于最近的招聘冻结，公司目前有很多职位没有空缺。| The hotel reported nonvacancies for the entire weekend.  这家酒店报告整个周末都没有空房。| 近义词: fullness, occupancy, no openings | 反义词: vacancies, openings, availability | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "vacancies" (plural of "vacancy").  "Vacancy" comes from Old French "vacance" and ultimately Latin "vacantia," meaning "emptiness."
