co-ordinative:/kəʊˈɔːrdɪnətɪv/| adj.| 协调的，同等的|Relating to coordination; involving the harmonious functioning of different parts or elements.|The dancer's co-ordinative abilities were impressive, allowing her to execute complex movements with grace.  这位舞者的协调能力令人印象深刻，让她能够优雅地完成复杂的动作。|Example 2: Co-ordinative dysfunction can make everyday tasks difficult. 协调功能障碍会使日常工作变得困难。|近义词：coordinated, harmonious, collaborative, synergistic|反义词：incoordinated, disjointed, discordant|词性: adjective

词源: From "co-ordinate" + "-ive". "Co-ordinate" comes from Latin "coordinatus", past participle of "coordinare" ("to arrange together"), from "co-" ("together") + "ordinare" ("to arrange").
