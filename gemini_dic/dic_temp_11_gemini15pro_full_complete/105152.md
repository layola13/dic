ketone:/ˈkiːtoʊn/| n. |酮|An organic compound containing a carbonyl group (=C=O) bonded to two hydrocarbon groups.  A ketone is produced by the oxidation of a secondary alcohol.|Acetone is the simplest ketone. 丙酮是最简单的酮。|synonyms: carbonyl compound|antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound like ketone)|noun
