feed:/fiːd/
n.|食物; 饲料|The farmer gave the pigs their feed.|农民给猪喂了饲料。
n.|（尤指广播或网络的）信息流|I subscribe to several news feeds.|我订阅了几个新闻提要。
v.|喂养; 饲养|She feeds her cat twice a day.|她每天喂两次猫。
v.|（为机器）提供; 输入|The data is fed into the computer.|数据被输入到计算机中。
v.|（使）感到满足|The book fed his imagination.|这本书满足了他的想象力。
v.|（使）加剧|Her fears were fed by the news.|这个消息加剧了她的恐惧。
例句：The baby needs to be fed every three hours.|这个婴儿需要每三个小时喂一次。

例句：The birds were feeding on the seeds.|鸟儿们正在吃种子。

近义词：nourish, nurture (v.); food, fodder (n.)
反义词：starve (v.)
词性:n. v.
词源：Old English *fēdan (West Saxon fēdan) "to give food to, nourish, support, bring up," from Proto-Germanic *fodjanan (source also of Old Frisian feda, Old Saxon fodian, Old High German fuotan, German füttern, Old Norse fæða, Gothic fodjan "to feed"), from PIE root *pa- "to protect, feed."
