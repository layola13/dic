biscuitlike: /ˈbɪskɪtlaɪk/
adjective
像饼干的；饼干状的
Resembling a biscuit in texture, shape, or taste.
The cake had a dry, biscuitlike texture.  这蛋糕口感很干，像饼干一样。
The clouds were small and white, almost biscuitlike in appearance. 云朵又小又白，外观看起来几乎像饼干。
近义词:  crispy, crunchy, dry, crumbly
反义词:  soft, moist, chewy
词性: adjective
