collator:/ˈkɒleɪtə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːleɪtər/ (US)| n.| 校对员，整理员，排序器| A person or machine that collates printed sheets of paper. | The collator quickly arranged the pages into the correct order. 校对员迅速地把页面按正确的顺序排列好。| The collator sorted the documents alphabetically. 整理员按字母顺序整理了文件。| Synonyms: assembler, organizer, compiler | Antonyms: disorganizer, scatterer | noun

collator:/ˈkɒleɪtə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːleɪtər/ (US)| n.|（尤指不同语言文本的）校勘工具，校勘器| A tool or program used to compare and arrange different versions of texts, especially in different languages. | The collator helped identify discrepancies between the original manuscript and the translated version.  校勘工具帮助识别了原稿和译本之间的差异。 | The online collator facilitated the comparison of multiple translations. 在线校勘器方便了对多个翻译版本的比较。| Synonyms: comparator, analyzer | Antonyms: N/A | noun
