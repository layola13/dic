kazakhs:/ˈkɑːzɑːks/ | n. | 哈萨克人 |  A member of a Turkic people of central Asia, traditionally nomadic pastoralists, many of whom now live in the Republic of Kazakhstan. |  Many Kazakhs maintain their traditional nomadic lifestyle. 许多哈萨克人仍然保持着他们传统的游牧生活方式。|  Kazakhs are known for their horsemanship. 哈萨克人以其骑术而闻名。 |Synonyms:  Kazakhstani (when referring to nationality) | Antonyms:  N/A | Noun
