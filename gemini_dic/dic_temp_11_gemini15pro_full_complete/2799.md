advances:/ədˈvænsɪz/ /ədˈvɑːnsɪz/
n.|前进，进展；预付款；求爱|progress, development; payments made before they are due; attempts to start a romantic relationship|The company has made significant advances in the field of artificial intelligence.  该公司在人工智能领域取得了重大进展。|近义词:progress, development, headway, improvement|反义词:retreats, declines, setbacks|词性:名词(plural)

advances:/ədˈvænsɪz/ /ədˈvɑːnsɪz/
v.|前进；推进；预付；提出|move forward; help the progress of something; pay (someone) money before it is due; put forward (a theory or suggestion)|The army advanced slowly towards the enemy.  军队慢慢地向敌人推进。|近义词:proceed, progress, further, promote|反义词:retreat, withdraw, recede, hinder|词性:动词(third-person singular present)


词源: From Old French *avancer, from a- "to" (from Latin ad) + avancer "to put forward," from Late Latin abante "in front."
