abolishment:/əˈbɒlɪʃmənt/
n.
废除，取消
the act of officially ending a law, system, or practice
The abolishment of slavery marked a turning point in American history.  美国奴隶制的废除标志着美国历史的一个转折点。

Etymology:  Late Middle English: from Old French abolissement, from abolir ‘abolish’, from Latin abolere.
近义词: abolition, eradication, elimination, termination
反义词: establishment, institution, creation, implementation
词性: noun
