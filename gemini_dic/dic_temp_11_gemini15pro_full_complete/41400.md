corncrake: /ˈkɔːnkreɪk/ (UK), /ˈkɔːrnkreɪk/ (US) | n. | 秧鸡; 长脚秧鸡 | A small, secretive bird of the rail family, with a distinctive rasping call, found in meadows and grasslands.  It is known for its loud, harsh, repetitive call, often heard at night. |例句：The corncrake's call echoed across the meadow.  秧鸡的叫声在草地上回荡。

例句：Conservation efforts are underway to protect the endangered corncrake.  保护濒危秧鸡的保育工作正在进行中。|近义词：rail, crake |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

词源: From "corn" + "crake,"  "crake" being an imitative name for birds with harsh calls.
