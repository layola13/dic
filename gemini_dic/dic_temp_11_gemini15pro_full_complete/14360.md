autopilot:/ˈɔːtəʊpaɪlət/ (UK) /ˈɔːt̬əʊˌpaɪlət/ (US)| n. |自动驾驶仪; 自动驾驶状态|A device that automatically steers ships, aircraft, and spacecraft. Also refers to a similar system in cars or the state of being controlled by such a system.  |The plane was on autopilot for most of the flight.  这架飞机大部分飞行时间都在自动驾驶状态。|Synonyms: automatic pilot, self-steering, autonomous control|Antonyms: manual control|noun


autopilot:/ˈɔːtəʊpaɪlət/ (UK) /ˈɔːt̬əʊˌpaɪlət/ (US)| adj. |自动的|Operating automatically, especially of a system or process.|The car's autopilot features are still in the beta testing phase. 这款车的自动驾驶功能仍处于测试阶段。|Synonyms: automatic, self-operating, autonomous|Antonyms: manual|adjective
