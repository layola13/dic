bioengineering:/ˌbaɪoʊˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/| n. |生物工程；生物工程学|The application of engineering principles to biological systems, especially in medicine and agriculture.  The manipulation of living organisms or their components to produce useful products or systems.| Bioengineering holds immense promise for developing new medical treatments. 生物工程在开发新的医疗方法方面拥有巨大的潜力。|近义词：biomedical engineering, genetic engineering|反义词: N/A|名词
