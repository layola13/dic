innuendo:/ˌɪnjuˈɛndoʊ/ | n. |影射，暗讽，含沙射影|An indirect or subtle, usually derogatory, implication in expression; an insinuation.|Her statement was filled with innuendo about her rival's personal life. 她的声明中充满了对她竞争对手私生活的影射。|Synonyms: insinuation, implication, suggestion, hint, allusion|Antonyms: direct statement, explicit statement|noun
