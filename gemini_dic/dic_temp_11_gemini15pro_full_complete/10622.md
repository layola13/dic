approbation: /ˌæprəˈbeɪʃən/ (US) /ˌæprəˈbeɪʃn/ (UK)| n. |批准，认可；赞许|The act of approving formally or officially; commendation.|The proposal received the approbation of the committee.  这项提议得到了委员会的批准。|He sought his father's approbation for his career choice. 他寻求父亲对他职业选择的认可。|近义词：approval, sanction, endorsement, commendation|反义词：disapproval, rejection, condemnation|词性: 名词
