/ˌɒbdʒɛkˈtiː/ | n. | 被动对象，被作用者 |  A person or thing to which a specified action or feeling is directed. | The objectee of the king's wrath was the unfortunate messenger. 国王的愤怒指向了那个不幸的信使。 | synonyms: target, recipient, victim | antonyms: subject, agent, perpetrator | noun


/ˌɒbdʒɛkˈtiː/ | n. | (语法)宾语 | The noun phrase or pronoun that receives the action of a verb or completes the meaning of a preposition. |  In the sentence "The dog chased the ball," "the ball" is the objectee. 在句子“狗追球”中，“球”是宾语。 | synonyms: object, patient | antonyms: subject | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin objectivus, from Latin objectus (“thrown before, presented”), past participle of objicere (“to throw before, present”), from ob- (“before, towards”) + jacere (“to throw”).
