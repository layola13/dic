danaite:/ˈdeɪnaɪt/| n.|砷钴矿|A mineral, cobalt arsenide (CoAs₂), that forms a series with cobaltite. It is steel-gray to silver-white with a metallic luster and occurs in granular masses or disseminated grains.|Danaite is a relatively rare mineral found in hydrothermal veins.|近义词：cobaltite, smaltite|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
