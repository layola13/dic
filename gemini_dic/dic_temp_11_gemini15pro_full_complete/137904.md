nonviviparousness:/ˌnɒnˌvɪvɪˈpærəsnəs/
n.
非胎生
The state or condition of not being viviparous; not bringing forth living young.
Snakes exhibit varying reproductive strategies, including oviparity and ovoviviparity, but not nonviviparousness. 蛇表现出不同的繁殖策略，包括卵生和卵胎生，但不是非胎生。
