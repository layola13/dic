floodings:/ˈflʌdɪŋz/| n. |洪水，泛滥|An overflowing of water onto land that is normally dry.  The result of heavy rainfall or a breached dam, causing widespread damage and displacement.|例句：The floodings caused by the hurricane devastated the coastal communities. 飓风引起的洪水摧毁了沿海社区。

例句：The recent floodings have left many people homeless. 最近的洪水使许多人无家可归。|近义词：inundations, deluges, overflows|反义词: droughts|词性:名词
