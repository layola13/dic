oompahs:/ˈuːmpɑːz/| n.|伴奏声，节奏声，尤指欢快的音乐|A rhythmic accompaniment, especially to lively music.  It evokes the sound of the Oompa Loompas' music in "Charlie and the Chocolate Factory."|The oompahs of the band filled the room with energy. 乐队的伴奏声让整个房间充满了活力。|The oompahs of the carnival music made everyone want to dance.  嘉年华音乐的节奏声让每个人都想跳舞。|synonyms: beat, rhythm, pulse, cadence|antonyms: silence, quiet|noun

oompah:/ˈuːmpɑː/| n. adj.|欢快的音乐，节奏，伴奏;  欢快的，有节奏的|A lively piece of music, a rhythm, or accompaniment; lively and rhythmic.|The band played a catchy oompah. 乐队演奏了一首动听的欢快乐曲。|She danced to the oompah music. 她随着欢快的音乐跳舞。|The oompah beat got everyone tapping their feet.  欢快的节奏让每个人都跟着打拍子。|synonyms: upbeat, rhythmic, lively, cheerful|antonyms: slow, mournful, somber|noun, adjective

词源:  Imitative of a deep, resonant sound, likely popularized by the Oompa Loompas in Roald Dahl's "Charlie and the Chocolate Factory."  模仿一种低沉、洪亮的聲音，可能是由羅爾德·達爾的《查理和巧克力工廠》中的奧帕·倫帕斯推廣開來的。
