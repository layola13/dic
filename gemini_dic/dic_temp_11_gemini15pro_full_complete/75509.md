fse:/ˌɛfɛsˈiː/ |abbr.| FSE|  Abbreviation for Full Service Equivalent.  A unit of measure used in the airline industry representing the work of one full-time employee. | The airline reduced its FSEs by 10% last year due to budget cuts. 航空公司去年因预算削减而减少了 10% 的全职员工当量。 | synonyms: FTE (Full-Time Equivalent) | antonyms: N/A | abbreviation
