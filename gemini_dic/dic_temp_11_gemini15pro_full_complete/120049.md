millo:/ˈmɪloʊ/ (US), /ˈmɪləʊ/ (UK)| n.|小米，高粱|A type of grain, typically sorghum or a variety of sorghum, cultivated for its edible starchy seeds.|Example 1: In some parts of Africa, millo is a staple food. 在非洲的一些地区，小米是主食。

Example 2: The farmer harvested a bumper crop of millo this year. 今年农民收获了大丰收的小米。|近义词：sorghum, millet|反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of grain)|词性:noun
