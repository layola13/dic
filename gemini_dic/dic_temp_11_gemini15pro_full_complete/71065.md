firstest:/ˈfɜːrstɪst/ | adj. | 最第一的，最早的（非标准用法） |  A nonstandard superlative form of "first". While grammatically incorrect, it is sometimes used informally for emphasis, particularly by children. |  "I want to be the firstest in line!"  我想要排在最最前面！| 近义词: first, earliest | 反义词: last, latest | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "first" + "-est" (superlative suffix). The standard superlative of "first" is simply "first."
