aporia:/əˈpɔːriə/ /eɪˈpɔːriə/
n.
疑惑；窘境；难题；悖论
An expression of real or pretended doubt or uncertainty especially for rhetorical effect. A state of puzzlement or uncertainty, especially as to what to do or believe. An insoluble contradiction or paradox in a text's argument.
The detective faced an aporia in the case; there seemed to be no motive for the crime.  侦探在这个案件中遇到了难题；这起犯罪似乎没有动机。
The philosopher's argument led to an aporia, a point where logic seemed to break down.  哲学家的论证导致了一个悖论，一个逻辑似乎崩溃的点。

近义词:dilemma, predicament, quandary, impasse, puzzle, enigma
反义词:solution, certainty, clarity
词性:noun
