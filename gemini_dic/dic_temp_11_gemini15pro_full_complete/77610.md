geest:/ɣeːst/ | n. | 冰碛土，荒地 | An area of elevated sandy ground, often infertile, formed by wind-blown deposits of glacial outwash.  It contrasts with the surrounding lower-lying, more fertile marshland or clay soils. | The geest provided well-drained land suitable for settlement and cultivation, unlike the surrounding marshy areas.  冰碛土为定居和耕种提供了排水良好的土地，不像周围的沼泽地区。| synonyms:  sandy heath, heathland, upland | antonyms:  marsh, fen, bog | noun
