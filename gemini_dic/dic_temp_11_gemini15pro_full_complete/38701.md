condescendency:/ˌkɑːndɪˈsendənsi/ | n. | 屈尊；俯就；恩赐态度 | An attitude of patronizing superiority; disdainful graciousness. |  He spoke to the workers with a tone of condescendency. 他以一种屈尊俯就的语气对工人们说话。

She showed condescendency towards her less fortunate relatives. 她对不太幸运的亲戚表现出恩赐的态度。 | 近义词：patronizing, condescension, superciliousness | 反义词：humility, respect, egalitarianism | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *condescendere, from Latin condescendere "to descend, condescend," from com- "together" + descendere "to descend."
