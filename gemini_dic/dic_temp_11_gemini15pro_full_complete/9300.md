antipiracies:/ˌæntiˈpaɪrəsiːz/
|n.|反盗版措施；打击盗版行为|Actions or measures taken to combat or prevent piracy, particularly copyright infringement or software piracy.|The government has stepped up its antipiracies efforts. 政府加强了反盗版力度。|近义词: anti-piracy measures, copyright protection, intellectual property protection|反义词: piracy, copyright infringement|词性:名词

antipiracy:/ˌæntiˈpaɪrəsi/
|n.|反盗版；打击盗版|The act of combating piracy.|Antipiracy software is becoming increasingly sophisticated. 反盗版软件正变得越来越复杂。|近义词: copyright protection, anti-piracy measures|反义词: piracy|词性:名词


词源:  "anti-" (against) + "piracy" (robbery at sea, later extended to unauthorized copying).  "Anti-" comes from Greek "anti" (against, opposite). "Piracy" comes from  Late Latin "piratia", from Greek "peirateia" "piracy, robbery," from "peiratēs" "pirate," related to "peiran" "to attempt, attack."
