mineralogy:/ˌmɪnəˈrælədʒi/ | n. | 矿物学 | The study of minerals, including their formation, composition, properties, classification, and occurrence. |  Example 1: He studied mineralogy in college and became a geologist. 他在大学学习矿物学，后来成为了一名地质学家。

Example 2:  Mineralogy is a crucial field for understanding the Earth's composition. 矿物学是理解地球组成的关键领域。 | 近义词: mineral science | 反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a field of study) | 词性: noun
