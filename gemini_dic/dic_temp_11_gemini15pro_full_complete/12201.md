as well:/æz ˈwɛl/ | adv. | 也，还，又 |  Used to add something to a statement.  It is similar in meaning to "too" or "also," but usually placed at the end of a sentence. | I'm going to the store, and I'll get some milk as well. 我要去商店，也会买些牛奶。| synonyms: too, also, in addition, additionally | antonyms: neither, nor | adverb

as well:/æz ˈwɛl/ | adv. | 同样地，一样好地 | Used to indicate that something is done to the same standard or degree as something else. |  She sings as well as her sister. 她唱歌和她姐姐一样好。 | synonyms: equally well, just as well | antonyms: worse, poorly | adverb
