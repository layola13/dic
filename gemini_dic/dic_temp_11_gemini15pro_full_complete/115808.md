markable:/ˈmɑːrkəbl/ (US) /ˈmɑːkəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|显著的，明显的，值得注意的| noticeable or important enough to be noticed or mentioned |The improvement in her writing skills was markable. 她的写作技巧的进步是显著的。|The event was markable for its historical significance.  这次事件因其历史意义而值得注意。|近义词：noticeable, significant, noteworthy, outstanding, remarkable, prominent|反义词：insignificant, unremarkable, inconspicuous, negligible|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From late Middle English: from mark + -able.
