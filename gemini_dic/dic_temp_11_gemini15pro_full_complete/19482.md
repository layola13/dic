biddery:/ˈbɪdəri/
n.
旧货店；廉价商店;  a shop selling cheap or secondhand goods
The old woman runs a small biddery selling vintage clothes and antique furniture.  这位老妇人经营着一家小旧货店，出售复古服装和古董家具。
junk shop, thrift store
antique shop, boutique
