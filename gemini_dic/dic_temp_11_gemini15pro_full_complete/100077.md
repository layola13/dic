interpolished:/ˌɪntərˈpɒlɪʃt/ | adj. |  相互抛光的，互相磨光的 | Polished between or among; mutually polished.  | The interpolished surfaces of the gemstones created a dazzling effect. 宝石相互抛光的表面营造出耀眼的效果。 | The gears were interpolished for smoother operation. 齿轮经过相互抛光以实现更平稳的运转。 | 近义词: polished, smoothed, refined | 反义词: rough, unpolished, coarse | 词性: adjective

词源:
"inter-" (prefix) + "polished"
"inter-" comes from Latin, meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally."
"polished" comes from "polish," ultimately from Latin "polire," meaning "to smooth," "to refine."
