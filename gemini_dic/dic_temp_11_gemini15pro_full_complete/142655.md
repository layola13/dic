ostracize: /ˈɑːstrəsaɪz/ (US) /ˈɒstrəsaɪz/ (UK)
|v.|排斥，孤立|To exclude from a group or society by common consent.|She was ostracized by her colleagues for whistleblowing.|Synonyms: banish, exclude, shun, blackball|Antonyms: include, welcome, embrace
|verb|
