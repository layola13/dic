depersonalization:/diːˌpɜːrsənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /diːˌpɜːrsənələˈzeɪʃən/
n.
人格解体；失去个性
A mental disorder characterized by a persistent or recurring feeling of being detached from one's body or mental processes, or as if one is an outside observer of one's life.
She experienced episodes of depersonalization after the traumatic event.  她在创伤事件后经历了人格解体发作。
He described his depersonalization as feeling like he was watching himself in a movie. 他将他的人格解体描述为感觉像在电影中看着自己。
近义词: detachment, alienation, dissociation
反义词:  connection, engagement, integration
词性: noun
