nonapprovals:/ˌnɒnəˈpruːvəlz/
n.
不批准，不认可，否决
lack of approval; instances of something not being approved.
The project faced numerous nonapprovals from the board.  这个项目遭到董事会多次否决。
The nonapprovals of the budget proposal led to further revisions. 预算提案未获批准，导致需要进一步修订。
synonyms: rejections, disapprovals, denials
antonyms: approvals, endorsements, sanctions
noun
