outdebate:/ˌaʊt dɪˈbeɪt/
verb
胜过辩论，在辩论中击败
to defeat someone in a debate.
He outdebated his opponent with his sharp wit and extensive knowledge. 他以敏锐的机智和渊博的知识在辩论中击败了对手。
She outdebated everyone in the room and won the argument. 她在辩论中胜过了房间里的所有人，赢得了这场争论。
近义词: outargue, defeat, vanquish
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb
