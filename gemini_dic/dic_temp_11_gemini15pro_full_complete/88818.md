hogchain:/ˈhɒɡtʃeɪn/ (UK) /ˈhɑːɡtʃeɪn/ (US)| n. |大桶，猪链|A large cask or barrel, especially one holding a specific volume of liquid (historically varying but often around 63 gallons).  It was originally used for transporting wine, ale, or other liquids.|The hogchain was once a common unit of measurement for wine.|近义词：cask, barrel, tun|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps container of a smaller size like keg, jug)|名词
