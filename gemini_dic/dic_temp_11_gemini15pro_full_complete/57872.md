dribble:/ˈdrɪbəl/ (US), /ˈdrɪbl/ (UK)| n.|运球，点滴，口水|A small quantity of liquid; the act of dribbling a ball. |There was a dribble of blood from his cut.  他的伤口流出一点血。|近义词：drip, trickle|反义词:flood, torrent|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *driblen, frequentative of drippen (“to drip”), from Old English *dryppan, from Proto-Germanic *druppaną (“to drip”).
