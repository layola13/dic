flatbread:/ˈflætˌbred/ | n. | 薄饼，扁面包 | A thin, flat bread, often unleavened or lightly leavened, cooked on a griddle or in a hot pan. |  We had some delicious flatbread with hummus. 我们吃了一些美味的鹰嘴豆泥配薄饼。| synonyms: pita, tortilla, naan, lavash | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "loaf" as a contrasting bread shape) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "flat" + "bread".  "Flat" meaning having a level surface, and "bread" referring to a staple food made from flour, water, and usually yeast.
