decategorizes:/diːˈkætəɡəˌraɪzɪz/
|v.| 去分类，取消分类|To remove something from a category or to abolish the categories that something belongs to.  It refers to the act of making something no longer fit into a specific category or system of classification. |The new software decategorizes user data based on behavior rather than demographics.  这款新软件根据用户行为而不是人口统计数据对用户数据进行去分类。

The museum decategorized its collection, displaying art by theme rather than era.  博物馆取消了藏品的分类，按主题而不是时代展示艺术品。|近义词:reclassify, regroup, reorganize|反义词: categorize, classify, label|词性:动词
