Matabele:/ˌmætəˈbiːliː/ /ˌmætəˈbiːleɪ/
| n. | 马塔贝莱人 | A member of a Bantu-speaking people of southwestern Zimbabwe and northeastern Botswana, traditionally a warrior people.  Originally part of the Zulu kingdom, they broke away in the 1820s under the leadership of Mzilikazi. |  The Matabele established a powerful kingdom in the 19th century.  马塔贝莱人在19世纪建立了一个强大的王国。| 近义词: Ndebele (more common modern term) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a people group) | 词性: noun
