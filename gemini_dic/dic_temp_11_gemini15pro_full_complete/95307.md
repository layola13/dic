incasement:/ɪnˈkeɪsmənt/
noun
装箱；包裹；围住；封入
the act of encasing something or the state of being encased
The jewels were kept in a glass incasement.  这些珠宝被保存在一个玻璃箱里。

The ancient sword was displayed in a protective incasement. 这把古剑被陈列在一个保护性的外壳里。

casing, enclosure, covering
uncovering, exposure
