destock:/diːˈstɒk/ /diːˈstɑːk/
n. |去库存，清仓销售| The act of reducing the amount of stock held by a business, often by selling it at a reduced price. |The company announced a major destocking effort to clear out its warehouses.|近义词：clearance, liquidation, sell-off|反义词：stockpiling, accumulation|词性：名词

destock:/diːˈstɒk/ /diːˈstɑːk/
v. |去库存，减少库存| To reduce the amount of stock held by a business. |Retailers are destocking winter clothes to make way for spring fashions.|近义词：liquidate, clear out, reduce inventory|反义词：stock up, accumulate|词性：动词
