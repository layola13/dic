maund:/mɔːnd/ | n. | 一种重量单位；大量；篮子 | A unit of weight varying in different localities; a large indefinite quantity; a basket. | The merchant sold his goods by the maund. 商人按孟德出售他的货物。|  The farmer brought a maund of potatoes to market. 农民带了一孟德的土豆到市场上去卖。 | 近义词: unit, load, basket | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑少量 small amount) | 词性: 名词


maund:/mɔːnd/ | v. | 悲叹；哀悼 | To lament; to mourn. (古旧用法，现在很少使用) |  She maunded the loss of her beloved pet. 她哀悼她心爱宠物的离去。(古旧用法) | He maunded over his misfortunes. 他悲叹自己的不幸。(古旧用法) |近义词: mourn, grieve, lament | 反义词: rejoice, celebrate | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi मन (man, “maund”), from Sanskrit मन (mana). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *men- (“to think, remember”).  Doublet of mind.
