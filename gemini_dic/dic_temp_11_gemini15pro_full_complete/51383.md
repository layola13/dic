determinate:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnət/| adj.|确定的，一定的；限定的|Having exact limits or boundaries; fixed or defined. determinate:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪt/| n.|限定词|A limiting factor or element; a determinant.|The availability of funding was a key determinate in the project's success.  资金的可用性是项目成功的关键因素。|近义词:determinant, factor, element|反义词:N/A|noun
