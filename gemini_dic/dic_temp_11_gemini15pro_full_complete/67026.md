explain:/ɪkˈspleɪn/ /ɛkˈspleɪn/| v. | 解释，说明 | To make something clear or easy to understand by describing or giving information about it. |例句：Could you explain this concept to me? 你能向我解释一下这个概念吗？

例句：The teacher explained the rules of the game. 老师解释了游戏规则。

近义词：clarify, elucidate, interpret, illustrate, demonstrate|反义词: confuse, obscure, complicate |词性:动词
