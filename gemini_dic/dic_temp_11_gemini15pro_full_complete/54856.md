displayer:/dɪˈspleɪər/ | n. | 展示者，陈列者，显示器 | someone or something that displays something | The museum is a displayer of ancient artifacts.  博物馆是古代文物的展示者。|  The new phone has a vibrant displayer. 新手机有一个鲜艳的显示器。 | synonyms: exhibitor, presenter, shower, screen, monitor | antonyms: hider, concealer | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "display" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Display" comes from the Old French "despleier" meaning "to unfold, spread out."
