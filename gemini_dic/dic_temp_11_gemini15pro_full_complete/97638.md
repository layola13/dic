heritabilities:/ˌhɛrɪtəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 遗传力，遗传度 | The proportion of phenotypic variation in a population that is attributable to genetic variation among individuals.  It measures how much of the differences between individuals for a trait are due to differences in their genes. | High heritability of intelligence suggests that genetic differences contribute significantly to differences in IQ scores among individuals. 智力的遗传力高表明基因差异对个体智商分数的差异有显著贡献。

The heritabilities of different traits vary widely. 不同性状的遗传力差异很大。 | synonym:  inheritability, genetic variance | antonym: environmental variance | noun


inheritable:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtəbl/ | adj. | 可遗传的，可继承的 |  Able to be passed from parent to offspring; capable of being inherited. | Eye color is an inheritable trait. 眼睛的颜色是一个可遗传的特征。

Many diseases are inheritable. 许多疾病是可遗传的。 | synonym: hereditary, transmissible, genetic | antonym: acquired, non-genetic | adjective

inheritableness:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtəblnəs/ | n. | 可遗传性，可继承性 | The quality or state of being inheritable.  The degree to which a trait is genetically determined. | The inheritableness of certain personality traits is a subject of ongoing research. 某些性格特征的可遗传性是一个正在进行的研究课题。

The study explored the inheritableness of musical talent. 这项研究探讨了音乐天赋的可遗传性。| synonym: heritability, heredity | antonym: non-heritability | noun
