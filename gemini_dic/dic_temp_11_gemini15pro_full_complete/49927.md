/diːˈneɪtʃə(r)/ | /diːˈneɪtʃər/ | v. | 使变性，使改变性质 | to take away or alter the natural qualities of; to make (something) unnatural or ineffective |  Cooking denatures proteins in food.  烹饪会使食物中的蛋白质变性。| alter, change, modify | preserve, maintain | verb

/ˈdiːneɪtʃəd/ | /ˈdiːneɪtʃərd/ | adj. | 变性的 |  having been altered in nature, especially having had the natural properties removed by heat, acidity, or other treatment | Denatured alcohol is poisonous. 变性酒精有毒。| altered, modified | natural, unchanged | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin 'de' (down, away from) + 'natura' (nature)
