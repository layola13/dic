nondepletory:/ˌnɒndɪˈpliːtəri/
adjective
非消耗性的，不损耗的
Not depleting or reducing something; not causing depletion.
Using nondepletory energy sources like solar and wind power can help protect the environment.  使用像太阳能和风能这样的非消耗性能源可以帮助保护环境。
Sustainable fishing practices aim for nondepletory harvesting of fish stocks. 可持续的捕捞方法旨在对鱼类资源进行非消耗性捕捞。
近义词: sustainable, renewable, inexhaustible
反义词: depleting, consumptive, exhaustive
词性: adjective

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "depletory" (adjective from "deplete," meaning "to reduce the amount of something," from Latin "deplēre" meaning "to empty").
