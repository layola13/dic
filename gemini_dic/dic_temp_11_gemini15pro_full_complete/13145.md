asymmetrical: /ˌeɪsɪˈmetrɪkəl/ /ˌæsɪˈmetrɪkəl/ | adj. | 不对称的，非对称的 |  Not symmetrical; having parts that fail to correspond to one another in shape, size, or arrangement; lacking symmetry. | The building has an asymmetrical design, with one wing much longer than the other.  这栋建筑的设计不对称，一侧的翼比另一侧长很多。|  uneven, lopsided, irregular, unbalanced | symmetrical, even, balanced | adjective

asymmetrous: /ˌeɪsɪˈmetrəs/ /ˌæsɪˈmetrəs/ | adj. | 不对称的，非对称的 |  Not symmetrical; having parts that fail to correspond to one another in shape, size, or arrangement; lacking symmetry. | The artist intentionally made the sculpture asymmetrous to create a sense of imbalance.  艺术家故意使雕塑不对称，以创造一种不平衡感。| uneven, lopsided, irregular, unbalanced | symmetrical, even, balanced | adjective
