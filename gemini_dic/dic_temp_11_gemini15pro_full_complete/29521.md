catatonic:/ˌkætəˈtɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 紧张性精神分裂症的；僵直的；呆若木鸡的 | Relating to or characterized by catatonia, a state of apparent unresponsiveness to external stimuli in a person who is apparently awake.  |  The patient remained catatonic, unresponsive to any external stimuli.  病人仍然处于紧张性精神分裂症状态，对外界刺激没有任何反应。 |  He sat catatonic in front of the television. 他呆若木鸡地坐在电视机前。 | Synonyms: stuporous, unresponsive, immobile, rigid, frozen | Antonyms: responsive, alert, active, mobile | adjective

catatonic:/ˌkætəˈtɑːnɪk/ | n. | 紧张症患者 | A person suffering from catatonia. | The catatonic was unresponsive to any stimuli.  这位紧张症患者对任何刺激都没有反应。|  The hospital ward housed several catatonics.  医院病房里住着几个紧张症患者。| Synonyms: sufferer | Antonyms: None | noun
