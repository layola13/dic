loyalness:/ˈlɔɪəlnəs/| n.|忠诚，忠实|The state or quality of being loyal; faithfulness.|His loyalness to the company was commendable. 他对公司的忠诚值得称赞。|近义词：fidelity, allegiance, devotion|反义词：treachery, disloyalty, infidelity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *loyalnesse*, from Old French *loialte*  (Modern French *loyauté*), from *loial* (“loyal”), from Latin *lēgālis* (“legal, pertaining to the law”), from *lēx* (“law”).
