centralized:/ˈsɛntrəˌlaɪzd/| adj.|集中的，中央集权的|Brought together at or directed to a common center; concentrated. centralize:/ˈsɛntrəˌlaɪz/| v.|集中，中央集权|To bring together at or direct to a common center; concentrate.
