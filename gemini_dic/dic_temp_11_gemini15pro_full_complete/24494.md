bro:/broʊ/ (US) /brəʊ/ (UK)| n.|兄弟；哥们儿|A short form of "brother," used as a term of address for a male friend or peer, often expressing camaraderie or solidarity.|"Hey bro, what's up?" 嘿，兄弟，怎么了？|近义词：dude, pal, mate, buddy|反义词:enemy, foe|名词
