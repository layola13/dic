mocked-up: /mɑːkt ˈʌp/ (US) /mɒkt ˈʌp/ (UK)| adj.| 模拟的，仿制的|A mocked-up version is a model or a draft version of something that is intended to look like the real thing but is not fully functional. It's often used for testing, demonstration, or presentation purposes.|  Example 1:  They used a mocked-up website for the user testing. 他们用一个模拟网站进行用户测试。

Example 2: The architect presented a mocked-up model of the proposed building.  建筑师展示了拟建建筑的模型。| 近义词: simulated, dummy, prototype, imitation, replica| 反义词: real, genuine, authentic|词性:形容词
