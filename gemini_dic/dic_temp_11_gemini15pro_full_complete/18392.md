belong /bɪˈlɒŋ (UK), bɪˈlɔːŋ (US)/| vi.| 属于，应在…（地方）|  Something belongs somewhere means it is in the correct or appropriate place or situation.  It can also mean that someone feels comfortable and accepted in a particular place or group. | This book belongs to me. 这本书属于我。| synonyms: fit in, be part of, be a member of | antonyms: be excluded from, be alien to | verb

belong /bɪˈlɒŋ (UK), bɪˈlɔːŋ (US)/| vi.|  附属，隶属|  If something belongs to a particular person, group, or organization, it is owned by them or is connected with them. |  The islands belong to Denmark. 这些岛屿属于丹麦。 | synonyms: be owned by, be the property of, be connected with | antonyms: be independent of, be separate from | verb
