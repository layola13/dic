decarbonized: /diːˈkɑːrbənaɪzd/ | adj. | 脱碳的，去碳的 |  Having had carbon removed, or relating to the process of removing carbon.  This usually refers to reducing or eliminating carbon dioxide emissions to mitigate climate change. | The company aims to have a fully decarbonized supply chain by 2030.  该公司旨在到2030年实现完全脱碳的供应链。 |  low-carbon, green, sustainable | carbon-intensive, polluting | adjective
