maenad:/ˈmiːnæd/ | n. | 女祭司；狂女；(复数)酒神的狂女信徒 | A female follower of Bacchus (Dionysus), the Greek god of wine and revelry, known for their wild, ecstatic, and often frenzied rituals. |  The maenads danced with abandon in the moonlight. 月光下，女祭司们狂放地跳舞。|  The play depicted the maenads tearing Orpheus apart.  这出戏描绘了酒神的狂女信徒将奥尔pheus撕碎的场景。| Synonyms: bacchante, devotee, worshipper | Antonyms: prude, ascetic | noun
