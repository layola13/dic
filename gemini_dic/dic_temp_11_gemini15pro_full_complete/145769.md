overcuriously: /ˌoʊvərˈkjʊəriəsli/
| adv. | 过分好奇地；过于好奇地 |  In a manner marked by excessive curiosity. | She overcuriously peeked into her neighbor's window. 她过分好奇地偷看邻居的窗户。 | Synonyms: inquisitively, nosily, pryingly | Antonyms: indifferently, apathetically | 词性: 副词


Etymology:
From "over" (meaning excessively) + "curiously" (from "curious," ultimately from Latin "cura" meaning care, concern).
