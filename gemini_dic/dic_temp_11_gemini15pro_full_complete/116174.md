masculinization:/ˌmæskjʊlɪnəˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 男性化，雄性化 | The process of becoming or causing something to become more masculine.  |  The masculinization of female athletes can sometimes be a side effect of performance-enhancing drugs. 女性运动员男性化有时可能是增强体能药物的副作用。 | Synonyms: virilization, masculinising | Antonyms: feminization, feminising | noun

masculinise:/ˈmæskjʊlənaɪz/ | v. | 男性化，雄性化 | To make or become masculine in appearance, character, or behavior. |  Exposure to high levels of testosterone can masculinise the female body.  暴露于高水平的睾酮会使女性身体男性化。 | Synonyms: virilize | Antonyms: feminize | verb
