catfights:/ˈkætfaɪts/| n.|猫打架，女人之间的激烈争吵|A noisy, aggressive quarrel or fight, especially between women.  It's often used metaphorically to describe any petty, spiteful argument.|The reality show was notorious for the frequent catfights between the contestants.  这档真人秀节目因参赛者之间频繁的争吵而臭名昭著。|近义词：squabble, brawl, spat, altercation, tiff|反义词:agreement, harmony, peace|noun
