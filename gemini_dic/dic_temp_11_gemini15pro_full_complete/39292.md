conical:/ˈkɒnɪkəl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnɪkəl/ (US)| adj.|圆锥形的；圆锥体的|Having the shape of a cone; resembling a cone.|
The roof of the tower was conical. 塔的屋顶是圆锥形的。

The volcano has a perfectly conical shape. 这座火山拥有完美的圆锥形状。

近义词:cone-shaped, tapered|反义词:cylindrical, flat|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conicus, from Latin conus (“cone”) + -icus.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek κῶνος (kônos, “cone”).
