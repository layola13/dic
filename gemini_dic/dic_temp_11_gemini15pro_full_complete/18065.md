beguilers:/bɪˈɡaɪlərz/| n. |欺骗者，诱惑者|People who deceive or trick others, often through charm or cunning. |The beguilers promised great riches, but delivered nothing. 这些骗子承诺了巨大的财富，但什么也没兑现。|近义词：deceivers, charmers, seducers, tricksters, con artists|反义词: honest people, truth-tellers|Noun, plural
