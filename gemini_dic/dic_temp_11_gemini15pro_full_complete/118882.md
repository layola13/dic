metastatic:/ˌmɛtəˈstætɪk/| adj.|转移性的，扩散的|Relating to or characterized by metastasis; the spread of a disease (especially cancer) from one organ or part to another non-adjacent organ or part.|The patient has metastatic breast cancer, meaning it has spread beyond the breast tissue.  病人患有转移性乳腺癌，这意味着它已经扩散到乳腺组织之外。|例句：The cancer became metastatic and spread to her lungs. 癌症发生转移，扩散到她的肺部。|例句：Metastatic melanoma is a serious form of skin cancer. 转移性黑色素瘤是一种严重的皮肤癌。|近义词：cancerous, malignant, disseminated|反义词：benign, localized|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Greek *μετάστασις (metástasis, “change of position, transfer, migration”) from *μεθίστημι (methístēmi, “I change the position of, transfer, remove”)
