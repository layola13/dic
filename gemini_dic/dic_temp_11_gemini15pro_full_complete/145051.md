overagitations:/ˌoʊvərˌædʒɪˈteɪʃənz/
|n.|过度激动，过度焦虑|A state of excessive agitation or restlessness.|His overagitations were a clear sign of his underlying anxiety. 他的过度焦虑清楚地表明了他潜在的焦虑。|The constant overagitations made it difficult for him to focus.  持续的过度激动让他难以集中注意力。|近义词：overexcitement, restlessness, nervousness, anxiety|反义词：calmness, tranquility, serenity|词性:名词

词源:
From "over-" (excessive) + "agitation" (a state of anxiety or nervous excitement).  "Agitation" comes from the Latin word "agitare," meaning "to put in motion, drive, excite."
