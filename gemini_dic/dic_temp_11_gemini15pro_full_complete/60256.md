effusiveness:/ɪˈfjuːsɪvnəs/
noun
热情洋溢；奔放
The quality of expressing feelings of gratitude, pleasure, or approval in an unrestrained or heartfelt manner.
Her effusiveness made him uncomfortable. 她的热情奔放让他感到不自在。
Synonyms: exuberance, warmth, ardor, passion, gushiness
Antonyms: reserve, restraint, coldness, aloofness, indifference
