keck:/kek/
verb
呕吐;作呕
to make the sound of vomiting, or to vomit a small amount
例句：He keckked at the sight of the blood. 他一看到血就作呕。

例句：The smell made her keck. 这气味让她想吐。
近义词：retch, gag, heave
反义词: None (呕吐没有明确的反义词)
词性: verb


keck:/kek/
noun
干呕;恶心
the act of kecking
例句：He gave a keck of disgust. 他厌恶地干呕了一声。

例句：She suppressed a keck. 她忍住了呕吐。
近义词：retch, gag
反义词: None
词性: noun


词源:  From Middle English *kekken, likely of imitative origin.  Related to Dutch kikken (“to cough, gasp”), German keichen (“to gasp, pant”).
