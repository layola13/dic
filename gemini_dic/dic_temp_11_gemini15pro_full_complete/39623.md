consentive:/kənˈsɛntɪv/ | adj. | 同意的，一致的 | Based on or marked by consent; agreed upon.  | Example: The decision was made after consentive agreement between all parties.  所有各方一致同意后做出了这个决定。| Example:  They entered into a consentive relationship. 他们建立了一种双方同意的关系。 | Synonyms: consensual, accordant, agreed, unanimous | Antonyms: unconsenting, involuntary, forced, coerced | word property: adjective

consentive:/kənˈsɛntɪv/ | n. | 同意者 | A person who consents. (Rare usage) | Example:  The consentives signed the agreement.  同意者签署了协议。 | Example:  All the consentives were present at the meeting. 所有同意者都出席了会议。 | Synonyms: consenter, approver | Antonyms: dissenter, objector | word property: noun
