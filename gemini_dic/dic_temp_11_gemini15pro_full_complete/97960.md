innumerably:/ɪˈnjuːmərəbli/
adverb
无数地，数不清地
Too numerous to be counted.  Existing in numbers too great to be counted.
The stars in the night sky are innumerably many. 夜晚天空中的星星数不胜数。
The grains of sand on the beach are innumerably many. 海滩上的沙粒数不胜数。
Near synonyms: countless, myriad, numerous, infinite, immeasurable, uncountable
Antonyms: countable, few, limited, measurable, numerable, scarce
词性: 副词
