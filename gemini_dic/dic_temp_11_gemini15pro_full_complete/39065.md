conformers:/kənˈfɔːrmərz/
n.
顺从者；遵从者；一致者
Those who conform to accepted behavior or rules.
Many teenagers are conformers, eager to fit in with their peers. 许多青少年都是顺从者，渴望融入同龄人。
Conforming species adapt to environmental changes by altering their internal conditions.  一些物种通过改变自身的内部条件来适应环境变化。
synonyms:  adapters, compliers, followers
antonyms:  rebels, nonconformists, individualists
noun

conformist:/kənˈfɔːrmɪst/
n.
墨守成规者；循规蹈矩的人
A person who conforms to accepted behavior or established practices.
He's such a conformist – always following the crowd. 他真是个墨守成规的人——总是随大流。
She felt pressured to be a conformist in her conservative community. 在她保守的社区里，她感到要循规蹈矩的压力。
synonyms:  traditionalist, conventionalist, follower
antonyms:  rebel, maverick, nonconformist
noun

conformity:/kənˈfɔːrməti/
n.
一致；遵从；符合
Behavior in accordance with socially accepted conventions or standards.
Conformity is often expected in professional settings. 在职业场合，通常期望人们循规蹈矩。
There is pressure on teenagers to dress in conformity with their peers. 青少年面临着穿着要与同龄人一致的压力。
synonyms:  compliance, obedience, acquiescence
antonyms:  rebellion, nonconformity, individuality
noun

conform:/kənˈfɔːrm/
v.
遵从；顺应；符合；使一致
Comply with rules, standards, or laws.
All students must conform to school regulations. 所有学生都必须遵守学校规章制度。
The building conforms to safety regulations. 这座建筑符合安全规范。
synonyms:  comply, obey, adhere
antonyms:  rebel, disobey, defy
verb
