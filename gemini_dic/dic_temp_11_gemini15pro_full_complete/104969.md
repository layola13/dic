kempha:/ˈkɛmfə/ | n. | 坎普法, 一种梳理羊毛的工具 |  A kempha is a tool used for combing or carding wool, particularly in the Highlands of Scotland.  It resembles a small hand-held rake. |  The shepherd used a kempha to prepare the wool before spinning. 牧羊人用坎普法在纺线前准备羊毛。 | 近义词: wool comb, card | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Scottish Gaelic *ceambach*
