chipotle: /tʃɪˈpoʊtleɪ/ (US) /tʃɪˈpɒtlɪ/ (UK)| n.|墨西哥辣椒，烟熏干辣椒，奇波雷辣椒|A smoked, dried jalapeño chili pepper.  It is used in Mexican and Tex-Mex cuisine.|I love the smoky flavor of chipotle peppers in salsa. 我喜欢莎莎酱里烟熏辣椒的味道。|Synonyms: jalapeño (fresh), smoked chili, chipotle pepper|Antonyms: bell pepper (milder), sweet pepper|noun


chipotle: /tʃɪˈpoʊtleɪ/ (US) /tʃɪˈpɒtlɪ/ (UK)| adj.|烟熏辣椒味的|Flavored with chipotle peppers.|We ordered chipotle chicken for dinner. 我们点了烟熏辣椒鸡肉作为晚餐。|Synonyms: smoky, spicy, peppery|Antonyms: mild, bland|adjective



ETYMOLOGY->
From Nahuatl *chilpoctli (meaning smoked chili), from chilli + poctli (meaning smoked).
