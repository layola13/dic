criterial:/kraɪˈtɪəriəl/| adj.|标准的，准则的|Relating to or serving as a criterion; involving or dependent on a criterion.|The professor explained the criterial attributes of a good research paper. 教授解释了一篇好的研究论文的标准属性。|近义词：standard, normative, decisive|反义词:noncriterial, unimportant|词性:adjective
词源: From criterion + -al.  "Criterion" comes from the Greek "kritērion," meaning "means for judging."
