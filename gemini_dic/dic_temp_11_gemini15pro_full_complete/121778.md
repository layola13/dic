ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrongly, badly") + read. The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-," which is related to Old High German "missa-" (wrong, different). "Read" comes from Old English "rǣdan" (to advise, counsel, interpret, read).
