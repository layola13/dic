catholicization: /kəˌθɒlɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /kəˌθɑːləsəˈzeɪʃən/
n.
天主教化，皈依天主教
The process of converting to Catholicism or making something more Catholic.
The catholicization of England was a long and complex process. 英格兰的天主教化是一个漫长而复杂的过程。
The catholicization of the liturgy caused some controversy.  礼拜仪式的天主教化引起了一些争议。

Synonyms: conversion, proselytization
Antonyms: secularization, apostasy
词性: noun
