heights:/haɪts/
n.
高地，高处；高度；顶点
a high place or area. The highest point or level.
例句：The climber reached dizzying heights. 登山者到达了令人眩晕的高度。
例句：She reached the heights of her profession. 她达到了她职业的顶峰。
近义词：altitudes, peaks, summits, elevations
反义词：depths, lows
词性：名词
