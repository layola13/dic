nonmeditative:/ˌnɒnˌmedɪˈteɪtɪv/
adjective
非冥想的，不沉思的
Not characterized by meditation or contemplation; not meditative.
She took a nonmeditative walk, focusing on the sights and sounds around her rather than her inner thoughts. 她散步时没有沉思，而是专注于周围的景象和声音，而不是她的内心想法。
His nonmeditative approach to problem-solving often led to quick, practical solutions. 他非冥想式的解决问题的方法通常会带来快速、实用的解决方案。
近义词: unreflective, thoughtless, inattentive
反义词: meditative, contemplative, reflective
词性: adjective
