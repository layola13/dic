deep-fry: /diːpˈfraɪ/ | v. | 油炸 | To cook food by submerging it in hot oil. |  例句：They deep-fried the chicken until it was golden brown. 他们把鸡油炸到金黄色。

例句：I love deep-fried onion rings. 我喜欢炸洋葱圈。

| 近义词：fry, French fry | 反义词: bake, boil, steam | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From *deep* + *fry*.  *Deep* meaning "fully submerged" and *fry* meaning "to cook in hot fat."  First recorded in the early 20th century.
