bistro:/ˈbiːstroʊ/ (US), /ˈbɪːstrəʊ/ (UK)| n.|小酒馆，小餐馆|A small, informal restaurant, often serving moderately priced simple meals.  It originated in France.|We had a delicious meal at the local bistro. 我们在当地的小酒馆吃了一顿美味的饭。|近义词：cafe, restaurant, eatery|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) formal restaurant|Noun
