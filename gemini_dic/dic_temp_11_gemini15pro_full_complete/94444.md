implosion:/ɪmˈploʊʒən/ /ɪmˈpləʊʒən/| n.|内爆；崩溃；剧烈收缩|A sudden inward collapse; a sudden, violent compression.|The demolition experts triggered the implosion of the old building. 爆破专家引爆了旧楼，使其向内坍塌。|Synonyms: collapse, downfall, cave-in|Antonyms: explosion, expansion, eruption|noun

implosion:/ɪmˈploʊʒən/ /ɪmˈpləʊʒən/| n.|比喻义：崩溃，垮台|A sudden and complete failure of an organization, system, or plan.|The company's implosion was caused by a combination of bad management and a declining market. 该公司的垮台是由糟糕的管理和市场衰退共同造成的。|Synonyms: downfall, collapse, failure|Antonyms: success, growth, expansion|noun
