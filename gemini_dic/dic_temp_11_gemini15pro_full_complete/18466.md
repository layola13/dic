bemused:/bɪˈmjuːzd/| adj.|困惑的，茫然的，发呆的|A state of being confused, bewildered, or lost in thought; preoccupied.|She looked at him with a bemused expression on her face. 她一脸茫然地看着他。|Synonyms: bewildered, puzzled, perplexed, confused, lost in thought, preoccupied|Antonyms: clear-headed, alert, focused, aware, comprehending|adjective
