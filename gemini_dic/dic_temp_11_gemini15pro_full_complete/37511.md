commoditized:/kəˈmɒdətaɪzd/
adjective
商品化的
Made into a commodity; treated as a mere commodity, interchangeable with other goods of the same type and having little or no brand differentiation.
Generic drugs have commoditized the pharmaceutical industry.  仿制药使制药行业商品化了。
synonyms: generic, standardized, undifferentiated, mass-produced
antonyms: specialized, niche, custom, bespoke
词性: adjective


commoditize:/kəˈmɒdətaɪz/
verb
使商品化
To make (something) into a commodity; to treat (something) as a mere commodity.
The internet has commoditized information. 互联网使信息商品化了。
synonyms: commercialize, market, merchandise
antonyms: specialize, customize
词性: verb
