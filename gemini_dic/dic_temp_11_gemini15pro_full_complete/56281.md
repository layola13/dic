dogger:/ˈdɒɡə(r)/ (UK) /ˈdɑːɡər/ (US)| n.|多格尔船; 两桅渔船; (尤指)从事北海渔业的渔船; 顽固分子; 保守分子|A fishing boat, especially one used in the North Sea fishery. Also, a person with stubbornly held opinions, especially a political conservative.|例句：The dogger sailed out into the rough North Sea. 这艘多格尔渔船驶入了波涛汹涌的北海。

例句：He's a real dogger when it comes to his political views. 在政治观点方面，他是个十足的顽固分子。|近义词：trawler, fisherman, conservative, diehard|反义词:liberal, progressive|词性: noun
