fragments:/ˈfræɡmənts/ (US), /ˈfræɡmənts/ (UK)| n.|碎片，片段|A small part broken off or detached from something. |The archaeologist carefully examined the fragments of pottery.|Synonyms: pieces, scraps, shards, remnants|Antonyms: whole, complete|Noun

fragments:/ˈfræɡment/ (US), /ˈfræɡmənt/ (UK)| v. |破碎，分裂|To break or cause to break into fragments. |The impact fragmented the glass into tiny pieces.|Synonyms: shatter, splinter, disintegrate|Antonyms: unite, combine|Verb
