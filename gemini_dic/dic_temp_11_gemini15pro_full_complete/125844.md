Simple Present:
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned so far? = 到目前为止你学到了什么？
What has she eaten today? = 她今天吃了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about earlier? = 她之前在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you arrived? = 你到达之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before dinner? = 晚饭前她吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? = 他们已经讨论了几个小时什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气会怎样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? = 他们将在会议上讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have accomplished by next year? = 到明年你将完成什么？
What will they have done by then? = 到那时他们将做什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for five years? = 你将学习五年什么？
What will she have been doing for a living by then? = 到那时她将靠什么谋生？
