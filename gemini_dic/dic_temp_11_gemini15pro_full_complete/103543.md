jogglers:/ˈdʒɒɡlərz/ (US) /ˈdʒɑːɡlərz/ (UK)| n. |杂耍者；玩杂耍的人；同时做很多事情的人|A person who juggles objects, often while jogging. Also, someone who manages multiple tasks simultaneously.|例句：The jogglers amazed the crowd by juggling clubs while running a marathon.  杂耍者一边跑马拉松一边玩杂耍，令观众叹为观止。

例句：She's a real joggler, balancing work, family, and her hobbies. 她真是个高手，能同时兼顾工作、家庭和爱好。|近义词：multitasker, juggler |反义词: specialist, procrastinator |词性:noun
