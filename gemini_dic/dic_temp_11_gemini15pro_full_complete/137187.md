nonsufferances:/nɒnˈsʌfərənsɪz/
n.
不容忍；不宽容；无法容忍的事情；令人无法忍受的事情
Lack of tolerance or patience; things that are not tolerated or are unbearable.
The king's nonsufferance of dissent led to many executions. 国王对异议的不容忍导致了许多人被处决。

The constant noise was a nonsufferance to the residents.  持续不断的噪音让居民们无法忍受。

Near Synonyms: intolerance, impatience, forbearance
Antonyms: tolerance, patience, forbearance
