hyper-linked:/ˌhaɪpərˈlɪŋkt/| adj.| 超链接的 |Connected to another web page or part of a web page by a link. | Example: The website is hyper-linked to several other resources.  这个网站通过超链接连接到其他几个资源。| Example: Click on the hyper-linked text to learn more. 点击超链接文本了解更多信息。 | Synonyms: linked, connected | Antonyms: unconnected, isolated | adjective

/ˌhaɪpərˈlɪŋkt/| n.| 超链接 |A link from a hypertext file or document to another location or file, typically activated by clicking on a highlighted word or image on the screen.| Example:  This hyper-link will take you to the company's homepage. 这个超链接会带你到公司的主页。| Example: I included several hyper-links in my email. 我的电子邮件中包含了几个超链接。 | Synonyms: link, hyperlink, web link | Antonyms: N/A | noun
