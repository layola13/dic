overpresumptively:/ˈoʊvərprɪˈzʌmptɪvli/
| adv. | 过于冒昧地，过分放肆地 |  In a manner that is excessively or unwarrantedly presumptuous; too boldly or forwardly. |  He overpresumptively assumed he would be welcomed with open arms, only to be met with cold indifference. 他过分自信地认为他会受到热烈欢迎，结果却只遇到了冷淡。| 近义词: arrogantly, presumptuously, boldly, audaciously | 反义词: humbly, modestly, respectfully, cautiously | 词性: 副词

overpresumptively: /ˈoʊvərprɪˈzʌmptɪvli/
| 词源 | From "over" (excessively) + "presumptively" (in a presumptuous manner). "Presumptuous" derives from Late Latin "praesumptuosus," from "praesumptio" (anticipation, arrogance), from "praesumere" (to anticipate, take for granted). |  由"over"（过度地）+ "presumptively"（以冒昧的方式）组成。"Presumptuous"源自晚期拉丁语"praesumptuosus"，来自"praesumptio"（预期，傲慢），来自"praesumere"（预期，想当然）。|-|-
