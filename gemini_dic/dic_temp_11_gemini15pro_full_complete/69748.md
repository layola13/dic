feretory: /ˈfɛrɪtəri/ | n. | 棺架，灵柩架 | A bier or framework for supporting a coffin. | The feretory was draped in black velvet. 棺架上覆盖着黑色天鹅绒。| Synonym: bier, catafalque | Antonym: None | Noun
