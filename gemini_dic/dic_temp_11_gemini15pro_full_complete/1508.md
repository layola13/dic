acetol:/ˈæsɪtɒl/ (US) /ˈæsɪtɔːl/ (UK)| n.|丙酮醇; 羟基丙酮; 乙酰甲醇|A colorless liquid, CH₃COCH₂OH, usually made by reducing pyruvic acid; hydroxyacetone. It is used as a solvent and in the synthesis of other organic compounds.|Acetol is an intermediate in the metabolism of acetone.|近义词: hydroxyacetone, 1-hydroxy-2-propanone|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From acetyl + -ol (indicating an alcohol).
