jailors:/ˈdʒeɪlɚz/ (US), /ˈdʒeɪləz/ (UK)| n.|狱卒，看守|A jailor is a person who is in charge of a jail and the prisoners in it. |The jailor brought the prisoner his meal. 狱卒给囚犯送来了饭。|The jailors patrolled the corridors. 狱卒在走廊里巡逻。|近义词：warden, guard, keeper|反义词:prisoner, inmate|Noun
