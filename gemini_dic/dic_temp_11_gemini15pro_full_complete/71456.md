flamelike:/ˈfleɪmlaɪk/
adjective
像火焰的，火焰般的
Resembling or characteristic of a flame.
The dancer's movements were flamelike, fluid and vibrant. 舞者的动作像火焰一样，流畅而充满活力。
The aurora borealis painted flamelike streaks across the night sky. 北极光在夜空中描绘出火焰般的条纹。
近义词: fiery, flaming, ablaze
反义词: subdued, dull, extinguished
词性: adjective
