antennae:/ænˈteniː/
n.
触角；天线
A pair of long, thin sensory appendages on the heads of insects, crustaceans, and some other invertebrates. Also, a metal rod or wire used to transmit or receive radio or television signals.
Butterflies use their antennae to smell. 蝴蝶用它们的触角来闻气味。
The television's antennae was damaged in the storm. 电视的天线在暴风雨中损坏了。
近义词:feelers, aerials
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性:名词

antenna:/ænˈtɛnə/
n.
触角；天线
A long, thin sensory appendage on the head of an insect, crustacean, or some other invertebrates. Also, a metal rod or wire used to transmit or receive radio or television signals. (单数形式)
The ant cleaned its antenna meticulously. 蚂蚁小心翼翼地清洁它的触角。
A car antenna helps receive radio signals. 汽车天线有助于接收无线电信号。
近义词:feeler, aerial
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin antenna ‘sail yard’, from Latin antemna. The insect's appendage was so named from a fancied resemblance.
