funkily:/ˈfʌŋkɪli/
adjective
古怪地，时髦地，别致地
in a funky way; with a strong rhythmic beat and syncopation characteristic of funk music.  It can also mean unconventionally stylish and fashionable.
She danced funkily to the music. 她随着音乐古怪地跳着舞。
The room was decorated funkily with vintage furniture and bright colors.  房间用复古家具和鲜艳的色彩装饰得别致时尚。
近义词:groovily, stylishly, trendily, eccentrically
反义词: plainly, conventionally, dully, boringly
词性:adjective
