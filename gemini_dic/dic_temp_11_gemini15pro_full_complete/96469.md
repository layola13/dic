individualized:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzd/ | adj. |个性化的；个体化的| Designed or tailored to meet the specific needs of an individual. |  例句：The school offers individualized learning programs for each student.  学校为每个学生提供个性化的学习计划。

例句：She received individualized attention from her tutor. 她得到了导师的个性化关注。

近义词：personalized, customized, tailored, bespoke | 反义词：standardized, generic, uniform | 词性: adjective

词源: From individual + -ize + -ed.  "Individual" comes from Late Latin *individuālis, from Latin individuus "indivisible," from in- "not" + dividuus "divisible."
