hermes:/ˈhɜːrmiːz/ (US) /ˈhɜːmiːz/ (UK)| n.|赫尔墨斯；爱马仕|Hermes is the Greek god of trade, heraldry, merchants, commerce, roads, thieves, trickery, sports, athletes, and border crossings, and is the son of Zeus and Maia.
