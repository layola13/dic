chutnees:/ˈtʃʌtniːz/| n. |酸辣酱|A condiment originating in India, made of fruits, vegetables, spices, and often vinegar or tamarind. It's often served with curries or other savory dishes.|We had mango chutney with our curry. 我们咖喱配了芒果酸辣酱。|近义词：relish, sauce, condiment|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bland food")|Noun
