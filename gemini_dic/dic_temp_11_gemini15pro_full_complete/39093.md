confraternity's:/ˌkɑːnfrəˈtɜːrnətiz/
n.
兄弟会, 宗教团体, 同业公会
a brotherhood or association, especially a religious group devoted to charitable work or a group of people with a shared interest or profession.
The confraternity's annual meeting was held in the cathedral.  兄弟会的年会在教堂举行。
The painter's confraternity's exhibition showcased a diverse range of styles. 画家同业公会的展览展示了各种各样的风格。
Synonyms: brotherhood, fellowship, association, guild, society, league
Antonyms:  division, disunity, discord, isolation
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin confraternitas (“brotherhood”), from Latin con- (“with, together”) + fraternitas (“brotherhood”), from frater (“brother”).
