nonvacantly:/ˌnɒnˈveɪkəntli/
adv.
非空地，不空缺地
in a way that is not vacant or empty.
The position was filled nonvacantly.  这个职位被不空缺地填补了。
The apartment was nonvacantly occupied by a family of four.  这套公寓被一个四口之家不空缺地占据着。
近义词:occupiedly, filled
反义词:vacantly, emptily
词性:副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  not, opposite of  非，相反的
vacant (adjective): empty, unoccupied  空的，未被占用的
-ly (suffix):  forms adverbs  构成副词
