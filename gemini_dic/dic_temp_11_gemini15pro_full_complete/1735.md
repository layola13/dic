acquiesce:/ˌækwiˈes/| v. |默许；勉强同意|to accept something reluctantly but without protest|例句：He acquiesced in the decision. 他默许了这个决定。|例句：She finally acquiesced to their demands. 她最终勉强同意了他们的要求。|近义词：consent, comply, agree, assent|反义词：resist, oppose, object, dissent|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acquiescere*, from *ad-* ("to, towards") + *quiescere* ("to rest, be quiet")
