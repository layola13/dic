granaries:/ˈɡrænəriːz/| n.|粮仓，谷仓|A storehouse for threshed grain.|The farmer stored the wheat in his granaries. 农民把小麦储存在他的粮仓里。|近义词：silos, barns, storehouses|反义词: N/A|名词
词源：From Middle English *granarie, from Anglo-Norman granarie, Old French granarie, from Medieval Latin granarium, from Latin granum (“grain”).
