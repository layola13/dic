hydrocarbostyril: /ˌhaɪdrəʊkɑːrboʊˈstaɪrɪl/ | n. | 氢化喹啉酮;二氢-2(1H)-喹啉酮 | a crystalline compound, C9H9NO, obtained from carbostyril by reduction, or from o-aminohydrocinnamic acid by dehydration. It is used in organic synthesis. | Hydrocarbostyril is an important intermediate in the synthesis of various pharmaceuticals. 氢化喹啉酮是合成各种药物的重要中间体。 |  Hydrocarbostyril derivatives have shown promising antibacterial activity. 氢化喹啉酮衍生物已显示出良好的抗菌活性。 | synonyms: 1,2,3,4-tetrahydroquinolin-2-one, 2-oxo-1,2,3,4-tetrahydroquinoline | antonyms: N/A | noun
