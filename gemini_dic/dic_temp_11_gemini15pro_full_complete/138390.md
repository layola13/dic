notorious:/noʊˈtɔːriəs/ | adj. | 臭名昭著的；声名狼藉的 | Widely and unfavorably known. |例句：He's notorious for his bad temper. 他因脾气暴躁而臭名昭著。|例句：The city is notorious for its high crime rate. 这座城市因高犯罪率而声名狼藉。| 近义词：infamous, disreputable, scandalous | 反义词：famous, celebrated, reputable | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *notorius*, from Latin nōtus (“known”).
