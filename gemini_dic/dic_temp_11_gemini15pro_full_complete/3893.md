agreee:/əˈɡriː/ | v. |同意，赞成|To have the same opinion or feeling as someone else; to consent or approve.|例句：I agree with you on this matter. 我同意你在这件事上的看法。

例句：They agreed to meet the following week. 他们同意下周见面。

|近义词：concur, consent, assent|反义词：disagree, dissent, oppose|词性：动词

词源:
From Middle English agreen, from Old French agreer (“to please, consent to, agree to”), from à (“to”) + greer (“to please”), from Late Latin gratus (“pleasing, agreeable, thankful”).
