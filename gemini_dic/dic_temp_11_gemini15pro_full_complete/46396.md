daemonistic:/ˌdiːmɒˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. |恶魔的；魔鬼似的；着魔的 | Of or relating to demons; demonic.  Exhibiting the characteristics of a demon, especially in being malicious or cruel.  | He had a daemonistic gleam in his eyes. 他眼中閃爍著惡魔般的光芒。|  The villain's daemonistic laughter echoed through the halls. 坏蛋恶魔般的笑声在走廊里回荡。 |  近义词: demonic, satanic, fiendish, devilish | 反义词: angelic, divine, heavenly | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *daemonisticus, from Ancient Greek δαίμων (daímōn, “deity, spirit, demon”) + -istic.
