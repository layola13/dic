miscalculated:/ˌmɪskælkjəˈleɪtɪd/| adj. v. (past tense)|计算错误的，估算错误的|Made an error in calculating; judged or planned wrongly.|He miscalculated the distance and ran out of gas before reaching the next town. 他错误地估计了距离，在到达下一个城镇之前就没油了.|Synonyms: misjudged, underestimated, overestimated, misestimated|Antonyms: calculated correctly, estimated accurately|Verb (past tense)

miscalculate:/ˌmɪskælkjəˈleɪt/| v.|错误地计算，估算错|To calculate or judge something incorrectly.|I miscalculated how much time it would take to drive there. 我错误地估计了开车去那里所需的时间。|Synonyms: misjudge, underestimate, overestimate, misestimate|Antonyms: calculate correctly, estimate accurately|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrongly, badly") + calculate (from Latin calculāre, "to reckon, compute").
