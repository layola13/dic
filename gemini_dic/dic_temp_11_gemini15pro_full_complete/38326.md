conceited:/kənˈsiːtɪd/| adj.|自负的，骄傲自满的|Excessively proud of oneself; vain.|例句：He's so conceited; he thinks he's the best at everything. 他太自负了，觉得自己什么都最厉害。|近义词：arrogant, narcissistic, vain, egotistical, self-important|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from obsolete conceit ‘concept, opinion’ (from Old French conceite , past participle of concevoir ‘conceive’) + -ed. The original sense was ‘having a high opinion of oneself’.
