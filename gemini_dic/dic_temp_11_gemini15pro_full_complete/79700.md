gloze:/ɡloʊz/| v. n.|掩饰，注释，欺骗|(v.) to explain or comment on something, often in a misleading way; to minimize or gloss over; (n.) a comment or explanation, often misleading; a deceptive appearance|He glozed over the company's financial difficulties. 他掩饰了公司的财务困难。

The politician glozed the scandal with vague promises of reform.  这位政客用含糊的改革承诺掩饰了丑闻。

He added a gloze to the difficult passage in the textbook. 他在课本的难句处加了注释。

Her apparent calm was merely a gloze over her deep anxiety. 她表面上的平静只是掩盖了她内心深处的焦虑。 |近义词：explain, comment, gloss over, disguise, conceal|反义词：reveal, expose|词性：动词, 名词
