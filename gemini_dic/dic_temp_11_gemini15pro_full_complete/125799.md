murkest:/ˈmɜːrkɪst/ | adj. | 最黑暗的，最阴沉的 |  Superlative form of murky: dark and gloomy, especially due to thick mist or smoke. | The murkiest depths of the ocean remain largely unexplored. 海洋最黑暗的深处很大程度上仍未被探索。| The story takes a sinister turn in its murkiest chapters.  这个故事在其最阴沉的章节中发生了险恶的转折。 | 近义词: darkest, gloomiest, dimmest | 反义词: brightest, lightest, clearest | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *mirke, from Old English *myrce (“dark, murky”), from Proto-Germanic *murkaz (“dark”), from Proto-Indo-European *merg- (“to blink, shimmer, disappear”). Cognate with West Frisian mirk (“dark”), Dutch merk (“mark, sign”), German merk- (“dark”), murky.
