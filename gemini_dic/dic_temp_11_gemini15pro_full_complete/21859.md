bodiliness:/ˈbɒdɪlɪnɪs/ /ˈbɑːdɪlinɪs/
n.|肉体性，物质性；肉欲|The state or quality of being bodily or physical; physicality; sensuality.|He explored the themes of bodiliness and spirituality in his art. 他在他的艺术中探索了肉体性和精神性的主题。|近义词：physicality, corporality, fleshiness|反义词：spirituality, immateriality|词性:名词

Etymology:
From "bodily" + "-ness".  "Bodily" comes from Old English "bodilic,"  from "bodig" (body) + "-lic" (like).  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
