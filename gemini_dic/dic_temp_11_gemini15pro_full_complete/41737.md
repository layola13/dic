corrosive:/kəˈroʊsɪv/
adjective
腐蚀性的；侵蚀性的
tending to cause corrosion
Example: The effect of the acid was highly corrosive. 这种酸的腐蚀性很强。

Example:  Rust is corrosive to metal.  铁锈对金属有腐蚀作用。

近义词:caustic, erosive, abrasive
反义词:protective, preservative
词性:adjective
