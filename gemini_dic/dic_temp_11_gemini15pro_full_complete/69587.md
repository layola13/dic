felter:/ˈfɛltər/| n. v. |毡匠；使缠结；毡合；|A person who makes or works with felt.  To become matted or tangled. To join or compact (material) by felting. |The felter carefully shaped the wool into a hat. 毡匠小心地把羊毛塑造成一顶帽子。 He felted the two pieces of fabric together. 他把两块布毡合在一起。 The sheep's wool had felted in the rain. 羊的羊毛在雨中缠结在一起了。|近义词：mat, tangle,  (v.) interweave,  (n.) milliner|反义词: smooth, untangle, separate (v.)|词性:名词, 动词
