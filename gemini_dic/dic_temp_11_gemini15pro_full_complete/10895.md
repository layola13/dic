This style is often associated with light and airy structures.|The temple was built in the araeosystyle, creating a sense of spaciousness. 这座寺庙采用疏柱式建造，营造出一种宽敞的感觉。|The architect chose an araeosystyle design for the colonnade to maximize the view.  建筑师选择了疏柱式设计柱廊以最大限度地拓宽视野。|近义词:wide-spaced, open, airy|反义词:pycnostyle, close-spaced|词性:adjective
