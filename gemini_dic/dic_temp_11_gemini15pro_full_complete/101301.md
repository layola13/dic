invulnerableness:/ɪnˌvʌlnərəbəlnəs/
n.
刀枪不入；不会受伤害；无懈可击
The state of being invulnerable; immunity to attack or harm.
His emotional invulnerableness made him seem cold and distant. 他情感上的无懈可击让他看起来冷漠疏远。
近义词:impenetrableness, immunity, impassivity, insusceptibility
反义词:vulnerability, weakness, susceptibility
词性:名词

Etymology:
From invulnerable + -ness.  Invulnerable comes from Late Latin invulnerabilis, from in- "not" + vulnerabilis "vulnerable," from vulnerare "to wound," from vulnus "wound."
