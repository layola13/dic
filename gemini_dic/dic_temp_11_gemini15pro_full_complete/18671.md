bepuffed:/bɪˈpʌft/
adjective
肿胀的，傲慢的
inflated with pride or vanity; puffed up.
Her bepuffed ego made it difficult for her to accept criticism. 她膨胀的自我让她难以接受批评。
The bepuffed sleeves of the dress gave her a regal air.  这件衣服膨胀的袖子让她显得雍容华贵。
synonyms:inflated, pompous, arrogant, conceited, vain, swollen
antonyms:humble, modest, unassuming
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + puffed, past participle of puff.  "Puff" comes from Old English *pyffan, of imitative origin.
