fleshen:/ˈflɛʃən/ | v. |充实，使具体化，赋予血肉 |To give substance or detail to something; to make something more concrete or realistic. |The author fleshed out the character by describing his childhood.  作者通过描述他的童年使角色更加丰满。

The playwright fleshed out the scene with vivid dialogue. 剧作家用生动的对话使场景更加充实。|近义词：expand, develop, elaborate, enrich |反义词：abridge, condense, summarize|词性:动词
