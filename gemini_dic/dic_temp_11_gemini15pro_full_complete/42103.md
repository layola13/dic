cotswold:/ˈkɑːtsˌwoʊld/ | n. |科茨沃尔德|A region in south central England known for its picturesque villages, rolling hills, and honey-colored limestone buildings. |例句：We spent a week exploring the Cotswolds. 我们花了一周时间探索科茨沃尔德。|例句：The Cotswolds are famous for their charming villages. 科茨沃尔德以其迷人的村庄而闻名。|近义词：countryside, rural area |反义词:city, urban area |词性:noun

cotswold:/ˈkɑːtsˌwoʊld/ | adj. |科茨沃尔德式的|Relating to or characteristic of the Cotswolds region, especially its traditional architecture and way of life. |例句：We stayed in a charming Cotswold cottage. 我们住在一个迷人的科茨沃尔德小屋里。|例句：The Cotswold stone gives the houses a warm, honey-colored hue. 科茨沃尔德的石头赋予房屋温暖的蜂蜜色调。|近义词：rustic, traditional |反义词:modern, contemporary |词性:adjective
