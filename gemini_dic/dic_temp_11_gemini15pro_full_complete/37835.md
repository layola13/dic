competency:/ˈkɒmpɪtənsi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpɪtənsi/ (US)| n.|能力，胜任，权限|The ability to do something successfully or efficiently.|She demonstrated competency in managing difficult clients. 她展现了管理难缠客户的能力。|近义词：ability, capability, skill, aptitude, proficiency|反义词：inability, incompetence, inadequacy|noun
