discompany:/ˌdɪsˈkʌmpəni/
verb
使失去同伴,使孤单
to leave the company of; abandon
例句：His wife discompanied him after twenty years of marriage.  他妻子在结婚二十年后离开了他。

例句：The soldier was discompanied by his unit during the retreat.  这名士兵在撤退期间与他的部队失散了。

近义词：abandon, desert, forsake, leave
反义词：accompany, join, escort

词性：v.
