bodyworkers:/ˈbɒdiwɜːkəz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdiwɜːrkərz/ (US)| n.|按摩师，身体工作者|People who work with the body, such as massage therapists, physical therapists, chiropractors, etc.|例句：Many bodyworkers use a variety of techniques to help their clients. 许多身体工作者使用各种技术来帮助他们的客户。|例句：She trained as a bodyworker after recovering from a serious injury herself. 她自己从重伤中恢复后，接受了身体工作者的培训。|近义词：massage therapists, physical therapists, healers, practitioners|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered  office worker, sedentary worker depending on context)|词性: Noun
