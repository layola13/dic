garret:/ˈɡærɪt/| n. |阁楼；顶楼；小室|A small, uncomfortable room at the top of a house, typically under a sloping roof.|He lived in a tiny garret at the top of the old building. 他住在老房子顶层的一个小阁楼里。|The artist worked in a cramped garret, filled with canvases and paintbrushes.  这位艺术家在一个狭小的阁楼里工作，里面堆满了画布和画笔。|近义词：attic, loft|反义词:basement, cellar|词性:noun
词源:From Old French "garite" (watchtower), from "garir" (to protect).
