intervisited:/ˌɪntərˈvɪzɪtɪd/
verb
互相访问；互访
Having exchanged visits with each other.  The two families intervisited frequently.  这两个家庭经常互相拜访。
例句：The two tribes intervisited during the annual festival.  这两个部落在一年一度的节日里互相访问。

例句：The scientists intervisited their labs to share research findings.  科学家们互相访问彼此的实验室，以分享研究成果。

近义词：visited each other, exchanged visits
反义词：isolated, separated
词性：verb
