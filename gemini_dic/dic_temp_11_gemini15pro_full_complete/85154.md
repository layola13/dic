hardtail:/ˈhɑːrdteɪl/
n.
硬尾（自行车）
A bicycle, especially a mountain bike, that does not have rear suspension.
He prefers the responsiveness of a hardtail mountain bike on technical trails. 他更喜欢在技术性赛道上骑硬尾山地车的灵敏性。
hardtail bicycle, hardtail mountain bike, rigid frame
full-suspension
noun
