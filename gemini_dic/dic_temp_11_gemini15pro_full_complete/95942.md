incubator: /ˈɪŋkjubeɪtər/ (US), /ˈɪŋkjubeɪtə/ (UK)| n.|孵化器；保育箱；恒温箱|An apparatus used to hatch eggs or grow microorganisms under controlled conditions. Also refers to an organization that helps startup companies develop by providing resources and support.|例句：The startup thrived in the business incubator. 这家初创企业在商业孵化器中蓬勃发展。

例句：The eggs were placed in an incubator to hatch.  这些蛋被放在孵化器里孵化。


近义词：brooder, hatchery, nurturer|反义词:|词性:noun

incubator: /ˈɪŋkjubeɪtər/ (US), /ˈɪŋkjubeɪtə/ (UK)| n.|早产儿保育箱|An enclosed apparatus in which premature or sick infants are kept at a controlled temperature. |例句：The premature baby was kept in an incubator for several weeks.  早产儿在保育箱里待了好几个星期。

例句：The incubator helped the baby regulate its temperature. 保育箱帮助婴儿调节体温。

近义词：isolette|反义词:|词性:noun
