boggle:/ˈbɒɡəl/ (British), /ˈbɑːɡəl/ (American)| v. n.| 使困惑；犹豫；吃惊；难以置信的事物 | To hesitate or have difficulty because of confusion, fear, or scruples; to overwhelm or bewilder with wonder or strangeness; something that causes wonder or bewilderment. | The sheer complexity of the task boggles the mind. 这项任务的绝对复杂性令人难以置信。|Synonyms: amaze, astound, bewilder, confound, nonplus, perplex, puzzle|Antonyms: clarify, enlighten, explain, understand|verb
