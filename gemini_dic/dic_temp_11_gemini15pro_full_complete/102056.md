isometric:/ˌaɪsəˈmetrɪk/ | adj. | 等距的，等长的，等轴的 |  Relating to or characterized by equality of measure.  Of or having equal dimensions. Involving muscular contraction against resistance without significant change of length of muscle.  |  Isometric exercises help build strength. 等长运动有助于增强力量。 The crystals are isometric. 这些晶体是等轴的。 |  equal, uniform, congruent |anisometric, unequal  | adjective
