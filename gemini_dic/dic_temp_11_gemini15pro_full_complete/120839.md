misawarded:/ˌmɪsəˈwɔːrdɪd/
adjective
错误地授予的，不当授予的
incorrectly given or awarded, especially a prize, honor, or other accolade.
The prize was misawarded to the wrong contestant.  这个奖项被错误地颁给了错误的参赛者。
The medal was misawarded due to a clerical error.  由于文书错误，奖牌被错误地颁发了。
Synonyms: wrongly awarded, improperly awarded, mistakenly awarded
Antonyms: correctly awarded, justly awarded, deservedly awarded
词性: adjective
