frizzed:/frɪzd/ | adj. | 卷曲的，蓬乱的 |  formed into small, tight curls, often in a messy or uncontrolled way.  Hair that has become dry, brittle, and tightly curled. |  Her hair was frizzed from the humidity. 她的头发因为潮湿而卷曲了。 | synonyms:  curly, kinky, fuzzy, bushy, unruly | antonyms: straight, smooth, sleek | adjective

frizz:/frɪz/ | v. | （使）卷曲，（使）蓬乱 | to form tight curls, often in a messy or uncontrolled way, usually because of humidity or dryness.  | The humid weather frizzed my hair. 潮湿的天气使我的头发卷曲了。 | synonyms: curl, kink, fluff up | antonyms: straighten, smooth | verb

frizz:/frɪz/ | n. | 卷发，蓬乱的头发 | a state of being tightly curled, often messy and uncontrolled, especially of hair. |  There's too much frizz in my hair today. 今天我的头发太蓬乱了。 | synonyms:  curl, kink, fuzz | antonyms: straightness, smoothness | noun
