interpenetrant:/ˌɪntərˈpɛnɪtrənt/| adj.|互相渗透的，互相穿透的|mutually penetrating; permeating one another.  This describes something that penetrates or pervades another thing mutually, so that they are intertwined or mixed throughout.|The polymer network is formed by two interpenetrant polymers.  这种聚合物网络是由两种互相渗透的聚合物形成的。|The roots of the two trees were interpenetrant. 这两棵树的根互相缠绕。|intertwined, interlaced, intermingled, pervasive|separate, distinct|adjective
