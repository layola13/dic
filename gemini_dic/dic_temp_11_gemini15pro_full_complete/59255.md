e-ticket:/ˈiːtɪkɪt/
n.
电子客票
An electronic ticket, typically for air travel, stored in a database and not issued as a paper document.
Did you print out your e-ticket for the flight? 你打印了你的电子机票了吗？
Ticket, voucher
Paper ticket
