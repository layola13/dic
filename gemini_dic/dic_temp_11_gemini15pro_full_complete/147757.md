overprogram:/ˌoʊvərˈproʊɡræm/| v.|过度安排；安排过多的节目；超量编程|To schedule too many activities or tasks within a given timeframe; In computing, to write excessive or redundant code.|例句：The summer camp overprogrammed the children with activities, leaving them exhausted. 夏令营给孩子们安排了过多的活动，让他们筋疲力尽。

例句：The software engineer overprogrammed the application, resulting in performance issues.  软件工程师为应用程序编写了过多的代码，导致性能问题。|近义词：overschedule, overload|反义词: underprogram, underschedule|词性:动词
