clarion:/ˈklæriən/| adj. n.|嘹亮的，清晰的；号角|A clarion call is a strong and clear request for people to do something.  A clarion is a type of medieval trumpet.|The king issued a clarion call to arms. 国王发出嘹亮的战斗号召。|近义词：clear, resonant, ringing, trumpet|反义词：muffled, indistinct, faint|词性:形容词,名词
