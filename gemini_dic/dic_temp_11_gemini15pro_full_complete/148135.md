oversell: /ˌoʊvərˈsel/ | v. | 过度销售；吹嘘过度 | To sell or promote more of something than can be delivered or is realistic. | The company oversold its new product and couldn't meet the demand.  这家公司过度宣传了它的新产品，无法满足需求。 |  The salesman oversold the car's features, and I was disappointed when I bought it.  销售员过分夸大了这辆车的性能，我买下后很失望。 | 近义词：exaggerate, hype, overstate | 反义词: undersell, downplay | 词性: 动词


词源: From over- + sell.  "Over-" prefix meaning "too much" combined with "sell".
