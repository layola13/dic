orbiters:/ˈɔːrbɪtərz/
noun
轨道飞行器；绕轨运行的航天器
A spacecraft designed to orbit a celestial body, such as a planet or moon.
The orbiters sent back stunning images of Mars. 轨道飞行器发回了令人惊叹的火星图像。
