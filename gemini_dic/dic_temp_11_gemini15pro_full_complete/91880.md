hyperintelligent:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənt/
adjective
超级智能的
possessing or showing extremely high intelligence
例句：The hyperintelligent computer quickly solved the complex equation.  超级智能的计算机很快解决了这个复杂的方程式。

例句：Scientists are exploring the potential dangers of hyperintelligent AI. 科学家们正在探索超级智能人工智能的潜在危险。
近义词：superintelligent, exceptionally intelligent, highly intelligent, brilliant, genius
反义词：unintelligent, stupid, dull, simpleminded
词性: adjective
