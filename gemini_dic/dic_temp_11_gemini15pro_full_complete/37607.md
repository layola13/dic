communicative:/kəˈmjuːnɪkətɪv/ | adj. | 健谈的；沟通的；爱说话的 | Willing to talk and share information.  | She's a very communicative child. 她是一个非常健谈的孩子。 |  He's not very communicative, is he? 他不太健谈，是吗？| 近义词:talkative, outgoing, sociable, expressive | 反义词:reserved, reticent, withdrawn, uncommunicative | 词性:形容词
词源: Late 18th century: from communicate + -ive.
