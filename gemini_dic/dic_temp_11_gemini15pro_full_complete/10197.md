apostatic:/əˈpɒstætɪk/ /ˌeɪpəˈstætɪk/| adj.|叛教的，脱党的，变节的|Abandoning or renouncing a religious or political belief or principle.|例句：His apostatic views led to his excommunication from the church. 他的叛教观点导致他被逐出教会。

例句：The apostatic writer was ostracized by his former comrades.  这位变节的作家被他以前的同志们排斥。

|近义词：heretical, dissident, renegade, traitorous, faithless|反义词：orthodox, faithful, loyal|词性: adjective


apostate:/əˈpɒsteɪt/ /ˈeɪpəˌsteɪt/| n.|叛教者，脱党者，变节者|A person who renounces a religious or political belief or principle.|例句：The apostate was shunned by his former community.  这个叛教者被他以前的社区所回避。

例句：He was declared an apostate and banished from the kingdom. 他被宣布为叛教者并被驱逐出王国。

|近义词：heretic, dissident, renegade, traitor|反义词：believer, loyalist|词性: noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *apostaticus*, from Greek apostatikós (“rebellious”), from apostátes (“apostate, deserter”).  Ultimately from Greek apostasthai (“to stand away from, revolt”).
