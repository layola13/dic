exploded:/ɪkˈsploʊdɪd/
exploded|v.|爆炸，爆发，激增| to burst or shatter violently and noisily as a result of rapid combustion, decomposition, or overpressure; to increase suddenly and rapidly in number or intensity|The bomb exploded in the city center. 炸弹在市中心爆炸了。|近义词：burst, detonated, erupted, blew up|反义词:implode, collapse|词性:Verb (past tense of explode)

exploded:/ɪkˈsploʊdɪd/
exploded|adj.|爆炸的，激增的|having burst or shattered violently and noisily; having increased suddenly and rapidly|The exploded firework left debris all over the lawn. 爆炸的烟花在草坪上留下了碎片。|近义词：burst, ruptured|反义词:intact, whole|词性:Adjective (past participle of explode)
