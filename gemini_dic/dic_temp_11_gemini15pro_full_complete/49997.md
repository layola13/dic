denizens:/ˈdɛnɪzənz/
noun
居民，栖息动物
inhabitants or occupants of a particular place.
The denizens of the forest include deer, foxes, and owls. 森林的栖息动物包括鹿、狐狸和猫头鹰。
inhabitants, occupants, residents, dwellers
aliens, foreigners, outsiders
