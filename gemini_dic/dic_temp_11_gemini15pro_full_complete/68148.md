failer:/ˈfeɪlər/| n.|失败者；失败的事物|a person or thing that fails to succeed. |例句：He was a failer in business. 他在生意上是个失败者。

例句：The project was deemed a failer. 这个项目被认为是失败的。|近义词：loser, flop, failure|反义词:success, winner|词性:noun
fail:/feɪl/|v.|失败；不及格；未能；衰退|to be unsuccessful in achieving one's goal.|词性:verb
