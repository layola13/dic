melitose: /ˈmelɪˌtoʊs/ | n. | 蜜三糖 | A trisaccharide sugar found in some honeys, notably that of Eucalyptus. It is composed of two glucose units and one fructose unit. | Melitose is a less common sugar than sucrose.  蜜三糖是一种比蔗糖少见的糖。| Example: The honey was analyzed and found to contain a high percentage of melitose.  对蜂蜜进行了分析，发现其中含有高比例的蜜三糖。| Synonym: raffinose, melezitose | Antonym: N/A | Noun
