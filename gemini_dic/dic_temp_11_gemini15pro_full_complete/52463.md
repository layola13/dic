dieselization:/ˌdiːzəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|柴油化，柴油机化|The process of converting gasoline engines to diesel engines, or of adopting diesel engines more widely.  The dieselization of the fleet resulted in significant fuel savings.|The dieselization of the trucking industry has been ongoing for decades.| 近义词: conversion, transition|反义词: gasolineization (not commonly used)|词性:名词

词源: diesel + -ization

diesel: named after Rudolf Diesel, inventor of the diesel engine.
-ization: a suffix forming nouns meaning the action or process of making or becoming.
