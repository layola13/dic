overflood:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌd/ | v. | 淹没，泛滥 | To flood excessively; to inundate. | The river overflooded its banks after the heavy rain. 河水在大雨过后漫过河岸。| The dam burst and overflooded the valley. 大坝决堤，淹没了山谷。 | 近义词: inundate, deluge, swamp | 反义词: drain, dry | 词性: 动词

overflood:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌd/ | n. | 泛滥，溢流 | An excessive flood; an inundation. | The overflood caused significant damage to the crops. 洪水泛滥对农作物造成了重大损害。 | The overflood from the burst pipe ruined the furniture. 爆裂的管道溢出的水毁坏了家具。 | 近义词: deluge, inundation, flood | 反义词: drought, dryness | 词性: 名词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "flood" (noun/verb referring to an overflow of water)
