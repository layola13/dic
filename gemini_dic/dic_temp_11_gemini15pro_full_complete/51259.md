desubsidize:/diːˈsʌbsɪdaɪz/
verb
取消补贴；停止资助
to stop giving money to a company or industry to help it continue to function
The government plans to desubsidize the farming industry over the next five years. 政府计划在未来五年内取消对农业的补贴。
The university desubsidized the student newspaper due to budget cuts. 由于预算削减，大学停止了对学生报纸的资助。
synonyms: defund, withdraw funding, discontinue aid
antonyms: subsidize, fund, support
词性: 动词
