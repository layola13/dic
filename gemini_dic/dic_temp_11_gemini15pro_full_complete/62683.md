engendered:/ɪnˈdʒɛndərd/
verb
产生，引起
caused or gave rise to (a feeling, situation, or condition).
The issue engendered controversy.  这个问题引起了争议。
The movie engendered a feeling of sadness. 这部电影引发了一种悲伤的感觉。
synonyms: cause, create, produce, generate, arouse, provoke, incite, inspire, precipitate, bring about, give rise to, lead to, result in, occasion, effect
antonyms: prevent, suppress, stop, halt, quell
v.
