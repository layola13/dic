interdeceive:/ˌɪntərdiˈsiːv/
verb
互相欺骗
To deceive one another; to engage in mutual deception.
The two spies interdeceived each other, each believing they had the upper hand. 两个间谍互相欺骗，每个人都认为自己占了上风。
They interdeceived the public with their elaborate scheme. 他们用精心策划的计划欺骗了公众。
近义词: mislead, delude, trick, double-cross
反义词: be honest, tell the truth
词性: verb
