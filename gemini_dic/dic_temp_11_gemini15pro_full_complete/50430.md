depraving:/dɪˈpreɪvɪŋ/
adjective
堕落的，腐化的
making someone morally bad
例句：The depraving influence of violence in the media.  媒体中暴力腐蚀人心的影响。

例句：He denounced the depraving effects of pornography. 他谴责了色情作品败坏道德的影响。

近义词：corrupting, debasing, degrading, perverting, vitiating
反义词：ennobling, elevating, improving
词性：adjective
