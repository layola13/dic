auxiliary:/ɔːɡˈzɪliəri/ | adj. | 辅助的; 补充的; 附加的 | Providing supplementary or additional help and support. |  The hospital has an auxiliary power supply. 这家医院有备用电源。 | synonyms: supplementary, additional, supporting | antonyms: primary, main, principal | adjective
