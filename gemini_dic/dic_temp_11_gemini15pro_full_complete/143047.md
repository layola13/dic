outblundering: /ˌaʊtˈblʌndərɪŋ/
verb
犯更大的错误；在失误方面胜过
making bigger or more mistakes than someone else; surpassing in blunders
He outblundered his opponent in the chess match, making three critical errors that cost him the game. 他在国际象棋比赛中比对手犯了更大的错误，三个关键的失误让他输掉了比赛。
The government outblundered the previous administration, creating an even bigger economic crisis.  这届政府比上一届政府犯了更大的错误，制造了更大的经济危机。
近义词: outdoing, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: perfecting, improving
词性: verb (present participle)
