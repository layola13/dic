hypertragical:/ˌhaɪpərˈtrædʒɪkəl/
adjective
极其悲惨的，过度悲剧的
Exaggeratedly or excessively tragical.  Marked by an over-the-top or melodramatic sense of tragedy.
例句：His hypertragical pronouncements about the spilled milk were met with rolled eyes. 他对洒出的牛奶的过度悲惨的声明引来了众人的白眼。

例句：The play was so hypertragical it bordered on parody.  这出戏过于悲惨，近乎滑稽剧。
近义词：melodramatic, overdramatic, histrionic
反义词：comic, humorous, lighthearted
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From hyper- "over, excessive" + tragical.  "Hyper-" comes from the Greek "hyper" (ὑπέρ) meaning "over, above, beyond." "Tragical" comes from the Greek "tragikos" (τραγικός) relating to tragedy.
