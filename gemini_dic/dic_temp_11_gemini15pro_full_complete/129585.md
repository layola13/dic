non-collegiate:/ˌnɒn kəˈliːdʒɪət/ | /ˌnɑːn kəˈliːdʒiət/| adj.|非学院的，非大学的|Not pertaining to or connected with a college or university.|He took some non-collegiate courses to improve his skills. 他参加了一些非大学课程来提高自己的技能。|Many successful entrepreneurs are non-collegiate educated. 许多成功的企业家都没有接受过大学教育。|近义词：non-academic, extramural|反义词：collegiate, academic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
collegiate (adjective): from Late Latin collegiatus "belonging to a collegium," from Latin collegium "partnership, association, guild, society," from collega "partner in office," from com- "with, together" + legare "to depute, send as an ambassador, bequeath"
