half-admitted:/ˌhæf ədˈmɪtɪd/ | adj. | 半承认的，不完全承认的 | Partially acknowledged; confessed to a limited extent; implied but not openly stated. |  He gave a half-admitted glance at her, acknowledging her presence but not fully engaging. 他半承认地瞥了她一眼，承认她的存在，但没有完全参与。

She offered a half-admitted apology, suggesting regret but not taking full responsibility. 她表达了半承认的歉意，暗示了后悔，但没有承担全部责任。 | Near Synonyms: partially acknowledged, tacitly confessed, implicitly admitted, hinted at | Antonyms: fully admitted, openly confessed, explicitly stated, denied | adjective
