ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin adulatio (“fawning, flattery”), from the verb adulor (“to fawn upon, flatter”), from ad- (“to”) + ulos (“tail-wagger”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wel- (“to turn, roll”).
