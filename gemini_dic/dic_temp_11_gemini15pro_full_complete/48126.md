decode:/diːˈkoʊd/ (US), /diːˈkəʊd/ (UK)| v. n.|解码，译码，破译|To convert (a coded message) into intelligible language. To interpret or give meaning to.|例句：The spy decoded the enemy's message.  间谍破译了敌人的信息。

例句：It took days to decode the complex cipher.  破译复杂的密码花了几天时间。


近义词：decipher, decrypt, interpret, translate|反义词: encode, encrypt|词性:动词


decode:/ˈdiːkoʊd/ (US), /ˈdiːkəʊd/ (UK)| n.|解码，译码|The act or process of decoding.|例句：The decode of the message revealed a secret plan.  信息的解码揭示了一个秘密计划。

例句：The team worked tirelessly on the decode.  团队不知疲倦地进行解码工作。


近义词：deciphering, decryption, interpretation, translation|反义词: encoding, encryption|词性:名词
