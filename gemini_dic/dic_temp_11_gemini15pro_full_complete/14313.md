automatic:/ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk/ | adj. n. |自动的；无意识的；自动机械；自动手枪| operating by itself; done without thinking | The car has automatic transmission. 这辆车是自动变速的。| automatic doors 自动门| The response was automatic. 反应是无意识的。| synonyms: automated, self-acting, involuntary | antonyms: manual, controlled, voluntary | adjective, noun

automatic:/ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk/ | n. | 自动机械；自动手枪 | a machine that operates automatically, especially a gun | He pulled out an automatic. 他掏出了一把自动手枪。| automatic rifle 自动步枪 |  She used the automatic setting on the camera. 她使用了相机的自动设置。| synonyms: machine, pistol | antonyms: manual, revolver | noun
