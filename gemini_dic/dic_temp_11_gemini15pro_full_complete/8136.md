anti-busing:/ˌæntiˈbʌsɪŋ/
adjective
反校车接送的
Opposed to the practice of busing students to achieve racial integration in schools.
The anti-busing protests became violent. 反校车接送的抗议活动变得暴力起来。
synonyms: segregationist, discriminatory
antonyms: integrationist, pro-busing
词性: adjective
