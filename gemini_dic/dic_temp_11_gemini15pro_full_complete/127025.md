nasion:/ˈneɪziən/| n. |鼻根点|The midpoint of the frontonasal suture, where the nasal bones and the frontal bone meet.  It's an important landmark in craniofacial anthropometry and surgery. |The nasion is used as a reference point in facial measurements.  鼻根点在面部测量中被用作参考点。

The surgeon carefully marked the patient's nasion before the rhinoplasty procedure.  外科医生在鼻整形手术前仔细标记了患者的鼻根点。|近义词:frontonasal point|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology: From New Latin *nāsiō, from Latin nāsus (“nose”).
