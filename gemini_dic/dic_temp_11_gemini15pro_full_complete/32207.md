chicaned:/ʃɪˈkeɪnd/
Verb
欺骗；耍花招；阻挠
Deliberately delaying or obstructing someone or something.
The lawyer chicaned to have the case dismissed.  律师耍花招让案子被驳回。
Synonyms: obstructed, delayed, stalled, hampered, impeded
Antonyms: expedited, facilitated, aided, assisted
词性: 动词


chicanery:/ʃɪˈkeɪnəri/
Noun
欺骗；耍花招；诡计
The use of trickery to achieve a political, financial, or legal purpose.
The company resorted to chicanery to avoid paying taxes. 该公司 resorted to chicanery to avoid paying taxes。
Synonyms: trickery, deception, subterfuge, fraud, guile
Antonyms: honesty, integrity, forthrightness, openness
词性: 名词


词源:  From French *chicaner*, from *chicane* “dispute, quarrel,” originally “riding at rings” (a game requiring dexterity), perhaps of Arabic origin.
