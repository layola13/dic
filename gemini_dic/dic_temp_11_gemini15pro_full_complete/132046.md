|n.| incorruptibility, impeccability | 不可腐败性；廉洁性；正直 |The noncorruptiblenesses of the judges were crucial to the fairness of the trial. |法官的廉洁对审判的公正至关重要。| 近义词: incorruptibility, integrity, uprightness, probity | 反义词: corruptibility, dishonesty, venality | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From non- + corruptible + -ness.
"non-"  prefix meaning "not"
"corruptible" from Latin "corruptus" (past participle of "corrumpere" - to break up, destroy, spoil) + "-ible" suffix meaning "capable of being"
"-ness" suffix forming nouns from adjectives.
