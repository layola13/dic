hematoidin:/ˌhiːməˈtɔɪdɪn/
noun
血红素
A reddish-brown crystalline pigment, C16H18N2O4, derived from hemoglobin and found in old blood clots.
Hemoglobin is broken down into hematoidin in bruises.  血红蛋白在瘀伤中分解成血红素。
Hematoidin crystals were observed in the tissue sample. 在组织样本中观察到血红素晶体。
synonyms: bilirubin
antonyms: None (It's a chemical compound, not a concept with an opposite)
词性: noun
