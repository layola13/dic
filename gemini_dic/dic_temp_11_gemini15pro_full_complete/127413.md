nearsightedly:/ˌnɪrˈsaɪtɪdli/
adv.
近视地；目光短浅地
in a nearsighted manner; without looking ahead or considering the long-term consequences.
He nearsightedly focused on short-term profits, ignoring the long-term sustainability of the business. 他目光短浅地专注于短期利润，忽略了企业的长期可持续性。
Synonyms: shortsightedly, myopically
Antonyms: farsightedly, presciently
词性: 副词
