caricatured:/ˈkærɪkətʃərd/
adj.
被夸张化的；漫画式的
represented in a comically exaggerated way, often to satirize or ridicule.
His caricatured portrayal of the politician was hilarious. 他对这位政客的漫画式描绘滑稽可笑。
近义词: exaggerated, distorted, lampooned, satirized, mocked
反义词: realistic, accurate, lifelike
词性: adjective


caricatured:/ˈkærɪkətʃərd/
v.
(caricature的过去式和过去分词)
被夸张化；漫画化
past participle of "caricature" which means to represent someone in an exaggerated or distorted way, especially in order to make them look ridiculous.
The mayor felt he was unfairly caricatured in the newspaper cartoon. 市长觉得他在报纸漫画中被不公平地丑化了。
近义词: exaggerated, distorted, lampooned, satirized, mocked
反义词: portrayed accurately, depicted realistically
词性: verb (past participle)
