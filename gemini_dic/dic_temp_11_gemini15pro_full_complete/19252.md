bhavan:/ˈbʌvən/ | n. | 大厦，房屋，宫殿 | A large building, often a stately or impressive one.  Derived from Sanskrit "bhavana" meaning "dwelling, house, existence". |  The Prime Minister's bhavan is a magnificent structure.  总理的官邸是一座宏伟的建筑。| Synonyms: building, edifice, mansion, palace | Antonyms: hut, shack | noun
