greenly:/ˈɡriːnli/| adv.| 绿色地，青葱地，稚嫩地|In a green manner; covered with green vegetation; in an inexperienced or naive way.|The hills rolled greenly into the distance.  群山青翠地绵延至远方。|近义词：verdantly, lushly|反义词：barrenly, aridly|词性:副词
Etymology: From green + -ly (adverbial suffix). The word "green" comes from Old English "grēne," of Germanic origin, related to "grow."
