exhibited:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtɪd/
verb
展示，展出
past tense and past participle of exhibit, meaning to show or display something publicly.
The museum exhibited a collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆展出了一批古代文物。
Her paintings were exhibited at the local gallery. 她的画作在当地画廊展出。
近义词:displayed, shown, presented, demonstrated
反义词:concealed, hidden, withheld
词性:v.


exhibited:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtɪd/
adjective
展出的
Showing qualities or characteristics that are not typical.
He exhibited signs of stress and fatigue. 他表现出压力和疲劳的迹象。
The patient exhibited unusual symptoms. 病人表现出不寻常的症状。
近义词:displayed, shown, manifested
反义词:concealed, hidden, suppressed
词性:adj. (rare usage as an adjective)
