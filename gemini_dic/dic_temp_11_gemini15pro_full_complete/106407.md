kopang:/ˈkoʊpɑŋ/| n.|扣板; (马来西亚)一种小手鼓|A type of small hand drum used in Malaysia, similar to a tambourine, often used in traditional music and dances. It produces a sharp, percussive sound when struck.|She played the kopang skillfully during the wedding ceremony.  她在婚礼上熟练地演奏了扣板。|近义词：rebana (another type of Malay drum), tambourine|反义词: N/A|名词
