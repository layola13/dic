metamorphosis:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔrfəsɪs/| n.|变形，变态，蜕变|A complete change of form or substance; a transformation. |The caterpillar underwent metamorphosis into a butterfly. 毛毛虫蜕变成了蝴蝶。

|Synonyms: transformation, change, alteration, conversion, mutation, transmutation|Antonyms: stagnation, fixity, immutability|noun

metamorphosis:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔrfəsɪs/| n.|(比喻)彻底的改变|A dramatic change in form, character, or circumstances. |He underwent a metamorphosis from a shy boy to a confident young man. 他从一个害羞的男孩变成了一个自信的年轻人。

|Synonyms: revolution, rebirth, reformation|Antonyms: stagnation, fixity, immutability|noun
