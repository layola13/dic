dastardly: /ˈdɑːstərdli/ (US) /ˈdɑːstədli/ (UK)| adj. | 卑鄙的；懦弱的；恶毒的 | Wicked and cowardly. Showing or characterized by treachery or cruelty. | He committed a dastardly act of betrayal. 他犯下了卑鄙的背叛行为。 | The dastardly villain plotted to overthrow the king.  这个卑鄙的恶棍密谋推翻国王。 | 近义词: cowardly, treacherous, villainous, despicable, base, mean, evil, wicked | 反义词: courageous, brave, honorable, noble, heroic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From *dastard* + *-ly*.  *Dastard* is of uncertain origin, possibly from Old Norse *dæstr*, meaning "exhausted, worn out," suggesting a cowardly person is one who lacks the spirit to fight.
