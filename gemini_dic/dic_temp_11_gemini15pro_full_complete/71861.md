fleshiness:/ˈflɛʃɪnɪs/
n.
丰满，肥胖
The state of being plump or fleshy.
Her fleshiness was evident in her rounded cheeks and ample figure. 她的丰满体现在她圆润的脸颊和丰腴的身材上。

He worried about his increasing fleshiness. 他担心自己日益发胖。

近义词: plumpness, stoutness, chubbiness, rotundity, obesity
反义词: thinness, leanness, slenderness
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From flesh + -iness.  Flesh (meaning "meat, animal tissue") comes from Old English *flæsc, from Proto-Germanic *flaiska-.  The suffix -iness forms nouns indicating a state or quality.
