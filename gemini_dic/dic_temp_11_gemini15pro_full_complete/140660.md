ombudsman:/ˈɒmbʊdzmən/ (UK) /ˈɑːmbʊdzmən/ (US)| n. | 申诉专员；巡视员；督察员 | An official appointed to investigate individuals' complaints against maladministration, especially that of public authorities. | The ombudsman received numerous complaints about the new policy. 申诉专员收到了许多关于新政策的投诉。| synonyms: mediator, arbitrator, conciliator, adjudicator | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "perpetrator" in some contexts) | noun

ombudsmen:/ˈɒmbʊdzmən/ (UK) /ˈɑːmbʊdzmən/ (US)| n. |申诉专员；巡视员；督察员（ombudsman的复数形式）| The plural form of ombudsman.  | The ombudsmen worked tirelessly to resolve the disputes. 巡视员们不知疲倦地工作以解决争端。 | synonyms: mediators, arbitrators, conciliators, adjudicators | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Swedish *ombudsman*, from *ombud* "commission, agency" + *man* "man."  Borrowed into English in the mid-20th century.
