out-talk: /ˌaʊtˈtɔːk/ | v. | 以更高的音量或更长时间地说服，比...说得更多| to defeat or silence someone by talking longer or louder than them | He out-talked his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中说得比对手多，从而赢得了胜利。| synonyms: outargue, outspeak | antonyms: be quiet, listen | verb

out-talk: /ˌaʊtˈtɔːk/ | v. | 比...说得更多，说得更久 | to speak more than someone else, usually for a longer period of time | She tends to out-talk everyone in meetings.  她在会议上总是说得比其他人都多。| synonyms: dominate the conversation, monopolize | antonyms: listen attentively, be concise | verb
