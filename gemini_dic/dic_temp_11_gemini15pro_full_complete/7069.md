anemorphilous:/ˌænɪˈmɒfɪləs/ | adj. | 风媒的 | Pollinated by wind.  Relating to plants whose pollen is dispersed by wind. |  The oak tree is an anemophilous species. 橡树是一种风媒物种。| Many grasses are anemophilous and rely on the wind for pollination. 许多草本植物是风媒的，依靠风来授粉。|  wind-pollinated | self-pollinated, insect-pollinated | adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ánemos* (wind) + *phílos* (loving).
