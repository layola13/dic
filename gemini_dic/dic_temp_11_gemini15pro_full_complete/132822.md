noneconomists:/ˌnɒnɪˈkɒnəmɪsts/ /ˌnɑːnɪˈkɑːnəmɪsts/| n. |非经济学家|People who are not specialists in economics.  Those who have not studied or are not professionally engaged in the field of economics.|Many noneconomists find the subject of macroeconomics daunting. 许多非经济学家觉得宏观经济学令人生畏。|Synonyms:laypeople, nonspecialists|Antonyms:economists, specialists|Noun
