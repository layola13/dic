noun
锗（germanium的复数）
plural of germanium, a chemical element with symbol Ge and atomic number 32. It is a lustrous, hard-brittle, grayish-white metalloid in the carbon group.
Germaniums are used in transistors and other electronic devices.  锗被用于晶体管和其他电子设备中。
Near Synonyms: metalloids, semiconductors
Antonyms: metals, nonmetals
