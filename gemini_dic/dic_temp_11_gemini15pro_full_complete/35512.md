coadunition:/ˌkoʊˌædʒʊˈnɪʃən/| n.|共同警告，联合劝诫|The coadunition from both parents finally convinced the teenager to change his behavior.|父母双方的共同劝诫最终说服了这个青少年改变他的行为。|近义词：joint admonition, combined warning|反义词:encouragement, approval|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *coadunitio*, from coadunire "to unite," from com- "together" + adunare "to unite," from ad- "to" + unus "one."
