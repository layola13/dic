geysering:/ˈɡaɪzərɪŋ/ | n. | 间歇泉喷发；类似间歇泉的喷发 | An eruption resembling that of a geyser; the action of erupting like a geyser. | The geysering of the hot spring attracted many tourists. 温泉的间歇喷发吸引了许多游客。 | synonyms: eruption, outburst, gushing, spurting | antonyms: stagnation, stillness | noun

geysering:/ˈɡaɪzərɪŋ/ | adj. | 像间歇泉一样喷发的 | Erupting like a geyser. | The geysering mud pot was a fascinating sight.  像间歇泉一样喷发的泥浆池是一个迷人的景象。 | synonyms: eruptive, gushing, spurting | antonyms: stagnant, still | adjective
