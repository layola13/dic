corroboratorily:/kəˈrɒbəreɪtərɪli/ | adv. | 证实地，确证地 |  In a way that confirms or supports something; in a corroborative manner. | The witness corroborated the victim's story corroboratorily, providing key details that strengthened the case.  证人确证地证实了受害者的说法，提供了加强案件的关键细节。

|近义词: confirmingly, supportively, validly|反义词: contradictorily, disprovingly|词性: adverb
