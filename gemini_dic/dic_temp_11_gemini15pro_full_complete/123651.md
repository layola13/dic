monumentality:/ˌmɒnjʊmɛnˈtælɪti/ /ˌmɑːnjʊmɛnˈtælɪti/
n.|不朽性；巨大；雄伟|The quality of being monumental, especially in terms of size, grandeur, or lasting significance.  指事物具有纪念意义的特性，尤指其规模、宏伟或持久的重要性。|The monumentality of the pyramids is awe-inspiring. 金字塔的雄伟令人敬畏。|近义词：immensity, grandeur, vastness, magnitude|反义词：insignificance, smallness, triviality|词性:名词

monumental:/ˌmɒnjʊˈmɛntəl/ /ˌmɑːnjʊˈmɛntəl/
adj.|不朽的；巨大的；雄伟的|Resembling a monument, especially in size or importance.  像纪念碑一样，尤其指规模或重要性。|Building the new library was a monumental task. 建造新图书馆是一项艰巨的任务。|近义词：immense, grand, vast, massive|反义词：insignificant, small, trivial|词性:形容词
