claustrophobic:/ˌklɔːstrəˈfəʊbɪk/| adj.|幽闭恐惧症的，患幽闭恐惧症的|Having an extreme or irrational fear of confined places.|She felt claustrophobic in the crowded elevator. 她觉得在拥挤的电梯里很幽闭恐惧。|近义词：confined, stuffy, cramped|反义词:spacious, open, airy|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin claustrum "bolt, bar, enclosed place" + -phobia.
