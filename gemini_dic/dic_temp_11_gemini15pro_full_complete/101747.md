irresolution:/ɪˌrezəˈluːʃn/
n.
优柔寡断；犹豫不决
The state of being unable to decide; lack of resolve.
His irresolution was agonizing to watch as he paced back and forth.  看着他来回踱步，优柔寡断的样子真让人着急。
indecision, hesitancy, vacillation, wavering
resolution, decisiveness, determination
noun
