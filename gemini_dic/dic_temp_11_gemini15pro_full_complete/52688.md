dignifies:/ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪz/
verb
使显得尊贵；使有尊严
to make something or someone seem worthy and impressive
The ceremony was dignified by the presence of the Queen. 女王的出席使仪式更显庄重。
近义词:ennoble, exalt, elevate
反义词:degrade, demean, humiliate
词性:v.

dignifies:/ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪz/
verb
抬举；看得起
to consider something worthy of attention or respect
I wouldn't dignify his comments with a reply. 我不屑于回复他的评论。
近义词:acknowledge, recognize
反义词:ignore, disregard
词性:v.
