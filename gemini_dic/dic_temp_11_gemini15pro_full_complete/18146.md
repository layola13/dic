beiges:/ˈbeɪʒɪz/
noun
米黄色；浅褐色(beige的复数)
Plural form of beige, referring to multiple instances or shades of the pale yellowish-brown color.
The living room was decorated in various shades of beiges and creams.  客厅用各种米黄色和奶油色装饰。
Near Synonyms: tans, fawns, ecrus
Antonyms: blacks, browns (darker shades)
