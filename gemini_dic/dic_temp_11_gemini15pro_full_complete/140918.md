oncology:/ɒŋˈkɒlədʒi/ /ɑːŋˈkɑːlədʒi/
n.
肿瘤学
The study of tumors, including their development, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention.
例句：He specializes in oncology and treats patients with cancer. 他专攻肿瘤学，治疗癌症患者。

例句：Advances in oncology have led to more effective cancer treatments. 肿瘤学的进步带来了更有效的癌症治疗方法。

近义词：cancerology
反义词: 无
词性: noun
