one's: /wʌnz/ | possessive pronoun | 某人的；自己的 |  Used to indicate that something belongs to or is associated with a particular person, previously mentioned or easily identified. |  She brought one's own lunch. 她带了自己的午餐。|  my, his, her, its, our, their, your |  none (not a direct antonym, represents lack of possession) | pronoun
