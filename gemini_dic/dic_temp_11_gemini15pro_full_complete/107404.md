lamination:/ˌlæmɪˈneɪʃn/
n.|叠层；层压；层压板；薄片|the process of bonding two or more layers of material together, especially with the aid of heat and pressure, or the material produced by this process.|The desk has a plastic lamination. 这张桌子有一层塑料覆膜.|近义词：overlay, coating, layering|反义词:separation, delamination|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *laminatio, from laminare (“to make into thin plates”), from lamina (“thin plate, leaf, layer”).
