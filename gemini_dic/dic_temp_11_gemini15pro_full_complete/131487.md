noncollaborations:/ˌnɒnkəˌlæbəˈreɪʃənz/ (British), /ˌnɑːnkəˌlæbəˈreɪʃənz/ (American)
noun
不合作，缺乏合作
Instances where individuals or groups do not work together or cooperate.  The absence of collaboration.
The project's failure was due to noncollaborations between departments.  该项目的失败是由于部门之间缺乏合作。
Several noncollaborations within the team hindered their progress. 团队内部的几次不合作阻碍了他们的进展。
noncooperation, lack of cooperation
collaboration, cooperation
n.
