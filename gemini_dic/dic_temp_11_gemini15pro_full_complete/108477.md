lay-open:/ˌleɪ ˈoʊpən/ | adj.|敞开的，公开的|Exposed; revealed; not concealed.  Relating to something that is easily accessible or understandable.|The company's finances were lay-open to the public after the scandal.  这家公司的财务状况在丑闻曝光后被公之于众。| The lay-open design of the engine allowed for easy maintenance.  这种发动机的敞开式设计方便维护。 | synonyms: exposed, revealed, uncovered, public, open, transparent | antonyms: concealed, hidden, secret, private, closed | adjective


lay open:/ˌleɪ ˈoʊpən/ | v.|揭露，暴露；摊开|To expose or reveal something, often something unpleasant or secretive; to spread something out flat.|The investigation laid open the corruption within the organization. 调查揭露了该组织内部的腐败。|He laid open the map on the table. 他把地图摊开在桌子上。| synonyms: expose, reveal, uncover, divulge, unveil, unfurl, spread out | antonyms: conceal, hide, cover up, protect | verb
