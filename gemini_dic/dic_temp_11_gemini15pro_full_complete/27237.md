calmucks: /kɑːlˈmʌks/ | n. | 卡尔梅克人 |  A member of a group of Western Mongols, traditionally nomadic pastoralists, now living mainly in the Republic of Kalmykia, a federal subject of Russia.  |  The Calmucks migrated westwards in the 17th century. 卡尔梅克人在17世纪向西迁徙。 | synonyms: Kalmyks | antonyms: N/A | noun

calmuck: /kɑːlˈmʌk/ | adj. | 卡尔梅克的；卡尔梅克人的 | Relating to the Calmucks or their language. | The calmuck language is closely related to Mongolian.  卡尔梅克语与蒙古语密切相关。 | synonyms: Kalmyk | antonyms: N/A | adjective
