gigglier:/ˈɡɪɡliər/| adj.|更加咯咯笑的，更加傻笑的|Comparative form of "giggly," meaning laughing lightly in a silly or nervous way, often repeatedly.|She was even gigglier than usual after drinking the bubbly. 她喝了起泡酒后比平时笑得更厉害了。|The children became gigglier as the party went on.  随着派对的进行，孩子们变得更加爱笑了。|近义词：more amused, more lighthearted|反义词：more serious, more somber|词性:形容词

giggle:/ˈɡɪɡ(ə)l/| n. vi.|咯咯笑，傻笑|A light, silly laugh, often repeated.|She let out a giggle. 她咯咯地笑了出来。|He giggled nervously. 他紧张地傻笑。|近义词：chuckle, titter|反义词：sob, cry|词性:名词，动词

词源:  From the Middle English *giglen*, of imitative origin.
