nonmetallurgically:/ˌnɒnˌmɛtəlˈɜːrdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 非冶金地 |  In a way that does not involve metallurgy (the science and technology of metals). | The material was processed nonmetallurgically.  这种材料是用非冶金方法处理的。| The components were assembled nonmetallurgically using advanced adhesives. 这些组件是用先进的粘合剂非冶金组装的。 | Synonym: non-metallurgically | Antonym: metallurgically | adverb
