disgallant:/dɪsˈɡælənt/
adjective
无礼的；失礼的；不殷勤的
Lacking in courtesy and consideration, especially to women; ungentlemanly.
He made a disgallant remark about her dress. 他对她的着装发表了无礼的评论。
近义词: rude, impolite, ungentlemanly, discourteous, inconsiderate, churlish
反义词: gallant, courteous, polite, chivalrous
