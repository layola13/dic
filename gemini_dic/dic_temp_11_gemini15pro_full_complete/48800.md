deflorate:/diːˈflɔːreɪt/ /diːˈflɔːrət/
verb
剥夺…的童贞；摘掉…的花朵
to deprive of virginity; to take away the flowers of
The young girl was tragically deflorated.  这个年轻的女孩不幸被夺走了贞洁。
The wind deflorated the cherry blossoms. 风吹落了樱花。
近义词: ravish, defile
反义词: None (The concept doesn't have a direct antonym)
词性: verb

defloration:/diːfləˈreɪʃn/
noun
剥夺童贞；摘花
the act of deflorating; removal of flowers
The defloration of the bride was a significant ritual in some ancient cultures.  在新娘的贞操被夺走，在一些古代文化中是一个重要的仪式。
The defloration of the rose bushes by the storm was a sad sight.  暴风雨摧残了玫瑰丛，这真是令人悲伤的景象。
近义词: rape, violation
反义词: None
词性: noun
