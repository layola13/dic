fardage:/ˈfɑːrdɪdʒ/| n. |填塞物，压舱物，（尤指用作压舱物的）稻草、旧麻布等|Material, especially straw or old canvas, used as packing or ballast.|The ship used fardage to stabilize its cargo during the stormy voyage. 这艘船在暴风雨航行中使用填塞物来稳定货物。|近义词：ballast, packing, dunnage|反义词: cargo|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle French *fardage, from farder ("to paint, to pack, to load"), from Old French farder ("to paint the face, to disguise"), from Frankish *fardōn ("to paint, to adorn"), from Proto-Germanic *fardijaną ("to paint, to adorn"), from Proto-Indo-European *preh₂-tóm ("painted, adorned").
