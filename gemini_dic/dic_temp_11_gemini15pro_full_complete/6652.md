anaglyphic:/ˌænəˈɡlɪfɪk/ | adj. | 浮雕的；透视浮雕的 | Relating to or denoting a process that creates a three-dimensional image by superimposing two slightly different images, typically one red and one cyan, which are then viewed through correspondingly colored filters. | Example: I watched a movie in 3D using anaglyphic glasses. 我用浮雕眼镜看了一部3D电影。
 | Example:  Anaglyphic images are created using two slightly offset images. 浮雕图像的创建是使用两个略微偏移的图像。 | Near Synonyms: stereoscopic, 3D | Antonyms: 2D, flat | Word type: adjective
