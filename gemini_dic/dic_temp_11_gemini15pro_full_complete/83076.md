gurry:/ˈɡʌri/| n.| 内脏，鱼的内脏，残渣，杂物 |Fish gurry is used to make fertilizer. 鱼内脏用来做肥料。| The gurry from the factory polluted the river. 工厂的废渣污染了河流。|例句：The fisherman threw the gurry back into the sea. 渔夫把鱼内脏扔回了海里。|近义词：offal, waste, refuse, remains|反义词:cleanliness, purity|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *gurry, from Old French *gurre (“filth, mire”), of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old North French gurroi (“pigsty”).
