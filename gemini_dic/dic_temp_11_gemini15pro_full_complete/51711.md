deviser:/dɪˈvaɪzər/| n.|设计者,发明者,策划者|A person who devises or invents something, especially something new or ingenious.|例句：He was the deviser of a new system of communication. 他是一种新型通讯系统的设计者。

例句：The deviser of this plan deserves much credit.  这个计划的策划者值得称赞。|近义词：inventor, creator, designer, architect, planner, originator, author|反义词：imitator, copier|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French *deviser "to distribute, arrange, direct, order, contrive, invent," from Late Latin divisare "to divide, distribute," frequentative of Latin dividere "to divide." The meaning shifted from "arrange, contrive" to "invent."
