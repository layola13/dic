fuel-injected:/ˈfjuːəl ɪnˈdʒɛktɪd/
adjective
燃油喷射的
equipped with a fuel injection system, which sprays fuel into the engine cylinders.
例句：
My new car has a fuel-injected engine. 我的新车有一个燃油喷射引擎。

He bought a fuel-injected motorcycle for better performance. 他买了一辆燃油喷射的摩托车以获得更好的性能。

近义词：none (The term is specific to the technology)
反义词：carbureted (although not a direct opposite, it represents an older technology)
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
fuel (n.) from Old French feuaile "fuel, firewood,"  from Vulgar Latin *focalia, neuter plural of *focalis "of a hearth," from Latin focus "hearth, fireplace"
inject (v.) from Latin injectus, past participle of injicere "to throw or cast in, put or bring into," from in- "into" + jacere "to throw"
