barrio:/ˈbɑːrioʊ/ (US), /ˈbærioʊ/ (UK)| n. |街区，社区，街坊；(尤指西班牙语国家的)居民区|A barrio is a district of a town or city, especially one inhabited by a Spanish-speaking population.

例句：
The vibrant murals brightened up the barrio.
充满活力的壁画使这个街区焕然一新。

The barrio is known for its lively music scene.
这个街区以其热闹的音乐景象而闻名。


近义词：neighborhood, district, community, quarter
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could be considered the opposite of affluent areas, perhaps "uptown" or "wealthy suburbs" in specific contexts)
词性:noun
