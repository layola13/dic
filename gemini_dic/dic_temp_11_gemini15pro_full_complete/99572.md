interindicated:/ˌɪntərˈɪndɪkeɪtɪd/
adj.
相互禁忌的，相互不宜的
Indicating that two or more drugs or procedures should not be used together because of the potential for harmful interactions.  The use of one treatment makes the use of another inadvisable.
Example:  Taking this medication is interindicated with alcohol consumption.  服用这种药物与饮酒相互禁忌。
Example:  Surgery and certain blood thinners are interindicated. 手术和某些血液稀释剂是相互禁忌的。
Near Synonyms: contraindicated, incompatible, inadvisable
Antonyms:  indicated, compatible, advisable
word property:adjective
