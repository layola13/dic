distanced:/ˈdɪstənst/ /ˈdɪstənst/| adj.|有距离的；疏远的；冷漠的|He felt distanced from his family after moving abroad. 移居国外后，他感到与家人疏远了。|近义词：remote, aloof|反义词：close, intimate|adjective
