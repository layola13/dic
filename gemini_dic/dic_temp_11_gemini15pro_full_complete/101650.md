irregeneracy:/ˌɪrɪdʒɛnˈərəsi/ | n. | 不可再生性；无法挽回 | The irregeneracy of some natural resources makes conservation efforts crucial.  某些自然资源的不可再生性使得保护工作至关重要。| The speaker lamented the irregeneracy of the damage done to the ecosystem. 演讲者对生态系统遭受的不可挽回的损害表示悲痛。 | 近义词: non-renewability, irreversibility | 反义词: renewability, reversibility | 词性: noun

irregenerate:/ˌɪrɪˈdʒɛnərɪt/ | adj. | 不可救药的；顽固不化的；堕落的 |  The irregenerate criminal showed no remorse for his actions.  那个不可救药的罪犯对自己的行为毫无悔意。 |  Despite numerous attempts at rehabilitation, he remained utterly irregenerate. 尽管进行了多次改造尝试，他仍然完全执迷不悟。| 近义词: incorrigible, unreformable, depraved | 反义词: reformed, repentant | 词性: adjective
