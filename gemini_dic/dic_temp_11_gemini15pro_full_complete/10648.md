appropriations: /əˌproʊpriˈeɪʃənz/
n.
拨款，专款；挪用
An act of setting aside money for a specific purpose.  The action of taking something for one's own use, typically without the owner's permission.
Congress approved appropriations for defense spending. 国会批准了国防开支的拨款。
The artist's appropriation of another's work sparked controversy.  这位艺术家挪用他人作品的行为引发了争议。
近义词: allocation, allotment, funding, grant, earmark, seizure
反义词:  donation, contribution, restitution
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English appropriacion, from Late Latin appropriātiō, from Latin appropriāre (“to make one's own”), from ad- (“to”) + proprius (“one's own”).
