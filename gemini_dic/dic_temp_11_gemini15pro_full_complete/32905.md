choo-chooed:/ˌtʃuːˈtʃuːd/
verb
发出火车行驶的声音；（火车）发出咔嚓咔嚓声
Made the sound of a train.
The toy train choo-chooed around the track. 玩具火车在轨道上发出咔嚓咔嚓的声音。
近义词: chugged, puffed
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps silenced)
词性: verb

choo-choo:/ˈtʃuːˈtʃuː/
noun
火车（儿语）
A child's word for a train.
The toddler pointed at the train and shouted, "Choo-choo!"  幼儿指着火车大喊：“啾啾！”
近义词: train, locomotive (more formal)
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


词源:  Onomatopoeic, imitating the sound of a steam train.
