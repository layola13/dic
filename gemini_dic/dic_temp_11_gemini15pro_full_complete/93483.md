illustratively:/ɪˈlʌstrətɪvli/| adv.|说明性地，举例说明地|in a way that explains or makes something clear by giving examples|The teacher illustratively explained the concept using diagrams. 老师用图表举例说明了这个概念。|例句：He illustratively described the process of making wine. 他举例说明了酿酒的过程。|例句：The author illustratively used anecdotes to support his argument. 作者用轶事来支持他的论点。|近义词：exemplifyingly, representatively|反义词:unclearly, vaguely|词性:副词
