mycia: /ˈmɪtsɪə/ | n. | washing, wash (Polish) |  The Polish word for "washing" or "wash". It refers to the act of cleaning something, usually with water and soap, or the result of this action. |  例句：Mama robi mycie. (Mom is doing the washing.) | 近义词：pranie (washing), czyszczenie (cleaning) | 反义词: brudzenie (dirtying), zanieczyszczanie (polluting) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Proto-Slavic *myti ("to wash"). Cognate with Czech mýt, Slovak myť, Russian мыть (mytʹ).
