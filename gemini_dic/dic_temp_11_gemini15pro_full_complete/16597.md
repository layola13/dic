baroque: /bəˈroʊk/ (US) /bəˈrɒk/ (UK)| adj. n.| 巴洛克式的，巴洛克风格；巴洛克时期| Relating to or denoting a style of European architecture, music, and art of the 17th and 18th centuries that followed Mannerism and is characterized by ornate detail.  As a noun, it refers to the Baroque period or the Baroque style.| The palace is built in the baroque style. 这座宫殿是以巴洛克风格建造的。|  近义词: ornate, elaborate, flamboyant| 反义词: simple, plain| 词性: adjective, noun| 词源: From French *baroque* "irregular pearl," from Portuguese *barroco*, Spanish *barrueco* "irregular pearl," perhaps from Vulgar Latin *verruca* "wart."
