escapees:/ɪˌskeɪˈpiːz/
n.
逃脱者，逃跑者
People who have escaped from somewhere, especially from prison or captivity.
Three escapees from the prison were caught within 24 hours. 三名越狱逃犯在24小时内被抓获。
fugitives, runaways
captors, prisoners
