overrideable:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪdəbl/| adj.|可覆盖的，可重写的|Able to be overridden or superseded.  This often refers to methods in programming that can be redefined in a subclass.|The method `calculateTotal()` is overrideable in derived classes.|Synonyms: redefinable, supersedable, overridable|Antonyms: final, non-overrideable|Word type: Adjective

overrideable:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪdəbl/| adj.|可推翻的，可撤销的|Able to be overturned or reversed (a decision, a law, etc.). Though less common than the programming context, this meaning is also valid.|The judge's ruling was overrideable by a higher court.|Synonyms: reversible, revocable, repealable|Antonyms: irreversible, irrevocable, final|Word type: Adjective
