cancelling:/ˈkænsəlɪŋ/
verb|取消，撤销|The act of making something void or of no effect.
The airline is cancelling all flights due to the storm. 航空公司由于暴风雨取消了所有航班。
synonyms: revoking, rescinding, voiding, abandoning, countermanding
antonyms: confirming, approving, ratifying
v.
