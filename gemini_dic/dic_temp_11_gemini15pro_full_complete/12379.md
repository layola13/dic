ashrams:/ˈɑːʃrɑːmz/ (US), /ˈæʃrɑːmz/ (UK)
n.
修行处，静修院
A place of religious retreat or community living, typically associated with Hinduism or other Indian religions.
He spent several months meditating in an ashram in India. 他在印度的一处静修院冥想了几个月。

近义词: monastery, convent, retreat, sanctuary
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "city" or "secular world")
词性: noun (plural)
