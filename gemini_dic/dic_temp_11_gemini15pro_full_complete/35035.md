close-knit:/ˌkloʊsˈnɪt/| adj.|紧密的，关系密切的|United by strong relationships and common interests.  Characterized by close personal bonds and support within a group.|例句：They are a close-knit family who support each other through thick and thin. 他们是关系非常紧密的家庭，无论顺境逆境都互相扶持。

例句：The close-knit community rallied together to help the victims of the fire. 这个紧密团结的社区齐心协力帮助火灾的受害者。|近义词：close, intimate, tight-knit, bonded|反义词:distant, estranged, alienated|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "close" (meaning near or intimate) + "knit" (past participle of "knit," originally referring to the physical act of knitting fabric, implying a tight connection).  The metaphorical use of "knit" to describe relationships developed in the late 16th century.
