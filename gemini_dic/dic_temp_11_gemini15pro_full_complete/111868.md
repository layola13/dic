logjammed:/ˈlɒɡdʒæmd/ (US) /ˈlɒɡdʒæmd/ (UK)| adj.| 阻塞的，停滞不前的|A situation that has become blocked or stalled, like logs jamming a river.  It often refers to a situation where progress is difficult or impossible due to a large number of obstacles.|Traffic was completely logjammed due to the accident.  由于事故，交通完全堵塞了。|Synonyms: blocked, stalled, gridlocked, congested, bottlenecked|Antonyms: flowing, clear, unobstructed|adjective

logjam:/ˈlɒɡdʒæm/ (US) /ˈlɒɡdʒæm/ (UK)| n.| 阻塞，僵局|A blockage or standstill, especially in business or politics.|The negotiations reached a logjam over the issue of pay.  谈判在薪酬问题上陷入了僵局。|Synonyms: deadlock, standstill, impasse, bottleneck, gridlock|Antonyms: breakthrough, progress, solution|noun
