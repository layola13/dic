asynartete: /əˌsɪnˈɑːrtɪt/ | adj. | 非对称的，不对称关节的 |  Having unequal axes or parts; not symmetrical. Specifically (Botany), applied to diatoms and desmids where the two halves are dissimilar in form.  | Example 1: The asynartete diatom displayed a unique pattern under the microscope.  显微镜下，不对称的硅藻呈现出独特的图案。

Example 2:  The biologist identified the species as asynartete due to its asymmetrical valves. 生物学家根据其不对称的瓣膜将该物种鉴定为非对称的。 | Near Synonyms: asymmetrical,  anisometric, irregular | Antonyms: symmetrical, regular | Adjective
