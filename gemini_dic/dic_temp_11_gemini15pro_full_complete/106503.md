kraut:/kraʊt/
noun
德国泡菜，（尤指）酸菜；德国人（贬义）
fermented cabbage, especially sauerkraut; (derogatory) a German
He loves sauerkraut with sausages. 他喜欢香肠配酸菜。
synonyms: sauerkraut, cabbage
antonyms: None (no direct antonym for a food or nationality)
