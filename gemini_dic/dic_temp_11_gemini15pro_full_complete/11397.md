argentamin:/ɑːrdʒɛnˈtæmɪn/ | n. | 银蛋白胺 | A silver-binding protein found in reticulum fibers. It is used in histology to stain these fibers. |  Reticulin fibers stained black with argentamin are a key diagnostic feature. 网状纤维被银蛋白胺染成黑色是一个关键的诊断特征。 | Synonyms: silver protein, argyrophilic fiber stain | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
