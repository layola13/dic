multilaminate:/ˌmʌltiˈlæmɪneɪt/ | adj. n.|多层的，多层压板|Consisting of many layers or laminae; a material made of many layers bonded together. |The artist created a multilaminate sculpture using thin sheets of wood. 艺术家使用薄木片创作了一个多层雕塑。| The multilaminate material provided excellent insulation.  这种多层材料提供了极佳的隔热性能。|layered, stratified|monolithic, single-layer|adjective, noun
