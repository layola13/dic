multiplex-cinema:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleks ˈsɪnəmə/| n.|多厅影院，复合式电影院|A movie theater complex with multiple auditoriums, usually showing a variety of films at the same time.| We went to the multiplex-cinema to see the latest blockbuster. 我们去多厅影院看了最新的巨片。|synonyms: multiplex, megaplex, movie theater complex|antonyms: single-screen cinema, art house cinema|noun

multiplex:/ˈmʌltɪpleks/| n. adj.|多路的；多重的；复合的；多厅影院|Something with multiple parts or functions, often used as a shortened form of "multiplex cinema".| The multiplex showed five different movies simultaneously.  这家多厅影院同时放映五部不同的电影。|synonyms: multiple, compound, complex|antonyms: single, simple, singular|noun/adjective
