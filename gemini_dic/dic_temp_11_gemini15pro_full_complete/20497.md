bivalved:/baɪˈvælvd/
adjective
双壳的；有两瓣的
Having two valves or shells hinged together.
The bivalved mollusk closed its shell tightly.  这只双壳软体动物紧紧地合上了壳。
The pearl was found inside a bivalved oyster. 这颗珍珠是在一个双壳牡蛎里发现的。
近义词: two-shelled
反义词: univalved
词性: adjective


bivalve:/ˈbaɪvælv/
noun
双壳贝类
An aquatic mollusk that has a compressed body enclosed within a hinged shell, such as an oyster, clam, or mussel.
Bivalves are filter feeders, extracting plankton from the water. 双壳贝类是滤食动物，从水中提取浮游生物。
We found several different species of bivalve on the beach. 我们在海滩上发现了几个不同种类的双壳贝类。
近义词: clam, mussel, oyster
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider gastropods like snails)
词性: noun


Etymology: From bi- (two) + valve (shell, hinged part).  Ultimately from Latin valvae (folding doors).
