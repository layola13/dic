cultishnesses:/ˈkʌltɪʃnɪsɪz/
n.
狂热；邪教性质；膜拜
The quality of being cultish; excessive devotion to a person, group, or idea.
The band has attracted a cultishness among its fans.  这支乐队在其歌迷中引发了狂热的追捧。
近义词: fanaticism, zealotry, obsession
反义词: indifference, apathy
词性: 名词 (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
