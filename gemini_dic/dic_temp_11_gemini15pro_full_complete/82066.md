grittier:/ˈɡrɪtiər/ (US), /ˈɡrɪtɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更坚毅的；更勇敢的；更粗糙的；更现实的|Comparative adjective of gritty. Showing courage and resolve. Also, more realistic or raw.|The new detective series is grittier and more violent than the last one.  这部新的侦探剧比上一部更坚毅，也更暴力。|The documentary offered a grittier portrayal of life in the slums. 这部纪录片对贫民窟生活的描绘更加现实。|Synonyms: tougher, coarser, harsher, more realistic, raw, edgier|Antonyms: smoother, softer, gentler, more idealized, romanticized|adjective
