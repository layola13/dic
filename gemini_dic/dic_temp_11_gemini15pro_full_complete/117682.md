meistersinger:/ˈmaɪstərˌzɪŋər/| n.|歌唱大师；名歌手|A master singer, especially a member of one of the guilds established in German cities from the 14th to 16th centuries, which regulated the composition and performance of music.|Wagner's opera "Die Meistersinger von Nürnberg" tells the story of a meistersinger competition. 瓦格纳的歌剧《纽伦堡的名歌手》讲述了一个名歌手比赛的故事。|Synonyms: minstrel, bard, troubadour|Antonyms: amateur, novice|Noun
