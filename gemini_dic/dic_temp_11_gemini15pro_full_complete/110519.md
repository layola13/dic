limbiest:/ˈlɪmbiɪst/
adjective
最灵活的，四肢最长的
Having or involving the most or longest limbs.  Relatively informal; often used humorously or hyperbolically to describe someone agile or flexible.
例句：The limbiest gymnast on the team effortlessly performed the splits.  团队里肢体最柔韧的体操运动员轻松地完成了劈叉。

例句：The dancer, with her limbiest movements, captivated the audience. 那位舞者以她极为灵活的动作吸引了观众。

近义词：agile, flexible, supple, lithe
反义词：stiff, inflexible, rigid
词性：adjective

词源：limb + -iest.  "Limb" comes from Old English *lim.  "-iest" is a superlative suffix.
