diamondize:/ˈdaɪəməndaɪz/ |v. |使镶满钻石; 用钻石装饰; 使像钻石般坚硬|To cover or decorate with diamonds; to make as hard as diamond. |例句：The king diamondized his crown. 国王在他的王冠上镶满了钻石。

|例句：The process diamondizes the cutting edge of the tool. 这个过程使工具的切割刃像钻石一样坚硬。|近义词：bejewel, encrust, gem|反义词: N/A|词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From diamond + -ize.  Diamond (ultimately from Greek *adámas, "unbreakable") + -ize (a suffix forming verbs from nouns or adjectives, meaning "to make or become like," "to treat with," or "to cause to be").
