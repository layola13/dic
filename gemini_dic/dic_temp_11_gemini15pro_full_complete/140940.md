one-man:/ˈwʌn mæn/
adjective
单人操作的;一人包办的;只有一人的
Operated, performed, or done by one person alone.
例句：He runs a one-man business. 他经营一家一人公司。
例句：It was a one-man show.  这是一场独角戏。
近义词：solo, single-handed
反义词：multi-person, collaborative
词性: 形容词
