bloviation:/ˌbləʊvɪˈeɪʃən/ (US) /ˌbləʊviˈeɪʃən/ (UK)| n.|夸夸其谈，吹牛，冗长而空洞的讲话|Long, pompous, and empty speech or writing.|The politician's speech was full of bloviation and lacked any real substance. 这位政治家的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，缺乏任何实质内容。|Synonyms: bombast, rhetoric, verbosity, grandiloquence|Antonyms: conciseness, brevity, terseness|noun

bloviate:/ˈbləʊvɪeɪt/ (US) /ˈbləʊvieɪt/ (UK)| v.|夸夸其谈，吹牛|To speak or write verbosely and pompously.|He bloviated for hours about his accomplishments. 他滔滔不绝地吹嘘自己的成就好几个小时。|Synonyms: rant, rave, spout, declaim|Antonyms: be quiet, listen, be concise|verb
