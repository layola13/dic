instructive:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
有教育意义的；有启发性的
Providing or conveying information; tending to instruct.
This is an instructive experience. 这是一次有益的经历。

The documentary was highly instructive. 这部纪录片很有教育意义。

informative, educational, enlightening, enriching
uninformative, unhelpful, useless
