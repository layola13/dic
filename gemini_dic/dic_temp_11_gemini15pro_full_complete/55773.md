diversion:/daɪˈvɜːrʒən/ /dɪˈvɜːrʒən/| n.|转移；消遣；绕行|An activity that diverts the mind from tedious or serious concerns; a recreation or pastime.  Something that turns attention away from something else. A detour.|例句：Reading is a pleasant diversion. 阅读是一种愉快的消遣。

例句：The magician's sleight of hand was a clever diversion. 魔术师的手法是一种巧妙的转移注意力。|近义词： distraction, entertainment, pastime, recreation, detour, deviation|反义词： concentration, focus, work|词性: 名词


divert:/daɪˈvɜːrt/ /dɪˈvɜːrt/| vt.|转移；使…转向；使…消遣|To turn from one course or use to another.  To draw (the attention) to a different object or in a different direction.|例句：The loud noise diverted my attention. 巨大的噪音转移了我的注意力。

例句：Police diverted traffic around the accident scene. 警察将交通绕开了事故现场。|近义词： redirect, reroute, distract, entertain, amuse|反义词： focus, concentrate|词性: 动词
