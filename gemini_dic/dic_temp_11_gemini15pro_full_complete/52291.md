dichromatism: /daɪˈkroʊməˌtɪzəm/ | n. | 二色性，二色视觉 | The condition of having dichromatic vision, characterized by the ability to perceive only two primary colors. |  Some animals exhibit dichromatism, meaning they can only see two colors. 一些动物表现出二色性，这意味着它们只能看到两种颜色。

Dichromatism can make it difficult to distinguish between certain shades of red and green.  二色性会导致难以区分某些红色和绿色的阴影。 | 近义词: dichromacy | 反义词: trichromacy | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Greek *di- "two" + *chrōma "color" + *-ismos "condition."
