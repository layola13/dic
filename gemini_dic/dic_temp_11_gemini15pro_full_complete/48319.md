decretist:/dɪˈkriːtɪst/
|n.|法令学家；教令法学家|A specialist in decrees, especially in canon law.|The decretist argued the finer points of the papal bull.|近义词:canon lawyer, legal scholar|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) layman, non-specialist|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *decretista*, from Latin decretum ("decree") + -ista ("-ist").
