entry-level:/ˈɛntri ˌlɛvəl/| adj.|入门级的；初级的|Suitable for someone starting their career.  Designed for beginners or those with little experience.|She got an entry-level job at a law firm. 她在一间律师事务所获得了一份入门级的工作。|This software is perfect for entry-level users. 这个软件非常适合入门级用户。|近义词：beginner, basic, introductory, junior|反义词：advanced, senior, experienced|词性:adjective
词源: "entry" (entering or beginning) + "level" (a position in a hierarchy)
