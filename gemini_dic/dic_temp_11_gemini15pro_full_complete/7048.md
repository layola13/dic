anemia:/əˈniːmiə/ | n. | 贫血 |A condition in which there is a deficiency of red cells or of haemoglobin in the blood, resulting in pallor and weariness.|  She suffered from anemia after the surgery. 手术后她患上了贫血。|  synonyms: bloodlessness, deficiency of red blood cells | antonyms: plethora, polycythemia | noun

anemia:/əˈniːmiə/ | n. | 贫血症 |A lack of healthy red blood cells to carry adequate oxygen to your body's tissues.| He has iron-deficiency anemia. 他患有缺铁性贫血。| synonyms: bloodlessness, deficiency of red blood cells | antonyms: plethora, polycythemia | noun
