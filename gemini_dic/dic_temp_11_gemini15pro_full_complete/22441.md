bookholder:/ˈbʊkhəʊldər/ (UK) /ˈbʊkhˌoʊldər/ (US)| n.|书架；书挡；书签；读经台|A device or object used to hold a book open or upright.  A person who reserves or holds a book for someone else might also be referred to as a bookholder, although this is a less common usage.|She used a beautiful wooden bookholder to display her antique cookbooks.|近义词：bookstand, bookrest, bookmark (for holding a page open)|反义词: N/A|noun
