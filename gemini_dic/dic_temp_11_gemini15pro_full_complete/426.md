ablastemic:/ˌeɪbləˈstɛmɪk/
adjective
无胚层的; 非胚层的
Not arising from or composed of a blastema.  Relating to or denoting growth in which no blastema is involved.
例句：Ablastemic growth is characteristic of certain types of regeneration.  某些类型的再生以无胚层生长为特征。

例句：The researchers studied the ablastemic regenerative capacity of the organism. 研究人员研究了该生物体的非胚层再生能力。
近义词：ablastic
反义词：blastemic
词性: adjective
