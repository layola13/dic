misintends:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛndz/
|v.| 误解，曲解|To understand or interpret (something, such as a person's actions or words) wrongly. |She misintended his silence as disapproval, when he was simply deep in thought. 她误解了他的沉默是不赞成，而他只是在沉思。|
|v.| 怀有不良意图，心怀叵测|To have a wrong or improper intention.  |I don't think he misintended, he just expressed himself poorly. 我不认为他心怀叵测，他只是表达得不好。|
|近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret, misconstrue|
|反义词：understand, comprehend, grasp|
|词性：动词|
