agonist: /ˈæɡənɪst/ | n. | 促效剂；激动剂；主动肌 | A substance that initiates a physiological response when combined with a receptor.  A muscle that contracts while another relaxes.  One who strives or contends for superiority; a contestant. |  This drug acts as an agonist at dopamine receptors. 这种药物作为多巴胺受体的激动剂。 The biceps muscle is an agonist for flexing the elbow. 肱二头肌是屈肘的主动肌. He was a fierce political agonist. 他是一个激烈的政治竞争者。 |  protagonist, activator, stimulant | antagonist, inhibitor | noun

agonistic: /ˌæɡəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 竞争的；对抗的；好斗的 | Relating to or being an agonist; stimulating activity.  Combative or contentious. | Agonistic behavior is often observed in territorial animals.  在领地动物中经常观察到争斗行为。  He has a very agonistic personality. 他有着好斗的性格。| contentious, combative, belligerent | peaceful, cooperative, harmonious | adjective
