malagash:/ˈmæləɡæʃ/| n.|马拉加什(加拿大新斯科舍省的一个地方)|A small fishing village located in Nova Scotia, Canada, known for its beaches and Malagash Mine, which produces rock salt.|We spent our summer vacation in Malagash. 我们在马拉加什度过了暑假。|近义词:community, village|反义词:city, metropolis|词性:名词

Etymology:
The name "Malagash" is derived from the Mi'kmaq word *Malagawatchk*, meaning "place of the forked poles for spearing fish".
