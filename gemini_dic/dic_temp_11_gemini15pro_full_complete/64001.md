epistolical:/ɪˌpɪˈstɒlɪkəl/| adj.|书信的，书信体的|Of, relating to, or suitable to letters; contained in or carried on by letters. |例句：The novel is written in an epistolary style. 这部小说是以书信体写成的。

例句：Her epistolary relationship with her pen pal lasted for years. 她和笔友的书信来往持续了多年。|近义词：lettered, literary|反义词：oral, spoken|词性:adjective
