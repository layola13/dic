cowhided:/ˈkaʊhaɪdɪd/
| adj. |用牛皮鞭打的; 鞭打过的;  |Whipped or flogged with a cowhide whip.  Covered or made of cowhide. |The cowhided prisoner cried out in pain.  那个被牛皮鞭打的犯人痛苦地叫喊着。| The saddle was made of beautifully tooled cowhided.  这个马鞍是用制作精美的牛皮制成的。 |Synonyms:  whipped, flogged, lashed, beaten, thrashed|Antonyms:  unharmed, untouched, spared| Word type: Adjective

cowhide:/ˈkaʊhaɪd/
| n. |牛皮; 牛皮鞭 |The tough skin of a cow, often used for leather. A whip made from cowhide.|  He used a cowhide to keep the cattle in line. 他用牛皮鞭子把牛群赶成一排。|  The cowhide boots were expensive but durable. 牛皮靴很贵但很耐用。|Synonyms:  leather, hide, pelt (for the skin); whip, lash, scourge (for the whip) |Antonyms: N/A for the skin; caress, comfort (for the whip) | Word type: Noun
