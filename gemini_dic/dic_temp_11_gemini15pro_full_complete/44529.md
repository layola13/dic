crosstalk:/ˈkrɔːstɔːk/ | n. |串扰，串音，干扰|Unwanted transfer of signals between communication channels.|The crosstalk from the other line made it difficult to hear.  其他线路的串音使人很难听到。|近义词：interference, noise|反义词：clarity, silence|noun


crosstalk:/ˈkrɔːstɔːk/ | vi. |串扰，互相干扰|To interfere with another signal or conversation.|The signals from the two microphones were crosstalking. 两个麦克风的信号互相干扰。|近义词：interfere, disrupt|反义词：cooperate, harmonize|verb
