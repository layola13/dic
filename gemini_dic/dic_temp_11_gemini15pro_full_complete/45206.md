culler:/ˈkʌlər/| n.|剔除者，拣选者|A person or machine that removes inferior or unwanted items from a group.  A person who culls animals from a herd.|The culler removed the damaged fruit from the conveyor belt. 剔除者从传送带上移走了损坏的水果。|近义词：selector, picker, sorter|反义词:keeper, hoarder|noun
