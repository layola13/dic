beehive-ovens:/ˈbiːhaɪv ˌʌvənz/| n.|蜂窝煤炉;蜂窝状烤炉|A type of oven, typically made of brick or clay, with a domed shape resembling a beehive, used for baking.|Beehive ovens were traditionally used for baking bread and other goods. 蜂窝煤炉传统上用于烘烤面包和其他食物。|The bakery used several beehive ovens to produce their sourdough bread. 这家面包店用几个蜂窝煤炉来生产他们的酸面包。|Synonyms: beehive kilns, dome ovens|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps modern electric or gas ovens)|noun
