The nonuniformity of the paint job was noticeable. 油漆工作的不均匀性很明显。

The nonuniformity in the size of the apples made them difficult to pack. 苹果大小不均匀，难以包装。

| synonyms: inconsistency, unevenness, irregularity, variability, heterogeneity | antonyms: uniformity, consistency, evenness, regularity, homogeneity | noun
