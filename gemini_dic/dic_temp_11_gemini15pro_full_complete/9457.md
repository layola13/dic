antireformers:/ˌænti rɪˈfɔːrmərz/
noun
反改革者，反对改革的人
Those who oppose reforms or changes.
The antireformers blocked any attempts to modernize the education system. 反对改革者阻止了任何使教育系统现代化的尝试。
The antireformers clung to outdated practices. 反对改革者坚持过时的做法。
Synonyms: conservatives, traditionalists, reactionaries
Antonyms: reformers, progressives, radicals
n.
