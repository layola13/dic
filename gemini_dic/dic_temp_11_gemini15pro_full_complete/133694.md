nonfrequencies:/nɒnˈfriːkwənsiz/ | n. | 非频率;非常见频率;不常发生的频率 |  A term used to describe occurrences that do not happen regularly or frequently.  It refers to the absence or infrequency of specific events, oscillations, or patterns. | The researchers studied the nonfrequencies of certain genetic mutations in the population. 研究人员研究了人群中某些基因突变的非频率。| Near synonyms: rarities, infrequencies, anomalies | Antonyms: frequencies, commonalities | noun
