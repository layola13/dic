coniferous:/kəˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
针叶的；结球果的
(of a tree) bearing cones and having needle-shaped or scale-like leaves that do not fall in autumn.
The mountains are covered in dense coniferous forests. 这些山脉覆盖着茂密的针叶林。
The air smelled of pine resin in the coniferous woods. 针叶林里空气中弥漫着松脂的香味。
synonyms: evergreen, needle-bearing, cone-producing
antonyms: deciduous, broad-leaved
