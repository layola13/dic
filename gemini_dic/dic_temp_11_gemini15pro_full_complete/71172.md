fɪˈsɪpiːdiə/| n. | 裂脚类，陆生食肉目动物 |A suborder of carnivorous mammals characterized by having separate toes, as opposed to the pinnipeds (seals, sea lions, and walruses).  It includes dogs, cats, bears, weasels, and many others.| Dogs and cats are both fissipeds.  狗和猫都是裂脚类动物。 |Synonyms: land carnivores, terrestrial carnivores | Antonyms: pinnipeds| noun
