carnationed:/kɑːrˈneɪʃnd/
adjective
肉色的；粉红色的
having the pinkish color of flesh
Her carnationed cheeks suggested she'd been out in the cold. 她粉红色的脸颊表明她一直在外面受冻。
The baby's carnationed skin was soft and smooth. 婴儿粉嫩的肌肤柔软光滑。
近义词:pink, rosy, flesh-colored, blush
反义词:pale, wan, ashen
词性:adjective

词源:
From carnation + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin *caro, carnis (“flesh”).
