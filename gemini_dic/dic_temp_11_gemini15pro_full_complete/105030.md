kephalin:/ˈsɛfəlɪn/ | n. | 脑磷脂 | A type of phospholipid found in nerve tissue, especially brain tissue. It plays a role in blood clotting and cell membrane function. |  Cephalin is important for brain function. 脑磷脂对大脑功能很重要。|  Cephalin is a key component of cell membranes. 脑磷脂是细胞膜的关键组成部分。 | synonym: phosphatidylethanolamine, phosphatidylserine | antonym: None | noun
