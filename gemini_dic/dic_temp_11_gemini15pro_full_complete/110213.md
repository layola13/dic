life-threatening:/ˈlaɪfˌθrɛtnɪŋ/| adj.|危及生命的，致命的|Posing a threat to life; likely to cause death.|The patient was in a life-threatening condition. 患者病情危急。|例句：The doctor said it was a life-threatening illness. 医生说这是一种危及生命的疾病。|例句：He suffered a life-threatening injury in the car accident. 他在车祸中受了危及生命的伤。|近义词：mortal, fatal, lethal, perilous|反义词：harmless, safe, benign|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From "life" + "threatening". "Life" from Old English *līf, from Proto-Germanic *lībam. "Threatening" from "threaten" + "-ing". "Threaten" from Middle English threten, from Old English þrēatian.
