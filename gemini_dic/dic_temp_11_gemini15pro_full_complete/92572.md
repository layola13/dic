hythe:/haɪð/
noun
港口；登陆处；小港
a landing place; a small harbor
The hythe was bustling with activity as the fishing boats returned. 渔船返回时，港口熙熙攘攘。
synonyms: harbor, port, dock, wharf, pier
antonyms: inland


Etymology:
Old English *hȳþ, from Proto-Germanic *hūþą (“landing place”), from Proto-Indo-European *keh₂u- (“to observe, perceive”). Cognate with Old Frisian hēth (“dam”), Middle Dutch hiet, Dutch hijt (“dam”), Old High German hīt (“dam, weir, sandbank”), German Heit (“sandbank, dam”).
