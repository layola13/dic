demoniacally:/ˌdiːməˈnaɪkəli/ | adv. | 恶魔般地；疯狂地 | In a way that suggests a demon or demonic possession; with wicked or frenzied energy. | He laughed demoniacally and disappeared into the shadows. 他恶魔般地大笑一声，消失在阴影中。| The storm raged demoniacally, tearing at the trees and houses.  暴风雨疯狂肆虐，摧残着树木和房屋。| 近义词：fiendishly, satanically, diabolically, hellishly | 反义词：angelically, divinely, piously | 词性: 副词
