historiology:/hɪˌstɔːriˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 历史学理论;史学方法论 | The study of the writing of history; the principles and methods of historical research. |  Example: His course on historiology explored different approaches to understanding the past.  他的历史学理论课程探讨了理解过去的不同方法。

Example:  Debates in historiology often center on the objectivity of historical accounts. 史学理论的争论通常集中在历史叙述的客观性上。 | 近义词: philosophy of history, historical method | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "historical fabrication" if focusing on a contrast with rigorous methodology) | 词性: noun
