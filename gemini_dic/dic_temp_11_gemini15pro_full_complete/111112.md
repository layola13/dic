literalize:/ˈlɪtərəlaɪz/| v. |使字面化；逐字解释；按字面意义理解|To interpret something literally or word for word; to represent something concretely.|She tends to literalize metaphors, which can lead to misunderstandings. 她倾向于按字面意思理解比喻，这可能会导致误解。|Example: The director literalized the dream sequence by showing exactly what the character was imagining. 导演将梦境序列字面化，准确地展示了角色所想象的内容。|Synonyms: explicate, interpret literally|Antonyms:  figuratize, symbolize|verb

literalize:/ˈlɪtərəlaɪz/| v. |使具体化；实现|To make something concrete or real.|He literalized his vision by building the house he had always dreamed of.  他通过建造他一直梦想的房子，实现了愿景。|Example:  The author literalized the abstract concept of freedom by describing a bird escaping its cage. 作者通过描述一只鸟逃出笼子，将抽象的自由概念具体化。|Synonyms: realize, actualize, embody|Antonyms: abstract, conceptualize|verb
