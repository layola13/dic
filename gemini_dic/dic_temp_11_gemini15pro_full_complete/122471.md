moblog:/ˈmoʊblɒɡ/ (US), /ˈməʊblɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|手机博客，移动博客|A blog that is updated using a mobile phone or other portable device, allowing users to post text, images, and videos from anywhere with a network connection.|She started a moblog to document her travels around Southeast Asia. 她开设了一个手机博客，记录她在东南亚的旅行。|近义词：mobile blog, photoblog, microblog|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps traditional blog)|名词
