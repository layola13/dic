caress:/kəˈrɛs/| vt. n.|爱抚，抚摸；拥抱；奉承|To touch someone or something gently and affectionately.|She caressed his cheek tenderly. 她温柔地抚摸着他的脸颊。|He felt the gentle caress of the breeze on his face. 他感觉到微风轻柔地拂过他的脸。|近义词：stroke, fondle, pet, cuddle|反义词：strike, hit|词性:动词,名词|词源: From Old French *caresser "to touch, handle," from care "face" (from Latin cara "dear, beloved;" see cherish) + -ecer, frequentative suffix.
