anti-idealisms:/ˌænti aɪˈdiːəˌlɪzəmz/| n.|反理想主义|The rejection of idealism; doctrines or practices opposed to idealism.  It often emphasizes practicality, skepticism, or the belief that ideals are unattainable or undesirable.|His anti-idealisms led him to believe that pursuing utopian societies was futile.|近义词:cynicism, pragmatism, realism|反义词:idealism, utopianism|noun
