hyperelegant:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛlɪɡənt/
adj.
极其优雅的；过分讲究的
Excessively elegant; extremely refined and stylish.
She wore a hyperelegant gown to the gala, dripping in diamonds. 她穿着极其优雅的礼服参加晚会，浑身珠光宝气。
The hyperelegant decor of the hotel lobby was breathtaking. 酒店大堂极其优雅的装饰令人叹为观止。
近义词:  refined, sophisticated, polished, exquisite, very elegant
反义词:  sloppy, unrefined, casual, simple, plain
词性: adjective
词源: From hyper- "excessive" + elegant. "Hyper-" is from Greek hyper "over, beyond." "Elegant" is from Latin elegantem (nominative elegans) "choice, tasteful, fine, elegant," present participle of eligere "pick out, select," from ex "out" (see ex-) + legere "choose, gather, read" (see lecture (n.)).
