flameproof:/ˈfleɪmpruːf/
adjective
防火的，阻燃的
Treated or made with a substance to prevent or inhibit the spread of fire.
The curtains were flameproof.  这些窗帘是防火的。
Firefighters wear flameproof clothing. 消防队员穿着阻燃服。
synonym:fire-resistant, fireproof, non-flammable
antonym:flammable, inflammable
adjective
