haughty:/ˈhɔːti/ (US) /ˈhɔːti/ (UK)| adj.|傲慢的，自大的|Having or showing arrogant superiority to and disdain of those one views as unworthy.|She gave him a haughty look. 她傲慢地看了他一眼。|
近义词：arrogant, supercilious, disdainful, proud, snobbish|
反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|
词性: adjective|
词源: From Old French *halt* (modern French *haut*), from Latin *altus* ("high, deep, tall, noble").|
