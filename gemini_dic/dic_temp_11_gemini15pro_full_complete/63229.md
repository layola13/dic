enterocele: /ˈentərəˌsiːl/ | n. | 肠疝，子宫肠疝 |A hernia of the intestine into the vagina. It is a herniation of the peritoneal sac containing a portion of the small intestine into the rectovaginal septum, between the rectum and posterior vaginal wall.  |  She was diagnosed with an enterocele after experiencing pelvic pressure. 她被诊断出患有肠疝，此前她一直感到骨盆受压。

After childbirth, she developed an enterocele.  分娩后，她患上了肠疝。 | 近义词: vaginal hernia, hernia | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From entero- + -cele.  Entero- comes from Greek *énteron* meaning "intestine." -Cele comes from Greek *kēlē* meaning "tumor," "swelling," or "hernia."
