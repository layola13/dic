buzzes:/ˈbʌzɪz/| v.|嗡嗡声，发出嗡嗡声，(非正式)打电话|The bee buzzes around the flower. 蜜蜂绕着花嗡嗡叫。|近义词：hums, drones, whirs, rings (informal)|反义词:silences|verb (third person singular present)

Etymology: From Middle English *busien, from Old English *bysgian (“to occupy, busy”), from Proto-Germanic *busgijaną (“to be busy, occupy oneself diligently”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰus- (“to abound, sprout, swell”).  The sense of "humming sound" developed later, imitative of the sound.
