dura:/ˈdjʊərə/| n.| 硬脑膜 |The outermost and toughest of the three membranes (meninges) surrounding the brain and spinal cord. | The surgeon carefully repaired the tear in the dura. 外科医生仔细地修补了硬脑膜上的裂口。| synonyms:  meninx (general term for any of the meninges), pachymeninx| antonyms:  pia mater, arachnoid mater (other meninges, but not true opposites)| noun
