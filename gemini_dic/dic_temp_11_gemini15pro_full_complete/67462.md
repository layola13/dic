extollingly:/ɪkˈstəʊlɪŋli/
adverb
赞美地，颂扬地
In a manner that expresses high praise.
He spoke extollingly of her many virtues. 他热情地赞扬了她的诸多美德。
Synonyms: laudatorily, praisingly, glowingly
Antonyms: critically, disparagingly, negatively
词性: 副词
