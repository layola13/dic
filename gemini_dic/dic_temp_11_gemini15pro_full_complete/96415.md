indisputable:/ˌɪndɪˈspjuːtəbl/ /ˌɪndɪˈspjutəbl/ | adj. | 无可争辩的，不容置疑的 | Not able to be challenged or denied. |  Her talent is indisputable. 她的才华无可争辩。

The evidence against him was indisputable.  对他不利的证据是确凿无疑的。

近义词：undeniable, unquestionable, irrefutable, incontestable | 反义词：disputable, questionable, debatable | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *indisputable*, from Late Latin *indisputabilis*, from in- "not" + disputabilis "arguable, debatable" (from disputare "to discuss, examine").
