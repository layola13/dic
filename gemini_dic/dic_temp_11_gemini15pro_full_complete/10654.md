approprie:/əˈproʊprieɪt/| v.|占用，拨出(款项)；盗用|To set apart or authorize for some specific purpose; to take possession of or make use of exclusively for oneself, often without permission.|The government appropriated funds for the new hospital. 政府拨款兴建新医院。|近义词：allocate, earmark, seize, usurp|反义词: relinquish, return|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin appropriatus, past participle of appropriare "to make one's own," from Latin ad- "to" + proprius "one's own."
