disproportion:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃən/| n.|不均衡，不成比例|lack of proportion or equality|There is a huge disproportion between the number of men and women in the tech industry. 科技行业男女数量严重失衡。|imbalance, disparity, inequality|balance, equality, parity|noun


disproportionate:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənət/| adj.|不成比例的，不相称的|too large or too small in comparison with something else|The punishment was disproportionate to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不成比例。|excessive, unequal, uneven|proportionate, balanced, commensurate|adjective


disproportionately:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənətli/| adv.|不成比例地，不相称地|to a disproportionate degree|Minority groups are disproportionately affected by poverty. 少数群体受贫困的影响尤为严重。|excessively, unduly, unfairly|proportionately, equally, fairly|adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (not, opposite of) + proportion (balance, ratio). Ultimately from Latin pro- (for, forth) + portio (part, share).
