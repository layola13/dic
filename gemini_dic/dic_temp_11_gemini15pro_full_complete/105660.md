kingliest:/ˈkɪŋliɪst/
adjective
最像国王的；最高贵的；最雄伟的
most kingly; most regal or majestic
The kingliest of all the monarchs was known for his wisdom and benevolence. 所有君主中最有王者风范的那位以他的智慧和仁慈而闻名。
Near Synonyms: regal, majestic, noble, stately, august, imperial
Antonyms: base, common, ignoble, lowly, plebeian
