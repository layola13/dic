convergent:/kənˈvɜːrdʒənt/
adjective
趋同的，收敛的
Tending to move toward a common point or result.
例句：The two rivers are convergent, eventually joining to form a larger river.  这两条河流是汇合的，最终汇聚成一条更大的河流。

例句：Convergent evolution is a fascinating phenomenon in biology.  趋同进化是生物学中一个引人入胜的现象。

近义词：converging, meeting, uniting, coalescing, merging
反义词：divergent, disparate, differing

词性: adjective


converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/
verb
汇聚，集中
(of lines, paths, etc.) to come together at a point; to meet.
例句：The paths converged at the top of the hill.  这些小路在山顶汇合。

例句：The crowd converged on the stadium.  人群聚集在体育场。

近义词：meet, intersect, join, merge, unite
反义词：diverge, separate

词性: verb


convergence:/kənˈvɜːrdʒəns/
noun
汇合，趋同
The act of converging; the state of being convergent.
例句：The convergence of the two rivers created a fertile delta. 两条河流的汇合创造了一个肥沃的三角洲。

例句：The convergence of opinions among scientists led to a new theory.  科学家们意见的趋同导致了一个新理论的产生。

近义词：meeting, union, junction, confluence, merging
反义词：divergence, separation

词性: noun


词源:  From Latin *convergēns*, present participle of convergere  "to incline together," from com- "together" (see com-) + vergere "to bend, turn, incline" (see verge (n.)).
