coals:/koʊlz/ | n. |煤块，煤炭| Burning coals provide heat. 燃烧的煤炭提供热量。|The blacksmith stoked the coals in the forge. 铁匠在锻炉里拨弄煤块。|近义词:fuel, coal, embers|反义词:None|词性:名词

词源:
From Old English col, from Proto-Germanic *kula, from Proto-Indo-European *g(e)u-lo-, meaning "live coal, burning coal."
