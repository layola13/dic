indiscernibility:/ˌɪndɪˌsɜːrnəˈbɪləti/ /ˌɪndɪˌsɜːrnəˈbɪlədi/| n.|不可分辨性；不可区分性|The indiscernibility of the twins made it difficult to tell them apart. 这对双胞胎很难区分。|The principle of the indiscernibility of identicals states that if two things are identical, they must share all their properties. 完全同一律原则指出，如果两个事物是完全相同的，它们必须共享所有属性。|近义词：indistinguishability, sameness, identity|反义词：distinguishability, difference, distinctness|词性:noun

indiscernible:/ˌɪndɪˈsɜːrnəbl/ /ˌɪndɪˈsɜːnɪbl/| adj.|难以分辨的；不易察觉的|The differences between the two versions are almost indiscernible. 两个版本之间的差异几乎难以察觉。|The stars were indiscernible to the naked eye due to the light pollution. 由于光污染，肉眼无法分辨星星。|近义词：imperceptible, undetectable, invisible|反义词：perceptible, detectable, visible, obvious|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *indiscernibilis, from in- (“not”) + discernibilis (“distinguishable”), from discernere (“to distinguish”). Ultimately from Latin dis- (“apart”) + cernere (“to separate, distinguish”).
