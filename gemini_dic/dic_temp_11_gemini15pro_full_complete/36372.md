coiner:/ˈkɔɪnər/| n.|创造者，发明者，(尤指新词或新短语的) 创造者|A person who creates a new word or phrase. |例句：He is the coiner of the term "blogosphere". 他是“博客圈”一词的创造者。

例句：She's a prolific coiner of new words and expressions. 她是一位创作新词和表达方式的高手。|近义词：inventor, creator, originator|反义词:imitator, copier|词性:noun
