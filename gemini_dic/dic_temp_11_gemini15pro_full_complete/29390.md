cat:/kæt/ | n. |猫 |A small domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur, a short snout, and retractile claws. It is widely kept as a pet or for catching mice, and several breeds have been developed. |The cat sat on the mat. 猫坐在垫子上。

The cat chased a mouse.  猫追赶老鼠。|近义词：feline, kitty, pussycat |反义词: None (Animals don't have direct opposites) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *catte*, from Old English *catt* (male cat), *catte* (female cat), from Late Latin *cattus*, of uncertain origin, perhaps from a Nubian word *kaddîska* “wildcat,” or from an Afro-Asiatic word represented by Berber *kaddîs*.
