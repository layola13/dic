devas:/ˈdeɪvəz/| n. |天神，神灵|Plural of deva, a Sanskrit word referring to a deity or divine being.  It is often used in Hinduism, Buddhism, and Jainism. | The ancient texts described the devas as benevolent beings residing in celestial realms. 古代文献将天神描述为居住在天界的仁慈生命。| The devas are believed to possess supernatural powers and influence human affairs. 人们相信天神拥有超自然的力量并能影响人类事务。| 近义词: gods, deities, divinities | 反义词: demons, asuras | 词性: noun (plural)
