outscheming:/ˌaʊtˈskiːmɪŋ/
verb
用计谋胜过，智胜
defeating someone by being more clever or cunning in your plans
He outsmarted his opponents by outscheming them in the negotiations. 他在谈判中智胜了对手，用计谋胜过了他们。
outmaneuvering, outsmarting, outwitting
being outsmarted, being outmaneuvered
v.
