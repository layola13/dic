irremunerable:/ɪˌrɪˈmjuːnərəbl/ /ɪˌriˈmjuːnərəbl/
adjective
无数的，数不清的
Too numerous to be counted.  Incapable of being remunerated; not yielding a return.
The archaeologist discovered an irremunerable number of artifacts at the dig site.  考古学家在挖掘现场发现了无数的文物。
The stars in the night sky seemed irremunerable. 夜晚的天空中的星星看起来数不胜数。
synonyms: countless, innumerable, myriad, untold, infinite, immeasurable
antonyms: countable, few, limited, measurable, scant, scarce
词性: adjective
