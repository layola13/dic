braille: /breɪl/ | n. |盲文|A system of writing and printing for blind or visually impaired people, consisting of raised dots that can be read with the fingertips.  It is named after its inventor, Louis Braille. |例句：She learned to read braille at a young age. 她很小的时候就学会了阅读盲文。

例句：The library has a large collection of books in braille.  图书馆收藏了大量盲文书籍。|近义词：tactile writing, raised dots|反义词: print (in the context of reading for sighted people)|词性: noun
