draughtboard:/ˈdrɑːftbɔːrd/ /ˈdræftbɔːrd/| n. |国际象棋棋盘，西洋跳棋棋盘|A board marked with squares, typically 64, used for playing draughts or chess.|We played draughts on the draughtboard. 我们在棋盘上玩西洋跳棋。|Synonyms: checkerboard, chessboard|Antonyms: None|Noun

draughtboard:/ˈdrɑːftbɔːrd/ /ˈdræftbɔːrd/| n. |草图板，绘图板 (less common usage)|A board used for drawing or sketching.|The architect laid out his plans on the draughtboard. 建筑师把他的计划放在绘图板上。|Synonyms: drawing board|Antonyms: None|Noun
