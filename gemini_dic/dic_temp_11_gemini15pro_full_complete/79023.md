giusto:/ˈdʒuːstoʊ/ (US), /ˈdʒuːstəʊ/ (UK)| adj. |正确的，恰当的，公正的|Right, correct, fair, appropriate, just.  It refers to something that is morally right, legally correct, or appropriate for a particular situation.|例句：The judge made a giusto decision. 法官做出了公正的裁决。|近义词：right, correct, proper, appropriate, fair, just|反义词：wrong, incorrect, improper, inappropriate, unfair, unjust|词性:形容词
词源: From Italian *giusto*, derived from Latin *iustus* "just, upright, lawful".
