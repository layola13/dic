gladded:/ˈɡlædɪd/ |v. |使高兴，使欣喜 |Made glad; cheered. | The good news gladdened our hearts.  这好消息让我们很高兴。 | cheered, delighted, pleased | saddened, depressed | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English gladen, from Old English glæddian (“to make glad, gladden”), from glæd (“glad”).
