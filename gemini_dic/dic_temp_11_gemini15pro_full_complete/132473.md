nonderivatively:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɪvətɪvli/ | adv. | 非派生地，不经推导地 | In a way that is not derived or formed from something else; in an original or primary manner. | He used the term "red" nonderivatively, simply referring to the color. 他非派生地使用了“红色”一词，仅仅是指颜色。|  She experienced joy nonderivatively, not because of any external event. 她非派生地体验到了快乐，而不是因为任何外部事件。 | 近义词: originally, primarily, directly | 反义词: derivatively, secondarily, indirectly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "non-" (not) + "derivative" (derived from something else) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Derivative" ultimately comes from the Latin "dērīvāre" (to draw off, derive).
