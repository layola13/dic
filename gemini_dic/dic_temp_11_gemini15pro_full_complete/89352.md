homologue:/ˈhɒməlɒɡ/ (UK), /ˈhɑːməlɔːɡ/ (US)| n.|同系物；类似物；同源染色体|Something that corresponds to something else in a different context;  A compound belonging to the same series, usually with a fixed difference between molecular formulas, as the alkanes differing by CH2.  In biology, one of a pair of chromosomes.|Chromosome 2 in humans is the homologue of chromosome 2 in chimpanzees. 人类的2号染色体与黑猩猩的2号染色体是同源染色体。|synonyms: counterpart, equivalent, analog, parallel|antonyms: heterologue|noun

homologous:/həˈmɒləɡəs/ (UK), /həˈmɑːləɡəs/ (US)| adj.|同源的；类似的|Having the same relation, relative position, or structure.|The forelimb of a bat is homologous to the wing of a bird. 蝙蝠的前肢与鸟的翅膀是同源的。|synonyms: corresponding, analogous, similar|antonyms: heterologous|adjective
