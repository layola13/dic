alternation:/ˌɔːltərˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 交替，轮流，间隔 | the action or process of alternating or being alternated | The alternation of day and night is a basic natural phenomenon.  白天和黑夜的交替是一种基本的自然现象。 | synonyms: rotation, interchange, succession | antonyms: continuation, uniformity | noun


alternation:/ˌɔːltərˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 交替发生的事物；交替出现的两种状态之一 | one of a number of things that alternate with each other |  Heat and cold are alternations of temperature. 冷热是温度的交替状态。| synonyms: alternative, option, choice | antonyms: sameness, constancy | noun
