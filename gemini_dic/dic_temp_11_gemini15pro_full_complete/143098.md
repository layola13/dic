outbribes:/ˌaʊtˈbraɪbz/
verb
以更高的贿赂胜过;行贿超过
to bribe someone with a larger sum than another person has offered
He outbribed his rivals and secured the contract. 他以更高的贿赂胜过竞争对手，拿到了合同。
近义词: outbid, surpass in bribery
反义词: be outbribed, be underbid in bribery
词性:v.
