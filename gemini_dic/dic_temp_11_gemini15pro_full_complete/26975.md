calcinatory:/ˌkælˈsɪnətɔːri/ /ˌkælsɪˈneɪtəri/
noun
煅烧炉，焙烧炉
A furnace or kiln used for calcination.
The alchemist used his calcinatory to heat the ore. 炼金术士用他的煅烧炉加热矿石。
Synonyms: kiln, furnace, oven
Antonyms: cooler, refrigerator
词性: 名词


calcinatory:/ˌkælˈsɪnətɔːri/ /ˌkælsɪˈneɪtəri/
adjective
煅烧的，焙烧的
Relating to or used for calcination.
The calcinatory process removed the impurities from the metal. 煅烧过程去除了金属中的杂质。
Synonyms:  roasting, burning, incinerating
Antonyms: cooling, freezing
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *calcinatorium, from Latin calcinare "to burn lime," from calx "lime"
