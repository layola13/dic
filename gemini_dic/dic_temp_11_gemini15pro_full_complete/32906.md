choo-chooing:/ˌtʃuːˈtʃuːɪŋ/
|n./v.|（火车）发出咔嚓咔嚓的声音；模仿火车的声音|The sound a train makes, often imitated by children.  It can be used as a noun referring to the sound itself, or as a verb describing the act of making the sound.|
|The children were choo-chooing as they pushed their toy train along the floor. 孩子们一边推着玩具火车在地板上走，一边发出咔嚓咔嚓的声音。|
|The choo-chooing of the train echoed through the valley. 火车的咔嚓声回荡在山谷中。|
|近义词: chugging, rumbling|
|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps silence)|
|词性:  Onomatopoeia (拟声词) - used as both a noun and a verb|
