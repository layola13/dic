nonethnic:/nɒnˈɛθnɪk/
adjective
非种族的；不限于特定种族的
Not relating to or limited by ethnicity.
The organization promotes nonethnic policies to ensure equal opportunities for all.  这个组织提倡非种族政策，以确保所有人机会均等。
The city boasts a nonethnic cultural center that celebrates diversity.  这座城市拥有一座不限于特定种族的文化中心，颂扬多样性。
近义词: nonracial, multiethnic, multicultural
反义词: ethnic, racial
