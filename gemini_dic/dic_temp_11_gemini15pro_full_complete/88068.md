highlands:/ˈhaɪləndz/ | n. | 高地；丘陵地带；苏格兰高地 | A mountainous region or elevated plateau.  Often used to refer to the Scottish Highlands. | We travelled through the highlands of Scotland. 我们游览了苏格兰高地。| synonyms: uplands, hills, mountains, moorland | antonyms: lowlands, valleys | noun

highland:/ˈhaɪlənd/ | adj. | 高地的；山地的 | Of or relating to the highlands, especially of Scotland. |  The highland landscape is breathtaking. 高地景色令人叹为观止。| synonyms: mountainous, upland | antonyms: lowland | adjective
