clone:/kloʊn/ (US) /kləʊn/ (UK)| n. v.|克隆，复制；无性繁殖|A genetically identical copy of an organism or cell.  A person or thing that appears to be an exact copy of another.|
Scientists have successfully cloned a sheep. 科学家们成功地克隆了一只绵羊。
He cloned the hard drive to back up his data. 他克隆了硬盘来备份他的数据。
|近义词：copy, duplicate, replica, reproduce|反义词: original, prototype|词性: 名词, 动词
