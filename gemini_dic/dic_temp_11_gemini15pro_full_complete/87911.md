hieroglyphics:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪks/
n.
象形文字
A system of writing using picture-like symbols, especially that of the ancient Egyptians.
The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher Egyptian hieroglyphics.  罗塞塔石碑帮助学者们破译了埃及象形文字。

hieroglyphic:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk/
adj.
象形文字的
Written in or belonging to a system of hieroglyphics.
The hieroglyphic inscriptions on the tomb told the story of the pharaoh. 墓穴上的象形文字铭文讲述了法老的故事。
n.
象形符号
A picture-like symbol used in a system of hieroglyphics.
The ankh is a well-known Egyptian hieroglyphic.  安卡是著名的埃及象形文字符号。

近义词: hieroglyph, ideogram
反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑alphabet 字母)
词性: 名词(n.)，形容词(adj.)
