non-biodegradable:/ˌnɒnˌbaɪəʊdɪˈɡreɪdəbl/ | adj. | 不可生物降解的 | Not capable of being decomposed by bacteria or other living organisms. | Plastic bags are non-biodegradable and contribute to environmental pollution.  塑料袋是不可生物降解的，会导致环境污染。 | Example: Many plastic products are non-biodegradable, posing a threat to the environment. 许多塑料制品不可生物降解，对环境构成威胁。 | Synonyms: non-decomposable, non-putrefiable | Antonyms: biodegradable, decomposable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): from Latin *nōn* "not"
bio-: from Greek *bios* "life"
degradable: from French *dégradable*, from *dégrader* "to degrade," from Latin *dēgradārī* "to step down," from *dē-* "down" + *gradus* "step"
