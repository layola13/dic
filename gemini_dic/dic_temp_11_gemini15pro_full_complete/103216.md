jetsam:/ˈdʒɛtsəm/| n. |(海上)抛弃的货物，(船舶)失事时为减轻船重而抛弃的货物；废弃物；无用之物|Goods thrown overboard from a ship to lighten it in an emergency, especially to prevent it from sinking; discarded or useless material.|The storm washed ashore a collection of flotsam and jetsam.  暴风雨把一堆漂浮物和抛弃物冲上了岸。|The beach was littered with jetsam from the wrecked ship.  海滩上散落着失事船只的抛弃物。|Synonyms: flotsam, debris, wreckage, refuse|Antonyms: valuables, treasure|Noun
