derisiveness:/dɪˈraɪsɪvnəs/
n.
嘲笑, 嘲弄, 奚落
The state of being derisive; mockery or ridicule.
His derisiveness towards her ideas made her feel small. 他对她的想法的嘲笑让她感到渺小。
Near Synonyms: mockery, ridicule, scorn, contempt, disdain
Antonyms: respect, admiration, reverence
