nonfictitious:/ˌnɒnˈfɪkʃəs/| adj.|非虚构的，真实的|Based on facts; not made up; true.  Relating to or being writing that is about real events and not imagined ones. | This is a nonfictitious account of the war. 这是一个关于战争的非虚构的报道。| The book is a nonfictitious narrative of her life. 这本书是她生活的真实叙述。| synonyms: factual, true, real, actual, authentic, genuine, historical, truthful, veracious| antonyms: fictitious, fictional, fabricated, invented, imaginary, unreal, untrue, false, spurious| adjective
