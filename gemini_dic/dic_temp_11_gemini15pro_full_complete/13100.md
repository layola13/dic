astrophel: /ˈæstrəfɛl/ | n. | 星星的恋人；诗人 | A lover of the stars; a poet.  Specifically, a name used by Sir Philip Sidney to refer to himself (or a version of himself) in his sonnet sequence *Astrophel and Stella*. |  Example: He saw himself as an astrophel, yearning for his distant love. 他视自己为星辰恋人，渴望得到远方爱人的垂青。

Example:  The name "Astrophel" became associated with poetic yearning after Sidney's sonnets.  “Astrophel”这个名字在西德尼的十四行诗之后就和诗意的渴望联系在了一起。 | 近义词: poet, bard, versifier | 反义词: None (This is a specific term, not a general opposite) | 词性: Noun
