overlaudatory: /ˌoʊvərˈlɔːdəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 过分赞扬的，溢美之词的 | excessively praising; using too much praise | His overlaudatory remarks about the mediocre painting embarrassed everyone. 他对那幅平庸的画作的过分赞扬让每个人都很尴尬。| The overlaudatory review in the newspaper seemed insincere. 报纸上过分赞扬的评论显得不真诚。 | 近义词:  effusive, gushing, extravagant, hyperbolic | 反义词: critical, disparaging, understated | 词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "laudatory" (from Latin "laudatorius," meaning "praising").
