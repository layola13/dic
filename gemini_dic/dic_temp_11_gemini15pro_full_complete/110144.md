lieberkühn: /ˈliːbərˌkuːn/ | n. | Lieberkühn腺，小肠腺 | A simple tubular gland in the mucous membrane of the small intestine, opening between the bases of the villi.  |  The lieberkühn crypts secrete various enzymes important for digestion.  Lieberkühn腺分泌多种对消化很重要的酶。 | synonym: intestinal gland, crypt of Lieberkühn | antonym: N/A | noun

lieberkühn: /ˈliːbərˌkuːn/ | adj. |  Lieberkühn的，与Lieberkühn腺有关的 | Pertaining to the Lieberkühn crypts or glands in the small intestine. | The lieberkühn epithelium is responsible for absorbing nutrients. Lieberkühn上皮细胞负责吸收营养物质。| synonym: intestinal, enteric | antonym: N/A | adjective
