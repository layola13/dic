|n.|必要性；必然性；迫使；强制|The act of necessitating something; the state of being necessitated; something that necessitates something else. |Economic necessity often forces people to make difficult choices. 经济上的必要性常常迫使人们做出艰难的选择。|

necessitate:/nəˈsesɪˌteɪt/
|v.|使成为必要；需要|To make something necessary; to require.|The increasing demand necessitates expanding the factory.  不断增长的需求使得扩建工厂成为必要。|
|近义词：require, demand, oblige, compel|
|反义词：obviate, preclude|
|词性：动词|



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *necessitatus, past participle of necessitare (“to compel, force”), from Latin necessitas (“necessity”), from necesse (“unavoidable, necessary”).
