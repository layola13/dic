jubilantly:/ˌdʒuːbɪləntli/| adv.|欢欣鼓舞地，兴高采烈地|In a very happy and triumphant way, showing great joy, especially due to a success.|They cheered jubilantly as their team won the championship. 他们欢欣鼓舞地为他们赢得冠军的球队欢呼。|近义词：joyfully, triumphantly, ecstatically, gleefully|反义词: sadly, dejectedly, mournfully|词性:副词
jubilant:/ˈdʒuːbɪlənt/ | adj. | 欢欣的，喜气洋洋的 | Feeling or expressing great joy, especially due to a success. |  The jubilant crowd celebrated the victory.  欢腾的人群庆祝胜利。| 近义词：joyful, elated, triumphant | 反义词：sad, depressed, mournful | 词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *jubilantem, present participle of jubilare "to shout for joy," from Latin jubilare "to shout with joy," related to jubilus "a joyful shout," from Hebrew yobel "ram's horn," hence "trumpet," "jubilee."
