ostentatiousness:/ˌɒstɛnˈteɪʃəsnəs/ /ˌɑːstɛnˈteɪʃəsnəs/
|n.|炫耀，卖弄，招摇|The quality of being ostentatious; excessive showiness or display intended to impress others.|Her ostentatiousness was evident in her designer clothes and expensive jewelry. 她的炫耀体现在她穿的名牌服装和昂贵的珠宝上。|Synonyms: showiness, pretentiousness, flashiness, gaudiness|Antonyms: modesty, humility, simplicity, unpretentiousness|noun
