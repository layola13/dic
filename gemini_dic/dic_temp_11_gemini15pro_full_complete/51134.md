despoil:/dɪˈspɔɪl/| vt.|掠夺，抢劫，破坏|To steal or violently remove valuable or attractive possessions from a place; to make a place less attractive by damaging or destroying it.|The invaders despoiled the city of its treasures. 侵略者掠夺了这座城市的宝藏。

The loggers despoiled the forest. 伐木工人破坏了森林。|近义词：plunder, pillage, rob, loot, ravage, devastate|反义词：protect, preserve, conserve|词性：动词
