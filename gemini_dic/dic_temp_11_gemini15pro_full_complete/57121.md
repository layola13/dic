dout:/daʊt/| n. | 怀疑；疑惑；不确定 | Doubt is a feeling of uncertainty or lack of conviction. |  I have some doubts about his ability to do the job. 我对他的工作能力有些怀疑。

| 近义词：uncertainty, suspicion, misgiving | 反义词: certainty, confidence, belief | 词性: 名词


dout:/daʊt/| vt. | 怀疑；不相信 | To doubt is to question the truth or validity of something. | I doubt he'll come. 我怀疑他会不会来。

| 近义词：question, distrust, disbelieve | 反义词: trust, believe, accept | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *douter, from Latin dubitare “to hesitate, waver in opinion, be uncertain, doubt," frequentative of dubare “to be in doubt, waver”
