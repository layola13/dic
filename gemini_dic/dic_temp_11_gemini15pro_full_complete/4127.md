air-dried:/ˈeərˌdraɪd/| adj.|风干的；晾干的|Dried by exposure to the air, rather than by artificial heat.|The air-dried tomatoes had a concentrated, intense flavor. 这些风干的西红柿味道浓郁而强烈。|Synonyms: sun-dried, dehydrated, desiccated|Antonyms: fresh, wet, undried|adjective
