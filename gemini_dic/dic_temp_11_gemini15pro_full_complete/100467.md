intervene:/ˌɪntərˈviːn/| v. |介入，干预，插话|To come between disputing people or groups; to occur between two times or events.|例句：The central bank intervened in the currency markets. 中央银行对货币市场进行了干预。

例句：He intervened to stop the fight. 他介入以阻止这场争斗。|近义词：intercede, mediate, interfere|反义词：ignore, abstain|词性:动词
