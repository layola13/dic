hochepot:/ˈhɒtʃpɒt/ (UK), /ˈhɑːtʃpɑːt/ (US)| n.|大杂烩；混合物|A confused mixture; a jumble.|The city is a hochepot of architectural styles.|Synonyms: medley, mixture, blend, mishmash, potpourri, farrago, gallimaufry|Antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *hochepot, from hocher ("to shake") + pot ("pot").  Originally referred to a stew of various meats and vegetables cooked together in a pot.
