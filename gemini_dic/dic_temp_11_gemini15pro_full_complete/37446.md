commiserations:/kəˌmɪzəˈreɪʃənz/
|n.|慰问，同情(常用复数)|An expression of sympathy for someone, especially on their misfortune.|My commiserations on your loss.  我对你的损失表示慰问。|Synonyms: condolences, sympathy, solace|Antonyms: congratulations, felicitations|noun


commiseration:/kəˌmɪzəˈreɪʃən/
|n.|慰问，同情|An expression of sympathy for someone, especially on their misfortune.|He offered his commiseration to the family of the victim. 他向受害者家属表示慰问。|Synonyms: condolence, sympathy, solace|Antonyms: congratulation, felicitation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin commiseratio, from Latin commiseror "I pity," from com- "with" + miseror "I lament."
