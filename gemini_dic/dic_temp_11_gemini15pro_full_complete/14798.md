aws:/ˈɔːz/ (US) /ɔːz/ (UK)| n. |亚马逊网络服务 (Amazon Web Services)| Amazon Web Services (AWS) is a comprehensive, evolving cloud computing platform provided by Amazon. It includes a mixture of infrastructure as a service (IaaS), platform as a service (PaaS) and packaged software as a service (SaaS) offerings.| Amazon provides a suite of cloud-computing services, known as AWS. 亚马逊提供一套云计算服务，称为AWS。| synonym: cloud platform, cloud services | antonym: on-premises infrastructure | noun
