noteless:/ˈnəʊtlɪs/
adjective
无记载的；不值得注意的；默默无闻的
without notes; not worth noting; unremarkable, insignificant
His contributions to the project were noteless and quickly forgotten. 他对这个项目的贡献微不足道，很快就被遗忘了。
近义词:insignificant, unremarkable, obscure, unnoticed
反义词:noted, remarkable, significant, prominent
词性:adjective
