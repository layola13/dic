dressage:/drɛˈsɑːʒ/ /drəˈsɑːʒ/
noun
盛装舞步；马术训练
The art of riding and training a horse in a manner that develops obedience, flexibility, and balance.
例句：The rider demonstrated impressive skill in dressage.  骑手展示了令人印象深刻的盛装舞步技巧。

例句：Dressage is often considered the highest expression of horse training. 盛装舞步常被认为是马术训练的最高境界。
近义词：equestrianism, horsemanship
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性:n.


dressages:/drɛˈsɑːʒɪz/ /drəˈsɑːʒɪz/
noun (plural)
盛装舞步比赛（dressage的复数形式）
Multiple instances of the equestrian sport of dressage.
例句：The dressages at the Olympics were breathtaking. 奥运会的盛装舞步比赛令人叹为观止。

例句：Several dressages are scheduled throughout the year.  全年安排了几场盛装舞步比赛。
近义词：equestrian events, horse shows
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性:n.
