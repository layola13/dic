obstreperosity: /əbˌstrepəˈrɑːsəti/ /əbˌstrepəˈrɒsəti/
n.
喧闹，吵闹；难驾驭
Noisy and difficult to control behavior.
The obstreperosity of the children made it difficult for the teacher to conduct the class. 孩子们的吵闹使得老师难以进行课堂教学。
Synonyms: boisterousness, clamor, racket, uproar
Antonyms: quiet, calmness, peacefulness
词性: 名词


obstreperous: /əbˈstrepərəs/ /əbˈstrepərəs/
adj.
喧闹的，吵闹的；难驾驭的
Noisy and difficult to control.
The obstreperous crowd cheered loudly for their team. 喧闹的人群为他们的队伍大声欢呼。
Synonyms: unruly, boisterous, clamorous, vociferous
Antonyms: quiet, calm, peaceful, obedient
词性: 形容词
