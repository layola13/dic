ceruleum:/səˈruːliəm/ /səˈrʊliəm/| n. |天蓝色，蔚蓝色；天蓝色颜料|A deep sky-blue color; a pigment of this color.|The artist mixed ceruleum with white to create a lighter shade for the sky. 艺术家将天蓝色与白色混合，为天空创造了更浅的色调。|近义词：azure, sky-blue, cobalt blue|反义词:  None (Color, no direct opposite)|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *caeruleus* meaning "dark blue," ultimately related to *caelum* meaning "sky, heaven."
