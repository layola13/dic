crystallizers:/ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzərz/
n.
结晶器，晶体器
A crystallizer is a vessel or apparatus used for crystallizing substances, especially from solutions.
The solution was poured into the crystallizer to allow the crystals to form.  将溶液倒入结晶器中以形成晶体。
Crystallizers are essential equipment in the sugar refining process.  结晶器是糖精炼过程中必不可少的设备。
crystallization tanks, crystallizing vessels
dissolver


Etymology:
From crystallize + -er (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Greek *krustallos "ice," later "crystal," from PIE *kreus- "hard, cold."
