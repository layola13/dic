creamer:/ˈkriːmər/ (US) /ˈkriːmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|奶精，奶油罐|A liquid or powder added to coffee or tea to make it whiter and richer.| I take my coffee with creamer. 我喝咖啡要加奶精。 |Synonyms: coffee whitener, non-dairy creamer|Antonyms: black coffee (as a drink, not a literal opposite)|noun

creamer:/ˈkriːmər/ (US) /ˈkriːmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|奶油罐|A small jug or pitcher for serving cream.| Could you pass the creamer, please? 请把奶油罐递给我好吗？ |Synonyms: cream pitcher, creamer jug|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "sugar bowl" as a contrasting item on a table)|noun
