illuminance:/ɪˈluːmɪnəns/
n.
光照度，照度
The amount of light falling on a surface.
The illuminance on my desk is too low for me to read comfortably.  我的桌子上的光照度太低了，我看书不舒服。
illumination, light intensity, brightness
darkness, dimness
noun
