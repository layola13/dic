anabranch:/ˈænəˌbrɑːntʃ/ | n. | 支流；汊河 | A branch of a river that flows away from the main stream and rejoins it further down. | The Amazon River has numerous anabranches that create a complex network of waterways. 亚马逊河有许多支流，形成了复杂的水道网络。

The anabranch provided a shortcut for the boat. 这条支流为船提供了一条捷径。| 近义词: tributary, distributary, fork, offshoot | 反义词: main stream | 词性: 名词
