DPhil:/diːˈfɪl/
noun
哲学博士学位 (Doctor of Philosophy)
A DPhil is a doctoral research degree, the highest level of academic degree awarded by universities in certain countries, particularly the UK.  It is equivalent to a PhD.
She's studying for a DPhil in history at Oxford. 她正在牛津大学攻读历史学博士学位。
Synonym: PhD, doctorate
Antonym: N/A (There's no direct antonym for an academic degree)
词性: 名词
