opinionatedness:/əˈpɪnjəneɪtɪdnəs/| n. |固执己见，武断|The quality of being opinionated; adherence to one's own opinions, often in a stubborn or inflexible way.|His opinionatedness made it difficult to have a productive discussion. 他的固执己见使得很难进行富有成效的讨论。|近义词：dogmatism, inflexibility, stubbornness|反义词：open-mindedness, receptiveness|词性: 名词

Etymology: From opinionated + -ness.  "Opinionated" comes from "opinion" (from Old French opinion, from Latin opinionem, meaning "opinion, conjecture") + "-ated" (a suffix forming adjectives from nouns, indicating a state or condition).  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state, quality, or condition.
