aisled:/aɪld/
adjective
有通道的，有侧廊的
Having an aisle or aisles.  A long narrow space between rows of seats in an aircraft, cinema, or church.
The church was aisled and vaulted.  这个教堂有通道和拱顶。
近义词:galleried, arcaded
反义词: aisleless
词性: adjective
