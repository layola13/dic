intuitivism:/ɪnˈtjuːɪtɪvɪzəm/
n.
直觉主义；直觉论
The philosophical theory that primary truths and principles (especially those of ethics and metaphysics) are known directly by intuition.
例句：Intuitionism suggests that moral truths are self-evident and known directly through intuition. 直觉主义认为道德真理是不言自明的，可以直接通过直觉来认识。

例句：Some philosophers criticize intuitionism for lacking a clear method of verification. 一些哲学家批评直觉主义缺乏明确的验证方法。

近义词：instinctualism, non-cognitivism (in ethics)
反义词：empiricism, rationalism
词性：noun
