overemploying: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈplɔɪɪŋ/
| v. |过度雇佣；雇佣过多员工|The act of employing more people than necessary or than can be effectively utilized.|The company is overemploying and needs to downsize. 这家公司雇佣员工过多，需要裁员。|overstaffing, overmanning|underemploying, understaffing|verb
