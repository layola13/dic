adiantum:/ˌeɪdiˈæntəm/| n. |金 maidenhair fern; 铁线蕨|A genus of ferns, the maidenhair ferns, having delicate, fan-shaped fronds and wiry black or dark brown stalks.  |例句：The adiantum thrived in the humid environment of the greenhouse.  铁线蕨在温室潮湿的环境中茁壮成长。

例句：She admired the delicate fronds of the adiantum. 她欣赏铁线蕨纤细的叶子。|近义词：maidenhair fern|反义词:|noun
