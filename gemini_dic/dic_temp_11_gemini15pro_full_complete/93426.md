illuminable:/ɪˈluːmɪnəbl/
adjective
可照亮的，可阐明的
able to be illuminated.
The manuscript is written in illuminable ink, making it easy to read in low light.  这份手稿是用可照亮的墨水写的，在昏暗的光线下很容易阅读。
The dark corners of the room were no longer illuminable with the single candle. 房间的黑暗角落再也无法用一根蜡烛照亮了。
近义词:lightable, brightenable
反义词:dark, obscure
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Middle English *illuminable, from Late Latin illuminabilis, from Latin illuminare (“to illuminate”) + -abilis.
