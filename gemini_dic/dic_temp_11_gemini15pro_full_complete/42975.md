coves:/kəʊvz/ /koʊvz/| n. |小海湾；山谷；隐蔽处|A small sheltered bay, a valley, or a secluded place.|例句：The pirates hid their treasure in one of the many coves along the coast. 海盗把他们的宝藏藏在沿海的众多小海湾中的一个。

例句：We found a peaceful cove and spent the afternoon swimming. 我们找到了一个宁静的小海湾，在那里游泳度过了一个下午。|近义词：bays, inlets, harbors, valleys, shelters|反义词:plains, open areas|词性:名词
