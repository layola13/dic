fanzine:/ˈfænziːn/| n.|同人志；爱好者杂志|A non-official magazine or newspaper produced by fans of a particular cultural phenomenon (such as a musician, TV series, or literary genre), for other fans.|例句：He published a fanzine dedicated to his favorite band. 他出版了一本专门介绍他最喜欢的乐队的同人志。|例句：The science fiction fanzine featured short stories and artwork by amateur creators. 这本科幻迷杂志刊登了业余创作者的短篇小说和艺术作品。|近义词：magazine, journal, periodical|反义词:|noun
