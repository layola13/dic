hypermoralities:/ˌhaɪpərmoʊˈrælɪtiz/| n. | 超道德；极端道德；过度道德化|  A set of moral principles or beliefs that are considered excessively strict or rigid, often going beyond generally accepted norms.  These principles can sometimes be seen as impractical or even harmful in their application. |  Their hypermoralities prevented them from accepting any compromise. 他们的极端道德观使他们无法接受任何妥协。| synonyms:  extreme moralism, rigid morality, excessive scruples | antonyms:  moral laxity, amorality, immorality | noun
