overslavish:/ˈoʊvərˈsleɪvɪʃ/
adjective
过度奴性的；过于顺从的
Excessively submissive; too eager to please or obey.
He was criticized for his overslavish devotion to the company's president. 他因对公司总裁过度奴性的奉承而受到批评。
His overslavish adherence to the rules made him unpopular. 他对规则过于顺从，这让他不受欢迎。
synonyms: subservient, obsequious, fawning, sycophantic, servile
antonyms: independent, assertive, rebellious, defiant
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + slavish.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "slavish" refers to behaving like a slave, marked by abject obedience.
