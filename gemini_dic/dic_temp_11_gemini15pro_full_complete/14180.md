auto-immunity:/ˌɔːtəʊɪˈmjuːnɪti/ | n. |自身免疫性|The state in which the body's immune system attacks its own tissues.|Autoimmunity is the underlying cause of many diseases.| 近义词: hypersensitivity | 反义词: tolerance | noun

auto-immune:/ˌɔːtəʊɪˈmjuːn/ | adj. |自身免疫的|Relating to or caused by autoimmunity.|Rheumatoid arthritis is an autoimmune disease.|近义词: self-reactive | 反义词: non-autoimmune | adjective
