beefed:/biːft/
verb
增强；加强；充实
To make something stronger, more substantial, or more impressive.
They beefed up security after the robbery.  他们加强了抢劫后的安保。
The company beefed up its marketing campaign. 公司加强了它的营销活动。
近义词:strengthened, augmented, enhanced, improved
反义词:weakened, diminished, reduced
词性:v.

beefed:/biːft/
adjective
(非正式)肌肉发达的；强壮的
(Informal) Having well-developed muscles; strong.
He's got a beefed-up physique. 他身材魁梧。
Look at those beefed-up arms! 看看那些发达的胳膊！
近义词:muscular, strong, burly, brawny
反义词:weak, skinny, frail
词性:adj.

词源：
The verb "beef" originally meant "to complain" or "grumble."  The meaning "to strengthen" or "add substance to" emerged in the late 19th century, possibly influenced by the idea of adding beef to a meal to make it more substantial. The adjective meaning "muscular" is a more recent development.
