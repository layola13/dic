miniwarehouses:/ˈmɪniˈwɛərˌhaʊzɪz/
noun
迷你仓库，小型仓库
A storage facility offering small, individual units for rent.
Many small businesses use miniwarehouses to store excess inventory. 许多小企业使用迷你仓库来储存过剩的库存。
synonyms: self-storage, storage units
antonyms: warehouse (could be considered a partial antonym as it implies larger scale)
n.
