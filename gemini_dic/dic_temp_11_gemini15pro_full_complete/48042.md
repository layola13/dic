deckest:/ˈdɛkɪst/
adjective
最华丽的，装饰最华丽的
(archaic) Superlative form of "deck": most ornate, most adorned, most decorated.
The king's deckest chambers were reserved for special guests. 国王装饰最华丽的房间是留给特殊客人的。
Near synonyms: grandest, most elaborate, fanciest
Near antonyms: plainest, simplest, most austere
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Middle English *dekken, from Old Norse *dekka ("to cover"), from Proto-Germanic *þakjaną ("to thatch, cover").  The superlative ending "-est" is a standard Old English suffix.
