habitue:/həˈbɪtʃueɪ/ (US), /həˈbɪtjuːeɪ/ (UK)
n.
常客；老主顾
a person who regularly goes to a particular place
He is a habitue of the local cafe. 他是当地咖啡馆的常客。
regular, frequenter, patron
newcomer, stranger, outsider
noun
