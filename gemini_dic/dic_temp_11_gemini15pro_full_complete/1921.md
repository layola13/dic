actino:/ækˈtiːnoʊ/ /ækˈtaɪnoʊ/
prefix
放射性的，星形的
Relating to radial or ray-like structures, or to actinism (the chemical effects of radiation).
Actinolite is a bright green mineral.  阳起石是一种亮绿色的矿物。
The actinomorphic flower has radial symmetry.  辐射对称的花具有放射状对称性。
Synonym: radial, ray-like
Antonym: non-radial
part of speech: prefix
