bleatingly:/ˈbliːtɪŋli/
adverb|以咩咩叫的方式; 平淡无奇地; 哀诉地|in a bleating manner; plaintively; complainingly|The sheep bleated bleatingly as the farmer approached.|近义词: plaintively, whiningly, pathetically|反义词: assertively, confidently|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From bleat (v.) + -ing + -ly.  Bleat comes from Old English *blǣtan, of imitative origin.
