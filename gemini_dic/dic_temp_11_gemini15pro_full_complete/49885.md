demure:/dɪˈmjʊər/ /dɪˈmʊr/| adj.|端庄的，娴静的，严肃的|Reserved, modest, and shy in appearance or behavior; affectedly or coyly decorous.|例句：She gave him a demure smile. 她给了他一个端庄的微笑。|例句：Her demure manner attracted much attention. 她娴静的举止吸引了很多关注。|近义词：modest, reserved, shy, bashful, coy|反义词：bold, immodest, forward, brazen|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From Old French *demourer "to linger, stay, dwell," from Latin demorari "to delay, linger," from de- "down" + morari "to delay." The original sense was "grave, serious," later evolving to "modest, reserved."
