jam's:/dʒæmz/
noun
果酱(通常指复数形式，表示多种果酱)；拥挤；困境
Belonging to or associated with jam.  It can refer to the possessive form of "jam" (as in a type of fruit preserve) or less commonly, a shortened, informal form of "James."
例句：This is my favorite jam's flavor. 这是我最喜欢的果酱口味。

例句：I'm going to the farmer's market to buy some homemade jams. 我要去农贸市场买一些自制果酱。

近义词：preserves, marmalade (for citrus fruits), conserve
反义词: (No direct antonym for the fruit preserve meaning.  For the "crowded" meaning, the antonym would be "empty" or "spacious")
