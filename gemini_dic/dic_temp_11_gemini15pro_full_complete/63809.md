epidermatoid:/ˌɛpɪdɜːrˈmeɪtɔɪd/| adj.|表皮样的，类表皮的|Resembling or originating from the epidermis (the outer layer of skin).  Formed of or resembling epidermal cells, but not necessarily arising from the true epidermis.|The doctor identified the cyst as epidermatoid. 医生将囊肿鉴定为表皮样囊肿。|Similar:epithelial, squamous|Opposite: None|adjective

epidermatoid:/ˌɛpɪdɜːrˈmeɪtɔɪd/| n.|表皮样瘤|A benign cystic tumor consisting of keratinizing squamous epithelium, commonly found in the skin.|The patient had an epidermoid cyst, also known as an epidermatoid, removed from his scalp.  病人从头皮上切除了一個表皮样囊肿，也称为表皮样瘤。|Similar: epidermoid cyst, epidermal cyst|Opposite: None|noun
