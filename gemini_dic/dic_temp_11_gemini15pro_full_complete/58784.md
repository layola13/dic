dunno:/ˈdʌnəʊ/ (UK), /dəˈnoʊ/ (US) |  informal contraction | 不知道 | Contraction of "don't know".  It's a very informal way of saying "I don't know." |  "Where's John?" "Dunno."  “约翰在哪里？” “不知道。” | Synonyms:  I don't know, I haven't a clue, I'm not sure, beats me | Antonyms: I know, I'm certain | contraction


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I dunno what time it is. = 我不知道现在几点了。
He dunno where she lives. = 他不知道她住在哪里。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I dunno what he's doing. = 我不知道他在做什么。
She dunno what they're talking about. = 她不知道他们在说什么。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I dunno what has happened. = 我不知道发生了什么。
He dunno what she has done. = 他不知道她做了什么。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I dunno what they've been doing all day. = 我不知道他们一整天都在做什么。
She dunno what he's been working on. = 她不知道他一直在做什么。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I dunno what he said. = 我不知道他说了什么。
She dunno what happened yesterday. = 她不知道昨天发生了什么事。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I dunno what they were doing when I called. = 我打电话时我不知道他们在做什么。
She dunno what he was thinking about. = 她不知道他当时在想什么。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I dunno what had happened before I arrived. = 我不知道在我到达之前发生了什么事。
She dunno what they had discussed. = 她不知道他们之前讨论了什么。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I dunno what they had been doing before the accident. = 我不知道事故发生前他们一直在做什么。
She dunno what he had been working on. = 她不知道他之前一直在做什么。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I dunno what I'll do tomorrow. = 我不知道我明天要做什么。
He dunno what the weather will be like. = 他不知道天气会怎样。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I dunno what I'll be doing this time tomorrow. = 我不知道明天这个时候我会在做什么。
She dunno what they'll be discussing. = 她不知道他们会讨论什么。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I dunno what I'll have accomplished by next year. = 我不知道到明年我会完成什么。
He dunno what will have changed by then. = 他不知道到那时会有什么变化。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I dunno what I'll have been doing for ten years. = 我不知道到那时我将已经做这件事十年了。
She dunno what she'll have been studying. = 她不知道到那时她将已经学习什么专业多久了。


PHRASE->
"Dunno about that." =  对此表示怀疑。“对此我表示怀疑。”
"I dunno, maybe." =  表示不确定，也许。“我不知道，也许吧。”
"Just dunno." =  单纯表示不知道。“就是不知道。”
"Dunno what to do." =  不知道该怎么办。“不知道该怎么办。”
"Who dunno?" =  反问句，谁不知道呢？ “谁不知道呢？” (Rhetorical question, implying everyone knows)


注意事项:  "Dunno" is extremely informal and should be avoided in formal writing and professional settings.
