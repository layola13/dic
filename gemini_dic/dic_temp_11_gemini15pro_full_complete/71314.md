fizziest:/ˈfɪziɪst/ | adj. | 最冒泡的 | Most bubbly; containing the most bubbles.  | The fizziest soda I've ever had exploded all over me.  我最冒泡的汽水在我身上爆炸了。 | Synonyms: bubbliest, sparkling, effervescent | Antonyms: flat, still | Word Class: Adjective

Etymology: From *fizz* (verb, "to make a hissing or sputtering sound," from imitative origin) + *-y* (adjective suffix, forming adjectives from nouns) + *-est* (superlative suffix).
