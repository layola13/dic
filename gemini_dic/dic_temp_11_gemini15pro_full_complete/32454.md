chimneylike:/ˈtʃɪmniˌlaɪk/
adj.
像烟囱的; 类似烟囱的
Resembling a chimney, especially in being tall, narrow, and upright.
The factory has a chimneylike structure emitting smoke.  这家工厂有一个像烟囱一样的结构，不断排放烟雾。
The rock formation rose chimneylike from the desert floor. 这块岩层像烟囱一样从沙漠地面升起。
近义词: chimney-shaped, columnar, towering
反义词: low, flat, sprawling
词性: 形容词


词源:
From "chimney" + "-like". "Chimney" comes from Old French "cheminée" (fireplace), from Vulgar Latin *caminata (hearth, fireplace), from Latin "caminus" (furnace, forge). "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling," from Old English "-lic."
