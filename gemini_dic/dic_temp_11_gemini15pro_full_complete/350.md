abidance:/əˈbaɪdəns/ | n. | 遵守，持续，居留 |  The act of abiding by rules or laws; steadfast adherence.  |  His abidance by the rules was commendable. 他对规则的遵守值得称赞。 |  compliance, obedience, observance | violation, defiance | noun

abide:/əˈbaɪd/ | v. | 遵守，忍受，居留 |  Accept or act in accordance with (a rule, decision, or recommendation); (of a feeling or memory) continue. |  I said I would abide by their decision. 我说过我会遵守他们的决定。  She couldn't abide the thought of leaving him. 她无法忍受离开他的想法。 | tolerate, endure, obey | disobey, defy | verb
