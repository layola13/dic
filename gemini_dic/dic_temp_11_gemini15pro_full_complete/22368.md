booby:/ˈbuːbi/| n.|笨蛋；傻瓜；呆子；（尤指热带海鸟）鲣鸟|A booby is a person who is considered to be foolish or unintelligent.  It can also refer to a type of seabird, especially a gannet. |He made a real booby of himself at the party. 他在派对上出了个大洋相，让自己成了个傻瓜。|Synonyms: fool, idiot, dunce, nitwit, simpleton (for person); gannet (for bird)|Antonyms: genius, intellect, sage (for person)|noun

booby:/ˈbuːbi/| adj.|愚蠢的；傻的|Something that is booby is foolish or unintelligent.  This usage is less common than the noun form. |It was a booby mistake to leave the door unlocked. 没锁门真是个愚蠢的错误。|Synonyms: foolish, silly, stupid, unwise|Antonyms: wise, intelligent, sensible|adjective
