coruscating:/ˈkɔːrəskeɪtɪŋ/ /ˈkɔːrəˌskeɪtɪŋ/| adj.|闪耀的，闪烁的，才华横溢的|Flashing brilliantly; sparkling; glittering. Also used to describe someone's wit or intelligence as brilliantly and impressively displayed.|例句：The coruscating diamonds caught the light. 闪耀的钻石捕捉到了光线。|例句：Her coruscating wit charmed everyone at the party. 她闪耀的才智迷住了派对上的每一个人。|近义词：sparkling, glittering, shimmering, dazzling, brilliant, flashing|反义词:dull, lackluster, dim|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *coruscare* "to flash, glitter," related to *coruscus* "shaking, quivering, flashing."
