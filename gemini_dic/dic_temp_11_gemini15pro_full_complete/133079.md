nonequilibriums:/ˌnɒnˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəmz/ | n. | 非平衡状态 |  A state where a system is not in equilibrium, meaning it is dynamic and changing, often characterized by flows of energy or matter. | The researcher studies nonequilibrium systems in physics.  这位研究员研究物理学中的非平衡系统。| Synonyms: instability, disequilibrium, asymmetry | Antonyms: equilibrium, balance, stability | noun

nonequilibrium:/ˌnɒnˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəm/ | adj. | 非平衡的 | Not in a state of equilibrium; dynamic and changing. |  The ecosystem is in a nonequilibrium state due to constant environmental changes.  由于持续的环境变化，生态系统处于非平衡状态。 | Synonyms: unstable, dynamic | Antonyms: equilibrium, balanced, stable | adjective
