menobranchus:/məˈnoʊbræŋkəs/
n.
美西螈，泥螈
A large, permanently aquatic salamander (Necturus maculosus) of the eastern North America, having bushy external gills.
The menobranchus is a fascinating creature that retains its larval gills throughout its life.  美西螈是一种迷人的生物，它终生保留着幼虫的鳃。
Menobranchus are often studied by scientists due to their ability to regenerate lost limbs.  科学家们经常研究美西螈，因为它们有再生失去的肢体的能力。
