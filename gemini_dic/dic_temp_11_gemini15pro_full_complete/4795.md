algin:/ˈæl.dʒɪn/ | n. | 海藻酸;褐藻胶 | A gummy substance extracted from brown seaweed, used as a stabilizer and thickener in food, pharmaceuticals, and other products. | Algin is commonly used in the food industry to create gels and thicken sauces. 海藻酸通常用于食品工业中制造凝胶和增稠酱汁。| Algin is used in dental impressions to create a mold of the teeth. 海藻酸用于牙科印模，以创建牙齿的模具。 | synonyms: alginate, sodium alginate | antonyms: None | noun
