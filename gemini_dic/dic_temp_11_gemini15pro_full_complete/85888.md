headdresses:/ˈhɛdˌdrɛsɪz/
n.
头饰，头巾
A decorative covering worn on the head.
The Native American chief wore a magnificent headdress of eagle feathers.  这位美洲原住民酋长戴着华丽的鹰羽头饰。

The dancers wore elaborate headdresses adorned with jewels. 舞者们戴着饰有珠宝的精美头饰。

synonyms:  headgear, headpiece, tiara, crown
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly bare head/uncovered head)
noun
