overafflict:/ˌoʊvərəˈflɪkt/| vt. |过度折磨，使苦恼不堪|To afflict excessively or to an unusual degree.|The prolonged drought overafflicted the farmers, leaving them destitute. 长期干旱过度折磨了农民，使他们一贫如洗。

The constant noise overafflicted the residents of the apartment building.  持续不断的噪音让公寓楼的居民苦不堪言。|近义词：oppress, burden, overwhelm, harass|反义词：comfort, soothe, relieve|词性：动词
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + afflict (from Latin affligere, meaning "to strike down, weaken")
