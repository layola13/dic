asthenia: /æsˈθiːniə/ /æsˈθiːnjə/
n.
虚弱，无力
lack of strength; weakness
She suffered from asthenia after her surgery. 她手术后身体虚弱。
近义词：weakness, debility, fatigue, lassitude
反义词：strength, vigor, energy
词性：名词


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἀσθένεια (asthéneia, “weakness, feebleness, want of strength”),  from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + σθένος (sthénos, “strength”).
