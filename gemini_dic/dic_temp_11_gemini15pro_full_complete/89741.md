hopper:/ˈhɑːpər (US) /ˈhɒpə(r) (UK)| n.|料斗；漏斗；跳虫；单足跳者|A container shaped like a funnel, used for feeding grain or other material into a mill, furnace, etc.  A person or thing that hops.|The grain flowed down the hopper into the mill. 谷物沿着料斗流入磨坊。|近义词：funnel, chute, container|反义词: N/A|noun
