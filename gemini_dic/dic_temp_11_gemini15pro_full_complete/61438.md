embosom:/ɪmˈbʊzəm/ /ɛmˈbʊzəm/ | vt. | 拥抱；怀抱；隐藏；遮蔽；包围 | To enclose, cherish, or shelter, as in the bosom; to include.  | The village was embosomed in trees. 这个村庄被树木环绕。 | 近义词：enclose, cherish, shelter, embrace, envelop | 反义词：expose, reveal | 词性：动词

embosom:/ɪmˈbʊzəm/ /ɛmˈbʊzəm/ | vt. |  包含，包括 | To contain or include. | The report embosoms several important recommendations.  这份报告包含几项重要建议。 | 近义词：include, contain, incorporate | 反义词：exclude | 词性：动词
