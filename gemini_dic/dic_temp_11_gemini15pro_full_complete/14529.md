averaging:/ˈævərɪdʒɪŋ/
n.|平均;平均数;求平均值|the act of calculating an average|The averaging of grades is a common practice.|成绩平均是很常见的做法。|mean, median, norm|deviation, extreme
v.|平均为;计算…的平均数|to calculate the average of|My income averages $50,000 a year.|我的年收入平均为5万美元。|calculate the mean, compute the average|skew, distort
adj.|平均的|constituting or based on an average|The averaging period for rainfall is one year.|降雨量的平均周期是一年。|mean, median, normal|extreme, atypical
词性: Noun, Verb, Adjective
词源: From the Old French *avarie*, meaning "damage to ship or cargo," later influenced by Italian *avaria*, and ultimately from Arabic *'awār*, meaning "defect" or "damage."  The meaning shifted from calculating losses in shipping to calculating general averages.
