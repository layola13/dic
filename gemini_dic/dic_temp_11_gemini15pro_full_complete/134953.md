nonnarrations:/ˌnɒnˌnəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 非叙述部分；非叙事性内容 |  Sections of a film or performance that don't advance the plot through traditional storytelling, such as musical numbers or abstract sequences. | The film included several nonnarrations, including a dream sequence and a montage of cityscapes.  这部电影包含几个非叙事部分，包括一个梦境序列和城市景观的蒙太奇。 | synonyms:  non-diegetic elements, interludes, digressions | antonyms: narrations, storytelling, plot | noun
