cut no ice:/kʌt noʊ aɪs/
|idiomatic expression|
|没有影响；不起作用；不令人信服|
|To "cut no ice" means to have no effect or influence on someone or something; to fail to impress or persuade.|
|His apologies cut no ice with her. 他的道歉对她没有丝毫作用。|
|make no difference, carry no weight, fall flat, be ineffective|
|impress, persuade, influence, sway|
