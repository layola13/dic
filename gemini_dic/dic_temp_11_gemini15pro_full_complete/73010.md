footband:/ˈfʊtbænd/| n.|脚带，绑腿|A band worn around the foot or ankle.  It can be used for decoration, support, or to attach something to the foot. |The dancer wore a decorative footband.  舞者戴着装饰性的脚带。|Synonyms: anklet, strap, band|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps "unbound")|Noun
