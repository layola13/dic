extrudable:/ɪkˈstruːdəbl/
adjective
可挤出的; 可压出的; 可挤压成形的
Capable of being extruded; able to be shaped by forcing through a die.
The plastic is extrudable at high temperatures.  这种塑料在高温下可以挤压成型。
The mixture wasn't extrudable, it was too thick.  这种混合物不能挤压，它太稠了。
近义词:moldable, shapeable, ductile
反义词:rigid, inflexible
词性:adjective


extrudability:/ɪkˌstruːdəˈbɪləti/
noun
可挤压性; 可压出性
The property of a material that allows it to be extruded.
The extrudability of the clay is crucial for making pottery.  黏土的可挤压性对于制作陶器至关重要。
The high extrudability of the material makes it ideal for 3D printing.  这种材料的高可挤压性使其成为3D打印的理想选择。
近义词:malleability, plasticity
反义词:rigidity, stiffness
词性:noun


词源:
From extrude + -able.  Extrude comes from Latin extrudere, from ex- 'out' + trudere 'to thrust'.
