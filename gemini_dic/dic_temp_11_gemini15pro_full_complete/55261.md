dissing:/ˈdɪsɪŋ/| v.| 贬低，诋毁，说…的坏话 |To speak disrespectfully about; criticize or disparage. | He was dissing his former friend's new music video. 他在贬低他以前朋友的新音乐录影带。

He got dissed by the critics for his poor performance. 他因糟糕的表演而受到评论家的批评。| 近义词: criticizing, disparaging, slamming, bad-mouthing, trash-talking | 反义词: praising, complimenting, flattering | 词性: 动名词 (gerund) -  'dissing' is the present participle form of the verb 'diss', acting as a noun. It represents the act of dissing.
