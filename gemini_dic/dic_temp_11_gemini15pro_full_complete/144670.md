outwriting:/ˈaʊtraɪtɪŋ/
verb
超越写作；以写作胜过
The act of writing better than someone else, especially a rival.
He believes he can outwrite any of his contemporaries. 他相信他能比任何同时代的作家写得都好。
surpass in writing, excel in writing
be outwritten by
