anticonvulsant:/ˌæntikənˈvʌlsənt/ | n. adj. | 抗惊厥药，抗惊厥的 | A drug used to prevent or reduce the severity of epileptic or other convulsive seizures.  |  She was prescribed an anticonvulsant after her seizure. 她癫痫发作后被开了抗惊厥药。 | Synonyms: antiepileptic, antiseizure | Antonyms: convulsant, proconvulsant | noun, adjective

anticonvulsant:/ˌæntikənˈvʌlsənt/ | adj. | 抗惊厥的 | Having the effect of preventing or reducing the severity of epileptic or other convulsive seizures. | The anticonvulsant properties of the drug helped to control her seizures. 这种药物的抗惊厥特性帮助控制了她的癫痫发作。 | Synonyms: antiepileptic, antiseizure | Antonyms: convulsant, proconvulsant | adjective
