amotion:/əˈmoʊʃən/| n.|去除，移除; (法律)财产转移|The amotion of the CEO surprised everyone. 首席执行官的解职让所有人感到惊讶。|Removal, dismissal, discharge|Reinstatement, appointment|noun
Etymology->
From Old French *amotion, from Latin *amotionem (nominative amotio), noun of action from past-participle stem of amovere  "to remove, take away," from a- "off, away from" + movere "to move."
