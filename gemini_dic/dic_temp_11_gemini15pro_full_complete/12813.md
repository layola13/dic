assistive:/əˈsɪstɪv/| adj.|辅助的，帮助的|Providing assistance or support.  Designed to help someone with a disability or difficulty.|例句：Assistive technology can greatly improve the lives of people with disabilities. 辅助技术可以极大地改善残疾人的生活。

例句：He uses assistive devices to help him walk. 他使用辅助装置来帮助他行走。


|近义词：helpful, supportive, aiding, auxiliary|反义词：hindering, obstructive|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From assist (v.) + -ive.  "Assist" comes from Latin "assistere" meaning "to stand by, attend, assist."
