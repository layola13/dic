nonascendancy:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndənsi/
noun
非优势地位；非支配地位
lack of ascendancy; lack of dominance or controlling influence.
The nonascendancy of the Green Party in this region is evident in their low voter turnout.  绿党在该地区的非优势地位从其低投票率可见一斑。
The treaty ensured the nonascendancy of any single nation. 该条约确保了任何单一国家都不会占据支配地位。
synonym:subordination, inferiority, subjection
antonym: dominance, supremacy, ascendancy
词性:名词
