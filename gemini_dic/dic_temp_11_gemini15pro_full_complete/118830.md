metapeptone:/ˌmɛtəˈpɛptoʊn/ | n. |  胨化蛋白胨, 部分水解的蛋白质 | A partially hydrolyzed protein formed during digestion or prepared commercially for use in nutrient media for microorganisms. |  The scientist added metapeptone to the agar to provide nutrients for the bacteria. 科学家在琼脂中添加了胨化蛋白胨，为细菌提供营养。| synonym: peptone, tryptone | antonym: protein (in its complete, unhydrolyzed form) | noun
