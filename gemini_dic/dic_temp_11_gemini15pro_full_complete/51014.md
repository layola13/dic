deskill:/diːˈskɪl/
verb
降低技能；使技术性降低
To reduce the level of skill needed to do a job, especially by introducing technology.
Factory automation has deskilled many traditional manufacturing jobs. 工厂自动化降低了许多传统制造业工作的技能要求。

Many workers are worried about being deskilled by new technology. 许多工人担心新技术会降低他们的技能。

近义词: downgrade, simplify, automate
反义词: upskill, enhance, train
词性: verb
