burblier:/ˈbɜːrbliər/
adjective
更加起泡的，更加活跃的
More bubbly; more effervescent.
The champagne became burblier as it was poured.  香槟倒出来后更加起泡了。
The atmosphere at the party was burblier after the band started playing. 乐队开始演奏后，派对的气氛更加活跃了。
近义词:fizzier, more effervescent, livelier
反义词:flatter, less lively, duller
词性:adjective

burbliest:/ˈbɜːrbliɪst/
adjective
最起泡的，最活跃的
Most bubbly; most effervescent.
This is the burbliest champagne I've ever tasted. 这是我尝过的起泡最多的香槟。
The crowd was burbliest just before the main act came on stage.  在主要演员上台之前，人群最为活跃。
近义词:fizziest, most effervescent, liveliest
反义词:flattest, least lively, dullest
词性:adjective


词源: From "bubbly" + "-er" (comparative suffix) or "-est" (superlative suffix). Ultimately from "bubble."
