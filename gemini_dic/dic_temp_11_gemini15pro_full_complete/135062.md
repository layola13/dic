nonnotational:/nɒnnoʊˈteɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非符号的，非记谱的 |  Not relating to or expressed in a system of notation, especially in music.  |  His compositions are often nonnotational, relying on improvisation and graphic scores. 他的作品通常是非记谱的，依靠即兴创作和图形乐谱。 | near synonyms: improvisational, extemporaneous, unnotated | antonyms: notated, scored, written | adjective
