onstead:/ˈɒnsted/ | adv. | 代替；反而 | instead means in place of or as an alternative to. |  I couldn't find any coffee, so I drank tea instead. 我找不到咖啡，所以我改喝茶了。| synonyms: rather, alternatively, in lieu of | antonyms: additionally, as well as | adverb
