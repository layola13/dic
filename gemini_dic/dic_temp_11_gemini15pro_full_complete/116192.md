mashs: /mæʃɪz/ | n. | 土豆泥；糊状物 | Mashes are typically made by boiling starchy vegetables until soft and then crushing them. 土豆泥通常是通过将淀粉类蔬菜煮软然后压碎制成的。 | Example 1:  The chef prepared creamy mashed potatoes as a side dish. 厨师准备了奶油土豆泥作为配菜。

Example 2:  He accidentally stepped in a mash of overripe berries. 他不小心踩到了一堆熟透的浆果糊里。| 近义词: puree, pulp | 反义词: chunks, pieces | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "mash," from Old English *masc or *meox, akin to Middle Dutch *mesch, "mesh." 最初指网状物，后引申为糊状物。
