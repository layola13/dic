The word "comessation" doesn't exist in standard English dictionaries. It's likely a misspelling of "compensation."  Therefore, the following analysis will be for "compensation."

compensation: /ˌkɒmpenˈseɪʃən/ (UK) /ˌkɑːmpenˈseɪʃən/ (US)
compensation: n.
compensation: 补偿；赔偿；赔偿金
compensation: Something, typically money, awarded to someone as a recompense for loss, injury, or suffering.
compensation:  The company offered compensation to the victims of the accident. 公司向事故受害者提供赔偿。
compensation:  He received compensation for his injuries. 他因伤获得了赔偿。
compensation:  recompense, restitution, indemnity, redress, damages
compensation: penalty, forfeiture
