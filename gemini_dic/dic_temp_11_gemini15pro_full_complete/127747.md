negotiatrix: /nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪtrɪks/ /nɪˈɡəʊʃiːeɪtrɪks/
n.
女谈判者；女交涉者

例句：The skilled negotiatrix secured a favorable deal for her client.  这位熟练的女谈判代表为她的客户争取到了一项有利的交易。

例句：The negotiatrix worked tirelessly to resolve the conflict.  这位女谈判代表不知疲倦地工作以解决冲突。

近义词：negotiator, mediator, broker, intermediary
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps opponent, adversary in a specific context)
词性：noun
