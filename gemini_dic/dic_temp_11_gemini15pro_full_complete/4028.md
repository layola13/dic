ahungered:/əˈhʌŋɡərd/
adjective
饥饿的；渴望的
feeling or showing hunger; intensely desirous
例句：The children, ahungered after their long journey, devoured the food. 孩子们在长途旅行后饥肠辘辘，狼吞虎咽地吃着食物。

例句：He was ahungered for knowledge and spent hours in the library. 他渴望知识，在图书馆里待了几个小时。
近义词：hungry, starving, ravenous, eager, yearning
反义词：satiated, full, satisfied, apathetic
词性: adjective
