impassibleness:/ɪmˌpæsɪblənəs/
n.
无动于衷，冷漠，不动声色
The state of being impassible; inability to feel or suffer.
His impassibleness made him seem uncaring. 他的无动于衷让他看起来漠不关心。
近义词：impassivity, apathy, indifference, insensibility
反义词：sensitivity, emotionality, responsiveness


impassive:/ɪmˈpæsɪv/
adj.
无动于衷的，冷漠的，不动声色的
Not feeling or showing emotion.
He remained impassive throughout the trial.  在整个审判过程中，他都面无表情。
近义词：expressionless, emotionless, unmoved, stoic
反义词：emotional, expressive, passionate
