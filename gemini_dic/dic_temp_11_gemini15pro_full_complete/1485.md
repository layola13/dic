acetabulifera:/əˌsɛtəˌbjuːlɪˈfɛrə/
noun
髋臼动物
Animals possessing acetabula (hip sockets).
The acetabulifera include dinosaurs and birds. 髋臼动物包括恐龙和鸟类。
Synonyms:  acetabularian (less common)
Antonyms:  N/A (no direct opposite)
