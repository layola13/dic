ludicrous:/ˈluːdɪkrəs/| adj.|滑稽的，荒唐的，可笑的|So foolish, unreasonable, or out of place as to be amusing; ridiculous.|His suggestion was so ludicrous that everyone burst out laughing. 他的建议太荒谬了，大家都笑了出来。|近义词：absurd, ridiculous, preposterous, farcical, comical|反义词：reasonable, sensible, logical, serious|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *lūdicrus*, from *lūdus* ("play, game, sport").
