注意事项:
"Cornin'" is very informal and primarily used in spoken English, particularly in certain dialects.  It's generally best avoided in formal writing.  While grammatical examples can be constructed for various tenses, its actual usage is predominantly limited to simpler tenses in everyday conversation.
