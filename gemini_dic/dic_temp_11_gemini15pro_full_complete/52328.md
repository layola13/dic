dicotyledon:/ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ /ˌdaɪˌkɑːtɪˈliːdən/| n.|双子叶植物|A flowering plant with two embryonic seed leaves or cotyledons.  Characterized by net-veined leaves, a ring of vascular bundles in the stem, and flower parts in multiples of four or five.|Example 1: Roses are dicotyledons, displaying the characteristic net-like veins in their leaves. 玫瑰是双子叶植物，它们的叶子展现出典型的网状脉络。

Example 2: The bean seed, with its two distinct halves, is a classic example of a dicotyledon. 豆类种子，有两个明显的瓣，是双子叶植物的典型例子。|近义词：dicot|反义词: monocotyledon (monocot)|词性:noun
