disciplinableness:/ˈdɪsəplɪnəbəl.nəs/
n.
可训练性；纪律性；可管教性
The quality of being able to be disciplined or trained.
Her disciplinableness made her an ideal student for the rigorous program.  她的可训练性使她成为这个严格项目的理想学生。
The dog's disciplinableness allowed him to learn many tricks. 这只狗的可训练性使它学会了很多技巧。
synonyms: trainability, docility, manageability, obedience
antonyms: unruliness, wilfulness, intractability, disobedience
noun
