cockloft:/ˈkɒklɒft/ (UK) /ˈkɑːklɔːft/ (US)| n. |阁楼；顶楼；屋顶下的小房间|A small, often unfinished room or space below the roof of a building, typically used for storage.| The old letters were discovered in the cockloft above the garage.  旧信是在车库上方的阁楼里发现的。|Synonyms: attic, garret, loft|Antonyms: basement, cellar|noun

cockloft:/ˈkɒklɒft/ (UK) /ˈkɑːklɔːft/ (US)| n. |(尤指鸡舍的)顶楼，最高层|The highest loft or story of a building, especially of a barn or stable.| The chickens roosted in the cockloft of the barn.  鸡在谷仓的顶楼栖息。|Synonyms: hayloft, top floor|Antonyms: ground floor, basement|noun
