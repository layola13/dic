antiadministration:/ˌæntiədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/
| adj. | 反政府的；反对现任政府的 | Opposed to the current government or its policies. | The senator's antiadministration stance cost him the election.  这位参议员的反政府立场让他输掉了选举。 | Synonyms:  dissenting, oppositional, rebellious, insubordinate, defiant | Antonyms: pro-government, supportive, loyalist | adjective


antiadministration:/ˌæntiədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/
| n. | 反对派；反政府活动 | Opposition to the current government or its policies. | The growing antiadministration within the party threatened to split it.  党内日益增长的反政府情绪有可能导致党的分裂。| Synonyms: dissent, opposition, rebellion, insubordination, defiance | Antonyms: support, loyalty | noun


Etymology:
anti- (prefix) "against, opposite" (from Greek) + administration (noun) from Late Middle English: from Old French administracion, from Latin administratio(n-), from administrare ‘manage, direct’, from ad- ‘to’ + ministrare ‘serve’.
