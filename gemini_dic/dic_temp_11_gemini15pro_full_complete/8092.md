anthropophagical:/ˌænθrəpəˈfædʒɪkl/
adjective
食人的，同类相食的
Relating to anthropophagy, the practice of eating human flesh.
The tribe's anthropophagical rituals horrified the explorers.  这个部落的食人仪式吓坏了探险者们。

The evidence suggested anthropophagical practices in the ancient civilization.  证据表明古代文明中存在食人行为。

Synonym:cannibalistic
Antonym:herbivorous
词性:adjective
