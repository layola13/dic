forested:/ˈfɔːrɪstɪd/ /ˈfɑːrɪstɪd/| adj.|树木繁茂的；覆盖着森林的|Covered with forest; wooded.|例句：The area is densely forested.  这个地区森林茂密。|例句：We hiked through the forested mountains. 我们徒步穿越了森林覆盖的山脉。|近义词：wooded, timbered, sylvan|反义词：barren, deforested, treeless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From forest + -ed.  "Forest" ultimately comes from the Late Latin word "foresta," meaning "outside woods," referring to land reserved for hunting.
