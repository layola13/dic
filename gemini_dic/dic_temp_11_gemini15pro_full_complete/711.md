absented: /æbˈsɛntɪd/
|v.|缺席的，心不在焉的|being away from a place or not attending an event; showing a lack of attention to what is happening around you|He absented himself from the meeting. 他缺席了会议。 She had an absented look on her face. 她脸上带着心不在焉的表情。|近义词: missing, away, preoccupied, distracted|反义词: present, attentive|词性: 动词的过去分词，也可作形容词

absent: /ˈæbsənt/
|adj.|缺席的，不在场的|not present in a place, at an event, or in a group|He was absent from school today. 他今天没来上学。|近义词: missing, away|反义词: present|词性: 形容词

absent: /æbˈsɛnt/
|v.|缺席，不在场|to deliberately not go somewhere or to not be present at an event|He absented himself from the meeting. 他缺席了会议。|近义词: miss, skip|反义词: attend|词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin *absentare, frequentative of abesse “to be away,” from ab “away, from” + esse “to be.”
