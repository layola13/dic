journeyman:/ˈdʒɜːr.ni.mən/ | n. | 熟练工人，雇工，（尤指过了学徒阶段的）满师工匠 | A skilled worker who has successfully completed an apprenticeship but is not yet a master craftsman. | He worked as a journeyman electrician for several years before starting his own business.  他做了几年熟练电工后，自己开了公司。 | Synonyms: craftsman, artisan, tradesman, skilled worker | Antonyms: apprentice, novice, beginner, amateur | noun

journeyman:/ˈdʒɜːr.ni.mən/ | adj. | 平庸的，缺乏独创性的 |  Competent and skilled but lacking originality or inspiration. |  He gave a journeyman performance, nothing spectacular. 他表现平平，没什么出彩的地方。| Synonyms: average, mediocre, ordinary, commonplace | Antonyms: exceptional, outstanding, brilliant, extraordinary | adjective
