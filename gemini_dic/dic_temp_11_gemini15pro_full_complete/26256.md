button:/ˈbʌtn/ (US) /ˈbʌtən/ (UK)| n. v.|按钮，纽扣；扣纽扣|A small, usually round or oblong object that is pressed to operate a device or fasten clothing.|She pressed the button to call the elevator. 她按下按钮叫电梯。|近义词：switch, knob, fastener|反义词:unzip, unfasten|noun

button:/ˈbʌtn/ (US) /ˈbʌtən/ (UK)| v.|扣纽扣|To fasten something with buttons.|He buttoned his coat against the cold. 他扣上外套御寒。|近义词：fasten, secure|反义词:unbutton|verb
