forar:/foˈɾaɾ/ (Brazilian Portuguese) /fɔˈrar/ (European Portuguese)| v.|钻孔，穿孔，打洞|To make a hole in something, to pierce, to bore, to drill. |例句：Ele forou a parede para pendurar o quadro.  他为了挂画在墙上钻了个孔。

例句：A abelha forou a fruta. 蜜蜂把水果叮了个洞。


近义词：perfurar, furar, brocar|反义词: tapar, fechar, obstruir|词性:动词
