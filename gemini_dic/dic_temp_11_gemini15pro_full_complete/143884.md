outpopulate:/ˌaʊtˈpɒpjʊleɪt/ | v. | 超过…的人口 | to exceed in population; to have a larger population than another group or area |  The rabbits quickly outpopulated the foxes on the island. 兔子在这个岛上的人口数量迅速超过了狐狸。| synonyms: surpass, exceed, outnumber | antonyms: depopulate | verb
