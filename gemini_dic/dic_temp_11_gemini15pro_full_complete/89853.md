hornstone:/ˈhɔːrnˌstoʊn/| n.|燧石，角岩|A hard, dense, fine-grained type of quartz, often resembling flint and used for similar purposes.  A variety of chert.|Example 1:  The ancient people used hornstone to make tools. 古代人用燧石制作工具。

Example 2:  The geologist identified the rock as hornstone. 地质学家将这块岩石鉴定为角岩。|Synonym: chert, flint, silex|Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a type of rock)|Noun
