inconsistencies:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪstənsiz/
|n.|不一致，矛盾|lack of consistency or agreement; discrepancies|There are some inconsistencies in his statements. 他的陈述中有一些矛盾之处。|近义词：discrepancies, contradictions, conflicts, variances|反义词：consistencies, agreements, harmonies|词性:名词

inconsistency:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪstənsi/
|n.|不一致，矛盾|lack of consistency or agreement; discrepancy|There is an inconsistency between his words and actions. 他的言行不一致。|近义词：discrepancy, contradiction, conflict, variance|反义词：consistency, agreement, harmony|词性:名词

词源：
Late Middle English: from late Latin inconsistentia, from inconsistent- ‘not standing together’ (see inconsistent).
