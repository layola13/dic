gemmation:/dʒeˈmeɪʃən/
n.
出芽, 芽殖, 发芽
The formation of new organisms by budding or sprout development.
Hydras reproduce by gemmation. 水螅通过芽殖繁殖。

近义词:budding, sprouting, proliferation
反义词:fission, fragmentation

词性:noun

词源:From Late Latin *gemmātiōn-, *gemmātiō, from Latin gemmātus ‘having buds,’ from the verb gemmāre ‘to bud,’ from gemma ‘bud.’
