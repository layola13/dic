bushido:/ˈbuːʃiːdoʊ/ /ˈbʊʃiːdoʊ/
n.
武士道；武士道精神
The way of the warrior, referring to the samurai code of conduct, emphasizing loyalty, martial arts mastery, and honor unto death.
He lived by the tenets of bushido. 他遵循武士道精神生活。
近义词：chivalry, code, ethic
反义词: cowardice, dishonor
词性:noun
