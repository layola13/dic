expounding:/ɪkˈspaʊndɪŋ/

expound:/ɪkˈspaʊnd/
|v.|阐述，解释，详细说明|To explain or discuss something in detail.|He expounded his views on the subject at great length. 他详细阐述了他对这个主题的看法。|Synonyms: explain, interpret, elucidate, clarify|Antonyms: summarize, condense, obscure, confuse|Verb

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *expounen*, *expounden*, from Old French *espondre*, *expondre*, from Latin *expōnere* (“to set forth, explain”), from *ex- (“out”) + pōnere (“to put, place”)*.
