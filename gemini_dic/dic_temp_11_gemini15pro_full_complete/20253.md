birt:/bɜːrt/| n.|出生，诞生|An archaic word meaning birth.  It is rarely used in modern English.|He celebrated the birt of his son. 他庆祝了他儿子的出生。|近义词：birth, nativity|反义词:death, demise|名词

词源: From Middle English *birthe*, *berte*, from Old English *byrþ*, *gebyrd* (“birth, origin, descent, lineage”), from Proto-Germanic *birþiz* (“birth”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰer-*, *bʰr̥h₁tis* (“to bear”).
