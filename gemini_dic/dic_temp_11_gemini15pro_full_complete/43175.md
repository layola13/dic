cpra:/ˈsiːprə/| n. |加州隐私权法案 (California Consumer Privacy Act)|The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives consumers more control over the personal information that businesses collect about them.|例句：Businesses must comply with the CPRA.  企业必须遵守加州隐私权法案。

例句：The CPRA strengthens consumer privacy rights. 加州隐私权法案加强了消费者的隐私权。|近义词：data privacy law, consumer protection law|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) data vulnerability, data exploitation|词性:noun
