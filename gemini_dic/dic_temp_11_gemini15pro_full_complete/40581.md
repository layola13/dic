conurbation:/ˌkɒnɜːˈbeɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːnɜːrˈbeɪʃən/
|n.|
|大都市区，集合城市|
|A large urban area formed by the merging of several towns or cities.|
|The Tokyo conurbation is one of the largest in the world. | 东京大都市区是世界上最大的都市区之一。

|The rapid growth of the conurbation has put a strain on local resources. |  集合城市快速发展给当地资源带来了压力。

|Synonym:|metropolis, megalopolis, urban area, city region|
|Antonym:|rural area, countryside, backwater|
|noun|
