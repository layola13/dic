interwoven:/ˌɪntərˈwoʊvən/ (US) /ˌɪntəˈwəʊvən/ (UK)
adjective
交织的，交错的
Blended or combined closely, especially in a complex or subtle way.
The history of these two families is interwoven. 这两个家族的历史交织在一起。
近义词: interlaced, intertwined, intermingled, blended
反义词: separate, disentangled, distinct
词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *interweven, from Old English interweafan (“to interweave”), equivalent to inter- + weave.  Cognate with Dutch interweven, German interweben.
