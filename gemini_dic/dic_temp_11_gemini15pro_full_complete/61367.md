embillow:/ɪmˈbɪloʊ/
verb
(使)鼓起，(使)膨胀
to cause to swell or billow out
The wind embillowed the sails. 风鼓起了船帆。

例句：
The curtains embillowed in the breeze.  微风吹拂着窗帘鼓了起来。

The smoke embillowed from the chimney. 烟从烟囱里滚滚而出。

近义词：billow, swell, inflate, expand
反义词：deflate, shrink, contract
词性：v.
