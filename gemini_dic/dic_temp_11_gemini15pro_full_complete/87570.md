heterophemy:/ˈhɛtərəˌfiːmi/ |n.|异词同音词，用婉转说法代替粗俗说法|The use of a substituted word in place of another that is considered offensive or taboo.  It's a form of euphemism that specifically involves using a different word entirely, not just a gentler phrasing. |He used heterophemy to avoid saying the offensive word "damn," opting instead for "darn." 他用异词同音词“darn”来代替粗俗的词语“damn”。| Example: Using "heck" instead of "hell" is a common example of heterophemy.  用“heck”代替“hell”是异词同音词的一个常见例子。|Synonym: euphemism, circumlocution|Antonym: dysphemism|Noun
