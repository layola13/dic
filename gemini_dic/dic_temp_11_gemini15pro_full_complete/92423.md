hypomaniac:/ˌhaɪpəʊˈmeɪniæk/ | n. adj. |轻躁狂患者;轻度躁狂的|A person experiencing hypomania, a mood state characterized by persistent mild elevation of mood, increased activity and talkativeness, heightened self-confidence, decreased need for sleep, and sometimes irritability and distractibility.|He was diagnosed as hypomaniac after several weeks of erratic behavior. 他在几周的不稳定行为后被诊断为轻躁狂。|The hypomaniac energy propelled her through several all-nighters. 轻躁狂的能量驱使她熬了几个通宵。|近义词:manic, overexcited, elated|反义词:depressed, lethargic, apathetic|词性:名词, 形容词
词源: From Greek *hypo* (under, less than) + *mania* (madness, frenzy)
