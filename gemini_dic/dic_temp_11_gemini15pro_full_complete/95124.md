inappealable:/ˌɪnəˈpiːləbl/
adj.
不可上诉的
not subject to appeal; final.
The judge's decision is inappealable.  法官的判决是不可上诉的。
The Supreme Court's ruling on the matter is inappealable. 最高法院对此事的裁决是不可上诉的。
Synonyms: final, conclusive, definitive, binding, unchallengeable
Antonyms: appealable, reversible, challengeable
词性: 形容词
