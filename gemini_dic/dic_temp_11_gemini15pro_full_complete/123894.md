mordacious:/mɔːˈdeɪʃəs/ | adj. | 尖刻的；辛辣的；讽刺的 | Given to biting or sarcastic remarks; incisive; caustic. |  She delivered a mordacious critique of the play, leaving the playwright in tears. 她对这部戏剧进行了尖刻的批评，把剧作家弄哭了。

|  His mordacious wit was both feared and admired. 他那尖刻的机智既令人敬畏又令人钦佩。

| 近义词：caustic, biting, sarcastic, trenchant, acerbic, scathing | 反义词：kind, gentle, complimentary, flattering | 词性：形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *mordāx*, from mordēre "to bite."  Related to the word "morsel."
