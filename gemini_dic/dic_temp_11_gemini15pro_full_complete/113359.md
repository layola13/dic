例句：The movie portrayed the lycanthrope as a tormented creature.  这部电影将狼人描绘成一个饱受折磨的生物。|近义词：werewolf, shapeshifter|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *lycanthropus, from Ancient Greek λυκάνθρωπος (lukánthrōpos, “wolf-man”), from λύκος (lúkos, “wolf”) + ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos, “human being”).
