dewing:/ˈdjuːɪŋ/ | n. | 露水；结露 | The formation of dew. The dew settles on the grass in the early morning. |  The dewing on the spiderwebs made them sparkle in the sunlight. | 近义词: condensation | 反义词: evaporation | 词性: 名词

词源: From the verb "dew" + "-ing" (present participle suffix).  "Dew" comes from Old English "dēaw," from Proto-Germanic "*dawwaz," from Proto-Indo-European "*dheus-" (to flow, run, drip).
