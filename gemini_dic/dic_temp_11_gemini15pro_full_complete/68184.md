faintless:/ˈfeɪntləs/| adj.|无畏的，不屈不挠的，不昏厥的|Showing no sign of faintness; tireless; resolute.  Not liable to faint.|The faintless soldier continued fighting despite his injuries.  这位不屈不挠的士兵尽管受伤了，仍然继续战斗。|近义词：resolute, steadfast, unwavering, indefatigable, tireless|反义词：faint, weak, irresolute, wavering|词性:形容词
词源:  From faint + -less.  Faint itself comes from Old French *feint, past participle of feindre "to feign, pretend," from Latin fingere "to shape, form, invent, feign."  The suffix -less means "without."
