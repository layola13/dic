agrostological:/ˌæɡrəˈstɒlədʒɪkəl/
adjective
农业生态学的；草本学的
Relating to agrostology; the study of grasses.
Agrostological research is crucial for developing sustainable grazing practices.  农业生态学研究对于发展可持续放牧实践至关重要。
The botanist specialized in agrostological studies, focusing on the identification and classification of various grass species.  这位植物学家专门从事农业生态学研究，专注于各种草种的鉴定和分类。
近义词: graminological, gramineous
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *agrós* (field) + *stólos* (flock) + *-ikos* (relating to) + *-al* (forming adjectives).
