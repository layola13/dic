arcaneness:/ɑːrˈkeɪnnəs/ | n. | 神秘; 晦涩难懂 | The arcaneness of the ancient manuscript made it difficult to decipher.  这古老手稿的神秘性使它难以破译。 |例句：The arcaneness of the ritual added to its mystique.  这个仪式的晦涩难懂增加了它的神秘感。

arcane:/ɑːrˈkeɪn/ | adj. | 神秘的; 晦涩难懂的 |  Known only to a select few, the arcane knowledge was passed down through generations.  只有少数人知道的秘密知识代代相传。 | 例句：The arcane symbols on the wall remained a puzzle to archaeologists. 墙上的神秘符号对考古学家来说仍然是一个谜。

例句：She spoke of arcane matters that no one else understood. 她谈到了一些其他人都不懂的深奥事情。| 近义词：mysterious, esoteric, obscure | 反义词：common, known, familiar | 词性：形容词


词源：From Late Latin *arcanus, from Latin arcānus (“secret, hidden, enclosed”), from arca (“chest, box”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂erk- (“to enclose, confine”).
