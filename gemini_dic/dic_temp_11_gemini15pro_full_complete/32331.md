child-proofing: /ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːfɪŋ/
child-proofing: n.|儿童安全防护；防儿童措施|The act of making something safe for children, by removing or covering things that might be dangerous to them.|We spent the weekend child-proofing the house. 我们周末花了时间做房屋的儿童安全防护。|近义词: child safety, safety measures|反义词: endangering, hazarding
child-proofing: v.|进行儿童安全防护|To make something safe for children.|They've completely child-proofed their home. 他们已经完全对他们的家进行了儿童安全防护。|近义词: secure, protect|反义词: endanger, expose
