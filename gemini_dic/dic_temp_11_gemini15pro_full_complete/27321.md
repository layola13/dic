calyptra:/kəˈlɪptrə/| n. |帽状体；根冠|A hood or cap-like covering, especially in plants, such as the root cap or the hood covering the spore case of a moss.|The calyptra protects the growing tip of the root. 根冠保护根的生长尖端。|The moss's calyptra had fallen off, revealing the mature sporophyte. 苔藓的帽状体脱落了，露出了成熟的孢子体。|Synonyms: cap, hood, covering|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps exposed if referring to the protected part)|noun
