frenzying:/ˈfrɛnziɪŋ/ | adj. | 使狂乱的，使疯狂的 | Causing or marked by frenzy; driving to frenzy. | The frenzying pace of modern life can be overwhelming. 现代生活的疯狂节奏可能让人难以承受。|  The constant noise was frenzying the animals.  持续不断的噪音使动物们狂躁不安。 | 近义词: maddening, infuriating, frantic, frenetic | 反义词: calming, soothing, tranquilizing | 词性: adjective

frenzy:/ˈfrɛnzi/ | n. | 狂暴，狂乱 | A state of uncontrolled excitement or wild behavior. |  The shoppers were in a frenzy to grab the bargains. 购物者们疯狂地抢购便宜货。 | He was in a frenzy of activity, trying to finish the project on time. 他疯狂地忙碌着，试图按时完成项目。 | 近义词: madness, rage, fury, hysteria | 反义词: calmness, composure, tranquility | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English frenesie, from Old French frenesie, from Late Latin phrenesis, from Greek phrenēsis ‘inflammation of the brain’, from phrēn ‘mind’.

frenzying 主要用作形容词，描述某种事物使人或动物变得疯狂或狂暴的状态。虽然frenzy也可以用作动词，但frenzying更常见。
