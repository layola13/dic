cumbersome:/ˈkʌmbərsəm/| adj.|笨重的，累赘的，难处理的|Large and heavy or awkward to carry; difficult to use or deal with.|The application process was cumbersome and time-consuming. 申请流程繁琐且耗时。|近义词：awkward, unwieldy, bulky, clumsy|反义词：convenient, easy, manageable, simple|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the Middle English *comberous*, from *comber* "to trouble, to disturb," related to Old English *cumbrian* "to oppress, to confine."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kumbaz* "short."  The sense evolution likely went from "restricting movement" to "burdensome."
