insipidity:/ɪnsɪˈpɪdɪti/ | n. | 平淡无奇；乏味；清淡 | Lack of flavor, zest, or interest; dullness. | The insipidity of the movie made me fall asleep. 这部电影的平淡无奇让我睡着了。| Synonyms: blandness, dullness, vapidity, flatness | Antonyms: excitement, zest, piquancy, flavor | Noun


insipid:/ɪnˈsɪpɪd/ | adj. | 平淡无奇的；乏味的；清淡的 | Lacking flavor, zest, or interest; dull. | The soup was insipid and needed more salt. 这汤太淡了，需要加点盐。| Synonyms: bland, dull, vapid, flat | Antonyms: exciting, zesty, piquant, flavorful | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insipidus* "tasteless," from *in-* "not" + *sapidus* "tasty, savory," from *sapere* "to taste."
