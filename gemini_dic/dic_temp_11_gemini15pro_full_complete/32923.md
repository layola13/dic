choosinesses:/ˈtʃuːzinɪsɪz/

Etymology: From "choosy" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state) + "-es" (plural suffix).  "Choosy" comes from "choose" (Old English *cēosan) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).
