christened:/ˈkrɪs(ə)nd/ | v. | 为…施洗礼；命名；给…起绰号 | to give a name to something, especially at a ceremony such as a christening or baptism |  The ship was christened "The Mayflower."  这艘船被命名为“五月花号”。 | synonyms: name, baptize, dedicate, dub | antonyms:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 unname,  deconsecrate 在特定语境下) | verb

christened:/ˈkrɪs(ə)nd/ | adj. | 已受洗礼的；已命名的 | having been given a name, especially at a christening or baptism | The christened baby slept peacefully.  受洗后的婴儿睡得很安详。 | synonyms: named, baptized | antonyms: unnamed, unbaptized | adjective
