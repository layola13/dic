achromatin:/ˈeɪkroʊmətɪn/ | n. |非染色质；非染色质| The poorly staining, genetically inactive regions of a chromosome.  染色体中染色较浅、遗传上不活跃的区域。 | Example 1:  Achromatin is less condensed than heterochromatin. 非染色质比异染色质的浓缩程度低。

Example 2: The function of achromatin is still being researched. 非染色质的功能仍在研究中。 | Near Synonyms: euchromatin | Antonyms: heterochromatin | Noun
