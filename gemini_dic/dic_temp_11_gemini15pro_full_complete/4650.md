aleck: /ˈælɪk/ | n. | 聪明人;自作聪明的人;爱出风头的人 | A person, typically a man, who is regarded as clever, cocky, or showing off. |  He's such an aleck, always trying to impress everyone with his knowledge. 他真是个自作聪明的人，总是试图用他的知识给每个人留下深刻印象。

| Example: Don't be such an aleck!  别那么自作聪明！
| Example:  He acted like a real aleck, correcting everyone's grammar. 他表现得像个真正的聪明人，纠正每个人的语法。

| Synonym: smart aleck, wise guy, know-it-all, show-off
| Antonym: humble, modest, unassuming
| word type: noun
