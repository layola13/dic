nonsisters:/ˌnɒnˈsɪstəz/
n.
非姐妹
Individuals who are not sisters, either biologically or through adoption.
Mary and Jane are nonsisters; they are just close friends. 玛丽和简不是姐妹；她们只是亲密的朋友。
Near Synonyms: non-siblings, unrelated females
Antonyms: sisters, siblings
