counterblockading:/ˌkaʊntərblɒˈkeɪdɪŋ/ | adj. | 反封锁的 |  The act of blockading a port or coast that is itself already blockaded.  This usually involves breaking through the original blockade. | The British fleet, facing a counterblockading force, struggled to maintain its position.  英国舰队面对反封锁力量，难以维持其阵地。|  counterblockade (n.), blockade-running | blockading | adjective
