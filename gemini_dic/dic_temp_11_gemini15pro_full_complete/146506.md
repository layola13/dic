overgeneralization:/ˌoʊvərˌdʒɛnərələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度概括，过度泛化 | The process of extending a rule or observation to cases where it does not apply.  | Example: He made an overgeneralization about women based on his limited experience. 他根据有限的经验对女性做出了过度概括。 | Example: The child's overgeneralization of grammatical rules led to errors like "goed" instead of "went".  这个孩子对语法规则的过度概括导致了像用 "goed" 而不是 "went" 这样的错误。 | Synonyms: overextension, generalization error | Antonyms: specification, differentiation | noun

overgeneralize: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过度概括，过度泛化 | To draw a conclusion or make a statement about a larger group based on too few examples. | Example: It's dangerous to overgeneralize from a single incident. 从单一事件过度概括是危险的。 | Example: She overgeneralized about men after a bad dating experience.  在一次糟糕的约会经历后，她对男人过度概括了。 | Synonyms: overextend,  extrapolate incorrectly | Antonyms: specify, differentiate | verb
