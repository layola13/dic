overcriticizes: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
过度批评；吹毛求疵
To criticize excessively, finding fault with even minor details.
He overcriticizes his employees, which demotivates them. 他过度批评他的员工，这让他们很沮丧。
My teacher overcriticizes my essays; I can never seem to please her. 我的老师对我的文章吹毛求疵；我似乎永远无法让她满意。
Synonyms: nitpick, carp, cavil, faultfind
Antonyms: praise, commend, laud
词性: 动词
