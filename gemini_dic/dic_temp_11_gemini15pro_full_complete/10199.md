apostatically:/ˌæpəˈstætɪkli/ | adv. | 叛教地；背信地 | In a manner that involves abandoning one's religious beliefs or political principles. | He apostatically renounced his faith. 他叛教地放弃了他的信仰。 | Near Synonyms: traitorously, faithlessly, heretically, unfaithfully | Antonyms: faithfully, loyally, devoutly | Word Type: Adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *apostaticus, from Greek ἀποστατικός (apostatikos, “standing away from, falling away from”), from ἀπόστασις (apostasis, “desertion, revolt”), from ἀφίσταμαι (aphistamai, “I stand away from”), from ἀπό (apo, “away from”) and ἵστημι (histēmi, “I stand, I set”).
