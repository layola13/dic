nonaccessions:/ˌnɒnækˈseʃənz/
n.
未登录藏品；未入藏书籍；未加入的事物
Items not added to a collection, especially in a library or museum.
The report detailed the nonaccessions for the year, which included several valuable manuscripts.  该报告详细列出了本年度未入藏的物品，其中包括几份珍贵的手稿。
The museum's nonaccessions are stored in a separate facility.  博物馆未入藏的物品存放在一个单独的设施中。
Near Antonyms: accessions, acquisitions
