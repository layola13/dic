hammerkop:/ˈhæmərkɒp/ (UK), /ˈhæmərˌkɑːp/ (US)| n. |锤头鹳|A medium-sized wading bird found in Africa, Madagascar and Arabia, named for its hammer-shaped head.|The hammerkop builds a massive nest of sticks and mud. 锤头鹳用树枝和泥土筑造巨大的巢穴。|例句：The hammerkop uses its unusual bill to forage for food in shallow water. 锤头鹳用它奇特的喙在浅水中觅食。
