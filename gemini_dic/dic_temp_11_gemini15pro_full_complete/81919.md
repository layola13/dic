grilled:/ɡrɪld/| adj.|烤的；烧烤的|Cooked over direct heat, usually on a grill. |例句：We had grilled chicken for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了烤鸡。|例句：Grilled vegetables are a healthy side dish. 烤蔬菜是健康的配菜。|近义词：barbecued, broiled, roasted|反义词:boiled, fried, steamed|词性:形容词
词源:From the verb "grill," which comes from the Old French "griller" (to broil). Ultimately from the Late Latin "craticula" (small gridiron).
