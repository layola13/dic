oedematously: /ˌiːdɪˈmætəsli/ /ˌɛdɪˈmætəsli/
adjective
水肿地，肿胀地
Characterized by or exhibiting edema; swollen.  Marked by an excessive accumulation of watery fluid in cells, tissues, or serous cavities.
The patient's hand was oedematously swollen after the bee sting.  病人被蜜蜂蜇伤后，手肿胀了。
Her ankles were oedematously distended due to the long flight.  由于长时间的飞行，她的脚踝肿胀了。
Synonyms: swollen, puffy, bloated, distended
Antonyms: shrunken, deflated, contracted
Word Origin: From oedema + -ous + -ly.  Oedema comes from the Greek "oidēma" meaning "swelling".
