calmed:/kɑːmd/ | v. | 平静下来，镇定下来 |to make someone feel more relaxed and less emotional | The tea calmed her nerves.  这杯茶使她平静下来。| 近义词：soothed, pacified, tranquilized | 反义词：agitated, excited, riled | verb

Etymology:
From calm + -ed.  "Calm" originates from Middle French "calme," ultimately from Late Latin "cauma," meaning "heat of the midday sun," which led to the sense of stillness associated with that time of day.
