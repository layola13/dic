nontrust:/ˌnɑːnˈtrʌst/ | n. | 不信任，缺乏信任 | Absence or lack of trust; distrust. |  The nontrust between the two companies hindered the merger. 两家公司之间的不信任阻碍了合并。|  The lawyer's nontrust of his client was palpable. 律师对客户的不信任显而易见。 | 近义词: distrust, mistrust, suspicion | 反义词: trust, confidence, faith | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "trust" (from Old Norse "traust," meaning "confidence, support")
