deviltry:/ˈdɛvəltri/ | n. | 恶作剧；捣蛋；胡闹 | Mischievous or malicious behavior; playful tricks or pranks, often with a hint of wickedness. |  The children's deviltry resulted in a broken vase. 孩子们的恶作剧打碎了一个花瓶。

The mischievous imps were known for their deviltry. 这些淘气的小鬼以其恶作剧而闻名。


近义词: mischief, prank, shenanigans, naughtiness, roguery,  misconduct,  malice, wickedness | 反义词: obedience, goodness, virtue | 词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from devil + -ry.
