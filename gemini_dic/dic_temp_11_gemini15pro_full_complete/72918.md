fondant:/ˈfɑːndənt/ (US) /ˈfɒndənt/ (UK)| n. adj.| 翻糖，软糖；融化的，熔化的|A thick, creamy sugar paste used for icing cakes and pastries, or a type of soft candy.|The wedding cake was covered in a smooth, white fondant. 婚礼蛋糕上覆盖着一层光滑的白色翻糖。|近义词：icing, frosting|反义词: (As a noun, no direct opposite. As an adjective, perhaps "hardened" or "solid")|词性: noun, adjective
