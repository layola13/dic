amblyopic:/ˌæmbliˈɒpɪk/ | adj. |弱视的| Having amblyopia; relating to or characteristic of amblyopia, a condition in which the vision in one eye is reduced because the eye and brain are not working together properly. |  Example 1: The child's amblyopic eye was treated with an eye patch.  这个孩子的弱视眼用眼罩治疗。

Example 2: Early detection is crucial in treating amblyopic patients. 早期发现对于治疗弱视患者至关重要。 | Near Synonyms: weak-sighted, visually impaired | Antonyms:  keen-sighted, sharp-sighted | adjective
