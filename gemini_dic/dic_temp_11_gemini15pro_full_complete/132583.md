nondiffusible:/ˌnɒndɪˈfjuːzəbl/ | adj. | 不可扩散的；不能渗透的 | Not able to diffuse or spread out, especially through a membrane. |  Red blood cells are nondiffusible through the capillary walls under normal conditions.  红细胞在正常情况下不能透过毛细血管壁扩散。

The larger protein molecules are nondiffusible. 较大的蛋白质分子是不可扩散的。 | synonyms: impermeable, non-permeable | antonyms: diffusible, permeable | adjective
