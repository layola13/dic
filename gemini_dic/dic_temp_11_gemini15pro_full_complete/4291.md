airworthy:/ˈɛərwɜːrði/ (US), /ˈɛːwɜːði/ (UK)| adj.|适航的，飞行安全的|Fit or safe to fly.|The aircraft was declared airworthy after a thorough inspection.  这架飞机经过彻底检查后被宣布适航。| The pilot confirmed the helicopter was airworthy before takeoff.  飞行员在起飞前确认了直升机是适航的。|近义词：safe, flyable|反义词:unairworthy, unsafe|词性:形容词
Etymology: air + worthy.  From "air" (meaning the atmosphere) + "worthy" (meaning having merit or value, suitable for).
