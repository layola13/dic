flutterable:/ˈflʌtərəbl/| adj.|可飘动的，易飘动的|Something that is flutterable is light and airy enough to flutter in the wind. | The butterfly's wings were so delicate and flutterable. 蝴蝶的翅膀如此纤细，易于飘动。|The flags were flutterable in the gentle breeze. 旗帜在微风中飘扬。|近义词：diaphanous, gossamer, airy|反义词:heavy, dense|词性:形容词

flutter:/ˈflʌtər/| v. n.|飘动，振动；（鸟）拍翅；焦急不安|From Middle English *flotren, frequentative of  *floten (“to float”), from Old English flotian (“to float”), from Proto-Germanic *flotaną (“to float”).|The leaves fluttered in the breeze. 树叶在微风中飘动。|She felt a flutter of excitement. 她感到一阵兴奋。|近义词：wave, flap, tremble|反义词:still, steady|词性:动词,名词
