motherless:/ˈmʌðəlɪs/| adj.|没有母亲的，失去母亲的|Without a mother; orphaned.|The motherless child was taken in by his grandparents. 这个失去母亲的孩子被他的祖父母收养了。|Example:  She felt motherless after her mother passed away. 她母亲去世后，她感到自己像个没妈的孩子一样。|Synonyms: orphaned, bereaved|Antonyms: mothered|Word Class: Adjective
