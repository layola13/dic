overpower:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊər/| adj.(rare, literary) |压倒性的，不可抗拒的|Excessively intense or powerful.|The overpower fragrance made her head spin. 那股过于强烈的香味让她头晕目眩。|近义词： overwhelming, irresistible|反义词: weak, feeble|adjective
