comelily:/ˈkʌmlɪli/| adv.|优美地，合适地|In a comely manner; gracefully; becomingly.  She moved comelily across the stage.  她优雅地穿过舞台。|近义词：gracefully, elegantly, attractively|反义词:awkwardly, clumsily, ungainly|词性:副词
comely:/ˈkʌmli/| adj.|美丽的，漂亮的，合适的|Pleasant to look at; attractive.  A comely young woman.  一位美丽的年轻女子。|近义词：attractive, beautiful, pretty, handsome|反义词:unattractive, plain, homely|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *comeli, from Old English *cȳmlīc (“comely, beautiful, lovely, splendid, fine, choice, noble, honorable, fitting, suitable”), from Proto-Germanic *kōmlīkaz (“fitting, suitable”), equivalent to come +‎ -ly.  More at come.
