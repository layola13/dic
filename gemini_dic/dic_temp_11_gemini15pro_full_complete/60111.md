efface:/ɪˈfeɪs/ /əˈfeɪs/| vt.|擦掉，抹去；使…不明显|To remove (a mark, writing, etc.) from a surface. To make (oneself or one's actions) inconspicuous.|She tried to efface the memory of that terrible night. 她试图抹去那个可怕夜晚的记忆。|synonyms: erase, obliterate, expunge, wipe out|antonyms: emphasize, highlight|verb
