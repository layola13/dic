overinsistent:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪstənt/| adj.|过分坚持的，过于执着的|Persisting too much; insistent to an excessive degree.  Characterized by excessive insistence or persistence, especially to the point of annoyance.|He was overinsistent on getting his way, which alienated his colleagues. 他过分坚持己见，疏远了他的同事。|Synonyms: persistent, tenacious, pertinacious, insistent, importunate|Antonyms: yielding, compliant, acquiescent|adjective
