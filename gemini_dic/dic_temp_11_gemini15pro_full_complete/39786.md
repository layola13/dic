consonantize:/kənˈsɒnəntaɪz/ | v. | 使子音化；添加子音 | To make (a sound) more consonantal; to add a consonant or consonants to (a word or syllable). |  The sound /i/ is sometimes consonantized to /j/ before another vowel.  /i/这个音有时会在另一个元音前子音化为/j/。

The insertion of a /w/ consonantizes the vowel.  /w/ 的插入使元音子音化。| 近义词: harden | 反义词: vocalize | 词性: 动词
