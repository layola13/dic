flockless:/ˈflɑːkləs/
adjective
无羊群的，没有羊群的
Without a flock; not gathered or living in flocks.
The lonely shepherd felt utterly flockless after the wolves attacked. 孤独的牧羊在狼群袭击后感到完全没有羊群了。
The barn was eerily flockless after the farmer sold his sheep.  谷仓在农民卖掉他的羊后变得异常空旷。
solitary, alone, isolated
gregarious, social, flocked
adjective
