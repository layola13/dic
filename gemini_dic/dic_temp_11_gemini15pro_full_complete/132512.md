nondetentions:/ˌnɒndɪˈtɛnʃənz/ | n. |非拘留；不拘留（尤指学生）|  The policy of nondetentions ensures that all students can progress through the grades regardless of academic performance.  This refers to the practice of not holding someone, especially a student, in detention. | 例句：The school's policy of nondetentions has been controversial. 这所学校的非拘留政策一直备受争议。| 例句：Nondetentions can lead to a decline in academic standards. 非拘留会导致学业水平下降。 | 近义词：release, freedom, non-incarceration | 反义词：detention, confinement, imprisonment | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "detention" (from Old French "detencion," meaning "holding back")
