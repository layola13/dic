dichroic:/daɪˈkroʊɪk/ | adj. | 二色的，双色的 | Exhibiting dichroism; capable of exhibiting two colors, especially two different colors by transmitted and reflected light. | The gemstone exhibited a dichroic effect, appearing blue when viewed from one angle and green from another. 这颗宝石呈现出二色性效果，从一个角度看是蓝色，从另一个角度看是绿色。 |  Synonyms: dichromatic, two-colored | Antonyms: monochromatic, one-colored | adjective


dichroitic:/daɪˈkrɒtɪk/ | adj. | 双色的，二色的 (尤指脉搏) | Exhibiting two expansions in one heartbeat.  Relating to or exhibiting dichroism. | The patient's pulse was described as dichroitic. 病人的脉搏被描述为双色的。| Synonyms: dichromatic, two-colored, dichroic | Antonyms: monochromatic, one-colored | adjective
