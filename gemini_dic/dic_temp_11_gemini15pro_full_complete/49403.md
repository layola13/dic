deliverances:/dɪˈlɪvərənsɪz/
noun
解救，释放，解脱; 演说，讲话; 分娩
acts of delivering; rescues, releases, or sets free from danger or difficulty. Also, speeches or pronouncements.  It can also refer to the act of giving birth.
The climbers celebrated their deliverance from the blizzard. 登山者们庆祝他们从暴风雪中获救。
The preacher's deliverances were inspiring. 牧师的讲话很鼓舞人心。
synonyms: rescues, liberations, releases, salvations, emancipations, pronouncements, speeches, addresses
antonyms: imprisonments, confinements, captivities, enslavements
n.
