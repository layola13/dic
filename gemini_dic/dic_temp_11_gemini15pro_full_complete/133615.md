nonfluxes:/ˈnɒnˌflʌksɪz/ /ˈnɑːnˌflʌksɪz/ | n. | 非通量; 非流動 |Something that does not flow or change.  A state of stability or equilibrium where no significant changes occur.  Technically, the absence of flux, which is the rate of flow of energy or particles across a given surface.  | The nonfluxes in the system indicate a state of equilibrium. 系统中的非通量表明了一种平衡状态。 The researcher observed nonfluxes in the magnetic field. 研究人员观察到磁场中的非通量。|  stability, equilibrium, stagnation | flux, flow, change | noun
