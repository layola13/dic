crunchier:/ˈkrʌntʃiər/ (US), /ˈkrʌntʃɪər/ (UK)| adj.|更脆的|Comparative form of crunchy: more crunchy.  Describes something that makes a louder and more distinct crunching sound when bitten or crushed.| The new chips are crunchier than the old ones.  这些新薯片比旧的更脆。| The apples in the fridge are crunchier than the ones on the counter. 冰箱里的苹果比放在柜台上的更脆。 |crispier, firmer|softer, chewier|adjective
