ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *manyfold, *manifold, from Old English manigfeald (“manifold, various, complicated”), from Proto-Germanic *managfalþaz (“manifold”), equivalent to many +‎ -fold. Cognate with Dutch menigvuldig (“manifold”), German mannigfaltig (“manifold, diverse”).
