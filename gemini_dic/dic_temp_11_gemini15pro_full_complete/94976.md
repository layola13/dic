in-the-cards:/ɪn ðə kɑːrdz/
adjective
注定发生的；很可能的
likely to happen; predictable
It's in the cards that they'll get married eventually. 他们最终结婚是注定的。
It wasn't in the cards for us to win that game. 我们注定赢不了那场比赛。
Synonyms: destined, likely, probable, foreseeable
Antonyms: unlikely, improbable, doubtful
词性: 形容词
