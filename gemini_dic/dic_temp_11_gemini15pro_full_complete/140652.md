omar:/ˈoʊmɑːr/ (US), /ˈəʊmɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. |奥马尔（男子名）|A male given name, of Arabic origin, meaning "long-lived," "flourishing," or "populous."|Omar is a common name in many parts of the world. 奥马尔是世界许多地方的常见名字。|Omar Khayyám was a famous Persian poet.  奥马尔·海亚姆是一位著名的波斯诗人。|近义词: (No direct synonyms for names, but similar sounding names could be considered) Ammar, Umar|反义词: (Names do not have antonyms)|Noun
