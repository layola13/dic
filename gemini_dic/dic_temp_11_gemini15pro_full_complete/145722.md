overcredits:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdɪts/ | n. | 超额学分；多修的学分 | Excess academic credits earned beyond the minimum requirements for a degree or program. | Example:  She accumulated overcredits by taking summer courses. 她通过参加暑期课程积累了超额学分。

Example: He used his overcredits to graduate early. 他用多修的学分提前毕业了。 | Synonym: extra credits, surplus credits | Antonym: missing credits, lacking credits | noun
