associator:/əˈsoʊʃiˌeɪtər/ /əˈsəʊsieɪtə(r)/| n.|结合者，联想者，合伙人|A person or thing that associates or connects things together. |例句：The associator linked the two ideas in a novel way. 结合者以一种新颖的方式将这两个想法联系起来。

例句：He is an associator in the law firm. 他是这家律师事务所的合伙人。|近义词：partner, colleague, affiliate, connector |反义词: opponent, adversary|noun
