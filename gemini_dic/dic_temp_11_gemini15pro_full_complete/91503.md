hymnlike:/ˈhɪmlaɪk/
adjective
像赞美诗的；赞美诗似的
Resembling or characteristic of a hymn; suggesting a hymn.
The music had a hymnlike quality, solemn and reverent.  这音乐有一种赞美诗般的特质，庄严而虔诚。
The slow, stately melody sounded hymnlike.  缓慢而庄重的旋律听起来像赞美诗。
近义词:hymnal, devotional, reverent
反义词:irreverent, secular, profane
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From hymn +‎ -like.
