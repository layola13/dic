chorea:/kɔːˈriːə/ /kəˈriːə/ | n. | 舞蹈症；科雷亚病 | A neurological disorder characterized by jerky involuntary movements affecting especially the shoulders, hips, and face. |  Her chorea made it difficult for her to perform everyday tasks. 她的舞蹈症使她难以进行日常活动。

Huntington's disease is a progressive disorder that causes chorea and dementia.  亨廷顿舞蹈症是一种进行性疾病，会导致舞蹈症和痴呆。| 近义词：dyskinesia, athetosis | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a medical condition) | 词性: noun
