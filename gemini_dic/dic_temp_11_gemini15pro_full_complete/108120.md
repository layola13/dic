latifolious: /ˌlætɪˈfəʊliəs/ (British), /ˌlætɪˈfoʊliəs/ (American)
| adj. | 宽叶的；阔叶的 | Having broad leaves, as opposed to needles or scales.  |  例句：Latifolious trees are dominant in many temperate forests.  阔叶树在许多温带森林中占主导地位。 |  例句：The latifolious forest provided ample shade for the understory plants. 这片阔叶林为林下植物提供了充足的荫蔽。 | 近义词：broad-leaved, broadleaf | 反义词：coniferous, needle-leaved, acicular | 词性：adjective

词源: From Latin *latifolius*  'broad-leaved', from *latus* 'broad' + *folium* 'leaf'.
