overexpresses:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛsɪz/
verb
过度表达
causes a gene to be transcribed more often than normal, resulting in an increased amount of the gene product (RNA or protein).
The tumor overexpresses a particular growth factor receptor.  肿瘤过度表达一种特定的生长因子受体。
The engineered bacteria overexpresses the enzyme needed for biofuel production.  经过基因工程改造的细菌过度表达了生物燃料生产所需的酶。
synonyms: upregulates, amplifies
antonyms: downregulates, suppresses
v.
