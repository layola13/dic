interferingly:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərɪŋli/
adv.
干扰地，妨碍地
in a way that interferes or obstructs
The loud music was interferingly playing while I was trying to study.  喧闹的音乐在我试图学习时干扰着我。
Synonyms: obstructively, disruptively, hinderingly
Antonyms: helpfully, supportively, constructively
词性: 副词
