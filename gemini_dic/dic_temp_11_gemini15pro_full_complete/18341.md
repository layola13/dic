belligerently:/bəˈlɪdʒərəntli/
adverb
好战地，挑衅地
in a hostile and aggressive way
He stared belligerently at the other driver. 他好斗地盯着另一位司机。
belligerently:/bəˈlɪdʒərəntli/
adverb
好战地，挑衅地
acting in a hostile and aggressive way
"What do you want?" he demanded belligerently. “你想要什么？”他挑衅地问道。
近义词: aggressively, hostilely, combatively, antagonistically, pugnaciously
反义词: peacefully, amicably, calmly, passively, submissively
词性: adverb
