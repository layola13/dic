noncaking:/ˌnɒnˈkeɪkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈkeɪkɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|不结块的，防结块的|Preventing formation of lumps or cakes.  This term is often used to describe substances like powders or granules that are treated to remain free-flowing and prevent them from sticking together.|This flour is noncaking, so it's easy to measure. 这种面粉是不结块的，所以很容易测量。|Near synonyms: free-flowing, loose, powdery|Antonyms: caking, lumpy, clumpy|adjective
