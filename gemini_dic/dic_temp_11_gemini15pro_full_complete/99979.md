interownership:/ˌɪntərˈoʊnərʃɪp/
n.
相互拥有，互相拥有，交叉持股
The situation where two or more entities own shares in each other.
Example: Interownership between companies can create complex financial relationships.  公司之间的交叉持股会造成复杂的财务关系。
Example: The interownership of these two companies makes them less vulnerable to hostile takeovers. 这两家公司的交叉持股使它们更不容易受到恶意收购。
Synonym: cross-ownership, reciprocal ownership, joint ownership
Antonym: sole ownership, independent ownership
noun
