anti-plague:/ˌæntiˈpleɪɡ/
adjective
抗瘟疫的，防瘟疫的
Preventing or protecting against plague.
The anti-plague measures were put in place quickly. 抗疫措施迅速到位。
New anti-plague medications are being developed. 新的抗瘟疫药物正在研发中。
近义词:plague-resistant, anti-pestilential
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective

Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix) + "plague" (noun)

"anti-" comes from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of, instead of."

"plague" comes from Old French "plage" meaning "affliction, calamity, plague" ultimately from Latin "plaga" meaning "stroke, wound, plague, region."
