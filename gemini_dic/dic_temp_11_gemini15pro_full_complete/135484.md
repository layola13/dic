nonphilosophic:/ˌnɒnˌfɪləˈsɒfɪk/ | adj. | 非哲学的，不具哲学意味的 | Not philosophical; not dealing with or concerned with philosophy. | Her approach to the problem was entirely nonphilosophic, focusing on practical solutions rather than abstract theories. 她处理这个问题的方式完全是非哲学的，专注于实际的解决方案而不是抽象的理论。 | Example 2: The book was a nonphilosophic account of the war, concentrating on the daily lives of soldiers. 这本书是对战争的非哲学描述，集中讲述了士兵的日常生活。 | Synonyms: unphilosophical, pragmatic, practical | Antonyms: philosophical, abstract, theoretical | adjective
