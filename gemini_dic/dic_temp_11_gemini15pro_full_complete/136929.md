nonsovereigns:/ˌnɒnˈsɒvrɪnz/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɑːvrɪnz/
n.|非主权实体；非独立国家|Entities or states that lack full sovereignty and independence.|The conference brought together representatives from various nonsovereigns, including territories and dependencies.|近义词:dependencies, territories, protectorates|反义词:sovereign states, independent nations|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non*, "not"
sovereign: from Old French *soverain*, ultimately from Latin *superanus*, "supreme," from *super*, "above"
-s (suffix): plural marker
