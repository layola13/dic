fiendful:/ˈfiːndfəl/| adj.|极恶的；残忍的；凶恶的|Extremely evil or cruel; wicked.|The fiendful dictator ordered the execution of all political prisoners.  这个恶魔般的独裁者下令处决所有政治犯。|Example: The movie depicted the fiendful acts of a serial killer.  这部电影描绘了一个连环杀手的滔天罪行。|Synonyms:  diabolical, devilish, demonic, infernal, satanic, wicked, evil, atrocious, heinous|Antonyms: benevolent, kind, compassionate, merciful|Etymology: From "fiend" (meaning an evil spirit or a cruel person) + "-ful" (a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by").|Word Type: Adjective
