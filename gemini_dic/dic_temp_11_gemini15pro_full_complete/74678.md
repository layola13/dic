freeborn:/ˈfriːbɔːrn/
adjective
自由出生的；生而自由的
Born free, not a slave.  A person who is freeborn is not born into slavery or serfdom.
例句：He was a freeborn citizen of Rome. 他是罗马的自由民。

例句：The freeborn citizens enjoyed rights denied to slaves. 自由出生的公民享有奴隶被剥夺的权利。

近义词：independent, free, liberated
反义词：enslaved, captive, bond
词性：adjective
