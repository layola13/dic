not breathe a word:/nɒt briːð ə wɜːrd/ | idiom |  不透露一个字；守口如瓶 | to keep completely silent about something; not tell anyone about a secret | "I'm getting engaged, but please don't breathe a word to anyone yet." 我要订婚了，但请先不要告诉任何人。 | keep quiet, keep mum, stay silent, keep it under your hat | reveal, divulge, tell, let slip | idiom
