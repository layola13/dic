cenobitic:/sɪnəˈbɪtɪk/| adj.|修道院的，集体生活的|Of or relating to community living, especially in a monastery or convent; monastic.|The monks lived a cenobitic life, sharing meals and prayers in the monastery. 这些僧侣过着集体生活，在修道院里一起吃饭和祈祷。|近义词：monastic, cloistered, communal|反义词：eremitic, solitary, anchoritic|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *coenobitēs*, from Greek *koinobitēs* "living in a community,"  from *koinobios*, from *koinos* "common" + *bios* "life."
