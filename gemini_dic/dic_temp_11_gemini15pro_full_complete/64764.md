esculin:/ˈeskjuːlɪn/ | n. | 七叶苷; 七叶灵 | A coumarin glycoside found in the bark of the horse chestnut tree, used in medicine and as a fluorescent indicator. |  Esculin is used in microbiology to aid in the identification of certain bacteria. 七叶苷在微生物学中用于帮助鉴定某些细菌。

Esculin can be used to test for the presence of fecal coliforms in water.  七叶苷可以用来检测水中粪大肠菌群的存在。| 近义词:aesculin | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
