lutheranism:/ˈluːθərənɪzəm/
n.
路德教,信义宗
The major Protestant denomination adhering to the tenets of Martin Luther.
Lutheranism is a major branch of Protestant Christianity. 路德教是基督教新教的一个主要分支。
Martin Luther's teachings sparked the Protestant Reformation and led to the development of Lutheranism. 马丁·路德的教义引发了宗教改革，并导致了路德教的发展。
近义词:Protestantism
反义词:Catholicism
词性:noun
