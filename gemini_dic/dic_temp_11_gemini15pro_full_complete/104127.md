jumpy:/ˈdʒʌmpi/| adj.|神经质的，容易激动的，跳跃的|Nervous and easily startled; tending to make sudden, jerky movements.|例句：The loud noise made the horse jumpy.  巨大的噪音使马儿受到了惊吓。

例句：He felt jumpy before the exam. 他在考试前感到紧张不安。

近义词：nervous, anxious, jittery, restless, apprehensive, edgy, skittish, twitchy|反义词：calm, relaxed, composed, tranquil|词性：形容词

jumpy:/ˈdʒʌmpi/| n.|跳跃|A jump; a sudden, jerky movement.|例句：The cat made a jumpy onto the table.  猫猛地跳上了桌子。

例句：The film was full of jumpies and scares. 这部电影充满了跳跃和惊吓的场景。

近义词：leap, bound, hop, spring|反义词：-(没有明确的反义词)|词性：名词 (较少用)
