adj. | 非葡萄牙的；非葡萄牙人的
Not of or relating to Portugal, its people, or their language. |
例句：The restaurant offers a wide selection of non-Portuguese wines.  这家餐厅提供种类繁多的非葡萄牙葡萄酒。

例句：She speaks several languages, including a few non-Portuguese ones. 她会说几种语言，包括一些非葡萄牙语。

近义词：non-Lusitanian
反义词：Portuguese
词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non "not."
Portuguese (adjective): From Portugal, the country name. Ultimately derived from Latin Portus Cale "Port of Cale," an ancient Roman name for the area around present-day Porto.
