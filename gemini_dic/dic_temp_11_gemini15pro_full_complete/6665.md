anagram:/ˈænəɡræm/| n.|字谜，变形词|A word or phrase formed by rearranging the letters of another word or phrase.|例句： "Listen" is an anagram of "silent".  “listen”是“silent”的变形词。

例句：She challenged me to find the anagram of "dormitory". 她向我挑战找出“dormitory”的变形词。|近义词：transposition, permutation|反义词:|noun
