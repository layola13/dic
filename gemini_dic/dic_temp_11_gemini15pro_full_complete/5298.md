allotropical:/ˌæləˈtrɒpɪkəl/
adjective
同素异形体的
Relating to or exhibiting allotropy; existing in different forms, especially physical forms, while in the same state or phase (solid, liquid, or gas).
Diamond and graphite are allotropical forms of carbon. 钻石和石墨是碳的同素异形体。
近义词:polymorphic
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From allo- (variant of all- “different”) + -tropic (from Greek tropos "a turn, way, manner").
