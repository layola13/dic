anarchies:/ˈænərkiz/
noun
无政府状态;混乱
a state of disorder due to absence or nonrecognition of authority.
The collapse of the government resulted in total anarchies. 政府的垮台导致了彻底的无政府状态。
synonyms: lawlessness, disorder, chaos, nihilism, pandemonium
antonyms: order, government, control, law, stability
