equant:/ˈiːkwənt/| n.|均轮; 等距点|A point in the Ptolemaic system of astronomy, off-center from the Earth, around which the center of a planet's epicycle was thought to move uniformly.|Ptolemy introduced the equant to explain the observed non-uniform motion of the planets.|近义词: deferent |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "heliocentric center")|词性:noun
