nightest:/ˈnaɪtɪst/ | adj. (non-standard) | 最夜晚的，最黑的 |  This word is a non-standard superlative form of "night," attempting to express the idea of something being the most like night or the darkest. While grammatically incorrect, it might be used informally for emphasis.  It is not considered proper English. |  "It was the nightest night I'd ever seen." = 这是我见过的最黑的夜晚。 | Near Synonyms: darkest, blackest | Antonyms: brightest, lightest | Word Type: Adjective
