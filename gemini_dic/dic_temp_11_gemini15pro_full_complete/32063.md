cherub:/ˈtʃɛrəb/ /ˈtʃɜːrəb/| n.|小天使，胖乎乎可爱的小孩|A cherub is a type of angel, often depicted as a young child with wings, or a beautiful or innocent child.|The painting depicted a cherub playing a harp. 这幅画描绘了一个小天使在弹奏竖琴。|He slept soundly, looking like a little cherub. 他睡得很香，看起来像个小天使。|Synonyms: angel, seraph, putto|Antonyms: devil, demon|Noun

cherubim:/ˈtʃɛrəbɪm/ /ˈtʃɜːrəbɪm/| n.|小天使（cherub的复数）|Cherubim is the plural form of cherub, referring to multiple cherubs.|The ceiling was adorned with paintings of cherubim. 天花板上装饰着小天使的画。|Two cherubim flanked the altar. 两个小天使位于祭坛两侧。|Synonyms: angels, seraphs|Antonyms: devils, demons|Noun
