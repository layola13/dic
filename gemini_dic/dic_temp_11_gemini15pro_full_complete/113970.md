maggotish:/ˈmæɡəˌtɪʃ/| adj.|蛆虫似的；充满蛆虫的；令人作呕的|Resembling or infested with maggots; disgusting.|The dumpster behind the restaurant was maggotish and reeked.  餐厅后面的垃圾箱里满是蛆虫，散发着恶臭。|近义词：grub-ridden, wormy, revolting, disgusting, nauseating|反义词：clean, pristine, appetizing|词性:形容词

词源: From maggot + -ish.  来自 maggot（蛆）+ -ish（形容词后缀，表示“像…的”）。
