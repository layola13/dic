calisthenics:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪks/
|n.|健美操，徒手体操|Calisthenics are exercises done using only your body weight. 健美操是只使用你的体重进行的锻炼。|Calisthenics is a great way to improve strength and flexibility. 健美操是提高力量和柔韧性的好方法。|Synonyms: bodyweight training, exercises, workout|Antonyms: weight training|noun

calisthenical:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪkəl/
|adj.|健美操的，徒手体操的|Calisthenical exercises are beneficial for overall fitness. 健美操对整体健康有益。|She prefers a calisthenical workout to lifting weights. 她更喜欢徒手体操而不是举重。|Synonyms: gymnastic, athletic|Antonyms: sedentary|adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *kallos* "beauty" + *sthenos* "strength".
