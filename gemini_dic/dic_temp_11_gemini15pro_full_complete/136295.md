nonrepresentative:/ˌnɒnˌreprɪˈzɛntətɪv/ | adj. | 非代表性的；不典型的 | Not accurately reflecting the character or qualities of a larger group or entity. | This small group is nonrepresentative of the population as a whole. 这个小组不能代表全体人口。| synonyms: atypical, uncharacteristic, biased, skewed | antonyms: representative, typical, characteristic | adjective


nonrepresentatives:/ˌnɒnˌreprɪˈzɛntətɪvz/ | n. | 非代表性的人或物 | People or things that do not accurately reflect the character or qualities of a larger group or entity. | The study's findings were criticized because the nonrepresentatives in the sample skewed the results. 这项研究的结果受到批评，因为样本中的非代表性个体扭曲了结果。| synonyms: outliers, anomalies | antonyms: representatives, exemplars | noun
