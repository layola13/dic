overmellowness:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛloʊnəs/| n. |过度成熟；过分伤感；过分多愁善感|The state of being excessively mellow, often to the point of sentimentality or mawkishness.  It can refer to fruit that is past its prime, or to a person who is excessively sentimental or emotional.|The overmellowness of the peaches made them mushy and unappetizing.  桃子过熟，变得软烂不好吃。|近义词：sentimentality, mawkishness, mushiness|反义词：tartness, firmness, astringency|词性:noun
