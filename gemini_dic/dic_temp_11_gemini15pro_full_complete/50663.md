derivativenesses: /dɪˌrɪvətɪvnɪsɪz/ | n. |  衍生性，缺乏独创性 | The state or quality of being derivative; unoriginality.  Lack of originality; the quality of being derived from something else. |  The derivativenesses of the movie's plot were obvious to anyone who had seen the original. 这部电影情节的缺乏独创性对于任何看过原版电影的人来说都是显而易见的。 | synonyms: unoriginality,  banality, staleness, triteness,  commonplaceness | antonyms: originality,  novelty, freshness,  innovation, uniqueness | noun
