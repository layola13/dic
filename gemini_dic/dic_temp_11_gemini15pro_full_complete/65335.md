euhemeristic:/ˌjuːˈhɪmərɪstɪk/ | adj. |  释义论的，历史还原论的 | Interpreting mythical accounts as based on historical persons and events.  |  Many scholars consider the accounts of the Greek gods to be euhemeristic interpretations of real historical figures. 许多学者认为希腊诸神的记载是对真实历史人物的释义论解释。 | synonymous: reductionist, historical | antonymous: mythological, mythical | adjective
