bullfice:/ˈbʊlfɪs/| n. |吓唬，恐吓; 虚张声势|An act of bullying or intimidation; empty bluster. |He tried to bullfice his way into the exclusive club, but the bouncer saw through his pretense. 他试图虚张声势地进入这家高级俱乐部，但保安识破了他的伪装。|近义词：intimidation, bluster, bullying, menace, threat|反义词: appeasement, conciliation, diplomacy|词性:名词

bullfice:/ˈbʊlfɪs/| v. |吓唬，恐吓; 虚张声势|To bully or intimidate someone; to engage in empty bluster. |The manager tried to bullfice his employees into working overtime without pay. 经理试图恐吓他的员工加班不给工资。|近义词：intimidate, bluster, bully, menace, threaten|反义词: appease, conciliate|词性:动词
