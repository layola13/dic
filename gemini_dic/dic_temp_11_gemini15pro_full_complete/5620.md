alternatively:/ɔːlˈtɜːrnətɪvli/ | adv. | 或者，要不然 |  Used to suggest another possibility or choice. | You could drive, or alternatively, you could take the train. 你可以开车，或者，你可以坐火车。|  You could fly directly to Paris, alternatively, you could make a stopover in London. 你可以直接飞往巴黎，或者你可以在伦敦中途停留。| Synonyms: otherwise, instead, or else | Antonyms: similarly, likewise | adverb

Etymology->
From late Middle English *alternatif*, from late Latin *alternātīvus*, from Latin *alternāre*, *alternō* ("I do by turns")  + *-tīvus*.
