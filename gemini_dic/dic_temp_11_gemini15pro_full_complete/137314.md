nontactility:/ˌnɒntækˈtɪləti/
n.
非触觉性；无触觉
Lack of the sense of touch; inability to perceive through touch.
The artwork emphasized the nontactility of digital experiences, prompting viewers to consider the limitations of online interaction. 这件艺术品强调了数字体验的非触觉性，促使观众思考在线互动的局限性。
The phantom limb syndrome can create a sense of nontactility where the limb used to be.  幻肢综合症会导致患者感觉之前肢体所在的位置失去了触觉。
近义词:intangibility, impalpability
反义词:tactility, tangibility
词性:noun
