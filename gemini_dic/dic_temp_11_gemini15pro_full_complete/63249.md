enterprising:/ˈentərpraɪzɪŋ/
adjective
有进取心的，有事业心的，有创业精神的
Characterized by initiative and a willingness to undertake new ventures and projects.  Showing a readiness to take risks and pursue opportunities.
She is an enterprising young woman who started her own business. 她是一位有进取心的年轻女性，创办了自己的公司。

He had an enterprising spirit and wasn't afraid to take risks. 他有进取精神，不怕冒险。

近义词:ambitious, resourceful, energetic, driven, motivated, proactive, assertive, go-getting
反义词:lazy, unambitious, passive, timid, hesitant, cautious, unmotivated

词性:adjective
