fusilier:/ˌfjuːzəˈlɪər/ | n. | 火枪手；轻步兵 | A soldier armed with a fusil, historically a type of light musket. |  The fusiliers held their position against the enemy advance. 火枪手坚守阵地，抵御敌人的进攻。

The Royal Welch Fusiliers is a famous British Army regiment. 皇家威尔士火枪团是英国陆军著名的一个团。 | 近义词: infantryman, rifleman, soldier | 反义词: civilian | 词性: noun
