forkedly:/ˈfɔːrkɪdli/
adverb
分叉地，叉状地
in a forked manner; like a fork.
The lightning forkedly flashed across the sky. 闪电像叉子一样划过天空。
近义词: branchingly, dividingly
反义词: straightly, linearly
词性: adverb

词源:
From fork (n.) + -ed + -ly.  "Fork" comes from Old English *forca, from Proto-Germanic *furko, from Proto-Indo-European *perk- "pole, beam."  The suffix "-ed" forms adjectives from nouns, and "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives.
