braai-er:/ˈbraɪər/| n.|烧烤架，户外烤肉架|A metal framework used for cooking food over an open fire, especially outdoors.  It is particularly common in South Africa.|We cooked the boerewors on the braaier. 我们在烤肉架上烤香肠。|Synonyms: barbecue, grill, barbeque, outdoor grill|Antonyms: oven, stove|noun

braai-er:/ˈbraɪər/| n.|烤肉的人|A person who cooks food on a braai.|My dad is the designated braaier for all family gatherings. 我爸爸是所有家庭聚会的指定烧烤师。|Synonyms: barbecuer, grill master, cook|Antonyms: N/A|noun
