overfattening: /ˌoʊvərˈfætnɪŋ/ | adj. | 使过度肥胖的；容易使人发胖的 |  Causing excessive fatness; tending to make one fat.  | The overfattening diet of modern society contributes to health problems. 现代社会容易使人发胖的饮食导致了健康问题。|  Overfattening foods can lead to obesity.  容易使人发胖的食物会导致肥胖。 | 近义词: fattening, obesogenic | 反义词: slimming, healthy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "fatten" (verb meaning "to make or become fat") + "-ing" (suffix forming present participle and adjective)
