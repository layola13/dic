eupione:/ˈjuːpiːoʊn/ | n. | 優品蠟，石蠟油 | A white crystalline saturated hydrocarbon found in paraffin wax.  It is a mixture of alkanes, primarily C20H42. |  Eupione was used in early candles due to its clean burning properties. 優品蠟因其清潔的燃燒特性而被用於早期的蠟燭製造。| Synonyms: paraffin wax, hard paraffin | Antonyms: N/A | noun

eupione:/ˈjuːpiːoʊn/ | n. | 石蠟油 | A colorless, odorless, oily liquid obtained from the distillation of petroleum or coal tar.  It is used as a lubricant and in the manufacture of various products. | Eupione was a popular lubricant for delicate machinery. 石蠟油曾是一種用於精密機械的常用潤滑劑。| Synonyms: mineral oil, liquid paraffin | Antonyms: N/A | noun
