domiciliate:/ˈdɒməsɪleɪt/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməsəˌleɪt/ (US)| v. |使定居，(尤指正式地)安置|To establish someone in a permanent residence, especially officially.|The refugees were domiciled in temporary housing. 这些难民被安置在临时住所。|The insurance company requires you to be domiciled in the state to qualify for the policy. 这家保险公司要求你必须是本州居民才能获得保险。|locate, settle, reside|--- |verb

domicile:/ˈdɒməsaɪl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməˌsaɪl/ (US)| n. |住所，住处(尤指正式的、合法的)|A person's fixed, permanent, and principal home for legal purposes.|The company's domicile is in Delaware. 该公司的注册地是特拉华州。|He changed his domicile from the UK to France. 他把住所从英国改到了法国。|residence, abode, dwelling|--- |noun
