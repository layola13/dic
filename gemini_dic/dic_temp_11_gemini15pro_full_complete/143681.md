outlawest: /ˈaʊtlɔːɛst/ | v. | (archaic) declare (someone) an outlaw |  An archaic form of the verb "outlaw," meaning to declare someone an outlaw, used for the second person singular present tense. | Example: Thou outlawest him from the kingdom. 你把他驱逐出王国，宣布他为不法之徒。 | Synonym: banish, exile, proscribe | Antonym: pardon, legalize | Verb


词源：From Old Norse *ūtlagi, from *ūt "out" + *lagi "law."  Meaning literally "outside the law."
