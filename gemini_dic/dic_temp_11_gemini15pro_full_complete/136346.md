nonresilient:/ˌnɒnriˈzɪliənt/ | adj. | 非弹性的，缺乏韧性的，脆弱的 | Not resilient; unable to recover quickly from difficulties; fragile. | The nonresilient material cracked under pressure. 这种非弹性材料在压力下破裂了。| The drought had a devastating impact on the nonresilient farming community.  干旱对缺乏韧性的农业社区造成了毁灭性的影响。 | 近义词: fragile, brittle, weak, vulnerable, susceptible | 反义词: resilient, strong, tough, robust, adaptable | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
resilient (adjective): from Latin resiliens, present participle of resilire "to spring back, rebound," from re- "back" + salire "to leap, jump"
