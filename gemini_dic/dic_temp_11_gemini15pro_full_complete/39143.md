confusion:/kənˈfjuːʒən/ | n. |  困惑，混乱，混淆 | a state of not being certain about what is happening, what to do, or what something means |  There was some confusion about which flight they were supposed to be on.  对于他们应该乘坐哪个航班有些困惑。 | synonyms: bewilderment, disorientation, uncertainty, perplexity | antonyms: clarity, certainty, order, understanding | noun

confusion:/kənˈfjuːʒən/ | n. |  混乱，骚乱 | a situation in which there is a lot of noise, excitement, and activity, often involving people behaving in a disorganized way | The sudden power outage caused confusion in the city. 突然的停电导致了城市的混乱。 | synonyms: chaos, disorder, mayhem, turmoil | antonyms: order, peace, tranquility, calm | noun
