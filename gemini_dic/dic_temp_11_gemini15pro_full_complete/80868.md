goutiness:/ˈɡaʊtinəs/
noun
痛风, 患痛风的倾向
The state of being gouty; affected with gout.
He suffered from goutiness in his later years. 他晚年饱受痛风之苦。
近义词:gout, arthralgia
反义词:(None - absence of a condition doesn't have a direct antonym)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gout + -iness.  Gout ultimately derives from the Latin *gutta* meaning "drop," based on the medieval belief that the disease was caused by drops of morbid humor falling into the joints.
