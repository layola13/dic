obliteratingly: /əˈblɪtəˌreɪtɪŋli/
adv.
毁灭性地，彻底地
in a way that destroys something completely
The evidence was obliteratingly clear.  证据确凿无疑。
近义词: devastatingly, completely, utterly, totally
反义词: slightly, partially, mildly
词性: 副词


obliterate: /əˈblɪtəˌreɪt/
v.
消灭，抹去，摧毁
destroy utterly; wipe out
The bomb obliterated the town. 炸弹摧毁了这座城镇。
近义词: destroy, erase, annihilate, extinguish
反义词: create, build, establish, preserve
词性: 动词


词源: From Latin obliteratus, past participle of obliterare (“to erase, blot out, efface, destroy”), from ob- (“over, against”) + littera (“letter, character”).
