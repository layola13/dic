一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The concert begins at 8 pm.  音乐会晚上8点开始。
He always begins his day with a cup of coffee. 他总是以一杯咖啡开始新的一天。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The ceremony is beginning now.  仪式现在开始。
The sun is beginning to set. 太阳开始落山了。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The play has already begun.  戏剧已经开始了。
The meeting has begun without us. 会议已经开始了，没有等我们。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The rain has been beginning and stopping all day. 雨一整天都在断断续续地下。
He has been beginning to understand the complexities of the issue. 他开始理解这个问题的复杂性。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The movie began on time. 电影准时开始。
The storm began suddenly.  暴风雨突然来临。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The band was beginning to play when the power went out.  乐队刚开始演奏就停电了。
It was beginning to snow when we left. 我们离开时开始下雪了。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The concert had already begun by the time we arrived.  我们到达时音乐会已经开始了。
The game had begun before I could find my seat.  比赛在我找到座位之前就开始了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been beginning to suspect him before the evidence was found.  在找到证据之前，他们就开始怀疑他了。
She had been beginning to feel ill before the trip.  旅行前她就开始感觉不舒服了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The class will begin in five minutes.  课程五分钟后开始。
The trial will begin next week.  审判将于下周开始。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time tomorrow, we will be beginning our journey.  明天的这个时候，我们将开始我们的旅程。
He will be beginning his new job next month.  他下个月将开始他的新工作。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, he will have begun his graduate studies.  到明年，他将开始他的研究生学习。
They will have begun construction by the spring.  到春天，他们将已经开始施工。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By the time the project is finished, she will have been beginning to implement the changes.  到项目完成时，她将已经开始实施这些改变。
By next year, they will have been beginning to see the results of their hard work.  到明年，他们将已经开始看到他们努力的成果。



虚拟语气:
If he begins early, he can finish on time. 如果他早点开始，他就能按时完成。
I wish he would begin his work soon. 我希望他快点开始工作。


被动语态:
The project was begun last year. 这个项目是去年开始的。
Preparations are being begun for the festival.  节日准备工作正在开始。


疑问句:
When does the meeting begin?  会议什么时候开始？
Why did the argument begin? 争论为什么开始？


否定句:
The game hasn't begun yet.  比赛还没有开始。
He didn't begin his homework until late at night.  他直到深夜才开始做作业。
