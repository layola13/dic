dip:/dɪp/
n.|浸；蘸；下降；倾斜|a quick swim or immersion in water|I took a quick dip in the pool. 我在游泳池里快速游了一会儿。|immersion, plunge|rise, ascent|noun
dip:/dɪp/
v.|浸；蘸；下降；倾斜|put or let something down quickly or briefly in or into (liquid).|He dipped his hand in the water. 他把手浸在水里。|immerse, submerge|raise, lift|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dyppan "to dip, immerse," from Proto-Germanic *duppjanan, related to Old Norse dyppa "to dip."
