annulene:/ˈæn.juːˌliːn/ |n.|环烯|A class of cyclic conjugated hydrocarbons containing alternating single and double bonds.  Specifically, [n]annulene refers to a monocyclic molecule containing n conjugated pi electrons.|Benzene is an example of a [6]annulene.|Synonym: macrocycle|Antonym: acyclic compound|Noun
