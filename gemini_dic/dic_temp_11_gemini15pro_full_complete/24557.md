broadway: /ˈbrɔːdˌweɪ/ (US) /ˈbrɔːdweɪ/ (UK) | n. | 百老汇；百老汇大街(位于纽约市，是美国戏剧和音乐剧的重要中心) | A street in New York City famous for its theaters. | Example:  She dreamed of performing on Broadway. 她梦想在百老汇演出。

Example:  Broadway shows are known for their elaborate costumes. 百老汇的演出以其精美的服装而闻名。

近义词: theater district | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "off-Broadway" or "off-off-Broadway" to denote smaller, less commercial theater) | 词性: noun
