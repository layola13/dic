cadenza:/kəˈdɛntsə/ | n. | 华彩乐段；自由发挥的装饰性乐段 | A virtuosic passage, usually near the end of a piece of music, often improvised or unaccompanied, and allowing the performer to display their skill. | The pianist dazzled the audience with a brilliant cadenza. 钢琴家以一段精彩的华彩乐段使观众眼花缭乱。 |  The guitarist's cadenza was the highlight of the concerto.  吉他手的华彩乐段是协奏曲的亮点。 | synonyms: flourish, embellishment, ornamentation, solo, improvisation | antonyms: refrain, theme, melody | noun
