|n.|共济失调，运动失调|Loss of muscle control in voluntary movements, such as walking or picking up objects.  It can affect various parts of the body, including the limbs, trunk, eyes, and speech mechanism.|
|例句：The patient's ataxia made it difficult for him to walk unaided. 病人的共济失调使他难以独立行走。
