词源：From Old English ceorl "freeman of the lowest rank," from Proto-Germanic *karlaz "man, free man," from PIE root *ker- (1) "to grow."  The meaning deteriorated from "freeman, peasant" to "rude, ill-bred person" by the late 12c.
