autographal:/ˌɔːtəˈɡræfɪkəl/

亲笔的；自传的
Written by a person about their own life.  Relating to or characteristic of an autobiography.
Example:  This is an autographal letter written by the author herself.  这是一封作者亲笔写的信。
Example:  Her latest novel is deeply autographal, drawing heavily on her childhood experiences. 她最新的小说带有浓厚的自传色彩，大量取材于她童年的经历。

近义词:autobiographical, self-written
