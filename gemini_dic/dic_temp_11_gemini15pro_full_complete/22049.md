词源：From Middle English *bald*, *bold*, from Old English *beald* (“bold, brave, strong, confident”), from Proto-Germanic *balþaz* (“bold, brave, strong”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰelǵʰ-*. Cognate with Scots bauld (“bold”), West Frisian bald (“soon, quickly”), Dutch bald (“soon”), German bald (“soon”). Related also to Old English beldo (“boldness, confidence”). The semantic shift from "brave" to "impudent" arose in Middle English.  The typographical sense emerged in the 16th century.
