illuminary:/ɪˈluːmɪneri/ | n. | 先驱，杰出人物， luminary | A person who inspires or influences others, especially one prominent in a particular sphere. |  Albert Einstein was a true illuminary in the field of physics. 阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦是物理学界真正的杰出人物。

| Example: The conference was attended by illuminaries from the world of science and technology.  这次会议有来自科技界的杰出人士参加。| Synonym: luminary, leading light, guiding light, beacon, star | Antonym: nobody, nonentity, unknown | noun
