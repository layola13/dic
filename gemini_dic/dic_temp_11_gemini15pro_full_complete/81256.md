granitas:/ɡræˈniːtəs/
n.
granite (plural)
花岗岩（复数）
A very hard, crystalline, igneous rock, gray to pink in color, consisting mainly of feldspar, quartz, and mica, and often used as a building stone.
一种非常坚硬的结晶火成岩，颜色从灰色到粉红色，主要由长石、石英和云母组成，常用于建筑石材。
The countertops were made of polished granitas.  这些台面是由抛光花岗岩制成的。
The builders used various granitas for the facade of the building. 建筑工人使用了各种花岗岩来装饰建筑物的外墙。
近义词:stone, rock
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of rock)
