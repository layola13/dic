icicled:/ˈaɪsɪkəld/ | adj. | 结冰的，被冰覆盖的 | Covered with icicles; resembling an icicle. | The roof was icicled after the freezing rain. 屋顶在冻雨过后结满了冰柱。 | The branches were icicled and glistening in the sun. 树枝上结满了冰柱，在阳光下闪闪发光。 | 近义词：frozen, icy, frosty | 反义词：melted, thawed | 词性: 形容词

icicle:/ˈaɪsɪkəl/ | n. | 冰柱 | A hanging, tapering piece of ice formed by the freezing of dripping water. |  The icicles hung from the eaves of the roof. 屋檐上挂着冰柱。| The melting icicles dripped onto the sidewalk. 融化的冰柱滴落在人行道上。 | 近义词：ice pendant | 反义词： (No direct antonym, perhaps "puddle" for the melted result) | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old English *īs + gicel (icicle), from Proto-Germanic *jekilaz
