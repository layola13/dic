citizen's arrest: /ˈsɪtɪzənz əˈrɛst/ | n. | 公民逮捕 | An arrest made by a private citizen, not a police officer, of a person who has committed a crime. | Example: The shopkeeper made a citizen's arrest after witnessing the theft.  店主在目睹盗窃后进行了公民逮捕。| Example:  A citizen's arrest can be risky, so it's important to be certain of the facts. 公民逮捕可能会有风险，所以确定事实很重要。 | Synonym: private arrest, civilian arrest | Antonym: N/A | Noun
