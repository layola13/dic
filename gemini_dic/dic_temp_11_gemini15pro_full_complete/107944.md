laryngoscopy:/ˌlærɪŋˈɡɒskəpi/ (US) /ˌlærɪŋˈɡɑːskəpi/ (UK)| n.|喉镜检查|The visual examination of the larynx, typically using a laryngoscope.|The doctor performed a laryngoscopy to examine the patient's vocal cords.|近义词:laryngoscopic examination|反义词:None|词性:noun

laryngoscope:/ˌlærɪŋˈɡɒskoʊp/ (US) /ˌlærɪŋˈɡɒskoʊp/ (UK)| n.|喉镜|An instrument used for examining the larynx.|The surgeon used a laryngoscope to visualize the vocal cords.|近义词:None|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Modern Latin *laryngoscopia, from Ancient Greek λάρυγξ (lárunx, “larynx, throat”) + -scopia, from σκοπέω (skopéō, “I view, examine”).
