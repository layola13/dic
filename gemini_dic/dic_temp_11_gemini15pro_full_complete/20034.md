biometrics:/ˌbaɪoʊˈmɛtrɪks/| n. |生物识别技术；生物统计学|The study and application of measuring and analyzing unique physical or behavioral characteristics (such as fingerprints, voice patterns, or facial features) to identify and authenticate individuals.|例句：Biometrics is becoming increasingly important in security systems.  生物识别技术在安全系统中变得越来越重要。

例句：The company uses biometrics to control access to its secure areas.  该公司使用生物识别技术来控制对其安全区域的访问。|近义词：biometric authentication, biological identification|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:  From bio- (life) + -metrics (measurement).  Ultimately from Greek *bios* (life) and *metron* (measure).
