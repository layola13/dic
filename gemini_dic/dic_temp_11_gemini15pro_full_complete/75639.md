fuguelike:/ˈfjuːɡlaɪk/
adjective
赋格曲似的，像赋格曲的
Resembling or characteristic of a fugue, a musical form in which a theme is introduced by one part and then repeated by others in overlapping succession.
Her piano piece had a fuguelike quality, with interwoven melodies building to a crescendo. 她的钢琴曲有一种赋格曲般的特质，交织的旋律逐渐增强到一个高潮。
The intricate plot of the novel was almost fuguelike in its complexity.  这部小说的复杂情节几乎像赋格曲一样复杂。
近义词:  contrapuntal, canonic, polyphonic
反义词: homophonic, monophonic
词性: adjective
词源:  From "fugue" (from Italian *fuga*, from Latin *fuga* "flight," because the parts seem to chase each other) + "-like" (suffix meaning "resembling").
