deliquium:/dɪˈlɪkwiəm/
n.
昏厥，昏倒；失职；放弃
A sudden and temporary loss of consciousness caused by reduced blood supply to the brain; dereliction of duty; abandonment.
The heat caused the old man to suffer a deliquium.  高温导致老人昏厥。
He was accused of deliquium of duty when he failed to report the incident. 他因未能报告该事件而被指控失职。
synonym: faint, swoon, syncope, lapse, failure, neglect
antonym: consciousness, alertness, diligence, attention
