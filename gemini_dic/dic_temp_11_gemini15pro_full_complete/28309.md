carbine:/ˈkɑːrbiːn/ /ˈkɑːrbaɪn/| n.|卡宾枪|A short-barreled lightweight rifle, often used by cavalry or special forces.  It is derived from a longer rifle or musket.|例句：The soldier carried a carbine slung over his shoulder.  士兵肩上扛着一支卡宾枪。

例句：Carbines are preferred for close-quarters combat. 卡宾枪更适合近距离作战。|近义词：rifle, short rifle|反义词:long rifle (not a direct opposite, but represents the other end of the spectrum)|词性:noun
