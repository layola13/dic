biflagellate:/baɪˈflædʒəˌleɪt/
adjective|双鞭毛的|Having two flagella. |Many algal species are biflagellate, using the flagella for locomotion. 许多藻类是双鞭毛的，用鞭毛来移动。|biflagellated|uniflagellate, multiflagellate|adjective
biflagellate:/baɪˈflædʒəˌleɪt/
noun|双鞭毛生物|An organism that has two flagella. |Biflagellates play a crucial role in marine ecosystems. 双鞭毛生物在海洋生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。|biflagellated organism| --- |noun
