gipseian:/dʒɪpˈsiːən/ | adj. n.|吉普赛的；吉普赛人|Of or relating to Gypsies or their way of life; a Gypsy.  A person of Romani origin, traditionally nomadic. |She had a gipseian spirit, always yearning to travel. 她有一种吉普赛人的精神，总是渴望旅行。| He lived a gipseian lifestyle, moving from place to place. 他过着吉普赛式的生活方式，四处迁徙。 |近义词：Roma, Romani, Romany|反义词:settled, non-nomadic|词性:adjective, noun
Etymology: From Gypsy + -an.  Ultimately from Egyptian (because Gypsies were mistakenly thought to have originated in Egypt).
