herling:/ˈhɜːrlɪŋ/
n.
投掷; 赫林球(爱尔兰的一种类似曲棍球的运动)
The act of hurling something; a team sport played in Ireland, similar to hockey.
He won the herling match with a powerful throw. 他以强力一掷赢得了这场赫林球比赛。
The herling final drew a huge crowd. 赫林球决赛吸引了大批观众。
近义词:throwing, casting, flinging
反义词:catching, receiving

词性:noun
