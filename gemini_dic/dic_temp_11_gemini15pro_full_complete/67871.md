fabricates:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪts/
v.
制造，捏造，编造
to invent or produce something, especially something false, in order to deceive someone
例句：He fabricated a story about being attacked. 他编造了一个关于自己被袭击的故事。

例句：The evidence was fabricated to make him look guilty.  证据是伪造的，目的是让他看起来有罪。

近义词：invent, concoct, forge, manufacture, construct
反义词：disprove, debunk, expose

词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fabricātus, past participle of fabricāre (“to build, construct, fashion, frame, forge, fabricate”), from fabrica (“workshop, forge; structure, fabric”), probably originally *facbrica, from facere (“to do, make”).
