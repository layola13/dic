forestaller:/fɔːrˈstɔːlər/ | n. | 囤积者；预先阻止者；抢先占有者 | Someone who forestalls something prevents it from happening by doing something first. | The company acted as a forestaller, buying up all the available supplies.  这家公司 acted as a forestaller, buying up all the available supplies。这家公司抢先收购了所有可用的物资。| synonyms: anticipator, preventer | antonyms: promoter, facilitator | noun

forestall:/fɔːrˈstɔːl/ | v. | 预先阻止；先发制人；垄断 | To prevent or obstruct (an anticipated event or action) by taking action ahead of time. |  They forestalled criticism by offering a full explanation in advance. 他们事先提供完整的解释以避免批评。| synonyms: prevent, preclude, obviate, avert | antonyms: encourage, promote, facilitate | verb
