cylindraceous: /ˌsɪlɪnˈdreɪʃəs/ | adj. | 圆柱形的，圆柱状的 | Having the form of a cylinder; cylindrical. | The cylindraceous stem of the plant was smooth and green.  这种植物的圆柱形茎光滑而呈绿色。| The bacteria formed long, cylindraceous colonies. 这些细菌形成了长长的圆柱形菌落。| 近义词: cylindrical, columnar | 反义词: spherical, globular | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin *cylindrāceus, from cylindrus (“cylinder”) + -āceus. Ultimately from Ancient Greek κύλινδρος (kúlindros, “roller, cylinder”).
