diacritical:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɪtɪkəl/
adjective
变音的，附加符号的
A diacritical mark is a symbol added to a letter to change its pronunciation.
The word "résumé" uses a diacritical mark on the first 'e'.  “résumé”这个词在第一个'e'上使用了变音符号。
Diacritical marks are common in many languages, including French and Spanish.  变音符号在许多语言中很常见，包括法语和西班牙语。
Synonyms: accentual,  accenting
Antonyms: unaccented, unmarked
词性: adjective
