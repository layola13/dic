cumberbunds:/ˈkʌmbərbʌndz/
n.
腰带，腰封
A broad sash worn around the waist, typically over a man's formal eveningwear.
He wore a tuxedo with a matching cumberbund. 他穿了一件燕尾服，配了一条相配的腰封。
