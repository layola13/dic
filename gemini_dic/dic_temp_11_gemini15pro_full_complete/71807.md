fleecer:/ˈfliːsər/| n.|敲诈者，诈骗者|Someone who cheats or swindles another person, especially by charging too much money.|The fleecer charged tourists exorbitant prices for trinkets.|近义词: swindler, cheat, con artist, fraudster|反义词: philanthropist, benefactor|词性:noun
Etymology: From the verb "fleece," from Old English *flysan "to strip of fleece," related to Old Norse fljosa "to flay."  The original meaning referred to shearing sheep for their wool, but it later evolved figuratively to mean "to rob or swindle."
