monocle:/ˈmɒnəkl/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəkəl/ (US)| n. |单片眼镜|A single eyeglass, kept in place by the muscles around the eye socket.|例句：The gentleman adjusted his monocle and peered at the painting.  这位绅士调整了一下他的单片眼镜，仔细地看着这幅画。

例句：Wearing a monocle, he looked like a character from a bygone era. 他戴着单片眼镜，看起来像来自过去时代的人物。


近义词：eyeglass, lens|反义词:binoculars|词性:noun
