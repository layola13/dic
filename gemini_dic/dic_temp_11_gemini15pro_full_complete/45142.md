cuddliest:/ˈkʌdliɪst/ | adj. |最令人想拥抱的，最可爱的|  Superlative form of cuddly: most cuddly.  It describes something or someone that elicits a strong desire to cuddle due to its softness, warmth, or perceived vulnerability. | That kitten is the cuddliest creature I've ever seen. 那只小猫是我见过的最可爱的生物。| The giant panda is surprisingly one of the cuddliest animals.  大熊猫出人意料地是最想让人拥抱的动物之一。|  adorable, huggable, lovable, snuggly |  unpleasant, repulsive | adjective
