callable:/ˈkɔːləbl/ | adj. | 可调用的，可赎回的 |  Something that is callable can be called, summoned, or brought into action. In finance, a callable security can be redeemed by the issuer before its maturity date. | The bonds are callable after five years. 这些债券五年后可以赎回。 |  redeemable, due | non-callable, irrevocable | adjective

callable:/ˈkɔːləbl/ | n. | 可调用对象 | In programming, a callable object is an object that can be called like a function.  |  The `map` function takes a callable and an iterable as arguments. `map`函数接受一个可调用对象和一个可迭代对象作为参数。| function, method | non-callable | noun
