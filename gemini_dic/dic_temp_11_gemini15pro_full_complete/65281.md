euchroic:/juːˈkroʊɪk/| adj.|常染色质的|Relating to euchromatin, a lightly packed form of chromatin (DNA, RNA, and proteins) that is rich in genes and is often (but not always) under active transcription.  It stains lightly with basic dyes.|The euchroic regions of the genome are generally more actively transcribed than heterochromatic regions.|近义词:  actively transcribed, gene-rich|反义词: heterochromatic|词性: adjective
