consistorial:/ˌkɒnsɪˈstɔːriəl/ | adj. |主教法庭的；教会会议的；宗教法庭的| Relating to a consistory, which is a church council or court, especially one convened by a bishop or other high-ranking cleric. |  The consistorial court dealt with matters of church law. 主教法庭处理教会法事务。|  The bishop issued a consistorial decree. 主教发布了一项宗教法庭法令。|  synonym: ecclesiastical, clerical, churchly | antonym: secular, lay | adjective
