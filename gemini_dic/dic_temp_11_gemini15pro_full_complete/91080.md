hutting:/ˈhʌtɪŋ/
n.
小屋生活，住在小屋里
The act of staying in a hut, especially for a vacation or temporary period.
They went hutting in the Scottish Highlands. 他们在苏格兰高地的小屋里度假。
近义词:camping, cabinning
反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps) city living, urban life
词性:名词


hutting:/ˈhʌtɪŋ/
v.
住在小屋里
Staying in a hut.
We're hutting this weekend.  我们这周末要住在小屋里。
近义词:camping, cabinning
反义词:(No direct opposite, perhaps) staying in a hotel
词性:动词 (gerund or present participle)
