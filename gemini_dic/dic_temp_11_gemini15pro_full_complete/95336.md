incenses:/ɪnˈsɛnsɪz/
| n. |香，香味；焚香；奉承，恭维|A sweet-smelling substance that is burned, especially in religious ceremonies, or the smoke from this. Also, excessive flattery or praise.|The smell of incense filled the air. 空气中弥漫着香火的味道。|近义词：aroma, fragrance, flattery, adulation|反义词：stench, criticism|名词

incenses:/ɪnˈsɛnsɪz/
| vt. |激怒；使愤怒|To make someone very angry.|His constant criticism incensed her. 他不断的批评激怒了她。|近义词：enrage, infuriate, anger|反义词：calm, soothe, placate|动词
