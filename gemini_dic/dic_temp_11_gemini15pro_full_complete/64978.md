estimator:/ˈestɪmeɪtər/| n.|估计者，估算者|A person who estimates; one who calculates or judges the value or extent of something.|The insurance estimator assessed the damage to the car.  保险估算员评估了汽车的损坏。|近义词：appraiser, evaluator, assessor|反义词: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From estimate + -or.  Estimate from Latin aestimāre (“to value, appraise”), from aestimus (“valued, esteemed”).
