armoires:/ɑːrˈmwɑːrz/
n.
衣橱，衣柜
A large wardrobe or movable cupboard, typically one that is ornate or antique.
The bedroom was furnished with an antique bed and matching armoires. 卧室里摆放着一张古董床和配套的衣橱。
synonyms: wardrobe, cupboard, closet, cabinet
antonyms: shelf, rack
noun
