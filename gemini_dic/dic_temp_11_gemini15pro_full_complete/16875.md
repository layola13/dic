basilican:/bəˈsɪlɪkən/
adjective
巴西利卡式的；长方形的
Relating to or resembling a basilica (a large oblong hall or building with double colonnades and a semicircular apse).
The church's basilican form reflected the architectural styles of the Roman Empire.  教堂的巴西利卡式样反映了罗马帝国的建筑风格。
The ancient Roman building had a basilican plan. 这座古罗马建筑采用了巴西利卡式的平面布局。
近义词:rectangular, oblong
反义词:circular, round
词性:adjective

basilican:/bəˈsɪlɪkən/
noun
巴西利卡式教堂
A large oblong hall or building with double colonnades and a semicircular apse, especially one used as a Christian church.
St. Peter's Basilica in Rome is a renowned example of basilican architecture. 罗马的圣彼得大教堂是巴西利卡式建筑的著名例子。
The town's main church is a basilican structure dating back to the 12th century. 该镇的主要教堂是一座可追溯到12世纪的巴西利卡式建筑。
近义词:cathedral, church
反义词:N/A
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *basilicanus, from Latin basilica, from Greek basiliké (stoá) ‘royal (portico)’.
