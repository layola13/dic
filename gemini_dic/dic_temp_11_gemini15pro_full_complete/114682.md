malignify: /məˈlɪɡnɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使恶化，使有害 | To make something worse or more harmful. | The rumors malignified the situation. 谣言使情况恶化了。| Synonym: worsen, aggravate, exacerbate | Antonym: improve, ameliorate, alleviate | verb
