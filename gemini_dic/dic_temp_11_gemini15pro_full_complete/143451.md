outfoxed:/ˌaʊtˈfɑːkst/ | v. | 智胜，以计谋胜过 | to defeat or trick someone by being more clever than they are | The young chess player outfoxed the grandmaster with a brilliant move. 年轻的棋手以一个精彩的棋步智胜了特级大师。| Synonyms: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver | Antonyms: be outwitted, be outsmarted | Verb

outfoxed:/ˌaʊtˈfɑːkst/ | adj. | 被智胜的 | having been defeated or tricked by someone more clever |  He felt completely outfoxed by her cunning plan. 他觉得自己完全被她狡猾的计划智胜了。 | Synonyms: outsmarted, outwitted | Antonyms: triumphant, victorious | Adjective
