cable:/ˈkeɪbəl/| n. v.| 电缆，缆绳；用缆绳系住 |A thick rope of wire or nonmetallic fiber, typically used for mooring, hoisting, or towing; also, a similar insulated assembly of wires used for transmitting electricity or telecommunication signals.| The ship was secured by a heavy cable.  这艘船用一根粗缆绳固定住了。 | synonyms: rope, cord, wire, line | antonyms: wireless | noun

cable:/ˈkeɪbəl/| v.| 用电缆发送；用缆绳系住 |To send a message by cable; to fasten or secure with a cable.| I cabled my congratulations to the newlyweds. 我用电报向新婚夫妇表示祝贺。| synonyms: wire, telegraph | antonyms: detach, unfasten | verb
