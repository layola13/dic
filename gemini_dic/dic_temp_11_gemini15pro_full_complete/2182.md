addicting:/əˈdɪktɪŋ/
adjective
使人上瘾的；令人着迷的
So enjoyable that one wants more of it; habit-forming.
This game is so addicting, I can't stop playing it.  这个游戏太让人上瘾了，我停不下来。
The rhythm of the music is addicting. 这音乐的节奏令人着迷。
Synonyms:  habit-forming, captivating, compelling, enthralling, fascinating
Antonyms:  repulsive, boring, tedious, uninteresting
词性: adjective


addictive:/əˈdɪktɪv/
adjective
使人上瘾的；令人着迷的
likely to cause addiction
This game is so addictive, I can't stop playing it.  这个游戏太让人上瘾了，我停不下来。
Nicotine is a highly addictive substance. 尼古丁是一种高度成瘾的物质。
Synonyms:  habit-forming, captivating, compelling, enthralling, fascinating
Antonyms:  repulsive, boring, tedious, uninteresting
词性: adjective
