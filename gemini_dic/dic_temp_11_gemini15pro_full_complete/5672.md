altruism: /ˈæltruɪzəm/ | n. |利他主义；无私 | The principle or practice of unselfish concern for or devotion to the welfare of others. |  Mother Teresa was known for her altruism. 特蕾莎修女以其利他主义而闻名。

例句：
Volunteering your time is a great act of altruism.  奉献你的时间是一种伟大的利他行为。

例句：
He showed remarkable altruism in donating a kidney to a stranger. 他向陌生人捐赠了一个肾脏，表现出非凡的利他主义。


近义词：selflessness, benevolence, generosity, charity | 反义词：selfishness, egoism, greed | 词性：noun
