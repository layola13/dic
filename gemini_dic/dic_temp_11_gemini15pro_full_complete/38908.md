confessers:/kənˈfɛsərz/| n.|忏悔者；告解者；承认者|People who confess something, often a sin or crime.  那些承认某事的人，通常是罪过或罪行。|例句：The confessers sought absolution from the priest. 忏悔者寻求牧师的赦免。

例句：The police questioned the confessers for hours. 警察对认罪者进行了数小时的审讯。|近义词：admitters, acknowledgers|反义词：deniers, liars|词性:noun
