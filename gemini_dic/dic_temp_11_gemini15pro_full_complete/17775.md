bedlinen:/ˈbedˌlɪnɪn/
n.
床上用品
Household textiles used to cover a bed, typically consisting of sheets, pillowcases, and a duvet cover or blanket.
I need to buy new bedlinen for the guest room. 我需要为客房买新的床上用品。
The hotel's bedlinen was crisp and white.  酒店的床上用品洁白挺括。
近义词:bedding, sheets
反义词:(无)
词性:名词


bedlinen:/ˈbedˌlɪnɪn/
词源:  Compound of 'bed' and 'linen.' 'Linen' originally referred to fabric made from flax, but now often encompasses other materials used for bed coverings.  源于“bed”（床）和“linen”（亚麻布）的组合。“linen”最初指的是由亚麻制成的织物，但现在通常包含用于床罩的其他材料。
