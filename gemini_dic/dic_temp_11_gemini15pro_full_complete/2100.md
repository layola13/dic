adamesque:/ˌædəˈmɛsk/
adjective
像亚当的；裸体的；无罪的；纯真的
Resembling Adam, especially in nudity or innocence.  Relating to or characteristic of the first man, Adam, particularly his state of innocence before the Fall.
His adamesque physique was admired by all.  他像亚当一样健美的体格受到所有人的钦佩。
The painting depicted the man in an adamesque pose. 这幅画描绘了这个男人像亚当一样裸体的姿势。
近义词:naked, nude, innocent, pure, pristine
反义词:clothed, dressed, guilty, corrupt, sinful
词性:adjective
词源:From Adam + -esque (suffix meaning "in the style of").
