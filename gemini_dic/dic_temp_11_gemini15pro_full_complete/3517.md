afterword:/ˈɑːftərwɜːrd/ | n. | 后记；结语；跋 | A concluding section in a book, typically by a person other than the author. |  Example 1: The author's wife wrote a moving afterword to his final novel. 作者的妻子为他最后一部小说写了一篇感人的后记。

Example 2:  The afterword provided valuable context for the historical events described in the book. 后记为书中描述的历史事件提供了宝贵的背景。 | synonym: epilogue, postscript, concluding remarks | antonym: foreword, preface, introduction | noun
