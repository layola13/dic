nonpejorative:/ˌnɒnpɪˈdʒɒrətɪv/| adj.|无贬义的，不带贬义的|Not having negative connotations; not meant to disparage or belittle.|例句：The term "nerd" is increasingly used in a nonpejorative sense.  “书呆子”这个词越来越多地被用在没有贬义的语境中。|近义词：neutral, objective, non-derogatory|反义词：pejorative, derogatory, negative|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- (not) + pejorative (having negative connotations).  The word "pejorative" comes from the Latin word "pejorare," meaning "to make worse."
