coalesces:/ˌkoʊəˈlɛsɪz/| v. |合并，联合，结合|To come or grow together to form one thing or system.|The puddles coalesced into a small stream. 水坑汇聚成一条小溪。|近义词：merge, unite, combine, fuse, blend|反义词：separate, divide, split, disperse|词性：动词

Etymology: From Late Latin coalescere, from Latin co- + alescere "to grow up," from alere "to nourish."
