half-bath:/ˈhæf bæθ/ |n.| 半卫；洗手间 |A bathroom with only a toilet and sink, and no bathtub or shower.  |例句：The house has two full baths and one half-bath. 这房子有两个完整的浴室和一个半卫。

例句：The half-bath is located downstairs, convenient for guests. 半卫位于楼下，方便客人使用。|近义词：powder room, restroom, lavatory|反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) full bath|词性: 名词
