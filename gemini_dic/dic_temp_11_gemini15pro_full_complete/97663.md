inhibition:/ɪn.hɪˈbɪʃ.ən/ | n. |抑制，阻止，压抑 |The action of inhibiting or the state of being inhibited.  A feeling that makes someone self-conscious and unable to act in a relaxed and natural way.|  Shyness is one of the most common inhibitions. 害羞是最常见的抑制之一。| synonyms: restraint, suppression, repression | antonyms: encouragement, stimulation, liberation | noun

inhibit:/ɪnˈhɪb.ɪt/ | v. |抑制，阻止 | To prevent (an action or process) or to slow down.|  This drug inhibits the growth of tumors. 这种药物抑制肿瘤生长。| synonyms: hinder, impede, obstruct | antonyms: promote, encourage, facilitate | verb
