outsought:/ˌaʊtˈsɔːt/
adjective
广受欢迎的；非常需要的；热切希望得到的
Much sought-after; in great demand.
例句：Antique furniture is outsought by collectors. 古董家具受到收藏家的追捧。

例句：Her advice is outsought by many people. 她的建议受到很多人的追捧。

近义词：sought-after, desired, popular, in demand
反义词：undesirable, unpopular, unwanted
词性：adjective

词源：
"Outsought" is formed from the prefix "out-" (meaning "exceeding; surpassing") and the past participle of the verb "seek" ("sought"). It literally means "sought beyond others" or "exceedingly sought."
