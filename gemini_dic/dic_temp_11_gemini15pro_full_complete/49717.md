democratic:/ˌdɛməˈkrætɪk/ | adj. |民主的；民主政体的；大众的；平民的 | Relating to or supporting democracy or its principles.  Characterized by or based on the principles of democracy, such as equality, freedom, and the rule of law.  Accessible to or enjoyed by all social classes. |  The United States is a democratic republic. 美国是一个民主共和国。|  She believes in democratic ideals. 她信仰民主的理念。|  Synonyms: egalitarian, representative, popular, populist  | Antonyms: autocratic, dictatorial, totalitarian, tyrannical | adjective
