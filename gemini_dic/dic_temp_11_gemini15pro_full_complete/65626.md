evangelise: /ɪˈvændʒəlaɪz/ | v. | 传福音；宣传；推广 | To preach the gospel and convert others to Christianity, or to promote something with enthusiasm. | He spent years evangelising in Africa. 他在非洲传教多年。| Synonyms: proselytize, preach, promote, advocate | Antonyms: suppress, conceal, discourage | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *evangelizare, from Greek euangelizesthai "bring good news," from euangelion "good news," from eu- "good" + angelos "messenger."
