acquittance: /əˈkwɪtəns/ | n. | 解除义务；释放；收据；(略有交情的人)熟人 | a written or spoken statement that shows that someone has paid a debt or has been released from a duty or obligation; a person whom one knows slightly |  I gave him an acquittance for the debt. 我给了他一张解债的收据。 | synonyms: discharge, release, receipt, acquaintance | antonyms:  charge, debt, obligation, stranger | noun

acquittance: /əˈkwɪtəns/ | n. | 熟人 | a person one knows slightly, but who is not a close friend | He's an acquittance of mine from work. 他是我工作上的一个熟人。 | synonyms: acquaintance, associate, contact | antonyms: friend, intimate, close friend | noun
