catharist:/ˈkæθərɪst/| n.|卡特里派教徒，清洁派信徒|A member of a medieval Christian sect that believed in a dualistic cosmology of good and evil and practiced extreme asceticism.|The Cathars rejected the material world, seeing it as the creation of an evil god.|近义词：dualist, ascetic, heretic|反义词:orthodox believer|noun

cathartic:/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/| adj.|宣泄的，净化心灵的|Providing psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions; relating to catharsis.|Listening to music can be a cathartic experience.|近义词：cleansing, purifying, therapeutic|反义词:repressive|adjective
