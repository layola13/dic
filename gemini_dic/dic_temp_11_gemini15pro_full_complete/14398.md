autosuggesting:/ˌɔːtəʊsəˈdʒɛstɪŋ/
| adj. | 自动提示的 |  The act of a software program predicting what a user is typing and offering suggestions for completing the word or phrase. | The search bar is autosuggesting popular keywords as I type.  搜索栏在我输入时自动提示热门关键字。 | 近义词:predictive, autocomplete | 反义词:manual | 词性:形容词

autosuggesting:/ˌɔːtəʊsəˈdʒɛstɪŋ/
| n. | 自动提示 | The function or feature that provides automatic suggestions. | Autosuggesting helps users find information quickly. 自动提示帮助用户快速找到信息。 | 近义词:autocomplete, prediction | 反义词:manual entry | 词性:名词


词源: auto- (self) + suggesting (from suggest, meaning to put forward an idea)
