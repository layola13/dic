hypersensitizes:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtaɪzɪz/
verb
使…过敏；使…过分敏感
To make excessively sensitive.  To increase the sensitivity of something or someone.
The allergy test hypersensitized her skin.  过敏测试使她的皮肤过敏。
Exposure to certain chemicals can hypersensitize the immune system. 接触某些化学物质会使免疫系统过度敏感。
sensitize, heighten sensitivity
desensitize, numb
v.
