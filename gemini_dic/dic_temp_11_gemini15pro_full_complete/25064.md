bual: /buˈɑːl/ | n. |  闲聊，聊天; (尤指朋友间的) 谈话，谈天 | A friendly conversation, especially between friends. |  We had a good bual about old times. 我们聊了聊过去的美好时光。 | 近义词: chat, talk, conversation, gossip | 反义词: silence, quiet | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Irish and Scottish Gaelic *bual, meaning "act of striking, beating."  The semantic shift likely occurred from the idea of exchanging words like blows.
