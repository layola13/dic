gorce:/ɡɔːrs/| n.|戈尔斯（一种虚构的生物）|A gorce is a fictional creature invented by American writer Edward Gorey.  It is a furry, vaguely bear-like creature with a long snout and sharp teeth. It often appears in Gorey's macabre and humorous illustrations and stories, and is typically depicted causing mischief or meeting an untimely end. |The gorce stalked its prey through the fog.  戈尔斯在雾中跟踪它的猎物。|近义词:monster, beast, creature (due to its fictional nature, direct synonyms are difficult)|反义词:None (fictional creature)|词性:Noun
