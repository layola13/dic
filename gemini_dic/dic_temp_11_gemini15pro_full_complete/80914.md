gowl:/ɡaʊl/
noun
怒视，瞪眼；（猫头鹰等的）鸣叫声
an act of staring angrily at someone or something; a long, low, mournful cry, especially of an owl or dog
He gave me a fierce gowl. 他狠狠地瞪了我一眼。
The gowl of the owl echoed through the forest. 猫头鹰的鸣叫声在森林中回荡。
synonyms: glare, scowl, stare, howl, hoot
antonyms: glance, smile, whisper
