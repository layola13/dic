blot:/blɑt/ (US), /blɒt/ (UK)| n. vt.|污点，墨水渍；缺点，污点；吸墨纸|a mark made by ink or other staining substance; a fault or flaw; a piece of blotting paper|The ink made a blot on the paper. 墨水在纸上留下了一个污点。| synonyms: stain, spot, blemish, flaw, mark, speck, smudge | antonyms: perfection, cleanliness| noun

blot:/blɑt/ (US), /blɒt/ (UK)| vt.|弄脏，涂污；吸干；遮蔽|to make a blot or stain; to dry with absorbent paper; to obscure or hide|She blotted the spilled ink with a napkin. 她用餐巾纸吸干了洒出的墨水。| synonyms: stain, spot, blemish, soak up, dry, obscure | antonyms: clean, purify| verb

词源: From Middle English *blotten, from Old Norse *blotta (“to strike, sacrifice”), from Proto-Germanic *blautjaną (“to strike, beat”).
