de-sludge: /ˌdiːˈslʌdʒ/
verb
除污；清除淤泥
to remove sludge from something
The wastewater treatment plant needs to be de-sludged regularly. 污水处理厂需要定期清除淤泥。
The septic tank was de-sludged last month. 化粪池上个月清理了淤泥。
Synonyms: clean, clear, purge
Antonyms: contaminate, pollute
词性：动词

de-sludging: /ˌdiːˈslʌdʒɪŋ/
noun
除污；清除淤泥（的过程）
the process of removing sludge from something
Regular de-sludging is essential for maintaining the efficiency of the system. 定期清除污泥对于维持系统效率至关重要。
The de-sludging operation took several days. 清淤作业花了几天时间。
Synonyms: cleaning, clearing, purging
Antonyms: contamination, pollution
词性：名词
