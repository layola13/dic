half-mumbled:/ˌhɑːf ˈmʌmbəld/ | adj. | 含糊不清的，咕哝的 | Spoken in a low, indistinct voice, as if reluctant to be heard clearly. | He gave a half-mumbled apology. 他含糊不清地道歉了一句。| She half-mumbled a greeting and hurried past. 她含糊地打了个招呼，匆匆走过。 | 近义词: muttered, murmured, indistinct | 反义词: clear, articulate | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "half-" (Old English healf) + "mumbled" (past participle of "mumble," from Middle Dutch mommelen, frequentative of mommen "to mumble").  The prefix "half-" indicates that the mumbling is not complete or fully realized.
