interpenetrable:/ˌɪntərˈpɛnɪtrəbl/| adj.|互相穿透的，可渗透的|Able to penetrate each other mutually; permeable.|例句：The two cultures are so interpenetrable that it's hard to distinguish between them. 这两种文化互相渗透，很难区分。|例句：The interpenetrable nature of online and offline worlds is becoming increasingly apparent.  线上和线下世界的互相渗透性正变得越来越明显。|近义词：permeable, pervading, interpermeating|反义词：impermeable, impenetrable|词性:形容词

词源:
From inter- + penetrable.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "penetrable" comes from the Latin "penetrabilis," meaning "that can be penetrated."
