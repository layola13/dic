helping-out:/ˈhelpɪŋ aʊt/ |v. | 帮助；援助 | Giving assistance or support to someone with a task or problem. |  I'm helping out my parents with the housework. 我正在帮父母做家务。

My neighbor is helping out with the garden. 我的邻居正在帮忙打理花园。| 近义词: assisting, aiding, supporting | 反义词: hindering, obstructing | 词性: 动词(不及物动词短语)
