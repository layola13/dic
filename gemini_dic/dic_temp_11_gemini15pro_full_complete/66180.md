exchanged:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒd/
verb
交换过的，已交换的
past participle of exchange: having been given and received in return for something else.
They exchanged gifts.  他们交换了礼物。
The two countries exchanged ambassadors. 这两国交换了大使。
Synonyms: swapped, traded, bartered
Antonyms: kept, retained, withheld
词性: 动词(过去分词)
