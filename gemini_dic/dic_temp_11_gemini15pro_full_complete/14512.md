avenor:/ˈeɪvənər/| n.|燕麦管理员；粮草官；地方官|An officer of the royal stables who was responsible for providing oats for the king's horses.  A local official in some areas.| The avenor ensured the king's horses were always well-fed. 燕麦管理员确保国王的马匹总是吃得饱饱的。 The avenor also oversaw the distribution of grain in the village.  这个地方官也监督村里的谷物分配。|近义词：purveyor, supplier, provider|反义词:None|Noun
