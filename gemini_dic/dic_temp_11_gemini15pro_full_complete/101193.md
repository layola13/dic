inviolability:/ɪnˌvaɪə.ləˈbɪl.ɪ.ti/
n.
不可侵犯性；神圣性
The state of being inviolable; immunity from violation or profanation.
The inviolability of human rights is a fundamental principle. 人权不可侵犯是一项基本原则。

The constitution guarantees the inviolability of private property. 宪法保障私有财产不可侵犯。

Synonyms: sanctity, sacredness, immunity, impregnability
Antonyms: vulnerability, violability, weakness, exposure
词性: 名词
