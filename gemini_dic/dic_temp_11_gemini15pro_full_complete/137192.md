nonsuggestion:/ˌnɒnsəˈdʒɛstʃən/ | n. | 非建议，未被提出的建议 | The absence of a suggestion; something not suggested.  It signifies that something has not been put forward as an idea or possibility. |  The committee's nonsuggestion on the matter was surprising. 委员会对此事没有提出建议，令人惊讶。|  His nonsuggestion of any alternative plan left the team in a difficult position. 他没有提出任何替代方案，这让团队陷入了困境。 | 近义词: omission, lack of suggestion | 反义词: suggestion, proposal | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + suggestion
