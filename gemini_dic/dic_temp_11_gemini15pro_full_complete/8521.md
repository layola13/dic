anticatholic:/ˌæntikˈæθlɪk/
adjective
反天主教的；敌视天主教的
Opposed to or hostile towards the Catholic Church or its beliefs.
His anticatholic sentiments were evident in his writings. 他的反天主教情绪在他的著作中显而易见。
近义词: anti-Catholic, anti-papal, anti-Roman Catholic
反义词: pro-Catholic, Catholic
词性: adjective

anticatholic:/ˌæntiˈkæθəlɪk/
noun
反天主教者；敌视天主教的人
A person who is opposed to or hostile towards the Catholic Church or its beliefs.
He was known as a staunch anticatholic in the community.  他在社区里是出了名的坚定反天主教者。
近义词: opponent of Catholicism
反义词: supporter of Catholicism, Catholic
词性: noun
