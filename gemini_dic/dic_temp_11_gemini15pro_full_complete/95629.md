incompatibly:/ˌɪnkəmˈpætəbli/
| adv. |不兼容地，不相容地|In a manner that is not compatible; in a way that cannot exist or be true together.|She dressed incompatibly with the occasion. 她穿的衣服与场合不符。|
|近义词：incongruously, discordantly, conflictingly|
|反义词：compatibly, harmoniously, consistently|
|词性: 副词|

incompatible:/ˌɪnkəmˈpætəbl/
| adj. |不兼容的，不相容的|Not able to exist or be true together; not able to be used together.|This software is incompatible with my operating system. 这个软件与我的操作系统不兼容。|
|近义词：conflicting, discordant, inconsistent|
|反义词：compatible, harmonious, consistent|
|词性: 形容词|
