grubby:/ˈɡrʌbi/| adj.|肮脏的，邋遢的|Covered with dirt; grimy.  (Covered in dirt or appearing unclean.)|The child's grubby hands reached for the cookie.  这孩子脏兮兮的手伸向饼干。|近义词：dirty, filthy, grimy, soiled|反义词：clean, spotless, immaculate|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From grub (insect larva) + -y (suffix forming adjectives). The original sense was "infested with grubs," later extended to mean generally dirty. 词源: 来自grub（昆虫幼虫）+ -y（构成形容词的后缀）。最初的意思是“满是幼虫的”，后来扩展到一般指肮脏的。
