overneutralized: /ˌoʊvərˈnuːtrəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度中和的 | Having been neutralized to an excessive degree, resulting in a pH higher than 7 (for acids) or lower than 7 (for bases). | The solution was overneutralized, making it slightly alkaline.  这个溶液被过度中和，使其略呈碱性。| The overneutralized soil became unproductive. 过度中和的土壤变得贫瘠。 | 近义词: overbalanced, overcompensated | 反义词: underneutralized | 词性: 形容词
