draconin:/ˈdrækənɪn/| n. |龙血; 血竭;|A dark red resin obtained from various plants, especially the dragon trees (Dracaena species) of the Canary Islands and Socotra.  It is used in varnishes, photoengraving, and as a folk medicine.|The artist used draconin to create a rich, deep red in his painting.|近义词：dragon's blood|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific resin)|词性:noun
