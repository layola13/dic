heliconia:/ˌhɛlɪˈkoʊniə/ | n. | 赫蕉属植物；蝎尾蕉 | A tropical American plant with large, brightly colored bracts and small flowers.  A genus of flowering plants in the Heliconiaceae family. |  Example 1: The heliconia's vibrant red flowers attracted hummingbirds.  赫蕉鲜艳的红色花朵吸引了蜂鸟。

Example 2:  He planted a variety of heliconias in his garden. 他在花园里种植了各种各样的赫蕉。 | 近义词: lobster-claw, false bird-of-paradise | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *Ἑλικώνιος (Helikṓnios)*, relating to Mount Helicon, the seat of the Muses in Greek mythology.  This is likely due to the showy nature of the flowers, reminiscent of the Muses' artistic inspiration.
