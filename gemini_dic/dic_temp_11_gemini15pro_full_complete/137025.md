nonstatement:/ˈnɒnˌsteɪtmənt/ | n. | 不表态，未声明，没有事实根据的陈述 | An expression that avoids making a clear or definite statement.  A lack of a statement. Something that is not a statement of fact. |  The politician's response was a nonstatement, offering no real insight into his position.  这位政治家的回应等于没说，没有真正表明他的立场。

|  Her silence on the matter was interpreted as a nonstatement of support. 她对此事的沉默被解读为不支持的表态。 | 近义词： evasion, equivocation, silence, ambiguity | 反义词： statement, declaration, affirmation, assertion | 词性: noun
