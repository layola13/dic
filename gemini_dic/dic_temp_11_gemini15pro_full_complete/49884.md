demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/ (US) /dɪˈmɜː(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|反对；提出异议；犹豫|To express doubt or objection to something; to hesitate.|She demurred at the idea of working on weekends. 她反对周末工作的想法。|近义词：object, protest, hesitate, waver|反义词：accept, agree, consent, comply|词性：动词

demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/ (US) /dɪˈmɜː(r)/ (UK)| n.|反对；异议；犹豫|An objection or hesitation.|Her demur to the proposal was politely expressed. 她对这项提议的反对表达得很委婉。|近义词：objection, protest, hesitation|反义词：acceptance, agreement, consent|词性：名词

词源: From Old French *demorer "to linger, delay," from Latin demorari "to delay, linger," from de- "down, completely" + morari "to delay."
