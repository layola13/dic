languishment:/ˈlæŋɡwɪʃmənt/| n.|衰弱；萎靡；憔悴；苦思； languishing|The prolonged heat caused the plants to languish. 长时间的酷热导致植物枯萎。| synonyms: decline, deterioration, decay, atrophy, pining, yearning | antonyms: flourishing, thriving, prospering | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *languisshen*, from Old French *languiss-,* present participle stem of *languir* (“to languish”), from Latin *languēscō* (“I become faint”), inchoative of *langueō* (“I am faint, weak, or weary”).
