nonorganics:/ˌnɒnɔːrˈɡænɪks/ | n. | 无机物；无机肥料 | Substances that are not derived from living organisms and do not contain carbon-hydrogen bonds. |  Farmers often use nonorganics to supplement the soil. 农民经常使用无机肥料来补充土壤养分。|  The study compared the effects of organics and nonorganics on plant growth.  这项研究比较了有机物和无机物对植物生长的影响。| synonyms: inorganics, minerals | antonyms: organics, organic matter | noun
