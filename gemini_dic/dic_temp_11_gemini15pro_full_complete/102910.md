jawfoot:/ˈdʒɔːfʊt/
n.
颚足动物，尤指某些甲壳类动物
A jawfoot is a type of crustacean with modified appendages around its mouth that resemble jaws.
Jawfeet are small crustaceans found in marine environments. 颚足动物是海洋环境中发现的小型甲壳类动物。
These jawfeet are essential for filter feeding. 这些颚足对于滤食至关重要。
近义词: maxilliped (颚足类)
反义词:  (无明确的反义词)
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "jaw" + "foot."  "Jaw" comes from Old English "ceowan" (to chew). "Foot" comes from Old English "fōt."
