inanely:/ɪˈneɪnli/| adv.|愚蠢地；空洞地；无意义地|In a silly, empty, or meaningless way.  Derived from "inane" (meaning empty or silly) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  The root of "inane" is Latin "inanis" meaning "empty".|He grinned inanely at the camera. 他对着镜头傻傻地咧嘴一笑。|Synonyms: foolishly, absurdly, senselessly, vacuously|Antonyms: sensibly, wisely, meaningfully|Adverb
