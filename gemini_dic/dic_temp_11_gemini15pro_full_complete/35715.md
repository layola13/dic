cocaptaining:/ˌkoʊˈkæptənɪŋ/ | n. | 共同担任队长；联合队长 |  The act of sharing the captaincy of a team or group with another person. |  Example: Cocaptaining the team brought its own unique set of challenges. 共同担任队长带来了其独特的挑战。| Example:  She found cocaptaining with her rival surprisingly rewarding. 她发现与她的竞争对手共同担任队长竟然很有收获。 |Synonyms: joint leadership, shared captaincy | Antonyms: sole leadership, sole captaincy | noun
