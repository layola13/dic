arcboutant:/ˌɑːrkbuˈtɑːnt/| n.|飞扶壁，扶拱|A masonry support in the form of a half-arch that transmits the outward thrust of a vault or roof to an outer support.|The cathedral's arcboutants were essential to its structural integrity.  这座大教堂的飞扶壁对其结构完整性至关重要。|Synonyms: buttress, flying buttress, prop, support|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps weakness, instability)|noun
