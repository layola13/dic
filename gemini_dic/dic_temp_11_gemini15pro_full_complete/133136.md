nonet:/noʊˈnet/ | n. | 九重奏；九人小组；九行诗 | A group of nine musicians or instruments playing together; a piece of music written for such a group; a poem with nine lines. | The composer wrote a beautiful nonet for strings. 作曲家写了一首优美的弦乐九重奏。| synonyms: ennead, group of nine | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider duo, trio for smaller groups depending on context) | noun

nonet:/noʊˈnet/ | n. | 九行诗 | A poem with nine lines.  | She penned a poignant nonet about lost love. 她写了一首关于失恋的凄美九行诗。| synonyms: nine-line poem | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
