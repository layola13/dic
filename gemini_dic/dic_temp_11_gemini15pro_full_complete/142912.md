out-sainting:/ˌaʊtˈseɪntɪŋ/
|v.| 比...更圣洁，胜过...的圣洁|
|To act more saintly than someone else; to surpass someone in apparent virtue, often in a hypocritical or excessive way.|
|She was out-sainting all the other nuns with her constant prayers and fasting.| 她不断地祈祷和禁食，比其他修女都更显圣洁。
|outdo, surpass, excel, outstrip|
|underperform, fall short|
|verb|
