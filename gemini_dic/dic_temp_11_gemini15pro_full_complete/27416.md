camerlingo:/ˌkæməˈlɪŋɡoʊ/ /ˌkæməˈlɛŋɡoʊ/
noun
枢机主教；教皇的管家；(旧时)教皇宫廷总管
A high-ranking dignitary of the Roman Catholic Church, formerly the papal chamberlain.
The camerlingo had significant power during the interregnum between popes.  这位枢机主教在教皇空位期间拥有很大的权力。
The camerlingo oversaw the administration of the Vatican during the sede vacante.  在教皇职位空缺期间，这位枢机主教负责监督梵蒂冈的行政管理。
Synonyms: chamberlain, chancellor, administrator
Antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a specific role)
