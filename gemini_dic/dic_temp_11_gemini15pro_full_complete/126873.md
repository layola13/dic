nappinesses:/ˈnæpinɪsɪz/
noun
睡意，瞌睡，小睡 (plural form of nappiness)
The state of being nappy; drowsiness or inclination to sleep, especially in children.
After a big lunch, he experienced waves of nappiness.  午饭后，他一阵阵地犯困。
The baby's nappiness made it difficult to get anything done. 婴儿的睡意使人难以完成任何事情。
Synonyms: sleepiness, drowsiness, doziness
Antonyms: wakefulness, alertness, vigilance
词性: 名词
