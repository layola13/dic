discrepancy:/dɪˈskrepənsi/| n. |差异，不符，不一致|A discrepancy is a difference or inconsistency between two things that should be the same.|There was a discrepancy between the two accounts of the accident.  两个关于事故的描述之间存在差异。|The figures showed a wide discrepancy. 这些数字显示出很大的差异。|近义词：difference, inconsistency, variation, divergence, disagreement|反义词：agreement, consistency, conformity, similarity, correspondence|词性:名词
