emporiums:/ɪmˈpɔːriəmz/
n.
大型商场，商业中心
A large retail store, often selling a wide variety of goods.
The city boasts several emporiums selling everything from clothes to furniture.  这座城市拥有几家大型商场，从服装到家具，应有尽有。
The old market had transformed into a bustling emporium.  旧市场已经变成了一个熙熙攘攘的商业中心。
Synonyms: store, market, shop, bazaar, mart
Antonyms: stall, kiosk, booth
