overexaggeration: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˌzædʒəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 过分夸张；夸大其词 | An act of exaggerating something to an excessive degree. |  He told the story with a great deal of overexaggeration. 他讲故事时添油加醋，夸大其词。| synonyms: hyperbole, exaggeration, embellishment, overstatement | antonyms: understatement, minimization | noun

overexaggerate: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt/ | v. | 过分夸大；夸张 | To exaggerate something to an excessive degree. |  Don't overexaggerate your achievements on your resume. 不要在你的简历上过分夸大你的成就。 | synonyms: exaggerate, overstate, embellish, amplify | antonyms: understate, minimize | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + exaggeration.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "exaggeration" comes from the Late Latin "exaggerationem," meaning "a heaping up."
