hayrake:/ˈheɪreɪk/| n.|干草耙，搂草机|A farm implement used for gathering or spreading hay.|The farmer used a hayrake to gather the hay into rows.|近义词：windrower, tedder|反义词:N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "hay" + "rake". "Hay" comes from Old English "hēg" (grass, hay). "Rake" comes from Old English "racian" (to scrape together).
