cholecystis: /ˌkɒləsɪˈstaɪtɪs/ | n. |胆囊炎|Inflammation of the gallbladder, often caused by gallstones.|He was diagnosed with cholecystis and had to have his gallbladder removed. 他被诊断出患有胆囊炎，不得不切除胆囊。|Near synonyms: gallbladder inflammation, biliary colic|Antonym: None (cholecystis describes a condition, not a state with an opposite)|noun
