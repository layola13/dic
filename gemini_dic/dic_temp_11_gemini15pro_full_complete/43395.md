crankily:/ˈkræŋkɪli/| adv.|坏脾气地，暴躁地|In a bad-tempered or irritable way. |She answered crankily, clearly not in a good mood. 她没好气地回答，显然心情不好。|He stomped off crankily after being told he couldn't have any more candy. 被告知不能再吃糖后，他生气地跺脚走开了。|近义词：grumpily, irritably, peevishly|反义词：cheerfully, pleasantly|词性:副词
Etymology->
From crank (bad-tempered) + -ily (adverbial suffix).  "Crank" itself likely originated from an earlier sense of "a bend or twist," perhaps metaphorically applied to a person's twisted or contorted mood.
