gorgedly:/ˈɡɔːrdʒɪdli/
adverb
华丽地；灿烂地；极好地
in a gorgeous manner; splendidly; beautifully.
She was gorgedly dressed in a silk gown. 她穿着丝绸礼服，华丽极了。
近义词：beautifully, splendidly, magnificently, exquisitely
反义词：plainly, simply, dully, unattractively
词性: adverb

词源：From gorgeous + -ly. Gorgeous traces back through Old French gorgias "elegant, fashionable" to the Latin gurgulio "gullet, throat".  The original meaning referred to a ruffled or ornamented neckline.
