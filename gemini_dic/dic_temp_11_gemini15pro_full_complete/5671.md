altrices: /ˈæltrɪsiːz/
noun
female birds that feed their young
Female birds that provide nourishment to their offspring.
The altrices diligently gathered insects to feed their hungry chicks.
近义词: nurturers, carers
反义词: none (This word is specific to the role of female birds feeding young)
词性: Noun (plural)

altrix: /ˈæltrɪks/
noun
a female animal that nourishes its young
A female animal that provides food and care to its offspring, especially birds.
The altrix tirelessly flew back and forth to the nest, bringing food to her chicks.
近义词: mother, nurturer
反义词: none (This word describes a biological role, not a moral quality)
词性: Noun (singular)
