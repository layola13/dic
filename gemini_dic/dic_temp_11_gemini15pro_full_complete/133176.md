nonevangelic:/ˌnɒnɪvænˈdʒɛlɪk/ | adj. | 非福音派的，非福音主义的 | Not of or relating to evangelical Christianity; not characterized by or emphasizing evangelical beliefs or practices. |  Many mainline Protestant denominations are considered nonevangelic. 许多主流的新教教派被认为是非福音派的。|  The conference attracted both evangelical and nonevangelic Christians.  这次会议吸引了福音派和非福音派的基督徒。 | 近义词: non-evangelical, liberal, mainline | 反义词: evangelical, fundamentalist | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "evangelic" (from Late Latin evangelicus, from Greek euangelikos, from euangelion "good news," from eu- "good" + angelos "messenger").
