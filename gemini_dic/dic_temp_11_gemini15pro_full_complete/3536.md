against the grain:/əˈɡenst ðə ɡreɪn/ | idiom |违背意愿；格格不入；逆着潮流 |Going against the grain means doing something that is difficult or unpleasant because it contradicts one's natural inclinations or the prevailing norms.|例句：Working on weekends goes against the grain for me.  周末工作违背我的意愿。|例句：Asking for help goes against the grain for him; he's always been fiercely independent.  寻求帮助让他很不自在；他一直非常独立。|近义词：against one's nature, contrary to one's inclination, counterintuitive, unnatural |反义词: naturally, instinctively, along with the grain, with the flow | idiom
