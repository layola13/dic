dropstones:/ˈdrɒpˌstəʊnz/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːpˌstoʊnz/ (US)| n. |坠石，落石|Fragments of rock transported by icebergs and deposited into finer-grained sediments.|例句：Dropstones are a key indicator of past glacial activity. 坠石是过去冰川活动的關鍵指標。

例句：The geologist found several dropstones in the sedimentary layers. 地质学家在沉积层中发现了几块坠石。|近义词：erratics, ice-rafted debris|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "in situ deposits" - deposits formed in their original place)|noun
