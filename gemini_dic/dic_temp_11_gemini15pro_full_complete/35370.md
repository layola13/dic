co-morbidity:/ˌkoʊmɔːrˈbɪdɪti/| n.|共病，并发症|The presence of one or more additional diseases or disorders co-occurring with a primary disease or disorder.|例句：Diabetes is a common co-morbidity in patients with heart disease. 糖尿病是心脏病患者常见的并发症。

例句：The study examined the effects of co-morbidities on patient outcomes. 这项研究调查了并发症对患者预后的影响。

|近义词：co-occurring illness, concomitant disorder, accompanying condition|反义词: health|词性: noun
