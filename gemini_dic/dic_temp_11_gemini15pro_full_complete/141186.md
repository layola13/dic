oolitic:/oʊəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. |鲕状的，由鱼卵石组成的| Composed of oolites or resembling oolites.  Oolites are small, rounded grains of calcium carbonate formed in shallow marine environments. | The geologist examined the oolitic limestone. 地质学家检查了鲕状灰岩。| The beach was covered in oolitic sand.  海滩上覆盖着鲕状沙。| 近义词: pisolitic | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-oolitic" or describing a different rock texture like "crystalline") | 词性: adjective
