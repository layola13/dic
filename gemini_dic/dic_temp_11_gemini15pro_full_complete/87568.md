heterophasia:/ˌhɛtərəˈfeɪʒə/ /ˌhɛtəroʊˈfeɪʒə/| n. |异语症|A speech disorder in which words are unintentionally replaced by others that are similar in sound but unrelated in meaning, or in which words are unintentionally transposed.| The patient with heterophasia said "telephone" when he meant "television."|近义词：paraphasia, jargon aphasia|反义词: normal speech|词性:noun
