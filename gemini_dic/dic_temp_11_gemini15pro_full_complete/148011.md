overrigorousnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈrɪɡərəsnəsɪz/ | n. | 过分严谨, 过于严格 | The state of being excessively rigorous or strict.  |  His overrigorousnesses in applying the rules alienated his staff.  他过于严苛地执行规定疏远了他的员工。 |  Over-scrupulousness, over-strictness, excessive rigor |  Leniency, laxity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessively) + "rigorous" (strict, severe) + "-ness" (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  "Rigorous" comes from the Latin "rigor" meaning stiffness or hardness.
