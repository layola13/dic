baritone: /ˈbærɪtoʊn/ (US) /ˈbærɪtən/ (UK)| n. adj.|男中音; 男中音歌手; 男中音乐器|A male singing voice between tenor and bass; a singer with such a voice; an instrument having a range of this pitch. |He has a rich baritone voice. 他拥有浑厚的男中音嗓音。|Synonyms: bass-baritone, low tenor|Antonyms: soprano, tenor, alto|noun

baritone: /ˈbærɪtoʊn/ (US) /ˈbærɪtən/ (UK)| adj.|男中音的|Having the range of a baritone. |A baritone saxophone. 男中音萨克斯管。|Synonyms: deep, low-pitched|Antonyms: high-pitched, soprano|adjective
