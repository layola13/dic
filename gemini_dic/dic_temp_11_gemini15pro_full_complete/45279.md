cults:/kʌlts/ | n. | 邪教，异教；狂热崇拜；狂热的追随者 | a system of religious veneration and devotion directed toward a particular figure or object. A relatively small religious group, especially one having beliefs or practices regarded by others as strange or sinister. |  Example 1: Several cults sprang up in the wake of the comet's appearance.  彗星出现后，涌现出几个邪教。

Example 2: He was drawn into a cult that promised enlightenment. 他被一个承诺启迪的邪教所吸引。 | synonyms: sects, factions, denominations | antonyms: mainstream religions, orthodoxy | noun



cult:/kʌlt/ | n. | 狂热的崇拜；狂热的追随者 | a misplaced or excessive admiration for a particular person or thing | Example 1:  The singer has a huge cult following.  这位歌手拥有大量的狂热追随者。

Example 2: There's a cult of personality surrounding the new CEO. 新任首席执行官周围围绕着一种个人崇拜。| synonyms: following, fandom, devotees | antonyms: indifference, apathy | noun
