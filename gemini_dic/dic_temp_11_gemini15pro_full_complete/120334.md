minimal:/ˈmɪnɪməl/| adj.|最小的，极少的|Smallest possible; the least possible.|The damage to the car was minimal. 车辆的损坏很小。|minimal effort was required. 只需要很少的努力。 |近义词：slight, negligible, least, smallest, minimum|反义词：maximal, maximum, extensive, substantial|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *minimālis*, from Latin minimus (“smallest”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mey- (“small”).
