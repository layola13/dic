overdosed: /ˌoʊvərˈdoʊst/ | v. n. adj. | 过量；过量服用；服药过量的 | Having taken or administered an excessive amount of a substance, especially a drug.  As a noun: an instance of taking or being administered too much of a substance, especially a drug. As an adjective: characterized by having taken an excessive amount of a substance. | The musician overdosed on sleeping pills.  这位音乐家服用安眠药过量。 | Synonyms: overdose, overconsumption, glut | Antonyms: underdose, moderation, abstinence | verb/noun/adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (meaning "excessive") + "dose" (a measured quantity of medicine).  The verb form developed from the past participle of "overdose," signifying the state of having overdosed.
