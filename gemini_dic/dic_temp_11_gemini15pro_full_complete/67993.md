factionally:/ˈfækʃənəli/
adverb
派系地，宗派地
in a way that relates to factions or creates factions
The company was factionally divided, hindering effective decision-making.  这家公司派系林立，阻碍了有效决策。
The political party was factionally split on the issue of healthcare reform. 该政党在医疗改革问题上出现了派系分裂。
synonym: divisively, sectarianly
antonym: unitedly, harmoniously
词性: 副词
