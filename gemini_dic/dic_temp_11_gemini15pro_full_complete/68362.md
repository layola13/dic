faller:/ˈfɔːlər/| n.| 跌倒者；伐木工人；落下的东西|A person or thing that falls.  Someone who cuts down trees. Something that has fallen.|例句：The faller was injured when the tree fell on him.  那个伐木工人在树倒在他身上时受了伤。

例句：The rain caused many fallers on the icy sidewalk. 雨水导致在结冰的人行道上摔倒的人很多。|近义词：stumbler, timberman, logger, lumberjack (for someone who cuts down trees), casualty (for someone injured in a fall)|反义词:climber, riser|词性:noun

faller:/ˈfɔːlər/| n.| 失败者；倒闭者|(informal) A person or enterprise that fails.|例句：Many dot-com companies were fallers in the early 2000s. 许多网络公司在21世纪初倒闭了。

例句：He was a faller in business but successful in love. 他在生意上是个失败者，但在爱情上却很成功。|近义词：failure, loser, bankrupt|反义词:winner, success|词性:noun
