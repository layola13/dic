did: /dɪd/ | v. | 做，进行；完成；发生 |  past tense of "do". Used to form questions and negative statements in the simple past tense, and also for emphasis. | I did my homework yesterday. 我昨天做了我的家庭作业。|近义词： performed, executed, completed, acted, made, effected |反义词: failed, neglected, omitted | 词性: verb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
*Do* you like apples? 你喜欢苹果吗？
She *does* not eat meat. 她不吃肉。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Not applicable. "Did" is not used in the present continuous tense.


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Have you *done* your homework yet? 你完成作业了吗？
She has *done* a great job. 她做得很好。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Not applicable. "Did" is not used in the present perfect continuous tense.


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I *did* my homework last night.  我昨晚做了我的作业。
He *did* not go to the party. 他没有去参加派对。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Not applicable. "Did" is not used in the past continuous tense.


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already *done* the dishes when she arrived. 她到达时我已经洗完碗了。
He had *done* his best before he gave up.  在他放弃之前，他已经尽力了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Not applicable. "Did" is not used in the past perfect continuous tense.


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Not applicable. "Did" is not used in the simple future tense.


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Not applicable. "Did" is not used in the future continuous tense.


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Not applicable. "Did" is not used in the future perfect tense.


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Not applicable. "Did" is not used in the future perfect continuous tense.


虚拟语气:
If I *did* that, I would be in trouble. 如果我那样做，我会惹麻烦。
I wish I *did* not have to work today. 我希望我今天不用工作。



被动语态:
Not applicable as "did" is an auxiliary verb and does not have a passive form.


疑问句:
*Did* you see the movie? 你看了那部电影吗？
What *did* she say? 她说了什么？


否定句:
I *did* not go to the party. 我没去参加派对。
She *did* not finish her work. 她没有完成她的工作。



PHRASE->
What *did* you do that for? 你为什么那样做？
How *did* you do? 你做得怎么样？
*Did* you have a good time? 你玩得开心吗？
I *did* not know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
What *did* he say his name was? 他说他叫什么名字？


词源：Old English *dīd*, past tense of *dōn* "to do," from Proto-Germanic *deda-, past tense of *do- (source also of Old Frisian dede, Old Saxon deda, Old High German teta, Old Norse gerða, Gothic gada-).
