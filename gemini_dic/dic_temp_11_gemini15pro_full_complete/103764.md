journaling:/ˈdʒɜːrnəlɪŋ/
noun
日志记录；日记；日志
the activity of regularly writing down your thoughts and feelings
例句：Journaling can be a helpful way to process your emotions.  写日记可以帮助你处理情绪。

例句：She finds journaling to be a therapeutic practice. 她发现写日记是一种有益身心的做法。

近义词：diary writing, logging, recording
反义词: None (No direct antonym for the act of journaling)
词性: noun
