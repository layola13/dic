fluoridation:/ˌflʊərɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. |加氟；氟化作用|The act or process of adding fluoride to something, typically water, to prevent tooth decay.|The fluoridation of water has been shown to reduce cavities in children.|Synonyms: fluoridization|Antonyms: defluoridation|noun
