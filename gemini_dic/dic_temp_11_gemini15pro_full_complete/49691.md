demivill:/ˈdɛmiˌvɪl/| n.| 半城区; 郊区 |A residential area or town located on the outskirts of a larger city, often characterized by a mix of urban and suburban characteristics.  It can be considered a transitional zone between the city center and the countryside. |She grew up in a demivill, enjoying both the peace of the suburbs and proximity to city amenities. 她在一个半城区长大，既享受郊区的宁静，又靠近城市的便利设施。|Near-synonyms: suburb, exurb, outskirts, periphery| Antonyms: city center, downtown, urban core|noun
