impeaching:/ɪmˈpiːtʃɪŋ/
verb
弹劾；控告；质疑
charging someone with a serious crime, especially a public official, or challenging the validity or honesty of something.
The House of Representatives is impeaching the president for obstruction of justice. 众议院正以妨碍司法公正的罪名弹劾总统。
accusing, indicting, denouncing
exonerating, defending, upholding
verb
