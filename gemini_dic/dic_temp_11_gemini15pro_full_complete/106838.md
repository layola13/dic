laboriousness:/ləˈbɔːriəsnəs/
noun
辛苦；费力；勤勉
The quality of requiring much effort and time.
The laboriousness of the task made her want to give up.  这项工作的艰巨让她想要放弃。
近义词: difficulty, arduousness, toil, exertion, painstakingness
反义词: ease, facility, simplicity
词性: noun

Etymology:
From laborious + -ness. Laborious comes from the Late Latin laboriōsus, from Latin labor "work, toil, hardship" + -ōsus "-ous."
