noncongealing:/ˌnɒnkənˈdʒiːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 不凝固的，不冻结的 | Not solidifying or thickening, especially not changing from a liquid to a solid or semisolid state. | The noncongealing oil was ideal for the machinery in sub-zero temperatures. 这种不凝固的油非常适合在零下温度下运行的机械。|  The noncongealing liquid remained fluid even in the freezer. 即使放在冰箱里，这种不凝固的液体仍然保持流动状态。 | 近义词：non-coagulating, non-solidifying | 反义词：congealing, coagulating, solidifying | 词性: adjective


Etymology:
From non- (not) + congeal (from Old French congeler, from Latin congelare "to freeze," from com- "together" + gelare "to freeze").
