imperilled:/ɪmˈpɛrəld/ /ɪmˈpɛrɪld/ | adj. | 濒临灭绝的，处于危险中的 |  Put in peril; endangered. Exposed to imminent risk of disaster or ruin. | The imperilled species is on the brink of extinction.  这个濒临灭绝的物种正处于灭绝的边缘。 | The rising floodwaters imperilled the lives of the villagers. 上涨的洪水危及村民的生命。 | synonyms: endangered, threatened, jeopardized, at risk | antonyms: safe, secure, protected | adjective
