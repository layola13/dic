edacity: /ɪˈdæsɪti/ | n. | 贪吃; 贪婪; 狼吞虎咽 |  The quality of being excessively eager to eat or wanting more than one needs or deserves. | He ate with an edacity that shocked everyone at the table.  他狼吞虎咽的样子让在座的人都感到震惊。 | 近义词: gluttony, voracity, greed | 反义词: abstinence, moderation | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Latin *ēdācitās, from ēdāx (“gluttonous”).  来自拉丁语 *ēdācitās，源自 ēdāx（“贪吃的”）。
