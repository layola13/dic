nonnervousness:/ˌnɑːnˈnɜːrvəsnəs/
n.
镇静，沉着，不紧张
The state of being not nervous; calmness or composure.
Her nonnervousness before the big presentation impressed everyone.  她在大型演讲前的镇静给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
He showed remarkable nonnervousness during the emergency. 在紧急情况下，他表现出非凡的镇定。
Synonyms: calmness, composure, tranquility, serenity, aplomb, poise, self-possession
Antonyms: nervousness, anxiety, apprehension, agitation, unease, tension
noun
