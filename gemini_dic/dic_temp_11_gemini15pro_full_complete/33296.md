chubbier:/ˈtʃʌbiər/| adj.|更胖的，更圆润的|Comparative adjective of chubby, meaning more plump or round.  It implies a slightly higher degree of plumpness than "chubby."|The baby's cheeks are chubbier than they were last week.  婴儿的脸颊比上周更胖了。

The puppies are getting chubbier every day.  小狗们每天都越来越胖。|近义词：plumper, rounder, fuller|反义词：thinner, slimmer, leaner|词性:形容词
chubby:/ˈtʃʌbi/| adj.|胖乎乎的，圆胖的|Having a full rounded shape; plump.  Often used to describe babies and children in a positive and affectionate way.|The baby has chubby cheeks.  婴儿有着胖乎乎的脸颊。
