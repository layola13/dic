mockup:/ˈmɑːkʌp/ (US) /ˈmɒkʌp/ (UK)| n. |模型，样机|A mockup is a model or a replica of a design or device, used for demonstration, evaluation, or testing.| We created a mockup of the new website to get client feedback. 我们创建了一个新网站的模型来获得客户反馈。|The team built a mockup of the aircraft to test its aerodynamics.  团队建造了一个飞机模型来测试它的空气动力学。| synonym: prototype, model, sample, dummy| antonym: final product, finished version|noun
