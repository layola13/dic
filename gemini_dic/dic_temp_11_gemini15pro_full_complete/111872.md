logo:/ˈloʊɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈlɒɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 商标，标识，徽标 | A symbol or other small design adopted by an organization to identify its products, uniform, vehicles, etc. |  Example: The Nike swoosh is a globally recognized logo. 耐克的 swoosh 标志是全球公认的。

Example:  The company redesigned its logo to appeal to a younger market.  这家公司重新设计了它的标志，以吸引更年轻的市场。| synonym: emblem, symbol, trademark, brand | antonym: N/A (Logos don't have direct opposites.) | noun
