grovel:/ˈɡrɒvəl/ (UK), /ˈɡrɑːvəl/ (US)| vi.| 卑躬屈膝，匍匐；奴颜婢膝| To behave in a way that shows you are very sorry for something, especially by being too humble.| He groveled to his boss to keep his job. 他向老板卑躬屈膝以保住工作。|近义词：crawl, fawn, cringe|反义词：defy, resist|verb

grovel:/ˈɡrɒvəl/ (UK), /ˈɡrɑːvəl/ (US)| n.| 卑躬屈膝，匍匐；奴颜婢膝| The act of groveling.|  His grovel was pathetic. 他的卑躬屈膝真是可悲。|近义词：fawning, subservience|反义词：resistance, defiance|noun
