enshrining:/ɪnˈʃraɪnɪŋ/| v.|把…奉为神圣；珍藏；铭记；|The constitution enshrines the rights of all citizens. 宪法 enshrines 所有公民的权利。|例句：The document enshrines the basic rights of the individual.  这份文件 enshrines 个人的基本权利。

例句：This principle is enshrined in the constitution.  这项原则 enshrines 在宪法中。|近义词：preserving, protecting, safeguarding, cherishing, sanctifying|反义词：desecrating, violating,  profaning|词性:动词

词源: From en- + shrine, from Old French escrin, from Frankish *skrina "chest, box," of uncertain origin.  The meaning shifted from "placing in a shrine" to the more figurative "preserving as sacred."
