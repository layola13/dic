devitrify:/diːˈvɪtrɪfaɪ/
v.
(使)失透，(使)脱玻，晶化
to change from a glassy to a crystalline state; to lose the characteristics of glass
The porcelain devitrified during firing.  瓷器在烧制过程中失透了。

近义词:crystallize, solidify
反义词:vitrify

词性:verb (动词)
