euctical:/juːˈtɛktɪkəl/
adjective
低共熔的；易熔的
Relating to a eutectic mixture or alloy; having the composition or properties of a eutectic.
The eutectic point is the lowest temperature at which the mixture becomes completely liquid.  低共熔点是混合物完全变成液体的最低温度。
The eutectic composition allows for a lower melting point than either of the pure components. 低共熔成分使得熔点比任何一种纯组分的熔点都低。
近义词:fusible
反义词:infusible
词性:adjective
