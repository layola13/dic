frictionlessly:/ˈfrɪkʃənləsli/
adv.
无摩擦地，流畅地
without friction; smoothly and effortlessly.
The puck slid frictionlessly across the ice.  冰球在冰面上平滑地滑行。
The gears turned frictionlessly, a testament to the quality of the engineering. 齿轮运转流畅，证明了工程质量的优良。
近义词:smoothly, effortlessly, easily
反义词:roughly, with difficulty, with resistance
词性:adverb
