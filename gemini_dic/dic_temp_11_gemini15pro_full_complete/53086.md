diphtheria:/dɪfˈθɪəriə/| n.|白喉|A serious infectious bacterial disease typically affecting the mucous membranes of the nose and throat, characterized by the formation of a grayish membrane in the throat that can obstruct breathing.|例句：Diphtheria is a highly contagious disease. 白喉是一种高度传染性疾病。

例句：Vaccination has greatly reduced the incidence of diphtheria. 疫苗接种大大降低了白喉的发病率。|近义词：None (specific disease)|反义词:None (specific disease)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diphtheria, from Greek διφθέρα (diphthéra) "leather, parchment," referring to the leathery membrane that forms in the throat.
