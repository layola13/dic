interpolishes:/ˌɪntərˈpɒlɪʃɪz/
verb
相互抛光；互相润色；（尤指）相互恭维
polishes between or among; especially : exchanges compliments
The two rivals interpolished each other's egos before the debate.  这两个竞争对手在辩论前互相恭维，抬高对方的 ego。
They spent the entire meeting interpolishing each other's ideas. 他们整个会议都在互相润色彼此的想法。
Synonyms: flatter, compliment, praise, laud
Antonyms: criticize, condemn, disparage
词性: 动词
