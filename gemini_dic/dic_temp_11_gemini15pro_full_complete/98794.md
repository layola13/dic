integrations:/ˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃənz/| n. |集成，整合，一体化|The process of combining different parts or systems into a whole.  The act of uniting or bringing together, especially people of different races.|例句：The company is working on the integration of its new software. 公司正在努力整合其新软件。

例句：The school promotes the integration of students with disabilities. 学校促进残疾学生的融合。|近义词：combination, unification, amalgamation, incorporation, synthesis|反义词：separation, segregation, division, disunity|词性:noun

integrations:/ˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃənz/| n. |合并，归并|The act of combining two or more things together.|例句：The integrations of several smaller companies made the corporation stronger. 几家小公司的合并使公司更加强大。

例句：The report discussed the integrations of different data sources. 报告讨论了不同数据源的合并。|近义词：merger, consolidation|反义词：division, separation|词性:noun
