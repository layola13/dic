crouchingly:/ˈkraʊtʃɪŋli/
| adv. | 蹲伏地，蜷缩地 |  In a crouching manner; in a low, bent posture.  | The cat crouchingly approached the mouse. 猫蹲伏着靠近老鼠。|  stealthily, furtively, surreptitiously | openly, boldly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "crouching" (present participle of "crouch") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Crouch" comes from Old French "crochir" (to bend, hook), ultimately from a Germanic source.
