cannibalised:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪzd/| v. |拆用，挪用，（为制造或维修其他机器或设备）拆卸（另一机器或设备）的零件|To reuse (parts from an old or broken machine, vehicle, etc.) to build or repair another one.|The company cannibalised parts from older machines to keep the production line running. 公司拆用旧机器的零件来维持生产线的运转。|The airplane was cannibalised for parts.  这架飞机被拆卸了零件。| synonyms: strip, dismantle, salvage| antonyms: assemble, construct, build| verb
