eclipse:/ɪˈklɪps/ (US), /ɪˈklɪps/ (UK)| vt.|使黯然失色，使相形见绌|Her performance eclipsed all the others. 她的表演使所有其他人都黯然失色。|近义词：outshine, surpass, overshadow|反义词:be outshone, be surpassed|verb
