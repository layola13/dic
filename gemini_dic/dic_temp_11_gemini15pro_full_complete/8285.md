anti-lock:/ˌæntiˈlɒk/ (US) /ˌæntiˈlɑːk/| adj.|防抱死的，防滑的|A system that prevents the wheels of a vehicle from locking up during braking, allowing the driver to maintain steering control.|The car's anti-lock brakes helped me avoid a collision. 这辆车的防抱死刹车帮助我避免了碰撞。|Synonyms: non-locking, ABS (Anti-lock Braking System)|Antonyms: locking|Adjective

anti-lock:/ˌæntiˈlɒk/ (US) /ˌæntiˈlɑːk/| n.|防抱死系统|A system that prevents the wheels of a vehicle from locking up during braking.|The anti-lock failed, causing the car to skid. 防抱死系统失灵了，导致汽车打滑。|Synonyms: ABS (Anti-lock Braking System)|Antonyms: None (system itself doesn't have a direct opposite)|Noun


Etymology:  "anti-" (prefix meaning "against, opposing") + "lock" (verb meaning "to become fixed or jammed").  The term refers to preventing the wheels from locking up or becoming fixed in a non-rotating state.
