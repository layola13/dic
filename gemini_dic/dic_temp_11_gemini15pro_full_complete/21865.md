bodleian: /bɒdˈliːən/ | adj. n. | 博德利图书馆的; 博德利图书馆 | Relating to the Bodleian Library at Oxford University; the Bodleian Library itself. |  He spent hours researching in the Bodleian. 他在博德利图书馆花了几个小时做研究。 |  The Bodleian collection is vast and impressive. 博德利图书馆的馆藏浩瀚而令人印象深刻。 | 近义词: Oxford University Library (more general) | 反义词:  None (specific library) | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: Named after Sir Thomas Bodley (1545–1612), who restored and expanded the library at Oxford University.
