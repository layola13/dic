ecology: /ɪˈkɒlədʒi/ (UK) /iˈkɑːlədʒi/ (US) | n. |生态学；生态；社会生态学|The study of the relationships between organisms and their environment.  It encompasses the distribution, abundance, and interactions of organisms with each other and their physical surroundings.|例句：The ecology of the rainforest is incredibly complex.  雨林的生态系统极其复杂。

例句：Urban ecology is a growing field of study. 城市生态学是一个不断发展的研究领域。|近义词：environment, ecosystem, bionomics|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
