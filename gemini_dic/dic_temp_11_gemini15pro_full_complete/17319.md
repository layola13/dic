be-a-law-unto-oneself:/biː ə lɔː ˈʌntuː wʌnˈself/
idiomatic expression
我行我素；自行其是；独断独行
behaves independently and follows one's own rules, regardless of what is generally considered appropriate.
He's always been a law unto himself, doing whatever he pleases. 他总是独断独行，想做什么就做什么。
She's a law unto herself; she doesn't follow any rules. 她我行我素，不遵守任何规则。
synonyms:nonconformist, maverick, individualist, eccentric, free spirit
antonyms:conformist, follower, traditionalist
