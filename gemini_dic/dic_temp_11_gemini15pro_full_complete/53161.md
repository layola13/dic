dipole:/ˈdaɪpoʊl/| n.|偶极子；双极子|A pair of equal and opposite charges separated by a small distance.  A molecule having two such charges or poles.|The water molecule is a dipole. 水分子是一个偶极子。|synonyms: dipole moment, doublet|antonyms: monopole|noun
