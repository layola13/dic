monopyrenous:/ˌmɒnəˈpaɪrənəs/
adjective
单核的;单细胞核的
Having a single nucleus or formed from a single nucleus.
This species is characterized by monopyrenous conidia.  这个物种的特点是具有单核分生孢子。
The fungus produces monopyrenous ascospores.  这种真菌产生单核子囊孢子。
Near Synonyms: uninucleate, mononuclear
Antonyms: multinucleate, polynuclear
word type: adjective
