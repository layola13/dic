caudle:/ˈkɔːdəl/| n. |温热的甜饮料；产妇或病人喝的粥|A warm drink of thin gruel, often sweetened and spiced, given to sick people, especially women after childbirth.|She was given a caudle made with wine and spices after the birth of her child. 她生完孩子后喝了一碗用葡萄酒和香料调制的热饮。|近义词:gruel, drink|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "solid food")|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *caudel*, from Old North French *chaudel*, from Late Latin *caldellum*, diminutive of *caldus* (“warm”), from Latin *calidus*.
