hyperconservatives:/ˌhaɪpər.kənˈsɜːrvətɪvz/ | n. |极端保守派，超级保守派 | People or groups who hold extremely conservative political views, often characterized by resistance to social change and adherence to traditional values.  |  The hyperconservatives blocked the legislation for expanding healthcare.  极端保守派阻止了扩大医疗保健的法案。 |  The hyperconservatives in the party resisted any changes to the existing tax laws.  党内的极端保守派反对任何对现有税法的修改。 |  reactionaries, right-wingers, ultraconservatives |  liberals, progressives | noun
