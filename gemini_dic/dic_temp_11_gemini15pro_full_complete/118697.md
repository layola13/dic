metacentre:/ˈmetəˌsentər/| n.|稳心，定倾中心|The point of intersection of the vertical line through the centre of buoyancy of a floating body when upright with the vertical line through the new centre of buoyancy when the body is slightly tilted.  一个漂浮物体直立时浮力中心通过的垂线与物体略微倾斜时新的浮力中心通过的垂线的交点。|The ship's metacentre is calculated to ensure its stability.  这艘船的稳心被计算出来以确保其稳定性。|近义词:center of stability|反义词:None|词性:noun
