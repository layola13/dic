chiastolite:/kaɪˈæstəlaɪt/
noun
空晶石，十字石
A variety of andalusite containing carbonaceous impurities arranged in a cross-shaped pattern.
Chiastolite is a fascinating mineral with a distinctive cross-shaped pattern. 空晶石是一种迷人的矿物，具有独特的十字形图案。
This pendant features a polished piece of chiastolite. 这款吊坠的特点是一块抛光的空晶石。
Synonyms: cross-stone, macle
