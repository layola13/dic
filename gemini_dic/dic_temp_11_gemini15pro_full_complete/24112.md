brewer:/ˈbruːər/ (US) /ˈbrʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.|啤酒制造商；酿酒人|A person or company that makes beer.|例句：The local brewer produces a variety of craft beers.  当地啤酒制造商生产各种各样的精酿啤酒。

例句：He learned the trade from his father, a skilled brewer. 他从他父亲那里学到了酿酒这门手艺，他父亲是一位技艺精湛的酿酒师。|近义词：beermaker,  vintner (for wine)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

brewing:/ˈbruːɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbruːɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|酿造；酝酿|The process of making beer.|例句：The brewing process takes several weeks. 酿造过程需要几个星期。

例句：He's interested in the science of brewing. 他对酿造的科学很感兴趣。|近义词：fermentation,  production|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

brew:/bruː/ (US) /bruː/ (UK)| n. v.| 酿造；酿造物；混合物| (n.) A brewed beverage, especially beer or tea. (v.) To make (beer, tea, coffee) by mixing it with hot water, etc.|例句：(n.)  He ordered a dark brew. 他点了一杯黑啤酒。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *brēowan "to brew," from Proto-Germanic *breuwan, from Proto-Indo-European *bhreu- "to boil, bubble, effervesce."
