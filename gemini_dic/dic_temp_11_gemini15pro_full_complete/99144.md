intercessor:/ˌɪntərˈsesər/| n.|调解人，说情者，代祷者|A person who intervenes on behalf of another, especially by prayer.|The priest acted as an intercessor between God and the people.  牧师充当上帝与人民之间的调解人。|Synonyms: mediator, advocate, pleader|Antonyms: opponent, adversary|Noun

intercessor:/ˌɪntərˈsesər/| n.|说情者|Someone who pleads with someone in power on behalf of another person.|She acted as an intercessor for the employees during the negotiations. 在谈判期间，她为员工说情。|Synonyms: advocate, mediator, negotiator|Antonyms: opponent, enemy|Noun
