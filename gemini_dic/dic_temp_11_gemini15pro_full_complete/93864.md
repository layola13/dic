immoderately:/ɪˈmɑːdərətli/| adv.|过分地，过度地，无节制地|Excessively; beyond reasonable limits; to an undue degree.|She drinks immoderately, which is damaging her health. 她酗酒，这正在损害她的健康。|He spends immoderately on luxury goods. 他过度挥霍在奢侈品上。|近义词：excessively, unduly, inordinately|反义词：moderately, reasonably, temperately|词性:副词
词源: Late Middle English: from immoderate + -ly.
