oratorian:/ˌɔːrəˈtɔːriən/
n.
雄辩家，演说家；奥拉托利会会员
a person distinguished for skill and power as a public speaker; a member of the Congregation of the Oratory.
The renowned oratorian captivated the audience with his powerful speech.  这位著名的演说家以他强有力的演讲吸引了观众。
He joined the Oratorians and devoted his life to prayer and service. 他加入了奥拉托利会，一生致力于祈祷和服务。
近义词：speaker, rhetorician, declaimer
反义词：listener, audience
词性：noun

oratorian:/ˌɔːrəˈtɔːriən/
adj.
奥拉托利会的；雄辩家的
relating to or characteristic of an orator or oratory; of or relating to the Congregation of the Oratory.
The oratorian style of preaching emphasizes emotional appeal and dramatic delivery. 雄辩的布道风格强调情感诉求和戏剧化的表达。
He lived in an oratorian community, following a strict rule of life. 他生活在一个奥拉托利会社区，遵循着严格的生活准则。
近义词：rhetorical, eloquent
反义词：inarticulate, plain
词性：adjective


词源:  Late 16th century: from French oratorien, from late Latin oratorius ‘of an orator,’ from Latin orator.
