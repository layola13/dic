intersporal:/ɪntərˈspɔːrəl/ | adj. | 孢子间的 | Situated between spores.  Relating to the area or interval between spores. | The intersporal ridges are clearly visible under the microscope.  在显微镜下，孢子间的脊清晰可见。| The intersporal distance is crucial for dispersal. 孢子间的距离对于传播至关重要。 |  近义词: intersporogenous | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly intrasporal - within a spore) |词性: adjective
