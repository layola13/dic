bylining:/ˈbaɪˌlaɪnɪŋ/
n.
署名；冠名；署名文章
The act of putting a byline on an article.  Adding a journalist's name to the beginning of a story.
The article carried her bylining. 这篇文章署了她的名字。
attribution, credit
anonymity
