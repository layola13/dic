communal:/kəˈmjuːnl/
adjective
公共的，共有的，社区的
Shared by all members of a community; for common use.
例句：
The communal gardens are a popular spot for residents.
居民们都很喜欢公共花园。

They share a communal kitchen and bathroom.
他们共用一个厨房和一个卫生间。

近义词：shared, collective, joint, public
反义词：private, individual, personal
词性: adjective
