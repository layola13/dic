imbay:/ɪmˈbeɪ/| v. |嵌入，包埋|To enclose or embed something within something else.  The jewel was imbayed in the golden setting. 宝石被嵌入黄金底座中。 The ship was imbayed in ice. 船被冰封住了。|近义词：embed, encase, enclose, insert|反义词：extract, remove|词性: 动词

Etymology: From Old French *embaer, from en- "in" + baer "to gape, open wide". Ultimately from Latin *batare "to gape".  This sense evolved to mean "to enclose within a bay or recess," and then generalized to "enclose or embed."
