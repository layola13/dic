fissility:/fɪˈsɪləti/| n. |裂变性，分裂性|The property of being fissile, capable of undergoing nuclear fission.|Uranium-235 has high fissility and is used in nuclear reactors. 铀-235具有高裂变性，用于核反应堆。|Synonyms: cleavability, divisibility|Antonyms: stability, indivisibility|noun
