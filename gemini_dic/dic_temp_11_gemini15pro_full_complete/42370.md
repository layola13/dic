counterattackers:/ˌkaʊntərəˈtækərz/
noun
反击者，还击者
Those who launch a counterattack.  Those who attack in response to an initial attack.
The counterattackers quickly regained the lost ground. 反击者迅速夺回了失地。
The general praised the bravery of his counterattackers. 将军赞扬了他的反击者的勇敢。
synonyms: attackers, assailants, aggressors
antonyms: defenders, protectors
