discorrespondent:/ˌdɪskɔːrəˈspɑːndənt/
adjective
不一致的，不符的
Not corresponding or agreeing; incongruous; disagreeing.
The witness's testimony was discorrespondent with the physical evidence.  证人的证词与物证不符。
The two versions of the story are discorrespondent. 这两个版本的故事不一致。
近义词:inconsistent, discrepant, divergent, conflicting
反义词:consistent, concordant, corresponding, congruent
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation, lack, or reversal) + correspondent (agreeing, similar, or analogous).  The word "correspondent" itself comes from the Latin "correspondēre," meaning "to answer together" or "to agree."
