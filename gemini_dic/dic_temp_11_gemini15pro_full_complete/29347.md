castleward:/ˈkɑːs(ə)lwəd/
adjective
朝向城堡的
Facing or situated towards a castle.
The castleward side of the hill offered the best views. 山坡上朝向城堡的一侧视野最好。
近义词: castle-facing, towards the castle
反义词: away from the castle
词性: adjective

castleward:/ˈkɑːs(ə)lwəd/
adverb
朝向城堡地
Towards the castle.
They rode castleward. 他们骑马朝城堡的方向走去。
近义词: towards the castle
反义词: away from the castle
词性: adverb

castleward:/ˈkɑːs(ə)lwəd/
noun
城堡守卫；城堡方向
The defense or garrison of a castle; the direction or position facing a castle.
The castleward was strong. 城堡的守卫很坚固。
近义词: garrison, defense, castle guard
反义词: attacker, enemy
词性: noun

词源: From "castle" + "ward" (meaning "towards").  "Ward" comes from Old English "weard," related to Old High German "wart" (guard).
