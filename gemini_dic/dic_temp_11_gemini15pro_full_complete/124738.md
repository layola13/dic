mpegs:/ˈempeɡz/
noun
MPEGs (Moving Picture Experts Group files)
digital video files that use MPEG compression standards.  These standards are created by the Moving Picture Experts Group, hence the name.
Many video files downloaded from the internet are in MPEG format.
video files, movie files, digital videos, multimedia files
analog videos
n.
