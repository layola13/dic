confutable:/kənˈfjuːtəbl/
adjective
可驳倒的；可反驳的
Capable of being disproved or refuted.
例句：The theory was confutable based on new evidence.  这个理论基于新的证据是可以被驳倒的。

例句：His claims were easily confutable. 他的说法很容易被反驳。

近义词：refutable, disprovable, challengeable
反义词：irrefutable, indisputable, unchallengeable
词性:adjective
