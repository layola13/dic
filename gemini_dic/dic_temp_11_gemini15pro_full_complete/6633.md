anaesthesiologist: /ˌænɪsˌθiːziˈɒlədʒɪst/ (UK) /ˌænɪsˌθiːziˈɑːlədʒɪst/ (US)| n. |麻醉师|A medical doctor trained to administer anesthetics (drugs that cause temporary loss of sensation) before, during, and after surgery or other medical procedures.|The anaesthesiologist explained the procedure before the surgery. 麻醉师在手术前解释了手术过程。| The anaesthesiologist monitored the patient's vital signs throughout the operation.  麻醉师在整个手术过程中监测病人的生命体征。 |Synonyms: anesthetist (more common in British English)| Antonyms: None | Noun
