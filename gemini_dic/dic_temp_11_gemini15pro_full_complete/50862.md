deselection:/ˌdiːsəˈlɛkʃən/
noun
取消选择，撤销选择，落选
The act of removing someone from a position, typically one that they have been elected to.
The deselection of the candidate caused a lot of controversy.  候选人的落选引起了很大的争议。
The committee voted for the deselection of several members. 委员会投票决定取消几名成员的资格。
近义词：removal, dismissal, rejection, ousting
反义词：selection, election, appointment
词性:n.

deselection:/ˌdiːsəˈlɛkʃən/
verb (passive)
被取消选择，被撤销选择，被落选
To be removed from a position, typically one that one has been elected to.
He was deselected as a candidate after the scandal.  丑闻曝光后，他被取消了候选人资格。
She was deselected from the team due to poor performance. 由于表现不佳，她被从团队中除名。
近义词：removed, dismissed, rejected, ousted
反义词：selected, elected, appointed
词性:v.
