circumvene:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈven/ /ˌsər-​kəm-​ˈven/ | v. | 绕过，规避，回避 | To find a way around (an obstacle); To avoid (something) especially by using clever or dishonest methods. |  The company found a way to circumvent the regulations. 这家公司找到了规避规定的方法。

They circumvented the security system by disabling the cameras. 他们通过禁用摄像头绕过了安全系统。

| 近义词：avoid, bypass, evade, dodge, outmaneuver, sidestep | 反义词：confront, face, meet, comply with, obey | verb
