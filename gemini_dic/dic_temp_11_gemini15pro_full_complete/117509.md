meek:/miːk/| adj.|温顺的，谦恭的，顺从的|Quiet, gentle, and easily imposed on; submissive.|例句：She gave him a meek smile. 她对他露出了温顺的微笑。|例句: He was a meek and mild-mannered man. 他是一个温顺谦和的人。|近义词：gentle, mild, submissive, humble, unassuming|反义词：aggressive, assertive, bold, arrogant|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *mjúkr (whence also Danish *myg*), from Proto-Germanic *mjukiz (“soft, pliant, weak”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)meyg- (“to bend, turn, twist”). Cognate with Swedish mjuk (“soft”), Dutch muik (“meek”), German weich (“soft”).
