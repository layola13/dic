beltway:/ˈbeltˌweɪ/ /ˈbelˌtweɪ/| n.|环城公路；（尤指华盛顿的）首都环线；政治圈内人士|A highway that circles an urban area. Often used to refer to the Capital Beltway around Washington, D.C., and by extension, to the political insiders and culture associated with it.|例句：Traffic on the beltway was particularly heavy this morning. 今早环城公路的交通特别拥堵。

例句：The beltway is often used as a metaphor for the political establishment. 环城公路常被用作政治建制派的隐喻。|近义词：ring road, bypass, loop|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "city center" or "downtown" could be considered conceptually opposite) |词性:noun
