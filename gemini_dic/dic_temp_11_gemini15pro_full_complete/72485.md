fluor:/ˈflʊər/, /ˈflɔːr/ | n. | 氟 | A nonmetallic univalent element belonging to the halogens; usually a pale yellow gas.  It is the most electronegative element and the most reactive of all the elements. | Fluorine is used in toothpaste to prevent cavities.  氟被用于牙膏中以防止蛀牙。| synonyms: fluorine | antonyms: None | noun
