decries:/dɪˈkraɪz/
verb
公开谴责，强烈批评
to publicly criticize something as being bad, wrong, etc.
The report decries the lack of investment in education.  这份报告谴责了对教育缺乏投资。
He decried the government's handling of the crisis. 他谴责政府对这场危机处理不当。
synonym: condemn, denounce, criticize, censure
antonym: praise, commend, laud, extol
词性: 动词
