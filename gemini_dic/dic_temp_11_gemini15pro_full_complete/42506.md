counterinstances:/ˈkaʊntərˌɪnstənsɪz/
noun
反例；反证
Examples that contradict a general statement or rule.
This theory is disproven by numerous counterinstances.  这个理论被许多反例证伪了。
The professor presented several counterinstances to challenge the student's argument.  教授举了几个反例来反驳学生的论点。
synonyms: counterexamples, exceptions
antonyms: examples, proofs, confirmations
n.
