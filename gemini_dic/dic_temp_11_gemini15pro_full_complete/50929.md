desiccator:/ˈdɛsɪkeɪtər/| n. |干燥器，保干器|A sealed container containing a desiccant used for preserving moisture-sensitive items.  A desiccator creates a dry atmosphere for storing things that might be damaged by moisture.|The chemist stored the hygroscopic chemical in a desiccator.|Synonyms: drying chamber, drier|Antonyms: humidifier|noun
