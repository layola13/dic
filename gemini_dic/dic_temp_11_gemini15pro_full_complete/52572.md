diffusibility:/ˌdɪfjuːzəˈbɪləti/ | n. |扩散性；弥散性|The property of a substance that allows it to spread out or mix with other substances.  The diffusibility of a gas is much greater than that of a liquid.|The diffusibility of the new cleaning product makes it very effective.  这种新型清洁剂的扩散性使它非常有效。|Synonyms: dispersibility, spreadability|Antonyms: impermeability, impenetrability|noun
