lapideous:/ləˈpɪdiəs/
adjective
石头的；石质的；像石头的
Resembling stone; stony.  Made of stone.
The ancient statue had a lapideous appearance, weathered and gray.  古代雕像外观呈石头状，饱经风霜，呈灰色。
The explorers discovered a lapideous inscription on the cave wall. 探险家们在洞穴壁上发现了一块石刻。
synonyms: stony, rocky, petrous, lithic
antonyms: soft, fleshy, pliable
Etymology: From Latin *lapideus*, from *lapis* ("stone").
词性: adjective
