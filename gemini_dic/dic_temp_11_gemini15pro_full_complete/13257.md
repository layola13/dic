atelier: /ˌætəˈljeɪ/ (US), /əˈtɛljeɪ/ (UK) | n. |工作室；画室；工坊|A workshop or studio, especially one used by an artist or designer. |例句：The artist spent hours in her atelier, perfecting her latest masterpiece.  这位艺术家在她的工作室里花了几个小时，完善她最新的杰作。

例句：The fashion designer's atelier was filled with colorful fabrics and sketches.  时装设计师的工作室里摆满了五颜六色的布料和草图。|近义词：studio, workshop, workroom|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
