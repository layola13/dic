hiragana:/ˌhɪrəˈɡɑːnə/ |n.| 平假名 |Hiragana is one of two sets of kana, the phonetic writing systems of the Japanese language, along with katakana. It is used to write native Japanese words, and grammatical elements. | Hiragana is used to write okonomiyaki (お好み焼き). 平假名用来书写御好烧。|Synonym: None (as it's a unique writing system)|Antonym: None (as it's a unique writing system)|noun
