hicksite:/ˈhɪksaɪt/| n.|希克斯派教徒；希克斯派教友|A member of the Hicksite branch of the Society of Friends (Quakers).|Elias Hicks was a prominent Hicksite.  伊莱亚斯·希克斯是一位著名的希克斯派教徒。|The Hicksite meeting house was simpler than the Orthodox one. 希克斯派教徒的聚会场所比正统派的更简单。|近义词: Quaker (泛指所有贵格会教徒)|反义词: Orthodox Quaker (正统派贵格会教徒)|词性:noun

Etymology:
From the name of Elias Hicks (1748-1830), an American Quaker minister whose followers separated from the main body of the Society of Friends in 1827-1828.
