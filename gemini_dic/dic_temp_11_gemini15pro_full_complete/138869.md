nursingly:/ˈnɜːrsɪŋli/
adverb
护理地，照顾地，抚育地
in a way that shows care and concern for someone who is ill or injured or for a young child
She nursed her sick mother nursingly. 她细心地照料她生病的母亲。
近义词: tenderly, carefully, attentively
反义词: roughly, carelessly, neglectfully
词性: adverb

词源:
From nurse + -ing + -ly.  The word "nurse" comes from Old French "norice" meaning "wet nurse, foster-mother," which in turn comes from Latin "nutrire" meaning "to nourish, suckle, rear, bring up, cherish, foster."
