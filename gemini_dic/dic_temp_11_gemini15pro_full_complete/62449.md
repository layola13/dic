endorse:/ɪnˈdɔːrs/ /ɪnˈdɔːrs/| v. |支持，认可；签署；背书；赞同|To give support or approval to somebody/something, especially publicly.|The proposal was endorsed by the majority of members. 这项提议得到了大多数成员的支持。|近义词：support, approve, back, champion, advocate, sanction, ratify|反义词：oppose, reject, disapprove, condemn|verb

endorsed:/ɪnˈdɔːrst/ /ɪnˈdɔːrst/| adj. |被认可的；被签署的|Having received support or approval.|The endorsed candidate won the election. 得到认可的候选人赢得了选举。|近义词：supported, approved, backed|反义词：opposed, rejected, disapproved|adjective


词源:
From Anglo-French endosser, from Old French endosser "to write on the back of," from en- "on" + dos "back," from Latin dorsum.
