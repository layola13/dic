cubatory:/ˈkjubətɔːri/ /ˈkjubətəri/
adjective|孵化的，用于孵化的|Relating to incubation or brooding; used for incubation.|The zoo has a special cubatory for the penguin chicks. 动物园里有一个专门为企鹅幼崽准备的孵化室。|incubating, brooding|non-incubating, non-brooding|adjective

cubatory:/ˈkjubətɔːri/ /ˈkjubətəri/
noun|孵化场，孵化器|A place or device for incubation.|The farmer keeps his eggs in a warm cubatory. 农民把他的鸡蛋放在一个温暖的孵化器里。|incubator, hatchery| |noun
