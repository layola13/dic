cravat:/krəˈvæt/| n. |领巾，围巾|A piece of cloth worn around the neck, typically made of silk or linen, and tied in various styles.  Often worn as a decorative accessory or as part of a formal outfit.|He wore a stylish cravat with his suit. 他穿西装配了一条时髦的领巾。|近义词：necktie, scarf, neckerchief|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly open collar)|noun
