macroseismic:/ˌmækroʊˈsaɪzmɪk/| adj.|宏观的，大范围的地震的|Relating to the effects of an earthquake that are perceptible over a large area, such as changes in ground level or damage to buildings.  It describes the large-scale effects of an earthquake, observed over a wide area.|例句：Macroseismic observations helped determine the epicenter of the earthquake.  宏观地震观测帮助确定了地震的震中。

例句：The macroseismic intensity of the earthquake was VII on the Modified Mercalli scale.  这次地震的宏观烈度是修正麦加利地震烈度表的七级。|近义词：regional, large-scale|反义词:microseismic, local|词性:adjective

词源: From Greek *makros* (large) + *seismos* (shaking).
