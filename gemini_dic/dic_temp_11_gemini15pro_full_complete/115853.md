marksmanship:/ˈmɑːrksmənʃɪp/| n. |枪法; 射击术|The skill of shooting accurately.|Her marksmanship was outstanding in the competition. 她在比赛中的枪法非常出色。|近义词：accuracy, precision, proficiency|反义词：inaccuracy, incompetence|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From marksman + -ship.  Marksman comes from mark (target) + man.  The suffix "-ship" denotes skill or ability.
