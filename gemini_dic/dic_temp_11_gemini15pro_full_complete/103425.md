jitters:/ˈdʒɪtərz/| n. |紧张不安；神经过敏|A feeling of extreme nervousness. |例句：I always get the jitters before a big performance.  我在大型演出前总是会感到紧张。

例句：He had pre-wedding jitters. 他有婚前恐惧症。

近义词：nerves, anxiety, apprehension, uneasiness|反义词:calmness, composure, tranquility|词性:名词
