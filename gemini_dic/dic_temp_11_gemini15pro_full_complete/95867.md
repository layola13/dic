incorruptly:/ˌɪnkəˈrʌptli/
adverb
廉洁地，正直地
in a way that is not dishonest or immoral; in a way that avoids any illegal or morally wrong acts.
He served the public incorruptly for over thirty years. 他廉洁地为公众服务了三十多年。
近义词:honestly, ethically, uprightly, virtuously
反义词:dishonestly, corruptly, unethically


词性:adverb

词源:
From in- (not) + corrupt (from Latin corruptus, past participle of corrumpere "to destroy, spoil, bribe") + -ly (adverbial suffix).
