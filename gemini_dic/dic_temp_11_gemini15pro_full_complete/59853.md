ectostosis:/ˌɛktɒˈstəʊsɪs/
n.
外生骨疣，骨赘
An abnormal benign bony growth that projects outward from the surface of a bone.
Example: The doctor identified an ectostosis on the patient's tibia. 医生在病人的胫骨上发现了一个外生骨疣。

Example:  Ectostoses can sometimes cause discomfort or limit movement. 骨赘有时会导致不适或限制活动。
synonym: exostosis, osteoma
antonym:  (No direct antonym, but could consider bone resorption or osteolysis as opposite processes)
noun
