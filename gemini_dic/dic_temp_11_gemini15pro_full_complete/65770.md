everydaynesses:/ˈevriˌdeɪnɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
日常琐事；平凡生活
The quality or state of being everyday or commonplace; the ordinary routines and occurrences of daily life.
She found beauty in the everydaynesses of life, appreciating the small moments that often go unnoticed. 她发现了日常生活中平凡的美好，欣赏那些经常被忽视的小瞬间。
synonyms: ordinariness, commonplaceness, mundanity, routine, humdrum
antonyms: extraordinariness, unusualness, excitement, novelty, adventure
词性: 名词
