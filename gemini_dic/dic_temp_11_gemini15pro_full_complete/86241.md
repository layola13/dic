heatstroke:/ˈhiːtstroʊk/ (US) /ˈhiːtstrəʊk/ (UK)| n. |中暑|A condition marked by fever and often by unconsciousness, caused by failure of the body's temperature-regulating mechanism when exposed to excessively high temperatures.|Example 1:  The construction worker suffered from heatstroke after working outside in the scorching sun all day. 建筑工人整天在烈日下户外工作后中暑了。

Example 2:  Heatstroke can be fatal if not treated promptly. 中暑如果不及时治疗可能会致命。|近义词：sunstroke, heat exhaustion|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a medical condition)|词性: noun
