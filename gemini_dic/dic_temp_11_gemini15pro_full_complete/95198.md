inauspiciousness:/ˌɪnɔːˈspɪʃəsnəs/ | n. |不吉利，不幸|The state of being inauspicious; unfavorableness, particularly relating to an event or circumstance that is deemed unlucky or foreboding.|The inauspiciousness of the dark clouds gathering overhead suggested the picnic would be cancelled.  头顶乌云密布的不祥之兆预示着野餐会被取消。|Near synonyms: unfavorableness, ominousness, adversity|Near antonyms: auspiciousness, favorableness, prosperity|Noun
