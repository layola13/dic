effusively:/ɪˈfjuːsɪvli/
adverb
热情洋溢地；奔放地
In a manner overflowing with unrestrained emotion or enthusiasm.
She greeted him effusively, showering him with hugs and kisses. 她热情地迎接他，拥抱亲吻他。
He thanked her effusively for her help. 他热情地感谢她的帮助。
近义词: enthusiastically, warmly, passionately, exuberantly
反义词: reservedly, coldly, indifferently
词性: adverb

effusive:/ɪˈfjuːsɪv/
adjective
热情洋溢的；奔放的
Overflowing with unrestrained emotion or enthusiasm.
She gave an effusive welcome to her guests. 她对客人们表示了热烈的欢迎。
His effusive praise made her blush. 他热情洋溢的赞美让她脸红了。
近义词: enthusiastic, warm, passionate, exuberant
反义词: reserved, cold, indifferent
词性: adjective

effusion:/ɪˈfjuːʒən/
noun
流出；泻出；(感情的)迸发
An instance of giving off something such as a liquid, light, or smell.  An unrestrained expression of emotion.
There was an effusion of blood from the wound. 伤口大量出血。
Her effusion of gratitude was touching. 她表达的感激之情令人感动。
近义词: outpouring, outburst, overflow, discharge
反义词: suppression, restraint
词性: noun


词源: From Latin effusus, past participle of effundere "to pour out," from ex- "out" + fundere "to pour."
