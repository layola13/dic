drip-drying:/ˈdrɪp ˌdraɪɪŋ/
adjective
滴干的; 晾干的
The process of drying clothes by hanging them up to allow the water to drip off.
The shirts were drip-drying on the clothesline. 衬衫在晾衣绳上滴干。
近义词: air-drying, line-drying
反义词: tumble-drying, machine-drying
词性: adjective


drip-drying:/ˈdrɪp ˌdraɪɪŋ/
noun
滴干; 晾干
The act of drying something by letting the water drip off.
Drip-drying is more environmentally friendly than using a dryer. 滴干比用烘干机更环保。
近义词: air-drying, line-drying
反义词: tumble-drying, machine-drying
词性: noun
