antiliterary: /ˌæntiˈlɪtərəri/ | adj. | 反文学的，反对文学的 | Opposed to or hostile to literature or literary values; lacking literary qualities. |  He has a surprisingly antiliterary attitude for a professor of English.  他作为一名英语教授，却有着令人惊讶的反文学态度。 |  The politician's speeches were crude and antiliterary. 这位政客的演讲粗俗且毫无文学性。 | 近义词: philistine, unliterary, lowbrow | 反义词: literary, intellectual, highbrow | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From anti- + literary.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Literary" relates to literature.
