lablab:/ˈlæblæb/| n. | 腊豆, 扁豆，雪豆 | A tropical Asian vine (Dolichos lablab) widely cultivated in warm regions for its edible pods and seeds, also called hyacinth bean. |  Lablab beans are a good source of protein.  腊豆是良好的蛋白质来源。|  Lablab is a popular vegetable in many Asian cuisines. 腊豆在许多亚洲菜系中是一种很受欢迎的蔬菜。 | synonyms: hyacinth bean, bonavist bean, Indian bean, Egyptian kidney bean | antonyms: N/A | noun
