cue-in:/ˈkjuː ɪn/ | n. | 提示，暗示；(广播或电视节目中的)提示信号；(尤指演员的)入场提示 | A signal for someone to do something, especially a signal for an actor to begin speaking or doing something. | The director gave the actor a cue-in to start the scene. 导演给了演员一个提示，让他开始表演这场戏。| synonyms: signal, prompt, cue | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider perhaps "interruption" or "silence") | noun
