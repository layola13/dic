enisled:/ɪnˈaɪld/
adjective
孤立的；被隔离的
Isolated, as if on an island.  Surrounded by something that isolates or cuts one off.
The remote village felt enisled after the heavy snowstorm blocked the mountain pass.  大雪封山后，这个偏远的村庄感觉与世隔绝了。
The writer felt enisled in his study, cut off from the distractions of the city.  这位作家感到在他的书房里被孤立了，远离了城市的喧嚣。
Synonyms: isolated, marooned, secluded, insulated
Antonyms: connected, integrated, involved
词性: adjective
