flacks:/flæks/
| n.|公关人员，新闻发言人；吹捧者，宣传员|A person, especially a press agent, whose job is to promote or generate publicity for a person, organization, product, etc., sometimes by aggressive or dubious methods. |The movie star's flacks kept the reporters away.  这位电影明星的公关人员让记者们远离。|Synonyms: publicist, press agent, spokesperson, promoter|Antonyms: critic, detractor|noun


flacks:/flæks/
| v. |(informal) 做公关工作；吹捧|To act as a publicist; to promote or generate publicity.|He flacked for the new tech startup. 他为这家新的科技创业公司做公关。|Synonyms: publicize, promote, hype|Antonyms: criticize, denounce|verb
