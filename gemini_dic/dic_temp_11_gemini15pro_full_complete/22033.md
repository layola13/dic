boke:/boʊk/ | n. | (俚语)精神错乱; 精神恍惚 | A state of mental confusion or absent-mindedness. (slang) | After a long day at work, I was in a complete boke.  工作了一整天后，我完全精神恍惚了。| Synonym: daze, stupor | Antonym: alertness, lucidity | noun
