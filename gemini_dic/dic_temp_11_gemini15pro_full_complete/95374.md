incestuous:/ɪnˈsɛstʃuəs/| adj.|乱伦的，近亲相奸的|Characterized by or involving sexual relations between people too closely related to marry legally.  It also can be used figuratively to describe relationships that are too close or inappropriately intimate.|The play explored the incestuous relationship between the protagonist and his sister.  这出戏探讨了主人公和他妹妹之间的乱伦关系。|Synonyms:  inbred, endogamous|Antonyms: exogamous|adjective

incestuous:/ɪnˈsɛstʃuəs/| adj.|（比喻）排外的，封闭的|Figuratively used to describe a group or organization that is closed and exclusive, often to its detriment.|The academic department was criticized for its incestuous hiring practices, rarely looking outside its own ranks. 这个学术部门因其封闭的招聘做法而受到批评，很少从外部招聘。|Synonyms:  cliquey, clannish, insular|Antonyms: open, inclusive|adjective
