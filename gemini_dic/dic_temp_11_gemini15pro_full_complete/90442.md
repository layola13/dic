fighting: /ˈfaɪtɪŋ/ | adj. n.| 战斗的，斗争的；战斗，斗争 |  The word "fighting" can be used as an adjective to describe something related to combat or struggle, or as a noun referring to the act of combat itself. It derives from the verb "fight." |  Example:  The fighting spirit of the team was admirable.  团队的战斗精神令人钦佩。  They were engaged in fierce fighting.  他们进行了激烈的战斗。 | 近义词: combat, struggling, battling, contending | 反义词: peaceful, harmonious, conciliatory | 词性: 形容词, 名词


词源:
From Middle English *fightinge, verbal noun from fighten (“to fight”), equivalent to fight +‎ -ing. Cognate with West Frisian fjochtsjen (“fighting”), Dutch vechten (“to fight”), German fechten (“to fight, fence”).  Related to Old English feohtan.
