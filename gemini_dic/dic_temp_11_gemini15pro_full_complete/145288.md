overbraces:/ˈoʊvərˌbreɪsəz/| n.|大括号，花括号|A pair of upright curly brackets, { }, used to group a set of items or indicate a set in mathematical notation.|The overbraces enclose the set of prime numbers less than 10: {2, 3, 5, 7}. 大括号括起来的是小于10的质数集：{2, 3, 5, 7}.|近义词:curly brackets, braces|反义词:None|词性:noun
