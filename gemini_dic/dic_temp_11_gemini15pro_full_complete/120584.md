miror:/ˈmɪrər/ | n. |镜子；反映；真实的写照 |A polished surface that reflects images. A surface, especially of glass coated with a metal amalgam, that reflects a clear image.| I looked at myself in the mirror. 我照了照镜子。| synonym: looking-glass, reflector | antonym:  none | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mirour, from Late Latin *miratorium, from Latin mirari (“to wonder at, admire”), from mirus (“wonderful, astonishing”). Doublet of miracle.
