deadlier:/ˈdɛdliər/ (US), /ˈdɛdliə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更致命的，更危险的|Comparative adjective of 'deadly'.  It means "more likely to cause death" or "more dangerous".|The virus mutated into a deadlier strain. 病毒变异成了更致命的毒株。|The new variant is considered deadlier than the original.  这种新的变种被认为比原来的更致命。|Synonyms: more lethal, more dangerous, more perilous, more harmful|Antonyms: less lethal, less dangerous, safer, healthier|adjective
