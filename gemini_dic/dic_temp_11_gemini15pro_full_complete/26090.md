busily:/ˈbɪzɪli/| adv.|忙碌地，勤奋地|In a busy manner; actively engaged in work or activity.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed.|She was busily preparing for the exam. 她正忙着准备考试。|近义词：actively, diligently, industriously|反义词：lazily, idly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bisili*, from Old English *bisiglice* (“busily, diligently”), equivalent to busy +‎ -ly.
