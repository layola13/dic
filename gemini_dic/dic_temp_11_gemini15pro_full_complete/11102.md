archbishoprics: /ˌɑːrtʃˈbɪʃəprɪks/ | n. |大主教的职位；大主教的管区|The office and jurisdiction of an archbishop.  It can also refer to the collection of such offices or jurisdictions.  |The archbishoprics of Canterbury and York are historically significant.  坎特伯雷和约克的大主教管区具有重要的历史意义。|synonyms: dioceses (for bishops, broader term), sees, provinces |antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'parish' as a much smaller unit) | noun
