atacamite:/ætəkəˌmaɪt/ | n. |  氯铜矿，翠绿氯铜矿 | A bright green mineral consisting of a basic copper chloride.  It is found in arid and semi-arid regions as a secondary mineral in copper deposits. | Atacamite is a relatively rare copper mineral. 氯铜矿是一种相对稀有的铜矿物。

Atacamite forms as a weathering product of other copper minerals. 氯铜矿是其他铜矿物风化的产物。 | 近义词: paratacamite, botallackite | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) | 词性: noun
