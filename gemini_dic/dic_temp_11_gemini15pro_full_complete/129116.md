nixing:/ˈnɪksɪŋ/
verb
取消，否决
rejecting or stopping something
例句：The boss ended up nixing the project due to budget cuts. 老板最终因为预算削减而取消了这个项目。

例句：She nixed the idea of going out for dinner, preferring to stay home. 她否决了外出吃饭的想法，更喜欢待在家里。

近义词：canceling, vetoing, rejecting, scrapping
反义词：approving, accepting, confirming
词性: verb (gerund or present participle)
