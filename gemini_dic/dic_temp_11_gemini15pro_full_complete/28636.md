cargos:/ˈkɑːrɡoʊz/ (US) /ˈkɑːɡəʊz/ (UK)
n.
货物（cargo的复数形式）
Goods carried by a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.
The ship was carrying a cargo of bananas. 这艘船装载着一批香蕉。
The plane arrived with its cargo of emergency supplies. 飞机带着紧急物资抵达。
近义词：freight, shipment, merchandise, goods
反义词: N/A
词性: 名词

cargo:/ˈkɑːrɡoʊ/ (US) /ˈkɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)
n.
货物
Goods carried by a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.

词源: From Spanish *cargo*, from *cargar* "to load," from Late Latin *carricare*, from Latin *carrus* "wagon."
