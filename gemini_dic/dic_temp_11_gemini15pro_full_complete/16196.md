banlieue:/bɑ̃ljœ/ (French pronunciation)|n.|郊区，城郊|A banlieue is a suburb, especially one of the poorer residential areas outside a large French city.|Many young people living in the banlieues feel marginalized. 许多住在郊区的年轻人感到被边缘化了。|synonyms: suburb, outskirts, periphery|antonyms: city center, downtown|noun
