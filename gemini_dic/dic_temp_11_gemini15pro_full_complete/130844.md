nonappreciativenesses:/ˌnɒnəˌpriːʃiəˈtɪvnɪsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnəˌpriːʃiˈeɪtɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.|缺乏感激之情，不领情|The state of being unappreciative or lacking in gratitude; multiple instances of this condition.|Her constant nonappreciativenesses made him resentful. 她不断地不领情让他感到愤恨。|ungratefulness, thanklessness, lack of appreciation|gratitude, appreciation, thankfulness|noun
Etymology->
From non- + appreciativeness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "appreciativeness" is the noun form of "appreciative," meaning "feeling or showing gratitude or pleasure." Ultimately derived from Latin "appretiare" meaning "to set a price to, appraise."
