gladiator:/ˈɡlædɪeɪtər/| n. |角斗士；斗士|A Roman fighter who fought against other men or wild animals in an arena for public entertainment.|The gladiators fought bravely in the arena. 角斗士们在竞技场上英勇作战。|近义词：fighter, warrior, combatant|反义词:pacifist|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *gladiātor*, from *gladius* ("sword").
