extinguishing:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪŋ/
v. | 熄灭; 扑灭; 消除; |The firefighters are extinguishing the fire. 消防员正在灭火。|近义词：dousing, quenching, suppressing, smothering |反义词：igniting, kindling, lighting |词性: 动词
extinguishing:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪŋ/
adj. | 熄灭的; 扑灭的; |The extinguishing agent quickly put out the fire.  灭火剂迅速扑灭了大火。|近义词：smothering, suppressing |反义词：igniting, kindling |词性: 形容词


词源: From Late Latin extinguere, from ex- 'out' + stinguere 'to quench'.
