insightfulnesses:/ˌɪnˈsaɪtfʊlnəsɪz/ | n. | 洞察力，深刻的见解 | The quality of having or showing deep understanding; the state of being insightful. | Her insightful analyses of the market trends earned her a promotion. 她对市场趋势的深刻分析为她赢得了晋升。 | 近义词: perceptiveness, acumen, wisdom, understanding | 反义词: obtuseness, shallowness, superficiality | 词性: 名词 (plural)

词源: From insightful + -ness.  "Insightful" comes from "insight," which originated in the early 16th century from "in-" (into) + "sight" (the ability to see).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns indicating a state or quality.
