leitmotifs:/laɪtmoʊˌtiːfs/ | n. |主导动机；主题；中心思想|A recurrent theme throughout a musical or literary composition, associated with a particular person, idea, or situation.  A dominant recurring theme.|The composer uses leitmotifs to represent the different characters in the opera.|Synonyms: theme, motif, refrain, trope|Antonyms: variation, digression|Noun
