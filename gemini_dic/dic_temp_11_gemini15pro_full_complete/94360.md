implacability:/ɪmˌplækəˈbɪləti/
n.|无情；冷酷无情；固执|The implacability of the enemy's advance terrified the villagers.  敌军无情的推进吓坏了村民们。|
例句：
The judge was known for his implacability in sentencing.  这位法官以判决无情而闻名。

The implacability of her refusal left him no hope. 她断然拒绝，让他彻底绝望了。

近义词：relentlessness, inflexibility, ruthlessness, intransigence
反义词：mercy, leniency, compassion, flexibility
词性: noun


implacable:/ɪmˈplækəbl/
adj.|无情的；冷酷无情的；固执的|His implacable enemy vowed revenge. 他不共戴天的仇人发誓要报仇。|
例句：
Despite our pleas, he remained implacable. 尽管我们恳求，他仍然无动于衷。

She faced implacable opposition from her family. 她面临着来自家人的坚决反对。

近义词：relentless, unyielding, inexorable, merciless
反义词：merciful, compassionate, forgiving, yielding
词性: adjective


词源:
From Late Latin *implacabilis, from in- "not" + placabilis "able to be appeased," from placare "to appease"  来自晚期拉丁语 *implacabilis，由 in- “不” + placabilis “可被安抚的” 组成，源自 placare “安抚”。
