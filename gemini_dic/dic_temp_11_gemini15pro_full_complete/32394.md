chillest:/ˈtʃɪlɪst/| adj.|最冷静的，最放松的，最酷的|Superlative form of chill (adjective): most chill.  Describes someone or something extremely relaxed, laid-back, and not easily upset or excited.|例句：He's the chillest guy I know. 他是我认识的最冷静的人。|例句：That beach has the chillest vibe. 那个海滩有最令人放松的氛围。|近义词：calmest, most relaxed, coolest, laid-back|反义词：most stressed, most anxious, most uptight|词性:形容词
