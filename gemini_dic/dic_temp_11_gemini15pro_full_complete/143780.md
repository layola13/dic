outmouthed:/ˌaʊtˈmaʊðd/
adjective
大声说出; 坦率说出; 喧哗的;
Having spoken freely and frankly, sometimes indiscreetly.  Characterized by loud and unrestrained speech.
She outmouthed her opinion on the new policy, surprising everyone with her candor. 她坦率地表达了对新政策的看法，她的坦诚让每个人都感到惊讶。
He was known for his outmouthed pronouncements on any subject. 他以在任何话题上都大声发表声明而闻名。
近义词:outspoken, frank, candid, blunt, vocal, clamorous
反义词:reserved, reticent, quiet, subdued, inhibited

词性:adjective
