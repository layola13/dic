词源: From Middle English clensen, clensyn, from Old French clenser, from Late Latin clēnsāre, present active infinitive of clēnsō (“I cleanse, purify, make bright”), frequentative of Latin clēo (“I purge, cleanse, purify”).

注意事项:  Cleanse often carries a connotation of spiritual or ritual purification in addition to physical cleaning.
