marsupial: /mɑːrˈsuːpiəl/ /mɑːrˈsuːpiəl/ | n. adj.| 有袋动物；有袋的 | A mammal of an order whose members are born incompletely developed and are typically carried and suckled in a pouch on the mother's belly. |  例句：Kangaroos are marsupials. 袋鼠是有袋动物。| 近义词：pouched mammal | 反义词: placental mammal | 词性: noun, adjective


marsupial: /mɑːrˈsuːpiəl/ /mɑːrˈsuːpiəl/ | adj.| 有袋的 | Relating to or denoting marsupials. | 例句：The koala is a marsupial native to Australia.  考拉是一种原产于澳大利亚的有袋动物。| 近义词：pouched | 反义词: placental | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *marsupium, from Latin marsupium "pouch, purse," perhaps from Greek marsipos "small bag, pouch."
