off-grid: /ˌɒfˈɡrɪd/ (US) /ˌɒfˈɡrɪd/ (UK)| adj.|离网的，不依赖公共设施的|Not connected to public utilities such as the electricity grid, water supply, or sewage system.|例句：They live an off-grid life in a remote cabin. 他们在偏远的小屋里过着离网生活。|近义词：self-sufficient, independent, autonomous|反义词：on-grid, connected|词性:形容词
