benevolence:/bəˈnevələns/| n.|仁慈，善心，慈善|The king showed benevolence to his prisoners by ordering their release. 国王展现了他的仁慈，下令释放囚犯。|Kindness, generosity, charity, compassion, goodwill|malice, cruelty, hostility, malevolence|noun
Etymology->
From Old French *benevolence*, from Latin benevolentia (“well-wishing”), from bene volens, present participle of bene velle, literally “to wish well”. Equivalent to bene- +‎ volence.
