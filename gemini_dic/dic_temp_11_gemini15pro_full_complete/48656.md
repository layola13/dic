defensibility:/dɪˌfɛnsɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
可防御性；可辩护性；正当性
The quality of being able to be defended against attack or criticism.
The castle's defensibility was enhanced by its high walls and strategic location. 这座城堡的高墙和战略位置增强了它的防御能力。
The lawyer questioned the defensibility of the client's actions. 律师质疑委托人行为的正当性。

近义词:protectiveness, justifiability, vindicability
反义词:vulnerability, weakness

词性:noun
