leg-up:/ˈleɡ ˌʌp/| n.|帮助，提携；优势|A helping action; a boost or advantage. |My mentor gave me a leg-up in my career. 我的导师在我的职业生涯中给了我很大帮助。|

The internship provided a leg-up for her job search.  这份实习为她的求职提供了优势。|近义词：boost, assistance, advantage, head start|反义词：disadvantage, hindrance, setback|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the action of giving someone a boost by placing a hand under their leg to help them mount a horse or climb over something.  源于帮助某人上马或翻越某物时用手托其腿的动作。
