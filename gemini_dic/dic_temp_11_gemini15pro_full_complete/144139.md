outselling:/ˌaʊtˈsɛlɪŋ/
verb
超过...的销量
selling more than something or someone else
例句：Product A is outselling Product B by a large margin. 产品A的销量远远超过产品B。

例句：The new phone is outselling all its competitors.  这款新手机的销量超过了所有竞争对手。

近义词：surpassing, exceeding, beating
反义词：undersold, being outsold
词性：v.
