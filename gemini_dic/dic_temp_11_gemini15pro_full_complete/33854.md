circumrotary:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈroʊtəri/
adjective
旋转的，绕圈旋转的
Rotating around a central point or axis.
The circumrotary motion of the planets around the sun. 行星绕太阳的旋转运动。

近义词:rotary, revolving, gyrating
反义词:stationary, fixed, immobile
词性:adjective

词源:
From circum- (around) + rotary (rotating).
