kabbalistic:/ˌkæbəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|卡巴拉的；神秘的；玄奥的|Relating to or characteristic of Kabbalah, a system of Jewish mystical interpretation of the Bible.|Her jewelry incorporates kabbalistic symbols. 她的珠宝融合了卡巴拉的符号。|Synonyms: mystical, esoteric, occult, arcane|Antonyms: mundane, ordinary, common, exoteric|adjective
