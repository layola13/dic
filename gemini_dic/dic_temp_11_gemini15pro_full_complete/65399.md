euphemize: /ˈjuːfɪmaɪz/ | v. |委婉地说，用委婉语表达 | To express an unpleasant or offensive idea in a more pleasant or socially acceptable way. |  The government euphemized the war as a "police action". 政府委婉地将这场战争称为“警察行动”。

She euphemized her firing by saying she was "exploring new opportunities." 她用“探索新机会”来委婉地表达她被解雇了。 | 近义词: palliate, gloss over, whitewash, sugarcoat | 反义词:  blurt out, be blunt | 词性: 动词
