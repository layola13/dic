misrewarding: /ˌmɪsrɪˈwɔːrdɪŋ/ | adj. |  给予不当回报的；徒劳无益的；得不偿失的 | Giving a reward that is not appropriate or deserved, or not producing a satisfactory result. | The project was ultimately misrewarding, demanding a lot of effort for very little return.  这个项目最终得不偿失，付出了很多努力却收效甚微。 | Example:  Trying to please everyone is a misrewarding task. 试图取悦每个人是一项徒劳无功的任务。 | Synonyms: fruitless, unproductive, unrewarding, futile, vain | Antonyms: rewarding, fruitful, productive, profitable | adjective
