choicely:/ˈtʃɔɪsli/| adv.|精心地；优美地；讲究地|In a choice or carefully selected manner; with excellent taste.  Carefully selected; of fine quality.|She was choicely dressed in a silk gown. 她穿着精心挑选的丝绸长袍。|近义词：elegantly, exquisitely, meticulously, carefully, tastefully|反义词:carelessly, sloppily, crudely|词性:副词
Etymology: From choice + -ly.  "Choice" ultimately comes from the Old French word "chois" meaning "chosen."
