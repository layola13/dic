intomb:/ɪnˈtuːm/
verb
埋葬，安葬
To place (a corpse) in a tomb.  To serve as a tomb for.
The ancient king was intombed in a pyramid. 古代国王被安葬在一座金字塔里。

The miners were intombed by the cave-in. 矿工们被坍塌的洞穴掩埋了。
近义词: bury, inter, entomb
反义词: exhume, disinter
词性:v.
