baroquely:/bəˈrɒklɪ/ /bəˈroʊkli/
adverb|华丽地；繁复地|In a baroque style or manner, characterized by extravagance, complexity, and ornamentation.|The palace was baroquely decorated with gold leaf and intricate carvings. 宫殿装饰华丽，金箔和复杂的雕刻随处可见。|The music was baroquely ornamented with trills and runs. 这首音乐装饰华丽，充满了颤音和快速音阶。| 近义词: ornately, elaborately, extravagantly|反义词: simply, plainly, austerely|词性:副词

baroquely:/bəˈrɒklɪ/ /bəˈroʊkli/
adjective|巴洛克式的|Resembling or characteristic of the Baroque style, especially in art, architecture, or music.|The baroquely carved furniture was a testament to the craftsman's skill. 巴洛克风格雕刻的家具证明了工匠的技艺。|The opera was a baroquely staged spectacle. 这部歌剧是一场巴洛克式的壮观演出。|近义词: ornate, elaborate, flamboyant|反义词: simple, plain, austere|词性:形容词
