Characterized by being too heavy and slow-moving. | The overponderous machinery was difficult to move.  这台过于笨重的机器很难移动。 |  The professor's lectures were overponderous, full of jargon and long-winded explanations.  教授的讲座冗长乏味，充满了术语和冗长的解释。 |  cumbersome, unwieldy, ponderous, laborious | light, agile, succinct, concise | adjective
