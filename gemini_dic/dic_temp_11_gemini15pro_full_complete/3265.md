affirmant:/əˈfɜːrmənt/
n.|宣誓人；立誓人；确证者|A person who affirms something, especially in a legal context, rather than swearing an oath.|The affirmant signed the document in front of a notary. 宣誓人在公证人面前签署了文件。|synonym: deponent, witness|antonym: denier, objector|noun

affirmant:/əˈfɜːrmənt/
adj.|肯定的；确认的|Serving to affirm or confirm.|His affirmant nod showed he agreed. 他肯定的点头表明他同意了。|synonym: positive, confirmatory|antonym: negative, denying|adjective
