inventively:/ɪnˈvɛntɪvli/
adverb
创造性地，有创造力地
In a manner that shows creativity and originality.
She solved the problem inventively, using materials she found around the house. 她创造性地解决了这个问题，用的是她在房子周围找到的材料。
The artist inventively combined different mediums to create a unique masterpiece. 这位艺术家创造性地结合了不同的媒介，创作出一件独特的杰作。
creatively, imaginatively, resourcefully
unimaginatively, conventionally, dully
词性: 副词
