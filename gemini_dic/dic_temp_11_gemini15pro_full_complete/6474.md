ampulliform:/æmˈpʌlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
壶形的；瓶形的
Shaped like an ampulla; flask-shaped.
The ampulliform vase was a unique piece of art.  这个壶形的花瓶是一件独特的艺术品。
The plant had ampulliform leaves which collected rainwater.  这种植物有壶形的叶子，可以收集雨水。
近义词:flask-shaped, bottle-shaped
反义词:linear, straight
词性:adjective
