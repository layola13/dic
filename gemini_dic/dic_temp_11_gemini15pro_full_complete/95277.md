incapacitations:/ɪn.kəˌpæs.ɪˈteɪ.ʃənz/ | n. |丧失能力；无力；无能|The state of being incapacitated; inability to function normally.  The plural form of incapacitation.|例句：The incapacitations resulting from the accident caused significant delays.  事故造成的丧失能力导致了严重的延误。

例句：Multiple incapacitations within the team led to project failure.  团队中多人的丧失能力导致了项目失败。|近义词：disabilities, impairments, weaknesses|反义词：abilities, capabilities, strengths|词性: Noun (plural)


Etymology:
From Late Latin *incapacitatio, from Latin incapacitas (“incapacity”), from in- (“not”) + capacitas (“capacity”).
