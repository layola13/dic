durit:/ˈdjʊərɪt/| n. |硬质合金|A hard alloy, specifically a tungsten carbide alloy used for cutting tools. |The drill bit was made of durit, allowing it to cut through the tough metal.  钻头由硬质合金制成，使其能够切割坚硬的金属。|近义词：tungsten carbide, cemented carbide|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider softer materials like steel or iron) |词性:noun

词源：Durit is a trade name, likely derived from the German word "hart" meaning "hard."  It's often used generically to refer to tungsten carbide alloys, especially in certain European contexts.
