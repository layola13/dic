evangelism: /ɪˈvændʒəlɪzəm/ | n. |福音主义；传福音|The spreading of the Christian gospel by public preaching or personal witness.  The act of spreading good news or promoting a cause with fervor and zeal. |例句：
The church's evangelism program is very successful.
这个教会的传福音项目非常成功。

Her evangelism for the environmental cause inspired many.
她对环保事业的热情宣传鼓舞了很多人。

|近义词：preaching, proselytizing, advocacy, promotion |反义词：apathy, indifference |词性: noun
