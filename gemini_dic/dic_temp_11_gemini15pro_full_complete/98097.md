inquisitorship:/ɪnˌkwɪzɪtərʃɪp/
n.
职权；审判官的职位；调查制度
The position or office of an inquisitor; the system or practice of inquisition.
During the Spanish Inquisition, the inquisitorship held immense power. 在西班牙宗教裁判所时期，审判官的职位拥有巨大的权力。
The inquisitorship was abolished in the 19th century.  调查制度在19世纪被废除。
近义词: inquisition, investigation, scrutiny
反义词: tolerance, acceptance
