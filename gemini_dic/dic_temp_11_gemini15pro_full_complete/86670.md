hellfire:/ˈhɛlˌfaɪər/| n.|地狱之火；烈火；极度的痛苦|An intense fire; the fires of hell, often used figuratively to describe intense suffering or punishment.|The preacher warned of the hellfire that awaited sinners. 传教士警告说，罪人将面临地狱之火。|近义词：inferno, blaze, conflagration|反义词:paradise, heaven|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old English *helfire (attested only in compounds like helfirena ‘of hell-fire’), from hell + fire.  Cognate with Old Norse helviti.
