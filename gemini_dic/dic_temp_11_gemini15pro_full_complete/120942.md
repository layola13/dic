miscellany:/ˌmɪsəˈleɪni/ | n. | 杂集；混合物；各种各样 | A collection of different items, especially literary works or musical pieces. |  The book is a miscellany of poems, essays, and short stories. 这本书是诗歌、散文和短篇小说的杂集。| synonyms: mixture, assortment, collection, variety, medley, anthology | antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity | noun

miscellany:/ˌmɪsəˈleɪni/ | adj. | 各种各样的 |  Consisting of diverse things or members. | This shop sells miscellany goods. 这家商店出售各种各样的商品。| synonyms: diverse, various, assorted, mixed | antonyms: uniform, homogeneous | adjective
