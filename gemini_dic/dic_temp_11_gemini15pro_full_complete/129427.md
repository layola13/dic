nomineeism:/ˌnɒmɪˈniːɪzəm/
n.
任命主义；被提名人的身份或状态
The practice of appointing someone to a position or office, especially based on political patronage or loyalty rather than merit.
The governor's reliance on nomineeism has led to accusations of cronyism. 州长对任命主义的依赖导致了任人唯亲的指控。
近义词: patronage, cronyism, favoritism
反义词: meritocracy
词性: 名词
