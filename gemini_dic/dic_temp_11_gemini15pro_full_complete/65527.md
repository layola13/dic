eustyle:/ˈjuːstaɪl/| adj.|优美的，合比例的，(尤指)古典式样的|Having or showing good taste or style, especially in architecture, referencing classical Greek principles of harmony and proportion.  Specifically, it describes a column that rests directly on the stylobate (floor) of a temple without a base. |例句1：The Parthenon is a perfect example of eustyle architecture.  帕台农神庙是优美古典式建筑的完美典范。

例句2：The architect aimed for a eustyle design, emphasizing simplicity and balance.  这位建筑师的目标是一种优美的设计，强调简洁和平衡。|近义词：elegant, graceful, harmonious, classical, well-proportioned|反义词：clumsy, awkward, disproportionate, inelegant|词性:形容词
