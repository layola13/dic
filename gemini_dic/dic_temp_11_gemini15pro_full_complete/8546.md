anticholesterol:/ˌænti.kəˈlɛstə.rɒl/ (US) /ˌænti.kəˈlɛstər.ɒl/ (UK)| adj.| 抗胆固醇的，降低胆固醇的 | Relating to or intended to reduce levels of cholesterol in the body. |  Many doctors recommend an anticholesterol diet for patients with high cholesterol. 许多医生建议胆固醇高的病人采用抗胆固醇饮食。

This medication has anticholesterol properties. 这种药物具有抗胆固醇的特性。

|近义词：cholesterol-lowering, lipid-lowering |反义词:cholesterol-raising |词性:adjective
