cleared:/klɪərd/ | adj. | 清晰的，清澈的，无罪的，晴朗的 | free from obstruction or unwanted items; easy to understand; proven innocent; bright and sunny | The sky cleared after the storm.  暴风雨过后天空放晴了。|  clear, transparent, innocent, bright | unclear, opaque, guilty, cloudy | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cleren, from Old French cler "clear, bright, pure," from Latin clārus "bright, clear, famous."
