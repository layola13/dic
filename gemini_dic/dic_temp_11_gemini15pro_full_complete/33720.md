circinate:/ˈsɜːrsɪnət/ | adj. | 圆形的，环状的，卷曲的 | Arranged in a circle or spiral; rolled inwards from the tip. | The vine tendrils grew in a circinate pattern around the branch. 藤蔓的卷须绕着树枝呈环状生长。 | synonyms: coiled, curled, spiral, involute | antonyms: straight, uncoiled, unfurled | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *circinatus*, past participle of *circinare* "to make round, to turn around," from *circinus* "pair of compasses, circle."
