invoices:/ˈɪnvɔɪsɪz/
noun
发票; 账单
A list of goods sent or services provided, with a statement of the sum due for these; a bill.
Please send me all the invoices for this month. 请把这个月所有的发票寄给我。
bills, statements, accounts
credits
