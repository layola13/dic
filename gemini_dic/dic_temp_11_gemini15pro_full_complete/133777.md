nongeminate:/nɒnˈdʒɛmɪnɪt/
adjective
非双生的，非重叠的
Characterized by the articulation of two successive sounds without intervening closure or friction, especially of two successive vowels.
The phoneme /aɪ/ in "eye" is a nongeminate vowel sequence.  "eye"中的音素/aɪ/是一个非双生元音序列。
This language has many nongeminate consonant clusters. 这门语言有很多非重叠辅音丛。
Synonyms: simple, single
Antonyms: geminate, double
词性: adjective
