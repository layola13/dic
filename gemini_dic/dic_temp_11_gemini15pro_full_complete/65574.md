evacuates: /ɪˈvækjueɪts/ | v. | 撤离，疏散 |  To remove (someone) from a dangerous place. | The authorities evacuated the town before the hurricane hit.  当局在飓风来袭前疏散了镇上的人。 | synonyms: remove, clear, withdraw | antonyms: occupy, inhabit, populate | verb

evacuate: /ɪˈvækjueɪt/ | v. | 排空，清除 | To discharge or empty (something) from a place. |  He evacuated the air from the chamber. 他排空了腔室内的空气。 | synonyms: empty, discharge, void | antonyms: fill, load, pack | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *evacuare, from ex- "out" + vacuare "to empty," from vacuus "empty."
