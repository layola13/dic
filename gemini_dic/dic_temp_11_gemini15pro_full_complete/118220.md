mercantilely:/mɜːrˈkæn.tɪl.li/ /mɝːˈkæn.təl.li/
| adv. | 商业地，重商主义地 | In a way that relates to trade or commerce; from the perspective of profit or financial gain. |  The company acted mercantilely, prioritizing profit over environmental concerns.  这家公司以商业利益为重，将利润置于环境问题之上。 | Example: He approached the problem mercantilely, focusing on the potential financial rewards. 他以商业的眼光来看待这个问题，专注于潜在的经济回报。|Synonyms: commercially, profitably, tradingly | Antonyms: altruistically, charitably, philanthropically
|词性: 副词
