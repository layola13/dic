hygrodeik:/ˈhaɪɡroʊdaɪk/ | n. |湿度计；干湿表|An instrument that indicates both temperature and relative humidity.|例句：The hygrodeik showed a humidity of 60%. 干湿表显示湿度为60%。

例句：She checked the hygrodeik to ensure the greenhouse environment was suitable for the orchids. 她查看了干湿表，以确保温室环境适合兰花生长。
