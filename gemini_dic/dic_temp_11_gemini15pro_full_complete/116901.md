mayhaps:/ˌmeɪˈhæps/| adv.|或许，可能|An archaic or humorous adverb meaning "perhaps."  It is formed from "may" + "haps" (meaning chance, fortune).|Mayhaps we'll meet again someday. 也许有一天我们会再见。|Mayhaps she'll change her mind. 也许她会改变主意。|近义词：perhaps, maybe, perchance|反义词：certainly, definitely|词性：副词
