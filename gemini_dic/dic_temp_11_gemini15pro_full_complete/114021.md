magnanimous:/mæɡˈnænɪməs/| adj.|宽宏大量的；慷慨的；高尚的|Showing or suggesting a lofty and courageous spirit as well as a disposition to overlook injuries or insults; free from petty resentfulness or vindictiveness.|例句：He was magnanimous in victory, praising his opponent's skill. 他在胜利中表现得很大度，称赞对手的技术。|例句：She made a magnanimous gesture by donating her winnings to charity. 她慷慨地把赢得的奖金捐给了慈善机构。|近义词：generous, forgiving, noble, charitable|反义词：petty, mean, vindictive, unforgiving|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin magnanimus (“great-souled, high-minded”), from magnus (“great, large, big, important”) + animus (“mind, soul, spirit”).
