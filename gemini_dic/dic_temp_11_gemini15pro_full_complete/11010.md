arborist: /ˈɑːrbərɪst/ (US), /ˈɑːbərɪst/ (UK)| n. |树艺师，树木栽培家| A specialist in the cultivation and care of trees, shrubs, and other woody plants.  An arborist is knowledgeable about tree biology, diseases, pruning techniques, and safe removal procedures.| The arborist examined the oak tree for signs of disease. 树艺师检查了橡树是否有病害的迹象。

The city hired a certified arborist to manage the trees in the park.  市政府聘请了一位 certified arborist 来管理公园里的树木。|Synonyms: tree surgeon, tree expert, tree specialist|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
