chichan:/ˈtʃiːtʃɑːn/ | n. | 小鸡; 小孩; (尤指)小女孩 (often used as a term of endearment) | A term of endearment, often used for a young child, especially a girl.  It can also refer to a baby chick. |  例句：Look at that cute chichan playing in the park! 看看那个在公园里玩耍的可爱小女孩！

例句：The farmer's wife was feeding the chichans.  农夫的妻子正在喂小鸡。

| 近义词：child, kid, little one, tot, chick (for a baby bird) | 反义词: adult, grown-up | 词性: noun
