legal:/ˈliːɡl/ (US), /ˈliːɡəl/ (UK)| adj.|合法的，法律的|permitted by law; relating to the law|例句：It's not legal to drive without a license.  没有驾照开车是不合法的。

例句：The legal system is complex and often confusing. 法律体系很复杂，而且经常令人困惑。

近义词：lawful, legitimate, licit|反义词：illegal, illicit, unlawful|词性: adjective
