octennial: /ɒkˈtɛnɪəl/ /ɑːkˈtɛnɪəl/
adjective
八年的；每八年的；第八年的
lasting eight years; recurring every eight years
例句：The octennial festival drew visitors from all over the world.  这个八年一度的节日吸引了来自世界各地的游客。

例句：The town held an octennial celebration to commemorate its founding.  该镇举行了八年一度的庆祝活动以纪念其成立。
近义词：octogenarian (referring to a person), eight-year
反义词：annual, biennial
词性: adjective


octennial: /ɒkˈtɛnɪəl/ /ɑːkˈtɛnɪəl/
noun
八年庆典
an eighth anniversary or its celebration
例句：The city is preparing for its octennial.  这个城市正在准备它的八年庆典。

例句：The octennial of the museum's founding will be a grand affair.  博物馆成立八周年庆典将是一场盛大的活动。
近义词：eighth anniversary celebration
反义词：N/A
词性: noun
