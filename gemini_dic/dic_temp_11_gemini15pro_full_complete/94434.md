imploratory:/ɪmˈplɔːrətɔːri/
adjective
恳求的；哀求的
Making an earnest or desperate plea.
She cast an imploratory glance at the judge, hoping he would reduce her sentence. 她向法官投以恳求的目光，希望他能减轻她的刑罚。
Synonyms: beseeching, pleading, supplicating, entreating
Antonyms: demanding, commanding, insistent, assertive
词性: adjective
