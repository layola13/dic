five-spice:/ˌfaɪv ˈspaɪs/ | n. | 五香粉 | A mixture of ground spices, typically including star anise, cloves, cinnamon, Sichuan pepper, and fennel seeds. |  I added a pinch of five-spice to the marinade. 我在腌料里加了一撮五香粉。

Five-spice is a key ingredient in many Chinese dishes. 五香粉是许多中国菜肴的关键成分。
