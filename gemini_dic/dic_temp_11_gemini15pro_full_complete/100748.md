intransmutability:/ˌɪntrænsˌmjuːtəˈbɪlɪti/| n.|不可改变性，不变性|The quality or state of being incapable of being transmuted or changed.|The intransmutability of his beliefs made him resistant to new ideas. 他的信念不可改变，使他难以接受新思想。|Synonyms: immutability, unchangeableness, permanence, fixity|Antonyms: mutability, changeability, variability, fluidity|noun
