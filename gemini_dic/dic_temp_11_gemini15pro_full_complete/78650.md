giddy:/ˈɡɪdi/| adj.|头晕的，眼花的；轻率的，轻浮的|Feeling dizzy or unsteady, or behaving in a silly or frivolous way, often because of excitement or happiness.|The heights made him giddy. 高度让他感到头晕。|近义词：dizzy, lightheaded, frivolous, silly|反义词：serious, solemn, steady|词性:形容词

giddy:/ˈɡɪdi/| v.|使头晕目眩；使轻浮|To make someone feel dizzy or unsteady, or to make them behave in a silly or frivolous way.|The champagne giddied her. 香槟让她有些飘飘然。|近义词：intoxicate, confuse|反义词：sober, steady|词性:动词
