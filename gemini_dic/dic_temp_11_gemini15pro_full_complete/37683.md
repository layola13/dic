companiable:/kəmˈpeɪniəbl/ /kəmˈpænɪəbl/
adjective
友善的；好交际的；适宜作伴的
Enjoyable to be with; friendly and sociable.
She's a very companionable person and I always enjoy spending time with her. 她是一个非常友善的人，我很喜欢和她在一起。
近义词：sociable, gregarious, affable, amiable, congenial
反义词：unsociable, unfriendly, aloof, reserved
词性：adjective
