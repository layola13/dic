betrayer:/bɪˈtreɪər/| n.|背叛者，叛徒|Someone who betrays someone else's trust or is disloyal.|He felt betrayed by his closest friend, who was the betrayer of their secrets. 他感到被他最亲密的朋友背叛了，这个朋友是他们秘密的泄露者。

|近义词：traitor, defector, turncoat, quisling, renegade, backstabber|反义词:loyalist, patriot|词性:noun

betray:/bɪˈtreɪ/| vt.|背叛，出卖；泄露（秘密）；流露（感情）|To be disloyal to someone or something, or to reveal something that should be kept secret.|Her trembling hands betrayed her nervousness. 她颤抖的双手暴露了她的紧张。
|近义词：deceive, mislead, double-cross, sell out, reveal, disclose|反义词:support, protect, defend|词性:verb
