outfished:/ˌaʊtˈfɪʃt/
verb
捕鱼胜过；比…捕到更多鱼
to catch more fish than someone else
例句：He outfished his brother on their last fishing trip. 他在上次钓鱼之旅中比他兄弟钓到的鱼更多。

例句：She consistently outfishes everyone in the competition. 她在比赛中总是比任何人都钓到更多鱼。

近义词：outdo, surpass, beat
反义词：be outfished by

词性：verb
