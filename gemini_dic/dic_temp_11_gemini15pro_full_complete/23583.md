break-out:/ˈbreɪkaʊt/| n. |爆发，突围，越狱|A sudden escape from prison or confinement; a sudden increase or occurrence.|例句：There was a breakout at the local prison last night. 昨晚当地监狱发生了一起越狱事件。|例句：There has been a breakout of measles in the city. 该市爆发了麻疹。|近义词：escape, eruption, outbreak|反义词：imprisonment, containment|noun

break out:/breɪk aʊt/| v. |爆发，突然发生|To erupt or occur suddenly.|例句：War broke out in 1939. 战争于1939年爆发。|例句：A fire broke out in the kitchen. 厨房里发生了火灾。|近义词：erupt, occur, start|反义词：subside, end|verb
