comeback:/ˈkʌmbæk/| n.|回归，复出，反驳|a return to a former position of power, success, or fame; a quick and witty reply.|例句：The band made a comeback after ten years.  这支乐队十年后复出了。

例句：She had a witty comeback for every insult.  对于每一次侮辱，她都有机智的反驳。|近义词：return, revival, recovery, retort, reply|反义词:decline, downfall, departure|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From come + back.  First recorded in the early 20th century.  Initially meaning a retort or reply, the sense of a return to success developed later.
