jadelike:/ˈdʒeɪdlaɪk/
adjective
像玉的；玉一般的
Resembling jade in appearance or quality.
Her eyes were a jadelike green. 她的眼睛是翡翠般的绿色。
Near synonyms: jade-green, jade-colored, emerald, viridian
Opposite: dull, murky
词性: 形容词
