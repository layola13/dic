curassow:/ˈkjʊərəˌsaʊ/ /ˈkʊrəˌsaʊ/| n. |凤冠雉|A large, long-tailed, pheasant-like bird of the tropical forests of Central and South America.| We saw a crested curassow while hiking in the rainforest. 我们在雨林徒步旅行时看到了一只凤冠雉。|近义词：guan, chachalaca |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
etymology: From Spanish *curaçao*, ultimately from a Cariban language.
