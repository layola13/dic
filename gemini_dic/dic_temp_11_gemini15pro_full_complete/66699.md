exonerate: /ɪɡˈzɑːnəreɪt/ /ɪɡˈzɒnəreɪt/
verb
免除；赦免；证明…无罪
To clear from blame or accusation; to prove innocent.
The court exonerated him of all charges. 法庭宣布他所有罪名都不成立。
近义词: absolve, acquit, clear, vindicate
反义词: accuse, blame, convict, incriminate
词性: verb

exonerated: /ɪɡˈzɑːnəreɪtɪd/ /ɪɡˈzɒnəreɪtɪd/
adjective
免罪的；被证明无罪的
Cleared from blame or accusation; declared innocent.
He felt exonerated after the investigation proved his innocence. 调查证明了他的清白后，他感到无罪释放。
近义词: absolved, acquitted, cleared, vindicated
反义词: accused, blamed, convicted, incriminated
词性: adjective
