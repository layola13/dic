词源：From modern Latin *Chlamydiales (plural), from Chlamydia + -ales.  Chlamydia is from Greek *chlamýs, *chlamýdos ("cloak, mantle") + modern Latin -ia.  The bacteria were named this due to the appearance of the inclusion bodies they form within infected cells, which resemble a cloak.
