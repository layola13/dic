locator:/ˈloʊkeɪtər/ (US), /ləʊˈkeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|定位器；探测器；定位程序|A device or program that determines the position of something.|The GPS locator helped us find our lost dog. 全球定位系统定位器帮助我们找到了丢失的狗。|Synonyms: finder, detector, positioner|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "confuser" in a figurative sense)|Noun

locator:/ˈloʊkeɪtər/ (US), /ləʊˈkeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|定位符；标识符|Something that identifies or points out the position of something, especially in code or data. |The locator in the HTML code specifies the image source.  HTML代码中的定位符指定了图像来源。|Synonyms: identifier, pointer, tag|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms)|Noun
