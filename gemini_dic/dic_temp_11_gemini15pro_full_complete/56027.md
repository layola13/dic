doable:/ˈduːəbl/ (US), /ˈduːəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|可行的，可做的|Something that is doable is possible to do. |例句：This project is ambitious but doable. 这个项目雄心勃勃但可行。|例句：Climbing that mountain is doable with the right equipment. 有合适的设备，爬那座山是可行的。|近义词：achievable, feasible, possible, practicable, viable|反义词：impossible, impracticable, unachievable, infeasible|词性:形容词
词源:From do + -able, influenced by French faisable.
