mudfish:/ˈmʌdfɪʃ/| n. |泥鱼，弹涂鱼|A fish that lives in muddy water and can breathe air, often found in swamps and estuaries.  It can also move on land for short periods.|The mudfish wriggled across the mudflat. 泥鱼在泥滩上扭动着前进。|近义词：bowfin, dogfish|反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific type of fish) |noun
