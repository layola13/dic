fire-guard:/ˈfaɪəɡɑːrd/| n.|防火罩，火炉围栏|A metal frame placed in front of a fireplace to prevent burning embers from falling out and causing a fire. |例句：The fire-guard prevented the sparks from setting the carpet alight. 防火罩防止火星点燃地毯。

例句：Make sure the fire-guard is securely in place before leaving the room. 离开房间前，确保防火罩牢固地安装到位。|近义词：fire screen, spark guard|反义词: None|词性: noun
