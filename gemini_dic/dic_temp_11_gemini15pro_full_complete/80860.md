gourmand:/ˈɡʊərmɑːnd/ (US), /ˈɡɔːmənd/ (UK)| n.| 美食家；贪吃的人|A person who enjoys eating and drinking, especially to excess; a connoisseur of good food.|例句：He's a gourmand who loves to try new restaurants. 他是个喜欢尝试新餐馆的美食家。

例句：The gourmand savored every bite of the exquisite meal.  这位美食家细细品味了每一口精致的菜肴。

近义词：epicure, gourmet, foodie|反义词: ascetic|词性: noun


gourmands:/ˈɡʊərmɑːndz/ (US), /ˈɡɔːməndz/ (UK)| n.| 美食家们；贪吃的人们|Plural form of gourmand.|例句：The gourmands gathered for a lavish feast. 美食家们聚集在一起享用盛宴。

例句：The gourmands were impressed by the chef's culinary skills. 美食家们对厨师的烹饪技巧印象深刻。

近义词：epicures, gourmets, foodies|反义词: ascetics|词性: noun
