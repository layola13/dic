candidating:/ˈkændɪdeɪtɪŋ/
noun, verb (present participle)
参选，候选，申请
The act of being a candidate; applying for or seeking a position or office.  This word is less common than "applying" or "running for".
He is candidating for the position of mayor. 他正在竞选市长职位。
She is candidating for a seat on the board. 她正在申请董事会席位。
近义词: applying, running (for office), vying
反义词: withdrawing, resigning
词性: noun, verb (present participle)

Etymology: From the Latin word "candidatus," meaning "clothed in white," referring to the white togas worn by Roman office seekers.  The word evolved to mean "candidate" and the "-ing" suffix creates the present participle form "candidating."
