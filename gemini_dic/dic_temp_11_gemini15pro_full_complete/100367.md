interstitial:/ˌɪntərˈstɪʃəl/| adj. n.|间质的，间隙的；间隙物|Existing in the interstices or gaps between things, especially between the cells of a tissue.  Pertaining to, situated in, or forming an interstice.|例句：Interstitial fluid bathes the cells of the body.  组织液浸润着身体的细胞。|近义词：intervening, intermediary|反义词:continuous, solid|词性:adjective, noun

interstitial:/ˌɪntərˈstɪʃəl/| n.|间隙物，间隙广告|An advertisement that appears on a screen while a user is browsing online, often between pages or during a game level.|例句：The website displayed an interstitial ad before loading the video.  该网站在加载视频前显示了一个插页式广告。|近义词：pop-up, advertisement|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin interstitialis, from Latin interstitium "interval," from inter "between" + sistere "to stand."
