absences:/ˈæbsənsɪz/
noun
缺席，不在；缺乏，不存在
the state of being away from a place or person
例句：His absences from class are becoming more frequent.  他缺课越来越频繁了。

例句：The prolonged absence of rain caused a severe drought. 长期缺乏降雨造成了严重的干旱。

近义词：nonattendance, nonappearance, lack, want
反义词：presence, attendance, existence
