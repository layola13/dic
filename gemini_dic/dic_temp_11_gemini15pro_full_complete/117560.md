megadeath:/ˈmeɡədeθ/| n. |百万人死亡；大屠杀|One megadeath equals one million deaths.  A megadeath is a unit of measurement used to quantify the number of deaths caused by a catastrophic event, such as a nuclear war.|例句：The projected megadeaths from a nuclear winter are horrifying. 核冬天预计的百万人死亡数令人恐惧。

例句：The study estimated potential megadeaths from a global pandemic. 这项研究估计了全球大流行病可能造成的百万人死亡数。|近义词：mass death, mass fatality, holocaust|反义词: survival, life|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mega- +‎ death.  Mega- is a combining form meaning “large; a million; amplified by one million,” used in the formation of compound words (megawatt; megabyte; megaton);  Death is from Old English dēaþ.
