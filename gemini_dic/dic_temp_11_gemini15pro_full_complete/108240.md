laughingstock:/ˈlɑːfɪŋ stɒk/ (US) /ˈlɑːfɪŋ stɒk/ (UK)| n. |笑柄，嘲笑的对象|someone or something that seems stupid or silly and that everyone laughs at|例句：After his disastrous presentation, he became the laughingstock of the entire company.  他糟糕的演讲过后，成了全公司的笑柄。

例句：The team's poor performance made them the laughingstock of the league.  这支队伍糟糕的表现使他们成为了联盟的笑柄。|近义词：butt of jokes, object of ridicule, mockery|反义词: example, model|词性:名词
