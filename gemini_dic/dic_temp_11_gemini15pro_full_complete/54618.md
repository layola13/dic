dismemberer:/dɪsˈmɛmbərər/| n. |肢解者，分割者|A person who dismembers something, especially a body.|The police are searching for the dismemberer of the victim. 警方正在搜寻受害者的肢解者。|近义词：mutilator, butcher|反义词: assembler, uniter|词性: noun

Etymology:  From the verb "dismember" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Dismember" comes from the Old French "desmembrer," from "des-" (apart) + "membre" (member, limb), ultimately from Latin "membrum."
