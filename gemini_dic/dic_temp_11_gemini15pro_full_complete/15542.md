baggie:/ˈbæɡi/| n.|小塑料袋，密封袋|A small, thin, transparent plastic bag, typically sealed on three sides and used for storing food or other small items.|例句：I packed my sandwich in a baggie. 我把我的三明治装在一个小塑料袋里。

例句：He kept his loose change in a small baggie. 他把零钱放在一个小塑料袋里。|近义词：plastic bag, sandwich bag, Ziploc bag (trademark)|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly container, box)|词性:noun
