meretriciousness:/ˌmɛrɪˈtrɪʃəsnəs/| n. |俗艳；华而不实；虚有其表|The meretriciousness of the decorations was overwhelming, all glitter and no substance.  装饰的俗艳令人难以忍受，全是金光闪闪，毫无实质内容。|例句：The meretriciousness of the reality TV star's lifestyle was apparent in her constant need for attention.  这位真人秀明星生活方式的华而不实体现在她对关注的持续需求上。

The film's meretriciousness masked a weak plot and shallow characters. 这部电影的虚有其表掩盖了其情节的薄弱和人物的肤浅。|近义词：gaudy, tawdry, flashy, ostentatious, superficiality, showiness|反义词：simplicity, genuineness, authenticity, substance, understatement|词性:noun
