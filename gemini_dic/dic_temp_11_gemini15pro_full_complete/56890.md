dossel:/ˈdɒsl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːsl/ (US)| n. |华盖；帐幔；蚊帐|A canopy or hanging curtain, especially one placed over a bed, throne, or altar.|The king sat upon his throne beneath a rich dossel. 国王坐在华盖下的宝座上。

The bridal bed was draped with a dossel of white silk. 婚床披着白色的丝绸帐幔。|近义词：canopy, drapery, hanging, curtain|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "exposed")|noun
