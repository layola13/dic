disclusion:/dɪsˈkluːʒən/
n.
排除；不包含；去除
The act of preventing something from being included or considered.
The disclusion of certain evidence from the trial caused controversy.  从审判中排除某些证据引起了争议。

近义词: exclusion, omission, rejection
反义词: inclusion, admittance, acceptance
词性: 名词
