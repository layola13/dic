narcissisms: /ˈnɑːrsɪsɪzəmz/ | n. | 自恋，自我陶醉 | Excessive self-love or admiration.  A state of being excessively preoccupied with oneself and one's appearance, often at the expense of others.  Plural form of narcissism.  |  His narcissisms were evident in his constant need for praise. 他的自恋表现在他不断需要赞扬。| synonyms: vanity, egotism, self-centeredness | antonyms: humility, modesty | noun
