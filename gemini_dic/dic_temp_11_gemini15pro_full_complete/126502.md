myxomycetes:/ˌmɪksəˈmaɪsiːtiːz/ | n. | 粘菌 |  A group of organisms belonging to the kingdom Protista, commonly known as slime molds. They exhibit characteristics of both fungi and protozoa, existing as a mass of protoplasm called a plasmodium that can move and engulf food particles.  | Myxomycetes are fascinating organisms that can be found in damp environments. 粘菌是可以在潮湿环境中发现的迷人生物。 |  They observed the myxomycetes growing on the decaying log. 他们观察到粘菌生长在腐烂的原木上。 | Synonyms: slime molds, plasmodial slime molds | Antonyms: N/A | noun
