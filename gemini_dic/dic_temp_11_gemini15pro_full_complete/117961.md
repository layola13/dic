mementos:/məˈmɛntoʊz/| n. |纪念品；纪念物|An object kept as a reminder or souvenir of a person or event.|She treasured the mementos of her trip to Europe. 她珍藏着她在欧洲旅行的纪念品。|
例句：
The box was filled with mementos of her childhood.
盒子里装满了她童年的纪念品。

These photographs are my only mementos of that trip.
这些照片是我那次旅行仅有的纪念品。

|近义词：souvenirs, keepsakes, reminders|反义词:|词性:名词
