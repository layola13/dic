nonenviously:/ˌnɒnˈɛnviəsli/
adv.
没有嫉妒地，不带嫉妒地
Without envy; in a way that does not express or involve envy.
She nonenviously admired her friend's success. 她毫不嫉妒地钦佩朋友的成功。
近义词: ungrudgingly, generously
反义词: enviously, jealously
词性: 副词

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "envious" (adjective meaning "feeling or showing envy") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
