cyclostomi:/ˌsaɪkləˈstoʊmaɪ/ /ˌsaɪkləˈstəʊmi/| n. |圆口纲动物|A group of primitive jawless fish that includes lampreys and hagfish. They are characterized by a circular, sucker-like mouth.|Lampreys are cyclostomi that are often parasitic. 七鳃鳗是圆口纲动物，通常是寄生的。|近义词:Agnatha|反义词:Gnathostomata|词性:noun

Etymology: From the Greek κύκλος (kýklos, “circle”) + στόμα (stóma, “mouth”).
