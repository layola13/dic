gelatinously:/dʒɪˈlætɪnəsli/
adj.
胶状地；像果冻般地
Resembling gelatin; having a jelly-like consistency.
The jellyfish drifted gelatinously through the water. 水母像果冻一样在水中漂流。
The substance quivered gelatinously in the petri dish.  培养皿中的物质像果冻一样颤动着。
近义词:jelly-like, viscous, gooey
反义词:firm, solid, rigid
词性:adjective

gelatin:/ˈdʒɛlətɪn/
n.
凝胶；明胶
A translucent, colorless, brittle (when dry), flavorless foodstuff, derived from collagen obtained from various animal by-products.
Gelatin is used in making desserts. 明胶用于制作甜点.
He added gelatin to thicken the sauce. 他加了明胶来使酱汁变稠。
近义词:jelly, agar-agar
反义词:(none)
词性:noun

-ous:/əs/
suffix
...的；有...特性的；像...的
Used to form adjectives.
Dangerous 危险的
Famous 著名的
近义词:(none - depends on base word)
反义词:(none - depends on base word)
词性:suffix

词源:
From Late Latin *gelatinosus, from Latin gelatus (past participle of gelare "to freeze"), from gelu "frost, ice."  The suffix "-ous" comes from Latin "-osus," meaning "full of" or "having the quality of."
