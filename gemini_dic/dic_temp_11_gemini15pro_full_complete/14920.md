azole: /ˈæzəʊl/ /ˈeɪzɒʊl/ | n. | 氮唑 | A class of five-membered heterocyclic compounds containing three or four nitrogen atoms and at least one other non-carbon atom (usually nitrogen, sulfur, or oxygen) in the ring. | Azoles are commonly used in antifungal medications. 氮唑类常用于抗真菌药物。| 近义词: heterocycle | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a chemical structure) | 词性: noun

azole: /ˈæzəʊl/ /ˈeɪzɒʊl/ | suffix | 唑（后缀）| A suffix used in organic chemistry to denote a five-membered ring containing three or four nitrogen atoms and one or two other heteroatoms. |  The suffix "-azole" is used to form the names of many antifungal drugs. 后缀“-唑”用于构成许多抗真菌药物的名称。 | 近义词: (No direct synonym for a chemical suffix) | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical suffix) | 词性: suffix
