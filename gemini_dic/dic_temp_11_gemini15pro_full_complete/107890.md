larkers:/ˈlɑːrkərz/ | n. |  爱开玩笑的人，爱闹着玩的人 | People who enjoy playing pranks or having fun, often in a mischievous way. | The larkers tied tin cans to the vicar's car. 那些爱开玩笑的人把罐头绑在了牧师的车上。|  jokers, pranksters, mischief-makers | killjoys, sticks-in-the-mud | noun

larkers:/ˈlɑːrkərz/ | n. |  云雀（lark）的复数 | Plural form of "lark," a type of songbird. |  We saw a flock of larkers soaring above the fields. 我们看到一群云雀在田野上空翱翔。| songbirds | (No direct antonym for a type of bird) | noun
