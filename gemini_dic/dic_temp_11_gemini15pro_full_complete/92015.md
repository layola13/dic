hyperoxygenated:/ˌhaɪpərˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtɪd/ /ˌhaɪpərˈɑːksɪdʒəneɪtɪd/
adjective
过度氧化的；富氧的
Having an abnormally high level of oxygen.  The process of increasing the oxygen content beyond normal levels.
The patient was hyperoxygenated before the procedure.  病人手术前进行了过度氧合。
Hyperoxygenated blood can be toxic. 富氧血可能有毒性。
近义词:oxygen-rich, supersaturated (with oxygen)
反义词:hypoxic, anoxic
词性:adjective
