nonsurfaces:/nɒnˈsɜːfɪsɪz/
| n. |非表面；非表面物|Things that are not surfaces; typically abstract concepts or things lacking a physical form.|The artist's work explored nonsurfaces like emotions and memories.  这位艺术家的作品探索了诸如情感和记忆之类的非表面事物。|近义词:abstractions, intangibles|反义词:surfaces, physical objects|noun


nonsurface:/nɒnˈsɜːfɪs/
| adj. |非表面的|Not relating to or involving a surface.|The nonsurface aspects of the sculpture were just as important as its physical form.  这座雕塑的非表面方面与其物理形态同等重要。|近义词:deep, profound|反义词:superficial, surface|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + surface.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of". "Surface" comes from Old French "sur-" (over) + "face" (face).
