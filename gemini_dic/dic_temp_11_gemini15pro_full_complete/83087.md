词源:
From Middle English *guschen, *goschen, from Old Norse gjósa (“to gush, spout”), from Proto-Germanic *geutaną (“to pour”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰewd- (“to pour, libate”). Cognate with Old English ġēotan (“to pour”), Dutch gieten (“to pour, cast”), German gießen (“to pour, water, cast”), Danish gyde (“to pour, spawn”).
