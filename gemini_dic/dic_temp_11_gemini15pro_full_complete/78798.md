gilt:/ɡɪlt/ | adj. n. | 镀金的；金色的；金边证券；| Covered with gold or a gold-like substance; a gold-edged security. | The gilt frame enhanced the beauty of the painting.  镀金的画框衬托出这幅画的美。|  The government issued new gilts. 政府发行了新的金边证券。 | 近义词:golden, gilded | 反义词:tarnished, dull | 词性:形容词，名词


gilt:/ɡɪlt/ | n. | 小母猪 | A young female pig. | The farmer sold his gilt at the market. 农民在市场上卖掉了他的小母猪。| 近义词:sow, piglet | 反义词:boar | 词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gylt, from Proto-Germanic *gulþijaną "to gild," from *gulþą "gold."  Related to gold. Cognate with Dutch vergulden, German vergolden.
