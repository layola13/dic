bibulously:/ˈbɪbjələsli/| adj.|嗜酒地，海量地|Given to or marked by the consumption of alcohol, especially habitually or excessively; spongy, absorbent.|He led a bibulous life, frequenting bars and always having a drink in hand. 他过着嗜酒的生活，经常光顾酒吧，手里总是拿着酒。|近义词：alcoholic, drunken, intemperate, sottish|反义词: temperate, abstemious, sober|adjective

bibulously:/ˈbɪbjələsli/| adv.(rarely used)||In a bibulous manner.|He laughed bibulously, spilling his drink. 他大笑着，把酒洒了。|近义词：drunkenly|反义词: soberly|adverb (rarely used)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin bibulus ("drinking, absorbent") + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Bibulus is from the verb bibō ("I drink").
