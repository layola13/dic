continently:/ˈkɒntɪnəntli/ | adv. | 节制地，克制地，禁欲地 | In a manner exercising restraint, especially sexually; chastely. | He lived continently after taking religious vows. 他在发了宗教誓愿后过着禁欲的生活。| 近义词: chastely, abstinently, soberly, temperately | 反义词: incontinently, intemperately, immoderately | 词性: 副词

continently:/ˈkɒntɪnəntli/ | adv. |  (古用法) 持续不断地，连接地 |  Without interruption; continuously. |  The rain fell continently for three days.  雨持续不断地下了三天。(古用法，现今很少使用) | 近义词: continuously, constantly, perpetually | 反义词: intermittently, sporadically | 词性: 副词


词源:  From Late Latin *continenter, from Latin continens, present participle of continēre (“to hold together, contain, restrain”).
