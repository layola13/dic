hopperdozer:/ˈhɑːpərˌdోʊzər/| n. |料斗推土机；料斗平地机|A hopperdozer is a piece of heavy construction equipment combining a dozer blade with a large hopper for carrying material. It is primarily used for moving and spreading materials like topsoil, gravel, or other aggregates.|The hopperdozer efficiently spread the gravel across the construction site.|Synonym: hopper tractor,  dozer, earthmover|Antonym: N/A (lack of a direct opposite for a specialized piece of equipment)|noun
