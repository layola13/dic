disassembling:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛmblɪŋ/
v.|拆卸，分解|The process of taking something apart.
|例句：He is disassembling the engine to find the problem. 他正在拆卸引擎以找出问题所在。


|例句：Disassembling the computer requires patience and the right tools. 拆卸电脑需要耐心和合适的工具。


|近义词：taking apart, dismantling, breaking down
|反义词：assembling, putting together, constructing
|词性：动词的现在分词形式

disassemble:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛmbl/
v.|拆卸，分解|To take something apart.
|例句：I disassembled the clock to clean it. 我拆卸了钟表来清洁它。

|例句：The technician disassembled the machine to repair it.  技术人员拆卸机器进行修理。

|近义词：take apart, dismantle, break down
|反义词：assemble, put together, construct
|词性：动词


词源: dis- (apart) + assemble (to put together)
