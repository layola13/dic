conformate:/kənˈfɔːrmeɪt/ | n. | 整合体；整合油藏；整合矿床 | A geological formation, especially one containing oil or gas, in which the strata are conformable, having been deposited without interruption. | The geologists discovered a large conformate containing natural gas. 地质学家发现了一个蕴藏天然气的大型整合体。| 近义词: deposit, formation | 反义词: unconformity | 词性: noun

conformate:/kənˈfɔːrmeɪt/ | adj. | 整合的；整合层的 |  (of strata, formations, etc.) deposited without interruption. | The sedimentary rocks in this area are conformate, indicating a continuous depositional environment.  这个地区的沉积岩是整合的，表明这是一个连续的沉积环境。| 近义词: conformable | 反义词: unconformable | 词性: adjective

conformate:/ˈkɒnfɔːmeɪt/ | v. | 使一致，使符合 | To cause something to be similar or identical; to bring into agreement or conformity. |  He conformated his behavior to the company's rules. 他使自己的行为符合公司的规定。| 近义词: adapt, adjust, comply | 反义词: deviate, differ | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conformātus, past participle of conformāre (“to conform”), from Latin com- (“together”) + formāre (“to form”), from forma (“form, shape”).
