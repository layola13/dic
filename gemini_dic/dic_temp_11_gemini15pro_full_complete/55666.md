ditrochean:/daɪˈtrəʊkiːən/
adjective
双短长格的，双扬抑格的
A metrical foot consisting of two trochees.  A line of verse consisting of two trochees.
The poem is written in ditrochean tetrameter. 这首诗是用双短长四音步格写成的。
