dimetrodon:/ˌdaɪˈmɛtrədɒn/ /ˌdaɪˈmɛtrədɑːn/ | n. | 异齿龙 | An extinct reptile of the Permian period, characterized by a large sail-like fin on its back.  It's a synapsid, a group more closely related to mammals than to reptiles like dinosaurs. | Dimetrodon was a dominant predator of its time. 异齿龙是那个时代的顶级掠食者。|  Synonym:  pelycosaur (more general) | Antonym: None (as it is a specific extinct animal) | Noun
