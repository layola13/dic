interrailway:/ˌɪntərˈreɪlweɪ/
adjective
铁路间的，国际铁路的
Relating to or involving railways of different countries or systems.
The interrailway agreement facilitated travel between the two nations.  铁路间的协议促进了两国间的旅行。
Interrailway cooperation is essential for smooth cross-border transportation. 国际铁路合作对于顺畅的跨境运输至关重要。

近义词: international (regarding railways), cross-border (regarding railways)
反义词: domestic (regarding railways), national (regarding railways)
词性: adjective
