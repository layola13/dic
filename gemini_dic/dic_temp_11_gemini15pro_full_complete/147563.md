overpeopling:/ˌoʊvərˈpiːpəlɪŋ/ | n. |人口过剩，人口过多|The overpeopling of the planet is a serious concern. 地球人口过剩是一个严重的问题。| Overpeopling can lead to resource depletion. 人口过剩会导致资源枯竭。| 近义词：overpopulation | 反义词：underpopulation | 词性：名词

Etymology:  From over- +‎ peopling,  from people + -ing.  "Over-" signifies "excessively" or "too much," while "peopling" refers to the act of populating a place.  Thus, "overpeopling" literally means populating a place excessively.
