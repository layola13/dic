ouzos:/ˈuːzoʊz/ (US) /ˈuːzəʊz/ (UK)| n. |茴香酒（复数）|Plural form of ouzo, referring to more than one glass or bottle of the anise-flavored aperitif.|They sampled several different ouzos at the Greek restaurant. 他们在希腊餐厅品尝了几种不同的茴香酒。|Synonyms: aperitifs, anise liqueurs|Antonyms: None|Noun
