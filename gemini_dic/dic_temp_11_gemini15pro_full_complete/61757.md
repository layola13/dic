empathic:/ɛmˈpæθɪk/ | adj. | 有同理心的，感同身受的 | Showing an ability to understand and share the feelings of another. | She was very empathic when I told her about my problems. 她在我讲述我的问题时非常有同理心。| synonyms: compassionate, understanding, sympathetic | antonyms: unfeeling, apathetic, indifferent | adjective

empathic:/ɛmˈpæθɪk/ | adj. |善解人意的| Having the ability to understand other people's feelings and problems, especially because you have had similar experiences.| He gave her an empathic look. 他给了她一个充满同理心的眼神。| synonyms: sensitive, perceptive, insightful | antonyms: insensitive, callous, unsympathetic | adjective
