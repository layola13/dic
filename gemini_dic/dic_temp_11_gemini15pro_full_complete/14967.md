baaed: /beɪd/
verb
baited (past tense)
引诱，使上钩；激怒，逗弄
To deliberately annoy or taunt someone.  To entice someone into a trap or dangerous situation.
He baited the dog by waving a piece of meat in front of it. 他用一块肉在狗面前晃来晃去，逗弄它。
The police baited the criminal with a fake ransom drop. 警方用假的赎金引诱罪犯上钩。
近义词: provoked, taunted, lured, enticed
反义词: soothed, calmed, placated
