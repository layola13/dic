hyenic:/haɪˈiːnɪk/| adj.|鬣狗似的，残忍的，贪婪的|Resembling or suggestive of a hyena, especially in being cruel, greedy, or scavenging.|The hyenic laughter of the villain sent chills down my spine. 恶棍鬣狗般的笑声让我脊背发凉。|近义词：cruel, rapacious, predatory, greedy, scavenging|反义词：kind, generous, compassionate|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *hyaeninus, from Latin hyaena + -inus. Ultimately from Ancient Greek ὕαινα (hýaina).
