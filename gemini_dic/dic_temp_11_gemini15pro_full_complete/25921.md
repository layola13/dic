burningly:/ˈbɜːnɪŋli/
adverb
热烈地，强烈地
with intense emotion or conviction
She looked at him burningly. 她热切地看着他。
Synonyms: intensely, passionately, fervently, ardently
Antonyms: apathetically, indifferently, coldly
