fretwise:/ˈfrɛtwaɪz/| adj.| 横按式的，指板方向的|Concerning or relating to the frets of a stringed instrument, especially a guitar.  It usually refers to the direction parallel to the frets.|He played a fretwise glissando, sliding his finger along the string without lifting it.|近义词:horizontally (on a guitar), along the fretboard|反义词:lengthwise, vertically (on a guitar), perpendicular to the fretboard|词性:adjective
