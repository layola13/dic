fudgy:/ˈfʌdʒi/| adj.|软糖似的；浓郁的；湿软的|Having a dense, moist, and chewy texture like fudge.|The brownies were deliciously fudgy.|Synonyms: chewy, gooey, moist, dense, rich|Antonyms: dry, crumbly, cakey|adjective

fudgy:/ˈfʌdʒi/| adj.|含糊不清的；躲躲闪闪的|Vague or evasive.|His answers were fudgy and unconvincing.|Synonyms: vague, unclear, ambiguous, evasive, noncommittal|Antonyms: clear, precise, direct, forthright|adjective
