nonperceptiveness:/ˌnɒnpərˈsɛptɪvnəs/
noun
缺乏洞察力；感觉迟钝
lack of perceptiveness; inability to perceive or understand things clearly.
His nonperceptiveness led him to miss the obvious clues. 他的缺乏洞察力使他错过了明显的线索。
unawareness, insensitivity, obtuseness
perceptiveness, acuity, sensitivity
n.
