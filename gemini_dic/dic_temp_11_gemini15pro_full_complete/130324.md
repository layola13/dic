non-teutonic:/ˌnɒn tjuːˈtɒnɪk/ | adj. | 非条顿的，非日耳曼的 | Not of or relating to the Teutonic peoples or their languages. | The architecture of the building was distinctly non-Teutonic, showcasing Mediterranean influences.  这座建筑的风格明显不是条顿式的，展现了地中海的影响。|  The museum's collection includes non-Teutonic artifacts from around the world.  博物馆的藏品包括来自世界各地的非条顿文物。 | 近义词: non-Germanic | 反义词: Teutonic, Germanic | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix) from Latin "non" meaning "not"
Teutonic (adjective) derived from Latin "Teutonicus," relating to the Teutons, an ancient Germanic people.
