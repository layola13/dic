duffel:/ˈdʌf(ə)l/ | adj. |  粗呢制的 | Made of duffel fabric. | She wore a duffel coat. 她穿着一件粗呢大衣。 | synonyms: woolen, coarse | antonyms: silk, fine, smooth | adjective
