coy:/kɔɪ/ | adj. | 害羞的，腼腆的；忸怩作态的；故作神秘的 |  Reluctant to give details about something, especially out of shyness or coquettishness.  Also, suggesting something bad but in an indirect way. |  She gave a coy smile and looked away. 她害羞地笑了笑，然后把目光移开了。| synonyms: shy, bashful, demure, reticent, reserved | antonyms: bold, brazen, forward, immodest | adjective

coy:/kɔɪ/ | adj. | 狡猾的，躲躲闪闪的 |  Cunning, evasive, or deceptive. |  He was coy about his plans. 他对自己的计划闪烁其词。 | synonyms:  elusive, secretive, cagey | antonyms: frank, open, straightforward | adjective
