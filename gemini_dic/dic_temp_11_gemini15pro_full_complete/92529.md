hypoxia:/haɪˈpɒksiə/ (UK), /haɪˈpɑːksiə/ (US)| n.|缺氧|A deficiency in the amount of oxygen reaching the tissues.|例句：The climber suffered from hypoxia at high altitude. 登山者在高海拔地区 دچار کمبود اکسیژن شد.

例句：Prolonged hypoxia can cause brain damage. 长时间缺氧会导致脑损伤.

近义词：anoxia, asphyxia|反义词：hyperoxia|词性：名词
