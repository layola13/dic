glacierization:/ˌɡleɪʃiəˈreɪʃən/ |n.|冰川作用；冰川化|The process of becoming covered by glaciers or ice sheets.  The glacierization of the area carved out deep valleys.|例句：The glacierization of the valley resulted in the formation of a U-shaped valley.  山谷的冰川化导致了U形谷的形成。

例句：Scientists are studying the effects of glacierization on the landscape. 科学家正在研究冰川化对地貌的影响。|近义词：glaciation|反义词: deglaciation|词性: noun
