meanly:/ˈmiːnli/| adv.|卑鄙地，吝啬地，刻薄地|In a mean or ungenerous manner; poorly or shabbily.|He meanly refused to help his own sister. 他卑鄙地拒绝帮助自己的妹妹。|Synonyms: ungenerously, selfishly, basely, contemptibly, despicably, nastily, shabbily|Antonyms: generously, kindly, nobly, charitably|词性:副词

Etymology: From Middle English *menely*, from Old English *mǣnlīċe* (“meanly, vilely, basely, wickedly”), equivalent to *mean* + *-ly*.
