bereft:/bɪˈrɛft/| adj.| 丧失的，被剥夺的；感到失落的| Deprived of or lacking something, especially a nonmaterial asset. Feeling a sense of loss.|  The flood victims were bereft of all their possessions. 洪水的受害者失去了所有的财产。

The team was bereft of its star player due to injury.  由于受伤，该队失去了它的明星球员。


|近义词：deprived, bereaved, lacking, destitute|反义词：replete, full, rich|词性:形容词
