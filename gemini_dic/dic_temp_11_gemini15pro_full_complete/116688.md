maturely:/məˈtʃʊrli/ /məˈtʃʊərli/
adverb|成熟地，稳重地|In a manner characteristic of maturity; in a way that shows good judgment and understanding.|She handled the difficult situation very maturely.|近义词:responsibly, sensibly, prudently, wisely|反义词:immaturely, childishly, foolishly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "mature" + "-ly."  "Mature" comes from the Latin "maturus" meaning "ripe" or "timely."
