baths:/bæðz/
n.
浴室；浴缸；洗澡
Bathrooms or bathtubs; the act of bathing.
The house has two baths. 这房子有两个浴室。
The hotel offers private baths. 这家酒店提供私人浴室。
synonyms: restrooms, lavatories, washrooms, toilets
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object.  Perhaps "outside," or in context of cleanliness, "dirtiness" could be considered a contrasting concept.)
