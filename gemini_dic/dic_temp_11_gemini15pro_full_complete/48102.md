declinator:/ˈdɛklɪˌneɪtər/
n.|赤纬仪，磁偏角仪|An instrument used to measure the magnetic declination, or the angle between magnetic north and true north.|The surveyor used a declinator to determine the precise direction.|Synonym:  magnetic compass, magnetometer|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps  inclinometer which measures inclination)|noun

n.|（语法）变格词尾|A suffix or ending of a noun, pronoun, or adjective indicating its grammatical case, number, and gender.|Latin uses declinators to show the function of words in a sentence.|Synonym:  inflection, case ending|Antonym:  (No direct antonym)|noun
