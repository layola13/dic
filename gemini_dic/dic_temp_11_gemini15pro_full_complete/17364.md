beadhouse:/ˈbiːdhaʊs/| n.|救济院，养老院|A charitable institution providing housing for elderly people, often founded by a benefactor.|The old woman lived in a beadhouse founded by a wealthy merchant.  这位老妇人住在一位富商创办的养老院里。|Synonyms: almshouse, poorhouse, hospice|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: private residence, luxury apartment)|noun
