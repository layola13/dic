arillus:/əˈrɪləs/| n.|假种皮|A fleshy outgrowth from the seed coat that partially or completely covers the seed.  It is often brightly colored and may aid in seed dispersal by attracting animals.|The yew tree's seed is encased in a bright red arillus.|近义词:seed covering, sarcotesta|反义词:|名词
