hallucinates:/həˈluːsɪneɪts/ | v. | 幻觉，产生幻觉 | To experience hallucinations; to see, hear, or sense things that are not really there. | The patient hallucinates due to high fever.  病人因为高烧而出现幻觉。

The drug caused him to hallucinate vividly.  这种药物使他产生生动的幻觉。

| 近义词:  imagine, envision, fantasize, perceive, see things | 反义词: perceive clearly, see realistically | 词性: verb
