noncurdling:/ˌnɒnˈkɜːdlɪŋ/ | adj. | 不凝固的，抗凝结的 | Not curdling; preventing curdling.  This describes something, typically a substance or ingredient, that prevents milk or other liquids from separating into curds and whey. | This recipe uses a noncurdling milk substitute.  这个食谱使用了一种不凝固的牛奶替代品。

This noncurdling yogurt is perfect for smoothies.  这种不凝结的酸奶非常适合做冰沙。 | 近义词: non-coagulating, stabilizing | 反义词: curdling, coagulating | 词性:形容词


Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + curdling (present participle of the verb "curdle," from Middle English *crudlen, frequentative of crudden "to curdle," probably from Old Norse krydda "to crowd, press together").
