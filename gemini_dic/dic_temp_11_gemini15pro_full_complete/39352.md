conjugacy:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡəsi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːndʒʊɡəsi/ (US)| n. |共轭性，共轭关系|The state or condition of being conjugate.  It often refers to a relationship between elements of a group where one element can be transformed into another by an operation within the group.|The conjugacy of the two matrices is demonstrated through a similarity transformation.|近义词：relationship, connection, association|反义词:dissociation|noun
