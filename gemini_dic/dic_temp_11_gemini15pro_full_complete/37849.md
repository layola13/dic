competitively:/kəmˈpɛtɪtɪvli/
adverb
竞争地；好竞争地；有竞争力地
In a way that involves competition.
They played competitively throughout the tournament. 他们在整个锦标赛中都表现得很有竞争力。
近义词: aggressively, fiercely, vigorously
反义词: cooperatively, noncompetitively
词性: adverb

词源: From compete (v.) + -itive (adj. suffix) + -ly (adv. suffix).  Ultimately from Latin competere "to strive in common, come together, agree, suit," later "co-incide, fall together, happen at the same time," from com- "together" + petere "to go to, seek, strive after."
