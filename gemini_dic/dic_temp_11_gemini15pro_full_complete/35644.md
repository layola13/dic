coauthorships:/koʊˈɔːθərʃɪps/
n.
共同作者身份；共同著作；合著关系
The project resulted in several coauthorships between the researchers. 这个项目促成了研究人员之间的几次合作。
This paper explores the dynamics of coauthorships in academic research.  这篇文章探讨了学术研究中合著关系的动态。
Synonyms: joint authorships, collaborations, partnerships
Antonyms: sole authorship, independent work
词性: 名词
