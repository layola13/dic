acted:/ˈæktɪd/| v.|扮演；行动；表现；充当|Past tense and past participle of "act".  Meaning performed a role, behaved in a certain way, or carried out a function. |She acted surprised when she saw the gift. 她看到礼物时装作很惊讶。|synonyms: performed, behaved, functioned, reacted, pretended|antonyms: refrained, idled, ignored|verb

act:/ækt/| n. v.|法令；行为；行动；假装；表演；扮演|To do something; to perform a particular role or function.  As a noun, it refers to a thing done, a deed, or a part of a play.|He acted bravely in the face of danger. 面对危险，他表现得很勇敢。
The second act of the play was more exciting. 这部剧的第二幕更精彩。|synonyms: perform, behave, deed, law, statute|antonyms: inaction, refrain, idleness|noun, verb
