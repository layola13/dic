dysaesthesia:/dɪsɪsˈθiːʒə/ /dɪsɪsˈθiːziə/ | n. |感觉迟钝，感觉异常|An unpleasant abnormal sensation, typically tingling, burning, or prickling.| Example: Chemotherapy caused her to experience dysaesthesia in her hands and feet. 化疗导致她手脚感觉异常。| Example:  He complained of dysaesthesia in his legs, describing it as a constant burning sensation. 他抱怨腿部感觉异常，描述为持续的灼烧感。|Synonym: paresthesia, neuropathy| Antonym:  normal sensation | noun
