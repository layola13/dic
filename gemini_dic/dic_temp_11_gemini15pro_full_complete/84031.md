| adv. | 半期待地，略带期待地 |  With a mixture of hope and doubt; anticipating something but not fully convinced it will happen. | She glanced half-expectantly towards the door, hoping he would appear. 她半期待地朝门口瞥了一眼，希望他会出现。| synonyms: hopefully, somewhat hopefully, with some anticipation | antonyms: without expectation, despairingly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" + "expectantly."  "Half" meaning partially or to some degree, and "expectantly" being the adverbial form of "expect," which comes from the Latin "expectare" (to look out for, await).
