feeing:/ˈfiːɪŋ/| n.|喂食; 喂养; 喂养费; 饲养费|the act of giving food to a person or animal|例句：The feeing of the animals takes place twice a day. 动物一天喂两次。|例句：The farmer is responsible for the feeing and care of the livestock. 农民负责牲畜的喂养和照料。|近义词：feeding, nourishment, sustenance|反义词：starvation|词性：名词

词源: From the verb "feed," ultimately from Old English *fēdan*.

注意事项:

While "feeing" is grammatically correct, "feeding" is much more commonly used.  "Feeing" often appears in older texts or in very specific contexts related to payment for services, especially in legal contexts.  For general usage related to providing food, "feeding" is the preferred term.
