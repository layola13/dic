levelnesses:/ˈlɛvəlnəsɪz/
n.
平坦；水平；稳定；镇定
The levelness of the playing field is crucial for a fair game.  比赛场地的平整度对于公平比赛至关重要。
Her levelness of temperament impressed everyone during the crisis. 在危机期间，她沉着冷静的气质给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
Synonyms: evenness, flatness, stability, calmness, composure
Antonyms: unevenness, roughness, instability, agitation, anxiety
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From level + -ness.  Level (n.) comes from Old French livel, ultimately from Latin libella "level, plummet," diminutive of libra "balance, scales."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
