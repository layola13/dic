gallipoli:/ɡəˈlɪpəli/ | n. | 加里波利 | A peninsula in European Turkey, forming the western side of the Dardanelles.  It was the scene of heavy fighting in 1915 between Turkish and Allied forces. | The Gallipoli campaign was a disastrous defeat for the Allies. 加里波利战役是盟军的惨败。| Synonym:  Dardanelles (often used interchangeably, though technically the Dardanelles is the strait, Gallipoli the peninsula) | Antonym:  N/A | noun

gallipoli:/ɡəˈlɪpəli/ | adj. (less common) | 加里波利的; 与加里波利相关的 | Relating to the Gallipoli campaign or peninsula. | The Gallipoli landings were a key moment in World War I. 加里波利登陆是第一次世界大战中的关键时刻。| Synonym: Dardanelles (in some contexts) | Antonym: N/A | adjective
