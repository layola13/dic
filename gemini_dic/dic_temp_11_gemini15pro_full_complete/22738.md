boroglyceride:/ˌbɔːroʊˈɡlɪsəraɪd/
n.|硼酸甘油酯|A compound formed by heating boric acid with glycerin. It is used as an antiseptic and preservative.|Boroglyceride is commonly used in pharmaceutical preparations.|近义词:glycerin borate, boric acid glycerin|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
Etymology:  "boro-" (from boric acid) + "glyceride" (a compound of glycerol)
