conscientious:/ˌkɒnʃiˈenʃəs/ /ˌkɑːnʃiˈenʃəs/| adj.|认真的，勤勉的，尽责的|Guided by or in accordance with the dictates of conscience; principled.  Meticulous; careful.|She is a conscientious student who always does her homework. 她是一位认真的学生，总是认真完成作业。|例句：He is a conscientious worker, always striving to do his best. 他是一个尽责的员工，总是力求做到最好。|近义词：meticulous, diligent, thorough, careful, scrupulous|反义词：careless, negligent, remiss, irresponsible|词性:形容词
