objectivenesses: /əbˈdʒɛktɪvnɪsɪz/ | n. |客观性（复数）| The state or quality of being objective; lack of bias or prejudice.|  The scientist maintained utmost objectivenesses in his research, meticulously documenting every step and avoiding personal interpretations. 科学家在研究中保持了最大的客观性，一丝不苟地记录每一步，避免个人解释。

The judge's objectivenesses in handling the case was questioned due to his prior relationship with the defendant.  由于法官此前与被告的关系，他在处理此案时的客观性受到了质疑。| 近义词: impartiality, neutrality, fairness | 反义词: subjectivity, bias, prejudice | 词性: 名词

词源: From objective + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Objective" comes from Late Latin objectivus, meaning "pertaining to an object presented to the mind."
