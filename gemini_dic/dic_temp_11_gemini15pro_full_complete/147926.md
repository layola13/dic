overreliance:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlaɪəns/
n.
过度依赖，过分信赖
Excessive dependence on something or someone.
The company's overreliance on a single supplier caused problems when the supplier went bankrupt.  这家公司过度依赖单一供应商，导致该供应商破产时出现问题。
Overreliance on technology can sometimes hinder creativity. 对科技的过度依赖有时会阻碍创造力。
dependence, overdependence
independence, self-reliance
noun
