cubo:/ˈkuːboʊ/ (US), /ˈkjuːboʊ/ (UK)| n.| 立方体；立方；三次幂 |A three-dimensional shape with six square faces. | The volume of a cubo is calculated by multiplying its length, width, and height.  立方体的体积是通过将其长度、宽度和高度相乘计算得出的。| cube, block | sphere, pyramid | noun
