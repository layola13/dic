murderous:/ˈmɜːrdərəs/| adj.|凶残的；杀人的；蓄意谋杀的|Characterized by or involving murder; likely to murder. |例句：He gave her a murderous look. 他恶狠狠地瞪了她一眼。

例句：She had murderous thoughts. 她动了杀机。|近义词： homicidal, bloodthirsty, savage, brutal|反义词:  peaceful, gentle, kind|词性: adjective
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French *murdereus (from murdre ‘murder’) + -ous.
