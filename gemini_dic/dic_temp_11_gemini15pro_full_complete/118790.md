metallurgy:/ˈmɛtəlɜːrdʒi/ (US), /məˈtælərdʒi/ (UK)| n.|冶金学；冶金术|The science and technology of extracting metals from their ores, refining them, and preparing them for use.|例句：He studied metallurgy in university. 他在大学学习冶金学。|例句：Advances in metallurgy have led to the development of stronger and lighter alloys. 冶金学的进步导致了更坚固、更轻的合金的发展。|近义词：metalworking, smelting|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *métallurgie*, from Ancient Greek *μεταλλουργία* (metallourgía, “working of metals”), from *μέταλλον* (métallon, “metal, mine, quarry, ore”) + *-ουργία* (-ourgía, “-work, -working, -urgy”), from *ἔργον* (érgon, “work”).
