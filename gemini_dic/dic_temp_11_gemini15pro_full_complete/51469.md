detortion:/dɪˈtɔːrʃən/ /dɪˈstɔːrʃən/ | n. | 失真 |a change in the quality of sound, an electrical signal, or an image caused by unwanted changes in the signal. | The guitar amplifier produced a heavy detortion. 吉他放大器产生了严重的失真。| distortion, fuzz, static | clarity, fidelity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dētortiōn- (stem of dētortiō), from Latin dētortus (past participle of dētorquēre 'to twist or bend awry'), from dē- 'away from' + torquēre 'to twist.'
