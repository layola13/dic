amahs:/ˈɑːmɑːz/
noun
女佣；保姆；阿妈（尤指中国或印度的）
A woman employed as a domestic servant, especially in East and Southeast Asia.
例句：The amahs were busy preparing the evening meal. 女佣们正忙着准备晚餐。

例句：She employed two amahs to help with the children. 她雇了两个阿妈帮忙照顾孩子。

近义词：maids, servants, housekeepers, nannies
反义词：(No direct antonym, but could consider "employer")
词性：n. (noun)
