duplicating:/ˈdjuːplɪkeɪtɪŋ/ /ˈduːplɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
复制；使成双
making an exact copy of something
The machine is duplicating the document.  这台机器正在复制文件。
synonyms: copying, replicating, reproducing
antonyms: deleting, erasing
v.

duplicating:/ˈdjuːplɪkeɪtɪŋ/ /ˈduːplɪkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
复制的；二重的
identical; existing in two corresponding or identical parts
The duplicating machine is broken. 复印机坏了。
synonyms: copied, replicate, double
antonyms: original, unique
adj.
