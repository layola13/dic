bawls:/bɔːlz/ | v. | 大哭，嚎啕大哭 | to cry loudly and unrestrainedly | The child bawled when his toy broke.  孩子玩具坏了就嚎啕大哭。 |  近义词: cries, sobs, weeps, wails | 反义词: laughs, giggles | 词性: 动词

bawls:/bɔːlz/ | n. |  大哭，嚎啕大哭(较少用作名词) | a loud cry; a fit of weeping |  The baby's bawls woke the whole house. 婴儿的哭声吵醒了整栋房子的人。 | 近义词: crying, sobbing, weeping | 反义词: laughter | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *baullen, of uncertain origin; perhaps imitative.
