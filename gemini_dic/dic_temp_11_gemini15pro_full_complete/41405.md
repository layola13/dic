corneal:/ˈkɔːrniəl/ (US) /ˈkɔːniəl/ (UK)| adj.|角膜的|Relating to the cornea (the transparent front part of the eye).|Example 1: The doctor diagnosed a corneal abrasion. 医生诊断出角膜擦伤。

Example 2: Corneal transplants can restore vision. 角膜移植可以恢复视力。|Synonyms: ocular, ophthalmic|Antonyms: N/A|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin corneus "horny," from Latin cornu "horn."  The cornea is named for its horn-like texture.
