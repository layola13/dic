latexes: /ˈleɪtɛksɪz/ | n. | 胶乳; 乳液; |Milky fluid found in many plants, such as poppies and spurges, that is the source of rubber and similar substances.  许多植物（如罂粟和大戟）中发现的乳状液体，是橡胶和类似物质的来源。|The latexes of some plants are poisonous.  某些植物的乳胶是有毒的。|synonyms: latex, emulsion, sap, milk | antonyms: solid | noun

latexes: /ˈleɪtɛksɪz/ | n. | 胶乳制品; 橡胶制品; | Products made from latex, especially rubber. 由乳胶制成的产品，尤指橡胶制品。|Surgical gloves are often made of latexes.  外科手套通常由胶乳制成。|synonyms: rubber goods, latex products | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps natural materials) | noun
