brabantine: /brəˈbæntɪn/ /brəˈbæntaɪn/
adjective|布拉班特的；布拉班特人的；布拉班特方言的|Relating to Brabant, a region in the Netherlands and Belgium; a person from Brabant; the dialect spoken in Brabant.|The brabantine horses are known for their strength. 布拉班特马以其强壮而闻名。|近义词:Flemish, Dutch, Belgian|反义词:(No specific antonym, possibly non-Brabantine)|词性:adjective

brabantine: /brəˈbæntɪn/ /brəˈbæntaɪn/
noun|布拉班特人；布拉班特方言|A native or inhabitant of Brabant; the dialect of Brabant.|He is a Brabantine, proud of his heritage. 他是一个布拉班特人，为自己的传统感到自豪。|近义词:Fleming, Dutchman, Belgian|反义词:(No specific antonym)|词性:noun

词源: From Brabant + -ine.  Brabant itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from Frankish *brac "swamp" + *ban "region".
