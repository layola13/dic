accommodativeness:/əˌkɒməˈdeɪtɪvnəs/| n. |随和；乐于助人；通融；适应性|The quality of being willing to help and do things for other people; the quality of being adaptable or flexible. |Her accommodativeness made her a popular colleague. 她的随和让她成为受欢迎的同事。|近义词：helpfulness, obligingness, flexibility, adaptability|反义词:intransigence, inflexibility|词性:noun

accommodating:/əˈkɒmədeɪtɪŋ/| adj. |乐于助人的；随和的；通融的|Used to describe a person who is willing to help and do things for other people. |He was very accommodating and offered to drive us to the airport. 他非常乐于助人，主动提出开车送我们去机场。|近义词：helpful, obliging, cooperative|反义词:unhelpful, uncooperative|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From accommodate + -ive + -ness.  "Accommodate" ultimately derives from Latin "commodus" meaning "suitable, convenient."
