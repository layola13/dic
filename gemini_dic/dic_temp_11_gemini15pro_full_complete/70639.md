filtertron:/ˈfɪltərtrɒn/| n. |滤波器；（尤指Gretsch吉他使用的）菲尔特龙拾音器|A type of guitar pickup, specifically a humbucking pickup design known for its clear, bright tone, commonly associated with Gretsch guitars.  It is known for its unique, sparkling tone.|例句：My Gretsch 6120 has Filtertron pickups, and they sound amazing. 我的Gretsch 6120吉他装有菲尔特龙拾音器，音色很棒。

例句：Filtertrons are known for their jangle and clarity. 菲尔特龙拾音器以其清脆明亮的音色而闻名。|近义词：humbucker, pickup|反义词:single-coil (pickup)|词性:名词
