noniconoclastic:/ˌnɒnˌaɪkɒnəˈklæstɪk/
adjective
非反传统的，不打破传统的
Not iconoclastic; characterized by reverence for tradition and established beliefs or institutions.
Her noniconoclastic approach to art was evident in her realistic paintings of traditional subjects. 她对艺术的非反传统方法在她对传统主题的现实主义绘画中显而易见。
synonyms:  traditional, conventional, orthodox, conservative
antonyms: iconoclastic, radical, revolutionary, rebellious
词性: adjective
