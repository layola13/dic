breathlessly:/ˈbreθləsli/ | adv. | 气喘吁吁地，屏息地 | In a way that shows someone is out of breath, either from physical exertion or excitement. | She ran breathlessly into the room with the good news. 她气喘吁吁地跑进房间，带来了好消息。| Example: He whispered breathlessly, "I love you." 他气喘吁吁地低语道：“我爱你。” | Synonyms: gaspingly, pantingly | Antonyms: calmly, serenely | Part of Speech: Adverb

breathless:/ˈbreθləs/ | adj. | 气喘吁吁的，屏息的 | Out of breath, or making someone out of breath; or very exciting or suspenseful. | After running up the stairs, he was breathless.  跑上楼梯后，他气喘吁吁。| Example: The breathless pace of the movie kept me on the edge of my seat.  这部电影令人窒息的节奏让我一直坐在座位的边缘。| Synonyms: gasping, panting | Antonyms: calm, composed | Part of Speech: Adjective
