osteochondroma: /ˌɒstiːəʊkɒnˈdrəʊmə/ (UK) /ˌɑːstioʊkɑːnˈdroʊmə/ (US)| n. |骨软骨瘤|A benign tumor that contains both bone and cartilage and typically occurs near the end of a long bone.| Example: The doctor diagnosed the lump near his knee as an osteochondroma. 医生诊断他膝盖附近的肿块为骨软骨瘤。| Example:  Osteochondromas are the most common benign bone tumors in children. 骨软骨瘤是儿童最常见的良性骨肿瘤。|Synonym: exostosis, cartilage-capped bony projection|Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific tumor type.  Could consider "healthy bone tissue")|Noun
