daaj:/dɑːʒ/ | n. |  Judge (informal, often derogatory) | A person who judges others, often implying self-righteousness or unfair criticism. |  "Don't be such a daaj about it, let people live their own lives."  别对这件事评头论足的，让人们过自己的生活吧。 | synonyms: critic, judge, moralizer | antonyms: supporter, advocate | noun

Etymology: Likely a shortened and altered form of "judge," influenced by slang or dialect.
