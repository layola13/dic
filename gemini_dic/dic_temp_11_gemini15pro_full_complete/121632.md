misparses:/ˌmɪsˈpɑːrzɪz/ | v. | 错误地解析，误解 | The software misparses the data, leading to incorrect results.  该软件错误地解析了数据，导致结果不正确。|  The algorithm frequently misparses complex sentences.  该算法经常错误地解析复杂句子。 | misinterprets, misunderstands | correctly parses, correctly interprets | verb

Etymology:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "parse" (from Latin "pars orationis" meaning "part of speech"). The word "parse" originally referred to the grammatical analysis of a sentence, and "misparse" extends this meaning to any kind of incorrect analysis or interpretation, especially in the context of computing.
