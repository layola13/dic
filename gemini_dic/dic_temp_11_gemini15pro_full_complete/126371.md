myriopoda:/ˌmɪriˈɒpədə/ | n. | 多足类，多足纲 | A class of arthropods characterized by having numerous segments, each bearing one or two pairs of legs.  This includes millipedes and centipedes. | Myriopoda are an ancient group of invertebrates. 多足类是一种古老的无脊椎动物。| The giant African millipede is a member of the Myriopoda. 巨型非洲千足虫是多足纲的成员。| synonyms: Myriapoda (alternative spelling) | antonyms: None (no direct opposite for a taxonomic class) | noun
