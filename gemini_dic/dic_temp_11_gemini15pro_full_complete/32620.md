词源：
From Middle English *chirpen, *cherpen, from Old English *cierpan, *ceorpan (“to chirp, make a noise as a bird”), from Proto-Germanic *kirpijaną (“to chirp”), from Proto-Indo-European *ger- (“to cry out, call”). Cognate with Dutch tjirpen (“to chirp”), German zirpen (“to chirp”). Related to chirp.
