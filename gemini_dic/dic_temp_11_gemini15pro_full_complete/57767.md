dreamtime: /ˈdriːmtaɪm/ (AU) /ˈdrɪmtaɪm/ (US)| n. | 梦幻时期，创世时期 |A term from Australian Aboriginal culture referring to a period in the distant past when ancestral spirits created the world and everything in it.  It is also used to refer to the stories and beliefs about this time.  It's a complex concept encompassing mythology, spirituality, and law.|  The Aborigines believe that their ancestors still inhabit the dreamtime.  土著居民相信他们的祖先仍然居住在梦幻时期。

The stories of the dreamtime explain the creation of the world. 梦幻时期故事解释了世界的创造。 | 近义词: The Dreaming | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
