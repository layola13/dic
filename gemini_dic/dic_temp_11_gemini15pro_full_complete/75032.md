friezed:/friːzd/
| adj. | 有雕饰带的；饰以雕带的 | Decorated with a frieze (a horizontal band of sculpted or painted decoration, especially on a wall near the ceiling). | The temple was friezed with elaborate carvings of mythical creatures.  这座寺庙饰有精美的神话生物雕刻。 |例句：The building was friezed with intricate patterns.  这栋建筑饰有复杂的图案。| 近义词：decorated, ornamented, embellished | 反义词：plain, undecorated | 词性: adjective
friezed:/friːzd/
| v. | (freeze的过去式和过去分词) 冻结，凝固 | Past tense and past participle of "freeze," meaning to become hardened into ice or a solid state because of low temperature, or to preserve food by freezing. | The lake friezed over during the winter. 湖在冬天结冰了。|例句：The pipes friezed and burst. 管道冻裂了。| 近义词：frozen, solidified, congealed | 反义词：thawed, melted, liquefied | 词性: verb
