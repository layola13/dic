as-a-rule:/æz ə ruːl/ | adv. | 通常；一般来说；照例 | As a general principle or practice; typically. |  As a rule, I don't work on weekends.  通常情况下，我周末不工作。 | usually, generally, typically, normally | exceptionally, rarely, infrequently | 副词
