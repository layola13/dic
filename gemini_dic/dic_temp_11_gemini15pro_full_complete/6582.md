anacamptically:/ˌænəkæmˈtɪkli/ | adv. |回响地，反射地| Relating to or exhibiting anacamptics, the reflection of light or sound waves back to their source.  It describes something that returns to its origin, often used in the context of echoes or reflections.| The sound reverberated anacamptically through the canyon. 声音在峡谷中回响。|Synonyms: resonantly, reverberantly| Antonyms: dully, faintly| Adverb
