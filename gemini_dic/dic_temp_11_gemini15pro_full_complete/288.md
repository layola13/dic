abelonian: /ˌeɪbɪˈloʊniən/
adjective
阿贝耳的; 阿贝耳学派的;
Relating to Abelard, a French philosopher and theologian (1079-1142), or his philosophical doctrines.
Abelard's philosophy, while groundbreaking, was deemed heretical by some of his contemporaries.  阿贝拉尔的哲学虽然具有开创性，但在当时的一些人看来是异端的。
His abeloian arguments focused on the importance of intention in moral acts.  他的阿贝耳式论证集中在道德行为中意图的重要性上。
近义词: scholastic, medieval
反义词: modern, contemporary
词性: adjective

词源:  Derived from the proper noun "Abelard," referring to Peter Abelard (1079-1142), a French scholastic philosopher and theologian. The suffix "-ian" is used to form adjectives and nouns meaning "related to," "characteristic of," or "a follower of."
