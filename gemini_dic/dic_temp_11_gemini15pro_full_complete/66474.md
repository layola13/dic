exercised: /ˈɛksərsaɪzd/ | adj. | 焦虑的；担忧的 |  He was exercised about the upcoming exam. 他对即将到来的考试感到焦虑。 | Near synonyms: worried, concerned, anxious, apprehensive, uneasy | Antonyms: calm, relaxed, unconcerned, unworried | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *exercisen*, from Old French *exerciser*, from Latin *exercitāre*, frequentative of *exercēre* (“to keep busy, train, practice”), from *ex* (“out”) + *arcēre* (“to keep, hold”).
