nonassertion:/ˌnɑːnəˈsɜːrʃən/
n.
不维护，不坚持，不主张
lack of assertion; failure to assert oneself
Example: Her nonassertion allowed others to take advantage of her. 她不维护自己的权利让别人有机可乘。

Example:  His nonassertion in the meeting led to the project going in a direction he didn't agree with. 他在会议上没有坚持自己的意见，导致项目走向了他不赞同的方向。

synonym:passivity, submissiveness, acquiescence
antonym:assertion, assertiveness, insistence
词性: noun
