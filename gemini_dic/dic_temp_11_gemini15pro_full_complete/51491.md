detraction:/dɪˈtrækʃən/
n.
诽谤；诋毁；贬低
the act of taking away from the value or importance of something; lessening the merit or reputation of a person or thing
例句：He resorted to detraction to discredit his rivals. 他为了败坏对手的名誉而进行诋毁。

例句：The campaign was marred by personal detraction. 这场竞选活动因人身攻击而受损。

近义词：slander, defamation, libel, disparagement, calumny
反义词：praise, commendation, acclaim, applause
词性：noun
