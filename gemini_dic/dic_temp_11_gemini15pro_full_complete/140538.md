oleoresin:/ˌoʊliːˈoʊˈrɛzɪn/ /ˌɒliːˈɒrɪzɪn/
noun
油树脂
A natural or artificial mixture of a resin and an essential oil, occurring in or obtained from certain plants.
The oleoresin is extracted from the paprika fruit.  这种油树脂是从红辣椒果实中提取的。
