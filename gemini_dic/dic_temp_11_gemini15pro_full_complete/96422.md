indissoluble:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊbl/ /ˌɪndɪˈsɑːljəbl/| adj.|不能溶解的；牢不可破的|Not able to be dissolved; permanent and unable to be broken.|例句：Their friendship was an indissoluble bond. 他们的友谊牢不可破。|近义词：inseparable, unbreakable, permanent|反义词：dissoluble, separable, breakable|词性:形容词

indissoluble:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊbl/ /ˌɪndɪˈsɑːljəbl/| adj.|牢不可破的；永久的|Permanent and unable to be broken, especially of a relationship or agreement.|例句：The two countries formed an indissoluble alliance.  这两个国家结成了牢不可破的联盟。|近义词：inviolable, indestructible, immutable|反义词：breakable, fragile, temporary|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *indissolubilis, from in- "not" + dissolubilis "dissoluble," from dis- "apart" + solvere "to loosen, dissolve."
