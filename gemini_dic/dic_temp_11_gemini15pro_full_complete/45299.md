culvertail: /ˈkʌlvərˌteɪl/ (US), /ˈkʌlvəteɪl/ (UK)| n. v.|鸠尾榫; 鸠尾接合; 用鸠尾榫接合|A type of joint used in woodworking and other crafts where two pieces are joined together by interlocking projections, resembling a dove's tail.|The drawers of the antique dresser were crafted with strong culvertails. 古董梳妆台的抽屉是用结实的鸠尾榫制作的。 |He culvertailed the two pieces of wood together. 他用鸠尾榫把两块木头连接在一起。|Synonyms: dovetail, interlocking joint|Antonyms: butt joint, lap joint|noun, verb
