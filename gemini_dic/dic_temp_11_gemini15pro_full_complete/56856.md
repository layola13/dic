dorsibranchiate: /ˌdɔːrsɪˈbræŋkiət/ | adj. n. | 背鳃类的；背鳃类动物 | Having gills on the back; belonging or pertaining to the Dorsibranchia, an order of annelid worms characterized by gills arranged along the back. | Example: Dorsibranchiate worms are predominantly marine. 背鳃类蠕虫主要生活在海洋中。 | Example: The dorsibranchiate anatomy is unique among annelids. 背鳃类动物的解剖结构在环节动物中是独一无二的。 | 近义词: polychaete | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps a specific group of annelids that don't have dorsal gills) | 词性: adjective, noun | 词源: From Latin *dorsum* (back) + Greek *branchia* (gills)
