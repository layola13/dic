obstructed:/əbˈstrʌktɪd/
adjective
阻塞的，堵塞的
blocked, closed, or difficult to get through
例句：The road was obstructed by a fallen tree. 道路被一棵倒下的树堵住了。
例句：His view was obstructed by the tall building.  他的视线被高楼挡住了。
近义词：blocked, impeded, clogged, hindered
反义词：clear, open, unobstructed
词性: adjective


obstruct:/əbˈstrʌkt/
verb
阻碍，阻塞，妨碍
to block or close up something so that nothing can pass through
例句：A large crowd obstructed the entrance to the stadium.  一大群人堵住了体育场的入口。
例句：The new building will obstruct our view of the mountains. 新建筑会挡住我们看山的视线。
近义词：block, impede, hinder, prevent
反义词：aid, assist, clear, facilitate
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obstructus*, past participle of obstruere (“to build in front of, block up, obstruct”), from ob (“against, in front of”) + struere (“to pile up, build”).
