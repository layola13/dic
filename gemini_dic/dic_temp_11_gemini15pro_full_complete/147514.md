overpark:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːrk/ | v. | 过度停车，停车时间过长 | To park a vehicle for longer than the permitted time. | Example: I overparked by 15 minutes and got a ticket. 我停车超时了15分钟，收到了罚单。

Example: Be careful not to overpark in the city center, the wardens are very strict. 在市中心要注意不要停车超时，那里的管理员非常严格。| 近义词: park illegally, exceed the parking limit | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "park briefly" or "park legally") | 词性: 动词
