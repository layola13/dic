dopinesses:/ˈdoʊpinəsɪz/
n.
昏沉；迟钝；困倦
A state of being drowsy, sluggish, or mentally slow, often due to the effects of drugs or illness.
His dopinesses after the surgery were concerning.  他手术后一直昏昏沉沉，令人担忧。
The medication caused uncomfortable dopinesses. 药物导致令人不适的困倦。
近义词：drowsiness, lethargy, sluggishness, stupor
反义词：alertness, wakefulness, energy, vivacity
词性：名词 (plural of dopiness)

词源: From dope + -iness.  Dope originally referred to a thick liquid or lubricant, later slang for drugs, and then used to describe someone under the influence of drugs, hence sluggish or drowsy. The suffix -iness turns the adjective "dopy" into the noun "dopiness."
