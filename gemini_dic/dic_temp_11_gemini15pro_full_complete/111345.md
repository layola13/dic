livelinesses:/ˈlaɪvlɪnɪsɪz/
n.
活力, 生动, 活泼
The quality or state of being lively; animation; briskness.
The livelinesses of the children filled the room with joy. 孩子们的活泼给房间充满了欢乐。
近义词:animations, vibrancies, energies, spirits
反义词:dullness, lifelessness, inactivity, apathy
词性:名词(plural)

Etymology: From liveliness + -es (plural suffix).  Liveliness from lively + -ness. Lively from life + -ly. Ultimately from Old English līf.
