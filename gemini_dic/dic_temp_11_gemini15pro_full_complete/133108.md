nonerroneousness:/ˌnɒnɛrˈəʊniəsnəs/ | n. | 正确性；无误性 | The nonerroneousness of the data is crucial for the success of the experiment.  数据的正确性对于实验的成功至关重要。 | 近义词: correctness, accuracy, validity, rightness, infallibility | 反义词: erroneousness, fallacy, inaccuracy, incorrectness | 词性: 名词

词源:  non- (not) + erroneous (containing error) + -ness (forming nouns expressing a state or quality)  来自拉丁语 errare "to wander, stray, err."
