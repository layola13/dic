nonvertebrate:/nɒnˈvɜːtɪbrət/ | /nɑːnˈvɜːrtəbrət/ | adj. n. |无脊椎动物的；无脊椎动物|An animal that has no backbone or spinal column. |例句：Invertebrates make up 97% of all animal species. 无脊椎动物占所有动物物种的97%。

例句：Spiders, worms, and insects are all invertebrates. 蜘蛛、蠕虫和昆虫都是无脊椎动物。|近义词：invertebral |反义词: vertebrate |词性: adjective, noun
