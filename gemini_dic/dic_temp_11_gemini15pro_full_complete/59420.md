earthlier:/ˈɜːrθliər/
adjective
更早的; 更尘世的
earlier in time; more worldly
例句：We arrived earthlier than expected. 我们比预期更早到达。

例句：Her concerns were more earthlier, focused on practical matters. 她的关注点更尘世化，集中于实际问题。
近义词：earlier, sooner, more worldly, more mundane
反义词：later, more spiritual, more heavenly
词性: adjective
