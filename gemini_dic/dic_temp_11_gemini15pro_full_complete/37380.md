commentate:/ˈkɒmənteɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmənteɪt/ (US)| v. |评论，解说|To provide a spoken description and analysis of an event, especially a sporting event, as it happens.|The journalist commentated on the football match.|近义词：report, narrate, describe, analyze|反义词: listen, observe|verb

commentary:/ˈkɒməntri/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmənteri/ (US)| n. |评论，解说|A spoken description of an event, especially a sporting event, as it happens; a series of comments or explanations.|The commentary on the Olympics was very informative. |近义词：explanation, analysis, exposition, description|反义词: silence|noun

commentator:/ˈkɒmənteɪtə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmənteɪtər/ (US)| n. |评论员，解说员|A person who provides a spoken description and analysis of an event, especially a sporting event, as it happens.|The commentator's voice was clear and engaging.|近义词：reporter, analyst, announcer, narrator|反义词: listener, audience|noun
