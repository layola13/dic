goldless:/ˈɡəʊldlɪs/
adjective
无金的；不含金的；没有金子的
Without gold; not containing gold.
The prospector returned from the Klondike goldless and broke.  那个探矿者从克朗代克空手而归，身无分文。
Synonyms:  impoverished, destitute, penniless, broke
Antonyms:  wealthy, rich, affluent, prosperous
词性: 形容词
