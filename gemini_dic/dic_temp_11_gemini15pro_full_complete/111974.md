lona:/ˈlona/| n.| 帆布；油布；防水帆布|A strong, closely woven canvas, typically made of cotton, flax, or hemp, used for sails, tents, tarpaulins, and other protective coverings.|The truck was covered with a heavy lona to protect its cargo from the rain. 卡车盖着厚厚的帆布，以保护货物免受雨淋。|近义词：canvas, tarpaulin|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "delicate fabric")|词性:noun
