flushnesses:/ˈflʌʃnɪsɪz/
noun
潮红，脸红；齐平
The state or quality of being flush.  This refers to multiple instances of being flush.
Her flushnesses were frequent due to the hot weather. 由于天气炎热，她经常脸红。
The carpenter checked the flushnesses of the cabinets. 木匠检查了橱柜是否齐平。
Synonyms:  rednesses, blushes (for the meaning related to skin); levelness, evenness (for the meaning related to surfaces)
Antonyms: paleness, unevenness
