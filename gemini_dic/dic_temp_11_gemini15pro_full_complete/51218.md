destocked:/diːˈstɒkt/
verb
减少库存，清仓
to reduce the level of stock or inventory, typically by selling it off at a reduced price
The company destocked its warehouse before the end of the financial year.  公司在财政年度结束前清仓了仓库。
The retailer destocked summer clothing to make way for the autumn collection.  零售商清仓了夏季服装，为秋季系列腾出空间。
synonym: cleared out, liquidated, reduced inventory
antonym: stocked up, replenished, increased inventory
词性: 动词
