horniest:/ˈhɔːrniɪst/| adj.|最淫荡的，最性兴奋的|Feeling or showing strong sexual desire, especially in a crude or vulgar way.  It's the superlative form of "horny."|That movie had some of the horniest scenes I've ever seen. 那部电影有一些我见过的最色情的场景。|
He told the horniest jokes at the party. 他在聚会上讲了最黄的笑话。|近义词：lustful, lecherous, lascivious, randy|反义词：frigid, asexual, chaste|adjective

词源: From "horn" (referring to the horns of a cuckold) + "-y" (forming adjectives) + "-est" (superlative suffix).  The connection to "horn" likely arises from the perceived link between sexual arousal and aggression in male animals.
