firster:/ˈfɜːrstər/ (Pronunciation may vary regionally)| n.| 第一名，领先者 (Informal)| Someone or something that is first or in the lead, particularly in a race or competition. This word is informal and not commonly used in formal settings.| He was a firster in the 100-meter dash. 他是百米赛跑的第一名。| The firster in the chess tournament was awarded a trophy. 国际象棋比赛的冠军被授予了奖杯。| synonyms: winner, champion, leader, front-runner | antonyms: loser, last, follower| noun
