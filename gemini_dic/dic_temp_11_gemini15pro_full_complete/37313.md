commeasurable:/kəˈmɛnʃərəbəl/
adjective
可比较的；相称的
able to be measured by the same standard; proportionate
例句：The two distances are commensurable. 这两个距离是可以比较的。

例句：Their contributions to the project were commensurable. 他们对这个项目的贡献是相称的。

近义词：comparable, proportionate, similar, analogous
反义词：incommensurable, disparate, dissimilar
词性: adjective
