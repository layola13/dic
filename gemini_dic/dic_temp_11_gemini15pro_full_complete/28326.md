carageen: /ˈkærəɡiːn/ /ˈkærəˌɡin/
n.
角叉菜；爱尔兰苔藓；鹿角菜胶
A type of red seaweed (Chondrus crispus) found on the coasts of the North Atlantic, also called Irish moss. It is a source of carrageenan, a gelling agent.
Carageen is used in many food products as a thickening agent. 角叉菜在许多食品中用作增稠剂。
The traditional Irish dessert blancmange is made with carageen.  传统的爱尔兰甜点牛奶冻是用角叉菜做的。
近义词: Irish moss, seaweed
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: noun

carrageenan: /ˌkærəˈɡiːnən/
n.
鹿角菜胶；角叉菜胶
A gummy substance extracted from carageen seaweed and used as a thickening or emulsifying agent in food products.
Carrageenan is widely used in the food industry. 鹿角菜胶在食品工业中被广泛使用。
Ice cream often contains carrageenan to improve its texture. 冰淇淋通常含有鹿角菜胶以改善其质地。
近义词:  (作为增稠剂) agar, gelatin, pectin
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: noun


词源:  From Irish *carraigín "little rock", diminutive of carraig "rock".
