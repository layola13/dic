nongreasier:/nɒnˈɡriːsiər/ | adj. | 更不油腻的 | Less greasy; containing less fat or oil. | This new lotion is nongreasier than the old one.  这款新乳液比旧的更不油腻。 |  The chicken is nongreasier because I baked it instead of frying it. 这鸡肉不油腻，因为我烤了而不是炸了。 | 近义词: less greasy, oil-free, non-oily | 反义词: greasier, oilier, fatty | 词性: 形容词

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "greasy" (adjective meaning "covered with or containing fat or oil") + "-er" (comparative suffix)
