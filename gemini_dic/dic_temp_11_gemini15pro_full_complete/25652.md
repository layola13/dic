bundles:/ˈbʌndlz/| n.|捆, 束, 包, 很多, 大量|A bundle is a number of things tied or wrapped together.  A bundle can also mean a large amount of something.|She carried a bundle of laundry. 她抱着一捆待洗的衣物。|The flowers were tied in a bundle. 这些花被捆成一束。|Synonyms: bunch, package, sheaf, stack, pile, lot, mass|Antonyms: few, little|noun
