dichogamy:/daɪˈkɒɡəmi/ /daɪˈkɑːɡəmi/
noun
雌雄异熟
The condition in which the male and female reproductive organs of a flower mature at different times, thus preventing self-fertilization.
Many plants exhibit dichogamy to prevent self-pollination. 许多植物表现出雌雄异熟以防止自花授粉。
Protandry and protogyny are two forms of dichogamy. 雄蕊先熟和雌蕊先熟是雌雄异熟的两种形式。
