intervening:/ˌɪntərˈviːnɪŋ/
adjective
干预的，介于其间的
coming or existing between two events, dates, objects, etc.
例句：
The intervening years saw many changes.
这期间的几年发生了很多变化。

During the intervening period, the company's stock price doubled.
在这段 intervening 期间，公司的股票价格翻了一番。

近义词：intermediate, intermediary, intervening, median, medial
反义词：non-intervening

词性: adjective
