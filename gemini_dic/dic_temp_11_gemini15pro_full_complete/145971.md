overdiversifies:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪz/
|v.|过度多元化|invests in too many different assets, spreading resources too thinly and potentially reducing returns.|The company overdiversified its portfolio and ended up with lower returns than expected.  这家公司过度分散了投资组合，最终回报低于预期。|overextends, spreads too thin|focuses, specializes|verb
overdiversification:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˌvɜːrsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
|n.|过度多元化|The act of overdiversifying.|The analyst cautioned against overdiversification, suggesting it could harm the portfolio's performance. 分析师告诫不要过度多元化，认为这可能会损害投资组合的表现。|overextension|focus, specialization|noun
