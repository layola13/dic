deviously:/ˈdiːviəsli/| adv.|迂回地，不光明正大地|in a devious way; not straightforwardly|He achieved his aims by devious means. 他通过不正当的手段达到了目的。|近义词：cunningly, slyly, craftily, indirectly|反义词：honestly, straightforwardly|词性: 副词

devious:/ˈdiːviəs/| adj.|迂回的，不光明正大的|showing a skillful use of underhanded tactics to achieve goals.|He took a devious route to avoid the traffic. 他绕道而行以避开交通堵塞。|近义词：cunning, sly, crafty, indirect|反义词：honest, straightforward|词性: 形容词


词源:
From Late Latin *devivius "out of the way," from Latin de- "off" + via "way"
