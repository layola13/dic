Hübner:/ˈhʏbnər/ (German pronunciation)| n.| Hübner (surname), a butterfly or moth |A surname of German origin, also used in the scientific names of some butterflies and moths (as part of a binomial name). It is derived from "Hübner," a German word for "small farmer" or "cottager."|例句：Jakob Hübner was a German entomologist. 雅各布·休布纳是一位德国昆虫学家。

例句：*Melitaea hubneri* is a butterfly species.  *蜜环蝶*是一种蝴蝶。|近义词：(For the surname meaning) No direct synonyms, as surnames are unique identifiers.  (For the scientific name usage)  The closest synonym would be the common name of the butterfly or moth species. |反义词:None|词性:Noun
