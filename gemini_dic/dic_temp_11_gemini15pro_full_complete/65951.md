exactly:/ɪɡˈzæktli/ | adv. | 恰好地；精确地；正是；确实 |  Used to emphasize the accuracy of a figure or description. |  She knew exactly what he was thinking. 她确切地知道他在想什么。

| 近义词: precisely, accurately, specifically | 反义词: approximately, roughly, vaguely | 词性: 副词


exactly:/ɪɡˈzæktli/ | interj. | 确实如此；正是 | Used to express agreement. | "It's a difficult situation." "Exactly." “情况很困难。”“确实如此。”

| 近义词: precisely, quite, absolutely | 反义词: not at all, certainly not | 词性: 感叹词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exacte, from Latin exactus past participle of exigere “to demand, require, drive out, weigh, measure, complete,” from ex- “out” + agere “to drive, lead, act, do.”
