frowstinesses:/ˈfraʊstinɪsɪz/
n.
闷热，污浊，陈腐
The state of being frowsty; staleness and mustiness, often combined with a lack of fresh air.
The frowstinesses of the attic made her cough.  阁楼的闷热让她咳嗽。
近义词:mustiness, stuffiness, staleness, closeness
反义词:freshness, airiness
