empair:/ɪmˈpɛər/| vt.|损害，削弱|To weaken or damage something, especially a faculty or function.|The illness impaired his ability to think clearly. 这种疾病损害了他清晰思考的能力。|近义词：damage, harm, weaken, undermine, compromise, diminish, deteriorate|反义词:improve, enhance, strengthen, bolster|verb
