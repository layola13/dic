kilovolt:/ˈkɪləˌvoʊlt/| n. |千伏|A unit of electromotive force, equal to 1,000 volts.  It is a measure of electrical potential difference, often used in the context of high-voltage equipment and systems.|The X-ray machine operates at 100 kilovolts.  这台X光机的工作电压为100千伏。

The power lines carry several hundred kilovolts. 这些输电线路 carrying 几百千伏的电压。|近义词: kV (abbreviation)|反义词: millivolt|词性:noun
