nonathletically:/ˌnɒnæθˈletɪkli/
adverb
非运动地；不擅长运动地
in a way that does not relate to or involve athletics; in a way that lacks athletic skill.
He walked nonathletically across the field. 他笨拙地走过田野。
Near Antonyms: athletically, nimbly, skillfully, gracefully
Antonyms: athletically
词性: 副词
