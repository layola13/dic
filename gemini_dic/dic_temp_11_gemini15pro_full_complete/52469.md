diestock:/ˈdaɪˌstɒk/ (US) /ˈdaɪˌstɒk/ (UK)| n.|板牙架，螺丝攻板牙架|A tool used for holding and turning threading dies, which are used to cut external threads on metal rods or pipes.|The plumber used a diestock to cut threads on the pipe. 水管工用板牙架在管子上切螺纹。|Synonym: stock, die holder|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps tap wrench for cutting internal threads)|noun
