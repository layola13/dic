computerized:/kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzd/ | adj. | 电脑化的，计算机化的 | Operated or controlled by computer. |  例句：The library has a computerized catalog. 图书馆有一个电脑化的目录。| 例句：Many factories are now fully computerized. 许多工厂现在已经完全计算机化了。 | 近义词：automated, digital, electronic | 反义词：manual, analog | 词性:形容词

Etymology->
From compute + -ize + -ed.  Compute comes from Latin computare "to count, sum up, reckon," from com "with, together" + putare "to prune, cleanse, reckon."
