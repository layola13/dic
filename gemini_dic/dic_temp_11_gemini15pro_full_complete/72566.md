fluvialist:/ˈfluːviəlɪst/| n.|河流学家；河流作用论者|A geographer who specializes in the study of rivers and their processes.|The fluvialist studied the meandering path of the river.  河流学家研究了河流蜿蜒的路径。|近义词：geomorphologist, river scientist|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

fluvial:/ˈfluːviəl/| adj.|河流的；由河流形成的|Relating to or produced by rivers.|Fluvial deposits are rich in minerals. 河流沉积物富含矿物质。|近义词：riverine|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fluvialis*, from fluvius (“river”).
