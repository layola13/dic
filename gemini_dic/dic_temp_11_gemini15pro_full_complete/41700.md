corrivalry:/kəˈraɪvəlri/
n.
竞争；对抗；竞赛
Competition between rivals.
Example:  There is a fierce corrivalry between the two companies for market share.  两家公司之间为争夺市场份额展开了激烈的竞争。

Example: The corrivalry between the two teams has been going on for decades. 这两支球队之间的竞争已经持续了几十年了。

近义词: rivalry, competition, contention, contest
反义词: cooperation, collaboration, partnership, alliance
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + rivalry.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "together" or "jointly," suggesting a rivalry shared or engaged in by multiple parties.  "Rivalry" comes from the Old French "rivalite," ultimately from the Latin "rivalis," meaning "of a brook," later extended to mean "using the same stream" and then to competitors.
