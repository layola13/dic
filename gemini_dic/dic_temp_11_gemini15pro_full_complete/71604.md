flat-pack: /ˈflætˌpæk/ (UK) /ˈflætˌpæk/ (US)| n. adj.| 平板包装；扁平包装的 | Furniture that is sold unassembled and packed flat in a box, requiring the buyer to assemble it at home.  | I bought a flat-pack wardrobe from IKEA. 我从宜家买了一个平板包装的衣柜。 |Synonyms:  knock-down, ready-to-assemble, RTA | Antonyms: assembled, pre-assembled | noun, adjective

flat-pack: /ˈflætˌpæk/ (UK) /ˈflætˌpæk/ (US)| v. | 把…扁平包装 |To pack (something) flat, typically furniture for transportation and subsequent assembly by the purchaser. | The company flat-packs all their furniture to reduce shipping costs.  这家公司把所有的家具都扁平包装，以降低运输成本。 |Synonyms:  disassemble, knock down | Antonyms: assemble, pre-assemble | verb
