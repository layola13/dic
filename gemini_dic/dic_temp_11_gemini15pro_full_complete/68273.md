fakes:/feɪks/| n. |赝品；骗子；假货；伪造品| something that is not what it appears to be; a copy of something valuable that is intended to deceive people |The painting was a clever fake. 这幅画是一件精妙的赝品。|synonyms: forgery, counterfeit, sham, imitation | antonyms: original, genuine | noun
fakes:/feɪks/| v. |伪造；假装；冒充；(使)显得像| to make something that seems real but is not; to pretend to have a particular feeling, illness, etc. |He faked his father's signature on the check. 他在支票上伪造了他父亲的签名。| synonyms: forge, counterfeit, feign, simulate | antonyms: authenticate, verify | verb
