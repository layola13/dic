mewer:/ˈmjuːər/| n.|喵喵叫的人/动物;爱哭的人;|A person or animal that meows.  Can also refer to someone who cries frequently, especially a baby. |The kitten was a loud mewer, constantly demanding attention. 小猫喵喵叫个不停，一直想要人关注它。

The baby was a mewer, crying all night.  这个婴儿总是哭，哭了一整夜。|近义词：crier, whiner, mewler|反义词: (For animal) purrer (For person) stoic|noun
