comprehension:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhenʃn/ (UK), /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhenʃn/ (US)| n.|理解；理解力；领悟|The ability to understand something.|She has excellent reading comprehension. 她有优秀的阅读理解能力。|Synonyms: understanding, grasp, apprehension|Antonyms: misunderstanding, confusion|noun

comprehension:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhenʃn/ (UK), /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhenʃn/ (US)| n.|包容；包含|The act of including or encompassing something.|The price includes travel and accommodation, but meals are not within the comprehension of the tour package. 价格包含旅行和住宿，但不包含餐食。|Synonyms: inclusion, encompassment|Antonyms: exclusion|noun
