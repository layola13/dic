outjuggling:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌɡlɪŋ/
verb
胜过；以更精妙的杂耍胜过
surpassing in juggling; outdoing in cleverness or skill, especially in manipulating or maneuvering.
He outjuggled his rival in the competition, earning a standing ovation. 他在比赛中以更精妙的杂耍胜过了对手，赢得了全场起立鼓掌。
outmaneuver, outsmart, surpass, eclipse
outperform
