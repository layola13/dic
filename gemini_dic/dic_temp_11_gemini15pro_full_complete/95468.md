incitement:/ɪnˈsaɪtmənt/| n.|煽动，鼓动|The act of encouraging or urging someone to do something, especially something violent or unlawful. |His fiery speech was seen as an incitement to violence. 他的激昂的演讲被视为煽动暴力。|近义词：provocation, instigation, encouragement|反义词:dissuasion, deterrence|noun
