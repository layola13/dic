decry:/dɪˈkraɪ/
verb
公开谴责；强烈批评
to publicly criticize something or someone strongly
The report decries the lack of investment in education. 这份报告强烈谴责对教育缺乏投资。
Synonyms: denounce, condemn, criticize, censure
Antonyms: praise, commend, laud, extol
词性: 动词
