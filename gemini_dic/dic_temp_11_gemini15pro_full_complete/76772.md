gamesome:/ˈɡeɪmsəm/| adj.|嬉戏的，爱玩的，好开玩笑的|Inclined to playfulness; playful; sportive.  Characterized by or exhibiting a playful, lighthearted, or teasing disposition.|The gamesome puppy chased its tail relentlessly. 这只爱玩的小狗不停地追着自己的尾巴。|The children, full of gamesome energy, raced around the park.  充满嬉戏能量的孩子们在公园里跑来跑去。|近义词：playful, frolicsome, sportive, mischievous|反义词：serious, solemn, grave|词性:形容词
Etymology: From game + -some.  "Game" in this context refers to play or amusement, not hunting.
