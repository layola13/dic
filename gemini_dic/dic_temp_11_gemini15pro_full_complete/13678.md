attractor:/əˈtræktər/ | n. | 吸引子，吸引物 | Something that attracts people or things.  |  Tourists are attracted to the city by its vibrant cultural scene.  游客被这座城市充满活力的文化景象所吸引。 | synonyms: magnet, draw, lure, appeal | antonyms: repellent, deterrent | noun

attractor:/əˈtræktər/ | n. | (数学、物理)  吸引子 | In mathematics and physics, a point or set towards which a system tends to evolve, regardless of the starting conditions.  | The Lorenz attractor is a classic example of a chaotic system. 洛伦兹吸引子是混沌系统的典型例子。 | synonyms:  focal point, center of attraction | antonyms: repeller | noun
