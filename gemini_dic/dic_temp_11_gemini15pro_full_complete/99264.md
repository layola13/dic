intercondensers:/ˌɪntərˈkənˈdensərz/ | n. | 中间冷凝器；级间冷凝器 |A device located between two condensers, especially in a fractional distillation system, used to cool and condense vapors before they enter the next stage of condensation. | The intercondensers in the distillation column helped to improve the separation efficiency of the different components.  分馏塔中的中间冷凝器有助于提高不同组分的 separation 效率。| The chemical plant utilizes a series of intercondensers to maximize product purity.  这家化工厂利用一系列中间冷凝器来最大程度地提高产品纯度。 | synonyms: intermediate condensers | antonyms: N/A | noun
