chromidrosis:/ˌkroʊmɪˈdroʊsɪs/| n. |色汗症|A rare condition characterized by the secretion of colored sweat, usually black, blue, green, yellow, red, or brown.  It's typically caused by lipofuscin or melanin. |Example 1: The patient presented with chromidrosis, with blue sweat staining his clothes. 病人表现出色汗症，蓝色汗液染了他的衣服。

Example 2:  Chromidrosis can be a distressing condition due to its cosmetic implications. 色汗症会因为它的外观影响而令人苦恼。|近义词: pseudochromhidrosis (sometimes)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
