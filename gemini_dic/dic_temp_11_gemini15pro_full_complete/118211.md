mephitical: /məˈfɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 恶臭的；有害的；地狱般的 | Foul-smelling; noxious; relating to hell.  | The mephitical odor emanating from the swamp made us gag.  从沼泽散发出的恶臭使我们作呕。 | Synonyms: noxious, poisonous, fetid, toxic, pestilential | Antonyms: fragrant, sweet-smelling, wholesome, salubrious | adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *mephiticus, from mephitis (“foul-smelling exhalation”),  ultimately from Greek *mēphitis (“noxious exhalation”).
