hopscotched:/ˈhɒpskɒtʃt/ (UK) /ˈhɑːpskɑːtʃt/ (US)| v.|跳房子游戏；快速或轻巧地移动|To have moved quickly or lightly from one place or thing to another, often skipping or ignoring intermediate steps.  The word derives from the children's game of hopscotch.|She hopscotched through the city, visiting all the major landmarks in a single day. 她快速地游览了这座城市，一天之内就参观了所有主要的地标。|近义词：skipped, darted, jumped, flitted|反义词:plodded, trudged|词性:动词
