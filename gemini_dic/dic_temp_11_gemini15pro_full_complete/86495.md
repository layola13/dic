heinousness:/ˈheɪnəsnəs/| n.|邪恶; 凶恶; 滔天大罪|The heinousness of the crime shocked the nation.  这起罪行的凶恶震惊了全国。|近义词：atrocity, wickedness, evil, depravity|反义词：goodness, righteousness|noun

Etymology->
From heinous + -ness.  Heinous itself comes from Old French *haineus, from haine ("hatred"), from hair ("to hate"). Ultimately from Frankish *hatjan.
