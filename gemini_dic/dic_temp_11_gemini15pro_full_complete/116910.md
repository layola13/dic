mayonnaised:/ˌmeɪəˈneɪzd/ | adj. |涂了蛋黄酱的；加蛋黄酱的|Covered or dressed with mayonnaise.|例句：I had a mayonnaised salad for lunch. 我午餐吃了一份加了蛋黄酱的沙拉。|例句：He prefers mayonnaised potatoes. 他更喜欢加蛋黄酱的土豆。|近义词：dressed (with mayonnaise)|反义词：(plain), (without mayonnaise)|词性:形容词

mayonnaise:/ˌmeɪəˈneɪz/ | n. |蛋黄酱|A thick, creamy sauce made from oil, egg yolks, vinegar or lemon juice, and seasonings.|例句：I always put mayonnaise on my sandwiches. 我总是在我的三明治上涂蛋黄酱。|例句：Would you like some mayonnaise with your fries? 你的薯条要加蛋黄酱吗？|近义词：mayo (informal)|反义词: (无明确的反义词，可用 ketchup, mustard 等其他酱料)|词性:名词
