cuculoid:/ˈkjuːkjʊlɔɪd/| adj.|杜鹃类的，属于杜鹃科的|Relating to or resembling the cuckoos; belonging to the Cuculidae family.|The cuculoid bird laid its eggs in another bird's nest.  这种杜鹃类的鸟把蛋下在别的鸟巢里。|The cuculoid species is known for its brood parasitism.  这种杜鹃科的物种以其巢寄生而闻名。|近义词: cuckoo-like|反义词: (No specific antonym, could consider non-parasitic birds)|词性: adjective
