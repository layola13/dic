agamic:/əˈɡæmɪk/
adjective
无性的，无配子生殖的
Reproducing without the union of male and female gametes; asexual.
例句：Agamic reproduction is common in some plants.  无性繁殖在某些植物中很常见。

例句：The agamic propagation of the species ensures its survival in harsh conditions.  该物种的无配子繁殖确保了其在恶劣条件下的生存。

近义词：asexual, vegetative
反义词：sexual
词性：adjective

词源:  From Greek *agamos "unmarried," from *a- "without" + *gamos "marriage."
