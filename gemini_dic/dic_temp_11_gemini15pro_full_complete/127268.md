navarino:/ˌnævəˈriːnoʊ/| adj.|纳瓦里诺式的；纳瓦里诺羊肉的|Relating to or characteristic of Navarino, a style of lamb or mutton stew.|The restaurant's navarino dish was their specialty. 这家餐厅的纳瓦里诺羊肉是他们的特色菜。|Synonyms: None|Antonyms: None|Adjective
