outbeg:/ˌaʊtˈbeɡ/
verb
超越乞求; 乞求胜过
to surpass in begging; to beg more earnestly than
He outbegged all the other panhandlers on the street. 他在街上乞讨比所有其他乞丐都更卖力。
近义词: outdo, surpass, exceed
反义词: be outdone, be surpassed
