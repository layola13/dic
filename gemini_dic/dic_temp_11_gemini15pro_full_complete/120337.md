minimalistic:/ˌmɪnɪməˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 极简主义的；简约的 | Characterized by extreme spareness and simplicity. |  She decorated her apartment in a minimalistic style, with very little furniture and neutral colors. 她用极少的家具和中性色装饰了她的公寓，风格极简。

| Example: The website has a minimalistic design, focusing on content over flashy graphics.  这个网站的设计极简，注重内容而非华丽的图形。 | Synonyms: simple, spare, austere, uncluttered, streamlined | Antonyms: ornate, elaborate, cluttered, maximalist | word property:adjective


minimalism:/ˈmɪnɪməlɪzəm/ | n. | 极简主义 |  A style or technique that is characterized by extreme spareness and simplicity. | The minimalism of the building's design was striking. 这栋建筑设计的极简主义风格令人印象深刻。

| Example:  She embraced minimalism, getting rid of most of her possessions. 她信奉极简主义，处理掉了大部分财产。 | Synonyms: simplicity, austerity | Antonyms: maximalism, ornateness | word property:noun
