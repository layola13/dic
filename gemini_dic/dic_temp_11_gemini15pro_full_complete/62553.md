enepidermic:/ɛnˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmɪk/
adjective
表皮上的；经皮的
Existing on or applied to the skin.
This enepidermic cream soothes irritated skin.  这种表皮乳霜可以舒缓受刺激的皮肤。
The medication was administered enepidermically.  药物是经皮给药的。
近义词:cutaneous, dermal, percutaneous
反义词:hypodermic (皮下的)
词性:adjective
词源:From Greek *ἐν- (en-, “in, on”) +  *ἐπιδερμίς (epidermis, “epidermis”).
