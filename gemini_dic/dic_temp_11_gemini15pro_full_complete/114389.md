make-a-show-of:/meɪk ə ʃoʊ əv/ | phrasal verb | 假装；装腔作势；炫耀 | To ostentatiously display or pretend something, often insincere. | He made a show of checking his watch, as if he was in a hurry. 他假装看了看手表，好像很匆忙似的。 |  He made a big show of giving money to the charity, but it was a very small amount. 他大张旗鼓地给慈善机构捐款，但数额却很小。 | Synonyms: pretend, feign, affect, ostentate, flaunt | Antonyms: conceal, hide, downplay | verb phrase
