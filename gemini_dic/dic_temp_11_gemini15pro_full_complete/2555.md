admissibly:/ədˈmɪsɪbli/
|adv.|可容许地，可接受地|In a way that is considered acceptable or allowable. |The evidence was admissibly obtained.  证据是合法取得的。|Synonyms: acceptably, allowably, legitimately, properly, justifiably |Antonyms: inadmissibly, unacceptably, improperly, illegally, unjustly
|词性: 副词

admissible:/ədˈmɪsɪbl/
|adj.|可容许的，可接受的|Acceptable or allowable, especially in a court of law. |The judge ruled the evidence admissible. 法官裁定证据可被采纳。|Synonyms: acceptable, allowable, legitimate, proper, justifiable |Antonyms: inadmissible, unacceptable, improper, illegal, unjust
|词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *admissibilis, from Latin admittere “to admit.”  The suffix -ible comes from Latin -ibilis, meaning "capable of being."
