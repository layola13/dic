haematic:/hɪˈmætɪk/ /hiːˈmætɪk/
adjective
血液的; 血的; 与血液有关的
relating to the blood or blood vessels.
Example: Haematic diseases are conditions that affect the blood. 血液疾病是影响血液的疾病。
Example:  The doctor ordered a haematic screening test. 医生要求进行血液筛查测试。
Synonym: bloody, sanguineous, sanguinary
Antonym: bloodless
word type: adjective
