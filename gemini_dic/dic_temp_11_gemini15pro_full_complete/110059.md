licentiously: /laɪˈsɛnʃəsli/ /laɪˈsɛnʃəsli/
adverb
放荡地；放肆地；淫荡地
in a way that is not considered morally acceptable, especially relating to sexual behavior
He behaved licentiously throughout his life. 他一生放荡不羁。
Synonyms:  dissolutely, debauchedly, wantonly, promiscuously, immorally, loosely, wildly
Antonyms: chastely, modestly, morally, purely, virtuously
词性: 副词
