nebulous:/ˈnɛbjʊləs/ | adj. | 模糊的，朦胧的；星云的 | cloudy or hazy; indistinct or vague | The plan was still nebulous, lacking concrete details.  该计划仍然模糊不清，缺乏具体的细节。| The nebulous shape in the distance turned out to be a fog bank. 远处朦胧的形状原来是一团雾。 | synonyms: vague, hazy, unclear, indistinct, amorphous, ambiguous | antonyms: clear, distinct, precise, definite, concrete | adjective


nebula:/ˈnɛbjʊlə/ | n. | 星云；云状物 | a cloud of gas and dust in outer space, visible in the night sky either as an indistinct bright patch or as a dark silhouette against other luminous matter. | The Orion Nebula is a popular target for amateur astronomers. 猎户座星云是业余天文学家的热门目标。 | The nebula swirled with vibrant colors. 星云中充满了鲜艳的色彩。 | synonyms: galaxy, cloud, mist | antonyms: clarity, clearness | noun
