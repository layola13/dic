antilogarithm: /ˌæntiˈlɒɡəˌrɪðəm/ (UK) /ˌæntiˈlɔːɡəˌrɪðəm/ (US) | n. | 反对数 |  The number that corresponds to a particular logarithm.  It is the inverse function of a logarithm. | The antilogarithm of 2 (base 10) is 100. 反对数以10为底，2的对数是100。| synonym: inverse logarithm, exponential | antonym: logarithm | noun

antilogarithm: /ˌæntiˈlɒɡəˌrɪðəm/ (UK) /ˌæntiˈlɔːɡəˌrɪðəm/ (US) | n. | 反对数 | The result of raising the base of a logarithm to a given power.  |  If log₁₀ x = 2, then the antilogarithm of 2 is x = 10².  如果以10为底x的对数是2，那么2的反对数是x=10²。 | synonym: inverse logarithm, exponential | antonym: logarithm | noun
