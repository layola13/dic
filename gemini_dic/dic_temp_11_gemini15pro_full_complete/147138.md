overlook:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊk/ | vt. n.| 忽视，俯瞰，宽容 | To fail to notice or consider something; to have a view of from above. |  I overlooked an important detail in the contract. 我忽略了合同中一个重要的细节。

The hotel room overlooked the ocean.  酒店房间俯瞰大海。 | 近义词：miss, ignore, neglect, condone, excuse, pardon, forgive | 反义词：notice, observe, scrutinize | 词性: 动词, 名词

overlook:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊk/ | n. | 俯瞰的地方；高地 | A place from which a view can be had. |  We enjoyed the scenic overlook from the mountaintop. 我们喜欢从山顶俯瞰的景色。| 近义词：viewpoint, vantage point | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *overloken, from Old English oferlōcian (“to look over, look down upon, despise, oversee, rule over”), from ofer (“over”) + lōcian (“to look”).
