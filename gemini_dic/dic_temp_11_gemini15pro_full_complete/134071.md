nonilluminating:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
无启发性的，不具启发性的
Not providing any insight or understanding; not helpful or informative.
The expert's testimony was surprisingly nonilluminating.  这位专家的证词令人惊讶地没有启发性。
His explanation was completely nonilluminating. 他的解释完全没有启发性。

synonym
uninformative, unhelpful, unenlightening, useless, pointless
antonym
informative, helpful, enlightening, useful, insightful
