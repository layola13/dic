ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *archidiaconus, from Greek *ἀρχιδιάκονος (archidiákonos), from *ἄρχι- (árchi-, “chief”) + *διάκονος (diákonos, “deacon”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *deḱ- (“to take, accept”).
