kamikaze:/ˌkɑːmɪˈkɑːzi/ /ˌkæmɪˈkɑːzi/
n.|神风特攻队；敢死队队员；鲁莽的人|A Japanese aircraft loaded with explosives and making a deliberate suicidal crash on an enemy target.|The kamikaze pilots sacrificed their lives during World War II.  二战期间，神风特攻队的飞行员牺牲了他们的生命。|synonyms: suicide bomber, suicide attacker|antonyms: pacifist, conscientious objector|noun
kamikaze:/ˌkɑːmɪˈkɑːzi/ /ˌkæmɪˈkɑːzi/
adj.|自杀式的；不要命的|Done with reckless disregard for the consequences, as if on a suicide mission.|His kamikaze business strategy ultimately led to the company's downfall. 他自杀式的商业策略最终导致了公司的垮台。|synonyms: reckless, desperate, suicidal|antonyms: cautious, prudent, careful|adjective
