effection:/əˈfekʃən/| n.|喜爱，钟爱；感情；影响|A feeling of liking and caring for someone or something.|She felt a deep affection for her grandparents. 她对祖父母有着深厚的感情。|近义词：fondness, love, attachment, tenderness|反义词：aversion, dislike, hatred|noun

effection:/əˈfekʃən/| n.|喜爱，钟爱；感情；影响|The act of affecting or influencing something.|The drug had little affection on the disease. 这种药对这种疾病几乎没有影响。 (这个用法比较少见)|近义词：influence, impact, effect|反义词：inaction,  neglect|noun
