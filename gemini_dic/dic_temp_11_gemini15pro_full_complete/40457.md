contravene:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈviːn/
verb
违反，违背
to do something that is not allowed by a law or rule
The company’s actions contravene international law.  这家公司的行为违反了国际法。

The film contravenes broadcasting regulations. 这部电影违反了广播条例。

synonym: violate, breach, infringe, disobey, defy
antonym: comply with, obey, uphold, observe
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contravenire, from Latin contra (“against”) + venire (“to come”).
