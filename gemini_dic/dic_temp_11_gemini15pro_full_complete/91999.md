hyperoartia:/ˌhaɪpəroʊˈɑːrtiə/ | n. |七鳃鳗纲|A class of jawless fish-like vertebrates comprising the lampreys, characterized by a cartilaginous skeleton, a round sucking mouth, and seven pairs of gill pouches.| Lampreys, members of Hyperoartia, are often parasitic. 七鳃鳗，属于七鳃鳗纲，通常是寄生的。| 近义词: Petromyzontida (sometimes considered a synonym)|反义词: None (lack of a direct antonym for a taxonomic classification)|词性: Noun
