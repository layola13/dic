curative:/ˈkjʊərətɪv/| adj. n.|治疗的，治病的；治疗药|Relating to or used in the cure of diseases; something that cures.|例句：The doctor prescribed a curative medicine for her infection. 医生给她开了治疗感染的药。|近义词：healing, remedial, therapeutic|反义词: harmful, damaging|词性:adjective

curative:/ˈkjʊərətɪv/| n.|治疗药，药物|A medicine or remedy that cures.|例句：This herb is a popular curative for colds. 这种草药是一种流行的感冒治疗药。|近义词：remedy, medicine, cure|反义词: poison, toxin|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *curatif*, from Old French *curatif*, from Latin *cūrātīvus*, from *cūrō* (“I care for, attend to, heal”).
