antiparasitics:/ˌæntiˌpærəˈsɪtɪks/ | n. | 抗寄生虫药 |  Substances used to kill or inhibit the growth of parasites. |  The veterinarian prescribed antiparasitics for the dog's heartworm. 兽医给这只狗开了治疗心丝虫的抗寄生虫药。| Synonyms: antihelminthics, parasiticides | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "growth stimulants" for parasites) | noun


antiparasitic:/ˌæntiˌpærəˈsɪtɪk/ | adj. | 抗寄生虫的 | Having the property of destroying or inhibiting the growth of parasites. | This shampoo has antiparasitic properties. 这款洗发水具有抗寄生虫特性。 | Synonyms: antihelminthic, parasiticidal | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | adjective
