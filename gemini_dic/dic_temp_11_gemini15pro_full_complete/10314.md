appeal:/əˈpiːl/ (US) /əˈpiːl/ (UK)| n. v.| 吸引力；呼吁；上诉 | An earnest or urgent request for something.  The quality of being attractive or interesting. To make a serious or formal request to the public for money, information, or help. To formally request that a decision be reversed.|

例句：
The charity launched an appeal for donations to help the victims of the earthquake.
该慈善机构发起呼吁，为地震灾民募捐。

Her sense of humor has a wide appeal.
她的幽默感很吸引人。

He appealed to the court to overturn his conviction.
他向法院提出上诉，要求推翻他的定罪。

近义词：attraction, charm, request, plea, petition, entreaty|
反义词：repulsion, disgust, rejection, denial |
词性：noun, verb|


appeal:/əˈpiːl/ (US) /əˈpiːl/ (UK)| v.| 上诉；申诉；有吸引力 |To formally request that a decision be reversed. To be attractive or interesting.|

近义词： attract, allure, fascinate, petition, entreat|
反义词：repel, disgust, repulse, deter|
