defraudment:/dɪˈfrɔːdmənt/| n. |欺诈，诈骗|The act of defrauding someone of something.  The illegal obtaining of money from someone by deception.|He was found guilty of defrauding investors of millions of dollars. 他被判犯有诈骗投资者数百万美元的罪行。|Synonyms: fraud, swindle, deception, cheat, trickery|Antonyms: honesty, integrity|noun
