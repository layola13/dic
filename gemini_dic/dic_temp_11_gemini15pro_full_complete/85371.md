harrumphs:/hɑːˈrʌmfs/ | v. | 清嗓子；哼哼；表示不满 | To clear the throat noisily, especially to express disapproval or doubt. | He harrumphed and folded his arms. 他哼了一声，交叉着胳膊。| 近义词：cough, clear one's throat, grunt, grumble | 反义词:  agree, approve |词性: 动词

harrumph:/hɑːˈrʌmpf/ | n. | 哼声；表示不满的声音 | A sound made by clearing the throat noisily, typically to express disapproval or doubt. |  She gave a loud harrumph at his suggestion. 她对他的建议大声哼了一声。| 近义词：grunt, grumble, cough | 反义词: cheer, applause |词性: 名词
