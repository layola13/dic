contentiousness:/kənˈtenʃəsnəs/| n. |争论性，好争辩 |The contentiousness of the issue made it difficult to reach a consensus.  这个问题的争议性使得难以达成共识。|Example: The contentiousness of the political climate made reasoned debate almost impossible. 政治气候的争议性使得理性辩论几乎不可能。

Example:  Her inherent contentiousness often led to heated arguments. 她固有的好争辩的性格经常导致激烈的争论。|近义词：argumentativeness, combativeness, disputatiousness |反义词：agreeableness, amiability, peacefulness |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From contentious + -ness.  Contentious comes from Late Middle English contenciouse, from Old French contentieux, from Latin contentiosus “quarrelsome,” from contentus, past participle of contendere “to strive, contend.”
