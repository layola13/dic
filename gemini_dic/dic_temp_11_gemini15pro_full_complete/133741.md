nonfundamentals:/ˌnɑːnfʌndəˈmentəlz/ | n. | 非基本面因素 | Factors other than fundamental economic data that influence market prices, such as investor sentiment or technical analysis. | Example:  The recent market volatility is attributed to nonfundamentals, such as speculation and fear. 最近的市场波动归因于非基本面因素，例如投机和恐惧。

Example:  Traders often use nonfundamentals to predict short-term price movements. 交易员经常使用非基本面因素来预测短期价格走势。 | 近义词: technicals, sentiment, speculative factors | 反义词: fundamentals | 词性: noun
