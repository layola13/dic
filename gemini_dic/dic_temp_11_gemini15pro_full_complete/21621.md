blu-ray:/ˈbluːˌreɪ/ /ˈbluːrei/ | n. |蓝光光碟|A high-density optical disc format for storing digital data, especially high-definition video.|I bought the movie on Blu-ray. 我买了这部电影的蓝光光碟。|近义词: DVD, HD DVD|反义词: VHS (although not a direct comparison in terms of technology)|词性:名词

blu-ray:/ˈbluːˌreɪ/ /ˈbluːrei/ | adj. |蓝光的|Relating to or using Blu-ray technology.|This is a Blu-ray player. 这是一个蓝光播放器。|近义词: high-definition|反义词: standard-definition|词性:形容词
