launderabilities:/ˌlɔːndərəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可洗性；耐洗性|The launderabilities of different fabrics vary greatly. 不同织物的耐洗性差异很大。|例句：The launderabilities of these new shirts are excellent; they come out of the wash looking like new. 这些新衬衫的耐洗性非常好；洗完后看起来像新的。

例句：The manufacturer tested the launderabilities of the material before releasing the product.  制造商在发布产品之前测试了材料的耐洗性。|近义词：washability, cleanability|反义词: non-washable, delicate
