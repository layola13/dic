overlegislating: /ˌoʊvərˈlɛdʒɪsleɪtɪŋ/
| v. | 过度立法 | Creating excessive laws, often to the point of inefficiency or counter-productivity.  The act of a legislative body creating too many laws, often unnecessarily or with unintended negative consequences. | The government is overlegislating on environmental issues, creating a complex web of regulations that are difficult to enforce. 政府在环境问题上过度立法，创建了一个难以执行的复杂法规网络。

The city council's overlegislating has stifled small business growth. 市议会的过度立法扼杀了小型企业的增长。
| 近义词: overregulating | 反义词: underregulating | 词性: 动名词 (gerund)
