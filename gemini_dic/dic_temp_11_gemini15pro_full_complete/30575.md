ceratohyal: /ˌsɛrətoʊˈhaɪəl/ | n. | 角舌骨 | A bone or cartilage element of the hyoid arch that lies between the hypohyal and the epihyal. It is typically elongated. | The ceratohyal is a key component in the jaw structure of many fish. 角舌骨是许多鱼类颌骨结构中的关键组成部分。|  The ceratohyal bone can be easily identified in dissected specimens.  在解剖标本中可以很容易地识别出角舌骨。 | synonyms: hyoid bone element (更广义),  branchiostegal ray (部分鱼类) | antonyms: None (没有直接的反义词，因为它是一个特定的解剖学术语) | noun
