mediatrix:/ˌmiːdiˈeɪtrɪks/
noun
女调解人，女中保人
a woman who mediates, especially between two warring factions.
例句：She acted as mediatrix between the two families. 她充当了两个家庭之间的调解人。

例句：The queen was asked to be the mediatrix in the dispute. 女王被请求在争端中担任调解人。

近义词：mediator, intermediary, go-between, negotiator, arbitrator, peacemaker
反义词：agitator, instigator
词性：n.
