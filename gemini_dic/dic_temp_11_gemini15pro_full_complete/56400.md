doler:/doˈler/ |v. | (Spanish) To ache, to hurt, to be painful. | This verb is primarily used in Spanish and doesn't have a direct English equivalent with the same nuance. It describes a persistent, dull ache or pain. | Me duele la cabeza. (My head hurts/aches.) | Synonyms (English): ache, hurt, pain | Antonyms (English): soothe, relieve | verb

doler:/doˈler/ |v. | (Spanish) To cause emotional pain or grief. |  Beyond physical pain, "doler" can also refer to emotional distress or sorrow. | Me duele mucho su pérdida. (Their loss pains me deeply.) | Synonyms (English): grieve, mourn, sorrow | Antonyms (English): comfort, console | verb
