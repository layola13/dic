nonelopement:/ˌnɒnɪˈləʊpmənt/
n.
私奔(的反义词); 不私奔; 未私奔
The act of not eloping, or running away secretly to get married, especially without parental consent.
They celebrated their nonelopement with a large, traditional wedding. 他们以一场盛大而传统的婚礼庆祝了他们没有私奔。
The couple's nonelopement pleased their families. 这对情侣没有私奔让他们的家人很高兴。
近义词: staying, remaining, abiding
反义词: elopement
