mezzotint: /ˈmetsəʊtɪnt/ (US) /ˈmedzəʊtɪnt/ (UK)| n.| 铜版蚀刻版画；暗调版画；(用此法制成的)版画|A method of engraving copper or steel to produce prints in which the design is etched into the plate with a rocker, creating a textured surface that holds ink and produces a rich, velvety tone.
