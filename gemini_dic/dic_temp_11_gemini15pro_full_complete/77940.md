一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The human genome contains about 3 billion base pairs. 人类基因组包含大约30亿个碱基对。

Scientists often compare genomes to understand evolutionary relationships. 科学家经常比较基因组来理解进化关系。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Researchers are currently sequencing the genomes of several endangered species. 研究人员目前正在对几种濒危物种的基因组进行测序。

They are analyzing the genomes to identify potential disease-causing genes. 他们正在分析基因组以识别潜在的致病基因。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Scientists have mapped the genomes of many organisms, including humans and mice. 科学家已经绘制了许多生物体的基因组图谱，包括人类和小鼠。

The Human Genome Project has revolutionized our understanding of genetics. 人类基因组计划彻底改变了我们对遗传学的理解。
