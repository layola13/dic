anatomize:/ˈænətəˌmaɪz/ | v. | 解剖，仔细分析 | To dissect or analyze something in detail, typically a text or an idea. | The professor anatomized the poem, exploring its intricate metaphors and symbolism. 教授仔细分析了这首诗，探讨了其中复杂的隐喻和象征意义。 |  The biologist anatomized the frog to study its internal organs. 生物学家解剖了青蛙来研究它的内部器官。 | 近义词: dissect, analyze, examine, scrutinize | 反义词: synthesize, combine, construct | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin anatomizare, from Greek anatomē "dissection," from ana- "up" + temnein "to cut."
