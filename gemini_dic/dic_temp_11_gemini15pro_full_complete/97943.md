innovatively:/ˈɪnəveɪtɪvli/
adv.
创新地；有创意地
in a new and original way
They marketed the product innovatively. 他们以创新的方式推销该产品。

近义词: creatively, imaginatively, originally, ingeniously
反义词: traditionally, conventionally, routinely, uncreatively
词性: 副词
