nonchauvinists:/ˌnɒnˈʃoʊvɪnɪsts/ | n. |非沙文主义者|  A person who does not believe in the superiority of one group over another, especially not the superiority of one sex (typically men) over another (typically women). |  Example 1: The group of nonchauvinists advocated for equal pay for equal work. 这群非沙文主义者倡导同工同酬。

Example 2:  He considers himself a nonchauvinist and actively supports women's rights. 他认为自己不是沙文主义者，并积极支持妇女权利。 | 近义词: egalitarian, feminist (in some contexts) | 反义词: chauvinist, sexist | 词性: 名词
