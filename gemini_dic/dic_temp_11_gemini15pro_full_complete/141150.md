oolite:/ˈoʊəlaɪt/ | n. |鲕粒岩；鱼卵石|A sedimentary rock consisting of small, round grains (ooliths) cemented together. These grains are typically composed of calcium carbonate and are formed in shallow marine environments.|The geologists found a large deposit of oolite near the coast.|近义词: roe stone|反义词: None|词性: Noun
