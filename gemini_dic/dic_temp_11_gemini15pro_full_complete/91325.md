hydrophlorone:/ˌhaɪdrəˈflɔːroʊn/| n. |氢氟酮，对苯三酚|A crystalline solid, C6H6O3, isomeric with pyrogallol and phloroglucinol, used as a reducing agent and in the synthesis of other organic compounds.  It is a type of benzene-triol.|This study investigated the antioxidant properties of hydrophlorone. 这项研究调查了氢氟酮的抗氧化特性。|Hydrophlorone is a powerful reducing agent used in photographic developers. 氢氟酮是一种强还原剂，用于照相显影剂中。|Synonyms: 1,2,4-benzenetriol, 1,2,4-trihydroxybenzene |Antonyms: oxidizing agent|noun
