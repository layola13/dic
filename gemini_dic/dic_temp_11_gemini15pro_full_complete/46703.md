danalite:/ˈdænəˌlaɪt/| n. |锰铁矿，丹纳石|A reddish-brown mineral, a member of the helvite group, consisting of a silicate and sulfide of beryllium, iron, and manganese. It is named after James Dwight Dana (1813-1895), an American geologist and mineralogist.|The museum displayed a rare specimen of danalite.|近义词:helvite (a mineral of similar composition)|反义词:None (Specific antonyms for mineral names are uncommon.)|词性:noun
