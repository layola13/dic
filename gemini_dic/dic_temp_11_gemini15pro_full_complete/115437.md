mantram:/ˈmʌntrəm/ (US), /ˈmæntrəm/ (UK)| n. |咒语，真言，圣句|A word or sound repeated to aid concentration in meditation.  It is a sacred utterance believed to have spiritual and transformative power.|She repeated her mantram silently throughout the meditation. 她默默地在整个冥想过程中重复她的咒语。|例句：The guru gave his disciples a powerful mantram to chant. 上师给了他的弟子们一句强有力的咒语来吟诵。|近义词：chant, incantation, prayer, formula|反义词:curse, imprecation|词性:noun
mantra:/ˈmʌntrə/ (US), /ˈmæntrə/ (UK)| n. |咒语，真言，圣句; 口号; 信条|A word or sound repeated to aid concentration in meditation.  It is a sacred utterance believed to have spiritual and transformative power. Also, a statement or slogan repeated frequently.|His mantra is "early to bed, early to rise." 他的信条是“早睡早起”。|例句：The advertising mantra for the new product is "quality and affordability."  这个新产品的广告口号是“质量和价格实惠”。|近义词：chant, incantation, prayer, slogan, motto|反义词:curse, imprecation|词性:noun

注意事项:  Mantra and Mantram are both acceptable spellings. Mantram is the older, more traditional spelling, while mantra is now the more common spelling, especially in Western usage.  They are pronounced the same.
