offset's:/ˈɔːfˌsɛts/ | n. | 补偿，抵消，支管 | Something that counterbalances or compensates for something else.  It can also refer to a side branch or shoot, as in a plant.  In printing, it refers to the accidental transfer of ink from one printed sheet to another. |  The company's losses were offset by gains in other areas. 公司在其他领域的收益抵消了损失。 | synonyms: counterbalance, compensate, balance, neutralize | antonyms: enhance, augment, increase | noun

offset:/ˈɔːfˌsɛt/ | v. | 补偿，抵消，用支管连接 | To counterbalance, compensate for, or counteract something. In plumbing, to connect by means of a short pipe at an angle to the main pipe. | The higher costs were offset by increased sales.  更高的成本被增加的销售额抵消了。 | synonyms: counterbalance, compensate, balance, neutralize | antonyms: enhance, augment, increase | verb
