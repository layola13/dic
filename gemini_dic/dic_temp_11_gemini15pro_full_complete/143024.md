outbleated: /ˌaʊtˈbliːtɪd/
|v.| (胜过)大声叫，(比...更)大声地咩咩叫|To bleat louder than someone or something else.|The farmer's sheep outbleated the neighbor's goats. 农夫的羊叫声比邻居家的山羊还大。|近义词：outcry, outshout|反义词: whisper, murmur|词性: 动词


词源:
"out-" (prefix denoting exceeding or surpassing) + "bleat" (verb meaning to make the cry of a sheep or goat)
