overphilosophize: /ˌoʊvərˈfɪləˌsɒfaɪz/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈfɪləsəfaɪz/ (UK)| v. |过度哲学化；过度思辨|To think or talk about something in a philosophical way to an excessive or unnecessary degree.|He tends to overphilosophize simple problems, making them seem more complex than they are. 他倾向于过度思辨简单的问题，使它们看起来比实际更复杂。|
He was overphilosophizing about the meaning of life when he should have been studying for his exam. 当他应该为考试学习时，他却在过度思辨人生的意义。|近义词：overthink, overanalyze, intellectualize|反义词：simplify, underthink|词性：动词

overphilosophization: /ˌoʊvərˌfɪləˌsɒfɪˈzeɪʃən/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˌfɪləsəfaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |过度哲学化；过度思辨|The act of overphilosophizing.|His overphilosophization of the situation led to inaction. 他对情况的过度思辨导致了无所作为。|
The book was criticized for its overphilosophization of everyday events. 这本书因其对日常事件的过度哲学化而受到批评。|近义词：overthinking, overanalysis|反义词：simplification|词性：名词


词源：The prefix "over-" means "excessive" or "too much."  It is combined with "philosophize," which comes from "philosophy" (from Greek philosophia, "love of wisdom").  Therefore, "overphilosophize" literally means to philosophize too much.
