lese-majesty: /ˌliːz ˈmædʒɪsti/ | n. | 冒犯君主罪，大不敬罪 |  An offense against the dignity of a reigning sovereign or against a state.  It refers to actions or words that diminish the respect owed to a monarch or ruler. | The journalist was accused of lese-majesty after publishing an article critical of the king.  这位记者因发表批评国王的文章而被指控犯有大不敬罪。|  treason, sedition,  insubordination | reverence, respect | noun
