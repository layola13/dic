hypnotises:/ˈhɪpnəˌtaɪzɪz/ /ˈhɪpnəˌtaɪzəz/
verb
催眠；使着迷
to produce a state of hypnosis in (someone); to fascinate or mesmerize
The magician hypnotised his audience with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法催眠了他的观众。
