PHRASE->
"eth" is not typically used in modern phrases.  Its primary usage is as a suffix in ordinal numbers:

twentieth = 第二十
thirtieth = 第三十
fortieth = 第四十
fiftieth = 第五十
sixtieth = 第六十


注意事项:
The use of "eth" is archaic and generally limited to historical or literary contexts. It is not used in modern English conversation or writing.
