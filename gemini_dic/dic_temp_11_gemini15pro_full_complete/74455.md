franion:/ˈfrænɪən/| n.|朋友，伙伴，同志|A comrade, companion, or friend, especially a boon companion; a close associate.|He was a loyal franion to his king.|近义词：friend, companion, comrade, pal, buddy|反义词：enemy, foe|名词

词源:
From Middle English *franioun, from Old French *fraignon, from Frankish *frajo (“free”), from Proto-Germanic *frajaz (“free”).  The original sense was "free man", which later evolved to mean "companion, friend".
