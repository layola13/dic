barraging:/bəˈrɑːʒɪŋ/
| v. | 猛烈攻击，连续炮击；(用问题、批评等)围攻，轰炸 | Subjecting to a barrage; bombarding. | The reporters barraged the president with questions. 记者们向总统提出了一连串的问题。 | The enemy troops were barraged with artillery fire. 敌军遭到炮火猛烈攻击。 | 近义词：bombarding, peppering, attacking, assaulting | 反义词：protecting, shielding, defending | 词性：动词
