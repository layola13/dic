astringe:/əˈstrɪndʒ/ | v. | 收敛；使收缩 | To cause body tissues to contract, usually by the action of a medicine or astringent substance. | The medicine will astringe the blood vessels.  这种药会使血管收缩。 | synonyms: constrict, tighten, contract | antonyms: relax, loosen, dilate | verb

astringent:/əˈstrɪndʒənt/ | adj. | 收敛的；涩的 |  Having the property of causing tissues to contract; sharp or severe in manner or style. |  The witch hazel is a natural astringent. 金缕梅是一种天然的收敛剂。  | synonyms: constrictive, styptic, harsh, austere | antonyms: relaxing, soothing, mild | adjective

astringent:/əˈstrɪndʒənt/ | n. | 收敛剂 | A substance that causes tissues to contract, thus stopping bleeding or reducing inflammation. | She used an astringent to cleanse her face. 她用收敛剂清洁脸部。 | synonyms: styptic | antonyms: emollient | noun
