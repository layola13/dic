gascoines:/ɡæsˈkɔɪnz/ | n. | 宽松的裤子；马裤 | Wide, loose breeches. |  He wore a pair of gascoines and a doublet. 他穿了一条宽松的马裤和一件紧身上衣。| 近义词: breeches, slops, pantaloons | 反义词: tights, leggings | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Gascon, inhabitant of Gascony, a historical region in southwestern France, known for their distinctive style of dress.
