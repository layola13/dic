accustomance: /əˈkʌstəməns/ | n. | 习惯，适应 | The state of being accustomed to something; familiarity or custom. |  He showed an easy accustomance to the new environment.  他很容易就适应了新环境。 | 近义词:  habituation, custom, familiarity, adaptation | 反义词: unfamiliarity, strangeness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "accustom" + suffix "-ance".  "Accustom" comes from Old French "acostumer," meaning "to make familiar with."
