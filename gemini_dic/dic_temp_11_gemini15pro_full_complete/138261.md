not-so-bad:/ˌnɒt səʊ ˈbæd/ /ˌnɑːt soʊ ˈbæd/| adj.|还不错，不算太坏|Meaning "moderately good; acceptable," it's a form of understatement often used to downplay a positive situation.|The food was not so bad.  食物还不错。|Synonyms: alright, decent, tolerable, okay, passable|Antonyms: terrible, awful, horrible|Adjective
