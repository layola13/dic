accounting: /əˈkaʊntɪŋ/ | n. | 会计; 会计学 | The systematic and comprehensive recording of financial transactions pertaining to a business, and it also refers to the process of summarizing, analyzing and reporting these transactions to oversight agencies, regulators and tax collection entities. |  Example 1:  He studied accounting in college and now works for a major firm.  他大学时学的是会计，现在在一家大公司工作。

Example 2: The company's accounting department is responsible for managing all financial records.  该公司的会计部门负责管理所有的财务记录。 | synonyms: bookkeeping, accountancy | antonyms:  N/A (Accounting doesn't have a direct antonym) | noun
