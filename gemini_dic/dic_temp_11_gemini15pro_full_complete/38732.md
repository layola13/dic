conditioner:/kənˈdɪʃənər/| n. |护发素；调节器；空调|A substance that improves the quality or condition of something, especially hair or the atmosphere.|I always use conditioner after shampooing. 我洗发后总是使用护发素。|Synonyms: cream, balm, softener|Antonyms: irritant, drying agent|noun

conditioner:/kənˈdɪʃənər/| n. |调节器；空调|A device that controls or modifies the state of something, such as temperature or humidity.|The air conditioner keeps the room cool in summer. 空调在夏天让房间保持凉爽。|Synonyms: regulator, controller, unit|Antonyms: heater (in some contexts)|noun
