hand-me-up:/ˈhænd mi ˌʌp/| n.|旧衣服；施舍物；救济品|Something given to a younger sibling or other person after the original owner has grown out of it or no longer wants it.  It often implies something used or second-hand, and sometimes carries a slightly negative connotation of being of lower quality or undesirable.|My younger brother always gets my hand-me-ups.|近义词：castoff, secondhand|反义词:new, brand-new|词性:名词
