monorhina:/ˌmɒnəˈraɪnə/ | n. |单鼻孔亚目动物|Animals belonging to the suborder Monorhina, characterized by having a single nasal opening.|The echidna is a monorhina.  针鼹是单鼻孔亚目动物。|Synonym: Prototheria (in older classifications)|Antonym:  Metatheria (marsupials), Eutheria (placental mammals)|noun
