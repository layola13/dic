km:/ˈkeɪˈem/ /ˈkɪləˌmiːtər/| n. |公里；千米|A unit of length in the metric system, equal to 1000 meters.  The marathon is a race of just over 42 km.|例句：The speed limit is 50 km per hour.  限速是每小时50公里。

例句：It's about 10 km from here to the city center. 从这里到市中心大约10公里。|近义词：kilometer|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)
词性: noun
