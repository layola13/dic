dunk:/dʌŋk/| v. n.|扣篮，浸泡|To submerge something briefly in liquid.  To shoot a basketball by slamming it down through the hoop.|He dunked his donut in the coffee. 他把甜甜圈浸在咖啡里。 He dunked the ball with impressive power. 他以惊人的力量扣篮。|近义词：dip, submerge, slam (v.), dunk shot (n.)|反义词:remove, extract|词性:动词,名词
