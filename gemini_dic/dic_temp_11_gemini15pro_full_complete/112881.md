lucullite:/ˈluːkjʊlaɪt/ | n. |闪长岩; 卢库里式盛宴 | A rare, translucent marble, quarried in Egypt and highly prized by the Romans. Also refers to a lavish or luxurious lifestyle. | The emperor built his palace with gleaming lucullite. 皇帝用闪闪发光的闪长岩建造了他的宫殿。 | Synonym: luxury, extravagance, opulence | Antonym: austerity, poverty, simplicity | noun
