corporace:/ˈkɔːrpərəs/ /ˈkɔːrpəreɪs/ | n. | 语料库 | A large collection of written or spoken text used for linguistic research, especially in computational linguistics. | Example 1: The researchers used a large corpora of newspaper articles to study the evolution of language. 研究人员使用了一个大型的报纸文章语料库来研究语言的演变。

Example 2:  The corpora contained millions of words, providing ample data for their analysis.  这个语料库包含数百万个单词，为他们的分析提供了充足的数据。| 近义词: corpus, text database | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
