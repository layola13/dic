crates:/kreɪts/| n.|板条箱；装货箱|A crate is a large wooden or plastic box used for storing or transporting goods. |The workers loaded the crates onto the truck. 工人们把板条箱装上了卡车。|  The museum shipped the artifacts in sturdy crates. 博物馆用结实的板条箱运送文物。|近义词：box, container, case|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *crate (unattested) or directly from a Germanic source, perhaps related to hurdle.
