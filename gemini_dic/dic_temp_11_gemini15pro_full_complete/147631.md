overplus:/ˈoʊvərplʌs/| n.|剩余，过剩，盈余|An overplus of qualified candidates applied for the position.  职位空缺吸引了过多的合格候选人。|例句：The overplus of food was donated to a local charity.  剩余的食物捐赠给了一个地方慈善机构。

|例句：We had a large overplus of apples this year and made a lot of cider. 今年我们有多余的苹果，做了很多苹果酒。|近义词：surplus, excess, remainder|反义词:deficit, shortage|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *overplus*, from Anglo-Norman *overplus*, from Old French *sorplus*, *sobreplus* (“surplus”), from *sor*, *sobre* (“over”) + *plus* (“more”).  Ultimately from Latin *super* (“over”) and *plus* (“more”).
