etherate:/ˈiːθəreɪt/ | n. | 醚合物；醚化物 | A compound formed by the combination of a substance with ether. |  The chemist prepared an etherate of the magnesium compound.  化学家制备了镁化合物的醚合物。| synonyms: ether compound, ether complex | antonyms: None | noun

etherate:/ˈiːθəreɪt/ | v. | 使与醚化合 | To treat or combine with ether. | The Grignard reagent was etherated before the next step. 格氏试剂在下一步之前先用醚处理。 | synonyms: etherify, combine with ether | antonyms: None | verb
