kymograph:/ˈkaɪməɡræf/ | n. |记纹鼓，描记器 | An instrument for recording variations in pressure, such as blood pressure, or muscular contractions. It typically consists of a revolving drum bearing smoked paper on which a stylus moves up and down, recording the variations as a wavy line. | The kymograph showed a significant increase in blood pressure during the experiment.  实验中，记纹鼓显示血压显著升高。 |  polygraph, recorder | - | noun
