The lecture was so immemorial that I fell asleep halfway through.  讲座太枯燥了，我中途睡着了。|近义词：forgettable, unremarkable, insignificant, bland, ordinary|反义词：memorable, unforgettable, noteworthy, significant, remarkable|词性:形容词
