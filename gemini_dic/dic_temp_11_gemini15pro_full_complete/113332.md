luxive:/ˈlʌksɪv/| adj.|奢侈的，豪华的|Characterized by luxury; luxurious.  Relating to, or fond of luxury.|She leads a luxive lifestyle, filled with designer clothes and exotic vacations. 她过着奢侈的生活，到处都是名牌服装和异国度假。|近义词：luxurious, opulent, lavish, sumptuous|反义词：austere, spartan, frugal|词性:形容词

词源:  "Luxive" is a relatively modern word, derived from the Latin word "luxus," meaning "excess," "luxury," or "abundance."  The "-ive" suffix transforms the noun into an adjective, indicating a characteristic or quality.
