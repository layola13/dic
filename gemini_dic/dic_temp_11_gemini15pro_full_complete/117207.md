measuring-up: /ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ʌp/ | phrasal verb | 衡量；测量；符合(期望)；达到(标准) |The act of assessing something or someone against a standard or expectation.  It can refer to physical measurement, but also to a more figurative assessment of capabilities or performance. | He wasn't measuring up to the job, so he was fired. 他没有达到工作的要求，所以被解雇了。| meet the requirements, live up to expectations, fulfill, satisfy | fall short, disappoint, underperform | verb phrase

measuring-up: /ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ʌp/ | noun | 衡量；估量；评估 | The process or result of evaluating something or someone. |  The measuring-up of the new recruit showed he had the necessary skills. 对新员工的评估表明，他具备必要的技能。 | assessment, evaluation, appraisal | disregard, neglect | noun
