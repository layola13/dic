hofboy:/ˈhɔːfbɔɪ/| n. |酒店服务生，餐厅侍应生|A young man employed as a servant in a hotel or restaurant, especially in Germany or Austria. | He started his career as a hofboy in a grand Viennese hotel. 他在维也纳一家豪华酒店开始了他的酒店服务生生涯。|  The hofboy brought our luggage to the room.  服务生把我们的行李送到房间。|Synonyms: waiter, busboy, servant, attendant |Antonyms: guest, customer, patron|noun
