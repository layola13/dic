desynonymization:/diːsɪˈnɒnɪmaɪˌzeɪʃən/ | n. |去同义化，消除同义词|The process of replacing synonyms with more specific or distinct terms to avoid ambiguity or improve clarity.  It involves choosing the word that most accurately conveys the intended meaning and eliminating other words that might be interpreted differently.| Researchers performed desynonymization on the dataset to improve the accuracy of the model. 研究人员对数据集进行了去同义化处理，以提高模型的准确性。|synonyms: disambiguation, clarification|antonyms: synonymization, conflation|noun
