double-check:/ˈdʌbəl tʃek/ /ˈdʌbəl tʃɛk/ | v. |仔细检查，再次确认| To verify or examine something again to ensure accuracy. | I always double-check my work before submitting it. 我提交工作前总是仔细检查一遍。

double-check:/ˈdʌbəl tʃek/ /ˈdʌbəl tʃɛk/ | n. | 复查，再次确认 | The act of verifying something again. |  The pilot performed a double-check of the instruments before takeoff. 飞行员在起飞前对仪器进行了复查。


double-check:/ˈdʌbəl tʃek/ /ˈdʌbəl tʃɛk/ | adj. (less common) | 双重检查的| Checked twice for accuracy. | I prefer a double-check system for important documents. 对于重要文件，我更喜欢双重检查系统。 (This usage is less common than the verb and noun forms.)


double-check:/ˈdʌbəl tʃek/ /ˈdʌbəl tʃɛk/ |词源| From "double" + "check."  "Double" emphasizes the repetition of the action. |


double-check:/ˈdʌbəl tʃek/ /ˈdʌbəl tʃɛk/ |近义词| verify, confirm, recheck, review, scrutinize |


double-check:/ˈdʌbəl tʃek/ /ˈdʌbəl tʃɛk/ |反义词| overlook, neglect, skip, ignore |
