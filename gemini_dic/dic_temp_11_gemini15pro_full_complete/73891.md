foray:/ˈfɔːreɪ/
n.
突袭，袭击；涉足，尝试
A sudden attack or incursion into enemy territory, especially to obtain something; a venture or an initial attempt, especially outside one's usual area.
The troops made a foray into enemy territory. 部队对敌方领土进行了突袭。
Her foray into acting was not successful. 她涉足表演并不成功。
近义词：raid, incursion, venture, expedition
反义词：retreat, withdrawal
词性: 名词

foray:/ˈfɔːreɪ/
v.
进行突袭；冒险尝试
To make a foray; to venture or to make an initial attempt, especially outside one's usual area.
The soldiers forayed into the forest. 士兵们向森林进发突袭。
He decided to foray into the world of online business. 他决定尝试涉足网络商务。
近义词：raid, venture, try, attempt
反义词：retire, withdraw
词性: 动词
