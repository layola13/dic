forebreast:/ˈfɔːrbrɛst/ | v. |(用…)覆盖胸部 | She forebreasted herself with a shawl. 她用披肩裹住胸部。 | The knight forebreasted himself with his shield. 骑士用盾牌护住胸膛。| synonyms: cover, shield, protect | antonyms: expose, uncover | verb
