nonconcluding:/ˌnɒnkənˈkluːdɪŋ/ | adj. | 非结论性的，无结论的 | Not leading to a definite conclusion; inconclusive. | The meeting ended in a nonconcluding discussion, with no decisions made. 会议以无结论的讨论结束，没有做出任何决定。

The evidence was nonconcluding, leaving the jury unable to reach a verdict. 证据没有定论，陪审团无法作出裁决。 | 近义词: inconclusive, indecisive, unresolved, open-ended | 反义词: conclusive, decisive, definitive, final | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "concluding" (present participle of "conclude," from Latin "concludere" - to shut up, enclose, conclude).
