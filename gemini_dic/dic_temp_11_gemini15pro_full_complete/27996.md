canyoning:/ˈkænjənɪŋ/ /kænjəniːɪŋ/ | n. |峡谷漂流；溪降|The sport of descending canyons by a variety of techniques that may include rappelling, other ropework, swimming, and hiking.|We went canyoning in Utah last summer. 我们去年夏天在犹他州进行了峡谷漂流。|近义词：canyoneering, gorge walking|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider hiking on flat terrain)|词性:名词
