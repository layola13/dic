outskirmish: /ˌaʊtˈskɜːrmɪʃ/ | n. | 小规模战斗的胜利 | The act of outskirmishing someone or something; victory in a skirmish. |  Their outskirmish against the enemy patrol secured valuable intelligence. 他们对敌方巡逻队的小规模战斗胜利获得了宝贵的情报。| 近义词: victory, triumph, success | 反义词: defeat, loss, failure | 词性: 名词
