galage: /ɡəˈlɑːʒ/ | n. | 车库，汽车修理厂 | A building where a car is kept, or a business that repairs cars. |  He parked the car in the galage. 他把车停在了车库里。

The car is being repaired at the local galage. 这辆车正在当地汽车修理厂修理。 | 近义词: garage, auto repair shop | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:  Borrowed from French *galage*, perhaps from Old French *galage* (“a galosh; a clumsy shoe”), possibly of Germanic origin.
