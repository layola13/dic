argon:/ˈɑːrɡɑːn/| n.|氩|A chemical element, a noble gas (symbol Ar, atomic number 18), that is inert and present in the earth's atmosphere.|Argon is often used in welding to create an inert atmosphere.|近义词: (As an inert gas) helium, neon, krypton, xenon|反义词: (Generally, no direct opposite for a chemical element.  Could consider reactive gases as a contrasting group) oxygen, chlorine, fluorine|noun
