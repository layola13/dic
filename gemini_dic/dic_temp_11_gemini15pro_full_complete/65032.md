etagere:/ˌeɪtəˈʒɛər/ | n. | 书架，多层架 | A piece of furniture with open shelves for displaying ornaments, books, etc. |  She displayed her collection of china on the etagere. 她把她的瓷器收藏品陈列在多层架上。

| The etagere in the corner held a variety of potted plants. 角落里的书架上摆放着各种各样的盆栽。 | 近义词: bookshelf, bookcase, shelving unit, stand | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "cabinet" if referring to closed storage) | noun
