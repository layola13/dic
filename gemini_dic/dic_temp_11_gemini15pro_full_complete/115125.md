mangler:/ˈmæŋɡlər/| n. vt.|损坏者，毁坏者；压榨机|Someone or something that mangles things, often by crushing or twisting them.  A machine for pressing or crushing something, especially laundry.|The car was a mangled wreck after the accident. 这辆车在事故后变成了一堆废铁。|近义词：mutilator, destroyer, damager, crusher, wrecker|反义词：repairer, fixer, creator, builder|词性: noun, transitive verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Norwegian word "mangle" meaning "to roll with a mangle," from the Dutch "mangelen" meaning "to mangle," ultimately from the Old French "mangonel" meaning "a catapult." The sense of "to mutilate" arose from the idea of crushing or tearing something.
