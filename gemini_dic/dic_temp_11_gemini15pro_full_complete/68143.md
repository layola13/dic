fail-safe:/ˈfeɪlˌseɪf/| n. adj.|故障保护装置，自动保险装置; 保险的，万无一失的|A fail-safe is a device or system designed to prevent malfunctions from causing accidents or damage, or to minimize damage if an accident occurs.  It can also be used as an adjective to describe something designed with such a device or system.|The nuclear reactor has several fail-safes to prevent a meltdown. 核反应堆有几个故障保护装置以防止熔毁。|Synonyms: backup, safeguard, precaution, safety net|Antonyms: risky, dangerous|Noun, Adjective


fail-safes:/ˈfeɪlˌseɪfs/| n.|故障保护装置，自动保险装置|Plural form of fail-safe.  Refers to multiple devices or systems designed to prevent malfunctions.|The airplane's fail-safes ensured that even with engine failure, it could still land safely. 飞机的故障保护装置确保即使发动机出现故障，它仍然可以安全着陆。|Synonyms: backups, safeguards, precautions, safety nets|Antonyms: risks, dangers|Noun
