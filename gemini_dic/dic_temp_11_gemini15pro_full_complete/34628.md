cleistogamous: /ˌklaɪstˈɒɡəməs/ /ˌklaɪstˈɑːɡəməs/
adjective
闭花受精的
(of a plant) having flowers which do not open, and are self-pollinated.
例句：Cleistogamous flowers ensure seed production even in unfavorable conditions.  闭花受精的花朵即使在不利条件下也能确保种子生产。

例句：Violets produce both cleistogamous and chasmogamous flowers. 紫罗兰既会产生闭花受精的花，也会产生开放授粉的花。

近义词: self-pollinating
反义词: chasmogamous
词性: adjective
