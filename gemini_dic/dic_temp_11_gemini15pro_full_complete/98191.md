insecable:/ɪnˈsɛkəbl/ | adj. | 不可分的，不能分割的 | Not able to be separated or divided. |  The two concepts are insecable, intertwined in a way that makes it impossible to consider one without the other. 这两个概念是不可分割的，以一种使人们不可能只考虑一个而不考虑另一个的方式交织在一起。

The bond between mother and child is often described as insecable. 母子之间的联系常被描述为不可分割的。 | 近义词: indivisible, inseparable | 反义词: separable, divisible | 词性:形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *insecabilis, from Latin in- "not" + secabilis "that can be cut," from secare "to cut."
