make-free-with:/meɪk friː wɪð/
|phrasal verb|
|擅自使用；随意处置；放肆；不客气|
|To use or treat something in a casual or unauthorized way, often implying a lack of respect or permission.|
|He made free with the company's stationery, taking pens and paper home for his own use.|他擅自使用了公司的文具，把钢笔和纸带回家自己用。|
|She made free with my cosmetics without asking.|她未经允许就擅自使用了我的化妆品。|
|近义词: help oneself to, take liberties with|
|反义词: ask permission, respect boundaries|
|词性: 短语动词|
