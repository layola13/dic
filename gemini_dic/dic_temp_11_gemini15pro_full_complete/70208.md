fibrosis:/faɪˈbroʊsɪs/| n. |纤维化|The thickening and scarring of connective tissue, usually as a result of injury. |例句：Lung fibrosis can make it difficult to breathe. 肺纤维化会使呼吸困难。

例句：Liver fibrosis is often caused by chronic alcohol abuse. 肝纤维化通常是由长期酗酒引起的。|近义词：scarring, induration|反义词：resolution, healing|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin fibra ("fiber") + -osis (a suffix indicating a process or condition).
