delivering:/dɪˈlɪvərɪŋ/
v.|递送;发表;履行;接生|The postman is delivering a package. 邮递员正在送包裹。|近义词：carrying, distributing, presenting, giving, conveying, performing, fulfilling, executing|反义词：receiving, accepting, neglecting, failing|词性：动词的现在分词

delivering:/dɪˈlɪvərɪŋ/
adj.|传送的;分娩的|She has a delivering smile. 她有一个极具感染力的笑容。(比喻用法，表示传递快乐等)|近义词：contagious, infectious, communicative|反义词：uninspiring, dull|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *delyveren*, from Anglo-Norman *deliverer*, from Old French *delivrer* (“to free, deliver”), from Latin *dēliberāre* (“to free, liberate, set free”), from *dē* (“from, out of”) + *liberāre* (“to free”), from *liber* (“free”).
