arthropleura:/ˌɑːrθrəˈplʊərə/ | n. |节肢动物门多足亚门倍足纲的已灭绝的一属。| An extinct genus of millipede arthropods that lived from the late Carboniferous to early Permian periods (around 345 to 295 million years ago).  It is the largest known land invertebrate of all time. |例句：Arthropleura was a giant millipede that lived millions of years ago.  巨型马陆节胸属生活在数百万年前。

例句：Fossils of Arthropleura have been found in Europe and North America.  节胸属的化石在欧洲和北美都有发现。|近义词：millipede (泛指马陆), giant millipede (巨型马陆)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology->
From Ancient Greek ἄρθρον (árthron, “joint”) + πλευρά (pleurá, “rib” or “side”).  字面意思是“连接的肋骨”。
