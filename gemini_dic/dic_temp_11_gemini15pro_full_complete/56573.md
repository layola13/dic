dominoes:/ˈdɒmɪnəʊz/ (UK) /ˈdɑːmɪˌnoʊz/ (US)| n.|多米诺骨牌；多米诺骨牌游戏|A game played with small rectangular blocks, each marked with a number of spots, in which the blocks are arranged so that when one is knocked over, the others fall in sequence. Also, the blocks themselves.|We played dominoes all afternoon. 我们一下午都在玩多米诺骨牌。|近义词:tiles|反义词:None|词性:noun

domino:/ˈdɒmɪnəʊ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːmɪˌnoʊ/ (US)| n.|多米诺骨牌|One of the small rectangular blocks used in the game of dominoes.|He picked up a domino and looked at it. 他拿起一块多米诺骨牌看了看。|近义词:tile|反义词:None|词性:noun
