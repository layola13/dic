nonanaemic:/ˌnɒnəˈniːmɪk/
adjective
非贫血的
Not anemic; having a normal blood count.
The patient presented with nonanaemic symptoms, despite feeling fatigued.  这个病人表现出非贫血症状，尽管感到疲劳。
The doctor confirmed she was nonanaemic after reviewing her blood test results. 医生在查看了她的血检结果后，确认她没有贫血。
近义词: healthy, robust
反义词: anaemic
