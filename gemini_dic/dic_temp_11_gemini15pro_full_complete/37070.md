combativity:/kəmˈbætɪvɪti/
noun
好战性，战斗力，斗志
The quality of being eager to fight or argue.
The team showed great combativity throughout the match, despite being down by two goals.  这支球队整场比赛都表现出很强的斗志，尽管落后两球。
synonyms: aggressiveness, pugnacity, belligerence
antonyms:  passivity, docility, meekness
n.
