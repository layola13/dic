areopagitic:/ˌæriːəpəˈdʒɪtɪk/
adjective
雅典最高法院的；关于雅典最高法院的；类似雅典最高法院的
Pertaining to the Areopagus, the highest court in ancient Athens, or resembling its style or gravity.
The speaker delivered an areopagitic discourse on the importance of justice. 演讲者就正义的重要性发表了一篇类似雅典最高法院风格的庄严演讲。
Synonyms: judicial, solemn, grave, formal, authoritative
Antonyms: informal, casual, frivolous, lighthearted
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Greek Areios Pagos (Ἄρειος Πάγος) meaning "Ares' Rock," the hill where the court met.  The word entered English via Late Latin Areopagita and French aréopagitique.
