nominee:/ˌnɑːmɪˈniː/
n.
被提名人；候选人
a person who is nominated for an award, position, or honor
例句：She is a nominee for the Best Actress award. 她获得了最佳女演员奖的提名。

例句：The nominees for the presidency were announced yesterday.  总统候选人名单昨天公布了。

近义词：candidate, contender, applicant
反义词：nominator, elector

词性: noun
