defragmented:/ˌdiːfræɡˈmɛntɪd/ | adj. |碎片整理的，整理过的|Reassembled from fragments; no longer fragmented.  Put together again from many different pieces.|The defragmented hard drive now runs much faster. 整理后的硬盘现在运行得更快了。|reorganized, consolidated, unified|fragmented, scattered, disorganized|adjective


defragment:/ˌdiːfræɡˈmɛnt/ | vt. | 整理碎片，碎片整理|To reorganize files on a hard disk so that they are stored in contiguous blocks, thus improving performance. |I need to defragment my hard drive to improve its speed. 我需要整理我的硬盘碎片以提高它的速度。|reorganize, consolidate, optimize|fragment, scatter, clutter|verb

词源: de- (prefix meaning "removal" or "reversal") + fragment (noun meaning "a small part broken off or detached")
