non-inclusive:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkluːsɪv/ | adj. | 非包容性的，不包含所有人的 | Not including everyone or everything; exclusive. |  This price is non-inclusive of tax. 这个价格不含税。

The study used a non-inclusive sample of participants.  这项研究使用了不具有代表性的参与者样本。


|近义词：exclusive, restrictive, limiting |反义词: inclusive, comprehensive, all-encompassing |词性: adjective
