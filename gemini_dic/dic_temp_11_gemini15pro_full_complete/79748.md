glummest:/ˈɡlʌməst/ | adj. | 最阴郁的，最沮丧的 | Superlative adjective of gloomy; most gloomy.  Characterized by or expressing gloom. Feeling sad and discouraged. | The glummest day of the year was the day my dog died. 一年中我最沮丧的一天就是我的狗去世的那天。|  The weather was glummest I've ever seen. 我从未见过如此阴郁的天气。 | 近义词: saddest, most depressed, most dejected | 反义词: happiest, most cheerful, most joyful | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *glom, glombe (“twilight, dusk”), from Old Norse glám (“gleam, glimpse of light”), from Proto-Germanic *glēmaz (“gleam, shine”).  The sense shifted from a faint light to a subdued, melancholic mood.
