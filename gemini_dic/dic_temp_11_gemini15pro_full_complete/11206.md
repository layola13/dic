architectural:/ˌɑːrkɪˈtektʃərəl/
adjective
建筑的；建筑学的；有关建筑的
Relating to architecture, or the art and science of designing and constructing buildings.
The architectural style of the building is Gothic. 这座建筑的建筑风格是哥特式的。
The city is known for its unique architectural landscape.  这座城市以其独特的建筑景观而闻名。

synonyms: structural, building, constructional
antonyms: non-architectural, shapeless

词性: adjective
