deadweight:/ˈdɛdˌweɪt/| n.|自重；累赘；净重；重负|A deadweight is a heavy, useless burden or something that makes it more difficult for something to succeed.  It can also refer to the actual weight of an inert object.|The high taxes were a deadweight on the economy. 高额税收对经济造成了沉重负担。|Synonyms: burden, encumbrance, drag, load, impediment, obstacle|Antonyms: asset, advantage, boon, help, support|noun
