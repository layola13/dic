aberuncate: /ˌæbəˈrʌŋkeɪt/
adjective
除去枝杈的；剪除小枝的
having had small branches or twigs removed; pruned.
The gardener aberuncated the fruit trees in the orchard. 园丁修剪了果园里的果树。
近义词: pruned, trimmed, lopped
反义词: overgrown, unpruned
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *aberuncatus*, from ab- + runcare (“to weed out, clear away”), from runco (“a weeding hook”).
