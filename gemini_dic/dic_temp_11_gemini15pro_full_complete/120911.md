miscarry:/mɪsˈkæri/ | vi. | 流产；失败；未送达 | To fail to achieve the intended purpose; to end unsuccessfully, especially of a plan or project; (of a pregnancy) to end prematurely and result in the expulsion of a nonviable fetus. | The project miscarried due to lack of funding.  这个项目由于缺乏资金而失败了。 | Synonyms: fail, fall through, abort, backfire | Antonyms: succeed, flourish, thrive | verb
