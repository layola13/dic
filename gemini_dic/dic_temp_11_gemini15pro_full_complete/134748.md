nonmelodic:/ˌnɒn.məˈlɒd.ɪk/ /ˌnɑːn.məˈlɑː.dɪk/| adj.|非旋律的，无旋律的|Lacking melody; not melodic.|The piece was nonmelodic, focusing on rhythm and texture.|近义词：atonal, unmelodious|反义词: melodic, tuneful|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- + melodic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "melodic" refers to having a pleasing melody.
