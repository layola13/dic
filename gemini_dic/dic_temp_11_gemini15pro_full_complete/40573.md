contusing:/kənˈtjuːzɪŋ/
adjective
使挫伤的；使青肿的
causing a bruise or bruises.
The blow was contusing, leaving a large purple mark on his arm. 那一击造成了挫伤，在他的胳膊上留下了一大块紫色的痕迹。
contusing:/kənˈtjuːzɪŋ/
adjective
令人困惑的；令人费解的
confusing or baffling.
The instructions were poorly written and rather contusing. 说明书写得很差，而且相当令人费解。


近义词:bruising, damaging, confusing, perplexing
反义词:soothing, healing, clarifying
