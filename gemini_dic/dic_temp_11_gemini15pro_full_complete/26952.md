calcedonic:/kælˈsɛdɒnɪk/ /kælˈsɛdɑːnɪk/| adj.|玉髓的，玉髓质的|Of or relating to chalcedony, a microcrystalline variety of quartz.|The artist used a calcedonic glaze to achieve a unique effect. 艺术家使用玉髓釉来达到一种独特的效果。|近义词:chalcedony, agate|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *calcedonius, from Latin chalcedonius, from Greek khalkēdōn (χαλκηδών), of unknown origin. Possibly from Chalcedon (Χαλκηδών), an ancient port city in Bithynia (modern Turkey).
