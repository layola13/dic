hopperings:/ˈhɒpərɪŋz/
noun
跳跃；蹦跳；残渣；酒糟
Remnants or leftovers, especially from brewing or distilling. Also, the action of hopping or jumping, though this usage is less common.
The hopperings from the beer-making process were fed to the pigs.  啤酒酿造过程中剩下的酒糟被用来喂猪。
The children's hopperings in the leaves delighted their parents. 孩子们在树叶中蹦蹦跳跳，使他们的父母很高兴。
remnants, leftovers, dregs, leavings
completions, wholeness (for remnants meaning leftovers)
