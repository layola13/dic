croqueting:/kroʊˈkeɪɪŋ/ /krəʊˈkeɪɪŋ/| n. |槌球运动；玩槌球|The act of playing croquet.  This involves hitting wooden or plastic balls through hoops with a mallet.|例句：We spent a leisurely afternoon croqueting on the lawn. 我们在草坪上悠闲地度过了一个下午，打槌球。

例句：Croqueting is a popular pastime in the English countryside.  在英国乡村，槌球是一项很受欢迎的消遣活动。|近义词：playing croquet|反义词:|n.
