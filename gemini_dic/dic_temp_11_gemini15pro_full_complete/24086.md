bretwalda:/ˈbrɛtwɑːldə/
n.
霸主，宗主，支配者
An Anglo-Saxon king who held a position of overlordship over other Anglo-Saxon kingdoms.  A ruler of all Britain, or of a considerable part of it, during the Anglo-Saxon Heptarchy.
Offa of Mercia is considered one of the most powerful bretwaldas.  奥法是麦西亚王国最有权势的霸主之一。
Egbert of Wessex eventually established himself as bretwalda.  威塞克斯的埃格伯特最终确立了自己作为霸主的地位。
近义词: overlord, high king, suzerain
反义词: subject, vassal
