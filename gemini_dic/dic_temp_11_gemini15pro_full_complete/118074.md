mendicity:/mɛnˈdɪsɪti/| n. |乞讨；行乞|The practice of begging.  The state of being a beggar.|He resorted to mendicity to survive after losing his job.  他失业后为了生存 resorted to mendicity. |Synonyms: begging, panhandling, supplication, almsgiving, pauperism|Antonyms: affluence, wealth, prosperity, charity (as in giving, not receiving)|noun
