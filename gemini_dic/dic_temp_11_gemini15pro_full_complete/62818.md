enharmonically:/ɛnhɑːrˈmɑːnɪkli/
adv.
和声地，等音地
In music theory, enharmonically refers to the use of notes that sound the same but are written differently.  在音乐理论中，等音地指的是使用听起来相同但写法不同的音符。
The chord can be spelled enharmonically in several ways. 这个和弦可以用几种等音的方式来拼写。
近义词: equivalently (in terms of sound)
反义词: (No direct antonym, but the concept opposes distinct pitches)
词性: adverb
