irreversiblenesses:/ˌɪrɪˈvɜːrsəbəlˌnɛsɪz/
noun
不可逆性；不可逆转性
The quality or state of being irreversible; the fact that a process cannot be reversed.
The irreversiblenesses of the aging process are a constant reminder of our mortality.  衰老过程的不可逆性不断提醒着我们的死亡。
Climate change has reached a point of irreversiblenesses, demanding urgent action. 气候变化已经到了不可逆转的地步，需要紧急行动。
Synonym: irreversibility, immutability, finality
Antonym: reversibility, mutability
