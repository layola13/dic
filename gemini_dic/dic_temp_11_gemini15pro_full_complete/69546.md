fellow-feelings:/ˈfɛləʊ ˌfiːlɪŋz/
n.| 同情，同感，共鸣|A feeling of sympathy and understanding for someone, especially someone who is suffering.|Example: She felt strong fellow-feelings for the refugees. 她对难民深感同情。|Example:  A sense of fellow-feelings developed between the two war veterans. 两位退伍军人之间产生了同袍之情。|近义词：sympathy, empathy, compassion, commiseration|反义词：antipathy, indifference, apathy|词性: 名词

fellow:/ˈfɛləʊ/
n. adj.|同伴，家伙；同道的|A person in the same position, involved in the same activity, or otherwise associated with another.|
feeling:/ˈfiːlɪŋ/
n.|感觉，感情；触觉|An emotional state or reaction.|
