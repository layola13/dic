coercer:/koʊˈɜːrsər/
|n.|胁迫者，强迫者|A person who forces another to act in an involuntary way by using intimidation or threats.|The coercer demanded money from the victim. 胁迫者向受害者索要钱财。|
|Synonyms:|bully, intimidator, oppressor, tyrant|
