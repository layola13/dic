criers:/ˈkraɪərz/| n.| 哭泣者；叫卖者；传令官；公开宣称者 | People who cry, shout out announcements, or proclaim something publicly. | The town criers announced the king's arrival. 城镇传令官宣布国王驾临了。|  synonyms: weepers, wailers, hawkers, proclaimers | antonyms: laughers, rejoicers, concealers | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English criour, from Old French crieur, from crier "to shout," from Latin quiritare "to call for help, shriek," perhaps of imitative origin.
