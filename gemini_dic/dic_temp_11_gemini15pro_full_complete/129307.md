noggen:/ˈnɒɡən/| n.|诺格酒，一种加了鸡蛋、牛奶和酒的饮料|A strong ale brewed with eggs, milk, and spices.  A rich, creamy, and often alcoholic beverage.|He enjoyed a warm mug of noggen on a cold winter's night. 他在一个寒冷的冬夜享用了一大杯热腾腾的诺格酒。|近义词：eggnog, posset|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "soft drink" for a contrasting beverage)|名词

词源:
From Middle English *noggyn, probably from nogg "strong ale" + -in, diminutive suffix.
