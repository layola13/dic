fleecy:/ˈfliːsi/| adj.|羊毛制的，蓬松的，柔软的|Covered with fleece; soft and woolly.  Resembling fleece; soft and light. |The sheep had a thick, fleecy coat.  这只羊的毛厚厚的，像羊毛一样。|The blanket was warm and fleecy.  毯子既温暖又蓬松。|近义词：wooly, fluffy, downy, soft|反义词：coarse, rough, harsh|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *flesy, from Old English *flēsig, from flēos (“fleece”) +‎ -ig (“-y”). Cognate with Dutch vliesig (“fleecy”).
