blusteringly:/ˈblʌstərɪŋli/| adv.|咆哮地；狂妄地；喧闹地|In a loud, noisy, and boastful manner, often implying empty threats or bluster.|He blusteringly declared he would fire everyone who disagreed with him. 他咆哮着宣称要解雇所有不同意他的人。|近义词：boisterously, loudly, aggressively, vehemently|反义词：quietly, calmly, softly|词性: 副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From bluster + -ing + -ly.  "Bluster" comes from Middle English *blustren*, probably of imitative origin.
