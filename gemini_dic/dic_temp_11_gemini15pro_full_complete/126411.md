mystagogical:/ˌmɪstəˈɡɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 神秘的，玄妙的，奥秘的 | Relating to or characterized by mystagogy, which is the explanation or interpretation of religious mysteries.  It often implies a veiled or esoteric meaning, relating to spiritual or mystical teachings. |  The mystagogical interpretations of the ancient rituals were fascinating. 古代仪式的奥秘解释令人着迷。| Near Synonyms: mystical, esoteric, arcane, occult, cryptic | Antonyms: clear, obvious, straightforward, exoteric | adjective
