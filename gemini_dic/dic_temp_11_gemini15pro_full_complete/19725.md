bilk:/bɪlk/| vt.|欺骗，诈骗；躲债|To cheat or defraud someone of money or goods; to evade payment of a debt.|He bilked investors out of millions of dollars. 他骗取了投资者数百万美元。|近义词：cheat, defraud, swindle, deceive|反义词：repay, compensate|verb


bilk:/bɪlk/| n.|诈骗，欺诈行为|An act of cheating or defrauding someone.|The bilk cost the company thousands of dollars.  这起诈骗使公司损失了数千美元。|近义词：fraud, swindle, deception|反义词：repayment, compensation|noun


Etymology:  Probably from  bilk (n.), "nothing, a cheating at cribbage," of uncertain origin, perhaps related to balk.
