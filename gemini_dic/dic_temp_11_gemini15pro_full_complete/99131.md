interceptor:/ˌɪntərˈseptər/| n.|拦截器，截击机|A thing or person that intercepts something or someone.|The fighter jet acted as an interceptor, preventing the enemy bomber from reaching its target. 这架战斗机充当了拦截器，阻止了敌方轰炸机到达目标。|近义词：blocker, defender|反义词：facilitator, enabler|词性:noun

Etymology: From the verb 'intercept', which comes from the Latin 'intercipere' (to intercept, seize), from 'inter-' (between) + 'capere' (to take).
