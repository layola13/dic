loathsomely:/ˈloʊðsəmli/ | adv. | 令人厌恶地，讨厌地 | In a manner that inspires disgust or aversion. | He described the scene loathsomely, making everyone feel sick. 他令人作呕地描述了那个场景，让每个人都感到恶心。| The rotten meat smelled loathsomely. 腐烂的肉散发着令人作呕的气味。 | 近义词：disgustingly, repulsively, abhorrently, offensively | 反义词：pleasantly, attractively, appealingly | 词性: 副词

loathsome:/ˈloʊðsəm/ | adj. | 令人厌恶的，讨厌的 | Causing hatred or disgust; repulsive. |  The loathsome smell of the garbage made me gag. 垃圾令人作呕的气味让我作呕。 |  He had a loathsome habit of picking his nose in public. 他有一个令人讨厌的习惯，就是在公共场合挖鼻子。 | 近义词：disgusting, repulsive, abhorrent, offensive | 反义词：pleasant, attractive, appealing | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Middle English *lothsom, from Old English lāðsum (“hateful, loathsome, hostile”), from lāð (“hateful, hostile, evil, loath”) + -sum. Cognate with Scots laithsome (“loathsome”), West Frisian lêdsum (“troublesome, tiresome”), Dutch leedsam (“troublesome, tiresome”), German leid (“sorrowful, sorry”). More at loath, -some.
