oppressible:/əˈprɛsəbəl/
adjective
可压迫的；可抑制的
Capable of being oppressed or suppressed.
The oppressive regime silenced all dissent.  这个压迫性的政权压制了所有异议。
The small country felt oppressible under the rule of its larger neighbor.  这个小国在其强大邻国的统治下感到压抑。
synonyms: subjugable, suppressible, tyrannizable
antonyms:  unyielding, resistant, indomitable
词性: adjective
