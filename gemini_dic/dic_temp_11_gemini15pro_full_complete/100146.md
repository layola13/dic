interradial:/ˌɪntərˈreɪdiəl/| adj.|间辐的，位于辐间的|Situated between two radii, especially of a starfish or other radially symmetrical organism.|The interradial plates of the starfish were a vibrant orange. 海星的间辐板是鲜艳的橙色。|Synonyms: interambulacral|Antonyms: radial|adjective

interradial:/ˌɪntərˈreɪdiəl/| n.|间辐部|The area between two radii, especially of a starfish or other radially symmetrical organism.|The spines on the interradial are shorter than those on the radial. 间辐部上的棘比辐部上的短。|Synonyms: interambulacrum|Antonyms: radius|noun
