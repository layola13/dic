antivirals:/ˌæntiˈvaɪrəlz/ | n. | 抗病毒药 |  Medicines that are used to treat viral infections. These drugs work by interfering with the virus's ability to replicate or by boosting the body's immune response to the virus. |  The doctor prescribed antivirals to treat her influenza. 医生给她开了抗病毒药来治疗她的流感。 | synonyms: antiviral medications, antiviral drugs, virus inhibitors | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but conceptually: proviral) | noun

antiviral:/ˌæntiˈvaɪrəl/ | adj. | 抗病毒的 | Used to describe something that works against viruses. |  Antiviral drugs can help reduce the severity of some viral infections. 抗病毒药物可以帮助减轻某些病毒感染的严重程度。| synonyms: virus-fighting, virucidal, anti-virus| antonyms: proviral| adjective
