extends:/ɪkˈstɛndz/ | v. | 延伸，延长，扩大；给予，提供； |The road extends for miles. 这条路延伸数英里。|近义词: lengthen, prolong, expand, stretch, offer, grant |反义词: shorten, contract, limit, restrict | verb

Etymology:
From Middle English extenden, from Old French estendre, from Latin extendere (to stretch out, spread, enlarge), from ex- (out) + tendere (to stretch).
