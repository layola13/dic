megaphoning:/ˈmɛɡəˌfoʊnɪŋ/ | v. | 用扩音器喊叫；大声疾呼 | The activist was megaphoning his message to the crowd.  那名活动家正用扩音器向人群大声疾呼他的信息。 |  The politician was megaphoning about the need for change. 这位政治家正用扩音器大声疾呼变革的必要性。 | 近义词: shouting, proclaiming, amplifying | 反义词: whispering, muttering | 词性: 动词

词源:  From "megaphone" + "-ing" suffix. "Megaphone" comes from Greek "mega-" (large) + "phone" (sound).
