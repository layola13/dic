angelolatry: /ˌeɪndʒɪˈlɒlətri/ | n. | 天使崇拜；天使崇敬 | The worship or veneration of angels. |  His paintings seemed to verge on angelolatry, depicting winged beings with an almost divine reverence. 他的绘画似乎近乎天使崇拜，描绘了带有近乎神圣崇敬的有翼生物。|  Near synonyms: angel worship, veneration of angels | Antonyms: None (lack of veneration might be considered an opposite, but there isn't a single word for it) | noun
