overexert:/ˌōvərɪɡˈzɜːrt/| v.|过度劳累，用力过度|To exert (oneself or a bodily part) too much.|He overexerted himself while jogging and pulled a muscle. 他慢跑时用力过度，拉伤了肌肉。|近义词：strain, overwork, overstrain|反义词:relax, rest|verb
