opisthobranchia:/əˌpɪsθəˈbræŋkiə/
n.
后鳃亚纲
a subclass of marine gastropod mollusks, characterized by the partial or complete loss of the shell and the possession of gills and other respiratory structures located behind the heart.
Opisthobranchia include sea slugs, sea hares, and bubble snails. 后鳃亚纲包括海蛞蝓、海兔和泡螺。
Opisthobranchia are a fascinating group of mollusks. 后鳃亚纲是一组迷人的软体动物。
