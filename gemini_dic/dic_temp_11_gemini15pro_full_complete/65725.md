eventually:/ɪˈventʃuəli/ /ɪˈvenʧəli/| adv.|最终，最后|in the end, especially after a long delay, dispute, or series of problems.|After much deliberation, they eventually decided to go.|近义词:finally, ultimately, in the end, at last|反义词:initially, at first, immediately|词性:副词

词源: From Late Latin *eventualiter, from Latin eventualis (“final, ultimate”), from eventus (“outcome, issue, event”).
