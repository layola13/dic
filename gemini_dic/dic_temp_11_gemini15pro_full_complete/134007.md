nonhospital:/ˌnɒnˈhɒspɪtl/ | adj. | 非医院的，院外的 | Not relating to or occurring in a hospital. |  Patients can now receive many treatments in nonhospital settings. 患者现在可以在非医院环境中接受许多治疗。 | The clinic provides nonhospital care for minor injuries.  这家诊所为轻伤提供非医院护理。 | 近义词: outpatient, community-based | 反义词: hospital, inpatient | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix) + hospital

non-: from Latin *non, "not". This prefix negates the word it is attached to.
hospital: from Old French hospital, ultimately from Latin hospitale, "guest house," from hospes, "host."
