hold-to:/ˈhoʊld tuː/| phrasal verb|坚持，遵守，信守|To adhere to or maintain (a belief, principle, or promise).|He held to his promise to visit his grandmother every Sunday. 他信守承诺，每个星期天都去看望他的祖母。|例句：She held to her principles despite the pressure. 即使面临压力，她也坚持自己的原则。|例句：They held to their original plan despite the setbacks. 尽管遇到挫折，他们仍然坚持最初的计划。|近义词：adhere to, stick to, cling to, maintain, uphold|反义词：abandon, forsake, relinquish|词性:短语动词
