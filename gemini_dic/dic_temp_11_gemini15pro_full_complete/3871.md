agrammatist: /əˈɡræmətɪst/
n.
不懂文法的人；不会写字的人;文盲
A person who is ungrammatical or who makes grammatical errors; an illiterate person.
He was considered an agramatist because he couldn't construct a proper sentence. 他因为他无法构建一个合适的句子而被认为是一个文盲。
The agramatist struggled to write a coherent letter. 这个文盲努力写一封连贯的信。
近义词: illiterate, unlettered
反义词: grammarian, literate
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *agrammātos* (illiterate, unlearned), from *a-* (not) + *gramma* (letter)
