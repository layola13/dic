interoscillates:/ˌɪntərˈɒsɪleɪts/ | v. |相互振荡，互相摆动 | To swing or vibrate back and forth in relation to each other.  The two pendulums interoscillated, creating a mesmerizing pattern.  两个钟摆相互振荡，创造出一个迷人的图案。 | Example: The interconnected springs interoscillated with varying frequencies.  相互连接的弹簧以不同的频率相互振荡。

Example:  The electromagnetic fields interoscillated, causing interference. 电磁场相互振荡，造成干扰。 | Synonyms: interact, resonate, oscillate together | Antonyms: stabilize, steady, remain static | Verb
