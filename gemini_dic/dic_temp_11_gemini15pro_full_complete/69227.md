fears:/fɪrz/
n.|恐惧，害怕，担忧|An emotion induced by perceived danger or threat.|She has a fear of heights. 她有恐高症。|
近义词: anxieties, worries, concerns, apprehensions, terrors
反义词: courage, bravery, confidence, fearlessness
词性:名词(plural of fear)

fear:/fɪr/
n.|恐惧，害怕，担忧|An unpleasant emotion caused by the perception of danger, pain, or harm.|He felt a sudden fear. 他感到一阵突如其来的恐惧。|
近义词: terror, dread, fright, anxiety, apprehension
反义词: courage, bravery, confidence, boldness
词性:名词

fear:/fɪr/
v.|害怕，恐惧，担忧|Be afraid of (someone or something) as likely to be dangerous, painful, or threatening.|I fear for his safety. 我担心他的安全。|
近义词: dread, be afraid of, worry about, be anxious about, be apprehensive about
反义词:  be confident, be unafraid, be brave
词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fere*, from Old English *fǣr* (“danger, sudden attack, fear, terror”), from Proto-Germanic *fērą* (“danger, ambush, sudden attack”), from Proto-Indo-European *pér- (“to try, risk”). Cognate with West Frisian *fear* (“danger”), Dutch *vaar* (“danger”), German *Gefahr* (“danger”), Gothic 𐍆𐌰𐌹𐍂𐌰 (*faira*, “snare”).
