deactivate:/diːˈæktɪveɪt/
verb
使失效，使不活动，停用
to make something inactive or no longer working
The security system was deactivated.  安全系统被停用了。
synonyms: disable, shut down, turn off
antonyms: activate, enable, turn on
词性: 动词


deactivate:/diːˈæktɪveɪt/
verb
使(炸弹等)失效; 拆除(炸弹等)的引信
to make a bomb or other device unable to explode
The bomb squad deactivated the explosive device.  拆弹小组拆除了爆炸装置。
synonyms: disarm, defuse
antonyms: arm, activate
词性: 动词
