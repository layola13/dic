counterproductively:/ˌkaʊntərprəˈdʌktɪvli/
adverb
适得其反地
in a way that has the opposite effect to the one intended, and therefore harmful
Working late into the night can be counterproductive.  熬夜工作可能会适得其反。
Synonyms: harmfully, detrimentally, disadvantageously, adversely, negatively
Antonyms: productively, beneficially, helpfully, constructively
词性: adverb
