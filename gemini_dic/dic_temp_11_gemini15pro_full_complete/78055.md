geocacher:/ˈdʒiːoʊˌkæʃər/
n.
寻宝者，地理藏宝者
A person who participates in geocaching.
例句：The geocacher found the hidden container under a log.  寻宝者在一根木头下面找到了隐藏的容器。

例句：Experienced geocachers often hide caches in very clever locations.  经验丰富的寻宝者经常把宝藏藏在非常巧妙的地方。

近义词：treasure hunter, cacher
反义词：(None -  there isn't a direct opposite for someone who participates in a specific hobby)
词性: noun
