crinoline: /ˈkrɪnəlɪn/ /ˈkrɪnəlɪːn/ | n. | 裙撑; 衬裙 | A stiff fabric made of horsehair and linen or cotton, used especially formerly to make petticoats and to give skirts a full shape.  A woman's skirt stiffened with such material. |  She wore a crinoline under her wedding dress. 她穿着婚纱，里面衬着裙撑。| 近义词: petticoat, hoop skirt | 反义词: - | 名词

crinoline: /ˈkrɪnəlɪn/ /ˈkrɪnəlɪːn/ | adj. | 用裙撑支撑的 | Supported by a crinoline. | Her crinoline skirt swayed as she walked. 她穿着裙撑的裙子走起来摇曳生姿。 | 近义词: bouffant, full-skirted | 反义词: - | 形容词
