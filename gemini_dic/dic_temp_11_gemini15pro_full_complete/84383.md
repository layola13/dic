hall: /hɔːl/ (British) /hɑːl/ (American) | n. | 大厅，礼堂，走廊 | A large room or building used for public gatherings, events, or ceremonies. Also a corridor or passageway in a building. |  The wedding reception was held in a grand hall. 婚礼招待会在一个宏伟的大厅举行。

The students gathered in the entrance hall before class. 学生们在上课前聚集在入口大厅。


近义词: auditorium, lobby, corridor, foyer, entranceway
反义词: room, chamber, closet
词性: noun
