fripper:/ˈfrɪpər/| n. |旧货商；廉价服装；无价值的东西|A dealer in secondhand clothes, especially cheap or tawdry finery.  Something frivolous or worthless.|She found a great vintage dress at the fripper. 她在旧货商那里找到了一件很棒的复古连衣裙。| The project was dismissed as a mere fripper.  这个项目被认为是毫无价值的东西而被驳回了。|近义词：peddler, hawker, trinket, trifle|反义词:treasure, valuable|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *fripier, from Old French friper "to rub, wear away," of Germanic origin.
