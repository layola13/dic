chanan: /ˈkænən/ | n. | 领袖，首领; 宗族首领，氏族首领; 酋长; (苏格兰)氏族首领 |  A hereditary title of a chief, especially in Scotland and Ireland.  The word often implies leadership and respect within a clan or community. | 例句：The clanan led his people through difficult times.  氏族首领带领他的人民度过了艰难时期。

例句：The chanan's wisdom was revered by all.  酋长的智慧受到所有人的尊敬。| 近义词：chief, chieftain, leader, head | 反义词: follower, subordinate | 词性: noun

词源: From Scottish Gaelic *ceann* meaning "head" and *fhionn* meaning "fair" or "white." This evolved to *ceannaidh* meaning "leader". The word was then borrowed into English.
