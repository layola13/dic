garishness:/ˈɡɛərɪʃnəs/
noun
俗丽；艳俗；炫耀
The state or quality of being garish; excessive brightness or showiness.
The garishness of the decorations was overwhelming.  装饰的俗丽令人难以忍受。
近义词: gaudiness, flashiness, tawdriness
反义词: subtlety, understatement, elegance
词性: noun

garish:/ˈɡɛərɪʃ/
adjective
俗丽的；艳俗的；炫耀的
Excessively bright or showy, typically so as to be tasteless.
She wore a garish pink dress. 她穿了一件俗艳的粉红色连衣裙。
近义词: gaudy, flashy, tawdry
反义词: subtle, understated, elegant
词性: adjective
