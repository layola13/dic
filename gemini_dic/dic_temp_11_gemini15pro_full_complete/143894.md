outpourings:/ˈaʊtpɔːrɪŋz/
noun
流露；倾泻；涌出
An unrestrained expression of strong feelings.  A sudden or abundant flow or discharge.
There has been an outpouring of grief since his death. 自他去世以来，人们一直在倾诉悲痛。
effusion, overflow, outburst, discharge
suppression, concealment
