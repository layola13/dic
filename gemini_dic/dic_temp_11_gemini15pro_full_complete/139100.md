obduction:/əbˈdʌkʃən/| n.|逆冲，仰冲；（地质学）地壳的覆盖|The obduction of oceanic lithosphere onto continental margins is a fundamental process in plate tectonics. 海洋岩石圈向大陆边缘的逆冲是板块构造中的一个基本过程。|例句：The geologists studied the obduction of the oceanic plate. 地质学家研究了海洋板块的逆冲。

例句：The obduction process formed a mountain range. 逆冲过程形成了一座山脉。|近义词：overthrust, thrusting|反义词：subduction|词性:noun
