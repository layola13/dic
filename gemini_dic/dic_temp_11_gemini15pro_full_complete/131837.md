nonconsorting:/nɒnkənˈsɔːtɪŋ/
noun
不交往，不结交；避免与…来往
the act of avoiding the company of a particular person or group of people, often because they are considered undesirable or disreputable.
例句：The school implemented a policy of nonconsorting between students and known gang members. 学校实施了一项学生与已知帮派成员不交往的政策。

例句：Nonconsorting clauses are common in bail conditions for suspected criminals.  不交往条款在犯罪嫌疑人的保释条件中很常见。
近义词：avoidance, shunning, ostracism
反义词：association, fraternization
词性：n.
