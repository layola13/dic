fairest:/ˈfeə.rɪst/ /ˈfer.ɪst/| adj.|最美丽的，最公平的|superlative of fair, meaning most beautiful or most just|例句：She was the fairest of them all. 她是所有人中最美丽的。|近义词：prettiest, most beautiful, most just, most equitable|反义词：ugliest, most unjust, most inequitable|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *fairest*, superlative of *fair*, from Old English *fæger* (beautiful, pleasant, calm), from Proto-Germanic *fagraz* (fit, suitable), from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂ǵ-ró-s* (decorated, adorned).  The meaning shifted from "pleasing in appearance" to "morally good, just" in Middle English.
