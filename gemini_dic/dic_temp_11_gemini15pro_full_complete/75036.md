frieze:/friːz/| n. |雕带，饰带，横条|A decorative horizontal band, often along the top of a wall, either painted or sculpted in low relief.|The ancient Greek temple was adorned with a beautiful frieze depicting scenes from mythology.  这座古希腊神庙装饰着一条美丽的雕带，描绘了神话中的场景。|Synonyms: band, border, decoration, ornament|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun


frieze:/friːz/| v. |（使）结冰，（使）冻住|To become covered with or as if with a coating of ice; to freeze.|The cold wind friezed the surface of the puddle. 冷风使水坑的表面结冰了。|Synonyms: freeze, congeal, chill|Antonyms: thaw, melt|verb
