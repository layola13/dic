ambiances:/ˈæmbiənsɪz/ /ˈɑːmbiɑːnsɪz/
noun
气氛，氛围
The restaurant had a romantic ambiance. 这家餐厅有一种浪漫的氛围。
The soft music created a relaxing ambiance. 轻柔的音乐营造出轻松的氛围。
synonym: atmosphere, mood, setting, vibe
antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider "discord","cacophony" for opposite feeling)
词性: 名词
