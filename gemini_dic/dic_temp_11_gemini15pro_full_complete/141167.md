ooesporic: /ˌoʊoʊˈspɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 卵孢子的 |  Relating to or characterized by the production of oospores.  Derived from "oo-" (egg) + "spore."  | Example: The oosporic fungi reproduce sexually by forming oospores. 卵孢子真菌通过形成卵孢子进行有性繁殖。 | Example:  Oosporic reproduction is common in certain types of algae and fungi. 卵孢子繁殖在某些类型的藻类和真菌中很常见。 | Near synonyms: oogamous | Antonym: N/A | word type: adjective
