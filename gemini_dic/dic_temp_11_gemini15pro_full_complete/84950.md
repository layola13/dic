haphazardness:/ˌhæpˈhæzərdnəs/| n.|偶然性；随意性；杂乱无章|The haphazardness of the arrangement was apparent. 安排的随意性显而易见。|例句：The books were stacked with a certain haphazardness. 这些书堆放得很随意。  The haphazardness of the search and rescue operation was criticized.  搜救行动的杂乱无章受到了批评。|近义词：randomness, chance, disorganization, chaos|反义词：order, organization, system, planning|词性:名词

Etymology: From haphazard + -ness.  Haphazard itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from hap (chance, luck) + hazard.
