outswindles:/ˌaʊtˈswɪndəlz/
|v.| 骗取; 以诈骗胜过|To cheat or defraud someone more thoroughly or successfully than they cheat or defraud others.|He outswindled the other con men and made off with all the money. 他比其他的骗子更胜一筹，卷走了所有的钱。|Synonyms: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver, outfox|Antonyms: be cheated, be defrauded|verb
