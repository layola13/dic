fibulare:/fɪbjʊˈlɛəriː/ /fɪbjʊˈleri/
adjective |腓骨的；有关腓骨的|Pertaining to the fibula, the outer and smaller of the two bones between the knee and the ankle. |The fibulare artery supplies blood to the lateral compartment of the leg. 腓动脉为腿的外侧间隔供血。|
近义词:peroneal|反义词:tibial|词性:adjective

fibulare:/fɪbjʊˈlɛəriː/ /fɪbjʊˈleri/
noun |腓骨|The fibula, the outer and smaller of the two bones between the knee and the ankle. |A fracture of the fibulare can be a painful injury. 腓骨骨折是一种很痛苦的损伤。|
近义词:calf bone|反义词:tibia|词性:noun
