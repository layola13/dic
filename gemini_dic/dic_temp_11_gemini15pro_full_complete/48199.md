decompressor:/ˌdiːkəmˈpresər/ | n. | 减压器；解压缩程序 | A device or program that reduces pressure or expands compressed data. |  The diver used a decompressor to slowly ascend from the depths. 潜水员使用减压器缓慢地从深处上升。 | synonyms: pressure reducer, expansion tool, extractor | antonyms: compressor, condenser | noun

decompressor:/ˌdiːkəmˈpresər/ | n. | 解压缩软件 | A program that restores compressed data to its original size. | I need a decompressor to open this zip file. 我需要一个解压缩软件来打开这个zip文件。| synonyms: unzipper, archive extractor | antonyms: compressor, archiver | noun
