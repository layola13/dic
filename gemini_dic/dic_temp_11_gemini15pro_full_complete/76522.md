gales:/ɡeɪlz/| n.|大风，强风|Strong winds, typically used in the plural form. |The gales tore at the sails of the ship.  狂风撕扯着船帆。|
近义词:winds, blasts, gusts|反义词:calm, breeze|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old Norse *galr, meaning "magic song, incantation."  The connection to wind likely arose from the idea of a powerful, mysterious force.
