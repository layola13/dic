columelliform:/kɒlˌʌˈmɛlɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 小柱形的，小柱状的 | Shaped like a small column or columella (a small column or pillar-like structure, especially in plants and fungi). | The columelliform teeth of the ancient shark were fascinating.  古代鲨鱼的小柱状牙齿令人着迷。 |  columnar, pillar-shaped | - | adjective
词源->
From Latin *columella, diminutive of columna ("column") + -iform ("-shaped").  源自拉丁语 *columella，columna（“柱子”）的小词 + -iform（“-形状的”）。
