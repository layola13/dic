oddment:/ˈɒdmənt/ (UK) /ˈɑːdmənt/ (US)| n. | 零碎物品；残余物；零头；奇数| a piece of something left over, or small items that are not part of a larger set or group |  She rummaged through a drawer full of oddments. 她翻遍了装满零碎物品的抽屉。| remnants, scraps, leftovers, fragments | entirety, whole | noun

oddment:/ˈɒdmənt/ (UK) /ˈɑːdmənt/ (US)| n. | 奇数；单数| An odd number. | Three is an oddment. 三是一个奇数。 | odd number, singular | even number, pair | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *odd* (meaning "remaining, surplus") + *-ment* (a suffix forming nouns).  The original meaning referred to something left over or remaining.
