geeked:/ɡikt/ | adj. |兴奋的，狂热的，着迷的|Excited, enthusiastic, or obsessed, often about something specific like a hobby, activity, or upcoming event.  It can also mean high on drugs. |例句：I'm so geeked about the new Star Wars movie! 我对新的星球大战电影感到非常兴奋！

例句：He was geeked up on caffeine and ready for the all-nighter. 他喝了咖啡很兴奋，准备通宵熬夜。|近义词：excited, thrilled, pumped, hyped, stoked, amped, wired |反义词：bored, apathetic, unenthusiastic |词性：形容词

词源: Possibly a blend of "geeky" (enthusiastic about a particular subject) and "psyched" (excited).  The term gained popularity in the late 20th and early 21st centuries.
