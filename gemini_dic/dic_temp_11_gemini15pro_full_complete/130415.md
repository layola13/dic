non-woven:/ˌnɒnˈwəʊvən/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈwoʊvən/| adj. n.|非织造的，非编织的；非织造布，无纺布|Made of fibers bonded together by chemical, mechanical, heat, or solvent treatment rather than by weaving or knitting. |例句：Non-woven fabrics are commonly used in disposable products. 非织造布通常用于一次性产品。

|例句：This bag is made of non-woven material.  这个包是由无纺布材料制成的。|近义词：non-knitted, unwoven|反义词:woven, knitted|词性:adjective, noun


Etymology->
From non- + woven, the past participle of weave.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Weave means to form (fabric or a fabric item) by interlacing long threads passing in one direction with others at a right angle to them.
