boarding-school:/ˈbɔːrdɪŋ skuːl/ (US) /ˈbɔːdɪŋ skuːl/ (UK)| n.|寄宿学校|A school where students live and study during the school term.|例句：My son attends a boarding school in Switzerland. 我的儿子在瑞士的一所寄宿学校上学。

例句：Boarding school can teach children independence. 寄宿学校可以教会孩子们独立。

近义词：residential school|反义词:day school|词性:noun
