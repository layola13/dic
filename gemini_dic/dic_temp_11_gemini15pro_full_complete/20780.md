blankets:/ˈblæŋkɪts/
n.|毯子，覆盖物|A piece of soft fabric used for warmth, especially on a bed.|She snuggled under the warm blankets. 她依偎在温暖的毯子下。|近义词：covers, quilts, duvets|反义词：sheets (床单 - 功能不同，并非严格反义)|词性:名词

blanket:/ˈblæŋkɪt/
vt.|覆盖，掩盖|To cover something completely with a thick layer.|Snow blanketed the fields. 雪覆盖了田野。|近义词：cover, coat, envelop|反义词：uncover, expose|词性:动词

词源: From Old North French *blankete "white woolen cloth," diminutive of blanc "white." Originally referred to a white woolen bed covering.
