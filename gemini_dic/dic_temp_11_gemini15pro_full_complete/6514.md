amusers:/əˈmjuːzərz/
n.
娱乐者；逗乐者
People who amuse others; entertainers.
The clowns were the chief amusers at the children's party. 小丑是孩子们派对上的主要娱乐者。

近义词:entertainers, comedians, performers
反义词:bores, dullards

词性:Noun (Plural)
词源:From the verb "amuse," which comes from Old French "amuser" (to divert, entertain), itself from "a-" (to) + "muser" (to muse, loiter).
