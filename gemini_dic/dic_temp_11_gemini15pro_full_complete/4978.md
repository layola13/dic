alkalizate: /ˈælkəlaɪˌzeɪt/ | v. | 碱化；使成碱性 | To make something alkaline, or to increase its alkalinity. |  The chemist alkalized the solution by adding sodium hydroxide. 化学家通过添加氢氧化钠使溶液碱化。

The process alkalizes the soil, making it more suitable for certain crops. 这个过程使土壤碱化，使其更适合某些作物生长。 | 近义词: neutralize, basify | 反义词: acidify, acidulate | 词性: 动词


alkalizate: /ˈælkəlaɪˌzeɪt/ | n. | 碱化物 | A substance that has been made alkaline, or a product of alkalization. | The alkalizate was then used in the next stage of the experiment.  碱化物随后用于实验的下一阶段。

The resulting alkalizate is beneficial for certain industrial processes.  产生的碱化物对某些工业过程有益。 | 近义词: base | 反义词: acid | 词性: 名词
