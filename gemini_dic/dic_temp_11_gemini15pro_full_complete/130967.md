nonassumption:/ˌnɒnəˈsʌmpʃən/| n.|非假设，不臆断|The principle of nonassumption encourages us to gather evidence before forming conclusions.  非假设原则鼓励我们在得出结论之前收集证据。|例句：The scientist approached the experiment with a stance of nonassumption.  这位科学家以不臆断的态度进行实验。

例句：Her nonassumption about the situation allowed her to see all perspectives. 她对情况不带假设的态度让她能够看到所有角度。|近义词：neutrality, objectivity, open-mindedness|反义词：assumption, presumption, prejudice|词性:名词
