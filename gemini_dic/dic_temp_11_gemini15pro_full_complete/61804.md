emphatics:/ɪmˈfætɪks/ | n. |强调词;加强语气词| Words or phrases used to emphasize a point or feeling. |  "Really," "absolutely," and "certainly" are common emphatics. | 真正地，“绝对地”和“当然地”是常见的强调词。| synonyms: intensifiers,  stress words | antonyms: mitigators, softeners | noun
