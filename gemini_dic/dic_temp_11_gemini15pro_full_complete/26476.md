What do you do for a living? = 你以什么为生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been studying at university? = 他在大学里一直在学什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for lunch? = 你午饭吃了什么？
What time did she arrive? = 她几点到的？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

What had they discussed before the meeting? = 会议前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们将在会议上展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

What will they have finished by tomorrow? = 到明天他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

虚拟语气:
If I knew what to say, I would say it. = 如果我知道该说什么，我就会说。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
He doesn't understand what I'm saying. = 他不明白我在说什么。
