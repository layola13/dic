overrational:/ˌoʊvərˈræʃənəl/ | adj. | 过度理性化的 | Excessively rational; making decisions based purely on logic and disregarding emotions or other factors.  Derived from "over-" (excessively) + "rational" (based on reason). |  He made an overrational decision to sell his family home, neglecting the emotional attachments. 他过度理性地决定卖掉他的家，忽略了情感上的依恋。 |  logical, analytical, calculating | irrational, emotional, impulsive | adjective
