ampliate:/ˈæmplieɪt/
verb
扩大，扩展
to enlarge or extend (something) in scope or effect
The king ampliated his power by conquering neighboring territories. 国王通过征服邻近的领土扩大了他的权力。

The company ampliated its product line to include more varieties.  公司扩大了产品线，以包含更多品种。

近义词:expand, enlarge, extend, amplify, augment, broaden, widen
反义词:reduce, decrease, diminish, lessen, contract, restrict, narrow
词性:v.

Etymology:
From Late Latin *ampliatus, past participle of ampliare (“to enlarge”), from Latin amplus (“large, spacious, of great extent”).
