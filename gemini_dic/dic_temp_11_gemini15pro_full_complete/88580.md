hitherto:/ˌhɪðərˈtuː/
adv.
迄今为止，直到现在
up to this time; until now
例句：Hitherto, the project has been kept secret.  迄今为止，该项目一直保密。

例句：The disease was hitherto unknown to science. 这种疾病迄今为止在科学上是未知的。

近义词：previously, formerly, so far, thus far
反义词：henceforth, hereafter
词性：副词
