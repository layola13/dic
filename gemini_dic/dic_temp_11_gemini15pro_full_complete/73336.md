forefatherly:/ˌfɔːrˈfɑːðərli/
adjective
祖先的；像祖先的；可敬的
characteristic of or befitting a forefather; venerable.
例句：He gave us some forefatherly advice. 他给了我们一些像祖先一样可敬的建议。

例句：His forefatherly demeanor commanded respect. 他那副祖辈的风范令人肃然起敬。

近义词：ancestral, patriarchal, venerable
反义词：modern, contemporary, youthful
词性: adjective
