franchisees:/ˌfræn.tʃaɪˈziːz/
n.
特许经营人，加盟商
A person or company that holds a franchise for the sale of goods or the operation of a service.
Many franchisees operate fast-food restaurants. 许多加盟商经营快餐店。
synonyms: franchise holder, licensee
antonyms: franchisor
