formalists:/ˈfɔːrməlɪsts/| n.|形式主义者|People who adhere rigidly to prescribed forms, rules, or conventions, often at the expense of substance or meaning.|The formalists criticized the poet for his unconventional use of rhyme.|Synonyms:  sticklers, traditionalists, purists, doctrinaires|Antonyms:  innovators, reformers, revolutionaries, iconoclasts|Noun
