agar-agar:/ˈeɪɡɑːr ˌeɪɡɑːr/ | n. |琼脂，洋菜|A gelatinous substance obtained from certain red seaweeds and used in biological culture media and as a thickener in foods.|例句：Agar-agar is used as a vegetarian substitute for gelatin. 琼脂被用作明胶的素食替代品。

例句：The scientist prepared the agar-agar plates for the bacteria culture. 科学家准备了用于细菌培养的琼脂平板。|近义词：gelatin, carrageenan|反义词: (No direct antonym)|Noun
