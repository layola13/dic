instaure: /ɪnˈstɔːr/ /ɪnˈstɑːr/ | vt. | 建立，设立，实行 | To establish, introduce, or inaugurate something, such as a system, custom, or practice. | The new government instaurated a series of reforms. 新政府实行了一系列改革。

The company instaurated a new dress code. 公司制定了新的着装规范。


近义词: establish, introduce, inaugurate, institute, implement

反义词: abolish, discontinue, terminate

词性: verb (动词)

词源: From French *instaurer,* from Latin *instaurare* "to renew, restore, rebuild," from *in- "in" + *staurare* "to set up, establish."
