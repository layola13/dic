highty-tighty:/ˈhaɪti ˈtaɪti/ | adj. |傲慢的，自以为是的 |  Behaving in a haughty or supercilious manner; snobbish.  Characterized by arrogance and a sense of superiority. |  She gave him a highty-tighty look and walked away. 她傲慢地看了他一眼，然后走开了。|  He spoke in a highty-tighty tone, as if he were better than everyone else. 他说话的语气很傲慢，好像他比其他人都优越。 | 近义词：snobbish, arrogant, haughty, supercilious, condescending | 反义词：humble, modest, unassuming | 词性: 形容词

词源:  A reduplication of *highty* (itself a variant of *haughty*), perhaps influenced by *tighty*.
