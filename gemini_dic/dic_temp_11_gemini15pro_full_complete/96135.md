Etymology:
From in- (not) + demonstrable (capable of being demonstrated) + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  Demonstrable comes from Late Latin demonstrabilis, from Latin demonstrare "to point out, show, prove," from de- "fully" + monstrare "to show."
