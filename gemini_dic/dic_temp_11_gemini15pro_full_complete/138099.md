normativity:/ˌnɔːrməˈtɪvəti/
n.
规范性，常态
Relating to or deriving from a standard or norm, especially of behavior.  The quality or state of being normative.
The normativity of heterosexual relationships is being challenged.  异性恋关系的规范性正在受到挑战。
normality, standard, regularity
abnormality, irregularity
noun
