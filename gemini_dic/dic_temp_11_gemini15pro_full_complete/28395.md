carbon-dates:/ˈkɑːrbən deɪts/ | v. | 测定年代，用碳测年法确定年代 | determines the age of something, typically an artifact or organic material, using the method of radiocarbon dating. |  The archaeologist carbon-dates the ancient pottery shards. 考古学家用碳测年法测定了古代陶器的年代。

carbon-dates:/ˈkɑːrbən deɪts/ | n. |  碳测年结果 | The results of radiocarbon dating. |  The carbon-dates confirmed the artifact's age. 碳测年结果证实了文物的年代。


carbon-dates:/ˈkɑːrbən deɪts/ | n. |  碳测年的方法 | The method of radiocarbon dating. |  Carbon-dates are a useful tool for archaeology. 碳测年法是考古学的有用工具。


Etymology: carbon + date (verb). "Carbon" refers to carbon-14, a radioactive isotope used in the dating process. "Date" refers to the act of assigning a date or age.

Synonyms: age, determine the age of, radiocarbon date

Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but one might consider "misdate" or "backdate" as actions that produce incorrect dates)

Word Type:  Primarily used as a verb, can also function as a noun depending on context.
