duopolistic:/ˌdjuːɒpəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 双头垄断的；双寡头的 | Of or relating to a duopoly, a market situation in which two suppliers dominate. | The duopolistic nature of the market made it difficult for new companies to enter. 这个市场的双寡头性质使得新公司难以进入。|  The telecommunications industry in this country is largely duopolistic.  这个国家的电信行业很大程度上是双头垄断的。 | synonymous:  oligopolistic, two-supplier | antonymous: competitive, monopolistic | adjective
