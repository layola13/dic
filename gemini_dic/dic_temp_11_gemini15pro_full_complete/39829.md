conspiratorial:/kənˌspɪrəˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
阴谋的，密谋的
Suggesting the existence of a conspiracy; seeming to understand and agree with someone about something secret.
She gave me a conspiratorial wink. 她对我眨了眨眼睛，示意心照不宣。
The two men exchanged conspiratorial glances. 两人交换了心照不宣的眼神。
synonyms: secretive, clandestine, covert, furtive
antonyms: open, frank, aboveboard
词性: adjective
