fairyland:/ˈfeəriˌlænd/ | n. |仙境，奇境 |An enchanted realm of fairies, magic, and wonder.  A place or situation that is beautiful and magical, like a dream. |例句：The children imagined the forest as a fairyland full of talking animals. 孩子们把森林想象成一个充满会说话的动物的仙境。

例句：The garden, with its colorful flowers and butterflies, seemed like a fairyland.  这个花园，五彩缤纷的花朵和蝴蝶，看起来像一个仙境。|近义词：wonderland, dreamland, fantasyland, magic kingdom |反义词：reality, real world |词性:noun
