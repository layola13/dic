USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? = 他整个下午都在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会时穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten for breakfast? = 她早餐吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before you called me? = 你打电话给我之前一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the price be? = 价格会是多少？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing next weekend? = 下周末你会做什么？
What will they be serving at the wedding? = 婚礼上他们会提供什么食物？

Future Perfect:
What will you have finished by tomorrow? = 到明天你会完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底他们会取得什么成就？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for the past five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
