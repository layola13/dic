overexpressed:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛst/ | adj. | 过度表达的；过度表现的 |  Expressing a gene at a higher level than normal.  Meaning a gene is producing more protein than it usually would. | The HER2 gene is overexpressed in some breast cancers.  HER2基因在某些乳腺癌中过度表达。|  The oncogene was significantly overexpressed in the tumor cells.  该癌基因在肿瘤细胞中显著过度表达。| Near synonyms: upregulated, overproduced | Near antonyms: underexpressed, downregulated | Adjective

overexpress:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛs/ | vt. | 过度表达；过度表现 | To express (a gene) at a higher level than normal. |  The cells overexpress the growth factor receptor. 这些细胞过度表达生长因子受体。 | The study showed that the gene was overexpressed in diseased tissues.  研究表明，该基因在病变组织中过度表达。| Near synonyms: upregulate, overproduce | Near antonyms: underexpress, downregulate | Verb
