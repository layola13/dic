flowy:/ˈfloʊi/| adj.|飘逸的，轻盈的|Having a flowing or graceful movement, as of fabric or hair.|Her flowy dress danced in the breeze. 她的飘逸的裙子在微风中舞动。|例句：The curtains were made of a flowy, sheer fabric. 窗帘是用轻薄飘逸的布料做的。|近义词：flowing, draping, loose, airy|反义词:stiff, tight, rigid|词性:形容词
词源:From flow + -y, likely influenced by the visual similarity to flowing. 源于flow（流动）+-y，可能受到与flowing（流动的）视觉相似性的影响。
