hullers:/ˈhʌlərz/| n.|脱壳机; 去壳机|Machines used for removing the hulls or outer coverings from grains, seeds, or nuts.|The factory uses large hullers to process the rice crop.  这家工厂使用大型脱壳机来加工稻谷。|The coffee hullers separated the beans from the dried cherries. 咖啡脱壳机将咖啡豆从干樱桃中分离出来。|近义词：huskers, shellers|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


hullers:/ˈhʌlərz/| n.|脱壳工人|People who operate hulling machines.|The hullers worked long hours during the harvest season.  脱壳工人在收获季节长时间工作。|Experienced hullers can quickly identify and remove damaged grains.  经验丰富的脱壳工人可以快速识别并去除受损的谷物。|近义词：huskers, shellers|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
