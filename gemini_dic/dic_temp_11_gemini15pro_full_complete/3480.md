aftereffect:/ˈæftərɪˌfekt/| n.|后遗症，副作用，余波|An aftereffect is a change that continues to happen to a person or thing after the event that caused it.|The aftereffects of the earthquake were devastating. 地震的后遗症是毁灭性的。|synonyms: consequence, result, aftermath, side effect|antonyms: cause, origin, source|noun
