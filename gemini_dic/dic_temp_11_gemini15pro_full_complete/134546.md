nonliquidations:/ˌnɒnlɪkwɪˈdeɪʃənz/
noun
非清算；未清算资产
The nonliquidations of the company's assets caused concern among investors. 该公司资产的未清算引起了投资者的担忧。
nonliquidation is the absence or lack of liquidation, meaning assets or a company have not been sold off or wound up.  Nonliquidation can refer to a situation where a company continues to operate despite financial difficulties or where assets are held rather than being sold. This term is commonly used in finance and accounting.
Example: The company's nonliquidation allowed them to restructure and eventually become profitable.  公司的非清算使其能够进行重组并最终盈利。
Example: The nonliquidation of these assets will benefit the heirs in the long run. 这些资产的未清算从长远来看将使继承人受益。
Synonyms:  non-sale, retention, holding, preservation
Antonyms: liquidation, sale, disposal, wind-up
