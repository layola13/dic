circulates:/ˈsɜːrkjəleɪts/
verb
循环；流通；传播
to move around or through something, or to make something do this
Blood circulates throughout the body. 血液在全身循环。
Synonyms: flow, move, travel, spread, distribute
Antonyms: stagnate, stop, halt
词性: 动词

circulate:/ˈsɜːrkjəleɪts/
verb
(使)流传，(使)传阅；(使)散布
to pass or spread from one person or place to another
A rumour began to circulate about his resignation. 关于他辞职的谣言开始流传。
Synonyms: distribute, disseminate, spread, publicize
Antonyms: suppress, withhold, conceal
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin circulatus, past participle of circulare "to make round, to go around," from Latin circularis "circular," from circulus "small ring, circle," diminutive of circus "ring, circle."
