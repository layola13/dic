cephalostyle:/ˈsefələˌstaɪl/| n.|头柱，头状花柱|A structure resembling a head, especially the upper part of the style in certain plants.|The cephalostyle of this orchid is particularly prominent.  这种兰花的头柱尤为突出。|Near synonyms: style, stigma, pistil|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider parts outside the pistil, like stamen)|noun
