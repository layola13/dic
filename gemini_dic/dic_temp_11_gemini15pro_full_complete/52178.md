diarchy: /ˈdaɪɑːrki/ | n. | 双头政治；二元统治 | A system of government in which power is shared between two rulers or authorities. |  The Roman Republic, with its two consuls, could be considered a form of diarchy. 罗马共和国及其两名执政官可以被认为是一种双头政治的形式。|  The company operated under a diarchy, with two CEOs sharing equal power.  这家公司实行双头政治，两位首席执行官拥有平等的权力。| synonyms: duumvirate, dyarchy | antonyms: monarchy, autocracy | noun
