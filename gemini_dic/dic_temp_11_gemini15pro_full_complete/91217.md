hydrobiplane:/ˈhaɪdrəʊˌbaɪpleɪn/ /ˈhaɪdroʊˌbaɪpleɪn/| n. |水上飞机|An aircraft designed to take off and land on water.|例句：The hydrobiplane skimmed across the surface of the lake. 水上飞机掠过湖面。

例句：The pilot skillfully landed the hydrobiplane on the choppy water.  飞行员熟练地将水上飞机降落在波涛汹涌的水面上。|近义词：seaplane, floatplane |反义词:landplane|词性:noun
