homogeneous:/ˌhoʊməˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. |同质的，同类的，均匀的 | Of the same kind or nature; uniform in composition or structure throughout. |  The mixture was homogeneous, with no lumps or variations in color. 混合物是均匀的，没有块状物或颜色变化。| Milk is a homogeneous mixture. 牛奶是一种均匀的混合物。 | 近义词: uniform, consistent, similar, alike | 反义词: heterogeneous, diverse, varied | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *homogeneus, from Ancient Greek ὁμογενής (homogenḗs, “of the same race, family, or kind”), from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + γένος (génos, “race, stock, kind”).
