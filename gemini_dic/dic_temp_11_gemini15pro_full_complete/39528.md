conqueror:/ˈkɒŋkərə(r) (UK), /ˈkɑːŋkərər (US)| n. |征服者，胜利者|A person who conquers a place or people, especially by military force.|例句：Alexander the Great was a renowned conqueror. 亚历山大大帝是一位著名的征服者。

例句：The conquerors imposed their will on the defeated nation. 征服者将他们的意志强加于战败的国家。|近义词：victor, champion, winner, vanquisher|反义词：defeated, vanquished, loser|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French conquerour (Modern French conquérant), from Late Latin conqueror, from Latin conquirere "to seek out, procure, win, gain," from com- "together" (see com-) + quaerere "to seek, gain" (see query (n.)).
