fueling:/ˈfjuːəlɪŋ/
v.
给…加燃料；刺激，激起
The plane is fueling up for its transatlantic flight.  飞机正在为其跨大西洋飞行加油。
His anger was fueling his desire for revenge. 他的愤怒激起了他的复仇欲望。
近义词: powering, energizing, stoking
反义词: draining, depleting, extinguishing
词性: 动名词/现在分词

fuel:/ˈfjuːəl/
n.
燃料；刺激因素
Wood is a common fuel for heating. 木材是一种常见的供暖燃料。
The scandal provided fuel for his political opponents.  这起丑闻为他的政治对手提供了攻击的素材。
v.
给…加燃料；刺激
We need to fuel the car before we leave. 我们出发前需要给汽车加油。
The media fueled the controversy with their sensationalized reporting. 媒体以其耸人听闻的报道加剧了争议。
近义词: (n.) energy, power, gasoline; (v.) power, energize, incite
反义词: (n.) exhaust, depletion; (v.) drain, deplete, extinguish
词性: 名词/动词

词源: From Middle English *fowel, from Anglo-Norman *fuail, *fuaille, from Old French fuel, fouaille (“firewood, kindling”), from Vulgar Latin *focālia, neuter plural of *focālis, from Latin focus (“hearth, fireplace”).
