foisting:/ˈfɔɪstɪŋ/
foist:/fɔɪst/| vt.|把...强加于; 偷偷塞进; 蒙骗; |To foist something on someone is to impose something unwanted on them.  把某物强加于某人是指将不想要的东西强加于他们。|He foisted his unwanted opinions on everyone. 他把他不受欢迎的观点强加于每个人。|近义词：impose, inflict, palm off, pass off|反义词：accept, receive, take|v.
