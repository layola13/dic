flayer:/ˈfleɪər/| n.|剥皮者，剥皮刀，宣传单|A person or thing that flays; a tool used for flaying. Also, a leaflet or flyer used for advertising or promotion.|The flayer skillfully removed the animal's skin.  剥皮者熟练地剥下了动物的皮。

The butcher used a special flayer to prepare the meat.  屠夫用一把特殊的剥皮刀来准备肉。

Student volunteers handed out flayers advertising the upcoming concert. 学生志愿者分发了宣传即将到来的音乐会的传单。|近义词：skinner, butcher, leaflet, flyer, handbill|反义词:|noun
