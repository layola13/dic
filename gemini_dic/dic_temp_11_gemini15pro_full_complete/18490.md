bend an ear:/bɛnd ən ɪr/
|idiomatic phrase|
|倾听；仔细听|
|To listen attentively to someone; to give someone one's attention.|
|例句：My grandmother always bends an ear when I have a problem. 我祖母总是在我有问题的时候倾听我。|

|例句：He bent an ear to her troubles. 他倾听了她的烦恼。|

|近义词：listen attentively, pay attention, lend an ear|
|反义词：ignore, disregard, turn a deaf ear|
|phrase|
