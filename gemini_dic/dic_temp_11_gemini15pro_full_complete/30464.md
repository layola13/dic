centrifugals: /ˌsɛnˈtrɪfjuːɡlz/ | n. | 离心机，离心式机器 | Machines that use centrifugal force to separate substances.  These are typically plural of "centrifugal," referring to multiple centrifugal devices, or components within a single system (like the centrifugals in a uranium enrichment cascade).| The lab uses high-speed centrifugals to separate blood components.  实验室使用高速离心机分离血液成分。| synonyms: extractors, separators | antonyms: N/A (A true antonym for a physical machine is unusual.  One might consider "mixer" in certain contexts, but it's not a direct opposite.) | noun

centrifugal: /ˌsɛnˈtrɪfjuːɡl/ | adj. | 离心的 | Moving or tending to move away from a center.  Can also describe a machine that utilizes centrifugal force. |  A centrifuge uses centrifugal force to separate materials. 离心机利用离心力分离物质。 | synonyms:  outward, radial | antonyms: centripetal | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin "centrum" (center) + "fugere" (to flee).
