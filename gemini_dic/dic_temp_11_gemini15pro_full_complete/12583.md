如果你是想问 "as"，以下是解析：

as: /æz, əz/ | adv. conj. prep. |作为，像，当...时候|  Used to indicate that something happens during the time when something else is taking place. | 例句：As I was walking home, it began to rain. 当我走回家的时候，开始下雨了。| 例句：He works as a teacher. 他作为一名教师工作。| 近义词：like, since, because, while | 反义词: unlike |词性: 副词 (adverb), 连词 (conjunction), 介词 (preposition)
