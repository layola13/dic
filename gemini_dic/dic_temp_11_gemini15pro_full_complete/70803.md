fingerprinted:/ˈfɪŋɡərˌprɪntɪd/| adj.|指纹识别的，已采集指纹的|Having had one's fingerprints taken. The police fingerprinted the suspect.|例句：The suspect was fingerprinted and photographed. 嫌疑人被采集了指纹并拍照。

例句：All visitors are fingerprinted at the border. 所有游客在边境都被采集了指纹。|近义词：identified, scanned, documented|反义词：unanalyzed, unidentified|词性:形容词

Etymology:
finger (n.) Old English finger "finger," from Proto-Germanic *fingraz.
print (n.)  From Old French preinte "impression," from preindre "to press," from Latin premere "to press."
-ed (suffix) forming the past participle of verbs.
