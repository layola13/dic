dase:/deɪz/ | n. | 恍惚；麻木状态；昏迷 | A state of being dazed; a stupor. | He was in a dase after the accident. 他在事故后处于恍惚状态。|  He sat in a dase, staring into space. 他茫然地坐着，凝视着虚空。 | 近义词：stupor, daze, trance | 反义词：alertness, awareness | 词性：名词

词源:  From Middle English *dasen, from Old Norse *dasa (“to become exhausted, weary”), from Proto-Germanic *dasaną (“to become tired, exhausted”). Cognate with Icelandic dasa (“to doze, be sluggish”).
