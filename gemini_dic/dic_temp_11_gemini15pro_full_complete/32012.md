cheongsams: /ˌtʃɔːŋˈsæmz/ | n. | 旗袍 | A close-fitting dress with a high neck and a slit skirt, originating in China. |  She looked stunning in a red silk cheongsam. 她穿着一件红色的丝绸旗袍，看起来美极了。

| Example: Many women wore elegant cheongsams to the gala. 许多女性穿着优雅的旗袍参加了晚会。| Synonym: qipao | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "Western dress") | Noun
