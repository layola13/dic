casserole:/ˈkæsəˌrəʊl/ (UK), /ˈkæsəˌroʊl/ (US)| n.|砂锅；砂锅菜|A dish of meat and vegetables cooked in liquid in a covered dish, typically in an oven.  It can also refer to the dish itself.|例句：She made a chicken casserole for dinner. 她晚餐做了鸡肉砂锅。

例句：The casserole dish is oven-safe. 这个砂锅是烤箱安全的。|近义词：stew, hotdish|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
