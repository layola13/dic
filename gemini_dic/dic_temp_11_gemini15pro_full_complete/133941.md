nonhazardously:/nɒnˈhæzədəsli/
adverb
无害地
in a way that is not hazardous or dangerous.
The chemicals were disposed of nonhazardously.  这些化学品被无害地处理掉了。
The waste was packaged and transported nonhazardously. 废物被无害地包装和运输。
近义词:safely, securely
反义词:dangerously, hazardously
词性:副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  not; opposite of.  来自拉丁语 non.
hazardous (adjective):  risky; dangerous. 来自古法语 hasardous.
-ly (suffix):  forms adverbs from adjectives. 来自古英语 -lice.
