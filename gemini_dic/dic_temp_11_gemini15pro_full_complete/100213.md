interrogate:/ɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪt/| vt.|审问，讯问，盘问|To question someone formally and officially, often aggressively, in order to obtain information.|The police interrogated the suspect for hours. 警方讯问了嫌疑人几个小时。|近义词：question, examine, quiz, grill|反义词:answer, reply|verb

interrogate:/ɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪt/| vt.|询问，查询（机器或系统）|To ask a question of (a machine, system, etc.) or to seek information from (a database, etc.)|The program interrogates the sensor every minute to check for changes. 该程序每分钟查询一次传感器以检查是否有变化。|近义词：query, poll|反义词: N/A|verb
