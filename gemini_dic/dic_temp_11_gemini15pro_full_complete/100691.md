intracisternal:/ˌɪntrəsɪˈstɜːrnl/ | adj. | 胞质网内的，池内的 | Located or occurring within a cisterna, especially within the cisternae of the endoplasmic reticulum. |  Intracisternal A-type particles are retroviral elements found in the cisternae of the endoplasmic reticulum of mice.  胞质网A型颗粒是在小鼠内质网池中发现的逆转录病毒元件。| Synonyms: intraluminal | Antonyms: extraluminal | adjective


Etymology: intra- (within) + cisterna (cavity, reservoir) + -al (adjectival suffix)
