mannerism:/ˈmænərɪzəm/| n.|特殊习惯，怪癖；矫揉造作|A mannerism is a distinctive behavioral trait, often a habitual gesture or speech pattern, that can be peculiar to an individual or a characteristic of a particular group or period.  It can be either conscious or unconscious. |He had a peculiar mannerism of scratching his nose whenever he was nervous. 他有一个怪癖，每当紧张的时候就会挠鼻子。| synonym: affectation, peculiarity, idiosyncrasy, habit, quirk| antonym: naturalness, normality| noun
