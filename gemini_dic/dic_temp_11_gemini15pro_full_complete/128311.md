neurotic:/njʊəˈrɒtɪk/ /nʊˈrɑːtɪk/| adj. n.|神经质的，神经症患者|Suffering from, caused by, or relating to neurosis.  Excessively anxious, tense, and worried.|例句：He was a brilliant but neurotic artist. 他是一位才华横溢但神经质的艺术家。|近义词：anxious, nervous, tense, unstable, edgy|反义词：stable, calm, relaxed|词性:形容词, 名词

neurotic:/njʊəˈrɒtɪk/ /nʊˈrɑːtɪk/| n.|神经症患者|A person suffering from neurosis.|例句：She was a neurotic who constantly worried about her health. 她是一个神经症患者，总是担心自己的健康。|近义词：worrier|反义词：—|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "neuron" (nerve) + "-ōsis" (condition) -> "neurōsis" (nervous disorder)  ->  neurotic (adjective and noun)
