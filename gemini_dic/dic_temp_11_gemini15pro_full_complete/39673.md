conserved:/kənˈsɜːrvd/ | adj. | 保存的，保守的 |  maintained in safety from injury, decay, waste, or loss; not having changed much over a long period of time; not wasted | The town has worked hard to conserve its historical buildings.  这个城镇努力保护其历史建筑。 | synonyms: preserved, protected, saved, guarded | antonyms: wasted, squandered, destroyed, depleted | adjective

conserve:/kənˈsɜːrv/ | v. | 保存，保护 | protect (something, especially an area of natural beauty or a historical building) from change or damage; prevent the wasteful overuse of (a resource) |  We must conserve our forests for future generations. 我们必须为子孙后代保护我们的森林。 | synonyms: preserve, protect, save, safeguard | antonyms: waste, squander, destroy, deplete | verb
