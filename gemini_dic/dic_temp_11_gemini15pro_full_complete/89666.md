hooped:/huːpt/
adjective
环状的，箍状的；用箍圈箍住的
having a hoop or hoops; surrounded by a hoop
The barrel was hooped with iron.  这个桶用铁箍箍住。
近义词:ringed, banded, circled
反义词:unbound, loose
词性:adjective

hooped:/huːpt/
verb
用箍圈箍住
past tense and past participle of hoop
He hooped the barrel. 他用箍圈箍住桶。
近义词:bind, encircle, fasten
反义词: unfasten, loosen, free
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From hoop + -ed.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hūpaz.
