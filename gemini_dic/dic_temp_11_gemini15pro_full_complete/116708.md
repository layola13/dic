matzo:/ˈmɑːtsə(ʊ)/ (US) /ˈmɑːtsɔː/ (UK)| n. |无酵饼，逾越节薄饼| Unleavened bread traditionally eaten by Jews during Passover. |例句：We eat matzo during Passover. 我们在逾越节吃无酵饼。

例句：The matzo was crisp and dry.  这无酵饼又脆又干。

近义词：unleavened bread|反义词:leavened bread|词性:noun

matzah:/ˈmɑːtsə/ (US) /ˈmɑːtsɑː/ (UK)| n. |无酵饼，逾越节薄饼| Variant spelling of matzo. |例句：We bought a box of matzah for Passover. 我们为逾越节买了一盒无酵饼。

例句：She baked matzah for the Seder. 她为逾越节家宴烤了无酵饼。


近义词：unleavened bread, matzo|反义词:leavened bread|词性:noun
