gossaniferous:/ɡɒsəˈnɪfərəs/ | adj. | 含褐铁矿的；多褐铁矿的 | Containing or rich in gossan.  Gossan is a heavily oxidized, weathered rock, often capping sulfide ore deposits. | The gossaniferous outcrop hinted at the presence of valuable minerals beneath. 这片含褐铁矿的露头暗示了下面存在有价值的矿物。| Synonyms: ferruginous, iron-rich | Antonyms: non-ferruginous | adjective
