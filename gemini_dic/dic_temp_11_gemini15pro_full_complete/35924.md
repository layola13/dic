cocultivating:/ˌkoʊˈkʌltɪveɪtɪŋ/
v.
共同培育，共同培养
The process of growing two or more different species together in a shared environment.
Researchers are cocultivating different types of bacteria to study their interactions. 研究人员正在共同培养不同类型的细菌以研究它们的相互作用。

Researchers are cocultivating cancer cells with immune cells to explore potential new therapies. 研究人员正在将癌细胞与免疫细胞共同培养，以探索潜在的新疗法。

近义词:co-culturing, growing together, raising together
反义词:separating, isolating
词性:动词(present participle)
