glider:/ˈɡlaɪdər/| n.|滑翔机；滑翔员；滑翔导弹|A glider is a light aircraft without an engine, designed to glide through the air.|The glider soared gracefully through the sky. 滑翔机优雅地在空中翱翔。|The pilot skillfully controlled the glider. 飞行员熟练地操控着滑翔机。|近义词：sailplane|反义词：airplane, powered aircraft|词性:noun

glider:/ˈɡlaɪdər/| n.|滑翔者；滑翔物|Something that glides.|The sugar glider leaped from branch to branch. 蜜袋鼯从一个树枝跳到另一个树枝。|The paper glider flew across the room. 纸飞机飞过房间。|近义词：sailer|反义词：N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From glide + -er.  Glide comes from Old English *glīdan, of Germanic origin, related to Dutch glijden and German gleiten.
