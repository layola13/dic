butteries:/ˈbʌtəriz/
n.
奶油面包卷；（苏格兰大学）负责酒水供应的房间；（苏格兰大学）负责酒水供应的职员
Buttery rolls served warm with jam.  热奶油面包卷配果酱。
The butteries at St Andrews is a popular student hangout. 圣安德鲁斯大学的酒水供应处是学生们常去的地方。
He worked in the butteries during his time at university. 他大学期间在酒水供应处工作。
近义词: rolls, pantry, bar (depending on the meaning)
反义词:  None (no direct opposite)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *boterie "store of liquor," from bote "drink," from Late Latin butta "cask, bottle." The Scottish university sense developed from the buttery being the place where drinks and snacks were stored and served.
