nani:/ˈnɑːniː/ | interj. | 什么？/哪尼？(表示惊讶) | An informal, often humorous expression of surprise or disbelief, borrowed from Japanese.  It's the equivalent of "What?" or "Huh?" in English. | Example: Nani? You won the lottery? 哪尼？你中了彩票了？ | Example:  Nani? He actually apologized? 哪尼？他竟然道歉了？| Synonym: What?, Huh?, Really? | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for an exclamation of surprise) | interjection

注意事项:
nani is primarily used informally online and in anime/manga fan communities.  It is not a common word in standard English conversation.
