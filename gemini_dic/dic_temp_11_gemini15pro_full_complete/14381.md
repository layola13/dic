autor:/ˈɔːθər/ (UK), /ˈɑːθər/ (US)| n.|作者，作家|A person who originates or creates something, especially a book, play, or piece of music.|例句：The autor of the book is unknown. 这本书的作者不为人知。

例句：She is a successful autor. 她是一位成功的作家。|近义词：writer, novelist, composer, creator|反义词：reader, audience|词性：noun
