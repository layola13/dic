chef-d'oeuvre:/ˌʃeɪˈdəːvrə/ (US), /ˌʃefˈdɜːvrə/ (UK)| n. |杰作；代表作|A masterpiece; a supreme achievement, especially in art or literature.  It comes from French, literally meaning "chief of work".|例句：The Mona Lisa is considered Leonardo da Vinci's chef-d'oeuvre. 蒙娜丽莎被认为是莱昂纳多·达·芬奇的杰作。

例句：This symphony is the composer's chef-d'oeuvre. 这部交响曲是这位作曲家的代表作。

近义词：masterpiece, classic, magnum opus|反义词:failure, flop|词性:noun
