crustaceousness:/krʌˌsteɪʃəsnəs/
|n.| 甲壳性；甲壳类动物的特性 |The crustaceousness of the creature was evident in its hard shell.  这种生物的甲壳性体现在它坚硬的外壳上。|
|英文解析: The quality of being crustaceous; resembling or characteristic of a crustacean, especially in having a hard shell or exoskeleton.|
|例句：The lobster's crustaceousness made it difficult to crack.  龙虾的甲壳使它很难被敲开。|
|例句：The crustaceousness of the barnacles protected them from the waves. 藤壶的甲壳保护它们免受海浪的冲击。|
|近义词：shell-like, hard-shelled|
|反义词：soft-bodied|
|词性: noun|
