词源:
From Middle English *braggen, from Old Norse bragga (“to complain, grumble, murmur, show discontent; to brag”), from Proto-Germanic *braggōną (“to make a loud noise, roar”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰregʰ- (“to resound, roar, ring”). Cognate with Icelandic bragga (“to brag”), Swedish bragga (“to boast”), Danish brag (“boast”), German prahlen (“to boast”). Related to break.
