microcline: /ˈmaɪkroʊklaɪn/ | n. | 微斜长石 | A common feldspar mineral, KAlSi3O8, occurring in white or pale-colored crystals. It is a constituent of many igneous and metamorphic rocks. | Microcline is a significant component of granite. 微斜长石是花岗岩的重要组成部分。

Microcline can be found in various colors, including pink. 微斜长石可以有多种颜色，包括粉色。 | 近义词: orthoclase, feldspar | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) | 词性: noun
