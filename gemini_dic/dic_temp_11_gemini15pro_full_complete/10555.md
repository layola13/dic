appreciably:/əˈpriːʃəbli/| adv.|明显地，显著地|To a noticeable degree; significantly. |Her health has improved appreciably. 她的健康状况明显好转。|The temperature dropped appreciably overnight.  夜里温度显著下降。|近义词：significantly, noticeably, considerably, substantially, markedly|反义词：slightly, insignificantly, negligibly|词性:副词

词源:
From Late Latin appretiabilis, from Latin appretiare "to set a price to," from ad- "to" + pretium "price."  The sense evolution was from "capable of being valued" to "perceptible, noticeable" to "considerable, significant."
