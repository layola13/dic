blab:/blæb/| v. n.|泄露秘密，胡说八道|reveal secrets or talk foolishly|例句：She blabbed to her friends about the surprise party. 她向朋友们泄露了惊喜派对的消息。|近义词：gossip, chatter, prattle, tattle|反义词：keep quiet, be discreet|词性:动词/名词


词源:  From Middle Dutch *blabberen, frequentative of *blappen (“to blab, babble”). Cognate with Dutch blabberen (“to blab, babble”), German plappern (“to chatter, babble”), English blabber.
