murky:/ˈmɜːki/ (UK) /ˈmɝːki/ (US)| adj.|黑暗的；浑浊的；模糊的；阴暗的|Dark, cloudy, and gloomy; not clear.|例句：The murky water of the lake made it difficult to see the bottom. 湖水浑浊，难以看到底部。

例句：The details of the deal remained murky. 这笔交易的细节仍然模糊不清。

近义词：dark, gloomy, obscure, cloudy, dim, hazy|反义词：clear, bright, transparent, lucid|词性:形容词

murkiness:/ˈmɜːrkɪnɪs/ (UK) /ˈmɝːkɪnɪs/ (US)| n.|黑暗；浑浊；模糊不清|The state or quality of being murky.|例句：The murkiness of the water made it unsafe to swim. 水的浑浊使游泳不安全。

例句：The murkiness of his explanation left me with more questions than answers. 他含糊其辞的解释让我更加疑惑。

近义词：darkness, gloom, obscurity, cloudiness, dimness, haziness|反义词：clarity, brightness, transparency, lucidity|词性:名词
