metallurgists:/ˈmɛtəlɜːrdʒɪsts/
n.
冶金学家，冶金工程师
Experts in the science and technology of metals, including extraction, refining, processing, and application.
The metallurgists analyzed the alloy to determine its composition. 冶金学家分析了合金以确定其成分。
The team of metallurgists developed a new process for refining steel.  冶金学家团队开发了一种新的炼钢工艺。

近义词:metalworkers, metallurgic engineers
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "layperson" in a specific context)
词性:noun (plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From metallurgy + -ist.
Metallurgy: from French métallurgie, from Greek metallourgía, from métallon "metal" + érgon "work".
