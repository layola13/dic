hector:/ˈhɛktər/| v. |威吓；恐吓|to bully or intimidate someone; to try to force someone to do something using threats or insults.|He was hectoring the shop assistant into giving him a refund. 他恐吓店员，要她给他退款。|Synonyms: bully, intimidate, browbeat, harass, badger|Antonyms: encourage, support, soothe, comfort|verb

hector:/ˈhɛktər/| n. |恃强凌弱者；<古>勇士，英雄|A bully; a blustering, domineering person.  In Greek legend, Hector was a Trojan hero.|The neighborhood hector was always picking fights. 这个街区的恶霸总是挑起争端。|Synonyms: bully, intimidator, tyrant|Antonyms: coward, weakling|noun
