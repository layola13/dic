echopathy:/ɛkˈɒpəθi/| n. |回声性，模仿言语|The involuntary repetition of words or phrases spoken by another person.  This can be a symptom of certain neurological or psychiatric conditions. | He exhibited echopathy, repeating everything the doctor said. 他表现出模仿言语，重复医生说的每一句话。| 近义词：echolalia| 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider "independent speech") | noun
