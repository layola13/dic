outmoving:/ˌaʊtˈmuːvɪŋ/
adjective
向外移动的；迁出的
Relating to or involving the act of moving out of a place.
The outmoving tenants left the apartment spotless. 迁出的租户把公寓打扫得一尘不染。
The outmoving family packed their belongings into a truck.  搬家的家庭把他们的物品装进卡车。
近义词: departing, exiting, vacating
反义词: incoming, arriving
