inroad:/ˈɪnˌroʊd/| n. |进展，侵蚀，消耗|an advance or penetration, often at the expense of something else.  A hostile incursion.|例句：The company made significant inroads into the European market.  这家公司在欧洲市场取得了重大进展。|例句：Stress can make serious inroads into a person's health. 压力会严重损害一个人的健康。|近义词：advance, encroachment, incursion, penetration|反义词: retreat, withdrawal|词性:noun
