isobathic:/ˌaɪsəˈbæθɪk/
adjective
等深的，等深线的
Relating to or denoting a line on a map or chart connecting points having the same depth below the surface of the sea.
The isobathic lines on the chart showed the varying depths of the ocean floor.  海图上的等深线显示了海底的不同深度。
The submarine followed an isobathic contour. 潜水艇沿着等深线航行。
synonyms:  isobath, depth contour
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "variable depth" or "uneven seabed")
词性: adjective
