arborescence:/ˌɑːrbəˈrɛsəns/ | n. | 树状结构，树枝状 |The branching pattern of a tree or a diagram that resembles it.  A tree-like structure or arrangement.| The arborescence of the veins in the leaf was fascinating.  叶脉的树状结构令人着迷。 | synonyms: dendrite, branching, ramification | antonyms: linearity, straightness | noun
