kneepiece:/ˈniːpiːs/
n.
护膝，膝垫，跪板
A protective covering for the knee. / A padded support worn over the knee. / A small board or cushion used for kneeling.
The carpenter wore a kneepiece while installing the flooring.  木匠在安装地板时戴着护膝。
She used a kneepiece to protect her knees while gardening.  她在园艺时用护膝保护膝盖。
synonyms: knee pad, kneeler
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider: unprotected knee)
noun

kneepiece:/ˈniːpiːs/
n.
(Armor) 膝甲
A piece of armor protecting the knee.
The knight's kneepiece was intricately decorated.  骑士的膝甲装饰精美。
The museum displayed a collection of medieval kneepieces.  博物馆展出了一系列中世纪的护膝。
synonyms:  knee armor,  cuisse (for thigh armor which often included knee protection)
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider: unprotected knee)
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From knee + piece.  "Knee" comes from Old English *cneow. "Piece" comes from Old French piece.
