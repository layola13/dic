nonsustenance:/ˌnɒnsʌsˈtɛnəns/
n.
缺乏营养; 无营养;不能维持生命的东西
Lack of sustenance; inability to provide nourishment.
The refugees were suffering from nonsustenance and disease. 难民们正遭受着营养不良和疾病的折磨。
Synonyms:  malnutrition, starvation, inanition, deprivation
Antonyms: nourishment, sustenance, nutriment
noun
