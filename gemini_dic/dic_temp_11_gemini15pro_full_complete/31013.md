challenge:/ˈtʃælɪndʒ/ (US), /ˈtʃælɪndʒ/ (UK)| vt.|挑战，质疑，考验|To challenge someone means to invite them to compete or to question their authority or statement.|The lawyer challenged the witness's testimony. 律师质疑证人的证词. |She challenged him to a race. 她向他挑战赛跑. |synonyms: question, dispute, confront, oppose|antonyms: accept, agree, support|verb
