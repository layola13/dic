nontemporizing:/nɒnˈtɛmpəˌraɪzɪŋ/
adjective
不拖延的，立即行动的
Not delaying, acting immediately and decisively.
The surgeon’s nontemporizing actions saved the patient’s life.  外科医生毫不拖延的行动挽救了病人的生命。
The CEO made a nontemporizing decision to restructure the company. 首席执行官当机立断，决定重组公司。
近义词: decisive, prompt, immediate, forthright, direct
反义词: delaying, procrastinating, temporizing, hesitant, indecisive
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + temporizing, from temporize (v.) "to comply with the time or occasion; to yield to current opinion," from Late Latin temporizare "to comply with the times," from Latin tempus (genitive temporis) "time, season, proper time or season."
