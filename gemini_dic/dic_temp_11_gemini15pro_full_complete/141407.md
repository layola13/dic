ophiologist:/ˌɒfiˈɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌoʊfiˈɑːlədʒɪst/
n.
蛇类学家
A scientist who studies snakes and other reptiles, particularly snakes.
The ophiologist carefully handled the venomous cobra. 蛇类学家小心翼翼地处理着那条毒眼镜蛇。

近义词:herpetologist
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who fears or dislikes snakes intensely)
词性: Noun
