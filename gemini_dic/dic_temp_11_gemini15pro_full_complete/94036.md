impartable:/ɪmˈpɑːrtəbl/ | adj. | 可传授的，可告知的 | Capable of being imparted or communicated; able to be shared or transmitted. |  例句：The knowledge he possessed was impartable to his students. 他拥有的知识可以传授给他的学生。

例句：She had an impartable enthusiasm for her work. 她对工作有着可感染的热情。 | 近义词：communicable, transmissible, teachable, conveyable | 反义词：incommunicable, unteachable, withheld | 词性: 形容词
