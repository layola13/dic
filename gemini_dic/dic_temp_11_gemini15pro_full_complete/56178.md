dodecaphonically:/ˌdoʊˌdekəˈfɒnɪkli/| adv.|十二音地，十二音技法地|In a manner characterized by the twelve-tone technique of composition, where all twelve notes of the chromatic scale are treated as equally important and none are given prominence.|The piece was composed dodecaphonically, creating a dissonant yet intriguing sound. 这首曲子是用十二音技法创作的，创造了一种不和谐但引人入胜的声音。|Near synonyms: atonally, serially|Antonyms: tonally, traditionally|Adverb
