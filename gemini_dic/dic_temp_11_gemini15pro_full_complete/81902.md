griffon:/ˈɡrɪfən/ /ˈɡrɪfɪn/| n. |狮鹫，格里芬|A mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle.|例句：The griffon guarded the treasure. 狮鹫守护着宝藏。

例句：Legends say that griffons are incredibly strong and loyal. 传说狮鹫非常强壮和忠诚。|近义词：griffin, hippogriff|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *grifon*, from Late Latin *gryphus*, from Greek *γρύψ* (*grūps*), perhaps related to *γρυπός* (*grupós*) "hook-nosed".
