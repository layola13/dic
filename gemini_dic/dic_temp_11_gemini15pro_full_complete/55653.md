dithyramb:/ˈdɪθɪræm/ | n. |狂热赞歌；酒神颂；奔放的诗歌 | A wild, enthusiastic, or ecstatic speech or writing, often in praise of something.  Originally, a choral hymn sung and danced in honor of Dionysus, the Greek god of wine and revelry. |  His latest poem is a passionate dithyramb to nature.  他最新的诗歌是对大自然充满激情的赞美。| synonyms: eulogy, panegyric, accolade, encomium, paean | antonyms: criticism, condemnation, censure, diatribe | noun
