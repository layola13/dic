halve:/hæv/ | v. | 把…分成两半；减半 | to divide something into two equal parts |  The company halved its workforce. 公司将其员工人数减半。| synonyms: divide, bisect, reduce by half | antonyms: double, increase | verb

halve:/hæv/ | v. | 平分（尤指食物）| to share something equally | They halved the pizza between them. 他们把披萨平分了。| synonyms: share equally, split | antonyms: keep all, monopolize | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *healfan, from Proto-Germanic *halbjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- "to strike, divide."  Cognates include German halb and Dutch half.
