interlunary: /ˌɪntərˈluːnəri/ | adj. | 月缺的；新月之间的；朔望月间的 |  Relating to the period between old and new moons, when the moon is not visible.  It describes the dark period of the lunar cycle. |  The interlunary night was dark and silent. 月缺的夜晚黑暗而寂静。 | Synonym: dark, moonless | Antonym: bright, lunar | Adjective


interlunary: /ˌɪntərˈluːnəri/ | n. |  月缺期 | The period between the old moon and the new moon. |  During the interlunary, the nights are particularly dark. 在月缺期间，夜晚尤其黑暗。 | Synonym: dark moon | Antonym: full moon | Noun
