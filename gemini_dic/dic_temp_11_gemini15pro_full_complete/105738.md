kippa:/ˈkɪpə/| n.|圆顶小帽，犹太男子戴的无檐小帽|A small, round, brimless cap worn by Jewish men, especially during prayer.|He wore a kippa to show respect in the synagogue. 他戴着基帕在犹太教堂表示敬意。|Synonyms: yarmulke, skullcap|Antonyms: None|noun
