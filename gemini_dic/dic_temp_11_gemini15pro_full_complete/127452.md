nebulization:/ˌnɛbjʊlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |雾化，喷雾|The process of converting a liquid into a fine spray or mist. |The doctor prescribed nebulization therapy for her asthma. 医生给她开了雾化治疗哮喘。| 近义词：atomization, vaporization | 反义词: condensation | noun

nebulize:/ˈnɛbjʊlaɪz/ | v. |雾化，喷雾|To convert a liquid into a fine spray or mist. |She nebulizes her medication twice a day. 她每天两次雾化她的药物。| 近义词：atomize, vaporize | 反义词: condense | verb
