bipartite:/baɪˈpɑːrtaɪt/
adjective
二分的，两分的
Consisting of two parts.  Divided into two parts.  Involving two parties.
例句：A bipartite graph is a graph whose vertices can be divided into two disjoint and independent sets.  一个二分图是一个其顶点可以被分成两个不相交且独立的集合的图。

例句：The agreement was a bipartite contract between the buyer and the seller. 该协议是买方和卖方之间的双边合同。
近义词：two-part, dual
反义词：multipartite
词性：adjective


bipartite:/baɪˈpɑːrtaɪt/
noun
二分体
Something that is divided into two parts.
例句：The bipartite structure of the government ensured checks and balances. 政府的二元结构确保了制衡。

例句：The bipartite nature of the cell's nucleus was clearly visible under the microscope. 细胞核的两部分结构在显微镜下清晰可见。
近义词：dichotomy
反义词：unity
词性：noun
