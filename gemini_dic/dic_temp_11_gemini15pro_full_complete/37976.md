complic:/ˈkɒmplɪk/ (BrE) /ˈkɑːmplɪk/ (AmE)| vt.|使复杂化，使恶化|to make something more complicated or difficult|The situation is already complicated, don't complic it further.  情况已经很复杂了，不要使它进一步恶化。|The new regulations only complic matters. 新规定只会使事情更复杂。|近义词：complicate, worsen, aggravate|反义词：simplify, ease, improve|词性:动词
词源: From Latin complicare, from com- ‘together’ + plicare ‘to fold’.
