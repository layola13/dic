martlemas: /ˈmɑːrtlɪməs/ | n. | 圣马丁节，小阳春 |  A feast day celebrated on November 11, traditionally associated with the end of autumn and the slaughter of livestock before winter.  It is also sometimes associated with a brief period of mild weather. |  Martlemas was a time for feasting and preparing for the winter months. 圣马丁节是宴饮和准备过冬的时期。

The warm weather at Martlemas this year was a welcome surprise. 今年圣马丁节的温暖天气是一个惊喜。| 近义词: Martinmas | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
