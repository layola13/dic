conhydrine:/kənˈhaɪdriːn/|n.|毒芹碱；芹叶钩吻碱|A poisonous alkaloid found in poison hemlock (Conium maculatum) along with coniine.|Conhydrine is less toxic than coniine, but still dangerous. 毒芹碱的毒性比毒芹素小，但仍然危险。|Synonyms:  no exact synonyms, but related alkaloids include coniine, pseudoconhydrine|Antonyms:  None|Noun

conhydrine:/kənˈhaɪdriːn/|n.|毒芹碱；芹叶钩吻碱|A poisonous alkaloid occurring in poison hemlock along with coniine.|Ingestion of conhydrine can lead to paralysis.  摄入毒芹碱会导致瘫痪。|Synonyms:  no exact synonyms, but related alkaloids include coniine, pseudoconhydrine|Antonyms:  None|Noun
