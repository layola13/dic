misconnect:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛkt/
verb
错误连接，连接错误
to connect incorrectly or inappropriately
The telephone operator misconnected the call and I ended up talking to a stranger. 电话接线员接错了电话，结果我和一个陌生人说了话。
misjoin, misplug
connect correctly, join correctly
v.
