bookwork:/ˈbʊkwɜːrk/
noun
课本作业，书面作业
Study involving reading and writing, rather than practical work.
She spent the weekend catching up on her bookwork. 她周末都在赶写课本作业。
synonyms: studying, academic work, coursework, schoolwork
antonyms: fieldwork, practical work, lab work
n.
