anticodonic:/ˌæntɪkəˈdɒnɪk/
adjective
反密码子的
Relating to or involving an anticodon.  Specifically, complementary to a codon.
The anticodonic loop of tRNA binds to the mRNA codon. 反密码子环状tRNA与mRNA密码子结合。
The anticodonic sequence is crucial for accurate translation. 反密码子序列对于精确翻译至关重要。
Near Synonyms: complementary (to a codon)
Antonyms: codon
词性: adjective
