ortolan:/ˈɔːrtələn/ | n. | 欧洲朱顶雀; 烤圃鵐 | A small European songbird (Emberiza hortulana) that was formerly considered a delicacy and eaten as a game bird.  It's now a protected species. |  The ortolan was once a prized delicacy in French cuisine.  欧洲朱顶雀曾是法国菜中的一道珍馐。

 The practice of eating ortolans is now illegal in many countries.  在许多国家，吃欧洲朱顶雀现在是非法的。 | 近义词: bunting (general term for similar birds) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French *hortolan, from Late Latin hortulanus (“gardener”), from hortus (“garden”), because it frequents gardens.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gher- (“to grasp, enclose”).
