nonpunishment:/ˌnɒnˈpʌnɪʃmənt/ | n. | 非惩罚，免除惩罚 |  The act of not punishing someone for an offense or wrongdoing; exemption from punishment. | The policy of nonpunishment for minor offenses led to an increase in such behavior.  这项对轻微犯罪不予惩罚的政策导致此类行为增加。| synonyms: impunity, exemption, immunity, pardon, remission | antonyms: punishment, penalty, retribution, sanction | noun
