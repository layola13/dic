botching:/ˈbɒtʃɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɑːtʃɪŋ/ (UK)| v. | 拙劣地工作；搞砸 | spoiling something by doing it badly. |  He botched the repair job and now the faucet leaks even worse. 他把修理工作搞砸了，现在水龙头漏得更厉害了。

| He completely botched the presentation and lost the client.  他彻底搞砸了演讲，失去了客户。

| 近义词: messing up, bungling, ruining, spoiling | 反义词: perfecting, mastering, succeeding | 词性: 动名词 (gerund)
