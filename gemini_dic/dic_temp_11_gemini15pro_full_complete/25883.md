burlesquer:/bərˈlɛskər/
n.
滑稽模仿演员；作戏谑模仿的人
A performer of burlesque; someone who creates or performs burlesque.
The famous burlesquer brought the house down with his witty impersonations of political figures.  这位著名的滑稽模仿演员以他对政治人物的机智模仿博得了满堂喝彩。
Synonyms: parodist, satirist, mimic, impersonator
Antonyms: serious actor, dramatist
Noun

Etymology->
From French *burlesquer*, from Italian *burlescare* "to write burlesque," from *burla* "joke, mockery."
