inculcator:/ɪnˈkʌlkeɪtər/
n.|灌输者；谆谆教诲者|A person who instills an idea or habit by persistent instruction.|The teacher acted as an inculcator of moral values, constantly reminding students of the importance of honesty and integrity.|近义词:teacher, instructor, mentor, guide, instiller|反义词:corruptor, misleader
词性:名词
