chintzily:/ˈtʃɪntsɪli/ | adv. | 廉价地，俗气地 | In a cheap, gaudy, or showy manner, reminiscent of chintz fabric.  Often implies a sense of poor taste or excessive ornamentation. |  She dressed chintzily for the opera, wearing a bright pink dress with sequins. 她穿着一件亮粉色带亮片的连衣裙去歌剧院，显得俗不可耐。 |  garishly, gaudily, tawdrily, flashily | tastefully, elegantly, subtly, discreetly | adverb

chintz:/tʃɪnts/ | n. | 印花棉布 | A brightly printed and glazed cotton fabric. | The curtains were made of a floral chintz. 窗帘是用印花棉布做的。 | calico, glazed cotton | plain fabric | noun
