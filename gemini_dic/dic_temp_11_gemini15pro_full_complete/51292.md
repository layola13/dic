detachednesses:/dɪˈtætʃtɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
客观公正;超然;冷漠;分离
the state of being detached or aloof; objectivity.

The scientist observed the experiment with detachedness, carefully recording the results without emotional involvement.
科学家以超然的态度观察实验，仔细记录结果，不受情绪影响。


Her detachedness from the situation made it difficult for her to empathize with the victims.
她对这种情况的冷漠使她很难同情受害者。

The detachedness of the cabins provided a sense of privacy.
独立小屋的分离提供了私密感。


Synonyms: objectivity, impartiality, neutrality, aloofness, indifference, remoteness, separation
Antonyms: involvement, engagement, partiality, bias, connection, attachment
