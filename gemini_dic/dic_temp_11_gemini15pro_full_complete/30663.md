cerography:/sɪˈrɒɡrəfi/
n.|蜡画;蜡画法|The art or process of producing images or writing on wax.  This technique was used in ancient times and has seen revivals in various forms throughout history.|Ancient Egyptians practiced cerography for creating death masks and votive offerings.|近义词:waxwork, encaustic painting|反义词: (No direct antonym exists.
