demean:/dɪˈmiːn/| v. |贬低，降低身份|To cause a lowering of status, dignity, or self-respect.|例句：Don't demean yourself by lying. 不要用谎言贬低你自己。|近义词：degrade, belittle, humiliate|反义词：exalt, elevate, praise|词性:动词
demean:/dɪˈmiːn/| v. |举止，表现 (较少用)|To behave or conduct oneself (usually in a specified manner).|例句：He demeaned himself like a true gentleman. 他表现得像个真正的绅士。|近义词：behave, conduct, comport|反义词：misbehave|词性:动词
