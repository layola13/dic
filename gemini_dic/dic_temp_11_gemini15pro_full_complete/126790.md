nanoscopic:/ˌnænəˈskɒpɪk/ /ˌnænəˈskɑːpɪk/
adjective
纳米级的；纳米尺度的
relating to objects or structures that are nanoscale;  having dimensions of only nanometers.  A nanometer is one billionth of a meter.
Scientists are exploring the nanoscopic world of atoms and molecules. 科学家们正在探索原子和分子的纳米世界。
近义词: nanoscale, submicroscopic
反义词: macroscopic
词性: adjective
