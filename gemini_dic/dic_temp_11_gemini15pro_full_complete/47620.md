debenture:/dɪˈbɛntʃər/ /dɪˈbɛntʃɚ/| n.|债券；信用债券|A debenture is a type of bond or other debt instrument that is unsecured by collateral. Debentures are backed only by the creditworthiness and reputation of the issuer.|The company issued debentures to raise capital for its expansion.  公司发行债券为其扩张筹集资金。|Synonyms: bond, note, security|Antonyms: asset, equity|Noun

debentures:/dɪˈbɛntʃərz/ /dɪˈbɛntʃɚz/| n.|债券；信用债券（复数）|Plural form of debenture, referring to multiple debt instruments unsecured by collateral.|Investors are showing increasing interest in corporate debentures.  投资者对公司债券的兴趣日益浓厚。|Synonyms: bonds, notes, securities|Antonyms: assets, equities|Noun
