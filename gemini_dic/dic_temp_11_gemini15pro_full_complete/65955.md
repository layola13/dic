exactors:/ɪɡˈzæktərz/| n. |勒索者，强求者|  A person who demands something, often money or obedience, in a forceful or oppressive way.  Someone who extorts. |  The exactors threatened the villagers with violence if they didn't pay. 勒索者威胁村民，如果他们不付钱就使用暴力。|  The tax exactors were known for their ruthlessness.  这些税吏以冷酷无情而闻名。|Synonyms: extortionists, oppressors, demanders | Antonyms: donors, givers, philanthropists | noun
