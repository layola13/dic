amebae:/əˈmiːbiː/ /əˈmiːbə/
noun
变形虫
A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm. Amebae are either free-living in damp environments or parasitic in animals.
The pond water was teeming with amebae.  池塘里的水里有很多变形虫。
