intercorrelations:/ˌɪntərˌkɒrəˈleɪʃənz/ (US) /ˌɪntərˌkɔːrəˈleɪʃənz/ (UK)| n. |相互关联，相互关系|The statistical analysis examined the intercorrelations among the different variables.|例句：The study explored the intercorrelations between poverty, crime, and education levels.  这项研究探讨了贫困、犯罪和教育水平之间的相互关系。

例句：The intercorrelations of the various economic indicators can be complex. 各种经济指标之间的相互关系可能很复杂。|近义词：correlations, relationships, connections, interrelationships|反义词:independence, disconnection|词性:名词
