ignition:/ɪɡˈnɪʃən/| n.|点火，点燃；引燃；燃烧；着火；启动|The act of setting on fire or catching fire; the process of starting an engine.|The ignition of the campfire created a warm glow.  篝火的点燃发出温暖的光芒。|Synonyms: combustion, firing, kindling|Antonyms: extinguishing, quenching|noun


ignition:/ɪɡˈnɪʃən/| n.|点火装置，点火系统|The system in an internal combustion engine that ignites the fuel-air mixture.|There's a problem with the car's ignition.  这辆车的点火系统有问题。|Synonyms: starter, igniter|Antonyms: N/A|noun
