honester:/ˈɒnɪstər (UK), /ˈɑːnɪstər (US)| adj.|更诚实的|Comparative form of "honest."  Indicates a greater degree of honesty than another person or thing. While grammatically correct, "more honest" is generally preferred in modern English.|He claimed to be the honester of the two candidates. 他声称自己是两位候选人中更诚实的那个。|Synonyms: more honest, truthful, upright, sincere|Antonyms: more dishonest, deceitful, corrupt, insincere|adjective
