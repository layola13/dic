jailable:/ˈdʒeɪləbəl/
adjective
可监禁的
Suitable or deserving to be put in jail.
He committed a jailable offense. 他犯了可判处监禁的罪行。
近义词: imprisonable, indictable
反义词: non-jailable, pardonable
词性: adjective

词源:  From "jail" + "-able." "Jail" comes from Old French "jaiole," ultimately from Latin "caveola," meaning "small cage." "-able" is a suffix indicating capability or worthiness.
