jerrycan:/ˈdʒɛrɪˌkæn/ | n. | 金属油桶; 汽油桶 | A robust, usually metal, container with a handle, used for carrying liquids, especially petrol or water. |  He filled the jerrycan with petrol before setting off on his journey. 他出发旅行前把油桶装满了汽油。

The soldiers relied on jerrycans for their water supply. 士兵们依靠油桶供水。 |  Synonyms: fuel can, gas can, petrol can | Antonyms: N/A | noun
