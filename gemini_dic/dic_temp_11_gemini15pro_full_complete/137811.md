nonvernacular:/ˌnɒn.vɜːˈnæk.jʊ.lər/
adjective
非方言的，非本地语的
Not in the language commonly spoken by people in a particular region or country; using a language that is not the ordinary language of conversation.
The scientist presented her findings in nonvernacular language, ensuring a wider audience could understand her research.  科学家用非方言的语言介绍了她的发现，以确保更广泛的受众能够理解她的研究。
The textbook uses nonvernacular terms, making it more challenging for non-specialists to grasp the concepts.  这本教科书使用了非方言的术语，这使得非专业人士更难掌握这些概念。
近义词:formal, technical, learned, scholarly
反义词:vernacular, colloquial, informal
词性:adjective
词源:From non- + vernacular.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "vernacular" refers to the common language of a region.
