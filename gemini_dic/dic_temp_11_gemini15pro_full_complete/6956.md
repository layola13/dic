一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I am a student. = 我是一名学生。
You are tall. = 你很高。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is reading a book. = 她正在读一本书。
They are playing football. = 他们正在踢足球。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has been to London. = 他去过伦敦。
We have finished our work. = 我们已经完成了工作。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been studying English for three years. = 我学习英语三年了。
She has been waiting for an hour. = 她等了一个小时了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I was tired yesterday. = 我昨天很累。
They were at the party last night. = 他们昨晚在派对上。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He was watching TV when I called. = 我打电话时他正在看电视。
She was cooking dinner at that time. = 那时她正在做晚饭。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already eaten when he arrived. = 他到达时我已经吃过了。
She had finished her homework before she went out. = 她出去之前已经完成了作业。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been working for two hours when she called. = 她打电话时我已经工作两个小时了。
He had been playing video games all day. = 他整天都在玩电子游戏。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be there tomorrow. = 我明天会在那里。
They will go to the cinema next week. = 他们下周会去看电影。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be sleeping at this time tomorrow. = 明天这个时候我正在睡觉。
She will be working on the project next week. = 她下周将进行这个项目。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have finished the book by next week. = 我下周之前会读完这本书。
They will have moved to a new house by then. = 到那时他们会搬到新家。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been living here for five years next month. = 下个月我将在这里住满五年。
She will have been studying at this university for four years by the time she graduates. = 到她毕业的时候，她将在这所大学学习了四年。
