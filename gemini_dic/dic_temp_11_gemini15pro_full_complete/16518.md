bark:/bɑːrk/ (UK) /bɑːrk/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|树皮，吠叫，厉声说话|The rough outer covering of a tree trunk, branch, and root.  To make the short, loud cry of a dog or other animal.|The dog barked at the mailman. 狗对着邮递员吠叫。|近义词:(n.) rind, husk, covering; (v.) yelp, yap, howl|反义词:(v.) whisper, murmur|词性:名词, 动词
