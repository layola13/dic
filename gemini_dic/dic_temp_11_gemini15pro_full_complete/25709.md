bunniest:/ˈbʌniɪst/| adj.|最像兔子的，最可爱的|Superlative adjective of bunny.  Means most resembling a bunny, often used to describe something cute or cuddly.|"She has the bunniest smile I've ever seen." 她拥有我见过的最可爱的微笑。|近义词：cutest, sweetest, most adorable|反义词：ugliest, least appealing|词性:形容词

词源: bunny (兔子) + -est (最高级后缀)
