money:/ˈmʌni/ | n. | 金钱，货币 | A medium of exchange, typically in the form of coins and banknotes; wealth reckoned in terms of it. |  I need money to buy food. 我需要钱买食物。

|  He earns a lot of money. 他赚很多钱。 | synonyms: cash, currency, funds, capital, wealth | antonyms: debt, poverty | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *moneie*, from Latin *moneta* (“mint, coinage, money”), originally a surname of Juno, in whose temple in Rome money was coined.
