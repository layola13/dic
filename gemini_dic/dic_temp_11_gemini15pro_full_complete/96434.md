indistinctly:/ɪndɪˈstɪŋktli/| adv.|模糊地，不清楚地|Not clearly, vaguely; in a way that is difficult to see, hear, or understand.|She mumbled indistinctly, and I couldn't understand what she was saying. 她含糊不清地咕哝着，我听不懂她在说什么。|Near Synonyms: faintly, vaguely, obscurely, mutedly, fuzzily|Antonyms: clearly, distinctly, sharply, precisely|词性:副词

indistinctly:/ɪndɪˈstɪŋktli/| adv.|模糊地，不清楚地|Lacking clarity or precision; not easily discernible or understood.|The figures in the fog were indistinctly visible.  雾中的人影模糊不清。|Near Synonyms: hazily, blurrily, cloudily|Antonyms: clearly, distinctly, sharply, precisely|词性:副词
