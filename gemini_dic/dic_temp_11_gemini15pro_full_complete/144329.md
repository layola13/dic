outspurts:/ˈaʊtspɜːrts/| n.|迸发，冲刺，突然的加速|A sudden burst of activity or growth; a spurt.|The economy experienced several outspurts of growth during the decade.  这个十年中，经济经历了几次快速增长。|近义词：spurts, bursts, surges|反义词: declines, lulls|名词

outspurt:/ˌaʊtˈspɜːrt/| v.|胜过，超过，突然加速|To surpass or outdo in a spurt; to suddenly accelerate and move ahead of.|The runner outspurted his competitors in the final stretch of the race.  赛跑选手在最后冲刺阶段超过了他的对手。|近义词：outstrip, outrun, surpass|反义词: lag behind, fall behind|动词
