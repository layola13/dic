injoin:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪn/ | vt. | 命令；禁止；嘱咐 | To command; to prohibit; to urge strongly. | The judge enjoined the defendant from contacting the victim. 法官禁止被告联系受害人。|  The doctor enjoined him to rest. 医生嘱咐他要休息。 | 近义词: command, order, prohibit, forbid, urge | 反义词: allow, permit | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *enjoindre, from Latin iniungere "to join into, fasten upon, inflict," from in- "in" + iungere "to join."
