modestly:/ˈmɑːdɪstli/ /ˈmɒdɪstli/| adv.|谦虚地，适度地|In a way that shows you do not think you are very important or very good at something.|She spoke modestly about her achievements. 她谦虚地谈论她的成就。|Near Synonyms: humbly, unassumingly, unpretentiously|Antonyms: arrogantly, boastfully, proudly|词性:副词

modest:/ˈmɑːdɪst/ /ˈmɒdɪst/| adj.|谦虚的，适度的|Not talking much about your own abilities or possessions.|He's very modest about his success. 他对自己的成功非常谦虚。|Near Synonyms: humble, unassuming, unpretentious|Antonyms: arrogant, boastful, proud|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin modestus "keeping due measure, moderate, sober, orderly," from modus "measure, manner, fashion, way"
