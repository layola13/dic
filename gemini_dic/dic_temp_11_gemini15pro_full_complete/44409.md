cross-leggednesses:/ˌkrɒsˈlɛɡɪdnɪsɪz/ /ˌkrɔːsˈlɛɡɪdnɪsɪz/ | n. | 盘腿而坐的姿势（复数）|The state or quality of having legs crossed, typically at the ankles or knees.  This word is rarely used and considered awkward.  It is more common to use "cross-legged position" or similar phrases.| He observed the various cross-leggednesses of the meditators. 他观察了冥想者们各种盘腿的姿势。| Near synonyms:  crossed legs, cross-legged position | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "straight-legged") | noun

cross-legged:/ˌkrɒsˈlɛɡɪd/ /ˌkrɔːsˈlɛɡɪd/ | adj. | 盘腿而坐的 |With legs crossed, typically at the ankles or knees.|She sat cross-legged on the floor. 她盘腿坐在地板上。|Near synonyms:  with legs crossed | Antonyms: straight-legged | adjective
