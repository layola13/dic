cozy:/ˈkoʊzi/ (US) /ˈkəʊzi/ (UK)| adj.|舒适的，惬意的，温暖的|Giving a feeling of comfort, warmth, and relaxation.|例句：The room was warm and cozy with a fireplace. 房间里有一个壁炉，温暖舒适。|例句：We spent a cozy evening at home, reading by the fire. 我们在家度过了一个舒适的夜晚，围着炉火读书。|近义词：comfortable, snug, warm, intimate|反义词：uncomfortable, cold, drafty, impersonal|词性:形容词

词源:
From Scots cozie, cosy (“comfortable, snug”), probably a variant of Old English *cosig. Cognate with West Frisian koaze (“hut, cabin”), Dutch koos (“choice, dainty, delicate, snug”).
