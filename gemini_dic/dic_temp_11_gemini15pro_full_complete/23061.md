bouncingly:/ˈbaʊnsɪŋli/| adv.|有弹性地，活泼地|In a bouncing manner; with a springing or rebounding movement.  Describes an action done with energy and resilience, often implying a cheerful or playful attitude.|She walked bouncingly down the street, her spirits high. 她蹦蹦跳跳地走在街上，兴高采烈。|The ball bounced bouncingly across the court.  球在球场上弹来弹去。|近义词：resiliently, springily, energetically, playfully|反义词：sluggishly, listlessly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From bounce + -ing + -ly.  "Bounce" originates from Middle Low German or Middle Dutch "bunsen" or "bonzen," meaning "to beat, knock."
