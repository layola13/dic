cephalanthium:/ˌsefəˈlænθiəm/ | n. | 头状花序 | A type of inflorescence (flower cluster) in which the flowers are densely clustered on a receptacle, forming a head-like structure. | Example: The cephalanthium of the buttonbush is spherical.  白花灯台树的头状花序是球形的。 | Example:  The bees were attracted to the fragrant cephalanthium. 蜜蜂被芳香的头状花序所吸引。 | Synonym: flower head, capitulum | Antonym: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kephalē* (head) + *anthos* (flower)
