metathesis:/məˈtæθəsɪs/
n.
换位，置换
the transposition of sounds or letters in a word
例句：Metathesis is responsible for the pronunciation of "ask" as "aks."  换位是“ask”发音为“aks”的原因。

例句：The word "bird" was originally "brid," a metathesis having occurred over time.  “bird”这个词最初是“brid”，随着时间的推移发生了换位。
近义词：transposition, inversion

词性: noun
