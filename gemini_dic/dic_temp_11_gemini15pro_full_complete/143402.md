outflame:/ˌaʊtˈfleɪm/
verb
超过…的火焰；燃烧得比…更旺
surpass in fierceness or brilliance; burn more brightly than
The bonfire outflamed the neighboring torches.  篝火比附近的火把燃烧得更旺。
近义词: outshine, outblaze, surpass
反义词: pale, dim
词性: verb
