insectivore:/ɪnˈsɛktɪvɔːr/ (US), /ɪnˈsɛktɪvɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |食虫动物；食虫植物|An animal or plant that feeds on insects. |例句：Bats are nocturnal insectivores.  蝙蝠是夜行性食虫动物。

|例句：The Venus flytrap is a carnivorous insectivore.  捕蝇草是一种肉食性食虫动物。|近义词：carnivore, predator|反义词: herbivore|词性: noun


insectivorous:/ˌɪnsɛkˈtɪvərəs/ (US), /ˌɪnsɛkˈtɪvərəs/ (UK)| adj. |食虫的|Feeding on insects. |例句： Insectivorous plants trap insects for nutrients. 食虫植物捕捉昆虫以获取营养。

|例句：Shrews are small, insectivorous mammals.  鼩鼱是小型食虫哺乳动物。|近义词：carnivorous|反义词: herbivorous|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insect(i)-vorus*, from insectum (“insect”) + -vorus (“-eating, devouring”), from vorare (“to devour”).
