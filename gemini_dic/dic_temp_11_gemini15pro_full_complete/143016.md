outbids:/ˌaʊtˈbɪdz/ | v. | 出价高于（某人）；胜过 |  To offer a higher price than (someone else) at an auction or in a competitive situation. |  She outbid all the other collectors for the antique vase. 她出价比其他所有收藏家都高，买下了那只古董花瓶。 | synonyms: outprice, overbid | antonyms: underbid, lose to | verb

outbid:/ˌaʊtˈbɪd/ | v. |  胜过；超过 | To surpass or defeat someone in a competitive situation. | He outbid his rivals and won the contract. 他战胜了竞争对手，赢得了合同。 | synonyms: surpass, defeat, beat, outdo, outstrip | antonyms: lose to, be defeated by | verb
