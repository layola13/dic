monocarpic:/ˌmɒnəʊˈkɑːpɪk/ | adj. | 单次结实性的；一次性开花结果的 | (of a plant) flowering only once and then dying. |  The agave plant is monocarpic, meaning it blooms only once in its lifetime. 龙舌兰是单次结实植物，意味着它一生只开一次花。| synonyms: semelparous | antonyms: polycarpic | adjective
