misorienting:/ˌmɪsɔːriˈɛntɪŋ/
adj.
使人迷失方向的，令人迷惑的
Causing someone to lose their sense of direction or to become confused.
The dense fog was misorienting, making it difficult to find the trail. 浓雾令人迷失方向，很难找到小路。
近义词: confusing, bewildering, disorienting
反义词: orienting, guiding, clarifying
词性: adjective
