easterlings:/ˈiːstərliŋz/
noun
东方人，东部居民
People from the east, particularly in relation to those from western regions.  Specifically in Tolkien's works, it refers to men from the east of Middle-earth.
The easterlings allied with Mordor in the War of the Ring. 东方人在魔戒大战中与魔多结盟。
The king feared the invasion of the easterlings. 国王害怕东方人入侵。
Synonyms: Easterners, Orientals (in older, sometimes offensive usage)
Antonyms: Westerners
