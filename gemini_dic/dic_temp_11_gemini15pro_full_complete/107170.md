lagering: /ˈlɑːɡərɪŋ/ | n. | 陈酿；低温储藏 | The process of conditioning beer, especially lager, by storing it at a low temperature for an extended period. This allows the beer to clarify and develop its flavor. | The brewery uses a traditional lagering process for its award-winning pilsner. 这家啤酒厂采用传统的陈酿工艺酿造其屡获殊荣的比尔森啤酒。

The lagering period for this beer is six weeks. 这种啤酒的陈酿期为六周。 | 近义词: aging, maturing, conditioning | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From German *Lagern*, "to store," related to *Lager* "storehouse, warehouse."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *lagraną* "to lay, put, place."
