birdbolt:/ˈbɜːrdbəʊlt/| n.|弩箭，短箭；（比喻）闪电，霹雳|A short, thick arrow shot from a crossbow.  Figuratively, a lightning bolt.|The hunter carefully aimed his birdbolt at the pheasant. 猎人小心翼翼地将弩箭瞄准野鸡。

The sudden birdbolt startled everyone on the golf course. 突如其来的闪电吓坏了高尔夫球场上所有的人。|近义词：arrow, dart, bolt, thunderbolt (figurative)|反义词: None|词性:noun
