calorification:/kəˌlɔːrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |热化；生热作用；产热 | The process of producing heat or increasing the amount of heat. |  The calorification of the body is essential for maintaining a stable internal temperature.  人体产热对于维持稳定的内部温度至关重要。| synonyms:  thermogenesis, heat production | antonyms: cooling, refrigeration | noun
