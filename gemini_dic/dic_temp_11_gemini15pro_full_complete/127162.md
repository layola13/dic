naturalesque:/ˌnætʃərəˈlɛsk/
adjective
自然风格的，仿自然的
Resembling or imitating nature, often in a stylized or idealized way.
The painting had a naturalesque quality, capturing the essence of the forest. 这幅画有一种自然风格，捕捉到了森林的精髓。
Near synonyms: naturalistic, nature-inspired, nature-mimicking
Near antonyms: artificial, synthetic, unnatural
word class: adjective
