hataki: /hɑːˈtɑːkiː/ | n. | 拂尘，掸子 | A hataki is a traditional Japanese duster, often made of feathers or horse hair, used for cleaning dust from delicate surfaces. |  She used a hataki to dust the antique vase. 她用拂尘掸去古董花瓶上的灰尘。 | synonyms: duster, feather duster | antonyms: vacuum cleaner | noun

hataki: /hɑːˈtɑːkiː/ | v. | 用拂尘掸 | To clean or dust something with a hataki. | She hatakis the shelves every morning. 她每天早上都用拂尘掸架子。 | synonyms: dust, clean | antonyms: dirty | verb
