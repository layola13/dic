misrepresenters:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛntərz/
|n.|歪曲事实者，谎报者|People who misrepresent facts or information, often intentionally.  那些歪曲事实或信息的人，通常是故意的。|The misrepresenters were called out for their deceptive advertising. 这些歪曲事实者因其具有欺骗性的广告而被点名批评。|Synonyms: liars, deceivers, prevaricators|Antonyms: truth-tellers, honest people|Noun
