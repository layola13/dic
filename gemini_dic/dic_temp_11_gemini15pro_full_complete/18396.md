belongs:/bɪˈlɒŋz/ (UK) /bɪˈlɔːŋz/ (US)| v. |属于，应在…|To be the property of; to be a member of; to have its proper place (somewhere)|This book belongs to me. 这本书是我的。|synonyms: pertain, relate, fit, be owned by|antonyms: be alien to, be out of place|verb

belongs:/bɪˈlɒŋz/ (UK) /bɪˈlɔːŋz/ (US)| v. |应在…|To have its proper place (somewhere)|These files belong in the archive. 这些文件应该放在档案室里。|synonyms: fit in, have a place|antonyms: be misplaced, be out of place|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *belangen "pertain to," from be- + langen "to belong," which is related to long (adj.). The sense shifted from "pertain to" to "be the property of" in Middle English.
