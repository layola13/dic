multichambered:/ˌmʌltiˈtʃeɪmbərd/
adjective
多腔室的
Having multiple chambers or compartments.
The multichambered heart of a mammal is more efficient than the simpler hearts of reptiles. 哺乳动物的多腔室心脏比爬行动物简单的两腔室心脏更有效率。
The archaeologist discovered a multichambered tomb. 考古学家发现了一个多室墓穴。
multicompartment, multilocular
single-chambered, unicameral
adjective
