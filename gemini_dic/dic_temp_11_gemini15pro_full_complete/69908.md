fertilizes:/ˈfɜːrtəlaɪzɪz/
verb
施肥，使肥沃，使受精
causes something to become fertile or more fertile; to supply with fertilizer.
The farmer fertilizes his crops regularly to ensure a good harvest. 农民定期给他的作物施肥以确保丰收。
He fertilizes the lawn every spring. 他每年春天都给草坪施肥。
synonyms: enrich, nourish, feed, manure, compost
antonyms: deplete, impoverish, sterilize
词性: 动词
