forced:/fɔːrst/ /fɔːrst/| adj. v. |被迫的，强迫的|Made to happen or exist, especially by force or without choice.  Comes from the Old French "forcer" meaning "to force, break open, compel." Ultimately derived from Latin "fortis" meaning "strong."|
The pilot made a forced landing due to engine failure. 由于引擎故障，飞行员进行了迫降。
He gave a forced smile, trying to hide his disappointment. 他勉强笑了笑，试图掩饰自己的失望。
|近义词：compelled, obligatory, involuntary, constrained|反义词：voluntary, willing, unforced, natural|词性:形容词，动词(过去式和过去分词)
