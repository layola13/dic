inaptitude:/ɪˈnæptɪtjuːd/ | n. | 无能; 不称职; 不 aptitude | Lack of skill or ability. |  His inaptitude for mathematics was a major obstacle to his career in engineering. 他缺乏数学才能是他工程事业的主要障碍。| Example:  She demonstrated a surprising inaptitude for cooking. 她表现出惊人的烹饪无能。| Synonyms: incompetence, inability, ineptitude, unfitness, lack of skill | Antonyms: aptitude, ability, skill, talent, competence | noun
