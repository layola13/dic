increasing:/ɪnˈkriːsɪŋli/ | adv. | 越来越多地，日益 | to a progressively greater degree |  It is becoming increasingly difficult to find affordable housing. 找到经济适用房变得越来越难。 | synonyms: progressively, more and more, increasingly | antonyms: less and less, decreasingly | adverb
