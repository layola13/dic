aphorize:/ˈæfəˌraɪz/| v. |格言化；用格言表达；|To express a thought or observation in a concise, memorable, and often witty manner, typically as a general truth or principle.  To turn (something) into an aphorism.|He aphorized the lessons he'd learned in life. 他将他人生中学到的教训格言化。|Synonyms:  epigrammatize,  maximise,  sententiousise|Antonyms:  expound, elaborate,  explain at length|verb
