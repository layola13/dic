bulimia nervosa: /buˈlɪmiə nɜːrˈvoʊsə/ (US) /buˈlɪmiə nɜːˈvəʊsə/ (UK) | n. | 神经性贪食症 | An eating disorder characterized by binge eating followed by purging (self-induced vomiting, excessive use of laxatives, or excessive exercise) due to an excessive concern about body shape and weight. |  She was diagnosed with bulimia nervosa after years of struggling with her weight. 她与体重抗争多年后，被诊断出患有神经性贪食症。

My friend's bulimia nervosa is seriously affecting her health. 我朋友的神经性贪食症正严重影响着她的健康。

| 近义词: eating disorder | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "healthy eating habits") | 词性: noun
