foetal:/ˈfiːtl/ (US) /ˈfiːtəl/ (UK)| adj.|胎儿的，胎的|Relating to a fetus, a developing baby in the womb.|例句：Foetal development is crucial during the first trimester.  胎儿发育在孕早期至关重要。

例句：Doctors monitored the foetal heartbeat. 医生监测了胎儿的心跳。|近义词：embryonic, unborn|反义词：neonatal, postnatal|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *fetalis, from fetus (“offspring, pregnancy, childbirth”).
