dragonflies:/ˈdræɡənˌflaɪz/
|n.|蜻蜓|Fast-flying long-bodied predatory insect with large transparent wings. |Dragonflies hovered over the pond. 蜻蜓在池塘上盘旋。|近义词:darning needle, snake doctor|反义词:None|Noun


Etymology->
From dragon + fly.  The "dragon" part likely refers to their predatory nature, while "fly" refers to their ability to fly.  The term dates back to the late 16th century.
