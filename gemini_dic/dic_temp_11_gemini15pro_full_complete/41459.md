corniest:/ˈkɔːrniɪst/| adj.|最老套的，最陈腐的|Excessively sentimental, trite, or mawkish.  Characterized by predictable and hackneyed humor or emotion.|That joke was the corniest thing I've ever heard. 那个笑话是我听过的最老套的笑话了。|Synonyms:  banal, clichéd, hackneyed, trite, mawkish, saccharine, cheesy|Antonyms: original, fresh, innovative, novel, unconventional|Etymology:  From "corny" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Corny" originally referred to something rustic or unsophisticated, later evolving to mean sentimental and trite, possibly influenced by the perceived sentimentality of rural life as depicted in popular media.
