outweigh:/ˌaʊtˈweɪ/
verb
超过，胜过
to be greater or more important than something else
The benefits of this treatment far outweigh any risks. 这种治疗的好处远远超过任何风险。
outweigh
v.
比...更重要；胜过；超过
The advantages outweigh the disadvantages. 利大于弊。
synonyms: exceed, overbalance, preponderate, override, prevail over, surpass
antonyms: be inferior to, be outweighed by
