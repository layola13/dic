fugitive:/ˈfjuːdʒətɪv/ | n. adj.| 逃犯，逃亡者；短暂的，易逝的 | a person who has escaped from a place or is in hiding, especially to avoid arrest or persecution | The police are searching for the fugitive who escaped from prison. 警方正在搜捕越狱的逃犯。 |  近义词: escapee, runaway, deserter, refugee | 反义词: captive, prisoner | 词性: noun, adjective


fugitive:/ˈfjuːdʒətɪv/ | adj. | 短暂的，易逝的 | lasting for a short time; fleeting |  A fugitive thought crossed her mind. 一个短暂的想法掠过她的脑海。 | 近义词: fleeting, ephemeral, transient, momentary | 反义词: permanent, lasting, enduring | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fugitīvus, from Latin fugitīvus (“fleeing, fugitive”), from fugit (“he/she/it flees”), from the verb fugiō (“I flee, run away, escape”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰug- (“to flee, escape”).
