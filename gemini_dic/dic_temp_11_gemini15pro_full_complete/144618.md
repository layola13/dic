outwiggling:/ˌaʊtˈwɪɡlɪŋ/
verb
扭动胜过; 通过扭动超越
Moving with more vigorous wiggling motions than something else; surpassing in wiggling.
The baby outwiggled his older brother, escaping his grasp.  这个婴儿扭动得比他哥哥更厉害，逃脱了他的控制。
近义词: outmaneuvering, outsquirming
反义词: being outwiggled, being surpassed in wiggling
