disabled: /dɪsˈeɪbld/ | adj. | 残疾的，丧失能力的 |  Unable to perform certain actions or functions, often due to a physical or mental impairment.  Lacking a particular ability or power. |  The accident left him disabled.  事故使他残疾了。|  The website is designed to be accessible to disabled users. 该网站的设计是为了方便残疾用户访问。| synonyms: handicapped, impaired, incapacitated | antonyms: able, able-bodied, capable | adjective

disabled: /dɪsˈeɪbld/ | n. | 残疾人 | A person who has a physical or mental impairment that substantially limits one or more major life activities. |  The government is working to improve access for the disabled. 政府正在努力改善残疾人的出入境条件。|  The building has ramps and elevators for the disabled. 这栋楼有供残疾人使用的坡道和电梯。 | synonyms: handicapped person, person with a disability | antonyms: able-bodied person | noun
