opuntia:/ˌəʊˈpʌntɪə/| n. |仙人掌属植物；尤指仙人球|A genus of cacti, typically with flat, fleshy, spiny pads.  These plants are commonly known as prickly pear or nopales. |例句：The opuntia thrives in arid climates. 仙人掌属植物在干旱气候中茁壮成长。

例句：The fruits of some opuntia species are edible.  一些仙人掌属植物的果实是可食用的。|近义词：cactus, prickly pear, nopal|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
