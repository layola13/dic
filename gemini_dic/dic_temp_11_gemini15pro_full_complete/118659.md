mesymnicum:/mɛsɪmˈnaɪkəm/| n. |中温生物|An organism that thrives in moderately warm temperatures, typically between 20°C and 40°C.|The mesymnicum of the hot spring included various species of algae and bacteria. 温泉的中温生物包括各种藻类和细菌。|Synonyms: mesophile |Antonyms: thermophile, psychrophile|noun
