nibelungs:/ˈniːbəlʊŋz/ | n. | 尼伯龙根 |  The Nibelungs were a legendary race or dynasty of dwarves or giants in Germanic mythology, famous for their hoard of treasure. They feature prominently in the *Nibelungenlied*, a Middle High German epic poem. |  The curse of the Nibelungs' gold brought tragedy to many. 尼伯龙根的黄金宝藏给许多人带来了悲剧。 | synonyms: dwarves, giants | antonyms: gods,  humans | noun
