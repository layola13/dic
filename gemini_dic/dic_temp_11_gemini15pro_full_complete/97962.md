innumerate:/ɪˈnjuːmərət/ | adj. | 无数学知识的；不懂数学的 | Not good at working with numbers; lacking mathematical skills. | Many innumerate adults have difficulty with basic calculations. 许多没有数学知识的成年人对基本的计算有困难。 |  He's completely innumerate and can't even balance his checkbook. 他完全不懂数学，甚至连支票簿都无法结算。 | 近义词: non-numerate, mathematically illiterate | 反义词: numerate, mathematically literate | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From Latin *innumeratus*, from in- "not" + numeratus "counted," past participle of numerare "to count," from numerus "number."
