opinioned:/əˈpɪnjənd/
adjective
固执己见的；武断的
Having strong opinions, especially ones that are inflexible.
He was a very opinioned man, and it was difficult to change his mind. 他是一个非常固执己见的人，很难改变他的想法。
opinionative, dogmatic, assertive, insistent
open-minded, receptive, flexible, amenable
词性:形容词
