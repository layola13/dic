able:/ˈeɪbl/| adj.|能够，有能力的，能干的|Having the necessary power, skill, resources, or opportunity to do something.|She is able to speak three languages. 她能说三种语言。|近义词：capable, competent, skilled|反义词：unable, incapable, incompetent|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *able*, borrowed from Old French *able*, *hable* (“able, apt, suitable”), from Latin *habilis* (“easily handled, apt, fit, suitable, manageable, dexterous, skillful”), from *habēre* (“to have, hold”).
