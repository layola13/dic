defilable:/dɪˈfaɪləbl/
adjective
可遮蔽的，可防御的
able to be protected from enemy fire by an obstacle such as a hill or bank.
The troops found a defilable position behind the ridge. 部队在山脊后面找到了一个可防御的位置。
defensible, protected, shielded
exposed, vulnerable
adjective
