fogged:/fɑːɡd/ | adj. | 模糊的，起雾的 | Covered or obscured with fog; blurred or unclear.  | The windows were fogged up with condensation. 窗户上凝结了雾气，变得模糊不清。 | The camera lens was fogged, ruining the shot. 相机镜头起雾了，毁了这张照片。 | 近义词: misty, hazy, cloudy, blurred, obscured | 反义词: clear, transparent | 词性: 形容词

fogged:/fɑːɡd/ | v. |  (使)起雾；(使)模糊 | Past tense and past participle of "fog".  To become covered or obscured with fog; to make something blurred or unclear. |  My glasses fogged up when I entered the warm room. 我进入温暖的房间时，眼镜起雾了。 |  The steam from the shower fogged the mirror.  淋浴产生的蒸汽使镜子起雾了。 | 近义词: misted, clouded, obscured | 反义词: cleared | 词性: 动词
