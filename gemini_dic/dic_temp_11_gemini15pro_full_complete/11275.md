arctation:/ɑːrkˈteɪʃən/
noun
缩窄，狭窄
a narrowing or constriction of a passage or opening in the body.
例句：Coarctation of the aorta is a congenital heart defect. 主动脉缩窄是一种先天性心脏缺陷。

例句：The doctor diagnosed an arctation of the pulmonary artery. 医生诊断出肺动脉狭窄。
近义词：constriction, narrowing, stenosis
反义词：dilation, widening, expansion
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *arctātiō, from arctātus, past participle of arctāre (“to compress, constrict”), from arctus (“narrow, confined”), past participle of arcēre (“to enclose, confine, hold off”).
