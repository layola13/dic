advertise:/ˈædvərtaɪz/ (US) /ˈædvətaɪz/ (UK)| v. | 登广告；宣传；做广告 | To make something known generally or draw attention to it in order to sell it or encourage people to buy or use it. |  We advertised our products in the local newspaper. 我们在当地报纸上刊登了我们产品的广告。

The company is advertising for a new sales manager. 这家公司正在招聘一名新的销售经理。


近义词: promote, publicize, market | 反义词: conceal, suppress | 词性: verb

advertise:/ˈædvərtaɪz/ (US) /ˈædvətaɪz/ (UK)| n. | 广告;宣传 | The act or business of advertising something. |  There's too much advertising on television these days. 如今电视上的广告太多了。|近义词: publicity, promotion | 反义词: secrecy | 词性: noun


词源: From Late Middle English *advertissen*, from Anglo-French *advertiser*, from Late Latin *advertisare* "to turn the mind to," from Latin *advertere* "to turn toward," from *ad-* "to" + *vertere* "to turn."
