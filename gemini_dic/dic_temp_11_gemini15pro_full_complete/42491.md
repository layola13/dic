counterhypotheses:/ˌkaʊntərhaɪˈpɑːθəsiːz/
noun
反假设，对立假设
A counterhypothesis is a hypothesis that contradicts the original hypothesis.
The researchers tested several counterhypotheses to ensure the validity of their findings. 研究人员测试了几个反假设，以确保他们发现的有效性。
Synonyms: alternative hypothesis, competing hypothesis
Antonyms: original hypothesis, primary hypothesis
词性: 名词
