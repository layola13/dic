combinatorial:/ˌkɒmbɪnəˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. | 组合的，组合学的 | Relating to or involving combinations or the ways in which different things can be combined. |  The number of possible chess games is a combinatorial explosion.  国际象棋可能的棋局数量是一个组合爆炸。| Combinative, permutational |  - | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *combinatorius*, from combinat-, past participle stem of combinare "to combine," from com- "together" + bini "two by two."
