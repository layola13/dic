irrecured:/ˌɪrɪˈkɜːrd/
adjective
未复发的，未再发生的
Not having occurred again; not recurring.
The doctor was pleased to see that the patient's cancer was irrecurred after five years. 医生很高兴地看到病人的癌症五年后没有复发。
After successful surgery, the tumor remained irrecurred.  手术成功后，肿瘤没有复发。
近义词: unrecurred, nonrecurring
反义词: recurrent, recurring
词性: adjective
