bucolic:/bjuːˈkɒlɪk/ /buːˈkɑːlɪk/
adjective
田园的；乡村的；牧歌的
Relating to the pleasant aspects of the countryside and country life.
The painting depicts a bucolic scene of shepherds tending their flocks in a green meadow. 这幅画描绘了牧羊人在绿色的草地上放牧的田园风光。

The bucolic setting of the farmhouse was perfect for a relaxing weekend getaway.  农舍的田园环境非常适合周末度假放松身心。


synonym:rustic, pastoral, idyllic, country
antonym:urban, metropolitan, city, industrial
词性:adjective
