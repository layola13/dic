arches:/ˈɑːrtʃɪz/| n.|拱门，拱形结构，足弓|A curved structure supporting the weight of something above it, or a curved shape in general.  The bridge has beautiful arches. 这座桥有美丽的拱形结构。|近义词：curves, vaults, spans|反义词:beams, supports|noun

arch:/ɑːrtʃ/| n. |拱门，拱形结构|A curved structure supporting the weight of something above it. The arch of the bridge is very impressive. 桥的拱门令人印象深刻。|近义词：curve, vault, span|反义词:beam, support|noun
arch:/ɑːrtʃ/| v. |形成拱形，拱起|To form or bend into a curve. The cat arched its back. 猫拱起了背。|近义词：curve, bend, arc|反义词:straighten|verb
arch:/ɑːrtʃ/| adj. |主要的，调皮的，淘气的|Chief; principal; playfully mischievous or teasing.  He gave her an arch look. 他淘气地看了她一眼。|近义词：mischievous, playful, roguish|反义词:serious, solemn|adjective
