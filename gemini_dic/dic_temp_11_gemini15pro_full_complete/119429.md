micrographic: /ˌmaɪkrəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
微图形的;显微照片的;微写的
Relating to or produced by micrography; extremely small; written in very small characters.
Micrographic analysis revealed tiny cracks in the metal.  微观分析揭示了金属中的微小裂纹。
The micrographic details on the stamp were only visible with a magnifying glass. 邮票上的微小细节只有用放大镜才能看到。
近义词: miniature, microscopic, tiny
反义词: macroscopic, large, huge
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From micro- (small) + -graphic (relating to writing or drawing).  来自micro-（小的）+ -graphic（与书写或绘画相关的）。
