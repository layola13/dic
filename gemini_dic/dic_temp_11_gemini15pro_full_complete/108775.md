leaseless:/ˈliːslɪs/
adjective
无租赁的；无人承租的
Without a lease; not leased or rented.
The leaseless building stood empty for months. 这栋无人承租的大楼空置了好几个月。
Near Antonyms: leased, rented
Antonyms: occupied
