enforcers:/ɪnˈfɔːrsərz/ /ɛnˈfɔːrsərz/| n.|执行者，强制执行人|People who ensure that rules, laws, or agreements are followed, often through force or threats. |The company hired enforcers to collect debts from clients. 公司雇佣了一些执行者向客户收取债务。|  The government sent enforcers to shut down the illegal operation. 政府派执法人员关闭非法经营活动。 |Synonyms: executors, implementers, officers, agents| Antonyms: violators, lawbreakers| noun
