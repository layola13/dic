fortresses:/ˈfɔːtrəsɪz/| n. |堡垒，要塞|A large, fortified building or place, designed to be defended from attack.|The city was ringed with fortresses. 这座城市被堡垒环绕。|近义词：strongholds, castles, fortifications|反义词：vulnerability, weakness|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old French *forteresce*, from *fort* (strong) + *-esse* (suffix forming feminine nouns denoting place). Ultimately from Latin *fortis* (strong).
