distractingly:/dɪˈstræktɪŋli/
adverb
令人分心地
in a way that causes distraction
The music was distractingly loud.  音乐吵得让人分心。
近义词: disturbingly, annoyingly, obtrusively
反义词: soothingly, calmingly, focusingly
词性: adverb
词源: From distract + -ing + -ly. Distract from Latin *distractus, past participle of distrahere (“to draw apart, divide, separate”), from dis- (“apart, asunder”) + trahere (“to draw, pull”).
