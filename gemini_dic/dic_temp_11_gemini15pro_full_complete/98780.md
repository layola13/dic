integrable:/ˈɪntɪɡrəbl/ | adj. | 可积分的，可整合的 | Able to be integrated, especially able to be integrated mathematically.  A function is integrable if the definite integral over its domain has a finite value. | The function f(x) = x^2 is integrable over the interval [0, 1].  函数f(x) = x^2在区间[0, 1]上是可积的。| synonyms: summable, additive | antonyms: non-integrable, indivisible | adjective
