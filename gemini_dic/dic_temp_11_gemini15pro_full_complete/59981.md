edingtonite:/ˈɛdɪŋtəˌnaɪt/
n.
埃丁顿沸石
A rare zeolite mineral, a hydrated barium aluminum silicate.
Edingtonite crystals were discovered in the cavities of the basalt. 在玄武岩的孔洞中发现了埃丁顿沸石晶体。
近义词:zeolite（沸石）

词性:noun
