buttonholing: /ˈbʌtnˌhoʊlɪŋ/ | n. | 扣眼; 强拉别人谈话; |The act of buttonholing someone, or the state of being buttonholed.  强拉别人谈话的行为，或被强拉住谈话的状态 |  He tried to avoid the politician's buttonholing at the reception. 他试图避免在招待会上被政客拉住谈话。 | synonyms: accosting, waylaying, cornering, trapping, grabbing, seizing, detaining, holding captive | antonyms: releasing, freeing, avoiding, ignoring | noun

buttonhole: /ˈbʌtnˌhoʊl/ | vt. |  用扣眼缝合; 强拉别人谈话; | To sew buttonholes in (a garment).  在（衣服）上缝扣眼。/ To detain (someone) in conversation, typically against their will.  强留（某人）谈话，通常违背他们的意愿。|  She buttonholed him as he was leaving the office.  当他离开办公室时，她把他拉住谈话。 | synonyms: accost, waylay, corner, trap, grab, seize, detain | antonyms: release, free, avoid, ignore | verb
