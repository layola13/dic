outfool:/ˌaʊtˈfuːl/
verb
愚弄，胜过
to fool someone more successfully than they fool you
He tried to outfool me, but I was too clever for him. 他试图愚弄我，但我比他更聪明。
outsmart, outwit, trick
dupe, deceive
