nonloyalty:/ˌnɒnˈlɔɪəlti/
n.
不忠诚，背叛
The act of being disloyal; lack of loyalty; unfaithfulness.
His nonloyalty to the company was evident in his constant criticism.他对公司的不忠诚体现在他不断的批评中。
近义词:disloyalty, treachery, infidelity, faithlessness
反义词:loyalty, fidelity, faithfulness
词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + loyalty.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "lack of". "Loyalty" comes from Old French "loialte" meaning "legality, fidelity".
