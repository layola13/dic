nondenominationalisms:/ˌnɑːndɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəlɪzəmz/ | n. |非宗派主义，不分宗派的观点|The rise of nondenominationalisms reflects a growing desire for unity among different Christian groups. 非宗派主义的兴起反映了不同基督教团体之间日益增长的团结愿望。|例句：Many young people are drawn to nondenominationalisms because it focuses on shared beliefs rather than doctrinal differences. 许多年轻人被非宗派主义所吸引，因为它专注于共同的信仰，而不是教义上的差异。

例句：The growth of nondenominational churches is a significant trend in modern Christianity. 非宗派教会的增长是现代基督教的一个重要趋势。|近义词：interdenominationalism, ecumenism|反义词: sectarianism, denominationalism |词性: 名词

词源:  "nondenominational" + "-ism"
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "denominational" (adjective referring to a particular religious denomination) + "-ism" (suffix forming nouns that refer to doctrines, beliefs, or practices).  Ultimately derived from Latin "denominare" (to name).
