boatload:/ˈboʊtloʊd/| n.|大量，许多|A large number or quantity of something.|例句：They made a boatload of money. 他们赚了一大笔钱。|例句：She brought a boatload of snacks to the party. 她带了一大堆零食来参加聚会。|近义词：a lot, tons, heaps, a mountain, a plethora|反义词:a little, a few|词性:名词

Etymology:
From "boat" + "load," literally referring to the amount of cargo a boat can carry.  First recorded use in the early 20th century.
