concise:/kənˈsaɪs/| adj.|简明的，简洁的|Giving a lot of information clearly and in a few words; brief but comprehensive.|The chairman's summary of the issues was concise and to the point. 主席对问题的总结简明扼要。|近义词：brief, succinct, terse, compact|反义词：verbose, prolix, lengthy, rambling|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *concisus*, past participle of *concidere* "to cut down, cut short," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *caedere* "to cut"
