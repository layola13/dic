musically:/ˈmjuːzɪkli/ | adv. | 音乐上地；悦耳地；爱好音乐地 | In a musical manner; with regard to music; from a musical point of view. | She is musically gifted. 她很有音乐天赋。|  Synonyms: harmoniously, melodically, rhythmically | Antonyms: unmusically, discordantly | adverb

musically:/ˈmjuːzɪkli/ | adj. | 音乐的；爱好音乐的 (obsolete) | Relating to or consisting of music. |  He comes from a musically inclined family. 他来自一个爱好音乐的家庭。(This usage is now considered obsolete and "musical" is preferred.) | Synonyms: musical (preferred), harmonious | Antonyms: unmusical, discordant | adjective
