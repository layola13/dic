instilled:/ɪnˈstɪld/
verb
灌输，注入
gradually establish (an attitude, feeling, or idea) in someone's mind.
例句：The coach instilled a sense of discipline in his players. 教练向他的球员灌输了纪律意识。

例句：Her parents instilled in her a love of reading. 她的父母在她心中灌输了对阅读的热爱。

近义词：implant, infuse, inculcate, introduce, inspire
反义词：eradicate, remove, uproot
词性：verb
