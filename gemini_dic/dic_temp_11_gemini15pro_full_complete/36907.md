colorization:/ˌkʌləraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |着色，彩色化|The process of adding color to a black and white image or film.|例句：The colorization of the old movie brought it to a new audience. 这部老电影的彩色化使它吸引了新的观众。|例句：Digital colorization techniques have greatly improved over the years. 数字着色技术近年来有了很大的进步。|近义词：coloring, tinting|反义词：desaturation, bleaching|词性：名词

colorization:/ˌkʌləraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |彩色化，着色|The act or process of adding color to something, typically a black and white photograph or film.|例句：The colorization of classic films has been a controversial practice.  经典电影的彩色化一直是一个有争议的做法。|例句：He specialized in the colorization of old photographs. 他专门从事老照片的着色工作。|近义词：tinting, coloring|反义词：desaturation, bleaching|词性：名词
