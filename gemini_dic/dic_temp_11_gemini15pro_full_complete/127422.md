neater:/ˈniːtər/ (US), /ˈniːtə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更整洁的，更整齐的|comparative form of neat: more tidy and in order|Her handwriting is much neater than mine. 她的字迹比我的整洁得多。|The room looks neater after being tidied up.  房间整理后看起来更整洁了。|近义词：tidier, cleaner, more orderly|反义词：messier, dirtier, more untidy|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *netere, comparative of net (“neat”), from Old English nēat (“cattle, beast; property, possessions; useful animal”), from Proto-Germanic *nautą (“useful animal, cattle”), from Proto-Indo-European *newt- (“to be useful, to be of use, to make use of”).
