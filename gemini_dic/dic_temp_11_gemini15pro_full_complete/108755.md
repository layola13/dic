learnednesses:/ˈlɜːrnɪdnɪsɪz/
noun
博学；学识；学问
The state of being learned or erudite.  Extensive knowledge acquired through study.
His learnednesses in various fields impressed everyone. 他在各个领域的学识给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
erudition, scholarship, knowledge
