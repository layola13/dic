displeasance:/dɪsˈplezəns/| n.|不悦，恼怒，不满|A feeling of annoyance or displeasure.|His displeasance at the interruption was evident. 他对被打扰感到很不高兴。|近义词：displeasure, annoyance, irritation, dissatisfaction|反义词：pleasure, satisfaction, delight|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French desplaisance, from desplaisant (“displeasing”), present participle of desplaire (“to displease”), from Latin displicēre (“to displease”), from dis- (“not”) + placēre (“to please”).
