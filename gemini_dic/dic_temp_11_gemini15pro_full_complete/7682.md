anoetic:/ˌeɪnoʊˈɛtɪk/
adjective
无意识的；非认知的；不涉及思维的
Lacking awareness or understanding; not involving or related to mental processes.
She entered an anoetic state during deep meditation.  她在深度冥想中进入了一种无意识状态。
The patient's anoetic response suggested a neurological issue. 病人无意识的反应表明存在神经系统问题。
Near Synonyms: unconscious, insensate, mindless, unthinking
Antonyms: conscious, aware, cognitive, sentient
Word Origin:  From Greek *ἀ- (a-, "not") + *νοητικός (noētikos, "intellectual")
词性: adjective
