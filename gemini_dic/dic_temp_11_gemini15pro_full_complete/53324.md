disaccept:/ˌdɪsəkˈsept/
verb
拒绝接受；不承认
to refuse to accept or acknowledge
The court disaccepted the evidence. 法庭不接受该证据。
近义词:reject, refuse, decline
反义词:accept, approve, acknowledge
词性:v.

词源:
dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + accept (from Latin acceptare, "to take or receive willingly")
