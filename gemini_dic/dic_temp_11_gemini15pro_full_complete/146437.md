overfoully:/oʊvərˈfaʊlli/ | adv. | 过度污秽地；极度肮脏地 |  Excessively, extremely foully; in a very dirty or polluted manner. 词源:  "Over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "foul" (adjective meaning offensive to the senses, especially through having a disgusting smell or taste or being dirty) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
