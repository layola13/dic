countertripping:/ˈkaʊntərˌtrɪpɪŋ/
n.
反制，反击，对抗性旅行
The act of taking a trip or journey in response to another trip or journey, often with the intention of gaining an advantage or undermining the purpose of the original trip.
例句：His countertripping to Europe was a direct response to his competitor's expansion into the market. 他去欧洲的反击之旅是对竞争对手进军该市场的直接回应。

例句：The government engaged in countertripping to spread their own propaganda and counter the narrative being presented by the opposition. 政府进行了对抗性旅行，传播他们自己的宣传，并反击反对派提出的说法。
近义词：countermeasure, countermove, retaliation
反义词: cooperation, collaboration
词性:名词
