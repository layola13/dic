alter:/ˈɔːltər/ (US) /ˈɒltə(r)/ (UK)| v. |改变，更改|To change something, usually slightly.|She had to alter her dress because it was too big. 她不得不修改她的连衣裙，因为它太大了。| The tailor altered the trousers to make them shorter. 裁缝修改了裤子，使它们更短。 |modify, change, adjust|maintain, preserve|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *alterer*, from Latin *alterāre* ("to change, to make other"), from *alter* ("other").
