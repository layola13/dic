lerot:/ləˈroʊ/ | n. | 勒罗特，园睡鼠 | A small, nocturnal rodent native to Africa and Europe, also known as the garden dormouse. | The lerot is a cute, small animal with big eyes. 勒罗特是一种可爱的小动物，有着大大的眼睛。| The lerot scurried up the tree. 勒罗特 scurried up the tree. | 近义词: garden dormouse, dormouse | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *lerot*, ultimately from Latin *glīs*, *glīris* "dormouse."
