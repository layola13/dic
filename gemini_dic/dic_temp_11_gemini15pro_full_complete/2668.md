adorningly:/əˈdɔːrnɪŋli/
adverb
装饰地；装点地
in a way that decorates something and makes it look more attractive
The Christmas tree was adorned adorningly with lights and baubles. 圣诞树上装饰着彩灯和饰品，非常漂亮。
Synonyms: decoratively, ornamentally, embellishingly
Antonyms: plainly, simply, austerely
词性: 副词
