maylock: /ˈmeɪlɒk/ | n. |五月花; 五月盛开的花; |A flower that blooms in May.  Historically, also refers to the ship "Mayflower".| The maylock bloomed brightly in the garden. 五月花在花园里盛开得很鲜艳。| The Maylock brought the Pilgrims to America. 五月花号把清教徒带到了美洲。| 近义词: may blossom, spring flower | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider autumn flower) | 词性: noun
