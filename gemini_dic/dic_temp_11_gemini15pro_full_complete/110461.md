lilied:/ˈlɪlɪd/| adj.|百合花的，像百合的，纯洁的|Adorned or covered with lilies; resembling a lily, especially in purity or whiteness.|Her lilied hands gracefully held the bouquet. 她那像百合一样的手优雅地拿着花束。|近义词：pure, white, immaculate, unblemished|反义词：tarnished, sullied|词性:形容词

词源: From lily + -ed.  The suffix "-ed" is used to form adjectives meaning "having" or "characterized by" something.  Thus, "lilied" means "having lilies" or "characterized by lilies".
