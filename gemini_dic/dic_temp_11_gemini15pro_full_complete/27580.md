canard:/kəˈnɑːrd/ /kəˈnɑːrd/| n.|谣言，误传|A false or unfounded rumor or story, often deliberately circulated.|The newspaper published a canard about the mayor's personal life. 这家报纸刊登了一篇关于市长私生活的谣传。|近义词：rumor, fabrication, falsehood, lie, myth|反义词：truth, fact|noun

canard:/kəˈnɑːrd/ /kəˈnɑːrd/| n.|（飞机的）鸭式布局|An aeronautical arrangement whereby a small foreplane or forewing is placed forward of the main wing.|The Wright brothers' first successful airplane used a canard design.  莱特兄弟的第一架成功飞行的飞机采用了鸭式布局。|近义词：foreplane, forward wing|反义词：N/A|noun
