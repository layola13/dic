justiciable:/ˈdʒʌstɪʃəbl/ |adj.|可司法审查的；可受法院审理的|Suitable to be tried or settled in a court of law; capable of being subject to judicial review.|The question of whether the president's actions were justiciable was debated in the courts. 总统的行为是否可受司法审查的问题在法庭上进行了辩论。|reviewable, actionable|nonjusticiable|adjective
