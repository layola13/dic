mellowy:/ˈmeləʊi/ (US) /ˈmɛləʊi/ (UK)| adj.|柔和的，圆润的，成熟的|Having a smooth, rich, and pleasant taste or quality, often associated with ripeness or maturity; relaxed and easygoing.|The wine had a mellowy flavor, perfect for a quiet evening. 这款酒口感醇厚，非常适合在一个安静的夜晚享用.|近义词：soft, smooth, rich, mellow, gentle, relaxed|反义词：harsh, sharp, bitter, unripe, tense|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From mellow + -y.  "Mellow" itself comes from Old English *mearuw "soft, tender," from Proto-Germanic *malwaz "soft."
