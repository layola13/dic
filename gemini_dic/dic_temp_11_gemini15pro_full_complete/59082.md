dyable:/ˈdaɪəbl/ | adj. | 可染色的 | Capable of being dyed.  This means a material can absorb and hold color from a dye. | This fabric is very dyeable and takes color beautifully.  这种布料很容易染色，上色很漂亮。| The yarn is dyeable with natural dyes.  这种纱线可以用天然染料染色。 | colorable, stainable | non-dyable, undyeable | adjective
