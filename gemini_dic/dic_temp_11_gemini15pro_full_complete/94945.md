in-range:/ɪnˈreɪndʒ/ | adj. | 在范围内，在射程内 | Within the acceptable or specified limits; within the maximum effective distance of a weapon. | The target was in-range, so the soldier fired. 目标在射程内，所以士兵开火了。|  The temperature is in-range, so the experiment can proceed. 温度在范围内，所以实验可以继续进行。 | 近义词: within range, inside the limits, acceptable | 反义词: out of range, outside the limits, unacceptable | 词性: 形容词
