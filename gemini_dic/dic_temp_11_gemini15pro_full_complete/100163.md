interreflect:/ˌɪntərɪˈflekt/ | v. |互相反射；互相影响| To reflect each other; to have a mutual influence or effect.| The two mirrors interreflected, creating an endless tunnel of light. 两面镜子互相反射，创造出一条无尽的光隧道。 The cultures of the two countries interreflected each other, leading to a rich blend of traditions.  这两个国家的文化互相影响，形成了丰富的传统融合。|近义词: interact, influence, affect |反义词: isolate |词性: 动词

词源:
From inter- + reflect.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally," and "reflect" comes from the Latin "reflectere," meaning "to bend back."
