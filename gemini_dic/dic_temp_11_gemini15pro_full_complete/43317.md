cracker:/ˈkrækər/ (US), /ˈkrækə/ (UK)| n.|薄脆饼干; 爆竹; 骇客|A thin, crisp biscuit, often eaten with cheese.  A small firework that explodes with a sharp bang. A person who breaks into computer systems. |例句：I like to eat cheese and crackers. 我喜欢吃奶酪和饼干。

例句：The children were excited to light the firecrackers. 孩子们很兴奋要点燃爆竹。

例句：The cracker bypassed the security system and stole sensitive data.  骇客绕过了安全系统并窃取了敏感数据。|近义词：biscuit, wafer (for food); firecracker, banger (for firework); hacker (for person)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
