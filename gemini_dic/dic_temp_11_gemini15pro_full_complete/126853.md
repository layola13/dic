napkins:/ˈnæpkɪnz/
n.
餐巾纸；餐巾
A square piece of cloth or paper used at a meal to wipe the fingers or lips; a serviette.
Could you pass me some napkins, please?  请递给我一些餐巾纸，好吗？
近义词：serviettes, tissues
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性：名词
