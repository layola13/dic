activist:/ˈæktɪvɪst/| n.|活动家，积极分子|A person who campaigns to bring about political or social change.|例句：The activist fought for human rights.  这位活动家为人权而战。

例句：She is a well-known environmental activist. 她是一位著名的环保活动家。|近义词：campaigner, advocate, reformer, proponent|反义词：opponent, antagonist|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *activiste*, from Latin *activus* ("active").
