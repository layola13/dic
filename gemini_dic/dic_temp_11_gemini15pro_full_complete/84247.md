half-sister:/ˈhæfˌsɪstər/ | n. |同父异母或同母异父的姐妹|A sister who shares one parent with another person. | My father remarried and I now have a half-sister. 我父亲再婚了，现在我有一个同父异母的妹妹。

My half-sister and I share the same mother but have different fathers. 我和我的同母异父的姐妹有相同的母亲，但父亲不同。


|近义词: stepsister (if the shared parent remarried)|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Consider "full sister")|词性:noun
