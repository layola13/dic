encephalitis-lethargica:/ɛnˌsefəˈlaɪtɪs lɛˈθɑːrdʒɪkə/| n. |昏睡性脑炎|An illness, also known as "sleeping sickness," characterized by inflammation of the brain, often leading to prolonged periods of sleep or lethargy.|The 1918 flu pandemic was followed by an outbreak of encephalitis-lethargica. 1918年的流感大流行之后爆发了昏睡性脑炎。|Synonym: sleeping sickness|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider "wakefulness," "alertness")|noun
