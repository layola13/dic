bricklayers:/ˈbrɪkˌleɪərz/
n.
砌砖工，砖瓦匠
A person whose job is to build walls, houses, and other structures with bricks.
The bricklayers are working hard to finish the wall before the rain. 砌砖工正在努力工作，争取在下雨前完成这堵墙。
The new housing development created jobs for many bricklayers. 新的住宅开发项目为许多砌砖工创造了就业机会。

近义词:mason, brickmason
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性:名词
