infectiousness:/ɪnˈfekʃəsnəs/| n. |传染性，感染力|The quality of being infectious; the ease with which a disease can be spread.|The infectiousness of the new virus strain is alarming.  这种新病毒株的传染性令人担忧。|近义词：contagiousness, transmissibility|反义词：resistance, immunity|词性:名词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from infectious + -ness.
