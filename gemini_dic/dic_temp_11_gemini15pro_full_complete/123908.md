more's:/mɔːrz/ |n. |更多的东西; 更多的人;  (尤指数量) 更多;  |Something that belongs to More, usually referring to possessions, ideas, or followers. |Example: More's followers were persecuted for their beliefs. 摩尔的追随者因其信仰而受到迫害。 |Synonym: belongings, possessions, effects (when referring to possessions);  ideas, theories, philosophies (when referring to ideas);  supporters, adherents, disciples (when referring to followers) |Antonym: less, fewer |Possessive Noun
