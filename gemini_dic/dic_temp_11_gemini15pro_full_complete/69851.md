ferritic:/fəˈrɪtɪk/
adjective
铁素体的；铁氧体磁性的
Relating to or containing ferrite, a form of pure iron with a body-centered cubic crystal structure, typically occurring in low-carbon steel.  It is magnetic below the Curie point but not above.
The microstructure of the steel is ferritic.  这种钢的显微组织是铁素体的。
Synonyms:  ferric, ferrous (though these relate more to iron chemistry)
Antonyms:  austenitic (a different crystalline structure of steel)
词性: adjective
