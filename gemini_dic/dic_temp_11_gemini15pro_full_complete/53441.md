disappointment's:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntmənts/ | n. |失望，沮丧|The feeling of sadness or displeasure caused by the non-fulfillment of one's hopes or expectations.  It is the possessive form of "disappointment," indicating that something belongs to or is associated with the disappointment.| The disappointment's impact on her was profound.  这种失望对她的影响很深。|Synonyms: letdown, frustration, chagrin|Antonyms: satisfaction, contentment, joy|Noun, Possessive
