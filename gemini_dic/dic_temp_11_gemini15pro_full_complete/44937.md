cryptographic:/ˌkrɪptəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|密码的，加密的|Relating to or using cryptography, the art of writing or solving codes.|例句：Cryptographic techniques are used to protect sensitive data.  加密技术用于保护敏感数据。

例句：The message was encrypted using a cryptographic algorithm. 这条消息是用一种加密算法加密的。|近义词：encrypted, coded, ciphered|反义词：unencrypted, plain, clear|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From crypto- + -graphic.  Crypto- comes from the Greek kryptos, meaning "hidden" or "secret."  -graphic comes from the Greek graphein, meaning "to write."
