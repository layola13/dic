interchanger:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒər/| n. | 交换机；换乘站；立交桥 |A device or location for exchanging things, especially a junction of highways where traffic can enter or exit.| The highway interchanger was congested with traffic.  高速公路立交桥交通拥挤。| synonyms: junction, intersection, interchange, exchange | antonyms: terminus, dead end | noun

interchangers:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒərz/| n. | 交换机；换乘站；立交桥（复数） | Plural form of "interchanger."  |  Several interchangers along the highway were closed due to construction. 沿高速公路的几个立交桥因施工而关闭。| synonyms: junctions, intersections, interchanges, exchanges | antonyms: termini, dead ends | noun
