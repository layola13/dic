geminal:/ˈdʒɛmɪnəl/| adj.|孪生的；成对的；同位的|Identical atoms or groups attached to the same atom in a molecule.|The geminal protons in the methylene group have slightly different chemical shifts.|近义词：twin, paired, identical|反义词:single, separate|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *geminus* "twin, double," related to *gignere* "to beget."
