monopodium:/mɒnəˈpəʊdiəm/ | n. |单轴分枝; 主茎;|A stem continuing to grow as the main axis of a plant, as in a pine tree, giving rise to lateral branches.  A branching system in which the main stem continues to grow indefinitely at the tip and gives off lateral branches. |The coconut palm exhibits a typical monopodium. 椰子树展现了典型的单轴分枝。| The monopodial growth habit results in a tall, straight trunk. 单轴生长习性形成了高大挺拔的树干。| synonym:  main stem, central axis | antonym: sympodium | noun
