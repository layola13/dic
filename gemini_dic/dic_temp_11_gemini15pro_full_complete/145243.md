overblamed:/ˌoʊvərˈbleɪmd/
adjective
过度责备的
Having been blamed excessively or unfairly.
The employee felt overblamed for the project's failure, as several factors outside his control contributed to the problems.  这个员工觉得对项目失败负有过多的责任，因为一些他无法控制的因素导致了这些问题。
The overblamed child withdrew into himself.  这个被过度责备的孩子变得沉默寡言。
Synonym: overcriticized, unfairly blamed
Antonym: underblamed, excused, exonerated
词性: 形容词
