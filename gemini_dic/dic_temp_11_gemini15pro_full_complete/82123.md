groined:/ɡrɔɪnd/ | adj. | 拱形的，交叉拱形的，腹股沟的 | Having intersecting vaults or arches, typically in a ceiling or roof. Also refers to the groin area of the body. | The church's ceiling was beautifully groined. 教堂的天花板拱形结构很漂亮。 The athlete suffered a groined muscle injury.  这个运动员腹股沟肌肉受伤了。 |  arched, vaulted | flat | adjective

groin:/ɡrɔɪn/ | n. | 腹股沟；交叉拱 | The area between the abdomen and the thigh; the curved line formed by the intersection of two vaults. | He pulled a muscle in his groin. 他腹股沟的肌肉拉伤了。  The groin of the vault was decorated with intricate carvings.  拱顶的交叉拱装饰着复杂的雕刻。| crotch, intersection |  - | noun

groin:/ɡrɔɪn/ | vt. |  建成拱顶；使成穹棱 | To build or construct with groins or intersecting vaults. | They groined the ceiling of the cathedral.  他们把大教堂的天花板建成了拱顶。 The architects groined the roof using traditional techniques. 建筑师们用传统技术建造了拱形屋顶。| arch, vault | - | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *groigne, from Latin inguen "groin." The architectural sense developed from the resemblance of intersecting vaults to the human groin.
