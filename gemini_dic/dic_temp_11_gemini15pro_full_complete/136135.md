nonreflector:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈflek.tər/ | n. | 非反射体；不反射器 |  An object or surface that does not reflect light, heat, or sound. |  The stealth coating makes the aircraft a nonreflector to radar.  这架飞机的隐形涂层使其成为雷达的非反射体。 | synonyms: non-reflective material, absorber | antonyms: reflector, mirror | noun

nonreflective:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈflek.tɪv/ | adj. | 不反射的；非反射性的 | Not reflecting light, heat, or sound. |  The nonreflective surface of the material absorbs light.  这种材料的不反射表面吸收光线。 | synonyms: non-reflecting, matte, dull | antonyms: reflective, shiny, glossy | adjective
