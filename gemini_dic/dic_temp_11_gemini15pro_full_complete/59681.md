echoers:/ˈekəʊərz (UK), ˈɛkoʊərz (US)| n. |回声者；应声虫；附和者|People who repeat or imitate the words or actions of others.  Those who readily accept and repeat the opinions of others without independent thought.|The politician's speech was met with cheers from his echoers.  这位政客的演讲得到他的应声虫们的欢呼。|synonyms: imitators, followers, parrots, yes-men|antonyms: leaders, innovators, dissenters|noun
