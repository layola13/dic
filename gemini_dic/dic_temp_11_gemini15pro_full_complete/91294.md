hydrolysis:/haɪˈdrɒlɪsɪs/ (US) /haɪˈdrɑːləsɪs/ (UK)| n.|水解(作用)|The chemical breakdown of a compound due to reaction with water.|The hydrolysis of starch produces glucose. 淀粉的水解产生葡萄糖。|近义词：decomposition, degradation|反义词：condensation, dehydration|noun
