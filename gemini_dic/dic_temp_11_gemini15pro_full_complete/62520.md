enduement:/ɪnˈdjuːmənt/
n.
赋予，授予，加持
the act of endowing someone with a quality, ability, or right, often spiritual or supernatural in nature.
He believed he had received an enduement of power from the Holy Spirit. 他相信他已领受了圣灵的能力。
investment, bestowal, empowerment
deprivation, divestment
noun
