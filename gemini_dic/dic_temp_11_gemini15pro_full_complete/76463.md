gaited:/ˈɡeɪtɪd/| adj.|步态的，有特定步态的|Having or exhibiting a particular gait or manner of walking or moving.|The horse show featured several different classes for gaited horses.  马展上有几个不同的步态马类别。|The trainer specialized in gaited horses, known for their smooth, four-beat gaits.  这位训练员专门训练步态马，以其平稳的四拍步态而闻名。|近义词：rhythmic, coordinated|反义词:ungainly, awkward|词性:形容词

词源: From "gait" + "-ed."  "Gait" comes from Middle English "gate," from Old Norse "gata" (road, way).
