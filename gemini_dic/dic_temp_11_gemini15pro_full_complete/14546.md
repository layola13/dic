aversely:/əˈvɜːrsli/
adverb
不利地，负面地
in a way that shows unwillingness or opposition
High taxes aversely affect business investment. 高税收对商业投资产生不利影响。
Synonyms: negatively, unfavorably, badly, poorly
Antonyms: favorably, positively, well, beneficially
词性: 副词
