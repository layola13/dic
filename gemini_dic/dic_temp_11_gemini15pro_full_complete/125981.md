musketeer:/ˌmʌskəˈtɪr/| n. |火枪手；步兵|A soldier armed with a musket. |例句：The musketeers were renowned for their bravery. 火枪手以他们的勇敢而闻名。

例句：D'Artagnan joined the musketeers and became one of the king's most trusted guards. 达达尼昂加入了火枪手，成为国王最信任的卫队之一。|近义词：soldier, guardsman, rifleman|反义词:civilian|词性:noun
