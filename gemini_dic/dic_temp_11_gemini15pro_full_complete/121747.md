misprovoke: /ˌmɪsprəˈvoʊk/
verb
错误地激怒；无故挑衅
to provoke wrongly or without due cause.
His careless words misprovoked the already angry crowd. 他的粗心大意的话激怒了已经很愤怒的人群。
近义词:  provoke, irritate, annoy, aggravate
反义词: placate, soothe, calm, appease
词性: verb


misprovoke: /ˌmɪsprəˈvoʊk/
词源: mis- (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + provoke (from Old French provoquer, from Latin provocare "to call forth, challenge")
