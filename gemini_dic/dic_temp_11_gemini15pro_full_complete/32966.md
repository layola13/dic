chordata:/ˈkɔːrdeɪtə/ (US) /kɔːˈdeɪtə/ (UK)| n. |脊索动物门|Animals belonging to the phylum Chordata, possessing a notochord, a dorsal nerve cord, pharyngeal slits, and a post-anal tail at some point during their development.|例句：Humans, fish, and birds are all members of the chordata phylum. 人类、鱼类和鸟类都属于脊索动物门。

例句：The chordata phylum is incredibly diverse, encompassing a wide range of species. 脊索动物门极其多样化，包含了广泛的物种。|近义词：vertebrates (脊椎动物 - a subphylum within Chordata)|反义词: invertebrate (无脊椎动物)|词性: Noun
