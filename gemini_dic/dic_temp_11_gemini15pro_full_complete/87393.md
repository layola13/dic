herr:/hɛr/ (IPA may vary depending on dialect)| n.|先生; (对德国、奥地利、瑞士等德语国家男性的尊称)|A title of respect or form of address used for a man in German-speaking countries, equivalent to Mr. in English.|例句：Herr Schmidt is a renowned scientist. 施密特先生是一位著名的科学家。

例句：Please address him as Herr Professor Doktor Müller. 请称呼他为穆勒教授博士先生。

近义词：Mr. (English), Monsieur (French), Señor (Spanish)|反义词：Frau (German, for a married or widowed woman), Fräulein (German, for an unmarried woman)|词性：noun
