dramatises:/ˈdræmətaɪzɪz/
verb
戏剧化；使…戏剧化；将…改编成戏剧
Presents something in a theatrical or exaggerated way.
The movie dramatises the events of the war.  这部电影将战争事件戏剧化了。
dramatize, overstate, exaggerate, theatricalize
understate, downplay
v.
