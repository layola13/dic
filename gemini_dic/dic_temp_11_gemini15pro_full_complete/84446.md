halock:/ˈheɪlɒk/ | n. | 圣光; 光环 (旧) |A circle of light or glory surrounding the head or body of a holy person or a sacred object, especially in art.  It can also refer to a glorious or blessed state.  The word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. |The painting depicted a saint with a golden halock around his head. 这幅画描绘了一位圣人，他的头部周围环绕着金色的光环。| synonym: halo, aureole, nimbus | antonym: darkness, shadow | noun
