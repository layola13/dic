empearl:/ɪmˈpɜːrl/ /ɛmˈpɝl/| vt.|使成珍珠；用珍珠装饰；使光彩夺目|To adorn with or as if with pearls; to make something look like a pearl.  It can also mean to give something a beautiful, lustrous quality.|The morning dew empearled the spiderwebs in the garden.  晨露使花园里的蜘蛛网像珍珠一样闪闪发光。|Synonyms: adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament, bejewel|Antonyms: strip, denude, mar, blemish|verb
