monogeny:/məˈnɒdʒɪni/ | n. | 单源论，单性生殖 | The theory that all organisms are descended from a single common ancestor.  All life forms originated from a single source. |  The theory of monogeny contrasts with polygeny. 单源论与多源论相反。 |  Monogeny is a widely accepted principle in evolutionary biology. 单源论是进化生物学中广泛接受的原则。 |  monogenesis | polygeny | noun
