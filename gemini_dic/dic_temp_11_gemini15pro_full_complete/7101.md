anesthetizers:/əˈnɛsθəˌtaɪzərz/| n.|麻醉剂；麻醉师|Substances or people who administer anesthetics to cause a loss of sensation, especially during surgery.|The anesthetizers ensured the patient felt no pain during the procedure. 麻醉师确保病人在手术过程中没有感到疼痛。|The new anesthetizers are much safer than the older ones. 新的麻醉剂比旧的更安全。|Synonyms: narcotics, anesthetics, analgesics|Antonyms: stimulants|noun

anesthetizer:/əˈnɛsθəˌtaɪzər/| n.|麻醉师|A person who administers anesthetics.|The anesthetizer monitored the patient's vital signs throughout the surgery. 麻醉师在整个手术过程中监测病人的生命体征。|The skilled anesthetizer ensured a smooth and painless operation.  熟练的麻醉师确保了手术顺利无痛。|Synonyms: anesthetist, anesthesiologist|Antonyms: None|noun
