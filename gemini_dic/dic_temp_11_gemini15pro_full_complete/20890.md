blazonment:/ˈbleɪzənmənt/
n.
纹章的描绘；公开宣扬；炫耀
a formal description of a coat of arms
The blazonment of the royal arms was intricate and detailed. 皇家纹章的描绘错综复杂，非常详细。
The blazonment of his wealth embarrassed his modest friends.  他炫耀自己的财富，让他的朋友们很尴尬。
publication; proclamation; display
synonym: description, depiction, portrayal, announcement, proclamation, display, boast
antonym: concealment, suppression, hiding
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *blasonner (Modern French blasonner), from blason (“coat of arms”).  Ultimately from Frankish *blas, from Proto-Germanic *blēsaną (“to blow, blast”).  The original meaning was “to describe a coat of arms according to heraldic rules.”
