latreutical: /ˌlætˈruːtɪkəl/
adjective
药食同源的；具有药用和营养价值的
Relating to substances that are both food and medicine, having both nutritional and medicinal value.
Latreutical foods are believed to offer both health benefits and nutritional value.  药食同源的食物被认为既有益健康又有营养价值。
Consuming latreutical herbs can boost the immune system.  食用药食同源的草药可以增强免疫系统。
近义词:  functional food, nutraceutical
反义词:  junk food
词性:  adjective

词源:  A blend of "latria" (worship, service) and "pharmaceutical" (relating to drugs).  由"latria"（崇拜，服务）和"pharmaceutical"（与药物有关的）混合而成。
