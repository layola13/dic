condones:/kənˈdoʊnz/| v. |宽恕，原谅，默许|To accept or allow (behavior that is considered morally wrong or offensive) to continue.|例句：The college cannot condone any behavior that involves illicit drugs.  这所大学不能容忍任何涉及非法毒品的行径。|例句：She couldn't condone his actions, even though she understood his reasons. 即使她理解他的理由，她也无法原谅他的行为。|近义词：excuse, pardon, overlook, forgive, tolerate|反义词：condemn, denounce, oppose|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *condonare*, from Latin *com-* (together) + *donare* (to give, present, grant). Originally meaning "to give jointly," then "to remit (a debt), pardon (an offense)."
