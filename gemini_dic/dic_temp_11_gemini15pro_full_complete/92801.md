icon's:/ˈaɪkɒnz/ (UK) /ˈaɪkɑːnz/ (US)| n.|图标; 象征; 偶像; 圣像|A small picture or symbol on a computer screen that represents a program, file, or option.  A person or thing regarded as a representative symbol of something. A painting of Jesus Christ or another holy figure, typically in a traditional style on wood.|

例句：
Click on the printer icon to print the document.
点击打印机图标打印文档。

Her face is familiar from magazine icons.
她的脸庞在杂志的偶像照片中很熟悉。

近义词：symbol, emblem, image, idol
反义词： (No direct antonym, depends on the specific meaning.  For example, if 'icon' means 'idol', an antonym could be 'nobody')
词性: 名词
