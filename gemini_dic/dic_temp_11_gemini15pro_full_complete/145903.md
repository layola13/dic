overdiffusing:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈfjuːzɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度扩散，过度传播 | Spreading or disseminating something too widely or excessively.  It often implies a negative consequence, such as diluting the message or reaching an unintended audience. | The company's marketing campaign was overdiffusing its resources across too many channels, resulting in a weak impact. 该公司的营销活动过度分散了资源，导致影响力薄弱。


The charity overdiffused its message by targeting too broad an audience, losing its core message.  这个慈善机构的信息传播过于广泛，失去了其核心信息。 | 近义词：overspreading, oversaturating | 反义词：concentrating, focusing | 词性: adjective
