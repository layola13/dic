falcon:/ˈfɔːlkən/ (US) /ˈfɔːlkən/ (UK)| n. |猎鹰；隼|A bird of prey with long pointed wings and a short hooked beak, known for its swift flight and keen eyesight.|The falcon soared high above the mountains. 猎鹰在群山上空翱翔。|近义词：hawk, raptor|反义词:prey|noun
