comber:/ˈkoʊmər/ (US), /ˈkəʊmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|梳理者，清除者，浪花|A person or thing that combs, clears, or gathers, especially waves.|The comber swept the beach clean after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海浪把海滩冲刷干净了。|近义词：cleaner, collector, scavenger|反义词: polluter, scatterer|noun
