impinger:/ɪmˈpɪndʒər/| n.|冲击器，撞击器|A device that directs a gas or liquid stream against a surface for various purposes, such as collecting particles or cooling.|The impinger collected the air pollutants for analysis.  冲击器收集空气污染物用于分析。|Synonym: impactor, collider|Antonym: diffuser, spreader|noun

impinger:/ɪmˈpɪndʒər/| n.|冲击物，撞击物|Something that impinges or strikes against something else.|The hail was an impinger on the car's windshield. 冰雹撞击在汽车的挡风玻璃上。|Synonym: striker, hitter|Antonym: buffer, cushion|noun
