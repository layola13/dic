dual-carriageway:/ˌduːəl ˈkærɪdʒweɪ/ | n. | 双车道公路；双行道 | A road which has a central reservation to separate the two opposing streams of traffic. |  We were stuck in traffic on the dual carriageway for hours. 我们在双车道公路上堵了好几个小时。| synonym: divided highway, expressway, freeway | antonym: single-carriageway, one-way street | noun

dual-carriageway:/ˌduːəl ˈkærɪdʒweɪ/ | n. | 双车道 |  A major road with two lanes of traffic in each direction and a dividing strip down the middle. | The accident occurred on the dual carriageway just outside of town. 事故发生在城外的双车道上。| synonym: dual highway | antonym: single-lane road | noun
