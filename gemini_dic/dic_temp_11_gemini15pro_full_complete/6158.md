amidst:/əˈmɪdst/| prep.|在…之中；被…包围；在…过程中|surrounded by; in the middle of|例句：The house stood amidst a forest of trees.  房子矗立在一片树林之中。|近义词：among, amongst, in the middle of, surrounded by|反义词:outside, beyond|词性:介词
Etymology: From Middle English *amiddes, from Old English on middan, "in the middle".  The -st ending is a superlative suffix, giving the sense of "right in the middle."
