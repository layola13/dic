dousing:/ˈdaʊsɪŋ/
n.|浇灭，熄灭；浸泡|The act of pouring a liquid over something; immersion in liquid.|Firefighters are dousing the flames. 消防队员正在浇灭火焰.|近义词：extinguishing, soaking, immersing, quenching|反义词:igniting, kindling
v.|浇灭，熄灭；浸泡|To pour a liquid over something; to immerse in liquid.|He doused the campfire with water. 他用水浇灭了篝火.|近义词：extinguish, soak, immerse, quench|反义词:ignite, kindle
