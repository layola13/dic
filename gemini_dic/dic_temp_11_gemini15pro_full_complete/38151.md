compressive:/kəmˈprɛsɪv/| adj.|压缩的，加压的|Relating to or involving compression; tending to compress.|例句：Compressive forces can cause rocks to fold. 压缩力可以使岩石褶皱。|例句：The compressive strength of concrete is a key factor in construction. 混凝土的抗压强度是建筑中的一个关键因素。|近义词：squeezing, pressing, compacting|反义词：tensile, expansive|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *compressivus, from compress- ‘pressed together’ (see compress).
