anti-immigration:/ˌæntiˌɪmɪˈɡreɪʃn/
adj.
反移民的
Opposed to immigration.
The anti-immigration sentiment is growing in some countries. 一些国家反移民的情绪正在增长。
The politician's anti-immigration stance cost him the election.  这位政治家的反移民立场让他输掉了选举。
近义词: nativist, xenophobic
反义词: pro-immigration
词性: adjective


anti-immigration:/ˌæntiˌɪmɪˈɡreɪʃn/
n.
反移民
Opposition to immigration.
There has been a rise in anti-immigration in recent years. 近年来，反移民情绪有所上升。
The protest was fueled by anti-immigration. 抗议是由反移民情绪推动的。
近义词: nativism, xenophobia
反义词: pro-immigration
词性: noun



词源:
"anti-" (prefix meaning against) + "immigration" (noun from "immigrate," from Latin "immigrare," meaning "to remove into")
