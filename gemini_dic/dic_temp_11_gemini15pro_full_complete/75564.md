fuckup:/ˈfʌkʌp/| v. |搞砸，弄糟|To ruin or spoil something, to make a mistake.|He really fucked up the presentation. 他真的把演讲搞砸了。|synonyms: mess up, screw up, botch, ruin, spoil| antonyms: succeed, achieve, accomplish| verb
