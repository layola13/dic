noncompensations:/ˌnɒnkɒmpenˈseɪʃənz/ | n. | 无补偿；不赔偿 | The lack of noncompensations for the victims of the disaster caused public outrage.  灾难受害者缺乏赔偿引起了公众的愤怒。|  The report detailed the noncompensations suffered by workers as a result of the company's negligence.  该报告详细说明了由于公司的疏忽而给工人造成的损失，并且没有得到任何补偿。 |  nonpayment, lack of recompense | compensation, recompense | noun

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + compensation (from Late Latin compensātiōn-, compensātiō, from compensāre, meaning "to weigh together, to counterbalance").
