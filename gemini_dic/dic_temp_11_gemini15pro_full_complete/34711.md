clew:/kluː/
n.
线索，提示；线团
a fact or piece of evidence that helps to solve a mystery or puzzle; a ball of thread or yarn.
The detective followed the clew to find the thief.  侦探顺着线索找到了小偷。

近义词:clue, hint, lead, indication
反义词:obfuscation, confusion

词性:noun

词源:From Middle English clewe, from Old English cliwen (“ball of thread, yarn, sphere”), from Proto-Germanic *kliwaz (“ball, clew”), from Proto-Indo-European *glew- (“ball”). Cognate with Old High German kliuwa (“ball, lump”). Related to globe.
