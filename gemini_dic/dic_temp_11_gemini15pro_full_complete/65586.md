evades:/ɪˈveɪdz/| v. |逃避，规避，躲避|To escape or avoid something, especially by cleverness or trickery.|The fox evades the hunters by hiding in the bushes. 狐狸躲在灌木丛中躲避猎人。|Synonyms: avoid, dodge, elude, escape, circumvent|Antonyms: confront, face, meet, encounter|verb

evades:/ɪˈveɪdz/| v. |逃避，规避，躲避|To fail to be grasped or remembered.|The name of the author evades me. 我想不起作者的名字了。|Synonyms: escape, slip|Antonyms: remember, recall|verb
