candler:/ˈkændlər/| n.|蜡烛制造者；售卖蜡烛的人|A person who makes or sells candles.|The chandler supplied all the candles for the church. 教堂里的所有蜡烛都由这个蜡烛商提供。|近义词：candlemaker|反义词: 无|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *chandler*, from Anglo-Norman *chandeler*, from Old French *chandelier* "candle maker, candlestick maker," from *chandelle* "candle," from Late Latin *candela*.
