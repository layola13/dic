beche:/biːtʃ/ | n. | 海滩; 海滨 | A stretch of sandy or pebbly shore, especially by the sea. |  We spent the day at the beche. 我们在海滩上度过了一天。| synonyms: beach, shore, seaside, coast | antonyms: inland, interior | noun

beche:/biːtʃ/ | v. | (of a boat or its occupants) be driven or run aground. | The ship was beched on the rocks. 船搁浅在岩石上。| synonyms: beach, ground, strand, run aground | antonyms: launch, float | verb
