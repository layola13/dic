locate /ˈloʊkeɪt/ (US), /ləʊˈkeɪt/ (UK)| v. |定位，找出，位于|To find the exact place or position of something or someone. |例句：We need to locate the source of the leak. 我们需要找到泄漏的源头。

|例句：The company is located in the heart of the city.  这家公司位于市中心。
|近义词：find, pinpoint, situate, position|反义词: lose, misplace|词性: verb


locate /ˈloʊkeɪt/ (US), /ləʊˈkeɪt/ (UK)| v. |设立，设置|To establish something in a particular place. |例句：They decided to locate their new factory near the highway. 他们决定把新工厂设在高速公路附近。

|例句：The hospital is conveniently located near public transportation. 这家医院交通便利，靠近公共交通。
|近义词：establish, set up, place, position|反义词: remove, relocate|词性: verb
