caraboid:/ˈkærəbɔɪd/| adj. n.|步甲状的，步甲科的；步甲科昆虫|Resembling or characteristic of ground beetles (Carabidae); a ground beetle.|例句：The caraboid beetle scurried under the leaf. 步甲虫迅速钻到叶子下面。

例句：The entomologist specialized in caraboid morphology. 这位昆虫学家专门研究步甲科昆虫的形态学。|近义词：ground beetle|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From  "Carabidae" (the family of ground beetles) + "-oid" (a suffix meaning "resembling" or "like"). "Carabidae" is derived from the Greek "kárabos" (a kind of horned beetle).
