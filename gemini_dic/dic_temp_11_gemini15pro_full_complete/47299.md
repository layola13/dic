de-icing:/diːˈaɪsɪŋ/
noun|除冰|The process of removing ice, frost, or snow from a surface, typically an aircraft or road.|The airport was busy with de-icing operations due to the heavy snow.  机场由于大雪纷飞，忙于除冰作业。|Synonyms: defrosting, thawing|Antonyms: freezing, icing|n.

de-icing:/diːˈaɪsɪŋ/
adjective|除冰的|Relating to or used for the removal of ice.|De-icing fluid is essential for winter flights. 除冰液对于冬季航班至关重要。|Synonyms: defrosting, anti-icing|Antonyms: freezing|adj.
