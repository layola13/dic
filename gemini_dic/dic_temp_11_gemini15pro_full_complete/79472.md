gloatingly:/ˈɡloʊtɪŋli/| adv.|幸灾乐祸地，沾沾自喜地|In a manner expressing triumphant and often malicious satisfaction or delight at someone else's misfortune.|He smiled gloatingly as his rival's business failed. 他看着对手的生意失败，幸灾乐祸地笑了。|Synonyms: smugly, triumphantly, gleefully|Antonyms: humbly, modestly, sympathetically|词性:副词
