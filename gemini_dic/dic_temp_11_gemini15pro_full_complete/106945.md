lacily:/ˈleɪsɪli/| adv.|花边状地；精巧地；纤细地|In a lacy or delicate manner, resembling lace.  Characterized by intricate and delicate patterns or structures.|The bride's veil was lacily embroidered with tiny pearls.  新娘的面纱绣着精美的珍珠花边。|Synonyms: delicately, intricately, ornately|Antonyms: plainly, simply, crudely|adverb
