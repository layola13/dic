auditress:/ˈɔːdɪtrəs/| n.|女审计员；女旁听生|A woman who audits accounts or listens to a class without being formally enrolled.|例句：The auditress meticulously reviewed the company's financial records. 女审计员仔细审查了公司的财务记录。

例句：She attended the lectures as an auditress to expand her knowledge of the subject. 她作为旁听生参加了讲座，以扩展她对该学科的知识。|近义词：auditor, listener, observer|反义词: (No direct antonym; consider related concepts like "participant" for an auditress attending a class)|词性:noun
