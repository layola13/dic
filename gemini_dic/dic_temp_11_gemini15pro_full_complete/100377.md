interstream:/ˈɪntərˌstriːm/
adjective
溪流间的；数据流间的
occurring between streams, especially of data
The interstream data transfer was crucial for the synchronization of the two systems.  溪流间的数据传输对于两个系统的同步至关重要。
The interstream communication protocol ensures seamless data exchange.  流间通信协议确保无缝的数据交换。
近义词:intervening, between
反义词:intrastream
