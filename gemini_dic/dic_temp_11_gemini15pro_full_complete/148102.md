overseason:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːzn/ |v. |使（运动员）过度训练；使（水果、蔬菜）储存过久|To train (an athlete) excessively, especially during the off-season. To keep (fruits or vegetables) in storage for too long. | The coach overseasoned his players, resulting in injuries. 教练让他的球员过度训练，导致了伤病。 | The strawberries were overseasoned and had become moldy. 草莓储存太久了，已经发霉了。 |overtrain, overwork | undertrain|verb
