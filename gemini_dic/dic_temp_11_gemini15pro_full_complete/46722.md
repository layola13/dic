danda:/ˈdændə/| n. |棍棒；杖；惩罚；权威；势力|A stick, rod, or staff, especially one used as a weapon or symbol of authority.  It can also refer to punishment or authority itself.|He carries a danda as a symbol of his office. 他拿着一根棍棒作为他职位的象征。|Synonyms: stick, staff, rod, cane, baton, mace, sceptre, punishment, authority, power|Antonyms:  subservience, weakness, impotence|noun
