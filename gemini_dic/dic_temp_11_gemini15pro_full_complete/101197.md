inviolably:/ɪnˈvaɪələbli/
adjective
不可侵犯地，神圣地
in a way that cannot be violated or broken
The treaty declared the country's borders inviolably sacred.  该条约宣布该国的边界神圣不可侵犯。
Their privacy should be inviolably protected. 他们的隐私应该受到不可侵犯的保护。
Synonyms: sacredly, untouchably, securely
Antonyms: vulnerably, insecurely
词性:adjective
