lan:/læn/ | n. | 局域网 (Local Area Network) | A computer network that interconnects computers within a limited area such as a residence, school, laboratory, university campus or office building. |  Example 1: The office has its own lan, separate from the internet. 办公室有自己的局域网，与互联网分开。

Example 2: Data is transferred much faster over a lan than over the internet.  在局域网上传输数据比在互联网上传输数据要快得多。 | synonym: intranet | antonym: WAN (Wide Area Network) | noun
