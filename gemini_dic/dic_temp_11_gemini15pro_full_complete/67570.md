extrapolations:/ɪkˌstræpəˈleɪʃənz/
n.
推断；推测；外推
The act or process of estimating or concluding something by assuming that existing trends will continue or a current method will remain applicable.
Based on current growth rates, extrapolations suggest the population will double in 20 years. 根据目前的增长率，推断人口将在20年内翻一番。
predictions, projections, estimations, inferences, deductions
interpolations, observations
