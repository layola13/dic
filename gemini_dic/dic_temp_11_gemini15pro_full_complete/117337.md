medevacked:/ˌmɛdɪˈvækt/ | v. | medical evacuation的缩写，医疗后送；用医疗运输机后送| The injured climber was medevacked from the mountain. 受伤的登山者被医疗直升机从山上送了下来。| 近义词：airlifted, evacuated | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 动词

medevac:/ˈmɛdɪvæk/ | n. | 医疗后送；医疗运输机|  The medevac arrived quickly and transported the patient to the hospital. 医疗运输机迅速抵达，并将病人送往医院。| 近义词：air ambulance, medical transport | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源:  "medevac" is a shortening of "medical evacuation."  It originated in the military during the Vietnam War.
