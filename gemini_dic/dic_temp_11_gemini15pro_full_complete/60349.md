egotistic:/ˌiːɡəˈtɪstɪk/ | adj. | 自我中心的；自负的 |  Excessively conceited or absorbed in oneself; self-centered. | He was so egotistic that he couldn't see anyone else's point of view. 他太自负了，以至于看不到别人的观点。 |  She's so egotistic, she only talks about herself. 她太自负了，只谈论自己。 | Near synonyms:  conceited, narcissistic, self-centered, arrogant, vain | Antonyms: humble, modest, selfless | adjective
