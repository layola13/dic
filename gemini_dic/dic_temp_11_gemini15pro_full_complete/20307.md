biserial:/baɪˈsɪəriəl/
adjective
双列的，二列相关的
Relating to or involving two series or rows.  Specifically in statistics, it refers to a correlation coefficient used when one variable is continuous and the other is dichotomous (having only two categories).
The researcher calculated the biserial correlation between test scores and whether or not students passed the course. 研究人员计算了考试分数与学生是否通过课程之间的双列相关性。
The biserial correlation revealed a strong relationship between income level and home ownership. 双列相关性揭示了收入水平和房屋所有权之间的密切关系。
近义词: dichotomous, two-series
反义词:  multivariate, multi-series
词性: adjective
