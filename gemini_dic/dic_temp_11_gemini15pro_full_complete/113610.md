mach:/mɑːk/ /mæk/| n. |马赫数，速度单位|A unit of speed equal to the speed of sound in air. |例句：The jet flew at Mach 2. 这架喷气式飞机以2马赫的速度飞行。|例句：The Concorde could travel at speeds exceeding Mach 2.  协和式飞机能以超过2马赫的速度飞行。|近义词：speed of sound|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) |词性:noun
