herrings:/ˈherɪŋz/
n.
鲱鱼（复数）
A small, oily fish found in large schools in the North Atlantic and North Pacific oceans.
例句：Herrings are an important food source for many animals. 鲱鱼是许多动物的重要食物来源。

例句：The fishermen caught a boatload of herrings. 渔民捕获了一船鲱鱼。
近义词：fish, sardine, sprat
反义词:None
词性:noun
