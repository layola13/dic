PHRASE->
converge on (聚集在...上):  Protesters converged on the city square.  抗议者聚集在城市广场上。
converge with (与...汇合/一致):  My ideas converged with his on several key points.  我的想法在几个关键点上与他的一致。
converge towards (趋向于/汇聚于):  The discussion is converging towards a consensus.  讨论正趋于达成共识。
point of convergence (汇合点):  The town square became the point of convergence for the demonstrators.  城镇广场成了示威者的聚集点。
convergence of opinions (意见一致):  There was a surprising convergence of opinions among the different groups.  不同群体之间出人意料地达成了一致意见。
