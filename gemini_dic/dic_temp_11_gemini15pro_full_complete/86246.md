heave-ho:/ˌhiːv ˈhoʊ/ | int. & n. | 嘿呦 (号子声); 费力的举动 | An exclamation used when hauling or lifting something heavy; the act of heaving or hauling. |  "Heave-ho!" cried the sailors as they raised the anchor. 水手们起锚时喊着“嘿呦！” | 近义词: heave, haul, pull, lift | 反义词: drop, lower | 词性: 叹词 & 名词
词源: From the combination of "heave" (to lift or throw with effort) and "ho" (an exclamation of effort or surprise).  The term originated in nautical settings.
