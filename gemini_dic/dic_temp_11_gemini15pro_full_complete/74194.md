fourgon:/fuːrˈɡɒn/ /ˈfɔːrɡɔn/
n.
厢式货车，运货马车；囚车
a closed railway freight car or van; a van or wagon.
The fourgon carried supplies to the remote village.  这辆厢式货车把物资运送到偏远的村庄。
The prisoners were transported in a fourgon. 囚犯们被用囚车押送。
van, wagon, lorry, truck
--
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fourgon*, from Old French *forgon*, from Frankish *furgo*, related to Old High German *furgo* (cart).
