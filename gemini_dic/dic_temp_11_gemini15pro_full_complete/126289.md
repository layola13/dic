mynheer:/maɪˈnjɪər/ | n. |先生，阁下 (尤指对荷兰或南非荷兰裔人士的称呼)| A form of address equivalent to "sir" or "Mr.," used especially of or to a Dutchman or a South African of Dutch descent. |例句：Mynheer, may I offer you a drink? 先生，我可以为您提供一杯饮料吗？

|例句：The mynheer welcomed us into his home. 这位先生欢迎我们来到他的家。|近义词：sir, Mr., gentleman |反义词: N/A |词性:名词
