collaborating:/kəˈlæbəˌreɪtɪŋ/
verb.|合作，协作|The act of working together with someone or a group of people to achieve a shared goal. |We are collaborating with another company on this project. 我们正在与另一家公司合作开展这个项目。|cooperating, partnering, teaming up|competing, opposing|v.

collaborate:/kəˈlæbəˌreɪt/
verb.|合作，协作|To work jointly with others or together especially in an intellectual endeavor. |The two companies collaborated on the development of the new software.  两家公司合作开发了这款新软件。|cooperate, partner, team up|compete, oppose|v.

collaboration:/kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃn/
noun.|合作，协作|The action of working with someone to produce or create something. |The project was a collaboration between several universities. 该项目是几所大学的合作成果。|cooperation, partnership, teamwork|competition, opposition|n.

collaborative:/kəˈlæbəˌreɪtɪv/
adjective.|合作的，协作的|Produced or conducted by two or more parties working together. |The team took a collaborative approach to problem-solving.  该团队采取了一种合作的方式来解决问题。|cooperative, joint, shared|independent, solo|adj.

collaborator:/kəˈlæbəˌreɪtər/
noun.|合作者， collaborators|A person who works jointly on an activity or project. |He's a frequent collaborator with other musicians. 他经常与其他音乐家合作。|partner, colleague, associate|opponent, competitor|n.
