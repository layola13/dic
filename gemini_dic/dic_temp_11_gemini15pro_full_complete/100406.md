interteam:/ˌɪntərˈtiːm/
adjective
队际的，团队之间的
Existing or occurring between teams.
例句：The interteam rivalry was fierce.  队际竞争非常激烈。

例句：Interteam cooperation is essential for project success. 团队间的合作对项目的成功至关重要。

近义词：between-teams
反义词：intrateam
词性：adjective
