humeral:/ˈhjuːmərəl/ (US) /ˈhjuːmərəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|肱骨的；上臂的；肱骨|Relating to the humerus (the bone of the upper arm). humerus:/ˈhjuːmərəs/ (US) /ˈhjuːmərəs/ (UK)| n.|肱骨|The long bone of the upper arm or forelimb, extending from the shoulder to the elbow.|The humerus is the largest bone in the upper arm. 肱骨是上臂最大的骨头。|A fracture of the humerus can be quite painful. 肱骨骨折可能会很疼。|近义词:(no direct synonym, perhaps upper arm bone)|反义词:(no antonym)|词性:noun


词源:

From Late Latin *humeralis, from Latin umerus (“shoulder, upper arm”), from Proto-Indo-European *ōmos (“shoulder”).
