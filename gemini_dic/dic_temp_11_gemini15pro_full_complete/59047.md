dweeb:/dwiːb/
n.
书呆子，怪咖
a person who is regarded as socially inept or boringly studious.
例句：He was a real dweeb in high school, always studying and never going out. 他高中时是个真正的书呆子，总是学习，从不出去玩。

例句：She called him a dweeb for wearing those glasses. 她因为他戴着那副眼镜而称他为书呆子。

近义词：nerd, geek, dork
反义词：cool kid, popular person
词性: 名词
