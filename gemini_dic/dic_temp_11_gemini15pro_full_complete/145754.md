overcultivating: /ˌōvərˈkʌltɪveɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度耕作；过度种植
cultivating too much; exhausting the land by excessive tilling
The overcultivating of the soil led to erosion and reduced yields.  过度耕作导致土壤侵蚀和产量下降。
Overcultivating the land can deplete its nutrients. 过度耕作土地会耗尽其养分。
近义词: overfarming, overtilling
反义词: undercultivating
