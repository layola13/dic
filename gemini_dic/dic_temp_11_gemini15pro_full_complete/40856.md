cooey:/ˈkuːi/| n. vi. vt.|招呼声；呼唤；(澳洲)用叫喊声互相联系|A loud, clear, high-pitched call or shout, used especially in the Australian bush to attract attention.|Example: He gave a cooey to his friends across the valley. 他向山谷对面的朋友们发出一声呼唤。|近义词：shout, call, yell, holler|反义词:whisper|词性:名词，动词(不及物动词，及物动词)


Etymology: From Dharug *guwiy
