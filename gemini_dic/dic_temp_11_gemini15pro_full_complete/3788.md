what /wɒt; hwɒt/  /wʌt; hwʌt/
what | pron. det. adv.| 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information about something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| synonyms: which, how | antonyms: (没有直接的反义词) | pronoun, determiner, adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living?  她以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all afternoon? 你整个下午都在做什么？
What has he been working on recently? 他最近一直在做什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie? 他们在看电影之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for so long? 他们一直在讨论什么那么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到今年年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么多久了？
