deadworks:/ˈdɛdˌwɜːrks/
noun
废品，次品；无效的工程；无用的工作
Products, projects, or work that are flawed, useless, or no longer functional.
The factory had a pile of deadworks that couldn't be sold. 工厂里有一堆无法出售的废品。
Synonym: rejects, failures, duds
Antonym: masterpieces, successes
