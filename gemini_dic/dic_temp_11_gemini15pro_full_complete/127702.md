neglected:/nɪˈɡlɛktɪd/| adj.|被忽视的，疏忽的|Failing to care for properly.|The garden was neglected and overgrown. 花园疏于照料，杂草丛生。|近义词：abandoned, forsaken, ignored, overlooked|反义词：cherished, cared-for, nurtured|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *neglectus*, past participle of *neglegere* "disregard, neglect," from *nec* "not" + *legere* "to gather, choose, read."
