ingrately:/ɪnˈɡreɪtfəli/| adv.|忘恩负义地，不知感恩地|In an ungrateful or unthankful manner; without showing gratitude.  Expressing or showing a lack of gratitude.|He ingrately accepted the gift without even saying thank you. 他忘恩负义地接受了礼物，甚至没有说谢谢。|Synonyms: ungratefully, thanklessly, unappreciatively|Antonyms: gratefully, thankfully, appreciatively|Adverb
