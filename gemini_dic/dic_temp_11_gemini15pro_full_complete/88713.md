hobbyism:/ˈhɒbiɪzəm/| n.| 业余爱好主义；沉溺于业余爱好 |The excessive pursuit of hobbies, sometimes to the detriment of other aspects of life.  过度追求业余爱好，有时会损害生活的其他方面。|He indulged in hobbyism to the point of neglecting his family. 他沉溺于业余爱好，以至于忽略了家人。|近义词：avocationism, dilettantism |反义词: professionalism, careerism| noun
