calibrating:/ˈkælɪbreɪtɪŋ/
verb
校准，标定
the act of checking or adjusting (by comparison with a standard) the accuracy of a measuring instrument.
The technician is calibrating the equipment.  技术员正在校准设备。
synonyms: adjusting, standardizing, regulating
antonyms: deregulating

verb
(figuratively) determining, evaluating, or checking the quality or ability of something.
The teacher is calibrating the students' understanding of the material. 老师正在评估学生对材料的理解。
synonyms: assessing, evaluating, gauging
antonyms: misjudging


ETYMOLOGY->
From French calibrer, from Italian calibrare,  Ultimately from Arabic qālib "mold."
