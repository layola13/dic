fery:/ˈfɛri/ | adj. | 火一般的，燃烧的，炽热的; 含铁的 | Resembling fire; burning or glowing; containing iron. | The fery sun beat down on the desert. 炽热的太阳炙烤着沙漠。 |  The blacksmith plunged the fery metal into the water. 铁匠将炽热的金属 plunged 进水中。 | 近义词: fiery, burning, incandescent, igneous | 反义词: cold, icy, frigid | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *feri, from Old French feri, fieri (“fiery, burning”), from Latin ferreus (“of iron, iron-hard”), from ferrum (“iron”).
