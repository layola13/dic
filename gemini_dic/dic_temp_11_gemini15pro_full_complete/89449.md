honors:/ˈɒnərz/ (US) /ˈɒnəz/ (UK)| n.| 荣誉; 优等学位; 荣誉课程|A special award or recognition given for excellence in a particular field or activity.|He received many honors for his scientific contributions. 他因其科学贡献而获得许多荣誉。|近义词：accolades, awards, recognition|反义词：dishonor, censure|词性:名词


词源:
From Middle English *honurs, *honures, from Anglo-Norman honurs, honures, Old French honors, honurs (“honors, dignities, privileges, distinctions, reverence, respect”), plural of honor, honur (“honor”), from Latin honor, honōs (“honor, esteem, reputation, office, dignity, public office, official position”).
