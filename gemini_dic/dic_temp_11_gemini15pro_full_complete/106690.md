kwashiorkor:/ˌkwɑːʃiˈɔːrkɔːr/ |n.|夸休可尔症，恶性营养不良|A form of severe protein malnutrition characterized by edema, growth failure, and skin lesions.|The child suffered from kwashiorkor due to a lack of protein in his diet.  这个孩子由于饮食中缺乏蛋白质而患上了夸休可尔症。|Synonyms: protein malnutrition, nutritional edema|Antonyms: proper nutrition, healthy diet|noun
