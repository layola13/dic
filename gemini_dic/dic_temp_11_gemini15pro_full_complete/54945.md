dispossessory:/dɪspəˈzesəri/
adjective
剥夺占有的; 驱逐的
Relating to or causing dispossession; depriving someone of land or property.
The landlord filed a dispossessory action against the tenant. 房东对房客提起驱逐诉讼。
Synonyms: evictive, expropriative, ousting
Antonyms: granting, providing, bestowing
词性: adjective


dispossess:/ˌdɪspəˈzes/
verb
剥夺；使丧失；驱逐
To deprive someone of land, property, or other possessions.
The company was dispossessed of its assets. 公司的资产被剥夺了。
Synonyms: deprive, strip, divest
Antonyms: provide, grant, endow
词性: verb


dispossession:/ˌdɪspəˈzeʃən/
noun
剥夺；丧失；驱逐
The action of dispossessing; the state of being dispossessed.
The dispossession of the farmers from their land caused widespread unrest. 农民被剥夺土地引起了广泛的不安。
Synonyms: deprivation, divestment, eviction
Antonyms: provision, endowment, granting
词性: noun
