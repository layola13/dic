forejudger:/fɔːrˈdʒʌdʒər/| n.|预先判断者，先决者|Someone who judges something or someone prematurely, before having all the necessary information. |The forejudger condemned the defendant before hearing all the evidence.| 近义词：prejudge, anticipate, presuppose | 反义词:  postpone judgement, withhold judgement | noun

forejudge:/fɔːrˈdʒʌdʒ/| v.|预先判断，先决|To judge something or someone prematurely, before having all the necessary information. |Do not forejudge the outcome before the investigation is complete.| 近义词：prejudge, presume, anticipate | 反义词: reserve judgement,  postpone judgement | verb
