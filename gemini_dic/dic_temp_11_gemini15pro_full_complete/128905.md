nimbyism:/ˈnɪmbɪɪzəm/
n.
邻避症候群；邻避主义
The opposition of residents to a proposed development in their local area.
Local residents displayed strong nimbyism regarding the proposed prison.当地居民对拟建的监狱表现出强烈的邻避主义。
Synonyms: localism, parochialism, not in my backyard
Antonyms:  None (The opposite would be a welcoming attitude towards development, but there isn't a single word for that.)
词性: noun


nimbyisms:/ˈnɪmbɪɪzəmz/
n.
邻避症候群；邻避主义（复数）
Multiple instances of the opposition of residents to proposed developments in their local areas.
The council faced numerous nimbyisms regarding various infrastructure projects. 委员会在各种基础设施项目上面临着许多邻避主义。
Synonyms: localisms, parochialisms
Antonyms: None
词性: noun
