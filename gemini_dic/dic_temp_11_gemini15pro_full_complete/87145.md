herbicide: /ˈhɜːrbɪsaɪd/ (UK) /ˈɜːrbɪsaɪd/ (US)| n.|除草剂|A substance that is toxic to plants and is used to destroy unwanted vegetation. |The farmer used herbicide to control the weeds in his field. 农民使用除草剂来控制田里的杂草。|Synonym: weedkiller|Antonym: fertilizer|noun

herbicide: /ˈhɜːrbɪsaɪd/ (UK) /ˈɜːrbɪsaɪd/ (US)| adj.|除草的|Relating to or denoting herbicides. |The herbicide treatment effectively eliminated the invasive species. 除草剂处理有效地消除了入侵物种。|Synonym: weed-killing|Antonym: fertilizing|adjective
