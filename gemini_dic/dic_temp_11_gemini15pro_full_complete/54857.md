displayers:/dɪˈspleɪərz/| n. |展示者，显示器|People or devices that exhibit something.  Those who show or present something for viewing. |The museum displayers arranged the artifacts carefully. 博物馆的展出者仔细地布置了文物。| The digital displayers showed the latest stock prices.  数字显示器显示了最新的股票价格。| synonyms: exhibitors, presenters, monitors, screens | antonyms: concealers, hiders | noun
