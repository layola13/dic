nontenant:/ˌnɑːnˈtɛnənt/| n.|非承租人，非房客|A person who is not a tenant, especially one who occupies a property without a formal lease agreement.|The nontenant was asked to leave the premises. 非承租人被要求离开该处所。|Near Synonyms: Occupier (without lease), squatter, trespasser|Antonyms: tenant, lessee|Noun
