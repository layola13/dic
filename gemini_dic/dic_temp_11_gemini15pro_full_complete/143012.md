outbid:/ˌaʊtˈbɪd/
|v.|出价高于（某人）; 超过…的出价|To offer a higher price than (someone else) for something, especially at an auction.|例句：She was outbid by a Japanese collector. 她被一位日本收藏家出价超过了。|近义词：outdo, surpass, overbid|反义词：underbid|词性：动词

outbid:/ˌaʊtˈbɪd/
|v.|胜过|To surpass or defeat.|例句：He outbid his rivals for the contract. 他在竞标中胜过了对手，赢得了合同。|近义词：beat, defeat, triumph over|反义词：lose to|词性：动词
