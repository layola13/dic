non-itemized:/ˌnɑːnˈaɪtəˌmaɪzd/ | adj. | 未逐项列出的，未分项的 | Not listed item by item; not detailed or broken down into individual components. | The receipt was non-itemized, showing only the total amount.  收据未逐项列出，只显示了总金额。| Synonyms: unitemized, general, summarized | Antonyms: itemized, detailed, specified | adjective


Etymology: non- + itemized.  "Itemized" comes from the word "item," ultimately from Latin "item" meaning "likewise, also."
