begrease: /bɪˈɡriːs/ /biˈɡris/
verb
涂油脂于；使沾满油脂
To cover or smear with grease.
The mechanic begreased the gears. 机械师给齿轮上了油。
He begreased his hands before working on the car. 他在修车前给手上涂了油。
近义词: lubricate, oil, grease
反义词: clean, degrease
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + grease.  "Be-" is a prefix used to form transitive verbs, often meaning "to make" or "to apply something to." "Grease" comes from Old French "gresse," ultimately from Late Latin "crassus" meaning "fat."
