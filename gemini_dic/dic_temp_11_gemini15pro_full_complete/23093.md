bounties:/ˈbaʊntiz/| n.|赏金，奖金，慷慨之举|A sum of money offered as a reward, especially for killing or capturing a person or animal.|The government offered bounties for the capture of escaped prisoners. 政府悬赏捉拿逃犯。|Synonyms: rewards, premiums, prizes|Antonyms: penalties, fines|Noun


bounty:/ˈbaʊnti/| n.|慷慨，大方；丰富，丰饶|Generosity, liberality; something provided in abundance.|The bounty of nature provided them with ample food. 大自然的慷慨为他们提供了充足的食物。|Synonyms: abundance, profusion, generosity|Antonyms: scarcity, lack, stinginess|Noun
