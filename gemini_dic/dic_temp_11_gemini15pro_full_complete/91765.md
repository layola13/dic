hyperemotivity:/ˌhaɪpərɪˌmoʊtɪˈvɪti/ | n. |过度情绪化；情感过度敏感 | Excessive emotional reactivity; heightened sensitivity to emotions. | She displayed hyperemotivity after the accident, crying easily and experiencing mood swings. 她事故之后表现出过度情绪化，很容易哭，而且情绪波动很大。| The child's hyperemotivity made it difficult for him to cope with everyday stressors.  这个孩子的情绪过度敏感让他难以应对日常压力。 |近义词：hypersensitivity, emotional lability, oversensitivity |反义词：emotional stability, apathy |词性: noun

词源: hyper- (Greek: over, excessive) + emotivity (from emotion)
