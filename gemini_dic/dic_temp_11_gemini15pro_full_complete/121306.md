misfocussed:/ˌmɪsˈfəʊkəst/ /ˌmɪsˈfoʊkəst/| adj.|未聚焦的，目标不明确的|Not correctly focused; blurred. Also metaphorically, lacking concentration or direction.|The photograph was misfocussed and blurry. 这张照片失焦了，很模糊。 The company's misfocussed strategy led to its downfall. 公司不明确的战略导致了它的垮台。|近义词：blurred, out of focus, unfocused, aimless|反义词：focused, sharp, clear, purposeful|词性:形容词

misfocused:/ˌmɪsˈfəʊkəst/ /ˌmɪsˈfoʊkəst/| adj.|未聚焦的，目标不明确的|Not correctly focused; blurred. Also metaphorically, lacking concentration or direction.|The lens was misfocused, resulting in a blurry image. 镜头失焦了，导致图像模糊。  His misfocused attention caused him to miss the important details. 他注意力不集中，导致他错过了重要的细节。|近义词：blurred, out of focus, unfocused, aimless|反义词：focused, sharp, clear, purposeful|词性:形容词


词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "focus" (verb meaning "to adjust something to produce a clear image") + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  来源于"focus"，加上前缀"mis-"表示错误或不好，再加上过去分词后缀"-ed"构成。
