frat:/fræt/
n.
兄弟会；大学生联谊会
a social organization for male students at a college or university
例句：He joined a frat during his freshman year. 他在大一的时候加入了一个兄弟会。

例句：Fraternity life isn't for everyone. 兄弟会生活并不适合每个人。
近义词：fraternity, brotherhood
反义词: sorority
词性: 名词
