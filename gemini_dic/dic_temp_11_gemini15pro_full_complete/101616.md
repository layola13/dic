irreconcilement:/ɪˌrekənˈsaɪlmənt/
noun
不可调和，无法和解
The state of being unreconciled; inability to be reconciled.
Their irreconcilement seemed final. 他们的不和看起来是最终的了。
近义词:discord, disharmony, conflict, estrangement
反义词:reconciliation, accord, harmony, agreement
词性:n.


Etymology:
From Late Middle English *irreconcilement*, from irreconcilable + -ment.  Ultimately from Latin *irreconciliabilis*.
