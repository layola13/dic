hyperconcentration:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɑːnsənˈtreɪʃən/ | n. |过度集中，过度关注|The state of being excessively focused on one thing, often to the detriment of others.  |例句：His hyperconcentration on the video game made him oblivious to his surroundings. 他对电子游戏的过度专注使他对周围的环境浑然不觉。

例句：Hyperconcentration can be a symptom of certain mental health conditions. 过度集中可能是某些精神健康状况的症状。|近义词：fixation, obsession, preoccupation|反义词: distraction, inattention |词性: noun
