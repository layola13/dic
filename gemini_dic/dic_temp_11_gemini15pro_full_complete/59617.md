eccentricity: /ˌɛksɛnˈtrɪsɪti/ /ˌɛksənˈtrɪsɪti/ | n. |古怪；怪癖；偏心距|The quality of being eccentric. The amount by which something, such as an orbit, deviates from a perfect circle.| Her eccentricity makes her stand out from the crowd. 她的古怪使她在人群中脱颖而出. The eccentricity of the planet's orbit causes variations in its temperature. 行星轨道的偏心率导致其温度变化.|近义词: peculiarity, oddity, quirkiness, strangeness, abnormality|反义词: normality, conventionality|词性: noun

eccentricity: /ˌɛksɛnˈtrɪsɪti/ /ˌɛksənˈtrɪsɪti/ | n. |古怪行为；怪癖|An eccentric act, habit, or characteristic.|One of her eccentricities is collecting rubber ducks. 她其中一个怪癖就是收集橡皮鸭.|近义词: idiosyncrasy, foible, mannerism|反义词: normality, conventionality|词性: noun
