basmachi:/bɑːsˈmɑːtʃi/ | n. | 巴斯玛奇 | Members of a Central Asian Muslim guerrilla movement that fought against Russian and Soviet rule in the early 20th century.  They were primarily active in the region encompassing present-day Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Kyrgyzstan, and parts of Kazakhstan and Turkmenistan. | The Basmachi revolt posed a significant challenge to Soviet authority in Central Asia. 巴斯玛奇起义对苏联在中亚的权威构成了重大挑战。|  The Basmachi movement was ultimately suppressed by the Red Army. 巴斯玛奇运动最终被红军镇压。 | synonyms: rebels, insurgents, guerrillas | antonyms: loyalists, collaborators | noun
