nose-out:/noʊzˈaʊt/ | v. | 以微弱优势胜过; 险胜 | To defeat by a narrow margin; barely win. |  Example 1: The home team nosed out their rivals in the final seconds of the game. 主队在比赛的最后几秒险胜了他们的对手。

Example 2:  She nosed out the other candidate by just a few votes. 她以几票的微弱优势险胜了另一位候选人。 | 近义词: edge out, narrowly defeat, outstrip, beat by a whisker | 反义词: lose badly, suffer a crushing defeat | 词性: 动词
