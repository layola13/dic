nonelastic:/ˌnɒnɪˈlæstɪk/ | /ˌnɑːnɪˈlæstɪk/ | adj. | 非弹性的 | Not elastic; inelastic.  Unable to return to its original shape or size after being stretched or compressed. | The nonelastic bandage provided firm support.  非弹性绷带提供了牢固的支撑。|  The demand for gasoline is relatively nonelastic. 汽油的需求相对缺乏弹性。 | inelastic, rigid, inflexible | elastic, flexible, stretchy | adjective
