isoceles:/aɪˈsɒsəliːz/| adj.|等腰的；等边的|A triangle with two sides of equal length.|例句：An isosceles triangle has two equal sides and two equal angles.  一个等腰三角形有两条边相等，以及两个角相等。

例句：The roof of the house was an isosceles triangle. 房子的屋顶是一个等腰三角形。|近义词：equilateral (when all three sides are equal)|反义词：scalene|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἰσοσκελής (isoskelḗs, “having equal legs”), from ἴσος (ísos, “equal”) + σκέλος (skélos, “leg”).
