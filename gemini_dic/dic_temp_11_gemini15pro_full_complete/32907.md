choo-choos:/ˈtʃuːtʃuːz/ | n. | 火车（儿童用语）| An informal, childish term for a train.  |  例句：The little boy loves his choo-choos. 小男孩喜欢他的火车玩具。

例句：Look at all the choo-choos! 看所有的火车！

近义词：train, locomotive, engine | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "car" or "plane" as alternative modes of transport) | 词性: Noun
