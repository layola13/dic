hungover:/ˈhʌŋˌoʊvər/ (US) /ˈhʌŋˌəʊvə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|宿醉的，感到不舒服的 (因前一天晚上喝太多酒)|Feeling ill effects from excessive drinking of alcohol the night before.|例句：I was so hungover this morning after last night's party. 昨晚的派对后，我今天早上宿醉得很厉害。

例句：She called in sick, claiming to be hungover. 她打电话请病假，声称宿醉了。


近义词：ill, unwell, sick, seedy, rough, jaded|反义词:sober, fresh|词性:形容词
