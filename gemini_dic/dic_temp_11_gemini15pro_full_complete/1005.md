cater:/ˈkeɪtər/ (US), /ˈkeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| v.| 迎合；提供饮食；满足需要 |To provide food and drinks for an event, especially a large one; to satisfy the needs of.|  例句：The company caters for weddings and other special occasions.  这家公司承办婚礼和其他特殊场合的餐饮服务。| 近义词：provide, supply, serve| 反义词：neglect, ignore| verb

cater to:/ˈkeɪtər tuː/ (US), /ˈkeɪtə tuː/ (UK)| v.| 迎合；满足(某种需要或欲望) |To satisfy a particular need or desire, often one that is considered frivolous or unworthy.| 例句：The magazine caters to the interests of teenage girls. 这本杂志迎合了少女们的兴趣。| 近义词：indulge, pander to| 反义词：disregard, ignore| verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *catour*, from Anglo-Norman *acatour*, from *acater*, from Late Latin *capitare* (“to buy”), frequentative of Latin *capere* (“to take, seize”).
