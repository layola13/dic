nicely:/ˈnaɪsli/| adv.|很好地，恰当地，和蔼地|In a pleasant or satisfactory way; well.|She sings nicely. 她唱歌很好听。|近义词：well, pleasantly, agreeably, kindly|反义词：badly, poorly, unpleasantly, rudely|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *nicely,* from nice + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *nescius* ("ignorant").  The meaning evolved from "foolish" to "coy" to "fastidious" to "agreeable," and finally to the modern senses.
