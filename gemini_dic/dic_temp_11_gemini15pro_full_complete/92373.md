hypochondry:/ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriə/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriə/ (UK)| n.|疑病症，臆想病|A psychological disorder characterized by excessive worry about having a serious illness, despite medical reassurance to the contrary.|例句：Her hypochondry led her to believe she had a brain tumor, even after several doctors assured her she was healthy. 她的疑病症让她相信自己得了脑瘤，即使几位医生都向她保证她很健康。

例句：He suffered from hypochondry and was constantly checking himself for symptoms. 他患有疑病症，经常检查自己是否有症状。

近义词：health anxiety, illness anxiety disorder, nosophobia|反义词: robust health, wellness|词性: noun
