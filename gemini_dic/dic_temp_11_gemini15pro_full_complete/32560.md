chipping-in:/ˈtʃɪpɪŋ ɪn/| n. |凑钱;插嘴;捐助;帮助|The act of contributing a small amount of money or effort to a shared cost or task.  It can also refer to interrupting a conversation.|例句：Everyone chipped in for Sue's birthday present.  大家一起凑钱买了苏的生日礼物。

例句：He kept chipping in with unhelpful comments. 他不停地插嘴说一些没用的评论。|近义词：contributing, donating, interrupting, interjecting|反义词:withdrawing, withholding, ignoring
