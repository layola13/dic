brine:/braɪn/| n. vt.|盐水，卤水；海水；眼泪|Water saturated or strongly impregnated with salt.|The olives were preserved in brine. 橄榄用盐水保存。|近义词：saltwater, seawater, saline solution|反义词:freshwater|noun
brine:/braɪn/| vt.|用盐水浸泡|To soak or preserve in salty water.|Brine the turkey before roasting it. 在烤火鸡之前，先用盐水浸泡它。|近义词：pickle, soak, souse|反义词:|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *brýne "salt water," from Proto-Germanic *brunjo "salt spring, brine" (source also of Old Saxon brunia, Middle Dutch brine, Dutch brijn, German Brüne "mineral spring"), from PIE root *bhreu- "to boil, bubble, effervesce, burn."
