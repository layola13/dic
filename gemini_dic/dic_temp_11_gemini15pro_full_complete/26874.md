cajuput:/ˈkædʒəpʊt/ | n. | 白千层,白千层油 | An evergreen tree native to Australia, Southeast Asia, and India, yielding a medicinal oil.  | The cajuput tree is known for its distinctive aroma. 白千层树因其独特的香味而闻名。 | The oil derived from cajuput leaves is used in traditional medicine.  从白千层叶中提取的油用于传统医学。 | 近义词:  melaleuca (尤指白千层属植物), tea tree (茶树，尤指互叶白千层) | 反义词: None (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Malay *kayu putih* "white wood," referring to the tree's pale bark.
