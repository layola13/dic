osculatrix:/ˈɒskjʊlətrɪks/ | n. |密切相切的曲线,密切圆|A curve that has a high degree of contact with another curve at a given point.  It approximates the given curve very closely in the neighborhood of that point. |The osculatrix to the parabola at the vertex is a straight line.  抛物线在其顶点的密切圆是一条直线。| 近义词: tangent, approximating curve | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'divergent curve') | noun


osculating:/ˈɒskjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |密切相切的|Touching closely; kissing. In mathematics, having a high degree of contact at a point. |The osculating circle to a curve has the same curvature as the curve at the given point. 密切圆在给定点处与曲线具有相同的曲率。|近义词: tangent, touching |反义词: divergent | adjective
