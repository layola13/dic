commitment:/kəˈmɪtmənt/ /kəˈmɪtmənt/|n.|承诺，保证；投入，奉献；责任，义务|A pledge or promise; the state or quality of being dedicated to a cause, activity, etc.; an obligation or engagement.|She made a commitment to help the homeless. 她承诺帮助无家可归的人。|近义词：pledge, promise, dedication, devotion, obligation|反义词：indecision, apathy, disloyalty|词性:名词

commitment:/kəˈmɪtmənt/ /kəˈmɪtmənt/|n.|犯罪行为，罪行 (主要指不好的行为)|An act of committing, especially a crime or bad action.|His commitment to the cause went beyond mere words. 他对这项事业的投入不仅仅是说说而已。|近义词：act, deed, offense|反义词：restraint, abstention|词性:名词
