oblivionize: /əˈblɪviəˌnaɪz/ | vt. | 使遗忘；消除记忆 | To cause someone to forget something, typically using magic or other supernatural means.  |  Example: The wizard oblivionized the witness to protect his secret.  巫师消除了目击者的记忆以保护他的秘密。

Example:  She wished she could oblivionize the painful memory of the accident. 她希望她能消除事故带来的痛苦记忆。 | 近义词: erase, expunge, wipe out | 反义词: remember, recall, commemorate | 词性: 动词 (verb)

词源: From Latin *oblīviōnis, genitive of oblīviō (“forgetfulness”) (from oblīvīscor (“I forget”), from ob- + -līvīscor, inchoative from *līvor, whence līveō (“to be bluish”)), + -ize.
