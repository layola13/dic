nonornamentality:/ˌnɒnˌɔːrnəˌmɛnˈtælɪti/
n.
缺乏装饰性；朴素
the state or quality of being plain or undecorated.
The architect favored nonornamentality, preferring clean lines and simple forms.  这位建筑师崇尚朴素，喜欢简洁的线条和简单的形式。
The room's nonornamentality gave it a sense of peace and tranquility.  房间的朴素赋予它一种宁静祥和的感觉。
simplicity, plainness, austerity, unadornedness
ornateness, embellishment, decoration, elaborateness
noun
