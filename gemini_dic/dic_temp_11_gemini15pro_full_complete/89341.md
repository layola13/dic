homoiousian:/ˌhɒmɔɪˈuːsiən/ | adj. |  同质的，相似本质的 | Of the same or similar substance or essence.  Specifically used in theological contexts to describe the relationship between the Father and the Son in the Trinity. |  The Council of Nicaea declared that the Son is homoiousian with the Father. 尼西亚会议宣布圣子和圣父是同质的。|  近义词: consubstantial, similar, like | 反义词:  heterousian, dissimilar, unlike | 词性: adjective
