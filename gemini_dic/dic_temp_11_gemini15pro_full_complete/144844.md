over-morally:/ˈoʊvərˈmɔːrəli/
adjective
过分道德的，道德上过分的
Excessively moral; moral to an excessive degree.
His over-morally judgmental attitude alienated many of his friends. 他过分道德评判的态度疏远了他的许多朋友。
She sometimes comes across as over-morally righteous. 她有时表现得过分正直。
近义词:  puritanical, self-righteous, moralistic
反义词:  amoral, immoral, unethical
词性: adjective

over:/ˈoʊvər/
adverb, preposition, adjective
在...之上，超过，结束
Above, across, beyond, finished.
The plane flew over the city. 飞机飞过城市上空。
近义词: above, across, beyond, finished, complete
反义词: under, below
词性: adverb, preposition, adjective

morally:/ˈmɔːrəli/
adverb
道德上，道义上
In a moral manner; according to moral principles.
He acted morally even when it was difficult. 即使在困难的时候，他仍然行为端正。
近义词: ethically, righteously, virtuously
反义词: immorally, unethically
词性: adverb
