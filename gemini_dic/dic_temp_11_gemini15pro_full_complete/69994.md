fetchers:/ˈfɛtʃərz/
noun
获取者；拿来者；取物者
People or things that fetch something.
The fetchers brought back armfuls of firewood.  取柴的人带回了大量的柴火。
Retrievers are excellent fetchers.  寻回犬是很棒的取物者。
Synonyms: retrievers, collectors, gatherers
Antonyms: givers, providers
