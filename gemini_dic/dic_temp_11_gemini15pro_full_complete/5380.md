alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/ | n. | 冲积层；冲积土 | Clay, silt, sand, gravel, or similar detrital material deposited by running water. | The fertile alluvium of the Nile delta has supported agriculture for millennia. 尼罗河三角洲肥沃的冲积层支撑了数千年的农业发展。

The farmers cultivate the rich alluvium deposited by the river. 农民们耕种着河流沉积下来的肥沃的冲积土。| 近义词: deposit, sediment, silt | 反义词: bedrock | 词性: 名词
