confirmative:/kənˈfɜːrmətɪv/ | adj. | 证实的，肯定的 | Serving to confirm; corroborative. | The lawyer presented confirmative evidence to support his client's alibi. 律师提出了确凿的证据来支持他的委托人的不在场证明。| synonyms: corroborative, validating, substantiating, affirming | antonyms: negative, disconfirming, contradictory | adjective

confirmative:/kənˈfɜːrmətɪv/ | n. | 肯定词，肯定语 | A word or phrase expressing affirmation or agreement. |  "Yes" is a common confirmative. “是”是一个常见的肯定词。 | synonyms: affirmation, assent, agreement | antonyms: denial, negation, refusal | noun
