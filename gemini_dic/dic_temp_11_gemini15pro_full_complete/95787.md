incontrovertible:/ˌɪnkɒntrəˈvɜːtɪbl/ /ˌɪnkɑːntrəˈvɜːrtəbl/| adj.|无可争议的；不容置疑的|Not able to be denied or disputed.|Her logic was incontrovertible. 她的逻辑无可争议。|The evidence of his guilt was incontrovertible. 他犯罪的证据确凿无疑。|近义词：undeniable, indisputable, irrefutable, unquestionable|反义词：disputable, debatable, questionable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin incontrovertibilis, from in- "not" + controversus "disputed," past participle of controvertere "to turn against, dispute," from contra- "against" + vertere "to turn."
