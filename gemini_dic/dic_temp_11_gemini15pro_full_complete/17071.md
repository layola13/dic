batik: /bəˈtiːk/ (US), /ˈbætɪk/ (UK)| n. adj.|蜡染，印花布|A method of dyeing fabric by covering certain sections with wax so that they will not absorb color. Also refers to the fabric dyed in this way.|

例句：
She wore a beautiful batik dress to the party.
她穿着一件漂亮的蜡染连衣裙去参加聚会。

The museum has a large collection of Indonesian batiks.
这家博物馆收藏了大量的印尼蜡染布。


近义词：tie-dye, resist dyeing|
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain dyed" for the fabric)|
词性: noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Javanese *batik*, from *amba* "to write" + *titik* "dot, point".  The process involves drawing dots and lines with wax to create patterns.
