e-friend:/ˈiː frɛnd/ | n. | 网友 | A friend made and maintained through online communication, typically through social media, online games, or forums. | I've known my e-friend for five years, but we've never met in person. 我认识我的网友五年了，但我们从未见过面。

| Example:  She met her e-friend in an online gaming forum. 她在一个在线游戏论坛上认识了她的网友。

| Example:  He keeps in touch with his e-friends through social media. 他通过社交媒体与他的网友保持联系。

| Synonym: online friend, internet friend | Antonym:  offline friend, real-life friend | Noun
