coalitionist:/ˌkoʊəˈlɪʃənɪst/
n.
联盟主义者；联合主义者
A supporter of a coalition government.
The coalitionist argued for the benefits of parties working together. 这位联合主义者力陈各党派合作的好处。
Synonyms: ally, partner, collaborator
Antonyms: opponent, adversary
noun


coalitionist:/ˌkoʊəˈlɪʃənɪst/
adj.
联合的；联盟的
Relating to or supporting a coalition.
The coalitionist approach helped to resolve the crisis. 联合的方式有助于解决危机。
Synonyms:  joint, combined, united
Antonyms: separate, divided, independent
adjective
