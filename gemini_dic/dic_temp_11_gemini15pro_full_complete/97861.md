inline:/ɪnˈlaɪn/ /ˈɪnˌlaɪn/ | adj. adv.| 内联的，在线的，排成一行的 |Existing or occurring on a line, especially a line of text; integrated within a main text or body of something.  | The image was placed inline with the text. 图片与文字内联放置。 | 近义词:integrated, embedded, built-in | 反义词:offline, separate, detached | 词性:形容词/副词

inline:/ˈɪnlaɪn/ | v.| 使成一直线，使内联 | To arrange something in a line or integrate it within a main body. |  They inlined the images with the captions. 他们将图片与标题内联排列。 | 近义词:align, integrate, embed | 反义词:separate, detach | 词性:动词
