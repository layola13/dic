antipetalous:/ˌæntiˈpɛtələs/
adjective
对瓣的；与花瓣对生的
Positioned opposite the petals.
The antipetalous stamens are a key characteristic of this flower family.  这种花科的主要特征是雄蕊与花瓣对生。
Antipetalous stamens were observed in the dissected flower. 在解剖的花中观察到与花瓣对生的雄蕊。
近义词: opposite
反义词: alternipetalous

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (opposite) + petalous (having petals), from Greek anti- (against, opposite) + petalon (leaf, petal).
