overaffirms:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɜːrmz/| v.|过度肯定，过分确认|To affirm too strongly or too much; to assert with excessive emphasis.  It implies going beyond reasonable affirmation and potentially creating a sense of insincerity or exaggeration.|He overaffirmed his support for the candidate, making it sound insincere.|近义词：overstate, exaggerate, overemphasize|反义词:understate, downplay|词性:verb
