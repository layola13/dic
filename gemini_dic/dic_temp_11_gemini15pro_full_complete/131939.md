noncontractual:/ˌnɒnkənˈtræktʃʊəl/
adjective
非合同的；非契约的
Not involving or relating to a contract.
This is a noncontractual obligation. 这是一项非合同义务。
The payment was a noncontractual gift. 这笔付款是一份非合同性质的赠与。
近义词: informal, unwritten, nonbinding
反义词: contractual, binding
词性: adjective

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "contractual" (relating to a contract).  "Contractual" ultimately derives from the Latin "contractus" (drawn together).
