bushel:/ˈbʊʃəl/| n.|蒲式耳（容量单位，相当于8加仑或36.3687升）|A bushel is a unit of dry volume used in agriculture, equal to 8 gallons or approximately 36.37 liters.  It is typically used for measuring grains, fruits, and vegetables.|The farmer harvested 100 bushels of wheat this year. 今年农民收获了100蒲式耳小麦。|Synonyms: peck (1/4 bushel), dry quart, dry gallon|Antonyms: N/A (units of volume don't typically have opposites)|noun
