hearten:/ˈhɑːrtn/ (US), /ˈhɑːtn/ (UK)| v. |鼓舞，激励，振奋|To give someone courage or confidence; to make someone feel cheerful. |The coach's words heartened the team after their defeat. 教练的话在球队失利后鼓舞了他们。|

The news heartened us all. 这消息让我们都感到振奋。

|近义词：encourage, cheer, inspire, uplift|反义词：discourage, deject, depress, dispirit|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *herten*, from Old English *heartian* "to give heart to, encourage," from *heorte* "heart."  Cognate with German *herzen* "to gladden."
