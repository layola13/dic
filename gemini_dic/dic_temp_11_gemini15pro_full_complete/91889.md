hyperirritable:/ˌhaɪpərˈɪrɪtəbl/ | adj. | 过度易怒的，极易激动的 | Excessively or abnormally irritable; easily provoked or agitated. | The patient was hyperirritable after the surgery, reacting strongly to even slight noises.  手术后病人变得非常易怒，即使是轻微的噪音也会有强烈反应。 | The hyperirritable child cried at the slightest provocation.  这个极易激动的孩子稍一被激怒就哭了。| 近义词: irascible, touchy, testy, choleric | 反义词: calm, placid, serene | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
irritable (adjective): from Late Latin irritabilis, from irritare "to excite, provoke," from Latin irritus "invalid, void, undone"
