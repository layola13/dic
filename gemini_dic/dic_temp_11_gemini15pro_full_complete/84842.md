hang-together:/ˌhæŋ təˈɡeðər/| v. |连贯；一致；有条理|To be logical and consistent; to form a united whole.|The different parts of the story didn't hang together.  这个故事的不同部分之间不太连贯。|The film's plot hangs together surprisingly well, despite its complexity.  这部电影的情节尽管复杂，却出奇地连贯。|近义词：cohere, connect, be consistent|反义词: fall apart, be inconsistent|词性:动词

hang-together:/ˌhæŋ təˈɡeðər/| n. |连贯性；一致性|The state of being logical and consistent.|The hang-together of her argument was impressive.  她的论证条理清晰，令人印象深刻。|There's not much hang-together to his latest theory. 他的最新理论不太连贯。|近义词：coherence, consistency, unity|反义词: incoherence, inconsistency, disunity|词性:名词
