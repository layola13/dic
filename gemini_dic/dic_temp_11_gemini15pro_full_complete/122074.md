misthought:/ˌmɪsˈθɔːt/
noun
错误的想法；误解
an incorrect thought or idea
She realized her misthought and quickly corrected herself. 她意识到了自己的错误想法，并很快纠正了自己。
synonyms: misconception, misunderstanding, error, fallacy
antonyms: correct thought, accurate understanding
n.
