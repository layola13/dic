cautionary:/ˈkɔːʃənɛri/ | adj. | 警告的；警示的 | Serving as a warning. | The government issued a cautionary statement about the dangers of smoking. 政府发布了一份关于吸烟危害的警告声明。|  The label on the bottle had a cautionary message about the flammability of the contents. 瓶子上的标签有一个关于内容物易燃性的警告信息。 |近义词: warning, admonitory, advisory | 反义词: encouraging, reassuring | 词性: 形容词

Etymology->
From Late Middle English *causionarie*, from Anglo-Norman *caucionarie*, from Latin *cautiōnārius* (“of or pertaining to caution”), from *cautiō* (“caution”).
