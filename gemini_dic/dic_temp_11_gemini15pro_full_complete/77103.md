garrulously:/ˈɡærələsli/ | adv. | 喋喋不休地，絮絮叨叨地 | In a talkative and excessively wordy manner. | He talked garrulously about his childhood. 他喋喋不休地谈论他的童年。| 近义词：loquaciously, volubly, talkatively | 反义词：taciturnly, reticently | 词性: 副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *garrulus, from Latin garrulus (“talkative, chattering”), from garrire (“to chatter”).
