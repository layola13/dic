enduringly:/ɪnˈdjʊərɪŋli/
adv.
持久地，耐久地
in a way that lasts for a long time
Her name will enduringly be associated with the struggle for civil rights. 她的名字将永远与争取民权的斗争联系在一起。
近义词:lastingly, permanently, persistently
反义词:briefly, temporarily, fleetingly
词性:副词

词源: From endure (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Endure" comes from Old French "endurer" and ultimately from Latin "indurare" (to harden, make hard), from "in-" (in) + "durus" (hard).
