benelux:/ˈbɛnəˌlʌks/
n.
比荷卢经济联盟; 比荷卢三国
A politico-economic union of Belgium, the Netherlands, and Luxembourg.
The Benelux countries have a combined population of over 28 million. 比荷卢三国总人口超过2800万。
近义词: Belgium, Netherlands, Luxembourg (when referring to the individual countries)
反义词: None (as it's a proper noun representing a specific entity)
词性: noun

Etymology:  Formed from the first few letters of **Be**lgium, **Ne**therlands, and **Lu**xembourg.
