breastbeam:/ˈbrɛstbɪːm/| n.|胸梁，托梁|A horizontal beam or tie at breast height, especially one supporting a wall.|The old barn's breastbeam was starting to sag under the weight of the hayloft. 老谷仓的胸梁在干草棚的重压下开始下陷。|Synonym: summer, summertree, bressummer |Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "foundation" in a contrasting sense)|noun
