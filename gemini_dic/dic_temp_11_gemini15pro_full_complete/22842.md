bosss: /ˈbɒsɪz/ (This is likely a misspelling of "bosses") | n. | 老板们；上司们 |  Plural form of "boss," meaning a person who is in charge of a worker or organization. |  Example: The bosses are having a meeting. 老板们正在开会。| Example: All the bosses agreed on the new policy. 所有的老板都同意了新政策。 | Synonym: managers, superiors, executives | Antonym: subordinates, employees | noun
