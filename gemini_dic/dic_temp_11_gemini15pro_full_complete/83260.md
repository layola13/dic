gymnogen:/ˈdʒɪmnədʒən/| n. |裸子植物|A plant whose seeds are not enclosed within an ovary.  A plant that bears naked seeds, such as conifers.|

例句：
Pine trees are a common example of a gymnogen.
松树是裸子植物的一个常见例子。

The gymnogen flourished in the Mesozoic era.
裸子植物在中生代繁盛。


近义词：gymnosperm|
反义词：angiosperm (被子植物)|
词性：noun|
