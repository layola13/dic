bakkie:/ˈbæki/ (South African English)| n.|小货车，皮卡|A small truck with an open back, often used for transporting goods.  Derived from Afrikaans.|例句：He loaded the furniture into his bakkie. 他把家具装进了他的小货车。

例句：The farmer used his bakkie to transport produce to the market.  农民用他的小货车把农产品运到市场。|近义词：pickup truck, ute (Australian English)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
