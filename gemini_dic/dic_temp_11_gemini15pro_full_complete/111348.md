livelong:/ˈlaɪvlɒŋ/ (UK) /ˈlaɪvlɔːŋ/ (US)| adj.|终生的，毕生的|Existing or continuing for a lifetime; lasting throughout life.  The word often adds emphasis and sometimes a slightly poetic or archaic feel.|例句：He was her livelong friend.  他是她毕生的朋友。|例句：She spent her livelong days in the countryside. 她毕生都在乡下度过。|近义词：lifelong, enduring, lasting|反义词：short-lived, temporary, transient|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *livelong, from Old English līflang (“lifelong, lasting as long as life”), equivalent to *līf (“life”) + lang (“long”). Cognate with Scots livelang (“lifelong”), German leiblang (“lifelong”).
