cobranded:/koʊˈbrændɪd/| adj.|联合品牌的，共同品牌的|A product or service that is cobranded is produced or offered by two or more companies, with both their names or brands on it.|例句：The cobranded credit card offers rewards from both the airline and the hotel chain. 这张联合品牌的信用卡提供航空公司和连锁酒店的奖励。

例句：The cobranded product was a success, boosting sales for both companies. 联合品牌的产品很成功，促进了两家公司的销售。|近义词：jointly branded, dual-branded|反义词:solely branded, individually branded|词性:形容词
