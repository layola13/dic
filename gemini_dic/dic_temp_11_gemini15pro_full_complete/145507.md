overcluttered:/ˌoʊvərˈklʌtərd/
adjective
过于杂乱的，堆满东西的
overly filled or crowded with things; excessively cluttered.
The room was so overcluttered that it was difficult to move around.  这个房间太乱了，很难走动。
Her desk was overcluttered with papers, pens, and various knick-knacks. 她的桌子上堆满了纸张、钢笔和各种小玩意儿。
近义词: cluttered, crammed, overflowing, congested
反义词: tidy, organized, neat, minimalist
词性: adjective
