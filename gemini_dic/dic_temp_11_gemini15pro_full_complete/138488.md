novena:/noʊˈviːnə/| n.|九日敬礼；九日祈祷|A series of prayers or religious services held for nine consecutive days, especially in the Roman Catholic Church.|例句：She attended a novena for her ailing grandmother. 她参加了一个为她生病的祖母举行的九日祈祷。

例句：The family said a novena for the safe return of their son. 这家人为儿子平安归来念了九日经。|近义词：devotion, prayer|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a religious practice)|词性:noun
