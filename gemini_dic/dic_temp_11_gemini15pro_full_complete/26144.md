bustlingly:/ˈbʌslɪŋli/| adv.|热闹地，熙熙攘攘地|In a busy and energetic way, full of activity.|The market was bustlingly full of people buying and selling goods.  市场熙熙攘攘，挤满了买卖货物的人。|Example: The bees flew bustlingly around the hive. 蜜蜂在蜂巢周围忙碌地飞来飞去。|Synonyms: busily, actively, energetically|Antonyms: quietly, calmly, peacefully|Word Type: Adverb

bustle:/ˈbʌsl/| n. vi.|喧闹，熙攘；忙碌|1.(n.) Energetic and noisy activity. 2.(vi.) To move in an energetic or noisy manner.|1.(n.) The bustle of the city streets. 城市街道的喧嚣。 2.(vi.) She bustled about the kitchen, preparing dinner. 她在厨房里忙着准备晚餐。|Synonyms: activity, commotion, hustle|Antonyms: stillness, quiet, calm|Word Type: Noun, Verb
