dundees:/dʌnˈdiːz/
noun
邓迪人（Dundee 居民）
People from Dundee, Scotland.
Dundee is known for its jute, jam, and journalism, and its people, the Dundees, are proud of their city. 邓迪以黄麻、果酱和新闻业闻名，邓迪人以他们的城市为荣。
The Dundees have a strong sense of community. 邓迪人有很强的社区意识。
Synonym:Dundeonians
Antonym:N/A
词性:n.
