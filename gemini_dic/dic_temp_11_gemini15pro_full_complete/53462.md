A foolish or stupid person.
She called him a disard for believing such a ridiculous story. 她称他为傻瓜，因为他相信了这样一个荒谬的故事。
近义词：fool, idiot, dunce, simpleton, nincompoop
反义词：genius, sage, savant
词性：名词


词源:
From Middle French *disard, from Old French dis- (“lacking, without”) + ard (“bold, hardy”).  The original meaning was "lacking boldness," hence "timid, foolish."
