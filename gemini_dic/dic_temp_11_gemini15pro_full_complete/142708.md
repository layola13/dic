otherworldly:/ˌʌðərˈwɜːrldli/
adj.
超凡脱俗的；空灵的；非尘世的
relating to an imaginary or spiritual world; not of this world; ethereal
例句：The music had an otherworldly quality.  这音乐有一种超凡脱俗的特质。

例句：She has an otherworldly beauty. 她拥有一种空灵的美。

近义词：unearthly, supernatural, ethereal, spiritual, transcendent
反义词：worldly, mundane, earthly, terrestrial
词性: adjective
