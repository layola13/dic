libelluloid:/ˌlɪbəˈluːlɔɪd/| adj. |蜻蜓状的，类似蜻蜓的|Resembling or characteristic of a dragonfly; dragonfly-like.|The insect's wings had a libelluloid appearance.  这种昆虫的翅膀外观像蜻蜓。|Synonyms: dragonfly-like, similar to a dragonfly|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could use something like "dissimilar to a dragonfly")|adjective

libelluloid:/ˌlɪbəˈluːlɔɪd/| n. |类似蜻蜓的昆虫|An insect resembling a dragonfly.|The entomologist discovered a new species of libelluloid. 昆虫学家发现了一种新的类似蜻蜓的昆虫。|Synonyms: dragonfly-like insect|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun


Etymology: From Latin *libellula* (dragonfly) + *-oid* (resembling).
