lithotrity: /lɪˈθɒtrɪti/ (US) /ˈlɪθəˌtraɪti/ (UK)| n. | 碎石术; 碎石; |The medical procedure of crushing stones in the bladder or kidney. |例句：The patient underwent lithotripy to remove kidney stones.  病人接受了碎石术来去除肾结石。

例句：Lithotripy is a less invasive alternative to surgery for treating kidney stones.  碎石术是治疗肾结石的一种比手术创伤更小的方法。|近义词：lithotripsy, stone crushing |反义词: None |词性: Noun


Etymology:
From French *lithotripsie*, from litho- + -tripsy "crushing".  litho- comes from Greek *lithos* "stone". -tripsy comes from Greek *tripsis*  "a rubbing, wearing".
