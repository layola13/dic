marketer:/ˈmɑːrkɪtər/ (US) /ˈmɑːkɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |营销人员；市场商人|A person or company that markets goods or services.|The company is a leading marketer of organic products. 这家公司是有机产品的领先销售商。|Example: The experienced marketer developed a successful advertising campaign.  这位经验丰富的营销人员策划了一场成功的广告活动。|Synonyms:  salesperson, advertiser, promoter, distributor|Antonyms: consumer, buyer, purchaser|noun
