carjacking:/ˈkɑːrdʒækɪŋ/
n.
劫车，汽车抢劫
The act of stealing a car by force from its driver.
A man was arrested for carjacking after he forced a woman out of her car at gunpoint. 一个男人因持枪抢劫一名妇女的汽车而被捕。
近义词：auto theft, robbery, hijacking
反义词: (没有直接的反义词)
词性:名词
