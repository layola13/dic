kick-started:/ˌkɪkˈstɑːrtɪd/
verb
启动，开始，促进
To cause something to start quickly or suddenly, often a process or project.
The government's investment kick-started the economy. 政府的投资启动了经济。
The new advertising campaign kick-started sales.  新的广告活动促进了销售。
Synonym: jump-start, boost, launch, initiate, begin, start
Antonym: halt, stop, hinder, obstruct
词性:动词

kick-start:/ˈkɪkˌstɑːrt/
noun
启动，促进
The act of kick-starting something.
The project needed a kick-start to get it going.  这个项目需要一个启动来使其进行下去。
The tax cuts provided a kick-start for the economy. 减税为经济提供了推动力。
Synonym: boost, impetus, stimulus, jump-start
Antonym: hindrance, obstacle
词性:名词
