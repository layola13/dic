nonartists:/ˌnɑːnˈɑːrtɪsts/
n.
非艺术家；非艺术工作者
People who are not artists, lacking artistic skills or training.
The gallery show was surprisingly well-attended, even by nonartists.  这个画廊展览出人意料地吸引了很多人，甚至包括一些非艺术家。
synonyms:laypeople, amateurs, non-professionals
antonyms:artists, professionals, experts
词性:名词
