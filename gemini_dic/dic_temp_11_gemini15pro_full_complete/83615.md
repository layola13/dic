haematoxylon:/ˌhiːməˈtɒksɪlən/ /ˌheməˈtɑːksɪlən/| n. |苏木，苏方木，洋苏木|A tropical tree (Haematoxylon campechianum) of the legume family, the heartwood of which is a source of a red dye, hematoxylin.|The haematoxylon stain highlighted the nuclei of the cells. 苏木精染液突出了细胞核。|Synonyms: logwood, bloodwood, campeachy wood|Antonyms: N/A|noun

haematoxylin:/ˌhiːməˈtɒksɪlɪn/ /ˌheməˈtɑːksɪlɪn/| n. |苏木精（一种红色染料）|A reddish crystalline compound, C16H14O6, obtained from logwood and used as a biological stain and as an indicator.|Haematoxylin and eosin (H&E) stain is commonly used in histology. 苏木精和伊红 (H&E) 染色剂常用于组织学。|Synonyms: N/A|Antonyms: N/A|noun
