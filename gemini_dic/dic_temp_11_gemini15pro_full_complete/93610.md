imbedding:/ɪmˈbɛdɪŋ/ | n. |嵌入，埋置|The act of embedding something, or the state of being embedded.  The process of fixing something firmly and deeply in a surrounding mass.|The archaeologist found an imbedding of ancient coins in the rock. 考古学家在岩石中发现了一处古代硬币的嵌入物。|Synonyms: embedding, insertion, implanting|Antonyms: extraction, removal|noun

imbed:/ɪmˈbɛd/ | vt. |嵌入，埋置|To fix something firmly and deeply in a surrounding mass.|She imbedded the jewels in the wax. 她把珠宝嵌入蜡中。|Synonyms: embed, insert, implant|Antonyms: extract, remove|verb
