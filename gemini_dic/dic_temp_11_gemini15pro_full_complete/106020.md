knead:/niːd/
verb
揉; 捏; 按摩
to work dough or clay into a uniform mixture by pressing, folding, and stretching it
例句：
She kneaded the dough for ten minutes. 她揉面团揉了十分钟。

He kneaded the clay into a smooth ball. 他把黏土揉成一个光滑的球。

近义词：massage, work, mold, shape
反义词：flatten, smooth (in some contexts)
词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cnedan, from Proto-Germanic *knedaną, from Proto-Indo-European *gn̥ʰedʰ- (“to rub, press”). Cognate with Old Frisian kneda (“to knead”), Middle Dutch kneden (“to knead”), German kneten (“to knead”), Old Norse knoda (“to knot, twist together”), Albanian gdhend (“I carve, engrave”).
