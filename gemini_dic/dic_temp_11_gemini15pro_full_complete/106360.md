koloff:/ˈkɑːlɔːf/ | n. | 科洛夫（一种摔跤动作）| A wrestling hold in which the wrestler puts his or her head under the opponent's arm and lifts the opponent up and over, usually slamming them to the mat. |  He finished the match with a devastating koloff. 他以一个毁灭性的科洛夫动作结束了比赛。 |  Example 2: The wrestler surprised everyone by using a koloff, a move he rarely performs.  这位摔跤手用了一个科洛夫动作让所有人大吃一惊，这是他很少做的一个动作。| 近义词:  piledriver, suplex (though these are technically different moves) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: Named after Ivan Koloff, a professional wrestler who popularized the move.
