manslaughtering:/ˈmænˌslɔːtərɪŋ/
|v.| 屠杀; 过失杀人|The act of unlawfully killing another human being without malice aforethought.  It's often categorized as voluntary (in the heat of passion) or involuntary (due to negligence).| He was charged with manslaughtering his neighbor after a heated argument.  他与邻居发生激烈争吵后被指控过失杀人。|近义词:killing, slaying|反义词:saving, rescuing|词性:动词(gerund or present participle)
