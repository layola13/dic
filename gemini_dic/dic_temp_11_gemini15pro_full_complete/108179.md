latterkin:/ˈlætərˌkɪn/| n. |动物系/种族系|A person who identifies spiritually or psychologically as non-human, typically an animal.  A subculture that involves identifying with a particular animal species. |She identifies as latterkin, believing she has the soul of a wolf. 她认为自己有狼的灵魂，自认为是动物系。|近义词：therian, otherkin |反义词:human |词性:noun
