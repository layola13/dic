ditheist:/ˈdaɪθiːɪst/
n.
二神论者；相信两种神的人
A person who believes in two gods, typically a good god and an evil god.
He identified himself as a ditheist, believing in both a benevolent creator and a destructive force. 他自认为是二神论者，既相信仁慈的造物主，也相信破坏性的力量。
近义词:dualist
反义词:monotheist, polytheist
词性:noun

ditheism:/ˈdaɪθiːɪzəm/
n.
二神论；二神教
The belief in two gods, typically a good god and an evil god.
Ditheism offers an explanation for the presence of both good and evil in the world. 二神论解释了世界上善与恶的存在。
近义词:dualism
反义词:monotheism, polytheism
词性:noun

ditheistic:/ˌdaɪθiˈɪstɪk/
adj.
二神论的；二神教的
Relating to or characterized by ditheism.
Their cosmology was ditheistic, incorporating two opposing deities. 他们的宇宙观是二神论的，包含了两个对立的神。
近义词:dualistic
反义词:monotheistic, polytheistic
词性:adjective
