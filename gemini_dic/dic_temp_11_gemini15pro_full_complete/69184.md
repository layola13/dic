fazed:/feɪzd/| adj. | 慌乱的，不安的，扰乱的 | disturbed, disconcerted, perturbed; usually used in the negative *not fazed* |  The loud noise didn't faze her.  大声的噪音并没有让她感到不安。| synonyms: disturbed, bothered, unsettled | antonyms: unperturbed, calm, composed | adjective

faze:/feɪz/| v. |  打扰，使不安，使慌乱 | to disturb or disconcert someone | The pressure didn't faze him at all. 压力完全没有让他感到不安。 | synonyms: disturb, bother, unsettle | antonyms: reassure, comfort, calm | verb
