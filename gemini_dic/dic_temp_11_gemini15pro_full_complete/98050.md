inpatient:/ˈɪnˌpeɪʃənt/ | n. adj. |住院病人；住院的|A person who stays in a hospital while receiving treatment.  As an adjective, it describes treatment or care given in a hospital. | My grandmother is an inpatient at the local hospital. 我的祖母是当地医院的住院病人。| The hospital offers both inpatient and outpatient services. 这家医院提供住院和门诊服务。|Synonyms: hospitalized person, resident (n.); residential (adj.)|Antonyms: outpatient (n. adj.)| noun, adjective
