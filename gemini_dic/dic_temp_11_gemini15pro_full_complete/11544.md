aristarch:/ˈɑːrɪstɑːrk/| n.|批评家；权威；通晓文学或艺术的人|A severe critic or a person who holds strong opinions, especially about literary or artistic matters.  Derived from Aristarchus of Samothrace, a renowned scholar and critic of Homeric poetry.|例句：He was considered an aristarch of modern literature. 他被认为是现代文学的权威。|例句：The aristarch dismissed the young artist's work as derivative and uninspired.  这位批评家认为这位年轻艺术家的作品是模仿的，缺乏灵感。|近义词：critic, connoisseur, pundit, expert|反义词：philistine, ignoramus|词性:noun
