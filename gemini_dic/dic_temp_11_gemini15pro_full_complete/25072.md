bubonic:/bjuːˈbɒnɪk/ /buːˈbɑːnɪk/| adj.|腺鼠疫的，腹股沟腺炎的|Relating to or denoting bubonic plague, which is characterized by painful swellings of the lymph nodes (buboes).|例句：The bubonic plague devastated Europe in the 14th century.  十四世纪，腺鼠疫肆虐欧洲。

例句：Bubonic plague is still a concern in some parts of the world.  在世界某些地区，腺鼠疫仍然是一个令人担忧的问题。|近义词：pestilential, contagious|反义词: healthy, non-contagious|词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *bubonicus, from Greek boubōn 'groin', influenced by Latin bubo 'owl'.  来自晚期拉丁语 *bubonicus，源自希腊语 boubōn “腹股沟”，受拉丁语 bubo “猫头鹰”的影响。
