mossed:/mɒst/ | adj. | 生满苔藓的；苔藓覆盖的 | Covered with moss; mossy. | The old stone wall was mossed and crumbling.  古老的石墙上长满了苔藓，正在 crumbling。 | 近义词: mossy, overgrown | 反义词: bare, clean | 词性:形容词

Etymology: From moss + -ed,  the past participle ending used to form adjectives.  "Moss" comes from Old English *mos, from Proto-Germanic *musan-, of uncertain origin.
