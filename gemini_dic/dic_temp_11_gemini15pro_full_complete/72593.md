fly-by-night:/ˈflaɪ baɪ naɪt/| adj. n.|不负责任的，不可靠的；骗子，皮包公司|A fly-by-night company or person is one that is not reputable and likely to disappear or cheat people, especially to avoid paying debts.|例句：Beware of fly-by-night operators who offer deals that sound too good to be true.  小心那些提出好得令人难以置信的交易的皮包公司。|例句：They set up a fly-by-night operation selling fake designer goods. 他们建立了一个出售假冒名牌商品的欺诈性公司。|近义词：dishonest, unreliable, untrustworthy, shady, unscrupulous|反义词：reliable, trustworthy, reputable, dependable|词性：形容词, 名词

fly-by-night:/ˈflaɪ baɪ naɪt/| n.|骗子，皮包公司|A person or company that is dishonest and likely to disappear without paying debts.|例句：He was a fly-by-night who skipped town after cheating several people. 他是个骗子，在骗了几个人之后就逃离了小镇。|例句：The investment opportunity turned out to be a fly-by-night scheme.  这个投资机会原来是个皮包公司的骗局。|近义词：con artist, fraudster, swindler|反义词：reputable businessman, trustworthy entrepreneur|词性：名词
