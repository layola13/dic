lacertilia: /ˌlæsərˈtɪliə/ | n. | 蜥蜴亞目 |  A suborder of reptiles that includes lizards.  It is characterized by scaly skin, four legs (in most species), external ear openings, and movable eyelids. | Lizards, members of the Lacertilia suborder, are found in diverse habitats around the world. 蜥蜴，蜥蜴亚目的成员，分布在世界各地的不同栖息地。|  synonyms: Sauria | antonyms: None (as it's a taxonomic classification) | Noun
