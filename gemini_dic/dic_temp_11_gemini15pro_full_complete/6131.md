amiable:/ˈeɪmiəbl/| adj.|和蔼可亲的；友善的|Having or displaying a friendly and pleasant manner.|She has an amiable personality and is always ready to help. 她性格和蔼可亲，总是乐于助人.|近义词：friendly, affable, agreeable, pleasant, good-natured|反义词：unfriendly, disagreeable, hostile, unpleasant|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French amiable, from Late Latin amicabilis (“friendly”), from Latin amicus (“friend”) + -abilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning “capable of; worthy of; tending to”).
