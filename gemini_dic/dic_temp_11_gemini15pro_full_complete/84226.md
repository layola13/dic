half-sections:/ˌhæf ˈsɛkʃənz/ | n. | 半段；半部分 |  A half-section is one half of a section, typically referring to land division, especially in surveying and agriculture.  A section is usually a square mile. | The farmer planted wheat on one half-section and soybeans on the other.  这个农民在一块半段土地上种植小麦，在另一块上种植大豆。| synonyms: halves, parts, portions | antonyms: whole, entirety | noun

half:/hæf/ | n. adj. adv. | 一半；半个 | One of two equal parts of a whole. | I ate half the pizza. 我吃了一半的披萨。 | synonyms: moiety, one-half | antonyms: whole, double | noun/adjective/adverb
