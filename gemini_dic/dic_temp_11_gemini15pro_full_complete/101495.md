irisate:/ˈɪrɪseɪt/ /ˈaɪrɪseɪt/
adjective
彩虹色的，闪光的
displaying or resembling the colours of the rainbow.
The oil slick on the water irisated in the sunlight.  水面上的油污在阳光下呈现彩虹色。
The peacock's feathers are beautifully irisated.  孔雀的羽毛色彩斑斓。
近义词: iridescent, opalescent, shimmering, glittering
反义词: dull, drab, matte
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *irisatus, from iris (rainbow) + -atus (suffix forming adjectives).
