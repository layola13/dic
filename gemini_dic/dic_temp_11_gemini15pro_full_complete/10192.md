aposiopesis:/ˌæpəʊsaɪəˈpiːsɪs/| n. |  省略, 顿语, 欲言又止 | A sudden breaking off in speech, as if from inability or unwillingness to proceed.  |  Example:  "If you do that again..." (aposiopesis)  "如果你再那样做..."(欲言又止)


Example: His voice trailed off into aposiopesis, leaving the threat unspoken but clearly understood. 他的声音逐渐减弱，最终欲言又止，虽然威胁没有说出口，但大家都心知肚明。 | Synonym: ellipsis, interruption, break | Antonym: continuation, completion | noun
