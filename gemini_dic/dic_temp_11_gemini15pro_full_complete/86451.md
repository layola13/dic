heftiest:/ˈhɛftiɪst/| adj.|最重的，最大的，最健壮的|The heftiest wrestler won the match.  最重的摔跤选手赢得了比赛。|例句：The company faced its heftiest losses in a decade.  这家公司面临着十年来最严重的亏损。|近义词：heaviest, largest, bulkiest, most substantial|反义词：lightest, smallest, slightest|词性:形容词
词源: From hefty + -est (superlative suffix). Hefty itself comes from Old Norse *hepti  meaning “weight,” “importance,” related to *hefja "to lift."
