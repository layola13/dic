knobkerrie:/ˈnɒbkəri/ (UK), /ˈnɑːbkəri/ (US)| n. |短柄棒; 棍棒; |A short wooden club with a knobbed head, used as a weapon, especially in southern Africa.|  The warrior brandished his knobkerrie.  战士挥舞着他的短柄棒。| 近义词: club, cudgel, bludgeon | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "shield" as a defensive item) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Afrikaans *knopkierie*, from Dutch *knop* ("knob") + *kierie* ("cane, stick").
