channel-hop:/ˈtʃænəl hɒp/ (UK) /ˈtʃænəl hɑːp/ (US)| v.|换台，快速转换电视频道|To quickly switch between television channels, typically using a remote control, often in search of something more interesting.|He spent the evening channel-hopping, unable to find anything good to watch. 他一晚上都在换台，找不到什么好看的节目。|近义词：channel-surf, flick, zap|反义词: watch attentively, focus on one channel |词性: 动词
