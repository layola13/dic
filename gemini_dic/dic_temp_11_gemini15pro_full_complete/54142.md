disengagednesses:/ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒdnɪsɪz/
|n.|脱离，冷漠，不参与|The state of being disengaged; lack of involvement or emotional connection.|His disengagedness from the project worried his colleagues.他对这个项目的漠不关心让他的同事们感到担忧。|近义词：detachment, indifference, apathy|反义词：engagement, involvement, commitment|词性：名词

Etymology:
From disengage + -ness.  Disengage comes from the Old French desengager, from des- (reversal, removal) + engager (to pledge, to engage).
