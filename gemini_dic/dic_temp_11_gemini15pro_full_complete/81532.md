gravitational:/ˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃənəl/
adjective
重力的；引力的
relating to or resulting from the force of gravity.
例句：The gravitational pull of the moon affects the tides. 月球的引力影响潮汐。

例句：Gravitational waves were detected for the first time in 2015. 引力波在2015年首次被探测到。

近义词：gravity-related
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gravitatem (nominative gravitas) "weight, heaviness, pressure," from gravis "heavy" + -tional, a suffix forming adjectives from nouns ending in -tion.
