mamillion:/məˈmɪljən/ |n.| 百万; 极大数量 |A mamillion is an informal and exaggerated way of saying a very large number, similar to a zillion or a gazillion.|  There were a mamillion people at the concert last night. 昨晚的音乐会人山人海。| 近义词: million, zillion, gazillion, a lot, tons | 反义词: few, little | 词性: 名词

mamillion:/məˈmɪljən/ |adj.|  极多的，无数的 |Used informally to describe a very large, indefinite quantity.| I've told you a mamillion times to clean your room! 我已经跟你说了无数遍要打扫你的房间！| 近义词: numerous, countless, innumerable| 反义词: few, scarce| 词性: 形容词


词源:  "Mamillion" is not a formally recognized word. It's a humorous, informal exaggeration of "million," likely influenced by words like "zillion" and "gazillion," which are also informal terms for very large, unspecified numbers.
