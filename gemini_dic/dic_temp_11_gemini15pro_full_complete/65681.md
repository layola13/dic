evener's:/ˈiːvənərz/| n.| 均衡器；平地机| belonging to or associated with an evener (a device used to distribute weight evenly, especially in horse-drawn equipment).  It refers to something that belongs to or is related to an evener.  | The evener's main function was broken. 这个均衡器的主要功能坏了。 |Synonym: leveler's, equalizer's | Antonym:  unevener's | noun
evener's:/ˈiːvənərz/| adj.| 平衡的，均匀的 |  having the qualities of an evener; distributing weight evenly.  While less common, "evener's" can sometimes be used adjectivally to describe something that levels or equalizes. | The evener's mechanism kept the load balanced. 均衡器的机制保持了负载平衡。 |Synonym: leveling, equalizing | Antonym:  uneven, unbalanced | adjective
