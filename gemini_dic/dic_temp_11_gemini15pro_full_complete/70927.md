firebrand:/ˈfaɪərˌbrænd/| n.|煽动者，燃烧的木块|a piece of wood that is burning or is still hot after a fire; someone who causes trouble or violence, or a powerful idea or speech that causes people to feel strong emotions|The revolutionary was a firebrand, inspiring others to fight for freedom. 这位革命者是一位煽动者，激励其他人为自由而战。|synonyms: agitator, rabble-rouser, incendiary, troublemaker|antonyms: peacemaker, mediator, conciliator|noun

firebrand:/ˈfaɪərˌbrænd/| n.|燃烧的木块|a piece of wood that is burning or is still hot after a fire|Embers and firebrands glowed in the hearth. 余烬和燃烧的木块在壁炉里发出红光。|synonyms: ember, burning wood|antonyms: extinguished ember, ash|noun
