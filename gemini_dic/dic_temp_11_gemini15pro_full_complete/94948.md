in reality:/ɪn riˈælɪti/ | adv. | 实际上，事实上 | In reality, the situation is much more complex.  实际上，情况要复杂得多。| The film portrays a fictional world, but in reality, such places do not exist.  这部电影描绘了一个虚构的世界，但实际上，这样的地方并不存在。 | 近义词: actually, in fact, practically, truly | 反义词: theoretically, hypothetically, ideally | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *in realitee*, borrowed from Anglo-Norman and Old French *en realité*  ("in reality"), from Late Latin *in realitate*, from Latin *realis* ("real") + *-itate* (suffix forming abstract nouns).
