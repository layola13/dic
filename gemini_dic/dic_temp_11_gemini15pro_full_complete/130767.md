nonanalogously:/ˌnɒnənˈæləɡəsli/ | adv. | 非类比地，不同样地 | In a way that is not analogous; dissimilarly.  This means that something is happening or being done in a way that doesn't follow a similar pattern or comparison.  It emphasizes the difference in approach or outcome. | The two cases were handled nonanalogously, leading to different outcomes. 这两个案例处理方式不同，导致了不同的结果。 |  The twins, despite their identical appearance, reacted nonanalogously to the news.  这对双胞胎，尽管外表相同，但对这个消息的反应却截然不同。 | 近义词: differently, dissimilarly, distinctively | 反义词: analogously, similarly | 词性: 副词
