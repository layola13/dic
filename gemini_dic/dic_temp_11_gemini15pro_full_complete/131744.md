nonconformist:/ˌnɒnkənˈfɔːmɪst/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈfɔːrmɪst/
n. adj.
不墨守成规的人；非国教徒
a person who does not conform to prevailing ideas or established practice in behavior or thought; a Protestant who did not conform to the Church of England
例句：He was a nonconformist in his dress and manners. 他在衣着和举止上是个不墨守成规的人。
近义词：rebel, maverick, dissenter, individualist, freethinker
反义词：conformist, traditionalist
词性：名词, 形容词

nonconformity:/ˌnɒnkənˈfɔːməti/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈfɔːrməti/
n.
不墨守成规；不遵从
failure or refusal to conform to a prevailing rule or practice
例句：Her nonconformity made her an outcast. 她的不墨守成规使她成了一个被排斥的人。
近义词：dissidence, rebellion, unconventionality
反义词：conformity, obedience
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + conformist, from conform + -ist.  Ultimately from Latin *conformare "to form together," from com- "together" + formare "to form."
