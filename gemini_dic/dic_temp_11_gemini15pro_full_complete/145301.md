overbreed:/ˌoʊvərˈbriːd/| v. |过度繁殖；过量育种|To breed animals or plants in excessive numbers, often leading to undesirable characteristics or health problems.|  They overbred their dogs, resulting in many puppies with health issues. 他们过度繁殖他们的狗，导致许多小狗有健康问题。|The farmer overbred his prize-winning bull, weakening the bloodline.  这位农民过度繁殖了他的获奖公牛，削弱了血统。| 近义词:overproduce|反义词:underbreed|词性:动词
