lentiscus:/lɛnˈtɪskəs/
n.
mastic tree; mastic
A small evergreen tree (Pistacia lentiscus) of the cashew family, native to the Mediterranean region, yielding a fragrant resin.
The lentiscus provides a resin used in varnishes.
近义词: mastic tree, Pistacia lentiscus
词性: noun
