aciculiform:/əˈsɪkjʊlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
针状的
Resembling a needle; slender and pointed.
The plant had aciculiform leaves, making it difficult to handle.  这种植物的叶子呈针状，很难处理。
The cactus's aciculiform spines protect it from predators. 仙人掌的针状刺保护它免受捕食者的侵害。
Synonyms: needle-shaped, needle-like, acicular
Antonyms: broad, wide, rounded
