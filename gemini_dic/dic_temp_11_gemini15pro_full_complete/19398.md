bicaudal:/baɪˈkɔːdəl/
adjective
双尾的，两尾的
Having two tails, or resembling two tails.
A bicaudal mutation in fruit flies results in larvae with two posterior ends. 果蝇的双尾突变导致幼虫有两个后端。
The comet appeared bicaudal as it passed behind the sun. 这颗彗星在经过太阳后面时，出现了双尾的景象。
synonym: two-tailed, double-tailed
antonym: single-tailed, monocaudal
词性: 形容词
