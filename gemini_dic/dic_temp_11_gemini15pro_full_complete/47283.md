de-democratizing:/diː.dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪŋ/
| adj. |去民主化的；削弱民主的|The process of weakening or undermining democratic institutions, practices, or principles.| The authoritarian regime is actively de-democratizing the country by suppressing dissent and limiting free speech.  这个独裁政权正在积极地压制异议，限制言论自由，从而使国家去民主化。|近义词: undemocratic, authoritarian, totalitarian |反义词: democratizing, liberalizing| verb (present participle)


de-democratizing:/diː.dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪŋ/
| n. |去民主化|The act or process of de-democratizing.| The de-democratizing of the nation was met with international condemnation.  这个国家的去民主化遭到了国际社会的谴责。|近义词: backsliding, regression |反义词: democratization, progress| noun
