eisteddfod:/aɪˈstɛðvɒd/ (UK), /aɪˈstɛdfɑːd/ (US)| n. |音乐节，诗歌比赛会，(尤指威尔士的)|A Welsh festival of literature, music, and performance, including competitions.|例句：The National Eisteddfod of Wales is a major cultural event. 威尔士国家诗歌音乐节是一项重要的文化盛事。

例句：She won first prize in the singing competition at the local eisteddfod. 她在当地诗歌音乐节的歌唱比赛中获得了一等奖。|近义词：festival, competition|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology: From Welsh *eistedd- (sitting) + bod (being). Literally "a sitting".
