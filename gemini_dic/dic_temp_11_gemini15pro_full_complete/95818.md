incorporated: /ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtɪd/
adjective
已注册成立的；包含在内的
Combined into one body or whole; formed into a legal corporation.
Example: The incorporated company is now publicly traded. 这家注册成立的公司现在是上市公司。

Example:  All suggestions have been incorporated in the new plan. 所有建议都已纳入新计划中。

Synonym: integrated, included, combined, merged, consolidated
Antonym: separate, excluded, unincorporated

Etymology: From Late Latin incorporatus, past participle of incorporare "to embody," from in- "in" + corpus "body."

词性: adjective
