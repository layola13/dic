gymkhana:/dʒɪmˈkɑːnə/ | n. |  体育比赛，运动会，汽车技巧赛 | A public meeting or social gathering, especially in India, featuring or accompanied by sports and athletic competitions.  Also, a meet featuring motor vehicles in tests of skill, such as precision driving or timed runs through an obstacle course. |  The local gymkhana is a popular annual event. 当地一年一度的运动会很受欢迎。

The gymkhana featured a challenging obstacle course for the drivers.  这次汽车技巧赛为车手们设置了具有挑战性的障碍赛道。 | synonyms: meet, competition, rally, contest | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider words like "inactivity," "rest," or "apathy" in certain contexts) | noun
