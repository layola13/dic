cribbage:/ˈkrɪbɪdʒ/| n.|克里比奇牌戏；婴儿床|A card game, traditionally for two players, in which cards are discarded into a separate hand called the crib, and points are scored for combinations of cards in hands and during play.|We played cribbage all evening. 我们整晚都在玩克里比奇。|Synonyms: card game|Antonyms: None|Noun


crib:/krɪb/| n.|婴儿床；食槽；小屋；剽窃；小抄|A small bed with high sides for a baby or young child.  Also, a manger, a stall for animals, a small dwelling, or an act of plagiarism/cheating.|The baby slept soundly in her crib. 婴儿在婴儿床里睡得很香。|Synonyms: cot, cradle (for baby bed); manger, stall (for animals); hut, shack (for dwelling); plagiarism, cheating (for academic dishonesty)|Antonyms: None|Noun
