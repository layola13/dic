heliotrope:/ˈhiːliəˌtroʊp/ (US), /ˈhiːliəˌtrəʊp/ (UK)| n. adj.| 香水草，天芥菜；淡紫色|A plant of the borage family, cultivated for its fragrant purple flowers.
