directorially:/ˌdɪrɛkˈtɔːriəli/
| adv. |指导地；导演地|In a manner relating to the work or style of a director.  Pertaining to the art or process of directing. |The film was directorially stunning, with innovative camera angles and a strong visual style. 这部电影在导演方面令人惊叹，具有创新的镜头角度和强烈的视觉风格。|近义词：artistically, creatively, skillfully|反义词：clumsily, amateurishly|词性:副词

词源:  From "director" + "-ial" + "-ly". "Director" ultimately comes from the Latin word "dirigere," meaning "to direct."
