celebration:/ˌsɛləˈbreɪʃən/| n.|庆祝，庆典|the action of marking one's pleasure at an important event or occasion by engaging in enjoyable, typically social, activity.|We held a celebration for his birthday. 我们为他举办了生日庆祝活动。|近义词：festivity, observance, commemoration|反义词:mourning, grieving|noun
