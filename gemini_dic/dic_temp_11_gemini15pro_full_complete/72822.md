foliaged:/ˈfoʊliɪdʒd/
adjective
叶茂盛的，覆盖着树叶的
covered with foliage; leafy
例句：The foliaged trees provided ample shade.  枝繁叶茂的树木提供了充足的阴凉。

例句：We walked through the foliaged path, enjoying the dappled sunlight. 我们走在绿树成荫的小路上，享受着斑驳的阳光。

近义词：leafy, verdant, lush
反义词：bare, leafless
词性: adjective
