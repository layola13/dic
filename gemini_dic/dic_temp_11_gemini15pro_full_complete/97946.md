innovator:/ˈɪnəveɪtər/| n.|创新者，革新者|A person who introduces new methods, ideas, or products.|例句：Steve Jobs was a true innovator in the tech industry. 史蒂夫·乔布斯是科技行业真正的创新者。

例句：The company rewards its employees for being innovators. 公司奖励有创新精神的员工。

近义词：pioneer, inventor, creator, developer|反义词：follower, traditionalist|词性：noun
