labourable:/ˈleɪbərəbl/ (UK) /ˈleɪbərəbəl/ (US)| adj.|费力的，艰苦的，需要大量劳动的|requiring considerable effort and time; difficult.|例句：The project was a long and labourable process. 这个项目是一个漫长而艰苦的过程。|例句：Writing a novel can be a labourable task, requiring immense dedication. 写小说可能是一项艰巨的任务，需要极大的奉献精神。|近义词：arduous, strenuous, toilsome, demanding, taxing|反义词：easy, effortless, simple|词性:形容词

词源:
From late Middle English: laborous, from Old French laborieux, from Latin laboriōsus ‘industrious, painstaking’, from labor ‘labour’.
