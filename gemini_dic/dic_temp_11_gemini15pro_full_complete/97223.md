inflatingly:/ɪnˈfleɪtɪŋli/
| adv. |膨胀地，夸张地|In a way that inflates or expands something, often excessively or beyond reasonable limits.  It can also refer to exaggerating something in a boastful or pompous manner.|He spoke inflatingly about his own achievements, making them seem far grander than they actually were. 他夸大其词地谈论自己的成就，使它们看起来比实际情况要宏伟得多。|Synonyms: expansively, exaggeratingly, boastfully, pompously|Antonyms: modestly, humbly, realistically, deflatingly|词性:副词
