what:/wɒt/(BrE) /wʌt/(AmE)| pron. det. adv.|什么，多么| Used to ask for information about something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|synonyms: which, how, why| antonyms:  N/A (Interrogative words don't typically have antonyms)| pronoun, determiner, adverb

what:/wɒt/(BrE) /wʌt/(AmE)| interj.|多么| Expressing surprise or strong feelings.| What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！|synonyms: how, such| antonyms: N/A| interjection

USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing over there? = 他在那里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she eaten for lunch? = 她午饭吃了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before making a decision? = 在做决定之前他们讨论了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 会议上他们会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for by the time you graduate? = 到你毕业的时候，你将已经学习什么多久了？
What will she have been doing for the past ten years? = 过去十年她一直在做什么？
