ambo:/ˈæmboʊ/ | n. |讲台，教堂里的读经台|A raised platform or pulpit in a church, from which scriptures are read and sermons are sometimes preached.|The priest delivered his sermon from the ambo.| 近义词: pulpit, lectern, rostrum | 反义词: pew, nave | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ambo, from Greek *ἄμβων (ambōn) "raised platform," perhaps related to *ἀναβαίνω (anabainō) "I go up, mount."
