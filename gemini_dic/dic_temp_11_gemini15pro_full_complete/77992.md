gentisin: /ˈdʒɛntɪsɪn/ | n. | 染料木素; 金雀异黄素 | A trihydroxyflavone found in the heartwood of several trees, such as the dyer's broom (Genista tinctoria). It is used as a mild antiseptic and in the treatment of gout. | Gentisin is extracted from the roots of the dyer's broom plant. 染料木素是从染料木植物的根部提取的。

Gentisin has shown promise in inhibiting the growth of certain cancer cells. 金雀异黄素在抑制某些癌细胞的生长方面显示出前景。| 近义词: luteolin (木犀草素), apigenin (芹菜素) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
