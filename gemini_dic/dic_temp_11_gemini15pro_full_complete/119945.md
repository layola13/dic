milky:/ˈmɪlki/| adj.|乳白色的，牛奶的，像牛奶的|Resembling milk in color or consistency; containing or yielding milk.|The milky way stretches across the night sky. 银河横跨夜空。|近义词:lacteal, milk-white|反义词:clear, transparent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *milki, from Old English *milcic, from Proto-Germanic *milkijaz.  Ultimately from the same root as milk.
