cantrip:/ˈkæntrɪp/| n.|戏法，小魔术；把戏|A magic spell, typically one that is relatively simple to cast and has a minor effect.  A playful or mischievous trick.|The wizard cast a simple cantrip to light his pipe.|近义词：trick, spell, charm, hex|反义词: -(Not applicable, as a cantrip is a specific type of magic, not a concept with a direct opposite)|noun
