judgy:/ˈdʒʌdʒi/| adj.|爱评判的，爱批评的|Inclined to judge others, often in a negative or critical way.|She gave me a judgy look when I ordered a second dessert. 她在我点第二份甜点时给了我一个评判的眼神。|Example: He's so judgy about everyone's clothes. 他对每个人的衣服都很挑剔。|Synonyms: critical, judgmental, censorious, disapproving|Antonyms: accepting, tolerant, open-minded|Word type: Adjective
