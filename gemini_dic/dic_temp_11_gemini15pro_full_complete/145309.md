overbright:/ˌoʊvərˈbraɪt/| adj.|过亮的，过于明亮的|Excessively bright; brighter than is desirable or pleasant.|The overbright headlights of the oncoming car blinded me momentarily.  迎面而来的汽车过亮的 headlights 让我一时失明。|近义词：too bright, excessively bright, glaring|反义词: dim, faint, dull|词性:形容词

Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "bright" (Old English beorht, from Proto-Germanic *berhtaz, meaning "shining, bright").  The prefix "over-" indicates an excess or surpassing of the usual degree of brightness.
