caters:/ˈkeɪtərz/ (US), /ˈkeɪtəz/ (UK)| v.| 迎合；提供饮食；满足|To provide food and drinks for a social event, or to satisfy a particular need or demand.| The restaurant caters for weddings and other special occasions.  这家餐厅承办婚礼和其他特殊场合的餐饮服务。

The magazine caters to a young, female audience. 这本杂志迎合年轻女性读者。|近义词：provides for, serves, accommodates, satisfies|反义词：neglects, ignores|词性: verb

caters (third-person singular simple present caters, present participle catering, simple past catered, past participle catered)


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Middle English *catour*, meaning "buyer of provisions," from Anglo-French *acatour*, from *acater*, "to buy," from *a-*, "to" + *cater*, "four" (as in buying in fours). The original sense referred to buying provisions, which evolved into providing food and then to satisfying needs generally.
