gobemouche:/ˈɡoʊbəmʊʃ/ | n. | 轻信的人；容易受骗的人 | A gullible person; one who readily believes anything. | He's such a gobemouche, he believes everything he reads online. 他真是个轻信的人，他在网上看到什么都信。 | Synonyms: dupe, gull, simpleton | Antonyms: skeptic, cynic | Noun

Etymology:
From French *gobe-mouche*, literally "fly-swallower."  *Gober* means "to swallow" and *mouche* means "fly."  The image is of someone so naive they would swallow anything, even a fly.
