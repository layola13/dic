nonservileness:/ˌnɒnsɜːˈvaɪlnəs/ /ˌnɑːnsərˈvaɪlnəs/
n.| 非奴性；不卑躬屈膝|The state or quality of not being servile; lack of servility.  It implies independence and self-respect.|He displayed a refreshing nonservileness in his dealings with the powerful CEO.|近义词：independence, self-respect, dignity, autonomy|反义词：servility, subservience, obsequiousness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + servileness.  Servileness comes from the Latin "servilis" (of or belonging to a slave),  from "servus" (slave).
