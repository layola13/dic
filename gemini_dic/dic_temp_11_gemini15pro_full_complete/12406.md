asiento:/ɑːˈsjɛntoʊ/ (US), /əˈsjɛntoʊ/ (UK)| n.|座位; 席位; (尤指飞机、火车或剧院的) 预订座位; (尤指汽车的) 座椅; (正式协议的) 条款, 规定|A place to sit, especially a designated seat in a vehicle, theater, or airplane; a clause or provision, especially in a formal agreement.|例句：I booked an aisle asiento on the plane. 我在飞机上订了一个靠过道的座位。|例句：The asiento between the two countries allowed for trade.  两国之间的协议允许贸易。|近义词：seat, place, position, clause, provision|反义词:standing (antonym for seat aspect)|noun
