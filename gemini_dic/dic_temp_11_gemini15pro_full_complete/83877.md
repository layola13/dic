half-afloat:/ˌhæf əˈfloʊt/
adj.
半漂浮的，部分漂浮的
Partially afloat; not completely sunk or submerged.
The damaged boat was half-afloat in the harbor after the storm.  暴风雨过后，受损的船只半漂浮在港口。
The raft was half-afloat, half-submerged. 木筏半漂半沉。
Near synonyms: partially afloat, awash
Antonyms: submerged, sunk
词性: 形容词
