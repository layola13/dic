irreverently:/ˌɪrˈɛvərəntli/| adv.|不敬地，无礼地|Showing a lack of respect for people or things that are generally taken seriously.|He spoke irreverently about the royal family. 他对皇室成员说话很不敬。|Synonyms: disrespectfully, impiously, blasphemously, profanely|Antonyms: respectfully, reverently, piously|Adverb
