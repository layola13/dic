kopje:/ˈkɒpi/ (UK), /ˈkɑːpi/ (US)| n. | 小山，圆丘；（尤指南非的）岩石小山 |A small hill, especially a rocky hill in Southern Africa. | We climbed the kopje to get a better view of the savanna. 我们爬上小山，以便更好地欣赏大草原的景色。| The kopje offered a strategic vantage point. 这座小山提供了一个战略制高点。| synonym: hill, hillock, knoll | antonym: valley, plain | noun

Etymology: From Afrikaans *koppie*, diminutive of *kop* (“head, hilltop”), from Middle Dutch *cop* (“head, top”), from Proto-Germanic *kuppaz* (“cup, bowl, head”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)keubh- (“to bend, curve”).
