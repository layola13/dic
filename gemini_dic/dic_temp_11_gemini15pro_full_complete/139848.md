octospermous:/ˌɒktəˈspɜːməs/
adjective

This octospermous fruit is a rare find.  这种八粒种子的果实很罕见。

Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider) single-seeded, few-seeded
Word Origin: octo- (eight) + spermous (seeded)
