outshrieks:/ˌaʊtˈʃriːks/
|v.|发出比...更大的尖叫声；盖过...的尖叫声|The child outshrieked his mother in the supermarket.  孩子在超市里尖叫声盖过了他母亲。|Synonyms: outyell, outscream|Antonyms: whisper, murmur|verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + shriek.  "Out-" prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed". "Shriek" meaning a loud, sharp, piercing cry.
