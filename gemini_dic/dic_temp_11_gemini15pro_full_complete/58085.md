droopingly:/ˈdruːpɪŋli/| adv.| 垂头丧气地，无精打采地 |In a drooping manner; with a drooping posture or appearance.  Characterized by hanging or sinking down. | Her head hung droopingly after she heard the bad news. 她听到坏消息后，垂头丧气地低下了头。| Synonyms: dejectedly, listlessly, sadly, despondently | Antonyms: perkily, cheerfully, energetically, vibrantly | Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From droop + -ing + -ly.  "Droop" comes from Old Norse *drupa "to droop, sink," from Proto-Germanic *drupan, of uncertain origin.
