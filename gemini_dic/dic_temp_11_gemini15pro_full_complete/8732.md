antidemocrat:/ˌæntiˈdɛməkræt/ | n. | 反民主者；反民主主义者 | Someone who is opposed to democracy. | He was labelled an antidemocrat for his views on limiting voting rights.  他因为限制投票权的观点而被贴上反民主主义者的标签。 | synonyms: authoritarian, totalitarian, fascist, dictator | antonyms: democrat, liberal, egalitarian | noun

antidemocratic:/ˌæntiˌdɛməˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 反民主的；非民主的 | Opposed to or violating the principles of democracy. |  The new law was seen as an antidemocratic measure. 这项新法律被视为一项反民主的措施。 | synonyms: undemocratic, authoritarian, totalitarian, oppressive | antonyms: democratic, liberal, egalitarian | adjective
