daher:/daˈheːɐ̯/ (German)| adv.|因此，所以，由此，从那里|Therefore, hence, from there, thence. It indicates a conclusion or consequence based on something previously mentioned, or indicates a place of origin.|例句：Er war krank und blieb daher zu Hause.  He was sick and therefore stayed at home.|近义词：deshalb, deswegen, folglich, somit|反义词: dennoch, trotzdem|词性:副词

daher:/dəˈhɛːr/ (anglicized pronunciation)| adv.|therefore, hence|Used less frequently in English, retaining its original German meaning.|例句：The evidence is compelling, and the conclusion follows daher. The evidence is compelling, and the conclusion follows therefore.|近义词：therefore, hence, thus, consequently|反义词: nevertheless, however|词性:副词
