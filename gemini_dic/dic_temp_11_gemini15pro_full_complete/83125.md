gustoso:/ɡuˈstoʊsoʊ/ | adj. | 美味的，可口的 |  Pleasant-tasting; savory.  Derived from the Italian word "gusto" meaning taste or flavor. Often used to describe food that is well-seasoned and enjoyable to eat. | The pasta was gustoso, with just the right amount of spice. 这道意大利面很美味，香料用量刚刚好。| synonyms: delicious, tasty, savory, flavorful, palatable | antonyms: bland, tasteless, unappetizing, insipid | adjective
