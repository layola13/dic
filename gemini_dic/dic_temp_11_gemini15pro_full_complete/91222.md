hydrocarbon:/ˈhaɪdrəʊˌkɑːbən/ (US) /ˈhaɪdroʊˌkɑːrbən/ (UK)| n. |碳氢化合物；烃|An organic compound containing only carbon and hydrogen. |例句：Methane is a simple hydrocarbon. 甲烷是一种简单的碳氢化合物。

例句：Petroleum is a complex mixture of hydrocarbons. 石油是碳氢化合物的复杂混合物。|近义词：organic compound|反义词:inorganic compound|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- + carbon.  "Hydro-" comes from Greek "hydōr" (water), referring to hydrogen's presence in water. "Carbon" refers to the element carbon.
