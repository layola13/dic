hocus-pocussing:/ˌhoʊkəs ˈpoʊkəsɪŋ/
noun
念咒语；变戏法；欺骗
The act of performing magic tricks, often involving sleight of hand and misleading words.
The magician was hocus-pocussing with a deck of cards. 魔术师正在用一副牌变戏法。
trickery, conjuring, deception
legitimacy, honesty
n.
