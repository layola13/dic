anarchical: /ænˈɑːkɪkəl/
adjective
无政府的；无政府主义的
Characterized by anarchy; lacking any guiding principles or hierarchical structure.
The country descended into anarchical chaos after the coup.  这个国家在政变后陷入了无政府状态的混乱。
Rioters created an anarchical situation in the city center.  暴徒在市中心制造了无政府状态。
近义词: lawless, chaotic, unruly, disorderly, turbulent
反义词: orderly, organized, structured, governed, controlled
词性: adjective

anarchy: /ˈænəki/
noun
无政府状态；混乱；无秩序
A state of disorder due to absence or nonrecognition of authority.
The sudden death of the king led to anarchy in the kingdom. 国王的突然去世导致王国陷入无政府状态。
The classroom was in a state of anarchy with the teacher absent.  老师不在，教室里一片混乱。
近义词: chaos, disorder, lawlessness, turmoil, pandemonium
反义词: order, control, government, law, organization
词性: noun

词源: From French *anarchique*, from Greek *anarkhia* "lack of a leader," from *an-* "without" + *arkhos* "leader."
