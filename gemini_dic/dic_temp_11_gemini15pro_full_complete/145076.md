overanimated:/ˌoʊvərˈænɪmeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度活跃的，过于兴奋的
Excessively animated; too lively or enthusiastic.
Her overanimated gestures made it difficult to focus on what she was saying. 她过于夸张的手势让人很难集中注意力听她说话。
The children were overanimated after eating too much candy. 孩子们吃了太多糖后变得过于兴奋。
近义词:  hyperactive, excitable, overexcited, boisterous
反义词:  calm, subdued, quiet, restrained
词性: adjective

overanimated:/ˌoʊvərˈænɪmeɪtɪd/
词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "animated" (adjective meaning "full of life or excitement")  源自"over-"（前缀，意为“过度地”）+ "animated"（形容词，意为“充满活力或兴奋的”）
