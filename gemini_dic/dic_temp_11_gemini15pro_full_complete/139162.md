obiism: /ˈoʊbiɪzəm/
n.
Obiism; 对腰带的过度喜爱或迷恋;  An excessive fondness or obsession with obis (sashes worn with traditional Japanese clothing).
He displayed a pronounced obiism, collecting hundreds of antique sashes. 他表现出明显的对腰带的喜爱，收藏了数百条古董腰带。
近义词:  fetishism, obsession
反义词: indifference, apathy
词性: noun

Etymology:  From "obi" (Japanese: 帯, sash) + "-ism" (suffix forming nouns denoting doctrines, beliefs, practices, or characteristics).  The word is a neologism and not widely established.  It is formed by analogy with words like "fetishism."
