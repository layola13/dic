envoy:/ˈɛnvɔɪ/ (US) /ˈɒnvɔɪ/ (UK)| n.|使节，特使；外交代表|A person delegated by a government or other authority to represent it in another country or to a foreign government.|The French envoy met with the president to discuss trade relations. 法国特使会见了总统，讨论贸易关系。|synonyms: ambassador, diplomat, representative, delegate, emissary|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "rebel," "dissident" in certain contexts depending on the envoy's mission.)|noun
