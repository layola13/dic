fabbo:/ˈfæboʊ/| adj.|极好的，很棒的|British slang, short for "fabulous."  It expresses strong approval or admiration.|例句：That new car is fabbo! 那辆新车真是太棒了！

例句：The food at the restaurant was fabbo.  这家餐馆的食物很棒。|近义词：fantastic, brilliant, excellent, wonderful, superb, marvelous, awesome, amazing|反义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, horrible|词性:形容词
词源:Clipped form of fabulous.  First recorded in the late 20th century.
