distillates:/ˈdɪstɪləts/ | n. | 蒸馏物，馏出物 |  A liquid produced by distilling, condensation of the vapor produced by heating a mixture. |  The distillates from crude oil include gasoline and kerosene. 原油的蒸馏物包括汽油和煤油。 | synonyms: product, extract, concentrate | antonyms: mixture, solution | noun


distillate: /ˈdɪstɪlɪt/ | n. | 精华，蒸馏物 | The essential part of something, purified or concentrated. |  The book is a distillate of years of research. 这本书是多年研究的精华。 | synonyms: essence, gist, summary | antonyms: whole, entirety | noun
