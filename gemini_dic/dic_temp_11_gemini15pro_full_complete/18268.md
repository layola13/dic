belittlement:/bɪˈlɪtlmənt/
|n.|轻视，贬低|The constant belittlement of her abilities made her lose confidence. 她能力不断地被贬低，使她失去了信心。|
|英文解析:The act of making someone or something seem unimportant.|
|例句:He resented the belittlement of his efforts by his superiors. 他对上司轻视他的努力感到愤慨。|

|例句:She suffered years of belittlement and abuse. 她遭受了多年的贬低和虐待。|

|近义词:disparagement, denigration, depreciation, minimization|
|反义词:praise, commendation, exaltation, aggrandizement|
|词性:noun|
