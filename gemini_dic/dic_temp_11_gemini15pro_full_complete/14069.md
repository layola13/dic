australian-aborigine:/əˈstreɪliən ˌæbəˈrɪdʒɪni/ | n. |澳大利亚原住民|A person who is a member of the indigenous peoples of mainland Australia or Tasmania.  |例句：The Australian Aborigine has a rich cultural heritage. 澳大利亚原住民有着丰富的文化遗产。

例句：Australian Aborigines have lived on the continent for tens of thousands of years.  澳大利亚原住民在这个大陆上已经生活了数万年。|近义词：indigenous Australian, Aboriginal Australian,  First Nations people (Canada), Native American (US - use with caution as this is a broad term), First Nations (more general term)|反义词: non-indigenous, settler |词性:noun
