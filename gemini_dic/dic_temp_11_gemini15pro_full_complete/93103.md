if:/ɪf/| conj.|如果；即使；是否|A conjunction introducing a conditional clause.|If it rains, we'll stay home. 如果下雨，我们就待在家里。|近义词：provided that, assuming that, in case, whether|反义词:unless|连词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gif (attested only in compounds such as *gifburh "conditional pledge"), from Proto-Germanic *jibai (source also of Old Frisian jef, Middle Dutch of, Dutch of, Old High German ibu, German ob "whether"), from PIE *yébʰ- (source also of Sanskrit yádi "if", Latin ibī "there, then, in that place", Old Irish ibaim "I refuse", imb "butter"). The original sense was "in case that, on condition that".
