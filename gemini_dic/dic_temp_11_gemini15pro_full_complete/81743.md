greenwash:/ˈɡriːnwɒʃ/ (UK) /ˈɡriːnwɑːʃ/ (US)| n. v. |漂绿；洗绿|Disinformation disseminated by an organization so as to present an environmentally responsible public image.|The company was accused of greenwashing its products to appeal to environmentally conscious consumers. 这家公司被指控为了吸引环保意识强的消费者而对产品进行漂绿宣传。|近义词：eco-posturing, green sheen|反义词：genuine environmentalism|词性: noun, verb


greenwash:/ˈɡriːnwɒʃ/ (UK) /ˈɡriːnwɑːʃ/ (US)| v. |漂绿；洗绿|To make (something, such as a product or policy) appear to be more environmentally friendly than it really is.|The oil company tried to greenwash its record by donating to environmental charities. 这家石油公司试图通过向环保慈善机构捐款来粉饰其环境记录。|近义词：eco-posture, green sheen|反义词：demonstrate true environmental concern|词性: verb
