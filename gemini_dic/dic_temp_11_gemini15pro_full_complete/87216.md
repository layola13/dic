hereinafter:/ˌhɪərɪnˈæftər/ | adv. | 此后，在下文中 | from here + in + after.  Meaning "from this point onward (in this document or statement)." | The party of the first part, hereinafter referred to as "the landlord," agrees to lease the premises to the party of the second part.  甲方（以下简称“房东”）同意将房屋出租给乙方。| hereafter, subsequently, from now on | previously, before, earlier | adverb
