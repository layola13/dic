mantissa:/mænˈtɪsə/| n.|尾数，假数|The mantissa of a logarithm is the fractional part. 对数的尾数是小数部分。|例句：The logarithm of 200 is 2.3010, where 2 is the characteristic and .3010 is the mantissa. 200的对数是2.3010，其中2是首数，.3010是尾数。

|例句：In scientific notation, the mantissa is the part of the number that is multiplied by the power of 10. 在科学记数法中，尾数是数字中乘以10的幂的部分。|近义词：fractional part, significand|反义词: characteristic|noun
