deliverers:/dɪˈlɪvərərz/
n.
递送员，送货员，交付者
People or businesses that deliver something, such as goods, services, or messages.
The deliverers dropped off the packages at the front door.  送货员把包裹放在了前门。
The deliverers of justice ensured the criminals were apprehended.  正义的执行者确保罪犯被逮捕。
synonyms: couriers, messengers, carriers, distributors
antonyms: recipients, receivers
