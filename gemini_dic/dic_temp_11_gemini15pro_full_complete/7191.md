angioscope: /ˈændʒiəˌskoʊp/
noun
血管镜；血管内窥镜
An instrument for examining the interior of blood vessels.
The angioscope allowed the surgeon to clearly visualize the blockage in the artery.  血管镜使外科医生能够清楚地看到动脉中的阻塞。
The cardiologist used an angioscope to assess the extent of the damage. 心脏病专家使用血管镜来评估损伤的程度。
Synonyms: endoscope (more general), fiberscope
Antonyms: N/A
