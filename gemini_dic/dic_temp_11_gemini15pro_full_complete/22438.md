bookest:/ˈbʊkɪst/
adjective
最爱读书的 (nonstandard, humorous or archaic)
This word is a humorous or archaic superlative form of "bookish," meaning fond of reading or studying. It is not considered standard English.
He's the bookest person I know, always with his nose in a book. 他是我认识的最爱读书的人，总是埋头苦读。
The bookest of the three sisters, Mary spent most of her time in the library. 三姐妹中最爱读书的玛丽大部分时间都在图书馆里度过。

近义词:bookish, studious, well-read
反义词:unintellectual, unscholarly
词性:adjective
