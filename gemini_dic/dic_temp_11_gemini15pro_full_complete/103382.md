jingoism:/ˈdʒɪŋɡoʊɪzəm/
n.
极端爱国主义，沙文主义，侵略主义
Extreme patriotism, especially in the form of aggressive or warlike foreign policy.
The government's rhetoric was seen by many as blatant jingoism. 很多人认为政府的言辞是露骨的沙文主义。

Jingoism is an extreme form of patriotism that often advocates for aggressive foreign policy.  侵略主义是一种极端的民族主义，经常鼓吹侵略性的外交政策。

近义词：chauvinism, nationalism, warmongering
反义词：pacifism, internationalism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "Jingo," a figure in a popular British music hall song of 1878, expressing a belligerent attitude toward Russia. 源于1878年一首英国流行音乐厅歌曲中的人物“Jingo”，表达了对俄罗斯的敌对态度。
