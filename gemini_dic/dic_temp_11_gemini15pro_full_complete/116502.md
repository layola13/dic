maternalism: /məˈtɜːrnəlɪzəm/ | n. | 母性主义，母爱主义 | The principle or practice of maternal instincts and attitudes considered as the ideal which should dominate society.  A public policy based on the belief that society would benefit from the extension of motherly care and concern to all people, particularly the vulnerable.  |  例句：The historian explored the rise of maternalism in the early 20th century.  这位历史学家探讨了20世纪早期母性主义的兴起。

例句：Maternalism was a powerful force in the Progressive Era. 母性主义在进步时代是一股强大的力量。| 近义词：motherhood, maternal instinct, nurturing, social welfare  | 反义词：paternalism, individualism | 词性：名词
