nautiform: /ˈnɔːtɪfɔːrm/
adjective
船形的；似船的
Shaped like a ship or boat.
The nautiform sculpture reminded him of his seafaring days.  这艘船形的雕塑让他想起了他的航海生涯。
The architect designed a nautiform building, its curved roof resembling a ship's hull.  建筑师设计了一座船形建筑，其弯曲的屋顶类似于船体。
近义词: ship-shaped, boat-shaped
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider amorphous, shapeless)
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *nauticus* (from *nauta* "sailor," from Greek *naútēs*) + *-formis* "having the form of."
