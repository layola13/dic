antiretroviral: /ˌæntiˈrɛtrəʊˈvaɪrəl/ /ˌæntiˈrɛtroʊˈvaɪrəl/ | adj. n. | 抗逆转录病毒的；抗逆转录病毒药物 |  Acting against or inhibiting the growth and reproduction of retroviruses, such as HIV. |  The doctor prescribed antiretroviral therapy for the patient. 医生给病人开了抗逆转录病毒疗法。 |  Antiretroviral drugs are crucial in managing HIV. 抗逆转录病毒药物对控制艾滋病毒至关重要。 | Synonyms: anti-HIV, antiviral | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "proviral") | adjective, noun
