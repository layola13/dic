mustangs:/ˈmʌstæŋz/
noun
野马（尤指美国西部的小型野马）
A small, hardy horse of the western US, descended from horses brought over by the Spanish.
Wild horses, known as mustangs, roamed freely across the plains.  野马，被称为野马，在平原上自由漫游。
The mustang is a symbol of the American West.  野马是美国西部的象征。
Synonyms: wild horse, bronco, cayuse
Antonyms: domesticated horse
