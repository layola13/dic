forgivenesses:/fərˈɡɪvnɪsɪz/
noun
宽恕，原谅（forgiveness的复数形式）
The act of granting pardon for an offense or debt; The state of being pardoned.
He asked for forgivenesses for his past mistakes. 他请求原谅他过去的错误。
Multiple forgivenesses were offered to those who confessed.  对那些坦白的人给予了多次宽恕。
synonyms: pardons, absolutions, remissions, reprieves
antonyms: condemnations, punishments, retributions
