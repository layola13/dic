overcarry:/ˌoʊvərˈkæri/ | v. | 过度携带，过量运输 | To carry or transport more than is necessary or allowed.  | Example: The airline charged us extra because we overcarried our baggage allowance.  航空公司向我们收取了额外费用，因为我们超出了行李限额。| Example:  They overcarried supplies to the remote outpost, and much of it went to waste. 他们向偏远的前哨运送了过多的补给，很多都浪费了。 | 近义词: overpack, overload | 反义词: underpack |词性:动词
