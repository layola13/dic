mock-at:/mɒkˈæt/ (UK) /mɑːkˈæt/ (US)| phrasal verb | 嘲笑，嘲弄 | To tease or make fun of someone or something, often by mimicking them in a scornful way. |  He mocked at her attempts to speak French. 他嘲笑她说法语的尝试。

The children mocked at the clumsy way the clown walked. 孩子们嘲笑小丑笨拙的走路姿势。

近义词: scoff at, sneer at, deride, ridicule, jeer at | 反义词: admire, praise, respect | 词性: 动词短语
