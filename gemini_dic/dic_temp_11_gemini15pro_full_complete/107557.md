landlordism:/ˈlændlɔːrdɪzəm/
n.
地主制，地主阶级剥削制度
The system of land ownership and tenancy in which most of the land is owned by a small number of landlords who rent it out to tenant farmers.
The report criticized the abuses of landlordism in the region.  这份报告批评了该地区地主制下的种种弊端。

landlordism:/ˈlændlɔːrdɪzəm/
n.
地主的作为;地主的身份
The actions or status of a landlord, often with negative connotations.
He resented the landlordism that kept him trapped in poverty. 他憎恨地主身份让他陷于贫困。


landlordism:/ˈlændlɔːrdɪzəm/
n.
房东作风;房东身份
The actions or status of a landlord, often with negative connotations, specifically relating to rented properties like houses or apartments.
Her strict rules and constant inspections exemplified her landlordism. 她严格的规定和不断的检查体现了她的房东作风。


ETYMOLOGY->
From landlord + -ism.  Landlord from land + lord.

SYNONYMS->
landownership,  rent-seeking,  feudalism (in some contexts), proprietorship

ANTONYMS->
tenant farming, communal ownership, peasant proprietorship
