demonstrational:/ˌdemənˈstreɪʃənəl/
adjective
示范的，演示的
serving as a demonstration; exhibiting or proving something
例句：The teacher gave a demonstrational lecture on how to use the new software. 老师做了一个示范性讲座，讲解如何使用这款新软件。

例句：This is a demonstrational version of the software, with limited functionality. 这是一个演示版的软件，功能有限。
synonym: illustrative, exemplary, exhibitive
antonym: theoretical, hypothetical
词性: adjective
