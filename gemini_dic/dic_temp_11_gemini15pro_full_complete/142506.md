osphradium:/ɒsˈfreɪdiəm/
noun
嗅检器
A chemoreceptive organ found in aquatic mollusks, believed to be used to test the chemical composition of the surrounding water.
The osphradium of the snail helps it detect changes in water quality.  蜗牛的嗅检器帮助它检测水质的变化。

The osphradium allows the mollusk to assess the surrounding water.  嗅检器使软体动物能够评估周围的水质。

Synonym:  chemoreceptor
