confessedly:/kənˈfɛsɪdli/
adverb
坦白地，公开承认地
adverb indicating that something is acknowledged or admitted openly.
Confessedly, I don't know much about the subject.  坦白说，我对这个主题不太了解。
He confessedly had little experience in the field. 他坦率地承认他在这个领域经验不足。

Synonym: admittedly, avowedly, undoubtedly, undeniably
Antonym: secretly, covertly, privately

Etymology:
From confess + -ed + -ly.  From Late Latin confessus, past participle of confiteri "to confess," from com- "together" + fateri "to acknowledge."
