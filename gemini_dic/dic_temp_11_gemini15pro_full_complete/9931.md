aperture:/ˈæpərtʃər/ (US), /ˈæpətʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|孔，缝隙，光圈|An opening, hole, or gap, typically one that allows light or something else to pass through.|The camera's aperture controls how much light enters the lens. 相机的孔径控制进入镜头的光量。|synonyms: opening, hole, gap, orifice, slit, vent,  |antonyms: closure, blockage, obstruction|noun
