complexant:/kəmˈplɛksənt/ | n. |络合剂，螯合剂 |A substance that forms stable complexes with metal ions, often used to sequester or remove them from a solution. |The complexant EDTA is commonly used in detergents.  EDTA这种络合剂常用于洗涤剂中。| synonyms: chelating agent, sequestering agent | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "releasing agent") | noun

complexing agent:/kəmˈplɛksɪŋ ˈeɪdʒənt/ | n. |络合剂 |A substance, often organic, that combines with a metal ion to form a complex. |The complexing agent helped to dissolve the metal. 这种络合剂帮助溶解了金属。| synonyms: chelator, ligand, complexant | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From complex + -ant.  "Complex" ultimately derives from the Latin "complectere" meaning "to braid together."
