houp:/hup/ | int. |  (archaic) A cry of encouragement or excitement. |  Used to urge someone on, similar to "Go on!" or "Come on!" |  "Houp! Houp! cried the riders, urging their horses forward." | 近义词:  hurrah, huzza, come on, go on | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps expressions of discouragement like "Stop!" or "Give up!") | 词性: interjection

词源:  Likely imitative in origin, possibly related to similar-sounding exclamations in other languages.
