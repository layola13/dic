capuchin:/ˈkæpjuːtʃɪn/ (US), /ˈkæpʊtʃɪn/ (UK)| n. adj.| капуцинский монах (Russian), 卡布奇诺咖啡, 卷尾猴|A capuchin is a type of monkey found in Central and South America, known for its intelligence. It also refers to a type of coffee similar to a latte.  The word originates from the resemblance of the monkey's hood to the hoods worn by Capuchin friars.|The capuchin monkey cleverly used a stick to retrieve the food. 那只卷尾猴灵巧地用一根棍子取回食物。|近义词：monkey, primate (for the animal); latte, macchiato (for the coffee)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun, adjective

capuchin:/ˈkæpjuːtʃɪn/ (US), /ˈkæpʊtʃɪn/ (UK)| adj.| капуцинский (Russian), 卡布奇诺的|Relating to or characteristic of Capuchin friars or monkeys.|She ordered a capuchin latte. 她点了一杯卡布奇诺拿铁。|近义词：Capuchin (noun form)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:adjective
