burn-out:/ˈbɜːrnaʊt/ (US) /ˈbɜːnaʊt/ (UK)| n.|精疲力竭；倦怠|a state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged or excessive stress|She is suffering from burn-out after working too many hours. 她因工作时间过长而感到精疲力竭。|近义词：exhaustion, fatigue, breakdown|反义词:energy, vigor|词性:名词

burn-out:/ˈbɜːrnaʊt/ (US) /ˈbɜːnaʊt/ (UK)| adj.|精疲力竭的；倦怠的|related to or suffering from burn-out|He felt completely burned-out after the project.  这个项目结束后他感到筋疲力尽。|近义词：exhausted, fatigued|反义词:energetic, refreshed|词性:形容词
