nonmeeting:/ˌnɒnˈmiːtɪŋ/
n.
非会议，非正式会议
A gathering or interaction that is not a formal meeting, often impromptu and informal.
We had a quick nonmeeting in the hallway to discuss the issue. 我们在走廊里进行了一次快速的非正式会议来讨论这个问题。
non-meeting, informal gathering, casual discussion
formal meeting, scheduled meeting
noun
