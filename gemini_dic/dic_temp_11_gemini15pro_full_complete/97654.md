inhibition:/ˌɪnhɪˈbɪʃən/| n.|抑制，阻止，压抑|The medication caused inhibition of the enzyme's activity.  这种药物抑制了酶的活性。|Example:  His social inhibitions prevented him from speaking up. 他的社交拘谨让他不敢开口。

Example:  Alcohol lowers inhibitions and can lead to risky behavior.  酒精会降低抑制力，并可能导致危险行为。|Synonyms: restraint, suppression, constraint|Antonyms:  encouragement, stimulation, liberation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inhibitio, from Latin inhibere "to restrain, check, prohibit," from in- "in, on, upon" + habere "to hold."
