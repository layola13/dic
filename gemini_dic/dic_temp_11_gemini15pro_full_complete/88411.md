hirudinea:/ˌhɪrʊˈdɪniə/ | n. | 蛭纲; 水蛭 |A class of segmented worms, commonly known as leeches, characterized by suckers at both ends of the body and typically feeding on blood. | Leeches, members of the class Hirudinea, are often used in medical treatments. 水蛭，属于蛭纲，常用于医疗。| Hirudinea are fascinating creatures with unique adaptations. 水蛭是具有独特适应性的迷人生物。|Synonyms: leech, bloodsucker | Antonyms: None | Noun
