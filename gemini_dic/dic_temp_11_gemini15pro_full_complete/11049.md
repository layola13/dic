arch-abbot:/ˌɑːrtʃˈæbət/ | n. | 大修道院长；总院长 | The chief abbot over several abbeys or over the abbots of a particular region. |  The arch-abbot presided over the annual meeting of all the abbots. 大修道院长主持了所有修道院长的年度会议。| The arch-abbot's authority extended over numerous monasteries. 总院长的权力延伸到许多修道院。 | synonyms: chief abbot, head abbot | antonyms: subordinate abbot | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *archīabbās, from Ancient Greek ἀρχι- (arkhi-, “chief, arch-”) + Latin abbās (“abbot”).
