antioxidant:/ˌæntiˈɑːksɪdənt/ |n. adj.|抗氧化剂；抗氧化（的）|A substance that inhibits oxidation, especially one used to counteract the deterioration of stored food products.|例句：Antioxidants like vitamin C can help protect your cells from damage. 像维生素C这样的抗氧化剂可以帮助保护你的细胞免受损害。

例句：This cream contains powerful antioxidants to combat the signs of aging.  这款面霜含有强效抗氧化剂，可以对抗衰老迹象。

|近义词：preservative, inhibitor|反义词：oxidant, pro-oxidant|词性: 名词，形容词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) : against, opposing (from Greek anti-)
oxidant: oxidizing agent (from French oxydant)
