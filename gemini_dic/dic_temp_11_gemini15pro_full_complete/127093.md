A far-right political party in Germany active between 1920 and 1945 that created and supported the ideology of Nazism. |  Historians study the rise of the national-socialists in 1930s Germany.  历史学家研究20世纪30年代德国国家社会主义者的兴起。

The national-socialists' policies led to the Holocaust. 纳粹的政策导致了大屠杀。 | synonyms: Nazis, fascists | antonyms: democrats, liberals | Noun
