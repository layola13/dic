bipyramidal:/ˌbaɪpɪˈræmɪdəl/| adj.|双锥体的，双棱锥体的|Having the shape of a bipyramid, a polyhedron formed by joining two pyramids base to base.|Phosphorus pentachloride has a bipyramidal molecular geometry. 五氯化磷具有双锥体分子几何形状.|近义词:octahedral (sometimes)|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + pyramidal (shaped like a pyramid).  Ultimately from Greek *pyramis* (pyramid).
