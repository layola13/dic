harier:/ˈhɛəriər/ | adj. | 更毛茸茸的; 更粗糙的; 更坚韧的 | More hairy, rougher, or tougher than something else |  The new carpet is harier than the old one.  这块新地毯比旧的更毛茸茸。 | 近义词: hairier, rougher, coarser | 反义词: smoother, softer | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *harier, comparative of *harie (“hairy”), from Old English *hǣrie, from Proto-Germanic *hāriza, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱeh₂r- (“hair”).  The comparative suffix -er is from Proto-Germanic *-izô.
