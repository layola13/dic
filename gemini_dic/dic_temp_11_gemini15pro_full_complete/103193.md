It often implies a degree of intellectual dishonesty or manipulation. |  He used jesuitry to convince his boss to give him a raise. 他用诡辩说服老板给他加薪。 | synonyms:  chicanery, sophistry, casuistry, subterfuge, deception, guile, cunning | antonyms:  honesty, candor, frankness, openness, straightforwardness | noun
