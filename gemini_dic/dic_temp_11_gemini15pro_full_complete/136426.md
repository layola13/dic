nonretroactive:/ˌnɒnˌrɛtrəʊˈæktɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˌrɛtroʊˈæktɪv/| adj.|不溯及既往的|Not applying to the past; taking effect only from the present or a specified date.|The new tax law is nonretroactive, so it won't affect last year's filings. 新的税法不溯及既往，所以它不会影响去年的申报。|Synonyms: prospective, irretrospective|Antonyms: retroactive, retrospective|Adjective
