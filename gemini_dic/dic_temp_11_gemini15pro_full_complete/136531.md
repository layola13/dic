nonruminating:/nɒnˈruːmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非反刍的
Not chewing the cud; not engaging in rumination (thoughtful consideration).
Cows are ruminating animals, while pigs are nonruminating.  牛是反刍动物，而猪是非反刍动物。
Horses are nonruminating herbivores, relying on a large cecum to digest fiber. 马是非反刍食草动物，依靠大的盲肠来消化纤维。
近义词:non-ruminant
反义词:ruminating
词性:adjective
