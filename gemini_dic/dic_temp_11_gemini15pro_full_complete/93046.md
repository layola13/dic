ido:/ˈɪːdoʊ/ (eye-doh)| n. | 人造语言; 国际语 | An artificial language based on Latin and Romance languages, intended as a universal second language. | Example: He learned Ido in hopes of communicating with people from diverse backgrounds. 他学习国际语，希望能与来自不同背景的人交流。 | Example:  The Ido movement gained some popularity in the early 20th century. 国际语运动在20世纪初获得了一些人气。| Synonym: Esperanto | Antonym: natural language | noun
