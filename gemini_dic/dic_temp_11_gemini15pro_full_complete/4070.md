aigrette:/eɪˈɡrɛt/ | n. |  白鹭冠毛，羽饰，(尤指植物的)冠毛 | A spray of diamonds formed an aigrette on her hair.  钻石喷雾在她头发上形成了一个羽饰。 | 近义词: plume, crest, ornament | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From French aigrette, diminutive of aigron "heron," from Provençal aigron, from Vulgar Latin *acrio, from Latin accipiter "hawk."  The original meaning referred to the egret's plumes, then expanded to similar ornaments.
