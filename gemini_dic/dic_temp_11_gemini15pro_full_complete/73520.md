foreshores:/ˈfɔːrʃɔːrz/ (US) /ˈfɔːʃɔːz/ (UK)
n.
前滩，潮间带
The part of a shore between high- and low-water marks, or between the water and cultivated or developed land.
The children were playing on the foreshores, searching for shells. 孩子们正在前滩上玩耍，寻找贝壳。
近义词:beach, shoreline, coast, waterfront
反义词:hinterland
词性:名词
