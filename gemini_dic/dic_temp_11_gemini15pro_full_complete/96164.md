independent/ˌɪndɪˈpendənt/ | adj. | 独立的，自主的，不相关的 | Not influenced or controlled by other people or things; free and self-governing; not depending on something else for its existence |  She's a very independent young woman. 她是一个非常独立的年轻女性。|  The country declared its independence in 1948.  这个国家在1948年宣布独立。| synonyms: autonomous, free, self-governing, self-reliant, sovereign | antonyms: dependent, reliant, subordinate | adjective
