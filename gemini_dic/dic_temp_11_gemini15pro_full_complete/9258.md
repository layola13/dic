antipatriarchy:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriˌɑːrki/
n.
反父权制
Opposition to patriarchy; a social system in which men hold primary power and predominate in roles of political leadership, moral authority, social privilege and control of property.
The rise of antipatriarchy movements has challenged traditional power structures. 反父权运动的兴起挑战了传统的权力结构。
Feminist literature often explores themes of antipatriarchy. 女权主义文学经常探讨反父权制的主题。
近义词：feminism, anti-sexism
反义词：patriarchy, sexism
词性: 名词
