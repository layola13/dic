appealingly:/əˈpiːlɪŋli/
adverb
吸引人地，动人地
in a way that attracts attention or interest; in a way that makes someone want to know more or to have something
She smiled appealingly at him. 她对他嫣然一笑。
近义词:attractively, charmingly, engagingly, winsomely
反义词:unattractively, repellingly, off-puttingly
词性:副词

Etymology:
From appeal + -ing + -ly.  "Appeal" comes from Old French "apeler"  (to call, to accuse), ultimately from Latin "appellare" (to address, to call upon).
