inveigle:/ɪnˈveɪɡl/ /ɪnˈviːɡl/
verb
中文翻译：诱骗；哄骗
英文解析：To persuade someone to do something by means of deception or flattery.
例句：He inveigled her into going to the party with him. 他哄骗她和他一起去参加聚会。

例句：She inveigled him into giving her his car keys. 她骗取了他的车钥匙。

近义词：cajole, coax, wheedle, entice, lure
反义词：dissuade, deter
词性：verb
