noncircuitous:/ˌnɒnsərˈkjuːɪtəs/
adjective
直接的，不绕圈子的
Going straight to the point; not roundabout.
The professor gave a noncircuitous explanation of the complex theorem. 教授对这个复杂的定理作了一个直接的解释。
She took a noncircuitous route to the store. 她走了一条直达商店的路。
近义词:direct, straightforward, linear
反义词:circuitous, indirect, roundabout
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- + circuitous.  "Circuitous" comes from the Late Latin word "circuitus," meaning "a going around," from the Latin "circuire," "to go around."  "Non-" is a prefix of negation.
