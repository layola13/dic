absinthism:/ˈæbsɪnθɪzəm/| n.|苦艾酒中毒; 苦艾酒瘾|A chronic addiction to absinthe, often characterized by hallucinations and neurological damage.|He suffered from absinthism after years of heavy drinking. 他多年酗酒后患上了苦艾酒中毒。|Synonyms: addiction, dependence|Antonyms: sobriety, abstinence|noun

Etymology->
From French *absinthisme*, from *absinthe* ("absinthe") + *-isme* ("-ism"). Ultimately from Latin *absinthium* and Greek *ἀψίνθιον (apsínthion)*.
