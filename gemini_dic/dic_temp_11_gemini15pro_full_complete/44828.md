crust:/krʌst/| n. |外壳，面包皮，地壳|A hard outer covering of something.|The pie had a golden brown crust. 这个派的皮是金黄色的。|近义词：shell, covering, rind, exterior|反义词:interior, core, center|noun

crust:/krʌst/| v. |覆盖外壳，结硬皮|To become covered with a hard outer layer.|The snow crusted over during the night. 雪夜里结了硬皮。|近义词：harden, solidify, cake|反义词:soften, melt, liquefy|verb
