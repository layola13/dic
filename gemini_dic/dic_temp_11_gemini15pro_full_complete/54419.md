disincorporation:/ˌdɪsɪnkɔːrpəˈreɪʃn/
n.
解散，撤销法人资格
The act of dissolving a corporation.  A legal process by which a corporation ceases to exist.
The disincorporation of the company resulted in the loss of many jobs. 该公司的解散导致许多人失业。
The company voted for disincorporation after years of financial struggles.  这家公司在多年的财务困境后投票决定解散。
