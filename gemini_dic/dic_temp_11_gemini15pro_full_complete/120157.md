mind-bending:/ˈmaɪndˌbendɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人费解的；使人迷惑的；烧脑的 | Extremely difficult to understand; causing mental confusion. |  The plot of the movie was so mind-bending, I'm still trying to figure it out. 电影的情节太烧脑了，我现在还在努力弄明白。

The professor's lecture on quantum physics was mind-bending. 教授关于量子物理学的讲座令人费解。| 近义词: perplexing, baffling, bewildering, confusing, intricate, complex | 反义词: simple, straightforward, clear, easy to understand | 词性: 形容词
