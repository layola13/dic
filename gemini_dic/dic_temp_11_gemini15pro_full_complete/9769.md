antonomastic:/ˌæntənəˈmæstɪk/ | adj. | 代称的，用专有名词代替普通名词的 |  Referring to the use of a proper name to designate a member of a class or the use of an epithet or title in place of a proper name. |  "A Solomon" for a wise ruler is an antonomastic usage.  用“所罗门”来指代一位英明的统治者是一种代称用法。 | synonyms: eponymous, metonymic | antonyms:  N/A | adjective
