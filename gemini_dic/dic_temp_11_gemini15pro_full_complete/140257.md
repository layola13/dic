offshoot:/ˈɒfʃuːt/ (US) /ˈɔːfʃuːt/ (UK)| n.|分支；衍生物；旁系|A thing that develops from something else, a branch. |例句：Photography is an offshoot of painting. 摄影是绘画的一个分支。

例句：The company started as an offshoot of a larger corporation. 这家公司最初是一家大公司的分支机构。


近义词：branch, outgrowth, spin-off, derivative|反义词:source, origin|词性:noun
