nonexercisable:/ˌnɒnˈɛksəsaɪzəbl/
adjective
不可行使的；不可执行的
Not capable of being exercised or put into effect; not able to be used.
The contract contained a nonexercisable clause regarding early termination.  合同中包含一项关于提前终止的不可执行条款。
Stock options granted to employees are often nonexercisable for a certain period. 授予员工的股票期权通常在一定时期内不可行使。
Synonyms: unusable, inoperable, ineffective, invalid
Antonyms: exercisable, valid, usable, effective
词性: adjective
