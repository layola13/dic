nonhomogeneous:/ˌnɑːnhoʊmoʊˈdʒiːniəs/ /ˌnɒnhɒməˈdʒiːniəs/
adjective
非均匀的；非齐次的
Consisting of elements that are not of the same kind or nature.
例句：The mixture was nonhomogeneous, with clumps of undissolved sugar at the bottom. 混合物是非均匀的，底部有不溶解的糖块。

例句：The soil sample was nonhomogeneous, containing a mix of sand, clay, and organic matter. 土壤样本是非均匀的，包含沙子、粘土和有机物的混合物。

近义词：heterogeneous, diverse, varied
反义词：homogeneous, uniform, consistent
词性：adjective
