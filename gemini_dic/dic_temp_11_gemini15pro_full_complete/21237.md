bloke:/bləʊk/ (British English) | n. |家伙，小子，男人|A man, often used informally. |例句：He's a good bloke. 他是个好人。

例句：I saw a bloke walking down the street. 我看到一个男人在街上走。

近义词：chap, fellow, guy, man |反义词:woman, girl |词性:noun
