heteroptera:/ˌhɛtərˈɒptərə/ | n. | 异翅目昆虫;异翅亚目 | An order of insects characterized by forewings that are hardened at the base and membranous at the tips, including bugs such as stink bugs and assassin bugs. | Example 1: The heteroptera are a large group of insects with diverse feeding habits. 异翅目昆虫是一个种类繁多的群体，具有不同的摄食习性。

Example 2:  Many species of heteroptera are important agricultural pests. 许多异翅目昆虫是重要的农业害虫。 | 近义词:true bugs | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the Greek héteros (different) + pterón (wing), referring to the partly hardened, partly membranous forewings.
