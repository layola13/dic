channeling:/ˈtʃænəlɪŋ/| n. v.|引导，传递；沟通|The act of directing something into a particular course.|She believes she is channeling the spirit of her dead grandmother. 她相信她正在引导她已故祖母的灵魂。|近义词：transmitting, conveying, communicating|反义词:blocking, obstructing|词性:名词/动词
Etymology: From channel + -ing. "Channel" comes from Old French "chanel" (modern French "chenal"), from Latin "canalis" meaning "pipe, groove, canal."
