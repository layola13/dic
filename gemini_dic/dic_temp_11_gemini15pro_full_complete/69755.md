feria:/ˈfɛriə/ (US), /ˈfɪəriə/ (UK)| n.|节日；市集；庙会；(西班牙等国家的)圣日；(古罗马的)假日|A period of festivity or holiday, often associated with religious celebrations or a market/fair.  Originally a Roman holiday, now commonly refers to a festival or fair, especially in Spanish-speaking countries.|例句：The town held a week-long feria in honor of their patron saint.  小镇为纪念他们的守护神举行了一周的节日。

例句：We went to the feria and rode the Ferris wheel. 我们去了庙会，坐了摩天轮。|近义词：festival, fair, celebration, fiesta (especially in Spanish contexts)|反义词: workday|词性:名词
