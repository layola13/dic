dactylonomy:/ˌdæk.tɪˈlɒn.ə.mi/ | n. | 指算；指语；手语 |  The art or practice of communicating by signs made with the fingers; finger spelling.  | Deaf people sometimes use dactylonomy to communicate. 聋人有时使用指语交流。 |  finger spelling, sign language |  speech, verbal communication | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *daktylos* (finger) + *nomos* (law, custom).  Meaning "finger reckoning" or "counting on the fingers."  Later extended to mean "finger spelling" or "sign language."
