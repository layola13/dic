elementality:/ˌɛlɪmɛnˈtælɪti/
n.
元素性; 基本性; 基礎性
The state or quality of being elemental or basic; fundamental or essential character.
The artist explored the elementality of color in his latest exhibition, focusing on primary hues. 这位艺术家在他最新的展览中探索了色彩的元素性，重点关注原色。

近义词: essence, nature, character, fundamental, basic
反义词: complexity, intricacy
