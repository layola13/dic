demonizes:/ˈdiːmənaɪzɪz/
verb
妖魔化；将…描绘成恶魔
portrays someone or something as wicked or threatening.
The media often demonizes politicians it disagrees with. 媒体经常妖魔化与其意见相左的政客。
vilifies, denigrates, slanders
humanizes, glorifies
v.
