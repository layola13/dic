coarctate:/koʊˈɑːrkteɪt/| adj.|压缩的，狭窄的，束紧的|Pressed together; constricted; contracted.  Specifically, in botany, having the sepals or petals pressed close together.|The coarctate pupa is typical of certain fly species.  这种压缩的蛹是某些蝇类的典型特征。|Synonyms: constricted, compressed, narrowed, contracted|Antonyms: expanded, dilated, widened|adjective

coarctate:/koʊˈɑːrkteɪt/| v.|压缩，束紧，收缩|To press or draw together; constrict; contract.|The muscles coarctate the blood vessels. 肌肉收缩血管。|Synonyms: constrict, compress, narrow, contract|Antonyms: expand, dilate, widen|verb
