deforests:/diːˈfɔːrɪsts/
verb
砍伐森林；清除…的森林
To clear (an area) of forests or trees.
The company deforests large areas of the Amazon rainforest each year.  该公司每年砍伐亚马逊雨林的大片区域。
Logging companies are deforesting the area at an alarming rate.  伐木公司正以惊人的速度砍伐该地区的森林。
clear-cut, log, denude
reforest, afforest
v.
