non-symmetrical:/ˌnɒn sɪˈmetrɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːn sɪˈmetrɪkəl/| adj.|非对称的，不对称的|Not symmetrical; lacking symmetry.  Having parts that fail to correspond to one another in shape, size, or arrangement.|The artwork features a non-symmetrical design, with more weight on the left side.|近义词：asymmetrical, unbalanced, irregular, uneven|反义词: symmetrical, balanced, even, regular|词性:形容词
non-symmetrical:| | |From non- + symmetrical.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Symmetrical" relates to symmetry,  from Greek *symmetria* "agreement in dimensions, due proportion, arrangement," from *syn- "together" + *metron* "measure."| | | |
