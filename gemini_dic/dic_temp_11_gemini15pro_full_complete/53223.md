directionless:/dɪˈrɛkʃənlɪs/
adj.
没有方向的，无目标的
lacking a clear direction or aim; without guidance or purpose.
He felt directionless and lost after losing his job.  他失业后感到茫然和失落。
The team seemed directionless without a strong leader.  没有强有力的领导，团队似乎失去了方向。
近义词: aimless, adrift, lost, rudderless, unguided
反义词: purposeful, focused, driven, motivated, directed
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From direction + -less.  来自 direction + -less。
