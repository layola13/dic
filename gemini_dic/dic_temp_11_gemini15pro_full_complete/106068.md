knelled:/nɛld/| v. | 敲响（丧钟）；（钟）鸣;|The church bell knelled for the deceased soldier. 教堂的丧钟为死去的士兵而鸣。|例句：The bell knelled mournfully, announcing the death of the king. 丧钟悲哀地敲响，宣告了国王的逝世。

例句：They knelled the passing of the old year. 他们敲响钟声送别旧的一年。|近义词：tolled, rang, chimed|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑  silenced)|词性: 动词

Etymology: From Middle English *knellen*, from Old English *cnyllan* (“to ring, sound, knock”), from Proto-Germanic *knellaną* (“to resound, ring”).  Cognate with Dutch *knal* (“bang, explosion”), German *knallen* (“to bang, crack, pop”).
