eaas:/iːæs/ | n. | Everything as a Service | 一切皆服务 | EaaS encompasses all IT functionalities delivered to users as a service over the internet, including infrastructure, software, and platforms.  |  Many companies are transitioning to an eaas model for greater flexibility and scalability. 许多公司正在向 EaaS 模式转型，以获得更大的灵活性和可扩展性。 | 近义词: XaaS, cloud services | 反义词: On-premises, traditional IT | 词性: noun
