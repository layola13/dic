cassoulet:/ˌkæsʊˈleɪ/ (US), /ˈkæsʊleɪ/ (UK)| n. | 卡酥来砂锅, 法国焗豆 |A rich, slow-cooked casserole containing meat (typically pork sausages, goose, duck, and sometimes mutton), pork skin and white beans. It is named after its traditional cooking vessel, the *cassole*, a deep, round, earthenware pot.|We had a delicious cassoulet at the French restaurant. 我们在法国餐厅吃了一份美味的卡酥来砂锅。| The cassoulet was simmering on the stove for hours, filling the house with a savory aroma. 卡酥来砂锅在炉子上炖了几个小时，满屋子都是香味。 |Synonyms: casserole, stew|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific dish)|noun
