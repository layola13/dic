afterclap:/ˈæftərklæp/
noun
后续掌声；意外的或延迟的后果
A burst of applause following a performance, often after a period of silence or a less enthusiastic initial response; a later consequence, often unforeseen and unpleasant.
The comedian received a smattering of applause after his first joke, but the real afterclap came after his final punchline.  这位喜剧演员在他的第一个笑话后得到了一些掌声，但真正的掌声是在他最后一个妙语之后才出现的。
The economic afterclap of the pandemic was felt for years.  这场大流行病带来的经济后果持续了数年。
Synonyms: repercussion, consequence, aftermath, fallout
Antonyms: precursor, prelude, forerunner
