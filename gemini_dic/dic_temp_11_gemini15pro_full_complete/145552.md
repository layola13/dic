overcommunication:/ˌoʊvərˌkəmˌjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
过度沟通，沟通过度
The act of communicating too much, often to the point of redundancy or inefficiency.
Example: Overcommunication can sometimes lead to confusion rather than clarity. 过度沟通有时会导致混淆而不是清晰。
Example:  The team suffered from overcommunication, with everyone sending multiple emails about the same issue.  团队饱受过度沟通之苦，每个人都针对同一问题发送多封电子邮件。
Synonyms: excessive communication, oversharing
Antonyms: undercommunication, lack of communication
noun
