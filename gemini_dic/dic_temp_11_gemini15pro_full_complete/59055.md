dwell:/dwɛl/| vi.|居住，存在于，细想某事|to live in or at a specified place; to remain for a time; to linger over in thought or speech|He dwelled in a small cottage by the sea. 他住在海边的一间小别墅里。|近义词：reside, live, inhabit, abide, stay, linger|反义词:depart, leave, go|verb

dwell:/dwɛl/| n.|住所，居住|a dwelling place; abode|His dwell was a humble cabin. 他的住所是一个简陋的小屋。|近义词：residence, abode, dwelling, domicile|反义词: N/A |noun
