ethos:/ˈiːθɒs/ (UK) /ˈiːθɑːs/ (US)| n. |气质，精神特质；思想品格；道德准则|The characteristic spirit of a culture, era, or community as manifested in its beliefs and aspirations.|The ethos of the school emphasizes academic excellence. 这所学校的理念强调卓越的学术成就.|Synonyms: spirit, character, atmosphere, climate, mood, temper, tenor|Antonyms: None (Ethos describes a characteristic; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|Noun
