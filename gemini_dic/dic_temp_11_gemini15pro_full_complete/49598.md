demesne:/dɪˈmeɪn/ /dɪˈmiːn/| n.|领地，庄园，私有土地|A piece of land attached to a manor and retained for the owner's own use.  An area of land or territory over which a ruler or state has control or jurisdiction.|The king's vast demesne stretched as far as the eye could see. 国王的广阔领地一眼望不到边。|Synonyms: estate, domain, manor, property, territory|Antonyms: public land, common land|noun

demesne:/dɪˈmeɪn/ /dɪˈmiːn/| n.|领域，范围，活动范围|An area of activity or interest.  A sphere of action, influence, or knowledge.|That topic falls outside my demesne of expertise.  那个话题超出了我的专业领域。|Synonyms: realm, sphere, province, domain, field|Antonyms: N/A|noun
