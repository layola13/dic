monesin:/ˈmoʊnɪsɪn/ | n. |莫能菌素|A polyether antibiotic isolated from *Streptomyces cinnamonensis*. It is used as a coccidiostat and growth promoter in livestock. |Monesin is commonly used in poultry feed. 莫能菌素常用于家禽饲料。|Synonyms: Rumensin, Coban |Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a specific drug.  Could consider antonyms for its function, such as "growth inhibitor" or "coccidiosis-promoting agent," but these are not single words) |noun
