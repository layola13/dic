metallic:/məˈtælɪk/| adj.|金属的；金属般的；有金属特性的|Made of metal, containing metal, or having the appearance of metal.|The car has a metallic blue paint. 这辆车是金属蓝色漆。|近义词：metal, silvery, steely|反义词:nonmetallic, dull|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *métallique*, from Late Latin *metallicus*, from Latin *metallum* (metal) + *-icus* (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Greek *μέταλλον* (métallon, “mine, quarry, metal”).
