adviso:/ædˈvaɪzoʊ/ /ədˈviːzoʊ/
noun
advice, counsel, recommendation
An adviso is a piece of advice or counsel given, often formally or officially.
The president received an adviso from his national security team. 总统收到了他的国家安全团队的建议。
The committee issued an adviso on the proposed legislation. 委员会就拟议立法发布了建议。
Synonyms: counsel, guidance, recommendation, suggestion
Antonyms: dissuasion, discouragement
n.
