antestomach:/ˈæntiˌstʌmək/| n. |前胃|The part of the digestive system in some animals that precedes the stomach, such as the rumen or crop of a bird. |Cows have a complex antestomach system to digest cellulose.|Synonyms: forestomach, proventriculus|Antonyms: stomach (in a stricter sense, abomasum in ruminants)|noun
