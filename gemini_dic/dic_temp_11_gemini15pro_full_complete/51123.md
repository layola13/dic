despises:/dɪˈspaɪzɪz/
verb
鄙视，蔑视
feels intense dislike or contempt for.
He despises liars and cheats. 他鄙视说谎者和骗子。
Synonyms: loathes, abhors, detests, hates, scorns
Antonyms: admires, respects, loves, esteems
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *despiser, from Latin despicere (“to look down on”), from de- (“down from”) + specere (“to look at”).
