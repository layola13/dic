manifest:/ˈmænɪfest/ | adj. |明显的，显而易见的|Clearly apparent to the sight or understanding; obvious.|Her anxiety was manifest to everyone.|Synonyms: obvious, apparent, evident, clear, plain|Antonyms: hidden, concealed, obscure, unclear|adjective

manifest:/ˈmænɪfest/ | v. |表明，显示，证明|To show something clearly, through signs or actions.|The illness first manifested itself in a high fever.|Synonyms: demonstrate, reveal, display, exhibit, show|Antonyms: conceal, hide, disguise|verb

manifest:/ˈmænɪfest/ | n. |货单，旅客名单|A document listing the cargo, passengers, and crew of a ship, aircraft, or vehicle.|The ship's manifest listed all the passengers and crew.|Synonyms: list, register, inventory, catalog|Antonyms: N/A|noun
