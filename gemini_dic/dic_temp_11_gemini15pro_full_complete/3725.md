aggrievedness's:/əˈɡriːvdnəsɪz/
|n.|委屈，冤屈，愤懑|The state of feeling wronged or unjustly treated.  A noun formed from "aggrieved" (feeling resentment at having been unfairly treated) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality) + "'s" (possessive marker).|Example: The aggrievedness's intensity surprised everyone. 这委屈的强烈程度让每个人都感到惊讶。|Synonyms: resentment, indignation, umbrage, offense, pique|Antonyms: contentment, satisfaction, pleasure|noun
