antipatriotisms: /ˌæntiˌpeɪtriətɪzəmz/
|n.| 反爱国主义（复数） | Sentiments or actions that demonstrate a lack of love for or loyalty to one's country, often accompanied by opposition to its policies or ideals.  |  The recent surge in antipatriotisms has alarmed many citizens who fear for the future of the nation. 最近反爱国主义情绪高涨，使许多担心国家未来的公民感到震惊。|  disloyalty, sedition, treason | patriotism, nationalism | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + patriotism (love of one's country). The suffix "-ism" denotes a doctrine, belief, or practice.
