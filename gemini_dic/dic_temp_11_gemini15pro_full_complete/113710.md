macro-evolutionary:/ˌmækroʊˌɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri/
|adj.|宏观演化的，宏观进化的|Relating to major evolutionary change at or above the level of species, over long periods of time, resulting in the formation of new taxonomic groups.|The fossil record provides compelling evidence for macro-evolutionary processes. 化石记录为宏观进化过程提供了令人信服的证据。|Example: The development of wings in birds is a macro-evolutionary change. 鸟类翅膀的发育是一个宏观进化变化。|Synonyms: large-scale evolutionary, transpecific evolution|Antonyms: micro-evolutionary|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
macro- (large) + evolutionary (pertaining to evolution)
From Greek "makros" (large) and Latin "evolutio" (unrolling)
