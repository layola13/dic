Example 2:  The bindweed plant twines dextrorsally around its support. 旋花植物向右缠绕着它的支撑物。 |  Near Synonyms: clockwise, right-handed, right-winding | Antonyms: sinistral, left-handed, left-winding | Word Type: Adjective
