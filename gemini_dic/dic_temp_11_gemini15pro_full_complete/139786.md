octapla:/ˈɑːktəplə/ | n. |八倍, 八重奏, 八行诗 |A group or set of eight; specifically, a set of eight copies of something, often a book.  It comes from the Latin "octaplus" meaning "eightfold." | The printer accidentally produced an octapla of the document. 打印机意外地打印了八份文件。| synonyms: octuple, eightfold | antonyms: single, individual | noun
