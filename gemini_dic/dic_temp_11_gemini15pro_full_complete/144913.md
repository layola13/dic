over-sweetness:/ˈoʊvərˈswiːtnəs/
n.
过度甜味; 过度的甜度
Excessive sweetness.
The over-sweetness of the dessert made it difficult to finish. 这道甜点的甜度过高，让人难以吃完。
近义词:excessive sweetness, cloying sweetness
反义词:tartness, acidity
词性:名词

over:/ˈoʊvər/
adv. adj. prep.
在...之上，超过，结束
Over the bridge. 在桥上。
近义词:above, beyond, finished
反义词:under, below
词性:副词，形容词，介词

sweetness:/ˈswiːtnəs/
n.
甜味，甜蜜
The sweetness of the honey. 蜂蜜的甜味。
近义词:sweet, pleasantness
反义词:bitterness, sourness
词性:名词
