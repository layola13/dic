gumball:/ˈɡʌmˌbɔːl/
gumball: n.|泡泡糖球，球状口香糖|A gumball is a type of chewing gum that is round and usually brightly colored. |例句：The child eagerly put a quarter in the machine for a gumball. 孩子急切地把25美分投入机器买泡泡糖球。|例句：The gumball machine dispensed a bright red gumball. 泡泡糖机器掉出了一颗鲜红色的泡泡糖球。|近义词：chewing gum, bubble gum|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology:
"gum" + "ball".  "Gum" refers to the chewing gum substance, and "ball" refers to its spherical shape.
