incongruousness:/ˌɪnkɒŋˈɡruːəsnəs/ |n.|不协调，不一致，不相称|The incongruousness of his formal attire with the casual event was apparent.  他正式的服装与休闲的活动明显不协调。|例句：The incongruousness of her cheerful demeanor and the somber occasion was striking. 她欢快的神态与肃穆的场合形成鲜明的对比。

例句：The incongruousness of the modern sculpture in the ancient temple was jarring.  现代雕塑在古老寺庙中的不协调感令人不快。|近义词：inappropriateness, incompatibility, disharmony, disparity, inconsistency|反义词：appropriateness, suitability, harmony, congruity, consistency|词性: 名词

incongruous:/ɪnˈkɒŋɡruəs/ |adj.|不协调的，不一致的，不相称的|His incongruous behavior at the funeral was shocking. 他在葬礼上不协调的行为令人震惊。|例句：The bright pink house looked incongruous amidst the elegant brownstones.  那栋亮粉色的房子在优雅的褐砂石建筑中显得格格不入。

例句：Her incongruous laughter during the serious discussion was unsettling.  她在严肃的讨论中不合时宜的笑声令人不安。|近义词：inappropriate, unsuitable, incompatible, discordant, inconsistent|反义词：appropriate, suitable, fitting, harmonious, consistent|词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Late Latin incongruus  "incongruous, unsuitable," from in- "not" + congruus "agreeing, suitable."
