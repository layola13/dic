oversalted:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːltɪd/ | adj. | 过咸的 | Containing too much salt. | The soup was oversalted and I couldn't finish it. 汤太咸了，我喝不完。

The steak was so oversalted that it was inedible. 牛排太咸了，根本没法吃。

| 近义词：salty, too salty | 反义词：undersalted, bland | 词性：形容词
