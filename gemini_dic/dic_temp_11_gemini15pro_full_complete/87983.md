high-mindedness:/ˌhaɪˈmaɪndɪdnəs/ | n. |崇高；高尚；高洁 |The state of being high-minded; nobility of mind; having or showing high moral principles or ideals.|  He acted out of high-mindedness, not self-interest. 他出于高尚，而不是出于私利。 | 近义词：nobility, integrity, principle, virtue | 反义词：baseness, meanness, selfishness | 词性: 名词

high-minded:/ˌhaɪˈmaɪndɪd/ | adj. |高尚的；高洁的；崇高的 |Having or showing high moral principles or ideals. |She was a high-minded woman, always striving to do what was right. 她是一位高尚的女性，总是努力做正确的事。 | 近义词：noble, virtuous, principled, upright | 反义词：base, mean, selfish, ignoble | 词性: 形容词
