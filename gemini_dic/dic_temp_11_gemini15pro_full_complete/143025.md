outbleating:/ˌaʊtˈbliːtɪŋ/
verb
大声胜过；叫声盖过
surpassing in bleating; crying louder than
The shepherd outbleated the lambs, calling them back to the fold. 牧羊人大声呼喊，盖过了羊羔的叫声，把它们叫回羊圈。

The rival politicians outbleated each other at the rally.  在集会上，相互竞争的政客们互相叫嚣，试图盖过对方的声音。
近义词:outyelling, outshouting, outscreaming
反义词:whimpering, whispering
词性:v.
