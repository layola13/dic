ETYMOLOGY->
From helpful + -ness.  Helpful is from help + -ful. Help comes from Old English helpan "to help, support, aid, succor," of Germanic origin.  The suffix -ful means "full of" or "characterized by." The suffix -ness forms nouns meaning "state or quality of being."
