heptamerous:/ˌhɛptˈæmərəs/
adjective
七基数的；七数的
Having seven parts, especially of flowers or leaves arranged in sevens.
例句：The heptamerous flower is a rare and beautiful sight. 七基数的花是一种罕见而美丽的景象。

例句：Some species of cacti have heptamerous flowers. 一些种类的仙人掌有七基数的花。

近义词:septemerous
反义词:(No direct antonym, but consider terms like "pentamerous" or "hexamerous" for flowers with five or six parts respectively.)
词性:adjective
