no-balled:/ˌnəʊˈbɔːld/
verb (past participle)|未投好球，违规投球，（比喻）搞砸了|A no-ball is an illegal delivery in cricket.  In informal usage, it means to mess something up.|例句：The bowler no-balled, giving the batsman a free hit. 投球手投了一个坏球，给了击球手一个自由击球的机会。

例句：I completely no-balled that presentation. 我完全搞砸了那次演讲。|近义词：mess up, botch, screw up|反义词：succeed, ace, nail|词性:v.
