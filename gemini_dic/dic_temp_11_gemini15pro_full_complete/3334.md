afforestation:/əˌfɔːrɪˈsteɪʃən/
n.|造林，植树造林|The act or process of establishing a forest, especially on land not previously forested.|例句：Afforestation projects are crucial for combating climate change.  植树造林项目对于应对气候变化至关重要。

例句：The government has initiated a large-scale afforestation program. 政府启动了一项大规模的植树造林计划。
|近义词：reforestation, forestation, planting|反义词:deforestation|词性:noun
