outcatch:/ˌaʊtˈkætʃ/ |v.| 胜过，超过（在捕鱼、狩猎等方面）| To surpass or exceed someone in catching or hunting. | He outcaught his brother by three fish. 他捕到的鱼比他兄弟多三条。| surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform | be outcaught, lag behind | verb

Etymology: "out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "catch" (verb meaning to capture or seize).  The word is formed by combining the prefix "out-" with the verb "catch." The prefix "out-" implies exceeding or surpassing someone in an action.
