logogram:/ˈlɒɡəɡræm/ | n. | 表意文字 | A written character that represents a word or morpheme, as opposed to representing only a sound. |  Chinese characters are logograms. 汉字是表意文字。

The ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs are also considered logograms.  古埃及的象形文字也被认为是表意文字。

| 近义词：ideogram,  | 反义词: phonogram, alphabet | 词性: noun
