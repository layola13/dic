mozzarella:/ˌmɒtsəˈrɛlə/ | n. | 马苏里拉奶酪 | A soft white Italian cheese traditionally made from buffalo milk. |  Fresh mozzarella is delicious in salads. 新鲜的马苏里拉奶酪做沙拉很美味。

Etymology: From the Neapolitan dialect of Italian *mozzarella*, from *mozza* ("cut off"), past participle of *mozzare* ("to cut off"), referring to the process of shaping the cheese.

Synonyms:  buffalo mozzarella (if made from buffalo milk)

Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of cheese.  One could consider "hard cheese" a general antonym in terms of texture.)

Part of Speech: Noun
