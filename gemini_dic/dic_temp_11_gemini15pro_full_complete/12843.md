associative:/əˈsoʊʃiətɪv/ /əˈsəʊsiətɪv/| adj.|联想的，结合的，联合的|Relating to or characterized by association.  Able to make associations between different things or concepts. In mathematics, obeying the associative law (e.g., a + (b + c) = (a + b) + c).|例句：The brain's associative areas are responsible for higher mental functions.  大脑的联想区域负责高级心理功能。

例句：He has an associative memory; one thought leads to another. 他有联想记忆；一个想法引出另一个想法。|近义词：connected, related, linked, combined|反义词：dissociative, unrelated, independent|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *associativus, from Latin associare (“to join, unite, associate”), from ad- (“to”) + sociare (“to join, unite”), from socius (“companion, ally”).
