bepinch:/bɪˈpɪntʃ/
verb
勒紧，捏住，使困窘
To pinch tightly; to put in difficulty, especially financially.
The tight collar bepinched his neck.  勒紧的衣领卡住了他的脖子。
The sudden drop in sales bepinched the small business. 销售额的突然下降使这家小企业陷入了困境。
近义词: constrict, squeeze, pinch, straiten
反义词: loosen, release, free
词性: verb
