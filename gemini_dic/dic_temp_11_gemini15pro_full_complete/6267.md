among: /əˈmʌŋ/ | prep. |在…之中；在…之间 | surrounded by; in the company of |  The book was hidden among his other belongings. 这本书藏在他的其他物品中。

The bees buzzed among the flowers. 蜜蜂在花丛中嗡嗡作响。


近义词: amidst, amongst, between, amid | 反义词: outside, beyond | 词性: 介词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *on gemang "in the crowd," from on (prep.) "on, in" + gemang (n.) "crowd, throng."  The ge- prefix weakened and disappeared in Middle English.
