heartseed:/ˈhɑːrtsid/ | n. | 心之籽，心种 |A seed that metaphorically or literally represents the core or essence of someone's heart; often used in fictional contexts to symbolize love, connection, or emotional influence.  This word doesn't have a standard dictionary definition and is typically used in creative writing, particularly in fantasy or science fiction. | The heartseed blossomed within her, filling her with a sense of belonging. 心之籽在她心中绽放，让她充满了归属感。 |  The wizard planted a heartseed in the prince, hoping to sway his decisions.  巫师在王子心中种下了一颗心之籽，希望以此影响他的决定。 |Synonyms: core, essence, kernel, seed of love| Antonyms: apathy, indifference | Noun
