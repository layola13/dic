chondroma:/kɒnˈdrəʊmə/ (US: /kɑːnˈdroʊmə/) | n. | 软骨瘤 | A benign tumor formed entirely of cartilage. |  A chondroma is a benign cartilaginous tumor, and it is the most common benign tumor of the hand. 软骨瘤是一种良性软骨肿瘤，是手上最常见的良性肿瘤。| Example: The doctor diagnosed the lump as a chondroma. 医生诊断肿块为软骨瘤。|Synonyms: cartilage tumor, enchondroma (when found within bone)| Antonyms:  N/A (benign tumors don't have direct antonyms, but malignant cartilage tumors are called chondrosarcomas) | Noun
