irremediably:/ˌɪrɪˈmiːdiəbli/| adv.|无法挽回地，不可救药地|In a way that cannot be improved or corrected; beyond repair.|His reputation was irremediably damaged by the scandal. 他的名誉被丑闻彻底毁了。|The situation was irremediably lost. 局势已经无可挽回。|近义词:incurably, permanently, irretrievably, hopelessly|反义词:remediably, curably, fixably|词性:副词

词源:
From Late Latin *irremediabilis, from Latin in- (“not”) + remediabilis (“remediable, curable”).
