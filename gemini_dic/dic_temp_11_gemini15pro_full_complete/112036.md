long-winded:/ˌlɔːŋˈwɪndɪd/ | adj. |冗长的，啰嗦的|tediously long in speaking or writing; using or containing too many words|The professor's lectures were notoriously long-winded, often leaving students struggling to stay awake.  教授的讲座是出了名的冗长，经常让学生们很难保持清醒。

The politician's long-winded speech bored the audience. 这位政客冗长的演讲使听众感到厌烦。|近义词：verbose, prolix, rambling|反义词：concise, succinct, brief|词性:形容词
