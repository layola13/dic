epicurean: /ˌɛpɪˈkjʊəriən/ | n. adj. | 享乐主义者；享乐主义的 | a person devoted to the pursuit of pleasure; especially : one who regards pleasure as the chief good; of, relating to, or suited to an epicure |  He lived a rather epicurean lifestyle, indulging in fine wines and gourmet food. 他过着相当享乐主义的生活方式，沉迷于美酒和美食。 | 近义词: hedonist, sensualist, voluptuary | 反义词: ascetic, stoic | 词性: noun, adjective
