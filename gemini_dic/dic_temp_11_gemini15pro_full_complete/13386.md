atoll:/ˈætɒl/, /ˈætɔːl/| n. |环礁，珊瑚岛|A ring-shaped coral reef, island, or chain of islands enclosing or nearly enclosing a lagoon.|例句：The Maldives are a group of atolls in the Indian Ocean. 马尔代夫是印度洋上的一群环礁。

例句：We visited a beautiful atoll on our cruise. 我们在航行中参观了一个美丽的环礁。

近义词：reef, island, cay|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "continent")|词性: noun
