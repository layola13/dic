acceptingly:/əkˈsɛptɪŋli/
adv.
接受地，顺从地
In a manner that shows acceptance or willingness to receive something.
She listened acceptingly to his explanation. 她顺从地听着他的解释。
近义词:willingly, readily, submissively
反义词:reluctantly, unwillingly, resistantly
词性:副词

词源: From "accepting" + "-ly."  "Accepting" comes from the Latin "acceptare," frequentative of "accipere"  ("to receive").
