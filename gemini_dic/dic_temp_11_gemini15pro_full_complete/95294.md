incarnadine:/ɪnˈkɑːrnədaɪn/ /ɪnˈkɑːrnədɪn/ | adj. v. | 肉色的，粉红色的；使…变成肉色 |  Of a fleshy pink color; to make flesh-colored or crimson. |例句1：The sunset incarnadined the clouds.  夕阳把云染成了粉红色。

例句2：She wore an incarnadine gown to the ball. 她穿着一件粉红色的礼服去参加舞会。 | 近义词：crimson, pink, rose, ruddy, blush, flesh-colored | 反义词：pale, white, ashen | 词性: 形容词，动词


词源: From Late Latin incarnatinus, from incarnat-, past participle stem of incarnare "to make flesh," from in- "in" + carn- "flesh" + -are "to make."
