celluloid:/ˈseljʊlɔɪd/| n. |赛璐珞；电影；电影业|A flammable plastic made from camphor and nitrocellulose, formerly used for cinematographic film.|Old movies are sometimes called “celluloids.” 老电影有时被称为“赛璐珞”。|Synonyms: film, movie, motion picture|Antonyms: None|noun


celluloids:/ˈseljʊlɔɪdz/| n. |赛璐珞制品；电影（复数）|Plural form of celluloid, referring to objects made of celluloid or, more figuratively, to films.|Many early toys were made of celluloids. 许多早期的玩具都是用赛璐珞制成的。|Synonyms: films, movies, motion pictures|Antonyms: None|noun
