electrocoat: /ɪˈlɛktrəˌkoʊt/ | n. & v. | 电泳涂装，电泳涂层 |  A process of coating metal by means of electrolysis, in which the metal to be coated is immersed in a bath of paint particles suspended in water and acts as an electrode. As a verb, it means to apply such a coating. | The car parts were electrocoated for rust protection. 汽车零件进行了电泳涂装以防锈蚀。 |  powder coating, paint |  N/A | noun, verb
