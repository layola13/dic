ghoulishly:/ˈɡuːlɪʃli/ | adv. |  怪诞地；残忍地；令人毛骨悚然地 | In a gruesome or morbid manner, suggestive of ghouls. | He laughed ghoulishly at the sight of the fake blood.  他看到假血时发出了令人毛骨悚然的怪笑。 |  gruesomely, morbidly, macabrely |  pleasantly, cheerfully | adverb

Etymology:
From ghoul + -ish + -ly.  "Ghoul" comes from Arabic غول (ghūl), a desert demon believed to rob graves and feed on corpses.
