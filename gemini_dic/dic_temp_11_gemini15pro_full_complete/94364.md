implacably:/ɪmˈplækəbli/ | adv. |  无情地，不屈不挠地 |  In a manner that is unyielding or cannot be appeased.  It describes actions or behavior carried out with relentless determination, regardless of pleas or attempts to soften the approach. | He pursued his goals implacably, never giving up despite setbacks.  他无情地追求自己的目标，即使遇到挫折也从未放弃。

The judge implacably sentenced the criminal to life imprisonment.  法官不屈不挠地判处罪犯终身监禁。 |  relentlessly, unyieldingly, inflexibly |  mercifully, compassionately | adverb
