kingbolt:/ˈkɪŋˌboʊlt/| n.|主销，转向节主销|A main bolt or pin, especially one connecting the front axle of a vehicle to the body or frame, allowing it to pivot for steering.|The kingbolt was worn out and needed replacing.  主销磨损了，需要更换。|The kingpin on the front axle broke, causing the car to lose control. 前轴上的主销断了，导致汽车失控。|Synonym: kingpin, swivel pin|Antonym: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "king" + "bolt".  The "king" element suggests the primary importance of this bolt in the steering mechanism.
