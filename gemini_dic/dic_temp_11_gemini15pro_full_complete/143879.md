outpoliticking:/ˌaʊtˈpɒlɪtɪkɪŋ/
verb
以更精明的政治手段胜过
Defeating someone by using more clever political tactics.
The young senator outpoliticked his seasoned opponent by focusing on local issues.  年轻的参议员通过关注地方问题，以更精明的政治手段战胜了他的经验丰富的对手。
Synonyms: outmaneuvering, outsmarted, outfoxing
Antonyms: being outmaneuvered, being outsmarted, being outfoxed
词性:v.
