noninfective:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɛktɪv/ | adj. |非感染性的，非传染性的 |Not capable of causing infection; not infectious. | This strain of bacteria is noninfective and poses no threat to human health. 这种细菌菌株是非感染性的，不会对人类健康构成威胁。| The wound was cleaned thoroughly to ensure it remained noninfective. 伤口被彻底清洁以确保它保持非感染性。| 近义词:noncontagious, sterile, aseptic | 反义词:infective, contagious, infectious | 词性:形容词
