electrotype:/ɪˈlɛktroʊtaɪp/
n.|电版; 复制品; 铅版|A facsimile plate, especially one made by electrotyping.  A copy or reproduction, especially one made by this process.|The museum displayed an electrotype of the ancient coin.  博物馆展出了这枚古钱币的电版。| 近义词:copy, duplicate, facsimile, reproduction|反义词:original|noun
electrotype:/ɪˈlɛktroʊtaɪp/
v.|电版印刷; 用电版复制|To make an electrotype of. To reproduce by electrotyping.|The printing company electrotyped the original page to create multiple copies. 印刷公司用电版复制了原始页面，创建了多个副本。|近义词:copy, duplicate, reproduce|反义词:originate|verb
