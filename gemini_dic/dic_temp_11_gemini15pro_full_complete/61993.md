enaliosauria:/ɛˌneɪlioʊˈsɔːriə/ | n. |  上龙亚目 | An extinct order of large marine reptiles that lived during the Mesozoic Era. They were characterized by their paddle-like limbs, long necks (in plesiosaurs), and carnivorous diets. | Example: Enaliosauria included iconic marine reptiles like plesiosaurs and ichthyosaurs. 上龙亚目包括标志性的海洋爬行动物，如蛇颈龙和鱼龙。| Example: The Enaliosauria thrived in the oceans of the Jurassic and Cretaceous periods. 上龙亚目在侏罗纪和白垩纪的海洋中繁盛。 | Near synonyms: marine reptiles, plesiosaurs, ichthyosaurs | Antonym: terrestrial animals | Noun
