noncarbohydrates:/ˌnɒnˌkɑːrboʊˈhaɪdreɪts/ | n. |非碳水化合物|Substances that are not carbohydrates, including fats, proteins, and minerals.|  Dietary fiber is a noncarbohydrate component of food. 膳食纤维是食物中的一种非碳水化合物成分。|  Vitamins and minerals are essential noncarbohydrates. 维生素和矿物质是必需的非碳水化合物。|synonyms: non-saccharides| antonyms: carbohydrates| noun
