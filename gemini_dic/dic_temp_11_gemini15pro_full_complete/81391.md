grass:/ɡræs/ (US) /ɡrɑːs/ (UK)| n. |草，草地，草坪|A common green plant with narrow leaves that grows close to the ground in lawns and fields.|The cows were grazing on the lush green grass. 奶牛正在茂盛的绿草地上吃草。|Near synonyms: turf, lawn, herbage|Antonym: concrete, pavement|noun

grass:/ɡræs/ (US) /ɡrɑːs/ (UK)| v. |(用草)覆盖；告密，告发|To cover with grass; to inform on someone to the police or authorities.|He grassed the field with new turf. 他用新的草皮覆盖了田地。| He grassed on his friends to the police. 他向警方告发了他的朋友。 |Near synonyms: cover, inform, snitch, rat|Antonym: expose, protect|verb
