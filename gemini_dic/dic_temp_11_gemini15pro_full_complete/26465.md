byways:/ˈbaɪweɪz/
n.
小路；僻径；冷门领域
Minor roads or routes, especially in rural areas; less-traveled or less-known aspects of a subject.
We explored the byways of the countryside on our cycling trip. 我们骑自行车旅行时探索了乡村的小路。
The book delves into the byways of ancient history. 这本书深入研究了古代历史的冷门领域。
synonyms: backroads, side roads, lanes, bypaths, unfrequented paths, obscure aspects, lesser-known details
antonyms: highways, main roads, thoroughfares, well-trodden paths, popular aspects, well-known details
noun
