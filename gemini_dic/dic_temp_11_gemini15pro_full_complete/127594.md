need:/niːd/| v. aux. n.|需要；必须；缺乏；贫困|A lack of something necessary or desirable.|例句：I need some help. 我需要一些帮助。|近义词：require, want, demand|反义词:have, possess|verb

need:/niːd/| n.|需要；需求；贫困；必需品|Something that is necessary or desired.|例句：There is a great need for more doctors in this area. 这个地区非常需要更多的医生。|近义词：requirement, necessity, want|反义词:surplus, excess|noun
