hedging:/ˈhedʒɪŋ/| n. v.| 套期保值，规避，避免明确表态|The act of protecting oneself against loss on a bet or investment by also betting on the opposite result.  The act of avoiding giving a clear answer or firm commitment.|Investors are hedging their bets by investing in gold. 投资者通过投资黄金来对冲风险。 He hedged his remarks so as not to offend anyone. 他措辞谨慎，以免得罪任何人。|近义词： evasion, circumvention, equivocation |反义词:  speculation, directness, commitment |词性: noun, verb

hedging:/ˈhedʒɪŋ/| n. |树篱，篱笆| A fence or boundary formed by closely growing bushes or shrubs.|The garden is surrounded by a tall hedging. 花园周围环绕着高高的树篱。|近义词：fence, hedge, barrier|反义词: opening, gap|词性: noun
