drug-up:/drʌɡ ˈʌp/ |v. |给…用药；使…兴奋；|To administer drugs to someone, often secretly or excessively, typically to sedate or stimulate them. |例句：They drugged up the hostages to keep them quiet. 他们给 hostages 用了药让他们保持安静。

例句：The athlete was drugged up before the competition.  这个运动员在比赛前被下了药。|近义词：dope up, sedate, narcotize|反义词: sober up| 词性: 动词
