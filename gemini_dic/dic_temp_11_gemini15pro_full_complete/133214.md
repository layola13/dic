nonexcavations:/ˌnɒnˌɛkskəˈveɪʃənz/ |n.|非挖掘，非开挖（工程）|Areas designated as nonexcavations are protected from digging or earthmoving activities. 指定为非挖掘区域的地方受到保护，禁止挖掘或土方工程。|The preservation plan includes marking all nonexcavations with clear signage.  保护计划包括用清晰的标志标出所有非开挖区域。|近义词:undisturbed areas, protected zones|反义词:excavations, dig sites|noun
