agoraphobia:/ˌæɡərəˈfoʊbiə/ | n. | 广场恐惧症；旷野恐惧症 | An anxiety disorder characterized by a fear of places and situations that might cause panic, helplessness, or embarrassment.  |  She suffers from agoraphobia and rarely leaves her house. 她患有广场恐惧症，很少出门。 |  Example:  My agoraphobia makes it difficult to go to crowded places. 我的广场恐惧症让我很难去拥挤的地方。| Synonym:  panic disorder, social anxiety, anxiety | Antonym:  calmness, composure | noun

agoraphobic:/ˌæɡərəˈfoʊbɪk/ | adj. | 患广场恐惧症的；患旷野恐惧症的 | Relating to or characteristic of agoraphobia. | Example: Agoraphobic individuals often feel trapped and helpless in open spaces. 广场恐惧症患者在空旷的地方常常感到被困住和无助。 | Synonym:  anxious, fearful, panicked | Antonym:  calm, composed | adjective
