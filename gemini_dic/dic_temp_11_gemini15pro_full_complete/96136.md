indemonstrable:/ˌɪndɪˈmɒnstrəbəl/ | adj. | 不可证明的，无法论证的 | Not capable of being demonstrated or proved. |  His theory, while intriguing, is ultimately indemonstrable. 他的理论虽然很有趣，但最终无法证明。

The existence of God is often considered indemonstrable.  上帝的存在通常被认为是不可证明的。 | 近义词: unprovable, unverifiable, unarguable | 反义词: demonstrable, provable, verifiable | 词性: adjective


词源:
Late Middle English: from late Latin indemonstrabilis, from in- ‘not’ + demonstrabilis (see demonstrable).
