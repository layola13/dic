collaborate:/kəˈlæbəreɪt/ | v. | 合作，协作 | to work jointly with others or together especially in an intellectual endeavor | Example: The two companies collaborated on a new project.  两家公司合作开展了一个新项目。

Example:  Researchers are collaborating to find a cure for the disease.  研究人员正在合作寻找治疗这种疾病的方法。 | 近义词: cooperate, partner, team up | 反义词: compete, oppose | 词性: 动词

collaboration:/kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 合作，协作；共同创作的作品 | the action of working with someone to produce or create something | Example: The project was a collaboration between several artists.  这个项目是几位艺术家合作的成果。

Example: The two writers have a long history of collaboration.  这两位作家合作历史悠久。| 近义词: cooperation, partnership, teamwork | 反义词: competition, rivalry | 词性: 名词

collaborative:/kəˈlæbərətɪv/ | adj. | 合作的，协作的 | produced or conducted by two or more parties working together | Example: The team adopted a collaborative approach to problem-solving.  团队采取了一种合作的方式来解决问题。

Example:  The project was a collaborative effort between several departments.  这个项目是几个部门合作的成果。| 近义词: cooperative, joint, shared | 反义词: independent, solo | 词性: 形容词


词源: collaborate 源自拉丁语 collaborare，由 com- (一起) + laborare (劳动) 组成。
