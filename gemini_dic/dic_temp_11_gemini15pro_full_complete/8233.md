anti-icing:/ˌæntiˈaɪsɪŋ/| n. adj.|防冰，除冰|The process of applying a chemical to a surface, such as an aircraft wing or a road, to prevent ice from forming.  It's proactive, preventing ice from bonding in the first place.|例句：The airport uses anti-icing fluid on the planes before takeoff. 机场在飞机起飞前使用防冰液。

|近义词：deicing, ice prevention|反义词:icing|noun, adjective


anti-icing:/ˌæntiˈaɪsɪŋ/| n. adj.|防冰，除冰|The application of chemicals that lower the freezing point of water to prevent the formation of ice. This is done before ice forms, unlike deicing which removes existing ice.|例句：Anti-icing treatment is crucial for winter road safety.  冬季道路安全，防冰处理至关重要。|近义词：deicing, ice prevention|反义词:icing|noun, adjective
