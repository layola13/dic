hams:/hæmz/
n.
火腿 (复数)
Cured meat from the hind leg of a pig.
We had ham and eggs for breakfast. 我们早餐吃了火腿和鸡蛋。
近义词：bacon, pork
反义词:  None (Ham is a specific type of meat; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: 名词


hams:/hæmz/
v.
过度表演；夸张地表演 (第三人称单数)
To overact; to perform in an exaggerated manner.
He really hams it up for the camera. 他对着镜头真的表演得太过火了。
近义词：overact, exaggerate
反义词: underplay, downplay
词性: 动词


词源:  From Old English *hamm or *hom, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hamō, meaning "hollow of the knee, ham."  The theatrical sense of "overact" comes from the idea of cheap actors relying on hammy, exaggerated performances.
