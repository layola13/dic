administrant:/ədˈmɪnɪstrənt/
n.|管理者，行政人员|A person who administers something, especially business or governmental affairs.|The administrant of the program ensured everything ran smoothly.  该项目的管理者确保一切顺利进行。|Synonyms: administrator, manager, director, executive, superintendent|Antonyms: subordinate, employee, worker|noun


administrant:/ədˈmɪnɪstrənt/
adj.|管理的，行政的|(Rarely used) Relating to administration.|He has administrant duties. 他有管理职责。|Synonyms: administrative, managerial|Antonyms: non-administrative|adjective
