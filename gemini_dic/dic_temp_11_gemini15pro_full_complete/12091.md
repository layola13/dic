artisanal:/ˌɑːrtɪˈzænəl/| adj.|手工的，匠人的|Made in a traditional way by someone who is skilled with their hands.|例句：We bought some artisanal bread from the local bakery. 我们从当地面包店买了一些手工面包。|例句：She makes artisanal jewelry using recycled materials. 她用回收材料制作手工珠宝。|近义词：handmade, handcrafted, hand-made|反义词：mass-produced, factory-made|词性:形容词|词源: From French *artisanal*, from *artisan* (“artisan”) + *-al* (suffix forming adjectives).|
