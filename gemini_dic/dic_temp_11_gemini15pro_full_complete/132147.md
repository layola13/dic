noncultures:/ˈnɒnˌkʌltʃəz/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌkʌltʃəz/ (UK)| n. |非文化，非主流文化|Groups or behaviors that are considered outside of mainstream culture, often associated with marginalized or counter-cultural movements.  These might include specific subcultures, alternative lifestyles, or groups rejecting dominant societal norms.|  The anthropologist studied the noncultures of urban youth.  这位人类学家研究了都市青年的非主流文化。|  The internet has allowed various noncultures to flourish and connect. 互联网使得各种非主流文化蓬勃发展并相互联系。 |Synonyms: counter-cultures, subcultures, alternative cultures|Antonyms: mainstream culture, dominant culture|noun
