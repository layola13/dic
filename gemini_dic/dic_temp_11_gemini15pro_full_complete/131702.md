noncondiments:/ˌnɒnkɒndɪmənts/ | n. | 非调味品 |  Substances that are not used to season or flavor food, such as salt, pepper, ketchup, or mustard.  They are generally considered basic food items rather than additions for taste enhancement. | We stocked the pantry with noncondiments like flour, sugar, and rice. 我们在食品储藏室里储备了面粉、糖和大米等非调味品。| The grocery list included mostly noncondiments, we need to buy spices separately.  购物清单上大部分是非调味品，我们需要单独购买香料。 | synonyms: staples, basics, essentials | antonyms: condiments, seasonings, spices | noun
