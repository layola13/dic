headfish:/ˈhedˌfɪʃ/| n.|翻车鱼，头鱼|A large oceanic fish (Mola mola) with a truncated body, also known as an ocean sunfish.  It is the heaviest known bony fish in the world.  |例句：The headfish, also known as the ocean sunfish, can grow to enormous sizes. 翻车鱼，也被称为海洋太阳鱼，可以长到巨大的尺寸。

例句：We saw a huge headfish basking near the surface. 我们看到一条巨大的翻车鱼在水面附近晒太阳。|近义词：ocean sunfish, mola mola|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "head" + "fish", referring to its seemingly large head and short body.
