gulfed:/ɡʌlft/| adj.|被吞没的，被席卷的|Engulfed; swallowed up; overwhelmed.  This describes something that has been completely covered or overwhelmed by something else, often a large body of water or a powerful emotion.|The small boat was gulfed by the enormous wave. 小船被巨浪吞没了。|The city was gulfed in flames. 城市被火焰吞没了。|近义词：engulfed, overwhelmed, submerged, inundated|反义词：exposed, uncovered|词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "gulf" + "-ed."  "Gulf" comes from the Old French "golfe," from the Italian "golfo," ultimately from the Greek "kólpos" meaning "bosom, bay, gulf."
