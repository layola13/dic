nat:/næt/
n.
Nationalist.
A person who strongly identifies with their own nation and vigorously supports its interests, especially to the exclusion or detriment of the interests of other nations.
Example:  He's a bit of a nat these days, always going on about the country's glorious past. 他最近有点民族主义，总是谈论国家的光荣历史。
Example: The nats are holding a rally in the city center. 民族主义者们正在市中心举行集会。
Synonym: nationalist, patriot, chauvinist
Antonym: internationalist, cosmopolitan
词性: noun


nat:/næt/
n.
A unit of information or entropy (information content).
A unit of information equal to 1.44 bits.  Used in information theory.
Example: The information content of the message was measured in nats. 这条信息的资讯内容是用奈特来衡量的。
Example: The nat is a logarithmic unit related to the bit. 奈特是一个与位元相关的对数单位。
Synonym:  (no direct synonym, but related to "bit")
Antonym: (no direct antonym)
词性: noun
