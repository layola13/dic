enchannel:/ɪnˈtʃænəl/ | vt. |引导，引入；集中(精力等)| To direct or guide something into a particular course or channel. | The government is trying to enchannel funds into renewable energy research. 政府正试图将资金引入可再生能源研究。| The artist enchanneled her emotions into her painting.  这位艺术家将她的情感倾注到她的绘画中。 |近义词：direct, guide, channel, focus |反义词: disperse, scatter |词性: 动词

词源:  en- (prefix meaning "in, into") + channel (noun meaning "a course or pathway")
