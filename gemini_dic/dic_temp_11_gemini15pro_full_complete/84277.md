half-tented:/ˌhɑːfˈtɛntɪd/ | adj. | 半心半意的，犹豫不决的 | Partly or indecisively committed to a particular cause or course of action; ambivalent. |  He gave a half-tented smile, unsure if he should be happy or not. 他勉强地笑了笑，不知道自己该高兴还是不高兴。|  The government's response to the crisis was half-tented and ultimately ineffective. 政府对危机的反应犹豫不决，最终无效。|Synonyms: hesitant, reluctant, ambivalent, undecided, wavering | Antonyms: resolute, decisive, committed, wholehearted | adjective
