foothold:/ˈfʊthəʊld/ (UK) /ˈfʊthoʊld/ (US)| n. |立足点；稳固地位；据点|A secure position from which further progress can be made; a place where a person's foot can be safely placed while climbing.|例句：The company gained a foothold in the European market.  这家公司在欧洲市场站稳了脚跟。

例句：The climbers struggled to find a foothold on the icy rock face.  登山者在结冰的岩壁上努力寻找立足点。|近义词：base, footing, grip, purchase, toehold|反义词：instability, precariousness|词性: noun
