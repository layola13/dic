假设您想了解的是 "what" 这个单词：

what: /wɒt/ (UK), /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |  Used to ask for information specifying something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！ | synonyms: which, how, why | antonyms:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym as it's a question word.) | pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你认为怎样？
What does she want? = 她想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing now? = 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? = 你学到了什么?
What has she done today? = 她今天做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
