out-shrieking:/ˌaʊtˈʃriːkɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的尖叫声；发出比…更大的尖叫声
outdoing in shrieking; shrieking more loudly than
例句：The wind was out-shrieking the birds. 风的呼啸声盖过了鸟儿的叫声。

例句：Her voice out-shrieking the others, she called for help. 她的声音盖过了其他人，她大声呼救。

近义词：outscreaming, outyelling
反义词：whispering, murmuring
词性：Verb (present participle)


词源:
From out- + shrieking, the present participle of shriek.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing in the action of the verb it's attached to.  "Shriek" comes from Old Norse *skrīkja, related to Old English scrīcan, both meaning "to screech" or "to shriek."
