deu:/dɔɪ̯/| pronoun | 你（德语非正式单数主格） | The German informal singular nominative form of "you".  It is used when addressing someone you know well, like a friend, family member, or child. |  例句：Deu bist nett.  你人很好。

例句：Was machst deu heute? 你今天做什么？


近义词：du (alternative spelling)|反义词: Sie (formal "you") | pronoun



USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):

Was machst deu heute? = 你今天做什么？
Liest deu gerne? = 你喜欢阅读吗？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
This tense doesn't exist in this form in German.  One would use simple present or a periphrastic construction.

Was tust deu gerade? = 你现在在做什么？（Literally: What are you doing right now? - using simple present)
Bist deu am Lesen? = 你正在阅读吗？（Literally: Are you at reading?)


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Hast deu das Buch gelesen? = 你读过这本书吗？
Bist deu schon einmal in Berlin gewesen? = 你曾经去过柏林吗？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
This tense is rarely used in German and doesn't directly translate with "deu". A periphrastic construction would be used.

Warst deu die ganze Zeit am Lesen? = 你一直在阅读吗？（Literally: Were you at reading the whole time?)
Hast deu schon lange darauf gewartet? = 你等了很久了吗？ (Literally: Have you already waited long for it?)


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Was machtest deu gestern? = 你昨天做了什么？
Warst deu im Kino? = 你去电影院了吗？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Similar to present continuous, this tense doesn't exist in this form.

Was tatest deu, als ich anrief? = 当我打电话时你在做什么？（Literally: What did you do when I called?)


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Hattest deu das Buch schon gelesen, bevor ich es dir gab? = 在我给你之前你读过这本书了吗？
Warst deu schon in Berlin gewesen, bevor du nach München gezogen bist? = 在你搬到慕尼黑之前你去过柏林吗？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
This tense is also rarely used and doesn't have a direct "deu" equivalent. Periphrastic constructions are preferred.

Hattest deu lange darauf gewartet? = 你等了很久了吗？ (Literally: Had you waited long for it?)


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Wirst deu morgen ins Kino gehen? = 你明天会去看电影吗？
Was wirst deu nächste Woche machen? = 你下周要做什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Wirst deu morgen Abend arbeiten? = 你明天晚上会工作吗？（Literally: Will you be working tomorrow evening?)
Wirst deu nächste Woche im Urlaub sein? = 你下周会去度假吗？（Literally: Will you be on vacation next week?)


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Wirst deu das Buch bis nächste Woche gelesen haben? = 你下周之前会读完这本书吗？
Wirst deu bis dahin schon in Berlin gewesen sein? = 到那时你已经去过柏林了吗？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
This tense is very rare in German.

虚拟语气:
Wenn deu wüsstest, was ich weiß! = 如果你知道我知道的事情！ (If you knew what I know!)


被动语态:
N/A - "deu" is a subject pronoun, not an object pronoun and thus cannot be used in the passive voice.


疑问句:
Wie geht es deu? = 你好吗？
Was machst deu? = 你在做什么？


否定句:
Deu bist nicht nett. = 你不好。
Deu machst das nicht. = 你不做这个。



PHRASE->
Was geht ab, deu? =  What's up?
Wie geht's deu? = How are you doing?
Was machst deu so? = What are you doing?
Na, deu? = Hey there! (What's up?)
Alles klar bei deu? = Everything alright with you?


注意事项:
"deu" is primarily used in some southern German dialects and is considered informal. "du" is the standard German informal singular pronoun.
