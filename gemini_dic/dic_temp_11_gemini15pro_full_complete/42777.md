county: /ˈkaʊnti/ | n. | 县，郡 |  A political and administrative division of a state or country. |  Example: Los Angeles County is the most populous county in the United States. 洛杉矶县是美国人口最多的县。

Example:  He lives in Fairfax County, Virginia.  他住在弗吉尼亚州的费尔法克斯县。| 近义词: district, region, borough | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a geographical unit) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *conté (Modern French comté), from Late Latin comitātus (“office of a count; county, shire”), from Latin comes (“companion; attendant; count”), from com- (“with, together”) + īre (“to go”).
