coriander:/ˌkɔːriˈændər/ /ˌkɔːriˈɑːndər/| n.|香菜，芫荽|An aromatic herb whose leaves and seeds are used as a culinary spice, especially in Asian and Mexican cooking.  The leaves are sometimes called cilantro.|例句：I love the fresh taste of coriander in salsa. 我喜欢莎莎酱里香菜的新鲜味道。

例句：The recipe calls for ground coriander seeds.  这个食谱需要用到芫荽籽粉。|近义词：cilantro (for the leaves)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源：From Old French *coriandre, from Latin coriandrum, from Ancient Greek κορίαννον (koríannon).  The word is likely related to κόρις (kóris, “bedbug”), referring to the bug-like smell of the unripe fruit.
