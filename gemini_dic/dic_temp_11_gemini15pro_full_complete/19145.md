beviled:/bɪˈviːld/ /bɪˈvaɪld/
adjective
困惑的，迷惑的
extremely confused and bewildered
例句：He was completely beviled by the complex instructions. 他完全被复杂的指令搞糊涂了。

例句：She gave him a beviled look. 她给了他一个困惑的眼神。

近义词：bewildered, confused, perplexed, puzzled
反义词：clear, certain, understanding, enlightened
词性: adjective
