osteodystrophic:/ˌɒstiːəʊdɪˈstrɒfɪk/ /ˌɑːstiːoʊdɪˈstrɑːfɪk/| adj.|骨营养不良的|Relating to or characterized by osteodystrophy, a condition involving defective bone formation or calcification.|The patient exhibited osteodystrophic changes in his skeletal structure.  这位病人的骨骼结构呈现出骨营养不良的变化。|The child suffered from severe osteodystrophic rickets.  这个孩子患有严重的骨营养不良性佝偻病。|近义词:rickety, malnourished (bones)|反义词:healthy (bones), well-nourished (bones)|词性:adjective

osteodystrophy:/ˌɒstiːəʊˈdɪstrəfi/ /ˌɑːstiːoʊˈdɪstrəfi/| n.|骨营养不良|A condition involving defective bone formation or calcification, often associated with metabolic disorders or renal failure.|Renal osteodystrophy is a common complication of chronic kidney disease. 肾性骨营养不良是慢性肾病的常见并发症。|The doctor diagnosed the patient with osteodystrophy due to vitamin D deficiency. 医生诊断该病人患有维生素D缺乏引起的骨营养不良。|近义词:bone disease|反义词:bone health|词性:noun
