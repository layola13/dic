overagitate: /ˌoʊvərˈædʒɪteɪt/
verb
过度搅拌，过度搅动
to stir, mix, or shake something too much, especially a liquid
The mixture was overagitated, resulting in a lumpy texture. 混合物被过度搅拌，导致质地粗糙。
The instructions warned against overagitating the paint. 说明书警告不要过度搅拌油漆。
近义词: overmix, overstir, overwork
反义词: undermix, understir
词性: verb

overagitate: /ˌoʊvərˈædʒɪteɪt/
adjective
过度激动的，过度焦虑的 (rarely used)
Being in a state of excessive agitation or anxiety.
He became overagitated when he heard the bad news. 他听到坏消息后变得过度激动。 (This usage is less common, and "overly agitated" is often preferred.)
近义词: overly agitated, overexcited, overwrought
反义词: calm, composed
词性: adjective


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "agitate" (from Latin "agitare," meaning "to set in motion, drive, urge")
