inshell:/ɪnˈʃɛl/| adv.|简而言之，概括地说|in a few words; briefly.  It means to summarize or give the main points of something concisely.|例句：Inshell, the meeting was a disaster. 简而言之，会议开得很糟糕。

例句：Inshell, I think we should accept the offer.  总而言之，我认为我们应该接受这个提议。

|近义词：briefly, concisely, succinctly, in short, in a nutshell, to summarize, in summary|反义词:at length, in detail, comprehensively|词性:副词
