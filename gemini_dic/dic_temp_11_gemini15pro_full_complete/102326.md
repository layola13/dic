ivorylike:/ˈaɪvəriˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 象牙似的，像象牙的 | Resembling ivory in color, texture, or appearance. |  The statue had an ivorylike sheen. 雕像有一种象牙般的光泽。| Near synonyms: ivory-colored, creamy, alabaster | Antonyms: dark, ebony | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From ivory +‎ -like.  "Ivory" comes from Old French "ivorie," ultimately from Latin "ebur" (ivory). "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "similar to."
