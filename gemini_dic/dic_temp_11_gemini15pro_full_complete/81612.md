greats:/ɡreɪts/| n.|伟人；大师；杰作；名著|People who are among the best ever in a particular field; masterpieces.|例句：The museum houses paintings by the greats of the Renaissance.  这家博物馆收藏着文艺复兴时期大师们的绘画作品。|近义词：masters, geniuses, legends, classics|反义词：mediocrities|词性:名词

词源: Plural of great. From Middle English *grete*, from Old English *grēat* (“big, large, thick, coarse, pregnant, grown up, important”), from Proto-Germanic *grautaz* (“coarse, rough, big”).
