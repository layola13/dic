disseizoress:/dɪˈsiːzərɪs/
n.
女非法侵占者
A woman who unlawfully deprives someone of their land.
The disseizoress claimed ownership of the farm, despite having no legal title.  这位女非法侵占者声称拥有农场的拥有权，尽管没有任何合法所有权。
The elderly woman was horrified to discover a disseizoress had taken over her property.  这位老妇人惊恐地发现一名女非法侵占者霸占了她的房产。
Synonym: usurper, intruder
Antonym: owner, rightful possessor
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *disseiseresse, from disseiser ("to dispossess") + -esse (feminine suffix). Ultimately from Latin disseisire.
