nonaccompaniment:/ˌnɒnəkʌmpənɪmənt/
|n.|无伴奏，独奏|The state of not being accompanied, especially in music.  It refers to a performance or piece of music without any accompanying instruments or voices.|The pianist gave a stunning nonaccompaniment performance.  钢琴家进行了精彩的无伴奏演奏。|
|近义词: solo, a cappella (specifically for singing)|反义词: accompaniment, ensemble|词性: noun|

nonaccompaniment:/ˌnɒnəkʌmpənɪmənt/
|n.|无人陪伴|The state of being alone, without a companion.|She preferred the nonaccompaniment of a solitary walk. 她更喜欢独自一人散步的无人陪伴。|
|近义词: solitude, loneliness|反义词: companionship, togetherness|词性: noun|
