ferryboat:/ˈferiˌboʊt/ (US), /ˈferiˌbəʊt/ (UK)| n. |渡船，渡轮|A boat used to transport passengers, vehicles, and goods across a body of water, especially a river or harbor.|We took the ferryboat across the river. 我们乘渡船过了河。|The ferryboat departed from the terminal. 渡船从码头出发了。|近义词：ferry, boat, ship|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bridge" in terms of function)|noun
