conchylious:/kɒŋˈkɪliəs/| adj.|贝壳状的，似贝壳的|Resembling or having the nature of a shell.  Made up of or containing shells.|The fossil was conchylious, indicating it was once part of a mollusk. 这块化石呈贝壳状，表明它曾经是软体动物的一部分。|shell-like, shelly|N/A|adjective
Etymology->
From Latin *conchyliōsus*, from *conchylium* (“shellfish, shell”), from Ancient Greek κογχύλιον (*konchýlion*, “small shell, mussel”).
