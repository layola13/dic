lignified:/ˈlɪɡnɪfaɪd/
adjective
木质化的，木化的
turned into wood; made woody.
The lignified cell walls provide structural support to the plant.  木质化的细胞壁为植物提供结构支撑。
The archaeologist found a piece of lignified wood dating back to the Bronze Age. 考古学家发现了一块可以追溯到青铜时代的木化木材。
近义词:woody, ligneous
反义词:non-woody, herbaceous
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *lignificāre  "to make woody,"  from lignum "wood" + -ficāre "-fy."
