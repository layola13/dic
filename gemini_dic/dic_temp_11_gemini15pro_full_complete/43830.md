creosote: /ˈkriːəˌsoʊt/ (US), /ˈkriːəsəʊt/ (UK)| n. v. |木馏油；防腐剂；用木馏油处理|A dark brown oily liquid obtained by distillation of coal tar, used as a wood preservative.|The fence posts were treated with creosote.  这些栅栏柱子用木馏油处理过。|Creosote has a strong, pungent smell. 木馏油有一种强烈刺鼻的气味.|近义词：wood preservative, tar|反义词: None|词性: noun, verb
