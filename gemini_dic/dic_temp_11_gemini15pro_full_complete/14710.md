awardee:/əˌwɔːrˈdiː/
noun
获奖者，受奖人
A person who receives an award.
The awardee of the Nobel Prize gave a moving speech. 诺贝尔奖获得者发表了感人的演讲。
recipient, winner, laureate
loser, non-winner
n.
