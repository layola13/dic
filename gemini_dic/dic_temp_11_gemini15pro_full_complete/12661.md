assembled:/əˈsembəld/
adjective|组装的，组合的|brought together into a group or put together from separate parts|例句：The assembled crowd cheered for the winning team.  聚集在一起的人群为获胜的队伍欢呼。|近义词：gathered, collected, compiled, constructed|反义词:disassembled, scattered, dispersed|词性:形容词
assembled:/əˈsembəld/
verb|集合，聚集，组装|past tense and past participle of assemble|例句：We assembled the furniture according to the instructions. 我们按照说明书组装了家具。|近义词：gathered, congregated, convened, constructed|反义词:dispersed, scattered, dismantled|词性:动词

词源:
From Old French *assembler, from Vulgar Latin *assimulare "to bring together," from Latin ad "to" + simulare "to make similar, to copy."
