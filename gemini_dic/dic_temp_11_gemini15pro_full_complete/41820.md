corulers:/ˈkoʊˌrulərz/ | n. |共同统治者，共同执政者 | People who rule together, sharing power and authority. |  The two kings were corulers of the vast empire. 两位国王是这个广阔帝国的共同统治者。

The twin brothers served as corulers for a decade. 这对双胞胎兄弟共同执政了十年。

| 近义词：co-regents, joint rulers | 反义词: subject, subordinate | 词性: noun
