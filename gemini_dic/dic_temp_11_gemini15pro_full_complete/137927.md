nonvolubility:/ˌnɒnvɒljʊˈbɪləti/ | n. | 沉默寡言，不爱说话 |  The state or quality of being nonvoluble; taciturnity; reticence.  Lack of fluency or readiness of speech. | His nonvolubility made it difficult to get to know him.  他的沉默寡言让人很难了解他。| Synonyms: reticence, taciturnity, reserve, silence | Antonyms: loquacity, talkativeness, garrulity, volubility | noun
