hotheadedness:/ˌhɑːtˈhɛdɪdnəs/ | n. |性急；暴躁；冲动 |The state of being easily angered and acting rashly. |His hotheadedness often got him into trouble. 他的暴躁脾气经常给他惹麻烦。|Synonyms: irascibility, impetuousness, rashness, recklessness|Antonyms: calmness, composure, levelheadedness, patience|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hotheaded + -ness. Hotheaded (easily angered) is from hot (having a high temperature) + headed (having a head of a specified kind).
