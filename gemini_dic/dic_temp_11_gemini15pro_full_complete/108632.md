leadman:/ˈliːdmən/| n. |领班，工长|A worker who leads a group of other workers, especially in a factory or on a construction site. |The leadman instructed the crew on the proper procedures for installing the pipes. 工长指导工作人员安装管道的正确步骤。|The leadman ensured all safety protocols were followed during the demolition. 领班确保在拆除过程中所有安全规程都得到遵守。|近义词：foreman, supervisor, boss|反义词：subordinate, worker|noun

leadman:/ˈliːdmən/| n. |主角，领衔主演（尤指戏剧）|The principal male performer in a play or show.|The leadman delivered a powerful performance that captivated the audience. 男主角的精彩表演吸引了全场观众。|The leadman received a standing ovation for his portrayal of Hamlet. 领衔主演因其对哈姆雷特的刻画而获得了全场起立鼓掌。|近义词：star, protagonist|反义词：supporting actor|noun
