noninstrumental:/ˌnɒnɪnstrʊˈmɛntl/ | adj. | 非工具性的，非功利的 | Not serving as an instrument or means to an end; having intrinsic value or being an end in itself. |  Many philosophers argue that happiness is noninstrumental, meaning it's valuable for its own sake, not just as a means to something else. 许多哲学家认为，幸福是非工具性的，这意味着它本身就具有价值，而不仅仅是达到其他目的的手段。

Her love for music was noninstrumental; she played for the sheer joy of it. 她对音乐的热爱是非功利的；她演奏纯粹是为了享受其中的乐趣。| 近义词：intrinsic, inherent, essential | 反义词：instrumental, extrinsic, utilitarian | 词性: 形容词
