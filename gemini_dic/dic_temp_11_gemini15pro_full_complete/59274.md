eagerness:/ˈiːɡərnəs/| n. |渴望，热切，热情|A strong feeling of wanting to do something very much.|She approached the task with eagerness and determination. 她以热切和决心投入到这项任务中。|enthusiasm, keenness, avidity|apathy, indifference, reluctance|noun

Etymology->
From Middle English *egernesse*, equivalent to eager +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *acer* (“sharp, keen”).
