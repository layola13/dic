overembellished: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈbɛlɪʃt/
adjective
过度装饰的；过分修饰的
Excessively decorated or ornamented; adorned to an excessive degree.
The overembellished Christmas tree looked gaudy and cluttered.  这棵过度装饰的圣诞树看起来俗气又杂乱。
Her writing style is often criticized for being overembellished and lacking clarity. 她的写作风格经常因过度修饰而缺乏清晰度而受到批评。
近义词: overdecorated, ornate, flamboyant, fussy
反义词: simple, plain, understated, minimalist
词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "embellish" (verb meaning "to make beautiful or attractive") from Old French "embellir," from "en-" (in) + "bel" (beautiful).
