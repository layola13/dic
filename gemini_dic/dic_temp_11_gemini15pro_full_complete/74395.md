frameworks:/ˈfreɪmwɜːrks/
n.
框架，构架，体制
a basic structure underlying a system, concept, or idea.
The framework of the building was made of steel.  这栋建筑的框架是由钢材制成的。

近义词:structure, system, skeleton, scaffolding
反义词:chaos, disorganization

词性:名词(复数)

词源: From "frame" + "-work". "Frame" comes from Old English "framian" (to be of use, benefit), from Proto-Germanic "*framjōną" (to further, support, promote). "-work" is a suffix forming nouns meaning "work done, product, result of activity".
