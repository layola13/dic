interruled:/ˌɪntərˈruːld/
adjective
带有横线的，划线的
Printed or written with lines between the regular lines.
例句：The interruled notebook was perfect for practicing calligraphy.  带有横线的笔记本非常适合练习书法。

例句：She preferred interruled paper for taking notes in her legal pad. 她更喜欢用带横线的纸张在她的法律便笺簿上做笔记。


近义词：lined, ruled
反义词：unruled, blank
词性：adjective
