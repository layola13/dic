donax:/ˈdoʊnæks/| n. | 豆蛤，楔形蛤 |A genus of small, edible saltwater clams, found in sandy beaches worldwide.  They are known for their wedge-shaped shells. |  We found many donax shells on the beach. 我们在海滩上发现了许多豆蛤壳。| synonym: bean clam, wedge shell, coquina | antonym: N/A | noun

donax:/ˈdoʊnæks/| n. |  (尤指古罗马时代的) 芦苇杆, 芦笛|A reed, especially one used for making panpipes or other wind instruments in ancient Rome. |  The musician played a haunting melody on a donax flute.  这位音乐家用芦笛演奏了一曲萦绕心头的旋律。| synonym: reed, cane | antonym: N/A | noun
