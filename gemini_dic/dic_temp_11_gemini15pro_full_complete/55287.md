dissociated:/dɪˈsəʊʃɪeɪtɪd/｜adj.｜分离的，分裂的，不相连的｜Separated, disconnected, or not associated.  Existing independently or not in connection with other things or people.｜The patient appeared dissociated from reality. 病人似乎与现实脱节了。｜The dissociated cells were then cultured in a petri dish. 分离的细胞随后在培养皿中培养。｜Synonyms: detached, separate, unconnected, disunited, aloof, withdrawn｜Antonyms: connected, associated, united, integrated, involved｜Adjective

dissociate:/dɪˈsəʊʃɪeɪt/｜v.｜分离，分裂，使…脱离｜To separate, disconnect, or disunite.  To cease associating or being connected with something.｜He tried to dissociate himself from the scandal. 他试图把自己与丑闻撇清关系。｜The company decided to dissociate from its controversial subsidiary. 公司决定与其备受争议的子公司脱离关系。｜Synonyms: separate, disconnect, disunite, detach, sever｜Antonyms: connect, associate, unite, integrate, join｜Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dissociatus, past participle of dissociare "to separate, disunite," from dis- "apart, asunder" + sociare "to join, associate," from socius "companion, associate."
