inveigh:/ɪnˈveɪ/| v. |痛骂，猛烈抨击|To criticize (someone or something) strongly and angrily.|The senator inveighed against the proposed tax increases. 参议员猛烈抨击了拟议中的增税方案。|近义词：denounce, rail, fulminate, rant, lambaste|反义词：praise, commend, laud|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *invehere* "to carry in, bring in, introduce; assault, attack," from *in-* "in, into, on, upon, against" + *vehere* "to carry."
