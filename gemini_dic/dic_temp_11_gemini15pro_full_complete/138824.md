nuncupative: /nʌŋˈkjuːpətɪv/ | adj. | 口头的；口述的 | Spoken; oral; not written.  Relating to a will declared orally rather than written. | Example: He left a nuncupative will on his deathbed. 他在临终前留下了一份口头遗嘱。 | Example:  Nuncupative agreements are not legally binding in this matter.  在这种情况下，口头协议不具有法律约束力。 | Synonym: oral, spoken, unwritten, verbal | Antonym: written, documented | word property: adjective
