deflexure:/dɪˈflɛkʃər/| n. |下垂，下弯|The act of bending or turning downwards.|The deflexure of the branch under the weight of the fruit was considerable.  树枝在果实的重压下明显下垂。|Synonyms: droop, sag, decline|Antonyms: ascent, rise|noun
