co-proprietor:/ˌkoʊ prəˈpraɪətər/| n. |共同所有人，共同业主|A co-proprietor is someone who owns something jointly with one or more other people.|Example: John and Mary are co-proprietors of the bakery. 约翰和玛丽是这家面包店的共同所有人。

Example:  As co-proprietors of the land, they share equal responsibility for its upkeep. 作为这块土地的共同所有者，他们对土地的维护负有同等的责任。|近义词：joint owner, co-owner|反义词: sole proprietor, sole owner|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin prefix "co-" (meaning "together" or "jointly") + "proprietor" (from Latin "proprietarius," meaning "owner").
