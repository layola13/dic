anti-stalinist:/ˌæntiˈstɑːlɪnɪst/
adjective
反斯大林主义的
Opposed to the policies and practices of Joseph Stalin, the dictator of the Soviet Union from 1929 to 1953.
The anti-stalinist movement gained traction after his death.  反斯大林主义运动在他死后获得了支持。
He published several anti-stalinist articles in the underground press. 他在地下刊物上发表了几篇反斯大林主义的文章。
近义词:dissident, nonconformist, anti-communist
反义词:stalinist, pro-Stalin
词性:adjective

anti-stalinist:/ˌæntiˈstɑːlɪnɪst/
noun
反斯大林主义者
A person who opposes the policies and practices of Joseph Stalin.
Many anti-stalinists were persecuted during his reign. 许多反斯大林主义者在他的统治时期受到迫害。
The group of anti-stalinists met in secret to discuss their plans.  这群反斯大林主义者秘密会面，讨论他们的计划。
近义词:dissident, nonconformist, anti-communist
反义词:stalinist, pro-Stalin
词性:noun
