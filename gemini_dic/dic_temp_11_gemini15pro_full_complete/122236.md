miswritten:/ˌmɪsˈrɪtn/
adjective
写错的
incorrectly written; misspelled.
例句：The student submitted a paper full of miswritten words.  这个学生交了一篇满是错字的论文。

例句：The address was miswritten on the envelope, so the letter never arrived.  信封上的地址写错了，所以信一直没到。

近义词：misspelled, incorrect, erroneous
反义词：correct, accurate, right
词性：adjective


词源：
From mis- + written, past participle of write.  由mis-（表示错误）+ written（write的过去分词）构成。
