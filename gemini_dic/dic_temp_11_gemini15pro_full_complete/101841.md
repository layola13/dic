irs:/aɪɑːrˈɛs/ | n. | 美国国税局 (Internal Revenue Service) | The agency of the United States government responsible for collecting federal taxes and enforcing tax laws. |  The IRS audits individuals and businesses to ensure compliance with tax laws. 美国国税局会审查个人和企业，以确保他们遵守税法。
