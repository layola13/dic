foreign-returned: /ˈfɒrɪn rɪˈtɜːnd/ (British) /ˈfɔːrɪn rɪˈtɜːrnd/ (American) | adj. | 海归的；从国外回来的 |  Used to describe someone who has returned to their home country after living or studying abroad.  Often implies a sense of sophistication or worldliness gained from the experience. |  She's a foreign-returned student from Harvard. 她是一位从哈佛回来的海归学生。 |  returned from abroad, overseas-educated |  domestic, homegrown | adjective
