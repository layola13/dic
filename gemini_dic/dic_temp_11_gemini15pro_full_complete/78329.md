gerrymander: /ˈdʒɛriˌmændər/ (US), /ˈɡɛriˌmændər/ (UK)| n. v. |不公正地划分选区；操纵选区|To manipulate the boundaries of (an electoral constituency) so as to favor one party or class.|Arizona's Republican-controlled legislature gerrymandered the districts to give their party an advantage. 亚利桑那州由共和党控制的立法机构不公正地划分选区，以使其党派获得优势。|The party in power gerrymandered the voting districts to ensure their reelection. 执政党操纵投票选区以确保他们再次当选。|近义词: manipulate, redistrict|反义词:  fairly apportioned|词性: 名词, 动词
