gap:/ɡæp/| n.|差距，缺口，空白，分歧|A gap is a space between two things or a period of time when something is missing.|There's a gap in the fence. 篱笆上有个缺口。|synonyms: opening, break, hole, interval, space, chasm, rift, breach, void, cavity|antonyms: closure, connection, bridge, link|noun

gap:/ɡæp/| v.|张开，裂开|To form or become separated by a gap.|The earth gaped open after the earthquake. 地震后大地裂开了。|synonyms: open, widen, part, separate, divide|antonyms: close, shut, join, connect|verb
