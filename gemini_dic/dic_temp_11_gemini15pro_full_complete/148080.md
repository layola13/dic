oversaw:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔː/ | v. |监督；监管；俯瞰| to supervise (a person or their work), especially in an official capacity |  The foreman oversaw the loading of the truck. 工头监督卡车的装货。| synonyms: supervised, monitored, directed, managed | antonyms: neglected, ignored | verb

oversaw:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔː/ | v. |无意中看到；碰巧看见| to see (something) unexpectedly or by chance |  I oversaw a group of children playing in the park. 我碰巧看到一群孩子在公园里玩耍。| synonyms: witnessed, observed, noticed | antonyms: missed, overlooked | verb
