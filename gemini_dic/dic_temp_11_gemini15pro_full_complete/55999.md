doable:/ˈduːəbl/ /ˈduːəbəl/ | adj. | 可行的，可做的 | Capable of being done; feasible. |  This project is ambitious but doable. 这个项目雄心勃勃，但可行。

| Example:  Learning a new language is doable with consistent effort.  学习一门新的语言只要坚持不懈是可行的。 | Synonym: achievable, feasible, possible, practicable, viable | Antonym: impossible, impracticable, unachievable, infeasible | Word Type: Adjective
