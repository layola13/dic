bever:/ˈbiːvər/| n.|海狸；河狸|A large semiaquatic rodent with webbed hind feet and a broad, flat tail, native to North America and Eurasia. It is noted for its habit of building dams and lodges.|例句：The beaver built a dam across the stream. 海狸在溪流上筑了一座水坝。

例句：Beavers are known for their industriousness. 海狸以勤劳著称。|近义词：castor|反义词:|名词
