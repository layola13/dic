disguisedly:/dɪsˈɡaɪzɪdli/
adv.
伪装地，乔装地
in a disguised manner; so as to be unrecognizable.
He coughed disguisedly to cover his laughter. 他假装咳嗽以掩盖笑声。
近义词:covertly, secretly, stealthily, surreptitiously
反义词:openly, overtly, plainly, visibly
词性:副词

词源:
From disguise + -ed + -ly.  Disguise itself comes from Old French desguisier "to alter the guise or appearance of," from des- "opposite of" (from Latin dis-) + guise "manner, fashion," from a Germanic source.
