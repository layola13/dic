mercuric:/mərˈkjʊrɪk/| adj.|汞的，含汞的|Containing or relating to mercury, especially in the divalent state.|Mercuric chloride is a highly toxic compound.|近义词: hydrargyric (archaic)|反义词: mercurous|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *mercuricus*, from Mercurius (Mercury, the Roman god associated with the metal).
