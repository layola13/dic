inter-elements:/ˌɪntərˈɛləmənts/ | adj. | 元素间的，组件间的 | Existing or occurring between elements or components. | The inter-element spacing affects the overall performance. 元素间的间距会影响整体性能。| The inter-element interactions are complex. 元素间的相互作用很复杂。 | 近义词: between-elements, inter-component | 反义词: intra-element | 词性: adjective

词源: "inter-" (Latin, meaning "between") + "element" (from Latin "elementum")
