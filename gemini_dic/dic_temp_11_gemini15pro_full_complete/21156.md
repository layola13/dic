blizzarding: /ˈblɪzərdɪŋ/ | v. | 暴风雪般地下;  大量发送; 狂轰滥炸 | experiencing or involving a blizzard; sending a large volume of something, often aggressively. | The climbers were blizzarding during their ascent.  登山者在攀登过程中遭遇了暴风雪。|  The marketing team was blizzarding potential customers with emails. 营销团队向潜在客户发送了大量的电子邮件。 | 近义词: storming, pelting, bombarding | 反义词: calming, clearing | 词性: 动词

词源:  From blizzard + -ing.  "Blizzard" originated in the American Midwest in the 1870s, originally meaning "a violent blow" or "a volley of shots." It later evolved to refer specifically to a severe snowstorm.
