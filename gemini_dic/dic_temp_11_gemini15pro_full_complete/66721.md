exorable:/ˈɛksərəbəl/| adj.|可说服的，易受影响的|Susceptible to persuasion; capable of being moved or influenced by entreaty or prayer.|The king, once known for his hard heart, proved to be exorable and pardoned the prisoner. 国王，曾经以铁石心肠著称，最终被说服赦免了囚犯。|近义词：persuadable, susceptible, compliant|反义词：inexorable, implacable, unyielding|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin exorabilis, from Latin exorare (“to prevail upon by prayer”), from ex- (“out”) + orare (“to pray, entreat”).
