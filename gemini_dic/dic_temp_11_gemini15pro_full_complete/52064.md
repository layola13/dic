dialogize:/ˈdaɪəlɒdʒaɪz/ (US) /ˈdaɪəlɒɡaɪz/ (UK)| v. |使对话化；使具有对话形式|To put into the form of a dialogue; to represent as a dialogue.|The author dialogized the historical account, making it more engaging for readers.  作者将历史叙述对话化，使其更吸引读者。|Example: He dialogized the poem to make it more dramatic. 他将这首诗改写成对话形式，使其更具戏剧性。|Synonyms: dramatize, fictionalize|Antonyms: narrate, summarize|Verb

dialogize:/ˈdaɪəlɒdʒaɪz/ (US) /ˈdaɪəlɒɡaɪz/ (UK)| v. |使…参与对话|To engage in dialogue or conversation with.|The teacher dialogized with her students about the importance of critical thinking. 老师与学生就批判性思维的重要性进行了对话。|Example:  The company dialogized with its customers to understand their needs. 公司与客户对话以了解他们的需求。|Synonyms: converse, discuss, communicate|Antonyms: ignore, disregard|Verb
