overbearingly:/ˌoʊvərˈberɪŋli/
adverb
专横地；傲慢地；盛气凌人地
In a bossy and domineering manner; showing arrogant superiority to and disdain of those one views as unworthy.
He spoke to the waiter overbearingly. 他对服务员说话的语气很傲慢。
Synonyms: domineeringly, haughtily, imperiously, arrogantly, dictatorialy, high-handedly
Antonyms: humbly, modestly, submissively
词性: 副词

overbearing:/ˌoʊvərˈberɪŋ/
adjective
专横的；傲慢的；盛气凌人的
Bossy and domineering; showing arrogant superiority to and disdain of those one views as unworthy.
She found him overbearing and arrogant. 她觉得他专横傲慢。
Synonyms: domineering, haughty, imperious, arrogant, dictatorial, high-handed
Antonyms: humble, modest, submissive
词性: 形容词

bear (v.):/ber/
动词
忍受；承受；承担
Endure an ordeal or difficulty.
I can't bear the suspense. 我受不了这种悬念。
Synonyms: endure, tolerate, stand, abide, suffer, withstand
Antonyms: resist
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over + bearing (present participle of bear).  "Over" intensifies the meaning of "bearing," which in this context relates to carrying oneself or behaving.  Therefore, "overbearing" suggests an excessive or oppressive manner of carrying oneself.
