associationism: /əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃənɪzəm/ /əˌsəʊʃiˈeɪʃənɪzəm/
n.
联想主义; 联想心理学
The theory that mental processes, especially learning, can be explained by the laws of association.  
例句：Associationism played a significant role in the development of behaviorism. 联想主义在行为主义的发展中扮演了重要的角色。

例句：The philosopher explored the principles of associationism in his writings.  这位哲学家在他的著作中探讨了联想主义的原理。

近义词：connectionism, behaviorism
反义词：(No direct antonym, but contrasting theories include nativism and Gestalt psychology)
词性：noun

Etymology: From association + -ism.  "Association" comes from Late Latin "associatio," from "associare" meaning "to join with," from "ad-" (to) + "socius" (companion).
