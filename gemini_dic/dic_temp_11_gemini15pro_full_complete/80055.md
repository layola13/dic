goaled:/ɡoʊld/ | adj. | 有目标的，已设定目标的 | Having a goal or goals; aimed at a specific objective. |  The team was goaled to win the championship.  这个团队的目标是赢得冠军。|近义词: targeted, aimed, focused | 反义词: aimless, undirected | 词性:形容词

Etymology: From the noun "goal" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Goal" ultimately comes from the Old English "gāl" meaning "obstacle, barrier," later shifting to "end point in a race."
