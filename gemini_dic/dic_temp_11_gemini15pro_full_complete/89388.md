homoplastic:/ˌhoʊməˈplæstɪk/| adj.|同塑型的，趋同的| Exhibiting homoplasy; pertaining to homoplasy.  Similarity in characters found in different species that is due to convergent evolution, parallelism, or reversal, and not common descent.|The wings of bats and birds are homoplastic, having evolved independently for flight.|近义词：analogous, convergent|反义词：homologous|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *homos* (same) + *plastikos* (molded)
