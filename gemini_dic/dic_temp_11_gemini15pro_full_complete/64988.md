estoppel:/ɪˈstɒpəl/ (US) /ɪˈstɑːpəl/ (UK)| n.|禁止反言; 不得自认;|The principle which prevents a person from asserting something contrary to what is implied by a previous action or statement of that person or by a previous pertinent judicial determination.|He was estopped from denying the truth of his statement. 他被禁止否认其声明的真实性。

|Synonyms: preclusion, bar, prohibition|Antonyms: permission, allowance|noun
