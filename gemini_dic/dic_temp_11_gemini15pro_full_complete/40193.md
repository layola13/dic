conterminant:/kənˈtɜːrmɪnənt/
adjective
相邻的，接壤的，具有共同边界的
Having a common boundary; contiguous.
The conterminant properties extended for miles.  这些相邻的房产绵延数英里。
conterminant:/kənˈtɜːrmɪnənt/
noun
接壤之物，相邻地区
Something that has a common boundary.
The two countries are conterminants. 这两个国家是接壤的。

Etymology:
From Late Latin *conterminans, present participle of *conterminare "to border upon," from Latin con- "together" + terminare "to bound, limit," from terminus "boundary, limit."

Synonyms:
adjacent, adjoining, contiguous, abutting, bordering, neighboring, juxtaposed

Antonyms:
distant, remote, separate, detached, nonadjacent, discontiguous
