noninstrumentally:/ˌnɑːnɪnˌstrʊˈmɛntəli/
adverb
非工具性地; 非手段性地
Acting or done without regard to ulterior motives or instrumental value.  Done for its own sake rather than as a means to an end.
She valued learning noninstrumentally, for the sheer joy of acquiring knowledge. 她非工具性地重视学习，纯粹是为了获得知识的乐趣。
Synonyms: intrinsically, inherently, essentially
Antonyms: instrumentally, extrinsically, strategically
词性: 副词
