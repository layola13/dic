milotine: /ˈmɪləˌtiːn/
n.
美乐托宁;褪黑素; N-乙酰-5-甲氧基色胺
A hormone secreted by the pineal gland in the brain. It helps regulate sleep cycles.
Melatonin is a hormone primarily produced by the pineal gland in the brain.  It's known for its role in regulating sleep-wake cycles.
例句：He takes melatonin supplements to help him sleep. 他服用褪黑激素补充剂来帮助睡眠。

例句：Melatonin levels naturally decrease with age.  随着年龄的增长，褪黑激素水平自然下降。
