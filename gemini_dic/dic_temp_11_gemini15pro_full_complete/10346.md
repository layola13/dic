appeases:/əˈpiːzɪz/
| v. |平息，安抚，缓和|pacifies or satisfies (someone) by acceding to their demands.| The government tried to appease the protesters by promising reforms. 政府承诺改革以安抚抗议者。|近义词：placate, conciliate, mollify, soothe|反义词：provoke, aggravate, incite|词性:动词

词源:
From Old French *apaisier, from a- "to" + pais "peace," from Latin pax "peace."
