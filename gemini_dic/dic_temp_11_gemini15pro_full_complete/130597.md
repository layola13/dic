nonadapting:/ˌnɒnəˈdæptɪŋ/
adjective
非适应性的，不适应的
Not able to adapt or change to suit different conditions or environments.
The nonadapting species quickly died out when the climate changed.  这个不适应的物种在气候变化后迅速灭绝。
non-adaptive, inflexible, rigid
adapting, adaptable, flexible
adjective
