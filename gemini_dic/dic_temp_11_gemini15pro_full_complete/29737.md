catnapped:/ˈkætˌnæpt/| v. |绑架（尤指小孩），打盹 |To kidnap (someone), especially a child, for a short time. To have a short sleep, especially during the day.|例句：The kidnappers catnapped the mayor's son and demanded a ransom. 绑匪绑架了市长的儿子并索要赎金。

例句：I catnapped on the train and missed my stop. 我在火车上打了个盹，错过了我的站。|近义词：kidnap, abduct, snatch, doze, nap|反义词:release, return|词性:动词
