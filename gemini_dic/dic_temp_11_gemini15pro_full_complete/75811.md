fumetere:/ˈfjuːmɪtɛər/ | n. |  延龄草,  土延胡索 | A plant of the genus *Fumaria*, especially the common fumitory (*Fumaria officinalis*), a slender, climbing annual with greyish leaves and small purplish flowers. | The fumetere is a common weed in many gardens.  延龄草在许多花园里是一种常见的杂草。| Synonyms: fumitory, earth smoke | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
