impersonality:/ɪmˌpɜːrsəˈnælɪti/
n.
客观性；非个人性；冷淡
lack of human character or feeling; indifference to personal feelings
The impersonality of modern architecture often makes people feel alienated. 现代建筑的非个人性常常让人感到疏离。

近义词: detachment, objectivity, neutrality, coldness
反义词: personality, warmth, subjectivity, bias
词性: noun
