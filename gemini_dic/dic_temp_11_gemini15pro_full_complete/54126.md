disenclose:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkloʊz/
verb
打开；除去…的围墙；拆除…的遮蔽物
To remove from an enclosure; to free from enclosure.
The farmer disenclosed the pasture, allowing the cattle to roam freely. 农民打开了牧场的围栏，让牛群自由活动。
近义词: open, uncover, expose, release
反义词: enclose, fence in, confine
