comate:/ˈkəʊmeɪt/ (UK) /ˈkoʊmeɪt/ (US)| n.|同伴，伙伴；酒友|A close companion who shares one's revels or dissipations, especially a drinking companion.  Originally meaning someone who shares a room (from Latin 'con-' (with) + 'mata' (mat)).|例句：He and his comates spent many a night carousing in the taverns. 他和他的伙伴们在酒馆里度过了许多狂欢的夜晚。

例句：They were comates in college, sharing a dorm room and many adventures.  他们在大学里是室友，共享一个宿舍房间和许多冒险经历。

|近义词：companion, friend, pal, buddy, chum|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
