childproofing:/ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːfɪŋ/
noun
儿童防护
The act of making something safe for children, typically by preventing their access to hazards or by minimizing the risks associated with those hazards.
例句：We spent the weekend childproofing our house before the baby arrived.  我们周末在宝宝到来之前对房子进行了儿童防护。

例句：Thorough childproofing can prevent many accidents. 彻底的儿童安全措施可以预防许多事故。
近义词：child safety, safety measures
反义词: endangering, hazard creation
词性: n.
