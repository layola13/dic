celebrator:/ˈsɛləˌbreɪtər/| n. |庆祝者，庆祝的人|A person who celebrates something.  A person who observes a rite or festival. |例句：The celebrators gathered in the town square. 庆祝者聚集在城镇广场上。

例句：The celebrators were in high spirits. 庆祝的人们兴高采烈。|近义词：reveler, partygoer, merrymaker |反义词:mourner, griever|词性:noun
