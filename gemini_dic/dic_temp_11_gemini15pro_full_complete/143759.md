outmarch:/ˌaʊtˈmɑːrtʃ/ /ˌaʊtˈmɑːrtʃ/ | v. | 超越，胜过 | To surpass or defeat someone by marching faster or further; to outstrip in any competition. |  The smaller company outmarched its larger competitors in the race to develop new technology. 这家小公司在开发新技术的竞赛中超越了更大的竞争对手。

|  Our troops outmarched the enemy and gained a strategic advantage. 我们的部队行军速度超过了敌人，获得了战略优势。 | 近义词: outstrip, surpass, outperform, exceed | 反义词: lag behind, fall behind | 词性: 动词
