The process of organizing and preparing forces or resources to oppose or counteract the mobilization of another entity. | The country began countermobilization efforts after its neighbor declared war.  该国在邻国宣战后开始反动员工作。

The union initiated countermobilization strategies in response to the company's proposed layoffs. 工会在公司提出裁员后启动了反动员策略。 | 近义词: countermeasure, counteraction | 反义词: mobilization | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
counter- (prefix) + mobilization
"counter-" comes from Old French "contre-" and ultimately from Latin "contra" meaning "against".
"mobilization" comes from French "mobilisation" and ultimately from Latin "mobilis" meaning "movable".
