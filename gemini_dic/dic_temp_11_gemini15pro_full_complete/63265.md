entheal:/ɛnˈθiːl/ | v. |  使充满热情；使激动；鼓舞 | To inspire or fill with enthusiasm. |  The coach's speech enthealed the team before the big game. 教练的讲话在比赛前鼓舞了球队。|  The music enthealed the dancers. 音乐使舞者充满激情。| 近义词: inspire, excite, invigorate, animate | 反义词: discourage, dispirit, dampen | 词性: 动词

词源: From en- (使…) + theal, from Greek theos (god).  Literally, "to put a god in," hence "to inspire."
