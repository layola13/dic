hippobosca:/ˌhɪpəˈbɒskə/| n. |虱蝇|A louse fly, any of various bloodsucking flies of the family Hippoboscidae, parasitic on birds and mammals. They are characterized by flattened bodies and often reduced wings.|The hippobosca is a nasty parasite that can infest both birds and mammals. 虱蝇是一种令人讨厌的寄生虫，可以寄生在鸟类和哺乳动物身上。|Synonyms: louse fly, keds|Antonyms: None|Noun
