bonnell:/bəˈnɛl/ | n. |博内尔弹簧，邦内尔弹簧|A type of coil spring system used in mattresses, characterized by hourglass-shaped coils connected by helical wires.  It's known for its affordability and durability, but can be less conforming than other types of mattress springs. |  The mattress uses a bonnell spring system.  这床垫使用邦内尔弹簧系统。|Synonym: coil spring, innerspring | Antonym: pocket spring, memory foam | noun
