drawbridge:/ˈdrɔːbrɪdʒ/
noun
吊桥
A bridge, especially one over a castle's moat, that can be raised or lowered to prevent or allow passage.
The knights crossed the drawbridge to enter the castle. 骑士们穿过吊桥进入城堡。
synonyms: lifting bridge, bascule bridge
antonyms: fixed bridge
