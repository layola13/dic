doctrinal:/ˈdɒktrɪnəl/ /ˈdɑːktrɪnəl/| adj.|教义的；学说的；主义的|Relating to or based on doctrine; expressing a particular set of beliefs or principles.|例句：The book presents a doctrinal analysis of the political system. 这本书对政治制度进行了学理分析。

例句：His doctrinal views were considered controversial. 他的学说观点被认为是有争议的。|近义词：dogmatic, ideological, theoretical|反义词：practical, empirical, pragmatic|词性:adjective
