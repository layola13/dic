harvestman:/ˈhɑːrvɪstmən/ | n. | 长腿蜘蛛，盲蛛 | An arachnid of the order Opiliones, with very long legs and a small rounded body. Unlike spiders, they have only one pair of eyes and their abdomen and cephalothorax are fused. |  I saw a harvestman in the garden. 我在花园里看到了一只长腿蜘蛛。| synonym: daddy longlegs | antonym: N/A | noun

harvestman:/ˈhɑːrvɪstmən/ | n. | 收割者 | A person who harvests crops. (Less common usage) | The harvestmen worked tirelessly in the fields. 收割者不知疲倦地在田里工作。| synonym: reaper, harvester | antonym: N/A | noun
