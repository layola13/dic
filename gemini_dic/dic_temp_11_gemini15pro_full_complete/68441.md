faluns:/fəˈlʌnz/
|n.|  贝壳沙，贝壳灰|A soft, porous, calcareous rock composed of broken shells, corals, etc., used as a fertilizer.|The farmers used faluns to enrich the soil.  农民们用贝壳沙来肥沃土壤。|Synonyms: shell marl, calcareous sand|Antonyms: bedrock, clay|Noun
