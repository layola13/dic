affinitive:/əˈfɪnɪtɪv/
| adj.|
亲和的，有亲和力的；同源的，同宗的|
Having affinity; related by affinity; attracting. Showing a natural liking for or connection to something. |
She felt an affinitive connection to the quiet, introspective artist. 她对这位安静、内向的艺术家感到一种亲和力。
The two tribes, though geographically distant, shared affinitive customs.  这两个部落虽然地理位置遥远，但有着同源的习俗。
|
近义词：related, akin, connected, similar, kindred, sympathetic
|
反义词：unrelated, dissimilar, alien, different
|
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *affinitativus*, from Latin *affinitas* ("affinity") + *-ivus* (forming adjectives).
