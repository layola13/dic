lactating:/ˈlækteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
哺乳期的，分泌乳汁的
Producing or secreting milk.
The lactating mother needed to express milk regularly.  这位哺乳期的母亲需要定期挤奶。
A lactating cow produces several gallons of milk per day. 一头泌乳的奶牛每天产几加仑的牛奶。
近义词:nursing, breastfeeding
反义词:non-lactating, weaned
词性:adjective
