melodica:/məˈlɒdɪkə/ (UK) /məˈlɑːdɪkə/ (US)| n.|簧风琴; 口风琴|A small, rectangular wind instrument played by blowing into a mouthpiece and pressing keys on a keyboard. It produces a reedy sound similar to an accordion or harmonica.|He played a lively tune on his melodica. 他用他的簧风琴演奏了一首欢快的曲子.|近义词：pianica, blow-organ, keyboard harmonica|反义词: N/A|noun
