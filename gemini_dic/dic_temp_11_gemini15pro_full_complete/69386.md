feedable:/ˈfiːdəbl/| adj.|可喂养的，可饲养的|Suitable for being fed; able to be nourished. |The baby birds are now feedable and require a special diet.  这些雏鸟现在可以喂食了，需要特殊的饮食。|The rescued kittens were weak but feedable.  获救的小猫很虚弱，但可以喂食。|近义词:edible, consumable|反义词:inedible, non-consumable|词性:形容词
词源:From "feed" (verb) + "-able" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being").  源自动词 "feed" 加上后缀 "-able"（构成形容词，表示“能够被……”）。
