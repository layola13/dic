blabber:/ˈblæbər/ (US) /ˈblæbə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|喋喋不休，胡言乱语；泄密|To talk foolishly or excessively, or to reveal secrets indiscreetly.|He blabbered on about his new car for hours. 他喋喋不休地谈论他的新车好几个小时。|近义词：chatter, prattle, gabble, babble|反义词：be quiet, keep silent|词性:动词,名词

blabber:/ˈblæbər/ (US) /ˈblæbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|喋喋不休的人；泄密者|A person who talks foolishly or excessively, or reveals secrets.|He's such a blabber; he can't keep a secret. 他真是个大嘴巴，守不住秘密。|近义词：chatterbox, gossip|反义词:discreet person|词性:名词
