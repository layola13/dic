admiration:/ˌædməˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 钦佩；赞赏；羡慕 | A feeling of respect and approval. | She looked at him with admiration. 她钦佩地看着他。|  I have great admiration for her work. 我非常钦佩她的工作。 | 近义词: respect, esteem, appreciation, reverence | 反义词: contempt, disdain, scorn | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *admiratio, from Latin admirari "to wonder at," from ad- "to" + mirari "to wonder."
