nondeleterious:/ˌnɑːndɛləˈtɪəriəs/
adjective
无害的
Not harmful or injurious; harmless.
This mutation is nondeleterious and doesn't affect the protein's function.  这个突变是无害的，不会影响蛋白质的功能。
The change in the gene sequence proved to be nondeleterious. 基因序列的变化被证明是无害的。
近义词:benign, harmless, innocuous, safe
反义词:deleterious, harmful, injurious, damaging
词性:adjective
