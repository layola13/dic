dampener:/ˈdæmpənər (US), ˈdæmpnə(r) (UK)| n.|抑制因素，扫兴的人或事|Something that diminishes enthusiasm, excitement, or enjoyment.|The bad weather put a dampener on our picnic.  坏天气让我们野餐的兴致大减。|Synonyms: deterrent, discouragement, curb, restraint|Antonyms: encouragement, incentive, stimulus|noun

dampener:/ˈdæmpənər (US), ˈdæmpnə(r) (UK)| n.|减震器，阻尼器|A device used to reduce vibrations or oscillations.|The car's dampeners needed replacing.  这辆车的减震器需要更换。|Synonyms: shock absorber, muffler, silencer|Antonyms: amplifier, intensifier|noun
