cornified:/ˈkɔːrnɪfaɪd/| adj.|角化的，变得像角一样的|Converted into horn or a horny substance; keratinized.  The process of becoming hard and tough like horn.|The cornified layer of the skin is the outermost layer and provides a waterproof barrier.|近义词：keratinized, hardened, calloused|反义词:soft, pliable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *cornificāre* "to make horny," from *cornu* "horn" + *-ficāre*, a combining form meaning “to make or cause.”
