monomorphous:/ˌmɒnəˈmɔːfəs/| adj.|单形的，单态的|Having a single form or structure; exhibiting uniformity of structure.|The bacteria were monomorphous, appearing as uniform rods under the microscope. 这些细菌是单形体的，在显微镜下呈现为均匀的杆状。|Synonyms: uniform, homogeneous, consistent|Antonyms: polymorphic, heteromorphic, varied|adjective
