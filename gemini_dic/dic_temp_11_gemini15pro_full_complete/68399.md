false:/fɔːls/ (US) /fɒls/ (UK)| adj.|假的，错误的，不真实的，人造的，虚伪的|Not true or correct; artificial; not genuine; treacherous.|例句：The statement is demonstrably false. 证明该陈述是假的。|例句：He gave false information to the police. 他向警方提供了虚假信息。|近义词：untrue, incorrect, wrong, erroneous, artificial, fake, spurious, bogus|反义词：true, correct, genuine, real, authentic|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old French *fals, fals, faus, from Latin falsus “deceived, mistaken, counterfeit, untrue”, past participle of fallere “to deceive, disappoint, escape the notice of, let down, trip, cause to fall”.
