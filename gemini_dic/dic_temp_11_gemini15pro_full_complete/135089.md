nonobligatory: /ˌnɒnəˈblɪɡətɔːri/ (US) /ˌnɒnəˈblɪɡətri/ (UK)| adj.|非强制性的，非义务的|Not required or compulsory; optional.|Attendance at the meeting is nonobligatory.  参加会议是非强制性的。|It is nonobligatory to wear a tie to the party. 参加派对不必系领带。|近义词：optional, voluntary, elective|反义词：obligatory, compulsory, mandatory|词性:形容词
词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + obligatory (from Latin obligatus, past participle of obligare "to bind, oblige")
