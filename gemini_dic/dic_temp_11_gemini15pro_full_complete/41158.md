copulatory:/ˈkɒpjʊlət(ə)ri/ /ˈkɑːpjʊlətɔːri/
adjective
交配的；交尾的
Relating to or involving copulation; of or for sexual intercourse.
The male frog clasps the female around the body in a copulatory embrace. 雄蛙抱合雌蛙的身体进行交配。
Near Synonyms:  mating, reproductive, sexual, generative
Antonyms:  asexual, non-reproductive
词性：adjective
