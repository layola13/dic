lexiconist:/ˈlɛksɪkənɪst/| n.|词典编纂者；词汇学家|A person who compiles dictionaries or studies lexicography.  A lexiconist must have a deep understanding of language and etymology.|The lexiconist spent years researching the origins of various words for the new dictionary.|近义词：lexicographer, dictionary compiler, word expert|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From lexicon (dictionary) + -ist (one who does or studies).  Ultimately from Greek lexikon (word, speech) + -ikos (relating to) + -ist.
