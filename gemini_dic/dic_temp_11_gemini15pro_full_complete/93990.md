impairers:/ɪmˈpeərərz/
n.
损害物，损伤因素
Something that diminishes or weakens, especially a faculty or function.
Excessive alcohol consumption is one of the major impairers of driving ability. 过量饮酒是驾驶能力的主要损害因素之一。
Synonyms: weakeners, damagers, deteriorators
Antonyms: enhancers, improvers, strengtheners
noun

impair:/ɪmˈper/
v.
削弱，损害
To weaken or damage something, especially a faculty or function.
Lack of sleep can impair your concentration. 缺乏睡眠会损害你的注意力。
Synonyms: weaken, damage, harm
Antonyms: improve, enhance, strengthen
verb
