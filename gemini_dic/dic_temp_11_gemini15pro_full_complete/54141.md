disengaged:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒd/
adjective
脱离的，不感兴趣的，空闲的
separated, disconnected, not emotionally involved; free from duty or occupation
例句：He seemed disengaged from the conversation, staring out the window. 他似乎对谈话不感兴趣，凝视着窗外。

例句：The clutch is disengaged when you press the pedal.  当你踩下踏板时，离合器就分离了。

近义词：detached, aloof, withdrawn, indifferent, unoccupied, free
反义词：engaged, involved, interested, occupied, busy
词性: adjective


disengage:/ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/
verb
脱离，解开，使脱离
to separate or release (something or someone) from something to which it is attached or connected; to become free from an engagement or commitment
例句：The soldiers disengaged from the enemy. 士兵们脱离了敌人。

例句：She disengaged her hand from his grasp. 她把手从他的掌握中挣脱出来。

近义词：detach, disconnect, withdraw, extricate
反义词：engage, connect, attach, involve
词性: verb


词源:
From dis- + engage (from Old French engager, from en- "in" + gage "pledge").  The prefix "dis-" signifies reversal or negation.
