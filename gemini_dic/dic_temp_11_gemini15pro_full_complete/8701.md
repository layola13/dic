anticreatively:/ˌæntiˈkriːeɪtɪvli/
|adv.|反创造性地，扼杀创造力地|In a way that discourages or suppresses creativity.  It stifles innovation and imagination.|The company's rigid rules and regulations acted anticreatively, preventing employees from exploring new ideas.  公司死板的规章制度扼杀了创造力，阻止员工探索新想法。|
|anticreatively stifles innovation and limits imaginative thinking. 反创造性地扼杀创新，限制想象力思维。|
|Synonyms: uncreatively, unimaginatively, restrictively|Antonyms: creatively, imaginatively, innovatively|
|adverb|
