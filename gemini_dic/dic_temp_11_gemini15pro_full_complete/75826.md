fumigation:/ˌfjuːmɪˈɡeɪʃn/ | n. | 消毒，熏蒸 | The fumigation of the building was necessary to eliminate the pests.  这栋建筑进行熏蒸是必要的，以消除害虫。| The fumigation process took several hours. 消毒过程持续了几个小时。 |  disinfection, sterilization | pest control, airing | noun

fumigate:/ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪt/ | vt. | 熏蒸，消毒 | They fumigated the house to get rid of termites. 他们熏蒸房子以除掉白蚁。 |  We need to fumigate the soil before planting. 我们需要在种植前对土壤进行熏蒸消毒。| disinfect, sterilize | aerate, ventilate | verb
