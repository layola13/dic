certifier:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪər/| n.|认证者，核证者|A person or organization that officially attests or confirms something, especially the quality or standard of something.|The certifier confirmed the authenticity of the document.  认证者确认了文件的真实性。|synonyms: validator, authenticator, endorser, accreditor|antonyms:  challenger, disputer|noun

certify:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪ/| vt.|证明，保证；核证|To officially attest or confirm something, especially the quality or standard of something.|The doctor certified that the patient was fit to travel. 医生证明病人适合旅行。|synonyms: attest, confirm, validate, endorse, guarantee|antonyms:  deny, dispute,  invalidate|verb
