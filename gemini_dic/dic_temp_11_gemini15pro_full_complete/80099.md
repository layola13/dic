gobbing:/ˈɡɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| n. |吐痰; 吐口水; 唾弃; 大量; 许多 (informal)|The act of spitting, or a large amount of something (informal).|He was disgusted by the gobbing on the sidewalk. 他对人行道上的吐痰感到恶心。|近义词: spitting, expectoration (formal), loads (informal), heaps (informal)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

gobbing:/ˈɡɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |大量的; 许多的 (informal, rare)|A large amount of something (informal, rarely used).|There's a gobbing amount of paperwork to do.  有很多文书工作要做。(This usage is less common).|近义词: loads of, tons of, heaps of|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective


词源: From the verb "gob," meaning to spit, which originated in the 14th century.  The exact origin is uncertain, but it may be related to a similar word in Dutch or Low German.
