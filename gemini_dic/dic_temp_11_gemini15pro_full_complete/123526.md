monotonousnesses:/məˈnɒtənəsnɪsɪz/
|n.|单调乏味；千篇一律|The monotonousnesses of the desert landscape can be oppressive. 沙漠景观的单调乏味可能令人感到压抑。|The monotonousnesses of his daily routine made him feel depressed. 他日常生活单调乏味，让他感到沮丧。|近义词：monotony, tediousness, dullness, uniformity|反义词：variety, diversity, excitement, change|词性: 名词

monotonous:/məˈnɒtənəs/
|adj.|单调的；乏味的|The monotonous drone of the engine lulled me to sleep. 发动机单调的嗡嗡声让我昏昏欲睡。|The speaker's monotonous voice made it difficult to stay awake. 演讲者单调的声音让人难以保持清醒。|近义词：tedious, dull, boring, repetitive|反义词：varied, diverse, exciting, interesting|词性: 形容词

monotony:/məˈnɒtəni/
|n.|单调；乏味|She couldn't stand the monotony of her office job. 她无法忍受办公室工作的单调乏味。|The monotony of the landscape was broken only by the occasional tree.  只有偶尔出现的树木打破了景色的单调。|近义词：tediousness, dullness, boredom, sameness|反义词：variety, diversity, excitement, change|词性: 名词


词源:  From French *monotonie*, from Ancient Greek μονότονος (monótonos, “monotonous”), from μόνος (mónos, “alone, single”) + τόνος (tónos, “tone, pitch”).  最终源自希腊语，由“单一”和“音调”组成，表示单一不变的音调。
