mispurchases:/ˌmɪsˈpɜːrtʃəsɪz/

|近义词：wrong purchases, incorrect buys, mistaken purchases|反义词：correct purchases, desired purchases|词性: 名词

mispurchase:/ˌmɪsˈpɜːrtʃəs/
|v.|买错，错误地购买|To buy something mistakenly.|I mispurchased the wrong size shoes online. 我在网上买错了尺码的鞋子。|
|近义词：buy wrongly, purchase incorrectly|反义词：buy correctly, purchase correctly|词性: 动词
