caribou:/ˈkærɪbuː/ (US), /ˈkærɪbuː/ (UK)| n.|驯鹿|A large North American deer with large antlers in both sexes, found in the arctic and subarctic regions.|例句：The caribou migrated south for the winter. 驯鹿向南迁徙过冬。

例句：Caribou are an important food source for many indigenous peoples. 驯鹿是许多原住民的重要食物来源。|近义词：reindeer|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French Canadian caribou, from the Mi'kmaq qalipu meaning "snow shoveler" or "pawer," referring to its habit of pawing through snow to find food.
