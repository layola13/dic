brigands:/ˈbrɪɡəndz/
n.
土匪，强盗
A member of a gang of robbers, especially one operating in mountainous or forested regions.
The brigands held the travelers hostage for ransom.  土匪劫持了旅客为人质以索取赎金。

The stagecoach was attacked by a band of brigands.  驿站马车遭到一伙土匪的袭击。

近义词:
bandit, robber, outlaw, highwayman, thief
反义词:
lawman, police officer

词性:
noun (plural)

词源:
From French brigand, from Italian brigante, from Old Provençal brigant, from brigar "to fight," of uncertain origin.
