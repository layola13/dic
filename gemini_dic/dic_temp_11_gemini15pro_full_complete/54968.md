disproportionable:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənəbl/
adjective
不成比例的，不相称的
not in proportion; too large or too small in comparison with something else
The punishment was disproportionable to the crime.  对这项罪行而言，惩罚过重了。
disproportionate, excessive, inordinate, undue
proportionate, reasonable, moderate
adjective

disproportional:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənl/
adjective
不成比例的，不相称的
not having the correct or appropriate relationship between different parts or things
The kitchen is disproportionally small compared to the rest of the house.  厨房与房子的其他部分相比小得不成比例。
disproportionate, unequal, unbalanced
proportionate, equal, balanced
adjective
