ecological:/ˌiːkəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ /ˌɛkəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/
adjective
生态的；生态学的
relating to ecology or the environment
例句：The ecological balance of the rainforest is fragile. 雨林的生态平衡很脆弱。

例句：The company is trying to reduce its ecological footprint. 该公司正在努力减少其生态足迹。

近义词：environmental, green
反义词：none
词性：adjective
