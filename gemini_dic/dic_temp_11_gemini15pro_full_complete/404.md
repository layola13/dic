abjectness:/ˈæbdʒɛktnɪs/
n.
卑贱；凄惨； abjectness
the state of being extremely unhappy, poor, unsuccessful, etc.
His letter was full of abjectness and self-pity. 他的信充满了卑躬屈膝和自怜自艾。
近义词:degradation, humiliation, wretchedness
反义词:nobleness, dignity, pride
词性:名词

abject:/ˈæbdʒɛkt/
adj.
卑贱的；凄惨的； abject
experiencing or marked by extreme need or desperation
The refugees were living in abject poverty. 难民们生活在赤贫之中。
近义词:wretched, miserable, degraded
反义词:noble, dignified, proud
词性:形容词
