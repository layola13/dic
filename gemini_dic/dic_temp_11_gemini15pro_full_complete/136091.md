nonrecollections:/ˌnɑnˌrɛkəˈlɛkʃənz/ | n. | 没有记忆；未能想起的事情 | The gaps in her memory were filled with nonrecollections of the accident. 她对事故的记忆空白，完全不记得了。 |  Her testimony was full of nonrecollections and inconsistencies. 她的证词充满了遗忘和前后矛盾之处。 | 近义词: lapses in memory, forgotten memories, amnesia | 反义词: memories, recollections | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix) + recollection (noun)

*   "non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "lack of."
*   "recollection" comes from the Old French word "recollection" and ultimately from the Latin "recolligere," meaning "to gather again."
