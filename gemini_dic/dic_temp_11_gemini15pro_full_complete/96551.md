indorse:/ɪnˈdɔːrs/ | /ɪnˈdɔːrs/ | vt. | 背书，认可，签注 | To write one's signature on the back of (a check, bill of exchange, etc.) in order to make it payable to someone other than the stated payee or to accept responsibility for paying it. To give one's public approval or support to.  | He indorsed the check and handed it to the cashier. 他在支票上背书后交给了收银员。 The celebrity indorsed the new product.  这位名人为新产品代言。 | synonyms: approve, sanction, endorse, back, support, ratify, confirm | antonyms: disapprove, reject, oppose, condemn, repudiate | verb
