carted:/ˈkɑːrtɪd/
verb
用车运送, 购物车装载
Past participle of "cart."  Means to have transported something using a cart or other vehicle. It can also be used figuratively to mean to have carried something, especially something heavy or cumbersome.
The farmer carted the hay to the barn.  农民用车把干草运到谷仓。
They carted away the old furniture. 他们把旧家具运走了。
近义词: transported, hauled, conveyed
反义词:  (No direct opposite, perhaps "carried by hand")
词性: verb
