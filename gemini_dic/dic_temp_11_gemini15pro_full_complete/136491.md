nonriparian:/ˌnɒnraɪˈpɛəriən/
adjective
非河岸的，不属于河岸的
Not adjacent to or dependent on a riverbank.  Relating to or denoting land that does not border a river or stream.
The nonriparian vegetation was markedly different from the plants growing along the river. 非河岸植被与沿河生长的植物明显不同。
The developer was fined for illegally clearing nonriparian land.  开发商因非法清理非河岸土地而被罚款。

近义词: upland, inland
反义词: riparian

词性: adjective
