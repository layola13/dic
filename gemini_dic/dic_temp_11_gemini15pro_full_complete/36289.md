cohen:/ˈkoʊən/ (US), /ˈkəʊən/ (UK)| n.|祭司，科恩(犹太教祭司姓氏)|A Cohen is a Jewish priest, or a descendant of the priestly class.|例句：He is a Cohen, descended from Aaron. 他是祭司，是亚伦的后裔。

例句：Many Cohens trace their ancestry back to ancient Israel. 许多科恩家族追溯他们的祖先到古代以色列。|近义词：priest|反义词:|noun
