graniferous:/ɡrænˈɪfərəs/
adjective
含颗粒的; 颗粒状的
Containing granules; granular.
The soil sample was graniferous, composed of small, distinct particles. 土壤样本呈颗粒状，由小的、不同的颗粒组成。
The surface of the asteroid appeared graniferous in the telescope images. 在望远镜图像中，小行星的表面看起来呈颗粒状。
Synonyms: granular, grainy, particulate
Antonyms: smooth, uniform, homogeneous
词性: 形容词
