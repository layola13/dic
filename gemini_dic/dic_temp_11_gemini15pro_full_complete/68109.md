faffing:/ˈfæfɪŋ/| n. vi.|闲荡，磨蹭，浪费时间|The act of spending time doing unimportant things or doing something inefficiently.|I spent all morning faffing about/around and got nothing done. 我整个上午都在闲逛，什么也没做。|Synonyms: fiddling, dithering, messing about, pottering|Antonyms: working diligently, focusing, being productive|noun, verb (informal, chiefly British)
