nonevading:/ˌnɒnɪˈveɪdɪŋ/
adjective
不逃避的；不躲避的；直面的
Not evading; direct and unflinching.
His nonevading gaze met hers. 他毫不躲闪的目光与她的相遇。
近义词:direct, straightforward, unflinching, forthright
反义词:evasive, avoiding, elusive, shifty
词性:形容词

词源:
"nonevading" is formed by the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the present participle of the verb "evade."  "Evade" comes from the Latin "evadere," meaning "to escape, get away from."
