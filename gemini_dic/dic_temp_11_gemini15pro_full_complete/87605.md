heterotropal:/ˌhɛtərɒˈtrɒpəl/
adj.
异生的，倒生的
(of a plant ovule) inverted; anatropous.
The heterotropal ovule is common in most flowering plants.  大多数开花植物的胚珠是倒生的。
近义词:anatropous, inverted
反义词:orthotropous, atropous
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From hetero- (different, other) + -tropal (turning, directed).  Ultimately from Greek heteros (other) and trope (turning).
