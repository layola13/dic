heavy-handedness:/ˌheviˈhændɪdnəs/| n. |笨拙；粗暴；高压手段|The heavy-handedness of the government's response caused widespread resentment. 政府粗暴的应对方式引起了广泛的怨恨。 |The police officer's heavy-handedness during the arrest led to a complaint.  这名警察在逮捕过程中的粗暴行为导致了投诉。|近义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, brutality, oppression, authoritarianism|反义词: delicacy, tact, sensitivity, gentleness|词性: noun

Etymology:  From "heavy-handed" + "-ness."  "Heavy-handed" itself comes from "heavy" (meaning weighty or ponderous) + "hand" (referring to the physical hand or one's actions).  The suffix "-ness" turns the adjective "heavy-handed" into the abstract noun "heavy-handedness," indicating the state or quality of being heavy-handed.
