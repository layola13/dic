gnawings:/ˈnɔːɪŋz/| n. |啃咬；啮咬；持续的痛苦或焦虑|A persistent feeling of anxiety or discomfort, often likened to the sensation of being gnawed at.  It can also refer to the physical act of gnawing or the marks left by gnawing. |The gnawings of hunger kept him awake all night. 饥饿的折磨让他彻夜未眠。|Synonyms: pangs, qualms, anxieties, worries, nibblings|Antonyms: satisfaction, contentment, peace of mind|noun
