commercialization:/kəˌmɜːrʃələˈzeɪʃn/ |n.|商业化|The process of introducing a new product or service into the market for sale and profit.|The commercialization of the internet has changed the way we live. 互联网的商业化改变了我们的生活方式。|近义词：marketing, merchandising|反义词：non-commercialization|词性：名词

commercialize:/kəˈmɜːrʃəlaɪz/ |v.|商业化|To make something suitable for sale or to exploit something for profit.|The company commercialized the new technology quickly.  这家公司迅速将新技术商业化。|近义词：market, merchandise|反义词：decommercialize|词性：动词
