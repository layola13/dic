allayed:/əˈleɪd/| v. |减轻，缓和，使平静|To diminish or reduce the intensity of something, especially something unpleasant or negative, such as fear, anxiety, or pain.  It implies a calming or soothing effect.|The government's announcement allayed public fears about the virus. 政府的公告减轻了公众对病毒的恐惧。|synonyms: calmed, soothed, eased, relieved, mitigated|antonyms: aggravated, intensified, exacerbated, heightened|verb

allayed:/əˈleɪd/| v. |平息，消除|To put an end to or pacify something, such as anger, suspicion, or unrest.  It suggests a quieting or subduing influence.|His kind words allayed her anger.  他友善的话平息了她的怒火。|synonyms: quelled, subdued, appeased, pacified, tranquilized|antonyms: provoked, inflamed, agitated, aroused|verb
