dreadly:/ˈdredli/| adv.|可怕地，非常地，极其地|In a manner inspiring dread; terribly; extremely.|The situation was dreadly serious.  局势极其严峻。|The performance was dreadly boring. 演出非常无聊。|近义词: terribly, awfully, extremely, fearfully|反义词: wonderfully, delightfully|词性:副词
Etymology: From dread + -ly.  Dread comes from Old English *drǣdan, from Proto-Germanic *drōzaną (“to be anxious, troubled, afraid”).
