inter-talked:/ˌɪntərˈtɔːkt/ |v. |互相交谈，相互交谈 |The two groups inter-talked for hours, trying to reach an agreement.  两个小组互相交谈了几个小时，试图达成协议。| The children inter-talked excitedly about their holiday plans. 孩子们兴奋地互相交谈着他们的假期计划。 |Synonyms: conferred, discussed, conversed | Antonyms: ignored, avoided | verb

Etymology:  "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + "talked" (past tense of the verb "talk").  The prefix "inter-" comes from Latin.
