corporealist:/kɔːrˈpɔːriəlist/ /kɔːrˈpoʊriəlist/
n.
唯物主义者; 躯体主义者
A person who believes that the only reality is the physical world, or who emphasizes the physical and material aspects of life.
He was a corporealist, believing only in what he could see and touch. 他是一个唯物主义者，只相信他看得见摸得着的东西。
materialist, physicalist, sensualist
idealist, spiritualist
noun
