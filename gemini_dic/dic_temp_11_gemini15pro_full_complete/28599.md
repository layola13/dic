carelessness:/ˈkɛrləsnəs/| n.|粗心，疏忽，马虎|Lack of attention and thought, often resulting in mistakes or accidents. |Her carelessness led to the accident. 她的粗心导致了这起事故。|  The carelessness of the driver caused a serious collision. 司机的粗心大意造成了一起严重的碰撞。|近义词：negligence, inattention, heedlessness|反义词：carefulness, caution, diligence|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From care + -less + -ness.  "Care" comes from Old English *caru, from Proto-Germanic *karō, meaning "sorrow, anxiety, grief."  The suffix "-less" indicates a lack of something, and "-ness" turns the adjective "careless" into a noun.
