menispermic:/ˌmɛnɪˈspɜːrmɪk/ | adj. | 弯生胚珠的；月种子植物的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Menispermaceae, a family of flowering plants in which the ovules are curved or horseshoe-shaped. |  The botanist identified the plant as menispermic due to the shape of its ovules.  植物学家根据胚珠的形状将该植物鉴定为弯生胚珠的。| Synonym:  curved ovulate  (less precise) | Antonym: orthotropous (straight ovulate) | adjective
