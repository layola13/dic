bannition:/bæˈnɪʃən/
n.
放逐，驱逐
the act of sending someone away from their country or home, especially as an official punishment
例句：He was punished with bannition from his homeland. 他被处以放逐，离开了祖国。

例句：The bannition of the troublesome prince brought peace to the kingdom.  驱逐麻烦不断的王子给王国带来了和平。

近义词：exile, expulsion, deportation, ostracism
反义词： repatriation, return, welcome
词性：名词
