arseniferous:/ɑːrˈsɛnɪfərəs/
adjective
含砷的；砷的
containing arsenic.
The arseniferous pyrite was a dangerous material to handle. 这种含砷的黄铁矿是一种处理起来很危险的物质。
The mine was known for its arseniferous deposits.  这个矿以其含砷矿床而闻名。
近义词: arsenic-bearing, arsenic-containing
反义词: arsenic-free

词性: adjective
