creole:/ˈkriːoʊl/ (US), /ˈkriːɒl/ (UK)| n. adj.|克里奥尔人，克里奥尔语，克里奥尔式的|A person of mixed European and black descent, especially in the Caribbean.  A language that has developed from a mixture of different languages.|例句：The vibrant creole culture of New Orleans is fascinating. 新奥尔良充满活力的克里奥尔文化令人着迷。|近义词：hybrid, mixed|反义词:pure, unmixed|词性:名词, 形容词
creole:/ˈkriːoʊl/ (US), /ˈkriːɒl/ (UK)|adj.|克里奥尔式的，克里奥尔语的|Relating to or characteristic of Creoles or their language.|例句：She speaks creole French. 她说法语克里奥尔语。|近义词：hybrid, mixed|反义词:pure, unmixed|词性:形容词
