multisite:/ˈmʌltiˌsaɪt/| adj. n.|多站点，多中心的|Relating to or involving multiple sites or locations.  A setup involving multiple websites.|例句：The company uses a multisite architecture for its web presence.  这家公司使用多站点架构来构建其网络形象。

例句：We are planning a multisite clinical trial. 我们正在计划一项多中心临床试验。|近义词：distributed, decentralized, network, multi-location|反义词：single-site, centralized, localized|词性：adjective, noun


Etymology:
"multi-" (prefix meaning "many, multiple") + "site" (from Old French "site" meaning "situation, place")
