goaltender:/ˈɡoʊltendər/ (US), /ˈɡəʊltendər/ (UK)| n.|守门员|A player who defends the goal in sports like hockey, soccer, lacrosse, etc.  The goaltender's job is to prevent the opposing team from scoring.|The goaltender made a spectacular save. 守门员做出了精彩的扑救。

The team's goaltender was injured during the second period.  球队守门员在第二节比赛中受伤了。|近义词：goalie, keeper, netminder|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "goal" + "tender" (one who attends to something). "Goal" ultimately comes from Old English "gāl" meaning "obstacle, barrier." "Tender" comes from Old French "tendre" meaning "to hold, guard, protect."
