orbiculate:/ɔːrˈbɪkjʊlət/ | adj. | 圆形的，球形的 | Perfectly circular or spherical; resembling an orb. |  The planet appeared as a small, orbiculate disk in the telescope.  这颗行星在望远镜中呈现为一个小的圆形圆盘。 | The artist painted an orbiculate moon hanging in the night sky.  艺术家画了一个圆形的月亮挂在夜空中。 | 近义词: circular, spherical, round, globe-shaped | 反义词:  angular, irregular, asymmetrical | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin *orbiculatus, past participle of orbiculare (“to make round”), from orbis (“circle, orb”).
