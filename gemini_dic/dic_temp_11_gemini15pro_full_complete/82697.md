guffaw:/ɡəˈfɔː/| v. |哄笑，狂笑|To laugh loudly and boisterously.|He guffawed at the ridiculous sight. 他对着这滑稽的景象哈哈大笑。|Synonyms: roar with laughter, howl with laughter|Antonyms: chuckle, giggle, snicker|verb
