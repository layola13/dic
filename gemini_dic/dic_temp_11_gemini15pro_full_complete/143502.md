outglowed:/ˌaʊtˈɡloʊd/ | v. | 比...更亮，胜过，超过（光芒） | To shine more brightly than; to surpass in brightness or radiance. | The setting sun outglowed the city lights. 夕阳比城市灯光更亮。|  The fireworks outglowed the moon. 烟花比月亮更亮。 | synonyms: outshone, eclipsed, surpassed, exceeded | antonyms: dimmed, overshadowed, paled | verb

词源:

out- (prefix) + glow (verb)

"out-" 表示超过，胜过
"glow" 表示发光，辉光
