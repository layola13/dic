asportation:/ˌæspɔːrˈteɪʃən/
n.
搬运，移动，窃取
The act of carrying away or removing something; especially, the unlawful removal of property from its rightful place.
The thief's asportation of the jewels was caught on camera.  窃贼偷走珠宝的过程被摄像头拍了下来。
近义词:removal, transfer, theft, carrying away
反义词:leaving, abandoning
