illuminates:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪts/
v.
照亮，阐明
makes something visible or bright by shining light on it; makes something clearer and easier to understand
The streetlights illuminate the road at night. 路灯在夜间照亮道路。
The report illuminates the challenges facing the industry. 这份报告阐明了该行业面临的挑战。
light up, brighten, clarify, elucidate
darken, obscure, confuse
verb
