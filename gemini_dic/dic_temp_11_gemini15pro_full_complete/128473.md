newsletters:/ˈnjuːzˌletərz/ (US) /ˈnjuːzˌletəz/ (UK)
n.
新闻通讯，简报
A periodical publication containing news and information of interest to a particular group.
I subscribe to several newsletters about gardening.  我订阅了几份关于园艺的新闻通讯。
The company sends out a monthly newsletter to its clients. 公司每月向客户发送一份新闻通讯。
bulletins, reports, circulars
---
Etymology:
From news + letter.  The term gained popularity in the mid-20th century with the rise of direct mail marketing. 源自 news + letter。这个词在20世纪中期随着直邮营销的兴起而流行起来。
