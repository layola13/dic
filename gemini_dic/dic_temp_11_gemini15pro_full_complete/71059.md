first-time:/ˈfɜːrst taɪm/ (US) /ˈfɜːst taɪm/ (UK)| n.|第一次|It was a first-time experience for me. 这对我来说是第一次的经历。|近义词：debut, premiere|反义词: repetition, recurrence|词性:名词

词源:  From "first" (meaning earliest) + "time" (meaning instance or occasion).  "First" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *furistaz.  "Time" comes from Proto-Germanic *tīmaz.
