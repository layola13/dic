macroglossia: /ˌmækroʊˈɡlɒsiə/ /ˌmækroʊˈɡlɑːsiə/ | n. |巨舌症|Abnormal enlargement of the tongue.  It can be congenital or acquired, and may be associated with various underlying conditions.|例句：Macroglossia can cause difficulties with speech and swallowing. 巨舌症会导致说话和吞咽困难。

例句：The child's macroglossia was caused by Beckwith-Wiedemann syndrome.  这个孩子的巨舌症是由贝克威-魏德曼综合症引起的。|近义词：glossomegaly|反义词:microglossia|词性:noun
