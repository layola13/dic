manipulatory:/məˈnɪpjələˌtɔːri/ /məˈnɪpjʊləˌtɔːri/
adjective
控制的；操纵的；用手控制的
Exerting or involving manipulation; controlling or influencing cleverly or unscrupulously.
She used manipulatory tactics to get what she wanted. 她用操纵策略来得到她想要的东西。
The politician employed manipulatory language to sway public opinion.  这位政客使用操纵性的语言来左右公众舆论。
近义词: controlling, exploitative, scheming, manipulative
反义词: honest, straightforward, forthright
词性: adjective

manipulate:/məˈnɪpjəleɪt/
verb
操纵；控制；(熟练地)操作
Handle or control (a tool, mechanism, etc.), typically in a skillful manner.
He manipulated the controls of the aircraft with ease. 他轻松地操纵飞机的控制装置。
She manipulated the data to support her argument. 她操纵数据以支持她的论点。
近义词: control, influence, operate, maneuver
反义词: leave alone, neglect
词性: verb

manipulation:/məˌnɪpjəˈleɪʃn/
noun
操纵；控制；(熟练的)操作
The action of manipulating something in a skillful manner.
The manipulation of the stock market by large investors. 大型投资者对股票市场的操纵。
The puppet show involved the skillful manipulation of marionettes. 木偶戏需要熟练地操纵木偶。
近义词: control, influence, operation, maneuvering
反义词: neglect, inaction
词性: noun
