cephalometry:/ˌsefəˈlɒmətri/ | n. | 头影测量法 | The scientific measurement of the bones of the head, especially the skull and face, typically from X-ray images.  | Cephalometry is commonly used in orthodontics to plan treatment. 头影测量法常用于正畸治疗计划。 |  Cephalometrics is used to assess facial growth and development. 头影测量法用于评估面部生长发育。 | synonyms: craniometry | antonyms: N/A | noun
