coiffure: /kwɑːˈfjʊr/ (US), /kwɑːˈfjuːr/ (UK)| n.|发型，发式；理发；头饰|A style of arranging the hair; the act of arranging the hair.|Her elaborate coiffure must have taken hours to create. 她那精致的发型肯定要花好几个小时才能做好。|Synonyms: hairstyle, hairdo, coif|Antonyms: unkempt hair, messy hair|noun

coiffure: /kwɑːˈfjʊr/ (US), /kwɑːˈfjuːr/ (UK)| v.|给…做头发，做发型|To style or arrange the hair.|The stylist coiffured her hair for the wedding.  发型师为她的婚礼做了头发。|Synonyms: style, arrange, dress|Antonyms: unstyle, disarrange|verb
