lorimer:/ˈlɔːrɪmər/| n.|马具匠，制造马嚼子的人；金属制品工匠|A lorimer is a craftsman who makes bits, spurs, and other metalwork for horses, or a metalworker in general. |例句：The lorimer crafted a beautiful set of spurs for the young rider.  这位马具匠为年轻的骑手制作了一套漂亮的马刺。

例句：In medieval times, the lorimer was an essential craftsman in any town. 在中世纪，马具匠是任何城镇都必不可少的工匠。|近义词：metalworker, blacksmith, farrier|反义词:|n.
