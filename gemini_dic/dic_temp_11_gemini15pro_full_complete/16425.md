bardships: /ˈbɑːrdʃɪps/
noun
艰难困苦；诗人的苦难
difficulties; hardships experienced by poets.  Specifically, it refers to the struggles and challenges faced by poets, often in relation to their art, such as lack of recognition, poverty, or creative blocks. While the word itself isn't common, it's a legitimate formation combining "bard" (poet) and "hardships."
The young bard faced many bardships on his journey to become a renowned poet.  这位年轻的诗人在成为著名诗人的道路上，面临着许多艰难困苦。
synonyms:  struggles, trials, tribulations, difficulties, adversity
antonyms:  ease, comfort, prosperity, success
