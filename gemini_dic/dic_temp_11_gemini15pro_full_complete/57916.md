drilled:/drɪld/| v. | 钻孔，训练，反复灌输 | to make a hole in something using a drill; to teach someone something by repeating it many times; to train soldiers, etc. |  The carpenter drilled a hole in the wood. 木匠在木头上钻了个孔。

The coach drilled the team on their plays. 教练反复训练队员们的战术。 | 近义词: pierced, bored, trained, practiced | 反义词: filled, ignored | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English drillen, from Middle Dutch drillen, from Old English þyrlian (“to pierce, bore, drill”)
