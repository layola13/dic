espaliered:/ɪˈspæliərd/
adjective
成扇形的，棚架式生长的
trained to grow flat against a wall or other support.
The espaliered fruit trees lined the garden wall.  成扇形的果树排列在花园墙边。
The roses were espaliered against the fence. 玫瑰被 trained to grow against the trellis. 玫瑰被训练成沿着墙壁生长。

近义词:trained, supported
反义词:untrained, unsupported
词性:adjective

Etymology: From French *espalier*, from Old Italian *spalliera* "something to lean against," from *spalla* "shoulder," from Latin *spatula* "shoulder blade."
