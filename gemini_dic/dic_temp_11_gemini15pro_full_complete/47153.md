dawdle:/ˈdɔːdl/ (US) /ˈdɔːdəl/ (UK)| vi. n.|闲逛，游荡；拖延，磨蹭|To waste time; to be slow in action or movement; to linger idly.|例句：Stop dawdling and get ready for school! 别磨蹭了，准备上学！|近义词：linger, loiter, delay, procrastinate, tarry|反义词：hurry, hasten, rush|词性:动词(vi.)

dawdle:/ˈdɔːdl/ (US) /ˈdɔːdəl/ (UK)| n.|闲逛，游荡|The act of dawdling; slow movement or lingering.|例句：The scenic dawdle along the riverbank was a welcome respite. 沿着河岸的风景闲逛是一次受欢迎的休息。|近义词：procrastination, delay|反义词：haste, rush|词性:名词(n.)

ETYMOLOGY->
From perhaps a frequentative of *daddeln "to trifle," of imitative origin.
