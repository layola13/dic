overlubrications: /ˌoʊvərˌluːbrɪˈkeɪʃənz/
noun
过度润滑
The excessive application of lubricants.
The engine suffered damage due to overlubrication. 引擎由于过度润滑而受损。
Overlubrication can cause seals to fail. 过度润滑会导致密封件失效。
Synonyms: excessive lubrication
Antonyms: underlubrication, insufficient lubrication
