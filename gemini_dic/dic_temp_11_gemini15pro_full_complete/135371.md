nonpercussive:/ˌnɒnpəˈkʌsɪv/ (US) /ˌnɒnpəˈkʌsɪv/ (UK)| adj.|非打击的|Not involving or producing percussion (striking or hitting).  Relating to musical instruments that are not played by striking, such as wind or string instruments.|The flute is a nonpercussive instrument.|近义词：non-percussive|反义词：percussive|词性:形容词
