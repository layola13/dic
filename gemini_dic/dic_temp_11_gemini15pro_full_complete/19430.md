词源:
From Middle English *bikel, from Middle Dutch *bīkel, of unknown origin.  Possibly related to Middle Dutch pekel (“pickle, brine”).  Compare also West Frisian bikel (“cucumber”).  The word is attested earlier than pickle, and may have influenced its development.
