facetiousness:/fəˈsiːʃəsnəs/
noun
滑稽；诙谐；戏谑
The quality of being facetious; inappropriate humor, especially in a serious situation.
His facetiousness was not appreciated during the solemn ceremony.  他的滑稽在庄严的仪式上并不受欢迎。
近义词:levity, flippancy, wittiness, jocularity
反义词:seriousness, solemnity, gravity
词性:n.

facetious:/fəˈsiːʃəs/
adjective
滑稽的；爱开玩笑的；诙谐的
Treating serious issues with deliberately inappropriate humor; flippant.
He made a facetious remark about the boss, which didn't go over well. 他对老板开了个轻佻的玩笑，结果不太好。
近义词:flippant, frivolous, jocular, waggish
反义词:serious, solemn, earnest
词性:adj.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *facetiosus, from Latin facetiae ("witticisms"), from facetus ("witty, elegant"), perhaps from facere ("to do, make").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁- ("to put, place, set").
