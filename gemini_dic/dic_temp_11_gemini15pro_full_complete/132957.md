nonemotivenesses:/ˌnɒnɪˈmoʊtɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
缺乏感情；不动感情
The state or quality of being nonemotional; lack of emotion or emotional expression.
His nonemotivenesses made it difficult to tell how he truly felt. 他缺乏感情，让人很难知道他的真实感受。
The robot's nonemotivenesses were a key feature of its design.  这个机器人的不动感情是其设计的关键特征。
近义词: apathy, indifference, impassivity, coldness
反义词: emotionality, expressiveness, passion, fervor
词性: noun (plural)
