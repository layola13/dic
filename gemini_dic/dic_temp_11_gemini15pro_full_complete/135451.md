nonpertinency:/ˌnɑːnpɜːrtnənsi/
n.|不相关性；不切题；无关紧要|The nonpertinency of his remarks was evident. 他的话无关紧要，显而易见。|近义词：irrelevance, immateriality, extraneousness|反义词：relevance, pertinence, germaneness|词性:名词

nonpertinent:/ˌnɑːnpɜːrtnənt/
adj.|不相关的；不切题的；无关紧要的|His comments were nonpertinent to the discussion. 他的评论与讨论无关。|近义词：irrelevant, immaterial, extraneous|反义词：relevant, pertinent, germane|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + pertinent (relevant), from Latin pertinēre (to pertain).
