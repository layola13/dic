catre:/ˈkɑːtreɪ/ | n. |  手推车，运货马车 (especially in Spain or Latin America) | A catre is a small, usually two-wheeled cart used for transporting goods, particularly in Spain and Latin America. |  The farmer used a catre to bring his produce to market. 农民用手推车把农产品运到市场。 |Synonyms: cart, wagon, barrow | Antonyms: N/A | noun

catre:/ˈkɑːtreɪ/ | n. |  帆布床，简易床 (especially in Spain or Latin America) | A catre can also refer to a simple bed made of canvas stretched over a frame, similar to a cot. | He slept on a catre under the stars. 他睡在一张帆布床上，头顶是星空。 | Synonyms: cot, camp bed | Antonyms: N/A | noun
