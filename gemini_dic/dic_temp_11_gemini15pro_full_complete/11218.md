architraved: /ˈɑːrkɪtreɪvd/ | adj. | 有框缘的，有楣梁的 | Having an architrave; framed by an architrave.  An architrave is the molded frame around a door or window. | The architraved doorway gave the house a grand entrance.  这扇有框缘的门廊赋予了房子一个宏伟的入口。| Synonym: framed, bordered | Antonym: unframed, plain | adjective
