ineffervescibility:/ˌɪnɪfərvəˈsɛnsɪbɪlɪti/
n.
没有活力; 缺乏生气; 平淡无奇
lack of effervescence; the inability to effervesce; the quality of being flat or dull.
His ineffervescibility made him a poor choice for a motivational speaker. 他缺乏活力，使他成为激励人心的演讲者的糟糕人选。
近义词:dullness, flatness, lifelessness, insipidity
反义词:effervescence, vivacity, sparkle, liveliness
词性:名词

ineffervescible:/ˌɪnɪfərvəˈsɛnsɪbəl/
adj.
没有活力的; 缺乏生气的; 平淡无奇的
lacking effervescence; not effervescing; flat or dull.
The ineffervescible performance failed to capture the audience's attention.  这场毫无生气的表演未能抓住观众的注意力。
近义词:dull, flat, lifeless, insipid
反义词:effervescent, vivacious, sparkling, lively
词性:形容词
