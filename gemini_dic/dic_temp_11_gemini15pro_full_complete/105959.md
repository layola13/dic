kludgy: /ˈklʌdʒi/ (US), /ˈklʌdʒi/ (UK) | adj. | 笨拙的，粗糙的，拼凑的 | Awkward, clumsy, or inelegant, especially in design or construction; poorly made or put together.  A solution that is achieved in a clumsy or inelegant way, often involving unnecessary complexity. |  The software is kludgy and difficult to use.  这个软件很笨拙，难以使用。

The code was a kludgy workaround, but it got the job done. 这段代码是一个笨拙的权宜之计，但它完成了任务。 | 近义词: clumsy, awkward, inelegant, makeshift, jury-rigged, hacked | 反义词: elegant, refined, streamlined, sophisticated | 词性: 形容词
