| n. | 动脉硬化患者 | People who suffer from arteriosclerosis. | The study focused on a group of arteriosclerotics to understand the progression of the disease. 这项研究的重点是一组动脉硬化患者，以了解疾病的进展。 | 近义词: N/A (This usage is less common) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词 (plural)


词源: From arterio- (combining form of artery) + sclero- (combining form meaning hard) + -tic (adjective suffix).
