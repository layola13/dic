nagor:/ˈneɪɡɔːr/ | n. | 内裤；紧身裤 | A type of close-fitting trousers or leggings. |  She wore a pair of nagors under her skirt. 她穿着裙子，里面套了一条紧身裤。| synonyms: leggings, tights, trousers | antonyms: skirt, dress | noun

Etymology: From Hindi "nāgar," meaning "belonging to a town."  Originally referred to a type of tight trousers worn by men in India, later adopted into English to describe similar garments.
