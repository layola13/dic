deep-dyed:/ˌdiːpˈdaɪd/| adj.|根深蒂固的，彻底的，不妥协的|Completely imbued with a quality or belief; unwavering.|例句：He's a deep-dyed conservative. 他是一个根深蒂固的保守派。|例句：She is a deep-dyed romantic. 她是一个彻头彻尾的浪漫主义者。|近义词：inveterate, confirmed, entrenched, staunch|反义词：uncommitted, wavering, half-hearted|词性:形容词
词源:  From "deep" + "dyed,"  referring to fabrics dyed so thoroughly that the color penetrates completely and will not fade.  This figurative sense developed in the 17th century.
