fishery:/ˈfɪʃəri/| n.|渔业; 渔场; 水产业|A place where fish are raised for commercial purposes; the industry or occupation of catching fish.|例句：The fishery is an important part of the local economy.  这个渔场是当地经济的重要组成部分。

例句：Overfishing threatens the sustainability of the fishery. 过度捕捞威胁着渔业的可持续性。|近义词：fishing industry, aquaculture, fish farm|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


fishery:/ˈfɪʃəri/| n.|渔场（指捕鱼水域）|An area of water where fish are caught.|例句：The new regulations protect this fishery from commercial exploitation. 新规定保护这片渔场免受商业开发。

例句：Salmon return to this fishery every year to spawn. 鲑鱼每年回到这个渔场产卵。|近义词：fishing grounds, fishing area|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:  From Middle English *fisherie, from Anglo-Norman *fisherie, *pescherie, Old French pescherie (“fishery”), from peschier (“to fish”), from Late Latin piscāria (“fishing”), from Latin piscis (“fish”).
