misparaphrases:/ˌmɪspærəˈfreɪzɪz/ | n. | 误述; 歪曲; 曲解 | An act of paraphrasing something incorrectly, resulting in a distorted or inaccurate representation of the original meaning. |  He misparaphrased the scientist's findings, leading to a misunderstanding of the research. 他曲解了科学家的发现，导致对研究的误解。

| misinterpretations, distortions, misrepresentations | accurate paraphrases, correct interpretations, faithful representations | noun
