nonbeneficent:/ˌnɒnbɪˈnɛfɪsənt/
adjective
非仁慈的，不慈善的
Not doing or producing good; not beneficent.
A nonbeneficent policy that hurts the poor. 一项伤害穷人的不慈善政策。
Near Synonyms:  uncharitable, unkind, malevolent, harmful, detrimental
Antonyms: beneficent, benevolent, charitable, kind, altruistic
词性: adjective
