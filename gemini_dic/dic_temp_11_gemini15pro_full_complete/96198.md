indeterministic:/ˌɪndɪˌtɜːmɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 不确定的，非决定论的 | Not deterministic; having a random or unpredictable element; subject to chance. |  The future of the stock market is indeterministic and difficult to predict. 股票市场的未来是不确定的，难以预测。| The quantum world is inherently indeterministic. 量子世界本质上是不确定的。| Synonyms:  nondeterministic, stochastic, probabilistic, random | Antonyms: deterministic, predictable, certain | adjective
