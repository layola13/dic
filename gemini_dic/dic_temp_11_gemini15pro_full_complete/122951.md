mummery:/ˈmʌməri/| n.|滑稽古怪的举动；虚伪的仪式；装模作样|A hypocritical performance or ceremony; a ridiculous or farcical proceeding.  |The elaborate coronation ceremony seemed like mere mummery to the cynical reporter.|Synonyms: charade, pretense, farce, masquerade, sham|Antonyms: sincerity, genuineness, authenticity|noun
