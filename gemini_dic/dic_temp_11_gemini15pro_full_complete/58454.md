ducted:/ˈdʌktɪd/| adj.|管道式的，导管式的|Designed or provided with a duct or ducts for conveying air, fluids, etc.  Equipped with a tube or passage for conveying something.|例句：The house has ducted air conditioning. 这房子有管道式空调。|例句：Ducted fan provides thrust for VTOL aircraft. 管道风扇为垂直起降飞机提供推力。|近义词：channeled, piped, tubular|反义词:undirected, open|词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "duct" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Duct" comes from the Latin word "ductus," meaning "leading," which is the past participle of "ducere," meaning "to lead."
