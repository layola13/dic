incarcerated:/ɪnˈkɑːrsəreɪtɪd/
adjective
监禁的，关押的
Confined to prison; imprisoned.
例句：He was incarcerated for ten years for armed robbery.  他因持械抢劫被监禁了十年。

例句：Many innocent people were incarcerated during the war.  战争期间许多无辜的人被监禁。

近义词：imprisoned, jailed, confined, detained
反义词：released, freed, liberated
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin incarceratus, past participle of incarcerare "to imprison," from in- "in" + carcer "prison."
