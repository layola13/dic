liparian:/lɪˈpɛəriən/ | adj. | 河岸的，河边的；生长在河边的 | Relating to or situated on the banks of a river. | The liparian zone is crucial for maintaining water quality. 河岸带对于维持水质至关重要。 | The liparian forests provide habitat for a variety of species. 河岸森林为各种物种提供了栖息地。 |  近义词：riverside, riparian, riverine | 反义词: upland | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *riparius* (relating to a riverbank), from *ripa* (bank, shore).
