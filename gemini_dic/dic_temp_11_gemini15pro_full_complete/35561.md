coalitional:/ˌkoʊəˈlɪʃənəl/
adjective
联合的，同盟的
Relating to or formed by a coalition.
The coalitional government faced numerous challenges. 联合政府面临诸多挑战。
The parties formed a coalitional agreement to address the economic crisis. 各党派为了应对经济危机，达成了联合协议。
Synonyms:  allied, joint, combined, cooperative
Antonyms:  independent, separate, individual
词性: adjective
