molokane:/ˌmoʊloʊˈkɑːneɪ/ | n. | 摩洛卡内人；摩洛卡内教派成员 | A member of a Christian sect in South Africa that abstains from meat, alcohol, tobacco, and military service.  They believe in a direct, personal relationship with God without intermediaries. |  Example 1: The Molokane gathered for a peaceful prayer meeting. 摩洛卡内人聚集在一起举行了和平的祈祷会。

Example 2:  Many Molokane are farmers who live a simple life. 许多摩洛卡内人是过着简朴生活的农民。 | 近义词: pacifist, vegetarian (in some aspects) | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) carnivore, warmonger | 词性: 名词
