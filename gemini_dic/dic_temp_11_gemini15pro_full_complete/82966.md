gungy:/ˈɡʌŋɡi/| adj.|发霉的，腐烂的；潮湿的，不舒服的|Unpleasantly damp and sticky; musty and stale.  Often used to describe a smell or feeling of dampness. It can also refer to a place that is damp and moldy.|The old basement had a gungy smell.  那旧地下室有一股霉味。|近义词：damp, musty, moldy, clammy, sticky|反义词：dry, arid, fresh, clean|词性:形容词

词源:  Likely of imitative origin, suggesting the sticky, unpleasant feeling of something damp and decaying.  No clear etymological root has been established.
