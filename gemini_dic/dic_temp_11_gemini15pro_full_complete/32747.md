chloroplasts:/ˈklɔːrəˌplæsts/ /ˈklɔːrəˌplɑːsts/
n.|叶绿体|Organelles within plant cells and other eukaryotic organisms that conduct photosynthesis.|例句：Chloroplasts are essential for photosynthesis. 叶绿体对光合作用至关重要。

例句：The chloroplasts in the leaves capture sunlight. 叶子中的叶绿体捕获阳光。|近义词:plastids|反义词:None|词性:Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From chloro- + -plast.  Chloro- comes from the Greek "chloros" meaning "green."  -Plast comes from the Greek "plastos" meaning "formed" or "molded."
