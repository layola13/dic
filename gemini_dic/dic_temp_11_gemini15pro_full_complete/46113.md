cylindricality:/ˌsɪlɪnˈdrɪkəlɪti/
n.
圆柱度，圆柱性
The state or quality of being cylindrical.
The cylindricality of the pipe was measured using a laser scanner.  用激光扫描仪测量了管道的圆柱度。
The engineer checked the cylindricality of the piston. 工程师检查了活塞的圆柱度。
近义词: roundness, cylindrical form
反义词: asymmetry, irregularity
词性: noun
词源: From cylindrical + -ity.  Cylindrical comes from cylinder + -ical. Cylinder comes from the Greek kylindros, meaning "roller, cylinder."
