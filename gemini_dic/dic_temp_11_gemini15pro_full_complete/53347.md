disadvantageous:/ˌdɪsədvænˈteɪdʒəs/
adjective
不利的，有害的
Presenting a disadvantage; unfavorable.
例句：This policy is disadvantageous to small businesses.  这项政策对小型企业不利。

例句：The current economic climate is disadvantageous for investors. 当前的经济环境对投资者不利。


近义词：unfavorable, detrimental, adverse, harmful, prejudicial
反义词：advantageous, beneficial, favorable, helpful
