humanistic:/ˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪk/| adj.|人文主义的；人本主义的；人道主义的|Relating to humanism; emphasizing human potential and values.|例句：Humanistic psychology emphasizes personal growth and self-actualization. 人本主义心理学强调个人成长和自我实现。

例句：The museum boasts a rich collection of humanistic art and literature.  这家博物馆收藏了丰富的人文主义艺术和文学作品。

近义词：humanitarian, compassionate, benevolent|反义词：inhuman, cruel, uncaring|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *humanisticus*, from Latin humanus (“human”) + -isticus (suffix forming adjectives). Equivalent to human +‎ -istic.
