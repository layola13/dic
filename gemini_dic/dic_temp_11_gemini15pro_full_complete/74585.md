frazzling:/ˈfræzlɪŋ/| adj.|令人疲惫不堪的，令人紧张的|Extremely tiring and stressful, causing someone to feel frayed and worn out.|The frazzling pace of modern life can lead to burnout.  现代生活的快节奏会导致倦怠。

The constant noise was frazzling my nerves.  持续不断的噪音让我神经紧张。|近义词：exhausting, tiring, stressful, nerve-racking|反义词：relaxing, calming, soothing|词性:形容词

frazzle:/ˈfræzl/| vt.  n.|使疲惫不堪；磨损；焦慮；(美)油炸食品|To exhaust physically or emotionally; to wear out;  a state of being frazzled; (US) fried food.|The heat and the long journey completely frazzled me.  炎热的天气和漫长的旅程让我筋疲力尽。

He was in a complete frazzle after the meeting.  会议结束后，他完全崩溃了。

I ordered chicken frazzles at the diner. 我在小餐馆点了一份炸鸡柳。|近义词：(v.) exhaust, tire, wear out; (n.) exhaustion, fatigue|反义词：(v.) relax, refresh; (n.) calmness, composure|词性:动词，名词


词源:  From the earlier *frazel, probably from fray +‎ -le.  Related to the verb fray, meaning to unravel or wear away.
