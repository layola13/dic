bracingly:/ˈbreɪsɪŋli/| adv.|令人振奋地，清新地|In a way that is invigorating and refreshing.|The cold wind blew bracingly across the moors. 冷风令人振奋地吹过荒野。|近义词：invigoratingly, refreshingly, stimulatingly|反义词：depressingly, weakeningly|词性:副词
Etymology: From brace (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Brace" comes from Old French "bracier" meaning "to embrace," ultimately from Latin "bracchium" meaning "arm."
