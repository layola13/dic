jutted:/ˈdʒʌtɪd/
jutting:/ˈdʒʌtɪŋ/
verb (past tense), verb (present participle)|突出；伸出|To extend sharply outward or upward; project.|The rocky cliffs jutted out over the sea.  悬崖峭壁突出在海面上。|The pier jutted into the lake.  码头伸入湖中。|protrude, stick out, project|recede, retract|v.

jut:/dʒʌt/
noun|突出部分；伸出物|Something that juts out; a projection.|The jut of the cliff offered a spectacular view.  悬崖的突出部分提供了壮观的景色。|projection, overhang, protrusion|indentation, recess|n.
verb|突出；伸出|To extend sharply outward or upward; project.|Rocks jut out from the cliff face. 岩石从悬崖面上突出。|The balcony juts out over the street. 阳台伸出到街道上方。|protrude, stick out, project|recede, retract|v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *jutten*, perhaps from Old French *jeter* (“to throw, cast”), from Latin *iactāre*, frequentative of *iacere* (“to throw”).
