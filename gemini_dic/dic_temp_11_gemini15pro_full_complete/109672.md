leucophane:/ˈluːkəfeɪn/ | n. | 白硅钙石 | A mineral, a silicate of calcium, sodium, and beryllium, occurring in yellow or greenish masses.  It fluoresces bright green under UV light. | This leucophane specimen displays a vibrant green fluorescence. 这块白硅钙石标本呈现出鲜艳的绿色荧光。 | Synonym: none (specific mineral name) | Antonym: none | noun
