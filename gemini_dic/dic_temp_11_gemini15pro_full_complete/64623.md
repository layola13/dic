erysipelatoid:/ˌɛrɪsɪˈpɛləˌtɔɪd/
adjective
类丹毒的，丹毒样的
Resembling erysipelas; characterized by superficial inflammation of the skin.
The rash appeared erysipelatoid, with clearly demarcated borders.  皮疹呈类丹毒状，边界清晰。
The infection presented with an erysipelatoid appearance, prompting concerns about erysipelas itself. 感染呈现出类丹毒的外观，引发了对丹毒本身的担忧。
近义词:erysipelas-like
反义词:(None - describes a visual characteristic, not a state with a clear opposite)
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From erysipelas + -oid.  Erysipelas comes from Greek *erysipélas, from erythrós "red" + pélla "skin".  The suffix -oid means "resembling" or "like".
