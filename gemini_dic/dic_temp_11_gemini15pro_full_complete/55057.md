disrank:/dɪsˈræŋk/
verb
降低…的等级；贬黜
to reduce in rank; degrade
He was disranked from sergeant to private. 他从军士降级为列兵。
近义词:demote, degrade, downgrade
反义词:promote, elevate, upgrade
词性:v.

词源:
From dis- + rank (n.).  The prefix "dis-" indicates reversal or negation.  The word "rank" refers to position in a hierarchy. Therefore, "disrank" literally means to remove from a rank.
