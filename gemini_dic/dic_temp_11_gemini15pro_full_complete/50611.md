deregulated:/diːˈreɡjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
撤销管制的；解除管制的
freed from regulation or control.
The deregulated electricity market has led to more competition.  撤销管制的电力市场导致了更多的竞争。
The government deregulated the banking industry in the 1990s. 政府在20世纪90年代撤销了对银行业的管制。
Synonyms: liberalized, uncontrolled, freed, open
Antonyms: regulated, controlled, restricted
词性: adjective
