counterweigh:/ˌkaʊntərˈweɪ/
verb|抵消，平衡|To act against with equal force; offset or balance.|例句：The advantages counterweigh the disadvantages. 优点抵消了缺点。|近义词：offset, balance, compensate, neutralize|反义词：enhance, augment, aggravate|v.
counterweigh:/ˈkaʊntərweɪt/
noun|平衡物，砝码|Something that counterbalances or offsets something else.|例句：The government's spending cuts acted as a counterweight to inflation. 政府的削减开支措施起到了抑制通货膨胀的作用。|近义词：counterbalance, offset|反义词：supplement, addition|n.
