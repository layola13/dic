interlabeled:/ˌɪntərˈleɪbəld/
adjective
相互标记的，互相贴标签的
marked or labeled with respect to each other.
The samples were interlabeled to avoid confusion. 这些样品相互标记以避免混淆。
近义词: cross-labeled, reciprocally labeled
反义词: unlabeled
