finestiller:/ˈfaɪnˌstɪlər/
|n.|
|精馏器；精密蒸馏器|
|An apparatus designed for fine or precise distillation, often used to separate and purify liquids based on their boiling points.|
|The finestiller allowed for precise separation of the volatile components.  精馏器可以精确分离挥发性成分。|
|They invested in a new finestiller to improve the quality of their product. 他们投资了一个新的精馏器来提高产品质量。|
|近义词: distillation apparatus, refining still|
|反义词: N/A|
|词性: noun|


词源:
"fine" (meaning high-quality, precise) + "still" (a device for distilling liquids).  The word combines the concept of a still with the added precision and refinement implied by "fine."  "Still" itself comes from the Old English word "stillan" meaning "to drip, trickle."
