dyking:/ˈdaɪkɪŋ/
n.
筑堤；堤防工程；用堤坝围住
the act of building dikes or walls to hold back water
例句：The dyking project protected the town from flooding.  筑堤工程保护了小镇免受洪水侵袭。

例句：Coastal dyking is essential for protecting low-lying areas. 沿海筑堤对于保护低洼地区至关重要。

近义词：embankment, levee construction
反义词：dredging, excavation
词性：名词

dyking:/ˈdaɪkɪŋ/
v.
筑堤；用堤坝围住
to build a dike or dikes
例句：They are dyking the river to prevent flooding.  他们正在给河筑堤以防止洪水。

例句：The farmers dyked their fields to protect the crops. 农民们为保护庄稼，给田地筑了堤坝。

近义词：embanking, leveeing
反义词：dredging, excavating
词性：动词
