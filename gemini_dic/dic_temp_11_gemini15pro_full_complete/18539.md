beneficial:/ˌbɛnɪˈfɪʃəl/| adj.|有益的，有利的|Producing good or helpful results or effects.|例句：Regular exercise is beneficial to your health.  规律的运动对你的健康有益。|近义词：advantageous, helpful, profitable, salutary|反义词：detrimental, harmful, injurious|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *beneficialis, from Latin beneficium (“benefit, kindness, favor”) + -alis (suffix forming adjectives).
