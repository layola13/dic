estovers:/ˈɛstəvərz/
n.
必需的生活必需品（尤指燃料木材）;生存必需品
The right of a tenant to take necessary wood from the landlord's property for fuel, repairs, etc.
Example: The tenant exercised his right of estovers by gathering fallen branches for firewood.  房客行使其必需品权利，收集落下的树枝作为柴火。

Example:  In the old lease, the estovers were specifically defined as including wood for cooking and heating. 在旧的租约中，必需品被明确定义为包括用于烹饪和取暖的木材。
Synonyms: necessities, provisions, supplies, allowance
Antonyms: luxuries, nonessentials
