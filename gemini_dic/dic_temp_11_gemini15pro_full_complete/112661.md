lower-case:/ˈloʊər keɪs/| adj.|小写的；小写字母的|Characterized by letters that are not capitals.  Refers to letters of the basic alphabet that are not majuscule (upper-case) letters.|The password must contain lower-case letters.  密码必须包含小写字母。|近义词:lowercase, minuscule|反义词:upper-case, majuscule, capital|词性:adjective


词源:  "Lower" and "case" refer to the position of the small letters in a traditional compositor's type case.  The small letters were kept in the lower portion of the case, while the capital letters were kept in the upper case.
