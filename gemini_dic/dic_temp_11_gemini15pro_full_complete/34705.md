cleverish:/ˈklɛvərɪʃ/| adj.|略微聪明的，有点小聪明的|Somewhat clever; showing a degree of cleverness, often in a superficial or pretentious way.  It implies a slight or affected cleverness, sometimes suggesting a lack of genuine depth or wisdom.|He made a cleverish remark, but it didn't really contribute to the conversation. 他说了一句略显聪明的话，但这并没有真正对谈话做出贡献。|近义词：smart, bright, ingenious, shrewd|反义词：stupid, foolish, dull, unintelligent|词性:形容词
