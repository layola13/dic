numismatics: /ˌnuːmɪzˈmætɪks/ /ˌnjuːmɪzˈmætɪks/
n.
钱币学；钱币收藏
The study or collection of coins, paper money, and medals.
He is a keen student of numismatics. 他是一位热衷于钱币学研究的学生。

My grandfather's passion for numismatics led him to amass a vast collection of rare coins. 我祖父对钱币学的热情使他积累了大量稀有硬币。


近义词: coin collecting, notaphily
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider spending or squandering as conceptually opposite)
词性: noun
