measly:/ˈmiːzli/| adj.|少得可怜的；微不足道的；低劣的|Contemptibly small or few. Characterized by or suggestive of being affected with measles.|He offered me a measly sum for my hard work. 他为我的辛勤工作只给了我一点点钱。|Synonyms: paltry, meager, insignificant, trifling, pitiful, derisory, negligible|Antonyms: generous, substantial, considerable, ample, abundant|adjective
