obsoletion:/ˌɒbsəˈliːʃən/ /ˌɑːbsəˈliːʃən/ | n. | 过时，废弃，淘汰 |The obsoletion of old technology is a constant process.  旧技术的淘汰是一个持续的过程。|  The planned obsoletion of this model will make way for a newer version.  这个型号的计划淘汰将为更新的版本让路。| 近义词： obsolescence, phasing out, discontinuation | 反义词：  renewal, revival, restoration | 词性: noun


Etymology->
From Latin *obsoletus*, past participle of *obsolescere* ("to fall into disuse, become obsolete"), from *ob-* ("toward, against") + *solescere* ("to grow accustomed"), from *solere* ("to be accustomed").
