nonsalvageable:/ˌnɒnsælˈvɪdʒəbl/ /ˌnɑːnsælˈvɪdʒəbl/
adjective
不可挽救的，无法抢救的
Not capable of being salvaged or saved.
The fire destroyed everything; the building was completely nonsalvageable.  大火摧毁了一切；这栋楼完全无法挽救了。
The car was so badly damaged in the crash that it was deemed nonsalvageable.  这辆车在车祸中损毁严重，被认为无法修复。
Synonyms: irreparable, unrecoverable, ruined, destroyed, beyond repair
Antonyms: repairable, recoverable, salvageable, restorable
词性: 形容词
