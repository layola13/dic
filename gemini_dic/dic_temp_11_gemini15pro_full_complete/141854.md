orchestras:/ˈɔːrkɪstrəz/
n.
管弦乐队
A large group of musicians who play various instruments together, typically including strings, woodwinds, brass, and percussion.
The orchestra played a beautiful symphony. 管弦乐队演奏了一曲优美的交响乐。
synonyms:  band, ensemble, philharmonic
antonyms:  soloist, single performer
noun
