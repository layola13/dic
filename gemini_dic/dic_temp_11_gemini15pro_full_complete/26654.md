cocoon: /kəˈkuːn/
n.|茧;  保护层; 舒适安全的地方|A silky case spun by the larva of many insects, as a protection for the pupa.|The caterpillar spins a cocoon around itself. 毛毛虫在自己周围吐丝结茧。|近义词:chrysalis, shell, case, covering|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 exposed, vulnerable 表示没有保护的状态)|词性: 名词

Etymology: From French *cocon*, from Provençal *coucoun*, ultimately from Latin *coccum* "berry; kermes," referring to the resemblance of the kermes insect's protective covering to a berry.
