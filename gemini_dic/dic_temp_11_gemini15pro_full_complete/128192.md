neurasthenia:/ˌnʊərəsˈθiːniə/ | n. |神经衰弱|A condition characterized by fatigue, weakness, poor concentration, and other symptoms, often attributed to psychological stress or emotional conflict. | Example:  She was diagnosed with neurasthenia after months of intense pressure at work. 她工作几个月后被诊断为神经衰弱。

Example:  The doctor believed her neurasthenia was caused by a combination of stress and poor diet. 医生认为她的神经衰弱是由压力和不良饮食共同引起的。 | 近义词: nervous exhaustion, fatigue, debility | 反义词: vigor, energy | 词性: noun
