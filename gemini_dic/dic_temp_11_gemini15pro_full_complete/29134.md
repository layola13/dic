caseharden:/ˈkeɪsˌhɑːrdən/ /ˈkeɪsˌhɑrdn/| v.|表面硬化，使坚硬|To harden the surface of (metal) by carburizing and quenching.|The gears were casehardened to improve wear resistance. 齿轮经过表面硬化处理以提高耐磨性。|近义词：harden, toughen, temper, reinforce|反义词:soften, weaken|词性:verb

Etymology:
case (n.) + harden (v.)

case (n.): from Old French *casse "box, container," from Latin capsa "box, case"

harden (v.): from Old English heardian "to make hard," from heard "hard"
