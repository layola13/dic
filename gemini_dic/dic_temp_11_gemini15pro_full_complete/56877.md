dosed:/dəʊst/ | v. |给…服药；(使)服用(毒品) | given a dose of medicine or a drug |  He dosed the child with cough syrup. 他给孩子服用了咳嗽糖浆。| synonyms: medicated, treated | antonyms: untreated | verb

dosed:/dəʊst/ | adj. |(非正式)（尤指吸毒后）兴奋的，飘飘然的 | (informal) (especially of a person) under the influence of drugs, especially in an excited or euphoric state |  He seemed pretty dosed. 他看起来相当兴奋。| synonyms: high, stoned, intoxicated | antonyms: sober, straight | adjective
