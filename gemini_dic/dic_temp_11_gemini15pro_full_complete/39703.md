consignable:/kənˈsaɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可寄售的，可托运的 |  Suitable or allowed to be consigned, especially for sale or delivery. | The goods are consignable by rail.  这些货物可以通过铁路托运。|  Synonyms: shippable, transportable, deliverable | Antonyms: non-deliverable, non-transportable | adjective

consignable:/kənˈsaɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可交付的 | Something that can be handed over to the care of another. | The documents were consignable to the lawyer. 文件可交付给律师。 | Synonyms: transferable, deliverable | Antonyms: non-transferable, undeliverable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French consignable, from consigner "to consign," from Italian consignare, from Latin consignare "to seal, sign, mark," from com- "together" + signare "to mark."
