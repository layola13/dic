misbehaver:/ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪvər/| n. |不守规矩的人；行为不端者|A person who behaves badly, especially a child.|例句：The teacher sent the misbehaver to the principal's office. 老师把这个不守规矩的学生送到了校长办公室。

例句：The flight attendant warned the misbehavers to settle down. 空乘人员警告行为不端者安顿下来。


|近义词：delinquent, troublemaker, rascal, culprit |反义词:well-behaved person, angel|词性:noun
