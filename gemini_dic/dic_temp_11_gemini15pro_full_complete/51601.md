devastating:/ˈdevəsteɪtɪŋ/| adj.|毁灭性的，破坏性的|Causing widespread destruction or damage; extremely effective in a destructive way.|The earthquake had a devastating effect on the city. 地震对这座城市造成了毁灭性的影响。|近义词：destructive, ruinous, catastrophic, calamitous|反义词：beneficial, constructive, helpful|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "devastate," which comes from the Latin word "devastare," meaning "to lay waste," from "de-" (completely) + "vastare" (to lay waste).
