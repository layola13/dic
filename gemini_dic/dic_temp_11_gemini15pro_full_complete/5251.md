allod:/əˈlɒd/ |n.|  份地，保有地| An allodial title is one that is free and clear of any superior landlord.  It designates absolute ownership of real property. | He inherited the land as an allod, meaning it was entirely his own. 他继承了这块土地作为一份保有地，意味着它完全属于他自己。| synonym: freehold, fee simple | antonym: fief, feudal tenure | noun
