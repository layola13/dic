gofer:/ˈɡoʊfər/ (US), /ˈɡəʊfər/ (UK)| n.|跑腿的，杂工；囊鼠|A person whose job is to fetch and carry things, especially on a film set or in an office.  Also, a burrowing rodent of North and Central America.|例句：He started out as a gofer at the studio. 他最初在电影制片厂当跑腿的。

例句：The gophers had dug holes all over the lawn. 囊鼠在草坪上到处挖洞。|近义词：errand boy/girl, runner, lackey, flunky (for a person); ground squirrel, prairie dog (for the animal)|反义词:boss, manager (for a person)|词性:noun
