of-that-ilk:/əv ðæt ɪlk/ | adj.| 同名的；同类的；出身相同的 | Of the same name or place; of the same kind.  Originally meaning "of the same place," referring to a person from an estate of the same name as their surname. Later extended to mean "of that kind." | The Laird of Glencairn and Glencairn of that ilk. 格伦凯恩领主和同名的格伦凯恩。| synonyms: of the same kind, of the same sort, of the same type, of the same class, of the same ilk, of a piece, similar, alike, comparable, parallel, equivalent, identical, uniform, consistent, homogeneous | antonyms: different, dissimilar, unlike, contrasting, disparate, various, diverse, heterogeneous | adjective
