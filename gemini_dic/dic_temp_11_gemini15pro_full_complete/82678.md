guesswork:/ˈɡɛswɜːk/| n.|猜测，臆测|An estimate, judgment, or opinion made without adequate information; a conjecture.|His theory is based entirely on guesswork. 他的理论完全建立在猜测之上。|近义词：conjecture, speculation, supposition, assumption|反义词：knowledge, certainty, fact|词性:名词

Etymology: From guess + work.  "Guess" originates from Middle Dutch "gissen" meaning "to guess, suppose." "Work" comes from Old English "weorc" meaning "work, action, deed."
