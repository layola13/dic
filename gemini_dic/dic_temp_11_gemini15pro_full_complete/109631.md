letterwood: /ˈlɛtərˌwʊd/ | n. | 字母木 |  A type of wood, typically from the tropics, that exhibits markings resembling letters or hieroglyphs when cut.  These markings are caused by the tree's reaction to fungal infections. | The craftsman used letterwood to create a unique and beautiful box. 工匠用字母木制作了一个独特而美丽的盒子。|  snakewood, zebrawood, figured wood | plain wood | noun
