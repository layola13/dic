nonstandardization:/ˌnɒnˌstændərdɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 非标准化 | The lack of uniformity or adherence to a standard.  The process of something not being standardized. | The nonstandardization of medical procedures can lead to inconsistencies in patient care.  医疗程序的非标准化会导致病人护理的不一致性。| lack of standardization, inconsistency,  variation,  diversity | standardization, uniformity, conformity | noun


Etymology->
non- (prefix): meaning "not" or "opposite of" - from Latin *non*
standard (noun/adjective): from Old French *estandard*, ultimately from Frankish *standhard*
-ization (suffix): forming nouns of action or process - from French *-isation*
