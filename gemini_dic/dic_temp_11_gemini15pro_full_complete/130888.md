nonarrival:/ˌnɒnəˈraɪvəl/| n.|未到达，未抵达|The nonarrival of the shipment caused a delay in production. 这批货物的未到达导致生产延误。|例句：The nonarrival of the expected guests caused some concern. 预计的客人没有到达，引起了一些担忧。

The flight's nonarrival left hundreds of passengers stranded.  航班未抵达，导致数百名乘客滞留。|近义词：absence, default, failure to arrive|反义词：arrival, presence|词性:名词

Etymology:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + arrival (from Old French arriver, "to arrive," ultimately from Latin arripare, "to come to shore")
