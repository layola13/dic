layover:/ˈleɪˌoʊvər/ (US) /ˈleɪˌəʊvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|中途停留，短暂停留|A short stay in a place between parts of a journey, especially a journey by air.|例句：I had a long layover in Chicago. 我在芝加哥有一个很长的中途停留。

例句：The flight includes a two-hour layover in Dubai.  这趟航班包括在迪拜的两小时中途停留。


近义词：stopover, stop, break, intermission|反义词: nonstop, direct flight|词性:noun
