arraigning:/əˈreɪnɪŋ/
verb
控告，传讯；责难，指责
The act of formally accusing someone of a crime in a court of law, or criticizing someone severely.
The suspect was arraigned on charges of murder. 嫌疑犯被传讯，被指控犯有谋杀罪。
He arraigned the government for its inaction. 他谴责政府不作为。
synonyms:  indict, accuse, charge, prosecute, censure, criticize, denounce
antonyms:  exonerate, acquit, defend, praise, commend
v.
