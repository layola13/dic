chevronel:/ˈʃɛvrənəl/| n.|人字形, 尖角饰, 椽尾饰, 小带状饰|A chevronel is a miniature chevron, often used in heraldry as a mark of distinction.  It is a V-shaped figure, smaller than a chevron.|The coat of arms featured three gold chevronels on a blue background.  这个盾形纹章的特征是蓝色背景上的三个金色人字形。|近义词：chevron, badge, insignia|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
