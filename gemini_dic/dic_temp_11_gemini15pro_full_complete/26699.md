caddow:/ˈkædəʊ/ (UK), /ˈkædoʊ/ (US)| n. | 绒球，帽饰，军帽上的穗 | A woolen ball or tuft used as an ornament, especially on a hat or bonnet.  A caddow was traditionally worn on Scottish bonnets. | The soldier's bonnet had a bright red caddow.  士兵的帽子上有一个鲜红色的绒球。| synonyms: pompom, tassel, tuft | antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps plain, undecorated) | noun
