butterfish:/ˈbʌtərˌfɪʃ/| n. |黄油鱼，鲳科鱼类|A fish with smooth, oily skin, often considered a delicacy.  The term "butterfish" can refer to several different species. |例句：The butterfish was pan-fried to perfection. 黄油鱼煎得恰到好处。

例句：We had grilled butterfish for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了烤黄油鱼。|近义词：pompano, harvestfish|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From butter + fish, referring to the smooth, oily texture of the fish's skin.
