complainable:/kəmˈpleɪnəbl/| adj.|可抱怨的，值得抱怨的|Something that is complainable is something that you have a right to complain about.  It implies that there's a valid reason for a complaint.|The restaurant's slow service was complainable.  这家餐厅的服务速度很慢，值得抱怨。|synonyms: objectionable, unacceptable, undesirable|antonyms: satisfactory, acceptable, pleasing|adjective
