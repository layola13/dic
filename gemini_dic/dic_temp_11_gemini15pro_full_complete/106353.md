kokuya: /koʊˈkuːjə/ | n. | 故乡；祖国 |  A person's native land or village; homeland.  It evokes a sense of nostalgia and belonging. kokuya: /koʊˈkuːjə/ | adj. | 故乡的；祖国的 | Related to or characteristic of one's native land or village. | The kokuya traditions were still practiced in the community.  这个社区仍然保留着故乡的传统。 | Synonyms: native, home, local | Antonyms: foreign, alien | adjective
