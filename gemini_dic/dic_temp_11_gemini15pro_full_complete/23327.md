boyish:/ˈbɔɪɪʃ/ (US), /ˈbɔɪɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|男孩似的；幼稚的|Resembling or characteristic of a boy, typically in a playful or mischievous way.|She had a boyish charm with her short hair and playful attitude. 她短短的头发和爱玩耍的态度让她有一种男孩般的魅力。|例句：He retained a boyish enthusiasm for life. 他对生活保持着男孩般的热情。|近义词：childlike, youthful, puerile, immature|反义词：mature, womanly, manly, grown-up|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From boy + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives meaning "somewhat like" or "having the quality of."
