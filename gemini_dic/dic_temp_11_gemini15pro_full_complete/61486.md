embrocate:/ɪmˈbrəʊkeɪt/ /ɛmˈbroʊˌkeɪt/| vt.|涂擦；用…涂擦；|To rub a lotion or ointment into (a part of the body).|例句：He embrocated his aching muscles with a soothing balm. 他用舒缓的药膏涂抹疼痛的肌肉。

例句：She embrocated her horse's legs with liniment after the race. 赛后她用搽剂涂抹了马的腿。

近义词：rub, massage, anoint, apply|反义词: - |词性: verb

embrocation:/ˌɛm.broʊˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /ˌɪm.brəʊˈkeɪ.ʃən/| n.|涂擦；涂擦剂；|The act of rubbing a lotion or ointment into a part of the body, or the lotion or ointment itself.|例句：The embrocation soothed his sore muscles.  这种涂擦剂舒缓了他酸痛的肌肉。

例句：He applied a soothing embrocation to his injured knee. 他在受伤的膝盖上涂抹了舒缓的涂擦剂。

近义词：liniment, balm, ointment, lotion|反义词: - |词性: noun
