overbulkiness:/ˌoʊvərˈbʌlkinɪs/
n.
体积过大；过分庞大
The overbulkiness of the package made it difficult to handle.  这个包裹体积过大，难以搬运。
The overbulkiness of the furniture made it impossible to fit through the doorway. 这件家具过于庞大，无法穿过门口。
近义词: bulkiness, massiveness, hugeness
反义词: compactness, smallness, diminutiveness
词性: 名词

overbulky:/ˌoʊvərˈbʌlki/
adj.
过于庞大的；体积过大的
The overbulky equipment was difficult to transport. 这台过于庞大的设备很难运输。
The crate was too overbulky to fit in the elevator.  这个板条箱体积太大，放不进电梯。
近义词: bulky, massive, huge
反义词: compact, small, diminutive
词性: 形容词


词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "bulky" (adjective meaning "large and unwieldy")  "over-"（前缀，意为“过度”）+ "bulky"（形容词，意为“庞大而笨重的”）
