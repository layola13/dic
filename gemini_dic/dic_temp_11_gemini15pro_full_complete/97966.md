innutritious:/ˌɪnjuːˈtrɪʃəs/
adjective
缺乏营养的;没有营养的
Lacking nutrients; not nutritious.
例句：
Eating innutritious food can lead to health problems.
吃缺乏营养的食物会导致健康问题。

The child's diet was innutritious, consisting mainly of junk food.
这孩子的饮食缺乏营养，主要由垃圾食品组成。

近义词：unhealthy, unwholesome, nourishmentless
反义词：nutritious, healthy, wholesome
词性：adjective
