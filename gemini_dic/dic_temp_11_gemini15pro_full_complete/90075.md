hostelry: /ˈhɒstəlri/ (US) /ˈhɒstəlri/ (UK)| n. |旅店，客栈；酒店|A place that provides lodging and often food and drink, especially an inn or hotel.|例句：The weary travelers sought refuge in a cozy hostelry.  疲惫的旅行者们在一间舒适的旅店里寻求庇护。

例句：The hostelry offered a warm welcome and a hearty meal. 这家旅店提供了热情的欢迎和丰盛的餐点。|近义词：inn, hotel, lodge, tavern, public house|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 private residence 私人住宅)|词性: noun
