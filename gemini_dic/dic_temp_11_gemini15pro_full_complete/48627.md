defences:/dɪˈfɛnsɪz/
noun
防御；防卫；辩护；防护措施
The city's defences were strong. 这座城市的防御工事很坚固。
synonyms: protections, safeguards, fortifications
antonyms: vulnerabilities, weaknesses
n.

defence:/dɪˈfɛns/
noun
防御；防卫；辩护；防护措施
He spoke in defence of his actions. 他为自己辩护。
synonyms: protection, safeguard, justification
antonyms: vulnerability, weakness, prosecution
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *defenses*, plural of *defense*, from Latin dēfēnsa (“protection, defense”), from dēfendere (“to defend, protect”), from de- (“from, away”) + fendere (“to strike, ward off”).
