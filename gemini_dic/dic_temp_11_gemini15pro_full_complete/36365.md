coincidentally:/koʊˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəli/ /kəʊˌɪnsɪˈdentəli/
adverb
巧合地；碰巧地
happening by chance at the same time or in the same way
例句：Coincidentally, I was wearing the same dress as my friend. 巧合的是，我和我的朋友穿着同样的裙子。

例句：We coincidentally met at the airport. 我们在机场不期而遇。

近义词：accidentally, by chance, surprisingly
反义词：intentionally, deliberately
词性：副词
