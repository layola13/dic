metaphor:/ˈmɛtəfɔːr/ (US), /ˈmɛtəfə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 比喻，隐喻 |A figure of speech in which a word or phrase is applied to an object or action to which it is not literally applicable. |例句：Life is a journey.  人生是一场旅程。

例句：The snow is a white blanket. 雪像一张白色的毯子。

近义词：simile, analogy, figure of speech|反义词: literal meaning|词性: noun
