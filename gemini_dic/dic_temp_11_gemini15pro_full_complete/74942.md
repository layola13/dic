freudianism:/ˈfrɔɪdiənɪzəm/ | n. | 弗洛伊德学说; 弗洛伊德主义 |  The theories and doctrines of Sigmund Freud, especially those concerning the unconscious mind, the interpretation of dreams, and the role of sexuality in human behavior. | His art is full of Freudianism. 他的艺术充满了弗洛伊德主义。 |Synonyms: psychoanalysis, Freudian psychology, Freudian theory | Antonyms:  None (It's difficult to have a direct antonym for a complex theory like Freudianism) | Noun
