kayoes:/ˈkeɪoʊz/| n.| 击倒, 昏迷; (非正式) 厕所|A state of unconsciousness or stupor; (informal) a toilet.|He took a hard blow to the head and suffered a kayo. 他头部受到重击，昏迷了。|Near synonyms: knockout, KO, unconsciousness|Antonym: consciousness|noun


kayoed:/ˈkeɪoʊd/| v. |击倒，使昏迷|Past tense of "kayo," meaning to knock unconscious.|The boxer kayoed his opponent in the third round. 拳击手在第三回合击倒了对手。|Near synonyms: knocked out, KO'd|Antonym: revived|verb
