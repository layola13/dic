lusts:/lʌsts/| n.| 欲望，贪欲，渴望 |Intense desires, especially for sexual pleasure, power, or wealth. |He was consumed by lusts for power and wealth. 他被对权力和财富的渴望所吞噬。|近义词：desires, cravings, yearnings, passions|反义词：aversion, disgust, indifference| noun

Etymology: From Old English *lust*, meaning "pleasure, delight," and later "desire, appetite."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *lustuz, related to Old Norse *lyst* "pleasure," and Latin *lascivus* "wanton."
