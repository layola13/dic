mumbly:/ˈmʌmbli/ (US) /ˈmʌmbli/ (UK)| adj.|含糊不清的，咕哝的|Speaking or spoken in a low, indistinct voice, often with the mouth partly closed.|
He gave a mumbly reply. 他含糊地回答。
She mumbly apologized for being late. 她咕哝着道歉迟到了。

|近义词：inarticulate, indistinct, muffled, slurred, muttered|反义词：clear, articulate, distinct|词性:形容词|

mumble:/ˈmʌmbəl/ (US) /ˈmʌmbəl/ (UK)| v. n.|咕哝，含糊地说；咕哝，含糊的话|To speak in a low, indistinct voice, often with the mouth partly closed.|
He mumbled something about being tired. 他咕哝着说他累了。
She mumbled an apology. 她咕哝着道了歉。
The mumble of voices grew louder. 嗡嗡的说话声越来越大。

|近义词：mutter, murmur, slur|反义词：enunciate, articulate|词性:动词/名词|
