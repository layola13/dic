cuneiform:/ˈkjuːnɪfɔːrm/ /kjuˈniːəˌfɔrm/ | adj. n. |楔形的；楔形文字|A system of writing first developed by the ancient Sumerians of Mesopotamia, characterized by wedge-shaped marks made by pressing a stylus into clay tablets. |The museum has a fascinating collection of cuneiform tablets.  博物馆收藏了令人着迷的楔形文字泥板。| 近义词: wedge-shaped| 反义词: (adj.) rounded|词性: adjective, noun

cuneiform:/ˈkjuːnɪfɔːrm/ /kjuˈniːəˌfɔrm/ | n. |楔形文字|A system of writing first developed by the ancient Sumerians of Mesopotamia, characterized by wedge-shaped marks made by pressing a stylus into clay tablets. |Deciphering cuneiform has unlocked secrets of ancient civilizations.  破译楔形文字解开了古代文明的秘密。| 近义词: (for the writing system itself)  no single perfect synonym, but related terms include hieroglyphs, pictographs | 反义词:  (for the writing system itself) alphabet |词性: noun
