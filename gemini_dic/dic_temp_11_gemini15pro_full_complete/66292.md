excoriate: /ɪkˈskɔːrieɪt/ /ekˈskɔːrieɪt/
verb
严厉谴责；痛斥
to criticize severely and harshly
例句：The newspaper excoriated the mayor for his mishandling of the budget crisis.  这家报纸严厉谴责市长对预算危机的处理不当。

例句：The film critic excoriated the movie for its poor acting and predictable plot.  影评人痛斥这部电影演技拙劣，情节老套。

近义词：denounce, condemn, criticize, lambaste, castigate
反义词：praise, commend, laud, extol
词性：verb
