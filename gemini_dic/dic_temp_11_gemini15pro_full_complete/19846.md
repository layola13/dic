bindable:/ˈbaɪndəbl/| adj.|可绑定的，可装订的|Something that is bindable can be bound, often referring to documents or materials that can be fastened together to form a book or booklet.  It can also refer to something that is capable of being legally or morally obligated. |The loose sheets were bindable into a report.  这些散页可以装订成一份报告。|近义词：attachable,  fastenable|反义词: loose, unbound|词性: adjective

bindable:/ˈbaɪndəbl/| adj.|有约束力的|Something that is bindable can create a legal or moral obligation. |The agreement is bindable on all parties. 该协议对所有各方均具有约束力。|近义词：obligatory, mandatory|反义词: non-binding, optional|词性: adjective
