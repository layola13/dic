gibbously:/ˈɡɪbəsli/| adv.|凸状地，隆起地|Resembling the shape of a gibbous moon; more than half but less than fully illuminated.  像凸月形状的；一半以上但不到完全照亮。|The gibbously swelling moon hung in the sky. 凸状隆起的月亮挂在天上。|The moon shone gibbously through the trees. 月亮凸凸地透过树林照耀着。|近义词：convexly, roundedly|反义词：concavely|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin gibbosus (“humpbacked”), from Latin gibbus (“hump, protuberance”).  源自晚期拉丁语 gibbosus（“驼背的”），来自拉丁语 gibbus（“驼峰，突起”）。
