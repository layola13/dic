elemental:/ˌelɪˈmentl/ | adj. | 基本的；元素的；自然力的 | Relating to or resembling the powers of nature; primary or basic. | The elemental force of the hurricane was devastating. 飓风的自然力量是毁灭性的。 | The artist captured the elemental beauty of the landscape. 这位艺术家捕捉到了风景的基本美。 | 近义词: basic, fundamental, essential, primal, rudimentary | 反义词: complex, sophisticated, advanced, refined | 词性: 形容词

elemental:/ˌelɪˈmentl/ | n. | 精灵；自然力 | A mythical being, especially one associated with a particular natural force. |  Ancient cultures believed in elementals like fire spirits and water nymphs. 古代文化相信精灵的存在，比如火精灵和水仙女。| Legends told of powerful elementals guarding sacred groves. 传说中有强大的元素守护着神圣的小树林。| 近义词: spirit, being, entity | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, depends on the specific elemental being) | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin elementalis, from Latin elementum ‘element’.
