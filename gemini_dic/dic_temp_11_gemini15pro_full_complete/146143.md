overencourage: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒ/ | v. | 过度鼓励，过分鼓励 | To encourage someone excessively, often to the point of hindering their development or creating a false sense of security. |  He overencouraged his son, praising him even for mediocre efforts, which ultimately made the boy less motivated to strive for excellence. 他过度鼓励他的儿子，即使是平庸的努力也赞扬他，这最终使男孩缺乏 striving for excellence. | 近义词: coddle, pamper, spoil | 反义词: discourage, underwhelm, neglect | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "encourage" (from Old French "encoragier," meaning "to make heartened or bold")
