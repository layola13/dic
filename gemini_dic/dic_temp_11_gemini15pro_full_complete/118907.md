metavolcanic:/ˌmɛtəvɒlˈkænɪk/ | adj. | 变质火山岩的 | Formed from volcanic rock by metamorphism.  Relating to or denoting rock of volcanic origin that has been altered by metamorphism. | The metavolcanic rocks in this area are rich in minerals.  这个地区的变质火山岩富含矿物质。| Synonyms: metaigneous, metamorphic (in part) | Antonyms: sedimentary, igneous (in a strict sense) | adjective
