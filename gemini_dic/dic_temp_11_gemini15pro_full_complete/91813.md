hyperflexion:/ˌhaɪpərˈflɛkʃən/| n.|过度屈曲，超屈|Excessive bending of a limb or joint, beyond its normal range of motion.  This can cause strain or injury.|The gymnast suffered a hyperflexion injury to her knee.|近义词：overflexion|反义词: hyperextension|词性:noun

Etymology:  From "hyper-" (Greek:  ὑπέρ, hupér, "over, beyond") + "flexion" (from Latin *flexiō, from flectere, "to bend").
