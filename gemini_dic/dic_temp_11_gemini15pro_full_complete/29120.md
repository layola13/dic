cascarilla:/ˌkæskəˈrɪlə/| n.|卡斯卡里拉树皮，卡斯卡里拉酒|The aromatic bark of a West Indian shrub (Croton eluteria), formerly used as a tonic and febrifuge.  Also, a liqueur flavored with this bark.|The bartender mixed a delicious cocktail with cascarilla.  调酒师用卡斯卡里拉调制了一款美味的鸡尾酒。|Synonyms:  sweetwood bark, eleutheria bark|Antonyms:  None|noun
