ballsy:/ˈbɔːlzi/| adj.|有胆量的，大胆的，有魄力的|Showing courage, guts, and determination, often in a way that is considered brazen or reckless.  It is informal and can sometimes be considered vulgar.|例句：It was a ballsy move to start his own business with no experience.  开创自己的事业，没有经验，这真是一个大胆的举动。

例句：She made a ballsy decision to quit her stable job and pursue her dream. 她做了一个大胆的决定，辞去了稳定的工作去追求自己的梦想。|近义词：gutsy, courageous, daring, audacious, bold, brave|反义词：timid, cowardly, cautious, apprehensive|词性:adjective
