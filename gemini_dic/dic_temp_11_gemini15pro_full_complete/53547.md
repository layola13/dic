disband:/dɪsˈbænd/
v. |解散，遣散|To break up or dissolve (a group, organization, or assembly).|The band disbanded after their farewell tour. 乐队在告别巡演后解散了。|
v. |解散，遣散|The army was disbanded after the war. 战后军队被解散了。|
近义词:break up, dissolve, disperse, scatter
反义词:assemble, gather, unite, form
词性:verb

disband:/dɪsˈbænd/
n. |解散，遣散|The act of disbanding or the state of being disbanded.|The disbandment of the group left many fans disappointed.  该团体的解散让许多粉丝失望。|
近义词:dissolution, breakup
反义词:formation, union
词性:noun


词源:
From dis- "apart" + band "a band."  Meaning "to break up a band (of soldiers)" is from mid-15c.  The general sense of "to break up (any assembly)" is from late 15c.
