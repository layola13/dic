bioethical:/ˌbaɪoʊˈɛθɪkəl/
adjective
生物伦理的
Relating to the ethical implications of biological research and applications, especially in medicine.
Bioethical concerns surround the use of genetic engineering.  基因工程的使用引发了生物伦理方面的担忧。
Bioethical dilemmas are increasingly common in modern medicine.  生物伦理困境在现代医学中越来越普遍。
近义词:moral, ethical
反义词:unethical, immoral
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
bio- (life) + ethical (relating to morals)
From Greek "bios" (life) + "ethikos" (moral)
