myzontes:/maɪˈzɒntiːz/ | n. | 盗贼，强盗 |  A group of ancient Cilician pirates. They disrupted Roman trade in the Mediterranean, especially during the Mithridatic Wars.  They were notorious for their brutality and extensive network of hidden bases. | The myzontes plundered merchant ships and coastal towns, causing significant disruption to Roman trade.  Myzontes were feared throughout the Mediterranean for their ruthless tactics and elusive nature. | 近义词: pirates, buccaneers, freebooters, corsairs | 反义词: merchants, traders, sailors | 词性: 名词
