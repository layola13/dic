delectables:/dɪˈlɛktəblz/
| n. |美味佳肴；好吃的東西|Pleasing to the senses, especially to the taste; delicious.  Things that are extremely pleasing to the senses, especially to taste.|The baker filled the window with delectables of all kinds. 麵包師在櫥窗裡擺滿了各式各樣的美味佳肴。|近义词：treats, delicacies, goodies, delights|反义词：inedibles, unappetizing food|词性:名词
delectable:/dɪˈlɛktəbl/
| adj. |美味的；令人愉悦的|Delicious; highly pleasing. Extremely pleasing to the senses, especially to taste.|The chef prepared a delectable meal.  廚師準備了一頓美味的佳餚。|近义词：delicious, tasty, savory, palatable, exquisite|反义词：unappetizing, distasteful, unsavory|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Middle English: from Old French delectable, from late Latin delectabilis, from Latin delectare ‘to delight’, from de- (expressing intensive force) + lactare ‘to entice, allure’.
