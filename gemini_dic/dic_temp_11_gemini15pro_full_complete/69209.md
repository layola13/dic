feared:/fɪrd/| adj.|害怕的，恐惧的|Feared is the past participle of the verb "fear," used as an adjective to describe something that is causing fear or dread.|The feared dictator ruled with an iron fist.  这位令人恐惧的独裁者用铁腕统治。|近义词：dreaded, awesome, formidable|反义词：loved, adored, admired|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *fered, from Old English *færed, past participle of færan (“to frighten, terrify”), from Proto-Germanic *fazjaną (“to frighten”), from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂s- (“to frighten”).
