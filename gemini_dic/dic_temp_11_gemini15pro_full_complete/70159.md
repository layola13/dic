fibbed:/fɪbd/
verb
撒谎，编造
tell a lie; invent something untrue
例句：He fibbed about his age to get into the club. 他为了进俱乐部谎报了年龄。

例句：She fibbed, saying she had been to the doctor. 她撒谎说她去看过医生了。

近义词：lied, prevaricated, fabricated
反义词：told the truth
词性：verb
