discifloral:/dɪsɪˈflɔːrəl/
adjective
花盘状的；盘状花的
Having the structure or form of a disc floret; relating to or characterized by disc florets.
The sunflower has both ray florets and discifloral florets.  向日葵既有舌状花也有盘状花。
The botanist identified the plant as discifloral based on the arrangement of its petals.  植物学家根据其花瓣的排列方式将其鉴定为盘状花的。
synonym:discoid
antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "ray floret" as a contrasting structure within the same flower head)
word type: adjective
