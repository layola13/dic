issuable:/ˈɪʃuːəbl/
adjective
可发行的；可提出的；可争论的
capable of being issued; suitable for discussion or debate
New shares are issuable to existing shareholders. 新股可向现有股东发行。
The question of funding is not issuable at this stage. 资金问题在这个阶段不值得争论。
synonyms: distributable, deliverable, available, valid
antonyms: unissuable, invalid, unavailable
词性: 形容词
