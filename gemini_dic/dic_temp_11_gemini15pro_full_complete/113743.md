macrography:/məˈkrɒɡrəfi/
n.|宏观摄影术；肉眼可见的书写|The study of, or a description of, objects large enough to be visible with the naked eye.|He specialized in macrography, capturing the intricate details of everyday objects.|近义词:macrophotography|反义词:micrography|词性:noun

Etymology:
From macro- (large) + -graphy (writing, description).  Ultimately from Greek makros (large) + graphein (to write).
