holing:/ˈhəʊlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhoʊlɪŋ/ (US)| v. | 打洞；进洞；藏匿 |  The act of making a hole or putting something into a hole.  Also, the act of hiding oneself. |  The golfer was holing a tricky putt.  高尔夫球手正在打一个棘手的短推。


The rabbits were holing up in their burrows.  兔子们藏在它们的洞穴里。|近义词：burrowing, digging, hiding, sheltering |反义词: exposing, uncovering |词性: 动词 (verb)
