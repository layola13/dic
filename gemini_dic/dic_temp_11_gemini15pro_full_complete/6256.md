amoebicide:/əˈmiːbɪsaɪd/| n.|阿米巴杀虫剂; 杀阿米巴剂|A substance that kills amoebas, a type of single-celled organism.|The doctor prescribed an amoebicide to treat the patient's amoebic dysentery.|近义词：antiamoebic|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially growth promoter for amoebas)|词性:noun

amoebicide:/əˈmiːbɪsaɪd/| adj.|杀阿米巴的|Having the property of killing amoebas.|The amoebicidal properties of the drug were tested in the laboratory.|近义词：antiamoebic|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially growth promoting for amoebas)|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From amoeba + -cide.  Amoeba comes from the Greek "amoibe," meaning "change," referring to the amoeba's constantly changing shape.  -cide comes from the Latin "cida," meaning "killer" or "a cutting."
