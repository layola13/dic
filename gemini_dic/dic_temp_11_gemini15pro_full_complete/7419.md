animgraph:/ˈænɪmɡræf/
noun
动画图
A visual representation of a character's animation logic, typically used in game development.  It allows developers to create complex animation behaviors by connecting nodes that represent different animation states, transitions, and blending.
The animgraph allowed the character to seamlessly transition between running, jumping, and attacking.  动画图允许角色在跑步、跳跃和攻击之间无缝切换。
animation blueprint, animation state machine
(No direct antonym)
