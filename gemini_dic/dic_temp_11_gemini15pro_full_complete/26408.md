bye-election:/ˌbaɪ ɪˈlɛkʃən/ | n. |补选|A special election held between regular elections to fill a vacancy, typically caused by the death or resignation of an elected official. |例句：The bye-election was called after the sitting MP resigned. 补选是因为现任议员辞职而举行的。

例句：There are two bye-elections taking place today. 今天有两个补选在进行。|近义词：special election, by-election |反义词:general election| noun
