conjecturally:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərəli/
adv.
推测地，猜想地
based on conjecture; by guessing.
The date of the manuscript can be conjecturally placed in the late 12th century. 这份手稿的日期可以推测为12世纪后期。
Synonyms: hypothetically, presumably, supposedly, putatively
Antonyms: certainly, definitely, undoubtedly, factually
词性: 副词
