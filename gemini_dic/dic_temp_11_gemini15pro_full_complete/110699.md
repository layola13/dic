linearization:/ˌlɪniərɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |线性化，线性化处理|The process of making something linear, often by approximation.  This involves finding a linear approximation of a function at a specific point. |The linearization of the system allowed for simplified calculations.  系统线性化后简化了计算。|近义词：linear approximation, approximation |反义词: nonlinearization |词性: noun
