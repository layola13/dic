hired:/ˈhaɪərd/| v. |雇佣的；租用的|engaged for service under a term of contract; rented for temporary use.|例句：She was hired as a consultant. 她被聘为顾问。|例句：We hired a car for the weekend. 我们租了一辆车过周末。|近义词：employed, rented, leased|反义词：fired, dismissed|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *hiren, from Old English *hȳrian, from Proto-Germanic *hūrijaną (“to hire, rent”). Cognate with West Frisian hiere (“to hire”), Dutch huren (“to rent”), German heuern (“to hire (farmhands)”).
