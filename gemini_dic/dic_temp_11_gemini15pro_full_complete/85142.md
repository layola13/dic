hardock:/ˈhɑːrdɒk/ (UK) /ˈhɑːrdɑːk/ (US)| n.| 硬毛蓟; 牛蒡 |A biennial European plant (Arctium minus) of the daisy family, with prickly heads of purplish flowers and large basal leaves.  The root is edible and has medicinal properties. It is related to, and easily confused with, burdock (Arctium lappa). |The hardock grows wild in fields and along roadsides.| 近义词: burdock| 反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant) | noun

hardock:/ˈhɑːrdɒk/ (UK) /ˈhɑːrdɑːk/ (US)| n.| 牛蒡根; 硬毛蓟根 |The root of the hardock plant. |Hardock root is used in some traditional herbal remedies.|近义词: burdock root| 反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant part) | noun
