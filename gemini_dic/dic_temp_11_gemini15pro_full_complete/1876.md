acrostic: /əˈkrɒstɪk/ /əˈkrɑːstɪk/ | n. adj. | 藏头诗；离合诗；以首字母组成的 | A poem, word puzzle, or other composition in which certain letters in each line form a word or words. |

Example 1: He wrote an acrostic poem for his mother's birthday.  他为他母亲的生日写了一首藏头诗。

Example 2:  The first letters of each line of the acrostic spelled out her name.  这首离合诗每行的第一个字母拼出了她的名字。


|近义词: abecedarius,  |反义词: N/A | 词性: noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *acrostichis, from Greek akrostikhis (sc. poiesis 'poetry'), from akrostikhos 'of or pertaining to the end of a line', from akron 'end, extremity' + stikhos 'row, line of verse'.
