japonism:/ʒəˈpɒnɪzəm/
n.
日本主义;
The adoption or imitation of Japanese motifs, styles, or techniques in Western art, especially in painting and the decorative arts.
The influence of Japanese art, fashion and aesthetics on Western culture.
The artist's work shows a strong influence of Japonism.  这位艺术家的作品展现了浓郁的日本主义影响。
Her paintings reflect the Japonism prevalent in Europe at the time. 她的画作反映了当时在欧洲盛行的日本主义。
synonyms: Japonisme
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider Occidentalism as a loose contrasting concept)
