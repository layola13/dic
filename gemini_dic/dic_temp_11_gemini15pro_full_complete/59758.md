economicus:/ˌɛkəˈnɒmɪkʊs/ | adj. | 经济人; 理性经济人 |  A hypothetical individual assumed to be perfectly rational and self-interested, making decisions solely to maximize their own utility. |  The theory of homo economicus assumes that people always act in their own best interest. 经济人理论假设人们总是按照自身最大利益行事。| Synonyms: rational agent, utility maximizer | Antonyms: altruistic individual | adjective
