misaddressing:/ˌmɪsəˈdrɛsɪŋ/
verb
错误地称呼；写错地址
The act of addressing someone or something incorrectly, either by using the wrong name, title, or address.
The letter was returned because it was misaddressed. 这封信被退回来了，因为地址写错了。
misdirecting, mislabeling
correctly addressing
v.
