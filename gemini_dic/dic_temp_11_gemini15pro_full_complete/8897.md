antigays:/ˌæntiˈɡeɪz/
|n.|反同性恋者|People who hold strong prejudices against homosexuals.  Those who discriminate against or oppose the rights of gay people.|Protesters called the group "antigays" due to their discriminatory rhetoric. 抗议者因该团体带有歧视性的言论而称他们为“反同性恋者”。|
|近义词：homophobes|反义词：gay rights supporters|词性:名词|

antigay:/ˌæntiˈɡeɪ/
|adj.|反同性恋的|Opposed to or prejudiced against homosexuality or homosexuals.|The politician faced criticism for his antigay views.  这位政治家因其反同性恋的观点而面临批评。|
|近义词：homophobic|反义词：pro-gay|词性:形容词|


词源:
anti- (prefix) + gay (adjective)

"anti-" comes from Greek "anti" meaning "against" or "opposite".
"gay" originally meant "happy" or "bright", but its meaning evolved in the 20th century to primarily refer to homosexual.
