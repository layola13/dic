decentralizing:/diːˈsɛntrəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
v. |去中心化|The process of distributing power or control away from a central authority. |The company is decentralizing its operations to give more autonomy to regional offices.| 近义词: distributing, dispersing, devolving | 反义词: centralizing, consolidating | 词性: 动词现在分词

Etymology:  From de- + centralize + -ing.  "Centralize" comes from "central" (from French and ultimately Latin "centralis") + "-ize".
