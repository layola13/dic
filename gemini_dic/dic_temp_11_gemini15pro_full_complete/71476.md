flamingoes:/fləˈmɪŋɡoʊz/ /fləˈmɪŋɡəʊz/
noun
火烈鸟（复数）
A type of wading bird with long legs, a long neck, and pink or reddish feathers.  They are known for their vibrant color and unique feeding habits.
Flamingos gather in large flocks near shallow water. 火烈鸟聚集在浅水区的大群中。
We saw a flamboyance of flamingoes at the zoo. 我们在动物园看到了一群火烈鸟。
近义词:flamingos
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，因为它是物种名称)
词性:n.
