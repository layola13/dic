inconveniently:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəntli/| adv.|不方便地，麻烦地|In a way that causes problems or difficulty.|例句：The meeting was inconveniently scheduled for early Monday morning. 会议安排在星期一早上很早，很不方便。|近义词：awkwardly, unfortunately, unsuitably|反义词: conveniently, suitably, opportunely|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + convenient + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Convenient" comes from the Latin "conveniens," present participle of "convenire" (to come together, be suitable).
