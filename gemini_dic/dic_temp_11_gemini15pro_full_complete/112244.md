looted:/ˈluːtɪd/
verb|掠夺的，被洗劫的|past participle of loot|The museum was looted during the war. 战争期间博物馆被洗劫一空。|pillaged, ransacked|protected, preserved
verb|洗劫，掠夺|to steal goods from (a place), typically during a war or riot.|Rioters looted shops and set fire to cars. 暴徒洗劫了商店并放火烧了汽车。|ransack, plunder|protect, defend

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi लूट (lūṭ) meaning "booty, plunder," ultimately from Sanskrit लोप्त्र (loptra) meaning "stolen goods."
