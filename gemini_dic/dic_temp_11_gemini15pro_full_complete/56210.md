dodman:/ˈdɒdmən/ | n. |蜗牛，行动迟缓的人|A snail; a person who moves slowly.|例句：He's such a dodman, always the last to arrive. 他真是个慢性子，总是最后一个到。

例句：The dodman slowly made its way across the leaf.  这只蜗牛慢慢地爬过叶子。|近义词：snail, slowpoke, laggard|反义词：speedster, fast worker|词性:名词

词源:  Likely from "dod" (possibly a dialectal variant of "dot" or "tod," meaning a small amount or piece) + "man," suggesting someone who moves in very small increments. The connection with snails is likely due to their slow pace.
