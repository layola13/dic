motleyer:/ˈmɒtliər (UK), ˈmɑːtliər (US)/
|n.|穿着杂色衣服的人；小丑；弄臣|A person who wears motley; a jester or fool.|The motleyer entertained the king with jokes and juggling. 弄臣用笑话和杂耍娱乐国王。|近义词：jester, fool, clown|反义词：king, monarch|词性:名词

motley:/ˈmɒtli (UK), ˈmɑːtli (US)/
|adj.|杂色的；混杂的|Made up of many different people or things.|A motley crew of musicians gathered for the jam session. 一群形形色色的音乐家聚集在一起进行即兴演奏。|近义词：diverse, varied, heterogeneous|反义词：uniform, homogeneous|词性:形容词

词源:  From Middle English *motlee, from Old French *motelé, past participle of *moteler (“to variegate”), from Old French motel (“speck, spot, particle of dust”), diminutive of mot (“mound, clod”).
