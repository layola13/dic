obituarily:/əˈbɪtʃuˌɛrɪli/| adv.|讣告地，在讣告中，关于讣告地|In a manner relating to or characteristic of an obituary; in a mournful or elegiac way.  |He spoke obituarily of the deceased author. 他以讣告的语气谈论了已故的作家。|近义词：mournfully, elegiacally|反义词:joyfully, cheerfully|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *obituarius (“relating to a death”), from Latin obitus (“death”), from obit- (“he, she, it has died”), perfect active stem of obīre (“to die, perish”), from ob- (“towards”) + īre (“to go”).
