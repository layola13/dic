daisy's:/ˈdeɪziz/| n.|雏菊的；戴西的|Belonging to or associated with a daisy or a person named Daisy.  |例句：Daisy's dress is yellow.  戴西的裙子是黄色的。|例句：He picked a bouquet of daisy's for his mother. 他为他的母亲摘了一束雏菊。|近义词：Daisy's (when referring to a person), floral (when referring to a flower)|反义词: N/A|词性:Possessive noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English dægeseage, meaning "day's eye," because the flower opens in the morning and closes at night.
