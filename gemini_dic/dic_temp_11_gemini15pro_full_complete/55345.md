dissympathy:/dɪsˈsɪmpəθi/
n.
缺乏同情; 冷漠; 格格不入
lack of sympathy; indifference; lack of rapport
Example:  He showed a marked dissympathy for the plight of the refugees. 他对难民的困境表现出明显的冷漠。

Example:  There's a growing dissympathy between the two political parties.  两个政党之间越来越格格不入。

近义词:antipathy, apathy, indifference, coldness
反义词:sympathy, empathy, compassion
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation, lack) + sympathy.
