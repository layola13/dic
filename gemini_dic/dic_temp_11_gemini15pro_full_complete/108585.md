leaching:/ˈliːtʃɪŋ/| n. |过滤，沥滤；淋溶；浸出|The process of removing a soluble substance from a solid by dissolving it in a liquid.  The process of percolating a liquid through something.|例句：The leaching of minerals from the soil can be caused by acid rain. 土壤中矿物质的淋溶可能是由酸雨造成的。

例句：The coffee maker works by leaching hot water through ground coffee beans. 咖啡机的工作原理是让热水渗滤过磨碎的咖啡豆。|近义词：extraction, percolation, filtration|反义词:absorption, deposition|词性:noun

leach:/liːtʃ/| v. |过滤，沥滤；淋溶；浸出|To remove a soluble substance from a solid by dissolving it in a liquid. To percolate a liquid through something.|例句：The rain leached the nutrients out of the soil. 雨水把土壤中的养分淋失了。

例句：Chemicals are leached from the plastic into the food. 化学物质从塑料中浸入食物。|近义词：extract, percolate, filter|反义词:absorb, deposit|词性:verb
