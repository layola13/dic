cancelled: /ˈkænsəld/ (US) /ˈkænsld/ (UK)| v. adj.|取消的，撤销的|To decide that an arranged event will not take place; to order that something already planned or started should be stopped.|例句：The flight was cancelled due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，航班被取消了。|近义词：abandoned, aborted, withdrawn, revoked|反义词：approved, confirmed, reinstated|词性: 动词/形容词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *cancellen*, from Old French *canceller*, from Latin *cancellare* "to make like a lattice," from *cancelli* "lattice." The original sense was to cross out writing with intersecting lines.
