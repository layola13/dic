munging:/ˈmʌndʒɪŋ/
n.
数据清洗，数据整理
The process of transforming or manipulating data in a haphazard or incorrect way, often due to a lack of understanding or care.  This can involve altering, deleting, or corrupting data, sometimes unintentionally.
The report was full of errors due to careless data munging.  由于粗心大意的数据清洗，报告中充满了错误。
data cleaning, data wrangling, data processing
data corruption, data loss
noun
