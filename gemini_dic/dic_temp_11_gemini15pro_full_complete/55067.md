disregard:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrd/| v. n.| 忽视，漠视；不理会|To ignore or pay no attention to something.|例句：He disregarded the doctor's advice and continued to smoke. 他无视医生的建议，继续吸烟。

例句：She completely disregarded my warnings. 她完全无视我的警告。


近义词：ignore, neglect, overlook, dismiss|反义词：heed, regard, consider, acknowledge|词性：动词


disregard:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrd/| n.| 忽视，漠视；不理会|The act of ignoring or paying no attention to something.|例句：His disregard for the rules resulted in his dismissal. 他对规则的漠视导致他被解雇。


例句：She treated my suggestions with complete disregard. 她完全无视我的建议。


近义词：neglect, indifference, contempt|反义词：regard, respect, consideration|词性：名词


词源：From the French *desregarder*, from *des-* (expressing negation) + *regarder* "to look at," from *re-* (intensive prefix) + *garder* "to guard, keep, watch."
