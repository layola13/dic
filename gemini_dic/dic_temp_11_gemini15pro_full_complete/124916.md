mudir:/muːˈdɪər/ (also spelled moodir)| n.| 校长；学院院长；(尤指伊斯兰教学校的)负责人|A head of a school, college, or other institution, especially an Islamic one. | The mudir addressed the students at the assembly. 校长在集会上向学生们讲话。| The mudir met with parents to discuss school improvements. 校长与家长们会面，讨论学校的改进。|近义词：principal, headmaster, director, head, leader|反义词: subordinate, follower|词性: noun
