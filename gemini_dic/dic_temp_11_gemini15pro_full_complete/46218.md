cysticule: /ˈsɪstɪˌkjuːl/
noun
包囊，小囊，(尤指鱼类)皮质小囊
A small cyst or cyst-like sac, especially in fishes, formed from a pouch of the skin.
The researchers discovered numerous cysticules embedded in the fish's skin. 研究人员在鱼的皮肤中发现了许多包囊。
