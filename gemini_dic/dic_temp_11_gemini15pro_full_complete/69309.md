febrility:/fɛˈbrɪlɪti/| n. |狂热，兴奋，发烧|A state of nervous excitement or mental activity.  A high degree of emotion or activity; feverish agitation.|The febrility of the political climate worried many citizens.  政治气候的狂热让许多公民感到担忧。|Near Synonyms: fervor, passion, intensity, excitement, agitation|Near Antonyms: calmness, tranquility, apathy, indifference|noun

febrile:/ˈfiːbraɪl/ /ˈfɛbraɪl/| adj. |发烧的，狂热的|Marked or caused by fever; feverish. Showing nervous excitement or energy.|His febrile mind raced with ideas. 他狂热的头脑充满了各种想法。|Near Synonyms: feverish, heated, fervent, passionate|Near Antonyms: calm, cool, collected, composed|adjective
