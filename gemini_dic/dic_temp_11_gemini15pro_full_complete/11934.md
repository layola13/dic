arsenious:/ɑːrˈsiːniəs/ |adj.|亚砷的；三价砷的|Containing or relating to arsenic with a valence of three.|The arsenious oxide is a highly toxic substance.  三氧化二砷是一种剧毒物质。|Synonyms: trivalent arsenic, arsenic(III)|Antonyms: arsenic, arsenic(V)|adjective

arsenious:/ɑːrˈsiːniəs/ |n.|亚砷酸|An acid formed from arsenic trioxide.|Arsenious acid is a weak acid. 亚砷酸是一种弱酸。|Synonyms: arsenic trioxide, white arsenic|Antonyms: arsenic acid|noun
