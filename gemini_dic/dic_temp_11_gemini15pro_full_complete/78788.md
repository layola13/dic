gillless:/ˈɡɪllɪs/| adj.|无鳃的，没有鳃的|Lacking gills; having no gills.  This describes organisms, typically aquatic animals, that do not possess gills for respiration.|Example: Some salamanders are gillless and breathe through their skin.  一些蝾螈没有鳃，通过皮肤呼吸。

Example:  The gillless tadpoles developed rapidly in the stagnant water.  无鳃的蝌蚪在死水中迅速发育。|近义词：abranchial|反义词:gilled|词性:形容词
