geomancers:/ˈdʒiːoʊˌmænsərz/
n.
风水师；地占师
A person who practices geomancy, a method of divination that interprets markings on the earth or by throwing sand.
The geomancers were consulted before the building was constructed.  风水师在建筑施工前进行了咨询。
The king sought the advice of his geomancers before making any important decisions.  国王在做出任何重要决定之前都会征求他的风水师的意见。

近义词: diviner, soothsayer, augur
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "skeptic" or "scientist")
词性: noun (plural)
