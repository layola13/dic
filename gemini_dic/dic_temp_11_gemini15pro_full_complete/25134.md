buckeye: /ˈbʌkˌaɪ/ | n. | 七叶树果实; 七叶树;  俄亥俄州人(昵称) | A small, shiny, dark brown nut with a lighter patch that resembles a deer's eye, produced by the buckeye tree. Also, a nickname for a resident of Ohio, where the buckeye tree is the state tree. |  The buckeye is the state tree of Ohio.  七叶树是俄亥俄州的州树。

The Ohio State Buckeyes won the football game. 俄亥俄州立七叶树队赢得了这场橄榄球赛。| 近义词: horse chestnut (for the nut) | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词
