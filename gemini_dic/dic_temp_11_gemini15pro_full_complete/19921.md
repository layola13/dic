biocentric:/ˌbaɪoʊˈsɛntrɪk/
adjective
以生物为中心的；生物中心论的
Considering all forms of life as having intrinsic value and deserving of moral consideration.  Focusing on the inherent value of living beings and ecosystems, regardless of their usefulness to humans.
Biocentric thinkers advocate for environmental protection.  生物中心论的思想家提倡环境保护。
A biocentric worldview emphasizes the interconnectedness of all living things.  生物中心的世界观强调所有生物的相互联系。
近义词:ecocentric, nature-centered
反义词:anthropocentric
词性:adjective
