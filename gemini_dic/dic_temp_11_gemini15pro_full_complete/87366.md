heroku: /həˈroʊkuː/
n.
Heroku | Heroku是一个平台即服务 (PaaS) 云平台，允许开发人员构建、运行和操作应用程序 entirely in the cloud. |  I deployed my web application to Heroku. 我把我的网络应用程序部署到Heroku。| 近义词:  Netlify, AWS Elastic Beanstalk, Google App Engine,  Azure App Service | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "on-premise server" or "self-hosting") | 词性: 名词

词源:  The name "Heroku" is a portmanteau of "hero" and "haiku".  It has no direct etymological roots in any other languages.
