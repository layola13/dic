anurous:/əˈnʊərəs/
adjective
无尾的；无尾目动物的
Relating to or denoting an order (Anura) of amphibians that comprises the frogs and toads, characterized by a short body, long hind legs adapted for leaping, webbed feet, and the absence of a tail in the adult.
Frogs and toads are anurous amphibians. 青蛙和蟾蜍是无尾两栖动物。
Synonyms: tailless, frog-like, toad-like
Antonyms: caudate (有尾的)
词性: adjective
