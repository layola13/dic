obnoxiously:/əbˈnɑːkʃəsli/ | adv. | 讨厌地，令人反感地 | in a very unpleasant or rude way that makes people annoyed | He behaved obnoxiously at the party, constantly interrupting people and bragging about himself.  他派对上的行为令人讨厌，不断打断别人讲话，自吹自擂。 | Example: The music was obnoxiously loud.  音乐吵得令人讨厌。| Synonyms: offensively, rudely, unpleasantly, disagreeably | Antonyms: pleasantly, politely, agreeably, considerately | 词性: 副词

obnoxious:/əbˈnɑːkʃəs/ | adj. | 讨厌的，令人反感的 | extremely unpleasant |  He's an obnoxious man who always makes rude comments. 他是个讨厌的家伙，总是说些粗鲁的话。 | Example: Her obnoxious laughter grated on my nerves. 她那令人讨厌的笑声让我神经紧张。 | Synonyms: offensive, unpleasant, disagreeable, irritating | Antonyms: pleasant, agreeable, delightful, charming | 词性: 形容词
