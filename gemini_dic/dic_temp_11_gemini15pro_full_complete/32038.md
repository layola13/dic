cherishment:/ˈtʃɛrɪʃmənt/| n. |珍爱，爱护，珍惜|The cherishment of traditional values is important.  珍视传统价值观很重要。|例句：Her cherishment of their friendship was evident. 她对他们友谊的珍视是显而易见的。  The museum is dedicated to the cherishment and preservation of local history.  这家博物馆致力于珍视和保护当地历史。|近义词：treasure, value, adore, appreciate, prize|反义词：neglect, disregard, contempt|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "cherish," which comes from the Old French "cherir" (to hold dear), ultimately from the Latin "carus" (dear).
