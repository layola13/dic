noncorrespondents:/ˌnɒnkɒrɪˈspɒndənts/
n.
未通讯者；未通信者；不符者
People or things that do not correspond or match.
例句：The noncorrespondents in the database were flagged for review. 数据库中不符的条目被标记出来以供审查。

例句：The survey revealed a significant number of noncorrespondents between the two datasets.  调查显示，这两个数据集之间存在大量不匹配项。
近义词：non-matches, discrepancies, disparities
反义词：correspondents, matches
词性：名词
