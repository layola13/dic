overconsolidated:/ˌoʊvərˈkənˈsɑːlɪdeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度固结的
A soil is overconsolidated if it has been subjected to a greater effective stress in the past than it is currently experiencing.  This typically occurs when overlying material has been removed by erosion or other geological processes.
The overconsolidated clay exhibited high shear strength.  过度固结的粘土表现出高抗剪强度。

The engineer determined that the site contained overconsolidated soil. 工程师确定该场地包含过度固结的土壤。

近义词: preconsolidated, overcompacted
反义词: normally consolidated, underconsolidated
词性: adjective
