libeling: /ˈlaɪbəlɪŋ/
verb
诽谤，中伤
The act of making a false and damaging statement about someone.
He is libeling me by saying I stole the money. 他说我偷了钱是在诽谤我。

libeling: /ˈlaɪbəlɪŋ/
gerund
诽谤，中伤
The act of making a false and damaging statement about someone.
Libeling someone can have serious consequences. 诽谤别人可能会造成严重后果。


Etymology:
From Middle English *libelen, from Old French libeler (“to write a petition, to satirize”),  from Late Latin libellus (“little book, pamphlet”), diminutive of Latin liber (“book”).

Synonyms:
defaming, slandering, vilifying, calumniating, aspersing, smearing, maligning, traducing, denigrating

Antonyms:
praising, commending, lauding, extolling, eulogizing
