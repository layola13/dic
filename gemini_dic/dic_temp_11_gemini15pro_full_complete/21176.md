blobby:/ˈblɒbi/ (UK) /ˈblɑːbi/ (US)| adj.|一团团的，不成形的，粘糊糊的|Having a rounded, irregular shape; amorphous and somewhat sticky or viscous.|The monster in the movie was a huge, blobby mass of slime.  电影里的怪物是一大团粘糊糊的软泥。|The pudding was a bit too blobby.  这布丁有点太粘稠了。|近义词：shapeless, amorphous, formless, globular, lumpy|反义词：sharp, defined, distinct|词性:形容词

词源:  From blob + -y.  "Blob" itself likely originates from imitative or expressive language, suggesting the sound or appearance of a drop of something thick.
