let-outs:/ˈlɛtˌaʊts/
noun
解脱；逃避条款；出租的房屋
A means of escape or release from a difficult situation or obligation.  A clause in a contract allowing for termination under specified conditions.  Houses or apartments available for rent.
The contract includes a let-out clause in case of unforeseen circumstances.  合同中包含一个在不可预见情况下允许终止的条款。
There are a few let-outs available for short-term rental in the area. 该地区有一些短期出租的房屋。
Synonyms: escape clauses, loopholes, exits, rental properties
Antonyms: commitments, obligations, responsibilities
n.
