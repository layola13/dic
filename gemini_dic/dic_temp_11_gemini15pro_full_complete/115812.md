marked-down:/ˌmɑːrkt ˈdaʊn/ | adj. | 降价的，减价的 | Reduced in price. | The store had a sale on marked-down items.  这家商店对降价商品进行促销。|  reduced, discounted, on sale | inflated, marked-up | adjective

marked-down:/ˌmɑːrkt ˈdaʊn/ | adj. | 价值被降低的 | Reduced in value or worth. |  The company's stock was marked-down after the scandal.  丑闻曝光后，该公司的股票贬值了。| devalued, depreciated | appreciated, increased | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "mark" (to make a visible impression) + "down" (indicating a decrease). The combination signifies a reduction in the marked price.
