interplaits:/ˌɪntərˈpleɪts/ | v. | 交织，缠绕 | To weave or twine together; interlace. |  She interplaits the strands of yarn to create a complex pattern. 她交织纱线，创造出复杂的图案。

The artist interplaits different colors of light in his installation. 艺术家在他的装置中交织了不同颜色的光。 | 近义词：interweave, intertwine, braid, plait | 反义词：separate, untangle, unravel | 词性：动词
