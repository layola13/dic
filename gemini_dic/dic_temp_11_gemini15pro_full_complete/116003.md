marshalsea:/ˈmɑːrʃəlsiː/
n.
监狱，负债人监狱
a prison, especially one for debtors
Dickens's father was imprisoned in the Marshalsea for debt.  狄更斯(Charles Dickens)的父亲因负债而被关押在马夏尔西监狱。
prison, jail, gaol
(无常用反义词，监狱的反义词可以是"freedom"自由)
noun
