词源：From Middle English *neighinge, *neying, present participle of neighen, neighen (“to neigh”), from Old English hnagan, hnægan (“to neigh”). Cognate with West Frisian neie (“to neigh”), Dutch neighen (“to neigh”), German wiehern (“to neigh”).
