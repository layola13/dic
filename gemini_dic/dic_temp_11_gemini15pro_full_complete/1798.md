acridest:/ˈækrɪdɪst/| adj.|最辛辣的，最刺激的，最刻薄的|superlative form of acrid;  most sharp or biting to the taste or smell;  most bitter and caustic|The acridest fumes rose from the burning tires.  燃烧的轮胎冒出最刺鼻的浓烟。|The critic's acridest remarks were directed at the film's weak plot.  这位评论家最尖刻的评论是针对这部电影的薄弱情节。|近义词：bitterest, harshest, sharpest, most caustic|反义词: mildest, sweetest, blandest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *ācer* (“sharp, pungent, keen”) + *-idus* (suffix forming adjectives).
