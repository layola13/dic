cod:/kɒd/ (British) /kɑːd/ (American)| n.|鳕鱼|A large marine fish with a small barbel on the chin, important as a food fish.|We had cod and chips for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了炸鱼薯条。|近义词：haddock, pollock|反义词: None|noun

cod:/kɒd/ (British) /kɑːd/ (American)| v. (informal, dated)|欺骗，戏弄|To deceive or trick someone.|He was codding me the whole time. 他一直都在骗我。|近义词：deceive, trick, hoax|反义词: be honest, be truthful|verb
