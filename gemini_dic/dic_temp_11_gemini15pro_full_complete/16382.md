barbie-doll: /ˈbɑːrbi dɒl/ (US) /ˈbɑːbi dɒl/ (UK) | n. | 芭比娃娃 | A fashion doll manufactured by Mattel, Inc. and launched in March 1959. Barbie has been a prominent part of the toy fashion doll market for over sixty years and has been the subject of numerous controversies and lawsuits, often involving parodies of the doll and her lifestyle.
