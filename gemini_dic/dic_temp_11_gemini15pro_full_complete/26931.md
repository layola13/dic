calando:/kəˈlɑːndoʊ/ /kəˈlændoʊ/ | adv. adj.| 渐弱 | Gradually softer and slower.  Indicates a decrease in both volume and tempo. | The piece ends with a beautiful calando passage.  这首曲子以一段优美的渐弱乐段结束。

The conductor signaled a calando for the final bars. 指挥示意最后几小节渐弱。| 近义词： diminuendo, decrescendo | 反义词: crescendo | 词性: 副词，形容词

词源: Italian, present participle of calare 'to lower,' from Latin chalare.
