enring:/ɪnˈrɪŋ/| vt.|包围，环绕|To surround or enclose with a ring.  The mountains enring the valley.|例句：The soldiers enringed the enemy camp. 士兵们包围了敌营。

例句：A high wall enrings the entire estate.  一堵高墙环绕着整个庄园。|近义词：encircle, surround, encompass, enclose|反义词: liberate, free|词性:动词

词源: From en- + ring (v.).  "En-" is a prefix meaning "to put into or onto," or "to cause to be."  "Ring" as a verb means to surround.  Therefore, "enring" literally means to cause something to be surrounded by a ring.
