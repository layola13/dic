make a spectacle of oneself: /meɪk ə ˈspɛktəkəl əv wʌnˈsɛlf/ | idiom | 出丑；丢人现眼 | To behave in a foolish or embarrassing way that attracts a lot of attention.  |  He made a spectacle of himself at the party by singing karaoke off-key. 他在派对上唱歌跑调，出了洋相。 | synonyms: embarrass oneself, humiliate oneself, make a fool of oneself | antonyms: behave oneself, act with decorum, maintain one's composure | idiom
