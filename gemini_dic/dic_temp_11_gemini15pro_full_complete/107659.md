languishing:/ˈlæŋɡwɪʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 衰弱的，萎靡不振的，苦思的 | failing to make progress or be successful; losing strength or vitality |  The economy has been languishing in recent months. 近几个月来，经济一直萎靡不振。|  He has been languishing in jail for years. 他多年来一直被关在监狱里苦熬。 | synonyms: declining, deteriorating, weakening, fading, failing, stagnating, atrophying, pining, yearning | antonyms: thriving, flourishing, prospering | adjective
