enlarging: /ɪnˈlɑːrdʒɪŋ/ /ɛnˈlɑːrdʒɪŋ/
verb
扩大；放大；扩展
making or becoming larger.
例句：They are enlarging their house by adding a new wing. 他们在扩建他们的房子，增加了一个新的侧翼。

例句：The company is enlarging its market share. 这家公司正在扩大其市场份额。

近义词：expanding, increasing, augmenting, amplifying
反义词：shrinking, reducing, diminishing, decreasing
词性：动词

enlarging: /ɪnˈlɑːrdʒɪŋ/ /ɛnˈlɑːrdʒɪŋ/
adjective
放大的；扩大的
making something larger.
例句：The enlarging lens made the image appear bigger. 放大镜头使图像看起来更大。

例句：The enlarging market provides more opportunities. 不断扩大的市场提供了更多的机会。

近义词：expanding, increasing, growing
反义词：shrinking, reducing, diminishing
词性：形容词 (通常作为现在分词使用)
