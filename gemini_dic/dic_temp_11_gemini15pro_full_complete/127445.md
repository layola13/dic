nebulae:/ˈnɛbjʊliː/
n.
星云（复数）
A cloud of gas and dust in outer space, visible in the night sky either as an indistinct bright patch or as a dark silhouette against other luminous matter.
The Horsehead Nebula is a dark nebula in the constellation Orion.  马头星云是猎户座中的一个暗星云。
Near synonyms: galaxy, star cluster
Antonyms: void (although not a direct opposite, it represents the absence of matter)
