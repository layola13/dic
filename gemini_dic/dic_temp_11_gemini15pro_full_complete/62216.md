PHRASE->
increase in = ...方面的增加 (e.g., an increase in sales 销售额的增加)
increase by = 增加... (e.g., increase by 10% 增加10%)
increase to = 增加到... (e.g., increase to 100 增加到100)
increase with = 随着...增加 (e.g., increase with age 随着年龄增长)
on the increase = 正在增加 (e.g., Crime is on the increase. 犯罪正在增加。)
