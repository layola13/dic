biotapestry:/ˌbaɪoʊˈtæpɪstri/ | n. | 生物挂毯; 生物多样性图谱 | A representation of the interconnectedness of life, often visualized as a complex web or tapestry. It highlights the relationships between organisms and their environment, emphasizing biodiversity and ecological processes. |  The Amazon rainforest is a complex biotapestry of interacting species. 亚马逊雨林是一个相互作用的物种组成的复杂生物挂毯。| synonyms: ecosystem, web of life, biodiversity | antonyms: monoculture | noun
