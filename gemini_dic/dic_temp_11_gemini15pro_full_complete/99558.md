interhyal: /ˌɪntərˈhaɪəl/ | adj. | 透明质酸间的；透明质酸内的 | Situated between layers of hyaluronan or hyaluronic acid. | The interhyal region of cartilage contains less collagen than the territorial matrix. 软骨的透明质酸间区域比区域基质含有更少的胶原蛋白。 |  近义词: interhyaluronic | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
inter- (Latin, prefix meaning "between") + hyal- (from Greek hyalos, meaning "glass," referring to the glassy appearance of hyaluronic acid)
