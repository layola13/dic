gateways:/ˈɡeɪtweɪz/
n.
通道，途径，网关
Points of entry or access to something, often a system, place, or opportunity.
These online courses are gateways to new careers. 这些在线课程是通往新职业的途径。
The city is a gateway to the rest of the country. 这座城市是通往该国其他地区的通道。
portals, entrances, access points
barriers, obstacles
noun
