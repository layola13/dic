lamely:/ˈleɪmli/| adv.|无力地，蹩脚地，站不住脚地|in a way that shows weakness or lack of skill; unconvincingly|He argued lamely that he had been too busy to call. 他无力地辩解说他太忙了，没时间打电话。|近义词：weakly, feebly, poorly, inadequately, unconvincingly|反义词：strongly, effectively, convincingly|词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *lamely, from Old English *lamelice (“lamely”), equivalent to lame +‎ -ly.
