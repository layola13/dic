erythrochroic:/ɪˌrɪθrəˈkrəʊɪk/ | adj. |  红变色的，红变应激的 | Relating to or exhibiting erythrochroism, a change of color (typically to red) in response to a stimulus such as light or temperature. |  This species of lizard is erythrochroic, turning a vibrant red when threatened.  这种蜥蜴会红变，受到威胁时会变成鲜红色。 |  Synonyms: color-changing, thermochromic (if related to temperature), photochromic (if related to light) | Antonyms:  monochromatic | adjective

erythrochroism:/ɪˌrɪθrəˈkrəʊɪzəm/ | n. | 红变色，红变应激 | The property of some substances to change color (typically to red) in response to a stimulus such as light or temperature. | The erythrochroism of this mineral makes it a popular collector's item.  这种矿物的红变色特性使其成为热门的收藏品。 | Synonyms: color change, thermochromism (if related to temperature), photochromism (if related to light) | Antonyms: monochromatism | noun
