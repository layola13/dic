colossal:/kəˈlɒsəl/ (UK) /kəˈlɑːsəl/ (US)| adj.|巨大的，庞大的|Extremely large or great.|例句：The statue was colossal in size. 这座雕像尺寸巨大。|例句：They faced colossal challenges in rebuilding the city. 他们在重建城市时面临着巨大的挑战。|近义词：enormous, gigantic, huge, immense, massive, monumental, tremendous|反义词：tiny, small, minuscule|词性:形容词

colossal:/kəˈlɒsəl/ (UK) /kəˈlɑːsəl/ (US)| n.|巨大之物|Something colossal; a thing of colossal size.|例句：(Rarely used as a noun) The colossal was too large to comprehend.  这巨大的东西太大了，无法理解。|例句：(Rarely used as a noun) The pyramids are colossals of ancient engineering. 金字塔是古代工程的巨大之物。|近义词：giant, behemoth, leviathan|反义词：dwarf, miniature|词性:名词 (rarely used)
