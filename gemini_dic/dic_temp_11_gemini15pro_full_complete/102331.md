ivs: /ˈaɪˌviːˈɛs/ | n. | 静脉输液;  intravenous infusions (的缩写) | Intravenous infusions are commonly used in hospitals to administer fluids, medications, and nutrients directly into a patient's bloodstream. 静脉输液通常用于医院，将液体、药物和营养物质直接注入患者的血液。| Example: The patient was on IVs for three days.  病人输液三天了。| Synonym: drips, infusions | Antonym: N/A | Noun
