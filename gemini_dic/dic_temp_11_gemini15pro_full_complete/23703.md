brasse: /bræs/ (No standard English pronunciation, primarily used in French)| n.| 蛙泳 |A swimming stroke in which the arms are extended forward and then swept back in a circular motion while the legs are drawn up and kicked out.  Primarily used in French, less common in English. | Example: Il nage la brasse. (He swims the breaststroke.) | 近义词: breaststroke | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider other swimming strokes like crawl, backstroke) | 词性: noun
