dividedly:/dɪˈvaɪdɪdli/
adv.
分裂地，不一致地
in a divided or disunited manner; with disagreement or disunity.
The committee voted dividedly on the proposal. 委员会在提案上投票意见不一。
近义词: disunitedly, discordantly, contentiously
反义词: unanimously, harmoniously, concordantly
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From divide + -ed + -ly.  "Divide" comes from the Latin "dividere," meaning "to force apart, distribute."
