absolutist:/ˈæbsəluːtɪst/| n. adj.|专制主义者；绝对主义的|A person who holds absolute principles in political, philosophical, or religious matters.  A supporter of a political system in which a ruler or government has unlimited power.|The king was an absolutist, believing he had a divine right to rule. 国王是一个专制主义者，相信他有神圣的统治权。|近义词：autocrat, dictator, tyrant, despot|反义词：democrat, liberal|词性: noun, adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin *absolūtista*, from Latin absolūtus (“absolute”) + -ista.
