convocation:/ˌkɒnvəˈkeɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈkeɪʃn/
n.
集会，召集；会议；(大学的)毕业典礼
a large formal assembly of people, or the act of assembling a large formal group of people, especially for a particular purpose; a ceremony at a university or college at which degrees or diplomas are officially given to students who have completed their courses.
The university convocation was held in the grand hall. 大学毕业典礼在大礼堂举行。
The president called for a convocation of parliament. 总统号召召开议会。
近义词：assembly, gathering, meeting, ceremony, commencement
反义词：dispersal, adjournment
词性：名词

convoke:/kənˈvəʊk/ /kənˈvoʊk/
vt.
召集，召开(会议)
to call a group of people to a formal meeting
The chairman convoked the meeting. 主席召开了会议。
The council has power to convoke a meeting. 理事会有权召开会议。
近义词：assemble, convene, summon
反义词：dismiss, adjourn
词性：动词
