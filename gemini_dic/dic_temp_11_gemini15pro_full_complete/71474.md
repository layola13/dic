flamingly:/ˈfleɪmɪŋli/| adv.|炽烈地，燃烧般地，鲜艳地|In a flaming, fiery, or intensely emotional manner.  Describes something done with great passion, intensity, or striking visibility.|例句：She spoke flamingly about the injustices she had suffered. 她炽烈地讲述了她所遭受的不公正待遇。

例句：The sunset painted the sky flamingly red. 日落将天空染成了火红色。|近义词：passionately, intensely, vividly, brightly, fiercely|反义词：mildly, calmly, dully|词性:副词

Etymology:
From flame + -ing + -ly.  "Flame" comes from the Old French "flambe," ultimately from the Latin "flamma."
