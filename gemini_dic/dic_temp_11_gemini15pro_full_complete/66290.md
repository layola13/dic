excoriate: /ɪkˈskɔːrieɪt/ (US), /ɛkˈskɔːrɪeɪt/ (UK)| vt.| 谴责，严厉批评；剥皮，擦破皮肤|To criticize severely and harshly; to remove the skin or surface of something by abrasion or scraping.|The newspaper excoriated the mayor for his mishandling of the budget. 这家报纸严厉批评了市长对预算的处理不当。

The hiker's knees were excoriated after falling on the rough trail.  徒步旅行者的膝盖在崎岖的小路上摔倒后被擦破了皮。|近义词：denounce, condemn, lambaste, censure, criticize, abrade, chafe|反义词：praise, commend, laud, heal, soothe|词性: 动词
