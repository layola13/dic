ETYMOLOGY->
From French *balustrade*, from Italian *balaustrata*, from *balaustro* "baluster," of uncertain origin, perhaps from Vulgar Latin *balaustium* "flower of the wild pomegranate," from Greek *balaústion* (same meaning), from the resemblance of the swelling form of the baluster to the unopened flower.
