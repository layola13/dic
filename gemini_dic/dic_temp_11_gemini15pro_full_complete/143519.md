outgreened:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnd/
verb
胜过（在环保方面）; 比...更环保
To surpass someone in terms of environmental consciousness or practices.
Example:  The company outgreened its competitors by implementing a zero-waste policy.  这家公司通过实施零浪费政策，在环保方面胜过了竞争对手。

Example:  She tried to outgreen her neighbors by installing solar panels and composting. 她试图通过安装太阳能电池板和堆肥来比邻居更环保。
synonym:outshine, surpass, exceed
antonym:lag behind, fall behind
词性:v.
