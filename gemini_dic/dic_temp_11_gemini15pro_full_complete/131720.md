nonconferences:/nɒnˈkɒnfərənsɪz/ | n. | 非会议，(通常指)参与者主导的会议 |  Informal meetings or events, typically participant-led, focused on collaboration, sharing, and community building, often contrasting with traditional, top-down conferences. |  Example:  The unconference format allowed for more organic discussions. 非会议的形式允许更多有机的讨论。

Example:  Many nonconferences use open space technology to set the agenda. 许多非会议使用开放空间技术来设置议程。 | 近义词: unconferences, workshops, meetups | 反义词: conferences, lectures, symposiums | 词性: 名词(复数)
