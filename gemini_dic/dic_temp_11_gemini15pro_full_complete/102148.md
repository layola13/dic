issuably:/ˈɪʃuəbli/
adverb|可發佈地，可爭論地|In a manner that can be issued or contested.|The matter is issuably before the court. 这件事在法庭上是可以争论的。|Synonyms: disputably, arguably, debatably|Antonyms: indisputably, undoubtedly, incontrovertibly|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From issue (verb) + -ably (suffix).  "Issue" comes from Old French "issue" (exit, outcome), from Latin "exire" (to go out). "-ably" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives, meaning "in a manner that can be...".
