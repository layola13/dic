atheological:/ˌeɪˌθiːəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective

Not pertaining to or concerned with theology.
His atheological views were not popular in the small town. 他在小镇上的无神论观点并不受欢迎。
Some scientists hold atheological perspectives. 一些科学家持有无神论的观点。

反义词:theological, religious
