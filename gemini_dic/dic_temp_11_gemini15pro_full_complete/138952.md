nuzzle: /ˈnʌzəl/ (US), /ˈnʌzl/ (UK) | v. | 偎依；用鼻子轻触；紧贴 | To rub or push gently with the nose or face, especially as a sign of affection. | The cat nuzzled against my leg.  猫在我的腿上蹭来蹭去。|  The child nuzzled his head into his mother's shoulder. 孩子把头偎依在母亲的肩膀上。 | synonyms: cuddle, snuggle, nestle | antonyms: detach, separate | verb
