minimalism:/ˈmɪnɪməlɪzəm/| n. |极简主义|A style or technique (as in music, architecture, or literature) that is characterized by extreme spareness and simplicity.|She adopted minimalism in her home decor, getting rid of everything but the essentials. 她在家居装饰中采用了极简主义，除了必需品之外，其他东西都处理掉了。|近义词：simplicity, austerity|反义词：maximalism, extravagance|词性:noun
Etymology:  From minimal + -ism.  "Minimal" traces back through French to Latin "minimus" (least).  "-ism" is a suffix indicating a doctrine, principle, or practice.
