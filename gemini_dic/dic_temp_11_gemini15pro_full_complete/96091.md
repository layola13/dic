indefensible:/ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsəbl/ | adj. | 站不住脚的，无法辩护的 | Not justifiable; unable to be defended or excused. |  His actions were completely indefensible. 他的行为完全无法辩护。|  The company's decision to pollute the river was indefensible.  这家公司污染河流的决定是站不住脚的。| 近义词: unjustifiable, inexcusable, untenable | 反义词: justifiable, defensible, excusable | 词性: adjective

indefensible:/ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsəbl/ | adj. | 易受攻击的，无防御能力的 | Vulnerable to attack; lacking defenses.  |  Their position on the hill was indefensible. 他们在山上的位置很容易受到攻击。| The city's indefensible walls made it an easy target. 这座城市毫无防御能力的城墙使它成为一个容易攻击的目标。 | 近义词: vulnerable, exposed, unprotected | 反义词: protected, secure, fortified | 词性: adjective
