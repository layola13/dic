infrastructurability:/ˌɪnfrəˈstrʌktʃərəˈbɪləti/
n.
可基础设施化性；基础设施构建能力
The capacity of something to be developed with infrastructure.  The ease or difficulty with which infrastructure can be built or established in a particular area or for a particular project.
The infrastructurability of the remote region posed a significant challenge to the development project.  这个偏远地区的基础设施建设能力对发展项目构成了重大挑战。
The government is assessing the infrastructurability of the proposed high-speed rail network. 政府正在评估拟建高铁网络的基础设施建设能力。
buildability, constructability, feasibility
unfeasibility, impracticability
noun
