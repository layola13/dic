clonked:/klɒŋkt/ /klɑːŋkt/ | v. |发出沉闷的撞击声；（非正式）撞坏，弄坏；（非正式）打，击 | To hit something, especially the head, making a dull sound; (informal) to break or damage something; (informal) to hit or strike. | I clonked my head on the low doorway. 我把头撞到低矮的门框上了。| 近义词：bang, bash, hit, strike, thump | 反义词: repair, fix | 词性: 动词

clonk:/klɒŋk/ /klɑːŋkt/ | n. |沉闷的撞击声 | A dull, heavy sound, as of a blow. | There was a loud clonk as the metal pipe hit the floor. 金属管撞到地板上发出一声巨响。 | 近义词：bang, thud, thump | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑silence安静) | 词性: 名词
