civilist:/səˈvɪliən/| n.|平民，百姓|A person not in the armed services or the police force.|例句：Many civilians were killed in the bombing. 很多平民在轰炸中丧生。|例句：The soldiers were ordered not to harm any civilians. 士兵们被命令不要伤害任何平民。|近义词：noncombatant, citizen|反义词：soldier, military personnel|词性:noun
Etymology->
From Middle French *civiliste*, from Late Latin *civilis* (civil). Ultimately from Latin *civis* (citizen).
