meteoric:/ˌmiːtiˈɒrɪk/ /ˌmiːtiˈɔːrɪk/| adj.|流星的；迅疾的；昙花一现的|Relating to a meteor; resembling a meteor in speed or brilliance; transiently brilliant or spectacular.|Her meteoric rise to fame surprised everyone. 她迅速成名让每个人都感到惊讶。|Synonyms: rapid, swift, spectacular, dazzling, fleeting, ephemeral|Antonyms: slow, gradual, steady, enduring, persistent|adjective
