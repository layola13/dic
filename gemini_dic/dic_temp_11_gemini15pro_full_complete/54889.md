词源:
From Latin despondēre (“to lose heart, give up hope”), from de- (“down, away from”) + spondere (“to promise, engage oneself”).


注意事项:
Dispond is an intransitive verb, meaning it cannot take a direct object.  You cannot "dispond something." You can, however, "dispond over something."
