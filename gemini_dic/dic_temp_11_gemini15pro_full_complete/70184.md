fibrillose:/ˈfaɪbrɪləʊs/ (US) /ˈfɪbrɪləs/ (UK)| adj.|纤维状的，纤维的|Composed of or resembling small fibers or fibrils.  Characterized by a fibrous structure or appearance.|The mushroom had a fibrillose cap.  这种蘑菇的菌盖呈纤维状。|Synonyms: fibrous, filamentous, stringy, thready|Antonyms: smooth, non-fibrous|adjective

fibrous:/ˈfaɪbrəs/ (US) /ˈfaɪbrəs/ (UK)| adj.|纤维的，纤维状的|Containing or consisting of fibers.|Celery is a fibrous vegetable.  芹菜是一种富含纤维的蔬菜。|Synonyms: stringy, filamentous, fibrillose, thready|Antonyms: smooth, non-fibrous|adjective
