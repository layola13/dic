nonenumerated:/nɒnɪˈnjuːməreɪtɪd/
adjective
未列举的；未计数的
not listed or counted among others
The Constitution protects numerous nonenumerated rights. 宪法保护许多未列举的权利。
Synonyms: unlisted, unspecified, unmentioned
Antonyms: listed, enumerated, specified
词性: 形容词
