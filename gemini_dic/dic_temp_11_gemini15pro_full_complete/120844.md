misbalancing:/ˌmɪsˈbælənsɪŋ/
verb
使失衡，使失去平衡
The act of causing something to be unbalanced or out of equilibrium.
The misbalancing of the ecosystem caused by pollution. 污染导致生态系统失衡。
Overfishing is misbalancing the marine ecosystem. 过度捕捞正在破坏海洋生态系统的平衡。
Synonyms: destabilizing, upsetting, disrupting
Antonyms: balancing, stabilizing, equilibrating
