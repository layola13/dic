left's:/lɛfts/ | contraction | 让我们 | Contraction of "let us," used to make a suggestion or proposal. | Let's go to the movies tonight. 今晚我们去看电影吧。| Let's grab some dinner. 我们去吃点晚饭吧。| Synonyms: let us | Antonyms: N/A | contraction


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Let's eat dinner together. 我们一起吃晚饭吧。
Let's start the meeting now.  我们现在开始开会吧。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Let's not be talking about this now. 我们现在先别谈这个了。
Let's be having fun, not arguing. 我们应该开开心心地玩，而不是争吵。  (This usage is less common and often implies a playful or slightly sarcastic tone.)

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Let's have finished this project by Friday. 我们争取在星期五之前完成这个项目吧。(Expresses a desired outcome by a specific time)
Let's have cleared up this misunderstanding. 我们把这个误会解开吧。（Expresses a desired state to have been achieved)

PHRASE->

Let's go = 我们走吧
Let's see = 让我们看看/想想
Let's do it = 我们开始吧/就这么办吧
Let's not = 我们还是别...吧
Let's hope = 希望如此/但愿如此


词源： "Let's" is a contraction of "let us." "Let" comes from the Old English "lætan," meaning "to allow" or "to cause."  The use of "let us" in the sense of a proposal or suggestion developed over time.
