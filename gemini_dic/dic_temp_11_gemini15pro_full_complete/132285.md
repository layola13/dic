nondefensibility:/ˌnɒndɪˌfɛnsɪˈbɪlɪti/ (British) /ˌnɑːndɪˌfɛnsɪˈbɪləti/ (American)| n. |不可辩护性，无法辩护|The state or quality of being unable to be defended or justified.|His actions demonstrated the utter nondefensibility of his position. 他的行为完全表明了他的立场的不可辩护性。|Synonyms: indefensibility, unjustifiability, inexcusability|Antonyms: defensibility, justifiability, excusability|noun

nondefensible:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɛnsɪbl/ (British) /ˌnɑːndɪˈfɛnsəbl/ (American)| adj. |不可辩护的，无法辩护的|Not able to be defended or justified.|His argument was completely nondefensible. 他的论点完全站不住脚。|Synonyms: indefensible, unjustifiable, inexcusable|Antonyms: defensible, justifiable, excusable|adjective
