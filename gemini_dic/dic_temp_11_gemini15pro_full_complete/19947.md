biodiesel:/ˈbaɪoʊˌdiːzəl/| n. |生物柴油|A fuel made from vegetable oils, animal fats, or recycled restaurant grease. It can be used in diesel engines.|Many buses now run on biodiesel. 许多公共汽车现在使用生物柴油。|Biodiesel is a renewable fuel that can be used in standard diesel engines. 生物柴油是一种可再生燃料，可以在标准柴油发动机中使用。|Synonyms: biofuel, renewable diesel|Antonyms: petroleum diesel|noun
