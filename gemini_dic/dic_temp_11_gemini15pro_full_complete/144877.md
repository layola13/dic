over-represent:/ˌoʊvərˌrɛprɪˈzɛnt/ |v.|过度代表；人数过多；比例过高|To represent disproportionately; to have a greater representation in a group or sample than in the population or whole from which it is taken.|Women are over-represented in caring professions. 女性在护理行业中人数过多。|Synonyms: overstate, exaggerate, inflate|Antonyms: underrepresent, understate, downplay|verb
