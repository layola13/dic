decameric:/ˌdekəˈmɛrɪk/
adjective
十聚体的，由十个单体组成的
Composed of ten monomers.
A decameric protein complex is formed by the association of ten protein subunits.  十个蛋白质亚基结合形成十聚体蛋白复合物。
The virus has a decameric capsid structure.  这种病毒具有十聚体衣壳结构。
Synonyms:  ten-membered, decuple
Antonyms: monomeric
词性: 形容词
