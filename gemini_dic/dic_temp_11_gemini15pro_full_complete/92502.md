hypothesises:/haɪˈpɒθɪsaɪzɪz/ | v. | 假设，假定 |  To suggest a possible explanation for something, but without knowing whether it is correct. | Scientists hypothesise that the universe began with a Big Bang. 科学家们假设宇宙始于一次大爆炸。|  The police hypothesise that the robbers escaped through the back door. 警方推测劫匪是从后门逃跑的。| synonyms: theorize, postulate, conjecture, speculate | antonyms: prove, demonstrate | verb

hypotheses:/haɪˈpɒθɪsiːz/ | n. | 假设，假说 | A proposed explanation for something that can be tested. | Several hypotheses have been put forward to explain the extinction of the dinosaurs.  已经提出了几种假说来解释恐龙的灭绝。 |  Her hypothesis was that stress levels would increase with workload. 她的假设是压力水平会随着工作量的增加而增加。 | synonyms: theory, supposition, assumption, premise | antonyms: fact, proof | noun
