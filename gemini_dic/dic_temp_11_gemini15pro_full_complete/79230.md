glaring:/ˈɡlɛərɪŋ/ /ˈɡlerɪŋ/| adj.|耀眼的，刺眼的；显眼的，明显的；怒目而视的| emitting or reflecting a strong or dazzling light; very obvious or conspicuous; staring fiercely or angrily|The glaring sun made it difficult to see. 耀眼的阳光使人难以看清。|Synonyms: dazzling, bright, obvious, conspicuous, blatant, staring, piercing |Antonyms: dim, faint, subtle, inconspicuous, discreet|adjective

glare:/ɡlɛər/ /ɡler/ | n. vi.| 刺眼的光；怒视；闪耀 | a very bright, unpleasant light; a fierce or angry stare; to shine with a very bright, unpleasant light; to stare fiercely or angrily | The glare of the headlights blinded me.  车头灯的强光使我眼花缭乱。 | Synonyms: blaze, dazzle, shimmer, stare, frown | Antonyms:  soft light, glance, smile | noun, verb
