expectorate: /ɪkˈspɛktəreɪt/ (US), /ɪkˈspɛktəreɪt/ (UK)| v.|咳出，吐痰|To cough or spit out phlegm from the throat or lungs.|He expectorated a large wad of phlegm into the tissue. 他把一大口痰吐在了纸巾里。|近义词: cough up, spit, hawk|反义词: swallow, ingest|词性: 动词

expectoration: /ɪkˌspɛktəˈreɪʃən/ (US), /ɪkˌspɛktəˈreɪʃən/ (UK)| n.|咳出，吐痰（的动作或痰）|The act of expectorating, or the matter expectorated.|The doctor examined the patient's expectoration. 医生检查了病人的痰。|近义词: sputum, phlegm|反义词: (无明确反义词)|词性: 名词

词源: From Latin expectorare ("to drive out of the breast, expel by coughing"), from ex- ("out") + pectus, pector- ("breast").
