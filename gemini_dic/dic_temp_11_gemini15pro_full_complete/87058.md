heptagynia:/ˌheptəˈɡɪniə/
n.
七雌蕊植物
A plant having seven pistils or styles in each flower.
The rare *Trientalis borealis* exhibits heptagynia, possessing seven styles.  *七瓣莲这种稀有植物展现了七雌蕊植物的特性，拥有七个花柱。*
