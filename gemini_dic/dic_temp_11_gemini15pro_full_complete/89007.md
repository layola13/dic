hollo:/ˈhɒləʊ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːloʊ/ (US)| interjection, verb | 喂；大声叫喊 |A loud shout or cry to attract attention.  |例句：He holloed to his friend across the field. 他隔着田野对他的朋友大声喊叫。


例句：She holloed at the children to stop running. 她大声叫喊让孩子们停止奔跑。


近义词：shout, yell, call |反义词: whisper, murmur |词性: interjection, verb
