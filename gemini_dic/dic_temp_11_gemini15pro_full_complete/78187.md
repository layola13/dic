geostatic:/ˌdʒiːəʊˈstætɪk/ | adj. | 对地静止的；与地球同步的 | Relating to or designating a satellite that orbits the earth at the same speed as the earth rotates, so that it appears to remain stationary over a particular point. |  A geostatic satellite orbits the Earth directly above the equator. 对地静止卫星绕地球轨道运行，正好位于赤道上方。| Synonyms: geosynchronous | Antonyms: N/A | word type: adjective
