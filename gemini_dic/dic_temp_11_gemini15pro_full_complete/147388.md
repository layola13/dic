overneutralizers:/ˌoʊvərˈnuːtrəˌlaɪzərz/
| n. |过度中和剂|Substances that neutralize more acid than intended or necessary.|The overneutralizers in the antacid caused a rebound effect, increasing stomach acid production.|Synonyms: excessive neutralizers, overcorrecting agents|Antonyms: underneutralizers, insufficient neutralizers|noun
