extinguishes:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪz/
verb
熄灭，扑灭，压制，消灭
puts out (a fire or a light); causes (something) to cease to exist
The firefighters quickly extinguished the blaze. 消防员迅速扑灭了大火。
synonyms: put out, quench, douse, smother, stifle
antonyms: ignite, kindle, light, start
词性: 动词
