multisonant:/ˈmʌltɪsənənt/ | adj. | 多音的，多声的，响亮的 | Having many sounds; resonant. | The multisonant choir filled the cathedral with music. 多音合唱团的歌声充满了整个教堂。 | The multisonant orchestra played a beautiful symphony. 多音的管弦乐队演奏了一曲优美的交响乐。 | 近义词: resonant, sonorous, polyphonic | 反义词: quiet, silent, muted | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *multisonans*, from multi- "many" + sonans, present participle of sonare "to sound".
