chuk:/tʃʌk/| n.|扔, 抛, 轻推;卡盘;木材;食物;宝贝;亲爱的人 (主要用于英式非正式)|A short, thick piece of wood; a throw; a gentle push; a term of endearment (chiefly British informal).|He gave the log a chuk into the fire. 他把木头块扔进了火里。|近义词：toss, throw, push, nudge, piece, chunk, dear, darling, sweetheart (depending on the meaning)|反义词：pull, catch (depending on the meaning)|名词

chuk:/tʃʌk/| v.|扔, 抛, 轻推;发出卡哒声 (主要用于英式非正式)|To throw something casually; to make a clicking sound. |He chukked the ball to his friend. 他把球扔给了他的朋友。|近义词：toss, throw, fling, chuck (variant spelling)|反义词：catch, hold|动词
