bezel:/ˈbezəl/| n. | 斜面，边框，嵌框 | The sloping edge of a cut gem. A grooved rim holding a transparent covering, as of a watch crystal. | The bezel of her watch was studded with diamonds. 她的手表边框镶嵌着钻石。| synonym: rim, frame, border, setting | antonym: center, interior | noun


bezel:/ˈbezəl/| v. | 用斜角切割；给…镶边 | To cut facets on. To enclose (a gem, watch crystal, etc.) in a bezel. | The jeweler bezeled the diamond expertly. 珠宝匠熟练地将钻石切割成斜面。| synonym: encase, frame, mount | antonym: dismount, remove | verb
