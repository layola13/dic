chamferer:/ˈtʃæmfərər/| n.|倒角器，倒角钳，去角器|A tool or machine used to create a chamfer (a beveled edge) on a piece of material.|The carpenter used a chamferer to smooth the edges of the wooden beam. 木匠用倒角器磨平了木梁的边缘。|近义词：beveling tool, chamfering machine|反义词:|noun

chamfer:/ˈtʃæmfər/| n. vt.|倒角，斜面；给…倒角|A beveled edge or corner.|He chamfered the edge of the table to make it safer. 他把桌子的边缘倒角以使其更安全。|近义词：bevel, slope|反义词:|noun, verb
