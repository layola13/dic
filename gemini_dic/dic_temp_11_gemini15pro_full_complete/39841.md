conspurcate:/kənˈspɜːrkeɪt/
verb
玷污，污损
to make impure or unclean; defile; pollute.
The scandal conspurcated the politician's reputation. 这桩丑闻玷污了这位政治家的名誉。
synonyms: defile, pollute, taint, sully, besmirch, contaminate
antonyms: purify, cleanse, sanctify
VERB

Etymology:
From Latin *conspurcāre*, from con- ‘together’ + spurcāre ‘to make foul’, from spurcus ‘dirty’.
