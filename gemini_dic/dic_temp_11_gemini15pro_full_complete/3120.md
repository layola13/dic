aerugo:/ɪˈruːɡoʊ/ /eɪˈruːɡoʊ/
n.
绿锈，铜绿
a green rust-like deposit on copper or brass, especially the basic carbonate formed when exposed to moist air.
The statue was covered in aerugo, giving it an antique look. 这座雕像覆盖着铜绿，使其具有古色古香的外观。
The aerugo on the old coins made them difficult to clean. 旧硬币上的铜绿使它们难以清洗。
patina, verdigris
rust
noun
