noncoercions:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈɜːʃənz/
|n.|非强迫, 非胁迫|The principle of noncoercion is central to a free society.  非强迫原则是自由社会的核心。|Noncoercions are essential for genuine consent. 真正的同意必须建立在非胁迫的基础上。|近义词:noncompulsion, voluntariness, freedom|反义词:coercion, compulsion, force|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
coercion (noun): from Latin coercere "to restrain, confine," from com- "together" + arcere "to enclose, confine."
