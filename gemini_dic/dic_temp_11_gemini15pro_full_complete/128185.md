Example 2: The doctor prescribed medication for his neuralgic condition. 医生给他开了治疗神经痛的药物。| 近义词: painful, sharp, stabbing | 反义词: painless, numb | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *névralgique*, from *névralgie* ("neuralgia") + *-ique* (adjective-forming suffix), from Greek *νεῦρον* (*neûron*, "nerve") + *ἄλγος* (*álgos*, "pain").
