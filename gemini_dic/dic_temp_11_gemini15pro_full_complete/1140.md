accidentalism: /ˌæksɪˈdɛntəlɪzəm/
n.
偶然主义，意外主义
A philosophy or artistic style that emphasizes the role of chance and accident in creation.
The painting seemed to be a product of accidentalism, with splashes of color seemingly applied at random. 这幅画似乎是偶然主义的产物，颜色的泼洒似乎是随机应用的。
synonyms: indeterminism, randomness, chance, fortuity
antonyms: determinism, intentionality, purposefulness
noun
