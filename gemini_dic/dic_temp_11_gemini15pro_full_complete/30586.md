cercopod: /ˈsɜːrkəpɒd/ (US) /ˈsɜːrkəpɒd/ (UK)| n. |尾附器；尾肢；尾节|A cercopod is one of a pair of appendages at the end of the abdomen in some arthropods, such as springtails and bristletails. | Springtails use their cercopods to jump. 弹尾目昆虫用它们的尾附器跳跃。| The cercopod of the bristletail helps it to sense its environment.  缨尾目昆虫的尾肢帮助它感知周围环境。 | synonyms: appendage,  cercus (when referring to insects specifically) | antonyms: N/A | noun
