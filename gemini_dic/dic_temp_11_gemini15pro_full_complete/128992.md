nippitate: /ˈnɪpɪteɪt/
nippitate: v.
nippitate:  To nip off the apex of (a plant); to pinch back.
nippitate:  The gardener nippitated the basil plants to encourage bushier growth.
nippitate: prune, pinch, clip, trim
nippitate:  let grow wild, allow to grow unchecked
nippitate: verb
