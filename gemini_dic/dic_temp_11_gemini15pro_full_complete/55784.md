divert:/daɪˈvɜːrt/ /dɪˈvɜːrt/| v. |转移；使转向；使分心；娱乐，消遣|To turn aside from a course or direction; to distract; to amuse or entertain.|The loud noise diverted my attention from my work. 喧闹的声音转移了我工作的注意力。|近义词：redirect, distract, entertain, amuse|反义词：focus, concentrate|词性:动词

diversion:/daɪˈvɜːrʒən/ /dɪˈvɜːrʒən/| n. |转移；转向；分心的事物；娱乐，消遣|The act of diverting or turning aside; something that diverts attention; a pastime or amusement.|Reading is my favorite diversion. 阅读是我最喜欢的消遣。|近义词：deviation, distraction, entertainment, amusement|反义词：concentration, focus|词性:名词

词源:
From Latin dīvertere, from dī- "aside" + vertere "to turn."
