niggardly: /ˈnɪɡərdli/ | adj. | 吝啬的，小气的 | Reluctant to give or spend; stingy or mean. | He was niggardly with his praise, giving only grudging compliments. 他吝于赞美，只给予勉强的称赞。|  She gave a niggardly donation to the charity. 她只捐了一点钱给慈善机构。 | 近义词: stingy, miserly, parsimonious, mean | 反义词: generous, liberal, lavish | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *nigard*, from Old Norse *hnǫggr* ("stingy") + *-ly*.
