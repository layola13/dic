cleare:/klɪr/| adj. v.|清晰的，清楚的；清除，放晴|Clear pronunciation is essential for effective communication.  清晰的发音对于有效沟通至关重要。 The sky cleared after the storm.  暴风雨后天空放晴了。|例句：The instructions were clear and easy to follow. 说明很清楚，容易理解。  He cleared his throat before speaking. 他说话前清了清嗓子。|近义词：transparent, lucid, distinct, obvious, evident, remove, eliminate|反义词：opaque, obscure, vague, ambiguous, add, clutter|词性:形容词，动词
词源: From Middle English *clere, from Old French cler, clair (“clear, bright, shining; light in color; distinct, articulate; free from obstruction”), from Latin clārus (“clear, bright, loud”).
