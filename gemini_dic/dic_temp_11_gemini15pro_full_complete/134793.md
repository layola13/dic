nonmetro:/ˌnɑːnˈmɛtroʊ/ (US), /ˌnɒnˈmɛtrəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|非都市的，非大都市区的|Relating to or characteristic of areas outside large cities.  It typically refers to rural areas and smaller towns, not considered part of a metropolitan statistical area.|Many nonmetro counties are experiencing population decline.|近义词：rural, exurban, non-urban|反义词：metro, metropolitan, urban|词性:形容词

nonmetro:/ˌnɑːnˈmɛtroʊ/ (US), /ˌnɒnˈmɛtrəʊ/ (UK)| n.|非都市地区|An area outside of a large city; a non-metropolitan area.|Job growth has been slow in the nonmetro.|近义词：countryside, hinterland|反义词：metropolis, city|词性:名词
