ceks: /tʃɛks/
Noun
checks (plural)
检查；核对；支票
An act of making sure that something is correct or in good condition.  A written order to a bank to pay a stated sum from the drawer's account.
例句：I made a quick check of the doors and windows before leaving. 我离开前快速检查了门窗。

例句：He wrote me a check for $100. 他给我开了一张 100 美元的支票。
近义词：examination, inspection, verification; draft, payment order
反义词：N/A
词性: n.

ceks: /tʃɛks/
Verb
checks (third-person singular simple present)
检查；核对；阻止；抑制
To examine something to make sure that it is correct or safe. To stop or slow down the progress of something.
例句：Please check your work before handing it in.  请在交作业前检查一下。

例句：The government is trying to check the spread of the disease. 政府正在努力控制疾病的传播。
近义词：examine, inspect, verify; control, restrain, curb
反义词：ignore, neglect; accelerate, promote
词性: v.
