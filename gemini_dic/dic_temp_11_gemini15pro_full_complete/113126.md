lungi:/ˈlʌŋɡi/| n.|隆基，纱笼，围腰布|A garment consisting of a length of cotton or silk cloth wrapped around the waist and reaching to the ankles, worn especially by men in India, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, and Myanmar.|He wore a brightly coloured lungi.|近义词：sarong, dhoti|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western clothing like trousers/pants)|noun
