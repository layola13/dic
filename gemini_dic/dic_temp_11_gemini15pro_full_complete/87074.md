heptarchy:/ˈhɛptɑːrki/| n.|七王国时代; 七个君主的统治|A heptarchy is a government of seven people.  Specifically, it refers to the period in Anglo-Saxon England, roughly from the 6th to the 9th centuries, when England was divided into seven kingdoms.|例句：The Heptarchy was a complex period in English history. 七王国时代是英国历史上一个复杂的时期。

例句：Historians often debate the precise nature of the Heptarchy.  历史学家经常争论七王国时代的准确性质。|近义词：oligarchy, rule|反义词:monarchy, democracy|词性:noun
