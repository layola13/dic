esemplastic:/ˌɛsɪmˈplæstɪk/ | adj. |  塑造的；赋予形态的；有塑造力的 | Having the power to shape or mold; formative.  Relating to the imagination's power to shape disparate experiences into a unified whole. |  "Coleridge described the imagination as 'esemplastic,' able to unify and reconcile opposites."  柯勒律治将想象力描述为“塑造性的”，能够统一和调和对立面。 |  近义词: formative, shaping, molding, creative, synthesizing | 反义词: destructive, uncreative, disintegrating | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *es-, "into" + emplassein "to mold," from en- "in" + plassein "to mold."
