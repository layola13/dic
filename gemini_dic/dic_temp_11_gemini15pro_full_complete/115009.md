mandator:/ˈmændətɔːr/ (US), /ˈmændətə(r)/ (UK)| n.|授权人；受托者；命令者|A person who is given a mandate to carry out a specific task or duty; someone who has the authority to command or give orders.|The mandator instructed the delegate to negotiate the treaty. 授权人指示代表进行条约谈判。|Synonyms: commissioner, delegate, representative, agent, envoy, emissary, proxy, mandatory, appointee, director, governor, ruler, commander|Antonyms: subject, subordinate, follower|noun

mandator:/ˈmændətɔːr/ (US), /ˈmændətə(r)/ (UK)| adj.(古语，罕用)|强制的|Obligatory; required by law or mandate.|Attendance at the meeting is mandator. (Archaic usage - now generally replaced by "mandatory")  出席会议是强制性的。|Synonyms: mandatory, compulsory, obligatory, required|Antonyms: optional, voluntary, discretionary|adjective
