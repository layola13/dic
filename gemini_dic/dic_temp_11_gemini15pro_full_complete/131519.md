noncombustion:/ˌnɒnkəmˈbʌstʃən/
n.
非燃烧，不燃烧
The process of producing energy without burning fuel, such as in a fuel cell.  非燃烧，例如在燃料电池中不燃烧燃料而产生能量的过程。
Nuclear power is a form of noncombustion energy generation. 核能是一种非燃烧能源。
non-ignition, non-burning
combustion, burning
noun
