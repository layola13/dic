hektograph:/ˈhɛktəɡræf/ /ˈhɛktoʊɡræf/| n.|复写器，油印机|A duplicating machine that produces copies from a gelatinous surface.  It involves transferring an original, written with special ink, onto a pan of gelatin. From this gelatin copy, multiple prints can be made.|The teacher used a hektograph to make copies of the exam.|duplicator, mimeograph| |noun
Etymology: From German Hektograph, from Greek hekaton ‘hundred’ + -graph.
