bashi:/ˈbɑːʃi/| n.| 巴什，头儿，领班 |A term of address used in some parts of Asia, especially India and Pakistan, for a person of authority or respect, such as a teacher, a headman, or a boss. It is derived from the Persian word "bashi" meaning "head" or "chief".| The workers called their foreman "bashi". 工人们称他们的工头为“巴什”。 | synonym: chief, leader, boss, headman | antonym: subordinate, follower | noun
