accommodately:/əˈkɒmədeɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 善解人意地，乐于助人地；体贴地 | in a way that is helpful and willing to fit in with someone's needs or wishes | She always dealt with her clients accommodately, ensuring their comfort and satisfaction. 她总是体贴地对待她的客户，确保他们的舒适和满意。 |  The hotel staff behaved very accommodately, allowing us to check in early. 酒店工作人员非常乐于助人，允许我们提前入住。 | 近义词: obligingly, helpfully, considerately, graciously | 反义词: unhelpfully, inconsiderately, rudely | 词性: 副词

accommodating:/əˈkɒmədeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 乐于助人的；随和的 | willing to help and fit in with someone's wishes or needs | He was an accommodating host, always ready with a smile and a helpful suggestion. 他是一位乐于助人的主人，总是面带微笑，乐于提供有益的建议。 | The hotel staff were very accommodating and allowed us to check in early. 酒店工作人员非常乐于助人，允许我们提前入住。| 近义词: obliging, helpful, considerate, cooperative | 反义词: uncooperative, unhelpful, inconsiderate | 词性: 形容词

词源：来源于拉丁语accommodatus，过去分词accommodare，意为“使合适，调整”。
