hortation:/hɔːrˈteɪʃn/
n.
劝告；激励；训诫
An exhortation is a communication intended to urge or persuade someone to do something.
The coach's pre-game exhortation inspired the team to victory. 教练在赛前的激励鼓舞了球队，最终取得了胜利。
The president delivered an exhortation for peace and unity. 总统发表了关于和平与团结的劝告。
encouragement, urging, admonition
dissuasion, discouragement
noun
