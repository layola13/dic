disapproves:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvz/
verb
不赞成，反对
expresses an unfavorable opinion of.
The school disapproves of students wearing jeans. 学校不赞成学生穿牛仔裤。
近义词: objects to, condemns, criticizes, opposes
反义词: approves, supports, commends, praises
词性: verb (third person singular simple present)

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + approve (from Latin approbare, from ad- "to" + probare "to prove, test").
