misspeaks:/ˌmɪsˈspiːks/
| v. | 说错; 失言; |To say something incorrectly or unintentionally.|  He misspoke and accidentally revealed the surprise. 他说漏了嘴，不小心透露了惊喜。 | synonyms:  blunders, errs, slips up | antonyms: articulates, enunciates, expresses | verb


misspeaking:/ˌmɪsˈspiːkɪŋ/
| n. |  说错话; 失言; | The act of saying something incorrectly or unintentionally. |  His misspeaking caused a lot of confusion. 他的失言造成了很多困惑。| synonyms:  blunder, error, slip of the tongue | antonyms: articulation, enunciation, expression | noun
