latigo:/ˈlætɪɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈlætɪɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 马鞭，皮鞭|A long, narrow strap of leather, usually braided, used especially as a whip or part of a saddle girth in Spanish America. |He expertly cracked the latigo, urging his horse forward. 他熟练地挥动马鞭，催促他的马前进。|近义词：whip, lash, strap|反义词: N/A|名词
