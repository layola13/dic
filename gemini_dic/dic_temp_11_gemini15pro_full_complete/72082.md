floatative:/ˈfloʊtətɪv/| adj.|漂浮的；浮动的；不稳定的|Relating to floating or tending to float; buoyant.  Characterized by instability or fluctuation.|The floatative properties of the raft kept it above water. 木筏的漂浮特性使它保持在水面上。|近义词：buoyant, floating, unstable|反义词： sinking, stable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *flotativus, from *flotare (frequentative of flare "to blow"), from Proto-Italic *flā- “to blow, swell”, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₁- "to blow, swell".  The suffix "-ative" forms adjectives meaning "tending to" or "having the quality of".
