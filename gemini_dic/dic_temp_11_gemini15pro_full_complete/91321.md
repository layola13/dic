hydroperitoneum:/ˌhaɪdroʊˌpɛrɪtəˈniːəm/
n.
腹水
The accumulation of fluid in the peritoneal cavity, the space between the lining of the abdomen and the abdominal organs.
The patient presented with severe hydroperitoneum due to liver cirrhosis. 患者因肝硬化出现严重腹水。

ascites, peritoneal effusion
 (No direct antonym)
