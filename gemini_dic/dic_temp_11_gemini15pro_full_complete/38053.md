composedness:/kəmˈpoʊzdnɪs/| n.|镇静；沉着；泰然自若|The state of being calm, controlled, and self-possessed.|She maintained her composedness throughout the crisis.  她在整个危机中保持镇静。|Synonyms: calmness, composure, equanimity, poise, self-possession|Antonyms: agitation, anxiety, nervousness, distress, unease|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From compose + -ed + -ness.  Compose ultimately comes from Latin componere "to put together." The suffix "-ed" forms the past participle (used here adjectivally), and "-ness" forms a noun denoting a state or quality.
