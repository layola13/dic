fiascoes:/fiˈæskoʊz/ /fiˈæskoʊz/
noun
彻底的失败；惨败
a complete failure, especially one that is embarrassing or ridiculous
The party was a complete fiasco; the band didn't show up, and the food was terrible.  派对彻底失败了；乐队没有出现，食物也很糟糕。
近义词:failure, disaster, debacle, flop
反义词:success, triumph, victory
词性:n. (plural of fiasco)
