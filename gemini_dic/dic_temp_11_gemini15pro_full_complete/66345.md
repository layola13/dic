excurrent:/ɛksˈkʌrənt/ | adj. | 外向的，向外伸出的；(指树)树干笔直，枝条向上的 | Extending outwards beyond the main body; (of a tree) having a trunk that remains the main stem without dividing, and branches that grow upwards. | The pine tree has an excurrent growth habit. 这棵松树有向外生长的习性。| 近义词：extrusive, projecting, outward | 反义词: incurved, introrse | 词性: adjective

excurrent:/ɛksˈkʌrənt/ | adj. | (动/植物学)向顶端延伸的，(指主轴)延伸到顶端的 | (botany, zoology) extending to the apex; (of a main axis) continuing to the apex. | The sponge has an excurrent canal system.  这块海绵有一个向顶端延伸的管道系统。 | 近义词：apical | 反义词: basal | 词性: adjective
