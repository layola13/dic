bufflehead:/ˈbʌfəlˌhɛd/| n.|小丑鸭|A small North American diving duck (Bucephala albeola) having a large, puffy head.|例句：We saw a pair of buffleheads diving for fish in the lake. 我们看到一对小丑鸭在湖里潜水觅食。

例句：The male bufflehead is a striking bird with its iridescent head feathers. 雄性小丑鸭是一种引人注目的鸟，它的头部羽毛闪着虹彩。|近义词：duck, sea duck|反义词:|名词
