keeps:/kiːps/| v.|保持，保存，保留，经营，遵守，饲养|She keeps a diary. 她写日记。|近义词：maintains, preserves, retains, operates, observes, raises|反义词:loses, abandons, neglects|verb (third-person singular simple present keeps, present participle keeping, simple past kept, past participle kept)


Etymology: From Middle English *kēpen*, from Old English *cēpan* (“to observe, attend to, care for, heed, hold, keep, retain, guard”), from Proto-Germanic *kōpijaną* (“to buy, bargain, traffic”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱōp- (“to buy, exchange”).
