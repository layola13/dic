gutturalities: /ɡʌtəˈrælɪtiz/ | n. | 喉音；喉音化的发音 | Harsh, throaty sounds produced in the back of the throat. |  His speech was filled with gutturalities, making him difficult to understand. 他的讲话充满了喉音，使人难以理解。 | Example: The singer's gutturalities added a raw, primal edge to the music.  这位歌手的喉音为音乐增添了原始的、粗犷的色彩。| Synonyms: growls, rasps, croaks | Antonyms: melodious sounds, smooth sounds | noun
