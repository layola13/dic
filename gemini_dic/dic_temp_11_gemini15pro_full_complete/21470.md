blowy:/ˈbloʊi/| adj.|刮风的，多风的|Characterized by strong winds; windy.|例句：It was a blowy day at the coast.  海岸边风很大的一天。

例句：The blowy conditions made it difficult to sail.  刮风的天气使航行变得困难。|近义词：windy, breezy, gusty|反义词：calm, still|词性:形容词
词源: From blow + -y, a suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of."  Ultimately from Old English blāwan.
