leopard:/ˈlepərd/ (US), /ˈlepəd/ (UK)| n. |豹|A large, solitary cat with a yellowish-brown coat marked with black rosettes, native to Africa and Asia.|The leopard stalked its prey through the tall grass. 豹子在高高的草丛中跟踪猎物。|Synonyms: panther, jaguar (in some contexts)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

leopard:/ˈlepərd/ (US), /ˈlepəd/ (UK)| adj. |豹纹的|Resembling the spotted coat of a leopard.|She wore a leopard-print dress. 她穿了一件豹纹连衣裙。|Synonyms: spotted, mottled|Antonyms: plain, solid|adjective
