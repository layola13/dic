erroneous: /ɪˈroʊniəs/ (US) /ɪˈrəʊniəs/ (UK)| adj.|错误的，不正确的|Containing or characterized by error; incorrect; mistaken.|例句：The report was based on erroneous information. 这份报告是基于错误的信息。|例句：His erroneous assumptions led to disastrous consequences. 他错误的假设导致了灾难性的后果。|近义词：incorrect, false, inaccurate, flawed, fallacious, mistaken, untrue, invalid|反义词：correct, accurate, true, valid, right|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *erroneus, from Latin errōneus (“wandering, straying”), from errō (“I wander, I stray, I err”).  Doublet of errant.
