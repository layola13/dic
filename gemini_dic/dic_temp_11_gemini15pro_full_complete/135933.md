nonpunishing:/ˌnɒnˈpʌnɪʃɪŋ/
adj.
不惩罚的，不处罚的
Not involving or inflicting punishment.
Parents often adopt a nonpunishing approach to discipline. 父母通常采用非惩罚性的管教方式。
Near Synonyms: lenient, merciful, forgiving, tolerant
Antonyms: punishing, penalizing, disciplinary, punitive
词性: 形容词
