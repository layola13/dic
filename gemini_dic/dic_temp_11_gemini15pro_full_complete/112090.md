longiroster:/ˌlɒŋɪˈrɒstər/ (British), /ˌlɔːŋɪˈrɑːstər/ (American)| adj.|长名册的；长名单的|Having a long roster; characterized by a long list of names, especially of players on a team.|The team had a longiroster this year, with many promising new recruits. 今年这支队伍的名单很长，有很多有前途的新成员。|近义词：extensive, lengthy|反义词：short, limited|词性:形容词

词源: "longiroster" is a combination of "long" (meaning extended in length) and "roster" (a list of names). "Roster" comes from the Dutch word "rooster," meaning "gridiron" or "grate," which was used to refer to a list of names because it resembled the lines on a grid.
