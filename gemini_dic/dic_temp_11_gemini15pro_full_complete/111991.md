loneliest:/ˈləʊnliɪst/ (UK) /ˈloʊnliɪst/ (US)| adj.|最孤独的，最寂寞的|Superlative form of lonely: most lonely.  It describes the state of being the most isolated, solitary, or without company. |例句：He felt loneliest on holidays when everyone else was with their families.  他感到最孤独的时候是在假期，那时其他人都在和家人在一起。

例句：She described the experience as the loneliest time in her life. 她形容这段经历是她人生中最孤独的时光。|近义词：most isolated, most solitary, most forlorn|反义词：most sociable, most gregarious, most accompanied|词性:形容词
