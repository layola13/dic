oversentimentally:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəli/ | adv. | 过分伤感地，多愁善感地 |  In a way that is excessively or inappropriately sentimental. | She spoke oversentimentally about her childhood pet. 她過於感傷地談論她童年時的寵物。| 近义词： mawkishly, cloyingly, effusively | 反义词： unsentimentally, pragmatically | 词性: 副词

oversentimentality:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛntɪmɛnˈtælɪti/ | n. | 过分伤感，多愁善感 | The quality of being excessively or inappropriately sentimental. |  Her oversentimentality made it difficult to have a serious conversation. 她的多愁善感使人难以和她进行严肃的谈话。| 近义词： mawkishness, sentimentality | 反义词： pragmatism, detachment | 词性: 名词
