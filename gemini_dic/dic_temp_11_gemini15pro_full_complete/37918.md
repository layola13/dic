complementizers:/ˈkɒmplɪmɛntaɪzəz/ | n. | 补语化成分 | Words that introduce a subordinate clause, linking it to a main clause.  These subordinate clauses function as complements. | Example: I know *that* he is coming. 我知道他就要来了. | Synonym: subordinators, linking words | Antonym:  (No direct antonym; perhaps coordinating conjunctions which link independent clauses) | Noun
