mosaicked:/məʊˈzeɪkt/ /moʊˈzeɪkt/| adj.|马赛克的，镶嵌的|Decorated or covered with mosaic; resembling a mosaic.|The artist created a mosaicked tabletop using tiny pieces of colored glass. 艺术家用彩色玻璃小块创作了一个镶嵌的桌面。|The courtyard was mosaicked with colorful tiles. 院子用彩色瓷砖铺成马赛克。|近义词：inlaid, tessellated, tiled|反义词: plain, simple|词性:形容词

词源: From French *mosaïque*, from Italian *mosaico*, from Late Latin *musaicum* ("mosaic work"), neuter of *musaicus* ("of the Muses"), from Latin *Musa* ("Muse").  Ultimately from Greek *Μοῦσα* (Moûsa, “Muse”).
