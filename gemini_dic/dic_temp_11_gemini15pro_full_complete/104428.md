kafir:/ˈkæfər/ (US), /ˈkɑːfɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.|卡菲勒，异教徒|A derogatory term used by Muslims to refer to non-Muslims, especially Christians and Jews.  Now generally considered offensive due to its historical association with slavery and colonialism.  The word literally means "one who disbelieves" or "one who conceals the truth."|He was labeled a kafir by the extremists.|Near synonyms: infidel, unbeliever, non-believer, heathen|Antonyms: believer, Muslim, faithful|Noun
