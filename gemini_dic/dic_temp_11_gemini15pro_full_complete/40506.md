contrives:/kənˈtraɪvz/| v. | 设计；发明；图谋；设法做到 | to invent or plan cleverly; to manage to do something difficult |  He contrived a way to escape. 他设法逃脱了。 | synonyms: devise, engineer, plan, scheme, plot | antonyms: fail, neglect | verb

contrived:/kənˈtraɪvd/| adj. |  人为的；做作的；不自然的 | artificial and difficult to believe | Her story sounded rather contrived. 她的故事听起来很不自然。| synonyms: artificial, forced, unnatural | antonyms: natural, genuine | adjective
