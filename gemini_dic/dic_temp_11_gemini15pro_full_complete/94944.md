in-question:/ɪn ˈkwɛstʃən/ | adj.|正在讨论的，有疑问的，不确定的|being discussed or considered; subject to doubt or uncertainty|The matter in question is the budget for next year. 正在讨论的问题是明年的预算。|Synonyms: under consideration, at issue, in doubt, disputed, debatable |Antonyms: certain, definite, undisputed, resolved|adjective

in-question:/ɪn ˈkwɛstʃən/ | pron.|正在讨论的人或事物|the person or thing being discussed or considered|The document in question has been lost.  正在讨论的文件丢失了。|Synonyms: under discussion, at hand, concerned |Antonyms: irrelevant, unrelated|pronoun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *in questioun*, from Anglo-Norman *en questiun*, from Latin *in quæstione* (“under investigation”). Equivalent to *in* + *question*.
