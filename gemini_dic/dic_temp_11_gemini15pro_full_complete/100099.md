interposition:/ˌɪntər.pəˈzɪʃ.ən/
n.|插入；干预；介入；调解|The act of placing something between or among other things; intervention, especially of a state in the affairs of another.|The government's interposition in the labor dispute helped to avert a strike. 政府对劳资纠纷的介入有助于避免罢工。|intervention, mediation, interference|non-intervention, inaction
