aetiological:/ˌiːtiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (US) /ˌiːtiəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|病因学的，病原学的|Relating to the causes or origins of diseases.  Exploring the underlying factors that contribute to the development of a condition.| The aetiological factors of lung cancer are complex and include smoking, genetics, and environmental exposure. 肺癌的病因很复杂，包括吸烟、遗传和环境暴露。|synonyms: causal, causative|antonyms: resultant, consequential|adjective
