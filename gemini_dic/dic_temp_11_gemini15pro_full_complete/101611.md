irreconcilable:/ˌɪrɛkənˈsaɪləbl/ /ˌɪrɛkənˈsaɪləbəl/| adj.|不可调和的，无法和解的|Not able to be brought into agreement or harmony; incompatible.|The two sides held irreconcilable positions on the issue. 双方在这个问题上持有不可调和的立场。|例句：Their differences were irreconcilable, leading to the breakup of the partnership. 他们的分歧无法调和，导致了合作关系的破裂。|近义词：incompatible, conflicting, antagonistic, unresolvable|反义词：compatible, harmonious, reconcilable|词性:形容词

irreconcilable:/ˌɪrɛkənˈsaɪləbl/ /ˌɪrɛkənˈsaɪləbəl/| n.|不可调和的人或事物|A person or thing that is irreconcilable.|The irreconcilables in the group prevented any compromise.  小组中不可调和的人阻碍了任何妥协。|例句：The mediator tried to find common ground, but the irreconcilables refused to budge. 调解员试图找到共同点，但那些不可调和的人拒绝让步。|近义词：opponent, adversary, antagonist|反义词：supporter, ally|词性:名词
