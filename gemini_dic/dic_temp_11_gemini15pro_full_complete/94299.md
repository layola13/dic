imperturbable:/ˌɪmpərˈtɜːrbəbl/ | adj. |  沉着的，冷静的，泰然自若的 | Not easily upset or excited; calm and composed. |  Despite the chaos around him, he remained imperturbable. 尽管周围一片混乱，他仍然保持镇静。 | Synonyms: calm, composed, serene, unruffled | Antonyms: agitated, excitable, nervous, perturbed | adjective

imperturbable:/ˌɪmpərˈtɜːrbəbl/ | adj. |  沉着的，冷静的，泰然自若的 | Unable to be upset or excited; calm. |  She remained imperturbable even under intense pressure. 即使在巨大的压力下，她也保持镇定。 | Synonyms: composed, cool, collected, unflappable | Antonyms: agitated, disturbed, flustered, ruffled | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin imperturbabilis, from in- ‘not’ + perturbare ‘to disturb’ (from per- ‘thoroughly’ + turbare ‘to disturb, confuse’).
