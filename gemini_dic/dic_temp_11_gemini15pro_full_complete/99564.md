interview: /ˈɪntərvjuː/ (UK) /ˈɪntərˌvjuː/ (US)| n. vt.|面试，采访；面谈|A formal meeting in which someone is asked questions to see if they are suitable for a particular job, or for a course of study at a college, university, etc.  A meeting in which someone is asked questions about their life, experiences, or opinions for a newspaper article, television program, etc.|例句：I have a job interview tomorrow. 我明天有一个工作面试。

例句：The journalist interviewed the president. 记者采访了总统。


近义词：meeting, discussion, audition|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, verb
