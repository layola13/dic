influentials:/ˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəlz/
n.
有影响力的人；有影响力的人物
People who are influential, especially in a particular field.
Influentials are invited to the product launch event. 有影响力的人士被邀请参加产品发布会。

influentials:/ˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəlz/
n.
影响者；有影响力的人
People who exert influence, particularly in shaping opinions or trends.
Marketing campaigns often target influentials to reach a wider audience. 营销活动通常针对有影响力的人来覆盖更广泛的受众。


近义词:leaders, opinion-makers, trendsetters
反义词:followers, subordinates
词性:noun
