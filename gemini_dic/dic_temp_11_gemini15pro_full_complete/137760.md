nonvaried:/ˌnɒnˈveərid/ /ˌnɑːnˈveriːd/
adjective
不变的，无变化的
Not varied; uniform or monotonous.
The terrain was flat and nonvaried.  地形平坦且一成不变。
The diet was nonvaried and consisted mainly of rice.  饮食单调，主要由米饭组成。
近义词: unvaried, uniform, monotonous, unchanging, constant
反义词: varied, diverse, changing, fluctuating
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + varied, past participle of vary (from Latin variāre “to change, alter, make different”).
