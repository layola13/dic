nonvegetation:/ˌnɒnˌvedʒɪˈteɪʃən/
noun
非植被，非植物覆盖区
Areas of land not covered by plants.  This includes bare ground, rock, urban areas, water bodies, etc.
The researchers studied the effects of erosion on nonvegetation areas. 研究人员研究了侵蚀对非植被区域的影响。
The satellite imagery showed a significant increase in nonvegetation areas due to the drought. 卫星图像显示，由于干旱，非植被区域显著增加。
Synonyms: barren land, bare ground, unvegetated area
Antonyms: vegetation, plant cover
