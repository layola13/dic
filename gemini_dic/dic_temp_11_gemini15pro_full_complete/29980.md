cavilings: /ˈkævəlɪŋz/ | n. | 吹毛求疵，无端指摘 | Petty or frivolous objections; fault-finding. |  His constant cavilings about the food became tiresome. 他对食物不断的吹毛求疵令人厌烦。| The editor's cavilings about my manuscript were mostly trivial. 编辑对我稿件的吹毛求疵大多无关紧要。 |  carping, nitpicking, quibbling |  praise, approval | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cavele*, from Old French *caveler* "to dispute, argue," from Late Latin *cavillari*, frequentative of Latin *cavilla* "jest, scoff," of uncertain origin.
