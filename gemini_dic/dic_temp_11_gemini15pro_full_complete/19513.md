biennials:/baɪˈɛniəlz/
n.
双年展；两年一次的事情；两年生植物
Plants that complete their life cycle in two years are called biennials.  完成生命周期需要两年的植物被称为两年生植物。
The Venice Biennale is a major contemporary art exhibition. 威尼斯双年展是一个重要的当代艺术展览。
The report is published biennially. 这份报告每两年出版一次。
近义词:two-year
反义词:annuals, perennials
词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *biennalis, from Latin bi- "two" + annus "year".  源自晚期拉丁语 *biennalis，来自拉丁语 bi- “二” + annus “年”。
