overreacting:/ˌoʊvərriˈæktɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
反应过度
reacting too strongly or showing too much emotion to something that has happened
例句：He was overreacting to the news. 他对这个消息反应过度了。

例句：She tends to overreact in stressful situations.  她在压力大的情况下容易反应过度。

近义词：overdramatizing, exaggerating, making a fuss
反义词：underreacting, downplaying
词性：动词的现在分词
