fleshiest:/ˈflɛʃiɪst/
adjective
最肉的；最肥厚的
having the most flesh; plumpest
例句：He bit into the fleshiest part of the peach. 他咬了一口桃子最肉的部分。

例句：The fleshiest grapes are often the sweetest.  最肉的葡萄通常最甜。

近义词：plumpest, meatiest, thickest
反义词：thinnest, boniest, leanest
词性：adjective

词源：From flesh + -y + -est.  来自"flesh"(肉) + "-y"(形容词后缀，表示“充满...的”) + "-est"(形容词最高级后缀)
