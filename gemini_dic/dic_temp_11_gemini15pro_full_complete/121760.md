misqualify:/mɪsˈkwɒlɪfaɪ/ |v. |使失去资格，取消资格 |To declare someone ineligible or unfit for something, usually due to a violation of rules or regulations. |例句：The athlete was misqualified for using performance-enhancing drugs.  这位运动员因使用兴奋剂而被取消资格。

例句：His outburst misqualified him from consideration for the promotion. 他的爆发使他失去了晋升的资格。|近义词：disqualify, bar, preclude |反义词：qualify, entitle |词性: 动词
