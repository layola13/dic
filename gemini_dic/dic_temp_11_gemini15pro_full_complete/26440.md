byproducts:/ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkts/ /ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkts/
n.
副产品；附带产生的结果
Something produced in the making of something else; a secondary result.
例句：
One of the byproducts of unemployment is an increase in crime.
失业的副产品之一是犯罪率上升。

例句：
Buttermilk is a byproduct of making butter.
酪乳是制作黄油的副产品。

近义词：
derivatives, spin-offs, side effects
反义词:
main product, primary product
词性
noun
