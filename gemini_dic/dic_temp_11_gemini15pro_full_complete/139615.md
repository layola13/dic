occasioner:/əˈkeɪʒənər/
n.
引发者，造成者

The occasioner of the accident was never identified.  事故的肇事者从未被确认。
The heavy rain was the occasioner of the flood.  暴雨是洪水的起因。
近义词：cause, author, originator
反义词：preventer
词性: noun
