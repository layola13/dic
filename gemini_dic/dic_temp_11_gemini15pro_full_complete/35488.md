coactive:/koʊˈæktɪv/
adjective
共同作用的；协同的
acting in cooperation or concert.
例句：The coactive efforts of the team led to their success. 团队的共同努力使他们取得了成功。

例句：Coactive learning encourages students to work together. 协作学习鼓励学生一起工作。
synonym: collaborative, cooperative, joint, combined, concerted
antonym: individual, separate, independent, solitary
词性: adjective
