defrayable:/dɪˈfreɪəbl/
adjective
可支付的，可报销的
Capable of being defrayed; payable.
The company will cover all defrayable expenses for the business trip. 公司将承担所有商务旅行的可报销费用。
The museum gratefully accepts donations to defray the defrayable costs of the new exhibit. 博物馆感激地接受捐款，以支付新展览的费用。
reimbursable, payable
non-defrayable, non-reimbursable
adjective
