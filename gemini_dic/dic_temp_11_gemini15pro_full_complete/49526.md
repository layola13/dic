demantoid:/ˈdɛmənˌtɔɪd/ | n. |翠榴石，翠绿榴石 | A rare and highly valued green gemstone, a variety of andradite garnet.  It is known for its brilliant, emerald-like brilliance. |  This ring features a stunning demantoid garnet surrounded by diamonds. 这枚戒指的特点是一颗令人惊艳的翠榴石，周围环绕着钻石。

Demantoid garnets are prized for their intense green color. 翠榴石因其浓郁的绿色而备受珍视。 | 近义词:  emerald, tsavorite | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Demant* (meaning "diamond") + *-oid* (meaning "resembling").  The name refers to its diamond-like brilliance.
