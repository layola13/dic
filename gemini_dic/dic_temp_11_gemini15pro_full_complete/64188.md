equestrian:/ɪˈkwɛstriən/ /ɪˈkwɛstriən/| adj. n.|骑马的，骑术的；骑手|Relating to horseback riding; a person who rides horses.|例句：She has always had an equestrian interest. 她一直对骑马很感兴趣。|例句：He's a skilled equestrian. 他是一位技艺娴熟的骑手。|近义词：rider, horseman, horsewoman|反义词:pedestrian|词性:形容词, 名词
词源:From Latin *equester* (mounted, belonging to a knight), from *eques* (horseman), from *equus* (horse).
