graininess:/ˈɡreɪnɪnɪs/ | n. | 粒状，颗粒感，粗糙 | The graininess of the photo was due to the high ISO setting.  照片的颗粒感是由于高 ISO 设置造成的。 | The old film had a certain graininess that added to its charm.  老式胶片有一种特殊的颗粒感，增加了它的魅力。| 近义词:granularity, roughness, texture | 反义词:smoothness, fineness | noun

graininess:/ˈɡreɪnɪnɪs/ | n. | 模糊不清，不确定性 | There's a certain graininess to the future, a lack of clarity about what might happen. 未来充满了不确定性，对可能发生的事情缺乏清晰的认识。 | The graininess of the evidence made it difficult to reach a definitive conclusion. 证据的模糊性使得难以得出明确的结论。 | 近义词:vagueness, uncertainty, ambiguity | 反义词:clarity, certainty, precision | noun
