demisuit:/ˈdɛmiˌsuːt/
noun
半正式套装
A man's suit less formal than a business suit, often of a lighter fabric or color.
He wore a demisuit to the garden party. 他穿着一套半正式套装去参加花园派对。
Near synonyms: lounge suit, business casual
Antonyms: formal suit, tuxedo
