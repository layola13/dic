boomburb:/ˈbuːmbɜːrb/| n.|快速发展的郊区，繁荣郊区|A rapidly growing suburb, typically located some distance from a major city, and characterized by a mix of residential, commercial, and light industrial development.|The families moved to a boomburb for more space and better schools.  这些家庭为了更大的空间和更好的学校搬到了一个快速发展的郊区。|Synonyms: exurb, outer suburb, edge city|Antonyms: inner city, urban core|noun
