carinaria:/ˌkærɪˈnɛəriə/
n.
carinaria, heteropod
A genus of pelagic marine gastropod mollusks with a transparent, gelatinous body and a small, cap-like shell.  They are sometimes called glass snails or sea elephants.
Carinaria lamarckii is a species found in tropical and subtropical waters.  Carinaria are active predators, feeding on other pelagic animals.
The carinaria gracefully propelled itself through the water.  这只玻璃蜗牛优雅地在水中推进。
Carinaria japonica is another species of this genus.  Carinaria japonica是该属的另一个物种。
近义词:heteropod, glass snail, sea elephant
反义词:None
词性:noun
