kamp:/kæmp/ | n. | 营地，帐篷，阵营 | A place where people stay in tents or other temporary shelters, usually for recreation or military purposes. | We set up our kamp near the river. 我们在河边扎营。| synonyms: campsite, campground, bivouac | antonyms: city, home | noun

kamp:/kæmp/ | v. | 露营，扎营 | To stay in a tent or temporary shelter, usually outdoors. | We kamped in the mountains for a week. 我们在山里露营了一个星期。| synonyms: camp, tent, bivouac | antonyms: reside, dwell | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From Dutch *kamp*, from French *camp*, from Latin *campus* meaning "field, plain".
