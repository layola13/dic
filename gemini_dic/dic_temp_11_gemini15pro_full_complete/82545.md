guang:/ɡwɑːŋ/ | n. | 光芒，光 | Radiance, brilliance, or light emitted. | The guang of the setting sun painted the sky in vibrant hues. 夕阳的光芒将天空染上了鲜艳的色彩。| 近义词: radiance, brilliance, glow, light, shine | 反义词: darkness, dullness | 词性: 名词

词源: "Guang" is a transliteration of the Chinese word 光 (guāng), meaning "light" or "ray."  It has no direct etymology in English and is borrowed directly from Mandarin Chinese.
