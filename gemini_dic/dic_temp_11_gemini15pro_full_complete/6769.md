anamorphosis:/ˌænəmɔːrˈfoʊsɪs/| n. |变形，失真；变形图像，变形物|A distorted projection or drawing that appears normal when viewed from a particular point or with a suitable mirror or lens.  A change of form or structure in an organism. |例句：The artist created an anamorphosis of a skull on the pavement. 艺术家在人行道上创作了一个头骨的变形图像。

例句：The tadpole undergoes anamorphosis into a frog. 蝌蚪经历变形成为青蛙。|近义词：distortion, deformation, metamorphosis|反义词: conformity, regularization |词性:noun
