bullpout:/ˈbʊlˌpaʊt/ (US), /ˈbʊlpaʊt/ (UK)| n.| 牛头鱼，闷葫芦，生闷气的人 |A bottom-dwelling fish with a large head and a wide mouth, also a person who is sullen and uncommunicative. |例句：The bullpout lay motionless on the riverbed. 牛头鱼一动不动地躺在河床上。

例句：He's a real bullpout; he hardly ever speaks. 他真是个闷葫芦，几乎不说话。|近义词：sulk, pout, grump, catfish (fish), sculpin (fish) |反义词：extrovert, chatterbox| noun
