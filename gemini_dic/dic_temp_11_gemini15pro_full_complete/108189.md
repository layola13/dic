latticewise:/ˈlætɪswaɪz/| adv.| 格状地；像格子一样| In a manner resembling a lattice; in a grid-like pattern. | The garden was planted latticewise with rows of colorful flowers. 花园里种满了五颜六色的花，像格子一样排列。| 近义词: gridwise, crisscross, checkerwise | 反义词: randomly, haphazardly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:  From "lattice" (from Old French *lattis, from latte "lath") + "-wise" (Old English -wīs "manner, direction").
