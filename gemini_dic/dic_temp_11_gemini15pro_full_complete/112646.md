lowbell:/ˈloʊˌbel/| n.|牛铃；低沉的铃声|A bell, typically a large one, hung around the neck of a grazing animal, especially a cow, producing a low, resonant sound. |The lowbell clanged as the cow ambled across the field. 牛在田野中漫步时，牛铃发出叮当声。|Synonyms: cowbell, cattle bell, stock bell|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps silence, high-pitched bell)|noun
