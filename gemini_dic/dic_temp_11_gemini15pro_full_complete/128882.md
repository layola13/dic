nilotic:/naɪˈlɒtɪk/
adjective
尼罗河的；尼罗河流域的
Relating to the Nile River or the Nile Valley, or to the people who live there.
The Nilotic languages are spoken in the Nile basin. 尼罗河流域语言在尼罗河流域使用。
The Nilotic peoples are known for their tall stature.  尼罗河流域的人们以身材高大而闻名。
synonyms:Nile, riverine
antonyms:none
词性:adjective
