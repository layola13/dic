dst:/ˌdiːɛsˈtiː/ | n. |夏令时（Daylight Saving Time）| Daylight Saving Time, the practice of advancing clocks during summer months so that evening daylight lasts longer. | Example: DST starts in March and ends in November. 夏令时从三月开始，到十一月结束。| Synonym: summer time | Antonym: standard time | noun

dst:/ˌdiːɛsˈtiː/ | abbreviation |目的地（destination）| Destination, the place to which someone or something is going or being sent. | Example: Please enter your dst in the navigation system.  请在导航系统中输入您的目的地。| Synonym: terminus, goal | Antonym: origin, source | abbreviation
