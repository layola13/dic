Example 2: She had a loathful expression on her face after stepping in something sticky. 她踩到黏糊糊的东西后，脸上露出了厌恶的表情。|Synonyms: abhorrent, detestable, repugnant, hateful, odious|Antonyms: appealing, attractive, pleasant, delightful|adjective
