admixture:/ædˈmɪkstʃər/ | n. | 混合；混合物；掺杂 |  The building is an admixture of various architectural styles.  这栋建筑是多种建筑风格的混合体。 | Example: The recipe calls for an admixture of spices.  这个食谱需要混合各种香料。 | Synonyms: blend, mixture, compound, combination | Antonyms: separation, division, purity | noun

admix:/ædˈmɪks/ | v. |  混合；掺杂 |  Admix the dry ingredients before adding the liquids. 在加入液体之前先混合干的配料。 | Example:  She admixed a small amount of poison into his drink.  她在他的饮料里掺了一点毒药。 | Synonyms: blend, mix, combine, mingle | Antonyms: separate, divide, extract | verb
