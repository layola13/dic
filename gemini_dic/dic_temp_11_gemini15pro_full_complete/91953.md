hypermodern:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɒdən/ (UK) /ˌhaɪpərˈmɑːdərn/ (US)| adj.|超现代的，极端现代主义的|Characterized by an unconventional or extreme approach to modernism, especially in chess, rejecting established principles in favor of dynamic and often provocative strategies.|The hypermodern opening in chess sacrifices the center early on to control it from a distance.|近义词：avant-garde, ultramodern, cutting-edge|反义词：traditional, conventional, classical|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From hyper- + modern.  "Hyper-" is a Greek prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessive."
