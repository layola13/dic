driers:/ˈdraɪərz/| n.|干燥剂；烘干机；使干燥的人（或物）|Substances or devices used to remove moisture, or people/things that cause drying.|The paint driers helped the artwork dry quickly. 这些油画干燥剂帮助画作快速干燥。|近义词：desiccants, dehydrators, drying agents|反义词：humidifiers, moisteners|noun
