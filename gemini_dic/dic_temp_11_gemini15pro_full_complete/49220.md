delegacy: /ˈdɛlɪɡəsi/ | n. | 代表团，派遣代表; (正式地) 委托，授权 | A group of people who are chosen to represent a larger group, especially at a meeting or conference; the act of giving someone the authority to do something for you. |  The trade union sent a delegacy to the national conference. 工会派了一个代表团参加全国会议。|  He has the delegacy to negotiate a new contract on our behalf. 他被授权代表我们谈判一份新合同。 | synonyms: delegation, mission, deputation, representatives | antonyms:  retention (of power),  centralization | noun
