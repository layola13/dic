overoxidizes:/ˌōvərˈɒksɪdaɪzɪz/ | v. | 过度氧化 | To oxidize something excessively, beyond the desired or optimal level.  The process of combining with oxygen to an excessive degree. | The chemist accidentally overoxidized the compound, ruining the experiment. 化学家不小心过度氧化了化合物，毁了实验。| The iron overoxidized, forming a thick layer of rust. 铁过度氧化，形成了厚厚的一层锈。 | synonyms: overburn, hyperoxidize | antonyms: reduce | verb
