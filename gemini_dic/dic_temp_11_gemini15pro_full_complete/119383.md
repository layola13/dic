microdegree:/ˈmaɪkroʊdɪɡriː/
n.
微学位，微证书
A mini-qualification, less extensive than a full degree, that focuses on a specific skill set.  It is often offered online and designed to address industry needs.
She earned a microdegree in data analysis to enhance her job prospects. 她获得了数据分析的微学位，以提高她的就业前景。
He completed a microdegree in web development in just six months. 他在短短六个月内完成了网络开发的微学位。
近义词：nanodegree, certification, mini-qualification
反义词：degree, diploma
