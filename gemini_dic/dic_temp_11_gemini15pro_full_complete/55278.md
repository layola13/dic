dissipator:/ˈdɪsɪpeɪtər/| n.|散热器，消散器|A device or material that absorbs or disperses energy, especially heat.|The computer's CPU has a large heat dissipator to prevent overheating. 电脑的中央处理器有一个大型散热器以防止过热。|Synonyms: heat sink, radiator, diffuser|Antonyms: insulator, retainer|noun


dissipators:/ˈdɪsɪpeɪtərz/| n.|散热器，消散器（复数）|Plural form of dissipator, referring to multiple devices or materials that absorb or disperse energy, especially heat.|The server room requires multiple dissipators to manage the heat generated by the equipment.  服务器机房需要多个散热器来管理设备产生的热量。|Synonyms: heat sinks, radiators, diffusers|Antonyms: insulators, retainers|noun
