ideative:/aɪˈdiːətɪv/
adj.
构思的，观念的
Relating to the formation of ideas; conceptual.  Pertaining to ideas; existing in or characterized by ideas.
His ideative powers are very strong. 他的构思能力很强。
Synonyms: conceptual, imaginative, creative, innovative
Antonyms: unimaginative, uncreative, practical
词性: 形容词
