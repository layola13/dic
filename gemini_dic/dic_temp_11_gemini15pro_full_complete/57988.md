driveler:/ˈdrɪvələr/| n.|胡言乱语者，说废话的人|Someone who talks nonsense or childishly; a person who drivels.|He dismissed his opponent's speech as the ramblings of a driveler. 他认为对手的演讲只是一个胡言乱语者的呓语。|近义词：babbler, prattler, windbag|反义词:sage, scholar|词性:noun

drivel:/ˈdrɪvəl/| n. vi.|胡言乱语，废话|Silly or meaningless talk; to talk nonsense or childishly.|He was driveling on about some conspiracy theory. 他在胡言乱语地谈论某种阴谋论。|近义词：nonsense, twaddle, balderdash|反义词:sense, wisdom|词性:noun, intransitive verb
