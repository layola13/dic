hypercatharsis:/ˌhaɪpərˈkəθɑːrsɪs/
n.
过度宣泄，极度净化
An extreme purgation or release of emotional tension, often through artistic expression or intense experience.
The play provided a sense of hypercatharsis for the audience, leaving them emotionally drained but strangely refreshed. 这部戏为观众提供了一种过度宣泄的感觉，让他们情绪枯竭，但又感到奇怪的清爽。
近义词：catharsis, purgation, release
反义词：repression, suppression
词性：名词

Etymology:
From hyper- + catharsis.  "Hyper-" is a Greek prefix meaning "over, above, beyond," and "catharsis" comes from the Greek "katharsis," meaning "purification" or "cleansing."
