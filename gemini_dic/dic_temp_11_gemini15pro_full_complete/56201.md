dodgiest:/ˈdɒdʒiɪst/| adj.|最可疑的，最狡猾的，最不可靠的|Superlative form of dodgy: most dodgy.  Used to describe something or someone that seems suspicious, unreliable, or likely to be illegal or dishonest.|That guy looks a bit dodgy, I wouldn't trust him with my money. 那家伙看起来有点可疑，我不会把我的钱托付给他。|The dodgiest pub in town was known for its watered-down beer and frequent brawls. 城里最破旧的酒吧以其掺水的啤酒和频繁的斗殴而闻名。|Synonyms: suspicious, shady, shifty, dubious, unreliable|Antonyms: reliable, trustworthy, reputable|adjective
