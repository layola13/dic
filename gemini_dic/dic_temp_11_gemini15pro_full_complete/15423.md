bacteriologists:/ˌbækˌtɪəriˈɒlədʒɪsts/ /ˌbækˌtɪriˈɑːlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 细菌学家 | Scientists who study bacteria.  A bacteriologist researches the growth, structure, development, and other properties of bacteria. | The bacteriologists identified the strain of bacteria causing the outbreak. 细菌学家们确定了引起疫情爆发的细菌菌株。 |  The bacteriologists cultured the bacteria in a petri dish. 细菌学家在培养皿中培养细菌。 | synonyms: microbiologists, germ specialists | antonyms: N/A | noun
