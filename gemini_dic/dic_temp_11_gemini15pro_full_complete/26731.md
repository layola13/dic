cadi:/ˈkɑːdi/ /ˈkeɪdi/ | n. |球童|A person who carries a golfer's clubs. |He worked as a cadi at the local golf course during the summer. 他夏天在当地的高尔夫球场当球童。|Synonym: caddy|Antonym: golfer|noun
