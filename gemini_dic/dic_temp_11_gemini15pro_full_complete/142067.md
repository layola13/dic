orgeat:/ɔːrˈʒɑːt/ | n. | 杏仁糖浆 | A sweet syrup made from almonds, sugar, and rose water or orange flower water, often used in cocktails. |  Orgeat is a key ingredient in a Mai Tai. 杏仁糖浆是迈泰鸡尾酒的关键成分。

Orgeat adds a unique nutty flavor to desserts. 杏仁糖浆为甜点增添了独特的坚果风味。 | 近义词: almond syrup | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *orgeat*, from Old French *orjat*, from *orge* "barley," because the original drink was made from barley.  The modern version is typically made with almonds.
