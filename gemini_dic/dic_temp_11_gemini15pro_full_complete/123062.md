monetized:/ˈmʌnɪtaɪzd/ | adj. | 已货币化的，已变现的 |  Converted into or used as money; having realized a profit from. | The company monetized its user data by selling it to advertisers.  这家公司通过向广告商出售用户数据实现了盈利。| synonyms: commercialized, capitalized, profited from | antonyms:  unmonetized, unprofitable | part of speech: adjective

monetize:/ˈmʌnɪtaɪz/ | v. | 货币化，变现 |  To earn money from something, especially a website, blog, etc. |  The website monetizes its content through advertising. 该网站通过广告将其内容货币化。| synonyms: commercialize, capitalize, profit from | antonyms: demonetize | part of speech: verb
