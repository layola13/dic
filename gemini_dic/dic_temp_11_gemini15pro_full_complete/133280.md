nonexpansion:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspænʃən/ | n. |非扩张，非膨胀|A transformation on a metric space that does not increase distances between points.  A function is nonexpansive if the distance between the images of any two points is less than or equal to the distance between the original points.|The projection onto a convex set is a nonexpansive mapping.  投影到凸集上是一个非扩张映射。| synonyms: contraction (in some contexts), non-increasing | antonyms: expansion, dilation | noun

nonexpansions:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspænʃənz/ | n. |非扩张，非膨胀（复数）|Multiple instances of transformations on a metric space that do not increase distances between points.|The paper discusses several different nonexpansions and their properties. 这篇论文讨论了几种不同的非扩张及其性质。| synonyms: contractions (in some contexts), non-increases | antonyms: expansions, dilations | noun
