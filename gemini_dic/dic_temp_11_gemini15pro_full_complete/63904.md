epiphanies: /ɪˈpɪfəniz/ | n. |顿悟，感悟| A sudden, intuitive perception of or insight into the reality or essential meaning of something, often initiated by some simple, commonplace occurrence or experience. |  She had an epiphany that changed her life forever. 她顿悟了，这永远地改变了她的人生。| synonyms: realization, insight, revelation, awakening, inspiration | antonyms: confusion, delusion, ignorance | noun

epiphanies: /ɪˈpɪfəniz/ | n. |显现节（指主显节）| A Christian festival, observed on January 6, commemorating the manifestation of Christ to the Gentiles in the persons of the Magi; the Twelfth Day. |  They celebrated Epiphanies with a special church service.  他们以一场特别的教会仪式来庆祝主显节。| synonyms: Twelfth Day, Three Kings' Day | antonyms: N/A | noun
