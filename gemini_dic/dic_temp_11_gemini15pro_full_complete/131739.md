nonconfiscation:/ˌnɒnkɒnfɪˈskeɪʃən/ |n.| 非没收 |The principle of nonconfiscation protects private property from seizure by the government. 非没收原则保护私有财产免受政府的没收。| non-seizure, non-expropriation | confiscation, seizure | noun


Etymology:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "confiscation" (from Late Latin "confiscatio," meaning "seizure")
