factioner:/ˈfækʃənər/| n. |派系斗争者，宗派主义者|A person who promotes internal conflict or division within a group or organization.  A person who belongs to a faction and actively promotes its interests, often at the expense of the larger group.| The factioner was expelled from the party for sowing discord.  这个派系斗争者因为散布不和而被开除党籍。|Synonyms: partisan, sectarian, dissident, schismatic|Antonyms: unifier, conciliator|noun
