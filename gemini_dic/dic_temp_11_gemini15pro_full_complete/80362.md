goldstone:/ˈɡoʊldˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈɡəʊldstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|金砂岩，人造宝石，金星石|A type of glittering glass containing small crystals of metallic copper, which give it a sparkly appearance. It is often used in jewelry and ornaments.  Also, in physics, a massless particle associated with broken symmetry.|例句：She wore a beautiful goldstone necklace. 她戴着一条漂亮的砂金石项链。

例句：Physicists are studying the properties of goldstones. 物理学家正在研究金星石的特性。|近义词：aventurine, sunstone (for the gemstone sense)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


goldstone:/ˈɡoʊldˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈɡəʊldstəʊn/ (UK)| adj.|金砂岩的，金星石的|Made of or resembling goldstone.|例句：The vase was made of goldstone glass.  这个花瓶是用金砂岩玻璃制成的。

例句：She admired the goldstone effect in the painting. 她很欣赏画作中的金砂岩效果。|近义词：glittery, sparkly (in the descriptive sense)|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
