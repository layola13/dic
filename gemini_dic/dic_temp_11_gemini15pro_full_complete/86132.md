heartfelt:/ˈhɑːrtfɛlt/ | adj. | 衷心的，真诚的 |  Deeply and sincerely felt; genuine. | He offered heartfelt condolences to the bereaved family. 他向丧失亲人的家庭表示由衷的慰问。| sincere, genuine, earnest | insincere, artificial, feigned | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From heart + felt.  "Felt" here functions as a past participle used as an adjective, meaning "experienced."  So "heartfelt" literally means "felt in the heart."
