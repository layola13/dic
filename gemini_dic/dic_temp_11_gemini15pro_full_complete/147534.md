overpatriotic:/ˌoʊvərˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪk/
adjective
过度爱国的
Excessively patriotic; showing too much patriotism.
His overpatriotic speeches alienated some members of the audience. 他的过度爱国主义的演讲疏远了一些听众。
The overpatriotic fervor in the stadium was palpable. 体育场内过度爱国的热情显而易见。
近义词: jingoistic, chauvinistic, ultranationalistic
反义词: unpatriotic, apathetic, indifferent
词性: adjective

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "patriotic" (from "patriot" + "-ic")
