outchatter:/ˌaʊtˈtʃætər/
verb
胜过…的讲话；比…说得更多
to talk longer or more loudly than someone else
例句：He outchattered everyone at the party. 他在聚会上比任何人都健谈。

例句：She outchattered him and won the argument. 她比他说得更多，赢得了这场争论。

近义词：outtalk, outspeak
反义词：be quiet, listen

词性
v.
