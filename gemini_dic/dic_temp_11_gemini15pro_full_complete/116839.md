maximally:/ˈmæksɪməli/| adv.|最大程度地，最大限度地|To the greatest possible extent; to the maximum degree. |She wanted to utilize her skills maximally. 她想最大限度地利用她的技能。|例句：The room was maximally utilized for storage.  这个房间被最大限度地利用来储存东西。|近义词：fully, completely, totally, utterly, entirely|反义词:minimally, partly|词性:副词

词源:
From Late Latin *maximalis, from Latin maximus (“greatest”).
