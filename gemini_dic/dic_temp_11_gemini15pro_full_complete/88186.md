hilum:/ˈhaɪləm/| n. | 脐；核；门 |A depression or pit on an organ marking the point of entrance or exit of ducts, vessels, nerves, etc.|The hilum of the lung is where the bronchi, pulmonary arteries, and pulmonary veins enter and leave. 肺门是支气管、肺动脉和肺静脉进出的地方。| synonym: notch, indentation | antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider "projection" in some contexts) | noun
