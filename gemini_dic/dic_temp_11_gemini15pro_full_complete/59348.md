earthship:/ˈɜːrθʃɪp/
n.
地球船，生态建筑
A type of passive solar house that is made of natural and recycled materials.  It is designed to be off-grid and self-sufficient.
The earthship was built using tires, cans, and bottles.  这艘地球船是用轮胎、罐头和瓶子建造的。

近义词:eco-house, sustainable home
反义词:conventional house, traditional building
词性:noun
