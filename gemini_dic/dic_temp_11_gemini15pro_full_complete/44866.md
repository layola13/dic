例句：Identifying the crux of the problem is the first step to solving it.  找出问题的症结是解决问题的第一步。|近义词：core, heart, essence, gist, nub, kernel|反义词:periphery, exterior, surface|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *crux*, meaning "cross, torture, affliction".  The sense of "a vital or decisive point" arose from the image of a crossroad.
