customizable:/ˈkʌstəˌmaɪzəbl/| adj.|可定制的，可个性化的|Able to be changed to suit a particular individual or task.|This software is customizable to fit the needs of different businesses. 这个软件可以定制以满足不同企业的需要。|例句：The car is customizable, allowing you to choose the color, interior, and features. 这辆车是可定制的，你可以选择颜色、内饰和功能。|近义词：adaptable, modifiable, tailor-made, personalized|反义词：fixed, inflexible, standardized|词性:形容词
