accosts:/əˈkɒsts/ /əˈkɑːsts/| vt.|搭讪，上前和（陌生人）攀谈，质问，责难|To approach and speak to someone, often in a bold or aggressive way, especially a stranger, typically to ask for something or to make a complaint.|例句：A man accosted me on the street and asked for money. 一个男人在街上拦住我，向我要钱。

例句：She was accosted by a group of journalists as she left the court. 她离开法庭时，一群记者上前质问她。|近义词：approach, address, confront, hail, buttonhole|反义词：avoid, shun, ignore|词性：动词
