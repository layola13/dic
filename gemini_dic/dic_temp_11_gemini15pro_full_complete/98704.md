insupportableness:/ˌɪnsəˈpɔːrtəbəlnəs/
n.
无法忍受；难以忍受
The insupportableness of the situation drove her to tears.  这种情况的难以忍受使她泪流满面。
The insupportableness of the pain made him cry out. 疼痛的难以忍受使他哭了出来。
Synonyms: intolerableness, unbearableness, unendurableness
Antonyms:  tolerableness, bearableness,  supportability
Etymology: From in- (not) + supportable + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin supportare (to support).
