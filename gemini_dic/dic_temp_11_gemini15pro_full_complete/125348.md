multilingualisms: /ˌmʌltiˈlɪŋɡwəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 多语现象；多语制 | The coexistence of multiple languages within a society or individual.  |  The city's multilingualisms are a reflection of its diverse population.  这座城市的多语现象反映了其人口的多样性。 | Synonyms: multilingualism, plurilingualism | Antonyms: monolingualism | noun

multilingualisms: /ˌmʌltiˈlɪŋɡwəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 多语能力 | The ability to use multiple languages. |  Her multilingualisms are a great asset in her career. 她的多语能力是她职业生涯中的一大优势。| Synonyms: multilingualism, plurilingualism | Antonyms: monolingualism | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (many) + lingual (relating to language) + -ism (a doctrine, belief, or practice). Ultimately from Latin *multus* (many) and *lingua* (tongue, language).
