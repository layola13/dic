carunculous: /kəˈrʌŋkjʊləs/ | adj. | 肉质的，似肉瘤的 |  fleshy, resembling a small fleshy outgrowth; having caruncles. | The rooster's comb is a carunculous appendage.  公鸡的鸡冠是一个肉质的附属物。|  The turkey's wattle is a carunculous growth. 火鸡的肉垂是一个肉质的生长物。 | 近义词: fleshy, wattled | 反义词: smooth | 词性: 形容词

caruncle: /ˈkærʌŋkl/ | n. | 小肉瘤，肉赘 | a small, fleshy outgrowth on the body, especially on the head of a bird. | The bird had a prominent caruncle on its beak. 这只鸟的喙上有一个突出的肉瘤。|  Caruncles are common on certain breeds of chickens. 肉瘤在某些品种的鸡身上很常见。 | 近义词: wattle, papilla | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词
