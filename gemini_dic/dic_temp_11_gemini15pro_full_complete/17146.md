battleaxe:/ˈbæt(ə)lˌæks/| n.|战斧；泼妇；悍妇|A large axe formerly used as a weapon, especially in medieval times.  A formidable or domineering woman.|She wielded her words like a battleaxe, cutting down anyone who dared to disagree. 她言辞犀利如战斧，驳倒任何胆敢反对的人。|Synonyms: shrew, virago, harridan|Antonyms: pushover, doormat|noun
