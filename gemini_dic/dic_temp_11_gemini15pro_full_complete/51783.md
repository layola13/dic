devotee:/ˌdɛvəˈtiː/
noun
信徒，爱好者，热衷者
A devotee is a person who is very enthusiastic about something.
例句：He is a devotee of classical music. 他是古典音乐的爱好者。

例句：She's a devotee of the yoga lifestyle. 她是一位瑜伽生活方式的爱好者。

近义词：fan, enthusiast, admirer, follower
反义词：critic, detractor
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *dévoté*, past participle of *dévoter* "to dedicate, vow," from Latin *dēvōtus*, past participle of *dēvovēre* "to dedicate, vow, curse," from *dē-* "away, down" + *vovēre* "to vow."
