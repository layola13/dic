monopolist:/məˈnɑpəlɪst/ | n. | 垄断者，独占者 | A person or company that has a monopoly. |  Example 1: The monopolist controlled the entire market for that particular product.  这个垄断者控制了那种特定产品的整个市场。

Example 2:  The government is investigating the alleged monopolist for unfair business practices. 政府正在调查这个被指控的垄断者是否存在不正当商业行为。 | 近义词:  monopolizer, trust, cartel | 反义词: competitor | 词性: noun


monopolize:/məˈnɑpəlaɪz/ | v. | 垄断，独占 | To have or take control of the entire supply or trade in a commodity or service. | Example 1: The company monopolized the banana trade on the island. 这家公司垄断了岛上的香蕉贸易。

Example 2: He tends to monopolize the conversation at dinner parties. 他倾向于在晚宴上垄断谈话。 | 近义词: control, dominate, corner | 反义词: share, compete | 词性: verb


monopoly:/məˈnɑpəli/ | n. | 垄断，独占 | The exclusive possession or control of the supply or trade in a commodity or service. | Example 1: The government granted the company a monopoly on tobacco sales. 政府授予了该公司烟草销售的垄断权。

Example 2:  The board game Monopoly is named after this economic concept.  棋盘游戏“大富翁”就是以这个经济概念命名的。 | 近义词:  exclusivity, control, domination | 反义词: competition, free market | 词性: noun


monopolistic:/məˌnɑpəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 垄断的，独占的 | Relating to or constituting a monopoly. | Example 1: The company engaged in monopolistic practices to eliminate competition. 这家公司采取垄断行为来消除竞争。

Example 2: The government is trying to regulate monopolistic industries. 政府正试图对垄断行业进行监管。 | 近义词: exclusive, controlling, dominating | 反义词: competitive | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *monopolium, from Greek monopōlion, from monos "alone, single" + pōlein "to sell."

PHRASE->
1. have a monopoly on (拥有...的垄断权): The company has a monopoly on the production of this drug. 这家公司拥有这种药物的生产垄断权。
2. break up a monopoly (打破垄断): The government is trying to break up the monopoly in the telecommunications sector. 政府正试图打破电信行业的垄断。
3. create a monopoly (创造垄断):  Their aggressive business practices created a monopoly in the market. 他们激进的商业行为在市场上创造了垄断。
4. near monopoly (接近垄断): The company enjoys a near monopoly in the local market.  该公司在当地市场享有近乎垄断的地位。
5. accuse of monopoly (指控垄断):  The company was accused of monopoly and unfair competition.  该公司被指控垄断和不正当竞争。
