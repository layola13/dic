baritonal: /ˌbærɪˈtoʊnl/ | adj. | 男中音的；像男中音的 | Having the range of a baritone; resembling the timbre of a baritone voice. |  His baritonal voice filled the room. 他的男中音嗓音充满了整个房间。|  The cello has a rich, baritonal quality. 大提琴音色浑厚，像男中音。| deep, resonant, rich | high, shrill | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *baritonale*, from *baritono* (baritone) + *-ale* (adjectival suffix).  Ultimately from Greek *βαρύτονος* (barytonos, “deep-sounding”), from *βαρύς* (barys, “heavy, deep”) + *τόνος* (tonos, “tone, pitch”).
