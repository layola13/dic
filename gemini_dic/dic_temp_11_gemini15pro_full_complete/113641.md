machine-gun:/məˈʃiːn ɡʌn/ /məˈʃin ɡʌn/| n. |机关枪|An automatic gun that fires bullets rapidly and continuously as long as the trigger is pressed.|The soldiers used a machine-gun to suppress the enemy fire. 士兵们用机关枪压制敌人的火力。|The sound of machine-gun fire echoed through the valley. 机关枪的声音在山谷中回荡。|近义词：automatic rifle, submachine gun|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps peace symbol?)|noun


machine-gun:/məˈʃiːn ɡʌn/ /məˈʃin ɡʌn/| v. |用机关枪扫射|To shoot with a machine gun.|They machine-gunned the enemy position. 他们用机关枪扫射了敌人的阵地。|The car was machine-gunned by unknown assailants. 汽车被不明身份的袭击者用机关枪扫射。|近义词：strafe, riddle (with bullets)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|verb
