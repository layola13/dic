mooch:/muːtʃ/| v.  n.| 乞讨；闲逛；偷窃；揩油；白吃白喝；(尤指为找乐子或打发时间)闲荡，徘徊| To ask for or obtain (something) without paying for it or intending to return it.  To wander or loiter, especially in search of amusement or distraction.  A person who mooches or cadges.|例句：He always mooches cigarettes off his friends. 他总是向朋友们讨烟抽。

例句：He's been mooching around the house all day. 他整天都在房子里闲逛。|近义词：cadge, sponge, beg, loiter, wander|反义词：give, provide, work|词性：动词，名词

mooch:/muːtʃ/| n.| 乞讨；揩油；白吃白喝；闲逛；闲荡| The act of mooching; a person who mooches.|例句：He's always on the mooch for free food. 他总是白吃白喝。

例句：She went for a mooch around the shops. 她去商店闲逛了一圈。|近义词：scrounge, sponger|反义词：donor, provider|词性：名词
