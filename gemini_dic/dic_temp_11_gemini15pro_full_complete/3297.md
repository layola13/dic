afflicted:/əˈflɪktɪd/| adj.|痛苦的，受折磨的|Severely affected by some troublesome condition or problem. |例句：He was afflicted with a rare disease. 他患有一种罕见的疾病。|例句：The afflicted areas received aid from the government. 受灾地区得到了政府的援助。|近义词：troubled, suffering, distressed, plagued|反义词：healthy, well, sound, untroubled|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin afflictus, past participle of affligere "to damage, injure, vex," from ad- "to" (see ad-) + fligere "to strike" (see flagellate).
