nullifies:/ˈnʌlɪfaɪz/
verb
使无效；取消；废除
to make legally null and void; invalidate
The judge nullified the contract. 法官宣布合同无效。
synonyms: invalidate, annul, void, cancel, repeal
antonyms: validate, confirm, ratify, uphold
词性: 动词
