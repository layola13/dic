counter-plea: /ˈkaʊntərˌpliː/
noun
反驳；抗辩
a plea made in reply to another plea, especially one made by a defendant in a legal case in answer to the plaintiff's replication.
The defendant entered a counter-plea of self-defense.  被告提出了自卫的反驳。
The lawyer crafted a compelling counter-plea to the prosecution's arguments. 律师精心设计了一个令人信服的反驳，以回应控方的论点。

synonym: defense, rejoinder, reply, response
antonym: accusation, charge, claim, allegation
n.
