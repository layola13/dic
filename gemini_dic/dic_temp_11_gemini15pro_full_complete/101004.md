invalids:/ˈɪnvəlɪdz/| n.| 病人，残疾人，伤员；无效票，废票|People who are sick or disabled, or things that are not valid. |The hospital cares for many invalids. 这家医院照顾许多病人。|The election was marred by a large number of invalids. 选举因大量废票而受损。|Synonyms: patients, the disabled, the sick, the injured, void, null |Antonyms: healthy people, the able-bodied, valid |noun


invalid:/ɪnˈvælɪd/| adj.| 无效的；病弱的；站不住脚的|Not legally or officially acceptable; (of a person) made weak or disabled by illness or injury; (of an argument or point) not well grounded.|Your passport is invalid. 你的护照无效。|He was an invalid for the last few years of his life. 在他生命的最后几年里，他是一个病弱的人。|Her argument was completely invalid. 她的论点完全站不住脚。|Synonyms: void, null, worthless, disabled, frail, weak, unsound, illogical |Antonyms: valid, legal, sound, healthy, strong, logical |adjective
