interocean:/ˌɪntərˈoʊʃən/ | /ˌɪntəˈrəʊʃən/ | adj. | 海洋间的，大洋间的 | Situated between oceans; connecting oceans. |  The interocean canal facilitated trade between the two continents.  海洋间运河促进了两大洲之间的贸易。 | The interocean currents affect global weather patterns. 洋流影响着全球的天气模式。 | 近义词: transoceanic | 反义词:  coastal, inland | 词性: adjective

词源: From inter- + ocean.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "ocean" refers to a vast body of salt water.
