emerging:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 新兴的，出现的 | Appearing, developing, or becoming known. |  The emerging artist quickly gained recognition.  这位新兴艺术家迅速获得了认可。| 近义词: developing, nascent, burgeoning, evolving | 反义词: declining, fading, disappearing, established | 词性: adjective

emerging:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 紧急的，突发的 (less common) | Arising or occurring suddenly and unexpectedly. | Emerging situations require quick thinking.  突发情况需要快速思考。| 近义词: sudden, urgent, unexpected | 反义词: planned, expected, foreseen | 词性: adjective
