half-radical:/ˌhæf ˈrædɪkəl/ | adj. | 半激进的; 温和改革的 |  Supporting or advocating moderate or partial political or social reform; not fully radical. | The party adopted a half-radical stance on environmental issues, proposing some changes but avoiding more drastic measures. 该党在环境问题上采取了半激进的立场，提出了一些改变，但避免了更激烈的措施。 | Example 2: His half-radical approach to education reform pleased neither the conservatives nor the progressives. 他对教育改革的半激进做法既没有取悦保守派，也没有取悦进步派。| 近义词: moderate, reformist, gradualist | 反义词: radical, extremist, revolutionary | 词性: 形容词

词源:
"half-" (Old English healf) + "radical" (from Late Latin radicalis "of or pertaining to the root").  The term combines the idea of "half" or partial with "radical," meaning fundamental or extreme change.
