nonreimbursements:/ˌnɑːnriːɪmˈbɜːrsmənts/ | n. | 未报销款项，未偿付的费用 | Expenses that are not reimbursed, or the act of not reimbursing expenses. | The company's policy on nonreimbursements is very strict. 公司关于未报销款项的政策非常严格。|  The employees were upset about the nonreimbursements for their travel expenses. 员工们对他们的差旅费未报销感到不满。 | Synonyms: nonpayments, unreimbursed expenses | Antonyms: reimbursements, payments | Noun
