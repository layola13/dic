anticentralist:/ˌæntiˈsɛntrəlɪst/
adjective
反中央集权的；地方分权主义的
Opposed to centralism; favoring decentralization of power.
The anticentralist movement gained traction during the period of political upheaval.  反中央集权运动在政治动荡时期获得了支持。

The governor, a staunch anticentralist, advocated for greater autonomy for the states.  这位州长是一位坚定的反中央集权主义者，主张赋予各州更大的自治权。

Synonym: decentralist, regionalist
Antonym: centralist, federalist
word type: adjective
