nonpercipience:/ˌnɒnpərˈsɪpiəns/
|n.|无知觉; 缺乏洞察力; 缺乏感知|The state of not perceiving or being unaware; lack of perception or insight.|His nonpercipience of the danger led to the accident. 他对危险的无知觉导致了这起事故。|
|近义词: unawareness, insensitivity, obliviousness, ignorance|
|反义词: awareness, perception, sensitivity, consciousness|
|词性: 名词|


nonpercipient:/ˌnɒnpərˈsɪpiənt/
|adj.|无知觉的; 缺乏洞察力的|Not perceiving or being unaware; lacking perception or insight.|He seemed nonpercipient of the tension in the room. 他似乎没有察觉到房间里的紧张气氛。|
|近义词: unaware, insensitive, oblivious, ignorant|
|反义词: aware, perceptive, sensitive, conscious|
|词性: 形容词|


Etymology:
From non- (not) + percipient (perceiving), from Latin percipere (to perceive).
