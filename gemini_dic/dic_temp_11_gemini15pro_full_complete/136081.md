nonrecitals:/ˌnɒnriˈsaɪtəlz/
noun
不提及，不叙述（通常指合同中）
The absence of specific statements or affirmations within a contract.  These omitted statements are intentionally excluded to avoid potential misinterpretations or legal ramifications.
The contract included a clause of nonrecitals regarding previous agreements.  该合同包含一项关于先前协议的不提及条款。
The nonrecitals ensured the contract focused solely on the current terms. 不提及条款确保合同只关注当前条款。
synonyms: omissions, exclusions, exceptions
antonyms: inclusions, stipulations, declarations
n.
