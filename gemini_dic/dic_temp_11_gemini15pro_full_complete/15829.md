balladic:/bəˈlædɪk/| adj.|叙事诗的；民谣的；像民谣的|Resembling or characteristic of a ballad; suggestive of a ballad in subject matter, simplicity, or sentiment.|The song had a balladic quality, telling a story of love and loss.|近义词：narrative, story-like, lyrical, poetic|反义词：modern, contemporary|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From ballad + -ic. Ballad comes from Old French balade, from Occitan balada, from Late Latin *ballare "to dance," from Greek ballizein.
