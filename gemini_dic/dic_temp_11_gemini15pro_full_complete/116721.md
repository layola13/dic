maudlinisms:/ˈmɔːdlɪnɪzəmz/
|n.|感伤主义；滥情；过分的伤感|Excessively sentimental expressions or behavior, often associated with drunkenness or other emotional distress.|He indulged in maudlinisms about his lost love. 他沉溺于对失去的爱情的感伤之中。|近义词：sentimentality, emotionalism, sappiness|反义词：stoicism, reserve, restraint|词性: 名词

maudlin:/ˈmɔːdlɪn/
|adj.|感伤的；多愁善感的|Excessively sentimental, often to the point of being tearful or silly, typically as a result of drunkenness or other emotional distress.|She became maudlin after a few drinks. 她喝了几杯酒后变得多愁善感。|近义词：sentimental, tearful, weepy|反义词：stoic, reserved, unemotional|词性: 形容词
