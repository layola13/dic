clearable:/ˈklɪrəbl/
adjective
可清除的，可清理的
able to be cleared, especially of something unwanted or unpleasant
The virus is clearable with medication. 这种病毒可以通过药物清除。
近义词:removable, eradicable, disposable
反义词:permanent, fixed, ingrained
词性:adjective

clearable:/ˈklɪrəbl/
adjective
可结清的，可清算的
able to be settled, as of debts or accounts
The debt is clearable within a year. 这笔债务可以在一年内结清。
近义词:payable, settleable
反义词:unpayable, outstanding
词性:adjective
