outjourneys:/ˈaʊtˌdʒɜːniz/ | n. | 外出旅行，远行 |  Journeys away from home, especially for a long period or a specific purpose. | They documented their outjourneys in a travel blog.  他们在一个旅游博客上记录了他们的远行。 | 近义词: expeditions, travels, voyages, trips | 反义词: homecomings, returns | 词性: 名词

词源:  A combination of "out" (meaning away from home) and "journeys" (meaning trips).
