gallerylike:/ˈɡæləriˌlaɪk/
adjective
像画廊的；适于画廊展出的
Resembling or suitable for a gallery; having the qualities of an art gallery.
The room was spacious and gallerylike, with high ceilings and plenty of natural light.  这个房间宽敞明亮，像画廊一样，有很高的天花板和充足的自然光。
The minimalist décor gave the apartment a gallerylike feel. 极简主义的装饰使公寓有一种画廊般的感觉。
近义词: museumlike, exhibitionlike
反义词: cluttered, messy
词性: adjective
