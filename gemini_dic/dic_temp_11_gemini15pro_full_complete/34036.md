citronlike:/ˈsɪtrənˌlaɪk/
adj.
类似香橼的，柠檬色的
Resembling a citron in appearance, smell, or taste.
The cake had a citronlike aroma.  蛋糕有一种类似香橼的香味。
The fruit had a citronlike flavor. 这种水果的味道像香橼。
近义词:lemonlike, citrusy
反义词:(No clear antonym exists, possibly bland, tasteless if referring to flavor)
词性:adjective
