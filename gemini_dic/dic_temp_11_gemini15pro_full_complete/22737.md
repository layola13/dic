borofluoride:/ˌbɔːroʊˈflʊəraɪd/| n.|硼氟化物|A compound containing the BF4- anion.|Sodium borofluoride is used as a flux in metal brazing. 硼氟化钠用作金属钎焊中的助熔剂。|Synonyms: tetrafluoroborate|Antonyms: N/A|noun
etymology->
