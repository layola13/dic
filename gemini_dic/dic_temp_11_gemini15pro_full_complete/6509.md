amusedly:/əˈmjuːzɪdli/| adv.| 愉悦地，好笑地|In a way that shows you are amused or think something is funny.|She watched him amusedly as he struggled with the large package. 她看着他费力地搬着大包裹，觉得好笑。|近义词：merrily, playfully, humorously|反义词：seriously, solemnly|词性:副词
词源: From amuse (verb) + -ed (suffix forming past participles and adjectives) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs).  Ultimately from Old French amuser "to divert, entertain," from à "to" + muser "to muse, ponder."
