envois:/ˈɒ̃vwɑːz/ (US), /ˈɒ̃vwaːz/ (UK)| n.|作品寄语; 馈赠; 诗歌; (尤指)附诗;|A literary work, especially a short poem, sent to someone.  Specifically, envois often refer to the short concluding stanzas of certain poetic forms, particularly the French ballade.  More generally, it can refer to anything sent or presented, especially as a gift.|She included a charming envoi with the gift, a short poem expressing her best wishes.|近义词：gift, offering, poem, dedication, message|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
