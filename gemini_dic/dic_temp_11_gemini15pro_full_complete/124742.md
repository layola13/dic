以下是关于 "import" 的解析：

import: /ɪmˈpɔːrt/ (UK) /ˈɪmpɔːrt/ (US) | n. vt. | 进口，输入；重要性；意思，含义 | To bring (goods or services) into a country from abroad for sale. To transfer (data) into a file or document, especially from another application.  |

例句：
The US imports a lot of oil from other countries. 美国从其他国家进口大量石油。

We import data from various sources for our analysis. 我们从各种来源导入数据进行分析。


近义词：bring in, introduce, significance, meaning
反义词: export
词性: noun, verb
