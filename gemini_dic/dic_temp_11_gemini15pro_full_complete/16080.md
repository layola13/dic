bandoleer:/ˌbændəˈlɪər/| n.|子弹带，弹药带|A belt worn over the shoulder and across the chest, typically used to carry ammunition.|The soldier adjusted his bandoleer before heading into battle. 士兵在进入战斗前调整了他的子弹带。|Synonyms: cartridge belt, ammunition belt|Antonyms: None|Noun
