demonological:/ˌdiːmənəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ |adj.|恶魔学的，魔鬼学的|Relating to the study of demons or beliefs about demons. |The book delves into demonological lore and traditions.|Synonyms: demonic, infernal, satanic|Antonyms: angelic, divine|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *daemonologicus*, from Greek *daimōn* (demon) + *-logos* (treating of).
