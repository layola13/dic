lipothymous: /ˌlɪpəˈθaɪməs/ | adj. | 昏厥的，快要晕倒的 | Feeling faint and close to losing consciousness.  Characterized by a sensation of faintness or lightheadedness, often accompanied by weakness and dizziness. |  He felt lipothymous after standing in the sun for too long. 他在太阳底下站得太久，感到快要晕倒了。

The hiker became lipothymous due to dehydration.  由于脱水，徒步旅行者快要晕倒了。| Near synonyms: faint, lightheaded, dizzy, weak, swooning | Antonyms: alert, energetic, vigorous | word class: Adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *leipothumos*, from *leipo-* (leave, fail) + *thumos* (spirit, mind).
