culpable:/ˈkʌlpəbl/ | adj. |应受谴责的；有罪的；有过失的 | deserving blame; guilty of wrongdoing | The driver was found culpable for the accident. 司机被认定应对事故负责。 | The jury found him culpable of murder. 陪审团认定他犯有谋杀罪。|  guilty, blameworthy, responsible | innocent, blameless | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *culpābilis, from Latin culpa ("fault, blame") + -ābilis ("-able, capable of").
