outfeast:/ˌaʊtˈfiːst/ | v. | 宴请胜过，吃得比…多 | To surpass someone in feasting; to eat or drink more than another. | He outfeasted all his guests, consuming a whole boar himself. 他在宴会上吃得比所有客人都多，自己吃掉了一整头野猪。|  We outfeasted our rivals at the celebratory dinner.  我们在庆祝晚宴上吃得比我们的对手多。| 近义词: outdo, surpass, eclipse | 反义词: be outdone, be surpassed | 词性: 动词


词源:

"out-" (prefix) + "feast" (noun/verb)

"out-" 表示超越，胜过。
"feast" 表示盛宴，尽情享用。
