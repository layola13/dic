marmorate:/ˈmɑːrməreɪt/| v. |使成大理石状，使像大理石一样|To vein or stain like marble; to give something a marble-like appearance.|The artist marmorated the clay sculpture to resemble ancient stone. 艺术家将黏土雕塑做成大理石状，使其像古代的石头。|近义词：marbleize, mottle|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑plain, unadorned)|词性:动词

marmorated:/ˈmɑːrməreɪted/| adj. |大理石状的，像大理石一样的|Having the appearance of marble.|The marmorated walls gave the room a regal feel.  大理石状的墙壁赋予房间一种帝王般的感觉。|近义词：marbleized, mottled|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑plain, unadorned)|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *marmoratus*, past participle of *marmorare* (“to overlay with marble”), from *marmor* (“marble”).
