asseverative:/əˈsɜːrtɪv/| adj.|断言的，肯定的|Affirming or stating positively; asserting.|He spoke in an asseverative tone, leaving no room for doubt. 他以肯定的语气说话，不容置疑。|近义词：affirmative, positive, assertive, declaratory|反义词：uncertain, hesitant, doubtful|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *asseverativus, from Latin asseveratus, past participle of asseverare "to affirm, assert earnestly," from ad- "to" (see ad-) + severus "serious, earnest" (see severe).
