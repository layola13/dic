de-Americanized:/diːəˈmɛrɪkənaɪzd/
|adj.|去美国化的，非美国化的|The process of removing American influence or characteristics from something.  It can refer to cultural aspects, economic policies, or political systems.|The government is trying to de-Americanize the education system.|denationalize, deglobalize|Americanize|adjective
