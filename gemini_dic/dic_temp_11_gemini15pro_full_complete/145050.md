overagitation:/ˌoʊvərˌædʒɪˈteɪʃən/
noun
过度激动，过度兴奋
a state of excessive agitation; extreme restlessness and excitement.
The patient exhibited signs of overagitation after the procedure.  手术后，病人表现出过度激动的迹象。
The overagitation of the crowd led to a stampede. 人群的过度激动导致了踩踏事件。
Synonyms:  frenzy, hysteria, overexcitement, restlessness
Antonyms: calmness, composure, tranquility
