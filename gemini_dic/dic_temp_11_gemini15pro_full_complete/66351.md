excursionize:/ɪkˈskɜːʃənaɪz/
verb
进行短途旅行，远足
to go on an excursion; to make a short journey or trip, especially for pleasure.
例句：We excursionized through the countryside, enjoying the scenic views. 我们在乡间远足，欣赏美丽的景色。

例句：They decided to excursionize to the nearby mountains for the weekend. 他们决定周末去附近的山上远足。

近义词：tour, journey, travel, explore, trek, hike
反义词：stay, remain, settle
词性：v.
