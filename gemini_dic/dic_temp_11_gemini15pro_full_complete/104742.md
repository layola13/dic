kauffman:/ˈkɔːfmən/| n. |商人，小贩|(chiefly US) A merchant or trader, especially one of German descent.|Many Pennsylvania Dutch kauffmans prospered in the colonial era. 许多宾夕法尼亚德裔商人在殖民时代繁荣昌盛。|近义词：merchant, trader, businessman, dealer|反义词:customer, buyer|词性:名词

Etymology: From German Kaufmann, literally "merchant," from Kauf "purchase" + Mann "man."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kaupōną "to bargain, buy," and *mann- "man, person."
