beastish:/ˈbiːstɪʃ/| adj.|野兽般的；残忍的；粗野的|Resembling or characteristic of a beast; brutal; savage.|He displayed beastish behavior, snarling and snapping at anyone who came near. 他表现出野兽般的行为，对任何靠近的人都咆哮着，试图咬人。|The dictator's beastish regime terrorized the population.  这个独裁者的残暴统治使民众恐惧。|近义词：animalistic, brutal, savage, ferocious, cruel|反义词：humane, kind, gentle, civilized, compassionate|词性:形容词

词源:
From beast + -ish.  来自 beast（野兽）+ -ish（形容词后缀，表示“像…的”）。
