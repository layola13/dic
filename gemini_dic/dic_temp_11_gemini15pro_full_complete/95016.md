inability's:/ˌɪnəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |无能，无力|The possessive form of "inability," referring to the state or quality of being unable to do something. It highlights that the inability *belongs* to someone or something.  This form is less common than simply using "inability." |  The inability's impact on the team was significant.  这个无能对团队的影响是巨大的。|Synonyms: powerlessness, impotence, incapacity, incompetence | Antonyms: ability, capability, competence, capacity | noun
