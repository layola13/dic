homomorphism:/ˌhɒməˈmɔːfɪzəm/ /ˌhoʊməˈmɔːrfɪzəm/| n. |同态，同态映射|A mapping from one algebraic structure to another of the same type, that preserves the operations of the structures.|The logarithm function is a homomorphism from the multiplicative group of positive real numbers to the additive group of real numbers.|近义词：morphism, mapping|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *homo-* (same) + *-morph-* (form) + *-ismos* (forming nouns of action or condition).
