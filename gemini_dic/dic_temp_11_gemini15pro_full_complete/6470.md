ampullar:/æmˈpʌlər/ | adj. | 瓶状的；烧瓶状的；膨大的 | Relating to or shaped like an ampulla.  | The ampullar part of the fallopian tube is where fertilization usually occurs. | 输卵管的壶腹部通常是受精发生的地方。 |  近义词: flask-shaped, bulbous | 反义词: narrow, tubular | 词性: adjective

ampulla:/æmˈpʌlə/ | n. | 壶腹；安瓿 | A dilated portion of a canal or duct, especially of the semicircular canals of the ear or the vas deferens. Also, a small sealed glass vial containing a solution for injection. | The doctor broke the ampulla to prepare the injection. | 医生打破安瓿准备注射。 | 近义词: vial, ampoule | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


词源:  From Latin *ampulla, diminutive of amphora “jar, jug.”
