deflow:/diːˈfloʊ/ | v. |  向下流动，减少，衰退 | To flow downwards; to decrease or decline. | The river deflowed into the valley. 河流向下流入山谷。| The company's profits have deflowed in recent years.  近年来，该公司的利润一直在下降。| 近义词：decline, decrease, ebb, dwindle | 反义词：increase, rise, grow, surge | 词性: 动词

词源:  "de-" (down) + "flow" (to move in a stream).  Prefix "de-" indicates downward motion or reduction, combined with the common verb "flow."
