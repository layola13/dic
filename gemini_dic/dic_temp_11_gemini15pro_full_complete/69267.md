featheriness:/ˈfeðərɪnɪs/
n.
轻盈；羽毛状；柔软
The quality of being light and airy like a feather; fluffiness.
The featheriness of the down comforter made it incredibly warm.  羽绒被的轻盈使其异常保暖。
lightness, fluffiness, airiness, softness
heaviness, denseness, solidity
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From feather + -iness.
