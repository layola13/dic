imagerially:/ɪˌmædʒɪˈrɪəli/ | adv. | 形象地；想象地 | In a way that relates to or involves mental images. | She described the scene imagerially, making it easy to visualize. 她形象地描述了那个场景，使人很容易想象。 | Example:  He spoke imagerially, painting vivid pictures with his words. 他说话形象生动，用语言描绘出栩栩如生的画面。

Example: The author imagerially depicted the protagonist's inner turmoil. 作者形象地描绘了主人公内心的挣扎。 | Synonyms: figuratively, metaphorically, pictorially, visually | Antonyms: literally, plainly, concretely | Part of Speech: Adverb

Etymology: From "image" (ultimately from Latin "imago") + "-erial" (forming adjectives) + "-ly" (forming adverbs).
