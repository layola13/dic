canaling:/ˈkænəlɪŋ/| n.|引导，通灵，沟渠化|The act of channeling, especially supposed communication with a deceased person, a spirit, etc. 特别指与死者、灵魂等的假定交流的行为。|She claims to be channeling messages from her spirit guide. 她声称正在传达来自她精神导师的信息。|近义词：channelling, communicating, conveying|反义词:blocking, obstructing|词性:名词

canaling:/ˈkænəlɪŋ/| n.|（水）渠化，开凿运河|The process of creating channels or canals, often for irrigation or drainage.  开凿渠道或运河的过程，通常用于灌溉或排水。|The canaling of the river helped control flooding. 该河流的渠化有助于控制洪水。|近义词：channelization, ditching|反义词:filling, damming|词性:名词
