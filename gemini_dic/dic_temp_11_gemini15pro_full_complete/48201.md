deconcentrate:/diːˈkɒnsəntreɪt/ | v. | 分散（注意力）；使分散；降低…的浓度 | To draw away the attention or reduce the concentration of. | The noise deconcentrated me from my studies. 噪音分散了我的学习注意力。 |  The company decided to deconcentrate its operations and open smaller offices in several cities. 公司决定分散业务，在几个城市开设小型办事处。 | 近义词: distract, disperse, diffuse | 反义词: concentrate, focus, centralize | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (expressing reversal) + concentrate.  "Concentrate" comes from Late Latin concentratus, past participle of concentrare "to unite in a center," from com- "together" + centrum "center."
