hyperobtrusiveness:/ˌhaɪpərəbˈtruːsɪvnəs/ | n. | 过分冒昧；过分强加于人 |  The state or quality of being excessively obtrusive; extreme pushiness or forwardness.  It describes someone or something that is overly assertive and intrusive in a way that is unwelcome or annoying. |  Her hyperobtrusiveness made it difficult to have a conversation with anyone else at the party. 她在聚会上过分冒昧，让人很难与其他人交谈。|  pushiness, intrusiveness, forwardness, officiousness |  reserve, discretion, modesty, reticence | noun
