non-native:/ˌnɒnˈneɪtɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˈneɪtɪv/
adjective
非本地的；非母语的
Not belonging originally to a particular place or country; not of native origin.  (Not a person's first language)
例句：He speaks English fluently, but he's a non-native speaker. 他英语说得流利，但他不是母语为英语的人。

例句：Non-native plants can sometimes become invasive.  非本地植物有时会变得具有侵略性。

近义词：foreign, alien, exotic, introduced
反义词：native, indigenous, endemic
词性：adjective
