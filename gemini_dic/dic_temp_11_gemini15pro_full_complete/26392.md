by-the-book:/ˌbaɪ ðə ˈbʊk/
adjective
循规蹈矩的；按部就班的；一丝不苟的
Strictly according to the rules, regulations, or procedures.
The audit was conducted by-the-book, ensuring every procedure was followed correctly. 审计工作是按规定进行的，确保每个程序都正确执行。
He's a by-the-book kind of guy, so don't expect him to bend the rules for you. 他是个一丝不苟的人，所以别指望他会为你破例。

近义词:strict, meticulous, rigorous, inflexible
反义词:unconventional, unorthodox, flexible, lax

词性:adjective
