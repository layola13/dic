entangled:/ɪnˈtæŋɡld/ | adj. | 缠绕的，纠缠的；复杂的，难解的 | Involved in a complicated or confusing situation; twisted together |  The fishing line became entangled in the seaweed.  钓鱼线缠在了海藻里。 | 近义词: tangled, involved, snarled, intertwined | 反义词: disentangled, free, clear | 词性: 形容词

entangled:/ɪnˈtæŋɡld/ | v. | 缠绕，使纠缠；使卷入，使陷入 | Cause to become twisted together with or caught in something; involve (someone) in difficulties or a complicated situation |  The bird became entangled in the net.  这只鸟被网缠住了。 | 近义词: tangle, snarl, intertwine, involve | 反义词: disentangle, extricate, free | 词性: 动词
