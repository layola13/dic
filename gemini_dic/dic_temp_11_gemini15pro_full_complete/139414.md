observing:/əbˈzɜːrvɪŋ/ | adj. | 观察的；注意的；善于观察的 |  Paying close attention to something or someone.  |例句：She has an observing eye for detail. 她有敏锐的观察力，注重细节。| 近义词：watchful, attentive, vigilant | 反义词：inattentive, unobservant | 词性: adjective

observing:/əbˈzɜːrvɪŋ/ | n. | 观察；监视 | The act of watching something or someone carefully. | 例句：The scientist spent years observing the behavior of chimpanzees.  这位科学家花了数年时间观察黑猩猩的行为。 | 近义词：monitoring, surveillance, scrutiny | 反义词：neglect | 词性: noun (gerund)
