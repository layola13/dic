lucking:/ˈlʌkɪŋ/ | adj. | 幸运的; 交好运的; 吉祥的 | Having good luck; fortunate.  Characterized by or resulting from good luck.  | He was lucking out when he found a twenty-dollar bill on the sidewalk. 他在人行道上发现一张二十美元的钞票，真是走运。| 近义词: fortunate, lucky, blessed | 反义词: unlucky, unfortunate, cursed | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "luck" + "-ing" suffix, forming a present participle used adjectivally.  "Luck" itself comes from Middle Dutch *luc, meaning "happiness, fortune."
