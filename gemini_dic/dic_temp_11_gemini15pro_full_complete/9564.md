antiskeptical:/ˌæntiˈskɛptɪkəl/
| adj. |反怀疑论的；对抗怀疑论的|Opposed to or resisting skepticism; showing a tendency to believe readily.|He had an antiskeptical attitude, accepting most claims without rigorous questioning. 他有一种反怀疑论的态度，几乎不加严格质疑地接受大多数主张。|Near Synonyms: credulous, trusting, unquestioning|Antonyms: skeptical, doubting, questioning
|词性: 形容词
