gruffed: /ɡrʌft/
verb
粗声地说; 低声咆哮
to say something in a rough, low voice
"Get out!" he gruffed. “滚出去！”他粗声说道。
近义词: growled, grumbled, muttered
反义词: whispered, murmured
词性: verb

Etymology:  From gruff (adjective) + -ed (verb suffix).  "Gruff" likely originated as an imitative word, echoing a rough, low sound.
