abstractionists:/æbˈstrækʃənɪsts/
n.
抽象主义者
Artists who create abstract art, focusing on form, color, and composition rather than realistic representation.
The abstractionists of the early 20th century revolutionized the art world.  20世纪早期的抽象主义者彻底改变了艺术界。
近义词:nonrepresentational artists, abstract artists
反义词:realists, representational artists
词性:noun (plural)


abstractionist:/æbˈstrækʃənɪst/
n.
抽象主义者
An artist who practices abstractionism.
He was a renowned abstractionist, known for his bold use of color. 他是一位著名的抽象主义者，以大胆运用色彩而闻名。
近义词:nonrepresentational artist, abstract artist
反义词:realist, representational artist
词性:noun (singular)


ETYMOLOGY->
From abstraction + -ist.  Abstraction from Latin abstractus, past participle of abstrahere "to draw away," from ab- "away" + trahere "to draw."
