omnipotently:/ˌɒmˈnɪpətəntli/ | adv. | 全能地；无所不能地 | In a manner suggesting unlimited power.  |  He spoke omnipotently, as if he controlled the very universe. 他无所不能地说着，仿佛他掌控着整个宇宙。 | Example: God is often described as acting omnipotently. 上帝常被描述为无所不能地行事。| Synonyms: all-powerfully, almighty | Antonyms: powerlessly, weakly | Part of Speech: Adverb

omnipotent:/ɒmˈnɪpətənt/ | adj. | 全能的；无所不能的 | Having unlimited power; able to do anything. |  God is omnipotent. 上帝是全能的。| Example:  She believes in an omnipotent being who controls everything. 她相信一个掌控一切的全能的存在。 | Synonyms: all-powerful, almighty | Antonyms: powerless, weak | Part of Speech: Adjective
