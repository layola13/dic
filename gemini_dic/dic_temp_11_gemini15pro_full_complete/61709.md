emoter:/ɪˈmoʊtər/ | n. | 表情符号使用者;感情外露者 |  A person who expresses emotions readily or excessively, especially online. | He's a prolific emoter on social media, always using emojis and reacting to posts. 他是一个在社交媒体上非常爱用表情符号的人，总是使用表情符号并对帖子做出反应。 | 近义词: expresser, displayer | 反义词: inhibitor, suppressor | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From the verb "emote" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Emote" comes from the back-formation of "emotion," ultimately from French "émouvoir" (to stir up, excite).
