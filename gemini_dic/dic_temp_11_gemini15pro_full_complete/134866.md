nonmonetarists:/ˌnɒnˈmʌnɪtərɪsts/ | n. | 非货币主义者 | Economists who don't believe that changes in the money supply are the primary driver of economic activity. |  Many nonmonetarists argue that fiscal policy is more effective than monetary policy. 许多非货币主义者认为财政政策比货币政策更有效。| synonyms: Keynesians, fiscalists | antonyms: monetarists | noun
