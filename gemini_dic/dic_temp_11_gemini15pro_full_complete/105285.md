kiaa: /ˈkiːɑː/ | n. | 基因 |  KIAA is an abbreviation often used in gene names, particularly those identified in large-scale sequencing projects.  It stands for "KIAA" followed by a number, representing the order in which the gene was identified in a particular study. These genes often have unknown functions initially. | Example: The function of the KIAA0319 gene is being researched in connection with dyslexia. 基因KIAA0319的功能正在被研究与阅读障碍的联系。 | Example:  Scientists are working to determine the role of various KIAA genes in human development. 科学家们正在努力确定各种KIAA基因在人类发育中的作用。 | Synonym: gene, (sometimes) protein | Antonym: N/A | noun
