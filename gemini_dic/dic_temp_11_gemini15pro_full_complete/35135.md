cloud:/klaʊd/| n.|云；云状物；阴影；一大群|A visible mass of condensed water vapor floating in the atmosphere, typically high above the ground.|例句：The sun was hidden behind the clouds. 太阳躲在云的后面。|近义词：mist, fog, haze|反义词:sunshine, clear sky|Noun

clouds:/klaʊdz/| n.|云|Plural form of "cloud".  A visible mass of condensed water vapor floating in the atmosphere, typically high above the ground.|例句：Dark clouds gathered in the sky.  乌云密布。|近义词：mist, fog, haze|反义词:sunshine, clear skies|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *clud, akin to clod.  Originally referred to a mass of rock or hill. The sense transfer to atmospheric clouds happened later.
