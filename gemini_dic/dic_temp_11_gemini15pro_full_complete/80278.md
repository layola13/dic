going-over:/ˈɡəʊɪŋ ˈəʊvər/| n.|检查，复习，演练|A thorough examination or review of something.|例句：The car needs a going-over before we take it on a long trip. 这辆车在长途旅行前需要彻底检查一下。

例句：We had a going-over of the plan before we put it into action.  我们在实施计划之前对它进行了仔细的审查。|近义词：inspection, review, examination, rehearsal|反义词: neglect, disregard|词性:名词
