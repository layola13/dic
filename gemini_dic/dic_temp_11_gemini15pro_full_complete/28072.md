capex:/ˈkæpɛks/| n. |资本支出|Capital Expenditure, funds used by a company to acquire, upgrade, and maintain physical assets such as property, industrial buildings, or equipment.|The company's capex for the new factory was substantial. 该公司新工厂的资本支出相当大。|Example:  Investing in new machinery is a significant capex for any manufacturing business.  投资新机器对任何制造企业来说都是一笔重要的资本支出。|Synonyms: capital spending, capital investment|Antonyms: operating expenses (opex)|noun
