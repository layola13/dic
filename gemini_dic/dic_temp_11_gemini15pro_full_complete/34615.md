clee:/kliː/
n.
爪
A bird's claw.
例句：The eagle grasped its prey with its powerful clees.  老鹰用它强有力的爪子抓住了猎物。

例句：The cat sharpened its clees on the tree trunk. 猫在树干上磨爪子。

近义词：claw, talon
反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical attribute like "clee")

词性: noun
