noncaring:/nɒnˈkɛərɪŋ/ | adj. |漠不关心的，冷淡的 |  Not displaying sympathy, concern, or interest; lacking in care or compassion. | He had a noncaring attitude towards his studies. 他对学习漠不关心。|  indifferent, apathetic, uncaring, callous, heartless | caring, compassionate, concerned, sympathetic | adjective
Etymology-> From non- + caring.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without." "Caring" comes from the Middle English "carien," meaning "to be anxious or solicitous."
