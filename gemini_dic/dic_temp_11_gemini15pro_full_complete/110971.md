liquidambar:/ˈlɪkwɪdˌæmbər/| n. |枫香树；枫香树脂；苏合香|A type of tree known for its star-shaped leaves and fragrant resin.  It's also the name of the resin itself, often called sweetgum.|The leaves of the liquidambar turn vibrant shades of red and orange in the fall. 秋天，枫香树的叶子会变成鲜艳的红色和橙色。
The liquidambar resin has a distinct, sweet aroma. 枫香树脂有一种独特的甜香味。|近义词：sweetgum, redgum, stargum|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *liquidus ambar* "liquid amber," referring to the fragrant resin.  *Liquidus* means "liquid" and *ambar* comes from Arabic *'anbar* meaning "ambergris," later applied to other fragrant resins.
