cirsoid:/ˈsɜːrˌsɔɪd/ | adj. | 蔓状的，曲张的 | Resembling a varix; varicose or dilated.  Characterized by a dilated and tortuous course, as of blood vessels. | The patient presented with a cirsoid aneurysm.  病人出现蔓状动脉瘤。| The surgeon carefully examined the cirsoid veins.  外科医生仔细检查了曲张的静脉。 |近义词：varicose, tortuous, dilated |反义词: straight, normal |词性: 形容词

词源: From French *cirsoïde*, from Latin *cirsoīdēs*, from Greek *kirs(o)-* (varix) + *-eidēs* (-oid).
