loon:/luːn/
n.|潜鸟；疯子|A large waterbird with a short tail, webbed feet, and a black and white plumage. Also, an informal term for a crazy person.|例句：We saw a loon diving for fish on the lake. 我们看到一只潜鸟在湖中潜水捕鱼。|近义词：crazy person, lunatic|反义词:sane person|词性:名词

loon:/luːn/
n.|笨蛋|An informal term for a stupid person.|例句：He acted like such a loon at the party. 他在派对上表现得像个笨蛋。|近义词：fool, idiot|反义词:genius|词性:名词
