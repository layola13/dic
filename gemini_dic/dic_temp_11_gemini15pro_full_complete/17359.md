beacon:/ˈbiːkən/ (US) /ˈbiːkən/ (UK)| n.|灯塔，信号灯，烽火，指路明灯|A fire or light set up in a high or prominent position as a warning, signal, or celebration. |The lighthouse beacon swept across the dark sea. 灯塔的光束扫过黑暗的大海。|synonyms: signal, light, flare, warning, guide |antonyms: obscurity, darkness|noun


beacon:/ˈbiːkən/ (US) /ˈbiːkən/ (UK)| v.|指引，照亮|To guide or summon with a beacon.  (Often used figuratively.)|His courage beaconed others to follow. 他的勇气激励着其他人跟随。|synonyms: guide, lead, direct, signal |antonyms: mislead, confuse|verb
