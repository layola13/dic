conjecturers:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərərz/
n.
推测者，猜想者
People who form conjectures or guesses; those who speculate.
The conjecturers debated the possible outcomes of the election.  推测者们就选举的可能结果进行了辩论。
Many conjecturers believed the lost city was buried beneath the sands. 许多推测者认为这座失落的城市被埋在沙子下面。
近义词: speculators, theorists, guessers, hypothesizers
反义词: analysts, researchers, knowers (those with certain knowledge)
词性: noun (plural)


conjecture: /kənˈdʒɛktʃər/
n.
推测，猜想
An opinion or conclusion formed on the basis of incomplete information.
The article is just conjecture and not based on facts. 这篇文章只是推测，并非基于事实。
His conjecture about the future of the company proved to be accurate. 他对公司未来的推测被证明是准确的。
近义词: guess, speculation, supposition, hypothesis, theory
反义词: fact, certainty, knowledge, proof
词性: noun

conjecture: /kənˈdʒɛktʃər/
v.
推测，猜想
Form an opinion or supposition about (something) on the basis of incomplete information.
We can only conjecture about the true meaning of his words. 我们只能推测他话语的真正含义。
Scientists conjectured that the universe began with a big bang. 科学家们推测宇宙起源于一次大爆炸。
近义词: guess, speculate, suppose, hypothesize, theorize
反义词: know, prove, ascertain, confirm
词性: verb
