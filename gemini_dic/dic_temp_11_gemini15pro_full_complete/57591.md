drams:/dræmz/| n.|少量液体; 一小口(尤指酒); 少量|A small quantity of liquid, especially a drink of alcohol; a small amount.|He took a few drams of whisky. 他喝了几小口威士忌。|近义词：sip, drop, mouthful|反义词:gallon, liter|名词

drams:/dræmz/| n.|药剂的计量单位; 打兰(等于1/8盎司或3.888克)|A unit of apothecaries' weight, equal to 1/8 of an ounce or 3.888 grams.|The prescription called for three drams of the powder.  处方要求三打兰的药粉。|近义词：unit, measure|反义词: N/A|名词
