defibrillate: /diːˈfɪbrɪleɪt/ /diːˈfaɪbrɪleɪt/
|v.| 除颤；使（心脏）恢复正常节律|To restore the rhythm of a fibrillating heart by applying a brief electric shock.|The paramedics defibrillated the patient's heart.|近义词:resuscitate, revive|反义词: harm, injure
|verb|除颤；使（心脏）恢复正常节律|
defibrillates: /diːˈfɪbrɪleɪts/ /diːˈfaɪbrɪleɪts/
|v.|(第三人称单数) 除颤；使（心脏）恢复正常节律|Third-person singular present tense form of "defibrillate".|The doctor defibrillates the patient when their heart stops.|近义词: resuscitates, revives|反义词: harms, injures
|verb|(第三人称单数) 除颤；使（心脏）恢复正常节律|

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (Latin prefix meaning "removal or separation") + fibrillation (irregular rapid contractions of the heart muscle fibers) + -ate (verb-forming suffix).
