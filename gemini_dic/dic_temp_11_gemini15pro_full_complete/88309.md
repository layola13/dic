hipped:/hɪpt/| adj.|熟悉；了解；赶时髦的；消息灵通的；|Aware of or informed about the latest trends or developments.|She's very hipped on the latest fashion trends. 她对最新的时尚潮流非常了解。|近义词：trendy, stylish, fashionable, informed, aware|反义词：unaware, uninformed, out-of-touch, old-fashioned|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From *hip (n.) + *-ed*.  *Hip (n.)* meaning "aware, informed" is of uncertain origin, possibly from Wolof *xip*  "to see, understand" or perhaps related to *hep*.
