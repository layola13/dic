interclub:/ˈɪntərˌklʌb/| adj.|俱乐部间的，跨俱乐部的|Existing or conducted between different clubs.|例句：The interclub tournament attracted players from all over the region.  俱乐部间的锦标赛吸引了来自该地区各地的选手。

例句：Interclub competitions foster friendly rivalry and sportsmanship. 跨俱乐部比赛促进了友好的竞争和体育精神。|近义词：intercollegiate, interschool|反义词：intraclub|词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + club. "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among."  "Club" refers to an association of people united by a common interest or goal.
