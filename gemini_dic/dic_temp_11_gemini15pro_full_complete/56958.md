douar:/ˈdwɑːr/| n.|杜瓦尔（北非村庄）|A small village or settlement, especially in North Africa.|Example: The douar was nestled in the foothills of the Atlas Mountains. 这个杜瓦尔坐落在阿特拉斯山脉的山麓。

Example:  Many Berber people live in douars scattered across the countryside. 许多柏柏尔人住在散布在乡村的杜瓦尔里。|近义词：village, hamlet, settlement|反义词:city, metropolis|词性:noun

词源: From Arabic دوار (duwār, “circle, circuit”).  Originally referred to the nomadic practice of pitching tents in a circle.
