arabat:/ˈærəbæt/| n.| 禁令，禁止，阻止|A prohibition or hindrance.|The king issued an arabat against gambling. 国王颁布了禁止赌博的禁令。|近义词：prohibition, ban, interdiction, veto|反义词：permission, allowance|词性: 名词

词源: From French *arabat*, from Arabic *ʻarabaṭ* (“obstacle, impediment”).
