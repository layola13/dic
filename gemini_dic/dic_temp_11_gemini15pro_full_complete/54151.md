disenroll:/ˌdɪsɪnˈroʊl/
verb
取消注册，登记
To officially remove someone's name from the list of members of a course, college, or group.
例句：
He disenrolled from the online course because it was too difficult.
他从在线课程中取消了注册，因为它太难了。

She disenrolled her son from the school after he was expelled.
在她儿子被开除后，她让他从学校退学了。

近义词：unregister, withdraw, unsubscribe
反义词：enroll, register, subscribe
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (meaning "reverse, remove") + enroll.
