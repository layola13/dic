endothelial:/ˌɛn.dəˈθiː.li.əl/ | adj. | 内皮的 | Relating to or consisting of endothelium. |  The endothelial cells lining the blood vessels play a crucial role in regulating blood flow. 内皮细胞排列在血管内，对调节血流起着至关重要的作用。

| Example 2: Damage to the endothelial lining can lead to atherosclerosis.  内皮层的损伤会导致动脉粥样硬化。 | Synonym: lining, vascular | Antonym: N/A |  adjective
