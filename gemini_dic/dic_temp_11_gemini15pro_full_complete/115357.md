manorialisms:/mæˈnɔːriəlɪzəmz/
|n.|
|庄园制度；庄园制度的残余；|
|The system of rights and duties in the medieval manor, or its surviving features.|
|The historian detailed the various manorialisms that persisted in rural England even into the 19th century.  这位历史学家详细描述了即使到了19世纪仍然在英格兰农村存在的各种庄园制度的残余。|
|The decline of manorialisms marked a significant shift in the social and economic structure of the country. 庄园制度的衰落标志着国家社会和经济结构的重大转变。|
|Near synonyms: feudalism, serfdom, vassalage|
|Antonyms:  modernity, capitalism, industrialism|
