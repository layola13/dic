mujahideen:/ˌmuːdʒɑːhɪˈdiːn/
|n.| 圣战者；穆斯林游击队员|  Guerrilla fighters in Islamic countries, especially those who are fighting against non-Muslim forces. |The mujahideen fought against the Soviet occupation of Afghanistan.  圣战者反抗苏联对阿富汗的占领。|
| | |Synonyms:  guerrillas, fighters, militants, insurgents|
| | |Antonyms:  pacifists, civilians|
| | |noun|


mujahed:/ˌmuːdʒɑːˈhɪd/
|n.| 圣战者；穆斯林战士| A Muslim guerrilla fighter, especially one fighting against non-Muslim forces. |A single mujahed bravely faced the enemy tank. 一名圣战者勇敢地面对敌人的坦克。|
| | |Synonyms:  warrior, soldier, fighter, militant|
| | |Antonyms:  pacifist, civilian|
| | |noun|
