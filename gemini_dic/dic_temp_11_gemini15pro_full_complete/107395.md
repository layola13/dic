laminar:/ˈlæmɪnər/| adj.|层流的，薄片状的|Arranged in thin layers or plates; smooth and regular, as in the flow of a fluid.|The laminar flow of air over the wings creates lift.|近义词：layered, stratified, smooth, streamlined|反义词:turbulent|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin lamina ("thin plate, leaf, layer") + -ar.
