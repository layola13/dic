luck-out:/ˌlʌk ˈaʊt/ | phrasal verb | 幸运;走运;交好运 | To have an unexpected piece of good fortune. |  I really lucked out when I found that parking space right in front of the store. 我真的很幸运，在商店门口就找到了一个停车位。


| Example: He lucked out by finding the last ticket to the concert. 他很幸运地买到了音乐会的最后一张票。| Synonym:  get lucky, strike it lucky, be fortunate, hit the jackpot | Antonym:  be unlucky, have bad luck, be unfortunate | phrasal verb
