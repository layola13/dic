monostichous: /ˌmɒnəˈstɪkəs/ | adj. | 单行的，单列的 | Arranged in a single row or series.  Relating to a plant having leaves arranged alternately in two rows on opposite sides of the stem, appearing to be in a single row. | Example 1: The monostichous arrangement of the leaves gave the plant a streamlined appearance.  叶子单列的排列方式使这种植物看起来很流线型。

Example 2: The botanist identified the plant as monostichous due to its unique leaf pattern.  植物学家根据其独特的叶片图案将该植物鉴定为单列的。 | Near synonyms: uniseriate, single-ranked | Antonym: distichous (two-ranked), multiseriate (many-ranked) | word type: adjective
