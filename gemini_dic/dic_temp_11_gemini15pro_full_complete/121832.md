misreligion:/ˌmɪsrɪˈlɪdʒən/
n.
错误的宗教信仰; 邪教; 误信
A belief in a false or wrong religion.  It can also refer to the practice of a false religion.
The cult leader promoted misreligion and led his followers astray.  邪教头目宣扬错误的宗教信仰，使他的追随者误入歧途。
The pamphlet denounced the practices of the tribe as misreligion.  这本小册子谴责该部落的做法是错误的宗教信仰。
近义词: heresy, heterodoxy, false religion, fallacy
反义词: true religion, orthodoxy
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrong, bad) + religion.  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-", meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss."  "Religion" comes from Old French "religion" and ultimately from Latin "religio" (respect for what is sacred, reverence for the gods, obligation, bond between man and gods).
