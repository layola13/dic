huffy:/ˈhʌfi/ | adj. | 生气的，恼火的，发怒的 | easily offended; touchy. Characterized by a state of anger or annoyance, often displayed through sullenness or petulance. |  She got huffy when I suggested she might be wrong. 她当我暗示她可能错了的时候，她生气了。

| Example: He stormed off in a huffy mood after the argument.  他争吵后怒气冲冲地离开了。 | Synonym: annoyed, indignant, irritated, miffed, offended, petulant, piqued, sullen | Antonym: cheerful, happy, pleased, amiable, good-natured | adjective
