fumidness:/ˈfjuːmɪdnəs/| n. |烟雾弥漫; 烟熏状态|The state or quality of being fumid; smokiness.|The fumidness of the room made it difficult to breathe.  房间里烟雾弥漫，让人难以呼吸。|Synonyms: smokiness, haziness, fogginess |Antonyms: clarity, clearness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fumidus ("smoky"), from fumus ("smoke").
