obstruction:/əbˈstrʌkʃən/| n.|阻碍，障碍物，阻塞|Something that blocks or closes a road, passage, entrance, etc., or the act of blocking something in this way. |The fallen tree caused an obstruction in the road.  倒下的树阻塞了道路。|近义词：obstacle, barrier, blockage, impediment|反义词:clearance, opening|noun

obstruction:/əbˈstrʌkʃən/| n.|妨碍，阻挠|The action of deliberately hindering or delaying something. |They accused the opposition party of obstruction. 他们指责反对党阻挠议事。|近义词：hindrance, interference, opposition|反义词:assistance, help|noun
