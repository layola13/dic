malaika:/məˈlaɪkə/ | n. | 天使; (尤指)黑人模特 | Swahili word meaning "angel." Often used as a name, particularly for girls.  It can also refer to a beautiful person, often a Black model. |  例句：Her name is Malaika, and she's a rising star in the fashion industry. 她的名字叫玛莱卡，是时尚界冉冉升起的新星。

例句：She's a true malaika, both inside and out. 她内外兼修，是一位真正的天使。| 近义词：angel, beauty, stunner | 反义词：demon, devil | 词性：名词
