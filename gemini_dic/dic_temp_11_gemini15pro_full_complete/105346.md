kickapoo:/ˌkɪkəˈpuː/ (US), /ˌkɪkəˈpuː/ (UK)
n.|基卡普人|A member of an Algonquian people originally of the Great Lakes region, now living primarily in Kansas, Oklahoma, and Texas. Also refers to the language they speak.|The Kickapoo tribe has a rich history.|近义词: (As a group) Algonquian, Native American, Indigenous people|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

kickapoo:/ˌkɪkəˈpuː/ (US), /ˌkɪkəˈpuː/ (UK)
adj.|基卡普人的；基卡普语的|Relating to the Kickapoo people or their language.|The Kickapoo language is considered endangered.|近义词: Algonquian|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
