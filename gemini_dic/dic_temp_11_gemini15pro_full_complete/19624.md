bigy: /ˈbɪɡi/ | n. |  大个子; 大东西 (Informal, often humorous) | A term of endearment or playful nickname for someone big or tall.  It can also refer to a large object. | Example: Hey there, bigy! How's it going?  嘿，大个子！你好吗？

Example: Look at that bigy of a truck! 看看那辆大卡车！ | 近义词: giant, hulk, behemoth (for people);  whopper (for objects)| 反义词: small fry, shrimp, midget (for people); tiny, little (for objects) | 词性: noun
