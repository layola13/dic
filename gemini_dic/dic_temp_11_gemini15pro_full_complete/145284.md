overbounteousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈbaʊntiəsnəsəz/
n.
过度的慷慨；过分的丰饶
Excessive generosity; overabundance.
His overbounteousnesses led him to donate excessively to charities, sometimes to his own detriment.  他过度的慷慨使他向慈善机构捐款过多，有时甚至损害了自己的利益。
The overbounteousnesses of the harvest led to a surplus of food, which unfortunately went to waste.  丰收的过度丰饶导致食物过剩，不幸的是，这些食物被浪费了。
近义词:  excess, superfluity, lavishness, prodigality
反义词:  stinginess, meagerness, scarcity, paucity
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

overbounteous: /ˌoʊvərˈbaʊntiəs/
adj.
过分慷慨的；过分丰富的
Excessively generous; overly abundant.
The overbounteous host ensured that every guest left with an armful of gifts.  过分慷慨的主人确保每位客人都带着满满的礼物离开。
The land was overbounteous, yielding crops far beyond what was needed. 这片土地非常肥沃，产出的作物远远超过了所需。
近义词: generous, lavish, abundant, plentiful
反义词: stingy, meager, scarce, sparse
词性: 形容词


词源:  From "over-" (excessive) + "bounteous" (generous, plentiful), ultimately from French "bonté" (goodness).
