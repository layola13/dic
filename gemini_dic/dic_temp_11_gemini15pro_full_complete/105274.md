khond:/kɒnd/ | n. |贡德人，孔德人|A member of a large tribal group of central India, mostly inhabiting the hilly tracts of Madhya Pradesh, Orissa, and Andhra Pradesh.  They speak Dravidian languages and are traditionally agriculturalists.| The khond people have a rich cultural heritage. 贡德人拥有丰富的文化遗产。| The government is working to improve the living conditions of the khond communities. 政府正在努力改善贡德族社区的生活条件。|Synonyms: Gond, Kandh| Antonyms: N/A | noun
