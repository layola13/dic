The word "exquire" does not exist in standard English.  It's likely a misspelling of "inquire" or perhaps "require."  I will provide analyses for both.

**Inquire:**

/ɪnˈkwaɪər/ (UK) /ɪnˈkwaɪr/ (US)| v. | 询问，打听 | To ask for information about something. | Example: I inquired about the price of the car. 我询问了汽车的价格。

Example: She inquired after his health. 她询问了他的健康状况。

近义词: ask, query, question | 反义词: answer, reply | 词性: verb

**Require:**

/rɪˈkwaɪər/ (UK) /rɪˈkwaɪr/ (US) | v. | 需要，要求 |  To need something; to demand something. | Example: The job requires a lot of patience. 这份工作需要很大的耐心。

Example:  The law requires you to wear a seatbelt. 法律要求你系安全带。

近义词: need, demand, necessitate | 反义词: offer, provide, give | 词性: verb
