illuminous:/ɪˈluːmɪnəs/| adj.|发光的，明亮的；启发的，启迪的| emitting light, especially soft, glowing light; spiritually or intellectually enlightening|例句：The moon was full and luminous, casting long shadows across the lawn. 满月明亮皎洁，在草坪上投下长长的影子。|例句：The professor's lectures were luminous, providing clear insights into complex topics. 教授的讲座很有启发性，对复杂的话题提供了清晰的见解。|近义词：bright, radiant, brilliant, enlightening|反义词：dark, dim, obscure|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin illuminosus, from Latin illuminare (“to illuminate”).
