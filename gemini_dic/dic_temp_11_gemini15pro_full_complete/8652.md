anticonservationists:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪsts/
n.
反环保主义者；反对环境保护的人
Those who oppose environmental conservation efforts.
The anticonservationists lobbied against the new national park proposal.  反环保主义者游说反对新的国家公园提案。
The meeting was disrupted by a group of vocal anticonservationists. 会议被一群声音很大的反环保主义者打断。
近义词: environmental antagonists, opponents of conservation
反义词: conservationists, environmentalists
词性: 名词 (plural)
