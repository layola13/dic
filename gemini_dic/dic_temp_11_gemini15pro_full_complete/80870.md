goutweed:/ˈɡaʊtwiːd/| n.|落葵，羊蹄草，痛风草|A common perennial weed with small white flowers and creeping stems, also known as ground elder, bishop's weed, or Aegopodium podagraria.  It is considered invasive in many areas. Its leaves were historically used to treat gout.|例句：The goutweed has taken over the entire garden. 落葵已经占领了整个花园。

例句：He tried using goutweed to soothe his aching joints. 他尝试用落葵来舒缓他疼痛的关节。|近义词：ground elder, bishop's weed, Aegopodium podagraria|反义词:|n.
