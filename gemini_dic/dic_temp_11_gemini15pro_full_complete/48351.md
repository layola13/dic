decryption:/diːˈkrɪpʃən/
n.|解密，解码|The decryption of the enemy's code was a major victory.  敌方密码的解密是一次重大胜利。|
例句：The decryption of the message took several hours.  这条消息的解密花了几个小时。
例句：The software performs decryption of encrypted files.  该软件对加密文件进行解密。
近义词：deciphering, decoding
反义词：encryption, encoding
词性:名词


decryption:/diːˈkrɪpʃən/
词源:  From Late Latin *dēcrȳptiōn- (stem of dēcrȳptiō), from Latin dēcrȳpt-, past participle stem of dēcrȳptare (“to decipher”), from dē- (“un-”) + crȳptāre, from Ancient Greek κρυπτός (kruptós, “hidden”).  源自晚期拉丁语 *dēcrȳptiōn-（dēcrȳptiō 的词干），来自拉丁语 dēcrȳpt-，dēcrȳptare（“解密”）的过去分词词干，来自 dē-（“非-”）+ crȳptāre，来自古希腊语 κρυπτός (kruptós，“隐藏的”)。
