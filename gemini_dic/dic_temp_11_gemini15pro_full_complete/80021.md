go-the-vole:/ɡəʊ ðə vəʊl/ | n. |  走田鼠；冒险尝试；孤注一掷| A colloquial expression signifying taking a risk, a gamble, or venturing into the unknown.  It evokes the image of a vole boldly venturing out, possibly into dangerous territory. | He decided to go the vole and invest all his savings in the new startup. 他决定孤注一掷，把所有积蓄都投资于这家新创公司。 | 近义词: take a chance, gamble, risk it all, go for broke | 反义词: play it safe, be cautious | 词性: noun (informal)
