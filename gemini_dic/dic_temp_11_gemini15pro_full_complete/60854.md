electroviscous:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈvɪskəs/
adjective
电粘性的
Relating to or denoting a change in the viscosity of a fluid caused by an electric field.
The electroviscous effect can significantly alter the flow properties of certain fluids. 电粘效应可以显著改变某些流体的流动特性。
Electroviscous fluids are being explored for use in advanced damping systems.  电粘流体正在被探索用于先进的阻尼系统。
近义词: electrorheological
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "Newtonian" for fluids with constant viscosity)
词性: adjective

Etymology:
electro- (from Greek ēlektron, meaning "amber", referring to electricity) + viscous (from Latin viscosus, meaning "sticky", referring to viscosity)
