colleagues:/ˈkɒliːɡz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːliːɡz/ (US)| n. |同事|A person with whom one works, especially in a profession or business.|例句：I enjoy working with my colleagues. 我喜欢和我的同事一起工作。

例句：My colleagues and I are working on a new project. 我和我的同事们正在做一个新项目。


近义词：co-workers, associates, peers|反义词:opponent, competitor|词性:名词
