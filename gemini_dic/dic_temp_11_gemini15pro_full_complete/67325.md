extemporises:/ɪkˈstɛmpəraɪzɪz/ /ɛkˈstɛmpəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
即兴创作；即席演奏；临场发挥
To compose, perform, or produce something such as music or a speech without preparation; improvise.
He extemporised a speech at the wedding. 他在婚礼上即兴致辞。
Synonyms: improvise, ad-lib, wing it, improvise
Antonyms: prepare, rehearse, plan
词性: 动词(verb)
