coexisting:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstɪŋ/| adj.|共存的，共处的|Existing together at the same time or in the same place.|例句：Coexisting religions often share common values. 共存的宗教通常有共同的价值观。

例句：The two species are coexisting peacefully in the same habitat. 这两个物种在同一栖息地和平共处。|近义词：cohabiting, living together, concurrent, simultaneous|反义词：conflicting, clashing|词性:形容词
coexist:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪst/| v.|共存，共处|To exist together at the same time or in the same place.|例句：Different cultures can coexist harmoniously. 不同的文化可以和谐共存。

例句：Predators and prey often coexist in a delicate balance. 捕食者和猎物通常在微妙的平衡中共存。|近义词：cohabit, live together|反义词：clash, conflict|词性:动词
词源: co- (together) + exist (to have real being)
