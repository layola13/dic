downsampled:/ˌdaʊnˈsæmpəld/| adj.|下采样的，降采样的|Reduced in resolution or data rate by sampling at a lower frequency.|The image was downsampled to reduce the file size. 图像被下采样以减小文件大小。|The audio was downsampled from 48kHz to 44.1kHz. 音频从48kHz下采样到44.1kHz。|近义词: subsampled, decimated|反义词: upsampled, interpolated|词性:形容词
downsample:/ˌdaʊnˈsæmpəl/| vt.|下采样，降采样|To reduce the resolution or data rate of a signal by sampling at a lower frequency.|We downsampled the audio to save storage space. 我们对音频进行下采样以节省存储空间。|The video was downsampled for streaming. 视频被下采样以用于流媒体传输。|近义词: subsample, decimate|反义词: upsample, interpolate|词性:动词
