blindside:/ˈblaɪndˌsaɪd/| vt. n.|使措手不及，攻击弱点； 盲点|To attack someone from a place or direction where they cannot see you coming; a place or direction where you cannot see what is happening|The news of her resignation completely blindsided us. 她辞职的消息完全让我们措手不及。|近义词: ambush, surprise, catch off guard|反义词:forewarn,  anticipate|verb
blindside:/ˈblaɪndˌsaɪd/| n.| 盲点|a place or direction where you cannot see what is happening|His political opponents exploited his blindside. 他的政敌利用了他的盲点。|近义词: weakness, vulnerability|反义词:strength, foresight|noun
