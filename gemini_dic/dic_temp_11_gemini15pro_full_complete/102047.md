isologous: /aɪˈsɑːləɡəs/ | adj. | 同基因的，同系移植的 | Genetically identical; derived from the same inbred strain.  Used especially of tissues or organs suitable for transplantation because of genetic similarity. |  Isologous bone marrow transplantation is less likely to cause graft-versus-host disease.  同基因骨髓移植不太可能引起移植物抗宿主病。|  syngeneic, homografted | heterologous, allogeneic | adjective
