equalizer:/ˈiːkwəlaɪzər/ (US), /ˈiːkwəlaɪzə/ (UK)| n.|均衡器|A device used to adjust the volume of different frequencies in sound, typically to improve the overall quality. |The sound engineer adjusted the equalizer to boost the bass. 音响工程师调整了均衡器以增强低音。|synonyms: balancer, regulator, leveler|antonyms: distorter, amplifier (in some contexts)|noun


equalizers:/ˈiːkwəlaɪzərz/ (US), /ˈiːkwəlaɪzəz/ (UK)| n.|均衡器（复数）|Plural form of "equalizer." Refers to multiple devices or settings used to adjust sound frequencies. |He uses several equalizers in his recording studio. 他的录音棚里使用多个均衡器。|synonyms: balancers, regulators, levelers|antonyms: distorters, amplifiers (in some contexts)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From equalize + -er.  Equalize comes from equal + -ize. Equal ultimately derives from the Latin "aequalis," meaning "uniform," "level," or "equal."
