colliders:/kəˈlaɪdərz/| n. |对撞机；碰撞器|A collider is a machine that accelerates particles to very high speeds and then smashes them together. Scientists use colliders to study the fundamental nature of matter and energy.|The Large Hadron Collider is the world's largest and most powerful particle collider. 大型强子对撞机是世界上最大、能量最高的粒子对撞机。|The scientists analyzed the data from the particle collider. 科学家们分析了粒子对撞机的数据。|synonyms: accelerator, smasher|antonyms: N/A|noun
