decategorizing:/diːˈkætəɡəˌraɪzɪŋ/
|v.|去分类化；取消分类；消除类别|The process of decategorizing involves removing items or concepts from predefined categories. 去分类化的过程包括将项目或概念从预定义的类别中移除。|Decategorizing information can sometimes make it harder to find.  去分类化信息有时会使其更难找到。|近义词：reclassifying, regrouping, reorganizing|反义词：categorizing, classifying, grouping|词性:动词

decategorization:/diːˌkætəɡərɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|去分类化；取消分类|Decategorization of mental illness can help reduce stigma. 精神疾病的去分类化有助于减少污名化。|The decategorization of data led to confusion. 数据的去分类化导致了混乱。|近义词：reclassification, regrouping, reorganization|反义词：categorization, classification, grouping|词性:名词

词源:  From de- + categorize + -ing/-ation.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse".  "Categorize" comes from "category" which ultimately derives from the Greek "katēgoria" meaning "predicate, accusation."
