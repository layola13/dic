acquitted:/əˈkwɪtɪd/
verb
宣告无罪; acquitted of
freed from a criminal charge by a verdict of not guilty.
The jury acquitted the defendant of all charges. 陪审团宣告被告所有罪名不成立。
近义词:exonerated, absolved, cleared
反义词:convicted, condemned
词性:Verb (Past Participle)


acquit:/əˈkwɪt/
verb
表现；履行职责；脱卸义务；还清(债务)
behave or perform in a specified way.
He acquitted himself admirably in the debate. 他在辩论中表现出色。
近义词:conduct, behave, perform
反义词:misbehave
词性:Verb
