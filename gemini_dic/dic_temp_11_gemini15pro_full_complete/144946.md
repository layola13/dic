overabstains:/ˌoʊvərəbˈsteɪnz/ | v. | 过度节制；过分克制 | To abstain excessively or to an unusual degree.  | He overabstains from social activities, preferring solitude. 他过分克制自己不参加社交活动，更喜欢独处。|  Example:  Concerned about her health, she overabstains from sweets and fatty foods. 担心自己的健康，她过分节制甜食和高脂肪食物。 | 近义词: overindulge (ironically), refrain excessively | 反义词: overindulge, binge | 词性: 动词
