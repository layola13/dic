hypotarsus:/ˌhaɪpəˈtɑːrsəs/ /ˌhaɪpoʊˈtɑːrsəs/
n.
跗蹠骨; 雀类的跗蹠骨
A plate of small bones in the back of the tarsus in birds, supporting the toes.
The hypotarsus is a prominent bony structure on the back of the chicken's leg.  鸡的腿后部有一个突出的骨骼结构，即跗蹠骨。
The hypotarsus provides attachment points for tendons that control the toes. 跗蹠骨为控制脚趾的肌腱提供附着点。
Synonym: tarsometatarsus
Antonym: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific anatomical feature)
