dexterous:/ˈdɛkstərəs/ /ˈdɛkstrəs/| adj.|灵巧的，熟练的|Showing or having skill, especially with the hands.|She is a dexterous pianist with a light, rapid touch. 她是一位灵巧的钢琴家，触键轻快敏捷。|Synonyms: skillful, adroit, adept, nimble, deft|Antonyms: clumsy, awkward, inept|adjective
