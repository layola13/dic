elatery:/ɪˈlætəri/ | n. | 彈跳力，彈性 | The inherent elasticity or springiness of a body.  The ability of something to return to its original shape after being stretched or compressed. | The elatery of the rubber band allowed it to launch the projectile.  橡皮筋的彈力使它能夠發射彈丸。 | 近义词: elasticity, springiness, resilience, bounciness | 反义词: rigidity, stiffness | 词性: noun

词源: From Modern Latin *ēlaterius, from Latin ēlātus (“lifted up, raised”), past participle of efferō.
