eking:/ˈiːkɪŋ/
verb
补充；勉强维持；增加；
Supplementing; barely maintaining; increasing;
He was eking out a living by selling vegetables. 他靠卖菜勉强维持生计。
近义词: supplementing, stretching, augmenting
反义词: squandering, wasting, depleting
词性: verb

词源: From Middle English *eken, from Old English ēacan (“to increase, augment, add”), from Proto-Germanic *aukjaną (“to augment”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ewḱ- (“to increase, augment”). Cognate with Scots eik (“to add, increase”), West Frisian oekje (“to increase”), Dutch ophogen (“to raise, increase”), German erhöhen (“to increase, raise”), Icelandic auka (“to increase”). Related to eke (adverb).
