motoneuronal:/ˌməʊtəʊˈnjʊərənəl/ | adj. | 运动神经元的 | Relating to a motor neuron, a nerve cell that transmits signals from the central nervous system to muscles, causing movement. |  Motoneuronal activity is essential for voluntary and involuntary movements. 运动神经元活动对于随意运动和非随意运动至关重要。|  Damage to motoneuronal pathways can lead to paralysis.  运动神经元通路受损会导致瘫痪。| Synonyms: motor, efferent | Antonyms: sensory, afferent |  adjective
