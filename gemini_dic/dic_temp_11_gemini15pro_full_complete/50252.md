depender:/dɪˈpɛndər/| n.|依赖者，依靠者|Someone who relies on another person for support, especially financial support. |例句：He is a depender on his parents. 他是一个依靠父母的人。

例句：She's a single mother with two young dependers. 她是一位有两个年幼孩子的单亲妈妈。|近义词：dependent, reliant|反义词：independent, self-sufficient|词性:noun

Etymology:  From depend + -er. Ultimately from Latin *dēpendere*, from dē- "down" + pendere "to hang".
