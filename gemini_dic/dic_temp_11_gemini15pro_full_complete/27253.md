calorificient: /ˌkælərɪˈfɪʃənt/
adjective
产热的；生热的
Producing heat; increasing heat.
This calorificient food helps to keep you warm in cold weather.  这种产热食物有助于你在寒冷的天气里保暖。
The calorificient properties of chili peppers are well known. 辣椒的产热特性是众所周知的。
Synonyms:  heating, warming, thermogenic
Antonyms:  cooling, refrigerant
词性: adjective
