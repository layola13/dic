forgery:/ˈfɔːrdʒəri/ (US) /ˈfɔːdʒəri/ (UK)| n.|伪造；伪造品；伪造罪|The act of making a copy of something, such as a document, painting, etc., in order to deceive people into thinking that it is real.|The painting was a blatant forgery. 这幅画是一幅公然的赝品。|Synonyms: counterfeit, fake, imitation, sham|Antonyms: original, genuine|noun

forgery:/ˈfɔːrdʒəri/ (US) /ˈfɔːdʒəri/ (UK)| n.|伪造罪|The crime of making a copy of something, such as a document, painting, etc., in order to deceive people into thinking that it is real, or an example of something made in this way.|He was found guilty of forgery. 他被判伪造罪。|Synonyms: counterfeiting, faking, imitation, sham|Antonyms: authenticity, genuineness|noun
