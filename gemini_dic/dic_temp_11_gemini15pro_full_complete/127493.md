neckerchiefs:/ˈnɛkərˌtʃiːfs/| n. |围巾，领巾|A piece of cloth worn around the neck, often decorative or part of a uniform. |例句：The boy scouts wore neckerchiefs as part of their uniform. 男童子军戴着围巾作为制服的一部分。

例句：She tied a colorful neckerchief around her neck. 她脖子上系了一条色彩鲜艳的围巾。|近义词：scarf, bandana, kerchief |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
