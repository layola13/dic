abjures:/əbˈdʒʊərz/| v.|正式放弃，发誓放弃|To solemnly renounce or reject something, typically a belief, cause, or claim.|He abjured his former life of crime.  他发誓放弃以前犯罪的生活。|The king abjured his claim to the throne. 国王放弃了对王位的要求。|近义词：renounce, repudiate, forswear, recant|反义词：affirm, embrace, uphold|verb
