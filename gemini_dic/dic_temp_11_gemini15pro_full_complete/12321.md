asell:/əˈsɛl/| adv.| 出售；廉价地 | for sale; cheaply.  |He bought the car asell. 他廉价地买了这辆车。 | 近义词:cheaply, inexpensively | 反义词:expensively, dearly | 词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *aselle, equivalent to a- + sell. Compare Dutch te koop (“for sale”).
