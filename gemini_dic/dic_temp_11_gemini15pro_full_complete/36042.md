codling:/ˈkɒdlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːdlɪŋ/ (US)| n.|未成熟的小苹果；幼鳕鱼|A young, unripe apple, especially one suitable for cooking.  A young cod fish.|例句：She baked the codlings with cinnamon and sugar. 她用肉桂和糖烤了小苹果。

例句：Codlings are a popular dish in some coastal regions. 幼鳕鱼在一些沿海地区是一道受欢迎的菜肴。|近义词：(for apple) crab apple, pippin; (for fish) young cod, codlet|反义词:(for apple) ripe apple; (for fish) adult cod|词性:noun
