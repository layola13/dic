lapped:/læpt/
lapped|v.|搭接，重叠，包围，舔舐，拍打(水)，领先一圈|The waves lapped gently against the shore. 海浪轻轻地拍打着海岸。|近义词：overlapped, washed, licked, beat|反义词:separated|词性:动词

lapped:/læpt/
lapped|adj.|搭接的，重叠的|The shingles on the roof were lapped. 屋顶上的瓦片是搭接的。|近义词：overlapping|反义词:separate|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lappian, from Proto-Germanic *lappōną (“to lick, lap”), from Proto-Indo-European *lab- (“to lick, lap”). Cognate with Dutch lappen (“to lap, patch”), German lappen (“to lap, patch”), Old Norse lepja (“to lick”).
