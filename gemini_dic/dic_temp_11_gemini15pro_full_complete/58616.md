dulnesses:/ˈdʌlnɪsɪz/
n.
迟钝，沉闷，无聊
lack of sharpness or brightness; the state of being dull
The dulnesses of the lecture made many students fall asleep.  这沉闷的讲座使许多学生昏昏欲睡。
近义词：monotony, boredom, tediousness, apathy
反义词：excitement, liveliness, vibrancy, interest
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *dulnes*, from Old English *dulnes*, from *dul* ("dull") + *-nes* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  The plural form is formed by adding the regular English plural suffix *-es*.
