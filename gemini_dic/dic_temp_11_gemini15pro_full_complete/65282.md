euchroite:/ˈjuːkroʊˌaɪt/
noun
翠砷铜矿
A vitreous, emerald-green to bluish-green hydrous copper arsenate mineral, Cu2AsO4(OH)·3H2O.
Euchroite is a relatively rare mineral, prized by collectors for its vibrant green color. 翠砷铜矿是一种相对稀有的矿物，因其鲜艳的绿色而受到收藏家的珍视。
Synonym:none (as it's a specific mineral)
Antonym:none
