ciggies:/ˈsɪɡiz/| n. |香烟，烟卷 (非正式，俚语)|Informal and chiefly British term for cigarettes.  A shortened and more casual way to refer to cigarettes. |例句：Fancy a couple of ciggies?  想来几根烟吗？|近义词：cigarettes, smokes, fags (British slang)|反义词: None (Cigarettes don't have a direct opposite.)|Noun
