hero: /ˈhɪəroʊ/ (US) /ˈhɪərəʊ/ (UK) | n. | 英雄；男主角；勇士 | A person who is admired or idealized for courage, outstanding achievements, or noble qualities. |  He was a hero in the war. 他是战争中的英雄。

He is the hero of this novel. 他是这部小说的男主角。

近义词: champion, idol, legend, protagonist, star

反义词: coward, villain

词性: noun
