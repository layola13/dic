floccular:/ˈflɒkjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈflɒkjʊlə/ (UK)| adj.|絮状的；小脑絮状体的|Relating to or resembling a flocculus; specifically, relating to the flocculus of the cerebellum.  It is characterized by a woolly or flaky appearance.|The floccular lobe of the cerebellum is involved in balance and eye movements.|Synonyms: flaky, woolly, tufted|Antonyms: smooth, solid|adjective

flocculus:/ˈflɒkjʊləs/ (US) /ˈflɒkjʊləs/ (UK)| n.|小片；絮状物；小脑絮状体|A small lobe at the posterior base of the cerebellum, involved in the control of balance and eye movements.  Also refers generally to a small fluffy mass or flake.|Damage to the flocculus can cause problems with balance and coordination.|Synonyms: flake, tuft, lobe|Antonyms: N/A|noun
