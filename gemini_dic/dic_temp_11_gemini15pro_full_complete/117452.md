meditative:/ˈmɛdɪteɪtɪv/| adj.|冥想的，沉思的，反思的|Given to meditation; contemplative. Characterized by or promoting meditation.|She sat in a meditative pose, contemplating the meaning of life. 她以冥想的姿势坐着，思考着生命的意义。|近义词：contemplative, reflective, thoughtful, pensive|反义词：unthinking, impulsive, rash|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *meditativus*, from Latin meditatus (past participle of meditari “to meditate”), from *meditari*, frequentative of mederi “to heal, remedy.”
