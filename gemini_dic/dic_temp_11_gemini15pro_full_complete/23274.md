boxcar:/ˈbɑːksˌkɑːr/ (US), /ˈbɒksˌkɑːr/ (UK)| n.|棚车，闷罐车|A railroad freight car, typically enclosed, used to transport goods.|The boxcar was filled with crates of oranges. 这节棚车装满了成箱的橘子。|近义词：freight car, goods wagon|反义词:passenger car|noun

boxcar:/ˈbɑːksˌkɑːr/ (US), /ˈbɒksˌkɑːr/ (UK)| adj.|(Informal) exceptionally large; huge|An exceptionally large amount or number of something.|He made a boxcar figure on that deal. 他在那笔交易中赚了一大笔钱。|近义词：huge, enormous, massive|反义词:small, tiny|adjective
