accountably:/əˈkaʊntəbli/
adverb| 负责地；可解释地；有责任地 | in a way that makes somebody responsible for their actions; in a way that can be explained | The government should be held accountably for its spending. 政府应该对其支出负责。| 近义词: responsibly, answerably | 反义词: irresponsibly, unaccountably | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From accountable + -ly.  Accountable traces back to Old French *accontable, from Late Latin accomptābilis "liable to render account," from accomptāre "to count, reckon," from ad- "to" + computāre "to compute."
