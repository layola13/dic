cabled:/ˈkeɪbəld/
adjective
发送电报的；用电缆连接的
sent by cable; connected by cable
The news was cabled to London. 这条消息被电报发往伦敦。
The equipment is cabled to the computer. 设备用电缆连接到计算机。
近义词:wired, connected
反义词:disconnected, wireless
词性:adjective

cabled:/ˈkeɪbəld/
verb
用电缆连接；发送电报
connect with a cable; send by cable
They cabled the news to their headquarters. 他们用电报把消息发到了总部。
The house is cabled for high-speed internet. 这房子铺设了高速互联网电缆。
近义词:wired, connected
反义词:disconnected, wireless
词性:verb
