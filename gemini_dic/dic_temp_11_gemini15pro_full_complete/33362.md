chumship: /ˈtʃʌmʃɪp/
n.
友谊；交情；伙伴关系
The state of being chums; close friendship.
例句：Their chumship began in college and lasted a lifetime. 他们的友谊始于大学，并持续了一生。

例句：A strong chumship developed between the two boys over the summer.  在那个夏天，两个男孩之间建立了深厚的友谊。

近义词：friendship, camaraderie, companionship, fellowship
反义词：enmity, animosity, hostility
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From chum +‎ -ship.  "Chum" originally referred to a roommate or chamber-mate, evolving to mean a close friend.  "-ship" is a suffix denoting a state or condition.
