ostracization:/ˌɒstrəsaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌɑːstrəsaɪˈzeɪʃn/
n.
排斥，放逐，孤立
Exclusion from a society or group.
She suffered years of ostracization after speaking out against the company. 她因为公开反对公司而遭受了多年的排斥。
The ostracization of the whistleblower made other employees afraid to speak up. 对告密者的排斥使其他员工不敢说出真相。
近义词: exclusion, banishment, shunning, rejection
反义词: inclusion, acceptance, welcome
