fail-safe:/ˈfeɪlˌseɪf/ | adj. |自动防故障的;故障保险的;故障安全的|Designed to prevent malfunction or to minimize damage in the event of failure. |The nuclear reactor has a fail-safe cooling system. 核反应堆有一个故障安全冷却系统。|Synonyms: foolproof, reliable, secure, dependable |Antonyms: risky, dangerous, unreliable, hazardous |adjective
