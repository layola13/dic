boldo:/ˈboʊldoʊ/ /ˈbɒldoʊ/| n. |博尔多树，博尔多叶|An evergreen tree (Peumus boldus) native to Chile, whose leaves are used medicinally and to flavor food and beverages.  The leaves themselves are also called boldo.|The boldo tree is drought-tolerant. 博尔多树耐旱。|近义词:Peumus boldus|反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant species)|noun
