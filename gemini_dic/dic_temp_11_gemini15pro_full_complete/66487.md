exertment:/ɪɡˈzɜːtmənt/ | n. |  exertion; the act of exerting |  The exertment of force caused the object to move.  用力导致物体移动。 | 近义词: exertion, effort, strain | 反义词: relaxation, inactivity, rest | 词性: noun

Etymology: From exert + -ment.  Exert comes from the Latin *exserere*, "to thrust out, put forth".
