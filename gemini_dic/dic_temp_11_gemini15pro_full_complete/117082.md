meadows:/ˈmedəʊz/ (UK) /ˈmedəʊz/ (US)| n. |草地，牧场|A piece of grassland, especially one used for hay or grazing.|例句：The wildflowers bloomed in the meadows. 野花在草地上盛开。

例句：We walked through the meadows, enjoying the fresh air. 我们穿过草地，享受着新鲜的空气。

近义词：fields, pastures, grasslands|反义词：woods, forests|词性：名词
