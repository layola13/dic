diversenesses:/daɪˈvɜːrsnɪsɪz/
|n.|
|多样性，差异性（通常指diverseness的复数形式，但diverseness本身就包含复数含义，所以diversenesses比较少用）|
|The diversenesses of cultures represented at the festival were truly impressive.  这个节日所展现的文化多样性真是令人印象深刻。 (这个例句用diversenesses略微不自然，更自然的表达是The diversity of cultures...或The diverseness of cultures...) |
|The exhibition showcased the diversenesses of artistic expression throughout history. 展览展示了历史上艺术表达的多样性。(这个例句也略有不自然，更自然的表达是The exhibition showcased the diversity of artistic expression...或The exhibition showcased the diverseness of artistic expression...)|
|近义词: diversities, varieties, differences|
|反义词: uniformity, homogeneity, sameness|
|词性: 名词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From diverse + -ness.  Diverse ultimately comes from Latin  divertere "to turn aside, go different ways," from dis- "apart" + vertere "to turn."
