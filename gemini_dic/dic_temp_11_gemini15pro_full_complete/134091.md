nonimaginational:/ˌnɒnɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃənəl/
adjective
缺乏想象力的
Lacking in imagination; unimaginative.
He gave a rather nonimaginational account of the event. 他对这件事的描述相当缺乏想象力。

近义词:unimaginative, uninspired, pedestrian, prosaic, mundane
反义词:imaginative, creative, inspired, innovative, visionary
词性:adjective
