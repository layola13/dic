apostate:/əˈpɒsteɪt/ (US) /əˈpɑːsteɪt/ (UK)| n. adj.|叛教者，变节者|Someone who renounces a religious belief or principle, or a political party or cause.|例句：He was declared an apostate by the church after publicly denouncing its teachings.  他公开谴责教会的教义后，被教会宣布为叛教者。

例句：She was considered an apostate after leaving the political party and joining the opposition.  她离开政党加入反对派后，被认为是变节者。|近义词：renegade, defector, traitor, heretic|反义词：believer, loyalist, adherent|词性: noun, adjective


apostate:/əˈpɒsteɪt/ (US) /əˈpɑːsteɪt/ (UK)| adj.|叛教的，变节的|Relating to or characteristic of an apostate.|例句：His apostate views led to his expulsion from the group.  他的叛教观点导致他被逐出该团体。

例句：The apostate writer was shunned by his former colleagues.  这位变节的作家被以前的同事们所回避。|近义词：renegade, traitorous, heretical|反义词：faithful, loyal, orthodox|词性: adjective
