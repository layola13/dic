meditatist:/ˈmedɪteɪtɪst/
n.|冥想者，沉思者|A person who meditates, especially one who practices meditation regularly as a religious or spiritual discipline.|例句：The meditatist sought inner peace through daily practice.  这位冥想者通过日常练习寻求内心的平静。

例句：The meditatist retreated to the mountains for a week of silent contemplation. 这位冥想者 retreated to the mountains for a week of silent contemplation. 这位冥想者退到山中进行为期一周的静默沉思。|近义词：contemplative, reflector, ponderer|反义词：extrovert, socializer|词性：名词

词源：From the verb "meditate" + "-ist" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a person who practices or is skilled in something).  "Meditate" comes from Latin "meditari" meaning "to ponder, consider, reflect."
