heterozygotic:/ˌhɛtərəʊzaɪˈɡɒtɪk/ (US) /ˌhɛt̬əroʊzaɪˈɡɑːtɪk/| adj.|杂合的|Having two different alleles of a particular gene or genes.|Example: The heterozygotic individual carried one dominant and one recessive allele.  这个杂合子个体携带一个显性等位基因和一个隐性等位基因。

Example: Heterozygotic advantage can protect against certain diseases. 杂合子优势可以预防某些疾病。|Synonym: hybrid|Antonym: homozygous|Part of Speech: Adjective
