minglements: /ˈmɪŋɡlmənts/
| n. |混合，交融，交往|The act or process of mingling; a mixture or mingling of things or people.|The minglements of different cultures created a vibrant atmosphere. 不同文化的交融创造了充满活力的氛围。| 近义词: mixtures, blendings, interactions, interminglings | 反义词: separations, divisions |词性:名词

Etymology:
From mingle (v.) + -ment (suffix forming nouns of action or result).  Mingle itself comes from Old English *gemengan, from Germanic *mangjan, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *meik- "to mix."
