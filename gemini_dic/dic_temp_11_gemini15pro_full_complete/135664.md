nonpreferentialisms:/ˌnɒnˌprɛfərˈɛnʃəlɪzəmz/
noun
非优惠主义
The absence or opposite of preferential treatment; the principle or practice of not granting preferential treatment.
Many developing countries are advocating for nonpreferentialisms in international trade, believing it fosters fair competition. 许多发展中国家提倡国际贸易中的非优惠主义，认为这能促进公平竞争。
The new trade agreement embodies the spirit of nonpreferentialisms, treating all member states equally. 新的贸易协定体现了非优惠主义的精神，平等对待所有成员国。
Synonyms: impartiality, neutrality, egalitarianism
Antonyms: favoritism, preferential treatment, bias
