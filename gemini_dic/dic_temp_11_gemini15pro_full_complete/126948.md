narratively:/ˈnærətɪvli/
adv.|叙述地，故事性地|In a way that tells a story; by means of a narrative.|He narratively described his journey through the Amazon. 他以叙事的方式描述了他穿越亚马逊的旅程。|
近义词:story-like, narrational, descriptively
反义词:factually, objectively
词性:副词

词源: From narrative + -ly.  "Narrative" comes from Late Middle English, from Old French narratief, from Latin narrativus, from narrare "to tell," from gnarus "knowing."
