exsolution:/ˌɛksəˈluːʃən/ | n. |析出；固溶体分解|The process by which one mineral separates from another to form a distinct crystalline phase within the original host mineral.  The exsolution of albite from orthoclase is a common example. |Example 1: Exsolution lamellae of ilmenite are commonly observed in magnetite.  磁铁矿中常可观察到钛铁矿的析出薄片。

Example 2: The exsolution of copper minerals from sulfide ores is an important geological process. 硫化矿石中铜矿物的析出是一个重要的地质过程。|Near synonyms: precipitation, separation, unmixing|Antonyms: dissolution, solution, mixing |noun
