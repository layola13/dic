himselve:  This is not a standard English word. The correct form is **himself**.

himself: /hɪmˈsɛlf/ | pron. | 他自己 | Used as the reflexive form of *he* or as an emphatic form. | He hurt himself playing football.  他踢足球时伤到了自己。|  He himself admitted it. 他自己承认了。 | 近义词: (none - reflexive pronouns are unique) | 反义词: (none - reflexive pronouns are unique) | 词性: pronoun

Etymology: Old English *him selfum*, dative case of *he* + *self*.
