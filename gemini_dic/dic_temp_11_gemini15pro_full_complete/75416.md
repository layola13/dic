frugality:/fruːˈɡæləti/| n. |节俭，俭省|The quality of being economical with money or food; thriftiness.|  Her frugality allowed her to save a significant portion of her income. 她的节俭使她能够节省很大一部分收入。|  He lived a life of frugality, avoiding unnecessary expenses. 他过着节俭的生活，避免不必要的开支。|synonyms: thrift, economy, prudence, austerity |antonyms: extravagance, lavishness, prodigality|noun
