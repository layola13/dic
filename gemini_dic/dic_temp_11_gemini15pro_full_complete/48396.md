dedesignation:/diːˌdɛzɪɡˈneɪʃən/
noun
撤销指定;取消名称
the act of removing a designation or title from someone or something.
The dedesignation of the area as a national park sparked protests.  该地区被取消国家公园的指定引发了抗议。
The dedesignation of Pluto as a planet disappointed many.  冥王星被取消行星的指定让很多人失望。
近义词: removal, revocation, cancellation
反义词: designation, appointment, nomination
词性: noun

Etymology:  "de-" (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + "designation" (from Latin "designatio,"  meaning "marking out, appointment")
