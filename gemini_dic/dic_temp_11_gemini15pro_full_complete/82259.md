groundhog:/ˈɡraʊndhɒɡ/ (US) /ˈɡraʊndhɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|土拨鼠|A rodent of the squirrel family, Marmota monax, found in North America, known for its hibernation and its purported ability to predict the arrival of spring. It is also called a woodchuck.|例句：The groundhog saw its shadow, predicting six more weeks of winter. 土拨鼠看到了自己的影子，预示着还有六个星期的冬天。

groundhog:/ˈɡraʊndhɒɡ/ (US) /ˈɡraʊndhɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|土拨鼠日|Groundhog Day, February 2nd, is the day on which the groundhog's emergence from hibernation is observed. Tradition holds that if the groundhog sees its shadow and returns to its burrow, there will be six more weeks of winter. |例句：Groundhog Day is a popular tradition in North America. 土拨鼠日是北美的一个流行传统。
