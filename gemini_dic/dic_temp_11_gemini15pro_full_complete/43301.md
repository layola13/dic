craggier:/ˈkræɡiər/| adj.|更崎岖的，更陡峭的|Comparative form of craggy: more craggy.  Meaning having many crags; rugged and rocky.|The path became craggier as we climbed higher.  这条路随着我们越爬越高变得更加崎岖。|例句：The craggier peaks of the mountains were shrouded in mist. 山上更崎岖的山峰笼罩在薄雾中。|近义词：rougher, steeper, rockier|反义词：smoother, flatter, gentler|词性:形容词
词源: From crag + -y + -er. Ultimately from the Celtic *kraggos.
