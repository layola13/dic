butternut:/ˈbʌtərˌnʌt/ (US), /ˈbʌtənʌt/ (UK)| n. adj.|白胡桃；白胡桃树；黄油坚果色的|A type of nut with a hard shell and a sweet, buttery flavor. Also, the tree that produces this nut, or a light yellowish-brown color resembling the nut.|例句：I love butternut squash soup in the fall. 我秋天喜欢喝白胡桃南瓜汤。|近义词：walnut, squash (when referring to the squash)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun, adjective
