outglittered:/ˌaʊtˈɡlɪtərd/
verb
胜过…的光辉，比…更闪耀
surpassed in glitter or brilliance; outshone
Her dress outglittered everyone else's at the party. 她在派对上的礼服比其他任何人的都闪耀。
outshone, eclipsed, surpassed
overshadowed, dimmed
词性: 动词
