overliberalization: /ˌoʊvərˌlɪbərələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |过度自由化|The act of liberalizing something, such as an economy or a political system, to an excessive or detrimental degree.  It implies that the deregulation or opening up has gone too far, leading to negative consequences. |The overliberalization of the financial markets contributed to the economic crisis. 过度开放金融市场导致了经济危机。| The overliberalization of trade policies led to a flood of cheap imports, harming domestic industries. 贸易政策的过度自由化导致廉价进口产品泛滥，损害了国内产业。 | deregulation, excessive liberalization | restriction, regulation | noun
