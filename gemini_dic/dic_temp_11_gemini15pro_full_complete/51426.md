detest:/dɪˈtest/ | v. | 憎恶，厌恶 | To dislike intensely; to abhor. |  I detest hypocrisy. 我厌恶虚伪。

She detests spiders. 她讨厌蜘蛛。 | 近义词：loathe, abhor, despise, hate | 反义词：love, adore, cherish, admire | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin detestari "to curse, execrate, denounce, abominate," from de- "down, away" + testari "to bear witness, call to witness," from testis "witness"
