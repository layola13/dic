hypermetamorphosis:/ˌhaɪpərˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəsɪs/ | n. | 超变态 | A type of complete insect metamorphosis in which the larva passes through numerous instars, each markedly different in form and habits.  |  The blister beetle undergoes hypermetamorphosis, with its larval stages including a triungulin, a caraboid larva, and a scarabaeidoid larva.  水泡甲虫经历了超变态，其幼虫阶段包括三爪蚴、步甲幼虫和金龟子幼虫。 |  complete metamorphosis, complex metamorphosis | incomplete metamorphosis | noun
