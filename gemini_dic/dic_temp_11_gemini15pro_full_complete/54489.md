disinter:/dɪsɪnˈtɜːr/
verb
掘出；挖出
To dig up (something buried, especially a corpse) from the earth.
The bodies were disinterred for forensic examination.  尸体被挖出来进行法医检查。
近义词: exhume, unearth
反义词: bury, inter
词性: verb
