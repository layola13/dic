hyper-sophisticatedly:/ˌhaɪpər səˈfɪstɪkeɪtɪdli/
adverb|超级精密地；超级复杂地|in a manner that is extremely sophisticated; with extreme sophistication|The system was hyper-sophisticatedly designed to adapt to any environment.|近义词: elaborately, intricately, complexly|反义词: simply, crudely, unsophisticatedly|词性: adverb
Etymology:  "hyper-" (Greek > Latin > Old French > Middle English) meaning "over, beyond, excessive" + "sophisticated" (Latin sophisticatus, past participle of sophisticare "to adulterate, make artificial, tamper with," from sophistes "sophist") + "-ly" (Old English -lice, forming adverbs)
