invoker:/ɪnˈvoʊkər/| n.|调用者，祈求者，施行者|One who invokes something, such as a god, a spirit, or a law.|The programmer invoked a function to calculate the sum.|近义词：caller, user, petitioner, supplicant|反义词: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *invoken*, from Old French *invoquer*, from Latin *invocare* "to call upon, invoke," from *in-* "upon" + *vocare* "to call."
