done:/dʌn/ | adj. | 完成的，结束的 | past participle of do, meaning finished, completed or cooked sufficiently |  The work is done. 工作完成了。| finished, completed, over | unfinished, incomplete | adjective

done:/dʌn/ | interjection |  好了，行了 | used to express agreement or to indicate the end of something | Done! I agree to your terms. 好了！我同意你的条件。 | agreed, settled |  | interjection


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *don, *doon, from Old English *gedōn, past participle of dōn (“to do”), from Proto-Germanic *dōną (“to do”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁- (“to put, place, do”). Cognate with Dutch doen (“to do”), German tun (“to do”), Gothic 𐍄𐌰𐌿𐌾𐌰𐌽 (taujan, “to do”).
