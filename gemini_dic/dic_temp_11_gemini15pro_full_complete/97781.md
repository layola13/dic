injudicable:/ɪnˈdʒuːdɪkəbl/ | adj. | 不可审判的，无法裁决的 | Not capable of being judged or decided; not subject to judicial determination. |  The issue of divine existence is often considered injudicable by human courts.  上帝是否存在的问题通常被认为是人类法庭无法裁决的。| Synonyms: nonjusticiable, unjudgeable, undecidable | Antonyms: judicable, justiciable, decidable | adjective
