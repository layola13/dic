nonveterans:/ˌnɒnˈvɛtərənz/ /ˌnɑːnˈvɛtərənz/
noun
非退伍军人；非老兵
people who have not served in the military.
Nonveterans often don't understand the challenges faced by soldiers returning home.  非退伍军人通常不理解士兵返乡所面临的挑战。
The program is open to both veterans and nonveterans. 该项目对退伍军人和非退伍军人开放。
synonyms:civilians
antonyms:veterans
