introspectionist:/ˌɪntrəˈspɛkʃənɪst/
n.|内省者；自省者|A person who examines their own thoughts and feelings.  Someone who practices introspection.|She considered herself an introspectionist, constantly analyzing her motivations and emotions.|近义词:self-examiner, analyst, contemplative|反义词:extrovert|词性:noun

Etymology:
From introspection + -ist. Introspection from intro- "inward" + spection "looking."  Ultimately from Latin introspicere "to look within," from intro- "within" + specere "to look."
