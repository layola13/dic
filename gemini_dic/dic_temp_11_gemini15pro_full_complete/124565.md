mourid:/ˈmʊərid/ | n. |穆里德，穆里德教徒 | A follower of the Muridiyya Sufi order, particularly common in Senegal.  | Example: The mourid made the pilgrimage to Touba.  穆里德教徒前往图巴朝圣。 | Example: Many mourids are also successful businesspeople. 许多穆里德教徒也是成功的商人。 | Synonym: disciple, follower, adherent | Antonym: opponent, detractor | Noun

Etymology:
From Arabic *murīd* (مُرِيد) meaning "desiring," "aspirant," or "seeker."  It refers to a seeker of spiritual guidance.
