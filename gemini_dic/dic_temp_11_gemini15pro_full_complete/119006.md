methodical:/məˈθɒdɪkəl/ (US) /məˈθɑːdɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|有条理的，井井有条的|Done according to a systematic or established procedure, marked by thoroughness and regularity.|She is a methodical worker, always planning and organizing her tasks carefully. 她是一个有条理的工作者，总是仔细地计划和组织她的任务。|例句：He adopted a methodical approach to solving the problem. 他采用有条理的方法来解决这个问题。|近义词：systematic, organized, orderly, structured|反义词：disorganized, chaotic, haphazard, random|词性:形容词

词源: Late 16th century: from French méthodique or late Latin methodicus, from Greek methodos ‘pursuit of knowledge, method’ (see method).
