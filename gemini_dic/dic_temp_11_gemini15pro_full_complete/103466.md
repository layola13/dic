jobation:/dʒoʊˈbeɪʃən/
n.
训斥，责骂，冗长的批评
a long and angry or critical speech
例句：He gave me a real jobation about being late. 他因为我迟到狠狠地训斥了我一顿。

例句：She received a jobation from her boss for missing the deadline.  她因为错过了截止日期而受到了老板的责骂。

近义词：scolding, reprimand, lecture, dressing-down, rebuke, reproof
反义词：praise, commendation, compliment
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From job, meaning "a piece of work," + -ation, a suffix forming nouns of action or process.  Originally, "jobation" referred to a piece of work, especially a difficult or tedious one.  Over time, the meaning shifted to refer to the tedious and often unpleasant experience of being scolded, as if it were a burdensome task.
