adjective

Covered with large scales or plates, like a shield; belonging to the Holaspidea, a suborder of marine gastropods characterized by a shell completely covered by the mantle.
The holaspidean sea slug has a distinctive flattened, shield-like shape.  全盾海参有着独特的扁平盾状外形。

反义词: unshielded, exposed
