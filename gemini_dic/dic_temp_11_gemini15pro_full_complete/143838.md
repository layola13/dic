outpitched:/ˌaʊtˈpɪtʃt/
verb
压制投球，在投球方面胜过
To surpass in pitching; to pitch better than.
The young ace outpitched the veteran in a thrilling duel.  这位年轻的王牌投手在一场扣人心弦的决斗中胜过了老将。
The home team's pitcher consistently outpitched their opponents throughout the series. 主队投手在整个系列赛中一直压制着对手。
近义词: outperformed, bested, defeated
反义词: underperformed, lost to

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'out-' (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + 'pitched' (past tense of 'pitch,' meaning to throw a baseball).
