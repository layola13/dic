leucoturic:/ˌluːkəˈtjʊərɪk/| adj.|白色的；与尿酸相关的|Relating to or designating a group of colourless or white crystalline nitrogenous organic acids, especially uric acid.|Leucoturic acid is a product of the oxidation of uric acid.|近义词:colorless, white, achromatic|反义词:colored, chromatic|词性:adjective

词源:  "leuco-" (Greek leukos, meaning "white") + "-turic" (related to uric acid)
