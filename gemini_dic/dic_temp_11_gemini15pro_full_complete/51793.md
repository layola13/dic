devourment:/dɪˈvaʊəmənt/ | n. |吞噬，吞食；狼吞虎咽 |The act of devouring something; consumption of something rapidly and voraciously. | The devourment of the cake by the children was quick and messy. 孩子们狼吞虎咽地把蛋糕吃得一团糟。 | 近义词: consumption, ingestion, devouring | 反义词: abstinence, fasting | 词性: 名词

词源: From the verb "devour," which comes from Old French "devorer" (to devour), from Latin "devorare" (to swallow down, consume), from "de-" (down) + "vorare" (to devour).
