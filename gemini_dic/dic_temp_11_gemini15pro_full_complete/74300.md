fractionating:/ˈfrækʃəneɪtɪŋ/
verb, adjective|分馏的，分级的|The process of separating a mixture into its component parts, especially by fractional distillation.  This can also be used as an adjective describing something that performs this process.|
The fractionating column separated the crude oil into different components. 分馏塔将原油分离成不同的组分。

The chemist was fractionating the mixture to isolate the desired compound. 化学家正在分馏混合物以分离所需的化合物。

近义词:separating, distilling, refining
反义词:combining, mixing
词性:verb (动词), adjective (形容词)
词源:From the verb "fractionate," which comes from the Latin "fractionatus," past participle of "fractionare," meaning "to break into pieces."  Ultimately from "frangere" meaning "to break."
