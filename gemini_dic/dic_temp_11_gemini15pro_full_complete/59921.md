edentulous:/iːˈdɛntʃʊləs/| adj.| 无牙的，缺齿的 | Lacking teeth; toothless. | The edentulous patient required dentures.  这位无牙病人需要假牙。|Synonyms: toothless,  |Antonyms: dentate, toothed | adjective

edentulous:/iːˈdɛntʃʊləs/| n.| 无齿类动物 | A mammal of the order Edentata (now Xenarthra), which includes sloths, anteaters, and armadillos. Many, but not all, members of this group lack teeth. |  The sloth is an edentulous animal.  树懒是一种无齿类动物。|Synonyms: xenarthran |Antonyms: N/A | noun
