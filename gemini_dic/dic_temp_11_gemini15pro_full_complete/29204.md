cashoo:/kæˈʃuː/ | n. | 腰果（坚果）; 腰果树 | A tropical American evergreen tree (Anacardium occidentale) bearing kidney-shaped nuts that are edible only when roasted.  The cashew nut is also the source of a caustic oil used in resins, varnishes, and insecticides. |  Example: I love to snack on cashews. 我喜欢吃腰果零食。

| Example:  The cashew tree is native to Brazil. 腰果树原产于巴西。 | Synonym: cashew nut | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific nut.  Could use a general antonym like "fruit" if contrasting categories) | noun
