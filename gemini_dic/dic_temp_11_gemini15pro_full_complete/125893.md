muscularities:/ˌmʌskjʊˈlærɪtiz/
n.
肌肉发达（状态），强壮（程度）
The state or quality of being muscular; muscular development.
He admired the muscularities of the weightlifters. 他羡慕举重运动员的肌肉发达程度。
近义词:muscles, brawn, strength
反义词:weakness, frailty
词性:名词
