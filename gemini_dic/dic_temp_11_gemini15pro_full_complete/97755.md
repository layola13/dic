initiates:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪts/ | v. | 开始，创始；发起；接纳；使入门 | to begin or cause something to begin |  The government initiated a new program to help the homeless. 政府启动了一项新的帮助无家可归者的计划。| 近义词: begins, starts, commences, introduces | 反义词: finishes, ends, concludes | 词性: 动词

initiates:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪts/ | n. | 新加入者，入门者 | a person who has recently joined a group or organization | The initiates were given a special welcome ceremony. 新成员们受到了特别的欢迎仪式。| 近义词: beginners, novices, learners | 反义词: experts, veterans | 词性: 名词
