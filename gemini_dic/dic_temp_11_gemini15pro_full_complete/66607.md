exigible:/ˈɛksɪdʒəbəl/
adjective
可要求的；应立即支付的；可强制执行的
Due or payable immediately; demandable.  Something that is exigible is required to be done or paid immediately.
The loan payment is exigible on the first of each month.  每月一号需支付贷款。
The invoice stated that the full amount was exigible within 30 days. 发票上写明全部款项需在30天内支付。
近义词: due, payable, demandable, claimable
反义词: deferrable, postponable
词性: adjective
词源: From Old French *exigible, from Latin exigibilis (“demandable”), from exigere (“to demand, exact, require, enforce”).
