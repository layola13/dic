encyclopediacal:/ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdiəkl/ /ɛnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪk(ə)l/
adjective
百科全书式的；知识渊博的
covering a wide range of knowledge, like an encyclopedia; comprehensive
例句：His knowledge of history is encyclopediacal.  他的历史知识非常渊博。

例句：She has an encyclopediacal grasp of French literature. 她对法国文学有着百科全书式的了解。

近义词：comprehensive, exhaustive, all-embracing, wide-ranging, vast, broad, extensive
反义词：limited, narrow, restricted, superficial
词性：adjective
