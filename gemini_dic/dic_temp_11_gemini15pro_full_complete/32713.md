chlorination:/ˌklɔːrɪˈneɪʃən/ /ˌklɔrəˈneɪʃən/| n.|氯化（作用）|The process of adding chlorine to something, typically water, to purify or disinfect it.|The chlorination of the swimming pool is done regularly. 游泳池定期进行氯化消毒。|近义词：disinfection, sterilization|反义词:dechlorination|noun
