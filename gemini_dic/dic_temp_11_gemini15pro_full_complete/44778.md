crumpy:/ˈkrʌmpi/| adj.|脾气暴躁的，易怒的|bad-tempered; irritable.  A person who is crumpy is easily annoyed and complains a lot. |He's been very crumpy all day. 他一整天都脾气很暴躁。|近义词：grumpy, irritable, bad-tempered, crabby, surly|反义词：cheerful, good-natured, pleasant|词性:形容词

词源:
Likely a blend of "crabbed" and "grumpy."  "Crabbed" has its roots in Old English and refers to something crooked or perverse. "Grumpy" is of uncertain origin, possibly imitative.
