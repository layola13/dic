cell-like:/ˈsɛlˌlaɪk/
adjective
细胞样的，类似细胞的
Resembling a cell in structure or function.
The researchers discovered a cell-like structure within the meteorite.  研究人员在陨石中发现了一个类似细胞的结构。
The artificial tissue had a cell-like appearance under the microscope.  在显微镜下，人造组织具有细胞状外观。
近义词:cellular, cell-shaped
反义词:acellular
