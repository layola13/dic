nonremediably:/ˌnɒnrɪˈmiːdiəbli/ | adv. | 不可挽回地，无法补救地 |  In a way that cannot be remedied or corrected. |  The bridge was nonremediably damaged by the flood. 这座桥被洪水破坏得无法修复。 |  irreparably, incurably, permanently, irretrievably | remediably, fixably | adverb

Etymology:
From non- + remediable + -ly.  "Remediable" comes from Late Latin *remediabilis, from Latin remedium ("remedy").  "Non-" is a prefix of negation.
