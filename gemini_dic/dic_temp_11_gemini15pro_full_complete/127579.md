nectarious:/nɛkˈtɛəriəs/
adjective
甜蜜的，富含花蜜的
Producing or containing nectar; sweet.
The nectarious blossoms attracted a multitude of bees.  这些富含花蜜的花朵吸引了大量的蜜蜂。
The hummingbird dipped its beak into the nectarious flower.  蜂鸟把喙伸进富含花蜜的花朵里。
近义词:sweet, honeyed, sugary
反义词:bitter, sour
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *nectar+ -ious.  From Latin nectar "drink of the gods," from Greek nektar, generally explained as *nē- + -ktar, from *kter- "to kill," hence originally "death-overcoming" (because of its supposed power to confer immortality).
