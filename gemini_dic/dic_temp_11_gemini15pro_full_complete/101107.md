invertebrate:/ɪnˈvɜːrtɪbrət/ /ɪnˈvɜːrtɪˌbreɪt/
adjective|无脊椎的|lacking a spinal column or backbone|Invertebrate animals include insects, spiders, and worms.|无脊椎动物包括昆虫、蜘蛛和蠕虫。
invertebrate:/ɪnˈvɜːrtɪbrət/ /ɪnˈvɜːrtɪˌbreɪt/
noun|无脊椎动物|an animal lacking a backbone|Many invertebrates live in the ocean.|许多无脊椎动物生活在海洋中。


Etymology->
From Latin *invertebratus*, from in- (“not”) + vertebratus (“vertebrated”).
