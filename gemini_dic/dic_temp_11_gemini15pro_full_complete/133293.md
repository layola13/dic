nonexpendable:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspendəbl/ /ˌnɑːnɪkˈspendəbl/| adj. n.|非消耗性的,非消耗品|Not meant to be expended or used up; designed for continued use or preservation.|例句：Nonexpendable supplies like tools are inventoried regularly.  像工具这样的非消耗性物资会被定期清点。|近义词：durable, reusable, permanent, essential|反义词：expendable, consumable, disposable|词性:形容词

nonexpendable:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspendəbl/ /ˌnɑːnɪkˈspendəbl/| n.|非消耗品|Something that is nonexpendable, such as military equipment or personnel.|例句：Trained soldiers are considered nonexpendables in modern warfare. 受过训练的士兵在现代战争中被认为是非消耗品。|近义词：resource, asset|反义词：expendable|词性:名词

词源: non- + expendable.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "expendable" comes from the Late Latin "expendere," meaning "to pay out."
