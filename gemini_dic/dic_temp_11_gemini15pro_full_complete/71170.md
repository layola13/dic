fissiped:/ˈfɪsɪped/| adj. n.|裂足的，裂足动物|Having separate toes or digits; divided into toes.  Relating to or denoting a fissiped mammal.|The dog is a fissiped animal. 狗是一种裂足动物。|Synonyms: digitigrade, toe-walking|Antonyms: ungulate|adjective
fissiped:/ˈfɪsɪped/| n.|裂足动物|A mammal having separate toes or digits, such as a dog or cat.|Fissipeds are a diverse group of mammals. 裂足动物是哺乳动物中种类繁多的一个群体。|Synonyms: digitigrade|Antonyms: ungulate|noun
