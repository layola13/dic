immorally:/ɪˈmɔːrəli/
adverb|不道德地，邪恶地|in a way that is not moral; wickedly|He acted immorally by accepting the bribe. 他接受贿赂的行为是不道德的。|Example:  The dictator used his power immorally, enriching himself while his people starved. 独裁者不道德地利用他的权力，让自己富裕起来，而他的人民却在挨饿。

Example:  She knew it was immorally to cheat on the exam, but she did it anyway. 她知道考试作弊是不道德的，但她还是这么做了。|Synonyms: unethically, wrongly, wickedly, sinfully|Antonyms: morally, ethically, righteously, virtuously|词性: 副词
