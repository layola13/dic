faculae:/ˈfækjʊliː/
noun
光斑; 光亮区
Bright patches or spots on the sun's surface, appearing brighter than the surrounding areas.
Faculae are often associated with sunspots and are regions of higher temperature and magnetic activity.
The astronomer observed several faculae near the sun's equator. 天文学家在太阳赤道附近观察到几个光斑。
synonyms:bright spots, plages
antonyms:sunspots (somewhat, as they often appear together)
