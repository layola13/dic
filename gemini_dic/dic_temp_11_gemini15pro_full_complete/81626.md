greco:/ˈɡreɪkoʊ/ /ˈɡriːkəʊ/
adjective
希腊式的;希腊风格的;希腊文化的
Of or relating to Greece, its people, or their language.
The museum has a fascinating collection of Greco-Roman artifacts.  博物馆收藏了引人入胜的希腊罗马文物。
The restaurant serves Greco-Turkish cuisine. 这家餐厅供应希腊土耳其菜肴。
近义词:Greek, Hellenic
反义词:Roman, Latin
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *greco*, from Latin *Graecus*, from Ancient Greek *Γραικός (Graikós)*.
