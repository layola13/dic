halvers:/ˈhɑːvərz/ | n. | 平分者；对分者 | People who divide something into two equal parts. | The two halvers split the winnings from the lottery. 这两个平分者瓜分了彩票的奖金。 | synonyms: dividers, splitters | antonyms: unifiers, joiners | noun

halves:/hɑːvz/ | n. | 一半；二分之一 | One of two equal parts of something.  This is also the plural of "half". | The two halves of the orange were not equal in size.  这个橙子的两半大小不一样。| synonyms: portions, sections | antonyms: wholes, totals | noun
