oversell: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛl/ /ˌəʊvəˈsel/
verb
过度销售；吹嘘
to sell more of something than can be delivered or to praise something too much
The salesman oversold the car, claiming it could get 50 miles per gallon when it really only got 30.  这个销售员夸大了这辆车的性能，声称它每加仑汽油可以行驶50英里，而实际上只有30英里。
The company oversold its shares in the IPO. 公司在首次公开募股中超额发售了股票。
Synonyms: exaggerate, hype, overstate, overpraise
Antonyms: undersell, downplay, understate
词性: 动词
