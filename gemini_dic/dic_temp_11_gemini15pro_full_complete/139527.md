obtains:/əbˈteɪnz/
verb
获得，得到
comes into possession of; acquires
The company obtained a grant for the project.  公司获得了该项目的拨款。
He obtained his degree in 1995. 他于1995年获得了学位。
synonyms: acquire, gain, secure, procure, attain
antonyms: lose, forfeit, relinquish
词性:动词
