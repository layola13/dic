outbidding:/ˌaʊtˈbɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 出价高于（别人）; 超过(别人)的出价 | the act of offering a higher price than someone else for something, especially at an auction |  She won the auction by outbidding everyone else. 她通过出价比其他人高赢得了拍卖。 | synonyms: overbidding, topping | antonyms: underbidding | noun

outbidding:/ˌaʊtˈbɪdɪŋ/ | v. |  出价高于（别人） | to offer a higher price than someone else for something |  He outbid me for the antique chair. 他出价超过了我，买下了那把古董椅子。| synonyms: overbid, top | antonyms: underbid | verb
