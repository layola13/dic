organoid:/ˈɔːrɡənɔɪd/| n. |类器官|A miniature, simplified version of an organ produced in vitro in three dimensions that shows realistic micro-anatomy. |Scientists are growing brain organoids to study neurological diseases.|Synonyms: mini-organ, tissue culture, 3D cell culture|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "whole organ," "in vivo organ")|Noun
