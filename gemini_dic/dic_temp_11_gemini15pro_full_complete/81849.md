grid-ironed:/ˈɡrɪdˌaɪərnd/| adj.|熨得笔挺的，刻板的，一丝不苟的|Having a stiff, rigid, or excessively neat appearance, as if pressed with a grid iron.  This often implies an unnatural or overly formal quality.  It comes from the image of using a grid iron (originally used for cooking) to press clothes, resulting in a very crisp, even, and inflexible appearance.|His grid-ironed shirt made him look uncomfortable in the casual setting.  他熨得笔挺的衬衫让他在轻松的环境中显得格格不入。|Synonyms: crisp, starched, pressed, immaculate, impeccable|Antonyms: rumpled, wrinkled, creased, dishevelled, unkempt|adjective
