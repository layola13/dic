meandered:/miˈændərd/ | v. | 漫步，蜿蜒而行 | followed a winding or indirect course; wandered aimlessly | The river meandered through the valley. 这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。| 近义词：wandered, rambled, strayed, roamed | 反义词：rushed, hurried | 词性: 动词

词源: From the Greek Μαίανδρος (Maiandros), the name of a winding river in Phrygia (now Turkey), known for its winding course.
