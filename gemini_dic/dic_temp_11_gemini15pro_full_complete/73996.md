fortuitousnesses:/fɔːrˌtuːɪtəsnəsɪz/
|n.|偶然性；意外事件|The quality of being fortuitous; the state of occurring by chance.|The series of fortuitousnesses that led to their meeting seemed like fate.  他们相遇的一系列偶然事件像是命中注定。|Synonyms:  accidents, chances, coincidences, contingencies, flukes, fortunes, happenings, lucks, strokes of luck|Antonyms:  intentionalities, planned events, certainties|noun

fortuitous:/fɔːrˈtuːɪtəs/
|adj.|偶然的；意外的；幸运的|Happening by accident or chance rather than design.|It was fortuitous that he was home when I called. 我打电话时他碰巧在家，真是幸运。|Synonyms:  accidental, casual, chance, coincidental, contingent, fluky, fortunate, happy, lucky, providential, random, serendipitous, unintentional, unplanned, unpremeditated|Antonyms:  calculated, deliberate, designed, intentional, planned, premeditated|adjective

词源:

From Latin *fortuitus* "happening by chance," from *forte* "by chance," ablative of *fors* "chance."
