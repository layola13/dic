deferring:/dɪˈfɜːrɪŋ/
v. | 推迟，延期； 顺从，遵从|  The company is deferring investment in new equipment.  该公司正在推迟对新设备的投资。| 近义词：postponing, delaying, yielding | 反义词：expediting, advancing, resisting | 词性：动词 (present participle of defer)


defer:/dɪˈfɜːr/
v. | 推迟，延期； 顺从，遵从|  I have decided to defer my university entrance. 我决定推迟我的大学入学。| 近义词：postpone, delay, yield | 反义词：expedite, advance, resist | 词性：动词

词源: From Old French differer, from Latin differre "to set apart, scatter, spread, put off, delay," from dis- "apart" + ferre "to carry, bear."
