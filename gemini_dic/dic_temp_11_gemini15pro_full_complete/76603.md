gallivant:/ˈɡælɪvænt/ /ˈɡæləˌvænt/| v. | 漫游，游荡，闲逛 | To wander or travel around for pleasure and without a definite purpose. | He spent his twenties gallivanting around Europe. 他二十多岁的时候都在欧洲各地游荡。


She's been gallivanting all over the country instead of looking for a job. 她一直在全国各地游荡，而不是去找工作。

| 近义词： roam, wander, traipse, gad about | 反义词： stay, remain, settle down | 词性: 动词
