noncommutativities:/ˌnɑːnkəˌmjuːtəˈtɪvɪtiz/ | n. | 非交换性(复数) | The property of not being commutative;  referring to operations where the order of application matters.  Specifically, if operation * is noncommutative, then a * b ≠ b * a for some a and b. | The noncommutativities of matrix multiplication are crucial in quantum mechanics. 矩阵乘法的非交换性在量子力学中至关重要。 | synonyms: non-commutabilities | antonyms: commutativities | noun
