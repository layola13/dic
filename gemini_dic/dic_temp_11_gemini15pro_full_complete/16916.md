basketworks:/ˈbæskɪtwɜːrks/
n.
手工编织品；柳条制品；篮筐工艺
Objects made of interwoven twigs, reeds, or other flexible materials, typically used for carrying or storing things.  The craft of making such objects.
The museum displayed intricate Native American basketworks.  博物馆展出了复杂的美国原住民手工编织品。
近义词：wickerwork, basketry
