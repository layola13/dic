floodwater:/ˈflʌdwɔːtər/ /ˈflʌdˌwɑːtər/
n.
洪水
The water that overflows, typically from rivers or seas, during a flood.
The floodwater reached the rooftops of some houses. 洪水淹到了有些房子的屋顶。
近义词：flood, deluge, inundation
反义词：(None -  lack of floodwater would simply be normal water levels)
词性：名词
