callisthenics:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪks/| n. |健美操，徒手体操|Systematic rhythmic bodily exercises performed usually without apparatus.|例句：She practices callisthenics every morning to stay fit. 她每天早上做健美操以保持健康。

例句：Callisthenics is a great way to build strength and flexibility. 健美操是增强力量和柔韧性的好方法。|近义词：exercises, workout, gymnastics|反义词:inactivity, laziness|词性:noun
