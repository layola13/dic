mucilage:/ˈmjuːsɪlɪdʒ/| n. |黏液；胶水；植物分泌的粘性物质|A thick, sticky, gelatinous substance produced by plants and some microorganisms.  It is often used as an adhesive or as a thickener in various products.|The cactus uses mucilage to retain water. 仙人掌利用黏液来保持水分。|Synonyms: glue, paste, gum, adhesive|Antonyms: solvent|noun

mucilage:/ˈmjuːsɪlɪdʒ/| n. |黏液；胶水；植物分泌的粘性物质|A thick, sticky, gelatinous substance produced by plants and some microorganisms. It can also refer to a solution of a gum in water.|The seeds are coated in a mucilage that helps them stick to the ground. 种子被一层黏液包裹，帮助它们粘在地上。|Synonyms: slime, goo, gel|Antonyms: dryness|noun
