cobaltite:/ˈkoʊbɔːltaɪt/| n. |钴矿，辉钴矿|A mineral consisting essentially of cobalt sulfarsenide (CoAsS). It is an important ore of cobalt.|Cobaltite is a significant source of cobalt, a metal crucial for battery production. 钴矿是钴的重要来源，钴是电池生产的关键金属。|Synonyms: cobalt glance|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
