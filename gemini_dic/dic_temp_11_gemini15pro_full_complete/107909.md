larum:/ˈlɛərəm/ | n. | 喧嚣，警报，号角 |A loud noise or a call to arms, especially one made by a bell or trumpet. |The larum of the fire alarm woke everyone in the building. 火灾警报的喧嚣声吵醒了大楼里的所有人。|Synonym: alarm, alert, clamor, din |Antonym: silence, quiet |noun

larum:/ˈlɛərəm/ | v. | (古)发出警报，惊动 |To sound an alarm or make a loud noise. |The watchman larumed the town when he saw the approaching enemy. 守夜人看到敌人逼近时，向全镇发出警报。|Synonym: alarm, warn, alert |Antonym: soothe, calm |verb
