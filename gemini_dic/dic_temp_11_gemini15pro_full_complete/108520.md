layshaft:/ˈleɪʃæft/| n.|中间轴，副轴|A secondary shaft that transmits motion to or from a main shaft.|The layshaft in the gearbox allows for different gear ratios. 变速箱中的中间轴允许多种不同的齿轮比。|The layshaft drives the rear wheels. 中间轴驱动后轮。|countershaft, jackshaft|mainshaft|noun
