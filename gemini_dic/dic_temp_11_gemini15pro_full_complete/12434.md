askewnesses:/əˈskjuːnɪsɪz/| n. |偏度，歪斜度|A measure of the asymmetry of the probability distribution of a real-valued random variable about its mean.  It describes the degree to which a distribution is skewed to one side or the other. |The askewnesses of the data suggest a non-normal distribution.|近义词：skewness, asymmetry, lopsidedness|反义词:symmetry, evenness|词性:noun
