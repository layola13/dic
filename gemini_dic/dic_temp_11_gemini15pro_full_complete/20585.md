blackened:/ˈblækənd/| adj.|变黑的，熏黑的，诽谤的|Made black or dark, often by burning or charring; also, tarnished or defamed.|The fire blackened the walls of the room. 火把房间的墙壁熏黑了。|Synonyms: charred, scorched, darkened, tarnished, defamed|Antonyms: whitened, brightened, cleaned, purified|Word Type: Adjective

blacken:/ˈblækən/| vt. vi.|使变黑，熏黑，诽谤|To make or become black or dark, often by burning or charring; also, to tarnish or defame someone's reputation.|The smoke blackened the ceiling. 烟把天花板熏黑了.  He tried to blacken her name with false accusations. 他试图用不实的指控败坏她的名声。|Synonyms: char, scorch, darken, tarnish, defame|Antonyms: whiten, brighten, clean, purify|Word Type: Verb
