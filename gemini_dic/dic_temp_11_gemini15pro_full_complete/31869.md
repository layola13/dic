cheeriness:/ˈtʃɪriːnɪs/
n.
快乐，高兴，兴高采烈
The state of being cheerful; happiness and good spirits.
Her cheeriness was infectious, brightening everyone's day. 她的快乐很有感染力，让每个人都开心了起来。
近义词：happiness, gaiety, merriment, joyfulness, lightheartedness
反义词：gloom, sadness, dejection, melancholy, glumness
词性: 名词
