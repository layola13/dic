apagogic:/ˌæpəˈɡɒdʒɪk/ | adj. |  间接论证的，反证法的，归谬法的 | Relating to or involving apagogy, a method of proof by showing the absurdity of denying the proposition to be proved. | The apagogic argument demonstrated the theorem by proving that its negation led to a contradiction.  间接论证法通过证明其否定导致矛盾来证明这个定理。|  The lawyer used an apagogic line of reasoning to prove his client's innocence. 律师用反证法来证明他的客户是无辜的。 | synonyms: indirect, reductio ad absurdum | antonyms: direct, deductive | adjective
