lovelier:/ˈlʌvliər/| adj.|更可爱的，更美丽的|Comparative form of lovely.  Used to describe something that is more beautiful or attractive than something else.|The sunset tonight is even lovelier than last night's.|近义词：prettier, more attractive, more beautiful|反义词：uglier, less attractive, less beautiful|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *loveliker, comparative of lovely; equivalent to lovely + -er. Ultimately from Old English luflic.
