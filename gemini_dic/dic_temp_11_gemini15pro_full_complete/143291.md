outdraught: /ˈaʊtdrɑːft/ | n. | 外吹气流；通风；泄风 | A current of air moving outwards, as from a room or building. |  The outdraught from the open window extinguished the candle.  从敞开的窗户吹进来的风吹灭了蜡烛。| synonyms: draft, draught, current of air | antonyms: indraught | noun

outdraught: /ˈaʊtdrɑːft/ | n. | (尤指啤酒的) 外销 |  The sale or export of goods, especially beer, to other countries. | The brewery increased its outdraught to meet the growing international demand. 这家啤酒厂增加了外销量以满足日益增长的国际需求。| synonyms: export, sale abroad | antonyms: import | noun
