ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forboden, from Old English forbēodan (“to forbid, prohibit”), from for- (“for-, fore-”) + bēodan (“to command, offer, bid”).  The sense of foreboding developed from the idea of a prohibition or warning against something.
