decimals: /ˈdɛsɪməlz/ | n. | 小数 | A fraction whose denominator is a power of ten and whose numerator is expressed by figures placed to the right of a decimal point. |  The number 3.14 is a decimal.  这个数字 3.14 是一个小数。 | synonyms: fraction, decimal fraction | antonyms: whole number, integer | noun

decimals: /ˈdɛsɪməlz/ | n. | 小数位 | The figures to the right of the decimal point. |  Round to two decimal places.  四舍五入到两位小数。 | synonyms: decimal places, fractional part | antonyms: integral part | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *decimalis, from Latin decimus (“tenth”), from decem (“ten”).
