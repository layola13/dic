commitments:/kəˈmɪtmənts/
n.
承诺，保证，义务；(时间、金钱等的)花费，使用
agreements or promises to do something; amounts of time, money etc. that are used for a particular purpose
I have a lot of commitments. 我有很多承诺。
commitments (to somebody/something)  承担的义务；承诺的事情
I've got too many commitments at the moment. 我现在承担的义务太多了。
financial commitments 财务承诺
synonym: obligations, pledges, undertakings
antonym:  disavowals, renunciations
noun
