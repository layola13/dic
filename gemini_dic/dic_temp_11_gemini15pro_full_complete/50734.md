derogatorinesses:/dɪˈrɒɡətərɪnɪsɪz/| n. |贬低；贬损；轻蔑|The quality or state of being derogatory; instances of derogatory language or behavior.  The plural form of "derogatoriness." |His speech was filled with derogatorinesses aimed at his opponent.|近义词：disparagements, deprecations, slights|反义词:praises, compliments|Noun

Etymology:
From derogatory + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Derogatory" comes from Late Latin *derogatorius, from Latin derogare "to repeal (a law), detract from," from de- "away" + rogare "to ask, propose a law."
