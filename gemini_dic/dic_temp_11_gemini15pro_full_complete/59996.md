editorialises:/ˌedɪˈtɔːriəlaɪzɪz/
verb
发表社论；在文章、讲话等中发表个人意见
expresses opinions in an editorial manner, often with a critical or subjective perspective
The news anchor editorialised on the recent political scandal.  新闻主播就最近的政治丑闻发表了个人意见。
comments, opines, offers opinions
reports, states facts
v.
