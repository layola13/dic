inexhausted:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔːstɪd/
adjective
未耗尽的，不疲倦的
Not exhausted; tireless.
His seemingly inexhaustible energy amazed everyone. 他似乎用不完的精力让每个人都感到惊讶。
近义词: tireless, unwearied, indefatigable
反义词: exhausted, tired, weary
词性: adjective
