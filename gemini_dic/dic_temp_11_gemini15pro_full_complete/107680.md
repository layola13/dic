lanioid:/ˈlæniɔɪd/| adj.|羊毛状的，似羊毛的|Resembling wool; woolly.  Covered with long, soft hairs like wool.|The laniioid caterpillar was covered in fine, silky hairs.  这只羊毛状的毛虫身上覆盖着细细的丝状毛。|近义词：woolly, fleecy, downy|反义词:bald, hairless|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *lāna (“wool”) + -oid (“resembling”).
