agape:/əˈɡɑːpeɪ/ | adj. | 张着嘴的，目瞪口呆的；(精神上)渴望的 | With mouth wide open, especially showing surprise or wonder; feeling or showing great love for someone |  He stared at her agape. 他目瞪口呆地盯着她。 | 近义词: open-mouthed, astonished, amazed, loving | 反义词: closed, uninterested, apathetic | 词性: 形容词

agape:/ˈæɡəpi/ | n. |  (基督教)博爱，神爱；爱宴 |  Christian love, especially as distinct from erotic love or emotional affection; a love feast held by early Christians in commemoration of the Last Supper |  He spoke of the importance of agape in the Christian faith. 他谈到了博爱在基督教信仰中的重要性。 | 近义词: charity, brotherly love, unconditional love | 反义词: hatred, malice | 词性: 名词

词源:
From Ancient Greek ἀγάπη (agápē, “love, affection”).
