hawklike:/ˈhɔːklaɪk/| adj.|像鹰的；鹰一般的；目光锐利的|Resembling a hawk in appearance or behavior, especially having keen eyesight or a predatory nature.|He had a hawklike nose and piercing blue eyes. 他有着鹰钩鼻和锐利的蓝眼睛。|近义词：aquiline, sharp-eyed, keen, piercing, predatory|反义词：dovish, peaceful, gentle|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From hawk +‎ -like.
