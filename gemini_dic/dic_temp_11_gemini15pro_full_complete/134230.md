nonindustry:/ˌnɑːnˈɪndəstri/
noun
非工业
Not belonging to or involved with industry; not industrial.
例句：The nonindustry sectors of the economy, such as services and tourism, are growing rapidly.  经济的非工业部门，如服务业和旅游业，正在快速增长。

例句：Many people in the town find employment in nonindustry jobs.  镇上的许多人在非工业领域找到了工作。

近义词：non-industrial
反义词：industry, industrial
词性：noun
