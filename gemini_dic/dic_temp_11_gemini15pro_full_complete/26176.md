butcherly:/ˈbʊtʃərli/
adjective
残暴的; 屠夫般的; 笨手笨脚的
Resembling or characteristic of a butcher; crudely or clumsily done; brutally cruel or violent.
The surgery was performed in a butcherly manner.  手术进行得非常粗暴。
The dictator's butcherly regime was responsible for countless deaths.  这个独裁者的残暴政权造成了无数人的死亡。
近义词: brutal, savage, cruel, violent, bloody, gory, callous
反义词: gentle, kind, merciful, compassionate, humane
词性: adjective
词源: From butcher + -ly.  源自 butcher（屠夫） + -ly（形容词后缀）。
