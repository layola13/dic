demagogies: /ˈdɛməɡɒɡiz/ (UK) /ˈdɛməɡɑːdʒiz/ (US) | n. | 煽动政治，蛊惑人心 |  The plural form of demagogy, referring to the practices or instances of appealing to popular desires and prejudices rather than using rational argument. |  His speeches were filled with demagogies, designed to inflame public sentiment against immigrants. 他的演讲充满了煽动性的言论，旨在激起公众对移民的仇恨情绪。 | The recent rise in political demagogies is a worrying trend for democratic societies. 最近政治煽动行为的增加是民主社会的一个令人担忧的趋势。 |  Near synonyms: rabble-rousing, incendiary rhetoric, appeals to prejudice  | Near antonyms: reasoned discourse, logical argumentation, appeals to reason | noun
