The doctrine or belief that there is only one God.|Judaism, Christianity, and Islam are examples of monotheisms. 犹太教、基督教和伊斯兰教都是一神教的例子。|Near synonyms: monotheistic religions, monotheistic beliefs|Antonyms: polytheisms, paganisms|noun
