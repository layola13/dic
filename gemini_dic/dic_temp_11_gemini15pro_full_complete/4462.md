albe:/ˈɔːlbɪ/ |conj. |虽然；即使| Although; even if; notwithstanding. | Albe he was tired, he continued working. 虽然他很累，但他继续工作。| He finished the race, albe it took him longer than expected. 虽然他花的时间比预期的长，但他完成了比赛。| 近义词: although, though, even though, notwithstanding, albeit | 反义词: yet, but, however | 词性: 连词


词源:
Middle English *al be it, from al all + be subjunctive of be + it.  Essentially a shortened form of "albeit."
