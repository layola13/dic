christocentric:/ˌkraɪstoʊˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|以基督为中心的|Having Christ as the central focus.|例句：His theology is profoundly christocentric. 他的神学深受以基督为中心的思想影响。

例句：The christocentric approach emphasizes the importance of Jesus in understanding the Bible. 以基督为中心的方法强调耶稣在理解圣经中的重要性。|近义词：Christian-centered, Jesus-centered|反义词: secular, worldly|词性:adjective

词源:
From Greek *Christos* (Christ) + *kentron* (center) + *-ikos* (forming adjectives).
