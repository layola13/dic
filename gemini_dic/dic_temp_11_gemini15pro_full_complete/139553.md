obtunder:/əbˈtʌndər/| v. |使迟钝；使减弱；压抑|To dull or reduce the intensity or acuteness of (a sensation, faculty, etc.); to deaden.|The heavy meal obtunded our senses, making us sleepy and sluggish.|近义词：dull, blunt, weaken, suppress, dampen, deaden|反义词：sharpen, heighten, intensify, stimulate|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *obtundere, from ob- 'against, towards' + tundere 'to beat, blunt'.
