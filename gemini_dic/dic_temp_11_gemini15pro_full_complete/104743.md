USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Who comes here often? = 谁经常来这里？
Who knows the answer? = 谁知道答案？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Who is knocking at the door? = 谁在敲门？
Who is talking on the phone? = 谁在打电话？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Who has eaten all the cookies? = 谁吃了所有的饼干？
Who has finished the assignment? = 谁完成了作业？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Who has been using my computer? = 谁一直在用我的电脑？
Who has been playing the piano? = 谁一直在弹钢琴？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Who called me last night? = 昨晚谁给我打电话了？
Who broke the vase? = 谁打碎了花瓶？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Who was sleeping in my bed? = 谁睡在我的床上？
Who was singing that song? = 谁在唱那首歌？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Who had arrived before me? = 谁在我之前到了？
Who had taken my book? = 谁拿走了我的书？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Who had been waiting for the bus? = 谁一直在等公共汽车？
Who had been living in this house? = 谁一直住在这所房子里？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Who will go to the party? = 谁将去参加聚会？
Who will win the election? = 谁将赢得选举？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Who will be attending the meeting? = 谁将出席会议？
Who will be cooking dinner tonight? = 今晚谁将做饭？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Who will have finished the project by Friday? = 谁将在星期五之前完成这个项目？
Who will have left by the time I arrive? = 在我到达之前谁将离开？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Who will have been working on this problem for the longest time? = 谁将在这个问题上工作最长时间？
Who will have been studying English for 10 years by then? = 到那时谁将已经学习英语10年了？


虚拟语气:
If I knew who did it, I would tell you. = 如果我知道是谁做的，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew who to ask for help. = 我希望我知道该向谁寻求帮助。

被动语态:
By whom was this letter written? = 这封信是谁写的？（正式）
Who was this book written by? = 这本书是谁写的？

疑问句:
Who are you? = 你是谁？
Who is that woman? = 那个女人是谁？

否定句:
I don't know who he is. = 我不知道他是谁。
She doesn't know who to trust. = 她不知道该相信谁。



PHRASE->
Who knows? = 谁知道呢？
Who cares? = 谁在乎？
Who's there? = 谁在那儿？
Who are you kidding? = 你在开玩笑吧？
Who do you think you are? = 你以为你是谁？

注意事项:
kaun不是英语单词，而是印地语和乌尔都语单词。以上例句和用法说明都是基于英语中疑问代词 "who" 的用法，因为 "kaun" 在印地语和乌尔都语中的作用与 "who" 在英语中的作用相同。
