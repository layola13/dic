fumeless:/ˈfjuːmləs/
adjective
无烟的
Producing or emitting no fumes; smokeless.
The company is developing a fumeless cigarette. 这家公司正在开发一种无烟香烟。
Synonyms: smokeless, odorless, scentless
Antonyms: smoky, fuming
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fume + -less.  Fume comes from Old French fum, ultimately from Latin fumus "smoke."  -less is a suffix meaning "without."
