chit-chat:/ˈtʃɪt tʃæt/
n.|闲聊，聊天|Light informal conversation.|We spent the afternoon chit-chatting about nothing in particular. 我们一下午都在闲聊一些无关紧要的事情.
|synonyms: gossip, small talk, chitchat|antonyms: debate, discussion, argument|noun

chit-chat:/ˈtʃɪt tʃæt/
vi.|闲聊，聊天|To engage in light informal conversation.|They were chit-chatting by the water cooler. 他们在饮水机旁闲聊。
|synonyms: gossip, chatter, gab|antonyms: debate, discuss, argue|verb
