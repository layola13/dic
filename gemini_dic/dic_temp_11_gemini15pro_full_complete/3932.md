agritourism:/ˈæɡrɪtʊərɪzəm/| n.|农业旅游|A form of tourism in which tourists stay on farms or in agricultural areas, and participate in farm activities or experience rural life.|Example 1: Agritourism is becoming increasingly popular, offering city dwellers a chance to connect with nature. 农业旅游越来越受欢迎，为城市居民提供了与自然接触的机会。

Example 2:  The family enjoyed their agritourism vacation, picking apples and learning about farm animals. 这家人很享受他们的农业旅游假期，摘苹果，了解农场动物。|Synonym: farm tourism, rural tourism|Antonym: urban tourism|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *agri-* (combining form meaning "agriculture") + *tourism*. *Agri-* is derived from the Latin word *ager*, meaning "field".
