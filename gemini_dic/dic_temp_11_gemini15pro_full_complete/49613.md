demi-puppet:/ˈdɛmiˌpʌpɪt/ | n. | 半傀儡；受人操纵的人 | A person or organization that appears to have some autonomy but is actually largely controlled by another power or influence.  | The mayor was seen as a demi-puppet of the powerful business interests in the city. 市长被认为是这座城市强大商业利益集团的半傀儡。

| The seemingly independent country was in reality a demi-puppet of its larger neighbor.  这个看似独立的国家实际上是其较大邻国的半傀儡。 |  puppet, pawn, tool | independent, autonomous | noun
