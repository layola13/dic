gauzelike: /ˈɡɔːzlaɪk/ | adj. | 薄纱似的，像纱布的 | resembling gauze; thin and translucent |  The dress was made of a gauzelike material that flowed in the breeze. 这件衣服是用一种像纱布一样的材料做的，在微风中飘动。| The curtains were a gauzelike white, allowing sunlight to filter through. 窗帘是薄纱般的白色，让阳光可以透过。 |  sheer, filmy, diaphanous, transparent | opaque, thick, dense | adjective

Etymology:
From gauze +‎ -like. Gauze itself comes from Old French gaze, from Gaza, a city in Palestine where it was originally made.
