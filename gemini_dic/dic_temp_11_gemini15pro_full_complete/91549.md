hyper-femininity:/ˌhaɪpərˌfɛmɪˈnɪnɪti/ | n. |过度女性化；极端女性化 |  An exaggerated adherence to stereotypically feminine gender roles, often involving an emphasis on physical appearance, submissiveness, and emotional expressiveness. | Her hyper-femininity, with the long flowing dresses and delicate jewelry, seemed out of place in the boardroom. 她极端的女性化，穿着飘逸的长裙，戴着精致的珠宝，在董事会会议室里显得格格不入。 |例句： The marketing campaign played on notions of hyper-femininity to target a specific demographic.  这个营销活动利用了过度女性化的概念来针对特定的人群。 | 近义词: ultra-femininity, exaggerated femininity | 反义词: masculinity, androgyny | 词性: noun

hyper-feminine:/ˌhaɪpərˈfɛmɪnɪn/ | adj. | 过度女性化的；极端女性化的 |  Excessively or exaggeratedly feminine. |  She adopted a hyper-feminine persona for her performance. 她在表演中展现了一个极度女性化的角色。 | 例句：The character's hyper-feminine attire contrasted sharply with her assertive personality.  这个角色过度女性化的服装与她自信的个性形成了鲜明的对比。 | 近义词: ultra-feminine, exaggeratedly feminine | 反义词: masculine, androgynous | 词性: adjective
