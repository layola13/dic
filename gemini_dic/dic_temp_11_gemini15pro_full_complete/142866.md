out-mastered:/ˌaʊtˈmæstərd/
|v.|胜过，技高一筹|To surpass or defeat someone through greater skill or mastery, especially in a game, competition, or other skillful activity.|He out-mastered his opponent in the chess tournament. 他在国际象棋比赛中技高一筹，战胜了对手。| The young apprentice quickly out-mastered his teacher in the art of calligraphy.  年轻的学徒很快在书法艺术上超过了他的老师。| 近义词：outdo, surpass, excel, defeat, conquer, triumph over|反义词：lose to, be defeated by|词性:动词
