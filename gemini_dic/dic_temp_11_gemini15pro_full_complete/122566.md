moderately:/ˈmɒdərətli/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərətli/ (US)| adv.|适度地，中等地|In a moderate manner; not extremely or excessively.  It describes something done to a medium extent or degree.|The hike was moderately difficult.  这次徒步旅行难度适中。|Synonyms: reasonably, fairly, tolerably, quite, somewhat|Antonyms: extremely, excessively, immoderately|adverb
