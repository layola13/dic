angelus: /ˈændʒələs/| n.|三钟经；天使钟声|A Catholic devotion commemorating the Incarnation by the recitation of three Hail Marys at morning, noon, and sunset, often accompanied by the ringing of a bell.|The church bells rang the Angelus at noon. 教堂的钟声在中午响起了三钟经。|Synonyms: prayer, devotion|Antonyms: blasphemy, irreverence|Noun
