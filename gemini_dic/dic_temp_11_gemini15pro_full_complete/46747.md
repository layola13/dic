dandruffy:/ˈdændrʌfi/| adj.|头皮屑多的，有头皮屑的|Covered with or producing dandruff; flaky.|例句：He has a dandruffy scalp. 他头皮屑很多。|近义词：flaky, scaly|反义词: clean, clear|词性:形容词
词源：From dandruff + -y.  Dandruff itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to an obsolete word "dandriff," perhaps of Scandinavian origin.
