organ-grinder:/ˈɔːrɡənˌɡraɪndər/ | n. |街头手摇风琴演奏者；街头卖艺人 |A person who plays a barrel organ, typically on the streets, to entertain passersby and solicit money. |The organ-grinder cranked the handle, and a jaunty tune filled the air. 街头手摇风琴演奏者摇动手柄，欢快的曲调弥漫在空气中。 | The organ-grinder's monkey danced for the amused crowd.  街头艺人的猴子为逗乐的人群跳舞。 |Synonyms: busker, street musician, street performer |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "concert musician" as a contrast) | noun
