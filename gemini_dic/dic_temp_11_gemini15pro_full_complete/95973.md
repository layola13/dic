incumbency:/ɪnˈkʌmbənsi/| n.|任期；责任；义务；现任；在职|The state of holding an office or position; the duties and responsibilities associated with an office or position; the period during which someone holds an office or position.|The president's incumbency was marked by economic growth. 总统的任期以经济增长为标志。|Synonyms: tenure, term, office, position, duty, responsibility|Antonyms: vacancy, unemployment|noun


incumbencies:/ɪnˈkʌmbənsiz/| n.|任期（复数）；责任（复数）；义务（复数）；现任（复数）；在职（复数）|Plural form of incumbency, referring to multiple periods of holding office, multiple sets of responsibilities, etc.|The various incumbencies of the board members overlapped. 董事会成员的各个任期重叠了。|Synonyms: tenures, terms, offices, positions, duties, responsibilities|Antonyms: vacancies, unemployment|noun
