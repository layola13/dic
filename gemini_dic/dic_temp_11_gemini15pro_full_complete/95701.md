inconformity's:/ˌɪnkənˈfɔːrmətiz/ | n. |不整合,不一致| The state or quality of not conforming; nonconformity; lack of agreement or harmony.  It particularly refers to a geological discontinuity. |  The geologist pointed out the unconformity's significance in understanding the region's history.  地质学家指出了不整合面对于理解该地区历史的重要性。|Near synonyms:  discordance, disharmony, variance, discrepancy,  nonconformity | Near antonyms: conformity, agreement, harmony, concordance | noun
