contrapuntist:/ˈkɒntrəpʌntɪst/ (US) /ˈkɑːntrəpɔɪntɪst/| n. |对位法家；复调音乐家|A person skilled in or specializing in counterpoint, the art of combining different melodic lines in a harmonious way. | Bach was a master contrapuntist. 巴赫是一位对位法大师。|  The young contrapuntist impressed the judges with his intricate composition.  这位年轻的对位法家以其复杂的作曲给评委留下了深刻的印象。|Synonyms: polyphonist, composer | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "monophonist" in a limited sense) | noun
