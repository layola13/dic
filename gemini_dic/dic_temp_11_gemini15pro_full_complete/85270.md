harmonies:/ˈhɑːrməniz/| n.|和声，和谐，协调|a combination of musical notes that sound pleasant when played or sung together; a state of agreement or balance between people or groups; a pleasing arrangement of different things|The harmonies in the song were beautiful. 歌曲中的和声很优美。|近义词：chords, melodies, agreement, unity, balance|反义词：discord, dissonance, conflict, disagreement|noun
