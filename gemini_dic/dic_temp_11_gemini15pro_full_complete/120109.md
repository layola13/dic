mimi:/ˈmiːmiː/
noun
咪咪; 小猫咪 (child's word); 亲爱的; 宝贝
A term of endearment, often used for a cat or a child.  It can also be a nickname.
例句：My little mimi is so cute. 我的小咪咪真可爱。

例句：Come here, mimi, and let me give you a hug.  过来，宝贝，让我抱抱你。
近义词：dear, darling, sweetie, honey, kitty, pussycat
反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on context. Could be enemy, stranger, etc. if referring to a person)
词性: noun


mimi:/ˈmiːmiː/
verb (informal, often childish)
学舌; 模仿 (to mimic)
To imitate someone or something, often in a playful way.
例句：The parrot mimis everything I say. 这只鹦鹉模仿我说的一切。

例句：Stop miming me!  别学我了！
近义词：mimic, imitate, copy, ape
反义词: originate, create
词性: verb (less common usage)
