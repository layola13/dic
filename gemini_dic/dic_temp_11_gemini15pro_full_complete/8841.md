antiexpressively:/ˌæntiɪkˈspresivli/
| adv. | 反表达地，抑制表达地 |  In a way that avoids or suppresses expression. |  She sat antiexpressively, her face a mask of indifference. 她面无表情地坐着，一副漠不关心的样子。|  reservedly, inhibitedly, unemotionally | expressively, openly, demonstratively | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (prefix meaning "against, opposite of") + expressively (adverbial form of expressive, from express + -ive).  The root "express" comes from Latin "expressus," past participle of "exprimere," meaning "to press out, portray, represent."
