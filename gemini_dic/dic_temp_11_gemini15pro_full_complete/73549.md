forespurrer:/fɔːrˈspɜːrər/
n.|先兆，预示者|Something that foreshadows or presages a future event.|The raven was seen as a forespurrer of doom.|近义词：harbinger, precursor, omen, portent|反义词：None|词性: noun

Etymology:
From fore- + spur (v.) "to urge forward," from Old English spurian "to urge on," from Proto-Germanic *spuron.
