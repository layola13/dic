heatstroke:/ˈhiːtstroʊk/| n. |中暑|A condition caused by overheating of the body, typically when exposed to high temperatures or strenuous physical activity for a prolonged period.|The marathon runner suffered from heatstroke after collapsing near the finish line. 马拉松运动员在终点线附近晕倒后中暑了。|近义词：sunstroke, heat exhaustion|反义词:hypothermia|词性:noun
