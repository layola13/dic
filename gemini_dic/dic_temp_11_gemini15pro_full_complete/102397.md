jable: /ˈdʒeɪbəl/
noun
闲聊，饶舌，空谈
trivial or idle talk; chatter.
Example: They spent the afternoon in pleasant jable. 他们一下午都在愉快地闲聊。
Example:  The constant jable from the next table made it difficult to concentrate. 隔壁桌不停的闲聊让我很难集中注意力。
Synonyms: chatter, prattle, gab, gossip
Antonyms: silence, quiet
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
Possibly from Old French *jabler (to chatter), a variant of gabler.  Related to gab.  The word is now considered archaic or dialectal.
