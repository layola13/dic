onprogress:/ɑːnˈprɑːɡrɛs/ | event handler | 进行中 | An event handler in web development that is triggered periodically as the browser loads a document or resource (such as an image).  |  The `onprogress` event allows you to monitor the download progress of a file.  `onprogress` 事件允许你监控文件的下载进度。 | 近义词: onloading, in progress | 反义词: onload, completed |  n.
