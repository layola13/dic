giving:/ˈɡɪvɪŋ/| n. adj.|给予，捐赠，赠送；有弹性的，柔软的|The act of bestowing something.  The quality of being yielding or flexible.|例句：Giving to charity is a rewarding experience.  捐赠给慈善机构是一次有益的经历。|近义词：donating, bestowing, presenting, yielding, flexible|反义词:receiving, taking, rigid, stiff|词性:noun, adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *geving, *yevyng, from Old English ġiefu (“gift, giving”), from Proto-Germanic *gebō (“gift”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰebʰ- (“to give, take”).  Related to give.
