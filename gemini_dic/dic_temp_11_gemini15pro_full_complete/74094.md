fosterers:/ˈfɒstərərz/ (UK), /ˈfɑːstərərz/ (US)| n.|寄养者，抚养者|People who provide temporary care for children or animals.|The fosterers provided a loving home for the abandoned kittens.  这些寄养者为被遗弃的小猫提供了一个充满爱的家。|近义词：carers, guardians, custodians|反义词: biological parents|Noun
