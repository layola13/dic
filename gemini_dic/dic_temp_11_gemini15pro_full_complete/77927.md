genitive:/ˈdʒɛnɪtɪv/
n. adj.
所有格，生格的
relating to or denoting a case of nouns and pronouns (and words agreeing with them) typically serving to express possession, origin, or similar relations.  A grammatical case that typically marks a noun as modifying another noun.
例句：The genitive case in English is often indicated by adding 's to a noun.  英语中的所有格通常通过在名词后加's来表示。

例句：The word "father's" in the phrase "father's house" is in the genitive case.  短语“father's house”中的“father's”一词是所有格。
近义词：possessive
反义词: nominative
词性: noun, adjective
