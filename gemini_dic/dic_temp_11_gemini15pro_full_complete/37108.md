comboloio:/ˌkɒmboˈlɔɪ.oʊ/ (US), /ˌkɒmbəˈlɔɪ.oʊ/ (UK)| n. |火车，列车 (尤指长的或慢的)|A long, often slow-moving train. This word is primarily used in Italian.  It is rarely used in English, and if used, it often carries a slightly humorous or ironic connotation. |例句：The comboloio chugged slowly through the Tuscan countryside.  这列长长的火车缓慢地穿过托斯卡纳乡村。

例句：We were stuck on a comboloio for hours. 我们被困在一列火车上好几个小时。|近义词：train, locomotive|反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific type of train)|词性:noun
