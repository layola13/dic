magisterial:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriəl/ | adj. | 威严的；权威的；专横的；有权威的 | Having or showing great authority; domineering; dictatorial. |例句：He spoke with a magisterial tone. 他说话的语气很威严。|例句：The judge delivered a magisterial summing-up. 法官作了权威性的总结。|近义词：authoritative, commanding, imperious, august, dignified |反义词：humble, meek, subservient |词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *magisterialis*, from Latin magister ("master, teacher").
