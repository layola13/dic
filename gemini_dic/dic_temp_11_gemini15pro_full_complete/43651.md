creatrix:/kriˈeɪtrɪks/
n.
女创造者；女性创始人
A female who creates or brings something into being.
例句：She is the creatrix of this magnificent artwork. 她就是这幅宏伟艺术品的创作者。

例句：The company recognizes her as the creatrix of its most successful product line. 公司认为她是最成功产品线的创始人。

近义词：creator, founder, originator, maker, author
反义词：destroyer

词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *creatrix*, feminine of *creator* ("creator"), from *creare* ("to create").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerh₂- (“to grow”).
