automatons:/ɔːˈtɒmətənz/ (UK) /ɔːˈtɑːmətɑːnz/ (US)| n. |自动装置，机器人|A moving mechanical device made in imitation of a human being.  A machine that performs a function according to a predetermined set of coded instructions, especially one capable of a range of programmed responses to different circumstances.|The factory floor was filled with automatons welding and painting car parts. 工厂车间里到处都是自动焊接和喷漆汽车零件的机器人。|Synonyms: robots, androids, machines, bots|Antonyms: humans, living beings|Noun
