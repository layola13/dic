dubnium: /ˈdʌbniəm/ | n. |  дубний(钅杜) |A synthetic chemical element with the symbol Db and atomic number 105. It is highly radioactive and extremely short-lived.  | Dubnium is a transuranium element. 钅杜是一种超铀元素。|  Dubnium isotopes decay rapidly. 钅杜同位素衰变迅速。 | synonyms: element 105, hahnium (obsolete), unnilpentium (obsolete) | antonyms: N/A | noun
