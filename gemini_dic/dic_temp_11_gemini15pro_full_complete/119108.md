metromania:/ˌmɛtroʊˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 地铁狂热;  对地铁的过度热情 | An excessive enthusiasm for subways or underground transportation systems. |  He has a severe case of metromania and can spend hours discussing different subway systems around the world. 他患有严重的地铁狂热症，可以花几个小时讨论世界各地的不同地铁系统。|  Near synonyms: trainspotting (more general for rail transport),  transit fanaticism | Antonyms:  car dependency, fear of enclosed spaces | noun
