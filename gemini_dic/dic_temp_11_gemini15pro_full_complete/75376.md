frowners:/ˈfraʊnərz/
noun
皱眉的人；不赞成的人
People who frown; those who disapprove.
The frowners in the audience clearly disapproved of the speaker's message.  观众中皱眉的人显然不赞成演讲者的信息。
Near synonyms: disapprovers, critics, objectors, dissenters
Antonyms:  approvers, supporters, smilers
