nondistracted:/ˌnɒndɪˈstræktɪd/
adjective
未分心的，专心的
Free from distractions; focused.
She worked nondistractedly for hours, fully immersed in her writing. 她连续工作了几个小时，完全沉浸在写作中。
近义词: focused, attentive, concentrated, undistracted
反义词: distracted, inattentive, unfocused, preoccupied
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + distracted (from distract, from Latin distractus, past participle of distrahere "to draw apart," from dis- "apart" + trahere "to draw")
