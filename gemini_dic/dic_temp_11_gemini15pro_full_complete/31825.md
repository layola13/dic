cheddaring: /ˈtʃɛdəɹɪŋ/ | n. | 干酪成熟, 切达干酪化 | The process of stacking and turning slabs of cheese curd to expel whey and develop texture, characteristic of cheddar cheese production.  这是切达干酪生产过程中，将凝乳块堆叠和翻转以排出乳清并形成质地的过程。 |  The cheesemaker began the cheddaring process.  奶酪制作者开始了干酪成熟过程。|  maturing, ripening |  - | noun
