counteraccusations:/ˌkaʊntərˌækjʊˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |反控，反指控|An accusation brought by a defendant against a plaintiff or accuser, typically in response to the original accusation.|例句：The trial became bogged down in counteraccusations. 这场审讯陷入了相互指责的泥潭。

例句：He responded to the allegations with a series of counteraccusations. 他用一系列的反指控回应了这些说法。|近义词：countercharges, recriminations|反义词: admissions, confessions |词性:noun
