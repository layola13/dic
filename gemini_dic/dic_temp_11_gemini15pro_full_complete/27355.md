camboose:/kæmˈbuːs/ | n. | 火车尾部厨房; (船上的) 炉灶 | A caboose is a small kitchen or cooking area in the back of a train or on a ship. | The train's caboose served as a small kitchen and living quarters for the crew. 火车的尾部厨房是工作人员的小厨房和生活区。

The ship's camboose was cramped but functional.  船上的炉灶很狭窄，但功能齐全。 | 近义词: galley, kitchen | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *kombuis, from Middle Low German kambuse, kabuse, from Middle Dutch cabuse, probably a borrowing of an earlier form of Occitan camboza, camboza, from Late Latin *cambusia "galley, ship's kitchen," from Greek kamptē "bending, vault," from kampein "to bend."
