misprise:/mɪsˈpraɪz/| n. vt.|轻视，蔑视；误解，误判|To underestimate or undervalue someone or something; to misunderstand or misinterpret something.|He misprised her kindness for weakness. 他把她的善良误认为软弱。|Synonyms: underestimate, undervalue, misjudge, misinterpret, scorn, disdain|Antonyms: appreciate, value, esteem, admire|noun, verb


misprise:/mɪsˈpraɪz/| vt.|犯罪，犯法（指玩忽职守或轻蔑王室权威等）|To commit a crime, especially one involving neglect of official duty or contempt for royal authority.|In the old days, misprision of treason was a serious offense. 在过去，对叛国罪知情不报是严重的罪行。|Synonyms: offend, transgress|Antonyms: obey, comply|verb
