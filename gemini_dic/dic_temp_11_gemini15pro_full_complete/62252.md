encumbered:/ɪnˈkʌmbərd/| adj.|负担的，受妨碍的|Loaded down; burdened.  Weighted down with obligations, duties, or anxieties.|例句：The hikers were encumbered by heavy backpacks.  徒步旅行者被沉重的背包所累。

例句：He was encumbered by debts and responsibilities. 他被债务和责任所累。|近义词：burdened, hampered, weighed down, laden|反义词：unburdened, free, unencumbered|词性:形容词
