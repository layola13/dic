doughface:/ˈdoʊfeɪs/| n. |迎合奴隶主利益的北方人；伪君子|A Northerner who sympathized with the Southern slaveholding interests before the Civil War, especially one who sought to appease Southern slaveholders for political gain.  A derogatory term. |例句：He was labeled a doughface for his refusal to condemn slavery. 他因为拒绝谴责奴隶制而被贴上迎合奴隶主利益的北方人的标签。

例句：Many abolitionists saw the doughfaces as obstacles to progress. 许多废奴主义者认为迎合奴隶主利益的北方人是进步的障碍。|近义词：appeaser, compromiser, timeserver |反义词：abolitionist, radical |词性:名词
