bract:/brækt/| n. |苞片|A modified leaf associated with a flower or inflorescence, typically small and located below the calyx.|The bright red bracts of the poinsettia are often mistaken for petals. 一品红的鲜红色苞片经常被误认为是花瓣。

The bracts of the Bougainvillea are very colorful. 九重葛的苞片非常鲜艳。|synonym:scale, leaf|antonym:petal|noun
