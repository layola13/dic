hypophosphate:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈfɒsfeɪt/ | n. |次磷酸盐|A salt or ester of hypophosphorous acid (H3PO2).  It contains the hypophosphite anion (H2PO2−).|Sodium hypophosphite is used as a reducing agent in electroless nickel plating. 次磷酸钠在化学镀镍中用作还原剂。|近义词:phosphite (though chemically distinct)|反义词:phosphate (though not a true opposite)|noun
