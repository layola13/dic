moonshining:/ˈmuːnˌʃaɪnɪŋ/
n.| 私酒酿造；走私酒；月光照耀|The act of making and distributing illegal liquor.  It can also refer to working a second job secretly, often at night.|He was arrested for moonshining in the backwoods.|近义词：bootlegging, illicit distilling|反义词: legal distilling
词性: 名词

moonshining:/ˈmuːnˌʃaɪnɪŋ/
adj.|从事私酒酿造的，走私酒的|Relating to the production of illegal liquor.|The moonshining operation was discovered by the police.|近义词：bootlegging|反义词: licensed distilling
词性: 形容词
