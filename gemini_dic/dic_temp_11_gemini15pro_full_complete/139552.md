obtundent:/ɒbˈtʌndənt/ /ɑːbˈtʌndənt/ | adj. n.| 钝的，减轻感觉的；钝化剂 | Reducing sensation or bluntness; an agent that dulls or reduces sensation. | The doctor prescribed an obtundent to ease the patient's pain. 医生开了钝化剂来缓解病人的疼痛。 | Synonym:  numbing, soothing, analgesic, anesthetic  adj. dulling, deadening | Antonym: stimulant, irritant, intensifying | adjective

obtundent:/ɒbˈtʌndənt/ /ɑːbˈtʌndənt/ | n.| 钝化剂，减轻感觉的药物 | An agent that dulls or reduces sensation. | The obtundent helped to reduce the inflammation and swelling.  这种钝化剂帮助减少了炎症和肿胀。 | Synonym: analgesic, anesthetic, narcotic | Antonym: stimulant, irritant | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obtundent-, present participle stem of obtundere "to beat against, blunt, dull," from ob- "against" + tundere "to beat".
