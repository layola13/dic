babeldom: /ˈbeɪbəldəm/ | n. |混乱；喧嚣；嘈杂；（语言的）混杂 |A scene of noise and confusion; a place or situation characterized by a multiplicity of languages. |The construction site was a babeldom of shouting workers and clanging machinery.  建筑工地充斥着工人们的叫喊声和机器的叮当声，一片混乱。|  The United Nations is sometimes described as a babeldom of languages. 联合国有时被描述为一个语言混杂的地方。| 近义词: pandemonium, bedlam, cacophony | 反义词: quiet, silence | 词性: noun

babeldom: /ˈbeɪbəldəm/ | n. | 巴别塔; 通天塔 (figuratively, a confusion of sounds or languages) | Referring to the Tower of Babel in the Bible, a structure intended to reach heaven, whose construction was thwarted by God, who caused the builders to speak different languages, thus making communication impossible. | The conference descended into a babeldom of conflicting opinions. 会议陷入了意见冲突的混乱之中。 |  Trying to understand the technical jargon was like navigating a babeldom. 试图理解这些技术术语就像在语言的迷宫中航行。 | 近义词: confusion, disorder, chaos | 反义词: order, harmony, clarity | 词性: noun
