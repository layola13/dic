outpolitick: /ˌaʊtˈpɑːlətɪk/ /ˌaʊtˈpɒlɪtɪk/
verb
以智取胜，玩弄权术，在政治上胜过
to defeat someone by being more clever and skillful in political matters
The young senator outpoliticked his more experienced opponent.  这位年轻的参议员以智取胜，击败了经验更丰富的对手。
The mayor was outpoliticked by his deputy. 市长被他的副手以权术击败了。
近义词: outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
反义词: be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted
