overaccentuate: /ˌoʊvərˈæksɛnˌtʃueɪt/ /ˌəʊvərˈæksɛntʃueɪt/
verb
过分强调；过分突出
To emphasize or stress something excessively, to a degree that is unnatural or disproportionate.
He tends to overaccentuate the negative aspects of every situation. 他倾向于过分强调每种情况的消极方面。
The media overaccentuated the violence at the protest. 媒体过分强调了抗议中的暴力行为。
overemphasize, overstress, exaggerate
downplay, understate, minimize
v.
