incestuously:/ɪnˈsɛstʃuəsli/
| adv.|乱伦地，近亲相奸地|Involving or guilty of incest; in a manner suggesting incest.  Characterized by sexual relations between people too closely related to marry legally. |The family relationships were described, perhaps somewhat incestuously, as intensely close.  这个家庭的关系被描述为极其亲密，也许有点乱伦的意味。|Near synonyms: improperly, immorally, wrongly |Near antonyms: properly, morally, appropriately
