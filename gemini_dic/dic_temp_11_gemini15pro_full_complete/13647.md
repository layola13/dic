attorney:/əˈtɜːrni/ /əˈtɝːni/| n.|律师；代理人|A person legally appointed to act on behalf of another. |例句：My attorney filed the paperwork with the court. 我的律师向法院提交了文件。

|例句：The attorney advised her client to plead guilty.  律师建议他的客户认罪。

|近义词：lawyer, solicitor, barrister, counsel|反义词: defendant, client |noun
