breakfasting:/ˈbrɛkfəstɪŋ/
n.|吃早餐|The act of eating breakfast.|We were breakfasting on the patio when the phone rang. 我们正在露台上吃早餐时，电话响了。|近义词：having breakfast|反义词:fasting|词性:noun (动名词)

Etymology:
From breakfast + -ing.  "Breakfast" itself comes from Old English "fæstenbryce," literally "fast-break," referring to the first meal breaking the overnight fast.
