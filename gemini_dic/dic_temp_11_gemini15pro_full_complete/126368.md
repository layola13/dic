myriologist:/ˌmɪriˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 万物学家，博学者 | A person who studies a vast range of subjects; a polymath. |  He was a true myriologist, knowledgeable in everything from astrophysics to zoology. 他是一位真正的万物学家，从天体物理学到动物学无所不知。


Myriologist John was able to converse intelligently on almost any topic. 万物学家约翰几乎可以就任何话题进行 intelligent 的交谈。


|近义词：polymath, scholar, savant, intellectual, pundit |反义词: specialist, ignoramus|词性:noun
