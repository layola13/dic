lemmy:/ˈlɛmi/| n.|莱米; 伊恩·弗雷泽·基尔米斯特 (Ian Fraser Kilmister)|A British musician, singer, and songwriter, best known as the founder, lead singer, bassist, and primary songwriter of the rock band Motörhead. He was noted for his distinctive gravelly voice, appearance (including prominent facial moles and mutton chops), and stage persona.| Lemmy was a true rock icon. 莱米是一位真正的摇滚偶像。|Synonyms: icon, legend, frontman|Antonyms: follower, nobody|noun

lemmy:/ˈlɛmi/| adj.|莱米式的; 具有莱米风格的|Characteristic or reminiscent of Lemmy Kilmister, typically in terms of music, appearance, or attitude.|His gravelly voice was very lemmy. 他的沙哑嗓音非常有莱米风格。|Synonyms: gravelly, gritty, hard-rocking|Antonyms: smooth, melodic, soft|adjective
