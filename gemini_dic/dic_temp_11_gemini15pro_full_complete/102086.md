isopogonous:/ˌaɪsoʊˈpɒɡənəs/
adjective
等须的，同须的
Having similar or equal beards or barbs (usually referring to plant structures like the pappus of a flower).
The isopogonous seeds are easily dispersed by the wind.  这些等须的种子很容易被风传播。
The botanist identified the plant as a member of the Asteraceae family due to its isopogonous pappus.  这位植物学家根据其等须的冠毛，将该植物鉴定为菊科植物。
近义词:homogonous
反义词:heterogonous
词性:adjective
