affect: /əˈfɛkt/ | v. | 影响；感染；假装；感动 | to have an influence on; make a difference to.  | The weather affects my mood. 天气影响我的心情。 | synonyms: influence, impact, alter, change | antonyms: leave unchanged, ignore | verb

affect: /ˈæfɛkt/ | n. | 情感；感情；态度 |  A set of observable manifestations of a subjectively experienced emotion. | He displayed a wide range of affects during the interview.  他在面试中展现了丰富的情感。| synonyms: emotion, feeling, sentiment | antonyms: apathy, indifference | noun
