kantian:/ˈkæntiən/ | adj. | 康德的；康德哲学的 | Relating to or characteristic of the German philosopher Immanuel Kant or his philosophy. |  His moral philosophy is distinctly Kantian. 他的道德哲学明显是康德式的。 | synonyms: transcendental,  idealist | antonyms: empirical, utilitarian | adjective

kantianism:/ˈkæntiənɪzəm/ | n. | 康德哲学；康德主义 | The philosophy of Immanuel Kant. |  Kantianism emphasizes the importance of reason and moral duty. 康德主义强调理性和道德义务的重要性。| synonyms:  transcendentalism,  critical philosophy | antonyms: empiricism, utilitarianism | noun
