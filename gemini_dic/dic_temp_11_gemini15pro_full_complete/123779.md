mootable:/ˈmuːtəbl/
adjective|可辩论的，可讨论的|Open to discussion or debate; debatable.
|The legality of the action is mootable.  这项行动的合法性是可以辩论的。
|The question of whether to build a new stadium is a mootable point.  是否要建一个新体育场是一个可以讨论的问题。
|debatable, arguable, disputable, contestable|incontestable, indisputable, unquestionable|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From moot + -able.  Moot itself comes from Old English mōt, meaning "meeting, assembly."
