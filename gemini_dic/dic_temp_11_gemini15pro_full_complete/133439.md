nonfashionably:/nɒnˈfæʃənəbli/
adverb
不时尚地，过时地
Not in a fashionable manner; out of style.
She dressed nonfashionably, preferring comfort to style. 她穿着不时尚，更喜欢舒适而不是时尚。
He wore a nonfashionably wide tie. 他戴着一条过时的宽领带。
Synonyms: unfashionably, outmodedly, datedly
Antonyms: fashionably, stylishly, trendily
词性: 副词
