navajo:/ˈnævəˌhoʊ/ /ˈnævəˌhəʊ/ | n. adj. |纳瓦霍人；纳瓦霍语|A member of a Native American people of the southwestern US, especially Arizona and New Mexico, or their language. |例句：The Navajo Nation is the largest Native American reservation in the United States. 纳瓦霍族是美国最大的印第安人保留地。

|例句：She speaks fluent Navajo. 她能说流利的纳瓦霍语。|近义词：Diné (Navajo word for themselves) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun, adjective

Etymology: From Spanish *Navajó*, from Tewa *Navahu'u* 'planted fields'.
