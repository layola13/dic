nailers:/ˈneɪlərz/
n.
钉子, 钉锤, 敲钉子的人, 钉牢物, 非常棒的人或物
People who use nail guns or hammers to drive nails, especially as a trade; also can refer to the tools themselves or something excellent or decisive.
Those nailers worked quickly and efficiently to finish the roof. 那些钉子工快速高效地完成了屋顶的建造。
He hit that golf shot perfectly - what a nailer! 他完美地击中了那个高尔夫球——真是太棒了！
synonyms: hammerers, carpenters, builders, studs, aces
antonyms:  (For people) amateurs, beginners. (For things) failures, duds
noun
