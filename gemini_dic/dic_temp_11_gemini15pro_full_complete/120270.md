miniard:/ˈmɪnjərd/| adj. n.|娇小的，优雅的；娇小玲珑的人|A miniard person or thing is small and delicately formed.  A miniard is a small and delicately formed person.|She was a miniard woman with delicate features. 她是一位五官精致的娇小女子。|The miniard figurines were exquisitely crafted. 这些小巧的雕像制作精美。|近义词：petite, dainty, diminutive, small, delicate|反义词：large, bulky, hulking|词性：形容词, 名词|词源：From Middle French *miniard*, from Old French *migniard*, from *migne*, from Frankish *minn* (“memory, love”) + *-iard*. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *minnō* (“memory, love”).
