idling:/ˈaɪdlɪŋ/ | n. adj.| 空转，闲置，无所事事 |The act of running an engine without engaging gears, or remaining inactive. | The car was idling in the driveway. 汽车在车道上空转。| synonyms: dawdling, loitering, coasting | antonyms: working, active, operating | noun

idling:/ˈaɪdlɪŋ/ | adj.| 空转的，闲置的 | Not active or in use. | An idling engine wastes fuel.  空转的引擎浪费燃料。| synonyms: inactive, unused, dormant | antonyms: active, working, functioning | adjective
