checkerboards:/ˈtʃɛkərbɔːrdz/ | n. |棋盘 |A board divided into squares of alternating colors, typically black and white, used for playing checkers or chess. | The checkerboards were set up for the tournament. 棋盘已经为比赛准备好了。| The children played checkers on the checkerboard. 孩子们在棋盘上玩跳棋。 | Synonyms: board, gameboard | Antonyms: None | Noun


checkerboard:/ˈtʃɛkərbɔːrd/ | adj. |棋盘格的 |Marked with a pattern of alternating squares, typically black and white, resembling a checkerboard. | She wore a checkerboard patterned skirt. 她穿了一条棋盘格图案的裙子。| The floor was laid in a checkerboard pattern. 地板铺成了棋盘格图案。| Synonyms: checkered, tessellated | Antonyms: plain, solid | Adjective


Etymology:  From "checker" (a piece in the game of checkers) + "board."  "Checker" ultimately derives from the Old French "eschequier," meaning "chessboard."
