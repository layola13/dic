atypical: /ˌeɪˈtɪpɪkəl/ | adj. | 非典型的，异常的 | Not representative of a type, group, or class. Deviating from what is usual or expected. |  Her symptoms were atypical, which made diagnosis difficult. 她的症状非典型，这使得诊断变得困难。

The unusually warm weather is atypical for this time of year.  这种异常温暖的天气对于每年的这个时候来说是不典型的。 | 近义词: unusual, abnormal, irregular, deviant, anomalous | 反义词: typical, normal, usual, standard, regular | 词性: 形容词
