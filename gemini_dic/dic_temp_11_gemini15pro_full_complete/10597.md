apprise: /əˈpraɪz/ | vt. | 通知，告知 | To inform or give notice to someone, typically of something important. |  The president was apprised of the latest developments. 总统被告知了最新的进展。

The manager apprised the staff of the new company policy. 经理告知了员工新的公司政策。

| 近义词: inform, notify, advise, tell | 反义词: withhold, conceal | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *aprisier, from a- (from Latin ad 'to') + prisier 'to value, prize', from pris 'price'.  The original meaning was "to appraise," but the current meaning, "to inform," developed in the 17th century.
