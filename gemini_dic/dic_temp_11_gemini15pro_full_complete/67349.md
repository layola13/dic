extendible:/ɪkˈstɛndəbl/ | adj. | 可扩展的，可延长的 | Able to be extended or made larger; capable of being stretched or lengthened. |  例句：This table has extendible legs. 这张桌子有可伸缩的桌腿。| 近义词：expandable, stretchable, prolongable | 反义词：fixed, inextensible | 词性: adjective

extendible:/ɪkˈstɛndəbl/ | adj. | 可延期的，可展期的 | Able to be made longer in time or duration. | 例句：The deadline for the project is extendible.  这个项目的截止日期可以延期。| 近义词：deferrable, postponable | 反义词：fixed, non-extendible | 词性: adjective
