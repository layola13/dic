feminine-rhymes:/ˈfɛmɪnɪn raɪmz/ | adj. | 阴性韵，女性韵 |  Refers to rhymes that use two or more syllables, where the stress falls on the penultimate (second to last) syllable. This is often associated with a more flowing, musical quality, sometimes considered more "feminine" in traditional poetics.  This term is somewhat outdated and can be considered sexist. | Her poem used feminine rhymes, creating a soft, lyrical effect. 她用阴性韵写了首诗，营造出一种柔和抒情的效果。 | 近义词: double rhyme, falling rhyme | 反义词: masculine rhyme | 词性: adjective
