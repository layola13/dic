overfrustrations:/ˌoʊvərfrʌˈstreɪʃənz/

Experiencing frustration to an overwhelming degree.|He suffered from overfrustrations after failing the exam multiple times. 他多次考试不及格后感到极度沮丧。|Synonym: extreme frustration, excessive frustration|Antonym: contentment, satisfaction|Noun
