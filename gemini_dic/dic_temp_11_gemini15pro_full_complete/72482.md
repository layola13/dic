fluocerite:/ˈfluːsəˌraɪt/| n. |氟铈矿|A mineral, cerium fluoride (CeF₃), occurring as brownish-red hexagonal crystals.|Fluocerite is a rare earth mineral found in some alkaline igneous rocks.|近义词:cerium fluoride|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:  From *fluor* (referring to fluorine) + *cerium* (the element) + *-ite* (a common suffix for minerals).
