omnivorousness's:/ɒmˈnɪvərəsnəsɪz/ | n. |杂食性；无所不吃 |The quality or state of being omnivorous.  It refers to the habit of consuming both animal and plant matter as food. |The bear's omnivorousness's allowed it to thrive in diverse environments. 熊的杂食性使它能够在不同的环境中茁壮成长。|Synonym: omnivory |Antonym: herbivorousness, carnivorousness |Noun


omnivorous:/ɒmˈnɪvərəs/ | adj. |杂食的；什么都读的；无所不吃的 | Eating both plants and animals; taking in everything indiscriminately. |Pigs are omnivorous animals. 猪是杂食动物。| Synonym: all-devouring, polyphagous | Antonym: herbivorous, carnivorous |Adjective
