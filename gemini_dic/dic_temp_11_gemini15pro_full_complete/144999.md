overacutely:/ˌoʊvərəˈkjutli/
| adv. | 过于尖锐地，过于敏感地 |  In an excessively sharp or sensitive manner.  Characterized by extreme or exaggerated acuteness. | He reacted overacutely to the criticism, bursting into tears.  他对批评反应过度敏感，突然哭了起来。| The author overacutely portrays the character's anxieties. 作者极其尖锐地刻画了人物的焦虑。 | hypersensitively, excessively, extremely | dully, insensitively | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessively) + "acute" (sharp, sensitive) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Acute" comes from Latin "acutus," past participle of "acuere" (to sharpen), from "acus" (needle).
