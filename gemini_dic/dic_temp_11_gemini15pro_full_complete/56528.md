domiculture: /ˈdɒmɪˌkʌltʃər/ (British), /ˈdɑːmɪˌkʌltʃər/ (American) | n. |  家养，驯养; 居住;  | The act of cultivating plants or breeding animals for human use, often implying a degree of control over their environment and reproduction; the fact of residing in a particular place. |  The domiculture of silkworms has been practiced for centuries.  家蚕的驯养已经实行了几个世纪了。  The professor studied the domiculture of ancient civilizations. 教授研究了古代文明的居住情况。 | 近义词: domestication, cultivation, habitation, residence | 反义词: wildness, feralness | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin "domus" (house) + "cultura" (cultivation).
