gobsmack:/ˈɡɒbˌsmæk/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːbˌsmæk/ (US)| v. n. adj. (informal)| 使…目瞪口呆；震惊；令人震惊的事情| to utterly astonish or overwhelm someone | 例句：The news of the lottery win completely gobsmacked him.  彩票中奖的消息让他完全惊呆了。

例句：I was gobsmacked when I heard she was getting married. 当我听到她要结婚的消息时，我惊呆了。

近义词：astonish, astound, amaze, stun, shock, flabbergast|反义词:  bore, unexcite |词性：verb, noun, adjective
