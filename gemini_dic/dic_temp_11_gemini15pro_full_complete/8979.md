antileprosy:/ˌæntiˈleprəsi/ | adj. | 抗麻风病的；治疗麻风病的 |  Relating to or used in the treatment of leprosy. | The doctor prescribed antileprosy drugs. 医生开了抗麻风病的药物。 |  Antileprosy medication is crucial for controlling the spread of the disease. 抗麻风病药物对于控制疾病的传播至关重要。 | 近义词: antileprous | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "pro-leprosy" in a hypothetical, though unethical, context) | 词性: adjective
