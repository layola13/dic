bookmaking: /ˈbʊkmeɪkɪŋ/
n.
the business or practice of accepting and paying off bets on horse races, sporting events, etc. ; 制定赔率、接受和支付赌注的生意或行为;  the production of books, especially considered as a commercial activity;  书籍的制作，尤指作为一种商业活动
Bookmaking is illegal in some countries. 在一些国家，博彩是非法的。
The bookmaking industry is a multi-billion dollar business. 博彩业是一个数十亿美元的产业。
The rise of online bookmaking has changed the gambling landscape. 在线博彩的兴起改变了赌博的格局。
His family has a long history in bookmaking. 他的家族在博彩业有着悠久的历史。
Modern bookmaking involves complex algorithms and risk assessment. 现代博彩涉及复杂的算法和风险评估。
The printing press revolutionized bookmaking. 印刷机的发明彻底改变了书籍制作。
Bookmaking requires skilled craftspeople.  书籍制作需要熟练的工匠。
The bookmaking course covers everything from design to binding. 这门书籍制作课程涵盖了从设计到装订的所有内容。
synonyms:  betting, wagering, gambling, publishing
antonyms: abstaining
