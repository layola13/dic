at-home:/æt hoʊm/ (US), /æt həʊm/ (UK)| adj.|在家的，家用的；舒适自在的|Suitable for or done at home; comfortable and relaxed. |例句：I prefer at-home workouts. 我更喜欢在家锻炼。|例句：The restaurant has a very at-home atmosphere. 这家餐厅的氛围很温馨舒适。|近义词：domestic, home-based, comfortable, cozy|反义词：away, out, uncomfortable|词性:形容词
词源: "at" (preposition) + "home" (noun).  The preposition "at" indicates location or position, and "home" refers to one's dwelling place.  The combination creates an adjective describing something as being located at home or suitable for use in a home environment.
