cross-fingering:/ˈkrɒsˌfɪŋɡərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌfɪŋɡərɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|交叉指法|The technique of playing a musical instrument, especially a keyboard instrument, by passing one finger over another.|The pianist used cross-fingering to reach the high notes. 钢琴家用交叉指法弹奏高音。|Synonym: overlapping fingers|Antonym: (No direct antonym, could be considered opposite of standard fingering)|noun

cross-fingering:/ˈkrɒsˌfɪŋɡərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌfɪŋɡərɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|交叉指法的|Relating to or involving the technique of passing one finger over another when playing a musical instrument.|This piece requires some complex cross-fingering patterns. 这首曲子需要一些复杂的交叉指法。|Synonym: overlapping|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"cross-" + "fingering". "Cross-" meaning across or over, and "fingering" referring to the use of fingers, especially in playing a musical instrument.
