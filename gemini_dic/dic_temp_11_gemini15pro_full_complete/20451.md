bitterness:/ˈbɪtərnəs/| n. |苦味；辛酸；怨恨|A sharp, unpleasant taste; a feeling of resentment or cynicism.|The coffee had a surprising bitterness. 这咖啡苦味出奇。 She still felt the bitterness of the divorce. 她仍然感受到离婚的痛苦。|近义词：acrimony, resentment, rancor, animosity, hostility|反义词：sweetness, kindness, forgiveness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bitternesse*, from Old English *biterness*, equivalent to *biter* (bitter) + *-ness* (suffix denoting a state or quality).  The word "bitter" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *bitraz*, meaning "to bite."
