cyclometer:/saɪˈklɒmɪtər/ (US) /saɪˈklɑːmɪtər/ (UK)| n. |自行车里程表；车轮转数计|An instrument attached to a bicycle wheel that records the distance travelled.|I checked my cyclometer to see how far we'd cycled. 我查看了我的自行车里程表，看看我们骑了多远。|近义词：odometer, milometer|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:  From cycle + -meter.  "Cycle" ultimately comes from the Greek "kyklos" meaning "circle, wheel". "-meter" comes from the Greek "metron" meaning "measure".
