intertie: /ˈɪntərtaɪ/
vt.|互联，连接|To connect two or more things, especially power systems.|The engineers intertied the two power grids to increase stability. 工程师们将两个电网互联以提高稳定性。|connect, link, join|disconnect, separate|verb
