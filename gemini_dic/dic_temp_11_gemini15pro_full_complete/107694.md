lanner:/ˈlænər/ | n. |  猎隼，矛隼 | A small, slender falcon (Falco biarmicus) found in Africa, southeastern Europe, and Asia. It is known for its speed and agility in flight. |  The lanner is a skilled hunter, capable of taking down birds in mid-air.  猎隼是一种熟练的猎手，能够在半空中捕获鸟类。| 近义词: falcon, peregrine | 反义词: prey | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *lanier, from Late Latin laniarius (“butcher”), from Latin lanius (“butcher bird, shrike”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂énh₂- (“to lacerate”).
