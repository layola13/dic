necrophobia:/ˌnekrəˈfoʊbiə/
n.
恐惧死亡；尸体恐惧症
An abnormal fear of death or dead things.
She suffers from necrophobia and can't even visit a cemetery. 她患有尸体恐惧症，甚至无法参观墓地。
近义词:thanatophobia
反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly necrophilia - an abnormal attraction to corpses)
词性:noun
