lithological:/ˌlɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
岩性的
Relating to the study of rocks, especially their formation, composition, and classification.
The geologist studied the lithological characteristics of the region.  地质学家研究了该地区的岩性特征。

The lithological map showed different types of rock formations. 岩性图显示了不同类型的岩层。

synonym:petrological, petrographic
antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a descriptive scientific term)
