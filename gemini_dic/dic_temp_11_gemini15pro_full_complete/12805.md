assistants:/əˈsɪstənts/| n.|助手，助理，帮手|People who help or support someone else, often in a professional setting. They perform various tasks to aid their superiors or colleagues.|The CEO has two assistants who manage her schedule and correspondence. 首席执行官有两个助理，负责管理她的日程安排和信件往来。|synonyms: aides, helpers, supporters|antonyms: superiors, bosses, managers|noun
