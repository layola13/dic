mounting:/ˈmaʊntɪŋ/| adj. n.|上升的，增长的；安装，装配|increasing, rising; the act of fixing something in place|例句：Mounting evidence suggests he is guilty.  越来越多的证据表明他有罪。|近义词：increasing, rising, escalating, assembling, installing|反义词:decreasing, declining, dismantling, removing|词性:形容词

mounting:/ˈmaʊntɪŋ/| n.|坐骑，底座|a backing, setting, or support for something|例句：The diamond had a beautiful gold mounting. 这颗钻石有一个漂亮的黄金底座。|近义词：support, base, frame, setting|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *monter (to mount, ascend), from Vulgar Latin *montare, frequentative of Latin mons (mountain).
