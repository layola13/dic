claspered:/ˈklæspərd/| adj.|有抱器的，带钩的|Having or provided with claspers (usually refers to the modified pelvic fins of male sharks, rays, and chimaeras, used in mating).|The male shark's claspers aid in reproduction. 雄性鲨鱼的抱器有助于繁殖。|近义词：prehensile, grasping|反义词:smooth, unclasped|词性:形容词

clasper:/ˈklæspər/| n.|抱器，钩状物，扣子|1. A modified pelvic fin of male sharks, rays, and chimaeras, used in mating. 2. A device or organ used for grasping or holding.  3. A fastener, such as a buckle or hook.|例句：The shark's clasper was clearly visible. 鲨鱼的抱器清晰可见。|近义词：grip, holdfast, fastener|反义词:release|词性:名词
