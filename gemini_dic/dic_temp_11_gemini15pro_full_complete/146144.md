overencouraged: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒd/

overencouraged: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒd/
| v. | 过度鼓励 | To encourage someone excessively, leading to inflated confidence or unrealistic expectations. | The coach overencouraged his team, making them think they were invincible. 教练过度鼓励他的团队，让他们觉得自己是无敌的。| overstimulate, overpraise | underencourage, discourage | verb
