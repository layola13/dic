desolatingly:/ˈdesələtɪŋli/
adverb
凄凉地，荒凉地
In a way that causes or suggests desolation; bleakly and depressingly.
The wind howled desolatingly across the empty plains.  风凄凉地呼啸着掠过空旷的平原。
The abandoned house stood desolatingly on the hill.  那栋废弃的房子凄凉地矗立在山上。
近义词: bleakly, drearily, forlornly, miserably
反义词: cheerfully, joyfully, happily
词性: adverb

词源: From desolate + -ing + -ly.  Desolate comes from Latin *desolatus, past participle of desolare “to abandon, forsake,” from de- “completely” + solare “to make lonely,” from solus “alone.”
