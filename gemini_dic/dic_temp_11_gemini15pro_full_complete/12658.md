assemblages:/əˈsɛmblɪdʒɪz/
|n.|组合; 集合; 装配; 集锦;|A collection or gathering of things or people.|例句：The museum boasts a fascinating assemblage of antique toys.  这家博物馆收藏了引人入胜的古董玩具。

例句：An assemblage of onlookers gathered at the scene of the accident. 一群旁观者聚集在事故现场。
|近义词：collection, compilation, aggregate, gathering, assembly|反义词：dispersal, separation|词性：名词

assemblage:/əˈsɛmblɪdʒ/

例句：The conference attracted an assemblage of experts from around the world. 这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的专家。
