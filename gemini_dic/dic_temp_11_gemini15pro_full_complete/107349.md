lamebrains:/ˈleɪmˌbreɪnz/
noun
笨蛋，傻瓜
A foolish or stupid person.
例句：Those lamebrains forgot to bring the map! 那些笨蛋忘了带地图！

例句：Don't listen to those lamebrains; they don't know what they're talking about.  别听那些傻瓜的，他们不知道自己在说什么。

近义词：idiot, moron, imbecile, fool, dunce
反义词：genius, mastermind, intellectual
