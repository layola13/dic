louisianians:/luːˌiːziˈæniənz/
n.
路易斯安那州人
People who live in or are from the state of Louisiana in the United States.
Many Louisianians are known for their Cajun or Creole heritage. 很多路易斯安那人以他们的卡津或克里奥尔文化遗产而闻名。
Louisianians are proud of their unique culture. 路易斯安那人为他们独特的文化感到自豪。
synonyms: Louisiana residents, Louisiana natives
antonyms: non-Louisianians
