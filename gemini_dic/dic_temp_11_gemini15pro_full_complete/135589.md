nonporous:/ˌnɑːnˈpɔːrəs/ | adj. | 无孔的，不透水的 | Not allowing liquid or air to pass through. |  This type of container is nonporous and keeps liquids fresh.  这种容器是无孔的，可以保持液体新鲜。|  The nonporous surface of the material makes it easy to clean.  这种材料无孔的表面使它易于清洁。|  impermeable, impervious, airtight | porous, permeable | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + porous.  "Porous" comes from the Late Latin "porosus," meaning "full of pores," from "porus" (pore) + "-osus" (full of).
