chattery: /ˈtʃætəri/ (US), /ˈtʃætəri/ (UK)| adj. n.|喋喋不休的，饶舌的；闲聊；|Characterized by frequent and informal conversation, often about unimportant matters.  Can also refer to the act of chattering itself. |The room was filled with the chatter of excited children. 房间里充满了孩子们兴奋的喋喋不休。|Synonyms: talkative, loquacious, garrulous, voluble|Antonyms: quiet, silent, taciturn|adjective

chattery: /ˈtʃætəri/ (US), /ˈtʃætəri/ (UK)| n.|喋喋不休，饶舌；|The act of talking rapidly and informally about unimportant matters.|The constant chattery of the monkeys was distracting. 猴子不断的喋喋不休让人分心。|Synonyms: chatter, jabber, prattle|Antonyms: silence, quiet|noun
