incanton:/ɪnˈkæntən/ | vt. | 编入乡镇；安置于营地 | To divide (a region) into cantons or similar administrative divisions; to assign (troops) to quarters in a town. |  The government incantoned the troops in the nearby village after the battle. 政府在战斗结束后将部队安置在附近的村庄。

The area was incantoned into smaller administrative districts.  该地区被划分为更小的行政区。| 近义词: quarter, billet, lodge | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 动词
