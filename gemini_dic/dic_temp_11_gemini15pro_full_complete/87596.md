heterosyllabic:/ˌhɛtərəsɪˈlæbɪk/
adjective
异音节的
Having different syllables; pertaining to or characterized by heterosyllabism.  A heterosyllabic word is one in which the syllables are made up of different combinations of vowels and consonants.
Example: The word "heterosyllabic" itself is heterosyllabic.  这个单词“heterosyllabic”本身就是异音节的。

Example:  "Water" is a heterosyllabic word because the first syllable has a short 'a' sound and the second syllable has a schwa sound.  “Water”是一个异音节词，因为第一个音节是短元音/æ/，第二个音节是弱元音/ə/。
Near Antonyms: monosyllabic, homosyllabic
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but the concept contrasts with homosyllabic)
词性: adjective
