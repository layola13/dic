monkism:/ˈmʌŋkɪzəm/
n.
僧侣主义，修道生活
The practice or system of monastic life.  A devotion to or adherence to the principles of monasticism.
He embraced monkism after a period of deep personal reflection.  他经过一段时间的深刻反思后，皈依了僧侣主义。
monasticism, religious life
secularism, worldliness
