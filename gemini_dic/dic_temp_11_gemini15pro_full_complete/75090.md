frilliest:/ˈfrɪliɪst/
adjective
最饰有褶边的，最花哨的
Superlative form of frilly: most frilly.  Having the most or a lot of frills.  Often used to describe something overly decorated or ornate, sometimes implying excessive or unnecessary embellishment.
She wore the frilliest dress at the party. 她穿着晚会上最花哨的裙子。
The curtains were the frilliest I'd ever seen. 我从未见过如此饰有褶边的窗帘。

近义词:lacy, ornate, fussy, elaborate
反义词:plain, simple, austere, unadorned
词性:adjective
