/ˈkoʊˌhɛdɪŋ/ | n. | 共同标题，联名标题 | A heading shared by two or more articles, chapters, or sections. | Example: The coheading for the two articles was "Climate Change: A Global Crisis."  这两个文章的共同标题是“气候变化：一场全球危机”。|Synonyms: joint heading, shared heading | Antonyms: separate heading| noun

/ˈkoʊˌhɛdɪŋ/ | v. | 共同署名，联名署名 | To provide a shared heading for two or more articles, chapters, or sections. | Example:  They decided to coheading the chapters to show their collaboration. 他们决定共同署名章节以显示他们的合作。|Synonyms: jointly head, share a heading | Antonyms: separately head | verb
