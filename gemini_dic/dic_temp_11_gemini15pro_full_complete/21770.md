boatyard:/ˈboʊtjɑːrd/| n.|船厂，造船厂|An area with docks and equipment for building and repairing boats.  A place where boats are built, repaired, or stored.|The boatyard was busy with activity as workers prepared the yachts for the summer season.  船厂里一片繁忙，工人们正为夏季的游艇做好准备。|Synonyms: shipyard, marina, dockyard|Antonyms: None|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From boat + yard.  "Yard" in this context originally referred to an enclosed space.
