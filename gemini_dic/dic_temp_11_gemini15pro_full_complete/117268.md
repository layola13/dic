mechanism:/ˈmɛkənɪzəm/ | n. | 机制，原理，机械装置 |A system of parts working together in a machine; a piece of machinery; a way in which something works. | The mechanism of a clock is intricate.  时钟的机制很复杂。

The mechanism by which the disease spreads is still unknown. 这种疾病传播的机制仍然未知。| 近义词: process, system, apparatus, device | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mechanisma, from Greek mēkhanē (“machine, contrivance, device”), related to mēkhos (“means, expedient”).
