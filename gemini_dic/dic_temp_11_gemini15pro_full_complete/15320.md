backshish:/ˈbækʃiːʃ/| n.|小费，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially one given in a country where it is expected, or a bribe.|Example 1:  He offered the waiter a small backshish to ensure a good table. 他给了服务员一点小费，以确保能得到一个好桌子。

Example 2: The official hinted that a backshish would expedite the process.  这位官员暗示，一点贿赂可以加快办理进程。|Synonym: tip, gratuity, bribe, kickback|Antonym:  penalty, fine|Noun
