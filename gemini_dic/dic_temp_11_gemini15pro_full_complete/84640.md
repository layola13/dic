hand-over:/ˈhændˌoʊvər/| n.|移交，交接|The hand-over of power to the new government was peaceful. 政权向新政府的移交是和平的。|例句：The hand-over of the project to the new team went smoothly.  这个项目向新团队的交接进行得很顺利。

例句：There will be a formal hand-over ceremony for the new building next week. 下周将举行新大楼的正式移交仪式。

近义词：transfer, handover (one word), transition|反义词: takeover, retention|词性:名词

hand-overs:/ˈhændˌoʊvərz/| n.|移交，交接（复数）|The company has experienced several hand-overs of leadership in recent years.  近年来，该公司经历了多次领导层的交接。|例句：Multiple hand-overs of responsibility can sometimes lead to confusion. 多次责任交接有时会导致混乱。


例句：Smooth hand-overs are essential for maintaining productivity.  顺利的交接对于保持生产力至关重要。


近义词：transfers, transitions|反义词: takeovers|词性:名词


词源:  "Hand-over" comes from the simple combination of the words "hand" and "over".  "Hand" refers to the physical act of giving something to someone, while "over" indicates the transfer of possession or control.  It literally describes the act of handing something over to another person.
