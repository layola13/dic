dingoes:/ˈdɪŋɡoʊz/ (US), /ˈdɪŋɡəʊz/ (UK)| n. |野狗，澳洲野狗|A wild dog native to Australia, characterized by a sandy-yellow coat and a bushy tail. It is believed to be a descendant of domesticated dogs brought to Australia by Aboriginal people.|Dingoes hunt kangaroos and other animals. 野狗捕猎袋鼠和其他动物。

|近义词：wild dog, feral dog|反义词:domesticated dog|词性:noun
