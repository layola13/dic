chare:/tʃɛər/ | n. v. | 家务杂活；差事；（尤指）日常琐事；分担工作；负责；<古>转动； | A routine task, especially a household chore; an odd job. To assign a task or responsibility to someone.  |  She hates doing household chares. 她讨厌做家务。 He was chared with the task of organizing the meeting. 他被委派组织这次会议的任务。 | 近义词：task, job, duty, chore, errand | 反义词：leisure, recreation | 词性：名词；动词

Etymology: From Middle English *charien*, from Old North French *charer*, *charier* (“to cart, carry”), from Late Latin *carrāre* (“to cart”), from Latin *carrus* (“cart, wagon”)
