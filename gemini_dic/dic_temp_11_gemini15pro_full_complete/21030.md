blickey:/ˈblɪki/| interj.|哎呀；我的天；哇！ (表示惊讶、害怕、兴奋等)|An exclamation of surprise, fear, or excitement, especially in Australian and New Zealand English.  It's similar to "crikey" or "gosh".| Blickey! That was a close call! 哎呀！好险！|近义词：crikey, gosh, gee whiz, good heavens|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's an exclamation)|interjection
词源: Possibly a euphemism for "bloody," or perhaps related to "blink."  The origin is uncertain.
