nonsensible:/nɒnˈsɛnsɪb(ə)l/
adjective
无意义的，荒谬的
Not making sense; illogical or foolish.
That's a nonsensical argument. 那是无稽之谈。
近义词: absurd, foolish, illogical, irrational, silly
反义词: sensible, rational, logical, reasonable
词性: adjective
词源:  From non- + sensible, from Late Latin sensibilis "perceptible," from Latin sentire "to feel."  Meaning "absurd, foolish" is from the early 17th century.
