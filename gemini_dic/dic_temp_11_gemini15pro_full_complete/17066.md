bathymetric:/ˌbæθɪˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|水深测量的，测深的|Relating to the measurement of the depth of water in oceans, seas, or lakes.|例句：Bathymetric surveys are essential for safe navigation. 水深测量对于安全航行至关重要。

例句：The bathymetric map showed a steep drop-off just beyond the reef. 水深图显示礁石外有一个陡峭的落差。|近义词：depth-sounding,  hydrographic|反义词:topographic|词性:形容词

bathymetry:/bəˈθɪmətri/| n.|水深测量；测深学|The measurement of the depth of water in oceans, seas, or lakes.|例句：Bathymetry is crucial for understanding the ocean floor. 水深测量对于了解海底至关重要。

例句：Modern bathymetry uses sonar technology to create detailed maps. 现代水深测量使用声纳技术创建详细的地图。|近义词：depth sounding, hydrography|反义词:topography|词性:名词
