antiglobalization:/ˌæntiˌɡloʊbələˈzeɪʃən/
adj.|反全球化的|Opposed to the increase of global trade and economic interdependence among countries.|He holds strong antiglobalization views. 他持有强烈的反全球化观点。|近义词: anti-globalist|反义词: pro-globalization|形容词
