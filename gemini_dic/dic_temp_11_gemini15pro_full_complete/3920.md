agriculturism:/ˈæɡrɪkʌltʃərɪzəm/
n. |农业主义;重农主义|The belief in the importance of agriculture and farming.  A philosophy or policy that emphasizes the importance of agriculture. |The government's policies reflected a strong agriculturism, prioritizing rural development over industrial growth. 政府的政策反映了强烈的农业主义，优先发展农村而不是工业增长。|近义词: agrarianism, physiocracy |反义词: industrialism |词性: noun
