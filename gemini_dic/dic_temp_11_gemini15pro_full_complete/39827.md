conspirative:/kənˈspɪrətɪv/
adjective
阴谋的，密谋的
showing that you share a secret
例句：They exchanged conspirative glances. 他们交换了心照不宣的眼神。

例句：She had a conspirative smile on her face. 她脸上带着一丝神秘的微笑。

近义词：secret, clandestine, furtive, covert, collusive
反义词：open, forthright, aboveboard
词性: adjective


conspire:/kənˈspaɪər/
verb
密谋，共谋
to plan secretly with other people to do something bad or illegal
例句：They conspired to overthrow the government. 他们密谋推翻政府。

例句：The evidence suggests that they conspired together to commit the crime. 证据表明他们合谋犯下了这起罪行。

近义词：plot, scheme, intrigue, collude
反义词：cooperate, collaborate
词性: verb
