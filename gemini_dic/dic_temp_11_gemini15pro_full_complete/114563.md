malax:/məˈlæks/ | v. | 揉捏；软化；按摩 | To soften or knead (something, especially clay or dough) by working or mixing it with the hands or a machine.  | The potter malaxed the clay before shaping it into a pot. 陶工在将黏土塑造成锅之前先揉捏它。

The baker malaxed the dough for several minutes to develop the gluten. 面包师揉捏面团几分钟以形成面筋。 | 近义词: knead, work, massage, manipulate | 反义词: harden, solidify | 词性: 动词
