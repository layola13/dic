involucre:/ˈɪnvəlʊkər/| n. |总苞；苞片|A whorl or collection of bracts surrounding a flower cluster or fruit.|The sunflower's involucre is made up of numerous green bracts. 向日葵的总苞由许多绿色苞片组成。|
The involucre protects the developing flower bud. 总苞保护正在发育的花蕾。|近义词：bract, sheath|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
