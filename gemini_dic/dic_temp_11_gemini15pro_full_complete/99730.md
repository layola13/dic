intermanorial:/ˌɪntər.məˈnɔːri.əl/
adjective
庄园间的；不同庄园之间的
Existing or occurring between manors.  Relating to affairs conducted between different manors.
The intermanorial disputes over grazing rights were common in medieval England.  中世纪英格兰，不同庄园之间关于放牧权的纠纷很常见。
inter-manor, cross-manorial
intramanorial
adjective
