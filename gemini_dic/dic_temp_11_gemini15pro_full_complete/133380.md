nonextrinsically:/ˌnɒnɛkˈstrɪnsɪkli/
adverb
非外在地；内在地
Acting or operating internally; not influenced by external factors.  Driven by internal motivation or inherent qualities, rather than by external rewards or pressures.
Her motivation to learn was nonextrinsically driven, stemming from a genuine curiosity. 她学习的动力是非外在的，源于真正的好奇心。
He chose to help nonextrinsically, simply because it was the right thing to do. 他选择帮助别人并非出于外部原因，只是因为他觉得这样做是对的。
Synonyms: inherently, intrinsically, naturally, innately
Antonyms: extrinsically, externally
词性: 副词
