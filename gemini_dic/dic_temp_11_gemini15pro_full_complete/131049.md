nonaxiomatical:/ˌnɒnˌæksiəˈmætɪkəl/
adjective
非公理的
Not based on or involving axioms; not axiomatic.  A statement or system that is not self-evident or accepted as a fundamental truth without proof.
This theory rests on a few nonaxiomatical assumptions.  这个理论基于一些非公理的假设。
The principle of least action is nonaxiomatical in classical mechanics.  最小作用量原理在经典力学中是非公理的。
Synonyms: non-self-evident, unproven, assumed
Antonyms: axiomatic, self-evident, proven
词性: adjective
