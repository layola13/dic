harmonite:/ˈhɑːrmənɪt/ (US), /ˈhɑːmənɪt/ (UK)| n.|谐波；泛音|A component frequency of an oscillation or wave.  A wave can be broken down into a fundamental frequency and its harmonics, which are multiples of the fundamental frequency.|The instrument produced a rich sound with many harmonics.|近义词：overtone, partial|反义词: fundamental|名词

harmonicity:/hɑːrˈmɒnɪsɪti/ (US), /hɑːˈmɒnɪsɪti/ (UK)| n.|谐波性；和谐性|The state or quality of being harmonic; harmoniousness.|The harmonicity of the chords created a peaceful atmosphere.|近义词：harmony, consonance|反义词: disharmony, dissonance|名词
