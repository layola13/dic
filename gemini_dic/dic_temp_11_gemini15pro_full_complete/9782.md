antrovert: /ˈæntrəvɜːrt/
n.|性格内向的人|A shy, reticent person.|She's a bit of an antrovert and prefers small gatherings to large parties. 她有点内向，喜欢小型聚会而不是大型派对。|近义词: introvert, shy person, reserved person|反义词: extrovert, outgoing person|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin *introvertere "to turn inward," from intro- "inward" + vertere "to turn."  The term was coined in the early 20th century by Carl Jung.
