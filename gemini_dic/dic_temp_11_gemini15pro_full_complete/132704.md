nondistinguished:/ˌnɒndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt/
adjective
无特色的；普通的；不杰出的
lacking distinction; ordinary; not prominent or eminent
The book received nondistinguished reviews. 这本书得到的评价很普通。
Synonyms: undistinguished, ordinary, commonplace, average, unexceptional, run-of-the-mill
Antonyms: distinguished, prominent, eminent, notable, remarkable, outstanding
词性: 形容词
