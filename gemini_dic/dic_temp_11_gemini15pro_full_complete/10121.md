apolitically:/ˌeɪpəˈlɪtɪkli/
ad.|非政治地，不关心政治地|without interest or involvement in politics; in a way that is not political.|He apolitically donated to both parties.|Synonyms: neutrally, impartially|Antonyms: politically, partisanly|adverb

apolitically:/ˌeɪpəˈlɪtɪkli/
adv.|非政治地，不关心政治地|in a way that avoids political considerations or involvement.|She made an apolitical donation to the charity.|Synonyms: non-politically, neutrally|Antonyms: politically, partisanly|adverb
