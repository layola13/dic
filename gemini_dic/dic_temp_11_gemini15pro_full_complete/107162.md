lag-behind:/ˌlæɡ bɪˈhaɪnd/| phrasal verb |落后，滞后|to fail to keep up with someone or something|Example: Some students lag behind in their studies. 一些学生学习落后。|Example: The company's profits are lagging behind expectations.  该公司的利润落后于预期。|Synonyms: fall behind, trail, straggle|Antonyms: lead, excel, surpass|verb phrase

lag-behind:/ˌlæɡ bɪˈhaɪnd/| n. |落后，滞后（名词形式较少使用）|the act of lagging behind; the state of being behind|Example: The lag-behind in technology caused the company to fail. 技术上的落后导致了这家公司的失败。|Example:  There's a significant lag-behind in infrastructure development in rural areas. 农村地区的基础设施建设存在显著滞后。|Synonyms: shortfall, deficit, gap|Antonyms:  advance, progress, lead|noun
