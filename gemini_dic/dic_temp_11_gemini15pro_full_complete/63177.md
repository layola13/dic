entailment:/ɪnˈteɪlmənt/| n.|蕴涵，包含|A logical relationship between two sentences, where the truth of one (the premise) requires the truth of the other (the conclusion).|Building a new airport entails diverting the river.  修建新机场需要改道河流。|synonyms: implication, consequence, necessity|antonyms: irrelevance, unrelatedness|noun

entailment:/ɪnˈteɪlmənt/| n.|牵连，承担|The act of entailing or the state of being entailed.|His inheritance of the estate entailed certain responsibilities.  他继承了这笔遗产，也就承担了某些责任。|synonyms: involvement, commitment, obligation|antonyms: freedom, release, exemption|noun
