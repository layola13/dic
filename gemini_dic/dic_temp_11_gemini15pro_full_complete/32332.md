child-proofs:/ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːfs/
|v. (child-proof, child-proofed, child-proofing)|使防儿童开启; 使儿童安全|To make something safe for children, so that they cannot open it, start it, or be hurt by it.|She child-proofed the house before her toddler started walking. 她在她的小孩开始学走路之前就对房子做了安全防护。|The cupboard has been child-proofed to protect the cleaning supplies. 为了保护清洁用品，橱柜已安装了儿童安全锁。|近义词:secure, safeguard, protect|反义词: endanger, expose|词性:动词

child-proof:/ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːf/
|adj.|防儿童开启的; 儿童安全的|Designed to prevent children from opening, starting, or being hurt by something.|The medicine bottle had a child-proof cap. 药瓶有一个防儿童开启的瓶盖。|These cabinets are child-proof, so you don't have to worry about your little one getting into them. 这些柜子是防儿童开启的，所以你不必担心你的小孩会打开它们。|近义词:safe, secure|反义词:unsafe, dangerous|词性:形容词
