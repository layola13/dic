clients:/ˈklaɪənts/| n.|客户，委托人|A client is a person or organization that uses the services of a professional person or company.| Our law firm represents many high-profile clients. 我们的律师事务所代理许多知名客户。| customers, patrons| opponents, adversaries | noun

Etymology:  From Old French *client, from Latin clientem, accusative of cliēns (“dependent, follower”), present participle of cluēre (“to obey, listen to, heed”). The original sense was of a Roman citizen who placed himself under the legal and social protection of a more powerful patronus.
