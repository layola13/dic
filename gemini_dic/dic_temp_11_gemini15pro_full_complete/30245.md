cellulitis:/ˌsɛljuˈlaɪtɪs/
n.
蜂窝织炎
Inflammation of the connective tissue cells, especially in the skin, caused by bacteria.
The patient presented with pain, redness, and swelling consistent with cellulitis. 病人出现疼痛、发红和肿胀，与蜂窝织炎的症状一致。
近义词:inflammation, erysipelas
反义词:(None -  Cellulitis is a specific medical condition; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cellulitis, from Latin cellula ("small room, cell") + -itis (suffix indicating inflammation).
