lopas:/ˈloʊpɑːs/ | n. |  (废弃词) 歌曲，民谣；故事，传说 | A song, ballad, or tale.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. |  He sang a lopas of ancient heroes. 他唱了一首古代英雄的歌谣。 | 近义词: song, ballad, tale, story, narrative | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists for a genre of storytelling.) | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *lopas, from Old French *lopaz (unattested), probably from a Germanic source.  Related to Dutch *lopen (to run). The connection may be through the idea of a narrative that "runs" or develops.
