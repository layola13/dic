orthognathous: /ˌɔrθəɡˈnæθəs/ | adj. | 直颌的；正颌的 | Having a straight jaw; not prognathous.  The face is flat or only slightly projecting in profile. |  Example: Modern humans are generally orthognathous, unlike their Neanderthal ancestors. 现代人类通常是直颌的，不像他们的尼安德特人祖先。

Example: The skull found in the cave was determined to be orthognathous.  在山洞里发现的头骨被确定为直颌的。 | 近义词: straight-jawed | 反义词: prognathous, retrognathous | 词性: adjective | 词源: From Greek *orthos* (straight) + *gnathos* (jaw).
