chidingly:/ˈtʃaɪdɪŋli/
adv.
责备地，斥责地
in a way that expresses disapproval or blames someone gently
"You're late again," she said chidingly. “你又迟到了，”她责备地说。
近义词:reproachfully, rebukingly, scoldingly
反义词:approvingly, praisingly, encouragingly
词性:副词
