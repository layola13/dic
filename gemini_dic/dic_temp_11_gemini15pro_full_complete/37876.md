complacency:/kəmˈpleɪsənsi/ | n. | 自满，沾沾自喜 | A feeling of smug satisfaction with one's current state or situation, often unaware of potential dangers or deficiencies. |  His complacency about his job security was shattered when the company announced layoffs.  他对于工作安全的自满情绪在公司宣布裁员时破灭了。| synonyms: smugness, self-satisfaction, satisfaction | antonyms: anxiety, insecurity, humility | noun

complacent:/kəmˈpleɪsənt/ | adj. | 自满的，沾沾自喜的 | Showing smug or uncritical satisfaction with oneself or one's achievements. | He was complacent about his exam results, not realizing how close he had come to failing. 他对自己的考试成绩感到自满，没有意识到自己离不及格有多近。| synonyms: smug, self-satisfied, uncritical | antonyms: anxious, insecure, humble | adjective
