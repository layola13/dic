cleavages:/ˈkliːvɪdʒɪz/
n.
分裂，裂缝；乳沟；分歧
a sharp division; a split.  In anatomy, a cleavage is a division or separation of parts, as of the skin. It can also refer to the separation of two parts of a developing embryo.  More commonly, it refers to the separation between a woman's breasts, especially as revealed by a low-cut garment.
The geologists studied the rock cleavages in the cliff face.  地质学家研究了悬崖面的岩石裂缝。

The deep political cleavages within the country led to civil war.  该国国内深刻的政治分歧导致了内战。

Her dress emphasized her cleavage. 她的连衣裙突出了她的乳沟。
近义词:division, split, rift, chasm, separation, gap, gulf
反义词:unity, agreement, joining, fusion
词性:名词(plural)
