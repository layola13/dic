caesura:/sɪˈzjʊərə/ /siːˈzʊərə/ | n. | 休止；停顿；诗行中间的停顿 | A break or pause in a line of verse, typically near the middle, or any interruption or break. | The poem flowed smoothly, with only a slight caesura in the middle of each stanza. 这首诗流畅地流动，每节诗的中间只有一个轻微的停顿。

The speaker paused, creating a dramatic caesura before announcing the winner. 演讲者停顿了一下，在宣布获胜者之前制造了一个戏剧性的停顿。| 近义词：pause, break, interruption, rest | 反义词：continuation, flow | 词性：名词
