hyperabsorption:/ˌhaɪpərəbˈzɔːrpʃn/ | n. |过度吸收|The process of absorbing something to an excessive degree.  The sponge exhibited hyperabsorption, quickly soaking up all the spilled water.|例句：The hyperabsorption of nutrients can sometimes lead to toxicity. 过度吸收营养有时会导致中毒。

例句：The scientist studied the phenomenon of hyperabsorption in certain types of soil. 科学家研究了某些类型土壤中的过度吸收现象。|近义词：overabsorption, excessive absorption|反义词: hypoabsorption, poor absorption|词性: noun
