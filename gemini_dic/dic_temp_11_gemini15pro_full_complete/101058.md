invented:/ɪnˈvɛntɪd/| v. adj.|发明（的），创造（的）|Past participle of "invent," meaning to create or design something that has not existed before.| Alexander Graham Bell invented the telephone.  亚历山大·格雷厄姆·贝尔发明了电话。|  The story he told was completely invented. 他讲的故事完全是虚构的。| synonyms: created, devised, originated, fabricated (when referring to a lie) | antonyms: discovered, found, unearthed|verb, adjective
