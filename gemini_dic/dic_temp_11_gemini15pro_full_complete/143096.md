outbribe: /ˌaʊtˈbraɪb/
verb
贿赂胜过；用更大的贿赂胜过
To bribe someone more than another person has bribed them.
He outbribed his rivals to secure the contract. 他用更大的贿赂胜过对手，从而获得了合同。
近义词: outbid, overbid
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "underbid" in specific contexts)
词性: verb
