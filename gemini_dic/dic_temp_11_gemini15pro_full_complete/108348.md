lavendered:/ˈlævəndərd/
adjective
薰衣草色的，有薰衣草香味的
Perfumed with or having the scent of lavender; having the pale purplish color of lavender flowers.
The lavendered sheets smelled fresh and clean.  薰衣草色的床单闻起来清新干净。
The air was lavendered with the scent of the nearby fields.  空气中弥漫着附近田野的薰衣草香味。
近义词:lavender-scented, lilac
反义词:unscented, odorless
词性:adjective

词源: From lavender + -ed.
