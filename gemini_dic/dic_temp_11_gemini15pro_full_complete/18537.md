beneficently:/ˌbɛnɪˈfɪsəntli/
adverb
仁慈地；慈善地；好心地
in a way that is kind, generous, and helpful
The wealthy businessman beneficently donated a large sum of money to the local hospital.  这位富商慷慨地向当地医院捐赠了一大笔钱。
She smiled beneficently at the children. 她慈祥地对孩子们笑了笑。
近义词: kindly, generously, charitably, humanely
反义词: cruelly, harshly, unkindly
词性: adverb

词源:
From Latin *beneficentia* "well-doing, kindness,"  from *bene facere*  "to do good," from *bene* "well" + *facere* "to do."
