abstractional:/æbˈstrækʃənəl/ | adj. | 抽象的，抽象化的 | Relating to abstraction; considered apart from concrete realities, specific objects, or actual instances.  Characterized by the process of abstracting. |  His work is highly abstract and deals with complex, abstraction concepts.| Synonyms: abstract, theoretical, conceptual, notional, ideal, philosophical | Antonyms: concrete, tangible, real, physical | adjective

abstraction:/æbˈstrækʃən/ | n. | 抽象，抽象化，抽象概念 |  The act or process of abstracting; the state of being abstracted. Something that exists only as an idea.  | The artist's abstraction of the human form was fascinating.  | Synonyms: concept, idea, notion, generalization, theory | Antonyms: concretion, reality, specification, particularity | noun
