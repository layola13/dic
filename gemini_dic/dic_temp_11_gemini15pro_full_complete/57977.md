drive-by:/ˈdraɪv baɪ/ | n. |飞车射击；路过式服务；得来速|A shooting carried out from a moving vehicle; a business that allows customers to purchase products without leaving their cars; something done quickly or casually, often without much thought or effort.|例句：The police are investigating a series of drive-by shootings. 警方正在调查一系列飞车枪击事件。|例句：We grabbed some burgers from the drive-by on our way home. 我们在回家的路上从得来速买了些汉堡。|近义词：drive-through, curbside, takeout|反义词: dine-in | noun

drive-by:/ˈdraɪv baɪ/ | adj. |路过式的；飞车…的| Done or carried out from a moving vehicle; performed quickly and casually.|例句：The restaurant offers drive-by service. 这家餐厅提供路过式服务。|例句：The witness reported seeing a drive-by shooting. 目击者报告说看到了一起飞车枪击事件。|近义词：drive-through, curbside |反义词: sit-down | adjective
