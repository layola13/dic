crammer:/ˈkræmər/| n.| 填鸭式学习的人；应试突击者 |A person who studies intensively over a short period of time, especially for an examination.|He's a real crammer, spending the week before the exam locked in his room. 他是一个真正的填鸭式学习者，考试前一周都把自己锁在房间里学习。| synonyms: swot, grind | antonyms: slacker | noun
cram:/kræm/| v.| 塞满；填鸭式地学习 |To force a lot of things into a small space; to study intensively over a short period of time.|I crammed all my clothes into the suitcase. 我把所有的衣服都塞进了手提箱。|She's been cramming for her exams all week. 她整个星期都在为考试突击学习。| synonyms: stuff, pack, cram | antonyms:  | verb
