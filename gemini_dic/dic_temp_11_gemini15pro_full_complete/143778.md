outmount:/ˌaʊtˈmaʊnt/ | v. | 超越，胜过 | To surpass or exceed, especially in a significant or impressive way.  It often implies a superiority in numbers, power, or achievement.  It is less commonly used than synonyms like "surpass" or "exceed". |  The challenger outmounted the champion in the final round. 挑战者在最后一轮胜过了冠军。

The new technology outmounted all previous attempts at efficiency. 新技术超越了以往所有提高效率的尝试。| 近义词: surpass, exceed, eclipse, outstrip, transcend | 反义词:  be inferior to, be surpassed by, fall short of | 词性: 动词
