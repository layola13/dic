dentolingual:/ˌdɛntoʊˈlɪŋɡwəl/
adjective
舌侧的；牙舌侧的
Pertaining to or located near the surface of a tooth that is adjacent to the tongue.
The dentist recommended a dentolingual appliance for her overbite.  牙医建议她使用牙舌侧矫治器来矫正她的覆咬合。
The dentolingual surface of the incisors was examined for wear.  检查了门牙的牙舌侧表面是否有磨损。
Near Antonyms: dentofacial, dentolabial
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider) buccal, labial
