christianness:/ˈkrɪstʃənɪsnəs/
n.
基督教精神; 基督徒品质
The quality or state of being Christian; Christian character or spirit.
He showed his christianness by helping the poor. 他通过帮助穷人展现了他的基督教精神。
近义词: Christianity, piety, devoutness
反义词: ungodliness, impiety, wickedness
词性: 名词

词源:  From Christian + -ness.  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns meaning 'the state or quality of being...'
