lemniscate: /ˈlɛmnɪskeɪt/ | n. | 双纽线; 横向8字形; |A lemniscate is a plane curve defined by a Cartesian equation of the form (x^2 + y^2)^2 = a^2(x^2 - y^2)  or in polar coordinates by r^2 = a^2cos(2θ). It resembles a figure eight or the symbol for infinity. | The orbit of a binary star system can sometimes resemble a lemniscate. 双星系统的轨道有时类似于双纽线。

The infinity symbol is a form of a lemniscate. 无穷大符号是双纽线的一种形式。 | 近义词: figure eight, infinity symbol | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
