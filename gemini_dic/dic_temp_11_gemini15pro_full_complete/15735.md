blackshirts:/ˈblækʃɜːrts/| n.| 黑衫党（通常指意大利法西斯党员）| Members of a fascist organization, particularly associated with Mussolini's Italy, who wore black shirts as a uniform. |  Mussolini's blackshirts marched on Rome.  墨索里尼的黑衫党向罗马进军。 | synonyms: fascists, Nazis| antonyms: anti-fascists, democrats| noun

Etymology: Compound word from "black" + "shirts," referring to the black shirts worn as a uniform.  词源：由“black”和“shirts”组成的复合词，指的是作为制服穿着的黑衬衫。
