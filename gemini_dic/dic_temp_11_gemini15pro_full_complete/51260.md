desubsidized:/ˌdiːˈsʌbsɪdaɪzd/
adjective
去补贴的；取消补贴的
Having had its subsidy removed.  Removed from a previous state of being subsidized.
The desubsidized housing market is facing challenges.  去补贴的住房市场正面临挑战。
Farmers are struggling after the government desubsidized the agriculture industry.  政府取消对农业的补贴后，农民的日子很不好过。
Synonyms: unsubsidized, unsupported
Antonyms: subsidized, supported
词性: adjective
