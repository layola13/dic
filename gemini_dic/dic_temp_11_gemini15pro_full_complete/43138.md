coz:/kəz/| informal contraction|因为|Short for "because".  It's used in informal writing and speech.| Coz I was tired, I went to bed early. 因为我累了，所以早早睡觉了。|近义词：because, as, since, for|反义词: (No direct antonym for a conjunction)|conjunction

I go to school coz it's important. = 我去上学因为这很重要。
She eats vegetables coz they're healthy. = 她吃蔬菜因为它们健康。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):

虚拟语气:
I would go to the party coz I want to see my friends. = 如果可以的话，我会去派对因为我想见我的朋友们。
If I had more time, I would travel coz I love exploring. = 如果我有更多时间，我会去旅行因为我喜欢探索。


被动语态:
The meeting was cancelled coz there weren't enough people. = 会议被取消了因为没有足够的人。
The cake was eaten coz it was delicious. = 蛋糕被吃掉了因为它很美味。


疑问句:
Why are you leaving so early, coz you have something else to do? = 你为什么这么早走，因为你还有其他事要做吗？
Are you going to the gym coz you want to get fit? = 你要去健身房因为你想变得健康吗？

否定句:
I'm not going to the party, not coz I don't want to, but coz I'm busy. = 我不会去派对，不是因为我不想去，而是因为我很忙。
She didn't call me back, not coz she's angry, but coz she's busy. = 她没有回我电话，不是因为她生气，而是因为她很忙。


PHRASE->
Just coz = 只是因为
Coz of = 因为
Not coz = 不是因为
PHRASE->
Coz why not? = 为什么不呢？ (Informal, often rhetorical)
Coz I said so. = 因为我说了算。(Informal, often used by parents)

注意事项:  As noted, "coz" is very informal.  Avoid using it in formal writing or professional settings.
