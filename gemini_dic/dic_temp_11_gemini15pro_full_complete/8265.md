anti-Irish:/ˌæntiˈaɪrɪʃ/
adjective
反爱尔兰的，仇视爱尔兰人的
Demonstrating prejudice or hostility towards Irish people or Irish culture.
Example:  Anti-Irish sentiment was prevalent in some parts of Britain during the 19th century.  19世纪，反爱尔兰情绪在英国某些地区盛行。

Example:  The graffiti included anti-Irish slogans.  涂鸦中包含反爱尔兰的标语。
Synonym:  Hibernophobic
Antonym:  Pro-Irish
词性: 形容词
