counterclaim:/ˈkaʊntərkleɪm/ /ˈkaʊntərklɛɪm/| n. v.|反诉，反驳，反要求|An assertion made to rebut a previous claim.|The defendant filed a counterclaim against the plaintiff.  被告对原告提出了反诉。|Synonyms: countercharge, countersuit, rebuttal, rejoinder|Antonyms: claim, allegation, assertion|noun
counterclaim:/ˈkaʊntərkleɪm/ /ˈkaʊntərklɛɪm/| v.|提出反诉，反驳，反要求|To assert a claim against someone in response to their claim against you.|He counterclaimed that the accident was her fault. 他反驳说事故是她的错。|Synonyms: retort, contradict, dispute|Antonyms: admit, concede, accept|verb
