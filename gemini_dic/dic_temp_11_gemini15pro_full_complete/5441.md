alms:/ɑːmz/ | n. |施舍，救济金|Something given freely to relieve the poor.| The beggar asked for alms. 乞丐乞求施舍。| synonyms: charity, donation, offering | antonyms:  greed, selfishness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English ælmesse, from Late Latin eleemosyna, from Greek eleēmosynē "pity, alms,"  from eleēmōn "merciful, compassionate."
