bellows:/ˈbeləʊz/ (UK), /ˈbɛloʊz/ (US)| n.|风箱；吼叫声；肺|A device that produces a strong current of air when its sides are pressed together, typically used for blowing air into a fire.  Also, a loud, deep roar or shout. Can be used metaphorically to refer to lungs.|The blacksmith worked the bellows to heat the iron. 铁匠拉动风箱给铁加热。|
synonyms: blower, air pump, roar, shout|
antonyms: whisper, murmur|
noun


bellows:/ˈbeləʊz/ (UK), /ˈbɛloʊz/ (US)| v.|吼叫；怒吼|(Of a person or animal) emit a loud, deep roar or shout.|The bull bellowed in rage. 公牛愤怒地吼叫。|
synonyms: roar, bellow, yell, shout|
antonyms: whisper, murmur|
verb
