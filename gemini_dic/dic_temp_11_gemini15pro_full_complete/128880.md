nilometer:/naɪˈlɒmɪtər/ | n. | 尼罗河水位计 | A structure for measuring the height of the Nile River, used in ancient Egypt to predict the success of the annual flood. |  The nilometer at Elephantine Island was crucial for predicting the harvest.  象岛上的尼罗河水位计对于预测收成至关重要。| synonyms:  water gauge, river gauge | antonyms: None | noun
