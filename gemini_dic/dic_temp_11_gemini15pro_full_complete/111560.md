lobed:/loʊbd/| adj.|分叶的，有裂片的|Having a lobe or lobes; divided into lobes.|The oak leaf is lobed. 橡树叶是分叶的。|近义词：divided, cleft, segmented|反义词:whole, entire|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *lobed, from lobe + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin *lobus*, meaning "pod, husk".
