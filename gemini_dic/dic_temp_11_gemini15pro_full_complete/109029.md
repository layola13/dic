leg-puller:/ˈleɡˌpʊlər/| n.|开玩笑的人；爱捉弄人的人|Someone who tells jokes and plays tricks on people, often making them believe something that is not true.|He's a terrible leg-puller; don't believe a word he says. 他是个很爱开玩笑的人；别相信他说的任何话。|Synonyms: joker, prankster, teaser, kidder|Antonyms: serious person, straight person|noun
