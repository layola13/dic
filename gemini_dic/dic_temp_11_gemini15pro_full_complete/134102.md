nonimmanent:/nɒnˈɪmənənt/
adjective
非内在的，外在的
Not inherent or intrinsic; external.
God, in most Western religions, is considered nonimmanent, existing outside of the created universe. 上帝，在大多数西方宗教中，被认为是非内在的，存在于被创造的宇宙之外。
The artist's inspiration was nonimmanent, derived from the natural world around him.  这位艺术家的灵感是非内在的，源于他周围的自然世界。
近义词: extrinsic, external, transcendent
反义词: immanent, inherent, intrinsic
词性: adjective
词源: From non- + immanent, from Late Latin immanens, present participle of immanēre "to remain in," from in- "in" + manēre "to stay."
