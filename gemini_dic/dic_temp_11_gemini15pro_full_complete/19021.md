bet-your-boots:/ˌbet jər ˈbuːts/
idiom
确信无疑；毫无疑问；肯定的
Be so sure of something that you would bet all you have on it being true.
You can bet your boots she'll be late. 你可以肯定她会迟到。
synonyms:be certain, be sure, be positive, be confident, be convinced, have no doubt, know for certain, know for sure
antonyms:doubt, be uncertain
