eschewing:/ɪsˈtʃuːɪŋ/
verb
避开，回避
deliberately avoid using; abstain from.
He eschewed publicity and lived a quiet life. 他避开公众注意，过着平静的生活。

近义词: avoiding, shunning, abstaining from, refraining from, forgoing, forsaking, renouncing, rejecting, declining, spurning, dodging
反义词: embracing, accepting, indulging in
词性: verb (present participle)
