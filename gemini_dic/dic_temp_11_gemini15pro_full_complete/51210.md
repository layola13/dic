destitute:/ˈdestɪtjuːt/ /ˈdestɪtuːt/
adjective
赤贫的，穷困潦倒的；缺乏的，没有的
lacking the necessities of life; without the basic necessities of life; lacking something needed or desirable
例句：The war left many people destitute. 战争使许多人一贫如洗。
例句：The flood victims were left destitute. 洪水的受害者们一无所有。

近义词：impoverished, indigent, penniless, poor, needy, deprived
反义词：wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous

词性: adjective
