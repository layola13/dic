motility:/moʊˈtɪləti/| n. |能动性，运动性|The ability of an organism or fluid to move or cause movement.  The inherent power of movement.|例句：The sperm's motility is essential for fertilization. 精子的运动性对于受精至关重要。

例句：The drug affects the motility of the digestive tract.  这种药物会影响消化道的运动性。|近义词：mobility, movement, locomotion|反义词：immobility, stasis|词性:noun


motile:/ˈmoʊtaɪl/| adj. |能动的，运动的|Capable of self-propelled movement.|例句：Motile sperm are necessary for reproduction.  能动的精子对繁殖是必要的。

例句：Amoebas are motile single-celled organisms.  变形虫是能动的单细胞生物。|近义词：mobile, moving|反义词：immotile, sessile|词性:adjective
