nonsuffrage:/ˌnɑːnˈsʌfrɪdʒ/ | n. | 无选举权；不享有选举权 | Lack of the right to vote, especially for women.  |  In the early 20th century, women's nonsuffrage was a major political issue.  20世纪初，妇女没有选举权是一个主要的政治问题。 | synonyms: disfranchisement, ineligibility to vote | antonyms: suffrage, franchise | noun

Etymology:  From non- (not) + suffrage (right to vote).  Suffrage comes from the Latin word *suffragium* meaning "vote, political support."
