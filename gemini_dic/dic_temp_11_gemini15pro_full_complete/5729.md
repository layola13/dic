alveolate:/ˈælviəleɪt/ | adj. | 蜂窝状的；肺泡状的；小孔状的 | Having small cavities or pits; resembling alveoli. | The surface of the bone appeared alveolate under the microscope.  骨骼表面在显微镜下呈现蜂窝状。| 近义词: pitted, porous, honeycombed | 反义词: smooth, solid | 词性: adjective

alveolate:/ˈælviəleɪt/ | n. | 囊泡虫类 | Any of a group of single-celled eukaryotes most of which have alveoli lying beneath the cell membrane. |  Dinoflagellates are a type of alveolate. 甲藻是一种囊泡虫类。 | 近义词: protist (this is a broader term) | 反义词: N/A (Difficult to provide a meaningful antonym for a biological group) | 词性: noun
