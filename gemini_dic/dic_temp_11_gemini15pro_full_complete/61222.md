一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been reading lately? = 你最近一直在读什么？
What has he been working on all week? = 他整个星期都在做什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for lunch? = 你午饭吃了什么？
What did she say to you? = 她对你说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been studying before you changed your major? = 你转专业前一直在学什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你会完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow afternoon? = 到明天下午他们会完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做这件事五年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？



虚拟语气:

I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。


被动语态:
What was discussed at the conference? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？


否定句:

He didn't say what he wanted. = 他没说他想要什么。
