despair-of:/dɪˈspɛər əv/| phrasal verb | 绝望；放弃希望 |To lose all hope for something or someone.  It implies giving up on the possibility of a positive outcome.|例句：He despaired of ever finding a job. 他对找到工作感到绝望。|例句：She despaired of her son ever changing his ways. 她对她儿子改变他的方式感到绝望。|近义词：give up on, lose hope in|反义词: have hope for, be optimistic about|词性:短语动词
