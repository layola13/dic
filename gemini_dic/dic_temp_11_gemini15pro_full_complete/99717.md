interlucent:/ˌɪntərˈluːsənt/
adjective
半透明的；透光的
Allowing light to pass through but not transparent.
例句：The artist used interlucent paper to create a stained-glass effect.  艺术家用半透明的纸来创造彩色玻璃的效果。

例句：The interlucent panels in the ceiling diffused the sunlight beautifully. 天花板上的半透明嵌板将阳光散射得很美。

近义词：translucent, semitransparent
反义词：opaque
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin inter- "between" + lucent- "shining," from the verb lucēre "to shine."
