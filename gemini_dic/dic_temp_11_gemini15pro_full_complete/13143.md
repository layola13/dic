asymmetric:/ˌeɪsɪˈmetrɪk/ /ˌæsɪˈmetrɪk/ | adj. | 不对称的，非对称的 | Lacking symmetry; not symmetric. Having parts that do not correspond to each other in size, shape, or arrangement | The building has an asymmetric design, with one wing much larger than the other. 这座建筑的设计不对称，一侧的翼比另一侧大得多。| Synonyms: unbalanced, uneven, irregular, lopsided | Antonyms: symmetric, symmetrical, balanced, even | adjective
