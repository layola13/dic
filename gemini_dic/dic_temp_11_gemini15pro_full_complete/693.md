absconding: /æbˈskɒndɪŋ/ /æbˈskɑːndɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
潜逃；逃匿；躲藏
leaving hurriedly and secretly, typically to avoid detection of or arrest for an unlawful action such as theft.
The cashier absconded with the weekend's receipts.  出纳携周末的收入潜逃了。
He absconded from the country with the stolen jewels. 他带着偷来的珠宝逃离了这个国家。

近义词: fleeing, escaping, decamping, bolting, running away, making off, taking flight, vanishing
反义词: remaining, staying, surrendering

词性: verb (present participle)
词源: From Latin abscondere, from abs- ‘away’ + condere ‘to put, store, hide.’
