overexpansivenesses:/ˌoʊvərɪkspænsɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
过度扩张；过度膨胀
The state of being excessively expansive, often in terms of behavior, emotions, or ambitions.  This refers to instances where someone goes beyond reasonable limits in their actions or expressions.
His overexpansiveness alienated some of his colleagues. 他的过度扩张疏远了一些同事。
The company's overexpansiveness led to its eventual downfall.  这家公司的过度扩张导致了它最终的垮台。
近义词:exuberance, extravagance, immoderation
反义词:restraint, moderation, reserve
词性:noun


overexpansiveness:/ˌoʊvərɪkspænsɪvnɪs/
n.
过度扩张；过度膨胀
The state of being excessively expansive, often in terms of behavior, emotions, or ambitions. This refers to instances where someone goes beyond reasonable limits in their actions or expressions.
His overexpansiveness made him seem arrogant. 他的过度扩张让他看起来很傲慢。
Her overexpansiveness in spending led to debt. 她在消费上的过度扩张导致了债务。
近义词:excessiveness, lavishness, profligacy
反义词:conservatism, frugality, modesty
词性:noun

词源:  From "over-" (excessively) + "expansive" (tending to expand or enlarge) + "-ness" (forming a noun denoting a state or quality).  Ultimately derived from Latin "expandere" (to spread out).
