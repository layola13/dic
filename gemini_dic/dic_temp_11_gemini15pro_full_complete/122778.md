molar:/ˈmoʊlər (US), ˈməʊlə(UK)/| adj. n.|磨牙的，臼齿的；摩尔的，克分子的|Relating to moles or molars.  A grinding tooth at the back of a mammal's mouth.|例句：The dentist examined my molars. 牙医检查了我的臼齿。
