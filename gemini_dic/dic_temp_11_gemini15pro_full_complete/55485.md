distractful:/dɪˈstræktfəl/
adjective
分散注意力的
Causing distraction; tending to take away one's attention.
The constant notifications on my phone are very distractful. 我的手机不断弹出通知，非常分散注意力。
It's a distractful environment for studying.  这是一个不利于学习的环境，很容易分心。

近义词: distracting, disturbing, diverting
反义词: focusing, concentrating
词性: adjective
