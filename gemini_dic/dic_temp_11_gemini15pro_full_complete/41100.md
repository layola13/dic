copresenting:/ˌkoʊprɪˈzɛntɪŋ/
verb
共同呈现；共同主持；共同介绍
presenting something together with someone else.

例句：
They are copresenting a new television show.
他们共同主持一个新的电视节目。

例句：
The two scientists are copresenting their findings at the conference.
两位科学家在会议上共同展示他们的研究成果。

近义词：co-hosting, jointly presenting
反义词：solo presenting, individually presenting
词性：v.
