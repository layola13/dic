arseniuret:/ɑːrˈsɛnjuˌraɪt/
n.
砷化物
A binary compound of arsenic with a more electropositive element or group.
Gallium arsenide is a useful arseniuret. 砷化镓是一种有用的砷化物。

近义词:arsenide
反义词:None
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *arséniurétte*, from arsenic + -uret.  Ultimately from Greek arsenikon (arsenic).
