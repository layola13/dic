calorimotor:/ˌkæləriˈmoʊtər/ | adj. | 热运动的；热动力的 | Relating to or involving both heat and motion; caused by heat and resulting in motion. |  The calorimotor effect of the explosion was immense. 爆炸的热动力效应是巨大的。 |  Thermogenic |  Anergic | adjective


calorimotor:/ˌkæləriˈmoʊtər/ | n. | 热量计 | An apparatus for measuring the heat generated by a chemical reaction, change of state, or formation of a solution.  | The scientist used a calorimotor to measure the heat produced. 科学家使用热量计来测量产生的热量。| calorimeter, thermometer |  -- | noun
