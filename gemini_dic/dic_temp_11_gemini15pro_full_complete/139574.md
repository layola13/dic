obversely:/əbˈvɜːrsli/ /ɑbˈvɜːrsli/
adverb
相反地; 反面地
in a way that is the opposite of something else.
Obversely, the more educated you are, the less likely you are to be prejudiced. 相反，你受的教育越多，你就越不可能有偏见。
Obversely, if you spend less, you'll save more. 反过来说，如果你少花钱，你就会存更多钱。
Synonyms: conversely, inversely, contrarily
Antonyms: similarly, likewise
词性: 副词
