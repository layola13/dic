alleyed:/ˈælaɪd/ | adj. |结盟的，有关联的|Having an alliance, especially a political or military one.  Related or connected in some way.|The two alleyed nations signed a treaty. 这两个结盟的国家签署了一项条约。|近义词: allied, affiliated, associated, connected, related |反义词: unaffiliated, unconnected, unrelated, opposed, enemy |词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb "ally" + "-ed" suffix, ultimately from Old French "alier" (to unite, combine).
