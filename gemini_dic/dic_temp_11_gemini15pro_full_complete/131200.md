nonbureaucratically: /ˌnɒnˌbjʊərəˈkrætɪkli/ (US) /ˌnɒnˌbjʊərəˈkrætɪkəli/ (UK)
| adv. | 非官僚地 | In a way that avoids excessive bureaucracy or official procedures; informally and efficiently. | The issue was resolved nonbureaucratically, with a simple phone call.  这个问题通过一个简单的电话非正式地解决了。| Synonyms: informally, efficiently, simply, directly | Antonyms: bureaucratically, formally, officially | Word Type: Adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix) + bureaucratic + -ally (suffix)
* "non-" means "not" or "opposite of".
* "bureaucratic" comes from "bureaucracy," which originates from the French word "bureaucratie," combining "bureau" (desk, office) and "-cratie" (rule, power).
* "-ally" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
