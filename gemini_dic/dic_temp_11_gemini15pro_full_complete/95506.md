inclined:/ɪnˈklaɪnd/| adj.|倾向的，倾斜的，有…意愿的|Having a natural tendency or disposition towards something; sloping.|例句：She is inclined to be shy. 她有点害羞。|近义词：disposed, prone, apt, likely, tending|反义词：disinclined, averse, unwilling|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *inclinātus, past participle of inclināre "to bend, bow, incline," from in- "into, towards" + clināre "to lean, bend."
