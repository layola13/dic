matrimonially:/ˌmætrɪˈmoʊniəli/
adverb
婚姻地，在婚姻方面
Relating to marriage or the state of being married.
They were matrimonially bound for fifty years. 他们在婚姻中共同生活了五十年。
近义词: maritally, conjugally
反义词:  unmarried, single
词性: adverb

词源:  From Late Latin *matrimonialis, from Latin matrimon?um "marriage," from mater "mother" + -monium "-mony."
