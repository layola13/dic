brevirostrate:/ˌbrɛvɪˈrɒstreɪt/
adjective

Having a short beak or snout.
The brevirostrate bird had difficulty reaching the nectar deep within the flower.  这只短喙的鸟难以够到花朵深处的花蜜。
近义词:short-beaked, short-billed
反义词:longirostrate, long-beaked
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
