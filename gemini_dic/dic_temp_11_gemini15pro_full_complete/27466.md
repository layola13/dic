campaigner:/kæmˈpeɪnər/ | n. | 运动者，活动家，竞选者 | A person who actively participates in or supports a campaign, especially a political or social one. |  Example 1: The campaigners worked tirelessly to raise awareness about climate change.  活动家们不知疲倦地工作，以提高人们对气候变化的认识。

Example 2:  She's a well-known campaigner for animal rights. 她是一位著名的动物权利活动家。|Synonyms: activist, advocate, supporter, promoter | Antonyms: opponent, adversary, critic | noun
