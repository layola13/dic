expense:/ɪkˈspens/ /ɛkˈspɛns/| n.|费用，支出，花费|Something that requires payment of money; cost.|例句：The travel expenses were high. 旅行费用很高。|近义词：cost, charge, outlay, expenditure|反义词:income, profit|noun

expense:/ɪkˈspens/ /ɛkˈspɛns/| n.|开销，花费|The act of spending or using money.|例句：He kept a record of his expenses. 他记录了他的开销。|近义词：spending, outlay|反义词:saving|noun

expense:/ɪkˈspens/ /ɛkˈspɛns/| n.|损失，损害|Detriment, loss, or damage.|例句：He learned from the experience at great expense. 他以巨大的代价从这次经历中吸取了教训。|近义词：loss, detriment, sacrifice|反义词:gain, benefit|noun
