hyperacuities:/ˌhaɪpərəˈkjuːɪtiz/
|n.|超常敏锐，超常的感知力|The ability to perceive sensory stimuli with extraordinary accuracy or sensitivity, exceeding normal human capabilities.|Bats have hyperacuities in hearing that allow them to navigate in darkness.  蝙蝠拥有超常敏锐的听力，使它们能够在黑暗中导航。|
| | |Hyperacuities in vision allow some birds of prey to spot small animals from great distances.  某些猛禽的超常视觉敏锐度使它们能够从很远的地方发现小动物。|
| | |synonyms: supersensitivities, heightened sensitivities|
| | |antonyms: insensitivities, dullness|
| | |noun|
