dispensary:/dɪˈspensəri/ | n. | 药房；诊疗所 | A place where medicine or medical treatment is dispensed. |  例句：She went to the dispensary to get her prescription filled. 她去药房配药。

例句：The free dispensary offered medical care to the poor.  免费诊疗所向穷人提供医疗服务。

近义词：pharmacy, clinic, health center | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

dispensary:/dɪˈspensəri/ | n. | (尤指机构的)饮料食品供应处 | A place in a building such as a school or hotel where drinks and snacks are sold. | 例句：The students lined up at the dispensary for their afternoon snack. 学生们在零食供应处排队领取下午茶点。

例句：The hotel dispensary offered a variety of beverages and light snacks. 酒店的饮料食品供应处提供各种饮料和小吃。

近义词：canteen, cafeteria | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dispensaria, from Latin dispensare "to distribute, weigh out," from dis- "out, apart" + pensare "to weigh, pay."  The original sense was "a place where things are weighed out," later specialized to medicine.
