monodialectal:/ˌmɒnəʊdaɪˈlɛktəl/ | adj. |单方言的| Speaking or using only one dialect.  Characterized by the use of only one dialect. |  She grew up in a relatively monodialectal community, so her accent is very distinct. 她在一个相对单一方言的社区长大，所以她的口音很独特。

The research focused on monodialectal speakers of Standard American English. 这项研究的重点是只说标准美国英语的单方言使用者。 | 近义词: unilingual | 反义词: multidialectal, polydialectal | 词性: adjective
词源:  "mono-" (one) + "dialect" + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives). From Greek "monos" (single, alone) and "dialektos" (conversation, dialect).
