overeasy:/ˌōvərˈēzē/| adj.|煎一面半熟的;单面煎的|Fried on one side, then flipped and cooked just until the white is set but the yolk is still runny.|I'd like my eggs overeasy, please. 我想要单面煎的鸡蛋。|近义词:sunny-side up (更生的)|反义词:over hard, well-done|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over" + "easy". "Over" in this context implies flipping the egg over, and "easy" refers to cooking it lightly.
