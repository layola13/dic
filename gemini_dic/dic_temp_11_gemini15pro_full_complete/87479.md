hetarism:/ˈhɛtərɪzəm/ | n. |异教，异端; 异性恋 | The state or quality of being a hetarist.  A belief or practice different from the accepted norm, especially in religion. In a narrower sense, exclusive sexual attraction to the opposite sex. |  Ancient Greeks sometimes viewed other cultures' religious practices as hetarism.  /  His hetarism led him to question the established doctrines. | 近义词: heresy, heterodoxy, unorthodoxy, paganism (in some contexts),  heterosexuality | 反义词: orthodoxy, conformity, homosexuality | 词性: noun
