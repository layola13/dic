hedged:/hɛdʒd/ | v. adj.| 限制，规避；用树篱围住；避免直接回答|To limit or qualify something; to avoid giving a direct answer; surrounded by a hedge. |例句：He hedged his bets by investing in a variety of stocks. 他通过投资各种股票来规避风险。|近义词：evade, avoid, equivocate, fence, qualify, circumvent, limit, restrict|反义词：commit, confront, face, encounter|词性:verb, adjective


hedged:/hɛdʒd/ | adj.| 限制的，规避的；用树篱围住的|Limited or qualified; surrounded by a hedge. |例句：The company gave a hedged response to the journalist's question. 公司对记者的提问给出了一个含糊其辞的回答。|近义词：guarded, cautious, noncommittal, restricted, limited|反义词：unqualified, unrestricted, unlimited, direct, forthright|词性:adjective

词源: From Middle English heggen, from Old English hecgian (“to hedge”), from hecg (“hedge”).
