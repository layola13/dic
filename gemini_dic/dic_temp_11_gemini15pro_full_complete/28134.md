capitolina:/kæpɪtəˈliːnə/| adj.|卡比托林山的，朱庇特神庙的|Relating to the Capitoline Hill in Rome, especially the Temple of Jupiter.|The Capitolina Triad consisted of Jupiter, Juno, and Minerva.  卡比托林三神由朱庇特、朱诺和密涅瓦组成。|近义词:Roman, Jovian|反义词:None|词性:adjective

Etymology->
From Latin *Capitolinus*, derived from *Capitolium* ("Capitol"), ultimately from *caput* ("head"). 源于拉丁语 *Capitolinus*，派生自 *Capitolium*（“卡比托林山”），最终源于 *caput*（“头”）。
