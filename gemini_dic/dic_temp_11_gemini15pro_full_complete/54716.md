disownments:/dɪsˈoʊnmənts/
n.
断绝关系，否认，抛弃
The act of refusing to acknowledge or accept something as one's own; repudiation.
His disownment of his family shocked everyone. 他与家人断绝关系震惊了所有人。
Synonyms: repudiations, rejections, denials, renunciations
Antonyms: acknowledgements, acceptances, embraces
noun
