do-goodery:/ˈduːˌɡʊdəri/
noun
做好事，慈善行为，（尤指）不切实际的慈善行为
The act of doing good works, often in a self-righteous or ineffective way.
Her do-goodery annoyed her family. 她的慈善行为让她的家人很恼火。
Synonyms: altruism, philanthropy, charity, benevolence
Antonyms: selfishness, malevolence, malice, cruelty
