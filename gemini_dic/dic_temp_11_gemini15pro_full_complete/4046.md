aided:/ˈeɪdɪd/
verb|辅助的，帮助的|given help or assistance.|The project was aided by a generous grant. 这个项目得到了慷慨的资助。|assisted, supported, helped|hindered, obstructed|v.

Etymology:
From the past participle of *aid*, which comes from Old French *aider*, from Latin *adiūtāre* "to help, assist," frequentative of *adiuvāre*  "to help," from *ad-* "to" + *iuvāre* "to help, please."
