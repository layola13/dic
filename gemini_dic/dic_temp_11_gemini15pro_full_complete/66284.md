excommunications:/ˌɛks kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz/
|n.|逐出教会；绝罚|The act of officially excluding someone from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.|The pope threatened excommunication for anyone who disobeyed his orders. 教皇威胁要将任何不服从他命令的人逐出教会。|exclusions, banishings|inclusion, acceptance|noun

excommunication:/ˌɛks kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/
|n.|排斥；断绝关系|The act of excluding or banning someone from a group or community.|His excommunication from the scientific community was a result of his fraudulent research. 他被科学界排斥是因为他的研究造假。|ostracism, expulsion|inclusion, acceptance|noun
