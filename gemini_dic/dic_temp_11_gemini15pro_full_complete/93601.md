imbathe:/ɪmˈbeɪð/
|v.|浸泡，沐浴，沉浸|To immerse or bathe something, often in light or a feeling.  To surround or envelop.|The setting sun imbathed the mountains in a golden glow. 落日给群山披上了一层金色的光辉。|近义词：bathe, immerse, submerge, suffuse, drench|反义词:dry|词性:动词

Etymology:
From im- (prefix meaning "in, into") + bathe.
