machineries:/məˈʃiːn(ə)riz/
n.
机械, 机器（总称）
A collective term for machines.  Usually considered non-standard or archaic.  "Machinery" (singular) is generally preferred.
The farmer invested in new machineries to improve his harvest. (Non-standard usage)  农民投资购买了新机器以提高收成.
The factory was filled with loud machineries. (Non-standard usage) 工厂里充满了嘈杂的机器。
