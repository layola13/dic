fossilizes:/ˈfɒsəlaɪzɪz/ /ˈfɑːsəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使...变成化石；使...僵化
to become a fossil; to preserve an animal or plant as a fossil.  Also, to become rigid and unchanging.
The dinosaur bones fossilized over millions of years.  恐龙骨骼经过数百万年变成了化石。

The traditions of the tribe have fossilized over time.  这个部落的传统随着时间的推移已经僵化。
synonyms: petrify, mineralize
antonyms: revitalize, modernize
词性: 动词
