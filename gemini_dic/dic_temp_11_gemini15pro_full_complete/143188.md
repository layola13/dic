outcompete: /ˌaʊtkəmˈpiːt/
verb
胜过，超越，在竞争中胜过
to compete more successfully than someone or something else
例句：
Smaller companies are often outcompeted by larger corporations with more resources.
小型公司经常在竞争中输给拥有更多资源的大公司。

The new supermarket is outcompeting the smaller shops in the area.
新的超级市场在竞争中胜过该地区的较小商店。

近义词：beat, defeat, surpass, outdo, outstrip, outrival
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：v.
