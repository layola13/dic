overlier:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪər/| n.|异常值，离群值|A data point that differs significantly from other observations.|This house is an overlier in the local property market, as it's much more expensive than similar-sized houses in the area. 这栋房子在当地房地产市场上是一个异常值，因为它比该地区类似大小的房子贵得多。|Example: Removing the overliers from the data set gave a more accurate representation of the trend. 从数据集中移除异常值可以更准确地表示趋势。|Synonyms: anomaly, outlier, exception, aberration|Antonyms: norm, average, standard|noun

overlie:/ˌoʊvərˈlaɪ/| v.|覆盖，叠加|To lie on top of something; to cover or overlap.|Sedimentary rock often overlies older igneous rock. 沉积岩通常覆盖在较老的火成岩之上。|Example: The new carpet overlies the original wooden floor. 新地毯覆盖在原来的木地板上。|Synonyms: cover, overlay, superimpose|Antonyms: uncover, expose|verb
