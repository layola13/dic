closedness:/ˈkloʊzdnɪs/
n.
封闭性，闭合性
The closedness of the community made it difficult for outsiders to integrate.  这个社区的封闭性使得外来者难以融入。
The mathematical concept of closedness is crucial in topology. 封闭性的数学概念在拓扑学中至关重要。
例句：The closedness of the system prevented any outside interference. 系统的封闭性防止了任何外部干扰。
例句：The closedness of the group made them seem exclusive.  这个群体的封闭性使他们看起来很排外。
近义词：insularity, exclusivity, impermeability
反义词：openness, accessibility, permeability
词性: 名词
