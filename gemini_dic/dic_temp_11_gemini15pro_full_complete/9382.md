antiquatedness:/ˈæn.tɪ.kweɪ.tɪd.nəs/
n.
过时，陈旧
The state of being antiquated or outdated.
The antiquatedness of their computer system made it difficult to compete.  他们的计算机系统过于陈旧，难以与人竞争。
antiquity, obsoleteness, archaism
modernity, currency, newness
noun
