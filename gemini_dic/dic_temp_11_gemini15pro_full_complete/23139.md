bourse:/bʊrs/ /bʊəs/| n.|交易所，证券交易所|A stock exchange, especially a European one.|例句：The Paris Bourse is one of the oldest stock exchanges in the world. 巴黎证券交易所是世界上最古老的证券交易所之一。

例句：Investors anxiously watched the activity on the bourse. 投资者焦急地看着交易所的活动。|近义词：stock exchange, stock market, exchange, market|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "private market")|词性:noun
