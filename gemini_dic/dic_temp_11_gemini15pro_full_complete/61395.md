emblematiccize: /ˌɛmbləˈmætɪsaɪz/
| v. | 使…成为象征；使…象征化 | To make something emblematic; to cause something to be a symbol or representative of something else. |  The artist emblematiccized the struggle for freedom through the image of a soaring bird. 艺术家通过翱翔的鸟的形象象征了对自由的渴望。

The company emblematiccized its commitment to sustainability by planting trees.  公司通过植树来象征其对可持续发展的承诺。 | 近义词: symbolize, typify, represent | 反义词: obscure, conceal | 词性: verb

词源:  From "emblematic" + "-ize".  "Emblematic" comes from the French "emblématique" and ultimately from the Greek "emblematikos," meaning "pertaining to an emblem."  The suffix "-ize" forms verbs meaning "to make or cause to be."
