metropoles:/məˈtrɒpəliːz/
n.
主要城市；大都市
A major city, especially the chief city of a country, region, or state.
London and New York are two of the world's leading metropolises. 伦敦和纽约是世界领先的两大都市。
metropolis, megacity, cosmopolis
village, town, hamlet
noun
