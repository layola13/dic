haste:/heɪst/| v.| 催促，赶快|To hurry; to move or act with haste.|He hastened to assure her that everything would be alright. 他急忙向她保证一切都会好起来的。|近义词：hurry, rush, speed, expedite|反义词：delay, slow, dawdle|verb
