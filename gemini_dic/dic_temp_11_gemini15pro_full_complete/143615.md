outissuing:/ˌaʊtˈɪʃuːɪŋ/
adjective
过度发行的；过度发放的
Issuing or supplying in excess.
The outissuing of banknotes caused inflation.  过度发行钞票导致了通货膨胀。
近义词: overissuing
反义词: underissuing
词性: adjective

outissuing:/ˌaʊtˈɪʃuːɪŋ/
noun
过度发行；过度发放
The act of issuing or supplying in excess.
The outissuing of permits led to overcrowding.  许可证的过度发放导致了过度拥挤。
近义词: overissue
反义词: underissue
词性: noun

词源:
"out-" (prefix) + "issuing" (present participle of "issue")

"out-" 表示“超过，过度”
"issue" 源自古法语 "issue"，意为“出口，结果”。
