hesitantly:/ˈhezɪtəntli/ | adv. | 犹豫地，迟疑地 | In a way that shows hesitation or uncertainty. | She hesitantly agreed to go with him. 她犹豫地同意和他一起去。| 近义词: tentatively, cautiously, reluctantly | 反义词: confidently, decisively, resolutely | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "hesitant," which comes from the Latin "haesitare" meaning "to stick fast, to hesitate."
