omnilateral:/ˌɒmnɪˈlætərəl/
adjective
全面的，多方面的
involving all sides or parties; all-sided.
例句：The treaty requires omnilateral disarmament.  该条约要求全面裁军。

例句：The government took an omnilateral approach to the economic crisis. 政府对经济危机采取了全面的方法。

近义词：comprehensive, multilateral, all-encompassing
反义词：unilateral, limited, partial
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From omni- (all, every) + lateral (of or pertaining to the side).  "Omni-" comes from Latin omnis "all".  "Lateral" comes from Latin lateralis "belonging to the side," from latus "side."
