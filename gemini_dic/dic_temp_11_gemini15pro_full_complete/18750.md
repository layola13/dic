berime:/bɪˈraɪm/ | v. | 责骂，斥责 | To criticize or rebuke someone harshly. |  He berimed the waiter for the slow service. 他严厉斥责了服务员上菜太慢。

He was berimed by his boss for missing the deadline. 他因为错过截止日期而被老板责骂。 | 近义词：scold, rebuke, reprimand, chide, chastise | 反义词：praise, commend, laud |词性：动词

词源:  From Middle English *berimen, from Old English *berȳman, from *be- + *hrȳman (“to cry out”). Cognate with Old Frisian berōma (“to boast”), Old Saxon birōmian (“to boast”), Middle Dutch berœmen (“to boast”), Old High German ruomen (“to boast, praise”), German rühmen (“to praise, boast”). More at room.  最初含义更接近“大声呼喊”，后来演变为带有负面意义的“斥责”。
