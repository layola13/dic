词性: 名词 (derived from verb)


airfreighting: /ˈɛərfreɪtɪŋ/
v.|空运|Transporting goods by aircraft.|They are airfreighting the emergency supplies to the disaster zone.  他们正在空运紧急物资到灾区。|近义词: airlifting, shipping by air|反义词: shipping by sea, trucking
词性: 动名词 (present participle of the verb 'airfreight')


词源:  'airfreight' is a combination of 'air' and 'freight'. 'Air' refers to the medium of transport (by aircraft), and 'freight' refers to goods transported commercially.
