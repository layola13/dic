USAGE->

词源：This compound noun originated in the late 16th century, literally referring to the act of splitting hairs, which is obviously a very difficult and pointless task.  This literal meaning then evolved metaphorically to describe over-scrupulous attention to detail or fine distinctions in reasoning.
