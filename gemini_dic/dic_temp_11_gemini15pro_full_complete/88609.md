hivelike:/ˈhaɪvlaɪk/| adj.|蜂巢状的，像蜂巢的|Resembling a beehive, especially in being densely populated or intricately structured.|The apartment building was hivelike, with hundreds of tiny units packed together.|近义词：crowded, dense, packed, swarming|反义词:sparse, empty, deserted|词性:形容词
词源: hive + like.  'Hive' comes from Old English *hīfe, related to Old High German hūba 'hut, beehive'. 'Like' comes from Old English *līc 'having the same form, similar,' from Proto-Germanic *līkaz.
