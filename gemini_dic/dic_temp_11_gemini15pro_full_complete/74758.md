freewill:/ˌfriːˈwɪl/| n.|自由意志|The power of acting without the constraint of necessity or fate; the ability to act at one's own discretion.|Do we really have freewill, or are our actions predetermined? 我们真的有自由意志吗，还是我们的行为是预先注定的？|近义词：autonomy, volition, self-determination|反义词：determinism, fatalism|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *frei wille*, from Old English *frēo willa* ("free will"), equivalent to *free* + *will*. Cognate with Dutch *vrije wil* ("free will"), German *freier Wille* ("free will").
