non-stoics:/nɒnˈstəʊɪks/ | n. | 非斯多葛学派的人 |  People who do not adhere to the philosophy of Stoicism.  Stoicism emphasizes virtue, reason, and living in harmony with nature. Non-stoics, therefore, might be more prone to emotional reactions, be less focused on self-control, or not prioritize a life guided by reason. |  The non-stoics in the group were more visibly upset by the bad news.  这群人中非斯多葛主义者对坏消息的反应更加明显。

The play explored the emotional responses of the non-stoics to tragedy. 这部戏剧探讨了非斯多葛主义者对悲剧的情感反应。 | 近义词: emotionalists, non-philosophers (in this context) | 反义词: Stoics | 词性: 名词

non-stoic:/nɒnˈstəʊɪk/ | adj. | 非斯多葛学派的 | Not characteristic of a Stoic; not adhering to the principles of Stoicism. | He gave a very non-stoic response, bursting into tears at the news. 他做出了一个非常不像斯多葛学派者的反应，听到这个消息后泪流满面。
