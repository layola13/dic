linarite:/ˈlɪnəˌraɪt/| n. | 铜蓝|A deep blue mineral consisting of a basic sulfate of lead and copper. |Linarite is a prized mineral among collectors for its vibrant color. 铜蓝因其鲜艳的颜色而成为收藏家们珍视的矿物。|Synonyms: none (highly specific mineral) |Antonyms: none |noun
