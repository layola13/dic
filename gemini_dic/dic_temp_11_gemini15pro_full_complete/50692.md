dermatology:/ˌdɜːrməˈtɒlədʒi/ (UK) /ˌdɜːrməˈtɑːlədʒi/ (US)| n. |皮肤病学|The branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of skin disorders.|She specializes in dermatology and treats various skin conditions. 她专门研究皮肤病学，治疗各种皮肤疾病。|synonyms: skin medicine, skin care|antonyms: None|noun

dermatological:/ˌdɜːrmətəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (UK) /ˌdɜːrmətəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ (US)| adj. |皮肤病学的；皮肤的|Relating to the skin or dermatology.|The dermatological condition requires specialized treatment.  这种皮肤病需要专门治疗。|synonyms: cutaneous, dermal|antonyms: None|adjective

dermatologist:/ˌdɜːrməˈtɒlədʒɪst/ (UK) /ˌdɜːrməˈtɑːlədʒɪst/ (US)| n. |皮肤科医生|A medical practitioner qualified to diagnose and treat skin disorders.|I made an appointment with a dermatologist to examine the rash. 我预约了一位皮肤科医生来检查皮疹。|synonyms: skin doctor, skin specialist|antonyms: None|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French dermatologie, from Ancient Greek δέρμα (dérma, “skin”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).
