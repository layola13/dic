lodesman:/ˈloʊdzmən/ | n. |领港员，引水员 | A person, especially one licensed or appointed, who pilots ships in and out of a harbor or through difficult waters. | The lodesman skillfully guided the massive tanker through the treacherous strait. 领港员熟练地引导着大型油轮穿过危险的海峡。|  The experienced lodesman knew the local currents and tides well. 经验丰富的引水员对当地的水流和潮汐非常了解。 | synonyms: pilot, harbor pilot, navigator | antonyms: passenger, crew (although not direct opposites, these represent roles other than piloting) | noun
