bangled:/ˈbæŋɡld/| adj.|戴着手镯的|Adorned with bangles; wearing bangles.  Decorated or ornamented with bangles.|Her bangled wrists jingled as she gestured. 她戴着手镯的手腕在她做手势时发出叮当声。|近义词：ornamented, decorated, adorned|反义词:bare, unadorned|词性:adjective
Etymology: From bangle + -ed.  "Bangle" comes from Hindi "bangṛī," ultimately from Sanskrit "valaya," meaning "bracelet."
