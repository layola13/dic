melliphagous:/məˈlɪfəɡəs/| adj.|食蜜的|Feeding on honey; honey-eating.|例句：The melliphagous bat feeds on nectar. 食蜜蝙蝠以花蜜为食。

例句：Melliphagous insects play a crucial role in pollination. 食蜜昆虫在授粉中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：nectarivorous, honey-eating|反义词:carnivorous, herbivorous|词性:adjective
词源:From Greek *méli* (honey) + *-phagos* (eater)
