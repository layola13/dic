nauseates:/ˈnɔːzieɪts/ | v. | 使恶心，使作呕 | To cause someone to feel nausea; to make someone feel sick to their stomach. | The smell of the fish nauseated me.  鱼的味道让我恶心。| synonyms: sickens, disgusts, repulses | antonyms: pleases, delights | verb

nauseating:/ˈnɔːzieɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人作呕的， nauseous 的 | Causing nausea; sickening. | The sight of the rotting food was nauseating. 腐烂食物的景象令人作呕。| synonyms: sickening, disgusting, repulsive | antonyms: pleasant, delightful | adjective

词源:

From Latin *nauseatus*, past participle of *nauseare* "to be seasick," from Greek *naus* "ship," *nausia* "seasickness."
