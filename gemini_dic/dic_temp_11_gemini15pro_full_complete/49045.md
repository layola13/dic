dehisce:/dɪˈhɪs/| v. |裂开，开裂| (of a pod, wound, or seam) gap open or split apart. | The seed pods dehisce to release their seeds. 种荚裂开释放种子。|
The surgeon made an incision, and the wound dehisced a few days later.  外科医生做了一个切口，几天后伤口裂开了。|
Synonyms: split, burst, rupture, gape, crack | Antonyms: close, seal, heal | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dehiscere "to gape," from de- "completely" + hiscere "to yawn, gape."
