accruing:/əˈkruːɪŋ/
verb
积累，增加
(of sums of money or benefits) be received by someone in regular or increasing amounts over time.
Interest is accruing on my savings account. 我的储蓄账户正在累积利息。
Synonyms: accumulate, collect, gather, build up, grow, increase, mount up, pile up
Antonyms: decrease, diminish, dwindle, lessen, reduce
