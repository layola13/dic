isoallele:/ˈaɪsoʊəˌlil/ | n. | 同等位基因 | An allele that is functionally equivalent to another allele of the same gene.  It produces the same phenotype despite a difference in DNA sequence. | Example 1: The two isoalleles at this locus produce the same enzyme activity. 这两个位点上的同等位基因产生相同的酶活性。

Example 2:  Researchers identified several isoalleles responsible for the same coat color in mice. 研究人员发现了几个导致小鼠相同毛色的同等位基因。 | 近义词: allele, variant | 反义词: null allele | 词性: noun
