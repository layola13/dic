judeka:/dʒuˈdiːkə/
n.
犹大居民；犹太人
A person from Judea, an ancient region in southern Palestine.
Judas was a Judean.  犹大是犹太人。
Judeans built the Second Temple. 犹太人建造了第二圣殿。
近义词:Jew, Israelite
反义词:Gentile
词性:noun
