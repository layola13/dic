a-fault:/əˈfɔːlt/| adj.|有故障的，出毛病的|Being a-fault means that something is not working correctly or is broken. It often refers to mechanical or electrical systems.|The robot was a-fault and had to be shut down. 机器人出故障了，必须关掉。|The a-fault engine caused the plane to crash. 故障引擎导致飞机坠毁。|近义词：malfunctioning, broken, defective, faulty|反义词：working, functional, operational|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French "falte," meaning "lack, want, default."  The prefix "a-" is an intensifier, emphasizing the state of being faulty.
