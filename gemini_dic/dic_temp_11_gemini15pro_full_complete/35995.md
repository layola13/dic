codevelopers:/ˌkoʊdɪˈvɛləpərz/| n.|共同开发者|People or organizations who develop something together.|Example 1: The codevelopers of the new software worked tirelessly to meet the deadline. 新软件的共同开发者不知疲倦地工作以赶上最后期限。

Example 2: The two companies acted as codevelopers for the electric car project.  这两家公司作为电动汽车项目的共同开发者。|近义词：collaborators, partners, joint developers|反义词:competitors, rivals|Noun
