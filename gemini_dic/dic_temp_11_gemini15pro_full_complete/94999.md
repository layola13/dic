in-transit:/ɪn ˈtrænzɪt/ | adj.|运输中的，在途的|Being transported; in the process of being moved from one place to another.|The package is currently in-transit and should arrive within three business days. 包裹目前正在运输中，应该会在三个工作日内到达。|My luggage got lost in-transit. 我的行李在运输过程中丢失了。|近义词：in transit, on the way, en route, being shipped, being transported|反义词：delivered, arrived, received|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *in transitu*, from in + transitus (“a passing over”), past participle of transire (“to go across, to pass over”).
