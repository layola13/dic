devaporation:/diːˌvæpəˈreɪʃən/
n.
去蒸发，逆蒸发；（液化）
The process of changing from a vapor to a liquid.  It is the opposite of evaporation.
The devaporation of the humid air resulted in condensation on the windows. 潮湿空气的去蒸发导致窗户上出现冷凝。
近义词：condensation, liquefaction
反义词：evaporation, vaporization
词性：名词

Etymology:  'de-' (prefix indicating reversal) + 'evaporation' (from 'evaporate', from Latin 'evaporare' - to disperse in vapor)
