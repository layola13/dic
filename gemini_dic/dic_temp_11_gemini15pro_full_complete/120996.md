misclassification:/ˌmɪsklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ |n.|错误分类，误分类|The misclassification of the document led to significant delays.  该文件的错误分类导致了严重的延误。| The algorithm's misclassification rate was surprisingly high.  该算法的误分类率高得惊人。|近义词：wrong classification, incorrect categorization, erroneous classification|反义词：correct classification, accurate categorization|词性: noun

Etymology:
mis- (prefix): meaning "wrong" or "badly" (from Old English and Old French)
classification (noun): from classify (verb) + -ation (suffix forming nouns of action or process)
classify (verb): from class (noun) + -ify (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make" or "to cause to be")
class (noun): from Latin "classis" meaning "division," "group," or "fleet"
