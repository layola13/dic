doggiest:/ˈdɒɡiɪst/ (US) /ˈdɒɡɪɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最像狗的，最狗的|Most resembling or characteristic of a dog, especially in a negative or derogatory way.  Often used to describe a person who is fawning or obsequious.|He gave me the doggiest look, all pleading and big-eyed. 他给了我一个非常像狗的眼神，充满了恳求，眼睛瞪得很大。|Synonyms: canine, servile, fawning, obsequious|Antonyms: independent, dignified, aloof|adjective
