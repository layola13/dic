coffeeman:/ˈkɒfiːmæn/ (UK) /ˈkɑːfiːmæn/ (US)| n.|咖啡师，咖啡爱好者，经常喝咖啡的人|A person who makes coffee, especially one who works in a coffee shop, or someone who is very fond of coffee and drinks it regularly.|例句：The coffeeman expertly poured latte art into my cup. 咖啡师熟练地在我的杯子里倒入了拿铁拉花。

例句：He's a real coffeeman; he can't start his day without a cup. 他是个真正的咖啡爱好者；他一天不喝咖啡就无法开始。|近义词：barista, coffee lover, coffee aficionado|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who dislikes coffee or tea drinker)|noun
