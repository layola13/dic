outpraising:/ˌaʊtˈpreɪzɪŋ/
verb
过分赞扬，胜过…的赞美
Praising someone or something more than is deserved or more than another person or thing.
He was outpraising his rival's latest product, perhaps to make his own seem better. 他过分赞扬了竞争对手的最新产品，或许是为了让自己的产品显得更好。
Synonyms: overpraising, exaggerating the praise of, lauding excessively, eulogizing too much
Antonyms: criticizing, understating, downplaying
词性: 动词
