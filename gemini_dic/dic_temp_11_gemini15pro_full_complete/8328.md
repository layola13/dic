anti-pestilence:/ˌæntiˈpɛstɪləns/
adj. | 防疫的，抗瘟疫的 | Preventing or protecting against pestilence (a deadly epidemic disease). |  The doctor prescribed anti-pestilence medication during the outbreak.  医生在疫情爆发期间开了抗瘟疫药物。 | Example: The community implemented anti-pestilence measures to prevent the spread of the disease. 社区实施了防疫措施以防止疾病传播。| Synonyms: anti-epidemic, prophylactic | Antonyms:  pestilential, contagious | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- "against" + pestilence "deadly epidemic disease".  "Anti-" comes from Greek, and "pestilence" comes from Old French "pestilence", ultimately from Latin "pestilentia".
