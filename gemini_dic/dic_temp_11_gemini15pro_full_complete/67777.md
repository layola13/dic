eyeleting: /ˈaɪlɪtɪŋ/
n.|穿孔加固；铆眼；眼孔|The act of reinforcing holes in fabric or other material with metal rings.|The tailor is skilled in eyeleting delicate fabrics.  裁缝擅长在精致的面料上穿孔加固。|近义词: grommeting, reinforcing|反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:名词

eyelet: /ˈaɪlɪt/
n.|眼孔；小孔|A small hole in material, often reinforced with a metal ring.|The shoes have metal eyelets for the laces.  鞋上有用于鞋带的金属眼孔。|近义词: grommet, hole|反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:名词


eyelet:/ˈaɪlɪt/
vt.|给…打孔；给…装眼扣|To reinforce a hole in fabric or other material with a metal ring.|She eyeletted the curtains before hanging them.  她在挂窗帘之前给窗帘打了孔。|近义词: grommet|反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:动词



词源: From "eyelet," a diminutive of "eye," referring to the small, round shape.
