millable:/ˈmɪləbəl/| adj.|可碾磨的，可 milling 的|Suitable for milling or grinding; capable of being milled or ground.  Able to be shaped or processed by a milling machine.|This type of grain is highly millable and produces excellent flour. 这种谷物非常适合碾磨，能生产出优质的面粉.|近义词：grindable, workable, machinable|反义词:unmillable, unworkable|词性:adjective
词源:From mill (n.) + -able.  Ultimately from Latin mola "millstone."
