hedonistic:/ˌhiːdənˈɪstɪk/
adjective
享乐主义的；快乐主义的
engaged in the pursuit of pleasure; sensually self-indulgent.
例句：He lived a hedonistic lifestyle, indulging in every pleasure imaginable.  他过着享乐主义的生活，沉溺于各种可以想象的快乐之中。

例句：The hedonistic philosophy emphasizes the importance of pleasure and happiness.  享乐主义哲学强调快乐和幸福的重要性。

近义词：pleasure-seeking, sybaritic, indulgent, epicurean
反义词：ascetic, self-denying, puritanical
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hēdonikos* "pleasant," from *hēdonē* "pleasure"
