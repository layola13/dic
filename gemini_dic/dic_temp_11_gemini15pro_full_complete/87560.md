heteronymous:/ˌhɛtərˈɒnɪməs/ | adj. | 异名的，名字不同的，词形不同义也不同的 | Having different names, or having a different name from the one given at birth; (of a word) having a different spelling and meaning but the same pronunciation as another word. |  Example 1: The twins were heteronymous, one named Oliver, the other Amelia. 这对双胞胎是异名的，一个叫奥利弗，另一个叫阿米莉亚。

Example 2: "Lead" (the metal) and "lead" (to guide) are heteronymous words. “铅”（金属）和“领导”（引导）是异形异义词。 | Synonyms: different, dissimilar, unrelated, diverse | Antonyms: homonymous, synonymous, identical, same | word property: adjective
