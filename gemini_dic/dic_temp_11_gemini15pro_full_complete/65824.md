evilness:/ˈiːvɪlnəs/| n.|邪恶; 恶毒; 凶恶|The evilness of the dictator's regime was evident in the countless atrocities committed against his own people.  这个独裁者政权的邪恶体现在对人民犯下的无数暴行中。|例句：The evilness of his actions shocked everyone. 他的行为的邪恶震惊了所有人。 The movie portrayed the pure evilness of the villain. 这部电影描绘了反派的纯粹邪恶。|近义词：wickedness, depravity, malevolence, iniquity, villainy|反义词：goodness, virtue, righteousness|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *evelnes*, from Old English *yfelnes*, equivalent to *yfel* (evil) + *-nes* (-ness).  Cognate with German *Übelnis*.
