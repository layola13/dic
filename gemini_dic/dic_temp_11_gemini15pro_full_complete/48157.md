decommercialized:/ˌdiːkəˈmɜːʃəlaɪzd/
adjective
去商业化的；非商业化的
Removed from the influence of commerce; not focused on profit or commercial success.
The festival aimed to create a decommercialized space for artists to share their work.  这个节日旨在为艺术家创造一个去商业化的空间来分享他们的作品。
The community sought to create a decommercialized holiday celebration focused on family and tradition.  这个社区试图创造一个去商业化的节日庆典，专注于家庭和传统。
近义词:non-commercial, non-profit
反义词:commercialized, for-profit
词性:adjective

decommercialize:/ˌdiːkəˈmɜːʃəlaɪz/
verb
去除商业性质；使非商业化
To remove from the influence of commerce; to make less commercial.
They hope to decommercialize Christmas by focusing on its religious significance. 他们希望通过关注圣诞节的宗教意义来去除其商业性质。
The city council voted to decommercialize the park, banning vendors and advertising. 市议会投票决定将公园去商业化，禁止商贩和广告。
近义词:demonetize
反义词:commercialize
词性:verb

decommercialization:/ˌdiːkəˌmɜːʃələˈzeɪʃən/
noun
去商业化
The process of removing something from the influence of commerce.
The decommercialization of education is a complex issue with strong arguments on both sides. 教育的去商业化是一个复杂的问题，双方都有强烈的论点。
The festival's decommercialization led to a more authentic and community-focused event.  这个节日去商业化后，成为了一个更具真实性和社区性的活动。
近义词:demonetization
反义词:commercialization
词性:noun
