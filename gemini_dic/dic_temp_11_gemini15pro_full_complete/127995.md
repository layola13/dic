nephelodometer:/ˌnɛfɪloˈdɒmɪtər/ | n. |浊度计，混浊度计| An instrument for measuring the cloudiness of a liquid or transparent solid, especially the amount of suspended particulate matter in a sample of air. |例句：The nephelodometer measured the density of the fog. 浊度计测量了雾的密度。

例句：Scientists used a nephelodometer to assess air pollution levels. 科学家们使用浊度计来评估空气污染水平。|近义词：turbidimeter |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

nephelometer:/ˌnɛfɪˈlɒmɪtər/ | n. |浊度计| An instrument for measuring the scattering of light by suspended particles in a liquid or gas, used to determine the turbidity of a substance. |例句：The nephelometer showed a high concentration of particles in the water sample. 浊度计显示水样中颗粒物浓度很高。

例句：The lab used a nephelometer to analyze the clarity of the solution. 实验室使用浊度计来分析溶液的透明度。|近义词：turbidimeter |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
