dwarfed:/dwɔːrft/
|v.|使显得矮小；使相形见绌|
|Made to seem small or insignificant in comparison.|
|The skyscrapers dwarfed the surrounding buildings. 这些摩天大楼使周围的建筑物显得矮小。|
|overshadowed, eclipsed, diminished|
|magnified, amplified, enhanced|
|verb|

dwarfed:/dwɔːrft/
|adj.|矮小的；相形见绌的|
|Made to seem small or insignificant in comparison.|
|Next to the giant redwood trees, I felt completely dwarfed. 在巨大的红杉树旁，我感觉自己非常渺小。|
|miniature, tiny, insignificant|
|gigantic, immense, enormous|
|adjective|
