famine:/ˈfæmɪn/| n.|饥荒，饥馑|extreme scarcity of food|The famine caused widespread suffering. 饥荒造成了广泛的苦难。|近义词：starvation, hunger, scarcity|反义词:abundance, plenty|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *famine (Modern French famine), from Latin famēs (“hunger, famine”), related to famulus (“servant, slave”) and possibly to Greek θάνατος (thánatos, “death”).
