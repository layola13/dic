acarina:/ˈækərɪnə/| n. |螨虫；蜱螨亚纲|A subclass of arachnid arthropods that includes mites and ticks.|Mites, like dust mites, belong to the Acari subclass, also known as Acarina.  像尘螨这样的螨虫属于蜱螨亚纲，也称为Acarina。|The acarina population exploded due to the humid conditions. 由于潮湿的环境，螨虫数量激增。|Synonyms: mite, tick|Antonyms: None|Noun
