infernalities:/ˌɪnfɜːrˈnælɪtiz/
n.
负外部性；外部成本；外部不经济
Costs or benefits imposed on others by the actions of an individual or group, but not reflected in market prices.
Pollution from a factory is an example of an externality. 工厂的污染是一个外部性的例子。
近义词:external costs, negative externalities, spillovers
反义词:positive externalities, external benefits
词性:名词
