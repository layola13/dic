apartnesses:/əˈpɑːrtnɪsɪz/
n.
隔离，分离状态 (尤指种族隔离)
The state of being apart; separateness, especially racial segregation.
The system of apartheid enforced racial apartnesses. 种族隔离制度强制实行种族隔离。
segregation, isolation, division
integration, unity, togetherness
noun
