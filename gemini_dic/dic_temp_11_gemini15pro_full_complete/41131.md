copsed:/kɒpst/ | v. | 被砍伐的，矮林作业的 |  Cut or managed as coppice. | The area was traditionally copsed for firewood.  这个地区传统上被砍伐以获取柴火。 |  cut, harvested, pollarded | regrown, untouched | verb
Etymology: From Middle English *copsen, from Old French coper (“to cut”), from Vulgar Latin *cuppare, *colpare, from Latin colaphus (“a blow, cuff”).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek κόλαφος (kólaphos, “a blow”).
