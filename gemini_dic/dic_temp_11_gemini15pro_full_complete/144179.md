outshrilling:/ˌaʊtˈʃrɪlɪŋ/
verb
发出比...更大的喊叫声；盖过...的喊叫声
outdoing in shrilling; shouting louder than
The soprano outshrilled the other singers. 女高音的歌声盖过了其他歌手。
Synonyms: outshouting, overpowering, drowning out
Antonyms: being drowned out, being quieter, being subdued
词性: 动词
