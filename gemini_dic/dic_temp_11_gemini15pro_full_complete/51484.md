detoxing:/diːˈtɒksɪŋ/
verb
解毒，排毒
the process of removing toxic substances or qualities.
She's detoxing from sugar. 她正在戒糖。
近义词: cleansing, purifying
反义词: contaminating, polluting
词性: verb (present participle)


detox:/ˈdiːtɒks/
noun
解毒，排毒
a period of time when you stop taking unhealthy or harmful foods, drinks, or drugs into your body
He went on a detox diet. 他开始了一种排毒饮食。
近义词: cleanse, purification
反义词: contamination, pollution
词性: noun, verb
