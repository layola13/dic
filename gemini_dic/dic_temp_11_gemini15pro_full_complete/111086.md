listlessnesses:/ˈlɪstləsnəsɪz/
n.
无精打采，倦怠，冷漠
A state of lacking energy or enthusiasm.
The long illness resulted in a period of listlessnesses.  长期卧病导致了一段时间的无精打采。
His listlessnesses worried his parents. 他的无精打采让他的父母很担心。
近义词: apathy, lethargy, languor, lassitude, inertia, torpor
反义词: energy, enthusiasm, vigor, vitality, animation
词性: 名词 (plural of listlessness)

词源: From listless + -ness.  Listless from list (desire, inclination) + -less.
