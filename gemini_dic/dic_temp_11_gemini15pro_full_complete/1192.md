acclivitous:/əˈklɪvɪtəs/
adjective
陡峭的，向上倾斜的
Characterized by an upward slope; rising.
The acclivitous terrain made the hike challenging.  陡峭的地形使这次徒步旅行充满挑战。
The road became increasingly acclivitous as we neared the summit.  随着我们接近山顶，道路变得越来越陡峭。
synonyms: steep, uphill, ascending, sloping
antonyms: declivitous, descending, downhill, flat
