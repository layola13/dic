croutons:/ˈkruːtɒnz/ (US) /ˈkruːtɒ̃z/ (UK)| n. |油煎面包块，烤面包丁|Small cubes of fried or toasted bread, often served in salads or soups.|例句：The salad was topped with crunchy croutons. 沙拉上面撒了脆脆的烤面包丁。

例句：She added croutons to her soup for extra texture. 她为了增加口感在汤里加了面包丁。|近义词：bread cubes, toast cubes|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "soft bread")|词性:noun
