hypermorality:/ˌhaɪpərmoʊˈrælɪti/
noun
极端道德主义; 过度道德化
An excessive concern with morals, often to the point of being overly critical or judgmental.  A state of exaggerated moral scruples.
She accused the media of hypermorality, claiming they overemphasized minor infractions. 她指责媒体道德主义泛滥，称他们过分强调了轻微的违规行为。
近义词:rigidity, puritanism, moralism
反义词:immorality, amorality
