overdescriptiveness:/ˌoʊvər.dɪˈskrɪp.tɪv.nəs/
|n.| 过度描述; 过于详尽的描述 |The overdescriptiveness of the passage made it tedious to read.  这段文字描述过于详尽，读起来很乏味。| English Explanation: The quality of being excessively descriptive; using too many words or details to describe something. | Example: The overdescriptiveness of the witness's account made the lawyer suspicious. 证人描述的过于详尽让律师起了疑心。| Synonyms: verbosity, prolixity, wordiness | Antonyms: conciseness, brevity, succinctness | noun
