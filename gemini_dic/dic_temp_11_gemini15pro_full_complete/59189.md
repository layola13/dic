dysodile:/ˈdɪsəˌdaɪl/| n. |泥炭土，页岩，臭泥煤|A kind of earthy or shaly coal, often laminated and more or less bituminous and elastic, burning with a disagreeable odor.|The dysodile burned with a thick, pungent smoke. 这块泥炭土燃烧时冒出浓烈刺鼻的烟雾。|Synonyms: boghead coal, torbanite, shale|Antonyms: anthracite (无烟煤)|noun
