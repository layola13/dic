attemptless:/əˈtɛmptlɪs/| adj.|未尝试的，没有尝试的|Lacking any attempt; not attempted.  Characterized by a lack of effort or initiative.|He made an attemptless shot at the basket, barely even looking at the hoop.|Near synonyms: effortless (though this implies ease, not lack of trying), half-hearted, perfunctory, unmotivated, lackadaisical|Near antonyms: ambitious, determined, persistent, striving, vigorous|adjective
