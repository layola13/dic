dissertationists:/ˌdɪsəˈteɪʃənɪsts/
n.
论文作者；学位论文撰写人
A person who writes a dissertation, especially as part of the requirements for a doctoral degree.
Many dissertationists struggle with writer's block. 许多学位论文撰写人都会与写作障碍作斗争。
The dissertationists presented their findings at the conference. 论文作者们在会议上展示了他们的研究结果。
Synonyms: doctoral candidates, PhD students, researchers
Antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct opposite)
