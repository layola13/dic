disrobement:/dɪsˈroʊbmənt/
n.
脱衣，脱掉礼服;卸下盛装
the act of taking off one's clothes, especially ceremonial robes or garments.
The king's disrobement before the court was a symbolic act of humility. 国王在朝廷上的脱衣仪式是谦卑的象征性举动。

近义词:undressing, stripping
反义词:dressing, robing
词性:名词
