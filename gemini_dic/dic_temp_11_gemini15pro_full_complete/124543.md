mountebank:/ˈmaʊntɪˌbæŋk/| n.|江湖骗子；吹牛大王|A person who deceives others by trickery or pretensions; a charlatan.|He was a mountebank, selling fake cures to desperate people. 他是一个江湖骗子，向绝望的人兜售假药。|Synonyms: charlatan, swindler, fraud, imposter, quack|Antonyms: honest person, truthful person|noun
mountebank:/ˈmaʊntɪˌbæŋk/| n.|江湖郎中；卖假药的|A person who sells quack medicines, typically from a platform in a public place.|The mountebank promised his elixir would cure all ills. 这个江湖郎中承诺他的灵丹妙药可以治愈所有疾病。|Synonyms: quack, charlatan, fraud|Antonyms: doctor, physician|noun
