outpasses:/ˈaʊtˌpæsɪz/
n.
通行证，外出许可证
A document or permit allowing someone to leave a restricted area.
The prisoners were given outpasses to visit their families.  囚犯们获得了外出许可证去看望他们的家人。
permits, passes
restrictions, confinements
noun
