kibed:/kaɪbd/
adjective
冻疮的；皲裂的
Having chilblains, small, itchy swellings on the skin that occur as a reaction to cold temperatures.
My fingers got kibed after playing in the snow without gloves. 我的手指在没有戴手套的情况下玩雪后长了冻疮。
近义词: chapped, frostbitten
反义词: healthy, warm
