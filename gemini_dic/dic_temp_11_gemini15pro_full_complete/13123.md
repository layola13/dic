astuteness:/əˈstjuːtnəs/
n.
精明; 敏锐; 狡猾
The quality of being astute, or having an ability to assess situations or people accurately.
Her astuteness in business matters is admirable.  她在商业事务上的精明令人钦佩。
Synonyms: shrewdness, sharpness, acumen, perspicacity, insight
Antonyms: naiveté, gullibility, obtuseness
