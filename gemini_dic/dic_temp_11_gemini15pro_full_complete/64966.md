estimable:/ˈestɪməbl/| adj.|值得尊敬的，可估计的|Deserving of respect and admiration; capable of being estimated.|She is an estimable woman with high moral standards. 她是一位值得尊敬的女性，拥有很高的道德标准。|The damage caused by the storm is estimable. 暴风雨造成的损害是可以估算的。|近义词：admirable, respectable, calculable, assessable|反义词：contemptible, despicable, incalculable|词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin ōestimabilis, from Latin aestīmō (“I estimate, value”) + -bilis.
