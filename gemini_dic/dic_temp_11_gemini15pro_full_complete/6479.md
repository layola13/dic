amputating: /ˈæmpjʊteɪtɪŋ/
verb
截肢; 切断
The surgeon is amputating the patient's leg. 外科医生正在截除病人的腿。
Present participle of amputate.  是动词amputate的现在分词形式。
The doctor was amputating the soldier's arm. 医生正在截掉士兵的胳膊。
Synonyms: severing, excising, removing
Antonyms: attaching, joining, grafting
v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin amputare, from Latin amb- (“around”) + putare (“to prune, to cut”).
