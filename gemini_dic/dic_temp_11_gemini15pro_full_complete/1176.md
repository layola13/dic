acclimating:/əˈklaɪmeɪtɪŋ/
verb
适应环境; 驯化;
The process of becoming accustomed to a new climate or environment.
They are acclimating to the hot weather.  他们正在适应炎热的天气。
accustoming, adapting, adjusting
disadjusting

acclimate:/əˈklaɪmeɪt/
verb
使适应环境; 使驯化;
To make or become accustomed to a new climate or environment.
The climbers spent a few days acclimating to the altitude. 登山者花了几天时间来适应海拔高度。
adapt, adjust, accustom
disadjust

词性: verb (动词)
