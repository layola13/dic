diaspore:/ˈdaɪəˌspɔːr/ | n. |  硬水铝矿; 离散人群 | A mineral consisting of aluminium hydroxide, AlO(OH), occurring in prismatic orthorhombic crystals. Also: a variety of this mineral, used as a gemstone.  A group of people who have spread or been dispersed from their homeland. | The diaspore deposits are located in the eastern part of the region. 该地区的硬水铝矿矿床位于东部。

The Jewish diaspora has communities all over the world. 犹太人散居在世界各地。 | 近义词:emigrant, exile, refugee (for the people sense); boehmite (for the mineral sense) | 反义词:native, indigenous (for the people sense)| 词性:noun


diaspora:/daɪˈæspərə/ | n. |  离散，散居 | The dispersion or spread of something that was originally localized.  Specifically (often initial capital): the dispersion of the Jews beyond Israel. |  The diaspora of African cultures has enriched many nations. 非洲文化的传播丰富了许多国家。
