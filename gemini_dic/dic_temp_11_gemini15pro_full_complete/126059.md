mustard:/ˈmʌstərd/ (US) /ˈmʌstəd/ (UK)| n. adj.|芥末；芥末黄；辛辣调味品|A pungent-tasting yellow or brown paste made from the crushed seeds of certain mustard plants, typically eaten with meat or used as a cooking ingredient.  Also, a dark yellow color. |例句：I like my hotdog with plenty of mustard. 我喜欢我的热狗加很多芥末。|近义词：condiment, spice|反义词: (As a condiment, no direct opposite) |词性:noun, adjective

mustards:/ˈmʌstərdz/ (US) /ˈmʌstədz/ (UK)| n. |芥末（复数）；各种芥末酱|Plural form of mustard, referring to different varieties of mustard preparations. |例句：The deli offered a selection of mustards.  熟食店提供各种芥末酱。|近义词：condiments, spices|反义词: (As condiments, no direct opposites) |词性:noun
