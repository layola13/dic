manipulatable:/məˈnɪpjʊlətəbl/
adjective
可操纵的，易受摆布的
able to be manipulated or controlled by someone.
The software is very manipulatable, allowing users to customize it to their needs.  这个软件非常容易操作，允许用户根据自己的需要进行定制。
The results of the study were easily manipulatable, leading to questions about its validity.  这项研究的结果很容易被操纵，导致人们对其有效性产生质疑。
近义词: controllable, manageable, pliable, influencable
反义词: unmanageable, intractable, resistant, inflexible
词性: adjective

manipulate:/məˈnɪpjʊleɪt/
verb
操纵，控制
handle or control (a tool, mechanism, etc.), typically in a skillful manner.
He manipulated the controls of the aircraft with ease. 他熟练地操纵着飞机的控制装置。
She manipulated the data to support her hypothesis. 她操纵数据来支持她的假设。
近义词: control, handle, operate, manage
反义词: leave alone, neglect


词源:
From Late Latin *manipulatus, past participle of manipulare (“to handle”), from Latin manipulus (“handful, bundle, company of soldiers”) + -able.
