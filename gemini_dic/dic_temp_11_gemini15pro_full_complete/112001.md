loners:/ˈloʊnərz/ (US), /ˈləʊnəz/ (UK)| n. |独来独往的人；不合群的人|A person who prefers to be alone and avoids the company of others.  A person who is not part of a group.|He was always a bit of a loner, preferring his own company to that of others. 他总是有点不合群，喜欢独处而不是与他人相处。|

The artist was a loner, finding inspiration in solitude. 这位艺术家是个独行侠，在孤独中寻找灵感。|

近义词：solitary, introvert, recluse|反义词：extrovert, social butterfly|词性: noun (plural)
