indaba:/ɪnˈdɑːbə/
noun
会议，讨论，商议
A conference or discussion, especially on important matters, typically involving community leaders in Southern Africa.
The village elders held an indaba to discuss the drought. 村里的长老们举行了一次会议来讨论旱情。
conference, meeting, discussion, council, assembly
argument, disagreement
