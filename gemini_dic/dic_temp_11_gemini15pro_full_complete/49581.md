demerge:/diːˈmɜːrdʒ/ | v. | 分拆，拆分（公司）| To separate one part of a company from the rest. | The company was demerged into three separate entities.  该公司被拆分为三个独立的实体。| The telecommunications division was demerged from the parent company.  电信部门从母公司中拆分出来。 | 近义词:  separate, divide, split, divest | 反义词: merge, combine, consolidate | 词性: 动词

Etymology:  "de-" (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + "merge" (meaning "combine").
