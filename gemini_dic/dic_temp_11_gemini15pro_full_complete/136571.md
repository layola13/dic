nonsalubriously:/ˌnɒnsæˈljuːbriəsli/ /ˌnɑːnsæˈljuːbriəsli/
adverb
不利于健康地；有害健康地
in a way that is not healthy or pleasant
The prisoners were kept nonsalubriously in overcrowded cells.  囚犯们被不健康地关押在过度拥挤的牢房里。
The stagnant water lay nonsalubriously in the ditches. 这些污水停滞不前地躺在沟渠里，对健康有害。
近义词: unhealthily, insalubriously, deleteriously
反义词: healthily, salubriously, beneficially
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- + salubriously.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "salubriously" is an adverb meaning "in a healthy way." The root word is "salubrious," which comes from the Latin word "salubris," meaning "healthful."
