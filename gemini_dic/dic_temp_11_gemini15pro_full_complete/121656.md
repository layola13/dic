misphrasing:/ˌmɪsˈfreɪzɪŋ/
noun
措辞错误，表达失当
The lawyer's misphrasing of the question confused the witness.  律师措辞不当的问题让证人感到困惑。
Synonyms: misstatement, miswording, faulty phrasing, inaccurate expression, verbal slip
Antonyms: accurate phrasing, correct wording, precise expression
n.

misphrase:/ˌmɪsˈfreɪz/
verb
措辞错误，表达失当
I misphrased my request and accidentally offended her. 我措辞不当的请求无意中冒犯了她。
Synonyms: misstate, misword, express poorly, phrase incorrectly
Antonyms: phrase correctly, word accurately, express clearly
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + phrase (to express in words).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-", meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss." "Phrase" comes from Late Latin "phrasis," from Greek "phrasis" meaning "diction, style of speech," from "phrazein" meaning "to tell, declare."
