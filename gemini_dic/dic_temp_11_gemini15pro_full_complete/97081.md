inferrible:/ɪnˈfɜːrəbl/ /ɪnˈfərəbl/
adjective
可推断的;能推理的
capable of being inferred
Her guilt is inferrible from the evidence.  她的罪行可以从证据中推断出来。
inferable, deducible, deductive
--
adjective

中文翻译：可推断的；可推论的

英文解析：Capable of being inferred; deducible.  Something that is inferrible can be concluded based on reasoning and evidence.

例句：

His displeasure was inferrible from his silence. 他很不高兴，这可以从他的沉默中推断出来。


The conclusion is readily inferrible from the premises.  结论很容易从前提中推断出来。



近义词：deducible, derivable, inferable, gatherable

反义词：uninferable, nondeductible

词源：Late 16th century: from late Latin inferribilis, from inferre ‘bring in, infer’ (see infer).
