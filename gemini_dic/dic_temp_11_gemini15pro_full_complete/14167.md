autistic: /ɔːˈtɪstɪk/ | adj. | 自闭的，患自闭症的 | Affected by autism, a developmental disorder that impairs communication and social interaction. |  Example 1: He is an autistic child who needs special care. 他是一个需要特殊照顾的自闭症儿童。

Example 2:  Autistic people may have difficulty understanding social cues. 自闭症患者可能难以理解社交暗示。 | Near Synonyms:  on the spectrum, neurodivergent | Antonyms: neurotypical | Word Type: Adjective

autism: /ˈɔːtɪzəm/ | n. | 自闭症 | A developmental disorder of variable severity that is characterized by difficulties in social interaction and communication and by restricted or repetitive patterns of thought and behavior. | Example 1:  Autism is often diagnosed in early childhood. 自闭症通常在儿童早期被诊断出来。

Example 2:  There is growing awareness about autism and its impact on individuals and families. 人们对自闭症及其对个人和家庭的影响的认识日益增强。 | Near Synonyms:  autistic spectrum disorder (ASD) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | Word Type: Noun
