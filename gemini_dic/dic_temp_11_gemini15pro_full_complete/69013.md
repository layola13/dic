fatteners:/ˈfætənərz/
n.|增肥物；催肥剂|Substances used to promote weight gain, typically in livestock.|Farmers use various fatteners to prepare their animals for market.  农民们使用各种增肥剂来使他们的牲畜上市。|The company produces a range of fatteners for poultry. 这家公司生产一系列的家禽增肥剂。|synonyms:  growth promoters, supplements|antonyms:  diet aids, appetite suppressants
词性: 名词 (plural)
