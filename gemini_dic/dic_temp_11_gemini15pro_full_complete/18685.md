bequest:/bɪˈkwest/| n.|遗产，遗赠物|Something, especially personal property, left to someone in a will.|She left a bequest of $10,000 to her niece. 她留给侄女一万美元的遗产。|Synonyms: legacy, inheritance, endowment|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "disinheritance")|noun
