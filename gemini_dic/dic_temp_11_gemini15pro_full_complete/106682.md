如果想了解英文中"what"的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) |pron. det. adv.| 什么，多么 |

中文翻译:  什么，多么，何等

英文解析:  Used to ask for information specifying something.  Can be used as a pronoun, determiner, or adverb.

例句：

What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What do you want for dinner? = 你晚餐想吃什么？


近义词：which, how

反义词:  N/A (What doesn't have a direct antonym)

词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb
