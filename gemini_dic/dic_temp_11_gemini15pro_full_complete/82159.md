grooved:/ɡruːvd/| adj.|有凹槽的，开槽的；(非正式)习惯于…的，适应了的|Having a groove or grooves; (informal) comfortable and familiar with something.|The record was badly grooved and skipped. 这张唱片槽刻得很差，会跳针。|近义词：furrowed, channeled, rutted, settled, established|反义词：smooth, flat|词性:形容词

grooved:/ɡruːvd/| v.|开槽于，刻槽于（groove的过去式和过去分词）|(past tense and past participle of groove) to make a groove in something.|He grooved the wood for the inlay. 他在木头上刻槽以便镶嵌。|近义词：furrowed, channeled, rutted|反义词：smoothed, flattened|词性:动词

词源: From groove + -ed.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grubaną ("to dig").
