demi-culverin:/ˌdɛmiˈkʌlvərɪn/| n.|半加农炮|A type of cannon, smaller than a culverin, used from the 16th to 18th centuries.  It fired a ball weighing about 9 pounds.|The demi-culverin was effective against fortifications but less so against ships.|Synonym:cannon, artillery piece|Antonym:N/A|noun

demi-culverin:/ˌdɛmiˈkʌlvərɪn/| n.|半蛇炮|一种比蛇炮小的加农炮，用于16世纪到18世纪。它发射的炮弹重约9磅。|半蛇炮对防御工事有效，但对船只效果较差。|近义词：加农炮，火炮|反义词：无|名词
