cryingly:/ˈkraɪɪŋli/| adv.|哭着地，泪汪汪地|in a way that shows that you are crying or about to cry|例句：She looked at him cryingly. 她泪眼汪汪地看着他。|例句：He spoke cryingly about his lost dog. 他哭诉着丢失的爱犬。|近义词：tearfully, weepingly, sobbing|反义词：smilingly, laughingly|词性:副词
cry:/kraɪ/| v. n.|哭，喊，叫；哭泣，叫喊|from Old French crier "to shout, cry, proclaim," from Latin quiritare "to scream, shriek," originally "to call upon the Quirites for help," from Quirites, plural of Quiris "Roman citizen"|词性:动词/名词
