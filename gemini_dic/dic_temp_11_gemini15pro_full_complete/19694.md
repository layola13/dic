bilharzia:/ˌbɪlˈhɑːrziə/ (US) /ˌbɪlˈhɑːʒə/ (UK)| n. |血吸虫病|A tropical disease caused by parasitic flatworms called schistosomes, contracted through contact with contaminated fresh water.  It is characterized by inflammation of the urinary tract or intestines.|例句：Bilharzia is a serious health problem in many developing countries. 血吸虫病在许多发展中国家是一个严重的健康问题。

例句：He contracted bilharzia while swimming in the lake. 他在湖里游泳时感染了血吸虫病。|近义词：schistosomiasis|反义词: (None - diseases don't typically have antonyms)|词性:noun
