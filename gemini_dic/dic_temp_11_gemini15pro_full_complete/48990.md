deglazes:/diːˈɡleɪzɪz/
verb
给…去釉；(烹饪)将煎锅(或烤盘)内的食物碎屑溶解于液体中制成调味汁
To remove glaze from; (Cooking) To loosen the browned bits from a pan by adding liquid.
After searing the steak, she deglazed the pan with red wine to make a rich sauce.  煎完牛排后，她用红酒给锅去釉，做成浓郁的酱汁。
dissolves, loosens
coats, glazes
v.
