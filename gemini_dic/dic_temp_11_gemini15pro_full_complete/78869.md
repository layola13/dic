gingkoes: /ˈɡɪŋkoʊz/ | n. |银杏树（复数）|The plural form of "gingko," referring to multiple gingko trees.  Gingkoes are known for their unique fan-shaped leaves and resistance to pollution.|例句：The gingkoes in the park turn a beautiful golden color in the fall. 公园里的银杏树在秋天变成了美丽的金色。

例句：Several gingkoes were planted along the street.  沿着街道种植了几棵银杏树。
