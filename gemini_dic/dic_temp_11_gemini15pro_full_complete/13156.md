asyndetic:/ˌeɪsɪnˈdɛtɪk/
adjective
无连词的；连接词省略的
Characterized by asyndeton (the omission of conjunctions).
He spoke in short, asyndetic sentences. 他说话简短，省略连接词。

She delivered a powerful, asyndetic list of grievances. 她一口气列举了种种委屈，中间没有使用任何连接词。

Near synonyms:  unconnected, disjointed
Antonyms:  syndetic, connected
词性: adjective
