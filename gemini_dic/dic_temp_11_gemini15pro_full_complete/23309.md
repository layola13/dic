boyce: /bɔɪs/ | n. | 男孩; (尤指)仆人,男服务员 | A boyce is a male servant, especially a young one.  It's an archaic word, rarely used in modern English. |  In medieval times, the lord of the manor had several boyces attending to his needs. 在中世纪时期，庄园的领主有几个男仆照顾他的需要。|  The boyce brought a tankard of ale to the table. 男仆端来了一大杯麦芽酒到桌上。 | synonyms: servant, attendant, valet, footman | antonyms: master, lord, employer | noun
