flacking:/ˈflækɪŋ/ | n. |公关宣传，危机公关；严厉批评，抨击 |The act of providing publicity or acting as a publicist, especially in dealing with the media; harsh criticism or censure. | The company hired a PR firm for flacking after the product recall.  这家公司在产品召回后聘请了一家公关公司进行危机公关。| 近义词: publicizing, promoting, defending, shielding, criticizing, attacking | 反义词: concealing, suppressing | 词性: 名词 (derived from flack)

flacking:/ˈflækɪŋ/ | v. |做公关宣传；严厉批评，抨击 |Acting as a publicist; criticizing harshly. |  She's been flacking for the new movie. 她一直在为这部新电影做宣传。| 近义词: publicizing, promoting, defending, shielding, criticizing, attacking | 反义词: concealing, suppressing | 词性: 动词 (derived from flack)
