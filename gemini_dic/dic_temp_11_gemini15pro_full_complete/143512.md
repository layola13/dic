outgoing:/ˈaʊtɡoʊɪŋz/ (US) /ˌaʊtˈɡəʊɪŋz/ (UK)| n. (plural)|支出；流出物|amounts of money spent; things that flow out|The company's outgoings exceeded its income.  公司的支出超过了收入。|synonyms: expenditure, expenses|antonyms: income, revenue|noun
