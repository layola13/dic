apres-ski:/ˌæpreɪ ˈskiː/ /ˌɑːpreɪ ˈskiː/
noun
滑雪后的社交活动
Social activities and entertainment following a day's skiing.
例句：The resort is famous for its lively apres-ski.  这个度假村以其热闹的滑雪后活动而闻名。

例句：We enjoyed some mulled wine at the apres-ski bar. 我们在滑雪后的酒吧里享用了一些热红酒。
近义词：after-ski
反义词：(无)
词性:n.

etymology: From French, literally "after ski".
