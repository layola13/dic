disbark:/dɪsˈbɑːrk/
verb
下船;登陆
To go ashore from a ship.
The passengers were instructed to disbark at the next port.  乘客们被指示在下一个港口下船。
The troops disbarked onto the beach.  部队在海滩登陆。
disembark, land, go ashore
