favicon:/ˈfeɪvɪˌkɒn/ (US) /ˈfeɪvɪkən/ (UK)| n. |网站图标，收藏夹图标|A small icon associated with a particular website or web page, typically displayed in the browser's address bar, tabs, and bookmarks.|Example: The favicon for this website is a blue circle. 这个网站的favicon是一个蓝色圆圈。|Example:  Make sure your favicon is easily recognizable and represents your brand. 确保你的网站图标易于识别并代表你的品牌。|Synonym: website icon, bookmark icon|Antonym: N/A|Noun
