ordinal:/ˈɔːrdɪnl/ | adj. n.| 序数的，顺序的；序数 |  Relating to order or position in a series; a number that tells the position of something in a list, such as first, second, third. | The ordinal number for 31 is thirty-first.  31的序数词是三十一。|  The books are arranged in ordinal sequence. 这些书是按顺序排列的。| synonym: ranking, sequenced | antonym: cardinal | adjective, noun
