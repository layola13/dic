countries:/ˈkʌntriz/ | n. | 国家（复数）|  A plural form of "country," referring to multiple nations or regions. | Many countries participated in the climate summit. 许多国家参加了气候峰会。| synonyms: nations, states, territories | antonyms: (No direct antonym for a plural noun.  Consider "single state/nation" for contrast.) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "cuntrée" (modern French "contrée"), ultimately from Latin "contra" (against, opposite), indicating land "lying opposite" or "spread out before one."  Evolved to denote a region, then a nation.
