flowery:/ˈflaʊəri/ (US), /ˈflaʊəri/ (UK)| adj.|华丽的，花哨的；多花的|Full of flowers or decorated with flowers; excessively ornate or elaborate in style or language.|The writer's flowery language made the simple story seem overly complicated.  作者华丽的辞藻使得这个简单的故事显得过于复杂。|Synonyms: ornate, elaborate, embellished, flamboyant, ostentatious|Antonyms: plain, simple, unadorned, austere|adjective
