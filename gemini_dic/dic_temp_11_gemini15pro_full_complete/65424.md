euphoric:/juːˈfɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 欣快的；狂喜的 | Feeling or expressing intense excitement and happiness. | She was euphoric after winning the lottery. 她中了彩票后欣喜若狂。|  I felt euphoric as I crossed the finish line. 当我冲过终点线时，我感到欣喜若狂。 | 近义词: elated, overjoyed, thrilled, ecstatic | 反义词: depressed, despondent, miserable | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *euphoros* "bearing well, healthy," from *eu-* "well" + *pherein* "to bear."
