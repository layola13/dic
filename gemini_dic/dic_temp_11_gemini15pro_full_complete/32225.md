chichier:/ˈʃiːʃɪər/ | adj. | 更 شیک的，更时髦的 | More chic, more stylish; more elegantly and stylishly fashionable. |  She wore a chichier dress than usual. 她穿了一件比平时更时髦的裙子。 | Synonyms: more stylish, more fashionable, trendier, smarter | Antonyms: less stylish, less fashionable, dowdy, frumpy | adjective

chichi:/ˈʃiːʃiː/ | adj. |  别致的; 花哨的；过分装饰的；俗丽的；装腔作势的 | Chic, stylish, but sometimes overly elaborate or fussy.
