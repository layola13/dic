mesencephalon:/ˌmɛsɛnˈsɛfəlɒn/ /ˌmɪdˌbrān/| n. |中脑|The mesencephalon, or midbrain, is the portion of the brainstem that connects the hindbrain and the forebrain. It plays important roles in motor movement, particularly eye movements, and in auditory and visual processing.|The patient presented with a lesion in the mesencephalon.  病人中脑出现损伤。|Synonym: midbrain|Antonym: N/A|noun
