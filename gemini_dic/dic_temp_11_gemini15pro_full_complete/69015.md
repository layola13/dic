fattenings:/ˈfætənɪŋz/
n.
肥育; 养肥; 肥育期
The process of making animals fatter, usually for slaughter.  
The farmer invested heavily in fattenings for his cattle.  农民在他的牛的肥育上投入了大量资金。

近义词: feeding, enriching, fattening up
反义词: slimming, dieting, weight loss
词性: 名词 (Noun)


Etymology:
From the verb "fatten," which comes from the Old English word "fættan," meaning "to make fat."  The "-ing" suffix turns it into a noun representing the action or process.
