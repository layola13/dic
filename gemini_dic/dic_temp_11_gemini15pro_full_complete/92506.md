hypothesize:/haɪˈpɒθəsaɪz/ (UK) /haɪˈpɑːθəsaɪz/ (US)| v.|假设，假定|to suggest a possible explanation for something, but without knowing whether it is really true|Scientists hypothesize that the universe began with a big bang. 科学家们假设宇宙起源于一次大爆炸。|Synonyms: postulate, theorize, conjecture, speculate|Antonyms: prove, demonstrate|verb
hypotheses:/haɪˈpɒθəsiːz/ (UK) /haɪˈpɑːθəsiːz/ (US)| n.|假设，假说|a supposition or proposed explanation made on the basis of limited evidence as a starting point for further investigation.|Several hypotheses for global warming have been suggested.  关于全球变暖已经提出了几种假设。|Synonyms: theory, supposition, conjecture, speculation|Antonyms: proof, fact|noun
