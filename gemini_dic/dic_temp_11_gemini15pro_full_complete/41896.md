cosine:/ˈkəʊsaɪn/ (UK) /ˈkoʊsaɪn/ (US)| n.|余弦|A trigonometric function that is equal to the ratio of the side adjacent to an acute angle (in a right-angled triangle) to the hypotenuse.|The cosine of 30 degrees is 0.866.|近义词: (None - cosine is a specific mathematical function)|反义词: (None - cosine is a specific mathematical function)|词性:noun
