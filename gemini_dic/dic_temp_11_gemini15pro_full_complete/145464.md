overclaiming:/ˌoʊvərˈkleɪmɪŋ/
n.|过分宣称，夸大其词|The act of claiming more than is true or justified.  Exaggerating one's accomplishments, contributions, or entitlements.|He was accused of overclaiming his expenses. 他被指控虚报费用。|近义词：exaggerating, embellishing, inflating, overstating|反义词：understating, downplaying|词性: 名词 (gerund or verbal noun derived from the verb "overclaim")
overclaim:/ˌoʊvərˈkleɪm/
v.|过分宣称，夸大|To claim more than is true or justified.|He overclaimed his travel expenses. 他夸大了他的旅行费用。|近义词：exaggerate, embellish, inflate, overstate|反义词：understate, downplay|词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, too much") + "claim" (from Old French "clamer" meaning "to call, cry out, proclaim").
