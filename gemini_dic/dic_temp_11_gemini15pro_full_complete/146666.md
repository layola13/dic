overhaughty: /ˌoʊvərˈhɔːti/ | adj. | 过分傲慢的，极其自负的 | Excessively proud and arrogant; disdainful. | He gave an overhaughty sniff and turned away. 他傲慢地哼了一声，转过身去。|  近义词: arrogant, supercilious, disdainful, haughty, presumptuous | 反义词: humble, modest, unassuming | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *overhauty, from over- + haughty, from Anglo-Norman hautein, from Old French hautain, from haut “high” (from Latin altus) + -ain.
