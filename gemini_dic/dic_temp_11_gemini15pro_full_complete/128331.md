neuterers:/ˈnjuːtərərz/
| n. |绝育者，阉割者| People who neuter animals, especially pets.  Those who perform the act of neutering. |例句：The neuterers were busy at the clinic today. 今天诊所的绝育人员很忙。|例句：Many responsible pet owners take their animals to neuterers. 许多负责任的宠物主人会带他们的动物去找绝育人员。|近义词：castrators, sterilizers|反义词:None (The opposite action would be breeders, but not directly applicable to the people performing the procedure)|词性:Noun
