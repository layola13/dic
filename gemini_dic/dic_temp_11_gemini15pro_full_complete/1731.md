acquaintednesses:/əˈkweɪntədnəsɪz/
n.
熟人;相识的人;认识;了解
The state of being acquainted; personal knowledge as a result of study, experience, etc.  A person with whom one is acquainted.
I renewed my acquaintance with him after many years. 多年之后我又和他重新认识了。
She has a wide circle of acquaintances. 她有一个很大的熟人圈。
synonyms:  acquaintanceship, familiarity, knowledge,  contacts, associates, connections
antonyms: unfamiliarity, ignorance, strangers, enemies
noun
