forgather:/fɔːrˈɡæðər/
verb
聚集，集合
to come together; assemble or meet
例句：Friends and family forgathered for the holiday celebrations.  亲朋好友聚集在一起庆祝节日。

例句：The club members forgather every Thursday evening. 俱乐部成员每周四晚上聚会。
近义词：assemble, congregate, gather, meet
反义词：disperse, scatter
词性：v.
