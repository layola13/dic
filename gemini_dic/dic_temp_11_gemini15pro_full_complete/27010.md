calculations:/ˌkælkjʊˈleɪʃənz/| n. |计算; 测算; 估计; 推测|The process of using mathematics to determine a number, amount, or value.  It can also refer to the result of this process.  |The architect made complex calculations to ensure the building's structural integrity. 建筑师进行了复杂的计算以确保建筑物的结构完整性。

The calculations showed a significant increase in profits. 计算结果显示利润大幅增长。|近义词：computation, estimation, reckoning, figuring|反义词:guess, conjecture|noun
