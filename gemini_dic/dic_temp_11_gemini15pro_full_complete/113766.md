macropodian:/ˌmækrəˈpoʊdiən/ | adj. n. |  长足的；长足动物 | Relating to or resembling a macropodian; a macropodian animal. |  Example: Kangaroos are macropodian. 袋鼠是长足动物。 | Synonym: megapod, long-legged | Antonym: short-legged | adjective, noun
