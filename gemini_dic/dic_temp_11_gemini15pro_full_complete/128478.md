newspaper:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpər/ (US), /ˈnjuːsˌpeɪpə/ (UK)| n. |报纸|A printed publication consisting of folded sheets of paper that contain news, articles, advertisements, etc., and is issued regularly.|I read the newspaper every morning to stay informed. 我每天早上读报纸以了解时事。|近义词：paper, journal, gazette|反义词: None|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "news" + "paper."  "News" itself comes from Middle English "newes," plural of "newe," meaning "new thing."  "Paper" originates from Middle English "papir," from Old French "papier," ultimately from Latin "papyrus," referring to the ancient Egyptian writing material.
