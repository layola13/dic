basinal:/ˈbeɪsɪnl/| adj.|盆地的，流域的|Relating to or characteristic of a basin, especially a geological basin.|The basinal sediments contained numerous fossils. 这些盆地沉积物包含大量的化石。|近义词:basin-shaped, depressed|反义词:uplifted, elevated|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From basin + -al.  "Basin" comes from Old French "bacin" and ultimately from Late Latin "baccinus," meaning "basin, bowl."
