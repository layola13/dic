forgoes:/fɔːrˈɡoʊz/
verb
放弃，抛弃
to do without; abstain from; give up
She forgoes dessert to maintain her weight. 她为了保持体重而放弃甜点。
近义词：relinquishes, abandons, sacrifices, waives
反义词：indulges in, embraces, accepts, keeps
词性：动词
