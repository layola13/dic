noncastigating:/ˌnɒnkæˈstɪɡeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不责备的，不惩罚的
Not criticizing or punishing someone for their actions or behavior.
She offered noncastigating advice, focusing on solutions rather than blame. 她提供了一些不带责备意味的建议，侧重于解决问题而不是追究责任。

The therapist provided a noncastigating environment for her patients to share their struggles.  这位治疗师为她的病人提供了一个不带责备意味的环境，让他们可以分享他们的困境。
