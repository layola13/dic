demit:/dɪˈmɪt/
verb
辞职; 放弃(职位、议员席位等)
resign from (a position, especially a parliamentary seat).
He was forced to demit office. 他被迫辞职。
近义词:resign, relinquish, renounce, quit, step down, stand down, bow out, leave office, give up office
反义词:assume, undertake, take on
词性:v.

demit:/dɪˈmɪt/
adjective
(尤指窗扇、拱顶)半截的，半圆的
(especially of a window or vault) forming a half arch.
A demit arch. 半截拱顶。
近义词:half, semi-
反义词:full, whole
词性:adj.
