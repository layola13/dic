androphagi:/ænˈdrɒfədʒaɪ/
n.
食人族，食人者
People who eat human flesh; cannibals.
Herodotus described the Androphagi as a savage tribe.  希罗多德将安德罗法吉人描述为野蛮的部落。
The androphagi were feared by all the surrounding tribes. 食人族令周围所有部落都感到恐惧。
synonyms: cannibals, man-eaters
antonyms: vegetarians, herbivores
noun
