overfiltering:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪltərɪŋ/ |n./v. (present participle)|过度过滤|The excessive use of filters, especially in photography or image processing, resulting in a loss of detail or an unnatural appearance.|例句：Overfiltering can make photos look artificial. 过度过滤会使照片看起来很假。|例句：She's overfiltering her selfies so much that they barely look like her anymore. 她过度美颜自拍，以至于看起来都不像她了。|近义词：overprocessing, excessive filtering|反义词: underfiltering |noun/verb

overfiltering:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪltərɪŋ/ |adj.|过度过滤的|Characterized by excessive filtering, resulting in a loss of detail or an unnatural appearance.|例句：The overfiltered image lacked depth and texture. 这张过度过滤的图像缺乏深度和质感。|例句：His Instagram feed is full of overfiltered photos of his food. 他的Instagram动态充满了过度滤镜的美食照片。|近义词：overprocessed|反义词: underfiltered |adjective
