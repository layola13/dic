facundity:/fəˈkʌndɪti/
n.
流利；雄辩；多产；丰饶
the quality of being able to speak easily and well; the ability to produce a lot of something
例句：He was known for his facundity and wit. 他以其口才和机智而闻名。

例句：The facundity of the novelist is impressive; she has published 15 novels in 20 years.  这位小说家的多产令人印象深刻；她在20年里出版了15部小说。

近义词：eloquence, fluency, prolificness, abundance, fertility
反义词：inarticulacy, taciturnity, barrenness, sterility
词性: noun
