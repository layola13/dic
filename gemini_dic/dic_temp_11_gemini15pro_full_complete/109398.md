lentisk: /ˈlɛntɪsk/ | n. | 乳香黄连木，玛蹄脂 | An evergreen shrub or small tree (Pistacia lentiscus) of the cashew family, native to the Mediterranean region and yielding a fragrant resin (mastic). | The lentisk tree thrives in dry, rocky areas. 乳香黄连木生长在干燥多石的地区。| The resin of the lentisk is used in varnishes. 乳香黄连木的树脂用于制作清漆。| 近义词: mastic tree | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific plant) | 词性: noun
