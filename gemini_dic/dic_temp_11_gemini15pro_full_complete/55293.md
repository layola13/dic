dissoluble:/dɪˈsɒljʊbl/ /dɪˈsɑːljəbl/| adj.|可溶解的；可分解的；可废除的|Capable of being dissolved or liquefied; capable of being broken up or decomposed; capable of being annulled or terminated.|The scientist experimented with various dissoluble substances in the lab.  科学家在实验室里用各种可溶解的物质做实验。

The contract is dissoluble under certain conditions.  该合同在某些条件下可以解除。|近义词：soluble, decomposable, terminable|反义词：insoluble, indecomposable, permanent|词性:形容词
