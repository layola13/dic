biodegradability:/ˌbaɪoʊˌdiːˌɡreɪdəˈbɪləti/
n.|生物降解性|The capability of a substance to be broken down into simpler substances by microorganisms like bacteria and fungi.|This plastic bag boasts excellent biodegradability, decomposing within a few months.  这种塑料袋拥有极佳的生物降解性，几个月内就能分解。|decomposability, disintegrability|non-biodegradability|noun
