irrecognizable:/ˌɪrɪˈkɒɡnɪzəbl/ /ˌɪrɛkəɡˈnaɪzəbl/
adjective
无法辨认的，认不出的
not able to be identified because of change
例句：
He was unrecognizable after the accident.
事故之后他变得面目全非。

The city was unrecognizable after the bombing.
轰炸之后，这座城市面目全非。

近义词：unidentifiable, altered, changed, transformed
反义词：recognizable, identifiable, familiar
词性: adjective
