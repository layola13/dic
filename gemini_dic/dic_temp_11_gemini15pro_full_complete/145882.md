overdestructive:/ˌōvər dɪˈstrʌktɪv/ | adj. | 过度破坏性的，破坏性极大的 | Excessively destructive; causing more destruction than is necessary or warranted. | The overdestructive force of the hurricane left the coastal town in ruins.  飓风的过度破坏力使沿海城镇成为废墟。 | The overdestructive logging practices decimated the ancient forest. 过度破坏性的伐木行为摧毁了古老的森林。| 近义词：ruinous, devastating, cataclysmic | 反义词: constructive, beneficial | 词性: 形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "destructive" (adjective)
"over-" 表示“过度”、“超过”，源自古英语 "ofer"。
"destructive" 源自拉丁语 "destruct-"，意为“拆除、摧毁”。
