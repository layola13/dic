hitch-up:/ˈhɪtʃ ʌp/ |v. n.| 套上挽具; 搭便车; 结婚;  | To fasten an animal to a vehicle or other object with a hitch; to connect a trailer to a vehicle; (informal) to get married. |  We hitched up the horses and set off. 我们套上马匹，出发了。| Synonyms: attach, connect, couple, yoke, marry | Antonyms: detach, disconnect, uncouple, separate, divorce |verb

hitch-up:/ˈhɪtʃ ʌp/ |n.| 故障; 障碍; 问题;  | A temporary difficulty or problem. | There was a hitch-up with the delivery, and the package arrived late. 交付过程中出现了一些问题，包裹到晚了。 | Synonyms: problem, snag, difficulty, glitch | Antonyms: solution, advantage, benefit | noun
