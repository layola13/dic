greave: /ɡriːv/
n.
护胫，腿甲
A piece of armor that protects the shin.
The knight fastened his greaves before the battle.  骑士在战斗前系紧了他的护胫。

The Roman soldier's greaves were made of bronze.  罗马士兵的护胫是由青铜制成的。

近义词: armor, leg guard, shin guard
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 vulnerability)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "greve" (shin, greave), from Frankish "*greva" (shin), from Proto-Germanic "*graebo" (groove, ditch), from Proto-Indo-European "*ghrebh-" (to dig, scratch).  The connection is likely due to the shape of the shin bone resembling a groove.
