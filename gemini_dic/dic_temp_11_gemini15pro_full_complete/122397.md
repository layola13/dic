mmo:/ˈɛm ɛm ˈoʊ/ | n. | 大型多人在线游戏 | Massively Multiplayer Online (game), a genre of role-playing video games in which a very large number of players interact with one another within a virtual world. |  I'm addicted to this new MMO. 我沉迷于这款新的大型多人在线游戏。| 近义词: MMORPG, online game | 反义词: single-player game | 词性: noun

Etymology:  An initialism of "Massively Multiplayer Online", which itself is derived from "Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game" (MMORPG). The term "MMO" has become a more general term encompassing various online game genres beyond just role-playing.
