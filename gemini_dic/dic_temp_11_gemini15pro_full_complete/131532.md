noncommemorations:/ˌnɒnkəˌmɛməˈreɪʃənz/
n.
非纪念；不纪念；未被纪念的事物
The act of not commemorating something, or the state of not being commemorated.
The museum's exhibit on noncommemorations explored events often overlooked by history.  博物馆关于非纪念的展览探索了历史上经常被忽视的事件。
The city council's decision to hold noncommemorations for the controversial figure sparked debate. 市议会决定不对这位有争议的人物举行纪念活动，引发了争论。
Near Antonyms: commemorations, celebrations, remembrances
Antonyms: commemorations
