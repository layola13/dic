disembowered:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊərd/| adj.| 无树荫的，失去bower的；被剥夺庇护的| Deprived of a bower; stripped of foliage or covering, especially of trees that provide shade or shelter; exposed and unprotected.| The storm left the birds disembowered, their nests destroyed.  暴风雨过后，鸟儿们失去了家园，它们的巢穴被摧毁了。|  The once lush garden was now disembowered and barren. 曾经郁郁葱葱的花园现在光秃秃的，寸草不生。| 近义词：exposed, unprotected, barren, desolate | 反义词： sheltered, protected, leafy, wooded | 词性：形容词

disembower:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊər/| vt.|  剥夺…的树荫；剥夺…的庇护；使失去遮蔽| To deprive of a bower; to strip of foliage or covering, especially of trees that provide shade or shelter; to expose and leave unprotected.| The strong winds disembowered the trees, leaving them bare and vulnerable.  强风吹走了树木的枝叶，使它们变得光秃秃的，不堪一击。|  The invading army disembowered the villagers, burning their homes and destroying their crops. 入侵的军队摧毁了村民们的家园，烧毁了他们的房屋，摧毁了他们的庄稼。| 近义词：expose, strip, denude | 反义词：shelter, protect, cover | 词性：动词


词源：disembower 由前缀 dis- (表示否定或相反) + bower (树荫，凉亭) 构成。  Bower 源自古英语 būr, 指的是居住的地方，后来引申为有树荫或遮蔽的地方。
