nonjurorism:/nɒnˈdʒʊərɪzəm/
n.
不宣誓效忠主义；拒誓者行为
The refusal to take an oath of allegiance, especially the oath of allegiance to the English monarchs William and Mary following the Glorious Revolution of 1688.
The nonjurorism movement caused a schism in the Church of England.  不宣誓效忠主义运动导致了英国教会的分裂。
Near synonyms: recusancy, dissent, nonconformity
Antonyms: loyalty, allegiance, conformity
noun
