llano:/ˈljɑːnoʊ/ /ˈljænoʊ/| n. | (尤指南美北部和中部的) 大草原，平原 | An extensive grassy plain with few or no trees, especially in South America and southwestern North America. |  The vast llanos of Venezuela are home to a diverse array of wildlife.  委内瑞拉广袤的大草原是各种野生动物的家园。

| Example: Cattle graze on the llanos. 牛群在平原上吃草。| Synonym: plain, grassland, prairie, savanna | Antonym: mountain, forest, woodland | Noun
