asteism:/ˈæstiːɪzəm/
n.
巧妙的讽刺，机智而温和的嘲讽
A polite and ingenious manner of criticizing or reproving another, frequently under the appearance of praise; delicate irony.
"He delivered a witty asteism about her fashion sense, complimenting her bold choice of neon green." 他巧妙地讽刺了她的时尚感，称赞她大胆选择了霓虹绿。
近义词: irony, sarcasm, witticism, banter
反义词: praise, compliment, flattery
词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *asteismus, from Greek asteismos "urbanity, wit," from asteios "urbane, witty," from astu "town."
