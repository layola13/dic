lamplit:/ˈlæmpˌlɪt/| adj.|灯火通明的，灯光照亮的|Lit by a lamp or lamps.  Illuminated by lamps, creating a soft, warm glow. |The lamplit streets of Paris were romantic. 灯火通明的巴黎街道很浪漫。|近义词：illuminated, lit|反义词:dark, unlit|词性:形容词

Etymology:
lamp (n.) + -lit (suffix)

lamp (n.): Old English *lampe, from Late Latin lampas, from Greek lampas "torch, lamp," from lampein "to shine."
-lit (suffix):  from light (v.) past participle. Meaning "illuminated by."
