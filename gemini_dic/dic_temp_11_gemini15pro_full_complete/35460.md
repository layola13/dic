coachabilities:/ˌkoʊtʃəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|可指导性，可教练性|The quality of being open to and responsive to coaching or instruction.  The degree to which someone can be effectively coached.|This team demonstrates high coachabilities, readily adopting new strategies.  这支队伍展现出很高的可指导性，很容易接受新策略。|coachability, trainability, receptiveness, responsiveness|uncoachability, resistance, intractability|noun
