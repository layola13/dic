meteoritic:/ˌmiːtiəˈrɪtɪk/
adjective
陨石的；流星的
Relating to or derived from meteorites.
The museum has a large collection of meteoritic rocks.  博物馆收藏了大量的陨石岩石。
The scientist studied the meteoritic impact crater. 科学家研究了陨石撞击坑。
Synonyms: meteoric
Antonyms: terrestrial
词性: adjective
