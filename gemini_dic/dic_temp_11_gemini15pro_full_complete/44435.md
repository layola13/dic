cross-reading:/ˌkrɔːs ˈriːdɪŋ/
n.|交叉阅读|The act of reading multiple texts on the same topic or related topics to compare, contrast, and synthesize information.|She engaged in cross-reading to gain a deeper understanding of the historical event. 她进行交叉阅读以更深入地了解这一历史事件。|近义词: comparative reading,  intertextual reading|反义词:  close reading|词性:名词

cross-reading:/ˌkrɔːs ˈriːdɪŋ/
v.|交叉阅读|To read multiple texts on the same topic or related topics to compare, contrast, and synthesize information.|Students were asked to cross-read several articles before writing their essays.  学生们被要求在写论文之前交叉阅读几篇文章。|近义词: compare, contrast, synthesize|反义词: focus|词性:动词
