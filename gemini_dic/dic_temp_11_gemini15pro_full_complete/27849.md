canonist:/ˈkænənɪst/| n.|教会法学家；教规专家|An expert in canon law, especially one who studies or teaches it.|例句：The canonist advised the bishop on matters of church law.  这位教会法学家就教会法事宜向主教提供建议。

例句：He was a renowned canonist, consulted by popes and emperors. 他是一位著名的教会法学家，教皇和皇帝都曾向他咨询。|近义词：legal expert, ecclesiastical lawyer|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps layman in a general sense)|词性:noun
