antiperistasis:/ˌæntiˌpɛrɪˈsteɪsɪs/
noun|逆向强化；反向增强；反向作用|The increase of any quality, as heat, by the opposition of a contrary quality, as cold.  The doctrine that cold intensifies the activity of surrounding heat.|The scientist explained the phenomenon using the principle of antiperistasis, arguing that the coldness of the surrounding air intensified the heat within the enclosed system. 科学家用逆向强化原理解释了这一现象，认为周围空气的寒冷加剧了封闭系统内部的热量。| intensification, augmentation |reduction, diminution|n.

antiperistasis:/ˌæntiˌpɛrɪˈsteɪsɪs/
noun|逆向强化；反向增强；反向作用|An obsolete medical term referring to the supposed increase in the strength of a weak body part by the surrounding weakness of other parts.|The old medical text referred to the concept of antiperistasis to explain the supposed strengthening of a limb after an adjacent limb had been amputated. 这本古老的医学文献提到了逆向强化作用的概念，以解释邻近肢体被截肢后，另一肢体力量增强的假设。|strengthening, reinforcement |weakening, impairment|n.
