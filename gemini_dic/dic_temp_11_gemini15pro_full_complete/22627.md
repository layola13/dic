bopped:/bɑːpt/ | v. |轻敲，轻打；(非正式)去(某处)；(非正式)击败，打败 |To hit something lightly; (informal) to go (somewhere); (informal) to defeat or beat up. |例句：I bopped him on the head playfully. 我开玩笑地轻拍了一下他的头。|例句:  She bopped over to the store to get some milk. 她去了趟商店买牛奶。|近义词：tapped, hit, went, defeated |反义词:missed, lost|词性:动词

词源:
"bop" likely originated as an onomatopoeia, imitating the sound of a light blow. Its informal meanings of "go" and "defeat" developed later, likely in the mid-20th century, influenced by jazz and popular music.
