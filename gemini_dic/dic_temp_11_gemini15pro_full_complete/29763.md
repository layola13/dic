catsuit: /ˈkætsuːt/ (US), /ˈkætsjuːt/ (UK)
noun
紧身衣
a close-fitting one-piece garment that covers the torso and legs, often made of stretchy material like spandex or lycra.
She wore a black catsuit for the performance. 她穿了一件黑色紧身衣表演。
The spy wore a sleek catsuit to infiltrate the party.  间谍穿了一件光滑的紧身衣潜入派对。
近义词: bodysuit, jumpsuit, leotard
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps loose clothing or baggy clothes)
词性: noun
