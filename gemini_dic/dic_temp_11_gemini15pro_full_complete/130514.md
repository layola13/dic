nonaccommodatingnesses:/ˌnɒnəkɒməˈdeɪtɪŋnɪsɪz/
noun
不妥协；不迁就；不顺应
lack of willingness to adapt or compromise; inflexibility.
Her nonaccommodatingnesses made negotiations difficult. 她的不妥协使得谈判变得困难。
近义词:intransigence, inflexibility, rigidity
反义词:flexibility, accommodation, compromise
词性:noun
