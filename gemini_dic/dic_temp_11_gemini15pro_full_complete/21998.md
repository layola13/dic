boiler:/ˈbɔɪlər/ (US) /ˈbɔɪlə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 锅炉；烧水壶；热水器 |A closed metal container in which water is heated and steam is generated, used for heating buildings or powering machinery. |The boiler broke down in the middle of winter, leaving us without heat. 冬天中间锅炉坏了，我们没有暖气了。

The factory uses a high-pressure boiler to generate electricity.  这家工厂使用高压锅炉发电。|synonyms: heater, furnace, kettle |antonyms: cooler, refrigerator |noun
