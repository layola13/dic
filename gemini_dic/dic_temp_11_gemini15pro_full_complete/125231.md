multiculturally:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəli/
adv.
多文化地
in a way that relates to or includes many different cultures
例句：The school strives to educate its students multiculturally.  这所学校努力对学生进行多元文化的教育。

例句：The city is known for its multiculturally diverse population. 这座城市以其多元文化的人口而闻名。

近义词：interculturally, cross-culturally
反义词：monoculturally, homogeneously
词性：副词


ETYMOLOGY->
multi- (many) + cultural + -ly (adverbial suffix)
"multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many"
"cultural" comes from "culture" which ultimately derives from Latin "cultura" meaning "cultivation, agriculture"
