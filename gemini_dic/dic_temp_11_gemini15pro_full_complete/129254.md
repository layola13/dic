nod-out:/nɒdˈaʊt/ | vi. | 打瞌睡；昏昏欲睡（尤指因吸毒）| To fall asleep or become semi-conscious, especially as a result of drug use. | He nodded out on the couch after taking too many painkillers. 他服用了太多止痛药后在沙发上睡着了。|  The heroin addict nodded out on the park bench.  那个海洛因成瘾者在公园长椅上昏睡过去。| 近义词：doze off, drift off,  pass out | 反义词: wake up, become alert | 词性: 动词

nod-out:/nɒdˈaʊt/ | n. | 打瞌睡；昏睡（尤指因吸毒）| The act of falling asleep or becoming semi-conscious, especially as a result of drug use. |  He had a nod-out in the middle of the meeting. 他在会议中途打了个瞌睡。| Her frequent nod-outs were a clear sign of her addiction. 她频繁的昏睡是她上瘾的明显迹象。| 近义词：doze, nap | 反义词: awakening | 词性: 名词
