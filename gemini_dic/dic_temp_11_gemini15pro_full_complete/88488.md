historicist:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪst/ | n. | 历史主义者；历史相对主义者 |  Someone who believes that all historical events and phenomena are relative to their time and place, and cannot be judged by universal standards.  |  The historicist argued that the values of ancient Greece cannot be applied to modern society.  这位历史主义者认为古希腊的价值观不能应用于现代社会。 |  relativist, contextualist | absolutist, universalist | noun

historicist:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪst/ | adj. | 历史主义的 |  Relating to historicism, the belief that all historical events and phenomena are relative to their time and place. |  He took a historicist approach to understanding the French Revolution. 他采取了历史主义的方法来理解法国大革命。 | contextual, relativistic | absolute, universal | adjective
