acreages:/ˈeɪkərɪdʒɪz/
n.
土地面积;  耕地面积;  英亩数
Land measured in acres; the size of a piece of land in acres.
The farm has large acreages of corn and soybeans.  这个农场有大面积的玉米和大豆种植地。
Synonyms: land, area, expanse, property, territory
Antonyms:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑少量，狭小等类似概念) small area, limited space
词性: 名词 (Noun)


acreage:/ˈeɪkərɪdʒ/
n.
土地面积;  耕地面积;  英亩数
Land measured in acres; the size of a piece of land in acres.
The acreage of the farm is about 100 acres.  这个农场的面积大约是100英亩。

词性: 名词 (Noun)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *akerage, from acre + -age.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *akraz (meaning "field")
