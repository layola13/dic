husked:/hʌskt/
adjective
去壳的；剥了皮的
Having had the husk removed.
例句：The farmer sold husked corn. 农民卖掉了去壳的玉米。

例句：She bought a bag of husked almonds. 她买了一袋去壳的杏仁。

近义词：shelled, dehusked, shucked
反义词：unhusked

词性: adjective


词源: From husk + -ed.  "Husk" comes from Middle Dutch "huuske," meaning "covering, pod, shell."
