neutrino:/njuːˈtriːnəʊ/ (US) /njuːˈtriːnoʊ/ (UK)| n.|中微子|A subatomic particle with no electrical charge and very little mass, produced in nuclear reactions such as radioactive decay.|Neutrinos are extremely difficult to detect because they rarely interact with matter. 中微子极难探测，因为它们很少与物质相互作用。|Synonyms: (No direct synonyms, as it's a specific particle) |Antonyms: (No antonyms, as it's a specific particle)|noun
