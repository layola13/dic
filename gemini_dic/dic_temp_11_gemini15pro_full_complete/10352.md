appellable:/əˈpiːləbl/
adjective
可上诉的
Subject to appeal; that may be appealed against.
The decision of the lower court is appellable to the High Court. 下级法院的判决可以向高等法院上诉。
appealable to a higher court, subject to appeal, reviewable
non-appealable, final, conclusive
adjective
