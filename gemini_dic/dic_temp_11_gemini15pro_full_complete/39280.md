congruence: /ˈkɒŋɡruəns/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡruəns/ (US)| n. |一致性，相合性，全等|agreement or harmony; compatibility. In geometry, the quality of being isometric, i.e., exactly the same shape and size.|The congruence between the two triangles was proven. 这两个三角形的全等已被证明。|近义词：agreement, harmony, conformity, correspondence, compatibility, consistency|反义词：disagreement, incongruity, incompatibility, inconsistency|词性: noun

congruent: /ˈkɒŋɡruənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡruənt/ (US)| adj. |一致的，相合的，全等的|agreeing or harmonious; compatible.  In geometry, (of figures) identical in form; coinciding exactly when superimposed.|Her actions were congruent with her values. 她的行为与她的价值观一致。|近义词：agreeable, harmonious, conforming, corresponding, compatible, consistent|反义词：disagreeable, incongruous, incompatible, inconsistent|词性: adjective

词源:  Late 16th century: from Latin congruentia, from congruent- ‘agreeing, meeting together’, from the verb congruere.
