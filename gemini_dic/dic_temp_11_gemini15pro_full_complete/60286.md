egghead:/ˈeɡhed/| n.|书呆子，知识分子；过于重视学术的人|A person who is highly intellectual or studious, often seen as impractical or out of touch with real life. |He's a real egghead, always with his nose in a book. 他真是个书呆子，总是埋头苦读。|Synonyms: intellectual, highbrow, academic, scholar, bookworm|Antonyms: simpleton, ignoramus|noun
