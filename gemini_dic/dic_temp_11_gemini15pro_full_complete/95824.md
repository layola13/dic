incorporation:/ɪnˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃn/ (US) /ɪnˌkɔːpəˈreɪʃən/ (UK)| n.|公司，法人，结合，合并|The act of forming a corporation or the state of being incorporated.  The inclusion of something as part of a whole.|例句：The incorporation of the new technology into the product design was a success.  这项新技术成功地融入到产品设计中。

例句：The company's incorporation in Delaware provided certain tax advantages.  公司在特拉华州注册成立，享有某些税收优惠。|近义词：inclusion, integration, formation, establishment|反义词：exclusion, separation, dissolution|词性:名词

incorporate:/ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/ (US) /ɪnˈkɔːpəreɪt/ (UK)| vt.|包含，合并，组成公司|To include as part of something larger; combine; to form into a legal corporation.|例句：We will incorporate your suggestions into the final design. 我们会将你的建议纳入最终设计中。

例句：They decided to incorporate their business in Nevada. 他们决定在内华达州注册公司。|近义词：include, integrate, combine, merge|反义词：exclude, separate, divide|词性:动词
