nonsensification:/ˌnɒnsenˌsɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|脱敏；减敏；降低敏感性|The process of reducing or eliminating sensitivity, especially in the context of allergies or immune responses.  This involves gradually exposing the individual to increasing amounts of the allergen under controlled conditions, with the aim of building tolerance and reducing the severity of allergic reactions.|Allergy shots are a common form of nonsensification therapy. 过敏针剂是一种常见的脱敏疗法。|The nonsensification program helped her tolerate peanuts. 脱敏程序帮助她耐受花生。|desensitization, hyposensitization|sensitization|noun
