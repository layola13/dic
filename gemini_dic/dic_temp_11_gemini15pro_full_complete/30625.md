ceremonialism: /ˌserɪˈmoʊniəlɪzəm/ | n. | 仪式主义；礼仪主义 | A strict adherence to outward forms and ceremonies, often at the expense of genuine feeling or sincerity. | The excessive ceremonialism of the coronation bored many viewers.  加冕典礼过多的仪式让许多观众感到无聊。| synonyms: ritualism, formalism, pageantry | antonyms: informality, casualness | noun

ceremonial: /ˌserɪˈmoʊniəl/ | adj. | 仪式的；礼仪的 | Relating to or used in a ceremony. | The president wore ceremonial robes for the inauguration. 总统穿着礼服参加就职典礼。 | synonyms: ritual, formal, conventional | antonyms: informal, casual | adjective
