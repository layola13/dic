flimflammed:/ˌflɪmˈflæmd/
verb
欺骗，哄骗
to deceive or trick someone, especially by telling them lies
He flimflammed me out of $20. 他骗了我20美元。
Synonyms: swindled, cheated, defrauded, tricked, deceived, hoodwinked
Antonyms:  honored, helped, aided, assisted
词性: verb

flimflam:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/
noun
欺骗，哄骗；骗局
nonsense, deceptive or evasive talk; a trick or deception
That whole story was a flimflam.  整个故事都是骗人的。
Synonyms:  hoax, sham, fraud, deception, trickery
Antonyms:  truth, honesty, reality
词性: noun

flimflam:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/
adjective (rarely used)
骗人的，欺诈的
relating to or characterized by deceptive or evasive talk; tricky or deceptive
His flimflam promises turned out to be worthless. 他那些骗人的承诺到头来一文不值。(这个用法比较少见)
Synonyms:  deceptive, fraudulent, misleading, tricky
Antonyms:  honest, truthful, genuine
词性: adjective
