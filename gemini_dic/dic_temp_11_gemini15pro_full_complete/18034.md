begone:/bɪˈɡɑːn/ | v. | 离去，滚开 |Used as a strong command to leave, implying annoyance or dismissal.  It carries a sense of archaic or literary style.| Begone, you villain! 滚开，你这恶棍！| 近义词: leave, depart, get out, go away | 反义词: arrive, come, stay, welcome | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *begon*, from Old English *be- + gan* "to go."  The prefix *be-* intensifies the meaning.

被动语态:
*Begone cannot be used in the passive voice.*

疑问句:
*Begone is not typically used in questions.*

否定句:
*Begone is already a command implying negation.  A double negative wouldn't be grammatically correct.*
