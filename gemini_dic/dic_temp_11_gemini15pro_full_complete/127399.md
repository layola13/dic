nearer:/ˈnɪərər (US)/ /ˈnɪərə (UK)| adj., adv.|更近的，更接近的|Comparative form of "near."  Indicates a smaller distance or closer relationship in space, time, or degree.|例句：My house is nearer to the school than yours. 我的房子比你的房子离学校更近。

例句：As the deadline drew nearer, the pressure increased. 随着截止日期的临近，压力也越来越大。|近义词：closer, nigher|反义词：farther, further|词性：形容词，副词
