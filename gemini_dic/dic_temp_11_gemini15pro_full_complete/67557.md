extraordinarily:/ɪkˈstrɔːrdɪnərɪli/ /ˌɛkstrəˈɔːrdəˌnɛrəli/| adv. |非常地，极其地，格外地 |  In a way that is not usual or ordinary; remarkably. | The scenery was extraordinarily beautiful. 景色非常美丽。| 近义词: remarkably, exceptionally, unusually, incredibly, surprisingly | 反义词: ordinarily, commonly, usually, typically | 词性: 副词

词源:  From extra- + ordinary + -ly.  "Extra-" comes from Latin, meaning "outside" or "beyond." "Ordinary" comes from Latin "ordinarius," meaning "regular" or "usual."  "-ly" is a suffix used to form adverbs.
