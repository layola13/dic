chardonnay:/ʃɑːr.dəˈneɪ/ (US), /ˌʃɑː.dənˈeɪ/ (UK)| n.|霞多丽（一种白葡萄酒）|A dry white wine made from green-skinned grapes, originally from the Burgundy region of France.|例句：We enjoyed a bottle of chilled Chardonnay with dinner. 我们晚餐喝了一瓶冰镇霞多丽。|例句：This Chardonnay has a crisp, fruity flavor.  这款霞多丽口感清爽，果味浓郁。|近义词：white wine, Chablis (similar style)|反义词: red wine|词性:noun

词源:
From the French place name Chardonnay, a commune in the Mâconnais subregion of Burgundy.  The name is ultimately derived from the Latin *Cardonnacum, meaning "estate of Cardo" (a personal name).
