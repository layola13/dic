cabbed:/kæbd/
verb
乘出租车；
Took a taxi; traveled by cab.
He cabbed home after the party. 他聚会后乘出租车回家。
She cabbed it to the airport. 她乘出租车去了机场。
近义词: taxied, took a cab
反义词: walked, drove, cycled
词性: verb (past tense of "cab")

cabbed:/kæbd/
adjective
<非正式> 疲惫不堪的；
Exhausted, worn out.
I felt completely cabbed after the long flight.  长途飞行后，我感到筋疲力尽。
He was cabbed from working all night. 他整夜工作后疲惫不堪。
近义词: exhausted, tired, worn out, fatigued
反义词: energetic, refreshed, invigorated
词性: adjective (past participle of "cab" used as an adjective)
