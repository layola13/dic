inconvenience:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəns/ | n. vt.|不便，麻烦；打扰|Something that causes difficulty or trouble.|例句：The flight delay caused a great inconvenience to the passengers. 航班延误给乘客带来了极大的不便。

例句：I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause. 我为此可能造成的任何不便表示歉意。|近义词：trouble, difficulty, bother, hassle, nuisance|反义词:convenience, ease, comfort|noun

inconvenience:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəns/ | vt.|给…带来不便；打扰|To cause trouble or difficulty for someone.|例句：I hope I'm not inconveniencing you by asking for a ride. 我希望我要求搭车不会给你带来不便。

例句：Don't inconvenience yourself on my account.  别为了我而麻烦自己。|近义词：bother, disturb, trouble, impose upon|反义词:accommodate, assist, help|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: inconvenient, from Old French inconvenient, from Latin inconvenient-, inconveniens, present participle of inconvenire ‘be unsuitable’, from in- ‘not’ + convenire ‘come together, agree’.
