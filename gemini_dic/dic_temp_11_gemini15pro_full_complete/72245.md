floriferous:/ˌflɔːˈrɪfərəs/
adjective
多花的；开花繁茂的
Bearing many flowers; blooming profusely.
The floriferous garden was a riot of color. 开花繁茂的花园色彩缤纷。
The plant is floriferous, producing masses of small white flowers.  这种植物开花繁茂，能开出大量的小白花。
近义词: blooming, flowering, blossoming
反义词: barren, sterile
词性: adjective

floriferous:/ˌflɔːˈrɪfərəs/
词源: From Latin *florifer, from flōs (“flower”) + -fer (“bearing”).
