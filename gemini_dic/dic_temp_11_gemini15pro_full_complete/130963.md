nonassonances:/ˌnɒnˈæsənənsɪz/| n.|不谐和音；不协调的声音|The piece was full of nonassonances, making it unpleasant to listen to.| This experimental music is characterized by its jarring nonassonances. 这首实验音乐的特点是其刺耳的不谐和音。|近义词：dissonances, discords|反义词：consonances, harmonies|词性: 名词

Etymology:  "Non-" (Latin, not) + "assonance" (from Old French "asonance", from "assoner" to resound, ultimately from Latin "adsonare" to sound to, from "ad-" to + "sonare" to sound).  Thus, "nonassonance" literally means "not resounding together harmoniously".
