incompetent: /ɪnˈkɑːmpɪtənt/ /ɪnˈkɒmpɪtənt/ | adj. | 不胜任的，无能力的 | Not having the necessary skills or ability to do something successfully. |  He was fired for being incompetent. 他因不胜任工作而被解雇。|  The incompetent worker ruined the project.  这个不称职的工人毁了这个项目。 | inept, unskilled, incapable, unqualified | competent, skilled, capable, qualified | adjective


incompetence: /ɪnˈkɑːmpɪtəns/ /ɪnˈkɒmpɪtəns/ | n. | 无能力，不胜任 | Lack of skill or ability to do something successfully. | His incompetence led to the company's downfall. 他的无能导致了公司的垮台。 | The project's failure was due to the team's incompetence. 项目的失败是由于团队的无能。| incapability, inability, ineptness | competence, capability, ability, skill | noun
