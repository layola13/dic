impracticalities:/ɪmˌpræktɪˈkælɪtiz/
|n.|不切实际之处；不可行性；|The impracticalities of the proposal soon became apparent.  这项提议的不切实际之处很快就显现出来了。|例句：The impracticalities of the plan made it impossible to implement.  这个计划的不切实际性使其无法实施。|近义词：difficulties, obstacles, hindrances, drawbacks, disadvantages|反义词：practicalities, advantages, benefits|词性:名词

impracticality:/ɪmˌpræktɪˈkælɪti/
|n.|不切实际；不可行性；|The impracticality of the idea was obvious from the start.  这个想法的不切实际性从一开始就很明显。|例句：She recognized the impracticality of her youthful dreams. 她意识到了她年轻时的梦想的不切实际性。|近义词：unfeasibility, unworkability|反义词：practicality, feasibility|词性:名词

impractical:/ɪmˈpræktɪkəl/
|adj.|不切实际的；不实用的；|It's impractical to wear high heels for hiking. 穿高跟鞋去远足是不切实际的。|例句：His suggestions were interesting but ultimately impractical. 他的建议很有趣，但最终不切实际。|近义词：unrealistic, unworkable, infeasible|反义词：practical, realistic, feasible|词性:形容词
