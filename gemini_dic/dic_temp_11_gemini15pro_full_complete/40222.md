contextualist:/kənˈtekstʃuəlɪst/ | n. | 语境主义者；语境论者 | Someone who believes that the meaning of words, actions, or texts depends on the context in which they occur. | The contextualist argued that the author's intentions were irrelevant to the meaning of the text. 语境主义者认为作者的意图与文本的意义无关。 | 近义词: interpretivist | 反义词: formalist | 词性: 名词

contextualistic:/kənˌtekstʃuəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 语境主义的；语境论的 | Relating to contextualism. | Her contextualistic approach to literary criticism emphasized the historical and social context of the work. 她对文学批评的语境主义方法强调了作品的历史和社会背景。 | 近义词: interpretive | 反义词: formal | 词性: 形容词
