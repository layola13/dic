nocturne:/ˈnɒktɜːn/ (UK) /ˈnɑːktɜːrn/ (US)| n. |夜曲；夜景画|A piece of music, typically for piano, that is expressive of the night. Also, a painting of a night scene.| Chopin's nocturnes are famous for their beauty and melancholy. 肖邦的夜曲以其优美和忧郁而闻名。| synonym: serenade, lullaby | antonym:  march, fanfare | noun
