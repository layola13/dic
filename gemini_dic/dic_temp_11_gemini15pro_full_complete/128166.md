nettling:/ˈnetlɪŋ/
adjective
刺激的，恼人的
Causing a stinging or prickling sensation, either physically or emotionally.
Her nettling remarks irritated everyone in the room. 她刺激性的言论激怒了房间里的每个人。

nettling:/ˈnetlɪŋ/
noun
荨麻疹
A skin condition characterized by an itchy rash and raised, red welts.
She developed a severe case of nettling after touching a poisonous plant.  接触了有毒植物后，她得了严重的荨麻疹。

nettling:/ˈnetlɪŋ/
verb (nettle + -ing)
激怒，惹恼
To irritate or annoy someone.
His constant complaining was nettling to his coworkers. 他不断的抱怨惹恼了他的同事。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *netele, from Proto-Germanic *natilōn, from Proto-Indo-European *nēdlā (“needle”). Related to needle. The sense development is from the stinging hairs of the nettle plant, which are similar to needles.
