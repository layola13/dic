crooked:/ˈkrʊkɪd/ (US), /ˈkrʊkɪd/ (UK)| adv.|弯曲地，歪斜地|in a bent or twisted way|He smiled crookedly. 他咧嘴一笑。|synonyms: askew, awry|antonyms: straight|adverb
