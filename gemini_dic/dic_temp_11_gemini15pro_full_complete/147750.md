overprogram:/ˌoʊvərˈproʊɡræm/ | v. | 过度安排；安排过多的节目；超量编程 | To schedule too many activities or events; to overload with instructions or data. | The conference was overprogrammed, leaving little time for networking. 会议安排过多的活动，几乎没有时间进行交流。| The computer was overprogrammed and crashed. 计算机被超量编程，然后崩溃了。 | 近义词: overload, oversaturate | 反义词: underprogram, simplify | 词性: 动词
