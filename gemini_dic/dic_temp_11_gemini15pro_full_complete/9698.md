antitonic:/ˌæntiˈtɒnɪk/ | adj. | 抗张的，对抗性调节的 | Relating to or characterized by antitony; opposing or counteracting tonic muscular contraction. |  The antitonic effect of the drug helped to relax the muscle spasms.  这种药物的抗张作用有助于放松肌肉痉挛。| synonyms: relaxing, spasmolytic | antonyms: tonic, stimulating | adjective

antitonic:/ˌæntiˈtɒnɪk/ | n. | 抗张剂 | A substance that reduces muscle tone. | The doctor prescribed an antitonic to alleviate the patient's muscle stiffness. 医生开了一种抗张剂来缓解病人的肌肉僵硬。| synonyms: muscle relaxant, spasmolytic | antonyms: tonic, stimulant | noun
