ensnarers:/ɪnˈsnɛərərz/
|n.|诱捕者；陷害者|People who trap or lure others into difficult situations.  Those who entangle others, either literally or figuratively.|The internet can be a web of ensnarers for unsuspecting young people.  互联网对毫无戒心的年轻人来说可能是一个充满陷阱的网络。|trappers, deceivers, predators|liberators, rescuers|noun
