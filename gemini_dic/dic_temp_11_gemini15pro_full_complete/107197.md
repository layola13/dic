lagrangian: /ləˈɡrɑːndʒiən/ /ləˈɡreɪndʒiən/ | n. adj.| 拉格朗日量，拉格朗日的 |A function that summarizes the state of a physical system. In mechanics, the difference between the kinetic and potential energies.  |The Lagrangian of this system is easy to calculate.  这个系统的拉格朗日量很容易计算。|Synonym:  Lagrange function|Antonym: Hamiltonian|noun

lagrangian: /ləˈɡrɑːndʒiən/ /ləˈɡreɪndʒiən/ | adj.| 拉格朗日的 |Relating to or characteristic of the French mathematician and astronomer Joseph Louis Lagrange or his work. |Lagrangian mechanics is a reformulation of classical mechanics. 拉格朗日力学是经典力学的重新表述。|Synonym: Lagrange's|Antonym: N/A|adjective
