nongratuitousness:/ˌnɒnˌɡrætjuˈɪtəsnəs/ | n. | 非无偿性；非免费性 | The state of not being gratuitous; not given or done freely; having a purpose or motive behind it.  The nongratuitousness of the act suggested a hidden agenda. | 例句：The nongratuitousness of the lawyer's services was made clear in the contract.  律师服务的非无偿性在合同中已明确规定。

例句：Her nongratuitousness in offering help made me suspicious. 她主动提供帮助的非无偿性让我起了疑心。| 近义词:  calculatedness, deliberateness, intentionality | 反义词: gratuitousness, freeness,  unmotivatedness | 词性: noun
