ethnologically:/ˌeθnəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb
人种学地
in a way that relates to the study of the characteristics of different peoples and the relationships between them
例句：The customs were examined ethnologically. 这些习俗从人种学角度进行了考察。

例句：The researcher analyzed the data ethnologically to understand the cultural practices. 研究人员从人种学角度分析了数据，以了解文化习俗。

近义词：culturally, anthropologically
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider terms like "non-culturally" or "ahistorically" as loosely contrastive in specific contexts.)
词性: adverb
