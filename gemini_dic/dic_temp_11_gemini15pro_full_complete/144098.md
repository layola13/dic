outsatisfy: /ˌaʊtsætɪsˈfaɪ/
verb
使更满意；超过…的满意度
to satisfy someone more than someone or something else satisfies them
例句：The new restaurant outsatisfied all the others in the area with its delicious food and impeccable service.  这家新餐厅以其美味的食物和无可挑剔的服务，比该地区的其他所有餐厅都更令人满意。

例句：Their customer service team aims to outsatisfy clients with personalized attention and prompt responses. 他们的客户服务团队旨在通过个性化的关注和及时的回应，让客户获得比其他公司更高的满意度。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform
反义词：disappoint, dissatisfy, underperform
词性：verb (动词)
