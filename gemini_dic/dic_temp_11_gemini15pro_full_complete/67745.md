eyeballing:/ˈaɪˌbɔːlɪŋ/
verb
目测，粗略估计
Estimating something by sight, without precise measurement.
I eyeballed the distance and figured I could make the jump. 我目测了一下距离，觉得我能跳过去。
The carpenter eyeballed the wood before cutting it. 木匠在切割木头之前目测了一下。
approximate, estimate, gauge
measure, calculate
v.
