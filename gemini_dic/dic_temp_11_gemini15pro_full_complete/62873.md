enlarged:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒd/
adjective
放大的，扩大的
Increased in size or scale.
The photograph was enlarged to poster size.  照片被放大到海报大小。
The museum has an enlarged collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆扩大了古代文物的收藏。
bigger, expanded, amplified, augmented
smaller, reduced, diminished, shrunk
adjective
