nonpunishments:/ˌnɒnˈpʌnɪʃmənts/ | n. | 非惩罚，免于惩罚 | Absence of punishments; instances where punishment is not applied or administered. |  The focus on rehabilitation and restorative justice led to a system of nonpunishments for minor offenses.  这个侧重于康复和恢复性司法的系统导致对轻微犯罪不予惩罚。| synonyms:  pardons, reprieves, exemptions, amnesties, absolutions | antonyms: penalties, sanctions, chastisements, disciplines, retributions | noun

Etymology:
"non-" (Latin prefix) meaning "not" + "punishment" (from Old French "punissement")
