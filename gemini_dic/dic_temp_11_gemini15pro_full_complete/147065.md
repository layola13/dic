overlending:/ˌoʊvərˈlɛndɪŋ/ | n. |过度放贷|The act of lending more money than is prudent or safe, especially by banks or other financial institutions. This can lead to financial instability and economic crises.|  Excessive lending by banks contributed to the housing bubble. 银行过度放贷导致了房地产泡沫。| synonyms: excessive lending, imprudent lending | antonyms: responsible lending, conservative lending | noun
