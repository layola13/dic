corruptible:/kəˈrʌptɪbl/| adj.|可腐败的，易腐败的，可贿赂的|Susceptible to corruption, decay, or bribery; capable of being morally compromised.|The officials were corruptible and easily swayed by bribes. 这些官员很容易被贿赂，容易动摇。|Synonyms: dishonest, venal, bribable, unscrupulous, shady|Antonyms: honest, incorruptible, upright, virtuous|adjective

corrupt:/kəˈrʌpt/| adj. vt.|腐败的，堕落的；贿赂，使腐败|Immoral or dishonest, especially as a result of bribery; to cause someone to become dishonest or immoral.|The corrupt politician was eventually exposed. 腐败的政客最终被曝光了。 The company corrupted several officials to win the contract. 该公司贿赂了几名官员以赢得合同。|Synonyms: dishonest, crooked, fraudulent, contaminated, rotten|Antonyms: honest, upright, virtuous, pure, uncontaminated|adjective, verb
