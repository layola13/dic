dinette:/daɪˈnɛt/ | n. | 小餐桌，小餐厅 | A small area or alcove, often in or near a kitchen, used for dining.  A small, informal dining set. | We have a cute little dinette in the corner of our kitchen. 我们在厨房的角落里有一个可爱的小餐桌。| The dinette came with four chairs and a table. 这套小餐桌配有四把椅子和一张桌子。 | synonyms: breakfast nook, dining area, kitchen table | antonyms: dining room, formal dining area | noun
