epimere: /ˈɛpɪˌmɪər/ | n. | 上段; 背段; 体节的背部 |  In embryology, an epimere refers to the dorsal portion of a mesodermal somite, which gives rise to the skeletal muscles of the back. |  The epimere develops into the deep muscles of the back.  胚节发育成背部深层肌肉。 | The cells of the epimere differentiate to form myotomes. 胚节细胞分化形成肌节。 |  paraxial mesoderm, myotome | hypomere | noun
