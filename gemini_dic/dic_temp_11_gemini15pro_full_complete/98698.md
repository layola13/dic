insuperableness:/ˌɪnsuːpərəblnəs/
n.
不可克服性；无法超越
The insuperableness of the challenge made many people give up.  这个挑战的不可克服性让很多人放弃了。
The team was daunted by the insuperableness of their opponent's defense.  团队被对手防守的不可逾越性吓倒了。
invincibility, insurmountable nature
surmountable nature, conquerableness
noun
