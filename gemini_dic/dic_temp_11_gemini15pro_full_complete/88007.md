high-speed:/ˌhaɪˈspiːd/| adj.|高速的|Moving or operating at high speed.|例句：The high-speed train arrived on time. 高速列车准时到达。|例句：We need a high-speed internet connection. 我们需要高速互联网连接。|近义词：fast, rapid, quick, swift|反义词：slow, low-speed|词性:形容词
Etymology:  'high' (Old English *hēah) + 'speed' (Old English spēd, meaning 'success, prosperity, wealth, opportunity, quickness, speed') - The compound 'high-speed' emerged later, likely in the late 19th or early 20th century with advancements in technology.
