overexplanation:/ˌoʊvərˌɛkspləˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 过度解释；解释过多 | The overexplanation made the simple concept confusing. 过度解释反而把简单的概念弄混了。|  He provided an overexplanation of the rules, boring everyone. 他对规则解释得过于详细，让大家都感到厌烦。 | 近义词：verbosity, prolixity | 反义词：conciseness, brevity | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "explanation" (noun from the verb "explain," ultimately from Latin "explanare," meaning "to make plain or level").
