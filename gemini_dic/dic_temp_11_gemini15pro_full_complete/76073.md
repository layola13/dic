furnus:/ˈfɜːrnəs/| n.|烤炉，熔炉|A structure or appliance used for heating, especially for smelting metal or baking.|The blacksmith worked diligently at his furnus, shaping the glowing metal. 铁匠在他炉子旁辛勤地工作，塑造着发光的金属。|近义词：oven, kiln, forge|反义词: refrigerator, freezer|noun
