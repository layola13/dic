nihilist: /ˈnaɪɪlɪst/ (US), /ˈnɪɪlɪst/ (UK)| n. |虚无主义者； нигилист|A person who believes that life is meaningless and rejects all religious and moral principles. |例句：He's such a nihilist, he doesn't believe in anything. 他真是个虚无主义者，什么都不相信。

例句：The nihilist argued that all values are baseless and that nothing can be known or communicated.  这个虚无主义者认为所有价值观都是毫无根据的，没有什么东西是可以被认识或交流的。|近义词：pessimist, cynic, skeptic|反义词:optimist, believer|词性:noun
