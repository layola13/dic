heartburn:/ˈhɑːrtbɜːrn/ (US) /ˈhɑːtbɜːn/ (UK)| n.|胃灼热，烧心|A burning sensation in the chest, caused by acid refluxing from the stomach into the esophagus.|例句：I ate too much spicy food and now I have terrible heartburn. 我吃了太多辣的食物，现在胃灼热得厉害。

例句：He often suffers from heartburn after a big meal.  他经常在大餐后感到烧心。


近义词：acid indigestion, dyspepsia, pyrosis|反义词: (No direct antonym, but relief could be considered an opposite state)|词性:noun
