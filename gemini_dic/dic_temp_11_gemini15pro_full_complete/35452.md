co-work:/koʊˈwɜːrk/| v. |共同工作，协同工作|To work together on a shared project or in a shared space.|Example 1: We co-worked on a presentation for the conference. 我们共同准备了会议的演示文稿。

Example 2: They co-worked to develop a new marketing strategy. 他们合作开发了一个新的营销策略。|近义词：collaborate, cooperate, partner|反义词: work alone, work independently|词性:动词

co-working:/ˌkoʊˈwɜːrkɪŋ/| n. adj. |共同工作，联合办公|The act of working together on a shared project or in a shared space; Also used as an adjective to describe such spaces or arrangements. |Example 1: Co-working spaces are becoming increasingly popular. 联合办公空间越来越受欢迎。

Example 2:  The co-working environment fosters creativity. 共同工作的环境 fosters creativity.  |近义词：collaboration, joint work|反义词:solo work, independent work|词性:名词/形容词
