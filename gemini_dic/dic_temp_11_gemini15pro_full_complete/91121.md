hibernate:/ˈhaɪbəneɪt/| vi.|冬眠，蛰伏|To spend the winter in a dormant state, characteristic of some animals.  The bear hibernates during the cold winter months.|例句：Bears hibernate in winter. 熊在冬天冬眠。

例句：The groundhog hibernated for several months. 土拨鼠冬眠了几个月。|近义词：sleep, rest, slumber|反义词:awaken, arouse|verb
