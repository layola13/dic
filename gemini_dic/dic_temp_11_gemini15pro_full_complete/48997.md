deglutinate:/diːˈɡluːtɪneɪt/
verb
使分离；使脱离；分开；解除粘着；去胶
to separate or make something separate from something that it is stuck to.
The enzyme deglutinates the blood cells. 这种酶能使血细胞分离。

deglutinate:/diːˈɡluːtɪneɪt/
verb
去胶，去粘性
to remove the glue or sticky substance from something
The stamp has been deglutinated from the envelope. 这张邮票已从信封上揭下来了。


近义词:separate, detach, unglue, unstick
反义词:adhere, stick, glue, agglutinate
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + gluten + -ate.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse". "Gluten" refers to a sticky substance, like glue. "-ate" is a suffix used to form verbs.  Thus, "deglutinate" literally means "to remove the sticky substance".
