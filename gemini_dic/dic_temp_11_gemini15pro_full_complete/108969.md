leet:/liːt/ | adj. n. | 精英的；高手；leetspeak（一种网络语言） |  Derived from "elite" with the vowels removed, it signifies skill or superiority, particularly in online gaming and computing. It can also refer to the specialized alphabet used in leetspeak. | He's a leet gamer. 他是一位精英玩家。 | Synonyms: elite, expert, pro, skilled | Antonyms: newbie, novice, beginner, amateur | adjective/noun

leet:/liːt/ | n. | leetspeak（一种网络语言）|  A specialized alphabet used primarily online, characterized by replacing letters with similar-looking numbers and symbols (e.g., "leet" becomes "1337").  |  He wrote the message in leet. 他用leetspeak写了这条信息。 | Synonyms: leetspeak | Antonyms: standard English | noun
