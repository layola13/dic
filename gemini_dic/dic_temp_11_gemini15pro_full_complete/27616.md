cancelment:/ˈkænsəlmənt/ | n. | 取消，撤销 |The act of canceling something; annulment. |The cancelment of the flight left many passengers stranded. 航班取消导致许多乘客滞留。|近义词：cancellation, annulment, revocation, rescinding|反义词：approval, confirmation, ratification|词性: 名词

词源: From the verb "cancel" + "-ment" (suffix forming nouns of action or result).  "Cancel" comes from Old French "canceler," from Late Latin "cancellare" "to cross out lattice-wise," from "cancelli" "lattice."
