gipsylike:/ˈdʒɪpsiˌlaɪk/
adjective
像吉普赛人的; 流浪的;  无拘无束的
Resembling or characteristic of a Gypsy, especially in being nomadic, unconventional, or colorful.
She had a gipsylike love of freedom and travel. 她有一种像吉普赛人一样热爱自由和旅行。
Synonyms: nomadic, wandering, roving, itinerant, peripatetic, bohemian, free-spirited
Antonyms: settled, sedentary, conventional, conformist
word type: adjective
