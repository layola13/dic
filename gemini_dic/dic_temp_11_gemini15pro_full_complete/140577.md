oligoclase: /ˈɒlɪɡəˌkleɪz/ /ˈɑːlɪɡoʊˌkleɪz/ | n. |  奥长石 | A plagioclase feldspar mineral with a composition between albite and andesine.  | Oligoclase is a common constituent of granitic rocks. 奥长石是花岗岩的常见成分。|  The shimmering effect in sunstone is often due to inclusions of oligoclase. 日光石的闪光效果通常是由于含有奥长石。| synonyms: plagioclase, feldspar | antonyms: N/A | noun
