masterworks:/ˈmæstərwɜːrks/| n.|杰作；名作|A person's greatest works, especially those of an artist, writer, or composer.|例句：The museum exhibited a collection of masterworks by Renaissance painters.  博物馆展出了文艺复兴时期画家们的杰作收藏。

例句：His later masterworks surpassed all his previous efforts. 他后期的杰作超越了他以前所有的努力。|近义词：masterpieces, classics, chef-d'oeuvres|反义词:failures, duds|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *masterwork*, from master + work.  Attested since the late 16th century.
