echinoderm:/ɪˈkaɪnəˌdɜːrm/ | n. | 棘皮动物 | Any of a phylum (Echinodermata) of radially symmetrical coelomate marine animals including starfish, sea urchins, and sea cucumbers.  |  Starfish and sea urchins are echinoderms. 海星和海胆是棘皮动物。 | synonyms: invertebrate, marine animal | antonyms: vertebrate | noun
