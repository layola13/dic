kheyti: /ˈkeɪti/ | n. |  凯蒂（Kheyti）| Kheyti is the name of a social enterprise that designs, adapts, and implements low-cost farming solutions for smallholder farmers in India.  It's primarily known for its "Greenhouse-in-a-Box" product. | Kheyti helps farmers grow seven times more food while using 90% less water. | 近义词:  agricultural technology, sustainable farming, precision agriculture | 反义词: traditional farming, unsustainable agriculture | 词性: noun
