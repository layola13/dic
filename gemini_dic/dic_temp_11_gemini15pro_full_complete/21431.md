blower:/ˈbloʊər/ (US) /ˈbləʊər/ (UK)| n.|吹风机；鼓风机；吹制玻璃工人|A machine or device that produces a stream of air or gas.|The blower on the furnace stopped working, and the house got cold.  炉子上的鼓风机坏了，房子变冷了。|
The glassblower skillfully shaped the molten glass.  玻璃吹制工匠熟练地塑造熔化的玻璃。|synonyms: fan, ventilator, pump, bellows|antonyms: vacuum, extractor|noun
