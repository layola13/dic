corundum:/kəˈrʌndəm/ | n. |刚玉|A very hard crystalline form of aluminum oxide, Al₂O₃, occurring naturally as ruby, sapphire, and emery, and used as an abrasive and refractory material. | Rubies and sapphires are both varieties of corundum. 红宝石和蓝宝石都是刚玉的变种。|  Emery is a granular form of corundum. 金刚砂是刚玉的一种颗粒状形式。| 近义词: aluminum oxide, alumina | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Tamil *kuruntam* (“ruby, sapphire”), ultimately from Sanskrit *kuruvinda* (“ruby”).
