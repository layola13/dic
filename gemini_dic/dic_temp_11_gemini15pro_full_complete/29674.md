cathedral:/kəˈθiːdrəl/
n.
大教堂；主教座堂
A large and important church, especially one that is the center of a diocese and is presided over by a bishop.
The cathedral is a magnificent example of Gothic architecture. 这座大教堂是哥特式建筑的宏伟典范。

The cathedral dominates the city skyline. 这座大教堂俯瞰着城市的天际线。


近义词:church, basilica, minster
反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 chapel 小教堂)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cathédrale*, from Late Latin *cathedralis* (“belonging to a bishop's seat”), from Latin *cathedra* (“seat, chair”), from Ancient Greek *καθέδρα (kathédra, “seat, chair”)*.
