decisively:/dɪˈsaɪsɪvli/
adverb
果断地；决然地
in a way that shows the ability to make decisions quickly and effectively.
The battle was decisively won.  这场战斗取得了决定性的胜利。
The government acted decisively to control inflation. 政府采取果断行动控制通货膨胀。
近义词:conclusively, definitely, resolutely
反义词:indecisively, hesitantly
词性:adverb

decide:/dɪˈsaɪd/
verb
决定；决心
come to a resolution in the mind as a result of consideration.
We decided to go to the beach. 我们决定去海滩。
She decided against buying a new car. 她决定不买新车了。
近义词:determine, resolve, settle
反义词:waver, hesitate
词性:verb

decision:/dɪˈsɪʒn/
noun
决定；决心
a conclusion or resolution reached after consideration.
The decision to close the factory was a difficult one. 关闭工厂的决定是一个艰难的决定。
He made the decision to quit his job. 他决定辞掉工作。
近义词:resolution, determination, judgment
反义词:indecision, hesitation
词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *dēcisīvus, from Latin dēcīsus (past participle of dēcīdere (“to decide, cut off, settle”)), from dē (“down, away from”) + caedere (“to cut”).
