bisexual:/baɪˈsɛkʃuəl/
adjective
双性的；对两种性别都有性欲的
Sexually attracted not exclusively to people of one particular gender; attracted to both men and women.
例句：She identifies as bisexual. 她认为自己是双性恋。

例句：He has had bisexual relationships in the past. 他过去有过双性恋关系。

synonym: ambisexual, pansexual
antonym: heterosexual, homosexual
词性: adjective

bisexual:/baɪˈsɛkʃuəl/
noun
双性恋者
A person who is sexually attracted not exclusively to people of one particular gender; a person attracted to both men and women.
例句：The study surveyed a group of bisexuals. 这项研究调查了一群双性恋者。

例句：Many bisexuals feel marginalized by both the heterosexual and homosexual communities. 许多双性恋者感到被异性恋和同性恋群体边缘化。

synonym:  bi
antonym: heterosexual, homosexual
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (prefix meaning "two") + sexual.  The "bi-" prefix ultimately comes from Latin.
