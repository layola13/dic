hips:/hɪps/
n.|屋脊|The roof had wide, overhanging hips. 屋顶有宽阔的、悬垂的屋脊。|Synonym: ridge, crest|Antonym: trough, valley|noun


Etymology->
From Old English *hyppes (plural), akin to Old Norse *hjǫppr.  Ultimately related to hip (joint).  The sense of "roof hip" is likely due to resemblance to the human hip joint.
