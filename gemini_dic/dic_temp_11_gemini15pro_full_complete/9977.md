aphony:/ˈeɪfəni/ | n. |失声，声音嘶哑 |Loss of voice; inability to speak. |例句：The singer suffered from aphony due to a throat infection. 歌手因喉咙感染而失声。

例句：Aphony can be a symptom of various medical conditions. 失声可能是各种疾病的症状。

近义词：dysphonia, voicelessness |反义词：phonation, speech | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀφωνία (aphōnía, “voicelessness”), from ἄφωνος (áphōnos, “voiceless”), from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + φωνή (phōnḗ, “voice, sound”).
