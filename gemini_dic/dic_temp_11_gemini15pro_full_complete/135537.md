nonpliantness:/nɒnˈplaɪəntnəs/| n.|不顺从，不服从；固执；倔强|The nonpliantness of the material made it difficult to work with.  这种材料的不顺从性使它难以加工。|Synonyms: inflexibility, rigidity, stubbornness, obstinacy, intransigence|Antonyms: flexibility, pliability, compliance, obedience, docility|noun

Etymology:  From non- + pliant + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Pliant" comes from Old French "pliant" meaning "bending, flexible," ultimately from Latin "plicare" meaning "to fold." "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives.
