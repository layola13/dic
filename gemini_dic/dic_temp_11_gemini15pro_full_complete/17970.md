befoul:/bɪˈfaʊl/| vt.|玷污，弄脏，败坏（名声）|To make something dirty or impure, especially by contact with something unpleasant; to damage the purity or integrity of something.|The oil spill befouled the pristine coastline.  石油泄漏污染了原始的海岸线。|The scandal befouled his reputation.  这桩丑闻败坏了他的名声。|近义词：contaminate, pollute, sully, tarnish, taint|反义词：cleanse, purify, sanctify|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + foul.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "to make" or "to cause to be." "Foul" means offensively dirty or unpleasant.
