cerumens:/səˈruːmɛnz/
noun
耳垢（复数）
Earwax; the yellowish waxy substance secreted in the ear canal.
Excessive cerumen can cause hearing loss. 过多的耳垢会导致听力损失。
Synonyms: earwax, cerumen
Antonyms: None
n.
