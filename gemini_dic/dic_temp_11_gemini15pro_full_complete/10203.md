Etymology:  From Late Latin *apostematio, from Latin apostema (“abscess”), from Ancient Greek ἀπόστημα (apóstēma, “abscess”), from ἀφίστημι (aphístēmi, “I put away, separate”), from ἀπό (apó, “away from”) + ἵστημι (hístēmi, “I stand, place”).
