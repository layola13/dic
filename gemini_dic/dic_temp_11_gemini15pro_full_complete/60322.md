egocentric:/ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. n.|自我中心的；自私自利的；以自我为中心的（人）|Thinking only of oneself, without regard for the feelings or desires of others; self-centered.  A person who is egocentric.|例句：He's so egocentric, he never considers anyone else's feelings. 他太以自我为中心了，从不考虑别人的感受。|近义词：self-centered, narcissistic, selfish|反义词：altruistic, selfless|词性：形容词, 名词

egocentrics:/ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪks/ | n. |自我中心者；自私自利的人|Plural form of egocentric, referring to a group of people who are egocentric.|例句：The egocentrics only cared about their own success. 这些以自我为中心的人只在乎他们自己的成功。|近义词：egotists, narcissists|反义词：altruists|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From ego + centric.  Ego, from Latin, meaning "I". Centric, meaning relating to a center, ultimately from Latin centrum "center".
