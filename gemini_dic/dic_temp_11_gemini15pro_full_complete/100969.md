inutterable:/ɪˈnʌtərəbl/
adjective
难以言喻的；不可言说的；说不出口的
Too great, extreme, or awful to be expressed in words.
例句：
She felt an inutterable sadness.
她感到一种难以言喻的悲伤。

例句：
He experienced inutterable joy at the birth of his child.
孩子出生时，他体验到了难以言喻的喜悦。

近义词：indescribable, inexpressible, unspeakable
反义词：utterable, expressible


词性: adjective
词源:  From Late Latin *inutterabilis, from Latin in- "not" + utterabilis "speakable".
