asepticism:/əˈsɛptɪˌsɪzəm/ | n. |无菌；无菌法；防腐法|The state of being free from disease-causing microorganisms. The practice of maintaining sterile conditions, especially in surgery and medicine, to prevent infection.|例句：The surgeon ensured asepticism throughout the procedure. 外科医生在整个手术过程中确保无菌。

例句：Strict asepticism is crucial in the operating room.  在手术室中，严格的无菌操作至关重要。|近义词：sterility, asepsis|反义词：sepsis, infection|词性: noun
