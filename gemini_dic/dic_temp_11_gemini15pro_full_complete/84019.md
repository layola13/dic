half-drowning:/ˌhɑːf ˈdraʊnɪŋ/ | n. | 半溺水；濒死体验 |  The state of having nearly drowned but been resuscitated.  It can cause serious health complications due to lack of oxygen. |  He survived a half-drowning incident as a child, which left him with a fear of water. 他小时候经历了一次半溺水事件，这让他对水产生了恐惧。 | near-drowning, almost drowning | survival, resuscitation | noun
