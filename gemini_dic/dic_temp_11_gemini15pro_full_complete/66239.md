exclamation:/ˌɛkskləˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 惊叹，感叹，惊叹词 | a sudden cry or remark, especially expressing surprise, anger, or pain |  She gave an exclamation of delight when she saw the present. 她看到礼物时发出一声喜悦的惊叹。 |  cry, shout, yell | murmur, whisper | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exclamatio, from Latin exclamare "to cry out," from ex- "out" + clamare "to cry, shout."
