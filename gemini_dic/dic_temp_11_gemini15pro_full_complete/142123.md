origanum:/əˈrɛɡənəm/ /ɔːˈrɪɡənəm/| n. |牛至，奥勒冈草|A pungent aromatic herb of the mint family, used in Mediterranean cooking.|例句：The recipe calls for fresh oregano.  这食谱需要新鲜的牛至。

例句：I sprinkled dried oregano over the pizza. 我在披萨上撒了干牛至。|近义词：marjoram|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin origanum, from Latin orīganum, from Ancient Greek ὀρίγανον (oríganon), from ὄρος (óros, “mountain”) + γάνος (gános, “brightness, sheen”).
