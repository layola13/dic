anticaking:/ˌæntiˈkeɪkɪŋ/
adjective
防结块的
Preventing something, especially a powdered substance, from forming lumps or sticking together.
Anticaking agents are added to salt to keep it free-flowing.  食盐中添加了抗结剂以保持其自由流动。
Talcum powder has anticaking properties. 滑石粉具有防结块的特性。
synonym: free-flowing, non-caking
antonym:  caking, clumping, coagulating
词性: adjective
