expansional:/ɪkˈspænʃənəl/ | adj. |扩张的，膨胀的| Relating to or characterized by expansion; tending to expand. | The company is in an expansional phase, opening new branches across the country.  这家公司正处于扩张阶段，在全国各地开设新的分店。| The government's expansional economic policies led to a period of rapid growth. 政府扩张性的经济政策带来了经济快速增长的时期。 | 近义词: expansive, growth-oriented, developing | 反义词: contractionary, shrinking, declining | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From expansion + -al.  Expansion comes from Late Middle English: from Old French expansion, from late Latin expansio(n-), from the verb expandere.
