desponsate:/dɪˈspɒnseɪt/ /dɪˈspɑːnseɪt/
verb
正式订婚；许配
to promise (someone) in marriage; betroth.
例句：He was desponsated to a woman he had never met. 他与一个素未谋面的女子订了婚。

例句：The princess was desponsated to the prince of a neighboring kingdom. 公主被许配给了邻国的王子。

近义词：betroth, engage, affiance
反义词： divorce, separate
词性：verb (及物动词)
