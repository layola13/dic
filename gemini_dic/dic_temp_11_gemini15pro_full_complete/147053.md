overleap:/ˌoʊvərˈliːp/| v. |超越，跳过，忽略|To pass over without touching; to go beyond|He overleaped the fence with ease. 他轻松地跳过了篱笆。|近义词：bypass, ignore, exceed, surpass, jump over|反义词: acknowledge, address, face, confront|词性:动词

overleap:/ˌoʊvərˈliːp/| n. |跳跃，超越|The act of leaping over something; an act of going beyond|His overleap of conventional morality shocked many. 他对传统道德的超越震惊了许多人。|近义词：jump, leap, bound, spring|反义词:conformity|词性:名词
