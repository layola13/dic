outstudy: /ˌaʊtˈstʌdi/
verb
超越学习，在学习上胜过
to study harder or more effectively than someone else
例句：She outstudied all her classmates and got the highest grade on the exam. 她比所有同学都更努力学习，并在考试中获得了最高分。

例句：He outstudied his competition and earned a scholarship to a prestigious university. 他在学习上胜过竞争对手，并获得了 prestigious 大学的奖学金。

近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, outlearn
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
词性：动词
