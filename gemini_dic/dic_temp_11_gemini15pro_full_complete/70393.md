fiftieth:/ˈfɪftiəθ/ (US), /ˈfɪftiɪθ/ (UK)| adj. |第五十的|Being the ordinal number corresponding to fifty. |例句：This is her fiftieth birthday. 这是她的五十岁生日。|例句：He finished in fiftieth place in the marathon. 他在马拉松比赛中获得了第五十名。|近义词: N/A (ordinal numbers don't have direct synonyms)|反义词: N/A (ordinal numbers don't have direct antonyms)|词性: Adjective|词源: From Old English *fīftigoða, from fīftig (fifty) + -oða (ordinal suffix).
