immenseness:/ɪˈmɛnsnəs/| n.|巨大；广阔；无垠|The immenseness of the universe is difficult to grasp. 宇宙的浩瀚难以理解。|例句：She was overwhelmed by the immenseness of the ocean. 她被大海的浩瀚所震撼。

例句：The immenseness of the task before them was daunting. 面前任务的艰巨性令人生畏。

近义词：vastness, enormity, hugeness, immensity|反义词:smallness, tininess|词性:名词

Etymology: From immense + -ness.  "Immense" comes from the French "immense," derived from the Latin "immensus," meaning "immeasurable," from "in-" (not) + "mensus" (measured), past participle of "metiri" (to measure).
