argy-bargy:/ˈɑːɡi ˈbɑːɡi/
noun
争吵，口角
A noisy quarrel or disagreement.
例句：They were having a right argy-bargy about whose turn it was to wash up. 他们正在大声争吵轮到谁洗碗了。

例句：The kids were having an argy-bargy over a toy. 孩子们正在为一个玩具争吵。

近义词：quarrel, argument, squabble, row, tiff
反义词：agreement, harmony, peace
