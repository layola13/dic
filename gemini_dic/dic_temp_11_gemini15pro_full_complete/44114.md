crispin:/ˈkrɪspɪn/| n.|克里斯平; 鞋匠; 圣克里斯平|A shoemaker; St. Crispin, the patron saint of shoemakers.|例句：The Crispin's Day speech in Henry V inspired generations.  亨利五世在圣克里斯平节的演讲激励了一代又一代人。

例句：He worked as a humble crispin in a small village. 他在一个小村庄里当一个谦逊的鞋匠。|近义词：shoemaker, cobbler|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially factory worker if contrasting handcrafted vs. mass-produced)|n.
