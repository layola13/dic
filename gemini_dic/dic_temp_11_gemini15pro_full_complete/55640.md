dithecous:/daɪˈθiːkəs/
adjective
双药的，有两个药室的
Having two thecae or pollen sacs.  Relating to an anther that has two lobes, each with two pollen sacs.
The dithecous anther is a common feature of many flowering plants.  双药花药是许多开花植物的共同特征。
The botanist identified the flower by its dithecous anthers.  植物学家通过其双药花药识别了这朵花。
Synonyms: bithecal
Antonyms: monothecous
词性: adjective
