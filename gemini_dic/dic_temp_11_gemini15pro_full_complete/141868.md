orchideous:/ɔːrˈkɪdiəs/| adj.|兰花的，似兰花的|Resembling an orchid, especially in color or shape.  Having the characteristics of an orchid.|The florist created an orchideous centerpiece for the wedding, with vibrant purple and white blooms.| 近义词:orchid-like, floral, flowery|反义词:plain, simple|词性:adjective

Etymology: From Latin *orchideus*, from Ancient Greek ὀρχιδ- (orkhid-), stem of ὄρχις (orkhis, “testicle”), because of the shape of the root tubers of some species. + -ous (suffix forming adjectives).
