noncorporation:/ˌnɑːnkɔːrpəˈreɪʃn/
n.
非公司；非法人团体
An entity that is not incorporated and thus not recognized as having a legal personality separate from its owners.
The artists formed a noncorporation to manage their shared studio space.  艺术家们组成了一个非公司团体来管理他们共享的工作室空间。
Near synonyms: unincorporated association, unincorporated society, partnership, collective
Antonyms: corporation, incorporated entity
词性: 名词
