guardlike:/ˈɡɑːrdlaɪk/ | adj. | 像卫兵的，警戒的 | Resembling or characteristic of a guard; vigilant. |  He stood guardlike at the door, his arms crossed. 他像卫兵一样站在门口，双臂交叉。 | The dog stood guardlike, watching the strangers approach.  狗像卫兵一样站着，看着陌生人靠近。 | 近义词：watchful, vigilant, alert | 反义词：inattentive, careless, negligent | 词性: 形容词

词源:
guard (n.) + -like (suffix)

guard: From Old French *garde (Modern French garde), from Frankish *warda or some other Germanic source, from Proto-Germanic *wardōn (“to watch, guard”), from Proto-Indo-European *wer- (“to perceive, watch”).

-like:  From Old English -līc (“having the form or appearance of; similar, like”), from Proto-Germanic *-līkaz (“having the form or appearance of”), from Proto-Indo-European *-likos (“equal”). Cognate with Dutch -lijk, German -lich, Gothic -leiks.
