disturbing:/dɪˈstɜːbɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人不安的，令人担忧的，烦扰的 | Causing anxiety; worrying.  | The news report was deeply disturbing. 这则新闻报道令人深感不安。 |  The images of the war were very disturbing. 战争的画面令人非常不安。 | synonyms: upsetting, troubling, worrying, distressing, alarming | antonyms: calming, soothing, reassuring, comforting | adjective

disturb:/dɪˈstɜːrb/ | vt. | 打扰，妨碍，使不安 |  Interrupt the peace or quiet of. |  Please do not disturb the meeting. 请勿打扰会议。 |  The noise disturbed my sleep. 噪音打扰了我的睡眠。 | synonyms: interrupt, bother, disrupt, interfere with | antonyms: soothe, calm, comfort | verb
