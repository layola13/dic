curios:/ˈkjʊərioʊz/ (US), /ˈkjʊəriəs/ (UK)| adj.|好奇的，求知的；古怪的，奇特的|Eager to know or learn something. Strange or unusual.|
例句：
She was curious about his past. 她对他的过去很好奇。

He had a curious way of speaking. 他说话的方式很古怪。

近义词：inquisitive, nosy, peculiar, odd, strange, bizarre|
反义词：indifferent, apathetic, ordinary, common|
词性：形容词

词源:
From Middle French *curios* (modern French *curieux*), from Latin *cūrĭōsus* (“careful, diligent, inquisitive, meticulous”), from *cūra* (“care, concern, attention”).
