indifferentism:/ɪnˈdɪfərənˌtɪzəm/| n. |冷漠主义；无兴趣；不关心|The state of being indifferent, especially in matters of religion or morality.  Apathy or lack of concern, especially regarding matters considered important by others. |He displayed an indifferentism to the suffering of others that was shocking. 他对他人苦难表现出的冷漠令人震惊。|Synonyms: apathy, unconcern, indifference, detachment|Antonyms: zeal, fervor, passion, engagement|noun
