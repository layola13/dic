arillate: /ˈærɪˌleɪt/ | adj. | 假种皮的；有假种皮的 | Having an aril.  An aril is a fleshy outgrowth from the outer coat of a seed that partially or completely covers the seed. | The yew tree has bright red arillate seeds. 紫杉有鲜红色的假种皮种子。|  arillated |  non-arillate | adjective
aril: /ˈærɪl/ | n. | 假种皮 |  A fleshy outgrowth from the outer coat of a seed that partially or completely covers the seed. | The aril of the nutmeg is the source of mace. 肉豆蔻的假种皮是肉豆蔻衣的来源。|  seed covering |  | noun
