christener:/ˈkrɪsənər/ | n. |施洗者；命名者|A person who christens someone or something.  A person who gives a name to someone or something, especially at a baptism.| 例句：He was the christener of the ship. 他是这艘船的命名者。|例句：She was chosen as the baby's christener. 她被选为这个婴儿的施洗者。| 近义词：baptizer, namer | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
