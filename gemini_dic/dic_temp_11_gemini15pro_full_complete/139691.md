oceanful:/ˈoʊʃənˌfʊl/ | n. | 满满一海洋；大量的 | A vast amount, as much as an ocean could hold. |  He inherited an oceanful of troubles from his predecessor. 他从前任那里继承了大量的麻烦。

| The charity received an oceanful of donations after the disaster. 灾难过后，慈善机构收到了大量的捐款。 | 近义词: abundance, plethora, a lot, a great deal | 反义词: little, scarcity | 词性: noun

词源:  A compound word formed from "ocean" + "-ful". "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
