mallowwort:/ˈmæloʊwɜːrt/ | n. |锦葵；蜀葵 |A plant of the mallow family, especially one of the genus *Malva*.  These plants typically have hairy stems and leaves, and showy flowers with five petals.| The mallowwort grew profusely in the neglected garden. 锦葵在无人照料的花园里茂盛地生长着。|Synonym: mallow| Antonym: N/A | noun
