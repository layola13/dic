flanging:/ˈflændʒɪŋ/| n.|镶边，凸缘；（声音的）镶边效果|A sound effect produced by mixing two identical sound recordings together, one of which is delayed slightly.|The flanging on the guitar solo gave it a psychedelic feel. 吉他独奏中的镶边效果赋予它一种迷幻的感觉。|近义词：phasing, chorusing|反义词:|noun
