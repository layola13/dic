inter-polishing:/ˌɪntərˈpɒlɪʃɪŋ/ | adj. |  互相抛光；相互润色； |The process of polishing two surfaces against each other to achieve a smooth finish. | Example: The inter-polishing of the gears ensured smooth operation. 齿轮之间的相互抛光确保了平稳运转。 | Example:  Inter-polishing of the lenses reduced light scattering.  透镜的相互抛光减少了光散射。 | Synonym: mutual polishing, cross-polishing | Antonym: roughening, abrading | word property: adjective

inter-polishing:/ˌɪntərˈpɒlɪʃɪŋ/ | n. | 互相抛光；相互润色； |The act or process of inter-polishing. | Example: The inter-polishing of the gemstones enhanced their brilliance.  宝石的相互抛光增强了它们的光彩。| Example:  The final stage involved inter-polishing to achieve a perfect fit.  最后阶段包括相互抛光以达到完美契合。 | Synonym: mutual polishing, cross-polishing | Antonym: roughening, abrading | word property: noun


Etymology:  "inter-" (Latin) meaning "between, among" + "polishing" (from "polish," ultimately from Latin "polire" to smooth).
