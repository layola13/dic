arandora:/ˌærənˈdɔːrə/ | n. | 阿兰多拉星号 | The Arandora Star was a British registered passenger and refrigerated cargo liner. It was converted to an armed merchant cruiser at the start of the Second World War and was later used as a troop transport. It was sunk by a German U-boat in 1940, resulting in a significant loss of life, including many Italian and German internees. |  The sinking of the Arandora Star was a tragedy. 阿兰多拉星号的沉没是一场悲剧。 |  The Arandora Star was torpedoed in the Atlantic. 阿兰多拉星号在大西洋被鱼雷击中。 | synonym: ship, vessel, liner | antonym: N/A | noun
