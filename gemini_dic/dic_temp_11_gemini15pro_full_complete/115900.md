marmorosis:/ˌmɑːrməˈroʊsɪs/
noun
大理石病，石末沉着病
A lung disease caused by inhaling marble dust, characterized by the deposition of marble particles in the lung tissue.
例句：Marmorosis is an occupational hazard for marble cutters.  大理石病是切割大理石工人的职业病。

例句：The doctor diagnosed him with marmorosis after reviewing his chest X-ray.  医生检查了他的胸部X光片后，诊断他患有大理石病。
近义词：pneumoconiosis,  dust disease
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific disease)
词性: noun
