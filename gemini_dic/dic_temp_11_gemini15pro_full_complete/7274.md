angor:/ˈæŋɡɔːr/| n.| 心绞痛；极度痛苦|A feeling of extreme distress and oppression, especially of the chest.|He suffered from angor and shortness of breath.|近义词：anguish, agony, torment, distress|反义词: comfort, relief, ease|词性:名词
Etymology: From Latin *angor*, from *angere* "to strangle, throttle, torment, vex."
