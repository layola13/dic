masquerader:/ˌmæskəˈreɪdər/ | n. | 伪装者；假扮者 | A person who wears a mask or disguise, especially for a party or other festive occasion; someone who pretends to be someone or something they are not. |  He was a masquerader at the Halloween party, dressed as a vampire. 他在万圣节派对上扮成吸血鬼，是个伪装者。

The spy was a masquerader, living a double life.  这个间谍是个伪装者，过着双重生活。 |  pretender, impostor, impersonator, deceiver | revealer, exposer | noun
