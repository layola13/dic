georgettes:/ʒɔːrˈʒɛts/
n.
乔其纱（一种薄而透明的丝绸织物）
A sheer, lightweight crêpe fabric, typically made of silk or polyester, having a slightly crinkled surface.
She wore a beautiful georgette dress to the party. 她穿了一件漂亮的乔其纱连衣裙去参加派对。
近义词：chiffon, crepe, voile
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a fabric type, perhaps something heavy/thick like: velvet, tweed)
词性: noun
