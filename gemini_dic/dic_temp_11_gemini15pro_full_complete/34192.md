clampdown:/klæmpˈdaʊn/| v.|压制，取缔，严厉打击|To suddenly take strict action to stop or control something|The police clamped down on drug trafficking in the city. 警方严厉打击了该市的毒品贩运。|近义词：crack down on, suppress, repress|反义词:liberalise, deregulate|verb
