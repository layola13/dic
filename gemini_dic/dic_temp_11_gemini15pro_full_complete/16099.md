bandster:/ˈbændstər/| n. |乐队成员;  乐手;  音乐家; (尤指)流行乐队成员|A musician, especially a member of a popular music band. |例句：The bandster tuned his guitar before the show.  乐队成员在演出前调了吉他。

例句：The young bandster dreamed of playing sold-out stadiums.  这位年轻的乐队成员梦想着在座无虚席的体育场演奏。|近义词：musician, player, instrumentalist, artist|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-musician, amateur) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "band" + "-ster," a suffix forming agent nouns. "Band" itself comes from Old Norse "band," meaning "bond, tie, fetter."  The "-ster" suffix originally denoted a female agent (like "spinster"), but later lost its gender specificity and came to be used more generally.
