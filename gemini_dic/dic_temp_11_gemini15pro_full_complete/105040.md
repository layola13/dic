keramic:/kəˈræmɪk/ | adj. n.|陶瓷的；陶瓷制品|Made of clay and hardened by heat.  Relating to or characteristic of ceramics.|(adj.) The museum has a fascinating collection of keramic art.  博物馆收藏了引人入胜的陶瓷艺术品。

(n.)  The artist displayed several new keramics at the gallery.  这位艺术家在画廊展出了几件新的陶瓷作品。|近义词：pottery, earthenware, porcelain|反义词: None (lacks a direct opposite)|词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Greek "keramikos" meaning "of pottery," from "keramos" meaning "potter's clay." Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*cher-," meaning "to harden."
