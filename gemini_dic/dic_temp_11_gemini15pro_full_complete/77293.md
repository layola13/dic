gatefold: /ˈɡeɪtfəʊld/ (US) /ˈɡeɪtfəʊld/ (UK)| adj. n.|折叠式的；折叠页；|A gatefold is a record, book, or magazine cover or page that is larger than the others and folded in half.| The vinyl record came in a lavish gatefold sleeve. 这张黑胶唱片装在一个华丽的折叠封套里。|近义词: foldout, bifold|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'single-page' or 'unfolded')|词性: adjective, noun


Etymology:
From 'gate' (referring to the way the folded pages open like gates) + 'fold'.
