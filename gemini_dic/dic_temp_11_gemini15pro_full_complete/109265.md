lemming-like:/ˈlɛmɪŋˌlaɪk/
adjective
像旅鼠一樣的；盲目跟风的
Resembling the behavior of lemmings, especially in blindly following others, often to disastrous consequences.  This implies an unthinking, herd-like mentality.
The investors followed the market trends in a lemming-like rush, ultimately losing their savings when the bubble burst. 投资者像旅鼠一样盲目跟风市场趋势，最终在泡沫破裂时损失了积蓄。
synonyms:  blindly following, herd-like, unthinking, imitative, uncritical, slavish
antonyms: independent, individualistic, critical, discerning, rebellious, nonconformist
词性: 形容词
