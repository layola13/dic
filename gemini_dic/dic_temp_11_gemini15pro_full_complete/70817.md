finialled:/ˈfɪniəld/| adj.|装有顶饰的，饰以顶饰的|Having a finial or finials; decorated with a finial. |The finialled gateposts gave the entrance a grand appearance.  装有顶饰的门柱使入口显得宏伟。|The newly finialled roof added a touch of elegance to the building. 新装上顶饰的屋顶为建筑增添了一丝优雅。|Synonyms: ornamented, decorated, adorned|Antonyms: plain, undecorated, simple|adjective

finial:/ˈfɪniəl/| n.|顶饰，顶尖|A distinctive ornament at the apex of a roof, gable, pinnacle, canopy, or similar structure.|The finial on top of the church spire glittered in the sunlight. 教堂尖顶上的顶饰在阳光下闪闪发光。|The antique clock was topped with a delicate brass finial. 古董钟的顶部装饰着一个精致的黄铜顶饰。|Synonyms: ornament, decoration, adornment|Antonyms: base, bottom|noun
