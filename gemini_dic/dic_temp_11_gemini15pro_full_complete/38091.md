composure:/kəmˈpoʊʒər/ | n. |沉着；镇静； composure |The speaker maintained his composure despite the heckling. 演讲者尽管受到诘难，仍保持镇静。 |  synonyms: calmness, equanimity, poise, self-possession | antonyms: agitation, anxiety, nervousness, panic | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compōsūra ("a putting together"), from Latin compōnere ("to put together, arrange, settle").  Equivalent to com- + posture.  The notion is of having one's mental and emotional faculties "put together" and under control.
