fraternizers:/ˈfrætərnʌɪzərz/
n.
交友者；联谊者；与敌方友好往来者
People who socialize with others, especially members of a different group, often in a friendly and informal way.  Specifically, it can refer to soldiers socializing with the enemy.
The general issued a strict order against fraternizing with the enemy. 将军发布了严禁与敌人 fraternize 的命令。
socializers, mixers
isolates, loners
noun
