avoidance:/əˈvɔɪdəns/| n.|避免，回避，躲避|The avoidance of unnecessary risks is paramount. 避免不必要的风险至关重要。|例句：His avoidance of eye contact made her suspicious. 他回避眼神接触让她起了疑心。

例句：Tax avoidance is a serious crime. 逃税是一项严重的罪行。

近义词：evasion, dodging, shunning, circumvention|反义词:confrontation, acceptance|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French avoidaunce, from avoider.
