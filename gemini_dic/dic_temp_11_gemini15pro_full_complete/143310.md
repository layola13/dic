outduelling:/ˌaʊtˈdʒuːəlɪŋ/
verb
战胜，胜过（尤指用剑）
The knight outduelled his opponent in a thrilling contest. 这位骑士在一场激动人心的比赛中战胜了他的对手。
He outduelled his rival for the woman's hand. 他在决斗中胜过情敌，赢得了美人芳心。
近义词: outfighting, defeating, vanquishing, conquering
反义词: losing to, being defeated by
词性: v.

Etymology:
From "out-" (meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "duel" (from Old French duel, from Latin duellum, meaning war).
