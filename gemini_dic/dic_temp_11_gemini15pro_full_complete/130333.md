non-topographical:/ˌnɒnˌtɒpəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
非地形学的，非地貌的
Not related to or based on the physical features of a place or region.
The artwork is non-topographical, focusing on abstract concepts rather than depicting specific locations. 这件艺术品是非地形学的，专注于抽象概念而不是描绘特定地点。
non-representational, abstract
topographical, representational
