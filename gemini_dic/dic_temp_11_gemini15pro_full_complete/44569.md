crouchant:/ˈkraʊtʃənt/| adj.|蹲伏的，蜷伏的|Lying close to the ground with the limbs bent, as an animal when hiding or preparing to spring.|The lion lay crouching in the grass, ready to pounce. 狮子蹲伏在草丛中，准备突袭。|近义词：squatting, crouched, recumbent|反义词：standing, upright|adjective

crouching:/ˈkraʊtʃɪŋ/| adj.|蹲着的，蜷缩的|In a position with the limbs bent and close to the ground.|The cat adopted a crouching posture before leaping onto the table. 猫在跳到桌子上之前摆出了蹲伏的姿势。|近义词：squatting, crouched, recumbent|反义词：standing, upright|adjective
