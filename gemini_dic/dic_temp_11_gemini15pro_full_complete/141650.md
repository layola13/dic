optimity:/ˈɒptɪmɪti/ | n. |乐观，乐观主义|The state or quality of being optimistic; hopefulness and confidence about the future.|She maintained her optimity despite the difficult circumstances. 她即使在困境中也保持乐观。| 近义词:optimism, hopefulness, positivism | 反义词: pessimism, negativity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From optimistic + -ity.  Ultimately from Latin optimus "best".
