nonidealistic:/ˌnɒnaɪˌdiːəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非理想主义的
Not idealistic; practical or pragmatic.  Focusing on realistic goals and possibilities rather than on theoretical or idealistic ones.
His nonidealistic approach to business proved successful. 他务实的商业方法证明是成功的。
Synonyms: pragmatic, practical, realistic, down-to-earth, hardheaded
Antonyms: idealistic, utopian, romantic, quixotic
词性: adjective
