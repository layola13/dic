expectedly:/ɪkˈspektɪdli/
adverb|意料之中地，不出所料地|As expected, the outcome was predictable.|例句：He expectedly arrived late.  他不出所料地迟到了。|近义词：predictably, unsurprisingly, naturally|反义词:unexpectedly, surprisingly, shockingly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "expected" + "-ly". "Expected" comes from the Latin "expectare" (to look out for, await).
