outsteamed:/ˌaʊtˈstiːmd/
verb
以蒸汽胜过；在蒸汽方面超过；蒸汽更多；（比喻）彻底击败
to surpass or defeat someone decisively, especially in a competitive situation.  Often used metaphorically to suggest a significant victory, like outperforming someone significantly.
例句：
The new engine easily outsteamed the old one.  新引擎轻易地超过了旧引擎。

The chef outsteamed his competitor in the culinary competition with his innovative dish.  这位厨师凭借其创新菜肴在烹饪比赛中彻底击败了对手。

近义词: outmatched, outperformed, surpassed, defeated, trounced, overwhelmed
反义词: lost to, defeated by

词性: verb (动词)

词源:  A combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning to surpass or exceed) and the verb "steam" (referring to the power of steam).  Thus, the word literally means "to surpass in steam power," which evolved to the more general meaning of "to surpass decisively."
