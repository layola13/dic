countercomplaints:/ˌkaʊntərəmˈpleɪnts/
noun
反诉，反抱怨
Complaints made in response to other complaints.
The company filed countercomplaints against the former employee.  这家公司对这位前雇员提出了反诉。
The judge dismissed the initial complaint along with all the countercomplaints. 法官驳回了最初的诉讼以及所有的反诉。
synonyms: countercharges, counterclaims, counteraccusations
antonyms: admissions, concessions
n.
