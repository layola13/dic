acidify: /əˈsɪdɪfaɪ/| v. |酸化; 使变酸|To make something more acidic by increasing the concentration of hydrogen ions.|Acid rain acidifies the soil and damages plants. 酸雨使土壤酸化并损害植物。|近义词: sour, acidulate|反义词: alkalize, neutralize|词性:动词
Etymology: From Late Latin *acidificare, from Latin acidus (“sour”) + -ficare (from facere, “to make”).
