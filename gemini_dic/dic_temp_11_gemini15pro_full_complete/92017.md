hyperoxygenating:/ˌhaɪpərˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtɪŋ/
verb
过度充氧；超氧合
Increasing the oxygen saturation of something, typically blood or tissues, beyond normal levels.
The patient was hyperoxygenating before the surgery.  病人手术前正在过度充氧。
The device hyperoxygenates the blood before it's returned to the body.  该设备在血液返回体内之前对其进行过度充氧。
近义词: oxygenating, superoxygenating
反义词: deoxygenating
词性: verb (动词)
