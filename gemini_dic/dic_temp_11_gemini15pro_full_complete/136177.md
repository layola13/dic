nonreinstatement:/ˌnɒnriːɪnˈsteɪtmənt/ |n.| 不恢复，不复职|The nonreinstatement of his driver's license caused him significant hardship.  他的驾照不被恢复给他带来了很大的困难。| The policy clearly stated the nonreinstatement of membership after expulsion.  该政策明确规定开除后不恢复会员资格。|近义词: nonrenewal, cancellation|反义词: reinstatement, restoration|词性: 名词


词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "reinstatement" (noun from "reinstate," from "re-" (prefix meaning "again") + "in" + "state" (verb meaning "to establish"))
