megalerg:/ˈmɛɡəlɜːrɡ/ |n.| 百万尔格 |A unit of energy equal to one million ergs.  It is a CGS (centimeter-gram-second) unit of energy. |The earthquake released energy equivalent to several megalergs.  地震释放的能量相当于几百万尔格。|Synonyms:  erg (x 1,000,000), joule (1 megalerg = 0.0001 joules)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
