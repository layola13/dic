amorally:/əˈmɔːrəli/
adv.
不道德地，无道德原则地
Without moral principles; in a way that shows no concern about whether behavior is morally right or wrong.
He acted amorally when he cheated on the exam. 他考试作弊的行为是不道德的。
The company behaved amorally by dumping toxic waste into the river.  这家公司向河里倾倒有毒废物，其行为是不道德的。
近义词:immorally, unethically, wickedly
反义词:morally, ethically, virtuously
词性:副词

Etymology:
From a- (not) + morally.  "Moral" ultimately derives from Latin "mores" (customs).
