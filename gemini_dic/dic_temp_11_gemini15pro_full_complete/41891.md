cosignificative:/ˌkoʊsɪɡˈnɪfɪkətɪv/
adjective
共同表示的; 具有共同意义的
having the same meaning; jointly significant
例句：The words "happy" and "joyful" are cosignificative. “快乐”和“喜悦”这两个词具有共同的意义。

例句：The cosignificative gestures and words emphasized the speaker's point.  共同表达的手势和言语强调了演讲者的观点。

近义词：synonymous, equivalent, similar
反义词：antonymous, opposite, dissimilar
词性: adjective

词源: co- (together) + significative (significant)
