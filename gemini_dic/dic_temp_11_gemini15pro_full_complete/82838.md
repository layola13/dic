gules:/ˈɡjuːlz/| n. |红色|In heraldry, the color red.  It is represented in engravings by vertical lines.|The shield is gules, with a silver lion rampant.|近义词：red, vermeil, ruby|反义词:azure, vert|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *gueles "red furs," plural of gueule "mouth, throat," from Latin gula "throat." The connection between "throat" and "red" is thought to come from the color of the open mouth of a predatory animal.
