multivoiced:/ˌmʌltiˈvɔɪst/| adj.|多声的，多声部的，众声喧哗的|Having or characterized by many voices or viewpoints; polyphonic.|The novel presents a multivoiced narrative, incorporating perspectives from various characters across different social classes.|近义词：polyphonic, multifarious, diverse, varied|反义词：monophonic, uniform, homogenous|词性:形容词
词源: "multi-" (many) + "voiced" (having a voice). "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" (much, many). "Voiced" comes from the verb "voice," which ultimately derives from Latin "vox" (voice).
