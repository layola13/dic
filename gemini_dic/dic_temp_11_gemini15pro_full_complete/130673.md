nonaerobic:/ˌnɒnˌeəˈrəʊbɪk/ | adj. | 非需氧的，厌氧的 | Not requiring free oxygen for life or growth; anaerobic.  | Example: Nonaerobic bacteria thrive in environments without oxygen. 非需氧细菌在无氧环境中 thrive。 Example:  The process of fermentation is nonaerobic. 发酵过程是非需氧的。 | 近义词: anaerobic | 反义词: aerobic | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix): not, absence of.  From Latin *non*.
aerobic (adj.): relating to or involving oxygen. From Greek *aēr* (air) + *bios* (life).
