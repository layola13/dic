例句：
The imposter was eventually caught and sent to prison.  这个骗子最终被抓并送进了监狱。

例句：
She felt like an imposter in her new job, unsure if she was qualified enough. 在她的新工作中，她觉得自己像个冒名顶替者，不确定自己是否足够胜任。

近义词：fraud, charlatan, fake, pretender, cheat |反义词:genuine person, honest person|词性:noun
