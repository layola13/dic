cerebriform: /sɛˈriːbrɪfɔːrm/ /sɛˈriːbrəˌfɔrm/
adjective
脑状的，似脑回的
Resembling the brain in form, specifically having convolutions like those of the brain.  Characterized by rounded and convoluted surfaces resembling the surface of the brain.
The tumor had a cerebriform appearance.  这个肿瘤外观呈脑状。
The surface of the mineral was cerebriform. 这种矿物的表面呈脑状。
近义词: convoluted, gyriform
反义词: smooth, flat
词性: adjective
