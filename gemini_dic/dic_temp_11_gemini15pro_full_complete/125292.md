multiframe:/ˈmʌltiˌfreɪm/
noun|多帧，多画面|A sequence of multiple frames, often used in animation or video.  A multiframe image consists of a series of frames, each slightly different, which when viewed in rapid succession create the illusion of motion.|The animation was created using a multiframe technique.|Synonyms:  sequence, series, animation, montage|Antonyms: still frame, single frame|n.
