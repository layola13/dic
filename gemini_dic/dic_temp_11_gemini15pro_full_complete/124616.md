mousquetaire:/ˌmʌskɪˈtɛər/| n. |火枪手；火枪队士兵；（女式）高筒靴|A soldier armed with a musket, especially one of the French royal bodyguard in the 17th and 18th centuries; a type of woman's high boot.|例句：The Three Musketeers is a classic adventure novel. 《三个火枪手》是一部经典的冒险小说。

例句：She wore a pair of stylish mousquetaire boots. 她穿了一双时髦的高筒靴。|近义词：guardsman, soldier, trooper (for the soldier meaning); boot, high boot (for the boot meaning)|反义词:civilian (for the soldier meaning)|词性:noun
