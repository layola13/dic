despume:/dɪˈspjuːm/ /dɪˈspuːm/
verb
去除泡沫；撇去浮沫
To remove froth or scum from the surface of a liquid.
The winemaker despumed the fermenting grape juice.  酿酒师撇去了发酵葡萄汁上的浮沫。
The chef carefully despumed the stock before adding the vegetables.  厨师在加入蔬菜之前小心地撇去了高汤上的浮沫。
Synonyms: skim, clarify, purify
Antonyms:  contaminate, pollute
词性: 动词
