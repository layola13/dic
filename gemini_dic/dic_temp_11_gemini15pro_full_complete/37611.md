communicator:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtər/| n.|沟通者，传播者|A person who communicates information or ideas, especially one who is skilled at doing this.|She is an excellent communicator and can easily explain complex topics. 她是一位优秀的沟通者，能够轻松解释复杂的话题。|近义词：speaker, presenter, spokesperson, orator|反义词：receiver, listener|noun
