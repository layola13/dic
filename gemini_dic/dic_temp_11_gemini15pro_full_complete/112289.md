loquaciousness:/ˌloʊkweɪʃəsnəs/| n. |多话，健谈|The quality of being loquacious; talkativeness.  It refers to the characteristic of talking a great deal, or tending to talk too much.|His loquaciousness made it difficult to get a word in edgewise. 他的喋喋不休让我很难插上一句话。|近义词：talkativeness, garrulity, volubility|反义词: taciturnity, reticence|noun

loquacious:/loʊˈkweɪʃəs/| adj. |多话的，健谈的|Tending to talk a great deal; talkative.|The loquacious barber kept up a steady stream of chatter while he cut my hair.  健谈的理发师在我理发时不停地唠叨。|近义词：talkative, garrulous, voluble|反义词: taciturn, reticent|adjective
