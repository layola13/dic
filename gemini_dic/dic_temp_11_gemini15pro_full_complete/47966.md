deciduousness:/dɪˈsɪdʒuːəsnəs/| n. | 落叶性；脱落性 | The quality of being deciduous; the shedding of leaves annually.  The deciduousness of the trees in the park makes for a beautiful display of color in the fall. 公园里树木的落叶性在秋天构成了一幅美丽的彩色景象。 | Synonyms: caducity, defoliation | Antonyms: evergreenness, persistence | noun

deciduous:/dɪˈsɪdʒuːəs/| adj. | 落叶的；脱落的 | (of a tree or shrub) shedding its leaves annually.  Oak trees are deciduous. 橡树是落叶树。 |  Synonyms: leaf-shedding, shedding | Antonyms: evergreen, persistent | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deciduus,  from decidere  "to fall off," from de- "down" + cadere "to fall."
