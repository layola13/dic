drainages:/ˈdreɪnɪdʒɪz/
n.
排水系统，排水，污水
The system of drains and pipes that carry away sewage and waste water.  The process of draining something.
The city's drainage system is outdated and needs to be replaced. 这个城市的排水系统已经过时了，需要更换。
Heavy rain caused flooding due to poor drainage.  大雨导致排水不良而引发洪水。
drainage systems, drainage ditches, sewage systems, runoff, outflow
inflow, blockage
noun
