jobber:/ˈdʒɒbər/ (British) /ˈdʒɑːbər/ (American)| n.| 零工; 批发商; 证券经纪人; 做零工的人|Someone who does jobs of various kinds, especially small or temporary jobs.  A wholesaler, especially one who deals in odd lots of goods or specializes in a particular line of merchandise. A stockbroker who buys and sells securities for other brokers or dealers.|例句：He worked as a jobber for a construction company. 他在一家建筑公司做零工。

例句：The jobber supplied the small shops with their goods.  这个批发商向小商店供应货物。|近义词：worker, contractor, wholesaler, middleman, broker|反义词: employer, retailer, client|词性:noun
