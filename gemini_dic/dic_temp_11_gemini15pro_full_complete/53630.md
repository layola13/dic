discernible:/dɪˈsɜːrnɪbl/ /dɪˈsɝːnəbl/
adjective
可辨别的；可识别的
able to be recognized or understood
例句：The improvement in his health was barely discernible. 他的健康状况略有好转。

例句：There is a discernible difference between the two versions of the software. 这两个版本的软件之间存在明显的差异。

近义词：perceptible, noticeable, detectable, apparent, evident, visible, obvious
反义词：imperceptible, invisible, undetectable, inconspicuous
词性: adjective
