canopy:/ˈkænəpi/| n.|天篷；遮篷；树冠层|A covering, usually of fabric, supported on poles or suspended above a bed, throne, etc., or a similar covering over an outdoor area or entrance.|The market stalls were sheltered by a striped canopy.  市场摊位上方有条纹遮阳篷遮挡着。|The dense canopy of the rainforest shielded the forest floor from the sun. 稠密的雨林树冠遮蔽了阳光，使森林地面阴暗。|synonyms: awning, covering, roof, shade, shelter|antonyms: base, floor, bottom|noun

canopy:/ˈkænəpi/| v.|用天篷遮盖；覆盖|To cover with or as if with a canopy.|The trees canopied the path, creating a tunnel of green.  树木覆盖了小路，形成了一条绿色的隧道。|Clouds canopied the sky. 云层覆盖了天空。|synonyms: cover, overspread, shade, shelter|antonyms: expose, uncover|verb
