hypersocially:/ˌhaɪpərˈsoʊʃəli/ | adv. | 过度社交地，超社交地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive social interaction, often online. | He was behaving hypersocially on the forum, posting dozens of times a day. 他在论坛上表现得过度社交，每天发几十个帖子。| She's been hypersocially active on social media lately, constantly updating her status. 她最近在社交媒体上非常活跃，不断更新她的状态。| 近义词: excessively sociable, overly gregarious | 反义词: antisocial, withdrawn | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
"hyper-" (Greek): over, above, beyond
"social" (Latin: socialis): pertaining to companionship or allies
"-ly" (Old English: -lice): adverbial suffix
