lido:/ˈliːdoʊ/ (US), /ˈliːdəʊ/ (UK)| n.|露天游泳池；（尤指海边的）公共户外游泳池|An open-air swimming pool, especially one by the sea.|We spent the afternoon at the lido. 我们下午在露天游泳池度过。|The lido was crowded with sunbathers. 露天泳池挤满了日光浴者。|近义词：swimming pool, outdoor pool, bathing area|反义词: indoor pool|词性:名词
