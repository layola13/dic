glaciations:/ˌɡleɪsiˈeɪʃənz/
n.
冰川作用;冰河时期
a process or period of time during which glaciers covered a large part of the earth's surface
The last ice age involved several glaciations.  最后一次冰河时期包含数个冰川作用期。
The Earth has experienced several glaciations throughout its history. 地球在其历史上经历了多次冰川作用。

近义词:glacial periods, ice ages
反义词:interglacial periods
