elastical:/ɪˈlæstɪkəl/| adj.|弹性的，有弹力的|Having the property of elasticity; springy; rebounding readily.|The elastical band snapped back into place.  弹性带迅速弹回原位。|例句：The material is highly elastical and can be stretched to twice its original length.  这种材料弹性很好，可以拉伸到其原始长度的两倍。

例句：The elastical properties of the rubber made it perfect for the ball. 橡胶的弹性使其成为制作球的完美材料。|近义词：elastic, resilient, flexible, springy|反义词:rigid, stiff, inflexible|词性:adjective
词源: From Late Latin *elasticus, from Latin elasticus (“propelling, driving forward”), from Ancient Greek ἐλαστικός (elastikós, “elastic, driving, propelling”), from ἐλαύνω (elaúnō, “I drive, set in motion”).
