nonplanetary:/ˌnɑːnˈplænɪˌteri/
adjective
非行星的
Not related to or characteristic of a planet.
例句：
Nonplanetary objects, such as asteroids and comets, also orbit the sun.
非行星物体，例如小行星和彗星，也绕太阳运行。

例句：
The research focused on nonplanetary bodies within the solar system.
这项研究的重点是太阳系内的非行星体。

近义词：extraplanetary, non-planetary
反义词：planetary

词性: adjective
