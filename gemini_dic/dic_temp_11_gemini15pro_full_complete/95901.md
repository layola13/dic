incredulously:/ɪnˈkrɛdʒələsli/| adv.|怀疑地，不相信地|In a manner indicating disbelief or skepticism.|He stared at her incredulously, unable to believe what she was saying. 他怀疑地盯着她，不敢相信她所说的话。|例句：She looked at him incredulously. 她怀疑地看着他。|近义词：disbelievingly, skeptically, doubtfully|反义词：believingly, trustingly|词性:副词
incredulous:/ɪnˈkrɛdʒələs/| adj.|怀疑的，不相信的|Not willing or able to believe something; skeptical.|She gave him an incredulous look. 她给了他一个怀疑的眼神。|例句：He was incredulous at her story. 他不相信她的故事。|近义词：disbelieving, skeptical, doubtful|反义词：believing, trusting, credulous|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *incredulus, from in- (“not”) + credulus (“believing, trusting”), from credere (“to believe”).
