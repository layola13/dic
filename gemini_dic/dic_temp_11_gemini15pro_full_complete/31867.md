cheerio:/ˈtʃɪəriəʊ/ (UK), /ˈtʃɪrioʊ/ (US)| int. n.|再见；干杯；（用作表示感谢的感叹词） 谢谢；|Used as an informal way of saying goodbye. Also used as an exclamation of thanks.|例句：Cheerio, see you next week! 再见，下周见！

例句：Cheerio, and thanks for everything. 再见，感谢你所做的一切。

近义词：goodbye, bye, bye-bye, so long, ta-ta (chiefly British)|反义词:hello, hi|词性:interjection, noun


cheerio:/ˈtʃɪəriəʊ/ (UK), /ˈtʃɪrioʊ/ (US)| n.|（早餐吃的）脆麦圈；|A small crisp piece of toasted or fried dough, typically eaten for breakfast.|例句：He had a bowl of cheerios for breakfast. 他早餐吃了一碗脆麦圈。

例句：She prefers cheerios with milk and honey. 她喜欢加牛奶和蜂蜜的脆麦圈。

近义词：cereal, breakfast cereal|反义词:None (Not applicable for this sense)|词性:noun


词源:  A shortening of "cheerioh," an alteration of "cheer ho," expressing cheer.  Ultimately from "cheer" + "ho," both exclamations of joy or encouragement.
