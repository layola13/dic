lobe:/loʊb/ (US) /ləʊb/ (UK)| n. | 叶; 肺叶; 耳垂 | A rounded projection or division, especially of an organ such as the brain, lung, or liver. | The frontal lobe of the brain is responsible for higher cognitive functions.  大脑的额叶负责高级认知功能。| synonyms: segment, section, division | antonyms: whole, entirety | noun

lobe:/loʊb/ (US) /ləʊb/ (UK)| n. | (尤指植物的)裂片 | A rounded projection that is part of a larger structure, particularly in plants. | The maple leaf has five distinct lobes. 枫叶有五个明显的裂片。| synonyms: projection, protuberance | antonyms: indentation, recess | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *lobus, from Ancient Greek λοβός (lobós, “pod, husk, lobe”).
