constipated: /ˈkɑːnstɪpeɪtɪd/ /ˈkɒnstɪpeɪtɪd/
adj.
便秘的；拥挤的
Affected with constipation; unable to defecate easily.  Figuratively, slow or blocked.
例句：I've been feeling constipated for a few days. 我这几天一直感觉便秘。

例句：The traffic was constipated due to the accident.  由于事故，交通拥堵。
近义词：blocked, bunged up (informal), bound up (informal)
反义词：loose (bowels)
词性：adjective


constipate: /ˈkɑːnstɪpeɪt/ /ˈkɒnstɪpeɪt/
verb
使便秘
To cause constipation.
例句：Certain medications can constipate you. 某些药物会让你便秘。

例句：A lack of fiber in your diet can constipate you.  饮食中缺乏纤维会让你便秘。
近义词：clog, obstruct
反义词：loosen
词性：verb


词源: From Latin constipatus, past participle of constipare (“to press closely together, cram, condense”), from com- (“together”) + stipare (“to press, pack”).
