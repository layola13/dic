expose:/ɪkˈspəʊz/ (UK) /ɪkˈspoʊz/ (US)| v. n.|暴露，揭露，使曝光; 暴露，揭发|To reveal or unmask something hidden, or to leave something unprotected from the elements.|例句：The newspaper exposed the corruption within the government. 这家报纸揭露了政府内部的腐败。|近义词：reveal, uncover, disclose, unveil|反义词: conceal, hide, protect|verb

expose:/ɪkˈspəʊz/ (UK) /ɪkˈspoʊz/ (US)| n.|暴露，揭露|The act of exposing or the state of being exposed.|例句：The expose of the politician's affair ruined his career. 对这位政治家婚外情的揭露毁了他的职业生涯。|近义词：revelation, disclosure|反义词: cover-up, concealment|noun
