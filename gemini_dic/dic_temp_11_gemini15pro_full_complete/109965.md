liberator:/ˈlɪbəreɪtər/| n.|解放者|A person who liberates a person or place, especially from imprisonment or foreign occupation.|例句：The liberators were greeted with cheers and tears of joy. 解放者受到了欢呼和喜悦的泪水的迎接。

例句：He is considered a liberator of the oppressed. 他被认为是被压迫人民的解放者。|近义词：emancipator, rescuer, deliverer, savior|反义词：oppressor, conqueror, enslaver|词性:noun
