minefield:/ˈmaɪnˌfiːld/ | n. | 雷区；危险地带；棘手问题 | A situation or subject that is full of hidden dangers or difficulties. |  例句：The Middle East is a political minefield. 中东是一个政治雷区。


例句：Negotiating a new contract can be a real minefield.  谈判一份新合同可能是一个真正的雷区。


近义词：danger zone, hazardous area, tricky situation | 反义词：safe zone, secure area | 词性：名词
