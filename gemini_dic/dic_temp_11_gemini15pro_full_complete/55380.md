distemperately:/dɪsˈtɛmpərɪtli/
adv.
放肆地，无节制地，不理性地
in a distempered or immoderate manner; excessively; without restraint.
He behaved distemperately, shouting and throwing things around the room.  他举止放肆，在房间里大喊大叫，乱扔东西。
近义词:immoderately, excessively, intemperately, unrestrainedly
反义词:moderately, temperately, reasonably, calmly
