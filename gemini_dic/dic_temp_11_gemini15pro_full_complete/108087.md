lateward:/ˈleɪtwərd/| adj.|迟的，晚的；后来的；最近的|Referring to a relatively late period; posterior.  Relating to the latter part of a period of time or a series of events.|The lateward shift at the factory was particularly difficult. 工厂的后半夜班特别难熬。|近义词：later, latter, subsequent, following|反义词：early, prior, former|词性:形容词

词源:  late + -ward.  "Late" meaning occurring at a relatively advanced time. "-ward" is a suffix indicating direction, in this case towards the later part.
