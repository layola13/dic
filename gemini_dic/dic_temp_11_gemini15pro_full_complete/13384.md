atole:/ɑːˈtoʊleɪ/ (US), /əˈtɔːleɪ/ (UK)| n. |阿托莱，玉米粥|A hot corn-based beverage originating in Mesoamerica, made from masa (corn dough), water, and often spices, sweeteners, or other flavorings.|例句：My grandmother makes delicious atole every morning.  我祖母每天早上都做美味的阿托莱。

例句：Atole is a comforting drink on a cold day. 在寒冷的日子里，阿托莱是一种令人感到舒适的饮料。|近义词：gruel, porridge, champurrado (when chocolate is added)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
