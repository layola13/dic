bacteria:/bækˈtɪəriə/ | n. | 细菌 |A member of a large group of unicellular microorganisms that have cell walls but lack organelles and an organized nucleus, including some that can cause disease. | Example 1: The bacteria in the yogurt are beneficial to your gut health.  益生菌对你的肠道健康有益。

Example 2:  Harmful bacteria can cause infections. 有害细菌会引起感染。 | Synonyms: microbe, germ, microorganism | Antonym: none (bacteria are a broad category, not having a direct opposite) | Noun
