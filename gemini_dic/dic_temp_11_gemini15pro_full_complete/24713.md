brontosaurus:/ˌbrɒntəˈsɔːrəs/ (US) /ˌbrɒntəˈsɔːrəs/ (UK)| n.|雷龙|A huge herbivorous dinosaur of the late Jurassic period, now called Apatosaurus.  It was formerly thought to be a distinct genus. |The brontosaurus roamed the earth millions of years ago.  雷龙数百万年前在地球上漫游。|Synonym: Apatosaurus|Antonym: N/A|noun
