loricate:/ˈlɔːrɪkeɪt/ /ˈlɒrɪkeɪt/| adj.| 甲壳状的，披甲的| Covered or protected with scales or plates like a lorica (a cuirass or corselet of leather or metal scales).| The loricated lizard scurried under a rock.  披甲蜥蜴 scurried 在一块岩石下。| The knight's loricated armor protected him from harm.  骑士的甲胄保护他免受伤害。|近义词：armored, scaled, plated|反义词: unprotected, vulnerable|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin *loricatus*, past participle of *loricare* "to clothe in mail," from *lorica* "cuirass, corselet."
