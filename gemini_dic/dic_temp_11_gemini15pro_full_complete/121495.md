misleadingness:/ˌmɪsˈliːdɪŋnəs/| n.|误导性；欺骗性|The misleadingness of the advertisement caused many customers to complain. 这则广告的误导性导致许多顾客投诉。|Example: The misleadingness of the advertisement caused a lot of confusion.  这则广告的误导性造成了很多困惑。
Example: The study exposed the misleadingness of the company's claims. 这项研究揭露了该公司声明的误导性。|近义词：deceptiveness, fallaciousness, deceptiveness|反义词：accuracy, truthfulness, honesty|词性:名词

Etymology: From mislead (v.) + -ing (suffix forming present participles and adjectives) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Ultimately from Old English mislædan "to misdirect," from mis- "badly, wrongly" + lædan "to lead."
