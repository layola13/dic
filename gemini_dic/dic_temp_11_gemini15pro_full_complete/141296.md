open-hearted:/ˌoʊpənˈhɑːrtɪd/| adj.|慷慨的，热诚的，坦率的|Kind, generous, and frank in one's affections; warm and sympathetic.|She greeted everyone with an open-hearted smile. 她用热情的微笑迎接每一个人。|Synonyms: generous, kind, warm, sympathetic, welcoming, hospitable, charitable, altruistic, benevolent, philanthropic|Antonyms: cold, unfeeling, mean, selfish, unkind, inhospitable, uncharitable|adjective

open-hearted:/ˌoʊpənˈhɑːrtɪd/| adj.|无偏见的，没有成见的|Free from prejudice or bias.|He took an open-hearted approach to understanding different cultures.  他以一种没有成见的方式去理解不同的文化。|Synonyms: unbiased, unprejudiced, tolerant, accepting, open-minded, impartial, objective, fair|Antonyms: prejudiced, biased, intolerant, narrow-minded, bigoted|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From open +‎ hearted.  "Open" in the sense of receptive and "hearted" relating to one's emotions and feelings.
