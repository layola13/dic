laemodipoda:/ˌliːməˈdɪpədə/| n. | 薄甲目 | An order of small crustaceans, commonly known as whale lice, that are ectoparasites of cetaceans. |  Whale lice, belonging to the order Laemodipoda, are often found on whales. 薄甲目，也就是鲸虱，通常寄生在鲸鱼身上。 | synonym: whale lice | antonym: N/A | noun
