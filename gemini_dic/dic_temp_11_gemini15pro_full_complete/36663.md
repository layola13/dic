colleterium:/kɒlɪˈtɪəriəm/ | n. | 粘液腺，胶腺 | A glandular structure in some insects, especially bristletails and cockroaches, that secretes a sticky substance used for various purposes such as capturing prey or forming protective coverings for eggs. |  The colleterium of the female cockroach secretes a substance that forms the ootheca. 雌性蟑螂的胶腺分泌一种物质，形成卵鞘。|  The bristletail uses its colleterium to capture small insects.  毛刷虫用它的粘液腺捕捉小昆虫。 | synonyms: gland, secretory organ | antonyms: N/A | noun
