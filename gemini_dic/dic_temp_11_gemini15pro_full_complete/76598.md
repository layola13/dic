galliot: /ˈɡæliət/ | n. | 荷兰平底货船，一种小型帆船 | A small, fast sailing ship, especially one used in the Mediterranean.  Specifically, a Dutch cargo vessel with a broad beam, flat bottom, and rounded bows. | The galliot sailed swiftly across the calm waters. 这艘荷兰平底货船平稳地航行在平静的水面上。| The merchant's galliot was laden with spices from the East. 商人的荷兰平底货船满载着来自东方的香料。 | synonym: sloop, ketch, barge | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps) ocean liner, warship | noun
