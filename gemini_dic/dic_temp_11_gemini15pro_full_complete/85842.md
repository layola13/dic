hci:/ˌeɪtʃ siːˈaɪ/ | n. | 人机交互 | Human-Computer Interaction, a multidisciplinary field of study focusing on the design and use of computer technology, focused on the interfaces between people (users) and computers. | HCI researchers observe the ways in which humans interact with computers and design technologies that let humans interact with computers in novel ways. | 近义词: user interface design, interaction design, usability engineering | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps) machine-machine interaction | 词性: noun
