lingerer:/ˈlɪŋɡərər/| n.|逗留者；徘徊者|Someone who stays in a place longer than necessary.|The lingerers at the museum were asked to leave.  博物馆里的逗留者被要求离开。|近义词：loiterer, dawdler|反义词: leaver, goer|词性:noun
lingering:/ˈlɪŋɡərɪŋ/| adj.|拖延的；迟迟不去的；挥之不去的|Lasting for a long time or slow to end.|She gave him a lingering kiss. 她给了他一个迟迟不去的吻。|近义词：remaining, persistent, enduring|反义词: fleeting, brief, ephemeral|词性:adjective
linger:/ˈlɪŋɡər/| v.|逗留；徘徊；磨蹭；缓慢消失|Stay in a place longer than necessary.|The smell of smoke lingered in the air. 烟味在空气中久久不散。|近义词：loiter, tarry, remain, persist|反义词: leave, depart, vanish|词性:verb
