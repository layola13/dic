mistressless: /ˈmɪstrəsləs/
adjective
无女主人的；无情妇的；无女教师的
Without a mistress; having no mistress.  This term can refer to a household, a school, or a similar situation where a female figurehead is typically present.
The mistressless house felt empty and cold.  无女主人的房子感觉空荡荡的，很冷清。
The mistressless school was in chaos.  没有女校长的学校一片混乱。
近义词: ownerless, unmanaged, unsupervised
反义词: mistress-led, mistress-run
词性: adjective
