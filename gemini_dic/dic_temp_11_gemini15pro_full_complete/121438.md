misintended:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛndɪd/
adjective
误解的，曲解的
Having an unintended meaning or effect; misunderstood.
The misintended comment caused a lot of confusion. 这句被误解的话引起了很多混乱。
She apologized for her misintended remark. 她为她被误解的言论道歉。
近义词:misunderstood, misconstrued, misinterpreted
反义词:intended, meant, purposeful
词性:adjective

misintend:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛnd/
verb
误解，曲解
To understand or interpret (something) wrongly.
I think you misintend my words. 我想你误解了我的话。
He misintended her silence as agreement. 他误以为她的沉默是同意。
近义词:misunderstand, misconstrue, misinterpret
反义词:intend, mean
词性:verb
