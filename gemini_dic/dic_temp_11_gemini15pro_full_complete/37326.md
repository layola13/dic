commemorativenesses:/kəˌmɛməˈreɪtɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
纪念性； 纪念意义
The quality of being commemorative; serving to commemorate something.
The commemorativenesses of the monument reminded everyone of the sacrifices made.  这座纪念碑的纪念意义提醒着每个人所做的牺牲。
The various commemorativenesses of the museum's exhibits made it a powerful experience. 博物馆展品的各种纪念意义使其成为一次强烈的体验。
近义词:remembrance, commemoration
反义词:forgetfulness, oblivion
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From commemorate + -ive + -ness + -es.
Commemorate comes from the Latin commemorare, meaning "to call to mind, remember."
