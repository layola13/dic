foretell:/fɔːrˈtel/ /fɔːrˈtɛl/| v. |预言，预测|To predict or say what will happen in the future.|例句：The fortune teller foretold a long and prosperous life for her. 算命先生预言她会长寿而且富裕。

例句：No one could have foretold the sudden change in the weather. 没有人能预料到天气的突然变化。

近义词：predict, forecast, prophesy, foresee, anticipate|反义词: - |词性:动词
