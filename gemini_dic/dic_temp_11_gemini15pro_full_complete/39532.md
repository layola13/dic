conquests:/ˈkɒŋkwests/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋkwests/ (US)| n.|征服，战胜；征服的土地/地区；战利品|The conquests of Alexander the Great changed the course of history. 亚历山大大帝的征服改变了历史的进程。|例句：
The Roman conquests extended throughout much of Europe. 罗马帝国的征服遍及欧洲的大部分地区。
His latest conquest is a beautiful actress. 他最新的征服对象是一位漂亮的女演员。|近义词：victories, triumphs, acquisitions|反义词:defeats, losses, failures|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *conqueste*, from Latin *conquaestus*, past participle of *conquerere*, meaning "to seek diligently, gain, win, subdue."
