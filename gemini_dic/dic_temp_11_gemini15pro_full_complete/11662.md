armistice:/ˈɑːrmɪstɪs/ | n. |停战，休战 |An agreement made by opposing sides in a war to stop fighting for a certain time; a truce. |The two countries signed an armistice after years of conflict. 这两个国家在多年的冲突后签署了停战协议。|Synonym: truce, ceasefire |Antonym: war, hostility |noun
