friaries:/ˈfraɪəriz/
n.
男修道院，修士团
A building or buildings occupied by a community of friars.
The Franciscan friaries were often located in towns and cities.  方济各会的男修道院通常位于城镇中。
The king ordered the dissolution of the friaries. 国王下令解散所有的男修道院。
近义词: monasteries, convents
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: noun
