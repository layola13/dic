constructure: /kənˈstrʌktʃər/
n.
构建; 构造; 构想; 观念
An abstract or general idea inferred or derived from specific instances.  A mental representation.
Example:  He developed a complex constructure of the universe based on his research. 他根据自己的研究构建了一个复杂的宇宙观。

Example: Her constructure of the ideal family didn't match the reality she experienced. 她对理想家庭的构想与她所经历的现实并不相符。

Synonym: concept, framework, model, schema, notion
Antonym: reality, actuality, concrete, fact
词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *constructura, from constructus, past participle of construere "to heap up, build," from com- "together" + struere "to pile up."
