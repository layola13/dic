optimize:/ˈɒptɪmaɪz/ (British) /ˈɑːptɪmaɪz/ (American)| vt.|优化；最佳化|to make something as good as possible|例句：We need to optimize our website for mobile devices. 我们需要优化我们的网站以适应移动设备。

例句：The company optimized its production process to increase efficiency. 公司优化了生产流程以提高效率。

近义词：improve, enhance, refine, perfect, maximize|反义词：worsen, impair, degrade, minimize|词性：动词
