dentiform:/ˈdɛntɪfɔːrm/
adj.
齿状的，牙形的
Shaped like a tooth.
The dentiform leaves of the plant are unique.  这种植物的齿状叶子很独特。
近义词: tooth-shaped, toothlike
反义词:  (No clear antonym, perhaps "smooth" or "rounded" in certain contexts)
词性: adjective
