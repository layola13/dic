huntsmanship:/ˈhʌntsmənˌʃɪp/| n. |狩猎技术；猎师的技艺|The duke was renowned for his superb huntsmanship. 公爵以其高超的狩猎技术而闻名。| Huntsmanship involves more than just killing game; it encompasses respect for nature and ethical treatment of animals.  狩猎技术不仅仅是猎杀猎物；它还包括尊重自然和对动物的道德对待。|近义词：hunting skill, hunting prowess,  venery|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) disregard for wildlife, cruelty to animals|词性:noun

Etymology:
From "huntsman" + "-ship." "Huntsman" comes from Old English "hunta" (hunter) + "mann" (man).  "-ship" is a suffix denoting skill, state, or quality.
