apeshit:/ˈeɪpʃɪt/| adj. | 疯狂的，暴怒的 | Extremely angry, excited, or agitated. Origin: 1950s, American slang, from ape (suggesting uncontrolled behavior) + shit (intensifying the sense of extremity). |例句：He went apeshit when he found out his car had been stolen. 他发现他的车被偷了，气得暴跳如雷。|例句：The crowd went apeshit when the band played their hit song. 当乐队演奏他们的热门歌曲时，人群变得疯狂起来。|近义词：furious, enraged, livid, ballistic, bananas |反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil | 词性: 形容词
