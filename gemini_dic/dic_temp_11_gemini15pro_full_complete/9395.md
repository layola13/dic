antiques:/ænˈtiːks/
noun
古董，古玩
objects, furniture, and other items that are old and valuable, especially those considered to be works of art or of historical interest
My grandmother collects antiques. 我的祖母收藏古董。
synonyms: relics, artifacts, vintage items, heirlooms
antonyms: modern pieces, contemporary items
