marrowy:/ˈmærəʊi/ (UK) /ˈmæroʊi/ (US)| adj.|多骨髓的；充满精髓的；|Full of marrow; pithy; rich in essential elements.  Relating to or resembling marrow.|The marrowy bones of the oxtail made a rich broth. 牛尾的多骨髓骨头熬制出了浓郁的肉汤。|Synonyms: pithy, substantial, rich, meaty|Antonyms: insipid, bland, insubstantial|adjective
