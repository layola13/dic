amphoral:/ˈæmfərəl/| adj.|双耳细颈椭圆形的，坛形的|Shaped like an amphora; a tall, two-handled jar with a narrow neck used for storing wine or oil in ancient Greece and Rome.|The museum displayed several amphoral vases from the ancient world.  博物馆展出了几个来自古代的双耳细颈椭圆形花瓶。|The archaeologist unearthed an amphoral vessel containing ancient olive oil. 考古学家挖掘出一个装有古代橄榄油的双耳细颈椭圆形容器。|近义词：vase-shaped, jar-shaped|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *amphoralis, from Latin amphora, from Greek amphoreus (ἀμφορεύς).  Ultimately from amphi- (ἀμφί-) "on both sides" + phoreus (φορεύς) "bearer," from pherein (φέρειν) "to carry."
