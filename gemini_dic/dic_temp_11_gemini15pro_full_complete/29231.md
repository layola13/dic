cassandra:/kəˈsændrə/ | n. |卡桑德拉|A female given name, derived from Greek mythology. Cassandra was a Trojan princess cursed to utter true prophecies but never be believed. |Cassandra warned the Trojans about the dangers of the Trojan Horse, but no one listened. 卡桑德拉警告特洛伊人关于特洛伊木马的危险，但没有人听。| 近义词: prophetess, seer | 反义词: liar, deceiver | noun
