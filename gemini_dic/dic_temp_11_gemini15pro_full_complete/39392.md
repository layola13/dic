conjure-up:/ˈkʌndʒər ʌp/ | phrasal verb | 使想起；想象出；变出 | To create or bring something into existence as if by magic; to cause something to appear in someone's mind | The magician conjured up a rabbit out of his hat. 魔术师从帽子里变出一只兔子。 |  She conjured up images of her childhood. 她想起了童年的景象。 | evoke, summon, create, imagine | banish, dispel | verb

conjure:/ˈkʌndʒər/ | verb |  变戏法；使想起；恳求 | To perform magic tricks; to make something appear as if by magic; to implore or earnestly request | The magician conjured a dove out of thin air. 魔术师凭空变出一只鸽子. | The smell of baking bread conjured up memories of my grandmother's kitchen. 烤面包的味道让我想起了祖母的厨房。 |  He conjured them to help him. 他恳求他们帮助他。 | evoke, summon, implore | banish, dispel | verb
