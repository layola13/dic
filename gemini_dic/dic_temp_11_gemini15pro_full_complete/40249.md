continental:/ˌkɑːntɪˈnɛntl/ | adj. n.|大陆的，大陆性的；欧洲大陆人|Relating to or characteristic of a continent.  A person from mainland Europe.|
例句：
The continental climate is characterized by large temperature differences between summer and winter.
大陆性气候的特点是夏季和冬季温差很大。

They prefer a continental breakfast.
他们更喜欢欧式早餐。

近义词：mainland, European (when referring to people)|
反义词：insular, island|
词性：adjective, noun
