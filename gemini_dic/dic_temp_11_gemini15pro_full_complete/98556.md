instinctively:/ɪnˈstɪŋktɪvli/
adverb
本能地；直觉地
Acting on an inner impulse or natural tendency, without conscious thought.
She instinctively knew something was wrong. 她本能地感觉到有什么不对劲。
The cat instinctively chased the mouse. 猫咪本能地追赶老鼠。
近义词:automatically, intuitively, spontaneously, naturally
反义词:consciously, deliberately, rationally
词性:adverb

词源: From instinct + -ive + -ly.  "Instinct" comes from Late Latin instinctus "impulse, instigation," from past participle stem of instinguere "to incite, impel," from in- "on, upon" + stinguere "to prick, incite."
