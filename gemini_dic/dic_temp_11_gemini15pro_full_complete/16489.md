barged:/bɑːrdʒd/
|v.|闯入， barge的过去式和过去分词|To enter a place rudely or forcefully, interrupting what someone else is doing.|He barged into the meeting without knocking. 他没敲门就闯进了会议室。|近义词：burst,  intrude, interrupt, storm|反义词:leave, exit|verb


barged:/bɑːrdʒd/
|adj.|驳船运输的，满载的，（非正式）笨重的|Carried by barge; heavily loaded; (informal) clumsy or unwieldy.|The barged goods arrived late. 通过驳船运输的货物晚到了。 (This usage is less common and may sound archaic.)|近义词：laden, loaded, bulky|反义词:light, empty|adjective (less common)
