disputable:/dɪˈspjuːtəbl/ /dɪˈspjʊtəbl/
adjective
有争议的；可争辩的
Open to argument or debate; not definitely true.
例句：It's disputable whether he was the first person to do this.  他是否是第一个这样做的人是有争议的。

例句：The facts of the case are not disputable.  这个案件的事实是无可争辩的。

近义词：debatable, arguable, questionable, contestable
反义词：indisputable, undeniable, irrefutable
词性：adjective

词源：From Late Latin *disputabilis, from Latin disputare "to discuss, debate," frequentative of disputare "to reckon, calculate, discuss," from dis- "apart" + putare "to think."
