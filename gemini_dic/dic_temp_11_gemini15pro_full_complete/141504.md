capitulation: /kəˌpɪtʃʊˈleɪʃən/
n.|（原则、信仰等的）放弃|His capitulation to their demands angered his supporters. 他对他们要求的让步激怒了他的支持者。|concession, yielding|resistance, defiance|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *capitulationem, accusative of *capitulatio (“agreement, surrender”), noun of action from past-participle stem of capitulari (“to draw up in chapters, arrange conditions of surrender”), from Latin capitulum, diminutive of caput (“head”).
