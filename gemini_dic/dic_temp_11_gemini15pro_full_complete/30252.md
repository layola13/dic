cellulosity:/ˌsɛljuˈlɒsɪti/ | n. |纤维素含量，纤维素性质|The degree to which something is composed of cellulose.  The state or quality of being cellulosic.|The cellulosity of the paper makes it strong and durable.  这种纸的纤维素含量使其坚固耐用。|Synonyms: cellulose content, fiber content|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like lignin content for contrasting components of plant material)|Noun
