embalmment:/ɪmˈbɑːm.mənt/ |n.|防腐处理，尸体保存|The process of preserving a dead body from decay, usually by treating it with chemicals.|The embalmment of the pharaoh was a complex ritual.  法老的防腐处理是一个复杂的仪式。|Synonyms: preservation, mummification|Antonyms: decay, decomposition|noun
