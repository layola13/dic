imprints:/ˈɪmprɪnts/| n. |印记，痕迹，印象|A mark made by pressing something onto a softer substance; a lasting effect or influence.|The horse's hooves left imprints in the soft mud. 马蹄在柔软的泥土上留下了印记。|synonyms: marks, impressions, traces, vestiges|antonyms: blanks, erasures|noun
imprints:/ɪmˈprɪnts/| v. |印，压印，铭刻|To mark or impress (something) onto a surface or into a substance; to fix (an idea, image, etc.) firmly in someone's mind.|The experience was imprinted on her memory. 这段经历铭刻在她的记忆中。|synonyms: stamps, engraves, etches, instills|antonyms: erases, obliterates|verb
