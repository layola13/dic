beseech:/bɪˈsiːtʃ/
verb
恳求；哀求
To ask (someone) urgently and fervently to do something; implore; entreat.
He beseeched her to stay, but she refused. 他恳求她留下，但她拒绝了。
近义词: implore, entreat, beg, plead
反义词: demand, command
词性: verb

beseech:/bɪˈsiːtʃ/
vt.
恳求；哀求
To ask (someone) urgently and fervently to do something; implore; entreat.
She beseeched the king for mercy. 她恳求国王开恩。
