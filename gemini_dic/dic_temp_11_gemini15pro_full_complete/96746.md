ineloquent:/ɪnˈɛləkwənt/
adjective
不善言辞的，表达不清的
not fluent or persuasive in speech or writing
Her ineloquent speech failed to convey the urgency of the situation. 她笨拙的演讲未能传达情况的紧迫性。

His ineloquent response made him appear unprepared. 他不善言辞的回答让他显得毫无准备。

近义词:inarticulate, tongue-tied, uncommunicative
反义词:eloquent, articulate, fluent
词性:adjective
