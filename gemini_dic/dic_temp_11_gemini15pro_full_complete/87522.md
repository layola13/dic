heterogamous:/ˌhɛtərˈɒɡəməs/ | adj. |异配的，异型交配的 | Relating to or characterized by heterogamy; relating to a type of reproduction in which the gametes, or reproductive cells, are of different sizes. | Example:  The heterogamous nature of the species leads to distinct male and female roles.  该物种的异配性导致了明显的雄性和雌性角色。

Example:  Many algae exhibit heterogamous reproduction. 许多藻类表现出异型交配繁殖。 | 近义词: anisogamous | 反义词: isogamous | 词性: adjective
