incompatiblenesses:/ˌɪnkəmˌpætɪˈbɪlɪtɪz/| n. |不兼容性，不相容性|The state of being incompatible; inability to exist or be used together.|There were numerous incompatiblenesses between the two software systems, making integration difficult.|近义词：inconsistencies, conflicts, clashes, discrepancies|反义词：compatibilities, harmonies, consistencies|词性:名词

Etymology:
From incompatible + -ness.  Incompatible is from in- (not) + compatible (able to exist or be used together).  Compatible is from Late Latin compatibilis, from com- (together) + pati (to suffer, endure) + -bilis (able).  The original sense of "compatible" was "able to suffer together."
