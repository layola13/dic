graduation:/ˌɡrædʒuˈeɪʃn/| n.|毕业，毕业典礼|The successful completion of a course of study or training, often marked by a ceremony; the marking off of a scale or series of measures.|Her graduation ceremony was held in June. 她的毕业典礼在六月举行。|The graduation on the thermometer is very precise. 温度计上的刻度非常精确。|近义词：commencement, completion|反义词：matriculation|词性:名词
