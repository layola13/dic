eurostrategic:/ˌjʊərəstrəˈtiːdʒɪk/ | adj. |欧洲战略的|Relating to the strategic military considerations of European countries, especially during the Cold War, concerning nuclear weapons and the balance of power.|  The eurostrategic balance was a key concern during the Cold War.  欧洲战略平衡是冷战期间的一个关键问题。|  The deployment of Pershing II missiles in Europe was a eurostrategic decision. 在欧洲部署潘兴II型导弹是一个欧洲战略决策。 |近义词: geopolitical, strategic |反义词: tactical|词性:形容词

Etymology: euro- (combining form meaning "European") + strategic.  "Euro-" comes from Greek "Europa." "Strategic" comes from Greek "stratēgia" (generalship).
