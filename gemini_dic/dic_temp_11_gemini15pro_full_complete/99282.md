intercontradiction:/ˌɪntərˌkɑːntrəˈdɪkʃən/ | n. |相互矛盾，内在矛盾 |A situation or statement characterized by inherent contradictions within itself.| The politician's speech was filled with intercontradiction, making it difficult to understand his stance. 这位政治家的演讲充满了自相矛盾之处，很难理解他的立场。|例句：His theory is riddled with intercontradiction. 他的理论充满了内在矛盾。

例句：The witness's testimony was full of intercontradiction, leading the jury to question his credibility.  证人的证词充满了自相矛盾，导致陪审团质疑他的可信度。

|近义词：inconsistency, paradox, contradiction, conflict|反义词: consistency, agreement, harmony |词性: 名词
