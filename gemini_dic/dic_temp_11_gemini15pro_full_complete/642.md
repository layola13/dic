abreuvoir:/ˌɑːbrəˈvwɑːr/ (US), /əˈbreɪvwɑː(r)/ (UK)| n.|饮水槽，水坑，自动饮水器|A watering place for animals, especially livestock.  A trough or basin supplied with fresh water for animals to drink.|The farmer filled the abreuvoir with fresh water for the cows.|Synonym: watering trough, water tank, drinking fountain (for animals)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "dry trough" in a specific context)|noun
