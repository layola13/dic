fattenable:/ˈfætənəbl/
adjective
可肥育的; 可养肥的
Capable of being fattened; able to gain weight or become plump.
The farmer bought a dozen fattenable piglets.  农场主买了一打可以养肥的小猪。
The land is fattenable and good for raising cattle. 这片土地适合育肥，很适合养牛。
近义词:plumping, thickening
反义词:lean, thin
词性:adjective


fatten:/ˈfætən/
verb
养肥；使肥沃
1. To make (an animal) fat or fatter, especially for slaughter.
2. To make (something) richer or more productive.
The farmer fattened the pigs for market. 农场主为了上市而养肥了猪。
Manure helps to fatten the soil. 肥料有助于使土壤肥沃。
近义词:enrich, nourish, plump
反义词:emaciate, starve, thin
词性:verb
