attainder:/əˈteɪndər/| n.|剥夺公民权利; 腐败玷污|The act of attainting; the forfeiture of all civil rights by a person convicted of treason or felony.  A stain or corruption.|例句：His attainder meant he lost all his property and titles.  他的剥夺公民权意味着他失去了所有的财产和头衔。

例句：The attainder of the former CEO cast a shadow over the entire company. 前任首席执行官的腐败玷污给整个公司蒙上了阴影。|近义词：forfeiture, confiscation, corruption, stigma|反义词:absolution, pardon|词性:noun
