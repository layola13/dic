如果你是想了解疑问词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what /wɒt; hwɒt/ (US) /wʌt; hwʌt/
|pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information about something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|  synonyms: which, how, why | antonyms: N/A | pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么认为？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is she doing right now? = 她现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? = 你学到了什么？
What has he eaten today? = 他今天吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing yesterday at 5 pm? = 昨天下午五点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before he arrived? = 在他到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before the dinner party? = 晚宴前她吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? = 在我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? = 下学期她会学什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学了什么多久了？



PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/你好吗？
What about...? = ...怎么样？/ ...怎么办？
What if...? = 如果...会怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？
So what? = 那又怎样？
