indeliberated: /ˌɪndɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪd/
adjective
未经深思熟虑的，仓促的
Not carefully thought out or planned; done without deliberation.
He made an indeliberated remark that he later regretted. 他说了一句未经深思熟虑的话，后来后悔了。
Near Synonyms:  impulsive, rash, hasty, unpremeditated, spontaneous, offhand, extemporaneous
Antonyms: deliberate, planned, premeditated, considered, thoughtful
词性: adjective
