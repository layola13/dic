justices:/ˈdʒʌstɪsɪz/
n.
法官；司法；正义；合理
Judges of the Supreme Court are called justices. 最高法院的法官被称为大法官。
The justices of the peace were responsible for local administration.治安法官负责地方行政。
Synonyms: judges, magistrates, fairness, righteousness, right
Antonyms: injustice, unfairness, wrong
