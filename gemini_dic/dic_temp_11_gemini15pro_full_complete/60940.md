elevations:/ˌɛləˈveɪʃənz/| n.|海拔；高度；上升；高地；崇高；(尤指道德、智力或文化素质的)提高|An increase in height above sea level or ground level.  A raised area or a place higher than the surrounding area.  An increase in the level of something, especially in moral, intellectual, or cultural quality.|例句：The elevations of the mountains range from 5,000 to 8,000 feet. 这些山脉的海拔高度从5000英尺到8000英尺不等。

例句：The building site is at an elevation of 2000 meters. 建筑工地海拔2000米。|近义词：heights, altitudes, rises,  uplifts, improvements, enhancements|反义词:depressions, descents, declines|词性:noun
