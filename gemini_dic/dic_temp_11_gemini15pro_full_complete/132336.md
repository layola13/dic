nondefunct:/ˌnɑːndɪˈfʌŋkt/
adjective
未倒闭的；仍在运作的
Not having ceased to exist or live; still in existence or operation.
The company, though struggling, is nondefunct and continues to operate. 尽管这家公司举步维艰，但它仍然存在并且继续运营。
Near Synonyms: extant, surviving, operating, functioning, existing
Antonyms: defunct, extinct, nonexistent, obsolete
词性: adjective
