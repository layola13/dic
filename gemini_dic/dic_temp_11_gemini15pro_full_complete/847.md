abstraction:/æbˈstrækʃən/ /əbˈstrækʃn/| n.|抽象, 抽象概念, 抽象化, 心不在焉|The process of considering something independently of its associations, attributes, or concrete accompaniments.  The act of removing or separating something.|His theory is an abstraction from his observations. 他的理论是从他的观察中抽象出来的。|Synonyms: concept, idea, notion, generalization, idealization|Antonyms: concretion, specification, particularization|noun

abstraction:/æbˈstrækʃən/ /əbˈstrækʃn/| n.|提取, 抽取|The act of taking something away, especially something non-material.|The abstraction of nitrogen from the air. 从空气中提取氮。|Synonyms: removal, extraction, separation, withdrawal|Antonyms: addition, insertion, inclusion|noun
