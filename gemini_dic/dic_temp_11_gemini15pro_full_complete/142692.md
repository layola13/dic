other fish to fry:/ˈʌðər fɪʃ tə fraɪ/ | idiom | 有更重要的事情要做；另有要事 |  This means having more important or pressing matters to attend to.  | I can't help you with that right now; I have other fish to fry. 我现在不能帮你做那件事，我还有其他更重要的事情要做。 |  I've got other fish to fry at the moment. 我现在有其他更重要的事情要处理。 | more important things to do, other priorities | None | idiom
