deacon:/ˈdiːkən/ (US) /ˈdiːkən/ (UK)| n.|执事，助祭|A deacon is a member of the clergy, ranking below a priest, in some Christian churches.  A deacon assists the priest in various duties, including preaching and administering certain religious rites.|He was ordained a deacon last year. 他去年被任命为执事。|The deacon assisted the priest during the service. 执事在仪式中协助神父。|synonyms: church officer, clergyman, ecclesiastic|antonyms: layman, layperson|noun
