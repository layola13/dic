crisper:/ˈkrɪspər/| n. |保鲜盒，保鲜贮菜器; 脆片盒; 蔬果保鲜盒|A container, drawer, or compartment, often refrigerated, for keeping fruits and vegetables crisp. |例句：I put the lettuce in the crisper to keep it fresh. 我把生菜放在保鲜盒里保鲜。

例句：The crisper drawer in my refrigerator is broken. 我冰箱里的保鲜盒抽屉坏了。|近义词：vegetable drawer, produce drawer, refrigerator drawer|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "drying rack" if focusing on opposite function)|词性: noun
