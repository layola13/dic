improvisers:/ˈɪmprəvaɪzərz/
n.
即兴表演者，即兴创作的人
People who create and perform something spontaneously, without preparation.
The jazz improvisers were incredibly talented, creating beautiful melodies on the spot.  这些爵士乐即兴演奏者才华横溢，当场创作出优美的旋律。
Synonyms: ad-libbers, extemporizers,  performers
Antonyms: planners, preparers
