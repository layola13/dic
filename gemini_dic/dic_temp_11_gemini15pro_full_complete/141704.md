orabassu:/ˌɔːrəˈbɑːsuː/ | n. | 巴巴苏油 | A pale yellow, waxy oil extracted from the seeds of the orabassu palm (Attalea speciosa), native to South America, used in cosmetics and as a cooking oil. |  Orabassu oil is a sustainable alternative to palm oil. 巴巴苏油是棕榈油的可持续替代品。|Synonyms: babassu oil, cusi oil | Antonyms: N/A | noun

orabassu:/ˌɔːrəˈbɑːsuː/ | n. | 巴巴苏棕榈 | The palm tree (Attalea speciosa) from which orabassu oil is extracted, native to South America. | The orabassu palm thrives in the humid tropics. 巴巴苏棕榈在潮湿的热带地区生长茂盛。 | Synonyms: babassu palm, cusi palm | Antonyms: N/A | noun
