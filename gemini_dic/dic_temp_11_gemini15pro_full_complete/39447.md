connectively:/kəˈnɛktɪvli/
adverb
连接地，连贯地
in a connected manner; in a way that forms a connection or relationship.
The chapters are connectively linked by recurring themes. 章节通过反复出现的主题连接在一起。
近义词: jointly, unitedly, coherently
反义词: separately, disjointedly, incoherently
词性: adverb

词源:
From connect + -ive + -ly.  Ultimately from the Latin "connectere" meaning "to bind together".
