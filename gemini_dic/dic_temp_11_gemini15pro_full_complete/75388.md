frowy:/ˈfraʊi/ | adj. | 霉臭的；闷气的；有霉味的 | Musty; stale, having a stale smell. | The frowy attic smelled of dust and old clothes.  阁楼里霉味很重，一股灰尘和旧衣服的味道。 |  例句：The old books in the library had a frowy smell.  图书馆里的旧书有一股霉味。

例句：The room was dark and frowy, with a distinct lack of fresh air. 房间里又黑又闷，明显缺乏新鲜空气。


近义词：musty, stale, stuffy, fusty | 反义词：fresh, airy, fragrant | 词性：形容词
