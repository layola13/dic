circuit:/ˈsɜːrkɪt/ (US), /ˈsɜːkɪt/ (UK)| n. |电路；环形线路；巡回；赛车道|A complete path through which an electric current can flow.  A route that goes in a circle, ending where it began. A regular series of places visited or traveled to, especially by a judge or preacher. A track for motor racing. |The circuit board was fried. 电路板烧坏了。|Synonyms: loop, circle, route, track, course|Antonyms: line, straight|noun


circuit:/ˈsɜːrkɪt/ (US), /ˈsɜːkɪt/ (UK)| v. |绕...环行|To go around something in a circle.|The plane circuited the airport before landing. 飞机在降落前绕机场盘旋了一圈。|Synonyms: circle, circumnavigate, orbit|Antonyms:  -|verb
