epiplastron:/ˌepɪˈplæstrən/| n.|腹甲前叶，上腹甲|A large bony plate in the plastron of certain turtles and tortoises, situated anterior to the entoplastron.|The epiplastron of this fossil turtle is remarkably well-preserved. 这块龟化石的腹甲前叶保存得非常完好。|Synonym: preplastron|Antonym: hypoplastron|noun
