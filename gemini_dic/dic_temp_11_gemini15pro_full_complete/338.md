abhorrency:/əˈbɔːrənsi/
n.
憎恶，厌恶
A feeling of extreme repugnance or disgust.
He looked at the rotting food with abhorrency. 他厌恶地看着腐烂的食物。
abhorrence, detestation, loathing, aversion, repugnance
liking, fondness, affection, love
noun
