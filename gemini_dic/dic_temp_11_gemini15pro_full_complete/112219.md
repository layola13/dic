loopy:/ˈluːpi/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻的；疯狂的；古怪的|crazy or silly|例句：She had a loopy grin on her face. 她脸上带着傻笑。

例句：He came up with some loopy ideas. 他想出了一些疯狂的想法。|近义词：crazy, silly, foolish, wacky, eccentric|反义词：sensible, rational, sane|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From loop (n.) + -y (adj. suffix).  Originally referring to something full of loops, the meaning extended to "dizzy, giddy" and then to "crazy, silly."
