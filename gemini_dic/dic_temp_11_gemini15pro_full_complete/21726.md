boarish:/ˈbɔːrɪʃ/ (US) /ˈbɔːrɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|粗鲁的；像野猪的；粗野的；贪婪的|Resembling a boar in coarseness, rudeness, or gluttony.|He behaved in a boarish manner, pushing and shoving his way to the front of the line. 他表现得很粗鲁，推推搡搡地挤到了队伍的最前面。

The boarish behavior of some of the guests was quite shocking. 一些客人粗鲁的行为令人震惊。

|近义词：rude, uncouth, vulgar, crass, insensitive, swinish|反义词：polite, courteous, refined, cultivated, considerate|词性:形容词
