judicialize:/dʒuːˈdɪʃəlaɪz/
verb
司法化；使司法化；诉诸司法
To make something (especially a political issue) into a legal matter; to bring something under the jurisdiction of a court of law.
The union threatened to judicialize the dispute over wages. 工会威胁要将工资纠纷诉诸司法。
Synonyms: litigate, legalize
Antonyms: decriminalize, deregulate
词性: 动词
