nonprofession:/ˌnɒn.prəˈfeʃ.ən/
n.|非专业，非职业|A nonprofession is an activity or occupation that is not considered a profession.  It usually lacks the specialized knowledge, training, and formal qualifications associated with professional fields.|Many people engage in nonprofession activities like gardening or knitting in their free time.|近义词：avocation, hobby, pastime|反义词：profession, vocation|词性:名词

nonprofessional:/ˌnɒn.prəˈfeʃ.ən.əl/
adj.|非专业的，不熟练的|Not related to or characteristic of a profession; amateurish.|His nonprofessional approach to the project led to many problems.|近义词：amateur, unprofessional, unskilled|反义词：professional, skilled, expert|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + profession.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Profession" comes from the Old French "profession" and ultimately from the Latin "professio," meaning "a public declaration" or "avowal."
