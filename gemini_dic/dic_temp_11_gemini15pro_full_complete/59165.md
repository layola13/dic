dyscrasy: /dɪˈskreɪzi/ /dɪsˈkreɪzi/ | n. |失调，紊乱|An abnormal state of the body or of a bodily part, especially arising from the imbalance of its constituent elements.|The doctor diagnosed a blood dyscrasy. 医生诊断出血液失调。|Near synonyms: disorder, imbalance, malfunction, abnormality|Antonyms: balance, equilibrium, harmony|noun
