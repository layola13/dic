malawians: /məˈlɔːwiənz/
noun
马拉维人
People from Malawi, a country in southeastern Africa.
Malawians are known for their warmth and hospitality. 马拉维人以热情好客而闻名。
Many Malawians rely on agriculture for their livelihood. 许多马拉维人的生计依赖农业。
