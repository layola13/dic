connective:/kəˈnɛktɪv/ | adj. n. |连接的，连接词 |Serving or tending to connect; connecting.  A word or morpheme that connects other words, phrases, clauses, or sentences.| The bridge serves as a connective between the two towns.这座桥是连接两个城镇的纽带。 | synonyms: joining, linking, uniting | antonyms: disconnecting, separating, dividing | 词性: adjective/noun

connective:/kəˈnɛktɪv/ | n. |连接词 | A word or morpheme that connects other words, phrases, clauses, or sentences.| "And," "but," and "or" are common connectives. “和”、“但是”和“或”是常见的连接词。| synonyms: conjunction, link, tie | antonyms: disjunction | 词性: noun
