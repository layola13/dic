nonleprously:/ˌnɒnˈlɛprəsli/
| adv. |无麻风地；非麻风病地|In a manner not characteristic of leprosy; without signs of leprosy.  It describes an action or state that is free from the symptoms or characteristics of leprosy.| He walked nonleprously, showing no signs of the disease. 他走路的姿态显示他没有麻风病。|Near Antonyms: leprously|Antonyms: N/A (The direct antonym would imply having leprosy, which would be insensitive and potentially stigmatizing.)
