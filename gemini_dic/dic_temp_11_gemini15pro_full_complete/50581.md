derailer:/diˈreɪlər/| n.|脱轨器；使人偏离正轨的人或事|Something that causes a train to leave its tracks; something that causes a plan or process to fail. |The investigation focused on a possible derailer placed on the tracks.  调查的重点是轨道上可能放置的脱轨器。

The constant interruptions were a major derailer to our project.  不断的干扰是我们项目的主要障碍。|近义词：obstacle, impediment, disruption, saboteur|反义词：support, aid, assistance|词性:noun
