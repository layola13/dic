chrestomathic:/ˌkrɛstəˈmæθɪk/ | adj. |选集的；汇编的；适于入选读本的|Relating to or characteristic of a chrestomathy (a collection of literary passages, especially one compiled for the study of a language).  Suitable for inclusion in a chrestomathy.|This chrestomathic work gathers the most representative poems of the Romantic era. 这部选集汇集了浪漫主义时期最具代表性的诗歌。|Synonyms:anthological, compiled, collected|Antonyms:original, uncollected, dispersed|adjective


chrestomathy:/ˌkrɛsˈtɒməθi/ | n. |选集；文选|A collection of literary passages, especially one compiled for the study of a language.|The professor recommended a chrestomathy of medieval literature for the course. 教授推荐了一本文选中世纪文学选集作为课程教材。|Synonyms:anthology, compilation, collection|Antonyms:single work, individual piece|noun
