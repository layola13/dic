fly-tip:/ˈflaɪtɪp/ | n. & v. | 乱丢垃圾；非法倾倒垃圾 | The act of illegally dumping rubbish, often on public land or in other people's property. |  Example (n.): Fly-tipping is a serious environmental problem. 乱丢垃圾是一个严重的环境问题。

Example (v.):  They were caught fly-tipping old furniture by the roadside.  他们被抓到在路边乱丢旧家具。| 近义词: illegal dumping,  dumping, littering | 反义词: proper waste disposal, recycling | 词性: noun, verb

Example: The council is cracking down on fly-tipping in the area.  市政局正在严厉打击该地区的乱倒垃圾行为。| 近义词: illegal dumping,  dumping, littering | 反义词: proper waste disposal, recycling | 词性: noun



ETYMOLOGY->

From "fly" (in the sense of "on the sly," i.e., secretly) + "tip" (in the sense of "dump" or "unload"). This usage of "tip" likely originated from tipping rubbish from a cart.
