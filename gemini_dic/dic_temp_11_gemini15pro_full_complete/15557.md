bagnio:/ˈbænjəʊ/ (UK), /ˈbænioʊ/ (US)| n. |浴室；澡堂；妓院|A public bath or bathing house; a brothel.  Historically, bagnios were often associated with both bathing and prostitution.|He frequented the local bagnio, more for the company than the cleanliness. 他经常光顾当地的澡堂，与其说是为了干净，不如说是为了陪伴。|The bagnio was a place of ill repute. 这家澡堂名声不好。|Synonyms: bathhouse, sauna, brothel, bordello|Antonyms: church, library|noun

bagnio:/ˈbænjəʊ/ (UK), /ˈbænioʊ/ (US)| n. |监狱；拘留所|A prison or place of confinement.|He was thrown into the bagnio for his crimes. 他因犯罪被投入监狱。|The conditions in the bagnio were deplorable.  监狱里的条件很糟糕。|Synonyms: jail, prison, lockup|Antonyms: freedom, liberty|noun
