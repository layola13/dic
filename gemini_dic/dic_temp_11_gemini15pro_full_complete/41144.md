copublishes:/ˌkoʊˈpʌblɪʃɪz/
verb
共同出版
publishes something jointly with another publisher or organization.
Example: The university copublishes a journal with a commercial press.  大学与一家商业出版社共同出版了一份期刊。
Example:  They copublished a series of books on contemporary art. 他们共同出版了一系列关于当代艺术的书籍。
Synonyms: joint publish, co-publish
Antonyms:  solely publish, independently publish
词性: verb
