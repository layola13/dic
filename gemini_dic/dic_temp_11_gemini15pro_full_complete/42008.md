coster:/ˈkɒstə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːstər/ (US)| n. |叫卖小贩；沿街叫卖的水果蔬菜商|A person who sells goods, especially fruit and vegetables, from a barrow in the street or market. |例句：The coster arranged his apples attractively on his barrow.  叫卖小贩把苹果诱人地摆放在他的手推车上。

例句：The coster's loud cries advertised his fresh produce. 叫卖小贩大声吆喝，宣传他的新鲜农产品。|近义词：vendor, hawker, peddler, street seller|反义词: buyer, customer|词性:noun
