misperforms:/ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrmz/ /ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrmz/
verb
执行不佳，表现欠佳
To perform poorly or below expectations.
The company misperformed last quarter due to supply chain issues.  由于供应链问题，该公司上个季度业绩不佳。
The investment fund misperformed compared to the market benchmark.  与市场基准相比，该投资基金表现不佳。
Synonyms: underperforms, falters, fails, disappoints
Antonyms: excels, succeeds, thrives, outperforms
词性: 动词
