households:/ˈhaʊshəʊldz/ (UK) /ˈhaʊshˌoʊldz/ (US)| n.| 家庭，户;|A household refers to all the people living together in a single dwelling, regardless of whether they are related.  |The government sent a survey to 1,000 households. 政府向1000户家庭发送了一份调查问卷。

The average household income has increased. 平均家庭收入有所增加。|近义词：families, homes |反义词: individuals| noun
