carnassa: /kɑːrˈnæsə/ | n. |  屠杀，大屠杀 |  A great slaughter; a massacre.  Derived from Italian *carnassa*, from *carne* "flesh" + *-assa* (augmentative suffix).  | The carnassa left the battlefield strewn with bodies.  战场上尸横遍野，惨不忍睹。 | synonyms: massacre, slaughter, carnage, butchery | antonyms: peace, truce, amnesty | noun
