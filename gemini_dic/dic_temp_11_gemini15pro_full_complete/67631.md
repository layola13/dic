extremisms: /ɪkˈstrimɪzəmz/
| n. | 极端主义，极端行为 | The holding of extreme political or religious views; fanaticism. |  Extremisms of all kinds must be condemned. 各种极端主义都必须受到谴责。 | 近义词: radicalism, fanaticism, fundamentalism | 反义词: moderation, tolerance | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *extremus, from Latin exter 'outer'.  The suffix -ism denotes a doctrine, belief, or practice.
