harker:/ˈhɑːrkər/ (US), /ˈhɑːkə/ (UK)|n.|倾听者，注意者；侍从，侦察员|Someone who listens attentively or keeps watch; an attendant or scout.|The harker strained to hear the faint sound in the distance. 倾听者努力想听到远处微弱的声音。| synonyms: listener, observer, scout, attendant|antonyms: speaker, announcer, broadcaster|noun
