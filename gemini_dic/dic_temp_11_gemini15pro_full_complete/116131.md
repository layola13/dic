mary:/ˈmɛri/| n.|玛丽|A female given name, derived from Maria, the Latin form of the Greek Μαρία (María), which was in turn probably derived from the Egyptian name Mry.  It is of uncertain ultimate etymological origin, possibly related to Hebrew מרים (Miriam).|Mary had a little lamb. 玛丽有一只小羊羔。|近义词: Maria (as a related form),  No direct synonyms for names.|反义词: N/A (Names generally don't have antonyms).|n.
