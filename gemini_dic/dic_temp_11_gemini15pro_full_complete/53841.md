disconsolations:/dɪsˌkɒnsəˈleɪʃənz/
n.
悲痛，伤心事，无法慰藉的事情
A state of being deeply unhappy and unable to be comforted.
Her disconsolations were evident in her tear-stained face. 她的脸上满是泪痕，显然非常悲伤。
The team's loss brought a wave of disconsolations to the fans. 球队的失利给球迷带来了巨大的悲伤。
近义词: grief, sorrow, sadness, distress, anguish
反义词: comfort, consolation, solace, joy, happiness
词性: 名词(plural noun)

词源:
From Late Latin disconsolationem, accusative of disconsolatio, from dis- ‘not’ + consolatio ‘consolation’.
