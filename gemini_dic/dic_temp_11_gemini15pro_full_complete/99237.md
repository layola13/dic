intercombining:/ˌɪntərˈkəmˌbaɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 互相结合，互相穿插 | The act or process of combining or merging with each other. | The intercombining of different art forms created a unique performance. 不同艺术形式的互相结合创造了一场独特的表演。|  interweaving, interlacing, intertwining | separation, detachment | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (prefix meaning "between, among") + combine + -ing (present participle suffix). "Combine" comes from the Latin "combinare," meaning "to join two by two, to unite."
