heimer:/ˈhaɪmər/| suffix|（姓氏后缀）|A German surname suffix, often associated with Jewish families. It doesn't have a direct English translation as a stand-alone word.  It forms part of surnames and indicates familial descent. |Example: Oppenheimer is a surname ending in -heimer. 奥本海默是一个以-heimer结尾的姓氏。|近义词: N/A (Suffixes don't have synonyms)|反义词: N/A (Suffixes don't have antonyms)|词性 suffix
