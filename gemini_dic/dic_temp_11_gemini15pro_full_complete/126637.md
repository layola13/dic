naissant:/ˈneɪsənt, nɛˈsɑ̃/| adj.|初生的，新兴的|Beginning to exist or develop; coming into being.|The nascent democracy struggled to establish itself.  这个新生的民主政体艰难地确立自己的地位。|例句：The internet provided fertile ground for nascent businesses. 互联网为新兴企业提供了肥沃的土壤。|近义词：emerging, budding, developing, incipient|反义词：declining, dying, mature, established|词性:形容词

Etymology: From French *naissant*, present participle of *naître* "to be born," from Latin *nasci* "to be born."
