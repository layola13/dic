inobservant:/ˌɪnəbˈzɜːrvənt/ | adj. | 不善于观察的；不留心的；疏忽的 | Not good at noticing things; failing to pay attention. |  He was so inobservant that he didn't even notice the elephant in the room. 他太不留心了，甚至没有注意到房间里的大象。

| Example 1: She's quite inobservant and often misses important details. 她很不细心，经常错过重要的细节。

| Example 2:  Because he was so inobservant, he failed to see the car coming. 因为他太粗心了，没有看到汽车开来。

| Synonym: unobservant, unmindful, heedless, oblivious, unaware | Antonym: observant, attentive, mindful, alert, vigilant | 词性: 形容词
