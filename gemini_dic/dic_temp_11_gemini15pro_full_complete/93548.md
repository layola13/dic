imaginatively:/ɪˈmædʒɪnətɪvli/ | adv. | 想象力丰富地，富有创造性地 | In a way that uses imagination or shows imagination. | She described the scene imaginatively, bringing it to life for her listeners. 她富有想象力地描述了这个场景，使之在听众面前栩栩如生。| creatively, inventively, resourcefully | dully, unimaginatively, plainly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From *imaginative* + *-ly*.
*imaginative* comes from Late Middle English *imaginatif*, from Old French *imaginatif*, from Late Latin *imaginativus*, from Latin *imaginari* "to imagine," from *imago* "image."
