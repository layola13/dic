apologete:/əˈpɒləˌdʒiːt/ | n. | 辩护者，护教者 | A person who offers an argument in defense of something controversial. |  He was a staunch apologete for the new economic policy. 他是这项新经济政策的坚定辩护者。|  Example: The author, a known apologete for vegetarianism, presented a compelling case for eliminating meat from our diets. 作者是知名的素食主义辩护者，他提出了一个令人信服的案例，呼吁我们停止食用肉类。|Synonyms: defender, advocate, champion, proponent | Antonyms: critic, opponent, detractor | noun


apologete:/əˈpɒləˌdʒiːt/ | n. | 辩护者，护教者 | Someone who speaks or writes in defense of a cause, especially a religious doctrine. |  He became an apologete for Christianity after a profound spiritual experience.  在一次深刻的灵性体验后，他成为了基督教的护教者。|  Example:  Early Christian apologetes sought to defend their beliefs against Roman persecution. 早期的基督教护教者试图捍卫他们的信仰以对抗罗马人的迫害。 |Synonyms: defender, advocate, champion, proponent | Antonyms: critic, opponent, detractor | noun
