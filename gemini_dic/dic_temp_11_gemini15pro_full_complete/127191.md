natures:/ˈneɪtʃərz/
|n.|性质，本性，种类|The inherent qualities or characteristics of something or someone; the essential character of a person or thing; a type, kind, or class of things.|例句：The nature of the problem is complex. 这个问题的性质很复杂。|近义词：characteristics, essence, type, kind, sort, character, disposition|反义词：nurture (sometimes considered an antonym in the "nature vs. nurture" debate)|词性:名词

natures:/ˈneɪtʃərz/
|n.|本性，天性|The basic or inherent features of something, especially when seen as characteristic of it.|例句：It's not in his nature to be unkind.  他不天生刻薄。|近义词：character, disposition, temperament, personality|反义词：artificiality|词性:名词
