overgloom: /ˌoʊvərˈɡluːm/ | n. | 忧郁的氛围；阴暗的笼罩 | A feeling of melancholy or depression that pervades a place or situation. | A sense of overgloom settled on the town after the factory closed. 工厂倒闭后，小镇笼罩着一片忧郁的气氛。|  The overgloom in the house was palpable after the death of their pet. 宠物死后，房子里的忧郁气氛 palpable.| 近义词：gloom, melancholy, depression, despondency | 反义词：cheerfulness, joy, happiness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From 'over-' (meaning "excessively," "completely") + 'gloom' (from Old English 'glom,' meaning "twilight, dusk").
