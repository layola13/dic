descriptor:/dɪˈskrɪptər/ | n. | 描述符，描述子 | A word or phrase that describes something or someone.  A descriptor is a label, often a keyword, that categorizes and describes an item or piece of information, making it easier to search and retrieve. | The scientist used several descriptors to classify the newly discovered plant species.  科学家用几个描述符来对新发现的植物物种进行分类。 | synonyms: label, tag, identifier, keyword, attribute | antonyms:  N/A (Descriptive words don't typically have direct antonyms) | noun
