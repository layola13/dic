denigrators:/ˈdɛnɪˌɡreɪtərz/
n.
诽谤者，诋毁者
People who criticize or belittle someone or something unjustly.
The denigrators of modern art often fail to understand its nuances.  现代艺术的诋毁者往往无法理解它的细微之处。
近义词:critics, detractors, slanderers, backbiters
反义词:supporters, admirers, proponents, champions
词性:noun

denigrate:/ˈdɛnɪˌɡreɪt/
v.
诋毁，诽谤
To criticize unfairly; belittle.
He was quick to denigrate anyone who disagreed with him. 他很快就会诋毁任何与他意见不合的人。
近义词:belittle, disparage, deprecate, defame
反义词:praise, commend, laud, extol
词性:verb


词源:
From Late Latin *denigrāre, from Latin de- + nigrāre (“to blacken”), from niger (“black”).
