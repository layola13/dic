overmaster:/ˌoʊvərˈmæstər/| vt.|制服，压倒，征服|To overpower or overwhelm completely; to subdue or conquer.|His anger overmastered him and he lashed out. 他的愤怒压倒了他，他猛烈抨击。|近义词：overpower, overwhelm, subdue, conquer, vanquish|反义词: surrender, yield, submit|verb

overmaster:/ˌoʊvərˈmæstər/| adj.(古用法，罕见)|压倒性的，不可抗拒的|Extremely powerful or compelling; irresistible.|an overmastering urge to escape  一种难以抗拒的逃离冲动|近义词：overpowering, overwhelming, compelling, irresistible|反义词: weak, feeble|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + master (v.).  "Over-" acts as a prefix intensifying the meaning of "master," implying complete domination or control.
