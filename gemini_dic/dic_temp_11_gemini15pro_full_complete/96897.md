inexperience:/ˌɪnɪkˈspɪəriəns/ | n. |缺乏经验，不熟练|Lack of experience; lack of knowledge or skill resulting from lack of experience.|His inexperience made him nervous during the job interview. 他的缺乏经验让他在面试时感到紧张。|Synonyms: rawness, greenness, immaturity, unfamiliarity|Antonyms: experience, expertise, skill, proficiency|noun

inexperienced:/ˌɪnɪkˈspɪəriənst/ | adj. |缺乏经验的，不熟练的|Lacking experience; not knowledgeable or skilled as a result of a lack of experience.|The inexperienced hiker got lost in the woods.  没有经验的徒步旅行者在树林里迷路了。|Synonyms: raw, green, immature, unskilled|Antonyms: experienced, expert, skilled, proficient|adjective
