misjudges:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒɪz/
|v.|错误地判断；误判|To form an incorrect opinion or conclusion about someone or something.|She misjudged the distance and drove off the road. 她错误地判断了距离，把车开下了路。|
|
|He misjudged her character completely. 他完全误判了她的性格。|
|
|Synonyms: miscalculate, underestimate, overestimate, misread, misinterpret|
|
|Antonyms: judge correctly, assess accurately, understand properly|
|Verb|
