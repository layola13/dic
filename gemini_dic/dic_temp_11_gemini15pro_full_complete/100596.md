initiate:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪt/ /ɪˈnɪsiˌeɪt/| vt.  n.|开始，创始；发起；接纳；启蒙|To begin or cause something to begin. To formally admit someone into an organization or group, often through a ceremony. To teach someone about something, or to introduce someone to a skill or activity. |例句：The government initiated a new program to combat poverty. 政府启动了一项新的扶贫计划。|近义词：begin, start, commence, launch, inaugurate, induct, introduce|反义词:terminate, end, conclude, finish|词性:verb, noun

initiate:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪt/ /ɪˈnɪsiˌeɪt/| n.|新加入组织的人|A person who has been formally admitted into an organization or group. |例句：The initiates were sworn into secrecy.  新加入的人被要求宣誓保密。|近义词：newcomer, beginner, novice, tyro|反义词:veteran, expert|词性:noun
