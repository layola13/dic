bossed:/bɒst/ /bɑːst/ | v. | 支配，指挥，发号施令 | To act as the boss of; to control or supervise. |  She bossed the team around, making sure everyone knew their tasks. 她对团队发号施令，确保每个人都知道自己的任务。

The foreman bossed the construction workers. 工头指挥建筑工人。| 近义词：managed, supervised, directed, controlled, commanded, ordered, dictated | 反义词：obeyed, followed, submitted | 词性: 动词

bossed:/bɒst/ /bɑːst/ | adj. |  (非正式) 被控制的，受支配的 | Controlled or dominated by someone. |  He felt bossed around by his older sister. 他觉得被姐姐控制着。

She hated feeling bossed and wanted more independence. 她讨厌被控制的感觉，想要更多独立性。 | 近义词：controlled, dominated, managed | 反义词：independent, autonomous, free | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the noun "boss," which originated in Dutch "baas" meaning "master."
