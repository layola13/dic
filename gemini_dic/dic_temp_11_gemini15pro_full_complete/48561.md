defamous:/ˈdefəməs/ | adj. | 诽谤的，中伤的 | (of a statement) falsely and injuriously harmful to someone's reputation | The article was full of defamous allegations. 这篇文章充满了诽谤性的指控。 | synonyms: libelous, slanderous, calumnious, derogatory, insulting | antonyms: complimentary, flattering, laudatory | adjective

Etymology:
Late Middle English: from Old French diffamoux, dif fameus, based on Latin diffamare ‘spread abroad’, from dis- ‘expressing negation’ + fama ‘reputation’.
