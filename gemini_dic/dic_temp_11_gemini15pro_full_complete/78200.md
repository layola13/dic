geotic:/dʒiˈɑːtɪk/ | adj. |生命的，生物的，与生物有关的| Relating to life or living organisms; biotic. | The geotic environment includes all living things.  生物环境包括所有生物。| The researchers studied the geotic factors influencing the ecosystem. 研究人员研究了影响生态系统的生物因素。| 近义词: biotic, living, organic | 反义词: abiotic, nonliving, inorganic | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek *gē* (earth) + *-ōtikos* (suffix forming adjectives).  来自希腊语 *gē*（地球）+ *-ōtikos*（构成形容词的后缀）。
