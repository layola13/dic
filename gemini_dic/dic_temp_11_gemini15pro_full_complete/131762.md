nonconjugal: /ˌnɒnkɒnˈdʒuːɡəl/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnkɑːnˈdʒuːɡəl/ (US)| adj.|非婚姻的，非夫妻的|Not related to marriage or the relationship between husband and wife; not having sexual relations within marriage.|They lived in a nonconjugal relationship. 他们生活在非夫妻关系中。|The study focused on nonconjugal families. 这项研究的重点是非婚家庭。|近义词：unmarried, single, extramarital|反义词：conjugal, marital|词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + conjugal.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "conjugal" comes from the Latin word "conjugalis," meaning "of marriage" or "pertaining to the joining together."
