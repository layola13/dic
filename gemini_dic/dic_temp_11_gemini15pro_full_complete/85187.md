harengiform:/ˌhɛrɪŋˈɪfɔːrm/ | adj. n. |鯡形目的；鯡形目鱼类 |Relating to or denoting a large order of mainly marine ray-finned fish, the Clupeiformes, which includes the herrings, sardines, pilchards, anchovies, menhadens, and shads, typically having a single soft dorsal fin and a deeply forked tail.|例句：The harengiform fish are important commercially. 鯡形目鱼类具有重要的商业价值。|例句：Herring are a classic example of a harengiform fish. 鲱鱼是鯡形目鱼类的典型例子。|近义词：clupeiform|反义词：(无明确的反义词，可根据具体语境选择例如“non-harengiform”) |词性: 形容词, 名词


词源:

From  *harengus* (Latin 'herring') + *-formis* (Latin 'shaped').
