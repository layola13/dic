indiscrimination:/ˌɪndɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪʃn/| n.|不加区别，不分皂白，滥杀滥伤|Lack of careful choice or consideration; treating all alike, often in a harmful or unfair way.|The indiscriminate bombing of the city resulted in many civilian casualties. 对该市不分皂白的轰炸造成许多平民伤亡。|近义词：bias, prejudice, partiality|反义词: discrimination, discernment, selectivity|词性:名词
