advancements:/ədˈvɑːnsmənts/
n.
进步，进展
progress or development in a particular field or area.
Recent advancements in medical technology have saved countless lives. 最近医学技术的进步拯救了无数生命。
synonyms: progress, development, improvement, breakthrough, innovation
antonyms: setbacks, declines, regressions, stagnation
noun
