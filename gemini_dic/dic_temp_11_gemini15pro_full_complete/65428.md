euphrates:/juːˈfreɪtiːz/
proper noun
幼发拉底河
A river in western Asia, one of the longest and historically most important rivers of the region. It is over 2,700 km (1,700 mi) long.
The Euphrates and the Tigris are the two main rivers of Mesopotamia.
幼发拉底河和底格里斯河是美索不达米亚的两条主要河流。
近义词:River (in this context, referring to a major river of historical significance)
