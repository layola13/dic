curtail:/kərˈteɪl/| v. |缩减，削减|To reduce in extent or quantity; to shorten.|例句：The government curtailed spending on education. 政府削减了教育经费。|例句：She had to curtail her visit because of the emergency. 由于紧急情况，她不得不缩短访问时间。|近义词：reduce, shorten, decrease, diminish, abbreviate, truncate|反义词：expand, increase, lengthen, extend, prolong, amplify|词性:动词
词源: From Old French *curtailler, from curt "short" (from Latin curtus) + -ailler (frequentative suffix).
