disclaimant:/dɪsˈkleɪmənt/
n.
放弃者，拒绝者，否认者
a person who renounces or rejects a legal claim or right
The disclaimant signed a document relinquishing all rights to the property.  放弃者签署了一份放弃所有财产权利的文件。
The disclaimant stated that he had no involvement in the crime. 否认者声称他没有参与犯罪。
近义词: renouncer, repudiator, abnegator
反义词: claimant, applicant
