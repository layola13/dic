filmstrips:/ˈfɪlmˌstrɪps/
n.
电影胶片；幻灯片；连环画片
A length of film containing a sequence of images, typically used for educational or entertainment purposes.
We watched old filmstrips about the solar system in elementary school. 我们在小学看过关于太阳系的旧幻灯片。
The museum displayed filmstrips depicting life in the 1920s.  博物馆展出了描绘 1920 年代生活的幻灯片。
synonyms: slide show, film roll, slide reel
antonyms: movie, video, digital presentation
noun
