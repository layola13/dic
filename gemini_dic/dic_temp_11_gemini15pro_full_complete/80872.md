gouty: /ˈɡaʊti/ | adj. |痛风的；似痛风的；暴躁的 | Affected with gout; resembling gout; bad-tempered, irascible. |  He walked with a slow, gouty gait. 他走路缓慢，像患了痛风。| synonyms: arthritic, inflamed, irascible, choleric | antonyms: healthy, good-natured, placid | adjective

Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French goutos, from Latin gutta ‘drop’ (the disease was thought to be caused by drops of morbid matter in the joints).
