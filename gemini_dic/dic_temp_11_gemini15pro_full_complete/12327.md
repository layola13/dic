aseptic:/eɪˈsɛptɪk/
adjective
无菌的；防腐的；冷漠的
free from contamination caused by harmful bacteria, viruses, or other microorganisms; surgically sterile; lacking emotion or warmth
The aseptic environment of the operating room is crucial for patient safety. 手术室的无菌环境对病人的安全至关重要。

The nurse prepared the aseptic bandages. 护士准备了无菌绷带。

近义词:sterile, antiseptic, hygienic, sanitized
反义词:septic, contaminated, infected
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From French aseptique, from a- "not" + Greek sēptikos "putrefying," from sēpein "to make rotten."
