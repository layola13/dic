moneron:/məˈnɪərɒn/ (US) /ˈmɒnərɒn/ (UK)| n. |原核生物，单细胞生物|A single-celled organism belonging to the kingdom Monera (a classification no longer used); a prokaryote, such as a bacterium or cyanobacterium.|Example: Monerans, like bacteria, are essential for nutrient cycling in ecosystems.  原核生物，例如细菌，对于生态系统中的营养循环至关重要。

Example:  In older biological classifications, monerans were considered a distinct group. 在较旧的生物分类中，原核生物被认为是一个独特的群体。|近义词：prokaryote|反义词: eukaryote|词性:noun
