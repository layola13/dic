kuda:/ˈkuːdə/| n.| 马来语：哪里；去哪里 |A Malay word meaning "where" or "to where".|例句：Kuda kamu pergi?  你去哪里？|近义词：mana (where in Malay)|反义词: sini (here in Malay)|名词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Kuda dia tinggal? = He lives where? 他住在哪里？
Kuda awak bekerja? = Where do you work? 你在哪里工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Dia sedang pergi kuda? = Where is he going? 他正要去哪里？
Mereka sedang pergi kuda sekarang? = Where are they going now? 他们现在要去哪里？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Kuda awak sudah pergi? = Where have you been? 你去过哪里？
Dia sudah pergi kuda? = Where has he gone? 他去了哪里？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Kuda awak sudah pergi selama ini? = Where have you been going all this time?  你这段时间一直去哪里？
Dia sudah pergi kuda sepanjang hari? = Where has he been going all day? 他整天都去哪里了？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Kuda dia pergi semalam? = Where did he go yesterday? 他昨天去了哪里？
Kuda awak pergi minggu lepas? = Where did you go last week? 你上周去了哪里？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Kuda awak pergi semasa saya telefon? = Where were you going when I called? 我打电话时你正要去哪里？
Kuda dia pergi semasa hujan? = Where was he going when it rained? 下雨时他要去哪里？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Kuda dia pergi sebelum saya tiba? = Where had he gone before I arrived? 在我到达之前他去了哪里？
Kuda awak sudah pergi sebelum makan malam? = Where had you gone before dinner? 晚饭前你去了哪里？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Kuda awak sudah pergi sebelum kemalangan itu? = Where had you been going before the accident? 事故发生前你一直要去哪里？
Kuda dia sudah pergi sepanjang hari sebelum saya jumpa dia? = Where had he been going all day before I met him? 在我见到他之前他整天都去哪里了？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Kuda awak akan pergi esok? = Where will you go tomorrow? 你明天要去哪里？
Kuda dia akan pergi minggu depan? = Where will he go next week? 他下周要去哪里？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Kuda awak akan pergi esok pada waktu ini? = Where will you be going this time tomorrow? 明天这个时候你会去哪里？
Kuda dia akan pergi semasa mesyuarat? = Where will he be going during the meeting? 会议期间他要去哪里？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Kuda awak akan pergi sebelum hujung tahun? = Where will you have gone before the end of the year? 年底之前你会去哪里？
Kuda dia akan pergi sebelum saya balik? = Where will he have gone before I return? 在我回来之前他要去哪里？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Awak akan pergi kuda selama sepuluh tahun? = Where will you have been going for ten years? 你将要去哪里十年？ (This tense is less common with "kuda")
Dia akan pergi kuda sepanjang tahun? = Where will he have been going all year? 他将要去哪里一整年？ (This tense is less common with "kuda")


虚拟语气:
Kalau saya tahu kuda hendak pergi, saya akan pergi. = If I knew where to go, I would go. 如果我知道要去哪里，我就会去。
Saya harap saya tahu kuda hendak pergi. = I wish I knew where to go. 我希望我知道要去哪里。


被动语态:  (Not applicable to "kuda")


疑问句:
Kuda? = Where? 哪里？


否定句:
Saya tak tahu kuda hendak pergi. = I don't know where to go. 我不知道要去哪里。


PHRASE->
Dari kuda? = From where? 从哪里？
Ke kuda? = To where? 去哪里？
Kuda saja? = Anywhere? 任何地方？
Entah kuda. = I don't know where. 我不知道去哪里。(Informal)
Tak kuda. = Nowhere. 没有地方。(Informal - meaning "not going anywhere")


注意事项:
"kuda" is primarily used in informal Malay conversations.  The examples provided utilize standard Malay grammar where applicable.  The usage of future perfect continuous with "kuda" is less common.
