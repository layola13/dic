chicos:/ˈtʃikoʊs/ (US), /ˈtʃiːkoʊs/ (UK)| n.|男孩们，小伙子们，孩子们（主要指男性）|A Spanish word meaning "boys," "guys," or "kids" (primarily male). It can also be used as a term of endearment.|例句：Los chicos están jugando al fútbol.  The boys are playing soccer.|近义词：muchachos (lads, guys), niños (children - gender neutral)|反义词:chicas (girls)|词性:Noun
