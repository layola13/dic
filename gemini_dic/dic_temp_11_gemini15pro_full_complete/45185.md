cuisine:/kwɪˈziːn/ | n. | 菜肴，烹饪，菜系 | A style or method of cooking, especially as characteristic of a particular country, region, or establishment. |  This restaurant is known for its French cuisine. 这家餐厅以其法国菜而闻名。

The cuisine of Thailand is known for its spicy flavors. 泰国菜以其辛辣的味道而闻名。| synonyms: cooking, gastronomy, fare | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cuisine*, meaning "kitchen," "cooking," from Old French *cuisine*, from Latin *coquina*, feminine of *coquinus* "of or for cooking," from *coquere* "to cook."
