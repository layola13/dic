anthropologize:/ˌænθrəˈpɑlədʒaɪz/ | v. |  使人类学化；从人类学的角度研究；以人类学方法处理 | To study or interpret from an anthropological point of view; to imbue with anthropological principles. | Example:  Researchers are attempting to anthropologize the effects of social media on human interaction. 研究人员正试图从人类学的角度研究社交媒体对人类互动的影响。

| Example:  The author anthropologized the cultural practices of the remote tribe. 作者用人类学方法研究了这个偏远部落的文化习俗。 | Near Synonyms: study, analyze, interpret, examine, investigate | Antonyms: ignore, disregard, neglect | Verb
