identifiers:/aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪərz/| n.|标识符，识别符|A name that identifies a variable, function, class, module, or other entity in a computer program.  A symbol or name used to represent something, especially in computing.| Example:  In Python, `x = 5` uses `x` as an identifier for the value 5. |Synonyms: names, labels, tags|Antonyms: N/A|noun
