hemipeptone:/ˌhɛmiˈpɛptəʊn/ (US) /ˌhɛmiˈpɛptəʊn/ (UK)| n. |半蛋白胨|A water-soluble product of partial protein hydrolysis, containing proteoses, peptones, and amino acids. It is used in culture media for microorganisms.|The bacteria thrived in the hemipeptone broth. 细菌在半蛋白胨肉汤中茁壮成长。|Synonyms: peptone, protein hydrolysate|Antonyms: protein|noun
