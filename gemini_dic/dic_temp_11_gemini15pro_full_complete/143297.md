outdressing:/ˌaʊtˈdrɛsɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的穿着；打扮得比…漂亮
dressing more impressively or stylishly than another person or group of people.
例句：She consistently outdresses everyone at the office. 她总是办公室里穿得最漂亮的人。

例句：He tried to outdress his rival for the promotion. 他试图在穿着上胜过他的竞争对手以获得晋升。

近义词：outshining, surpassing, eclipsing
反义词：underdressing

词性:v.
