neatherd:/ˈniːðərd/ | n. | 牧牛人；牛倌 | A person who herds cattle. |  The neatherd led his cows to the pasture. 牛倌把他的牛带到牧场。| Synonyms: cowherd, herdsman | Antonyms: None | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *nēathīrde, from *nēat ("cattle, beast") + *hīrde ("herder").  The word "neat" (meaning cattle) is also derived from the same root.
