insinuates:/ɪnˈsɪnjuːeɪts/ | v. |暗示，含沙射影地说；使潜入，使渗透|suggests or hints (something bad or reprehensible) in an indirect and unpleasant way; maneuver oneself into (a position of favor or office) by subtle and often devious means|He insinuated that I was lying. 他暗示我在撒谎。|synonyms: imply, suggest, intimate, hint|antonyms: declare, state, assert|verb
