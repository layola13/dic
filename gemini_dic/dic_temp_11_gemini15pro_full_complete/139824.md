octogenarians:/ˌɑːktəˈdʒɛnəriənz/
noun
八十多岁的人；八旬老人
People who are between 80 and 89 years old.
Many octogenarians are still active and enjoy life. 许多八十多岁的人仍然活跃并享受生活。
Octogenarians often have a wealth of life experience to share. 八旬老人通常有很多生活经验可以分享。
synonyms: octogenarian, octagenarian
antonyms: youngsters, children
n.
