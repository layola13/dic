arachnophobes:/əˈræknəˌfoʊbz/| n. |蜘蛛恐惧症患者|People who have an extreme or irrational fear of spiders are called arachnophobes. |例句：Arachnophobes often find it difficult to even look at pictures of spiders. 蜘蛛恐惧症患者通常觉得即使看蜘蛛的照片也很困难。

例句：Even the smallest spider can trigger a panic attack in some arachnophobes. 即使是最小的蜘蛛也能引发一些蜘蛛恐惧症患者的恐慌症发作。|近义词：arachnophobics|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps spider lovers/enthusiasts)|词性:Noun
