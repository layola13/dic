empath:/ˈɛmpæθ/ | n. | 共情者，移情者 | A person who has the ability to understand and share the feelings of another. |  She's a true empath, always able to sense the emotions of others. 她是一个真正的共情者，总是能够感受到别人的情绪。| 近义词：sympathizer, compassionate person | 反义词：narcissist, sociopath | 词性: noun

empathic:/ɛmˈpæθɪk/ | adj. | 共情的，有同理心的 | Showing an ability to understand and share the feelings of another. | He gave her an empathic look, understanding her pain. 他给了她一个充满同理心的眼神，理解她的痛苦。| 近义词：sympathetic, compassionate, understanding | 反义词：apathetic, uncaring, indifferent | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek empatheia "passion, state of emotion,"  from em- "in" + pathos "feeling."  Coined in 1904 in German (Einfühlung) and translated into English.
