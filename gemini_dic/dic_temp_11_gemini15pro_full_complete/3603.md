age-old:/ˈeɪdʒˌoʊld/| adj.|古老的，由来已久的|Very old; ancient.|The festival is part of an age-old tradition.  这个节日是一个古老传统的一部分。|The castle walls bear witness to age-old conflicts. 城堡的墙壁见证了古老的冲突。|近义词：ancient, antique, time-honored, long-standing|反义词：modern, recent, new|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "age" + "old."  "Age" comes from Old French and ultimately from Latin "aetas." "Old" comes from Old English "ald."
