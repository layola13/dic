briber:/ˈbraɪbər/| n. |行贿者，贿赂者|A person who gives a bribe. Someone who offers money or gifts to someone in a position of power or authority in order to influence their decisions or actions.|The briber offered the official a large sum of money to secure the contract. 行贿者向官员提供巨额资金以确保合同.|近义词：corrupter, paymaster|反义词：bribee, recipient|noun
