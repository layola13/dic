church-goer:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌɡoʊər/ (US) /ˈtʃɜːtʃˌɡəʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.|教堂常客，经常去教堂的人|A person who regularly attends church services.|例句：She's a devout church-goer and attends services every Sunday. 她是一位虔诚的教徒，每个星期天都去做礼拜。

例句：The church-goers gathered for the annual picnic. 教堂的常客们聚集在一起参加一年一度的野餐。

近义词：worshipper, congregant|反义词:non-believer, atheist|词性:noun
