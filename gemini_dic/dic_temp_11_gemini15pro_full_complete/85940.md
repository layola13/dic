headmaster:/ˈhedˌmæstər/ (US), /ˈhedˈmɑːstər/ (UK)| n. |校长（尤指私立男校）|The principal teacher in charge of a private school, especially a boys' school.|The headmaster addressed the students at the assembly. 校长在集会上向学生们讲话。|近义词：principal, head teacher, head, schoolmaster|反义词: N/A|noun

headmistress:/ˌhedˈmɪstrəs/ (US), /ˌhedˈmɪstrɪs/ (UK)| n. |女校长（尤指私立女校）|A woman who is the principal teacher in charge of a private school, especially a girls' school.|The headmistress congratulated the graduating class. 女校长祝贺毕业班。|近义词：principal, head teacher, head, schoolmistress|反义词: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hed maister*, equivalent to *head* + *master*.  The term originally referred to the male head of a household, later evolving to specifically denote the leader of a school.
