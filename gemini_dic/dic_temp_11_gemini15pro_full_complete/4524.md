albuminuria:/ˌælbjʊmɪˈnjʊəriə/
noun
蛋白尿
The presence of albumin in the urine, typically a symptom of kidney disease.
Example: The doctor detected albuminuria in the patient's urine sample, suggesting a potential kidney problem. 医生在病人的尿液样本中检测到蛋白尿，表明可能存在肾脏问题。

Example:  Persistent albuminuria can be a sign of diabetic nephropathy.  持续性蛋白尿可能是糖尿病肾病的征兆。

Synonym: proteinuria
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but the absence of albumin in urine indicates healthy kidney function)
词性: 名词
