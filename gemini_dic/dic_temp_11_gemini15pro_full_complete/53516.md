disassociation:/dɪˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən/ /dɪˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən/
n.
分离，分裂，脱离关系
The act of disassociating; separating from association or union with another; disunion.  The state of being disassociated. A disruption in the usually integrated functions of consciousness, memory, identity, or perception of the environment.
He announced his disassociation from the group. 他宣布退出该组织。
Synonyms: detachment, separation, disconnection, alienation
Antonyms: association, connection, union
词性: 名词

disassociations:/dɪˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃənz/ /dɪˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃənz/
n.
分离，分裂，脱离关系（复数）
Plural form of disassociation.  Multiple instances of separating from association or union with another; disunions.
The patient experienced several disassociations during therapy.  这位病人在治疗期间经历了几次分离。
Synonyms: detachments, separations, disconnections, alienations
Antonyms: associations, connections, unions
词性: 名词
