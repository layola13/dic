livestock:/ˈlaɪvˌstɒk/ (US) /ˈlaɪvˌstɒk/ (UK)| n. |牲畜；家畜|Animals, such as cows, sheep, and pigs, that are kept on a farm for their meat, milk, wool, etc.|The farmer raises livestock for meat and dairy products.  这位农民饲养牲畜以获取肉类和奶制品。|Synonyms: farm animals, cattle, stock|Antonyms: wild animals|Noun
