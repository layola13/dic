lunitidal:/ˌluːnɪˈtaɪdəl/
adjective
月潮的，受月球潮汐影响的
Relating to or influenced by the tides, especially those caused by the moon.
The lunitidal interval is the time between the moon's transit and the next high tide. 月潮间隔是指月亮经过子午线到下一次涨潮之间的时间。
The study focuses on the lunitidal influence on marine organisms. 这项研究的重点是月潮对海洋生物的影响。
近义词: tidal
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-tidal")
词性: adjective
