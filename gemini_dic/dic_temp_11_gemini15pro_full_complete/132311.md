nondefinable:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪnəbl/ |adj.| 不可定义的，无法下定义的 |Not able to be defined or explained clearly. |Some concepts, like love or beauty, are nondefinable.  有些概念，比如爱或美，是无法定义的。| The artist's work explored the nondefinable essence of human experience.  这位艺术家的作品探索了人类经验中不可名状的本质。 |近义词：indeterminable, indefinable, inexpressible |反义词：definable, describable |词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + definable (from define + -able).  "Define" comes from the Latin "definire," meaning "to limit, determine, explain."
