carbonaceous:/ˌkɑːrbəˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
碳的；含碳的
Containing or composed largely of carbon.
Carbonaceous chondrites are a class of chondritic meteorites comprising at least 8 known groups and many ungrouped meteorites.  碳质球粒陨石是球粒陨石的一类，包含至少8个已知群和许多未分组的陨石。

The filter is made of a carbonaceous material.  这个过滤器是由一种碳质材料制成的。

synonym:carbonic, graphitic
antonym: non-carbonaceous

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin carbōnāceus (“of coal or charcoal”), from Latin carbō (“coal, charcoal”). Surface analysis:  carbōn- +‎ -āceus.
