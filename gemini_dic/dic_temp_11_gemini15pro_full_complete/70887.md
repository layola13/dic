fior:/fɪˈɔːr/ /fjɔːr/
n.
flower, blossom
A fior is a flower or blossom.  It's a less common, more poetic or archaic word for flower.
The apple tree was covered in delicate fior in the springtime. 春天，苹果树上开满了精致的花朵。
The gentle fior of the jasmine scented the air. 茉莉花的芬芳弥漫在空气中。
近义词: flower, blossom, bloom
反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical flower.  Could consider "decay," "wilt," or "death" in a metaphorical sense.)
词性: noun
