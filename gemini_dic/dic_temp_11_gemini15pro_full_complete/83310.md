gynobase:/ˈdʒaɪnəˌbeɪs/| n. |雌蕊基|The fleshy, elevated or elongated base of a single pistil or gynoecium, to which the other floral parts are sometimes attached.|The gynobase of the flower is quite prominent.  这朵花的雌蕊基非常突出。|Synonyms: receptacle (in some cases), torus (in some cases)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
