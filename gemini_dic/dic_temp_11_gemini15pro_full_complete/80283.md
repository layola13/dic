going on:/ˈɡoʊɪŋ ɑːn/ (US) /ˈɡəʊɪŋ ɒn/ (UK)| idiom|发生，进行，进展|Something that is happening; an event or series of events.|例句：I don't know what's going on. 我不知道发生了什么。|近义词：happening, occurring, taking place|反义词:stopping, ceasing|idiom

going on:/ˈɡoʊɪŋ ɑːn/ (US) /ˈɡəʊɪŋ ɒn/ (UK)| adj. (informal)|进行中的，持续的|Continuing to happen.|例句：There's a lot going on at work right now.  现在工作上有很多事情正在进行。|近义词：continuing, ongoing, in progress|反义词:finished, completed|adjective
