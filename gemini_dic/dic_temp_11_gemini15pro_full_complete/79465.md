gloaming:/ˈɡloʊmɪŋ/| n.|暮色，黄昏|The gloaming is a beautiful time of day, when the sky is filled with soft colors. 黄昏是一天中美丽的时刻，天空充满了柔和的色彩。|例句：

The birds were singing their last songs of the day in the gloaming.
鸟儿们在暮色中唱着一天的最后一首歌。

As the sun dipped below the horizon, the gloaming deepened.
随着太阳落到地平线以下，暮色渐深。

|近义词：twilight, dusk, eventide|反义词:dawn, daybreak|词性:名词
