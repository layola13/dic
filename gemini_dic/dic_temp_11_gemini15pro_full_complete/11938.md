arsenopyrite:/ˈɑːrsənəʊˌpaɪraɪt/ /ˈɑːrsnəˌpaɪraɪt/| n. |毒砂|A common iron sulfarsenide mineral, FeAsS, with a metallic luster, tin-white to steel-gray in color. It is the most important ore of arsenic.|

Arsenopyrite is a common source of arsenic. 毒砂是砷的常见来源。

The miners discovered a vein of arsenopyrite.  矿工们发现了一条毒砂矿脉。

ETYMOLOGY->
From *arsenic* + *pyrite*.  *Arsenic* ultimately from Greek *arsenikon*, meaning "masculine, potent," referring to the strong properties of arsenic compounds. *Pyrite* from Greek *pyr* meaning "fire," because it emits sparks when struck.
