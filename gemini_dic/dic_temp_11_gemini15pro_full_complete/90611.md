humane:/hjuːˈmeɪn/ | adj. | 仁慈的，人道的 | Having or showing compassion or benevolence. |  例句：It's important to treat animals in a humane way.  对待动物要人道。

例句：The organization promotes humane treatment of prisoners. 该组织提倡人道地对待囚犯。


近义词：compassionate, kind, benevolent, merciful, empathetic | 反义词：cruel, inhumane, brutal, callous, merciless | 词性：形容词


词源: Late Middle English: from French humain, or from late Latin humanus, from Latin humanus ‘human’.
