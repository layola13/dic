diminishing:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
逐渐减少的，递减的
becoming smaller, weaker, or less important
The company's profits are diminishing.  这家公司的利润正在减少。
Her influence is diminishing. 她的影响力正在减弱。
synonyms: decreasing, declining, dwindling, shrinking, contracting, lessening
antonyms: increasing, growing, expanding, rising, escalating
adjective
