constitutionalisms:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənəlɪzəmz/
n.
宪政主义；立宪主义；宪政；立宪
The principles and practices of constitutional government.  A commitment to limitations on ordinary political power, usually codified in a written document.
The rise of constitutionalisms in the 18th century marked a significant shift in political thought. 18世纪宪政主义的兴起标志着政治思想的重大转变。
Different countries have adopted various forms of constitutionalisms. 不同国家采用了不同形式的宪政主义。
近义词:constitutional government, limited government
反义词:absolutism, authoritarianism
词性:noun
