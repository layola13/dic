goddamn:/ˌɡɑːdˈdæm/ | adj. int. | 讨厌的；该死的；(表示愤怒、厌烦、惊讶等) | Used as a mild oath or to express anger, annoyance, or surprise. | Example: "Goddamn it, I missed the bus!" 该死的，我错过了公共汽车！ | Example: "This goddamn rain won't stop."  这该死的雨下个不停。| 近义词:damned, cursed, bloody (British) | 反义词:blessed, holy | 词性:形容词,感叹词

goddamn:/ˌɡɑːdˈdæm/ | vt. | 诅咒 | To curse or damn someone or something. | Example: "He goddamned the day he was born." 他诅咒他出生的那一天。 | Example: "She goddamned him for his betrayal." 她因为他背叛她而诅咒他。| 近义词:curse, damn | 反义词:bless | 词性:动词

词源: From "God damn," a shortening of "God damn me (to hell)."  源自“God damn”，是“God damn me (to hell)”的缩写。
