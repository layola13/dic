cantons:/ˈkæntɒnz/ (UK), /ˈkæntɑːnz/ (US)| n.|州; 区; 行政区; 辖区|A canton is a subdivision of a country established for political or administrative purposes.  A canton can be a type of administrative division of a country, similar to a state or province.|Switzerland is divided into 26 cantons. 瑞士被划分为26个州。|
The canton of Geneva is known for its international organizations. 日内瓦州以其国际组织而闻名。|synonyms: state, province, district, region, prefecture|antonyms: country, nation, federation|noun

cantons:/ˈkæntɒnz/ (UK), /ˈkæntɑːnz/ (US)| n.| (纹章) 角|A corner or section of an escutcheon.
