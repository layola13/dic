kindliest:/ˈkaɪndliɪst/| adj.|最亲切的，最和蔼的|Superlative form of "kindly," meaning exhibiting or motivated by sympathy, generosity, or goodwill to the highest degree.|She gave me the kindliest smile I'd ever seen. 她给了我我见过的最亲切的微笑。|Synonyms: most benevolent, most compassionate, most amiable, most gentle|Antonyms: cruelest, harshest, unkindest, most ruthless|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *kindli, from kind + -ly.  The "-est" suffix is the superlative ending.
