carnatic:/kɑːrˈnætɪk/ | adj. n. | 卡纳提克的，卡纳提克音乐 | Of or relating to Carnatic music, a style of classical South Indian music.  Also, the music itself. |  She is a renowned carnatic singer. 她是一位著名的卡纳提克歌手。 |  The carnatic concert was mesmerizing. 这场卡纳提克音乐会令人着迷。| 近义词: South Indian classical | 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly Hindustani classical as a contrasting style) | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Kannada *karṇāṭaka*  "traditional" or "of/from Karnataka," the South Indian state where this musical tradition is prominent.
