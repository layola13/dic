liturgies:/ˈlɪtərʤiz/| n.|礼拜仪式，礼拜规程|A liturgy is a fixed set of rituals, prayers, readings, and other actions used in public worship.  It provides a structured framework for religious ceremonies.|The church follows the traditional liturgy for Sunday services.  教堂遵循传统的周日礼拜仪式。|The priest explained the symbolism within the liturgy.  牧师解释了礼拜仪式中的象征意义。|synonyms:rituals, ceremonies, rites, services|antonyms: informality, improvisation|noun
