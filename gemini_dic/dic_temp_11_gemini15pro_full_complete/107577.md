landmine:/ˈlændmaɪn/ | n. | 地雷；隐患 | An explosive mine laid on or just under the surface of the ground, detonated by contact or proximity.  A hidden danger or difficulty. |  The soldiers carefully cleared the area of landmines. 士兵们小心翼翼地清除了该地区的地雷。| synonyms: mine, booby trap, explosive device, hazard, peril, pitfall | antonyms: safety, security | noun

landmine:/ˈlændmaɪn/ | v. | 布雷 | To place landmines in (an area). | The enemy forces landmined the border. 敌军在边境布雷。 | synonyms: mine, booby-trap | antonyms: demine, clear | verb
