appropriation:/əˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən/| n.|拨款，挪用，盗用|The act of officially taking money for a particular purpose; the act of taking something that belongs to somebody else, especially without permission.|The government approved the appropriation of funds for the new hospital. 政府批准了新医院的拨款。|synonyms: allocation, allotment, earmarking, assignment, requisition, seizure, confiscation, expropriation, appropriation, assumption, arrogation|antonyms: bestowal, donation, contribution, restitution|noun

appropriation:/əˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən/| n.|占用，私占|The act of taking something for your own use, usually without permission.|Cultural appropriation is the adoption of elements of a minority culture by members of the dominant culture. 文化挪用是指主流文化成员采用少数族裔文化的元素。|synonyms: adoption, borrowing, taking, using|antonyms: returning, restoring, giving back|noun
