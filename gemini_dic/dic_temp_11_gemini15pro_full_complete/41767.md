corruptibly:/kəˈrʌptɪbli/
| adv. | 可腐败地，易堕落地 | In a way that can be corrupted; susceptible to corruption. | The officials acted corruptibly, accepting bribes and favors. 官员们行为腐败，接受贿赂和好处。| 近义词: dishonestly, venally, crookedly | 反义词: honestly, uprightly, ethically | 词性: 副词
corrupt:/kəˈrʌpt/
| adj.  vt. | 腐败的，堕落的；使腐败，使堕落 | Having or showing a willingness to act dishonestly in return for money or personal gain; make morally depraved. | The corrupt politician was eventually exposed and removed from office. 腐败的政客最终被揭露并被免职。| 近义词: dishonest, crooked, immoral | 反义词: honest, upright, moral | 词性: 形容词，动词

词源:
From Late Middle English *corruptible*, from Old French *corruptible*, from Latin *corruptus* (past participle of *corrumpere* ‘to break up, destroy, spoil, bribe’), from *com-* (expressing intensive force) + *rumpere* ‘to break’.
