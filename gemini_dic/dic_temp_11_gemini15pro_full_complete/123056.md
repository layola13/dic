monetaristic:/ˌmʌnɪˈtærɪstɪk/
monetaristic| adj.|货币主义的|Relating to monetarism; a macroeconomic theory holding that variations in the money supply have major influences on national output in the short run and on price levels over longer periods.|The government adopted a monetaristic approach to control inflation. 政府采取了货币主义的方法来控制通货膨胀。|近义词: monetary, fiscal|反义词: Keynesian|词性:形容词

monetarism:/ˈmʌnɪtərɪzəm/
monetarism| n.|货币主义|A macroeconomic theory holding that variations in the money supply have major influences on national output in the short run and on price levels over longer periods.  It suggests that the best way to encourage economic growth is to control inflation by regulating the growth of the money supply.|Monetarism was a popular economic theory in the 1980s. 货币主义在20世纪80年代是一种流行的经济理论。|近义词: monetary policy|反义词: Keynesianism|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From monetary + -ist + -ic.  "Monetary" ultimately derives from Latin "moneta" (mint, coinage).
