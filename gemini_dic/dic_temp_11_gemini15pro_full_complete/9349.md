antiprofiteering:/ˌæntiˌprɒfɪˈtɪərɪŋ/
n. |反暴利，防止牟取暴利|The act of preventing or opposing profiteering; legislation designed to prevent it.  The practice of preventing businesses from making excessive profits, especially during times of emergency or shortage.|
例句：The government introduced antiprofiteering measures during the war. 政府在战争期间采取了反暴利措施。

例句：Antiprofiteering laws are designed to protect consumers. 反暴利法旨在保护消费者。
近义词：price control, regulation, market intervention
反义词：profiteering, exploitation, price gouging
词性：名词

词源：
"anti-" (against) + "profiteering" (making excessive profits, especially unfairly or illegally)

The word "profiteering" itself comes from "profit" + "-eer" (one who engages in a specified activity). "Profit" ultimately derives from the Latin "profectus" (progress, advance).
