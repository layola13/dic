overlogically:/ˌoʊvərˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 过于逻辑地，过度理性地 | Reasoning or analyzing something in an excessively logical or analytical manner, often to the point of neglecting emotional or practical considerations. | He approached the problem overlogically, failing to consider the human element. 他过于逻辑地处理这个问题，忽略了人为因素。 |  She analyzed the situation overlogically, missing the underlying emotional currents. 她过度理性地分析了情况，忽略了潜在的情感暗流。 | 近义词: excessively logically, too rationally, hyper-rationally | 反义词: intuitively, emotionally, illogically | 词性: 副词
