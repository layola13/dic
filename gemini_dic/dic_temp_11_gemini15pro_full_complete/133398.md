nonfactor:/ˈnɑːnˌfæktər/| n. |无关紧要的因素，不重要的因素|Something that is not a contributing factor; something irrelevant.|例句：Her age was a nonfactor in her hiring. 她的年龄在录用过程中不是一个因素。

例句：The weather became a nonfactor once the game moved indoors. 一旦比赛移到室内，天气就变得无关紧要了。|近义词：irrelevancy, insignificance, unimportance|反义词:factor, influence, element|词性:名词
