noght:/nɔːxt/ | archaic form of naught | 无，零，没有价值的东西 |  An archaic form of "naught," meaning nothing or zero; also used to express worthlessness or wickedness. |  He gave me noght for my troubles. 他对我的麻烦不屑一顾。 | 近义词: nothing, zero, nil | 反义词: something, everything | 词性: pronoun, noun, adverb

词源:  From Middle English *noght*, *naught*, from Old English *nāwiht*, *nōwiht* (“nothing, not a thing, naught”), contraction of *ne* (“not”) + *āwiht*, *ōwiht* (“anything, aught”). Cognate with Old Frisian *naucht* (“nothing”), Old Saxon *niwiht* (“nothing”), Old High German *niwiht* (“nothing”), Gothic *𐌽𐌹𐍈𐌰𐌹𐍈𐍄𐍃* (ni waihts, “nothing”).
