curio:/ˈkjʊərioʊ/ (US), /ˈkjʊəriəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 古董，珍品，古玩|A rare, unusual, or intriguing object.| She collects curios from all over the world. 她从世界各地收集古玩。| synonyms: antique, artifact, collectible, oddity, rarity | antonyms: commonplace, ordinary, usual | noun


curio:/ˈkjʊərioʊ/ (US), /ˈkjʊəriəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 奇人，怪人 (rare usage)|A strange or unusual person.|He was considered a bit of a curio by his neighbors. 他的邻居认为他是个怪人。| synonyms: eccentric, oddball, character | antonyms: normal person, conformist | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin "curiosus" meaning "careful, inquisitive, curious," which in turn comes from "cura" meaning "care, concern." The meaning shifted over time from "something carefully made" to "something unusual and interesting."
