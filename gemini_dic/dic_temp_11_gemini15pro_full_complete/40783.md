convivialist:/kənˈvɪviəlɪst/
noun
好交际的人，宴饮者
A person who enjoys convivial company; a person who is fond of feasting, drinking, and good company.
She was a renowned convivialist, always hosting lively dinner parties. 她是一位有名的交际家，总是举办热闹的晚宴。
The convivialist regaled the group with stories from his travels.  这位宴饮者用他的旅行故事款待大家。
synonyms:  socializer,  extrovert,  bon vivant, party animal,  gregarious person
antonyms:  introvert,  recluse,  hermit, loner,  wallflower
n.
