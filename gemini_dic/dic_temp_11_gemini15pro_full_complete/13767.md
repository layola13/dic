audibleness:/ˈɔːdəbəlnəs/| n.|可闻度，清晰度|The audibleness of the whisper was surprising given the distance.  在这么远的距离下，这声耳语竟然如此清晰，真是令人惊讶。|The audibleness of the music was perfect for the small venue.  对于这个小场地来说，音乐的清晰度非常完美。|近义词：clarity, distinctness, volume|反义词：inaudibility, faintness|词性:名词
Etymology:  From audible + -ness.  Audible comes from Late Middle English, from Latin *audibilis, from audīre "to hear."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
