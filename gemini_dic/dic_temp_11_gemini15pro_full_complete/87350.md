词源：
From heroic + -al + -ness + -es.  "Heroic" ultimately derives from the Greek word "hērōs" (ἥρως), meaning "hero" or "demigod".  The suffix "-al" forms adjectives, "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state or quality, and "-es" marks the plural.
