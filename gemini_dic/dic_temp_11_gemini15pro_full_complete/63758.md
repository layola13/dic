epichirema:/ˌɛpɪkaɪˈriːmə/
|n.|复杂三段论；带有证明的推论|A rhetorical syllogism in which the premises are supported by reasons.|The lawyer presented a complex epichirema to prove his client's innocence. 律师提出了一个复杂的三段论来证明他的客户无罪。|Synonym:  polysyllogism, chain argument|Antonym: enthymeme|noun
