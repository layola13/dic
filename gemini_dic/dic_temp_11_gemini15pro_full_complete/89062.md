holophotal:/ˌhɒləˈfəʊtl/ /ˌhoʊləˈfoʊtl/| adj.|全光的，全部照明的| Relating to or denoting the total luminous flux incident on a surface, from all directions.|The holophotal illumination of a plant is crucial for its growth. 植物的全部光照对于其生长至关重要。|Example 2:  Scientists measured the holophotal radiation received by the coral reef. 科学家测量了珊瑚礁接收到的全部辐射。|近义词：overall illuminated, totally illuminated|反义词:partially illuminated, shaded|词性:adjective

词源:  From Greek *hólos* "whole" + *phôs*, *phōtós* "light".
