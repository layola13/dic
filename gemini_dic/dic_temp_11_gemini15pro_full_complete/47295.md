de-iced:/diːˈaɪst/
verb (past participle)
除冰（的）
Removed ice from something, typically an aircraft or road.
The plane was de-iced before takeoff. 飞机起飞前已除冰。
The roads were de-iced to prevent accidents. 道路已除冰以防止事故发生。
近义词:defrosted, thawed
反义词:iced, frozen
词性:Verb
