clinostat:/ˈklaɪnəˌstæt/| n. |旋转器，回转器|A device used to negate the effects of gravity on plant growth, typically by slowly rotating a plant specimen so that all sides are equally exposed to gravity.|The researchers used a clinostat to study the growth of roots in microgravity. 研究人员使用旋转器来研究微重力下根的生长。|Synonym: rotating apparatus, clinoscope|Antonym: N/A|noun
