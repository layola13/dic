honed:/hoʊnd/| v. |磨练，磨砺；使完美|Made more acute or effective; sharpened.  |例句：He has honed his skills over many years. 他多年来不断磨练自己的技能。|例句：She honed her debating skills at university. 她在大学里磨练了她的辩论技巧。|近义词：sharpened, refined, perfected, polished|反义词:dulled, blunted|词性:动词
词源:From Old English *hanian "to stone," from Proto-Germanic *hanōną "to whet, rub," from PIE root *kane- "to sing, sound." Originally referring to sharpening a blade on a whetstone.
