fowling:/ˈfaʊlɪŋ/

捕鸟;猎鸟

例句：Fowling was a common way to supplement one's diet in the past.  在过去，捕鸟是补充人们饮食的一种常见方式。

近义词：birding, hunting
反义词: conservation
