habiture:/ˈhæbɪtʃʊr/ (US), /ˈhæbɪtʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|居住；习惯；习性|The act or practice of inhabiting or dwelling in a place; the state of being inhabited.  Also, customary manner of behaving or living; habit. |The habiture of the nomadic tribes changed with the seasons.  游牧部落的居住地随着季节而变化。|近义词：residence, dwelling, abode, habit, custom|反义词: homelessness, transience|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *habitūra, from Latin habitus (“condition, character, habit, dress”), past participle of habēre (“to have, hold”).
