napa:/ˈnæpə/ | n. |纳帕|A valley in Northern California known for its vineyards and wineries.  It is also a type of  smooth-grained leather, usually from a lamb or sheep, used for clothing, upholstery, and bookbindings. |例句：We spent the weekend wine tasting in Napa. 我们周末在纳帕品酒。

例句：My new gloves are made of soft napa leather. 我的新手套是用柔软的纳帕皮革制成的。|近义词：(for the place) wine country, Sonoma; (for the leather) lambskin, sheepskin |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


napa:/ˈnæpə/ | adj. |纳帕的|Relating to or characteristic of Napa Valley, especially its wine industry.  Also used to describe items made from napa leather.|例句：Napa Cabernet Sauvignon is highly regarded. 纳帕赤霞珠备受推崇。

例句：She wore a stylish napa leather jacket. 她穿着一件时髦的纳帕皮革夹克。|近义词:  (for the place) Californian; (for the leather) leather, lambskin |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Patwin (a Native American language) *napá: meaning "home."  The leather meaning derives from Napa, California, where early leather tanneries were established.
