glutean:/ˈɡluːtiən/ /ˈɡluːtɪən/| adj.|麸质的，含麸质的| Relating to or containing gluten, a protein composite found in wheat and related grains, including barley and rye.|例句：She follows a glutean-free diet due to celiac disease. 她因为患有乳糜泻而遵循无麸质饮食。

例句：Glutean foods can cause digestive issues for some people. 对某些人来说，含麸质的食物会导致消化问题。

|近义词：glutenous|反义词:gluten-free|词性:形容词
