decidedly:/dɪˈsaɪdɪdli/| adv.|明确地，坚决地，无疑地|clearly and definitely; without question.  It means in a way that is clear and definite.|例句：She was decidedly unhappy with the outcome. 她对结果明显不满意。|近义词：definitely, clearly, certainly, undoubtedly, absolutely, unequivocally|反义词：indecisively, hesitantly, vaguely, ambiguously|词性:副词
词源: From decide + -ed + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin decidere "to determine."
