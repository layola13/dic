glue:/ɡluː/
n.|胶水；粘合剂|An adhesive substance used for sticking things together.|The carpenter used glue to fix the broken chair leg. 木匠用胶水粘合断了的椅腿。|adhesive, cement, paste|solvent
v.|粘合；粘贴|To stick or fasten with glue.|She glued the pieces of paper together to make a collage. 她把纸片粘在一起做成拼贴画。|stick, attach, bond|separate, detach
词性:名词,动词

词源:
From Middle English *glu, from Old French glu, from Late Latin glūten (“glue”), from Latin glūten (“glue, birdlime”), from PIE *ǵley- (“to stick, smear”). Cognate with gluten.
