hydropic:/haɪˈdrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 水肿的，积水的 | Characterized by dropsy; edematous.  Suffering from, or exhibiting symptoms of, dropsy. | The hydropic patient's legs were severely swollen.  水肿病人的腿严重肿胀。 | The hydropic tissues showed signs of severe inflammation. 水肿组织显示出严重的炎症迹象。|近义词：edematous, swollen |反义词:  dehydrated |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hydropicus, from Greek ὑδρωπικός (hudrōpikós), from ὑδρωπία (hudrōpía, “dropsy”), from ὕδωρ (húdōr, “water”).
