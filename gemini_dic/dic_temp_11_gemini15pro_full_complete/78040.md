genuflectors:/ˈdʒɛnjuːˌflɛktərz/
n.
跪拜者，屈膝者
People who genuflect; those who bend the knee, typically in worship or as a sign of respect.
The genuflectors bowed their heads in prayer. 跪拜者低头祈祷。
The king acknowledged the genuflectors as he entered the hall. 国王进入大厅时向屈膝者致意。
synonyms: worshippers, kneelers, supplicants
antonyms: standers, dissenters
noun
