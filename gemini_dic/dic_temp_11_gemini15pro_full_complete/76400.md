gag-reined:/ˈɡæɡˌreɪnd/
adjective
戴着嚼子的；被压制言论的
Having a gag (something put in or over the mouth to prevent speaking) and reins (straps used to control a horse), either literally or figuratively; suppressed, silenced, or censored.
The gag-reined witness was unable to testify truthfully.  戴着嚼子的证人无法说出真相。
The journalist felt gag-reined by the government's strict censorship laws. 这位记者感到自己的言论被政府严格的审查法压制了。
Synonyms: silenced, muzzled, censored, suppressed, restrained
Antonyms: outspoken, liberated, uncensored, free
part of speech: adjective
