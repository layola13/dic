kludgey:/ˈklʌdʒi/ | adj. | 笨拙的，粗糙的，凑合的 | Awkward, clumsy, or inelegant, especially in design or construction; makeshift.  A kludgey solution is one that is patched together, often hastily and in a way that is not ideal, but works. |  The code was kludgey and difficult to maintain. 代码很笨拙，难以维护。|  The website's design was kludgey and unprofessional.  这个网站的设计很笨拙，很不专业。 |  clumsy, awkward, makeshift, inelegant, patched-together | elegant, streamlined, sophisticated, well-designed | adjective
