integument:/ɪnˈteɡjʊmənt/| n.|外皮，覆盖物；（动物的）皮，皮肤；（植物的）外壳|A natural covering, such as skin, a shell, or a rind.|The integument of the seed protects the embryo. 种子的外皮保护着胚胎。|synonyms: covering, skin, rind, shell, coat, casing|antonyms: interior, inside|noun
