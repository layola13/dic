monotonous: /məˈnɒtənəs/ (UK) /məˈnɑːtənəs/ (US)| adj. |单调的，乏味的|Lacking in variety and interest; tediously uniform or unvarying.|The lecture was so monotonous that I almost fell asleep. 这场讲座太单调了，我差点睡着了。|Synonyms: tedious, boring, dull, repetitive, unvaried, tiresome, wearisome|Antonyms: interesting, exciting, varied, stimulating, diverse|adjective

monotonous: /məˈnɒtənəs/ (UK) /məˈnɑːtənəs/ (US)| adj. |单调的，乏味的|Characterized by a tedious sameness or repetitiveness.|The monotonous drone of the engine lulled him to sleep.  引擎单调的嗡嗡声使他昏昏欲睡。|Synonyms:  dreary, humdrum, flat|Antonyms: engaging, captivating, dynamic|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *monotonus, from Greek μονότονος (monotonos) "having one tone," from μόνος (monos) "single, alone" + τόνος (tonos) "tone, pitch."
