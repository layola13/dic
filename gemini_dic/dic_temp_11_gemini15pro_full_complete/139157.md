obfuscating:/ˈɒbfʌskeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 使模糊的，使混乱的 | Making something unclear or difficult to understand.  | The company used obfuscating language to hide the truth about their product.  这家公司用含糊其辞的语言来掩盖其产品的真相。| Synonyms: confusing, bewildering, obscuring, mystifying | Antonyms: clarifying, elucidating, simplifying | adjective


obfuscate:/ˈɒbfʌskeɪt/ | v. | 使模糊，使混乱 | To make something unclear or difficult to understand.  | The lawyer deliberately obfuscated the issue with complex legal jargon. 律师故意用复杂的法律术语混淆视听。 | Synonyms: confuse, bewilder, obscure, mystify | Antonyms: clarify, elucidate, simplify | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *obfuscatus, past participle of obfuscare (“to darken”), from Latin ob (“in front of, against, towards”) + fuscare (“to darken”), from fuscus (“dark”).
