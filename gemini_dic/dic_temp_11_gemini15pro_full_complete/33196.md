chromosomally:/ˌkroʊməˈsoʊməli/
adjective
染色体地，在染色体上
Relating to or involving chromosomes.  Determined by or carried on the chromosome.
Example: The disease is chromosomally transmitted. 这种疾病是通过染色体传播的。
Example: Chromosomally, males have one X and one Y chromosome. 从染色体上来说，男性有一条X染色体和一条Y染色体。
Near Synonyms: genetic, hereditary, inherited
Antonyms: non-genetic, acquired
词性: 形容词
