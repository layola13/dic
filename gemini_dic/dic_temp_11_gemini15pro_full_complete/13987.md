auricula:/ɔːˈrɪkjʊlə/ | n. |耳廓；耳房；报春花|The outer ear, also called the pinna or auricle, collects sound waves.|例句：The auricle of his ear was pierced. 他的耳廓上穿了孔。

例句：He carefully examined the auricle for any signs of infection. 他仔细检查了耳廓是否有感染的迹象。|近义词：pinna, outer ear|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

词源: From Latin *auricula*, diminutive of *auris* ("ear").
