furtherance:/ˈfɜːrðərəns/
n.
推进，促进
the advancement of a scheme or interest.
He took these actions purely in (the) furtherance of his own career. 他纯粹是为了推进自己的事业而采取这些行动。
近义词：advancement, promotion, progression, development
反义词：hindrance, obstruction, impediment
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English fortherance, from Anglo-Norman, from Old French forterer (“to further”), from Vulgar Latin *fortiāre, from Latin fortis (“strong”).
