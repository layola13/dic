defragment:/diːˈfræɡmənt/
verb|整理碎片；消除碎片；|To reorganize files on a disk so that they are stored in contiguous blocks, thus improving performance.|I need to defragment my hard drive to speed up my computer. 我需要整理我的硬盘碎片以加快我的电脑速度。|consolidate, optimize|fragment|v.

defragmentation:/diːˌfræɡmənˈteɪʃn/
noun|碎片整理；消除碎片；|The process of reorganizing files on a disk so that they are stored in contiguous blocks.|Regular defragmentation can improve your computer's performance. 定期整理碎片可以提高计算机的性能。|consolidation, optimization|fragmentation|n.
