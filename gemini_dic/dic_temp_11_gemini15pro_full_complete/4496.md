albinoism:/ˈælbɪˌnɪzəm/ | n. |白化病|A group of inherited disorders characterized by little or no melanin production. This can affect the color of the skin, hair, and eyes.|Example 1: Albinism is a genetic condition. 白化病是一种遗传性疾病。

Example 2:  The albino rabbit stood out against the dark grass. 白化兔子在深色的草地上很显眼。|Synonym: achromia, achromasia|Antonym: pigmentation|noun
