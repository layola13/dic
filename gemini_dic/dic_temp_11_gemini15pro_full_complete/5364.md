allusion:/əˈluːʒən/ | n. | 典故；暗示；提及 | An indirect or passing reference to some event, person, place, or artistic work, the nature and relevance of which is not explained by the writer and relies on the reader’s familiarity with what is thus mentioned. | The poem is full of allusions to classical mythology. 这首诗充满了对古典神话的典故。| synonyms: reference, hint, implication, suggestion | antonyms: explicit statement, direct mention | noun
