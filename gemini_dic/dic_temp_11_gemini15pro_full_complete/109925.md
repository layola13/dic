libellee:/ˌlɪbəˈliː/
n.
被告; 被诽谤者
the person who is accused of having written or published something that is defamatory
The libellee denied all the accusations made against him.  被告否认了所有对他的指控。
The libellee filed a countersuit. 被告提出了反诉。
synonym:defendant, accused
antonym: plaintiff, accuser
noun
