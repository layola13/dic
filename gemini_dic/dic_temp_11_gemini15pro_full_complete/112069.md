longhorns:/ˈlɔːŋhɔːrnz/
noun
长角牛；(尤指)得克萨斯长角牛
A breed of cattle with long horns, especially the Texas Longhorn.
The longhorns grazed peacefully on the range. 长角牛在牧场上平静地吃草。
