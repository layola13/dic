accretive:/əˈkriːtɪv/
adjective
增值的，累积的，附加的
Adding to or increasing something, especially profits or value.
The acquisition was accretive to earnings per share. 这项收购增加了每股收益。
近义词: additive, cumulative, augmentative
反义词: dilutive, subtractive
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin accrescere "to grow larger," from ad- "to" + crescere "to grow."
