microbiologies: /ˌmaɪkroʊbaɪˈɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 微生物学；微生物学研究 |  The study of microorganisms, such as bacteria, viruses, archaea, fungi, and protozoa.  This field includes fundamental research on the biochemistry, physiology, cell biology, ecology, evolution, and clinical aspects of microorganisms, including the host response to these agents. | Recent advances in microbiologies have led to new treatments for infectious diseases.  微生物学的最新进展带来了治疗传染病的新方法。 |  Near synonyms: bacteriology, virology, mycology | Antonym: macrobiology | Noun
