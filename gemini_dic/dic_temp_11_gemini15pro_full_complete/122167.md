mistruths:/ˌmɪsˈtruːθs/
n.
谎言，不实之词
false statements; lies.
The politician was accused of spreading mistruths about his opponent.  这个政客被指控散布关于他对手的谎言。
The report was full of mistruths and exaggerations.  这份报告充满了谎言和夸张。
近义词:lies, falsehoods, untruths, fabrications
反义词:truths, facts, realities
词性:名词 (Noun)

Etymology:
From mis- + truth.  Mis- is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "false." Truth comes from Old English "trēowth," meaning "faithfulness, constancy, loyalty." Thus, "mistruth" literally means a "false truth" or a "lack of truthfulness."
