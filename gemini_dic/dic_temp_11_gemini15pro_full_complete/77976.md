gentians:/ˈdʒɛnʃənz/
n.
龙胆花; 龙胆属植物
A flowering plant with deep blue trumpet-shaped flowers, typically growing in mountain regions.  Several species are cultivated as alpines.
Gentians bloom in late summer. 龙胆花在夏末开花。
The meadow was dotted with vibrant blue gentians. 草地上点缀着鲜艳的蓝色龙胆花。
synonyms:  alpine plants, wildflowers
antonyms: N/A
