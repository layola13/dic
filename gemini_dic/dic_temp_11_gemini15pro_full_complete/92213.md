hypertelorism: /ˌhaɪpərˈtɛləˌrɪzəm/ /ˌhaɪpərˈtiːləˌrɪzəm/
| n. | 眼距过宽，远视症 | An abnormally increased distance between two body parts, especially the eyes. |  The child's hypertelorism was noticeable from across the room.  这个孩子的眼距过宽从房间的另一头就能看出来。| synonym: wide-set eyes | antonym: hypotelorism | noun


hypertelorism: /ˌhaɪpərˈtɛləˌrɪzəm/ /ˌhaɪpərˈtiːləˌrɪzəm/
