doubtless:/ˈdaʊtləs/| adv.|无疑地，肯定地，想必|Without doubt; certainly.  It expresses a strong belief that something is true.|He will doubtless succeed. 他肯定会成功。|近义词:certainly, definitely, undoubtedly, surely, probably, presumably|反义词:doubtful, unlikely, improbable|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *doutles*, equivalent to *doubt* + *-less*. Cognate with Dutch *daatloos*, German *dauerlos*.
