bovid:/ˈboʊvɪd/| n. adj.|牛科动物；牛科的|A ruminant mammal of the family Bovidae, such as an ox, sheep, goat, or antelope.|例句：The cow is a bovid. 牛是一种牛科动物。|例句：Bovid horns are never branched. 牛科动物的角从不分叉。|近义词：ox, cow, buffalo, sheep, goat, antelope|反义词:|noun, adjective
