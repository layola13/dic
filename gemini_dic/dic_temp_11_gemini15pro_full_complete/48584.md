defeatist:/dɪˈfiːtɪst/
adjective|失败主义的；悲观的|Showing an attitude of accepting defeat as likely or inevitable.|He had a defeatist attitude about the upcoming game.|近义词:pessimistic, negative, resigned|反义词:optimistic, positive, hopeful
noun|失败主义者；悲观主义者|A person who expects or accepts defeat.|Don't be such a defeatist; we can still win this!|近义词:pessimist, cynic|反义词:optimist
