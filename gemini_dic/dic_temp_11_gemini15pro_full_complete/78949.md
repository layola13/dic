girdles:/ˈɡɜːrdlz/ (US) /ˈɡɜːdlz/ (UK)| n. | 腰带，束腹，环绕物 | A belt or band worn around the waist; something that encircles or confines. | She wore a girdle under her dress. 她穿着裙子，里面系着束腹。The mountains form a girdle around the city. 群山环绕着这座城市。| 近义词: belt, sash, band, corset, cinch | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 looseness, freedom) | noun


girdles:/ˈɡɜːrdlz/ (US) /ˈɡɜːdlz/ (UK)| v. | 束缚，环绕，包围 | To encircle or surround something with a belt or band; to enclose or confine. |  A stone wall girdles the castle. 一堵石墙环绕着城堡。  Trees girdle the lake. 树木环绕着湖泊。| 近义词: surround, encircle, enclose, encompass | 反义词:  unbound, free, release | verb
