chares:/ˈtʃɛəriz/| n.|家务,杂务;琐事(通常指日常的或令人不愉快的事情)|Chores are routine tasks, often unpleasant ones, that must be done regularly.|例句：I hate doing household chores. 我讨厌做家务。

例句：He had a few chores to do before leaving for work. 他在上班前还有几件杂事要做。|近义词：tasks, duties, errands|反义词:leisure, fun|词性:名词
