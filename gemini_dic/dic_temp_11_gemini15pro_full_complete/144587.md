outwave:/ˈaʊtweɪv/| n.|外波，输出波|A wave that travels outward from a source.  A wave that is transmitted, rather than reflected or absorbed.|The outwave from the earthquake was felt miles away. 地震的外波几英里外都能感觉到。|近义词：outgoing wave, transmitted wave|反义词：inwave, reflected wave|词性:名词
