over-flattening:/ˌoʊvərˈflætənɪŋ/
adjective
过度扁平化
Excessive flattening; flattening beyond what is desirable or appropriate.
The over-flattening of the organizational structure led to confusion about roles and responsibilities. 过度扁平化的组织结构导致角色和职责混乱。
The artist criticized the over-flattening of the image in the digital reproduction.  这位艺术家批评了数字复制品中图像的过度扁平化。
近义词: excessive flattening, oversimplification
反义词: complexification, layering
词性: adjective
