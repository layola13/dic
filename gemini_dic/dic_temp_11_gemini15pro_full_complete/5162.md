The allergenicity of this new protein is currently being studied.  这种新蛋白质的过敏性目前正在研究中。

Researchers are working to reduce the allergenicity of certain foods. 研究人员正在努力降低某些食物的过敏性。|近义词：allergic potential, sensitizing capacity|反义词:hypoallergenicity|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From allergen + -icity.  Allergen comes from  allerg(y) (from Greek  allos "other" +  ergon "work") + -gen (from Greek  -genes "born of, produced by"). -icity is a suffix forming nouns of quality, state, or degree.
