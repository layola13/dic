landslide:/ˈlændslaɪd/ (US), /ˈlændslaɪd/ (UK)| n.|山体滑坡；压倒性胜利|A landslide is a geological phenomenon that includes a wide range of ground movements, such as rockfalls, deep failure of slopes and shallow debris flows.  山体滑坡是一种地质现象，包括各种各样的地面运动，例如落石、深层边坡破坏和浅层泥石流。|例句：The heavy rain caused a landslide.  暴雨引发了山体滑坡。|近义词：rockslide, mudslide, avalanche|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用stability 表示地质结构的稳定性)|词性:noun


landslide:/ˈlændslaɪd/ (US), /ˈlændslaɪd/ (UK)| adj.|压倒性的，一边倒的|Achieved by a very large margin.  以非常大的优势取得。|例句：The election resulted in a landslide victory for the ruling party. 这次选举导致执政党取得压倒性胜利。|近义词：overwhelming, decisive|反义词:narrow, close|词性:adjective
