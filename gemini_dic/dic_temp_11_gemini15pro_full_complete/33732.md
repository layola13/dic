circuit:/ˈsɜːrkɪt/ (US) /ˈsɜːkɪt/ (UK)| n.| 电路; 环形路线; 巡回; 巡回区|A complete path through which an electric current can flow. A roughly circular line, route, or movement that starts and finishes at the same place. An area or district including a number of places that someone visits regularly as part of their job. |The circuit was overloaded and caused a power outage. 电路超载导致停电。|synonyms: loop, circle, tour, route|antonyms: line, straight|noun

circuit:/ˈsɜːrkɪt/ (US) /ˈsɜːkɪt/ (UK)| v.|绕...环行|To go around something.|The race car driver circuited the track at high speed. 赛车手高速绕赛道行驶。|synonyms: circle, circumnavigate, orbit|antonyms: stop, halt|verb
