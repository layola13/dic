apodosis:/əˈpɒdəsɪs/| n.|结果，结论；（条件句中的）主句|The main clause of a conditional sentence, stating the result or consequence.  It follows the "if" clause (protasis).|If you study hard, you will succeed. (You will succeed is the apodosis)|Synonyms: conclusion, consequence, outcome, result|Antonyms: protasis, premise|noun
