condoning:/kənˈdoʊnɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
宽恕；纵容；认可
accepting and allowing (behavior that is considered morally wrong or offensive) to continue.
例句：She would never condone violence. 她绝不会纵容暴力。
例句：The college cannot condone any behavior that involves illicit drugs.  学院不能容忍任何涉及非法药物的行为。
近义词：forgiving, pardoning, overlooking, excusing, accepting, tolerating
反义词：condemning, denouncing, censuring, criticizing, opposing
