idyll:/ˈɪdɪl/ /ˈaɪdl/ | n. |田园诗; 牧歌; 浪漫插曲; |A short description of a peaceful or romantic scene, especially in the countryside.  A happy, peaceful, or romantic period or situation. |例句：The poem is an idyll of rural life. 这首诗是一首田园生活的颂歌。

例句：Their honeymoon was an idyll of romantic walks and quiet evenings. 他们的蜜月是一段浪漫散步和宁静夜晚的田园诗般的时光。|近义词：pastoral, romance, poem, scene|反义词：tribulation, hardship|词性: noun

idyls:/ˈɪdɪlz/ /ˈaɪdlz/ | n. |田园诗( idyll的名词复数 ); 牧歌; 浪漫插曲; |Plural form of "idyll".|例句：He wrote several idyls celebrating the beauty of nature. 他写了几首田园诗歌颂大自然的美。

例句：Her childhood memories were filled with idyls of summer days spent by the lake. 她童年的记忆充满了在湖边度过的夏日时光的田园诗般的回忆。|近义词：pastorals, romances, poems, scenes|反义词：tribulations, hardships|词性: noun
