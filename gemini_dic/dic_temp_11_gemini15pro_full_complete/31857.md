cheerfully:/ˈtʃɪrfəli/| adv.|高兴地，愉快地|In a cheerful way; with cheerfulness.  Showing or expressing good spirits.|She cheerfully greeted her guests. 她高兴地迎接客人。|happily, merrily, joyfully, gladly|sadly, gloomily, miserably|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cherfulli, equivalent to cheerful +‎ -ly.  Cheerful itself comes from Old English *ċēorful, from *ċēor "mood, mind" + -ful.
