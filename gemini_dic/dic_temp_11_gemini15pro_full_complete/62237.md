encrustation:/ɪnˌkrʌˈsteɪʃən/| n. |结壳；硬壳；包壳|A hard coating or deposit of minerals or other substances on a surface.|The ancient vase was covered in an encrustation of dirt and grime. 古老的花瓶上覆盖着一层污垢和尘垢。|近义词：coating, covering, crust, deposit, layer, scale|反义词: erosion, wearing away|词性:noun

encrust:/ɪnˈkrʌst/| vt. |使结壳；用…包壳|To cover or decorate with a hard coating.|Barnacles encrusted the hull of the ship. 藤壶包裹了船体。|近义词：coat, cover, overlay|反义词: strip, uncover|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incrustātiōn- (stem of incrustātiō), from Latin incrustātus (past participle of incrustāre ‘to encrust’), from in- ‘into, upon’ + crusta ‘crust, rind’.
