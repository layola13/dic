antioptimistic:/ˌæntiˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪk/
adjective
反乐观主义的；悲观的
expecting the worst; not optimistic.
例句：He had a rather antioptimistic view of the future. 他对未来持相当悲观的看法。

例句：Her antioptimistic attitude made it difficult to plan anything fun. 她悲观的态度使得计划任何有趣的事情都很困难。

近义词：pessimistic, cynical, negative, defeatist
反义词：optimistic, hopeful, positive
词性: adjective

Etymology:
anti- (prefix meaning "against, opposite of") + optimistic
