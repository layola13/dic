desexualization:/diːˌsɛkʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |去性化，非性化 | The act of removing sexual characteristics or connotations from something or someone; the process of making something or someone less sexual. |  The desexualization of women in advertising is a common feminist critique.  女性在广告中被去性化是常见的女性主义批评。| 近义词: neutralization, devitalization | 反义词: sexualization | 词性: 名词

desexualize:/diːˈsɛkʃuəlaɪz/ | vt. | 去性化，非性化 | To deprive of sexual characteristics or connotations; to make less sexual. | The marketing campaign aimed to desexualize the product and appeal to a wider audience. 这场营销活动旨在去除产品的性暗示，以吸引更广泛的受众。 | 近义词: neuter, sanitize | 反义词: sexualize | 词性: 动词
