let slip:/lɛt slɪp/
v.
无意中泄露，不小心说出
To reveal something unintentionally, often a secret.
He let slip that he was planning to resign. 他无意中透露了他打算辞职的消息。
synonyms: reveal, divulge, disclose, blurt out, spill the beans
antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress
verb

let slip:/lɛt slɪp/
v.
错过（机会）
To miss or fail to take advantage of an opportunity.
She let slip a golden opportunity to invest in the company. 她错过了投资这家公司的绝佳机会。
synonyms: miss, lose, forfeit, pass up
antonyms: seize, grasp, take advantage of
verb
