inferobranchiate:/ɪnˌfɛroʊˈbræŋkiət/
adjective
下鳃的，鳃位体下方的
having gills located beneath the body, typically used to describe certain mollusks.
Inferobranchiate snails are found in the intertidal zone. 下鳃蜗牛发现于潮间带。
近义词:hypobranchial
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "epibranchiate" if referring to gills located above or on the body.)
