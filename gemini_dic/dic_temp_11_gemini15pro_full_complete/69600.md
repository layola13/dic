femalely:/ˈfiːmeɪlli/ | adv. | 女性地，像女人一样地 | In a way that is considered typical or appropriate for a woman. | She dressed femalely. 她穿着很女性化。|  She spoke in a soft, femalely voice. 她用柔和的女性嗓音说话。 | 近义词：womanly, femininely | 反义词：masculinely | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From female + -ly.  "Female" comes from Old French "femelle," ultimately from Latin "femella," a diminutive of "femina" meaning "woman."  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
