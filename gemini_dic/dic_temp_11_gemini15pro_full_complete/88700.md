hobbled:/ˈhɑːbəld/ (US) /ˈhɒbəld/ (UK)| v. |蹒跚而行；阻碍；限制| to walk with difficulty, especially because of an injury to the leg or foot; to prevent something from making progress or developing|例句：The injured horse hobbled painfully across the field. 受伤的马痛苦地蹒跚穿过田野。|近义词：limp, stumble, falter, hinder, impede|反义词:run, sprint, facilitate, aid|词性:动词

hobbled:/ˈhɑːbəld/ (US) /ˈhɒbəld/ (UK)| adj. |跛的，受阻的|lame, crippled, hampered|例句：The hobbled economy struggled to recover.  步履蹒跚的经济难以复苏。|近义词：crippled, hampered, restricted|反义词:healthy, strong, unimpeded|词性:形容词
