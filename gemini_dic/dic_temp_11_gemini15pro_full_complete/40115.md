contemper:/kənˈtɛmpər/ | vt. | 调和；使缓和；使温和；减轻 | To moderate or mitigate; to reduce in intensity. |

The king contempered his anger with mercy. 国王以仁慈缓和了他的愤怒。

She contempered her criticism with praise. 她用赞扬来缓和她的批评。


近义词: moderate, mitigate, alleviate, temper, soften, lessen

反义词: aggravate, exacerbate, intensify, worsen

词性: verb (及物动词)


ETYMOLOGY->

From Latin *contemperare* "to temper, mingle in due proportion," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *temperare* "to moderate, restrain, mix," related to *tempus* "time, season."
