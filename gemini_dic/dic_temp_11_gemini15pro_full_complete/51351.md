detenebrate:/dɪˈtɛnɪˌbreɪt/| v. |使黑暗；使昏暗；遮蔽|To darken or obscure; to cast a shadow over.|The heavy storm clouds detenebrated the sky, making it appear as though twilight had arrived early. 厚重的暴雨云使天空变得昏暗，仿佛黄昏提前到来。


The dense fog detenebrated the landscape, obscuring the view of the mountains. 浓雾遮蔽了景色，使山脉的景色变得模糊。|近义词：darken, obscure, dim, shadow, eclipse|反义词：illuminate, brighten, lighten|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dētenebrāre*, from *dē-* (from, away from, down) + *tenebrāre* (to darken), from *tenebrae* (darkness).
