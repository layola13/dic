inefficiencies:/ˌɪnɪˈfɪʃənsiz/
|n.|低效，效率低下|The inefficiencies in the system are costing the company a lot of money. 系统的低效使公司损失了很多钱。|
|英文解析:Plural form of inefficiency.  Lack of efficiency; the state of not achieving maximum productivity; wastefulness.|
|例句：The report highlighted the inefficiencies in the current system.  报告强调了现有系统的效率低下。|
|例句：We need to address the inefficiencies in our production process. 我们需要解决生产过程中的效率低下的问题。|
|近义词：incompetence, ineffectiveness, inadequacy|
|反义词：efficiency, effectiveness, productivity|
|词性: 名词|
