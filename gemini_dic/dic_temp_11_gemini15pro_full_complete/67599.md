extravagantly:/ɪkˈstrævəɡəntli/| adv.|奢侈地，挥霍无度地|In a manner that spends more money than is necessary or reasonable; excessively.|She lived extravagantly, spending thousands of dollars on clothes each month. 她生活奢侈，每个月花费数千美元买衣服。|He furnished his apartment extravagantly with antique furniture and original artwork.  他用古董家具和原创艺术品奢侈地装饰了他的公寓。|近义词：lavishly, profusely, exorbitantly|反义词：frugally, economically, modestly|词性: 副词

词源:
From Late Latin *extravagans, present participle of extravagari "to wander outside or beyond," from Latin extra "outside of, beyond" + vagari "to wander, stray, roam."
