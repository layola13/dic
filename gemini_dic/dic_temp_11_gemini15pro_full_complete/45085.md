cubited:/ˈkjuːbɪtɪd/
adjective
以肘为单位的; 量为一肘的
measured in cubits (an ancient unit of length based on the forearm from the elbow to the tip of the middle finger)
The ark was three hundred cubits long, fifty cubits wide and thirty cubits high. 方舟长三百肘，宽五十肘，高三十肘。
Synonyms: measured, gauged
Antonyms: unmeasured

Etymology: From Late Latin *cubitatus, past participle of cubitare (“to lie down, recline”), from Latin cubitus (“elbow; forearm; cubit”).
