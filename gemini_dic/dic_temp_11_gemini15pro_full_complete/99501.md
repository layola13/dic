interfluous:/ˌɪntərˈfluːəs/| adj.|河流间的，两河之间的|Situated between rivers; flowing between rivers.  Referring to the region of land between two rivers. |The interfluous region supported a diverse ecosystem.  这片河流间的区域支撑着一个多样化的生态系统。|The interfluous plateau provided fertile land for agriculture. 这片河流间的平原为农业提供了肥沃的土地。|Synonyms: interfluvial|Antonyms: riparian, riverine|adjective
