dolphin:/ˈdɒlfɪn/ (UK), /ˈdɑːlfɪn/ (US)| n.|海豚|A highly intelligent marine mammal related to whales and porpoises, known for its playful behavior and ability to communicate through clicks and whistles.|例句：We saw a pod of dolphins swimming alongside our boat. 我们看到一群海豚和我们的船一起游。

|近义词：porpoise, cetacean|反义词:None|词性:Noun

dolphin:/ˈdɒlfɪn/ (UK), /ˈdɑːlfɪn/ (US)| n.|海豚座|A constellation in the northern sky.|例句：The dolphin constellation is relatively small and faint. 海豚座是一个相对较小和微弱的星座。

|近义词：None|反义词:None|词性:Noun
