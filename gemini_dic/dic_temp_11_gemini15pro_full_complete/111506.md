loam:/loʊm/ | n. v. |壤土，肥土；用壤土覆盖|A rich, friable soil containing a relatively equal mixture of sand and silt and a somewhat smaller proportion of clay.  To cover or dress with loam. |The gardener enriched the loam with compost. 园丁用堆肥改良壤土。|The farmer loamed the field before planting. 农民在播种前用壤土覆盖田地。|近义词：soil, earth, dirt|反义词:sand, clay|词性:名词,动词

loaming:/ˈloʊmɪŋ/ | n. |用壤土覆盖；漫游|The act of covering or dressing with loam; roaming, wandering. |The farmer was loaming the fields in preparation for planting. 农民正在用壤土覆盖田地，为播种做准备。| His loaming through the countryside brought him peace. 他在乡间漫游，内心感到平静。|近义词：covering, spreading, wandering, roaming|反义词: uncovering|词性:名词


词源:
From Middle English *lom, from Old English lām ("clay, loam"), from Proto-Germanic *laimaz, from Proto-Indo-European *ley- ("to smear, stick, or adhere").
