menstruated:/ˈmen.struː.eɪ.tɪd/
verb
月经来潮
Having experienced menstruation; having had a period.
She menstruated for the first time when she was twelve. 她十二岁第一次来月经。
menstruated for the first time when she was twelve.
近义词: had her period, had a period
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider pre-menarcheal for someone who hasn't yet started menstruating)
词性: verb
