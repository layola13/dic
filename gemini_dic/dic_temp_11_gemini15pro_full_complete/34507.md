clean slate:/kliːn sleɪt/ | n. |新的开始；清白的记录；干净的石板|A clean slate refers to a fresh start, as if starting over with a blank writing slate.  It implies a situation where past mistakes or debts are forgiven or forgotten. |He was given a clean slate after he apologized. 他道歉后被给予了新的开始。| synonyms: fresh start, new beginning, blank slate | antonyms: criminal record, bad history | noun
