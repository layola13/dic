adjoin:/əˈdʒɔɪn/
verb|毗邻；邻接；贴近|be next to or have a common boundary with.|例句：Their house adjoins ours. 他们的房子与我们的房子相邻。|近义词：border, abut, neighbor, join, connect|反义词:separate, detach, divide|v.

adjoin:/əˈdʒɔɪn/
verb|添加；附加|add or attach.|例句：The lawyer adjoined a codicil to the will. 律师在遗嘱上附加了一个附录。|近义词：add, append, attach, annex|反义词:remove, subtract, detach|v.
