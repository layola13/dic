amendment:/əˈmendmənt/ | n. | 修改，修正，修正案 | a minor change or addition designed to improve a text, piece of legislation, etc. | The First Amendment to the US Constitution guarantees freedom of speech. 美国宪法第一修正案保障言论自由。| synonyms: alteration, modification, revision | antonyms:  preservation, retention | noun

amendment:/əˈmendmənt/ | n. | 改正，改进 | the act of amending something |  Parliament is currently debating amendments to the education bill. 议会目前正在讨论对教育法案的修正。| synonyms: improvement, correction, betterment | antonyms: damage, worsening | noun
