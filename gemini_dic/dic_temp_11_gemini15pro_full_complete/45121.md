cucumber:/ˈkjuːkʌmbər/ (US) /ˈkjuːkʌmbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|黄瓜|A long, green fruit with a smooth skin, usually eaten raw in salads or pickled.|She sliced the cucumber thinly for the salad. 她把黄瓜切成薄片做沙拉。|近义词:gherkin (specifically a small pickled cucumber)|反义词: N/A|noun

cucumber:/ˈkjuːkʌmbər/ (US) /ˈkjuːkʌmbə(r)/ (UK)| adj. (informal)|冷静的，镇定的|Remaining calm and composed, especially in a difficult situation.|He stayed cool as a cucumber during the emergency.  他在紧急情况下保持冷静。|近义词:calm, composed, collected|反义词:agitated, nervous|adjective
