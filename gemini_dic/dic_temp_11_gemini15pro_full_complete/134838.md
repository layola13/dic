nonmitigative:/ˌnɒnˈmɪtɪɡətɪv/
adjective
非减轻的，不缓和的
Not serving to mitigate, alleviate, or lessen.  Failing to reduce the severity of something.
The company's nonmitigative response to the crisis further angered the public.  公司对危机不缓和的反应进一步激怒了公众。
The judge imposed a nonmitigative sentence due to the severity of the crime.  由于罪行的严重性，法官判处了不得减轻的刑罚。
Near Synonyms:  unmitigated, non-alleviating, exacerbating, aggravating
Antonyms:  mitigative, alleviating, lessening, reducing
词性: adjective
