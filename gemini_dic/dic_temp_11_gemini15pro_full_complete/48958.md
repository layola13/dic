degelled:/diːˈdʒɛld/
adjective
去胶的，脱胶的
having had the gelatinous coating removed.
Degelled linseed meal is commonly used in animal feed.  脱胶亚麻籽粕通常用于动物饲料。
Degelled sesame seeds are often used in baking. 脱胶芝麻通常用于烘焙。
近义词：husked, shelled, decorticated
反义词：gelled, coated
