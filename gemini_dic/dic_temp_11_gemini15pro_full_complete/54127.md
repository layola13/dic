discouragement:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒmənt/
n.
沮丧；气馁；令人沮丧的事物
Lack of confidence or enthusiasm; the act of discouraging someone.
His constant criticism was a source of discouragement to the team. 他不断的批评让团队成员感到很沮丧。
近义词:dejection, despondency, disappointment
反义词:encouragement, hope, inspiration
词性:noun

discourage:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒ/
vt.
使气馁；劝阻；阻碍
To cause someone to lose confidence or enthusiasm; to try to persuade someone not to do something.
The rain discouraged us from going for a walk.  下雨让我们打消了去散步的念头。
近义词:deter, dissuade, hinder
反义词:encourage, promote, support
词性:verb
