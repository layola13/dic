noncontrolling:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrəʊlɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈtroʊlɪŋ/
adjective
非控制性的
Not having control or authority over something, especially in a business context.
The noncontrolling interest in the subsidiary is held by other shareholders.  这家子公司的非控制性权益由其他股东持有。
Minority shareholders hold a noncontrolling stake in the company. 少数股东持有公司的非控制性股份。
近义词:minority, subordinate
反义词:controlling, majority
词性:adjective

non-controlling:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrəʊlɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈtroʊlɪŋ/
noun
非控制性权益
The portion of a subsidiary's equity that is not attributable to the parent company.
The financial statements reflect the noncontrolling interest in the company's subsidiaries. 财务报表反映了公司子公司的非控制性权益。
The non-controlling interest represents the ownership held by outside shareholders. 非控制性权益代表外部股东持有的所有权。
近义词:minority interest
反义词:controlling interest
词性:noun
