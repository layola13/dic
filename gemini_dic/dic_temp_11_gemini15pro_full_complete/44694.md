crude:/kruːd/| adj. n.|粗糙的；天然的；粗鲁的；原油|
Of raw or natural state; not refined or processed.  Also, lacking culture or refinement; vulgar. Can refer to crude oil, the unrefined petroleum product.|
例句：The furniture was crude and poorly made. 家具很粗糙，做工很差。
例句：He made a crude joke that offended everyone. 他讲了个粗俗的笑话，冒犯了所有人。
近义词：raw, unrefined, rough, vulgar, unpolished, coarse|
反义词：refined, sophisticated, polished, cultured, elegant|
词性:形容词, 名词

crude:/kruːd/| n.|原油|
Unrefined petroleum.|
例句：The price of crude has been fluctuating wildly. 原油价格一直在剧烈波动。
例句：The pipeline carries crude from the oil fields to the refinery. 这条管道将原油从油田输送到炼油厂。
近义词：crude oil, petroleum|
反义词: refined oil|
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *crūdus*  meaning "raw, bloody, unripe, immature," related to *cruor* "gore, blood."
