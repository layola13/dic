mesothorium:/ˌmeɪzəˈθɔːriəm/ | n. | 中钍 | A radioactive isotope of radium formed from thorium by radioactive decay.  It was historically used in luminous paint. | Mesothorium was used in some clock dials before the dangers of radioactivity were fully understood. 中钍在人们充分了解放射性的危险之前曾被用于一些钟表表盘。 | synonyms: radium isotope, radioactive isotope | antonyms: stable isotope | noun
