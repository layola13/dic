doxie:/ˈdɑːksi/| n.|腊肠犬;达克斯猎犬|A dachshund, a breed of dog with a long body and short legs. |例句：My doxie loves to burrow under blankets. 我的腊肠犬喜欢钻到毯子下面。

例句：The doxie's short legs make it difficult for him to climb stairs.  腊肠犬的短腿使它爬楼梯很困难。|近义词：dachshund, sausage dog|反义词:|名词
