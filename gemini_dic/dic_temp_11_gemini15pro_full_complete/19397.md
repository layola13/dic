bicarinate:/baɪˈkærɪneɪt/
adjective
双脊的，有两条隆起的
Having two keels or ridges.  Referring specifically to a structure, typically a bone or shell, featuring two prominent longitudinal ridges.
The bicarinate sternum of the bird is a key feature for flight.  这种鸟的双脊胸骨是飞行的关键特征。
The fossil revealed a bicarinate skull, unlike any seen before. 这块化石展现了一个双脊的头骨，这与之前见过的任何头骨都不一样。
近义词:ridged, keeled
反义词:smooth, flat
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *bi- "two" + carina "keel".
