circumspectively:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈspektɪvli/
adverb
谨慎地；小心地；周到地
In a cautious and prudent manner, considering all possible consequences.
He moved circumspectively through the dark alley, wary of any hidden dangers. 他小心翼翼地穿过黑暗的小巷，提防任何隐藏的危险。
The lawyer circumspectively reviewed the contract before advising his client to sign. 律师在建议客户签字之前，谨慎地审查了合同。
Synonyms: cautiously, prudently, carefully, discreetly, gingerly
Antonyms: recklessly, carelessly, rashly, impulsively
词性: 副词
