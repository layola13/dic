cypsela:/sɪpˈsiːlə/| n. |瘦果，菊科植物的果实|A dry, one-seeded fruit formed from a double ovary, of which only one develops a seed, as in the dandelion. It is characteristic of the Compositae family (also known as Asteraceae).|例句：The dandelion seed you blew away is technically a cypsela. 你吹走的蒲公英种子实际上是一个瘦果。

例句：Cypselas are often dispersed by the wind thanks to their feathery pappus. 瘦果通常借助它们羽毛状的冠毛被风传播。|近义词：achene, fruit|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *κύψελος (kúpselos, “chest, box”)
