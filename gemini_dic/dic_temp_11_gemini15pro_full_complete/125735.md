munster:/ˈmʌnstər/ (US), /ˈmʌnstə/ (UK)| n.|芒斯特奶酪; 芒斯特; 怪物; 恶棍|A type of semi-soft cheese with a strong aroma and flavor, originally from Munster, France. Also, a monstrous being, a villain, or something exceptionally large.|I tried a bit of munster cheese, but it was too strong for me. 我尝了一点芒斯特奶酪，但对我来说味道太浓了。

The movie featured a terrifying munster. 这部电影以一个可怕的怪物为主角。

|近义词：(for cheese) livarot, pont l'évêque; (for monster) monster, beast, villain|反义词:(for monster) hero, angel|词性:noun
