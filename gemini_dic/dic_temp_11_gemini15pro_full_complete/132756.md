nondominant:/ˌnɒnˈdɒmɪnənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈdɑːmɪnənt/| adj.|非优势的，非支配的|Not dominant; less powerful or important than the other.|She writes with her nondominant hand. 她用她的非惯用手写字。|Near synonyms: subordinate, secondary, weaker|Antonyms: dominant, primary, stronger|adjective

nondominance:/ˌnɒnˈdɒmɪnəns/ /ˌnɑːnˈdɑːmɪnəns/| n.|非优势，非支配地位|The state or quality of being nondominant.|The nondominance of her left hand was evident in her writing. 她左手不灵活在她写字时很明显。|Near synonyms: subordination, secondariness|Antonyms: dominance, primacy|noun
