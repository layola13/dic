lampadrome:/ˈlæmpəˌdroʊm/
n.
回文数
A number that remains the same when its digits are reversed.
The number 121 is a lampadrome because it reads the same forwards and backwards. 121 这个数字是回文数，因为它正着读和倒着读都一样。
