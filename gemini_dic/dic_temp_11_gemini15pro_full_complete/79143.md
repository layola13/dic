gladnesses:/ˈɡlædnɪsɪz/
n.
快乐，高兴
The state of being glad; happiness.
Her face shone with gladness. 她的脸上洋溢着喜悦。
近义词：joys, happiness, delights
反义词：sadnesses, sorrows, griefs
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

Etymology:
From Middle English *gladnes, from Old English *glædnes, from Proto-Germanic *glaidiniz. Equivalent to glad +‎ -ness.
