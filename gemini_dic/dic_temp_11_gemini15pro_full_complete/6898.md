anchoritess: /ˈæŋkərɪtɪs/ | n. | 女隐士 | A woman who withdraws from the world for religious reasons and lives in seclusion, often in a small cell attached to a church. |  She lived as an anchoritess for fifty years, dedicating her life to prayer and contemplation. 她作为一名女隐士生活了五十年，将一生奉献给了祈祷和沉思。| synonym: recluse, hermit (female), nun | antonym: socialite, extrovert | noun
