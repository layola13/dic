intertrades:/ˈɪntərˌtreɪdz/| n.|行业间交易，不同行业间的贸易|Transactions or commerce occurring between different industries or sectors of the economy. |Example 1: Intertrades between the agricultural and manufacturing sectors have increased significantly. 农业和制造业之间的行业间贸易显著增加。

Example 2: The government is encouraging intertrades to stimulate economic growth. 政府正在鼓励行业间贸易以刺激经济增长。|近义词：cross-industry trade, intersectoral trade|反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly "intratrade" - trade within a single industry) |词性:noun
