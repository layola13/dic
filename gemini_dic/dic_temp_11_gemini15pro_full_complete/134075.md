nonillusive:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːsɪv/
adjective
非虚幻的；真实的
Not illusory; real.
The photograph provided nonillusive proof of his presence at the scene. 这张照片提供了他在现场的真实证据。
近义词:real, actual, genuine, authentic, verifiable
反义词:illusory, deceptive, misleading, unreal, false
词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of  非，相反的
illusive (adjective): deceptive, misleading  虚幻的，迷惑人的
