lagging:/ˈlæɡɪŋ/| adj.|落后的，迟缓的，滞后的|falling behind; progressing too slowly|The company's lagging sales are a cause for concern. 该公司滞后的销售额令人担忧。|例句：The lagging economy is affecting many businesses. 经济滞后影响了许多企业。

例句：His lagging performance in school worried his parents. 他在学校落后的表现使他的父母担心。|近义词：slow, delayed, sluggish, trailing|反义词：leading, advanced, ahead, rapid|词性:形容词

lag:/læɡ/| v. n.|落后，滞后|to fall behind in movement, progress, or development; a falling behind; the amount by which someone or something falls behind|The project is lagging behind schedule. 该项目落后于计划。|例句：He lagged behind the other runners. 他落后于其他跑步者。

例句：There's a time lag between infection and the appearance of symptoms. 感染和症状出现之间有一段时间的滞后。|近义词：delay, linger, trail, fall behind|反义词：lead, precede, hasten, accelerate|词性:动词/名词
