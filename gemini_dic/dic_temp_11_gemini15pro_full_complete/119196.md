mg:/ˈemˈdʒiː/| n. |毫克|milligram, a unit of mass equal to one thousandth of a gram.  It's used to measure small weights, especially in scientific contexts and for medications.|The doctor prescribed 500 mg of the antibiotic.|Synonym: milligram|Antonym: kg (kilogram) (opposite in terms of scale, not a direct opposite)|abbreviation
