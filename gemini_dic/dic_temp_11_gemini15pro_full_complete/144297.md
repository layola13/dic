outspirit:/ˌaʊtˈspɪrɪt/| n.|优越的精神；压倒性的勇气|Superior spirit or courage.|His outspirit was evident in his unwavering determination. 他坚定不移的决心显示出他的非凡勇气。|Synonyms: mettle, fortitude, resolve|Antonyms: cowardice, timidity|Noun
