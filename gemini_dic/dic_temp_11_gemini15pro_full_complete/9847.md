aoristic:/ˌeɪəˈrɪstɪk/
| adj.|非限定时间的，未完成体的|Referring to an action or state without specifying its beginning or end; timeless; unbounded.|The aoristic use of the verb gives the narrative a sense of immediacy.|Synonyms: timeless, indefinite, unspecified|Antonyms: specific, defined, limited|adjective
