irritatory:/ˈɪrɪteɪtəri/ | adj. |刺激性的， раздражающий|Causing irritation; annoying.|The dust was irritatory to her eyes.  这灰尘刺激了她的眼睛。|The constant noise was irritatory.  持续不断的噪音令人烦躁。|近义词:irritating, annoying, vexing, bothersome|反义词:soothing, calming, pleasant|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *irritatorius, from Latin irritatus (past participle of irritare “to irritate”) + -orius.
