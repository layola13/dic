articulating:/ɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪŋ/
articulating| adj.|清晰表达的，善于表达的|Expressing oneself readily, clearly, and effectively.|She is very articulating when it comes to expressing her opinions. 她非常善于表达自己的观点。|近义词：eloquent, fluent, expressive|反义词：inarticulate, mumbled, unclear|词性:形容词
articulate:/ɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
articulate| v.|清晰地表达，清楚地说|To express (an idea or feeling) fluently and coherently.|He articulated his concerns to the board. 他向董事会清楚地表达了他的担忧。|近义词：express, voice, communicate|反义词：mumble, slur, stammer|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin articulatus, past participle of articulare "to utter distinctly," from Latin articulus "small joint, division," diminutive of artus "joint."
