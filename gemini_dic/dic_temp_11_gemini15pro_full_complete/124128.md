mortgagers:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒərz/
noun
抵押人，按揭人
A person who mortgages property.  A mortgager gives a mortgage to a mortgagee (typically a lender) as security for a loan.
The mortgagers were struggling to keep up with their monthly payments. 抵押人正在努力跟上每月的付款。
The bank reassured the mortgagers that their homes were safe. 银行向抵押人保证他们的房屋是安全的。
synonyms: borrower, debtor, loan holder
antonyms: lender, creditor, mortgagee
