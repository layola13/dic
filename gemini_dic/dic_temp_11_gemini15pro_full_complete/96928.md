inexpressiveness:/ˌɪnɪkˈsprɛsɪvnəs/| n.|缺乏表情，不善表达，无表情|lack of expressiveness; inability to express one's thoughts or feelings readily.|Her inexpressiveness made it difficult to know what she was thinking. 她的不善表达让人很难知道她在想什么。|Synonyms: impassivity, reserve, taciturnity, reticence|Antonyms: expressiveness, eloquence, volubility|noun
