ashlar: /ˈæʃlər/ | n. | 方石；琢石；砌石 | A squared building stone, typically one of many forming part of a wall. | The walls were built of finely dressed ashlar.  这些墙是用精细加工的方石建造的。 |  The exterior of the building is clad in ashlar. 这座建筑的外墙是用方石覆盖的。 | Synonyms: facing stone, dressed stone, block | Antonyms: rubble, uncut stone | noun

ashlar: /ˈæʃlər/ | adj. | 方石的；琢石的 | Made of or resembling ashlar. | The ashlar walls gave the building a grand appearance. 方石墙使这座建筑显得宏伟。 |  The architect specified an ashlar finish for the facade.  建筑师指定了立面要用方石饰面。| Synonyms:  squared, dressed, hewn | Antonyms: rough, uncut, natural | adjective
