imbow:/ɪmˈboʊ/
verb
拱起，使成弓形；装饰，点缀
to curve or bend something into a bow shape; to adorn or decorate something.
The trees imbow over the path. 树木在小路上方拱起。
The church was imbowed with flowers for the wedding. 教堂里为了婚礼装饰着鲜花。
Synonyms: arch, curve, bend, adorn, decorate, embellish
Antonyms: straighten, flatten, strip, bare
词性: 动词
