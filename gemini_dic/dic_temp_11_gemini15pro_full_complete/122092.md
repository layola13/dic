mistihead: /ˈmɪstihɛd/ | n. | 迷糊; 神志不清的状态; 困惑 | A state of mental confusion or bewilderment. |  He wandered in a mistihead after the blow to his head. 他头部受击后，在神志不清的状态下游荡。|  She was in a mistihead after the long flight. 长途飞行后，她感到迷糊。| 近义词: confusion, bewilderment, daze, fog | 反义词: clarity, lucidity | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Mistihead" is a Scottish word derived from "mist" (fog, obscurity) + "head."  The connection is clear: a head filled with mist implies mental confusion.
