overconscious:/ˌoʊvərˈkɒnʃəs/| adj.|过度在意; 过分自觉的; 神经过敏的|Excessively aware of oneself and one's actions or appearance, often to the point of being self-conscious and awkward.|She was overconscious of her appearance after the accident, always adjusting her clothes and checking her reflection.  事故之后她过分在意自己的外表，总是整理衣服，照镜子。|近义词：self-conscious, insecure, sensitive, uneasy|反义词：confident, relaxed, oblivious|词性:形容词
