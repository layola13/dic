bipolarity: /baɪˌpoʊˈlærɪti/ | n. | 双极性，两极分化 | The state of having two opposite or contrasting poles or aspects.  A system of world order in which the majority of global economic, military and cultural influence is held between two states. | The world was in a state of bipolarity during the Cold War. 冷战时期世界处于两极分化的状态。| The bipolarity of the political landscape has led to gridlock.  政治格局的两极分化导致了僵局。 | duality, dichotomy | unity, uniformity | noun
