一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? = 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does she think about the new policy? = 她对新政策有什么看法？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are they doing in the garden? = 他们在花园里做什么？
What is he cooking for dinner tonight? = 他今晚晚餐做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? = 她做了什么值得获得这个奖项？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the meeting? = 会议上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the lecture? = 讲座期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before making the decision? = 他们在做决定之前讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen if we don't act now? = 如果我们现在不采取行动会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候她将已经学习什么多久了？
