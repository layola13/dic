osmic:/ˈɒzmɪk/ (US); /ˈɒzmɪk/ (UK)| adj.|锇的；含锇的；四价锇的|Relating to or containing osmium, especially in its +4 oxidation state.|例句：Osmic acid is a highly toxic compound. 锇酸是一种剧毒化合物。

例句：The osmic stain is used in microscopy to visualize lipids. 锇酸染色剂用于显微镜观察脂质。


近义词:osmous|反义词:None|词性:adjective
