grosses:/ˈɡroʊsɪz/ (US), /ˈɡrəʊsɪz/ (UK)| n. |总收入，票房收入|The total amounts received (as for tickets) before any deductions are made.  It refers specifically to revenue from ticket sales in the entertainment industry.|The movie's grosses exceeded all expectations. 这部电影的票房收入超过了所有预期。|近义词：receipts, takings, earnings|反义词:losses, expenses|noun

grosses:/ˈɡroʊsɪz/ (US), /ˈɡrəʊsɪz/ (UK)| v. |总收入达…|To earn a specified amount of money as gross revenue.|The film grossed over $100 million worldwide. 这部电影的全球总收入超过了1亿美元。|近义词：earn, make, bring in|反义词:lose|verb
