overmodernizations:/ˌoʊvərˌmɑːdərnaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
noun
过度现代化
The excessive and often inappropriate application of modern technologies, methods, or ideas to something, often resulting in negative consequences or the loss of traditional values.
The overmodernizations of the historic city center led to a sterile and characterless environment.  过度现代化改造导致历史悠久的市中心变得毫无生气，缺乏特色。
The architect lamented the overmodernizations that had stripped the building of its original charm.  这位建筑师对过度现代化改造使建筑失去了原有的魅力感到惋惜。
Synonyms: excessive modernization, inappropriate modernization
Antonyms: preservation, conservation
