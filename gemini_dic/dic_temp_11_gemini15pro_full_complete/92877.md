idealises:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzɪz/
verb
理想化，将…理想化
regards or represents as perfect or better than in reality.
She idealises her childhood. 她将她的童年理想化了。
idealize, romanticize, glorify
denigrate, vilify, disparage
v.
