burdensomenesses:/ˈbɜːrdənsəmnɪsɪz/
n.
沉重；繁重；累赘；麻烦
The state or quality of being burdensome; troublesomeness.  A burdensome or onerous thing.
The burdensomenesses of caring for a sick relative can be overwhelming. 照顾生病的亲戚的繁重负担有时会让人难以承受。
Near Synonyms: burdens, encumbrances, onerousness, troublesomeness
Antonyms: easiness, lightness, simplicity
Noun
