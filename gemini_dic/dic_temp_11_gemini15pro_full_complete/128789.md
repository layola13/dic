nightclubbers:/ˈnaɪtklʌbərz/
noun
夜总会常客，夜店咖
People who frequent nightclubs.
例句：The nightclubbers danced until dawn. 夜总会常客们跳舞跳到天亮。

例句：The street was crowded with nightclubbers after midnight.  午夜过后，街上挤满了夜店咖。

近义词：partygoers, revelers, clubbers
反义词：homebodies
词性:n.
