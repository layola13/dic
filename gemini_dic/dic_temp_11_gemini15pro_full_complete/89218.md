homework: /ˈhoʊmwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈhəʊmwɜːk/ (UK) | n. | 家庭作业 | Schoolwork that students are assigned to do at home. |  I have a lot of homework to do tonight.  今晚我有很多家庭作业要做。 | assignments, schoolwork, studies | leisure, playtime | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "home" + "work".  First recorded in the early 19th century.  Originally referred to any work done at home, but later specialized to mean school assignments done outside of class.
