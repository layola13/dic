blurter:/ˈblɜːrtər/| n.|脱口而出的人；快嘴的人|A person who blurts out something suddenly and without thinking.|He's a blurter, always saying the wrong thing at the wrong time. 他是个快嘴的人，总是在错误的时间说错话。|近义词：loudmouth, chatterbox|反义词：reticent person, quiet person|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "blurt" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Blurt" comes from Middle English *blurten*, probably imitative of the sound of sudden speech.
