monosyllabic:/ˌmɒnəsɪˈlæbɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəsɪˈlæbɪk/| adj.|单音节的|Consisting of one syllable.  A word or utterance having only one syllable.|例句：The word "cat" is monosyllabic.  单词"cat"是单音节词。|例句：He gave a monosyllabic reply. 他只用一个音节回答。|近义词：short, brief|反义词：polysyllabic|词性:形容词
