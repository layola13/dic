mediaeval:/ˌmediˈiːvəl/ /ˌmiːdˈiːvəl/| adj.|中世纪的|Relating to the Middle Ages, the period in European history from about AD 500 to 1500.|例句：Mediaeval castles are fascinating structures. 中世纪的城堡是令人着迷的建筑。|例句：He studied mediaeval literature and philosophy. 他研究中世纪文学和哲学。|近义词：medieval|反义词：modern, contemporary|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old French *medieval, from Late Latin medievālis, from Latin medius (“middle”) + Latin aevum (“age”).  The spelling with the "ae" digraph reflects the Latin origin, while "medieval" is a more modern variant.
