goaling:/ˈɡoʊlɪŋ/
noun
目标设定；目标管理
The process of setting and achieving goals.
Example: Goaling is an important part of project management.  目标设定是项目管理的重要组成部分。

Example: Effective goaling requires clear objectives and measurable outcomes. 有效的目标设定需要明确的目标和可衡量的结果。

近义词: goal setting, target setting, objective setting
反义词: aimlessness, drifting, lack of direction
词性: noun
