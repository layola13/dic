howlet:/ˈhaʊlɪt/ | n. | 猫头鹰（尤指仓鸮）| An owl, especially a barn owl. |例句：The howlet hooted eerily in the night.  夜里，猫头鹰发出令人毛骨悚然的叫声。

例句：A lone howlet perched atop the weathered barn. 一只孤独的猫头鹰栖息在风化的谷仓顶上。|近义词：owl, barn owl|反义词: N/A (Animals don't typically have antonyms) |词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *houlet*, *owlet*, from Old English *ūlet* (owl), diminutive of *ūle* (owl), from Proto-Germanic *uwwalǭ* (owl), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ówHs* (owl).
