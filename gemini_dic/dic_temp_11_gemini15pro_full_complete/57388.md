dozens:/ˈdʌzənz/| n.|几十，许多|A large but indefinite number (usually more than 20).  A group of twelve.|She bought dozens of eggs. 她买了许多鸡蛋。|synonyms: lots, many, numerous, scores, heaps|antonyms: few, little|noun

dozens:/ˈdʌzənz/| pronoun|许多|Used to refer to many people or things (generally more than twenty).|Dozens of people attended the concert.  许多人参加了音乐会。|synonyms: lots, many, numerous, scores, heaps|antonyms: few, little|pronoun
