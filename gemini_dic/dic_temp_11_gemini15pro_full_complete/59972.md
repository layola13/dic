edifying:/ˈedɪfaɪɪŋ/| adj.|启迪的，有教益的|Providing moral or intellectual instruction.|例句：I found the lecture very edifying. 我觉得这次讲座很有启发性。|例句：Reading good literature can be an edifying experience. 阅读优秀的文学作品可以是一次有益的体验。|近义词：instructive, enlightening, informative, enriching|反义词：unhelpful, pointless, frivolous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle French *edifier*, from Latin aedificāre ("to build," also figuratively "to construct, instruct, improve").
