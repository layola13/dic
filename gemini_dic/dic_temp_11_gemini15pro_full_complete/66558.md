exhibitioner:/ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənər/
n.
奖学金获得者；展览者
A person who receives a scholarship or grant, especially one that allows them to study or exhibit their work.
She was a talented artist and received an exhibitioner grant to showcase her paintings in Paris. 她是一位才华横溢的艺术家，获得了展览者奖金，得以在巴黎展示她的画作。
近义词:scholar, recipient, exhibitor
反义词:None
词性:Noun
