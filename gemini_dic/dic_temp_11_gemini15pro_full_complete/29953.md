cavendish:/ˈkævəndɪʃ/| n.|卡文迪许（姓氏；英国物理学家；一种香蕉品种）|A British physicist known for his discovery of hydrogen and accurate determination of the density of the Earth. Also a type of banana.|例句：Henry Cavendish was a brilliant scientist. 亨利·卡文迪许是一位杰出的科学家。

例句：Cavendish bananas are popular for their sweet taste. 卡文迪许香蕉因其甜美的味道而受欢迎。|近义词：(For the banana) plantain, Gros Michel|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


cavendish:/ˈkævəndɪʃ/| adj.|卡文迪许的；与卡文迪许有关的|Relating to or associated with Henry Cavendish or the Cavendish family.|例句：The Cavendish experiment measured the gravitational constant. 卡文迪许实验测量了万有引力常数。

例句：Cavendish Laboratory is a renowned physics research facility. 卡文迪许实验室是一个著名的物理研究机构。|近义词:N/A|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From a place name, probably Cavendish in Suffolk, England, derived from Old English *Cafnan dic "Cafna's ditch".
