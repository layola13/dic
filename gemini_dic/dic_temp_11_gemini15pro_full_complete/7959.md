antheridium:/ˌænθəˈrɪdɪəm/ | n. | 囊 | The male sex organ of algae, mosses, ferns, fungi, and other nonflowering plants. |  The antheridium releases sperm that swim to the archegonium, where fertilization takes place. |  gametangium |  archegonium | noun
Etymology: From New Latin, from Greek *antheros 'flowering' + -idium.
