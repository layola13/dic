nexus:/ˈnɛksəs/| n.|联系，连接，中心，枢纽|a connection or series of connections linking two or more things.|The airport is a major nexus for international travel.  这个机场是国际旅行的主要枢纽。|synonyms: center, core, hub, focus, link|antonyms: periphery, edge, boundary|noun

nexus:/ˈnɛksəs/| n.|核心，焦点|the central and most important point or place.|The nexus of the problem is a lack of communication.  问题的核心是缺乏沟通。|synonyms: heart, crux, kernel|antonyms: fringe, margin|noun
