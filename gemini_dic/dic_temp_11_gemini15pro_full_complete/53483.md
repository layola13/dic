disarray's:/dɪsəˈreɪz/
noun
混乱，凌乱的状态
The possessive form of "disarray," indicating the disarray belonging to something or someone.
The room was in complete disarray's after the party. 房间在派对后一片狼藉。(This example is grammatically incorrect.  Possessives are rarely used with abstract nouns like "disarray."  A better sentence would be: The room was in complete disarray after the party.)
Near Synonyms: disorder, chaos, confusion, mess
Antonyms: order, tidiness, organization
