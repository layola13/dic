divulge: /daɪˈvʌldʒ/ /dɪˈvʌldʒ/
verb
泄露；透露
To make known (private or sensitive information).
The company divulged its plans for a new product.  公司透露了其新产品的计划。

He refused to divulge the source of his information. 他拒绝透露消息来源。

近义词: reveal, disclose, unveil, expose
反义词: conceal, hide, suppress, withhold
词性: verb
