drawler:/ˈdrɔːlər/ | n. | 慢吞吞的人；磨蹭者 | Someone who speaks or acts slowly and lazily. | He's such a drawler; it takes him forever to tell a simple story. 他真是个慢吞吞的人，讲个简单的故事都要花很长时间。|  近义词: slowpoke, laggard, procrastinator | 反义词: go-getter, hustler | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From the verb "drawl" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Drawl" comes from  imitating the slow, lengthened way of speaking.
