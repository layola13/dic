lindiform:/ˈlɪndɪfɔːrm/| adj.| リンジ状の (Japanese, meaning lindiform); shaped like a lind; resembling a pool or sheet of water|Having a smooth, level surface resembling a sheet of water or a pool.  This term is often used in descriptive botany or geology. | The lindiform expanse of the salt flats stretched to the horizon.  盐滩的广阔水面延伸到地平线。| The geologist described the lindiform lava flow. 地质学家描述了片状熔岩流。| 近义词：planar, flat, level, sheet-like | 反义词: uneven, irregular, bumpy | 词性: adjective
