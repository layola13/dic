archimedes:/ˌɑːrkɪˈmiːdiːz/| n.|阿基米德|A Greek mathematician, physicist, engineer, inventor, and astronomer.  Considered one of the greatest mathematicians of all time, he is known for his work on levers, buoyancy, and the approximation of pi.| Archimedes discovered the principle of buoyancy. 阿基米德发现了浮力原理。|Synonyms:  mathematician, scientist, inventor|Antonyms: N/A|noun
