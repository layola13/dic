internist:/ˈɪnˌtɜːrnɪst/ /ɪnˈtɜːrnɪst/ | n. |内科医生|A physician who specializes in the diagnosis and medical treatment of adults, especially internal diseases/organs.| My family doctor referred me to an internist for further evaluation. 我的家庭医生让我去看内科医生做进一步检查。|  He saw an internist about his persistent cough. 他因为持续咳嗽去看了一位内科医生。 | 近义词:physician, doctor, general practitioner | 反义词: surgeon, specialist (although an internist *is* a specialist, they are a specialist of general internal medicine, as opposed to a specific area like cardiology) | 词性: noun
