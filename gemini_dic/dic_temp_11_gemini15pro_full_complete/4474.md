albertite: /ˈælbərˌtaɪt/
n.
沥青精；亚伯达沥青
A black, asphalt-like hydrocarbon mineral, a variety of asphaltum, found in Albert County, New Brunswick, Canada.  It is jet-black in colour, has a brilliant lustre, and a hardness of 1–2.
Albertite is found in veins and is mined for use in the production of oil and gas. 沥青精存在于矿脉中，被开采用于石油和天然气的生产。
Albertite was once an important source of kerosene. 沥青精曾经是煤油的重要来源。

近义词: asphaltum, gilsonite, uintaite
反义词: None

词性: noun
