hospitalised:/ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːspɪtəˌlaɪzd/ (US)| v. | 住院，住院治疗 |Admitted to a hospital for treatment. |例句：He was hospitalised for a week after the accident. 他在事故后住院一周。|例句：She was hospitalised with a serious infection. 她因严重感染住院。|近义词：admitted, hospitalized (US) |反义词:discharged, released|词性:动词

hospitalised:/ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːspɪtəˌlaɪzd/ (US)| adj. | 住院的 |Receiving treatment in a hospital. |例句：The hospitalised patient is recovering well. 住院的病人恢复良好。|例句：She visited her hospitalised grandmother every day. 她每天都去看望住院的祖母。|近义词：inpatient, admitted|反义词:outpatient, discharged|词性:形容词
