bulldogged:/ˈbʊldɒɡd/ (UK) /ˈbʊldɔːɡd/ (US)| adj. |顽强的，坚韧不拔的|Showing the tenacious and resolute qualities of a bulldog; persistent.|He bulldogged his way through the crowd. 他顽强地挤过了人群。|Synonyms: tenacious, persistent, resolute, determined, persevering|Antonyms: irresolute, wavering, hesitant|adjective

bulldogging:/ˈbʊldɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbʊldɔːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n. | 摔牛；顽强地对付|The act of wrestling a steer to the ground by seizing its horns and twisting its neck, or the act of persistently tackling a problem.|He won the bulldogging competition at the rodeo. 他赢得了牛仔竞技表演中的摔牛比赛。|Synonyms: wrestling, grappling, tackling, confronting|Antonyms: yielding, surrendering, giving up|noun
