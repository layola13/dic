circumambulate:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈæmbjʊleɪt/ /ˌsɜːrkəmˈæmbjəleɪt/| v. | 绕行，环行|To walk all the way around something, especially for religious or ceremonial reasons.|例句：Pilgrims circumambulate the sacred shrine. 朝圣者绕着圣殿环行。

例句：They circumambulated the city walls. 他们绕着城墙走了一圈。

|近义词：circumnavigate, walk around, circle, orbit|反义词: - |词性：动词
