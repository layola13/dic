buoyage:/ˈbɔɪɪdʒ/| n.|航标系统，浮标系统|A system of buoys used to mark channels, dangers, and other features of navigable waters. |The buoyage system in this harbor is well-maintained.  这个港口的航标系统维护良好。|Near synonyms:  navigation aids, channel markers, beacon system|Antonym: None|Noun

buoyage:/ˈbɔɪɪdʒ/| n.|浮标，航标|Buoys collectively. |The buoyage warned of shallow water. 这些浮标警告水浅。|Near synonyms: buoys, markers, beacons|Antonym: None|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bouéage*, from bouée (“buoy”) + -age. Ultimately from Latin *boia*, of uncertain origin.
