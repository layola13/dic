lumpier:/ˈlʌmpiər/| adj.|更块状的，更粗糙的|more lumpy; having more or larger lumps|The porridge was lumpier than usual this morning. 今早的粥比平时更块状。|The road was lumpier and harder to drive on after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，道路变得更加坑洼不平，难以行驶。|近义词：chunkier, knobbier, uneven|反义词:smooth, even, flat|词性:形容词

词源:
From lump + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  "Lump" comes from Middle English *lumpe, of uncertain origin, possibly related to a Scandinavian word meaning "shapeless mass."
