injector:/ɪnˈdʒɛktər/| n.|注射器；喷射器；注射者|A device used to inject something, such as fuel into an engine or a drug into a patient.  A person who injects something.|例句：The fuel injector is faulty. 燃料喷射器坏了。

例句：The doctor is an experienced injector. 这位医生是一位经验丰富的注射者。|近义词：syringe, needle, nozzle, jet|反义词:|noun
