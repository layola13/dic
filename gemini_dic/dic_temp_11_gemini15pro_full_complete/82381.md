gru:/ɡruː/| n.|GRU (Gated Recurrent Unit)|A type of recurrent neural network (RNN) used in deep learning, particularly in natural language processing.  It's known for its simpler architecture and faster training compared to LSTMs, while still effectively capturing long-term dependencies in sequences.|The researcher explained how GRUs can improve machine translation. 研究人员解释了GRU如何改进机器翻译。|近义词：LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory), RNN (Recurrent Neural Network)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but can contrast with simpler feedforward networks)|词性:noun
