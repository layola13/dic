ETYMOLOGY->
Coined by Lewis Carroll in "Through the Looking-Glass" (1871), possibly a blend of "gallop" and "lump."  由刘易斯卡罗尔在《爱丽丝镜中奇遇记》（1871）中创造，可能是“gallop（疾驰）”和“lump（块）”的混合。
