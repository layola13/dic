barcode:/ˈbɑːrˌkoʊd/ (US) /ˈbɑːˌkəʊd/ (UK)| n.|条形码|A machine-readable code in the form of numbers and a pattern of parallel lines of varying widths, printed on a commodity and used especially for stock control.|The cashier scanned the barcode on the milk carton.|Synonyms: bar code, Universal Product Code (UPC), product code|Antonyms: None|Noun
