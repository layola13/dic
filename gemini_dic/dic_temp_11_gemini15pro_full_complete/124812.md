muciform:/ˈmjuːsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
粘液状的；似粘液的
Resembling mucus; covered with or secreting mucus.  A muciform substance was found at the site of the infection.
The infection produced a muciform discharge.
Synonyms: mucous, mucilaginous, slimy, viscid
Antonyms: dry, parched, desiccated
词性: adjective
