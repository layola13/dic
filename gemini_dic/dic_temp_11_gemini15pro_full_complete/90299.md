housemistress:/ˈhaʊsˌmɪstrɪs/| n.|舍监，女舍监，女管家|A woman who is in charge of a house in a boarding school or other institution. |例句：The housemistress was very strict about bedtime. 舍监对就寝时间非常严格。

例句：The students went to the housemistress with their complaints. 学生们带着他们的抱怨去找舍监。|近义词：matron, superintendent, housekeeper|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps resident/student)|词性:noun
