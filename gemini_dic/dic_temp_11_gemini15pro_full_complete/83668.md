haft:/hæft/ | n. | 柄, 把手 | The handle of a tool or weapon, especially the handle of a knife, axe, or sword. | The knight grasped the haft of his sword. 骑士抓住了他的剑柄。| synonyms: handle, grip, hilt | antonyms: blade, tip | noun

haft:/hæft/ | vt. | 给…装柄 | To provide with a haft. |  He hafted the axe head securely. 他牢固地给斧头装上了柄。| synonyms: attach, fix, fit | antonyms: detach, remove | verb
