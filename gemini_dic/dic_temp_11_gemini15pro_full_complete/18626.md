bentwood:/ˈbentˌwʊd/| adj. n.|弯木制的；弯木家具|Made of wood that has been bent into a curved shape.  Also refers to furniture made from this wood.|
例句：The cafe had elegant bentwood chairs.  这家咖啡馆有优雅的弯木椅子。

例句：Bentwood is a popular material for rocking chairs. 弯木是摇椅的常用材料。

近义词：curved wood, molded wood|反义词:straight wood|词性:形容词, 名词
