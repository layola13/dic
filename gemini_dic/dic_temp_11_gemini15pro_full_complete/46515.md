dak:/dæk/
n.|麻布袋;粗麻布|A coarse jute fabric. |例句：The potatoes were delivered in a dak. 土豆是用麻布袋运来的。|近义词：burlap, Hessian, gunny|反义词: None.|noun

dak:/dɑːk/
n.|邮政;邮递| (historical, South Asia) The mail or postal service. |例句：The dak arrived late today. 今天邮件到晚了。|近义词：mail, post|反义词: None.|noun
