celsius: /ˈselsiəs/ /ˈsɛlsiəs/ | adj. n. |摄氏的；摄氏|Relating to, or having a scale of temperature in which water freezes at 0° and boils at 100° under standard conditions.|Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius. 水在100摄氏度沸腾。|The temperature is 25 degrees Celsius. 温度是25摄氏度。| synonym: centigrade | antonym: Fahrenheit | adjective, noun
