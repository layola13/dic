nonphotobiotic:/ˌnɒnˌfoʊtoʊbaɪˈɒtɪk/ | adj. | 非光合生物的 | Not deriving energy from sunlight; not involving or dependent on photosynthesis.  |  Deep-sea hydrothermal vent communities are often nonphotobiotic, relying on chemosynthesis instead. 深海热液喷口群落通常是非光合生物的，依靠化学合成生存。 | Near Antonyms: photobiotic, photosynthetic | Antonyms:  None | adjective
