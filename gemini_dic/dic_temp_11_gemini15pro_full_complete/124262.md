一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? = 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been reading lately? = 你最近一直在读什么？
What has he been working on all week? = 他整个星期一直在做什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What did she do last night? = 她昨晚做了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will she study in college? = 她大学要学什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be presenting at the conference? = 她将在会议上展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将完成什么？
What will she have written by the deadline? = 到截止日期前她将写完什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been researching by the time she publishes her paper? = 到她发表论文时，她将已经研究什么多久了？
