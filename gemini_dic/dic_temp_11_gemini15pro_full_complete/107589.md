landscape:/ˈlændskeɪp/ | n. |风景；景色；山水画；地貌；形势|A picture of natural scenery, the visible features of an area of land, or a particular situation or set of circumstances.|We admired the beautiful landscape of the Scottish Highlands. 我们欣赏了苏格兰高地的美丽景色。|近义词:scenery, view, vista, panorama, terrain |反义词:seascape, cityscape |noun

landscape:/ˈlændskeɪp/ | vt. |美化…的景观；对…进行景观美化|To improve the appearance of an area of land by planting trees, shrubs, etc.|They landscaped the garden with a variety of flowers and shrubs. 他们用各种各样的花卉和灌木美化了花园。|近义词:beautify, embellish, adorn |反义词:uglify, spoil, damage |verb
