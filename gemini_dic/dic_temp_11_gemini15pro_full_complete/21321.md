bloodwite:/ˈblʌdˌwaɪt/| n. |血价，赎罪金|A fine paid to the family of a murdered person, especially in Anglo-Saxon law, as compensation for the loss of life.| The king imposed a heavy bloodwite on the murderer. 国王对凶手处以重金血价。|Synonyms: wergild, blood money, compensation|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

bloodwite:/ˈblʌdˌwaɪt/| n. |血债|A debt incurred through bloodshed; a life for a life.|The bloodwite demanded swift and brutal retribution. 血债需要迅速而残酷的报复。|Synonyms: vendetta, feud|Antonyms: peace, reconciliation|Noun
