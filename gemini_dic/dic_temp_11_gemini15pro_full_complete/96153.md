indenture:/ɪnˈdɛntʃər/| n. vt.|契约，合同；正式凭证；学徒期|A formal legal agreement, contract, or document.  A contract binding one person to work for another for a given period of time (especially in the past).|例句：The apprentice signed an indenture with the master craftsman. 学徒与工匠大师签订了契约。

|近义词：contract, agreement, covenant, deed, bond|反义词:release, freedom|词性:noun, verb


indenture:/ɪnˈdɛntʃər/| vt.|以契约约束；签订契约|To bind (someone) by an indenture as an apprentice or laborer.|例句：He was indentured to a farmer for seven years. 他被契约约束给一个农民干七年活。

|近义词：bind, contract, obligate|反义词:release, free|词性:verb
