manic-depressive:/ˌmænɪk dɪˈprɛsɪv/ | adj. n. | 躁狂抑郁的；躁狂抑郁症患者 | Characterized by alternating periods of extreme elation and deep depression. | He was diagnosed with manic-depressive illness. 他被诊断患有躁狂抑郁症。| 近义词: bipolar | 反义词: stable, balanced | 词性: 形容词, 名词

manic-depressive:/ˌmænɪk dɪˈprɛsɪv/ | n. | 躁狂抑郁症患者 | A person suffering from manic-depressive illness. |  The support group is for manic-depressives and their families. 这个互助小组是为躁狂抑郁症患者及其家人设立的。 | 近义词: bipolar patient | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可用 mentally healthy individual) | 词性: 名词
