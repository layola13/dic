conferred:/kənˈfɜːrd/
verb
授予，赋予；商谈，商议
Grant or bestow (a title, degree, benefit, or honor); Discuss (something) with someone, typically in order to reach a decision.
例句：The university conferred an honorary degree on the renowned scientist. 大学授予这位著名科学家荣誉学位。

例句：The lawyers conferred with their client before entering the courtroom. 律师们在进入法庭前与他们的客户进行了商议。

近义词：bestow, award, grant, discuss, consult
反义词：revoke, withdraw, withhold

词性: v.

词源：From Latin conferre "to bring together, compare, contribute," from com- "together" + ferre "to carry, bear."
