calver:/ˈkælvər/| n.|版本号（基于日历日期的版本控制方案）|A versioning scheme where the version number is based on the calendar date, typically in the format YYYY.MM.DD or YY.MM.DD.|Example: We're releasing version 2023.10.27 of the software today. 我们今天发布了该软件的 2023.10.27 版本。|Example: Using calver allows for easy identification of the release date. 使用 calver 可以轻松识别发布日期。|近义词:versioning, semantic versioning|反义词:None|词性:名词
