navigo:/ˈnævɪɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈnævɪɡəʊ/ (UK)| v. |航行，导航，指引|to sail, to steer a ship, to find one's way, to travel, to direct, to manage|Example: Ancient Polynesians navigated by the stars. 古代波利尼西亚人依靠星星导航。

Example:  I navigated the complex city streets with the help of GPS. 我借助GPS导航穿梭于复杂的城市街道。|近义词：sail, steer, pilot, guide, direct, traverse, journey, travel|反义词: drift, wander, stray|词性:verb
