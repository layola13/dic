flighter:/ˈflaɪtər/| n.| 逃跑者；短程航班；轻快的马；容易激动的人|Someone who flees or runs away, especially from danger or unpleasantness; a short-distance flight; a lively, spirited horse; someone easily excited or aroused.|The flighter abandoned his post in the face of enemy fire.|近义词：escapee, runaway, deserter|反义词: remainer, stayer|词性:noun
Etymology: From the verb "to flight," meaning to flee or run away, ultimately from Old English *flēoht, related to "fly."
