disfurniture:/dɪsˈfɜːrnɪʃər/ | vt. | 撤掉家具；使空无家具 | To remove the furniture from (a room or building). |  They disfurnished the apartment before moving out. 他们搬出去之前把公寓里的家具都搬走了。

The landlord disfurnished the property before putting it on the market.  房东在把房子放到市场上之前，把家具都搬走了。| 近义词：strip, clear out | 反义词：furnish | 词性：动词
