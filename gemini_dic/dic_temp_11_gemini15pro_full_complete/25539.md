bullterriers:/ˈbʊlˌtɛriərz/
n.|斗牛犬（复数）|A medium-sized breed of dog in the terrier family, originally developed in England for bull-baiting and dogfighting. Characterized by its egg-shaped head and muscular build.|Bull Terriers are known for their loyalty and playful nature. 斗牛犬以其忠诚和爱玩的天性而闻名。
|近义词: pit bull (informal, often inaccurate), Staffordshire Bull Terrier|反义词: None (breeds are not opposites)|词性: Noun
