chemosmotic:/ˌkɛmoʊzˈmɑːtɪk/ | adj. | 化学渗透的 | Relating to or involving chemiosmosis, the movement of ions across a selectively permeable membrane, down their electrochemical gradient.  |  Chemiosmotic processes are fundamental to energy production in cells. 化学渗透过程是细胞能量产生的基础。 |  The chemiosmotic theory explains how ATP is synthesized in mitochondria. 化学渗透理论解释了线粒体中ATP是如何合成的。 | synonymous:  chemiosmosis-driven, chemiosmosis-based | antonymous:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "non-chemiosmotic" or mechanisms not involving chemiosmosis) | adjective
