arthrosis:/ɑːrˈθroʊsɪs/ /ɑːˈθrəʊsɪs/
n.
关节病
A degenerative joint disease, characterized by the deterioration of cartilage and the formation of new bone at the joint surfaces.
Example:  Osteoarthritis, a common form of arthrosis, can cause significant pain and stiffness. 骨关节炎是一种常见的关节病，会导致明显的疼痛和僵硬。

Example:  His arthrosis made it difficult for him to climb stairs. 他的关节病使他爬楼梯很困难。

近义词:arthritis, joint disease
反义词:(None -  Arthrosis describes a condition, not a state. There is no direct opposite.)
词性:noun
