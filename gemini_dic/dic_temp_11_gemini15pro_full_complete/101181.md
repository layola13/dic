invigorations:/ɪnˌvɪɡəˈreɪʃənz/
|n.|鼓舞；激励；使精力充沛|The invigorations of the mountain air made him feel alive again. 山间空气的清新使他再次感到活力焕发。|近义词：refreshments, stimulations, revitalizations|反义词：enervations, debilitations|词性:名词

词源: From Latin *invigoratus*, past participle of *invigorare* "to give vigor to," from *in-* "in" + *vigor* "vigor, strength."
