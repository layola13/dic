excavation:/ˌekskəˈveɪʃn/| n.|挖掘，发掘，出土文物|the act of digging something out of the ground, especially an archaeological site|例句：The excavation of the ancient city took several years.  古城的发掘工作花了数年时间。

例句：The museum displayed artifacts from the recent excavation.  博物馆展出了最近一次挖掘出土的文物。|近义词：unearthing, digging, disinterment|反义词:burial, interment|noun

excavate:/ˈekskəveɪt/| v.|挖掘，发掘|to dig something out of the ground, especially an archaeological site|例句：Archaeologists are excavating the site of a Roman villa.  考古学家正在挖掘一座罗马别墅的遗址。

例句：The workers excavated a large hole for the foundation of the building.  工人们挖了一个大洞来打地基。|近义词：unearth, dig, disinter|反义词:bury, inter|verb
