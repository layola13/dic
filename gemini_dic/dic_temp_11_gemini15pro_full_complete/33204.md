chromule: /ˈkroʊmjul/ | n. | 色素体，载色体 | A chromule is a colored plastid other than a chloroplast, typically containing yellow or orange pigments. |  Diatoms store their chromules in vesicles. 硅藻将它们的色素体储存在囊泡中。| The chromules within the cells give the algae its distinctive color. 细胞内的色素体赋予藻类独特的颜色。| synonyms: plastid, chromatophore | antonyms: N/A | noun
