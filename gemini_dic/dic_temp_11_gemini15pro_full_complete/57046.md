doubtably:/ˈdaʊtəbli/| adv. |可疑地；怀疑地| In a doubtful manner; questionably.  It describes something that is open to doubt or uncertainty.| The information he provided was doubtably accurate. 他提供的信息准确性值得怀疑。|Synonyms: questionably, dubiously, uncertainly| Antonyms: certainly, definitely, undoubtedly| Adverb

Etymology:
From doubt + -able + -ly.  "Doubt" comes from Old French "douter" (to doubt), ultimately from Latin "dubitare".
