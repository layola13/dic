cliffhangers: /ˈklɪfˌhæŋərz/
| n. | 悬念，扣人心弦的结局 | A cliffhanger is a dramatic and exciting ending to an episode of a serial, leaving the audience in suspense and anxious not to miss the next episode. | The season finale ended on a cliffhanger, leaving viewers wondering if the main character would survive.  季终集以一个悬念结尾，让观众想知道主角是否能活下来。| synonyms: suspense, climax, hook | antonyms: resolution, conclusion | noun

cliffhanger: /ˈklɪfˌhæŋər/
