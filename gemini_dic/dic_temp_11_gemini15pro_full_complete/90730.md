humiliations:/ˌhjuːˌmɪliˈeɪʃənz/
n.
羞辱；蒙羞；丢脸；耻辱
The act of humiliating someone or the state of being humiliated.  This word refers to multiple instances of humiliation.
He suffered many humiliations throughout his childhood.  他童年时期遭受了许多羞辱。
Synonyms: disgraces, indignities, mortifications, shames
Antonyms: honors, dignities, triumphs
词性: 名词
