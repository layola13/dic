consenter:/kənˈsɛntər/| n.|同意者，赞同者|A person who consents; one who gives permission or assent.|例句：The consenters signed the agreement. 同意者在协议上签了字。|近义词：approver, assenter, concurrer|反义词:dissenter, objector|noun

consenter:/kənˈsɛntər/| v.|同意，赞同|To agree to something; to give permission or assent.|例句：She consented to the proposal. 她同意了这个提议。|近义词：agree, assent, accede|反义词:refuse, object, disagree|verb
