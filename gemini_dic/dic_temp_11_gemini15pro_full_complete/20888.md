blazoning: /ˈbleɪzənɪŋ/ | n. | 装饰; 炫耀; 公开宣扬 | the act of displaying or proclaiming something widely | The company's logo was blazoning across the banner.  公司的标志在横幅上醒目地展示着。 | synonyms: displaying, proclaiming, exhibiting, broadcasting | antonyms: concealing, hiding, suppressing | noun

blazoning: /ˈbleɪzənɪŋ/ | adj. |  炫耀的; 灿烂的 |  conspicuously bright and colorful; characterized by showy display |  The blazoning colors of the sunset filled the sky.  灿烂的日落色彩充满了天空。| synonyms:  bright, dazzling, flamboyant, showy | antonyms: dull, drab, subdued, understated | adjective
