attender:/əˈtɛndər/| n.|出席者，参加者；侍者|A person who attends a meeting, event, or place; someone who looks after or helps another person.|例句：The attender listened attentively to the speaker. 出席者专心听演讲者讲话。

例句：The nurse acted as an attender to the elderly patient.  护士充当老年病人的护理员。|近义词：participant, attendee, helper, assistant|反义词: absentee|词性:noun
