niggardish:/ˈnɪɡərdɪʃ/| adj.|吝啬的，小气的|Reluctant to give or spend; stingy; miserly.|He was niggardish with praise, rarely offering a compliment.|近义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious, frugal, tight-fisted, penny-pinching|反义词：generous, lavish, extravagant|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *nigardish, from Old Norse *hnǫggr (“stingy”) + -ish. Cognate with niggard.

注意事项:
