gabber:/ˈɡæbər/ (US), /ˈɡæbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|喋喋不休的人，爱说话的人|A person who talks a lot, especially rapidly and enthusiastically.|She's a real gabber; she can talk for hours. 她真是个话痨，能说上好几个小时。|近义词：chatterbox, talker, conversationalist|反义词：quiet person, introvert|名词

gabber:/ˈɡæbər/ (US), /ˈɡæbə(r)/ (UK)| vi.|喋喋不休，闲聊|To talk rapidly and enthusiastically, especially about unimportant things.|They gabbered away for hours about nothing in particular. 他们闲聊了几个小时，也没说什么正经事。|近义词：chatter, prattle, babble|反义词：be silent, listen|动词
