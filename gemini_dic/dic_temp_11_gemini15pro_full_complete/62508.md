endowing:/ɪnˈdaʊɪŋ/ | v. | 赋予，捐赠 | to provide with a quality, ability, or asset |  He was endowed with a natural talent for music. 他天生具有音乐才能。

The wealthy businessman is endowing a new scholarship fund.  这位富商正在捐赠一项新的奖学金基金。 | 近义词：bestowing, granting, donating, investing | 反义词：depriving, divesting | 词性：动词

词源: From Old French *endouer, from en- "in" + douer "to give, endow," from Latin dotare "to give a dowry," from dos "dowry."
