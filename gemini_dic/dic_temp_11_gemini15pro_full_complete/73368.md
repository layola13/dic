foregone:/fɔːrˈɡɒn/ /fɔːrˈɡɔːn/| adj.|已过去的，注定的，不可避免的|Something that is foregone is certain to happen. |The outcome of the election was a foregone conclusion. 选举的结果已成定局。|Synonyms: inevitable, unavoidable, predetermined, certain, predictable|Antonyms: uncertain, unpredictable, doubtful, dubious|adjective

foregone:/fɔːrˈɡɒn/ /fɔːrˈɡɔːn/| n.|放弃了的权利; 牺牲|Something that is foregone is given up or sacrificed.|His foregone earnings were substantial. 他放弃的收入很可观。|Synonyms: sacrificed, relinquished, abandoned, waived|Antonyms: gained, acquired, obtained|noun
