banishes:/ˈbænɪʃɪz/
verb
放逐，驱逐
sends someone away from a place as a punishment.
The king banishes the traitor from the kingdom. 国王将叛徒驱逐出王国。

The dictator banishes all political opponents. 独裁者放逐了所有的政治对手。

近义词:expels, exiles, outlaws, ostracizes, deports
反义词:welcomes, admits, readmits, reinstates
词性:v.
