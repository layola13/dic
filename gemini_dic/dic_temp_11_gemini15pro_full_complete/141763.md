Uranus: /ˈjʊərənəs/ (US), /ˈjʊərənəs/ (UK)| n.|天王星；（希腊神话）优拉纳斯（天神）|Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun. 天王星是距离太阳第七远的行星。|Uranus, in Greek mythology, was the personification of heaven and ruler of the world. 在希腊神话中，天王星是天堂的化身和世界的统治者。|synonyms: (for the planet) none; (for the god) heaven, sky |antonyms: (for the planet) none; (for the god) earth, underworld |noun
