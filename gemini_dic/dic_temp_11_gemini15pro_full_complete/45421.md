cuprite:/ˈkjuːpraɪt/| n. |赤铜矿|A reddish mineral consisting of cuprous oxide Cu2O, an important ore of copper.|Cuprite is a secondary copper mineral formed by the oxidation of copper sulfides.|近义词:red copper ore|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cuprite*, from Latin *cuprum* ("copper") + *-ite* (mineral suffix). Ultimately from Greek *Kypros* ("Cyprus"), where copper was mined in antiquity.
