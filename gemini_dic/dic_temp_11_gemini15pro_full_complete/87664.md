hexaemeric:/ˌhɛksəˈmɛrɪk/
|adj.|
|六聚体的，由六个单体组成的|
|Composed of six subunits; specifically (Biochem) designating a protein composed of six monomers.|
|Hemoglobin is a hexaemeric protein in some invertebrates.|血红蛋白在某些无脊椎动物中是一种六聚体蛋白质。|
