multiblade:/ˈmʌltɪˌbleɪd/ | adj. n.| 多叶片的，多刀片的；多叶片工具/多刀片工具 | A multiblade razor has several blades. 多刀片剃须刀有多个刀片。| multi-bladed, multiple-blade | single-blade | adjective, noun
Etymology:  "multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many") + "blade" (Old English "blæd," meaning a leaf, blade, or cutting edge)
