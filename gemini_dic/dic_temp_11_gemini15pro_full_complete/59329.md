earlduck:/ˈɜːrldʌk/| n.|伯爵鸭（一种称呼，通常指拥有或管理大量鸭子的富有或有影响力的人，或指对鸭子特别喜爱或痴迷的人）|A term used to refer to a wealthy or influential person who owns or manages a large number of ducks, or someone who is particularly fond of or obsessed with ducks. This word is humorous and not a formal title.|He's become the earlduck of the village, with all those ducks waddling after him. 他成了村里的“伯爵鸭”，身后跟着一大群鸭子摇摇摆摆地走。| 近义词: duck enthusiast, duck lover (loosely) | 反义词: N/A |词性:noun
