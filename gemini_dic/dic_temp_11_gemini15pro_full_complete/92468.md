hypostome:/ˈhaɪpəˌstoʊm/| n. |口下片，下唇|A mouthpart, especially the labium or fused pair of maxillae forming the lower lip of many invertebrates, or the projection below the mouth in some hydroids.|The hypostome of the hydra is used for both ingestion and egestion.|Synonyms: labium, rostrum|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, potentially dorsal side depending on context)|Noun
