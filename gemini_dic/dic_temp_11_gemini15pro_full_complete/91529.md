hyostylic:/ˈhaɪoʊˈstaɪlɪk/| adj.|舌接型|Having the jaw connected to the skull by the hyomandibular cartilage, allowing greater mobility.|Example: Sharks exhibit a hyostylic jaw suspension. 鲨鱼展现出舌接型的颌骨悬挂方式。 Example: The hyostylic jaw suspension is a key feature of many fish species. 舌接型颌骨悬挂是很多鱼类物种的关键特征。|近义词: amphistylic|反义词: autostylic|词性: adjective
