harefoot:/ˈhɛərfʊt/ | n. adj. |野兔, 胆小鬼, 偷偷摸摸的人 |A hare's foot, especially one carried as a good luck charm; a person who is timid or moves stealthily.  It comes from the hare's swift, silent movements. |He moved through the forest like a harefoot, leaving no trace. 他像野兔一样穿过森林，没有留下任何痕迹。 |近义词：timid, cowardly, stealthy |反义词：brave, bold |词性: 名词，形容词

harefoot:/ˈhɛərfʊt/ | adj. | 赤脚的 | Having bare feet. This meaning is less common than the "timid/stealthy" meaning. | The children ran harefoot through the grass. 孩子们赤脚跑过草地。| 近义词：barefoot | 反义词：shod |词性: 形容词
