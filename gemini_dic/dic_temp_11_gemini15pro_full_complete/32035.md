cherishes:/ˈtʃɛrɪʃɪz/
verb
珍爱，珍惜
to hold dear; to feel or show great love for; to protect and care for
She cherishes the memory of her grandmother. 她珍爱着她祖母的记忆。
近义词：treasure, value, adore, prize, hold dear
反义词：neglect, disregard, despise, abandon
词性:v.
