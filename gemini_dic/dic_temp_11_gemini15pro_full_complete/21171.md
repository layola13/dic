bloaty:/ˈbloʊti/ (US) /ˈbləʊti/ (UK)| adj.|臃肿的，肿胀的；感觉腹胀的；（软件等）过于庞大复杂的|Feeling full and uncomfortable because you have eaten too much; (of software) excessively large and complex.|例句：I felt bloated after eating too much pizza. 我吃了太多披萨，感觉肚子胀。

例句：The software has become bloated with unnecessary features. 这个软件因为不必要的特性变得过于庞大。|近义词：swollen, distended, inflated, puffed up (for physical bloating); cumbersome, unwieldy (for software)|反义词：slim, trim (for physical bloating); streamlined, compact (for software)|词性:形容词
