janitorial: /ˌdʒænɪˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. | 清洁的，门房的 | Relating to a janitor or to the work of a janitor; of or relating to the maintenance of a building. |  The janitorial staff keeps the building spotless. 清洁人员保持大楼一尘不染。|  The school's janitorial supplies were stored in a small closet. 学校的清洁用品存放在一个小壁橱里。 |Synonyms: cleaning, custodial, maintenance | Antonyms: managerial, executive | adjective

janitor: /ˈdʒænɪtər/ | n. | 清洁工，门房 | A person employed to clean and maintain a building. | The janitor sweeps the floors every evening. 清洁工每天晚上扫地。| The school janitor was well-liked by the students. 学校的清洁工很受学生们的喜爱。 | Synonyms: custodian, caretaker, cleaner | Antonyms: manager, director | noun
