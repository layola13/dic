harpagon:/ˈhɑːrpəɡɒn/| n.|守财奴，吝啬鬼|A miser; a greedy person.  Derived from the character Harpagon in Molière's play "L'Avare" (The Miser).|He's such a harpagon, he refuses to spend money on anything but the bare necessities.|近义词：miser, skinflint, tightwad, penny-pincher|反义词：spendthrift, philanthropist|noun
