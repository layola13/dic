karroo:/kəˈruː/
n.
南非高原的半沙漠地区; 卡鲁
A plateau in southern Africa.
The Karroo is known for its unique flora and fauna. 卡鲁以其独特的动植物群而闻名。
veld, savanna
desert, forest
noun
