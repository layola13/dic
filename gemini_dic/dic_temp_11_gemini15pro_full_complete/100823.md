introducer:/ˌɪntrəˈd(j)uːsər/| n.|介绍人，引进者，引导者|Someone who introduces another person to a group or organization, or who introduces something new.|The chairman acted as the introducer of the guest speaker. 主席担任了客座演讲者的介绍人。|Synonyms: presenter, host, usher, facilitator|Antonyms: excluder, preventer|noun

introducer:/ˌɪntrəˈd(j)uːsər/| n.|引言，导言，绪论|A short piece of writing or speech at the beginning of a book, program, etc., that introduces it.|The introducer to the anthology provides valuable context.  选集的引言提供了宝贵的背景。|Synonyms: preface, foreword, prologue, preamble|Antonyms: epilogue, afterword|noun
