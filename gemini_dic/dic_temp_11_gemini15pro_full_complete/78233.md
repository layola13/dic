gerbils:/ˈdʒɜːrbɪlz/| n. |沙鼠；沙土鼠|A small rodent of the family Cricetidae, native to arid regions of Africa and Asia, often kept as a pet.  They are characterized by their long hind legs, which allow them to jump and hop. |例句：My daughter loves her pet gerbils. 我女儿很喜欢她的宠物沙鼠。
