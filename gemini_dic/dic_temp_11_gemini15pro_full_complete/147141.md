overlooking:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 俯视的，忽视的 | having a view from above; failing to notice or consider something | The hotel room had a balcony overlooking the sea.  酒店房间有一个俯瞰大海的阳台。

The company was accused of overlooking the safety concerns of its employees.  该公司被指控忽视了员工的安全问题。 | 近义词：ignoring, neglecting, missing, dominating (view) | 反义词：noticing, heeding, observing, below (view) | 词性: adjective

overlook: /ˌoʊvərˈlʊk/ | v. | 俯瞰，忽视，宽恕 | to have a view of from above; to fail to notice or consider something; to forgive or ignore (a fault or offense) |  My apartment overlooks the park. 我的公寓俯瞰公园。

She overlooked a crucial detail in the contract.  她在合同中忽略了一个关键的细节。

He overlooked her rudeness, deciding not to make a fuss. 他宽恕了她的无礼，决定不小题大做。 | 近义词：ignore, neglect, forgive, pardon, excuse | 反义词：notice, heed, observe | 词性: verb


词源: From Old English *oferlocian "look over, look down upon," from ofer- "over" + locian "to look."  来自古英语 *oferlocian “俯视，轻视”，由 ofer- “在...之上” + locian “看” 组成。
