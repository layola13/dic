farfetched:/ˌfɑːrˈfetʃt/ | adj. |牵强的；难以置信的；不切实际的 |unlikely and unconvincing; implausible |例句：His explanation seemed farfetched. 他的解释似乎很牵强。|例句：The plot of the movie was too farfetched to be believable. 这部电影的情节太牵强，令人难以置信。|近义词：improbable, unlikely, unbelievable, incredible, dubious, questionable |反义词：likely, probable, believable, credible, plausible |词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From far + fetch, originally meaning 'brought from afar'.  The current meaning developed in the late 16th century, implying something strained or improbable because it has been 'fetched' from too far a distance.
