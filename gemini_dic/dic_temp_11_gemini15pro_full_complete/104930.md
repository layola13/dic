kelling:/ˈkɛlɪŋ/| n.| 打鱼 (苏格兰方言), 棍打, 殴打|A traditional Scottish method of fishing involving beating the water with sticks to scare fish into nets. Also refers to beating or striking with a stick in general.|例句：The kelling of the salmon was a common practice in the past. 过去，棍打鲑鱼是一种常见的做法。

例句：He gave the dog a kelling for chasing the sheep.  他因为狗追羊而用棍子打了它。|近义词：beating, thrashing, clubbing|反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be considered as "protecting" or "saving" in the context of fishing) |词性:名词
