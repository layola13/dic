noncondiment:/nɒnˈkɒndɪmənt/ | n. | 非调味品 |  A substance that is not a condiment, meaning it is not typically used to enhance the flavor of food.  It refers to any food item that isn't used primarily as a seasoning or flavor enhancer. |  This stew is hearty enough to be a noncondiment; it doesn't need any hot sauce.  这道炖菜足够丰盛，可以作为非调味品；它不需要任何辣酱。 |  main dish, staple food | condiment, seasoning | noun
