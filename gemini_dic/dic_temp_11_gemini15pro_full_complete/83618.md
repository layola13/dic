haemic:/ˈhiːmɪk/ (US) /ˈhiːmɪk/ (UK)| adj.|血的；含铁的；与血有关的|Relating to blood or containing iron, especially in the form of heme.|例句：Haemic iron is more readily absorbed by the body than non-haemic iron. 人体更容易吸收血红素铁，而不是非血红素铁。

例句：The doctor ordered a haemic profile to check the patient's blood count. 医生要求做血液分析来检查病人的血细胞计数。|近义词：bloody, sanguine|反义词:anaemic|词性:adjective
