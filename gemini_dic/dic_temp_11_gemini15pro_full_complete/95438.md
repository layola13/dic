incircle:/ˈɪnsɜːkl/ | n. | 内切圆 |A circle that touches all sides of a polygon internally. | The incircle of a triangle is the circle inscribed in the triangle.  三角形的内切圆是内接于三角形的圆。|  The incircle of the square has a radius of half the side length. 正方形的内切圆半径是其边长的一半。 | inscribed circle | circumcircle | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- + circle.  "In-" signifies within or inside.
