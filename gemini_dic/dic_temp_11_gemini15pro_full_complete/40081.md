containable:/kənˈteɪnəbl/
adjective
可容纳的; 可控制的
able to be contained
The virus is containable if swift action is taken. 如果迅速采取行动，这种病毒是可以控制的。
The spilled chemicals were thankfully containable within the laboratory.  谢天谢地，泄漏的化学物质被控制在实验室范围内。
近义词: controllable, manageable, restrainable
反义词: uncontainable, uncontrollable, rampant
词性: adjective
