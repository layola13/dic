nonmason:/ˌnɒnˈmeɪsən/
noun
非共济会会员
a person who is not a Freemason.  A Freemason is a member of a fraternal organization called Freemasonry.
He was a nonmason, so he wasn't allowed to attend the lodge meeting. 他不是共济会会员，所以不允许参加集会。
Synonyms:non-Freemason, outsider
Antonyms:Freemason, Mason
词性:n.
