mycologist:/maɪˈkɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌmaɪˈkɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |真菌学家|A biologist who studies fungi.|例句：The mycologist discovered a new species of mushroom.  这位真菌学家发现了一种新的蘑菇。

例句：She consulted a mycologist about the mold growing in her basement. 她咨询了一位真菌学家关于她地下室里生长的霉菌的问题。

|近义词：fungi expert, mushroom expert|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From myco- +‎ -logist.  "myco-" comes from the Greek "mykēs" meaning "fungus". "-logist" denotes one who studies or is an expert in a particular field.
