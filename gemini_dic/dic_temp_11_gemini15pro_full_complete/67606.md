extravasate: /ɪkˈstrævəseɪt/| v. |渗出，漏出（尤指血液或体液）|To leak or escape from a vessel into the surrounding tissues, especially blood or other fluids.|The chemo extravasated and caused tissue damage. 化疗药物渗出并导致组织损伤。|Synonyms: leak, seep, escape, ooze|Antonyms: contain, confine, restrict|verb

extravasate: /ɪkˈstrævəseɪt/| adj. |渗出的，漏出的|Having leaked or escaped from a vessel into the surrounding tissues.|The extravasated blood caused a large bruise. 渗出的血液导致了大面积瘀伤。|Synonyms: leaked, seeped, escaped, oozed|Antonyms: contained, confined, restricted|adjective
