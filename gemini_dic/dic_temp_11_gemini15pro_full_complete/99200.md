interclasps:/ˌɪntərˈklæsps/
|v.|互相紧握, 交握,  扣紧|
|The lovers interclasped their hands.|恋人互相紧握双手。|
|近义词：interlock, intertwine, clasp, link|
|反义词：separate, unclasp, disconnect|
|词性: 动词(verb)|
|词源:  From inter- + clasp.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "clasp" means to grasp or hold tightly. Thus, "interclasp" literally means to clasp together or between.|
