dictatus:/dɪkˈteɪtəs/ | n. | 指令，命令，声明 | An authoritative or dogmatic statement or decree. | The king issued a dictatus outlining new tax laws. 国王发布了一项指令，概述了新的税法。 |  The professor's dictatus on the subject left no room for debate.  教授关于这个主题的声明不容置疑。 | 近义词: decree, edict, pronouncement, mandate | 反义词: request, plea, suggestion | noun
