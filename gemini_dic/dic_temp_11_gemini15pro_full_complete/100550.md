interweds:/ˌɪntərˈwɛdz/
verb
结婚；互相结婚
When two families interwed, their ties become stronger.  两个家族互相联姻，他们的关系变得更加牢固。
The two royal families interwed to secure peace. 这两个皇室家族联姻以确保和平。
近义词:marry, intermarry
反义词:divorce, separate
词性:v.

词源:
From inter- + wed.  "Inter-" prefix meaning "between, among" and "wed" meaning "to marry."
