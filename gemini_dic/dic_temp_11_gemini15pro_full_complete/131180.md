nonbrowser:/nɒnˈbraʊzə(r)/ | n. | 非浏览器程序；非浏览器用户 | A software application that is not a web browser, or a user who does not use a web browser. | Example: The company developed a nonbrowser application for accessing its services.  该公司开发了一个非浏览器应用程序来访问其服务。

Example:  Many nonbrowsers prefer dedicated email clients over webmail. 许多非浏览器用户更喜欢专用电子邮件客户端而不是网络邮件。 | 近义词: dedicated application, standalone program | 反义词: browser, web browser | 词性: noun
