beachcombing:/ˈbiːtʃˌkoʊmɪŋ/ | n. | 海滩拾荒，海滩寻宝 | the activity of searching for things that have been washed up on a beach |  We spent the morning beachcombing for shells and driftwood. 我们早上在海滩拾荒，寻找贝壳和浮木。 | 近义词：foraging, scavenging | 反义词: discarding | 词性: 名词

beachcomb:/ˈbiːtʃˌkoʊm/ | v. | 在海滩拾荒，在海滩寻宝 | to search a beach for things that have been washed up | He beachcombed every morning, hoping to find something valuable. 他每天早上都在海滩寻宝，希望能找到一些值钱的东西。 | 近义词：forage, scavenge | 反义词: discard | 词性: 动词


词源： beach + comb.  "Comb" here means to search thoroughly, like combing through hair.
