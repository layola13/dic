disir:/ˈdiːsɪr/| n. |女神; 女精灵 (复数)| Norse mythology female deities associated with fate and weaving. They are often associated with Valkyries. |The disir wove the threads of destiny. 女神们编织着命运的丝线。|近义词：goddess, Valkyrie, spirit|反义词: (无明确反义词，可以考虑mortal)|词性:noun
