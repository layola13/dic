orientally:/ˌɔːriˈentəli/ | adv. |东方地；像东方人一样地|In a way that relates to or comes from the East, or in a way that is thought to be typical of the East. |She dressed orientally in silk robes. 她穿着丝绸长袍，打扮得像个东方人。| synonym: easternly | antonym: westernly | adverb

orientally:/ˌɔːriˈentəli/ | adj. |东方的；东方人的|Relating to or characteristic of the countries of Asia, especially East Asia.  (This usage is now generally considered offensive.)|  He had an orientally styled house. 他有一座东方风格的房子。（这种用法现在通常被认为具有冒犯性。）| synonym: eastern | antonym: western | adjective
