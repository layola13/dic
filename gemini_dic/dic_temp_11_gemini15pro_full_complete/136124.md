nonrefined:/ˌnɒnrɪˈfaɪnd/ | adj. | 未精炼的，未提纯的，粗糙的 | Not refined; unpurified or crude. |  The nonrefined sugar still contained molasses.  未精炼的糖仍然含有糖蜜。 | Example:  They used nonrefined coconut oil in their soap. 他们在肥皂中使用了未精炼的椰子油。 | Synonyms: crude, unrefined, unprocessed, raw, natural | Antonyms: refined, processed, purified | adjective
