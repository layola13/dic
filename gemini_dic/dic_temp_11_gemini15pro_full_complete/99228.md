intercom:/ˈɪntərkɒm/ (UK) /ˈɪntərˌkɑːm/ (US)| n.|内部通话系统，对讲机|A two-way communication system, typically within a building or vehicle, allowing people in different locations to speak to each other.|例句：The pilot spoke to the cabin crew via the intercom. 飞行员通过内部通话系统与机组人员通话。|近义词：interphone, speakerphone|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "face-to-face communication")|词性:noun
intercom:/ˈɪntərkɒm/ (UK) /ˈɪntərˌkɑːm/ (US)| v.|通过内部通话系统通话|To communicate with someone using an intercom.|例句：He intercommed the control room to report the incident. 他通过对讲机向控制室报告了这起事件。|近义词：page, contact|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:verb
