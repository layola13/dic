interlending:/ˌɪntərˈlɛndɪŋ/ | n. |馆际互借；图书互借|The act of lending and borrowing materials between libraries.|例句：Interlending is a vital service for researchers needing access to specialist materials.  馆际互借对于需要使用专业资料的研究人员来说是一项至关重要的服务。

例句：The library's interlending service allows patrons to borrow books from other institutions.  图书馆的馆际互借服务允许读者从其他机构借阅书籍。|近义词：resource sharing, library cooperation|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (prefix meaning "between, among") + lending (from the verb "lend," meaning "to grant the use of something temporarily").
