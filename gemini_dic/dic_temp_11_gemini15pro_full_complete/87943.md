higgledy-piggledy:/ˈhɪɡəldi ˈpɪɡəldi/ | adj. | 乱七八糟的，杂乱无章的 | In a confused and disordered state; jumbled.  From a reduplication of 'piggle', of uncertain origin, perhaps related to 'pick'. | The toys were scattered higgledy-piggledy across the floor. 玩具散落在地板上，乱七八糟。| 近义词: disorderly, chaotic, jumbled, messy | 反义词: orderly, neat, tidy | 词性:形容词


higgledy-piggledy:/ˈhɪɡəldi ˈpɪɡəldi/ | adv. | 乱七八糟地，杂乱无章地 | In a confused and disordered manner. | The books were stacked higgledy-piggledy on the shelf. 书架上的书堆得乱七八糟。| 近义词: disorderly, chaotically, jumbledly, messily | 反义词: orderly, neatly, tidily | 词性:副词
