carjacker:/ˈkɑːrdʒækər/ | n. |劫车者|A person who forcibly steals a car from its driver. | The carjacker forced the driver out of the car at gunpoint. 劫车者用枪指着司机，迫使他离开汽车。|Synonym: car thief, auto thief | Antonym:  N/A (No direct antonym. One could consider "law-abiding citizen" as a loose antonym.) | Noun

Etymology:  A blend of "car" and "hijacker."  "Hijacker" itself comes from "hijack,"  which originated in the early 20th century, possibly from the command "Hi, Jack!" used by robbers to stop vehicles.
