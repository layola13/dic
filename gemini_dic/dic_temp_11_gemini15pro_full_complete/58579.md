dulcify:/ˈdʌlsɪfaɪ/ /ˈduːlsɪfaɪ/| vt.|使变甜，使甜蜜，使缓和|to make something sweeter or more pleasant; to soften or make milder|The government tried to dulcify the bad news with promises of future improvements. 政府试图用未来改进的承诺来缓和坏消息。

The company tried to dulcify the bitter pill of layoffs with generous severance packages. 公司试图用优厚的遣散费来减轻裁员的痛苦。|近义词：sweeten, soften, mitigate, alleviate, mollify|反义词：exacerbate, worsen, aggravate, embitter|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dulcificāre, from Latin dulcis (“sweet”) + -ficāre (from facere, “to make”).
