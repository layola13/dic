inflammatorily:/ɪnˈflæmətɔːrɪli/ | adv. | 炎症地；煽动地 | in a way that relates to or causes inflammation; in a way that excites or angers people |  Her article was written inflammatorily, designed to provoke a reaction. 她的文章写得煽动性很强，旨在引起反应。|  The politician spoke inflammatorily about the opposing party.  这位政客煽动性地谈论了反对党。 | 近义词:provocatively, incendiary, agitatingly | 反义词: soothingly, calmly, peacefully | 词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *inflammātōrius, from Latin inflammō (“I set on fire, inflame”), from in (“in, on”) + flammō (“I burn, blaze”).
