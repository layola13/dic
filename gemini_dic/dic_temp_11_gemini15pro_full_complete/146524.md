overgesticulative: /ˌoʊvərˌdʒɛsˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪv/
adjective
过分打手势的，过于夸张的
Characterized by excessive or exaggerated use of gestures.
The overgesticulative speaker made it difficult to focus on his message.  这位演讲者过分打手势，让人很难集中注意力听他的演讲内容。
She's known for her overgesticulative style of teaching.  她以夸张的教学风格而闻名。
synonym: demonstrative, exuberant, theatrical
antonym: restrained, subdued, impassive
词性: adjective
