chambering:/ˈtʃeɪmbərɪŋ/
n.|膛室;膛室加工;膛线;（子弹）入膛|the process of cutting spiral grooves inside a gun barrel to give the bullet spin, or the spiral grooves themselves.  Also, the act of loading a cartridge into the chamber of a firearm.|The gunsmith's meticulous chambering ensured the rifle's accuracy.  枪匠细致的膛线加工确保了步枪的精度。|grooving, rifling|smoothing|noun
chambering:/ˈtʃeɪmbərɪŋ/
n.|（尤指乐队）排练;合奏|rehearsing, especially of a musical group.|The orchestra's chambering sessions were intense and productive.  管弦乐队的排练紧张而富有成效。|rehearsal, practice|performance|noun
