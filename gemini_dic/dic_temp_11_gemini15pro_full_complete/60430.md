eighty:/ˈeɪti/| num.|八十|A numerical value representing the number after seventy-nine and before eighty-one.  Derived from Old English "hundeahtatig" meaning "eighty."|There are eighty students in the class. 班级里有八十名学生。|Eighty apples were picked from the tree. 从树上摘了八十个苹果。|近义词: fourscore (literary)|反义词: twenty (as an example of a contrasting number)|词性: Numeral (primarily), can function adjectivally
