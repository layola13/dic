noninfluential:/ˌnɒnɪnfluˈɛnʃəl/| adj.|无影响力的，没有影响的|Not having or exerting influence; unimportant.|His role in the decision-making process was noninfluential. 他在决策过程中的作用微乎其微。|The noninfluential committee was eventually disbanded.  这个没有影响力的委员会最终被解散了。|近义词：insignificant, unimportant, powerless, ineffective|反义词：influential, powerful, important|词性: adjective
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + influential (from influence, from Old French influence, from Latin influentia "a flowing in," from influere "to flow into," from in- "into" + fluere "to flow")
