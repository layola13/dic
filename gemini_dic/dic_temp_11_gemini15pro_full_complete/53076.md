dioxindol:/daɪˈɒksɪndɒl/ |n.|二氧吲哚|A heterocyclic organic compound containing a benzene ring fused to a pyrrole ring with two oxygen atoms incorporated.  It is a structural motif found in various natural products and pharmaceuticals.|Dioxindol derivatives exhibit promising anti-cancer activity. 二氧吲哚衍生物表现出有希望的抗癌活性。|Near Synonyms: indole-2,3-dione |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) | Noun
