overgraded:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪdɪd/
adjective
过高评价的，等级过高的
rated too highly; given a higher grade than deserved
The student felt overgraded in the class, believing the assignments were too easy.  这个学生觉得这门课的评分过高，认为作业太简单了。
The wine was overgraded and overpriced.  这款酒的评级过高，价格也过高。
近义词: overrated, overestimated
反义词: underrated, underestimated
词性: adjective
