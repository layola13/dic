irreplaceablenesses:/ˌɪrɪˈpleɪsəbəlnəsɪz/
n.
不可替代性
the state or quality of being irreplaceable; the fact that something or someone cannot be replaced
The irreplaceableness of old-growth forests is a key argument for their preservation.  原始森林的不可替代性是保护它们的关键论点。
uniqueness, indispensability, singularity
replaceability, dispensability
noun
