breezily:/ˈbriːzɪli/| adv.|轻快地，轻松地|In a breezy manner; casually and cheerfully.|She breezily dismissed his concerns. 她轻描淡写地打消了他的顾虑。|Example: He breezily walked into the room, whistling a tune. 他轻快地走进房间，吹着口哨。|Synonyms: airily, casually, cheerfully, lightly, nonchalantly|Antonyms: seriously, solemnly, heavily, anxiously|Word Type: Adverb

breezily:/ˈbriːzɪli/| adv.|通风良好地|Characterized by a breeze; airy.|The room was breezily ventilated. 房间通风良好。|Example: The curtains billowed breezily in the open window. 窗帘在敞开的窗户里轻盈地飘动。|Synonyms: airily, freshly|Antonyms: stuffily, stiflingly|Word Type: Adverb
