lirelliform:/lɪˈrɛlɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 琴形的，百合形的 | Shaped like a lyre or lily; narrow and pointed at both ends, widening in the middle. | The petals of the flower were distinctly lirelliform.  这朵花的花瓣明显呈琴形。| Near Synonyms: lyre-shaped, lily-shaped, spatulate, obovate | Antonyms:  round, circular, ovate | Adjective
