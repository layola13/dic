harbour/ˈhɑːrbər/ (US) /ˈhɑːrbər/ (UK)| n. v.| 港口，避难所; 庇护，窝藏|A place on the coast where ships may moor in shelter, especially one protected from rough water by piers, jetties, and other artificial structures. To give shelter to. |The ship sailed into the harbour at dawn. 这艘船在黎明时分驶入了港口。|近义词：port, haven, shelter, refuge|反义词：expose|词性: 名词, 动词

harbrough /ˈhɑːrbrə/ (obsolete)| n. | 港口，避难所 (古语，同harbour)|Same as harbour. This spelling is now archaic.| The ship sought harbrough from the storm. 这艘船寻找避风港躲避风暴。|近义词：harbour, port, haven, shelter, refuge|反义词：expose|词性: 名词
