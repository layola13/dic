hung-out:/hʌŋˈaʊt/ | adj. |  疲惫不堪的；筋疲力尽的；焦虑不安的 | Exhausted, tired, or anxious, often from overwork or stress.  | I felt completely hung-out after that 12-hour shift. 我在12小时的轮班后感到筋疲力尽。|  例句：She was hung-out after studying all night for the exam. 她整晚都在为考试学习，累坏了。| 近义词：exhausted, worn out, stressed out, drained, spent | 反义词：energetic, refreshed, invigorated | 词性:形容词


hung-out:/hʌŋˈaʊt/ | n. |  (非正式)休闲场所；常去的地方 | (informal) A place where someone spends a lot of time; a favorite haunt. | The cafe is my favorite hung-out. 这家咖啡馆是我最喜欢的去处。 | 例句：Their hung-out was a small diner downtown.  他们常去的地方是市中心的一家小餐馆。 | 近义词：hangout, haunt, spot, joint | 反义词：N/A | 词性:名词
