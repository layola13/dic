creepiest: /ˈkriːpiɪst/ | adj. | 最令人毛骨悚然的 | Superlative form of creepy: most creepy.  Evoking an unpleasant feeling of fear or unease. | The abandoned house was the creepiest place I'd ever been.  那栋废弃的房子是我去过的最令人毛骨悚然的地方。 |  近义词: eeriest, spookiest, most unsettling | 反义词: comforting, reassuring | 词性: 形容词

creep: /kriːp/ | v. n. | 爬行，蠕动；蔓延；毛骨悚然的感觉 | From Old English *crēopan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *kreupaną (“to creep”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ḱrep- (“to creep, crawl”). | The ivy crept up the wall. 常春藤爬上了墙。/  A creepy feeling came over me. 我有一种毛骨悚然的感觉。 | 近义词: crawl, slither (v.); unsettling, eerie (adj.) | 反义词:  run, sprint (v.); comforting, reassuring (adj.) | 词性: 动词，名词
