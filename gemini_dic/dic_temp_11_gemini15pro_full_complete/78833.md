gimmickry: /ˈɡɪmɪkri/ | n. | 花招，噱头，小玩意儿 |  The use of gimmicks; attracting attention by means of a gimmick.  It often implies something is not genuine or of real value. |  The politician's speech was filled with gimmickry, but little substance.  这位政客的演讲充满了噱头，但没什么实质内容。 |  trickery, artifice, subterfuge |  authenticity, genuineness | noun
