acidifying:/əˈsɪdɪfaɪɪŋ/
v. |酸化；使变酸|Making something more acidic.
The burning of fossil fuels is acidifying the oceans. |燃烧化石燃料正在酸化海洋。
Pollution is acidifying the lakes and rivers. |污染正在使湖泊和河流酸化。
近义词:souring, acidulating
反义词:alkalizing, neutralizing
词性:动词现在分词/形容词

acidify:/əˈsɪdɪfaɪ/
v. |酸化；使变酸|To make something more acidic.
The sulfur dioxide emissions acidify the rain. |二氧化硫排放使雨水酸化。
Lemon juice can acidify milk. |柠檬汁可以酸化牛奶。
近义词:sour, acidulate
反义词:alkalize, neutralize
词性:动词

词源: From acid + -ify.  "Acid" ultimately comes from the Latin word "acidus," meaning "sour" or "sharp." The suffix "-ify" means "to make or become."
