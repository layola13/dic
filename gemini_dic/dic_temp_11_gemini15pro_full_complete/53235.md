director:/dəˈrɛktər/ (US), /daɪˈrɛktə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 导演，主管，负责人 |A person who is in charge of an activity, department, or organization; a person who supervises the actors and other staff in a film, play, or similar production.|The director of the film won an Oscar.  这部电影的导演获得了奥斯卡奖。|Synonyms: manager, supervisor, administrator, leader, conductor, superintendent |Antonyms: subordinate, follower|noun

director:/dəˈrɛktər/ (US), /daɪˈrɛktə(r)/ (UK)| n.|董事|A member of the board of directors of a company.|She's a director of a large multinational corporation. 她是一家大型跨国公司的董事。|Synonyms: board member, executive, trustee |Antonyms: employee, staff|noun
