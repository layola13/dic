malgracious:/mælˈɡreɪʃəs/
adjective
不礼貌的，粗鲁的，没风度的
Lacking grace or graciousness; discourteous or ungracious.
He gave a malgracious acceptance of the award.  他很不客气地接受了奖项。
She offered a malgracious apology. 她很不情愿地道歉。
近义词:rude, impolite, ungracious, churlish, surly
反义词:gracious, polite, courteous, civil
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From mal- (bad, ill) + gracious.  Ultimately from Latin gratia (grace, favor).
