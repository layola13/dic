blinkered:/ˈblɪŋkərd/ | adj. | 思想狭隘的，心胸狭窄的，缺乏远见的 |  Having or showing a narrow or limited outlook, unable or unwilling to consider different perspectives. Origin:  From "blinkers," leather flaps placed on a horse's bridle to prevent it from seeing sideways. |  He has a rather blinkered view of the world. 他对世界的看法相当狭隘。|  narrow-minded, prejudiced, intolerant, insular, parochial | open-minded, broad-minded, tolerant, receptive | adjective

blinkered:/ˈblɪŋkərd/ | adj. | 戴着眼罩的 | Wearing blinkers (eye coverings for a horse). | The blinkered horse plodded down the road. 戴着眼罩的马沿着路慢慢走。 |  -- | -- | adjective
