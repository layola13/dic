larvas:/ˈlɑːrvəz/| n. |幼虫（复数）|The larval stage of an insect. 昆虫的幼虫阶段。|The pond teemed with mosquito larvas. 池塘里满是蚊子幼虫。|近义词：grubs, caterpillars, maggots|反义词:imago, adult|noun

larva:/ˈlɑːrvə/| n. |幼虫（单数）|The immature, wingless, and often worm-like feeding form of many insects. 许多昆虫的未成熟、无翅、通常类似蠕虫的摄食形式。|The butterfly larva is called a caterpillar. 蝴蝶的幼虫被称为毛毛虫。|近义词：grub, caterpillar, maggot|反义词:imago, adult|noun
