overarm:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrm/ | n. | 上手投球（或击球）| An overhand throw or stroke. |  His overarm was fast and accurate. 他的上手投球又快又准。| 近义词: overhead | 反义词: underarm | 词性: 名词
