acceptant:/əkˈsɛptənt/
adjective
接受的，容纳的，认可的
Willing to accept something or someone.
例句：She has a very acceptant attitude towards different cultures. 她对不同的文化持非常接受的态度。

例句：The company was acceptant of the new employee's suggestions. 公司接受了新员工的建议。

近义词：accepting, receptive, tolerant, open-minded
反义词：rejecting, intolerant, closed-minded
词性: adjective


acceptant:/əkˈsɛptənt/
noun
接受者，认可者
A person who is willing to accept something or someone.
例句：He is an acceptant of new ideas. 他是一个乐于接受新想法的人。

例句：The acceptants of the award were very grateful.  获奖者非常感激。

近义词：receiver, recipient
反义词：rejector
词性: noun
