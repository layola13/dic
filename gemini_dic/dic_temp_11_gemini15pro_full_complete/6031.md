amen:/ˌeɪˈmɛn/ /ˈɑːˈmɛn/| int. n.| 阿门；诚心所愿；但愿如此；真的；的确| Used at the end of a prayer or a statement to express solemn ratification or agreement.  Used to express agreement or to emphasize the truth of a statement. |例句：The congregation responded with a resounding "Amen."  会众以响亮的“阿门”回应。

例句：Amen to that!  我完全同意！|近义词：so be it, truly, verily|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's an expression of affirmation)|词性: interjection, noun

词源: From Late Latin āmēn, from Ecclesiastical Greek ἀμήν (amḗn), from Hebrew āmēn (“truly, verily”), used adverbially, meaning "certainly," "truly," "verily," "so be it," expressing agreement, affirmation, or a solemn wish.
