luxuriation:/lʌɡʒʊəriˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 奢华，享受，沉溺 | the act of luxuriating; a state of great comfort and pleasure | She spent the afternoon in quiet luxuriation by the pool. 她整个下午都在泳池边尽情享受宁静的时光。 | 近义词: indulgence, reveling, wallowing, basking | 反义词: austerity, deprivation, hardship | 词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *luxuriātiō*, from *luxuriāre* (“to be luxuriant, to grow rankly, to abound”), from *lūxuria* (“luxury, abundance”).
