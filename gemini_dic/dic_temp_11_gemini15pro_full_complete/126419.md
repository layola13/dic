mysterioso:/ˌmɪstɪəˈrəʊsoʊ/ (UK) /ˌmɪstɪˈriːoʊsoʊ/ (US)| adj. |神秘的；诡秘的|Used as a musical direction to indicate a mysterious and secretive style of performance.|The composer marked the passage "mysterioso." 作曲家将这段标为“神秘的”。|近义词：mysterious, enigmatic, secretive, clandestine|反义词：obvious, clear, transparent|词性:形容词
Etymology: Italian, from *misterioso*, from Latin *mysterium* "secret rite, mystery," from Greek *mysterion* "secret rite, mystery."
