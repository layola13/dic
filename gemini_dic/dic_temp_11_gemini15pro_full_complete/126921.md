narier:/ˈneɪriər/ | adj. | 更窄的，更狭窄的 | narrower, more constricted.  A narier path through the woods. | 例句：The path became narier as we climbed higher.  随着我们爬得越高，小路变得越窄。| 近义词：narrower, tighter, thinner, slenderer | 反义词：wider, broader, larger | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle English *narer, comparative of nare (“narrow”), from Old English nearu (“narrow, constricted, oppressive; close, intimate; small, slight; niggardly, stingy”), from Proto-Germanic *narwaz (“narrow”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰner- (“to tighten, constrict”). Cognate with Scots narra (“narrow”), West Frisian near (“narrow”), Dutch naar (“nasty, unpleasant”), German eng (“narrow”), Danish nær (“near”).
