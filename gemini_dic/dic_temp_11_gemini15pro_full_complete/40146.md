contemptibilities:/kənˌtɛmptɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
轻蔑；可鄙之处；卑劣
The state or quality of being contemptible; deserving of contempt.  A contemptible act or characteristic.
His contemptibilities were numerous, including lying, cheating, and stealing. 他的卑劣行为不胜枚举，包括撒谎、欺骗和偷窃。
Synonyms:  despicableness, vileness, baseness, meanness, worthlessness
Antonyms:  nobility, worthiness, respectability, admirability
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

contemptible:/kənˈtɛmptɪbl/
adj.
可鄙的；卑劣的；令人轻视的
Deserving contempt; despicable.
His behavior was contemptible and earned him the scorn of his peers. 他的行为卑劣，遭到了同龄人的鄙视。
Synonyms: despicable, loathsome, detestable, abject, base, vile, low
Antonyms: admirable, respectable, honorable, praiseworthy
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *contemptibilitas, from Latin contemptibilis (“contemptible”), from contemnere (“to despise”), from con- (“together”) + temnere (“to despise”).
