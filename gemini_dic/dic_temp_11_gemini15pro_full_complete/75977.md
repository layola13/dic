funnel-like:/ˈfʌn(ə)lˌlaɪk/| adj.|漏斗状的，像漏斗的|Resembling a funnel in shape; narrowing towards one end.|The tornado formed a funnel-like cloud in the sky. 龙卷风在空中形成了一个漏斗状的云。|The flower's petals formed a funnel-like shape. 这朵花的瓣形成了一个漏斗状的形状。|近义词：conical, tapered, cone-shaped|反义词：cylindrical, tubular|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "funnel" + "-like". "Funnel" comes from the Old French "funel" and ultimately from the Latin "infundibulum", meaning "funnel". "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
