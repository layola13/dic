fungicide:/ˈfʌndʒɪsaɪd/| n.|杀菌剂|A substance that destroys fungi or inhibits their growth.|例句：The farmer sprayed the crops with a fungicide to prevent disease. 农民用杀菌剂喷洒农作物以预防疾病。

例句：This fungicide is effective against a wide range of fungal pathogens.  这种杀菌剂对多种真菌病原体有效。|近义词：antifungal|反义词:fungistat (抑菌剂, 注意并非完全相反)|词性:noun
