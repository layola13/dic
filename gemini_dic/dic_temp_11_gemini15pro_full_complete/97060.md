inferentially:/ˌɪnfəˈrɛnʃəli/
adverb
推论地，推断地
By reasoning from evidence; by inference.
We can inferentially derive his intentions from his actions. 我们可以从他的行为中推断出他的意图。
Synonyms: deductively, by inference, implicitly
Antonyms: explicitly, directly, observably
词性: 副词
