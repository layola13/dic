diabolical:/ˌdaɪəˈbɒlɪk(ə)l/ /ˌdaɪəˈbɑːlɪk(ə)l/| adj.|恶魔般的；极坏的；糟透的|Showing wickedness typical of the Devil; extremely bad or unpleasant.|The traffic was diabolical.|近义词：evil, wicked, fiendish, atrocious, terrible, awful|反义词:angelic, divine, heavenly, wonderful, excellent|词性:adjective
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin *diabolicus, from Greek diabolikos, from diabolos ‘devil’ (see devil).
