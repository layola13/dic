nonstellar:/ˌnɑːnˈstelər/
adjective
非恒星的；非星体的
Not of or pertaining to a star; not stellar.
Example:  Nonstellar objects, such as planets and asteroids, also orbit the sun.  非恒星物体，例如行星和小行星，也绕太阳运行。

Example: The astronomer was interested in the nonstellar phenomena occurring in the nebula.  这位天文学家对星云中发生的非星体现象很感兴趣。

Synonym: non-star, astral (in some contexts)
Antonym: stellar, star

词性: adjective
