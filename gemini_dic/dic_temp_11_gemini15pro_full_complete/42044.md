costumier:/kɒˈstjuːmiə(r)/ /ˈkɑːstuːmər/| n.|服装师，戏服设计师|A person who makes or provides costumes, especially for theatrical productions.|The costumier created stunning period dresses for the play.  这位服装师为这部戏剧创作了令人惊艳的时代服装。|Example: The film's costumier won an award for her intricate designs.  这部电影的服装设计师因其复杂的设计而获奖。|Synonyms:  costume designer, wardrobe mistress, wardrobe master|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps) audience member, actor/actress|Noun
