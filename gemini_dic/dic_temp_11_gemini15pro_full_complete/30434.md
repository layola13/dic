centralizers:/ˈsɛntrəlaɪzərz/ | n. |中心化器，中心化装置，集中器;  (数学)中心化子|A centralizer in a group is the set of all elements that commute with a given element.  In a ring, the centralizer of a subset is the set of all elements that commute with every element of the subset.|The centralizers of certain elements in the group are important for understanding its structure.  这个群中某些元素的中心化子对于理解其结构很重要。|Synonyms: commutators (in some contexts)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, the concept is related to group structure, not opposition)|Noun

centralizer:/ˈsɛntrəlaɪzər/ | n. |中心化器，中心化装置，集中器;  (数学)中心化子|A centralizer in a group is the set of all elements that commute with a given element.  In a ring, the centralizer of a subset is the set of all elements that commute with every element of the subset.|The centralizer of that particular transformation is a subgroup.  那个特定变换的中心化子是一个子群。|Synonyms: commutators (in some contexts)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym)|Noun
