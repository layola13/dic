apron: /ˈeɪprən/ (US), /ˈeɪprən/ (UK)| n. |围裙；(舞台的)台口；停机坪|A garment worn over the front of the body to protect clothing from spills or stains. Also, the area of an airport where aircraft are parked, loaded, and unloaded.|She wore an apron while cooking. 她做饭时穿着围裙。 The plane taxied to the apron. 飞机滑行到停机坪。|近义词：pinafore, coverall, bib, tarmac (for airport apron)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From Old French *naperon (modern French napperon "small tablecloth"), diminutive of nappe "tablecloth", from Latin mappa "napkin, cloth".  The initial "n" was lost through misdivision of the phrase "a napron" as "an apron".
