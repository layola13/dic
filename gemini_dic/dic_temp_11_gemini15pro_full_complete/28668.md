caring:/ˈkɛərɪŋ/| adj.|关心他人的，体贴的，乐于助人的|Displaying kindness and concern for others.|She is a caring and compassionate nurse. 她是一位充满爱心和同情心的护士。|He has a caring nature. 他天性善良，关心他人。|近义词：compassionate, kind, sympathetic, empathetic, solicitous|反义词：uncaring, indifferent, callous, apathetic|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *caryinge, present participle of caryen (“to care”), from Old English carian (“to care, be anxious, grieve”), from Proto-Germanic *karōną (“to lament, care, grieve”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰer- (“to shout, scream”). Cognate with Scots cair (“to care”), Saterland Frisian koarje (“to care”), West Frisian koarje (“to care”), Dutch keuren (“to approve, inspect”), German kehren (“to sweep, turn, return”).
