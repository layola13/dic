cert:/sɜːrt/ | n. | 证书；凭证 | A document attesting to the truth of certain stated facts, especially one issued officially. | Example: He earned a Microsoft certification. 他获得了微软认证。 | Example:  She needed a birth certificate to apply for a passport. 她需要出生证明来申请护照。| Synonyms: certificate, document, license, diploma | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object, could be considered conceptually as "denial," "rejection," or "invalidation") | noun
