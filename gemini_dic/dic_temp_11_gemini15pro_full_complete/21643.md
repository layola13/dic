blurt-out:/blɜːrt aʊt/
verb
脱口而出；突然说出
to say something suddenly and without thinking, usually because you are excited or nervous
He blurted out the secret before he could stop himself. 他还没来得及阻止自己就脱口说出了秘密。
synonyms: exclaim, divulge, spill, let slip
antonyms: conceal, withhold
词性: 动词短语
