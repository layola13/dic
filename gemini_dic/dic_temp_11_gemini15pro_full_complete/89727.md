hoper:/ˈhoʊpər/ | n. | 希望者;抱有希望的人 |  Someone who hopes. A person who has hope or expectation, especially of something desired. |  Example:  The hopers lined up for the audition, each dreaming of stardom.  希望者们排队参加试镜，每个人都梦想着成为明星。

Example:  She remained a hoper, even in the face of adversity. 即使面对逆境，她仍然充满希望。 | 近义词: optimist, aspirant, dreamer | 反义词: pessimist, cynic | 词性: 名词

词源: From "hope" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting persons who perform the action of the verb, or who are characterized by the quality denoted by the noun or adjective).  源自“hope”（希望）+“-er”（后缀，构成表示执行动词动作的人或以名词或形容词表示的品质为特征的人的名词）。
