deallocation's:/ˌdiːæləˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 释放，解除分配 | The deallocation's of memory is crucial for preventing memory leaks.  内存的释放对于防止内存泄漏至关重要。 |  例句：The deallocation's process returned the resources to the system. 释放过程将资源返回给系统。

词源:  Comes from the prefix "de-" (meaning "removal" or "reversal") + "allocation" (the act of assigning resources).  "Allocation" itself comes from Old French "alocation" and ultimately from Latin "locare" (to place).
