deynte:/ˈdeɪnti/| adj.|美味的；精致的；娇美的|Something dainty is small, delicate, and pretty.|例句：She wore a dainty silver necklace. 她戴着一条精致的银项链。

例句：The cafe served dainty sandwiches and cakes. 这家咖啡馆供应精致的三明治和蛋糕。

近义词：delicate, exquisite, elegant, refined|反义词：coarse, crude, rough, clumsy|词性：adjective

词源: From Old French *daintie, from Latin *dignitatem (worthiness, dignity), related to dignus (worthy).
