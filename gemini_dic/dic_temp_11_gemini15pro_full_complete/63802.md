epidemiologist:/ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 流行病学家 | A scientist who studies the patterns, causes, and effects of health and disease conditions in defined populations. | Epidemiologists are working hard to understand the spread of the new virus. 流行病学家正在努力了解这种新病毒的传播。| The epidemiologist traced the source of the outbreak to contaminated water.  这位流行病学家将疫情的源头追溯到受污染的水。 | synonyms: public health physician, disease specialist | antonyms: N/A | noun
