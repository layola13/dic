hundredth's-places: /ˈhʌndrədθs pleɪsɪz/ | n. | 百分位 | The position after the decimal point that represents hundredths of a unit.  Specifically, it is the second digit to the right of the decimal point. | Example: In the number 3.14, the 4 is in the hundredth's place. 在数字 3.14 中，4 位于百分位。| Example: Rounding 2.567 to the hundredth's place gives you 2.57. 将 2.567 四舍五入到百分位得到 2.57。| 近义词: hundredths position | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
