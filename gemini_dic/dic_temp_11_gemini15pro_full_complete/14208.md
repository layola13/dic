autocephalous:/ˌɔːtəˈsɛfələs/| adj.|自主的，独立的（指教会）|Self-governing; independent (especially of a church).  Referring to an Eastern Orthodox church that is independent and recognizes no higher authority.|The autocephalous Orthodox Church of Greece is one of the fourteen autocephalous churches that make up the Eastern Orthodox communion.|Synonyms: independent, autonomous, self-governing|Antonyms: dependent, subordinate|Adjective
