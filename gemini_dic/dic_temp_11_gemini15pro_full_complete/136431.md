nonreusables:/ˌnɒnriˈjuːzəblz/| n.|不可重复使用物；一次性用品|Items that are designed to be used only once and then discarded.  These are things that are not intended to be reused. |Many restaurants switched to nonreusables during the pandemic. 很多餐馆在疫情期间改用一次性用品。|Synonyms: disposables, single-use items|Antonyms: reusables, durables|Noun

nonreusable:/ˌnɒnriˈjuːzəbl/| adj.|不可重复使用的；一次性的|Not capable of being reused; intended for a single use.  Describes something that cannot or should not be used more than once.|Nonreusable coffee cups contribute to a lot of waste.  一次性咖啡杯造成了大量的浪费。|Synonyms: disposable, single-use|Antonyms: reusable, durable|Adjective
