affrayment:/əˈfreɪmənt/
n.
恐吓，惊吓
The state of being affrayed; fright; terror.
The affrayment caused by the sudden noise made her jump.  突然的噪音引起的惊吓让她跳了起来。
近义词：fright, fear, terror, intimidation, dismay
反义词：calm, composure, peace, tranquility
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *affraiement, from Anglo-Norman afraiement, from Old French esfreement, from esfreer (“to frighten”), from Late Latin exfridāre, from Latin ex- + frigidus (“cold”).
