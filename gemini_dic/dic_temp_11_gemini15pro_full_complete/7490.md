Example 2: The doctor diagnosed him with ankylostomiasis after examining his stool sample.  医生检查了他的粪便样本后，诊断他患有钩虫病。 | Synonym: hookworm infection, hookworm disease | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a disease.  One could consider "health" or "wellness" as a general opposite state.) | Noun
