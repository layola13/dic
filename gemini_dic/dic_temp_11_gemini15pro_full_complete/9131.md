antimysticalness:/ˌæntiˈmɪstɪkəlnəs/
|n.| 反神秘主义 |The quality or state of being opposed to mysticism. |His antimysticalness led him to reject any explanation that couldn't be empirically verified. 他的反神秘主义让他拒绝任何无法通过经验验证的解释。|Near synonyms: rationalism, empiricism, skepticism |Antonyms: mysticism, spirituality, occultism|noun
