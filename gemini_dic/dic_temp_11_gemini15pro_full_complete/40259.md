contingency:/kənˈtɪndʒənsi/| n.| 偶然性，意外事故，可能性，应急措施 |A contingency is a future event or circumstance which is possible but cannot be predicted with certainty.| We must consider all possible contingencies. 我们必须考虑所有可能发生的意外事件。| synonyms: eventuality, incident, possibility, emergency, accident | antonyms: certainty, predictability, planned event| noun

contingent:/kənˈtɪndʒənt/| adj.| 偶然的，依情况而定的，可能的 | Contingent describes something that is dependent on something else happening.|  The success of the project is contingent upon funding.  这个项目的成功取决于资金。| synonyms: dependent, conditional, subject to, possible | antonyms: independent, unconditional, certain| adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contingentia*, from Latin contingens, present participle of contingere "to touch, befall," from com- "together" (see com-) + tangere "to touch" (see tangent).
