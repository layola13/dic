iambical:/aɪˈæmbɪk(ə)l/
adjective
抑扬格的
Of or relating to an iamb, a metrical foot consisting of one unstressed syllable followed by one stressed syllable.
Shakespeare's sonnets are written in iambic pentameter.  莎士比亚的十四行诗是用抑扬格五音步写成的。

The poem's iambical rhythm creates a soothing effect. 这首诗的抑扬格节奏创造了一种舒缓的效果。

synonym: iambic
antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider trochaic)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *iambicus, from Greek iambikos, from iambos ("iamb") + -ikos ("-ic").
