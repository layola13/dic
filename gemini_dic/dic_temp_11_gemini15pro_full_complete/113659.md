machinima:/məˈʃiːnɪmə/ /məˈʃɪnɪmə/| n.|机造影片；游戏影片|A genre of film and animation created using real-time 3D graphics engines, often those from video games.|例句：The machinima used footage from the game "World of Warcraft." 这部机造影片使用了游戏《魔兽世界》的画面。

例句：Many aspiring filmmakers start by creating machinima. 许多有抱负的电影制作人都是从创作机造影片开始的。|近义词：gameplay video, in-game cinematic, rendered movie|反义词:|词性:noun
