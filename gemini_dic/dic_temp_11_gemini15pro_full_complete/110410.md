like-a-house-afire:/laɪk ə haʊs əˈfaɪər/
adverbial phrase
像着了火的房子一样；迅速地；猛烈地
Burning intensely or spreading rapidly, like a house on fire.  It describes something happening quickly and intensely.
The rumor spread like a house afire.  谣言像野火一样蔓延开来。
The business took off like a house afire. 生意红红火火地发展起来了。
近义词:rapidly, quickly, swiftly, speedily, like wildfire
反义词:slowly, gradually
词性:adverbial phrase
