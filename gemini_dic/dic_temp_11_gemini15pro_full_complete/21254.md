blood-brother:/ˈblʌdˌbrʌðər/| n.|歃血为盟的兄弟；结拜兄弟|A male who has sworn allegiance to another male in a ritual involving the mingling of blood.|例句：They became blood-brothers as teenagers and remained close friends throughout their lives. 他们十几岁时结为歃血兄弟，一生都是挚友。

例句：The two warriors made a pact and became blood-brothers. 两名战士立下誓约，结为兄弟。|近义词：sworn brother, close friend|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
