baddies:/ˈbædiz/| n.|坏蛋，恶棍|A slang term, often playful or ironic, referring to villains, antagonists, or people who behave badly.  It can also be used to refer to attractive women in a slightly provocative or edgy way. |例句：The movie featured a colorful cast of baddies.  电影中有一群形形色色的坏蛋。

例句：She's one of those Instagram baddies with millions of followers. 她是Instagram上拥有数百万粉丝的辣妹之一。|近义词：villains, antagonists, miscreants, scoundrels,  (if referring to attractive women) femme fatales|反义词:heroes, good guys, protagonists|词性:Noun
