downscale:/ˈdaʊnskeɪl/ | adj. | 缩小规模的，低档的 | relating to or involving a reduction in size, scope, or extent | They moved to a more downscale neighborhood. 他们搬到了一个更低档的社区。 | smaller, reduced, modest | upscale, luxurious, extravagant | adjective
