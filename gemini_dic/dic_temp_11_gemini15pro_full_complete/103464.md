job-out:/ˈdʒɒb aʊt/ | v. | 外包；将…转包出去 | To contract (work) to an outside supplier.  |  The company jobbed out the data entry work to a firm in India. 公司将数据录入工作外包给了一家印度公司。| Example:  Many companies choose to job out their IT support. 许多公司选择将IT支持外包。| Synonyms: outsource, subcontract, contract out | Antonyms: insource | verb

job-out:/ˈdʒɒb aʊt/ | n. | 外包；转包 | The act of contracting work to an outside supplier. | The job-out of manufacturing jobs has caused unemployment in the region. 制造业岗位的外包导致了该地区的失业。| Example: The recent job-out led to protests from the employees. 最近的业务外包引发了员工的抗议。| Synonyms: outsourcing, subcontracting | Antonyms: insourcing | noun
