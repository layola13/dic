garlandless:/ˈɡɑːrləndləs/| adj.|没有花环的，没有装饰的|Lacking garlands; not adorned with garlands.  Derived from "garland" (a wreath of flowers and leaves) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).|The Christmas tree looked sad and garlandless.|Synonyms: undecorated, unadorned, bare, plain|Antonyms: decorated, adorned, festooned, ornamented|adjective
