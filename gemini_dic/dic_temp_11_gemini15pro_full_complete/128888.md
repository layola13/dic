NIMBY:/ˈnɪmbi/| n. |邻避症患者；邻避主义者|A person who objects to the siting of something perceived as unpleasant or potentially dangerous in their own neighborhood, such as a landfill or hazardous waste facility, especially while not objecting to the need for it somewhere.|The NIMBYs protested the construction of the new prison.|近义词：localist, resident, protester|反义词:supporter, advocate|名词

Etymology:  Acronym from "Not In My Back Yard."
