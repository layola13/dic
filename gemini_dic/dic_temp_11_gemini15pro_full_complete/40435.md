contrast:/ˈkɑːntræst/ (US), /ˈkɒntrɑːst/ (UK)| n. v. |对比; 差异; 对照; 反差|the state of being strikingly different from something else, typically something in juxtaposition or close association.|The contrast between the two cities is striking. 这两个城市之间的反差很明显。|synonyms: difference, distinction, disparity, dissimilarity|antonyms: similarity, resemblance, likeness|noun

contrast:/ˈkɑːntræst/ (US), /ˈkɒntrɑːst/ (UK)| v. |对比; 对照; 形成对照|compare in such a way as to emphasize differences.|The poet contrasted the joys of youth with the pains of old age. 这位诗人将青春的欢乐与老年的痛苦进行了对比。|synonyms: compare, differentiate, juxtapose|antonyms: equate, assimilate|verb
