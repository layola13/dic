appellee:/əˌpeˈliː/ /əˌpɛˈliː/ | n. |被上诉人， appellee |The appellee argued against the appeal. 被上诉人对上诉提出了反对意见。|The appellee won the case. 被上诉人赢得了这场官司。|Synonym: respondent |Antonym: appellant |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *apelee, past participle of apeler (“to appeal to”), from Latin appellō (“I address, call upon, appeal to”). Equivalent to appeal + -ee.
