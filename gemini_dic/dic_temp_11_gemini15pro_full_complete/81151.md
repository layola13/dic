词源:  "Grand-daddy" is a colloquial and affectionate term for "grandfather". "Grand" comes from the Old French "grand", meaning "large" or "great", and "daddy" is a childish term for "father".  The possessive form, "grand-daddy's", simply adds the possessive 's to the end.
