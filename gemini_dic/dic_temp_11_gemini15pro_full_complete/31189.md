changemakers:/ˈtʃeɪndʒˌmeɪkərz/
noun
变革者，变革推动者
People who are actively working to bring about positive change in the world.
These young changemakers are developing innovative solutions to address climate change.  这些年轻的变革者正在开发创新的解决方案来应对气候变化。
The organization supports changemakers who are working to improve access to education.  该组织支持致力于改善教育机会的变革者。
synonyms: reformers, innovators, pioneers, activists
antonyms: conservatives, traditionalists
n.
