lucre:/ˈluːkər/| n.|金钱，利润（尤指不正当的）|Money, especially when regarded as sordid or distasteful; profit.|Example 1: He made his lucre by exploiting the poor. 他靠剥削穷人赚钱。
Example 2: She wasn't interested in lucre, only in helping people. 她对金钱不感兴趣，只对帮助别人感兴趣。|近义词：money, profit, gain, riches, wealth|反义词：loss, debt, poverty|词性:noun

词源:
From Middle English *lucre*, from Old French *lucre*, from Latin *lucrum* (“gain, profit, advantage”), related to *lucrari* (“to gain”).
