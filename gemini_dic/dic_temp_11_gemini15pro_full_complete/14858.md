axon: /ˈæksɒn/ (UK) /ˈæksɑːn/ (US) | n. | 轴突 | The long threadlike part of a nerve cell along which impulses are conducted from the cell body to other cells. | The axon transmits electrical signals to other neurons. 轴突将电信号传递给其他神经元。 |  The axon is essential for communication between nerve cells. 轴突对于神经细胞之间的交流至关重要。 | 近义词: nerve fiber | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider dendrite as a functionally opposite part of the neuron) | 词性: noun
