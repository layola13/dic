indebting:/ɪnˈdɛtɪŋ/
adjective
使负有义务的；使受恩惠的
placing someone in debt; causing someone to feel grateful and obliged
I am greatly indebted to my family for their support. 我非常感谢我的家人对我的支持。
synonyms: obliged, beholden, grateful, thankful
antonyms: ungrateful, unappreciative
词性: 形容词
