conformingly:/kənˈfɔːrmɪŋli/ | adv. | 顺应地，相应地 | In a way that is in agreement with or follows something; obediently or compliant. |  She dressed conformingly for the occasion, wearing a conservative suit. 她穿着保守的套装，穿着符合场合。|  He nodded conformingly, though he didn't really agree. 他顺从地点了点头，尽管他并不真正同意。| 近义词: accordingly, correspondingly, appropriately, suitably, obediently | 反义词: rebelliously, defiantly, unconventionally, nonconformingly | 词性: 副词
