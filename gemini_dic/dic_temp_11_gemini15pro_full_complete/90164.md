hothousing:/ˈhɒthaʊzɪŋ/ /ˈhɑːˌθaʊzɪŋ/| n.|温室培养，揠苗助长|The practice of forcing children to learn at a faster rate than is considered normal, often to achieve specific goals like early admission to university.|Many parents resort to hothousing their children in the hope of getting them into prestigious universities.|近义词：intensive training, accelerated learning, cramming|反义词:natural development, self-paced learning|词性:名词

hothouse:/ˈhɒthaʊs/ /ˈhɑːˌθaʊs/| n.|温室，暖房|A heated glass building used for growing plants, especially tropical plants.|The orchids thrived in the humid atmosphere of the hothouse.|近义词：greenhouse, conservatory|反义词:|词性:名词
