aeroclub:/ˈɛəroʊˌklʌb/| n.|航空俱乐部|A social organization for people interested in aviation.|例句：He joined the local aeroclub to learn how to fly. 他加入了当地的航空俱乐部学习飞行。

例句：The aeroclub organizes flying lessons and social events.  航空俱乐部组织飞行课程和社交活动。

近义词：flying club|反义词:|词性:noun

词源: aero- (Greek, "air") + club (Old Norse, "club")
