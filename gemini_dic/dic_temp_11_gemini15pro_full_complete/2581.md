admonishing:/ədˈmɒnɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
劝诫的，警告的
Giving or conveying a gentle warning or reprimand.
The teacher gave the students an admonishing look. 老师给了学生们一个警告的眼神。
The letter contained an admonishing tone regarding his recent behavior. 这封信对他最近的行为带有警告的语气。

近义词: warning, cautionary, reproving, reproving, scolding, chiding
反义词: praising, commending, encouraging, laudatory

词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin admonit-, past participle stem of admonēre "to remind, advise, warn," from ad- "to" + monēre "to warn."
