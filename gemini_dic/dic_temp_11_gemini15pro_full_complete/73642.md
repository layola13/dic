forfend:/fɔːrˈfɛnd/ | v. |  防止，避免，阻止 | To avert, prevent, or ward off (something evil or unpleasant). |  May God forfend such a tragedy! 愿上帝阻止这样的悲剧发生！ | 近义词: prevent, avert, preclude, obviate | 反义词:  cause, incite, encourage | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Middle English *forfenden*, from Old English *forfēon* (“to despise, renounce, forbid, prohibit”), from *for-* (“away, against”) + *fēon* (“to hate, abhor”).  The sense shifted from "prohibit" to "prevent something from happening" over time.
