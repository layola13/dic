analogal: /ænəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 类似的，相似的；推理的，类推的 | Relating to or based on analogy; using reasoning based on the similarities between things. | Example: The lawyer made an analogical argument, comparing the current case to a previous precedent. 律师做了一个类比论证，将当前的案件与之前的先例进行了比较。

Example:  The development of wings in birds and bats is an analogal feature, arising through convergent evolution. 鸟类和蝙蝠翅膀的发育是一个类似的特征，是通过趋同进化产生的。 | 近义词: analogous, comparable, similar, parallel | 反义词: dissimilar, different, unlike | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Late Latin *analogicalis, from Latin analogia (“proportion, analogy”) + -alis.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek ἀναλογία (analogía, “proportion”).
