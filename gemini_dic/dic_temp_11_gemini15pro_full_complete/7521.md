annelida:/əˈnɛlɪdə/| n.|环节动物门|A large phylum comprising the segmented worms, such as earthworms, leeches, and marine worms. They are characterized by a body divided into ring-like segments.|Earthworms, members of the annelida phylum, are beneficial to soil health. 蚯蚓，环节动物门的一员，对土壤健康有益。|Synonyms: segmented worms|Antonyms: None|Noun
