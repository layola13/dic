diverberate: /daɪˈvɜːrbəreɪt/ /daɪˈvɝːbəreɪt/
verb
回响， reverberate, resound, echo
To cause to reverberate; resound. To be diffused or spread widely.  The sound diverberated through the valley. 声音在山谷中回荡。
The cheers diverberated around the stadium. 欢呼声在体育场内回荡。
近义词: reverberate, resound, echo, resonate
反义词: muffle, dampen, suppress
词性: verb
