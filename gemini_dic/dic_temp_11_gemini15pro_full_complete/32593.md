chiromonic: /ˌkaɪrəˈmɒnɪk/ | adj. | 手相的；与手相术有关的 | Relating to or characterized by chiromancy (palmistry). |  Chiromantic readings are said to reveal personality traits and predict the future. 手相解读据说可以揭示性格特征并预测未来。| Synonym: palmistic | Antonym:  (No direct antonym) | adjective

chiromancy: /ˈkaɪrəmænsi/ | n. | 手相术；掌纹术 | The art or practice of supposedly telling fortunes by examining the lines and other features of a person's hand. | She practices chiromancy and claims to be able to see your future. 她练习手相术，并声称能够看到你的未来。 | Synonym: palmistry | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
