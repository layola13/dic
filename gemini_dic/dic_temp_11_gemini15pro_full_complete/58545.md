dugongs:/ˈduːɡɒŋz/ (UK), /ˈduːɡɔːŋz/ (US)| n. |儒艮|A large, herbivorous marine mammal related to the manatee, found in warm coastal waters of the Indian and western Pacific Oceans. It has a distinctive rounded tail and tusks in the males.|Dugongs graze on seagrass in shallow coastal waters. 儒艮在浅海沿岸吃海草。|
Dugongs are often mistaken for manatees. 儒艮经常被误认为是海牛。|
Synonyms: sea cow|
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for an animal species)|noun
