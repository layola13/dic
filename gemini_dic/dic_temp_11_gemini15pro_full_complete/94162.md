impenetrablenesses:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtrəblənəsɪz/
noun
不可渗透性；难以理解
the quality or state of being impenetrable.  The impenetrablenesses of the jungle made it difficult to navigate.  丛林的难以穿透性使航行变得困难。
例句：The impenetrablenesses of his argument made it hard to refute. 他的论证难以理解，很难反驳。

例句：The impenetrablenesses of the fortress walls ensured its safety.  堡垒城墙的坚不可摧确保了它的安全。

近义词：inaccessibility, imperviousness, opacity
反义词：accessibility, permeability, transparency
词性: noun
