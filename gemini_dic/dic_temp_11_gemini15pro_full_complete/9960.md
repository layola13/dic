aphetic:/əˈfɛtɪk/ /eɪˈfɛtɪk/| adj.|省略首音的；首音脱落的|Of or relating to aphesis, the loss of an unstressed vowel at the beginning of a word.|The word 'squire' is an aphetic form of 'esquire'. “squire”一词是“esquire”的省略首音形式。|近义词:apocopated, clipped|反义词:None|词性:adjective

aphetic:/əˈfɛtɪk/ /eɪˈfɛtɪk/| adj.|省略首音的；首音脱落的|Characterized by aphesis.|The word 'cause' is aphetic from 'because'. “cause”一词是由“because”省略首音而来。|近义词:shortened, reduced|反义词:None|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *aphetikos*, from *aphiemi* "I send forth," from *apo* "off, away from" + *hiemi* "I send."
