epopee:/ˈɛpəpiː/
n.
史诗；叙事诗
A long narrative poem, typically one derived from ancient oral tradition, celebrating the deeds of a legendary or historical hero.
Homer's *Odyssey* is considered one of the greatest epopees in Western literature.  荷马的《奥德赛》被认为是西方文学中最伟大的史诗之一。

epic, saga, narrative poem
short story, novel
