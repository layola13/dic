gumboot:/ˈɡʌmˌbuːt/ (US) /ˈɡʌmbʊːt/ (UK)| n. |橡胶靴，胶鞋|A waterproof boot, typically made of rubber, extending to the knee or higher.|例句：He pulled on his gumboots before going out into the rain. 他穿上橡胶靴才出去淋雨。

例句：Children love splashing in puddles wearing their bright yellow gumboots. 孩子们喜欢穿着亮黄色的橡胶靴在水坑里溅水。|近义词：Wellington boots, wellies, rain boots|反义词: sandals, slippers|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From "gum" (referring to the rubber material) + "boot."  The use of gum to describe rubber dates back to the 18th century.
