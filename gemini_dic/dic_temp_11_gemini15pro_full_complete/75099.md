fringeless:/ˈfrɪnʤləs/
adjective
无边的，无穗的，无流苏的
Without a fringe or fringes; lacking a border of decorative threads, cords, or loops.
The fringeless curtains looked modern and minimalist.  无边的窗帘看起来很现代，很简约。
She preferred a fringeless scarf for a cleaner look. 她更喜欢无边的围巾，看起来更干净利落。
近义词: unbordered, edgeless, plain
反义词: fringed, tasseled, bordered
词性: adjective
