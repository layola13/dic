over-reach:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃ/ | v. | 过度伸展；超越；不自量力；做过头 | To defeat (oneself) by seeking or trying to achieve more than is possible or reasonable. |  The company overreached itself by expanding too quickly.  这家公司扩张太快，不自量力。| 近义词: overextend, exceed, surpass | 反义词: underestimate, restrain | verb

over-reach:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃ/ | n. | 过度扩张；过分行为 | The act of overreaching. | The company's overreach into new markets led to financial difficulties.  这家公司过度扩张到新市场导致了财务困难。| 近义词: excess, overextension | 反义词: restraint, moderation | noun
