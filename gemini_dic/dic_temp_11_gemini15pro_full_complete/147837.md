overpunishments: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌnɪʃmənts/
| n. | 过度惩罚 | Excessive punishments, penalties that are too harsh or severe for the offense committed. | The overpunishments meted out to minor offenders sparked public outrage. 对轻微违法者施加的过度惩罚引发了公众的愤怒。|  The school's overpunishments for minor infractions led to student protests. 学校对轻微违规行为的过度惩罚导致了学生抗议。| 近义词: excessive punishments, draconian penalties, disproportionate sanctions | 反义词: leniency, clemency, light sentences | 词性: 名词

词源:
"over-" (prefix): from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, excessively."
"punishment": from Old French "punissement," derived from the verb "punir" (to punish), ultimately from Latin "punire" (to punish, inflict pain).
