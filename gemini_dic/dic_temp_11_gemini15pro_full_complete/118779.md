metalloidal:/ˌmɛtəlˈɔɪdəl/
adjective
类金属的
Resembling or characteristic of a metalloid; intermediate in properties between a metal and a nonmetal.
Silicon is a metalloidal element used extensively in electronics.  硅是一种类金属元素，广泛用于电子产品。
metalloid, semimetallic
metallic, nonmetallic
