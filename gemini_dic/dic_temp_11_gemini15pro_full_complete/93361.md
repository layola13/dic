USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你觉得怎么样？
What does he want? = 他想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are they doing now? = 他们现在在做什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has he eaten for breakfast? = 他早餐吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 PM last night? = 你昨晚八点在做什么？
What was he thinking about when I saw him? = 我看到他时他在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? = 会议前他们讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 打扰之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? =  在她换专业之前，她一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气会怎样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议将讨论什么？



将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by next year? = 到明年你会完成什么？
What will they have finished by the deadline? = 截止日期前他们会完成什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？




虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she say if she knew the truth? = 如果她知道真相她会说什么？


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
She doesn't understand what you mean. = 她不明白你的意思。
