dismastment:/dɪsˈmɑːstmənt/
n.
失掉桅杆; 失去桅杆的事件; 桅杆的毁坏
The act of removing or the state of being without a mast or masts, as of a ship.
The storm resulted in the dismastment of the small yacht.  暴风雨导致了这艘小游艇的桅杆断裂。
近义词: destruction, wreckage, demolition
反义词: construction, repair
