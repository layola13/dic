merry-go-round:/ˈmeri ɡoʊ ˌraʊnd/| n.|旋转木马|A revolving amusement ride consisting of a circular platform with seats, often shaped like animals, that go up and down as the platform rotates.|The children loved riding the merry-go-round at the fair. 孩子们喜欢在游乐场里玩旋转木马。|Synonyms: carousel, roundabout|Antonyms: None|Noun

merry-go-round:/ˈmeri ɡoʊ ˌraʊnd/| n.|一连串的活动; 令人眼花缭乱的活动|A rapid succession of activities or events.|It's been a merry-go-round of meetings and conferences all week.  整个星期都是一连串的会议。|Synonyms: whirl, cycle, whirlwind|Antonyms: None|Noun
