diaphoresis:/ˌdaɪəfəˈriːsɪs/| n.|出汗，盗汗|Profuse sweating, typically caused by a medical condition or drug.  Excessive sweating.|例句：The patient experienced diaphoresis during the night. 病人在夜间出现了盗汗。

例句：Diaphoresis is a common side effect of this medication.  出汗是这种药物的常见副作用。|近义词：perspiration, sweating|反义词:anhidrosis|词性:noun
