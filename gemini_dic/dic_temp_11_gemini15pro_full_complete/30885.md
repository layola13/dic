chaffier:/ˈtʃæfiər/ | adj. | 更轻浮的，更爱开玩笑的 | more prone to lighthearted banter or teasing; more playful and frivolous |  He became chaffier as the evening wore on.  随着夜幕降临，他变得更加轻浮了。 |  lighter, jokier, more playful | more serious, more somber | adjective

chaffier:/ˈtʃæfiər/ | adj. |  (of grain) containing more chaff | (指谷物)含有更多谷壳的 | The chaffier the grain, the less flour you'll get. 谷物里谷壳越多，能磨出的面粉就越少。|  huskier, containing more chaff | containing less chaff | adjective
