lull's:/lʌlz/ | n. | 平静；间歇；暂停；【罕用】催眠曲 | A lull is a temporary period of quiet or inactivity.  It can also refer to a soothing sound or song used to calm someone, especially a baby. | The lull in the storm gave us a chance to regroup.  暴风雨的间歇给了我们重新集结的机会。 | synonyms: pause, respite, break, calm, stillness | antonyms: activity, commotion, disturbance, turbulence | noun
