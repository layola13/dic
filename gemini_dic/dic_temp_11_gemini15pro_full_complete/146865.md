overinflate:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪt/| v. |过度充气；过分夸大|To inflate something beyond a safe or recommended limit; to exaggerate or overstate something.|例句：He overinflated the tires, which caused them to burst. 他给轮胎充气充过了头，导致轮胎爆裂。

例句：The media overinflated the importance of the incident. 媒体夸大了事件的重要性。|近义词：inflate, overestimate, exaggerate, pump up|反义词: deflate, underestimate, understate|词性:动词
