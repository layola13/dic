disdainfully:/dɪsˈdeɪnfʊli/| adv.|轻蔑地，不屑一顾地|Showing contempt or lack of respect.|She disdainfully tossed her head and walked away. 她轻蔑地甩了甩头，走开了。|Synonyms: contemptuously, scornfully, haughtily|Antonyms: respectfully, admiringly, reverently|词性: 副词

disdain:/dɪsˈdeɪn/| n. vt.|轻蔑，鄙视；蔑视，鄙弃|A feeling of contempt or lack of respect.|She treated his offer with disdain. 她对他的提议嗤之以鼻。|Synonyms: contempt, scorn, derision|Antonyms: respect, admiration, reverence|词性: 名词，动词

词源: From Old French *desdeigner*, from *des-* (expressing negation) + *deigner* "to deign," from Latin *dignari* "to deem worthy."
