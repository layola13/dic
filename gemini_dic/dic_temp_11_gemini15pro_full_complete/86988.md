henlike:/ˈhenlaɪk/
adj.
像母鸡的；胆小的；
Resembling a hen, especially in being timid or fussy.
The child was henlike in his fear of the dark.  这孩子像母鸡一样害怕黑暗。
Synonyms: timid, cowardly, fearful, apprehensive, faint-hearted
Antonyms: brave, courageous, bold, daring, fearless
词性: 形容词
