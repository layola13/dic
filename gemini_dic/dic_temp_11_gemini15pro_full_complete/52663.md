digitizations:/ˌdɪdʒɪtɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|数字化，数位化|The process of converting analog information into digital form.  The act or process of digitizing something.|例句：The library's digitizations of its rare books have made them accessible to a wider audience. 图书馆将其珍稀书籍数字化，使更多读者能够接触到它们。

例句：Recent digitizations of historical documents have provided new insights into the past.  最近对历史文献的数字化为我们了解过去提供了新的视角。|近义词：conversions, encodings|反义词:analogizations
