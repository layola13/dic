grapevines:/ˈɡreɪpˌvaɪnz/ | n. |葡萄藤; 葡萄树; 小道消息; |A climbing plant that bears grapes.  A way in which news or information spreads quickly from one person to another. | The grapevines were heavy with fruit. 葡萄藤上果实累累。| synonyms: vine, creeper, tendril, rumor mill, gossip | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the plant itself.  For the informal meaning, perhaps "official channels") | noun
