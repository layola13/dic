digastric: /daɪˈɡæstrɪk/ | adj. n.| 二腹肌的；二腹肌 |  Relating to or denoting a muscle with two fleshy bellies separated by a central tendon, especially the digastric muscle, which opens the mouth. | The digastric muscle plays a crucial role in chewing and swallowing.  二腹肌在咀嚼和吞咽中起着至关重要的作用。 |  Synonym: biventer | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "single-bellied" in a descriptive context) |  adjective, noun
