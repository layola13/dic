agrief:/əˈɡriːf/
noun
悲痛，悲伤
A strong feeling of sadness, typically for a deceased person.
She was agrief after the death of her husband. 她丈夫去世后她非常悲痛。
近义词：grief, sorrow, distress
反义词：joy, happiness
词性：名词

Etymology:  From a- (intensifier) + grief.  The prefix "a-" emphasizes or intensifies the meaning of "grief".
