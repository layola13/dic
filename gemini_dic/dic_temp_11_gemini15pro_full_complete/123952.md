morice:/ˈmɔːrɪs/| n.|Morris dance; a Moorish dance; a kind of dance performed by men wearing bells and waving handkerchiefs|A traditional English folk dance, often performed with bells and sticks. It is believed to have originated from Moorish dances.|The men performed a lively morice in the town square.|近义词：folk dance, traditional dance|反义词:|n.
