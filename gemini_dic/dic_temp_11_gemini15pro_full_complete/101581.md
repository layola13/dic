irp:/ɜːrp/ | n. | Individual Retirement Plan | 个人退休计划 | An individual retirement plan (IRP) is a way to save for retirement that provides tax advantages. | Example:  He maximized his contributions to his IRP every year. 他每年都将他的个人退休计划缴款额最大化。

Example:  She rolled over her 401(k) into an IRP. 她把她的401(k)计划转入了个人退休计划。| 近义词: retirement plan, pension plan | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun
