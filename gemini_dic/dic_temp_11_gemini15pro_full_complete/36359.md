coincidence: /koʊˈɪnsɪdəns/ (US) /kəʊˈɪnsɪdəns/ (UK)| n.|巧合，一致，同时发生|a situation in which two or more similar things happen at the same time by chance|例句：It was pure coincidence that we met at the airport. 我们在机场相遇纯属巧合。|例句：By coincidence, we both went to the same university.  巧的是，我们都上了同一所大学。|近义词：chance, accident, fluke|反义词：intention, plan|词性: 名词
