beatitude:/biːˈætɪtjuːd/ /biːˈætɪtuːd/
n.
至福;祝福;幸福
Supreme blessedness or happiness.

例句：
She experienced a state of pure beatitude.
她体验到了一种纯粹的幸福。

The beatitudes in the Sermon on the Mount describe the qualities of those who are blessed.
登山宝训中的祝福描述了那些有福之人的品质。


近义词：bliss, happiness, felicity, blessedness
反义词：misery, unhappiness, sorrow
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin beatitudo (“blessedness”), from Latin beatus (“blessed, happy”).
