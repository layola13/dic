iodoquinine:/ˌaɪoʊdoʊˈkwɪniːn/
noun
碘喹啉
A halogenated hydroxyquinoline, C9H5IN2O, formerly used as an antiseptic and amebicide.
Iodoquinine was once a common treatment for amoebic dysentery. 碘喹啉曾经是治疗阿米巴痢疾的常用药物。
Iodoquinine is no longer widely used due to potential side effects. 由于潜在的副作用，碘喹啉不再被广泛使用。
Synonyms: diiodohydroxyquin, clioquinol
Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for a specific chemical compound)
