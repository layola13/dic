countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ | v. | 撤销，取消（命令） | To revoke or cancel (a command, order, etc.); to recall by a contrary order. | The general countermanded the order to attack. 将军撤销了进攻的命令。 |  The company countermanded the shipping order after realizing the customer's address was incorrect. 公司在意识到客户地址不正确后取消了发货单。|  rescind, revoke, cancel, overrule, repeal |  affirm, approve, confirm, endorse, ratify | verb
