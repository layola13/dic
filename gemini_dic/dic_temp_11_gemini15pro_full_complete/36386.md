experiencing the same intense feeling or happening at the same time with great intensity.
The cointense grief shared by the community after the tragedy was palpable.  这起悲剧发生后，整个社区共同的强烈悲痛显而易见。

反义词:separate, asynchronous, non-concurrent
词性:adjective
词源:  A blend of "co-" (together) and "intense".
