over-securities: /ˈoʊvər-sɪˈkjʊrɪtiz/
noun
过度担保，超额担保
An excess of securities pledged as collateral for a loan or other obligation.  This means more collateral is offered than the value of the loan or obligation.
The bank demanded over-securities to mitigate their risk.  银行要求过度担保以降低风险。
The over-securities provided a cushion against market fluctuations. 超额担保为市场波动提供了缓冲。
synonyms: excess collateral, surplus security
antonyms: insufficient collateral, under-collateralization
n.
