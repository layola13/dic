corrugator:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪtər/ | n. | 皱肌，波纹形成器 | A muscle that wrinkles or contracts a part of the body, particularly the corrugator supercilii which furrows the brow. | The corrugator supercilii muscles contract, causing vertical wrinkles between the eyebrows.  皱眉肌收缩，导致眉毛之间出现垂直皱纹。|  The patient's corrugator muscles were highly active, suggesting tension. 病人的皱肌非常活跃，表明他很紧张。 | synonym:  wrinkler | antonym: relaxer | noun
