amarantaceous:/ˌæmərænˈteɪʃəs/
adj.
苋科的; 紫红色的
Of or relating to the amaranth family (Amaranthaceae), which includes plants such as amaranth, pigweed, and cockscomb.  The term can also describe the purplish-red color characteristic of the flowers of some amaranth species.
The amarantaceous weeds are choking the life out of my garden. 这些苋科的杂草正在扼杀我花园里的植物。
The sunset painted the sky in amarantaceous hues. 日落将天空染成了紫红色。
近义词:magenta, purplish-red, crimson
反义词:(For color) green, blue, yellow
词性:adjective
