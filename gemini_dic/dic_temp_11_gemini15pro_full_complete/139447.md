obsoleted: /ˈɒbsəˌliːtɪd/ (US) /ˈɒbsəliːtɪd/ (UK)| v. |过时的，废弃的|Made no longer useful or relevant, typically by something newer or more effective.|例句：The old software has been obsoleted by the new version.  旧软件已经被新版本淘汰了。

例句：Many of these old customs are now obsoleted. 许多旧习俗现在已经过时了。

近义词：outdated, superseded, archaic, deprecated, old-fashioned, obsolete

反义词：current, modern, contemporary, new, updated, state-of-the-art

词性：动词 (past participle)


词源：From Latin *obsoletus*, past participle of *obsolescere* "to fall into disuse," from ob- "away" + solescere "to grow accustomed, grow old," related to solere "to be accustomed."
