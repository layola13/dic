outsiderish:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdərɪʃ/
adjective
局外人的；不合群的；像个局外人的
Characteristic of or resembling an outsider; feeling or appearing detached or excluded from a group.
He had an outsiderish quality that made it hard for him to fit in.  他有一种不合群的特质，使他很难融入。
She felt outsiderish at the exclusive club.  她在那个高级俱乐部里感到格格不入。
synonym: alienated, detached, excluded, isolated, unsociable
antonym: belonging, included, integrated, sociable, insider
词性: 形容词
