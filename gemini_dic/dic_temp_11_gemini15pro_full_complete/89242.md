homilist:/ˈhɒmɪlɪst/ (US) /ˈhɑːməlɪst/| n. |讲道者，布道者|A person who delivers homilies (sermons).|The homilist delivered a powerful message about forgiveness.  讲道者发表了一篇关于宽恕的有力信息。

The young homilist captivated the congregation with his inspiring words.  这位年轻的讲道者用他鼓舞人心的话语吸引了会众。|近义词：preacher, minister, sermonizer|反义词: None|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *homilista, from Latin homilia (“homily”), from Ancient Greek ὁμιλία (homilía, “intercourse, conversation, instruction”), from ὁμιλέω (homiléō, “I converse, associate with”), from ὁμός (homós, “same, common”) + ἴλη (ílē, “troop, company”).
