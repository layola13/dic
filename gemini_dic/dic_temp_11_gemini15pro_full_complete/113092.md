lunch:/lʌntʃ/
n.|午餐|A meal eaten in the middle of the day.|I usually have lunch at noon. 我通常中午吃午餐。|
近义词：midday meal, luncheon|反义词:dinner, supper|词性:noun


lunch:/lʌntʃ/
v.|吃午餐|To eat lunch.|Let's lunch together tomorrow. 明天我们一起吃午饭吧。|
近义词：have lunch|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "fast", "skip a meal") |词性:verb


Etymology:
From Middle English *lunchen, *nonchen (“a midday snack”), from Old English *nuncheon, *nonmete (“noon-meat”), from non (“noon”) + schench (“drink, cup”), from Old English scencan (“to pour, pour out a drink”).  Influenced by and ultimately shortened from luncheon.
