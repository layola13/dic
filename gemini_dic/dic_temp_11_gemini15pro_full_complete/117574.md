megalith:/ˈmɛɡəlɪθ/| n.|巨石|A large prehistoric stone that has been used to construct a structure or monument, typically arranged in a ritualistic manner.|Stonehenge is a famous example of a megalithic structure. 巨石阵是巨石建筑的著名例子。|Synonyms: monolith, giant stone|Antonyms: pebble, small stone|noun
