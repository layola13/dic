noncollaboration:/ˌnɑːnkəˌlæbəˈreɪʃn/
n.
不合作，不配合
Lack of cooperation or joint action, especially between parties who might normally be expected to work together.
The noncollaboration of the witness with the police hindered the investigation.  证人不与警方合作阻碍了调查。
noncooperation, refusal to cooperate
collaboration, cooperation
noun
