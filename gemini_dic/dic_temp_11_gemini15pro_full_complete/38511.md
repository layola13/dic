conciliation:/kənˌsɪliˈeɪʃən/
n.
和解，调解
The act of bringing two opposing sides together, especially in an argument or dispute, to make peace.
The union and management reached a conciliation after a long strike. 工会和管理层在长期罢工后达成了和解。
reconciliation, compromise, mediation
conflict, discord
noun

conciliations:/kənˌsɪliˈeɪʃənz/
n.
和解，调解（复数）
Multiple acts of bringing opposing sides together to make peace.
The various conciliations between the warring factions eventually led to a lasting peace. 交战各派之间的多次和解最终带来了持久的和平。
reconciliations, compromises, mediations
conflicts, discords
noun
