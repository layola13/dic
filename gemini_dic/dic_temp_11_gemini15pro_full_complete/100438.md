intertrafficking: /ˌɪntərˈtræfɪkɪŋ/
noun
相互贩运；相互买卖
The illegal movement of goods or people between different groups or entities.
Intertrafficking of drugs and weapons between gangs is a serious problem. 帮派之间相互贩运毒品和武器是一个严重的问题。
Near Synonyms:  trading, exchanging, dealing, trafficking
Antonyms:  boycotting, embargo, sanction
Word Origin:  "inter-" (between) + "trafficking" (illegal trade)
