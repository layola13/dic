amerce:/əˈmɜːrs/| vt.|惩罚；处罚；课以罚金|To punish by inflicting a fine, especially an arbitrary one.|The judge amerced the defendant a hefty fine for contempt of court. 法官因被告藐视法庭而对其处以巨额罚款。|Synonyms: fine, penalize, punish, mulct|Antonyms: reward, compensate|Verb

amerce:/əˈmɜːrs/| n.|罚金|A fine imposed at the discretion of a court.|The amerce imposed by the king was substantial. 国王课以的罚金数额巨大。|Synonyms: fine, penalty, forfeiture|Antonyms: reward, compensation|Noun
