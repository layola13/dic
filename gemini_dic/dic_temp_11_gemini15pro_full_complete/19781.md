billowing:/ˈbɪloʊɪŋ/| adj.|滚滚的，波涛汹涌的，（像波浪般）翻滚的| characterized by great swelling waves or surges, as of smoke, fabric, or water.|例句：The billowing sails of the ship caught the wind.  船上翻滚的风帆兜住了风。|例句：Billowing smoke poured from the burning building. 滚滚浓烟从燃烧的建筑物中涌出。|近义词：surging, swelling, rolling, waving, fluttering|反义词：flat, still, calm|词性:形容词
Etymology->
From Middle English *billowen*, from Old Norse *bylgja* ("billow, wave"), from Proto-Germanic *bulgijǭ* (“bag, sack, bulge, swelling”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰelǵʰ- (“to swell”). Cognate with German *wölben* (“to arch, vault”), Dutch *bol* (“ball, bulb”), Latin *follis* (“bellows, leather bag”).
