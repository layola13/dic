overdecorating:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkəˌreɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度装饰
Decorating a space with too many ornaments or elements, resulting in a cluttered or gaudy appearance.
The new homeowners were warned against overdecorating their small living room.  新房主被警告不要过度装饰他们的小客厅。
She was so excited about Christmas that she ended up overdecorating the house. 她对圣诞节太兴奋了，结果把房子装饰得太多了。
Synonyms: over-embellishing, over-ornamenting, cluttering
Antonyms: underdecorating, simplifying, minimalizing
verb
