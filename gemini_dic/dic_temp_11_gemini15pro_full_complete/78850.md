ginger-up:/ˈdʒɪndʒər ʌp/
verb
使活跃，使有生气，激励
to make something more exciting or interesting
例句：The coach tried to ginger up the team at half time. 教练在中场休息时试图激励球队。

例句：They’re trying to ginger up their image. 他们正试图提升他们的形象。

近义词：liven up, invigorate, stimulate, energize, pep up
反义词：dampen, discourage, dispirit
词性: verb

词源：
From ginger (n.) + up (adv.).  The original sense was "to make (a horse) go faster by applying ginger to its anus."  Figurative sense of "invigorate, stimulate" is from 1825.
