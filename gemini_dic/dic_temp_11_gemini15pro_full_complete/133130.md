nonesthetic:/ˌnɒnɛsˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 非审美的，不美的 | Lacking aesthetic qualities; not pleasing to the eye. | The building's nonesthetic design was criticized by many. 这栋建筑缺乏美感的设计受到了很多人的批评。 |  The nonesthetic plastic chairs clashed with the elegant decor of the room. 这些毫无美感的塑料椅子与房间优雅的装饰格格不入。 | 近义词: unaesthetic, inartistic, ugly, unsightly | 反义词: aesthetic, artistic, beautiful, attractive | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + aesthetic (from Greek aisthētikos, meaning "sensitive," "perceptive")
