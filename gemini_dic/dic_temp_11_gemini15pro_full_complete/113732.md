macroeconomic: /ˌmækroʊˌiːkəˈnɑːmɪk/ | adj. |宏观经济的|Relating to the branch of economics concerned with large-scale or general economic factors, such as interest rates and national productivity.|  Macroeconomic policies are designed to influence the overall economy.  宏观经济政策旨在影响整体经济。|Synonym: large-scale, aggregate, overall|Antonym: microeconomic|adjective

macroeconomics: /ˌmækroʊˌiːkəˈnɑːmɪks/ | n. |宏观经济学|The branch of economics concerned with large-scale or general economic factors, such as interest rates and national productivity.|The study of macroeconomics helps us understand economic growth and recession. 对宏观经济学的研究有助于我们理解经济增长和衰退。|Synonym: macro-economics|Antonym: microeconomics|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From macro- + economic.  "Macro-" comes from the Greek "makros" meaning "large".
