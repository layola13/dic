overpays:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪz/ | v. | 多付，付得过多 |  Pays someone more than they deserve or are worth. | The company overpays its executives.  这家公司给高管的工资过高。| Synonyms: overcompensate, overreward | Antonyms: underpay, shortchange | Verb

overpaid:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪd/ | adj. | 付得过高的，报酬过高的 |  Receiving more money than deserved or is usual for the work done. |  Many believe that professional athletes are overpaid. 很多人认为职业运动员的薪水过高。 | Synonyms: overcompensated, overrewarded | Antonyms: underpaid, shortchanged | Adjective
