egyptological:/ˌiːdʒɪptəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 埃及学的 | Relating to the study of ancient Egypt, its language, culture, and history. | He has a vast egyptological library. 他拥有一个庞大的埃及学图书馆。 |  She is an egyptological expert. 她是一位埃及学专家。 |  近义词:  Pharaonic, ancient Egyptian | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Egypt + -ology (study of) + -ical (adjective suffix).  Ultimately derived from the Greek Aigyptos (Egypt) and logos (word, study).
