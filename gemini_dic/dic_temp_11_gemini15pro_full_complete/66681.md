exogamies:/ˌeksəˈɡeɪmiz/
n.
异族通婚(exogamy的复数)
The practice of marrying outside of one's own group. Exogamies are often practiced to strengthen alliances between different groups or to avoid genetic issues associated with inbreeding.
Exogamies were common between tribes in ancient times. 古代部落之间异族通婚很常见。
The various exogamies practiced by the tribes helped to maintain genetic diversity. 各部落实行的各种异族通婚有助于保持基因多样性。
近义词:intermarriage
反义词:endogamy, homogamy
词性:名词
