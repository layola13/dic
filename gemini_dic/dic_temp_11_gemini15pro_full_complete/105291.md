一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? 你觉得怎么样？
What does he do for a living? 他靠什么谋生？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? 你在看什么？
What is she doing here? 她在这里做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? 你学到了什么？
What has she been up to? 她一直在忙什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? 你一直在做什么？
What has he been thinking about? 他一直在想什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? 你说什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing yesterday? 你昨天在做什么？
What was she thinking about at the time? 她当时在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption?  被打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她转专业之前一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):

What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
