corkscrewing:/ˈkɔːrkskruːɪŋ/| adj.  v. (present participle of corkscrew)|螺旋形的，开瓶器式的; 螺旋式行进/旋转| The plane corkscrewed down towards the runway.  飞机螺旋式下降朝跑道飞去。| The corkscrewing motion of the drill bit made a clean hole. 钻头的螺旋运动钻出了一个干净的孔。|近义词：spiralling, twisting, winding|反义词: straightening| 词性: 动词(现在分词), 形容词

corkscrew:/ˈkɔːrkskruː/| n. v. |开瓶器; 螺旋形|He used a corkscrew to open the wine. 他用开瓶器打开了葡萄酒。|The path corkscrewed up the mountain. 这条小路盘旋而上。| 近义词：bottle opener (n.), spiral, twist, wind (v.)| 反义词: unwind, straighten (v.)| 词性: 名词, 动词
