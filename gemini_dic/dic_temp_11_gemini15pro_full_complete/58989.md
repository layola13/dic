duty-free:/ˈdjuːti friː (British English), /ˈduːti friː (American English)| adj. n.|免税的，免税商品|Exempt from the payment of taxes or duties, especially import duties.  Can also refer to goods or shops selling such goods. |She bought some duty-free perfume at the airport. 她在机场买了一些免税香水。|Synonyms: tax-free, tax-exempt|Antonyms: taxable, dutiable|adjective, noun

duty-free:/ˈdjuːti friː (British English), /ˈduːti friː (American English)| n.|免税店|A shop, typically at an airport or port, that sells goods without taxes or duties. |They stopped at the duty-free to buy some whiskey. 他们在免税店停下来买了一些威士忌。|Synonyms: tax-free shop, bonded shop|Antonyms: N/A|noun
