hust:/hʌst/| v. n.| 努力工作；喧嚣；兜售；催促；强迫；妓女|To work hard and energetically; to push or force aggressively, especially to sell something or gain business.| He hustled to finish the project before the deadline. 他为了在截止日期前完成项目而拼命工作。 | 近义词: hustle, work hard, strive, push, pressure, tout, solicit | 反义词: relax, rest, dawdle | 词性: 动词, 名词

hust:/hʌst/| n.| 忙碌；喧嚣；骗局；妓女|Energetic activity; a fraud or swindle.|The city is full of hustle and bustle.  这座城市熙熙攘攘。| 近义词: bustle, activity, commotion, scam, fraud | 反义词: quiet, stillness, calm | 词性: 名词


词源:  Likely from Dutch *husselen "to shake, toss," related to hutselen "to shake, to hurry."
