botched:/ˈbɒtʃt/ /ˈbɑːtʃt/| adj.|拙劣的，搞砸的|carried out badly or carelessly.|The botched surgery left him with a permanent limp. 拙劣的手术使他永久性跛足。|Synonyms: bungled, messed up, ruined, spoiled, fouled up|Antonyms: successful, perfect, flawless|adjective

botch:/bɒtʃ/ /bɑːtʃ/| vt. n.|笨手笨脚地做；拙劣的工作|to spoil something by doing it badly.|He botched the repair job and now the leak is worse. 他把修理工作搞砸了，现在漏水更严重了。|Synonyms: bungle, mess up, ruin, spoil, foul up|Antonyms: succeed, perfect, complete|verb, noun
