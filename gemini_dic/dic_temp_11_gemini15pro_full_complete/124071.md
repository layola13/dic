morphosis:/mɔːrˈfoʊsɪs/| n. |变形，变态，变质|A change of the form or nature of a thing or person into a completely different one, by natural or supernatural means.|The butterfly undergoes a metamorphosis from caterpillar to winged insect. 蝴蝶经历了从毛毛虫到有翅昆虫的变态。|Synonyms: transformation, transmutation, alteration, conversion, evolution, change|Antonyms: stagnation, fixity, immutability|noun
