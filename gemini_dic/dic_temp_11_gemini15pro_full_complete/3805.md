agnomination:/ˌæɡnɒmɪˈneɪʃn/
n.
命名，称号
The act of naming, especially the giving of a name or epithet, often derogatory.
His political opponents bestowed upon him the agnomination "Tricky Dick." 他的政敌给他起了个绰号叫“狡猾的迪克”。
近义词: appellation, designation, sobriquet, nickname, moniker
反义词: anonymity

词性: noun
