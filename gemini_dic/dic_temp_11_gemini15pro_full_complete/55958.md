djinnee:/dʒɪˈniː/ /ˈdʒiːniː/
n.
精灵，妖怪
A supernatural creature in Arabian and Muslim mythology, often able to assume human or animal form and to grant wishes.
例句：The djinnee emerged from the lamp in a swirl of smoke. 精灵从灯里冒出一团烟雾。

例句：In the story, Aladdin rubbed the lamp and a djinnee appeared. 在故事中，阿拉丁擦了擦神灯，一个精灵出现了。

近义词：genie, jinn, spirit, demon
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French djinn, from Arabic jinnī.  Ultimately from a Semitic root meaning "to conceal."
