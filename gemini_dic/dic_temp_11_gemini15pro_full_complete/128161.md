nettles:/ˈnet(ə)lz/
n.
荨麻；令人恼火的事
a herbaceous plant which has jagged leaves covered with stinging hairs.
例句：
The nettles stung her legs as she walked through the long grass.
她穿过高高的草丛时，荨麻刺痛了她的双腿。

Be careful not to touch the nettles, they sting!
小心别碰荨麻，它们会刺痛你！


近义词：irritants, annoyances
反义词:soothers, palliatives
词性:noun (plural)


nettle:/ˈnet(ə)l/
v.
激怒，惹恼
irritate or annoy (someone).
例句：
She was nettled by his condescending tone.
她被他居高临下的语气激怒了。

His constant criticism nettled her.
他不断的批评惹恼了她。


近义词：irritate, annoy, provoke, vex
反义词:soothe, placate, calm
词性:verb
