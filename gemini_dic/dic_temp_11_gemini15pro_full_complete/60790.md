electromotor:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈmoʊtər/ | n. |电动机|A machine that converts electrical energy into mechanical energy, typically through the interaction of magnetic fields and conductors.|The electromotor whirred to life, powering the conveyor belt. 电动机嗡嗡作响，驱动着传送带。|Synonyms: electric motor, motor |Antonyms: generator |noun
