overjoyous: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪəs/ | adj. | 欣喜若狂的，极度高兴的 | Extremely joyful; filled with great happiness. |  She was overjoyous at the news of her son's graduation. 她听到儿子毕业的消息欣喜若狂。 | The children were overjoyous to see the snow. 孩子们看到雪欣喜若狂。 | 近义词: ecstatic, elated, thrilled, jubilant | 反义词: miserable, despondent, dejected, sorrowful | 词性: 形容词

overjoyous: 词源来自于 over- (过度，超过) + joyous (快乐的)。 Joyous 源自古法语 joius，最终源自拉丁语 gaudium (喜悦)。
