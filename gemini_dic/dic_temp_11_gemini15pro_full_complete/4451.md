albinism:/ˈælbɪˌnɪzəm/ | n. |白化病|A group of inherited disorders characterized by little or no melanin production.  It results in a lack of pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes.|  Example 1: Albinism affects people of all races. 白化病影响所有种族的人。

Example 2: The child's albinism made him sensitive to sunlight.  这个孩子的白化病使他对阳光敏感。|Synonym: achromasia, achromatosis|Antonym: pigmentation|Noun
