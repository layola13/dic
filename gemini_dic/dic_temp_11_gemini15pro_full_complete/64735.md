eschynite:/ˈɛskɪˌnaɪt/| n. |黑稀金矿|A complex mineral, a niobate, titanate and tantalate of cerium, calcium, iron, and thorium.|The eschynite was discovered in a remote mine in Russia.  这个黑稀金矿是在俄罗斯一个偏远的矿井中发现的。|Synonyms:  priorite, euxenite|Antonyms:  N/A|noun
