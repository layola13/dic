overreadiness:/ˌoʊvərˈrɛdinɪs/| n. |过度准备；过分积极|The state of being excessively or prematurely ready; excessive eagerness or preparedness.|His overreadiness to please his boss was annoying to his colleagues. 他过分殷勤地讨好老板，惹恼了同事。|近义词：eagerness, impatience, alacrity|反义词：hesitancy, reluctance, apathy|词性:名词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + readiness (noun from ready)
