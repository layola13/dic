hickory-stripe:/ˈhɪkəri straɪp/ | adj. n. | 山核桃条纹（的）; 山核桃条纹布 | A pattern of alternating stripes, typically of different widths, resembling the grain of hickory wood. Can also refer to fabric with this pattern. |  He wore a hickory-stripe shirt. 他穿着一件山核桃条纹衬衫。 | synonyms: striped, pinstriped | antonyms: plain, solid | adjective, noun
hickory-stripe:/ˈhɪkəri straɪp/ |  n. | 山核桃条纹布料 | Fabric with a hickory-stripe pattern. | She bought some hickory-stripe to make curtains. 她买了一些山核桃条纹布料做窗帘。 | synonyms: striped fabric, pinstriped fabric | antonyms: plain fabric, solid fabric | noun
