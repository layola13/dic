disarticulation:/dɪsˌɑːrˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/
n.|脱臼，关节离断|The act of separating bones at a joint.  The state of being disjointed or disconnected.|The surgeon performed a disarticulation of the shoulder.  外科医生进行了肩关节离断手术。|disjointing, separation|connection, articulation|noun


disarticulation:/dɪsˌɑːrˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/
v.|使脱臼，使关节离断|To separate bones at a joint; to disjoin.|The pathologist disarticulated the skeleton for further study. 病理学家将骨骼脱臼以便进一步研究。|disjoint, separate|connect, articulate|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *disarticulatus*, past participle of *disarticulare*, from dis- (“apart, asunder”) + articulare (“to joint”), from articulus (“joint”), diminutive of artus (“joint, limb”).
