PHRASE->
improve on/upon:  改进，胜过  例句：It will be difficult to improve on this performance. 很难超越这次的表演。
improve in: 在…方面有所改进  例句：He has improved in his studies. 他的学习有所进步。
room for improvement: 有改进的余地  例句：There is still room for improvement in your work. 你的工作还有改进的余地。
improve with age: 随着年龄增长而改进  例句：Wine improves with age. 酒越陈越香。
improve upon/on something:  在…的基础上改进  例句：They improved upon the original design. 他们在原有设计的基础上进行了改进。
