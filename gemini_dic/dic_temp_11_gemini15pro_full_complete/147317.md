overmoralize: /ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəˌlaɪz/ /ˌəʊvəˈmɒrəlaɪz/ | v. | 过分道德化；过分说教 | To moralize excessively; to make moral judgments too readily or too severely. |  He tends to overmoralize simple situations, making them seem more complex than they are. 他倾向于对简单的情况过分道德化，使它们看起来比实际情况更复杂。

| Example:  She overmoralized the children's behavior, making them feel guilty for minor infractions. 她对孩子们的行为过分说教，让他们对一些小错误感到内疚。| Synonym: preach, moralize excessively | Antonym:  be amoral, be immoral | Verb
