flatboat:/ˈflætboʊt/ (US) /ˈflætˌbəʊt/ (UK)| n.|平底船，驳船|A large, flat-bottomed boat used for transporting goods, especially on rivers. Typically, flatboats were not powered by engines but drifted downstream or were pulled by animals along the shore. |例句：The settlers loaded their belongings onto a flatboat and headed down the river.  定居者们把他们的 belongings 装到一艘平底船上，然后顺流而下。

例句：Flatboats were a common sight on the Mississippi River in the 19th century.  在19世纪的密西西比河上，平底船是常见的景象。|近义词：barge, scow|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps steamboat as a powered vessel) |词性:noun
