ignominy: /ˈɪɡnəmɪni/ | n. |耻辱，不名誉，屈辱 | Deep personal humiliation and disgrace. | He suffered the ignominy of being expelled from school. 他遭受了被学校开除的耻辱。|  She endured the ignominy of defeat. 她忍受了失败的耻辱。 | 近义词: shame, disgrace, humiliation, dishonor | 反义词: honor, glory, respect | 词性: 名词

ignominious: /ˌɪɡnəˈmɪniəs/ | adj. |可耻的，不光彩的，丢脸的 | Marked with or characterized by disgrace or shame. |  They retreated in ignominious defeat. 他们狼狈不堪地撤退了。 | The team suffered an ignominious loss. 这个队伍遭受了可耻的失败。 | 近义词: shameful, disgraceful, humiliating, dishonorable | 反义词: honorable, glorious, respectable | 词性: 形容词
