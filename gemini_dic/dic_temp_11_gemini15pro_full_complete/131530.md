noncommemoration:/ˌnɒnkəˌmɛməˈreɪʃən/
n.
不纪念，不庆祝
The act of not commemorating or the state of not being commemorated.  The deliberate absence of an act of remembrance or celebration.
The community's noncommemoration of the tragic event sparked controversy. 这个社区对这起悲惨事件的不纪念引发了争议。
The noncommemoration of the war's end was a political statement.  不纪念战争结束是一种政治声明。
near synonyms:  neglect, disregard, omission, oversight
antonyms: commemoration, remembrance, celebration
noun
