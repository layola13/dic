insomniac: /ɪnˈsɒmniæk/ (UK) /ɪnˈsɑːmniæk/ (US)| n. adj.|失眠症患者，失眠的|A person who regularly finds it difficult to sleep.|例句：He's a terrible insomniac and often wanders around the house at night. 他有严重的失眠症，经常晚上在房子里走来走去。

例句：She became an insomniac after the accident. 事故发生后，她失眠了。|近义词：sleepless, restless|反义词：sleeper|词性：名词，形容词

insomniac: /ɪnˈsɒmniæk/ (UK) /ɪnˈsɑːmniæk/ (US)| adj.|失眠的|Characterized by or suffering from insomnia.|例句：Insomniac nights can be incredibly frustrating. 失眠的夜晚会让人非常沮丧。

例句：He spent another insomniac night tossing and turning. 他又度过了一个辗转反侧、无法入睡的夜晚。|近义词：sleepless, restless|反义词：sleeper|词性：形容词
