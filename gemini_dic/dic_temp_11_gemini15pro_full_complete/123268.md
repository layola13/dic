monodramatic:/ˌmɒnədrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 独角戏式的；夸张的；情绪化的 | Characterized by exaggerated or excessively emotional behavior, often centered around a single person or theme, as in a monodrama.  A monodrama is a theatrical piece played by one actor.  The term monodramatic can describe a person who behaves as if they are the central figure in a dramatic performance, often overreacting to situations. | Her reaction to the spilled coffee was quite monodramatic; she acted as if it were a life-altering tragedy. 她对洒出的咖啡的反应相当夸张；她表现得好像这是一场改变人生的悲剧。

She has a rather monodramatic personality; everything is a crisis. 她性格相当情绪化；一切都像是一场危机。 | 近义词:histrionic, melodramatic, theatrical, overwrought | 反义词:understated, restrained, calm, composed | 词性:adjective
