goov:/ɡuːv/| n.| 凹槽，细沟，最佳状态|A long narrow cut or indentation in a surface.  A state of being comfortable or proficient in something.|The carpenter carved a goov in the wood for the inlay.  木匠在木头上刻了一道凹槽，用于镶嵌。  He's finally found his goov in his new job. 他终于在新工作中找到了最佳状态。|近义词：groove, channel, rut, furrow|反义词：ridge, projection|词性:名词

词源:  Probably a variant of *grove. Related to grave.
