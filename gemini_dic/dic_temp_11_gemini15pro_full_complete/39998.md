consultantships:/kənˈsʌlt(ə)ntʃɪps/
n.
咨询服务; 顾问工作; 顾问职位
A consultantship is a position as a consultant, or the period of time during which someone works as a consultant.

He held several consultantships with major corporations.  他曾在多家大公司担任顾问。

She gained valuable experience during her consultantship with the UN.  她在联合国担任顾问期间获得了宝贵的经验。

Synonym: consultancy, advisory position, consulting work
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider: permanent position, staff role)
词性: noun
