bespangle: /bɪˈspæŋɡəl/
verb
以亮片装饰；使闪耀
To cover or adorn with spangles or something sparkly.
The night sky was bespangled with stars.  夜空布满了星星。
The dancer's costume was bespangled with sequins.  舞者的服装上缀满了亮片。
近义词: adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament
反义词: strip, bare
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + spangle, from Middle English *spangel, from Old North French espangler "to glitter," probably from Frankish *spang "buckle, clasp," from Proto-Germanic *spangiz.
