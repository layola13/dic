delitescency:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɛsənsɪ/
n.
液化；潮解性
The process by which a substance absorbs moisture from the air and becomes liquid.
The delitescency of the chemical caused it to dissolve into a puddle.  这种化学物质的潮解性使它溶解成一滩液体。
近义词：liquefaction, dissolving
反义词：drying, dehydration
词性：名词

delitescent:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɛsənt/
adj.
潮解的；液化的
Becoming liquid by absorbing moisture from the air.
Calcium chloride is a delitescent substance. 氯化钙是一种潮解性物质。
近义词：liquefying, deliquescent
反义词：desiccant, drying
词性：形容词

词源：From Latin dēlītēscere (“to melt away, vanish”), from dē- (“down, away”) + līquēscere (“to become liquid”).
