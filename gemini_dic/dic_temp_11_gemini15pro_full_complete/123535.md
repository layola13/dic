monotrochal:/ˌmɒnəˈtrɒkəl/ /ˌmɑːnəˈtroʊkəl/| adj.|单轮的；单鞭毛的|Having or relating to a single flagellum (whip-like appendage) at one end of a cell, typically a bacterium. It describes a bacterial cell that has only one flagellum. |例句：The bacterium is monotrochal and moves with a distinctive spinning motion. 这种细菌是单鞭毛的，以独特的旋转运动方式移动。

例句：Monotrochal bacteria are less common than those with multiple flagella. 单鞭毛细菌不如有多条鞭毛的细菌常见。|近义词：uniflagellate|反义词：peritrichous, lophotrichous, amphitrichous|词性:adjective

词源:
From mono- (one) + Greek trokhós (wheel, anything that turns).
