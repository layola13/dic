masseuses:/məˈsuːzɪz/ |n.|女按摩师|A person who gives massages, typically a woman.|例句：The masseuses at the spa were highly skilled.  这家水疗中心的女按摩师技艺高超。

例句：Many masseuses specialize in specific types of massage. 许多女按摩师专攻特定类型的按摩。


近义词：massage therapists, masseurs (if referring to both men and women) |反义词:None (no direct opposite)|词性:Noun


词源：From French *masseuse*, feminine of *masseur* "masseur," from *masser* "to massage." Ultimately from Late Latin *massa* "lump, piece of dough."
