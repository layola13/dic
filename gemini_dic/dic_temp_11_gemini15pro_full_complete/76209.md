fustiness:/ˈfʌstɪnɪs/| n.|霉臭；陈腐；污浊|A stale, musty smell or quality.  The fustiness of the old attic was overwhelming.|The library had a distinct fustiness from all the old books.|近义词: mustiness, staleness, stuffiness|反义词: freshness, airiness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *fusty* + *-ness*. *Fusty* comes from Middle English *fustie*,  from Old French *fust* (tasting of the cask), ultimately from Latin *fustis* (club, staff).  The semantic shift likely occurred due to the association of stale smells with old wooden casks.
