frolicking:/ˈfrɒlɪkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfrɑːlɪkɪŋ/ (US)| adj.  v. (present participle)|嬉戏的，玩耍的|Playful and full of energy; gamboling merrily.|The lambs were frolicking in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏。|近义词：playing, gamboling, romping, cavorting|反义词：sullen, morose, gloomy|词性: adjective, verb (present participle)

Etymology: From Middle English *froliken*, from Old English *frēolic* (“joyful, merry”), from *frēo* (“free, noble”) + *-lic* (suffix forming adjectives).
