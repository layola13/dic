cassavas:/kəˈsɑːvəz/
n.
木薯（复数）
The starchy tuberous roots of a tropical tree, used as food in tropical countries.
Cassavas are a staple food in many parts of Africa. 木薯是许多非洲地区的主食。
Cassavas are peeled, boiled, and eaten. 木薯去皮、煮熟后食用。
近义词：yuca, manioc
反义词: (No direct antonym for a food item)
词性: noun
