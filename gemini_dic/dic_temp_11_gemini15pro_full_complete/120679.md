misadjudicated:/ˌmɪsəˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
错误判决的，审判不公的
incorrectly judged or decided, especially in a legal context.
The case was misadjudicated, leading to an innocent man's imprisonment.  这起案件被错误判决，导致一名无辜男子入狱。
The misadjudicated claim resulted in a significant financial loss for the company. 错误判决的索赔导致公司遭受重大经济损失。
近义词: wrongly judged, wrongly decided, unfairly judged
反义词: correctly judged, fairly judged
词性: adjective

mis-/mis-/ prefix.  badly, wrongly.  来自古英语，意为“错误地”、“坏地”。
adjudicate/əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪt/ verb.  make a formal judgement on a disputed matter.  来自拉丁语 *adjudicare*, 由 ad- (“to”) + judicare (“to judge”) 组成。 -ate 是动词后缀。
