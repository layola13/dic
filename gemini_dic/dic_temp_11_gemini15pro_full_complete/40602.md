convective:/kənˈvɛktɪv/| adj.|对流的，传送的|Relating to, caused by, or operating through convection.|例句：Convective clouds are formed by rising warm air. 对流云是由上升的暖空气形成的。|例句：The convective heat transfer is significant in this system. 在这个系统中，对流传热非常重要。|近义词：thermal, circulatory|反义词：conductive, radiative|词性:形容词
convection:/kənˈvɛkʃən/| n.|对流，传送|The movement caused within a fluid by the tendency of hotter and therefore less dense material to rise, and colder, denser material to sink under the influence of gravity, which consequently results in transfer of heat.|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convectivus, from Latin convectus (“carried together”), past participle of convehere (“to carry or bring together”), from con- (“together”) + vehere (“to carry”).
