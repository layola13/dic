acclimatises:/əˈklaɪmətaɪzɪz/
verb
适应气候，习惯环境
to become used to a new climate or environment
The climbers acclimatised themselves to the high altitude before attempting the summit.  登山者在尝试登顶之前先适应了高海拔。
acclimatize, adapt, adjust
--
词性:v.

acclimatise:/əˈklaɪmətaɪzɪz/
verb
使适应气候，使习惯环境
to cause someone or something to become used to a new climate or environment
The zookeepers are trying to acclimatise the panda to its new enclosure.  动物园管理员正试图让熊猫适应它的新围栏。
accustom, habituate
--
词性:v.
