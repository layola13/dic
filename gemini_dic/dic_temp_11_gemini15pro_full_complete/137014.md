nonstability:/ˌnɒnstəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不稳定性；非稳定性 | The lack of stability or the state of being unstable.  It refers to a system or situation that is prone to change, fluctuation, or disruption. | The nonstability of the political situation made investors nervous. 政治局势的不稳定性使投资者感到紧张。| The nonstability of the weather led to several flight cancellations. 天气的不稳定导致了几个航班取消。| 近义词：instability, volatility, precariousness | 反义词：stability, firmness, steadiness | 词性: 名词


词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + stability (from Late Middle English: stablenes, from stable + -nes)
