nonsedentarily:/ˌnɒnsedɛnˈtɛrɪli/
adverb
非久坐地，不固定地
in a way that is not sedentary; actively.
She worked nonsedentarily, pacing around her office while on calls. 她非久坐地工作，在打电话时会在办公室里来回走动。
He lives a nonsedentarily life, constantly traveling and exploring. 他过着非久坐的生活，不断地旅行和探索。
近义词: actively, dynamically, energetically
反义词: sedentarily, passively, inactively
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- + sedentary + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "sedentary" refers to sitting or remaining in one place, and "-ly" is an adverbial suffix.
