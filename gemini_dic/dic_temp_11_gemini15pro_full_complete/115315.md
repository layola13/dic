mannerly:/ˈmænəli/| adj.|有礼貌的，举止端正的|Behaving in a polite and respectful way.|She was very mannerly and always said please and thank you. 她很有礼貌，总是说“请”和“谢谢”。|近义词：polite, courteous, civil, respectful|反义词：rude, impolite, disrespectful, uncivil|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *manerly*, from *manere* (“manner”) + *-ly* (adjectival suffix). Ultimately from Latin *manus* (“hand”).
