houdini:/huːˈdiːni/ | n. | 胡迪尼；逃脱大师 |  Refers to Harry Houdini (1874-1926), a Hungarian-born American illusionist and stunt performer, noted for his sensational escape acts.  It is often used figuratively to describe someone who is skilled at escaping from difficult or dangerous situations. | He's a real Houdini when it comes to getting out of trouble. 他真是个逃脱大师，总能摆脱困境。 | synonyms: escapologist, magician | antonyms: captive, prisoner | noun
