archipelagic:/ˌɑːrkɪpəˈlædʒɪk/ | adj. |群岛的；多岛屿的|Relating to or consisting of an archipelago; having many islands.|例句：Indonesia is an archipelagic nation. 印度尼西亚是一个群岛国家。

例句：The archipelagic waters are rich in marine life.  这些群岛水域海洋生物丰富。


近义词：insular, island |反义词:continental|词性:adjective

词源：From French *archipélagique*, from *archipel* (“archipelago”) + *-ique* (English *-ic*).  Ultimately from Greek *ἄρχι-* (*arkhi-*, “chief, main”) + *πέλαγος* (*pélagos*, “sea”).
