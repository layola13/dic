cushiony:/ˈkʊʃəni/ | adj. | 垫子似的，柔软舒适的 | Like a cushion; soft and comfortable. |  The sofa was wonderfully cushiony and inviting. 沙发非常柔软舒适，很吸引人。 | The bed had a wonderfully cushiony mattress. 床垫非常柔软舒适。 | 近义词: soft, plush, comfy, cozy | 反义词: hard, firm, stiff | 词性: adjective

词源: From cushion + -y.  From Middle English *cuschyn, from Old French coissin, coussin, from Vulgar Latin *coxinum, from Latin coxa (“hip”).
