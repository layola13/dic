eluvium:/ɪˈluːviəm/| n.| 冲积物，残积土| Material resulting from in-situ weathering, consisting of the finer particles remaining after the coarser particles have been removed by erosion.| The eluvium at the base of the cliff was rich in minerals.  悬崖底部的残积土富含矿物质。| The archaeologist carefully sifted through the eluvium, searching for artifacts. 考古学家仔细筛选残积土，寻找文物。| synonyms:  weathering product, residual soil, regolith | antonyms: alluvium, colluvium| noun
