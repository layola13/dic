antielitisms:/ˌæntiɪˈliːtɪzəmz/| n. |反精英主义|Sentiments or actions opposing or hostile to elites, especially in politics or culture.  It often involves a distrust of established institutions and those in positions of power.|The candidate's populist rhetoric fueled antielitisms among voters.  这位候选人的民粹主义言论激起了选民的反精英主义情绪。

The rise of antielitisms can be attributed to a variety of factors. 反精英主义的兴起可以归因于多种因素。|近义词：populism, anti-establishment sentiment|反义词: elitism|词性:noun
