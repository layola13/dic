nonbeneficialnesses:/ˌnɒnbɛnɪˈfɪʃəlɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
无益处；无益性；无益之处
lack of beneficial qualities or effects; the state of being not beneficial.
The nonbeneficialnesses of the new policy were quickly apparent. 这项新政策的弊端很快就显现出来了。
non-benefit, detriment, disadvantage
benefit, advantage
n.
