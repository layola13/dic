advocacy: /ˈædvəkəsi/ | n. | 倡导，拥护，辩护 | public support for or recommendation of a particular cause or policy. |  She is known for her advocacy of human rights. 她以倡导人权而闻名。

The organization's advocacy for environmental protection has led to significant policy changes.  该组织对环境保护的倡导导致了重大的政策变化。

近义词: support, promotion, defense, championship
反义词: opposition, resistance, objection
词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *advocatia, from Latin advocatus (“called to one's aid,” past participle of advocare), from ad- (“to, toward”) + vocare (“to call”).
