erythrosin:/ɪˈrɪθrəsɪn/ /ɛˈrɪθrəsɪn/ | n. |赤藓红; 荧光素(钠)|A cherry-pink to yellowish-red dye used in foods, drugs, and cosmetics, and as a biological stain.|例句：Erythrosin is used as a food coloring. 赤藓红用作食用色素。

例句：The scientist used erythrosin to stain the cells for microscopy.  科学家使用赤藓红对细胞进行染色以便显微镜观察。|近义词：iodeosin, phloxine|反义词: N/A |词性:noun

词源: From Ancient Greek ἐρυθρός (eruthrós, “red”) + -sin, a common chemical suffix.
