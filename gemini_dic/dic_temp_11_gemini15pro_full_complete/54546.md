dislimb:/dɪsˈlɪm/
verb
肢解, 割断(动物)的四肢
To tear the limbs from; to dismember.
The butcher dislimbed the carcass. 屠夫肢解了动物的尸体。

The serial killer dislimbed his victims. 连环杀手肢解了他的受害者。
dismember, mutilate
n/a
