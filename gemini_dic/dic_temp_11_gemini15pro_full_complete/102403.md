jacal:/həˈkæl/ | n. |棚屋，简陋的小屋| A small, roughly built hut, typically with a thatched roof. |  The jacal provided minimal shelter from the elements. 这个棚屋只能提供极少的遮风挡雨的庇护。

|  They built a jacal near the river for the summer. 他们在河边建了一个棚屋以度过夏天。

| 近义词: hut, shack, cabin | 反义词: mansion, palace | 词性: noun
