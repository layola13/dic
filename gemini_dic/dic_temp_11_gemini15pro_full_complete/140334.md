oh-so:/ˌoʊ ˈsoʊ/ | adj. | 非常；极其；太…了 |  Used to emphasize how great or extreme a quality is, often in a slightly ironic or critical way.  It combines "oh" (an exclamation) and "so" (an intensifier). |  She gave him an oh-so-sweet smile. 她给了他一个甜得腻人的微笑。 |  extremely, very, exceedingly | mildly, slightly | adjective

oh-so:/ˌoʊ ˈsoʊ/ | adv. | 非常；极其；太…了 | Used as an adverb to intensify an adjective. | The restaurant was oh-so-chic. 这家餐厅非常别致。| extremely, very, exceedingly | mildly, slightly | adverb
