morphonomy:/mɔːrˈfɒnəmi/ | n. | 形态命名学 | The study of the rules governing the formation of words, especially the relationship between form and meaning.  It's a branch of linguistics that deals with the internal structure of words and how they are formed. |  The professor's lecture on morphonomy explained how prefixes and suffixes contribute to word meaning. 教授关于形态命名学的讲座解释了前缀和后缀如何构成单词含义。 |  morphology, word formation | N/A | noun
