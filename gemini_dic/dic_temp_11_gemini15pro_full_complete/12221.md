asbestic: /æsˈbɛstɪk/
adjective
石棉的；含石棉的
Relating to, containing, or resembling asbestos.
Asbestic materials were commonly used in building insulation.  石棉材料普遍用于建筑隔热。
The old house contained asbestic tiles. 这栋老房子包含石棉瓦片。
Synonyms: asbestos-containing, asbestos-based
Antonyms: asbestos-free
词性: adjective
