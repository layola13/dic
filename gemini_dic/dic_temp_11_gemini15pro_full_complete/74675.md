freebooting:/ˈfriːbuːtɪŋ/
n.
掠夺;海盗行为;非法复制
The act of piracy or plundering, especially at sea.  Also, the illegal copying and distribution of copyrighted material.
The software company took legal action against websites engaging in freebooting of their products.  这家软件公司对从事其产品盗版的网站采取了法律行动。

piracy, plundering, looting, bootlegging, copyright infringement
legitimate business, authorized distribution
