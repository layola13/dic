embalm:/ɪmˈbɑːm/ /ɪmˈbɔːm/| v. |防腐，保存（尸体）|To treat a dead body with preservatives in order to prevent decay.|The body was embalmed and placed in a coffin. 尸体经过防腐处理后被放入棺材。|例句：The ancient Egyptians embalmed their pharaohs. 古埃及人对他们的法老进行防腐处理。

例句：His body was embalmed and shipped back home. 他的遗体经过防腐处理后被运回家乡。|近义词：preserve, mummify|反义词: decompose, decay|词性:动词
