lutes:/luːts/| n. | 鲁特琴（复数）| A stringed instrument having a pear-shaped body and a fretted fingerboard, typically played by plucking. |例句：The lutes were beautifully crafted with intricate inlays. 这些鲁特琴制作精美，镶嵌工艺复杂。

例句：He inherited a collection of antique lutes from his grandfather. 他从祖父那里继承了一批古董鲁特琴。|近义词：stringed instruments, plucked instruments|反义词: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *luth, from Arabic العود (al-ʿūd, “the wood”), referring to the wood used to make the instrument.  Ultimately from Aramaic  עוּדָא (ʿūdā).
