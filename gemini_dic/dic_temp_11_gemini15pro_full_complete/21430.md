blowen:/ˈbloʊən/| adj.|开花的，盛开的；（金属）膨胀的；气喘吁吁的|A past participle of "blow," primarily used in dialects or archaic language, meaning "in bloom" or "swollen."  It can also describe someone out of breath.|The meadow was full of blowen buttercups. 草地上开满了盛开的毛茛花。|近义词：blooming, blossomed, flowering (for plants); swollen, inflated (for other objects); breathless, winded (for people) |反义词：wilted, withered (for plants); deflated (for objects)

注意事项:
