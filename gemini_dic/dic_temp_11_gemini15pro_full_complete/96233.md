indias:/ˈɪndiəz/| n. |印度（复数，罕用，通常用India）|A plural form of India, rarely used.  The standard plural is India when referring to multiple political entities or instances related to India. |Example:  The indias of the past were very different. (This usage is uncommon and might sound awkward.  Better: The India of the past was very different.)|近义词:  (None, as 'indias' is non-standard) |反义词: (None)| noun
