a-plenty:/əˈplɛnti/| adj.|大量的，充足的|A large quantity; more than enough.  It emphasizes abundance, often in an informal or literary style.|例句：There were apples a-plenty in the orchard. 果园里苹果多的很。|近义词：abundant, plentiful, copious, ample|反义词:scarce, meager, insufficient|词性:形容词
词源: From "a" (meaning "on" or "in") + "plenty" (meaning "fullness or abundance").  The "a-" prefix is an archaic form, giving the word a slightly old-fashioned feel.
