araroba:/ˌærəˈroʊbə/ | n. | 南美粉树, 戈奥粉, 柯桠粉 | A brownish-red crystalline substance obtained from the heartwood of a South American tree (Andira araroba), used medicinally as a fungicide and in the treatment of psoriasis and other skin diseases.  It is the source of chrysarobin. |  Araroba powder is effective in treating certain skin conditions.  南美粉树粉末可有效治疗某些皮肤疾病。| Synonyms:  Goa powder, Bahia powder, chrysarobin | Antonyms: None | noun
