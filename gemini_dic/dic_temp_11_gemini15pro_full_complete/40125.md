contemplatingly:/ˈkɒntəmpleɪtɪŋli/ /ˈkɑːntəmpleɪtɪŋli/
adverb
沉思地，若有所思地
in a contemplating manner; thoughtfully
She gazed contemplatingly out the window. 她若有所思地凝视着窗外。
He stroked his chin contemplatingly. 他若有所思地抚摸着下巴。
近义词: thoughtfully, pensively, meditatively, reflectively
反义词: thoughtlessly, unthinkingly, impulsively
词性: adverb

词源: From contemplate + -ing + -ly.  Contemplate comes from Latin contemplatus, past participle of contemplari "to gaze attentively, observe, contemplate," originally "to mark out a space for observation (of auguries)," from com- "together" + templum "temple, consecrated place."
