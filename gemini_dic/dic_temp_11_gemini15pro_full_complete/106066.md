kneippism:/ˈknaɪpɪzəm/
n.
水疗法;克奈普疗法
A system of naturopathic medicine based on the therapeutic use of water, including hot and cold baths, herbal remedies, and exercise.
Kneippism emphasizes the body's ability to heal itself through natural methods. 水疗法强调身体通过自然方法治愈自身的能力。
She practices kneippism to improve her circulation. 她采用水疗法来改善血液循环。
Synonym: hydrotherapy, naturopathy
Antonym: allopathy
