coombe:/kuːm/| n.|深谷，狭窄的山谷|A deep, narrow valley, especially one with wooded sides.|We walked through the shady coombe, enjoying the cool air. 我们穿过荫蔽的山谷，享受着凉爽的空气。|近义词：valley, dell, ravine|反义词:peak, summit|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cumb, from Proto-Germanic *kumbaz "valley, bowl," from Proto-Indo-European *kemb- "to bend, curve." Cognate with Welsh cwm "valley."
