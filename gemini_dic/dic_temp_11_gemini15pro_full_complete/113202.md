lurking:/ˈlɜːrkɪŋ/| adj. v.|潜伏的，暗藏的；潜伏，埋伏|being present or nearby without being noticed or making oneself known; to wait somewhere secretly so that you can see or hear what happens|The crocodile was lurking beneath the surface of the water. 鳄鱼潜伏在水面之下。|Synonyms: hiding, concealed, prowling, skulking|Antonyms: apparent, obvious, visible|verb

lurking:/ˈlɜːrkɪŋ/| adj.|潜伏的，暗藏的|existing but not yet active or obvious|There is a lurking danger in this plan. 这个计划中存在着潜在的危险。|Synonyms: latent, dormant, underlying, hidden|Antonyms: overt, manifest, apparent|adjective
