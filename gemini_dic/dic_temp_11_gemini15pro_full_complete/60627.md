eldorado:/ˌɛldəˈrɑːdoʊ/ /ˌɛldəˈreɪdoʊ/
n.|黄金国，理想中的富饶之地|A place of great wealth or opportunity.|He believed Silicon Valley was his Eldorado. 他相信硅谷是他的黄金国。|近义词：utopia, paradise, promised land|反义词：wasteland, dystopia|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *El Dorado* "the gilded one", originally referring to a mythical tribal chief covered in gold dust.  Later used to describe the legendary city or country of gold sought by the Spanish in South America.
