empurple:/ɪmˈpɜːrpl/| v. |使成紫色，使紫红|To make something purple or purplish in color.|The setting sun empurpled the clouds. 落日把云染成了紫色。|近义词：purple, redden, color|反义词:bleach, whiten|词性:动词

词源:  From em- (meaning "to cause to be") + purple.  The prefix *em-* is a variant of *en-*, which comes from French and ultimately from Latin *in-*.
