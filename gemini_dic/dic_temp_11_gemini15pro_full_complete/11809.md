arrasene:/ˌærəˈsiːn/ | n. | 金属线; 金属线绣花; | A type of embroidery made with metallic threads, or the threads themselves. | Example 1:  The antique dress was adorned with delicate arrasene embroidery. 这件古董连衣裙上装饰着精致的金属线刺绣。

Example 2:  She used gold arrasene to create a shimmering effect on the cushion. 她用金色的金属线在垫子上绣出了闪闪发光的效果。 | Near synonyms: metallic thread, embroidery thread | Antonyms: plain thread | Noun
