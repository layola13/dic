grebe:/ɡriːb/| n.|鸊鷉|Any of various diving birds of the family Podicipedidae, with lobed toes, short wings, and a rudimentary tail.|We saw a grebe diving for fish in the lake. 我们看到一只鸊鷉在湖里潜水捕鱼。|近义词：diver, dabchick|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑飞行能力强的鸟类) eagle, hawk|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *grèbe*, of uncertain origin, perhaps from a Romance form of Frankish *grip* (“griffin, vulture”).
