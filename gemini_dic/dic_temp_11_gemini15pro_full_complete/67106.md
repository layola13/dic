exploring:/ɪkˈsplɔːrɪŋ/
v. |探索，探险，探究|The act of searching, investigating, or traveling through an unfamiliar area or subject.
|例句：They are exploring new ways to treat cancer. 他们正在探索治疗癌症的新方法。

|例句：The team is exploring the Amazon rainforest.  这个团队正在探索亚马逊雨林。

|近义词：investigating, researching, searching, traversing
|反义词：ignoring, neglecting
|词性：动词(present participle)

exploring:/ɪkˈsplɔːrɪŋ/
n.|探索，探险|The activity of exploring.
|例句：The exploring of space is an ongoing endeavor.  太空探索是一项持续进行的事业。

|例句：His exploring led him to discover a hidden cave. 他的探索使他发现了一个隐藏的洞穴。

|近义词：investigation, research, search, traversal
|反义词：neglect
|词性：名词(gerund)

词源: From the Old French *esplorer*, from Latin *explorare* "search out, investigate," from *ex- "out" + *plorare* "to cry out, wail, weep, complain; to explore."  The original sense was perhaps "to scout out by calling."
