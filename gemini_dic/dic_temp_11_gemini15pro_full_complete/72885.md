follow-throughs:/ˈfɑːloʊ θruːz/
noun
完成；后续行动；(击球后的)随挥动作
the act of continuing an action to its completion
He demonstrated excellent follow-through on his golf swing. 他在高尔夫挥杆中展现了出色的随挥动作。
The project failed due to a lack of follow-through.  这个项目由于缺乏后续行动而失败了。
completion, execution, implementation
inaction, negligence
n.
