deejay: /ˈdiːdʒeɪ/ (US), /ˈdiːdʒeɪ/ (UK) | n. vt.|  唱片骑师，播放音乐的人; 担任唱片骑师|  A person who plays recorded music for an audience, typically at a nightclub or party; to work as a disc jockey. |例句：The deejay played a great mix of music at the party.  派对上，唱片骑师播放了一系列很棒的音乐。

|例句：He deejayed at a local club every weekend. 他每个周末都在当地一家俱乐部担任唱片骑师。|近义词：DJ, disc jockey| 反义词: None|词性:名词, 动词
