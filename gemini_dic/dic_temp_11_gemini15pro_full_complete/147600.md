overplausible: /ˌoʊvərˈplɔːzəbl/ | adj. | 过于貌似有理的，貌似可信的 | Appearing plausible but actually unconvincing because it is too elaborate or perfect.  Seeming reasonable or probable on the surface, but too contrived or lacking in naturalness to be genuinely believable. |  His explanation was overplausible, making me suspect he was hiding something. 他的解释过于貌似有理，让我怀疑他隐瞒了什么。

His alibi was so overplausible that the police became suspicious. 他的不在场证明过于完美，以至于警方起了疑心。

| 近义词: too convincing, contrived, artificial, specious, far-fetched | 反义词: implausible, unbelievable, unconvincing, doubtful | 词性: adjective
