clubhouses:/ˈklʌbhaʊzɪz/
n.
俱乐部会所；会馆
A place where members of a club can gather for social events and activities.  A physical or virtual space for a group with shared interests.
The clubhouses at the golf course are beautifully maintained.  高尔夫球场的俱乐部会所维护得很好。
New social media platforms like Clubhouse offer virtual clubhouses for discussions.  像Clubhouse这样的新型社交媒体平台为讨论提供了虚拟的俱乐部会所。
近义词:  club, meeting place, venue, forums (especially for online clubhouses)
反义词:  None (A direct opposite doesn't exist.  One might consider "home" or "private residence" as contrasting locations, but not true antonyms.)
词性: noun (plural)


clubhouse:/ˈklʌbhaʊs/
n.
俱乐部会所；会馆
A place where members of a club can gather for social events and activities. A physical or virtual space for a group with shared interests.
The clubhouse is the heart of the community.  俱乐部会所是社区的核心。
The baseball team gathered in the clubhouse after the game. 棒球队赛后聚集在俱乐部会所。
近义词: club, meeting place, venue, forum (especially for online clubhouses)
反义词: None (A direct opposite doesn't exist. One might consider "home" or "private residence" as contrasting locations, but not true antonyms.)
词性: noun (singular)

词源:  "Club" (from 17th-century British slang, denoting a group of people) + "house" (a building).
