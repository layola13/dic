civet:/ˈsɪvɪt/ | n. | 灵猫；灵猫香 |A slender nocturnal carnivorous mammal of tropical Asia and Africa, having a long body, short legs, and a bushy tail. It secretes a musky fluid used in perfumery. | Civets are known for their distinctive scent. 灵猫以其独特的气味而闻名。| The civet's musk is highly valued in some cultures.  灵猫香在某些文化中非常珍贵。 | mongoose, genet |  (No direct antonym) | noun
