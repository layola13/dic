agrope:/əˈɡroʊp/ | adj. | 惊恐的，吓呆的 |  Feeling stunned or bewildered, as if struck with terror; terrified. |  He stood agrope, unable to speak after witnessing the accident.  他目睹了事故后吓得呆若木鸡，说不出话来。| 近义词: terrified, horrified, stunned, bewildered, aghast | 反义词: calm, composed, unfazed | 词性: adjective

词源:  From a- (meaning "on" or "in a state of") + grope (meaning "to feel about blindly or uncertainly").  源自 a-（表示“在...状态”）+ grope（表示“盲目或不确定地摸索”）。
