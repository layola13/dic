arsesmart:/ˈɑːrsˌsmɑːrt/| n.|辣蓼, 水蓼, 聪明鬼 (贬义)|A common weed (Polygonum hydropiper) with a pungent, peppery flavor.  Also used figuratively to describe someone who thinks they are clever, often in a derogatory way.|例句：The arsesmart grew thick along the riverbank. 河岸边长满了辣蓼。

例句：He called her an arsesmart after she pointed out his mistake. 她指出他的错误后，他叫她聪明鬼（贬义）。|近义词：smart alec, wise guy, know-it-all|反义词:fool, idiot|词性:noun
