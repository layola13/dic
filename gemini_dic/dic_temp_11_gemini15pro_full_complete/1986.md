actorly:/ˈæktərli/
adjective
演员似的；做作的；夸张的
Relating to or characteristic of an actor; theatrical.  Often used to describe behavior that seems artificial or exaggerated, as if performed for an audience.
He delivered his lines with an actorly flourish. 他以演员般的夸张姿态说出了他的台词。
近义词: theatrical, dramatic, histrionic, affected
反义词: natural, unaffected, genuine
词性: adjective
