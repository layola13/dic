heterogeneous:/ˌhetərəˈdʒiːniəs/| adj.|异质的，由不同成分组成的|Consisting of dissimilar or diverse ingredients or constituents.|The population of the city is quite heterogeneous, with people from all over the world.|近义词：diverse, varied, mixed, composite|反义词：homogeneous, uniform|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the Greek "heterogenēs," from "heteros" (other, different) + "genos" (kind, race).
