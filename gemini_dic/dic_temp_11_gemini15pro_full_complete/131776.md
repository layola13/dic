nonconnectively:/ˌnɑːnkəˈnɛktɪvli/
adverb
非连接地，不连贯地
in a way that is not connected or continuous
The ideas were presented nonconnectively, making it difficult to follow the argument.  这些想法的提出缺乏连贯性，很难理解论证的过程。
non-sequentially, disjointedly
connectively, coherently
adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + connective + -ly.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not". Connective relates to connection, from Latin connectere "to bind together."  The suffix -ly forms adverbs.
