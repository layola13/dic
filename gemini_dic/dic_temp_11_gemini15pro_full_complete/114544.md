malapterurus:/ˌmæləpˈtɛrərəs/ | n. |电鲶|A genus of African freshwater fish capable of producing electric shocks.|The malapterurus can stun its prey with a powerful electric discharge. 电鲶可以用强大的电流击晕猎物。|近义词:electric catfish|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-electric fish)|词性:noun
