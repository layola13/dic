cymric:/ˈkɪmrɪk, ˈkʌmrɪk/ |adj. n.|威尔士的；威尔士人（或语）的；金吉拉长毛猫|Relating to Wales, or the Welsh language or people. A longhaired breed of cat similar to a Persian, bred to have a silver coat tipped with black.| The cymric cat has a dense, luxurious coat.  金吉拉长毛猫有着浓密而华丽的皮毛。|Synonyms: Welsh (adj.), Cambrian (adj.), Cymry (n. - plural)|Antonyms: English (adj. - in some contexts), non-Welsh (adj.)|adjective, noun
