neo-hellenic:/ˌniːoʊˈhɛlɪnɪk/
adjective
现代希腊的；新希腊语的
Relating to Modern Greek language and culture, specifically the period after the Byzantine era.
The neo-Hellenic state emerged after the Greek War of Independence. 现代希腊国家在希腊独立战争后出现。
Modern Greek literature often explores themes of neo-Hellenic identity.  现代希腊文学经常探索新希腊身份的主题。
