incontrovertibility:/ɪnˌkɑːntrəvɜːrtəˈbɪləti/
|n.|不可争议性；不容置疑|The quality of being incontrovertible; not able to be denied or disputed.|The incontrovertibility of the evidence led to a swift conviction.  证据的不可争议性导致了迅速定罪。|Synonyms:  indisputability, irrefutability, undeniable, unquestionable|Antonyms: disputability, uncertainty, doubtfulness|noun
