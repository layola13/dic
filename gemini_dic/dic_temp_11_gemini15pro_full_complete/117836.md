melliloquent:/məˈlɪləkwənt/
adj.
流利甜美的；口齿伶俐的；雄辩的
Having a smooth, sweet flow of language; fluent and eloquent.
Her melliloquent voice captivated the audience. 她甜美流畅的声音迷住了观众。
Synonyms: eloquent, articulate, fluent, silver-tongued
Antonyms: inarticulate, tongue-tied, halting

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *melliloquus* (honey-speaking), from *mel* (honey) + *loqui* (to speak).
