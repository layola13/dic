firsttime:/ˈfɜːrst taɪm/ (British), /ˈfɝːst taɪm/ (American)| n., adv.|第一次；初次|The first time I saw her, I knew she was special. |This is my first time in New York.  It's a very exciting experience.|近义词：initial time, maiden voyage (for a journey), debut (for a performance)|反义词: last time, final time|词性:名词, 副词

Etymology:  From Old English "fyrst" (first) and "tima" (time).
