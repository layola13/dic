conceptualizes:/kənˈsɛptʃuəˌlaɪzɪz/
v.
概念化；形成概念
forms a concept or idea of something.
She conceptualizes the problem as a conflict between two opposing forces. 她将这个问题概念化为了两股对立力量之间的冲突。
conceptualize, conceive, envision, imagine
