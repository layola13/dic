bogwood:/ˈbɒɡwʊd/ (UK) /ˈbɑːɡwʊd/ (US)| n. |沼泽木，泥炭木|Wood that has been preserved in a peat bog, often acquiring a dark color.  It is often used in aquariums and terrariums.|The bogwood in the aquarium provided a natural hiding place for the fish.  水族箱里的沼泽木为鱼提供了一个天然的藏身之处。|Synonyms: driftwood,  swampwood|Antonyms:  fresh wood, green wood|noun
