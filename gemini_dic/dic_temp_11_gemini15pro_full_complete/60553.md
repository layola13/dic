elapsed:/ɪˈlæpst/| adj.| 过去的，逝去的，流逝的 | past (time): having passed or gone by | Three years have elapsed since he left.  他离开已经三年了。 | synonyms: passed, gone by, bygone, expired | antonyms: future, upcoming | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *elapsus*, past participle of *elabi* "to slip away," from *e-* "out" + *labi* "to slip, glide."
