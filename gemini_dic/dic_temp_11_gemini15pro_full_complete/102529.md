jadedness:/ˈdʒeɪdɪdnəs/| n.|疲倦，厌倦，腻烦|The state of being tired, bored, or lacking enthusiasm, especially as a result of having had too much of something.|His jadedness with city life led him to move to the countryside.  他对城市生活感到厌倦，于是搬到了乡下。|近义词：weariness, exhaustion, cynicism, apathy|反义词：enthusiasm, eagerness, freshness|词性:名词

词源: From jade (v.) + -ness.  The verb "jade" originally meant "to tire or wear out a horse."  This meaning was later extended to people, referring to a state of being worn out or tired, especially from overindulgence or excess.
