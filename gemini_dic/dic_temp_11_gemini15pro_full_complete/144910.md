over-subtle:/ˈoʊvərˈsʌt(ə)l/| adj.| 过于微妙的；过于精细的；过分巧妙的| Too subtle; excessively refined or ingenious. over-subtle:/ˈoʊvərˈsʌt(ə)l/| adj.| 过于细致的；过分讲究的|  Marked by or requiring mental acuteness or ingenuity.| The over-subtle plot twists made the movie confusing. 过于细致的情节转折让这部电影变得令人困惑。| synonyms: intricate, complex, convoluted | antonyms: simple, straightforward, clear| adjective
