ineffervescent:/ˌɪnɪfərˈvɛsənt/| adj.|沉闷的，无生气的，不兴奋的|Lacking effervescence; not bubbly or lively.  Not showing enthusiasm or excitement.|Her ineffervescent personality made her seem dull and uninteresting. 她沉闷的性格让她显得枯燥无味。|Synonyms: dull, flat, lifeless, vapid, insipid|Antonyms: effervescent, lively, bubbly, sparkling, dynamic|adjective

ineffervescent:/ˌɪnɪfərˈvɛsənt/| adj.|平淡的，无味的|Lacking flavor or zest.|The champagne had gone flat and was now ineffervescent.  这香槟已经走了气，现在变得平淡无味了。|Synonyms: flat, stale, insipid|Antonyms: flavorful, zesty, sparkling|adjective
