approaching:/əˈproʊtʃɪŋ/ (US), /əˈprəʊtʃɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |接近，靠近|the act of coming nearer|The approaching of the deadline caused a flurry of activity. 截止日期的临近引起了一阵忙乱。|advance, arrival, nearing| retreat, withdrawal|noun
