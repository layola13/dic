meltingness:/ˈmɛltɪŋnɪs/ | n. | 熔化; 融化性; 溶解性 | The quality or state of being melted or readily melted; the tendency to melt. | The meltingness of the butter in the hot pan was apparent.  黄油在热锅里的融化性很明显。| Synonyms: fusibility, liquefaction, meltability | Antonyms: solidity, firmness, hardness | noun
