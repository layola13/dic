criminalised:/ˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzd/ | v. |使…成为犯罪；定…为非法 |To make something illegal or treat something as a crime. |例句：They criminalised drug use. 他们将吸毒定为犯罪。|例句：The government has criminalised certain forms of protest. 政府已将某些形式的抗议定为非法。 |近义词：proscribe, outlaw, ban, prohibit |反义词: legalize, decriminalize |词性:动词
