nonvirtues:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːtʃuːz/
n.
缺点，非美德
lack of virtues; bad qualities or habits.
Her nonvirtues include laziness and a quick temper. 她的缺点包括懒惰和脾气暴躁。
Synonyms: vices, flaws, shortcomings, defects, failings
Antonyms: virtues, merits, strengths, assets
词性: 名词
