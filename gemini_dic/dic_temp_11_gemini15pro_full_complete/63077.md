ensample:/ɪnˈsæmpl/ | n. |榜样，例子，样本|An example or model, especially one that is intended to be imitated. |The teacher used the student's essay as an ensample for the class. 老师用这个学生的文章作为全班的范例。|Synonyms: example, model, paradigm, instance, specimen |Antonyms: warning, caution |noun

ensample:/ɪnˈsæmpl/ | n. |警告，鉴戒（古语）|A warning or cautionary example. |The king's fate served as an ensample to ambitious nobles. 国王的命运对野心勃勃的贵族来说是一个警示。|Synonyms: warning, deterrent, cautionary tale |Antonyms: encouragement, incentive |noun
