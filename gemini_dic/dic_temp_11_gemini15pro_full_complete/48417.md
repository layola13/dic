deducement:/dɪˈdjuːsmənt/
n.
推论；推断；推断的结果
The act or process of deducing; something deduced; an inference.
From the evidence, the deducement was that the suspect was guilty. 从证据来看，推断结果是嫌疑人有罪。
deduction, inference, conclusion
induction
