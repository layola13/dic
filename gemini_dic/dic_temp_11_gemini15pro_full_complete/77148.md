gashed:/ɡæʃt/| v. | 划伤，割破 |  to make a long deep cut in something | The knife slipped and gashed his hand. 刀子滑了一下，划破了他的手。| 近义词：cut, lacerate, slash, slit | 反义词：heal, mend | 词性: 动词

gashed:/ɡæʃt/| adj. | 被划伤的，有深切口的 | having a long deep cut | a gashed arm 一条被划伤的胳膊 | 近义词：cut, lacerated, slashed, slit | 反义词：unblemished, intact | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old French *gaschier, frequentative of Old North French *gacher, from Frankish *gas, related to Old High German *gessōn (German giessen, "to pour").
