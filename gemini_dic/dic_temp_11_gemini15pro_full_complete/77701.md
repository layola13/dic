geminiflorous:/ˌdʒɛmɪnɪˈflɔːrəs/
adjective
成对开花的，双生花的
Bearing flowers in pairs.  Developing or arranged in pairs, as flowers or leaves.
The geminiflorous plant produced a beautiful display of paired blossoms.  这种成对开花的植物开出了一对对美丽的花朵。
The botanist identified the rare orchid as a geminiflorous species.  植物学家将这种稀有的兰花鉴定为双生花品种。
近义词: paired, twin-flowered
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "solitary-flowered")
词性: adjective
