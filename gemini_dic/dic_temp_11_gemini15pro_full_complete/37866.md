compiled:/kəmˈpaɪld/ | adj. |  编译的，汇编的 |  Put together from various sources, especially into a list or book. Created by combining several things. | The report was compiled from a survey of 500 people. 这份报告是从对500人的调查中汇编而成的。 | collected, assembled, gathered | original, separate | adjective
