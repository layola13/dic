beekeepers:/ˈbiːˌkiːpərz/
n.
养蜂人
A person who keeps bees, especially for the production of honey.
The beekeepers extracted the honey from the hives. 养蜂人从蜂箱中提取蜂蜜。

The beekeepers were busy tending to their hives. 养蜂人正忙着照料他们的蜂箱。


近义词:apiarist, apiculturist
反义词:None
词性:noun
