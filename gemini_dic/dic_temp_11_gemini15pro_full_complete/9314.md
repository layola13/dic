antipodes:/ænˈtɪpəˌdiːz/
n.
对跖点；正相反的事物
Places on opposite sides of the earth.  Also used figuratively to describe opposite extremes.
例句：
Australia and New Zealand are often referred to as the antipodes of Europe.
澳大利亚和新西兰常被称为欧洲的对跖点。

例句：
His views are the antipodes of mine.
他的观点与我的截然相反。

近义词：opposite, contrary
反义词：same, similar
词性：名词


antipodean:/ˌæn.tɪ.pəˈdiː.ən/
adj.
对跖的；正相反的
Relating to the antipodes; diametrically opposite.
例句：
The antipodean seasons mean Christmas is in the summer there.
对跖的季节意味着那里的圣诞节在夏天。

例句：
He has an antipodean view of the world compared to mine.
与我相比，他对世界的看法截然相反。

近义词：opposite, contrary
反义词：same, similar
词性：形容词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *antipodes, from Greek antipodes (plural),  from anti- 'opposite' + pous 'foot'.  Literally "having opposite feet."
