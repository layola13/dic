flummoxing: /ˈflʌməksɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人困惑的，令人慌乱的 |  Extremely confusing and bewildering, causing someone to be unable to think clearly. | The flummoxing question left the student speechless. 令人困惑的问题让学生哑口无言。|  The instructions were so flummoxing that I couldn't assemble the furniture.  说明书太令人困惑了，我无法组装家具。 | confusing, perplexing, bewildering, baffling | clear, straightforward | adjective

flummox: /ˈflʌməks/ | v. | 使困惑，使慌乱 | To confuse or bewilder someone greatly. | The sudden change in plans completely flummoxed me.  计划的突然改变让我完全不知所措。 |  The complex math problem flummoxed even the smartest students. 这道复杂的数学题甚至难倒了最聪明的学生。 | confuse, bewilder, perplex, baffle | clarify, enlighten | verb
