mouldery:/ˈmoʊldəri/ (US), /ˈməʊldəri/ (UK)| adj.|发霉的；霉烂的|Covered with or smelling of mould.  Characterized by the presence, growth, or smell of mold. |The bread was mouldery and had to be thrown away. 面包发霉了，不得不扔掉。|The old books in the basement had a mouldery smell. 地下室里的旧书有一股霉味。|近义词:musty, mildewy, fusty|反义词:fresh, clean|词性:形容词

词源:
From mould + -ery.  "Mould" itself comes from Old English mold, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *muldą, meaning "dust, loose earth."
