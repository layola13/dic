affront:/əˈfrʌnt/| n. |冒犯，侮辱；公然蔑视|An action or remark that causes outrage or offense.|His refusal to shake hands was a deliberate affront. 他拒绝握手是故意的侮辱。|近义词：insult, offense, slight|反义词：compliment, praise|noun
affront:/əˈfrʌnt/| vt. |冒犯，侮辱；面对|To offend or insult someone openly and deliberately; to face or confront boldly.|She was affronted by his rude remarks. 她被他粗鲁的言辞冒犯了。|近义词：insult, offend, provoke|反义词：respect, honor|verb
