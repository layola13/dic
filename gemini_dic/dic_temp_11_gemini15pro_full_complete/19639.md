bikh:/biːk/ | n. | 印度乌头，毒药 |  A highly poisonous plant native to the Himalayas, also known as Indian aconite or monkshood. Its roots contain a powerful toxin used in traditional medicine and as a poison.  |  The bikh plant is considered sacred in some cultures but is extremely dangerous if ingested.  印度乌头在某些文化中被认为是神圣的，但如果摄入则非常危险。 |  aconite, monkshood, wolfsbane |  (no clear antonym for a specific plant) | noun
