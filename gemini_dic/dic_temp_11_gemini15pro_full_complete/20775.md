blanketer:/ˈblæŋkɪtər/
n.|覆盖者；毛毯制造商；提供全面服务的人|A person or thing that blankets something.  A maker of blankets. Someone who provides a comprehensive service. |The snow blanketed the fields. (雪覆盖了田野). The company is a blanketed of insurance services. (这家公司提供全面的保险服务).|近义词：coverer, manufacturer|反义词: uncoverer|词性: 名词

Etymology: From the verb "to blanket" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Blanket" itself comes from Old North French "blankete," diminutive of "blanc" (white), referring to white woolen bed coverings.
