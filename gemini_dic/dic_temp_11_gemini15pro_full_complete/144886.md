over-rule:/ˌōvərˈruːl/ | vt. | 驳回；否决；统治；支配 | To reject or disallow a decision, argument, or plea. ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *overreulen*, from *over-* (over) + *reulen* (to rule).  Ultimately from Latin *regere* (to rule).
