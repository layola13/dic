denizen:/ˈdɛnɪzən/ | n. |居民，居住者；常客|A person, animal, or plant that lives or is found in a particular place; a regular visitor or inhabitant of a particular place.|例句：The denizens of the forest were startled by the loud noise. 森林里的居民被巨大的噪音吓了一跳。

例句：He was a denizen of the local pub, known to everyone by his first name. 他是当地酒吧的常客，每个人都叫他的名字。|近义词：inhabitant, resident, occupant, frequenter|反义词：foreigner, stranger, alien|词性: noun
