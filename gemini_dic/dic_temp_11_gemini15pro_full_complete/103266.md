jewishly:/ˈdʒuːɪʃli/
adv.
像犹太人一样地；从犹太人的角度；以犹太人的方式
In a manner considered typical of Jewish people; from a Jewish perspective.
She celebrated the holidays Jewishly, lighting candles and saying prayers. 她以犹太人的方式庆祝节日，点燃蜡烛并祈祷。
Near Synonyms:  in a Jewish manner, in a Jewish way, like a Jew
Antonyms:  non-Jewishly, secularly
词性: 副词
