in-the-air:/ɪn ðiː ɛər/
adj.
在空中；悬而未决；流传；不切实际的
Uncertain or unresolved; current or circulating, as an idea or rumor; impractical
The future of the project is still in the air.  这个项目的未来仍然悬而未决。
There's a feeling of excitement in the air.  空气中弥漫着兴奋的感觉。
近义词:uncertain, unresolved, pending, circulating, rumored, hypothetical
反义词:certain, resolved, decided, concrete

词性:adjective
