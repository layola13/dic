disillusionment:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənmənt/
n.
失望；醒悟；理想破灭
A feeling of disappointment resulting from the discovery that something is not as good as one believed it to be.
The election results led to widespread disillusionment among young voters. 选举结果导致年轻选民普遍失望。
disappointment, disenchantment, dismay
hope, optimism, idealism
noun
