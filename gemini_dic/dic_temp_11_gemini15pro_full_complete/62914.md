enlivener:/ɪnˈlaɪvənər/| n.| 使活跃的人或物；激励者；兴奋剂 |Something that enlivens something else. It makes it more interesting, exciting, or attractive. |The comedian was the enlivener of the party.  这位喜剧演员是聚会的活跃气氛制造者。|The bright colors were an enlivener for the dull room.  鲜艳的色彩为沉闷的房间增添了活力。|Synonyms: stimulant, energizer, invigorator|Antonyms: depressant, sedative|noun
