doze off:/doʊz ˈɔːf/ /dəʊz ˈɒf/| vi. | 打瞌睡；不知不觉睡着 | To fall into a light sleep, typically unintentionally. |  The lecturer was so boring that I dozed off. 演讲者太无聊了，我不知不觉睡着了。

I dozed off during the movie. 我在看电影的时候打瞌睡了。

| 近义词：nod off, drift off, drop off | 反义词: wake up, stay awake | 词性: 动词(不及物动词)
