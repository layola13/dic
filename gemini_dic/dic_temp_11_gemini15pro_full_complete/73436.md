foremast:/ˈfɔːrmæst/ |ˈfɔːrmɑːst/| n. |前桅，前帆|The foremast of the ship was damaged in the storm. 船的前桅在暴风雨中受损了。|English Explanation: The principal mast of a sailing vessel located nearest the bow.  It's the first and often tallest of the masts on a ship with more than one mast.|例句：The foremast carried the largest sails. 前桅挂着最大的帆。

The sailors climbed the foremast to get a better view. 水手们爬上前桅以获得更好的视野。


|近义词：mainmast (if it's the only or primary mast) |反义词: mizzenmast (rear mast)|词性:noun
