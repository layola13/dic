conquer:/ˈkɒŋkə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋkər/ (US)| vt. |征服，战胜，克服|To defeat or overcome by force, especially in war; to gain control of (a place or people); to overcome (a feeling or problem).|例句：
The Normans conquered England in 1066.  诺曼人于1066年征服了英格兰。

He finally conquered his fear of heights. 他终于克服了恐高症。

|近义词：defeat, overcome, vanquish, subdue, master|反义词：surrender, yield, submit, lose|词性:动词


conquer:/ˈkɒŋkə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋkər/ (US)| n. |征服，战胜|The act of conquering. (Less common as a noun)|例句：

The conquest of Everest was a remarkable feat. 征服珠穆朗玛峰是一项了不起的壮举。

The book details the Norman conquest of England. 这本书详细描述了诺曼征服英格兰的历史。


|近义词：victory, triumph|反义词：defeat, loss|词性:名词
