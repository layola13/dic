displacer:/dɪˈspleɪsər/| n.|置换器；排出器；取代者|Something that displaces something else.  Something that forces people or things to leave their home or position.|The rising water level acted as a displacer, forcing the animals to higher ground. 上升的水位就像一个置换器，迫使动物们前往更高的地方。|synonyms: ejector, expeller, supplanter|antonyms: holder, retainer|noun
