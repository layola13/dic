airplays:/ˈɛəpleɪz/| n. |播放次数;播放;空中播放;无线播放|The act of playing audio or video content wirelessly, typically from a mobile device to a compatible receiver like an Apple TV or smart speaker.  It can also refer to the number of times a song or video is played on the radio or other media. |My new song got a lot of airplays on the local radio station. 我的新歌在当地电台播放了很多次。|Synonyms: broadcasts, streams, plays|Antonyms: silences, mutes|noun
