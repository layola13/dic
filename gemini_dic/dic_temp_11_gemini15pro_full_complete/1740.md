acquiescent:/ˌækwiˈɛsənt/| adj.|默许的，顺从的，默认的|Ready to accept something without protest, or without a strong objection.|She was too acquiescent to argue with her boss. 她太顺从了，不敢与老板争论。|Synonyms: compliant, agreeable, amenable, submissive, yielding|Antonyms: resistant, defiant, objecting, uncooperative|adjective

acquiescent:/ˌækwiˈɛsənt/| adj.|默许的，顺从的，默认的|Inclined to give in without protest.|He gave an acquiescent nod. 他默许地点了点头。|Synonyms: passive, resigned, accepting|Antonyms: opposing, dissenting, protesting|adjective
