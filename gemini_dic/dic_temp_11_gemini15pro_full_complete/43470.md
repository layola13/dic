crassament:/kræsəmənt/ | n. | 凝块；血块 | The thick, coagulated part of blood, especially after clotting.  It's essentially a synonym for clot, particularly referring to the solid mass left after blood coagulates. |  The crassament from the wound was substantial.  伤口处的凝块很大。|  clot, coagulum | serum, plasma | noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *crassamentum, from Latin crassus ("thick").
