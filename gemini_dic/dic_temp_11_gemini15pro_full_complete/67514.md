extracting:/ɪkˈstræktɪŋ/ | v. | 提取，提炼，摘录 |  The process of removing or obtaining something from a source. |  They are extracting oil from shale. 他们正在从页岩中提取石油。|  removing, obtaining, deriving | inserting, implanting | verb

extracting:/ɪkˈstræktɪŋ/ | adj. | 提取的，提炼的 | Used to describe something that extracts or is related to extraction. |  The extracting process is complex. 提炼过程很复杂。 | extractive,  derivative |  synthetic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin extrahere (to draw out), from ex (out of) + trahere (to draw).
