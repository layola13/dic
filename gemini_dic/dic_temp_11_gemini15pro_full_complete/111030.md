lished:/lɪʃt/ | adj. | 光滑的，有光泽的; 抛光的 |  Having a smooth, glossy surface, often as a result of polishing.  | The car had a lished paint job. 这辆车有光滑的油漆工作。 | 近义词: polished, glossy, sleek, shiny | 反义词: dull, matte, rough | 词性:形容词

词源:  "lished" is likely a variant or misspelling of "polished."  "Polish" comes from Old French "polir," from Latin "polire," meaning "to smooth, refine."
