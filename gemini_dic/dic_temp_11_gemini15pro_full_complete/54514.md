disjudication:/dɪsˌdʒuːdɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
裁决；判决；剥夺权利
The act of depriving someone of a right or privilege by judicial decree.
The disjudication of his voting rights followed his conviction for felony.  他的投票权在他被判重罪后被剥夺。
deprivation, forfeiture, divestiture
granting, bestowal, conferment
noun
