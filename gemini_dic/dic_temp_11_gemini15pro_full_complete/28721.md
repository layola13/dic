carminic:/kɑːrˈmɪnɪk/| adj.|胭脂红的，洋红的|A vivid crimson or scarlet pigment obtained from cochineal insects.|The artist used a carminic dye for the vibrant sunset in the painting. 艺术家在画中使用了胭脂红染料来描绘鲜艳的日落。|近义词：crimson, scarlet, vermilion|反义词:pale, dull|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *carminique*, from carmin (“carmine”) + -ique. Ultimately from Arabic قِرْمِز‎ (qirmiz, “crimson, kermes”).
