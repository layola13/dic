metazoan:/ˌmɛtəˈzoʊən/ | n. | 后生动物 | An animal of the Metazoa division; a multicellular animal having cells differentiated into tissues and organs and usually a digestive cavity and nervous system. | Example 1: Sponges are among the simplest metazoans. 海绵是最简单的后生动物之一。

Example 2: The study focused on the evolution of metazoans. 这项研究的重点是后生动物的进化。 | Synonym: animal | Antonym: protozoan | noun
