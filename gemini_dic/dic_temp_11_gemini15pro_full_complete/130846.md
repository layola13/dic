nonapprehensions:/ˌnɒnˌæprɪˈhɛnʃənz/
|n.|缺乏忧虑；无所畏惧；泰然自若
|Absence of anxieties or fears; a state of being unafraid or unconcerned.|
|His nonapprehension about the future was remarkable, considering the circumstances. 考虑到当时的情况，他对未来泰然自若的态度非常引人注目。|
|近义词: calmness, composure, serenity, tranquility, fearlessness, intrepidity|
|反义词: apprehension, anxiety, fear, worry, dread, trepidation|
|词性: noun|

nonapprehension:/ˌnɒnˌæprɪˈhɛnʃən/
|n.|缺乏理解；未能理解
|Lack of comprehension or understanding; failure to grasp something.|
|His nonapprehension of the complex subject matter was evident in his questions. 他对复杂主题缺乏理解，从他的问题中可以明显看出。|
|近义词: incomprehension, misunderstanding, confusion, bewilderment|
|反义词: comprehension, understanding, grasp, apprehension|
|词性: noun|
