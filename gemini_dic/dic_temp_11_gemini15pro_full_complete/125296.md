multifunctional:/ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃənəl/
adjective
多功能的
Having or fulfilling several functions.
This new tablet is multifunctional; it can be used as a phone, a camera, and a GPS device. 这款新的平板电脑是多功能的；它可以用作手机、相机和GPS设备。
This is a multifunctional space used for meetings, presentations, and social gatherings. 这是一个多功能空间，用于会议、演示和社交聚会。

近义词:versatile, multipurpose, all-purpose
反义词:single-purpose, specialized, limited
