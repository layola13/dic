evasiveness:/ɪˈveɪsɪvnəs/
| n. |逃避，躲避，推诿|The quality of being evasive; a tendency to avoid commitment or self-revelation.  指逃避，躲避，不愿承担责任或透露自己的倾向。|His evasiveness made it difficult to get a straight answer. 他的推诿使得很难得到一个直接的答案。|近义词：avoidance, elusiveness, prevarication|反义词：directness, forthrightness, honesty|词性:名词

evasive:/ɪˈveɪsɪv/
| adj. |逃避的，躲避的，推诿的|Tending to avoid commitment or self-revelation, especially by responding only indirectly. 指逃避，躲避，不愿承担责任或透露自己的，特别是只间接回应。|He gave an evasive answer to the question. 他对这个问题给出了一个含糊其辞的答案。|近义词：elusive, shifty, cagey|反义词：direct, forthright, honest|词性:形容词

evade:/ɪˈveɪd/
| v. |逃避，躲避|Escape or avoid, especially by cleverness or trickery.  指逃避，躲避，尤指通过聪明或诡计。|He evaded the police for several weeks. 他躲避了警察好几个星期。|近义词：avoid, dodge, elude|反义词：confront, face, meet|词性:动词
