caudad:/ˈkɔːdæd/| adv. adj.|尾侧地，向尾部|Toward the tail or posterior end of the body. |The incision was made caudad to the nipple. 切口在乳头尾侧。|近义词：posterior, distal|反义词：cephalad, cranial|词性:副词，形容词
Etymology: From Latin cauda (“tail”) + -ad (suffix indicating direction toward).
