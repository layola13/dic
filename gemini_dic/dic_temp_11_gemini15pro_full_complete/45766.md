cushite:/ˈkʊʃaɪt/| n. adj.| 古实人；库施特语的|A descendant of Cush, a son of Ham in the Bible, or a member of any of the peoples traditionally held to be descended from him, especially the ancient Ethiopians.|The museum displayed artifacts from the Cushite period.|近义词：Ethiopian (in some contexts)|反义词: None|词性:noun, adjective

cushite:/ˈkʊʃaɪt/| adj.| 库施特语的|Relating to the Cushitic branch of the Afro-Asiatic language family, or to the peoples who speak these languages. |The Cushite languages are spoken across a wide area of East Africa.|近义词：Cushitic|反义词: None|词性:adjective
