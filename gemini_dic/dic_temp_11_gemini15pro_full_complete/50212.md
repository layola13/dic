departmentally:/ˌdiːpɑːrtˈmɛntəli/ | adv. |部门地，按部门| In a way that is related to or involves different departments within an organization. | The issue was handled departmentally, with each team responsible for their own area.  这个问题是由各部门分别处理的，每个团队负责自己的领域。 |  例句：The budget is allocated departmentally. 预算是按部门分配的。| 近义词：separately, individually, independently | 反义词：centrally, collectively | 词性：副词

departmentally:/ˌdiːpɑːrtˈmɛntəli/ | adv. |部门地，按部门| Relating to or managed by departments. | Decisions were made departmentally, rather than by a central authority.  决策是按部门作出的，而不是由中央机构作出的。| 例句：Information is shared departmentally. 信息在部门内部共享。| 近义词：sectionally, divisionally | 反义词：organizationally, universally | 词性：副词
