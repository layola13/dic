gladiatorial:/ˌɡlædiəˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
角斗士的；格斗的
Of or relating to gladiators or their combats.
The gladiatorial games were a popular form of entertainment in ancient Rome. 古罗马的角斗比赛是一种流行的娱乐形式。
The film depicted the gladiatorial contests with brutal realism.  这部电影以残酷的现实主义手法描绘了角斗士的比赛。

近义词: combative, pugilistic, martial
反义词: peaceful, pacifistic
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *gladiātōrius, from gladiātor (“gladiator”) + -ius (adjective suffix).
