italicising:/ɪˈtælɪsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 斜体化，用斜体印刷 |  The author italicized several words for emphasis. 作者用斜体字强调了几个词。 | Example:  She was *italicising* the important terms in her essay.  她在论文中将重要的术语斜体化。| synonyms: italicizing, emphasizing, highlighting | antonyms: de-emphasizing, downplaying | verb
