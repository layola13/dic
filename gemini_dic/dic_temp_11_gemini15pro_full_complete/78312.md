gerontes:/dʒɪˈrɒntiːz/ | n. |  长老，老年人 |  A council of elders, especially in ancient Greece.  It comes from the Greek word "gerōn" (γέρων), meaning "old man." |  The gerontes held significant power in Spartan society.  斯巴达社会的长老们掌握着重要的权力。

The gerontes advised the king on important matters.  长老们就重要的事情向国王提出建议。 | 近义词: elders, seniors, veterans | 反义词: youngsters, youth | 词性: Noun
