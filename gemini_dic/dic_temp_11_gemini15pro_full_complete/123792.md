mopa:/ˈmoʊpə/ (US), /ˈmɒpə/ (UK)| n.|拖把|A tool with a long handle and a bunch of thick strings or a sponge at the bottom, used for washing floors.|I used a mopa to clean up the spilled juice. 我用拖把清理了洒出来的果汁。|Synonyms: mop, floor cleaner|Antonyms: broom, brush|noun
