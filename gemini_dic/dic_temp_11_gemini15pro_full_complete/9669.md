antitax:/ˌæntiˈtæks/
adjective
反税收的；反对纳税的
Opposed to taxes or taxation.
The antitax movement gained momentum as the government proposed another tax increase. 反税收运动随着政府提议再次增税而获得了动力。
The candidate's antitax policies resonated with many voters.  候选人的反税收政策引起了许多选民的共鸣。

近义词:tax-resistant, tax-adverse
反义词:pro-tax

词性:adjective
