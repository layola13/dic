alertness:/əˈlɜːrtnɪs/| n.|警觉性，机敏性|The state of being watchful and prompt to meet danger or emergency.|He maintained a high level of alertness throughout the night. 他整夜保持高度警觉。|近义词：vigilance, watchfulness, awareness|反义词：inattentiveness, drowsiness, apathy|词性:名词

Etymology: From alert + -ness.  "Alert" comes from Italian "all'erta" meaning "on the watch" or "on the lookout".
