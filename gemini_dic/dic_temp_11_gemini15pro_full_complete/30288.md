注意事项:

cen 主要用于某些欧洲国家(例如比利时、荷兰等)表示货币的最小单位，在其他地区，通常使用 cent 或 centime。


While "cen" is a valid term, its usage is limited. "Cent" is much more common and widely understood.  Therefore, using "cent" in most contexts is recommended.
