de-evolution:/diːˌɛvəˈluːʃən/
noun
退化; 逆进化
The process of reverting to a less developed or less complex state, the opposite of evolution.
Some argue that reality TV represents the de-evolution of popular culture.  一些人认为真人秀电视代表了流行文化的退化。
The zoologist disagreed with the idea of de-evolution, citing other evolutionary mechanisms at play.  动物学家不同意退化的观点，引用了其他正在起作用的进化机制。
devolution, regression, deterioration
evolution, advancement, progress
n.
