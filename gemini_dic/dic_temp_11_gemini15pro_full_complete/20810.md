blasphemous:/ˈblæsfɪməs/ /ˈblæsfəməs/| adj.|亵渎神明的，渎神的|Showing disrespect toward God or sacred things.|His blasphemous remarks shocked the devout worshippers. 他亵渎神明的言论震惊了虔诚的信徒。|Example:  Burning a holy book is considered a blasphemous act.  焚烧圣书被认为是亵渎神明的行为。

|Synonyms: sacrilegious, profane, irreverent, impious|Antonyms: reverent, pious, devout|adjective
