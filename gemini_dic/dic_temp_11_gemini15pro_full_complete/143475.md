outgallops:/ˌaʊtˈɡæləps/
verb
胜过奔驰；以奔驰取胜
to gallop faster than (someone or something else)
The young horse easily outgalloped the older ones. 这匹年轻的马轻易地超过了那些老马。
近义词: outrun, outstrip, surpass
反义词: lag behind, trail
词性: verb

词源: out- + gallop.  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed."  "Gallop" comes from Old Norse *galoppa.
