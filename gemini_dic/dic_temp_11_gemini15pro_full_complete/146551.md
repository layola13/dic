overglutting:/ˌoʊvərˈɡlʌtɪŋ/
verb
过度供应，过饱和
The market is overglutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价进口商品。
The overglutting of the market caused prices to fall.  市场供过于求导致价格下跌。
oversupply, saturate, flood
undersupply, lack
