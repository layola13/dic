antiskeptic:/ˌæntiˈskɛptɪk/
| adj. n.| 抗怀疑论的；抗怀疑论者 | Opposing or resisting skepticism; one who opposes skepticism. |  He took an antiskeptic stance, arguing that certainty is attainable. 他采取了一种反怀疑论的立场，认为确定性是可以达到的。|  The philosopher presented himself as an antiskeptic, claiming we can have knowledge of the external world. 这位哲学家自称是一位反怀疑论者，声称我们可以拥有外部世界的知识。| 近义词:believer,  dogmatist | 反义词:skeptic, doubter | 词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:
From anti- + skeptic.  "Anti-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "against," "opposite of," or "opposed to." "Skeptic" comes from the Greek "skeptikos," meaning "inquiring" or "thoughtful," later evolving to mean "doubter."
