gilders:/ˈɡɪldərz/| n.|镀金工人；荷兰盾|A person who gilds; someone who coats something with a thin layer of gold.  Formerly, a Dutch monetary unit.|The gilders were hard at work preparing the frame for the painting.|近义词：goldsmiths, platers|反义词:|noun
