bovinely:/ˈboʊvaɪnli/| adv.|像牛一样地；迟钝地；愚蠢地|In a manner resembling that of cattle; sluggishly; stupidly.|He stared bovinely at the television. 他呆呆地盯着电视。|近义词：stupidly, dully, vacantly, blankly|反义词：intelligently, brightly, sharply, acutely|词性:副词
Etymology: From bovine + -ly. Bovine comes from Latin bovinus, from bos, bovis "ox, cow."
