open-mouthed:/ˌoʊpənˈmaʊðd/ /ˌəʊpənˈmaʊθt/| adj.|张大嘴巴的，目瞪口呆的|Being with one's mouth wide open, especially as an expression of surprise or wonder.|She stared open-mouthed at the astonishing sight. 她目瞪口呆地盯着那令人震惊的景象。|Synonyms: agape, aghast, astonished, dumbfounded, surprised|Antonyms: unimpressed, unfazed|adjective


open-mouthed:/ˌoʊpənˈmaʊðd/ /ˌəʊpənˈmaʊθt/| adv.|张着嘴地|With the mouth wide open.|He slept open-mouthed. 他张着嘴睡觉。|Synonyms: agape|Antonyms: closed-mouthed|adverb
