antimoralist:/ˌæntiˈmɔːrəlɪst/
n.
反道德主义者;不道德者
One who opposes or rejects the principles of morality.
He argued that the antimoralist's rejection of traditional values was leading to societal decay.  他认为反道德主义者对传统价值观的摒弃导致了社会衰败。
Synonyms: immoralist, libertine, nihilist
Antonyms: moralist, puritan
