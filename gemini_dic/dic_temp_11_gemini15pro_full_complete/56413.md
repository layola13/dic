dolioform:/ˈdɒliəfɔːm/| adj.|桶形的，似桶状的|Shaped like a barrel or cask; barrel-shaped. |The dolioform body of the insect made it easy to identify. 这种昆虫的桶状身体使其易于识别。|The wine was aged in dolioform oak barrels.  这种酒是在桶状的橡木桶中陈酿的。|近义词：barrel-shaped, cask-shaped, tun-shaped|反义词：slender, elongated|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *doliiformis*, from *dolium* (barrel, cask) + *-formis* (having the form of).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰol- (hollow).
