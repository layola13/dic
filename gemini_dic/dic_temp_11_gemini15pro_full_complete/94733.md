improvably:/ɪmˈpruːvəbli/
| adv. | 可改进地 | Capable of being improved; in a way that is capable of being improved. | The system is improvably inefficient.  这个系统效率低下，可以改进。 | The process can be improvably streamlined. 这个流程可以改进得更加精简。 | 近义词: enhanceable, betterable,  amenable | 反义词: unimprovable, fixed, immutable | 词性: 副词

词源:
From "improve" + "-able" + "-ly". "Improve" comes from Old French "emprouwer" meaning "to make a profit, to turn to one's advantage."  "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being," and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
