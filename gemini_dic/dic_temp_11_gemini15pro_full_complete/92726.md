ichnography: /ɪkˈnɒɡrəfi/ /ɪkˈnɑːɡrəfi/
n.
平面图；水平投影图
A representation of a building or other structure drawn to scale, showing the floor plan or layout.
The architect presented an ichnography of the proposed museum, clearly outlining the location of each gallery and exhibit space. 建筑师展示了拟建博物馆的平面图，清晰地勾勒出每个画廊和展览空间的位置。
The ichnography of the ancient city revealed a sophisticated network of streets and buildings. 这座古城的平面图揭示了一个复杂的街道和建筑网络。
近义词:  plan, blueprint, floor plan, ground plan
反义词: elevation, section
词性: noun
