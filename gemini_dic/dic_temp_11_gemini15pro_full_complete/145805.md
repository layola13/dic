overdecorativeness:/ˌoʊvərˌdekərəˈtɪvnəs/
n.
过度装饰; 过分装饰
Excessive or overly elaborate decoration.
The overdecorativeness of the room made it feel cluttered.  这个房间的过度装饰使它感觉很杂乱。
Her overdecorativeness in dressing sometimes borders on gaudy. 她穿衣打扮的过度装饰有时近乎俗艳。
近义词: gaudiness, ornateness, excessiveness
反义词: simplicity, minimalism, understatement
词性: noun


overdecorate:/ˌoʊvərˈdekəreɪt/
v.
过度装饰; 过分装饰
To decorate excessively or too elaborately.
Be careful not to overdecorate the cake, or it will look messy. 小心不要把蛋糕装饰得太过花哨，否则会看起来很乱。
They overdecorated their house for Christmas, covering every surface with tinsel and lights. 他们在圣诞节期间过度装饰了他们的房子，用金箔和彩灯覆盖了每一个表面。
近义词: overdo, embellish excessively
反义词: understate, simplify
词性: verb


overdecorative:/ˌoʊvərˈdekərətɪv/
adj.
过度装饰的; 过分装饰的
Excessively or overly decorated.
The overdecorative style of the furniture didn't suit the modern apartment.  家具过度装饰的风格不适合现代公寓。
I found the Christmas tree a bit overdecorative. 我觉得圣诞树装饰得有点过多了。
近义词: gaudy, ornate, excessive
反义词: simple, minimalist, understated
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively," "too much") + "decorative" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
"decorative" comes from the Late Latin "decorativus," from "decorare" (to decorate), ultimately from "decus" (ornament, grace).
