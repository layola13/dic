fens:/fɛnz/
noun
沼泽，湿地
low land covered wholly or partly with water; boggy ground; marsh.
The birdwatchers observed several rare species in the fens.  观鸟者在沼泽中观察到了几种稀有物种。
近义词:marshes, swamps, bogs
反义词: uplands, highlands
词性:n.


fens:/fɛnz/
verb (third-person singular simple present fens, present participle fensing, simple past and past participle fened)
排干沼泽地
to drain (fens or marshland).
They fened the land to create more farmland.  他们排干了沼泽地，以创造更多的农田。
近义词:drain, reclaim
反义词:flood, irrigate
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fenn, from Proto-Germanic *fanją (“mud, dirt”). Cognate with West Frisian fean (“peat”), Dutch veen (“peat, bog, fen”), German Fehn (“fen”).
