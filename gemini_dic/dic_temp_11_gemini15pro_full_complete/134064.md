nonignominious:/ˌnɒnɪɡnəˈmɪniəs/
adjective
非耻辱的，不丢脸的
Not ignominious; not involving or causing disgrace.
He retired from the company after a long and nonignominious career. 他在公司经历了漫长而光荣的职业生涯后退休了。
The team suffered a nonignominious defeat, losing by only one point. 该队虽败犹荣，仅以一分之差输掉了比赛。

近义词: respectable, honorable, creditable, reputable
反义词: ignominious, shameful, disgraceful, humiliating
词性: adjective
