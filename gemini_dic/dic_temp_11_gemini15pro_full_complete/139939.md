odometries: /ˌoʊdəˈmɛtriz/ | n. | 里程测量法；里程计读数 | The process of determining the position and orientation of a mobile robot by measuring the wheel rotations and other related parameters. It involves using data from sensors, such as wheel encoders, to estimate the robot's change in position over time. | The robot's odometries indicated it had travelled 10 meters.  机器人的里程计显示它已经行驶了10米。 | synonyms: dead reckoning,  position estimation | antonyms: N/A | noun

odometry: /ˌoʊˈdɑːmətri/ | n. | 里程测量法；里程计读数 | The use of wheel rotations to measure distance traveled. | The odometry of the car was inaccurate. 汽车的里程计读数不准确。 | synonyms: dead reckoning,  position estimation | antonyms: N/A | noun
