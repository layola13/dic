co-operation:/kəʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃn/ /koʊˌɑːpəˈreɪʃn/
n.
合作，协作
The project requires co-operation between several departments.
这个项目需要几个部门之间的合作。

Co-operation between the two countries has led to significant advancements in medical research.
两国间的合作促进了医学研究的重大进展。

Etymology: From Latin "co-" (together) + "operari" (to work).
词源：来自拉丁语 "co-"（一起） + "operari"（工作）。

Synonym: collaboration, teamwork, partnership
近义词：协作，团队合作，伙伴关系

Antonym: conflict, opposition, antagonism
反义词：冲突，反对，对抗

Word type: Noun
词性：名词
