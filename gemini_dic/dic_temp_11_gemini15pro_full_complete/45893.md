cuticular:/kjuːˈtɪkjʊlə(r)/
adjective
表皮的
of, relating to, or resembling a cuticle
例句：The cuticular layer of the leaf prevents water loss. 叶子的表皮层防止水分流失。

例句：Insects have a cuticular exoskeleton. 昆虫有一个表皮外骨骼。
近义词：epidermal, cutaneous
反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly "internal")
词性: adjective

cuticle:/ˈkjuːtɪkl/
noun
表皮
the outer layer of living tissue, in particular
例句：The cuticle of the plant protects it from harsh weather.  植物的表皮保护它免受恶劣天气的影响。

例句：Apply cuticle oil to soften and moisturize your nails.  涂抹表皮油来软化和滋润你的指甲。
近义词：epidermis
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性: noun
