indigenity:/ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛnɪti/ | n. |土著性，本土性|The state or quality of being indigenous; indigenousness.  The fact of originating or occurring naturally in a particular place. |The artwork reflects the indigenity of the region. 这件艺术品反映了该地区的本土性。|近义词：indigenousness, nativeness, aboriginality |反义词: foreignness, alienness |词性: noun
