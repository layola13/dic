inequation:/ˌɪnɪˈkwɪʒən/ | n. |不等式；不平衡|A statement that compares two quantities using inequality symbols such as < (less than), > (greater than), ≤ (less than or equal to), ≥ (greater than or equal to), or ≠ (not equal to).|The inequation 2x + 3 < 7 represents all values of x that make the expression 2x + 3 less than 7.|近义词:inequality|反义词:equation|词性:名词
