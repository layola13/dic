councillors:/ˈkaʊnsələrz/ (US) /ˈkaʊnsləz/ (UK)| n. |市议员，顾问|A member of a council, especially a local government council. |例句：The councillors voted to approve the new budget. 市议员投票通过了新的预算。

例句：The councillors debated the proposed development for hours. 市议员们就拟议的开发项目辩论了数小时。

近义词：council members, councilors, representatives|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French conseiller (“to counsel”), from Latin consiliarius (“of counsel”), from consilium (“counsel, advice, deliberation”). Ultimately from com- (“together”) + -silire (“to sit”).
