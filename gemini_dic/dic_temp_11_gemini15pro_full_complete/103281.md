jharal:/dʒəˈrɑːl/| n.|塔尔羊，鬣羚|A Himalayan goat antelope (Hemitragus jemlahicus) with long, shaggy, dark brown hair and backward-curving horns.|例句：The jharal nimbly navigated the steep cliffs. 塔尔羊灵巧地穿梭于陡峭的悬崖。

例句：Jharal are often seen grazing on high-altitude meadows.  人们经常能看到塔尔羊在高海拔的草地上吃草。|近义词：Himalayan tahr, tahr |反义词: N/A|词性:noun
