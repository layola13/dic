airtight:/ˈɛərtʌɪt/| adj.|密封的，无懈可击的|Impermeable to air or nearly so; completely sealed. Also, having no weaknesses or flaws. |例句：The container is airtight to keep the food fresh.  这个容器是密封的，以保持食物新鲜。

例句：His alibi was airtight. 他的不在场证明无懈可击。|近义词：sealed, hermetic, watertight, foolproof, impeccable|反义词：leaky, permeable, porous|词性:形容词

词源: air + tight.  "air" 来自古英语 *ær, 指的是我们呼吸的气体。 "tight" 来自古英语 þiht, 意思是 "dense, close, compact, watertight."
