noncarriers:/ˈnɒnˌkæriərz/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌkæriəz/ (UK)
|n.|非携带者|Individuals who do not carry a particular gene or trait, especially one associated with a disease.|
|例句：The study compared the health outcomes of carriers and noncarriers of the gene. 这项研究比较了携带和不携带该基因的人的健康结果。|
|例句：Noncarriers of the sickle cell trait are more susceptible to malaria. 不携带镰状细胞性状的人更容易感染疟疾。|
|近义词：non-carriers, unaffected individuals|
|反义词：carriers|
|词性：名词|
