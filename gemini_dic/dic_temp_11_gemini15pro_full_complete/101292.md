involver:/ɪnˈvɒlvər/ | n. | 参与者，牵涉其中的人 | someone who is involved in an activity, event, or situation | The police are trying to identify all the involvers in the incident. 警方正试图确认所有卷入该事件的人。 |  近义词: participant, contributor | 反义词: bystander, onlooker | 词性: noun

involve:/ɪnˈvɒlv/ | vt. | 包含；牵涉；使参与；需要 | to include or affect someone or something | The job involves a lot of travelling. 这份工作需要经常出差。 | 近义词: include, entail, require | 反义词: exclude, omit | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *envolver, from Latin involvere (“to wrap up, envelop, surround”), from in- (“in, into”) + volvere (“to roll, turn”).
