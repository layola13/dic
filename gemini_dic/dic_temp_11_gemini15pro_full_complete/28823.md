carouses:/kəˈraʊzɪz/
noun
狂欢；畅饮
a noisy, lively drinking party
The king and his knights caroused late into the night. 国王和他的骑士们狂欢到深夜。
近义词: revels, feasts, parties
反义词: mourns, grieves, abstains
词性:n.


carouse:/kəˈraʊz/
verb
狂欢；畅饮
to engage in a noisy, drunken revel
They caroused all night after winning the championship. 赢得冠军后，他们整夜狂欢。
近义词: revel, celebrate, party
反义词: abstain, mourn, grieve
词性:v.


Etymology: From Middle French *carouser "to drink completely," from Old French carous, from German gar aus "completely out," i.e., drink the cup completely empty.
