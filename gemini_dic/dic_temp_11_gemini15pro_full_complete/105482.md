killings:/ˈkɪlɪŋz/
n.
杀戮，谋杀(复数)
The act of killing someone or something, or the fact of being killed.
The killings shocked the entire community.  这些杀戮震惊了整个社区。
The police are investigating the recent killings. 警方正在调查最近发生的几起谋杀案。
synonyms: murders, slaughters, massacres, assassinations
antonyms: births, creations, saving, rescuing
noun
