escrod:/ˈɛskrɑːd/| n. | 小鳕鱼，幼鳕鱼 | A young cod, especially one split and boned for cooking. |  例句：The escrod was baked to perfection. 小鳕鱼烤得恰到好处。

例句：We had escrod for dinner last night. 我们昨晚吃了小鳕鱼。

近义词：cod, haddock | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑其他种类的鱼) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *escrute, possibly related to Old Norse skrútr ("boaster," referring to the fish's large head) or skrá ("dried skin").
