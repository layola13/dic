irksomeness:/ˈɜːrksəmnəs/ | n. | 厌烦，讨厌，令人厌烦的性质 | The irksomeness of the repetitive task made him want to quit. | The constant noise was the irksomeness of the journey. | synonyms: tediousness, monotony, boredom, dreariness | antonyms: excitement, interest, entertainment | noun

Etymology: From irksome + -ness.  Irksome comes from Middle English irk + -some.  Irk is from Old Norse *irkja "to weary of," related to yrkja "to work."
