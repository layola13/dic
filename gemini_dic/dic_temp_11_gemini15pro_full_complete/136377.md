nonresponsive:/ˌnɒnrɪˈspɒnsɪv/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈspɑːnsɪv/| adj.|无反应的，不回应的|Not responding or reacting to stimulus or treatment; failing to react or respond.|The patient was nonresponsive to the medication.  病人对药物没有反应。|Synonyms: unresponsive, unreactive, insensitive, inert, impassive|Antonyms: responsive, reactive, sensitive, alert|adjective

nonresponsive:/ˌnɒnrɪˈspɒnsɪv/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈspɑːnsɪv/| adj.|冷漠的，不配合的|Showing no interest or enthusiasm; unwilling to cooperate.|He was nonresponsive to my suggestions. 他对我的建议无动于衷。|Synonyms: uncooperative, indifferent, apathetic, aloof|Antonyms: cooperative, interested, enthusiastic, engaged|adjective
