gracias: /ˈɡrɑːθiəs/ (US), /ˈɡræsiæs/ (UK) | interjection | 谢谢，感谢 | An expression of gratitude, used in Spanish. | Example: Gracias for your help. 谢谢你帮忙。| Synonym: thank you, thanks | Antonym: N/A | Part of Speech: Interjection

gracias: /ˈɡrɑːθiəs/ (US), /ˈɡræsiæs/ (UK) | noun |  thanks, gratitude (less common as a noun) |  Can sometimes be used as a noun, though less frequently than as an interjection. | Example:  He expressed his gracias. 他表达了他的感谢。| Synonym: gratitude, appreciation | Antonym: ingratitude | Part of Speech: Noun
