larcener:/ˈlɑːrsənər/| n. |小贼，窃贼|A person who steals, especially one who steals relatively small amounts of money or goods.|The larcener was caught red-handed stealing a loaf of bread.|近义词：thief, robber, pilferer|反义词:policeman, lawman|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *larciner, from Old French larcin (“theft”), from Latin latrocinium (“robbery”), from latro (“robber, mercenary soldier”).
