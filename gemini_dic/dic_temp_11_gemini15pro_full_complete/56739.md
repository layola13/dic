doornail:/ˈdɔːrneɪl/| n. |门钉|A large-headed nail used to strengthen doors, especially old wooden ones.  Now used primarily in the idiom "dead as a doornail."|The old chest was secured with sturdy doornails.|近义词:nail, rivet|反义词:None|词性:noun
