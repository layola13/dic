monolingually:/ˌmɒnəˈlɪŋɡwəli/ | adv. | 单语地 | In a way that uses only one language.  She spoke monolingually in English. 她只用英语讲话。 | Example: The conference was conducted monolingually in French. 会议全程使用法语进行。

Example: He responded to the question monolingually, despite being fluent in several languages. 尽管精通多种语言，他还是用单一语言回答了问题。|Synonyms: unilingually | Antonyms: bilingually, multilingually | Part of Speech: Adverb
