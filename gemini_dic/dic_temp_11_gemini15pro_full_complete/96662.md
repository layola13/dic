industrially:/ɪnˈdʌstriəli/ | adv. | 工业地，产业化地 | In a way relating to industry; on a large scale. |  Many food products are industrially produced. 许多食品都是工业化生产的。|  The region is industrially developed.  这个地区工业发达。 | 近义词：commercially, manufacturingly | 反义词：domestically, agriculturally | 词性: 副词

词源:
From industry + -al + -ly. Ultimately from Latin industria.
