duress:/dʊˈrɛs/, /ˈdjʊərɛs/| n.|胁迫，强迫|Constraint or coercion by which a person is forced to perform an act against their will.|He signed the confession under duress. 他是在胁迫下签署了供词的。|Synonyms: coercion, compulsion, force, pressure|Antonyms: freedom, liberty|noun

duresses:/dʊˈrɛsɪz/, /ˈdjʊərɛsɪz/| n.|胁迫，强迫（duress的复数形式，较少使用）|Instances of constraint or coercion.|The witness claimed the confessions were obtained under duresses. 证人声称供词是在胁迫下获得的。|Synonyms: coercions, compulsions|Antonyms: freedoms, liberties|noun
