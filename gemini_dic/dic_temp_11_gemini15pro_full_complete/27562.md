canaliculated:/kəˌnælɪkjʊˈleɪtɪd/
adjective
有细管的，有沟渠的
having a small channel or groove, especially in bone or a plant stem.
The bone is canaliculated for the passage of blood vessels.  这块骨头有细管供血管通过。
The stem of the plant is canaliculated.  这种植物的茎有沟槽。
近义词: grooved, channeled, furrowed
反义词: smooth, flat
词性: adjective
