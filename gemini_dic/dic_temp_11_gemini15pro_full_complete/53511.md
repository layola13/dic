disassociated:/dɪsəˈsoʊʃiːeɪtɪd/ /dɪsəˈsəʊsiːeɪtɪd/
adjective
分离的，不相连的；无联系的
Separated, disconnected, or lacking connection.
He felt disassociated from his former life. 他感觉与以前的生活脱节了。
近义词: detached, unconnected, separate, aloof
反义词: connected, associated, linked, joined
词性: adjective

disassociate:/dɪsəˈsoʊʃiːeɪt/ /dɪsəˈsəʊsiːeɪt/
verb
使分离；使脱离关系
To separate; to disconnect from an association.
She tried to disassociate herself from the scandal. 她试图将自己与丑闻撇清关系。
近义词: detach, separate, disconnect, disengage
反义词: connect, associate, link, join
词性: verb
