manorialism:/məˈnɔːriəlɪzəm/
n.
庄园制度
The social and economic system by which the lord of the manor exploited his landed estate and his peasant tenants.
The decline of manorialism in England began long before the Black Death.  英国庄园制度的衰落早在黑死病之前就开始了。
feudalism, seignorialism
