nonamphibious:/ˌnɒnæmˈfɪbiəs/
adjective
非两栖的
Not able to live both on land and in water.
The desert tortoise is a nonamphibious reptile.  沙漠龟是一种非两栖爬行动物。
Specialized vehicles were required to transport the nonamphibious equipment across the river. 需要专门的车辆来运送非两栖设备过河。
近义词: terrestrial, land-dwelling
反义词: amphibious, aquatic
词性: adjective
