lapidarious:/ˌlæpɪˈdɛəriəs/| adj.|简洁的；言简意赅的；刻在石头上的|Relating to inscriptions on stone; suitable for inscriptions on stone; concise or terse, like an inscription.|例句：His lapidarious style contrasted sharply with the verbose pronouncements of his colleagues. 他的简洁的风格与他同事的冗长声明形成了鲜明的对比。

例句：The inscription on the tombstone was lapidarious, simply stating the deceased's name and dates. 墓碑上的铭文很简洁，只写了死者的姓名和日期。|近义词：concise, terse, succinct, pithy, epigrammatic|反义词：verbose, prolix, lengthy, wordy|词性: adjective
