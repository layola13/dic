nonsegmented:/nɒnˈsɛɡmɛntɪd/ | adj. | 非分段的，未分段的 | Not divided into segments or sections; continuous. | The nematode worm has a nonsegmented body.  这种线虫的身体是非分段的。| The virus has a nonsegmented genome. 这种病毒拥有非分段基因组。| continuous, whole, undivided | segmented, divided | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + segmented (from segment, ultimately from Latin segmentum "a piece cut off").
