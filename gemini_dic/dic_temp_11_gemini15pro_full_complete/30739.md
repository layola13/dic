cesarean section:/sɪˈzɛəriən ˈsɛkʃən/| n.|剖腹产|A surgical operation for delivering a child by cutting through the wall of the mother's abdomen.|The baby was delivered by cesarean section.  这个婴儿是剖腹产出生的。|Synonyms: C-section, Cesarean delivery|Antonyms: Vaginal birth|noun

cesarean:/sɪˈzɛəriən/| adj.|剖腹产的|Relating to or involving a cesarean section.|She had a cesarean delivery. 她进行了剖腹产。|Synonyms: C-section|Antonyms: Vaginal|adjective

section:/ˈsɛkʃən/| n.|部分，章节|A distinct part or portion of something.|The final section of the book is the most interesting. 这本书的最后一部分最有趣。|Synonyms: Part, segment, portion|Antonyms: Whole, entirety|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "caesareus" (of Caesar), supposedly because Julius Caesar was born this way (though this is disputed).  "Section" comes from Latin "sectio" (a cutting).
