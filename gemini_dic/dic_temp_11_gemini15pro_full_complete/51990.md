diaglyphic:/ˌdaɪˈɡlɪfɪk/
adjective
雕刻的，阴雕的
Carved in intaglio; incised.  Relating to or denoting a style of carving in which the design is sunk below the surface.
The sarcophagus was covered in diaglyphic designs of mythical creatures.  石棺上覆盖着神话生物的阴雕图案。
Near synonyms: incised, engraved, carved, etched
Antonyms: embossed, raised, relief
