cataphysical:/ˌkætəˈfɪzɪkəl/
adj.

Cataphysical theories often explore the nature of reality and existence beyond the observable universe.  形而上理论经常探索可观测宇宙之外的现实和存在的本质。

The author's cataphysical musings delve into the realm of abstract thought and the nature of being.  作者形而上的沉思深入到抽象思维和存在本质的领域。
