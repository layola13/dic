half-true:/ˌhæf ˈtruː/
adjective
半真半假的；部分真实的
Partially true; containing elements of truth and falsehood.
The politician's statement was half-true, omitting key details.  这位政治家的声明半真半假，省略了关键细节。
His explanation was half-true; some of it was accurate, but he also told a few lies. 他的解释半真半假；一部分是准确的，但也掺杂了一些谎言。

synonym
misleading, deceptive, partially true
antonym
completely true, entirely accurate, wholly truthful
词性
adjective
