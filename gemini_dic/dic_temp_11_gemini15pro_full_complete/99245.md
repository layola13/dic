intercommunicate:/ˌɪntərəˈkəmjuːnɪkeɪt/
verb
互相交流，互相沟通
To communicate with each other; exchange information or ideas.
The various departments within the company intercommunicate regularly to ensure smooth operation.  公司内部各个部门定期互相沟通，以确保运作顺畅。
The two computers can intercommunicate using a network cable.  这两台计算机可以通过网线互相通讯。
近义词: communicate, interact, connect, network
反义词: isolate
词性: verb
