hygrostatics:/ˌhaɪɡroʊˈstætɪks/ | n. | 静水力学; 液体静力学 | The study of the equilibrium of liquids at rest, especially the pressure exerted by liquids.  It's a branch of fluid mechanics that deals with the conditions of equilibrium of liquids at rest under the action of gravity. | Hygrostatics is important for understanding the behavior of hydraulic systems.  静水力学对于理解液压系统的行为很重要。| Near synonyms: fluid statics, hydrostatics | Antonyms: hydrodynamics, fluid dynamics | noun
