confirmednesses:/kənˈfɜːrmdnɪsɪz/
n.
确认；证实（的状态或性质）
The state or quality of being confirmed; the degree to which something is confirmed.
The confirmednesses of the multiple eyewitness accounts made the evidence irrefutable. 多位目击证人的证词的确定性使得证据无可辩驳。
近义词: certainty, corroboration, validation
反义词: uncertainty, doubt
