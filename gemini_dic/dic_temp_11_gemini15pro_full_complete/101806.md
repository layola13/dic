词源:
"Irritable" comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin irritabilis, from irritare ‘excite, provoke.’
"Bowel" comes from Old French boel, from late Latin botellus ‘sausage, intestine,’ from Latin botulus.
"Syndrome" comes from modern Latin syndroma, from Greek sundromē ‘concurrence,’ from sun- ‘together’ + dromos ‘running.’
