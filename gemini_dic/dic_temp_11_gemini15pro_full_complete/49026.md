degrease:/diːˈɡriːs/
verb
去除油脂
To remove grease from something.
The mechanic degreased the engine. 机械师给发动机除油。
近义词:clean, scour, cleanse
反义词:grease
词性:v.

degrease:/diːˈɡriːs/
noun
脱脂剂
A substance used to remove grease.
Use a commercial degreaser to clean the oven.  用商用脱脂剂清洁烤箱。
近义词:cleaner, solvent
反义词:grease
词性:n.
