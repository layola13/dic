haptically:/ˈhæptɪkli/| adv.|触觉地，通过触觉|Relating to the sense of touch; by touch.|She explored the sculpture haptically, running her fingers over its smooth surface. 她通过触觉探索雕塑，手指抚摸着光滑的表面。|例句：The device provides haptically rich feedback, allowing users to feel the texture of virtual objects.  该设备提供丰富的触觉反馈，使用户可以感受到虚拟物体的纹理。

例句：Haptically, the material felt like velvet. 触觉上，这种材料感觉像天鹅绒。|近义词：tangibly, tactilely|反义词:visually, auditorily|词性:副词
