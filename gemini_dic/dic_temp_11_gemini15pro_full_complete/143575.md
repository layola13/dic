outhouses:/ˈaʊtˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
户外厕所；附属建筑
A small building, such as a shed or toilet, that is separate from the main building.
The outhouse smelled strongly of disinfectant.  户外厕所里充满了消毒剂的味道。
The tools were stored in the outhouse. 工具存放在附属建筑里。
shed, privy, latrine
main building, indoor toilet
noun
