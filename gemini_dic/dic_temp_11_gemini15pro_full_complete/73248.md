fords:/fɔːrdz/| n.| 浅滩，涉水处|A shallow place in a river or stream that can be crossed by wading or driving.|We drove the jeep across the fords. 我们驾车越过了浅滩。|近义词：crossing, shallows|反义词:depths|名词

fords:/fɔːrdz/| v. (third-person singular simple present fords, present participle fording, simple past and past participle forded)| 涉水，渡过|To cross a river or stream at a shallow place.|The pioneers forded the river with their wagons.  拓荒者们用马车渡过了河。|近义词：wade, traverse|反义词:avoid|动词
