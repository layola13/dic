calotte:/kəˈlɒt/ | n. | 头盖骨；（僧侣等的）圆顶帽；冰帽 | A skullcap, or a small, close-fitting cap, especially one worn by Roman Catholic ecclesiastics. Also, a spherical cap or crown, especially the ice cap of a glacier. | The surgeon carefully removed a section of the calotte to access the brain.  外科医生小心地移除了一块头盖骨以便进入大脑。| synonym: skullcap, cap, ice cap, dome | antonym: base, bottom | noun

calotte:/kəˈlɒt/ | n. | (尤指冰川的) 顶盖 | The top part of something, especially the ice on top of a glacier. | The calotte of the glacier was melting rapidly due to rising temperatures. 冰川的顶盖由于气温上升正在迅速融化。| synonym: ice cap, crown, top | antonym: base, bottom | noun
