eternities:/ɪˈtɜːrnɪtiz/
n.
永恒; 永世; 无穷无尽的时期
a state of endless or infinite time.
例句：He spoke of the mysteries of life and death and the eternities beyond.  他谈到了生死之谜和来世。

例句：She believed their love would last for all eternities. 她相信他们的爱将永恒。

近义词：infinity, forever, perpetuity
反义词：finitude, brevity

词性: 名词 (plural of eternity)


词源: From Middle English *eternyte, from Old French eternité, from Latin aeternitās (“eternity”), from aeternus (“eternal”).
