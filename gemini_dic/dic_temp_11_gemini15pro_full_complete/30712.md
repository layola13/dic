cerulean: /səˈruːliən/ | adj. | 天蓝色的，蔚蓝色的 |  A deep blue color, like that of a clear sky.  Derived from Latin "caeruleus" meaning dark blue or blue-green. | The cerulean waters of the Caribbean were breathtaking. 加勒比海蔚蓝的海水令人叹为观止。 | synonyms: azure, sky-blue, sapphire | antonyms: murky, dark, black | adjective
