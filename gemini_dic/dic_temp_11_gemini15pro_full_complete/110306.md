词源:  "light-footedly" is derived from "light-footed," an adjective meaning "nimble and graceful in movement."  The adverbial suffix "-ly" is added to form "light-footedly."  "Light" refers to a lack of weight or burden, while "foot" relates to the way one steps or moves.  The combination suggests a nimble and effortless manner of movement.
