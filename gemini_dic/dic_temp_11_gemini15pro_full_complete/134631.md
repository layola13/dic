nonmajor:/ˈnɑːnˌmeɪdʒər/ /nɒnˈmeɪdʒə(r)/ |adj. n.|非专业的，非主修的；非主修学生|A student who is not specializing in a particular academic subject.  A subject that is not a student's main area of study. |例句：He's a nonmajor in economics. 他不是经济学专业的学生。|近义词：elective, optional, minor|反义词:major|词性:形容词
