alcoholate:/ˈælkəˌhɑːleɪt/ | v. |使醇化|To react (a substance) with an alcohol, especially to form an alcoholate. |The metal was alcoholated to form an alkoxide.  这种金属被醇化形成醇氧化物。 |近义词: None|反义词: None|词性: verb
