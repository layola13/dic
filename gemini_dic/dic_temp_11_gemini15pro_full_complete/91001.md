ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *hūsbōndi (“master of the house”), from hūs (“house”) + bōndi (“resident farmer, freeholder”). Cognate with Old English hūsbōnda.  The modern sense of "married man" developed from the meaning "master of the house."
