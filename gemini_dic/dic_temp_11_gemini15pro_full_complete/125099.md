mullioned:/ˈmʌljənd/| adj.|有竖框的，多竖框的|Divided by mullions (vertical bars between panes of glass in a window).|The mullioned windows gave the old house a stately appearance. 这座老房子有竖框的窗户，使它显得庄严气派。|Example:  Sunlight streamed through the mullioned windows. 阳光透过竖框窗户流泻进来。|Synonyms: multi-paned, lattice-paned|Antonyms: single-paned|Word type: adjective
