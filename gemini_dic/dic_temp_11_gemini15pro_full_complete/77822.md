generationally:/ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənəli/
| adv.| 代际地；在几代人之间|Relating to or affecting several generations.  It describes differences or similarities between different age groups. | The attitudes towards technology differ generationally.  科技方面不同年代的人态度迥异。|  Generationally, millennials are more tech-savvy than baby boomers.  就几代人而言，千禧一代比婴儿潮一代更精通技术。 | synonym: across generations, intergenerationally| antonym: intragenerationally|词性: 副词
