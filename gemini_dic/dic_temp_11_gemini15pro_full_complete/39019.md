confiteor:/ˌkɒnfɪˈtiːɔːr/ /kənˌfɪtiˈɔːr/ | n. |忏悔; 忏悔祷文 |A form of confession used in Roman Catholic and some Anglican services.  A prayer in which a person acknowledges their sins and asks for forgiveness. |例句：The priest began the Mass with the Confiteor. 神父以忏悔祷文开始了弥撒。

例句：She whispered the Confiteor before receiving communion.  她在领圣餐前低声念了忏悔祷文。|近义词：confession, penitence, acknowledgment |反义词: denial, boast, justification |词性:noun
