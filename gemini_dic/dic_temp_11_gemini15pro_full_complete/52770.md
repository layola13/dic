dilatorily:/ˈdɪləˌtɔːrɪli/ /ˌdaɪləˈtɔːrɪli/
adverb|拖延地，缓慢地|in a slow and delayed manner; tardily|He worked dilatorily, taking frequent breaks. 他工作拖拖拉拉，经常休息。|
The project proceeded dilatorily due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目进展缓慢。|
近义词: slowly, tardily, leisurely, sluggishly|
反义词: promptly, quickly, expeditiously, efficiently|
词性: adverb


Etymology:
From Late Latin *dīlātōrius, from Latin dīlātus (past participle of dīferre “to postpone, defer”), from dis- + lātus (“carried, borne”).
