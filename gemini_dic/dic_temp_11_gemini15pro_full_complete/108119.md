latifoliate: /ˌlætɪˈfəʊliət/ /ˌlætɪˈfoʊliət/
adjective
阔叶的；具有阔叶的
Having broad leaves, as opposed to needles or scales.
Deciduous trees are typically latifoliate. 落叶树通常是阔叶的。

The latifoliate forest was alive with birdsong. 阔叶林里鸟鸣声不绝于耳。

近义词: broad-leaved, broadleaf
反义词: needle-leaved, coniferous, acicular (针状的)
词性: adjective
