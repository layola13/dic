coproprietors:/ˌkoʊprəˈpraɪətərz/
n.
共同所有人，共有者
Joint owners; people who own something together.
John and Mary are coproprietors of the bakery.
约翰和玛丽是这家面包店的共同所有人。
The coproprietors decided to sell the business.
共同所有人决定出售这家企业。
近义词:joint owners, co-owners
反义词:None
