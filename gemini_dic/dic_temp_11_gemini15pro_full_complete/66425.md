exegeses:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒiːsiːz/
|n.| 诠释；注释；(尤指对圣经的)解释 |A critical explanation or interpretation of a text, especially of scripture.|The professor's exegeses of the ancient texts were insightful and thought-provoking. 教授对古代文献的诠释深刻且发人深省。|Synonyms: interpretations, explanations, commentaries, analyses|Antonyms: misinterpretations, distortions, obfuscations|noun
