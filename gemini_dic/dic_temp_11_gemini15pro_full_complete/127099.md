nationalist:/ˈnæʃənəlɪst/ (US), /ˈnæʃnəlɪst/ (UK)| n. adj.|民族主义者；国家主义者|A person who strongly identifies with their own nation and vigorously supports its interests, especially to the exclusion or detriment of the interests of other nations. |例句：The nationalist movement gained momentum after the war. 战后，民族主义运动势头强劲。

例句：She was accused of being a nationalist. 她被指责为民族主义者。|近义词：patriot, chauvinist|反义词：cosmopolitan, internationalist|词性: noun

nationalist:/ˈnæʃənəlɪst/ (US), /ˈnæʃnəlɪst/ (UK)| adj.|民族主义的；国家主义的|Relating to or supporting nationalism. |例句：Nationalist sentiment is on the rise. 民族主义情绪正在高涨。
