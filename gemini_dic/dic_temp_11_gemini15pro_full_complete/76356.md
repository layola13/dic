gade:/ɡeɪd/| n.|刺棒，尖棒，|A stick with a sharp point, used for goading animals.|The farmer used a gade to herd the cattle.|近义词：goad, prod, stick|反义词:None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *gaddr (meaning "spike, nail"), related to Old English *gād (meaning "goad"). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gaidō, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰɑ́y-dʰh₂- (meaning "to pierce, stab").
