filigree:/ˈfɪlɪɡriː/| adj. n.| 镂空的，金银丝细工；| Delicately or elaborately ornamented.| The cabinet was inlaid with silver filigree.  这个橱柜镶嵌着银丝细工。| 近义词: ornate, delicate, intricate| 反义词: plain, simple| 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Old French *filigraine (Modern French filigrane), from Italian filigrana "filigree," from Latin filum "thread" + granum "grain".
