economies:/ɪˈkɒnəmiz/ /ɪˈkɑːnəmiz/| n. |经济；节约；经济制度；经济体|The state of a country or region in terms of the production and consumption of goods and services and the supply of money.  A frugal and efficient use of resources.|
例句：The global economy is facing many challenges. 全球经济面临许多挑战。

例句：The economies of several Asian countries are growing rapidly.  几个亚洲国家的经济正在快速增长。

近义词：finances, savings, thrift, frugality|
反义词: extravagance, waste, spending|
词性: noun
