animation:/ˌænɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 动画；动画片；活跃；生气 | The process of making films/movies, videos, or computer games in which drawings or models seem to move.  The quality of being lively and enthusiastic. |  例句：The animation in that new movie is incredible. 那部新电影的动画效果令人难以置信。

例句：Her animation and enthusiasm were contagious. 她的活力和热情很有感染力。 | 近义词：liveliness, vibrancy, cartoon, animated film | 反义词：inactivity, dullness | 词性：名词
