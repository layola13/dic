outstrives:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪvz/
|v.|超过，胜过|To strive more successfully than; surpass in striving; excel.|He outstrives everyone in the class. 他在班上超过了所有人。|近义词：surpasses, excels, outdoes, transcends|反义词:fails, loses, underperforms|词性:动词

Etymology:
From out- + strive.  "Out-" acts as a prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed." "Strive" comes from Middle English striven, from Old French estriver "to debate, quarrel," probably of Germanic origin.
