examinership:/ɪɡˈzæmɪnərʃɪp/| n.|审查员的职位；审查员资格；审查员身份；（爱尔兰共和国的）公司审查程序|A legal process in the Republic of Ireland allowing a company experiencing financial difficulties to be placed under the protection of the court while it attempts to restructure its debts.|The company entered examinership in an attempt to avoid liquidation. 这家公司进入审查程序以避免清算。|Synonyms: receivership, administration|Antonyms: solvency, prosperity|noun
