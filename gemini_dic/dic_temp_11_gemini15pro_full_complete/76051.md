furly:/ˈfɜːrli/| adj.|毛茸茸的，覆盖着毛皮的|Covered with or resembling fur; furry.|The kitten was small and furly. 小猫很小，毛茸茸的。|近义词：furry, downy, hairy|反义词:bald, smooth, hairless|词性:形容词

词源: From fur + -ly (suffix forming adjectives).  Essentially, "like fur."
