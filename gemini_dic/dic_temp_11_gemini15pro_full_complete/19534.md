biflorous:/baɪˈflɔːrəs/
adjective
双花的；有两朵花的
Having or bearing two flowers.
The biflorous stem produced two beautiful blossoms.  这个双花的茎开出了两朵美丽的花。
The orchid is biflorous, displaying two delicate blooms. 这株兰花是双花的，开着两朵精致的花朵。
近义词: two-flowered
反义词: uniflorous (单花的), multiflorous (多花的)
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *biflorus, from bi- ("two") + flos ("flower").
