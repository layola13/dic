imperialisms: /ɪmˈpɪəriəlɪzəmz/
|n.|帝国主义（复数）|The policy, practice, or advocacy of extending the power and dominion of a nation especially by direct territorial acquisitions or by gaining indirect control over the political or economic life of other areas; also, the extension or imposition of power, authority, or influence|European imperialisms of the 19th century had a devastating impact on many parts of the world. 19世纪的欧洲帝国主义对世界许多地区造成了毁灭性的影响。|Synonyms: colonialism, expansionism|Antonyms: anti-imperialism, self-determination|Noun
