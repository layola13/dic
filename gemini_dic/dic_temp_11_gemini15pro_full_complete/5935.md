ambler:/ˈæmblər/| n.|漫步者，缓行者|A person who ambles; someone who walks at a slow, relaxed pace.|例句：The ambler enjoyed the scenic route through the park. 漫步者享受着穿过公园的风景路线。

例句：She was an ambler by nature, never in a hurry. 她生性是个漫步者，从来不慌不忙。|近义词：stroller, wanderer, saunterer|反义词:runner, sprinter|词性:noun
