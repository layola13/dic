overcoolness:/ˌəʊvəˈkuːlnəs/ | n. | 过分冷静；过分沉着；冷漠 | Excessive coolness; a state of being too calm or detached, often perceived as aloofness or indifference. |  He reacted to the crisis with an unsettling overcoolness. 他对危机的反应是令人不安的过分冷静。

| 近义词：aloofness, detachment, indifference, impassivity | 反义词：enthusiasm, warmth, passion, excitement | 词性：名词


overcool:/ˌəʊvəˈkuːl/ | vt. |  过度冷却 | To cool something below its normal or desired temperature. | Don't overcool the engine, it could damage it.  不要过度冷却发动机，可能会损坏它。


| 近义词：chill, refrigerate | 反义词：heat, warm | 词性：动词
