multicounty:/ˌmʌltiˈkaʊnti/
adjective
多县的；跨越多个县的
spanning or encompassing more than one county.
The multicounty transit system serves a large area.  这个多县的交通系统服务范围很广。
The multicounty investigation involved several police departments. 这项跨越多个县的调查涉及多个警察部门。
multi-jurisdictional, regional
local, single-county
adjective
