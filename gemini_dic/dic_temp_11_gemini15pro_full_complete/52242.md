dibasic:/daɪˈbeɪsɪk/
adjective
二元的;二碱基的
Having two acidic hydrogen atoms or groups that can be replaced by basic atoms or groups to form salts.
Dibasic acids can react with two moles of a base to form a salt. 二元酸可以与两摩尔的碱反应形成盐。
Sulfuric acid is a dibasic acid. 硫酸是一种二元酸。
近义词: diprotic
反义词: monobasic
词性: adjective
