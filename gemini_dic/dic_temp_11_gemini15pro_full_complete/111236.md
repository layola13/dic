lithotype:/ˈlɪθətaɪp/| n.|岩型|A rock type or category defined by its macroscopic physical characteristics, such as texture, structure, and composition.  It's a term used in coal petrography and sometimes in broader geological contexts.|The lithotype of this coal sample is predominantly vitrain.|Synonyms: rock type, petrographic type|Antonyms: N/A|noun
