interceding:/ˌɪntərˈsiːdɪŋ/
verb
调解，代为求情，斡旋
pleading on behalf of another; mediating
The lawyer is interceding for his client. 律师正在为他的客户求情。
近义词: mediating, intervening, advocating, pleading
反义词: ignoring, neglecting, opposing
词性: Verb

intercede:/ˌɪntərˈsiːd/
verb
调解，代为求情，斡旋
to plead on behalf of another; to mediate
I interceded on her behalf. 我为她求情。
近义词: mediate, intervene, advocate, plead
反义词: ignore, neglect, oppose
词性: Verb
