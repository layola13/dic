hexylic:/hɛkˈsɪlɪk/
adjective
己基的，与己烷相关的
Relating to or derived from hexane, especially containing six carbon atoms in a straight chain.
Hexylic acid is a carboxylic acid with a six-carbon chain. 己酸是一种具有六个碳链的羧酸。
The hexylic alcohol is commonly used in fragrances. 己醇常用于香水中。
近义词:  hexyl-, caproic (specifically for the six-carbon acid)
反义词:  N/A
词性:  adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hexyl + -ic.  Hexyl ultimately derives from Greek *héx (six) + -yl (indicating a radical or group). The suffix -ic forms adjectives meaning "pertaining to" or "derived from".
