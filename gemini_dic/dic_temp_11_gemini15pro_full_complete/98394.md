insolubility:/ɪnˈsɒljʊˈbɪləti/| n.|不溶性，不可解性|The insolubility of the problem frustrated the scientists.  这个问题的不可解性使科学家们感到沮丧。|例句：The insolubility of the chemical in water made the experiment difficult.  这种化学物质在水中的不溶性使实验变得困难。

The insolubility of the mystery kept everyone guessing. 这个谜团的不可解性让每个人都在猜测。|近义词：insolvability, indissolubility (for certain meanings)|反义词：solubility, dissolvability|词性：noun

Etymology:
From in- (not) + soluble (capable of being dissolved) + -ity (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Ultimately from Latin insolubilis (insoluble) and -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).
