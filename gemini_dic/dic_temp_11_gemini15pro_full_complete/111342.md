livelihoods:/ˈlaɪvlihʊdz/| n. |生计，生活来源|Means of supporting oneself, especially financially; a job or other source of income that provides the necessities of life.|Many people in the village lost their livelihoods after the factory closed. 村里许多人在工厂倒闭后失去了生计。|Synonyms: income, living, sustenance, maintenance, support |Antonyms: unemployment, poverty, destitution|noun
