melanorrhoea:/ˌmɛlənəˈriːə/ | n. |黑漆树属；黑漆树脂|A genus of trees in the cashew family (Anacardiaceae) native to Southeast Asia and Australia, known for producing a black resin used in varnishes and lacquers.  The resin itself can also be referred to as melanorrhoea. | The melanorrhoea tree is highly valued for its durable resin. 黑漆树因其耐用的树脂而备受重视。

The melanorrhoea resin is used to make traditional lacquerware. 黑漆树脂用于制作传统的漆器。| 近义词: lacquer tree | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a genus of trees) | 词性: noun
