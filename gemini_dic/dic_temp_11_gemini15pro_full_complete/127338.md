nazification:/ˌnɑːtsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ |n.|纳粹化|The process of imposing Nazi ideology and policies on a country or organization.  The forced adoption of Nazi principles and practices.|
The nazification of Germany in the 1930s was a rapid and terrifying process. |20世纪30年代德国的纳粹化是一个迅速而可怕的过程。|
Historians continue to debate the extent of nazification in occupied France. |历史学家仍在争论纳粹化在被占领的法国的程度。|
Synonyms:  fascization, totalitarianism|
Antonyms: democratization, liberalization|
Word type: Noun
