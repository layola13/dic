flagellation:/ˌflædʒəˈleɪʃən/
n.|鞭笞; 鞭打; 责骂; (宗教苦修的)自我鞭笞|The act of hitting someone with a whip, especially as a religious punishment or as a way of getting sexual pleasure.  |The monk practiced flagellation as a form of penance.  这位僧侣进行自我鞭笞作为一种忏悔的方式。|近义词：whipping, flogging, scourging, lashing|反义词: commendation, praise
词性:名词
