carvol: /ˈkɑːrvɒl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːrvɑːl/ (US)| n.|拼车，顺风车|The practice of transporting people in a private vehicle, especially to and from work, reducing the number of cars on the road.|Example:  I use carvol to commute to work, which saves money on gas and parking. 我用拼车上下班，这样可以节省汽油和停车费。

Example:  The company encourages carvol among its employees to reduce their carbon footprint. 公司鼓励员工拼车以减少碳足迹。|近义词：ridesharing, carpooling, lift-sharing (UK)|反义词: driving alone, solo driving|词性:noun
