condensedly:/kənˈdensɪdli/ | adv. | 简洁地，简要地 | In a condensed or concise manner; briefly and compactly. | He explained the complex theory condensedly, making it easy to understand. 他简洁地解释了这个复杂的理论，使它易于理解。| concisely, briefly, succinctly, compactly | verbosely, lengthily | adverb

condensedly:/kənˈdensɪdli/ | adv. | 简洁地，简要地 |  Presented in a shortened form, containing only the most important information.  | She condensedly summarized the main points of the meeting. 她简要地总结了会议的要点。 | succinctly, compactly, tersely, briefly | elaborately, extensively | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From condense + -ly.  Condense comes from Late Latin condensare "to make dense," from Latin com-, intensive prefix (see com-), + densare "to make thick," from densus "thick, dense, crowded."
