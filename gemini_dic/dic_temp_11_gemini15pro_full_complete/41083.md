coppery: /ˈkɒpəri/ (US) /ˈkɒpəri/ (UK)| adj.|铜色的；像铜的；含铜的|Resembling copper in color or containing copper.|The sunset painted the sky in coppery hues. 夕阳把天空染成了铜色。|The old penny had a coppery sheen. 那枚旧硬币有一种铜色的光泽。|近义词：bronze, copper-colored, reddish-brown|反义词：silvery, golden|词性:形容词
Etymology: From copper + -y (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'having the qualities of', 'full of', 'like', 'covered with').  源自 copper + -y（后缀构成形容词，意为“具有…的性质”、“充满”、“像”、“覆盖着”）。
