frigefaction: /ˌfrɪdʒɪˈfækʃən/
n.
冷却，冷冻
the act of making something cold or the state of being cold.
The frigefaction of the food preserved it for months.  食物的冷冻使其保存了几个月。

近义词: cooling, chilling, refrigeration
反义词: heating, warming

词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *frigefactus (past participle of *frigefacere 'to make cold'), from frigus 'cold' + facere 'to make.'
