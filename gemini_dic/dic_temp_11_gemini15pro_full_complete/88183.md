hiltless:/ˈhɪltləs/| adj.|无柄的，没有剑柄的|Lacking a hilt (the handle of a sword or dagger).|The hiltless dagger was difficult to wield effectively in combat.  无柄的匕首在战斗中很难有效地挥舞。|The antique sword, sadly hiltless, was displayed in a glass case.  这把古董剑，可惜没有剑柄，被陈列在一个玻璃柜里。|Near synonyms: handleless, grip-less |Antonym: hilted|adjective
