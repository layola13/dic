adage:/ˈædɪdʒ/| n.|谚语，格言|A traditional saying expressing a common experience or observation.|例句： "A stitch in time saves nine" is an old adage.  “小洞不补，大洞吃苦”是一句古老的谚语。

例句：The adage "look before you leap" is good advice. 谚语“三思而后行”是一个很好的忠告。|近义词：proverb, saying, maxim, aphorism|反义词:None|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *adage*, from Old French *adage*, from Latin *adagium* "proverb, maxim," perhaps from *ad- "to" + *aio* "I say."
