geniously:/dʒiːniəsli/
adverb|天才地，巧妙地|In a way that shows great intelligence and skill; cleverly.|He solved the problem geniously. 他巧妙地解决了这个问题。|近义词：cleverly, ingeniously, brilliantly, skillfully|反义词：stupidly, foolishly, clumsily|词性:副词
词源：From genius + -ly.
