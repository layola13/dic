chalkcutter:/ˈtʃɔːkˌkʌtər/| n.|粉笔雕刻师，雕刻粉笔的人|A person who carves chalk, often creating intricate designs.|He was a renowned chalkcutter, his works displayed in museums around the world. 他是一位著名的粉笔雕刻师，他的作品在世界各地的博物馆展出。|近义词：chalk carver, artist, sculptor|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "chalk" (Old English *cealc, from Latin calx "lime, limestone") + "cutter" (from "cut" (Old Norse kuta "to cut") + "-er" (agent suffix)).  Essentially meaning "one who cuts chalk".
