irreducible:/ˌɪrɪˈduːsəbl/ | adj. | 不可约的，不可简化的 | Not able to be reduced or simplified further. | This problem involves irreducible complexity.  这个问题涉及不可简化的复杂性。| The fraction 1/3 is irreducible. 这个分数1/3是不可约的。 |  近义词: indivisible, unsimplifiable | 反义词: reducible, divisible | 词性: 形容词
词源:  From Late Latin *irreducibilis, from in- "not" + reducibilis "able to be led back," from reducere "to lead back" (see reduce).
