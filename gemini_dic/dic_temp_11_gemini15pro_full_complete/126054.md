mustanging:/ˈmʌstæŋɪŋ/
verb
野马放牧；捕捉野马
The act of herding or capturing wild horses, specifically Mustangs.
He spent his summers mustanging in the Nevada desert. 他夏天在内华达州的沙漠里放牧野马。
近义词:  wild horse wrangling, horse capturing
反义词: horse releasing
词性: verb
