metaplast:/ˈmɛtəˌplæst/
noun
变质岩，变形岩石
A rock that has undergone metamorphism, meaning it has been changed by heat, pressure, and/or chemically active fluids.
Metamorphic rocks like metaplast are formed deep within the Earth. 变质岩，如变质岩，形成于地球深处。
Metaplast is a type of metamorphic rock. 变质岩是一种变质岩。
Synonyms: metamorphic rock
Antonyms: igneous rock, sedimentary rock
