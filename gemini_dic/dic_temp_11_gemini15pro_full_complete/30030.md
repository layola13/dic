cayuse:/kaɪˈjuːs/| n.|卡尤斯马；劣马|A small, strong horse of the western United States, originally bred by the Cayuse people.  It often refers to an inferior or undesirable horse.|例句：The old cowboy rode his trusty, if somewhat scruffy, cayuse across the plains.  老牛仔骑着他那匹可靠的、虽然有点邋遢的卡尤斯马穿过平原。

例句：That cayuse is too stubborn for a beginner rider. 那匹劣马对初学者来说太倔强了。|近义词：horse, pony, nag, steed|反义词: None (No single antonym encapsulates the specific meaning of a Cayuse horse or a poor-quality horse.)|词性:noun
