flitter:/ˈflɪtər/| v. n.|轻快地飞舞，飘动；一闪而过；浪费(时间、金钱)；碎片，小片|To move lightly and swiftly; to dart about; to waste time or money frivolously; a small piece or fragment.|Butterflies flittered among the flowers. 蝴蝶在花丛中飞舞。|近义词：flutter, dart, flit, squander, trifle|反义词：plod, trudge|verb

flitter:/ˈflɪtər/| n.|碎片，小片|A small piece or fragment.|Glittering flitters of foil decorated the Christmas tree.  闪闪发光的箔片装饰着圣诞树。|近义词：fragment, shard, sliver|反义词：whole|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fliteren, frequentative of fliten (“to fly, flutter”), from Old Norse *flytja (“to cause to float, fly”), from Proto-Germanic *fliutjaną (“to float, fly”), from Proto-Indo-European *plew- (“to flow, float, fly”).
