hallowednesses:/ˈhæləʊdnəsɪz/ |n.|神圣性；圣洁性|The state of being hallowed; sanctity.  It refers to the quality of being holy, consecrated, or revered.|The hallowednesses of the ancient burial grounds filled her with awe.  古代墓地的神圣让她充满敬畏。|近义词: sanctity, holiness, sacredness|反义词: profanity, irreverence|词性: noun

词源:  From hallowed + -ness.  Hallowed itself comes from Old English *hālgod, past participle of hālgian "to make holy," from hālig "holy."
