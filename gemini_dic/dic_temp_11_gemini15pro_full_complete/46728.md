dandie:/ˈdændi/| n.|丹迪犬; 花花公子; 纨绔子弟|A small long-bodied short-legged dog of a breed originating in Scotland, with a large head, large pendant ears, and a rough coat.|例句：He showed his prize-winning dandie at the dog show. 他在狗展上展示了他获奖的丹迪犬。

例句：He was known as a bit of a dandie, always impeccably dressed. 他有点花花公子的名声，总是穿得无可挑剔。|近义词：fop, dandy, dude, swell, popinjay, coxcomb, beau|反义词:sloven, unkempt person|词性:noun
