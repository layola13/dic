overgrow:/ˌoʊvərˈɡroʊ/| v. |过度生长，蔓延；（指植物）长得太茂盛|The garden is overgrown with weeds. 花园里杂草丛生。|近义词：overrun, cover, engulf|反义词:clear, prune|词性:动词
overgrown:/ˌoʊvərˈɡroʊn/| adj. |长满…的；长得过大的|The path was overgrown and difficult to follow. 小路长满了杂草，很难走。|近义词：overrun, rampant|反义词:trimmed, pruned|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ofergrōwan (attested only in past participle ofergrōwen), from ofer- (over) + grōwan (to grow).
