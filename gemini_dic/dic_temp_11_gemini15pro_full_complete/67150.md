exposer:/ɪkˈspoʊzər/| n.|暴露者；揭露者|Someone who exposes wrongdoing or secrets.  A person who reveals something discreditable.|The exposer of the scandal remained anonymous.  这个丑闻的揭露者仍然保持匿名。|Synonym: whistleblower, informant, revealer|Antonym: concealer, suppressor|noun

expose:/ɪkˈspoʊz/| vt.|暴露；揭露|To make something visible or known, typically something discreditable.|The journalist exposed the corruption within the company.  记者揭露了公司内部的腐败。|Synonym: reveal, uncover, unveil|Antonym: conceal, hide, cover up|verb
