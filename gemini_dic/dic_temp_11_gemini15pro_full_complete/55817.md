divests:/daɪˈvɛsts/
verb
剥夺，使摆脱，出售
To deprive or dispossess of power, rights, or possessions; to sell off holdings (usually of an asset).
The company divested itself of its failing subsidiaries.  这家公司出售了其亏损的子公司。
synonyms: sell off, dispose of, relinquish, unload, liquidate
antonyms: acquire, invest, obtain, purchase
词性：动词


divest:/daɪˈvɛst/
verb
脱去，卸下
To strip or deprive (someone or something) of clothing, ornament, or equipment.
He divested himself of his coat and hat. 他脱掉了外套和帽子。
synonyms: strip, undress, disrobe
antonyms: dress, clothe, adorn
词性：动词
