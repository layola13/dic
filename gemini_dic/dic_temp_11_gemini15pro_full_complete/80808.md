gothicly:/ˈɡɒθɪkli/ | adv. | 哥特式地 | In a gothic manner; resembling or suggestive of Gothic architecture, art, or literature. | The mansion was decorated gothicly, with pointed arches and stained-glass windows. 这座大厦以哥特式风格装饰，有尖拱和彩色玻璃窗。 |  The old church was gothicly impressive.  这座古老的教堂哥特式风格令人印象深刻。 | 近义词: gloomily, darkly, ominously | 反义词: brightly, cheerfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "Gothic" + "-ly". "Gothic" ultimately derives from the Goths, an East Germanic people.  The term was originally used pejoratively to describe medieval art and architecture, considered barbaric compared to the classical styles.
