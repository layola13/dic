automating:/ˈɔːtəmeɪtɪŋ/
verb|使自动化, 自动化|the process of making something operate automatically|例句：The company is automating its production line.  公司正在使其生产线自动化。|例句：Automating repetitive tasks frees up employees for more creative work.  将重复性任务自动化可以让员工腾出时间去做更有创造性的工作。|近义词：mechanizing, computerizing|反义词：manual, hand-operated|v.
