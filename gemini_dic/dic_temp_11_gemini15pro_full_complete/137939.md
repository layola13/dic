nonwalking:/ˌnɑːnˈwɔːkɪŋ/
adjective
非步行的，不走的
Not able to walk; not designed for walking.
The hospital provides nonwalking patients with wheelchairs.  医院为无法步行的病人提供轮椅。
The hotel offers nonwalking tours of the city.  这家酒店提供城市非步行游览。
近义词:immobile, sedentary
反义词:walking, ambulatory
词性:adjective
