noncorrelations:/ˌnɒnkɒrəˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 无关性；非相关性 | The absence of a statistical relationship between two or more variables.  It indicates that changes in one variable are not associated with changes in the other(s). |  The study found noncorrelations between income and happiness in that specific population. 这项研究发现，在特定人群中，收入和幸福感之间没有相关性。

The researchers observed noncorrelations between the two sets of data, leading them to conclude that the phenomena were unrelated. 研究人员观察到两组数据之间没有相关性，因此得出结论，这些现象之间没有关系。 | 近义词: independence, lack of correlation, uncorrelatedness | 反义词: correlation, relationship, association | 词性: 名词


词源:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non "not."
correlation (noun): From Late Latin correlātiōn- (stem of correlātiō ), from correlātus (past participle of correlāre  "bring into mutual relation") + -ion.
