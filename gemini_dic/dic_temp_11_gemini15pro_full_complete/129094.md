nitrum:/ˈnaɪtrəm/
noun
硝石，鉀硝
Potassium nitrate, a white crystalline salt used especially in making gunpowder, fertilizers, and glass.
Saltpeter was used in the manufacture of gunpowder.  硝石曾用于制造火药。
synonyms: saltpeter, niter, potassium nitrate
