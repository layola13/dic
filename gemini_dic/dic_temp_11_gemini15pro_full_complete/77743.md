gendarmery:/ʒɑːnˈdɑːrməri/
|n.|宪兵队， жандармерия|A body of soldiers or police with military organization, especially one used for policing a civilian population.|The gendarmery was called in to restore order after the riot. 骚乱发生后，宪兵队被召来恢复秩序。|synonyms: police force, constabulary, military police|antonyms:  civilian force|noun
