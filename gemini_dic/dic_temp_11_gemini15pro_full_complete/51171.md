desquamate:/ˈdɛskwəˌmeɪt/ /dɪˈskweɪmeɪt/| v. |脱皮，脱屑|To shed or peel off in scales or flakes, as the skin does after sunburn or certain diseases.| After a week of sunburn, his skin began to desquamate.  晒伤一周后，他的皮肤开始脱皮。| The bark of the tree desquamates annually.  这棵树的树皮每年都会脱落。| synonyms: peel, shed, flake, exfoliate | antonyms: adhere, stick, cling | verb
