flakiest:/ˈfleɪkiɪst/| adj.|最易碎的，最薄的，最不稳定的|superlative form of flaky: most flaky.  Having many flakes or thin layers that break off easily; tending to break or separate easily into flakes.|The flakiest pastry I've ever eaten. 我吃过最酥脆的糕点。|Synonyms: crumbly, brittle, fragile, flimsy|Antonyms: tough, sturdy, solid, stable|adjective

flaky:/ˈfleɪki/| adj.|易碎的，薄的，不稳定的|Tending to break or separate easily into flakes; behaving in a way that is not responsible or expected.|The pie crust was too flaky. 派皮太酥了。|Synonyms: crumbly, brittle, fragile, unreliable, eccentric|Antonyms: tough, sturdy, solid, reliable, stable|adjective
