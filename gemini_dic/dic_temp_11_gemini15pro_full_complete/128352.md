neutralizing:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzɪŋ/
neutralize: /ˈnjuːtrəlaɪz/ |v. | 中和；抵消；使无效 | To make something neutral, especially by combining it with something that has the opposite effect. | The acid was neutralized by the alkali. 酸被碱中和了。| synonyms: counteract, offset, balance, negate | antonyms: activate, enhance, intensify | verb
neutralized:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzd/ |adj. | 被中和的；无效的 | Having been made neutral; no longer effective. | The neutralized solution was safe to handle. 中和后的溶液可以安全处理。| synonyms: deactivated, ineffective, inert | antonyms: active, effective, potent | adjective
neutralization:/ˌnjuːtrələˈzeɪʃən/ |n. | 中和；抵消 | The act or process of neutralizing something. | The neutralization of the acid took several minutes. 酸的中和过程花了数分钟。| synonyms: counteraction, offsetting | antonyms: activation, intensification | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *neutralizare, from Latin neuter (“neither”).
