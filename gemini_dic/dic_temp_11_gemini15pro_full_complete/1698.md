acopic: /eɪˈkɒpɪk/ | adj. |  无症状的，不显症状的 |  Relating to or denoting a disease or condition that is not accompanied by symptoms.  Derived from Greek *a-*, "without," and *skopein*, "to look at." Literally, "without visible signs." | The infection was acopic in its early stages. 感染早期没有症状。|  asymptomatic, subclinical | symptomatic, manifest | adjective
