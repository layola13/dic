flat-screen:/ˈflæt skriːn/
adjective|平板的；屏幕扁平的|having a flat screen, especially a television or computer monitor|We bought a new flat-screen TV. 我们买了一台新的平板电视。|近义词:flat-panel, widescreen|反义词:curved-screen
noun|平板屏幕；平板显示器|a flat screen, especially a television or computer monitor|The flat-screen is much lighter than our old TV. 这台平板显示器比我们的旧电视轻得多。|近义词:flat-panel display|反义词:CRT monitor
词性|adjective, noun
