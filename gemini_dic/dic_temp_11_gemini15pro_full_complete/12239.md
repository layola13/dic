ascence: /əˈsens/
n.
上升，升高
The ascendence of the new government brought hope to the people.
新政府的崛起给人民带来了希望。
The gradual ascendence of the sun warmed the earth.
太阳的逐渐升起温暖了大地。
近义词: rise, ascent, climb, elevation
反义词: descent, decline, fall
词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *ascendere, from Latin ascendere "to climb, mount, ascend," from ad- "to" + scandere "to climb."
