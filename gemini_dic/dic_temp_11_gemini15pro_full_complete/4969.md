alkalimetric:/ˌælkəlɪˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
碱量测定的，碱量滴定的
relating to or determined by alkalimetry.
The alkalimetric method is used to determine the concentration of an acid.  碱量滴定法用于测定酸的浓度。
The chemist performed an alkalimetric titration. 化学家进行了碱量滴定。
Synonyms: acidimetric (less common for bases)
Antonyms: acidimetric (when referring to acid determination)
词性: adjective
