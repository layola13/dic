calceiform:/kælˈsiːɪfɔːrm/
adjective
鞋状的，似拖鞋的
shaped like a slipper or shoe
The calceiform structure of the bone made it easy to identify.  这块骨头的鞋状结构使其易于识别。
The orchid has a calceiform petal.  这株兰花有一个拖鞋状的花瓣。
Near Synonyms: slipper-shaped, shoe-shaped
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider shapes that are dissimilar to a slipper, e.g., spherical, cuboidal)
词性: adjective
