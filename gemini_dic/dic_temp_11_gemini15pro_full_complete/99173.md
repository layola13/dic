intercident:/ˈɪntərˌsɪdənt/| n. |事件，插曲|An event that is either unpleasant or unusual.|例句：There was a minor incident involving a spilled drink. 发生了一件饮料泼洒的小插曲。

例句：The police reported several incidents of vandalism over the weekend. 警方报告了周末发生的几起故意破坏事件。|近义词：event, occurrence, episode, happening|反义词:  None (Incidents are neutral; their opposites depend on their nature - e.g., peace for a violent incident)|词性: noun
