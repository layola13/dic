nonvoluble:/ˌnɒnˈvɒljʊb(ə)l/ | adj. | 不健谈的，沉默寡言的 | Not talkative; reticent. | He was a nonvoluble man, preferring to listen rather than speak. 他是个沉默寡言的人，喜欢倾听而不是说话。 |  She gave a nonvoluble nod, clearly uncomfortable with the topic. 她默默地点了点头，显然对这个话题感到不舒服。 | 近义词: reticent, taciturn, quiet, reserved | 反义词: talkative, loquacious, voluble, garrulous | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From non- + voluble (fluent and smooth in speech), from French or Latin volubilis "easily turned, rolling, flowing," from volvere "to roll, turn around."
