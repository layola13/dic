autonomism:/ɔːˈtɑːnəˌmɪzəm/
n.
自治主义；自主性
The belief in or practice of self-government; the right or condition of self-government, especially in a particular sphere.
Autonomism is a political philosophy that emphasizes the importance of individual and local autonomy. 自治主义是一种强调个人和地方自治重要性的政治哲学。
The region's autonomism has led to conflicts with the central government. 该地区的自治主义导致了与中央政府的冲突。
近义词: autonomy, independence, self-governance, self-determination
反义词: dependence, subordination, heteronomy
词性: noun
