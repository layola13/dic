consolidatory:/kənˈsɒlɪdəˌtɔːri/ /kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪˌtɔːri/| adj.|巩固的，加强的，团结的|Serving to consolidate; strengthening. |The government introduced consolidatory measures to strengthen the economy after the recession. 政府在经济衰退后采取了巩固措施来加强经济。|近义词：strengthening, reinforcing, uniting, combining|反义词:weakening, dividing, separating|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin consolidatorius, from Latin consolidare "to make solid," from con- "together" + solidare "to make solid," from solidus "solid."
