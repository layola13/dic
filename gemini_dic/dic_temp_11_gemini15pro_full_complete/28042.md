caparisoned:/kəˈpærɪs(ə)nd/
adjective
披着华丽马衣的；盛装打扮的
dressed in rich decorative coverings or clothes; richly ornamented
The knight's caparisoned warhorse pawed the ground impatiently.  骑士那披着华丽马衣的战马不耐烦地刨着地。
The general rode a magnificently caparisoned stallion. 将军骑着一匹装饰华丽的骏马。
近义词: adorned, decorated, ornamented, embellished
反义词: plain, simple, unadorned
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *caparasson* (Modern French *caparaçon*), from Spanish *caparazón* "large saddle cloth covering the horse's back," from *capara* "hood, cloak," perhaps of pre-Roman origin.
