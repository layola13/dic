detrimentalnesses:/ˌdɛtrɪˈmɛntəlnɪsɪz/ | n. | 损害性，有害性 | The state or quality of being detrimental; harmfulness.  | The detrimentalnesses of smoking are well documented. 吸烟的危害性已被充分证明。| 近义词: harmfulness, injuriousness, perniciousness | 反义词:  beneficialness, helpfulness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From detrimental + -ness.  Detrimental comes from the Late Latin *dētrimentālis, from dētrimentum "loss, damage," from dētērere "to rub away, wear off," from dē- "away" + terere "to rub."
