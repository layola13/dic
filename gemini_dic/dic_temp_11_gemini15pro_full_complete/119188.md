mezzotint:/ˌmetsəˈtɪnt/ | n. | 美柔汀；铜版雕刻 |  A method of engraving on copper or steel by scraping and burnishing a roughened surface to produce light and shade. Also, a print made using this technique. | The mezzotint captured the subtle gradations of light and shadow. 这幅美柔汀作品捕捉到了光影的微妙变化。| engraving, etching | --- | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *mezzotinto*, from *mezzo* (half) + *tinto* (tinted).  The technique creates a half-toned effect.
