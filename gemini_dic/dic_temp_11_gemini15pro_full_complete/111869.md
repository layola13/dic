logjamming:/ˈlɒɡˌdʒæmɪŋ/ /ˈlɑːɡˌdʒæmɪŋ/| n.| 木材堵塞；僵局|A situation resembling a logjam, especially one in which something is blocked or delayed.|The logjamming of timber in the river caused flooding upstream. 河里的木材堵塞导致上游洪水泛滥。|近义词：blockage, obstruction, deadlock, gridlock|反义词:flow, progress|noun

logjam:/ˈlɒɡˌdʒæm/ /ˈlɑːɡˌdʒæm/| n.| 木材堵塞；僵局|A mass of logs that have jammed together in a river or other waterway, obstructing passage.|The heavy rains caused a logjam in the narrow creek.  暴雨导致狭窄的小溪中木材堵塞。|近义词：blockage, obstruction, deadlock, gridlock|反义词:flow, progress|noun
