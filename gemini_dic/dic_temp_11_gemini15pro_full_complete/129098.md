nitsa: /ˈnɪtsə/ | n. |虱子 (nits的复数形式)|  Nitsa is the plural form of nit, referring to the eggs of lice. |  The child's hair was infested with nitsa.  这个孩子的头发里长满了虱子卵。 | 近义词: lice eggs | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle English *nite, from Old English hnitu, from Proto-Germanic *knutīz (compare West Frisian nit, Dutch neet, German Nisse “nit”), from Proto-Indo-European *kney- (“to scratch, rub”).
