lubriciously:/luːˈbrɪʃəsli/
adverb
淫荡地；下流地；光滑地
in a way that is sexually suggestive; lewdly or lasciviously; smoothly, slipperily.
She danced lubriciously, her movements intended to arouse. 她跳着淫荡的舞蹈，她的动作意在挑逗。
His lubriciously worded compliments made her uncomfortable. 他用淫词艳语恭维她，让她感到很不舒服。
近义词:lewdly, lasciviously, salaciously, wantonly
反义词:chastely, modestly, purely
词性:adverb

词源:
From Latin *lubricus* "slippery, smooth," from *lubricum* "slipperiness," related to *labor* "to slip, glide." The sexual sense developed in Late Latin.
