ablation:/əˈbleɪʃn/ | n. | 消融，切除，去除 | the surgical removal of body tissue or an organ |  The ablation of the tumor was successful. 肿瘤的切除手术很成功。 | synonyms: removal, excision, eradication | antonyms: addition, implantation, augmentation | noun

ablation:/əˈbleɪʃn/ | n. | 烧蚀 | the removal of material from the surface of an object by vaporization, chipping, or other erosive processes |  Laser ablation is used in various industrial applications. 激光烧蚀用于各种工业应用。| synonyms: erosion, vaporization | antonyms: deposition, accretion | noun
