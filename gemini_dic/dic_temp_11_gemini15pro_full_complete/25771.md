burdenless:/ˈbɜːrdənlɪs/
adjective
无负担的，轻松的
Free from burdens; without anything that causes worry or difficulty.
She felt burdenless after finishing her final exams. 她完成期末考试后感到轻松自在。
He walked with a burdenless stride. 他步伐轻快地走着。
近义词: carefree, untroubled, lighthearted
反义词: burdened, weighed down
词性: adjective

词源: burden (负担) + -less (无，没有)  The suffix "-less" is used to form adjectives meaning "without" or "lacking."
