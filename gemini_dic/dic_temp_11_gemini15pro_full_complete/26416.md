word property:  interjection

English explanation: "Byest" is a misspelling of "bye," which is a shortened, informal way of saying "goodbye."  It's likely a typo or a casual, perhaps humorous, variation.  It's not considered standard English.
Example: "Byest, gotta go!" 再见，得走了！
Synonyms: bye, goodbye, farewell, later, see ya
Antonyms: hello, hi, greetings
part of speech: interjection
