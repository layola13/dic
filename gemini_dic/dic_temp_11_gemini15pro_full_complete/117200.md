measurement:/ˈmeʒərmənt/ (US), /ˈmeʒəmənt/ (UK)| n.|测量；尺寸；测量值|The act or process of ascertaining the extent, dimensions, quantity, capacity, etc., of something.  A dimension, quantity, or capacity ascertained by measuring. |例句：The measurement of the room is 10 feet by 12 feet. 房间的尺寸是10英尺乘12英尺。

|例句：Accurate measurement is essential in scientific experiments.  在科学实验中，精确的测量至关重要。|近义词：dimension, size, extent, quantity|反义词: estimation, guess|词性:noun
