heun:/hɔɪn/ | n. | 海恩法; 海恩公式; 海恩方法 | A numerical method for solving ordinary differential equations. It is a predictor-corrector method that uses two evaluations of the derivative function per step. | Example: The Heun method is used to approximate the solution to the differential equation. 海恩法用于近似求解微分方程。 | Synonym: Improved Euler method, trapezoidal rule | Antonym: N/A | Noun
