fireweed:/ˈfaɪəˌwiːd/| n.|火烧草，柳兰|A tall plant with pink or purple flowers that often grows in areas that have been burned by fire.|例句：The fireweed quickly colonized the burned area. 火烧草迅速在烧毁的地区蔓延生长。

fireweed:/ˈfaɪəˌwiːd/| n.|火烧草，柳兰|A tall plant with pink or purple flowers that often grows in areas that have been burned by fire.|例句：Fireweed is a pioneer species, often the first to appear after a fire. 火烧草是一种先锋物种，通常是火灾后最早出现的。
