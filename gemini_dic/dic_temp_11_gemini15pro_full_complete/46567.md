dalmatica:/dælˈmætɪkə/ | n. | 达尔马提卡长袍 | A wide-sleeved tunic, especially one worn by deacons in some Christian churches. |  The deacon wore a beautifully embroidered dalmatica during the service.  执事在仪式中穿着一件精美刺绣的达尔马提卡长袍。|近义词:tunic, robe | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "everyday clothes") | 词性:noun

dalmatic:/dælˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 达尔马提亚的 | Relating to Dalmatia, a historical region on the eastern coast of the Adriatic Sea. | The dalmatic coast is known for its stunning scenery. 达尔马提亚海岸以其迷人的景色而闻名。 | 近义词:Dalmatian | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性:adjective


词源: From Late Latin *dalmatica vestis "Dalmatian garment," from Latin Dalmaticus "of Dalmatia".
