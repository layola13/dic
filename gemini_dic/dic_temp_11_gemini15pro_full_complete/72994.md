fools:/fuːlz/
n.|傻瓜，愚人|A person who acts unwisely or is easily tricked.|Those fools believed his lies. 那些傻瓜相信了他的谎言。|synonyms: idiots, morons, dunces|antonyms: geniuses, sages|noun

fools:/fuːlz/
v.|愚弄，欺骗|(third-person singular simple present fools, present participle fooling, simple past and past participle fooled)|To trick or deceive someone.|He fooled me into thinking he was rich. 他骗我以为他很有钱。|synonyms: deceives, tricks, dupes|antonyms: enlightens, informs|verb
