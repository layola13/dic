His overcautiousnesses prevented him from taking the necessary risks to succeed. 他过度谨慎，以至于不敢为了成功而去冒险。

词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + cautiousness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much."  "Cautiousness" is a noun derived from the adjective "cautious," meaning "careful to avoid potential problems or dangers." Ultimately, "cautious" comes from the Latin word "cautus," meaning "wary" or "prudent."
