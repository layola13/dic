cupboard-lovers:/ˈkʌbərd lʌvərz/ | n. |橱柜爱好者; 偷偷摸摸吃东西的人; |Someone who enjoys eating snacks or treats secretly, often from a cupboard.  This term often implies a slightly childish or indulgent behavior. | Example 1: He's a real cupboard-lover; I always catch him sneaking cookies late at night. 他真是个橱柜爱好者；我总是抓到他深夜偷吃饼干。

Example 2: My sister is such a cupboard-lover, she hides her favorite candies in the back of the pantry. 我妹妹真是个橱柜爱好者，她把她最喜欢的糖果藏在食品储藏室的后面。| 近义词: snacker, nibbler, secret eater | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered opposite of "dieter") dieter | 词性: noun
