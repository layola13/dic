certain:/ˈsɜːrtn/ (US) /ˈsɜːtən/ (UK)| adj. pron.|确定的，肯定的；某，某些|Fixed, settled; without doubt; specific but not explicitly named or stated.|例句：It is certain that he will come. 他肯定会来。|近义词：sure, definite, positive, specific|反义词：uncertain, doubtful, unsure|词性:形容词

certain:/ˈsɜːrtn/ (US) /ˈsɜːtən/ (UK)| pron.|某些，若干|Some; a certain number or amount.|例句：Certain people disagree with this plan. 某些人不同意这个计划。|近义词：some, several|反义词：none, all|词性:代词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *certein* (Modern French certain), from Latin *certus* "settled, fixed, sure, resolved," from PIE root *ker- (1) "hard."
