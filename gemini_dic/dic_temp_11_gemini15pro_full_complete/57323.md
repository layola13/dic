downtrends:/ˈdaʊntrendz/
noun
下降趋势
A downward trend in something is a general tendency for it to decrease in number, amount, or quality.
The latest economic data confirms the downtrend in consumer spending. 最新的经济数据证实了消费者支出呈下降趋势。
synonyms: decline, decrease, fall, slump
antonyms: uptrend, increase, rise
词性: 名词
