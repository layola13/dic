discorded:/dɪˈskɔːrdɪd/
adjective|不一致的，不和谐的，冲突的|Characterized by conflict, disagreement, or disharmony; out of harmony.|The discorded notes of the untuned piano grated on my ears.  走音钢琴不和谐的音符刺激着我的耳朵。|Synonyms:  discordant, inharmonious, conflicting, clashing, jarring, divergent, disagreeing|Antonyms: harmonious, concordant, agreeable, consistent, unified|adjective

discorded:/dɪˈskɔːrdɪd/
verb (past participle)|使不一致，使不和谐|(past participle of discord) To cause disagreement or conflict; to put out of harmony.|Their views on the project were discorded from the start.  他们对这个项目的看法从一开始就不一致。|Synonyms:  disunited, estranged, alienated|Antonyms: united, reconciled, harmonized|verb
