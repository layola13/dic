chisleu: /ˈtʃɪzluː/ (This pronunciation is a guess based on likely Romanian pronunciation, as the word appears to be of Romanian origin) | n. | 凿子; 錾子 | A chisel is a metal tool with a sharpened edge, used for shaping or cutting stone, wood, or metal. | Example: The sculptor used a chisel to carve the intricate details of the statue. 雕塑家用凿子雕刻雕像的复杂细节。

Example:  He used a chisel to remove the old grout from between the tiles. 他用凿子去除瓷砖间的旧水泥浆。


近义词: graver, burin, gouge | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源:  Likely from Romanian "daltă," meaning "chisel."  Romanian is a Romance language, and "daltă" is believed to be related to the Latin word "dolabra," meaning "axe" or "mattock."
