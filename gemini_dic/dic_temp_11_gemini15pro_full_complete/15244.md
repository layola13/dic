backgammon:/ˈbækˌɡæmən/| n.|西洋双陆棋|A board game for two players, played by moving pieces according to the throw of dice and capturing or blocking the opponent's pieces.|例句：We played backgammon all afternoon. 我们整个下午都在玩西洋双陆棋。

例句：She beat me at backgammon. 她玩西洋双陆棋赢了我。|近义词：board game, table game|反义词: None|词性:noun
