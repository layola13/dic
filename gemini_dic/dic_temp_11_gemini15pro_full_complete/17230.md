sawhorse:/ˈsɔːhɔːrs/| n.|锯木架，支架|A sawhorse is a four-legged frame used to support a board or plank, typically for sawing or other woodworking.|I set the plank across two sawhorses to cut it.|近义词：trestle, support, stand|反义词:|noun
