doublethink:/ˈdʌbəlθɪŋk/ | n. | 双重思想 | The acceptance or mental capacity to accept contrary opinions or beliefs at the same time, especially as a result of political indoctrination.  |  The Party's control over language and thought through doublethink is absolute.  党的通过双重思想对语言和思想的控制是绝对的。 | Near synonyms:  contradiction, hypocrisy, cognitive dissonance,  | Antonyms:  consistency, logical thinking, clarity | noun
