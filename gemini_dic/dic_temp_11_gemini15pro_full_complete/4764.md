alfresco:/ælˈfrɛskoʊ/ (US) /ælˈfreskəʊ/ (UK)
adjective
户外用餐的；露天；在户外
in the open air; outdoors.
例句：We dined alfresco on the patio. 我们在露台上露天用餐。

例句：Alfresco dining is popular in the summer. 夏天户外用餐很受欢迎。

近义词：outdoors, outside, open-air
反义词：indoors, inside

词性：adjective


alfresco:/ælˈfrɛskoʊ/ (US) /ælˈfreskəʊ/ (UK)
adverb
在户外；在露天
in the open air; outdoors.
例句：We ate alfresco last night. 我们昨晚在户外用餐。

例句：They held the concert alfresco. 他们在户外举办了音乐会。

近义词：outdoors, outside, open-air
反义词：indoors, inside

词性：adverb

词源: From Italian *a fresco* "in the fresh (air)," from *a* "to, at" + *fresco* "fresh, cool."
