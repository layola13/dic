ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessive) + "preoccupation" (the state of being preoccupied).  "Preoccupation" comes from the Latin "praeoccupatio," from "praeoccupare"  (to seize beforehand), from "prae-" (before) + "occupare" (to occupy).
