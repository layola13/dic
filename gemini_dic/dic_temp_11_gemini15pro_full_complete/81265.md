granitoids: /ˈɡrænɪˌtɔɪdz/
| n. |花岗岩类；类花岗岩|A group of igneous, crystalline-grained rocks similar to granite in composition and origin, including granite, granodiorite, tonalite, and quartz monzonite. |Granitoids are the most abundant igneous rock type in the continental crust. 花岗岩类是大陆地壳中最丰富的火成岩类型。|The geologist studied the various granitoids in the region. 地质学家研究了该地区的各种花岗岩类。|Synonyms: plutonic rocks, intrusive rocks|Antonyms: volcanic rocks, extrusive rocks|noun
