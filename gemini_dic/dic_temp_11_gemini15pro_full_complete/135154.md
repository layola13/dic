nononerousness:/ˌnɒnˈəʊnərəsnəs/
n.|轻松，不繁重|The nononerousness of the task made it enjoyable.  任务的轻松使其变得愉快。|例句：The nononerousness of the job appealed to him. 这份工作的轻松对他很有吸引力。|近义词：easiness, simplicity, lightness|反义词：onerousness, burden, difficulty|词性:名词
Etymology:  From non- + onerous + -ness.  "Onerous" comes from Latin "onerosus" meaning "burdensome," from "onus" meaning "burden."  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning.
