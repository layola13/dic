lucky: /ˈlʌki/ (US) /ˈlʌki/ (UK)| adj.|幸运的，侥幸的|Having or bringing good luck; fortunate.|例句：He was lucky to escape the fire. 他很幸运地逃脱了火灾。|近义词：fortunate, auspicious, blessed|反义词：unlucky, unfortunate, hapless|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *lukky*, from lukke (“luck, fortune”) +‎ -y.  The word "luck" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *lukōn (“to close, lock”), the idea being that fate is "locked in".
