apposition:/ˌæpəˈzɪʃən/
n.
同位语
A grammatical construction in which two noun phrases are placed side by side, with one identifying or explaining the other.
My best friend, Sarah, is a doctor. 我的最好的朋友，莎拉，是一位医生。
Shakespeare, a famous playwright, wrote many plays.  莎士比亚，一位著名的剧作家，写了很多戏剧。
Synonyms: juxtaposition, equivalence, coordination
Antonyms: disjunction, separation
noun
