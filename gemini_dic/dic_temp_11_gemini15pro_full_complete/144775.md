over-emoting: /ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度表达情感
Expressing emotions in an excessive or exaggerated way.
She was over-emoting during the play, and it felt unnatural.  她在戏剧中过度表达情感，感觉很不自然。
He tends to over-emoting when he's tired. 他累的时候容易情绪过度。
近义词: overacting, exaggerating, dramatizing
反义词: understating, downplaying
词性: verb (present participle)
