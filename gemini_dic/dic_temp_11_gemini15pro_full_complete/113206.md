lurky:/ˈlɜːrki/| adj.| 潜伏的，鬼鬼祟祟的，令人毛骨悚然的| Appearing or behaving in a way that suggests someone is planning something unpleasant or harmful; shifty.| The man gave a lurky smile that made me uneasy.  那个男人露出鬼鬼祟祟的笑容，让我感到不安。 |  creepy, shady, shifty, suspicious, furtive | open, honest, trustworthy | adjective
词源:  Probably from lurk + -y.  可能源自 lurk（潜伏） + -y（形容词后缀）。
