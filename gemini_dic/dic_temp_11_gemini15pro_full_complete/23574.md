braising:/ˈbreɪzɪŋ/| v. | 焖；炖；|To cook (meat or vegetables) by browning in fat and then simmering in liquid.|例句：She braised the beef in red wine for three hours. 她用红酒炖牛肉三个小时。|例句：Braising the short ribs made them incredibly tender.  炖小排让它们变得非常嫩。|近义词：stewing, simmering|反义词：roasting, grilling|词性:动词

braise:/breɪz/| n. | 焖菜；炖菜；|A dish of meat or vegetables braised.|例句：The lamb braise was the highlight of the meal. 羊肉焖菜是这顿饭的亮点。|例句：He prepared a hearty beef braise for dinner. 他准备了一份丰盛的炖牛肉作为晚餐。|近义词：stew|反义词：roast|词性:名词
