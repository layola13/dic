nondeflationary:/ˌnɒnˌdɪˈfleɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 非通货紧缩的 | Not deflationary; characterized by stable or rising prices.  Relating to or denoting economic conditions characterized by a stable price level or by inflation. |  The government is pursuing nondeflationary policies to stimulate economic growth. 政府正在推行非通货紧缩政策以刺激经济增长。 | The central bank implemented nondeflationary measures to prevent a recession. 中央银行实施了非通货紧缩措施以防止经济衰退。 | Synonyms: inflationary, expansionary | Antonyms: deflationary, contractionary | adjective
