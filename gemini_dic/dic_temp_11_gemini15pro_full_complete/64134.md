equable:/ˈiːkwəbəl/ | adj. | 平静的；稳定的；不变的 | Not easily disturbed or angered; calm and even-tempered;  Characterized by lack of variation or change; uniform. | The teacher has an equable temper and rarely gets angry. 老师脾气温和，很少生气。

He lives in an area with an equable climate. 他住在一个气候温和的地区。 | 近义词：calm, composed, serene, steady, uniform, consistent | 反义词：volatile, erratic, fluctuating, changeable, uneven | 词性: adjective

Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin aequabilis, from Latin aequare ‘make equal’, from aequus ‘equal’.
