manoeuvre:/məˈnuːvər/ (UK) /məˈnuːvər/ (US)| n. v.|策略，调动，操纵|A movement or series of moves requiring skill and care.|例句：The driver performed a skillful manoeuvre to avoid the accident. 司机熟练地操作以避免事故。

例句：The company's latest manoeuvre is to buy out its biggest competitor. 该公司最新的策略是收购其最大的竞争对手。|近义词：tactic, strategy, operation, maneuver (US)|反义词:inaction, stillness|词性:名词,动词

manoeuvre:/məˈnuːvər/ (UK) /məˈnuːvər/ (US)| v.|操纵，调动，策划|To move skillfully or carefully.|例句：She managed to manoeuvre the car into a tight parking space. 她设法把车停在一个狭小的停车位里。

例句：He's trying to manoeuvre himself into a position of power. 他正试图把自己操纵到一个有权势的地位。|近义词：manipulate, navigate, steer, guide|反义词:fail, neglect|词性:动词
