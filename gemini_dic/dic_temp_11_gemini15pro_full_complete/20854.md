blatant:/ˈbleɪtnt/| adj.|公然的；明目张胆的；喧嚣的| done openly and unashamedly, especially in a way that shows no respect or sensitivity for others; offensively noisy and loud.|例句：a blatant lie  一个弥天大谎|近义词：flagrant, obvious, unconcealed, glaring, overt, conspicuous, shameless, brazen|反义词：subtle, inconspicuous, discreet, veiled|词性:形容词

blatant:/ˈbleɪtnt/| adj.|喧闹的；俗丽的；炫耀的|(of noise) offensively loud and insistent.|例句：blatant pop music  喧嚣的流行音乐|近义词：loud, noisy, clamorous, boisterous, raucous|反义词：quiet, soft, subdued|词性:形容词
