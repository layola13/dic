iterative:/ˈɪtərətɪv/ (US), /ˈɪtərətɪv/ (UK)| adj.|迭代的，重复的|Marked by or involving repetition or reiteration, typically as a means of approaching a desired goal or target.|The software development process was iterative, with each version building upon the previous one. 软件开发过程是迭代的，每个版本都建立在前一个版本的基础上。

The iterative design process allowed for constant feedback and improvement. 迭代设计过程允许持续的反馈和改进。|近义词：repetitive, recursive, cyclical|反义词：non-iterative, linear, one-time|词性: adjective
