overanimations: /ˌoʊvərˌænɪˈmeɪʃənz/
n.
过度动画; 过度活跃
Excessive or exaggerated animations, often appearing unnatural or frantic.
The character's overanimations made the scene look comical rather than dramatic.  这个角色的过度动画让场景看起来很滑稽，而不是戏剧化。
The overanimations in the low-budget cartoon were distracting. 这部低成本动画片中过度的动画效果让人分心。
近义词: exaggerations, overacting
反义词: understatements, subtlety
词性: 名词

词源:
"Over-" (prefix) + "animation" + "-s" (plural suffix)
"Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much."
"Animation" derives from the Latin "animare," meaning "to give life to."
