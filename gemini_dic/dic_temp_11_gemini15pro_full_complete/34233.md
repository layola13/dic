clanless:/ˈklæn.ləs/| adj.|无氏族的，无家族的，没有归属感的|Without a clan; not belonging to a clan; lacking the support and connection of a family or similar group.  Figuratively, it can mean lacking a sense of belonging or community.|He felt clanless and alone in the bustling city. 他在这个繁华的城市感到孤立无援，没有归属感。|Synonyms: outcast, isolated, alienated, rootless, adrift|Antonyms: affiliated, connected, rooted, belonging|adjective
