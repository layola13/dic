microvillous:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈvɪləs/| adj.|微绒毛的|Covered with microvilli; having the character of microvilli.  Relating to or consisting of microvilli, which are tiny hairlike projections on the surface of certain cells, especially those lining the small intestine, that increase the surface area for absorption.|The microvillous border of the small intestine is essential for nutrient absorption.|近义词：villous|反义词: smooth|词性: adjective
