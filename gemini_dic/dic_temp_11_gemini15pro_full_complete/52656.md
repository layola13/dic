digitation:/ˌdɪdʒɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |指状突起；指状分裂；手指状 |A finger-like or finger-shaped projection or division. |例句：The leaves of some ferns exhibit digitation. 一些蕨类植物的叶子呈现指状分裂。

例句：The surgeon carefully examined the digitation of the patient's hand. 外科医生仔细检查了病人手的指状突起。|近义词：process, projection, lobe |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "smoothness" in certain contexts) |词性: noun


Etymology->
From Latin *digitātiō*, from digitātus (“having fingers”), from digitus (“finger”).
