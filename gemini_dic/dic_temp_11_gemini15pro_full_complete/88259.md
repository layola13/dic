hinges:/ˈhɪndʒɪz/ (US), /ˈhɪndʒɪz/ (UK)| n. |铰链;关键;枢纽|A piece of metal or other material that fastens two objects together, typically a door and its frame, allowing one to swing open or closed on the other.|The door hinges are rusty. 门的铰链生锈了。|Synonyms: joints, pivots, connectors|Antonyms: separations, disconnections|noun

hinges:/ˈhɪndʒɪz/ (US), /ˈhɪndʒɪz/ (UK)| v. (hinges, hinging, hinged) |取决于;以...为转移|To depend entirely on.|The success of the project hinges on funding.  这个项目的成功取决于资金。|Synonyms: depends, rests, pivots|Antonyms:  is independent of, is unaffected by|verb
