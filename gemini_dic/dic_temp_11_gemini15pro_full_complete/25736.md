buoyancy:/ˈbɔɪənsi/ | n. | 浮力，轻松的心情，恢复力 | The ship's buoyancy kept it afloat.  这艘船的浮力使它漂浮着。 | The buoyancy of the housing market is encouraging. 房地产市场的繁荣令人鼓舞。 | Synonyms: lightness, floatability, resilience, cheerfulness | Antonyms: heaviness, density, depression, despair | noun

Etymology:
From Middle French *boiancie, from boiant (“floating”), present participle of boier (“to float”), from Vulgar Latin *boiare, from Late Latin boia (“buoy”), from Greek βύος (búos, “ox hide”).
