briquetting:/brɪˈkɛtɪŋ/
n.
压块，制成煤球
The process of forming fine material into briquettes.
The briquetting of coal dust reduces dust pollution and makes transportation easier.  煤尘压块减少了粉尘污染，并使运输更容易。
compaction, compression, agglomeration
-
noun

briquette:/brɪˈkɛt/
n.
煤球，煤饼
A block of compressed coal dust or other combustible material.
We use briquettes in our fireplace because they burn longer than logs. 我们在壁炉里使用煤球，因为它们比木柴燃烧的时间更长。
coal cake, compressed block, fuel block
-
noun
