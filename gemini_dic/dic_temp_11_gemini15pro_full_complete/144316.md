outsprings:/ˈaʊtˌsprɪŋz/
n.
泉水，源泉;
A place where water springs forth from the earth; a source.
The outsprings of the river are high in the mountains. 这条河的源头在高山上。
synonyms: source, spring, fountainhead, wellspring, origin
antonyms: mouth, end, terminus
noun
