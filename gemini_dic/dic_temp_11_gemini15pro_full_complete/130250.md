non-specific:/ˌnɒn spəˈsɪfɪk/ /ˌnɑːn spəˈsɪfɪk/| adj.|非特异性的，非具体的|Not relating to a particular thing or aimed at a specific target; general.|The symptoms are non-specific and could indicate a number of different illnesses. 症状是非特异性的，可能表明许多不同的疾病。|The doctor prescribed a non-specific antibiotic. 医生开了一种非特异性抗生素。|近义词：general, unspecific, broad, vague|反义词：specific, particular, precise, definite|词性:形容词
词源:non- (prefix meaning "not") + specific (from Latin *species*, meaning "kind, sort")
