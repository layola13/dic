impluvium:/ɪmˈpluːviəm/
n.|蓄水池，天井|A rectangular basin in the floor of the atrium in a Roman house, to receive rainwater from the roof.|The impluvium collected rainwater, which was then stored for household use.  罗马房屋中庭地板上的一个长方形盆地，用于接收屋顶的雨水。|Synonyms: basin, cistern, reservoir|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "roof" or "gutter" in terms of function)|noun
