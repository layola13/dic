murk:/mɜːrk/ /mɝːk/| n. |黑暗; 阴暗; 昏暗; 浑浊; 污秽; 隐晦; 含糊不清|darkness or thick mist that makes it difficult to see; something that is not clear and is difficult to understand|The boat disappeared into the murk. 船消失在黑暗中。|近义词：darkness, gloom, obscurity, haze, fog, muddiness|反义词: clarity, brightness, light|名词

murk:/mɜːrk/ /mɝːk/| v. |使变暗; 使模糊|to make something dark or difficult to see clearly|The fog murked the coastline. 雾使海岸线变得模糊不清。|近义词：obscure, darken, cloud|反义词: clarify, illuminate, brighten|动词

murky:/ˈmɜːrki/ /ˈmɝːki/| adj. |黑暗的; 昏暗的; 浑浊的; 污秽的; 隐晦的; 含糊不清的|dark and dirty, or difficult to see through; not clearly expressed or understood|The water was murky and full of debris. 水浑浊，满是碎屑。|近义词：dark, gloomy, obscure, hazy, foggy, muddy|反义词: clear, bright, transparent|形容词
