endable:/ˈɛndəbl/
adjective
可结束的，可终止的
capable of being ended or terminated.
The contract is endable with 30 days' notice.  这份合同可以提前30天通知终止。
This pain is finally endable. 这种痛苦终于可以结束了。
近义词:terminable, concludable
反义词:interminable, perpetual
词性:adjective
