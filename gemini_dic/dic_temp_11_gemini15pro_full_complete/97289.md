influxes:/ˈɪnflʌksɪz/| n.|涌入；流入；汇集|A sudden arrival or increase of large numbers of people or things.|例句：The country experienced a large influx of tourists during the summer months. 这个国家在夏季经历了大量游客的涌入。

例句：There has been a recent influx of new ideas into the company. 最近公司涌现了许多新想法。|近义词：inflow, arrival, rush, surge, flood|反义词：outflow, exodus, decline|noun
