mildest:/ˈmaɪldɪst/| adj.|最温和的，最轻微的|The mildest winter on record.  有记录以来最暖和的冬天。|例句：This is the mildest curry on the menu. 这是菜单上最不辣的咖喱。|例句：She gave him the mildest of rebukes. 她只是稍微责备了他一下。|近义词：gentlest, calmest, softest|反义词：harshest, strongest, severest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *myldest, superlative of *mylde, from Old English milde (“mild, gentle, kind, merciful; moderate, temperate, not severe or rigorous”), from Proto-Germanic *mildijaz (“soft, mild”), from Proto-Indo-European *meldʰ- (“soft, mild; to melt”). Cognate with Dutch mild (“mild”), German mild (“mild”), Swedish mild (“mild”).
