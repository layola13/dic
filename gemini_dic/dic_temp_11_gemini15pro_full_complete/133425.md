nonfarcical:/ˌnɒnˈfɑːsɪk(ə)l/
adjective
非闹剧的; 严肃的
Not farcical; serious or realistic.
The play offered a nonfarcical portrayal of political intrigue.  这出戏对政治阴谋进行了非闹剧式的刻画。
The documentary presented a nonfarcical account of the war. 这部纪录片对战争进行了严肃的描述。
Synonyms: serious, realistic, genuine, authentic, sober, earnest
Antonyms: farcical, comical, ludicrous, absurd, ridiculous
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From non- (not) + farcical (from farce, meaning a light dramatic work in which highly improbable plot situations, exaggerated characters, and often slapstick elements are combined for humorous effect).  Ultimately from Latin farcire "to stuff."
