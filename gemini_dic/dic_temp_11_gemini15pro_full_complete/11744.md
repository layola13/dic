aromatics:/ˌærəˈmætɪks/
n.|芳香剂；芳香物质；芳烃|Substances or compounds that have a pleasant smell, especially those used in perfumes and flavorings. Also refers to organic compounds with a benzene ring. |The aromatics in the wine gave it a complex bouquet. 葡萄酒中的芳香物质使其具有复杂的香味。|synonyms: fragrances, perfumes, scents, spices|antonyms: odors, stenches|noun

aromatic:/ˌærəˈmætɪk/
adj.|芳香的；芬芳的|Having a pleasant and distinctive smell.|The aromatic spices filled the kitchen with a warm, inviting scent. 芬芳的香料使厨房充满了温暖诱人的香味。|synonyms: fragrant, perfumed, scented, spicy|antonyms: odorless, fetid, stinky|adjective
