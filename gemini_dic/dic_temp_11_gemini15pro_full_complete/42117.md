cotting:/ˈkɒtɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈkɑːtɪŋ/ (US)| n.|小屋，小别墅；棚屋; (苏格兰) 小农场|A small house, cottage, or shed; (Scotland) a small farm. |The old cotting by the river was charming. 河边的小屋很迷人。|Synonyms: cottage, cabin, hut, shed|Antonyms: mansion, palace|noun

cotting:/ˈkɒtɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈkɑːtɪŋ/ (US)| n.|棉籽饼|The residue of cotton seeds after the oil has been extracted, used as animal feed.|The farmer used cotting to supplement the cows' diet. 农民用棉籽饼补充奶牛的饮食。|Synonyms: cottonseed cake, cottonseed meal|Antonyms: N/A|noun
