hulloes:/həˈloʊz/ | n. | 呼喊声，叫喊声 | A loud shout or cry, often used to attract attention. |  The hulloes of the children echoed through the valley. 孩子们的叫喊声在山谷中回荡。| synonyms: shouts, cries, yells | antonyms: whispers, murmurs | noun

hullo:/həˈloʊ/ | int. n. v. | 你好；喂 | Used as a greeting or to express surprise.  Can also be a noun meaning a greeting. As a verb it means to greet someone. | "Hullo," she said, surprised to see him. “你好，”她说道，很惊讶地看到他。| synonyms: hello, hi, hey | antonyms: goodbye, farewell | interjection, noun, verb
