forsythia: /fɔːrˈsɪθiə/ | n. |连翘；金钟花|A shrub with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. | The forsythia bushes are in full bloom, heralding the arrival of spring. 连翘盛开，预示着春天的到来。| synonym: golden bells | antonym: N/A | noun

forsythia: /fɔːrˈsɪθiə/ | adj. |连翘色的；金钟花色的 | Having the bright yellow color of forsythia flowers. |  She wore a forsythia-colored dress to the spring festival. 她穿着一件连翘色的连衣裙去参加春季节。 | synonym:  primrose yellow, buttercup yellow | antonym: N/A | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after William Forsyth (1737-1804), a Scottish botanist.
