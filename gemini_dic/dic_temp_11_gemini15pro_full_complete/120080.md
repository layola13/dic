miltonic:/mɪlˈtɒnɪk/ | adj. |弥尔顿式的；像弥尔顿的；具有弥尔顿风格的 |Of or relating to the English poet John Milton or his works, especially Paradise Lost, characterized by sublimity of style and subject, loftiness of thought, and grandeur of expression.  |  His style is distinctly Miltonic, echoing the epic grandeur of Paradise Lost. 他的风格明显是弥尔顿式的，与《失乐园》的史诗般的宏伟相呼应。

The poem's Miltonic language and themes evoke a sense of awe and wonder. 这首诗的弥尔顿式语言和主题唤起了一种敬畏和惊奇的感觉。 | 近义词：epic, grand, sublime, majestic | 反义词：simple, plain, humble, colloquial | 词性: adjective
