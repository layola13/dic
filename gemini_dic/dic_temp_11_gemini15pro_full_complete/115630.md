marching:/ˈmɑːrtʃɪŋ/ (US) /ˈmɑːtʃɪŋ/ (UK)| n. adj.|行进，行军；游行示威|The act of walking with regular steps, especially in a group, often with music.  It can also refer to a continuous series of something.|例句：The soldiers were marching in perfect formation. 士兵们正以完美的队形行进。|近义词：walking, parading, processing, advancing, |反义词: retreating, halting, standing.
词性: 名词
marching:/ˈmɑːrtʃɪŋ/ (US) /ˈmɑːtʃɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|行进的，游行的|Relating to or involving marching.|例句：We heard the marching band approaching. 我们听到了行进乐队的临近声。|近义词：advancing, progressing|反义词: retreating, static.
词性: 形容词


词源: From Middle English *marchen, from Old French marcher (“to walk, tread, trample”), of Germanic origin.
