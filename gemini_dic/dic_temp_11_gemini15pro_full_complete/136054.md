nonrecalcitrancy:/ˌnɒnˌrɪkælˈsɪtrənsi/
noun
不倔强，不反抗，顺从
The state or quality of not being recalcitrant; obedience, compliance.
His nonrecalcitrancy made him an ideal student. 他的不倔强使他成为一个理想的学生。
nonresistance, compliance, obedience, tractability, acquiescence
recalcitrance, defiance, resistance, disobedience, obstinacy
n.
