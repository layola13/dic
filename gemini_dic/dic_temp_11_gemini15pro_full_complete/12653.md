assegaied: /ˈæsɪɡaɪd/ | adj. | 被投矛刺伤的; 用投矛攻击的 | Pierced or attacked with an assegai (a slender, iron-tipped spear). |  The assegaied warrior fell to the ground.  被投矛刺伤的战士倒在了地上。 |  The lion was assegaied by the hunters. 狮子被猎人们用投矛刺伤了。 | 近义词: speared, stabbed | 反义词: unharmed, untouched | 词性: 形容词

词源: From assegai + -ed.  Assegai comes from the Berber tazagayt, borrowed into Arabic as azzaghaya, and thence into Portuguese and ultimately English.
