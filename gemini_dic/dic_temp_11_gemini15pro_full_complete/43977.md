cries:/kraɪz/| n. v.| 哭泣，叫喊，呼喊 |An act of crying; a fit of weeping; a loud utterance or outcry; a shout; a yell. | The baby's cries woke me up. 婴儿的哭声把我吵醒了。 | 近义词: weeps, sobs, shouts, yells | 反义词: laughs, cheers | 词性: 名词(plural), 动词(third-person singular simple present)

cries:/kraɪz/| v.| 哭泣，叫喊，呼喊 |To shed tears, typically as an expression of distress, pain, or sorrow; to utter a loud inarticulate sound or shout, typically from pain, grief, or anger. | She cries every time she watches that movie. 她每次看那部电影都会哭。 | 近义词: weeps, sobs, shouts, yells | 反义词: laughs, cheers | 词性: 动词(third-person singular simple present)


词源: From Middle English *crien, from Old French crier, from Latin quiritare (“to shriek, scream, wail”), perhaps ultimately of imitative origin.
