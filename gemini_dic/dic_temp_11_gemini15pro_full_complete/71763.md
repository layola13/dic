flea:/fliː/
n.
跳蚤
A small, wingless, bloodsucking insect that lives on animals and humans.
The dog was scratching constantly because it had fleas.  这只狗不停地挠痒，因为它身上有跳蚤。
近义词：parasite
反义词: None
