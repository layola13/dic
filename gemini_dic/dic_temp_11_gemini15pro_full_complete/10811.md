aqueousness:/ˈeɪkwiəsnəs/
n.
水性，含水性，水溶性
The state or quality of being aqueous or containing water.
The aqueousness of the solution allowed it to dissolve the salt.  这种溶液的水性使其能够溶解盐。
近义词: wateriness, liquidity
反义词: dryness, aridity
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Latin *aquosus (“watery”), from aqua (“water”).
