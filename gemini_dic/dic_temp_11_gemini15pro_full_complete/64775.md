ESG:/iːɛsˈdʒiː/ | n. | 环境、社会和治理 | Environmental, Social, and Governance.  A set of standards for a company’s operations that socially conscious investors use to screen potential investments. |  Many investors are now considering ESG factors when making investment decisions. 许多投资者现在在做投资决策时都会考虑ESG因素。 |  Synonyms: sustainability, corporate social responsibility (CSR), responsible investing | Antonyms: unethical investing, unsustainable practices | noun
