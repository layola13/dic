"Non-" negates "subtle," meaning not delicate, understated, or nuanced.  "Subtle" itself comes from the Latin "subtilis," meaning "fine, delicate."  The "-est" ending marks it as a superlative adjective.| Her nonsubtlest hint was practically shouting the answer. 她最明显的提示几乎就是在喊答案。|  The advertising campaign was nonsubtlest, with its garish colors and loud music.  这广告宣传活动一点也不微妙，色彩艳丽，音乐喧闹。|Synonyms: obvious, blatant, overt, transparent, conspicuous, pronounced| Antonyms: subtle, nuanced, understated, delicate, discreet, inconspicuous | adjective
