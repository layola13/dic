lustrate:/ˈlʌstreɪt/| v. | 净化，使洁净；照亮；（古罗马）举行赎罪仪式 | To purify, especially ritually; to make clear or bright; (in ancient Rome) to perform a ceremony of purification. | The priest lustrated the temple with holy water.  牧师用圣水净化了寺庙。

The rising sun lustrated the valley.  冉冉升起的太阳照亮了山谷。| 近义词: purify, cleanse, sanctify, illuminate | 反义词: defile, pollute, contaminate | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Latin *lūstrāre*, frequentative of *lūstrāre* "to purify, enlighten," from *lūstrum* "purification."
