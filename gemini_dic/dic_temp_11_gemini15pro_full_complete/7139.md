angelic:/ænˈdʒɛlɪk/
adjective
天使般的；似天使的；天国的
Resembling or characteristic of an angel, especially in beauty, purity, or innocence.
She has an angelic face and a sweet voice. 她有一张天使般的脸庞和甜美的声音。

Her angelic smile made everyone feel at peace. 她天使般的微笑让每个人都感到平静。


近义词:divine, heavenly, seraphic, ethereal, pure, innocent
反义词:demonic, devilish, fiendish, wicked, evil
词性:adjective
