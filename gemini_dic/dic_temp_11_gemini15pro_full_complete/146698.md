overheavily: /ˌoʊvərˈhɛvɪli/ | adv. | 过重地，负担过重地 | excessively heavily; to an excessive degree | The company was overheavily indebted.  这家公司负债过重。| The market is overheavily reliant on government spending.  这个市场过分依赖政府支出。| 近义词: excessively, unduly, overly | 反义词: lightly, moderately | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer) meaning "above, beyond, excessively" + "heavy" (Old English *hefig) meaning "having great weight" + "-ly" (Old English *-lice) adverbial suffix.
