analogist:/əˈnælədʒɪst/
n.
类比者，类推者
A person who makes analogies or uses analogical reasoning.
She's a skilled analogist, able to draw insightful comparisons between seemingly disparate concepts. 她是一位熟练的类比者，能够在看似不同的概念之间进行深刻的比较。
analogizer, comparer
differentiator
