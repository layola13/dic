nonadvocate:/ˌnɒnˈædvəkət/ | n. | 非倡导者；不提倡者 |  A person who does not advocate or support a particular cause or policy.  Someone who doesn't actively promote or speak in favor of something. |  He was a nonadvocate of the proposed tax increase. 他不提倡拟议中的增税。|  non-supporter, opponent | advocate, supporter | noun
