cutlets:/ˈkʌtlɪts/| n. |炸肉排，肉饼|A small, thin piece of meat, usually veal or lamb, that has been flattened and often coated in breadcrumbs before being fried or grilled.  A flat croquette of minced meat, fish, or vegetables.|例句：We had veal cutlets for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了小牛肉饼。|例句：She prepared fish cutlets with a lemon sauce. 她准备了柠檬酱鱼饼。|近义词：chop, patty, escalope |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "roast" as a contrasting cooking method)|noun
