dislimn: /dɪsˈlɪm/ | v. |  抹去, 使模糊;  使轮廓不清 | To obscure or blur the outlines of something; to make less distinct or clear. | The fog dislimned the distant mountains.  浓雾使远山变得模糊不清。 The setting sun dislimned the edges of the buildings. 落日使建筑物的边缘变得模糊。 | 近义词: obscure, blur, efface, obliterate | 反义词: illuminate, clarify, define, sharpen | 词性: 动词
