harmotome:/ˈhɑːrmətoʊm/ | n. | 交沸石 |A zeolite mineral, a hydrated barium aluminum silicate.  It occurs as white, yellow, pink, or brown crystals, often twinned. | Harmotome crystals are prized by collectors for their unique cross-shaped formations. 交沸石晶体因其独特的十字形结构而受到收藏家的珍视。| The harmotome specimen displayed a classic cruciform twinning.  展出的交沸石标本呈现出典型的十字形孪晶。 | synonyms:  cross-stone (archaic),  morpholine (imprecise) | antonyms: N/A | noun
