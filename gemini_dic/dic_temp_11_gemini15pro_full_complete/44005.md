criminally:/ˈkrɪmɪnəli/| adv.| 犯罪地；罪恶地；可耻地|In a criminal manner; excessively or outrageously bad.|He was criminally negligent in his duties. 他玩忽职守，罪责难逃。|近义词：illegally, unlawfully, wrongly, wickedly, sinfully|反义词：legally, lawfully, righteously|词性：副词

词源: From crime + -al + -ly.  Ultimately from the Latin word "crimen" meaning "accusation, charge, crime."
