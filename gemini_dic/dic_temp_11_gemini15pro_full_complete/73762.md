formable:/ˈfɔːrməbl/ | adj. | 可成形的，可塑的，易成型的 | Capable of being formed or molded into a particular shape. |  The clay is soft and formable.  这种粘土很软，易成形。| malleable, pliable, ductile | rigid, inflexible | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *formable, from Old French formable, from Latin formābilis (“capable of being formed”), from formō (“I form, shape, fashion, build, make”) + -ābilis.
