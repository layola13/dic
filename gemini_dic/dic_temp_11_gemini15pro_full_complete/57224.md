downfalls: /ˈdaʊnfɔːlz/
n.
衰败，垮台；缺点；暴雨
A sudden and complete loss of power, status, or success. A weakness or disadvantage. A heavy fall of rain.
The empire's downfall was caused by internal strife and external pressure.  这个帝国的衰败是由内乱和外来压力造成的。
One of the biggest downfalls of this plan is its high cost. 这个计划最大的缺点之一是成本高。
The sudden downfall flooded the streets. 突如其来的暴雨淹没了街道。
近义词: collapse, failure, weakness, defect, deluge
反义词: success, strength, advantage, rise

词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:  From "down" + "fall".  "Down" indicates a downward direction or movement, while "fall" signifies a descent or decline.
