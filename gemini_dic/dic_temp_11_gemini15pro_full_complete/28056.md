capellmeister:/ˈkɑːpəlˌmaɪstər/ (US), /kəˈpɛlˌmaɪstə/ (UK)| n.|乐队指挥，管弦乐队指挥|A person who directs an orchestra or choir, especially in Germany or Austria.  A conductor or director of music.|例句：The renowned capellmeister led the orchestra with precision.  这位著名的乐队指挥精确地指挥了乐队。|例句：The capellmeister chose a challenging piece for the orchestra's next performance. 乐队指挥为乐队的下一场演出选择了一首富有挑战性的曲目。|近义词：conductor, director of music|反义词: N/A|noun
