chowder:/ˈtʃaʊdər/ (US), /ˈtʃɔːdə(r)/ (UK)| n.|杂烩浓汤；海鲜浓汤|A thick soup or stew made with milk or cream and typically containing fish, clams, or vegetables.|例句：We had a delicious clam chowder for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了美味的蛤蜊浓汤。


例句：She made a hearty corn chowder for the chilly evening.  她为寒冷的夜晚做了一份丰盛的玉米浓汤。


近义词：soup, stew, bisque|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑thin broth)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Cornish *jowter* (compare Breton *chaod*, "hot milk poured on bread"), itself from Old French *juter*, "to pour broth or water on". The spelling *chowder* is first attested in 1751.
