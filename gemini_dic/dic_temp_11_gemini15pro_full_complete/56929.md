dotingness:/ˈdoʊtɪŋnɪs/ | n. |溺爱，宠爱 |The state of being excessively fond and indulgent.|  His dotingness towards his granddaughter was evident in the way he showered her with gifts. 他对孙女的溺爱体现在他给她送礼物的方式上。| 近义词: fondness, indulgence, affection | 反义词: neglect, indifference | 词性: noun

词源: From "dote" (to be excessively fond of) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ness" (noun-forming suffix).  Ultimately from Middle English doten, of uncertain origin.
