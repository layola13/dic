nucleolus:/njuːˈkliːələs/ /nuːˈkliːələs/| n. |核仁|A small, dense spherical structure in the nucleus of a cell during interphase. It is the site of ribosomal RNA (rRNA) synthesis and ribosome biogenesis.|The nucleolus is clearly visible under a microscope.  在显微镜下核仁清晰可见。|The prominent nucleolus in the cell suggests active protein synthesis.  细胞中显著的核仁表明蛋白质合成活跃。|Synonyms:  (No direct synonyms, but related terms include: nuclear body, nuclear organizer)|Antonyms: (No antonyms)|Noun
