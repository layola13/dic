flustered:/ˈflʌstərd/ | adj. | 慌乱的，紧张的 | Agitated and confused. |  He became flustered when he realized he had forgotten his lines.  当他意识到自己忘了台词时，他变得慌乱起来。| 近义词: agitated, nervous, disconcerted | 反义词: calm, composed, collected | 词性: adjective
