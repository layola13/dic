halfannoyingly:/ˌhæf əˈnɔɪɪŋli/| adv.|略微恼人地；有点烦人地|Halfway between annoying and not annoying.  It suggests a mild irritation or bother.|Her constant humming was halfannoyingly distracting. 她不停地哼歌，有点烦人地分散了我的注意力。|Synonyms: mildly annoying, somewhat irritating, slightly bothersome|Antonyms: pleasing, delightful, soothing|Adverb
