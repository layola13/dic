overfamed:/ˌōvərˈfēmd/ | adj. | 过誉的，名过其实的 | Having fame or a reputation beyond what is deserved; excessively famous. |  The overfamed restaurant turned out to be a disappointment. 这家名过其实的餐厅结果令人失望。| 近义词：overrated, overhyped, overpraised | 反义词：underrated, underappreciated | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "famed" (past participle of "fame," meaning "to make famous").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "famed," suggesting an excessive or undeserved level of fame.
