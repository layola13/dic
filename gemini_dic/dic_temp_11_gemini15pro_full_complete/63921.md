epiphytic:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪtɪk/| adj.|附生的|Growing or living non-parasitically on another plant (such as a tree).  Growing on the surface of a plant, but not feeding off it.|例句：Orchids and bromeliads are examples of epiphytic plants.  兰花和凤梨是附生植物的例子。

例句：The epiphytic ferns thrived in the humid rainforest. 附生的蕨类植物在潮湿的雨林中茁壮成长。|近义词：airborne, aerophytic|反义词:terrestrial, rooted|词性:adjective
