impreciseness:/ɪmˌprɪˈsaɪsnəs/| n. |不精确性，不准确性|The impreciseness of the data makes it difficult to draw any firm conclusions.  数据的不精确性使得难以得出任何确定的结论。|例句：The impreciseness of his language led to misunderstandings.  他语言的不精确导致了误解。

The impreciseness of the measurements caused problems in the experiment. 测量的不精确性给实验带来了问题。

|近义词：inaccuracy, vagueness, ambiguity, uncertainty|反义词：precision, accuracy, exactness, clarity|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From im- (not) + precise + -ness (noun suffix).  "Precise" comes from the Old French "precis" and ultimately from the Latin "praecisus," meaning "cut off in front," implying a sharp, clear definition.
