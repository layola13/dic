biracial:/baɪˈreɪʃəl/
adjective
双种族混血的
Of or relating to people whose parents are of two different races.
例句：She identifies as biracial, with a Black mother and a White father. 她认为自己是双种族混血儿，母亲是黑人，父亲是白人。

例句：The biracial community faces unique challenges. 双种族混血群体面临着独特的挑战。

近义词：multiracial, mixed-race
反义词：monoracial
词性：adjective
