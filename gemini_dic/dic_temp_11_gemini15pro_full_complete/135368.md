nonpercipiencies:/ˌnɑːnpərˈsɪpiənsiz/
|n.|无知觉；无感知；缺乏洞察力|The nonpercipiencies of youth often lead to poor decisions. 年轻人的缺乏洞察力常常导致糟糕的决定。  It's hard to overcome the nonpercipiencies surrounding mental health.  很难克服围绕心理健康的无知觉。|近义词: unawareness, ignorance, insensitivity, obtuseness |反义词: awareness, perception, insight, sensitivity| 词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:
From non- + percipiency.  "Percipiency" comes from the Latin "percipere" meaning "to perceive" or "to grasp thoroughly."  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning, leading to "lack of perception."
