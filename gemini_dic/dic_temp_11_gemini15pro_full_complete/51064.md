desolders:/diːˈsɑːldərz/ /diːˈsɒldərz/
verb
拆焊
removes solder from (something).
The technician desolders the faulty component from the circuit board.  技术员从电路板上拆焊了故障元件。
近义词: unsolders
反义词: solders
词性:v.

desoldering:/diːˈsɑːldərɪŋ/ /diːˈsɒldərɪŋ/
noun
拆焊
the act of removing solder.
Desoldering the chip required a steady hand and specialized tools. 拆焊芯片需要稳定的手和专用工具。
近义词: unsoldering
反义词: soldering
词性:n.
