murmuring: /ˈmɜːrmərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɜːrmərɪŋ/ (US) | n. | 喃喃低语；低声抱怨；潺潺声 | a low, continuous, indistinct sound; a complaint uttered in a low and indistinct tone | The murmuring of the stream was soothing. 小溪的潺潺声令人舒缓。 | synonyms: whispering, muttering, grumbling, babbling, purling | antonyms: shouting, yelling, roaring | noun

murmuring: /ˈmɜːrmərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɜːrmərɪŋ/ (US) | adj. | 喃喃低语的；低声抱怨的；潺潺作响的 | characterized by a low, continuous, indistinct sound; expressing complaint in a low and indistinct tone | The murmuring crowd expressed their discontent. 窃窃私语的人群表达了他们的不满。 | synonyms: whispering, muttering, grumbling, babbling, purling | antonyms: shouting, yelling, roaring | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *murmurynge*, from Old French *murmurer*, from Latin *murmurāre* (“to murmur, mutter, grumble”).
