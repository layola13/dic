magnetist:/ˈmæɡnɪtɪst/
noun
磁化师；磁学家；相信磁力疗法的人
A person who studies magnetism or believes in the healing power of magnets.
Franz Mesmer, often considered an early magnetist, believed he could cure illnesses by manipulating magnetic fluids in the body.  弗朗兹·梅斯梅尔，通常被认为是一位早期的磁化师，他相信可以通过操纵体内的磁性流体来治愈疾病。

Mary Baker Eddy, before founding Christian Science, was a practicing magnetist and believed in magnetic healing. 玛丽·贝克·埃迪在创立基督教科学之前是一位执业的磁化师，并且相信磁力疗法。

近义词: mesmerist, magnetic healer
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
