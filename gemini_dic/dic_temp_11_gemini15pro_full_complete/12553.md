aspiration:/ˌæspəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |渴望，抱负；送气；吸气|A strong desire to achieve something high or great; the action of pronouncing a sound with an exhalation of breath.|She has aspirations to become a doctor. 她渴望成为一名医生。|Synonyms: ambition, goal, desire, aim|Antonyms: apathy, indifference|noun


aspiration:/ˌæspəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |吸入; 抽吸|The action or process of drawing breath.|The doctor used a syringe for aspiration of fluid from the cyst. 医生用注射器从囊肿中抽吸液体。|Synonyms: inhalation, suction, withdrawal|Antonyms: exhalation, expiration|noun
