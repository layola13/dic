clutchy:/ˈklʌtʃi/| adj.|抓人的，扣人心弦的，紧张的|Causing or characterized by a sudden feeling of anxiety or nervousness, especially in a performance situation.  Often used to describe performances that are tense or anxious, but can also be used more positively to describe something gripping or compelling.|The singer gave a clutchy performance in the final round, her voice trembling slightly. 歌手在最后一轮的表演很紧张，她的声音微微颤抖着。

The basketball game was clutchy in the final minutes, with both teams fighting for the win.  篮球比赛在最后几分钟非常扣人心弦，两队都在为胜利而战。|近义词：tense, nervous, gripping, compelling|反义词：relaxed, calm, uneventful|词性:形容词
