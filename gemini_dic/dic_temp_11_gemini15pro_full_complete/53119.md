diplocardiac:/ˌdɪploʊˈkɑːrdiæk/
adjective
双心的，有两颗心脏的
Having two hearts or a double heart.  Relating to a condition where the heart appears to be doubled, usually due to a deep interventricular groove.
Diplocardiac anomalies are rare in humans. 双心畸形在人类中很少见。
The diplocardiac frog was a fascinating subject of study. 这只双心青蛙是一个引人入胜的研究对象。
近义词: bicardiac (though less common)
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) single-hearted, normal heart structure
词性: adjective
