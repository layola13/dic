outpacing:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪsɪŋ/
verb
超过，胜过
going, increasing, or developing faster than someone or something else.
China's economy is outpacing many other countries in the world. 中国的经济增长速度超过了世界上许多其他国家。
The company is outpacing its competitors in the development of new technology.  该公司在新技术开发方面超过了其竞争对手。
surpassing, exceeding, outrunning, overtaking
lagging behind, falling behind
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + pace.  "Out-" prefix meaning "beyond" or "exceeding," combined with "pace," meaning speed or rate of progress.
