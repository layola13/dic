fizzer:/ˈfɪzər/| n.|失败；哑炮；冒泡饮料|A fizzer is something that fails to live up to expectations, or a firework that fails to explode, or a fizzy drink.|The new product launch was a total fizzer.  新产品的发布完全失败了。|近义词：flop, dud, failure, washout|反义词:success, triumph|noun
