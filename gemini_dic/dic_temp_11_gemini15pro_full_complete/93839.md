immit: /ɪˈmɪt/
verb
模仿,效仿
To copy the behavior or actions of someone or something.
He tried to immit his father's deep voice. 他试图模仿他父亲低沉的声音。
synonyms: mimic, copy, emulate, impersonate
antonyms: originate, invent, create
词性: 动词
