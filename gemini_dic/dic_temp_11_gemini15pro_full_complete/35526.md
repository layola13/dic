coagulated: /kəʊˈæɡjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
凝固的，凝结的
Thickened or solidified.
The blood had coagulated around the wound.  血液在伤口周围凝固了。
近义词: clotted, thickened, congealed, jellied
反义词: liquid, fluid
词性: adjective

coagulate: /kəʊˈæɡjʊleɪt/
verb
凝结，凝固
To change from a liquid to a thickened or solid state.
The milk coagulated when we added lemon juice.  我们加了柠檬汁后，牛奶凝结了。
近义词: clot, thicken, congeal, jell
反义词: liquefy, thin
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin coagulātus, past participle of coagulāre (“to curdle, congeal”), from co- (“together”) + agulāre (“to drive into a point”), from acus (“needle”).
