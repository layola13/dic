domesticates:/dəˈmɛstɪkeɪts/| n. |驯养动植物|Animals or plants that have been tamed and bred by humans for their use.|例句：The farmer's domesticates included chickens, cows, and pigs. 农夫的驯养动物包括鸡、牛和猪。|近义词：tamed animals, cultivated plants|反义词：wild animals, wild plants|词性:名词

domesticate:/dəˈmɛstɪkeɪt/| v. |驯化，驯养|To tame (an animal) and keep it as a pet or for use, or to adapt (a plant) to be grown for human use.|例句：Humans domesticated the dog thousands of years ago.  人类在几千年前驯化了狗。|近义词：tame, cultivate|反义词：wild, untamed|词性:动词
