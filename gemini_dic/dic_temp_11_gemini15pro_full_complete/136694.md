nonsegmental:/ˌnɑːnˌsɛɡˈmɛntəl/ /ˌnɒnˌsɛɡˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 非音段的，超音段的 |  Relating to or being a feature of pronunciation, such as stress or intonation, that accompanies or extends over more than one sound segment.  |  Intonation is a nonsegmental feature of pronunciation. 语调是发音的非音段特征。|  suprasegmental | segmental | adjective
