criticism:/ˈkrɪtɪsɪzəm/| n.|批评，批判；评论|the expression of disapproval of someone or something based on perceived faults or mistakes|The film received harsh criticism from the press. 这部电影受到了来自媒体的严厉批评。|synonyms: censure, condemnation, disapproval, reprimand, rebuke, reproach, stricture|antonyms: praise, commendation, approval|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *critique* (noun) and *critiquer* (verb), from Ancient Greek *κριτικός (kritikos)*, meaning "able to discern, judge," from *κρίνω (krino)*, meaning "I separate, distinguish, judge."
