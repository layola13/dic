desist:/dɪˈzɪst/
verb
停止，终止
cease to proceed or act, especially deliberately
The soldiers were ordered to desist from firing their guns. 士兵们被命令停止开火。

近义词: cease, stop, discontinue, abstain, refrain
反义词: continue, persist, persevere
词性: verb

词源: From Old French desister, from Latin desistere (“to stand off, leave off”), from de- (“from, away”) + sistere (“to cause to stand, place, set”).
