interflashings:/ˌɪntərˈflæʃɪŋz/
n.
交光，闪烁，互相闪耀
A rapid succession of flashes of light, especially from different sources.  The interflashings of the lighthouse beam warned the ships of the approaching storm.
The interflashings of lightning illuminated the dark sky.  灯塔光束的交替闪烁警告船只风暴即将来临。
闪电的互相闪耀照亮了黑暗的天空。
flickerings, flashings, shimmerings
steadiness, constant light
noun
