overdepressive:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈprɛsɪv/
adjective
过度抑郁的
Excessively or unduly depressed; more depressed than is warranted by circumstances.  Characterized by excessive or disproportionate sadness, dejection, or low spirits.
She felt overdepressive after losing her job, even though she knew it wasn't the end of the world. 她失业后感到过度沮丧，尽管她知道这不是世界末日。
He was overdepressive about the situation, considering the minor setback. 考虑到只是一个小挫折，他对这种情况过于沮丧了。
近义词:melancholy, despondent, dejected
反义词: cheerful, elated, joyous
词性: adjective


词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "depressive" (adjective from "depress," ultimately from Latin "depressus," past participle of "deprimere," meaning "to press down").
