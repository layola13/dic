harborough:/ˈhɑːrbərə/ | n. | 港口，海港，避难所 | A place on the coast where ships may moor in shelter, especially one protected from rough water by piers, jetties, and other artificial structures. |  例句：The fishing boats returned to the harborough after a long day at sea. 渔船在海上度过漫长的一天后返回港口。

例句：The town's harborough is a vital part of its economy.  该镇的港口对其经济至关重要。


近义词：port, harbor, haven, refuge | 反义词:  exposure, vulnerability| 词性: noun
