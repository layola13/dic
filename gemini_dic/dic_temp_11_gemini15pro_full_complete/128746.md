niggardship:/ˈnɪɡərdʃɪp/| n.|吝啬，小气|The state of being a niggard; stinginess or extreme frugality.|His niggardship prevented him from donating to the charity. 他的吝啬使他没有向慈善机构捐款。|Synonyms: miserliness, parsimony, stinginess, meanness|Antonyms: generosity, liberality, munificence|noun
