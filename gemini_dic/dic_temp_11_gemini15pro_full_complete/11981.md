artesian:/ɑːrˈtiːʒən/ | adj.| 自流的，喷泉式的 | Relating to or denoting a well bored perpendicularly into water-bearing strata lying at an angle, so that natural pressure produces a constant supply of water at the surface. |  This area is known for its artesian wells. 这个地区以其自流井而闻名。|  The artesian spring provides fresh water to the village. 这眼自流泉为村庄提供淡水。|  flowing, gushing, spouting | confined, stagnant | adjective

artesian well:/ɑːrˈtiːʒən wel/ | n.| 自流井 | A well bored perpendicularly into water-bearing strata lying at an angle, so that natural pressure produces a constant supply of water at the surface. | The farmers rely on the artesian well for irrigation. 农民们依靠这口自流井进行灌溉。|  The artesian well provides a reliable source of drinking water. 这口自流井提供了可靠的饮用水源。|  flowing well, spring | dry well | noun
