habile:/ˈhæbaɪl/ (US), /ˈhæbaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|熟练的，灵巧的|Skilled, dexterous, especially with the hands.|She is a habile pianist with a delicate touch. 她是一位技艺娴熟、触感细腻的钢琴家。|近义词：skillful, adept, dexterous, proficient|反义词：clumsy, awkward, inept|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From French habile, from Latin habilis "apt, fit, suitable," from habēre "to hold, have, handle."  The original sense implied being mentally fit or capable, but the word has evolved to primarily describe manual dexterity.
