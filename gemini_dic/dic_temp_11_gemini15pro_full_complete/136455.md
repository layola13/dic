nonreversibly:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈvɜː.sə.bli/ /ˌnɑːn.rɪˈvɝː.sə.bli/
adverb
不可逆地
in a way that cannot be reversed or changed back to its original state.
The chemical reaction occurred nonreversibly. 该化学反应发生了不可逆转的变化。
This decision will nonreversibly alter the landscape.  这个决定将不可逆转地改变景观。
近义词:irreversibly, permanently, irretrievably
反义词:reversibly
词性:adverb
