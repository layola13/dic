abdicable:/ˈæbdɪkəbl/
adj.
可放弃的，可 abdicate 的
That which can be abdicated or renounced; as, an abdicable right.
The throne is abdicable. 王位是可以放弃的。
Synonyms: resignable, renunciable
Antonyms: non-abdicable, unresignable

词性: 形容词
