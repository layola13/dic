lend-a-hand:/lɛnd ə hænd/| v.|帮忙，伸出援手|To help someone with a task, especially a physical one.|例句：Could you lend a hand with moving this furniture? 你能帮忙搬一下这件家具吗？

例句：He lent a hand with the cooking. 他帮忙做了饭。


近义词：help, assist, aid|反义词:hinder, obstruct|词性:verb
