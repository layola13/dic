boted:/ˈboʊtɪd/| adj.|穿靴的；乘船的|Equipped with boots or having traveled by boat.|例句：The boted riders traversed the muddy terrain. 穿着靴子的骑手们穿越了泥泞的地形。|例句：The boted travelers arrived at the island after a long journey. 乘船旅行的旅客们在长途跋涉后抵达了该岛。|近义词：booted, shod (for "wearing boots"); shipped, sailed (for "having traveled by boat")|反义词：barefoot, unshod (for "not wearing boots");|词性:形容词

词源:  From the verb "boot" (to put boots on) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Old French "bote." For the nautical sense, related to "boat."
