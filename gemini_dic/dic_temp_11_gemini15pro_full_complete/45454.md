curateships:/ˈkjʊərɪtʃɪps/
n.
管理(职位)；策展(工作)
The position and duties of a curator, especially in a museum or art gallery.  The act or process of curating something.
The museum has several curateships available.  博物馆有几个策展职位空缺。
She took on the curateship of the modern art collection. 她承担了现代艺术收藏的策展工作。
synonyms: curation, management, administration, supervision, oversight
antonyms: neglect, abandonment

ETYMOLOGY->
From curate + -ship.  "Curate" ultimately derives from Latin *cura* (care, concern).
