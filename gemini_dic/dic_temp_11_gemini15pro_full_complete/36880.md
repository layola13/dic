coloratura:/ˌkʌlərəˈtʊərə/ (US), /ˌkʌlə.rəˈtuː.rə/ (UK)| n.| 花腔；炫技的演唱技巧；花腔女高音|A rapid melodic passage with many notes, typically sung by a soprano.|The soprano's coloratura in the aria was breathtaking.  女高音在咏叹调中的花腔令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: ornamentation, embellishment, flourish|Antonyms: simplicity, plainness|noun


coloraturas:/ˌkʌlərəˈtʊərəz/ (US), /ˌkʌlə.rəˈtuː.rəz/ (UK)| n.| 花腔（复数）；炫技的演唱技巧（复数）；花腔女高音（复数）|Rapid melodic passages with many notes, typically sung by a soprano.|The opera featured dazzling coloraturas throughout. 这部歌剧自始至终都以令人眼花缭乱的花腔为特色。|Synonyms: ornamentations, embellishments, flourishes|Antonyms: simplicities, plainnesses|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *coloratura*, from *colorare* "to color," from Latin *color* "color."  The musical sense arose from the idea of "coloring" or embellishing a melody.
