oaky: /ˈoʊki/ | adj. |橡木味的；像橡木的| Having a flavor or aroma reminiscent of oak, typically used to describe wines aged in oak barrels. | The wine has a pleasant, oaky flavor. 这款酒有令人愉悦的橡木味。|  The Chardonnay had a distinct oaky aroma. 这款霞多丽葡萄酒有明显的橡木香气。 | 近义词: woody, oaken | 反义词: fruity, smooth | 词性: 形容词
