abettal:/əˈbɛtl/
noun
教唆；煽动；怂恿
The act of encouraging or assisting someone to do something wrong or illegal.
He was charged with aiding and abetting the robbery. 他被指控协助和教唆抢劫。
synonyms: encouragement, assistance, support, incitement, instigation
antonyms: discouragement, dissuasion, deterrence
