namby-pamby:/ˈnæmbi ˈpæmbi/| adj. n.|婆婆妈妈的，优柔寡断的；娇气的人|Someone who is namby-pamby is weak and too emotional.  A namby-pamby action or statement is weak and ineffectual.|He’s a namby-pamby liberal. 他是个优柔寡断的自由主义者。|近义词：sentimental, mawkish, feeble, weak, indecisive|反义词：strong, decisive, resolute|形容词，名词
