ensnarer:/ɪnˈsnɛərər/ | n. | 陷阱, 诱捕者 |  Someone or something that traps or catches something else. | The spider is an ensnarer of flies. 蜘蛛是捕捉苍蝇的陷阱。 | Synonyms: trapper, capturer | Antonyms: liberator, rescuer | noun

ensnare:/ɪnˈsnɛər/ | v. | 诱捕, 缠住 | To trap or catch something in a snare or entanglement. | The hunter ensnared the rabbit in a net. 猎人用网捕获了兔子。 | Synonyms: trap, entangle | Antonyms: free, release | verb
