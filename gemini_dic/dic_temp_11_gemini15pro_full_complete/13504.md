词源:  From Middle English *attacken, from Old French ataquer (“to attach, fasten, apply oneself to”), from a- (from Latin ad (“to, towards”)) + taquer (“to touch, strike”), of Germanic origin.

注意事项:  "attacking" 作为动名词时，可以做主语、宾语或表语。 作为形容词时，通常修饰名词，表示“正在攻击的”或“具有攻击性的”。
