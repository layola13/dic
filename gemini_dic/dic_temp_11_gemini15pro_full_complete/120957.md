mischannelling:/ˌmɪsˈtʃænəlɪŋ/
n.
误导，滥用
The mischannelling of funds led to the project's failure. 资金的滥用导致了项目的失败。
Mischannelling of resources can have devastating consequences. 资源的误导会造成毁灭性的后果。
近义词: misuse, misdirection, misapplication
反义词: proper use, correct application
