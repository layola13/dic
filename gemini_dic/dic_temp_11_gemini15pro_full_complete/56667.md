dood:/duːd/| n.|家伙，哥们，老兄|(informal) A man; a guy.  A term of address, often used casually between friends.|例句：Hey, dood, what's up? 嘿，哥们，怎么了？

例句：That dood owes me twenty bucks. 那家伙欠我二十块钱。|近义词：guy, fellow, chap, bloke (more British)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "enemy" in a specific context)|词性:noun
