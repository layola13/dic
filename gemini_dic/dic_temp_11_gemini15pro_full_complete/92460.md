hypostasis: /haɪˈpɒstəsɪs/ (UK), /haɪˈpɑːstəsɪs/ (US)
n.
本质，实体，真实的存在；基质；(神学)位格
An underlying reality or substance, especially one considered as the essence of something; sediment;  (in Christian theology) each of the three persons of the Trinity.
The hypostasis of the argument is that all men are created equal. 这段论证的本质是人人生而平等。
essence, substance, reality, actuality
appearance, illusion, unreality
noun
