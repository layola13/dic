co-signatory:/ˌkoʊˈsaɪnətɔːri/ /ˌkəʊˈsɪɡnətəri/
noun
共同签署人，联署人
A person who signs a document along with another person or other people, especially a loan or other financial agreement.
My father was co-signatory on the loan. 我父亲是贷款的共同签署人。

co-signatory:/ˌkoʊˈsaɪnətɔːri/ /ˌkəʊˈsɪɡnətəri/
noun
共同签署人，联署人
One of two or more people who have signed something together, making them jointly responsible for it.
Both co-signatories are liable for the debt. 共同签署人都要对债务负责。
