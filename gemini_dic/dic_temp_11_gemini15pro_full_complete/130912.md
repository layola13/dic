nonascendence:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndəns/
n.|非超越性，非优越性|The state or quality of not transcending or surpassing ordinary limits or powers; lack of superiority or dominance.|His theory emphasizes the nonascendence of human beings over nature. 他的理论强调人类对自然并非超越性的。|Near synonyms: immanence, mundanity|Antonyms: transcendence, superiority|noun

nonascendence:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndəns/
n.|非支配性，非主导性|The state or quality of not being dominant or controlling.|The nonascendence of any single political party ensured a more balanced government.  任何单一政党都不占主导地位，确保了政府更加平衡。|Near synonyms: equality, parity|Antonyms: dominance, hegemony|noun
