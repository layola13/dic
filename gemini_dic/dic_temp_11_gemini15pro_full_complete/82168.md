grooving:/ˈɡruːvɪŋ/| v. n. adj.|开槽；享受；感到愉快；有感染力的|Enjoying oneself, especially through dancing or listening to music.  The act of making grooves. Having grooves.|例句：The band was really grooving, and the crowd was loving it. 乐队演奏得非常带劲，观众都很喜欢。

例句：I was grooving to the music all night. 我整晚都沉浸在音乐中。

例句：He's grooving a new design into the wood. 他正在木头上刻一条新的槽。

例句：The music was grooving and made everyone want to dance.  这音乐很有感染力，让每个人都想跳舞。|近义词：enjoying, digging, vibing, rocking, jamming,  |反义词:disliking, hating|词性:verb, noun, adjective
