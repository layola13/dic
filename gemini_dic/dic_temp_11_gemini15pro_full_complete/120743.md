misanthrope: /ˈmɪsənˌθroʊp/ (US) /ˈmɪsənˌθrəʊp/ (UK)| n. |厌世者；愤世嫉俗者|A person who dislikes humankind and avoids human society.|例句：He was a misanthrope who lived alone in the woods. 他是一个厌世者，独自住在树林里。

例句：The old misanthrope glared at the children playing in the park.  这位老厌世者怒视着在公园里玩耍的孩子们。

近义词：cynic, pessimist, recluse|反义词:philanthropist, humanitarian|词性:noun
