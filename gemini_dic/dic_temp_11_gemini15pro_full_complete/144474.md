outthought:/ˌaʊtˈθɔːt/
verb
智胜；在思考上胜过
to defeat someone by thinking more cleverly than they do
She outthought her opponents and won the negotiation. 她智胜了对手，赢得了谈判。

outthought:/ˌaʊtˈθɔːt/
verb
想得比…周到；比…更深谋远虑
to consider something more thoroughly than someone else
He had outthought everyone and anticipated their every move. 他比所有人都想得周到，预料到了他们的一举一动。


近义词:outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
反义词:be outwitted, be outsmarted
