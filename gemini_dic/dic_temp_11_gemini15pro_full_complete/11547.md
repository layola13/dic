aristate:/ˈærɪsteɪt/| adj.|芒状的，有芒的|Having a stiff, bristle-like appendage, especially at the tip of a leaf or other plant part.|The foxtail barley has aristate spikelets.  狐尾大麦有芒状的小穗。|Synonyms: awned, bristly, barbed|Antonyms: smooth, glabrous|adjective
