boycotters:/ˈbɔɪkɒtəz/ (UK) /ˈbɔɪkɑːtərz/ (US)| n. |抵制者，联合抵制者|People who participate in a boycott, refusing to buy, use, or participate in something as a form of protest.|Many boycotters refused to buy products from the company due to its unethical labor practices. 许多抵制者因为该公司不道德的劳工行为而拒绝购买其产品。|Example: The boycotters picketed outside the store, urging others to join their cause. 抵制者在商店外设置纠察线，敦促其他人加入他们的行列。|Synonyms: protesters, dissenters, objectors|Antonyms: supporters, patrons, customers|Noun
