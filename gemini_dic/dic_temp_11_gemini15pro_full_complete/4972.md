alkaline:/ˈælkəlaɪn/ /ˈælkəˌlaɪn/| adj. n.|碱性的；碱的；碱|Having a pH greater than 7; basic.  Relating to, containing, or having the properties of an alkali.  A substance with a pH greater than 7.|例句：Alkaline batteries are commonly used in electronic devices. 碱性电池常用于电子设备。

例句：The soil in this area is alkaline.  这个地区的土壤是碱性的。|近义词：basic, caustic|反义词：acidic, acid|词性:形容词, 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *alcalin* (modern French *alcalin*), from Medieval Latin *alcalinus*, from Arabic *al-qalī* (“the ashes, soda ash”), from *qalā* (“to roast in a pan”).
