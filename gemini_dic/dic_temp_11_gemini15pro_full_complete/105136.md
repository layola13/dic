ketamine:/ˈkiːtəmiːn/ (US), /ˈkiːtəmiːn/ (UK)| n.|氯胺酮;克他命|A synthetic drug used as an anesthetic, especially for animals, and sometimes abused as a recreational drug. It can cause hallucinations and feelings of detachment from reality.|The cat was given ketamine before the operation. 这只猫在手术前被注射了氯胺酮。| The patient was sedated with ketamine.  这个病人被用氯胺酮镇静了。|近义词：anesthetic, narcotic|反义词: stimulant|词性:noun
