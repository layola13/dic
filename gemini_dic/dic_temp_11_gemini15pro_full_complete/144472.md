outthinking:/ˌaʊtˈθɪŋkɪŋ/
v.
智胜；在思考上胜过
To defeat or surpass someone by thinking more cleverly or strategically.
He outthought his opponents and won the chess match. 他智胜了对手，赢得了象棋比赛。
近义词：outsmarting, outmaneuvering
反义词：being outwitted, being outsmarted
词性：动词
