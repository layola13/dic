hammerer's:/ˈhæmərərz/
n.
锤工的，使用锤子的人的
belonging to or associated with a hammerer; characteristic of a hammerer.
The hammerer's hands were calloused and strong. 锤工的双手老茧又强壮。
近义词:blacksmith's, smith's
反义词: None
词性:名词，所有格


hammerer's:/ˈhæmərərz/
adj.
锤工的，使用锤子的人的
characteristic of or relating to a hammerer.
He had a hammerer's grip, firm and unshakeable. 他有着锤工般的紧握，坚定不移。
近义词:blacksmith's, smith's
反义词:None
词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From hammerer + 's (possessive suffix).  Hammerer from hammer + -er (agent suffix). Hammer ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hamaraz, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱem- (to strike).
