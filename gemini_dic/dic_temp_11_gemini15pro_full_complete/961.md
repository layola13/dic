academist:/ˈækədəmɪst/| n.|学者，学院派人士|A person who works or teaches in a college or university, or who is a member of a learned society. Especially one who is thought to be too concerned with theory rather than practical matters.|He's a renowned academist in the field of linguistics. 他是语言学领域的著名学者。|The academist spent years researching the ancient texts.  这位学者花了数年时间研究古代文献。|Synonyms: scholar, intellectual, academic, learned person|Antonyms: practitioner, pragmatist|Noun
