overcoatless:/ˌoʊvərˈkoʊtlɪs/
adjective
无外套的; 没穿外套的
without an overcoat.
He braved the cold weather overcoatless. 他没穿外套就冒着严寒天气。
Synonyms: coatless
Antonyms: coated, wearing an overcoat
词性: 形容词
