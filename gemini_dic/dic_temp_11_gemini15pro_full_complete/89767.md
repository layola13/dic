horatian:/həˈreɪʃən/ /hɔːˈreɪʃən/| adj.|贺拉斯的；贺拉斯风格的；乐天的；风趣的|Of or relating to the Roman poet Horace (Quintus Horatius Flaccus) or his works, especially his odes; characterized by a polished style and a tone of genial urbanity.|His poetry is Horatian in its wit and grace. 他的诗歌以其机智和优雅体现了贺拉斯风格。|Synonyms: lighthearted, witty, graceful, urbane, elegant, charming, pleasant, agreeable|Antonyms: pessimistic, gloomy, melancholic, serious, somber|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Horace, the Roman poet (Quintus Horatius Flaccus) + -an (suffix forming adjectives).
