craglike:/ˈkræɡlaɪk/
adjective
像峭壁的；崎岖的
Resembling a crag; rugged and steep.
The craglike cliffs offered a challenging climb for experienced mountaineers.  像峭壁一样的悬崖为经验丰富的登山者提供了挑战性的攀登。
The old man's face was craglike, weathered by years of sun and wind. 老人的脸像峭壁一样，饱经风霜。
synonyms:  rocky, rugged, precipitous, sheer, clifflike
antonyms: smooth, flat, even
詞性: adjective
