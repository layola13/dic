flimflam:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/
flimflam: n. |欺骗；花招；胡说八道|An act of deception or trickery; nonsense.|He tried to sell me some flimflam about a miracle cure.|近义词：trickery, deception, fraud, swindle, hoax|反义词：honesty, truthfulness|词性:名词
flimflam:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/
flimflam: vt. |欺骗；诈骗|To deceive or swindle.|He flimflammed me out of my savings.|近义词：deceive, cheat, swindle, defraud, hoodwink|反义词：be honest with|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, perhaps imitative of rapid or nonsensical talk. First recorded in the mid-16th century.
