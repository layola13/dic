ballad:/ˈbæləd/| n. |民谣，歌谣；叙事诗|A ballad is a poem or song narrating a story in short stanzas. |例句：The singer performed a beautiful ballad about lost love. 歌手演唱了一首关于失恋的优美民谣。

例句：She wrote a ballad commemorating the historic event. 她写了一首民谣来纪念这一历史事件。

近义词：song, poem, ditty, lay|反义词: |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *balade* (French *ballade*), from Old Provençal *balada*, from *balar* "to dance," from Late Latin *ballare*.  Ultimately from Greek *βαλλίζω* (ballizō) "to dance, jump about."  The sense shifted from a "dancing song" to a "narrative song."
