cheep:/tʃiːp/| vi. n.|吱吱叫；廉价的；唧唧声|A short, high-pitched sound made by a young bird.  Also can mean inexpensive.|The chicks cheeped loudly. 小鸡们大声地吱吱叫。|近义词：chirp, peep, tweet, inexpensive, cheap|反义词:expensive, dear|verb, noun

cheep:/tʃiːp/| adj.|廉价的|Inexpensive.|I bought a cheep dress at the market. 我在市场上买了一件便宜的连衣裙。|近义词：inexpensive, cheap, low-cost|反义词:expensive, dear, costly|adjective
