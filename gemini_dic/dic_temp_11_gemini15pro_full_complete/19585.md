bigged:/bɪɡd/ | v. | 夸大; 吹嘘;  | To exaggerate or boast about something. |  He bigged up his achievements in the interview.  他在面试中夸大了自己的成就。| 近义词: exaggerated, boasted, inflated, hyped | 反义词: downplayed, minimized, understated | 词性: 动词

词源: "Bigged" is the past tense form of the informal verb "big up," which originated in Jamaican English and gained wider popularity in the late 20th century.  It's formed by combining the adjective "big" with the particle "up," which intensifies the meaning.
