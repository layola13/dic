lyrical:/ˈlɪrɪkl/ | adj. |抒情的；如诗的；感情丰富的|Expressing deep personal emotion or observations; resembling or suitable for poetry or song. |Her writing style is lyrical and evocative. 她的写作风格抒情且引人入胜。|  The song has a beautiful, lyrical melody. 这首歌的旋律优美抒情。 | poetic, expressive, emotional, musical, melodious | prosaic, dull, unimaginative | adjective

词源：
From French *lyrique*, from Latin *lyricus*, from Greek *lyrikos*, from *lyra* ("lyre").  The original meaning relates to poetry or song accompanied by the lyre.
