feeble-minded:/ˈfiːblˌmaɪndɪd/
adjective
智力低下的，愚笨的
Having significantly below average intelligence.  Lacking in judgment or good sense.
He was considered feeble-minded and unable to care for himself. 他被认为智力低下，无法照顾自己。
The feeble-minded policies of the government led to economic disaster. 政府愚蠢的政策导致了经济灾难。
Synonyms: simpleminded, foolish, unintelligent, witless, slow-witted, half-witted, dim-witted, stupid
Antonyms: intelligent, bright, clever, smart, astute, sharp
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From feeble + minded.  "Feeble" comes from Old French "feble," ultimately from Latin "flebilis" meaning "lamentable." "Minded" signifies having a particular mental disposition.
