nematode:/ˈnɛməˌtoʊd/ (US), /ˈnɛmətəʊd/ (UK)| n.|线虫|A worm of the phylum Nematoda, such as a roundworm or threadworm.|例句：The nematode is a common parasite in many animals.  这种线虫是许多动物体内常见的寄生虫。

例句：Root-knot nematodes can cause significant damage to crops.  根结线虫会对农作物造成重大损害。|近义词：roundworm, threadworm|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


nematoidea:/ˌnɛməˈtɔɪdiə/ (US), /ˌnɛməˈtɔɪdɪə/ (UK)| n.|线虫纲|A taxonomic class within the phylum Nematoda, comprising a large and diverse group of roundworms.|例句：The class Nematoidea includes numerous species of parasitic worms.  线虫纲包括许多种类的寄生虫。

例句：Scientists are still discovering new species within the Nematoidea class.  科学家们仍在发现线虫纲中的新物种。|近义词：Nematoda|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
