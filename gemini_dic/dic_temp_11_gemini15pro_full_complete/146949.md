overinvest:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛst/ | v. | 过度投资 | To invest excessively, often beyond the point of diminishing returns or at an unacceptably high level of risk. | The company overinvested in new technology and neglected its core business.  这家公司在新技术上过度投资，而忽略了其核心业务。| 近义词：overspend, squander | 反义词: underinvest | 词性: 动词


overinvest:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛst/ | n. | 过度投资 | The act of overinvesting. |  There was evidence of overinvest in the dot-com bubble. 在互联网泡沫时期，有过度投资的证据。| 近义词：overspending, extravagance | 反义词: underinvestment | 词性: 名词
