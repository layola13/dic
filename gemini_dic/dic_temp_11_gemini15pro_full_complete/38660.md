concusses:/kənˈkʌsɪz/
verb
震荡；使脑震荡
To shake or jar violently, especially so as to cause injury to the brain.
The boxer concussed his opponent with a powerful blow to the head. 拳击手重重一击打在对手的头上，使他脑震荡。
近义词: shakes, jars, jolts, stuns, dazes
反义词: stabilizes, calms, steadies
词性: verb (动词)


concuss:/kənˈkʌs/
noun
脑震荡
A violent shaking or jarring, especially of the brain.
He suffered a concuss in the car accident. 他在车祸中遭受了脑震荡。
近义词: concussion
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 recovery 康复)
词性: noun (名词)


词源: From Latin *concutere, frequentative of concutere  "to shake violently," from com- "together" + quatere "to shake."
