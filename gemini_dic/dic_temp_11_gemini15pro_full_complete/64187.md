equestrian:/ɪˈkwɛstriən/ /ɪˈkwɛstriən/
adjective
骑马的；马术的
Of or relating to horseback riding.
例句：She has always had an equestrian interest. 她一直对骑马很感兴趣。

例句：The equestrian statue of the duke dominated the square. 公爵的骑马雕像在广场上占主导地位。

近义词：riding, horsemanship
反义词：pedestrian

词性：adjective


equestrian:/ɪˈkwɛstriən/ /ɪˈkwɛstriən/
noun
骑手；骑术运动员
A person who rides on horseback.
例句：He is a skilled equestrian. 他是一位技艺精湛的骑手。

例句：The equestrian vaulted gracefully onto the horse. 骑手优雅地跃上了马背。

近义词：horseman, rider, jockey
反义词：pedestrian

词性：noun
