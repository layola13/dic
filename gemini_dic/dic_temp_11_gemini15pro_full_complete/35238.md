clump:/klʌmp/
n.|团，块，丛，簇|A compact mass or cluster of something.|The daffodils grew in clumps. 水仙花长成一丛丛的。
近义词：cluster, bunch, mass, group
反义词：scattering, dispersal
词性: 名词

clump:/klʌmp/
v.|聚集，丛生|To form or gather into a clump or clumps.|The children clumped together for warmth. 孩子们挤在一起取暖。
近义词：cluster, gather, bunch
反义词：scatter, disperse, separate
词性: 动词

词源:  From Middle Low German *klumpe and Middle Dutch clumpe ("lump, clod").
