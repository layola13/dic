nonincumbents:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkʌmbənts/ | n. | 非现任者；非在职者 |  A person who does not currently hold a particular office or position.  It particularly refers to those challenging an incumbent in an election. |  The nonincumbents are campaigning hard to unseat the current mayor.  非现任者们正在努力竞选，试图取代现任市长。

The debate gave the nonincumbents a platform to share their ideas. 这场辩论为非现任者提供了一个分享他们想法的平台。| 近义词：challenger, contender, aspirant | 反义词：incumbent | 词性：名词
