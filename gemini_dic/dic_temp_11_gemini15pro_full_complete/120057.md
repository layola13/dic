mills:/mɪlz/| n.|磨坊，工厂，製造廠|A building equipped with machinery for grinding grain into flour.|The old water mills are still in use. 那些古老的水磨坊仍在使用。|近义词：factories, plants, works|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"farm"农场，如果磨坊是用来加工农产品的)|词性:名词

mills:/mɪlz/| n.|磨粉机，研磨机|A machine for grinding grain into flour or other substances into powder.|The pepper mills are filled with fresh peppercorns. 胡椒研磨机里装满了新鲜的胡椒粒。|近义词：grinders, crushers|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性:名词

mills:/mɪlz/| n.|轧钢机，轧机|A machine for rolling metal into sheets or bars.|The steel mills are working day and night. 轧钢机日夜运转。|近义词：rolling mills|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性:名词


词源:
From Old English *mylen, from Proto-Germanic *mulinō, from Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (“to grind, crush”). Cognate with Dutch molen (“mill”), German Mühle (“mill”), Latin molō (“grind”).
