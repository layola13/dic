couranto:/kuːˈrɑːntoʊ/ |n.|库兰托舞曲|A couranto is a fast-moving, triple-meter dance of Italian or French origin, popular in the 17th and 18th centuries. It is characterized by its running or flowing character and often features complex rhythmic patterns.|The musicians played a lively couranto to start the ball. 乐手们演奏了一首活泼的库兰托舞曲来开场舞会。|Synonym: corrente|Antonym: sarabande|noun
