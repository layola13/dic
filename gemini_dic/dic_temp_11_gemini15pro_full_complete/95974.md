incumbency: /ɪnˈkʌmbənsi/
n.
在职;任职;现任
the fact of holding an official position.
The president's incumbency has lasted for four years.  总统的任期已经四年了。

The senator lost his incumbency in the last election.  这位参议员在上次选举中失去了自己的席位。

近义词: tenure, office, position
反义词: vacancy

词性: noun

词源:  From Late Latin incumbentia, from incumbentem (nominative incumbens), present participle of incumbere “to lie or lean upon, apply oneself to, devote oneself to,” from in- “in, on” + -cumbere, related to cubare “to lie down.”
