integumentation: /ɪnˌtɛɡjʊmɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. |  外皮形成，体被形成 | The process of forming or developing a natural covering, such as skin, shell, or rind. | The integumentation of insects provides protection and support. 昆虫的体被提供了保护和支撑。 |  近义词: covering, coating, skin, rind | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be considered the opposite of "excoriation" or "flaying" - the removal of skin) | 词性: noun
