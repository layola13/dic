outpoints:/ˈaʊtˌpɔɪnts/
verb| 胜过，优于|to surpass in the number of points scored or accumulated|例句：The team outpointed their rivals in the final match.  该队在决赛中胜过对手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outperform, beat, defeat|反义词: lose to, be defeated by|词性:v.

outpoints:/ˈaʊtˌpɔɪnts/
noun|优势，胜过之处|advantages or superiorities|例句：The candidate's outpoints were her experience and charisma.  这位候选人的优势在于她的经验和魅力。|近义词：advantages, strengths, merits|反义词: disadvantages, weaknesses, flaws|词性:n.
