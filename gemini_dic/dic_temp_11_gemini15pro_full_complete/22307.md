boniform:/ˈbɒnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
骨状的; 良性的
having the form or nature of bone; benign.
The tumor was thankfully boniform.  谢天谢地，肿瘤是良性的。
近义词:osseous, bony
反义词:malignant
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *bonus* "good" + *-formis* "-shaped," from *forma* "form."
