overfreighting:/ˌoʊvərˈfreɪtɪŋ/| n.|超载，装载过多|The overfreighting of the ship caused it to capsize. 这艘船的超载导致它倾覆。|例句：The overfreighting of trucks is a common problem on this road.  这条路上卡车超载是常见问题。

Overfreighting can damage goods and delay delivery. 超载会损坏货物并延误交货。|近义词：overloading, overburdening|反义词: underloading|noun
