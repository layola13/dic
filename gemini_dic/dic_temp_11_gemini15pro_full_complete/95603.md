incommodious:/ˌɪnkəˈmoʊdiəs/| adj.|不方便的，不舒适的；狭小的|Not spacious; inconveniently small or cramped.  Lacking sufficient space or room; inconvenient.|The attic room was dark and incommodious. 阁楼房间又黑又窄。|近义词：cramped, confined, inconvenient, uncomfortable|反义词：spacious, roomy, comfortable, convenient|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incommodiosus, from Latin incommodus (“inconvenient, troublesome”), from in- (“not”) + commodus (“convenient, suitable”).
