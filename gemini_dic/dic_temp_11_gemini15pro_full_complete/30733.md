cervus: /ˈsɜːrvəs/ | n. |鹿, 雄鹿 |A deer, especially a male deer; the genus that includes the red deer and elk. |  The majestic cervus stood proudly in the clearing.  雄伟的雄鹿骄傲地站在空地上。

The antlers of a cervus can be quite impressive. 雄鹿的鹿角相当令人印象深刻。


| 近义词: deer, stag, hart | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "doe" if referring specifically to a male deer) | 词性: noun
