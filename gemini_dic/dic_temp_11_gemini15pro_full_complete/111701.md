lockouts:/ˈlɒkaʊts/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkaʊts/ (US)| n.|停工，闭厂； lockout |The company initiated lockouts to pressure the union. 公司发起停工以向工会施压。|The union members were subjected to a lockout by the company. 工会成员遭到公司的停工。|近义词：shutdown, closure, strike|反义词：opening, reopening|词性:名词

词源: From lock + out.  The original sense was "the exclusion of a person from his or her property", later specifically applied to industrial disputes.
