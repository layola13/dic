disbelief:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːf/| n.|不信，怀疑，疑惑|The news was met with widespread disbelief.  这条消息受到了广泛的质疑。|She stared at him in disbelief. 她难以置信地盯着他。|近义词：doubt, skepticism, incredulity|反义词：belief, faith, trust|词性:名词

词源:  From dis- (expressing negation) + belief.  来自dis-（表示否定）+ belief（相信）。
