brachydiagonal:/brækiːdaɪˈæɡənəl/ | n. |短对角线|A diagonal shorter than the others in a quadrilateral figure.|The brachydiagonal of the kite is the shorter of the two diagonals.  风筝的短对角线是两条对角线中较短的一条。|The crystal's brachydiagonal was easily identifiable under the microscope. 在显微镜下，晶体的短对角线很容易识别。 |short diagonal|long diagonal|noun
