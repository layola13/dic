hemelytron:/ˈhɛmɪlaɪtrən/ | n. | 半鞘翅 | The hemelytron is the modified forewing of certain insects, particularly in the order Hemiptera (true bugs). It is thickened and leathery at the base and membranous at the tip. |  The stink bug's hemelytra help protect its delicate hindwings.  椿象的半鞘翅有助于保护其脆弱的后翅。| synonym: forewing (in some contexts) | antonym: hindwing | noun


hemelytra:/ˈhɛmɪlaɪtrə/ | n. | 半鞘翅（复数） |  Hemelytra are the modified forewings of Hemipteran insects. | The hemelytra of many bugs overlap, forming an "X" shape on their back. 许多昆虫的半鞘翅重叠，在其背部形成“X”形。 | synonym: forewings (in some contexts) | antonym: hindwings | noun
