lawfulnesses:/ˈlɔːfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
合法性，守法性
The state of being lawful or in accordance with the law.
The lawfulnesses of the proceedings were questioned by the defense.  辩方质疑了诉讼程序的合法性。
The judge confirmed the lawfulnesses of the arrest. 法官确认了逮捕的合法性。
legality, validity, legitimacy
illegality, unlawfulness
