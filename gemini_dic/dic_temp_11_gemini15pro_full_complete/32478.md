chinchilla:/tʃɪnˈtʃɪlə/ | n. | 栗鼠，南美栗鼠毛皮 | A small rodent native to the Andes Mountains in South America, known for its soft, dense fur. |  I saw a chinchilla at the pet store. 我在宠物店看到一只栗鼠。

chinchilla:/tʃɪnˈtʃɪlə/ | n. | 栗鼠毛皮，栗鼠皮衣 | The soft, dense fur of the chinchilla, often used in clothing. |  She wore a chinchilla coat. 她穿着一件栗鼠皮大衣。
