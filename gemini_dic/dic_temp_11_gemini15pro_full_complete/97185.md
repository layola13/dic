infixes:/ˈɪnfɪksɪz/| n.|中缀|An infix is an affix that is inserted within a word stem (an existing word).  It contrasts with prefixes, suffixes and circumfixes.|Example:  "Absogoddamlutely!" is an example of infixation, inserting "goddamn" into "absolutely".|Synonym: none (this is a specific linguistic term)|Antonym: none (this is a specific linguistic term)|noun
