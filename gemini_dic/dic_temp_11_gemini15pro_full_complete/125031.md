mukene:/muˈkeɪneɪ/| n.|银汉鱼科的一种小鱼，通常被称为“mukene”，主要分布在维多利亚湖。|A small fish of the silver cyprinid family, commonly known as "mukene," primarily found in Lake Victoria. |例句：Mukene are a staple food source for many communities around Lake Victoria.  银汉鱼是维多利亚湖周围许多社区的主要食物来源。

例句：The fishermen brought in a large catch of mukene this morning. 渔民今天早上捕获了大量的银汉鱼。|近义词：dagaa (in Tanzania), omena (in Kenya)  |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
