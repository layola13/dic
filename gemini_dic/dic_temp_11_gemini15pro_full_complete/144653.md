outwrenched:/ˌaʊtˈrɛntʃt/
verb
扭伤，扳伤，猛扭
past tense of outwrench: injured by a violent twisting or wrenching.
His ankle was outwrenched in the fall. 他的脚踝在摔倒时扭伤了。
近义词: sprained, wrenched, twisted
反义词: healed, mended
词性: verb (past tense)
