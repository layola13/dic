lawyered:/ˈlɔɪərd/
verb|用法律手段对付；搬弄法律条文；|To argue or act like a lawyer, often in a tedious or excessive way, especially to exploit technicalities or loopholes.|例句：They lawyered up as soon as they got the subpoena.  他们一收到传票就请了律师。

例句：He tried to lawyer his way out of the parking ticket. 他试图利用法律条文来逃避停车罚单。|近义词： litigate, dispute, argue|反义词: concede, yield|词性:v.
