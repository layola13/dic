cremationist:/krɪˈmeɪʃənɪst/
n.
火葬师，火化工
A person whose job is to cremate dead bodies.
例句：The cremationist prepared the body for the cremation. 火葬师准备将尸体火化。

例句：My uncle is a cremationist, and he finds his work to be quite rewarding. 我的叔叔是一位火葬师，他觉得他的工作很有意义。
