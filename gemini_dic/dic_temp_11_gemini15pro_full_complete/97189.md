inflame:/ɪnˈfleɪm/| vt.|激怒，使发炎|to arouse or excite feelings and passions, especially anger or hostility; to cause inflammation in (a part of the body)|The politician's speech inflamed the crowd. 政治家的演讲激怒了人群。|His rash was inflamed by the sun.  他的皮疹被太阳晒得发炎了。|近义词：anger, irritate, aggravate, incite, enrage|反义词：soothe, calm, alleviate|词性：动词
inflame:/ɪnˈfleɪm/| v.|燃烧，着火|(literary) to burst into flame|The dry wood inflamed quickly. 干木迅速燃烧起来。|His heart inflamed with love for her. 他的心中燃起了对她的爱。(比喻用法)|近义词：ignite, kindle|反义词：extinguish|词性：动词
