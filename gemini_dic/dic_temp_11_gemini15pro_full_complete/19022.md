beta:/ˈbeɪtə/ (US) /ˈbiːtə/ (UK)| n. adj.| 贝塔，测试版|The second letter of the Greek alphabet (Β, β).  Also used to refer to a trial or test version of a product, particularly software, before its general release.|The company released a beta version of their new software to a select group of users.|近义词：trial, test, experimental|反义词:final, released|noun, adjective
