hypochondriac: /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriæk/ (UK) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɑːndriæk/ (US)| n. adj.|疑病症患者，疑病症的|A person who is abnormally anxious about their health, often believing they have a serious illness despite medical reassurance.  A person exhibiting or characterized by hypochondria.|例句：He's a terrible hypochondriac; every time he gets a headache he's convinced he has a brain tumor. 他是一个严重的疑病症患者；每次头痛，他都确信自己得了脑瘤。

例句：She's become quite hypochondriac in her old age. 她老了以后变得疑神疑鬼的，总担心自己有病。|近义词：valetudinarian, worrier, neurotic|反义词: stoic|词性: noun, adjective
