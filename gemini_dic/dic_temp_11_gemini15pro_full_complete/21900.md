bodysuit:/ˈbɒdiˌsuːt/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdiˌsuːt/ (US)| n. |紧身衣|A close-fitting, one-piece garment that covers the torso and crotch, often extending down the legs to varying degrees. It can be worn as lingerie, swimwear, or as a top with other clothing.|She wore a black bodysuit under her jeans. 她穿了一件黑色紧身衣在牛仔裤里面。|

|近义词：leotard, unitard, catsuit|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps loose clothing, separates)|noun|


bodysuit:/ˈbɒdiˌsuːt/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdiˌsuːt/ (US)| n. |连体衣|A close-fitting, one-piece garment for babies and young children, typically fastening with poppers or snaps at the crotch.|The baby wore a cute animal print bodysuit. 宝宝穿了一件可爱的动物图案连体衣。|近义词：onesie, romper|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps separates - two-piece clothing)|noun|
