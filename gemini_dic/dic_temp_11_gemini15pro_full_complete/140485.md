old-womanly:/ˈoʊldˈwʊmənli/| adj.|老妇人的，像老妇人的|Characteristic of or resembling an old woman, often in a negative or stereotypical way, suggesting qualities like fussiness, frailty, or conservatism.|例句：Her old-womanly gait was slow and deliberate. 她老态龙钟的步态缓慢而从容。

例句：She had an old-womanly habit of hoarding useless items. 她有一个老妇人般的习惯，喜欢囤积无用的东西。|近义词：elderly, matronly, ancient|反义词：youthful, girlish, boyish|词性:形容词

词源:  From "old" + "woman" + "-ly" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Old" traces back to Proto-Germanic *aldaz. "Woman" comes from Old English wīfmann (wife + man).  "-ly" comes from Old English -līc.
