intertrading:/ˌɪntərˈtreɪdɪŋ/
n.
相互贸易，内部贸易
The act or practice of trading between different departments or divisions within a company or organization, or between members of a particular group.
Intertrading between departments can lead to greater efficiency.  部门间的内部贸易可以提高效率。
Intertrading among the member states has increased significantly. 成员国之间的相互贸易显著增加。
internal trade, intracompany trade, interdepartmental trade
external trade
noun
