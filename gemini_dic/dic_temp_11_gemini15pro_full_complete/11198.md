ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *architectonicus*, from Greek *ἀρχιτεκτονικός (arkhitektonikos)*, from *ἀρχιτέκτων (arkhitektōn)* "master builder, architect," from *ἀρχι- (arkhi-)* "chief, master" + *τέκτων (tektōn)* "builder, carpenter."
