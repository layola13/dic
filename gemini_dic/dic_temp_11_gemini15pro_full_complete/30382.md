centicipitous:/ˌsɛntɪˈsɪpɪtəs/ | adj. | 陡峭的，险峻的；急促的，仓促的 | Characterized by a steep slope; precipitous; happening suddenly and quickly. |

例句：
The path became increasingly centicipitous as they climbed higher.
随着他们爬得越高，道路变得越来越陡峭。

The centicipitous decline in stock prices caused panic among investors.
股价的暴跌引发了投资者的恐慌。


近义词：precipitous, steep, sheer, abrupt |
反义词：gradual, gentle, sloping |
词性: adjective |


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *centiceps, from centum "hundred" + caput "head,"  referring to the many heads or peaks of a mountain.  The word is likely a later formation, influenced by "precipitous."
