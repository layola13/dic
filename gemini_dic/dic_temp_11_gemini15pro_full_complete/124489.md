mouldiest:/ˈmoʊldiɪst/ | adj. | 最发霉的 | Covered with mould to the greatest degree; most stale or musty. | The bread in the back of the cupboard was the mouldiest I'd ever seen.  橱柜后面的面包是我见过的最发霉的。|  The cheese was so mouldiest it had blue veins running through it.  奶酪太霉了，上面都长满了蓝色的霉菌。 | Synonyms: musty, fusty, stale, mildewy | Antonyms: fresh, new, clean | adjective

mouldy:/ˈmoʊldi/ | adj. | 发霉的 | Covered with or smelling of mould; stale or musty. | The old books in the attic smelled mouldy. 阁楼里的旧书闻起来有霉味。| The bread had gone mouldy because it had been left out too long. 面包发霉了，因为放的时间太长了。 | Synonyms: musty, fusty, stale, mildewy | Antonyms: fresh, new, clean | adjective
