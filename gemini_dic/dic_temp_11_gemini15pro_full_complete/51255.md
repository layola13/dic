desublimates:/ˌdiːˈsʌblɪmeɪts/
verb
凝华，沉积
The process by which a substance changes from a gas or vapor directly to a solid without first becoming a liquid.
The water vapor in the air desublimated into frost on the window.  空气中的水蒸气在窗户上凝华成了霜。

verb
(比喻)使(感情)具体化；使(理想)下降
To make (a feeling) concrete or (an ideal) less abstract.
The artist desublimated his emotions into a powerful painting.  艺术家将他的情感凝华成一幅强有力的画作。

近义词:deposit, condense, solidify
反义词:sublime, vaporize
词性:v.
