datival:/deɪˈtaɪvəl/| adj.|与格的，格的|Of, relating to, or being the dative case.  A dative construction or relationship. |The indirect object of a verb is in the dative case. 动词的间接宾语用与格。|近义词:indirect|反义词:nominative|词性:adjective

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The dative case marks the indirect object.  与格标记间接宾语。
He gives her a book. (Her is in the dative case) 他给她一本书。（Her 为与格）

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is writing a letter to him. (Him is indirectly affected and could be considered dative in some languages) 她正在给他写信。（Him 间接受到影响，在某些语言中可以被视为与格）
They are sending a package to their grandmother. (Their grandmother is indirectly affected)  他们正在给他们的祖母寄包裹。（他们的祖母间接受到影响）

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have given him the money. (Him is in the dative case) 我已经把钱给他了。（Him 为与格）
