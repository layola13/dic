inductric:/ɪnˈdʌktrɪk/ | adj. | 感应的；电感的 | Relating to inductance or induction, especially electromagnetic induction. | The inductric effect of the coil caused a voltage spike. 线圈的电感效应引起了电压尖峰。| 近义词: inductive | 反义词: non-inductive | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *inductus*, past participle of *inducere* ("to lead in"), combined with the suffix *-tric*, relating to electricity.  The word is not commonly used and "inductive" is generally preferred.
