drivers:/ˈdraɪvərz/| n.|司机，驾驶员；驱动程序；驱动器|A person who drives a vehicle.|例句：The bus driver is very experienced. 公共汽车司机经验丰富。|例句：She's a very careful driver. 她是一位非常谨慎的司机。|近义词：operator, chauffeur|反义词:passenger|noun

词源: From "drive" + "-er" (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Old English *drīfan.
