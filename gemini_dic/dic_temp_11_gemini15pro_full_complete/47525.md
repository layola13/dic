death-wish: /ˈdɛθ wɪʃ/ | n. |  死亡愿望; 求死慾望 | A desire for one's own death, either consciously or unconsciously expressed. | He seemed to have a death-wish, driving recklessly close to the edge of the cliff. 他似乎有求死的欲望，鲁莽地开车靠近悬崖边缘。| synonyms: suicidal impulse, self-destructive urge | antonyms: will to live, survival instinct | noun


death-wish: /ˈdɛθ wɪʃ/ | adj. |  玩命的; 亡命的 | Characterized by reckless behavior that suggests a desire for death. |  His death-wish driving terrified the passengers. 他玩命的驾驶方式吓坏了乘客。| synonyms: reckless, suicidal, daredevil | antonyms: cautious, careful, prudent | adjective
