apocrustic: /ˌæpəˈkrʌstɪk/
adjective
外用的; 用于体表的
Applied externally; for external use.
Apocrustic medications are applied directly to the skin. 外用药物直接涂抹于皮肤。
Apocrustic ointments can provide relief from skin irritations. 外用药膏可以缓解皮肤刺激。
近义词: topical, external
反义词: internal, systemic
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek apokroustikos, from apokrouein "to strike off," from apo- "off" + krouein "to strike."
