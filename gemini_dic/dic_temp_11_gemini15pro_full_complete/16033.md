bandage: /ˈbændɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈbændɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. vt.|绷带；包扎|A strip of material used to bind a wound or injury. |She wrapped a bandage around her injured ankle. 她用绷带包扎了她受伤的脚踝。|The nurse bandaged his arm. 护士用绷带包扎了他的胳膊。|近义词：dressing, wrap, plaster|反义词：(n.) wound, injury (referring to the thing a bandage covers); (v.) uncover, expose|词性: noun, verb


bandages: /ˈbændɪdʒɪz/ (US) /ˈbændɪdʒɪz/ (UK)| n. |绷带（复数）|Plural form of bandage. |He needed several bandages for his cuts. 他的伤口需要几条绷带。|The bandages were stained with blood. 绷带上沾满了血。|近义词：dressings, wraps|反义词：wounds, injuries (referring to the things bandages cover)|词性: noun
