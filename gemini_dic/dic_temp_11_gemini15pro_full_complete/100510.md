interverbal:/ˌɪntərˈvɜːrbl/
adjective
字间的，词间的
Occurring between verbs or verbal elements.  Relating to or involving interaction between verbs.  Existing or occurring between words.
The interverbal pauses were brief. 字词间的停顿很短。
近义词: interlexical, between words
反义词: intraverbal (occurring within a word)
词性: adjective
