archive:/ˈɑːrkaɪv/ (US), /ˈɑːkɪv/ (UK)| n. vt. adj.|档案，档案馆；存档；将…存档|A collection of historical documents or records providing information about the past.  To store such documents or records.  Relating to or constituting historical documents or records.|The library has a vast archive of medieval manuscripts. 图书馆藏有大量中世纪的手稿。| synonyms: record, annals, chronicle, register| antonyms:  delete, erase| noun

archive:/ˈɑːrkaɪv/ (US), /ˈɑːkɪv/ (UK)| vt.|存档，归档|To transfer data to a less frequently used storage medium, typically for long-term preservation.|I archived all my old emails. 我把所有旧邮件都存档了。| synonyms: file, store, save| antonyms: delete, erase| verb

archive:/ˈɑːrkaɪv/ (US), /ˈɑːkɪv/ (UK)| adj.|档案的，存档的|Relating to or constituting historical documents or records.|The archive footage showed the president's first inauguration. 档案录像显示了总统的第一次就职典礼。| synonyms: historical, recorded, documented| antonyms: current, contemporary| adjective
