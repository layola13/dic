op-artists:/ˈɒp ɑːtɪsts/
noun
欧普艺术家
Artists who create Op Art, a style of visual art that uses optical illusions.
Bridget Riley and Victor Vasarely are two of the most famous op-artists. 布里奇特·莱利和维克多·瓦萨雷利是两位最著名的欧普艺术家。
synonyms: optical artists
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider artists of other movements, e.g., abstract expressionists)
词性: noun

op-artist:/ˈɒp ɑːtɪst/
