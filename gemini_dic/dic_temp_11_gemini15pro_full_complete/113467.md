lyra:/ˈlaɪrə/ /ˈlɪərə/ | n. | 天琴座; 七弦琴 |A constellation in the northern hemisphere near Cygnus and Draco, containing the bright star Vega.  A lyre. |  Example: The Lyra constellation is easily recognizable by its bright star, Vega. 天琴座很容易通过其明亮的恒星织女星来识别。

Example:  Orpheus played a beautiful melody on his lyra. 奥菲斯用他的七弦琴演奏了一曲优美的旋律。 | Near Synonyms: lyre, harp | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
