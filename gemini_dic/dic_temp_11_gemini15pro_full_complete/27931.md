cantilever bridge:/ˈkæntɪˌlivər brɪdʒ/ |n.|悬臂桥|A bridge built using cantilevers, which are horizontal beams supported at only one end.|The Forth Bridge in Scotland is a famous example of a cantilever bridge.  苏格兰的福斯桥是著名的悬臂桥的例子。|近义词:beam bridge, girder bridge|反义词:suspension bridge|noun
