hyposkeletal:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈskɛlɪtəl/
adjective
次骨骼的；骨骼减少的
Situated beneath or on the lower side of the skeleton.  Relating to or designating reduced skeletal structures.
The hyposkeletal element in some fish supports the lower fins.  某些鱼类的次骨骼元件支撑着下面的鳍。
The fossil showed evidence of hyposkeletal reduction, suggesting adaptation to a less active lifestyle. 这块化石显示了骨骼减少的证据，表明它适应了不太活跃的生活方式。
近义词: underdeveloped skeleton (发育不全的骨骼), reduced skeleton (骨骼减少的)
反义词: hyperskeletal (骨骼过度的), well-developed skeleton (发育良好的骨骼)
词性: adjective
