flashpoint:/ˈflæʃpɔɪnt/| n.| 临界点；危险点|Tensions in the region have reached flashpoint. 该地区的紧张局势已达到临界点。|The issue of immigration has become a political flashpoint. 移民问题已成为一个政治上的危险点。|Synonyms: crisis point, critical point, danger point|Antonyms: safe point, stability|noun
