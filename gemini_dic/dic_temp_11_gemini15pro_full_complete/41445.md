cornflour:/ˈkɔːnflɑʊər(UK) /ˈkɔːrnflaʊər(US)/| n. |玉米淀粉；玉米粉|A fine white flour made from corn, used in cooking as a thickener for sauces, soups, etc.|I used cornflour to thicken the gravy. 我用玉米粉来使肉汁变稠。|Synonym: cornstarch (US), corn starch|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps wheat flour as an alternative thickener)|noun

cornflour:/ˈkɔːnflɑʊər(UK) /ˈkɔːrnflaʊər(US)/| n. |玉米淀粉；玉米粉|Used to describe something resembling the texture of cornflour, especially in terms of fineness.|The sand was as fine as cornflour. 沙子像玉米粉一样细。|Synonym: powdery, fine|Antonym: coarse, granular|noun
