overbend:/ˌoʊvərˈbɛnd/| v. n.|过度弯曲，过分弯曲|To bend something beyond its normal or intended limit.  Also, the act of bending something too far. |The guitarist overbent the string, causing it to snap. 吉他手过度弯曲琴弦，导致琴弦断裂。|近义词：overflex, strain, overstrain|反义词:underbend, straighten|词性:动词,名词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "bend" (verb meaning to force something straight into a curved or angular shape).
