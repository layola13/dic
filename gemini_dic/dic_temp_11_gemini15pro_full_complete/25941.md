burntly:/ˈbɜːntli/
adverb
焦灼地，烧焦似地
in a burnt manner; as if burnt.  Characterized by the effects of burning.
The steak was burntly cooked. 牛排烤焦了。
近义词:scorched, charred, singed
反义词:raw, uncooked
词性:副词

词源: From "burnt" (past participle of "burn") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The suffix "-ly" transforms adjectives into adverbs, indicating manner or degree.
