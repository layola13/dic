deva:/ˈdeɪvə/| n.|神，神灵|A god or deity, especially in Hinduism.|The deva is revered by many. 这个神灵受到许多人的崇敬。|近义词：god, deity|反义词：demon, devil|词性:名词

Etymology: From Sanskrit *devaḥ* "god, celestial being," from Proto-Indo-European *deiwós* "god, bright," from the root *dyeu-* "to shine."  Related to Latin *deus* "god" and Greek *Zeus*.
