insalubrity:/ˌɪnsəˈlubrɪti/
n.
不健康，不卫生；有害健康
The state of being insalubrious; unhealthfulness.
The insalubrity of the swamp made it a breeding ground for mosquitoes.  沼泽地不健康的环境使其成为蚊子的滋生地。
近义词：unhealthfulness, unwholesomeness, noxiousness
反义词：healthfulness, salubrity, wholesomeness
词性：名词


insalubrious:/ˌɪnsəˈlubriəs/
adj.
不健康的，有害健康的
Not conducive to health; unhealthy.
The miners worked in insalubrious conditions. 矿工们在不健康的条件下工作。
近义词：unhealthy, unwholesome, noxious
反义词：healthy, wholesome, salubrious
词性：形容词

词源：
From French *insalubre*, from Latin *insalubris* (“unhealthy”), from *in-* (“not”) + *salubris* (“healthy”).
