academian:/ˌækəˈdiːmiən/
n.|学者，大学教师，研究院成员|A person who belongs to an academy or is a scholar of a particular academic discipline. | Example: The renowned academician delivered a lecture on the history of philosophy.  这位著名的学者做了一个关于哲学史的讲座。| Synonym: scholar, intellectual, academic | Antonym: ignoramus, philistine
词性: 名词
