firemen:/ˈfaɪərmən/ (plural firemen) /ˈfaɪəmən/ (plural firemen)| n. |消防员|A person whose job is to extinguish fires.|例句：The firemen bravely fought the blaze. 消防员勇敢地与大火搏斗。

例句：Firemen rushed to the scene of the accident. 消防队员冲到事故现场。|近义词：firefighter, smoke eater|反义词: arsonist (纵火犯 -  more of an antonym to the action, not the profession itself)|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fire + man.  The term "fireman" has become less common in modern usage, with the gender-neutral term "firefighter" being preferred.
