counterpicketing:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɪkɪtɪŋ/| n.|反纠察，对峙纠察|The union organized counterpicketing to oppose the company's lockout. 工会组织了反纠察来反对公司的停工。|例句：The counterpicketing was intense, with both sides shouting slogans. 反纠察活动非常激烈，双方都在高喊口号。

例句：The company hired security guards to protect its property during the counterpicketing.  公司雇佣了保安在反纠察期间保护其财产。


近义词：counter-demonstration, counter-protest|反义词:picketing|词性:名词

Etymology:
counter- (prefix) + picketing (noun)

"counter-" comes from Old French "contre-" meaning "against" and ultimately from Latin "contra".

"picketing" comes from the French "piquet" (pointed stake, picket) and refers to the practice of placing pickets (people) at a location as a form of protest.
