nonmonetarist:/ˌnɒnˈmʌnɪtərɪst/ | adj. | 非货币主义者的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a school of economic thought that does not regard control of the money supply as the chief means of stabilizing the economy. |  The government's nonmonetarist approach focused on fiscal policy rather than interest rates. 政府的非货币主义方法侧重于财政政策而不是利率。 |  non-Keynesian, supply-side | monetarist | adjective
