circularity:/ˌsɜːrkjəˈlærəti/ | n. |循环性，圆形，迂回 |The argument suffers from circularity.  这个论证存在循环论证的缺陷。|circularity is the property of being circular, or of moving in a circle. 圆形性是指呈圆形或圆周运动的特性。|例句：The circularity of his argument made it unconvincing. 他的论点循环论证，令人难以信服。

The project demonstrates a commitment to circularity in resource use. 该项目展示了对资源利用循环性的承诺。


近义词：roundness, circular motion, cycle, loop |反义词:linearity, straightness |词性:noun


Etymology: From Late Latin *circularitas, from Latin circularis (“circular”) + -itas. Equivalent to circular +‎ -ity.
