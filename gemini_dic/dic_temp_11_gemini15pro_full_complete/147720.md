overpresumptuous: /ˌoʊvərprɪˈzʌmptʃuəs/
adj.
过于自信的；自以为是的；放肆的
Excessively forward; taking liberties.  Characterized by presumption; arrogant.
He made an overpresumptuous remark about the boss's wife. 他对老板妻子的评论过于放肆。
She was overpresumptuous in assuming she would get the promotion. 她自以为是地认为她会得到晋升。
Synonyms: arrogant, presumptuous, audacious, impertinent, insolent, forward
Antonyms: humble, modest, reserved, diffident, unassuming
词性: 形容词
