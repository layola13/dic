albite:/ˈælbʌɪt/ | n. | 钠长石 | A plagioclase feldspar mineral, NaAlSi3O8, that is typically white or off-white. | Albite is a common constituent of many igneous and metamorphic rocks. 钠长石是许多火成岩和变质岩的常见成分。| Albite often occurs in granite pegmatites. 钠长石常产于花岗伟晶岩中。 | 近义词: plagioclase, feldspar | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *albus* meaning "white," referring to its typical color.
