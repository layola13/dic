illiberal:/ɪˈlɪbərəl/ | adj. | 不开明的，狭隘的，吝啬的 | Not liberal; narrow-minded, bigoted; stingy. |  例句：His illiberal views on immigration are offensive. 他在移民问题上不开明的观点令人反感。

例句：The illiberal donation showed his lack of generosity.  他那吝啬的捐赠表明了他缺乏慷慨。

近义词：narrow-minded, bigoted, intolerant, prejudiced, ungenerous, mean | 反义词：liberal, tolerant, open-minded, generous, magnanimous | 词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *illiberalis*, from in- "not" + liberalis "liberal."  The word "liberal" ultimately derives from Latin liber "free."
