aerolite:/ˈɛə(r)əˌlaɪt/ | n. |陨石；陨星|A stony meteorite. A meteorite consisting of stony material, as distinguished from a metallic one (a siderite).|例句：The museum displayed a large aerolite that had fallen in the previous century.  博物馆展出了一块上个世纪坠落的巨大陨石。

例句：Scientists analyzed the aerolite to determine its composition. 科学家们分析了这块陨石以确定其成分。|近义词：meteorite, meteor, falling star|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
