jarring:/ˈdʒɑːrɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdʒɑːrɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.| 刺耳的；令人不快的；震惊的；不和谐的|  Creating a harsh and unpleasant effect on the senses, especially the ears or the nerves; causing a feeling of shock or unease; incongruous or unsuitable.| The jarring sound of the alarm clock woke me up. 刺耳的闹钟声把我吵醒了。| synonyms: grating, harsh, strident, discordant, clashing, shocking, upsetting, disturbing| antonyms: soothing, harmonious, pleasant, agreeable, comforting| adjective

jarring:/ˈdʒɑːrɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdʒɑːrɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 震动；冲突；刺耳的声音| A harsh, unpleasant, or shocking effect.| The jarring of the train as it went over the tracks kept me awake. 火车经过铁轨时的震动让我无法入睡。| synonyms: jolt, shock, clash, discordance| antonyms: harmony, agreement, concord| noun
