HIPAA:/ˈhɪpə/ (US), /ˈhɪpɑː/ (UK)| n. (abbr.)|健康保险流通与责任法案 (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)|A US law designed to provide privacy standards to protect patients' medical records and other health information provided to health plans, doctors, hospitals and other health care providers.|例句：The hospital is HIPAA compliant. 这家医院遵守HIPAA规定。|近义词：patient privacy act, healthcare privacy law|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider concepts like "public disclosure," "data breach")|abbreviation
