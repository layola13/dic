deprecated:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.|已弃用的，不赞成的|No longer recommended or supported; discouraged from further use.  It often suggests that something better is available.|This function is deprecated and will be removed in the next version.  这个函数已被弃用，将在下一个版本中删除。|obsolete, discouraged, outmoded, superseded|supported, current, recommended|adjective

近义词: obsolete, discouraged, outmoded, superseded
反义词: supported, current, recommended
词性: adjective (形容词)

词源: From Latin *deprecatus*, past participle of *deprecari* "to pray against, avert by prayer," from *de-* "down, away" + *precari* "to pray." The meaning shifted from "to express disapproval of" to "to mark as obsolete."
