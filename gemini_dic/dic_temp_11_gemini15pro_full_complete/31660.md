chatteringly:/ˈtʃætərɪŋli/ | adv. |喋喋不休地，絮絮叨叨地 |In a manner characterized by constant or excessive talking, often about unimportant things. |She described the events of the day chatteringly to her friend. 她喋喋不休地向朋友讲述当天发生的事情。|例句：He chatteringly recounted his adventures abroad.  他喋喋不休地讲述了他在国外的冒险经历。| 近义词:loquaciously, volubly, garrulously | 反义词: taciturnly, quietly, silently | 词性: 副词

chatter:/ˈtʃætər/ | n. vi. vt.| 喋喋不休，饶舌 |rapid indistinct talk, typically about unimportant matters; talk rapidly and indistinctly. | The constant chatter of the children made it difficult to concentrate. 孩子们不停的叽叽喳喳让人难以集中注意力。|例句：The monkeys chattered excitedly in the trees. 猴子在树上兴奋地吱吱喳喳叫个不停。| 近义词: prattle, jabber, babble | 反义词: silence, quiet | 词性:名词，动词
