hippie's:/ˈhɪpiz/| n.|嬉皮士的（所有格）|Belonging to or associated with a hippie.  It's the possessive form of "hippie".|例句：The hippie's van was painted with psychedelic colors.  嬉皮士的货车被涂上了迷幻的色彩。

例句：I found a hippie's headband in the attic. 我在阁楼里发现了一个嬉皮士的头带。|近义词：bohemian's, counter-culture's|反义词：conformist's, traditionalist's|词性:noun (possessive)


ETYMOLOGY->
From "hippie," shortened from "hipster," which originated in the 1940s jazz scene.  "Hip" meant "aware" or "in the know," referring to those familiar with the subculture. "Hippie" gained popularity in the 1960s counterculture movement.
