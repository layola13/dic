drugged:/drʌɡd/
adjective
用药的；吸毒的；昏昏沉沉的
Affected by drugs; intoxicated.

The drugged driver was arrested after causing a serious accident.
那个吸毒的司机在造成严重事故后被捕了。

He stumbled around in a drugged stupor.
他脚步踉跄，一副吸毒后昏昏沉沉的样子。

Synonyms: intoxicated, doped, narcotized, sedated, stoned
Antonyms: sober, clear-headed
词性: adjective

Etymology: From drug + -ed.  "Drug" comes from Old French "drogue" of uncertain origin, possibly from Middle Dutch "droge vate," meaning "dry barrels," referring to dried herbs and plants used in medicine.
