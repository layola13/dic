convenient: /kənˈviːniənt/ (US) /kənˈviːnjənt/ (UK)| adj.|方便的，便利的|Suitable for one's needs; affording ease or advantage.|The store is conveniently located near my house. 这家商店很方便，就在我家附近。|It’s very convenient to pay by credit card.  用信用卡支付很方便。|synonyms: easy, handy, suitable, accessible, opportune|antonyms: inconvenient, awkward, difficult, cumbersome|adjective
