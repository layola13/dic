airily:/ˈɛərɪli/ | adv. | 轻快地，轻松地，无忧无虑地 | In a lighthearted and carefree manner; casually. | She airily dismissed his concerns. 她轻描淡写地打消了他的顾虑。|  Synonyms: lightly, casually, nonchalantly, flippantly | Antonyms: seriously, gravely, solemnly | 词性: 副词

airily:/ˈɛərɪli/ | adv. | 空气流通地，通风良好地 | With free movement of air; in a breezy way. | The curtains billowed airily in the breeze.  窗帘在微风中轻盈地飘动。| Synonyms: breezily, ventilated | Antonyms: stuffily, stiflingly | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From airy + -ly.  "Airy" comes from Middle English *airi, from Old English *ǣrġe, from ǣr "air" + -ġe (suffix forming adjectives).
