animosities:/ˌænɪˈmɒsɪtiz/
n.
敌意，仇恨
strong hostility; active hatred

There's no place for personal animosities in the workplace.  工作场所不容许个人恩怨。

反义词:friendship, goodwill, amity, cordiality
词性:noun (plural)
