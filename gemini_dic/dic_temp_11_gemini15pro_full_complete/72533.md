flustered:/ˈflʌstərd/| adj.|慌乱的，不安的|Agitated and confused.|She became flustered when she realized she had forgotten her lines. 她意识到自己忘了台词，变得慌乱起来。|The unexpected question left him flustered and unable to answer.  突如其来的问题让他不知所措，无法回答。|近义词：agitated, nervous, disconcerted, ruffled|反义词：calm, composed, collected|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb *fluster*, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse *flaustr* "hurry, haste".
