isoperimetrical:/ˌaɪsoʊˌpɛrɪˈmɛtrɪkəl/
adjective
等周的
Having the same perimeter.  Relating to figures having the same perimeter but different areas.
Example: Isoperimetrical problems in mathematics explore shapes with the same perimeter.  数学中的等周问题探讨了具有相同周长的形状。
Example:  The isoperimetrical theorem proves that a circle encloses the greatest area for a given perimeter. 等周定理证明，对于给定的周长，圆所包围的面积最大。
Synonyms:  equilateral (in some contexts)
Antonyms:  anisoperimetrical (rarely used)
词性: adjective
