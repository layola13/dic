bandlet: /ˈbændlɪt/
n.
小带，细带子；(尤指)发带
A small band or strip, especially one worn around the hair.
She wore a velvet bandlet in her hair. 她头上戴着一条天鹅绒发带。

近义词: ribbon, band, strap, fillet
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps something large and loose like "shawl" if contrasting size and fit)

词性: noun

词源:  From "band" + the diminutive suffix "-let." "Band" comes from Old Norse "band," meaning "bond, fetter."
