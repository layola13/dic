chico:/ˈtʃiːkoʊ/ (American English) /ˈtʃiːkəʊ/ (British English)| n. |男孩，小伙子，家伙|A boy or young man.  It's also a familiar or informal term of address.|Example: Hey chico, what's up? 嘿，小伙子，怎么了？|Synonym: boy, lad, fellow, guy|Antonym: girl, woman|Noun

chico:/ˈtʃiːkoʊ/ (American English) /ˈtʃiːkəʊ/ (British English)| adj. |小的|Small; little (primarily used in the American Southwest and California).|Example:  They live in a chico little town. 他们住在一个小城镇里。|Synonym: small, little, tiny|Antonym: large, big, huge|Adjective
