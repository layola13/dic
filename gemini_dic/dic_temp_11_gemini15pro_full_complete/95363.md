incertum:/ɪnˈsɜːrtəm/
adjective
不确定的，不肯定的，未决定的
uncertain, unsure, undetermined; not definitely known
例句：The outcome of the election remains incertum.  选举结果仍未确定。

例句：The future of the project is incertum.  这个项目的未来是不确定的。

近义词：uncertain, doubtful, dubious, questionable, indeterminate
反义词：certain, definite, sure, resolved, determined
词性：adjective
