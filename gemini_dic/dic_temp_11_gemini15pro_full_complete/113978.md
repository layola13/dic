magical:/ˈmædʒɪkl/| adj.|神奇的，不可思议的，迷人的|Having the qualities of magic; enchanting.|例句：The sunset painted the sky in magical hues.  日落将天空染上了神奇的色彩。

例句：It was a truly magical experience. 这真是一次神奇的体验。|近义词：enchanted, enchanting, charmed, spellbinding, wondrous, miraculous|反义词：mundane, ordinary, commonplace|词性：形容词

词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin *magicalis, from Latin magicus (see magic) + -al.
