discerners:/dɪˈsɜːrnərz/
n.
鉴赏家；识别者；洞察者
People who are able to judge the quality of something, or to understand something clearly.
The true discerners of art will appreciate the subtle nuances of this painting.  真正的艺术鉴赏家会欣赏这幅画的微妙之处。
Experienced wine discerners can identify different grape varieties by taste.  经验丰富的葡萄酒鉴赏家可以通过味道识别不同的葡萄品种。
synonyms: connoisseurs, judges, experts, analysts, critics
antonyms: ignoramuses, fools, simpletons
noun
