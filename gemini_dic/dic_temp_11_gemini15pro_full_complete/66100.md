exceeder:/ɪkˈsiːdər/
noun
超越者;胜过者
One that exceeds, especially one that surpasses a limit or expectation.
The company is an exceeder in customer service.  这家公司在客户服务方面非常出色。
Superior, surpasser, outdoer
Laggard, inferior
