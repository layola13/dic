eversive:/ɪˈvɜːsɪv/ | adj. | 颠覆性的；破坏性的 | Tending or seeking to overthrow, subvert, or destroy an established government, institution, or belief. |  The government accused the group of spreading eversive propaganda. 政府指控该组织散布颠覆性宣传。

The book was banned for its eversive ideas. 这本书因其颠覆性的思想而被禁。 | 近义词: subversive, revolutionary, rebellious, destructive, disruptive | 反义词: constructive, supportive, conservative, stabilizing | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ēversus (past participle of ēvertere, "to overturn"), from ex- ("out") + vertere ("to turn").
