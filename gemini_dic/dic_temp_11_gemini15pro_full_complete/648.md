abridges:/əˈbrɪdʒɪz/| v.| 缩短，删节，节略|  shortens, condenses, or reduces the length of (a book, film, speech, etc.) while preserving its basic content. |  The author abridges his lengthy manuscript to make it more suitable for publication.  作者删节了他冗长的手稿，以使其更适合出版。 |  shorten, condense, curtail | extend, lengthen, expand | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English abregen, abregge, from Old French abregier, from Latin abbreviāre (“to shorten”), from ad (“to”) + brevis (“short”).
