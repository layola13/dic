macruran:/məˈkrʊərən/| adj. n.|长尾的；长尾类动物|Relating to or denoting macruran crustaceans, which have a long abdomen and include shrimps, prawns, lobsters, and crayfish.  Having a long tail (especially of a crustacean).|The macruran was quickly identified as a lobster. 这只长尾类动物很快被鉴定为龙虾。|The fisherman caught a large macruran. 渔夫捕到了一只大型长尾类动物。|近义词：long-tailed, decapod|反义词: brachyuran|词性: adjective, noun
