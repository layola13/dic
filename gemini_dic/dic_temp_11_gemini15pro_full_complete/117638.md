megastructure:/ˈmɛɡəˌstrʌktʃər/| n.|巨型结构，超大规模结构|A very large artificial structure, such as a Dyson sphere or a ringworld, often hypothetical and associated with advanced civilizations.|例句：Scientists have theorized about the possibility of alien civilizations building megastructures.|科学家们已经对建造巨型结构的外星文明的可能性进行了理论研究。

megastructure:/ˈmɛɡəˌstrʌktʃər/| n.|巨型结构，超大规模结构|A vast and complex structure, typically a large-scale engineering project.|例句：The proposed bridge across the strait would be a megastructure of unprecedented scale.|提议的海峡大桥将是一个前所未有的巨型结构。|近义词：massive structure, colossal structure, immense structure|反义词: miniature, small structure|词性:noun
