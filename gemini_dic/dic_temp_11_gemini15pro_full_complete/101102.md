inversion:/ɪnˈvɜːrʒən/ | n. | 倒置，反转；逆序；(染色体)倒位 | a reversal of the normal order of something; a change in position of two things so that they are the opposite way round | The inversion of the social order led to chaos. 社会秩序的颠覆导致了混乱。| reversal, transposition, flip | order, arrangement | noun

inversion:/ɪnˈvɜːrʒən/ | n. | (气温)逆增 | a situation in which the air temperature increases with increasing altitude |  The temperature inversion trapped pollutants near the ground. 逆温导致污染物滞留在地面附近。|  layer, trapping | normal temperature gradient | noun
