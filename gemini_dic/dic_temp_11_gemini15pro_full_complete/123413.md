monophysitical: /ˌmɒnəˈfɪzɪkəl/ /ˌmɑːnəˈfɪzɪkəl/
adjective
一性论的
Relating to or characterized by Monophysitism, a theological doctrine asserting that Christ has only one nature, which is divine, as opposed to the orthodox doctrine of two natures, divine and human.
The Coptic Church, historically, has been considered monophysitical.  科普特教会在历史上一直被认为是一性论的。
The monophysitical doctrine was condemned at the Council of Chalcedon.  一性论教义在迦克墩会议上被定罪。
近义词: Miaphysite (this term is often preferred as it avoids negative connotations), Eutychian
反义词: Dyophysite
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *monos* (one) + *physis* (nature)
