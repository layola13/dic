anti-priest:/ˌæntiˈpriːst/
adjective
反教权主义的；反对神职人员的
opposed to priests or the priesthood; anticlerical.
The anti-priest sentiment grew during the revolution. 革命期间反教权主义情绪高涨。
Synonyms: anticlerical, secularist, irreligious
Antonyms: pro-clerical, religious, devout
adjective


anti-priest:/ˌæntiˈpriːst/
noun
反对神职人员的人
a person who is opposed to priests or the priesthood.
The anti-priests were vocal in their criticism of the church. 这些反教权主义者公开批评教会。
Synonyms:  critic, opponent, dissenter
Antonyms: supporter, advocate, devotee
noun
