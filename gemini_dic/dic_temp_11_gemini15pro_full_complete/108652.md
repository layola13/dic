leafiest:/ˈliːfiɪst/| adj.|最多叶的；枝叶最茂盛的|Having the most leaves; most leafy.  This adjective is the superlative form of 'leafy'.|The leafiest tree in the park provided ample shade. 公园里枝叶最茂盛的树提供了充足的阴凉。|近义词：lushest, greenest, densest|反义词：barest, sparsest|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From leaf + -y + -est.  "Leaf" comes from Old English *lēaf, of Germanic origin.  "-y" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of."  "-est" is a suffix forming the superlative degree of adjectives.
