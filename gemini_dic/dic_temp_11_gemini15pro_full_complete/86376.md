hederaceous:/ˌhedəˈreɪʃəs/
adjective
似常春藤的；有常春藤状叶子的
Resembling ivy; having leaves like ivy.
The hederaceous plant climbed up the wall.  这株似常春藤的植物爬上了墙。
The wall was covered with a hederaceous vine. 墙上覆盖着一种常春藤状的藤蔓。
近义词:ivy-like,  vine-like
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "non-climbing" or describing a specific leaf shape that contrasts with ivy)
词性: adjective
