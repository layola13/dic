misspecified:/ˌmɪsˈspesɪfaɪd/
adjective
指定错误的，设定错误的
incorrectly specified; having the wrong specifications.
The model was misspecified, leading to inaccurate predictions.  这个模型被指定错误，导致预测不准确。
The misspecified equation yielded nonsensical results.  指定错误的方程式产生了荒谬的结果。
Synonyms: misstated, inaccurate, incorrect, flawed, erroneous
Antonyms: correctly specified, accurate, correct, precise, valid
词性: adjective
