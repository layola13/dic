frangipani:/ˌfrændʒɪˈpɑːni/ | n. |鸡蛋花，素馨花 |A tropical American tree or shrub with clusters of fragrant white, yellow, or pink flowers. It is widely cultivated as an ornamental. |例句：The scent of frangipani filled the air. 空气中弥漫着鸡蛋花的香味。

例句：She wore a frangipani blossom in her hair. 她头发上戴着一朵鸡蛋花。|近义词：plumeria |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

frangipanis:/ˌfrændʒɪˈpɑːniz/ | n. |鸡蛋花（复数）|Plural form of frangipani.  Refers to multiple frangipani trees, shrubs, or flowers. |例句：The frangipanis in the garden were in full bloom. 花园里的鸡蛋花盛开了。

ETYMOLOGY->

来自16世纪的意大利贵族，侯爵穆齐奥·弗兰吉帕尼，他的香水闻起来和这种花很相似。
