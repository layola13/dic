evenly:/ˈiːvənli/| adv.|均匀地，平坦地，平等地|In a smooth, uniform, or consistent manner; equally balanced.|The food was evenly distributed among the guests. 食物被均匀地分给了客人们。|The paint was applied evenly to the wall. 油漆被均匀地涂抹在墙上。|近义词：uniformly, equally, smoothly, equitably|反义词:unevenly, irregularly, disproportionately|词性:副词
词源:From Old English *efenlīce (attested only in unefenlīce "unevenly"), from efen "even" +‎ -līce "-ly." Cognate with Dutch evenlijk, German ebenlich.
