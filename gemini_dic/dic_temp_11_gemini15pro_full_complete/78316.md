gerontocratic: /ˌdʒɛrənˈtɒkrætɪk/ | adj. | 老年政治的；由老人统治的 | Relating to or characterized by gerontocracy, a form of social organization in which the elderly hold the most political power. |  The country's gerontocratic leadership was resistant to change.  这个国家的老年政治领导层抵制变革。 |  The gerontocratic system hindered the progress of younger generations.  老年政治制度阻碍了年轻一代的进步。 | 近义词: patriarchal, oligarchical | 反义词: youthful, democratic | 词性: adjective

gerontocracy: /ˌdʒɛrənˈtɒkrəsi/ | n. | 老年政治；老人统治 | A state, society, or group governed by old people. | The island nation was essentially a gerontocracy, with all major decisions made by a council of elders.  这个岛国本质上是一个老年政治国家，所有重大决策都由长老会做出。 |  Some argue that the company's board has become a gerontocracy, stifling innovation.  有人认为，该公司的董事会已经变成了一个老年政治机构，扼杀了创新。 | 近义词: patriarchy, oligarchy | 反义词: democracy, meritocracy | 词性: noun
