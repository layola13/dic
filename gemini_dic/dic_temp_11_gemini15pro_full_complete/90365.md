一般现在时 (Simple Present):
How do you go to school? 你怎么去上学？
How does she cook so well? 她是怎么做得这么好吃的？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
How are you feeling today? 你今天感觉怎么样？
How is he doing with his project? 他的项目进展如何？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
How have you been? 你最近怎么样？
How has she managed to do that? 她是怎么做到的？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
How have you been spending your time? 你这段时间都在做什么？
How has he been coping with the stress? 他是如何应对压力的？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
How did you get here? 你是怎么来这儿的？
How did she solve the problem? 她是怎么解决这个问题的？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
How were you doing before the incident? 事故发生前你怎么样？
How was she handling the situation? 她当时是如何处理情况的？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
How had you prepared for the exam? 你考试前是怎么准备的？
How had she managed to finish so quickly? 她怎么这么快就完成了？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
How had you been feeling before you went to the doctor? 你去看医生之前感觉怎么样？
How had she been managing the company before she resigned? 她辞职前是如何管理公司的？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
How will you travel to Europe? 你将如何去欧洲旅行？
How will she manage without a car? 没有车她将如何应对？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
How will you be celebrating your birthday? 你将如何庆祝你的生日？
How will she be spending her vacation? 她将如何度过假期？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
How will you have finished the project by then? 到那时你将如何完成这个项目？
How will she have achieved so much in such a short time? 她在这么短的时间内将如何取得如此大的成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
How will you have been preparing for the competition? 你将如何为比赛做准备？
How will she have been managing her time with two jobs?  她将如何兼顾两份工作来安排她的时间？



疑问句:
How old are you? 你多大了？
How much does it cost?  这多少钱？

否定句:
I don't know how to do it. 我不知道怎么做。
She doesn't understand how it works. 她不明白这是怎么运作的。

虚拟语气:
I wouldn't know how to react if that happened. 如果发生那种事，我不知道该如何反应。
She wishes she knew how to fix it. 她希望她知道如何解决它。

被动语态: (How is rarely used in the passive voice relating to its core meaning.  The examples below demonstrate "how" in a passive construction, but the focus is not on the manner of action.)
How was the problem addressed?  这个问题是如何被处理的？
How is the information being disseminated? 信息是如何被传播的？
