heterotropous:/ˌhɛtərɒˈtrɒpəs/| adj.|异养的，倒生的| (of a plant ovule) inverted so that the micropyle is close to the funiculus. (植物胚珠)倒生的，珠孔靠近珠柄的。| Example 1:  The orchid has a heterotropous ovule.  这株兰花有倒生的胚珠。

Example 2: Heterotropous ovules are common in many flowering plants.  倒生胚珠在许多开花植物中很常见。|Synonym: inverted, anatropous (somewhat)|Antonym: orthotropous, atropous|Word type: adjective
