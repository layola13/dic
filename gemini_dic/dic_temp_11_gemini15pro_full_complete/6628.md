anaerobically:/ˌænəˈrəʊbɪkli/ (US) /ˌænəˈrɒbɪkli/ (UK)| adv.|厌氧地，无氧地|In a way that does not require oxygen for growth or survival.|Certain bacteria can live anaerobically.  某些细菌可以在无氧环境下生存。|The yeast ferments the sugar anaerobically. 酵母在无氧条件下发酵糖。|近义词: without oxygen|反义词: aerobically|词性:副词

词源:
From the Greek prefix *an-* (meaning "without"), *aer-* (meaning "air"), and *bios* (meaning "life"), combined with the suffix *-ically* to form an adverb.
