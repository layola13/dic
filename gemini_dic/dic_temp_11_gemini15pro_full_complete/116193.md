mashup:/ˈmæʃʌp/ (US) /ˈmæʃʌp/ (UK)| n.|混搭; 混合作品; (尤指)混搭歌曲|A mashup is a creative work, usually in digital form, that combines or blends pre-existing materials to create a new work.  Specifically referring to music, it's a song or composition created by blending two or more pre-recorded songs, usually by overlaying the vocal track of one song seamlessly over the instrumental track of another.|Example:  The DJ played a popular mashup of two classic rock anthems.  DJ播放了一首由两首经典摇滚歌曲混搭而成的流行歌曲。

Example: I listened to a mashup of my favorite pop songs on YouTube. 我在YouTube上听了我最喜欢的流行歌曲的混搭版本。|近义词：mix, blend, remix, fusion|反义词:original, separate|词性:名词
