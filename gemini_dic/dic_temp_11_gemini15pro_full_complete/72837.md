foliole:/ˈfoʊliˌoʊl/ | n. |小叶；复叶中的小叶|A leaflet of a compound leaf.  A small leaf-like part or division. |The fern's delicate fronds are composed of numerous folioles.|Synonyms: leaflet, pinna, pinnule|Antonyms: leaf (in the context of a simple leaf, not compound)|noun
