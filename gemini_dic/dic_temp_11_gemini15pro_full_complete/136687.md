nonsecure:/ˌnɒnsɪˈkjʊə(r)/ | adj. |不安全的，不牢靠的|Not safe or protected; vulnerable to attack or unauthorized access.  | 例句： The website uses a nonsecure connection, making it vulnerable to hackers.  该网站使用不安全的连接，容易受到黑客攻击。

例句：Storing sensitive information on a nonsecure server is a serious risk. 在不安全的服务器上存储敏感信息是一个严重的风险。 | 近义词：insecure, unsafe, unprotected, vulnerable | 反义词：secure, safe, protected | 词性:形容词

Etymology:  non- + secure.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Secure" comes from the Latin "securus," meaning "free from care, safe."
