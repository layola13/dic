odalisque:/ˈoʊdəlɪsk/ | n. | 女奴，侍妾 | A female slave or concubine, especially one in a harem.  Typically depicted in art as reclining sensuously. | The painter's most famous work featured a reclining odalisque.  画家最著名的作品以一个斜倚的女奴为特色。 | Synonyms: concubine, handmaid | Antonyms: master, mistress | noun
