affray:/əˈfreɪ/
noun
争吵，斗殴
a noisy, public fight or disturbance
例句：Two men were arrested after an affray outside a pub.  两个男人在酒吧外斗殴后被捕。

例句：The police were called to break up the affray. 警察被叫来制止这场斗殴。

近义词：brawl, fight, fray, melee, rumpus, scuffle
反义词：peace, calm, tranquility
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *esfreer "to frighten," from Vulgar Latin *exfridare, from Latin ex- "out" + *fridus "peace" (from Germanic *frithu- "peace"). The sense shifted from "frighten" to "disturb the peace" and finally to "a public fight."
