docu-drama: /ˈdɑːkjuːˌdrɑːmə/ | n. | 纪录片式戏剧；纪实剧 | A genre of film and television that dramatizes real-life events, often using a mixture of documentary techniques and fictionalized scenes. |  Example: The docu-drama about the Titanic disaster was both informative and moving.  泰坦尼克号灾难的纪录片式戏剧既信息丰富又感人至深。

Example:  The docu-drama blurred the lines between fact and fiction.  这部纪录片式戏剧模糊了事实与虚构的界限。 | 近义词: docudrama, documentary drama | 反义词: fiction, fantasy | 词性: 名词
