hyperromantic:/ˌhaɪpərroʊˈmæntɪk/ | adj. |过度浪漫的，极度浪漫的 | Excessively or unrealistically romantic; characterized by an idealized or exaggerated view of love.  | She has a hyperromantic view of relationships, expecting them to be like something out of a fairytale. 她对恋爱关系有着过度浪漫的看法，期望它们像童话故事里那样。 |  例句：He wrote her hyperromantic poems filled with flowery language and grand gestures. 他给她写了充满华丽辞藻和夸张姿态的过度浪漫的诗歌。 | 近义词：overly romantic, excessively sentimental, starry-eyed, idealistic | 反义词：realistic, pragmatic, cynical, down-to-earth | 词性:形容词
