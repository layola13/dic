makabayan: /ˌmɑːkɑːˈbaɪən/ | adj. | 爱国的，民族主义的 |  A Filipino word derived from the root word "bayan" (country or nation), meaning patriotic or nationalistic. It describes someone who loves their country and is dedicated to its welfare. |  Many Filipinos consider Jose Rizal a makabayan hero. 许多菲律宾人认为何塞·黎刹是一位爱国英雄。|  She showed her makabayan spirit by joining the rallies for national sovereignty. 她通过参加争取国家主权的集会来展现她的爱国精神。 | Synonyms: patriotic, nationalistic | Antonyms: unpatriotic, traitorous | adjective
