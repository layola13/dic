hollies:/ˈhɒliz/ (UK), /ˈhɑːliz/ (US)| n.|冬青树，冬青属植物|Evergreen shrubs or trees of the genus Ilex, typically having glossy, spiny-edged leaves, small white flowers, and red berries.|The hollies in the garden were covered with bright red berries. 花园里的冬青树上覆盖着鲜红色的浆果。
