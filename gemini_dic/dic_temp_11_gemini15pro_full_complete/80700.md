gorgonopsian: /ˌɡɔːrɡənˈɒpsiən/ /ˌɡɔːrɡəˈnɑːpsiən/ | n. | 戈尔贡兽, 兽孔目动物的一种 | An extinct group of therapsid reptiles, characterized by large, powerful jaws and saber-like canine teeth. They were apex predators during the Permian period. |  Example: The gorgonopsian was a fearsome predator of its time.  戈尔贡兽是其时代可怕的掠食者。

Example: Fossils of gorgonopsians have been found in Russia and Africa.  在俄罗斯和非洲发现了戈尔贡兽的化石。 | 近义词: therapsid (泛指兽孔目), predator (捕食者) | 反义词: prey (猎物) | 词性: noun


Etymology:  From Gorgon (Greek mythology, a monstrous feminine creature) + -ops (Greek, face) + -ian (suffix forming adjectives).  来自希腊神话中的蛇发女怪戈耳工 + 希腊语中的“脸” + 形容词后缀。
