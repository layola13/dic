amigos:/ɑːˈmiːɡoʊs/ (US) /əˈmiːɡɒs/ (UK)| n. |朋友（复数）|Spanish word for "friends," used informally in English, especially in the American Southwest.|例句：I'm going out with my amigos tonight.  今晚我要和我的朋友们出去玩。

例句：He greeted his amigos with a warm hug. 他热情地拥抱了他的朋友们。|近义词：friends, pals, buddies|反义词:enemies, foes|Noun
