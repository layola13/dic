astrobiological:/ˌæstroʊbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ | adj. | 天体生物学的 | Relating to astrobiology, the study of life in the universe, including its origin, evolution, distribution, and future. |  Astrobiological research seeks to answer fundamental questions about life beyond Earth. 天体生物学研究试图回答关于地球以外生命的基本问题。 |  The discovery of extremophiles on Earth has fueled astrobiological speculation about life on other planets. 在地球上发现极端微生物激发了人们对其他行星上生命的天体生物学推测。 | 近义词:exobiological | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "terrestrial biology") | 词性: adjective
