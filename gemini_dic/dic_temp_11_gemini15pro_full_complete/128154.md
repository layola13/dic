nettle:/ˈnetl/ (US), /ˈnetəl/ (UK)| n. v.|荨麻；激怒|A herbaceous plant which has jagged leaves covered with stinging hairs.  As a verb, it means to irritate or annoy someone.|例句：The nettles stung my legs as I walked through the long grass. 我走过长草时，荨麻刺痛了我的腿。|近义词：irritate, annoy, vex, provoke|反义词:soothe, please, calm|词性:noun, verb
