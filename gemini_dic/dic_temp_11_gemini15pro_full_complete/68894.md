fastidious:/fæˈstɪdiəs/ /fəˈstɪdiəs/| adj.|一丝不苟的，挑剔的，苛求的|Very attentive to and concerned about accuracy and detail.|例句：He is fastidious about his appearance. 他对自己的外表很在意。|例句：She has a fastidious approach to her work. 她对工作一丝不苟。|近义词：meticulous, scrupulous, picky, choosy, demanding|反义词：careless, sloppy, uncritical, undemanding|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *fastidiosus*, from *fastidium* ("loathing, disgust"), from *fastus* ("arrogance, pride") + *-idium* (a suffix forming abstract nouns).
