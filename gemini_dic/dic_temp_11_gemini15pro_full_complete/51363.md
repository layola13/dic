deterring:/dɪˈtɜːrɪŋ/
| adj. |威慑的，制止的|Discouraging someone from doing something, typically by instilling doubt or fear of the consequences.|The high security fence was deterring to potential intruders.|近义词：preventing, discouraging, inhibiting, hindering|反义词：encouraging, promoting, inciting|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From the verb "deter," which comes from the Late Latin "deterrere," from "de-" (from, away from) + "terrere" (to frighten).
