adulterant:/əˈdʌltərənt/| n. |掺杂物，伪劣品|A substance added to another substance, often fraudulently, to increase its bulk or weight, or to reduce its quality.  A substance that makes something impure or weaker.|The milk was found to contain a dangerous adulterant.|近义词:contaminant, impurity, additive|反义词:purifier|noun
