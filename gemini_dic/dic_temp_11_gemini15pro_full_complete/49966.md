deniers:/dɪˈnaɪərz/
n.
否认者，拒绝者
people who refuse to admit the truth of something
Climate change deniers ignore the overwhelming scientific evidence. 气候变化否认者忽视了压倒性的科学证据。
skeptics, doubters
believers, accepters
noun
