erasing:/ɪˈreɪsɪŋ/
verb
擦除，消除
the act of removing marks, writing, recorded material, or data.
He was erasing the blackboard. 他正在擦黑板。
近义词:deleting, removing, obliterating, wiping
反义词:writing, drawing, recording, restoring
词性:Verb (present participle of erase)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *esracer, from Vulgar Latin *ērasāre, from Latin ēradere (to scrape out, erase).  Ultimately from ex- (out) + rādere (to scrape).
