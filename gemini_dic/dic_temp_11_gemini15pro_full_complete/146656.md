overhastened: /ˌoʊvərˈheɪsənd/ | adj. | 过于仓促的，过分匆忙的 | Done too quickly and therefore not done well. |  The overhastened decision led to disastrous consequences. 过于仓促的决定导致了灾难性的后果。 | Example: The overhastened withdrawal of troops left the city vulnerable. 仓促撤兵使得城市很脆弱。| Synonyms: rushed, hurried, precipitate, premature | Antonyms: deliberate, considered, careful, measured | adjective
