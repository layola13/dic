cringingness:/ˈkrɪndʒɪŋnəs/
n.
畏缩，尴尬，难为情
The quality of being so embarrassing, awkward, or sentimental as to cause a feeling of acute discomfort or embarrassment.
His cringingness was palpable as he recounted the embarrassing story. 他讲述那个尴尬的故事时，他的难为情显而易见。
近义词：awkwardness, embarrassment, discomfort
反义词：confidence, poise, composure
词性：名词

cringe:/krɪndʒ/
v.
畏缩，感到尴尬
To shrink back in fear or embarrassment.
I cringed at the thought of my dad's embarrassing jokes.  一想到我爸爸那些令人尴尬的笑话，我就感到畏缩。
近义词： recoil, flinch, wince
反义词：embrace, relish, enjoy
词性：动词
