brining:/ˈbraɪnɪŋ/| n.| 盐水浸泡，腌制|The process of soaking food in a saltwater solution to preserve or flavor it.|The chef is brining the turkey for Thanksgiving.  厨师正在用盐水浸泡感恩节火鸡。|近义词：marinating, pickling, curing|反义词:|noun

brine:/braɪn/| n. vt.|盐水；用盐水处理|A strong solution of salt in water, used for preserving or pickling food; to treat with brine.|I brined the chicken overnight. 我把鸡在盐水里泡了一夜。|近义词：saltwater, saline solution|反义词:freshwater|noun, verb
