dicier:/ˈdaɪsiər/| adj.|更加冒险的，更加危险的，更加不确定的|More risky, uncertain, or unpredictable than something else.|例句：Investing in the stock market is dicier than putting your money in a savings account. 投资股票市场比把钱存入储蓄账户风险更大。

例句：The weather forecast makes the trip dicier than we anticipated. 天气预报使得这次旅行比我们预期的更加不确定。|近义词：riskier, more dangerous, more uncertain, more unpredictable|反义词：safer, more secure, more certain, more predictable|词性:形容词
词源:From dice + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Old French dé. The original meaning related to the unpredictable nature of dice throws.
