camouflagers:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒərz/
| n. |伪装者；进行伪装的人或物|People or things that camouflage something or someone. |The camouflagers expertly disguised the tanks. 伪装者熟练地伪装了坦克。|近义词：disguisers, concealers|反义词：exposers, revealers|词性:名词

camouflagers:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒərz/
| v. |伪装，掩饰|The act of camouflaging something or someone.|The soldiers camouflaged themselves with leaves and branches. 士兵们用树叶和树枝伪装自己。|近义词：disguise, conceal|反义词：expose, reveal|词性:动词
