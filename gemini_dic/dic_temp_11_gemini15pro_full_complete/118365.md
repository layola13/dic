meritocracy: /ˌmɛrɪˈtɒkrəsi/ (UK) /ˌmɛrɪˈtɑːkrəsi/ (US) | n. |精英管理；贤能统治；英才教育| A system in which people advance based on their abilities and achievements rather than on their social status or connections. |  The company claims to be a meritocracy, where promotions are based solely on performance. 这家公司声称是一个精英管理体制，晋升完全基于绩效。| synonyms:  talent system,  system of merit  | antonyms: aristocracy,  oligarchy,  plutocracy | noun
