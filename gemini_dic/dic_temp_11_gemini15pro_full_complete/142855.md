out-grossing:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊsɪŋ/
verb
超过...的票房收入
earning more revenue than (another film, play, etc.)
The new Marvel movie is out-grossing all other films this year.  这部新的漫威电影今年的票房收入超过了所有其他电影。
The sequel ended up out-grossing the original film.  续集最终超过了原版电影的票房收入。
surpassing, exceeding, outperforming, topping
underperforming, lagging behind
v.
