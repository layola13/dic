colleterial: /ˌkɒləˈtɪəriəl/ | adj. | 胶状的；分泌粘液的；由colleter分泌的 | Relating to or denoting colleters, glandular hairs on the buds or young leaves of many plants that secrete a sticky substance. | The colleterial secretions protect the developing bud. 这些粘液分泌物保护着发育中的芽。 | 近义词: glandular, mucilaginous | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps non-secretory) | 词性: adjective

colleterial: /ˌkɒləˈtɪəriəl/ | n. | (昆虫)胶腺 | A colleter, a gland in insects that secretes a sticky substance used in making egg cases or nests. | The colleterial glands produce a cement-like substance. 胶腺产生一种类似水泥的物质。 | 近义词: gland | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
