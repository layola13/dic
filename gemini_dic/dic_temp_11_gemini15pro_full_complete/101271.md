involucre:/ˈɪnvəlʊkər/| n. |总苞；苞片；包膜|A whorl or rosette of bracts surrounding an inflorescence, especially a capitulum, or at the base of an umbel.|The sunflower's involucre is made up of numerous small, green bracts.|Synonym: bract, sheath, wrapper|Antonym: flower, blossom|noun
