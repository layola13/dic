hoverer:/ˈhʌvərər/ (US), /ˈhɒvərər/ (UK)| n.|盘旋者，徘徊者|Someone or something that hovers.  This can refer to birds, insects, helicopters, or even a person who lingers in a place.|The kestrel is a skilled hoverer, able to stay perfectly still in the air.  红隼是一种技术娴熟的盘旋者，能够在空中保持完全静止。|近义词：lingerer, dawdler|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider words implying rapid movement, e.g., sprinter, racer) |词性:noun
