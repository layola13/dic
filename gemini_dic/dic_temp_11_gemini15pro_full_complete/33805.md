circumflant: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈflænt/ | adj. | 包围的，环绕流动的 | flowing around; encompassing |  The circumflant river encircled the ancient city. 包围的河流环绕着这座古城。| The circumflant air currents cooled the island.  环绕的气流使岛屿凉爽。| synonyms: surrounding, encompassing, encircling | antonyms: central, interior, internal | adjective

Etymology: From Latin circumflans, circumflant-, present participle of circumfluere "to flow around," from circum- "around" + fluere "to flow."
