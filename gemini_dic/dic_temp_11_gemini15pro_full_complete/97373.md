inframarginal:/ˌɪnfrəˈmɑːrdʒɪnl/ | adj. | 边际内的，边际下的 | Situated within the margin; not marginal.  Relating to a unit of a factor of production or a consumer whose economic behavior is unaffected by a change in a given parameter, such as price. |  Inframarginal firms are those that would continue to produce even if the price were lower. 边际内厂商是指即使价格下降也会继续生产的厂商。|  The inframarginal consumers are those who would buy the product even if the price were higher. 边际内消费者是指即使价格上涨也会购买该产品的消费者。 | near-marginal, submarginal | marginal, extramarginal | adjective
