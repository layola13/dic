engraving's:/ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋz/
n.
雕刻（作品），版画
The process, practice, or art of engraving.  A design or figure engraved. Something that is engraved, such as a print or inscription.
This museum has a large collection of Dürer's engravings.  这个博物馆收藏了大量的丢勒版画。
synonyms:  print, etching, inscription, carving
antonyms:  none (lack of a direct antonym for a process and its result)
noun
