ejaculating: /ɪˈdʒækjʊleɪtɪŋ/
verb
射精; (突然)说出
The act of ejecting semen from the body; also used figuratively to describe sudden or forceful utterance of words.
He ejaculated a stream of abuse. 他破口大骂了一通。
近义词:  expelling, emitting, blurting, exclaiming
反义词:  restraining, withholding
词性: verb (present participle)

To eject semen from the body; also used figuratively to describe sudden or forceful utterance of words.
"No!" he ejaculated.  “不！”他脱口而出。

ejaculation: /ɪˌdʒækjʊˈleɪʃən/
noun
射精; (突然)说出的话
The act of ejaculating semen; also, a sudden, short exclamation.
His ejaculation of surprise startled everyone. 他惊讶的喊叫吓了大家一跳。
近义词: exclamation, outburst, emission
反义词: suppression, silence
词性: noun

词源: From Latin *eiaculatus, past participle of eiaculari "to throw or dart out," from ex- "out" + iaculare "to throw, hurl," from iaculum "javelin," diminutive of iacus "dart."
