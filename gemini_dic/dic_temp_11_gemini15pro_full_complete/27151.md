call-it-a-day:/ˌkɔːl ɪt ə ˈdeɪ/| idiom | 结束一天的工作；到此为止；收工 | To stop working on something for the rest of the day. | After working for ten hours, we decided to call it a day.  工作了十个小时后，我们决定收工。| Synonyms: knock off, pack it in, wrap it up | Antonyms: start, begin | phrase

call-it-a-day:/ˌkɔːl ɪt ə ˈdeɪ/| idiom | 停止；结束；作罢 | To stop doing something, especially because you are tired of it or you do not want to do any more. | The meeting went on for hours, so we finally called it a day. 会议开了好几个小时，所以我们最终决定结束。| Synonyms: give up, quit, stop | Antonyms: continue, persist | phrase
