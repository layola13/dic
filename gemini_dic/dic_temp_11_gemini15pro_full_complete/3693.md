aggregated:/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪtɪd/
verb|(aggregate的过去式和过去分词) |to combine into a single mass or sum; to amount to a whole|The costs aggregated to over a million dollars. 成本总计超过一百万美元。|combined, totaled, amassed|separated, divided|verb
