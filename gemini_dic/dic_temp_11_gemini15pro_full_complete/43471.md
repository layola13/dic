crassamentum:/kræsəˈmɛntəm/
noun
血餅，凝血塊
A thick, coagulated mass, especially of blood; the clot formed in coagulated blood.
The crassamentum was clearly visible in the test tube after the blood sample had been centrifuged.  血液样本离心后，试管中清晰可见血饼。
Synonyms:  clot, coagulum
Antonyms:  serum, plasma
