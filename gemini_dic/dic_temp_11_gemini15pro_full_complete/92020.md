hyperoxygenized:/ˌhaɪpərˈɒksɪdʒənaɪzd/
adjective
过度氧化的，富氧的
Containing or having been exposed to an excessive amount of oxygen.
The hyperoxygenized blood is then pumped back into the patient's body.  过度氧合的血液随后被泵回患者体内。

The diver breathed hyperoxygenized air before the deep dive. 潜水员在深潜前呼吸了富氧空气。

近义词:oxygen-rich, superoxygenated
反义词:hypoxic, deoxygenated
词性:adjective
