altaic:/ælˈteɪɪk/
| adj. n.| 阿尔泰语系的；阿尔泰语系|Relating to or denoting the Altaic language family (a disputed grouping of the Turkic, Mongolic, and Tungusic families, sometimes also including Korean and Japanese).  Also, a member of the (purported) Altaic language family.|The genetic relationship of the Altaic languages has been questioned. 阿尔泰语系的亲属关系一直受到质疑。|The Altaic languages share some structural similarities. 阿尔泰语系有一些结构上的相似之处。|近义词: Turkic, Mongolic, Tungusic (if considering the narrower definition)|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps Indo-European if contrasting language families)
词性: adjective, noun
