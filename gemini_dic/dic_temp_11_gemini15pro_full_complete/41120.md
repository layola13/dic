coprolitic:/ˌkɒprəˈlɪtɪk/| adj.|粪化的，粪石的|Relating to or consisting of coprolites (fossilized feces).|The archaeologist discovered a coprolitic layer in the excavation site. 考古学家在挖掘现场发现了一层粪化层。|The museum displayed several coprolitic samples from the Jurassic period. 博物馆展出了几块侏罗纪时期的粪石样本。|Synonyms: fecal, excremental|Antonyms: None|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kópros* (dung) + *-lite* (stone).
