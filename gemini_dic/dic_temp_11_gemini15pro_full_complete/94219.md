imperial:/ɪmˈpɪəriəl/ | adj. | 帝国的，皇帝的，至高无上的，威严的 | relating to an empire or an emperor; majestic or impressive | The British imperial system included many colonies.  大英帝国体系包括许多殖民地。| The emperor wore his imperial robes. 皇帝穿着他的皇袍。 | 近义词：royal, majestic, sovereign, supreme | 反义词：provincial, local, humble | 词性:形容词

imperial:/ɪmˈpɪəriəl/ | n. |  帝国标准尺寸（尤指英制品脱），皇家尺寸 | a type of size or measurement, especially for British pints |  He ordered an imperial pint of beer. 他点了一英制品脱的啤酒。 | 近义词：pint (in certain contexts) | 反义词：metric (as a system) | 词性:名词
