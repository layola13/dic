overfurnishes:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrnɪʃɪz/
verb
过度装修；摆放过多的家具
to furnish (a room or house) with too many articles of furniture
The room was dark and overfurnished.  这个房间昏暗，家具太多。
overdecorate, overcrowd
underfurnish
v.
