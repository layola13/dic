dumbed-down:/ˌdʌmd ˈdaʊn/ | adj. | 简化的，通俗化的 | Simplified so as to be easily understood, often at the cost of accuracy or depth. | The textbook offered a dumbed-down version of complex scientific principles. 这本教科书提供了复杂科学原理的简化版本。 | The movie was a dumbed-down adaptation of the classic novel.  这部电影是对经典小说的简化改编。 | simplified, oversimplified, watered-down | complex, sophisticated, nuanced | adjective
