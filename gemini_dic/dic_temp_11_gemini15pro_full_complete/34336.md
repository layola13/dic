ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *claspen, *clapsen, from Old Norse *klapsa (“to clap, strike, beat, slap”), from Proto-Germanic *klapjaną (“to clap”).  Cognate with Icelandic klasa (“to clasp, grasp, gather”), Swedish klämma (“to pinch, squeeze”).  Related to clap.
