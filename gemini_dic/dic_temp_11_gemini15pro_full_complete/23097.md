bountifulness's:/ˈbaʊntɪfʊlnəsɪz/| n.|慷慨大方；丰富；丰饶|The bountifulness's of the harvest ensured no one went hungry. 丰收的慷慨确保没有人挨饿。|近义词：abundance, generosity, plentifulness|反义词：scarcity, meagerness, stinginess|词性:名词

Etymology:
From bountiful + -ness + -'s (possessive).  "Bountiful" comes from Middle English *bountifull, from Old French bounte + -ful.  "Bounte" meaning goodness, virtue, from Latin bonitas, from bonus "good."
