ostensive:/ɒˈstensɪv/ /ɑːˈstensɪv/| adj.|明显的；外表的；公开显示的|Characterized by outward display; readily apparent or perceived.|He made an ostentatious display of his wealth. 他炫耀性地展示了他的财富。|
ostensive:/ɒˈstensɪv/ /ɑːˈstensɪv/| adj.|以指示方式进行的|Relating to or involving definition by pointing to or showing examples.|The teacher used an ostensive definition, pointing to a chair to explain the word. 老师用指示性定义，指着椅子来解释这个词。|
ostensive:/ɒˈstensɪv/ /ɑːˈstensɪv/| adj.|做作的；炫耀的|Designed to impress or attract notice.|Her ostentatious jewelry attracted unwanted attention. 她炫耀的珠宝引起了不必要的注意。|
