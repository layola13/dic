eriometer:/ˌɪəriˈɒmɪtər/ | n. | 衍射环测量计；毛径计 | An instrument for measuring the diameters of minute particles, especially wool fibers, by observing the size of the colored rings produced by the diffraction of light passing through a sample. |  The scientist used an eriometer to determine the fineness of the wool. 科学家用毛径计测量羊毛的细度。| synonyms:  fiber diameter analyzer, wool fiber measuring instrument | antonyms: N/A | noun
