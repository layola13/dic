enkerchiefed:/ɪnˈkɜːrtʃɪft/ | adj. | 戴着头巾的，裹着头巾的 | Covered or adorned with a kerchief (a square of fabric worn as a head covering or scarf).  |  The enkerchiefed woman sat quietly on the park bench.  戴着头巾的妇女静静地坐在公园的长椅上。 |  veiled, headscarved | uncovered, bareheaded | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (meaning "in" or "into") + kerchief + -ed (forming an adjective).  Kerchief itself comes from Old French couvrechief, meaning "cover-head."
