filibusterisms:/ˈfɪlɪˌbʌstərɪzəmz/
n.
冗长演说，阻挠议事
act of obstructing progress on a legislative bill by unusual and dilatory tactics, especially using prolonged speechmaking
The senator's filibusterisms tied up the Senate for days, preventing a vote on the bill.  这位参议员的冗长演说让参议院停摆了数日，阻止了对该法案的投票。
prolonged speechmaking, delaying tactics, obstructionist maneuvers
cooperation, expediting, efficiency
noun
