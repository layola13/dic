maydays:/ˈmeɪdeɪz/| n.|求救信号；紧急呼救|A radiotelephone call for help, used especially by ships and aircraft in distress.|The pilot issued a mayday call as the engine failed. 飞行员在引擎失效时发出了求救信号。|Near synonyms: SOS, distress call, emergency call|Antonyms: all clear|noun
