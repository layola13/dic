landlouping:/ˈlændˌluːpɪŋ/ | adj. | 陆生的，不习惯航海的 | Living or working on land, especially after having been at sea; unused to the sea. |  He felt landlouping and awkward back on dry land after months at sea.  他几个月在海上待过后，回到陆地上感觉笨手笨脚，很不适应。 | 近义词：landsman-like, landlubberly | 反义词: seafaring, nautical | 词性: 形容词

landlouping:/ˈlændˌluːpɪŋ/ | n. | 陆地生活，陆上工作 | The activity of living or working on land. | After years of landlouping, he decided to return to the sea. 在多年的陆地生活后，他决定重返大海。| 近义词：landsman | 反义词: seafaring | 词性: 名词
