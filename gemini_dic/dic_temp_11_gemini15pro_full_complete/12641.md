assayed:/əˈseɪd/
verb
化验，分析; 尝试; 鉴定
examined or analyzed; attempted; evaluated
The chemist assayed the purity of the gold. 化学家化验了黄金的纯度。
The climbers assayed the difficult route. 登山者尝试了这条艰难的路线。
synonyms: analyzed, tested, evaluated, attempted, tried
antonyms: ignored, neglected
词性: 动词
