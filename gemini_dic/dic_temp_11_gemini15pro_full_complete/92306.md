hypnology:/hɪpˈnɒlədʒi/ /hɪpˈnɑːlədʒi/
n.|催眠术；催眠学|The study of sleep and hypnosis, especially in regard to its therapeutic applications.|Hypnology is a fascinating field that explores the power of the subconscious mind.|近义词:hypnotism, mesmerism|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
Etymology:  From Greek *hypnos* "sleep" + *-logia* "-logy," "study of."
