displacency:/kəmˈpleɪsənsi/ /kəmˈpleɪsənsi/ | n. |自满；沾沾自喜；|A feeling of smug satisfaction with one's achievements, possessions, or situation. |例句：His complacency about his job led to his being fired. 他对工作的自满导致他被解雇。

例句：The team's complacency after their early victories cost them the championship.  团队在早期胜利后的自满让他们失去了冠军。|近义词：smugness, self-satisfaction, satisfaction |反义词:anxiety, insecurity, humility| 词性:名词
