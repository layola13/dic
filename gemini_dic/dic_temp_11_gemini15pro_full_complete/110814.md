linnaeans:/lɪˈniːənz/
n.
林奈学说的追随者;  信奉林奈分类法的人; (尤指)动植物分类学家
Followers of Linnaeus; believers in the Linnaean system of classification (especially of plants and animals).
Linnaeus developed a system for classifying and naming plants and animals.  Many linnaeans still use his system today, although it has been modified over time. 林奈发展了一套动植物分类和命名的系统。许多林奈学说的追随者今天仍在使用他的系统，尽管它已经随着时间的推移而被修改。
taxonomists, biologists, naturalists, botanists, zoologists
anti-evolutionists (This is a somewhat complex contrast, as some early anti-evolutionists might have still used Linnaean classification. It's more about a difference in underlying philosophy.)
