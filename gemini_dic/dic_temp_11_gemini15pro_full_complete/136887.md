nonsociablenesses:/nɒnˈsəʊʃəbəlnəsɪz/
n.
不善交际；孤僻；不合群
The state or quality of being nonsociable; unsociability.  A lack of interest in social interaction.
Her nonsociablenesses made it difficult for her to make friends. 她不善交际，很难交到朋友。
Near Synonyms: unsociabilities, introversion, aloofness, reclusiveness
Antonyms: sociabilities, gregariousness
词性: noun (plural)
