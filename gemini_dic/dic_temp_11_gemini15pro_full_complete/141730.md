oralities:/ɔːˈrælɪtiz/ | n. | 口头表达；口述传统；口语文化 |  The study of oralities encompasses the various ways in which cultures transmit knowledge, stories, and traditions through spoken word rather than writing. |  The professor's research focuses on the oralities of ancient Celtic cultures. 教授的研究重点是古代凯尔特文化的口语传统。| synonyms: spoken traditions, verbal communication, oral lore | antonyms: written records, literacy | noun
