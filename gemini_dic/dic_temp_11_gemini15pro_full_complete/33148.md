chromatic:/krəˈmætɪk/| adj.|半音的，彩色的，着色的|Relating to colors, especially those of the rainbow; also, in music, relating to or using notes not belonging to the diatonic scale of the key in which a passage is written.|例句：The chromatic aberration in the lens created a rainbow effect. 镜头中的色差产生彩虹效应。|近义词：colorful, iridescent, tonal|反义词：achromatic, colorless|词性:adjective

chromatic:/krəˈmætɪk/| n.|半音阶|A chromatic scale, passage, or modulation.|例句：The guitarist played a fast chromatic run. 吉他手演奏了一段快速的半音阶跑句。|近义词：scale, run|反义词：diatonic|词性:noun
