constructs:/ˈkɒnstrʌkts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnstrʌkts/ (US)| n. |概念; 构想; 构念; (尤指理论或观念的)建构, 构建|Something that is constructed, especially an idea or theory. |The theory of social constructs argues that our understanding of the world is shaped by shared assumptions. 社会建构理论认为，我们对世界的理解是由共同的假设塑造的。|synonyms: concepts, ideas, frameworks, notions |antonyms: realities, facts| noun
