disturnpike:/dɪsˈtɜːrnpaɪk/ | n. | 地方收费公路；支线收费公路 | A turnpike that is smaller or less important than a main one.  It often refers to a road that was formerly a toll road but is no longer one. |  The old disturnpike is now a scenic byway.  这条旧的支线收费公路现在是一条风景优美的旁路。| Synonyms: secondary road, byroad, side road | Antonyms: highway, expressway, main road | noun

disturnpike:/dɪsˈtɜːrnpaɪk/ | adj. | 地方收费公路的；支线收费公路的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a disturnpike. | The disturnpike route was shorter but less well-maintained. 这条地方收费公路的路线较短，但维护得不太好。 | Synonyms: back road, country road | Antonyms:  interstate, freeway | adjective
