buzzing:/ˈbʌzɪŋ/ | adj.  n. | 嗡嗡响的，嘈杂的；兴奋的，热闹的 | making a low, continuous humming sound;  full of excitement and activity | The room was buzzing with conversation. 房间里嗡嗡作响，充满了谈话声. | synonyms: humming, vibrating, droning, lively, vibrant | antonyms: quiet, silent, still, dull | adjective

buzzing:/ˈbʌzɪŋ/ | n. | 嗡嗡声；兴奋，热闹 | a low, continuous humming sound; a feeling of excitement and activity |  There was a buzzing sound coming from the fridge. 冰箱里传来嗡嗡的声音. | synonyms: hum, drone, vibration, excitement, activity | antonyms: quiet, silence, stillness, dullness | noun

Etymology: From the verb "buzz," which imitates the sound of bees and other insects.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *buzzaną.
