kalium:/pəˈtæsiəm/| n.|钾|A soft, silver-white metallic element of the alkali metal group, occurring in nature only in compounds. Potassium is essential for plant growth and is also an important constituent of the human diet.|Potassium is an essential mineral for human health. 钾是人体健康必需的矿物质。|Potassium helps regulate fluid balance, muscle contractions and nerve signals. 钾有助于调节体液平衡、肌肉收缩和神经信号。|Synonyms: K (symbol), potash|Antonyms: None|noun
