musingly:/ˈmjuːzɪŋli/ | adv. |若有所思地，沉思地 | In a thoughtful and reflective manner, often with a hint of wistfulness or wonder. | She smiled musingly, remembering their last conversation. 她若有所思地笑着，回想起他们上次的谈话。|Synonyms: thoughtfully, pensively, contemplatively | Antonyms: unthinkingly, rashly | adverb

Etymology->
From muse (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Muse" comes from Old French "muser" (to ponder, dream), ultimately from Late Latin "musare" (to murmur, loiter, gape idly).
