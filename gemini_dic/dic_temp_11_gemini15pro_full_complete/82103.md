grock:/ɡrɒk/| v.|理解；领悟；深刻理解|To understand something completely and intuitively.|例句：I finally grocked the concept of quantum physics after reading several books.  我读了几本书后终于理解了量子物理的概念。

例句：It took me a while to grock the new software, but now I'm proficient.  我花了一段时间才掌握了这个新软件，但现在我已经很熟练了。|近义词：understand, comprehend, grasp, get, perceive, intuit|反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|词性：动词

词源：Coined by Robert A. Heinlein in his 1961 science fiction novel *Stranger in a Strange Land*, from the Martian word meaning "to drink" (as water), with the extended meaning of "to absorb" or "to take in."
