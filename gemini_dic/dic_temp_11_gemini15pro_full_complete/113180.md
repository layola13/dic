lure:/lʊr/ /ljʊr/| v. n.|诱惑；引诱；诱饵|To tempt or attract with the promise of something pleasing.|The fisherman used worms as lure to catch fish. 渔夫用蠕虫作诱饵来钓鱼。|近义词:tempt, entice, attract, seduce|反义词:repel, deter, discourage|词性:动词, 名词

lured:/lʊrd/ /ljʊrd/| v. (lure的过去式和过去分词)|诱惑；引诱|Past tense and past participle of "lure".|The thief lured her into the alley with promises of money. 小偷用钱的承诺把她引诱到小巷里。|近义词:tempted, enticed, attracted, seduced|反义词:repelled, deterred, discouraged|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *luerre "bait, decoy," probably from a Germanic source related to Old High German luoder "bait" and Old English luder "bait, enticement".
