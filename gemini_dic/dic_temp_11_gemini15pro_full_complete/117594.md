megalopsychy:/ˌmɛɡəloʊˈsaɪki/ | n. | 妄自尊大；夸大妄想 | A mental disorder characterized by delusions of grandeur. |  He suffered from megalopsychy, believing himself to be a king. 他患有夸大妄想症，认为自己是个国王。| Example: His megalopsychy led him to believe he could single-handedly solve the world's problems. 他的夸大妄想让他相信自己可以单枪匹马地解决世界上的所有问题。| Synonyms: grandiosity, megalomania | Antonyms: humility, modesty | Noun
