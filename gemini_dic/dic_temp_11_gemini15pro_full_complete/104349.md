juxtaposes:/ˌdʒʌkstəˈpoʊzɪz/
verb
并列， juxtaposition的第三人称单数形式
places two or more things together, especially in order to compare or contrast them
The exhibition juxtaposes Picasso's early works with some of his later pieces.  展览将毕加索的早期作品与他的一些后期作品并列展出。
compares, contrasts, places side by side
separates, divides
v.
