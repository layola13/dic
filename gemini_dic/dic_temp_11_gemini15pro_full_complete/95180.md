inaudibly:/ɪnˈɔːdəbli/ | adv. |听不见地|Not audible; unable to be heard.|She whispered inaudibly, and I had to lean closer to hear. 她低声细语，我不得不凑近才能听见。|例句：The actor muttered something inaudibly under his breath. 演员低声咕哝着什么，听不清楚。|近义词：softly, quietly, faintly, imperceptibly|反义词：audibly, loudly, clearly|词性：副词
