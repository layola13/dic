evasive:/ɪˈveɪsɪv/ | adj. | 逃避的，回避的，托辞的 | tending to avoid commitment or self-revelation, especially by responding only indirectly |  He gave an evasive answer when asked about his plans.  他被问到计划时，他的回答闪烁其词。 | synonyms: elusive, ambiguous, equivocal | antonyms: direct, forthright, honest | adjective

evasive:/ɪˈveɪsɪv/ | adj. | 难以捉摸的，难以找到的 | difficult to find or catch | The fox was evasive, darting through the bushes. 狐狸很难捉摸，在灌木丛中窜来窜去。 | synonyms: elusive, slippery | antonyms: accessible, approachable | adjective
