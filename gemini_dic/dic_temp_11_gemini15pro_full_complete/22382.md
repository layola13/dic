booer:/ˈbuːər/ | n. |起哄者，喝倒彩的人 |A person who boos, expressing disapproval or contempt. |The booers were relentless, drowning out the speaker's words.  起哄者毫不留情，把演讲者的话淹没了。| Example: The comedian ignored the booers and continued with his act.  那位喜剧演员无视喝倒彩的人，继续他的表演。|Synonyms: heckler, jeerer | Antonyms: supporter, fan | Noun
