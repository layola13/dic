aquarium: /əˈkwɛəriəm/ | n. | 水族馆；鱼缸；养鱼池 | A building or tank where fish and other aquatic creatures are kept for viewing by the public or for private enjoyment. |  例句：We visited the aquarium and saw sharks and dolphins. 我们参观了水族馆，看到了鲨鱼和海豚。

例句：He keeps tropical fish in an aquarium in his living room. 他在客厅的鱼缸里养热带鱼。| 近义词：fish tank, vivarium | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *aquārium, from aqua (“water”) + -ārium (suffix forming nouns denoting places or receptacles).
