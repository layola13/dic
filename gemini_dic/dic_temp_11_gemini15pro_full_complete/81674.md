greenback:/ˈɡriːnˌbæk/| n.| 美元，美钞 |A dollar bill; US paper currency, so called from the green ink used on the back.| Example:  He paid for the car in greenbacks. 他用美钞支付了车款。
Example:  She had a wad of greenbacks in her purse. 她的钱包里有一叠美钞。|Synonym: dollar, buck,  |Antonym: N/A|noun
