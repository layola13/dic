biofilm:/ˈbaɪoʊfɪlm/| n. |生物膜|A thin, slimy film of bacteria that adheres to a surface.|Dental plaque is a common example of a biofilm. 牙菌斑是生物膜的一个常见例子。|近义词：microbial community, slime layer|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "clean surface")|noun
