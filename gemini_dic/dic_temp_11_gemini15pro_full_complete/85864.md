head-restraint:/ˈhed rɪˈstreɪnt/
n.|头枕，头部约束装置|A safety device in a vehicle designed to limit the backward movement of the occupant's head in a collision, reducing the risk of whiplash.|The head-restraint in my new car is adjustable.|Synonyms: headrest, head support|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps lack of head-restraint)|Noun
