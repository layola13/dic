outtalking: /ˌaʊtˈtɔːkɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的讲话；比…说得更多
speaking longer or more forcefully than someone else
He outtalked his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中胜过了对手的讲话。
近义词: outspeak, overpower
反义词: be quiet, listen
