hybridizing:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzɪŋ/
v.
杂交，混合
The process of crossbreeding different varieties of plants or animals to produce hybrids.
Scientists are hybridizing plants to create new varieties. 科学家们正在将植物杂交以创造新品种。
crossbreeding, interbreeding, mixing
purebreeding, inbreeding
verb
