malthusian:/mælˈθjuːziən/| adj. n.| 马尔萨斯的；马尔萨斯学说的；马尔萨斯主义者 |Relating to the theories of Thomas Robert Malthus, especially his theory that population growth will tend to outrun the growth of food supply.|The current food crisis has revived Malthusian fears about the planet's capacity to feed a growing population. 当前的粮食危机再次引发了人们对地球养活不断增长的人口的马尔萨斯式担忧。|近义词:overpopulated, unsustainable|反义词:sustainable, cornucopian|词性:形容词, 名词


malthusian:/mælˈθjuːziən/| n.| 马尔萨斯主义者 |A person who believes in the theories of Thomas Robert Malthus.|He was a strict Malthusian, advocating for population control. 他是一个严格的马尔萨斯主义者，主张控制人口。|近义词:neo-Malthusian|反义词:cornucopian|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Thomas Robert Malthus (1766–1834), English economist and demographer + -ian.
