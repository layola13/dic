curve:/kɜːrv/ (British) /kɝːv/ (American)| n. v.|曲线，弯曲；使弯曲|A line that deviates from straightness in a smooth, continuous fashion.  To cause to bend or deviate from a straight line.|The car struggled to take the sharp curve. 汽车难以转过急弯。|近义词：bend, arc, arch, turn|反义词:straight, line, flat|Noun, Verb

curve:/kɜːrv/ (British) /kɝːv/ (American)| v.|使弯曲；使成曲线|To cause to bend or deviate from a straight line.|He curved the ball beautifully past the goalkeeper. 他漂亮地把球绕过守门员射入球门。|近义词：bend, twist, flex|反义词:straighten, flatten|Verb
