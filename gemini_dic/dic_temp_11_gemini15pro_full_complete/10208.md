apostle:/əˈpɒs(ə)l/ (US) /əˈpɑːs(ə)l/| n.|使徒，倡导者|One of the twelve disciples chosen by Jesus Christ to preach the gospel.  A pioneer or advocate of a particular belief, cause, or movement.|例句：Peter was one of the twelve apostles. 彼得是十二使徒之一。

例句：He is considered an apostle of modern art. 他被认为是现代艺术的倡导者。|近义词：disciple, follower, advocate, proponent, champion|反义词：opponent, adversary|词性:noun
