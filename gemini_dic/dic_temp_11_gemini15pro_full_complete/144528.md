outvanished: /ˌaʊtˈvænɪʃt/
verb
彻底消失，比消失更彻底
To disappear completely, to a greater degree than simply vanishing.
The magician outvanished his rival, leaving no trace of him whatsoever. 魔术师让他的对手彻底消失了，一点痕迹也没留下。
Synonyms:  eclipse, surpass, outdo, outstrip
Antonyms: appear, materialize, emerge
词性：动词
