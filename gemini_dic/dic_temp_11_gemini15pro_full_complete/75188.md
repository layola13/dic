frizzle:/ˈfrɪzl/ | v. |  (使)卷曲；(使)变脆;油炸 | To form tight curls or crisp.  Often used in the context of cooking, particularly frying. | The hair frizzled in the humidity. 头发在潮湿的环境中卷曲了。 The bacon frizzled in the pan. 培根在锅里滋滋作响。| 近义词: sizzle, curl, crisp | 反义词: straighten, soften | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Middle English *frisle*, frequentative of *fristen*, meaning "to fry".  Ultimately from Old French *frire*.
