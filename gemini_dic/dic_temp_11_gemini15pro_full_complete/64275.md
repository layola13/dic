equisetum:/ˌɛkwɪˈsiːtəm/ | n. | 木贼；问荆 | A genus of rush-like conspicuously jointed perennial herbs, the only extant genus of the order Equisetales.  Equisetum is a vascular plant that reproduces by spores rather than seeds.| Equisetum plants are often found in damp areas. 木贼植物通常生长在潮湿地区。| Equisetum arvense is a common species of horsetail.  问荆是一种常见的木贼种类。 | synonyms: horsetail, scouring rush | antonyms: N/A | noun
