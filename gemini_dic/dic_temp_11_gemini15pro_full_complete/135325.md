nonpedagogic:/ˌnɒnpɛdəˈɡɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 非教育学的，非教学的 | Not relating to teaching or education; not concerned with pedagogy. |  This book takes a nonpedagogic approach to explaining quantum physics, focusing on the core concepts without dumbing them down. 这本书采用非教学的方式解释量子物理学，重点关注核心概念，而不是简化它们。

The artist's nonpedagogic installation encouraged viewers to draw their own conclusions. 这位艺术家的非教育性装置鼓励观众得出自己的结论。

| 近义词: non-educational, unacademic, informal | 反义词: pedagogical, didactic, educational | 词性: adjective
