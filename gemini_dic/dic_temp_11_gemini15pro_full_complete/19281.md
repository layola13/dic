biased:/ˈbaɪəst/ | adj. | 有偏见的；偏心的；偏向的 | unfairly preferring one person or group over others |  The referee was accused of being biased towards the home team. 裁判被指责偏袒主队。| synonyms: prejudiced, partial, one-sided, unfair, subjective | antonyms: impartial, unbiased, objective, neutral, fair | adjective

biased:/ˈbaɪəst/ | adj. | 有偏见的；偏心的；偏向的 | showing an unreasonable liking for or dislike of something or someone |  He admitted he was biased against the proposal. 他承认他对这项提议有偏见。| synonyms: prejudiced, slanted, warped | antonyms: impartial, unbiased, objective, neutral, fair | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *biais "oblique, slant, across," perhaps from a Germanic source.  The sense of "prejudice" developed in the 16th century.
