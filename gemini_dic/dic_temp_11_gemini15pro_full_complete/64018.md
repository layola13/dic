epitaphical:/ˌɛpɪˈtæfɪk(ə)l/
adjective
墓志铭的；适合做墓志铭的；像墓志铭的
Of or relating to an epitaph; suitable for an epitaph; resembling or suggestive of an epitaph.
His speech was delivered in epitaphical tones, as if he were already mourning the loss. 他的演讲以墓志铭般的语调进行，仿佛他已经在哀悼失去的东西了。

The historian offered an epitaphical assessment of the fallen empire. 这位历史学家对衰落的帝国进行了墓志铭式的评价。

synonym: commemorative, memorial, elegiac, funereal
antonym: celebratory, joyous, lively, cheerful
词性: adjective
