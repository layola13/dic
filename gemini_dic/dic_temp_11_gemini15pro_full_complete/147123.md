overloading:/ˌəʊvərˈləʊdɪŋ/ | n. | 超载，过载，重载 | The act of loading something with too much of something; an excessive load.  | The overloading of the circuit caused the fuse to blow. 电路过载导致保险丝熔断。 | synonyms: surfeiting, overburdening, overcharging | antonyms: underloading | noun

overloading:/ˌəʊvərˈləʊdɪŋ/ | n. | (计算机)重载 | In computer science, the ability of one function or operator to perform different actions based on the context of its call, determined by the number, type, and order of its arguments. | Function overloading allows you to use the same function name for different purposes. 函数重载允许你将相同的函数名用于不同的目的。 | synonyms: polymorphism (in some contexts) | antonyms: N/A | noun
