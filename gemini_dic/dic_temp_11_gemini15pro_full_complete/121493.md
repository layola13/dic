misleading:/mɪsˈliːdɪŋ/
adj.
误导性的；使人产生误解的
giving the wrong idea or impression.
This advertisement is misleading, as it doesn't mention the product's side effects.  这个广告具有误导性，因为它没有提及产品的副作用。

The map was misleading, and we ended up getting lost. 这张地图有误导性，我们最终迷路了。

近义词:deceptive, fallacious, confusing
反义词:accurate, truthful, clear
词性:adjective

词源:
From mis- + leading, present participle of lead.
