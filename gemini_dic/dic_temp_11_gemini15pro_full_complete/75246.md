from afar:/frəm əˈfɑːr/
|adv.|从远处，从远方|From afar, the island looked like a paradise. 从远处看，这座岛屿就像一个天堂。|English Explanation:  Indicates a position or origin at a considerable distance.|例句：We saw the ship approaching from afar. 我们看到船从远处驶来。|近义词：from a distance, remotely|反义词：close up, nearby|词性:副词
from afar:/frəm əˈfɑːr/
|adj.(rarely used)|远方的|The from-afar mountains were capped with snow. 远方的山峰被雪覆盖。|English Explanation:  Indicates something located at a considerable distance. This usage as an adjective is less common.|例句：The from-afar sound of drums announced the festival's beginning.  远方传来的鼓声宣告了节日开始。|近义词：distant, remote|反义词：near, close|词性:形容词
