angariation:/ˌæŋɡɛəriˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 强征，强购；征用 | The forced requisitioning of supplies, especially during wartime. 例句：During the war, the angariation of private property was common.  战争期间，私人财产的征用很常见。 | 近义词：requisition, confiscation, expropriation, seizure | 反义词：restitution, return | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *angariātiō, from Latin angāriā (“forced service, courier service”), from Greek ἀγγαρεία (angareía, “compulsory public service, especially as a courier”). Ultimately from Old Persian.
