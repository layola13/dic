gripless:/ˈɡrɪplɪs/
adjective
无抓握力的；抓不住的；不稳的
Lacking a firm grip or unable to grip; insecure.
The climber felt gripless on the smooth rock face. 攀岩者感觉在光滑的岩壁上抓不住。
近义词: insecure, slippery, unstable
反义词: firm, secure, steady
词性: adjective

gripless:/ˈɡrɪplɪs/
adjective
无能为力的；没有控制力的
Powerless; lacking control.
He felt gripless as the car skidded on the ice. 当汽车在冰上打滑时，他感到无能为力。
近义词: powerless, helpless, impotent
反义词: powerful, in control, competent
词性: adjective
