incessantness:/ɪnˈsesəntnəs/ | n. |不停，持续不断 |The quality of being incessant; ceaselessness.  The state of never stopping. |The incessantness of the dripping faucet drove me crazy.  不断滴水的水龙头让我快疯了。|近义词：continuity, persistence, ceaselessness, constancy |反义词：intermittency, impermanence, brevity |词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *incessauntnes, from incessaunt + -nes.  Incessaunt from Old French incessant, from Latin incessant-, incessans, present participle of incessare (“to not cease, to continue without interruption”), from in- (“not”) + cessare (“to cease, to stop”).  -ness from Old English -nes, a suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives.
