jejune:/dʒɪˈdʒuːn/
adjective
幼稚的；枯燥无味的；缺乏营养的
(of ideas or writings) naive, simplistic, and superficial; (of food) lacking flavour; unsatisfying.
Her writing style is jejune and lacks depth. 她的写作风格幼稚，缺乏深度。

The jejune lecture bored the students.  枯燥乏味的讲座让学生们感到无聊。

The hospital food was jejune and tasteless. 医院的食物乏味无味。


synonym
naive, simplistic, childish, immature, puerile, insipid, vapid, bland, uninteresting
antonym
mature, sophisticated, complex, interesting, flavorful, stimulating
词性
adjective
