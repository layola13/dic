imaginings:/ɪˈmæʤɪnɪŋz/
n.
想象；幻想；空想
products of the imagination; things that are imagined
例句：His dark imaginings kept him awake at night. 他黑暗的幻想让他夜不能寐。

例句：Her childhood imaginings were filled with magical creatures. 她童年的幻想充满了神奇的生物。

近义词：fantasies, daydreams, visions, conceptions
反义词：realities, facts
词性：名词 (plural noun)

PHRASE->
what if = 如果...会怎么样
What if it rains tomorrow? 如果明天下雨怎么办？

what for = 为了什么
What did you buy this for? 你买这个是为了什么？

no matter what = 无论如何
No matter what happens, I'll be there for you. 无论发生什么，我都会在你身边。

what's more = 而且；更重要的是
He's intelligent, and what's more, he's very kind. 他很聪明，而且，他还很善良。

what about = ...怎么样
What about going to the cinema tonight? 今晚去看电影怎么样？
