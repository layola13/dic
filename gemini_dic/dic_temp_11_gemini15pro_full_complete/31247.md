chanty:/ˈʃænti/ (US), /ˈtʃænti/ (UK)| n.| 船歌；劳动号子|A rhythmical song sung by sailors in time with their work, especially one used to coordinate pulling on ropes.|例句：The sailors sang a chanty as they hoisted the sails. 水手们一边升帆一边唱着船歌。

例句：The rhythmic chanty helped the crew work together.  有节奏的船歌帮助船员们协同工作。

近义词：song, work song|反义词: None|词性:noun
