optimally:/ˈɒptɪməli/ (US) /ˈɑːptɪməli/ (UK)| adv.|最佳地，最优地|In the best or most effective way possible.|The system is designed to perform optimally under a variety of conditions.  该系统旨在在各种条件下都能发挥最佳性能。

The resources were allocated optimally to maximize efficiency. 资源得到最佳分配，以最大限度地提高效率。|近义词：ideally, perfectly, best|反义词：poorly, badly, inefficiently|词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *optimalis*, from Latin optimus (“best”).
