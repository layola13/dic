nonregulatory:/nɑːnˈreɡjələˌtɔːri/
adjective
非监管的；非调节的
Not subject to regulation or control by rules or laws.
The company operates in a nonregulatory environment.  这家公司在一个非监管的环境中运营。
The report focused on nonregulatory approaches to environmental protection.  该报告重点关注非监管的环境保护方法。
synonym: unregulated, uncontrolled, unsupervised
antonym: regulated, controlled, supervised
词性: 形容词
