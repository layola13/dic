digitule:/ˈdɪdʒɪtjuːl/
n.
小管，爪间突
A small, usually hollow, finger-like or pad-like appendage, especially one occurring on the feet of some insects and mites.
Aphids have digitules on their feet that secrete a sticky substance.  蚜虫的脚上有爪间突，会分泌一种粘性物质。

digitule:/ˈdɪdʒɪtjuːl/
n.
爪间突
A small flexible appendage, typically found on the feet of insects and other arthropods.
The digitule helps the insect grip smooth surfaces. 爪间突帮助昆虫抓住光滑的表面。
