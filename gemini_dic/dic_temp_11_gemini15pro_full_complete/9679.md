antitetanic: /ˌæntiˈtɛtənɪk/ | adj. | 抗破伤风的，破伤风抗毒素的 | Preventing or counteracting tetanus (lockjaw). | The doctor administered an antitetanic serum. 医生注射了破伤风抗毒素。 | Synonyms: antitetanus | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "tetanus-causing" as a contrasting concept) | adjective


Etymology:

From anti- + tetanic.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek. "Tetanic" relates to tetanus, a bacterial infection.
