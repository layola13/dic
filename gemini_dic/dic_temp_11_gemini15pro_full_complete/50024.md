denominationally:/dɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənəli/
adv.
在教派方面地；按面额地
From a denominational point of view; in terms of different denominations.
The country is denominationally diverse.  这个国家在教派方面很多元。
The funds were divided denominationally. 资金按面额划分。
Synonyms:  sectarianly, confessionally
Antonyms:  nondenominationally, ecumenically
词性: 副词
