nonpalliative:/ˌnɒnpæliətɪv/
adjective
非姑息的，非缓解的
Not designed to relieve symptoms or improve quality of life, especially in the context of serious illness.
The patient received nonpalliative chemotherapy aimed at curing the disease.  病人接受了旨在治愈疾病的非姑息性化疗。
The doctor recommended nonpalliative surgery to remove the tumor. 医生建议进行非姑息性手术切除肿瘤。
Near synonyms: curative, remedial, restorative
Antonyms: palliative, supportive
词性: 形容词
