herders:/ˈhɜːrdərz/ (US) /ˈhɜːdəz/ (UK)| n.|牧民|People who take care of a herd of animals, especially grazing animals like sheep, cattle, or goats.|The herders moved their flocks to higher ground for the summer. 牧民们夏天把羊群赶到更高的地方放牧。|
herders:/ˈhɜːrdərz/ (US) /ˈhɜːdəz/ (UK)| n.|牧民|They are nomadic herders, constantly moving in search of fresh pasture. 他们是游牧民族，不断地迁移寻找新的牧场。|近义词：shepherds, herdsmen, pastoralists|反义词:farmers (not always a direct opposite, but represents a different style of animal husbandry)|noun
