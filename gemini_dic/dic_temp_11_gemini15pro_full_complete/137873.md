nonvisceral:/ˌnɒnˈvɪsərəl/ | adj. | 非本能的；非内脏的；非发自肺腑的 | Not relating to deep inward feelings; not proceeding from or felt in the viscera. |  Her reaction to the news was surprisingly nonvisceral, more intellectual than emotional. 她对这个消息的反应出奇地平静，更多的是理智而非情感。|  The artist's work is known for its nonvisceral, almost detached, quality. 这位艺术家的作品以其非本能的、几乎是超然的气质而闻名。 | 近义词: unemotional, detached, intellectual, cerebral, objective | 反义词: visceral, emotional, instinctive, gut, intuitive | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + visceral (from Latin "viscera" meaning "internal organs")
