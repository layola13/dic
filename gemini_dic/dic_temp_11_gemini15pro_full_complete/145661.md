overcontributions:/ˌoʊvərˌkɑːntrɪˈbjuːʃənz/| n.|超额捐款，过度贡献|An amount contributed in excess of a prescribed limit, such as to a retirement plan. |Example:  He had to withdraw the overcontributions from his IRA to avoid penalties. 他不得不从他的个人退休账户中取出超额缴款，以免受罚。

Example: Overcontributions to the campaign fund raised some eyebrows.  对竞选基金的超额捐款引起了一些人的关注。|近义词：excess contributions, surplus contributions|反义词: undercontributions, insufficient contributions|词性:名词
