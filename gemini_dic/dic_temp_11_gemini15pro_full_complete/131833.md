nonconsoling:/ˌnɒnkənˈsəʊlɪŋ/
adjective
不令人安慰的，无法安慰的
Failing to console or comfort someone.
The news from the doctor was nonconsoling, leaving the family with little hope.  医生带来的消息并不令人安慰，让家人几乎失去了希望。
The child's cries were nonconsoling, even after receiving a hug from their mother.  即使在得到母亲的拥抱后，孩子的哭声仍然无法得到安慰。
近义词:uncomforting, disheartening, distressing
反义词:comforting, consoling, reassuring
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + consoling (present participle of console, from Latin consolari "to comfort, encourage, strengthen").
