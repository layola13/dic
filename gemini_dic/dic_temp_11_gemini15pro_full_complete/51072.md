despairful:/dɪˈspeərfəl/
adjective
绝望的，充满绝望的
Filled with or expressing despair.
She gave him a despairful look. 她绝望地看着他。
近义词:despondent, hopeless, desperate
反义词:hopeful, optimistic
词性:adjective

despairful:/dɪˈspeərfəl/
词性:adjective
中文翻译:绝望的，充满绝望的
英文解析:Filled with or expressing despair; feeling or showing a complete loss or absence of hope.  Often suggests a visible manifestation of despair.
例句:His despairful cries echoed through the empty house. 他绝望的哭喊声在空荡荡的房子里回荡。
例句:The refugees cast despairful glances towards the closed border.  难民们绝望地望着关闭的边境。
近义词:desperate, hopeless, despondent, forlorn
反义词:hopeful, optimistic, cheerful, buoyant
词源:From Middle English *disperful, from Old French desperé + -ful. Ultimately from Latin dēspērāre (“to be without hope”).
