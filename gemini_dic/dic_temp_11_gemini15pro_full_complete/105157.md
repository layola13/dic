kettle:/ˈketl/ (US), /ˈkɛtl/ (UK)| n. |水壶|A metal or plastic container with a handle and a spout, used for boiling water.|I put the kettle on to make some tea. 我把水壶放在炉子上烧水做茶。|synonyms: teakettle, boiler, pot|antonyms:  (no direct antonym, perhaps cooler, refrigerator in a broad sense)|noun
