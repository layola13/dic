Etymology:
From *labour* + *-ed* + *-ly*.  *Labour* comes from Old French *labour* "work, toil, trouble, hardship,"  from Latin *labor* "work, toil, hardship, exertion, fatigue." *-ed* is a suffix forming the past participle of weak verbs. *-ly* is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
