oversocializes: /ˌoʊvərˈsoʊʃəlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度社交
Engages in social interaction to an excessive degree.
She oversocializes at parties, trying to talk to everyone at once. 她参加聚会时过度社交，试图同时与所有人交谈。
He tends to oversocialize on business trips, blurring the lines between professional and personal. 他在出差时往往会过度社交，模糊了职业和个人之间的界限。
Synonym: overdoes socializing
Antonym: isolates, withdraws
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix): Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, excessively"
"socialize": from "social" (from Latin "socialis" - pertaining to allies or companions) + "-ize" (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become").
