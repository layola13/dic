biodynamic:/ˌbaɪəʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/| adj.|生物动力学的，生物动力法的|Relating to or characterized by biodynamics, a system of farming and gardening that follows ecological principles and emphasizes the interconnectedness of all living things on the farm or garden, including soil, plants, and animals. It often incorporates specific preparations made from natural materials and follows astronomical calendars.|例句：The biodynamic farm produces high-quality vegetables without the use of synthetic pesticides. 这家生物动力农场生产不使用合成农药的高质量蔬菜。

例句：She uses biodynamic practices in her garden, treating it as a self-sustaining ecosystem.  她在花园里使用生物动力法，将其视为一个自给自足的生态系统。

|近义词：organic, ecological, sustainable|反义词：conventional, industrial|词性：形容词
