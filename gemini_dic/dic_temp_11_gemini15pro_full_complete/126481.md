mythology:/mɪˈθɑːlədʒi/ /mɪˈθɒlədʒi/
n.
神话；神话学；神话集
A collection of myths, especially one belonging to a particular religious or cultural tradition.  The study of myths.
例句：Greek mythology is full of fascinating stories. 希腊神话充满了引人入胜的故事。

例句：He's fascinated by Norse mythology. 他对北欧神话着迷。

近义词：lore, legend, fable, folktale
反义词：history, reality, fact
词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mythologia, from Ancient Greek μυθολογία (muthología, “story-telling, mythology”), from μῦθος (mûthos, “story, tale, myth”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).
