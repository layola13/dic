nonpoors:/nɑːnˈpʊrz/ | n. |非穷人，富人|  A non-standard and informal term referring to people who are not poor; essentially, the wealthy or affluent.  This term is often used ironically or satirically to highlight economic disparity. |  Example: "The nonpoors are always complaining about taxes." 非穷人总是抱怨税收。| Synonym: rich, wealthy, affluent, privileged | Antonym: poor, impoverished, destitute | noun

nonpoors:/nɑːnˈpʊrz/ | adj. (informal, often satirical) | 非穷人的，富裕的 | Used to describe someone who is not poor, often with a connotation of privilege or wealth.  This usage is less common than the noun form and usually carries a sarcastic tone. | Example:  "They live in a nonpoors neighborhood." 他们住在富人区。 | Synonym: rich, wealthy, affluent, privileged | Antonym: poor, impoverished, destitute | adjective
