claymore:/ˈkleɪmɔːr/
n.
阔剑；大型双刃剑；定向雷
a large two-edged sword formerly used by Scottish Highlanders.  A type of directional anti-personnel mine.
The claymore was a fearsome weapon on the battlefield. 这把阔剑在战场上是一件可怕的武器。
The soldiers carefully placed the claymores around the perimeter. 士兵们小心翼翼地把定向雷布置在周边。
近义词：sword, broadsword, mine, explosive
反义词：(No direct antonym for a weapon)
词性: noun


claymores:/ˈkleɪmɔːrz/
n. (plural)
阔剑（复数）；定向雷（复数）
Plural form of "claymore."
The clan charged into battle, their claymores flashing in the sun.  这个家族冲进战场，他们的阔剑在阳光下闪闪发光。
Several claymores were triggered by the advancing enemy troops.  前进的敌军触发了几个定向雷。
近义词：swords, broadswords, mines, explosives
反义词：(No direct antonym for a weapon)
词性: noun


词源: From Scottish Gaelic *claidheamh mòr*  meaning "great sword".
