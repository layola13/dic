indecomposable:/ˌɪndɪkəmˈpoʊzəbl/
adj.
不可分解的，不可组合的
Not able to be separated into simpler parts or components.
This protein is made of indecomposable amino acids.  这种蛋白质是由不可分解的氨基酸组成的。
The argument presents an indecomposable whole.  这个论点呈现了一个不可分割的整体。
近义词: indivisible, inseparable, unbreakable
反义词: decomposable, divisible, separable
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From in- "not" + decomposable, from decompose + -able.  Decompose ultimately comes from Latin de- "down, away" + componere "to put together".
