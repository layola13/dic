extol:/ɪkˈstəʊl/ /ɪkˈstɔːl/ | vt. | 赞美；颂扬；称赞 | To praise highly; glorify. |

The critics extolled her performance. 评论家们高度赞扬了她的表演。

He extolled the virtues of a simple life. 他颂扬了简朴生活的优点。

近义词: praise, laud, commend, acclaim, celebrate, glorify

反义词: criticize, condemn, denounce, disparage, belittle

词性: verb
