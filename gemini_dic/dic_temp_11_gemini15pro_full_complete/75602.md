fuff:/fʌf/
n.
绒毛, 无聊的东西, 吹嘘
a breath of wind; something light or worthless; an act of puffing.
She dismissed his promises as mere fuff. 她认为他的承诺只是空话。
近义词: fluff, nonsense, bluster
反义词: sense, substance
词性: 名词

fuff:/fʌf/
v.
吹气, 喷出, 吹嘘
to puff; to blow in puffs; to swell or fill with air.
He fuffed up the pillows before lying down. 他躺下前把枕头拍松。
近义词: puff, blow, inflate
反义词: deflate, flatten
词性: 动词
