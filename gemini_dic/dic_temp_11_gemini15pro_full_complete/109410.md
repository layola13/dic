leon:/ˈliːɒn/ (UK), /ˈliːɑːn/ (US)| n. |狮子; (男子名)利昂|A large, powerfully built cat that is typically yellowish-brown in color and the male of which has a thick mane around its head and neck.  It is native to Africa and India.  Also a male given name.|例句：The leon stalked its prey through the tall grass. 狮子在高高的草丛中跟踪它的猎物。

例句：Leon is a common name in Spain.  利昂在西班牙是一个常见的名字。|近义词：lion|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
