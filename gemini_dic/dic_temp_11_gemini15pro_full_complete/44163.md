criticisms:/ˈkrɪtɪˌsɪzəmz/
n.
批评，批判
the act of expressing disapproval of someone or something
The film received harsh criticisms from the press. 这部电影受到了媒体的严厉批评。
近义词: censures, objections, complaints, attacks
反义词: praise, compliments, approvals
