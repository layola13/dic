bushwhacks: /ˈbʊʃˌwæks/ | v. | 伏击，开辟（道路），在丛林中开路 | to attack someone suddenly and unexpectedly, especially from a hidden position; to clear a path through dense woods or bushes by cutting away branches and other growth. |  Example: The soldiers were bushwhacked by the enemy as they marched through the jungle. 士兵们在穿过丛林时遭到敌人的伏击。

Example:  We had to bushwhack our way through the dense undergrowth to reach the waterfall. 我们不得不一路砍伐茂密的灌木丛才能到达瀑布。| 近义词: ambush, waylay, pioneer, blaze (a trail) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 动词

bushwhack: /ˈbʊʃˌwæk/ | n. | 伏击，丛林小路 | An act of bushwhacking; a rough path cleared through dense woods or bushes. | Example: The patrol was caught in a deadly bushwhack.  巡逻队遭遇了一场致命的伏击。


Example:  The bushwhack was barely visible, a narrow tunnel through the thick vegetation. 这条丛林小路几乎看不见，只是茂密植被中的一条狭窄通道。| 近义词: ambush, trail, path | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
