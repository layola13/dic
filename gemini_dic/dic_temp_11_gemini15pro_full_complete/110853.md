lintwhite: /ˈlɪntˌwaɪt/ | adj. |  洁白的，像棉絮一样白的 |  Resembling the white fluff of lint; extremely white.  | The freshly fallen snow created a lintwhite landscape. 新落下的雪创造了一片洁白的景象。 | 近义词: snowy, pure white, pristine, immaculate | 反义词: dirty, soiled, grimy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Compound of "lint" (referring to the soft white fibers from cotton) and "white."
