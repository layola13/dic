nondocumentary: /ˌnɒnˌdɒkjʊˈmentəri/ /ˌnɑːnˌdɑːkjəˈmentəri/
adjective
非纪录片的
Not of or relating to documentaries; fictional.  A film or television program that is not a documentary.
Example:  The film is a nondocumentary exploration of love and loss. 这部电影是对爱与失去的非纪录片式探索。

Example:  She prefers nondocumentary films because she finds them more entertaining. 她更喜欢非纪录片电影，因为她觉得它们更具娱乐性。

synonym: fictional, dramatic, narrative
antonym: documentary, factual, real
word type: adjective
