interwar:/ˌɪntərˈwɔːr/ | adj. | 两次世界大战之间的；战间的 | Of, relating to, or occurring during the period between World War I and World War II. | The interwar period saw significant social and political upheaval. 两次世界大战期间发生了重大的社会和政治动荡。|  The interwar years were a time of great uncertainty.  两次世界大战之间的那段时间充满了不确定性。| 近义词: between the wars | 反义词: prewar, postwar | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *inter* "between" + *war*.
