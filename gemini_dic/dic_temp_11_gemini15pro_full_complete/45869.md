cutaways:/ˈkʌtəˌweɪz/
n.
剖面图，断面图;  (电影、电视节目中的)切换镜头;  (服装的)挖剪部分
A cutaway is a drawing or model of something that has been cut in half so that you can see the inside.  A cutaway in a film or television programme is a short scene that shows something connected with the main story but not directly part of it. A cutaway is also part of a garment that has been cut away.
The diagram shows a cutaway of the engine.  该图显示了发动机的剖面图。

The documentary used cutaways to show the process of building the bridge.  这部纪录片使用切换镜头来展示建造桥梁的过程。

Her dress featured stylish cutaways at the shoulders.  她的连衣裙在肩部有时尚的挖剪设计。
