miryachit:/ˈmɪriəˌtʃɪt/| n.|米里亚契特，颤抖症（一种文化束缚综合征）|A culture-bound syndrome found primarily in Siberia, characterized by an uncontrollable urge to mimic the movements and speech of others, often accompanied by echolalia and echopraxia.|Example:  The documentary explored the phenomenon of miryachit in remote Siberian villages. 这部纪录片探讨了西伯利亚偏远村庄的米里亚契特现象。

Example: Researchers are studying the possible neurological basis of miryachit. 研究人员正在研究米里亚契特的可能的神经学基础。|近义词：jumping Frenchmen of Maine (similar syndrome), latah (similar syndrome)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
