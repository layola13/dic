fluxed:/flʌkst/
adjective
熔化的；处于不断的变化中的
fused; in constant change or flow.
The artist worked with fluxed metals to create the sculpture.  艺术家用熔化的金属创作了这座雕塑。
Synonyms: molten, fluid, volatile, unstable
Antonyms: solid, stable, fixed, static
词性: 形容词

fluxed:/flʌkst/
verb
用助熔剂处理；使熔化；使激动
treated with a flux; melted; made agitated
The jeweler fluxed the gold before shaping it into a ring.  珠宝商在将黄金塑造成戒指之前先用助熔剂处理了它。
Synonyms: fuse, melt, liquefy, agitate
Antonyms: solidify, freeze, stabilize, calm
词性: 动词
