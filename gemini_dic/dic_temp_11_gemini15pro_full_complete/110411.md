like-a-house-on-fire:/laɪk ə haʊs ɒn ˈfaɪə(r)/ | adv. | 迅速地，快速地；非常成功地 |  This idiom describes something that happens very rapidly or successfully. It evokes the image of a house burning quickly and intensely. | The business is doing like a house on fire. 生意做得红红火火。| synonyms: rapidly, quickly, successfully, briskly, like wildfire, like a bat out of hell | antonyms: slowly, gradually, poorly, unsuccessfully | adverb
