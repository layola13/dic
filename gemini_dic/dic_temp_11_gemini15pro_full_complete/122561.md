moder:/ˈmoʊdər/ (US), /ˈməʊdə(r)/ (UK)| adjective | 较新的; 现代的; 温和的; 适度的 | moderate; of recent origin or current; not extreme | The hotel offers all the modern conveniences. 这家酒店提供所有现代化的便利设施。|Synonyms: contemporary, recent, up-to-date, mild, temperate | Antonyms: ancient, old-fashioned, outdated, extreme, immoderate | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle French *moderne*, from Late Latin *modernus*, from Latin *modo* "just now".
