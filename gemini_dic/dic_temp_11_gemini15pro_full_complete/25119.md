bucholzite:/ˈbuːkˌhɔːlzaɪt/| n. |硅线石|A fibrous variety of sillimanite, Al2SiO5, usually found in metamorphic rocks.|Example 1: Bucholzite was identified in the rock sample.  岩石样本中发现了硅线石。

Example 2: The presence of bucholzite indicates high-temperature metamorphism. 硅线石的存在表明高温变质作用。|Synonym: sillimanite, fibrolite|Antonym: N/A|noun
