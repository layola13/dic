noncoordination:/ˌnɒnkəʊˌɔːdɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |不协调，缺乏协调|Lack of coordination or harmony between different elements or actions.  |The noncoordination between the departments led to a lot of wasted time and resources. 各部门之间缺乏协调导致大量时间和资源的浪费。

The noncoordination of their movements made the dance look clumsy.  他们动作的不协调让舞蹈看起来很笨拙。|近义词：disharmony, incoordination, disunity|反义词：coordination, harmony, unity|词性:名词

Etymology->
From non- + coordination.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "lack of".  "Coordination" comes from the Latin word "coordinatio," meaning "arrangement together."
