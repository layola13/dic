nondyspeptically:/nɒndɪsˈpɛptɪkli/
adverb
无消化不良地;舒适地
without dyspepsia; comfortably.
He ate his meal nondyspeptically, enjoying every bite. 他毫无消化不良地吃完了他的饭，享受每一口。
The new medication allowed him to eat nondyspeptically for the first time in years.  新的药物让他多年来第一次可以舒适地进食。
Synonyms: comfortably, easily, smoothly
Antonyms: dyspeptically, uncomfortably, with indigestion
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): not, without (from Latin)
dyspepsia (noun): indigestion (from Greek dys- "bad" + peptein "to digest")
-tically (suffix): in a manner relating to
