chaparreras:/ˌtʃæpəˈrɛrəz/
noun
皮套褲，皮革护腿
Leather leggings worn over trousers by cowboys as protection against thorns and brush.
The cowboy wore his chaparreras while riding through the dense brush. 牛仔穿着皮套褲骑马穿过茂密的灌木丛。

chaparreras:/ˌtʃæpəˈrɛrəz/
noun
恰帕雷拉斯（墨西哥一种灌木）
A dense growth of thorny bushes or small trees, especially in Mexico and Texas.  (Less common usage referring to the plant itself)
The horse struggled to make its way through the chaparreras. 马艰难地穿过茂密的灌木丛。

近义词: chaps, leggings
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a protective garment)
词性: n.
