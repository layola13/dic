coelenterate:/sɪˈlɛntəreɪt/ | n. |腔肠动物|An aquatic invertebrate animal of the phylum Cnidaria, which includes jellyfishes, corals, and sea anemones. Characterized by a radially symmetrical body with a single opening serving as both mouth and anus, and tentacles armed with stinging cells (cnidocytes).|例句：Coral reefs are built by coelenterates. 珊瑚礁是由腔肠动物建造的。

例句：Jellyfish are a type of coelenterate. 水母是一种腔肠动物。|近义词：cnidarian|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *koilos* (hollow) + *enteron* (intestine)
