breakableness:/ˈbreɪkəbəlnəs/
n.|易碎性；脆弱性|The breakableness of the vase made me handle it with extreme care. 花瓶的易碎性让我格外小心地处理它。|例句：The breakableness of the china is a concern when moving house. 搬家时瓷器的易碎性是一个问题。|近义词：fragility, brittleness, vulnerability|反义词：strength, durability, toughness|词性:名词

Etymology:
break (v.) + -able (suffix) + -ness (suffix)
From Middle English *breken, from Old English *brecan (“to break”), from Proto-Germanic *brekaną (“to break”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrégʰ- (“to break”).
-able: from Old French -able, from Latin -abilis.
-ness: from Old English -nes(s), from Proto-Germanic *-inassuz.
