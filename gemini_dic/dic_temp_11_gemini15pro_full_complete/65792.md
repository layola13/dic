| v. |驱逐，逐出|The act of forcing someone to leave a property, especially a rented one.|The landlord is evicting the tenants for not paying rent. 房东因租户未付房租而将其驱逐。|近义词：expelling, ousting, removing|反义词:admitting, welcoming|Verb
