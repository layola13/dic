authoritative: /əˈθɔːrɪtətɪv/ (US) /ɔːˈθɒrɪtətɪv/ (UK)
| adj. | 权威的；官方的；专断的 | Having due authority; having the sanction or weight of authority; entitled to obedience or belief; official; commanding and self-confident; dictatorial. |  The book is an authoritative guide to the region's birds. 这本书是该地区鸟类的权威指南。 | synonyms: official, reliable, definitive, trustworthy, credible, authentic, valid, legitimate, sound, conclusive, dependable, cogent, compelling, weighty | antonyms: unreliable, unofficial, questionable, dubious, invalid | word type: adjective
