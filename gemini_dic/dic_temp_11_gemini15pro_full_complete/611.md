abrading:/əˈbreɪdɪŋ/
verb
磨损，擦伤
The constant rubbing was abrading the surface of the rock. 不断的摩擦磨损了岩石的表面。
例句：The harsh winds were abrading the paint off the car.  狂风把车上的油漆刮掉了。

例句：Sandpaper is often used for abrading wood.  砂纸常用来打磨木材。
近义词：scraping, rubbing, grinding, wearing, eroding
反义词：polishing, smoothing, buffing
词性:v.

abraded:/əˈbreɪdɪd/
adjective
磨损的，擦伤的
The abraded surface of the table showed its age.  桌子磨损的表面显示出它的年代。
例句：The hiker's abraded knees were a testament to the difficult climb.  徒步旅行者擦伤的膝盖证明了这次攀登的艰难。

例句：The archaeologist discovered an abraded inscription on the ancient stone.  考古学家在古老的石头上发现了一处磨损的铭文。
近义词：worn, scratched, scraped, rubbed
反义词：polished, smooth, pristine
词性:adj.

abrasion:/əˈbreɪʒən/
noun
磨损，擦伤
The abrasion on his elbow was bleeding slightly.  他手肘上的擦伤在轻微出血。
例句：The constant friction caused abrasion on the gears.  持续的摩擦导致齿轮磨损。

例句：She suffered a minor abrasion to her knee when she fell.  她摔倒时膝盖轻微擦伤。
近义词：scrape, scratch, graze, chafing, erosion
反义词：polish, smoothness
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abradere, from ab- + radere "to scrape."
