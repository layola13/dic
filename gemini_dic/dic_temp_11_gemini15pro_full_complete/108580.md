leach:/liːtʃ/| v. n.|过滤，沥析；(使)渗滤，滤除；(养分)流失，溶失；|To remove (nutrients or salts) from soil or rock by the action of percolating water.|Heavy rains leached the soil of its minerals. 大雨将土壤中的矿物质滤掉了。|近义词：filter, percolate, drain, seep|反义词：accumulate, deposit|词性：动词/名词

leached:/liːtʃt/ | adj.|过滤的；沥析的；滤出的；(养分)流失的，溶失的|Depleted of soluble or movable constituents or qualities by or as if by percolation.|The leached soil was barren and unproductive. 滤过的土壤贫瘠且 unproductive。|近义词：drained, depleted|反义词：enriched, saturated|词性：形容词
