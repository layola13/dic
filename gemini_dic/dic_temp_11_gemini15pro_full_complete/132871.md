nonelaborately:/ˌnɑːnɪˈlæbərətli/ /ˌnɒnɪˈlæbərətli/
adv.
简单地；不精心地
in a way that is not elaborate or detailed
She dressed nonelaborately for the casual party. 她穿着随意地参加了这个轻松的聚会。
Synonyms: simply, plainly, unpretentiously, modestly
Antonyms: elaborately, ornately, complexly, intricately
词性: 副词
