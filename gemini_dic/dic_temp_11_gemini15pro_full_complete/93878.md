immolator:/ˈɪməleɪtər/| n. | 献祭者，自焚者 |A person who kills as a sacrificial victim, especially by fire. | The ancient immolator prepared the sacrifice for the ritual. 古代的献祭者为仪式准备祭品。

The monk, protesting the war, became an immolator in a public square.  这位僧侣为了抗议战争，在公共广场自焚。| 近义词：sacrificer, victimizer | 反义词： savior, rescuer | noun
