misgoverning:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnɪŋ/
verb
管理不善；治理不当
ruling badly or unjustly.
The country was misgoverning by a corrupt regime.  这个国家被腐败的政权统治不力。
The mayor was accused of misgoverning the city. 市长被指控治理城市不当。
mismanaging, misruling
governing well, administering effectively
verb
