jangle: /ˈdʒæŋɡəl/ | v. n. adj. | 刺耳地响，发出叮当声，(神经等)紧张 | To make a harsh, ringing metallic sound, or to cause something to make this sound;  A harsh, ringing metallic sound; On edge, nervous. | The keys jangled in his pocket. 他的钥匙在他口袋里叮当作响。| 近义词: clang, clash, grate, jar, rattle | 反义词: soothe, calm | 词性: 动词, 名词, 形容词
词源:  Late Middle English: probably imitative.

jangled: /ˈdʒæŋɡəld/ | adj. | 神经紧张的，烦躁不安的 | On edge, nervous, irritated. |  My nerves were jangled after the near accident.  险些发生事故后，我的神经非常紧张。 | 近义词: edgy, nervous, irritable, frazzled | 反义词: calm, relaxed, composed | 词性: 形容词
