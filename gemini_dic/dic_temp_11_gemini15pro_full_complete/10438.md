applauds:/əˈplɔːdz/| v.| 鼓掌，称赞 | To show approval or praise by clapping the hands. | The audience applauds the performance enthusiastically.  观众热情地为演出鼓掌。|Synonyms: clap, cheer, commend, praise | Antonyms: criticize, boo, condemn | verb

applause:/əˈplɔːz/| n.| 鼓掌，喝彩 | The showing of approval or praise by clapping the hands. | The applause was deafening after her speech. 她演讲结束后，掌声雷动。|Synonyms: clapping, cheering, ovation | Antonyms: criticism, booing | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *applaudīre, from Latin *adplaudere, from ad- (“to, towards”) + plaudere (“to clap, applaud”).
