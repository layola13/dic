cher:/ʃɛr/ |n.|亲爱的，宝贝； (法语) 昂贵的 |A term of endearment, often used for a loved one. Also means expensive in French, but this meaning is less common in English.| My cher, you look lovely tonight. 我的宝贝，你今晚看起来真漂亮。 | 近义词: darling, sweetheart, dear, honey | 反义词: (As a term of endearment, no direct opposite) | noun


cher:/ʃɛr/ |adj.| (法语) 昂贵的 |Expensive (from French). Less commonly used than the noun form in English.| That restaurant is too cher for me. 那家餐厅对我来说太贵了。| 近义词: expensive, costly, pricey | 反义词: cheap, inexpensive | adjective
