desophisticate:/diːsəˈfɪstɪkeɪt/
v.|使返璞归真；使淳朴；简化|To make less sophisticated; to simplify.|They tried to desophisticate the product to appeal to a wider market. 他们试图简化产品以吸引更广阔的市场。|simplify, streamline|complicate, elaborate|动词

desophisticate:/diːsəˈfɪstɪkeɪtɪd/
adj.|返璞归真的；淳朴的；简化的|Made less sophisticated; simplified.|He preferred a more desophisticated lifestyle. 他更喜欢简单的生活方式。|simple, unsophisticated|sophisticated, complex|形容词

词源:
de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + sophisticated
