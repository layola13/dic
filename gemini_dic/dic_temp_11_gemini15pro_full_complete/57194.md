down-on:/ˈdaʊn ɑːn/ | adj. | 缺少的，缺乏的 | lacking or deficient in something | We're a bit down-on funds at the moment. 我们现在资金有点短缺。 | 近义词：short of, lacking in | 反义词：abundant, plentiful | 词性：形容词
