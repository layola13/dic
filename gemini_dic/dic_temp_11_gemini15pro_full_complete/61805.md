emphatic:/ɪmˈfætɪk/| adj.|强调的，着重的，断然的|Done or said in a strong way and without any doubt.|She was emphatic that she had not seen him. 她强调说她没有见过他。|近义词：forceful, assertive, insistent, decided|反义词：hesitant, tentative, vague|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin emphaticus, from Greek emphatikos, from emphasis ‘emphasis.’
