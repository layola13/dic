justicer: /ˈdʒʌstɪsər/ | n. | 维持正义的人，执法者 |  A person who upholds or administers justice, especially a judge or magistrate.  | Example: The justicer listened carefully to both sides of the argument before making a ruling.  执法者在作出裁决前仔细听取了双方的论点。

Example:  The medieval justicer traveled the circuit, hearing cases in various towns.  中世纪的执法者巡回各地，在不同的城镇审理案件。

| 近义词: judge, magistrate, lawman, enforcer, arbiter | 反义词: criminal, lawbreaker, offender | 词性: 名词
