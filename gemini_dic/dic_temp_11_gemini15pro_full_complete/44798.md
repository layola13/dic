crunodal: /ˈkruːnoʊdəl/ | adj. | 交叉结点的，具有交叉结点的 | Having or relating to a crunode (a point of a curve where two branches meet and have the same tangent).  | The mathematician studied the crunodal curve on the graph.  数学家研究了图表上的交叉结点曲线。 |  Near synonyms:  nodal, intersecting | Antonyms:  smooth, continuous | adjective

crunode: /ˈkruːnoʊd/ | n. | 交叉结点;歧点 | A point on a curve where two branches meet and have the same tangent. | The crunode on the curve indicated a critical point in the system. 曲线上的交叉结点表明了系统中的一个临界点。 | Near synonyms: node, intersection point | Antonyms:  smooth point | noun
