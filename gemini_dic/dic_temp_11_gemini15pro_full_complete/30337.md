censuses:/ˈsɛnsəsɪz/
noun
人口普查；统计；调查
official counts of the population of a place, including other information such as age, sex, and occupation.
The United States conducts a census every ten years. 美国每十年进行一次人口普查。
The latest census shows a decline in the birth rate. 最新的人口普查显示出生率下降。
synonyms:  counts, surveys, polls, enumerations
antonyms: estimations, guesses, projections
n.
