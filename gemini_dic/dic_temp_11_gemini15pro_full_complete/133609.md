nonfluidities:/ˌnɒnfluːˈɪdɪtiz/
n.
缺乏流动性；僵化；不流畅
lack of fluidity; the state of being not fluid; inflexibility or stiffness.
The nonfluidities in the market made it difficult for new businesses to enter.  市场缺乏流动性，使得新企业难以进入。
stiffness, rigidity, inflexibility, immobility, viscosity
fluidity, liquidity, flexibility, mobility
noun
