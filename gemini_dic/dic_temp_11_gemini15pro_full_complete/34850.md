clinker:/ˈklɪŋkər/ /ˈklɪŋkɚ/| n.|熔渣，炉渣，熟料，（尤指烧制砖、水泥、陶器时产生的）烧结块，坚硬的块状物|A hard mass of fused material, especially brick, cement, or pottery that has been partly vitrified by heat. |The clinkers from the furnace were dumped into the quarry.  熔炉里的炉渣被倒进了采石场。|Synonyms: slag, residue, debris, cinder |Antonyms: raw material, ingredient|noun

clinker:/ˈklɪŋkər/ /ˈklɪŋkɚ/| n.|（尤指旧式木船的）重叠板条，外板|A strip of planking on a clinker-built boat that overlaps the one below.|The old boat was clinker built, with overlapping planks. 这艘旧船是重叠板条建造的，外板相互重叠。|Synonyms: plank, board, strake |Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun

clinker:/ˈklɪŋkər/ /ˈklɪŋkɚ/| n.|（口语）错误，失误|A mistake or blunder (informal).|He made a real clinker on the last hole of the golf course. 他在高尔夫球场的最后一个洞犯了一个真正的错误。|Synonyms: mistake, blunder, error, gaffe |Antonyms: success, triumph|noun
