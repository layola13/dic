in spades:/ɪn ˈspeɪdz/
adverbial phrase|大量地，绰绰有余地|A large amount or to a great degree.  Originating from card games, where spades are often the highest suit.|She has talent in spades. 她才华横溢。|近义词：abundantly, plentifully, profusely, copiously|反义词: sparsely, scarcely, barely, hardly|词性: 副词短语

in spades:/ɪn ˈspeɪdz/
adjective|大量的，绰绰有余的|Existing or available in large amounts. Less common usage than the adverbial phrase.|Opportunities are in spades in this new market.  在这个新市场，机会非常多。|近义词：abundant, plentiful, ample|反义词: scarce, limited, few|词性: 形容词 (较少用)
