outstrides:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪdz/
verb
大步超过，胜过，超越
surpass someone by taking longer steps, or to surpass in general.
The young racehorse easily outstrides the older ones. 这匹年轻的赛马轻易地超越了其他老赛马。
She outstrides all her competitors in the business world.  在商界，她胜过所有竞争对手。
近义词：surpass, outpace, exceed, outstrip
反义词：lag behind, trail, fall behind
词性：v.


outstrides:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪdz/
noun
大步
a long step or the distance covered by such a step. (Rare Usage)
His outstrides covered a considerable distance. 他的大步覆盖了相当长的距离。
With a few powerful outstrides, he reached the door.  几大步，他就到了门口。
近义词：stride
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "small steps")
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From "out-" (meaning beyond, exceeding) + "stride" (to walk with long steps)
