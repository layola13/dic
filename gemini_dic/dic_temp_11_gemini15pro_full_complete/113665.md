macho:/ˈmɑːtʃoʊ/ /ˈmætʃəʊ/| adj. n.|男子汉气概的，大男子主义的；大男子主义者|Showing aggressive pride in one's masculinity.  Excessively exhibiting or emphasizing manly characteristics, often in an assertive or domineering way.  Can also be used as a noun to refer to a man who behaves in this way.|

例句：
He had a macho attitude. = 他有很强的男子汉气概。

He likes to act macho in front of his friends. = 他喜欢在朋友面前表现得很man。


近义词：masculine, manly, virile, butch|
反义词：feminine, effeminate|
词性：adjective, noun|

词源：From Spanish *macho* "male," from Late Latin *masculus* "male."|
