derrings-do:/ˈdɛrɪŋz duː/
n.
勇敢的行为；大胆的冒险；英雄壮举
Daring and adventurous deeds, often of a heroic nature.
He was known for his derrings-do on the battlefield. 他因在战场上的英勇事迹而闻名。
近义词:heroism, bravery, valor, audacity, exploits
反义词:cowardice, timidity, fearfulness
词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *derring do*, from derring (present participle of *durren*, to dare) + do (noun), deed.  Essentially, "daring deeds".
