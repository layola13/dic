outwallop: /ˌaʊtwɑːˈlɑːp/ /ˌaʊtwɔːˈlɒp/
verb
彻底击败，重创
to defeat decisively; to trounce
例句：The underdog team outwalloped the reigning champions.  弱旅彻底击败了卫冕冠军。

例句：She outwalloped her opponent in the debate. 她在辩论中彻底击败了对手。

近义词：crush, trounce, rout, thrash, drub, clobber, lick, whip
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：v.
