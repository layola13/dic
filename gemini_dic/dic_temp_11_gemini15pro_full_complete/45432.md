curtailer:/kərˈteɪlər/
n.|限制者，缩减者|Someone or something that curtails or reduces something.|The budget curtailer was unpopular with the staff. 预算削减者不受员工欢迎。|近义词: restrictor, reducer, cutter|反义词: expander, increaser|词性:名词

curtail:/kərˈteɪl/
v.|缩减，限制|To reduce in extent or quantity; to shorten.|The company curtailed its spending. 公司削减了开支。|近义词: reduce, shorten, decrease|反义词: increase, expand, lengthen|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *curteillier* "cut short," from *curt* "short" (from Latin *curtus*) + *-eillier*, agent suffix.
