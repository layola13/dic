allophylic:/ˌæləˈfɪlɪk/
adjective
亲异族文化的；喜爱异族文化的
Attracted to or preferring what is foreign or different, especially other cultures.
She demonstrated allophylic tendencies by immersing herself in the local customs and traditions during her travels. 她通过在旅行期间沉浸在当地习俗和传统中展现出亲异族文化的倾向。
allophylic
adjective
亲异族的
Relating to or characterized by allophyly.  (Allophyly refers to the occurrence of similar or parallel biological traits in distinct groups or lineages, especially as a result of convergent evolution.)
The researchers found allophylic similarities in the wing structures of birds and bats. 研究人员发现鸟类和蝙蝠的翅膀结构存在亲异族的相似性。

近义词:xenophilic, cosmopolitan, multicultural
反义词:xenophobic, ethnocentric, parochial
词性:adjective
