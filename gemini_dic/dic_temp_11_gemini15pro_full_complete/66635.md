existential:/ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəl/ |adj.|存在主义的；有关存在的；存在性的|Relating to existence, especially human existence and its meaning, purpose, and value.  Concerned with the nature of human existence as determined by individual existence. |例句：The young man was having an existential crisis, questioning the meaning of life.  这个年轻人正经历着存在主义危机，质疑生命的意义。

例句：Existential philosophy explores questions of being and nothingness. 存在主义哲学探讨存在与虚无的问题。|近义词：philosophical, ontological|反义词：practical, concrete|词性: adjective
