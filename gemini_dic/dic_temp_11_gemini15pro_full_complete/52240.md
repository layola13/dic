diazotize: /daɪˈæzəˌtaɪz/ | v. | 重氮化 | To convert into a diazonium compound.  The process involves reacting a primary aromatic amine with nitrous acid. | Aniline was diazotized and then coupled with β-naphthol. 将苯胺重氮化，然后与β-萘酚偶联。 |  synonyms: convert, transform | antonyms: N/A | verb

diazotization: /daɪˌæzətɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 重氮化（作用） | The process of converting a primary aromatic amine into a diazonium compound. | The diazotization reaction is crucial in the synthesis of many azo dyes. 重氮化反应在许多偶氮染料的合成中至关重要。 | synonyms: conversion, transformation | antonyms: N/A | noun
