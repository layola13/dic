altis:/ˈæltɪs/
adjective
高的；高耸的
High, lofty, towering.  Referring to something of great height.
The altis oak tree provided shade for the entire garden.  高耸的橡树为整个花园提供了阴凉。
The altis mountains were capped with snow. 高耸的山脉被白雪覆盖。
近义词:high, lofty, towering, elevated
反义词:low, short, small
词性:adjective

altis:/ˈæltɪs/
noun
圣林；神殿
A sacred grove; a temple.  Specifically referring to the sacred enclosure at Olympia, Greece.
The athletes competed in the altis, a place revered by all Greeks.  运动员们在圣林中比赛，这是一个所有希腊人都敬畏的地方。
The altis was home to numerous temples and statues dedicated to the gods. 圣林里有许多供奉神灵的寺庙和雕像。
近义词:sanctuary, shrine, temple
反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps "profane ground")
词性:noun
