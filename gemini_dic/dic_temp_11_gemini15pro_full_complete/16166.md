banker: /ˈbæŋkər/ (US) /ˈbæŋkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |银行家；银行工作人员|A person who works in a bank, especially one who has an important job.|例句：He's an investment banker in New York. 他是纽约的一位投资银行家。

例句：She worked as a banker for twenty years before retiring. 她退休前当了二十年的银行职员。

近义词：financier, moneylender|反义词: borrower, debtor|词性: noun
