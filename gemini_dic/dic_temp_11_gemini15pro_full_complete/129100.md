nittily:/ˈnɪtɪli/
adv.
一丝不苟地，详细地，精确地
In a precise and detailed manner; meticulously.
He examined the contract nittily, looking for any loopholes.  他一丝不苟地检查了合同，寻找任何漏洞。
近义词: meticulously, precisely, carefully, scrupulously, painstakingly
反义词: carelessly, sloppily, haphazardly
词性: 副词

nittily:/ˈnɪtɪli/
词源: From the adjective "nitty," meaning "detailed" or "concerned with minor details," + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Nitty" itself is likely a back-formation from "nitpicking."
英文解析:  Nittily describes actions performed with extreme attention to detail, often to a degree that may seem excessive or overly meticulous. It emphasizes a focus on the smallest and most precise aspects of a task or situation.
例句：She nittily arranged the flowers in the vase, ensuring each stem was perfectly positioned. 她一丝不苟地把花插在花瓶里，确保每一根茎都摆放得恰到好处。
例句：The lawyer nittily reviewed the evidence, searching for any inconsistencies. 律师仔细审查了证据，寻找任何不一致之处。
近义词: meticulously, precisely, rigorously, scrupulously
反义词: carelessly, negligently, perfunctorily
词性: 副词
