dowse's:/ˈdaʊzɪz/
noun
探测者的（东西）; 探测术士的（东西）
belonging to a dowser.
例句：The dowse's rods twitched, indicating the presence of water. 探测者的杆子抽动着，表明水的存在。

例句：The dowse's intuition led him to a hidden spring. 探测术士的直觉引导他找到了一个隐藏的泉水。
近义词: diviner's, water witcher's
反义词:  None (lacks a direct opposite)
词性:  Possessive noun


dowse:/daʊz/
verb
探测（地下水源）
search for underground water or minerals using a divining rod.
例句：He learned to dowse for water from his grandfather. 他从祖父那里学会了探测地下水。

例句：She dowsed the field for a suitable well location.  她在田里探测一个合适的井位。
近义词: divine, water witch
反义词: None (lacks a direct opposite)
词性: verb
