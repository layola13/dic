gitana: /xiˈtɑːnə/ /hɪˈtɑːnə/ | adj. | 吉普赛的；吉普赛风格的 | Relating to or characteristic of Gypsies or their culture. (Less common usage as an adjective) | The music had a distinctly gitana flavor.  这音乐有着鲜明的吉普赛风格。 | Synonym: Gypsy, Roma | Antonym:  (No direct antonym) | adjective
