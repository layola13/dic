misguidance:/ˌmɪsˈɡaɪdəns/| n.|误导，错误引导|The misguidance from his mentor led him down the wrong career path.  他导师的误导使他走上了错误的职业道路。|The campaign was marred by misguidance and misinformation. 这场运动因误导和错误信息而受损。|近义词：misdirection, misinstruction, bad advice|反义词: guidance, direction, good advice|词性: noun
