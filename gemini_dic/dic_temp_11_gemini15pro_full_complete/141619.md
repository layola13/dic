optical:/ˈɒptɪkəl/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪkəl/ (US)| adj.|光学的；视觉的；眼睛的|Relating to sight, vision, or the science of light.|例句：Optical illusions can be very deceptive. 光学错觉可能非常具有欺骗性。
