baleen:/bəˈliːn/ /beɪˈliːn/| n. |鲸须，鲸须板|A horny substance growing in plates from the upper jaws of certain whales, forming a fringe-like sieve for straining plankton from the water.  鲸鱼上颚生长的一种角质物质，形成板状，像过滤器一样从水中过滤浮游生物。|例句：The whale uses its baleen to filter krill from the water. 鲸鱼用它的鲸须从水中过滤磷虾。

例句：Baleen whales are the largest animals on Earth. 须鲸是地球上最大的动物。|近义词：whalebone|反义词: None|词性: noun

baleen:/bəˈliːn/ /beɪˈliːn/| adj. |须鲸的|Of or relating to baleen whales.  关于或属于须鲸的。|例句：Baleen whales are filter feeders. 须鲸是滤食动物。

例句：The museum displayed a baleen whale skeleton.  博物馆展出了一具须鲸骨架。|近义词：None|反义词: toothed|词性: adjective
