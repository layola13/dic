oversatiety: /ˌoʊvərseɪˈʃaɪəti/ /ˌəʊvəseɪˈʃaɪəti/ | n. | 过饱；过度满足 | The state of being excessively full or satisfied beyond the point of comfort or pleasure. | After the Thanksgiving feast, I felt a sense of oversatiety. 感恩节盛宴后，我感到一阵过饱。|  He experienced oversatiety after eating the entire cake. 他吃完整个蛋糕后感到过度饱胀。 | 近义词: satiation, surfeit, gluttony | 反义词: hunger, starvation | 词性: noun

词源:  From Late Latin *oversatietas, from Latin oversatiāre (“to overfill, oversatisfy”), from over- + satiāre (“to satisfy, sate”).
