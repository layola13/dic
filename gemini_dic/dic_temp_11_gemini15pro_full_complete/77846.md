generous: /ˈdʒenərəs/ (US) /ˈdʒenərəs/ (UK)| adj.|慷慨的；大量的；丰富的|Showing a readiness to give more of something, as money or time, than is strictly necessary or expected; plentiful, ample.|She made a generous donation to the charity. 她向慈善机构慷慨捐赠。|近义词：liberal, bountiful, magnanimous, charitable|反义词：stingy, mean, miserly|词性:形容词

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French genereux, from Latin generosus ‘of noble birth’, from genus ‘stock, race’. The original sense ‘noble, high-born’ gave rise to ‘magnanimous, courageous’, later ‘liberal, bountiful’.
