dunderheads:/ˈdʌndərˌhɛdz/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A derogatory term for stupid or foolish people.  It implies a lack of intelligence or common sense.|Those dunderheads left the door wide open! 那些笨蛋把门敞开着！|The dunderheads forgot to bring the map. 这些傻瓜忘了带地图了。|近义词：idiots, morons, fools, nitwits|反义词：geniuses, intellectuals|词性:名词

词源:
Originating in the early 19th century, "dunderhead" combines "dunder," a now obsolete term for "dregs" or "refuse," with "head."  Thus, a "dunderhead" is someone with a head full of useless or foolish thoughts.
