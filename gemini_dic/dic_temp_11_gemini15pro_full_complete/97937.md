innovating:/ˈɪnəveɪtɪŋ/
v.|进行创新；改革；革新|Introducing new methods, ideas, or products.|The company is constantly innovating to stay ahead of the competition.  这家公司不断创新以保持领先地位。|近义词：modernizing, developing, pioneering, revolutionizing|反义词：stagnating, copying, imitating|词性: Verb (present participle)
innovate:/ˈɪnəveɪt/
v.|创新；改革；革新|Introduce new methods, ideas, or products.|The company needs to innovate to survive in the competitive market. 公司需要创新才能在竞争激烈的市场中生存。|近义词：modernize, develop, pioneer, revolutionize|反义词：stagnate, copy, imitate|词性: Verb

Etymology:
From Latin *innovatus*, past participle of *innovare* ("to renew, alter, change"), from *in-* ("in, into") + *novus* ("new").
