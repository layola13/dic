muffle-up: /ˈmʌfəl ʌp/
muffle-up: v.
muffle-up:  包裹严实；捂住；裹住
muffle-up: To wrap or cover oneself or something warmly, especially in order to protect from cold.
muffle-up:  I muffled myself up in a scarf and coat before going out into the cold.  我出门前用围巾和外套把自己裹得严严实实的。
muffle-up: bundle up, wrap up, cover up
muffle-up: uncover, expose
muffle-up: verb
