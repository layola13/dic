abomination:/əˌbɒmɪˈneɪʃn/
n.
憎恶，厌恶之物；可憎之事物
Something that causes disgust or hatred; a vile, shameful, or detestable action, condition, habit, etc.
The slaughter of innocent civilians is an abomination.  屠杀无辜平民是令人憎恶的行为。
His table manners were an abomination. 他的餐桌礼仪令人作呕。
synonym: atrocity, disgrace, horror, evil, outrage
antonym:  delight, pleasure, blessing, virtue, marvel
词性: 名词
