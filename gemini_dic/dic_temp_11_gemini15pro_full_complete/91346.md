hydropneumatic:/ˌhaɪdrəʊnjuːˈmætɪk/ (US) /ˌhaɪdroʊnuːˈmætɪk/
hydropneumatic: adj. | 液压气动的；液气压的 | Utilizing both hydraulic and pneumatic principles. | The car's hydropneumatic suspension provides a smooth ride. 这辆车的液压气动悬架提供了平稳的行驶。 | hydraulic, pneumatic | N/A | adjective
hydropneumatic:/ˌhaɪdrəʊnjuːˈmætɪk/ (US) /ˌhaɪdroʊnuːˈmætɪk/
hydropneumatic: n. | 液压气动装置 | A system or device that operates using both hydraulic and pneumatic principles. |  The hydropneumatic failed, causing the landing gear to malfunction. 液压气动装置失灵，导致起落架故障。 | hydraulic system, pneumatic system | N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- (water) + pneumatic (relating to air or gas under pressure).  The "hydro-" part refers to the use of a liquid, typically oil, and the "pneumatic" part refers to the use of compressed gas, usually nitrogen.
