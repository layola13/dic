dromedary:/ˈdrɒmədɛri/ (US) /ˈdrɒmɪdəri/ (UK)| n. |单峰骆驼|A large, fast-running camel of North Africa and Southwest Asia, having a single hump on the back.  It is used as a beast of burden and for riding. |例句：The dromedary is well adapted to desert life. 单峰骆驼非常适应沙漠生活。

例句：The caravan of dromedaries crossed the Sahara. 单峰骆驼商队穿越了撒哈拉沙漠。|近义词：camel|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dromedarius, from Latin dromadarius, from Greek dromas, drom- 'running'.
