counterthreats:/ˈkaʊntərˌθrɛts/
noun
反威胁，对抗威胁的措施

The military developed new counterthreats to address the evolving enemy tactics.  军队制定了新的反威胁措施以应对不断变化的敌人战术。
The government is investing in counterthreats to protect critical infrastructure. 政府正在投资反威胁措施以保护关键基础设施。
