algarovilla:/ˌælgəroʊˈviːjə/ | n. | 刺云实; 刺云实树; 刺云实的荚| A spiny shrub or small tree (Prosopis pallida) native to South America, having fragrant yellow flowers and long pods containing sweet pulp used for food and fodder. Also, the pods of this plant. | The algarovilla pods are a valuable source of tannins. 刺云实荚是单宁的宝贵来源。| The algarovilla tree is highly drought-resistant. 刺云实树非常耐旱。| Synonyms: mesquite, carob | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
