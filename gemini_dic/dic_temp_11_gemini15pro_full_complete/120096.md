mimer:/ˈmaɪmər/ | n. | 模仿者;演员;表演者 | A person who mimics, especially an actor or performer. | He was a skilled mimer, able to convey complex emotions without a word. 他是一位技艺娴熟的哑剧演员，能够不用语言表达复杂的情感。 | synonyms: mimic, impersonator, imitator | antonyms: original, innovator | noun
