ectozoic:/ˌɛktoʊˈzoʊɪk/
adjective
外生的，体外寄生的
Living on the outside surface of an animal.
Example: Ectozoic parasites, such as lice and ticks, can cause significant discomfort to their hosts.  体外寄生虫，如虱子和蜱虫，会给宿主带来极大的不适。

Example: The ectozoic protozoan attached itself to the fish's gills.  这种体外寄生的原生动物附着在鱼的鳃上。
synonym:epizoic, external
antonym:endozoic, internal
adjective
