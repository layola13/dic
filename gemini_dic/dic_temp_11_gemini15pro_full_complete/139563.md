obtusest:/əbˈtjuːsɪst/ /ɑːbˈtuːsɪst/| adj.|最迟钝的；最不敏锐的；最愚钝的|Superlative form of obtuse: most obtuse.  Having an angle greater than 90° and less than 180°.  Lacking sharpness or quickness of sensibility or intellect.|例句：He made the obtusest remark I've ever heard. 他说了我听过的最愚蠢的话。

例句：Of the three brothers, he was the obtusest.  在三兄弟中，他是最迟钝的。


近义词：dullest, stupidest, densest|反义词:sharpest, keenest, brightest|词性:形容词
