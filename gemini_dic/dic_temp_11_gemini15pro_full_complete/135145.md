nonoiliest: /ˌnɒnˈɔɪliɪst/
adjective
最不油腻的
superlative adjective of nonoily: least oily.  Formed by adding "-est" to "nonoily," which itself is formed by "non-" (not) + "oily."
This is the nonoiliest sunscreen I've ever used.  这是我用过最不油腻的防晒霜。
The baker searched for the nonoiliest flour to make the delicate pastry. 面包师寻找最不油腻的面粉来制作精致的糕点。
近义词: least greasy, driest
反义词: oiliest, greasiest
词性: adjective
