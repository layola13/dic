conscience-stricken:/ˈkɒnʃənsˌstrɪkən/ (US) /ˈkɒnʃənsˌstrɪkən/ (UK)| adj.|良心不安的，受良心责备的|Overwhelmed by feelings of guilt or remorse.|He was conscience-stricken after he lied to his mother.|近义词：guilty, remorseful, ashamed, repentant|反义词：unrepentant, shameless, unapologetic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From conscience + stricken (past participle of strike).  "Stricken" in this context means deeply affected or afflicted.
