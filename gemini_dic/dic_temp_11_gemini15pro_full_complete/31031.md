chamal:/tʃəˈmɑːl/| n. |粗磨的碎米，碎玉米 (尤指墨西哥或西南美洲)| Coarsely ground corn or maize, especially as used in Mexican and southwestern US cooking. | Example:  She used chamal to make tortillas. 她用粗磨的玉米粉做玉米饼。

Example: Chamal is a staple food in many parts of Mexico. 粗磨的玉米粉是墨西哥许多地区的主食。| 近义词: hominy grits, coarsely ground corn| 反义词: finely ground corn, flour |词性:noun
