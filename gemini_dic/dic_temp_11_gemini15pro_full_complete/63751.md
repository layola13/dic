epicenism:/ɪˈpɪːsɪˌnɪzəm/
n.|雌雄同体；通性|A grammatical term for words that have only one form for both masculine and feminine genders.|The word "student" is an example of epicenism, as it can refer to both male and female students.|近义词: common gender|反义词: gender distinction|词性:noun
