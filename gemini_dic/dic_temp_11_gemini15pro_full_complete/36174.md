coffers:/ˈkɔfərz/ (US), /ˈkɒfəz/ (UK)| n. |金库，资金；国库，公款|A strongbox or chest, especially one for storing valuables or money.  Figuratively, the funds or financial resources of a government, organization, or individual.|The museum's coffers were depleted after the expensive renovation.  博物馆的资金在昂贵的翻修后耗尽了。|Synonyms: treasury, funds, reserves, resources|Antonyms: debts, liabilities|Noun
