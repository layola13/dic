bapt:/bæpt/
prefix
浸礼的
related to baptism, from Greek "baptizein" to immerse
The baptistery is where baptisms are performed. 洗礼堂是举行洗礼的地方。
近义词: christen
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a prefix related to a religious rite)
词性: prefix
