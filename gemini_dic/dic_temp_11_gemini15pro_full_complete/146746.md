overhumanity:/ˌoʊvərˌhjuːˈmænəti/ | n. | 超人性，超越人性 | A state or quality of being beyond human; transcending human limitations or capabilities. |  The concept of overhumanity explores the potential for humans to evolve beyond their current physical and mental limitations.  这个概念探讨了人类超越其当前身心局限的潜力。

The author's vision of a future with advanced technology achieving overhumanity raises ethical concerns. 作者对未来先进技术实现超人类的愿景引发了伦理担忧。| 近义词：superhumanity, transhumanity | 反义词：humanity, subhumanity | 词性: 名词


词源:
"Over-" (prefix) + "humanity" (noun). "Over-" signifies "above," "beyond," or "excessive." "Humanity" refers to the human race or the characteristics that make us human.
