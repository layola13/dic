overfondle: /ˌoʊvərˈfɑːndəl/ | vt. | 过分爱抚，过分抚摸 | To fondle excessively or inappropriately. | He was accused of overfondling the child. 他被指控过分抚摸孩子。|  She overfondled the puppy, much to its annoyance. 她过分抚摸小狗，让它很烦。| 近义词: caress excessively, maul, paw | 反义词: neglect, ignore | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "fondle" (from "fond," meaning "affectionate," ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fanþijaną)
