lagomorph:/ˈlæɡəmɔːrf/| n. |兔形目动物|A mammal of the order Lagomorpha, such as a rabbit or hare, distinguished from rodents by two pairs of upper incisors, one behind the other.|The European rabbit is a common lagomorph.|近义词:rabbit, hare, pika|反义词: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group)|词性:noun
