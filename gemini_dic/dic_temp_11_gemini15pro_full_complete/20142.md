bipennate:/ˌbaɪˈpɛneɪt/| adj.|双羽状的|Having a feather-like arrangement with fibers on both sides of a central shaft, like certain muscles.|The rectus femoris is a bipennate muscle. 股直肌是一块双羽状肌。|Synonym:bipenniform|Antonym:unipennate|adjective
