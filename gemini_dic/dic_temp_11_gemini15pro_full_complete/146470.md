overfulfilled:/ˌoʊvərˈfʊlˈfɪld/ | adj. | 超额完成的，过度满足的 | Exceeding the requirements or expectations; more than fulfilled. | The company overfulfilled its sales target for the quarter.  这家公司超额完成了季度销售目标。 | The factory overfulfilled its production quota. 这家工厂超额完成了生产配额。 | 近义词: exceeded, surpassed, overachieved | 反义词: underfulfilled, unfulfilled | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "fulfilled" (past participle of "fulfill," from Middle English fulfillen, from Old English fullfyllan, meaning "to fill full, complete").
