boomingly:/ˈbuːmɪŋli/| adv.|兴旺地，蓬勃地|In a booming or prosperous manner; with a loud, resonant sound.|The business was boomingly successful.  这家生意兴旺发达。|近义词：prosperously, thrivingly, flourishingly, successfully|反义词:failingly, poorly, weakly|词性:副词

Etymology:  From "boom" (v.) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Boom" itself originates from a Middle Dutch word "bōm" referring to a beam or tree, later evolving to describe a loud, resonant sound and then extended metaphorically to economic growth.
