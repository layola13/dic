basaltic:/bəˈsɔːltɪk/| adj.|玄武岩的；似玄武岩的|Relating to, formed from, or containing basalt.  Made of basalt or resembling basalt.|例句：The island's coastline is characterized by dramatic basaltic cliffs.  这个岛屿的海岸线以壮观的玄武岩悬崖为特征。

例句：The geologists discovered a layer of basaltic rock beneath the sedimentary layers. 地质学家在沉积层下面发现了一层玄武岩。|近义词：volcanic, igneous|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French basaltique, from basalte (“basalt”) + -ique (“-ic”).  Basalte is from Late Latin basaltes, from Ancient Greek βασάλτης (basáltēs, “a very hard stone”), perhaps of Ethiopian origin.
