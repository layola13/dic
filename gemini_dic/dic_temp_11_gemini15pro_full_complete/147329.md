overmost:/ˈoʊvərˌmoʊst/| adj.|最上面的，最表面的|Situated or placed on the very top; outermost.|The overmost layer of rock was covered in moss. 最上面的一层岩石上覆盖着苔藓。|近义词：uppermost, topmost, outermost|反义词：bottommost, undermost|词性：形容词

Etymology: From Old English *ufermost (not attested), from ufer "over, above" + -most.  Cognate with Old High German ubarost.
