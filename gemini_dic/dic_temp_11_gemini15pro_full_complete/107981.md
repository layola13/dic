lashing:/ˈlæʃɪŋ/| n. | 鞭打，捆绑，责骂，大量 | a beating with a whip or something like a whip; the act of tying something firmly with rope or cord; a harsh scolding; a large amount | The sailor received a lashing for disobeying orders.  水手因不服从命令而受到鞭打。| synonyms: beating, flogging, whipping, tying, securing, scolding, berating, abundance, profusion | antonyms: praise, commendation, releasing, freeing, scarcity, dearth | noun

lashing:/ˈlæʃɪŋ/| adj. | 猛烈的，大量的 |  violent or intense; existing in large amounts |  A lashing wind and rain battered the coast. 猛烈的风雨袭击了海岸。| synonyms: torrential, driving, profuse, copious | antonyms: gentle, light, meager, scant | adjective
