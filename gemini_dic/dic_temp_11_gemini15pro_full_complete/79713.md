glucoside:/ˈɡluːkəsaɪd/ |n.| 葡糖苷; 糖苷 |A compound formed from a sugar and another organic compound by replacement of a hydroxyl group in the sugar molecule.  A glycoside in which the sugar component is glucose.|Amygdalin is a glucoside found in the kernels of apricots, peaches, and plums.  苦杏仁苷是一种存在于杏、桃和李子核中的葡糖苷。|Synonym: glycoside|Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists for a chemical compound like glucoside.)|noun
