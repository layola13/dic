bronchocele: /ˈbrɒŋkəˌsiːl/ (UK) /ˈbrɑŋkəˌsiːl/ (US)| n. | 甲状腺肿，支气管肿大 |  A swelling of the thyroid gland; goiter.  It refers to any enlargement of the thyroid gland, regardless of the cause. |  The doctor diagnosed a bronchocele after examining the patient's neck. 医生检查了病人的颈部后诊断为甲状腺肿。| Synonym: goiter, thyroid enlargement | Antonym: None (lack of swelling would be the opposite condition, but there isn't a specific single word) | noun
