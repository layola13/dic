franchiser:/ˈfræntʃaɪzər/| n.|特许人，授予特许权者|A franchiser grants a license to a franchisee to operate a business under the franchiser's brand and system.|McDonald's is a major franchiser with restaurants all over the world. 麦当劳是全球主要的授权商，在世界各地都有餐厅。|The franchiser provides training and support to its franchisees. 特许人向其特许经营者提供培训和支持。|Synonyms: licensor, grantor|Antonyms: franchisee, licensee|noun
