agenesis:/eɪˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
先天性缺失，发育不全
The complete failure of an organ or body part to develop during embryonic growth.
Renal agenesis is a condition in which a newborn is missing one or both kidneys.  肾脏发育不全是指新生儿缺少一个或两个肾脏的疾病。
The baby was born with agenesis of the corpus callosum. 这个婴儿出生时胼胝体发育不全。
aplasia, absence, nondevelopment
development, formation
noun
