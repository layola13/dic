harbourless: /ˈhɑːrbələs/ | adj. | 无港的，没有港口的 | Lacking a harbor or harbors; without a place of shelter or refuge. | The harbourless coast offered little protection from the storm.  无港的海岸几乎无法抵御风暴。| The shipwrecked sailors were stranded on a harbourless island.  失事的船员被困在一个没有港口的岛上。 | exposed, unsheltered | sheltered, protected | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From harbor + -less.  Harbor from Old English *hereberġ, from *herberġjan "to shelter," related to Old Saxon herberga "lodging, inn."  -less from Old English -lēas "free from, without."
