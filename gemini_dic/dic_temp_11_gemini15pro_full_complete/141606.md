/ɒpt/ /ɑːpt/ | v. |选择，挑选 | to make a choice from a range of possibilities |  She opted to study abroad. 她选择出国留学。

|近义词：choose, select, elect, pick |反义词：reject, decline, refuse |词性: 动词(verb)

/ˈɒpʃən/ /ˈɑːpʃən/ | n. |选择，选项；期权 | a thing that is or may be chosen | He had no option but to leave. 他别无选择，只能离开。

|近义词：choice, alternative, possibility |反义词：necessity, obligation |词性: 名词(noun)

/ˈɒpʃənəl/ /ˈɑːpʃənəl/ | adj. |可选择的，非强制的 | available to be chosen but not obligatory |  Attendance at the meeting is optional. 参加会议是可选的。

|近义词：elective, voluntary |反义词：compulsory, mandatory, required |词性: 形容词(adjective)

词源: From Latin *optiōn- (stem of optiō "choice, free will"), from optō "I choose, wish, desire"
