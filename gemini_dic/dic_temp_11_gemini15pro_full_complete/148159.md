overserious:/ˈoʊvərˌsɪriəs/| adj.|过于严肃的，过分认真的|Taking things too seriously; lacking a sense of humor or proportion.|He's a bit overserious about his work. 他对工作有点过于认真了。|近义词：too serious, solemn, grave, earnest, humorless|反义词：lighthearted, frivolous, flippant|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessive) + "serious" (grave, solemn).  The prefix "over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, across." "Serious" comes from Middle English "serius," from Old French "serieux," ultimately from Latin "serius," meaning "grave, weighty, important."
