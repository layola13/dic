fittednesses:/ˈfɪtɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
适合性，恰当性
The state or quality of being fitted or suitable.
The fittedness of the punishment to the crime was debated.  对罪行处罚的恰当性存在争议。
近义词:suitability, appropriateness, aptness
反义词:unsuitability, inappropriateness, inaptness
词性:noun
