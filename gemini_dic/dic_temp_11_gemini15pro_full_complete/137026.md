nonstatements:/ˈnɒnˌsteɪtmənts/
n.
非陈述句; 未声明; 不表态
Expressions that do not assert or deny anything.  They include questions, commands, and exclamations.
The lawyer argued that the defendant's utterances were nonstatements and therefore inadmissible as evidence.  被告的律师辩称，被告的言论并非陈述，因此不能作为证据。
Synonyms: nonassertions
Antonyms: statements, assertions
noun
