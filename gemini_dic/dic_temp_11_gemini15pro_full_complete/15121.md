bachelorlike:/ˈbætʃələrlaɪk/
adjective
像单身汉的; 适于单身汉的
Resembling or befitting a bachelor; unmarried.  Characteristic of a bachelor, especially in being untidy or neglectful of domestic comfort.
He lived a bachelorlike existence, with clothes strewn about and dishes piled in the sink. 他过着单身汉般的生活，衣服散落一地，碗碟堆在水槽里。
Synonyms: single, unmarried, unattached, independent
Antonyms: married, domesticated
词性: 形容词
