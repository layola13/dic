dambose:/dæmˈboʊs/ | n. | 糖醇; 达姆波糖; 菊芋糖 | A type of sugar alcohol, also known as inositol, found in some plants.  It is a crystalline, sweet, water-soluble alcohol. |  Dambose is found naturally in jackfruit.  达姆波糖天然存在于菠萝蜜中。|  Dambose can be used as a sweetener. 达姆波糖可以用作甜味剂。 | synonyms: inositol, cyclohexanehexol | antonyms: None | noun
