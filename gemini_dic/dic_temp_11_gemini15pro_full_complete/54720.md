disoxygenate:/diːˈɒksɪdʒəneɪt/
verb
脱氧；除去氧
To remove oxygen from something.
The blood is disoxygenated as it passes through the capillaries. 血液在流经毛细血管时被脱氧。
The process disoxygenates the water. 这个过程除去了水中的氧气。
deoxygenate, deoxydize
oxygenate
v.
Etymology:  dis- + oxygen + -ate
