omnidirectionality:/ˌɒmnɪˌdaɪˌrɛkʃəˈnælɪti/ | n. | 全向性，全方位性 | The ability to receive or transmit signals in all directions. | This microphone boasts omnidirectionality, making it ideal for recording group conversations.  这个麦克风以全向性而自豪，非常适合录制群组对话。 | synonyms: isotropy, spherical symmetry | antonyms: directionality, unidirectionality | noun
