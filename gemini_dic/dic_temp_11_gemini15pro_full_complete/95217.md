inbounds:/ˈɪnbaʊndz/ | adj. | 界内的; 有效的; 入站的 | Within the established boundaries or limits; legally playable; arriving | The ball was declared inbounds.  这个球被判界内。| Synonyms: inside, within, legal, incoming | Antonyms: out of bounds, outside, illegal, outgoing | adjective

inbounds:/ˈɪnbaʊndz/ | adv. | 向内; 入站地 | Towards the inside; in an incoming direction | The plane was flying inbounds. 飞机正朝着入站方向飞行。| Synonyms: inwards, inbound | Antonyms: outwards, outbound | adverb
