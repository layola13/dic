meditativeness:/ˌmedɪˈteɪtɪvnəs/
n.
冥想; 沉思; 默想
The state of being meditative; given to meditation.
She exuded an aura of meditativeness. 她散发出一种沉思的气氛。
近义词: contemplation, thoughtfulness, pensiveness
反义词: restlessness, distraction, agitation
词性: noun

Etymology: From meditate + -ive + -ness.  "meditate" comes from Late Latin *meditatus, past participle of meditari "to ponder," frequentative of meditari "to think, consider, reflect," from PIE root *med- "to take appropriate measures."
