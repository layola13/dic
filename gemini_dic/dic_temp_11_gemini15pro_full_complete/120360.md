minimizes:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪz/
|v.|最小化，使减到最少|Reduces something to the smallest possible amount or degree. |The company minimizes its costs by outsourcing production.  公司通过外包生产来最大限度地降低成本。|synonyms: reduces, decreases, lessens|antonyms: maximizes, increases, amplifies|verb (third-person singular simple present minimizes, present participle minimizing, simple past minimized, past participle minimized)

minimizes:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪz/
|v.|轻描淡写，淡化|Represents something as less significant than it is. |He minimized the importance of his contribution to the project. 他淡化了他对这个项目的贡献的重要性。|synonyms: downplays, understates, trivializes|antonyms: exaggerates, emphasizes, overstates|verb (third-person singular simple present minimizes, present participle minimizing, simple past minimized, past participle minimized)
