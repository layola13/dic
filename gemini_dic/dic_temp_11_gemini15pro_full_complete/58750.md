dungier:/ˈdʌndʒɪər/
adjective
更脏的，更像地牢的
more like a dungeon; darker, dirtier, and more unpleasant
The catacombs were far dungier than I expected.  这些地下墓穴比我想象的要脏得多。
近义词:dirtier, grimmer, filthier
反义词:cleaner, brighter, more pleasant
词性:adjective

词源:
From dungeon + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *dominionem, "lordship, property."
