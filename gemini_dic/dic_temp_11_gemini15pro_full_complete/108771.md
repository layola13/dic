leasehold:/ˈliːshəʊld/ (UK) /ˈliːshoʊld/ (US)| n. adj.|租赁产权，租赁期限|The ownership of a temporary right to hold land or property in which a lessee holds real property by some form of title from a lessor (or landlord) for a defined period.  It is essentially a long-term rental agreement.|例句：They own the leasehold on the property. 他们在该房产上拥有租赁产权。|近义词：tenancy, lease|反义词：freehold|词性:名词, 形容词

leasehold:/ˈliːshəʊld/ (UK) /ˈliːshoʊld/ (US)| adj.|租赁的|Held or occupied under a lease.|例句：We live in a leasehold apartment. 我们住在一套租赁公寓里。|近义词：rented, leased|反义词：freehold|词性:形容词
