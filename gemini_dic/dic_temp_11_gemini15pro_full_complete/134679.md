nonmarketable:/ˌnɒnˈmɑːkɪtəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɑːrkɪtəbl/| adj.|无销路的，滞销的；不值钱的|Not easily sold or not bringing a good price; difficult or impossible to market.|The property was deemed nonmarketable due to its dilapidated condition. 这处房产因其破败不堪的状况而被认为是无法出售的。|Synonyms: unsalable, unsellable, not marketable|Antonyms: marketable, salable, sellable|adjective

nonmarketable:/ˌnɒnˈmɑːkɪtəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɑːrkɪtəbl/| n.|滞销品|A product that is difficult or impossible to market.|The company had a warehouse full of nonmarketables.  这家公司仓库里堆满了滞销品。|Synonyms: unsalable goods, dead stock|Antonyms: marketable goods, bestsellers|noun
