invasiveness:/ɪnˈveɪsɪvnəs/ | n. | 侵略性，侵袭性 |The invasiveness of the cancer cells is a major concern. 癌细胞的侵袭性是一个主要问题。| The invasiveness of the new species threatens the native ecosystem.  这个新物种的入侵性威胁着本地生态系统。| 近义词：aggressiveness, intrusiveness | 反义词：passivity, harmlessness | 词性: noun

Etymology: From invasive + -ness.  "Invasive" comes from Late Latin *invāsivus, from invādere "to go into, enter, invade," from in- "into, in, on, upon" + vādere "to go, walk."
