outskirmishing:/ˌaʊtˈskɜːrmɪʃɪŋ/
n.
小规模战斗，前哨战
minor fighting or conflict, especially between small groups
The army engaged in some outskirmishing before the main battle.  军队在主要战役前进行了一些小规模战斗。

近义词:skirmishing, clashing, sparring
反义词:negotiating, compromising

词性:名词(noun)

词源:From out- + skirmish.  由out- + skirmish组成。
