gleams:/ɡliːmz/| n. v.|微光，闪光；闪烁，闪耀|A gleam is a faint or brief light, or a brief or slight manifestation of something (like a feeling or quality).|The sun gleamed on the water.  阳光在水面上闪耀。|近义词：glimmer, shimmer, flash, shine, sparkle|反义词：dullness, darkness|词性：名词复数；动词第三人称单数现在时

gleam:/ɡliːm/| n. v.|微光，闪光；闪烁，闪耀|A gleam is a faint or brief light, or a brief or slight manifestation of something (like a feeling or quality).|A gleam of hope appeared in his eyes. 他眼中露出一丝希望的光芒。|近义词：glimmer, shimmer, flash, shine, sparkle|反义词：dullness, darkness|词性：名词；动词单数

Etymology: From Middle English *glemen, from Old Norse gleima (“to gleam”), from Proto-Germanic *glaimiz (“gleaming, bright”). Related to Old English glǣm (“gleam”).
