branchiogastropoda:/ˌbræŋkiəʊɡæˈstrɒpədə/| n. |鳃腹足纲|A taxonomic class of mollusks, typically small snails, that breathe using gills located in a cavity formed by the mantle.  They are often found in freshwater habitats.|例句：Branchiogastropoda include many species of freshwater snails. 鳃腹足纲包括许多种类的淡水蜗牛。

例句：Research on Branchiogastropoda contributes to our understanding of freshwater ecosystems. 对鳃腹足纲的研究有助于我们理解淡水生态系统。|近义词：water snails (general term), freshwater gastropods|反义词: (None, as it's a taxonomic classification) |词性:noun
