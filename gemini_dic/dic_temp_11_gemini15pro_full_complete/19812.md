bimble:/ˈbɪmbəl/| vi. n.|漫步，闲逛；漫步，闲逛|To walk or travel leisurely, especially in the countryside, for pleasure.|We bimbled along the country lanes. 我们沿着乡间小路漫步。|近义词：stroll, amble, wander, meander|反义词：rush, hurry, dash|词性：动词, 名词
词源：Probably from a dialectal word, perhaps related to bumble.
