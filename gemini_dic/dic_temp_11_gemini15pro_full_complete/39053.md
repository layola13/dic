conformably:/kənˈfɔːməbli/
adv.
一致地，顺应地
in a way that follows the rules or customs of something; in agreement with something
The procedure was carried out conformably with the regulations.  这个程序是按照规章执行的。
The building has been altered conformably with modern building standards. 这座建筑已经按照现代建筑标准进行了改造。
近义词: accordingly, compatibly, consistently, appropriately
反义词: unconformably, incompatibly, inconsistently, inappropriately
词性: 副词
