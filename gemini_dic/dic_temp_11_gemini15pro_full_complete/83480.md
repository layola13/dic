hackable:/ˈhækəbl/| adj.|可入侵的，可被攻击的|Susceptible to being hacked or compromised, typically referring to computer systems or software.|This old software is notoriously hackable.  这个旧软件出了名的容易被攻击。|The company's website proved hackable due to a security flaw. 由于一个安全漏洞，该公司的网站被证明是可入侵的。|近义词：vulnerable, exploitable|反义词：secure, impenetrable|词性:形容词
