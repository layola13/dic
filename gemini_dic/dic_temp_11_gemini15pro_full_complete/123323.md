monographical:/ˌmɒnəˈɡræfɪkəl/ | adj. | 专著的；专题研究的 | Relating to a monograph; pertaining to a detailed written study of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it. |  This monographical study provides a comprehensive overview of the artist's early work. 这部专题研究著作全面概述了这位艺术家的早期作品。

| Example: The professor assigned a monographical paper on the impact of social media on political discourse. 教授布置了一篇关于社交媒体对政治话语影响的专题论文。 | Near Synonyms: specialized, in-depth, detailed, exhaustive, comprehensive | Antonyms: general, superficial, cursory, brief | Word type: Adjective
