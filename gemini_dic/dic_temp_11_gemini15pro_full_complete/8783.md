antieducational:/ˌæntiˌedʒuˈkeɪʃənl/
adjective
反教育的
hindering or opposing education; not conducive to education.
That policy is antieducational and will harm students in the long run.  这项政策是反教育的，从长远来看会损害学生。
The censorship of books in schools is an antieducational practice. 在学校审查书籍是一种反教育的做法。
近义词: counter-educational, uneducational
反义词: educational, pro-educational
词性: adjective
