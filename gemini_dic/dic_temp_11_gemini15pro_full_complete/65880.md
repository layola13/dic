evolutionisms: /ˌiːvəˈluːʃənɪzəmz/ /ˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪzəmz/
noun
进化论
Theories of evolution; specifically, a particular theory or system of theories concerning evolution.
Social Darwinism is one of the more controversial evolutionisms.  社会达尔文主义是更有争议的进化论之一。
Theories about how organisms change over time are called evolutionisms.  关于生物体如何随着时间变化的理论被称为进化论。
synonyms: evolutionary theories, Darwinism (in a broad sense)
antonyms: creationism, intelligent design
