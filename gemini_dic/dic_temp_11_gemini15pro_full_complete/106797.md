labiated: /ˈleɪbiˌeɪtɪd/
labiated: adj.
labiated: 唇形的，有唇瓣的
labiated: Having lips or lip-like parts; specifically, in botany, having the corolla or calyx divided into two unequal parts or lips, one projecting over the other.
labiated: The labiated flowers of the mint family are easily recognizable.  唇形科植物的唇形花很容易辨认。
labiated: The orchid has a distinctly labiated flower.  这朵兰花有一朵明显的唇形花。
labiated: lip-shaped, bilabiate
labiated: 


Etymology:
From Latin *labiatus, from labium (“lip”) + -atus (past participle suffix).
