cultivate: /ˈkʌltɪveɪt/ (US) /ˈkʌltɪveɪt/ (UK)
verb
耕种,培养,陶冶,建立(关系)
To prepare and use (land) for crops or gardening. To try to acquire or develop (a quality, sentiment, or skill).
He cultivated a small plot of land behind his house. 他在房子后面耕种了一小块地。
She has cultivated a taste for fine wines. 她培养了对优质葡萄酒的品味。
近义词: grow, nurture, develop, foster, refine
反义词: neglect, abandon, ignore
词性: verb
