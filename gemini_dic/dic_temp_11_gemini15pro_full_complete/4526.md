albums:/ˈælbəmz/ (US), /ˈælbəmz/ (UK)| n.|相册；专辑|A collection of photographs, stamps, or other items, often placed in a book; a collection of recordings issued as a single item on CD, vinyl, etc.|She showed us her wedding albums. 她给我们看了她的婚礼相册。|The band released their debut album in 1998.  这支乐队于1998年发行了他们的首张专辑。|synonyms: collections, compilations, records|antonyms: singles, individual items|noun
