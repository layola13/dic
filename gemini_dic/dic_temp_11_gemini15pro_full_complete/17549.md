beatify: /biˈætɪfaɪ/ /bɪˈætɪfaɪ/
| v. |使幸福；使快乐；宣布…列入真福品；美化|To make supremely happy; to bless; to ascribe a virtue to; to declare (a deceased person) to be among the blessed.|The birth of her child beatified her. 她孩子的出生让她无比幸福。|Synonyms: bless, exalt, glorify, sanctify |Antonyms: condemn, curse, damn, vilify
|Verb|


beatifying: /biˈætɪfaɪɪŋ/ /bɪˈætɪfaɪɪŋ/
| adj. |祝福的；使人幸福的|Making blessed or supremely happy; blissful.|She had a beatifying smile. 她拥有一个令人幸福的微笑。|Synonyms: blissful, blessed, rapturous, joyous |Antonyms: wretched, miserable, unhappy, sorrowful
|Adjective|
