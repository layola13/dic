maistress:/ˈmeɪstrɪs/| n. |女主人，女教师，情妇|A woman who has control or authority over someone or something, especially a woman who employs servants or teaches children. A woman with whom a man has a continuing sexual relationship outside marriage.|例句：The maistress of the house gave orders to the servants.  这家的女主人给仆人们下达了命令。

例句：He left his wife for his maistress. 他为了他的情妇离开了他的妻子。|近义词：mistress, landlady, governess, lover|反义词:servant, employee|词性:noun
