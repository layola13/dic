dephlogisticate:/diːflɒˈdʒɪstɪkeɪt/
verb
去除燃素; 使去燃素
To deprive of phlogiston (a hypothetical substance formerly believed to be present in all combustible materials and to be released during burning); to remove inflammation.
Priestley dephlogisticated air by heating mercuric oxide with sunlight and concentrating the resultant gas. 普里斯特利通过用阳光加热氧化汞并浓缩产生的气体来去除空气的燃素。
近义词:oxidize, burn
反义词:reduce
词性:v.
