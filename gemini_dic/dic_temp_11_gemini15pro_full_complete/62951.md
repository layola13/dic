enneandrous:/ɛnˈiːændrəs/
adjective
具九雄蕊的；九雄蕊的
Having nine stamens.
The enneandrous flower has nine stamens.  这种九雄蕊的花有九个雄蕊。
Near Synonyms: none specifically for stamen count.  General synonyms for flower structure description might include: "polyandrous" (having many stamens), "oligandrous" (having few stamens).
Antonyms: none specifically for stamen count.
词性: adjective
