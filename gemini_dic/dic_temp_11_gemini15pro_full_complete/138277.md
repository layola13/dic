notarially:/noʊˈtɛriəli/
adverb
以公证人的方式;经公证地
In a manner performed by, or requiring the involvement of, a notary public.
The document was notarially certified.  这份文件经过公证认证。
The signature was notarially witnessed. 签名由公证人见证。
Synonym: officially, legally
Antonym: unofficially, illegally
词性: 副词
