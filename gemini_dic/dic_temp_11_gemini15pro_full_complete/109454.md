lepidodendrid: /ˌlɛpɪdoʊˈdɛndrɪd/ | n. adj. |  鳞木；鳞木类的 | An extinct tree-like plant of the Carboniferous period, having a tall, thick trunk and scale-like leaf scars.  |  Example:  The fossilized lepidodendrid revealed much about the ancient swamp ecosystem. 这块鳞木化石揭示了古代沼泽生态系统的许多信息。

Example: Lepidodendrid forests were widespread during the Carboniferous period. 石炭纪时期，鳞木森林广泛分布。| 近义词: scale tree, lycopod | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *lepis*, *lepidos* "scale" + *dendron* "tree".
