formulated:/ˈfɔːrmjəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 配制的，阐述的 |  Created or devised methodically, especially as a formula or set of rules; expressed in a systematic or definite way. | The chemist formulated a new type of detergent.  化学家配制了一种新型洗涤剂。 |  developed, devised, articulated, expressed, specified | improvised, unplanned, unformed, vague | adjective

formulate:/ˈfɔːrmjəleɪt/ | v. | 阐述；制定；构想 | Create or devise methodically (a strategy or a proposal). Express (an idea or theory) in a clear or definite form. |  The committee formulated a new plan. 委员会制定了一个新计划。| devise, conceive, frame, develop, articulate | neglect, disregard | verb
