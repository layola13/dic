cyst:/sɪst/| n.|囊肿，胞囊|A cyst is a closed sac-like pocket of tissue that contains fluid, air, or other substances.  It can grow almost anywhere in your body or under your skin.|The doctor found a cyst on her ovary. 医生在她的卵巢上发现了一个囊肿。|近义词：sac, lump, growth|反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical abnormality like a cyst)|词性:noun

cyst:/sɪst/| n.|包囊，孢囊|(Biology) A resting or dormant stage of a microorganism, usually a bacterium or a protist, that helps the organism to survive in unfavorable environmental conditions.|The parasite exists as a cyst in contaminated water.  这种寄生虫以包囊的形式存在于受污染的水中。|近义词：spore, capsule|反义词: (No direct antonym for a biological structure like a cyst)|词性:noun
