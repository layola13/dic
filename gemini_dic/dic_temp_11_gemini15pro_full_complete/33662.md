cinerary:/ˈsɪnəˌrɛri/| adj.|骨灰的；用于盛放骨灰的；与火葬有关的|Of or relating to ashes, especially the ashes of the dead; used for or containing such ashes.|The museum has a large collection of cinerary urns from ancient Rome.  博物馆收藏了大量来自古罗马的骨灰瓮。|Synonyms:  funereal, sepulchral|Antonyms:  living, vital|adjective

cinerary:/ˈsɪnəˌrɛri/| n.|骨灰盒；骨灰瓮|A container for ashes, especially the ashes of the dead.|Her cinerary urn was placed on the mantelpiece. 她的骨灰瓮被放置在壁炉架上。|Synonyms: urn, ossuary|Antonyms:  N/A|noun
