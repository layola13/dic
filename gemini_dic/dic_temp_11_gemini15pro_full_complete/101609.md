irreconcilabilities:/ɪˌrɛkənˌsaɪləˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 不可调和性；矛盾；冲突 | The state of being impossible to reconcile; incompatibility.  |  The irreconcilabilities between the two groups led to the breakdown of negotiations.  这两个团体之间的不可调和性导致了谈判的破裂。 | Synonyms:  incompatibilities, conflicts, clashes, discords, differences, variances | Antonyms: compatibilities, harmonies, agreements, accords | noun

Etymology: From Late Middle English *irreconsilabilite, from Late Latin irreconciliabilitas, from irreconciliabilis (“irreconcilable”) + -itas (“-ity”).
