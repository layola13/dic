cityful:/ˈsɪtiˌfʊl/
n.|充满城市气息的；都市化的|A cityful is a large number of people or things associated with a city.  It emphasizes the quantity as if it could fill a city.  It's a less common word, often used poetically or figuratively.|A cityful of tourists descended upon the small town.  大批游客涌入小镇。|Synonym: urban, metropolitan|Antonym: rural, rustic|Noun
