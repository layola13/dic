cotenant:/ˈkoʊˌtɛnənt/| n.|共同租户，合租人|A cotenant is a person who owns property in conjunction with another person or persons.|Example: The cotenants decided to sell the property and split the profits. 合租人决定出售房产并平分利润。

Example:  As cotenants, they were equally responsible for the mortgage payments. 作为共同租户，他们对抵押贷款的支付负有同等责任。|近义词：joint tenant, co-owner, co-lessee|反义词:sole owner, sole proprietor|词性:noun
