malignancy:/məˈlɪɡnənsi/| n.|恶性肿瘤；恶意；恶毒|The doctor confirmed the malignancy of the tumor. 医生确认了肿瘤的恶性。|近义词:cancer, tumor, malevolence, viciousness, spite|反义词:benignancy, kindness|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English malignite, from Old French malignité, from Latin malignitās ("malignity, wickedness, badness"), from malignus ("ill-disposed, wicked, bad"), from male ("badly, ill") + -gnus (akin to genus "birth, race, kind").
