controversial:/ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃl/ | /ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃl/ | adj. | 有争议的；引起争论的 | Giving rise or likely to give rise to public disagreement. | The judge made a controversial decision.  法官做出了一个有争议的决定。 |  The politician's controversial remarks sparked a heated debate. 这位政治家的争议言论引发了激烈的辩论。 | 近义词: debatable, disputed, contentious | 反义词: undisputed, uncontroversial | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *controversia, from Latin controversus (“turned against, disputed”) (from contra (“against”) + versus, past participle of vertere (“to turn”)).
