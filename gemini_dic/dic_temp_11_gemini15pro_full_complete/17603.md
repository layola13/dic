beautify:/ˈbjuːtɪfaɪ/
|vt.|美化；使变美|To make something more beautiful.|例句：Flowers beautify the environment. 鲜花美化环境。|例句：The artist beautified the room with her paintings.  艺术家用她的画美化了房间。|近义词：adorn, embellish, enhance, decorate|反义词：uglify, spoil, mar|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *beautifer*, from beau ("beautiful") + -fier (from Latin -ficare). Ultimately from Latin bellus (“beautiful, handsome, agreeable”).
