continents:/ˈkɒntɪnənts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntɪnənts/ (US)| n.|大陆，洲|Large landmasses on Earth, traditionally considered to be seven in number.|例句：Asia and Africa are two of the world's largest continents.  亚洲和非洲是世界上最大的两个洲。|近义词：landmasses|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French continent, or from Latin continens, continent- ‘holding together, continuous’, present participle of continere (from con- ‘together’ + tenere ‘to hold’).  The noun use (originally meaning ‘continuous tract of land’) arose by ellipsis from terra continens ‘continuous land’.
