bullheaded:/ˈbʊlˌhɛdɪd/| adj.|顽固的，固执的|Resolutely stubborn; obstinate; headstrong.  Like a bull refusing to move.|He was so bullheaded that he refused to listen to anyone's advice. 他太固执了，不肯听任何人的劝告。|近义词: stubborn, obstinate, headstrong, pigheaded, inflexible, unyielding|反义词: compliant, amenable, docile, flexible, open-minded|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "bull" (the animal) + "headed," referring to the stereotypical image of a bull stubbornly refusing to move.
