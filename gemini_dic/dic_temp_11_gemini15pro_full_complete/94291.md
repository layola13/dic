impertinently:/ɪmˈpɜːrtnəntli/ /ɪmˈpɜːrtɪnəntli/
adjective
无礼地，鲁莽地
Not showing proper respect; rude.
She impertinently interrupted the speaker. 她无礼地打断了演讲者。
近义词:rudely, insolently, disrespectfully, impudently
反义词:politely, respectfully, courteously
词性:adverb

impertinent:/ɪmˈpɜːrtnənt/ /ɪmˈpɜːrtɪnənt/
adjective
无礼的，鲁莽的
Not showing proper respect; rude.
