alcedo:/ælˈsiːdoʊ/
noun
翠鸟
A brightly colored bird of the family Alcedidae, found near water and known for its swift dives to catch fish.
The alcedo plunged into the river, emerging moments later with a small fish in its beak.  翠鸟一头扎进河里，片刻之后嘴叼着一条小鱼浮出水面。
近义词:kingfisher
反义词:(No direct antonym for a specific bird species)
词性:n.
