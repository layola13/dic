nonequitable:/ˌnɒnˈɛkwɪtəbl/
adjective
不公平的，不公正的
Not fair or just; inequitable.
The nonequitable distribution of resources led to widespread discontent. 资源分配不公导致普遍不满。
The judge deemed the sentence nonequitable and overturned it. 法官认为判决不公正，将其推翻。
近义词: unfair, unjust, biased, discriminatory
反义词: fair, just, equitable, impartial
词性: adjective
