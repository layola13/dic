noveldom:/ˈnɒvəldəm/ (US) /ˈnɒvəldəm/ (UK)| n. |小说界; 小说领域|The state or realm of novels; the world of novelists and novel writing.  It refers to the collective body of novels, their authors, readers, and the culture surrounding them.| Her latest book has cemented her place in noveldom.  她的最新著作巩固了她在小说界的地位。| 近义词：literary world, world of fiction, literary scene| 反义词:non-fiction, reality| 词性:noun
