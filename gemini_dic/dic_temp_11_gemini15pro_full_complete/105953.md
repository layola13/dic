kludge:/klʌdʒ/
noun
临时凑合的方案；权宜之计
a clumsy or inelegant, yet effective, solution to a problem.
This software is a real kludge, but it gets the job done. 这个软件真是个权宜之计，但它能完成工作。
synonyms: workaround, makeshift, improvisation, hack
antonyms: elegant solution, streamlined solution
n.

kludge:/klʌdʒ/
verb
粗制滥造；临时凑合
to make or repair something in a clumsy or makeshift way.
I had to kludge together a solution with the parts I had on hand. 我不得不把我手头的零件拼凑在一起，凑合出一个解决方案。
synonyms: patch up, cobble together, jury-rig
antonyms:  craft, meticulously build
v.
