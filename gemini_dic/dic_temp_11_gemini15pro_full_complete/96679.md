indwelling:/ɪnˈdwɛlɪŋ/
adjective
内在的，固有的；居住的；留存的
Existing permanently within something or someone.
例句：
An indwelling catheter.
留置导尿管。

The indwelling spirit of God.
内在的上帝之灵。

近义词：inherent, innate, intrinsic, resident, abiding
反义词：external, extrinsic, transient
词性：adjective
