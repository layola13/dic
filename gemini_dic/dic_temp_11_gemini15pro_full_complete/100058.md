interpoint:/ˈɪntərˌpɔɪnt/| n.|中间点；区间点；分隔点|A point located between two other points, especially in a range or sequence.|The interpoint between the two cities is a small town. 这两个城市之间的中间点是一个小镇。|Synonyms: midpoint, halfway point|Antonyms: endpoint, extremity|noun

interpoint:/ˈɪntərˌpɔɪnt/| n.|（印刷）字距；字母间距|The space between letters or characters in printed or displayed text.|Adjusting the interpoint can improve the readability of the text. 调整字距可以提高文本的可读性。|Synonyms: kerning, letter-spacing|Antonyms: N/A|noun
