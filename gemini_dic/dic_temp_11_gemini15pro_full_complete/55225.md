disshiver:/dɪˈʃɪvər/| v. |使颤抖，使战栗；使瓦解，使分裂|To cause to tremble or shake; to break apart or disintegrate.|The cold wind disshivered him. 寒冷的风使他颤抖。 The empire began to disshiver after the king's death. 国王死后，帝国开始瓦解。|近义词：tremble, shiver, shatter, disintegrate|反义词：stabilize, unite|词性：动词

词源:
From dis- + shiver, likely influenced by words like dissolve.  "Dis-" prefix often indicates separation or reversal. "Shiver" refers to trembling, usually from cold or fear.
