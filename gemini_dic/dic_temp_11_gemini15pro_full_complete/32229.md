chichling:/ˈtʃɪklɪŋ/| n.|小鸡；雏鸡；（尤指）童子鸡；乳臭未干的小子；年轻而无经验的人|A young chicken, especially one suitable for roasting.  A young and inexperienced person, especially a young woman.|例句：The chichling was barely old enough to leave the coop. 这只小鸡还 barely够大到可以离开鸡舍。

例句：He was just a chichling when he started working here. 他刚开始在这里工作时，还只是个乳臭未干的小子。|近义词：chick, youngster, fledgling, newbie, rookie|反义词: adult, veteran, expert |词性: 名词

词源:
From Middle English *chikeling, equivalent to chick +‎ -ling.
