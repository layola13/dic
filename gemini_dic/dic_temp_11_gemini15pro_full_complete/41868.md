coscript:/ˈkəʊskrɪpt/ | n. | 共本；合著者；共同撰稿人 |A person who writes something jointly with another or others. | Example:  She and her brother were coscripts on the play. 她和她哥哥是这出戏的合著者。

Example: He was a coscript of the screenplay for the movie. 他是这部电影剧本的合著者之一。 | 近义词: co-author, collaborator, joint author | 反义词: ghostwriter (代笔人, 程度上是反义词) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "co-" (together) + "script" (writing). "Co-" comes from the Latin "com-" (with, together). "Script" comes from the Latin "scriptum" (something written), past participle of "scribere" (to write).
