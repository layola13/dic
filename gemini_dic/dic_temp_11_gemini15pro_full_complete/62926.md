enmesh:/ɪnˈmɛʃ/ | v. | 使陷入困境，使纠缠 | To involve someone or something in a complicated or unpleasant situation that it is difficult to escape from. |  例句：The whales were enmeshed in the fishing nets. 鲸鱼被渔网缠住了。

例句：He became enmeshed in a web of lies. 他陷入了一张谎言之网。 | 近义词：entangle, involve, trap | 反义词：extricate, disentangle, free | 词性：动词
