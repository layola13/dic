orchel:/ˈɔːrkəl/ | n. | 兰花 | A flowering plant with brightly coloured, unusually shaped flowers. Orchids are prized for their beauty and variety. |  Example 1: She received a beautiful orchid for her birthday. 她生日收到了一株美丽的兰花。

Example 2:  He cultivates rare orchids in his greenhouse. 他在温室里培育珍稀兰花。 | Synonyms:  flower, bloom, blossom | Antonyms: weed | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *orchelle, from Medieval Latin orchidella, diminutive of Latin orchis (“orchid”), from Ancient Greek ὄρχις (órkhis, “testicle”), from its root tubers resembling testicles.
