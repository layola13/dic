epoch's:/ˈiːpɒks/ | n. | 时代的，纪元的 |  Belonging to or characteristic of a particular epoch.  |  The epoch's defining moment was the invention of the printing press.  这个时代的决定性时刻是印刷机的发明。 | synonyms: era's, age's, period's | antonyms: modern, contemporary | possessive noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *epoque* (modern French *époque*), from Late Latin *epoca*, from Ancient Greek *ἐποχή (epokhḗ)*, meaning "pause, fixed point of time."
