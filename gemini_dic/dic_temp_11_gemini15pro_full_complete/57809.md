dreint:/dreɪnt/
adjective
耗尽的，筋疲力尽的
Drained of energy or vitality; exhausted.
After a long day of work, I felt completely dreint.  工作了一整天后，我感到筋疲力尽。
The marathon left the runners dreint and dehydrated. 马拉松比赛让选手们筋疲力尽，脱水严重。

近义词:exhausted, spent, depleted, fatigued
反义词:energetic, invigorated, refreshed


词性:adjective

词源:From Middle English *dreynten, from Old Norse *dreynta (“to drown, sink”), from Proto-Germanic *dranwijaną (“to make turbid, muddy; to drown”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrewh₁- (“to become muddy, turbid”). Cognate with Icelandic drekkja (“to drown”), Danish drukne (“to drown”), Swedish drunkna (“to drown”). More at drown.  Dreint likely developed as a figurative extension of the sense of being "drowned" or overwhelmed by exhaustion.
