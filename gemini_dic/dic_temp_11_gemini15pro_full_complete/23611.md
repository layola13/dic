branch-like:/ˈbrɑːntʃˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 枝状的，像树枝的 | Resembling a branch in form or appearance. | The lightning bolt was branch-like, spreading across the sky. 闪电像树枝一样，在天空中蔓延。| The coral had a delicate, branch-like structure. 珊瑚有着精致的枝状结构。 | 近义词: branched, dendritic, ramified | 反义词: unbranched, straight | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From "branch" + "-like". "Branch" comes from Old French "branche", ultimately from Vulgar Latin *branca, of uncertain origin. "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling," from Old English "-lic."
