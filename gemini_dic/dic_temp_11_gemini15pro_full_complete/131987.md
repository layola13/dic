n.
未转让，未输送，不搬运
The nonconveyance of the property led to a legal dispute.  该房产的未转让导致了一场法律纠纷。
The nonconveyance of the goods resulted in a loss for the company. 货物的未输送导致了公司的损失。
近义词:nondelivery, nontransfer, withholding
反义词:conveyance, transfer, delivery

nonconveyance:/ˌnɒnkənˈveɪəns/
n.
未转让，未输送，不搬运
Failure of conveyance; a state of not being conveyed.
例句：The nonconveyance of the deed caused problems for the buyer. 契约的未转让给买方带来了麻烦。
The nonconveyance of the mineral rights led to a lengthy lawsuit. 矿产权的未转让导致了一场旷日持久的诉讼。
近义词:non-transmission, non-transfer
反义词:conveyance, transmission, transfer
词性:名词


词源:
From non- + conveyance.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "conveyance" comes from the Old French word "conveiance," meaning "the act of carrying or transporting."
