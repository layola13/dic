goldcrest:/ˈɡoʊldˌkrɛst/ | n. | 金冠戴菊 | A very small European bird (Regulus regulus) with a bright yellow stripe on its head, the male having an orange centre to this stripe. | Example 1:  I spotted a goldcrest flitting amongst the branches of the fir tree. 我看到一只金冠戴菊在冷杉树枝间飞来飞去。

Example 2: The goldcrest is the smallest bird in Europe. 金冠戴菊是欧洲最小的鸟。 | 近义词: firecrest, kinglet | 反义词:  N/A (no opposite for a species of bird) | 词性: noun

firecrest:/ˈfaɪərkrɛst/ | n. | 火冠戴菊 | A small European bird (Regulus ignicapilla) closely related to the goldcrest, having a similar yellow stripe on the head but with a more prominent orange-red centre. | Example 1: The firecrest's song is high-pitched and difficult to hear. 火冠戴菊的歌声很高亢，很难听到。

Example 2:  I observed a firecrest catching insects in the foliage. 我观察到一只火冠戴菊在树叶中捉昆虫。| 近义词: goldcrest, kinglet | 反义词:  N/A (no opposite for a species of bird) | 词性: noun

kinglet:/ˈkɪŋlɪt/ | n. | 戴菊鸟 | Any of various very small songbirds of the genus Regulus, including the goldcrest and firecrest, typically having a brightly coloured crown patch. | Example 1:  Kinglets are known for their tiny size and active movements. 戴菊鸟以其体型小巧和活跃的活动而闻名。
