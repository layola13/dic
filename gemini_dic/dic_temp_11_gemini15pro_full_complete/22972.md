button: /ˈbʌtn/ (US) /ˈbʌtən/ (UK)| n. v. | 按钮，纽扣; 扣住 |A small, usually round, flat object that is sewn onto clothing and used for fastening two parts together. Also, a similar object that is pressed to operate a device. | She sewed a button onto her coat. 她把一颗纽扣缝在外套上。

He pressed the button to start the machine. 他按下按钮启动机器。 |近义词：switch, knob, fastener (n.); fasten, secure (v.) |反义词: unbutton, undo, detach (v.) |词性: noun, verb
