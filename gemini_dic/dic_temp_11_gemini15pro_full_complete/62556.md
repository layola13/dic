energetically:/ˌenərˈdʒetɪkli/
adverb
精力充沛地；积极地
With great energy and enthusiasm.
She danced energetically throughout the night. 她整晚都精力充沛地跳舞。
近义词:vigorously, actively, powerfully, dynamically, enthusiastically
反义词:lethargically, lazily, passively, listlessly, apathetically
词性:adverb

词源:
From energy + -ic + -al + -ly. Ultimately from the Greek word *enérgeia* meaning "activity".
