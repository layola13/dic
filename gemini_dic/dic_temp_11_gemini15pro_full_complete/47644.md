debituminization:/diːbɪˌtjuːmɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 脱沥青作用；脱沥青化 |The process of removing bitumen or asphalt from a substance, typically oil sands or shale. | The debituminization process separates the bitumen from the sand and other materials. 脱沥青过程将沥青与沙子和其他物质分离。|  The efficiency of debituminization is crucial for oil sands processing. 脱沥青效率对于油砂加工至关重要。|  de-asphalting, bitumen removal | asphaltation, bituminization | noun
