glow-worm:/ˈɡloʊwɜːrm/| n. |萤火虫|A beetle whose larva and wingless female emit light.|例句：I saw a glow-worm glimmering in the forest. 我看到一只萤火虫在森林里闪烁。

例句：The glow-worms lit up the night like tiny stars. 萤火虫像一颗颗小星星一样照亮了夜晚。|近义词：firefly, lightning bug|反义词: (无直接反义词)|词性:名词
