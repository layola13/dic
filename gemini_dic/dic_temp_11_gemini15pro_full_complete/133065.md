nonequal:/ˌnɒnˈiːkwəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈiːkwəl/
adjective
不相等的，不同的
Not equal; different.
The two groups were nonequal in size. 这两组人数不相等。
近义词:unequal, disparate, different, dissimilar
反义词:equal, identical, same
词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + equal.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Equal" comes from the Latin "aequalis," meaning "uniform," "level," or "equal."
