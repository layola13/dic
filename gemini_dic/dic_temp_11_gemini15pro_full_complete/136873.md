nonslippery:/ˌnɑːnˈslɪpəri/ |adj.|防滑的|Not slippery; providing a good grip.|The nonslippery surface of the mat ensured I didn't fall in the shower. 浴室垫的防滑表面确保我在淋浴时不会摔倒。|近义词：non-skid, grip, anti-slip|反义词:slippery, slick|词性:形容词
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + slippery (adjective meaning "smooth and difficult to hold on to")
