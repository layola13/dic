clatterer:/ˈklætərər/ (US) /ˈklætərə/ (UK)| n.|发出 clatter 声音的人或物; 喋喋不休的人|A person or thing that makes a clattering sound; a noisy talker|The clatterer upstairs was driving me crazy. 楼上发出 clatter 声音的人快把我逼疯了。|近义词：rattler, noisemaker|反义词:quiet one|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "clatter" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Clatter" originates from  Middle English *clateren*, of imitative origin.
