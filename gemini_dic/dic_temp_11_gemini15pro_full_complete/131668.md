nonconcentricity:/ˌnɒnkɒnsənˈtrɪsɪti/ | n. |非同心度，偏心率|The state of not having the same center; the amount or degree by which objects deviate from having the same center. |The nonconcentricity of the two circles caused vibration in the machine. 这两个圆的非同心度导致机器振动。|Near synonyms: eccentricity, off-center, misalignment|Near antonyms: concentricity, alignment|noun

nonconcentricities:/ˌnɒnkɒnsənˈtrɪsɪtiz/ | n. |非同心度（复数）|Plural form of nonconcentricity, referring to multiple instances of not having the same center. |The engineer identified several nonconcentricities in the design. 工程师在设计中发现了几个非同心度问题。|Near synonyms: eccentricities, off-centers, misalignments|Near antonyms: concentricities, alignments|noun
