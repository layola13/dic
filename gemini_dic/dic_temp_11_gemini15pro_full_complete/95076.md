inadvisedly:/ˌɪnədˈvaɪzɪdli/
adverb
不明智地；欠考虑地
in a way that is not sensible or wise; rashly.
He inadvisedly boasted of his skill. 他不明智地吹嘘自己的技能。
Synonyms: foolishly, unwisely, imprudently, rashly, thoughtlessly, carelessly, heedlessly, recklessly, injudiciously, ill-advisedly
Antonyms: wisely, prudently, judiciously
part of speech: adverb
