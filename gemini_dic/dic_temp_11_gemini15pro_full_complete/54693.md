disordination:/ˌdɪsɔːrdɪˈneɪʃn/

A disturbance of the regular or normal arrangement. |The disordination of his desk made it hard to find anything. 他的桌子很乱，很难找到任何东西。|近义词：disorder, chaos, confusion, mess, muddle|反义词：order, organization, tidiness, neatness
