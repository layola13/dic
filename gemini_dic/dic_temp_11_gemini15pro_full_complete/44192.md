croakinesses: /ˈkrəʊkɪnɪsɪz/
noun
沙哑(声)；嘶哑(声) (plural of croakiness)
The state or quality of having a rough or hoarse voice, typically due to illness or overuse.
Her croakinesses worsened as the cold progressed.  她的嗓子随着感冒的加重而变得更加沙哑。
The singer's croakinesses were evident after the concert.  演唱会后，这位歌手的声音明显沙哑。
Synonyms: hoarseness, huskiness, roughness
Antonyms: clarity, smoothness, resonance
词性: noun
