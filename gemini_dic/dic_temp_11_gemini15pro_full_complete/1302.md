accounts:/əˈkaʊnts/ | n. |账户；账目；报告；叙述|A record or statement of financial transactions, or a report or explanation of events.|例句：She kept detailed accounts of her expenses. 她详细记录了她的开支。|近义词：records, reports, statements, narratives |反义词: debts (in some contexts) |noun

accounts:/əˈkaʊnts/ | v. |认为；视为；说明；解释| To consider or regard in a specified way, or to provide an explanation or justification for something. | 例句：He accounted for the missing money by saying he had lost his wallet. 他说他丢了钱包，以此来解释丢失的钱。 | 近义词：explain, justify, describe, consider |反义词:  obfuscate, conceal|verb
