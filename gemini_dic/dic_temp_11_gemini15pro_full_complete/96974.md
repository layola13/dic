infantical:/ɪnfænˈtɪkəl/| adj.|幼稚的，孩子气的| behaving like a small child, especially in a way that seems silly| He threw an infantical tantrum when he didn't get his way. 他因为没有达到目的而像小孩一样大发脾气。|Example:  His infantical behavior embarrassed everyone at the meeting. 他的幼稚行为让会议上的每个人都很尴尬。

Example: She made an infantical face when she tasted the bitter medicine. 她尝到苦药时做了个孩子气的鬼脸。|近义词：childish, immature, puerile|反义词: mature, grown-up|词性:形容词
