fecundify:/ˈfɛkənˌdaɪfaɪ/ /ˈfiːkənˌdaɪfaɪ/ | vt. |使多产，使肥沃，使富饶|To make fruitful or fertile; to increase the productivity of.|The farmer used special fertilizers to fecundify the soil.  农民用特殊的肥料来使土壤肥沃。| The writer's imagination was fecundified by his travels. 作家的旅行使他的想象力更加丰富。 |近义词：fertilize, enrich, make productive|反义词：sterilize, barren, impoverish|词性：动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *fecundificare, from Latin fecundus (“fruitful”) + -ificare (“to make”).
