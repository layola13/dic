mild-mannered: /ˌmaɪldˈmænərd/ | adj. | 温和的；谦逊的 |  Characterized by a gentle and reserved demeanor; not given to extremes of behavior or expression. |  He was a mild-mannered man who rarely raised his voice. 他是一个温文尔雅的人，很少提高嗓门。 |  polite, courteous, respectful |  rude, impolite, disrespectful | adjective
