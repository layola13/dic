irremediablenesses:/ɪˌrɛmɪˈdiəbəlnəsɪz/

The quality of being irreparable. |  The irremediablenesses of the situation left them feeling hopeless.  这种情况的不可挽回性让他们感到绝望。 |  incurability, hopelessness, irreparability,  incurableness | remediableness, curability, reparability | noun
