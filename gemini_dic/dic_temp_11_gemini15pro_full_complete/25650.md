bundler:/ˈbʌndlər/| n.|捆扎机，打包机；捆绑软件；中间人|A person or thing that bundles; a program that bundles together the gems and other dependencies of a Ruby program.|The bundler automatically included all the necessary libraries for the project.  打包程序自动包含了项目所需的所有库。|Synonyms: assembler, collector, packer|Antonyms: separator, divider|noun
