intershop:/ˈɪntərʃɒp/ (UK) /ˈɪntərʃɑːp/ (US)| n.|公司间的商店；企业间的商店|A shop located within or serving a business park or industrial estate, often selling goods or services to employees of the businesses there. |Example 1:  The intershop sells everything from stationery to safety equipment. 公司间的商店出售从文具到安全设备的所有商品。

Example 2:  Many employees find the intershop convenient for grabbing lunch. 许多员工觉得公司间的商店很方便买午餐。|近义词：company store, staff shop, employee shop|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) public store, retail store|词性:noun
