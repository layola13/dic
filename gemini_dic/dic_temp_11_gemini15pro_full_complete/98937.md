inter-allied:/ˌɪntərˈælaɪd/
adjective
相互结盟的；同盟国之间的
Existing or occurring between allied nations.
The inter-allied debts after the war were substantial. 战后同盟国之间的债务相当巨大。
The inter-allied conference addressed crucial strategic matters. 同盟国之间的会议讨论了重要的战略问题。
近义词: allied, cooperative, joint
反义词: enemy, hostile, opposed
词性: adjective

词源:
"inter-" (prefix): between, among (from Latin)
"allied" (adjective): joined by or relating to an alliance (from Old French "alier")
