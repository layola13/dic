outmerchant:/ˌaʊtˈmɜːrtʃənt/
verb
vt. |在商业上胜过，比...更会做生意| to surpass in business acumen or trading; to sell more or at a better price than a competitor. |Example: They aimed to outmerchant their rivals by offering lower prices and better customer service. 他们旨在通过提供更低的价格和更好的客户服务来在商业上胜过他们的竞争对手。

Example: The small bookstore managed to outmerchant the larger chain by specializing in rare and antique books.  这家小书店通过专注于稀有和古董书籍，成功地比更大的连锁书店做得更好。
|近义词：outsell, outperform, surpass|反义词：be outdone, be surpassed|词性:动词

词源：
"out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "merchant" (noun, a person or company involved in trade)
