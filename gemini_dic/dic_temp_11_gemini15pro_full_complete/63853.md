epigrammatist:/ˌepɪˈɡræmətɪst/
n.
警句家，善于写警句的人
A person who writes or uses epigrams.
Oscar Wilde was a brilliant epigrammatist, his plays are full of witty and memorable lines.  奥斯卡·王尔德是一位杰出的警句家，他的戏剧充满了机智而令人难忘的台词。
He gained a reputation as a sharp-tongued epigrammatist. 他赢得了尖刻的警句家的名声。
Synonym:  wit, quipster, pundit
Antonym:  dullard, bore
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *épigrammatiste*, from Latin *epigramma* (epigram), from Greek *epigramma* (inscription), from *epi-* (upon) + *gramma* (something written).
