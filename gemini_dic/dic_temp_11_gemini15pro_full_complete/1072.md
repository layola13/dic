acceptable:/əkˈseptəbl/ | adj. |可接受的，合意的| satisfactory; adequate; agreeable; pleasing | The proposal was deemed acceptable by the board.  这个提案被董事会认为是可以接受的。|  Her performance was acceptable, but not outstanding. 她的表现尚可，但并不出色。 |近义词：satisfactory, adequate, agreeable, tolerable, decent| 反义词：unacceptable, unsatisfactory, inadequate, intolerable, objectionable |词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from late Latin acceptabilis, from acceptare ‘accept’.
