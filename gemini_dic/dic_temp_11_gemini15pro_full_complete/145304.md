overbridge:/ˈəʊvəbrɪdʒ/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərˌbrɪdʒ/ (US)| n.|天桥，立交桥|A bridge that carries one road or railway line over another.|We drove under the overbridge and onto the highway. 我们开车从立交桥下穿过，上了高速公路。|例句：The overbridge provided a safe way for pedestrians to cross the busy road. 这座天桥为行人穿越繁忙的道路提供了安全通道。

例句：The train rumbled across the overbridge, high above the traffic. 火车隆隆地驶过天桥，高高地凌驾于车流之上。|近义词：flyover, bridge, viaduct|反义词:underpass, subway|词性:名词
