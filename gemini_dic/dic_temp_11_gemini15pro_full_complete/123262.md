monodical: /məˈnɒdɪkəl/ (British), /məˈnɑːdɪkəl/ (American) | adj. | 单调的，单音调的 | Characterized by a single, unvarying tone or pitch; monotonous. | The monodical chanting of the monks became hypnotic after a while.  僧侣们单调的吟唱过了一会儿就变得催眠了。 |  The lecturer's monodical delivery made it difficult for the students to stay awake.  讲师单调的演讲让学生们很难保持清醒。 | 近义词: monotonous, droning, tedious, dull | 反义词: varied, diverse, interesting, stimulating | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Greek *μονῳδικός (monōdikos), from μόνος (monos, "single, alone") + ᾠδή (ōidē, "song, ode").
