femtosecond:/ˈfemtoʊˌsekənd/ | n. |飞秒|A unit of time equal to one quadrillionth (10⁻¹⁵) of a second.|Ultrafast lasers produce pulses of light lasting only a few femtoseconds. 超快激光器产生仅持续几飞秒的光脉冲。|Synonyms: quadrillionth of a second |Antonyms: N/A |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From femto- (prefix denoting one quadrillionth (10⁻¹⁵)), from Danish or Norwegian femten "fifteen", from Old Norse fimmtān, from Proto-Germanic *fimf- "five" + *tehun "ten", + second.
