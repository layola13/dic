caterwauling:/ˈkætərˌwɔːlɪŋ/| n. |猫叫春声，刺耳的叫声|A shrill, discordant howling or wailing sound, like that of a cat in heat.|The caterwauling of the alley cats kept me awake all night. 巷子里野猫的叫春声让我整夜没睡。|近义词：howling, wailing, screeching, shrieking|反义词：purring, meowing (softer cat sounds), silence|词性:noun

caterwaul:/ˈkætərˌwɔːl/| v. |发出猫叫春声，发出刺耳的叫声|To make a shrill, discordant howling or wailing sound.|The singers caterwauled their way through the song, making the audience cringe. 歌手们用刺耳的声音唱着歌，让观众感到畏缩。|近义词：howl, wail, screech, shriek|反义词：purr, meow (softer cat sounds), whisper|词性:verb
