jibba:/ˈdʒɪbə/| n.|胡说八道，废话；帆的三角形前帆|Meaningless or foolish talk; a triangular foresail on a sailing vessel.|He was talking a load of jibba about winning the lottery. 他胡扯了一堆关于中彩票的事。|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, balderdash|反义词:sense, truth|noun

jib:/dʒɪb/| n. v.|船首三角帆；轻敲；嘲讽|A triangular staysail set forward of the mast; to strike or poke lightly; to taunt or make fun of.|The jib caught the wind and the boat lurched forward. 三角帆兜住了风，船猛地向前冲去。  They jibbed him about his new haircut. 他们嘲笑他的新发型。|近义词：(n.) foresail, staysail; (v.) jab, poke, mock|反义词: (v.) praise, compliment|noun, verb
