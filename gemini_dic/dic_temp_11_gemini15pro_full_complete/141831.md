orbulina:/ɔːrˈbʊlɪnə/| n. |球房虫，圆房虫|A genus of planktonic foraminifera characterized by a spherical shell with numerous small openings.  A type of single-celled marine organism with a globular shell. |例句：Orbulina universa is a common species found in warm ocean waters.  球房虫是一种常见于温暖海域的物种。

例句：The abundance of orbulina in the sediment core provides clues to past climate conditions.  沉积物岩心中球房虫的丰度为过去的气候条件提供了线索。|近义词：Globigerina (another genus of foraminifera), foraminiferan|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
