curmudgeon: /kərˈmʌdʒən/ | n. | 脾气坏的老人；爱抱怨的人 | A bad-tempered, especially an elderly person.  | Example 1: My neighbor is a real curmudgeon; he complains about everything. 我的邻居真是个坏脾气的怪老头；他什么都抱怨。

Example 2:  He's become a bit of a curmudgeon in his old age. 他老了以后变得有点暴躁易怒。 | 近义词: grump, grouch, crank | 反义词: amiable person, optimist | 词性: 名词
