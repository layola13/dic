nonfelicitousnesses:/ˌnɒnfɪˈlɪsɪtəsnəsɪz/
noun
不幸；不恰当；不合适
The state or quality of being nonfelicitous; multiple instances of infelicity or inappropriateness.
His speech was full of nonfelicitousnesses, making the audience uncomfortable. 他的演讲充满了不恰当之处，让听众感到不舒服。
近义词: improprieties, inappropriatenesses, infelicities
反义词: felicities, appropriateness, suitabilities
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
From non- (not) + felicitous (apt, suitable) + -ness (forming nouns of quality or state) + -es (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *felix (lucky, fruitful).
