half-right:/ˌhæf ˈraɪt/ | adj. | 半对的，部分正确的 | Partly correct; correct in some respects but not completely. |  He was half-right in his assessment of the situation. 他对形势的评估一半是对的。| 近义词: partially correct, partly right | 反义词: wrong, incorrect, completely wrong | 词性: adjective

half-right:/ˌhæf ˈraɪt/ | adv. | 部分正确地 | In a way that is partly correct. | He answered the question half-right. 他回答这个问题答对了一半。| 近义词: partially, partly | 反义词: wrongly, incorrectly, completely wrongly | 词性: adverb
