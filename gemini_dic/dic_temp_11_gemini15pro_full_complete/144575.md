outwaste:/aʊtˈweɪst/
verb
过度消耗，浪费
to waste something excessively
He outwasted his rivals by spending vast sums on advertising. 他在广告上投入巨资，远超竞争对手，造成了浪费。
近义词: squander, dissipate, lavish
反义词: conserve, save, economize
词性: verb
