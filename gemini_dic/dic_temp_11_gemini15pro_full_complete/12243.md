ascendancy:/əˈsɛndənsi/ | n. |优势，支配地位，权势|A position of dominant power or influence.|The family's ascendancy in the community lasted for generations. 这个家族在社区的优势地位持续了几代人。|Example:  During the 19th century, Britain held political and economic ascendancy over many nations. 19世纪时，英国在政治和经济上对许多国家拥有支配地位。

|Example: The ascendancy of social media has dramatically changed the way news is spread. 社交媒体的兴起极大地改变了新闻传播的方式。|Synonyms: dominance, supremacy, control, power, sway |Antonyms: subordination, inferiority, weakness, impotence | Noun
