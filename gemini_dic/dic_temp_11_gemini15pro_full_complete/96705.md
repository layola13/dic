ineffectible:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktɪbl/
adjective
无效的，不起作用的
Not capable of being effected; unachievable or impracticable.  Unable to be brought about or made to happen.  Incapable of producing any effect; useless.
His attempts to change the company policy were ineffectible. 他试图改变公司政策的努力是无效的。
The medicine proved ineffectible against the virus. 这种药被证明对病毒无效。
ineffective, futile, useless, unavailing, impotent
effective, efficacious, potent, useful
adjective
