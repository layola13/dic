belatedly:/bɪˈleɪtɪdli/| adv.|迟来地，延误地|Done or occurring late; after the expected or usual time. |She belatedly realized she had forgotten his birthday. 她迟来地意识到她忘记了他的生日。|I belatedly sent him a birthday card. 我迟来地给他寄了一张生日贺卡。|近义词：late, tardily, overdue|反义词: promptly, early, timely|词性:副词
词源: From the Latin word "belatus," the past participle of "belare," meaning "to bellow" or "to bleat," which later acquired the sense of "to delay." The English word "belated" originally meant "delayed" or "retarded," and the adverb "belatedly" followed from this.
