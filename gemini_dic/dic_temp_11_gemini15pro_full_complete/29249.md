cassican:/ˈkæsɪkən/ | n. |鹊|A black and white Australian bird known for its melodious song and sometimes aggressive behavior.|The cassican warbled a complex tune from the gum tree. 澳洲喜鹊在桉树上婉转地唱着复杂的曲调。|Example: The cassican swooped down and attacked the cyclist.  澳洲喜鹊俯冲下来攻击了骑自行车的人。|Synonyms: magpie, butcherbird|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
