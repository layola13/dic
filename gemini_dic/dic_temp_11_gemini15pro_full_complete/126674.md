namaskar:/nʌməˈskɑːr/| n. int.|合十礼，问候(尤用于印度)|A respectful greeting or salutation, especially in India, made by bringing the palms together before the face or chest.|例句：She greeted us with a namaskar. 她双手合十向我们问好。|近义词：greeting, salutation, hello|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "disrespect")|noun
