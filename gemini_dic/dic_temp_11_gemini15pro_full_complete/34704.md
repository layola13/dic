cleverest:/ˈklɛvərɪst/| adj.|最聪明的|Superlative form of clever: most clever.  Showing intelligence and skill.|She's the cleverest student in the class. 她班上最聪明的学生。|近义词：brightest, smartest, sharpest, most intelligent, most ingenious|反义词：stupidest, dullest, most foolish|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old English *glēaw "wise, prudent, clever," of uncertain origin. The "-est" suffix is the superlative ending.
