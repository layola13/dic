alluvials:/əˈluːviəlz/ | n. | 冲积层；冲积土 | Deposits of earth, sand, and other matter left by flowing water. | The fertile alluvials of the Nile Valley support a large population. 尼罗河谷肥沃的冲积土养活了大量人口。| 近义词：deposit, sediment, silt | 反义词: bedrock | noun

alluvial:/əˈluːviəl/ | adj. | 冲积的；由流水沉积形成的 | Relating to or consisting of alluvium. | The alluvial plain is excellent for farming. 冲积平原非常适合耕种。| 近义词： sedimentary | 反义词: igneous | adjective
