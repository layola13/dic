outwoe:/ˌaʊtˈwoʊ/
verb
比...更悲伤；在悲伤上胜过
to surpass in woe; to be more sorrowful than.
She outwoed all her friends when her dog died. 她比所有朋友都更悲伤，当她的狗去世时。
近义词:outsorrow, outgrieve
反义词:outjoy, rejoice
词性:v.
