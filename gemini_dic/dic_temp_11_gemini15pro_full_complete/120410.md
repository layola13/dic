ministress:/ˈmɪnɪstrəs/ | n. | 女部长；女大臣；情妇 | A female minister of state or a woman in a position of authority, especially in government; a woman who has a continuing sexual relationship with a man who is married to someone else. | The Prime Minister appointed her as the new ministress of education. 首相任命她为新的教育部长。|  He kept his ministress in a luxurious apartment downtown. 他把他的情妇安置在市中心的一套豪华公寓里。 | 近义词: minister (in the government sense), lover, mistress | 反义词: (No direct antonym for the government sense) wife (in the infidelity sense) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *ministresse*, feminine form of ministre "minister," ultimately from Latin *minister* "servant, agent, assistant."
