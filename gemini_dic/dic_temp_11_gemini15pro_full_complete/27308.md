calycozoa:/ˌkælɪkəˈzoʊə/ | n. | 杯状动物 | A small group of marine invertebrates formerly classified as scyphozoan jellyfish but now considered a separate class or subphylum. They have a calyx (cup-shaped) body and lack the typical jellyfish medusa stage. | Calycozoans are fascinating creatures found in deep sea environments. 杯状动物是在深海环境中发现的迷人生物。 |  近义词: (None readily available - this is a specific taxonomic group) | 反义词: (None - taxonomic groups don't have opposites) | 词性: noun
