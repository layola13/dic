nonresolutions:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛzəˈluːʃənz/
noun
未完成的决心；未实现的目标；未履行的承诺
Things that one intended to do but didn't actually accomplish or commit to, especially regarding New Year's resolutions.
This year, I'm focusing on nonresolutions – things I'm *not* going to worry about. 今年，我关注的是“不作为”——我不打算担心的事情。
synonyms: unfulfilled intentions, abandoned goals, broken promises
antonyms: resolutions, achievements, accomplishments
