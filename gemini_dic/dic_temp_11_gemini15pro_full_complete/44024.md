crimper:/ˈkrɪmpər/| n.|卷发器；压接钳；卷边机；（非正式）卷发师|A tool used to crimp or curl hair, or to attach connectors to wires.|She used a crimper to style her hair. 她用卷发器做头发造型。|近义词：styler, curling iron (for hair), pliers (for wires)|反义词:straightener (for hair)|noun

crimp:/krɪmp/| vt.|使卷曲；使起皱；压接（电线）|To press or bend something into small folds or waves.|She crimped the edges of the pie crust. 她把馅饼皮的边缘弄成褶皱。|近义词：curl, wave, crimp, corrugate|反义词:straighten, flatten|verb

crimp:/krɪmp/| n.|卷曲；褶皱；压接|A small fold or wave in something, especially fabric or hair.|The crimp in the fabric added a touch of elegance. 布料上的褶皱增添了一丝优雅。|近义词：curl, wave, wrinkle, fold|反义词:smoothness, flatness|noun
