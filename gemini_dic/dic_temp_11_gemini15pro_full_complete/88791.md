hoecake:/ˈhoʊˌkeɪk/| n. |玉米饼，烙饼|A type of cornbread, originally cooked on a hoe blade over an open fire.|My grandmother used to make delicious hoecakes. 我祖母过去常常做美味的玉米饼。|近义词：cornbread, johnnycake|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "wheat bread" as a contrasting example) | noun
