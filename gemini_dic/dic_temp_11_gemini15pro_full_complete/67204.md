expressionless:/ɪkˈspreʃənləs/| adj.|毫无表情的；面无表情的|Not showing any emotion on the face; blank.|Her face was expressionless as she listened to the news. 她听着新闻，脸上毫无表情。|近义词：blank, impassive, deadpan, emotionless, vacant, wooden|反义词：expressive, animated, emotional|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From expression + -less.  "Expression" comes from Late Middle English, from Old French "expression," from Latin "expressio," meaning "a pressing out, representation."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without," from Old English "-lēas."
