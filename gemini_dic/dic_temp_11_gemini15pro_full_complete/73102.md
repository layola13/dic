foppish:/ˈfɒpɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːpɪʃ/ (US)| adj.|浮夸的；纨绔子弟的；过分讲究衣着的|Characterized by excessive or affected elegance, especially in dress and manner; dandyish.| He struck a foppish pose, twirling his cane. 他摆出一个浮夸的姿势，旋转着手杖。| The foppish young man spent hours preening himself in front of the mirror.  这个纨绔子弟在镜子前打扮了好几个小时。| 近义词：dandyish, effeminate, pretentious, ostentatious |反义词：masculine, unpretentious, modest, simple |词性: adjective

词源:
From fop + -ish.  "Fop" originates from the 17th century, likely related to Dutch "fop" (fool, simpleton).  The "-ish" suffix adds the meaning "like" or "having the qualities of."
