overintellectualness:/ˌoʊvərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlˌnɛs/
n.
过度理智；过度的理性；过分理性化
The state of being excessively intellectual or relying too heavily on intellectual processes.  This can sometimes lead to a disconnect from emotions or practical realities.
His overintellectualness sometimes prevents him from enjoying simple pleasures. 他过分的理性有时让他无法享受简单的快乐。
近义词: intellectualism, cerebrality, hyperintellectualism
反义词: practicality, emotionalism, simplicity
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + intellectual + -ness. "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much." "Intellectual" refers to the intellect or reasoning abilities. "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
