currier:/ˈkʌriər/| n.|皮革工，制革工人|A person who curries leather, applying oil and working it to make it supple.|The currier skillfully treated the hide, transforming it into soft, pliable leather. 皮革工熟练地处理着兽皮，将其变成了柔软的皮革。|近义词：leatherworker, tanner|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *corier, from Late Latin coriarius (“leather dresser”), from Latin corium (“hide, leather”).
