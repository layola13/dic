epichorial:/ˌɛpɪˈkɔːriəl/ | adj. | 绒毛膜的，限于胎盘的 | Relating to the outermost of the fetal membranes (the chorion) and its attachment to the uterine wall.  Specifically, it refers to a type of placentation where the chorion is in contact with the uterine wall but does not embed deeply into it. |  Epichorial placentation is found in horses and pigs.  马和猪的胎盘是绒毛膜胎盘。| Synonym: nondeciduous | Antonym: deciduous | adjective
