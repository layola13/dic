id'ed:/aɪˈdiːd/ | v. | 已识别的，已确认的 | Identified; confirmed.  Past participle of the verb "identify". | The police id'ed the suspect from the security camera footage. 警方通过监控录像确认了嫌疑人的身份。| Synonyms: recognized, distinguished, pinpointed | Antonyms: anonymous, unknown | verb

词源:  Shortened form of "identified," the past participle of the verb "identify."  "Identify" comes from the Late Latin "identificare,"  meaning "to make identical," from "idem" ("the same") + "facere" ("to make").
