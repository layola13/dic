hundred:/ˈhʌndrəd/ (US) /ˈhʌndrɪd/ (UK)| num. n. adj.|百，一百，许多|A cardinal number, 10 times ten. Can also be used to mean a large but indefinite number.|There are a hundred sheep in the field. 田里有一百只羊。|近义词：century, one hundred|反义词: (As a precise number, it has no direct opposite.  Consider "few" or "zero" as contextual antonyms.)|词性: Number, Noun, Adjective
