fulcrumed:/ˈfʊlkrəmd/ /ˈfʌlkrəmd/| adj.|支点式的，以...为支点的|Having a fulcrum; pivoted.  Acting as a fulcrum; pivotal. |例句：The lever was fulcrumed on a rock.  杠杆以一块岩石为支点。

fulcrum:/ˈfʊlkrəm/ /ˈfʌlkrəmd/| n.|支点，支柱|The point on which a lever rests or is supported and on which it pivots. A thing that plays a central or essential role in an activity, event, or situation.|例句：The fulcrum of the seesaw allowed the children to go up and down. 跷跷板的支点让孩子们可以上下移动。

例句：He was the fulcrum of the whole operation. 他是整个行动的关键人物。|近义词：pivot, center, support, core|反义词:edge, periphery|词性:名词
