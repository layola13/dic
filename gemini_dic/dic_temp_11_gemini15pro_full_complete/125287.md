multifoil:/ˈmʌltɪfɔɪl/ | adj. n. | 多叶的，多瓣的；多叶形饰 | Having many foils or leaves; resembling a multifoil. | The church window featured a beautiful multifoil design. 教堂的窗户上有一个漂亮的多叶形图案。| The architect incorporated a multifoil arch in the building's facade. 建筑师在建筑的正面融入了多叶形拱门。| 近义词: polyfoil, multilobate | 反义词: simple, plain | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
From multi- + foil.  "Multi-" is a combining form meaning "many," derived from Latin "multus." "Foil" in this context refers to a leaf-shaped ornament, originating from Old French "foil" (leaf), ultimately from Latin "folium."
