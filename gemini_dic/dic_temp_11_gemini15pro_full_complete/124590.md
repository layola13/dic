mousekin:/ˈmaʊskɪn/| n. |小老鼠，鼠宝宝|A diminutive of mouse, referring to a young or small mouse. It evokes a sense of cuteness or endearment.|The little mousekin scurried under the floorboards. 小老鼠嗖地一下跑到地板下面去了。|近义词：mousie, little mouse|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps large rat)|词性:noun
