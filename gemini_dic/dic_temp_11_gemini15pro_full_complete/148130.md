overseer:/ˈoʊvərˌsɪr/ | n. | 监督员；工头；监工 | A person who supervises others, especially workers. |  The overseer made sure the cotton was picked quickly and efficiently.  监工确保棉花被快速有效地采摘。

The plantation overseer was known for his cruelty.  种植园的监工以其残忍而闻名。 | 近义词: supervisor, superintendent, foreman, manager | 反义词: subordinate, worker, employee | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *overseere*, equivalent to over- + see + -er.  最终源自拉丁语supervidēre (“to oversee”).
