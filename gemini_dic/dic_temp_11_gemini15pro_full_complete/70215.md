fibrovascular:/ˌfaɪbroʊˈvæskjələr/
adjective
纤维血管的
Composed of or containing both fibrous and vascular tissue.  Relating to tissues that contain both fibers and blood vessels.
The fibrovascular bundles in the stem provide support and transport nutrients. 茎中的纤维血管束提供支撑和运输营养。
The plant's fibrovascular system is essential for its growth. 植物的纤维血管系统对其生长至关重要。
Synonyms: vascular, vasofibrous
Antonyms: None
adjective

fibrovascular:/ˌfaɪbroʊˈvæskjələr/
noun
纤维血管组织
Tissue composed of both fibrous and vascular elements.
The fibrovascular of the leaf is clearly visible under a microscope. 在显微镜下，叶子的纤维血管组织清晰可见。
Damage to the fibrovascular can hinder the plant's ability to transport water. 对纤维血管组织的损害会阻碍植物输送水分的能力。
Synonyms: vascular tissue, connective tissue (partially)
Antonyms: None
noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From fibro- + vascular.  "Fibro-" comes from Latin fibra, meaning "fiber." "Vascular" comes from Latin vasculum, meaning "small vessel," ultimately from vas, meaning "vessel."
