megasthenic:/ˌmɛɡəsˈθɛnɪk/ | adj. | 巨大的，强壮的 |  Of or relating to great strength; exhibiting unusual strength and abnormally large muscles.  Derived from Greek *megas* "great" + *sthenos* "strength." |  The megasthenic bodybuilder easily lifted the enormous weight.  这位身材魁梧的健美运动员轻松地举起了巨大的重量。 | Synonyms:  powerful, mighty, herculean, robust | Antonyms: weak, feeble, puny | word type: adjective

megasthenic:/ˌmɛɡəsˈθɛnɪk/ | n. | 体格魁梧的人 | A person with great strength and large muscles. | The megasthenic won the strongman competition.  这位体格魁梧的人赢得了大力士比赛。 | Synonyms: strongman, giant | Antonyms: weakling | word type: noun
