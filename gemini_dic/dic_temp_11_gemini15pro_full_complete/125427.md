multiphotons:/ˌmʌltiˈfoʊtɑːnz/ | n. | 多光子 |  Relating to or involving multiple photons, which are elementary particles of light.  It often refers to processes where multiple photons interact simultaneously with a target, such as in multiphoton microscopy or multiphoton ionization. | Multiphoton microscopy allows for deeper imaging into tissues. 多光子显微镜可以对组织进行更深层次的成像。| synonyms: polyphotons (less common) | antonyms: single-photon | noun
