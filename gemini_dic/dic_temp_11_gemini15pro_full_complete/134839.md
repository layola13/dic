nonmitigatory:/ˌnɒnˈmɪtɪɡətɔːri/
| adj. | 非减轻的，不缓和的 | Not serving to mitigate, alleviate, or lessen; not reducing the severity or seriousness of something.  | The judge imposed a nonmitigatory sentence due to the severity of the crime.  法官因罪行严重而判处了不减刑的刑罚。 | Near Synonyms: unmitigated, harsh, severe, relentless | Antonyms: mitigating, alleviating, lessening, reducing | adjective
