fistulae:/ˈfɪstjʊliː/ (also fistulas /ˈfɪstjʊləz/)
noun
瘘管；瘘
A fistula is an abnormal connection or passageway between two organs or vessels that do not normally connect.
The patient developed a fistula between the bowel and the bladder. 这位病人出现了肠道和膀胱之间的瘘管。
Synonyms: channel, passage, tract
Antonyms: closure, occlusion
