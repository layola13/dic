interfluent:/ˌɪntərˈfluːənt/
n.
河流汇合处；支流间的区域
The area between two confluent rivers.
The interfluent of the Mississippi and Missouri rivers is rich in biodiversity. 密西西比河和密苏里河的汇合处生物多样性丰富。
confluence, interfluve

词性: n.
