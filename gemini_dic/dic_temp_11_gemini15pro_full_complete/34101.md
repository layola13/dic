civism: /ˈsɪvɪzəm/ | n. | 公民意识，公民责任，公民美德 | The behavior and attitude of a responsible citizen. |  Civic education promotes good citizenship and encourages civic participation. 公民教育促进良好的公民意识，并鼓励公民参与。

|  High levels of civic engagement are essential for a thriving democracy. 高度的公民参与对于繁荣的民主至关重要。 | synonyms: citizenship, public spirit, civic responsibility, social responsibility | antonyms: apathy, indifference, negligence | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *civisme*, from Latin *civicus* ("civic") + *-isme* (equivalent to English *-ism*). Ultimately from Latin *civis* ("citizen").
