ignominiously:/ˌɪɡnəˈmɪniəsli/
adverb
耻辱地，不光彩地
in a way that causes a total loss of dignity and respect; humiliatingly.
He was ignominiously defeated in the first round. 他在第一轮就被耻辱地击败了。
ignominiously dismissed from his job. 他被不光彩地解雇了。

Synonyms: shamefully, disgracefully, humiliatingly, dishonorably, degradingly
Antonyms: honorably, gloriously, respectably, nobly

Etymology:
From Late Latin ignominiōsus + -ly.  Latin ignōminia "dishonor, disgrace, infamy"  (from in- "not" + gnōmen "name, reputation")
