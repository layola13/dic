cascabel:/ˈkæskəˌbɛl/| n.|响尾蛇的尾环，铃铛，球形小铃|A cascabel is a rattle at the end of a rattlesnake's tail.|The cascabel rattled ominously as the snake coiled. 响尾蛇盘绕起来时，尾环发出不祥的响声。|The dancer wore a dress adorned with cascabels. 舞者的裙子上装饰着许多小铃铛。|Synonyms: rattle, bell, jingle|Antonyms: silence, quiet|Noun

cascabel:/kæsˈkɑːbəl/| adj.|球状的，铃铛形的|Cascabel peppers are small and round. 球形辣椒小而圆。|The cascabel ornament tinkled in the breeze. 球形装饰品在微风中叮当作响。|Synonyms: spherical, globular, round|Antonyms: elongated, flat|Adjective
