misbelieve: /ˌmɪsbɪˈliːv/
verb
不信；怀疑；误信
To hold a false belief or have doubts about the truth of something.
He misbelieved the rumors and spread them further. 他误信了谣言，并进一步散播。
The child misbelieved that Santa Claus was real.  这孩子误以为圣诞老人是真的。
近义词: disbelieve, doubt, mistrust
反义词: believe, trust, accept
词性: verb

词源:
From mis- + believe.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Old French origin meaning "wrong," "bad," "wrongly," or "badly."  "Believe" comes from Old English "belīefan,"  meaning "to believe, trust, have confidence in."
