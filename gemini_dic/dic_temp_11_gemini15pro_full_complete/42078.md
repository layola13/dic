cotidal:/koʊˈtaɪdəl/| adj.|同潮的，等潮的|Relating to or having the same tides or occurring at the same time as the tide.  Specifically refers to lines on a map connecting points where the tide has the same phase.|The cotidal chart shows the progression of the tidal wave across the ocean. 这张等潮图显示了潮汐波穿过海洋的进程。|Synonyms:  isochronous (in a general sense of simultaneous), synchronous|Antonyms: asynchronous|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- (together) + tidal (relating to tides)
