apeman:/ˈeɪpmæn/| n.|猿人；原始人|A prehistoric human-like creature.  A term considered outdated and offensive due to its racist connotations, suggesting a link between Black people and primates. |The museum exhibit featured a reconstruction of an apeman skeleton. 博物馆展览展出了一个猿人骨骼的重建模型。|近义词：hominid, early human, prehistoric human|反义词:modern human|词性:noun


词源:
From ape + man. Coined in the late 19th century.
