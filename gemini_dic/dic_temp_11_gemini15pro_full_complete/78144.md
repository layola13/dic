geophagous:/dʒiˈɒfəɡəs/| adj.|食土的，食泥的|Feeding on earth or soil.  Relating to or characterized by the eating of earth or soil, especially clay.|Certain species of parrots are geophagous, consuming clay to neutralize toxins in their diet.| 近义词:soil-eating, clay-eating| 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms related to standard diets, e.g., herbivorous, carnivorous)|词性:形容词
