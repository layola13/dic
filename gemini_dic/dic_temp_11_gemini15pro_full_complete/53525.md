dissatisfied:/dɪsˈsætɪsfaɪd/
adjective
不满意的，不高兴的
Feeling or showing a lack of satisfaction.
She was dissatisfied with the service at the restaurant. 她对这家餐厅的服务不满意。
The customers were dissatisfied with the quality of the product. 顾客对产品质量不满意。
Near Synonyms: discontented, disgruntled, unhappy, displeased
Antonyms: satisfied, content, pleased, happy
词性: 形容词
