expansionary: /ɪkˈspænʃənˌɛri/ /ɪkˈspænʃəˌnɛri/ | adj. | 扩张的，膨胀的 |  Tending to expand or increase, especially in economic activity. Characterized by growth or expansion.  | The government adopted an expansionary fiscal policy to stimulate the economy. 政府采取了扩张性财政政策来刺激经济。 |  The company's expansionary strategy involves opening new branches in several cities.  该公司的扩张战略包括在几个城市开设新的分支机构。 | 近义词: inflationary, growth-oriented, expansionist | 反义词: contractionary, deflationary, restrictive | 词性: 形容词
