mousefish:/ˈmaʊsfɪʃ/| n. |鼠鱼，老鼠鱼|A type of small, bottom-dwelling fish, often kept in aquariums, belonging to several families, notably the family Callichthyidae (armored catfishes). They are named for their whisker-like barbels around their mouths.|例句：The mousefish scurried across the bottom of the tank. 鼠鱼在鱼缸底部快速游动。

例句：My mousefish are constantly sifting through the sand for food. 我的鼠鱼一直在沙子里觅食。

|近义词：Corydoras (a common genus of mousefish), catfish (a broader category)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
