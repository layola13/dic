appertain:/ˌæpərˈteɪn/
verb
属于，关于
relate to; concern; be appropriate or applicable
This information appertains to the case.  这条信息与该案件有关。
近义词:belong, relate, concern, pertain
反义词: be irrelevant
词性: verb
