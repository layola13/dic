epenthesis:/ɪˈpɛnθəsɪs/| n. |插音，增音|The insertion of a sound or letter within a word, e.g., the b in slumber.|例句：The word "empty" often undergoes epenthesis in spoken English, becoming "emp-ty."  “empty”这个词在英语口语中经常出现插音现象，变成“emp-ty”。

例句：Epenthesis is a common phonological process in many languages.  插音是许多语言中常见的语音过程。|近义词：insertion, addition|反义词: deletion, elision|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἐπένθεσις (epénthesis, “insertion”), from ἐπι- (epi-, “on, upon”) + ἐντίθημι (entíthēmi, “I put in”).
