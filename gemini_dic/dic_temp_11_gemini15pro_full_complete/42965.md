PHRASE->
cover up: 掩盖; 盖住 He tried to cover up his mistake. 他试图掩盖他的错误。
cover for: 顶替; 替...掩护  Can you cover for me at the meeting? 你能替我去开会吗？
cover story:  (尤指掩盖真相的)借口，托词 He made up a cover story about why he was late. 他编造了一个迟到的借口。
be covered with: 被…覆盖 The ground was covered with leaves. 地上落满了树叶。
cover ground:  (在讨论或学习中)涉及，处理，论及 The lecture covered a lot of ground.  这个讲座涵盖了很多内容。
