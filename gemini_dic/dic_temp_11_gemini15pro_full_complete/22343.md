bonobo:/bəˈnəʊboʊ/ |n.|倭猩猩，矮黑猩猩|A great ape, closely related to the chimpanzee, found in the rainforests of central Africa. It is known for its relatively peaceful social behavior and its high frequency of sexual activity, which plays a role in conflict resolution and social bonding.|Bonobos are known for their peaceful nature. 倭猩猩以爱好和平的天性而闻名。| 近义词: pygmy chimpanzee | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
