misplanting:/ˌmɪsˈplɑːntɪŋ/
noun
错误种植，种植错误
the act of planting something in the wrong place or at the wrong time.
The misplanting of the seedlings resulted in a poor harvest. 幼苗种植错误导致收成不好。
misplacement, wrong planting
correct planting
