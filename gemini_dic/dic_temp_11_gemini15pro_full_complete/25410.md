bulimia:/buˈlɪmiə/ (US), /bjuːˈlɪmiə/ (UK)| n.|暴食症|An eating disorder characterized by episodes of binge eating followed by inappropriate methods of weight control, such as vomiting, laxative use, fasting, or excessive exercise.|She struggled with bulimia for years before seeking help. 她与暴食症抗争多年才寻求帮助。|

|She kept her bulimia a secret from her family. 她对家人隐瞒了她的暴食症。|近义词: binge-eating disorder|反义词: anorexia (although not a direct opposite, it represents another type of eating disorder)|noun
