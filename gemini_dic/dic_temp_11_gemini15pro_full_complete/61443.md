embossers:/ɪmˈbɒsəz/ /ɪmˈbɑːsərz/
noun
压花机;压纹机;浮雕工人
A person or machine that creates raised designs on surfaces, typically paper, leather, or metal.
The company purchased new embossers to personalize stationery.  公司购买了新的压花机来个性化文具。
The embossers carefully crafted the intricate design on the leather cover.  压花工匠在皮革封面上精心制作了复杂的图案。
engravers, printers, stampers
