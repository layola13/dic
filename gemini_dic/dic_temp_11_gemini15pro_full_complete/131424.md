noncling:/nɒnˈklɪŋ/ | adj. | 非粘性的，不粘的 | Not clinging or adhering; resistant to sticking. | This noncling wrap is perfect for packing sandwiches.  这种不粘保鲜膜非常适合包装三明治。| The fabric is noncling and breathable. 这种织物不粘身，而且透气。 | 近义词: nonstick, nonadhesive | 反义词: clingy, adhesive | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "cling" (verb meaning "to adhere closely").  "Cling" comes from Old English "clingan."
