hemihedron:/ˌhɛmiˈhiːdrən/ | n. |半面体；半面晶体|A crystal form comprising half the number of faces of the normal form, due to systematic suppression of alternate faces.|Crystals of pyrite often occur as hemihedra. 黄铁矿晶体常以半面体的形式出现。|Synonyms: half-crystal |Antonyms: holohedron (全面体)|noun
