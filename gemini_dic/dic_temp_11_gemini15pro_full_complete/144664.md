outwriggling: /ˌaʊtˈrɪɡlɪŋ/
verb
扭动着逃脱; 挣扎着摆脱
Escaping by wriggling or twisting movements.
The fish outwriggled the fisherman's grasp.  鱼扭动着身体逃脱了渔夫的抓捕。
The snake outwriggled from under the rock. 蛇扭动着从石头下钻了出来。
Synonyms: wriggling free, squirming out, escaping, extricating
Antonyms: trapped, confined, stuck, caught
v.
