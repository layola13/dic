exposedness:/ɪkˈspoʊzɪdnəs/| n.|暴露，显露，无遮蔽状态|The exposedness of the cliff face made it dangerous for climbers.  悬崖面的暴露使攀登者很危险。|例句：The exposedness of the data raised privacy concerns. 数据的暴露引发了隐私担忧。

The exposedness of the house on the hill made it vulnerable to strong winds. 山上房子的暴露使它容易受到强风的袭击。|近义词：vulnerability, openness, unprotectedness|反义词: protection, shelter, concealment|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From exposed + -ness. Exposed ultimately derives from Latin *expausāre, meaning "to expose".
