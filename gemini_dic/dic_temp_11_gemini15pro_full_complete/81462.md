gratuitously:/ɡrəˈtuːɪtəsli/
adverb|无缘无故地，免费地|without good reason; unnecessarily; free of charge|He gratuitously insulted her in front of her friends. 他无缘无故地在朋友面前侮辱她.|Synonyms: unnecessarily, needlessly, wantonly, groundlessly|Antonyms: necessarily, justifiably, deservedly|词性: 副词

gratuitous:/ɡrəˈtuːɪtəs/
adjective|无缘无故的，免费的|given or done free of charge; uncalled for; unwarranted|The film was criticized for its gratuitous violence. 这部电影因其无端的暴力而受到批评.|Synonyms: free, complimentary, unnecessary, unwarranted|Antonyms: necessary, essential, justified|词性: 形容词
