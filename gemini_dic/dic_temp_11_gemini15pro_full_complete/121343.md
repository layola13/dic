misgotten:/ˌmɪsˈɡɒtn/ /ˌmɪsˈɡɑːtn/| adj.|非法获得的；不正当的| Obtained by improper or illegal means; wrongly acquired. | The police recovered the misgotten gains from the robbery. 警察找回了抢劫的不义之财。|  He spent his misgotten fortune on luxury cars. 他把非法获得的钱财花在了豪车上。| 近义词: ill-gotten, wrongly acquired, illegally obtained | 反义词: legitimate, legally obtained, honestly earned | 词性: adjective

词源:  From the Middle English *misgeten*, from the Old English *misgitan* "to get wrongly, obtain sinfully," from *mis-* "wrongly" + *gitan* "to get."
