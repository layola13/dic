nonthermoplastics:/ˌnɒnˌθɜːrməˈplæstɪks/ | n. | 非热塑性塑料 |  A class of polymers that do not soften and melt when heated, and cannot be reshaped repeatedly. They undergo irreversible chemical changes upon heating, typically becoming permanently hard. |  Nonthermoplastics, such as epoxy resins, are used in high-performance applications.  非热塑性塑料，例如环氧树脂，用于高性能应用。 | synonyms: thermosets, thermosetting plastics | antonyms: thermoplastics | noun
