horizontalization:/ˌhɔːrɪˌzɒntəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 水平化，横向化 | The process of making something horizontal or more horizontal; the act of spreading something out or extending it laterally; a shift towards a flatter, less hierarchical organizational structure. | The horizontalization of the company's management structure led to greater collaboration between departments. 公司管理结构的水平化促进了部门间的合作。 | synonyms: flattening, leveling, broadening, decentralization | antonyms: verticalization, centralization | noun
