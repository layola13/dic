narrow-gaged:/ˈnæroʊ ɡeɪdʒd/
adjective
窄轨距的
Having a narrower gauge than standard (especially of a railroad).
The narrow-gaged railway winds through the mountains.  窄轨铁路蜿蜒穿过群山。
The train chugged along the narrow-gaged tracks. 火车沿着窄轨轨道缓慢行驶。
Synonyms: narrow-gauge
Antonyms: broad-gaged, broad-gauge, standard-gauge
词性: adjective
