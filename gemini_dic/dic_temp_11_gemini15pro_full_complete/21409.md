bloviator:/ˈbloʊviˌeɪtər/| n.|空谈家，夸夸其谈的人|A person who talks at length, especially in an inflated or empty way.  Someone who bloviates. |The politician was known as a bloviator, making long speeches with little substance.|近义词：windbag, gasbag, blowhard|反义词：concise speaker, man/woman of few words|noun
