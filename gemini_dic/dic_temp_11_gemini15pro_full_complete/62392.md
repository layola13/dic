endocrinologist:/ˌɛndəkrɪˈnɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. | 内分泌学家 | A medical specialist who diagnoses and treats disorders of the endocrine system, which includes glands like the thyroid, pancreas, and adrenals. |  Example 1: My doctor referred me to an endocrinologist to manage my thyroid condition. 我的医生让我去看内分泌科医生来控制我的甲状腺疾病。

Example 2: The endocrinologist prescribed medication to regulate the patient's hormone levels. 内分泌学家开了药来调节病人的激素水平。 |  Synonym: hormone specialist | Antonym: N/A | noun
