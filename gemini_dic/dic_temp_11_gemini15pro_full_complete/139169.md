obituarian: /əˈbɪtʃuˌɛriən/ | n. | 讣告撰写人；讣告 | A person who writes obituaries, especially as a profession. | The obituarian captured the essence of the deceased's life in a touching tribute.  这位讣告撰写人在一篇感人的悼词中捕捉到了逝者生命的精髓。 | Synonyms: obituary writer, death notice writer | Antonyms: biographer (focuses on the whole life, not just death) | Noun

obituarian: /əˈbɪtʃuˌɛriən/ | adj. | 讣告的；有关讣告的 | Relating to or characteristic of obituaries. | He took an obituarian tone when discussing the recently deceased author.  在谈到最近去世的作家时，他用了讣告的语气。 | Synonyms: obituary, valedictory | Antonyms: celebratory, eulogistic (if focusing on positive aspects of life) | Adjective
