neglecter:/nɪˈɡlɛktər/| n.|疏忽者，忽略者|A person who neglects someone or something.  A person who fails to care for someone or something properly.|He was accused of being a neglecter of his children. 他被指控疏忽照顾孩子。|Synonyms: derelict, slacker, shirker, defaulter |Antonyms: caretaker, guardian, custodian|noun
