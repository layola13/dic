bewrayer:/bɪˈreɪər/
n.
背叛者，告密者
One who betrays; a traitor.
The bewrayer of the secret plan was never caught.  这个秘密计划的泄露者从未被抓住。
近义词:traitor, betrayer, informer, rat, snitch
反义词:loyalist, patriot
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *bewreyer, *bewreier, from bewreyen (“to betray, reveal, expose”) + -er. Equivalent to bewray +‎ -er.
