backspacing:/ˈbækˌspeɪsɪŋ/| n.|退格，回退|The constant backspacing showed her hesitation. 不断的退格显示出她的犹豫。|例句：She was backspacing furiously to correct her typo. 她狂按退格键纠正她的打字错误。

例句：The backspacing sound was the only noise in the quiet room.  退格的声音是安静房间里唯一的噪音。|近义词：deleting, erasing|反义词:typing, inserting|词性:名词 (Gerund)

词源: "backspace" comes from "back" (meaning backwards) and "space" (referring to the space bar or the space created by a character).  "Backspacing" is the present participle form of the verb "backspace," used as a noun (gerund). “backspace”来自“back”（意为向后）和“space”（指空格键或字符创建的空格）。 “backspacing”是动词“backspace”的现在分词形式，用作名词（动名词）。
