balcony:/ˈbælkəni/ /ˈbælkəni/| n.|阳台，露台|A platform projecting from the wall of a building, enclosed by a railing or balustrade.|We had breakfast on the balcony overlooking the sea. 我们在阳台上吃早餐，可以俯瞰大海。|近义词：veranda, terrace, porch|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps interior)|词性:noun

balcony:/ˈbælkəni/ /ˈbælkəni/| n.|包厢|A gallery or upper floor inside a building, especially a theater.|They had seats in the balcony for the opera. 他们在歌剧院的包厢里有座位。|近义词：gallery, mezzanine, loge|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps orchestra)|词性:noun
