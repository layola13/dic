forenames:/ˈfɔːrneɪmz/
n.
名字（尤指教名）
a person's first names (especially the ones used in official documents or registers)
His forenames are John and Paul. 他的名字是约翰和保罗。
synonyms: first names, given names, Christian names
antonyms: surname, last name, family name
noun
