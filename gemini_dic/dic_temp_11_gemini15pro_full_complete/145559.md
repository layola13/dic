overcompetitively: /ˌoʊvərˌkəmˈpɛtɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 过度竞争地，竞争过分地 |  In an excessively competitive manner; to a degree that is more competitive than is healthy or desirable. | He approached his studies overcompetitively, pushing himself to the point of exhaustion. 他学习过度竞争，把自己逼到筋疲力尽的地步。|  Example: She overcompetitively bid on the item, driving the price up far higher than its actual worth. 她过分竞争地竞拍这件商品，把价格抬高到远远超过其实际价值。 |Synonyms: excessively competitively, hypercompetitively, aggressively, ruthlessly | Antonyms: cooperatively, collaboratively, moderately | 词性: 副词


Etymology:
From "over" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "competitive" (from "compete" (Latin competere "to strive together") + "-itive" (suffix forming adjectives)) + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs).
