astute:/əˈstjuːt/ | adj. | 精明的，敏锐的，狡猾的 |  Having or showing an ability to accurately assess situations or people and turn this to one's advantage. |例句：He was an astute businessman who knew how to make a profit. 他是一位精明的商人，知道如何获利。 | 近义词：shrewd, sharp, clever, crafty, wily | 反义词：naive, foolish, gullible | 词性: 形容词

astutely:/əˈstjuːtli/ | adv. | 精明地，敏锐地，狡猾地 | In an astute manner. | 例句：She astutely observed the changing market trends. 她敏锐地观察到不断变化的市场趋势。 | 近义词：shrewdly, sharply, cleverly, craftily, wily | 反义词：naively, foolishly | 词性: 副词

astuteness:/əˈstjuːtnəs/ | n. | 精明，敏锐，狡猾 | The quality of being astute. | 例句：His astuteness allowed him to avoid the pitfalls of the investment. 他的精明让他避开了投资的陷阱。 | 近义词：shrewdness, sharpness, cleverness, craftiness, wiliness | 反义词：naivety, foolishness, gullibility | 词性: 名词
