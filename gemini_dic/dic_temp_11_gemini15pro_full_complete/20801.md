blase:/blɑːˈzeɪ/ /bleɪˈzeɪ/| adj.|厌倦的，玩腻的，漠不关心的|Unimpressed or indifferent to something because one has experienced or seen it so often before; world-weary.|例句：He became blase about the dangers of his job.  他对工作的危险性变得漠不关心了。

例句：Even the most jaded travelers were impressed by the beauty of the scenery. 即使是最厌倦的旅行者也对这美丽的景色印象深刻。|近义词：apathetic, indifferent, jaded, world-weary, uninterested|反义词：enthusiastic, excited, impressed, interested|词性:形容词

词源: French, past participle of blaser to satiate, probably of Germanic origin; akin to Old High German blasan to blow.
