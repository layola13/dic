chinoline: /ˈkɪnəliːn/ (US), /ˈkwɪnəliːn/ (UK)| n.|喹啉|A heterocyclic aromatic organic compound with the chemical formula C₉H₇N. It is a colorless hygroscopic liquid with a strong odor.  It is a structural isomer of isoquinoline.|Quinoline is used in the manufacture of dyes, pharmaceuticals, and other chemicals.|Synonym: 1-azanaphthalene, benzopyridine|Antonym: None|noun
