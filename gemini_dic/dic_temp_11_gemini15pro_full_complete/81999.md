grinting:/ˈɡrɪntɪŋ/ | adj. | 咬牙切齿的，咯吱作响的 | characterized by a grinding or gritting sound. |  He gave a grinting response. 他咬牙切齿地回答。 | synonyms: grinding, grating, rasping | antonyms: smooth, silent | adjective
