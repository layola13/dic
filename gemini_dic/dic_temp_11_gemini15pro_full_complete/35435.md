co-promoter:/ˌkəʊprəˈməʊtər/ | n. | 共同发起人，联合推广者 | A person or organization that promotes something jointly with another.  |  The concert was co-promoted by two local radio stations. 这场音乐会由两家当地广播电台联合推广。 |  The two companies were co-promoters of the new technology.  这两家公司是这项新技术的共同推广者。 | synonyms: joint promoter, co-organizer, partner | antonyms: sole promoter | noun
