co-labourer:/ˌkəʊˈleɪbərər/ (UK) /ˌkoʊˈleɪbərər/ (US)| n.|合作者，共同劳动者|A person who works together with another person or group, especially on a project or task.|例句：She found a willing co-labourer in her husband. 她丈夫是她乐意的合作伙伴。

例句：The project was a success thanks to the dedicated co-labourers.  由于尽职尽责的合作者们，该项目取得了成功。

近义词：collaborator, partner, associate, colleague, teammate|反义词:opponent, adversary, competitor|词性:noun
