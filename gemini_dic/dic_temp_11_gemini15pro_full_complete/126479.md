mythologizer:/ˌmɪθəˈlɒdʒaɪzər/
n.|神话创作者；神话讲述者；赋予神话色彩的人|Someone who mythologizes; a teller of myths; one who invests something with mythical qualities.|He was a great mythologizer, turning ordinary events into epic tales. 他是一位伟大的神话创作者，将平凡的事件变成了史诗般的故事。|myth-maker, storyteller, romanticizer|debunker, realist|noun


mythologize:/ˌmɪθəˈlɒdʒaɪz/
v.|将...神话化；赋予神话色彩；使...成为神话|To make something into a myth; to invest something with mythical qualities; to treat or describe as legendary.|The media tends to mythologize successful entrepreneurs, ignoring their struggles and failures. 媒体倾向于将成功的企业家神话化，而忽略了他们的奋斗和失败。|romanticize, idealize, glorify|demythologize, debunk|verb
