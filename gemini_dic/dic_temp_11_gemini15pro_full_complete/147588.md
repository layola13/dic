overpiteous:/ˌoʊvərˈpɪtiəs/| adj.|过度怜悯的，过于同情心的|Excessively or unduly piteous; exhibiting or feeling too much pity or compassion.  Characterized by excessive pity or sympathy, often to the point of being inappropriate or mawkish.|He cast an overpiteous glance at the beggar. 他对乞丐投以过度怜悯的目光。|Synonyms: overly compassionate, excessively sympathetic, mawkish, sentimental|Antonyms: unsympathetic, callous, hard-hearted|Word type: Adjective
