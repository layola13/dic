biarticulate:/ˌbaɪɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/
adjective
双关节的
Having two joints; joined by two articulations.
The biarticulate muscle spans two joints. 这块双关节肌肉跨越两个关节。

biarticulate:/ˌbaɪɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/
adjective
双音节的
Having two syllables.
The word "hello" is a biarticulate word.  “hello”是一个双音节词。


Etymology: From Latin bi- (two) + articulatus (jointed).

Synonyms: two-jointed (for anatomical sense), disyllabic (for phonetic sense)
Antonyms: monoarticulate, monosyllabic
