hands:/hændz/ | v. | 交；递；给 | Third-person singular simple present indicative form of hand. To pick something up and give it to someone. |  He hands her the book. 他把书递给她。 | Synonyms: pass, give, offer | Antonyms: take, receive, keep | Verb
