nonpreventions:/ˌnɒnprɪˈvɛnʃənz/
noun
非预防措施
The lack of nonpreventions led to a further spread of the disease.
缺乏非预防措施导致疾病进一步蔓延。
Nonpreventions refer to actions or strategies that do not specifically aim to prevent something from happening, but may indirectly contribute to a different outcome.  These are actions taken after an event or in response to an existing situation, rather than proactive measures to stop it from occurring in the first place.
例句：
The focus on nonpreventions, such as treatment and mitigation, rather than prevention, has been criticized.
与其关注预防，不如关注治疗和缓解等非预防措施的做法受到了批评。

The city invested heavily in nonpreventions after the flood.
洪水过后，这座城市在非预防措施上投入了大量资金。


近义词:mitigations,  remedies, responses, aftercare
反义词:preventions, proactive measures


词性:n.
