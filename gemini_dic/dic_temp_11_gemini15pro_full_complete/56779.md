dopiness:/ˈdoʊpinəs/| n. |昏沉，迟钝；困倦|A state of being drowsy, sluggish, or mentally slow, often due to the effects of drugs or medication. |例句：The patient exhibited dopiness after the surgery.  病人手术后显得很困倦。

例句：He blamed his dopiness on the allergy medication. 他把自己的迟钝归咎于过敏药物。|近义词：drowsiness, lethargy, sluggishness, stupor |反义词：alertness, wakefulness, vivacity|词性:名词
