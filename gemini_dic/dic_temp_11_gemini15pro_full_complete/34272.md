clar:/klær/ |  prefix | 清晰，清楚 |  Used as a prefix meaning "clear," "bright," or "loud." |  Clarify the instructions.  请说明指示。 | 近义词: clear, bright | 反义词: obscure, dim | 词缀

clarify:/ˈklærəfaɪ/ | vt. | 澄清，阐明 | To make (a statement or situation) less confused and more readily understandable. |  Could you clarify your earlier statement? 你能澄清一下你之前的陈述吗？

|  The new evidence clarified the situation. 新的证据澄清了情况。 | 近义词: explain, elucidate | 反义词: confuse, obscure | 动词

clarification:/ˌklærɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 澄清，说明 |  The act or process of making something clear or easier to understand. | We need some clarification on this issue. 我们需要对这个问题进行一些澄清。

|  His clarification was helpful. 他的澄清很有帮助。| 近义词: explanation, elucidation | 反义词: confusion, obfuscation | 名词

clarity:/ˈklærəti/ | n. | 清晰，明晰 | The quality of being clear or easy to understand. | The clarity of her writing is admirable. 她的写作清晰度令人钦佩。

|  The clarity of the water was amazing. 水的清澈度令人惊叹。 | 近义词: clearness, lucidity | 反义词: obscurity, vagueness | 名词
