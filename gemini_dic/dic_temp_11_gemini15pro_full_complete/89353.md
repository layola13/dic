homology:/həˈmɒlədʒi (UK), həˈmɑːlədʒi (US)/| n. |同源性，相似性|A degree of similarity, as in position or structure, and that may indicate a common origin.|The homology between the forelimbs of mammals and birds suggests a common ancestor. 哺乳动物和鸟类的前肢的同源性表明它们有共同的祖先。|synonyms: similarity, resemblance, likeness, correspondence|antonyms: difference, dissimilarity, divergence|noun

homology:/həˈmɒlədʒi (UK), həˈmɑːlədʒi (US)/| n. |(数学)同调|In mathematics, a relationship between mathematical objects, often used in topology and algebra.|The homology groups of a space provide information about its topological properties. 一个空间的同调群提供了关于其拓扑性质的信息。|synonyms: (in specific mathematical contexts) cohomology, isomorphism|antonyms: (lack of specific mathematical antonyms - depends on the context)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *homologie*, from Greek *homologos* "agreeing, corresponding," from *homos* "same" + *logos* "speech, word, reason."
