killock:/ˈkɪlək/| n.|小丘;小山;小岛(尤指苏格兰)|A small hill, mound, or knoll, especially in Scotland.|We climbed the killock to get a better view of the loch. 我们爬上小山，以便更好地欣赏湖景。|The sheep grazed peacefully on the killock. 羊群在小山丘上平静地吃草。|近义词：hillock, knoll, mound|反义词:valley, hollow|名词
