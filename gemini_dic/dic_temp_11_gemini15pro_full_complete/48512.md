deers:/dɪərz/ | n. | 鹿（复数，不标准用法）|  "Deers" is a non-standard plural form of "deer." The standard plural of "deer" is simply "deer." | Incorrect: I saw three deers in the field. | Correct: I saw three deer in the field. | 近义词: deer (correct plural), stag (male deer), doe (female deer), hart (male red deer) | 反义词: None (animal, no opposite) | 词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "dēor," of Proto-Germanic origin, related to Old Norse "dýr" and Dutch "dier," meaning "animal."
