aider's:/ˈeɪdərz/
noun
助手们的; 援助者们的
plural possessive form of aider (one who aids or helps) - belonging to or associated with multiple people who aid or help.
The aiders' contributions were invaluable to the success of the project.  援助者们的贡献对项目的成功至关重要。
The aiders' quick response saved many lives. 援助者们的快速反应拯救了许多生命。
Synonyms: helpers', assistants', supporters'
Antonyms: hinderers', obstructers'
词性: 名词


词源：
From the verb "aid," which comes from Old French "aider" (to help, assist), ultimately from Latin "adiutare" (to help, assist).  The "-er" suffix denotes someone who performs the action, and the "-s" marks the plural possessive.
