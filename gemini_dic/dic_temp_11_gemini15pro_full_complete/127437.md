nebbish:/ˈnɛbɪʃ/ (US), /ˈnɛbɪʃ/ (UK)| n. |懦弱的人；无用的人；窝囊废|A timid, inept, or ineffectual person. |例句：He was such a nebbish, always letting people walk all over him. 他真是个窝囊废，总是任人摆布。

例句：She felt like a nebbish for not speaking up at the meeting.  因为在会议上没有发言，她觉得自己很没用。


|近义词：wimp, milquetoast, weakling, pushover|反义词: assertive person, go-getter, leader|词性: noun
