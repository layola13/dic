endosperm:/ˈɛndəʊˌspɜːm/ (US) /ˈɛndoʊˌspɝːm/| n. |胚乳|The nutritive tissue surrounding the embryo within seeds of flowering plants.  It provides nourishment for the developing embryo.|The endosperm of a coconut is the white fleshy part we eat. 椰子中的胚乳就是我们食用的白色果肉部分。

|The wheat endosperm is ground into flour. 小麦的胚乳被磨成面粉。
|近义词: kernel, albumen|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) embryo|词性: noun
