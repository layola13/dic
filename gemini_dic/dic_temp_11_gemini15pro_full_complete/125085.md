mullerian:/mʌˈlɪəriən/ | adj. | 缪勒管的，缪勒氏的 | Relating to the Müllerian ducts, embryonic structures that develop into the female reproductive tract in mammals. | The mullerian ducts develop into the fallopian tubes, uterus, and upper vagina. 缪勒管会发育为输卵管、子宫和上阴道。|  The mullerian inhibiting hormone prevents the development of mullerian ducts in males.  抗缪勒管激素阻止男性体内缪勒管的发育。 | synonymous:  paramesonephric | antonymous: Wolffian | adjective

mullerian:/mʌˈlɪəriən/ | n. | 缪勒氏管；缪勒氏结构 |  The Müllerian duct; a paired duct of the embryo that gives rise to the female reproductive tract. |  Mutations in genes involved in mullerian development can cause various reproductive disorders. 参与缪勒管发育的基因突变会导致各种生殖系统疾病。 | The doctor explained the role of the mullerian ducts in female development. 医生解释了缪勒管在女性发育中的作用。 | synonymous: paramesonephric duct | antonymous: Wolffian duct | noun
