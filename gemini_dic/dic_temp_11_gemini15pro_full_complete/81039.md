graff:/ɡræf/| n.|涂鸦|Graffiti, typically illicit, consisting of words or drawings scratched or sprayed on a wall or other surface. |The walls were covered in colorful graff. 墙上满是五颜六色的涂鸦。|Synonym: graffiti, tagging, street art|Antonym: mural (sometimes), clean surface|noun
