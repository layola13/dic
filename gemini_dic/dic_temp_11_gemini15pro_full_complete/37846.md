competitioner:/kəmˈpɛtɪtər/| n.|竞争者，参赛者|A person who takes part in a sporting contest or other competition.|Example:  All the competitors lined up at the starting line. 所有参赛者都在起跑线上排队了。

Example:  He was a fierce competitor and always aimed for the top spot. 他是一个强劲的竞争者，总是以最高的名次为目标。|近义词:rival, contestant, opponent, participant|反义词:ally, partner, collaborator|词性:noun
