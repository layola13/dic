ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *feel, *fele, from Old French feel, feal (“faithful, loyal, trusty”), from Latin fidēlis (“faithful”), from fidēs (“faith, trust”).  Doublet of *fidelis*, *fidel*, and *fealty*.
