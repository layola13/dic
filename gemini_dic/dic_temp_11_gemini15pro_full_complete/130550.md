nonacquaintances:/ˌnɒnəˈkweɪntənsɪz/
noun
不熟悉的人；陌生人
People you do not know.
Example:  The party was full of nonacquaintances. 
聚会上满是陌生人。

Example:  She felt uncomfortable surrounded by so many nonacquaintances.
她被这么多不熟悉的人包围着，感到很不自在。

synonyms: strangers, outsiders
antonyms: acquaintances, friends, intimates
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + acquaintance (person one knows slightly).  Ultimately from Latin "ad" (to) + "cognoscere" (to know).
