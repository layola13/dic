nonenzymatically:/ˌnɑːnɛnzɪˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 非酶促地 | Without the involvement of enzymes.  This describes a chemical reaction or process that occurs without the catalytic action of an enzyme. | Glucose can react with proteins nonenzymatically. 葡萄糖可以与蛋白质非酶促地发生反应。|  Nonenzymatic browning of food occurs when sugars react with amino acids at high temperatures.  食物的非酶促褐变发生在糖类和氨基酸在高温下发生反应时。 | non-enzymatic, abiotically | enzymatically | adverb
