mythologize:/ˌmɪθəˈlɒdʒaɪz/
v.
将…神话化；将…神化
to make a person, place, or thing seem like a myth, or to create myths about them
The ancient Greeks mythologized their heroes. 古希腊人将他们的英雄人物神话化。
近义词：romanticize, idealize, glorify
反义词：demythologize, debunk
词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From mythology + -ize.  Mythology comes from the Greek mythologia, from mythos (story, tale) + -logia (speaking).
