austerities:/ɔːˈstɛrɪtiz/
|n.| 紧缩；苦行；严厉；朴素 |Sternness or severity of manner or attitude. Difficult economic conditions created by government measures to reduce public expenditure.|The government imposed austerity measures to reduce the deficit. 政府实施了紧缩措施以减少赤字。| synonyms:  stringency, asceticism, severity, hardship, rigor | antonyms: luxury, extravagance, indulgence, leniency | noun
