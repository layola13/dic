can't: /kɑːnt/ /kænt/ | modal verb | 不能，不会 | Contraction of "cannot," indicating inability or impossibility. | I can't swim. 我不会游泳。| 近义词: unable to, incapable of | 反义词: can, able to | 词性: modal verb

can't: /kɑːnt/ /kænt/ | modal verb | 不允许，禁止 | Contraction of "cannot," indicating prohibition or lack of permission. | You can't smoke here. 你不能在这里吸烟。| 近义词: mustn't, not allowed to | 反义词: can, may | 词性: modal verb


词源:  "Can't" is a contraction of "cannot," which originated from the Old English "ne can" (not be able).  "Can" itself comes from the Proto-Germanic *kunnaną (to know, be mentally able).
