edema:/ɪˈdiːmə/ (US), /iːˈdiːmə/ (UK)| n. |水肿|Swelling caused by excess fluid trapped in the body's tissues.|The patient presented with edema in the legs and feet. 病人的腿和脚出现了水肿。|Synonyms: swelling, puffiness, bloating|Antonyms: dehydration, shrinkage|noun

edema:/ɪˈdiːmə/ (US), /iːˈdiːmə/ (UK)| n. |浮肿|Swelling caused by excess fluid trapped in the body's tissues.|Facial edema can be a sign of an allergic reaction.  面部浮肿可能是过敏反应的迹象。|Synonyms: swelling, puffiness, bloating|Antonyms: dehydration, shrinkage|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek οἴδημα (oídēma) "swelling," from οἰδέω (oidéō) "I swell."
