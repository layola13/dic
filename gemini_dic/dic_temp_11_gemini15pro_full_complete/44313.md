croons:/kruːnz/
verb
低吟; 轻声唱; 哼唱
to sing or hum in a soft, gentle voice, especially in a sentimental way.
He crooned a lullaby to his baby daughter. 他低声哼唱摇篮曲哄他的宝贝女儿。
近义词: hums, sings softly, murmurs
反义词: shouts, yells, screams
词性: verb

croon:/kruːn/
noun
低吟; 轻声唱; 哼唱
a soft, gentle song or hum.
The mother's croon soothed the crying baby. 母亲的低吟声安抚了哭泣的婴儿。
近义词: hum, murmur, lullaby
反义词: shout, yell, scream
词性: noun
