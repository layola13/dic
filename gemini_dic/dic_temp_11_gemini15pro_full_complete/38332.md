conceitless:/kənˈsiːtlɪs/
adjective
没有自负的；谦逊的；不自傲的
Lacking conceit; modest; unassuming.
She was a remarkably conceitless woman, despite her considerable achievements.  她是一个非常谦逊的女人，尽管她取得了相当大的成就。
He gave a conceitless acceptance speech, thanking everyone who helped him. 他发表了谦逊的获奖感言，感谢了所有帮助过他的人。
近义词: humble, modest, unassuming, unpretentious
反义词: conceited, arrogant, vain, egotistical
词性: adjective
词源: From *conceit* (meaning "favorable opinion of oneself") + *-less* (suffix indicating absence).  由*conceit*（意为“对自己的好感”）+ *-less*（表示没有的后缀）构成。
