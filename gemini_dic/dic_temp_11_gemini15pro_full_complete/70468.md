figuro:/fiˈɡuːroʊ/ (US), /fɪˈɡjʊəroʊ/ (UK)| n.|人物；身影；外形；身材|A figure is the shape of a human body.  It can also refer to a person, especially a well-known or important one, or a character in a book, play, or film.  It can also mean a drawing or diagram, or a number.|

例句：
I saw a shadowy figure in the doorway.
我看到门口有一个模糊的身影。

A tall figure emerged from the fog.
一个高大的人影从雾中出现。


近义词：shape, form, outline, silhouette, person, individual, character, number, diagram|
反义词: shapelessness|
词性: noun
