nib:/nɪb/| n.|（钢笔的）笔尖|the writing point of a pen|The nib of my fountain pen is broken. 我的钢笔笔尖坏了。|synonyms: pen point, writing point|antonyms: N/A|noun

nib:/nɪb/| n.|少许，少量|a small amount of something|She added a nib of salt to the soup. 她往汤里加了一点盐。|synonyms: bit, dash, pinch|antonyms: lot, abundance|noun
