double-glaze:/ˈdʌbl ɡleɪz/ | v. | 双层玻璃 | To fit a window or door with double glazing. |  They decided to double-glaze all the windows in their house to improve insulation. 他们决定把房子里所有的窗户都装上双层玻璃以提高隔热性能。

They've double-glazed the front windows. 他们已经给前面的窗户装上了双层玻璃。 | 近义词: insulate | 反义词: single-glaze | 词性: 动词

double-glazing:/ˈdʌbl ˈɡleɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 双层玻璃 | Windows or doors that have two layers of glass with a space between them, designed to reduce heat loss and noise. | The double-glazing has made a huge difference to the warmth of the house. 双层玻璃使房子的保暖性大为改善。

New double-glazing will help reduce your energy bills. 新的双层玻璃将有助于减少您的能源费用。 | 近义词: insulation | 反义词: single glazing | 词性: 名词
