nonassaults:/nɒnəˈsɔːlts/ | n. |非攻击行为| Actions that do not involve physical or verbal attacks.  Absence of assaults. |例句：The police reported a decrease in nonassaults, suggesting a rise in more serious crimes. 警方报告非攻击性行为有所减少，这表明更严重的犯罪有所上升。

例句：Focusing on nonassaults, like property damage, can sometimes overshadow the need to address violent crime.  关注非攻击性行为，例如财产损失，有时会掩盖解决暴力犯罪的必要性。 | 近义词: nonviolence, pacifism | 反义词: assaults, attacks | 词性: noun

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "assaults" (noun, from Old French "asaut," meaning "attack").
