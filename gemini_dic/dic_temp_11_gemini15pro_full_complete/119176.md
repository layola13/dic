mezquita:/mesˈkiːtə/ /mɛθˈkitə/| n. |清真寺|A building used for Muslim worship.  From Spanish *mezquita*, from Arabic *masjid* (place of prostration in prayer).|The mezquita in Córdoba is a stunning example of Moorish architecture. 科尔多瓦的清真寺是摩尔式建筑的惊人典范。|mosque, masjid|church, temple|noun
