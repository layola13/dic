consanguineous:/ˌkɒnsæŋˈɡwɪniəs/ /ˌkɑːnsæŋˈɡwɪniəs/| adj.|血缘的，同血统的，有血亲关系的|Related by blood; descended from the same ancestor.|They were close friends, though not consanguineous. 他们是亲密的朋友，虽然没有血缘关系。|Synonyms: kindred, related, cognate|Antonyms: unrelated, non-consanguineous|adjective


consanguined:/kənˈsæŋɡwɪnd/ /kɑːnˈsæŋɡwɪnd/| adj.|有血缘关系的，同血统的|Related by blood; descended from the same ancestor.  Often used in a more literary or formal context than "consanguineous".|The consanguined families gathered for the annual reunion.  这些有血缘关系的家庭聚集在一起举行年度聚会。|Synonyms: kindred, related, consanguineous, cognate|Antonyms: unrelated, non-consanguineous|adjective
