deathlier:/ˈdɛdliər/ | adj. | 更致命的，更危险的 | More likely to cause death; more dangerous. | The virus mutated into a deathlier strain. 病毒变异成了更致命的毒株。|  The new weapon is deathlier than any before it.  这种新武器比以往任何武器都更致命。 | 近义词：more lethal, more dangerous, more deadly | 反义词：safer, less dangerous, less lethal | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Old English *deadlic, from dead "dead" + -lic "-ly."  The comparative form "deathlier" is formed by adding "-er."
