enquire:/ɪnˈkwaɪə(r)/ (British English), /ɪnˈkwaɪr/ (American English)| v.| 询问，打听 |To ask for information from someone. | I enquired about train times. 我询问了火车时刻表。|  synonyms: ask, inquire, query | antonyms: answer, reply, respond | verb

enquire:/ɪnˈkwaɪə(r)/ (British English), /ɪnˈkwaɪr/ (American English)| v.| 调查，查究 |To make a formal investigation. | The police are enquiring into the matter. 警方正在调查此事。| synonyms: investigate, examine, explore | antonyms: ignore, neglect, overlook | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French enquerre, based on Latin inquirere "to seek into, search for," from in- "into" + quaerere "to seek, ask."
