argentamine:/ɑːrˈdʒɛntəmiːn/| n.|银胺，银氨溶液|A solution of silver nitrate in ammonia, used as a reagent, antiseptic, and staining agent.  It is also called Tollens' reagent and is used to test for aldehydes.|Example: Argentamine is used to silver mirrors. 银胺用于镀银镜。

Example: Argentamine stains proteins brown. 银胺将蛋白质染成棕色。|近义词:Tollens' reagent, ammoniacal silver nitrate|反义词: None|词性:noun
