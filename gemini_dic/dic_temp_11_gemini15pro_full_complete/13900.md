augustean: /ɔːˈɡʌstiən/ | adj. | 奥古斯都式的，庄严的，古典的 | Relating to the Roman emperor Augustus or his age; marked by majestic dignity or grandeur; classically simple and harmonious. | The building has an augustean facade. 这座建筑有着奥古斯都式的宏伟正面。|  The museum's augustean halls were filled with ancient artifacts. 博物馆庄严的大厅里摆满了古代文物。 | 近义词: majestic, grand, stately, dignified, classical, imperial | 反义词: humble, modest, simple, plain, unpretentious | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Augustus (name of the first Roman emperor) + -an (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin *augere "to increase."
