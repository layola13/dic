gradualists:/ˈɡrædʒuəlɪsts/
n.
渐进主义者；渐变论者
Those who believe in gradual rather than sudden or revolutionary change.
Gradualists argue that change should happen slowly over time. 渐进主义者认为变革应该随着时间的推移缓慢发生。
The gradualists preferred a step-by-step approach to reform. 渐进主义者更喜欢逐步改革的方法。
近义词:moderates, conservatives
反义词:radicals, revolutionaries
词性:noun
