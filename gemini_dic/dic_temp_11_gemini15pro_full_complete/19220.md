bezier: /ˈbeɪzieɪ/ (US), /ˈbɛzɪeɪ/ (UK) | n. | 贝塞尔曲线 | A type of mathematically defined curve used in computer graphics and other fields. It is defined by a set of control points that influence the curve's shape. |  A Bézier curve is commonly used in vector graphics editors. 贝塞尔曲线常用于矢量图形编辑器。

The font designer used Bézier curves to create the smooth outlines of the letters. 字体设计师使用贝塞尔曲线来创建字母的平滑轮廓。 | 近义词: spline | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
