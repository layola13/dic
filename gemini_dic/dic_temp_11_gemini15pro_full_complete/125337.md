multilateralize: /ˌmʌltiˈlætərəlaɪz/ | v. | 多边化；使多边化 | To make something involve many different countries or groups. |  The government is trying to multilateralize the trade negotiations. 政府正试图将贸易谈判多边化。 |  The company decided to multilateralize its operations by opening branches in several countries.  该公司决定通过在几个国家开设分支机构来实现其运营的多边化。 | synonyms: internationalize, globalize | antonyms: unilateralize, bilateralize | verb

multilateralization: /ˌmʌltiˌlætərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 多边化 | The act or process of making something involve many different countries or groups. | The multilateralization of trade is seen as a positive development. 贸易多边化被视为一种积极的发展。 | The report discussed the multilateralization of peacekeeping operations. 该报告讨论了维和行动的多边化。 | synonyms: internationalization, globalization | antonyms: unilateralization, bilateralization | noun
