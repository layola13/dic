ado:/əˈduː/ | n. | 忙乱，骚动，麻烦 |fuss, trouble, commotion, excitement  |  There was a great ado about nothing.  没什么大不了的事，却闹得沸沸扬扬。 |fuss, commotion, bustle, flurry | calmness, peace, quiet | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *at do, from Old English æt dō (“at do”).  Essentially, it means "at doing," referring to a state of activity or fuss.
