hemispheric:/ˌhemɪˈsfɪrɪk/ /ˌhemɪˈsfɛrɪk/
adjective
半球的
Relating to or affecting a hemisphere of the earth or brain.
The hemispheric specialization of the brain is a fascinating area of study. 大脑的半球专门化是一个引人入胜的研究领域。

Scientists are studying the hemispheric differences in brain activity. 科学家正在研究大脑活动中的半球差异。

synonym:global, worldwide
antonym:local, regional
