fumerell:/ˈfjuːmərel/ | n. | 烟囱，通风口 | A small chimney or vent, especially for conveying smoke or fumes. | The fumerell on the roof vented the smoke from the fireplace. 屋顶上的烟囱排出了壁炉里的烟。| synonyms: vent, flue, chimney, outlet | antonyms: inlet, intake | noun
