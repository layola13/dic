mammonitish:/mæˈmɒnɪtɪʃ/
adj.
拜金的，贪婪的
Characterized by a devotion to riches or material wealth; greedy.
He lived a mammonitish lifestyle, always chasing the next big deal. 他过着拜金的生活，总是追逐下一个大交易。
The mammonitish banker cared little for the plight of the poor.  那个贪婪的银行家对穷人的困境漠不关心。
近义词: avaricious, greedy, materialistic, mercenary
反义词: generous, charitable, altruistic
词性: adjective
词源: From Mammon, the Aramaic word for riches, personified in the New Testament as a false god.
