counterblockades:/ˌkaʊntərblɒˈkeɪdz/ | n. |反封锁，对抗封锁| An action taken to prevent an enemy's blockade from being effective, typically by blockading their ports in return. | The navy implemented counterblockades to protect their own supply lines from the enemy's blockade. 海军实施了反封锁，以保护自己的补给线免受敌人的封锁。| The counterblockades were successful in disrupting the enemy's trade. 反封锁成功地扰乱了敌人的贸易。 |Synonyms: countermeasures, retaliation | Antonyms: blockade, siege | noun
