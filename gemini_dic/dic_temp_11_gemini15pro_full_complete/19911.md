bio:/ˈbaɪoʊ/| n. |个人简介，简历|A short biographical sketch, especially one used for social media or online profiles.  It gives a brief overview of someone's life and achievements. | Example: Her bio on Twitter is very witty. 她的推特简介非常诙谐。

Example: He needs to update his bio on LinkedIn. 他需要更新他的领英简介。|近义词：profile, résumé, CV|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


bio:/ˈbaɪoʊ/| prefix |生物，生命|A combining form meaning “life” occurring in loanwords from Greek (biology); used in the formation of compound words (biodegradable).| Example: Bioethics is a complex field. 生物伦理学是一个复杂的领域。
