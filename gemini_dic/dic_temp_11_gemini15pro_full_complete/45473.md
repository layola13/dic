curbed:/kɜːrbd/ /kɝːbd/| v. adj.|抑制；控制；约束；装有路缘的|To restrain or control effectively; to check or repress. Also, furnished with a curb, especially of a street.|例句：The government curbed spending on social programs. 政府控制了社会项目的开支。|近义词：restrained, checked, suppressed, controlled|反义词：unleashed, unrestrained, encouraged|词性:动词/形容词
curbed:/kɜːrbd/ /kɝːbd/| adj.|装有路缘的|Furnished with a curb, especially a street.|例句：The car parked along the curbed street. 汽车停在装有路缘的街道旁。|近义词：edged, bordered|反义词：uncurbed|词性:形容词
