noise's:/nɔɪzɪz/
noun
噪音的；喧闹的
Possessive form of noise.  Belongs to the noise.
The noise's intensity made it hard to sleep. 噪音的强度使人难以入睡。
The machine's noise's sudden increase alerted the workers.  机器噪音的突然增加使工人们警觉起来。
Synonyms: sound's, din's, racket's
Antonyms: silence's, quiet's
