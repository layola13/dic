macrosporic:/ˌmækrəˈspɔːrɪk/
adjective
大孢子的
Producing or pertaining to macrospores; having megaspores.
The macrosporic plant produces larger spores.  这种大孢子植物产生较大的孢子。
The fern exhibits a macrosporic life cycle.  这种蕨类植物表现出大孢子生命周期。
近义词:megasporic
反义词:microsporic
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From macro- (large) + -sporic (related to spores), ultimately from Greek *makros* (large) and *spora* (seed).
