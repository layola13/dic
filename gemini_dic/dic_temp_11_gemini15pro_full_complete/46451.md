daguerreotypic:/dəˈɡɛrətaɪpɪk/
adjective
银版照相法的；像银版照片的
Relating to or characteristic of a daguerreotype.
The museum displayed several daguerreotypic portraits of 19th-century figures. 博物馆展出了几幅19世纪人物的银版肖像。
The image had a daguerreotypic sharpness and clarity. 这幅图像具有银版照片般的清晰度。
Synonyms: photographic, antiquated, vintage
Antonyms: modern, contemporary
adjective


daguerreotype:/dəˈɡɛrətaɪp/
noun
银版照片；银版照相法
A photograph taken by an early photographic process employing an iodine-sensitized silvered plate and mercury vapor.
The daguerreotype was one of the earliest forms of photography. 银版照相法是最早的摄影形式之一。
He found an old daguerreotype of his great-grandparents in the attic. 他在阁楼里找到了一张他曾祖父母的旧银版照片。
Synonyms: photograph, image, picture
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could be considered the opposite of modern photographic methods)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Louis-Jacques-Mandé Daguerre (1787–1851), French artist and inventor of the process, + -type.
