cobiron:/ˈkoʊbaɪərn/| n. |烤肉叉，烤肉铁架|A cobiron is a metal stand used for supporting cooking pots or spits over a fire, especially in a fireplace.|The chef used a cobiron to roast the chicken over the open flames.|近义词：spit, andiron, fire dog|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *cobyrne, alteration of Middle English colyren, from Old French chaluëron, from Vulgar Latin *calidarium ferrum (“heating iron”).
