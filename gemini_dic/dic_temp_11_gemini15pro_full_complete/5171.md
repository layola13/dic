alleviates:/əˈliːvieɪts/
verb
减轻，缓和
makes something less severe, serious, or painful
Taking a hot bath alleviates muscle soreness.  泡热水澡可以缓解肌肉酸痛。
The medicine alleviates the symptoms of the flu.  这种药可以减轻流感的症状。
Synonyms: eases, relieves, lessens, mitigates, softens
Antonyms: aggravates, worsens, exacerbates, intensifies
词性:动词
