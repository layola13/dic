haruspice: /ˈhærəspaɪs/ | n. | 占卜者；预言家 | A religious official in ancient Rome who interpreted omens, especially from the entrails of sacrificial animals. |  The haruspice examined the liver of the sacrificed ox, seeking signs from the gods.  预言家检查了祭祀用的牛的肝脏，寻找来自神的迹象。

| Synonym: soothsayer, diviner, prophet, augur | Antonym: skeptic | noun
