forbearingly:/fɔːrˈbɛərɪŋli/
adv.
宽容地；忍耐地；克制地
in a patient and restrained manner; tolerantly.
He forbearingly accepted her constant interruptions during the meeting. 他在会议期间宽容地接受了她不断的打断。
The teacher forbearingly explained the concept for the third time. 老师耐心地第三次解释这个概念。
近义词: patiently, tolerantly, leniently, indulgently
反义词: impatiently, intolerantly, harshly, severely
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From forbear + -ing + -ly.  Forbear comes from Old English forberan, from for- "completely" + beran "to bear, carry, endure".
