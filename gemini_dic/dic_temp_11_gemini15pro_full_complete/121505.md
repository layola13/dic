mislead:/ˌmɪsˈliːd/ | v. | 误导；使产生错误想法 | to cause someone to have a wrong idea or impression | The map misled us and we ended up lost. 地图误导了我们，我们最终迷路了。| synonyms: deceive, delude, fool | antonyms: guide, enlighten, inform | verb
