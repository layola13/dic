frampoid:/ˈfræmpɔɪd/| adj.|古怪的，暴躁的|Resembling a framp; irritable and peevish.|He had a frampoid look on his face after being woken up so early. 他被这么早叫醒，脸上露出了暴躁的表情。|近义词：crabby, grumpy, irritable, cantankerous, churlish|反义词：pleasant, agreeable, affable|词性:形容词


Etymology:  Likely a humorous coinage, possibly influenced by "frampold" (also meaning peevish or irritable) and words like "rhomboid," suggesting a twisted or distorted shape, perhaps metaphorically relating to a sour disposition.  The exact origin is obscure.
