confederative:/kənˈfɛdərətɪv/
adjective
邦联的；联盟的
Relating to or based on a confederation, a union of states or groups, especially one where each member retains considerable independence.
The confederative structure allowed each state to maintain its own laws while still working together on common issues.  邦联结构允许每个州保持自己的法律，同时仍在共同问题上合作。
The newly formed confederative government faced numerous challenges. 新成立的邦联政府面临着许多挑战。
近义词: federal, allied, united, joint
反义词: centralized, unitary, independent
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *confederativus, from Latin confœderatus (past participle of confœderare ‘to unite by league’) + -ivus.
