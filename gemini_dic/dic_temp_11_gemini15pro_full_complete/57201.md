down-the-tubes:/ˌdaʊn ðə ˈtuːbz/| idiom | 失败；垮掉；完蛋 | Going down the tubes describes something that is failing or becoming ruined.  It implies a rapid and often irreversible decline. |  The company's profits are down the tubes this year.  这家公司今年的利润一落千丈。| synonyms: failing, collapsing, deteriorating, going to pot, going downhill | antonyms: improving, flourishing, thriving | idiom
