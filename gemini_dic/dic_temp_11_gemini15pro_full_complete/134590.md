nonlubricants:/ˌnɒnˈluːbrɪkənts/ | n. | 非润滑剂 | Substances that do not reduce friction between surfaces. They may even increase friction. |  These nonlubricants were used in the experiment to create a high-friction environment.  这些非润滑剂被用于实验中以创造高摩擦环境。 |  abrasives, desiccants | lubricants | noun
