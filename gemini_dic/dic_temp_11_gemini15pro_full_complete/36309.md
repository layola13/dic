cohesiveness:/koʊˈhiːsɪvnəs/| n. |凝聚力；黏着性|The quality of being cohesive; the state of sticking together.|The cohesiveness of the group was remarkable, despite their differing backgrounds.  这个群体的凝聚力非常强，尽管他们的背景各不相同。|Synonyms: unity, solidarity, coherence, adhesion|Antonyms: disunity, division, fragmentation|noun


cohesive:/koʊˈhiːsɪv/| adj. |有凝聚力的；黏着的|Characterized by or causing cohesion; sticking together.|A cohesive team is essential for success. 一个有凝聚力的团队对于成功至关重要。|Synonyms: united, connected, coherent, adhesive|Antonyms: disunited, disconnected, fragmented|adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *cohaesivus, from Latin cohaes-, past participle stem of cohaerēre "to cleave together," from co- "together" + haerēre "to stick."
