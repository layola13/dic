inequilateral:/ˌɪniˈkwɪlætərəl/
adjective
不等边的；不规则的
Having sides of unequal length.
例句：An inequilateral triangle has three sides of different lengths. 一个不等边三角形有三条不同长度的边。

例句：The building's inequilateral design made it stand out from the surrounding structures.  这座建筑不等边的设计使它在周围的建筑中脱颖而出。

近义词：asymmetrical, irregular, unequal
反义词：equilateral, regular, equal
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inaequilateralis*, from in- (not) + aequilateralis (equilateral), from aequus (equal) + latus (side).
