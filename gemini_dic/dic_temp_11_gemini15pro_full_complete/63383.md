entoplastic:/ˌɛntəˈplæstɪk/ | adj. | 内塑的，在体内发生的，由内部产生的 | Relating to processes of growth or change originating within an organism or cell. | Example 1: Entoplastic phenomena are crucial for understanding cellular development.  细胞发育的理解离不开内塑现象。 | Example 2: The scientist studied the entoplastic formation of bone tissue.  科学家研究了骨组织的内塑形成。 | 近义词: endogenous, internal | 反义词: exogenous, external | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek "ento-" (within) + "plastikos" (capable of being molded).  源自希腊语 "ento-" （在内部） + "plastikos" （可塑的）。
