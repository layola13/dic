impetus:/ˈɪmpɪtəs/ (US), /ˈɪmpɪtəs/ (UK)| n.|推动力；刺激；促进|The impetus for the change came from the new CEO. 变化的推动力来自新任首席执行官。|The discovery of gold gave a new impetus to the development of the town. 黄金的发现为该镇的发展注入了新的活力。|近义词：momentum, incentive, stimulus, drive, impulse|反义词：deterrent, hindrance, obstacle|词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *impetus*, from Latin *impetere* "to attack, assail," from *in-* "into, in, on, upon" + *petere* "to go to, seek, aim at, attack."
