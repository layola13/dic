antimethodical:/ˌæntiːməˈθɒdɪkəl/ | adj. | 没有条理的，杂乱无章的 | Lacking method or order; unsystematic. | Her antimethodical approach to packing resulted in a chaotic suitcase. 她杂乱无章的打包方式导致行李箱一片混乱。|  He has a very antimethodical way of working, jumping from one task to another without finishing anything. 他工作方式毫无条理，在没有完成任何事情的情况下从一项任务跳到另一项任务。 | synonyms: disorganized, disorderly, chaotic, haphazard, unsystematic | antonyms: methodical, systematic, organized, orderly | word type: adjective
