beheld:/bɪˈheld/| v.|注视，看见|To have seen or observed something, especially something impressive or remarkable.|例句：She beheld the magnificent sunset with awe. 她敬畏地注视着壮丽的日落。|例句：The crowd beheld the king's arrival.  人群注视着国王的到来。|近义词：saw, observed, witnessed, viewed|反义词：ignored, overlooked|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English biholden, from Old English bihealden (“to hold by, observe, watch, behold, retain, occupy, celebrate”), from be- + healdan (“to hold, keep, guard”). Cognate with Dutch behouden (“to retain, preserve”), German behalten (“to keep, retain”).
