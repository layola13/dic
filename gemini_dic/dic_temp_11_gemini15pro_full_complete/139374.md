obsequience:/ɒbˈsiːkwɪəns/ /əbˈsiːkwiəns/ | n. | 顺从，恭顺 |  The act of obeying or serving someone with excessive willingness; subservient compliance. |  His obsequience to the emperor was evident in his every word and gesture. 他对皇帝的顺从体现在他的一言一行中。| synonyms: subservience, compliance, deference, ductility | antonyms: defiance, disobedience, resistance | noun

obsequious:/əbˈsiːkwɪəs/ /ɒbˈsiːkwɪəs/ | adj. | 谄媚的，奉承的 |  Characterized by or showing servile complaisance or deference; fawning. |  The obsequious waiter bowed deeply as he presented the menu.  那个谄媚的侍者在递上菜单时深深地鞠了一躬。 | synonyms: servile, fawning, sycophantic, flattering | antonyms: assertive, independent, rebellious | adjective
