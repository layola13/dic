hoodednesses:/ˈhʊdɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
戴帽的状态；覆盖的状态；隐蔽的状态
The state or quality of being hooded or having a hood.  The degree to which something is hooded.
The hoodednesses of the figures added to the mystery of the scene.  人物戴着兜帽的状态增添了场景的神秘感。

近义词: concealment, covering, disguise
反义词: exposure, openness, revelation
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hooded + -ness.  "Hooded" comes from "hood" (Old English hōd), meaning a covering for the head.  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
