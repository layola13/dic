nonrepayable:/ˌnɒnriˈpeɪəbl/ /ˌnɑːnriˈpeɪəbl/
adjective
不可偿还的
Not required to be repaid; given as a gift or grant.
The government offered nonrepayable grants to small businesses affected by the pandemic. 政府向受疫情影响的小企业提供不可偿还的赠款。
This is a nonrepayable loan, essentially a grant. 这是一笔不可偿还的贷款，本质上是一笔赠款。
近义词:non-refundable, grant, gift
反义词:repayable, loan
