misrepresentation:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪzɛnˈteɪʃən/
|n.|虚假陈述，歪曲事实|The act of presenting something falsely or inaccurately. 对某事物进行虚假或不准确的描述的行为。|
|例句：He was accused of misrepresentation in his advertising. 他被指控在广告中歪曲事实。|
|例句：The lawyer argued that the witness’s testimony was a misrepresentation of the facts. 律师辩称证人的证词歪曲了事实。|
|近义词：distortion, falsification, lie, inaccuracy|
|反义词：truth, fact, accurate representation|
|词性：名词|

词源：
From mis- + representation.  由mis-（错误地）+ representation（陈述）构成。
