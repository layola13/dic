epizoon:/ˈɛpɪˌzoʊɒn/ | n. | 体外寄生动物 | An animal that lives on the exterior of another living animal.  |  Example 1: The epizoon was barely visible on the whale's skin. 这只体外寄生动物在鲸鱼的皮肤上几乎看不见。

Example 2: Several epizoa were found attached to the crab's shell.  在螃蟹壳上发现了几个体外寄生动物。 | Near synonyms: ectoparasite, commensal | Antonym: endoparasite | Noun
