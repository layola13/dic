for-their-part:/fɔːr ðɛər pɑːrt/| phrasal idiom |就他们而言；至于他们；对他们来说| This idiom is used to introduce the actions or opinions of a particular group, often in contrast to another group. It emphasizes the specific contribution or perspective of the group mentioned. |For their part, the students were eager to learn. 至于学生们，他们渴望学习。

|For their part, the company denied any wrongdoing. 就公司而言，他们否认有任何不当行为。|Synonyms: as for them, on their side, from their perspective|Antonyms: N/A (idioms generally don't have direct antonyms)|phrase
