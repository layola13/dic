albuminate:/ælˈbjuːmɪneɪt/
n.|白蛋白酸盐；白蛋白化合物|A compound of albumin with a base or an acid. |Albuminates are formed by the action of acids or alkalis on albumin. 白蛋白酸盐是由酸或碱作用于白蛋白而形成的。|The researchers studied the properties of various albuminates. 研究人员研究了各种白蛋白酸盐的性质。|近义词: protein salt, albumin compound|反义词: None|词性: noun

albuminate:/ælˈbjuːmɪneɪt/
v.|使成白蛋白化合物|To combine with albumin; to convert into albuminate. |The addition of acid albuminates the protein. 添加酸使蛋白质变成白蛋白化合物。|The process albuminates the milk proteins. 这个过程使牛奶蛋白变成白蛋白化合物。|近义词: combine with albumin|反义词: None|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *albuminatus*, past participle of *albuminare* ("to whiten"), from *albumen* ("white of egg") + *-atus* (past participle suffix).  The word "albumen" itself comes from *albus* ("white").
