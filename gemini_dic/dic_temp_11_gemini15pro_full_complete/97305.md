inform:/ɪnˈfɔːrm/ (US) /ɪnˈfɔːm/ (UK)| v. | 通知；告知；报告 | to give someone facts or information; to tell someone about something |  I informed him of my decision. 我告诉了他我的决定。

|近义词：tell, notify, apprise, advise |反义词: withhold, conceal |verb

inform:/ɪnˈfɔːrm/(US) /ɪnˈfɔːm/(UK) | adj. | 提供信息的；教育性的 | giving a lot of useful facts  | This is an incredibly informative book. 这是一本信息量很大的书。|近义词：instructive, enlightening, educational |反义词: uninformative |adjective
