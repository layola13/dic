cotise:/kɔˈtiz/| n.| 会费，分摊额，捐款 |A fixed amount of money that you regularly pay to be a member of a club, or to help a charity, or the act of paying this money. | Example: Members pay an annual cotise. 会员支付年费。| Example:  The monthly cotise is 20 euros. 每月的会费是20欧元。|Synonyms: contribution, dues, subscription, levy| Antonyms: receipt, income| Noun
