die:/daɪ/
verb
死亡，消亡
to stop living; to become dead.
He died of a heart attack. 他死于心脏病发作。

die:/daɪ/
noun
骰子 (常用复数 dice)
A small cube marked on each side with one to six spots, used in games of chance.
Roll the dice to see who goes first. 掷骰子决定谁先走。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *deyja, from Proto-Germanic *dawjaną (“to die”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰew- (“to disappear, cease”). Cognates include Old English dēaþ (“death”), Old Frisian dāia (“to die”), Old Saxon dōian (“to die”), Old High German touwen (“to die”), Gothic 𐌳𐌰𐌿𐌸𐍃 (dauþs, “death”).  Doublet of *dīe.


SYNONYMS->
perish, expire, decease, succumb

ANTONYMS->
live, survive, be born
