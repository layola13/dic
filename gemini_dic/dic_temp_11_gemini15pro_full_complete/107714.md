lanthopine:/ˈlænθəpaɪn/| n. |羊毛状生物碱|An alkaloid found in opium and some species of cacti.  It is a dimer of two molecules of laudanosine.|Lanthopine was isolated from the cactus. 从仙人掌中分离出了羊毛状生物碱。|Synonym:  opium alkaloid|Antonym: None|noun
