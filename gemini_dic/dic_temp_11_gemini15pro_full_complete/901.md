aburst:/əˈbɜːrst/
adj.
充满的，洋溢的
Full of; overflowing with.
The garden was aburst with color. 花园里充满了色彩。
近义词:awash, brimming, overflowing, teeming
反义词:empty, devoid, lacking
词性:形容词

Etymology: From a- (meaning "on," "in," "at," etc.) + burst (meaning "to break open suddenly and violently").  The sense evolved from the image of something so full that it is about to burst open.
