词源：This phrase originated in gambling, specifically in poker, where "broke" means having no money left.  Going "for broke" meant betting all of one's remaining chips in a single hand.  Over time, the phrase expanded to encompass any situation where someone risks everything to achieve a goal. 这个词组起源于赌博，尤其是在扑克牌游戏中，“broke”的意思是身无分文。“go for broke”指的是在一局牌中押上所有剩余的筹码。随着时间的推移，这个词组的含义扩展到任何为了实现目标而孤注一掷的情况。
