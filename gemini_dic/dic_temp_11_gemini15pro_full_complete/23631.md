branchiostege: /ˌbræŋkiˈɒstiːdʒ/ | n. |鳃盖|The bony operculum covering the gills of fishes.  A bony plate or ray supporting the gill membrane of a fish.|The branchiostege plays a vital role in respiration. 鳃盖在呼吸中起着至关重要的作用。|The fish flared its branchiostege. 这条鱼张开了鳃盖。|Synonym: operculum, gill cover|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "gill slit" as the opening it covers)|noun
