oligarchize: /ˌɒlɪˈɡɑːkaɪz/ | v. | 寡头政治化，形成寡头统治 | To cause to become an oligarchy; to concentrate power in the hands of a few. | The country's political system has been oligarchized by a small group of wealthy elites.  这个国家的政治体系已经被一小撮富有的精英寡头政治化了。|  The new laws will further oligarchize the media landscape. 新法律将进一步加剧媒体领域的寡头垄断。 | 近义词: monopolize, centralize | 反义词: democratize, decentralize | 词性: 动词

oligarchization: /ˌɒlɪˌɡɑːkaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 寡头政治化，形成寡头统治的过程 | The process of becoming an oligarchy; the concentration of power in the hands of a few. | The oligarchization of the media is a worrying trend. 媒体的寡头垄断是一个令人担忧的趋势。| The report detailed the oligarchization of the country's economy.  该报告详细描述了该国经济的寡头垄断过程。| 近义词: monopolization, centralization | 反义词: democratization, decentralization | 词性: 名词

词源:  From oligarchy + -ize.  Oligarchy comes from Greek *oligarkhia*, from *oligos* "few" + *arkhein* "to rule".
