aeration:/ˌeəˈreɪʃən/
|n.|通风，透气，曝气|The process of increasing the oxygen content of something, such as soil or water.|例句：Regular aeration is essential for a healthy lawn. 定期通风对健康的草坪至关重要。|近义词：ventilation, oxygenation|反义词:suffocation, asphyxiation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aerationem, from Latin aeratus, past participle of aerare "to air," from aer "air."
