clock-out:/klɑːk aʊt/ | n. vi. | 下班打卡，打卡下班 | The act of recording the time one finishes work, typically by using a time clock or electronic system. |  I clock out at 5 pm every day. 我每天下午5点下班打卡。

She clocked out and went home. 她打卡下班回家了。 | 近义词: clock off, punch out, sign out | 反义词: clock in, clock on, punch in, sign in | 词性: 名词, 动词
