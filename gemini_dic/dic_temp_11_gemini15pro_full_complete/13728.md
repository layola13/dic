aubade:/ɔːˈbɑːd/ | n. | 晨歌；清晨情歌 | A song or poem greeting the dawn, typically expressing the regret of lovers parting at daybreak. |  The poet composed a tender aubade to his beloved as the sun began to rise. 诗人在太阳升起时为他心爱的人创作了一首温柔的晨歌。

The musician played a beautiful aubade on his lute.  音乐家用他的鲁特琴演奏了一首优美的晨歌。 | 近义词: serenade, morning song | 反义词: nocturne, evensong | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aubade*, from Provençal *albada*, from *alba* "dawn," from Latin *alba* (feminine of *albus* "white").
