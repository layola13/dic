degraders:/dɪˈɡreɪdərz/| n.|降级器，降解剂，降解者|Substances or organisms that break down or degrade a material, often a complex organic molecule.  They can be enzymes, chemicals, or microorganisms.  Protein degraders are a class of drugs that induce targeted protein degradation.| Scientists are developing new degraders to target specific disease-causing proteins. 科学家正在开发新的降解剂来靶向特定的致病蛋白.|Synonyms: decomposers, destructors, disintegrators|Antonyms: builders, constructors, synthesizers|Noun

degrade:/dɪˈɡreɪd/| v.|降解，降低，退化|To break down or decompose a substance, typically a complex organic molecule, into simpler substances. Also, to reduce in quality or value; to demote or lower in rank or status.|The enzyme degrades the protein into amino acids. 这种酶将蛋白质降解成氨基酸.  His actions degraded his reputation. 他的行为降低了他的声誉.|Synonyms: decompose, deteriorate, break down, diminish, demote|Antonyms: enhance, improve, elevate, promote|Verb

degradation:/ˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃn/| n.|降解，退化，堕落|The process of being degraded; the state of being degraded.|Environmental degradation is a serious problem. 环境退化是一个严重的问题.|Synonyms: deterioration, decline, decay, decomposition|Antonyms: improvement, enhancement, elevation|Noun
