disendowment:/ˌdɪsɪnˈdaʊmənt/
n.
剥夺捐赠；停止资助
The act of depriving an institution of its endowment.
The college suffered a severe disendowment during the economic downturn. 这所大学在经济衰退期间遭受了严重的资金剥夺。

The disendowment of the art program led to its closure. 艺术项目的资金被剥夺导致其关闭。

Synonyms:  deprivation, divestiture, withdrawal, revocation
Antonyms: endowment, funding, support
noun
