desaturate:/diːˈsætʃəreɪt/
verb
降低饱和度；使不饱和；去饱和
To reduce the saturation of (a color); to make less saturated.
The photographer desaturated the image to give it a vintage feel. 摄影师降低了图像的饱和度，使其具有复古感。
synonyms: mute, dull, subdue, soften
antonyms: saturate, brighten, intensify
词性: 动词

desaturate:/diːˈsætʃəreɪt/
adjective
不饱和的；降低饱和度的
Reduced in saturation.
The desaturated colors of the painting created a calming effect.  这幅画不饱和的色彩营造出一种平静的效果。
synonyms: muted, dull, subdued, softened
antonyms: saturated, bright, intense
词性: 形容词
