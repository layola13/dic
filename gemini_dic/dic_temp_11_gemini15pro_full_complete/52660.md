digitised: /ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈdɪdʒɪˌtaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|数字化的|Converted from analog to digital form.  Representing data in a digital form, especially for use by a computer.|例句：The library has digitised its entire collection of rare books. 图书馆已将其全部珍藏书籍数字化。|近义词：digital, electronic, computerized|反义词: analog, non-digital|词性:形容词
digitize: /ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪz/ (UK) /ˈdɪdʒɪˌtaɪz/ (US)| v.|数字化|Convert (information or an image) to digital form.|例句：The company is digitizing its archives. 公司正在将其档案数字化。|近义词：convert, scan, encode|反义词: - |词性:动词
