chemosis:/kɪˈmoʊsɪs/
noun
结膜水肿
Swelling of the conjunctiva, the membrane that lines the eyelid and covers the white part of the eye.
Chemosis can be caused by allergies, infections, or eye irritation.  结膜水肿可能是由过敏、感染或眼睛刺激引起的。
The patient presented with severe chemosis after being stung by a bee.  病人被蜜蜂蜇伤后出现严重的结膜水肿。
Synonyms: conjunctival edema, eye swelling
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms describing a healthy conjunctiva, such as "clear conjunctiva" or "normal conjunctiva")
