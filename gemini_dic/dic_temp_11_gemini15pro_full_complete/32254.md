chicky:/ˈtʃɪki/| adj.|小鸡的; 胆小的; 优等的, 时髦的|A term of endearment, often used for a young woman, suggesting attractiveness or stylishness. Also can mean cowardly or like a chick (baby chicken).|例句：She looked very chicky in her new dress. 她穿着新裙子看起来很时髦。

例句：He was too chicky to ask her out. 他太胆小了，不敢约她出去。|近义词：stylish, fashionable, trendy, timid, cowardly|反义词：unfashionable, brave, courageous|词性:形容词
