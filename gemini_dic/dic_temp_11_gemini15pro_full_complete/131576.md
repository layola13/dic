noncomparability:/ˌnɑːnkəmˌpærəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可比性，无法比较
The noncomparability of the two studies made it difficult to draw any firm conclusions.  这两项研究的不可比性使得难以得出任何确定的结论。
The report highlighted the noncomparability of data across different countries. 报告强调了不同国家之间数据的不可比性。
Synonyms: incommensurability, disparity, difference, dissimilarity
Antonyms: comparability, similarity, likeness, resemblance
noun
