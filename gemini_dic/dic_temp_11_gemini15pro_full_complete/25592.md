bummer:/ˈbʌmər/| n.|令人失望的事；扫兴的事|Something that is disappointing or unpleasant.|例句：Losing the game was a real bummer. 输掉比赛真是太令人失望了。

例句：It's a bummer that you can't come to the party. 你不能来参加聚会真是太遗憾了。

近义词：disappointment, setback, letdown, drag|反义词:blessing, advantage|词性:名词

bummers:/ˈbʌmərz/| n.|令人失望的事；扫兴的事（复数）|Something that is disappointing or unpleasant (plural form).|例句：We've had a few bummers lately, but we're staying positive.  最近我们遇到了一些不顺心的事，但我们保持乐观。

例句：The cancelled flights were a series of bummers for the travelers.  航班取消对旅客来说是一系列的打击。
