mishmash: /ˈmɪʃmæʃ/ | n. | 混杂；大杂烩 | A confused mixture or jumble of things. | The room was a mishmash of furniture from different eras. 房间里摆放着不同年代的家具，显得杂乱无章。| synonyms: mixture, blend, jumble, hodgepodge | antonyms: order, organization | noun

Etymology: From Yiddish *מישמאַש‎ (*mishmash*), from Hebrew *מְשֻׁמָּשׁ‎ (*məšummāš*, “mixed”).
