endosmometer:/ɛnˌdɒzˈmɒmɪtər/ /ɛnˌdɑːzˈmɑːmɪtər/
noun
渗压计
An instrument for measuring osmotic pressure, especially of solutions of biological interest.
The endosmometer showed a significant difference in osmotic pressure between the two solutions. 渗压计显示两种溶液的渗透压存在显著差异。
