homely:/ˈhoʊmli/ (US) /ˈhəʊmli/ (UK)| adj.|家常的；朴素的；相貌平平的；不好看的|Simple and unpretentious; plain or unattractive in appearance.|例句：She wore homely clothes. 她穿着朴素的衣服。|例句：He had a homely face. 他长相平平。|近义词：plain, simple, unpretentious, unattractive|反义词：beautiful, attractive, glamorous, sophisticated|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *hāmlic "home-like, domestic," from hām "home." The negative sense of "unattractive" developed later.
