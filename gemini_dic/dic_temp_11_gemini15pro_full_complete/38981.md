confining:/kənˈfaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 限制的；局限的；狭窄的 | Restricting or limiting someone's movement or activities; causing someone to stay in a particular place. |  The illness was very confining, and she couldn't leave her bed.  这种病让她行动受限，她无法下床。

The small apartment felt confining after living in a house.  在住过房子之后，这间小公寓感觉很局限。 | 近义词: restrictive, limiting, restraining, cramped | 反义词: liberating, freeing, expansive, open | 词性: 形容词

confining:/kənˈfaɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 限制；禁闭（confine的现在分词）|  present participle of confine |  The illness is confining her to bed. 这种疾病把她限制在床上。

They are confining their research to the effects of stress.  他们将研究范围限定在压力的影响上。| 近义词: restricting, limiting | 反义词: freeing, releasing | 词性: 动词 (现在分词)
