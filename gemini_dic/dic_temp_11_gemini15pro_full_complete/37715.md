comparative:/kəmˈpærətɪv/ | adj. n.|比较的；相对的；比较级|  Relating to, or based on, comparison; estimated by comparison; not absolute or positive; relative.  A word form expressing an increase or decrease in the quality, quantity, or relation expressed by another word form (the positive degree).| This year's profits are comparative to last year's. 今年的利润与去年的利润相比。| synonyms: relative, comparable, contrastive | antonyms: absolute, positive | adjective

comparative:/kəmˈpærətɪv/ | n.|比较级| The comparative form of an adjective or adverb.|  He used the comparative "better." 他使用了比较级“更好”。| synonyms: comparative degree | antonyms: positive degree, superlative degree | noun
