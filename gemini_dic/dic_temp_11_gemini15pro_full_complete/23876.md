break-even:/breɪkˈiːvən/
noun
盈亏平衡点；保本点
The point at which cost and income are equal and there is neither profit nor loss.
The company reached break-even in the third quarter.  该公司在第三季度达到了盈亏平衡。
synonyms: balancing point, point of equilibrium
antonyms: profit, loss
n.

break-even:/breɪkˈiːvən/
adjective
盈亏平衡的；保本的
Relating to the point at which cost and income are equal.
The company's break-even point is 10,000 units.  该公司的盈亏平衡点是1万件。
synonyms: cost-covering, self-supporting
antonyms: profitable, loss-making
adj.
