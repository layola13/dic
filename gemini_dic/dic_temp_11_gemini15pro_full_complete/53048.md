dionysian:/ˌdaɪəˈnɪziən/ /ˌdaɪəˈnɪʒən/
adjective
放纵的，狂欢的，酒神的
Relating to the Greek god Dionysus, the god of wine and revelry; characterized by unrestrained revelry and emotional abandon.
His lifestyle was decidedly Dionysian, filled with parties, music, and uninhibited pleasure. 他过着典型的酒神式生活，充满了派对、音乐和无拘无束的快乐。
The Dionysian energy of the festival was infectious. 节日狂欢的酒神精神极具感染力。

近义词:bacchanalian, orgiastic, hedonistic, unrestrained, wild, abandoned
反义词: Apollonian, restrained, controlled, inhibited, austere, sober
词性: adjective
