cheeked:/tʃiːkt/| adj.|脸颊…的，厚脸皮的|Having or showing a particular type of cheek.|She's a rosy-cheeked girl. 她是一个脸颊红润的女孩。|近义词：ruddy, fresh-faced, impudent, bold|反义词:pale, timid|词性:形容词
词源: From the noun "cheek" + "-ed" suffix, meaning "having cheeks of a certain kind."  The word "cheek" itself comes from Old English *cēace "jaw, jawbone."
