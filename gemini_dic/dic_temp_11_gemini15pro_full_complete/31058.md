chamfered:/ˈtʃæmfəd/| adj.|倒角的，削边的|Having a chamfer; beveled.  An edge that has been cut at an angle to make it smoother or less sharp.|The table had chamfered edges to prevent splinters.  这张桌子的边缘经过倒角处理，以防止出现裂片。|beveled, angled, sloping|sharp, square|adjective
chamfer:/ˈtʃæmfə(r)/| n. v.|倒角，削边|A symmetrical sloping surface at an edge or corner; to cut a chamfer.|The carpenter chamfered the wood to create a decorative edge.  木匠将木头倒角以 tạo ra một cạnh trang trí。|bevel, slope, angle|sharpen, square|noun, verb
