gunpowdery:/ˈɡʌnˌpaʊdəri/ | adj. | 易怒的；暴躁的 | Easily angered or provoked; quick-tempered. (Figurative usage) | His gunpowdery temperament made him difficult to work with. 他暴躁的脾气让他很难与人共事。 | Synonyms: irascible, choleric | Antonyms: calm, placid | adjective
