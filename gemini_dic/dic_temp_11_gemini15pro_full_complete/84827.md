hang-back:/ˈhæŋˌbæk/| n.|犹豫，退缩；落后者|A person who hesitates to participate or is reluctant to move forward.  A person who lags behind.|例句：He was a hang-back when it came to asking for a promotion.  在要求升职方面，他很犹豫。

例句：The hang-backs were encouraged to join in the game.  那些犹豫不决的人被鼓励加入游戏。|近义词：hesitator, laggard, lingerer, dawdler|反义词：go-getter, enthusiast, leader|词性:名词
