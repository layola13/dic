oversaturating: /ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəreɪtɪŋ/
Verb
过度饱和；使过饱和
Causing something to become excessively saturated.
The market is oversaturating with new products.  市场上新产品过饱和。
They are oversaturating the market with cheap imports. 他们用廉价进口商品充斥市场。
Near Synonyms: glutting, flooding, overwhelming
Opposite: undersupplying
词性: 动词 (present participle)

oversaturated: /ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/
Adjective
过度饱和的；过饱和的
Excessively saturated.
The oversaturated market is making it difficult for new businesses to thrive. 过饱和的市场使新企业难以蓬勃发展。
The colors in the photograph were oversaturated, making it look unrealistic. 照片中的颜色过度饱和，看起来不真实。
Near Synonyms: saturated, glutted, flooded
Opposite: unsaturated
词性: 形容词

oversaturation: /ˌoʊvərˌsætʃəˈreɪʃən/
Noun
过度饱和；过饱和
The state of being oversaturated.
The oversaturation of the market led to a decrease in prices.  市场过度饱和导致价格下降。
Oversaturation of the soil with water can damage plant roots. 土壤水分过度饱和会损害植物根系。
Near Synonyms: glut, surfeit, excess
Opposite: undersupply, deficiency
词性: 名词

词源:  From over- + saturate, from Latin saturatus, past participle of saturare "to fill full, sate," from satur "full, enough."
