almeh:/ˈælmeɪ/| n.|歌女，舞女|A professional female entertainer in Egypt, Turkey, and other parts of the Middle East, skilled in singing, dancing, and playing musical instruments.|She was a renowned almeh, famed for her beauty and talent. 她是一位著名的歌女，以她的美貌和才华而闻名。|Synonyms:  dancing girl, geisha, courtesan|Antonyms: None|Noun
