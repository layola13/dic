nonpunctual: /ˌnɒnˈpʌŋktʃuəl/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈpʌŋktʃuəl/ (American)
| adj. |不守时的，不准时的|Not arriving or doing something at the expected or correct time; late.|
例句：He was constantly nonpunctual, always arriving late for meetings. 他总是迟到，开会总是迟到。

例句：Her nonpunctual habits frustrated her colleagues. 她不守时的习惯让她的同事们很 frustrated。

近义词：tardy, late, overdue, behind schedule
反义词：punctual, prompt, on time
词性: adjective


nonpunctual: /ˌnɒnˈpʌŋktʃuəl/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈpʌŋktʃuəl/ (American)
|Etymology:| From non- + punctual.  Punctual comes from Late Latin *punctualis "of or pertaining to a point," from Latin punctum "point."
词源: 来自non- + punctual。“Punctual”源于晚期拉丁语 *punctualis “属于点的”，来自拉丁语 punctum “点”。
