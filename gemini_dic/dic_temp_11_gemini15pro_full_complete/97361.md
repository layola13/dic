infracting:/ɪnˈfræktɪŋ/
verb
违反，违背
The act of breaking a rule or law.
He was infracting the company's policy by using his work computer for personal business. 他因将工作电脑用于私人事务而违反了公司政策。
She was infracting the terms of the agreement. 她违反了协议条款。

近义词: violating, breaching, breaking, transgressing, offending
反义词: obeying, complying, adhering, observing
词性: verb (present participle)
