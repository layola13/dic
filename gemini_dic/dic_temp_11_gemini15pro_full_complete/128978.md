nip-in-the-bud:/nɪp‿ɪn‿ðə‿bʌd/
|idiomatic expression|
|防患于未然; 扼杀在萌芽状态|
|To stop something bad from developing or continuing by dealing with it quickly and firmly at an early stage.|
|The company decided to nip the problem in the bud before it escalated.  公司决定将问题扼杀在萌芽状态，以免事态升级。|

|We need to nip this bad behavior in the bud before it becomes a habit. 我们需要趁早阻止这种不良行为，以免它成为习惯。|

|近义词: stop, prevent, thwart, suppress, stifle|
|反义词: encourage, promote, foster, cultivate|
