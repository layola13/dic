overexploit: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪt/ | vt. | 过度开发，过度利用 | To make excessive use of something, typically a natural resource, to the point of depletion or damage. |  The fishing industry has overexploited the cod population in the North Atlantic.  北大西洋的鳕鱼资源被渔业过度捕捞。

overexploit: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪt/ | n. | 过度开发，过度利用（较少用作名词）| The overexploit of natural resources is a serious problem. 自然资源的过度开发是一个严重问题。


近义词: overuse, deplete, exhaust
反义词: conserve, preserve, protect
词性:  verb (主要), noun (较少)


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "exploit" (from Old French "exploitier," meaning "to accomplish, achieve, make the most of").
