journeying:/ˈdʒɜːrniɪŋ/ | n. | 旅行，旅程 | The act of traveling, especially a long and arduous trip. |  She described her spiritual journeying in a moving blog post. 她在一篇感人的博客文章中描述了她的精神之旅。

|近义词：traveling, trekking, voyaging | 反义词：staying, remaining | 词性: Noun (Gerund/Present Participle)

journeying:/ˈdʒɜːrniɪŋ/ | v. | 旅行 | Traveling, especially on a long and arduous trip. | They are journeying across Europe by train. 他们正在乘火车游历欧洲。


|近义词：traveling, trekking, voyaging | 反义词：staying, remaining | 词性: Verb (Present Participle)


Etymology: From Middle English *journeien, from Old French journee (“a day, a day's work, a day's journey”), from Late Latin diurnum (“daily allowance”), from Latin diurnus (“daily”), from dies (“day”).
