appose:/əˈpoʊz/| vt.|并列，并置，贴近|To place side by side or in close proximity; juxtapose.|The artist apposed contrasting colors to create a vibrant effect. 艺术家并置对比鲜明的颜色来创造一种充满活力的效果。|Synonyms: juxtapose, place side by side, connect, abut|Antonyms: separate, detach, divide|verb

appose:/əˈpoʊz/| vt.|（纹章）相对放置|To place (charges in heraldry) side by side.|The shield apposed a lion and a unicorn. 盾牌上并排放置着一头狮子和一只独角兽。|Synonyms: juxtapose, place side by side|Antonyms: separate, detach|verb

From Late Latin *apponere, from Latin ad- + ponere "to put, place".
