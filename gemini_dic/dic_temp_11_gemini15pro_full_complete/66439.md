exemplarist:/ɪɡˈzɛmplərɪst/
n.
模范主义者；以身作则者
Someone who believes in or acts according to exemplarism (leading by example).
Nelson Mandela was an exemplarist, inspiring millions through his actions. 纳尔逊·曼德拉是一位模范主义者，他的行动激励了数百万人。
exemplar, model, paradigm
conformist, follower
noun
