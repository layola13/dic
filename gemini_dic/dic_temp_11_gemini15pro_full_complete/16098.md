bandstands:/ˈbændˌstændz/
n.
露天演奏台；乐队演奏台
A raised platform for a band to play on, typically in a park or other outdoor public space.
The band played a lively tune on the bandstand in the park.  乐队在公园的露天演奏台上演奏了一曲轻快的音乐。
The town's annual summer concert series takes place at the bandstand by the river.  该镇一年一度的夏季音乐会系列在河边的露天演奏台上举行。
近义词:  platform, stage, podium
反义词:  None (No direct opposite for a physical structure)
词性: Noun
