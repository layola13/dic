mummify:/ˈmʌmɪfaɪ/ /ˈmʌmɪˌfaɪ/ | v. | 使成木乃伊; (使)干瘪; | To preserve (a body) by embalming and wrapping it in cloth, as practiced in ancient Egypt.  To make (something) dry and shriveled. | The ancient Egyptians mummified their pharaohs. 古埃及人把他们的法老做成了木乃伊。 |  The hot desert air mummified the fruit. 炎热的沙漠空气使水果干瘪。 |  embalm, preserve | decay, decompose | verb
