overeducates:/ˌoʊvərˈɛdʒʊkeɪts/
verb
过度教育
To educate someone beyond what is necessary or useful for their intended career or life path.
The university system overeducates many students, leaving them with large debts and few job prospects.  大学体系过度教育了许多学生，让他们背负巨额债务，却几乎没有就业前景。
Many critics argue that the current education system overeducates students for the available jobs. 许多评论家认为，目前的教育体系对学生的教育程度超过了现有工作的需求。
近义词: overtrains, overqualifies
反义词: undereducates, undertrains
词性:v.
