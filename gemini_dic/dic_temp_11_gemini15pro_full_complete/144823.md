over-indexed:/ˌoʊvərˈɪndɛkst/ | adj. | 过度索引的，占比过高的 |  Having a higher representation or frequency in a particular category or group compared to a baseline or average. It often implies a disproportionately large presence. |  Millennials are over-indexed in online shopping compared to older generations. 千禧一代在网上购物的比例比老一辈高。| 近义词: overrepresented, disproportionately high | 反义词: under-indexed, underrepresented |词性:形容词


over-indexed:/ˌoʊvərˈɪndɛkst/ | v. | 过度索引 | To assign an excessively high index or weight to something.  This is less common than the adjective form. | The algorithm over-indexed certain keywords, leading to skewed search results.  该算法对某些关键词过度索引，导致搜索结果出现偏差。 | 近义词: overemphasize, overweight | 反义词: under-index, underemphasize |词性:动词
