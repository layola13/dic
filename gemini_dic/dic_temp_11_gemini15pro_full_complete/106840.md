laboris:/ləˈbɔːrɪs/
n.
labor (of), work, toil
laboris is the genitive singular form of the Latin noun "labor", meaning "work", "labor", or "toil".  It translates to "of labor" or "of work".
The fruits of their laboris were evident in the bountiful harvest.  他们劳动的成果在丰收中显而易见。
Synonyms: operis, industriae
Antonyms: otii, quietis
