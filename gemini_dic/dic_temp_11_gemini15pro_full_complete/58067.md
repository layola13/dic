词源： drone (n.)  "male bee," 1540s, from Old English *dran or a similar Scandinavian word (compare Old Norse *dran, Danish drone "male bee," Norwegian drone "bumble," Swedish dröna "drone, buzz"), of imitative origin. The sense of "idling person" is from 1570s. Meaning "pilotless aircraft" is recorded from 1946.  The verb meaning "to make a monotonous humming sound" is from c. 1400.
