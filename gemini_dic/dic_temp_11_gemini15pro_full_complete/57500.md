draggle:/ˈdræɡəl/| v. | 拖，拖拉；弄脏，弄湿| to make something wet and dirty by dragging it along the ground |  The dog draggled its muddy paws across the clean floor. 狗把沾满泥的爪子拖过干净的地板。| 近义词：trail, drag, soil | 反义词: clean, tidy | 词性:动词
draggle:/ˈdræɡəl/| n. | 拖，拖拉；拖拉的东西| something that trails or drags | The bride's long train draggled behind her.  新娘的长裙裾拖在她身后。| 近义词：trail | 反义词: - | 词性:名词
