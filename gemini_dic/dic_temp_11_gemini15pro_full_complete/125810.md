murmur:/ˈmɜːrmər/ (UK) /ˈmɜːrmər/ (US)| n. v. |低沉持续的抱怨|a low, continuous complaint|There was a murmur of discontent among the staff.  员工中弥漫着不满的情绪。|近义词：grumble, complaint|反义词:praise, acclaim|词性:名词，动词
