citronella:/ˌsɪtrəˈnɛlə/ | n. |香茅，香茅油 |An Asian grass whose fragrant oil is used as an insect repellent. |例句：The citronella candle kept the mosquitoes away. 香茅蜡烛驱赶了蚊子。

例句：She used citronella oil on her skin to prevent mosquito bites.  她在皮肤上涂抹香茅油以防止蚊虫叮咬。

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *citronelle*, diminutive of citron "citron," from Italian *citrone*, from Arabic *laymūn*, from Persian *līmūn*.  Referencing the lemon-like scent of the plant.
