fretty:/ˈfrɛti/ | adj. | 易怒的，烦躁的，装饰华丽的 | Characterized by excessive complaining, irritability, or discontent; also, ornamented with frets. | She was fretty and impatient while waiting for the delayed flight.  她因为航班延误而变得烦躁不安。| Synonyms: irritable, peevish, fractious, petulant, cross | Antonyms: patient, calm, tranquil, serene | adjective

fretty:/ˈfrɛti/ | adj. | （尤指装饰）格子状的 | Decorated with frets, a repeating geometric pattern. | The guitar had a fretty inlay around the sound hole. 这把吉他的音孔周围有格子状的镶嵌装饰。| Synonyms: latticed, crosshatched | Antonyms: plain, unadorned | adjective
