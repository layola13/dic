hemimetabola:/ˌhɛmiˌmɛtəˈbɒlə/ | n. | 半变态昆虫 | Insects that undergo incomplete metamorphosis, where the immature stages (nymphs) resemble the adults but lack wings and reproductive organs. | Example: Grasshoppers and cockroaches are hemimetabolous insects. 蚱蜢和蟑螂是半变态昆虫。| Example: The hemimetabola include insects like dragonflies and true bugs. 半变态昆虫包括蜻蜓和真正的虫子。 | Synonym:  Hemimetabolous insects, Exopterygota | Antonym: Holometabola, Endopterygota | noun

hemimetabolous:/ˌhɛmiˌmɛtəˈbɒləs/ | adj. | 半变态的 | Undergoing incomplete metamorphosis, where the immature stages resemble the adults but lack wings and reproductive organs. | Example:  A grasshopper is a hemimetabolous insect. 蚱蜢是一种半变态昆虫。| Example:  Hemimetabolous development is characteristic of many insect orders. 半变态发育是许多昆虫目的特征。 | Synonym:  Exopterygote | Antonym: Holometabolous, Endopterygote | adjective
