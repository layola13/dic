euchite:/ˈjuːkaɪt/
n.
圣餐者; (尤指)领圣餐教徒
A member of a Christian sect in the 4th century who believed that prayer was the only valid form of worship.
The Euchites believed that constant prayer would drive out the demon that inhabited the body of every person from birth. 圣餐者认为不断的祈祷会驱逐每个人出生时就寄居在体内的恶魔。
The Euchites were known for their ascetic practices and their rejection of material possessions. 圣餐者以其苦行和拒绝物质财富而闻名。
Synonym: Messalian
Antonym: None (specific antonym for this historical sect is not readily available)
