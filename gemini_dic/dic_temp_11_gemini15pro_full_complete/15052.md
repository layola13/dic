babydaddy:/ˈbeɪbi ˌdædi/| n. |孩子他爸；精子捐献者|A man who has fathered a child, especially one who is not married to the mother and may not be actively involved in the child's upbringing.  This term can sometimes carry negative connotations, suggesting a lack of commitment or responsibility.|例句：She refused to reveal the identity of the babydaddy. 她拒绝透露孩子父亲的身份。

例句：He's a babydaddy, but not a husband. 他是孩子的父亲，但不是丈夫。|近义词：biological father, sperm donor (in some contexts), father|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
