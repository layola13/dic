improvement:/ɪmˈpruːvmənt/ | n. |改进，改善；改进处，改进措施|An act of improving or the state of being improved. |The company has made significant improvements in customer service.  这家公司在客户服务方面取得了显著的改进。|  synonyms: betterment, enhancement, advance, progress | antonyms: deterioration, decline, regression | noun

etymology->
From Old French *emprovement, from emprouver "to improve," from en- "in" + prou "profit, advantage," from Latin prodest "it is of use."
