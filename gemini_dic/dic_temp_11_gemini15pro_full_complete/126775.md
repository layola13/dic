nanocomposite:/ˈnænoʊkəmˈpɑːzɪt/| n. |纳米复合材料|A material made from two or more constituent materials with at least one of the constituents having dimensions in the nanometer range (typically 1-100 nm).|例句：The nanocomposite material exhibited improved mechanical properties.  这种纳米复合材料表现出改进的机械性能。

例句：Researchers are developing new nanocomposites for use in aerospace applications.  研究人员正在开发用于航空航天应用的新型纳米复合材料。|近义词：nanomaterial, hybrid material|反义词:conventional material|词性:noun
