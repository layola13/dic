displaying:/dɪˈspleɪɪŋ/
|v.|展示，陈列，显示|presenting or exhibiting something|The museum is displaying a collection of ancient artifacts. 博物馆正在展出一批古代文物。|showing, exhibiting, presenting|concealing, hiding|verb

displaying:/dɪˈspleɪɪŋ/
|adj.|用于修饰名词，表示“正在展示的”|used to describe something that is being shown|The displaying artwork is a masterpiece.  正在展出的艺术品是一件杰作。|exhibiting, showing|hidden|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *displayen, from Anglo-Norman despleier, from Old French despleier, from Late Latin displicare, from Latin dis- (“apart, asunder”) + plicare (“to fold”).
