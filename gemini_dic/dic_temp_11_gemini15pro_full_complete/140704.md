omnibus:/ˈɒmnɪbʌs/ |n. adj.|公共汽车；总括的；多项式的|A bus or a collection of things, especially literary works or legislative bills, presented as a single entity.| The omnibus edition contained all three novels. 这本合订本包含了全部三部小说。|近义词: bus, collection, anthology, comprehensive |反义词: individual, specific | 词性: 名词，形容词


词源: From Latin *omnibus* "for all," dative plural of *omnis* "all."
