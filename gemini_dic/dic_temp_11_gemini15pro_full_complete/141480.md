opinionative: /əˈpɪnjənətɪv/ | adj. | 固执己见的，武断的 | Holding stubbornly to one's own opinions, especially without evidence. |  She's very opinionative and doesn't listen to other people's views. 她很固执己见，不听取别人的意见。|  He was opinionative and unwilling to compromise. 他很武断，不愿妥协。| synonyms: dogmatic, assertive, opinionated, insistent, uncompromising | antonyms: open-minded, receptive, flexible, agreeable | adjective
