creativenesses:/ˌkriːeɪˈtɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
创造力（复数）
The plural form of "creativeness," referring to multiple instances or types of creativeness.  This form is rarely used and often considered non-standard.  It's generally better to use "creative abilities" or "forms of creativity" instead.
His creativenesses manifest in both music and painting. (Not recommended usage) 他的创造力体现在音乐和绘画方面。（不推荐用法）
Schools should nurture students' diverse creativenesses. (Not recommended usage) 学校应该培养学生不同的创造力。（不推荐用法）
Near Synonyms: creative abilities, forms of creativity, imaginative powers, innovative capacities
Antonyms: lack of imagination, unoriginality, stagnation
