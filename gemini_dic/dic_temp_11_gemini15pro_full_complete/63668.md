epanthous:/ɛˈpænθəs/
adjective
花生于花上的；花着生的
Growing on or out of a flower.  Specifically, developing from the floral receptacle.
The orchid species is known for its epanthous inflorescence, with tiny flowers blooming directly on the old flower stalk.  这种兰花以其花生于花上的花序而闻名，小花直接在旧花茎上绽放。
Near synonyms:  floral, blossoming, flowering
Antonyms:  barren, sterile, infertile
词性: adjective
