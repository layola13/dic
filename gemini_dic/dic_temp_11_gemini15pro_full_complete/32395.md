chilli:/ˈtʃɪli/ (UK) /ˈtʃɪli/ (US)| n.|辣椒; 辣椒粉|A small hot-tasting pod of a variety of capsicum, used in cooking.  A powder made from dried chillis.|例句：I like to add chilli to my curry. 我喜欢在我的咖喱里加辣椒。

例句：The chilli was so hot it made me cry. 这辣椒太辣了，辣得我哭了。|近义词：chili, red pepper, hot pepper|反义词:bell pepper (甜椒 -  mild counterpart, not a true opposite)|词性:noun


chilli:/ˈtʃɪli/ (UK) /ˈtʃɪli/ (US)| adj.|辣的|Containing or tasting of chilli.|例句：This chilli sauce is very hot. 这辣椒酱很辣。

例句：He ordered a chilli con carne. 他点了一份辣味肉酱。|近义词：spicy, hot, peppery|反义词:mild, bland|词性:adjective
