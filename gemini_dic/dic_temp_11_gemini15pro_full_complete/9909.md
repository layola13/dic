apehood:/ˈeɪphʊd/
n.|猿的特性；猿的状态|The state or condition of being an ape; the characteristics of an ape.|He studied apehood to understand human evolution. 他研究猿的特性来理解人类进化。|近义词：apedom, simianity|反义词：humanity|词性: 名词

Etymology: ape + -hood.  "Ape" comes from Old English *apa (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *apon, of uncertain origin.  "-hood" comes from Old English -hād, meaning "state, condition, quality."
