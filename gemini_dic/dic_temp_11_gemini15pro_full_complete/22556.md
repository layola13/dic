boost:/buːst/| n. vt.|提升，增加；推动，促进|An increase in power or effectiveness. To improve or increase something.|The new advertising campaign gave sales a significant boost. 新的广告活动大大提升了销售额。|近义词:increase, enhance, improve, elevate, strengthen|反义词:decrease, reduce, diminish, weaken|词性:名词，动词

boosts:/buːsts/| n. vt.|提升，增加；推动，促进（boost的第三人称单数形式）|Third-person singular present tense form of "boost." To improve or increase something.|The coach boosts the team's morale with his encouraging words. 教练用鼓励的话语提升了球队的士气。|近义词:increases, enhances, improves, elevates, strengthens|反义词:decreases, reduces, diminishes, weakens|词性:动词

词源：From the earlier “boist,” probably of unknown origin.  Possibly related to a dialectal word meaning “beam” or “support.”
