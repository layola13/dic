defacement:/dɪˈfeɪsmənt/
n.|污损，毁损，丑化|The act of damaging the surface of something, especially by writing or drawing on it.
|The defacement of the statue with graffiti shocked the community. 用涂鸦污损雕像的行为震惊了社区。

|Synonyms: vandalism, disfigurement, mutilation, damage, impairment
|Antonyms: restoration, improvement, beautification
|noun


deface:/dɪˈfeɪs/
vt.|污损，毁损，丑化|To damage the surface of something, especially by writing or drawing on it.
|Vandals defaced the wall with obscene images. 破坏者用淫秽图像污损了墙壁。

|Synonyms: vandalize, disfigure, mar, spoil, ruin
|Antonyms: restore, improve, beautify
|verb
