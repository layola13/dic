megapolis:/ˌmɛɡəˈpɒlɪs/ (US) /ˌmɛɡəˈpɒlɪs/ (UK)| n. |大都市；特大城市|A very large city, typically one with a population of over ten million people.  A large conurbation, where two or more large cities have spread and joined together.|Tokyo is a sprawling megapolis with a population exceeding 37 million. 东京是一个人口超过3700万的庞大特大城市。

The Pearl River Delta megapolis in China is a major economic hub. 中国的珠江三角洲大都市是一个主要的经济中心。|近义词：megalopolis, metropolis, conurbation|反义词:village, hamlet|noun
