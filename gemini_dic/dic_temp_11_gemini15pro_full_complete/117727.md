melange:/meɪˈlɑːnʒ/ /məˈlɑːnʒ/
n.
混合物，大杂烩
a mixture; a medley
The market offered a melange of spices, fabrics, and pottery.  这个市场出售香料、布料和陶器的混合物。
The film is a strange melange of comedy, drama, and social commentary. 这部电影是喜剧、戏剧和社会评论的奇怪混合。
mixture, blend, medley, assortment, potpourri
homogeneity, uniformity
noun
