mollify: /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪ/ /ˈmɒlɪfaɪ/ |v. | 平息，抚慰，缓和| To soothe the anger or anxiety of someone. |  The manager tried to mollify the angry customer by offering a refund. 经理试图通过提供退款来安抚愤怒的顾客。

|  Her kind words mollified his hurt feelings. 她友善的话语抚慰了他受伤的感情。| 近义词: appease, soothe, placate, pacify | 反义词: aggravate, incite, provoke |词性: 动词
