dishonest:/dɪsˈɑːnɪst/ /dɪsˈɒnɪst/| adj.|不诚实的，欺骗的|Not honest; intending to deceive or cheat; untruthful.|He was fired for dishonest business practices. 他因不诚实的商业行为被解雇了。|She gave a dishonest account of the accident. 她对事故作了不诚实的描述。|近义词：deceitful, fraudulent, untruthful, lying, mendacious|反义词：honest, truthful, sincere, upright|词性:形容词

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + honest.  The word "honest" comes from Old French "honeste"  and ultimately from Latin "honestus," meaning "honorable" or "respected."
