hagfish:/ˈhæɡfɪʃ/| n. |盲鳗|A primitive eel-like marine fish with a slimy, scaleless body and a sucking mouth. It is a scavenger, feeding on dead or dying fish.|Hagfish are known to tie themselves in knots to gain leverage when feeding. 盲鳗以将自己打结以在进食时获得 leverage 而闻名。|The hagfish burrowed into the carcass of the dead whale. 盲鳗钻进了死鲸的尸体里。|Synonyms: slime eel, rock eel|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific animal)|noun
