make-belief:/ˈmeɪk bɪˈliːf/ | n. | 假装；虚构；幻想 |  The act of pretending or imagining something that is not real. |  The children engaged in a game of make-belief, pretending to be pirates. 孩子们玩起了假装游戏，假扮成海盗。| synonyms: fantasy, pretense, imagination | antonyms: reality, truth | noun

make-belief:/ˈmeɪk bɪˈliːf/ | adj. | 假装的；虚构的；幻想的 |  Not real; imagined or pretended. | They built a make-belief castle out of blankets and pillows. 他们用毯子和枕头建造了一座假装的城堡。 | synonyms: imaginary, pretend, fantastical | antonyms: real, actual, genuine | adjective
