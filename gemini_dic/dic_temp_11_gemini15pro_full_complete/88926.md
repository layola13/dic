holdback:/ˈhoʊldbæk/ (US) /ˈhəʊldbæk/ (UK)| n.|阻碍，障碍；抑制；扣留物；预备队|Something that hinders or restrains progress or release.|The main holdback is the lack of funds. 主要障碍是缺乏资金。|近义词：hindrance, obstacle, impediment, restraint, reservation|反义词：advantage, benefit, aid, assistance|词性:名词

holdback:/ˈhoʊldbæk/ (US) /ˈhəʊldbæk/ (UK)| n.|预留部分，提成|A portion of something (especially money) that is kept back or retained.|The company kept a 10% holdback on the contractor's payment. 公司扣留了承包商付款的10%。|近义词：reserve, retention, deduction|反义词：payment, release|词性:名词
