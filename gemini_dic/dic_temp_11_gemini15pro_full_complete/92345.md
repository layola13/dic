hypoblast:/ˈhaɪpəˌblæst/| n. |下胚层|The inner layer of the bilaminar embryonic disc in vertebrates.  It gives rise to the yolk sac and some extraembryonic membranes.|The hypoblast is a crucial structure in early embryonic development. 下胚层是早期胚胎发育中的一个关键结构。|The hypoblast differentiates to form the endoderm of the yolk sac. 下胚层分化形成卵黄囊的内胚层。|synonyms: primitive endoderm|antonyms: epiblast|noun
