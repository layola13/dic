Cambodia:/kæmˈboʊdiə/ | n. | 柬埔寨 |A country in Southeast Asia. |Cambodia is known for the Angkor Wat temple. 柬埔寨以吴哥窟寺庙而闻名。|  Cambodia has a rich cultural heritage. 柬埔寨拥有丰富的文化遗产。 |Near Synonyms:Kampuchea (former name) | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


Etymology: From French *Cambodge*, ultimately derived from Sanskrit *Kambujadeśa*, meaning "the land of Kambuja".  The name refers to the Kambuja kingdom, an ancient Khmer empire.
