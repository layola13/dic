agism: /ˈeɪdʒɪzəm/ (US), /ˈeɪdʒɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |年龄歧视；年龄主义|Prejudice or discrimination on the basis of a person's age.|例句：Ageism in the workplace can prevent older people from getting hired.  职场上的年龄歧视会阻止老年人被录用。

例句：She experienced agism when she was passed over for a promotion in favor of a younger colleague.  当她被跳过晋升而选择了一个更年轻的同事时，她经历了年龄歧视。|近义词：age discrimination, prejudice|反义词:  respect for elders, inclusivity|词性:noun
