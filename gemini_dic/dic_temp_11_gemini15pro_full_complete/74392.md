frames:/freɪmz/ | n. |框架，边框；眼镜框；（电影的）帧，画面；体制，结构 |A rigid structure that surrounds or encloses something.  | The painting's ornate frames added to its beauty. 这幅画华丽的画框增添了它的美感。 | synonyms: structures, frameworks, chassis, scaffolding | antonyms: content, filling, substance | noun

frames:/freɪmz/ | v. |给…装框；陷害；表达，措辞；制定，拟定 | To put a border around; to incriminate falsely; to express in words; to create or formulate | He framed the photo of his family. 他给家人的照片镶了框。 | synonyms: formulates, constructs, composes, designs | antonyms: dismantles, destroys, ruins | verb
