nondepressive:/ˌnɑːndɪˈprɛsɪv/ | adj. | 非抑郁的，非抑郁症的 | Not characterized by or suffering from depression; not causing or tending to cause depression. |  She described herself as feeling generally nondepressive and optimistic about the future. 她形容自己总体感觉良好，没有抑郁，并且对未来充满希望。|  The study focused on nondepressive individuals to establish a baseline for comparison.  这项研究的重点是非抑郁个体，以便建立比较基线。| 近义词: cheerful, happy, upbeat | 反义词: depressive, depressed, melancholic | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + depressive (adjective relating to depression)
