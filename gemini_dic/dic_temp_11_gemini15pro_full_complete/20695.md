bladelike:/ˈbleɪdlaɪk/| adj.|刀锋般的，像刀片一样的|Resembling a blade, especially in sharpness or shape. |The insect had bladelike legs.  这种昆虫有刀锋般的腿。|The surgeon made a bladelike incision.  外科医生做了一个像刀片一样的切口。|近义词：sharp, keen, edged|反义词:blunt, dull|词性:形容词

词源: "blade" + "-like". "Blade" comes from Old English *blæd, meaning "leaf, blade". "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of".
