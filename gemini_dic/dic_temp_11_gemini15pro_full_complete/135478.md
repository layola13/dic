nonphenomenal:/ˌnɒn.fəˈnɒm.ɪ.nəl/ | adj. | 非现象的；非经验的；超现象的 | Not pertaining to phenomena or experience; transcending the phenomenal world.  Relating to things that are not perceived by the senses or that are beyond the natural world. |  His research delves into the nonphenomenal realm of pure consciousness. 他的研究深入到纯粹意识的非现象领域。

The philosopher argued for the existence of nonphenomenal realities. 这位哲学家论证了非现象实在的存在。 | Near synonyms:  transcendental, metaphysical, noumenal, supernatural | Antonyms: phenomenal, empirical, observable, sensible | 词性: adjective
