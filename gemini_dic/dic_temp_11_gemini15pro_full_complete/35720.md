cocciferous:/ˌkɒksɪˈfɪərəs/
adjective
(生物学) 结球果的；带有球状果实的
Bearing cocci (spherical bacteria) or berry-like fruits.
Coccidia are cocciferous protozoa that can cause disease in animals. 球虫是可导致动物疾病的结球果原生动物。
The cocciferous plant produced small, round berries. 这种结球果植物结出小的圆形浆果。
Near synonyms: berry-bearing, fruit-bearing
Antonyms: non-fruiting, sterile
Word Origin: From Latin coccum (“berry”) + -ferous (bearing)
