bourgeoisifying:/ˌbʊərʒwɑːzɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
使资产阶级化
The process of making something or someone more bourgeois; imbuing with bourgeois values or characteristics.
The new shopping mall is bourgeoisifying the formerly working-class neighborhood.  这个新的购物中心正在使以前工人阶级的社区资产阶级化。
The family's increasing wealth is bourgeoisifying their lifestyle.  这个家庭日益增长的财富正在使他们的生活方式资产阶级化。
gentrifying, upgrading, refining
proletarianizing, impoverishing
v.
