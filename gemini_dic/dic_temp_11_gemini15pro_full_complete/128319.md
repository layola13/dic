neurotoxicity:/ˌnʊəroʊtɒkˈsɪsəti/ | n. | 神经毒性 | The quality of being neurotoxic; the ability of a substance to exert a destructive or poisonous effect on nerve tissue. |  Exposure to high levels of mercury can cause neurotoxicity.  接触高浓度的汞会导致神经毒性。| synonyms: nerve poison, neurotoxin | antonyms: neuroprotective | noun
