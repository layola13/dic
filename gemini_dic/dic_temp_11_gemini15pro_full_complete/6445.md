ampliative:/ˈæmpliətɪv/| adj.|扩展的，扩大的，增补的|Expanding or extending something beyond its original or explicit scope.|例句：Ampliative reasoning goes beyond the information given in the premises. 扩展推理超越了前提中给出的信息。|例句：His ampliative interpretation of the poem brought new meaning to the text. 他对这首诗的扩展性解读为文本带来了新的意义。|近义词：extensive, expansive, augmentative|反义词：restrictive, limiting|词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ampliativus, from Latin ampliatus, past participle of ampliare ("to enlarge, widen, increase"), from amplus ("large, spacious, great, ample").
