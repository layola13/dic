deadhearted:/ˈdɛdˌhɑːrtɪd/
adjective
无情的，冷酷的，铁石心肠的
Lacking compassion or feeling; unsympathetic.
例句：The deadhearted villain showed no remorse for his crimes.  冷酷无情的恶棍对自己的罪行毫无悔意。

例句：She gave him a deadhearted stare. 她冷冷地瞪着他。

近义词：heartless, cruel, unfeeling, callous, indifferent
反义词：kind, compassionate, sympathetic, warmhearted
词性: adjective
