lapsided:/læpˈsaɪdɪd/
adjective
不平衡的，一边倒的
Larger or heavier on one side than the other; unevenly balanced; unequal; disproportionately weighted in favor of one side.
The basketball game was a lopsided victory for the home team.  这场篮球赛是主队一边倒的胜利。
The painting hung lopsided on the wall. 这幅画歪歪斜斜地挂在墙上。
uneven, unbalanced, asymmetric, irregular, unequal
even, balanced, symmetrical, regular, equal
adjective
