nonexternality:/ˌnɑːnˌɛkstərˈnælɪti/ | n. | 非外部性 |  The absence of external costs or benefits associated with a particular activity or transaction. It implies that all costs and benefits are borne by the parties directly involved. | The company's internalization of waste disposal costs ensures nonexternality in its production process. 公司将废物处理成本内部化，确保其生产过程中不存在外部性。 |  Internalization, privatization | Externality | noun
