bipartite:/baɪˈpɑːrtaɪt/
adjective
二分的，两部分的
Consisting of two parts.
例句：A bipartite graph is a graph whose vertices can be divided into two disjoint and independent sets.  二分图是一种图，其顶点可以分成两个不相交且独立的集合。

例句：The agreement was a bipartite contract between the buyer and the seller. 该协议是买方和卖方之间的两方合同。
近义词：two-part, dual
反义词：multipartite
词性：adjective

bipartite:/baɪˈpɑːrtaɪt/
noun
二分体
Something that is divided into two parts.  (This usage is less common than the adjective form.)
例句：The bipartite structure of the organism allows for specialized functions.  该生物体的二分结构允许专门的功能。（较少用）

例句: The negotiators worked to create a workable bipartite from the initial proposal.  谈判者致力于从最初的提案中创建一个可行的二分体。（较少用）
近义词：dichotomy
反义词：unity, whole
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bipartitus*, from bi- (“two”) + partitus (“divided”), past participle of partiri (“to divide”).
