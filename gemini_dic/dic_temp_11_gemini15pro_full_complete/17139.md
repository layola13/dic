batting-around:/ˈbætɪŋ əˈraʊnd/| phrasal verb | 反复讨论，斟酌 | To discuss or consider an idea or plan informally and often without reaching a definite conclusion. | We were batting around some ideas for the new marketing campaign. 我们正在反复讨论一些新的营销活动的想法。

We spent the afternoon batting around possible solutions to the problem.  我们一下午都在斟酌解决这个问题的各种可能性。


|近义词：discussing, considering, brainstorming, debating, mulling over |反义词: deciding, concluding, finalizing |词性: 动词短语
