arest:/əˈrest/ | n. | 逮捕，拘留；停止，中止 | An arrest is the act of legally taking someone into custody, typically by police. It can also refer to the stopping or ceasing of a process or movement. | The police made an arrest in connection with the robbery. 警方逮捕了一名与抢劫案有关的人。| synonyms: apprehension, detention, seizure, capture, stop, halt | antonyms: release, liberation, freedom, continuation | noun

arest:/əˈrest/ | vt. | 逮捕，拘留；阻止，抑制；吸引（注意力） | To arrest someone is to take them into custody. It can also mean to stop or check something, or to capture someone's attention. | The officer arrested the suspect for speeding. 警官因超速逮捕了嫌疑人。| synonyms: apprehend, detain, seize, capture, stop, check, engage | antonyms: release, liberate, free, continue, ignore | verb
