morphemed:/mɔːrˈfiːmd/ | adj. |  形态的，词素化的 |  Formed by the combination of morphemes (the smallest meaningful units of language).  A word is analyzed into its constituent morphemes. |  The word "unbreakable" is morphemed as un-break-able.  这个单词“unbreakable”被词素化分析为un-break-able。 | The linguist presented a morphemed analysis of the word. 语言学家对这个词进行了词素化的分析。 |  analyzed, segmented | N/A | adjective
