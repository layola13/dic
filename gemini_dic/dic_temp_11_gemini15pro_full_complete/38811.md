condyloid:/ˈkɒndɪlɔɪd/ | adj.|髁状的|Resembling or relating to a condyle.|The condyloid joint allows for movement in two planes. 髁状关节允许在两个平面上运动。|近义词: condylar, |反义词: None.|adjective
