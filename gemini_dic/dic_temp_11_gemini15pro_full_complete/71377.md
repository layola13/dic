flaggers:/ˈflæɡərz/| n. |旗手；信号员；铺路工|People who place, maintain, or operate flags, especially those who signal traffic or mark hazards.  They may also be construction workers who direct traffic around roadwork.|The flaggers directed traffic around the accident.  旗手引导车辆绕过事故现场。|Near synonyms: signalmen, traffic controllers, road workers|Near antonyms: drivers, pedestrians|noun

flaggers:/ˈflæɡərz/| n. |衰弱者；萎靡者|People or things that are losing strength or weakening.|The flaggers in the heat needed water.  在高温下，那些衰弱的人需要水。|Near synonyms: weaklings, droopers, failers|Near antonyms: strongmen, energizers, thrivers|noun
