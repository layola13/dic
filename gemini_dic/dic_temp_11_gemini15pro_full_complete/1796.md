acrid: /ˈækrɪd/ (US) /ˈækrɪd/ (UK)| adj.|刺鼻的；辛辣的；刻薄的|Having a strong and unpleasant taste or smell; bitter and sharp.|The acrid smell of burning plastic filled the air.  燃烧塑料的刺鼻气味弥漫在空气中。|The critic's acrid remarks stung the young artist.  评论家刻薄的言辞刺痛了这位年轻的艺术家。|近义词:pungent, bitter, caustic, harsh|反义词:sweet, mild, pleasant|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *ācer* (sharp, pungent, bitter) + *-idus* (suffix forming adjectives).
