hurdle:/ˈhɜːrdl/ (US) /ˈhɜːdl/ (UK)| v.|克服；跳过|To jump over a hurdle; to overcome a difficulty or problem.|
He hurdled the fence. 他跳过了栅栏。
The company hurdled the financial crisis. 公司克服了金融危机。|
近义词：overcome, conquer, surmount, clear, negotiate|
反义词：fail, surrender, succumb, yield|
词性：动词

词源: From Middle English *hurdel, from Old English *hyrdel, hyrdl (“fence, sheepfold, hurdle”), from Proto-Germanic *hurdiz (“wickerwork, hurdle”), from Proto-Indo-European *kert- (“to weave, plait, twist”). Cognate with Dutch horde (“hurdle”), German Hürde (“hurdle”).
