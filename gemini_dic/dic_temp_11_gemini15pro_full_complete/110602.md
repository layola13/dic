limniad:/ˈlɪmniæd/| n.|湖泊的仙女，淡水水仙|A nymph inhabiting freshwater lakes and ponds. |例句：The limniad danced gracefully in the moonlight reflecting on the lake.  湖泊仙女在映照在湖面的月光下优雅地跳舞。

例句：Ancient Greeks believed limniads protected the purity of their waters. 古希腊人相信湖泊仙女保护着他们水域的纯洁。|近义词：naiad, water nymph|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
