despoilers:/dɪˈspɔɪlər/| n.|掠夺者，破坏者|People who pillage, plunder, or destroy something.  Those who deprive something of its value or beauty.|The despoilers looted the ancient city, leaving it in ruins. 掠夺者洗劫了这座古城，使它沦为废墟。|Synonyms: plunderers, pillagers, ravagers, looters, vandals|Antonyms: protectors, guardians, preservers|Noun
