ludificatory:/ˌluːdɪˈfɪkətɔːri/ | adj. | 嘲弄的；戏弄的；玩耍的 | Serving to mock, ridicule, or make sport of.  Designed for or intended to deceive or mislead in a playful or frivolous manner. |  His ludificatory remarks about her singing made her furious. 他对她歌声的嘲弄性评论让她大怒。 |  mocking, derisive, satirical, playful, joking | serious, respectful, reverent | adjective

ludification:/ˌluːdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 嘲弄；戏弄；玩耍 | The act of mocking, ridiculing, or making sport of someone or something. | The ludification of his efforts to learn the piano discouraged him.  对他在学习钢琴方面所做的努力的嘲弄使他灰心丧气。 | mockery, derision, ridicule | respect, reverence | noun
