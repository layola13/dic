humstrum:/ˈhʌmstrʌm/| n.  v.|  单调乏味的音乐（或讲话）;  弹奏单调乏味的音乐（或发表单调乏味的讲话）|A tedious, monotonous piece of music or speech; to perform or deliver such a piece.| He played a humstrum tune on the piano. 他在钢琴上弹奏了一曲单调乏味的曲子。| 近义词：monotonous, dull, tedious, boring | 反义词：interesting, exciting, stimulating | 词性: 名词, 动词
Etymology:  Likely a blend of "hum" (suggesting a monotonous sound) and "strum" (referring to playing a stringed instrument casually).
