aspic: /ˈæspɪk/ | n. | 肉冻，冻子 | A savory jelly made with meat stock, set in a mold and used to contain pieces of meat, fish, or vegetables. |  例句：The aspic was decorated with slices of hard-boiled egg. 肉冻上装饰着煮鸡蛋片。

例句：She served a salmon aspic as a starter. 她以三文鱼冻作为开胃菜。

近义词：jelly | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "broth" or "soup" in terms of consistency) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aspic*, from Old French *aspis*, ultimately from Latin *aspis* "asp, viper," referring to the asp's supposedly cold, motionless nature after its bite. The connection likely arose from the shivering caused by the venom.
