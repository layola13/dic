hubbing:/ˈhʌbɪŋ/
n.
中心化；轮毂加工；集线作用
The act of using a central hub or location for connecting or distributing things.
The airport serves as a hubbing point for international travel. 该机场是国际旅行的枢纽。
centralization, networking, connecting
decentralization, dispersal
