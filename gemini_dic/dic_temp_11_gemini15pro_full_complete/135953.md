nonpursuant:/ˌnɒnpərˈsuːənt/| adj.|不根据；不遵照；不相关的|Not pursuant to; not following or in accordance with (a particular law, regulation, or request).|The committee's decision was deemed nonpursuant to the company's bylaws. 委员会的决定被认为不符合公司的章程。

The judge ruled the evidence nonpursuant to the case. 法官裁定该证据与案件无关。|近义词：irrelevant, inapplicable, inappropriate, inconsistent|反义词：pursuant, according to, in accordance with|词性:形容词
