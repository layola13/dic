collectivisms:/kəˈlɛktɪvɪzəmz/
|n.|集体主义|The principles or system of ownership and control of the means of production and distribution by the people collectively, usually under the supervision of a government.|Many countries experimented with different forms of collectivisms in the 20th century.|Synonyms: communism, socialism, communalism|Antonyms: individualism, capitalism|noun
