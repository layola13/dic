correlational:/ˌkɔːrəˈleɪʃənəl/
|adj.|相关的，关联的|Relating to or based on correlation, a statistical measure of the relationship between two or more variables.  It indicates the extent to which changes in one variable are associated with changes in another. It does *not* imply causation.|The study found a correlational link between smoking and lung cancer. 这项研究发现了吸烟和肺癌之间的相关联系。|Synonyms: related, connected, associated, interdependent|Antonyms: unrelated, independent, unconnected|adjective
