nonnarratives:/nɒnˈnærətɪvz/ | n. | 非叙事作品；非叙述性内容 | Works that do not tell a story, such as essays, poems, or documentaries. |  Documentaries and scientific papers are examples of nonnarratives.  纪录片和科学论文是非叙事作品的例子。 | synonyms: expository writing, informational texts | antonyms: narratives, stories | noun

nonnarrative:/nɒnˈnærətɪv/ | adj. | 非叙事性的；非叙述的 | Not relating to or characterized by the telling of a story. | The painting's nonnarrative style focused on abstract shapes and colors. 这幅画的非叙事风格侧重于抽象的形状和颜色。 | synonyms: expository, informational, descriptive | antonyms: narrative, storytelling | adjective
