distichous: /dɪˈstɪkəs/
adjective
二列的，二行的，两列的
Arranged in two vertical rows or ranks on opposite sides of a stem.  Growing in two opposite vertical rows.
The distichous arrangement of leaves on the stem allows for maximum sunlight exposure. 茎上两列状排列的叶子可以最大限度地吸收阳光。
The plant's distichous leaves gave it a flattened appearance.  这种植物的二列状叶子使它看起来扁平。
近义词: two-ranked, bifarious
反义词: spiral, alternate, whorled
词性: adjective
