daring:/ˈdɛərɪŋ/ | adj. n. |  大胆的；勇敢的；冒险；大胆的行为 | (adj.) willing to take risks; adventurous. (n.) adventurous courage; boldness. |  (adj.) It was a daring escape from prison.  这是一次大胆的越狱。| (n.)  His daring impressed everyone. 他的勇气给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。 | 近义词: (adj.) bold, brave, courageous, adventurous (n.) bravery, courage, boldness | 反义词: (adj.) timid, cautious, cowardly (n.) timidity, cowardice | 词性: 形容词, 名词


词源: From Middle English *daring, present participle of daren (“to dare”), from Old English durran (“to dare”).
