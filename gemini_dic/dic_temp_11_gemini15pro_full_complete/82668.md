guessing:/ˈɡesɪŋ/| n. |猜测，推测|The act of trying to find the correct answer or solution without knowing all the facts.|Her guessing was correct. 她的猜测是正确的。|近义词：conjecture, supposition, surmise, speculation|反义词:knowing, certainty|名词

guessing:/ˈɡesɪŋ/| adj. |猜测的，推测的|Based on guessing, not on definite knowledge.|He gave a guessing look. 他投来一个猜测的目光。|近义词：conjectural, speculative, suppositional|反义词:certain, definite, sure|形容词
