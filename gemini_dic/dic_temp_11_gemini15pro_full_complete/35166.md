clowning: /ˈklaʊnɪŋ/ | n. | 小丑表演，扮小丑; 胡闹 | the act of behaving like a clown to make people laugh; silly behaviour |  The children enjoyed the clown's clowning. 孩子们喜欢小丑的表演。| synonyms: buffoonery, antics, tomfoolery | antonyms: seriousness, solemnity | noun


clowning: /ˈklaʊnɪŋ/ | v. | 扮小丑; 胡闹 |  behaving in a way intended to be funny | He was clowning around, trying to make her laugh. 他在扮小丑，试图让她笑。 | synonyms: joking, fooling, playing around | antonyms: behaving seriously | verb
