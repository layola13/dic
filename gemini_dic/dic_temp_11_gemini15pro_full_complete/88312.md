hippest:/ˈhɪpɪst/| adj.|最时髦的，最赶髦的，最酷的|Most fashionable, trendy, and stylish; aware of the latest trends and styles.|例句：She's the hippest girl in school. 她是学校里最时髦的女孩。|例句：That's the hippest new club in town. 那是城里最时髦的新俱乐部。|近义词：trendiest, coolest, most fashionable|反义词：unfashionable, outdated, old-fashioned|词性:形容词
词源: hip (n.) + -est (suffix). "Hip" originally meant "aware" in African American Vernacular English, evolving to mean "fashionable" in the mid-20th century.  The suffix "-est" is used to form the superlative degree of adjectives.
