go-after: /ɡoʊ ˈæftər/ /ɡəʊ ˈɑːftə(r)/

go-after: /ɡoʊ ˈæftər/ /ɡəʊ ˈɑːftə(r)/
| phrasal verb |批评，责备|To criticize or reprimand someone.|The boss went after him for being late. 老板因为他迟到而责备他。|Synonyms: criticize, reprimand, rebuke, scold|Antonyms: praise, commend, compliment|verb
