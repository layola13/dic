frippery:/ˈfrɪpəri/ | n. | 花哨的衣服；俗丽的装饰；虚饰；无聊琐事 | Showy or unnecessary ornament in architecture, dress, writing, etc.; something trivial or nonessential. |  She dismissed the novel as mere frippery. 她认为这部小说华而不实，不屑一顾。 | synonyms: finery, adornment, embellishment, gewgaws, trinkets, baubles, folderol | antonyms: simplicity, plainness | noun

frippery:/ˈfrɪpəri/ | n. | 旧衣服；廉价的衣服 | Secondhand clothes; cheap finery.  | She found some interesting frippery at the vintage clothing store. 她在复古服装店找到了一些有趣的旧衣服。 | synonyms: old clothes, secondhand clothes, rags | antonyms: new clothes, high fashion | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *friperie "old clothes, rags," from friper "to rub, wear down," of Germanic origin.
