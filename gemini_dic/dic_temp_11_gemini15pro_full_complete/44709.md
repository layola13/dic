crueler:/ˈkruːələr/ (US), /ˈkrʊələr/ (UK)| adj.|更残酷的，更残忍的|Comparative form of cruel. cruel:/ˈkruːəl/ (US), /ˈkrʊəl/ (UK)| adj.|残酷的，残忍的|Disposed to inflict pain or suffering; causing pain or suffering.|It was a cruel and unusual punishment. 这是一种残酷且不寻常的惩罚。|The children were cruel to the small animals. 孩子们对小动物很残忍。|近义词：brutal, heartless, savage, vicious|反义词：kind, gentle, compassionate, humane|词性:形容词
