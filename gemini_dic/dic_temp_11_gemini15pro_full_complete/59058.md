dweller:/ˈdwɛlər/| n.|居民，居住者|A person or animal that lives in a particular place. |例句：City dwellers often face high living costs. 城市居民通常面临高昂的生活成本。

例句：Cave dwellers sought shelter from the elements. 穴居人寻找躲避风雨的地方。

近义词：resident, inhabitant, occupant, tenant|反义词: nomad, transient|词性:noun
