carcinogen:/kɑːrˈsɪnədʒən/ /kɑːrˈsɪnəˌdʒɛn/| n. |致癌物|A substance capable of causing cancer in living tissue.|例句：Asbestos is a known carcinogen. 石棉是一种已知的致癌物。

例句：Many carcinogens are found in cigarette smoke.  香烟烟雾中含有许多致癌物质。


近义词：cancer-causing agent, cancer promoter|反义词:anticarcinogen|词性:noun
