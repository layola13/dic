duskier:/ˈdʌskɪər/ | adj. | 更昏暗的，更朦胧的 |Comparative form of dusky: more dusky.  Meaning even darker or more shadowy than something already described as dusky. | The room grew duskier as the sun began to set. 房间随着太阳开始落山而变得更加昏暗。| The duskier corners of the forest held an air of mystery. 森林里更昏暗的角落里弥漫着神秘的气氛。 | synonyms: darker, dimmer, gloomier | antonyms: brighter, lighter, clearer | adjective
