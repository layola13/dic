giantize:/ˈdʒaɪəntaɪz/ |v. |巨大化；使巨大|To make something gigantic or enormous.  To cause something to increase greatly in size, importance, or intensity.|They giantized the image of the ant for the movie poster.|近义词: enlarge, magnify, amplify, boost, augment|反义词: miniaturize, shrink, reduce, diminish|词性:动词
词源: giant (巨人) + -ize (动词后缀，表示“使成为”)
