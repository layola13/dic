bedrooms:/ˈbedruːmz/ /ˈbedrʊmz/
n.
卧室（复数）
A room furnished and used for sleeping.
The house has four bedrooms and two bathrooms. 这房子有四个卧室和两个浴室。

The hotel offers a variety of bedrooms, from single to suites.  这家酒店提供各种类型的卧室，从单人间到套房。

近义词: chambers, sleeping rooms
反义词:  living rooms, kitchens
词性: 名词
