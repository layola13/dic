nondecayed:/ˌnɒndɪˈkeɪd/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈkeɪd/
adjective
未腐烂的；未衰变的
Not having decayed or rotted.
The archaeologist found a nondecayed wooden artifact. 考古学家发现了一个未腐烂的木制工艺品。
The apples were surprisingly nondecayed after being stored for so long.  这些苹果储存了这么久竟然没有腐烂，真是令人惊讶。
近义词:undecayed, intact, preserved, fresh
反义词:decayed, rotten, decomposed, putrid
词性:adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
decayed (past participle of decay): from Old French *decair "to fall, decline, fail," from Latin *cadere "to fall."
