dittying:/ˈdɪtiɪŋ/
verb
哼唱小曲;  创作小曲
The act of composing or singing ditties.
She was dittying a cheerful tune as she walked along. 她一路走一路哼着欢快的小曲。
synonyms: singing, humming, composing, writing songs
antonyms:  being silent, quiet,  speechless
词性: 动词(gerund or present participle)
