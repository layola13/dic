bouilli:/ˈbuːjiː/ (US), /ˈbuːjiː/ (UK)| n.|炖肉，煮熟的牛肉|Boiled or stewed meat, especially beef.  It often refers to a cheaper cut of meat cooked for a long time.|The bouilli was surprisingly tender and flavorful. 这炖肉出奇地嫩滑可口。|Synonyms: stew, pot roast|Antonyms: rare, raw|Noun
