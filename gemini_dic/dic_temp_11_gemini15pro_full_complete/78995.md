gisle:/ˈɡɪslə/ | n. | 冰晶, 薄冰 | A thin coating of ice, especially formed from frozen dew or rain; a glaze. |  The trees were covered in a delicate gisle this morning. 今早树木上覆盖着一层薄薄的冰晶。| 近义词: glaze, rime, frost | 反义词: thaw | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old Norse *gisla (“to shine, glitter”). Related to Old English gīsl (“hostage”). The connection is probably via the sense of something that is “held fast”.
