amice: /ˈæmɪs/ | n. |  肩衣，披肩；朋友，伙伴 | A liturgical vestment, a white linen cloth worn over the cassock and under the alb by priests and deacons. Also, an archaic term for a friend or companion. | The priest carefully adjusted his amice before beginning the Mass.  神父在开始弥撒前仔细地调整了他的肩衣。|  Synonym: (for vestment) vestment, alb; (for friend) friend, comrade, companion | Antonym: (for friend) enemy, foe | noun
