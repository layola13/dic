lama:/ˈlɑːmə/ | n. |美洲驼；喇嘛|A South American mammal related to the camel, but smaller and without a hump. / A Tibetan Buddhist monk. |例句：The lama led the monks in prayer. 喇嘛带领僧侣们祈祷。

例句：We saw a herd of llamas grazing in the Andes. 我们看到一群美洲驼在安第斯山脉吃草。|近义词：(for monk) monk, priest; (for animal) alpaca, vicuña|反义词: None |词性:noun
