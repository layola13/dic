clingiest:/ˈklɪŋɡɪɪst/| adj.|最粘人的，最依赖的|Superlative form of clingy: most clingy.  Describes someone who is excessively attached or dependent on another person and demands a lot of attention and reassurance.|例句：My cat is the clingiest creature I've ever met; he follows me everywhere. 我的猫是我见过的最粘人的生物；他到处跟着我。

例句：She became incredibly clingiest after the breakup, constantly needing reassurance. 分手后，她变得异常粘人，不断需要安慰。|近义词：dependent, needy, attached, possessive|反义词：independent, detached, aloof|词性:形容词
