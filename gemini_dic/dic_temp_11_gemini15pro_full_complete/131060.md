nonbarbarian:/ˌnɒnˈbɑːrˈbeəriən/ | n. | 文明人 | A civilized person. | He considered himself a nonbarbarian, appreciating art and philosophy. 他认为自己是一个文明人，欣赏艺术和哲学。| Civilized person, cultured individual | Barbarian, savage | noun


Etymology:
From non- + barbarian.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "barbarian" comes from the Greek "barbaros," originally referring to someone who did not speak Greek, and later extended to mean uncivilized or savage.
