deadest:/ˈdedɪst/| adj.|最死的，最无生气的，最沉闷的|Superlative form of dead: most dead.  It emphasizes the absence of life, activity, or excitement to the greatest degree.|The party was the deadest I've ever been to.  这个派对是我参加过的最沉闷的。|The deadest town I've ever visited had only one store. 我去过的最死气沉沉的小镇只有一家商店。|近义词：lifeless, inanimate, dull, boring|反义词:liveliest, vibrant, exciting|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dēad, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch dood and German tot, from an Indo-European root shared by Latin de- in deficere ‘fail’, and Greek dēios ‘hostile’.
