agnation:/æɡˈneɪʃən/
n.
父系亲属关系
Relationship through the male line; kinship on the father's side.
The laws of inheritance favoured agnation over cognation. 继承法偏向于父系血亲关系而不是母系血亲关系。
近义词：kinship, lineage, descent
反义词：cognation
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin agnatio (“relationship by males”), from agnatus (“related on the father's side”), from ad- (“to”) + natus (“born”).
