incredulousness:/ˌɪnkrɪˈdʒuːləsnəs/| n. |怀疑；不相信；难以置信|The state of being unwilling or unable to believe something.|Her incredulousness was evident in her raised eyebrows and skeptical tone. 她的怀疑从她扬起的眉毛和怀疑的语气中显露无疑。|Synonyms: disbelief, skepticism, doubt|Antonyms: belief, faith, trust|noun

incredulous:/ɪnˈkrɛdʒʊləs/| adj. |怀疑的；不相信的；难以置信的|Unwilling or unable to believe something.|He gave me an incredulous look when I told him the story. 当我给他讲这个故事时，他给了我一个怀疑的眼神。|Synonyms: skeptical, doubtful, dubious|Antonyms: believing, trusting, credulous|adjective
