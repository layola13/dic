lacunal:/ləˈkjuːnl/ | adj. |腔隙的；陷落的；缺损的|Relating to, resembling, or characterized by a lacuna; containing lacunae.  A lacuna is a gap, missing part, or cavity.|The lacunal stroke affected his speech. 腔隙性中风影响了他的语言能力。|Synonyms: gap-like, cavity-ridden, perforated, pitted|Antonyms: complete, whole, solid, continuous|adjective

lacunar:/ləˈkjuːnər/ | adj. |腔隙的；陷落的；缺损的|Relating to, resembling, or characterized by a lacuna; containing lacunae. A lacuna is a gap, missing part, or cavity.|The radiologist identified lacunar infarcts in the patient's brain. 放射科医生在病人的大脑中发现了腔隙性梗死。|Synonyms: gap-like, cavity-ridden, perforated, pitted|Antonyms: complete, whole, solid, continuous|adjective
