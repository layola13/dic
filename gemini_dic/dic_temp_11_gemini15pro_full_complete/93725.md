immanifest:/ɪmˈmænɪfɛst/
adjective
未显现的，不明显的
Not manifested; not revealed or apparent.
The immanifest potential of the young artist was evident only to his teacher.  这位年轻艺术家的未显现的潜力只有他的老师才能看得出来。
Near Synonyms: latent, dormant, undeveloped, hidden, concealed
Antonyms: manifest, evident, obvious, apparent, clear
词性: 形容词
