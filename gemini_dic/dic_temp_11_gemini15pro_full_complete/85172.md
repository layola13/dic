harebell:/ˈhɛərˌbɛl/ | n. | 蓝铃花；圆叶风铃草 | A delicate bell-shaped flower, typically purplish-blue, of northern temperate regions.  | Example: The harebells swayed gently in the breeze.  蓝铃花在微风中轻轻摇曳。 | Example:  A patch of harebells carpeted the meadow. 一片蓝铃花覆盖了草地。 | Synonym: bluebell, bellflower | Antonym: N/A | noun
