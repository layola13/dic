energizes:/ˈɛnərˌdʒaɪzɪz/
verb
使充满活力；给予…能量
Gives energy or vitality to something or someone.
The coach's speech energized the team before the big game. 教练的讲话在比赛前激励了球队。
invigorates, stimulates, enlivens
drains, weakens, exhausts
词性:动词
