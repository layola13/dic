outflowing:/ˌaʊtˈfloʊɪŋ/
adjective
流出的，外流的
flowing out; moving outwards.
例句：The outflowing tide revealed hidden rocks. 退潮露出了隐藏的岩石。

例句：The outflowing river carried sediment downstream.  流出的河水将沉积物带到下游。

近义词：egressing, exiting, emanating, issuing, discharging
反义词：inflowing, incoming

词性: adjective

词源:  From "out-" + "flowing," present participle of "flow."  "Out-" indicates direction away from a source or center, while "flow" refers to the movement of a liquid or other substance.
