balloon:/bəˈluːn/ (US), /bəˈluːn/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|气球，热气球；迅速增加|A balloon is a bag made of thin rubber or other light material, which is inflated with air or gas so that it becomes light and floats in the air.  |The child's balloon floated up into the sky.  孩子的气球飘上了天空。|近义词：blimp, airship|反义词: - |noun, verb

balloon:/bəˈluːn/ (US), /bəˈluːn/ (UK)| vi. vt.|迅速增加，激增；膨胀|To increase rapidly in size or amount.|Her debts ballooned after she lost her job. 她失业后债务激增。|近义词：swell, expand, inflate|反义词: shrink, decrease|verb
