nonflux:/ˈnɑːnˌflʌks/
noun
无流动；静止；无变化
absence of flow or movement;stasis; lack of change
The nonflux of the market worried investors. 市场毫无波动，令投资者担忧。
近义词:stasis, stagnation, inactivity
反义词:flux, flow, movement
词性:n.


Etymology:
From non- + flux.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "absence of," and "flux" comes from the Latin "fluxus," meaning "a flowing."
