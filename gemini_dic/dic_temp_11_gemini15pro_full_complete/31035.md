chamberer:/ˈtʃeɪmbərər/| n.|侍从，管理房间的人|A person who attends to the private chambers of a sovereign or nobleman. |例句：The chamberer prepared the king's bedchamber for the night. 国王的侍从为国王准备好了夜间的卧室。

例句：The queen's chamberer was always at her side. 女王的侍从总是在她身边。|近义词：valet, attendant, servant|反义词:master, lord|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *chambriere, from chambre "chamber." Ultimately from Latin camera "vaulted room."
