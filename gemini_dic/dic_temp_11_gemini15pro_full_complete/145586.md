overconcentration: /ˌoʊvərˌkɑːnsənˈtreɪʃən/ | n. | 过度集中，过度关注 |  Excessive focus on one thing, often to the detriment of others. |  His overconcentration on work led to him neglecting his family.  他过度专注于工作导致他忽略了家人。 | 近义词: fixation, obsession | 反义词: diversification, balance | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "concentration" (from Latin "concentra" - to combine into a center).
