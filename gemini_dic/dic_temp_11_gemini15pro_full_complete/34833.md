clingingness:/ˈklɪŋɪŋnɪs/
n.
依恋; 黏人; 执着
The state of being clinging, excessively dependent, or emotionally needy.  A tendency to stay very close to someone, both physically and emotionally.
Her clingingness suffocated him. 她的黏人让他感到窒息。
近义词：neediness, dependence, attachment
反义词：independence, detachment, self-reliance
词性：名词

cling:/klɪŋ/
v.
粘附; 紧贴; 坚持
1. To hold on tightly to something. 2. To remain close to someone, often for emotional support or reassurance.
The wet shirt clung to his back. 湿衬衫紧贴在他的背上。
He clung to the hope that she would return. 他紧紧抓住她会回来的希望。
近义词：adhere, stick, hold, grasp
反义词：release, detach, let go
词性：动词
