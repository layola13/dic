devilish:/ˈdevəlɪʃ/ (US), /ˈdɛvəlɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|恶魔般的；邪恶的；恶作剧的；极糟糕的；很难对付的|Resembling or characteristic of a devil, especially in being mischievous or wicked; also, extremely difficult or unpleasant.|例句：She has a devilish sense of humor. 她有一种恶作剧般的幽默感。|例句：This puzzle is devilish hard. 这个谜题非常难。|近义词：wicked, evil, mischievous, fiendish, diabolical|反义词：angelic, saintly|词性:形容词
