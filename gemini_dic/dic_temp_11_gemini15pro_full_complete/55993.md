dns:/ˌdiː ɛnˈɛs/| n. |域名系统 (Domain Name System)|A hierarchical distributed naming system for computers, services, or other resources connected to the Internet or a private network. It associates various information with domain names assigned to each of the participating entities. |Example 1: The DNS server wasn't responding, so I couldn't access the website.  域名服务器没有响应，所以我无法访问该网站。

Example 2:  Configuring your DNS settings correctly is crucial for internet access. 正确配置 DNS 设置对于互联网访问至关重要。|近义词:domain name service|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
