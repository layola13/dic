nonscholastic:/ˌnɑːn skəˈlæstɪk/
adjective
非学业的;课外的
Not related to school or academics; extracurricular.
例句：Nonscholastic activities are important for a child's development. 课外活动对孩子的发展很重要。

例句：She excelled in nonscholastic pursuits like sports and music. 她在体育和音乐等非学业的追求上表现出色。

近义词：extracurricular, nonacademic
反义词：academic, scholastic
词性：adjective
