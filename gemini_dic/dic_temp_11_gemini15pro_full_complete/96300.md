indigenous:/ɪnˈdɪdʒənəs/| adj.|土生土长的，本地的，固有的|originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; native.|例句：The kangaroo is indigenous to Australia.  袋鼠是澳大利亚土生土长的动物。

例句：The indigenous people of this land have a deep respect for nature.  这片土地的原住民对自然怀有深深的敬意。|近义词：native, aboriginal, endemic|反义词：foreign, alien, exotic|词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *indigenus, from Latin indigena (“sprung from the land, native”), from indu (“in, within”) + gignere, genui, genitus (“to bear, beget”)
