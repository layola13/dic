hejira:/hɪˈdʒɪərə/ /hɪˈdʒiːrə/
noun
逃亡，迁徙; (尤指)穆罕默德从麦加逃往麦地那
An exodus or migration, especially Muhammad's flight from Mecca to Medina.
例句：The family made their hejira from the war-torn country.  这家人从饱受战争蹂躏的国家逃了出来。

例句：His sudden departure felt like a hejira, leaving his responsibilities behind. 他的突然离开就像是一场逃亡，抛下了他的责任。

近义词：exodus, flight, migration, escape
反义词：return, arrival, immigration
