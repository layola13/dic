low-income:/ˌloʊˈɪnkʌm/ |adj.|低收入的|Having little income; earning little money.|Many low-income families struggle to afford basic necessities. 许多低收入家庭难以负担基本生活必需品。|Low-income housing is often subsidized by the government. 政府通常会补贴低收入住房。|近义词：poor, impoverished, disadvantaged|反义词：wealthy, affluent, high-income|词性:形容词

词源:  "low" (Old English "lah," meaning "not high, humble") + "income" (from "in-" + "come," meaning money received)
