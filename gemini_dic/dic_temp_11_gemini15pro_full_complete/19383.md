bicallose:/baɪˈkæləʊs/| adj.|胼胝质的；富含胼胝质的|Relating to, or containing callose, a plant polysaccharide.  A plant carbohydrate that forms a hard protective layer.|Callose deposition is prominent at the sieve plates of bicallose sieve elements.|近义词: callosic|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-callosic, but this isn't a standard term)|词性: adjective
