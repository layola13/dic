non-regression:/ˌnɒn rɪˈɡrɛʃən/ | n. | 非回归 | The absence of regression; the state of not returning to a previous, usually worse or less developed state. |  After the bug fix, we ran non-regression tests to ensure the software still functioned correctly.  修复bug后，我们运行了非回归测试，以确保软件仍然正常运行。 | synonyms: stability, maintenance, continuation | antonyms: regression, relapse, deterioration | noun

non-regression:/ˌnɑːn rɪˈɡrɛʃən/ | adj. | 非回归的 |  Related to or designed to prevent regression. | We implemented non-regression testing as part of our development process. 我们在开发过程中实施了非回归测试。| synonyms: preventative, protective, stabilizing | antonyms: regressive | adjective
