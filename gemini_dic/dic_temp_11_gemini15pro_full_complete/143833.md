outperformance:/ˌaʊtpərˈfɔːrməns/ | n. | 超额表现，优于市场表现 | The fund's outperformance was attributed to its focus on technology stocks. 该基金的超额表现归功于其对科技股的关注。 | Example: The company's outperformance of its competitors was significant.  这家公司相对于其竞争对手的超额表现非常显著。 | Synonyms: exceeding, surpassing, beating | Antonyms: underperformance, lagging | noun

outperform:/ˌaʊtpərˈfɔːrm/ | v. | 超额表现，胜过 | The company has consistently outperformed the market. 这家公司一直以来都优于市场表现。| Example:  The investment portfolio outperformed the benchmark index.  该投资组合的表现优于基准指数。| Synonyms: excel, surpass, exceed  | Antonyms: underperform, lag | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + perform + -ance.  "Out-" prefix signifies exceeding or surpassing. "Perform" means to carry out, achieve, or fulfill. "-ance" creates a noun indicating the act or state of performing.
