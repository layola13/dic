deviant:/ˈdiːviənt/ /ˈdiːvjənt/| n. adj.|偏差者，异常的；离经叛道的|A deviant is a person who behaves in a way that is not considered normal or morally correct by most people.  As an adjective, it describes such behavior.|
例句：The study focused on the behavior of social deviants. 这项研究的重点是社会偏差者的行为。
例句：His deviant behavior led to his expulsion from school.  他偏离常规的行为导致他被学校开除。
近义词：abnormal, atypical, unconventional, nonconformist, aberrant, irregular, eccentric|
反义词：normal, conventional, typical, conformist|
词性：noun, adjective


deviants:/ˈdiːviənts/ /ˈdiːvjənts/| n.|偏差者，不正常的人|The plural form of "deviant," referring to multiple individuals who exhibit deviant behavior.|
例句：The researchers interviewed a group of social deviants to understand their motivations.  研究人员采访了一群社会偏差者，以了解他们的动机。
例句：Society often ostracizes deviants. 社会常常排斥不正常的人。
近义词：nonconformists, misfits, outcasts|
反义词：conformists|
词性：noun


词源:  From Late Latin *deviant- (stem of devians), present participle of deviare "to turn aside," from de- "off, away from" + via "way."
