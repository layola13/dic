interrogatingly:/ˌɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪtɪŋli/| adv.|疑问地，质问地|In a manner that suggests questioning or seeking information.  Derived from the verb "interrogate" (from Latin interrogare, from inter- 'between' + rogare 'ask'), with the suffix "-ingly" indicating manner.|She looked at him interrogatingly, waiting for an explanation.|近义词：questioningly, inquiringly, searchingly|反义词:affirmatively, declaratively|词性:副词
