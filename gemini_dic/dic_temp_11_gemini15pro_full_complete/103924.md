judgemental:/dʒʌdʒˈmentl/ | adj. | 判断的，爱评判的 | Having or displaying a tendency to make judgements (especially moral judgements) about others. |  She cast a judgemental eye over his scruffy clothes. 她带着评判的目光打量着他邋遢的衣服。

| Example: He made a judgemental remark about her lifestyle. 他对她的生活方式发表了评判性的言论。 | Synonyms: critical, censorious, disapproving | Antonyms: accepting, tolerant, non-judgemental | word type: adjective
