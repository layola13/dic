hath:/hæθ/
|v.|有; 持有 (古英语，相当于has)|Archaic third-person singular present tense of have. |He hath a horse. 他有一匹马。|Synonym: has|Antonym: lacks
|verb|


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He hath a book. 他有一本书。
She hath a beautiful voice. 她有一副好嗓子。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
This tense is not used with "hath" as it is an archaic form.  "Hath" cannot be used in a continuous tense.  现代英语不用hath表示进行时。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
This tense is not used with "hath." "Hath" is an archaic form and does not have perfect tense conjugations.  现代英语不用hath表示完成时。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
This tense is not used with "hath." "Hath" is an archaic form and does not have perfect continuous tense conjugations.  现代英语不用hath表示完成进行时。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
This would be "had."  "Hath" is specifically present tense.  过去时用had。
He had a book. 他曾经有一本书。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
This tense would use "had" with a different construction, as "hath" is present tense and cannot be used in continuous tenses. 过去进行时用was/were having.
He was having a good time. 他当时玩得很开心.

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
This tense would use "had."  过去完成时用had。
He had had a book. 他曾经有过一本书。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
This tense is not applicable to "hath." 过去完成进行时用had been having。
He had been having a good time. 他一直玩得很开心。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
This tense will use "will have". 将来时用will have。
He will have a book. 他将要有一本书。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This tense will use "will be having." 将来进行时用will be having。
He will be having a good time. 他将玩得很开心。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
This tense will use "will have had." 将来完成时用will have had。
He will have had a book. 他将已经有过一本书。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
This tense will use "will have been having." 将来完成进行时用will have been having.
He will have been having a good time. 他将一直玩得很开心。


虚拟语气:
If he hath the money, he will buy it. 如果他有钱，他会买它。（很少用，一般用If he has...）
I wish she hath a car. 我希望她有一辆车。（很少用，一般用I wish she had...）

被动语态:
被动语态不适用于"hath". 被动语态用is/are/was/were had (很少用，一般用is/are/was/were owned/possessed).

疑问句:
Hath he a car? 他有车吗? (古英语用法, 现代英语用Does he have a car?)

否定句:
He hath not a car. 他没有车。(古英语用法, 现代英语用He does not have a car.)
