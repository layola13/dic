delayers:/dɪˈleɪərz/| n.| 拖延者，延迟者| People who delay things; procrastinators. | The delayers always submit their work at the last minute.  拖延者总是最后一刻才交作业。| Near synonyms: procrastinators, laggards, stragglers | Antonyms: doers, go-getters, early birds | Noun

delayers:/dɪˈleɪərz/| n.| 延迟器，延时器| Devices or mechanisms that cause a delay. | The delayers in the circuit ensure the signal arrives at the correct time. 电路中的延迟器确保信号在正确的时间到达。 | Near synonyms: timers, retarders | Antonyms: accelerators, hasteners | Noun
