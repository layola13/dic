nimbose:/ˈnɪmbəʊs/ (US), /ˈnɪmbəʊs/ (UK)| adj.|雨云密布的，乌云密布的；蓬松的，浓密的|Covered with nimbus clouds; dark and stormy. Also, resembling a nimbus cloud in density or form.|The nimbose sky hinted at an impending storm.  乌云密布的天空预示着一场即将来临的暴风雨。|The peacock displayed its nimbose plumage.  孔雀展示了它浓密的羽毛。|近义词：cloudy, overcast, dark, stormy, dense, thick|反义词：clear, bright, sunny|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *nimbosus, from nimbus (“rain cloud”).
