dandling:/ˈdændlɪŋ/| adj.| 悬挂的，摇晃的，吊着的 | hanging loosely and swinging lightly; being treated in a playful or indulgent way, often by an older person | The monkey was dandling from the tree branch. 猴子悬挂在树枝上摇晃。 The grandfather was dandling his grandson on his knee. 祖父把孙子放在膝盖上逗弄。 | synonyms: swinging, dangling, swaying | antonyms: fixed, immobile, stationary | adjective
