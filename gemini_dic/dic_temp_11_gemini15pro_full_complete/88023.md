high-visibility:/ˌhaɪ vɪzəˈbɪləti/ | adj. |高能见度的；显眼的|Easily seen, especially because of bright colours. Designed to be easily seen, especially in poor light or dangerous conditions.|例句：Construction workers are required to wear high-visibility vests. 建筑工人被要求穿高能见度背心。

high-visibility:/ˌhaɪ vɪzəˈbɪləti/ | n. |(高能见度状态/事物)|The state or quality of being easily seen.|例句：The police presence ensured high visibility at the event.  警察的出现确保了活动的醒目性。


近义词：conspicuous, noticeable, prominent, bright, fluorescent|反义词:inconspicuous, hidden, concealed|词性:adjective, noun
