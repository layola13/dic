aquamarine:/ˌækwəməˈriːn/| n. adj.|海蓝宝石，碧绿色|A transparent blue-green variety of beryl used as a gemstone.  Also, the color of this gemstone.|例句：Her aquamarine eyes sparkled in the sunlight. 她的海蓝宝石般的眼睛在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：She wore an aquamarine pendant. 她戴着一个海蓝宝石吊坠。|近义词：beryl, turquoise, teal, cyan|反义词: (No direct antonym for a color, could consider contrasting colors like) red, orange|词性: noun, adjective
