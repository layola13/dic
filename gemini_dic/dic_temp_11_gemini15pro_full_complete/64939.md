established: /ɪˈstæblɪʃt/
adj.|已建立的，已确定的，公认的| firmly settled or accepted; having been in existence for a long time| The company is an established leader in the industry.  这家公司是业内公认的领导者。|例句：He's an established author with a large following. 他是一位拥有众多粉丝的知名作家。|近义词：recognized, accepted, proven, settled, founded|反义词：new, recent, unproven, unstable, fledgling
词性：形容词


established: /ɪˈstæblɪʃt/
v.|建立，设立，确定|to set up or create (something that lasts for a long time)| The company was established in 1990.  这家公司成立于1990年。|例句：They established a new research center at the university. 他们在大学里建立了一个新的研究中心。|近义词：found, create, set up, institute, organize|反义词：abolish, destroy, dismantle, end, terminate
词性：动词


词源:
From Middle English *establissen, from Old French establir, establiss-, present stem of establir, from Latin stabiliō (“I make stable, establish”), from stabilis (“stable, steadfast”).
