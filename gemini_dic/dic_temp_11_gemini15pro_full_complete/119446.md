microliths:/ˈmaɪkrəlɪθs/
n.
微石器;小型石器
Tiny stone tools, typically of the Mesolithic period, often part of composite tools.
Archaeologists unearthed numerous microliths at the prehistoric site. 考古学家在史前遗址挖掘出大量微石器。

microliths:/ˈmaɪkrəlɪθs/
n.
微结石
A minute concretion, especially in the kidney.
The patient was diagnosed with kidney stones composed of microliths.  该病人被诊断出患有由微结石组成的肾结石。

Etymology:  From Greek *mīkrolíthos*, from *mīkrós* "small" + *líthos* "stone".
近义词: flints, chips (in the archaeological sense)
反义词: megaliths (opposite in size)
词性: noun
