headships:/ˈhɛdʃɪps/| n.|领导地位；首领职位；领导权|The position or role of being a head, leader, or chief.  The concept often relates to authority, responsibility, and oversight within a specific group or organization.|She assumed the headship of the department after the previous director retired. 她接替前任主任担任了该部门的领导。|The headship of the family traditionally falls to the eldest son.  家族的领导权传统上落到长子身上。|synonyms: leadership, authority, command, control|antonyms: subordination, subservience|noun
