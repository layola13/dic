ascitical: /æsˈɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 苦行的；禁欲主义的 |  Like an ascetic, characterized by or suggesting the practice of severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons. |  He lived an ascitical life, devoid of all luxury. 他过着苦行的生活，没有任何奢侈享受。|  The monk's ascitical existence involved fasting and meditation.  这位僧侣的禁欲生活包括禁食和冥想。| 近义词: ascetic, austere, spartan | 反义词: hedonistic, luxurious, indulgent | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *asciticus, from Greek askētikos "practicing rigorous self-discipline," from askētēs "monk, hermit, athlete," from askein "to exercise, to train."
