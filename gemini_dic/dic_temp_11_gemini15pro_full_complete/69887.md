fertilely:/ˈfɜːrtaɪlli/ | adv. | 肥沃地，丰饶地，多产地 | In a fertile manner; productively. | The valley's fertilely irrigated fields yielded abundant crops.  山谷里肥沃的灌溉田地带来了丰收。| Synonyms: productively, abundantly, prolifically | Antonyms: barrenly, unproductively, sparsely | adverb

Etymology:
From *fertile* + *-ly*. *Fertile* comes from Old French *fertil*, from Latin *fertilis* "bearing in abundance, fruitful, fertile," from *ferre* "to bear, carry."
