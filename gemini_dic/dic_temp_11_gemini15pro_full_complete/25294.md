bugbear:/ˈbʌɡˌbɛr/ (US), /ˈbʌɡbɛː/ (UK)| n. | 讨厌的东西；令人烦恼的事物；难对付的问题 |Something that causes worry or irritation; a persistent source of annoyance. |例句：

Traffic jams are a real bugbear of mine.
交通堵塞真让我烦。

His main bugbear is people who talk loudly on their phones in public.
他最讨厌的是那些在公共场合大声打电话的人。


|近义词：pet peeve, annoyance, bête noire, bane, problem, difficulty|反义词：delight, pleasure, joy|词性:名词
