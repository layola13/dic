gatehouse:/ˈɡeɪthaʊs/ | n. | 门房，守卫室 | A small building at the entrance to a property, often attached to a gate, typically occupied by a gatekeeper or guard.  |  The gatehouse was manned by a security guard.  门房里有一个保安人员。 |  The old gatehouse provided a charming entrance to the estate.  古老的门房为庄园提供了一个迷人的入口。 | synonyms: lodge, gate lodge, porter's lodge | antonyms: N/A | noun
