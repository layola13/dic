apostille:/əˈpɒstiːl/ /əˈpɑːstiːl/
noun
认证，加签
A certificate that authenticates the signature of a public official on a document, so that the document can be recognized in another country that is a member of the Hague Convention.
The lawyer advised her to get an apostille for her diploma before moving abroad.  律师建议她在移居国外之前为她的文凭取得认证。

近义词:certification, authentication, legalization
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) invalidation, rejection
