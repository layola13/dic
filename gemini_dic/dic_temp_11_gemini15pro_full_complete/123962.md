moringic: /mɔːˈrɪndʒɪk/ | adj. | 晨露般的，清新的 | Relating to or resembling the dew of morning; fresh.  | The moringic air invigorated her as she stepped outside.  晨露般的空气让她走出屋外时精神焕发。| 近义词: dewy, fresh, crisp | 反义词: stale, musty | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Likely a neologism or a rare word formed from "morning" and the suffix "-ic," which forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characteristic of."  "Morning" comes from Old English "morgen," meaning "morning."
