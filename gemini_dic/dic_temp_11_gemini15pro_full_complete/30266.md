Celtics: /ˈsɛltɪks/ | n. | 波士顿凯尔特人队；凯尔特人（指古代欧洲的凯尔特人） |  A professional basketball team based in Boston, Massachusetts, also refers to the group of peoples who lived in ancient Europe. |  例句：The Celtics won the NBA championship.  波士顿凯尔特人队赢得了NBA总冠军。 | 近义词: Boston Celtics | 反义词:  (For the team, any rival team like Lakers)  (对于球队来说，任何竞争对手，比如湖人队)| 词性: noun


Celtics: /ˈkɛltɪks/ | n. | 凯尔特人（指古代欧洲的凯尔特人）| Referring to the group of peoples who lived in parts of Europe during the Iron Age. |例句：The Celts were known for their intricate metalwork. 凯尔特人以其复杂的金属制品而闻名。| 近义词: Gauls (部分情况下) | 反义词: Romans (历史上的对手) | 词性: noun
