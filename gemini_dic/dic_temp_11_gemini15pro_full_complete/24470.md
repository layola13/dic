britishnesses:/ˈbrɪtɪʃnɪsɪz/
noun
英国特质；英国气派；英国作风

He embodies several stereotypical Britishnesses, such as a love of tea and queuing. 他体现了几种典型的英国特质，例如热爱茶和排队。
Near Synonyms: Britishness, British traits, British characteristics
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider foreignness, un-Britishness)
