noneloquent:/ˌnɑnˈɛləkwənt/
| adj. |不雄辩的，缺乏口才的|Lacking eloquence; not fluent or persuasive in speech or writing.|His noneloquent speech failed to impress the audience. 他的不雄辩的演讲未能打动观众。|Synonyms: inarticulate, tongue-tied, halting, faltering|Antonyms: eloquent, articulate, fluent, persuasive|adjective
