hematosin:/ˌhiːməˈtoʊsɪn/ /ˌhɛməˈtoʊsɪn/| n. |血色素，血红素|A red coloring matter of the blood; heme.|Hematosin is essential for oxygen transport in the blood. 血红素对血液中的氧气输送至关重要。|The doctor explained that the patient's low hematosin levels were causing fatigue. 医生解释说，病人血红素水平低导致疲劳。|近义词：heme, hemoglobin|反义词: None|词性:noun
