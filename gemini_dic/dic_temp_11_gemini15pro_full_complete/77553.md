GDPP:/ˈdʒiː diː piː piː/ | n. | 人均国内生产总值 (Gross Domestic Product per capita) |  The total value of goods produced and services provided in a country during one year, divided by the country's average population. | The GDPP of the United States is significantly higher than that of many developing nations. 美国的人均国内生产总值明显高于许多发展中国家。|  GDP per capita, per capita income |  N/A | noun
