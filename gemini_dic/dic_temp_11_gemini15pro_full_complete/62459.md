endorsingly:/ɪnˈdɔːrsɪŋli/
adv.
认可地，赞同地
in a way that shows support or approval
He spoke endorsingly of the new policy. 他赞许地谈到了这项新政策。
近义词: approvingly, supportively, favorably
反义词: disapprovingly, critically, negatively
词性: 副词

词源:  From "endorse" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Endorse" comes from Old French "endoser"  meaning "to write on the back of," from "en-" (in) + "dos" (back).  The meaning shifted to "give support to" figuratively.
