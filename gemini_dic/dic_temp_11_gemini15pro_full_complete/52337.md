dictagraph:/ˈdɪktəɡræf/ |n.|录音机，侦听器|A dictagraph is a device that records sound, often secretly.  It can also refer to a type of interoffice communication system.|The detective planted a dictagraph in the suspect's office.  侦探在嫌疑人的办公室里安装了窃听器。|Synonyms:  recording device, wiretap, bug, listening device|Antonyms:  None (lack of a direct antonym for a recording device)|noun

dictaphone:/ˈdɪktəfəʊn/ |n.|口述录音机|A dictaphone is a small sound recorder used to record speech for later transcription, typically used for dictation.|She used a dictaphone to record her notes for the book. 她用口述录音机记录了书的笔记。|Synonyms: voice recorder, dictating machine|Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for a recording device)|noun
