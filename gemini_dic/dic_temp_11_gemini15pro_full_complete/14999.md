babe-in-the-woods:/beɪb ɪn ðə wʊdz/| n.|涉世未深的人；天真无邪的人|A naive, innocent, or inexperienced person, easily fooled or victimized.|She was a babe in the woods when she first moved to the big city, and quickly learned some hard lessons. 她刚搬到大城市时是个涉世未深的人，很快就学到了一些残酷的教训。|Synonyms: innocent, naive, ingenue, greenhorn, tenderfoot|Antonyms: sophisticated, worldly, experienced, shrewd|Noun

babe-in-the-woods:/beɪb ɪn ðə wʊdz/| adj.(比喻)|幼稚的；容易上当的|Figuratively used to describe someone naive and easily deceived. |He's a babe in the woods when it comes to dealing with car salesmen. 在与汽车销售员打交道时，他就像个容易上当受骗的孩子。|Synonyms: naive, gullible, unsuspecting|Antonyms: savvy, streetwise, astute|Adjective
