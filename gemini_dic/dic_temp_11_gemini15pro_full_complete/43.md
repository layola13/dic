Example 2:  Geologists studied the composition of the aa to understand the volcanic eruption. 地质学家研究了熔岩的成分以了解火山爆发。 | 近义词:lava, clinker | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a geological term) | 词性:名词


词源:
From Hawaiian ʻaʻā, imitative of the sound of walking on the rough lava. 源于夏威夷语 ʻaʻā，模仿走在粗糙熔岩上的声音。
