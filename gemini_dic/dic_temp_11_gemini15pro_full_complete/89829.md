hornfish:/ˈhɔːrnfɪʃ/| n. | 角鱼; 长吻鱼;|A marine fish with a long, slender body and a pointed snout, typically found in tropical and subtropical waters. |例句：The hornfish darted through the coral reef. 角鱼快速穿过珊瑚礁。

例句：Hornfish are known for their speed and agility. 角鱼以其速度和敏捷性而闻名。|近义词：garfish, needlefish|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑体型差异大的鱼类，例如sunfish翻车鱼)|词性:noun
