ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *forrier (recorded as forrer "to forage, to rummage"), from forre "fodder, forage."  Ultimately from Frankish *fōdar, from Proto-Germanic *fōdrą (“food, fodder”). Cognate with Old English fōdor (“food, fodder”).
