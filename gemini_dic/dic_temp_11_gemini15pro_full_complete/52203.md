diastem:/ˈdaɪəstɛm/ | n. |间隙，空隙; (尤指牙齿间的)齿隙; |A diastem is a space or gap between two teeth, most often the two upper front teeth. |She has a noticeable diastem between her front teeth. 她前牙之间有一个明显的牙缝。| synonym: gap, space, aperture, interval | antonym: closure, connection | noun

diastem:/daɪˈæstəm/ | n. |(地质学) 缺失地层; |In geology, a diastem refers to a break or interruption in the sedimentary geological record. This break represents a period of non-deposition or erosion. |The diastem in the rock strata indicates a period of significant erosion. 岩层中的缺失地层表明存在一段重要的侵蚀期。| synonym: hiatus, break, unconformity | antonym: continuity, deposition | noun
