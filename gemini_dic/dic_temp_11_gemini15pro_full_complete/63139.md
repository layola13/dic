ensnarl:/ɪnˈsnɑːrl/ /ɛnˈsnɑrl/
verb
使纠缠；使陷入困境；使落入圈套
to cause to become entangled in or as in a snare.
The fishing net ensnarled a large number of fish.  渔网缠住了大量的鱼。
The fox was ensnarled in the hunter's trap. 狐狸被猎人的陷阱困住了。
synonym: entangle, trap, snare
antonym: disentangle, free, release
词性: 动词

ensnarl:/ɪnˈsnɑːrl/ /ɛnˈsnɑrl/
noun
纠缠；困境；圈套
a tangled or confused state or condition. (Rarely used as a noun)
The ensnarl of branches made it difficult to pass.  树枝的缠绕使得难以通行。(较少用)
synonym: tangle, entanglement
antonym: order, clarity
词性: 名词
