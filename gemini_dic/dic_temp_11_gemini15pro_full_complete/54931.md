dispositions:/ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃənz/
n.
倾向，性格，部署
A person's inherent qualities of mind and character.  The arrangement or positioning of something.
例句：
He has a cheerful disposition. 他性格开朗。

His disposition of troops was criticized.  他的军队部署受到了批评。

近义词：
tendencies, inclinations, temperaments, arrangements, deployments
反义词：
aversions, disinclinations
