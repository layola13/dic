gregal:/ˈɡriːɡəl/| adj.|东北风的；格雷戈风的；|Relating to or denoting a strong northeasterly wind in the Mediterranean.|The gregal wind battered the small fishing boats. 格雷戈风猛烈地冲击着小型渔船。|近义词:northeasterly, northeastern|反义词:southwesterly, southwestern|词性:形容词

词源: From Italian *grecale* "Greek (wind)," from Medieval Latin *Graecalis* "Greek," from Latin *Graecus*.  So called because the wind blows from the direction of Greece.
