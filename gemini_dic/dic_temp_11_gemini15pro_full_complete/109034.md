legacies: /ˈlɛɡəsiːz/
n.
遗产，遗赠物；后遗症；影响
Something (such as property or money) that is received from someone who has died; something that happened in the past or that comes from someone in the past
His father left him a legacy of $10,000. 他的父亲给他留下了1万美元的遗产。

The war left a legacy of hatred. 战争留下仇恨的后遗症。

近义词: inheritance, bequest, heritage, aftereffects, impact
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 debt 债务，如果指物质遗产)
词性: 名词 (plural)
