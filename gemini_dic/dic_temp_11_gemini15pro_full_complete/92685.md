iceberg:/ˈaɪsbɜːrɡ/ (US), /ˈaɪsbɜːɡ/ (UK)| n.|冰山|A large floating mass of ice detached from a glacier or ice sheet and carried out to sea.|The Titanic sank after hitting an iceberg. 泰坦尼克号撞到冰山后沉没了。|近义词：glacier, ice floe|反义词: (no direct opposite, possibly warm water)|noun
