micro-environment:/ˌmaɪkroʊ ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt/
n.
微环境
The immediate surroundings of something, especially a small, specific place, affecting it directly.
The company pays close attention to the micro-environment in which it operates.  这家公司密切关注其运营的微环境。
setting, locale, surroundings, vicinity
