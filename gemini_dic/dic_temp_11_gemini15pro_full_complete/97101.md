infestivity:/ɪnˌfestɪˈvɪti/
noun
感染力；侵染力
The ability of a pathogen or parasite to establish itself in a host and cause an infestation.
The study examined the **infestivity** of the new strain of the virus.  这项研究检验了这种新病毒株的感染力。
The warm weather increased the **infestivity** of the parasites.  温暖的天气增加了寄生虫的感染力。
近义词：contagiousness, transmissibility, virulence
反义词：resistance, immunity
