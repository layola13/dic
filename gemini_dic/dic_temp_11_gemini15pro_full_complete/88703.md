hobblers:/ˈhɒblərz/ | n. | 跛行者；蹒跚而行者；步履艰难的人；爱尔兰轻骑兵 | People who hobble; those who walk with difficulty.  Historically, also a type of light Irish cavalryman. |  The hobblers struggled through the deep snow. 跛行者们在深雪中艰难前行。|  The hobblers were known for their swift raids.  轻骑兵以其迅速的突袭而闻名。 | synonyms: cripples, invalids, limpers (for those with difficulty walking); light cavalry, horsemen (historical usage) | antonyms:  sprinters, runners (for those with difficulty walking); infantry (historical usage)| noun
