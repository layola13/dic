desertlessly:/dɪˈzɜːtləsli/
adverb
无故地，没有正当理由地
Without deserving it; unjustifiably.
He was desertlessly punished for a crime he did not commit. 他因他没有犯下的罪行而受到不公正的惩罚。
近义词:unjustly, unfairly, wrongly
反义词:deservedly, justly, rightly
词性:副词


词源:
From "desert" (meaning deserving of reward or punishment) + "-less" (meaning without) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The word "desert" in this context comes from Old French "deserte," ultimately from Latin "desertum," meaning "something deserved."
