duncedom: /ˈdʌnsɪdəm/
n.
愚蠢；愚昧（的状态或领域）
A realm or condition of stupidity or ignorance.
He lamented the current state of political discourse, describing it as a descent into utter duncedom. 他对当前的政治讨论状况感到悲哀，将其描述为彻底沦为愚蠢的境地。
Near Synonyms: stupidity, ignorance, foolishness, idiocy, imbecility
Antonyms: intelligence, wisdom, brilliance, enlightenment
词性: 名词
