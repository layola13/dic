nonconsumptions:/ˌnɒnkənˈsʌmpʃənz/
n.|非消费; 未消费;|The study of nonconsumptions is a growing field in economics. 对非消费的研究是经济学中一个不断发展的领域。| Nonconsumptions, such as choosing to repair an item rather than replace it, can be beneficial for the environment. 非消费，例如选择修理一件物品而不是更换它，可能对环境有益。|近义词:non-purchase, non-spending, abstention|反义词:consumption, expenditure, spending|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "consumption" (from Latin "consumere," meaning "to consume").
