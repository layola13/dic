franchisers:/ˈfræntʃaɪzərz/
n.
特许经营人，授予特许经营权者
A franchiser is a company that grants a license to an individual or group to operate a business using the franchiser's name and business model.
McDonald's is a well-known franchiser with restaurants worldwide.  麦当劳是一家知名的特许经营商，在世界各地都有餐厅。
Many franchisers offer training and support to their franchisees. 许多特许经营商向其特许经营者提供培训和支持。
近义词：licensor, grantor
反义词：franchisee
词性：名词
