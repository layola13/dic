assimilators:/əˈsɪməˌleɪtərz/
|n.|同化者；吸收者
|People who assimilate or absorb information, cultures, or experiences.|
|These assimilators quickly adapted to the new environment.|这些同化者很快适应了新环境。
|近义词:integrators, absorbers, adapters|
|反义词:segregators, excluders|
|词性: noun (plural)|


assimilate:/əˈsɪməˌleɪt/
|v.|同化；吸收
|To absorb and integrate (people, ideas, or culture) into a wider society or culture.|
|The immigrants assimilated into the local community.|移民融入了当地社区。
|近义词:integrate, absorb, incorporate, adapt|
|反义词:segregate, exclude, reject|
|词性: verb|


assimilation:/əˌsɪməˈleɪʃən/
|n.|同化；吸收
|The process of absorbing and integrating (people, ideas, or culture) into a wider society or culture.|
|Cultural assimilation can be a complex process.|文化同化可能是一个复杂的过程。
|近义词:integration, absorption, incorporation, adaptation|
|反义词:segregation, exclusion, rejection|
