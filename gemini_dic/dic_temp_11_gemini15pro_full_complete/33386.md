chunter:/ˈtʃʌntər/ (UK) /ˈtʃʌn(t)ər/ (US)| n.| 抱怨，嘀咕|A low, persistent complaint or grumble.|Her constant chunter about the weather was getting on my nerves. 她不停地抱怨天气让我很烦。|例句：I could hear a chunter coming from the back of the room. 我能听到从房间后面传来的抱怨声。

例句：His chunter about the food was ignored.  他对食物的抱怨被忽视了。


近义词：grumbling, complaint, muttering|反义词:praise, commendation|词性:noun


词源:  Probably of imitative origin, suggesting a low, continuous murmuring sound.
