gigged:/ɡɪɡd/
verb
演出过；做过零工
performed a gig; worked a temporary job
例句：
The band gigged at a local club last night.
这支乐队昨晚在一个当地俱乐部演出。

He gigged as a delivery driver to earn extra money.
他做送货司机赚外快。

近义词:performed, played, worked
反义词:rested, relaxed
词性:v. (past tense and past participle of "gig")
