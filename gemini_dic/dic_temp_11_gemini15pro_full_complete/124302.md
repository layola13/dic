motherer:/ˈmʌðərər/| n.|过度溺爱孩子的母亲；爱管闲事的人|A person, typically a mother, who is perceived as excessively involved in the life of their child or other person, often to the point of being overprotective or controlling.  It can also refer to someone who interferes in others' affairs. |例句：She's such a motherer; she won't let her son do anything by himself. 她真是个溺爱孩子的母亲；她不让她的儿子自己做任何事。

例句：He's a bit of a motherer, always telling people what to do. 他有点爱管闲事，总是告诉别人该怎么做。|近义词：helicopter parent, overprotective parent, busybody, meddler |反义词: neglectful parent |词性: noun
