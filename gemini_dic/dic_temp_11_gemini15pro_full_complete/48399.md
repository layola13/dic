dedicated:/ˈdedɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.|专注的，献身的，专用的|Devoted to a particular purpose or cause; exclusively allocated to a specific use or person.|例句：She is a dedicated teacher who always puts her students first. 她是一位敬业的老师，总是把学生放在第一位。

例句：This lane is dedicated to buses only. 这条车道是公交车专用道。|近义词：committed, devoted, loyal, earmarked|反义词：uncommitted, indifferent, apathetic|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dedicatus, past participle of dedicare (“to dedicate, consecrate”), from de- (“down, away from”) + dicare (“to proclaim, dedicate”).
