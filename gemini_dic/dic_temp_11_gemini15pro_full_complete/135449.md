nonpertinences:/ˌnɒnpɜːrtnənsɪz/
| n. | 无关紧要的事物；不相干的事物；不恰当的言行 | Lack of relevance or appropriateness.  The state or quality of being nonpertinent.  Usually used in the plural to refer to multiple instances of irrelevant or inappropriate remarks or actions.  |  His speech was full of nonpertinences, and the audience quickly lost interest. 他的演讲充满了无关紧要的内容，观众很快就失去了兴趣。 |  irrelevancies, impertinences, digressions |  relevances, pertinences | noun
