legalists:/ˈliːɡəlɪsts/
n.
法家;法理學家;律法主義者
A person who adheres strictly to the law or legal principles.
例句：The legalists believed that strict adherence to the law was the key to a stable society. 法家认为，严格遵守法律是社会稳定的关键。

例句：Some legalists argued against any form of civil disobedience. 一些法学家反对任何形式的公民不服从。

近义词：lawyer, jurist, attorney, advocate
反义词：anarchist, rebel, revolutionary
词性：noun (plural)
