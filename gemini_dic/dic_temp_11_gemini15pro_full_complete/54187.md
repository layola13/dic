disestablisher:/ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃər/
n.
废除者，取消建制者
One who disestablishes something, especially an institution like a church.
The disestablishers of the Church of England in Wales argued for separation of church and state.  威尔士的英国国教会的废除者主张政教分离。

近义词:abolitionist, reformer
反义词:supporter, upholder
词性:noun
