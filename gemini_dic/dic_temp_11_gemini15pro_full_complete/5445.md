almsfolk: /ˈɑːmzfoʊk/ | n. | 受施者，接受救济的人 | People who receive alms; recipients of charity. | The almsfolk gathered outside the church, hoping for a warm meal. 受施者聚集在教堂外，希望能得到一顿热饭。|  The almsfolk were grateful for the donations. 受施者对捐赠表示感激。| synonyms: beggars, paupers, the needy, the poor, indigent | antonyms: philanthropists, donors, benefactors | noun

Etymology:
From Old English ælmesfolc, from ælmesse (alms) + folc (folk).
