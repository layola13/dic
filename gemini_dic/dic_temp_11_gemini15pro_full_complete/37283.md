commandeer:/ˌkɒmənˈdɪə(r)/ | v. | 征用，强占 | To seize (something, especially a vehicle or building) for military or official use. |  The army commandeered all the trucks to transport supplies. 军队征用了所有的卡车来运送物资。

The government commandeered the building for use as a temporary hospital. 政府征用了这栋楼作为临时医院。 | synonyms: seize, confiscate, appropriate, expropriate, requisition | antonyms: return, release, surrender | verb
