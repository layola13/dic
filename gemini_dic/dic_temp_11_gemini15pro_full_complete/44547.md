crotalo:/ˈkrɒtəloʊ/ | n. |响板，响尾蛇|A percussion instrument consisting of two small, concave pieces of wood or bone held together by a cord and clicked together to produce a sharp sound. Also, a rattlesnake. |例句：The dancer clicked the crotalo rhythmically. 舞蹈者有节奏地敲击响板。

例句：The crotalo of the rattlesnake warned us of its presence. 响尾蛇的响尾警告我们它的存在。|近义词：castanets, rattlesnake |反义词: None |词性: Noun


词源: From Spanish *crótalo*, from Latin *crotalum*, from Ancient Greek *κρόταλον* (krótalon, “rattle, castanet”). Ultimately from the verb *κροτέω* (krotéō, “to rattle, clap”).
