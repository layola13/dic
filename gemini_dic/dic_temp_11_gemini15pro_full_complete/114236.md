mailbags:/ˈmeɪlbæɡz/
n.
邮袋（mailbag的复数）
Large bags, typically made of canvas or other strong material, used for transporting mail.
The postman struggled to carry the heavy mailbags. 邮递员吃力地提着沉重的邮袋。

近义词：mailsacks, pouches
反义词：（无直接反义词）
词性：名词
