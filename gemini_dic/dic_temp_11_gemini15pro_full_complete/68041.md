factoryless:/ˈfæktrilɪs/ | adj. | 无工厂的；没有工厂的 |  Lacking a factory; operating or existing without owning a manufacturing facility.  A company that designs and sells products but outsources their manufacturing. | Apple is a factoryless company, focusing on design and marketing while relying on other companies to manufacture its products. 苹果是一家无厂公司，专注于设计和营销，同时依靠其他公司生产其产品。|Example: Many tech companies are factoryless, outsourcing their production to specialized manufacturers. 许多科技公司都没有工厂，将生产外包给专门的制造商。 | Synonyms: non-manufacturing, asset-light | Antonyms: manufacturing, factory-based | word type: adjective
