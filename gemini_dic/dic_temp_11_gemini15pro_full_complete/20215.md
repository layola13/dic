birdless:/ˈbɜːrdləs/| adj.|无鸟的；寂静的|Without birds; lacking birdsong.|The silent, birdless woods seemed ominous.  寂静无鸟的树林显得有些不祥。|The chemical spill created a birdless zone around the lake. 化学物质泄漏在湖泊周围形成了一个无鸟区。|近义词：deserted, quiet, still|反义词:bird-filled, lively|词性:形容词
Etymology: bird + -less (suffix meaning "without") 来自“bird”（鸟）和“-less”（后缀，表示“没有”）。
