holostome:/ˈhɒləˌstəʊm/ (US) /ˈhoʊləˌstoʊm/| n. |全寄生吸虫|A parasitic flatworm of the class Trematoda, typically having a complex life cycle involving multiple hosts, including mollusks, fish, and birds.  These flukes are characterized by having a posterior adhesive organ for attachment to their host.|The holostome completed its life cycle by infecting a bird.|近义词:trematode, fluke|反义词: None (lacks a direct antonym)|noun
