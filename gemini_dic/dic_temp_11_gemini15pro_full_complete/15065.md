babyishnesses:/ˈbeɪbiɪʃnɪsɪz/
n.
幼稚的行为；孩子气的举动
The state or quality of being babyish; childish behavior or characteristics.
She displayed a surprising number of babyishnesses for a woman her age.  她年纪一把了，还表现出令人惊讶的幼稚行为。
His babyishnesses were starting to annoy his colleagues. 他的幼稚行为开始惹恼他的同事。

近义词: childishness, immaturity, puerility, infantility
反义词: maturity, sophistication, adulthood
