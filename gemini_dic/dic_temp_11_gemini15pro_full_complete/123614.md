montezuma:/ˌmɒntəˈzuːmə/ | n. | 蒙特祖玛（阿兹特克皇帝的名字） |  The name refers to several Aztec emperors, most famously Montezuma II, who ruled when the Spanish conquistadors arrived. |  Montezuma II ruled the Aztec empire at its height. 蒙特祖玛二世统治着鼎盛时期的阿兹特克帝国。| synonym: Aztec emperor, ruler | antonym: subject, commoner | Noun


montezuma:/ˌmɒntəˈzuːmə/ | n. | 蒙特祖玛的复仇（一种腹泻疾病，通常由旅行者在访问发展中国家时感染引起） |  Montezuma's Revenge is a colloquial term for traveler's diarrhea. | I was worried about getting Montezuma's Revenge on my trip to Mexico. 我担心去墨西哥旅行时会得蒙特祖玛的复仇。 | synonym: traveler's diarrhea, the runs | antonym: constipation, regular bowel movements | Noun
