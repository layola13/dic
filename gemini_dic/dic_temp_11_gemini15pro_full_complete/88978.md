holiness:/ˈhoʊlinəs/| n.|神圣, 圣洁, 虔诚|The state of being holy, dedicated to God or a religious purpose; morally and spiritually pure.|例句：The holiness of the place was palpable. 这个地方的神圣感是显而易见的。|例句：They spoke of the holiness of marriage. 他们谈到了婚姻的神圣性。|近义词：sacredness, piety, sanctity, divinity|反义词：profaneness, wickedness, impiety|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hālignes (unattested), from hālig "holy" + -nes, a suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives.  The Old English hālig is from Proto-Germanic *hailagaz, from Proto-Indo-European *kailo- "whole, uninjured, of good omen."  Cognates include Old Norse heilagr, Old Frisian hēlig, Old Saxon hēlag, Old High German heilag, Gothic hailags.
