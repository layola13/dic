inspectoral:/ɪnˌspɛkˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
检查的；视察的
of or relating to inspection or an inspector.
The inspectoral visit to the factory revealed several safety violations. 对工厂的检查访问揭示了几项安全违规行为。
The board conducted an inspectoral review of the school's finances. 董事会对学校的财务进行了检查审查。
Synonyms: supervisory, examining, scrutinizing
Antonyms:  unobserved, unchecked, unexamined
词性: 形容词
