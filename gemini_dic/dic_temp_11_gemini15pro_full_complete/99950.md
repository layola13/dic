internuncioship:/ˌɪntərˈnʌnsiəʊʃɪp/
n.|教廷大使的职位；教廷大使的任期|The position or term of office of an internuncio.  An internuncio is a papal diplomat of lower rank than a nuncio, typically sent to smaller countries.|He completed his internuncioship in a small South American country.|近义词: diplomatic post, ambassadorship|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "civilian life")|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *internuncius*, from *inter-* (between) + *nuntius* (messenger, envoy).  The suffix *-ship* denotes a state, condition, or office.
