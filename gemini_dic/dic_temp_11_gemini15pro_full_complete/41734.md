corrosible:/kəˈroʊzəbl/ /kəˈrəʊzəbl/| adj.|腐蚀的，易腐蚀的|Susceptible to corrosion; easily corroded.  Able to be destroyed gradually by chemical action.|
例句：Iron is a corrosible metal. 铁是一种易腐蚀的金属。
例句：The acid rain made the metal roof corrosible. 酸雨使金属屋顶易腐蚀。
近义词：erodible, rustable, oxidizable|反义词：corrosion-resistant, inert, non-corrosive|词性:adjective

corrosive:/kəˈroʊsɪv/ /kəˈrəʊsɪv/| adj.|腐蚀性的，侵蚀性的|Having the power to cause corrosion; tending to corrode.  Harmful or destructive.|
例句：Sulfuric acid is a highly corrosive substance. 硫酸是一种高度腐蚀性的物质。
例句：The corrosive effects of salt water can damage metal structures.  盐水的腐蚀作用会损害金属结构。
近义词：caustic, erosive, destructive|反义词：protective, preservative|词性:adjective
