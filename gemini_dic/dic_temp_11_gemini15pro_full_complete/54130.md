disencumbrance:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkʌmbrəns/
n.|解除负担，摆脱累赘|The disencumbrance of the property allowed them to sell it quickly.  这处房产的解除负担使他们能够迅速将其出售。|
|The act of disencumbrance is crucial for a smooth transaction.  解除负担的行为对于顺利交易至关重要。|
近义词: liberation, release, freeing
反义词: encumbrance, burden, obstacle
词性: noun

disencumber:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkʌmbər/
vt.|使摆脱负担，解除…的累赘|He disencumbered himself of his heavy coat. 他脱掉了沉重的外套。|
|They disencumbered the ship of its cargo. 他们卸下了船上的货物。|
近义词: unburden, relieve, free
反义词: encumber, burden, load
词性: verb
