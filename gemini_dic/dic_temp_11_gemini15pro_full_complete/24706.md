brontology:/brɒnˈtɒlədʒi/ /brɑːnˈtɑːlədʒi/| n. |雷鸣学；雷暴学|The study of thunder.  It encompasses the physics, chemistry, and meteorology of thunderstorms, including the causes, development, structure, detection, forecasting, effects, and modification of thunderstorms.|Brontology is a specialized area of meteorology. 雷鸣学是气象学的一个专门领域。| Synonym: ceraunology| Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists) | noun
