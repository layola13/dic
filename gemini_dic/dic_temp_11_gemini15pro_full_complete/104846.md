keelhaul:/ˈkiːlhɔːl/| v.| 龙骨吊；严厉惩罚|To drag a person under the keel of a ship as punishment or torture.  将一个人作为惩罚或酷刑从船的龙骨下拖过。|The captain threatened to keelhaul any man caught stealing. 船长威胁要对任何被抓到偷窃的人进行龙骨吊。|The mutineers were keelhauled for their treachery. 叛变者因其背叛行为而被处以龙骨吊。| 近义词:flog, scourge, punish severely| 反义词:reward, praise|动词
