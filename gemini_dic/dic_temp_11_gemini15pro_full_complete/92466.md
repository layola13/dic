hyposternum:/ˌhaɪpəˈstɜːrnəm/ /ˌhaɪpoʊˈstɜːrnəm/| n. |后胸骨，下腹板|The hyposternum is a plate on the ventral side of the thorax in insects. 后胸骨是昆虫胸部腹侧的一块骨板。|例句：The entomologist pointed to the hyposternum on the insect specimen. 昆虫学家指着昆虫标本上的后胸骨。

|例句：The hyposternum plays a role in the insect's movement and support. 后胸骨在昆虫的运动和支撑中起作用。


|近义词：metasternum (sometimes used interchangeably), ventral plate|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
