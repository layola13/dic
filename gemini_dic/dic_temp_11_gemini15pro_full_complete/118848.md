metaphrasis:/ˌmɛtəˈfreɪsɪs/ | n. | 转述，逐字翻译 | A metaphrasis is a literal or word-for-word translation, as opposed to a paraphrase. | The translator's metaphrasis rendered the poem awkward and stilted. 译者的逐字翻译使得这首诗显得笨拙且生硬。 | synonyms: literal translation, word-for-word translation | antonyms: paraphrase, adaptation | noun
