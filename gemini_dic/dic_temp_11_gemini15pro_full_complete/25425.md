bulkheaded:/ˈbʌlkˌhɛdɪd/
adjective
用舱壁隔开的，分隔的
divided into compartments by bulkheads.
The ship's hold was bulkheaded for safety.  这艘船的货舱为了安全起见，用舱壁隔开了。
compartmentalized, partitioned
open-plan
adjective

bulkheaded:/ˈbʌlkˌhɛdɪd/
adjective
头脑简单的，顽固的
inflexible and resistant to new ideas.
He's so bulkheaded in his thinking, he refuses to consider any other options. 他思想僵化，拒绝考虑其他选择。
stubborn, inflexible, rigid
open-minded, flexible
adjective
