mesotrochal:/ˈmɛzəˌtroʊkəl/ | adj. | 中环轮的 | Having a band of cilia around the middle of the body (of a trochophore larva). | The mesotrochal larva swims freely in the water column.  中环轮幼虫在水柱中自由游动。 | The pelagic mesotrochal larva develops into a benthic adult.  远洋中环轮幼虫发育成底栖成虫。 |  synonym:  mid-ringed | antonym:  atelotrochal, peritrochal | adjective
