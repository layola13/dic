memorials:/məˈmɔːriəlz/ | n. |纪念物，纪念碑；纪念仪式| something, such as a monument or ceremony, that commemorates a person or event. |The war memorials are a poignant reminder of the sacrifices made. 战争纪念碑令人痛心地想起了所作出的牺牲。|
memorials:/məˈmɔːriəlz/ | n. |纪念馆|A building or site dedicated to the memory of a person or event. |They visited the Holocaust Memorial Museum in Washington. 他们参观了华盛顿的大屠杀纪念馆。|


近义词: monument, commemoration, remembrance, tribute
反义词: N/A
词性: noun (plural)

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin memoriale ‘something to be remembered’, neuter (used as a noun) of memorialis, from memoria ‘memory’.
