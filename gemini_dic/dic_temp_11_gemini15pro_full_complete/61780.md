emperil:/ɪmˈpɛrəl/
verb
危及，使陷于危险
to put something or someone at risk or in danger.
The toxic fumes emperiled the lives of the trapped miners.  有毒气体危及被困矿工的生命。
The rising floodwaters emperiled the entire village.  上涨的洪水危及了整个村庄。
synonyms: endanger, jeopardize, threaten, compromise
antonyms: protect, safeguard, secure
