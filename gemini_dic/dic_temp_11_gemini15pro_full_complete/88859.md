hoiden:/ˈhɔɪdn/| n.|野丫头；假小子|A boisterous, tomboyish girl; a romp.|例句：She was a real hoiden as a child, always climbing trees and getting into scrapes. 她小时候是个十足的野丫头，总是爬树惹麻烦。

例句：The hoiden refused to wear dresses, preferring overalls and mud-caked boots. 这个假小子拒绝穿裙子，更喜欢工装裤和沾满泥巴的靴子。|近义词：tomboy, romp, gamine|反义词：lady, debutante|词性:名词

词源: From Middle Dutch *hoyden, *heiden "rustic, heathen," related to Old Norse heiðinn "heathen." Originally referred to a rude, uneducated person, later specifically applied to girls.
