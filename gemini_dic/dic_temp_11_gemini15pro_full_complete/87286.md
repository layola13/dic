hermeneutist:/ˌhɜːrməˈnjuːtɪst/| n.|诠释学家，解释者|A specialist in interpretation, especially of the Bible or literary texts.|The hermeneutist offered a compelling interpretation of the ancient poem.  这位诠释学家对这首古诗提出了令人信服的解释。|近义词：interpreter, exegete, analyst, commentator|反义词:misinterpreter, distorter|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἑρμηνευτής (hermēneutēs, “interpreter”)  from ἑρμηνεύω (hermēneuō, “to interpret”), ultimately from Hermes, the messenger god in Greek mythology, believed to be the interpreter of the gods' will to humans.
