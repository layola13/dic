nonpliancies:/nɒnˈplaɪənsiz/
|n.|不符合项；违规行为|Instances of not complying with rules, regulations, or standards.|The inspection report listed several nonpliancies that needed to be addressed. 该检查报告列出了几个需要解决的不符合项。|The company was penalized for multiple nonpliancies.  这家公司因多项违规行为而受到处罚。|近义词：violations, infractions, breaches, defaults|反义词：compliances, conformities|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + compliance.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "compliance" refers to the act of conforming to rules or requests.
