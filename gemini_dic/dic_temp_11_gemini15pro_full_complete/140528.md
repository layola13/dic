oleiferous:/ˌoʊləˈɪfərəs/| adj.|产油的，含油的|Producing or containing oil.  Specifically, vegetable or animal oil, as opposed to mineral oil.|例句：Oleiferous seeds, such as sunflower and sesame, are important crops.  产油种子，如葵花籽和芝麻，是重要的农作物。

例句：The oleiferous glands of the plant secreted a sticky resin.  这种植物的含油腺分泌出一种粘稠的树脂。|近义词：oily, unctuous|反义词: dry, arid|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *oleifer* "oil-bearing," from *oleum* "oil" + *-fer* "bearing."  The suffix *-fer* is related to the verb *ferre* "to carry, bear."
