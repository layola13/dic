dalmanites: /ˈdælməˌnaɪtiːz/
noun
达尔曼虫（三叶虫的一种）
An extinct genus of trilobite, marine arthropods that lived from the Lower to Upper Silurian periods.  Characterized by a large tail section and often elaborate head ornamentation.
Dalmanites are commonly found as fossils in Silurian rocks.  达尔曼虫的化石常见于志留纪的岩石中。
Dalmanites fossils are prized by collectors.  达尔曼虫化石深受收藏家的喜爱。
Synonyms: trilobite (more general)
