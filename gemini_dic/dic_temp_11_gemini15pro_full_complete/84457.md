halomancy:/ˈhæləˌmænsi/ | n. | 盐占卜 | Divination by means of salt, typically involving observing the patterns formed by salt crystals or the crackling sounds made when salt is thrown into a fire. |  Example: The ancient Romans practiced halomancy to predict the future. 古代罗马人通过盐占卜来预测未来。

Example:  Halomancy, though less common today, was once a respected form of divination. 虽然现在不太常见，但盐占卜曾经是一种受人尊敬的占卜形式。| 近义词: divination, fortune-telling | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "scientific prediction") | 词性: noun
