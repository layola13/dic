boring:/ˈbɔːrɪŋ/ /ˈbɔːrɪŋ/| adj.|无聊的，令人厌烦的|Causing weariness and restlessness through lack of interest; tedious.|The lecture was so boring that I almost fell asleep.  这讲座太无聊了，我差点睡着了。|Synonyms: tedious, dull, monotonous, uninteresting, tiresome|Antonyms: interesting, exciting, stimulating, engaging, fascinating|adjective
