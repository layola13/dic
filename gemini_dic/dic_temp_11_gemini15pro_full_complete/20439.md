bitter:/ˈbɪtər/| adj.|苦的，痛苦的，辛酸的，严寒的，怀恨的|Having a sharp, pungent taste or smell, not sweet. Also, feeling or expressing resentment or disappointment.|The coffee was too bitter for me. 这咖啡对我来说太苦了.|Synonyms: acrid, harsh, resentful, hostile|Antonyms: sweet, pleasant, kind|adjective

bitter:/ˈbɪtər/| n.|苦味，苦酒|Something bitter, especially a beer.|He took a long swallow of the bitter. 他喝了一大口苦酒。|Synonyms:  bitterness|Antonyms: sweetness|noun
