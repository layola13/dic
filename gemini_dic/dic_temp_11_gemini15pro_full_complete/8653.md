anticonservation:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃən/
anticonservation|n.|反保护主义，反存续主义|Opposition to conservation, especially of the natural environment.|The developer's anticonservation stance led to the destruction of a vital wetland habitat.  开发商的反保护主义立场导致了重要湿地栖息地的破坏。|Example 2:  Her anticonservation views were met with strong opposition from environmental groups. 她的反保护主义观点遭到了环保组织的强烈反对。|近义词：exploitation, destruction|反义词：conservation, preservation|词性:名词
