fictionalise:/ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈfɪkʃənəˌlaɪz/ (US)| v. |使小说化；虚构化|To create a fictional account of something based on real events, or to present something in a fictional form. |例句：The author fictionalized his own experiences in the war.  作者将自己在战争中的经历小说化了。
