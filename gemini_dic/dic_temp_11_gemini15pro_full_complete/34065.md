civil:/ˈsɪvəl/ (US); /ˈsɪvl/ (UK)| adj.|公民的，民用的；文明的，有礼貌的|Relating to ordinary citizens and their concerns, as distinct from military or ecclesiastical matters; courteous and polite.|例句：Civil unrest is a major concern for the government. 政府主要担心民众的不安。|近义词：polite, courteous, civic, secular|反义词：rude, impolite, military, religious|词性:形容词

civil:/ˈsɪvəl/ (US); /ˈsɪvl/ (UK)| n.|民法，民事诉讼|A lawsuit between private parties or organizations.|例句：He filed a civil suit against his neighbor. 他对邻居提起了民事诉讼。|近义词：lawsuit, litigation|反义词：criminal case|词性:名词
