ascendable:/əˈsendəbl/
adjective
可攀登的，可上升的
Capable of being ascended; climbable.
The mountain is ascendable, but it will be a challenging climb. 这座山可以攀登，但这将是一次充满挑战的攀登。
The staircase is steep but ascendable.  这楼梯很陡，但可以爬上去。
近义词：climbable, scalable, surmountable
反义词：unclimbable, insurmountable, inaccessible
词性：adjective
词源：From ascend + -able.  Ascend comes from the Latin *ascendere*, from *ad- "to" + *scandere* "to climb."
