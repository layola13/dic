hydrogen-like:/ˈhaɪdrədʒənˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 类氢的 | Resembling hydrogen in chemical or physical properties, especially having only one electron surrounding the nucleus. |  The spectrum of singly ionized helium is hydrogen-like. 单电离氦的光谱是类氢的。|  The energy levels of hydrogen-like ions can be calculated. 类氢离子的能级可以计算出来。 | similar, analogous | dissimilar, different | adjective
