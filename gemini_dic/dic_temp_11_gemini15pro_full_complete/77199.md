gassily:/ˈɡæsɪli/
adverb
轻佻地，浮夸地
in a gassy manner; airily or boastfully.  Characterized by excessive talk or boasting, often without substance.
He spoke gassily about his achievements, but no one believed him. 他轻佻地谈论着自己的成就，但没有人相信他。
近义词: airily, frivolously, flippantly, superficially
反义词: seriously, solemnly, gravely
词性: adverb

gassy:/ˈɡæsi/
adjective
1.充满气体的，气态的
full of gas; gaseous. Containing or resembling gas.
The room was gassy after the experiment. 实验后房间里充满了气体。
2.引起胀气的，导致肠胃胀气的
causing flatulence.  Producing or tending to produce gas in the stomach or intestines.
Beans are a gassy food. 豆子是一种容易胀气的食物。
3.夸夸其谈的，浮夸的
given to or characterized by excessive talk or boasting, often without substance.  Talking a lot, often about unimportant things.
He gave a gassy speech full of empty promises. 他做了一个夸夸其谈的演讲，充满了空洞的承诺。
近义词: flatulent, windy, airy, inflated, boastful
反义词: solid, substantial, modest
词性: adjective

词源: From gas + -y.  The adjective form predates the adverb.
