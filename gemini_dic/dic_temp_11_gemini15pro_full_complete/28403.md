carbonate:/ˈkɑːrbənət/ /ˈkɑːr.bə.neɪt/| n. adj.| 碳酸盐；碳酸化的 |A salt of carbonic acid.  A carbonate rock.|例句：Calcium carbonate is a common component of seashells. 碳酸钙是贝壳的常见成分。

例句：The water is carbonate hard.  这水是碳酸盐硬水。|近义词：bicarbonate |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以根据语境选择酸性物质)|noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *carbonate*, from carbonic + -ate.  Ultimately from Latin carbo (“charcoal, coal”).
