nonsolids:/nɒnˈsɒlɪdz/ /nɑːnˈsɑːlɪdz/| n.|非固体|Substances that are not solid, typically liquids or gases.  Matter that does not hold a fixed shape or volume.|Milk and juice are examples of nonsolids. 牛奶和果汁是非固体的例子。|Synonyms: fluids, liquids, gases|Antonyms: solids|noun
