error: /ˈerər/ (US), /ˈɛrə/ (UK) | n. | 错误，差错 | a mistake that is made in something, such as a calculation, document, or measurement |  There was an error in my calculations. 我的计算中有一个错误。

| 近义词: mistake, blunder, fault, flaw | 反义词: correctness, accuracy | 词性: noun


error: /ˈerər/ (US), /ˈɛrə/ (UK) | v. | 犯错，出错 | to make a mistake | I erred in thinking she would help me.  我错在她会帮助我。| 近义词: mistake, blunder, slip up | 反义词: succeed, be correct | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *errer* "to wander, stray, err," from Latin *errare* "to wander, stray, err, go astray," of unknown origin.
