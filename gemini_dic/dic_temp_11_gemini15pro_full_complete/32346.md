childish:/ˈtʃaɪldɪʃ/ (US), /ˈtʃaɪldɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|幼稚的，孩子气的|Resembling or appropriate to a child especially in immaturity; silly and immature.|例句：His childish behavior embarrassed everyone at the meeting. 他幼稚的行为让会议上的每个人都很尴尬。

例句：It was childish of him to sulk because he didn't get his way.  他因为没有达到目的而生气，真是幼稚。

近义词：immature, infantile, juvenile, puerile|反义词：mature, grown-up, adult|词性:形容词
