degeneration:/dɪˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən/
n.|退化；恶化；变质|A decline or deterioration in physical, mental, or moral qualities.|The degeneration of the city's infrastructure was evident in the crumbling roads and bridges. 城市基础设施的退化在摇摇欲坠的道路和桥梁中显而易见。|近义词：decline, deterioration, decay|反义词：improvement, development, progress|词性:名词
词源: From Latin degenerare  "to depart from one's kind, deteriorate," from de- "down from, away from" + genus "race, kind, family, birth, origin."
