go-out:/ɡoʊ aʊt/| phrasal verb |外出；熄灭；过时；约会| To leave one's home or workplace for social or recreational activities; to cease to burn; to become unfashionable; to have a romantic relationship with someone.|例句：They went out for dinner last night. 他们昨晚出去吃晚饭了。|近义词：date, socialize, leave, exit, extinguish|反义词:stay in, come in, ignite|词性:动词短语

go-out:/ɡoʊ aʊt/| n. |外出；约会| An instance of leaving one's home for social or recreational activities; a romantic date.|例句：We had a fun go-out last weekend.  我们上周末出去玩得很开心。|近义词：date, outing, excursion|反义词:|词性:名词
