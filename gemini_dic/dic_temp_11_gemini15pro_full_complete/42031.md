costliness:/ˈkɒstlɪnəs/| n.|昂贵，代价高|The costliness of the project caused it to be shelved.  这个项目的昂贵导致它被搁置。|Synonyms: expense, expensiveness, dearness|Antonyms: cheapness, affordability, inexpensiveness|noun
Etymology: From Middle English *costelinesse*, from Old English *costlignes*, equivalent to costly +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kostaz "choice, condition".|
Example: The costliness of the medicine prevented many from accessing it.  这种药价格昂贵，许多人无法获得。
Example:  The costliness of the diamond necklace made it a target for thieves.  这条钻石项链价格昂贵，成为盗贼的目标。
