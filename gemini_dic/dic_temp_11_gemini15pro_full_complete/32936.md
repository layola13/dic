chophouse: /ˈtʃɒphaʊs/ (UK) /ˈtʃɑːphaʊs/ (US)
n.
牛排餐厅；餐馆；饮食店
A restaurant that specializes in serving chops, especially steaks and other grilled meats.
We went to a chophouse for dinner and had a delicious steak. 我们去了一家牛排餐厅吃晚饭，享用了一块美味的牛排。
The chophouse was known for its prime rib. 这家牛排餐厅以其上等肋排而闻名。

近义词: steakhouse, restaurant, grill
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "vegetarian restaurant" or "vegan restaurant" as a contrasting concept)
词性: noun
