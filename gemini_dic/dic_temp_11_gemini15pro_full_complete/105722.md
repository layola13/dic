kinship:/ˈkɪnʃɪp/| n.|亲属关系；血缘关系；相似；密切联系|A sharing of characteristics or origins.|例句：He felt a kinship with the other survivors. 他对其他幸存者感到一种亲切感。

例句：There is a strong kinship between humans and apes. 人类和猿类之间有很强的亲缘关系。

近义词：relationship, affinity, connection, tie, bond|反义词: estrangement, alienation|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cynscipe "kinship, relationship by birth," from cyn "kin" (see kin) + -scipe (see -ship).
