forestick:/ˈfɔːrˌstɪk/| n.|火棍，点火棒，引火柴|A stick used for lighting a fire, especially a piece of wood kindled for that purpose.|例句：He used a forestick to start the campfire. 他用一根火棍点燃了篝火。

例句：The forestick caught fire quickly and the flames began to grow. 火棍很快着火了，火焰开始变大。|近义词：kindling, tinder, firestarter|反义词:extinguisher|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fyrstecg (attested as fyr-stecg "fire-stick"), from fyr ("fire") + stecg ("stick").  The word "stecg" is related to Old High German steccho, and ultimately to Proto-Germanic *stikką.
