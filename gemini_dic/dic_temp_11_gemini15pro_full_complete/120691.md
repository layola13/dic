例句：The misadventurous hiker got lost in the woods.  不幸的徒步旅行者在树林里迷路了。|近义词：unlucky, unfortunate, hapless, ill-fated, disastrous, calamitous|反义词:fortunate, lucky, successful, safe|词性:adjective
