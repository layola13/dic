loganberry:/ˈlɒɡənbɛri/ (UK) /ˈlɔːɡənbɛri/ (US)| n. |洛根莓|A hybrid berry, a cross between a blackberry and a raspberry, with a dark red colour and a tart flavour.|I made a loganberry pie for dessert. 我做了一个洛根莓派作为甜点。|近义词: boysenberry, tayberry|反义词: N/A|noun

loganberry:/ˈlɒɡənbɛri/ (UK) /ˈlɔːɡənbɛri/ (US)| adj. |洛根莓口味的；洛根莓色的|Made with or flavored with loganberries; having the dark red colour of a loganberry.|The loganberry jam was delicious. 洛根莓酱很美味。|近义词: raspberry, blackberry (in terms of flavor/color)|反义词: N/A|adjective
