allantois:/əˈlæntɔɪs/ | n. | 尿囊 |  A fetal membrane lying below the chorion in many vertebrates, formed as an outgrowth of the hindgut and functioning as a respiratory organ, in the excretion of wastes, or in providing a major blood supply to and from the placenta. | The allantois helps the embryo exchange gases and handle liquid waste. 尿囊帮助胚胎交换气体和处理液体废物。 | The allantois is vital for the development of the fetus.  尿囊对胎儿的发育至关重要。 |  chorion, placenta | None | noun
