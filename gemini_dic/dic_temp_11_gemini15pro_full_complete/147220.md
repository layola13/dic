overmature:/ˌoʊvərˈməˈtʃʊr/ /ˌəʊvəməˈtʃʊə(r)/ | adj. | 过熟的；过度成熟的 | Developed beyond the point of optimal maturity.  | The overmature fruit had begun to rot.  这过熟的水果已经开始腐烂了。 |  The tomatoes were overmature and mushy.  西红柿过熟且糊状。 | 近义词: overripe, ripened, mellow | 反义词: unripe, immature, green | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From over- + mature.  "Over-"  indicates excessiveness. "Mature" comes from the Latin "maturus" meaning "ripe" or "timely."
