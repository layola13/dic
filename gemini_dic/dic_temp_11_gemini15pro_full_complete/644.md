abridge:/əˈbrɪdʒ/ | v. | 缩短，删节 | To shorten (a book, movie, speech, or other text) without losing the sense.  |  The author abridges his original manuscript to make it more suitable for a younger audience.  作者删节了原稿，使其更适合年轻读者。 | shorten, condense, curtail | expand, lengthen | verb

abridge:/əˈbrɪdʒ/ | v. | 削减，减少 | To lessen the duration or scope of something. |  The king abridged the nobles' power to prevent a rebellion. 国王削减了贵族的权力以防止叛乱。| diminish, reduce, decrease | increase, augment | verb
