carotene: /ˈkærətiːn/ | n. | 胡萝卜素 | An orange or red plant pigment found in carrots and many other fruits and vegetables, and a precursor of vitamin A. | Carrots are a rich source of beta-carotene. 胡萝卜富含β-胡萝卜素。|  Carotene is essential for healthy vision. 胡萝卜素对健康视力至关重要。| synonyms: carotenoid | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *carotine*, from *carotte* (“carrot”) + *-ine* (chemical suffix). Ultimately from Latin *carōta*, from Ancient Greek *καρωτόν (karōtón)*.
