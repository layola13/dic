coccolite:/ˈkɑːkəˌlaɪt/| n. |粒辉石|A granular variety of pyroxene, usually green, consisting of an aggregation of rounded crystalline grains.|The geologist identified the green mineral as coccolite.|近义词: pyroxene, augite|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek κόκκος (kókkos, “grain, berry”) + λίθος (líthos, “stone”).  Essentially, "grain-stone".
