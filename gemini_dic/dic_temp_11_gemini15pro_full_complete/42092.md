cotransfer:/koʊˈtrænsfɜːr/
n.
共同转移; 协同转移; 共转移
The simultaneous transfer of two or more genes from one bacterial cell to another.  Two or more different genes are transferred together from one bacterium to another.
The experiment demonstrated the cotransfer of antibiotic resistance genes.  这个实验论证了抗生素抗性基因的共转移。

近义词: joint transfer, simultaneous transfer
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "independent transfer" or "separate transfer")
词性: noun
