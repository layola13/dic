acclivous: /əˈklɪvəs/
adjective
倾斜的；上坡的
sloping upward; rising
The acclivous path made the hike challenging.  这条上坡的小路使徒步旅行变得很有挑战性。
The road became increasingly acclivous as we neared the summit.  随着我们接近山顶，道路变得越来越陡峭。
近义词: ascending, uphill, steep
反义词: declivous, descending, downhill
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin acclivis (“sloping upward”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + clivus (“slope”).
