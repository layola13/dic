fratricidal: /ˌfrætərɪˈsaɪdəl/
adjective
兄弟相残的，自相残杀的
Relating to or characterized by the killing of one's brother or sister, or to internal conflict within a group.
The country was torn apart by a fratricidal civil war. 这个国家被一场自相残杀的内战撕裂了。
The fratricidal struggle within the party threatened to destroy it.  党内兄弟阋墙的斗争有可能摧毁这个党。

近义词: internecine, internecive, internecine
反义词: peaceful, harmonious

词性: adjective
