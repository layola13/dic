bluish:/ˈbluːɪʃ/| adj.|浅蓝色的，带蓝色的|Somewhat blue.|The sky was a bluish gray. 天空是浅蓝灰色。|近义词：blue, azure, cerulean|反义词:reddish, yellowish|词性:形容词

词源:  From blue + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives indicating a slight degree of a quality,  so "bluish" means "somewhat blue".
