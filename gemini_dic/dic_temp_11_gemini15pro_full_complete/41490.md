cornstarches:/ˈkɔːrnˌstɑːrtʃɪz/ | n. |玉米淀粉|A fine white powder made from cornstarch, used for thickening sauces and gravies.|Cornstarches are often used in baking to create a light and fluffy texture. 玉米淀粉常用于烘焙，以创造轻盈蓬松的质地。|Synonyms: cornstarch, cornflour|Antonyms: None|Noun
