indispose:/ˌɪndɪˈspoʊz/ | v. | 使不想; 使不适; 使反感 | To make someone slightly unwell or unable to do something. |  I have a headache that indisposes me to work. 我头痛，不想工作。

The news indisposed her to go out. 这消息使她不想外出。 | 近义词: disable, disqualify, incapacitate, unfit, ailment | 反义词: incline, dispose, enable | 词性: 动词

indisposed:/ˌɪndɪˈspoʊzd/ | adj. | 不舒服的；不愿意的 | Slightly unwell or not in the mood to do something. |  I'm feeling a little indisposed today. 我今天感觉有点不舒服。

He seemed indisposed to talk about the matter. 他似乎不愿意谈论这件事。 | 近义词: unwell, ailing, ill, reluctant, averse | 反义词: well, healthy, keen, eager | 词性: 形容词
