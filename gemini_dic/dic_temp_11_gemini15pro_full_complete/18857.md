beseechingness:/bɪˈsiːtʃɪŋnɪs/
n.
恳求，乞求; 祈求
The quality of earnestly and humbly requesting or imploring something.
Her beseechingness was evident in her tear-filled eyes and trembling voice. 她泪眼婆娑、声音颤抖，恳求之情溢于言表。
The dog looked at its owner with utter beseechingness, hoping for a treat. 小狗满怀期待地望着主人，眼神里充满了乞求，希望能得到一些好吃的。
近义词: supplication, entreaty, imploration, pleading
反义词: demand, command, insistence
词性: noun

词源: From the verb "beseech" + "-ing" + "-ness". "Beseech" comes from Middle English *bisechen*, from Old English *besēcan* "seek, implore, ask, pray," composed of *be-* "by, around" + *sēcan* "seek." The suffix "-ing" forms a present participle or gerund, and "-ness" forms a noun from an adjective or other word, indicating a state or quality.
