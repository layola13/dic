linocutting:/ˈlaɪnəʊˌkʌtɪŋ/
n.
油印木刻；麻胶版雕刻
The art or process of cutting a design into a piece of linoleum, which is then inked and used to make prints.
She took a linocutting class at the local art center. 她参加了当地艺术中心开设的油印木刻课程。

The museum displayed a collection of her linocuttings.  博物馆展出了她的一系列油印木刻作品。

woodcutting, engraving, block printing
