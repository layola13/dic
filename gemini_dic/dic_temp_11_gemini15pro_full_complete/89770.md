horded:/ˈhɔːrdɪd/ /ˈhɔːrdəd/| v. |囤积，贮藏|To accumulate and store a large amount of something, often excessively. |She horded old newspapers and magazines in her attic. 她把旧报纸和杂志都囤积在阁楼里。|The squirrels horded nuts for the winter. 松鼠们为冬天贮藏坚果。|近义词：accumulated, amassed, stockpiled, stashed|反义词:distributed, dispersed, squandered|词性:动词

词源:From the noun "hoard" (meaning a hidden store or collection), which comes from Old English "hord" meaning "treasure, valuable object."
