metamorphic:/ˌmet̬əmˈɔːrfɪk/ | adj. | 变质的，变形的 | Characterized by metamorphosis or change in form, structure, or character, especially by a process involving heat and pressure. |  Marble is a metamorphic rock. 大理石是一种变质岩。| synonyms: changed, transformed, altered, transmuted | antonyms: unchanged, unaltered, static, fixed | adjective

metamorphism:/ˌmet̬əˈmɔːrfɪzəm/ | n. | 变质，变形 | The process of transformation or change in form, structure, or character, especially by a process involving heat and pressure. | The metamorphism of the caterpillar into a butterfly is a fascinating process. 毛毛虫变形成蝴蝶是一个迷人的过程。| synonyms: transformation, alteration, transmutation, change | antonyms: stagnation, fixity, immutability | noun
