nonagrian:/ˌnɒnəˈɡreəriən/ | adj. | 非农业的，非耕地的 | Relating to or characteristic of people who are not engaged in agriculture. |  Many nonagrarian societies rely on trade for food. 许多非农业社会依靠贸易获取食物。|  The growth of nonagrarian industries led to urbanization. 非农业产业的增长导致了城市化。| 近义词: nonagricultural, industrial | 反义词: agrarian, agricultural | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + agrarian.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "agrarian" comes from the Latin word "agrarius," meaning "relating to land or fields."
