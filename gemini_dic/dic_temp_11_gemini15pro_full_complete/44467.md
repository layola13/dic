crossbarred:/ˈkrɒsbɑːrd/ (UK), /ˈkrɑːsbɑːrd/ (US)| adj.|交叉遮挡的，有横档的，格子状的|Marked with crossed bars or lines; having a pattern of crossing bars or stripes.|The old jail had crossbarred windows.  那座老监狱的窗户上有横条。|Synonyms: barred, grated, latticed, gridded|Antonyms: open, unobstructed|Adjective


crossbar:/ˈkrɒsbɑːr/ (UK), /ˈkrɑːsbɑːr/ (US)| n.|横杆，横木|A horizontal bar, beam, or piece.|The football hit the crossbar and bounced out. 足球击中了横梁弹了出来。|Synonyms: bar, beam, rail|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
