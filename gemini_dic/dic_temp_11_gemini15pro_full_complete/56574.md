domino:/ˈdɒmɪnəʊ/ (UK), /ˈdɑːmɪnoʊ/ (US)| n. |多米诺骨牌；多米诺骨牌游戏；(事件)连锁反应；遮脸面具|A small rectangular block used in the game of dominoes; the game itself; a series of events that cause similar events to follow; a mask concealing the upper part of the face.|例句：He likes to play dominoes. 他喜欢玩多米诺骨牌。


The fall of the first company triggered a domino effect throughout the industry. 第一家公司的倒闭引发了整个行业的连锁反应。|近义词：tile, piece, block (for the object); chain reaction, cascade (for the effect)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the object or the game. For the effect, perhaps "isolation," "independence")|词性:noun
