bimmetry: /baɪˈmɛtri/
noun
双侧对称; 左右对称
The property of an organism or part of an organism having two sides that are mirror images of each other.
The butterfly exhibits perfect bimmetry.  这只蝴蝶展现出完美的双侧对称。
Bilateral symmetry, mirror symmetry
Asymmetry, radial symmetry
