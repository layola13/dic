incursion:/ɪnˈkɜːrʒən/ /ɪnˈkɜːrʃən/| n. |入侵，侵犯；袭击|An invasion or attack, especially a sudden or brief one.  A hostile entrance into or invasion of a place or territory, especially a sudden one; a raid.|例句：The enemy's incursions into our territory will not be tolerated. 敌人对我们领土的入侵是不可容忍的。

例句：The nightly incursions of wild animals into the village frightened the residents.  野生动物夜间入侵村庄吓坏了居民。|近义词：invasion, raid, attack, foray, encroachment|反义词：retreat, withdrawal|词性:名词
