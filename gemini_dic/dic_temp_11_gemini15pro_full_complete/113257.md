lustreful: /ˈlʌstrəsfl/ | adj. | 有光泽的；光亮的；灿烂的 | Having a sheen or gloss; shining. | Her lustreful hair cascaded down her back. 她光亮的头发像瀑布般披在背上。 |  The car had a lustreful finish after being polished.  抛光后，汽车拥有光亮的漆面。 | 近义词: shiny, glossy, gleaming, radiant, bright | 反义词: dull, matte, lackluster | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From lustre + -ful.  Lustre itself comes from Old French lustre "luster, shine," from Italian lustro, from Latin lustrare "to brighten, purify," related to lucere "to shine."
