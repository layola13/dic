multicostate:/ˌmʌltiˈkɒsteɪt/ | adj. | 多肋的；多中脉的 | Having multiple ribs or costa (midribs of leaves). | Example 1: The multicostate leaf structure is characteristic of certain ferns.  这种多肋的叶片结构是某些蕨类植物的特征。

Example 2:  The fossil showed evidence of a multicostate wing structure. 这块化石显示了多肋翼结构的证据。 | Near Synonyms: multiribbed, polycostate | Antonyms: unicostate | Word Type: Adjective
