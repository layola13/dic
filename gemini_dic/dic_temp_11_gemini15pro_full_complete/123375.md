monomerous:/mɒˈnɒmərəs/ | adj. | 单体的，单节的 | Having or consisting of a single part or unit; unsegmented. |  The monomerous algae consist of single cells.  单体藻类由单细胞组成。| Example: Some primitive organisms are monomerous, lacking distinct body segments.  一些原始生物是单体的，缺乏明显的身体节段。 |  Synonyms: single, unitary, undivided, unsegmented | Antonyms: segmented, polymerous, multipartite | adjective
