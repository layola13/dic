directive:/dɪˈrɛktɪv/, /daɪˈrektɪv/ | n. | 指令，指示 | An official instruction or order. | The government issued a new directive on environmental protection. 政府发布了一项新的环境保护指令。| instruction, order, command | suggestion, request | noun


directive:/dɪˈrɛktɪv/, /daɪˈrektɪv/ | adj. | 指导性的，指示性的 | Giving or intended to give direction. | The CEO's speech was directive, clearly outlining the company's goals. 首席执行官的讲话具有指导性，清晰地概述了公司的目标。 | guiding, instructing, leading | permissive, flexible | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *directivus, from Latin directus (past participle of dirigere “to direct”).
