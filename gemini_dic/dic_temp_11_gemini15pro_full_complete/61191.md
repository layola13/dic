elusory:/ɪˈluːsəri/ |adj.| 虚幻的；迷惑的；难以捉摸的 |Giving a false or misleading impression; deceptive. |The elusory nature of dreams makes them difficult to remember. 梦的虚幻本质使它们难以记住。|The elusory promise of a quick fix proved to be a scam.  快速解决问题难以捉摸的承诺被证明是一个骗局。| 近义词: deceptive, misleading, illusory, evasive, fleeting | 反义词: real, tangible, concrete, substantial, definite | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *elusus*, past participle of *eludere*  "to elude, mock, deceive," from *ex- "out" + *ludere* "to play."
