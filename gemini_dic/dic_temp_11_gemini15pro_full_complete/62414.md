endognathal:/ɛnˈdɒɡnəθəl/ | adj. | 内颚的；内口式的 | Having the mouthparts enclosed within the head, as in insects.  Refers to insects whose mouthparts are retracted within the head capsule. |  Endognathous insects, such as springtails, are believed to have evolved from ectognathous ancestors. 内颚昆虫，例如跳虫，被认为是从外颚昆虫进化而来的。|  The endognathal condition is a key characteristic of insects. 内颚的特征是昆虫的一个关键特征。 |  entognathous | ectognathous | adjective
