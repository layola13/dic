ejected: /ɪˈdʒɛktɪd/
verb
喷出; 弹出; 驱逐; 逐出
forced or thrown out, often suddenly
The pilot ejected from the failing aircraft. 飞行员从失灵的飞机中弹射出来。
expel, oust, remove
retain, keep, admit
verb
