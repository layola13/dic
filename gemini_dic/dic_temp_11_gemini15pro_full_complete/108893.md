lecturing:/ˈlektʃərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlektʃərɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v.|讲课，教训|the act of giving a lecture or a series of lectures|例句：He spent the morning lecturing to his students. 他早上都在给学生讲课。

例句：She's lecturing on the history of art.  她正在讲授艺术史。

近义词：teaching, instructing, preaching, addressing, speaking|反义词：listening, learning, studying|词性: noun, verb (present participle)

lecturing:/ˈlektʃərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlektʃərɪŋ/ (UK)| v.|讲课，教训|delivering a lecture; giving a long, serious talk, often as a reprimand|例句：He was lecturing them on the dangers of smoking. 他正在告诫他们吸烟的危害。

例句：My father is always lecturing me about my messy room. 我父亲总是教训我房间太乱。
