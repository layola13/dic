lactin:/ˈlæktɪn/
n.
乳泌素，催乳激素
A hormone produced in the pituitary gland that stimulates milk production in mammals.
Prolactin levels increase during pregnancy. 怀孕期间催乳素水平会升高。
synonyms: lactogenic hormone, luteotropic hormone, mammotropin
antonyms: prolactin inhibiting factor (PIF), dopamine
noun
