hendecasyllable:/ˌhɛndɪkəˈsɪləbəl/
n.
十一音节诗行，十一音节的
A line of verse consisting of eleven syllables.
The poet skillfully crafted a hendecasyllable.  诗人巧妙地创作了一行十一音节的诗。
Near synonyms: eleven-syllable line,  hendecasyllabic verse
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could contrast with lines of different syllable counts, e.g., pentameter, hexameter)
noun
