impounder:/ɪmˈpaʊndər/| n.|扣押者，保管人|A person who takes and holds something in legal custody, especially stray animals.|The impounder took the stray dog to the shelter. 扣押者把流浪狗带到了收容所。|近义词：confiscator, seizer|反义词:releaser|noun


impound:/ɪmˈpaʊnd/| vt.|扣押，保管|To take and hold something in legal custody, especially stray animals or illegally parked vehicles.|The police impounded the car. 警方扣押了这辆车。|近义词：confiscate, seize|反义词:release|verb
