entendre:/ɑ̃ˈtɑ̃dʁ/ (French)| v. |听见，理解，意思是|To hear; to understand; to mean. |例句：Je n'entends pas bien. 我听不太清楚。

例句：Qu'est-ce que vous entendez par là ? 你这是什么意思？|近义词：comprendre (to understand), ouïr (to hear - literary)|反义词: ignorer (to not know, to ignore), méconnaître (to not recognize, misunderstand)|词性: Verb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
J'entends la musique. = I hear the music.
Il entend les oiseaux chanter. = He hears the birds singing.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
*Not typically used with entendre in this sense.*  French uses the simple present for ongoing actions perceived by the senses.

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
J'ai entendu cette chanson avant. = I have heard this song before.
Elle a entendu parler de ce film. = She has heard of this movie.

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
*Not typically used with entendre in this sense.*

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
J'ai entendu un bruit étrange. = I heard a strange noise.
Nous avons entendu la nouvelle hier. = We heard the news yesterday.

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
*Not typically used with entendre in this sense.*

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
J'avais déjà entendu cette histoire. = I had already heard this story.
Ils avaient entendu parler de lui avant de le rencontrer. = They had heard of him before meeting him.

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
*Not typically used with entendre in this sense.*


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
J'entendrai ce concert avec plaisir. = I will gladly hear/listen to this concert.
Tu entendras bientôt la vérité. = You will hear the truth soon.

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
*Not typically used with entendre in this sense.*


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
J'aurai entendu toutes ses excuses d'ici là. = I will have heard all his excuses by then.
Ils auront entendu la nouvelle avant demain. = They will have heard the news before tomorrow.


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
*Not typically used with entendre in this sense.*

虚拟语气:
Si j'entendais mieux, je comprendrais. = If I heard better, I would understand.
Il faudrait qu'il entende raison. = He should listen to reason (literally, it would be necessary that he hear reason).

被动语态:
Ce bruit est entendu de loin. = This noise is heard from afar.
La chanson a été entendue à la radio. = The song was heard on the radio.

疑问句:
Entends-tu quelque chose ? = Do you hear anything?
Qu'avez-vous entendu ? = What did you hear?

否定句:
Je n'entends rien. = I don't hear anything.
Elle n'a rien entendu. = She didn't hear anything.



PHRASE->
entendre parler de = to hear about
entendre dire que = to hear tell that
faire entendre raison à quelqu'un = to make someone see reason
s'entendre bien avec quelqu'un = to get along well with someone
à s'entendre = by mutual agreement/understanding
