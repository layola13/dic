nonimmigration:/ˌnɒnɪmɪˈɡreɪʃn/ | n. | 非移民；非移民身份 |  The process of entering a country without the intent to immigrate or become a permanent resident.  It typically involves temporary visas for purposes like tourism, business, or education. |  She entered the country on a nonimmigration visa for business purposes. 她以商务目的持非移民签证入境。 |  Non-immigrant status allows him to work here temporarily. 非移民身份允许他在这里临时工作。 | near synonyms: temporary residency, visitor status | antonyms: immigration, permanent residency | noun
