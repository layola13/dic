antirationalists:/ˌæntiˈræʃənəlɪsts/
n.
反理性主义者
Those who oppose or distrust reason and logic, especially in favor of intuition, emotion, or tradition.
Anti-rationalists often prioritize feelings and beliefs over evidence and logic. 反理性主义者往往将感觉和信仰置于证据和逻辑之上。
Modern antirationalists often question the objectivity of science. 现代反理性主义者经常质疑科学的客观性。
近义词:irrationalists, mystics, skeptics
反义词:rationalists, logicians, empiricists
词性:noun
