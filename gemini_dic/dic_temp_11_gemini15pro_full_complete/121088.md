miscorrelations:/ˌmɪskɔːrɪˈleɪʃənz/| n. |错误关联，误相关|A spurious or misleading correlation between two variables that are not causally related but appear to be due to chance, a third variable, or other factors.|The study found several miscorrelations between diet and disease that were later debunked. 这项研究发现了饮食和疾病之间的几种错误关联，后来被揭穿了。|近义词：spurious correlations, false correlations|反义词：true correlations, valid correlations|词性:名词

Etymology:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "correlation" (from Latin "cor-" meaning "together" and "relat-" meaning "carried back").
