after-taste:/ˈæftərteɪst/ /ˈɑːftərteɪst/ | n. |回味，余味|The lingering sensation or flavor left in the mouth after eating or drinking something.  The aftertaste of the medicine was unpleasant.|例句：The coffee had a bitter after-taste. 这咖啡有苦涩的回味。

例句：The wine left a pleasant after-taste. 这酒留下令人愉悦的回味。|近义词：aftereffect, lingering flavor, residual taste|反义词:fore-taste|词性:noun

after-taste:/ˈæftərteɪst/ /ˈɑːftərteɪst/ | n. |后果，余波|An unpleasant consequence or result of an experience. The scandal left a nasty after-taste in the public's mind.|例句：The argument left a bad after-taste. 这场争论留下不好的余波。

例句：The election had a bitter after-taste for the losing party.  这次选举给失败的政党留下了苦涩的余波。|近义词：aftermath, consequence, repercussion|反义词:precursor|词性:noun
