nonexistentialism:/ˌnɒnɪɡzɪˈstɛnʃəlɪzəm/ | n. | 非存在主义 | The philosophical doctrine that rejects existentialism.  It denies the emphasis on individual existence, freedom, and responsibility that characterizes existentialism. |  His nonexistentialism manifested in a strict adherence to traditional values. 他严格遵守传统价值观，体现了他的非存在主义思想。|  Traditionalism,  Objectivism | Existentialism | noun
