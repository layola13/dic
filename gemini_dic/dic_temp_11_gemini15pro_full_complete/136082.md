nonrecitation:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛsɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 不背诵，不朗读 | Absence or omission of recitation; the act of not reciting something aloud, typically in a classroom or educational setting.  | The teacher allowed nonrecitation for students who had already memorized the poem. 老师允许已经记住这首诗的学生不背诵。| Near Synonyms:  non-recital, omission of recitation, silence, non-utterance | Antonyms: recitation, recital, declamation, delivery | noun
