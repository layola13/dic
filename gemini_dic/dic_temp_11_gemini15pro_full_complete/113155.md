lupin:/ˈluːpɪn/ (US), /ˈljuːpɪn/ (UK)| n.|羽扇豆; 羽扇豆属植物|A plant of the pea family, with deeply divided leaves and tall, colorful spikes of flowers. Lupins are grown as ornamentals and for their seeds, which are sometimes used as food. |例句：The lupins in the garden were a riot of color. 花园里的羽扇豆五彩缤纷。

例句：Lupin seeds can be ground into flour. 羽扇豆种子可以磨成粉。|近义词：lupine|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


lupine:/ˈluːpaɪn/ (US), /ˈljuːpaɪn/ (UK)| adj.|狼的; 像狼的; 残忍的|Of or relating to wolves; wolflike; ravenous.|例句：He had a lupine hunger after his long journey.  长途旅行后，他饿得像狼一样。

例句：The dog gave a lupine howl at the moon. 那只狗对着月亮发出狼一样的嚎叫。|近义词：wolfish, ravenous|反义词:gentle, tame|词性:adjective
