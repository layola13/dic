distill:/dɪˈstɪl/ /dəˈstɪl/| vt. vi. |蒸馏；提炼；渗出；滴下|To purify a liquid by heating it until it becomes a gas and then cooling the gas back into a liquid.  To extract the essential meaning or most important aspects of something.|They distill the whiskey from malted barley. 他们用麦芽大麦蒸馏威士忌。|synonyms: purify, refine, extract, concentrate|antonyms: combine, mix, dilute|verb

distill:/dɪˈstɪl/ /dəˈstɪl/| n. |蒸馏液；蒸馏物；精华|The product of distillation.|The distill of his research was published in a scientific journal.  他研究的精华发表在一份科学期刊上。|synonyms: essence, concentrate, extract|antonyms: mixture, blend|noun
