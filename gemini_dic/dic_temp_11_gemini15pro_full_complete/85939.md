headman:/ˈhedmən/| n.|头人，首领，工头|A person who is the leader of a tribe, village, or group of workers.|The headman called a meeting of the villagers. 村长召集了村民开会。|Synonyms: chief, leader, foreman|Antonyms: follower, subordinate|noun

headman:/ˈhedmən/| n.|头人，首领，工头|A person who is in charge of a group of workers, especially in a mine or on a farm.|The headman directed the miners to start work. 工头指挥矿工开始工作。|Synonyms: foreman, overseer, supervisor|Antonyms: worker, laborer|noun
