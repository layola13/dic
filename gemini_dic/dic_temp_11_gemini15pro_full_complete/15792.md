balk:/bɔːk/ (US) /bɔːlk/ (UK)| v. n.| 阻碍，不愿接受；突然止步不前；棒球术语（投手犯规）|To hesitate or refuse to proceed or comply; to stop short and refuse to go on; (in baseball) an illegal motion by the pitcher.| The horse balked at the jump. 这匹马在跳跃前畏缩不前。 | 近义词：hesitate, resist, recoil, shy, demur | 反义词：accept, comply, proceed | 词性：动词/名词

balk:/bɔːk/ (US) /bɔːlk/ (UK)| n.| 阻碍；障碍；梁木；错误 |An obstacle or hindrance; a roughly squared timber beam; a blunder or mistake. | His refusal to sign the contract was a major balk to the deal. 他拒绝签署合同是这笔交易的主要障碍。| 近义词：hindrance, obstacle, impediment | 反义词：aid, assistance | 词性：名词

词源:  Late Middle English: probably from a Scandinavian source such as Old Norse *balkr ‘ridge, partition’, of Germanic origin; related to balk2.
