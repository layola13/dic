healthier:/ˈhɛlθɪər/ (US), /ˈhelθɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更健康的|Comparative form of healthy.  Indicates a greater degree of health than something else.|Eating more vegetables is healthier than eating only fast food.  多吃蔬菜比只吃快餐更健康。|She looks healthier now that she's exercising regularly.  她现在经常锻炼，看起来更健康了。|近义词：fitter, stronger, well|反义词:sicker, weaker, unhealthier|词性:adjective
