cokenay:/ˈkəʊkni/| n. adj.|伦敦东区人，伦敦东区方言|A native or inhabitant of the East End of London; also, the dialect or accent traditionally spoken there.  A Cockney is often characterized by their use of rhyming slang.|He's a proper Cockney, born and bred within the sound of Bow Bells. 他是一个地道的伦敦东区人，在鲍尔斯钟声的范围内出生和长大。|Synonyms: East Ender, Londoner|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps someone from outside London/rural areas)|Noun, Adjective
