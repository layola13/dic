brandishing:/ˈbrændɪʃɪŋ/
verb
挥舞；炫耀
The act of waving or flourishing something, especially a weapon, in a menacing or excited way. Also, displaying something ostentatiously.
He was brandishing a knife at them. 他挥舞着刀子对着他们。
She brandished her new phone in front of her friends. 她在朋友面前炫耀她的新手机。
synonyms: waving, flourishing, displaying, flaunting
antonyms: concealing, hiding
