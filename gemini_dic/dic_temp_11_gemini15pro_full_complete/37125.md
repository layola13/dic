combusts:/kəmˈbʌsts/ | v. |燃烧，烧毁|burns; catches fire|The rocket fuel combusts violently upon contact with air. 火箭燃料与空气接触后猛烈燃烧。|近义词：burns, ignites, incinerates |反义词: extinguishes, smothers |词性:动词
词源: From Latin *comburere*, from *com-* (together) + *burere* (to burn).
