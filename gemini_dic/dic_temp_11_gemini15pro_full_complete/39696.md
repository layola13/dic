considered:/kənˈsɪdəd/ | adj. | 被认为的；经过仔细考虑的 | Thought about carefully; regarded as. | She is considered a brilliant scientist. 她被认为是一位杰出的科学家。|  The plan was considered impractical.  这个计划被认为是不切实际的。 |近义词：deemed, regarded, reputed, judged |反义词：ignored, disregarded, overlooked |词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin considerare "to look at closely, observe, contemplate," from com- "together" (see com-) + sidus "star, constellation."  Originally, it meant "to examine the stars, consult the stars," hence "to reflect, ponder."
