inwreathe:/ɪnˈriːð/
verb
环绕；盘绕；用…环绕
to encircle or decorate with a wreath or something resembling a wreath.
Her hair was inwreathed with flowers. 她的头发上环绕着鲜花。
The pillars were inwreathed with ivy.  柱子上缠绕着常春藤。
synonyms: encircle, twine, wreathe, festoon, garland, crown
antonyms: uncoil, unwind, disentangle
词性:v.
