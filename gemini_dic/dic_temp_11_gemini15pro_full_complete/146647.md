overharshly: /ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrʃli/ | adv. | 过于严厉地，过于苛刻地 |  In a way that is too harsh or severe.  | He criticized her overharshly for a minor mistake. 他因为一个小错误而过于严厉地批评了她。 | synonyms: excessively harshly, unduly severely, too critically | antonyms: leniently, gently, mildly | adverb

overharsh: /ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrʃ/ | adj. | 过于严厉的，过于苛刻的 | Too harsh or severe. | The judge's sentence was considered overharsh by many. 许多人认为法官的判决过于苛刻。| synonyms: excessively harsh, unduly severe, too critical | antonyms: lenient, gentle, mild | adjective

harsh: /hɑːrʃ/ | adj. | 严厉的，苛刻的 |  Unpleasantly severe, rough, or grating. | The harsh winter weather made life difficult. 严酷的冬季天气使生活变得艰难。 | synonyms: severe, austere, rigorous | antonyms: gentle, mild, lenient | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From *over- + harsh + -ly*.  *Over-* is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much." *Harsh* comes from the Middle English *harsch*, probably from the Middle Low German *harsch* meaning "rough," "coarse."  *-ly* is a suffix that forms adverbs.
