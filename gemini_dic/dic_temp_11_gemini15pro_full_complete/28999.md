carthorse:/ˈkɑːrthɔːrs/ /ˈkɑːrthɔːrs/| n.|挽马；重型马；（比喻）强壮的人或物|A carthorse is a large, strong horse traditionally used for pulling carts or other heavy loads. It can also be used figuratively to describe someone or something strong and dependable, but perhaps not very graceful or refined. |例句：The old carthorse plodded steadily along the road. 老挽马沿着路稳稳地走着。

例句：He's a real carthorse of a man, capable of incredible amounts of work. 他真是个壮汉，能干难以置信的活。|近义词：workhorse, draught horse|反义词:racehorse, thoroughbred|词性:noun
