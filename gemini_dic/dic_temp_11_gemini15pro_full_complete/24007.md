breathtaking:/ˈbreθteɪkɪŋ/| adj.| 令人叹为观止的；激动人心的|Something that is breathtaking is extremely exciting, beautiful, or surprising.|The view from the mountaintop was breathtaking. 从山顶望下去的景色令人叹为观止。|近义词：stunning, awesome, spectacular, amazing, incredible|反义词：unimpressive, ordinary, dull|词性:形容词
Etymology: breath + taking.  From the idea that something so beautiful or exciting literally takes your breath away.
