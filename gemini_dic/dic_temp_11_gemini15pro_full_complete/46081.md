cyclopedist:/ˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪst/
n.
百科全书编纂者; 百科全书式的人物
A person who compiles or contributes to an encyclopedia, or who is a comprehensive source of information.
Example: Diderot was the chief cyclopedist of the famous French Encyclopédie.  狄德罗是著名的法国百科全书的主编。

Example:  He was a true cyclopedist, knowledgeable on a vast range of subjects. 他是一位真正的百科全书式的人物，对各种各样的学科都了如指掌。


近义词: encyclopedist, polymath, scholar
反义词: specialist, ignoramus
词性: noun
