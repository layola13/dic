coumarou:/ˈkuːməruː/ | n. |香豆素,库马卢|A type of tropical tree (Dipteryx odorata) native to South America, whose seeds (tonka beans) contain coumarin and are used for flavoring. Also refers to the timber of this tree.|The coumarou tree is prized for its fragrant beans.|近义词:tonka bean tree, cumaru|反义词:N/A|名词
