odonata:/oʊdəˈneɪtə/ /ˌɒdəˈnɑːtə/
noun
蜻蜓目
An order of insects encompassing dragonflies and damselflies, characterized by large multifaceted eyes, two pairs of transparent membranous wings, and an elongated body.
Dragonflies and damselflies belong to the order Odonata. 蜻蜓和豆娘属于蜻蜓目。
The odonata are important predators of mosquitoes and other flying insects. 蜻蜓目是蚊子和其他飞行昆虫的重要捕食者。
synonyms:dragonflies, damselflies
antonyms: None (Odonata is a taxonomic classification, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
