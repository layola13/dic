nonappointment:/ˌnɒnəˈpɔɪntmənt/
n.
未任命；未约定
the failure or refusal to appoint someone to a position.
His nonappointment to the board caused a stir among the shareholders. 他未被任命进入董事会，在股东中引起了一阵骚动。
lack of an appointment or arrangement.
The nonappointment led to a delay in the project. 未能约定导致项目延期。
non-selection, failure to appoint, omission, rejection
appointment, selection, designation
noun
