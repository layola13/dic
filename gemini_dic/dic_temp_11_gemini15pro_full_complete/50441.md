deprecative:/ˈdeprɪkeɪtɪv/| adj.|贬低的，轻视的，自贬的|Expressing disapproval; apologetic or self-deprecating.|例句：He spoke in a deprecative tone about his own work. 他以一种自贬的语气谈论自己的工作。

例句：She gave a deprecative smile. 她露出了一个自谦的微笑。|近义词：disapproving, critical, apologetic, self-deprecating|反义词：approving, complimentary, boasting|词性:adjective
