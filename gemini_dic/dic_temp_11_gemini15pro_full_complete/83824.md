hajjes: /ˈhædʒɪz/ | n. | 朝圣者（尤指去麦加朝圣的穆斯林）| Pilgrims, especially Muslims who have made the pilgrimage to Mecca. |  Example:  The hajjes gathered in Mecca for the annual pilgrimage.  朝圣者聚集在麦加进行一年一度的朝圣。

Example: Many hajjes travel from all over the world to perform the Hajj. 许多朝圣者从世界各地赶来完成朝觐。 | 近义词: pilgrim, palmer | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Arabic حجّاج (ḥujjāj), plural of حاجّ (ḥājj, “pilgrim”).
