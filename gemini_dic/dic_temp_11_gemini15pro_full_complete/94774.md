imprudentnesses:/ɪmˈpruːdntnəsɪz/
noun
轻率的行为；鲁莽之举
lack of caution or good judgment; rashness.  The plural form of "imprudence," referring to multiple instances of unwise actions or decisions.
His youthful imprudentnesses cost him dearly later in life. 他年轻时的轻率行为使他在以后的生活中付出了高昂的代价。
Synonyms: follies, indiscretions, recklessnesses
Antonyms: cautiousness, prudence, wisdom
