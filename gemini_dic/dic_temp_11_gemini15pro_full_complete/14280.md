autographing:/ˈɔːtəˌɡræfɪŋ/
n./the action of writing one's signature on something/签名，亲笔签名/The author was autographing copies of her latest book./作家正在为她最新著作的副本签名。/signing, endorsing/forging/noun
autograph:/ˈɔːtəˌɡræf/
n./a signature, especially that of a celebrity written as a memento for an admirer/亲笔签名，手迹/I got Taylor Swift's autograph after the concert!/演唱会结束后我拿到了泰勒·斯威夫特的签名！/signature/anonymous/noun
autograph:/ˈɔːtəˌɡræf/
vt./write one's signature on/在…上签名/Could you autograph this book for me, please?/你能帮我在这本书上签名吗？/sign/erase/verb
