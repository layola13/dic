agave:/əˈɡɑːveɪ/
n.
龙舌兰；龙舌兰植物
A succulent plant with rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves and a tall flower stalk, native to Mexico and the southern US.  Many species are cultivated for their fiber or sap, which is used to make tequila and other alcoholic drinks.
Tequila is made from the agave plant.  龙舌兰酒是由龙舌兰植物酿造的。

The blue agave is used to make tequila. 蓝色龙舌兰被用来制作龙舌兰酒。

近义词：century plant (因其寿命长而得名)
反义词:  无
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *agavē, from Greek *agaue, from agauos “noble, illustrious, admirable.”
