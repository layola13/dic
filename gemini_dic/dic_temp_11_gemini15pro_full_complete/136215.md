nonremediable:/ˌnɒnriˈmiːdiəbl/ | adj. | 不可弥补的，无法补救的 | Not capable of being remedied, cured, or corrected. | The doctor informed the family that the patient's condition was nonremediable. 医生告知家人，病人的病情已无法治愈。 |  The damage to the ecosystem was nonremediable. 对生态系统的破坏是不可弥补的。 | 近义词: incurable, irremediable, irreparable, irreversible | 反义词: remediable, curable, fixable | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (not) + remediable (capable of being remedied) from Latin remedium (remedy)
