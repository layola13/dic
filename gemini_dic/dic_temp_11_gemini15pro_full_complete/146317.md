overfeels:/ˌoʊvərˈfiːlz/| v. |过度感受；反应过度|To experience or express emotions too intensely, often in a way that seems exaggerated or excessive.|例句：She overfeels everything, even minor setbacks. 她对每件事都反应过度，即使是小小的挫折。

例句：He tends to overfeel when watching sad movies. 他看悲伤电影时容易过度感伤。|近义词：overreact, exaggerate, be overly sensitive|反义词：underreact, downplay|词性:动词
