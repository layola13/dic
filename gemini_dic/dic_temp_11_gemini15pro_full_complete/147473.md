overopinionatedness:/ˌoʊvərəˈpɪnjəneɪtɪdnəs/
|n.| 过于武断；固执己见 |The state of being excessively opinionated, often to the point of being dogmatic or arrogant.|  His overopinionatedness made it difficult to have a productive conversation. 他的固执己见使得很难进行富有成效的对话。 |  Her overopinionatedness alienated many of her colleagues. 她的固执己见疏远了许多同事。|Synonyms: dogmatism, rigidity, inflexibility | Antonyms: open-mindedness, receptiveness, flexibility | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + opinionated + -ness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Opinionated" is an adjective describing someone who holds strong opinions and is unwilling to consider other viewpoints. "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
