barque:/bɑːrk/| n. |三桅帆船，轻舟 |A sailing vessel with three or more masts, all of which are square-rigged except the after mast, which is fore-and-aft rigged.|The barque sailed gracefully across the bay. 这艘三桅帆船优雅地航行过海湾。|近义词：ship, vessel, sailboat|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 land vehicle 陆地交通工具) |noun
