lightnings:/ˈlaɪtnɪŋz/| n. |闪电|Multiple flashes of lightning illuminated the sky. 多道闪电照亮了天空。|Lightning is a sudden electrostatic discharge during an electrical storm. 闪电是雷暴期间突然发生的静电放电。|例句：The lightnings flashed across the dark sky. 闪电划过黑暗的天空。  The lightnings illuminated the stormy night. 闪电照亮了暴风雨的夜晚。|近义词：bolt, flash, stroke |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 darkness 黑暗)|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *lightnyng, *leightnynge, *lehtninge, from 古英语 *līhting, *lēohting (“lightning”), from *līhtan, *lēohtan (“to shine, flash, gleam”). Cognate with West Frisian ljochting (“lightning”), Dutch lichten (“to shine”), German leuchten (“to shine”).
