furriest:/ˈfɜːriɪst/ | adj. | 最毛茸茸的 | Covered with the most fur; having a thick coat of soft hair.  | Example 1: The furriest kitten in the litter cuddled up to its mother.  最毛茸茸的小猫依偎着它的妈妈。

Example 2:  Of all the teddy bears, the brown one was the furriest.  在所有的泰迪熊中，棕色的那只最毛茸茸。 | Synonym: hairiest, fluffiest, shaggiest | Antonym: smoothest, baldest | 词性: 形容词
