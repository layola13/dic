audaciously: /ɔːˈdeɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 大胆地，鲁莽地 | In a way that shows a willingness to take risks or offend people. |  He audaciously challenged the chairman's decision. 他大胆地挑战了主席的决定。

She audaciously asked for a raise after only a week on the job. 她才工作一个星期就大胆地要求加薪。| 近义词: boldly, daringly, impudently, brazenly | 反义词: timidly, cautiously, hesitantly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Latin "audax" (bold) -> Old French "audace" -> audacious -> audaciously
