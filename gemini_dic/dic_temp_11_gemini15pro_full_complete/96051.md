indecible:/ˌɪndɪˈsaɪdəbl/| adj.|不可判定的，无法决定的|Not capable of being decided or settled; not decidable.  Relating to a problem in mathematics, logic, or computer science that cannot be solved by any algorithm.|The halting problem is a classic example of an indecidable problem in computer science.  停机问题是计算机科学中一个典型的不可判定问题的例子。|近义词：unsolvable, indeterminable, unprovable|反义词：decidable, solvable, determinable|词性:形容词
词源:  From in- (not) + decidable, from decide (to settle conclusively) + -able (capable of being).  Ultimately from Latin decidere (to cut off, determine).
