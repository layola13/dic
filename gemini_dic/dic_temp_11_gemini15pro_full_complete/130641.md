nonadmittedly:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 非公开地；未经承认地 | Not admittedly; without open acknowledgment.  In a way that is not openly admitted or acknowledged.  | He nonadmittedly used the information for his own gain. 他没有公开承认地将这些信息用于个人利益。 |  He nonadmittedly glanced at his neighbor's test paper. 他偷偷地看了一眼邻居的试卷。 | 近义词: secretly, covertly, privately | 反义词: openly, publicly, admittedly | 词性: 副词

词源:  "Nonadmittedly" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "admitted" (meaning "acknowledged" or "confessed") and the suffix "-ly" (forming an adverb). The root word "admit" comes from the Latin "admittere," meaning "to send to" or "to let in."
