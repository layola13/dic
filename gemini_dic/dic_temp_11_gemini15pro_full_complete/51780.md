devotionist:/dɪˈvoʊʃənɪst/
n.
虔诚者，献身者
A person who is extremely devoted to a cause, religion, or person.
She was a true devotionist, dedicating her life to the service of others. 她是一位真正的献身者，一生致力于服务他人。
The devotionist spent hours in prayer every day.  这位虔诚的信徒每天花几个小时祈祷。
近义词:  zealot, fanatic, enthusiast, adherent, believer
反义词:  skeptic, doubter, critic, cynic
词性: noun

devotion:/dɪˈvoʊʃən/
n.
奉献，虔诚，热爱
The act of dedicating oneself to a cause, activity, etc.
His devotion to his family was unwavering. 他对家人的爱是坚定不移的。
Her devotion to her art was inspiring. 她对艺术的热爱令人鼓舞。
近义词: dedication, commitment, piety, fervor, zeal
反义词: apathy, indifference, neglect
词性: noun


词源:  From Late Latin *devotionem, from Latin devotus "devoted".
