academia:/ˌækəˈdiːmiə/ | n. |学术界，学术环境| the environment or community concerned with the pursuit of research, education, and scholarship. | She spent her entire adult life in academia. 她成年后一直在学术界工作。|  The pressure to publish is intense in academia. 在学术界，发表论文的压力很大。| 近义词: scholarly world, university, educational institutions | 反义词: industry, business world | 词性: noun

词源:  From Latin academia "Plato's school, named after a hero Akademos", from Greek Akadēmeia, Akadēmos.
