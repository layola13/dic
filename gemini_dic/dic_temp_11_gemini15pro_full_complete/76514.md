galenic: /ɡəˈlɛnɪk/ /ɡəˈliːnɪk/
adjective
盖伦的; 盖伦医学体系的
Relating to or involving the medical theories or methods of Galen.
Galenic medicine emphasized the balance of the four humors. 盖伦医学强调四种体液的平衡。
Traditional galenic preparations use plant-based ingredients. 传统的盖伦制剂使用植物性成分。
近义词: traditional, herbal
反义词: modern, synthetic
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Galen, a prominent Greek physician, surgeon and philosopher in the Roman Empire (c. 129 – c. 200/c. 216).  His theories dominated Western medical science for well over a millennium.
