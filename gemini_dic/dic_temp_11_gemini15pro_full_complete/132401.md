nondenial:/ˌnɒndɪˈnaɪəl/ |adj.|不可否认的|The evidence is nondenial.  证据确凿，不可否认。|例句：The nondenial fact is that climate change is happening. 不可否认的事实是气候变化正在发生。|例句：His contribution to the project is nondenial. 他对这个项目的贡献是不可否认的。|近义词：undeniable, irrefutable, indisputable|反义词：deniable, disputable, refutable|词性:形容词
词源:  Formed from "non-" (not) + "denial" (the act of denying).  Ultimately from Latin "denegare" (to deny).
