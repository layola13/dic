nonsequentiality:/ˌnɒnˌsɛkwɛnʃiˈælɪti/ /ˌnɑːnˌsɪˈkwɛnʃiˈælɪti/
noun
不连贯性；不合逻辑；前后不一致
lack of a logical or coherent sequence
The nonsequentiality of his speech made it difficult to follow. 他的讲话不连贯，很难理解。
incoherence, illogicality, disjointedness
coherence, logicality, connectedness
n.
