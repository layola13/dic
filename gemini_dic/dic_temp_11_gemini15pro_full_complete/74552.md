fraudcast: /ˈfrɔːdkæst/
n.
欺诈性广播；诈骗广播
A fraudulent broadcast, typically one designed to spread misinformation or deceive the public.
The fraudcast claimed there was a cure for cancer, leading many desperate people to spend their life savings.  这个欺诈性广播声称有一种治疗癌症的方法，导致许多绝望的人花光了毕生积蓄。
The fraudcast spread rapidly on social media, causing widespread panic.  这则欺诈性广播在社交媒体上迅速传播，引起了广泛的恐慌。
近义词: hoax, scam, deception, disinformation
反义词: truth, fact, accuracy
词性: 名词

词源:  A blend of "fraud" and "broadcast".  由“fraud”（欺诈）和“broadcast”（广播）两个词混合而成。
