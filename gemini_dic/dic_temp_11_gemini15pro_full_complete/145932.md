overdisciplining: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪsəplɪnɪŋ/
|v.|过度管教，过分训练|The act of disciplining someone too much or too strictly.|He worried about overdisciplining his children, wanting them to have a happy childhood. 他担心对孩子管教过度，希望他们拥有快乐的童年。|近义词: overcontrolling, overregulating|反义词: underdisciplining, neglecting|动名词

overdiscipline: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪsəplɪn/
|v.|过度管教，过分训练|To discipline someone too much or too strictly.|The coach was accused of overdisciplining the young athletes. 教练被指控对年轻运动员进行过度训练。|近义词: overcontrol, overregulate|反义词: underdiscipline, neglect|动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + discipline.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much." "Discipline" comes from the Latin word "disciplina," meaning "instruction," "knowledge," or "training."
