intercirculate:/ˌɪntərˈsɜːrkjəleɪt/
verb
相互循环，互相流通
To circulate or cause to circulate among one another.
The blood intercirculates between the heart and the lungs. 血液在心脏和肺之间循环。
The rumors intercirculated quickly through the small town. 谣言在这个小镇上迅速传播开来。
近义词: circulate, distribute, spread
反义词: stagnate, localize
词性: verb
