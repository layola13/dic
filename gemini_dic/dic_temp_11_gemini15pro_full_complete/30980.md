chaldaic:/kælˈdeɪɪk/
adjective
迦勒底的; 阿拉姆语的
relating to Chaldea, its people, or their language.  Aramaic.
例句：The chaldaic inscriptions on the wall were difficult to decipher.  墙上的迦勒底铭文难以破译。

例句：He was studying the chaldaic origins of certain biblical texts. 他正在研究某些圣经文本的阿拉姆语起源。
近义词：Aramaic, Babylonian
反义词: None
词性: adjective
