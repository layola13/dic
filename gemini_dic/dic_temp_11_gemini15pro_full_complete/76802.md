gammon:/ˈɡæmən/ | n. |熏猪腿；火腿；胡说八道；废话 |A cured ham, especially from the hindquarters of a pig;  Nonsense; deceitful talk. |例句：We had gammon for Christmas dinner. 我们圣诞晚餐吃了熏猪腿。

gammon:/ˈɡæmən/ | vt. |欺骗；用谎言哄骗 |To deceive or hoax someone; to defeat soundly in backgammon. |例句：He gammoned me into lending him money. 他骗我借钱给他。
