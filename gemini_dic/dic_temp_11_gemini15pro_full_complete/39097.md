confront:/kənˈfrʌnt/ /kɑːnˈfrʌnt/| vt.|  遭遇，遇到| to be faced with or experience something unpleasant| The team was confronted with a major problem. 团队面临着一个重大问题。| synonyms: encounter, meet, experience | antonyms: avoid, miss | verb
