glyph:/ɡlɪf/ | n. | 字形，符号 |A symbolic figure or character, typically carved or incised.  A glyph is an element of writing. | The ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs are a complex system of glyphs. 古埃及的象形文字是一个复杂的字形系统。 | example: The software can generate a variety of glyphs for different fonts.  这款软件可以为不同的字体生成各种字形。| synonym: character, symbol, sign, mark | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "blank" in a specific context) | noun
