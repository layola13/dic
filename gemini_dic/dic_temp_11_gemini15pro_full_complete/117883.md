melodrame:/ˈmeləˌdrɑːmə/ | n. |情节剧，通俗剧|A sensational dramatic piece with exaggerated characters and exciting events intended to appeal to the emotions.|The play was a pure melodrama, full of improbable plot twists and overwrought emotions. 这部戏纯粹是情节剧，充满了不可能的情节转折和夸张的情感。|近义词：drama, play, theatrics|反义词：understatement|词性:名词

melodromatic:/ˌmelədrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. |情节剧的，夸张的|Characteristic of melodrama, especially in being exaggerated, sensationalized, or overemotional.|Her reaction was melodramatic, as if she were the star of a tragic opera. 她的反应很夸张，就像她是悲剧歌剧的主角一样。|近义词：dramatic, theatrical, exaggerated|反义词：realistic, understated|词性:形容词
