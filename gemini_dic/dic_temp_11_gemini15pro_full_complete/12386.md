ashy:/ˈæʃi/| adj.|灰白的；灰色的；苍白的|Resembling ashes in color; pale.|Her face was ashy with fear. 她的脸色因害怕而变得灰白。|近义词：pale, gray, wan|反义词：ruddy, flushed|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æscig (unattested), from æsce (“ash”) +‎ -ig. Cognate with Middle Low German asschich, aschich (“ashy”).
