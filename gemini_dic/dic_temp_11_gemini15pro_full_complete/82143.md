gronte:/ˈɡrɒnteɪ/ | n. |  暴风雨，雷暴 |  A sudden, violent storm, often with thunder and lightning. Derived from the Italian word "gronto," meaning "grumbling" or "rumbling."  |  The gronte lashed the coast, causing significant damage.  暴风雨袭击了海岸，造成了重大破坏。 |  The ship was tossed about in the fierce gronte.  船在猛烈的暴风雨中颠簸。 | 近义词: tempest, storm, thunderstorm | 反义词: calm, stillness | 词性: noun
