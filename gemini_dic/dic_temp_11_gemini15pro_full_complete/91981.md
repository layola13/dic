hypernatural:/ˌhaɪpərˈnætʃrəl/ | adj. | 超自然的，超越自然的 | Existing beyond or outside of nature; supernatural.  Relating to or characteristic of a hyperreal or surreal state or condition. | The artist's work explored hypernatural themes, depicting landscapes that defied the laws of physics. 艺术家的作品探索了超自然的主题，描绘了违反物理定律的景观。

|The feeling of connection was so intense it felt hypernatural. 这种联系的感觉如此强烈，以至于感觉超出了自然。| 近义词：supernatural, otherworldly, paranormal, transcendental | 反义词：natural, mundane, earthly | 词性: adjective
