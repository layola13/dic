dyad: /ˈdaɪæd/ | n. | 二分体；二人组；二元组 | A group of two people, a pair; something that consists of two elements or parts. |例句：The mother-child dyad is the most basic social unit. 母子二元组是最基本的社会单元。

例句：The researchers studied the interactions within the dyad. 研究人员研究了二元组内的互动。|近义词：pair, duo, couple |反义词: group, crowd | noun


dyadic: /daɪˈædɪk/ | adj. | 二元的；二体的；成双的 | Relating to a dyad; characterized by interaction between two people or elements. | 例句：Dyadic communication is fundamental to human interaction.  二元沟通是人类互动中至关重要的。

例句：The study focused on the dyadic relationship between therapist and client. 这项研究的重点是治疗师和客户之间的二元关系。| 近义词：dual, binary, twofold | 反义词: multiple, group | adjective
