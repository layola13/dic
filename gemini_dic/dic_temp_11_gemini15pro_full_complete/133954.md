nonheinousness:/ˌnɒnˈheɪnəsnəs/
n.
无罪性；非暴虐性；非极恶性
The nonheinousness of the crime led to a reduced sentence.  这起罪行的非暴虐性导致了减刑。
The judge considered the nonheinousness of the offense. 法官考虑了该罪行的非极恶性。
Near Synonyms: innocence, harmlessness, mildness
Antonyms: heinousness, atrocity, wickedness
Etymology:  From "non-" (not) + "heinous" (hatefully or shockingly evil) + "-ness" (forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition).  "Heinous" ultimately comes from the Old French "haineus," meaning "hateful."
