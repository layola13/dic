laryngealization:/ləˌrɪndʒiələɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|喉音化；喉化音|The process of making a sound using the larynx. This involves constricting the larynx, which changes the quality of the sound produced.|The laryngealization in her voice gave it a distinctive husky quality. 她的声音中的喉音化使它带有一种独特的沙哑感。|Synonyms:  creaky voice, vocal fry|Antonyms: clear voice
|noun
