illocality:/ˌɪloʊˈkælɪti/ |n.| 非定域性，非局域性 |The concept of illocality in quantum mechanics suggests that two entangled particles can instantaneously affect each other, regardless of the distance separating them. 量子力学中的非定域性概念表明，两个纠缠的粒子可以瞬间相互影响，而不管它们之间的距离有多远。|Quantum entanglement demonstrates illocality. 量子纠缠证明了非定域性。|近义词: nonlocality, non-separability|反义词: locality|词性: 名词
