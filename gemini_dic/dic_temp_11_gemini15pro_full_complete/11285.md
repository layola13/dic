arcuated:/ˈɑːrkjʊeɪtɪd/| adj.|拱形的，弓形的|Curved or shaped like an arch.|The arcuated doorway gave the building a grand entrance. 拱形的门口使建筑物显得宏伟。|例句：The bridge has an arcuated design. 这座桥有一个拱形的设计。|近义词：arched, curved, vaulted|反义词:straight, linear|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *arcuatus, past participle of arcuare "to bend like a bow," from Latin arcus "bow, arch."
