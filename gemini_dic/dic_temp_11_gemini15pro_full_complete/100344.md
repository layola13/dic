interstapedial:/ˌɪntərˈsteɪʃəl/ | adj. | 间冰期的 | Occurring during an interstadial, a relatively warm period during a glacial age. | The interstapedial deposits contained fossils of temperate plants. 间冰期沉积物中含有温带植物化石。| The archaeologist discovered evidence of human habitation during the last interstapedial. 考古学家发现了上一次间冰期人类居住的证据。 | 近义词: interglacial | 反义词: glacial, stadial | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From *inter- "between" + stadial "relating to a stadial (a cold period within a glacial age)."  Ultimately from Latin *stāre* "to stand."
