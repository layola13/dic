affirmance:/əˈfɜːrməns/ | n. | 确认，批准，肯定 |The court's affirmance of the lower court's decision was expected. 法院对下级法院判决的确认是意料之中的。|  The president's affirmance of his commitment to the project reassured investors. 总统对该项目承诺的肯定让投资者安心。 | 近义词：confirmation, ratification, approval, validation | 反义词：reversal, rejection, denial, negation | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *affermaunce*, from Anglo-French *affermaunce*, from *affermer* ("to affirm"), from Latin *affirmare* ("to strengthen, confirm"), from *ad-* ("to") + *firmare* ("to make firm, strengthen"), from *firmus* ("firm, strong").
