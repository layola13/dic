glimmered:/ˈɡlɪmərd/| v.|闪烁，微光|shine faintly; appear briefly or faintly|The candle glimmered in the dark room. 蜡烛在黑暗的房间里闪烁着。|Synonyms: shimmered, flickered, twinkled|Antonyms: blazed, shone, glared|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *glimeren*, from Old English *glīmrian* (“to shine dimly”), from Proto-Germanic *glemmianą* (“to gleam”). Cognate with Dutch *glimmeren* (“to glimmer”), German *glimmern* (“to glimmer”), Swedish *glimma* (“to shine, glitter”).
