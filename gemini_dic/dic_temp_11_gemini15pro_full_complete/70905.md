fire-hydrant:/ˈfaɪər ˈhaɪdrənt/ (US), /ˈfaɪə ˈhaɪdrənt/ (UK)| n.|消防栓|A connection point for a fire hose, usually located on a street corner and containing a valve and outlet for water under pressure used by firefighters to extinguish fires.|The firefighters quickly attached the hose to the fire hydrant. 消防队员迅速将水管连接到消防栓上。|The dog lifted its leg against the fire hydrant. 那条狗对着消防栓抬起了腿。|Synonym: fireplug|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps a broken or unusable fire hydrant)|Noun
