crib-deaths: /ˈkrɪb dɛθs/ | n. | 婴儿猝死综合症 | Sudden and unexpected death of an apparently healthy infant during sleep, typically between the ages of one month and one year, also called sudden infant death syndrome (SIDS).  |  Researchers are still trying to understand the causes of crib deaths. 研究人员仍在试图了解婴儿猝死的原因。 | synonym: SIDS, cot death | antonym: survival, live birth | noun
