beloochee:/bəˈluːtʃi/ /bəˈloʊtʃi/
noun, adjective|俾路支人; 俾路支语|  Relating to Balochistan, its people, or their language.  A member of a people inhabiting chiefly Balochistan. The language of the Baloch people, belonging to the Iranian branch of the Indo-European family.|He is a Beloochee. 他是一个俾路支人。|The Beloochee language is spoken in several countries. 俾路支语在几个国家使用。|Synonyms: Baluchi, Baloch|Antonyms: N/A|n., adj.
