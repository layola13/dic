hedra:/ˈhedrə/
n.
seats, faces, bases (plural)
surfaces, usually the plane surfaces bounding a solid figure, especially a polyhedron.
A crystal with 12 hedra.  一个有12个面的晶体。
近义词:faces, surfaces
反义词: (No direct antonym for a geometrical term like "hedra")
词性:Noun
