concertinaed: /ˌkɒnsəˈtiːnəd/ (UK), /ˌkɑːnsərˈtiːnəd/ (US)
|adj.|压缩的，折叠的，像手风琴一样折叠的|Compressed or folded in a way that resembles the action of a concertina (a musical instrument similar to an accordion).|The crash concertinaed the front of the car.  这次撞车把车的前部压扁了。|The train cars concertinaed into each other after the collision. 火车相撞后，车厢像手风琴一样折叠在一起。|近义词：compressed, crushed, crumpled, folded|反义词：expanded, extended, unfolded|词性: 形容词

词源:
From concertina + -ed.  The word "concertina" itself comes from "concerto," referring to the instrument's ability to play complex musical pieces.
