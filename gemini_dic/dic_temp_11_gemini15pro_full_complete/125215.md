multiconductor:/ˌmʌltiˈkənˈdʌktər/ | adj. n. | 多导体的，多芯的；多导体电缆 |  Composed of or containing multiple conductors, especially for carrying electrical current. |  This multiconductor cable is used for transmitting various signals simultaneously.  这条多芯电缆用于同时传输各种信号。 | Synonyms: multi-core, multi-wire | Antonyms: single-conductor, single-wire | adjective/noun

multiconductor:/ˌmʌltiˈkənˈdʌktər/ | n. | 多导体电缆 | A cable or wire assembly containing multiple conductors. | We need a 12-conductor multiconductor for this installation. 我们需要一根12芯多导体电缆用于此安装。 | Synonyms: multi-core cable, multi-wire cable | Antonyms: single-conductor cable, single-wire cable | noun
