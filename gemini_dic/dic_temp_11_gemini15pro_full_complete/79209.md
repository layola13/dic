glandiferous:/ɡlænˈdɪfərəs/
adj.
结坚果的；含坚果的
Bearing acorns or nuts; relating to or resembling acorns or nuts.
The glandiferous oak tree provided shade for the weary travelers.  结满橡子的橡树为疲惫的旅行者提供了阴凉。
Squirrels busily gathered the glandiferous bounty of the forest floor. 松鼠忙着收集森林地面上丰富的坚果。
Synonyms: nut-bearing, acorn-bearing
Antonyms: non-glandiferous


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *glandifer*, from *glans* (acorn, nut) + *-fer* (bearing).  源自拉丁语 *glandifer*，由 *glans*（橡子，坚果）+ *-fer*（带有）组成。
