escape:/ɪˈskeɪp/ /ɛˈskeɪp/| n. v.| 逃脱，逃跑；躲避；泄漏；(尤指气体、液体)逸出；|He made his escape through a back window. 他从后窗逃走了。|近义词：flight, getaway, breakout (n.); flee, evade, avoid (v.)|反义词: capture, imprisonment (n.); surrender, submit (v.)|词性:名词；动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French escaper, from Late Latin *excappare "to get out of one's cape," from Latin ex- "out" + cappa "cape." The original sense was "to slip out of one's cloak (as a means of escape)."
