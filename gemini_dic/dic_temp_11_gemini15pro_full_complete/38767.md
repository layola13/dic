condos:/ˈkɑːndoʊz/ (US), /ˈkɒndoʊz/ (UK)| n. |公寓；共管公寓|Short for "condominiums," condos are individually owned units within a larger building or complex. Owners typically share ownership of common areas like hallways, elevators, and recreational facilities.|例句：
She just bought a condo in the city. 她刚在城里买了一套公寓。

They are looking for condos with a view of the ocean. 他们在寻找可以眺望大海的公寓。|近义词：apartments, flats|反义词:house, detached house|Noun
