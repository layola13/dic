klutzy: /ˈklʌtsi/ | adj. | 笨拙的，笨手笨脚的 | Awkward and clumsy; likely to drop or bump into things. |  He's so klutzy, he always spills his drinks. 他太笨手笨脚了，总是把饮料洒出来。| She tripped and fell, which wasn't surprising considering how klutzy she is. 她绊倒了，考虑到她有多笨拙，这并不奇怪。 |  clumsy, awkward, ungainly | graceful, coordinated, deft | adjective
