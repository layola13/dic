antimonarchists:/ˌæntiˈmɒnəkɪsts/
noun
反君主主义者
People who oppose monarchy (the system of having a king or queen).
The antimonarchists protested the royal wedding. 反君主主义者抗议皇室婚礼。
synonyms: republicans, abolitionists
antonyms: monarchists, royalists
