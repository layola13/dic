halfer:/ˈhæfər/ | n. | 减半者，对半分成者；只完成一半路程的人| A person who divides something in half or who only completes half of a course, distance, or project. |例句：He was a halfer, always giving up halfway through a project.  他是一个半途而废的人，总是进行到项目一半就放弃了。

例句：The marathon halfer received a medal for completing half the race. 马拉松半程选手因完成一半赛程而获得奖牌。 |近义词：splitter |反义词: completer |词性: noun
