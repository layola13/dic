fallible:/ˈfæləbl/ (US), /ˈfælɪbl/ (UK)| adj.|易犯错误的，可能出错的|Liable to error; capable of making mistakes.  Derived from Late Latin *fallibilis, from Latin fallere "to deceive, to fail, to err."|例句：Humans are fallible creatures. 人类是容易犯错的生物。

例句：Even the most experienced doctors are fallible. 即使是最有经验的医生也可能犯错。

近义词：errant, flawed, imperfect, prone to error, susceptible to error|反义词:infallible, perfect, unerring|词性:adjective
