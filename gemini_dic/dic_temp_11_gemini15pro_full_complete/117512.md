meeknesses:/ˈmiːknəsɪz/
noun
温顺，谦逊，柔顺
The quality of being meek; submissiveness.
Her meekness made her an easy target for bullies. 她的温顺使她很容易成为欺凌的对象。
近义词:humility, gentleness, mildness, submissiveness
反义词:boldness, arrogance, aggressiveness, assertiveness
词性:n.


meekness:/ˈmiːknəs/
noun
温顺，谦逊，柔顺
The quality of being meek; submissiveness.
He accepted the criticism with meekness. 他温顺地接受了批评。
近义词:humility, gentleness, mildness, submissiveness
反义词:boldness, arrogance, aggressiveness, assertiveness
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *meknesse*, from Old Norse *mekkness*, from *mekkr* ("meek") + *-ness*. Cognate with Swedish *mjukhet*.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *mūkaz* (“soft, pliant”).
