cystocele: /ˈsɪstəˌsiːl/ | n. |膀胱膨出，膀胱脱垂|A cystocele occurs when the wall between a woman's bladder and her vagina weakens and allows the bladder to droop into the vagina. |例句：The doctor diagnosed her with a cystocele. 医生诊断她患有膀胱膨出。

例句：She had surgery to repair the cystocele. 她做了手术来修复膀胱膨出。|近义词：bladder prolapse|反义词:  (没有医学上的反义词，可以考虑用"normal bladder position" 表示正常膀胱位置) |词性: 名词
