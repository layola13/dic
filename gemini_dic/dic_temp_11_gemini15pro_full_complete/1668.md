以下是一个"what"的解析示例，请参考此格式：

what: /wɒt/ (UK), /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. adv.| 什么，多么 |  Used to ask for information specifying something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！| 近义词: which, who | 反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym) | 代词，限定词，副词


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you want? = 你想要什么？
What does she like? = 她喜欢什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing? = 你正在做什么？
What is he reading? = 他正在读什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you done? = 你做了什么？
What has she eaten? = 她吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all day? = 你一整天都在做什么？
What has he been working on? = 他一直在忙什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say? = 她说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when I called? = 我打电话时你在做什么？
What was she thinking about? = 她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before the movie? = 看电影之前她吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? = 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before changing majors? = 换专业之前她一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will she buy at the store? = 她会在商店买什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be wearing to the party? = 她会穿什么去参加派对？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have done by next year? = 到明年你将完成什么？
What will she have learned by graduation? = 到毕业时她将学到什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she finishes her PhD? = 到她完成博士学位时，她将已经学习了什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to say. = 我希望我知道该说什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what he's saying. = 她不明白他在说什么。



PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What about...? = ...怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？
So what? = 那又怎样？
What if...? = 如果...会怎么样？
