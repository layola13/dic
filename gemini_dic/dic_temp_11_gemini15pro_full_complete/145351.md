overbumptiously: /ˌoʊvərˈbʌmpʃəsli/
adjective
过于傲慢地；过分自信地
Excessively and offensively self-assertive.
He overbumptiously declared himself the winner before the votes were even counted.  他过分自信地在计票前就宣布自己是获胜者。
She overbumptiously interrupted the speaker to offer her own unsolicited opinion. 她傲慢地打断演讲者，主动提出自己不请自来的意见。

近义词: arrogantly, presumptuously, conceitedly, pompously
反义词: humbly, modestly, unassumingly
词性: adverb
