disarrangers:/dɪsəˈreɪndʒərz/
noun
扰乱者，混乱制造者
Those who cause disorder or disarrangement.
The disarrangers of the peaceful protest were quickly apprehended.  扰乱和平抗议的那些人很快就被逮捕了。
The disarrangers left the room in a chaotic state. 混乱制造者把房间弄得乱七八糟。
synonyms: disruptors, saboteurs
antonyms: organizers, arrangers
