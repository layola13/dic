out-in-the-cold:/aʊt ɪn ðə koʊld/
adjective
被冷落；被排斥；被忽视
excluded, neglected, or ignored, especially by others in a group.
He felt out in the cold when his friends went to the party without him. 他感到被冷落了，因为他的朋友们没有带他一起去参加聚会。
近义词: neglected, ignored, excluded, ostracized, sidelined
反义词: included, involved, welcomed
