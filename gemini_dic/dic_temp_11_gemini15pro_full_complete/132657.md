nondisingenuous:/ˌnɒndɪsɪnˈdʒɛnjuəs/
adjective
真诚的，坦率的
Not disingenuous; genuine, sincere, and honest.
Her nondisingenuous apology felt truly heartfelt. 她真诚的道歉让人感觉发自肺腑。
The politician gave a surprisingly nondisingenuous answer.  这位政治家给出了一个令人惊讶的坦率的答案。

近义词:sincere, honest, genuine, candid, frank, straightforward
反义词:disingenuous, insincere, dishonest, deceitful, duplicitous
词性:adjective

词源:
The prefix "non-" negates the adjective "disingenuous." "Disingenuous" comes from the Latin "disingenuus," meaning "insincere," formed from "dis-" (not) + "ingenuus" (frank, noble, freeborn).
