extra-parochial:/ˌekstrəpəˈroʊkiəl/
adjective
域外的；教区外的
Outside of any particular parish.  Relating to places or people not formally belonging to any specific parish.
The extra-parochial area was not subject to the same laws as the rest of the town. 这片域外地区不受该镇其他地区相同法律的约束。
The extra-parochial community maintained its own unique customs. 这个教区外的社区保持着自己独特的风俗。
近义词:non-parochial, extraterritorial
反义词:parochial
词性:adjective
