four-square:/ˌfɔːrˈskwɛər/
adj.|正直的，坦率的；坚固的，稳定的|Characterized by honesty and integrity; firm and steady.|He took a four-square stance against the corruption. 他对腐败采取了正直的立场。|近义词:honest, upright, forthright, stable, solid|反义词:dishonest, deceitful, unstable, shaky|词性:形容词

four-square:/ˌfɔːrˈskwɛər/
adv.|正直地，坦率地；坚固地，稳定地|In a straightforward and honest manner; firmly and steadily.|He faced the challenges four-square. 他坦然面对挑战。|近义词:honestly, uprightly, forthrightly, firmly, solidly|反义词:dishonestly, deceitfully, unstably, shakily|词性:副词

four-square:/ˈfɔːrˌskwɛər/
n.|一种儿童游戏（场地划成四个方格）|A children's game played on a court divided into four squares.|The children were playing four-square in the playground. 孩子们在操场上玩四格游戏。|近义词:hopscotch (similar game)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:名词


USAGE->

词源:  From "four" + "square," literally meaning having four sides and four right angles. The figurative meaning of "honest" and "straightforward" developed from the idea of stability and solidity.
