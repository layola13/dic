cocreation:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪʃən/| n.|共同创造，共同创作|The process of jointly creating something, typically involving collaboration between a company and its customers or users.|例句：The software was developed through a process of cocreation with users.  这款软件是通过与用户共同创造的过程开发出来的。

例句：Cocreation allows for a more diverse range of perspectives and ideas. 共同创造允许多样化的视角和想法。|近义词：collaboration, joint creation, partnership, teamwork|反义词：independent creation, solo work|词性:noun

co-create:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪt/| v.|共同创造，共同创作|To create something jointly, often involving collaboration between a company and its customers or users.|例句：We co-created this marketing campaign with our target audience. 我们与目标受众共同创建了这个营销活动。

例句：The artist co-created the installation with members of the community.  这位艺术家与社区成员共同创作了这个装置艺术。|近义词：collaborate, jointly create, partner, team up|反义词：create independently, work solo|词性:verb
