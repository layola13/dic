lockout: /ˈlɒkaʊt/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkaʊt/ (US)| n.|停工，闭厂；封锁； lockout |The company imposed a lockout after the union refused to accept the proposed pay cuts. 公司在工会拒绝接受提议的减薪后宣布停工。|The landlord carried out an illegal lockout of his tenants. 房东对他的租户进行了非法封锁。|近义词：shutdown, closure, strike|反义词：opening, admittance|词性: 名词

词源:  From lock + out.  The noun sense of "exclusion by locking out" is from the late 18th century.
