juggement:/ˈdʒʌdʒmənt/| n.|判断，判断力，判决，意见，看法|An act of judging; the cognitive process of assessing or evaluating something or someone.  The ability to make considered decisions or come to sensible conclusions.|He showed poor judgement in his choice of friends. 他在选择朋友方面表现出很差的判断力。|synonyms: discernment, perception, acumen, insight, wisdom, discrimination |antonyms: indecision, foolishness, imprudence|noun


juggment:/ˈdʒʌdʒmənt/| n.|（法律）判决，裁决|A decision of a court regarding the rights and liabilities of parties in a legal case or proceeding.|The judgement was in favor of the plaintiff. 判决有利于原告。|synonyms: verdict, ruling, decree, sentence, adjudication |antonyms: acquittal, pardon|noun
