hyperbarism:/ˈhaɪpərˌbærɪzəm/| n. |高压症，高压病|A medical condition resulting from exposure to increased environmental pressure, typically greater than atmospheric pressure, leading to excessive dissolved gases in the body tissues.|Divers can suffer from hyperbarism if they ascend too quickly. 潜水员如果上升过快，可能会患上高压症。|近义词:decompression sickness, barotrauma|反义词:hypobarism|noun
