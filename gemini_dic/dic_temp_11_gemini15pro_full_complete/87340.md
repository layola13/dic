herodotus:/hɪˈrɒdətəs/| n.|希罗多德|A Greek historian, considered the "Father of History". He wrote "The Histories", a detailed account of the Greco-Persian Wars.|Herodotus's account of the Battle of Thermopylae is both gripping and informative. 希罗多德对温泉关战役的描述既扣人心弦又信息丰富。|Synonyms: chronicler, annalist|Antonyms: N/A|noun
