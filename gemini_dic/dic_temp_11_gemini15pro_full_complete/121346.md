misgovernance: /ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnəns/
misgovernance| n.|管理不善；治理不当|The misgovernance of the company led to its bankruptcy. 该公司的管理不善导致其破产。|例句：The report highlighted the misgovernance and corruption within the organization.  报告强调了该组织内部的管理不善和腐败。|近义词：mismanagement, maladministration|反义词：good governance, sound management|词性:名词


词源:  "misgovernance" comes from the prefix "mis-" (meaning "badly" or "wrongly") and the verb "govern" (meaning "to rule or control").  Therefore, it literally means "bad governance."
