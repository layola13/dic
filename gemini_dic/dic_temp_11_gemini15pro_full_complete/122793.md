mold:/moʊld/ (US) /məʊld/ (UK)| n. v.|模子，霉菌；塑造，浇铸|A hollow container used to give shape to molten or hot liquid material when it cools and hardens; a growth of minute fungi forming on vegetable or animal matter.|The jelly was set in a decorative mold. 果冻被放在一个装饰性的模具里凝固。|  He molded the clay into a beautiful sculpture. 他把黏土塑造成一个美丽的雕塑。|近义词:cast, form, shape, matrix (n.); shape, form, fashion (v.)|反义词: (n.) none; (v.) deform, distort|词性:名词,动词
