fumes:/fjuːmz/ | n. | 烟雾，气体，一阵强烈的感情 | Strong, unpleasant, and sometimes dangerous gas or smoke.  Also, a fit of anger, excitement, etc. | The fumes from the car exhaust choked me. 汽车排出的废气呛得我难受。| synonyms: smoke, gas, vapor, exhaust, reek, stench | antonyms: fragrance, aroma, perfume | noun

fume:/fjuːm/ | v. | 冒烟，发怒 | To produce or give off fumes; to be in a state of anger or excitement. | The volcano fumed and spewed ash into the sky. 火山喷出烟雾和火山灰。 He was fuming about the unfair treatment. 他对不公平的待遇感到愤怒。| synonyms: smolder, seethe, rage, rant | antonyms: calm, soothe, pacify | verb
