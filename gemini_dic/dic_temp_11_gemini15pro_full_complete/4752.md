alfenide: /ˈælfəˌnaɪd/
noun
阿尔芬尼德
A topical antibacterial agent, silver sulfadiazine, used to prevent infection in burns.
Silver sulfadiazine cream is commonly used under the trade name Silvadene. Alfenide is the generic name.
Alfenide cream is applied to the burn wound once or twice daily.
synonym: silver sulfadiazine, Silvadene
antonym: None (as it's a specific medication, no direct opposite exists)
n.
