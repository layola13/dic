mesogastric:/ˌmezəˈɡæstrɪk/
adjective
中胃的
Relating to or situated in the middle region of the abdomen.  Specifically, in embryology, pertaining to the portion of the embryonic gut that develops into the stomach, duodenum, liver, pancreas, and spleen.
The mesogastric region gives rise to several important organs.  中胃区域会发育出几个重要的器官。
The surgeon identified the mesogastric vessels during the procedure.  外科医生在手术过程中识别出了中胃血管。
近义词:mid-abdominal, midgut (embryological context)
反义词:None
词性:adjective
