nonspeculation:/ˌnɒnˌspekjʊˈleɪʃn/
n.|非投机|The absence of speculation; avoidance of risky financial transactions.|The fund's investment strategy is based on nonspeculation and long-term growth. 该基金的投资策略基于非投机和长期增长。|  
近义词: caution, prudence, conservatism
反义词: speculation, risk-taking, gambling
词性: noun

nonspeculative:/ˌnɒnˈspekjʊlətɪv/
adj.|非投机的|Not involving or characterized by speculation.|They adopted a nonspeculative approach to investing. 他们采取了非投机的投资方式。|
近义词: cautious, prudent, conservative
反义词: speculative, risky, adventurous
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
speculation (noun): From Latin speculatio "contemplation, observation," from speculatus, past participle of speculari "to look at, observe," from specula "watchtower, lookout," from specere "to look at."
