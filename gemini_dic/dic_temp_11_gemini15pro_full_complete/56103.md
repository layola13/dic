doctorism: /ˈdɒktərɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈdɑːktərɪzəm/ (US)
|n.|医家作风；医生式的言谈；医生体|The use of jargon or a manner of speaking characteristic of doctors; an instance of this.|He spoke with a certain doctorism, using terms most people wouldn't understand. 他说话带着一种医生式的言谈，使用大多数人都不懂的术语。|近义词：jargon, medical terminology|反义词: plain language|词性:名词

词源:
From doctor + -ism.  Meaning "characteristic style or practice of a doctor" is from 1871.
