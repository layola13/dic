overexercised: /ˌoʊvərˈɛksərˌsaɪzd/
adjective
过度锻炼的
Having exercised too much.  Strained or injured through excessive exercise.
The overexercised athlete collapsed on the track.  过度锻炼的运动员在跑道上晕倒了。
Near synonyms: overworked, overtrained, exhausted
Near antonyms: rested, relaxed
词性: adjective


overexercised: /ˌoʊvərˈɛksərˌsaɪzd/
verb (past participle)
被过度锻炼的
Having been subjected to excessive exercise.  (Used in a passive construction.)
The horse's legs were overexercised and sore.  马的腿因过度运动而疼痛。
Near synonyms: overworked, overtrained, exhausted
