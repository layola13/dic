dilatoriness:/ˌdɪləˈtɔːrɪnɪs/
noun
缓慢；拖延；迟缓
The state of being slow or late in doing something; slowness.
His dilatoriness in responding to the email frustrated his colleagues.  他回复邮件的拖延让他的同事们感到沮丧。
The project was hampered by the dilatoriness of the local authorities.  该项目因地方当局的拖延而受阻。
Synonyms: slowness, tardiness, procrastination, delay, sluggishness
Antonyms: promptness, speed, alacrity, swiftness
