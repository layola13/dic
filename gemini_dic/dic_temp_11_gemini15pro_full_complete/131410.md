nonclassified:/ˌnɒnˈklæsɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 非机密的，未分类的 | Not formally assigned to a security classification. |  The information was nonclassified and available to the public.  该信息是非机密的，公众可以获取。| 近义词: unclassified, public, open | 反义词: classified, confidential, secret | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "classified" (past participle of "classify," from Latin "classis" meaning "class" + "-ficare" meaning "to make").
