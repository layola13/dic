diaeresis:/daɪˈɛrɪsɪs/ /daɪˈɪərɪsɪs/| n. |分音符|A mark ( ¨ ) placed over a vowel, especially a second vowel in a pair, to indicate that it is pronounced in a separate syllable, as in naïve, Brontë.  It can also be called a trema.|例句：The diaeresis in the word "naïve" indicates that the 'i' is pronounced separately from the 'a'.  分音符在单词 "naïve" 中表示 'i' 与 'a' 分开发音。

例句：The word "Brontë" uses a diaeresis to show the pronunciation of the final 'e'.  单词 "Brontë" 使用分音符来表示最后一个 'e' 的发音。|近义词：trema|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
