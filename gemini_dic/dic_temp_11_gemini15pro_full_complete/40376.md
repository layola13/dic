contradiction:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪkʃn/ | n. |矛盾，反驳，否认|A combination of statements, ideas, or features of a situation that are opposed to one another.|She is full of contradictions. 她充满了矛盾.|Synonyms: conflict, inconsistency, discrepancy, paradox, incongruity |Antonyms: agreement, harmony, consistency, conformity| noun

contradict:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪkt/ | v. |反驳，与…矛盾|Deny the truth of (a statement), especially by asserting the opposite.|The survey appears to contradict the industry's claims.  这项调查似乎与该行业的说法相矛盾。|Synonyms: deny, dispute, challenge, oppose, refute|Antonyms: confirm, support, corroborate, verify| verb
