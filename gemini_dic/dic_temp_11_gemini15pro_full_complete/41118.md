coproductions:/ˌkoʊprəˈdʌkʃənz/| n. | 联合制作；合作作品；合拍片 | A joint production, especially of a film or broadcast, by two or more production companies. | Example 1: The film was a coproduction between France and Italy. 这部电影是法国和意大利的合拍片。

Example 2: This documentary is a coproduction of several international broadcasters. 这部纪录片是几家国际广播公司的联合制作。 | Near Synonyms: joint ventures, collaborations, partnerships | Antonyms: solo productions, independent productions | Noun
