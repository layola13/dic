digressed:/daɪˈɡrɛst/ | v. | 离题，跑题 | to deviate or wander away from the main topic or purpose in speaking or writing |  The speaker digressed from his main point several times during the lecture. 演讲者在讲座中几次偏离主题。 | synonyms: deviated, strayed, rambled, wandered | antonyms: focused, concentrated, stayed on topic | verb

digression:/daɪˈɡrɛʃən/ | n. |  离题，跑题 | a temporary departure from the main subject in speech or writing | His frequent digressions made it difficult to follow his argument. 他频繁的跑题使得很难理解他的论点。 | synonyms: deviation, detour, aside | antonyms: focus, concentration | noun
