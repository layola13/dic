heightened:/ˈhaɪtnd/| adj.|加剧的，增强的，提高的|Increased in intensity, degree, or amount.  Derived from the verb "heighten," meaning to make higher or increase.|例句：The heightened security measures made it difficult to enter the building.  加强的安保措施使得进入大楼变得困难。|近义词：increased, intensified, elevated, amplified|反义词：reduced, diminished, lowered, decreased|词性:形容词

heighten:/ˈhaɪtn/| v.|提高，增加，加强|To make higher or increase.|例句：The music heightened the tension in the film.  音乐增加了电影的紧张感。|近义词：increase, intensify, elevate, amplify|反义词：reduce, diminish, lower, decrease|词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle English *heightnen*, from Old English *hēahþian* (“to heighten, exalt”), from *hēah* (“high”).
