clocker:/ˈklɒkər/ (UK) /ˈklɑːkər/ (US)| n.|计时员，记录员；（非正式）观察者，监视者|A person who times races or other events, especially in horse racing.  Informally, it can refer to someone who observes or monitors something closely.|例句：The clocker recorded a new track record. 计时员记录了一个新的赛道纪录。

例句：He's a keen clocker of the political scene. 他是政治舞台的敏锐观察者。|近义词：timer, observer, watcher|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "participant" in some contexts)|noun
