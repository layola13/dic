chain-smoke:/ˈtʃeɪn sməʊk/ (UK) /ˈtʃeɪn smoʊk/ (US)| v. | 连抽烟，一根接一根地抽烟 | To smoke cigarettes continuously, one after another, with little or no interval between them. | He was so nervous he chain-smoked the whole night. 他太紧张了，整晚都在不停地抽烟。| chain-smoke all day long.他整天都在不停地抽烟 | synonyms: smoke heavily, smoke continuously | antonyms: quit smoking, abstain from smoking | verb

chain-smoke:/ˈtʃeɪn sməʊk/ (UK) /ˈtʃeɪn smoʊk/ (US)| n. | 连抽烟，一根接一根抽烟的行为| The act of smoking cigarettes continuously. | The constant chain-smoke in the room made it hard to breathe. 房间里不断有人抽烟，让人难以呼吸。 |She engaged in a chain-smoke after hearing the bad news. 她听到坏消息后一根接一根地抽烟。| synonyms: heavy smoking, continuous smoking | antonyms: smoking cessation, abstinence from smoking | noun
