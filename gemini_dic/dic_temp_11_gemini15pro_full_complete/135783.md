nonprogressively:/ˌnɑːnprəˈɡrɛsɪvli/| adv.|非进行式地，非渐进地|Not in a progressive manner; not continuing or increasing.|He spoke nonprogressively, stating facts without elaboration. 他讲话没有展开，只是简单地陈述了事实。|The disease unfortunately progressed nonprogressively, remaining at a constant, albeit serious, stage. 不幸的是，这种疾病没有逐步发展，保持在一个恒定的、尽管很严重的阶段。|近义词：statically, constantly, unchangingly|反义词: progressively, gradually, increasingly|词性:副词
