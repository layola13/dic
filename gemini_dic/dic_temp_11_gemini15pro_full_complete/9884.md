apartness:/əˈpɑːtnəs/
n.
隔离，分离；冷漠
The state of being separate or at a distance from others.  A feeling of emotional distance or detachment.
例句：The couple's apartness was noticeable to everyone at the party.  这对夫妇在聚会上的疏离感每个人都注意到了。

例句：The physical apartness of the two communities contributed to their cultural differences.  这两个社区的物理隔离导致了他们的文化差异。
近义词：separation, detachment, isolation, aloofness, distance
反义词：togetherness, closeness, intimacy, connection
词性: 名词

apartness:/əˈpɑːtnəs/
n.
(尤指因种族、宗教等原因的)隔离，分离
The state of being separated, especially as a result of racial, religious, or other differences.
例句：Apartheid was a system of enforced racial apartness. 种族隔离是一种强制种族隔离的制度。

例句：The community lived in forced apartness from the rest of society.  这个社区被迫与社会其他部分隔离。
近义词：segregation, discrimination, prejudice
反义词：integration, inclusion, equality
词性: 名词
