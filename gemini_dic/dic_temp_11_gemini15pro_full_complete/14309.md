automate-out:/ˈɔːtəˌmeɪt aʊt/| v.|自动化输出; 自动化导出; 自动化淘汰;|(computing) To cause (a process or system) to produce output automatically.  To remove something through an automated process. To remove someone from a group or system through an automated process (usually based on some performance criteria).|例句：The system automates-out reports every Friday. 系统每周五自动输出报告。

例句：The factory automates-out defective products.  工厂自动淘汰有缺陷的产品。

例句：The algorithm automates-out low-performing employees after three warnings. 该算法在三次警告后自动淘汰绩效低的员工。|近义词：automate, export, output, eliminate, remove|反义词：input, import, manual|词性: verb
