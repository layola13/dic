availing:/əˈveɪlɪŋ/
v.
利用，有益于
making use of or benefiting from something
Many families are availing themselves of the opportunity to buy their council houses. 许多家庭正在利用这次机会购买他们的政府公屋。
近义词: utilizing, using, benefiting from
反义词: neglecting, ignoring, hindering
词性: Verb present participle

availing:/əˈveɪlɪŋ/
adj.
有效的，有用的
effective or useful
His efforts, though heroic, were not availing. 他的努力虽然英勇，但没有奏效。
近义词: effective, useful, helpful, beneficial
反义词: ineffective, useless, futile
词性: Adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *availen, from Old French *availer, from Vulgar Latin *advalere, from Latin ad + valēre (“to be strong, be worth”).
