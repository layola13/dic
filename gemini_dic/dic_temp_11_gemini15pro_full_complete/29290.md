cast-up:/ˈkɑːst ʌp/ | n. | 计算结果；呕吐物；(筑堤等的)抛掷土石 |  The result of adding numbers together; vomit; material thrown up (as in building an embankment). | The cast-up of the week's expenses showed they had overspent.  这周的开支计算结果显示他们超支了。 | The bird left a cast-up of undigested seeds on the windowsill. 鸟在窗台上留下了一些未消化的种子的呕吐物。 |  近义词：sum, total, vomit, ejecta | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词


cast-up:/ˈkɑːst ʌp/ | adj. | (古语) 计算出来的 | Calculated. |  He presented a cast-up account of the profits. 他提交了一份计算好的利润账目。 |  This is a cast-up figure, not an estimate. 这是一个计算出来的数字，而不是估计值。 |近义词：calculated, computed | 反义词: estimated | 词性: 形容词
