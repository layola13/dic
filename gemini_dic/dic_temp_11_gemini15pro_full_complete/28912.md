carriages:/ˈkærɪdʒɪz/
n.
四轮马车；（火车）客车厢；运输；运费；姿态；举止
a vehicle with four wheels, usually pulled by horses, used for carrying people
The queen rode in a magnificent carriage. 女王乘坐一辆华丽的马车。
a separate section of a train for carrying passengers
We travelled in a first-class carriage. 我们乘坐的是一等车厢。
the transporting of goods or people
The carriage of goods by road is expensive.  公路运输货物很贵。
the cost of transporting goods
The price includes packing and carriage. 价格包含包装和运费。
the way in which a person holds and moves their body; posture
She has a very elegant carriage. 她的姿态非常优雅。

近义词:coach, wagon, cart, vehicle, conveyance, transport, posture, bearing, demeanor
反义词: (For vehicle meaning) None. Posture meanings have various antonyms depending on context.

词性:noun
