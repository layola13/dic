miso:/ˈmiːsoʊ/ (US), /ˈmiːsəʊ/ (UK)| n.|味噌|A thick paste made from fermented soybeans, typically used in Japanese cooking.|例句：I love adding miso to my soup. 我喜欢在我的汤里加味噌。

例句：Miso ramen is a popular dish. 味噌拉面是一道很受欢迎的菜肴。

近义词：soybean paste, fermented soybean paste|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
