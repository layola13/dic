frag:/fræɡ/| n. |碎片，破片；片段|A small piece or part broken off or detached from something.|The explosion sent frags of glass and metal everywhere. 爆炸使得玻璃和金属碎片四处飞溅。|近义词：piece, shard, fragment, bit|反义词:whole|词性:名词

frag:/fræɡ/| v. |(游戏中)用破片手榴弹攻击；杀死|To kill or attack someone with a fragmentation grenade (in gaming).|He fragged his teammate accidentally. 他不小心炸死了他的队友。|近义词：kill, eliminate|反义词:revive, heal|词性:动词
