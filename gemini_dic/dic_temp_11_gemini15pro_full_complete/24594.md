brogle:/ˈbroʊɡəl/| n.|无意义词，胡编乱造的词|A non-existent word, a made-up word, often used as a placeholder or in a playful, nonsensical context.  It carries no actual meaning.|例句：He started brogling about brogles, and I had no idea what he was talking about. 他开始胡诌一些关于“布罗格尔”的事情，我完全不知道他在说什么。

例句：The children were brogling a silly song, full of made-up words. 孩子们在哼唱一首愚蠢的歌，里面充满了自编的词。|近义词：gibberish, nonsense, balderdash|反义词: (N/A - as it's a nonsense word, it doesn't have a true opposite)|词性:noun
