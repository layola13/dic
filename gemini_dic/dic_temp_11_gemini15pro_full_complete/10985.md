arbitress: /ˈɑːbɪtrɪs/ | n. |女仲裁人，女裁判 |A female arbitrator; a woman who settles a dispute or has ultimate authority in a matter. |例句：The arbitress listened carefully to both sides of the argument before making her decision. 女仲裁人仔细听取了双方的论点，然后才做出决定。

例句：She acted as arbitress in the dispute between the two companies.  她在两家公司之间的纠纷中担任仲裁人。|近义词：judge, mediator, umpire, referee |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


Etymology:
From Late Middle English *arbitresse, from Old French arbitresse, feminine of arbitre (“arbiter”), from Latin arbiter.
