assimilated: /əˈsɪməleɪtɪd/ | v. | 同化，吸收 |  To absorb and integrate (people, ideas, or culture) into a wider society or culture.  |  She assimilated the information quickly and was ready for the test. 她很快吸收了这些信息，准备好了考试。 | integrate, absorb, incorporate | segregate, isolate | verb
