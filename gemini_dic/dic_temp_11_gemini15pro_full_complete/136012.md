nonreactionaries:/ˌnɒnriˈækʃənɛriz/ | n. |非反动分子，不反对变革的人|People who are not opposed to change or progress and do not actively resist new ideas or political movements. |  The nonreactionaries in the group were open to the proposed reforms.  这个团体中不反对变革的人对提议的改革持开放态度。| Synonyms: progressives, liberals, reformers | Antonyms: reactionaries, conservatives | Noun

nonreactionary:/ˌnɒnriˈækʃənɛri/ | adj. |非反动的，不反对变革的|Not reactionary; not opposed to change or progress. | The government took a nonreactionary approach to the economic crisis. 政府对经济危机采取了非反动的方式。| Synonyms: progressive, liberal, reformist | Antonyms: reactionary, conservative | Adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
non- + reactionary
non-:  prefix meaning "not" (from Latin non "not")
reactionary: from reaction + -ary.  "Reaction" ultimately derives from Latin reagere "to act back, act in return," from re- "back" + agere "to do, drive, lead, act."
