bypassing:/ˌbaɪˈpɑːsɪŋ/ /ˌbaɪˈpæːsɪŋ/
verb
绕过，避开
going around or avoiding something
The new road bypasses the town. 新建的道路绕过了城镇。

近义词: circumventing, avoiding, dodging, skirting
反义词: confronting, facing, meeting
词性: verb (动名词)

bypassing:/ˌbaɪˈpɑːsɪŋ/ /ˌbaɪˈpæːsɪŋ/
noun
绕过，旁路
an act of bypassing something
The construction of the bypass caused some disruption. 旁路的建设造成了一些干扰。

近义词: detour, alternative route, shortcut
反义词: direct route, main route
词性: noun
