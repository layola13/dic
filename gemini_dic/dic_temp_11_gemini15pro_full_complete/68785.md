推进，促进

近义词: advancement, promotion, furtherance

词性:n.


Etymology:
From Middle English *furtheraunce*,  from Old French *fortheraunce* "support", from *furtherer* "to support," from *forth* "forward".  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *furþiz*  "forward".
