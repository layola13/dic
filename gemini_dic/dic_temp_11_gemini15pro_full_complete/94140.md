impediment:/ɪmˈpedɪmənt/| n. |障碍，妨碍，阻碍物|Something that makes progress or movement difficult or impossible.|The heavy traffic was an impediment to our journey.  交通拥堵是我们旅程的障碍。|Synonyms: obstacle, hindrance, obstruction, barrier|Antonyms: aid, help, assistance|noun

impediment:/ɪmˈpedɪmənt/| n. |(言语)障碍，缺陷|A physical defect that prevents or restricts normal speech.|His stammer was a serious impediment to his career as a lawyer. 他的口吃是他律师生涯的严重障碍。|Synonyms: defect, impairment, disability|Antonyms: advantage, asset|noun
