inflect:/ɪnˈflɛkt/ (US), /ɪnˈflekt/ (UK)| v. | 使(词)变形; 弯曲; 改变(声音)的音调 | To change the form of a word to express a grammatical function such as tense, mood, person, or number. To bend or curve something. To modulate or vary the tone or pitch of the voice. |  She inflected her voice to express sarcasm. 她改变了声调来表达讽刺。 The road inflects sharply to the left. 这条路向左急转弯。 Latin verbs inflect for tense, person, and number. 拉丁语动词因时态、人称和数而变形。 | 近义词: change, modify, vary, bend, curve | 反义词: straighten, maintain, fix | 词性: 动词
