maidling:/ˈmeɪdlɪŋ/| n.|少女，年轻姑娘|A young unmarried woman; a maiden.|例句：The fair maidling danced in the meadow.  美丽的少女在草地上跳舞。|例句：The maidling blushed at the compliment. 少女因赞美而脸红。|近义词：maiden, girl, lass, damsel|反义词: matron, crone|词性:noun
Etymology: From Middle English *maydenlyng, equivalent to maiden + -ling. Cognate with German *Mädlein.
