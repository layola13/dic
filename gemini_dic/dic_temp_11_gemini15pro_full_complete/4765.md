alga:/ˈælɡə/ (US), /ˈælgə/ (UK)| n. |藻类，海藻|A simple, non-flowering plant of a large group that lives in water or on damp surfaces.|Example: The pond was covered in green algae. 池塘里覆盖着绿藻。|Example:  Many types of algae are edible. 许多种类的藻类是可以食用的。|Synonym: seaweed, kelp|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a biological organism)|Noun
