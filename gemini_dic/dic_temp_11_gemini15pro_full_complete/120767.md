misappoints:/ˌmɪsəˈpɔɪnts/| v. |使失望|To fail to fulfill the hopes or expectations of (someone).|The movie misappointed me; it wasn't nearly as good as the reviews said.  这部电影让我很失望；它远不如评论说的那么好。|The team misappointed their fans by losing the final game.  这支球队输掉了决赛，让球迷们失望。|近义词：disappoints, frustrates, lets down|反义词：pleases, delights, satisfies|词性:动词
