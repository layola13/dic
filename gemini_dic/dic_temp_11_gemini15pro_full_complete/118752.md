metalinguistic:/ˌmetəˈlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk/
adjective
元语言的
Relating to the study of language and its relationship to other cultural behaviors.  Focusing on the properties and features of language itself, rather than on the meaning of specific words or sentences.
例句：The child's metalinguistic awareness allowed him to understand the concept of a palindrome. 这个孩子具有元语言意识，这使他能够理解回文的概念。

例句：Metalinguistic skills are crucial for learning a foreign language. 元语言技能对于学习外语至关重要。
近义词：linguistic, meta-communicative
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: adjective
