claustral:/ˈklɔːstrəl/| adj.|修道院的；隐居的；幽闭的|Relating to a cloister or monastery; secluded; shut off or confined.|The monks lived a claustral life, spending their days in prayer and contemplation.  僧侣们过着修道院般的生活，每天都在祈祷和沉思中度过。|The room had a claustral feel, small and dark with only one tiny window.  房间有一种幽闭的感觉，又小又暗，只有一扇小窗户。|近义词：monastic, secluded, confined, reclusive|反义词：open, public, outgoing|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *claustrālis*, from Latin *claustrum* ("cloister, enclosed space").
