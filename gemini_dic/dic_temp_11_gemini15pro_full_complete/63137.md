ensnaring:/ɪnˈsneərɪŋ/
adjective
诱捕的；使入圈套的
catching and holding something, or attracting someone into a situation from which they cannot easily escape
例句：The hunter set an ensnaring trap for the rabbit. 猎人为兔子设下了一个陷阱。

例句：Her beauty was ensnaring, and he found himself captivated by her charm. 她的美貌令人着迷，他发现自己被她的魅力所吸引。

近义词：trapping, captivating, alluring, enticing
反义词：releasing, freeing, liberating
词性：adjective
