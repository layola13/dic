antiicteric:/ˌænˌtɪˈɪktərɪk/
adjective
抗黄疸的，治疗黄疸的
Reducing or preventing jaundice.  Used to describe medications or treatments that combat jaundice.
The doctor prescribed an antiicteric medication for the newborn.  医生给新生儿开了抗黄疸的药。

synonym:  antijaundice
antonym:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider pro-jaundice, though this term is rarely used)
词性: adjective
