nemertina:/nɛmərˈtiːnə/
n.
纽形动物门; 纽形动物
A phylum of invertebrate animals, also called ribbon worms or proboscis worms, characterized by a long, slender body and a proboscis that is used for capturing prey.
Nemertines are fascinating creatures found in marine environments.  纽形动物是在海洋环境中发现的迷人生物。
The nemertine extended its proboscis to capture a small crustacean.  这条纽形动物伸出它的吻管捕捉一只小型甲壳类动物。
近义词:ribbon worm, proboscis worm
反义词: (无明显的反义词，可以考虑其他动物门类，例如：Annelida环节动物门)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Nemertes (genus name), from Greek Νημερτής (Nēmertēs), a sea nymph.
