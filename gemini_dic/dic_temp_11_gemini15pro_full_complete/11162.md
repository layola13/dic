archetype:/ˈɑːrkɪtaɪp/| n.|原型，典型|An original model or pattern from which other later copies are made, especially a character, an action, or situation that seems to represent common patterns of human life.|例句：The hero is a classic archetype in literature.  英雄是文学中的经典原型。

例句：The wicked stepmother is a common archetype in fairy tales. 恶毒的继母是童话故事中的常见原型。|近义词：prototype, original, model, exemplar|反义词：copy, imitation|词性: noun
