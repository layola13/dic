antiagglutinative:/ˌæntiəˈɡluːtɪnətɪv/ | adj. | 抗凝集的，防止凝集的 | Preventing or counteracting agglutination (the clumping together of cells, such as bacteria or red blood cells). |  This antiagglutinative agent prevents blood clots.  这种抗凝集剂可以防止血液凝块。 | synonyms: anticoagulant, anticlumping | antonyms: agglutinative, coagulant | adjective


antiagglutinative:/ˌæntiəˈɡluːtɪnətɪv/ | n. | 抗凝集剂 | A substance that prevents agglutination. | The doctor prescribed an antiagglutinative to prevent complications after the surgery. 医生开了抗凝集剂以防止术后并发症。 | synonyms: anticoagulant, anticlumping agent | antonyms: agglutinant, coagulant | noun


Etymology:  "anti-" (against) + "agglutinative" (from Latin "agglutinare" - to glue together).
