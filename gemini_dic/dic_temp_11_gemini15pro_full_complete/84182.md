half-quixotic:/ˌhæf kwɪkˈsɒtɪk/ | adj. | 半堂吉诃德式的；半浪漫主义的；有点不切实际的 |  Partly or somewhat resembling Don Quixote, the idealistic and impractical hero of Cervantes' novel; characterized by a mixture of romantic idealism and impracticality. | He had a half-quixotic notion of rescuing the damsel in distress, even though he knew she was perfectly capable of handling the situation herself. 他有一种半堂吉诃德式的想法，想要拯救那位遇险的少女，即使他知道她完全有能力自己处理这种情况。 |  idealistic, romantic, impractical, fanciful, chivalrous | pragmatic, realistic, practical, down-to-earth, cynical | adjective
