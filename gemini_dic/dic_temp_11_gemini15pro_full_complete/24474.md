britons:/ˈbrɪtnz/
noun
英国人
People who live in or are from Great Britain.
Britons voted to leave the European Union in 2016.  英国人在2016年投票决定离开欧盟。
synonyms:British people, UK citizens
antonyms:foreigners, non-Britons
