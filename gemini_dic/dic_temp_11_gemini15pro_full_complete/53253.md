direly:/ˈdaɪərli/
adverb
极其地，非常地
in a very serious or bad way that causes great worry or fear
The hospital is direly in need of nurses. 这家医院急需护士。
Synonyms: desperately, urgently, acutely, severely, critically
Antonyms: mildly, slightly, insignificantly
词性: 副词
