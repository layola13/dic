nondiplomacy:/ˌnɒndɪˈpləʊməsi/ | n. | 非外交；非外交手段 | Absence or lack of diplomacy; actions or approaches that are not diplomatic. | The president's nondiplomacy caused an international incident. 总统的非外交行为引发了一起国际事件。| The general's blunt statement was an act of pure nondiplomacy. 将军的直率言论纯粹是一种非外交行为。 |  Near synonyms:  intransigence, aggressiveness, belligerence | Antonyms: diplomacy, tact,  negotiation | noun
