intermundane: /ˌɪntərˈmʌndeɪn/
adjective
现世的; 位于世界之间的
Existing or occurring between worlds; relating to the affairs of this world.
The book explored the complex intermundane relationships between the different realms.  这本书探讨了不同领域之间复杂的现世关系。
The intermundane politics of the region were fraught with tension.  该地区的现世政治充满了紧张局势。
近义词: earthly, worldly, temporal, mundane
反义词: otherworldly, spiritual, supernatural, celestial
词性: adjective
