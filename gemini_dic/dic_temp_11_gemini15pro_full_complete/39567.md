consciously:/ˈkɒnʃəsli/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnʃəsli/ (US)| adv.|有意识地，故意地|In a deliberate and intentional manner, aware of one's actions and their potential effects.|She consciously chose to ignore his rude remarks. 她有意识地选择忽略他粗鲁的言论。|Synonyms: deliberately, intentionally, purposely, knowingly|Antonyms: unconsciously, unintentionally, unknowingly|adverb
