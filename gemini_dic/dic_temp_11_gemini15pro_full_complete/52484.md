dietician:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/ (US), /ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃn/ (UK)| n.|营养师，饮食学家|A health professional who is trained to give people advice about what they eat and drink.|例句：I consulted a dietician to help me create a healthy eating plan. 我咨询了一位营养师，帮我制定一个健康的饮食计划。

例句：The dietician recommended eating more fruits and vegetables. 营养师建议多吃水果和蔬菜。

近义词：nutritionist, dietitian|反义词: (无明确的反义词)|词性: noun
