jimmying:/ˈdʒɪmɪɪŋ/
verb
撬开，撬动
forcing open something, especially a lock, with a jimmy
He jimmied the lock open with a screwdriver. 他用螺丝刀撬开了锁。
近义词:forcing, prying, levering
反义词:locking, securing
词性:v.

jimmy:/ˈdʒɪmi/
noun
撬棍
a short crowbar
He used a jimmy to break into the car. 他用撬棍撬开了汽车。
近义词:crowbar, lever
反义词:N/A
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
The origin of "jimmy" is uncertain, but it likely comes from the name "James," perhaps a common name among burglars or locksmiths in the past.  The verb "to jimmy" arose from the noun, referring to the act of using a jimmy (crowbar).
