jingliest:/ˈdʒɪŋɡliɪst/
adjective
最响叮当的
Most full of or characterized by a cheerful ringing sound.
The jingliest sleigh I've ever heard rode through town. 我听到过最响叮当的雪橇穿过城镇。
近义词:merriest, most festive
反义词:quietest, stillest
词性:adjective
