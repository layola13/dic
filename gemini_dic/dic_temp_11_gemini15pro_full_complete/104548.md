kamik:/ˈkæmɪk/| n.|加拿大式雪地靴，高筒防水靴|A waterproof boot, traditionally made of sealskin or caribou hide, reaching to the knee or higher, worn in Arctic regions.|She wore her kamiks while walking through the deep snow. 她穿着她的雪地靴在深雪中行走。|Synonyms: mukluks, snow boots|Antonyms: sandals, flip-flops|noun

kamik:/ˈkæmɪk/| n.|袜子，长袜 (更古老的用法，现在少见)|A sock or stocking. (Archaic)|He pulled on his kamiks before putting on his boots. 他在穿上靴子之前先穿上了袜子。（古老用法）|Synonyms: socks, stockings|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for this archaic usage)|noun
