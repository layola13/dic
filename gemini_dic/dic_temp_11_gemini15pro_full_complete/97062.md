inferible:/ɪnˈfɜːrəbl/ | adj. | 可推断的，能推理的 |  Able to be inferred or deduced; derivable by reasoning. | From the evidence presented, it is inferible that the suspect was at the scene of the crime. 从提供的证据来看，可以推断嫌疑人在犯罪现场。| synonyms: deducible, derivable, concludable | antonyms: uninferable, indemonstrable | word type: adjective
