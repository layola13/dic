altiscope: /ˈæltɪskoʊp/ | n. | 高空观测镜，高倍望远镜 | A telescope for observing objects at a high altitude.  It can be used for observing celestial bodies or terrestrial landmarks from a high vantage point. |  We used an altiscope to observe the mountain range from the valley floor. 我们用高空观测镜从谷底观察山脉。

The altiscope offered breathtaking views of the cityscape. 这架高倍望远镜提供了令人叹为观止的城市景观。 | 近义词: telescope, periscope | 反义词: microscope | 词性: noun
