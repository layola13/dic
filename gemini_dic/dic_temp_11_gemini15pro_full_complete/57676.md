drawing-roomy: /ˈdrɔːɪŋˌruːmi/ | adj. | 像客厅的；舒适的；宽敞的 | Resembling or suggestive of a drawing room, especially in being spacious and comfortable. |  The new apartment has a drawing-roomy feel with its high ceilings and large windows.  这套新公寓有着高高的天花板和大大的窗户，感觉就像客厅一样。 | The vacation cabin had a surprisingly drawing-roomy atmosphere. 度假小屋的氛围出奇地像客厅一样舒适。 | spacious, comfortable, commodious, ample | cramped, small, confined | adjective

Etymology:
drawing-room + -y

drawing-room (n.): from drawing (n.) + room (n.). Originally a withdrawing room, a room to which one withdraws after dinner.  

-y (suffix): forms adjectives meaning "having the quality of," "full of," "characterized by."
