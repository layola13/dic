hybridous: /haɪˈbrɪdəs/ | adj. | 杂交的，混合的 |  Formed by combining different elements; of mixed origin.  Relates to or is characteristic of a hybrid. While "hybrid" is the standard and preferred term, "hybridous" is an older, less common variant that is still occasionally encountered. |  The hybridous plant showed characteristics of both parent species.  这种杂交植物表现出两种亲本物种的特征。 | hybrid, mixed, crossbred | pure, unmixed | adjective
