nonapplicability:/ˌnɒnˌæplɪkəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不适用性；不相关性 |The nonapplicability of this law to the current situation is clear. 这项法律不适用于当前的情况。| Example: The nonapplicability of the rule in this case is due to the unique circumstances.  在这个案例中，该规则的不适用性是由于其特殊情况。 Example:  He argued the nonapplicability of the treaty to the current dispute. 他辩称该条约不适用于当前的争端。 | Synonym: irrelevance, inapplicability, unsuitability | Antonym: applicability, relevance, suitability | noun
