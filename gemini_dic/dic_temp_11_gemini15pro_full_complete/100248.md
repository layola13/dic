interruptible:/ˌɪntəˈrʌptɪbl/ | adj. | 可中断的，可打断的 | Able to be interrupted or stopped. | The program is interruptible by pressing Ctrl+C.  这个程序可以通过按 Ctrl+C 来中断。|  The download is interruptible, so you can pause it and resume later. 下载是可中断的，所以你可以暂停它以后再继续。| synonyms: stoppable, terminable,  | antonyms: uninterruptible, continuous, non-stop | adjective
