hypobranchial:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈbræŋkiəl/
adjective
舌骨下的；鳃下骨的
Situated below the gills or the branchial arches.  Relating to the hypobranchial bone or cartilage.
The hypobranchial muscles are responsible for lowering the jaw in many fish species. 舌骨下肌肉负责许多鱼类的下颌运动。
Fish use their hypobranchial apparatus to help pump water over their gills.  鱼利用它们的舌骨下器官帮助将水泵过鳃。
