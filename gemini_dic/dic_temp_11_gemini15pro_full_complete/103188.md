jesuitically:/ˌdʒɛzjuˈɪtɪkli/
adv.|耶稣会式地；诡辩地；狡猾地|In a manner characterized by subtle, often deceptive, reasoning or argumentation, typically associated with the Jesuits.  This often implies casuistry or equivocation.|He argued his point jesuitically, twisting the facts to suit his purpose. 他诡辩地阐述自己的观点，歪曲事实以达到自己的目的。|Synonym: casuistically, sophistically, cunningly, deceptively|Antonym: honestly, frankly, forthrightly|词性: 副词

jesuit:/ˈdʒɛzjuɪt/
n.|耶稣会士|A member of the Society of Jesus, a Roman Catholic religious order.|He was educated by the Jesuits. 他是由耶稣会士教育的。|Synonym: Jesuit priest, member of the Society of Jesus|Antonym: N/A|词性: 名词
