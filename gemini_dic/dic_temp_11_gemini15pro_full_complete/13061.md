astrohatch:/ˈæstroʊhætʃ/
n.
天窗，太空舱舱口
A hatch, especially one in a spacecraft, designed for observation of or exit into space.
The astronaut opened the astrohatch and floated out into the void. 宇航员打开了太空舱舱口，飘进了虚空。
