cricketed:/ˈkrɪkɪtɪd/| adj.| 打板球的；与板球有关的；(非正式)筋疲力尽的，累垮的|Relating to the sport of cricket; (informal) exhausted, worn out.|例句：He's a very cricketed fellow. 他是个十足的板球迷。|例句：After a long day of hiking, I felt completely cricketed. 经过一天的长途跋涉，我感到筋疲力尽。|近义词：exhausted, tired, worn out, drained|反义词：energetic, refreshed, invigorated|词性:形容词
词源: From the noun "cricket" (the sport) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). The informal meaning of "exhausted" is likely a metaphorical extension, perhaps relating to the physical exertion involved in playing cricket.
