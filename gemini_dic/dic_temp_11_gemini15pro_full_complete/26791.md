cafetiere:/ˈkæfɪtɪər/ (UK), /ˈkæfətjɛər/ (US)| n. |咖啡壶；法压壶|A tall, cylindrical glass or metal pot with a plunger that separates coffee grounds from brewed coffee.|例句：I make coffee every morning with my cafetiere. 我每天早上都用法压壶煮咖啡。

例句：The cafetiere makes a rich and flavorful cup of coffee. 法压壶煮出的咖啡浓郁而美味。


近义词：French press, coffee press, plunger pot|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
