incysted:/ɪnˈsɪstɪd/| adj.|包囊的，被囊包围的|Enclosed in a cyst or sac; encysted.  A parasitic larva may become encysted in the muscles of its host.|The doctor found an incysted tumor in the patient's liver. 医生在病人的肝脏里发现了一个包囊的肿瘤。|The parasite was discovered in its incysted stage.  这种寄生虫是在其包囊阶段被发现的。|近义词：encased, enclosed, encapsulated|反义词: free, exposed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From in- + cyst + -ed. "Cyst" comes from the Greek word "kystis" meaning bladder or pouch.
