deceptively:/dɪˈsɛptɪvli/
adverb
欺骗性地；貌似…地；迷惑地
in a way that gives a misleading impression
The house looked deceptively small from the outside. 这房子从外面看起来很小，其实不然。
Synonyms: misleadingly, falsely, deceptively
Antonyms: genuinely, truly, honestly
词性: 副词


deceptive:/dɪˈsɛptɪv/
adjective
欺骗性的；迷惑的
giving a misleading impression
Appearances can be deceptive. 外表可能是骗人的。
Synonyms: misleading, fallacious, spurious
Antonyms: genuine, true, honest
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēceptīvus, from Latin dēcipere (“to deceive, ensnare, beguile”), from dē- (“down, away from”) + capere (“to take, seize”).
