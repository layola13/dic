hereditary:/hɪˈredɪteri/ /həˈredɪteri/| adj.|遗传的，世袭的|
Transmitted or capable of being transmitted genetically from parent to offspring. Passed down by inheritance.  Relating to inheritance or heredity. |
Example 1:  Hereditary factors play a role in determining height.  遗传因素在决定身高方面起作用。

Example 2: The disease is hereditary, passed down through generations. 这种疾病是遗传性的，世代相传。
|近义词：genetic, inherited, inborn, congenital|反义词：acquired, non-hereditary|词性:形容词
