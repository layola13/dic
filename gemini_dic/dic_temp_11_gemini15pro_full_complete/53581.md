disbrain:/dɪsˈbreɪn/ |v.| 使失去理智；使丧失思考能力 | To cause someone to lose their mental faculties; to make someone unable to think clearly. | The constant stress and lack of sleep disbrained him, making it difficult to focus on even simple tasks. 持续的压力和睡眠不足使他失去了理智，即使是简单的任务也难以集中精力。| The propaganda was designed to disbrain the population and make them more susceptible to manipulation. 这宣传的目的是使民众失去理智，使他们更容易被操纵。|  近义词: bewilder, confuse, muddle, befuddle | 反义词: enlighten, clarify, focus |词性: 动词
