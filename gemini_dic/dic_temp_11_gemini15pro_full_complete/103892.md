judaism: /ˈdʒuːdeɪɪzəm/ | n. | 犹太教 | The monotheistic religion of the Jewish people. | Judaism is one of the oldest monotheistic religions. 犹太教是最古老的一神教之一。

Judaism emphasizes ethical behavior and social justice. 犹太教强调伦理行为和社会正义。 | 近义词: Jewish faith, Hebrew religion | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French Judaïsme, via late Latin from Greek Ioudaismos, from Ioudaios ‘Jew’.
