outgeneral:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɛn(ə)rəl/
verb
以智取胜，智胜，将…的军
defeat by superior strategy or tactics
例句：The experienced chess player outgeneraled his opponent.  经验丰富的棋手以智取胜了他的对手。

例句：Napoleon outgeneraled the Austrian army at the Battle of Austerlitz.  拿破仑在奥斯特里茨战役中智胜了奥地利军队。

近义词：outmaneuver, outwit, outsmarted
反义词：be outgeneraled, be outsmarted


词源：out- + general (v.)
