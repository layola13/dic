nursery: /ˈnɜːrsəri/ (UK) /ˈnɜːrsəri/ (US)| n.|托儿所，苗圃|A place where young children are cared for during the day while their parents are at work; a place where plants are grown for sale.|例句：My son goes to a nursery near our house. 我的儿子去我们家附近的托儿所。

例句：She works at a plant nursery. 她在一家苗圃工作。

近义词：crèche, daycare, kindergarten (for children); garden center, greenhouse (for plants)|反义词: None|词性: noun
