grossed-out:/ɡroʊst aʊt/| adj.|感到恶心的，反胃的|Feeling disgusted or revolted, typically by something unpleasant.|The smell of the rotten eggs grossed me out.  腐烂鸡蛋的气味让我感到恶心。|近义词：disgusted, nauseated, repulsed, sickened|反义词:pleased, delighted|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "gross out," which is formed from "gross" (meaning unpleasant or disgusting) and the particle "out."  "Gross" ultimately derives from the Old French word "gros," meaning large or coarse.
