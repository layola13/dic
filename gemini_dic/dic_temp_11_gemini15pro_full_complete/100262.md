intersampling:/ˌɪntərˈsæmplɪŋ/
|n.|间采样，子采样|The process of sampling a signal at a lower rate than the original sampling rate.|Intersampling can reduce the amount of data needed to represent a signal. 间采样可以减少表示信号所需的数据量。|
| |Intersampling can lead to aliasing if the original signal contains frequencies above the Nyquist frequency of the lower sampling rate. 如果原始信号包含高于较低采样率奈奎斯特频率的频率，则间采样会导致混叠。|
| |downsampling, subsampling| | |n.|
