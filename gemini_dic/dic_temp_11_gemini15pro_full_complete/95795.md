inconvenience:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəns/ | n. vt. |不便，麻烦；打扰，使不便|Something that causes difficulty, trouble, or discomfort.  As a verb, to cause difficulty or trouble for someone.|The traffic jam inconvenienced us greatly. 交通堵塞给我们带来了极大的不便。|近义词：trouble, difficulty, nuisance, bother, hardship, problem, disadvantage, drawback,  discomfort|反义词: convenience, ease, comfort, advantage, benefit|词性:名词，动词


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French inconvenient, from in- ‘not’ + convenient.
