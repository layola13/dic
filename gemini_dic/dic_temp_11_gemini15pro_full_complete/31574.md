chasm:/ˈkæzəm/| n.|深渊，裂隙，巨大差距|A deep, steep-sided opening in the earth's surface; a marked difference of opinion, feeling, etc.|例句：There was a chasm between the two sides in the negotiations.  谈判双方存在巨大分歧。

例句：The earthquake created a chasm in the road. 地震在路上造成了一个裂缝。|近义词：abyss, gorge, rift, gap, divide|反义词：closure, connection, bridge|词性:noun
