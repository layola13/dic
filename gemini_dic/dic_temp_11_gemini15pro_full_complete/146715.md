overhomelinesses: /ˌoʊvərˈhoʊmlɪnɪsɪz/
| n. | 过度家常感；过分朴素；过分简陋 |  The state of being excessively homely or plain, often to the point of being unattractive or uninteresting.  A surplus of homely qualities. |  The overhomelinesses of the decor made the restaurant feel more like a cafeteria.  这过于家常的装饰风格，让这家餐厅感觉更像一个自助餐厅。 | The overhomelinesses of her dress made her blend into the background. 她那过于朴素的穿着让她融入到背景之中。 | 近义词: plainness, simplicity, drabness, dowdiness | 反义词: elegance, sophistication, stylishness, ornateness | 词性: 名词


词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "homely" (adjective meaning "simple and plain") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives) + "-es" (plural suffix)
