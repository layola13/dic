endoskeletal:/ˌɛndoʊˈskɛlɪtl/
adjective
内骨骼的
Relating to or having an internal skeleton.
Vertebrates have endoskeletons made of bone or cartilage. 脊椎动物有由骨骼或软骨组成的内骨骼。
Many animals, including humans, have endoskeletons.  许多动物，包括人类，都有内骨骼。
近义词: internal
反义词: exoskeletal
词性: adjective


endoskeleton:/ˌɛndoʊˈskɛlɪtn/
noun
内骨骼
An internal skeleton.
The endoskeleton provides support and protection for internal organs. 内骨骼为内脏提供支撑和保护。
The human endoskeleton is made up of over 200 bones. 人体骨骼由 200 多块骨头组成。
近义词: internal skeleton
反义词: exoskeleton
词性: noun
