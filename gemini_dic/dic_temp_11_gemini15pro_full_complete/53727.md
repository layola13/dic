discodactylia:/ˌdɪskoʊdækˈtɪliə/
n.
指(趾)粘合畸形; 并指(趾)
Fusion of digits (fingers or toes).
Example: The infant was born with discodactylia affecting two of his toes.  这个婴儿出生时有两个脚趾患有并指畸形。
Example:  Discodactylia can range in severity from a slight webbing to a complete fusion of the digits. 并指畸形的严重程度从轻微的蹼状到指(趾)完全融合不等。
Synonym: syndactyly, webbed digits
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but the opposite condition would be having separate digits)
词性: noun
