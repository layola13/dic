lipid:/ˈlɪpɪd/| n. |脂质；类脂|Any of a class of organic compounds that are fatty acids or their derivatives and are insoluble in water but soluble in organic solvents. They include many natural oils, waxes, and steroids.|例句：Cholesterol is a lipid that is essential for the body. 胆固醇是人体必需的脂质。

例句：Lipids are stored in the body as a source of energy. 脂质储存在体内作为能量来源。|近义词：fat, oil |反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑water-soluble compounds 水溶性化合物)|词性:noun
