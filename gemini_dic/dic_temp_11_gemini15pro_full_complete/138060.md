norm:/nɔːrm/ | n. | 规范，标准，准则 |A standard or pattern, especially of social behavior, that is typical or expected.| The social norms of the community dictate appropriate behavior. 社区中的社会规范规定了合适的行为。| synonyms: standard, rule, criterion, benchmark | antonyms: anomaly, aberration, exception | noun

norm:/nɔːrm/ | n. | 平均数，平均水平 |An average level or standard.|  Rainfall this year has been below the norm. 今年的降雨量低于平均水平。 | synonyms: average, mean, median | antonyms: extreme, outlier | noun
