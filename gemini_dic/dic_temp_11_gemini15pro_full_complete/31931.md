cheirosophy:/ˌkaɪroʊˈsɒfi/ /ˌkɪəroʊˈsɒfi/| n. | 手相术，手相学 |The study or reading of the lines on the palms of the hands, claiming to indicate the character or future of a person.|She consulted a cheirosophist to have her palm read. 她咨询了一位手相师，让他解读她的掌纹。|近义词：palmistry, chiromancy |反义词: None |词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *kheir* (hand) + *sophia* (wisdom).
