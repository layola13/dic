liquidating: /ˈlɪkwɪdeɪtɪŋ/
| v. adj.| 清算；清偿；清除，消灭 |  The process of selling off assets to pay debts, or of closing down a company.  |  The company is liquidating its assets.  这家公司正在清算其资产。| synonyms: dissolving, winding up, selling off, closing down | antonyms: establishing, founding, opening, starting | verb

liquidating: /ˈlɪkwɪdeɪtɪŋ/
| adj.| 清算的 |  Relating to the process of liquidating. | The company is in its liquidating phase.  这家公司正处于清算阶段。 | synonyms:  dissolving, winding-up | antonyms:  operational, functioning | adjective
