exhaustivenesses:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
彻底性；详尽性；穷尽性
The state or quality of being exhaustive; thoroughness, completeness.
The lawyer impressed the jury with the exhaustivenesses of his research.  律师以其研究的详尽性给陪审团留下了深刻的印象。
Near Synonyms: thoroughness, completeness, comprehensiveness, extensiveness
Antonyms: incompleteness, superficiality, cursoryness
