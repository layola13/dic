inequal:/ɪnˈiːkwəl/
adjective
不相等的，不平等的
not equal; uneven; not uniform in size, quantity, or quality.
The two pieces of cake were inequal. 这两块蛋糕大小不一样。
近义词:unequal, disparate, uneven, different, dissimilar
反义词:equal, equivalent, identical, uniform, similar
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *inaequalis*, from *in-* (“not”) + *aequalis* (“equal”).
