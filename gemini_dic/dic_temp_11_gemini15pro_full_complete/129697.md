non-doctrinaire:/ˌnɒnˌdɒktrɪˈnɛːr/ /ˌnɑːnˌdɑːktrɪˈnɛːr/| adj.| 非教条的，不拘泥于教条的| Not rigidly adhering to a set of doctrines or theories; pragmatic.|  例句：The senator took a non-doctrinaire approach to the issue.  这位参议员对这个问题采取了务实的态度。|例句：She is known for her non-doctrinaire views on education. 她以其不拘泥于教条的教育观点而闻名。|近义词：pragmatic, practical, flexible, open-minded, empirical|反义词：doctrinaire, dogmatic, rigid, inflexible, ideological|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + doctrinaire (from French, meaning "doctrinal")
