Refers to various socialist and non-socialist groups that opposed the Bolsheviks. |  The non-bolsheviki parties formed a fragile coalition after the Tsar's abdication.  非布尔什维克党派在沙皇退位后组成了一个脆弱的联盟。

The non-bolsheviki factions were eventually suppressed by the Bolshevik regime.  非布尔什维克派最终被布尔什维克政权镇压。| 近义词：Mensheviks (specifically), opponents, dissenters | 反义词: Bolsheviks | 词性：名词 (plural)
