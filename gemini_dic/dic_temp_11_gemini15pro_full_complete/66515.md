exhaled:/ɛksˈheɪld/
verb
呼出；吐气
(past tense and past participle of exhale) breathed out; having breathed out.
She exhaled slowly and tried to relax. 她慢慢地呼气，试图放松。
The diver exhaled a stream of bubbles. 潜水员呼出一串气泡。
synonyms: breathed out, let out, expelled
antonyms: inhaled, breathed in
