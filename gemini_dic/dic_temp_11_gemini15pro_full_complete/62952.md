enneapetalous:/ˌɛniəˈpɛtələs/
adjective
九瓣的；有九片花瓣的
Having nine petals.
The enneapetalous flower bloomed in the spring. 九瓣的花在春天盛开了。
Near Synonyms: nine-petaled, nine-petalled
Antonyms: apetalous (无花瓣的)
词性: adjective
