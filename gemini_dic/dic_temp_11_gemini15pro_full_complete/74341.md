fragmentary:/ˈfræɡmənteri/ | adj. | 片段的，零碎的，不完整的 | Consisting of fragments; incomplete; disconnected. | The archaeologist pieced together the fragmentary remains of the ancient pottery. 考古学家将古代陶器的碎片拼凑在一起。 |  The information we have is fragmentary and doesn't give us the whole picture. 我们掌握的信息是零散的，无法让我们看到全貌。 | 近义词: incomplete, partial, fragmented, broken, piecemeal | 反义词: complete, whole, entire | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fragmentarius, from Latin fragmentum (“fragment”) + -arius.
