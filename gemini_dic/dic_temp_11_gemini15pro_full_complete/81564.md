grazed:/ɡreɪzd/| v.|擦伤；放牧；吃草；轻擦|To touch or scrape lightly in passing; to feed on growing grass; to eat small portions of food throughout the day.|The car grazed the wall as it turned the corner. 汽车转弯时擦到了墙。|近义词：scraped, brushed, grazed (for animals: pastured, browsed)|反义词：smashed, crashed (for animals: starved)|verb

grazed:/ɡreɪzd/| adj.|擦伤的|Lightly scraped or brushed.|His knees were grazed from falling on the pavement. 他的膝盖因为摔倒在人行道上而擦伤了。|近义词：scraped, scratched|反义词：unblemished, intact|adjective
