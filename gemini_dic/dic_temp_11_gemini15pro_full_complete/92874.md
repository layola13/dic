idealess:/aɪˈdiːələs/
adjective
无主意的；缺乏想法的；没有创意的
Lacking ideas; unimaginative.
He was completely idealess when it came to planning the party. 他在策划派对方面完全没有主意。
近义词:unimaginative, uninventive, uninspired, uncreative
反义词:imaginative, inventive, inspired, creative
词性:形容词
