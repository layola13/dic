grease:/ɡriːs/ /ɡris/| n. v.|油脂，润滑油；油腻；贿赂|A thick oily substance, often used as a lubricant.  To lubricate or coat with grease.|The mechanic greased the bearings. 机械师给轴承上了润滑油。|近义词：lubricant, oil, fat|反义词:cleanliness|词性:名词,动词

greasy:/ˈɡriːsi/ /ˈɡriːzi/| adj.|油腻的；滑溜溜的；谄媚的|Covered with or containing grease; slippery; unpleasantly or insincerely polite.|The food was too greasy for me. 这食物对我来说太油腻了。|近义词：oily, slippery, slimy|反义词:clean, dry|词性:形容词
