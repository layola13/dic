halterlike:/ˈhɔːltərlaɪk/| adj.|像缰绳的，像halter的|Resembling a halter, a rope or strap used for leading or tying an animal, typically around the head.  It suggests something restrictive or controlling. |The dress had a halterlike neckline. 这件连衣裙有一个像缰绳一样的领口。|Synonyms: restrictive, confining, harnesslike|Antonyms: loose, freeing, liberating|adjective
