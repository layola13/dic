overobjectification: /ˌōvərəbdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度物化 |  The act of treating a person as an object, especially a sexual object, to an excessive degree.  It goes beyond simply noticing someone's physical attributes and reduces them to their body or body parts, ignoring their personality, intellect, and feelings. | The media's overobjectification of women contributes to unrealistic beauty standards. 媒体对女性的过度物化导致了不切实际的审美标准。 |  The overobjectification of models in advertising is a serious issue. 广告中对模特的过度物化是一个严重的问题。 |  objectification, sexualization | humanization, personification | noun
