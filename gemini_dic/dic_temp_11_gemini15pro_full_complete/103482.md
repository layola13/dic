jobs:/dʒɒbz/ (British) /dʒɑːbz/ (American)| n.|工作；职业；零工|A job is a regular activity performed in exchange for payment.  A person can have one or more jobs.|例句：He has a job as a teacher. 他是一位老师。

She's looking for a new job. 她正在寻找一份新工作。|近义词：occupation, profession, career, work, employment|反义词：unemployment, leisure, retirement|词性:名词
