disaffirm:/ˌdɪsəˈfɜːrm/| v. |否认，拒绝承认，废除，取消|To deny or repudiate; to declare void.|The teenager disaffirmed the contract he had signed without his parents' consent.  这名青少年拒绝承认他在未经父母同意的情况下签署的合同。|近义词：repudiate, reject, renounce, revoke, cancel, annul, invalidate|反义词：affirm, confirm, ratify, validate|词性: 动词

词源:
From Late Latin *disaffirmare, from dis- + affirmare "to affirm," from ad- "to" + firmare "to strengthen," from firmus "strong."
