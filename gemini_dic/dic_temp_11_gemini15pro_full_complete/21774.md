boba:/ˈboʊbə/| n.|珍珠奶茶; 波霸奶茶|Tapioca pearls in tea; bubble tea.  A Taiwanese tea-based drink with chewy tapioca balls.|例句：I love to drink boba in the summer. 我夏天喜欢喝珍珠奶茶。

例句：She ordered a strawberry boba with extra tapioca. 她点了一杯草莓珍珠奶茶，加了额外的珍珠。|近义词：bubble tea, pearl milk tea|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


boba:/ˈboʊbə/| adj.|(俚语) 丰满的，圆润的 (尤指女性身材)|Describing something plump or round, often referring to a woman's figure (slang).|例句：She has a boba figure. 她身材丰满。

例句：He prefers a more boba physique. 他更喜欢丰满的身材。|近义词：curvy, voluptuous (for body shape)|反义词:slim, thin|词性:adjective
