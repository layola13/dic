loveliness:/ˈlʌvlɪnɪs/| n. |可爱；美丽；魅力|The quality of being lovely; beauty.|Her loveliness captivated everyone in the room. 她的可爱迷住了房间里的每一个人。|近义词：beauty, charm, attractiveness, allure|反义词：ugliness, unattractiveness, plainness|词性:noun

词源: From Middle English *lovelinesse, from Old English *luflīcnes (“loveliness”), equivalent to lovely +‎ -ness. Cognate with Scots luvelines (“loveliness”), West Frisian leaflikens (“loveliness”).
