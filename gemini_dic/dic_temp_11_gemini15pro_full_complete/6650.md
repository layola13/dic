anaesthetizing:/əˈniːsθəˌtaɪzɪŋ/ /æˈnɛsθəˌtaɪzɪŋ/
verb
麻醉；使麻木
administering an anesthetic to (someone) so that they do not feel pain during a medical procedure.
The doctor is anaesthetizing the patient before the surgery. 医生正在手术前麻醉病人。
synonyms:numbing, desensitizing
antonyms:stimulating, sensitizing
verb

anaesthetic:/əˈniːsθɛtɪk/ /ænɪsˈθɛtɪk/
noun
麻醉剂
a substance that produces general or local insensibility to pain and other sensation.
The dentist used a local anaesthetic before extracting the tooth. 牙医在拔牙前使用了局部麻醉剂。
synonyms:narcotic, opiate
antonyms:stimulant
noun

anaesthesia:/ˌænɪsˈθiːʒə/ /ˌænɪsˈθiːziə/
noun
麻醉
insensitivity to pain, especially as artificially induced by the administration of gases or the injection of drugs before surgical operations.
The patient was under general anaesthesia during the operation. 病人在手术过程中处于全身麻醉状态。
synonyms:unconsciousness, numbness
antonyms:consciousness, sensation
noun
