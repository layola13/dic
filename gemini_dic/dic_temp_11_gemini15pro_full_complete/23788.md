brawniness:/ˈbrɔːninɪs/
n.
muscular strength; physical power
The brawniness of the weightlifter was impressive.  举重运动员的强壮令人印象深刻。
Example:  He admired the brawniness of the construction workers. 他钦佩建筑工人的强壮。
Example:  The boxer's brawniness was evident in his powerful punches. 拳击手的强壮从他强有力的拳击中可见一斑。
Synonym:  strength, muscularity, power, might
Antonym: weakness, frailty, feebleness
noun

brawn:/brɔːn/
n.
physical strength; muscles
He used his brawn to lift the heavy box. 他用他的力气举起了沉重的箱子。
Example:  The job required more brawn than brains. 这份工作需要更多的是体力而不是智力。
Example:  She relied on her brawn to move the furniture. 她依靠自己的力气搬家具。
Synonym:  muscle, strength, power
Antonym: weakness, frailty
noun
