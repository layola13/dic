inopercular:/ˌɪnɒˈpɜːkjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 无鳃盖的，无盖的 | Lacking an operculum; without a lid or cover. | Example 1:  The inopercular gills of the salamander were clearly visible.  蝾螈无盖的鳃清晰可见。

Example 2: Some species of snails have inopercular shells.  某些蜗牛的壳没有盖。 | Near synonyms: lidless, coverless, uncovered, open | Antonyms: operculate, covered, lidded | Adjective
