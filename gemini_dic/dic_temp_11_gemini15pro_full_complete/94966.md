in-store:/ˌɪn ˈstɔːr/
adjective
店内; 在店内进行的
Available or occurring within a retail store.
This offer is only available in-store.  这个优惠只在店内有效。
Customers can return items in-store for a full refund. 顾客可以将商品退回店内并获得全额退款。
synonym: in the shop, at the store
antonym: online, out of stock
词性: 形容词


in-store:/ˌɪn ˈstɔːr/
adverb
在店内
At or within a retail store.
You can purchase the tickets in-store or online. 你可以在店内或在线购买门票。
We offer free consultations in-store. 我们在店内提供免费咨询。
synonym: at the shop, inside the store
antonym: online, remotely
词性: 副词

词源： "in" (preposition) + "store" (noun). The preposition "in" indicates location or position inside something. "Store" refers to a retail establishment.
