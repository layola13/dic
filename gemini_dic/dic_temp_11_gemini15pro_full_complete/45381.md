cunningness:/ˈkʌnɪŋnɪs/| n.|狡猾，诡诈|The cunningness of the fox allowed it to steal the chickens. 狐狸的狡猾使它偷走了鸡。|例句：His cunningness was evident in the way he manipulated the situation. 他的狡猾体现在他操纵局势的方式上。

The lawyer's cunningness helped him win the case.  这位律师的狡猾帮助他赢得了这场官司。|近义词：craftiness, slyness, guile, deviousness|反义词：honesty, frankness, artlessness, naivety|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cunnyngnesse*, from Old English *cunnungnes*, equivalent to cunning +‎ -ness.
