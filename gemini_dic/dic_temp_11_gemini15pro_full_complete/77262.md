gastropodous:/ˌɡæstrəˈpoʊdəs/
|adj.| 腹足类的；有腹足的 |Relating to or characteristic of gastropods; having a broad foot on the ventral side of the body. | Snails and slugs are gastropodous mollusks.  蜗牛和蛞蝓是腹足类软体动物。|Synonyms: univalve, snail-like|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
