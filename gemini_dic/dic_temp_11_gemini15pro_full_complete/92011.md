Example 1: Hyperosmolar hyperglycemic state is a serious complication of diabetes. 高渗性高血糖状态是糖尿病的一种严重并发症。

Example 2: The patient was diagnosed with hyperosmolar nonketotic syndrome.  该病人被诊断为高渗性非酮症综合征。


近义词: hypertonic | 反义词: hypoosmolar | 词性: adjective
