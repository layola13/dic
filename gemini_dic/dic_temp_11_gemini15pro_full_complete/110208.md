life-raft:/ˈlaɪfˌrɑːft/ /ˈlaɪfˌræft/| n.|救生筏|A small inflatable or rigid boat used for emergency evacuation from a ship or aircraft.|The sailors escaped the sinking ship on a life-raft. 水手们乘坐救生筏逃离了正在下沉的船。

The pilot instructed the passengers on how to use the life-raft in case of an emergency. 飞行员指导乘客如何在紧急情况下使用救生筏。|近义词：lifeboat, inflatable boat, dinghy|反义词:|名词
