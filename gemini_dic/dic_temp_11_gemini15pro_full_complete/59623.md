ecclesia: /ɪˈkliːziə/ (UK), /ɪˈkliːʒə/ (US) | n. | 教会，集会 |A Christian church or congregation; an assembly or gathering, especially for religious purposes.  From the Greek ἐκκλησία (ekklēsia), meaning "an assembly" or "congregation," from ἐκ (ek, "out") and καλέω (kaleō, "I call"). Originally referred to a citizen's assembly in ancient Greece, later adopted by early Christians to denote their gatherings. | The ecclesia met every Sunday. 教会每周日聚会。| The local ecclesia is very welcoming.  当地教会非常热情好客。 | synonyms: church, congregation, assembly, gathering | antonyms: dispersal, scattering | noun
