ecosystem:/ˈiːkəʊˌsɪstəm/ | n. | 生态系统 | A biological community of interacting organisms and their physical environment. |  The rainforest is a complex ecosystem.  雨林是一个复杂的生态系统。 | synonyms: biocoenosis, biome, community | antonyms: N/A | noun

ecosystem:/ˈiːkoʊˌsɪstəm/ | n. | 生态系统 | A complex network or interconnected system. | The business ecosystem includes suppliers, distributors, and customers. 商业生态系统包括供应商、分销商和客户。 | synonyms: network, system, environment | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *oikos* (house) + system.  Combining form *eco-* represents ecology.
