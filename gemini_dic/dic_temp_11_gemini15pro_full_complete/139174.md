obituary: /əˈbɪtʃuˌɛri/ /oʊˈbɪtʃuˌɛri/
n.
讣告，讣闻
a notice of death, especially in a newspaper, typically including a brief biography of the deceased person.
The newspaper published an obituary for the renowned author.  这家报纸刊登了这位著名作家的讣告。
synonyms: death notice, announcement of death, necrology
antonyms: birth announcement
