fluctuability:/ˌflʌktʃuːəˈbɪləti/
n.
波动性；变动性
The quality of being likely to change, especially frequently and by a considerable amount.
The fluctuability of the stock market makes investing a risky business. 股票市场的波动性使得投资成为一项冒险的生意。

fluctuability:/ˌflʌktʃuːəˈbɪləti/
noun
波动性；变动性
The tendency of something to fluctuate.
The fluctuability of oil prices is a constant concern for businesses.  油价的波动性是企业一直关注的问题。


Etymology:  From Late Latin *fluctuabilitās, from Latin fluctuābilis (“fluctuating”) + -itās.  Ultimately from Latin fluctuāre ("to undulate, fluctuate"), from fluctus ("wave").

Synonyms: variability, instability, volatility, changeability, inconstancy
Antonyms: stability, constancy, steadiness, firmness
