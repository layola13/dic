depreciate:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪt/
verb
贬值，折旧；降低…的价值；轻视，贬低
to decrease in value over time; to lower the value of something; to belittle or undervalue.
My car depreciated by $2,000 in the first year.  我的车第一年贬值了2000美元。
The company's assets have depreciated significantly. 该公司的资产已大幅贬值。
He tends to depreciate his own achievements. 他倾向于贬低自己的成就。
synonyms: devalue, decline, diminish, lessen, underestimate, belittle
antonyms: appreciate, increase, enhance, raise, overestimate, value
词性:v.
