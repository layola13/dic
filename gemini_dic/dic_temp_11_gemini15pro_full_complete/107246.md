laisser-faire:/ˌlɛseɪˈfɛər/ | n.| 自由放任; 不干涉主义 |  An economic policy of non-interference by government in the working of the free market.  | The government's laissez-faire approach to the economy has been criticized. 政府对经济的自由放任态度受到了批评。 |  non-intervention, free enterprise | interventionism, regulation | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French, literally "let (people) do (as they choose)."
