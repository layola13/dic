foolhardier:/ˌfuːlˈhɑːrdiər/ | adj. | 更鲁莽的，更轻率的 | More foolishly bold or daring; more reckless. |  He made the foolhardier decision to climb the mountain alone. 他做出了更鲁莽的决定，独自攀登这座山。|  The foolhardier of the two brothers decided to jump off the cliff first.  两兄弟中更鲁莽的那个决定先跳下悬崖。 |  reckless, rash, impetuous, daredevil, imprudent | cautious, prudent, careful, circumspect | adjective

Etymology->
From foolhardy + -er (comparative suffix). Foolhardy itself comes from fool + hardy.
