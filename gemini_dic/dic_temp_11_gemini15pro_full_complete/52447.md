died:/daɪd/ | v. | 死亡，逝世 | past tense of "die," meaning to stop living. |  He died peacefully in his sleep. 他在睡梦中平静地去世了。| My grandmother died last year. 我的祖母去年去世了。 | synonyms: perished, expired, deceased | antonyms: lived, survived, born | verb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He dies in the movie. = 他在电影里死了。（不常用，通常用现在时描述虚构作品）
The flower dies without water. = 花没有水就死了。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is dying. = 他快要死了。
The plant is dying from lack of sunlight. = 这株植物因为缺乏阳光快要死了。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has died. = 他已经去世了。
Many trees have died because of the drought. = 许多树因为干旱已经死了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

The fish had died before I could clean the tank. = 我还没来得及清理鱼缸，鱼就死了。
