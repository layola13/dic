nonessential:/ˌnɑːnɪˈsɛnʃəl/ | adj. n.| 非必要的；不重要的；非必需品 | Not absolutely necessary; not essential. | Many people lost their jobs in nonessential industries during the pandemic. | 近义词: unnecessary, dispensable, inessential, superfluous | 反义词: essential, necessary, vital, crucial | 词性: 形容词, 名词

词源:  Late Middle English (in the sense ‘not pertaining to the essence of a thing’): from non- + essential.
