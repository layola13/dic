corpulently:/ˈkɔːpjələntli/ | adv. | 肥胖地，臃肿地 | In a corpulent manner; characterized by excessive body fat. | He sat corpulently in his armchair, a picture of contentment. 他臃肿地坐在扶手椅里，一副心满意足的样子. |  Near synonyms: stoutly, portly, obesely, fatly | Near antonyms: thinly, slenderly, leanly | adverb

corpulous:/ˈkɔːrpjələs/ | adj. | 肥胖的，臃肿的 | Large or bulky of body; stout; fat. |  The corpulous gentleman enjoyed a hearty meal.  这位肥胖的先生享用了一顿丰盛的晚餐。 | Near synonyms: obese, stout, portly, plump | Near antonyms: thin, slender, lean, skinny | adjective
