furnacing:/ˈfɜːrnɪsɪŋ/
n.
加热; 焙烧; 烧制
The process of heating or baking something in a furnace.
The furnacing of the clay bricks took several days.  粘土砖的烧制花了几天时间。
The pottery underwent a final furnacing before being glazed.  陶器在上釉前进行了最后一次烧制。
近义词: firing, baking, heating, smelting
反义词: cooling
