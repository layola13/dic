macro-evolution:/ˌmækroʊˌɛvəˈluːʃən/ | n. | 宏观进化 | Major evolutionary change at or above the level of species over geological time, resulting in the formation of new taxonomic groups.  |  Example: Macro-evolution explains the development of entirely new groups of organisms, like birds from dinosaurs. 宏观进化解释了全新生物群体的发展，例如鸟类从恐龙进化而来。

Example: The fossil record provides evidence for macro-evolutionary processes. 化石记录为宏观进化过程提供了证据。 | 近义词: large-scale evolution, transpecific evolution | 反义词: micro-evolution | 词性: noun
