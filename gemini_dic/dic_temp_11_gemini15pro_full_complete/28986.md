cartelisms:/kɑːrˈtɛlɪzəmz/
n.
卡特尔主义，卡特尔行为
The practice or principles of cartels; collusive behavior between businesses.
Several economists have argued that certain cartelisms are harmful to consumers and stifle competition. 一些经济学家认为某些卡特尔行为对消费者有害，并扼杀了竞争。
近义词：collusion, price-fixing, market-sharing, anti-competitive practices
反义词：competition, free market
词性: 名词
