half-willfulness's:/ˌhæfˈwɪlfəlnəsɪz/
noun
半任性; 不完全的任性
Possession of a state of being only partially willful, implying a mix of intention and yielding or a subdued form of stubbornness.
Her half-willfulness's was evident in her hesitant agreement to the plan. 她对这个计划犹豫不决的同意表明了她不完全的任性。
Near synonyms:  half-heartedness, reluctance, hesitancy
Antonyms: decisiveness, determination, resolve
