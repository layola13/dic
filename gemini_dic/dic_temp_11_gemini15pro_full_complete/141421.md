ophiurida:/ˌɒfiˈjʊərɪdə/ | n. | 蛇尾纲 |A class of echinoderms that includes brittle stars and basket stars, characterized by their long, slender arms sharply demarcated from a central disk. |  Brittle stars, members of the class Ophiurida, are common on the ocean floor. 蛇尾，蛇尾纲的成员，在海底很常见。 |  近义词: brittle stars, basket stars | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a taxonomic classification) | noun
