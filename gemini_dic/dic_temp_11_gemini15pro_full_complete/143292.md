outdraw:/ˌaʊtˈdrɔː/
verb
胜过；比...吸引更多人
to attract a larger audience or following than
例句：The new Western outdrew all the other films showing that week.  这部新的西部片比那周上映的所有其他电影都更吸引人。

例句：The rock concert outdrew the opera in ticket sales. 这场摇滚音乐会的门票销量超过了歌剧。
近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, beat
反义词：lose to, be outdone by
词性：v.
