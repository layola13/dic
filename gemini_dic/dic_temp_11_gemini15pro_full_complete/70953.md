firefly:/ˈfaɪərˌflaɪ/ (US), /ˈfaɪəflaɪ/ (UK)| n. |萤火虫|A soft-bodied beetle that produces a flashing light.|Example 1:  The children chased fireflies in the twilight. 孩子们在暮色中追逐萤火虫。
