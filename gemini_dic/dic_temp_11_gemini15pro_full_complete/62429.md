endophragmal:/ˌɛndoʊˈfræɡməl/
adjective
内膈的，phragmal的内部
Located or occurring within a phragma (a partition or dividing wall, especially in the shell of a mollusk or other invertebrate).
The endophragmal skeleton of the snail provides structural support.  蜗牛的内膈骨骼提供结构支撑。
The biologist studied the endophragmal deposits in the nautilus shell.  生物学家研究了鹦鹉螺壳中的内膈沉积物。
近义词: intraphragmal
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly extraphragmal, although not commonly used)
词性: adjective
