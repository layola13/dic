overchafe:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃeɪf/| v. |擦伤；激怒；过度磨损；过度激动|To make sore by rubbing; to chafe excessively. Also, to irritate or vex.|The tight collar overchafed the dog's neck.|近义词：abrade, irritate, vex, gall|反义词：soothe, mollify|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *overchaufen, from over- + chaufen (“to warm, to heat, to chafe”). Equivalent to over- +‎ chafe.  The sense development likely went from "to heat excessively by rubbing" to "to rub sore" to "to irritate."
