heteroscedastic:/ˌhɛtərəʊskəˈdæstɪk/ | adj. |异方差的|Exhibiting heteroscedasticity; having unequal error variance.  This means the variability of a variable is unequal across the range of values of a second variable that predicts it. |The residuals in the regression model appeared heteroscedastic, indicating a violation of one of the key assumptions. 回归模型中的残差表现出异方差性，表明违反了其中一个关键假设。|The data showed a heteroscedastic pattern, with greater variance at higher values of the independent variable.  数据显示出异方差模式，自变量值越高，方差越大。| 近义词: nonconstant variance, unequal variance | 反义词: homoscedastic, equal variance |词性: adjective
