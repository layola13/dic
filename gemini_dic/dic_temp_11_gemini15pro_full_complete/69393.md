feeders:/ˈfiːdərz/ | n. | 加料器；进料器 | A device or machine used to supply material to another machine or process. | The factory uses automated feeders to supply parts to the assembly line. 这家工厂使用自动加料器向装配线供应零件。| synonyms: hoppers, chutes, conveyors | antonyms: extractors, dischargers  | noun
