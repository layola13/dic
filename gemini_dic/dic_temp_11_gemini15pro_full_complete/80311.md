戈德堡一家
A surname, and the title of a US television sitcom.  The show is about a loud, loving, and chaotic family in the 1980s.
The Goldbergs is a hilarious show.  戈德堡一家是一部搞笑的电视剧。
The Goldbergs family is always getting into some sort of trouble. 戈德堡一家总是惹上各种各样的麻烦。
Synonyms: (For the TV show) family sitcom, period comedy
