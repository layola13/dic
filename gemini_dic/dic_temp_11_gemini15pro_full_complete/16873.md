basilicae:/bəˈsɪlɪkiː/
n.
basilicas（教堂的复数形式）
plural of basilica, large oblong hall or building with double colonnades and a semicircular apse, used in ancient Rome as a court of law or for public assemblies. Later adopted by the Christian Church as a model for many of its churches.
The archaeologist unearthed the remains of several ancient basilicae.  考古学家发掘出了几座古代长方形教堂的遗迹。
近义词:cathedrals, churches
反义词:None (no single antonym for a type of building)
词性:Noun (plural)


basilica:/bəˈsɪlɪkə/
n.
长方形教堂
A large oblong hall or building with double colonnades and a semicircular apse, used in ancient Rome as a court of law or for public assemblies.  Later adopted by the Christian Church as a model for many of its churches.
St. Peter's Basilica in Vatican City is a renowned example of Renaissance architecture.  梵蒂冈城的圣彼得大教堂是文艺复兴时期建筑的著名例子。
近义词:cathedral, church
反义词:None
词性:Noun
