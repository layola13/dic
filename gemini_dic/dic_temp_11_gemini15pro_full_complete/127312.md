nawab:/nəˈwɑːb/ | n. | 纳瓦卜；印度的穆斯林统治者或高官 | A Muslim ruler or nobleman in India, especially during the Mughal period. |  The nawab lived in a grand palace.  纳瓦卜住在一座宏伟的宫殿里。

The nawab hosted lavish banquets.  纳瓦卜举办了奢华的宴会。| 近义词：ruler, prince, nobleman | 反义词：commoner, peasant | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Urdu نواب (navāb), from Persian نواب (navāb, “plural of نائب (nā'ib)”), ultimately from Arabic نائب (nā'ib, “deputy, viceroy”).
