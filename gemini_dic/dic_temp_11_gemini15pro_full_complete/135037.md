nihilist:/ˈnaɪəˌlɪst/ /ˈniːɪlɪst/| n.|虚无主义者；无政府主义者|A person who believes that life is meaningless and rejects all religious and moral principles.|He's such a nihilist, he doesn't believe in anything. 他真是个虚无主义者，什么都不信。|synonyms: cynic, pessimist, skeptic|antonyms: optimist, believer|noun
