katana:/kəˈtɑːnə/| n. |日本刀|A Japanese sword characterized by a curved, single-edged blade with a circular or squared guard and long grip to accommodate two hands.|The samurai drew his katana and prepared for battle. 武士拔出他的武士刀，准备战斗。|The museum displayed a collection of antique katana. 博物馆展出了一批古董武士刀。|近义词：sword, blade|反义词: (no direct antonym, possibly peace symbol)|noun
