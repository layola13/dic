duplexing:/ˈduːpleksɪŋ/ /ˈdjuːpleksɪŋ/ | n. | 双工，双向通信 | The act of transmitting data in two directions simultaneously. |  The network cable supports full duplexing, allowing for simultaneous send and receive operations.  该网线支持全双工，允许同时发送和接收操作。 | synonyms: two-way communication, bidirectional transmission | antonyms: simplex | noun


duplexing:/ˈduːpleksɪŋ/ /ˈdjuːpleksɪŋ/ | adj. | 双重的，双工的|  Relating to or operating in a duplex system. | This printer offers duplexing capabilities, saving paper by printing on both sides. 这台打印机提供双面打印功能，通过双面打印节省纸张。 | synonyms: double, dual, two-sided | antonyms: single, simplex | adjective
