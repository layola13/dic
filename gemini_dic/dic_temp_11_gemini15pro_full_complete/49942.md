dendrochronology:/ˌdɛndroʊkrəˈnɒlədʒi/| n.|树木年代学|The science or technique of dating events, environmental change, and archaeological artifacts by using the characteristic patterns of annual growth rings in timber and tree trunks.|Researchers used dendrochronology to determine the age of the ancient wooden beams.|近义词:tree-ring dating|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek δένδρον (déndron, “tree”) + χρόνος (khrónos, “time”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
