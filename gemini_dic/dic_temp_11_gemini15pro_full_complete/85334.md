harpoon:/hɑːrˈpuːn/| n. vt.|鱼叉，捕鲸叉；用鱼叉刺|A harpoon is a long, heavy spear attached to a rope, used for hunting large fish or whales.  鱼叉是一种系在绳子上的长而重的矛，用于捕猎大型鱼类或鲸鱼。|The whalers harpooned the massive blue whale. 捕鲸人用鱼叉刺中了那头巨大的蓝鲸。|近义词：spear, lance|反义词: - |noun, verb


Etymology->
From Middle French *harpon* (modern French *harpon*), from Old French *harper* "to grasp, grapple, seize," of Germanic origin.
