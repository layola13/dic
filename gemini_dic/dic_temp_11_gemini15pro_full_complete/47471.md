deallocate:/diːˈæləkeɪt/
verb
释放，解除分配
To release (something, especially computer memory) from allocation.
The program deallocates memory when it's finished.  程序在完成后会释放内存。
The system will deallocate the resources after the job is done.  系统将在作业完成后释放资源。
synonyms: release, free, liberate
antonyms: allocate, assign, reserve
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (indicating reversal) + allocate (to set apart for a particular purpose).  The word "allocate" comes from Latin "allocare,"  meaning "to place, assign," from "ad-" (to) + "locare" (to place).
