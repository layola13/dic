bodyboarded: /ˈbɒdiˌbɔːdɪd/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdiˌbɔːrdɪd/ (US)
verb
(bodyboard的过去式和过去分词) 身体趴板冲浪过
rode a bodyboard; surfed with a bodyboard.
He bodyboarded all summer long. 他整个夏天都在玩趴板冲浪。
近义词: surfed (especially if using a bodyboard), rode (a wave)
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "swam", "dived", or activities not involving wave riding)
词性: verb


bodyboarded: /ˈbɒdiˌbɔːdɪd/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdiˌbɔːrdɪd/ (US)
adjective
Having ridden a bodyboard.
Describing someone who has engaged in the activity of bodyboarding.
She returned from the beach, sun-kissed and bodyboarded. 她从海滩回来，晒得黝黑，玩过趴板。
近义词:  None (as an adjective describing a person having done the activity)
反义词: None
词性: adjective (less common usage)
