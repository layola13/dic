nonadhesive:/ˌnɒnədˈhɪːsɪv/
adjective
不粘的，非粘性的
Not sticky; not adhering.
The surgeon used nonadhesive bandages.  外科医生使用了不粘的绷带。
The wallpaper is nonadhesive and easy to remove.  这种壁纸是不粘的，很容易去除。
近义词:nonsticky, non-sticking
反义词:adhesive, sticky
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + adhesive (from Latin adhaesivus, past participle of adhaerere "to stick to")
