dismarshal:/dɪsˈmɑːrʃəl/
|v.|解除编组，解散；使复员|To deprive of the rank of marshal; to degrade from the rank of a marshal.  To dismiss from military service. To break up the order or formation of (troops, etc.); to disband.|The general dismarshaled the troops after the victory parade. 将军在胜利游行后解散了部队。|Synonyms: disband, demobilize, dismiss|Antonyms: marshal, assemble, organize|verb

dismarshal:/dɪsˈmɑːrʃəl/
|n.|解除编组，解散；使复员|The act of dismarshaling; the state of being dismarshaled.|The dismarshal of the army was a complex process. 军队复员是一个复杂的过程。|Synonyms: disbandment, demobilization, dismissal|Antonyms: marshaling, assembly, organization|noun
