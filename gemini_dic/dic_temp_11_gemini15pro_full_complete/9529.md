antiseizure:/ˌæntiˈsiːʒər/ | adj. | 抗癫痫的，抗惊厥的 |  Preventing or controlling seizures (sudden attacks of uncontrolled electrical activity in the brain). |  She was prescribed antiseizure medication to control her epilepsy. 她被开了抗癫痫药来控制她的癫痫。| synonyms: anticonvulsant, antiepileptic | antonyms: convulsant | adjective

antiseizure:/ˌæntiˈsiːʒər/ | n. | 抗癫痫药物 | A medication used to prevent or control seizures. | The doctor increased the dosage of her antiseizure. 医生增加了她的抗癫痫药物的剂量。 | synonyms: anticonvulsant, antiepileptic | antonyms: convulsant | noun
