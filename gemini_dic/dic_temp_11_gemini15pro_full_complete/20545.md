blabbiest:/ˈblæbiɪst/ | adj. | 最爱唠叨的；最饶舌的 | Given to excessive and often indiscreet talk; most talkative. |  She was the blabbiest person in the office, always sharing personal details. 她办公室里最爱唠叨的人，总是分享个人细节。|  He was known as the blabbiest member of the team, often revealing confidential information unintentionally. 他是团队中最爱唠叨的成员，经常无意中泄露机密信息。| 近义词: talkative, garrulous, loquacious | 反义词: reticent, taciturn | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "blab" (to talk indiscreetly) + "-iest" (superlative suffix). 源自“blab”（喋喋不休）+“-iest”（最高级后缀）。
