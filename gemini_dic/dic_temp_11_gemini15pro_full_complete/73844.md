foremost:/ˈfɔːrmoʊst/| adj.|最前面的，最重要的| situated or placed first; most prominent; chief; leading.|例句：Safety is our foremost concern. 安全是我们的首要考虑。|近义词：leading, primary, chief, principal, paramount|反义词：hindmost, last, least important|词性:形容词
foremost:/ˈfɔːrmoʊst/| adv.|首先，居于首位地| in the first place.|例句：He is foremost among the composers of our time. 他是我们这个时代最重要的作曲家之一。|近义词：primarily, chiefly, mainly, principally|反义词：lastly, finally|词性:副词
