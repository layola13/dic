commensality:/ˌkɒmənˈsælɪti/ |n.| 共餐；共食；会食|The act or practice of eating together.  The social interaction and bonding that occurs during shared meals.|例句：Commensality plays a vital role in strengthening family bonds.  共餐在加强家庭联系方面起着至关重要的作用。

例句：The anthropology class discussed the importance of commensality in different cultures. 人类学课讨论了共餐在不同文化中的重要性。|近义词：conviviality, fellowship, togetherness|反义词: isolation, solitude|词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *commensālitās, from Latin commensālis (“eating at the same table”), from com- (“together”) + mensa (“table”).
