barrios:/ˈbɑːrioʊs/ (US), /ˈbærioʊs/ (UK)| n.|街区，社区（尤指西班牙裔美国人聚居区）|A barrio is a Spanish-speaking neighborhood in a city or town, especially in the US.  It often refers to a district of a town or city in Spanish-speaking countries. |Many artists find inspiration in the vibrant culture of their barrios.| 近义词: neighborhoods, communities, districts|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "affluent areas" in a contrasting context) | Noun
