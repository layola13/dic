mowing:/ˈmoʊɪŋ/ (US) /ˈməʊɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v.|割草，修剪草坪|The act of cutting down grass, typically with a lawnmower.  The process can also refer to cutting down other plants with similar tools.|例句：The mowing of the lawn is usually done on weekends.  修剪草坪通常在周末进行。

mow:/moʊ/ (US) /məʊ/ (UK)| v.|割，修剪(草坪)|To cut down (grass, grain, etc.) with a blade or machine.|例句：I need to mow the lawn before it gets too long. 我需要在草坪太长之前修剪它。
