busy: /ˈbɪzi/ (US) /ˈbɪzi/ (UK)| adj.|忙碌的，繁忙的|Engaged in activity; occupied.|例句：I'm busy with work today. 我今天工作很忙。|近义词：occupied, engaged, active|反义词：free, idle, available|词性:形容词

busy: /ˈbɪzi/ (US) /ˈbɪzi/ (UK)| v.|使忙碌，忙于|To occupy oneself with activity.|例句：She busied herself with preparing dinner. 她忙着准备晚餐。|近义词：occupy, engage|反义词：idle, relax|词性:动词
