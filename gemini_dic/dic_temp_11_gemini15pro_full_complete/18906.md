besmut:/bɪˈsmʌt/
verb
玷污，弄脏
to make something dirty or stained, especially with something unpleasant
例句：His reputation was besmutted by the scandal. 他的名誉被丑闻玷污了。

例句：The walls were besmutted with graffiti. 墙壁上涂满了污秽的涂鸦。

近义词：soil, stain, tarnish, sully, defile
反义词：clean, cleanse, purify
词性：v.


Etymology:
From be- + smut.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "all over."  "Smut" originally referred to soot or dirt, and later to obscenity.
