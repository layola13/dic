intertwining:/ˌɪntərˈtwaɪnɪŋ/
adjective
交织的，缠绕的
twisted together; interwoven.
The two lovers' lives became increasingly intertwined.  这两个恋人的生活越来越紧密地交织在一起。
interwoven, interlaced, entangled
separate, disentangled
adjective


intertwine:/ˌɪntərˈtwaɪn/
verb
交织，缠绕
twist or twine together.
The branches of the trees intertwined to form a natural arch.  树枝交织在一起，形成了一个天然的拱门。
interweave, interlace, entangle
separate, disentangle
verb


intertwining:/ˌɪntərˈtwaɪnɪŋ/
noun
交织，缠绕
the act of twisting or twining together.
The intertwining of their destinies was a recurring theme in the novel.  他们命运的交织是小说中反复出现的主题。
interweaving, interlacing, entanglement
separation, disentanglement
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between) + twine (to twist).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *twizwaz.
