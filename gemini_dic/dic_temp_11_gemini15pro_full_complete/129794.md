non-forgiving:/ˌnɒn fəˈɡɪvɪŋ/ |adj.|不宽恕的， unforgiving| This is a non-forgiving environment for mistakes.  这是一个不容许犯错的环境。| He has a non-forgiving nature. 他有不宽恕人的天性。| 近义词:unforgiving, relentless, implacable | 反义词: forgiving, merciful, lenient | 词性:形容词

词源:
"non-" (prefix) from Latin "non" meaning "not"
"forgiving" from Old English "forgiefan" meaning "to give up, remit, pardon"
