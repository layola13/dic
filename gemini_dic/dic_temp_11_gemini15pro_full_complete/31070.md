chamois:/ˈʃæmi/ /ʃæmˈwɑː/| n.| 羚羊, 麂皮|A type of small antelope native to mountains of Europe and the Caucasus, also known for its soft, pliable leather.|Example 1: The chamois leaped nimbly across the rocks. 羚羊轻巧地跳过岩石。

Example 2: She polished the car with a chamois cloth. 她用一块麂皮布擦了擦汽车。|近义词：antelope, goat, (for the leather) buckskin, suede|反义词:  (No direct opposite for the animal) |词性:noun

词源: From Old French *chamoiz, from Late Latin camox, from Gaulish *camox.
