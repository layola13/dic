brackened:/ˈbrækənd/| adj.|蕨类植物覆盖的；长满蕨类植物的|Covered with bracken; overgrown with ferns.|The hillsides were brackened and desolate. 山坡上长满了蕨类植物，一片荒凉。|例句：The brackened slopes provided good cover for the deer.  蕨类植物覆盖的山坡为鹿提供了良好的掩护。|近义词：ferny, overgrown|反义词：bare, clear|词性:形容词
词源：From bracken + -ed, meaning "covered in bracken."  Bracken itself comes from Old Norse *brakni. 由bracken + -ed组成，意思是“覆盖着蕨类植物”。Bracken本身来自古诺尔斯语*brakni。
