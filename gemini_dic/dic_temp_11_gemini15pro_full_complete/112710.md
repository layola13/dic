loxodromic:/ˌlɒksəˈdrɒmɪk/| adj.|斜航的，等角航线的|Of, relating to, or being a loxodrome; specifically:  making equal oblique angles with all meridians crossed.  A loxodromic curve on a sphere is a spiral that winds infinitely often around the poles. |A ship sailing a constant compass bearing follows a loxodromic curve. 一艘以恒定罗盘方位航行的船沿着等角航线航行。|The butterfly's migration pattern was surprisingly loxodromic. 蝴蝶的迁徙模式令人惊讶地呈螺旋状。|近义词：rhumb, spiraling|反义词:orthdromic|词性:adjective
