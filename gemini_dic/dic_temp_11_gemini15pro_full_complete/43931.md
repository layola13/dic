crewmanship:/ˈkruːmənʃɪp/
n.
团队合作精神；团队协作能力；船员素质
The skill and efficiency of a crew in working together.  The quality of teamwork and professionalism exhibited by a crew, especially in challenging situations.
The aircraft landed safely thanks to the pilot's skill and the crewmanship of the cabin crew.  由于飞行员的技术和机组人员的团队协作能力，飞机安全着陆。
近义词：teamwork, collaboration, coordination
反义词：discord, disharmony
