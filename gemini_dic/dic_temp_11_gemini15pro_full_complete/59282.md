eagles:/ˈiːɡlz/| n. |老鹰（复数）|Plural form of "eagle," referring to more than one eagle.  A large bird of prey with a hooked beak and powerful talons, known for its keen eyesight and soaring flight.|例句：The eagles soared high above the mountains. 老鹰在群山上空翱翔。

例句：We saw two eagles nesting near the river. 我们看到两只老鹰在河边筑巢。|近义词：raptors, birds of prey|反义词: (Eagles don't have a direct opposite, but perhaps "prey animals" could be considered contextually.)|词性:Noun
