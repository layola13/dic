befool:/bɪˈfuːl/
verb
愚弄，欺骗
To trick or deceive someone.
The con man befooled many people into investing in his fake company. 骗子愚弄许多人投资他的假公司。
They were befooled into thinking they had won the lottery. 他们被愚弄，以为自己中了彩票。
synonyms: deceive, trick, dupe, hoodwink, mislead
antonyms: enlighten, inform, educate
