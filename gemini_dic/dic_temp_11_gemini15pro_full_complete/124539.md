mountaintops:/ˈmaʊntɪnˌtɒps/ (US) /ˈmaʊntɪnˌtɒps/ (UK)| n. |山顶，山峰|The highest parts of mountains. |例句：The climbers reached the mountaintops after a long and arduous journey. 登山者经过漫长而艰辛的旅程到达了山顶。

例句：From the mountaintops, they could see the entire valley spread out below. 从山顶上，他们可以看到整个山谷展现在下面。|近义词：peaks, summits, crests|反义词:valleys, bases|名词
