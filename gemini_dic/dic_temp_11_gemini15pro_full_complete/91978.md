hypernationalistic:/ˌhaɪpərˈnæʃənəlˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 极端民族主义的 | Excessively or fanatically nationalistic; characterized by extreme nationalism.  |  The leader's hypernationalistic rhetoric stirred up dangerous sentiments.  这个领导人极端民族主义的言论激起了危险的情绪。 |  The country's hypernationalistic policies led to international isolation.  这个国家极端民族主义的政策导致了国际孤立。| 近义词: ultranationalistic, jingoistic, chauvinistic | 反义词: internationalist, cosmopolitan, globalist | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
hyper- (Greek: over, above) + nationalistic (from nation + -istic)
