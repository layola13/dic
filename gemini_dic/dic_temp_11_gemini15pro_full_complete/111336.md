liveableness:/ˈlɪvəbəlnəs/
n.
适居性，宜居性
The quality of a place that makes it easy and enjoyable to live in.  A place's liveableness is determined by factors like safety, affordability, and access to amenities.
The city boasts high liveableness due to its excellent public transportation and vibrant cultural scene.  这个城市因其优良的公共交通和充满活力的文化氛围而拥有高度的宜居性。

近义词:habitability, amenity, comfortability
反义词:uninhabitability, bleakness, desolation
词性:名词
