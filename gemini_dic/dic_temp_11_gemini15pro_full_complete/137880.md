nonviscous:/nɒnˈvɪskəs/ | adj. | 非粘性的，无粘性的 |  Not viscous; having little or no resistance to flow.  A nonviscous fluid flows easily. |  The lubricant used in the machine is nonviscous, allowing for smooth operation.  这种机器里使用的润滑剂是无粘性的，可以保证平稳运行。| Synonyms: inviscid, fluid, thin | Antonyms: viscous, thick, syrupy | adjective
