ethnographers:/ɛθˈnɒɡrəfərz/
n.
民族志学者，人种学者
A person who studies different cultures and societies, often by living among the people they are studying and observing their customs and behaviour.
Ethnographers spent years living with the tribe to understand their culture. 民族志学者们花了数年时间与该部落一起生活，以了解他们的文化。
Ethnographers documented the rituals and traditions of the community. 民族志学者记录了该社区的仪式和传统。
synonyms: anthropologists, cultural researchers, social scientists
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider someone who actively avoids or disregards other cultures)
