| 近义词: semiaquatic, dual | 反义词: terrestrial, aquatic | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin amphibius, from Greek amphibios "living a double life, living on land and in water," from amphi "on both sides" (see amphi-) + bios "life" (see bio-).
