kataf: /ˈkætæf/ | n. | 卡塔夫（一种阿拉伯语诗歌形式） |  A qasida or ode, especially a funeral elegy or lament, in classical Arabic poetry.  |  The poet recited a moving kataf for the fallen hero. 诗人为牺牲的英雄朗诵了一首感人的卡塔夫。 | 近义词: elegy, lament, qasida | 反义词:  paean, encomium | 词性: 名词


词源:

From Arabic قصيدة (qaṣīda, “ode”). Specifically قصيدة كِتَاب (qaṣīdat kitāb, “written ode”), referring to its formal, written nature.
