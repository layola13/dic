cullis:/ˈkʌlɪs/| n.|滤器，过滤器；（城堡等入口处的）吊闸|A strong grating or portcullis lowered to block a gateway to a castle or fortified town.|The heavy cullis creaked as it was raised, allowing the knights to enter the castle. 厚重的吊闸嘎吱作响地升了起来，让骑士们得以进入城堡。|近义词：portcullis, gate, grating|反义词:opening, entrance|noun

cullis:/ˈkʌlɪs/| n.|（尤指浓汤的）滤汤，清汤|A strained liquid, especially a rich broth of meat or fish.|The chef prepared a delicate cullis to accompany the roasted pheasant. 厨师准备了一份精致的清汤来搭配烤野鸡。|近义词：broth, stock, consommé|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "thick soup")|noun
