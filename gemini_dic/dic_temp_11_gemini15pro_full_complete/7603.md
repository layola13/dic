annoying:/əˈnɔɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 讨厌的，恼人的 | Causing irritation or annoyance. |  Her constant complaining is very annoying. 她不停地抱怨很烦人。

The annoying buzzing sound kept me awake all night. 恼人的嗡嗡声让我整夜没睡。

近义词: irritating, bothersome, vexing, troublesome, exasperating | 反义词: pleasant, agreeable, delightful, soothing, pleasing | 词性: adjective


词源:  From Old French *anoier "to annoy, vex, trouble, damage," from Vulgar Latin *inodiare "make loathsome," from Latin inodiare "to make hateful," from in- "into, upon" + odium "hatred."
