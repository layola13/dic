culm:/kʌlm/
n.
茎秆，秆；煤屑
The hollow stem of a grass or sedge.  Also, anthracite culm (a fine refuse of anthracite coal).
The farmer gathered the culms of the wheat after the harvest.  农民在收割后收集了麦秆。
Anthracite culm can be used as fuel. 无烟煤屑可以用作燃料。
近义词: stalk, stem
反义词: (No direct opposite for the plant part meaning. For the coal meaning, perhaps "lump")
