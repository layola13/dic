cumbersomeness:/ˈkʌmbərsəmnəs/
n.
笨重；累赘；麻烦
The cumbersomeness of the equipment made it difficult to transport.  这个设备的笨重使得运输很困难。
The cumbersomeness of the bureaucratic process frustrated everyone. 繁琐的官僚程序让每个人都很沮丧。
近义词:awkwardness, clumsiness, unwieldiness
反义词:handiness, convenience, portability
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cumbersome + -ness.  Cumbersome itself comes from Middle English  *comberous, from  comber "to encumber" (from Old French  encombrer) + -ous.
