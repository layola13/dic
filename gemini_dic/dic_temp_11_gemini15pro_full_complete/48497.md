deepmind:/diːpmaɪnd/| n. |深度思维|A British artificial intelligence company, acquired by Google in 2014.  It is known for its development of AlphaGo, a program that defeated a world champion Go player.|DeepMind has made significant advancements in AI research. 深度思维在人工智能研究方面取得了重大进展。|DeepMind created AlphaFold, a revolutionary protein folding prediction system. 深度思维创造了AlphaFold，一个革命性的蛋白质折叠预测系统。|近义词: Google AI, AI research lab|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology:
"Deep" refers to deep learning, a type of machine learning. "Mind" refers to intelligence and cognition. The name reflects the company's focus on developing artificial intelligence with a deep understanding of complex systems.
