meliphagous:/ˌmelɪˈfæɡəs/ /ˌmɛlɪˈfeɪɡəs/
adjective
食蜜的；以蜜为食的
Feeding on honey; honey-eating.
The meliphagous honeyeater flitted from flower to flower.  这只食蜜的吸蜜鸟在花丛中飞来飞去。
synonyms: nectarivorous, honey-eating
antonyms: carnivorous, herbivorous
