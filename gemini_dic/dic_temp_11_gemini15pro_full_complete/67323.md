extemporaneousness:/ɪkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəsnəs/
n.
即席性；即兴；无准备
The quality of being extemporaneous; impromptu or improvised character.
His extemporaneousness during the presentation was impressive, given the complex topic.  鉴于这个话题的复杂性，他在演讲中的即兴发挥令人印象深刻。
extemporaneousness:/ɪkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəsnəs/
n.
即兴；临场发挥；脱口而出
The state of being done or spoken without preparation.
The extemporaneousness of her speech made it all the more engaging. 她即兴的演讲使之更具吸引力。
近义词:impromptu, improvisation, off-the-cuff, spontaneity
反义词:preparedness, premeditation, rehearsal
词性:noun
