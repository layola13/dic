overshrink:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrɪŋk/ | v. |过度缩水，缩水过 much|To shrink excessively, usually referring to fabric.  The act of shrinking something too much, typically fabric, through washing or drying.|My new sweater overshrunk in the dryer and now it's too small for me. 我的新毛衣在烘干机里缩水过度，现在对我来说太小了。|The wool coat overshrunk after I washed it in hot water.  我的羊毛外套在热水洗涤后过度缩水了。|synonyms: shrink excessively, contract too much|antonyms: stretch, expand|verb
