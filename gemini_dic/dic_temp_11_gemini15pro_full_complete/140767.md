omnivorousnesses:/ˌɒmˈnɪvərəsnɪsɪz/
noun
杂食性
The state or quality of being omnivorous.
Example: The omnivorousnesses of pigs allows them to eat a wide variety of foods. 猪的杂食性使它们能够吃各种各样的食物。
Example:  The study examined the omnivorousnesses of different species of primates. 这项研究调查了不同种类灵长类动物的杂食性。
Synonym: omnivory
Antonym: herbivorousness, carnivorousness
