engrosses:/ɪnˈɡrəʊsɪz/ /ɪnˈɡroʊsɪz/
verb
全神贯注，使…着迷
occupies all the attention or interest of
The new video game completely engrosses him.  这款新电子游戏让他完全着迷。

engrossed:/ɪnˈɡrəʊst/ /ɪnˈɡroʊstɪd/
adjective
全神贯注的，着迷的
completely occupied or absorbed
He was so engrossed in his book that he didn't hear me come in. 他太专注于他的书了，以至于没听到我进来。

engrossment:/ɪnˈɡrəʊsmənt/ /ɪnˈɡroʊsmənt/
noun
全神贯注，着迷
the state of being engrossed
His engrossment in the movie was obvious. 他对电影的全神贯注显而易见。


近义词:absorb, captivate, engage, fascinate, immerse, occupy, preoccupy
反义词:bore, distract, tire
词性:verb (engrosses), adjective (engrossed), noun (engrossment)
词源:From Old French engrossir "to make thick, fatten," from en- "in" + grossir "to make large, thicken."  The meaning shifted to "absorb all one's attention" in the 16th century.
