gnomae:/ˈnoʊmiː/ | n. |格言，箴言；座右铭 |  A pithy saying or maxim; a motto.  Derived from the Greek "gnōmē," meaning "thought, opinion, judgment."  Often used in the plural form.  | The book was filled with ancient gnomae. 这本书充满了古老的箴言。 | synonyms: aphorism, adage, maxim, precept, saw | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "nonsense," "drivel," "fallacy") | noun
