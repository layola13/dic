frolic:/ˈfrɒlɪk/ (US) /ˈfrɑːlɪk/ (UK)| v. n.|嬉戏，玩耍；嬉戏的行为|To play and move about cheerfully; a playful action.|The lambs frolicked in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏。|近义词：play, romp, gambol, cavort|反义词：mope, sulk|词性:动词/名词
词源: From Middle Dutch wroelen "to move back and forth," of uncertain origin.
