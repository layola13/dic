coprocessing:/koʊˈprɑːsesɪŋ/| n.|协同处理，协作处理|The act of using two or more processors simultaneously to perform a single task, typically by assigning different parts of the task to different processors.|Coprocessing allows for faster and more efficient data processing. 协同处理能够实现更快、更高效的数据处理。|Coprocessing is often used in graphics cards to handle complex calculations. 协同处理常用于显卡以处理复杂的计算。|synonyms: parallel processing, concurrent processing|antonyms: serial processing|noun
