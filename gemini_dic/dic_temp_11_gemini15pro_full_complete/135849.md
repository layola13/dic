nonprosecutable:/ˌnɑːnˌprɑːsɪˈkjuːtəbl/
adjective
不可起诉的
Not suitable for or subject to prosecution.  Unable to be prosecuted, typically because of insufficient evidence or a legal technicality.
The case was deemed nonprosecutable due to lack of evidence.  由于缺乏证据，该案件被认为不可起诉。
The statute of limitations had expired, making the crime nonprosecutable.  诉讼时效已过，使得该罪行无法起诉。
近义词: unprosecutable, non-indictable
反义词: prosecutable, indictable
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
prosecute (verb): from Latin *prosequi "to follow, pursue," from pro- "forward" + sequi "to follow"
-able (suffix): meaning "capable of being"
