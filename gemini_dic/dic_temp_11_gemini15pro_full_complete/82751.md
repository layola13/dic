guiled:/ɡaɪld/
verb
欺骗，诱骗
having been tricked or deceived
例句：He was guiled into believing her lies. 他被骗相信了她的谎言。

例句：The unsuspecting tourists were guiled by the con artist.  毫无戒心的游客被骗子欺骗了。

近义词：deceived, tricked, fooled, misled, duped
反义词：undeceived, enlightened, informed
词性：v.
