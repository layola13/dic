frambaesia:/fræmˈbiːziə/| n. |雅司病，梅毒性雅司，热带梅毒|A chronic infectious tropical disease, closely related to syphilis, that mainly affects the skin, bones, and joints.  It's caused by the bacterium Treponema pallidum pertenue.|Example: Frambaesia is more common in young children in humid tropical areas. 雅司病在潮湿热带地区的幼儿中更为常见。
Example:  Early diagnosis and treatment of frambaesia are crucial to prevent long-term complications. 雅司病的早期诊断和治疗对于预防长期并发症至关重要。|近义词:yaws (most common synonym), pian, bouba, parangi|反义词:None (There is no direct antonym for a specific disease)|词性:noun
