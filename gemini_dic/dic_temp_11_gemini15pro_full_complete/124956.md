muffler:/ˈmʌflər/ (US), /ˈmʌflə(r)/ (UK)| n.|消声器；围巾；头巾|A device used to reduce noise, especially on a car's exhaust system, or a scarf worn around the neck for warmth.|例句：The car needs a new muffler. 这辆车需要一个新的消声器。

例句：She wrapped a thick muffler around her neck. 她脖子上围了一条厚厚的围巾。|近义词：silencer (for exhaust), scarf, neck warmer|反义词: amplifier (opposite of silencing)|词性:noun
