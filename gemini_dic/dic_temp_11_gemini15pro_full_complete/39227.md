conglutinative:/kənˈɡluːtɪnətɪv/ /kənˈɡluːtɪˌneɪtɪv/
adjective
粘着的；胶着的；会聚的
Relating to or denoting a language in which words are formed by stringing together morphemes, each of which corresponds as a whole to a single grammatical or semantic unit.  Many Native American languages are conglutinative.
Many Native American languages are conglutinative, building complex words by combining multiple morphemes.  许多美洲原住民语言是粘着语，通过组合多个语素来构成复杂的单词。

The language's conglutinative nature allows for the creation of highly specific and nuanced meanings.  这种语言的粘着特性允许创造高度特定和细致的含义。
