butterless:/ˈbʌtərləs/
adjective
无黄油的；不加黄油的
Without butter.  Lacking or containing no butter.
She ordered a butterless baked potato. 她点了一个不加黄油的烤土豆。
Near Synonyms: without butter, no butter
Opposite: buttered, with butter
词性: 形容词
