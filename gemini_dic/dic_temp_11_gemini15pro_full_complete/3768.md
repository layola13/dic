agitatingly:/ˈædʒɪteɪtɪŋli/
adverb
令人不安地；焦躁地
in a way that causes anxiety or disturbance.
Her hands fluttered agitatingly around her face. 她的双手在她脸部附近焦躁不安地挥舞。
近义词: nervously, anxiously, restlessly, disturbingly
反义词: calmly, peacefully, soothingly
词性: adverb

agitatingly:/ˈædʒɪteɪtɪŋli/
词源: From agitate + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *agitare, frequentative of agere "to drive, lead, act".
中文翻译: 令人不安地；焦躁地
英文解析: In a manner that causes someone to feel agitated or disturbed; in a way that stirs up or excites.
例句:
He paced the room agitatingly. 他焦躁不安地在房间里踱步。
The news spread agitatingly through the crowd.  这消息令人不安地在人群中传播开来。

近义词: disturbingly, upsettingly, nervously, anxiously
反义词: calmly, soothingly, peacefully, tranquilly
词性: adverb
