hematachometer:/ˌhiːmətækˈɒmɪtər/ | n. | 血流速度计 | An instrument for measuring the velocity of the blood current. |  The hematachometer provided valuable data on blood flow changes during exercise. 血流速度计提供了运动期间血流变化的宝贵数据。

The doctor used a hematachometer to assess the patient's circulatory health. 医生使用血流速度计来评估病人的循环系统健康状况。 | 近义词:rheometer, blood flow meter | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *haima*, *haimat-*  (blood) + *takhos* (speed) + *-meter* (instrument for measuring).
