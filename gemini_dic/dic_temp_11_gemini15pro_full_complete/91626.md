hyperbaric:/ˌhaɪpərˈbærɪk/| adj.|高压的，超过常压的|Relating to or involving increased pressure, especially greater than normal atmospheric pressure.|例句：Hyperbaric oxygen therapy can be used to treat carbon monoxide poisoning.  高压氧疗可以用来治疗一氧化碳中毒。

例句：Divers working at great depths breathe hyperbaric air.  在很深的地方工作的潜水员呼吸高压空气。|近义词：pressurized, compressed|反义词：hypobaric|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- "above, beyond" + -baric "pressure," from Greek baros "weight."
