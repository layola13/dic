/ˈmiː tuːɪzəm/ | n. |米兔运动；我也是运动| The practice of publicly accusing someone of sexual harassment or assault, especially by using the hashtag #MeToo.  It refers to the widespread phenomenon of women coming forward with stories of sexual harassment and assault, often sparked by allegations against a prominent figure. |例句：The #MeToo movement has empowered many survivors to speak out.  #MeToo运动让许多幸存者有勇气说出自己的经历。

例句：Me-tooism has brought about significant changes in workplace culture. 米兔运动给职场文化带来了显著的变化。|近义词：sexual harassment allegations, accusations of sexual misconduct |反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "silence" or "cover-up" in the context of these accusations) |词性: noun
