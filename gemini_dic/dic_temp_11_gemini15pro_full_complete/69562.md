fells:/fɛlz/
n.|荒野，丘陵地带|A high barren or stony moorland, especially in northern England. |The shepherd guided his flock across the fells.|近义词：moors, uplands, hills|反义词:valleys, lowlands|名词

fells:/fɛlz/
v. (fell的第三人称单数形式)|砍倒，打倒|(of a tree) cut down; (of a person) knock down.|The lumberjack felled the giant redwood. 伐木工人砍倒了那棵巨大的红杉树。|近义词：cuts down, knocks down, chops down|反义词:plant, erect|动词
