leveeing: /ˈlɛviɪŋ/
n.
筑堤；征税；征兵
the act of building a levee or embankment.  Also, the act of collecting a levy (tax or fee). Sometimes used figuratively to mean gathering or assembling something.
The leveeing of the riverbanks prevented flooding.  筑堤保护了河岸免受洪水侵袭。

The leveeing of a new tax caused public outcry.  新税的征收引起了公众的强烈抗议。

近义词:embanking, diking, raising, building, constructing, assembling, gathering, collecting
反义词:demolishing, destroying, dismantling
