dynam-/ˈdaɪnəm/ | n. |达因, 动力单位| A unit of force equal to the force that imparts an acceleration of one centimeter per second per second to a mass of one gram.| The force was measured in dynes.  这个力是用达因来测量的。 | synonyms: unit of force | antonyms: N/A | noun
