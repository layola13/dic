ostensibly:/ɑːˈstensɪbli/ /ɒˈstensɪbli/| adv.|表面上；貌似；看来| appearing or claiming to be one thing when it is really something else |He was ostensibly visiting a friend, but he was actually spying on her. 他表面上是去拜访朋友，但实际上是在监视她。|近义词：apparently, seemingly, outwardly, purportedly|反义词：actually, genuinely, truly, really|词性:副词

词源: From Late Latin *ostensībilis*, from Latin ostendere (“to show, display”), from ob- (“before, in front of”) + tendere (“to stretch”).
