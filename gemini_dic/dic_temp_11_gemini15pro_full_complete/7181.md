angio:/ˈændʒiːoʊ/
prefix
血管的；淋巴管的
Relating to blood vessels or lymph vessels.
Angiogenesis is the formation of new blood vessels. 血管生成是新血管的形成过程。
Angiography is a medical imaging technique used to visualize the inside, or lumen, of blood vessels and organs of the body, with particular interest in the arteries, veins, and the heart chambers. 血管造影是一种医学成像技术，用于观察体内血管和器官的内部或管腔，尤其关注动脉、静脉和心腔。
近义词: vaso-
反义词: None
词性: prefix (前缀)
