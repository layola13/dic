overfur:/ˈoʊvərˌfɜːr/
n.|过度毛皮，过多的毛皮|A thick or excessive growth of fur, especially on an animal.  This word is not commonly used and may be considered non-standard.|The rabbit had an overfur of thick, matted hair. 兔子长了一层厚厚的、乱蓬蓬的毛。|近义词：overgrowth, excess fur|反义词:thin fur, sparse fur|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
'Over-' (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) meaning "above, beyond, across" + 'fur' (Old French forre, from Frankish *foder) meaning "lining, sheath."  The word implies an excessive amount of fur, going "over" or "beyond" the usual amount.
