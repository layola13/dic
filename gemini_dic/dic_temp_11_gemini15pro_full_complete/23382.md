brachiosaurus:/ˌbrækiəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |腕龙|A genus of gigantic sauropod dinosaurs of the Late Jurassic period, having a very long neck and forelegs that were longer than the hind legs.  It's one of the largest animals known to have walked the earth.|例句：The brachiosaurus was a herbivore, feeding on plants high in the trees. 腕龙是食草动物，以树上的高大植物为食。

例句：The museum's centerpiece is a complete brachiosaurus skeleton.  博物馆的中心展品是一具完整的腕龙骨架。|近义词：sauropod, dinosaur|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
