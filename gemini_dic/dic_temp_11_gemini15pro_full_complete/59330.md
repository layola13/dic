earl:/ɜːrl/ | n. |伯爵；英国贵族的爵位|A British nobleman ranking above a viscount and below a marquess. |例句：The Earl of Essex was a favorite of Queen Elizabeth I.  埃塞克斯伯爵是伊丽莎白一世女王的宠臣。

例句：He was given the title of Earl for his services to the crown. 他因对王室的贡献而被授予伯爵爵位。|近义词：count, nobleman |反义词:commoner|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *eorl (unattested), from Old Norse jarl "chieftain, warrior, nobleman," from Proto-Germanic *erlaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *heros "protector." Cognate with Danish and Swedish jarl.  Replaced Old English ealdorman.
