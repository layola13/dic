deltic:/ˈdɛltɪk/| adj.|三角洲的；三角形的|Relating to or shaped like a delta, especially the Greek letter delta (Δ).|The deltic formation of migrating birds was breathtaking. 迁徙鸟类形成的三角形队列令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: triangular, deltaic, |Antonyms: circular, round|adjective


deltic:/ˈdɛltɪk/| n.|三角肌；三角形的东西|A delta-shaped object; a deltoid muscle.|The deltic muscle in his shoulder was strained. 他肩膀上的三角肌拉伤了。|Synonyms: delta, triangle, deltoid|Antonyms: N/A|noun

Etymology: From Greek *dēlta* (Δ), the fourth letter of the Greek alphabet, which is triangular in shape.
