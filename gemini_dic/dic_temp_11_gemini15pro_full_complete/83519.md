haddest:/ˈhædɪst/| v.|（have的过去完成时第二人称单数形式）拥有过；经历过|You haddest already left when I arrived. 你在我到达之前就已经离开了。|近义词:possessed, owned, experienced|反义词:lacked|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *haddest, *haddis, from Old English hæfdest (second-person singular past indicative of habban), from Proto-Germanic *habdaz.

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I have a book. = 我有一本书。
She has a car. = 她有一辆车。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am having a good time. = 我玩得很开心。
She is having lunch. = 她在吃午饭。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have had a busy day. = 我度过了忙碌的一天。
She has had enough. = 她受够了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been having trouble sleeping. = 我一直有睡眠问题。
She has been having headaches. = 她一直头痛。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I had a dream last night. = 我昨晚做了一个梦。
She had a meeting this morning. = 她今天早上开了一个会。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was having dinner when you called. = 你打电话的时候我正在吃晚饭。
She was having a bath. = 她在洗澡。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had had a long day before I met you. = 在我见到你之前，我度过了漫长的一天。
She had had several jobs before this one. = 在这份工作之前，她做过几份工作。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been having problems with my computer for weeks. = 我的电脑已经出问题好几个星期了。
She had been having nightmares. = 她一直做噩梦。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will have a party next week. = 我下周要举办一个派对。
She will have a new phone soon. = 她很快就会有一部新手机。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be having dinner with friends tomorrow. = 我明天要和朋友一起吃晚饭。
She will be having a meeting with her boss. = 她将要和她的老板开会。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have had enough time to finish the project by then. = 到那时，我将有足够的时间完成这个项目。
She will have had her birthday by the time we see her. = 我们见到她的时候，她就已经过完生日了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been having driving lessons for a year by next month. = 到下个月，我就已经上了一年的驾驶课了。
She will have been having therapy for two years. = 到那时，她将已经接受了两年的治疗。


虚拟语气:
If I had had more time, I would have gone to the concert. = 如果我有更多的时间，我就会去听音乐会了。
I wish I had had more money. = 我希望我以前有更多钱。

被动语态:  (have 的被动语态通常用"be had")
The car was had by my father. (这种用法比较少见) 车是我父亲买的。
A good time was had by all. 大家都玩得很开心。（更常见的用法）

疑问句:
Did you have a good day? = 你今天过得好吗？
Had she finished her work before leaving? = 她离开前完成工作了吗？

否定句:
I did not have enough time. = 我没有足够的时间。
She hadn't seen him before. = 她以前没见过他。
