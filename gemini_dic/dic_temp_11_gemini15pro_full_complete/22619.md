booziest:/ˈbuːziɪst/| adj.|最醉的，最爱喝酒的|Superlative form of boozy: most boozy.  It describes someone or something related to a high level of alcohol consumption, or a place where a lot of alcohol is consumed. |例句：It was the booziest party I've ever been to. 这是我去过的最醉人的派对。|例句：He's the booziest guy in the office. 他是办公室里最爱喝酒的人。|近义词：drunken, intoxicated, inebriated|反义词:sober, temperate|词性:形容词

词源: From boozy + -est (superlative suffix).  Boozy itself comes from booze (alcoholic drink), which is of uncertain origin, possibly from Middle Dutch *būsen (to drink heavily).
