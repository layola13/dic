balds:/bɔːldz/| n.|秃头，秃顶|A balding or bald area on the head; a bare patch in a field of crops or vegetation.|The man's balds were clearly visible in the sunlight.  这个男人的秃顶在阳光下清晰可见。|Synonyms: bald spots, bare patches|Antonyms: thick hair, full head of hair|noun

balds:/bɔːldz/| v. (third-person singular simple present balds, present participle balding, simple past and past participle balded)|秃顶，变秃|To become bald; to lose hair.|He is balding rapidly. 他正在迅速秃顶。|Synonyms: lose hair, go bald|Antonyms: grow hair|verb
