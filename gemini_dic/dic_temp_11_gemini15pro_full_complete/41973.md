cosset:/ˈkɒsɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːsɪt/ (US)| n. v.| 娇惯的人；宠儿|A person who is excessively pampered or indulged.  To treat someone as a cosset; to pamper. |例句：She was a cosset, used to getting her own way. 她是个娇惯的孩子，习惯于为所欲为。|近义词：pamper, indulge, spoil, coddle |反义词: neglect, ignore|词性: 名词, 动词

cosset sb from sth =  保护某人不受某事的影响 (较为少见)

词源:
From the verb 'cosset', derived from Old North French 'acoster' meaning "to come alongside".  The original meaning referred to keeping lambs close to the fireside for warmth and care, hence the modern sense of pampering.
