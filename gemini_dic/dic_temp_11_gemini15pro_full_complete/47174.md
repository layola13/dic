dawsonite:/ˈdɔːsənˌaɪt/ | n. |片钠铝石|A carbonate mineral, NaAlCO3(OH)2, occurring as a white to gray orthorhombic crystal.| Dawsonite is often found in low-temperature hydrothermal veins.  片钠铝石常发现于低温热液矿脉中。|Synonyms:  soda aluminum carbonate hydroxide | Antonyms: None | noun
