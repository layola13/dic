marshes:/ˈmɑːrʃɪz/ /ˈmɑːrʃəz/
n.
沼泽，湿地
An area of low-lying land that is flooded in wet seasons or at high tide, and typically remains waterlogged at all times.
The air was thick with the smell of the marshes.  空气中弥漫着沼泽地的气味。
swamps, bogs, fens
deserts, uplands
noun
