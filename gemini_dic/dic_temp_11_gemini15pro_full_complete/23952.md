breasting: /ˈbrɛstɪŋ/ | v. |  挺胸迎着, 与…齐胸高,  |  Moving forward with one's chest leading the way, often against resistance like wind or water. Also, being level with the height of one's chest. | The swimmer was breasting the waves with powerful strokes.  游泳运动员用强有力的划水姿势迎着波浪前进。

The snow was breasting the horses' chests.  雪积到了马的胸部那么高。 | 近义词: confronting, facing, meeting | 反义词: retreating, withdrawing | 词性: 动词
