eventuating:/ɪˈvɛntʃueɪtɪŋ/
verb
最终导致；结果是
Resulting in; culminating in.
The investigation eventually eventuated in the arrest of the suspect. 调查最终导致了嫌疑人的逮捕。
Synonyms: result in, culminate in, lead to, end in
Antonyms: begin, commence, initiate
词性: 动词
