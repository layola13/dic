gallipot:/ˈɡælɪpɒt/ (UK) /ˈɡælɪˌpɑːt/ (US)| n. |小药罐，软膏罐|A small glazed earthenware or porcelain pot, especially one used by apothecaries for ointments or medicines.|The apothecary carefully measured out the herbs and placed them in a gallipot.|近义词：jar, pot, container|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *galipot, from Old North French *galipot, probably a blend of Old French galie "galley, ship" and pot "pot." The original meaning referred to a resin collected from pine trees, often used to waterproof ships. Later, the term was transferred to the small pots used to store this resin and other medicinal substances.
