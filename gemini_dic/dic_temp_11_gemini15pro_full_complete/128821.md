nightmare:/ˈnaɪtˌmer/ /ˈnaɪtˌmɛər/| n. |噩梦，梦魇；可怕的经历，可怕的事情|A bad dream that causes feelings of fear and anxiety. Also, a very unpleasant and upsetting experience.|例句：I had a terrible nightmare last night. 昨晚我做了一个可怕的噩梦。

|例句：The traffic jam was a nightmare.  交通堵塞简直就是一场噩梦。|近义词：bad dream, horror, ordeal|反义词:daydream, pleasant dream|词性:noun
