jump-off:/ˈdʒʌmp ɒf/| n.|起跳点；出发点；(比赛的)开始|A jump-off is a place from which someone or something jumps. It can also refer to the starting point of a race or competition.|The ski jump provided a thrilling jump-off for the athletes.  滑雪跳台为运动员提供了一个惊险的起跳点。

The jump-off for the marathon is at the city hall. 马拉松的起跳点在市政厅。|近义词：starting point, beginning, outset|反义词:finish, end|noun


jump-off:/ˈdʒʌmp ɒf/| adj.|开始的；决定性的|Used to describe something that marks the beginning of an event or process, often a decisive one.|The jump-off round in the equestrian competition determined the winner.  马术比赛的附加赛决定了获胜者。


This meeting serves as a jump-off point for our new project. 这次会议是我们新项目的起点。|近义词：initial, beginning, starting|反义词:concluding, final|adjective
