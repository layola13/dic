heroine:/ˈherəʊɪn/ (UK) /ˈheroʊɪn/ (US)| n. |女英雄；女主角|A woman admired or idealized for her courage, outstanding achievements, or noble qualities; a female protagonist in a story, play, or movie.|例句：Joan of Arc is considered a heroine by many.  很多人认为圣女贞德是一位女英雄。

例句：The heroine of the novel overcomes many obstacles to achieve her goals.  小说中的女主人公克服了许多障碍，最终实现了她的目标。|近义词：champion, protagonist, star, icon|反义词：villain, antagonist|词性：名词
