basemap:/ˈbeɪsmæp/| n. |底图，基础地图|A basemap is a map depicting background reference information such as landforms, roads, landmarks, and political boundaries, on top of which other thematic information is layered.  It provides a context for other data.|Example: The basemap showed the major roads and rivers of the region. 底图显示了该地区的主要道路和河流。|Example:  They used a satellite image as a basemap for their geological survey. 他们用卫星图像作为地质勘探的底图。|近义词：background map, reference map|反义词: thematic map|词性: noun
