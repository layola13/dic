deliciousness:/dɪˈlɪʃəsnəs/
n.
美味;可口
the quality of being delicious; great taste.
The cake was baked to perfection, and its deliciousness was undeniable.  这个蛋糕烤得完美极了，它的美味无可否认。
近义词:taste, flavor, savor, savoriness
反义词:unpleasantness, distastefulness
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From delicious + -ness.  Delicious comes from Middle French delicieux, from Latin deliciosus "delightful, luxurious, dainty," from deliciae "pleasures, delights," from delicere "to allure, entice, delight," related to lacere "to entice, deceive."
