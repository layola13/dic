ingeniously:/ɪnˈdʒiːniəsli/| adv.|巧妙地，精巧地，独创地|In a clever, original, and inventive manner. |例句：The puzzle was ingeniously designed.  这个谜题设计得非常巧妙。

例句：She ingeniously solved the problem with limited resources. 她巧妙地利用有限的资源解决了问题。|近义词：cleverly, skillfully, inventively, creatively, resourcefully|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly|词性:副词

词源:
From Late Latin *ingeniōsus, from Latin ingenium (“natural capacity, talent, intelligence”) + -ōsus.
