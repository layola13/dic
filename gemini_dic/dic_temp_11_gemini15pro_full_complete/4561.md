alchemize:/ˈælkɪmaɪz/| v. |使发生彻底改变；将…点石成金|To transform something, often in a seemingly magical or profound way. To change a base metal into gold (in alchemy).|例句：He tried to alchemize his grief into poetry. 他试图将悲痛转化为诗歌。

例句：The company hopes to alchemize data into actionable insights. 公司希望将数据转化为可操作的见解。|近义词：transform, transmute, convert, change, metamorphose|反义词：preserve, maintain, stagnate|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *alchimizare*, from Arabic al-kīmiyā’ (“alchemy”).
