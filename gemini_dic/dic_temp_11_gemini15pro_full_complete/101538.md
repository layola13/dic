ironed-out:/ˈaɪrnd aʊt/| adj.|消除的，解决的，平整的|Having been made smooth, uncreased, or resolved; free of problems or difficulties.  The literal meaning refers to removing wrinkles from fabric with an iron, but figuratively it means to eliminate difficulties or resolve problems.|例句：After a long discussion, all the problems were ironed-out. 经过长时间的讨论，所有问题都解决了。|例句：The contract still has a few details to be ironed-out. 合同还有一些细节需要敲定。|近义词：resolved, settled, smoothed out, sorted out|反义词:complicated, unresolved, messy|词性:形容词
