buhlbuhl:/buːlbuːl/ | n. | 夜莺，画眉鸟 | A songbird of the thrush family, found in Africa and Asia, typically having a brown back, a pale front, and a melodious song. | The buhlbuhl's song filled the air with its sweet melody. 画眉鸟的歌声以其甜美的旋律充满了空气。 |  The buhlbuhl flitted from branch to branch. 画眉鸟在树枝间飞来飞去。 | 近义词: nightingale, songbird | 反义词: crow, raven | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Hindi बुलबुल (bulbul), from Persian بلبل (bolbol), ultimately imitative of the bird's song.
