obsequiousnesses:/ˌɒbˈsiːkwiəsnɪsɪz/
n.
过分殷勤，奴颜婢膝，谄媚
Excessive flattery or subservience.
His obsequiousnesses towards the boss were embarrassing to witness. 他对老板的奴颜婢膝令人尴尬。
Synonyms: servility, subservience, fawning, sycophancy
Antonyms: assertiveness, independence, defiance
noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin obsequiosus (“compliant, obedient”), from Latin obsequium (“compliance”), from ob- (“towards”) + sequi (“to follow”).
