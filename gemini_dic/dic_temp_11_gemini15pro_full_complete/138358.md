notifications:/ˌnoʊtɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |通知，通告|  The act of notifying or informing; the state of being notified. A message or other signal that delivers information. | I received several notifications on my phone. 我手机上收到了好几条通知。| synonyms: alerts, announcements, advisories, warnings | antonyms: silence, secrecy | noun
