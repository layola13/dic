disciferous:/dɪˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
持盘的；有盘状物的
bearing discs or disc-like structures.
The disciferous coral displayed a vibrant array of colors.  持盘珊瑚展现出一系列鲜艳的色彩。
The botanist examined the disciferous plant, noting its unique structure.  植物学家检查了这种有盘状物的植物，并注意到其独特的结构。
近义词:discoid, disc-shaped
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the lack of discs, e.g., "smooth," "undulating")
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *discus* (disc) + *-fer* (bearing).
