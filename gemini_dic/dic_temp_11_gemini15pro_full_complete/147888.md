overrealism:/ˈoʊvərˈriːəlɪzəm/ | n. | 超现实主义; 过度现实主义 | A genre of art characterized by depicting reality with exaggerated detail or intensity, often to a disturbing or unsettling effect.  It goes beyond surrealism by focusing on the mundane aspects of life and amplifying them to create a sense of unease or alienation. | The artist's overrealism captured the bleakness of urban life in unsettling detail.  这位艺术家的超现实主义以令人不安的细节捕捉了城市生活的凄凉。

The film's overrealism made the ordinary seem grotesque. 这部电影的超现实主义使普通事物显得怪诞。 | 近义词: hyperrealism, photorealism | 反义词: idealism, abstraction | 词性: noun
