chyluria: /kaɪˈljʊəriə/ /kaɪˈlʊriə/
noun
乳糜尿
The presence of chyle in the urine, giving it a milky appearance.  This is usually due to a blockage or disruption of the lymphatic system.
Chylous ascites and chyluria are rare complications of abdominal surgery. 腹水和乳糜尿是腹部手术的罕见并发症。
