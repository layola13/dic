geeks:/ɡiːks/| n.|极客，怪才；对某一领域痴迷的人|A person who is very knowledgeable and enthusiastic about a particular subject, especially one related to technology or computing.  They are often seen as being socially awkward or unconventional.|He's a real computer geek. 他是个真正的电脑极客。|近义词：nerd, enthusiast, buff|反义词:normie, layman|Noun
