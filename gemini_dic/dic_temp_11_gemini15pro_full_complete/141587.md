oppressor:/əˈprɛsər/| n. |压迫者，暴君|A person or group that oppresses someone or something.  Someone who subjects others to cruel or unjust treatment or control.|The dictator was a cruel oppressor who denied his people basic human rights.  这个独裁者是一个残酷的压迫者，剥夺了他的人民的基本人权。|Synonyms: tyrant, despot, dictator, autocrat|Antonyms: liberator, emancipator|noun
