euphemist:/ˈjuːfəmɪst/ | n. |委婉语使用者，婉言者 | A person who uses euphemisms.  A euphemist often substitutes a mild, indirect, or vague expression for one thought to be offensive, harsh, or blunt. | The euphemist referred to the layoffs as "right-sizing" the company.  这家婉言者将裁员称为公司的“规模调整”。| synonym:  understater,  whitewasher | antonym:  blunt speaker,  straight-talker | noun
