microtubular:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈtjuːbjələr/| adj.|微管的|Relating to or consisting of microtubules, which are microscopic hollow tubes found in most cells.|Microtubular proteins play a crucial role in cell division. 微管蛋白在细胞分裂中起着至关重要的作用。|Microtubular structures are essential for maintaining cell shape. 微管结构对于维持细胞形状至关重要。|近义词:tubular|反义词: None|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + tubular.  "Micro-" comes from Greek "mikros" meaning "small". "Tubular" comes from Latin "tubulus" meaning "small pipe".
