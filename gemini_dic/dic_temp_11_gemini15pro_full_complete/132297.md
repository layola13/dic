nondefiance:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪəns/ | n. | 不反抗，顺从 | The hostage's nondefiance ensured his safety.  |  Nondefiance is the opposite of resistance or disobedience. It implies a passive acceptance of authority or a situation.  |  His nondefiance to the robber's demands saved his life. 他对强盗要求的顺从救了他的命。

The dog showed nondefiance when its owner scolded it.  这只狗在主人责骂它时表现出顺从。|  obedience, submission, compliance, acquiescence | defiance, resistance, rebellion | noun
