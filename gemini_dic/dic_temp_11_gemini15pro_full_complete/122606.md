modernizer:/ˈmɒdənaɪzə(r) (UK), ˈmɑːdərnaɪzər (US)| n.|现代化者，革新者|A person or thing that modernizes something.|The company hired a modernizer to update their computer systems. 这家公司聘请了一位革新者来更新他们的计算机系统。|近义词: innovator, reformer, developer|反义词: traditionalist, conservative|词性:noun

modernize:/ˈmɒdənaɪz (UK), ˈmɑːdərnaɪz (US)| v.|使现代化，更新|To make something more modern; update.|They are modernizing the factory by installing new machinery. 他们正在通过安装新机器使工厂现代化。|近义词: update, upgrade, renovate, innovate|反义词: preserve, maintain, keep|词性:verb

modern:/ˈmɒdən (UK), ˈmɑːdərn (US)| adj.|现代的，近代的|Relating to the present or recent times as opposed to the remote past.|The building has a modern design. 这栋建筑具有现代化的设计。|近义词: contemporary, current, latest, new|反义词: ancient, old, outdated, traditional|词性:adjective

词源: From the French word *moderne*, ultimately from Late Latin *modernus*, meaning "of now".
