outpraised: /ˌaʊtˈpreɪzd/
verb
超越赞美；胜过赞美
having received more praise than someone or something else
The new opera outpraised the previous one. 这部新歌剧比上一部更受赞誉。
Synonyms: surpassed, exceeded, outdone, outshone
Antonyms: underpraised, underrated
词性：动词
