interruptingly:/ˌɪntəˈrʌptɪŋli/ | adv. | 打断地，干扰地 | In a manner that interrupts; by causing a disruption or break in continuity. | He spoke interruptingly, making it hard to follow his train of thought. 他断断续续地说着，让人很难跟上他的思路。 |  She kept interruptingly asking questions during the presentation.  在演示过程中，她不停地打断提问。 | synonyms: disruptively, intrusively, obtrusively | antonyms: smoothly, continuously, politely | adverb
