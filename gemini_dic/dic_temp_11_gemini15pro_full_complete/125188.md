multicell:/ˌmʌlˈtiˌsɛl/| adj. |多细胞的| Composed of multiple cells; having more than one cell. |Multicell organisms are more complex than single-celled organisms. 多细胞生物比单细胞生物更复杂。|例句：
Multicellular organisms exhibit greater complexity than unicellular organisms.
多细胞生物比单细胞生物表现出更大的复杂性。

The human body is a multicellular organism.
人体是一个多细胞生物。|近义词：pluricellular|反义词：unicellular|词性:形容词
