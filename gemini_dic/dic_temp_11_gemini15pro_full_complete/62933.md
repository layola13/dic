enmist:/ˈɛnmɪst/ | n. | 敌人，仇敌 | An enemy; a foe. | He saw her as his enmist after the betrayal. 他在背叛后视她为仇敌。|  The two countries were formerly enmists but now maintain a fragile peace. 这两个国家以前是敌人，但现在维持着脆弱的和平。 | 近义词：enemy, foe, adversary, opponent, rival | 反义词：friend, ally, supporter | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *enmist, enemist, from Old French enemist, enemi, from Latin inimīcus (“unfriendly, hostile; personal enemy, foe”), from in- (“not”) + amīcus (“friend”).
