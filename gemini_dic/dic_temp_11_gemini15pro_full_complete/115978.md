marsala:/mɑːrˈsɑːlə/ /mɑːrˈsælə/
n.|玛萨拉酒，玛萨拉色|A fortified wine from Sicily, Italy, similar to sherry. Also, a brownish-red color resembling this wine.|例句：We had chicken marsala for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了玛萨拉鸡肉。

例句：She wore a beautiful marsala dress to the wedding. 她穿了一件漂亮的玛萨拉色连衣裙去参加婚礼。|近义词：wine, color, brownish-red, reddish-brown|反义词: (无明确反义词，可以考虑颜色反义词，例如 light colors)|词性:noun
