chancery: /ˈtʃɑːnsəri/ (US) /ˈtʃɑːnsəri/ (UK)| n.| 大法官法庭；衡平法院；档案馆；（某些大学的）校长办公室|A court of equity, as distinguished from a common-law court.  A record office. The office of a chancellor, especially in a university.|The case was heard in chancery.  这个案件在大法官法庭审理。|Synonyms: court, tribunal, court of equity|Antonyms: N/A|noun

chancery: /ˈtʃɑːnsəri/ (US) /ˈtʃɑːnsəri/ (UK)| adj. (古)| 大法官法庭的；衡平法的|Relating to a court of equity or the system of law administered by it.|This is a chancery case. 这是一个衡平法案件。|Synonyms: equitable|Antonyms: legal (in the sense of common law)|adjective
