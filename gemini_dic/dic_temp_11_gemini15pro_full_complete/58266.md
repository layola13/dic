drumsticks:/ˈdrʌmˌstɪks/ | n. |鼓槌; 鸡腿; |A pair of sticks used for playing a drum. Also, the lower part of a cooked chicken leg. | He twirled the drumsticks in his hands before the performance. 他在表演前转动手中的鼓槌。|  She grabbed a drumstick and started eating. 她抓起一个鸡腿开始吃。| 近义词：(for musical instrument) drumstick, mallet, beater; (for food) chicken leg, drumstick | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
