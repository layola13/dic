dowdily:/ˈdaʊdɪli/ | adv. | 邋遢地，不整洁地 | In a dowdy or shabby manner; lacking smartness or elegance. | She dressed dowdily in an old, faded dress. 她穿着一条褪色的旧裙子，显得邋遢。| 近义词：shabbily, frumpily, unfashionably | 反义词：smartly, elegantly, fashionably | 词性: 副词

dowdy:/ˈdaʊdi/ | adj. | 邋遢的，不整洁的 |  Lacking smartness or elegance; shabby. |  She looked dowdy in her old coat. 她穿着旧外套看起来很邋遢。| 近义词：shabby, frumpily, unfashionable | 反义词：smart, elegant, fashionable | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the noun "dowd," meaning "an unfashionable or dowdily dressed woman," of uncertain origin.
