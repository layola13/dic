diversifiabilities:/dɪˌvɜːrsɪfaɪəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 多样化能力，可分散性 |  The quality or state of being diversifiable; the capacity for diversification.  This often refers to the extent to which investments, business activities, or other aspects of a portfolio can be spread across different areas to reduce risk. |  The company's diversifiabilities allow it to weather economic downturns. 这家公司的多元化能力使其能够抵御经济衰退。 | Synonyms: diversifiability, flexibility, adaptability | Antonyms: inflexibility, rigidity, concentration | noun
