nonflexible:/ˌnɑːnˈflɛksəbəl/
adjective
不灵活的，僵硬的
not flexible; rigid or unadaptable.
The company's nonflexible policies made it difficult for employees to take time off.  这家公司僵化的政策使得员工很难请假。
His nonflexible attitude made negotiations difficult.  他不灵活的态度使谈判变得困难。
inflexible, rigid, stiff, unyielding
flexible, adaptable, pliable, supple
