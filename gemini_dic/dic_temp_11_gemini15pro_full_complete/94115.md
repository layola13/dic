impeccably:/ɪmˈpɛkəbli/| adv.|完美地；无可挑剔地|In a flawless or faultless manner; perfectly.|She was impeccably dressed for the occasion. 她为这个场合穿得无可挑剔。|近义词：perfectly, flawlessly, faultlessly|反义词：poorly, badly, inadequately|词性:副词
impeccable:/ɪmˈpɛkəbəl/| adj.|无瑕疵的；完美的|Free from fault or error; perfect.|His English is impeccable. 他的英语无可挑剔。|近义词：perfect, flawless, faultless|反义词：faulty, flawed, imperfect|词性:形容词

词源:
Late Middle English: from late Latin impeccabilis, from in- ‘not’ + peccare ‘to sin’.
