dewy-eyed:/ˈdjuːi aɪd/ | adj. | 泪汪汪的，湿润的 | Having eyes wet with tears, often due to sentimentality or emotion.  This implies a gentle, innocent, or naive emotional state. | She gave him a dewy-eyed look of adoration. 她含情脉脉地望着他，眼里充满了崇拜。| 近义词：teary-eyed, misty-eyed, sentimental | 反义词：hard-hearted, cynical | 词性: adjective

dewy-eyed:/ˈdjuːi aɪd/ | adj. | 天真的，幼稚的 |  Figuratively used to describe someone innocent or naive. |  He had a dewy-eyed view of the world, unaware of its harsh realities.  他对世界抱有天真的看法，没有意识到世界的残酷现实。| 近义词：naive, innocent, unsophisticated | 反义词：worldly, sophisticated, experienced | 词性: adjective
