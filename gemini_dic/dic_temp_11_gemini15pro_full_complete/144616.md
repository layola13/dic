outwiggled:/ˌaʊtˈwɪɡəld/
verb
扭动得比...厉害; 以扭动胜过
To surpass or defeat someone by wiggling or maneuvering more effectively.
The dancer outwiggled his competitors with his energetic and fluid movements.  这位舞者以其充满活力和流畅的动作胜过了他的竞争对手。
The fish outwiggled the hook and escaped. 这条鱼扭动着摆脱了鱼钩，逃走了。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, outflank, surpass, defeat
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
