decompile:/ˌdiːkəmˈpaɪl/| v. |反编译|To convert (computer code) from low-level, difficult to understand machine language back into a higher-level language more easily understood by programmers.|The programmer decompiled the software to understand how it worked. 程序员反编译了软件以了解其工作原理。|近义词:reverse engineer, disassemble|反义词:compile|词性:动词
