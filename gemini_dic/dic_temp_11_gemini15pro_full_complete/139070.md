oath:/oʊθ/| n.|誓言，誓约；诅咒，咒骂|a solemn promise, often invoking a divine witness, regarding one's future action or behavior; a curse or profane exclamation.|He took an oath of allegiance to the king. 他向国王宣誓效忠。|近义词：vow, pledge, promise, swearword, curse|反义词：none|noun
