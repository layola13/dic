hypophosphoric:/ˌhaɪpəʊfɒsˈfɒrɪk/ | adj. |次磷酸的；焦磷酸的|Of or relating to hypophosphoric acid, its salts, or the hypophosphate anion, which contains phosphorus in the +4 oxidation state.|Hypophosphoric acid disproportionates to phosphorous acid and phosphoric acid when heated in aqueous solution. 次磷酸在水溶液中加热时会歧化为亚磷酸和磷酸。|近义词:tetraphosphoric (sometimes, as both contain P in +4 state)|反义词:phosphoric, phosphorous|词性:adjective

hypophosphate:/ˌhaɪpəʊˈfɒsfeɪt/ | n. |次磷酸盐；焦磷酸盐|A salt or ester of hypophosphoric acid.|Sodium hypophosphate is a reducing agent. 次磷酸钠是一种还原剂。|近义词:tetraphosphate (sometimes)|反义词:phosphate, phosphite|词性:noun
