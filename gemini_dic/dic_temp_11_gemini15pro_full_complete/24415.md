brisked:/brɪskt/| v.|使活跃，使加快， brisk的过去式和过去分词|The cold air brisked our steps. 冷空气让我们加快了脚步。|英文解析:past tense and past participle of brisk, meaning to make or become more active, lively, or faster.|例句：The wind brisked up. 风力增强了。|例句：The cool air brisked my walk. 凉爽的空气让我走得更快了。|近义词：enlivened, quickened, accelerated|反义词：slowed, sluggish|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *brisken, from earlier *brisk, from Old French brusque (“quick, lively”), from Italian brusco (“sour, tart”), of uncertain origin.
