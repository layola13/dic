inpatients:/ˈɪnˌpeɪʃənts/ | n. |住院病人|People who stay in a hospital while receiving treatment.|例句：The hospital has a separate wing for inpatients. 这家医院有一个单独的住院部。

例句：The number of inpatients has increased significantly this winter. 今年冬天住院病人数量显著增加。

|近义词：hospitalized patients|反义词：outpatients|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- + patient.  "In-" signifies "within" or "into". "Patient" derives from the Latin "patiens," meaning "suffering" or "enduring," ultimately from the verb "pati," "to suffer" or "endure."  Thus, "inpatient" refers to someone suffering/enduring treatment *within* a hospital.
