excellencies:/ˈɛksələnsɪz/
n.
阁下（复数）;优点;优秀之处
a title of honor given to certain high officials (plural).  The state or quality of excelling or being exceptionally good.
Your Excellencies, we are gathered here today... 阁下们，我们今天聚集在这里……
The excellencies of the Swiss educational system are widely recognized. 瑞士教育体系的优点得到了广泛认可。
近义词: merits, strengths, virtues, dignitaries (for people)
反义词: flaws, weaknesses, shortcomings, defects
