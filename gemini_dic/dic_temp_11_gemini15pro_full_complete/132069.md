noncovetousness:/ˌnɒnˈkʌvɪtəsnəs/| n.|不贪婪，无欲，知足|The state of not desiring something that belongs to another person; contentment with what one has.|He lived a life of noncovetousness, finding joy in simple things. 他过着不贪婪的生活，在简单的事物中寻找快乐。|Synonyms: contentment, satisfaction, detachment, abstinence|Antonyms: covetousness, greed, avarice, envy|noun
