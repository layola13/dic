cantoned:/kænˈtʊnd/ | v. | 被分派驻扎；被限制在 | to be assigned to or restricted to a particular place or district | The troops were cantoned in the village for the winter.  部队被派驻扎在这个村庄过冬。| The refugees were cantoned in a makeshift camp.  难民们被限制在一个临时搭建的营地里。| 近义词:quartered, billeted, stationed | 反义词:dispersed, scattered | 词性:动词

cantoned:/kænˈtʊnd/ | adj. | 分成区的 | divided into sections or districts |  The city is cantoned into several distinct neighborhoods. 这座城市被划分成几个不同的街区。|  The country was cantoned into a series of small principalities. 这个国家被划分成一系列小的公国。| 近义词:divided, partitioned, sectioned | 反义词:unified, combined, integrated | 词性:形容词
