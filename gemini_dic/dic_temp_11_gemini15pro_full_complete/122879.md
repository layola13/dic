mollusca:/məˈlʌskə/ | n. |软体动物|A large phylum of invertebrate animals (such as snails, clams, or squids) with a soft unsegmented body usually enclosed in a shell.  |例句：Mollusca are a diverse group of animals. 软体动物是动物的一个多样化群体。

例句：The study of mollusca is called malacology. 软体动物的研究被称为软体动物学。|近义词：invertebrate, shellfish (for some mollusca)|反义词: vertebrate|词性: Noun
