entomophagous: /ˌɛntəˈmɒfəɡəs/ | adj. | 食虫的 | Feeding on insects; insectivorous. | Ladybugs are entomophagous insects that help control aphid populations. 瓢虫是食虫昆虫，有助于控制蚜虫的数量。|  Entomophagous bats play a crucial role in controlling insect populations. 食虫蝙蝠在控制昆虫数量方面起着至关重要的作用。 | synonyms: insectivorous, carnivorous (in a limited sense) | antonyms: herbivorous, phytophagous | adjective
