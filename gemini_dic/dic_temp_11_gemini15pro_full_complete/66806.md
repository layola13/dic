expanse:/ɪkˈspæns/ /ɪkˈspæns/| n. |广阔; 浩瀚; 宽阔; 广袤|A wide and open area of something, especially land, sea, or sky.|例句：The vast expanse of the ocean stretched before them. 浩瀚无垠的大海展现在他们面前。

例句：The Sahara Desert is a vast expanse of sand. 撒哈拉沙漠是一片广阔的沙地。|近义词：area, stretch, extent, sweep, vastness|反义词： confinement, limit, restriction|词性: 名词
