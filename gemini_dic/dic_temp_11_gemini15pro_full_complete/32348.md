childishness:/ˈtʃaɪldɪʃnəs/| n. |幼稚，孩子气|The state or quality of being childish; immature behavior or thinking.|His childishness is sometimes endearing, but often frustrating. 他的孩子气有时很可爱，但经常令人沮丧。|Example:  She displayed surprising childishness for someone her age. 她表现出与她年龄不相符的惊人幼稚。

|Synonyms: immaturity, puerility, infantility|Antonyms: maturity, sophistication, adulthood|noun


childish:/ˈtʃaɪldɪʃ/| adj. |幼稚的，孩子气的|Of, like, or appropriate to a child.|Stop being so childish! 别这么幼稚！|Example: He made a childish face at his sister. 他对他的妹妹做了个鬼脸。|Synonyms: immature, puerile, infantile|Antonyms: mature, sophisticated, adult|adjective
