jamboree:/ˌdʒæmbəˈriː/| n. |欢庆活动，盛大集会|A large celebration or party, especially one involving a lot of people.|The scout troop went to the national jamboree. 童子军队伍参加了全国大会。|近义词：celebration, festival, fiesta, gathering|反义词：wake, funeral|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, perhaps from a fanciful creation based on jam + spree. First recorded in the late 19th century.
