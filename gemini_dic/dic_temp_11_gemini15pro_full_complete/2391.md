adiposities:/ˌædɪˈpɒsɪtiz/ | n. | (plural) 肥胖，脂肪过多 | Localized accumulations of fat. |  The doctor noted the patient's adiposities around the abdomen. 医生注意到病人腹部周围的脂肪堆积。| Fat deposits, fatty areas | Lean areas, thin areas | noun


Etymology:
From Latin *adiposus* ("fatty, greasy"), from *adeps* ("fat") + *-osus*.
