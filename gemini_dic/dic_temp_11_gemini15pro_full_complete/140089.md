off-beat:/ˌɒfˈbiːt/ /ˌɔːfˈbiːt/| adj.|不寻常的，另类的；节奏不规则的|Unconventional, unusual, or eccentric; not coinciding with the regular beat of music.|例句：She has a very off-beat sense of humor. 她有一种很不寻常的幽默感。

例句：The band played an off-beat rhythm that was hard to dance to. 乐队演奏了一种不规则的节奏，很难跟着跳舞。|近义词：unconventional, quirky, eccentric, irregular|反义词：conventional, ordinary, regular|词性:adjective

off-beat:/ˌɒfˈbiːt/ /ˌɔːfˈbiːt/| n.|弱拍；不规则的节奏|An unaccented beat in music.|例句：The drummer emphasized the off-beats, creating a syncopated rhythm.  鼓手强调了弱拍，创造了一种切分音节奏。

例句：The off-beat nature of the poem made it stand out.  这首诗不寻常的特性使它脱颖而出。|近义词：upbeat (sometimes, contrasting sense)|反义词：downbeat (sometimes, contrasting sense)|词性:noun
