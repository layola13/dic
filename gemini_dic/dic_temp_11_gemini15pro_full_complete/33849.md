circumnavigatory:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡətɔːri/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈnævɪɡətɔːri/
adjective
环球航行的；环绕…航行的
Relating to or involving circumnavigation; going around the world.
Magellan's circumnavigatory voyage proved that the Earth is round. 麦哲伦的环球航行证明了地球是圆的。
The circumnavigatory expedition lasted for three years.  这次环球探险持续了三年。

近义词:round-the-world, global
反义词:local, regional
词性:adjective
