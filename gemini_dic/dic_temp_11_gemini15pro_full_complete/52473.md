dieted:/ˈdaɪətɪd/
v. |节食过，控制饮食过|Past participle of "diet".  Indicates that someone has restricted their food intake, typically for health or weight-loss reasons.|She had dieted for months before the wedding. 她为了婚礼节食了好几个月。|近义词: restricted eating, followed a diet|反义词: overate, binged|Verb (past participle)

dieted:/ˈdaɪətɪd/
adj. |控制饮食的|Used adjectivally, though less common. Describes someone who has undergone a period of dietary restriction.|The dieted group showed significant weight loss.  控制饮食的那组体重明显下降。|近义词:  calorie-controlled, weight-reducing|反义词: unrestricted, uncontrolled|Adjective
