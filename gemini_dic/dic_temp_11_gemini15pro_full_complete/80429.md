gondola:/ˈɡɒndələ(r)/ (UK), /ˈɡɑːndələ(r)/ (US)| n.|贡多拉, (尤指威尼斯的)狭长小船；(载送滑雪者的)缆车|A long, narrow boat with a high point at each end, used on the canals of Venice in Italy. Also, an enclosed cabin suspended from a cable, used to transport skiers up mountainsides. |例句：We took a gondola ride through the canals of Venice. 我们乘坐贡多拉游览了威尼斯的运河。

例句：The gondola carried skiers to the top of the mountain. 缆车将滑雪者送到了山顶。|近义词：boat, barge (for Venetian gondolas), cable car, ski lift (for ski gondolas)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


gondolas:/ˈɡɒndələz/ (UK), /ˈɡɑːndələz/ (US)| n.|贡多拉(的复数形式)|Plural form of gondola.  Refers to multiple Venetian boats or ski lift cabins. |例句：Several gondolas glided along the Grand Canal. 几艘贡多拉沿着大运河滑行。

例句：The ski resort had several gondolas operating simultaneously. 滑雪场同时运行着几条缆车。|近义词：boats, barges, cable cars, ski lifts|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
