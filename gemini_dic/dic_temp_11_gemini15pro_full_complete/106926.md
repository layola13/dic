lacey:/ˈleɪsi/| adj.|花边的，蕾丝的；精致的，轻盈的|Delicate and intricate, often resembling lace.  Characterized by openwork or delicate patterns.|Her dress was adorned with lacey trim. 她的连衣裙装饰着蕾丝花边。|近义词：delicate, intricate, ornate, filigree, gossamer|反义词：plain, simple, heavy, coarse|词性:形容词

lacey:/ˈleɪsi/| n.|蕾丝, 花边|A fine open fabric of cotton or silk, made by looping, twisting, or knitting thread in patterns and used especially for trimming garments.|She bought some beautiful lacey for her wedding dress. 她为她的婚纱买了一些漂亮的蕾丝。|近义词：lace, openwork, netting|反义词：solid fabric|词性:名词
