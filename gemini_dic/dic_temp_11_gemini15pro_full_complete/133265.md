nonexhibitionisms:/ˌnɒnˌeksɪbɪˈʃənɪzəmz/| n. |非暴露癖，不爱出风头|The state or quality of not being exhibitionistic; a lack of desire to show off or attract attention.|Her nonexhibitionism made her a calming presence in the otherwise boisterous group. 她不爱出风头的性格使她在原本喧闹的群体中显得格外沉稳。| He preferred quiet nights at home, a testament to his nonexhibitionism. 他喜欢在家享受安静的夜晚，这证明了他不爱出风头。|近义词: modesty, reserve, unpretentiousness|反义词: exhibitionism, ostentation, showiness|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来自拉丁语 non.
exhibitionism (noun):  meaning "a tendency towards showing off" 来自 exhibit + -ion + -ism.
