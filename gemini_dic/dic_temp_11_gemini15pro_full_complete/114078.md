magnetotherapy:/ˌmæɡˌnɛtoʊˈθɛrəpi/
n.
磁疗，磁力疗法
Treatment of disease using magnets.
Magnetotherapy is a complementary therapy that involves applying magnets to the body to relieve pain and treat other health conditions. 磁疗是一种补充疗法，包括将磁铁应用于身体以缓解疼痛和治疗其他健康状况。
Magnetotherapy has been used to treat arthritis, back pain, and other musculoskeletal conditions. 磁疗已被用于治疗关节炎、背痛和其他肌肉骨骼疾病。
Synonyms: magnetic therapy, magnet therapy
Antonyms: conventional therapy, traditional medicine
