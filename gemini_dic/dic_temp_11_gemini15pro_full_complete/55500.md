distrain:/dɪˈstreɪn/| v.|扣押，征收（财物）|To seize (property) as security for payment of a debt or satisfaction of a claim.|The landlord distrained on the tenant's goods for unpaid rent. 房东因租户未付租金而扣押了其货物。|例句：The court ordered the bailiffs to distrain the debtor's goods. 法院命令执达吏扣押债务人的货物。|近义词：seize, confiscate, impound|反义词:release, return|动词

distraint:/dɪˈstreɪnt/| n.|扣押，征收|The seizure of someone's property to obtain payment of rent or other money owed.|The distraint of the farmer's equipment caused him great hardship.  对农民设备的扣押给他造成了很大的困难。|例句：The landlord threatened distraint if the rent was not paid immediately. 如果不立即支付租金，房东就威胁要扣押。|近义词：seizure, confiscation|反义词:release, return|名词
