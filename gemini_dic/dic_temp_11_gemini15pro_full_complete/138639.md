nucule:/ˈnjuːkjuːl/| n. |小坚果；坚果状的孢子囊|A small nut or nut-like structure, especially the hard-walled, one-seeded fruit of certain plants like charophytes.|The nucule of the stonewort contains the female gametes.|近义词:nutlet, achene|反义词:|noun
