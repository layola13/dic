delimitation:/diːˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃn/
n.
限定,界定
the act of fixing or marking the limits or boundaries of something.
The delimitation of the maritime borders was a complex process. 海上边界的划定是一个复杂的过程。
demarcation, definition, outlining
limitlessness, boundlessness
noun

delimit:/diːˈlɪmɪt/
vt.
划定…的界限
to fix or mark the limits or boundaries of.
The surveyor delimited the property lines with stakes. 测量员用木桩标出了地产线。
demarcate, define, bound
expand, extend
verb
