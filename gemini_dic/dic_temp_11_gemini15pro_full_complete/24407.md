briquetted:/brɪˈkɛtɪd/
adjective
压块的; 成块的
formed into a briquette.
例句：Briquetted coal is easier to transport and store than loose coal.  压块煤比散煤更容易运输和储存。

例句：The factory produces briquetted charcoal for barbecues.  这家工厂生产用于烧烤的压块木炭。
近义词：compacted, compressed, consolidated
反义词：loose, unpacked
词性: adjective
