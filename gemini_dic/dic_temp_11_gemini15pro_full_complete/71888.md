fleur-de-lis:/ˌflɜːr də ˈliː/ (also, fleurs-de-lis: /ˌflɜːr də ˈliːz/) | n. |鸢尾花（图案）|A stylized representation of a lily or iris, used as a decorative motif and a royal emblem, especially in French heraldry.| The fleur-de-lis is a prominent symbol on the Louisiana state flag. 路易斯安那州的州旗上显著地印有鸢尾花图案。| The king's robe was embroidered with hundreds of fleurs-de-lis. 国王的袍子上绣着数百朵鸢尾花。| synonym: iris, lily (as a plant); emblem, symbol (as a motif) | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a symbolic image) | noun
