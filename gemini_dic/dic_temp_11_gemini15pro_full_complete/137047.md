nonsticky:/ˌnɒnˈstɪki/ /ˌnɑːnˈstɪki/| adj.|不粘的，不粘性的|Not sticky; designed to prevent food from adhering. |例句：I always use a nonstick pan for frying eggs. 我煎鸡蛋总是用不粘锅。

例句：This nonstick spray is great for baking cakes.  这种不粘喷雾非常适合烤蛋糕。


近义词：nonstick, non-stick|反义词:sticky|词性:adjective

nonstick:/ˌnɒnˈstɪki/ /ˌnɑːnˈstɪki/| n.|不粘锅|A pan or other cooking utensil with a nonstick surface.  |例句：My new nonstick makes cooking so much easier. 我的新不粘锅让做饭变得容易多了。

例句：I need to buy a new nonstick. 我的不粘锅需要换新的了。


近义词：non-stick pan, nonstick pan|反义词: - |词性:noun
