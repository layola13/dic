bumping:/ˈbʌmpɪŋ/| adj. n.|碰撞的，颠簸的；(音乐)强劲的；(非正式)非常好的，极好的|A bumping sound.  一阵碰撞声。|The car ride was bumping due to the rough road. 因为路况不好，车颠簸得很厉害。|近义词：jolting, jarring, bouncing, thumping (for the physical sense), excellent, great, awesome (for the informal sense)|反义词：smooth, even (for the physical sense), terrible, awful (for the informal sense)|词性:adjective, noun


bumping:/ˈbʌmpɪŋ/| n.|碰撞，颠簸|The bumping of the car made her nauseous. 汽车的颠簸让她感到恶心。|We could hear the bumping noise from upstairs. 我们可以听到楼上传来的碰撞声。|近义词：jolting, jarring, bouncing, thumping|反义词：smoothness, evenness|词性:noun


Etymology:  From the verb "bump," which originated from Middle Low German or Middle Dutch *bumsen "to strike, make a dull sound."
