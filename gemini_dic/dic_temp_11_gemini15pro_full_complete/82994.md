gunnies:/ˈɡʌniz/
n.
粗麻布袋；装粗麻布袋的人
large sacks made of coarse material (burlap, hessian, jute)
The farmer filled the gunnies with potatoes. 农民把马铃薯装满了麻袋。
近义词：sacks, bags
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps containers made of finer materials)
词性: Noun


gunny:/ˈɡʌni/
n.
粗麻布
a strong, coarse material made from jute or hemp
The gunny was rough against his skin. 粗麻布磨着他的皮肤。
近义词：burlap, hessian, jute
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps silk, satin)
词性: Noun
