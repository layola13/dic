corduroyed:/ˈkɔːrdərɔɪd/ | adj. | 灯芯绒的；穿灯芯绒衣服的 | Made of or resembling corduroy; wearing corduroy clothing. |  He wore a corduroyed jacket. 他穿着一件灯芯绒夹克。 | 近义词: corduroy, ribbed | 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly smooth, flat) | 词性: adjective

词源: From corduroy + -ed, indicating "having the characteristics of."  Corduroy itself comes from French "corde du roi" (cord of the king).
