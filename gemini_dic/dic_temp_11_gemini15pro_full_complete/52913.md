dimorphous:/daɪˈmɔːrfəs/| adj.|二形的，二态的|Existing or occurring in two distinct forms.|Example: The dimorphous fungus exists as both a yeast and a mold.  这种二形真菌以酵母和霉菌两种形式存在。

Example:  Sexual dimorphism is common in many bird species.  性二态在许多鸟类中很常见。|近义词：dimorphic, biform|反义词:monomorphic|词性:adjective


dimorphism:/daɪˈmɔːrfɪzəm/| n.|二形性，二态性|The existence of two different forms of the same species, such as male and female.|Example: Sexual dimorphism is evident in the peacock's elaborate tail feathers.  孔雀精致的尾羽表现出明显的性二态。

Example:  The dimorphism in this species is manifested in the size difference between males and females.  该物种的二态性体现在雄性和雌性之间的大小差异。|近义词：biformity|反义词:monomorphism|词性:noun

词源: From Greek *dimorphos* "having two forms," from *di-* "two" + *morphē* "form, shape."
