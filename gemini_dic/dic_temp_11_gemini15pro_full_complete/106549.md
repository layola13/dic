kroner:/ˈkrəʊnə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkroʊnər/ (US)| n.|克朗（北欧国家货币单位）|The basic monetary unit of Denmark, Norway, Sweden, and Iceland.  It is subdivided into 100 øre (in Denmark and Norway) or 100 aurar (in Iceland). The Swedish krona is divided into 100 öre, although the öre is no longer in circulation.| I paid 100 kroner for the sweater. 我花了100克朗买了这件毛衣。|Synonyms:  crown (in the context of currency),  Danish krone, Norwegian krone, Swedish krona, Icelandic króna|Antonyms: N/A (currency units don't typically have antonyms)|noun
