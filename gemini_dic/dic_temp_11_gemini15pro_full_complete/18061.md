beguiled: /bɪˈɡaɪld/ /biˈɡaɪld/ | vt. | 欺骗；使着迷；消磨(时间) | To charm or enchant someone, sometimes in a deceptive way.  To trick someone into doing something. |  She was beguiled by his charm and good looks. 她被他英俊的外表和魅力所迷惑。


He beguiled them into signing the contract. 他骗他们在合同上签了字。 | 近义词: charm, enchant, captivate, deceive, mislead | 反义词: disenchant, repel, enlighten | 词性: 动词
