ferriferous:/fɛˈrɪfərəs/
adj.
含铁的
Containing or yielding iron.
The ferriferous rocks provided a rich source of iron ore. 这些含铁的岩石提供了丰富的铁矿石来源。
The soil in this region is ferriferous. 这个地区的土壤含铁。
近义词: iron-bearing, iron-rich
反义词: non-ferriferous



词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *ferrifer* (iron-bearing), from *ferrum* (iron) + *-fer* (bearing).  The suffix *-fer* is related to the verb *ferre* (to carry, bear).
