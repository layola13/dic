miscoin:/mɪsˈkɔɪn/
verb
铸造劣币; 误铸
To make a coin incorrectly, or to make a counterfeit coin.
He miscoined the silver dollar, making it worthless. 他铸造的银元是劣币，一文不值。
The counterfeiter was arrested for miscoining currency.  伪造者因铸造假币而被捕。
近义词: counterfeit, forge
反义词: mint (properly)
词性: verb
