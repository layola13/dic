fibrillar:/fɪˈbrɪlər/ /faɪˈbrɪlər/
adjective
纤维状的；由纤维组成的
Composed of or resembling fibrils.  Relating to or containing fibrils.
Amyloid plaques are dense, mostly insoluble deposits of fibrillar beta amyloid peptide.  淀粉样斑块是致密的、几乎不溶的纤维状β淀粉样肽沉积物。
The cell walls are composed of a fibrillar network. 细胞壁由纤维状网络组成。
近义词: fibrous, filamentous
反义词: nonfibrous, amorphous
词性: adjective


fibril:/ˈfaɪbrɪl/ /ˈfɪbrɪl/
noun
细纤维；原纤维
A small or slender fiber or filament. A component filament of a fiber.
Muscle fibrils are cylindrical organelles found within muscle cells. 肌原纤维是在肌肉细胞内发现的圆柱形细胞器。
Collagen fibrils provide structural support to tissues. 胶原纤维为组织提供结构支撑。
近义词: fiber, filament
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
