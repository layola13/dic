longheaded:/ˈlɒŋˈhɛdɪd/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋˈhɛdɪd/ (US)| adj.|精明的；有远见的；深谋远虑的|Having good judgment and foresight; shrewd and intelligent, especially in practical matters.|例句：She made a longheaded decision to invest in the company. 她做了一个精明的决定，投资了这家公司。|例句：He's a longheaded businessman who rarely makes a bad deal. 他是一位精明的商人，很少做亏本生意。|近义词：shrewd, astute, wise, farsighted, judicious, sagacious|反义词：shortsighted, foolish, imprudent|词性:形容词

词源:
long (adj.) + headed (adj.)
From Middle English *longheded, equivalent to long +‎ -headed.  "long" 指的是“长远的”，"headed"指的是“头脑”，引申为“有…头脑的”。
