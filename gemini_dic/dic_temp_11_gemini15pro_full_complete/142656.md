ostracizer: /ˈɒstrəˌsaɪzər/ (British) /ˈɑːstrəˌsaɪzər/ (American)
n.
排斥者；放逐者
A person who ostracizes someone; someone who excludes a person from a group or society.
例句：He became an ostracizer, cutting off all ties with his former friends.  他成了一个排斥者，切断了与以前所有朋友的联系。

例句：The ostracizer was eventually shunned by the very community he sought to control.  这个排斥者最终被他试图控制的社区所排斥。

近义词：excluder, shunner, banisher, boycotter
反义词：includer, welcomer, acceptor
词性：noun
