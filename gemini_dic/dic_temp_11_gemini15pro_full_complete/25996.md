bursting:/ˈbɜːrstɪŋ/ | adj. | 爆炸的，充满的，快要…的 |  Filled to the point of overflowing or extremely full of something; about to explode or break open. | The bag was bursting with groceries.  这个袋子装满了食品杂货。| synonyms: overflowing, crammed, packed, teeming | antonyms: empty, vacant, depleted | adjective

bursting:/ˈbɜːrstɪŋ/ | v. | 爆裂，突然出现 |  Breaking open or apart suddenly and violently, typically as a result of an internal pressure; appearing suddenly. |  The dam burst after days of heavy rain.  大坝在连日暴雨后决堤了。 | synonyms: explode, rupture, break open | antonyms: implode, collapse, mend | verb
