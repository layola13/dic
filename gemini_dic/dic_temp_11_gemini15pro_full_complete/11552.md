aristocrat: /ˈærɪstəkræt/ (US) /ˈærɪstəkrɑːt/ (UK)| n. |贵族；贵族成员|A member of the aristocracy, a ruling class or a noble family.  A person considered to be superior in terms of birth, intellect, or taste.| He behaved like an aristocrat, even though his family was quite ordinary. 他表现得像个贵族，即使他的家庭很普通。| synonyms: noble, peer, lord, lady, patrician, blue blood| antonyms: commoner, peasant, plebeian | noun

aristocratic: /ˌærɪstəˈkrætɪk/ (US) /ˌærɪstəˈkrætɪk/ (UK)| adj. |贵族的；贵族气派的| Of, relating to, or characteristic of the aristocracy.
