antidefamation: /ˌæntiˌdefəˈmeɪʃn/ | adj. | 反诽谤的 |  Relating to or directed against defamation; designed to counteract defamation.  It's most commonly used as part of the name "Anti-Defamation League (ADL)". | The Anti-Defamation League works to combat antisemitism and all forms of bigotry.  反诽谤联盟致力于打击反犹太主义和各种形式的偏见。 |  The organization published an antidefamation pamphlet. 该组织出版了一本反诽谤的小册子。 | Synonyms: counter-defamation, anti-libel, anti-slander | Antonyms: pro-defamation, libelous, slanderous | adjective
