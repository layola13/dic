generalizers:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzərz/| n.|概括者，泛化者|People who form general conclusions from specific instances.  Those who tend to generalize.|Researchers are often generalizers, looking for patterns in data. 研究人员通常是概括者，在数据中寻找模式。

Generalizers tend to see the bigger picture rather than the details. 泛化者倾向于看到更大的图景而不是细节。|近义词：synthesizers, abstractors|反义词: specialists, particularizers|词性: noun
