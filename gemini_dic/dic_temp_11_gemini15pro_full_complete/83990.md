half-deafening:/ˌhæfˈdɛfənɪŋ/ | adj. | 半聾的，震耳欲聾的 |  Partially deafening; extremely loud but not completely overwhelming the sense of hearing.  It describes a sound that is very loud and makes it difficult to hear other things, but not so loud that it completely blocks out all other sounds. | The music at the concert was half-deafening, making it hard to talk to my friend. 音乐会上的音乐震耳欲聋，让我很难和朋友交谈。| The construction noise outside was half-deafening, I could barely hear myself think. 外面的建筑噪音震耳欲聋，我几乎听不到自己的想法。 | Near synonyms:  loud, noisy, boisterous, clamorous, thunderous | Near antonyms: quiet, silent, hushed, muted | adjective
