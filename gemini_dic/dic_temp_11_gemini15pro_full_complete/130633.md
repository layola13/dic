nonadmissibility:/ˌnɒnədˌmɪsəˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːnədˌmɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可采纳性，不可接纳性 | The state or quality of being nonadmissible, especially in a legal context, meaning that something (evidence, testimony, etc.) cannot be accepted as valid or relevant. | The nonadmissibility of the evidence led to the case being dismissed.  证据不可采纳导致案件被驳回。| Near Synonyms:  inadmissibility, unacceptability, ineligibility | Antonyms: admissibility, acceptability, eligibility | noun
