classicalists:/ˈklæsɪklɪsts/ | n. | 古典主义者；古典学者 | Scholars or enthusiasts of classical literature, art, and culture, especially that of ancient Greece and Rome. |  Example: The classicalists debated the meaning of the ancient text.  古典主义者们争论着古代文本的含义。

Example: Many classicalists are also fluent in Latin and Greek. 许多古典学者也精通拉丁语和希腊语。 | Synonyms: humanist, scholar, academic | Antonyms: modernist, contemporary | Noun
