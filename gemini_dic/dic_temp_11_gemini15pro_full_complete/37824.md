compense:/ˈkɒmpənseɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpənseɪt/ (US)| v. |补偿，赔偿|to give something to someone in return for something that they have lost or damaged; to provide something good to balance or reduce the bad effects of damage, loss, etc.|例句：The company will compensate its employees for any losses they might incur. 公司将补偿员工可能遭受的任何损失。|例句：Nothing can compensate for the loss of a child. 没有什么能弥补失去孩子的痛苦。|近义词：make up for, atone for, recompense, offset|反义词：fail, lose, forfeit|词性:动词
