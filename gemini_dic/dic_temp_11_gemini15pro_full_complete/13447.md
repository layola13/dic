atrabiliary:/ˌætrəˈbɪliəri/
adjective
忧郁的；坏脾气的；易怒的
Affected with or marked by ill nature, bad temper; melancholic.  Characterized by or arising from black bile (in ancient physiology, one of the four humors, believed to be the cause of melancholy).
He gave me an atrabiliary look when I interrupted his work.  他打断他工作时，给了我一个恼怒的眼神。
synonyms: choleric, bilious, splenetic, irascible, irritable, cantankerous, grumpy, surly
antonyms: good-humored, cheerful, amiable, affable
词性: adjective
