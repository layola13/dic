counteroffensive:/ˌkaʊntərəˈfɛnsɪv/ | n. adj.|反攻，反击|An attack made in response to an enemy's attack.  A counteroffensive aims to regain lost ground or momentum.|The general launched a surprise counteroffensive. 将军发动了一次突袭反攻。|synonyms: counterattack, offensive, push, assault|antonyms: defense, retreat, withdrawal|noun

counteroffensive:/ˌkaʊntərəˈfɛnsɪv/ | adj.|反攻的，反击的|Relating to or constituting a counteroffensive.|The counteroffensive operation was successful. 反攻行动很成功。|synonyms: aggressive, attacking|antonyms: defensive, retreating|adjective
