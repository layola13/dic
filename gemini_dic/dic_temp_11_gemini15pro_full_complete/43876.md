crest: /krɛst/ | n. |  顶部，顶峰；浪尖；羽冠；徽章 |  The highest part of a wave, hill, or mountain range; a tuft or plume of feathers on the head of a bird; a symbol or emblem used to represent a family, organization, or nation. |  The hikers finally reached the crest of the mountain.  登山者们终于到达了山顶。 | synonyms: peak, summit, top, apex, crown, insignia, emblem | antonyms: trough, bottom, base, nadir | noun

crest: /krɛst/ | v. | 到达顶峰，达到顶点；形成浪尖 | To reach the highest point or level. |  The wave crested and then crashed onto the shore.  海浪达到顶峰，然后拍打在岸边。| synonyms: peak, climax, culminate | antonyms: decline, decrease, diminish | verb
