acquiescently:/ˌækwiˈɛsəntli/
adverb
默许地，顺从地
in a manner that shows acceptance without protest; passively
She acquiescently agreed to his demands. 她默许地同意了他的要求。
He nodded acquiescently, though he didn't really agree. 他顺从地点了点头，尽管他并不真正同意。
近义词:submissively, passively, compliantly, obediently
反义词:resistantly, defiantly, rebelliously
词性:adverb

acquiesce:/ˌækwiˈɛs/
verb
默许，顺从
accept something reluctantly but without protest
Management acquiesced to the workers' demands. 管理层默许了工人的要求。
She acquiesced in the decision. 她顺从了这个决定。
近义词:consent, comply, agree, submit
反义词:resist, oppose, defy, object
词性:verb

acquiescence:/ˌækwiˈɛsəns/
noun
默许，顺从
the reluctant acceptance of something without protest
Her acquiescence to their demands surprised everyone. 她对他们要求的默许让所有人感到惊讶。
He met their proposal with acquiescence. 他默许了他们的提议。
近义词:compliance, submission, agreement, consent
反义词:resistance, defiance, opposition, objection
词性:noun

词源: From Latin *acquiescere*, from *ad-* "to" + *quiescere* "to rest, be quiet"
