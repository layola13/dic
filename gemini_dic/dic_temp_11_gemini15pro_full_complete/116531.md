mathematically:/ˌmæθəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 数学上地；从数学角度|  In a way that relates to mathematics or uses mathematical methods. | The problem can be mathematically expressed as follows: x + y = z.  这个问题可以用数学表达式表示如下：x + y = z。| analytically, quantitatively | - | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mathematical + -ly.  "Mathematical" comes from Late Latin mathematicus, from Greek mathēmatikos, meaning  "mathematical," from mathēma, meaning "learning, study, science, knowledge."
