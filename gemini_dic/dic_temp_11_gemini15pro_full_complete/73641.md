forfeitures:/ˈfɔːrfɪtʃərz/
n.
没收物；罚金；丧失；被没收的东西
The loss of something as a penalty for wrongdoing.  Something that is lost or surrendered as a penalty.
The court ordered the forfeiture of the criminal's assets. 法院下令没收罪犯的资产。
He risked forfeiture of his driving licence. 他冒着被吊销驾照的风险。
Synonyms: penalty, loss, confiscation, surrender
Antonyms: reward, gain, acquisition
