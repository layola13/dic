faciend:/fɑːsiˈɛnd/ | n. |  (土地)租佃者；(尤指)拉丁美洲的大庄园主 | A tenant farmer, especially a large landowner in Latin America. | The haciendero controlled vast tracts of land and employed numerous workers.  庄园主控制着大片土地，雇用了许多工人。 | 近义词: landowner, rancher, planter | 反义词: tenant, laborer | 词性: noun

词源:  From Spanish *hacienda*, from Latin *facienda* "things to be done," from *faciendum* (neuter gerundive of *facere* "to do, make").
