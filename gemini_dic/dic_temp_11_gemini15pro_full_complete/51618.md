developer:/dɪˈvɛləpər/ (US) /dɪˈvɛləpə(r)/ (UK)| n.|开发者，开发商|A person or company that creates or builds something, especially computer software, buildings, or land.|The software developer created a new app.  这个软件开发者创建了一个新的应用程序。|近义词：programmer, coder, builder, constructor|反义词:user, consumer|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "develop" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Develop" comes from Old French "desveloper" meaning "to unwrap, unfold."  Ultimately from Latin "dis-" (apart) + "volvere" (to roll).
