brahma:/ˈbrɑːmə/ | n. |梵天; 婆罗摩; 造物主; 绝对者|The Hindu god of creation, the first member of the Hindu Trinity (Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva).|Brahma is often depicted with four heads and four arms. 梵天通常被描绘成有四个头和四只手臂。| The temple is dedicated to Brahma, the creator god. 这座寺庙是献给造物主梵天的。 |synonyms: creator, God, divinity| antonyms: destroyer | noun

brahmin:/ˈbrɑːmɪn/ | n. |婆罗门（印度种姓的最高级）; 婆罗门|A member of the highest Hindu caste, that of the priests.| Brahmins traditionally held a position of great respect in Indian society.  婆罗门在印度社会传统上享有很高的尊重。| Only Brahmins were allowed to perform certain religious rituals.  只有婆罗门才能进行某些宗教仪式。| synonyms: priest, cleric| antonyms: outcaste, pariah | noun
