bridgetree: /ˈbrɪdʒˌtriː/
n.
桥牌树，桥树 (一种用于记牌的辅助工具)
A bridgetree is a device used in the card game of bridge to help players keep track of the cards that have been played.
She used a bridgetree to help her remember which cards had been played. 她用桥牌树来帮助她记住哪些牌已经出过了。

近义词: scorepad, bidding box
