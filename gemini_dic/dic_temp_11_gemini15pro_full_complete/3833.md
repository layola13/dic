agonistics: /əˈɡɒnɪstɪks/ /əˈɡɑːnɪstɪks/ | n. |  竞争学; 争斗学;  | The study of animal behavior related to conflict, aggression, and competition, but excluding the lethal aspects of fighting.  | Example:  The researcher specialized in the agonistics of wolves, observing their dominance displays and territorial disputes. 研究人员专门研究狼的竞争学，观察它们的支配行为和领地争端。

Example:  Agonistics plays a crucial role in understanding social hierarchies within animal populations. 竞争学在理解动物种群中的社会等级制度方面起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: competition, rivalry, contest | 反义词: cooperation, collaboration | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *ἀγωνιστικός (agōnistikos, “fond of contesting”)  <  ἀγωνίζομαι (agōnizomai, “I contend for a prize”)  <  ἀγών (agōn, “contest”).
