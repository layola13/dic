impeders:/ɪmˈpiːdərz/| n.|阻碍者，妨碍者|People or things that impede progress or movement.  Those who obstruct or hinder something.|The impeders of progress must be dealt with decisively.  进步的阻碍者必须坚决处理。|Synonyms: obstructers, hinderers, blockers|Antonyms: facilitators, enablers|Noun
