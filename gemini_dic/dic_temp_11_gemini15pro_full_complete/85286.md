harmonization:/ˌhɑːrmənaɪˈzeɪʃn/ |n.|协调，统一，调和|The harmonization of international trade laws is a complex process. 国际贸易法的协调是一个复杂的过程。|例句：The harmonization of accounting standards across Europe is a key objective.  欧洲各国会计准则的协调是一个关键目标。

例句：The company is working on the harmonization of its different software systems.  公司正在努力使其不同的软件系统协调一致。|近义词：unification, standardization, coordination, integration, reconciliation|反义词：conflict, discord, disagreement, disharmony|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *harmonizātiōnem, from Latin harmonizātus, past participle of harmonizāre  "to harmonize," from Ancient Greek ἁρμονίζειν (harmonizein) "to fit together," from ἁρμονία (harmonia) "harmony."
