mauger:/ˈmɔːɡər/| prep. |尽管，不顾|in spite of; despite.  It happened mauger all our efforts to prevent it. 尽管我们尽力阻止，它还是发生了。|近义词:despite, notwithstanding, in spite of|反义词:because of, due to|词性:介词

Etymology->
From Middle English *maugre, from Anglo-Norman maugré, malgre, from Old French mal gre (“ill will, displeasure, spite”), from mal (“bad, evil”) + gre (“will, pleasure, liking”), from Late Latin gratum (“pleasing”).
