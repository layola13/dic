duriel:/ˈdjʊəriəl/ | adj. | 硬脑膜的; 坚硬的; | Relating to the dura mater, the tough outermost membrane surrounding the brain and spinal cord;  Resembling the dura mater in texture, tough or hard.|  The surgeon carefully repaired the *duriel* tear. 外科医生仔细地修补了硬脑膜的撕裂。|  The fossilized shell had a *duriel* texture.  这块石化的贝壳质地坚硬。 | 近义词: tough, hard, firm, rigid | 反义词: soft, pliable, flexible | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *durus* ("hard") +‎ *-alis*.  最终来自原始印欧语 *deru-* (“solid, firm”).
