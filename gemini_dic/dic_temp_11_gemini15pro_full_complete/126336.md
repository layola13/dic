myositis:/ˌmaɪəˈsaɪtɪs/| n. |肌炎|Inflammation of muscle tissue, often causing pain, weakness, and tenderness.  It can be caused by various factors, including infection, injury, and autoimmune diseases.|例句：Polymyositis is a type of myositis that affects multiple muscles. 多发性肌炎是一种影响多块肌肉的肌炎。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her with myositis after a thorough examination. 医生经过彻底检查后诊断她患有肌炎。|近义词：muscle inflammation|反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease condition)|词性:noun
