ETYMOLOGY->
From neo- + classic. "Neo-" is a combining form meaning "new," derived from the Greek "neos." "Classic" comes from the Latin "classicus," meaning "belonging to the highest class" and later associated with the art and literature of ancient Greece and Rome.
