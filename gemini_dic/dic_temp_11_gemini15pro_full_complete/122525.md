modable:/ˈmɒdəbəl/ (US) /ˈmɒdəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可改装的，可修改的|Able to be modded (modified).  This often refers to video games, software, or hardware that can be altered by users.|This game is highly modable, with a large community creating new content. 这款游戏具有很高的可玩性，有一个庞大的社区在创建新的内容。|The car's engine is surprisingly modable, allowing for significant performance upgrades.  这辆车的引擎出奇地容易改装，可以进行显著的性能升级。|近义词：modifiable, adaptable, customizable|反义词：fixed, unmodifiable, inflexible|词性:形容词
