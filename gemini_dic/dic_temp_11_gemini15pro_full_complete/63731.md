epiblast:/ˈɛpɪblæst/| n. |外胚层|The outermost layer of cells of the blastocyst, which gives rise to the embryo proper.|The epiblast is crucial for the development of the nervous system. 外胚层对于神经系统的发育至关重要。|The cells of the epiblast will differentiate into the three germ layers. 外胚层的细胞将分化成三个胚层。|Synonyms: primitive ectoderm|Antonyms: hypoblast|noun
