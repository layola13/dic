apropos:/ˌæprəˈpoʊ/ (US) /ˌæprəˈpəʊ/ (UK)| adj. adv.|恰当的；适时的；关于；就…而言|relevant; with regard to|例句：Her remarks were very apropos. 她的评论非常恰当。|近义词:relevant, pertinent, appropriate, germane, apposite|反义词:irrelevant, inappropriate, inapt|词性:adjective, adverb

apropos:/ˌæprəˈpoʊ/ (US) /ˌæprəˈpəʊ/ (UK)| prep.|关于；就…而言|concerning; with regard to|例句：Apropos of our conversation, I have some new information. 关于我们之前的谈话，我有一些新的信息。|近义词:concerning, regarding, respecting|反义词:N/A|词性:preposition

词源: French *à propos* (to the purpose).
