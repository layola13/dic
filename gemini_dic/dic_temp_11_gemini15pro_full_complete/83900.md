half-asleep:/ˌhɑːf əˈsliːp/ /ˌhæf əˈsliːp/| adj.|半睡半醒的，昏昏欲睡的|Being in a state between sleep and wakefulness, drowsy.|例句：I was half-asleep during the lecture.  我在讲座期间昏昏欲睡。|近义词：drowsy, sleepy, dozy|反义词：awake, alert, wide-awake|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "half" + "asleep". "Half" meaning partially, and "asleep" meaning in a state of sleep.
