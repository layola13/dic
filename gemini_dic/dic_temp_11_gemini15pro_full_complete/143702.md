outlengthens:/ˌaʊtˈlɛŋθənz/
verb
超过…的长度; 比…长
to exceed something in length.
The blue whale outlengthens all other animals. 蓝鲸比所有其他动物都长。
The new bridge outlengthens the old one by several meters. 新桥比旧桥长几米。
Synonyms: surpasses, exceeds, outstrips
Antonyms: falls short of, is shorter than
词性: 动词
