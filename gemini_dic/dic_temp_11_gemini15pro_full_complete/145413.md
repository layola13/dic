overcentralize: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛntrəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过度集中；过分中央集权 | To concentrate excessive power or control in a central authority or location. | The government overcentralized the education system, stifling local innovation. 政府过度集中教育系统，扼杀了地方创新。| The company overcentralized its decision-making process, leading to slow responses to market changes.  公司过度集中决策过程，导致对市场变化反应迟缓。 | centralize, consolidate | decentralize, distribute | Verb
