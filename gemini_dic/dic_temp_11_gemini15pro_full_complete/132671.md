nondisparities: /ˌnɒndɪˈspærɪtiz/ | n. | 无差异，平等 |  The absence of disparities; a state of equality or similarity.  This word is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to "disparities," which itself refers to noticeable and significant differences or inequalities. | The government is working towards nondisparities in healthcare access for all citizens. 政府正在努力为所有公民提供平等的医疗保健机会。 |  Equality, parity, uniformity | Disparities, inequalities, differences | noun
