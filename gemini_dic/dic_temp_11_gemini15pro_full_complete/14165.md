autisms:/ˈɔːtɪzəmz/
n.
自闭症（复数）
A developmental disorder characterized by difficulties with social interaction and communication, and by restricted or repetitive patterns of thought and behavior.  The plural form "autisms" acknowledges the diversity within the autism spectrum.
Researchers are studying different autisms to better understand the spectrum. 研究人员正在研究不同的自闭症，以便更好地理解自闭症谱系。
Many therapies are available for individuals with autisms. 许多疗法可用于患有自闭症的个体。
近义词: autism spectrum disorders (ASD)
反义词: neurotypicality
词性: noun
