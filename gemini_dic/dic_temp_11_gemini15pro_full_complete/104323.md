jutes:/dʒuːts/
n.
朱特人；黄麻纤维
People of Germanic origin who, along with the Angles and Saxons, invaded Britain in the 5th century AD.  Also, the strong fibre from an Asian plant, used to make rope, sacks etc.
The jutes settled in Kent and on the Isle of Wight. 朱特人定居在肯特郡和怀特岛。
Jute bags are strong and durable. 黄麻袋结实耐用。
近义词: (for the people) Angles, Saxons; (for the fibre)  hemp, sisal
反义词:  None
词性: noun


jute:/dʒuːt/
n.
黄麻; 黄麻纤维
A strong fibre from an Asian plant, used to make rope, sacks etc.
The sacks were made of jute. 这些麻袋是用黄麻做的。
Jute is a versatile natural fibre. 黄麻是一种用途广泛的天然纤维。
近义词: hemp, sisal
反义词: None
词性: noun
