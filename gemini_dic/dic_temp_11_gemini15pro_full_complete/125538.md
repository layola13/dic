multisourcing:/ˌmʌltiˈsɔːrsɪŋ/ | n. | 多源采购，多供应商采购 | The practice of using multiple suppliers for a product or service.  This can reduce risk, increase competition, and provide access to a wider range of expertise or resources. | Many companies are adopting multisourcing to mitigate supply chain disruptions. 许多公司正在采用多源采购以减轻供应链中断。|  multivendor sourcing, multiple sourcing | single sourcing, sole sourcing | noun
