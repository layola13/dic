grandsire:/ˈɡrændˌsaɪər/ (US), /ˈɡrændˌsʌɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.|祖父；祖先|A male ancestor, especially a grandfather.  It can also refer to any ancestor, not just a grandfather.|My grandsire was a blacksmith. 我的祖父是一位铁匠。|Synonyms: grandfather, forefather, ancestor|Antonyms: descendant, offspring|noun

grandsire:/ˈɡrændˌsaɪər/ (US), /ˈɡrændˌsʌɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.|雄性动物的祖先|A male animal from whom others are descended.|The prize-winning stallion was a renowned grandsire. 这匹获奖的种马是一位著名的雄性祖先。|Synonyms: progenitor, forebear|Antonyms: descendant, offspring|noun
