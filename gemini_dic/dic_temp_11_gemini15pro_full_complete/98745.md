insurrections:/ˌɪnsəˈrekʃənz/
n.
起义，暴动，叛乱
An act of rising in revolt, rebellion, or resistance against civil authority or an established government.
The government swiftly crushed the insurrections. 政府迅速镇压了起义。
Near synonyms: rebellion, revolt, uprising, mutiny
Antonyms: obedience, submission
