estop:/ɪˈstɒp/ | v. | 紧急停止；制止 | To stop something, typically a machine, in an emergency. | The operator hit the estop button when the conveyor belt malfunctioned. 操作员在传送带发生故障时按下了紧急停止按钮。

The robot was estopped immediately after detecting a human presence.  机器人在检测到有人存在后立即停止了。 | 近义词：halt, stop, cease, terminate | 反义词: start, begin, activate | 词性: verb

词源: From the French "estouper" meaning to stop up or plug.  Ultimately from the Vulgar Latin "stuppare."
