nummulites:/nʌˈmʊləˌtaɪts/ | n. |货币虫，钱币石 | A large, lens-shaped foraminifer of the genus Nummulites, common as fossils in Tertiary rocks, especially the Eocene.  These fossils resemble coins. |  Nummulites are common in Eocene limestone. 货币虫在始新世石灰岩中很常见。| The limestone contained numerous nummulites. 石灰岩中包含大量的货币虫。 | synonyms: foraminifer, fossil | antonyms: N/A | noun
