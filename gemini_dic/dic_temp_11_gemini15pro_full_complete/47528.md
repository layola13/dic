deathbird:/ˈdeθbɜːrd/| n. |死神鸟，厄运之鸟|A mythical bird often associated with death, destruction, or bad omens.  It is a fictional creature, not a real bird species. |The shaman spoke of the deathbird, a harbinger of the coming plague.  萨满提到了死神鸟，这是即将到来的瘟疫的预兆。

The villagers feared the deathbird’s shadow falling upon their homes. 村民们害怕死神鸟的影子落在他们的房屋上。|近义词：harbinger of doom, omen of death |反义词: symbol of life, phoenix|词性:noun
