crewing:/ˈkruːɪŋ/
n.
人员配备; 全体工作人员
the process of finding and employing the people needed for a particular project or activity.
The crewing of the film took several months.  这部电影的剧组人员配备花了几个月的时间。
staffing, manning
de-crewing
noun
