moistener:/ˈmɔɪsənər/ | n. |润肤剂，保湿剂|Something that moistens; a substance, especially a cosmetic, used to retain or restore moisture. |She applied a moisturizer to her dry skin. 她往她干燥的皮肤上涂了润肤霜。| synonyms: humectant, emollient, cream, lotion | antonyms: desiccant, drying agent | noun

moistener:/ˈmɔɪsənər/ | n. |加湿器|A device used to add moisture to the air, especially in a room. |The moistener helped alleviate her dry cough. 加湿器帮助缓解了她的干咳。| synonyms: humidifier | antonyms: dehumidifier | noun
