non-Newtonian:/nɒn njuːˈtəʊniən/ | adj. | 非牛顿的 |  Not following Newton's law of viscosity, which states that the viscosity of a fluid is independent of the shear rate.  |  Ketchup is a non-Newtonian fluid because it flows more easily when shaken. 番茄酱是一种非牛顿流体，因为它摇晃后更容易流动。 |  non-linear, complex | Newtonian | adjective
