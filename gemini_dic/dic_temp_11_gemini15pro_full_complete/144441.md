outsweetening:/ˌaʊtˈswiːtnɪŋ/
|v.|胜过…的甜美; 比…更甜 |Exceeding something else in sweetness.|例句：The new honey outsweetens any I've ever tasted.  这种新蜂蜜比我尝过的任何蜂蜜都甜。|近义词：surpass, excel, outdo|反义词：undersweeten|词性：动词

outsweetening:/ˌaʊtˈswiːtnɪŋ/
|adj.|更甜的|Sweeter than something else.|例句：This new variety of apple is outsweetening compared to the old one.  这种新品种的苹果比旧品种的更甜。|近义词：sweeter|反义词：less sweet|词性：形容词
