overmourning:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrnɪŋ/
|n.|过度悲伤，过分哀悼
|The act of mourning excessively or for too long a period.|
|She fell into a state of overmourning after her husband's death.|她丈夫去世后，她陷入了过度悲伤的状态。
|Excessive grieving, prolonged mourning|
|Acceptance, moving on|
|noun|


overmourning:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrnɪŋ/
|v.|过度悲伤，过分哀悼 (较少用作动词)
|To mourn excessively or for too long a period.|
