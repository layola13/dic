decompresser:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛsər/ | n. |解压缩器，减压器|A device or program that decompresses compressed data, such as a file or a stream of data.  It restores the data to its original size and format. |  Example:  I need a good decompresser to open these zipped files. 我需要一个好的解压缩器来打开这些压缩文件。

Example: The built-in decompresser couldn't handle the large archive. 内置的解压缩器无法处理这个大型存档。| 近义词: extractor, unzipper, inflater | 反义词: compressor | 词性: 名词

decompress:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛs/ | v. |解压缩，减压，放松|To reduce the pressure on something, or to restore compressed data to its original size and format.  Can also refer to relieving stress or tension. |Example:  I need to decompress these files before I can use them. 我需要解压缩这些文件才能使用它们。

Example: After a long week, I like to decompress with a good book.  漫长的一周后，我喜欢用一本好书来放松。| 近义词: extract, unzip, inflate, relax, unwind | 反义词: compress, tense | 词性: 动词

词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + compress + -er (suffix forming nouns indicating an agent or instrument)  来源于拉丁语 "compressus,"  "com-" (together) + "premere" (to press).
