overprovides:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvaɪdz/
verb
过度提供，供给过多
to provide more of something than is needed or wanted
The government overprovided hospital beds during the pandemic. 政府在疫情期间提供了过多的医院床位。

The charity overprovided food, and much of it went to waste.  慈善机构提供了过多的食物，很多都浪费了。

Synonym: oversupply, oversaturate
Antonym: underprovide, undersupply
词性: 动词
