chloride:/ˈklɔːraɪd/ /ˈklɔːrˌaɪd/| n.|氯化物；漂白剂|A binary compound of chlorine with another element or radical.|Sodium chloride is commonly known as table salt. 氯化钠通常被称为食盐。|近义词:  halide|反义词: None|词性: noun


chloride:/ˈklɔːraɪd/ /ˈklɔːrˌaɪd/| n.|漂白剂|(dated) A solution of chlorinated lime used for bleaching or disinfecting.|She used chloride to bleach the stained tablecloth. 她用漂白剂漂白了有污渍的桌布。|近义词: bleach, disinfectant|反义词: None|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chlorure*, from chlor- + -ure (equivalent of English -ide).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek χλωρός (khlōrós, “greenish-yellow”).
