intimidator:/ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtər/| n. |恐吓者，威胁者|A person who intimidates others, making them feel frightened or threatened.|The school bully was a notorious intimidator. 学校恶霸是一个臭名昭著的恐吓者。|Synonyms: bully, threat, menace|Antonyms: protector, defender, supporter|noun


intimidate:/ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪt/| vt. |恐吓，威胁|To frighten or overawe (someone), especially in order to make them do or stop doing something.|He tried to intimidate his opponent into forfeiting the match. 他试图恐吓他的对手放弃比赛。|Synonyms: frighten, threaten, browbeat, scare, terrorize|Antonyms: encourage, embolden, reassure|verb
