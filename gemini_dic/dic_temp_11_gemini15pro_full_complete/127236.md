nauseation:/ˌnɔːziˈeɪʃən/ /ˌnɔːʒiˈeɪʃən/| n. |恶心，作呕|A feeling of sickness with an inclination to vomit.|The smell of the hospital caused me nausea. 医院的气味让我感到恶心。|近义词：sickness, queasiness, vomiting|反义词: wellness, comfort|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *nauseatio, from Latin nauseare "to be seasick," from nausea "seasickness," from Greek nausia, from naus "ship."
