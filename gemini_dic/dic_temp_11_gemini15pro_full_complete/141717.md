oracularnesses:/ɔːˈrækjʊlərˌnɛsɪz/| n. |神谕性；预言性；玄妙莫测|The quality of being oracular; ambiguity or obscurity of meaning.|His pronouncements were filled with oracularnesses, leaving his followers to interpret their meaning. 他的声明充满了玄妙莫测，让他的追随者们自行解读其含义。|近义词：enigmatic nature, mysteriousness, ambiguity, obscurity|反义词：clarity, perspicuity, explicitness|词性:noun

Etymology: From "oracular" + "-ness".  "Oracular" comes from Late Latin "oracularis", from Latin "oraculum" (oracle),  ultimately from "orare" (to speak, pray).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
