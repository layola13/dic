hamper:/ˈhæmpər/ (US), /ˈhæmpə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 阻碍，妨碍；大篮子 | A large basket with a lid, often used for carrying food or laundry.  A restraint that confines or restricts movement or progress.|
The heavy rain hampered our progress.  暴雨阻碍了我们的前进。
She carried a hamper full of picnic goodies. 她提着一篮子野餐美食。|
近义词:obstruct, hinder, impede, basket, crate |
反义词:facilitate, aid, assist |
词性:noun, verb


hamper:/ˈhæmpər/ (US), /ˈhæmpə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 阻碍，妨碍 | To restrict the movement or progress of something or someone.|

Lack of funding hampered the project's development. 缺乏资金阻碍了项目的开发。|
