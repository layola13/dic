cottony:/ˈkɒtəni/ (US) /ˈkɑːtni/ (UK)| adj.|棉的；像棉花的；柔软的|Resembling cotton in texture, softness, or appearance.|The plant has cottony white flowers. 这种植物开着像棉花一样的白花。|The blanket had a soft, cottony feel. 这条毯子摸起来柔软如棉。|近义词：soft, downy, fluffy, fleecy|反义词：coarse, rough, harsh|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From cotton + -y.  The suffix "-y" is used to form adjectives meaning "having the quality of" or "full of."
