bootability:/ˌbuːtəˈbɪləti/
|n.|可引导性；可启动性|The capability of a system or device to be started or loaded into memory, typically referring to operating systems.|The bootability of the USB drive was compromised after the accidental format.  U盘意外格式化后，其可启动性受到了损害。|synonyms: startability, loadability|antonyms: unbootable|noun
