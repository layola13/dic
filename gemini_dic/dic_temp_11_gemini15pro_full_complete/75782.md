fumado:/fuˈmɑːdoʊ/ (Brazilian Portuguese)
adj.
smoked; smoky
This word describes something that has been smoked, or a place filled with smoke.  It's a past participle used as an adjective in Portuguese.
例句：
O peixe fumado estava delicioso. = The smoked fish was delicious.

A sala estava fumada depois do incêndio. = The room was smoky after the fire.

近义词: defumado (Portuguese), smoked (English)
反义词: fresco (fresh - in the context of food), limpo (clean - in the context of air)
词性: adjective


fumado:/fuˈmɑːdoʊ/ (Spanish - less common)
adj.
smoked
This usage is less common in Spanish and generally refers to something that has been smoked.
例句：
Carne fumada = Smoked meat

近义词: ahumado (Spanish)
反义词: fresco (fresh)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Portuguese and Spanish verb *fumar* (to smoke), ultimately from Latin *fumus* (smoke).
