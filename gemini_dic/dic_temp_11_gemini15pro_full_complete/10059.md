apocalypse:/əˈpɒkəlɪps/ | n. | 天启，启示；灾难，浩劫 |  A prophetic revelation, especially one concerning a cataclysm or the end of the world. It can also refer to any great disaster or cataclysmic event. | Many people fear a nuclear apocalypse. 很多人害怕核灾难。

| The earthquake caused an apocalypse in the region. 地震给该地区带来了巨大的灾难。 | 近义词：catastrophe, disaster, cataclysm, doom | 反义词：blessing, salvation | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *apocalypsis, from Ancient Greek ἀποκάλυψις (apokálupsis, “revelation, disclosure”), from ἀποκαλύπτω (apokalúptō, “I uncover, I reveal”), from ἀπό (apó, “from, off, away from”) + καλύπτω (kalúptō, “I cover, I conceal”).
