concoct:/kənˈkɒkt/ /kənˈkɑːkt/ | vt. n.|调制，配制；捏造，编造|To create or devise something, especially in a skillful or ingenious way, often by combining various elements; to invent a story or explanation, often fraudulently.| She concocted a delicious soup from leftover vegetables. 她用剩菜 concocted 出一碗美味的汤。

He concocted a story about being robbed. 他编造了一个关于被抢劫的故事。|近义词：devise, invent, fabricate, formulate, create, prepare, mix|反义词：discover, find, destroy, ruin|词性：动词，名词(较罕见，指调制品)

concoctor:/kənˈkɒktər/ /kənˈkɑːktər/| n. |调制者；捏造者；策划者|A person who concocts something, especially a plan, scheme, or mixture.|The concoctor of this plan deserves a medal. 这个计划的策划者应该得到一枚奖章。|近义词：inventor, creator, fabricator, author, planner|反义词：discoverer, finder|词性：名词
