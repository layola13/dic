non-operative:/ˌnɒnˈɒpərətɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˈɑːpərətɪv/
adjective
非手术的；不运作的；无效的
Not involving or requiring surgery; not working or functioning; not valid or effective.
The patient received non-operative treatment for his knee injury.  这个病人接受了非手术治疗他的膝伤。
The old law is now non-operative. 这项旧法律现在已失效。
Synonyms: inactive, inoperative, nonfunctional, nonsurgical, closed
Antonyms: operative, working, functional, surgical, open
词性: adjective
