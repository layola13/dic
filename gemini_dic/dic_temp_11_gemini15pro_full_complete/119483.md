micron:/ˈmaɪkrɒn/ (UK), /ˈmaɪkrɑːn/ (US)| n. |微米，百万分之一米|A unit of length equal to one millionth of a meter, typically used to measure very small distances or thicknesses.|The dust particles were less than 10 microns in size.|Synonyms: micrometer, µm|Antonyms: kilometer, meter|noun
