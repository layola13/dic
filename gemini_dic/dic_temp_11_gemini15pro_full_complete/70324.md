field-testing:/ˈfiːld ˌtɛstɪŋ/ |n.|现场测试，实地测试|The process of testing something (e.g., a product, a procedure, a theory) in a real-world environment, rather than in a laboratory or controlled setting.| The software underwent rigorous field-testing before its official release.  该软件在正式发布前经过了严格的现场测试。|近义词：beta testing, pilot testing, real-world testing|反义词: lab testing, simulated testing|词性: 名词

field-test:/ˈfiːld ˌtɛst/ |v.|现场测试，实地测试|To test something in a real-world environment.|The company field-tested the new car in extreme weather conditions.  该公司在极端天气条件下对新车进行了现场测试。|近义词：beta test, pilot test|反义词: lab test, simulate|词性: 动词
