dalits:/ˈdɑːlɪts/ /ˈdælɪts/
noun
贱民，达利特人
Members of the lowest caste in India, formerly known as "untouchables".  They have historically faced severe social discrimination and oppression.
The government has implemented policies to uplift the dalits and provide them with equal opportunities. 政府已经实施了政策来提升贱民的地位，并为他们提供平等的机会。
The dalits continue to face discrimination in many areas of life.  贱民在生活的许多领域仍然面临歧视。
synonyms: untouchables, oppressed, marginalized
antonyms: privileged, elite, upper class
