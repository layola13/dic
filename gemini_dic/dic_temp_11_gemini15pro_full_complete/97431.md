infuriate:/ɪnˈfjʊərieɪt/ /ɪnˈfjʊriˌeɪt/ | vt. | 激怒，使大怒 | To make someone extremely angry. |
His constant lateness infuriated his boss. 他总是迟到，这让他老板非常生气。

The airline's decision to cancel the flight infuriated the passengers. 航空公司取消航班的决定激怒了乘客。


近义词: enrage, anger, madden, provoke | 反义词: calm, soothe, placate | 词性: 动词
