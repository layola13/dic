nontemperately:/nɒnˈtɛmpərɪtli/
adverb
不节制地，无节制地
Not in a temperate or moderate manner; excessively or immoderately.
He indulged nontemperately in rich food and drink. 他毫无节制地沉溺于丰富的食物和饮料。
Near Synonyms: immoderately, excessively, intemperately, unrestrainedly
Antonyms: moderately, temperately, restrainedly, abstemiously
詞性: 副詞
