coreferential:/ˌkɔːrɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/| adj.|共同指代的，同指的|Referring to the same thing or person.|Example: "He" and "the president" are coreferential in the sentence "The president addressed the nation, and he spoke about the economy."  “他”和“总统”在这个句子中是同指的：“总统向全国发表讲话，他谈到了经济。”|Example:  The pronouns "she" and "her" are coreferential, both referring to Mary. 代词“她”和“她”是同指的，都指的是玛丽。|近义词：co-referring, synonymous, equivalent|反义词: non-coreferential|词性:adjective
