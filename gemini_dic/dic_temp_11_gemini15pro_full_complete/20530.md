bizarre:/bɪˈzɑːr/ (US), /bɪˈzɑː(r)/ (UK)| adj.|奇异的，古怪的，怪诞的| Strikingly out of the ordinary; unusual in appearance, style, or general character and often involving incongruous or unexpected elements; strange.|例句：The story had a bizarre ending.  这个故事的结局很离奇。

例句：She wore a bizarre outfit to the party. 她穿了一套怪诞的服装去参加派对。|近义词：strange, weird, odd, eccentric, unusual, unconventional, atypical|反义词：normal, ordinary, conventional, typical|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bizarre*, from Italian *bizzarro* "irascible, quick-tempered," of uncertain origin.  Possibly from Basque *bizar* "beard," perhaps via an intermediate sense of "proud, bold."  The shift in meaning to "strange, odd" occurred in French.
