adhere:/ədˈhɪr/ /ædˈhɪr/| vi.|粘附；坚持；遵守；依附；追随|To stick firmly to a surface or substance; or to follow a particular belief or set of rules.|The tiles won't adhere to the wall properly if the adhesive is not strong enough. 如果粘合剂不够牢固，瓷砖将无法正确粘附在墙上。
The doctor told him to adhere to a strict diet. 医生告诉他要严格遵守饮食规定。|近义词：stick, cling, comply, conform, abide by|反义词:detach, separate, deviate|verb

adherent:/ədˈhɪrənt/ /ædˈhɪrənt/| n.|追随者；拥护者|A person who supports a particular party, person, or set of ideas.|She is a strong adherent of the environmental movement. 她是环保运动的坚定拥护者。
He gained many adherents to his cause. 他为自己的事业赢得了许多支持者。|近义词：follower, supporter, advocate|反义词:opponent, critic|noun

adherence:/ədˈhɪrəns/ /ædˈhɪrəns/| n.|遵守；坚持；依附|The fact of someone behaving according to a particular rule, belief, or set of ideas.|Strict adherence to the rules is expected of all students. 所有学生都应严格遵守规则。
Her adherence to the vegan lifestyle is admirable. 她对纯素食生活方式的坚持令人钦佩。|近义词：compliance, conformity, observance|反义词:violation, breach, defiance|noun

adhesive:/ədˈhiːsɪv/ /ædˈhiːsɪv/| n. adj.|粘合剂；粘性的|A sticky substance that is used for joining things together; sticky.|Apply a strong adhesive to both surfaces. 在两个表面都涂上强力粘合剂。
The adhesive tape held the bandage in place.  胶带将绷带固定到位。|近义词：glue, paste, sticky|反义词:non-adhesive|noun, adjective
