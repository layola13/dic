bifariously:/baɪˈfɛəriəsli/
adverb
以两种方式地；分叉地
in two different ways or directions; branching in two directions.
The river bifariously flowed around the island. 这条河以两种方式绕着岛屿流动。
bifariously, the path split into two, leading to different destinations. 这条小路分叉成两条，通往不同的目的地。
近义词:dichotomously, forkedly
反义词:uniformedly, singularly
词性:副词
