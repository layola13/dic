gothamist:/ˈɡɑːθəmɪst/| n.|哥谭主义者；纽约市博客Gothamist的读者或撰稿人|A person interested in New York City news, culture, and events, often associated with the website Gothamist.|The gothamist posted a scathing review of the new restaurant. 这位哥谭主义者发表了一篇对这家新餐厅的尖刻评论。|近义词：New Yorker, city dweller|反义词:rural resident|词性:noun

Etymology:
Gotham + -ist

Gotham: a nickname for New York City, possibly derived from the village of Gotham in Nottinghamshire, England, whose inhabitants were reputed to be foolish.
-ist: a suffix forming nouns denoting a person who practices or is occupied with something, or holds certain principles.
