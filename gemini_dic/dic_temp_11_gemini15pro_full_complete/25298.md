bugger:/ˈbʌɡə(r)/ (UK) /ˈbʌɡər/ (US)| n. vt.|讨厌鬼；家伙；难事；（用于加强语气）该死的；与…性交|A term of endearment, annoyance, or contempt, depending on context. Also a mildly offensive verb meaning to damage or ruin something, or to have sexual intercourse with someone.|

例句：
That bugger stole my parking space!  那个混蛋偷了我的停车位！

He's a lazy bugger. 他是个懒鬼。


近义词：chap, fellow, guy, bloke (for person); damn, blast (for exclamation); ruin, spoil (for verb)|
反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the specific meaning used)|
词性:Noun, Verb


bugger:/ˈbʌɡə(r)/ (UK) /ˈbʌɡər/ (US)| vt.|搞砸；毁掉|To damage or ruin something.|

例句：
I've buggered up my computer. 我把我的电脑搞坏了。

Don't bugger about with my things! 别乱动我的东西！


近义词：ruin, spoil, damage, break|
反义词:fix, repair|
词性:Verb
