freezable:/ˈfriːzəbl/| adj.|可冷冻的，可冷藏的|Something that is freezable can be frozen (preserved by freezing) without being damaged.|This soup is freezable and can be stored for up to three months.  这汤可以冷冻，可以储存长达三个月。|Synonyms: refrigeratable, preservable|Antonyms: unfreezable, perishable|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From freeze + -able.  来自 freeze（冷冻）+ -able（可...的）
