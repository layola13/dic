overclutter:/ˌoʊvərˈklʌtər/| v. |使过于杂乱，堆满|To fill a space or area with too many things, making it messy and disorganized.|She overcluttered her desk with papers and books. 她把她的桌子堆满了文件和书籍。|The room was overcluttered with furniture. 房间里堆满了家具。|近义词：clutter, overcrowd, fill, stuff|反义词: declutter, tidy, organize|词性:动词

overcluttered:/ˌoʊvərˈklʌtərd/| adj. |过于杂乱的，堆满的|Filled with too many things, making it messy and disorganized.|Her overcluttered desk made it difficult to find anything. 她杂乱的桌子让她很难找到任何东西。|The overcluttered room felt suffocating. 杂乱的房间让人感到窒息。|近义词：cluttered, messy, disorganized, crammed|反义词: tidy, organized, neat, spacious|词性:形容词
