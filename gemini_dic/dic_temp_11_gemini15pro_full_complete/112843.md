luck-of-the-draw:/ˌlʌk əv ðə ˈdrɔː/
n.
运气; 抽签的结果; 命中注定的事
the result of chance rather than something you can control
It's just the luck of the draw who gets the prize.  得到奖品全凭运气。
Synonyms: chance, fate, fortune, destiny
Antonyms:  control, choice, intention
