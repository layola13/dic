nonnaturalist:/ˌnɒnˈnætʃrəlɪst/ | adj. | 非自然主义的 | Not pertaining to naturalism; rejecting the view that everything arises from natural properties and causes. |  Nonnaturalist ethical theories posit values that are not reducible to natural properties. 非自然主义伦理理论假设存在不可还原为自然属性的价值观。|  Some philosophers argue for a nonnaturalist account of moral properties. 一些哲学家主张对道德属性进行非自然主义的解释。 | antinaturalist, supernaturalist | naturalist | adjective
