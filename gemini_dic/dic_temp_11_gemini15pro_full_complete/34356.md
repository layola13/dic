classical: /ˈklæsɪkəl/ | adj. | 古典的；经典的；传统的 | Relating to ancient Greek or Roman literature, art, or culture. Also, of a kind that has been generally accepted as representing high standards of excellence; of recognized value or authority. |  She studied classical literature at university. 她大学时学习古典文学。|  Mozart's music is considered classical. 莫扎特的音乐被认为是古典音乐。| synonyms: traditional, conventional, standard, established | antonyms: modern, contemporary, avant-garde | adjective
