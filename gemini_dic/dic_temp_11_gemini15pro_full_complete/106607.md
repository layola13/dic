kufu:/ˈkʊfuː/ | n. | 库夫 | An Egyptian dish made of balls of fufu (boiled starchy vegetables, such as plantains or yams, pounded into a dough-like consistency). It is a staple food in many parts of Africa. | Example:  She served the rich stew with a side of fufu. 她配着浓汤吃了一份富富。| Synonym:  foufou, ugali (depending on the region) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific food) | noun
