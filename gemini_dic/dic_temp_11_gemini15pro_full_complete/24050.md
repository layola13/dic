breezy:/ˈbriːzi/| adj.|有微风的；轻松愉快的；活泼的|Characterized by a light and fresh breeze; lively and cheerful; conveying a sense of lightheartedness and ease.|The breezy weather made it perfect for a picnic. 微风习习的天气非常适合野餐。|近义词：airy, windy, fresh, lively, cheerful|反义词：calm, still, stuffy, serious, somber|词性:形容词
词源: From breeze + -y.  Ultimately from the Old French *brise.
