overgrades:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪdz/
v.|过高评价；评级过高|To assign a higher grade or rank to something than it deserves.|The teacher accidentally overgraded several of the essays.|近义词：overestimate, overvalue, exaggerate|反义词: underestimate, undervalue, downgrade|词性:动词

overgrades:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪdz/
n.|过高评价；评级过高（罕见）|The act of assigning a higher grade or rank than deserved.|The overgrades on the final exam skewed the class average.|近义词：overestimation, overvaluation|反义词: underestimation, undervaluation|词性:名词
