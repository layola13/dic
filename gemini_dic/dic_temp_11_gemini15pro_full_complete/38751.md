condominial:/ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəl/ | adj. | 公寓的；共同管理的；共有的 | Relating to or characteristic of a condominium; jointly owned or managed. |  The condominial association is responsible for maintaining the building. 公寓协会负责维护大楼。

Condominial fees often cover amenities like a pool or gym. 公寓费用通常包括游泳池或健身房等设施。 |  joint, shared, common | private, separate, individual | adjective
