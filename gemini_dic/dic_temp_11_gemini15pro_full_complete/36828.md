colonisable:/ˈkɒlənaɪzəbl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzəbl/ (US)| adj.|可殖民的；可开拓的|Able to be colonized or settled.  A place or environment suitable for establishing a colony. |Example 1: The newly discovered island was deemed colonisable due to its fertile land and abundant resources.  这个新发现的岛屿因其肥沃的土地和丰富的资源被认为是可殖民的。

Example 2:  Scientists are searching for colonisable planets in other solar systems. 科学家们正在其他太阳系中寻找适宜殖民的行星。|近义词：settleable, habitable|反义词：uninhabitable, uncolonisable|词性:形容词
