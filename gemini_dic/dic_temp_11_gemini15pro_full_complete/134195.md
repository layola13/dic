nonincrease:/ˌnɒnˈɪnkriːs/ | n. | 不增加；无增长 | Absence of an increase; the state of not increasing. | The nonincrease in sales this quarter is concerning.  这个季度销售额的无增长令人担忧。| The graph shows a period of nonincrease in profits. 图表显示了一段利润无增长的时期。 | 近义词: stagnation, standstill, plateau | 反义词: increase, rise, growth | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix) from Latin *non "not" + increase (from Old French encreistre, from Latin increscere "to grow in, increase").
