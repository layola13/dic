endothorax:/ˈɛndoʊˌθɔːræks/
n.
胸内骨骼
The internal skeletal framework of the thorax in insects.
The endothorax provides support for the flight muscles.  胸内骨骼为飞行肌肉提供支撑。
The entomologist studied the endothorax of the beetle. 昆虫学家研究了甲虫的胸内骨骼。
近义词: internal thoracic skeleton
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps consider exoskeleton)
词性: noun
