four-wheel-drive: /ˌfɔːr ˈwiːl ˈdraɪv/ /ˌfɔːr ˈhwiːl ˈdraɪv/
| n. adj.|四轮驱动|A system in which engine power is transmitted to all four wheels of a vehicle, providing increased traction.  Can also be used adjectivally to describe a vehicle with this system.|例句：The four-wheel-drive helped us navigate the muddy road. 四轮驱动帮助我们顺利通过了泥泞的道路。|近义词：all-wheel-drive, 4x4, 4WD|反义词: two-wheel-drive, 2WD|词性:名词，形容词
