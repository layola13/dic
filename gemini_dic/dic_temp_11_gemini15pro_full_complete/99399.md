interdigitate:/ˌɪntərˈdɪdʒɪteɪt/| v. |交错，互相穿插|To interlock like the fingers of clasped hands.|The branches of the two trees interdigitate. 这两棵树的树枝互相交错。|近义词：interlock, intertwine, mesh|反义词：separate, disengage|词性:动词
interdigitate:/ˌɪntərˈdɪdʒɪteɪt/| adj. |交错的，互相穿插的|Having the quality of being interlocked like the fingers of clasped hands.|The interdigitating branches formed a dense canopy. 交错的树枝形成了茂密的树冠。|近义词：interlocked, intertwined, meshed|反义词：separated, disengaged|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *interdigitātus*, past participle of *interdigitāre* (“to interweave the fingers”), from inter- (“between”) + digitāre (“to furnish with fingers”), from digitus (“finger”).
