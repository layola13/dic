disavouch:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊtʃ/
verb
否认，拒绝对…承担责任
to deny responsibility for or connection with
例句：He disavouched any knowledge of the plot. 他否认对该阴谋有任何了解。

例句：The company disavouched the employee's actions. 公司拒绝对该员工的行为承担责任。

近义词：deny, repudiate, renounce, disclaim
反义词：acknowledge, admit, avow, confess
词性：v.
