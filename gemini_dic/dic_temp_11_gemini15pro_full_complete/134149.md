nonimpressionability:/ˌnɑːnɪmˌprɛʃənəˈbɪləti/
n.
不易受影响；无动于衷
The state or quality of being nonimpressionable; resistance to being influenced or impressed.
His nonimpressionability made him a difficult person to persuade.  他的无动于衷使他很难被说服。
Near Synonyms: impassivity, imperturbability, insensitivity, aloofness, detachment, indifference
Antonyms: impressionability, sensitivity, responsiveness, susceptibility
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + impressionability.  Impressionability is derived from impressionable (capable of being impressed), from impression + -able. Impression ultimately comes from the Latin "impressio," meaning a pressing into.
