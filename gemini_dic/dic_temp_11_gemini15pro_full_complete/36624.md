collectives:/kəˈlɛktɪvz/| n.|集体；集体企业；集体农庄|A group of people or organizations working together; a farm, business, or other organization owned and run by its members|The government encouraged the formation of farming collectives.|Synonyms: groups, associations, cooperatives, unions|Antonyms: individuals, single entities|noun

collectives:/kəˈlɛktɪvz/| adj.|集体的；共同的|Of or relating to a collective; done or shared by all members of a group|Collective bargaining is a process of negotiation between employers and a group of employees.|Synonyms: joint, shared, combined, communal|Antonyms: individual, separate, private|adjective
