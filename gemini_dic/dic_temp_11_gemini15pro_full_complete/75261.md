frondent:/ˈfrɒndənt/ | adj. | 茂盛的；多叶的 | Having a large number of leaves; leafy. Characterized by abundance of leaves or foliage. |  The jungle was dense and frondent, teeming with life.  丛林茂密葱茏，充满了生机。

The ferns grew in frondent profusion along the stream bank. 沿着河岸，蕨类植物繁茂地生长。 | 近义词: leafy, luxuriant, lush | 反义词: bare, barren, sparse | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frondent-, present participle stem of frondēre "to put forth leaves," from frōns (genitive frondis) "leaf," especially of a fern.
