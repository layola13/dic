hatteria:/hæˈtɪəriə/ | n. | 喙头蜥；楔齿蜥 | A reptile of the order Rhynchocephalia, found only in New Zealand, resembling a lizard but having a third eye on the top of its head. | The tuatara is the only surviving species of hatteria.  喙头蜥是唯一幸存的楔齿蜥物种。| The hatteria is a fascinating example of a "living fossil". 喙头蜥是“活化石”的一个引人入胜的例子。 | synonyms: tuatara, sphenodon | antonyms: None (It's a unique species) | noun
