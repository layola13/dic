orogeny: /ɔːˈrɒdʒəni/ /ɔːˈrɑːdʒəni/
noun
造山运动，造山期
A process in which a section of the earth's crust is folded and deformed by lateral compression to form a mountain range.
The Himalayas are a result of the ongoing orogeny between the Indian and Eurasian tectonic plates. 喜马拉雅山脉是印度板块和欧亚板块之间持续造山运动的结果。

The Appalachian orogeny occurred hundreds of millions of years ago. 阿巴拉契亚造山运动发生在数亿年前。

Synonyms: mountain building, tectonic uplift
Antonyms: erosion, weathering
词性: 名词
