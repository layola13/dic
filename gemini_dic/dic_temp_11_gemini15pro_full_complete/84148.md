half-offended:/ˌhɑːf əˈfɛndɪd/ | adj. | 半生气；略有不悦的；有点被冒犯的 | Feeling slightly offended or insulted, but not deeply so.  A mixture of amusement and annoyance. |  He gave a half-offended, half-amused laugh. 他发出一种半生气半觉得好笑的笑声。 |  slightly offended, mildly insulted, a little annoyed, miffed |  delighted, pleased, thrilled | adjective
