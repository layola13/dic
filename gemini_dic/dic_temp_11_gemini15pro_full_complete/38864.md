confederation:/kənˌfedəˈreɪʃən/
n.
联盟，联邦
an organization that consists of a number of parties or groups united in an alliance or league.
The thirteen colonies formed a confederation. 十三个殖民地组成了一个联邦。
近义词:league, alliance, union
反义词:division, separation
词性:Noun

词源:
From Late Latin *confederatio, from Latin confoederatus past participle of confoederare “to unite by a league”,  from com-, intensive prefix + foederare “to make a treaty”, from foedus “treaty, league”
