Baháʼí: /bəˈhɑːʔiː/ (US), /bæˈhaɪ/ (UK)| adj. n.|巴哈伊教的；巴哈伊教徒|Relating to the Baháʼí Faith, a monotheistic religion emphasizing the spiritual unity of all humankind.  A follower of the Baháʼí Faith.|例句：She is a Baháʼí. 她是巴哈伊教徒。|例句：The Baháʼí teachings promote peace. 巴哈伊教的教义宣扬和平。|近义词：(for n.) believer, adherent|反义词: (for n.) non-believer, opponent|词性: adjective, noun|词源: From Persian بهائی (Bahāʼī), meaning "follower of Baháʼu'lláh," the founder of the Baháʼí Faith.
