overfoolish: /ˌoʊvərˈfuːlɪʃ/
adjective
过于愚蠢的，极其愚蠢的
Excessively foolish; more foolish than is usual or expected.
His overfoolish decision cost him dearly. 他极其愚蠢的决定让他付出了高昂的代价。

It was overfoolish of him to invest all his money in that scheme.  他把所有的钱都投资到那个计划中，真是太愚蠢了。


近义词: extremely foolish, excessively silly, ridiculously foolish, utterly stupid, incredibly naive
反义词: wise, sensible, prudent, intelligent, astute
词性: adjective
