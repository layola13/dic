microfibre: /ˈmaɪkrəʊˌfaɪbər/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊˌfaɪbər/ (US)
n.|超细纤维|A very fine synthetic fibre, thinner than a human hair, used to make fabrics.|This towel is made of microfibre and is very absorbent. 这条毛巾是用超细纤维做的，吸水性很好。|近义词：microfiber, synthetic fiber|反义词: natural fiber|词性:名词
Etymology: micro- + fibre. "Micro-" comes from Greek "mikros" meaning "small". "Fibre" comes from Old French "fibre" and Latin "fibra".
