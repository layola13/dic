and-all:/ændˈɔːl/ | adv. | 以及其他一切；等等；全部 | including everything else; et cetera; altogether |  He brought his wife and children and-all to the party. 他把妻子、孩子等等都带到了聚会上。 | 近义词: and everything, and so on, and the rest | 反义词: none, nothing | 词性: 副词

词源: "and all" 源自古英语 "and eall,"  "and" 意为“和”，"eall" 意为“全部”。
