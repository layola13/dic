frustrative:/ˈfrʌstreɪtɪv/| adj.|令人沮丧的，使人泄气的|Causing frustration; tending to frustrate.  Something that prevents someone from achieving something.|The lack of clear instructions was frustrative.|Synonyms: annoying, exasperating, irritating, vexing, trying|Antonyms: satisfying, gratifying, rewarding|adjective
