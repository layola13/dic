nought:| n. | 零；无；没有 |  Meaning zero, nothing, or of no value. |例句：It all came to nought.  这一切都化为乌有。| 近义词: zero, nothing, nil | 反义词: something, anything
naught:| n. | 零；无；没有 | Meaning zero, nothing, or of no value. |例句：Set the value to naught. 将值设置为零。| 近义词: zero, nothing, nil | 反义词: something, anything


**Etymology:**  From Old English *nāwiht*, *nōwiht*, meaning "nothing," with *nā* "no" and *wiht* "thing, creature."
