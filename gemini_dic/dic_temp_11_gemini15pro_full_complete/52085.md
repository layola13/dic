diamagnetic:/ˌdaɪəˌmæɡˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
抗磁性的
Relating to a substance or material that is repelled by a magnet.  A diamagnetic substance has a permeability less than that of a vacuum.
例句：Bismuth is a diamagnetic metal. 铋是一种抗磁性金属。

例句：Diamagnetic materials create an induced magnetic field in a direction opposite to an externally applied magnetic field. 抗磁性材料会产生一个与外加磁场方向相反的感应磁场。

近义词: nonmagnetic
反义词: paramagnetic, ferromagnetic
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dia- (Greek 'through, across') + magnetic.
