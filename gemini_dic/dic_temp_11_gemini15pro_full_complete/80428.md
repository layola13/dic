gondola:/ˈɡɒndələ(UK) /ˈɡɑːndələ(US)/| n.|贡多拉，狭长小船；吊舱|A traditional Venetian boat, long and narrow, propelled by a single oar. Also, an enclosed cabin suspended from a cable, used as a ski lift or for sightseeing.|例句：We took a gondola ride through the canals of Venice. 我们乘坐贡多拉穿过威尼斯的运河。

例句：The ski gondola offered breathtaking views of the mountains.  滑雪缆车的吊舱提供了令人叹为观止的山景。|近义词：boat, barge, cable car|反义词: None|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Venetian Italian *gondola*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Late Latin *cunula* (“small boat, cradle”), from Latin *cuneus* (“wedge”).  Alternatively, possibly from Greek *kondura* (“kind of Byzantine ship”), or from Slavic *ladja* (“boat”).
