micro-engineering:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/| n.|微工程; 微型工程|The design, construction, and manufacture of very small devices and systems, typically at the micrometer scale or smaller, involving the application of principles from engineering and other scientific disciplines.|Micro-engineering is crucial for the development of micro-robots.|近义词：microtechnology, nanotechnology|反义词:macro-engineering|noun
