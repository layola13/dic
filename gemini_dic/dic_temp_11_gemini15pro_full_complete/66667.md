exocarp:/ˈeksoʊkɑːrp/| n. |外果皮|The outermost layer of the pericarp of a fruit, i.e. the skin.|The exocarp of a peach is fuzzy. 桃子的外果皮是毛茸茸的。|Synonyms: epicarp, rind, skin|Antonyms: endocarp, mesocarp|Noun

epicarp:/ˈɛpɪˌkɑːrp/| n. |外果皮|The outer layer of the pericarp of a fruit.|The tough epicarp of the coconut protects the seed. 椰子的坚韧外果皮保护着种子。|Synonyms: exocarp, rind, skin|Antonyms: endocarp, mesocarp|Noun
