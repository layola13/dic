novaculite:/noʊˈvækjʊˌlaɪt/| n. |硅质岩，磨刀石|A hard, dense, fine-grained siliceous rock, often used for sharpening tools. |Example 1: The ancient Egyptians used novaculite to sharpen their tools. 古埃及人用硅质岩来磨快他们的工具。

Example 2: Novaculite is a highly valued material for making sharpening stones. 硅质岩是制作磨刀石的珍贵材料。|近义词：chert, flint, whetstone|反义词: N/A|Noun
