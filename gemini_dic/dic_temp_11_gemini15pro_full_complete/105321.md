kibosh:/ˈkaɪbɒʃ/ (US) /ˈkɪbɒʃ/ (UK)| n.|禁止，阻止，压制|A sudden or unexpected end to something.|The principal put the kibosh on the students' plan to have a party during school hours. 校长禁止了学生在上课时间举办派对的计划.|Synonyms: veto, stop, halt, block, nix, squash, put the lid on|Antonyms: allow, permit, encourage, promote|noun

kibosh:/ˈkaɪbɒʃ/ (US) /ˈkɪbɒʃ/ (UK)| v.|禁止，阻止，压制 (通常用 put the kibosh on sth.)|To put a stop to something.|The council put the kibosh on the development project. 理事会阻止了这个开发项目。|Synonyms: veto, stop, halt, block, nix, squash, put the lid on|Antonyms: allow, permit, encourage, promote|verb
