emphaticalness:/ɪmˈfætɪkəlnəs/
|n.|强调,坚决,着重|The quality or state of being emphatic; forcefulness of expression.  The speaker delivered his message with great emphaticalness.|She stated her refusal with considerable emphaticalness. 她非常坚决地表明了她的拒绝。|近义词：emphasis, forcefulness, insistence, vehemence|反义词：hesitancy, indecision, vagueness, weakness|词性:名词
Etymology: From emphatic + -ness. Emphatic derives from Late Latin emphaticus, from Greek emphatikos, from emphainein "to exhibit," from en- "in" + phainein "to show."
