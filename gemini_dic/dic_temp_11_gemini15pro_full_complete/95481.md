incivilly:/ɪnˈsɪvɪlli/
adverb
不文明地；粗鲁地
in a rude or impolite manner.
He behaved incivilly towards the waiter. 他对服务员很不礼貌。
Synonyms:rudely, impolitely, discourteously, disrespectfully, unmannerly
Antonyms:politely, civilly, courteously, respectfully
词性:副词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + civilly (in a civilized manner).  Ultimately from Latin *civis (citizen).
