arachnophobe:/əˈræknəˌfoʊb/| n. |蜘蛛恐惧症患者|A person with an extreme or irrational fear of spiders.|例句：The arachnophobe screamed when they saw a tiny spider in the corner. 那个蜘蛛恐惧症患者在角落里看到一只小蜘蛛时尖叫起来。

例句：Even the picture of a spider could trigger a panic attack in the arachnophobe. 即使是蜘蛛的照片也会引发蜘蛛恐惧症患者的恐慌症发作。|近义词：arachnophobia sufferer|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly someone who loves spiders)|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek *aráchnē* (spider) + *-phobos* (fear).
