dissimulating:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪtɪŋ/| adj. v.|掩饰的，假装的；掩饰，假装|concealing one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs.|He was dissimulating his true intentions. 他在掩饰他的真实意图。|近义词: feigning, pretending, masking, concealing|反义词: revealing, exposing, honest, truthful|词性: adjective (present participle of dissimulate, which can be used as a verb)

dissimulate:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪt/| v.|掩饰，假装|To conceal one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs.|She dissimulated her anger with a smile. 她用微笑掩饰她的愤怒。|近义词: feign, pretend, mask, conceal|反义词: reveal, expose|词性: verb

词源: From Latin dissimulare, from dis- "apart, away" + simulare "to make like, imitate," from similis "like."
