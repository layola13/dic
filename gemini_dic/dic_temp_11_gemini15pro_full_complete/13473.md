atrophic:/əˈtrɒfɪk/ (US) /əˈtrɒfɪk/ (UK)| adj.|萎缩的，衰退的|Relating to or characterized by atrophy (a decrease in size or wasting away of a body part or tissue).|例句：The doctor noted the patient's atrophic muscles. 医生注意到病人肌肉萎缩。

例句：The prolonged lack of use led to atrophic changes in the limb. 长期缺乏使用导致肢体出现萎缩性变化。|近义词：wasted, shrunken, withered, degenerate|反义词：developed, healthy, robust|词性:形容词

atrophy:/ˈætrəfi/ (US) /ˈætrəfi/ (UK)| n. v.|萎缩，衰退| (n.) The wasting away of a body organ or tissue; any progressive decline or failure; (v.) To undergo atrophy.|例句：Muscle atrophy can occur after prolonged inactivity.  肌肉会在长时间不活动后萎缩。

例句：His artistic skills seemed to atrophy from lack of use. 他的艺术技能似乎因缺乏使用而衰退。|近义词：(n.) degeneration, deterioration, decline; (v.) wither, shrink, decay|反义词：(n.) growth, development; (v.) flourish, thrive|词性:名词,动词
