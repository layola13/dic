opacus:/oʊˈpeɪkəs/| adj.|不透明的；晦涩的；暗的|Shaded or dark; not transparent or translucent; hard to understand; obscure.|例句：The liquid was opacus, like mud.  这种液体不透明，像泥巴一样。|例句：His explanation was opacus and confusing. 他的解释晦涩难懂。|近义词：opaque, obscure, murky, dim, dark|反义词：transparent, clear, lucid, bright|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *opacus* "shaded, dark, obscure," related to *ops* "power, might, wealth, resources," perhaps with a sense of "covered by wealth" (source: Watkins).  De Vaan connects it to *umbro-* "shadow" and ultimately to a Proto-Indo-European root meaning "shadow, cloud."
