luxuriations:/lʌɡˌʒʊəriˈeɪʃənz/
|n.|奢侈, 豪华, 沉溺|The act of indulging in luxury; luxurious behavior or lifestyle.  A state of great comfort and extravagant living.|He spent his days in idle luxuriations, enjoying fine wines and gourmet meals. 他终日沉溺于奢侈的生活，享受着美酒佳肴。|近义词：indulgences, extravagances, pleasures|反义词：austerities, privations, hardships|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *luxuriationem, accusative of *luxuriatio (“luxuriance, extravagance”), from Latin luxuriari (“to be luxuriant, to abound”), from luxus (“abundance, excess, luxury”).
