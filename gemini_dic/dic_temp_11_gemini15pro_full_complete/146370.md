overflogged: /ˌoʊvərˈflɒɡd/ (British) /ˌoʊvərˈflɑːɡd/ (American)| adj.|过度鞭打的；过度使用的；滥用的|Overused or exploited to the point of exhaustion or diminishing returns.|The overflogged topic of political corruption needs a fresh perspective.|近义词：overused, overworked, exhausted, stale, hackneyed|反义词: fresh, original, novel|词性:形容词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "flog" (verb meaning "to whip or beat severely"). The original sense referred to literal whipping, but the meaning extended metaphorically to overuse or exploitation.
