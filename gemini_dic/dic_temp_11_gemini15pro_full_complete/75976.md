funnel:/ˈfʌnəl/ (US), /ˈfʌn(ə)l/ (UK)| n. vt.|漏斗；漏斗云；烟囱|a cone-shaped utensil with a tube at the apex for conducting liquid or other substances through a small opening, or for filtering.|She used a funnel to pour the batter into the cake pan. 她用漏斗把面糊倒入蛋糕模具里。|The tornado formed a massive funnel cloud. 龙卷风形成了一个巨大的漏斗云。|近义词：channel, cone, tube|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用broad opening 宽口)|名词, 动词

funnel:/ˈfʌnəl/ (US), /ˈfʌn(ə)l/ (UK)| vt.|引导；使成漏斗状；汇集|to guide or move (something) through a narrow opening or into a particular place; to concentrate (something) into a particular area or activity.|The police funneled the crowd into the stadium. 警察将人群引导到体育场内。|The company is funneling resources into its new marketing campaign. 公司正将资源集中到新的营销活动中。|近义词：channel, direct, guide|反义词: disperse, scatter|动词
