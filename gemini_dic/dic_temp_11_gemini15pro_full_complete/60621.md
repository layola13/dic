elders:/ˈeldərz/| n.|长辈，长者，前辈|People often seek advice from their elders. 人们经常向长辈寻求建议。|例句：The village elders met to discuss the problem. 村里的长者们开会讨论这个问题。

例句：We should respect our elders. 我们应该尊重我们的长辈。

近义词：seniors, older people, ancestors|反义词:youngsters, juniors|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *eldres, plural of *eldre, from Old English *ieldran, plural of ieldra (“older, elder”), from Proto-Germanic *aldizōn (“older, elder”), comparative form of *aldaz (“old, of age”), from Proto-Indo-European *al- (“to grow, nourish”). Cognate with Dutch ouders (“parents”), German Eltern (“parents”), Gothic 𐌰𐌻𐌸𐌴𐌹𐍃 (alþeis, “old”).
