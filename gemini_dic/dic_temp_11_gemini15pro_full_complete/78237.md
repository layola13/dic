gerdell:/ˈɡɜːrdəl/| n.| 腰带，束带|A belt or girdle, especially a decorative one.|She wore a beautiful gerdell around her waist. 她腰上系着一条漂亮的腰带。|近义词：belt, girdle, sash|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 looseness 松弛)|词性:noun

词源: From Old French "gerdelle" meaning "girdle". Ultimately from Frankish "*girdil," from Proto-Germanic "*gurdila-" meaning "belt, girdle."
