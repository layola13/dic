bequeathers:/bɪˈkwiːðərz/| n.|遗赠者，立遗嘱的人|A bequeather is a person who leaves property or money to someone else in their will.|My grandfather was the bequeather of a significant sum of money to charity. 我的祖父是一位给慈善机构留下巨额资金的遗赠者。|The bequeather stipulated that the money be used for medical research.  这位遗赠者规定这笔钱必须用于医学研究。|Synonyms: testator, donor, grantor|Antonyms: beneficiary, heir, recipient|noun
