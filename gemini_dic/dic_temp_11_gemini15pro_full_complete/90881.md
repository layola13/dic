hunkies:/ˈhʌŋkiz/
n.
Central and Eastern European immigrants (often derogatory)
A derogatory term referring to immigrants, particularly manual laborers, from Central and Eastern Europe.
"The hunkies were hired to do the back-breaking work in the mines."  那些东欧移民被雇来做矿井里繁重的体力活。
近义词:Bohunks, foreigners (although less specific and not necessarily derogatory)
反义词:(No direct antonym, but perhaps "native-born" or "citizen" in certain contexts)
词性:Noun (plural)
