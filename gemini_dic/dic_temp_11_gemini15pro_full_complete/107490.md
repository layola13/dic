lanceolated:/lænˈsiːəˌleɪtɪd/
adjective
披针形的，狭披针形的
Shaped like a lance head; tapering to a point at both ends.
The lanceolated leaves of the willow tree hung gracefully. 柳树披针形的叶子优雅地垂下来。
近义词:lance-shaped, tapering, pointed
反义词:rounded, ovate, cordate
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lanceolatus*, from lanceola (“small lance”), diminutive of lancea (“lance”).
