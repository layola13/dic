lactoalbumin:/ˌlæktoʊˈælbjʊmɪn/
n.
乳白蛋白
A protein found in milk, specifically whey protein.  It is one of the major protein components of milk, along with casein.
Cow's milk contains about 3.5% protein, of which approximately 20% is lactoalbumin.  牛奶含有约 3.5% 的蛋白质，其中约 20% 是乳白蛋白。
Whey protein, including lactoalbumin, is often used in protein supplements. 乳清蛋白，包括乳白蛋白，常用于蛋白质补充剂中。
近义词:whey protein, milk protein
反义词:casein (not a true antonym, but a contrasting milk protein)
