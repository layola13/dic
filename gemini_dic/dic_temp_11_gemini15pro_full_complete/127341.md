nazifies:/ˈnɑːtsɪfaɪz/
verb
纳粹化;使纳粹化
to cause to resemble Nazi Germany or its policies, typically by the implementation of extreme nationalism, racism, and aggressive militarism.
The historian argued that the propaganda campaign effectively nazified public opinion.  这位历史学家认为，宣传运动有效地将公众舆论纳粹化。
He accused the opposition party of trying to nazify the country. 他指责反对党试图将国家纳粹化。
synonyms:  fascistize, totalitarianize
antonyms:  democratize, liberalize
v.
