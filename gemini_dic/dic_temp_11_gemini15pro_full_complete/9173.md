antineutrals:/ˌæntiˈnjuːtrəlz/| n.|反中立派；中立的反对者|Those who oppose neutrality, especially in international conflicts.  Those who believe taking sides is necessary.|During the Cold War, many antineutrals argued that remaining neutral was tantamount to supporting the Soviet Union.|近义词：interventionists, belligerents|反义词:neutralists, pacifists|Noun
