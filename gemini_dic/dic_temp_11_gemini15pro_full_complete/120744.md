misanthropes:/ˌmɪsənˈθrəʊps/ /ˌmɪsənˈθroʊps/ | n. | 厌恶人类者；遁世者 | A person who dislikes humankind and avoids human society. |  He became a misanthrope after being betrayed by several people he trusted.  他被几个他信任的人背叛后变成了一个厌世者。

The old hermit was considered a misanthrope by the villagers.  这位老隐士被村民们认为是一个遁世者。


| 近义词: cynic, pessimist, recluse | 反义词: philanthropist, humanitarian | 词性: noun
