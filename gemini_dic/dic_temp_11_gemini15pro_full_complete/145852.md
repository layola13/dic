overdepressed:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈprɛst/ | adj. | 过度沮丧的，极度抑郁的 |  Experiencing an extremely low mood, beyond the usual range of sadness or depression.  Characterized by excessive feelings of hopelessness, worthlessness, and despair. | She felt overdepressed after losing her job and her apartment in the same week. 她在一周内失去了工作和公寓后感到极度沮丧。|  The constant rain and lack of sunlight left him feeling overdepressed.  持续的阴雨和缺乏阳光让他感到极度沮丧。 | 近义词: extremely depressed, severely depressed, profoundly depressed | 反义词: elated, euphoric, joyful | 词性: 形容词

词源: The word "overdepressed" is formed by combining the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessive" or "too much") with the adjective "depressed."  "Depressed" itself comes from the Latin word "depressus," the past participle of "deprimere," meaning "to press down."
