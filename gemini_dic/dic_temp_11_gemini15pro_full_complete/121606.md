misorganization:/ˌmɪsɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən/
n.
混乱，组织不良
the state of being badly organized.
The misorganization of the event led to chaos.  这次活动的组织不善导致了混乱。
The office was in a state of complete misorganization. 办公室完全处于混乱状态。
disorganization, disorder, mess, chaos
organization, order, tidiness
noun
