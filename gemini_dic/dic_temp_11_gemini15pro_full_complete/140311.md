ogler:/ˈoʊɡlər/| n.| 盯着看的人；色迷迷地注视的人 |Someone who stares, especially in a lecherous way.|He's such an ogler, always staring at women.| 近义词: lecher, gawker, starer | 反义词: gentleman, admirer (with respect) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the verb "ogle," which comes from the Low German "oegeln" or Dutch "ogen," meaning "to eye."
