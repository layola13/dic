miss-paces:/ˌmɪsˈpeɪsɪz/ | v. | 步调不一致，失去节奏 |  To fail to maintain the correct or expected pace, especially in walking or running with another person.  It can also refer to a more general sense of being out of sync or failing to keep up with the demands of a situation. | The runner mis-paced himself in the marathon and ran out of energy before the finish line. 马拉松运动员在比赛中节奏失调，在终点线前耗尽了体力。

The team mis-paced their project and missed several deadlines.  该团队对项目的节奏把握失误，错过了几个截止日期。 |  falter, stumble, flounder | synchronize, coordinate, keep up | verb
