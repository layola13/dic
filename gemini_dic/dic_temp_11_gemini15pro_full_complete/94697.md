improbatory:/ɪmˈprɒbətəri/ | adj. | 表示认可的；赞许的；嘉许的 | Expressing or conveying approbation; approving. |  His improbatory nod gave me the confidence to proceed. 他的赞许点头给了我继续下去的信心。| The teacher gave an improbatory smile to the student who answered correctly. 老师对正确回答的学生报以赞许的微笑。 | 近义词: approving, commendatory, laudatory, affirmative | 反义词: disapproving, condemnatory, critical, negative | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *improbatorius, from improbare (“to approve”), from in- (“not”) + probare (“to test, prove, approve”).  The sense shift from "disprove" to "approve" occurred in Late Latin.
