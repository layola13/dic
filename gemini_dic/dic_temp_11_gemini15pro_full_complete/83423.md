habilitate:/həˈbɪlɪteɪt/ /rɪˈhəˈbɪlɪteɪt/
verb
使能够；使胜任；使具备资格；使身心恢复正常；使复职；使复原
to make someone able to do something again after they have been ill or injured
例句：The program is designed to habilitate patients with severe head injuries.  这个项目旨在使头部严重受伤的病人恢复正常生活。

例句：The aim is to rehabilitate patients so they can lead a normal life. 目的是使病人康复，以便他们能够过上正常的生活。
近义词：rehabilitate, restore, enable
反义词：disable, incapacitate
词性：v.
