headwaters:/ˈhedˌwɔːtərz/ (US), /ˈhedˌwɔːtəz/ (UK)| n. |源头，上游|The area where a river or stream begins; the source.|例句：The headwaters of the Amazon River are in the Andes Mountains. 亚马逊河的源头在安第斯山脉。

例句：Protecting the headwaters is crucial for maintaining the health of the entire river system. 保护源头对于维护整个河流系统的健康至关重要。

近义词：source, origin, fountainhead|反义词:mouth, delta|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From head + waters. "Head" in this context refers to the beginning or top, and "waters" refers to the body of water. The term literally means the "beginning waters" of a river or stream.
