bargainee:/ˌbɑːrɡəˈniː/
noun
受让人；廉价货买主
a person who buys something, especially something that is sold at a price lower than its real value.
The bargainee got a great deal on the antique car.  这位受让人以很便宜的价格买到了那辆老爷车。
Synonyms: buyer, purchaser, customer, client
Antonyms: seller, vendor
词性: 名词
