affectedly:/əˈfɛktɪdli/
| adv. |做作地，矫揉造作地|In a manner that is unnatural and intended to impress.  |She laughed affectedly, trying to appear amused. 她矫揉造作地笑着，试图显得开心。|近义词：pretentiously, artificially, theatrically|反义词：naturally, genuinely, sincerely|词性:副词

affectedly:/əˈfɛktɪdli/
| adv. |不自然地|In a manner that is not natural or genuine.|He spoke affectedly, using words he clearly didn't understand. 他说话很做作，用的词显然是他不明白的。 |近义词：artificially, unnaturally|反义词：naturally, genuinely|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From affect + -ed + -ly.  "affect" comes from the Latin "affectus" meaning "disposition, affection, state of mind." "-ed" is a suffix forming past participles and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
