honourless:/ˈɒnələs/ (British) /ˈɑːnərləs/ (American)| adj. |不光彩的，不名誉的，无耻的|Without honour; shameful; disgraceful.  Lacking a sense of honour or integrity.|He committed an honourless act of betrayal. 他做了一件不光彩的背叛行为。|Synonyms: shameful, disgraceful, dishonourable, ignoble, base, vile, despicable|Antonyms: honourable, noble, respectable, virtuous|adjective

honourless:/ˈɒnələs/ (British) /ˈɑːnərləs/ (American)| adj. |不名誉的|Bringing shame or disgrace; unworthy of respect.|Her honourless behaviour brought shame to her family. 她不名誉的行为给她的家庭带来了耻辱。|Synonyms:  disreputable, shady, infamous|Antonyms: reputable, respected, esteemed|adjective
