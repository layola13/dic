enascent:/ɪˈnæsənt/ /iˈneɪsənt/| adj.|初生的，新生的，萌芽的|Beginning to exist or develop; having recently come into existence.|例句：The nascent democracy is still fragile.  这个新生的民主制度仍然很脆弱。

例句：We are witnessing the nascent stages of a new technological era. 我们正在见证一个新技术时代的萌芽阶段。|近义词：emerging, burgeoning, developing, incipient, dawning|反义词：declining, dying, mature, established, fading|词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *nascēns*, present participle of *nāscī* (“to be born”).
