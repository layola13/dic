anglicised:/ˈæŋɡlɪsaɪzd/ |adj.| 英国化的；英语化的 |Made English in form or character.  Adapted to the English manner or way of life. |The restaurant serves an anglicised version of Chinese food.  这家餐馆供应英国化的中式菜肴。| Example 2:  His name was anglicised from Karl to Charles when he moved to England. 当他搬到英国后，他的名字从卡尔英语化成了查尔斯。|Synonyms: Anglified, Westernized, Adapted|Antonyms:  Localized,  Nativized| Adjective
