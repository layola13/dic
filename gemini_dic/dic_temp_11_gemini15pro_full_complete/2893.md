advertising: /ˈædvərtaɪzɪŋ/ (US) /ˈædvətaɪzɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|广告；广告业；做广告|the activity or profession of producing advertisements for commercial products or services|例句：The company invested heavily in advertising. 这家公司在广告上投入了巨资。|例句：They are planning a new advertising campaign. 他们正在计划一个新的广告活动。|近义词：promotion, publicity, marketing|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From advertise + -ing.  Advertise comes from Middle French *advertiser*, from Late Latin *advertere* "to turn toward," from *ad-* "to" + *vertere* "to turn."
