outride:/ˌaʊtˈraɪd/
verb
胜过；比…骑得更好；安然度过
to ride better, faster, or longer than (someone)
例句：He easily outrode his companions. 他轻松地超过了他的同伴们。

例句：She outrode the storm. 她安然度过了风暴。
近义词：surpass, outstrip, outdo
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
词性：v.


outride:/ˌaʊtˈraɪd/
noun
户外骑行；越野骑行
a ride in the open air, especially across country
例句：They went for an outride in the countryside. 他们在乡间进行了户外骑行。

例句：The outride was a refreshing experience.  这次越野骑行是一次令人耳目一新的体验。
近义词：ride, excursion
反义词：（无明显的反义词）
词性：n.
