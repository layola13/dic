admen:/ˈædˌmɛn/
noun
广告人；广告从业人员
Men who work in advertising.
The admen are working on a new campaign for a car company. 广告人正在为一家汽车公司策划新的广告活动。
Synonyms: advertisers, marketers, advertising executives
Antonyms: consumers, audience, customers
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From "ad" (short for advertisement) + "men."  "Ad" ultimately comes from the Latin "advertere" (to turn towards).
