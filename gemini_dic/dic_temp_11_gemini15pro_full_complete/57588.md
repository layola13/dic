dramedy: /ˈdræmədi/ (US), /ˈdrɑːmədi/ (UK)| n. |悲喜剧|A genre of film, television, or theatre that blends elements of drama and comedy. |例句：The movie is a dramedy that explores the complexities of family relationships. 这部电影是一部探讨家庭关系复杂性的悲喜剧。

例句：Her latest play is a dramedy about a group of friends dealing with loss and change. 她最新的戏剧是一部关于一群朋友应对失去和改变的悲喜剧。|近义词：tragicomedy|反义词: - |词性: noun
