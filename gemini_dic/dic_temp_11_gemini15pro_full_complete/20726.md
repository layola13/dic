blamelessly:/ˈbleɪmləsli/| adv.|无可非议地，无过失地|In a manner that is free from blame or fault.  Without deserving censure or criticism.|He acted blamelessly in the entire affair. 他在整件事情中无可非议。|近义词：irreproachably, unimpeachably, faultlessly|反义词：culpably, guiltily|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From blameless + -ly.  Blameless from blame + -less. Blame from Old French blasmer "to blame, censure, condemn," from Late Latin blasphemare "to blaspheme," from Greek blasphemein "to speak ill of, revile, calumniate," from blasphemos "evil-speaking," from blaptein "to hurt, harm, impede" + pheme "speech, voice, utterance."
