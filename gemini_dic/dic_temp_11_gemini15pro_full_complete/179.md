abat-voix: /a.ba.vwa/ (French pronunciation)
| n. |Sound deflector; lampshade; whisper mute|A device used to redirect or soften sound, often found on lamps or telephones. It can also refer to a whisper mute used on musical instruments.|The abat-voix on the lamp cast a warm glow.  灯罩散发出温暖的光芒。|
The musician used an abat-voix to create a muted effect on their trumpet.  音乐家用一个弱音器在他们的小号上制造一种弱音效果。|
Synonyms: shade, diffuser, mute, damper|Antonyms: amplifier|noun
