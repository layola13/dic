hydrae:/ˈhaɪdriː/
n.
九头蛇座的(所有格)；九头蛇的(所有格)
Genitive case of Hydra, a constellation in the southern sky representing a water snake. Also used poetically or figuratively to refer to something monstrous or multi-headed.
The constellation of Hydrae is one of the largest. 九头蛇座是最大的星座之一。
The many heads of the corporate hydrae made it difficult to defeat.  这个企业巨兽的众多头颅使其难以被击败。
近义词: Hydra's, of the Hydra
反义词: (No direct antonym for a possessive form)
词性: Noun (Possessive)
