archpresbyter:/ˌɑːrkˈprɛzbɪtər/ | n. | 首席司祭，大司铎 | A chief priest; a priest of high rank, especially in the early church. |  The archpresbyter presided over the regional synod.  首席司祭主持了地区宗教会议。

The archpresbyter was responsible for the spiritual well-being of a large district.  大司铎负责一大片区域的精神福祉。 | Synonyms: archpriest, chief priest | Antonyms: layman | noun
