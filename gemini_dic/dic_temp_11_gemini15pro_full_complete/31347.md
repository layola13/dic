characteristicness:/ˌkærɪktəˈrɪstɪknəs/ |n.| 特征性，典型性 |The characteristicness of this wine is its oaky flavor.  这种葡萄酒的特点是它的橡木味。| 近义词: typicality, distinctiveness, representativeness | 反义词: atypicality, uncharacteristicness |词性:名词

Etymology: From "characteristic" + "-ness" . "Characteristic" comes from Late Latin *characteristica* from Greek kharaktēristikos, from kharaktēr "engraved mark," from kharassein "to engrave," from PIE root *ghere- "to scratch, scrape."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality.
