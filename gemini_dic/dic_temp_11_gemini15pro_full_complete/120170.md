mindboggling:/ˈmaɪndˌbɑːɡlɪŋ/| adj.|令人难以置信的；令人震惊的|Overwhelmingly impressive, astonishing, or difficult to comprehend.|The complexity of the human brain is truly mindboggling. 人脑的复杂性真是令人难以置信。|近义词：astonishing, amazing, incredible, unbelievable, staggering|反义词：unimpressive, ordinary, mundane|词性:形容词

Etymology:
A combination of "mind" and "boggling." "Mind" refers to the intellect or thought processes, while "boggling" is derived from "boggle," meaning to hesitate or be startled, often due to confusion or astonishment. Thus, "mindboggling" describes something so overwhelming or complex that it startles or confuses the mind.
