bushy:/ˈbʊʃi/| adj.|浓密的，茂密的|Resembling a bush in appearance, especially by being thick and spreading.|He had a bushy beard. 他留着浓密的胡须.|thick, full, shaggy|sparse, thin, wispy|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bush + -y.  The suffix "-y" is used to form adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of".  "Bush" comes from Middle English busch, busk, from Old North French busche, buisson.
