indusial:/ɪnˈduːʒiəl/ |adj.|孢膜的；囊群盖的|Relating to or resembling an indusium, a membranous covering over a sorus (cluster of sporangia) in ferns.|The indusial covering protected the developing spores. 孢膜覆盖物保护了正在发育的孢子。|Synonyms: covering, protective|Antonyms: exposed, uncovered|adjective

indusium:/ɪnˈduːziəm/ |n.|囊群盖；孢膜|A thin membranous covering, especially the one that protects a developing sorus in ferns.|The indusium of this fern species is kidney-shaped.  这种蕨类植物的囊群盖是肾形的。|Synonyms: covering, sheath|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "exposed sporangia")|noun
