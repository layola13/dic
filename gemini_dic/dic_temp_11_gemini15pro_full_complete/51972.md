diactinic:/ˌdaɪækˈtɪnɪk/
adjective
透光的；可使化学射线透过的
Allowing the passage of actinic rays; transparent to chemically active radiation.
例句：Diactinic glass is used in photography.  透光玻璃用于摄影。

例句：The diactinic properties of quartz make it useful in certain scientific instruments. 石英的透光特性使其在某些科学仪器中很有用。

近义词：translucent, transparent
反义词：opaque
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dia- "through" + actinic "relating to chemical radiation," from Greek aktis "ray."
