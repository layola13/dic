commotional:/kəˈməʊʃənəl/
adjective
激动的，令人激动的
Relating to or causing commotion; agitated.  Characterized by excitement and disturbance.
The commotional atmosphere of the protest was palpable.  抗议现场激动的气氛是显而易见的。
The news caused a commotional response from the public. 这条新闻引起了公众激动的反应。
近义词:exciting, stirring, turbulent, tumultuous
反义词:calming, soothing, peaceful, tranquil
词性:adjective
词源: From commotion + -al. Commotion ultimately derives from Latin commovēre "to move completely, disturb greatly."
