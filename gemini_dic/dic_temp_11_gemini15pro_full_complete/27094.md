calicular:/kəˈlɪkjʊlər/
adjective
杯状的
Relating to or resembling a calyx, the cup-like structure formed by the sepals of a flower.
The calicular structure of the flower protects the developing bud.  这朵花的杯状结构保护着发育中的花蕾。
The plant has distinctive calicular markings. 这种植物有独特的杯状标记。
近义词:cup-shaped, cuplike, calycine
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., "linear," "stellate")
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "caliculus" (small cup), diminutive of "calix" (cup, chalice), related to Greek "kalyx" (cup of a flower).
