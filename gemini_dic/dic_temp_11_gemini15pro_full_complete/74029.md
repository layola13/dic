forweary:/fɔːrˈwɪəri/
adjective
极度疲倦的，筋疲力尽的
extremely tired; exhausted
The forweary travelers finally reached their destination.  筋疲力尽的旅行者终于到达了目的地。
After a long day's work, I felt forweary.  一天漫长的工作后，我感到筋疲力尽。
近义词: exhausted, worn out, fatigued, tired
反义词: energetic, refreshed, invigorated
词性: adjective
词源: From Old English *forwerig, from for- (thoroughly) + werig (tired).
