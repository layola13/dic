narcissus: /nɑːrˈsɪsəs/ /nɑːrˈsɪsɪs/ | n. | 水仙花；自恋者 | A bulbous Eurasian plant of the amaryllis family that typically has showy yellow flowers with a short corona.  A person who has an excessive interest in or admiration of themselves. |  例句：The narcissus is a beautiful spring flower. 水仙花是美丽的春天的花朵。

例句：He's such a narcissus, always looking at himself in the mirror. 他真是个自恋狂，总是照镜子。| 近义词：daffodil (for the flower), egotist, self-absorbed (for the person) | 反义词：N/A (for flower);  humble, modest (for person)| 词性：名词
