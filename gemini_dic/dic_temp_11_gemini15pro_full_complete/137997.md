noodly:/ˈnuːdli/| adj.|面条状的；软弱无力的；糊状的|Resembling noodles in shape, consistency, or texture; limp and floppy; lacking firmness.|The overcooked pasta was noodly and unappetizing.  煮过头的意大利面软塌塌的，一点也不好吃。|Synonyms: limp, floppy, flabby, stringy, soft|Antonyms: firm, stiff, rigid, tough|adjective
