applicant:/ˈæplɪkənt/| n. |申请人，应征者|A person who formally requests something, especially a job, or to study at a college or university.|例句：The applicant was well-qualified for the position.  这位申请人胜任这个职位。

例句：There were many applicants for the job. 有很多求职者。

近义词：candidate, aspirant, seeker|反义词:employer, recruiter|词性:noun
