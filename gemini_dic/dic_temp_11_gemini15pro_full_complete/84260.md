half-sterile:/ˌhæf ˈsterəl/ | adj. | 半不育的，部分不育的 | Partially sterile; exhibiting reduced fertility.  A condition where an organism or plant is not completely sterile, but has a significantly reduced capacity to reproduce. | The half-sterile hybrid plants produced fewer seeds than their parent species.  这些半不育的杂交植物比它们的亲本物种产生的种子少。 | The gardener experimented with half-sterile varieties of the flower. 园丁用这种花的半不育品种进行了实验。 |  Near synonyms: subfertile, partially fertile | Antonyms: fertile, fully fertile | Adjective
