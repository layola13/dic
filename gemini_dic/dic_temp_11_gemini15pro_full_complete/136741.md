nonsensorial:/ˌnɒnsɛnˈsɔːriəl/ /ˌnɑːnsɛnˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
非感官的; 非感觉的
Not relating to or involving the senses.
The patient experienced nonsensorial hallucinations, seeing things that weren't there despite having no sensory input to suggest their presence.  病人经历了非感官性幻觉，尽管没有任何感官输入表明它们的存在，却看到了不存在的东西。
Her nonsensorial intuition told her something was wrong, even though she couldn't explain why. 她的非感官直觉告诉她有些不对劲，即使她无法解释为什么。
近义词: extrasensory, supersensible, non-sensory
反义词: sensorial, sensory
词性: adjective
