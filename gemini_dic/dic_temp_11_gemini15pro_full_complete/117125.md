meandering:/miˈændərɪŋ/
adjective
蜿蜒的；曲折的；漫步的；不着边际的
Following a winding or indirect course.  Rambling, not staying focused on a topic.
The river follows a meandering path through the valley. 这条河沿着蜿蜒的路径穿过山谷。

His speech was long and meandering, never quite getting to the point. 他的演讲冗长而不着边际，一直没有说到点子上。

近义词:winding, twisting, rambling, indirect, circuitous
反义词:straight, direct, focused


meander:/miˈændər/
verb
漫步；蜿蜒而流；闲逛；迂回曲折地前进
To wander aimlessly or casually; to follow a winding course.
We meandered through the streets of the old town, enjoying the sights. 我们漫步在老城区的大街小巷，欣赏着景色。

The stream meanders gently through the meadows. 小溪缓缓地蜿蜒流过草地。

近义词:wander, stroll, ramble, roam
反义词:hurry, rush


meander:/miˈændər/
noun
漫步；曲流；迂回曲折的路线
A winding curve or bend of a river or road.  A leisurely wander.
The river's meanders created a series of oxbow lakes. 河流的曲流形成了一系列牛轭湖。

We took a leisurely meander through the park. 我们在公园里悠闲地漫步。

近义词:wind, curve, bend, turn, ramble
反义词:straight
