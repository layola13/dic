desecrator:/ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪtər/| n.|亵渎者，污损圣物者|A person who violates or treats something sacred with irreverence or contempt. |The vandals who spray-painted the church were desecrators of a holy place. 那些在教堂上喷漆的破坏者是亵渎圣地的人。|Synonyms: profaner, violator, defiler|Antonyms: protector, guardian, defender|noun

desecrate:/ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪt/| vt.|亵渎，污损|To treat (a sacred place or thing) with violent disrespect; violate. |They desecrated the graves by covering them with graffiti. 他们用涂鸦覆盖坟墓，亵渎了它们。|Synonyms: profane, defile, violate|Antonyms: consecrate, sanctify, venerate|verb
