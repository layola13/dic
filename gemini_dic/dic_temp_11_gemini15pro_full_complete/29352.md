castoreum:/kæsˈtɔːriəm/ | n. | 海狸香 | A yellowish-brown, unctuous substance with a strong odor, secreted by beavers and used in perfumery.  It is obtained from the castor sacs of the beaver. |  Beavers use castoreum to mark their territory. 海狸用海狸香来标记它们的领地。|  Castoreum is a fixative in some perfumes. 海狸香在一些香水中用作定香剂。 | synonyms: beaver scent, castor | antonyms: None | noun
