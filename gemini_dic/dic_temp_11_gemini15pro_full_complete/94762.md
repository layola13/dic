improvises:/ˈɪmprəˌvaɪzɪz/| v.| 即兴创作，即兴表演，临时凑合|to create and perform (music, drama, or verse) spontaneously or without preparation.|The comedian improvises his routines.  这位喜剧演员即兴表演他的节目。|  He improvised a shelter from branches. 他用树枝临时搭了个棚子。|synonyms: ad-lib, extemporize, wing it, make do|antonyms: plan, prepare, rehearse|verb
