neither-here-nor-there:/ˌniːðər ˈhɪər nɔːr ˈðɛər/ |adj.|无关紧要的，不相干的|Irrelevant, of no importance or consequence.  It doesn't matter one way or the other.|His opinion is neither here nor there; the decision has already been made. 他的意见无关紧要；决定已经做出了。|Synonyms: irrelevant, immaterial, unimportant, inconsequential|Antonyms: relevant, important, material, consequential|adjective


neither-here-nor-there:/ˌniːðər ˈhɪər nɔːr ˈðɛər/|adv.|既不在这里也不在那里|Neither in this place nor in that place; in no definite place.| The missing book was neither here nor there.  那本丢失的书既不在这里也不在那里。|Synonyms: nowhere, in no place|Antonyms: somewhere, everywhere|adverb
