lithologically:/ˌlɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkli/| adv.| 岩性地；就岩性而言| Referring to the physical characteristics of rocks, such as their composition, texture, and structure. | The area is lithologically diverse, containing both sedimentary and igneous rocks.  该地区岩性多样，包含沉积岩和火成岩。| 近义词: petrographically, geologically | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, as it describes a specific analytical approach) | 词性: 副词
