draughting: /ˈdrɑːftɪŋ/ (US) /ˈdrɑːftɪŋ/ (UK)
n.|起草，制图，通风|The act of preparing a preliminary version of a document or drawing.  The process of designing and drawing plans for buildings, machinery, etc.  A flow of air through a room or other enclosed space.|
例句：The draughting of the contract took several weeks. 合同的起草花了几个星期。

例句：He studied draughting in college. 他在大学学习制图。

近义词：drawing, sketching, planning, designing, ventilation, airing
反义词：finalizing, completing, stagnation
词性: noun
