labyrinthlike:/ˈlæbərɪnθlaɪk/| adj.|迷宫似的，复杂的|Resembling a labyrinth; intricate and confusing.|The building's labyrinthlike corridors made it easy to get lost. 这座建筑迷宫般的走廊很容易让人迷路。|The tunnels were dark and labyrinthlike. 这些隧道既黑暗又像迷宫。|近义词：mazelike, intricate, complex, convoluted|反义词：simple, straightforward|词性: 形容词

词源:
From labyrinth + -like.  "Labyrinth" comes from the Greek "labyrinthos," referring to the elaborate maze built for King Minos of Crete to house the Minotaur.  "-like" is a suffix of Germanic origin meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," or "similar to."
