cognizance:/ˈkɒɡnɪzəns/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡnɪzəns/ (US)| n. |认识；认知；审理；注意到|Knowledge or awareness; the fact of being aware of something; the range of things that someone knows about|The court has cognizance of such cases. 法院有权审理此类案件。|Synonyms: awareness, knowledge, consciousness, perception, attention, notice, observation|Antonyms: ignorance, unawareness, inattention|noun

cognizant:/ˈkɒɡnɪzənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡnɪzənt/ (US)| adj. |知道的；认识的；已了解的|Having knowledge or being aware of something.|He was cognizant of the difficulty. 他知道困难所在。|Synonyms: aware, conscious, informed, mindful|Antonyms: unaware, ignorant, oblivious|adjective
