imperforate:/ɪmˈpɜːrfəreɪt/ | adj. | 未穿孔的，无孔的 | Lacking a normal opening or perforation.  |  The stamp was imperforate, making it more valuable to collectors. 这枚邮票没有穿孔，这使得它对收藏家来说更有价值。 |  Imperforate stamps are prized by collectors. 未穿孔的邮票受到收藏家的珍视。 |  Synonyms: unperforated, solid, unbroken | Antonyms: perforated, pierced, punctured | adjective

imperforated:/ɪmˈpɜːrfəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未穿孔的，无孔的 | Not perforated; having no holes or openings. | The imperforated sheet of stamps was a rare find. 这张未穿孔的邮票是一项罕见的发现。 | The surgeon discovered an imperforated anus in the newborn.  外科医生发现新生儿有肛门闭锁。 | Synonyms: unperforated, solid, unbroken | Antonyms: perforated, pierced, punctured | adjective

Etymology: From Latin *imperforatus, from in- "not" + perforatus "pierced through," past participle of perforare "to bore through," from per- "through" + forare "to bore."
