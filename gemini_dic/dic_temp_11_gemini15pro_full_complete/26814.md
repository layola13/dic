cagelike:/ˈkeɪdʒˌlaɪk/| adj.|像笼子一样的，像牢笼一样的|Resembling a cage, especially in being confining or restrictive.|The small, dark room felt cagelike.|近义词：confining, restrictive, claustrophobic, imprisoning|反义词：open, spacious, liberating|词性:形容词
Etymology: From 'cage' + '-like'.  'Cage' comes from Old North French 'cage' (modern French 'cage'). '-like' is a suffix meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," from Old English '-lic'.
