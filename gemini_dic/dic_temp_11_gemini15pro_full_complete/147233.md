overmedicates: /ˌoʊvərˈmɛdɪkeɪts/ | v. | 过度用药；给…用药过量 | To prescribe or administer too much medication to someone. | The doctor overmedicated the patient, leading to adverse side effects. 医生给病人开了过多的药，导致了不良的副作用。 |  He was concerned that his elderly mother was being overmedicated. 他担心他年迈的母亲被过度用药。 | overtreat, overdose | undermedicate | verb
