embodied:/ɪmˈbɑːdid/ /ɛmˈbɒdid/| adj.|体现的，具体化的，包含的|Having a physical form; representing an idea or quality in a clear and obvious way.  Giving a concrete form to (an abstract notion, quality, or principle).|例句：

The spirit of hope is embodied in the Olympic Games.
奥林匹克运动会体现了希望的精神。

Her paintings embody the very essence of the natural world.
她的画作体现了自然世界的精髓。|近义词：personified, incorporated, manifested, realized, exemplified|反义词：abstract, disembodied, intangible|词性:adjective
