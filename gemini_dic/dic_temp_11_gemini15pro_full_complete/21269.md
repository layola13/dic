bloodied: /ˈblʌdid/ | adj. | 血淋淋的；沾满鲜血的 | Covered or stained with blood. | The bloodied boxer staggered back.  血淋淋的拳击手踉跄地后退。| 近义词: gory, bloodstained, bloody | 反义词: clean, unstained | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From blood + -ed,  meaning "having blood on it."  The word "blood" comes from Old English "blōd,"  of Germanic origin.
