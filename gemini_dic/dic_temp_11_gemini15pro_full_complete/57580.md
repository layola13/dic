dramaturges:/ˌdræməˈtɜːrdʒz/
noun
剧作家；戏剧顾问
A person who writes plays or advises a theatre company on literary matters.
The Royal Shakespeare Company employs several dramaturges to assist with their productions. 皇家莎士比亚公司雇佣了几位剧作家来协助他们的作品制作。
synonyms: playwright, dramatist, scriptwriter, adapter
antonyms: N/A
n.
