espy:/ɪˈspaɪ/| v. | 发现；瞥见；看出 | To catch sight of something, especially something distant or obscure. |  I espied a small boat on the horizon. 我看到地平线上有一艘小船。

She espied him entering the building. 她看到他走进了大楼。

| 近义词：spot, see, notice, perceive, discern, detect | 反义词：miss, overlook | 词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *espier, from Latin *spiare, from specere "to look at."
