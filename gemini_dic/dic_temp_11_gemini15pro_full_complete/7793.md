answering:/ˈɑːnsrɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɑːnsərɪŋ/ (UK)| v. |回答，答复；响应|Responding to a question or request. |She is answering the phone. 她正在接电话。|replying, responding|ignoring, disregarding|verb

answering:/ˈɑːnsrɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɑːnsərɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |回答的，答复的|Used to describe something that responds or replies. |The answering machine took the message. 答录机记录了留言。|responsive, reacting|unresponsive, nonreactive|adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *answeringe*, from Old English *andswarian* (to answer, swear in reply), from *and-* (against, in return) + *swerian* (to swear).
