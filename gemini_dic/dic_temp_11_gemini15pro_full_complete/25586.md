bumfs:/bʌmfs/ | n. | 碰撞声，重物落地声 |  A dull, heavy sound, as of a heavy object falling or landing. | The bumfs of the boxes being unloaded from the truck echoed through the street.  卡车卸货时箱子砰砰落地的声音在街道上回荡。 | 近义词：thud, thump, crash | 反义词: tinkle, whisper | 名词

bumfs:/bʌmfs/ | int. |  模拟重物落地声的拟声词 | Used to represent the sound of a heavy object falling or landing. | Bumfs! The sack of potatoes hit the floor. 砰! 一袋土豆掉在地上了。 | 近义词：thud, thump, crash | 反义词: tinkle, whisper | 感叹词

词源:  Likely imitative in origin, based on the sound itself.  很可能是拟声词，基于声音本身。
