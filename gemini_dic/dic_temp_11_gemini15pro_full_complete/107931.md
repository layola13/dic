laryngectomy:/ˌlærɪnˈdʒɛktəmi/| n. |喉切除术|Surgical removal of the larynx.|例句：The patient underwent a laryngectomy due to laryngeal cancer.  这位病人因喉癌接受了喉切除术。

例句：After the laryngectomy, he had to learn to speak using an electrolarynx. 喉切除术后，他不得不学习使用电子喉说话。|近义词：laryngectomy surgery|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  larynx + -ectomy.  Larynx comes from Greek "larynx" (upper windpipe).  -ectomy is a suffix denoting surgical removal, from Greek "ektomē" (excision).
