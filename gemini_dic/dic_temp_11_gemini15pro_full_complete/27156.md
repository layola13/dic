call-outs:/ˈkɔːlˌaʊts/ | n. | 标注；大声喊叫；(尤指)抗议；(对文本中重要信息的)醒目显示；(士兵的)出动 | Annotations, explanations, or labels added to an image or text to highlight specific details or features; also refers to the act of publicly criticizing or protesting something or someone, especially by shouting; or the summoning of troops or emergency services. | The architect used call-outs on the blueprint to explain the different structural elements.  建筑师在蓝图上使用标注来解释不同的结构元素。

| The union leader issued a series of angry call-outs against the proposed layoffs. 工会领导人对拟议的裁员发出了一系列愤怒的抗议。 | synonyms: annotations, labels, captions, shout-outs, protests, summons | antonyms: whispers, silence, concealments | noun
