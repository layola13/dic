homaloid:/ˈhoʊməlɔɪd/| adj.|均匀的，平坦的，近似平面的|Resembling a plane; approximately flat.  A homaloid projection in cartography preserves area. |The landscape was strangely homaloid, lacking any significant hills or valleys.|近义词:planar, flat, even, level, uniform|反义词:uneven, irregular, bumpy, hilly|词性:adjective

Etymology: From Greek *homalos* "even, level" + *-oid* "resembling".
