improbabilities:/ɪmˌprɒbəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 不可能性；不太可能发生的事情；没有希望的事 |The improbabilities of the situation made it even more exciting.  这种局势不太可能发生，反而使它更加令人兴奋。|例句：The improbabilities of winning the lottery are astronomical. 中彩票的可能性微乎其微。

例句：Despite the improbabilities, they managed to succeed. 尽管希望渺茫，他们还是设法成功了。|近义词：unlikelihoods, impossibilities, long shots |反义词: possibilities, likelihoods, probabilities |词性:名词
