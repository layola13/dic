astigmatic:/ˌæs.tɪɡˈmæt.ɪk/ | adj. | 散光的；乱视的；【比喻】目光短浅的 |  Suffering from or affected by astigmatism; unable to focus properly because of irregularities in the curvature of the cornea or lens of the eye. Figuratively, lacking in clarity or focus. |  My vision is astigmatic, so I need to wear glasses. 我的视力是散光的，所以我需要戴眼镜。 |  Myopic, blurry, distorted | Clear, focused, sharp | adjective

astigmatic:/ˌæs.tɪɡˈmæt.ɪk/ | n. | 散光患者 | A person with astigmatism. | The astigmatic needs special lenses to correct their vision.  散光患者需要特殊的镜片来矫正视力。|  Nearsighted person, myope |  Person with perfect vision | noun
