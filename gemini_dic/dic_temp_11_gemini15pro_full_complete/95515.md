inclose:/ɪnˈkloʊz/| vt.| 围住，封入，附上|To surround or enclose something, often with a fence or wall; to insert something in a letter or package.|He inclosed the garden with a high fence. 他用高高的栅栏围住了花园。 Please inclose a check with your order. 请随订单附上支票。|近义词：enclose, surround, insert, include|反义词:exclude, open, release|verb

inclose:/ɪnˈkloʊz/| vt.| 附上|To include something along with a letter or package.|She inclosed a recent photograph with her application. 她在申请书中附上了一张近照。|近义词：enclose, attach, add|反义词:exclude, omit, remove|verb
