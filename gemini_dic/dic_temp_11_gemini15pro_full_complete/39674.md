conserver:/kənˈsɜːrvər/ | n. |保护者，保存者；节约者|A person who conserves something, especially natural resources.  A person who supports conservation.| 例句：The conserver worked tirelessly to protect the endangered species.  这位环保人士不知疲倦地工作以保护濒危物种。

例句：She's a real conserver of energy, always turning off lights and appliances. 她是一个真正的节能者，总是关灯和电器。|近义词：preserver, protector, guardian, custodian, conservator |反义词：waster, squanderer, spendthrift| 词性: 名词
