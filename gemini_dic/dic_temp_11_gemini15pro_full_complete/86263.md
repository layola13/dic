heavens:/ˈhɛvənz/| n.|天堂，天空|A literary and sometimes archaic term for the sky or universe, often used for poetic or religious effect. It can also express surprise, dismay, or frustration.|例句：The heavens opened and the rain poured down. 天空裂开了，大雨倾盆而下。

例句：Oh heavens, what have I done?  我的天哪，我做了什么？|近义词：sky, paradise, cosmos|反义词:hell, underworld|词性:名词
