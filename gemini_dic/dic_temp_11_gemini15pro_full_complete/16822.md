baser:/ˈbeɪsər/ (US), /ˈbeɪsə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更差的，更卑劣的，低劣的|Morally low; without estimable personal qualities; inferior.|He spread baser rumors about his rival to discredit him. 他散布关于他对手的更卑劣的谣言来败坏他的名声。|Synonyms: inferior, low, mean, vile, ignoble, sordid|Antonyms: noble, virtuous, honorable|adjective

baser:/ˈbeɪsər/ (US), /ˈbeɪsə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|（金属）不纯的，贱的|Made of inferior metal; debased.|The coin was made of a baser metal than silver. 这枚硬币是由比银更不纯的金属制成的。|Synonyms: inferior, common, alloyed|Antonyms: pure, precious, refined|adjective
