disjection:/dɪsˈdʒɛkʃən/
n.
沮丧，灰心，气馁
The state of being disjected; depression of spirits; discouragement.
His disjection was evident after he lost the competition.  他输掉比赛后，沮丧之情溢于言表。
近义词: dejection, despondency, depression, discouragement
反义词: elation, joy, happiness, cheerfulness
