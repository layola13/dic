go-without-saying:/ˌɡoʊ wɪˈðaʊt ˈseɪɪŋ/ | idiom | 不言而喻，显而易见 | Something is so obvious that it doesn't need to be stated. | It goes without saying that you should wear a helmet when riding a motorcycle.  骑摩托车时应该戴头盔，这是不言而喻的。|近义词：obvious, self-evident, axiomatic | 反义词: debatable, questionable | phrase

It goes without saying that hard work is the key to success.  努力工作是成功的关键，这是不言而喻的。
