immundicity:/ˌɪmənˈdɪsɪti/
n.
不洁；污秽；肮脏
The state of being unclean, filthy, or impure.
The immundicity of the slums was appalling.  贫民窟的污秽令人震惊。

近义词:filth, dirt, squalor, uncleanliness, impurity
反义词:cleanliness, purity, hygiene

Etymology: From Late Latin *immundicitas, from Latin immundus (“unclean”), from in- (“not”) + mundus (“clean”).
