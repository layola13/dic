epigaeous:/ˌɛpɪˈdʒiːəs/| adj.|地上生的，生长在地面上的|Growing or occurring above ground; not subterranean.|例句：The epigaeous fruiting bodies of the mushroom were easily spotted. 这种蘑菇的地上子实体很容易被发现。|例句：Epigaeous germination is common in many plant species. 地上萌发在许多植物物种中很常见。|近义词：surface, aboveground|反义词：hypogaeous, subterranean|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *epi-* "upon, on" + *gaia* "earth".
