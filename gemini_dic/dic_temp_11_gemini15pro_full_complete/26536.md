cabesse:/kəˈbɛs/| n.| 头部，领袖，首领 |A cabesse is a leader or head, particularly of an expedition or group.  It derives from the Portuguese word "cabeça" meaning head.|The cabesse of the expedition led them through the jungle.|近义词：leader, chief, head, director|反义词:follower, subordinate|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *cabeça* ("head"), from Late Latin *capitia*, plural of *capitium* (“head covering, hood”), derived from Latin *caput* ("head").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\ḱaput-* (“head”).
