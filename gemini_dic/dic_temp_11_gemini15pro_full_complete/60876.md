eleidin:/ɪˈliːɪdɪn/
n.|角蛋白|A semi-fluid substance found in the stratum lucidum of the epidermis, believed to be a precursor to keratin.|The eleidin in the skin helps form a barrier against the environment.|近义词:keratinohyalin|反义词:None|词性:Noun

词源: From Greek *ēleídinon < *ēleídēs "oily" (compare Ancient Greek *ēlaíōn "oil").
