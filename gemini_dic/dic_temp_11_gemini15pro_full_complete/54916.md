disposedly:/dɪˈspəʊzɪdli/
adverb
有意地，故意地
In a manner indicating a willingness or inclination towards something.
She smiled disposedly at him, hinting at her interest. 她有意地对他笑了笑，暗示了她对他的兴趣。
He disposedly left the door unlocked, knowing his friend would arrive soon. 他故意没锁门，因为他知道他的朋友很快就会来。
近义词:intentionally, deliberately, purposely
反义词:unintentionally, accidentally, reluctantly
词性:adverb

词源:
From dispose + -ed + -ly.  Dispose comes from Old French *disposer (modern French disposer), from Latin dispōnere “put in different places, arrange, distribute, regulate,” from dis- “apart, asunder” + pōnere “to put, place.”
