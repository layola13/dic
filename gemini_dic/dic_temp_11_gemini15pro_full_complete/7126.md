angel:/ˈeɪndʒəl/ | n. | 天使；守护神；善良可爱的人 | A spiritual being believed to act as an attendant, agent, or messenger of God, conventionally represented in human form with wings and a halo.  A person of exemplary goodness or virtue. |  She looked like an angel in her white dress. 她穿着白裙，看起来像个天使。 He was an angel to us during that difficult time. 在那段艰难的时期，他就像我们的守护神。| synonyms: seraph, cherub, guardian, saint | antonyms: devil, demon, fiend | noun
