forsworn:/fɔːrˈswɔːrn/
adjective
背信弃义的；作伪证的
Having violated an oath or solemn promise.
He was a forsworn knight, banished from his kingdom. 他是一个背信弃义的骑士，被驱逐出他的王国。
The forsworn witness was found guilty of perjury.  作伪证的证人被判犯有伪证罪。
近义词：perjured, treacherous, faithless
反义词：loyal, faithful, true
词性: adjective

词源:
From Old English *forswerian (unattested), from for- + swerian "to swear."  由古英语*forswerian（未经证实）而来，由for- + swerian“发誓”组成。
