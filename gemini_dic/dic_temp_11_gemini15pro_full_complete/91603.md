hyperalgic:/ˌhaɪpərˈældʒɪk/ | adj. | 痛觉过敏的，感觉过敏的 |  Characterized by increased sensitivity to pain; exhibiting hyperalgesia.  Relating to or suffering from hyperalgesia, a condition in which a mild stimulus is perceived as very painful. |  The patient reported hyperalgic responses to even light touch after the surgery. 手术后，病人即使是轻微的触摸也会出现痛觉过敏反应。 | Example: After the burn, the skin became hyperalgic, making even the lightest touch excruciating.  烧伤后，皮肤变得痛觉过敏，即使是最轻微的触摸也令人难以忍受。 | Synonyms: hypersensitive, oversensitive | Antonyms: analgesic, pain-relieving | adjective
