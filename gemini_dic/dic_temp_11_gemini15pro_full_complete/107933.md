laryngitides: /ˌlærɪnˈdʒaɪtɪdiːz/
noun
喉炎（复数）
Inflammation of the larynx, typically resulting in huskiness or loss of the voice, and a cough.  It's the plural form of laryngitis.
Several children in the class were absent due to laryngitides. 班里几个孩子因为喉炎缺席了。
Near Synonyms:  inflammations of the larynx,  throat infections (more general)
Antonyms:  healthy larynx, clear voice
词性: noun
