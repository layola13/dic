PHRASE->
flatten out: 变平，变缓和  The road flattened out after the steep climb.  爬过陡坡后，道路变得平坦了。
flatten someone/something out: 击倒某人/某物  The boxer flattened his opponent out in the first round.  拳击手在第一回合就把对手击倒了。
flatten down: 压平，压下 Flatten down the edges of the pastry before baking.  烘烤前压平糕点的边缘。
flatten oneself against something: 紧贴某物  She flattened herself against the wall to avoid being seen.  她紧贴着墙，以免被人看见。
flatten the curve: 压平曲线  Social distancing measures were implemented to flatten the curve of the virus spread.  实施保持社交距离的措施是为了压平病毒传播曲线。
