overproducing: /ˌoʊvərprəˈduːsɪŋ/ | v. | 过度生产；生产过剩 | Producing more of something than is needed or wanted. | The factory was overproducing goods, leading to a surplus in the warehouse. 工厂过度生产货物，导致仓库积压。| The company is overproducing plastic toys, which are not selling well.  这家公司生产了过多的塑料玩具，但销量不好。| synonyms: oversupply, glut, saturate | antonyms: underproduce, undersupply | verb

Etymology:
From over- + producing, present participle of produce (v.).  "Over-" signifies "excessively," "too much."  "Produce" comes from Latin producere "to lead or bring forth, prolong, extend," from pro- "forth" + ducere "to lead."
