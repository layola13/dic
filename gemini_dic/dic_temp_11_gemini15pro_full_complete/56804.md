nighthawk:/ˈnaɪtˌhɔːk (US), ˈnaɪtˌhɔːk (UK)| n.| 夜鹰 |A nocturnal bird of the Americas, similar to a whip-poor-will, with long pointed wings, a short bill, and a gaping mouth.  It catches insects in flight.|例句：The nighthawk swooped through the air catching moths.  夜鹰在空中俯冲捕捉飞蛾。

例句：We heard the distinctive call of a nighthawk as we sat on the porch. 我们坐在门廊上时，听到了夜鹰独特的叫声。|近义词：nightjar, whip-poor-will|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific bird species) |词性:noun

nighthawk:/ˈnaɪtˌhɔːk (US), ˈnaɪtˌhɔːk (UK)| n.|夜游者，工作到很晚的人|A person who is active or works late at night.|例句：He's a real nighthawk, often working until dawn. 他是一个真正的夜猫子，经常工作到天亮。

例句：The city streets were still busy with nighthawks even after midnight. 即使在午夜之后，城市的街道上仍然挤满了夜游者。|近义词：night owl|反义词: early bird |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "night" + "hawk," referring to the bird's nocturnal habits and hawk-like flight.
