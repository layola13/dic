outcoach:/ˌaʊtˈkoʊtʃ/| v. |以更好的教练方式胜过；在教练方面胜过|To surpass someone in coaching ability. |例句：He outcoached his rival in the championship game, leading his team to victory. 他在冠军赛中以更好的教练方式战胜了对手，带领他的球队取得了胜利。

例句：She outcoached all the other contenders and secured the head coaching position. 她以更好的教练方式胜过了所有其他竞争者，获得了主教练的职位。|近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, surpass (in coaching)|反义词: be outcoached|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "out-" (meaning to surpass or exceed) + "coach" (meaning to train or instruct).
