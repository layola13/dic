hyperrationality:/ˌhaɪpərˌræʃəˈnælɪti/
noun
超理性
Extreme reliance on logic and reason, often to the exclusion of emotions, intuition, or other factors.
The character Spock from Star Trek is often cited as an example of hyperrationality.  斯波克，来自《星际迷航》的角色，经常被认为是超理性的一个例子。
He approached the problem with a degree of hyperrationality that bordered on the absurd. 他以一种近乎荒谬的超理性态度来处理这个问题。
近义词:  overthinking,  ultra-rationality,  calculating,  logical, analytical
反义词: irrationality, emotionalism, impulsiveness
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessively"
rationality (noun): from Latin rationalitas, from rationalis "reasonable," from ratio "reason, calculation"
