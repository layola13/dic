equivocated: /ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪtɪd/
verb (past tense)
含糊其辞；支吾其词；说模棱两可的话
Used the past tense of "equivocate," meaning to speak vaguely or ambiguously, often to avoid committing to a particular position or to mislead.
He equivocated when asked about his involvement in the scandal, leaving everyone unsure of the truth.  他被问及卷入丑闻时含糊其辞，让每个人都不确定真相。
The politician equivocated on the issue, refusing to give a straight answer.  这位政客在这个问题上含糊其辞，拒绝给出直接的答案。
近义词: prevaricated, evaded, dodged, hedged
反义词: stated clearly, articulated, avowed, declared
词性: verb (past participle)
