nonoccupation:/ˌnɒnˌɒkjuˈpeɪʃn/
n.
lack of occupation; unemployment; leisure
缺乏职业;失业;闲暇
The long period of nonoccupation led to feelings of restlessness and boredom. 长期没有工作导致他感到烦躁和无聊。
nonoccupation is not necessarily a bad thing, it can be a time for self-reflection and personal growth. 没有工作不一定是坏事，它可以是自我反省和个人成长的时期。
近义词: unemployment, idleness, inactivity, leisure
反义词: employment, occupation, work, activity
词性: noun

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + occupation (from Latin occupatio, meaning "seizing, taking possession of")
