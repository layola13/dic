milksoppy:/ˈmɪlkˌsɒpi/ | adj. | 多愁善感的，感情脆弱的 | Excessively sentimental or emotional, often to the point of being silly or weak. |  She gets milksoppy over those romantic comedies. 她对那些浪漫喜剧片非常多愁善感。| 近义词: sentimental, mawkish, soppy | 反义词: unsentimental, hard-hearted | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Milk" refers to milk's association with babies and childhood innocence. "Soppy" means overly sentimental.  The combination suggests a childish, overly sweet sentimentality.
