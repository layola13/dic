be-big-on:/biː bɪɡ ɒn/ | adj. phrasal verb | 热衷于，非常喜欢 | To be very enthusiastic about or interested in something. |  例句：She's be-big-on  yoga recently. 她最近迷上了瑜伽。| 近义词：keen on, fond of, enthusiastic about | 反义词：dislike, hate, be indifferent to | 词性：phrasal verb

Etymology: This phrase combines the verb "to be," the adjective "big," and the preposition "on."  "Big" in this context implies a strong interest or enthusiasm.  The preposition "on" indicates the object of this enthusiasm.
