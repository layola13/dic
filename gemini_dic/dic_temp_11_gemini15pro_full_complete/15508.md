baffin:/ˈbæfɪn/| adj. n.|巴芬的，巴芬岛；巴芬湾|Relating to or characteristic of Baffin Island, Baffin Bay, or the Baffin people. Also used as a noun to refer to Baffin Island or Baffin Bay.|例句：The Baffin caribou is a subspecies found on Baffin Island. 巴芬驯鹿是巴芬岛上发现的一个亚种。

Baffin Bay is known for its icy waters. 巴芬湾以其冰冷的海水而闻名。|近义词：Arctic, northern|反义词：southern|词性:形容词, 名词


Etymology: Named after William Baffin, a 17th-century English explorer.  Derived from the explorer's surname "Baffin."
