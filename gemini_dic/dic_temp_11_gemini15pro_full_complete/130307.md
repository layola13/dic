non-systematic:/ˌnɒnˌsɪstəˈmætɪk/ |adj.|非系统的，无系统的|Not following a system or regular plan; lacking organization or a consistent approach.|  The artist's approach to painting was non-systematic, relying more on intuition than technique.  这位艺术家的绘画方法是非系统的，更多地依赖直觉而不是技巧。

Her notes were non-systematic and difficult to follow. 她的笔记杂乱无章，难以理解。|近义词:random, haphazard, unsystematic, disorganized, chaotic|反义词:systematic, methodical, organized, structured, planned|词性:形容词

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "systematic" (from "system," ultimately from Greek "systēma" meaning "organized whole").  "Non-" 前缀（不）+ "systematic"（来自 "system"，最终来自希腊语 "systēma"，意思是“有组织的整体”）。
