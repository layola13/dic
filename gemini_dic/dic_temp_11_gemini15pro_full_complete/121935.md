misshipping:/ˌmɪsˈʃɪpɪŋ/
noun
误发货，错运
the act of shipping goods to the wrong address or destination.
The customer complained about the misshipping of their order.  顾客投诉他们的订单被误发了。
misdelivery, incorrect shipment
correct shipment, accurate delivery
n.
