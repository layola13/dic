interweave:/ˌɪntərˈwiːv/| vt. |交织，编织，穿插|To weave or blend together; to intermingle.|The author skillfully interwove the different storylines. 作者巧妙地将不同的故事情节交织在一起。|近义词：interlace, intertwine, intermingle|反义词：separate, disentangle|词性:Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *interweven*, from Old English *interwefan*, from *inter-* (between) + *wefan* (to weave).  The word has maintained its core meaning of weaving or blending together throughout its history.
