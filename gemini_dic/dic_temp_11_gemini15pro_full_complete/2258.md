adeli:/əˈdeɪlaɪ/ | n. | 阿黛利企鹅 |A species of penguin found in Antarctica.  It is characterized by a white ring around the eye and long tail feathers. | Example: We saw a colony of Adelie penguins on the Antarctic shore. 我们在南极海岸看到了一群阿黛利企鹅。| Example: The Adelie penguin is well adapted to the harsh Antarctic climate. 阿黛利企鹅非常适应南极恶劣的气候。|Synonym: penguin |Antonym: N/A |noun
