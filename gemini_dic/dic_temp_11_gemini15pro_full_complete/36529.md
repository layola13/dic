collaborated:/kəˈlæbəreɪtɪd/
verb
合作，协作
worked together on a project
例句：The two companies collaborated on the development of a new software.  两家公司合作开发了一款新软件。

例句：We collaborated with a local artist on the design of the mural. 我们和一位当地的艺术家合作设计了这幅壁画。

近义词：cooperated, partnered, teamed up, worked together
反义词：acted alone, worked independently
词性: v. (past tense)


collaborate:/kəˈlæbəreɪt/
verb
合作，协作
to work together on a project
例句：The two companies will collaborate on the development of a new software.  两家公司将合作开发一款新软件。

例句：We plan to collaborate with a local artist on the design of the mural. 我们计划和一位当地的艺术家合作设计这幅壁画。

近义词：cooperate, partner, team up, work together
反义词：act alone, work independently
词性: v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin collaboratus, past participle of collaborare (“to work together”), from com- (“together”) + laborare (“to work”).
