galipot:/ˈɡælɪpɒt/ (UK), /ˈɡælɪpɑːt/ (US)| n.|树脂，松脂，松香|A resinous exudation from various pine trees, especially the Aleppo pine.  It hardens on exposure to air and is used in making varnishes and lacquers.|The craftsman used galipot to seal the wooden boat. 工匠用松脂来密封木船。|Synonyms: resin, rosin, pitch|Antonyms: N/A|noun
