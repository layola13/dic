mansuetude:/ˈmæn.swɪ.tʃuːd/| n. |温顺，柔和，驯良|The king was known for his mansuetude and mercy towards his subjects. 国王以对臣民的温顺和仁慈而闻名。|例句：His mansuetude made him an easy target for bullies. 他的温顺使他很容易成为恶霸的目标。

例句：The dog's mansuetude was surprising, given its size and breed.  考虑到它的体型和品种，这只狗的温顺令人惊讶。

近义词：gentleness, meekness, docility, mildness, tameness|反义词：fierceness, wildness, aggressiveness, belligerence|词性:名词
