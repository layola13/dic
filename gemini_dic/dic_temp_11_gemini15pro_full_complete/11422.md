argillite:/ˈɑːrdʒɪlaɪt/ | n. |泥质板岩，硬泥岩 | A compact sedimentary rock composed predominantly of indurated clay particles.  It is essentially a hardened mudstone that does not readily split into layers. | The cliff face was composed of grey argillite.  悬崖表面由灰色的泥质板岩构成。| The museum displayed ancient tools carved from argillite.  博物馆展出了由泥质板岩雕刻的古代工具。 |Synonyms: mudstone, claystone, shale (when fissile) | Antonyms: sandstone, limestone | noun
