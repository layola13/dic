blennioid:/blɛniɔɪd/ | adj. n. |鳚科的；鳚科鱼 |Relating to or resembling a blenny, a type of fish in the family Blenniidae, characterized by elongated bodies, continuous dorsal fins, and often vibrant colors.  Can also refer to a fish of the blenny family. |This blennioid fish is camouflaged perfectly against the coral. 这条鳚科鱼完美地伪装在珊瑚中。| The blennioid clung to the rock with its modified pelvic fins.  这条鳚鱼用它变异的腹鳍紧紧地贴在岩石上。| synonyms: blenny-like, blenny | antonyms: (No direct antonym, could use a contrasting fish family like "salmonid") | adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Blenniidae, the family name of blennies,  + -oid, a suffix meaning "resembling" or "like."  Blenniidae is derived from the Greek blennos, meaning "mucus," referring to the slimy coating on the skin of many blenny species.
