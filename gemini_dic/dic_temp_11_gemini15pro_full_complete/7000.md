androgynous:/ænˈdrɒdʒɪnəs/ | adj. |雌雄同体的，阴阳兼具的 | Having both male and female characteristics; partly male and partly female in appearance; of indeterminate sex. |  例句：The androgynous model challenged traditional gender roles.  这位雌雄同体的模特挑战了传统的性别角色。| 近义词：unisex, epicene, hermaphroditic | 反义词：masculine, feminine | 词性: 形容词

androgynal: /ænˈdrɒdʒɪnl/ | adj. | 雌雄同体的，阴阳兼具的 | Relating to or exhibiting androgyny.  | 例句：The singer cultivated an androgynal image. 这位歌手塑造了一个雌雄同体的形象。 | 近义词: androgynous, unisex, epicene | 反义词: masculine, feminine | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Greek *ἀνδρ- (andr-)  "man" + γυν- (gyn-) "woman"  + -al (suffix forming adjectives).
