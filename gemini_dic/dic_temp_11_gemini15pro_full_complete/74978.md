friction:/ˈfrɪkʃən/| n.|摩擦；摩擦力；冲突，不和|The friction between the two surfaces produces heat. 两个表面之间的摩擦产生热量。|例句：The friction between the two countries led to war. 两国之间的摩擦导致了战争。

例句：There's a lot of friction between the management and the workers. 管理层和工人之间有很多摩擦。

近义词：abrasion, attrition, conflict, discord, resistance|反义词：harmony, agreement, accord|词性:名词

词源：From Latin *frictionem,  from fricare "to rub"
