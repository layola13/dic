bantingism:/ˈbæntɪŋɪzəm/
n.
班廷主义；班廷减肥法
A diet for losing weight by avoiding foods thought to contain starch and sugar, named after William Banting.
William Banting advocated this diet in an 1863 pamphlet, "Letter on Corpulence, Addressed to the Public."
例句：Bantingism became a popular weight-loss method in the Victorian era.  班廷主义在维多利亚时代成为一种流行的减肥方法。

例句：He attributed his weight loss to bantingism. 他将自己的减肥归功于班廷减肥法。

近义词：diet, weight-loss plan, low-carb diet
反义词：gluttony, overeating
