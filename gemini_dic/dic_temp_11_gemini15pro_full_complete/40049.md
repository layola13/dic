consumption:/kənˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. | 消费；消耗；消耗量 |The act of consuming, especially the purchase and use of goods and services.  |  Example 1: The consumption of gasoline has increased in recent years.  近年来汽油的消耗量有所增加。

Example 2:  High energy consumption contributes to climate change.  高能源消耗导致气候变化。 | synonyms:  use, utilization, expenditure, intake | antonyms: production, conservation, abstinence | noun
