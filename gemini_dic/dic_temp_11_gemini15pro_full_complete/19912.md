bio-gas:/ˈbaɪoʊɡæs/ |n.|沼气|A flammable gas produced by the anaerobic decomposition of organic matter, primarily consisting of methane and carbon dioxide.|Bio-gas is a renewable energy source. 沼气是一种可再生能源。|Bio-gas can be used for cooking and heating. 沼气可以用来做饭和取暖。|Synonyms: biogas, methane, marsh gas|Antonyms: natural gas, propane|noun
