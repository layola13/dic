frozennesses:/ˈfroʊzənɪsɪz/
n.
冰冻状态；僵硬状态
The state of being frozen, especially in a metaphorical sense; rigidity or inflexibility.
The frozennesses of their expressions revealed their fear. 他们的表情僵硬，显露出他们的恐惧。
近义词：rigidities, inflexibilities
反义词：fluidity, flexibility
词性：名词

词源：From frozen + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  "Frozen" itself comes from the Old English "froren," past participle of "freosan" (to freeze).
