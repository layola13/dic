monometallism: /ˌmɒnəˈmɛtəlɪzəm/ /ˌmɑːnəˈmɛtəlɪzəm/
n. |单本位制|A monetary system in which only one metal, typically gold or silver, is used as the standard of value and the basis for the country's currency.|The country adopted monometallism in the late 19th century, using gold as the standard.  这个国家在19世纪后期采用了单本位制，以黄金为标准。|monetary standard, single metal standard|bimetallism, dual monetary system
词性: noun
