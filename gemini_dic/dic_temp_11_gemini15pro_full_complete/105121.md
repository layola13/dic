kesar:/ˈkeɪsər/ /ˈkɛsər/ | n. |藏红花，番红花|Saffron, a spice derived from the flower of Crocus sativus.  It has a distinct aroma and flavor and is often used in cooking, particularly in Middle Eastern and Indian cuisine. It is also known for its vibrant reddish-orange color and is one of the most expensive spices in the world.|  The biryani was flavored with kesar.  这道印度香饭用藏红花调味。|Synonym: saffron|Antonym: N/A| Noun
