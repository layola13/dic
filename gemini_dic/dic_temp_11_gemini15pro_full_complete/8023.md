anthracometric: /ˌænθrəkəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
煤尘测量的；炭肺测定法的
Relating to the measurement of coal dust in the lungs, especially for the diagnosis of black lung disease.
Anthracometric measurements are crucial for assessing the severity of coal workers' pneumoconiosis. 煤尘测量对于评估煤矿工人尘肺的严重程度至关重要。
The study employed anthracometric techniques to analyze lung tissue samples.  这项研究采用煤尘测量技术来分析肺组织样本。
近义词:  pulmonary, respiratory (related to lungs)
反义词:  None (no direct antonym)
词性: adjective

anthracometer: /ˌænθrəkəˈmɛtər/
noun
煤尘测量器
An instrument used to measure the amount of coal dust in the lungs.
The anthracometer revealed a high concentration of coal dust in the miner's lungs. 煤尘测量器显示矿工肺部煤尘浓度很高。
New anthracometers are being developed to improve the accuracy of black lung diagnosis. 新的煤尘测量器正在开发中，以提高黑肺病诊断的准确性。
近义词: None (highly specialized instrument)
反义词: None
词性: noun


词源:  "anthraco-" (Greek: coal, charcoal) + "-metric" (Greek: pertaining to measurement)
