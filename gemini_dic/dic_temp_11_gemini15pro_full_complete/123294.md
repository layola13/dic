monogenic:/ˌmɒnəˈdʒɛnɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|单基因的|Caused by or controlled by a single gene.|Example: Cystic fibrosis is a monogenic disorder. 囊性纤维化是一种单基因疾病.|Synonyms: single-gene, Mendelian|Antonyms: polygenic, multifactorial|Adjective

monogenic:/ˌmɒnəˈdʒɛnɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈdʒɛnɪk/| n.|单基因性状|A characteristic or trait controlled by a single gene.|Example:  The study focused on the inheritance of monogenic traits in peas.  这项研究集中于豌豆单基因性状的遗传。|Synonyms: single-gene trait, Mendelian trait|Antonyms: polygenic trait, multifactorial trait|Noun
