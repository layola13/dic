lamprey:/ˈlæmpri/ | n. |七鳃鳗|A primitive eel-like vertebrate fish with a sucker mouth, parasitic on other fish.|The lamprey attached itself to the salmon. 七鳃鳗附着在鲑鱼身上。| 近义词: hagfish (loosely) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider fish preyed upon like salmon, trout)| noun
