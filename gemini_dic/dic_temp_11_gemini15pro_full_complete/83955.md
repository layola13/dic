half-consciousnesses:/ˌhæf ˈkɒnʃəsnɪsɪz/ |n.| 半昏迷状态; 模糊的意识 |A state of being only partially conscious; a reduced level of awareness.|  He drifted in and out of half-consciousness throughout the night. 他整夜都处于半昏迷状态。| Near-synonyms: semiconsciousness, dazedness, stupor | Antonyms: alertness, awareness, consciousness | Noun

half-conscious:/ˌhæf ˈkɒnʃəs/ |adj.| 半昏迷的; 模糊的 |Only partly conscious; not fully aware of one's surroundings. |  She was half-conscious after the blow to the head. 头部遭到撞击后，她处于半昏迷状态。| Near-synonyms: semiconscious, dazed, stuporous | Antonyms: alert, aware, conscious | Adjective



ETYMOLOGY->

"half-" (Old English *healf) + "conscious" (from Latin conscius 'knowing with,' from com- 'with' + scire 'to know') + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), suffix forming nouns of state or quality)
