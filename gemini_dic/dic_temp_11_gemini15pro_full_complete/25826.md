burglar: /ˈbɜːrɡlər/ (UK), /ˈbɜːrɡlər/ (US)| n.|窃贼，破门盗窃者|A person who commits burglary, which is the act of illegally entering a building with the intent to commit a crime, typically theft.|The burglar was caught trying to escape through the window. 窃贼试图从窗户逃跑时被抓住了。|近义词: thief, robber, housebreaker, intruder|反义词: police officer, security guard|词性: noun

burglar: /ˈbɜːrɡlər/|Etymology: From Anglo-French burglour, from Old French burgleor, from burgler "to rob," from burg "house."|
