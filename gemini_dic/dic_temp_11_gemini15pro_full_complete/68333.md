fall-off-the-roof:/fɔːl ɒf ðə ruːf/
idiomatic adjective| 暴跌，急剧下降|A dramatic and rapid decrease, typically used to describe prices, sales, or other measurable quantities.  It evokes the image of something falling quickly and uncontrollably, like from a roof. | Sales fell off the roof after the scandal.  丑闻曝光后，销量暴跌。|近义词：plummet, plunge, nosedive, crater|反义词:skyrocket, soar, surge|词性:idiomatic adjective

fall-off-the-roof:/fɔːl ɒf ðə ruːf/
idiomatic adverb| 暴跌地，急剧下降地|Describes the manner in which something decreases dramatically and rapidly. | Profits fell off the roof this quarter.  本季度利润暴跌。|近义词：sharply, steeply, drastically, precipitously|反义词: dramatically (increase), sharply (increase)|词性:idiomatic adverb
