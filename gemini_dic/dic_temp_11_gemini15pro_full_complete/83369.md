gyron:/ˈdʒaɪrɒn/ | n. | 旋转; 旋涡; (纹章学)燕尾形 | A triangular figure formed by two lines drawn from adjacent angles of a shield and meeting in the fess point.  A spiral or vortex. | The gyron on the shield indicated the knight's lineage. 盾牌上的燕尾形图案表明了骑士的血统。| The swirling waters formed a gyron. 旋转的水形成一个旋涡。 | synonyms: spiral, vortex, whirl, triangle (in heraldry) | antonyms: line, straight | noun
