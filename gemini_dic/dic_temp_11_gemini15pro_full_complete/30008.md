cawed:/kɔːd/| v. |发出鸦叫声；呱呱叫|The crows cawed loudly in the trees.  乌鸦在树上大声呱呱叫。|例句：The raven cawed ominously from the rooftop. 渡鸦在屋顶上发出不祥的叫声。|近义词：croaked, shrieked, squawked|反义词:chirped, tweeted|词性:动词

词源:  Imitative origin, echoing the sound of a crow's call.  来自于模仿乌鸦叫声的声音。
