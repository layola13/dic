hatlessnesses:/ˈhæt.ləs.nɪs.ɪz/
n.

the state or quality of being without a hat.
The hatlessnesses of the beachgoers were noticeable under the scorching sun.  沙滩游客们没戴帽子，在炎炎烈日下格外显眼。
The photograph captured the hatlessnesses of the children playing in the park.  这张照片捕捉到了孩子们在公园里玩耍时没戴帽子的情景。
近义词:  uncovered heads, bareheadedness
反义词:  wearing hats, hattedness
词性: 名词
