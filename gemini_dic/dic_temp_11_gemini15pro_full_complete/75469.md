frush:/frʌʃ/

frush:/frʌʃ/
| v. | 压碎；弄碎；使发出碎裂声 | To crush or crumble something, making a crunching sound.  He frushed the dry leaves under his feet. 他用脚踩碎了干树叶。| crush, crumble, pulverize |  mend, repair | verb
