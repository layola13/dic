inthronize:/ɪnˈθroʊnaɪz/ |v. | 登基；使…登基；使…就职；推崇，尊崇|to place (someone) on a throne, especially as part of a ceremony; to install (someone) in a position of authority or eminence|例句：The archbishop inthronized the new bishop.  大主教为新主教举行登基仪式。

例句：They inthronized their new leader with great ceremony. 他们隆重地为新领导举行就职典礼。|近义词：crown, invest, install, inaugurate|反义词：dethrone, depose|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English enthronizen, from Old French enthroniser, from en- + throne + -iser.  Ultimately from Greek thronos (seat, chair).
