multisourced:/ˌmʌltiˈsɔːrst/ | adj. | 多来源的，来自多个来源的 | Obtained from or supported by multiple sources.  This ensures a more comprehensive and balanced perspective. |  The report's conclusions are multisourced, drawing on academic research, government data, and industry reports.  这份报告的结论来自多个来源，包括学术研究、政府数据和行业报告。

The museum's collection is multisourced, with artifacts coming from private donations, archaeological digs, and purchases from other institutions.  这家博物馆的藏品来自多个来源，包括私人捐赠、考古挖掘和从其他机构购买的文物。 | 近义词:  diverse, varied, eclectic | 反义词: single-source, limited, restricted | 词性: adjective

Etymology: "multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much") + "sourced" (past participle of "source," meaning "to obtain from a source").
