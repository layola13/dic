apprising: /əˈpraɪzɪŋ/
verb
通知；告知
informing someone about something
例句：He kept me apprised of the situation. 他一直让我了解情况。

例句：She apprised him of the dangers of the journey. 她告知他旅途的危险。

近义词：informing, notifying, advising
反义词：concealing, withholding
词性：v.

apprise: /əˈpraɪz/
verb
通知；告知
to inform someone
例句：I thought it right to apprise Chris of what had happened. 我认为应该把发生的事情告诉克里斯。

例句：The president has been apprised of the situation. 总统已经获悉了情况。

近义词：inform, notify, tell
反义词：conceal, withhold
词性：v.
