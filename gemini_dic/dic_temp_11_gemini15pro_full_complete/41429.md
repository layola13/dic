cornerstones:/ˈkɔːrnərˌstoʊnz/ (US) /ˈkɔːnəˌstəʊnz/ (UK)| n.|基石，基础，根本|An important quality or feature on which a particular thing depends or is based.|例句：Trust and communication are the cornerstones of any good relationship. 信任和沟通是任何良好关系的基石。|例句：Hard work and dedication are the cornerstones of success. 努力和奉献是成功的基石。|近义词：foundation, basis, underpinning|反义词: irrelevance, insignificance |词性:noun
