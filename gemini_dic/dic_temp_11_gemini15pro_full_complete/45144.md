cuddling:/ˈkʌdlɪŋ/| n. |拥抱；依偎；搂抱|The act of holding someone close in one's arms, typically to express affection. |She enjoyed the cuddling and warmth of the fire. 她喜欢依偎在火炉旁的温暖感觉。|Synonyms: snuggling, embracing, hugging|Antonyms: separating, pushing away|noun

cuddling:/ˈkʌdlɪŋ/| adj. |拥抱的；依偎的；搂抱的|(Less common usage) Characterized by cuddling. |The cuddling couple looked happy and content.  拥抱的那对情侣看起来幸福而满足。|Synonyms: snuggling, embracing, hugging|Antonyms: distant, aloof|adjective
