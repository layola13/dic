haymaking:/ˈheɪmeɪkɪŋ/| n.|割草；制干草；干草季节|The process of cutting grass and drying it for hay.  The haymaking season is usually in June.|例句：The farmers were busy with haymaking all through June.  农民们整个六月都在忙着割草晒干草。

例句：Haymaking is hard work, but rewarding. 割草晒干草是辛苦的工作，但很有收获。|近义词：hay harvest, forage harvesting|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hay + making.  "Hay" comes from Old English  hēg,  meaning "hedge," later referring to grass cut and dried for fodder. "Making" comes from Old English  macian,  meaning "to make" or "to do."
