algol:/ˈælɡɒl/ (UK) /ˈælɡɔːl/ (US)| n.|算法语言，ALGOL|A high-level programming language designed for scientific computation. It was influential in the development of other programming languages like Pascal and C. |Algol was one of the first languages to introduce block structure and structured programming concepts.| Algol was used extensively in scientific computing in the 1960s and 70s.
