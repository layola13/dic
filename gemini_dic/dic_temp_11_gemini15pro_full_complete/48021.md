decisioned:/dɪˈsɪʒənd/ | adj. | 已决定的，已裁决的 |  While "decisioned" is grammatically correct as the past participle of the verb "to decision," it's considered non-standard and generally avoided in formal writing. It implies that a decision has been made about something.  Its use is primarily confined to informal contexts or specific jargon, particularly in business or legal settings. | The case was decisioned last week.  上周对这个案子作出了裁决。 | Near synonyms: decided, resolved, determined, settled | Antonyms: undecided, unresolved, pending, open | 词性:形容词
