oculiform:/ˈɒkjʊlɪfɔːm/
adjective
眼状的
Resembling an eye in form or appearance.
The oculiform markings on the butterfly's wings served as a defense mechanism. 蝴蝶翅膀上的眼状斑纹是一种防御机制。
近义词: eye-shaped, eyelike
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "shapeless" or describing a specific non-eye shape)
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *oculus* (eye) + *-formis* (form, shape).
