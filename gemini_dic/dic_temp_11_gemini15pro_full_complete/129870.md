non-influential:/ˌnɒn ˌɪnfluˈenʃəl/ | adj. | 无影响力的；不重要的 | Not having or exerting influence; unimportant. |  Her role in the project was non-influential. 她在这个项目中的作用微不足道。 | The non-influential committee was eventually disbanded. 这个没有影响力的委员会最终被解散了。 | 近义词: insignificant, unimportant, powerless, inconsequential | 反义词: influential, powerful, significant, important | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + influential (adjective derived from influence, from Old French influence "influx," from Late Latin influentia "a flowing in," from Latin influere "to flow into")
