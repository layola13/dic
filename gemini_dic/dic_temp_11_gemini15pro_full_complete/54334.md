disinherit:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪt/ | vt. |剥夺继承权，取消继承资格|To deprive (someone) of the right to inherit property, especially by making a will that excludes them.|例句：He disinherited his son for marrying against his wishes. 他剥夺了儿子的继承权，因为他违背了他的意愿结婚。

例句：She was disinherited by her father. 她被父亲剥夺了继承权。


|近义词：cut off, exclude|反义词: bequeath, endow |词性: 动词
