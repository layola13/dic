jacquard:/ˈdʒækɑːrd/ | n. adj. |提花织物，提花机；提花的 |A fabric with an intricately woven design, or the machine used to produce it.  It's named after its inventor, Joseph Marie Jacquard.|  This sofa is upholstered in a beautiful jacquard fabric. 这款沙发采用美丽的提花织物装饰。 | The jacquard loom revolutionized textile production. 提花织机彻底改变了纺织生产。 | synonyms: brocade, damask, tapestry | antonyms: plain, simple | noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the name of Joseph Marie Jacquard (1752-1834), a French weaver and inventor who invented the Jacquard loom.
