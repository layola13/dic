gigatons:/ˈɡɪɡətʌnz/ | n. |十亿吨 |A unit of mass equal to one billion (10^9) tons.  Specifically, it often refers to metric tons (tonnes), each of which is 1000 kilograms. |例句：Global CO2 emissions reached 36 gigatons in 2021.  全球二氧化碳排放量在2021年达到了360亿吨。

|例句：Scientists estimate that the Antarctic ice sheet contains enough water to raise global sea levels by several tens of gigatons. 科学家估计，南极冰盖包含足够的水，可以使全球海平面升高数十亿吨。 |近义词：billions of tons |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
