enslavement:/ɪnˈsleɪvmənt/| n.|奴役，束缚|The enslavement of African people is a dark chapter in human history. 非洲人民的奴役是人类历史上黑暗的一章。|例句：The enslavement of the indigenous population led to widespread suffering.  土著居民的奴役导致了广泛的苦难。

例句：Generations suffered under the brutal enslavement imposed by the conquerors.  几代人在征服者强加的残酷奴役下受苦。|近义词：captivity, bondage, servitude, oppression|反义词：freedom, liberation, emancipation|词性:noun

词源: From en- + slave + -ment.  Late Middle English: from en- ‘cause to be’ + slave + -ment.
