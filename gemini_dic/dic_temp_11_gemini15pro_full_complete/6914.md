ancientest:/ˈeɪnʃəntɪst/ | adj. |最古老的 |Superlative form of ancient.  While grammatically correct, it's considered archaic and rarely used.  "Most ancient" is generally preferred.| He claimed to have the ancientest manuscript in existence. 他声称拥有现存最古老的手稿。| 近义词: most ancient, oldest | 反义词: newest, youngest |词性: 形词


词源: From Middle English *auncientest, from Old French ancieneste, superlative of ancien (“ancient”).
