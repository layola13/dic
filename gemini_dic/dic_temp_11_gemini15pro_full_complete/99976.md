例句：The artist's sculpture featured an intricate interosculation of metal and wood.  艺术家的雕塑以金属和木材的复杂交织为特色。

|近义词：anastomosis, fusion, joining, union|反义词：separation, division|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Latin *interosculatio*, from interosculatus, past participle of interosculari (“to kiss mutually”), from inter- (“between”) + osculari (“to kiss”), from osculum (“little mouth, kiss”), diminutive of os (“mouth”).
