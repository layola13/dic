basest:/ˈbeɪsɪst/| adj.|最卑鄙的；最低劣的；最下等的|of the lowest quality, value, or rank|例句：He appealed to their basest instincts. 他激起了他们最低劣的本性。

例句：That was the basest act of betrayal I've ever witnessed. 那是我见过的最卑鄙的背叛行为。|近义词：vile, despicable, contemptible, mean, low|反义词：noble, virtuous, honorable|词性：形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *basest*, superlative of *bas*, from Anglo-Norman *bas*, from Late Latin *bassus* ("thick, low"), from Gaulish *bassos* ("low, short"), from Proto-Celtic *bassos* ("low, small, shallow"), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰas- ("small, little, short").
