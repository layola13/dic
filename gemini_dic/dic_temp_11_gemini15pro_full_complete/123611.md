montessori:/ˌmɒntəˈsɔːri/ | adj. n. | 蒙台梭利；蒙特梭利 |  Relating to or characteristic of an educational system developed by Maria Montessori, based on self-directed activity, hands-on learning and collaborative play.  It can also refer to schools or educational materials used in this system. |  Example 1:  My child attends a Montessori school. 我的孩子在一所蒙台梭利学校上学。

Example 2:  The Montessori method emphasizes learning through experience. 蒙台梭利方法强调通过经验学习。 | 近义词: child-centered, progressive | 反义词: traditional, conventional | 词性: adjective, noun

词源:  Derived from the name of Maria Montessori (1870-1952), the Italian physician and educator who developed the method.
