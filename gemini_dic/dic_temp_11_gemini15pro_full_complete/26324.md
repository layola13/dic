buys:/baɪz/| v.| 买; 购买 |He buys a new car every year. 他每年都买一辆新车。|近义词：purchases, acquires, obtains|反义词:sells, disposes of|verb

词源:
From Middle English *bien, *buggen, from Old English bycgan (“to buy, purchase, redeem, pay for”), from Proto-Germanic *bugananą (“to buy, exchange, barter”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰewg- (“to bend, curve, flex”). Cognate with Dutch buigen (“to bend, bow”), German biegen (“to bend, curve”), Swedish böja (“to bend”).
