energised: /ˈenədʒaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈenərˌdʒaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|充满活力的，精力充沛的|Having or showing a lot of energy and enthusiasm.|例句：After a good night's sleep, I felt energised and ready to tackle the day.  睡了一夜好觉后，我感到精力充沛，准备迎接新的一天。

例句：The team was energised by their coach's inspiring speech. 教练鼓舞人心的讲话让团队充满了活力。

近义词：invigorated, stimulated, revitalized, dynamic, lively|反义词：lethargic, tired, exhausted, drained, listless|词性:形容词
