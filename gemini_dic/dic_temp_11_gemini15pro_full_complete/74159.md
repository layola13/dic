fount:/faʊnt/
noun
源泉; 泉源; 根源
a source of something
例句：The college was a fount of radical ideas. 这所大学是激进思想的源泉。
例句：She was a fount of knowledge on the subject. 她在这个问题上是知识的源泉。
近义词：source, origin, spring, wellspring
反义词：end, conclusion, outcome
