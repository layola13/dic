impairment:/ɪmˈpeəmənt/ | n. | 损伤；减值；障碍 |  The state of being diminished, weakened, or damaged, especially mentally or physically. |  His hearing impairment made it difficult to understand conversations. 他的听力障碍使他难以理解对话。| synonyms: disability, handicap, deficiency, weakness | antonyms: enhancement, improvement, strengthening | noun

impairment:/ɪmˈpeəmənt/ | n. | 损害，损伤 |  The act of impairing or the state of being impaired; damage. | The accident caused impairment to his vision. 事故损害了他的视力。 | synonyms: damage, harm, injury, deterioration | antonyms: repair, restoration, improvement | noun
