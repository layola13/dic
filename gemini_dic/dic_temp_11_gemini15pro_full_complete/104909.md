kegs:/keɡz/| n. |小桶，啤酒桶|A small barrel, typically used for storing beer or other beverages. |例句：They tapped the kegs and the party began. 他们打开了啤酒桶，派对开始了。

例句：We need two more kegs for the picnic.  野餐我们还需要两个啤酒桶。|近义词：barrels, casks|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *kegge, from Old Norse kaggi (“cask, keg”), from Proto-Germanic *kagjō (“crooked, bent object”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)keng- (“to bend, crook”). Cognate with German Kage (“tub, pail”).
