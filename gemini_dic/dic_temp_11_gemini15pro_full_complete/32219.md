chicha:/ˈtʃiːtʃə/| n.|奇恰酒，玉米啤酒|A fermented (alcoholic) beverage usually derived from maize.  It is a traditional drink in the Andes region of South America.|例句：They brewed chicha for the festival. 他们为节日酿造了奇恰酒。|例句：Chicha is an important part of Andean culture. 奇恰酒是安第斯文化的重要组成部分。|近义词：beer, beverage|反义词:|名词
