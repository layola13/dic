infuscate:/ɪnˈfʌskeɪt/ | v. | 使模糊；使昏暗；使困惑 | To make something unclear, obscure, or confusing. | The writer infuscated the issue with extraneous details.  作者用无关紧要的细节混淆了问题。 | The dense fog infuscated the coastline. 浓雾使海岸线变得模糊不清。 | Synonyms: obscure, confuse, bewilder, muddle, obfuscate | Antonyms: clarify, elucidate, illuminate | Verb
