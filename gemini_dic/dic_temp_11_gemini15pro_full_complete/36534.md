collaborationist:/kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃənɪst/
n.
通敌者，合作者，卖国贼
A person who cooperates traitorously with an enemy; a person who collaborates with an enemy occupying force.
The journalist was labeled a collaborationist for writing articles supporting the enemy regime.  这名记者因为撰写支持敌方政权的文章而被贴上通敌者的标签。
During the war, some citizens became collaborationists to protect their families.  战争期间，一些公民为了保护家人而成了通敌者。
近义词: traitor, quisling, cooperator
反义词: resister, partisan, patriot
词性: noun


collaborationist:/kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃənɪst/
adj.
通敌的，合作的
Cooperating with an enemy occupying force.
He took a collaborationist stance during the occupation. 他在占领期间采取了通敌的立场。
The collaborationist government was quickly overthrown. 通敌政府很快被推翻了。
近义词: cooperative, complicit
反义词: resistant, opposing
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *collaborationniste*, from *collaboration* (collaboration), from *collaborer* (to collaborate), from Latin *collaborare* (to work together), from *com-* (with, together) + *laborare* (to work).
