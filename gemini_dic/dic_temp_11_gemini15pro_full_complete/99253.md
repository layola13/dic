intercomparing: /ˌɪntərəmˈpɛərɪŋ/
verb
相互比较
The process of comparing multiple things to each other.
Scientists are intercomparing data from different satellites to study climate change. 科学家们正在相互比较来自不同卫星的数据以研究气候变化。
