absolutism:/ˈæbsəluːtɪzəm/
n.
专制主义; absolutism
A political theory that absolute power should be vested in one or more rulers.
The king ruled by absolutism. 国王实行专制统治。
Synonyms: autocracy, despotism, tyranny, dictatorship
Antonyms: democracy, liberalism
noun
