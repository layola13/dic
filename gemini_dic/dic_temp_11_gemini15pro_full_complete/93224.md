ihram:/ɪˈhrɑːm/| n.|伊赫拉姆, 禁戒状态|A state of ritual purity that Muslims must enter before performing the Hajj or Umrah pilgrimage.  It involves wearing specific clothing and abstaining from certain actions.|Example: He entered the state of ihram before embarking on his Hajj journey. 他在开始朝觐之旅前进入了伊赫拉姆状态。

Example:  The pilgrims were all in ihram. 朝圣者都穿着伊赫拉姆服。|近义词:sacred state, consecrated state|反义词:halal (permissible state)|词性:noun
