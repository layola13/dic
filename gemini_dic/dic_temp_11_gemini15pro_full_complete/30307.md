cenozoic:/ˌsiːnəˈzoʊɪk/ /ˌsɛnəˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. n. |新生代的；新生代 |Relating to or denoting the most recent era in geological time, from the end of the Cretaceous period to the present, characterized by the development of mammals and flowering plants.  The Cenozoic Era itself. |The Cenozoic era began about 66 million years ago. 新生代始于大约6600万年前。| The Cenozoic is the age of mammals. 新生代是哺乳动物的时代。|近义词: Recent, modern |反义词: Paleozoic, Mesozoic |词性: adjective, noun
