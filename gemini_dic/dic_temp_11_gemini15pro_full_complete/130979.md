nonastronomically:/ˌnɑːnˌæstrəˈnɑːmɪkli/
adverb
非天文学地；在非天文学的尺度上
Relating to matters outside the field of astronomy; not on an astronomical scale.
The cost of the project, while high, was nonastronomically so.  这个项目的成本虽然很高，但并非天文数字。
The numbers involved were large, but nonastronomically large. 涉及的数字很大，但并非天文数字般大。
近义词:reasonably, moderately, affordably
反义词:astronomically, exorbitantly, immensely
词性:adverb
