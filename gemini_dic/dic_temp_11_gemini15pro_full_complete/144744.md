over-assumption:/ˌoʊvərəˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. | 过度假设，过分臆断 | The act of assuming too much; an assumption that goes beyond the available evidence. |  His over-assumption about her abilities led to her being given a task she couldn't handle. 他对她能力的过度假设导致她被分配了一项无法胜任的任务。| synonyms: presumption, conjecture, supposition | antonyms: understatement, fact, truth | noun
