free-range:/ˌfriː ˈreɪndʒ/
adjective
自由放养的
allowed to move around freely outside, rather than being kept in a small space
例句：We buy free-range eggs. 我们买散养鸡蛋。

例句：Free-range chickens are generally healthier. 散养鸡通常更健康。

近义词：organic, natural, outdoor-reared
反义词：battery, caged, confined
词性：adjective


free-range:/ˌfriː ˈreɪndʒ/
noun
自由放养
the practice of keeping animals, especially chickens, outdoors rather than in cages
例句：The farm practices free-range. 这个农场实行自由放养。

例句：There is a growing demand for free-range products. 对自由放养产品的需求不断增长。

近义词：free roaming
反义词：intensive farming, factory farming
词性：noun
