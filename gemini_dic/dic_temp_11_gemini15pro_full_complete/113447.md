lynchpin:/ˈlɪntʃpɪn/| n.|关键人物；核心人物；关键|A person or thing vital to an enterprise or organization.|She is the lynchpin of the whole team.  她是整个团队的核心人物。|The new factory is the lynchpin of the regional economy. 新工厂是地区经济的关键。|synonyms: cornerstone, keystone, linchpin, kingpin, backbone|antonyms:  nonessential, unimportant|noun
