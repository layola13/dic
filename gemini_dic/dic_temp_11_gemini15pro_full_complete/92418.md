hypohyal:/ˈhaɪpoʊˌhaɪəl/
noun
舌骨下的；舌骨下骨
situated below the hyoid bone; a bone or cartilage below the hyoid.
The hypohyal is a small, paired bone found in some vertebrates, located below the hyoid bone. 舌骨下骨是某些脊椎动物中发现的一块小的成对骨骼，位于舌骨下方。
The hypohyal cartilage contributes to the structure of the larynx. 舌骨下软骨构成喉部结构的一部分。
Synonyms: subhyal
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider terms referring to structures *above* the hyoid, such as "epihyal")
