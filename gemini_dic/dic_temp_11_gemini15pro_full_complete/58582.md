dulcimers:/ˈdʌlsɪmərz/ | n. |扬琴，齐特琴|A stringed musical instrument, similar to a zither, with metal strings stretched over a trapezoidal soundboard. It is played by striking the strings with small hammers held in the hands. |例句：The dulcimers created a beautiful, ethereal sound. 扬琴发出优美空灵的声音。

例句：She learned to play the dulcimers when she was a child. 她小时候学会了弹扬琴。|近义词：zither, psaltery, hammered dulcimer|反义词:|名词
