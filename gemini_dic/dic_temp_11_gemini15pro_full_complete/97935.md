innovated:/ˈɪnəveɪtɪd/
verb
创新，革新
past participle of innovate: introduce something new; make changes in anything established.
Apple has always innovated in the tech industry. 苹果一直在科技行业进行创新。
The company innovated a new production process.  这家公司创新了一种新的生产流程。
Synonyms: pioneered, introduced, developed, created, originated
Antonyms: copied, imitated, duplicated
