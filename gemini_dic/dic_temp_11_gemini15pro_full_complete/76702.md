gambadoes:/ˌɡæmbəˈdoʊz/
noun
突发奇想；古怪举动；（尤指马的）腾跃
A sudden energetic, playful, or capricious action; a prank or antic; (especially of a horse) a capering or curvetting.
He surprised everyone with his unexpected gambadoes on the dance floor. 他在舞池里出人意料的古怪举动让每个人都感到惊讶。
Synonym: antics, capers, pranks, frolics, escapades
Antonym: stillness, inactivity, seriousness
