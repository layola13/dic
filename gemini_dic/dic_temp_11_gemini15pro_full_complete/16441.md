barefacedly:/ˌbɛəˈfeɪsɪdli/
adv.
厚颜无耻地，公然地
in an open and shameless manner; brazenly
He barefacedly lied to the judge. 他厚颜无耻地对法官撒谎。
Synonyms: brazenly, audaciously, impudently, shamelessly
Antonyms: discreetly, subtly, modestly, shyly
词性: 副词


barefaced:/ˈbɛəˈfeɪst/
adj.
厚颜无耻的，公然的
shameless and undisguised
It was a barefaced lie. 这是一个弥天大谎。
Synonyms: blatant, flagrant, shameless, audacious
Antonyms: discreet, subtle, modest, shy
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bare + faced.  "Barefaced" originally (16th century) meant with the face uncovered, hence showing no concealment or shame.  The adverbial form "barefacedly" arose later.
