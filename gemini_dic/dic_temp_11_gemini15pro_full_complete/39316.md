conirostral:/ˌkɒnɪˈrɒstrəl/ | adj. | 锥嘴的；具有圆锥形喙的 | Having a conical beak; describing birds with strong, cone-shaped bills, typically seed-eaters. |  The finch is a conirostral bird.  雀科鸟类是锥嘴鸟。| 近义词: cone-billed | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider describing beaks of different shapes, e.g., long-billed, hooked-billed) | 词性: adjective
