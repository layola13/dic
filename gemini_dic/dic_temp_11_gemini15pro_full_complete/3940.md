agro-industrialist:/ˌæɡroʊɪnˈdʌstriəlɪst/
|n.|农工商企业家；农业产业家|A person who owns or manages a business involved in both agriculture and industry, often combining farming with processing or manufacturing.  This individual often has substantial holdings in both agricultural production and the industrial processing of agricultural products.|The agro-industrialist invested heavily in new machinery to process his crops.  这位农工商企业家投入巨资购买新机器来加工他的农作物。|Synonyms: agribusinessman, agricultural entrepreneur|Antonyms: subsistence farmer, smallholder|noun
