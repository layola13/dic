codriver:/ˈkoʊˌdraɪvər/| n.|副驾驶，领航员|A person who sits next to the driver in a vehicle and helps with navigation or other tasks.  A codriver assists the driver, often in rallies or long-distance races.|例句：The codriver reads the pace notes to the driver during the rally stage.  领航员在拉力赛阶段向驾驶员读领航记录。

例句：My brother acted as my codriver on the long road trip. 我哥哥在长途旅行中担任我的副驾驶。|近义词：navigator, co-pilot|反义词:driver, pilot|词性:noun
