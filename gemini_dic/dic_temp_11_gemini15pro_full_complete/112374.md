losenger:/ˈlɒzɪndʒə(r)/ /ˈlɑːzɪndʒər/| n.|奉承者，说甜言蜜语的人|a person who flatters or uses insincere praise to gain favour. |例句：He's such a losenger, always telling the boss how great he is. 他真是个马屁精，总是告诉老板他有多棒。

例句：The king was surrounded by losengers who only told him what he wanted to hear. 国王周围都是些奉承者，只告诉他想听的话。

|近义词：flatterer, sycophant, yes-man|反义词:critic, detractor|词性:noun
