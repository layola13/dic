half-irish:/ˌhæf ˈaɪrɪʃ/ | adj. | 半爱尔兰血统的 | Of or relating to a person having one parent of Irish descent and the other of a different descent. |  My friend is half-Irish; his mother was born in Dublin. 我的朋友有一半爱尔兰血统；他母亲出生在都柏林。

| Example: She has a half-Irish accent, a blend of Dublin lilt and London twang. 她带有半爱尔兰口音，混合了都柏林的轻快和伦敦的鼻音。 | Near Synonyms: part-Irish, half Irish descent | Antonyms: full-blooded Irish | Word Type: Adjective
