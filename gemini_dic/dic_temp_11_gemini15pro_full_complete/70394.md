fiftieth:/ˈfɪftiəθ/ (US), /ˈfɪftiɪθ/ (UK)| adj. n.|第五十的，第五十|Representing the number 50 in a sequence; the ordinal form of fifty.  The person or thing in the fiftieth position.|例句：This is her fiftieth birthday. 这是她的五十岁生日。

例句：He finished in fiftieth place. 他最终获得了第五十名。

近义词：quinquagesimal|反义词:first|词性:adjective, noun
