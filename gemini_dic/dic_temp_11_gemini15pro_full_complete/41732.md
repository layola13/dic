corroding:/kəˈrəʊdɪŋ/ | v. | 腐蚀，侵蚀 |  Gradually destroying or damaging (metal, stone, or other materials) by chemical action. | Acid rain is corroding the ancient monument. 酸雨正在腐蚀这座古老的纪念碑。| synonyms: eroding, rusting, decaying, deteriorating | antonyms: preserving, protecting, strengthening | verb

corroding:/kəˈrəʊdɪŋ/ | adj. | 腐蚀性的 | Having the power to corrode. | The corroding effects of salt water can damage metal structures. 盐水的腐蚀作用会损坏金属结构。| synonyms: caustic, erosive | antonyms: protective, preservative | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *corrodere*, from *com-* (together) + *rodere* (to gnaw).
