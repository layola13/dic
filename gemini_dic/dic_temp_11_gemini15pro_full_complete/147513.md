overpampers:/ˌoʊvərˈpæmpərz/ | v. | 过分溺爱，娇惯 | To pamper excessively; to spoil by indulging too much. | She overpampers her dog, letting it sleep in her bed and feeding it gourmet treats. 她太溺爱她的狗了，让它睡在自己的床上，还给它吃美味佳肴。 | He overpampers his children, buying them everything they want. 他过分溺爱他的孩子，给他们买任何他们想要的东西。 | 近义词: spoil, coddle, indulge | 反义词: neglect, deprive | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + pamper.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Pamper" comes from the Middle Dutch word "pampelen" meaning "to treat with excessive care."
