conditioning:/kənˈdɪʃənɪŋ/ | n. |  调节;  训练;  空调;  条件作用 | the process of training or influencing someone or something to behave in a particular way or to accept particular circumstances. | Example: The athlete underwent intense physical conditioning.  运动员进行了高强度的体能训练。

Example:  Air conditioning is essential in this climate. 在这种气候下，空调是必不可少的。 | Synonyms: training, preparation, acclimatization, adaptation | Antonyms: neglect, disregard | noun


conditioning:/kənˈdɪʃənɪŋ/ | adj. |  条件反射的;  调节的 | relating to or used for conditioning something or someone.  | Example:  Classical conditioning is a learning process. 经典条件反射是一种学习过程。

Example:  He used a conditioning shampoo. 他使用了一种护发素。 | Synonyms: modifying, adapting, adjusting | Antonyms:  N/A | adjective
