meditating:/ˈmedɪteɪtɪŋ/
v.
冥想，沉思
Engaging in contemplation or reflection, often in a quiet setting.
She was meditating in the garden. 她正在花园里冥想。
He was meditating on the meaning of life. 他在沉思人生的意义。
近义词：contemplating, pondering, reflecting
反义词：rushing, hurrying
词性：动词 (present participle of meditate)
