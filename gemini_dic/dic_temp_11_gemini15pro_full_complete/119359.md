micrococcal:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈkɒkəl/| adj.|微球菌的|Relating to or caused by micrococci (spherical bacteria).|例句：Micrococcal infections are typically treated with antibiotics. 微球菌感染通常用抗生素治疗。

例句：The lab identified a micrococcal strain in the sample. 实验室在样本中鉴定出一种微球菌菌株。|近义词：staphylococcal (somewhat), bacterial|反义词: None (lack of a direct opposite for a taxonomic adjective)|词性: adjective

词源: From "micrococcus" + "-al". "Micrococcus" comes from Greek "mikros" (small) + "kokkos" (berry, grain).
