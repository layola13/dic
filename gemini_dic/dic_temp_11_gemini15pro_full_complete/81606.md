greatest:/ˈɡreɪtɪst/
adjective
最伟大的；最大的
superlative of great
例句：He was the greatest scientist of his time. 他是他那个时代最伟大的科学家。

例句：This is the greatest challenge we have ever faced. 这是我们面临过的最大挑战。

近义词：best, finest, top, supreme, outstanding, remarkable, exceptional
反义词：worst, least, smallest, poorest

词性: adjective
