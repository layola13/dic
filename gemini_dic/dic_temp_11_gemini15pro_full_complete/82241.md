ground-plans:/ˈɡraʊnd plænz/
noun
平面图；地基图；初步方案
A drawing of the layout of a building at ground level. A basic plan or scheme.
The architect showed us the ground-plans for the new house.  建筑师给我们看了新房子的平面图。
The committee approved the ground-plans for the new project. 委员会批准了新项目的初步方案。
blueprints, layouts, diagrams
elevations, perspectives
