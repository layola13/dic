nursepond: /ˈnɜːrspɒnd/ (UK) /ˈnɜːrspɑːnd/ (US) | n. | 育苗池，养鱼池 | A pond used for rearing young fish or other aquatic animals before they are transferred to a larger body of water. | The fish farmer transferred the fingerlings from the nursepond to the main lake.  养鱼者将鱼苗从育苗池转移到主湖。| The trout were raised in a nursepond before being released into the river.  这些鳟鱼在被放生到河里之前是在育苗池里养大的。| synonyms: hatchery, breeding pond, fish farm | antonyms:  lake, ocean, river | noun
