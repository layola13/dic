biannually:/baɪˈænjuəli/
adverb
一年两次地；每半年一次地
occurring twice a year
The magazine is published biannually, in January and July. 这本杂志一年出版两次，分别在一月和七月。
近义词：semiannually, twice a year
反义词：biennially
词性：副词


biannually:/baɪˈænjuəli/
adjective
一年两次的
happening twice a year
The company holds biannual conferences, one in spring and one in autumn.  这家公司每年举行两次会议，一次在春季，一次在秋季。
近义词：semiannual
反义词：biennial
词性：形容词


词源:
From bi- (twice) + annual + -ly.  "Bi-" comes from Latin, meaning "two". "Annual" comes from Latin "annus", meaning "year".
