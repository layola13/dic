germ:/dʒɜːrm/ (US), /dʒɜːm/ (UK)| n.|细菌，病菌；起源，萌芽|A germ is a very small living thing that can cause disease. It can also refer to the origin or earliest stage of development of something.|例句：Wash your hands to remove germs.  洗手去除细菌。|例句：The germ of an idea began to form in his mind. 一个想法的萌芽开始在他脑海中形成。|近义词：bacteria, microbe, pathogen, seed, origin, beginning|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "cure" or "sterility" in specific contexts)|词性:noun
