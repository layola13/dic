northernnesses:/ˈnɔːrðərnɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
北方性，北方特质
The state or quality of being northern.
The northernnesses of the Scandinavian countries are evident in their long winters and short summers. 斯堪的纳维亚国家的北方性在漫长冬季和短暂夏季中显而易见。
Near Synonyms: northern qualities, northern characteristics
Antonyms: southernnesses, southern qualities
