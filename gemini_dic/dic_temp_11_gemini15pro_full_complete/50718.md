dernier:/dɛʁ.nje/ (French), /ˈdɜːrnjeɪ/ (Anglicized)| adj. n.|最后的，末尾的；最后的人（或事物）|Last; final; ultimate.  Referring to the end of a sequence, series, or time period. Can also refer to the last person or thing remaining.|例句：Ce soir, nous assisterons au dernier concert de la tournée.  Tonight, we will attend the last concert of the tour.|例句：He ate the dernier morceau of cake. 他吃了最后一块蛋糕。|近义词：last, final, ultimate, concluding|反义词：first, initial, beginning|词性: adjective, noun

词源: From Old French *derreinier, from Vulgar Latin *de retro, from Latin de "from" + retro "behind, backward."
