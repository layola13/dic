incising:/ɪnˈsaɪzɪŋ/
verb
切割；雕刻
The act of cutting or carving into a surface.
The surgeon is incising the skin.  外科医生正在切开皮肤。
Synonyms: carving, cutting, engraving, etching
Antonyms: joining, uniting, connecting
