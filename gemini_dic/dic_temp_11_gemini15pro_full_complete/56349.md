dogwatch:/ˈdɒɡwɒtʃ/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡwɑːtʃ/ (US)| n.|航海值班; 夜班 (尤指下午四点到八点的两班)|A period of watch duty on a ship, especially a two-hour period in the late afternoon or early evening.|The crew divided into two watches for the dogwatch. 船员分成两班值航海夜班。|The captain assigned me to the first dogwatch. 船长指派我值第一班航海夜班。|Synonyms: watch, shift|Antonyms: off-duty, rest|noun
