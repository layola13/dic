blustered:/ˈblʌstərd/| v.|咆哮，怒吼；狂吹，猛刮|To talk in a loud, aggressive, and often empty or threatening way; (of wind) to blow loudly and violently.|The coach blustered at the referee about the unfair call. 教练对着裁判咆哮，抱怨判罚不公平。

The wind blustered through the trees. 风猛烈地吹过树林。|近义词：raged, roared, stormed, shouted, yelled|反义词：whispered, murmured, calmed|词性:动词

bluster:/ˈblʌstər/| n.|咆哮，怒吼；狂风，大风|Loud, aggressive, and often empty or threatening talk; a strong wind.|His bluster didn't scare anyone. 他的咆哮并没有吓到任何人。

The bluster of the storm made it hard to walk. 暴风雨的狂风使行走困难。|近义词：rage, roar, storm, shouting, yelling|反义词：whisper, murmur, calm|词性:名词
