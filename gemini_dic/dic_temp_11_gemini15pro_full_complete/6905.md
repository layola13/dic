anchorwomen: /ˈæŋkərˌwɪmɪn/
n.
女主播；女主持人
A woman who presents and coordinates a live television or radio program, especially a news program.
Barbara Walters was one of the first and most respected anchorwomen in television news.  芭芭拉·沃尔特斯是电视新闻界最早也是最受尊敬的女主播之一。
The anchorwomen calmly reported the breaking news. 女主播平静地报道了突发新闻。

近义词: anchor, newsreader, presenter, broadcaster, journalist
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider roles like "audience," "viewer")
词性: noun
