词源:
From inappropriate + -ness.  "Inappropriate" comes from the prefix "in-" (not) + "appropriate" (suitable or proper). "Appropriate" derives from the Latin "appropriare" (to make one's own), from "ad-" (to) + "proprius" (one's own).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
