glanced:/ɡlænst/ | v. | 一瞥，扫视 | to look quickly or briefly at something or someone |  He glanced at his watch and realized he was late. 他看了一眼手表，意识到自己迟到了。|  She glanced over her shoulder to see if anyone was following her. 她回头瞥了一眼，看看是否有人跟着她。| synonyms: glimpse, peek, skim, scan | antonyms: stare, gaze, scrutinize | verb
