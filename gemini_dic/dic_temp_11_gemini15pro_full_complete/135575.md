nonponderously:/ˌnɒnpɒnˈdɪərəsli/ | adv. | 轻快地，不沉重地 |  In a manner that is not ponderous; lightly and easily. | She danced nonponderously across the stage, despite the heavy costume. 她轻盈地穿过舞台，尽管穿着沉重的戏服。| Synonyms: lightly, nimbly, agilely, gracefully | Antonyms: ponderously, heavily, clumsily, awkwardly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From non- + ponderous + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "ponderous" means heavy and awkward, and "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
