inalienableness:/ɪnˈeɪliənəbəl.nəs/
n.
不可剥夺性
The state or quality of being inalienable; impossiblity of being alienated or surrendered.
The inalienableness of human rights is a cornerstone of modern democracy.  人权的不可剥夺性是现代民主的基石。
Synonyms:  nontransferability,  unforfeitableness,  indefeasibility
Antonyms: transferability, alienability
词性: noun
