monument:/ˈmɑːnjumənt/ (US) /ˈmɒnjumənt/ (UK)| n. |纪念碑，纪念物；历史遗迹；丰碑|A structure, such as a building or statue, erected to commemorate a person, event, or achievement.  Something that serves as a lasting reminder of a person, achievement, or event.|例句：The Washington Monument is a towering obelisk. 华盛顿纪念碑是一座高耸的方尖碑。

例句：This building is a monument to Victorian architecture. 这座建筑是维多利亚时代建筑的纪念碑。|近义词：memorial, tribute, testament, relic|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
