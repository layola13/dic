juddery:/ˈdʒʌdəri/| adj.|颠簸的，震颤的，不稳定的|Characterized by or causing juddering; shaking or vibrating unsteadily.|The old bus made a juddery journey across the bumpy road.  这辆旧巴士在崎岖不平的道路上颠簸前行。|The film had a juddery effect due to the faulty projector.  由于投影机故障，电影画面抖动得厉害。|近义词：shaky, jerky, bumpy, unstable|反义词：smooth, steady|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "judder" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Judder" likely originates from a frequentative form of "jut," meaning to project or stick out. The repeated small movements or jolts implied by "jut" contribute to the sense of vibration and unsteadiness conveyed by "judder" and "juddery."
