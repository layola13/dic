gutturalisms:/ˈɡʌtərəlɪzəmz/| n. |喉音，喉音化|Sounds produced in the throat; harsh, throaty sounds.  The language is full of gutturalisms, making it difficult for some to pronounce.|His speech was filled with gutturalisms that made him sound angry.|近义词：growls, grunts, rasps|反义词: mellifluous sounds, smooth sounds|noun
