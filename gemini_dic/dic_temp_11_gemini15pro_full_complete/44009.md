criminate:/ˈkrɪmɪneɪt/| v. |控告；归罪于；暗示...有罪|To accuse of or implicate in a crime or fault; to make appear guilty.|His testimony tended to criminate the defendant. 他的证词倾向于控告被告。|近义词：incriminate, accuse, indict, charge|反义词：exonerate, absolve, acquit|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *criminatus*, past participle of criminare (“to accuse”), from Latin crimen (“accusation, charge, crime”).
