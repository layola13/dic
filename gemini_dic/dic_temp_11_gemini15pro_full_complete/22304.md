boniest:/ˈboʊniɪst/
adjective
最瘦骨嶙峋的
superlative adjective of bony: having little flesh; thin.
The boniest model in the agency looked like she hadn't eaten in weeks.  这家经纪公司最瘦骨嶙峋的模特看起来好像几个星期没吃东西了。
The boniest of the three dogs was adopted last. 三只狗中最瘦骨嶙峋的那只最后被领养了。
gaunt, skinny, emaciated
fleshy, plump, robust
