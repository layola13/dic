louchest:/ˈlaʊtʃɪst/
adjective
最懒散的；最邋遢的；最堕落的
Most louche; most decadent; most dissipated.
He was known for his louche lifestyle and extravagant parties. 他以其懒散的生活方式和奢华的派对而闻名。
近义词：dissolute, decadent, debauched, depraved, reprobate
反义词：virtuous, upright, moral, respectable, decent
词性: adjective

词源: From French *louche* "squinting, ambiguous, suspicious," perhaps from Latin *luscus* "one-eyed, blind in one eye".  The semantic shift towards "disreputable" likely happened in English.
