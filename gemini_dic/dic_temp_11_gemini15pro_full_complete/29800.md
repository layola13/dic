caudal:/ˈkɔːdl/| adj.|尾部的，尾状的|Of, relating to, or situated at or near the tail or posterior part of the body.|The caudal fin of the fish propelled it forward. 鱼的尾鳍推动它前进。|近义词：posterior, hind, rear|反义词：cranial, anterior, rostral|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin *caudālis*, from *cauda* ("tail").
