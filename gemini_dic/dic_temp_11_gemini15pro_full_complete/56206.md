dodgy:/ˈdɒdʒi/ (UK) /ˈdɑːdʒi/ (US)| adj.|狡猾的，躲闪的，不可靠的，危险的|Dishonest or unreliable; likely to cause problems; of low quality.|例句：The car looks a bit dodgy. 这辆车看起来有点不可靠。|近义词：suspicious, shady, unreliable, risky, questionable|反义词：reliable, trustworthy, safe, sound|词性:形容词
dodgy:|/ˈdɒdʒi/ (UK) /ˈdɑːdʒi/ (US)|adj.|逃避的，躲闪的|Avoiding something, especially in a dishonest way.|例句：He's been a bit dodgy about where he got the money. 他对于钱的来源有点躲躲闪闪。|近义词：evasive, shifty|反义词：forthright, honest|词性:形容词
