chinju: /ˈtʃɪndʒuː/
n.
镇守，守护神; 守护神(指动物、神或祖先)
A guardian spirit, often an animal, deity, or ancestor, believed to protect a place or people.
The chinju of the village was a white fox.  这个村庄的守护神是一只白狐。
The family venerated the snake as their chinju. 这个家族把蛇尊为他们的守护神。
Synonym: guardian, protector
Antonym: destroyer, assailant
词性: 名词
