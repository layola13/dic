margarone: /ˌmɑːrɡəˈroʊneɪ/ | n. | 玛格丽特酒（一种意大利干红葡萄酒） | A dry red Italian wine made from partially dried grapes, typically Valpolicella grapes.  It is known for its rich, intense flavor. |  I had a glass of  margarone with dinner last night. 昨晚我晚餐喝了一杯玛格丽特酒。 |  Example 2:  Margarone is one of Italy's most prestigious wines. 玛格丽特酒是意大利最负盛名的葡萄酒之一。 | 近义词: Amarone (often used interchangeably, though technically distinct), Recioto (a sweeter wine made in a similar style) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific wine type)  Perhaps a light-bodied red wine, like Pinot Noir. | 词性: noun
