missional:/ˈmɪʃənəl/| adj.|传教的，宣教的；肩负使命的|Characterized by or relating to a mission, especially a religious or charitable one; focused on actively spreading or promoting a particular belief or cause.|例句：The church adopted a more missional approach, focusing on outreach programs in the community. 教会采取了一种更具使命感的方式，专注于社区的外展项目。

例句：The organization has a missional focus, aiming to eradicate poverty in developing countries. 该组织以使命为中心，致力于消除发展中国家的贫困。|近义词：evangelistic, missionary, apostolic|反义词：secular, worldly|词性:形容词
