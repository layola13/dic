nonnegotiable:/ˌnɒn.nɪˈɡəʊ.ʃi.ə.bəl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn.nɪˈɡoʊ.ʃi.ə.bəl/
nonnegotiable: adjective
nonnegotiable: 不可协商的；不可谈判的
nonnegotiable: Not open to discussion or modification; unchangeable.
nonnegotiable: The price is nonnegotiable.  价格不可商量。
nonnegotiable:  Safety regulations are nonnegotiable. 安全规定不容商量。
nonnegotiable: 近义词: inflexible, unalterable, fixed, firm, rigid
nonnegotiable: 反义词: negotiable, flexible
nonnegotiable: 词性: adjective
