mobilizes:/ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzɪz/ | v. |动员，调动| To assemble and make ready for war or other emergencies; to bring resources into use. | The government mobilized the army for the impending war. 政府为即将到来的战争动员了军队。|  The charity mobilized volunteers to help with the relief effort.  慈善机构动员志愿者来帮助救灾工作。 |  activate, marshal, rally, deploy | demobilize, disband | verb
