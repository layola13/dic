outcatches:/ˌaʊtˈkætʃɪz/
verb
胜过；超过（尤指捕鱼或捕捉）
surpasses, especially in fishing or catching.
例句：He outcatches everyone on the lake. 他在湖上的捕鱼量超过了所有人。
例句：The experienced fisherman consistently outcatches the novices. 经验丰富的渔民总是比新手捕到更多鱼。
近义词：outfishes, surpasses, exceeds, betters
反义词：loses to, is surpassed by
词性：动词 (verb)

词源：
"out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "catch" (verb meaning "to seize or capture")
