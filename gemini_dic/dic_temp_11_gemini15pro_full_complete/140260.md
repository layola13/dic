offshore:/ˌɔːfˈʃɔːr/ |adj. adv.|近海的，离岸的；在近海|Situated at sea some distance from the shore. Also used as an adverb meaning away from or at a distance from the shore. |Many offshore islands are popular tourist destinations. 许多近海岛屿是热门的旅游目的地。  They sailed offshore to avoid the reefs.  他们驶向近海以避开礁石。|近义词：coastal, maritime|反义词：inland, onshore|词性: 形容词/副词


词源:  From "off" + "shore".  "Off" indicating a position away from and "shore" referring to the land bordering a body of water.
