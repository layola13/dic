what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)|pron. det. adv.|什么，多么，|Used to ask for information specifying something.|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词:which, |反义词: (无明确的反义词)|pronoun, determiner, adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living?  她以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? 你离开之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她转专业前一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们下次会议上会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你会完成什么？
What will she have learned by the time she finishes the course?  她完成课程时会学到什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做这件事十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she say if she knew the truth? 如果她知道真相，她会说什么？


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up?  怎么了？/你好吗？
What about going to the cinema? 去看电影怎么样？
What if it rains tomorrow? 如果明天下雨怎么办？
What for? 为了什么？
So what? 那又怎样？
