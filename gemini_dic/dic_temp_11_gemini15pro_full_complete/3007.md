aerates:/ˈeəreɪts/
verb
充气，透气
introduces air into (a material).
例句：The machine aerates the lawn.  这台机器给草坪充气。

例句：The gardener aerates the soil to improve drainage. 园丁给土壤透气以改善排水。

近义词：oxygenate, ventilate, air
反义词：suffocate, smother
词性：v.


aerates:/ˈeəreɪts/
noun
（葡萄酒）呼吸，氧化
The process of exposing wine to air.
例句：Allowing the wine to aerate improves its flavor. 让葡萄酒呼吸可以改善其风味。

例句：Decanting is a common method for aerating wine. 醒酒是葡萄酒氧化的一种常见方法。
近义词：oxidation, ventilation
反义词：reduction
词性：n.
