nonprosaicness:/ˌnɒnproʊˈzeɪɪknəs/
n.
非散文性；非平凡性；诗意；想象力
The quality of not being prosaic; imaginativeness or poetic spirit.
Her writing was marked by a distinct nonprosaicness, full of vivid imagery and metaphors. 她的写作以独特的非散文性为标志，充满了生动的意象和隐喻。

The nonprosaicness of the story captivated the readers.  这个故事的诗意吸引了读者。

近义词:imaginativeness, poeticness, creativity, originality, artistry
反义词:prosaicness, dullness, ordinariness, banality, commonplaceness
词性:noun

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + prosaic (adjective meaning "commonplace or dull") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
