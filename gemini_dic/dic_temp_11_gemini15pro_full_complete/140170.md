offensively:/əˈfen.sɪv.li/ | adv. | 攻击性地，冒犯地，令人不快地 | in a way that causes someone to feel resentful, upset, or annoyed |  He spoke offensively about her appearance. 他攻击性地谈论她的外貌。 | 近义词:  insultingly, rudely, aggressively | 反义词: politely, respectfully, inoffensively | 词性: 副词

offensively:/əˈfen.sɪv.li/ | adv. | 进攻地 | in a way that relates to attacking someone or something | The team played offensively throughout the game.  该队在整场比赛中都采取攻势。| 近义词: aggressively, attackingly | 反义词: defensively | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *offensif, from Old French offensif, from Late Latin offensivus (“offensive, aggressive”), from Latin offendere (“to strike against, offend”).
