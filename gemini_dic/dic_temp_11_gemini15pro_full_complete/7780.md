answer-helm:/ˈænsərˌhɛlm/| n.|答案之舵，解答之舵; 比喻提供指导或解决方案的人或物。|A metaphorical term referring to someone or something that provides guidance or solutions, like a helm steering a ship towards the correct answer. |The experienced consultant acted as the answer-helm for the struggling company.  经验丰富的顾问充当了这家苦苦挣扎的公司的答案之舵。|Synonyms: guide, mentor, advisor, solution, key, remedy|Antonyms: problem, obstacle, hindrance|Noun
