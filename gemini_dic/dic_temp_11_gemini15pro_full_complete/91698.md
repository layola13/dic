hyperconjugation:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɒndʒʊˈɡeɪʃn/ /ˌhaɪpərˌkɑːndʒʊˈɡeɪʃn/ | n. | 超共轭 | The delocalization of electrons from a sigma bond (usually C-H or C-C) into an adjacent empty or partially filled p-orbital or a pi-orbital.  This interaction stabilizes the system. | Hyperconjugation explains the stability of carbocations. 超共轭解释了碳正离子的稳定性。|  The unusual stability of the tert-butyl cation can be attributed to hyperconjugation.  叔丁基碳正离子的异常稳定性可以归因于超共轭。 | 近义词: resonance (in some contexts), no direct synonym | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From hyper- "over, beyond" + conjugation "joining together".
