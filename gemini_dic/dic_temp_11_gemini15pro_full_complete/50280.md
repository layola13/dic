depicted:/dɪˈpɪktɪd/
verb
被描绘的，被描述的
represented by a drawing, painting, or other art form.
The mural depicted scenes from the town's history.  壁画描绘了该镇历史上的场景。

The artist depicted the queen as a strong and wise leader.  这位艺术家将女王描绘成一位坚强而睿智的领袖。
Synonym: portrayed, represented, illustrated, pictured
Antonym: misrepresented, distorted
