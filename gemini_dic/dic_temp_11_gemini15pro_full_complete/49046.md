dehiscence:/diːˈhɪsəns/| n. |开裂，裂开|The bursting open of a pod, fruit, or anther, or of a surgical wound.  指豆荚、果实、花药或手术伤口的爆裂开。|例句：The surgeon was concerned about the dehiscence of the wound.  外科医生担心伤口的开裂。

例句：The dehiscence of the seed pod scattered the seeds far and wide. 种荚的开裂使种子四处散落。|近义词：splitting, bursting, rupture, opening|反义词：closure, sealing|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dehiscens, present participle of dehiscere, from de- "away from" + hiscere "to gape."
