bimana:/baɪˈmeɪnə/
n.
two-handed mammals; humans
Two-handed; having two hands.  Specifically refers to the order of mammals that includes humans, characterized by having two hands and two feet.
Humans are the only extant members of the bimana group.  人类是双足类动物中唯一现存的成员。
Humans are classified as bimana. 人类被归类为双足类动物。
近义词: hominid, human
反义词: quadruped
词性: noun
