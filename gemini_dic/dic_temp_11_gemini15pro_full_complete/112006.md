lonesome:/ˈloʊnsəm (US), ˈləʊnsəm (UK)| adj.|孤独的，寂寞的|Characterized by a feeling of loneliness or isolation; sad because one is alone.|例句：She felt lonesome after her children left home. 她孩子们离开家后，她感到很寂寞。

例句：The lonesome whistle of the train echoed through the valley. 火车的寂寞汽笛声在山谷中回荡。|近义词：lonely, solitary, isolated, alone|反义词：accompanied, sociable, gregarious|词性:形容词
