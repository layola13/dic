condensable:/kənˈdensəbl/ | adj. | 可冷凝的；可凝结的 | Able to be condensed; capable of being made more dense or compact, especially by cooling a gas or vapor to a liquid. |  The steam is condensable into water.  蒸汽可以冷凝成水。

The condensable vapors were removed from the system.  可冷凝的蒸汽从系统中移除。 | 近义词：liquefiable | 反义词：noncondensable, uncondensible | 词性: adjective

词源: From condense + -able.  来自 condense + -able.
