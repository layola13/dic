gurus:/ˈɡʊruːz/| n.|大师，专家，权威|A guru is a person who is followed as a leader or teacher, especially in religious matters.  A guru can also be someone who is an expert in a particular field.|The tech gurus predicted the rise of artificial intelligence.  这些科技大师预测了人工智能的兴起。|experts, masters, leaders|disciples, followers|noun
