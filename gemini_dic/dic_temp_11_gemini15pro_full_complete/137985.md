nonzoological:/ˌnɒnˌzuːəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非动物学的
Not pertaining to zoology; not connected with the study of animals.
The museum's nonzoological collection includes rocks, minerals, and fossils.  博物馆的非动物学藏品包括岩石、矿物和化石。
The researcher's nonzoological interests include botany and geology.  这位研究人员的非动物学兴趣包括植物学和地质学。
Synonyms: abiological, inanimate
Antonyms: zoological
