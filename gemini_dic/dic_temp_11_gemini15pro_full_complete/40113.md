contemningly:/kənˈtɛmnɪŋli/
adverb
轻蔑地，鄙夷地
in a way that shows you feel someone or something is worthless or deserving of contempt.
He looked at her contemningly. 他轻蔑地看了她一眼。

He spoke contemningly of his rivals. 他轻蔑地谈论他的对手们。
