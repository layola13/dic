andropetalous:/ˌændrəˈpɛtələs/
adjective
雄蕊着生于花瓣上的
Having stamens that are attached to the petals.
The andropetalous flower has a distinctive appearance.  雄蕊着生于花瓣上的花朵有着独特的外观。
The botanist identified the flower as andropetalous due to the stamen placement.  植物学家根据雄蕊的位置将这种花确定为雄蕊着生于花瓣上的。
Synonyms: epipetalous
Antonyms: None
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἀνδρό- (andró-)  "man," relating to the male reproductive parts of a flower (stamens), + πέταλον (petalon) "leaf, petal."
