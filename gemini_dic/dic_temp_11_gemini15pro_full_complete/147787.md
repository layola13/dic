overprosperous:/ˌoʊvərˈprɒspərəs/| adj.|过于繁荣的，过度繁荣的|Excessively prosperous; more prosperous than is desirable or healthy.|The overprosperous economy led to unsustainable growth and eventually a crash.|近义词：affluent, wealthy, booming|反义词：impoverished, poor, depressed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over, beyond") + "prosperous" (from Late Latin prosperus "fortunate, favorable," from pro- "forward" + spes "hope").
