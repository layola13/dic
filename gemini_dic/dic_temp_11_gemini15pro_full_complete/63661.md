epanadiplosis:/ˌɛpənədɪˈploʊsɪs/
n.
首尾重复（修辞手法）
Repetition of a word or phrase at the beginning and end of a clause or sentence.
"The king is dead, long live the king."  国王死了，国王万岁。

"Nothing can be created out of nothing."  无中不能生有。

Synonym:
repetition, epanalepsis

Antonym:
N/A (This is a rhetorical device; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
noun
