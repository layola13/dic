nonexempt:/ˌnɑːnɪɡˈzɛmpt/
adj.|非豁免的|Not exempt; specifically, (of an employee) covered by the Fair Labor Standards Act and therefore entitled to overtime pay.|例句：Nonexempt employees are entitled to overtime pay. 非豁免员工有权获得加班费。|近义词：non-exempt|反义词:exempt
词性：形容词
