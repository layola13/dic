enmuffle:/ɪnˈmʌfl/| v.|裹住，捂住，使声音低沉|To wrap or cover something so as to deaden sound, warmth, or light.|She enmuffled herself in a thick scarf. 她用一条厚厚的围巾把自己裹了起来。|近义词：muffle, wrap, cover, envelop|反义词:uncover, expose|动词

enmuffle:/ɪnˈmʌfl/| v.|使声音低沉|To make a sound quieter or less distinct.|The heavy curtains enmuffled the noise from the street. 厚厚的窗帘使街上的噪音减弱了。|近义词：mute, deaden, soften|反义词:amplify|动词
