divis:/ˈdɪvɪs/ | n. | 除数, 分母 | A divisor is a number that divides another number. | Example: In 10 / 2 = 5, 2 is the divisor. 在 10 / 2 = 5 中，2 是除数。 | Example: The divisor of 30 is 5, and the quotient is 6. 30 的除数是 5，商是 6。 | Synonym: factor | Antonym: dividend, quotient | Noun
