declare:/dɪˈkleər/| v. |宣布；声明；申报|To announce officially; to state firmly.|The government declared a state of emergency. 政府宣布进入紧急状态。|例句：He declared his love for her. 他向她表白了爱意。|近义词：announce, proclaim, state, affirm|反义词：deny, retract|词性：动词
declare:/dɪˈkleər/| v. |申报（应纳税的货物、收入等）|To report (something, especially dutiable goods or income) to the proper authorities.|I have nothing to declare. 我没有什么要申报的。|例句：She declared all her income to the tax authorities. 她向税务机关申报了她所有的收入。|近义词：report, register|反义词：conceal|词性：动词
