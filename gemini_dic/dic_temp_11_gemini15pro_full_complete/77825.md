generative:/ˈdʒɛnərətɪv/| adj.|生成的，生产的，有生产力的|Having the ability to produce or create something new.  Relating to or capable of production or reproduction.|例句：Generative AI is revolutionizing the creative industries. 生成式人工智能正在彻底改变创意产业。|例句：The generative power of the sun provides energy for all life on Earth. 太阳的生产力为地球上的所有生命提供能量。|近义词：productive, creative, fertile|反义词：unproductive, barren, infertile|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *generātīvus, from Latin generātus (past participle of generāre, "to beget, produce"), from genus ("birth, race, stock, kind") + -ātīvus, equivalent to -ative
