convincibility:/kənˌvɪnsɪˈbɪlɪti/ | n. |可信度，可说服性| The quality of being convincible; the extent to which something is believable or persuasive. | The convincibility of his argument was weak due to lack of evidence.  他的论点由于缺乏证据，可信度很低。|Synonyms: plausibility, credibility, believability | Antonyms: implausibility, incredibility, unbelievability | noun

convincibility:/kənˌvɪnsɪˈbɪlɪti/ | n. |可被说服性，易受影响性| The quality of being easily convinced or persuaded. |  Her convincibility made her a target for scammers. 她容易被说服，这使她成为骗子的目标。|Synonyms: gullibility, persuadability, susceptibility | Antonyms: skepticism, resistance, imperviousness | noun
