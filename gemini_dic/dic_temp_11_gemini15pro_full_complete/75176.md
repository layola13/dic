frize:/friːz/| n. | 横饰带，饰边；粗糙的毛织品 | A decorative band, typically made of sculpted or painted plasterwork, often used on the walls of a building. Also, a coarse woolen cloth. | The frize above the doorway depicted scenes from Roman mythology.  门口上方的横饰带描绘了罗马神话中的场景。| The old coat was made of a rough frize.  这件旧外套是用粗糙的毛织品做的。| synonyms: border, trim, band, fringe, decoration, coarse cloth | antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun
