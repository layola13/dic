autocat:/ˈɔːtəʊkæt/ | n. |自动催化剂; 自动催化剂 |A substance that increases the rate of a chemical reaction and is regenerated at the end of the reaction, thereby being able to catalyze further reactions. |The reaction was accelerated by an autocat. 这个反应是由一种自动催化剂加速的。|Example 2:  Autocats play a crucial role in many biological processes. 自动催化剂在许多生物过程中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonym: catalyst |Antonym: inhibitor |noun
