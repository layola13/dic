nonmischievousness:/ˌnɒnmɪsˈtʃiːvəsnəs/
n.
无恶意；不淘气
lack of mischievousness; the state of being not mischievous.
Her nonmischievousness was evident in her gentle demeanor. 她的无恶意从她温柔的举止中显而易见。
近义词: innocence, harmlessness, blamelessness
反义词: mischievousness, naughtiness, malice
词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + mischievous + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
"non-" (Latin origin) + "mischievous" (Old French origin) + "-ness" (Old English origin)
