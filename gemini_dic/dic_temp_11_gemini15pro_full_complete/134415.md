noninvincibility:/ˌnɒnɪnvɪnsɪˈbɪlɪti/ | n. | 非无敌性；非不可战胜性 | The state or quality of not being invincible; vulnerability.  |  His noninvincibility became apparent after his first defeat. 他的非无敌性在第一次失败后变得显而易见。 | Example:  The team's noninvincibility was exposed by their recent string of losses.  该队最近一连串的失利暴露了他们的非不可战胜性。 | Synonyms: vulnerability, susceptibility, fallibility | Antonyms: invincibility, invulnerability, impenetrability | noun
