nonmimetic:/ˌnɒnˌmɪˈmɛtɪk/ |adj.| 非模仿的；非拟态的 |Not imitative of natural sounds or forms; not representing external reality.  |The artist's work is deliberately nonmimetic, focusing on abstract shapes and colors rather than depicting recognizable objects. 这位艺术家的作品刻意采用非模仿的形式，专注于抽象的形状和颜色，而不是描绘可识别的物体。| 近义词: abstract, nonrepresentational | 反义词: mimetic, representational |词性:形容词
