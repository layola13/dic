disprover:/dɪsˈpruːvər/ | n. | 反驳者；证伪者；驳斥者 |  A person who disproves something, especially a theory or statement. |  The scientist presented evidence that acted as a disprover to the long-held theory.  这位科学家提供的证据驳斥了长期以来的理论。 |  refuter, contradictor, debunker | supporter, advocate | noun
