camwood:/ˈkæmwʊd/| n. |非洲红木，染料木|A red wood from the Baphia nitida tree, native to West Africa, used as a dyestuff and in traditional medicine.  It yields a red dye and is also valued for its timber.| The intricate carvings were made from camwood. 这些精美的雕刻是用非洲红木制成的。|近义词：barwood (another red dyewood)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From obsolete *cam, meaning "crooked, curved" (perhaps referring to the tree's shape or branches) + wood.
