mariculture:/ˈmærɪkʌltʃər/ (US), /ˈmærɪkʌltʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|海水养殖，海产养殖|The cultivation of marine organisms in their natural habitats or in controlled environments, usually for commercial purposes.|例句：Mariculture is becoming increasingly important as a source of seafood. 海水养殖作为海鲜来源正变得越来越重要。
