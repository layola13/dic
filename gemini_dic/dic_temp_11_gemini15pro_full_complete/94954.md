in respect that:/ɪn rɪˈspɛkt ðæt/ |conjunction|因为；鉴于|Used to introduce a reason or explanation for something. It means "because" or "considering that". |The plan was flawed in respect that it underestimated the cost.  该计划有缺陷，原因是它低估了成本。

The proposal was rejected in respect that it didn't meet the criteria.  该提案被拒绝，因为它不符合标准。|近义词：because, since, as, insofar as, inasmuch as|反义词: although, though, despite, in spite of|词性: conjunction
