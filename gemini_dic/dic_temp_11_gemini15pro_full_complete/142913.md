out-sallying:/ˌaʊtˈsæliɪŋ/
|n.|突围，出击|The out-sallying troops surprised the enemy.  突围的部队使敌人大吃一惊。|English Explanation: A sudden rushing or bursting forth, especially of troops from a besieged place to attack the besiegers.|例句：The out-sallying of the defenders broke the siege.  守军的突围打破了包围。|近义词：sortie, attack, charge, rush, eruption|反义词：retreat, withdrawal|词性:名词(gerund or verbal noun)

out-sally:/ˌaʊtˈsæli/
|v.|突围，出击|The besieged troops out-sallied and routed the enemy. 被围困的部队突围而出，击溃了敌人。|English Explanation: To rush or burst forth, especially of troops from a besieged place to attack the besiegers.|例句：The garrison out-sallied at dawn.  守军在黎明时分出击。|近义词：attack, charge, assault|反义词：retreat, withdraw|词性:动词
