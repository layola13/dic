azorian:/əˈzɔːriən/
adjective
亚速尔群岛的；亚速尔群岛人的
Relating to the Azores, a group of islands in the North Atlantic Ocean, or their inhabitants.
The Azorian archipelago is known for its volcanic landscapes. 亚速尔群岛以其火山景观而闻名。
The Azorian people are known for their seafaring traditions. 亚速尔群岛人民以其航海传统而闻名。
近义词: Portuguese (in a broader sense) 葡萄牙的（广义上）
反义词: None (specific to a geographical location) 无（特定于地理位置）
词性: adjective
