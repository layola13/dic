drith:/drɪθ/
n.
细雨，毛毛雨
A fine, light rain; drizzle.
The drith misted the windows of the car.  细雨模糊了车窗。
近义词: drizzle, mist, mizzle
反义词: downpour, deluge
词性: 名词


词源:
From Old English *dryþe, from the verb *drīþan, from Proto-Germanic *driupaną (“to drip”). Cognate with Old Norse dreyri (“blood, gore”), Old Saxon drīopan, Old High German triofan, German triefen (“to drip”), Gothic 滴流 (driupan).  Related to drip.
