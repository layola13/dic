mourned:/mɔːrnd/ |v.|哀悼，悲痛|To feel or express deep sorrow for someone who has died.|She mourned the loss of her beloved pet. 她哀悼她心爱宠物的离去。|The nation mourned the death of its leader. 全国人民都在哀悼他们领袖的逝世。|grieved, lamented|celebrated|verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *mournen*, from Old French *murner, morner*, from Vulgar Latin *murnare*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Latin *murmurare* ("to murmur, grumble").
