oligarch:/ˈɒlɪɡɑːk/ (UK), /ˈoʊlɪɡɑːrk/ (US)| n.|寡头，寡头政治执政者|A member of a small group of people who control a country or organization.|The Russian oligarchs amassed vast fortunes during the privatization of state assets in the 1990s.  俄罗斯寡头在20世纪90年代国有资产私有化期间积累了巨额财富。

The fall of the oligarch was swift and unexpected.  这位寡头的垮台迅速而意外。|近义词：magnate, tycoon, plutocrat|反义词: commoner, citizen|词性:noun
