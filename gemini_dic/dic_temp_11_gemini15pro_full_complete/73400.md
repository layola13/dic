foreignism:/ˈfɔːrənɪzəm/ (US) /ˈfɒrənɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |外来语；外国风俗习惯|A word or phrase borrowed from another language.  A custom or manner adopted from another country.|例句：The use of "rendezvous" is a foreignism in English.  “rendezvous”在英语中是一个外来语。|例句：Eating sushi with chopsticks is a foreignism that has become popular in many Western countries. 用筷子吃寿司是一种在许多西方国家流行起来的外来语。|近义词：loanword, borrowing|反义词:nativism (referring to language)|词性:noun
