cometarium: /ˌkɒmɪˈtɛːriəm/ (British) /ˌkɑːmɪˈtɛriəm/ (American)
noun
彗星馆；彗星仪
A building or device in which images of comets and other celestial bodies are projected onto a domed ceiling.
例句：The cometarium show was breathtaking, showcasing the beauty of the cosmos. 彗星馆的表演令人叹为观止，展现了宇宙之美。

例句：The school took a field trip to the local cometarium to learn more about astronomy. 学校组织了一次去当地彗星馆的实地考察，以了解更多关于天文学的知识。

近义词: planetarium
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: n.
