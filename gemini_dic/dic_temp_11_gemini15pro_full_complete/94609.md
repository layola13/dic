impregnably:/ɪmˈprɛɡnəbli/
adjective
坚不可摧地，牢不可破地
in a way that is impregnable; in a way that cannot be captured or broken into
The castle was impregnably fortified. 这座城堡被牢不可破地加固了。
Near synonyms: invulnerably, unassailably, securely, safely
Antonyms: vulnerably, weakly, insecurely
