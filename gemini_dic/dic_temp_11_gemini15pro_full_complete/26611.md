/kəˈkek.si.ə/ /kəˈkɛk.si.ə/
n.
恶病质，恶液质
A state of general ill-health and malnutrition, often associated with chronic diseases like cancer or AIDS, marked by progressive loss of weight, muscle atrophy, fatigue, weakness, and significant loss of appetite.
The patient exhibited signs of cachexia, including severe weight loss and muscle wasting.  病人表现出恶病质的迹象，包括严重的体重下降和肌肉萎缩。
Cachexia is a common complication of advanced cancer. 恶病质是晚期癌症的常见并发症。
近义词: wasting, emaciation, atrophy
反义词: robustness, health
词性: noun


/kəˈkek.si.ə/ /kəˈkɛk.si.ə/
词源:  From Greek  "kakos" (bad) + "hexis" (condition).
