一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is the meaning of being? = being的意义是什么？
Being kind is important. = 友善很重要。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is being difficult. = 他正在刁难。
She is being helpful. = 她正在帮忙。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has been being silly lately. = 他最近一直很傻。 (Note: This usage is somewhat controversial and often considered awkward.  "He has been acting silly lately." is often preferred.)
She has been being a good friend to me. = 她一直是我的好朋友。 (Again, this is awkward. "She has been a good friend to me." is better.)

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He has been being difficult all day. = 他一整天都在刁难。(Awkward.  "He has been acting difficult all day." is preferred.)
She has been being kind to the animals. = 她一直对动物很友善。(Awkward. "She has been kind to the animals" is better.)

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He was being rude. = 他当时很粗鲁。
She was being considerate. = 她当时很体贴。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
While I was talking, he was being disruptive. = 我说话的时候，他一直在捣乱。
She was being helpful when I needed it most. = 当我最需要帮助的时候，她一直在帮忙。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had been being difficult before he finally agreed. = 在他最终同意之前，他一直很刁难。(Awkward. "He had been difficult before he finally agreed" is preferred.)
She had been being kind to him even though he was rude. = 即使他很粗鲁，她以前也一直对他很友善。(Awkward. "She had been kind to him even though he was rude." is better.)


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He had been being difficult for weeks before they fired him. = 在他们解雇他之前，他已经连续几周都在刁难。(Awkward.  "He had been difficult for weeks before they fired him." is preferred.)
She had been being helpful all morning, so I bought her lunch. = 她整个上午都在帮忙，所以我给她买了午餐。(Awkward. "She had been helpful all morning, so I bought her lunch." is better.)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will be being interviewed tomorrow. = 他明天将接受采访。
She will be being assessed on her performance. = 她将根据她的表现进行评估。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time tomorrow, he will be being questioned by the police. = 明天这个时候，他将接受警方的讯问。
This time next week, she will be being treated for her illness. = 下周这个时候，她将接受疾病治疗。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, he will have been being a teacher for five years. = 到明年，他将已经当了五年老师。
By the end of the month, she will have been being treated for six months. = 到月底，她将已经接受了六个月的治疗。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By the time he retires, he will have been being a doctor for 40 years. = 到他退休时，他将已经当了40年的医生。
By the time she graduates, she will have been being a student for 16 years. = 到她毕业时，她将已经做了16年的学生。

注意事项:
"being" 后面常接形容词，构成进行时态的被动语态，或表示状态。

The forms with "been being" (present perfect continuous, past perfect continuous, future perfect continuous) are generally considered awkward and are often replaced by simpler constructions.  They are included here for completeness, but their use should be approached with caution.
