courteously: /ˈkɜːrtiəsli/ (US) /ˈkɜːtiəsli/ (UK)| adv.| 礼貌地，客气地 | In a polite and respectful manner. Showing good manners and consideration for others. | She courteously offered her seat to the elderly woman. 她礼貌地把座位让给了那位老妇人。| politely, respectfully, graciously | rudely, impolitely, disrespectfully | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *curteisly*, from Old French *corteisie* (courtesy), from *corteis* (courteous), from Vulgar Latin *cortis* (court), from Latin *cohors* (cohort, enclosure, company).
