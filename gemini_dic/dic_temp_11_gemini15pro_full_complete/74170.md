fountains:/ˈfaʊntənz/ | n. | 喷泉 |  A structure that sprays water into the air, often as a decorative feature in a park or garden. | The fountains in the park were lit up at night. 公园里的喷泉在晚上被点亮了。 | synonyms: springs, jets, sprays | antonyms: drains, sinks | noun

fountains:/ˈfaʊntənz/ | n. | 源泉；来源 | A source of something. | The encyclopedia is a fountain of knowledge. 这本百科全书是知识的源泉。 | synonyms: source, origin, wellspring | antonyms: end, conclusion | noun
