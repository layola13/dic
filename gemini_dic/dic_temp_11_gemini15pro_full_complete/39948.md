constructions:/kənˈstrʌkʃənz/
n.
建筑物，结构；建造；解释；句法结构
The city is undergoing major constructions.  这个城市正在进行大规模建设。
Etymology: From Latin constructus, past participle of construere "to heap up, build," from com- "together" + struere "to pile, build."
The bridge is a marvel of engineering and construction. 这座桥是工程和建筑的奇迹。
Synonym: buildings, structures, edifices, erections
Antonym: demolitions, destructions


constructions:/kənˈstrʌkʃənz/
n.
解释，理解
Her constructions of the events were biased. 她对事件的解释是有偏见的。
Etymology: From Latin constructus, past participle of construere "to heap up, build," from com- "together" + struere "to pile, build."  The metaphorical meaning of "interpretation, understanding" developed later.
Different people have different constructions of reality. 不同的人对现实有不同的理解。
Synonym: interpretations, understandings, readings, perspectives
Antonym: misinterpretations, misunderstandings


constructions:/kənˈstrʌkʃənz/
n.
(语法)句法结构
The grammar book explained various sentence constructions. 这本语法书解释了各种句子结构。
Etymology: From Latin constructus, past participle of construere "to heap up, build," from com- "together" + struere "to pile, build." This meaning refers to the "building" of sentences.
Complex grammatical constructions can be difficult to understand. 复杂的语法结构可能难以理解。
Synonym: syntax, formations, arrangements, formulations
Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for this sense)
