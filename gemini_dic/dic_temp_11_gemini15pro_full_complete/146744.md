overhumane:/ˌoʊvərhjuːˈmeɪn/| adj.| 过于人道的，过分仁慈的| Excessively humane; exhibiting or characterized by an exaggerated or misplaced sense of compassion, often to the detriment of practical considerations.  It implies a level of compassion that is impractical or even harmful.|  He was so overhumane that he refused to kill the mosquito, even though it was spreading disease. 他过于仁慈，即使蚊子在传播疾病，他也拒绝杀死它。| 近义词：oversensitive, overscrupulous, sentimental, compassionate| 反义词：cruel, inhumane, ruthless| 词性: 形容词

overhumane:/ˌoʊvərhjuːˈmeɪn/| adj.| 过于人道的，过分仁慈的|  His overhumane treatment of the prisoners allowed them to plan their escape.  他对囚犯过于人道的待遇使他们得以计划逃跑。| 近义词：excessively compassionate, overly sympathetic| 反义词：callous, hard-hearted| 词性: 形容词
