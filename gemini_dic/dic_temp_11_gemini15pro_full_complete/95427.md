incinerate:/ɪnˈsɪnəreɪt/ | v. |焚化，烧毁|To burn something completely to ashes.|The city incinerates its trash to reduce landfill waste.  这座城市焚烧垃圾以减少垃圾填埋场的浪费。|synonyms: combust,  immolate|antonyms: extinguish, conserve|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *incineratus*, past participle of *incinerare* ("to reduce to ashes"), from *in-* ("in, on") + *cinis*, *cineris* ("ashes").
