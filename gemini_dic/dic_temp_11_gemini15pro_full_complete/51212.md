destituteness:/ˈdɛstɪtjuːtnəs/| n. |赤贫；穷困；缺乏|The state of being destitute; extreme poverty and lack of resources.|The family's destituteness was apparent in their threadbare clothing and empty cupboards.  这个家庭的贫困状况从他们破旧的衣服和空空的橱柜里显而易见。|近义词：poverty, indigence, penury, impoverishment|反义词：wealth, affluence, prosperity|词性:名词

destitute:/ˈdɛstɪtjuːt/| adj. |贫困的；赤贫的；缺乏的|Without the basic necessities of life; lacking something needed or desired.|The earthquake left many people destitute. 地震使许多人一贫如洗。|近义词：impoverished, indigent, needy, penniless|反义词：wealthy, affluent, prosperous|词性:形容词

词源:  From Latin *destitutus*, past participle of *destituere* "to abandon, forsake," from *de* "down, away" + *statuere* "to set, place."
