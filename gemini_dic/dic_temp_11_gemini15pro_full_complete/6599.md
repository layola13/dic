anachronism:/əˈnækrənɪzəm/
n.
时代错误，年代误植，不合时代的人或物
Something that is mistakenly placed in a time where it does not belong in a story, movie, etc., or something that belongs to the past and does not fit in the present.
The movie is full of anachronisms, like characters using cell phones in ancient Rome.  这部电影充满了时代错误，比如古罗马时期的人物使用手机。
The sword is an anachronism in modern warfare.  剑在现代战争中是一种不合时宜的武器。
Synonyms: misplacement, misdating, solecism
Antonyms: contemporaneity, modernity
