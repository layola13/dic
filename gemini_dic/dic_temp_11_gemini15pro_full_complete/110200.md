life-enhancing:/ˈlaɪfɪnˌhɑːnsɪŋ/
adjective
改善生活的，提高生活质量的
Making life better; improving the quality of life.
例句：Regular exercise can be a life-enhancing experience.  规律的运动可以改善生活质量。

例句：Volunteering at the animal shelter was a life-enhancing decision. 在动物收容所做志愿者是一个提升生活质量的决定。

近义词：beneficial, enriching, rewarding
反义词：detrimental, harmful, damaging
词性: adjective


life-enhancing: /ˈlaɪfɪnˌhɑːnsɪŋ/
词源:  "life" (n.) + "enhancing" (adj., present participle of "enhance" (v.))
"life" from Old English "līf"
"enhance" from Old French "enhaucer" meaning "to raise," from "en-" (in) + "haucer" (to raise).
