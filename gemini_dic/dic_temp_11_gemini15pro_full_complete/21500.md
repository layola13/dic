bludgeoners:/ˈblʌdʒənərz/| n.| 施暴者，用棍棒打的人|People who use a bludgeon (a short, heavy club) to attack others.  Those who metaphorically force or coerce others into something, often using their power or influence.|The bludgeoners attacked the peaceful protesters.|近义词：thugs, assailants, aggressors, bullies|反义词: protectors, defenders|noun

bludgeoner:/ˈblʌdʒənər/| n.| 施暴者，用棍棒打的人|A person who uses a bludgeon (a short, heavy club) to attack another. Also used metaphorically to describe someone who uses their power or influence to force or coerce others.|The dictator acted like a bludgeoner, silencing any opposition.|近义词：attacker, assailant, aggressor|反义词: protector, defender|noun
