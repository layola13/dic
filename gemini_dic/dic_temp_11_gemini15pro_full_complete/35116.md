cloudburst:/ˈklaʊdbɜːrst/| n. |倾盆大雨，暴雨|A sudden, very heavy rainfall.|The cloudburst flooded the streets. 暴雨淹没了街道。|Synonym: downpour, deluge|Antonym: drizzle, sprinkle|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From cloud + burst, literally a bursting of a cloud.  First recorded in the early 19th century.
