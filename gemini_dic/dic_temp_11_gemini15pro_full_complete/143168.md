outclass:/ˌaʊtˈklæs/ | vt. | 超越，胜过 | to be much better than someone or something |  The new sports car easily outclassed all the others on the track. 这辆新的跑车在赛道上轻松超越了所有其他车辆。

Our team was completely outclassed by the opposition. 我们的队伍完全被对手超越了。 | 近义词：surpass, excel, outperform, eclipse | 反义词：be inferior to, be outdone by | 词性：动词
