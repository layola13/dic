analogon:/əˈnæləɡɒn/ | n. |类似物；相似体；对应物 |Something that is similar or comparable to something else. | This chemical reaction is an analogon of the biological process. 这个化学反应是生物过程的类似物。 |  The butterfly's wings are an analogon to the airplane's. 蝴蝶的翅膀和飞机的机翼是类似的。 |  analogue, counterpart, parallel | opposite, antonym | noun

analogon:/əˈnæləɡɑːn/ | n. |类似物；相似体；对应物 |Something that is similar or comparable to something else. |  This program is the software analogon of a physical calculator. 这个程序是实体计算器的软件对应物。 |  The human heart can be seen as an analogon to a pump. 人的心脏可以看作是一个泵的类似物。 |  equivalent, parallel, correlate |  contrast, difference | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Analogon*, from Ancient Greek *ἀνᾰ́λογος (análogos, “proportionate, analogous”)*, from *ἀνά (aná, “up, upon, throughout, according to, back, again”)* + *λόγος (lógos, “ratio, word, speech, reckoning”)*.
