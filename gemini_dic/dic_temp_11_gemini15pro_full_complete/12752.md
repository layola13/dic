assiege:/əˈsiːdʒ/
verb
围攻，包围
to surround a place, such as a city, with an army in order to capture it
例句：The army besieged the city for months.  军队围攻了这座城市数月。

例句：The castle was besieged by enemy forces. 城堡被敌军围困。

近义词：beset, beleaguer, surround, blockade
反义词：liberate, free, relieve

词性：v.
