dispraisingly:/dɪsˈpreɪzɪŋli/
adv.
贬低地，轻蔑地
in a manner that expresses disapproval or criticism.
He spoke dispraisingly of his opponent's record. 他贬低地谈论对手的记录。
近义词:critically, disapprovingly, scornfully, contemptuously
反义词:praisingly, approvingly, admiringly

词性: 副词 (adverb)

词源:  From "dispraise" + "-ing" + "-ly".  "Dispraise" comes from the Old French "despreisier," meaning "to undervalue," ultimately from Latin "dis-" (not) + "pretiare" (to value).
