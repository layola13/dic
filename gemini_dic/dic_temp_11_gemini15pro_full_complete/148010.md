overrigorousness:/ˌōvərˈrɪɡərəsnəs/ | n. | 过于严谨，过分严格 | The state of being excessively rigorous; extreme strictness or preciseness. | His overrigorousness in applying the rules alienated his staff.  他过分严格地执行规定，疏远了他的员工。 |  近义词：strictness, rigidity, inflexibility | 反义词：leniency, laxity, flexibility | 词性: noun

Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "rigorous" (from Latin rigor "stiffness, hardness," from rigere "to be stiff") + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), noun suffix).
