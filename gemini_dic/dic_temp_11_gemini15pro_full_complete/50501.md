deprecated:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
已弃用的，不赞成的
No longer recommended or supported; discouraged from further use because of being outdated or superseded.
This function is deprecated and will be removed in the next version.  这个函数已被弃用，并将在下一个版本中移除。
The use of this chemical is now deprecated due to its harmful effects. 由于其有害影响，现在不建议使用这种化学品。
近义词: obsolete, outmoded, superseded, discouraged
反义词: supported, recommended, current, modern
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *deprecatus*, past participle of *deprecari* ("to pray against, avert by prayer"), from *de-* ("down, away") + *precari* ("to pray").  The sense shift from "avert by prayer" to "express disapproval of" occurred in Late Latin.
