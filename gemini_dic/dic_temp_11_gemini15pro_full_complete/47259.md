ddp:/ˌdiː diː ˈpiː/ |abbr.| Delivered Duty Paid (完税后交货)|Delivered Duty Paid is an international trade term meaning the seller delivers the goods when the goods are placed at the disposal of the buyer, cleared for import on the arriving means of transport ready for unloading at the named place of destination. The seller bears all the costs and risks involved in bringing the goods to the place of destination and has an obligation to clear the goods
