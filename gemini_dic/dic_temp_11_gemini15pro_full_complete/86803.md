hematemesis: /ˌhiːməˈtemɪsɪs/ (US), /ˌhiːməˈtemɪsɪs/ (UK)
n.|呕血|The vomiting of blood.|The patient presented with hematemesis and abdominal pain. 病人出现呕血和腹痛。|
近义词: vomiting blood, blood vomiting
反义词:  None (Hematemesis describes a specific symptom; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: noun
词源: From Greek haima (blood) + emetos (vomiting).
