conformist:/kənˈfɔːrmɪst/ | n. adj. | 墨守成规者；循规蹈矩的人；顺从者 | A person who conforms to accepted behavior or established practices, especially in a way that shows a lack of originality or independent thinking. |  He's such a conformist – he always follows the crowd. 他真是个墨守成规的人——总是随波逐流。 | synonyms: follower, traditionalist, conventionalist, stick-in-the-mud | antonyms: rebel, nonconformist, maverick, individualist | noun

conformist:/kənˈfɔːrmɪst/ | adj. | 墨守成规的；循规蹈矩的；顺从的 | Behaving or thinking like a conformist. |  She has a very conformist attitude. 她的态度非常墨守成规。 | synonyms: conventional, traditional, orthodox, unoriginal | antonyms: rebellious, unconventional, nonconformist, original | adjective
