cursed:/ˈkɜːsɪd/ (US) /ˈkɜːst/ (UK)| adj.|被诅咒的；可恨的；讨厌的|Affected by a curse; damnable; extremely annoying.|The cursed treasure brought misfortune to all who sought it. 被诅咒的宝藏给所有寻找它的人都带来了不幸。|Synonyms: damned, doomed, hexed, jinxed, accursed|Antonyms: blessed, fortunate, lucky|Adjective

cursed:/ˈkɜːst/| v. (past tense and past participle of curse)|诅咒|Past tense and past participle of the verb "to curse".  Indicates the act of invoking evil or misfortune upon someone or something.|He cursed his bad luck. 他诅咒他的厄运。|Synonyms: damned, execrated|Antonyms: blessed, praised|Verb
