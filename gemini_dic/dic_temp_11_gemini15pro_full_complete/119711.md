ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *middeschipp, from Old English middscip ('midship'), from midd ('mid') + scip ('ship').  Cognate with Middle Dutch middelscip, Middle Low German middelschipp, Old Norse miðskip.

PHRASE->
amidships: in or into the middle of a ship. 在船的中间
midship section: the central part of a ship. 船的中心部分
midship house: a superstructure located amidships.  位于船舶中部的上层建筑
midship beam: the width of a vessel at its widest point, usually amidships. 船舶最宽处的宽度，通常位于船中部
midship frames: the transverse structural members of a ship's hull, located amidships. 船体横向结构构件，位于船中部
