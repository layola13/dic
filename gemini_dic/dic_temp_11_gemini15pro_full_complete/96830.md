inestimabilities:/ɪnˌɛstɪməˈbɪlətiz/| n.|无法估量的事物；无价之宝|The inestimabilities of love and friendship cannot be measured in monetary terms. 爱情和友谊的无价之宝无法用金钱来衡量。|近义词：pricelessness, invaluable things, immeasurable qualities|反义词:worthlessness, valueless things|词性:noun

inestimability:/ɪnˌɛstɪməˈbɪləti/| n.|无法估量；无价|The inestimability of her contribution to the project was acknowledged by everyone. 她对这个项目的不可估量的贡献得到了大家的认可。|近义词：pricelessness, invaluableness, immeasurability|反义词:worthlessness, valuelessness|词性:noun

词源: From in- (not) + estimable (capable of being estimated) + -ity (noun suffix).  Ultimately from Latin aestimare (to estimate).
