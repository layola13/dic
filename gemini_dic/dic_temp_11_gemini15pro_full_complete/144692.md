oval:/ˈoʊvəl/| adj. n.|椭圆形的；椭圆形|Having a rounded and slightly elongated shape, like that of an egg. |The mirror had an oval frame. 这面镜子的框架是椭圆形的。|The race track is oval. 这个赛道是椭圆形的。|近义词：elliptical, oblong, ovate|反义词:round, circular|adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *ovale*, from Medieval Latin *ōvālis*, from Latin *ōvum* (egg).
