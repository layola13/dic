clued-in:/ˌkluːdˈɪn/ | adj. | 知情的，了解内情的 | Well-informed and aware of what is happening, especially about something secret or private. |  Example 1: She's very clued-in about the latest fashion trends. 她对最新的时尚潮流非常了解。

Example 2: He's clued-in to all the office gossip. 他对办公室里所有的流言蜚语都了如指掌。| 近义词: informed, knowledgeable, aware, in the know, up to speed | 反义词: uninformed, unaware, out of the loop, ignorant, oblivious | 词性: 形容词
