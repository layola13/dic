overeating:/ˌōvərˈītiNG/ | n. |暴饮暴食; 吃得过多|The act of consuming more food than the body requires.  It can lead to weight gain and various health problems. | He regretted his overeating during the holidays. 他后悔假期里暴饮暴食了。|  Overeating can lead to obesity. 暴饮暴食会导致肥胖。|近义词：gluttony, binge eating, excessive eating|反义词: undereating, dieting|词性:名词

词源: From over- + eating,  "over" indicating excess.
