dusked:/dʌskt/
verb
变得昏暗，被暮色笼罩
(of daylight) fade into darkness.  The sky dusked.
The sky dusked as the sun began to set.  天空随着太阳开始落山而逐渐变暗。
The fields were dusked by the approaching storm. 即将到来的暴风雨使田野变得昏暗。
近义词: darkened, dimmed, twilight
反义词: brightened, lightened
词性: verb

dusked:/dʌskt/
adjective
昏暗的，暮色笼罩的
made dusky; darkened.  The dusked room was filled with shadows.
The dusked landscape was hauntingly beautiful. 昏暗的景色有一种令人难忘的美。
The dusked mountains loomed in the distance. 昏暗的山脉隐隐约约地出现在远处。
近义词: dark, dim, shadowy
反义词: bright, light, sunny
词性: adjective
