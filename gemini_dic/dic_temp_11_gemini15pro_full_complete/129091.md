nitrosylic:/ˌnaɪtrəˈsɪlɪk/ | adj. |亚硝酰的，含亚硝酰基的 | Relating to or containing the nitrosyl group (NO).  Nitrosylic compounds contain the NO group bonded to a metal. |  Nitrosylic sulfuric acid is an important intermediate in the lead chamber process. 亚硝酰硫酸是铅室法制硫酸的重要中间体。| 近义词: nitrosyl | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, potentially consider compounds *without* the nitrosyl group) | 词性: adjective

词源:  From nitrosyl + -ic.  Nitrosyl itself comes from nitro- (from nitre, ultimately from Greek nitron, "natron") + -syl (from Greek hylē, "wood, matter").  Therefore, it refers to the "matter" or "radical" associated with niter (potassium nitrate).
