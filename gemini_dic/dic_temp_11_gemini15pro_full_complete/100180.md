interreigning:/ˌɪntəˈreɪnɪŋ/
adjective
中文翻译：两朝国王统治之间的；摄政的；暂时统治的
英文解析：Occurring during an interregnum; of or relating to an interregnum.  Relating to the period between the reign of one monarch and the next.  Serving as a regent.
例句：The interreigning period was marked by political instability. 两朝国王统治之间的时期以政治不稳定为特征。

例句：The interreigning council struggled to maintain order. 摄政委员会努力维持秩序。

近义词：transitional, interim, provisional
反义词：permanent, reigning

词性: adjective
