acotyledon:/ˌeɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ | n. | 无子叶植物 |A plant whose embryo does not have cotyledons; one of the Acotyledones.  A plant that does not produce cotyledons in its seeds. |Example 1: Ferns and mosses are examples of acotyledons. 蕨类植物和苔藓是无子叶植物的例子。

Example 2:  The acotyledon reproduces through spores rather than seeds. 无子叶植物通过孢子而不是种子繁殖。| 近义词: cryptogam, spore-bearing plant | 反义词: cotyledonous plant,  flowering plant | 词性: noun
