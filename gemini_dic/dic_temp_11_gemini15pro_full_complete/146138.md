overemulate: /ˌoʊvərˈɛmjʊleɪt/
verb
过度模仿，过分效仿
To emulate someone or something excessively, often to the point of absurdity or ineffectiveness.
He overemulated his idol, copying even his flaws and mannerisms. 他过分模仿他的偶像，甚至连他的缺点和怪癖都照搬。
The young artist overemulated the old masters, losing his own unique style in the process.  这位年轻的艺术家过度模仿古代大师，在这个过程中失去了自己的独特风格。

近义词: ape, mimic, parrot, copy, imitate
反义词: originate, innovate, create

词性: v.

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "emulate" (from Latin "aemulari" meaning "to rival, to strive to equal").
