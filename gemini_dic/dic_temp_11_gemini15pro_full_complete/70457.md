figurehead:/ˈfɪɡjərˌhed/| n.| 名词 |傀儡；有名无实的领导人；船首像|A figurehead is a person who appears to be the leader of an organization but who has no real power.  A figurehead can also refer to a carved wooden figure on the bow of a ship. |The king was merely a figurehead; the real power lay with the military. 国王只是一个傀儡；真正的权力掌握在军队手中。

The ship's figurehead was a mermaid. 这艘船的船首像是美人鱼。|近义词：puppet, nominal leader, front, ornament|反义词：leader, authority, power|词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From figure (referring to the shape or form) + head. Originally referred to the carved figurehead on the prow of a ship.  The metaphorical meaning of a powerless leader developed later.  来自figure（指形状或形式）+ head。最初指的是船头上雕刻的船首像。后来发展出无权领导人的比喻意义。
