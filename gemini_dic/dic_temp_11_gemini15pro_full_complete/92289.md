hyphenation:/ˌhaɪfəˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 断字；连字符连接 |The hyphenation of this word is incorrect. 这个单词的断字是错误的。| The hyphenation rules in English can be quite complex.英语中的断字规则可能相当复杂。 |  syllabification, word division | N/A | noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *hyphenationem,  from hyphenatus, past participle of hyphenare (“to connect by a hyphen”), from Ancient Greek ὑφέν (huphén, “under one”),  from ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + ἕν (hén, “one”).
