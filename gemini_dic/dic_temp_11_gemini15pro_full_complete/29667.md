catharsis:/kəˈθɑːrsɪs/
n.
情感宣泄，精神净化
the process of releasing, and thereby providing relief from, strong or repressed emotions.
She went running as a form of catharsis. 她去跑步作为一种情感宣泄的方式。
近义词:release, purgation, cleansing
反义词:repression, suppression
词性:名词

catharsis:/kəˈθɑːrsɪs/
n.
通便，泻药
(Medicine) purgation or purification of the body by the elimination of morbid matter
The doctor prescribed a catharsis to clear out his system. 医生给他开了泻药来清理他的肠胃。
近义词:purgative, laxative
反义词:constipation
词性:名词
