exclusivism: /ɪkˈskluːsɪvɪzəm/ /ɪkˈskluːsɪvɪzəm/
n.|排他主义，排外主义|The belief that one's own group or belief system is superior to others, often leading to the exclusion or rejection of those outside the group.|His religious exclusivism prevented him from accepting other viewpoints.  他的宗教排他主义使他无法接受其他观点。|近义词: intolerance, bigotry, sectarianism|反义词: inclusiveness, tolerance, acceptance|词性: 名词
exclusivism:|/ɪkˈskluːsɪvɪzəm/ /ɪkˈskluːsɪvɪzəm/
