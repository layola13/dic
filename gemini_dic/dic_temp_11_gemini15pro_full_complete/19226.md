bezonian:/bɪˈzoʊniən/
noun
乞丐，流氓，无赖
A base, needy, and despicable person; a beggar; a rogue.
Example: "Away, you three-inch fool!"  "I am no three-inch fool, but a good five-inch beggar; I defy thee to thy face: thou bezonian! a base, proud, shallow, beggarly, three-suited, hundred-pound, filthy, worsted-stocking knave." (Shakespeare, 2 Henry IV, V. iii)  “滚开，你这三寸丁！” “我不是三寸丁，而是个堂堂五寸的乞丐；我当面蔑视你：你这无赖！卑鄙、傲慢、浅薄、乞丐般的、穿着三件套、一百磅重、肮脏、穿着毛线袜的恶棍。”
Synonym: beggar, vagabond, rogue, rascal, scoundrel
Antonym: gentleman, nobleman, aristocrat
词性: 名词
