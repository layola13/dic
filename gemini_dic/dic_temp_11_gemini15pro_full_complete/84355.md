halfway:/ˌhɑːfˈweɪ/ | adv. adj.|中途的，半途的|At or to a point equidistant between two others.|We met halfway between our two houses. 我们在我们两家之间的中间点相遇。|Near the middle point; approximately half.|The project is about halfway complete.  项目大约完成了一半。|synonyms: midway, partially, incompletely|antonyms: completely, fully, entirely|adverb, adjective

halfway:/ˌhɑːfˈweɪ/ | n.|中途，半路|The point equidistant between two others.|We stopped at the halfway mark for a break. 我们在半路上停下来休息。|synonyms: midpoint, halfway point|antonyms: beginning, end, starting point|noun
