amphitheatrical:/ˌæmfɪθiˈætrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
圆形的，圆形剧场式的
Resembling or characteristic of an amphitheater; having the form of an amphitheater, especially a semi-circular or oval shape with rising tiers of seats.
The amphitheatrical valley provided a natural setting for the concert.  圆形剧场式的山谷为音乐会提供了天然的场地。
The stadium has an amphitheatrical design. 这个体育场采用了圆形剧场式设计。
近义词:circular, rounded, oval, arena-like
反义词:linear, straight
