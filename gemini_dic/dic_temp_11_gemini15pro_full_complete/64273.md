equisetaceous:/ˌɛkwɪˈseɪʃəs/ | adj. | 木贼科的；像木贼的 | Resembling or related to the horsetails (genus *Equisetum*). | The fossil revealed an equisetaceous plant from the Carboniferous period. 这块化石揭示了一种来自石炭纪的木贼科植物。|  The plant had a distinctly equisetaceous appearance.  这种植物具有明显的木贼科植物的外观。| synonyms: horsetail-like | antonyms: (No direct antonym, could use terms like "unlike horsetails," "dissimilar to Equisetum") | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *equisētum* (“horsetail”), from *equus* (“horse”) + *saeta*, *seta* (“bristle”).  So literally "horse bristle".
