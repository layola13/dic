disexercise:/dɪsˈɛksərsaɪz/
verb
停止锻炼，缺乏锻炼
To cease exercising; to be out of practice.
After his injury, he disexercised for several months and lost muscle tone.  受伤之后，他停止锻炼好几个月，肌肉都萎缩了。
He disexercised his writing skills during his years abroad. 在国外的那几年，他的写作技巧荒废了。
近义词: neglect, abandon, discontinue
反义词: exercise, train, practice
词性: verb
