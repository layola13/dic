britania:/brɪˈtæniə/ | n. |不列颠，英国(诗歌用语) | An ancient name, poetic or rhetorical, for Great Britain. | Britannia rules the waves. 不列颠统治着波涛。| 近义词: Britain, Great Britain | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Latin *Britannia*, from Ancient Greek Βρεττανία (Brettanía), the name of the British Isles and their inhabitants. Ultimately from Proto-Celtic *Pritanī*, plural of *Pritanos* (“Briton”).
