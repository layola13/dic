centaurian:/sɛnˈtɔːriən/ | n. adj. | 半人马座人；半人马的 | A being from the constellation Centaurus, typically depicted as having the upper body of a human and the lower body of a horse.  Also used as an adjective to describe things related to Centaurus. |  例句：The centaurian warrior charged into battle. 半人马战士冲锋陷阵。| 近义词：centaur | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun, adjective

centaurian:/sɛnˈtɔːriən/ | adj. | 半人马座的；半人马的 | Relating to or resembling a centaur. | 例句：The centaurian statue stood proudly in the museum. 半人马雕像骄傲地矗立在博物馆里。| 近义词：centaurine | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective
