notchier:/ˈnɑːtʃiər/ (US), /ˈnɒtʃɪər/ (UK)| adj.|更有缺口的，更显著的|Having more notches or indentations; more pronounced or noticeable.  More advanced or impressive.|例句：The new design is notchier than the previous one. 新的设计比以前的设计更有特色。

例句：His latest album is a notchier effort than his debut.  他最新的专辑比他的首张专辑更上一层楼。|近义词：better, superior, enhanced, improved, more advanced, more sophisticated|反义词：worse, inferior, simpler, less developed|词性:形容词

词源:
From notch (n.) + -y (adj. suffix) + -er (comparative suffix).  "Notch" itself comes from Old French "oche" meaning a notch or groove.
