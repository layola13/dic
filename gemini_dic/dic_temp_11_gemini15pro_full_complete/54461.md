disintegrate:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪt/ /dɪsˈɪntəˌɡreɪt/
verb
瓦解，碎裂，崩溃
break into small pieces, fragments or particles; decay, decompose, lose cohesion or unity.
The missile disintegrated on impact.  导弹撞击时瓦解了。
The old papers had disintegrated into dust.  旧文件已经碎成了灰尘。
近义词: crumble, decompose, break down, fall apart, fragment
反义词: integrate, unite, combine, solidify

disintegrated:/dɪsˈɪntɪˌɡreɪtɪd/ /dɪsˈɪntəˌɡreɪtɪd/
adjective
瓦解的，碎裂的，崩溃的
having broken into small pieces, fragments or particles; decayed, decomposed, lost cohesion or unity.
The disintegrated statue lay in pieces on the ground.  破碎的雕像散落在地上。
The once powerful empire was now a disintegrated collection of warring states.  曾经强大的帝国如今已瓦解成一群交战的国家。
近义词: fragmented, broken, crumbled, decayed
反义词: intact, whole, united, solid


词性: verb, adjective


词源:  From Latin disintegratus, past participle of disintegrare ("to separate from the whole"), from dis- ("not, apart") + integrare ("to make whole"), from integer ("whole, untouched").
