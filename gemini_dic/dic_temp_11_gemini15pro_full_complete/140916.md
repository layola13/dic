oncologist:/ɑːŋˈkɑːlədʒɪst/ /ɒŋˈkɒlədʒɪst/
n.
肿瘤学家
A medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of cancer.
The oncologist recommended chemotherapy as the best course of treatment.  肿瘤学家建议化疗是最佳的治疗方案。

近义词:cancer specialist, cancer doctor
反义词:None
词性:Noun
