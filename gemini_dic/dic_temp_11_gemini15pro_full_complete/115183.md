manicheanism:/ˌmænɪˈkiːɪzəm/
|n.|摩尼教，二元论，善恶二元论|A dualistic philosophy dividing the world into good and evil principles, often with the implication of a cosmic struggle between them.  It often refers to a tendency to see things in terms of stark opposites, with no room for nuance or complexity.|His political philosophy is a crude form of Manicheanism, seeing the world purely in terms of "us" versus "them." 他将世界纯粹地视为“我们”与“他们”的对立，其政治哲学是一种粗糙的二元论形式。|The critic dismissed the film's simplistic Manicheanism, arguing that real life is rarely so black and white. 评论家驳斥了这部电影简单化的二元论，认为现实生活很少是非黑即白的。|dualism, bipolarity|pluralism, holism|noun
