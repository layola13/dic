inelligibly:/ɪnˈɛlɪdʒɪbli/
adjective
难以辨认地，模糊不清地
in a way that cannot be read or clearly understood.
Her handwriting was so messy it was almost inelligibly. 她的字迹潦草，几乎难以辨认。
近义词：unreadibly, illegibly, indecipherably
反义词：legibly, clearly, distinctly
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From in- "not" + legible + -ly.  The root "legible" comes from the Latin "legibilis" meaning "that can be read," from "legere" meaning "to read."
