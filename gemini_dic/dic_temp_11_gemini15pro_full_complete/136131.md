nonreflective:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈflektɪv/
adjective
非反射性的；不反射的
Not reflecting light or other radiation.
The stealth bomber has a nonreflective coating to make it harder to detect by radar.  隐形轰炸机有一种非反射涂层，使雷达更难探测到它。
The black surface was nonreflective and absorbed most of the light.  黑色的表面是非反射性的，吸收了大部分的光。
non-reflecting, non-reflective, matte, dull
reflective, shiny, glossy
adjective
