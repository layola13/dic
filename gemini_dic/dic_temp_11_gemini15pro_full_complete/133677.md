nonfraternities:/ˌnɒnfrəˈtɜːrnɪtiz/
n.
非兄弟会组织；独立学生组织
Groups of male students at a college or university that are not part of a fraternity, often choosing to live together or participate in shared activities.
Many nonfraternities provide social and academic support networks for their members. 许多非兄弟会组织为其成员提供社交和学术支持网络。
Synonyms: independent student organizations, student groups, societies, clubs
Antonyms: fraternities
词性: 名词
