nehushtan:/nɪˈhʌʃtən/ |n.|铜蛇; 无价值之物|A thing that is worshipped but has no value or power; an idol.  It comes from the biblical story of the bronze serpent made by Moses, which later became an object of idolatry.|The old ways had become a nehushtan, hindering true progress.|Synonym: idol, fetish, false god|Antonym:  sacred object, deity|noun
