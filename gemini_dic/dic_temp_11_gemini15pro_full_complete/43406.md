crankshaft: /ˈkræŋkʃæft/ (US), /ˈkræŋkʃɑːft/ (UK)| n. |曲轴|A shaft with one or more cranks, converting reciprocating motion to rotary motion and vice versa, especially in an internal combustion engine.|The crankshaft converts the up-and-down motion of the pistons into rotational motion. 曲轴将活塞的上下运动转换为旋转运动。

The car's crankshaft broke, requiring a costly repair. 汽车的曲轴坏了，需要昂贵的修理。|近义词: (Functional equivalent in some contexts) camshaft |反义词: None|词性: noun
