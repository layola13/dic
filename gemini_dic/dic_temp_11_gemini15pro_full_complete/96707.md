ineffectively:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktɪvli/| adv.|无效地，无用地|Without producing any significant or desired effect.  Failing to produce the desired result.|例句：The team worked ineffectively, failing to meet the deadline.  团队工作效率低下，未能按期完成任务。|例句：He argued ineffectively, failing to persuade the judge. 他辩护无效，未能说服法官。|近义词：fruitlessly, inefficiently, unsuccessfully, uselessly|反义词：effectively, efficiently, successfully|词性:副词
