non-discursive:/ˌnɒn dɪˈskɜːsɪv/ | adj. | 非论证性的，非阐述性的 | Not proceeding by reasoned argument or argumentation; not discursive.  Characterized by the absence of discourse or argument. Often used to describe art forms that convey meaning through direct experience or intuition rather than through logical reasoning or narrative. |  Example: Abstract art is often considered non-discursive, relying on visual elements rather than a narrative. 抽象艺术通常被认为是非论证性的，它依赖于视觉元素而不是叙事。

Example:  Non-discursive communication, like body language, can be just as powerful as words.  非论证性交流，比如肢体语言，可以和语言一样有力。| 近义词: intuitive, impressionistic, evocative, implicit, suggestive | 反义词: discursive, argumentative, logical, rational, explicit | 词性: adjective
