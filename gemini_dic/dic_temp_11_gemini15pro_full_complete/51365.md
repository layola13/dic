deteriorated: /dɪˈtɪəriəreɪtɪd/
verb, adjective
恶化，变坏; 已恶化的
Having become progressively worse.
例句：
His health deteriorated rapidly.
他的健康状况迅速恶化。

The building has deteriorated over the years.
这些年来这栋建筑已经破败了。

近义词: worsened, declined, degraded, decayed
反义词: improved, enhanced, ameliorated
词性:  Past participle of deteriorate
