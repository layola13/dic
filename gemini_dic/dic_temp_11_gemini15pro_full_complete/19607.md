bignoniaceous:/ˌbɪɡnoʊniˈeɪʃəs/ | adj. | 紫葳科的 | Belonging to or resembling the Bignoniaceae family of plants, characterized by often showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. | The jacaranda is a beautiful bignoniaceous tree. 蓝花楹是一种美丽的紫葳科树木。| The garden features a variety of bignoniaceous vines.  花园里种植了各种紫葳科藤本植物。 | 近义词: (No single direct synonym; use descriptive phrases like "trumpet-vine like," "belonging to the trumpet creeper family") | 反义词:  (No antonym) | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Bignoniaceus*, from *Bignonia* (the genus name), named after Jean-Paul Bignon (1662-1743), librarian to Louis XIV of France, + *-aceus* (suffix forming adjectives meaning "belonging to" or "resembling").
