graphologically:/ˌɡræfəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb
笔迹学地
in a way that relates to the study of handwriting, especially for the purpose of analyzing character and personality.
His personality was analyzed graphologically. 他的性格是通过笔迹分析的。
近义词:handwriting-wise,  through handwriting analysis
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider phrasing like "without graphological analysis")
词性:adverb
