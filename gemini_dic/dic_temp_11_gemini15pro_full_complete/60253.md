effusions:/ɪˈfjuːʒənz/
n.
流露；泻出；渗出物；（感情的）迸发
an instance of giving off something such as a liquid, light, or smell. An unrestrained expression of feeling.
例句：The poet's effusions of love were rather excessive. 这位诗人对爱情的表达有些过分。

例句：There was an effusion of blood from the wound.  伤口流了很多血。

近义词：outpouring, outburst, discharge, emission, emanation,  gush, overflow,  expression
反义词：suppression, concealment, repression
词性: 名词 (plural)


effusion:/ɪˈfjuːʒən/
n.
流露；泻出；渗出物；（感情的）迸发
An instance of giving off something such as a liquid, light, or smell. An unrestrained expression of feeling.
例句：His recent effusions of anger have alienated his friends. 他最近的愤怒爆发疏远了他的朋友们。

例句：The effusion of light from the rising sun was breathtaking.  冉冉升起的太阳发出的光芒令人叹为观止。

近义词：outpouring, outburst, discharge, emission, emanation,  gush, overflow, expression
反义词：suppression, concealment, repression
词性: 名词 (singular)


词源: Late Middle English: from Latin effusio(n-), from effundere ‘pour out’.
