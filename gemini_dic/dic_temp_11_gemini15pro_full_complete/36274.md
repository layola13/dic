cohabit:/koʊˈhæbɪt/ /kəʊˈhæbɪt/| v. |同居，共同居住|To live together in the same dwelling, especially as partners in a sexual relationship (without being married).|例句：They decided to cohabit before getting married. 他们决定在结婚前同居。

例句：Many unmarried couples cohabit these days. 如今很多未婚夫妇同居。

近义词：live together, cohabitate, shack up|反义词:live apart, separate|词性：动词

cohabiter:/koʊˈhæbɪtər/ /kəʊˈhæbɪtər/| n. |同居者|A person who lives with another, especially as partners in a sexual relationship (without being married).|例句：The landlord wasn't sure if both cohabiters signed the lease. 房东不确定两位同居者是否都签了租约。

例句：The cohabiters decided to buy a house together.  同居者决定一起买房子。


近义词：housemate, roommate, partner, live-in partner|反义词: N/A|词性：名词
