farcin:/ˈfɑːrsɪn/| n.| 鼻疽；马鼻疽|A contagious and often fatal bacterial disease of horses and other equine species, characterized by ulcerating nodules on the skin and mucous membranes, especially of the nose and limbs.  It is caused by *Burkholderia mallei*.|The farcin spread rapidly through the stable, affecting several horses. 鼻疽在马厩中迅速蔓延，感染了几匹马。|Synonyms: glanders (more common term), equine glanders|Antonyms: health, wellness|noun

farcin:/ˈfɑːrsɪn/| adj.| 鼻疽的|Relating to or affected with farcin.|The farcin horse was quarantined. 这匹患鼻疽的马被隔离了。|Synonyms: glandered|Antonyms: healthy, well|adjective
