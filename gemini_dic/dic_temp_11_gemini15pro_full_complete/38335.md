conceivabilities:/kənˌsiːvəˈbɪlətiz/
n.|可设想的事物；可能性；|The conceivabilities of future technology are endless. 未来科技的可能性是无限的。|近义词：possibilities, potentialities|反义词:impossibilities
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
英文解析: things that can be imagined or thought of as possible

例句：
She considered all the conceivabilities before making a decision.
她在做决定之前考虑了所有可能性。

The report explored the various conceivabilities for the future of the company.
该报告探讨了公司未来的各种可能性。



词源:  From conceivable + -ity (suffix forming abstract nouns) + -ies (plural suffix)  Conceivable itself comes from conceive (from Latin concipere 'to take in, conceive') + -able (suffix forming adjectives).
