一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? = 他整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What did she do yesterday? = 她昨天做了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before dinner? = 晚饭前她吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been studying before you changed your major? = 你转专业前一直在学什么？
What had she been doing before she retired? = 她退休前一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will she study in college? = 她大学会学什么专业？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be working on next month? = 她下个月会做什么工作？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你会取得什么成就？
What will she have finished by the end of the year? = 到年底她会完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years? =  过去十年你一直在做什么？
What will she have been studying for by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学了什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she say if she were here? = 如果她在这里，她会说什么？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是哪里？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. = 她不明白你的意思。
