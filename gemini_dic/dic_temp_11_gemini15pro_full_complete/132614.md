nondirigibles:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɪdʒɪbəlz/ | n. | 非硬式飞艇 |Airships or blimps, which are lighter-than-air aircraft that rely on internal pressure to maintain their shape, unlike rigid airships (Zeppelins).| The Navy used nondirigibles for coastal patrols during World War II.  海军在二战期间使用非硬式飞艇进行海岸巡逻。| Synonyms: blimps, airships | Antonyms: Zeppelins, rigid airships | noun

nondirigible:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɪdʒɪbəl/ | adj. | 不可驾驶的，无法控制的 |Not capable of being steered or controlled. This meaning is less common than the noun form referring to blimps.| The damaged aircraft became nondirigible and crashed.  受损的飞机变得无法控制并坠毁了。| Synonyms: uncontrollable, unsteerable | Antonyms: controllable, steerable | adjective
