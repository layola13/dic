| adv. | 离去；离开；外出 | aways is a less common, chiefly dialectal form of away, meaning "to or at a distance." | He's aways on business trips. 他总是出差在外。| synonyms: away, off, absent | antonyms: here, present | adverb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He always walks to school. 他总是步行上学。
She always forgets her keys. 她总是忘记带钥匙。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is always complaining. 他总是在抱怨。
She is always asking questions. 她总是在问问题。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has always been kind to me. 他一直对我很友善。
She has always loved music. 她一直热爱音乐。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He has always been working hard. 他一直努力工作。
She has always been striving for excellence. 她一直力求卓越。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He always walked to school. 他过去总是步行上学。
She always forgot her keys. 她过去总是忘记钥匙。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He was always complaining about the food. 他过去总是抱怨食物。
She was always asking for help. 她过去总是寻求帮助。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had always been a good student. 他一直是个好学生。
She had always wanted to travel the world. 她一直想环游世界。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He had always been dreaming of becoming a doctor. 他一直梦想着成为一名医生。
She had always been practicing the piano. 她一直练习钢琴。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will always remember this day. 他将永远记住这一天。
She will always be there for you. 她将永远在你身边。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
He will always be looking for new opportunities. 他将永远在寻找新的机会。
She will always be learning new things. 她将永远在学习新事物。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, he will always have lived in this city. 到明年，他将一直住在这个城市。  (This example is slightly awkward and might be better rephrased, as "always" with future perfect can be redundant.)
By next year, she will have always loved him. 到明年，她将一直爱着他。(Similar redundancy issue)


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By the time he retires, he will have always been working in this field. 到他退休的时候，他将一直在这个领域工作。 (Similar redundancy issue)
By the time she graduates, she will have always been studying hard. 到她毕业的时候，她将一直努力学习。(Similar redundancy issue)



虚拟语气:
If I always knew the answer, I would be a genius. 如果我一直知道答案，我就是天才了。
I wish I always had enough money. 我希望我总是有足够的钱。

被动语态:
The door is always left open. 门总是开着。
The rules are always being broken. 规则总是被打破。

疑问句:
Is she always late? 她总是迟到吗？
Are you always this busy? 你总是这么忙吗？

否定句:
He doesn't always tell the truth. 他并不总是说实话。
She isn't always happy. 她并不总是快乐。
