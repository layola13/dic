jaconet:/ˈdʒækənɪt/ | n. | 厚斜纹棉布; 一种厚而结实的棉布 | A strong, twilled cotton fabric. |  This jacket is made of durable jaconet. 这件夹克是用耐用的厚斜纹棉布制成的。 | The jaconet curtains filtered the sunlight beautifully. 厚斜纹棉布窗帘漂亮地过滤了阳光。 | 近义词: twill, cotton cloth, durable fabric | 反义词: delicate fabric, thin material | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "jaconet," ultimately from Jagannath (Puri), India, where the fabric was originally made.  The word is derived from the name of the Hindu deity Jagannath (Lord of the Universe), whose temple is located in Puri.
