nonprovocative: /ˌnɒnprəˈvɒkətɪv/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnprəˈvɑːkətɪv/ (US)
adjective
不具挑衅性的；不激怒人的；低调的
Not tending or intended to provoke, irritate, or cause resentment.
The government adopted a nonprovocative stance in the dispute. 政府在争端中采取了不具挑衅性的姿态。
The diplomat used nonprovocative language to avoid escalating the tension.  外交官使用不具挑衅性的语言来避免紧张局势升级。

近义词:  inoffensive, unobjectionable, neutral, diplomatic, conciliatory
反义词: provocative, inflammatory, aggressive, hostile, belligerent
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + provocative (from Latin provocare "to call forth, challenge")
