necklacing:/ˈnɛklɪsɪŋ/
n.
私刑处死，用轮胎焚烧
The extrajudicial killing of a suspected criminal by placing a burning tire around their neck.
Necklacing was a common practice during the apartheid era in South Africa.  在南非种族隔离时期，用轮胎焚烧是一种常见的做法。
execution, lynching, murder

用项链装饰
