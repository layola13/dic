invaluably:/ɪnˈvæljuəbli/
adverb
极有价值地，非常宝贵地
in a way that is extremely useful or valuable.
Her advice proved invaluable to me. 她的建议对我来说非常宝贵。
Synonyms: pricelessly, inestimably, preciously
Antonyms: worthlessly, uselessly
词性: 副词
