incidents:/ˈɪnsɪdənts/| n. |插曲|A usually minor event that occurs in the course of a larger event. |There were a few unpleasant incidents during the trip. 旅行中发生了几起不愉快的插曲。|Synonyms: episode, event, occurrence|Antonyms: peace, calm|noun
