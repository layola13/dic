greatcoat: /ˈɡreɪtkoʊt/ (US) /ˈɡreɪtkəʊt/ (UK)| n. |大衣；厚外套|A heavy overcoat, typically double-breasted and reaching to the knees or below.|He wore a thick greatcoat to protect himself from the biting wind. 他穿了一件厚厚的大衣来抵御刺骨的寒风。|Synonyms: overcoat, topcoat, ulster|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, consider light jacket, summer clothes)|noun
