aeneas:/ɪˈniːəs/| n. |埃涅阿斯|A Trojan hero, the son of Anchises and Venus, and the legendary ancestor of the Romans. He is the protagonist of Virgil's epic poem, the Aeneid, which recounts his journey from Troy to Italy after the Trojan War. |例句：Aeneas, after the fall of Troy, wandered for many years before founding a new city. 特洛伊沦陷后，埃涅阿斯漂泊多年，最终建立了一座新城。

例句：The Aeneid tells the story of Aeneas's travels and his eventual settlement in Italy.  《埃涅阿斯纪》讲述了埃涅阿斯旅行的故事以及他最终在意大利定居的故事。|近义词：hero, protagonist, warrior|反义词:villain, antagonist|词性:noun
