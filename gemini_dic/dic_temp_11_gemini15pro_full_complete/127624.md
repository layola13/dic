needlepoint:/ˈniːdlˌpɔɪnt/| n.| 刺绣,针尖,绣花|A form of counted-thread embroidery in which yarn is stitched through canvas or other stiff material. It also refers to the finished product of this craft.|She spent hours working on a complex needlepoint design. 她花了几个小时在一个复杂的针绣图案上。|Synonyms: embroidery, tapestry, cross-stitch|Antonyms: None|noun

needlepoint:/ˈniːdlˌpɔɪnt/| adj.| 尖的|Having a sharp point like a needle.|The needlepoint pain in his back was unbearable. 他背上的针刺般的疼痛难以忍受。|Synonyms: sharp, pointed, piercing|Antonyms: blunt, dull|adjective
