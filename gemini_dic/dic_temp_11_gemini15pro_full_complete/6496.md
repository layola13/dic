amuck:/əˈmʌk/| adv.|狂暴地，疯狂地|in a frenzied or uncontrolled state, especially characterized by violent behavior.  Originally from the Malay word "amuk".|The man ran amuck, attacking anyone in his path.  那个男人狂暴地奔跑，攻击他路上的每一个人。|近义词：berserk, frenzied, wild, violently, uncontrollably|反义词:calmly, peacefully, rationally|词性:副词
