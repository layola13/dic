ETYMOLOGY->
From diagram + -ed.  "Diagram" comes from  Greek *διάγραμμα (diágramma) meaning "something drawn out," from  *διαγράφειν (diagráphein), meaning "to mark out by lines, delineate," from *διά (diá), "through, across" + *γράφειν (gráphein), "to write, draw."
