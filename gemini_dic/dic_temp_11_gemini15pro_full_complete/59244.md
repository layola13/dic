e-cigarette:/iːˈsɪɡərɛt/ | n. | 电子烟 | An electronic device that simulates smoking by heating a liquid to produce an aerosol that is inhaled. |  Example 1: He switched from regular cigarettes to e-cigarettes. 他从普通香烟换成了电子烟。

Example 2: The use of e-cigarettes is becoming increasingly popular. 电子烟的使用正变得越来越流行。 | synonym: vape, vaporizer, electronic nicotine delivery system (ENDS) | antonym: traditional cigarette, tobacco cigarette | noun
