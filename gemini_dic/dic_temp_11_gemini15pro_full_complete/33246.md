chronometric:/ˌkrɒnəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 计时器的，精密计时的 | Relating to the measurement of time, especially in a precise way. | Example: Chronometric tests were used to assess the athletes' reaction times.  精密计时测试被用来评估运动员的反应时间。| Example: The chronometric study revealed subtle differences in cognitive processing speed.  计时研究揭示了认知处理速度的细微差异。| Synonym: chronographical, timing | Antonym: inaccurate, imprecise | Word Type: Adjective
