bloodbird:/ˈblʌdbɜːrd/| n. | 吸血雀，嗜血鸟 |A small finch (genus Geospiza) of the Galapagos Islands that occasionally drinks the blood of other birds, especially boobies.| The bloodbird supplements its diet with blood during periods of drought. 吸血雀在干旱时期会以血液补充其饮食。 | synonym: vampire finch, sharp-beaked ground finch | antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider herbivorous bird) | noun
