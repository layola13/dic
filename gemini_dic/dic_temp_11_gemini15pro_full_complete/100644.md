intolerantnesses:/ɪnˈtɒlərənsɪz/ /ɪnˈtɑːlərənsɪz/
|n.|不耐受，不宽容；过敏|The state of being unable to tolerate something, especially a particular substance (like a food or drug), or behaviour.|She has several food intolerances, including gluten and lactose. 她对几种食物不耐受，包括麸质和乳糖。|近义词: sensitivities, allergies|反义词: tolerances, acceptance|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From intolerant + -ness.  Intolerant itself comes from in- (not) + tolerant (able to endure). Tolerant ultimately derives from the Latin tolerare (to endure).
