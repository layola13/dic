boondoggling:/ˈbuːndɑːɡlɪŋ/
n.
闲混；浪费时间；做琐碎无聊的工作
Engaging in trivial or useless work, often to appear busy.
例句：The committee was accused of boondoggling and wasting taxpayers' money.  该委员会被指控闲混，浪费纳税人的钱。

例句：He spent the afternoon boondoggling instead of finishing the report. 他一下午都在闲混，而不是完成报告。
近义词：loafing, idling, dawdling, time-wasting
反义词：working, producing, achieving
词性：名词
