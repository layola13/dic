Example 2: The madrigalist performed a beautiful piece by Gesualdo.  这位牧歌演唱家演唱了一首由杰苏阿尔多创作的优美作品。|近义词：composer, singer, musician|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-musician) |词性:noun
