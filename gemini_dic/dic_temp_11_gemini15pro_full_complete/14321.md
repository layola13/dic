|近义词：mechanization, robotization, computerization|

|词性：名词|

automations:/ˌɔːtəˈmeɪʃənz/
|n.|自动化操作|The automatically controlled operation of an apparatus, process, or system by mechanical or electronic devices that take the place of human labor.|The automations in the new software saved us hours of work. 新软件中的自动化操作为我们节省了数小时的工作时间。|
|近义词：automated processes, automated systems|
|反义词：manual processes, manual systems|
|词性：名词|
