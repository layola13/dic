legume:/ˈlɛɡjuːm/ /ˈledʒuːm/| n.|豆科植物；豆类；豆荚|A plant in the pea family, or the seed of such a plant, used as food.  A legume is a simple dry fruit that develops from a single carpel and usually dehisces (opens along a seam) on two sides.|例句：Lentils, peas, and beans are all legumes.  扁豆、豌豆和豆类都是豆科植物。

例句：Legumes are a good source of protein. 豆类是蛋白质的良好来源。|近义词：pulse, bean|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
