froebelian:/froʊˈbiːliən/
adjective
弗雷贝尔式的；幼儿园式的
Relating to the educational theories of Friedrich Froebel, emphasizing self-activity and play in early childhood education.
The school uses a Froebelian approach to learning. 这所学校采用弗雷贝尔式的学习方法。
The kindergarten's curriculum is based on Froebelian principles. 这家幼儿园的课程是基于弗雷贝尔原则的。
近义词:play-based, child-centered, progressive
反义词:traditional, didactic, rote
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Friedrich Froebel (1782-1852), a German educator who founded the kindergarten system + -ian.
