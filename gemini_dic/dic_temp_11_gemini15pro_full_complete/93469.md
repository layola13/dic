illusorily:/ɪˈlusərɪli/ | adv. | 幻觉地，虚幻地 |  In a way that appears real but is not; deceptively. |  He clung illusorily to the hope that she would return. 他虚幻地 clinging 住她会回来的希望。| 近义词: deceptively, falsely, misleadingly | 反义词: genuinely, truly, really | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *illusorius (“deceptive”), from Latin illudere (“to mock, play with”).
