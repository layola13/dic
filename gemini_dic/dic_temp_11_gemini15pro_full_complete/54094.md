disembodied:/dɪsɪmˈbɑːdɪd/ | adj. | 脱离肉体的；无实体的；空洞的 | Separated from or existing without the body. |  The disembodied voice on the phone was unsettling. 电话里那个没有实体的声音令人不安。 |  The story featured a disembodied spirit seeking its lost love.  这个故事讲述了一个寻找失去的爱的游魂。 | 近义词: incorporeal, ethereal, spectral | 反义词: embodied, corporeal, physical | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + embody + -ed.  来自dis-（表示否定、反转）+ embody + -ed。
