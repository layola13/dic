mister:/ˈmɪstər/| n.|先生；mister|A polite or respectful form of address for a man.|例句：Good morning, Mister Smith. 早上好，史密斯先生。|例句：Excuse me, Mister, can you tell me the time?  打扰一下，先生，您能告诉我时间吗？|近义词：sir, gentleman|反义词:miss, mrs, ms (These are not true antonyms but rather contrasting forms of address.)|noun
