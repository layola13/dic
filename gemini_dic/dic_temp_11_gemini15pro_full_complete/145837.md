overdeliciously:/ˌoʊvərdiˈlɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 过于美味地；极其美味地 | Excessively deliciously; to a degree of deliciousness that is extreme.  This word emphasizes the extreme deliciousness of something. | The cake was overdeliciously rich and moist. 这蛋糕极其美味，浓郁又湿润。|  The chef prepared an overdeliciously seasoned steak.  厨师准备了一块调味极其美味的牛排。 | 近义词: incredibly deliciously, exquisitely deliciously, exceptionally deliciously | 反义词: blandly, unappetizingly | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->

"over-" (prefix): From Old English "ofer," meaning "above," "across," "beyond," "excessively."

"-delicious-" (adjective): From Old French "delicieus," from Latin "deliciosus," meaning "delightful," "pleasant to the senses," from "deliciae" (plural noun), meaning "delight," "pleasure," "charm."

"-ly" (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives.
