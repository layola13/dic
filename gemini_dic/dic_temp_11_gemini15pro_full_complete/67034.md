explanati:/ˌɛkspləˈneɪʃən/ |n.|解释，说明|A statement that makes something clear.|The teacher gave a detailed explanation of the theory. 老师对这个理论做了详细的解释。|近义词：clarification, elucidation, interpretation|反义词：mystification, confusion|词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *explanatio*, from Latin explanare "to make level, explain," from ex- "out" + planare "to make plain," from planus "level, flat."
