butlerage:/ˈbʌtlərɪdʒ/
noun
酒窖管理费，酒钱
A charge for storing wine, especially in a tavern or inn.
In the old days, inns would charge butlerage for storing and serving guests' wine.  在过去，旅店会收取客人的葡萄酒储存和服务的酒窖管理费。
The butlerage fees significantly increased the cost of their stay.  酒窖管理费大大增加了他们住宿的费用。
Synonyms: corkage, cellarage
Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym)
