labyrinthodon:/ˌlæbɪˈrɪnθədɒn/ (US) /ˌlæbəˈrɪnθədɑːn/| n. |迷齿螈|An extinct amphibian of the Paleozoic Era, characterized by complex tooth structure and sometimes plated skin.  They were among the first vertebrates to walk on land.|The museum displayed a fossil of a labyrinthodon.  博物馆展出了一块迷齿螈的化石。|Synonyms:  stegocephalian (more technical)|Antonyms:  N/A (extinct group, no direct opposite)|noun


labyrinthodont:/ˌlæbɪˈrɪnθədɒnt/ (US) /ˌlæbəˈrɪnθədɑːnt/| adj. |迷齿螈类的|Relating to or characteristic of a labyrinthodon.|The labyrinthodont teeth are a key feature for identification. 迷齿型的牙齿是识别的关键特征。|Synonyms: stegocephalian|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
