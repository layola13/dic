enigmatist:/ˌɛnɪɡˈmætɪst/ | n. |谜语家，出谜人|A person who makes or poses riddles; a person who is puzzling or enigmatic.|The enigmatist challenged the audience to solve his complex riddle. 谜语家挑战观众解开他复杂的谜语。|Synonyms: riddler, puzzle-maker, sphinx |Antonyms: solver, explainer|noun

enigmatist:/ˌɛnɪɡˈmætɪst/ | n. |神秘人物，高深莫测的人|A person who is difficult to understand or interpret; a mysterious person.|His enigmatic behavior made him an enigmatist in the eyes of his colleagues. 他神秘的行为使他在同事眼中成了一个谜一样的人物。|Synonyms: mystery, puzzle, conundrum |Antonyms: open book, transparent person|noun
