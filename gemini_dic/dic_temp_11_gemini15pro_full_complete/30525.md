cephalata:/ˌsɛfəˈleɪtə/ | n. | 头足纲动物 |Cephalata is a taxonomic group within the phylum Mollusca, encompassing mollusks characterized by a distinct head and often well-developed eyes and tentacles, such as squids, octopuses, and cuttlefish.  It comes from the Greek "kephalē" meaning "head."| Cephalopods, members of the Cephalata class, are fascinating creatures. 头足纲动物，即头足纲的成员，是迷人的生物。| The giant squid is a member of the Cephalata group.  大王乌贼是头足纲动物的一员。| synonyms: Cephalopoda | antonyms: None | noun
