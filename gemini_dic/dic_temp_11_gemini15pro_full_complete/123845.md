moralistically:/ˌmɔːrəˈlɪstɪkli/
adverb| 道德主义地，道学味十足地|In a manner concerned with morals, often in a self-righteous or preachy way.  以一种关注道德的方式，通常以自以为是或说教的方式。|He spoke moralistically about the dangers of drinking. 他道貌岸然地谈论酗酒的危害。|Synonyms: sanctimoniously, preachily, pietistically, self-righteously|Antonyms: pragmatically, realistically, amorally|词性: 副词
