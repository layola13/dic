anti-radiation:/ˌæntiˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən/| adj.|防辐射的|Used to describe something designed to protect against or reduce the harmful effects of radiation.| This phone case claims to have anti-radiation properties.  这款手机壳声称具有防辐射特性。|Synonyms: radiation-proof, radiation-resistant|Antonyms: radioactive|adjective

anti-radiation:/ˌæntiˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən/| n.|防辐射措施；防辐射材料|Measures or materials that protect against or reduce the harmful effects of radiation.|  The company specializes in anti-radiation shielding for medical equipment. 这家公司专门为医疗设备提供防辐射屏蔽。|Synonyms: radiation protection, radiation shielding|Antonyms: radiation source|noun
