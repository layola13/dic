mcl: /ɛm siː ɛl/ | abbreviation | 最大团块长度（maximum cluster length）| Maximum Cluster Length, a term often used in computer science, especially in file systems, referring to the largest contiguous block of disk space that can be allocated to a file. |  The MCL of the file system is limited to 64KB. 文件系统的最大团块长度限制为 64KB。| synonyms: cluster size, block size | antonyms: N/A | noun
