jurisprudence: /ˌdʒʊərɪsˈpruːdəns/ | n. | 法学；法理学；法律体系；审判权 |  The philosophy or science of law.  It deals with the principles on which law is based and the way it is applied. |  He studied jurisprudence at Harvard Law School. 他在哈佛法学院学习法学。| synonyms: law, legal theory, legal philosophy | antonyms: lawlessness, anarchy | noun
