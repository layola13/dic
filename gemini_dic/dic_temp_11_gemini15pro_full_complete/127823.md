nematocera:/ˌnɛməˈtɒsərə/ (US) /ˌnɛməˈtɒsərə/ (UK)| n.|长角亚目|A suborder of insects including mosquitoes, gnats, midges, and crane flies, characterized by their long antennae and slender bodies.|Mosquitoes, belonging to the Nematocera suborder, are known vectors of diseases.|近义词:lower Diptera|反义词:Brachycera|词性:noun

Etymology:
From New Latin Nematocera, from Ancient Greek νῆμα (nêma, “thread”) + κέρας (kéras, “horn”).
