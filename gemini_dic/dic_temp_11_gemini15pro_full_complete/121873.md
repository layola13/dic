misroutes:/ˌmɪsˈruːts/ |v. |错误路由，发送到错误的路线|The package was misrouted and ended up in Alaska. 包裹被错误地路由，最终到了阿拉斯加。|近义词: misdirects, reroutes incorrectly|反义词: routes correctly, directs properly|词性:动词

misroute:/ˌmɪsˈruːt/ |n. |错误路由，发送到错误的路线（名词形式较少用）|The misroute caused a significant delay in delivery.  错误的路线导致了交货的严重延误。|近义词: misdirection, incorrect routing|反义词: correct routing, proper direction|词性:名词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly") + "route" (from Old French "route," meaning "road" or "way")
