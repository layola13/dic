misintelligence:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/
|n.|错误情报；误报|Incorrect or misleading information, often disseminated deliberately to deceive.|The spread of misintelligence led to a disastrous military decision.|近义词: disinformation, misinformation, false information|反义词: intelligence, accurate information|词性:名词

词源:
mis- (prefix): meaning "wrong," "bad," or "lack of"
intelligence (noun): from Old French "intelligence" meaning "understanding," "knowledge"
