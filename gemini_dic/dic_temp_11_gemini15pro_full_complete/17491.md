bearishly:/ˈbɛərɪʃli/| adv.|看跌地，悲观地|In a way that expects prices to fall; pessimistically.|Investors are feeling bearishly about the future of the stock market. 投资者对股市的前景感到悲观。|Synonyms: pessimistically, negatively, gloomily|Antonyms: bullishly, optimistically, positively|词性:副词
