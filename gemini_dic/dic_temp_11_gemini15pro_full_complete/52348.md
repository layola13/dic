dictational:/dɪkˈteɪʃənəl/
adjective
支配的；命令的；听写式的
Characterized by or given to dictation; imperious.
The manager's dictational style made it difficult for employees to contribute their ideas.  经理的命令式风格让员工难以贡献自己的想法。
His dictational tone irritated everyone in the room. 他那命令的口吻激怒了房间里的每个人。
authoritarian, domineering, autocratic
democratic, collaborative, permissive
adj.
