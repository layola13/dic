cornishes:/ˈkɔːrnɪʃɪz/
n.
檐口，飞檐，（家具的）突出部
Projecting moldings along the top of a wall or building, or along the edge of a piece of furniture.
The cornices of the building were intricately carved.  这座建筑的飞檐雕刻精美。

近义词:moldings, eaves, ledges
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用“base” 或 “foundation” 表示与顶部相对的底部)
