hillers:/ˈhɪlərz/ | n. | 种植者；耕种者；居住在山上的人 | People who cultivate hills or live on hills. | The hillers terraced the slopes to prevent erosion. 山上居民在斜坡上修建梯田以防止水土流失。 |  The hillers grew hardy crops that could withstand the harsh climate. 山上居民种植能够抵御恶劣气候的耐寒作物。 |  近义词: cultivators, farmers, mountain dwellers | 反义词: lowlanders, plainsmen | noun
