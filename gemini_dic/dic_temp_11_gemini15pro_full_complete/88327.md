hippocampus:/ˈhɪpəˌkæmpəs/ (US), /ˌhɪpəˈkæmpəs/ (UK)| n. |海马体|A complex neural structure (shaped like a seahorse) located in the medial temporal lobe of the brain; crucial for forming new memories, particularly declarative memories, and spatial navigation.|The hippocampus plays a vital role in learning and memory.|近义词: memory center|反义词: N/A|名词
