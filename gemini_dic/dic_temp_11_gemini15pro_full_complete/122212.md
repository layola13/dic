misvaluing: /ˌmɪsˈvæljuːɪŋ/
verb
低估；错误估价
Undervaluing something or someone; assessing something or someone at less than its or their true worth.
He was misvaluing her contributions to the project. 他低估了她对这个项目的贡献。
Synonyms: undervaluing, underestimating, underappraising, depreciating
Antonyms: overvaluing, overestimating, appreciating
词性: 动词


misvalue: /ˌmɪsˈvæljuː/
verb
低估；错误估价
To underestimate the value of something or someone.
The company misvalued the property, leading to a significant financial loss.  公司错误估价了这处房产，导致了重大的经济损失。
Synonyms: undervalue, underestimate, underappraise, depreciate
Antonyms: overvalue, overestimate, appreciate
词性: 动词


misvaluation: /ˌmɪsˌvæljuˈeɪʃən/
noun
低估；错误估价
The act of misjudging the value of something.
The misvaluation of the assets resulted in a legal dispute. 对资产的错误估价导致了法律纠纷。
Synonyms: undervaluation, underestimation, underappraisal, depreciation
Antonyms: overvaluation, overestimation, appreciation
词性: 名词


词源:  The prefix "mis-" (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "value" (from Old French "valuer," meaning "to be worth") + "-ing" (present participle suffix) / "-ation" (noun suffix).
