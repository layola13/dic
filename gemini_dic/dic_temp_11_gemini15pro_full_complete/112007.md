lonest:/ˈloʊnɪst/ |adj.|最孤独的|superlative adjective of lonely; most lonely.  Feeling utterly isolated and alone.|例句：She felt lonest after her family moved away. 她一家搬走后，她感到无比孤独。

例句：It was the lonest Christmas I've ever had. 那是我经历过的最孤独的圣诞节。|近义词：loneliest, most solitary, most isolated|反义词：least lonely, most sociable, most gregarious|词性: 形容词
