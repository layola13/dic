fre:/friː/ | adj. | 免费的，自由的，空闲的 | Free means not costing any money, not being controlled or restricted, or not being occupied. | The museum offers free admission on Sundays.  博物馆周日免费入场。| synonyms: complimentary, gratis, unrestricted, unoccupied | antonyms: paid, restricted, occupied | adjective

fre:/friː/ | adv. | 免费地，自由地 | Free means without cost or restraint. | Children under five travel free. 五岁以下儿童免费旅行。| synonyms: freely, gratuitously | antonyms: expensively, restrictively | adverb

fre:/friː/ | v. | 释放，使自由 | Free means to release from captivity or constraint. | The hostages were finally freed. 人质终于获释了。| synonyms: release, liberate, emancipate | antonyms: imprison, confine, restrict | verb
