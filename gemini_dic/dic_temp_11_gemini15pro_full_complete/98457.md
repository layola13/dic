inspired:/ɪnˈspaɪərd/ | adj. | 受到鼓舞的；有灵感的 | Filled with the urge or ability to do or feel something, especially to do something creative; stimulated. | Her inspired performance won her the first prize. 她充满灵感的表演为她赢得了第一名。 | motivated, stimulated, encouraged | uninspired, unenthusiastic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inspirare*, from in- "in" + spirare "to breathe".  Originally meaning "divinely influenced," reflecting the belief that creative impulses were breathed into a person by a god or muse.
