naive:/nɑːˈiːv/ | adj. | 天真的，幼稚的 | Showing a lack of experience, wisdom, or judgment; innocent and unsuspecting. |  She was a naive young girl from the countryside. 她是一个来自乡下的天真少女。

The naive tourist was easily tricked out of his money.  天真的游客很容易就被骗走了钱。

近义词: innocent, ingenuous, unsophisticated, artless, guileless | 反义词: sophisticated, worldly, experienced, cunning, shrewd | 词性: adjective
