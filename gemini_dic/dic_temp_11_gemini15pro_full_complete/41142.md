copublisher:/ˌkoʊˈpʌblɪʃər/| n. |共同出版商；联合出版者|A copublisher is a publisher who works with another publisher to produce and distribute a book or other publication.|Example 1: The small independent press acted as a copublisher with the larger, more established company to reach a wider audience. 小型的独立出版社与规模更大、更成熟的公司合作出版，以接触更广泛的读者。

Example 2: The university press was the copublisher for the academic journal. 大学出版社是这本学术期刊的共同出版商。|近义词：joint publisher, co-publisher|反义词:|词性:noun
