cabinetmaking:/ˈkæbɪnɪtˌmeɪkɪŋ/ | n. | 家具制造；细木工 | The art or trade of designing and constructing cabinets and other fine furniture. | He learned cabinetmaking from his grandfather. 他从他祖父那里学会了家具制造。 | 近义词：joinery, woodwork, carpentry | 反义词: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From cabinet + making.  "Cabinet" comes from the Old French "cabinet" (small room, private chamber), diminutive of "cabine" (cabin). "Making" comes from the Old English "macian" (to make).
