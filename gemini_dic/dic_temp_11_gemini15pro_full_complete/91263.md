hydrofracturings:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfræktʃərɪŋz/
n.
水力压裂(复数)
The process of injecting fluid at high pressure into subterranean rocks, boreholes, etc., so as to force open existing fissures and extract oil or gas.
Hydraulic fracturing, or fracking, is a controversial method of extracting natural gas and oil from shale rock formations.  水力压裂，或称压裂，是一种从页岩岩层中提取天然气和石油的有争议的方法。
Several companies are investing in new hydrofracturings technologies.  几家公司正在投资新的水力压裂技术。
近义词: fracking, hydraulic fracturing
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其他能源获取方式对比，例如：renewable energy extraction 可再生能源提取)
词性: 名词
