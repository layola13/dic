exosseous:/ˌɛkˈsɒsiəs/
adjective
骨外的；骨外面的
Situated or occurring outside the bone.
例句：The exosseous portion of the tumor was easily removed. 肿瘤的骨外部分很容易切除。

例句：Exosseous lesions can sometimes mimic bone tumors. 骨外病变有时会模仿骨肿瘤。

近义词：extraosseous, periosteal
反义词：intraosseous
词性：adjective
