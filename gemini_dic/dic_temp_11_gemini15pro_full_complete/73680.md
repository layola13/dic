forgets:/fərˈɡɛts/| v. |忘记|Fails to remember.|She often forgets her keys. 她经常忘记带钥匙。|近义词: overlooks, misses, disremembers|反义词: remembers, recalls|verb (third person singular simple present forgets, present participle forgetting, simple past forgot, past participle forgotten)


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He forgets his glasses sometimes. 他有时会忘记他的眼镜。
She forgets to call her mother every week. 她每周都忘记给她母亲打电话。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am forgetting everything these days. 我这几天什么都忘记。
He is forgetting his manners. 他正在忘记他的礼貌。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have forgotten what I wanted to say. 我忘记了我想要说什么。
She has forgotten her password. 她忘记了她的密码。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been forgetting things lately. 我最近一直在忘记事情。
He has been forgetting to take his medicine. 他一直忘记吃药。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I forgot to buy milk. 我忘记买牛奶了。
He forgot his wife's birthday. 他忘记了他妻子的生日。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was forgetting all about it. 我正渐渐忘记这件事。
He was forgetting his lines in the play. 他在戏里忘记了他的台词。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had forgotten all about our agreement. 我完全忘记了我们的约定。
She had forgotten her purse at home. 她把钱包忘在家里了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been forgetting to lock the door. 我一直忘记锁门。
He had been forgetting to water the plants. 他一直忘记给植物浇水。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will forget all about this soon. 我很快就会忘记这一切。
He will forget to pick up the kids. 他会忘记去接孩子。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be forgetting all about this by next week.  下周我就会忘记这一切。
He will be forgetting his troubles on vacation. 他会在度假时忘记他的烦恼。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have forgotten all about this. 到明年，我就会忘记这一切。
He will have forgotten my name by then. 到那时，他就会忘记我的名字。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been forgetting things for years. 多年来我一直在忘记东西。  (This tense is less common with "forget").
He will have been forgetting his responsibilities. 他将一直忘记他的责任。(This tense is less common with "forget").


虚拟语气:
If I forgot her birthday, she would be very upset. 如果我忘记了她的生日，她会非常难过。
I wish I could forget that awful experience. 我希望我能忘记那段可怕的经历。


被动语态:
His name was forgotten. 他的名字被遗忘了。
The incident has been forgotten. 这件事已经被遗忘了。

疑问句:
Did you forget your password? 你忘记密码了吗？
Have you forgotten where you parked? 你忘记你把车停在哪里了吗？

否定句:
I didn't forget your birthday. 我没有忘记你的生日。
She hasn't forgotten the promise. 她没有忘记承诺。
