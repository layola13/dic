overindustrialism:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəlɪzəm/
n.
过度工业化
Excessive development of industry, often leading to negative social and environmental consequences.
The overindustrialism of the region led to severe pollution.  这个地区的过度工业化导致了严重的污染。
The documentary explored the negative impacts of overindustrialism on local communities.  这部纪录片探讨了过度工业化对当地社区的负面影响。
近义词: industrial excess, overdevelopment
反义词: underdevelopment, sustainable development
