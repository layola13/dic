equitableness:/ˈekwɪtəblnəs/
n.
公平,公正,合理
The quality of being fair and impartial.
The equitableness of the judge's decision was praised by all.  法官判决的公正性受到了所有人的称赞。
近义词：fairness, justice, impartiality, righteousness
反义词：unfairness, injustice, bias, partiality
词性: 名词

equitable:/ˈekwɪtəbl/
adj.
公平的,公正的,合理的
Characterized by equity or fairness; just and right; reasonable.
He made an equitable distribution of the property. 他公平地分配了财产。
近义词：fair, just, impartial, right
反义词：unfair, unjust, biased, partial
词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French equitable, from late Latin aequitabilis, from Latin aequitas ‘equity’.
