cynosure: /ˈsaɪnəʃʊr/ | n. | 指南; 吸引注意力的中心; 焦点 | something that strongly attracts attention by its brilliance, interest, etc. |  The Eiffel Tower is the cynosure of Paris. 埃菲尔铁塔是巴黎的焦点。| The new sports car was the cynosure of all eyes at the auto show. 这款新的跑车在车展上吸引了所有人的目光。 | synonyms: center of attention, focus, focal point, magnet | antonyms: obscurity, anonymity | noun
