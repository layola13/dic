overpromise: /ˌoʊvərˈprɑːmɪs/ /ˌəʊvəˈprɒmɪs/
verb
承诺过多；夸大承诺
to promise more than one can deliver or is realistic
The company overpromised and underdelivered, leading to dissatisfied customers.  这家公司承诺过多，交付不足，导致客户不满。

The salesman overpromised on the car's features.  销售员夸大了汽车的功能。

Synonyms: exaggerate, overstate, overcommit
Antonyms: underpromise, understate, downplay
词性: 动词
