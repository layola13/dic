affectible:/əˈfɛktɪbl/
adjective
易受影响的; 易动感情的
Capable of being affected or influenced emotionally.
She is an affectible young woman, easily moved to tears or laughter. 她是一个感情丰富的年轻女子，很容易哭或笑。
近义词:impressionable, susceptible, sensitive, emotional
反义词:unaffectible, impassive, stoic
词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin affectibilis, from affect- ‘influenced’ (see affect).
