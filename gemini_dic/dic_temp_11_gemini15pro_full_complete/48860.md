deformed:/dɪˈfɔːrmd/
adjective
畸形的，变形的
misshapen, distorted, or changed in shape from the normal or original form.
The accident left him with a deformed leg.  事故使他的腿畸形了。
deformed:/dɪˈfɔːrmd/
adjective
丑陋的，难看的
ugly or disfigured.
The painting depicted a deformed monster.  这幅画描绘了一个丑陋的怪物。

近义词:misshapen, distorted, twisted, contorted, mangled, disfigured
反义词:perfect, symmetrical, unblemished, normal

词性:adjective
