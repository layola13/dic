outspurting:/ˌaʊtˈspɜːtɪŋ/
verb
喷出，涌出
gushing or ejecting forcefully
The volcano was outspurting lava and ash. 火山喷发出熔岩和火山灰。

The wound was outspurting blood. 伤口涌出鲜血。
近义词: gushing, spurting, erupting, ejecting
反义词: trickling, dripping, oozing
词性: verb (present participle)


outspurt:/ˈaʊtˌspɜːt/
noun
喷出，涌出
a sudden gush or ejection
There was a sudden outspurt of water from the pipe. 管道里突然涌出一股水。

The geyser produced an impressive outspurt of hot water and steam. 间歇泉喷出一股令人印象深刻的热水和蒸汽。
近义词: gush, spurt, eruption, ejection
反义词: trickle, drip, ooze
词性: noun
