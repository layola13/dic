medicable:/ˈmɛdɪkəbl/| adj.|可医治的，可治疗的|Able to be cured or healed.|例句：His condition is medicable with the right treatment. 他的病情用正确的治疗方法是可以治愈的。

例句：Fortunately, the wound was medicable and healed quickly.  幸运的是，伤口是可以治愈的，并且很快就痊愈了。|近义词：curable, treatable, remediable|反义词：incurable, untreatable, irremediable|词性：形容词
