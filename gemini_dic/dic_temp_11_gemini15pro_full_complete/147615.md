overplenteously: /ˌoʊvərˈplɛntɪəsli/
adverb
过度丰富地；过分充足地
in an excessively plentiful manner; to an excessive degree of abundance.
The table was overplenteously laden with food. 桌子上摆满了食物。
The fields were overplenteously supplied with water after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，田野里水量充足。
Near Synonyms:  abundantly, profusely, copiously, lavishly, exuberantly
Near Antonyms: sparsely, meagerly, sparingly, insufficiently
词性: adverb
