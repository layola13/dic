interlanguages:/ˌɪntərˈlæŋɡwɪdʒɪz/
|n.| 中介语; 过渡语 |A language system developed by a learner of a second language (or L2) that preserves some features of their first language (or L1) and can also overgeneralize some L2 writing and speaking rules.  It is considered to be a dynamic, rule-based linguistic system that has evolved as the learner builds their L2 proficiency.| Learners of English often go through a series of interlanguages before they achieve native-like fluency.  英语学习者在达到母语般的流利程度之前通常会经历一系列的中间语。| intermediate languages, learner language| N/A| noun
