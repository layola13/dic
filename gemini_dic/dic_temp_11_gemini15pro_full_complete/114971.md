Manchu: /ˈmæntʃuː/ /mænˈtʃuː/ | n. adj. | 满族人，满语 |  A member of a Tungusic people of Manchuria who conquered China in the 17th century and established the Qing dynasty. Also refers to their language. |  The Manchu emperors ruled China for over 250 years. 满族皇帝统治了中国250多年。|  The Manchu language is now considered endangered. 满语现在被认为是濒危语言。 | Synonyms:  Qing (for the dynasty), Tungusic (related language group) | Antonyms:  Han (major ethnic group in China during Manchu rule) | noun
Manchu: /ˈmæntʃuː/ /mænˈtʃuː/ | adj. | 满族的，满语的 | Relating to the Manchu people or their language. |  Manchu cuisine is known for its hearty stews and dumplings. 满族菜以其丰盛的炖菜和饺子而闻名。 |  She is studying Manchu history and culture. 她正在学习满族历史和文化。| Synonyms: Qing (sometimes used for cultural aspects) | Antonyms:  Han (referring to the Han Chinese culture) | adjective
