bookstalls:/ˈbʊkstɔːlz/| n. |书摊；书报摊|A stall or small shop where books are sold, often secondhand or discounted. |例句：I found a first edition of my favorite novel at one of the bookstalls along the Seine. 我在塞纳河沿岸的一个书摊上找到了我最喜欢的小说初版。

例句：The bookstalls were filled with rare and antiquarian books. 这些书摊上摆满了珍本和古籍。|近义词：bookstands, newsstands, bookstores |反义词: library (a place for borrowing, not buying books) |词性:noun
