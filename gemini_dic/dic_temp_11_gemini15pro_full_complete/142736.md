otolitic:/ˌoʊtəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. |耳石的，与耳石有关的 | Relating to the otoliths, small crystals of calcium carbonate in the inner ear that are involved in sensing gravity and linear acceleration. | The otolitic organs help us maintain balance. 耳石器官帮助我们保持平衡。|  synonym: vestibular | antonym: N/A | adjective

otolith:/ˈoʊtəlɪθ/ | n. | 耳石 | A small calcareous concretion in the inner ear of vertebrates, involved in sensing gravity and movement. | Otoliths play a crucial role in our sense of balance. 耳石在我们保持平衡方面起着至关重要的作用。 | synonym: statoconium | antonym: N/A | noun
