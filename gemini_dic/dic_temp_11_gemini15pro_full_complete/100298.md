intershade:/ˈɪntərʃeɪd/
noun
中间色调;浓淡相间;过渡色
A color that is between two other distinct colors.  The gradation of color between light and shadow.
The artist used a subtle intershade of blue and green to depict the distant hills. 艺术家运用蓝绿色的微妙过渡色来描绘远处的山峦。
Synonyms: mid-tone, gradation, nuance
Antonyms: extreme, stark, saturated
n.
