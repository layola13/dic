lemminglike: /ˈlɛmɪŋˌlaɪk/
adjective
像旅鼠一样盲目跟风的；缺乏独立思考的
Resembling a lemming in blindly following others, especially to perceived self-destruction.
The investors behaved lemminglike, rushing to sell their stocks when the market dipped. 投资者们像旅鼠一样，在市场下跌时争相抛售股票。
Synonyms:  blindly following, herdlike, unthinking, sheeplike, imitative
Antonyms: independent, individualistic, discerning, critical, thoughtful
词性: 形容词
