homotropous:/hoʊˈmɑːtrəpəs/
adjective
同型生长的，顺生的
Having the micropyle and chalaza at opposite ends of the ovule, as in most flowering plants.  The ovule is straight, with the micropyle at the apex and the chalaza at the base.
The homotropous ovule is the most common type found in flowering plants.  同型胚珠是被子植物中最常见的类型。
Homotropous ovules are characteristic of most angiosperms.  同型胚珠是大多数被子植物的特征。
近义词:atropous, orthotropous
反义词:anatropous, campylotropous
词性:adjective
词源:From Greek *homos* "same" + *tropos* "turn, direction".
