archeology:/ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒi/ (US) /ˌɑːrkiˈɑːlədʒi/| n.|考古学|The study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.|例句：She is studying archeology at university. 她正在大学学习考古学。

例句：Archeology can reveal much about past civilizations. 考古学可以揭示很多关于过去文明的信息。
