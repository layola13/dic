glyconian:/ɡlaɪˈkoʊniən/
n.| 糖基化相关的; 糖基化的;  糖缀合物;|Relating to or characterized by glycosylation; a compound containing a carbohydrate component bound covalently to a non-carbohydrate moiety.|The researchers are studying glyconian structures on cell surfaces. 研究人员正在研究细胞表面的糖基化结构。|Synonyms: glycosylated, glycoconjugate|Antonyms: non-glycosylated|noun

glyconian:/ɡlaɪˈkoʊniən/
adj.| 糖基化的|Characterized by or relating to glycosylation.|Glyconian modifications play a crucial role in protein function. 糖基化修饰在蛋白质功能中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: glycosylated|Antonyms: non-glycosylated|adjective
