matutine:/ˈmætjʊtaɪn/| adj. n.|早晨的，清晨的；晨祷|Of or relating to the morning; occurring in the morning, especially before noon.  Also, a service of morning prayer.|The matutine air was crisp and cool. 清晨的空气清新凉爽。|Synonyms: early, morning, dawn, antemeridian|Antonyms: evening, nocturnal, vespertine|adjective

matutine:/ˈmætjʊtaɪn/| n.|晨祷，晨课|A service of morning prayer.|The monks gathered for matutines. 僧侣们聚集在一起做晨祷。|Synonyms: morning prayer, lauds|Antonyms: vespers, evensong|noun
