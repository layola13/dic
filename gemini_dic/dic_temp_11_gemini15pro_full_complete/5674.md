altruist:/ˈæltruːɪst/| n. |利他主义者；无私的人|A person unselfishly concerned for or devoted to the welfare of others.|例句：She's not an altruist; she expects something in return for every favor. 她不是一个利他主义者；她期望得到回报。

例句：The altruist donated a large sum of money to the charity.  这位利他主义者向慈善机构捐赠了大笔资金。|近义词：philanthropist, humanitarian, benefactor|反义词：egoist, narcissist|词性:noun

altruistic:/ˌæltruːˈɪstɪk/| adj. |利他的；无私的|Showing a disinterested and selfless concern for the well-being of others; unselfish.|例句：Altruistic behavior is often seen in social animals. 在群居动物中经常可以看到利他行为。

例句：His altruistic motives were questioned by some.  他利他的动机受到了一些人的质疑。|近义词：selfless, benevolent, charitable|反义词：selfish, egoistic, self-centered|词性:adjective

altruistically:/ˌæltruˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. |利他地；无私地 | In an altruistic manner; unselfishly. | 例句：She altruistically volunteered her time to help the homeless. 她无私地自愿奉献时间帮助无家可归的人。

例句：He altruistically donated his kidney to his brother. 他无私地把自己的肾脏捐给了他的兄弟。 | 近义词：selflessly, benevolently, charitably | 反义词：selfishly, egoistically |词性:adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *altruiste*, from *autrui* "other people," from Old French *altrui* "other people," originally *alteri*, from Latin *alteri*, dative plural of *alter* "other."  Coined by French philosopher Auguste Comte.
