alveoliform:/ˌælviˈoʊlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
蜂窝状的；小窝状的
Resembling alveoli; having the form of small cavities or pits.
The fossilized coral had an alveoliform structure. 这块珊瑚化石具有蜂窝状结构。
The surface of the rock was alveoliform, pitted with numerous tiny holes. 岩石表面呈蜂窝状，布满了许多小孔。
近义词: alveolar, pitted, honeycombed
反义词: smooth, flat
