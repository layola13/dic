gleamless:/ˈɡliːmlɪs/| adj.|无光泽的，黯淡的|Lacking gleam; dull or not shiny.|The old car's gleamless paint showed its age.|近义词：dull, mat, lackluster, lusterless|反义词:gleaming, shiny, bright, polished|词性:形容词
Etymology: gleam + -less.  From gleam (n.) + -less (suffix meaning 'without').  Gleam itself comes from Old English *glæm, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *glema- "bright."
