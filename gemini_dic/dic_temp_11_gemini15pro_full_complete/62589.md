enfeebled:/ɪnˈfiːbəld/ | adj. | 衰弱的，无力的 | Made weak or feeble. | The enfeebled old man could barely walk.  这位衰弱的老人几乎走不动了。| 近义词: weakened, debilitated, frail | 反义词: strengthened, invigorated, robust | 词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old French *enfeblir, from en- "in" + feble "weak" (from Latin flebilis "lamentable").
