dreariest:/ˈdrɪəriɪst/| adj.|最沉闷的，最阴郁的|superlative adjective of dreary; most dreary|The dreariest day of the year was also the coldest.|近义词：gloomiest, bleakest, dullest, most dismal|反义词：brightest, cheeriest, happiest, most exciting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *dreriest, superlative of drery (“sad, mournful, dismal”), from Old English *drēorig (“sad, sorrowful, afflicted”), from Proto-Germanic *drēuzigaz (“troubled, muddy”).
