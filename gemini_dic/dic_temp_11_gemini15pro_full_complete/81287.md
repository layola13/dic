granting:/ˈɡræntɪŋ/| v.|准予，授予；承认|The foundation is granting $50,000 to the project.  该基金会将向该项目拨款5万美元。|例句：Granting your request, we will proceed with the project. 准予你的请求，我们将继续进行这个项目。|近义词：allowing, conceding, awarding|反义词：refusing, denying, withholding|词性:动词(present participle of grant)

Etymology: From Middle English *graunten, *grawnten, from Anglo-Norman graunter, graanter, granter (also Old French graanter, greanter), from Vulgar Latin *credentāre (“to entrust, believe”), present active infinitive of *credentāre, from Latin crēdentāre, from crēdere (“to believe, entrust, lend”).
