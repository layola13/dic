fast-forwarding:/ˌfæstˈfɔːrwərdɪŋ/ | n./v.| 快进，快速前进 | The act of advancing rapidly through a recording or program.  It can refer to both the action and the feature that allows this action. | I hate all the commercials, so I'm always fast-forwarding through them. 我讨厌所有广告，所以我总是快进它们。 |  He used the fast-forwarding function to skip the boring parts of the movie. 他使用快进功能跳过了电影中无聊的部分。 | 近义词: skipping, advancing, zipping | 反义词: rewinding, reversing | 词性:  noun/gerund (when used as a noun), present participle (when used as a verb)


fast forward:/ˌfæstˈfɔːrwərd/ | v. | 快进，快速前进 | To advance rapidly through a recording or program. | I usually fast forward through the commercials. 我通常快进广告。 |  Can you fast forward to the end of the song? 你能快进到歌曲的结尾吗？ | 近义词: skip, advance, zip | 反义词: rewind, reverse | 词性: verb

Etymology:  A compound formed from "fast" (meaning quick) and "forward" (meaning in a direction ahead).  The term gained popularity with the advent of VCRs and cassette tapes.
