ill-advised:/ˌɪl ədˈvaɪzd/
adjective
不明智的，欠考虑的
not wise, and likely to cause problems
例句：Going out in a blizzard was an ill-advised decision.  在暴风雪中外出是一个不明智的决定。

例句：Investing all your money in one stock is ill-advised. 把你所有的钱都投资在一支股票上是不明智的。
近义词：unwise, imprudent, foolish, reckless
反义词：wise, prudent, sensible, judicious
词性: adjective
