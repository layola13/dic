endostome:/ˈɛndoʊstoʊm/| n. |内口|An inner opening, particularly the opening of the inner seed coat or the opening in the archegonium of some plants through which fertilization takes place. |The pollen tube enters the ovule through the endostome.  花粉管通过内口进入胚珠。| The endostome regulates the entry of water into the seed. 内口调节水分进入种子。|近义词: micropyle (especially in seed plants) |反义词: exostome| noun
