虚拟语气:
If he contradicted the evidence, he would be lying. = 如果他反驳证据，他就是在撒谎。
I wish he wouldn't contradict me all the time. = 我希望他不要总是反驳我。

被动语态:
The findings were contradicted by later research. = 这些发现被后来的研究反驳了。
The contract was contravened by their actions. = 他们的行为违反了合同。（Note: This uses "contraven-" which, while related, is a distinct root.)

疑问句:
What contradicts this theory? = 什么与这个理论相矛盾？
How do these two ideas contrast? = 这两个想法是如何对比的？

否定句:
His actions do not contradict his words. = 他的行为与他的言辞并不矛盾。
The evidence does not contrast significantly. = 证据没有显著的对比。


PHRASE->
contradict oneself = 自相矛盾
contrast with = 与...对比
contrary to = 与...相反
in contrast to = 与...形成对比
counter-argument = 反驳


注意事项:  "contr-" itself is not a word. It's a prefix. Examples above use full words containing "contr-" demonstrating its meaning in context. There are many words using this prefix, and the specific meanings can vary slightly. Be sure to consult a dictionary for precise definitions.
