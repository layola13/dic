hunkerisms:/ˈhʌŋkərɪzəmz/
n.
囤积主义；囤积癖；因囤积而产生的行为或观点
The practice of accumulating and holding onto a large number of items, often beyond their practical usefulness, driven by anxiety or fear of future scarcity.
During the pandemic, hunkerisms became more common as people stockpiled essential goods.  在疫情期间，由于人们囤积必需品，囤积主义变得更加普遍。
synonyms: hoarding, stockpiling, accumulating
antonyms: minimalism, purging, decluttering
noun
