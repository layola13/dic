materialistic:/məˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|物质主义的；拜金的|Excessively concerned with material possessions; valuing possessions above spiritual or intellectual values.|She's so materialistic; all she cares about are designer clothes and expensive cars. 她太物质了；她只在乎名牌服装和豪车。|Synonyms: acquisitive, greedy, mercenary, avaricious|Antonyms: spiritual, altruistic, ascetic|adjective
