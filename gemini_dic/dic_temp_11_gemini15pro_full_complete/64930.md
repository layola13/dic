estancia:/ɛsˈtɑːnsjə/ /ɛsˈtænsiə/
n.
大牧场；农场；(尤指南美)种植园；庄园
A large estate or ranch, especially in South America, devoted to farming and livestock raising.
We spent a week at a beautiful estancia in Argentina. 我们在阿根廷一个美丽的庄园里待了一周。
近义词：ranch, farm, plantation, estate
反义词：(无直接反义词)
词性：名词
