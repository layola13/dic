coinventing:/ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛntɪŋ/
verb
共同发明；合作发明
the act of inventing something together with someone else.
Marie Curie is credited with coinventing the theory of radioactivity. 玛丽·居里被认为是放射性理论的共同发明者。
coinvent, collaborate on an invention, jointly invent
solely inventing, independently inventing
v.
