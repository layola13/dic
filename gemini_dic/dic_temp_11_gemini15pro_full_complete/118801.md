metamere:/ˈmetəmɪər/ | n. |体节，体环|A segment of a body that is repeated in a series, such as the segments of an earthworm.|The earthworm's body is composed of numerous metameres.|Synonyms: segment, somite|Antonyms: whole, entirety|noun

metamere:/ˈmetəmɪər/ | n. |同源异形体|One of a series of homologous parts, as leaves or petals.|The petals of a flower can be considered metameres.|Synonyms: homologue|Antonyms: heterologue|noun
