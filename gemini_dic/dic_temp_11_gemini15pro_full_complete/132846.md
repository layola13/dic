noneffete:/ˌnɒnɪˈfiːt/

未丧失精力的；强健的；富有青春活力的
Not effete; strong and vigorous; retaining youthful energy.
He was a noneffete old man, still working at 90.  他是一位老当益壮的老人，90岁还在工作。

近义词:vigorous, energetic, robust, strong, youthful, dynamic
反义词:effete, weak, feeble, frail, decadent, exhausted

词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "effete" (adjective meaning "weak or effeminate").
