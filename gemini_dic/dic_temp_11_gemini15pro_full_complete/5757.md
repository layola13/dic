amah:/ˈɑːmə/ | n. | 女佣；保姆；（尤指东方国家的）家庭女仆 | A female domestic servant, especially in East Asia or India.  | She employed an amah to help with the children. 她雇了一个女佣来帮忙照顾孩子。|  My grandmother worked as an amah for a wealthy family in Hong Kong. 我的祖母曾在香港一个富裕家庭当女佣。 | Synonyms: maid, servant, housekeeper, nanny | Antonyms: employer, master | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *amá*, itself from Malay *əmak* ("mother").
