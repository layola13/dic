one-star:/ˈwʌn stɑːr/ /wʌn stɑːr/
adjective
一星级的；一颗星的
Rated with only one star, indicating low quality or poor performance.
The hotel received mostly one-star reviews online. 这家酒店在网上大部分评价都是一星。
The restaurant's food hygiene rating was a dismal one-star.  这家餐厅的食品卫生评级只有一颗星，惨不忍睹。
Synonyms: poor, low-quality, inferior, substandard
Antonyms: five-star, excellent, top-rated, high-quality
