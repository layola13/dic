outermost:/ˈaʊtərməʊst/ (US) /ˈaʊtəməʊst/ (UK)| adj.|最外面的，最外层的|situated farthest from the center or inside|The outermost layer of the onion was peeled off.  洋葱的最外层被剥掉了。

The outermost planet in our solar system is Neptune. 我们太阳系中最外层的行星是海王星。
