misaddresses:/ˌmɪsəˈdrɛsɪz/ | v. | 写错地址；称呼错误 |  To write the wrong address on (an envelope, letter, etc.); to address (someone) incorrectly. | The letter was misaddressed and never reached its intended recipient. 这封信写错了地址，从未到达预定的收件人手中。| synonyms: misdirect, mislabel | antonyms: address correctly, label correctly | verb

misaddress:/ˌmɪsəˈdrɛs/ | n. | 写错地址；称呼错误 | An instance of writing the wrong address or addressing someone incorrectly. | The misaddress caused a significant delay in the delivery. 地址错误导致投递严重延误。| synonyms: wrong address, incorrect address | antonyms: correct address | noun
