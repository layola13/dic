electrocutes:/ɪˈlɛktrəkjuːts/
verb
处死，电死
to kill someone by passing a strong electric current through their body.
The murderer was electrocuted.  凶手被处以电刑。
The faulty wiring electrocuted the dog.  错误的线路把狗电死了。
Synonyms: execute, kill by electric shock
Antonyms: revive, resuscitate
词性: 动词
