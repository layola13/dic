graphology:/ɡræˈfɒlədʒi/ (US) /ɡrəˈfɒlədʒi/ (UK)| n. |笔迹学|The study of handwriting, especially when considered as an expression of character.|例句：Graphology is used to analyze personality traits through handwriting. 笔迹学被用来通过笔迹分析性格特征。

例句：He studied graphology in hopes of understanding his clients better. 他学习笔迹学是为了更好地了解他的客户。

近义词：handwriting analysis|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
