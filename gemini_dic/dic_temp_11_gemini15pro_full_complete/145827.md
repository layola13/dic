overdeliberated: /ˌoʊvərdiˈlɪbəreɪtɪd/
Verb (past participle)
过度审议的；过度斟酌的
Having thought about or discussed something for too long, to the point of inaction or indecision.
The overdeliberated plan ultimately failed due to missed opportunities.  这个过度审议的计划最终因为错失良机而失败了。
The committee, having overdeliberated the issue, finally reached a decision. 委员会在对这个问题过度审议之后，终于做出了决定。
近义词: overthought, overanalyzed
反义词: impulsive, rash
词性:  Verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "deliberate" (from Latin "deliberare," meaning "to weigh in the mind, consider").
