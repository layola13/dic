outwatering:/ˌaʊtˈwɔːtərɪŋ/
n.|排水，脱水|The process of removing water from an area, typically a construction site or excavation, to allow for work to be carried out. |例句：The outwatering system failed, causing the excavation to flood. 排水系统失灵，导致挖掘现场被淹。|近义词：dewatering, drainage|反义词:watering, irrigation|词性:名词

Etymology:  "out-" (meaning "out of, from") + "water" (referring to the substance being removed) + "-ing" (forming a present participle/gerund, indicating the action or process).
