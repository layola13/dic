confusedly:/kənˈfjuːzɪdli/ | adv. | 困惑地，茫然地 | In a manner showing confusion or bewilderment. | She looked at him confusedly, unsure of what he meant. 她困惑地看着他，不确定他是什么意思。|  He stammered confusedly, trying to explain the situation. 他结结巴巴地、困惑地试图解释情况。| 近义词: bewilderedly, uncertainly, hesitantly | 反义词: clearly, confidently, decisively | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "confused" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Confused" comes from the past participle of the verb "confuse," which derives from the Latin "confusus," past participle of "confundere" (to pour together, mix up, confuse).
