neckers:/ˈnɛkərz/
n.
围巾，领巾，颈巾
A piece of cloth worn around the neck, often for warmth or decoration.
She wore a brightly colored neckers to brighten up her outfit. 她戴了一条颜色鲜艳的围巾来点亮她的装扮。
The football fans wore their team's neckers to the game. 足球迷们戴着他们球队的围巾去看比赛。
synonyms: scarf, bandana, neckerchief
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object.  Could consider "bare neck" conceptually)
