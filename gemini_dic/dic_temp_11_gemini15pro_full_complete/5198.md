alliant:/əˈlaɪənt/ | adj. n.|结盟的，联盟的；同盟者，盟友|Joined together in an alliance, especially a political or military one.  |例句：The two countries were close allies during the war. 这两个国家在战争期间是亲密的盟友。|近义词： allied, confederate, partner, collaborator |反义词：enemy, opponent, foe|词性：形容词/名词


Etymology:
From Old French *aliant (present participle of alier "to ally," from Latin alligare "to bind to"), from ad- "to" + ligare "to bind."
