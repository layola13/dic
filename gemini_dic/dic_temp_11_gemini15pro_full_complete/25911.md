burned: /bɜrnd/ (UK) /bɜrnt/ (US)| v. adj.|烧伤的，烧焦的；燃烧的|past tense and past participle of burn  (Meaning: to be damaged or destroyed by fire or extreme heat, or to cause this to happen)|The house burned down in the fire. 房子在火灾中烧毁了。|近义词：scorched, singed, charred|反义词：extinguished, quenched|词性: 动词(过去式和过去分词), 形容词

burned: /bɜrnd/ (UK) /bɜrnt/ (US)| adj.|烧伤的，烧焦的|damaged or injured by burning|He suffered burned hands. 他的手被烧伤了。|近义词：scalded, seared|反义词：unharmed, untouched|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *brennen*, from Old English *bærnan*, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *branden* and German *brennen*, also to brown.
