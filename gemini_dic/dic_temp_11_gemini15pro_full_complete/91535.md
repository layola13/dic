hypapophysis:/ˌhaɪpəˈpɒfɪsɪs/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈpɒfɪsiːz/ (UK)
n.
垂体突;垂体下突起
A ventral outgrowth of the infundibulum of the pituitary gland.  It develops from Rathke's pouch and is present in most vertebrates.
The hypapophysis is a small, ventral extension of the pituitary gland. 垂体突是垂体的一个小的腹侧延伸。
The function of the hypapophysis is not well understood in all species.  在所有物种中，垂体突的功能尚不清楚。
近义词:  infundibular process, pituitary process
反义词:  None (This is a specific anatomical term, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: noun
