contractive:/kənˈtræktɪv/| adj.|收缩的，收缩性的|Tending to contract; causing contraction.|例句：The contractive force of the muscles. 肌肉的收缩力。|例句：The contractive effect of the cold air. 冷空气的收缩作用。|近义词：constrictive, shrinking, tightening|反义词：expansive, dilative|词性：形容词
词源: From Late Latin *contractivus, from Latin contractus (past participle of contrahere “to draw together, conclude a bargain”).  From com- (“together”) + trahere (“to draw, pull”).
