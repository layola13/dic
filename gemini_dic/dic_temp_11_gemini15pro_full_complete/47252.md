dcms:/ˈdiː siː ɛmˈɛs/ |n.| 数据中心管理系统 (Data Center Management System)|A dcms is a software suite used to monitor and manage IT infrastructure within a data center. It typically includes tools for power management, cooling, security, and asset tracking. | Our new dcms allows us to monitor server performance in real time. 我们的新数据中心管理系统允许我们实时监控服务器性能。| 近义词: data center infrastructure management (DCIM) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
