demarcate:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪt/ /dɪˈmɑːrkeɪt/
verb
划定界限; 标出…的界限
To set the boundaries or limits of something.
例句：The boundary between the two countries is demarcated by a river.  两国之间的边界以一条河流为界。

例句：The police demarcated the crime scene with tape.  警察用胶带标出了犯罪现场。
近义词：define, delimit, separate, distinguish
反义词：merge, blend, confuse
词性：v.
