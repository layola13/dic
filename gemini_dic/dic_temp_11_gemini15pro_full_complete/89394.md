homopteran:/hɒˈmɒptərən/| n.|同翅目昆虫|An insect of the order Homoptera, such as an aphid, cicada, or leafhopper, having membranous forewings and hindwings and sucking mouthparts.| Aphids are common homopteran pests in gardens. 蚜虫是花园里常见的同翅目害虫。|近义词: hemipteran (though technically a broader category)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From the Greek homo- (same) + pteron (wing)
