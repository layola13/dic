appellants:/əˈpɛlənts/| n.|上诉人，上诉方|A person who applies to a higher court for a reversal of the decision of a lower court.|The appellants argued that the judge had misdirected the jury. 上诉人辩称法官误导了陪审团。|synonyms: petitioner, litigant, appellant, plaintiff in error |antonyms: appellee, respondent|noun
