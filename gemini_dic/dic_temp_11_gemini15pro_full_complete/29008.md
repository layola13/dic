cartist: /ˈkɑːrtɪst/
noun
购物车艺术家; 利用购物车进行创作的艺术家
An artist who uses shopping carts as a medium for their art.  They might create sculptures, installations, or other artworks using shopping carts.
The cartist transformed discarded shopping carts into a towering metal sculpture.  这位购物车艺术家将废弃的购物车改造成一座高耸的金属雕塑。
近义词: sculptor, installation artist
反义词: N/A
词性: n.
