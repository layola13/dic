overdeveloped: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈvɛləpt/ | adj. | 过度发达的；发育过度的 | Having developed too much; excessively developed.  This term often implies an imbalance or negative consequence of excessive development. |  The city's downtown is overdeveloped, causing traffic congestion and a lack of green space.  这个城市的市中心过度开发，导致交通拥堵和缺乏绿地。| The muscles in his right arm are overdeveloped from years of weightlifting.  由于多年的举重训练，他右臂的肌肉过度发达。 | 近义词: overbuilt, overgrown | 反义词: underdeveloped, undeveloped | 词性: adjective

词源:  "Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar) + "developed" (past participle of "develop," from French développer, from des- + envelopper "to wrap").
