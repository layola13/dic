half-willful:/ˌhæfˈwɪlfəl/
adj.
半故意的；带点任性的；有些执拗的
Partly intentional; done with a degree of stubbornness or capriciousness, but not entirely deliberate.
He gave her a half-willful shove. 他半故意地推了她一把。
Near Synonyms:  headstrong, willful, obstinate, perverse
Antonyms:  compliant, docile, obedient, submissive
词性: 形容词
