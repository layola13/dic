geelong:/ˈdʒiːlɒŋ/ (AU), /dʒɪˈlɔːŋ/ (US)| n.|吉朗（澳大利亚维多利亚州一城市）|A city located on Corio Bay and the Barwon River, in the state of Victoria in Australia. It is the second largest city in the state, and is known for its beautiful waterfront, vibrant arts scene, and as the home of the Geelong Cats Australian Football League club.|例句：Geelong is a beautiful city in Victoria. 吉朗是维多利亚州一座美丽的城市。
