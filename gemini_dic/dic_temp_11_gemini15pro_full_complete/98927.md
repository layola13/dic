intentively:/ɪnˈtɛntɪvli/| adv.|专注地，专心地|With close attention; purposefully.|She listened intently to the lecture, taking detailed notes. 她专心致志地听讲座，做了详细的笔记。|He studied the map intently, planning his route. 他专心地研究地图，计划他的路线。|近义词：attentively, carefully, diligently, earnestly|反义词：distractedly, carelessly, inattentively|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *intentīvus, from Latin intentus (“stretched out, strained, intent”), past participle of intendere (“to stretch out, strain, turn one's attention to”).
