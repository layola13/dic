despiting: /dɪˈspaɪtɪŋ/ | prep. | 尽管，虽然 | This word is not standard English. The correct form is "despite." "Despiting" is a non-standard back-formation from "despite." It attempts to create a verb form from the preposition.  While occasionally encountered, it is generally considered incorrect and should be avoided. |  Incorrect: Despiting the rain, we went for a walk. | Correct: Despite the rain, we went for a walk. | 近义词: despite, in spite of, notwithstanding | 反义词: because of, due to, owing to | 词性: preposition (incorrectly used as a participle sometimes)
