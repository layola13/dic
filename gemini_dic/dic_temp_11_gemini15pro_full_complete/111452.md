/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) |pron. det. int. adv.| 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information specifying something. | What time is it? 现在几点了？ | which, who | - | pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? 她晚饭做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to you? 她对你说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she felt sick? 她感到恶心之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about before I arrived? 我到达之前他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will she study at university? 她将在大学学习什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have written by next month? 到下个月她将写完什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will he have been studying by the time he graduates? 到他毕业时，他将已经学习了什么多久？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
What would she say if she knew the truth? 如果她知道真相，她会说什么？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What is your favorite color? 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? 你暑假的计划是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。
