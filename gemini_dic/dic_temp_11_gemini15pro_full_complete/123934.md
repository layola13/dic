morganatic:/ˌmɔːrɡəˈnætɪk/
adjective
贵贱通婚的，婚后妻子与子女无权继承丈夫爵位和财产的
Relating to a marriage in which a person of royal or noble birth marries someone of lower social rank, with the stipulation that neither the spouse nor any children of the marriage will inherit the royal or noble titles or property.
The prince entered into a morganatic marriage with a commoner. 王子与一位平民缔结了贵贱通婚。
Synonyms: left-handed marriage
Antonyms: regular marriage
词性: adjective
