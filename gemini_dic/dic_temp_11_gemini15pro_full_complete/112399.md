lost-to:/lɒst tuː/ /lɔːst tuː/ | adj. | 输给；败给；被…夺走 |  Describes something that has been taken away by something else, often in a competitive context like a game, disease, or natural disaster.  It signifies a loss or defeat. | The game was lost-to the opposing team due to a last-minute goal. 这场比赛由于最后一分钟的进球输给了对方球队。

The ancient city was lost-to the encroaching desert over centuries. 这座古城在几个世纪的时间里被不断侵蚀的沙漠吞噬了。 | 近义词：forfeited to, succumbed to, defeated by | 反义词：won against, victorious over | 词性：形容词 (Adjective)
