amylases: /ˈæmɪleɪzɪz/ | n. | 淀粉酶 |  A group of enzymes that catalyze the hydrolysis of starch into sugars.  | Saliva contains amylases that begin the digestion of starches. 唾液中含有开始消化淀粉的淀粉酶。|  Amylase levels in the blood can be elevated in certain medical conditions.  血液中淀粉酶水平在某些疾病情况下会升高。 | synonyms:  diastase | antonyms: (none, as it's a specific enzyme) | noun
