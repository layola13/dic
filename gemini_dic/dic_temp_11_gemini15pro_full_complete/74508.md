frappeed:/fræˈpiːd/
adjective
冰镇的，加冰沙的
Covered with or mixed with a frappé.  A drink chilled with crushed ice.
例句：I ordered a frappeed coffee on a hot summer day.  我在炎热的夏天点了一杯冰沙咖啡。

例句：The frappeed lemonade was refreshing. 这杯加冰沙的柠檬水很清爽。
近义词：iced, chilled, blended
反义词：hot, warm
词性: adjective

词源: Derived from the French word "frappé," past participle of "frapper" (to strike, chill).  源自法语单词 "frappé"，"frapper"（敲击、冷却）的过去分词。
