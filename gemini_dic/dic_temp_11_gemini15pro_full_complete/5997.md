ambushments:/ˈæmbʊʃmənts/| n.|埋伏，伏击；埋伏地点|An act of lying in wait to attack by surprise.  The act of concealing oneself in order to attack someone by surprise. Places where troops are positioned in ambush.|The soldiers prepared ambushments in the forest, hoping to surprise the enemy. 士兵们在森林里准备了埋伏，希望能突袭敌人。|Near synonyms: ambushes, traps, snares|Near antonyms: open ground, exposed positions|noun
