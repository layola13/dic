A stark, desolate appearance, as of a landscape.|The gauntnesses of the refugees were a stark reminder of the famine.  难民们的憔悴是饥荒的残酷写照。|Synonyms: emaciation, thinness, leanness, haggardness, boniness, starkness, barrenness|Antonyms: plumpness, stoutness, robustness, lushness, fertility|noun
