amorphous:/əˈmɔːrfəs/ | adj. | 无定形的，无组织的 | Having no definite form; shapeless.  Lacking a clear structure or focus. |  The artist preferred to work with amorphous blobs of paint rather than defined lines.  这位艺术家更喜欢用无定形的颜料块而不是清晰的线条进行创作。 |  formless, shapeless, unstructured, unorganized |  definite, structured, organized, crystalline | adjective
