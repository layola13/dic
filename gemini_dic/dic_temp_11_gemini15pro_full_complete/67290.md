exsertile:/ɛkˈsɜːrtaɪl/
adj.
可伸出的，能伸出的
capable of being protruded or extended, as certain insect mouthparts.
The snail's exsertile tentacles explored the surface.  蜗牛的可伸出触角探索着表面。

近义词:protrusible, extensible
反义词:retractile, non-protrusible
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exsertilis, from exsertus, past participle of exserere "to thrust out," from ex- "out" + serere "to join, link, bind, arrange."
