indicatrix: /ɪndɪˈkeɪtrɪks/ | n. |指示函数; 指示线; 指示量|A curve or other figure that indicates properties of a surface or other mathematical object. |The indicatrix of a surface at a point gives information about the curvature at that point.| 近义词: indicator, gauge |反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially something representing "obscuring" or "hiding" depending on context) | noun
