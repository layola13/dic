curiouser:/ˈkjʊəriəsər/| adj.|更奇怪的，更好奇的|A nonstandard or humorous form of "more curious," used for emphasis.|Example: "Curiouser and curiouser!" cried Alice (she was so much surprised, that for the moment she quite forgot how to speak good English). “越来越奇怪了！”爱丽丝喊道（她太惊讶了，以至于一时忘了怎么说好英语了）。|Example:  He gave me a curiouser look than I expected. 他给我的眼神比我想象的还要奇怪。|近义词：stranger, odder, more curious|反义词:less curious, more normal, more ordinary|词性:形容词
