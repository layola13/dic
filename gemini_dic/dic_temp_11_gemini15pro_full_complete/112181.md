Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他靠什么谋生？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching? 你在看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她正在做什么晚餐？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done with the money? 他用这些钱做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on? 你一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading lately? 她最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? 昨晚8点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about? 她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? 我来之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? 会议前他们讨论了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the interruption? 打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? 他们一直在争论什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have done by the time we arrive? 我们到达时他们将已经做了什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时她将已经学习了多久什么？


Subjunctive Mood:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to do. 我希望我知道该怎么办。

Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being built next to the library? 图书馆旁边正在建什么？

Interrogative Sentences:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是哪里？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？

Negative Sentences:
I don't know what to say. 我不知道说什么。
She doesn't understand what I mean. 她不明白我的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up?  怎么了？/你好吗？
What the hell?  搞什么鬼！
What if it rains? 如果下雨怎么办？
What for? 为了什么？
What about going to the cinema? 去看电影怎么样？


注意事项:  以上已按要求格式作答。 注意what作为疑问词，没有直接的反义词。
