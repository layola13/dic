bleaky:/ˈbliːki/| adj.|凄凉的，荒凉的，阴冷的|Exposed and barren and thus cheerless and depressing.  Characterized by or evocative of bleakness; offering little or no hope or encouragement.|The landscape was bleak and windswept.  那片风景荒凉且被风吹扫。|The economic outlook is bleak. 经济前景黯淡。|近义词：desolate, barren, grim, dreary, cheerless|反义词：cheerful, bright, promising, warm, comforting|词性:形容词
