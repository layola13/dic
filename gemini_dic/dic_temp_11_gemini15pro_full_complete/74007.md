fortunes:/ˈfɔːrtʃuːnz/ (US), /ˈfɔːtʃənz/ (UK)| n.| 财富，命运，运气|  A person's fate or luck, especially as it regards wealth or happiness.  It can also refer to a large amount of money or property.|She inherited a large fortune from her grandfather. 她从她祖父那里继承了一大笔财富。|Synonyms: wealth, riches, prosperity, assets, destiny, fate, luck|Antonyms: poverty, misfortune, adversity, hardship|noun

fortunes:/ˈfɔːrtʃuːnz/ (US), /ˈfɔːtʃənz/ (UK)| n.|  命运，际遇 (plural)|The varying occurrences that happen to a person during their life.|The fortunes of war can change quickly. 战争的命运瞬息万变。|Synonyms: fate, destiny, lot, circumstances|Antonyms: N/A (This sense doesn't have a direct antonym)|noun
