mesaticephalous: /ˌmɛsɪˈsɛfələs/ | adj. | 中头型的 | Having a head of medium proportions; specifically : having a cephalic index between 75 and 80.  |  Example: The skull found at the archaeological site was determined to be mesaticephalous.  考古现场发现的头骨被确定为中头型。 | Example:  Mesaticephalic individuals typically have head shapes that are neither particularly long nor particularly round. 中头型个体通常头部形状既不特别长也不特别圆。 | Synonym: mesocephalic | Antonym: brachycephalic, dolichocephalic | word type: adjective
