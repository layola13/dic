nonautonomous:/ˌnɒnɔːˈtɒnəməs/
adjective
非自主的；非自治的
Not autonomous; not self-governing; subject to external forces or control.
The nonautonomous region relies heavily on the central government for funding.  这个非自治地区严重依赖中央政府的资金。
A nonautonomous differential equation is one that explicitly depends on the independent variable. 一个非自治微分方程是一个明确依赖于自变量的方程。
近义词:dependent, subordinate, controlled
反义词:autonomous, independent, self-governing
词性:adjective
