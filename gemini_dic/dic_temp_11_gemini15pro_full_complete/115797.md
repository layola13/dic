maritime:/ˈmærɪtaɪm/ | adj. | 海事的，海运的，航海的 | Connected with the sea, especially in relation to seafaring commercial or military activity. |  例句：The country has a long and proud maritime tradition.  这个国家有着悠久而自豪的海上传统。

例句：Maritime law governs ships and sailors. 海事法管辖船舶和海员。 | 近义词：naval, marine, oceanic | 反义词：landlocked, inland | 词性：形容词
