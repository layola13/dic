gertas: /ˈɡɛrtɑːs/ | n. |  围裙; 工作服 |  A gertas is a type of apron or smock, often worn as a protective garment over clothing while working. It's derived from the Lithuanian word "gerda," meaning a type of woven fabric. Historically, gertas were common in Lithuania and surrounding regions, often decorated with intricate woven patterns.  |  She wore a gertas over her dress while cooking. 她做饭时在连衣裙外面穿了一件围裙。 |  apron, smock, overall |  - | noun
