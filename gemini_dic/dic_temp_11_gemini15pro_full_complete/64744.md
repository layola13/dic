escorting: /ɪˈskɔːrtɪŋ/ | n. |护送，陪同|The act of accompanying someone or something, especially for protection or guidance.|例句：The police are escorting the president. 警察正在护送总统。|近义词：accompanying, guiding, guarding, shepherding|反义词:abandoning, leaving|词性:noun (present participle of "escort")
escort: /ˈeskɔːrt/ | vt. n.|护送，陪同；护卫队，护送者|To accompany someone or something, especially for protection or guidance; a person or group of people who accompany someone or something for protection or guidance.|例句：He escorted her to the door. 他护送她到门口。|近义词：accompany, guide, guard, shepherd|反义词:abandon, leave|词性:verb, noun
词源: From Old French *escorter, from Italian scorta 'escort, convoy', from scorgere 'to perceive, guide', from Latin excorrigere 'to correct'.
