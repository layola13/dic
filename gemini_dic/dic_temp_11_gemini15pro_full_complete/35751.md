cochleare:/kɒkliˈɛəriː/ /kɑːkliˈɛəriː/
n.
spoon; spoonful; a spoon, especially as a measure in pharmacy
A cochleare is a unit of measurement, roughly equivalent to a teaspoon, used primarily in historical pharmaceutical contexts.
The recipe called for one cochleare of honey.  这药方需要一勺蜂蜜。

反义词:(none)
