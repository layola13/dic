contemplative:/ˈkɒntəmpleɪtɪv/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntəmpleɪtɪv/ (US)| adj.|沉思的；冥想的；深思熟虑的|Given to or characterized by contemplation; deeply or seriously thoughtful.|She sat in a contemplative pose, gazing out at the sea. 她坐着沉思的姿势，凝视着大海。|近义词：thoughtful, reflective, meditative, pensive, introspective|反义词：rash, impulsive, unreflective|词性:形容词

contemplative:/ˈkɒntəmpleɪtɪv/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntəmpleɪtɪv/ (US)| n.|沉思者|A person given to contemplation.|He was a contemplative, preferring solitude to social gatherings. 他是一个沉思者，喜欢独处胜过社交聚会。|近义词：thinker, meditator|反义词：extrovert|词性:名词
