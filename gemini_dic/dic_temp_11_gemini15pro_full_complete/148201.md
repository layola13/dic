overshoes:/ˈoʊvərˌʃuːz/ (US), /ˈəʊvəˌʃuːz/ (UK)| n.|套鞋，鞋套|A protective outer covering for shoes, typically made of rubber or plastic, worn over shoes to protect them from water, snow, or mud.|I always wear overshoes when it's raining.  下雨天我总是穿套鞋。
It's muddy outside, so put on your overshoes. 外面很泥泞，穿上你的套鞋。|近义词：galoshes, rubbers, boots|反义词: sandals, slippers|名词
