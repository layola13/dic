chanfrin:/ˈtʃænfrin/| n.| 鼻梁；马的额部|The horse's chanfrin was wide and flat. 这匹马的鼻梁又宽又平。|The blow landed squarely on the chanfrin. 这一击正好打在鼻梁上。|近义词：bridge of the nose, forehead (for horses)|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old French *chanfrein, from Vulgar Latin *camprena, from Latin *campa, "field, plain".  Originally referred to the piece of armor protecting a horse's forehead.
