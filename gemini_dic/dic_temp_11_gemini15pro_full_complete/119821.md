miko:/ˈmiːkoʊ/ (US), /ˈmiːkəʊ/ (UK)| n.|巫女（日本神道教神社中的女性侍奉者）|A shrine maiden in Japan, often a young woman, who assists in Shinto rituals and ceremonies. |例句：The miko performed a ritual dance at the shrine.  巫女在神社里表演了仪式舞蹈。

例句：She trained to become a miko at a young age. 她从小就接受训练成为一名巫女。|近义词：priestess, shrine maiden|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
