hybridist:/ˈhaɪbrɪdɪst/
n.
杂交者，杂交育种家
A person who produces hybrids, especially of plants or animals.
Luther Burbank was a famous hybridist who developed many new varieties of fruits and vegetables. 
路德·伯班克是一位著名的杂交学家，他培育了许多新的水果和蔬菜品种。

Gregor Mendel, through his experiments with pea plants, is considered the father of modern genetics, although he wasn't strictly a hybridist in the modern sense.
格雷戈尔·孟德尔通过豌豆植物实验被认为是现代遗传学之父，尽管严格意义上来说，他并不是现代意义上的杂交学家。


Synonym:
crossbreeder, hybridizer

Antonym:
(No direct antonym, but one could consider a purist who breeds only purebred organisms)
