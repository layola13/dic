haughtinesses:/ˈhɔːtinɪsɪz/
|n.|傲慢，自大|The state of being haughty; arrogance.  A display of superiority and disdain.|Her haughtinesses alienated her from her colleagues. 她的傲慢使她疏远了同事。|近义词: arrogance, pride, disdain, superciliousness|反义词: humility, modesty, humbleness|词性: 名词 (plural of haughtiness)
Etymology: From Middle English *hauteinesse*, from Old French *hauteinesse*, *hauteté*, from *haut* (“high”) ultimately from Latin *altus*.
