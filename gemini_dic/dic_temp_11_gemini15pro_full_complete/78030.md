gentry:/ˈdʒɛntri/| n.| 绅士阶层，贵族|People of good social position, specifically those ranking next below the nobility in position and birth.|The local gentry held a lavish ball. 当地绅士阶层举办了一场奢华的舞会。|近义词：aristocracy, nobility, elite|反义词：commoners, peasantry|词性:noun

词源:
From Old French *gentrie "noble birth, nobility," from gentil "noble" (see gentle (adj.)).
