lecanorin:/ˌlɛkəˈnɔːrɪn/| adj. |Lecanorin, 地衣酸的一种 |A crystalline compound present in various lichens.  It's a type of depsidone.|This lichen contains lecanorin.  这种地衣含有地衣酸。|近义词:depsidone (more general), or other specific lichen compounds like atranorin or usnic acid.|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From lecanoric acid, named after *Lecanora*, a genus of lichens, + -in (chemical suffix indicating a neutral substance). *Lecanora* comes from the Greek *lekane* (dish, basin), referring to the disc-like shape of the lichen's fruiting bodies.
