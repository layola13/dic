discrown:/dɪsˈkraʊn/
verb
废黜；革除王位
To remove a crown from; to depose from royal authority.
The rebellious subjects discrowned the king and banished him from the kingdom.  叛乱的臣民废黜了国王，并把他驱逐出王国。

discrown:/dɪsˈkraʊn/
verb
剥夺…的最高荣誉；使…失去冠军称号
To deprive of the highest honor; to dethrone from a position of supremacy.
The team was discrowned after their shocking defeat in the final.  在决赛中令人震惊的失利后，该队失去了冠军头衔。


近义词:depose, dethrone, unseat
反义词:crown, enthrone, invest
