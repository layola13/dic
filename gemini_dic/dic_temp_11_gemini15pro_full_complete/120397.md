ministerialism:/ˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəlɪzəm/
|n.|部长制；内阁制；牧师职务|The prevalence of ministerialism can sometimes lead to a lack of broader public engagement in policy decisions. 部长制的盛行有时会导致公众缺乏更广泛地参与政策决策。|Ministerialism is a system of government in which the executive power is vested in a cabinet of ministers. 部长制是一种政府体制，行政权属于内阁部长。|近义词: cabinet system, ministerial government|反义词: presidentialism|noun
