counter-disengage:/ˌkaʊntər dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/
| v. |反脱离接触，反解脱|To disengage from something in response to another's disengagement. It often implies a reciprocal action, where one party disengages after or because another party has disengaged.|例句：After the enemy troops counter-disengaged, our forces advanced.  敌军反脱离接触后，我军向前推进。

例句：The company counter-disengaged from the merger talks after the other party backed out. 在对方退出后，该公司也从合并谈判中撤出。|近义词：withdraw, retreat, disengage|反义词：engage, advance, attack|词性：动词
