geopoliticists:/ˌdʒiːoʊpəˈlɪtɪsɪsts/
| n. |地缘政治学家|A specialist in geopolitics, which is the study of the influence of geography on politics and international relations.|Geopoliticists predict increasing tensions in the Arctic as resources become more accessible. 地缘政治学家预测，随着资源变得更容易获取，北极地区的紧张局势将会加剧。|Synonyms: geopolitical analysts, international relations experts, foreign policy specialists|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps) pacifists, isolationists|noun
