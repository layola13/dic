enlightenedness:/ɪnˈlaɪtndnɪs/| n.|启迪，觉悟，开明|The state of being enlightened; having or showing a rational, tolerant, and unbiased outlook.|He showed great enlightenedness in his approach to the problem. 他在处理这个问题时表现出了极大的开明。|近义词：awareness, wisdom, understanding, insight, enlightenment|反义词：ignorance, blindness, prejudice, bigotry|词性:名词

词源:  From enlighten + -ed + -ness.  Enlighten comes from Old French enluminer, ultimately from Latin illuminare "to illuminate."
