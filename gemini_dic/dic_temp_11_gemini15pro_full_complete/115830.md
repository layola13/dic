marketeers:/ˈmɑːrkɪtərz/ | n. |市场营销人员；市场商人|People who work in marketing, promoting and selling products or services. | The marketeers are launching a new advertising campaign next week. 市场营销人员下周将开展新的广告宣传活动。 |  marketers, marketing professionals, sales representatives |  consumers, customers | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "market" (ultimately from Latin "mercatus," meaning "trade, market") + "-eer" (a suffix denoting a person engaged in a particular activity).
