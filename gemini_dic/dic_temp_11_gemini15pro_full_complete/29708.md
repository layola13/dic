Example:  His catholicity of mind allowed him to appreciate diverse perspectives. 他思想的包容性使他能够欣赏不同的观点。 | Synonyms: universality, comprehensiveness, breadth, ecumenism, tolerance | Antonyms: narrowness, intolerance, bigotry, provincialism | Noun
