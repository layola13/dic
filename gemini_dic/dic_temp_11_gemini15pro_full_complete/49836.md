demonstratorship:/ˌdɛmənˈstreɪtərʃɪp/
|n.|助教职位；助教工作|A position as a demonstrator, especially in a scientific subject at a university.|She held a demonstratorship in the physics department. 她持有物理系的助教职位。|synonyms: assistantship, instructorship|antonyms: professorship|noun


demonstratorship:/ˌdɛmənˈstreɪtərʃɪp/
|n.|演示；示范|An act of demonstrating something, typically a skill or process.|The chef gave a demonstratorship of cake decorating techniques.  这位厨师演示了蛋糕装饰技巧。|synonyms: demonstration, exhibition, display|antonyms: concealment, hiding|noun
