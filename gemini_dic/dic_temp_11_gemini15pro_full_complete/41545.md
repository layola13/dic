coronis:/kəˈroʊnɪs/ | n. | 曲线符号，省略符号 (Especially in ancient Greek texts: a mark used to indicate a contraction or other modification of the text.) | A hook-shaped mark used by ancient Greek scribes to indicate a contraction or other change in the text, typically the omission of one or more letters. |  The ancient scribe used a coronis to indicate that a section of text had been omitted. 古代抄写员用曲线符号表示省略了一段文字。

The editor noted the use of a coronis in the original manuscript. 编辑注意到原始手稿中使用了曲线符号。| 近义词:  brachylogy, ellipsis | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
