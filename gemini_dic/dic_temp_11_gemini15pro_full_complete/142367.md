orthophoric: /ˌɔːrθəˈfɒrɪk/ /ˌɔːrθəˈfɔːrɪk/
| adj. | 正向的，正常的 |  Relating to or characterized by normalphoria, the condition in which the visual axes of the two eyes are parallel when the eyes are at rest and focused on a distant object. |  After the surgery, her vision was orthophoric, and she no longer experienced double vision.  手术后，她的视力恢复正常，不再出现复视。| Synonyms: normal, aligned | Antonyms: heterophoric, strabismic | adjective
