overcurious:/ˌoʊvərˈkjʊəriəs/| adj.| 过分好奇的；爱管闲事的| Excessively inquisitive; unduly curious about the affairs of others.  Having too much curiosity, especially about other people's affairs.| She was overcurious about her neighbors' lives, always peeking through her curtains. 她对邻居的生活过分好奇，总是透过窗帘偷看。| The overcurious child kept asking "why?" after every explanation.  这个过分好奇的孩子在每次解释后都不断地问“为什么？” | 近义词：nosy, inquisitive, prying, meddling | 反义词：indifferent, apathetic, unconcerned | 词性: adjective
