attribute:/ˈætrɪbjuːt/ (US), /əˈtrɪbjuːt/ (UK)| n. vt.| 属性，特性；归属，归因|A quality, feature, or characteristic that belongs to a person or thing.  As a verb, it means to regard something as being caused by someone or something.|

例句：
Kindness is an important attribute.
善良是一个重要的品质。

He attributed his success to hard work.
他把他的成功归因于努力工作。


近义词：characteristic, quality, trait, feature (n.); ascribe, assign, credit (vt.)|
反义词: N/A (n.) ; deny, refute (vt.)|
词性: noun, verb|
