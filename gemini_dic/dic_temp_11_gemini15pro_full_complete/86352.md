hectocotylized:/ˌhɛktəˈkɒtɪlaɪzd/ | adj. | 交接腕化的，茎化腕化的 | Having a hectocotylus; specifically : having an arm specialized for transferring spermatophores.  A hectocotylus is one of the arms of a male cephalopod (such as an octopus or squid) that is modified for delivering sperm to the female.| The male octopus uses its hectocotylized arm to transfer sperm packets to the female. 雄性章鱼用其交接腕化的触手将精子包传递给雌性。| Near synonyms:  modified (arm), specialized (arm) | Antonyms: unmodified (arm)
词性: 形容词
