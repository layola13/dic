fits:/fɪts/| v. n.| (动词)适合，安装；(名词)一阵，发作|The dress fits her perfectly. 这件衣服非常适合她。| synonyms: suits, matches, becomes; antonyms: misfits, clashes| verb

fits:/fɪts/| n.| 一阵，发作|He suffers from fits of anger. 他容易发怒。| synonyms: seizures, spasms, convulsions; antonyms: calmness, tranquility| noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fitt, a division of a song or poem, from Proto-Germanic *fitiz.  The sense of "a sudden attack (of illness, emotion, etc.)" developed from the notion of a sudden onset or burst.  The sense of "something that fits" is from the verb "to fit."

PHRASE->
fit in: 融入；适应  She fits in well with her new colleagues. 她和新同事相处融洽。
fit out: 装备；配备 The ship was fitted out with the latest technology. 这艘船配备了最新的技术。
fit up:  诬陷；陷害 He claimed he had been fitted up by the police. 他声称他被警察陷害了。
fit (someone/something) with (something):  为…配备…  They fitted the car with new tires. 他们给汽车装了新轮胎。
fit the bill: 符合要求；正合适 This candidate fits the bill perfectly. 这个候选人完全符合要求。
