impart:/ɪmˈpɑːrt/ | vt. |给予，传授；告知，透露|To communicate information, knowledge, or a quality to someone or something.|She imparted her wisdom to her students. 她把自己的智慧传授给学生们。|The spice imparts an exotic flavor to the dish.  这种香料给菜肴增添了异国风味。|Synonyms: communicate, convey, transmit, disclose, reveal|Antonyms: withhold, conceal, suppress|verb
