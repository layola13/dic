georama:/ˌdʒɪəˈrɑːmə/ /ˌdʒɪəˈræmə/| n. |全景画，西洋景；立体透视画|A large, often cylindrical painting or other representation of a landscape or other scene, viewed from the inside, giving a panoramic effect. |例句：The georama provided a 360-degree view of the battle of Gettysburg. 这幅全景画提供了葛底斯堡战役的360度全景。

例句：Children were fascinated by the georama depicting the Amazon rainforest. 孩子们被描绘亚马逊雨林的全景画迷住了。|近义词：panorama, diorama, cyclorama|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *géorama*, from *géo-* (from Greek *gē* "earth") + *-orama* (from Greek *horama* "sight, spectacle").
