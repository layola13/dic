bushing:/ˈbʊʃɪŋ/| n.|衬套;套管;轴衬|A bushing is a type of bearing, a cylindrical lining that allows one part to turn inside another. It reduces friction and wear.|The mechanic replaced the worn bushing in the suspension.  机械师更换了悬架中磨损的衬套。

The door hinge squeaks because the bushing is dry. 门铰发出吱吱声，因为衬套干了。|近义词:bearing, sleeve, liner|反义词:(No direct antonym)|noun
