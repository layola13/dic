fortuitousness:/fɔːrˌtuːɪtəsnəs/
n.
偶然性；意外性
The quality or fact of being fortuitous; chance or luck.
The fortuitousness of the meeting led to a fruitful collaboration. 这次会议的偶然促成了一次富有成效的合作。
The professor stressed the fortuitousness of the discovery. 教授强调了这一发现的偶然性。
Synonyms:  chance, luck, accident, serendipity, coincidence
Antonyms:  intention, design, plan, purpose
noun
