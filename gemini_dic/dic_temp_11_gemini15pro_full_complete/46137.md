cymidine: /ˈsaɪmɪdiːn/ | n. | 西咪替丁 |  An antihistamine drug (trade name Tagamet) used to treat peptic ulcers by reducing the production of stomach acid. It's a histamine H2-receptor antagonist. |  He was prescribed cymidine for his stomach ulcers. 他被开了西咪替丁来治疗胃溃疡。| Synonyms: Tagamet, H2 blocker | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but potentially antacids which neutralize acid rather than prevent its production) | noun
