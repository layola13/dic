nonfrugally:/ˌnɒnˈfruːɡəli/ | adv. | 不节俭地，奢侈地 | Not frugally; in a way that spends or uses a lot of resources. |  They lived nonfrugally, spending lavishly on designer clothes and exotic vacations. 他们生活奢侈，在名牌服装和异国度假上挥霍无度。 |  She furnished her apartment nonfrugally, opting for antique furniture and imported rugs. 她不节俭地布置了她的公寓，选择了古董家具和进口地毯。 | 近义词: lavishly, extravagantly, profligately | 反义词: frugally, economically, thriftily | 词性: 副词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + frugally (adverb from frugal, from Latin *frugalis*, from *frux* "fruit, enjoyment, profit")
