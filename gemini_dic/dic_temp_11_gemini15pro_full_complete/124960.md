muffy:/ˈmʌfi/| adj.|闷热的，不通风的；(非正式) 优越的，自命不凡的|A stuffy room or building is unpleasantly warm and has no fresh air. (informal) A stuffy person is very serious, formal, and old-fashioned.|The room was hot and stuffy. 房间又热又闷。|近义词：close, airless, stifling, suffocating; pompous, formal, conventional|反义词：airy, breezy; informal, casual|词性:形容词

muffy:/ˈmʌfi/| n.|围巾，披肩；暖手筒|A muff is a tube-shaped piece of fur or other warm material, open at both ends, into which you put your hands to keep them warm.|She wore a fur muff. 她戴着毛皮暖手筒。|近义词：hand warmer|反义词:|词性:名词
