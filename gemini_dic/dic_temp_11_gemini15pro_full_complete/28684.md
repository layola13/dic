carjackings:/ˈkɑːrdʒækɪŋz/
|n.|劫车(行为), 汽车抢劫|The act of stealing a car by force or threat of force, often while the driver is still in the vehicle.|Several carjackings have been reported in the area recently. 最近该地区发生了多起劫车事件。|Synonyms: auto theft, car theft, grand theft auto|Antonyms: N/A (Difficult to have a direct antonym for a criminal act)|noun

carjacking:/ˈkɑːrdʒækɪŋ/
|n.|劫车(一次), 汽车抢劫(单次事件)|The act of stealing a car by force or threat of force, often while the driver is still in the vehicle.|The carjacking occurred at a stoplight. 劫车事件发生在红绿灯路口。|Synonyms: auto theft, car theft, grand theft auto|Antonyms: N/A (Difficult to have a direct antonym for a criminal act)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "car" + "hijack," altered by analogy with "skyjacking."  "Hijack" itself comes from "highjack," originally slang for robbing truckers or other highway travelers.
