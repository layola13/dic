humanizer:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzər/| n.|人性化者；使更具人情味的事物|Something that humanizes; makes more humane. |The novel served as a humanizer, showing the struggles of refugees. 这部小说起到了人性化的作用，展现了难民的挣扎。|synonyms: civilizer, refiner, softener|antonyms: brutalizer, dehumanizer|noun

humanize:/ˈhjuːmənaɪz/| v.|使人性化；使更具人情味|To make something more humane or civilized; to endow with human qualities or attributes.|The company tried to humanize its image by sponsoring a local charity.  这家公司试图通过赞助当地慈善机构来提升其形象，使其更具人情味。|synonyms: civilize, refine, soften|antonyms: brutalize, dehumanize|verb
