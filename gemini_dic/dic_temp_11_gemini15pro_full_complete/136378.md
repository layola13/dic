nonresponsively:/ˌnɒnrɪˈspɒnsɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈspɑːnsɪvli/
adjective
无反应地，不响应地
In a manner that does not respond or react.
The patient lay nonresponsively on the bed. 病人毫无反应地躺在床上。
He stared nonresponsively at the wall. 他毫无反应地盯着墙。
Synonyms: unresponsively, inertly, passively
Antonyms: responsively, reactively, actively
词性: 副词
