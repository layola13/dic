musculin:/ˈmʌskjʊlɪn/| n.| 肌球蛋白 |A globular protein that forms filaments that make up nearly half of the protein in muscle and are involved in muscle contraction.| Muscle fibers contain both actin and musculin filaments. |肌纤维包含肌动蛋白和肌球蛋白丝。| 近义词: myosin | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially actin as a functional opposite in muscle contraction) | 词性: noun
