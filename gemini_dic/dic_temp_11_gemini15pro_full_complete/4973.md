alkalinities:/ˌæl.kəˈlɪn.ɪ.tiz/
n.
碱度；碱性
the capacity of water to neutralize acids, measured by the amount of acid needed to bring the pH to a specified value.
例句：The alkalinities of the samples were measured using titration.  我们用滴定法测量了样品的碱度。

例句：High alkalinities can be detrimental to aquatic life.  高碱度可能对水生生物有害。

近义词：basicities, alkaline properties
反义词：acidities, acidic properties
词性：noun
