neighbor:/ˈneɪbər/ (US) /ˈneɪbə/ (UK)| n.|邻居；邻国；邻近的人或事物|A person who lives next door or very near to you.  A place or thing that is next to or near another.|例句：My neighbor always waters her garden in the morning. 我的邻居总是在早上浇灌她的花园。|例句：Canada is a neighbor to the United States. 加拿大是美国的邻国。|近义词：resident, dweller, local|反义词:stranger, foreigner|词性:noun

neighbor:/ˈneɪbər/ (US) /ˈneɪbə/ (UK)| v.|与…为邻；靠近|To live or be situated next to or near.|例句：We neighbored a family with three young children. 我们和一个有三个小孩的家庭为邻。|例句：Our property neighbors a national park. 我们的房产靠近一个国家公园。|近义词：adjoin, abut|反义词: - |词性:verb
