intextine:/ɪnˈtɛkstɪn/| n. |内壁；内膜|The inner layer of the pollen grain wall, lying between the exine and the intine.|The pollen tube emerges through a pore in the intextine. 花粉管从内壁上的一个孔中长出。|近义词:endospore|反义词:exine|词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin *intextus* (woven inside) from *in-* (in) + *textus* (woven), past participle of *texere* (to weave).
