archilochian: /ˌɑːrkɪˈlɒkiən/ | adj. |  阿基洛库斯式的；辛辣讽刺的；粗俗的 |  Of or relating to the Greek poet Archilochus, known for his personal and often bitter invective. Characterized by directness, personal invective, and a mixture of elevated and colloquial language. | His archilochian poems were full of biting satire and personal attacks.  他的阿基洛库斯式的诗歌充满了辛辣的讽刺和人身攻击。|  incisive, satirical, biting, sardonic, trenchant |  complimentary, flattering | adjective
