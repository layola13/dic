mortally:/ˈmɔːrtəli/| adv.|致命地；非常；极其|In a way that is likely to cause death; to a very great degree.|He was mortally wounded in the battle. 他在战斗中受了致命伤。|Synonyms: fatally, lethally, terminally|Antonyms: slightly, superficially|Adverb

mortally:/ˈmɔːrtəli/| adj.|(literary) Subject to death.|Humans are all mortally beings. 人类都是终有一死的。|Synonyms: human, earthly|Antonyms: immortal, eternal|Adjective
