overaffirmative:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɜːrmətɪv/ | adj. | 过于肯定的；过分积极的 | Excessively affirmative; too eager to agree or support.  Characterized by excessive or unwarranted affirmation. |  He had an overaffirmative attitude, agreeing to everything without considering the consequences. 他态度过于肯定，不考虑后果就同意一切。| 近义词: overenthusiastic, overeager, excessively positive | 反义词: negative, hesitant, skeptical | 词性: 形容词
