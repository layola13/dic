electrostatic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪk/| adj.|静电的；静电学的|Relating to stationary electric charges or fields as opposed to electric currents.|Electrostatic discharge can damage electronic components. 静电放电会损坏电子元件。|近义词:static|反义词:electrodynamic|词性:形容词

electrostatics:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪks/| n.|静电学|The branch of physics dealing with stationary electric charges or fields.|Electrostatics is a fundamental concept in physics. 静电学是物理学中的一个基本概念。|近义词:static electricity|反义词:electrodynamics|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From electro- + static.  "Electro-" comes from the Greek "elektron" meaning "amber" (referring to the early observation of static electricity through rubbing amber). "Static" comes from the Greek "statikos" meaning "causing to stand," referring to the stationary nature of the charges.
