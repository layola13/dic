inharmonicity:/ˌɪnhɑːrməˈnɪsɪti/
n.|不和谐，非谐性|Lack of harmony or agreement; dissonance.  The degree to which the frequencies of overtones of a note deviate from whole number multiples of the fundamental frequency.|The inharmonicity of the piano's upper register contributes to its distinctive sound. 钢琴高音的不和谐性构成了其独特的声音。|disharmony, dissonance, discord|harmony, consonance, concord|noun

inharmonic:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmɑːnɪk/
adj.|不和谐的，非谐的|Not harmonic; lacking harmony; discordant.|The inharmonic sounds grated on her ears. 不和谐的声音刺激着她的耳朵。|discordant, dissonant, jarring|harmonic, consonant, melodious|adjective
