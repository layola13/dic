foraminiferous:/ˌfɒrəmɪˈnɪfərəs/
adj.
有孔虫的；含/产有孔虫的
Containing or producing foraminifera.
Chalk is a foraminiferous rock. 白垩是一种含有有孔虫的岩石。
The ocean floor is covered with foraminiferous ooze. 海底覆盖着有孔虫软泥。
近义词: foram-bearing
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "barren" in specific contexts)
词性: Adjective
