fertilizabilities:/ˌfɜːrtɪlaɪzəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
受精能力；可施肥性
The fertilizabilities of different soil types vary greatly. 不同土壤类型的可施肥性差异很大。
The study examined the fertilizabilities of various organic composts.  这项研究检测了各种有机堆肥的可施肥性。
近义词: fertility, fecundity
反义词: sterility, infertility
词性: noun (plural)

fertility:/fərˈtɪləti/
noun
肥沃；生育力
The fertility of the soil is crucial for a good harvest. 土壤的肥沃对于丰收至关重要。
The couple struggled with infertility. 这对夫妇与不孕不育作斗争。
近义词: fecundity, productiveness
反义词: sterility, barrenness
词性: noun

fecundity:/fɪˈkʌndəti/
noun
多产；丰饶；肥沃
The fecundity of the rainforest is astonishing.  热带雨林的丰饶令人惊叹。
The rabbit's fecundity is well known. 兔子的繁殖力是众所周知的。
近义词: fertility, productiveness
反义词: sterility, barrenness
词性: noun


词源: From fertilize + -ability + -es (plural suffix).  Fertilize comes from  fertile (from Latin fertilis) + -ize (verb suffix).
