nosily:/ˈnɔɪzɪli/ (US), /ˈnɔɪzɪli/ (UK)| adv.|吵闹地；喧闹地|In a noisy manner; making a lot of noise.|The children played nosily in the garden. 孩子们在花园里吵闹地玩耍。|They ate their snacks nosily during the movie. 他们在看电影时大声地吃零食。|近义词：loudly, boisterously, clamorously|反义词：quietly, silently|词性:副词

nosy:/ˈnoʊzi/ (US), /ˈnəʊzi/ (UK)| adj.|爱管闲事的；八卦的|Too interested in other people's private affairs; inquisitive.|She's a nosy neighbor who always wants to know what everyone is doing. 她是一个爱管闲事的邻居，总是想知道每个人在做什么。|He gave me a nosy look. 他好奇地看了我一眼。|近义词：inquisitive, curious, prying|反义词：uninterested, indifferent|词性:形容词
