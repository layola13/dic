baroness:/ˈbærənɪs/| n. |男爵夫人；女男爵|A woman who holds the rank of baron or the wife of a baron. |例句：The baroness hosted a lavish party at her estate. 男爵夫人在她的庄园举办了一场奢华的派对。

例句：She was elevated to the peerage as a baroness. 她被提升为贵族，成为了一位男爵夫人。|近义词：noblewoman, peeress|反义词: commoner|词性:noun

词源: From Old French baronesse, from baron + -esse. Ultimately from Frankish *baro.
