mazes:/ˈmeɪzɪz/| n.|迷宫（maze的复数形式）|A complex network of paths or passages, typically having high walls, designed as a puzzle through which one has to find a way.|We got lost in the corn mazes at the farm. 我们在农场的玉米迷宫里迷路了。|The movie's plot was a confusing maze of twists and turns. 这部电影的情节错综复杂，充满了曲折。|labyrinths, networks|paths, roads|noun
