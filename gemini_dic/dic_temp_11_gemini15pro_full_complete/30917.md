chain-store:/ˈtʃeɪn stɔːr/ (US) /ˈtʃeɪn stɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |连锁店|A group of similar shops owned and operated by the same company, typically selling standardized merchandise.|Many high streets are dominated by chain-stores.|Synonyms: multiple store, retail chain, chain retailer|Antonyms: independent store, local business|noun

chain-store:/ˈtʃeɪn stɔːr/ (US) /ˈtʃeɪn stɔː(r)/ (UK)| adj. |连锁店的|Relating to or characteristic of a chain store.|The chain-store mentality often prioritizes efficiency over customer service.|Synonyms: multiple, corporate|Antonyms: independent, local|adjective
