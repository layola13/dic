honoraries: /ˈɑːnəreriz/ | n. | 荣誉称号；荣誉职位；酬金 | Fees paid to people who hold honorary positions, especially in a university.  A position given as an honour, without the usual requirements or salary. |  The university president receives no salary, only honoraries. 大学主席不领薪水，只领取荣誉津贴。| synonyms:  honorarium, stipend, fee, emolument | antonyms:  salary, wage | noun

honorary: /ˈɑːnəreri/ | adj. | 荣誉的；名誉的 |  Conferred as an honour, without the usual requirements or salary. | He was given an honorary doctorate from Harvard. 他被授予哈佛大学荣誉博士学位。| synonyms:  titular, nominal, ceremonial | antonyms:  earned, salaried, paid | adjective
