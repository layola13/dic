deformedly:/dɪˈfɔːrmɪdli/
adverb
畸形地；丑陋地
in a deformed manner; misshapenly.  The branch grew deformedly after being broken and then healing.
The sculpture was deformedly cast, resulting in a lopsided figure.
Synonym: grotesquely, misshapenly, contortedly
Antonym: perfectly, symmetrically, flawlessly
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From deform + -ed + -ly.  "Deform" comes from the Old French "deformer,"  and ultimately from the Latin "deformare"  (to disfigure), which combines "de-" (from, away from) and "formare" (to form, shape).
