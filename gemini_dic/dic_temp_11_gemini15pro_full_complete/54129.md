disencumber:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkʌmbər/| v. | 解除…的负担；使摆脱累赘；使脱离障碍 | To free from something that burdens or obstructs. |  The company is selling off assets to disencumber itself of debt.  该公司正在出售资产以摆脱债务。

The hiker disencumbered himself of his heavy backpack.  徒步旅行者卸下了沉重的背包。| 近义词：free, liberate, relieve, rid, unburden | 反义词：burden, encumber, hamper, obstruct |词性：动词
