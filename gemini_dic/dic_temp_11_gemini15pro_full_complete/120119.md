mimicry:/ˈmɪmɪkri/| n.|模仿; 拟态|The comedian's mimicry of the president was spot-on.  这位喜剧演员对总统的模仿惟妙惟肖。|The viceroy butterfly uses mimicry to protect itself from predators.  总督蝴蝶利用拟态来保护自己免受捕食者的侵害。|近义词：imitation, impersonation, copying, simulation|反义词：originality, authenticity|词性: noun

Etymology: From French *mimicrie*, from *mimique* (“mimicry”), from Greek *μιμικός* (*mimikos*, “imitating, mimetic”), from *μίμος* (*mimos*, “mime, imitator”).
