mortalism:/ˈmɔːrtəlɪzəm/| n. |必死性，死亡率；凡人说；人类中心说|The belief that the soul is mortal and dies with the body.  相信灵魂会死亡并随身体一起消亡的信念。|His mortalism led him to focus on earthly pleasures. 他的必死性使他专注于尘世的享乐。|近义词：mortality, finitude|反义词：immortality, eternalism|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *mortalismus, from Latin mortalis (“mortal”) + -ismus. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mer- (“to die”).
