nonpreferabilities:/ˌnɒnprɛfərəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 非偏好性，非优选性 | The state or quality of not being preferred; the collection of options that are not preferred.  This term is often used in economics and decision theory to describe options that are less desirable compared to others. |  The analysis of consumer choice often involves understanding nonpreferabilities. 消费者选择分析通常涉及理解非偏好性。

The economist discussed the nonpreferabilities of certain investment strategies. 经济学家讨论了某些投资策略的非优选性。 | 近义词: undesirabilities, disadvantages, drawbacks | 反义词: preferences, advantages, benefits | 词性: noun

词源:
non- (prefix): not
preferable (adjective): more desirable or suitable
-ity (suffix): forming nouns indicating state or quality
-s (suffix): plural form
