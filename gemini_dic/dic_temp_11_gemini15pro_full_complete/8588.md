anticlastic:/ˌæntiklæˈstɪk/ | adj. |  反向曲线的，鞍形的 |  Curved in opposite directions, like a saddle.  A surface that curves upward in one direction and downward in the perpendicular direction. | The Pringles potato chip is a familiar example of an anticlastic surface.  薯片就是一个常见的反向曲面例子。| The architect used anticlastic forms in the roof design. 建筑师在屋顶设计中使用了反向曲线的形式。 |  synonym: saddle-shaped, hyperbolic | antonym: synclastic, spherical | adjective
