dhoti:/ˈdʱoʊtiː/
n.
缠腰布，印度传统服饰
A single piece of cloth worn wrapped around the waist and legs, traditionally by men in India.
He wore a crisp white dhoti. 他穿着一件洁白的缠腰布。
synonyms: lunghi, mundu
antonyms: trousers, pants
noun

dhotis:/ˈdʱoʊtiːz/
n.
缠腰布（复数）
Plural form of dhoti.
He purchased several colorful dhotis at the market.  他在市场上买了几条色彩鲜艳的缠腰布。
synonyms: lungis, mundus
antonyms: trousers, pants
noun
