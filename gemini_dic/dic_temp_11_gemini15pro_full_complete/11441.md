what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么| Used to ask for information about something. |What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词: which |反义词: (no single antonym) | pronoun, determiner, adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the supermarket? 她在超市买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten for dinner? 她晚饭吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the power went out? 停电前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? 他们在会议上将要展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have written by then? 到那时她将写了什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years? 到那时你将已经学习了五年什么？
What will they have been working on by the deadline? 到截止日期前他们将一直在做什么？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. 我希望我知道她在想什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being built next to the library? 图书馆旁边正在建什么？

疑问句:
What is your favorite color? 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末的计划是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what I'm saying. 她不明白我在说什么。
