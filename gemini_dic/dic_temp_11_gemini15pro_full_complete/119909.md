militation:/ˌmɪlɪˈteɪʃən/| n.| 妨碍，不利，反对|The union's stance against new technology was a militation against progress. 工会反对新技术的立场阻碍了进步。|  His poor health was a militation against his career advancement. 他糟糕的健康状况阻碍了他的职业发展。|近义词：hindrance, obstacle, impediment, detriment, disadvantage|反义词：advantage, benefit, aid, assistance|词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Latin "militare" (to serve as a soldier), from "miles" (soldier).  The meaning shifted from "fighting" to "working against" or "hindering."
