afrikanerization:/əˌfrɪkɑːnərɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
南非化；布尔化
The process of imposing Afrikaner language and culture.  The systematic implementation of policies and practices aimed at promoting the Afrikaner language, culture, and political dominance, particularly in South Africa during the apartheid era.
The afrikanerization of the civil service led to the exclusion of many black South Africans. 公务员队伍的南非化导致许多南非黑人被排除在外。
Afrikanerization of place names occurred during the apartheid era. 地名的南非化发生在种族隔离时代。

近义词:Boerization, cultural imposition
反义词:decolonization, integration
词性:noun
