lacewing:/ˈleɪswɪŋ/ | n. | 草蛉，草蜻蛉 | An insect with delicate, lacy wings, often green or brown.  They are beneficial insects as their larvae prey on aphids and other garden pests. |  例句：The lacewing is a welcome sight in the garden. 草蛉在花园里是受欢迎的景象。

例句：Lacewings are natural predators of aphids. 草蛉是蚜虫的天敌。 | 近义词：aphid lion (referring to the larva),  | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an insect) | 词性: noun
