coccygeal:/kɒkˈsɪdʒiəl/ /kɑːkˈsɪdʒiəl/
adjective
尾骨的；尾椎的
Relating to the coccyx (tailbone).
The coccygeal nerve is located near the tailbone. 尾骨神经位于尾骨附近。
The patient complained of coccygeal pain after the fall. 病人抱怨摔倒后尾骨疼痛。
近义词: caudal, tailbone
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider cephalic - relating to the head)
词性: adjective
