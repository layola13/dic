counterblockaded:/ˌkaʊntərblɒˈkeɪdɪd/
verb
反封锁，对抗封锁
to blockade in return; to retaliate against a blockade.
The British Navy counterblockaded the French ports. 英国海军反封锁了法国港口。
Synonyms: retaliatory blockade
Antonyms: allow passage, permit trade
verb
