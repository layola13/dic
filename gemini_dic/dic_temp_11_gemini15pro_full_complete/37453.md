commissariat:/ˌkɒmɪˈsɛːrɪət/ /ˌkɑːmɪˈsɛːriət/| n.|军粮供应；军需品；粮秣部；(尤指旧时苏联的)人民委员部|a department, especially in a communist country, responsible for a particular area of administration|The army's commissariat was responsible for supplying food to the troops. 军队粮秣部负责向部队供应食物。|synonyms: supply, provisions, logistics|antonyms: scarcity, deprivation|noun

commissariat:/ˌkɒmɪˈsɛːrɪət/ /ˌkɑːmɪˈsɛːriət/| n.|（尤指旧时苏联的）人民委员；政治委员|an official of the Communist Party, especially in the former Soviet Union, responsible for political education and organization|The commissariat addressed the troops about the importance of their mission. 政治委员向部队讲述了他们任务的重要性。|synonyms: commissioner, officer, official|antonyms: civilian, non-combatant|noun
