abstainers:/əbˈsteɪnərz/
n.
禁欲者，节制者
People who abstain from something, especially alcohol or other potentially harmful substances.
Many abstainers find social gatherings difficult because of the pressure to drink. 许多禁欲者觉得社交聚会很难，因为有喝酒的压力。
The group consisted of both drinkers and abstainers. 这个小组既有饮酒者也有不饮酒者。
近义词:teetotalers, non-drinkers
反义词:drinkers, users
词性:名词(plural)

abstainer:/əbˈsteɪnər/
n.
禁欲者，节制者
A person who abstains from something, especially alcohol or other potentially harmful substances.
He's been an abstainer since his health scare. 自从他的健康受到惊吓后，他就成了一个禁欲者。
She's a lifelong abstainer and has never touched alcohol. 她是一个终生的禁欲者，从未碰过酒精。
近义词:teetotaler, non-drinker
反义词:drinker, user
词性:名词(singular)
