jaguars:/ˈdʒæɡwɑːrz/ (US), /ˈdʒæɡjuəz/ (UK)| n.|美洲虎（复数）|A large, spotted feline found in the Americas, known for its powerful build and ability to swim.|例句：Jaguars are apex predators in their habitat. 美洲虎在其栖息地是顶级掠食者。

例句：We saw two jaguars on our jungle safari. 我们在丛林探险中看到了两只美洲虎。|近义词：panthers, cougars, leopards|反义词:prey|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *jaguar*, from Tupi *jaguareté*, meaning "true beast of prey".
