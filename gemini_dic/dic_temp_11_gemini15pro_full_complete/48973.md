degenerationism:/dɪˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənɪzəm/| n. |退化论，堕落论|The theory that humans, institutions, etc., decline from a previous state of excellence or virtue.|Historians debated whether the decline of the Roman Empire was due to degenerationism. 历史学家们争论罗马帝国的衰落是否源于退化论。|近义词：decline, decay, deterioration, retrogression|反义词：progress, evolution, development|noun

degenerationist:/dɪˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənɪst/| n. adj. |退化论者，堕落论者|A person who believes in the theory of degenerationism. |Some degenerationists believed that modern society was in decline. 一些退化论者认为现代社会正在衰落。|近义词：pessimist, declinist|反义词：optimist, progressivist|noun/adjective
