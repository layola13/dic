de-deputize:/ˌdiːˈdɛpjʊtaɪz/ |v.|解除代理职务，撤销代理权|To remove someone's authority to act as a deputy.|The sheriff was de-deputized after the scandal.  郡长在丑闻后被撤销了代理权。|Synonyms: dismiss, discharge, remove, revoke|Antonyms: appoint, commission, authorize|verb

de-deputization:/ˌdiːˌdɛpjʊtɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|解除代理职务，撤销代理权的行为|The act of removing someone's authority to act as a deputy.|The de-deputization of the sheriff was a controversial decision. 郡长的代理权被撤销是一个有争议的决定。|Synonyms: dismissal, discharge, removal, revocation|Antonyms: appointment, commission, authorization|noun
