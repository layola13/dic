ceramics:/səˈræmɪks/ | n. |陶瓷; 陶瓷制品; 制陶艺术|Objects made of clay and hardened by heat, such as pottery, tiles, and bricks.  It can also refer to the art or technology of making these objects.|The museum has a fine collection of ancient ceramics.  这家博物馆收藏了精美的古代陶瓷。|近义词：pottery, earthenware, porcelain|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "metalwork" or "glassware" in specific contexts) |词性: noun
