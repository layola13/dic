discomposingly:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊzɪŋli/
adverb
令人不安地；令人难堪地
In a way that makes someone feel uncomfortable or embarrassed.
Her laughter died away discomposingly. 她的笑声令人不安地消失了。
Synonyms: disturbingly, unsettlingly, upsettingly, unnervingly
Antonyms:  comfortingly, reassuringly, soothingly
词性: adverb

discompose:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊz/
verb
使不安；使烦恼；使失态
To make someone feel anxious, upset, or embarrassed.
The news of his death had discomposed her. 他去世的消息让她很不安。
Synonyms:  unsettle, disturb, upset, agitate, fluster
Antonyms: calm, soothe, comfort, reassure
词性: verb

compose:/kəmˈpoʊz/
verb
组成；构成；创作（乐曲、诗歌等）；使平静；使镇定
1.  To form or constitute something.  2. To write music, poetry, etc.  3. To make oneself calm or tranquil.
The committee is composed of elected officials. 委员会由民选官员组成。
He composed a symphony. 他创作了一首交响曲。
She struggled to compose herself. 她努力让自己平静下来。
Synonyms: (for meaning 1) constitute, make up, form; (for meaning 2) create, write; (for meaning 3) calm, soothe, settle
Antonyms: (for meaning 3) agitate, upset, disturb
词性: verb
