aphyllous: /eɪˈfɪləs/
adj.
无叶的
Lacking leaves, especially after the normal growing season.  Referring to plants or parts of plants naturally without leaves, or appearing so.
The aphyllous stems of the cactus store water efficiently.  仙人掌的无叶茎能有效地储存水分。
近义词: leafless
反义词: leafy, foliose
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Greek *ἀ- (a-, "not, without") + *φύλλον (phúllon, "leaf").
