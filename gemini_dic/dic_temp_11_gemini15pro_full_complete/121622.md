mispacked:/ˌmɪsˈpækt/
adjective
包装错误的，装错的
incorrectly packed
The mispacked box contained the wrong items. 包装错误的盒子内装错了物品。
The customer complained about the mispacked order. 客户抱怨订单包装错误。
近义词:incorrectly packed, wrongly packaged
反义词:correctly packed, properly packaged
词性:adjective

词源:
mis- + packed
mis-: prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly"
packed: past participle of "pack," meaning "to put things into a container"
