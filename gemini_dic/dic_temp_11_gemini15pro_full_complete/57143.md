dovish:/ˈdʌvɪʃ/ (US), /ˈdʌvɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|鸽派的；爱好和平的|Favoring peace or a peaceful solution, opposed to war or the use of force.|例句：The president adopted a dovish stance on the conflict. 总统对这场冲突采取了鸽派的立场。

例句：The dovish senator argued against increasing military spending.  这位鸽派的参议员反对增加军费开支。|近义词：pacific, peaceable, conciliatory|反义词：hawkish, bellicose, aggressive|词性:形容词
Etymology: From dove + -ish.  "Dove" has long been a symbol of peace and gentleness.
