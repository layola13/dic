clumpy:/ˈklʌmpi/ | adj. | 块状的；结块的 | Forming or consisting of clumps; lumpy. | The sauce was thick and clumpy. 酱汁又稠又结块。|  The batter was too clumpy to pour smoothly. 面糊太稠了，倒不顺畅。 | lumpy, chunky, clotted | smooth, even | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From clump + -y.  Clump itself likely originates from a Scandinavian source, possibly related to Norwegian *klump, meaning "lump, block."
