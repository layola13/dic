jaunce:/dʒɔːns/ |/dʒɑːns/| vi. n.| 欢快地旅行，短途旅行；炫耀；游荡 |To travel or go briskly or merrily; to jaunt.  A brisk, short journey, especially one taken for pleasure; a jaunt. |例句：They jaunced through Europe during their summer vacation.  他们在暑假期间愉快地游遍了欧洲。

例句：He took a jaunt to the beach on his motorcycle. 他骑摩托车到海滩玩了一趟。|近义词：trip, excursion, journey, jaunt, ramble, swagger, strut |反义词：trudge, plod|词性:动词/名词
