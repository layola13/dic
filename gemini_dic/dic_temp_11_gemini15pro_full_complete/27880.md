candlestick:/ˈkændlstɪk/ | n. | 烛台； candlestick chart | A holder for a candle, often decorative.  A type of price chart used in financial technical analysis. | The dining table was adorned with silver candlesticks. 餐桌上装饰着银烛台。  He studies candlestick charts to predict market movements. 他研究烛台图来预测市场走势。 | synonyms: candleholder, candelabrum | antonyms: N/A | noun
