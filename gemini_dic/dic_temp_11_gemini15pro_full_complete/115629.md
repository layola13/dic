marchi:/ˈmɑːrki/| n.|侯爵，侯爵夫人；（意大利）侯爵（爵位）|A nobleman ranking below a duke and above a count or earl, or the wife of such a nobleman. In Italy, a nobleman of hereditary rank. |The marchi lived in a grand palazzo.  侯爵住在一座宏伟的宫殿里。| The marchi of Saluzzo was a powerful figure in medieval Italy.  萨卢佐侯爵是中世纪意大利的一个权势人物。|Synonyms: marquess, marquis|Antonyms: commoner|noun
