fluttery:/ˈflʌtəri/ /ˈflʌtərɪ/| adj.|飘动的，颤动的，不安的|Characterized by quick, light, flapping movements, like those of a butterfly's wings; also, feeling nervous and slightly excited.|例句：She had a fluttery feeling in her stomach before the performance. 演出前她感到胃里一阵颤动。


例句：The butterfly's wings were fluttery. 蝴蝶的翅膀飘动着。


近义词：tremulous, shaky, nervous, jittery, apprehensive|反义词：calm, steady, composed|词性:形容词
