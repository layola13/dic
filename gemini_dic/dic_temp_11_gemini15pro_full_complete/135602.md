nonpossession:/ˌnɒnpəˈzɛʃən/
n.
无占有，不拥有
lack of possession; absence of ownership.
The Buddhist concept of nonpossession emphasizes detachment from material goods.  佛教的无占有概念强调脱离物质财富。
The museum's nonpossession of the artifact led them to borrow it from another institution.  这家博物馆没有这件文物，只好从另一家机构借来。
synonyms:  lack, absence, dearth, void
antonyms: possession, ownership, holding
noun
