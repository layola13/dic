expectance:/ɪkˈspɛktəns/ /ɛkˈspɛktəns/| n. |期望，预期，期待|The state of expecting something to happen; anticipation.|The expectance of a large inheritance fueled his lavish spending. 巨额遗产的预期让他挥霍无度。|近义词：anticipation, expectation|反义词:despair, hopelessness|词性:名词

Etymology: From late Middle English: from Old French esperance, from late Latin expectantia, from expectare ‘await’.
