meine:/ˈmaɪnə/| possessive pronoun.|我的 (plural).|This word is the plural form of "mein" (my) in German and is used to indicate possession of multiple items. It declines based on the gender and case of the noun it modifies.|例句：Meine Bücher sind neu. 我的书是新的。|近义词:unser (our) (depending on context), |反义词:dein (your, singular), euer (your, plural), sein (his), ihr (her/their).|Pronoun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Meine Katzen schlafen viel. = My cats sleep a lot.
Meine Freunde wohnen in Berlin. = My friends live in Berlin.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Meine Kinder spielen gerade draußen. = My children are playing outside right now.
Meine Eltern kochen gerade das Abendessen. = My parents are cooking dinner right now.

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Meine Geschwister haben viel gereist. = My siblings have travelled a lot.
Meine Großeltern haben ein langes Leben geführt. = My grandparents have lived a long life.


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
This tense is rarely used with "meine" as it primarily describes actions.  It would be grammatically unusual.



一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Meine Freunde waren gestern im Kino. = My friends were at the cinema yesterday.
Meine Familie feierte letztes Jahr Weihnachten in den Bergen. = My family celebrated Christmas in the mountains last year.

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Meine Nachbarn sangen laut, als ich nach Hause kam. = My neighbors were singing loudly when I came home.
Meine Kollegen arbeiteten an dem Projekt, als der Strom ausfiel. = My colleagues were working on the project when the power went out.

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Meine Eltern hatten schon gegessen, bevor ich ankam. = My parents had already eaten before I arrived.
Meine Freunde hatten das Museum bereits besucht, als ich dazu stieß. = My friends had already visited the museum when I joined them.

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Again, this tense is rarely used with possessive pronouns like "meine."


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Meine Freunde werden morgen ins Konzert gehen. = My friends will go to the concert tomorrow.
Meine Familie wird nächstes Jahr nach Italien reisen. = My family will travel to Italy next year.

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Meine Kinder werden morgen Nachmittag im Park spielen. = My children will be playing in the park tomorrow afternoon.
Meine Eltern werden nächste Woche im Garten arbeiten. = My parents will be working in the garden next week.

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Meine Geschwister werden bis nächstes Jahr ihr Studium abgeschlossen haben. = My siblings will have finished their studies by next year.
Meine Großeltern werden bis dahin viele Enkelkinder gehabt haben. = My grandparents will have had many grandchildren by then.

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
This tense is also rarely used with possessive pronouns like "meine."

虚拟语气:
Wenn meine Freunde Zeit hätten, würden sie mich besuchen. = If my friends had time, they would visit me.
Ich wünschte, meine Familie wäre hier. = I wish my family were here.

被动语态:
(Passive voice is not applicable with possessive pronouns like "meine.")

疑问句:
Wo sind meine Schlüssel? = Where are my keys?
Sind meine Schuhe sauber? = Are my shoes clean?

否定句:
Das sind nicht meine Bücher. = These are not my books.
Ich habe meine Tasche nicht gefunden. = I didn't find my bag.
