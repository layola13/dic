boot-faced:/ˈbuːtˌfeɪst/ | adj. | 闷闷不乐的，板着脸的 | Having a sullen and gloomy expression; looking glum and unsmiling, as if wearing boots.  It suggests a dour, unhappy, or disapproving countenance. | He sat there, boot-faced, after his team lost the game. 他坐在那里，板着脸，因为他的球队输掉了比赛。| synonyms: glum, sullen, dour, morose, surly | antonyms: cheerful, happy, jovial, merry | adjective

boot-faced:/ˈbuːtˌfeɪst/ | adj. | 闷闷不乐的，板着脸的 |  Appearing stern, unfriendly, and unapproachable due to a sullen facial expression. |  She gave me a boot-faced stare when I asked her for a favor. 当我请她帮忙时，她板着脸盯着我。| synonyms: grim, sulky, sour-faced | antonyms: smiling, beaming, radiant | adjective
