hypergeusia:/ˌhaɪpərˈɡjuːziə/| n. |味觉亢进|A heightened sense of taste, often experienced as an abnormally increased sensitivity to flavors.|She experienced hypergeusia after starting the new medication, finding even bland foods overwhelmingly flavorful. 她开始服用新药后出现了味觉亢进，即使是清淡的食物也觉得味道浓烈。|近义词: Heightened taste sensitivity|反义词: Hypogeusia, Ageusia|词性: Noun

hypergeusia:/ˌhaɪpərˈɡjuːziə/| n. |味觉过敏|An abnormally increased sensitivity to certain tastes, sometimes to the point of unpleasantness.|Due to her hypergeusia, the slight bitterness in the coffee was amplified, making it undrinkable. 由于她的味觉过敏，咖啡中轻微的苦味被放大了，使她无法饮用。|近义词: Increased taste acuity|反义词: Hypogeusia, Ageusia|词性: Noun
