nock:/nɒk/ (UK), /nɑːk/ (US)| n. vt.| 弓的箭扣；箭槽；搭箭；(弓弦发出的)弦响|The archer fitted the nock of the arrow to the bowstring. 弓箭手把箭的箭扣搭在弓弦上。|He nocked an arrow and aimed at the target. 他搭上箭，瞄准目标。|近义词: notch, groove|反义词: (没有明确的反义词)|词性: 名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nokke, *nok, from Old English *nocc (“notch, nick”). Cognate with Middle Dutch nocke (“nock, notch”), Middle Low German nocke (“nock”), Old High German knohho (“knuckle”), German Knoche (“bone, knuckle”).
