musting:/ˈmʌstɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
发霉; 生霉; 长霉
becoming moldy; covered with or smelling of mold.
例句：The bread was musting in the damp cupboard.  面包在潮湿的橱柜里发霉了。

例句：I noticed a musting smell coming from the basement. 我注意到地下室传来一股霉味。
近义词：mildewing, molding, rotting
反义词：drying, preserving
词性：v.
