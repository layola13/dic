nonteachableness:/ˌnɒnˈtiːtʃəbəlnəs/ | n. | 不可教性；不驯服；难教 | The state or quality of being unteachable or resistant to instruction.  | His nonteachableness frustrated his tutors. 他的不可教性让他的导师们感到沮丧。|  The child's nonteachableness stemmed from a learning disability.  这个孩子的不可教性源于学习障碍。 | Synonyms: indocility, uneducability, intractability, stubbornness, obstinacy | Antonyms: teachability, docility, malleability, tractability | noun
