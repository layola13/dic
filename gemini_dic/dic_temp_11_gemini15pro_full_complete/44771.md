crumple:/ˈkrʌmpəl/ (US), /ˈkrʌmpl/ (UK)| v.  n. |弄皱，起皱；崩溃，垮掉|To crush something into folds or wrinkles; to collapse or give way.|例句：The paper crumpled in his hand.  他手里的纸揉皱了。|近义词：crush, rumple, crease, collapse, buckle|反义词：smooth, flatten, straighten|verb

crumple:/ˈkrʌmpəl/ (US), /ˈkrʌmpl/ (UK)| n. |皱纹，褶皱|A wrinkle or crease.|例句：There was a crumple in the fabric. 布料上有一个褶皱。|近义词：wrinkle, crease, fold|反义词：smoothness, flatness|noun
