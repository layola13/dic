consequent:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsɪkwent/ (US)| adj. n.|结果的，随之发生的；结果，推论|Something that is consequent happens as a result of something else. |例句：The drought and consequent famine killed thousands. 干旱和随之发生的饥荒夺走了数千人的生命。|近义词：resulting, ensuing, subsequent, following|反义词：prior, preceding, antecedent|词性:形容词/名词

consequents:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsɪkwents/ (US)| n.|结果，后果，推论（复数）|The results or effects of something.|例句：He didn’t consider the possible consequents of his actions. 他没有考虑他行为的可能后果。|近义词：results, outcomes, effects, repercussions|反义词：causes, antecedents, origins|词性:名词

USAGE->

词源: From Latin *consequentem*, present participle of *consequi* "to follow", from *com-* "together" + *sequi* "to follow".
