blouson:/ˈbluːzɒn/ (UK), /ˈbluːsɑːn/ (US)| n. |布尔松夹克，短夹克|A lightweight, waist-length jacket, typically gathered at the waist or hips.|He wore a stylish blouson jacket. 他穿着一件时髦的布尔松夹克。|近义词：jacket, bomber jacket|反义词:coat|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "blouson," meaning "jacket," ultimately from Old French "bloson," meaning "padded garment."
