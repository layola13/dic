inked:/ɪŋkt/
adjective
墨迹的，用墨水写的，签署的
marked or covered with ink; having signed a contract
例句：The contract was inked and the deal was finalized.  合同已签署，交易已完成。

例句：My arms are inked with various tattoos. 我的手臂上有很多纹身。

近义词：tattooed, signed, written
反义词:blank, unsigned

词性: adjective
