non-self-governing:/ˌnɒnˌsɛlfˈɡʌvənɪŋ/ | adj. | 非自治的 | Not governing itself; subject to the rule of another power.  A term used in international law to describe territories whose people have not yet attained a full measure of self-government. | Many non-self-governing territories are small islands. 许多非自治领土都是小岛。| The United Nations maintains a list of non-self-governing territories. 联合国保留一份非自治领土的清单。 | dependent, colonial, subject | autonomous, independent, sovereign | adjective
