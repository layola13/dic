annexes:/ˈænɛksɪz/
n.|附件，附属物|Something added or attached, especially a supplement to a document.|The treaty included several annexes detailing specific regulations. 该条约包含几个详细说明具体规定的附件。|
近义词:appendix, supplement, attachment, addendum
反义词:main body, core
词性:名词(plural of annex)

annex:/əˈnɛks/
vt.|吞并，附加|To attach, append, or add, especially to something larger or more important; to incorporate (territory) into the domain of a city, country, or state.|The city annexed the surrounding suburbs. 该城市吞并了周围的郊区。|
近义词:append, attach, incorporate, appropriate, seize
反义词:detach, separate, cede
词性:动词

annex:/ˈænɛks/
n.|附件，附属建筑|A building or part of a building added to a main structure.|The library has a new annex that houses the rare book collection. 图书馆有一个新的附属建筑，里面收藏着珍本书。|
近义词:addition, extension, wing, appendage
反义词:main building
词性:名词

词源:From the Latin word "annexus," past participle of "annectere," meaning "to tie or bind to."
