green-manures:/ˌɡriːn məˈnjʊərz/ | n. | 绿肥 | Plants, such as rye, clover, or alfalfa, that are plowed into the soil to improve its quality. |  Example: The farmer sowed green-manures to enrich the soil for next year's crop.  农民播种绿肥以丰富土壤，为明年的作物做准备。 | Synonym: cover crops, green manure crops | Antonym: chemical fertilizers | noun
