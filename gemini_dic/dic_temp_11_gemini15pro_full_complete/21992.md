boil:/bɔɪl/ (US) /bɔɪl/ (UK)| v. n.|煮沸，沸腾；煮，烹煮；(液体)沸腾；(人)激动，发怒|To heat a liquid until bubbles rise constantly to the surface.  As a noun, the act of boiling or the state of being boiled. |例句：The water is boiling. 水开了。|近义词：simmer, stew, cook, seethe, rage, fume|反义词:freeze, cool, calm|verb

boil:/bɔɪl/ (US) /bɔɪl/ (UK)| n.|沸腾；疖子|The act or state of boiling; a painful swollen area under the skin|例句：Bring the water to a boil.  把水烧开。|近义词：furuncle, abscess|反义词:N/A|noun
