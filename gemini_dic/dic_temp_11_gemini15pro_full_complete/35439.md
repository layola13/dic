co-regent:/ˌkoʊˈriːdʒənt/
noun
共同摄政者，共同执政者
A person who governs a kingdom or country jointly with another.
Example:  The co-regents ruled wisely and justly. 共同摄政者统治英明公正。

Example:  The two brothers served as co-regents until the rightful heir came of age. 两兄弟担任共同摄政者，直到合法继承人成年。
Synonym:  joint ruler, co-ruler
Antonym:  subject, subordinate
n.
