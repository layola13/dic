chills:/tʃɪlz/| n.| 寒冷，发冷，寒战|A sudden feeling of coldness, often accompanied by shivering. | I felt chills down my spine when I heard the scary story.  当我听到这个恐怖故事时，我感到背脊发凉。|Synonyms: shivers, goosebumps, tremors|Antonyms: fever, warmth|Noun

chills:/tʃɪlz/| n.| 兴奋，激动|A feeling of excitement or thrill. |The music sent chills down my spine. 这音乐让我脊背发凉（形容激动）。|Synonyms: excitement, thrill, frisson|Antonyms: boredom, apathy|Noun
