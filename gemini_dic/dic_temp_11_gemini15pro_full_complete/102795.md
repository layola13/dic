jardiniere:/ˌʒɑːrdɪˈnjɛər/ /ˌʒɑːrdnˈɪr/| n.|花盆；花架；装饰性花盆；（餐桌上盛蔬菜的）长盘|An ornamental container for plants, typically made of porcelain or terracotta.  A jardiniere is a decorative container or stand for displaying plants, often used in gardens, patios, or indoors.|She placed the fern in a beautiful white jardiniere.|Synonyms: planter, flowerpot, container, pot|Antonyms: N/A|noun
