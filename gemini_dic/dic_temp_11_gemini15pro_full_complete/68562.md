fangle:/ˈfæŋɡl/| n.|新玩意儿；新发明；新奇的装饰|A new invention or gadget; a novelty, especially one that is fashionable or showy. |例句：The latest fangle in kitchen appliances is a smart refrigerator that can order groceries for you. 厨房电器最新的玩意儿是一个可以为你订购杂货的智能冰箱。

例句：She was always adorned with the latest fangles. 她总是用最新的新奇装饰品来装扮自己。|近义词：novelty, gadget, gimmick, contrivance|反义词：antique, classic|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *fanglen*, perhaps frequentative of *fongen* "to seize, take," from Old English *fōn,* of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *vangen* and German *fangen*.  The original sense was probably "to busy oneself with trifles," hence "to make a fuss about new things."
