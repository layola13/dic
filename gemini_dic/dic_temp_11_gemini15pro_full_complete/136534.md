nonruminations:/ˌnɒnˌruːmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 非反刍，不沉思，不反复思考 |  The absence of rumination, or dwelling on negative thoughts and feelings.  It refers to a state of not overthinking or obsessively pondering. |  Her nonruminations about the past allowed her to move forward with her life. 她对过去的不再沉思让她能够继续前进。| synonyms:  non-reflection, unreflectiveness | antonyms: ruminations, reflections, brooding | noun
