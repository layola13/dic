misprovokes:/ˌmɪsprəˈvoʊks/
verb
激怒，挑衅
To provoke wrongly or mistakenly; to cause someone to become angry or upset through a misunderstanding or unintentional action.
His careless words misprovoked his friend, leading to an argument. 他的粗心大意的话激怒了他的朋友，导致了一场争论。
近义词: irritate, annoy, provoke, offend
反义词: placate, soothe, calm, appease
