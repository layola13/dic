anticonventionally:/ˌæntikənˈvɛnʃənəli/
| adv. |非传统地，反传统地| In an unconventional or unorthodox manner; contrary to convention or tradition.  Going against established norms or customs.|She dressed anticonventionally, eschewing traditional attire for vibrant, eclectic pieces. 她穿着反传统，抛弃了传统的服饰，选择了充满活力、不拘一格的单品。| synonym: unconventionally, unorthodoxly, unusually, idiosyncratically | antonym: conventionally, traditionally, typically, ordinarily |词性: 副词

anticonventional:/ˌæntikənˈvɛnʃənl/
| adj. |非传统的，反传统的| Not bound by or conforming to convention; unorthodox.|His anticonventional approach to art made him a controversial figure.  他反传统的艺术手法使他成为一个备受争议的人物。| synonym: unconventional, unorthodox, unusual, atypical | antonym: conventional, traditional, typical, orthodox |词性: 形容词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposite of (from Greek "anti-")
convention (noun): a generally accepted way of behaving or doing something (from Latin "conventio" - a coming together, agreement)
-al (suffix): relating to, characteristic of
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
