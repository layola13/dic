canaled:/kəˈnæld/ |v. |引导，输送；开凿运河|To convey or direct through a channel; to construct a canal. |The engineers canaled the river to prevent flooding. 工程师们疏浚了河流以防止洪水泛滥。|The water was canaled through a series of pipes. 水通过一系列管道输送。|近义词：channeled, directed, conveyed|反义词：blocked, obstructed|词性：动词

词源: From canal + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin canalis "pipe, channel."
