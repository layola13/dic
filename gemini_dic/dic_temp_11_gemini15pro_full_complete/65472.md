euromaidan:/ˌjʊərəʊˈmaɪdɑːn/| n. |乌克兰亲欧盟示威运动|A series of protests and civil unrest in Ukraine, primarily in Kyiv, that began in late November 2013, sparked by the Ukrainian government's decision to suspend the signing of an association agreement with the European Union.|The Euromaidan protests led to a change in government in Ukraine. 乌克兰亲欧盟示威运动导致了乌克兰政府的更迭。|The Euromaidan revolution was a pivotal moment in Ukrainian history. 乌克兰亲欧盟示威革命是乌克兰历史上的关键时刻。|Synonyms:  Ukrainian Revolution of Dignity, Maidan Uprising|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps pro-Russian movement in Ukraine)|Noun
