overexaggerate:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt/ | v. | 过分夸大；夸张 | To exaggerate to an excessive degree. | He tends to overexaggerate when telling stories about his travels. 他在讲述旅行故事时往往会夸大其词。|  She overexaggerated the difficulty of the exam. 她过分夸大了考试的难度。 | synonyms: overstate, overemphasize, amplify, embellish | antonyms: understate, downplay, minimize | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + exaggerate, from Latin exaggeratus, past participle of exaggerare "to heap up, increase, aggravate," from ex- "out" + aggerare "to heap up," from agger "heap, mound."
