kidnappees:/ˌkɪdnæˈpiːz/
n.
被绑架者；肉票
People who have been kidnapped.
The kidnappers released the kidnappees after receiving the ransom. 绑匪收到赎金后释放了人质。
近义词: hostages, captives
反义词: kidnappers
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From kidnap + -ee (a suffix forming nouns denoting the person who is the object or beneficiary of the action of the verb).  Kidnap itself comes from kid (meaning child, originally slang) + nap (meaning to snatch, steal).
