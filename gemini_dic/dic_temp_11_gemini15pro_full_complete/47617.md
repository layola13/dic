debellation:/ˌdiːbəˈleɪʃən/
n.
征服，击败，彻底战胜
The complete defeat of an enemy, especially in war.
The Roman legions achieved the debellation of their enemies. 罗马军团彻底击败了他们的敌人。

近义词：conquest, subjugation, vanquishment, rout, defeat
反义词：defeat, loss, surrender

词性：noun

词源：From Latin debellare, from de- + bellare “to wage war,” from bellum “war.”
