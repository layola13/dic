lapstreak:/ˈlæpˌstriːk/| adj. n.|搭接板的，叠瓦状的；搭接板|A method of boat building where the hull planks overlap each other.  一种船体木板相互重叠的造船方法。|The boat was built using the lapstreak method. 这艘船是用搭接板的方法建造的。|Near synonyms: clinker-built, overlapped|Antonyms: carvel-built (smooth-sided)|adjective, noun
