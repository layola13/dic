gabbler:/ˈɡæblər/| n.|爱说话的人；贪吃的人；火鸡（尤指雄性）|A person who talks a lot; a person who eats a lot; a turkey, especially a male one.|He's a real gabbler - he never stops talking. 他真是个话匣子 - 他从不停止说话。

The gabbler devoured the entire plate of cookies. 这个贪吃的人吞下了整盘饼干。

That old tom is a proud gabbler. 那只老雄火鸡很骄傲。|近义词：chatterbox, glutton, gourmand (for food), gobbler (for turkey)|反义词:quiet person, moderate eater|词性:noun
