checkerboard:/ˈtʃɛkərbɔːrd/| n. adj.|棋盘；方格图案|A board with squares of alternating colors, used for playing checkers or chess.  Also used to describe a pattern resembling such a board.|例句：The checkerboard pattern on the floor was striking. 地板上的棋盘图案引人注目。

例句：We played checkers on a red and black checkerboard. 我们在一个红黑相间的棋盘上玩跳棋。|近义词：grid, lattice|反义词: solid, plain|词性: noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "checker" (a piece in the game of checkers) + "board." "Checker" ultimately derives from the Old French "eschequier," meaning "chessboard."
