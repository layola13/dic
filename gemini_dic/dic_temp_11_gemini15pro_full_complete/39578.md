conscription:/kənˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. | 征兵，强制征募 | Compulsory enlistment for state service, typically into the armed forces. |例句：Conscription was introduced during the war.  战争期间实行了征兵制度。

例句：Many young men tried to avoid conscription. 许多年轻人试图逃避兵役。

近义词：draft, levy, recruitment | 反义词: voluntary enlistment | 词性: noun
