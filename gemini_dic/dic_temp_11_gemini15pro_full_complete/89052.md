holographs:/ˈhɒləɡrɑːfs/ (UK) /ˈhɑːləɡræfs/ (US)| n.|全息图；亲笔信|A holograph is a three-dimensional image formed by the interference of light beams from a laser or other coherent light source. It can also refer to a document written entirely by the person whose signature it bears.|The museum displayed fascinating holographs of extinct animals.|近义词：3D image, hologram|反义词: photograph (in the sense of a traditional 2D image)|词性: Noun
