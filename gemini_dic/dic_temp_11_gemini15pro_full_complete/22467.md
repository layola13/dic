bookman: /ˈbʊkmən/ | n. | 书商；书呆子；爱书人 | A person who sells or deals in books; a scholar; a person who is fond of reading. | He was a true bookman, with a library that filled an entire room. 他是一个真正的爱书人，他的书房里摆满了书。 |  Example: The old bookman knew the value of every volume in his shop.  这位老书商知道他店里每一本书的价值。

Example: My uncle is a real bookman; he spends all his free time reading. 我叔叔是个真正的书虫，他所有的空闲时间都用来读书。 | 近义词: bibliophile, book lover, bookworm, scholar | 反义词: illiterate, philistine | 词性: noun
