evocativeness:/ɪˈvɒkətɪvnəs/
n.
唤起性；引起回忆的特性；感染力
The quality of bringing strong images, memories, or feelings to mind.
The evocativeness of the music transported her back to her childhood. 这音乐的感染力把她带回了童年。
近义词：suggestiveness, resonance, poignancy
反义词：blandness, dullness, vapidity
词性: noun

Etymology:
From evocative + -ness.  Evocative comes from evoke (from Latin evocare "to call out, summon") + -ive.
