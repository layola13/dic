catechistic:/ˌkætɪˈkɪstɪk/ | adj. | 教义问答式的，问答教学法的 | Relating to, resembling, or characteristic of a catechism; consisting of questions and answers. |  The priest's catechistic method of teaching helped the children understand the basic tenets of their faith.  牧师的问答式教学方法帮助孩子们理解了他们信仰的基本原则。| synonyms: didactic, instructional, pedagogical | antonyms: n/a | adjective

catechism:/ˈkætɪkɪzəm/ | n. | 教义问答书，问答教学法 | A summary of the principles of Christian religion in the form of questions and answers, used for the instruction of Christians. | The children studied the catechism to prepare for their confirmation. 孩子们学习教义问答，为他们的坚振礼做准备。 | synonyms: primer, manual, handbook | antonyms: n/a | noun
