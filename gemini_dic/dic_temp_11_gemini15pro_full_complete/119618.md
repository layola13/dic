midder:/ˈmɪdər/| n.|正午, 中间, 中心点|A point or period of time midway between two others; the middle. It can also refer to a person or thing situated in the middle.  While less common than "middle," "midder" retains a certain archaic charm and can be found in dialectal English.|The sun was high in the sky at midder. 正午时分，太阳高挂在空中。|近义词：middle, midday, noon|反义词:edge, beginning, end|词性:noun
