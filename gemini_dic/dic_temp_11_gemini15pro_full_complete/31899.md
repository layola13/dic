cheesemonger:/ˈtʃiːzmʌŋɡər/ | n. | 奶酪商，干酪商 | A person who sells cheese.  A retailer specializing in cheese. | 例句：The cheesemonger recommended a sharp cheddar. 奶酪商推荐了一种浓郁的切达干酪。

例句：I asked the cheesemonger for advice on pairing cheese with wine. 我向奶酪商咨询了奶酪和葡萄酒的搭配建议。

近义词：cheese seller, cheese dealer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chesemangere*, equivalent to cheese +‎ monger.  "Monger" comes from Old English *mangere* meaning "merchant, trader," ultimately from Latin *mango* "dealer, trader".
