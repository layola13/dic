musicologist:/ˌmjuːzɪˈkɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. |音乐学家；音乐学者|A person who studies the history, theory, and forms of music.|例句：The musicologist specialized in the study of medieval chant.  这位音乐学家专门研究中世纪圣咏。

例句：She's a renowned musicologist and author of several books on the subject. 她是一位著名的音乐学家，也是几本关于这个主题的书籍的作者。|近义词：music scholar, music historian|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
