helleborism:/ˈhelɪbɔːrɪzəm/| n. |嚏根草疗法；用嚏根草治疗精神病|The practice of treating mental disorders with hellebore.  Hellebore was used in ancient Greece to purge the body and was believed to cure insanity.|In ancient times, helleborism was a common practice, though its effectiveness is questionable. 在古代，嚏根草疗法是一种常见的做法，尽管其有效性值得怀疑。|Synonym:  herbal remedy,  ancient medicine|Antonym: modern medicine|noun
