institutional:/ˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənəl/ | adj. |制度的；机构的；习俗的|Of, relating to, or characteristic of an institution. |例句：The hospital provides institutional care for the elderly. 这家医院为老年人提供机构护理。|例句：He criticized the institutional biases against women. 他批评了对女性的制度性偏见。|近义词：organizational, established, conventional|反义词：individual, personal, unconventional|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From Late Latin *institutionale, from Latin institutio "establishment, arrangement," from institutus, past participle of instituere "to set up, establish," from in- "in" + statuere "to set, place."
