conchifer:/ˈkɒŋkɪfər/| n.|贝壳动物，有壳动物|An animal that has a shell, especially a mollusk.|例句：Conchifers, like clams and snails, are an important part of the marine ecosystem.  贝壳动物，如蛤蜊和蜗牛，是海洋生态系统的重要组成部分。

例句：The beach was littered with the shells of various conchifers. 海滩上散落着各种贝壳动物的壳。|近义词：mollusk, shellfish|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑非贝壳动物 invertebrate)|词性:noun
