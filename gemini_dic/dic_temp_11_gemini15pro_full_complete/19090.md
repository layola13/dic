betrothment:/bɪˈtrəʊθmənt/ /bɪˈtroʊðmənt/ | n. |订婚，婚约 |The act of promising to marry someone.  A mutual promise or contract for a future marriage between two people. | Their betrothal was announced in the local newspaper. 他们的订婚在当地报纸上宣布了。

The betrothal lasted for two years before the wedding. 订婚持续了两年才举行婚礼。

|近义词：engagement, affiance |反义词: separation, divorce |词性: noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *betrouthement*, from Anglo-Norman *betrouthement*, from betrouther (to betroth), from be- (thoroughly) + trouther (to pledge one's faith), from Old French *troter* (to make a treaty, to engage)
