lolloped:/ˈlɒləpt/ (UK) /ˈlɑːləpt/ (US)| v. |慢跑，笨拙地奔跑|To move in a clumsy, rolling way, typically with long strides.|The dog lolloped across the field. 狗笨拙地在田野上奔跑。|
The bear lolloped toward the river. 熊笨拙地朝河边走去。|近义词：lumber, amble, gallop, bound|反义词：sprint, dart, scurry|词性:动词

lollop:/ˈlɒləp/ (UK) /ˈlɑːləp/ (US)| n. |笨拙的步伐|A clumsy, rolling gait.|The dog's lollop was quite comical. 狗笨拙的步伐相当滑稽。|
The bear's lollop carried it quickly across the field. 熊笨拙的步伐让它很快地穿过了田野。|近义词：lumber, amble|反义词：sprint, dart|词性:名词
