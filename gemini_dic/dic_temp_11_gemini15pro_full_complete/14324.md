automatists:/ɔːˈtɒmətɪsts/
n.
自动主义者；机械论者
People who believe in or practice automatism, a mode of creation in which the artist suppresses conscious control.
The exhibition showcased the works of several prominent automatists.  这次展览展示了几位杰出自动主义者的作品。
The automatists believed in creating art through spontaneous gestures. 自动主义者相信通过自发的姿态进行艺术创作。
synonyms: surrealists, Dadaists
antonyms: rationalists, traditionalists
noun
