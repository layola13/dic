ascent:/əˈsɛnt/| n.|上升，攀登，上坡路|An upward movement; a climb or walk to the summit of a mountain or hill. |The hikers began their ascent of the mountain early in the morning. 登山者一大早就开始登山了。|Synonyms: climb, rise, scaling,  upward movement|Antonyms: descent, decline, fall|noun

ascent:/əˈsɛnt/| n.|提高，提升，晋升|The act of rising to a higher social or professional position. |Her rapid ascent to the CEO position surprised everyone.  她迅速晋升到首席执行官的职位让每个人都感到惊讶。|Synonyms:  advancement, promotion, progress, rise|Antonyms: demotion, downgrade, fall|noun
