endorphin:/ɛnˈdɔːrfɪn/ /ɛnˈdɔːrfən/| n. |内啡肽|A hormone produced in the pituitary gland that acts as a natural painkiller and also creates a general feeling of well-being.|Exercise produces endorphins, which make you feel good.|Synonyms: opiate, analgesic|Antonyms: pain enhancer|noun
