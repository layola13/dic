counterscientific:/ˌkaʊntərˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/
adjective
反科学的
Opposing or contradicting science or scientific principles.
His counterscientific beliefs led him to reject well-established medical treatments.  他反科学的信念导致他拒绝接受公认的医疗方法。
She made the counterscientific claim that vaccines cause autism. 她提出了反科学的主张，认为疫苗会导致自闭症。
近义词:antiscientific, unscientific
反义词:scientific, pro-scientific
词性:adjective
