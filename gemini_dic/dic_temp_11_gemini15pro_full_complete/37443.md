commiserating: /kəˈmɪzəreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
表示同情或怜悯的
expressing or feeling sympathy or pity
She offered a commiserating pat on the shoulder. 她同情地拍了拍他的肩膀。
近义词: sympathetic, compassionate, empathetic
反义词: unsympathetic, indifferent, callous
词性: adjective


commiserate: /kəˈmɪzəreɪt/
verb
同情，怜悯
to express or feel sympathy or pity for someone
I commiserated with her over the loss of her job. 我对她失去工作表示同情。
近义词: sympathize, condole, empathize
反义词: ignore, disregard
词性: verb
