brisk:/brɪsk/| adj.|轻快的，敏捷的，活泼的；清新爽快的|Energetic and quick; lively.  Characterized by liveliness and quickness.|The brisk walk in the cold air invigorated her. 在寒冷的空气中轻快的散步让她精神焕发。|Synonyms: quick, rapid, swift, lively, energetic, vigorous|Antonyms: slow, sluggish, languid|Adjective
