annex:/əˈneks/ | n. | 附加物，附件；附属建筑物 | An annex is a building added to a larger one. |  The library has built a new annex to house its growing collection. 图书馆新建了一个附属建筑来存放不断增长的藏书。 | synonym: addition, extension, wing | antonym: main building | noun

annex:/əˈneks/ | vt. | 兼并，吞并；附加，添加 | To annex means to add something, typically territory, to one's own. |  Germany annexed Austria in 1938. 德国于1938年吞并了奥地利。 | synonym: seize, conquer, appropriate, append | antonym: relinquish, cede, detach | verb
