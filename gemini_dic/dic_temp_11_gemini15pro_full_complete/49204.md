delays:/dɪˈleɪz/
n.|延误，延迟，耽搁|a period of time by which something is late or postponed|The flight was subject to delays due to bad weather. 由于恶劣天气，航班延误了。|近义词：postponements, setbacks, hold-ups|反义词:advances, progressions
词性:名词复数

delay:/dɪˈleɪ/
n.|延误，延迟，耽搁|a period of time by which something is late or postponed|The project experienced a delay due to funding issues.  由于资金问题，该项目经历了延误。|近义词：postponement, setback, hold-up|反义词:advance, progression
词性:名词单数

delay:/dɪˈleɪ/
vt.| 延迟，推迟，延缓|make (someone or something) late or slow|Heavy traffic delayed my arrival.  交通拥堵延误了我的到达。|近义词：postpone, defer, hinder|反义词:expedite, hasten
词性:动词


词源: From Old French *delaier (Modern French délai), from Latin dilatare "to spread out, extend, enlarge," from dis- "apart" + latus "wide."
