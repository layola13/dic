firework:/ˈfaɪərwɜːrk/ (US), /ˈfaɪəwɜːk/ (UK)| n. |烟花；烟火；爆竹|A device containing gunpowder and other combustible chemicals that causes a spectacular explosion when ignited, used typically for display or celebration. |例句：The fireworks display on the Fourth of July was breathtaking. 七月四日的烟花表演令人叹为观止。

例句：Children are excited to light fireworks during the Spring Festival. 春节期间孩子们很兴奋地点燃烟花爆竹。

近义词：pyrotechnics, cracker, rocket|反义词: None (no direct antonym)|词性: Noun
