imperceptible:/ˌɪmpərˈseptəbl/ | adj. | 难以察觉的；感觉不到的 | Too slight or subtle to be perceived | The changes were almost imperceptible. 这些变化几乎难以察觉。 |  synonyms: unnoticeable, undetectable, invisible, inaudible, impalpable | antonyms: perceptible, noticeable, obvious, evident, clear | adjective

imperceptibles:/ˌɪmpərˈseptəblz/ | n. | 难以察觉的事物 | Things that are too slight or subtle to be perceived | The artist captured the imperceptibles of light and shadow.  这位艺术家捕捉到了光影之间难以察觉的微妙变化。 | synonyms: nuances, subtleties, minutiae | antonyms: obvious things, clear things | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *imperceptibilis, from Latin in- (“not”) + percept-, past participle stem of percipere (“to perceive”). Equivalent to in- +‎ perceptible.
