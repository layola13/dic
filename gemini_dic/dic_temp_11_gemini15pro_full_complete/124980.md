mugginess:/ˈmʌɡinəs/| n.|闷热，湿热|The mugginess of the tropical climate was oppressive.  热带气候的湿热令人窒息。|近义词：humidity, sultriness, closeness|反义词: dryness, coolness, freshness|词性:noun
词源: From muggy + -ness. Muggy itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to Norwegian *mugga "drizzle, mist".
