finicky:/ˈfɪnɪki/| adj.|挑剔的，过细的，苛求的|Excessively particular, difficult to please; fussy.|例句：He's a finicky eater and won't try anything new. 他是个挑剔的食客，什么新东西都不愿意尝试。

例句：She's very finicky about her clothes and spends hours getting ready. 她对衣着很挑剔，每次要花好几个小时准备。|近义词：fussy, picky, particular, choosy, fastidious|反义词：undemanding, easygoing, adaptable|词性：形容词
