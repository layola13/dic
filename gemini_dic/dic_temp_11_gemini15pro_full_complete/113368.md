lychee:/ˈliːtʃiː/ /ˈlaɪtʃiː/
n.
荔枝
A small rounded fruit with sweet white flesh and a thin, rough, red-brown rind.
例句：I love the taste of fresh lychees. 我喜欢新鲜荔枝的味道。

例句：Lychees are a popular fruit in Southeast Asia. 荔枝是东南亚的热门水果。

近义词：longan, rambutan
反义词: (No direct antonym for a fruit)
词性:noun
