clocks:/klɑːks/ /klɒks/
noun
时钟；计时器
instruments for measuring and indicating time, especially one with a dial and hands or a digital display.
The grandfather clocks chimed midnight. 祖父的时钟敲响了午夜。
synonyms: timepiece, timer, chronometer, watch
antonyms: none
n.
