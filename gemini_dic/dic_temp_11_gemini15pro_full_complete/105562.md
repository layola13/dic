kin:/kɪn/
n.|亲戚，家族，同族|One's family and relations.|例句：He is my kin. 他是我的亲戚。|例句：She feels a strong connection to her kin. 她感觉和她的家族联系很紧密。|近义词：relative, family, kindred|反义词：stranger, nonrelative|词性：名词
Etymology: From Old English *cynn "family, race, kind, nature," from Proto-Germanic *kunjam "family, offspring," from PIE *ǵen- "to give birth, beget."
