lasagna:/ləˈzɑːnjə/ /ləˈzɑːnɪə/ | n. |千层面；烤宽面条|A wide, flat pasta noodle, often baked in layers with cheese, meat, and tomato sauce. |例句：My mom makes the best lasagna. 我妈妈做的千层面最好吃。

例句：We had lasagna for dinner last night. 我们昨晚晚餐吃了千层面。|近义词：lasagne |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a food dish) |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *lasagna*, plural of *lasagna* "a strip of pasta", from Latin *lasanum* "cooking pot", from Greek *lasanon* "chamber pot".  Originally referring to the pot in which the dish was cooked, the word eventually came to mean the dish itself.
