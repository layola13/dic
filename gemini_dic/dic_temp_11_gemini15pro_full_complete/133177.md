nonevangelical:/ˌnɒnˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪkəl/
adj.
非福音派的，非福音主义的
Not evangelical; not holding or promoting evangelical principles.
Many mainline Protestant denominations are considered nonevangelical.  许多主流的新教教派被认为是非福音派的。
The conference attracted both evangelical and nonevangelical Christians.  这次会议吸引了福音派和非福音派的基督徒。
近义词:non-evangelical, liberal, mainline
反义词:evangelical, fundamentalist
词性:adjective
