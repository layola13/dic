outjinxed:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɪŋkst/
verb
胜过(某人)的厄运；比...更倒霉
surpass someone in having bad luck; to have worse luck than someone else
例句：She outjinxed him by breaking her leg the day before the lottery she'd won. 她比他更倒霉，在她赢得彩票的前一天摔断了腿。

例句：He thought he was having a bad day, but his friend outjinxed him by losing his wallet and phone. 他以为自己今天很倒霉，但他的朋友比他更倒霉，因为他丢了钱包和手机。


近义词：outluck,  have worse luck than
反义词：outlucky,  have better luck than
词性：v.
