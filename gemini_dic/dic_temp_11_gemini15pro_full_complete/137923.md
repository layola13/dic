nonvolition:/ˌnɒnvəˈlɪʃən/
noun
非自愿，无意志
the state of not making your own decisions or choices
His hand moved in a gesture of nonvolition. 他的手非自愿地做了一个手势。

The twitch was a nonvolition.  这个抽搐是非自愿的。

non-volitional:/ˌnɒnvəˈlɪʃənl/
adjective
非自愿的，无意识的
not done or made consciously
A nonvolitional shudder ran through her. 她不由自主地颤抖了一下。

He experienced a nonvolitional muscle spasm. 他经历了无意识的肌肉痉挛。


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + volition (the act of willing or choosing).  "Volition" comes from the Latin "volitio," from "velle" (to wish, will).

SYNONYMS->
involuntary, unintentional, automatic, reflex, instinctive, compelled, forced

ANTONYMS->
voluntary, intentional, conscious, deliberate, willful

PART OF SPEECH->
Noun (nonvolition)
Adjective (non-volitional)
