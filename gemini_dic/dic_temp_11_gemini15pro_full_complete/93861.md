immoderacy: /ɪˈmɒdərəsi/ (British) /ɪˈmɑːdərəsi/ (American)| n. |无节制，过度，放纵|Lack of moderation or restraint; excess. |His immoderacy in eating led to health problems. 他饮食无度导致了健康问题。|The immoderacy of his language offended many people. 他言语的过分激怒了很多人。|近义词：excess, extravagance, intemperance, overindulgence|反义词：moderation, restraint, temperance, abstemiousness|词性:名词

词源:
Late Middle English: from Old French immoderatie, from late Latin immoderatia, from immoderatus ‘immoderate’.
