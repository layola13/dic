commissive:/kəˈmɪsɪv/
adjective
承诺的；允诺的
Creating an obligation or commitment.  Relating to or containing a promise or pledge.
例句：A commissive utterance, such as "I promise to pay you back," creates an obligation for the speaker.  像“我保证还你钱”这样的承诺性话语，会给说话者带来义务。

例句：The signing of the treaty was a commissive act, binding both nations to its terms.  签署条约是一种承诺行为，使两国都受其条款约束。
近义词：binding, obligatory, pledging
反义词：non-binding, optional
词性：adjective
