floatability:/ˌfloʊtəˈbɪləti/| n.|浮性，漂浮性，漂浮能力|The floatability of an object depends on its density relative to the density of the liquid it is in. 一个物体的浮性取决于它相对于其所在液体的密度。|The life jacket's floatability ensured the swimmer's safety. 救生衣的浮力确保了游泳者的安全。| 近义词：buoyancy| 反义词：sinkability|词性:名词
