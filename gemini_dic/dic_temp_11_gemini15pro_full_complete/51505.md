detrainment:/diːˈtreɪnmənt/
n.
卸车;下火车;（尤指云中水汽的）移出;（气团的）脱离
the process of removing liquid water from a cloud or air mass.  It also refers to the act of passengers leaving a train.
Passengers began detrainment as soon as the train stopped.  旅客们在火车一停稳就开始下车了。
The detrainment of water vapor from the cloud led to heavy rainfall. 云中水汽的移出导致了强降雨。
removal, discharge, disembarkation, egress
entrainment, boarding
noun
