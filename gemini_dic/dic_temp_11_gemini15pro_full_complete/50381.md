deporters:/dɪˈpɔːrtərz/
noun
驱遣者，遣送者
People who deport others, especially from a country.
The deporters escorted the illegal immigrants to the plane. 驱遣者将非法移民押送上飞机。
deportation officers, immigration officials
returnees, immigrants
