freeware:/ˈfriːwɛːr/
n.
免费软件
Software that is available for use at no monetary cost.
I downloaded some useful freeware for editing photos. 我下载了一些用于编辑照片的有用的免费软件。
Many companies distribute freeware versions of their software to attract new users. 许多公司发行免费版本的软件来吸引新用户。
近义词：free software, shareware
反义词：commercial software, payware
词性: 名词
