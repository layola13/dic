-ific:/ˈɪfɪk/
suffix|causing or making; characterized by| 引起…的；产生…的；…的特性|
English explanation:  A suffix used to form adjectives meaning "causing" or "producing," or "characterized by."  It is often added to stems derived from Latin. |
Example: Scientific discoveries are changing the world.  科学发现正在改变世界。

Example:  The horrific accident left many injured.  可怕的事故导致许多人受伤。

Synonym: -genous, -making, -producing
Antonym: -less
词性: suffix (后缀)
