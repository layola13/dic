decrepitnesses:/dɪˈkrɛpɪtnɪsɪz/
n.
衰老；破旧；老朽
the state of being decrepit; feebleness from age, infirmity, or long use.
The decrepitnesses of the old buildings in the town were evident.  这个镇上的老建筑破败不堪，显而易见。
The decrepitnesses of his body made it difficult for him to walk. 他身体的衰弱使他行走困难。
近义词:frailty, infirmity, debility, weakness, feebleness
反义词:strength, robustness, vigor, soundness
