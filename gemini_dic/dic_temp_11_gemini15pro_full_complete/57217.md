downed:/daʊnd/| adj. |被击落的|shot down or brought to the ground. |The downed helicopter lay in a twisted heap. 被击落的直升机扭曲地堆在地上。|synonyms: fallen, crashed, destroyed |antonyms: airborne, flying, intact|adjective
