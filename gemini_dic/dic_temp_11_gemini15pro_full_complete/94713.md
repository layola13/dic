improperly:/ɪmˈprɒpəli/ /ɪmˈprɑːpərli/| adv.|不正确地，不适当地|in a way that is not correct or suitable|He was accused of improperly using company funds. 他被指控不当使用公司资金。|The equipment was improperly installed. 设备安装不当。|incorrectly, wrongly, inappropriately|properly, correctly, suitably|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + proper + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Proper" comes from Old French "propre" meaning "one's own, special, particular," ultimately from Latin "proprius."
