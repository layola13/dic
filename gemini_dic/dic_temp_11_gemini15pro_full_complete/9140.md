antinationalism:/ˌæntiˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |反民族主义，反国家主义|Opposition to nationalism or to a particular form of nationalism.  It often involves prioritizing global or other non-national affiliations over national ones.|His antinationalism led him to criticize his country's foreign policy. 他对国家的反民族主义立场导致他批评了国家的外交政策。|近义词：cosmopolitanism, internationalism, globalism|反义词：nationalism, patriotism, chauvinism|noun

antinationalism:/ˌæntiˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/| adj. |反民族主义的，反国家主义的|Characterized by opposition to nationalism or a specific form of nationalism.|Her antinationalism stance was controversial. 她的反民族主义立场备受争议。|近义词：cosmopolitan, internationalist, globalist|反义词：nationalist, patriotic, chauvinistic|adjective
