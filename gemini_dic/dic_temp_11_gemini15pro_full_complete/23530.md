brainchilds:/ˈbreɪnˌtʃaɪldz/
n.
创意，产物，心血结晶
A person's invention or creation, especially one that they are particularly proud of.
The new software is the brainchild of a team of young programmers.  这个新软件是一群年轻程序员的智慧结晶。
The festival was the brainchild of the town's mayor. 这个节日是镇长的创意。
