morphemic:/mɔːrˈfiːmɪk/ | adj. | 词素的；形态的 |  Relating to morphemes, the smallest meaningful units in a language.  Derived from "morpheme" + "-ic". |  A morphemic analysis of the word "unbreakable" would reveal the morphemes "un-", "break", and "-able".  对单词“unbreakable”进行词素分析会揭示出词素“un-”、“break”和“-able”。 | synonymous: morphological | antonymous: N/A | adjective
