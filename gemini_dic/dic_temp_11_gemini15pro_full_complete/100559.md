interwishing:/ˌɪntərˈwɪʃɪŋ/
|adj.|
互相祝福的；交相祝愿的|
Expressing mutual wishes or hopes.|
例句：They exchanged interwishing glances full of hope for the future. 他们交换了充满对未来希望的互相祝福的眼神。

例句：The interwishing atmosphere of the holiday season filled the air. 节假日里互相祝福的气氛弥漫在空气中。
|近义词：mutually wishing, reciprocally hoping|
|反义词：cursing, condemning|
|词性：形容词|
