nonelectivenesses:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktɪvnəsɪz/| n. |非选择性；非选举性|The state of not being elective or chosen.  This refers to situations, positions, or procedures that are not determined by voting or selection but are rather assigned, mandatory, or automatic.|The nonelectivenesses of the committee appointments caused some dissent. 委员会任命的非选择性引起了一些异议。|Near synonyms:  inevitability, necessity, compulsion, obligation|Antonyms:  electiveness, choice, optionality|Noun
