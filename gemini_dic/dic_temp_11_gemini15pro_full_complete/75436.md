fruitful:/ˈfruːtfəl/| adj.|富有成效的；多产的；丰硕的|Producing good results; producing a lot of fruit or crops.|例句：The meeting was very fruitful, and we made a lot of progress. 会议非常富有成效，我们取得了很大进展。|近义词：productive, profitable, beneficial, rewarding|反义词：unproductive, barren, fruitless|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fruteful, *fritful, from Old English *frēohtfull (“fruitful, fertile”), equivalent to fruit +‎ -ful. Cognate with Dutch vruchtbaar (“fruitful”), German fruchtbar (“fruitful”), Swedish fruktbar (“fruitful”).
