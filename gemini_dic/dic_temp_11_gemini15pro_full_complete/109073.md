legate: /ˈlɛɡɪt/ | n. | 使节；代表；特使 | A person sent as a representative, especially one on a diplomatic mission. | The Pope sent his legate to discuss the matter with the king. 教皇派他的使节与国王商讨此事。 | synonyms: envoy, emissary, ambassador, delegate, representative | antonyms: receiver, recipient | noun

legate: /ˈlɛɡeɪt/ | v. | 遗赠；委托 | To give (especially personal property) by will. (This meaning is less common in modern usage.) | He legated his entire estate to his nephew. 他把全部财产遗赠给了他的侄子。 | synonyms: bequeath, devise, will | antonyms: inherit, receive | verb
