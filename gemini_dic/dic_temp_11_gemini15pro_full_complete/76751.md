gamekeepers:/ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpəz/ /ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpɚz/| n. |猎场看守人；野生动物保护员|A person employed to protect game animals or birds on an estate, especially from poachers. | The gamekeepers patrolled the estate regularly to deter poachers.  猎场看守员定期巡逻庄园以阻止偷猎者。|  The head gamekeeper was responsible for managing the wildlife population. 首席猎场看守员负责管理野生动物的数量。 |Near Synonyms: warden, ranger, wildlife manager| Antonyms: poacher | Noun
