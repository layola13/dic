intershoot:/ˈɪntərʃuːt/
verb
插入拍摄;穿插拍摄
To insert (a scene, shot, etc.) into a film or television programme.
The director decided to intershoot a flashback scene to provide more context. 导演决定插入一个闪回镜头来提供更多背景。
The documentary intershoots archival footage with present-day interviews.  这部纪录片将档案片段与当今的采访穿插在一起。
近义词:insert, intercut
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "remove" in the context of editing)
词性:verb
