iffs: /ɪfs/ | conj. | 如果 |  A conjunction used to introduce a conditional clause.  Derived from Old English "gif," meaning "if."| If it rains, we will stay inside. 如果下雨，我们将待在室内。| If you study hard, you will pass the exam. 如果你努力学习，你将通过考试。| 近义词: provided that, assuming that, in case, on condition that | 反义词: unless | 词性: conjunction
