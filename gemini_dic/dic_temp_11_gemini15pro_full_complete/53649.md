dischargee:/ˌdɪsˌtʃɑːrˈdʒiː/
n.
被解雇者；被释放者；退伍军人
a person who has been discharged from a hospital, prison, the military, or a job
The hospital dischargee went home to recover.  这家医院的出院病人回家休养了。
The dischargee from the army struggled to find work.  退伍军人难以找到工作。
Synonyms: ex-employee, veteran, ex-prisoner, patient
Antonyms: employee, recruit, inmate
noun
