lau:/laʊ/ | n. | 熔岩; 火山岩 | Molten rock that erupts from a volcano or fissure, or solid rock resulting from the cooling of this. | The volcano erupted, sending streams of lau down its sides. 火山爆发了，熔岩顺着山坡流淌。 |  The lau cooled quickly, forming a black, glassy rock. 熔岩迅速冷却，形成了一种黑色的玻璃状岩石。| 近义词: lava, magma | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑 solid rock 固体岩石) | 词性: 名词
