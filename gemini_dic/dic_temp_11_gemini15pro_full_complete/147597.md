overplanted:/ˌoʊvərˈplæntɪd/
adjective
过度种植的
planted with too many plants, especially of one kind, in a particular area.
The market was overplanted with tomatoes, resulting in very low prices.  市场上西红柿种植过多，导致价格非常低。
The vineyard was overplanted, leading to smaller grapes and less flavorful wine.  葡萄园过度种植，导致葡萄变小，葡萄酒味道变淡。
synonyms: overcropped, oversown
antonyms: underplanted
