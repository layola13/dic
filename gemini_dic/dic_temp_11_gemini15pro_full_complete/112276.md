loppard:/ˈlɒpəd/ (British English), /ˈlɑːpərd/ (American English)| n. |豹; 豹子; 豹纹|A large, spotted feline native to Africa and Asia, known for its agility and hunting prowess. It is also used to describe things having a spotted pattern like a leopard.|例句：The leopard stalked its prey through the tall grass.  豹子在高高的草丛中跟踪猎物。

例句：She wore a leopard-print dress. 她穿了一件豹纹连衣裙。|近义词：panther, jaguar|反义词:  (No direct antonym for an animal. Could use "prey" in a specific context)|词性:noun
