nonoccupants:/ˌnɒnˈɒkjʊpənts/
n.|非居住者；非占用者|People who do not live in a particular place.
|The building was empty, except for a few nonoccupants seeking shelter from the storm.|这栋楼是空的，除了几个非住户在里面躲避暴风雨。
|近义词：nonresidents, outsiders|反义词：occupants, residents, inhabitants|词性:名词
