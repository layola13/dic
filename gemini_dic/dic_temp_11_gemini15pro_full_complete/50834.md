descrying:/dɪˈskraɪɪŋ/
verb
发现；看出；望见
Catching sight of something, especially something distant or obscure.
From a high vantage point, she was descrying ships on the horizon. 从一个高的有利位置，她可以望见地平线上的船只。
Synonyms: spotting, perceiving, discerning, detecting, noticing
Antonyms: overlooking, missing, ignoring
