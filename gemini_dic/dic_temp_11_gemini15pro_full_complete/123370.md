monomaniacally:/ˌmɒnəʊməˈnaɪəkli/ /ˌmɑːnəməˈnaɪək(ə)li/ | adv. |偏执地，着魔地|In a way that shows an obsessive preoccupation with one thing.|He pursued his research monomaniacally, neglecting all other aspects of his life. 他偏执地进行他的研究，忽略了他生活的其他所有方面。|Synonyms: obsessively, fanatically, single-mindedly|Antonyms: rationally, reasonably|Word type: Adverb

monomania:/ˌmɒnəʊˈmeɪniə/ /ˌmɑːnəˈmeɪniə/ | n. |偏执狂，狂热|An exaggerated or obsessive enthusiasm for or preoccupation with one thing.|His monomania for collecting stamps led him to spend his entire fortune. 他对集邮的狂热导致他倾家荡产。|Synonyms: obsession, fixation, craze|Antonyms: indifference, apathy|Word type: Noun

monomaniac:/ˌmɒnəʊˈmeɪniæk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈmeɪniæk/ | n. |偏执狂患者|A person exhibiting monomania.|He was a monomaniac when it came to his work, often forgetting to eat or sleep. 在工作方面，他是个偏执狂，经常忘记吃饭或睡觉。|Synonyms: fanatic, zealot, obsessive|Antonyms: moderate|Word type: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *monomanie*, from Greek *monos* "single" + *mania* "madness, frenzy".
