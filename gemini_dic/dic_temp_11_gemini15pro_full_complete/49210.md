delectable:/dɪˈlɛktəbəl/| adj.|美味的，令人愉悦的|Highly pleasing, especially to the taste or smell; delicious.  Giving great pleasure.|例句：The chef prepared a delectable meal. 厨师准备了一顿美味的佳肴。|例句：The bakery is filled with delectable aromas. 面包店里充满了令人愉悦的香味。|近义词：delicious, delightful, scrumptious, palatable, exquisite|反义词：unappetizing, disgusting, repulsive, unpalatable|词性:形容词
词源:From Late Latin *dēlectabilis, from Latin dēlectāre (“to delight”), from dē- + *lactāre, frequentative of lacere (“to entice, allure”).  Doublet of delectable and dilectable.
