anti-creationist's:/ˌænti kreɪˈeɪʃənɪsts/
noun
反创造论者的（所有格）
belonging to or associated with someone who opposes creationism (the belief that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution).
The anti-creationist's arguments were based on scientific evidence.  反创造论者的论点基于科学证据。
synonyms: opponent of creationism's,  evolutionist's (sometimes used, but can be imprecise), Darwinist's (often used pejoratively by creationists)
antonyms: creationist's,  pro-creationist's
