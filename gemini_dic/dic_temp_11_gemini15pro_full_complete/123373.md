monome:/ˈmɑːnoʊm/ /ˈmɒnəʊm/ | n. |单项式; 单体|A single term in an algebraic expression; a molecule that can be bonded to other identical molecules to form a polymer. | The expression 3x^2y is a monome.  这个表达式 3x^2y 是一个单项式。| The researchers are studying different types of monomes to create new polymers. 研究人员正在研究不同类型的单体来创造新的聚合物。| 近义词: term (in algebra) | 反义词: polynomial | 词性: noun

monomeric:/ˌmɑːnəˈmɛrɪk/ /ˌmɒnəˈmɛrɪk/ | adj. |单体的; 由单体构成的|Of or relating to a monome. |  The monomeric units combine to form a long chain polymer. 单体单元结合形成长链聚合物。 | Scientists are exploring the properties of monomeric compounds. 科学家正在探索单体化合物的特性。| 近义词: single-unit | 反义词: polymeric | 词性: adjective
