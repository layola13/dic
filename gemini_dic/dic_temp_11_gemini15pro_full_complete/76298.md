gabbing:/ˈɡæbɪŋ/
n.|闲聊，聊天|The act of talking idly or incessantly; chatter.|They spent the afternoon gabbing on the phone. 他们一下午都在电话里闲聊。|近义词：chatting, gossiping, prattling|反义词:being silent, taciturnity
v.|闲聊，聊天|(present participle of gab) Talking idly or incessantly; chattering.|They were gabbing away about the latest celebrity gossip. 她们一直在闲聊最新的名人八卦。|近义词：chatting, gossiping, prattling|反义词:being silent, taciturnity
