distressedly:/dɪˈstrɛsɪdli/| adv.|忧虑地，悲伤地，痛苦地|In a way that shows great worry, sadness, or pain.|She looked at him distressedly. 她忧虑地看着他。|近义词：anxiously, worriedly, sadly, sorrowfully|反义词：calmly, peacefully, happily|词性:副词
词源: From the adjective "distressed" + the adverbial suffix "-ly". "Distressed" comes from the Old French word "destresse," meaning "hardship" or "oppression."
