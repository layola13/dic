deportee:/ˌdiːpɔːrˈtiː/ /ˌdiːpɔːrˈteɪ/| n.|被驱逐者，被遣返者|A person who has been deported or exiled from a country.|The deportees were sent back to their country of origin. 这些被驱逐者被遣送回原籍国。|Near synonyms: exile, refugee, evacuee|Antonyms: citizen, resident|Noun

deportee:/ˌdiːpɔːrˈtiː/ /ˌdiːpɔːrˈteɪ/| n.|被驱逐出境者|A person who is expelled from a country.|Many of the deportees faced an uncertain future. 许多被驱逐出境者面临着不确定的未来。|Near synonyms: expellee, outcast, banished person|Antonyms: immigrant, resident|Noun
