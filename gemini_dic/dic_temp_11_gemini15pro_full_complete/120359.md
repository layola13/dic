minimizers:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzərz/ | n. | 最小化器，最小化程序，极小化子 |  People or things that minimize something; agents or tools that reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree. |  These special algorithms act as minimizers, efficiently finding the lowest energy state of the molecule. 这些特殊的算法充当最小化器，有效地找到分子的最低能态。 | synonyms: reducers, diminishers, lesseners | antonyms: maximizers, amplifiers, enlargers | noun
