conformity:/kənˈfɔːrməti/ /kənˈfɔːrmɪti/| n.|遵从，一致，符合|behavior in accordance with socially accepted conventions or standards.  The act of matching attitudes, beliefs, and behaviors to group norms.|例句：Conformity to social norms is expected in most cultures. 在大多数文化中，人们期望遵守社会规范。|例句：The pressure for conformity can be intense.  从众的压力可能很大。|近义词：obedience, compliance, acquiescence|反义词：nonconformity, individuality, rebellion|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French conformite, from conformer (see conform).
