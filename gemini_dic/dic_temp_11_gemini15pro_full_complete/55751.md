diverge:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒ/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒ/
verb
分叉；偏离；相异
to separate and go in different directions; to become different
The two roads diverge in the forest. 这两条路在森林里分叉了。
synonyms: separate, deviate, branch off, differentiate
antonyms: converge, meet, unite, intersect
词性: 动词

diverges:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒɪz/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒɪz/
verb
分叉；偏离；相异（第三人称单数形式）
Third person singular present tense form of "diverge".
The path diverges here, leading to two different waterfalls.  这条小路在这里分岔，通往两个不同的瀑布。
synonyms: separates, deviates, branches off, differentiates
antonyms: converges, meets, unites, intersects
词性: 动词
