ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + tyrannical + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Tyrannical" comes from the Greek "tyrannos," meaning "absolute ruler."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
