disorientates:/dɪsˈɔːriənˌteɪts/
verb
使迷失方向，使不知所措
to make someone confused about where they are and where they are going
The flashing lights disorientated me. 闪爍的灯光使我迷失了方向。
近义词: confuse, bewilder, perplex
反义词: orient, guide
词性: verb

disorientated:/dɪsˈɔːriənˌteɪtɪd/
adjective
迷失方向的，不知所措的
confused and not knowing where to go or what to do
He woke up feeling completely disorientated. 他醒来时感到完全不知所措。
近义词: confused, bewildered, lost
反义词: oriented, clearheaded
词性: adjective

disorientation:/dɪsˌɔːriənˈteɪʃn/
noun
迷失方向，迷惑
a state of mental confusion about time, place, or identity
She suffered from disorientation after the accident. 事故发生后，她感到迷失方向。
近义词: confusion, bewilderment
反义词: orientation, clarity
词性: noun
