aldermancies:/ˈɔːldərmənsiz/
noun
市议员职位；市政官职位
The office or position of an alderman.  It often refers to multiple such positions.
The corruption scandal swept through several aldermancies, leaving many seats vacant.  腐败丑闻席卷了几个市议员职位，导致许多席位空缺。
synonyms: aldermanships, council seats, municipal offices
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps consider: civilian positions, private sector jobs)
