folks:/foʊks/ (US) /fəʊks/ (UK)| n.|人们，家人，亲属，大伙儿|People in general or a person's family or relatives. |例句：How are your folks doing? 你家里人怎么样？

例句：Folks around here are friendly. 这附近的人都很友好。

近义词：people, family, relatives, parents|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly enemies or strangers depending on context)|词性:noun


folks:/foʊks/ (US) /fəʊks/ (UK)| n.|各位，伙计们 (Informal)|Used as a form of address, especially in North America. |例句：Okay, folks, let's get started. 好了，各位，我们开始吧。

例句：Listen up, folks, I have an announcement. 伙计们，听我说，我有个公告。

近义词：everyone, everybody, you guys|反义词: (No specific antonym)|词性:noun
