handsprung:/ˈhændsprʌŋ/| n.|手翻，手翻跳|An acrobatic movement in which a person launches themselves forward and over onto their hands and then back onto their feet.  A forward handspring is often part of a gymnastics routine.|The gymnast executed a perfect handsprung.|近义词：handspring, flip, cartwheel|反义词:|noun
