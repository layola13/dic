linguists:/ˈlɪŋɡwɪsts/| n.|语言学家|Experts in the field of linguistics, studying the structure, history, and social context of language.|Linguists are studying the evolution of language. 语言学家正在研究语言的演变。|synonyms: language scholars, philologists|antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *linguiste*, from Latin *lingua* (tongue, language) + *-ista* (a suffix forming agent nouns).
