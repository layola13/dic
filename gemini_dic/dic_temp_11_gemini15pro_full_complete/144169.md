outshowed:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊd/
verb
胜过，在表演方面超过
surpassed in a performance or show; outperformed
例句：She outshowed all the other contestants with her incredible talent. 她以惊人的才华胜过了所有其他参赛者。

例句：The young violinist outshowed the seasoned professionals.  这位年轻的小提琴家在表演上超过了经验丰富的专业人士。

近义词：outperformed, surpassed, eclipsed, excelled, triumphed over
反义词：underperformed, lost to, was outshone by
词性：verb (past tense of outshow)
