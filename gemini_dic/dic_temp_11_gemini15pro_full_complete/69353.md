federals:/ˈfɛdərəlz/
noun
联邦政府人员；联邦党人；联邦军队士兵
Officials of the federal government; supporters of a federal system of government; soldiers in a federal army.
The federals were sent in to quell the riot. 联邦军队被派去平息骚乱。
Synonyms: feds, government agents, federal agents
Antonyms: locals, state officials
