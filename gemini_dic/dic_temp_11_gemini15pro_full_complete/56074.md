dockers:/ˈdɒkəz (UK) / ˈdɑːkərz (US)| n.| 码头工人; (Dockers)多克尔裤|people employed in a port to load and unload ships; (Dockers) a brand of durable casual trousers|例句：The dockers went on strike for better pay. 码头工人为争取更高的工资而罢工。
