corniculate:/kɔːrˈnɪkjʊlət/ | adj. | 角状的；小角形的 | Having small horns or horn-like projections. | The corniculate cartilages are small, cone-shaped pieces of cartilage. 小角软骨是小的锥形软骨块。 |  cornute, horned | hornless | adjective

corniculate:/kɔːrˈnɪkjʊleɪt/ | n. | 小角软骨 | A small horn-shaped cartilage. | The corniculate cartilages sit on the apex of the arytenoid cartilages. 小角软骨位于杓状软骨的顶端。|  cornu | N/A | noun
