hypermodernist:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɒdərnɪst/ /ˌhaɪpərˈmɑːdərnɪst/| n. adj.|超级现代主义者；超级现代主义的|A person who adheres to hypermodernism, a cultural, artistic, or architectural style characterized by an extreme emphasis on modernism, often to the point of parody or exaggeration.  As an adjective, it describes something related to this style.|

例句：
The architect's hypermodernist designs were met with both praise and criticism.
这位建筑师的超级现代主义设计既受到赞扬，也受到批评。

Her hypermodernist apartment was filled with sleek, minimalist furniture and abstract art.
她超级现代主义的公寓里摆满了光滑的极简主义家具和抽象艺术。


近义词：ultramodernist, avant-garde, futurist|
反义词：traditionalist, classicist|
词性：noun, adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->
From "hyper-" (Greek, meaning "over, beyond") + "modernist" (from "modern" + "-ist").|
