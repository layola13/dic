iPhones:/ˈaɪfoʊnz/| n. |苹果手机|Plural form of "iPhone," referring to multiple units of Apple's smartphone device.  These devices are known for their sleek design, user-friendly interface, and integration with other Apple products and services.|I just bought two new iPhones for my parents. 我刚给我的父母买了两个新的苹果手机。|近义词：smartphones, cell phones, mobile phones|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "dumb phones" as a contrasting older technology)|noun
