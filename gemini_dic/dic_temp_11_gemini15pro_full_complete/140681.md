nomination: /ˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 提名；任命；提名权 |  The act of officially suggesting someone or something for a position, job, award, etc. |  His nomination for the award was unexpected.  他被提名获奖出乎意料。 | 近义词: appointment, designation, selection | 反义词: rejection, dismissal | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *nominatio*, from nominare "to name".
