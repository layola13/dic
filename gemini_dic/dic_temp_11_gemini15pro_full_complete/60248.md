effumability:/ˌɛfjuːməˈbɪləti/
n.
可蒸发性；可挥发性
The quality of being effumable; capability of being vaporized or dissipated.
The effumability of the perfume made the entire room fragrant.  这香水的挥发性使得整个房间都香气扑鼻。
近义词：volatility, evaporability
反义词：nonvolatility, stability
词性：noun
