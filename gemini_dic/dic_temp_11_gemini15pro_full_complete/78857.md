gingerlinesses:/ˈdʒɪndʒərlɪnəsɪz/
n.
谨慎, 小心翼翼, 谨小慎微
The quality or state of being gingerly; cautiousness.
She approached the injured animal with extreme gingerlinesses. 她极其小心翼翼地靠近那只受伤的动物。
近义词:caution, care, carefulness, wariness, chariness
反义词:recklessness, carelessness, boldness
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From gingerly + -ness.  Gingerly itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to Middle English *gengere "dainty, elegant" or Old French gensor "more noble, more beautiful." The original sense seems to have been "delicately, elegantly," later evolving to "cautiously."
