dime-a-dozen:/ˌdaɪm ə ˈdʌzən/
adjective
不值钱的；普通的；多的
common; inexpensive; readily available; of little value.  Something that is so common that it has little value.
Generic romance novels are a dime a dozen these days. 如今，通俗爱情小说多如牛毛。
Synonyms: commonplace, ordinary, run-of-the-mill, ubiquitous
Antonyms: rare, scarce, unique, valuable
词性: adjective
