chaetetes:/kiːˈtiːtiːz/
noun
丛状珊瑚化石，毛孔珊瑚
An extinct genus of tabulate corals characterized by closely packed, prismatic corallites.
Chaetetes fossils are common in Paleozoic rocks.  丛状珊瑚化石在古生代岩石中很常见。
synonyms:  tabulate coral,  fossil coral
