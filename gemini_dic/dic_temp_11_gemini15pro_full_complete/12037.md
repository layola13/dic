articulatory:/ɑːrˈtɪkjʊlətɔːri/ |adj.|发音的，清晰吐字的|Relating to the articulation of speech sounds;  of or relating to the joints or their component parts. |The articulatory phonetics course focuses on how speech sounds are produced. 这门发音语音学课程重点研究语音是如何产生的。|The damage to his articulatory system made it difficult for him to speak clearly.  他的发音系统受损，使他难以清晰地说话。|Synonyms: phonetic, pronounced, enunciated|Antonyms: inarticulate, mumbled, slurred|adjective
