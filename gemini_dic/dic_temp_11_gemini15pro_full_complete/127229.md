nausea:/ˈnɔːziə/ (US), /ˈnɔːsiə/ (UK)| n. |恶心，想吐|A feeling of sickness with an inclination to vomit.|例句：The smell of the hospital gave her a wave of nausea. 医院的气味让她一阵恶心。

例句：He experienced nausea after the chemotherapy treatment.  化疗后他感到恶心。

近义词：sickness, queasiness, vomiting (the act of vomiting, not the feeling) |反义词: wellness, comfort |词性: noun
