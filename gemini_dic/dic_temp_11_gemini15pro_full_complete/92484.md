hypothalamuses:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈθæləməsaɪ/
n.
下丘脑（复数）
A region of the forebrain below the thalamus that coordinates both the autonomic nervous system and the activity of the pituitary, controlling body temperature, thirst, hunger, and other homeostatic systems, and involved in sleep and emotional activity.
The hypothalamuses of the test subjects showed increased activity during the experiment.  实验期间，测试对象的多个下丘脑显示出活动增加。
The researchers studied the effects of stress on the hypothalamuses of mice. 研究人员研究了压力对小鼠下丘脑的影响。

近义词: (没有完全对等的近义词，可以列出功能相关的词) pituitary gland, brain stem
反义词: 无
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + θάλαμος (thálamos, “chamber, inner room”).
