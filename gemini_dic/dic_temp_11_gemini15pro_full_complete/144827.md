over-lauded:/ˌoʊvərˈlɔːdɪd/
| adj. | 过誉的，被吹捧过度的 | Excessively praised; praised more than is deserved. | The over-lauded movie turned out to be quite disappointing. 这部被吹捧过度的电影结果却令人失望。 | The over-lauded author's latest book received mixed reviews.  这位被过誉的作者的新书评价褒贬不一。| 近义词：overrated, overhyped, overpraised | 反义词：underrated, underappreciated | 词性: 形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "laud" (verb) + "-ed" (suffix)
"over-" 表示“过度”，“laud”源自拉丁语“laudare”（赞美），"-ed"是过去分词后缀，用于构成形容词。
