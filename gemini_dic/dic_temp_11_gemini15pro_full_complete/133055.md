nonepiscopally:/nɒnɪˈpɪskəpəli/
adv.
非主教地；不按照主教制度地
Not in accordance with episcopal church government or authority; in a manner that is not characteristic of or related to an episcopal church.
The church was governed nonepiscopally, with decisions made by a council of elders.  这个教会并非由主教治理，而是由长老会做决定。
The community organized their religious services nonepiscopally, focusing on lay leadership. 这个社区以非主教制的方式组织宗教仪式，注重平信徒的领导。
近义词: non-hierarchically, independently
反义词: episcopally
词性: 副词
