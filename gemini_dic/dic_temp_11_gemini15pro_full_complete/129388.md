nomenclator:/ˈnoʊmənˌkleɪtər/
n.
命名者；命名法；术语表
A person who assigns names (especially in scientific classification); a list of names or terms, especially a catalog or dictionary.
Linnaeus, the great nomenclator of plants and animals. 林奈，动植物命名法的伟大创始人。
Synonyms:lexicographer, taxonomist, classifier
Antonyms: None.
