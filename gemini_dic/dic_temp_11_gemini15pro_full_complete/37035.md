comanagers:/ˌkoʊˈmænɪdʒərz/ | n. |共同经理，共同管理者|Two people who share the responsibility of managing something, such as a business, a team, or a project. | Example: The comanagers decided to implement a new marketing strategy. 共同经理决定实施新的营销策略。
Example:  The comanagers worked well together, complementing each other's strengths.  两位共同经理合作良好，互补彼此的优势。| Synonyms: co-directors, joint managers, shared leaders | Antonyms: sole manager, single director | noun
