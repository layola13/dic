conflation:/kənˈfleɪʃn/ /kɑːnˈfleɪʃn/| n. |合并，混淆|The article was a conflation of several different sources. 这篇文章是几个不同来源的合并。|例句：The report is a conflation of two separate studies. 这份报告是两项独立研究的合并。

例句：His theory represents a conflation of several schools of thought. 他的理论代表了几个学派的融合。

近义词：amalgamation, fusion, blending, merging, combination |反义词:separation, distinction, differentiation|词性:名词

conflate:/kənˈfleɪt/ /kɑːnˈfleɪt/| v. |合并，混合|The author conflated two distinct historical periods. 作者将两个不同的历史时期混为一谈。|例句：Don't conflate correlation with causation. 不要把相关性和因果关系混淆。

例句：She conflated the two issues, which are actually quite distinct. 她把这两个问题混为一谈，实际上这两个问题截然不同。

近义词：combine, merge, fuse, blend, mix |反义词:separate, distinguish, differentiate|词性:动词
