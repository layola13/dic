dema:/ˈdiːmə/| n.|双移动平均线; 去除趋势移动平均|A double exponential moving average (DEMA) is a type of moving average used in technical analysis that attempts to reduce the lag associated with traditional moving averages.  It does this by placing more weight on recent data points.|The trader used the DEMA to identify potential trend reversals.  交易者使用DEMA来识别潜在的趋势反转。|近义词:TEMA (Triple Exponential Moving Average), EMA (Exponential Moving Average)|反义词:SMA (Simple Moving Average)|词性:名词
