fistularia:/ˌfɪstjʊˈlɛəriə/ | n. | 管口鱼；烟管鱼 |A slender, marine fish with a tubular snout and a long, filamentous tail.  It is found in tropical and subtropical waters worldwide. | Example: The fistularia hovered near the coral, its long snout probing for food.  管口鱼在珊瑚附近盘旋，用它长长的鼻子寻找食物。

Example:  Divers often spot fistularia in shallow reefs. 潜水员经常在浅礁看到管口鱼。 | Synonym: cornetfish, flutemouth | Antonym: N/A | Noun
