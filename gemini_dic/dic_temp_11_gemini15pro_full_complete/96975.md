infanticidal:/ɪnˌfæn.tɪˈsaɪ.dəl/
adjective
杀婴的
relating to or characterized by the killing of infants.
The infanticidal behavior of some animals, like hamsters, can be attributed to stress and overcrowding.  一些动物（例如仓鼠）的杀婴行为可归因于压力和过度拥挤。
The documentary explored the disturbing phenomenon of infanticidal mothers.  这部纪录片探讨了令人不安的杀婴母亲现象。
Synonyms: child-murdering,  infant-killing
Antonyms: protective, nurturing
词性: adjective
