anti-dancing:/ˌæntiˈdɑːnsɪŋ/ | adj. | 反对跳舞的，反对舞蹈的 | Opposed to dancing or the practice of dancing.  It describes someone who disapproves of dancing or something that hinders or discourages dancing. |  The anti-dancing sentiment in the community made it difficult to organize a prom. 社区里反对跳舞的情绪使得组织毕业舞会变得困难。

The strict, anti-dancing rules of the school were unpopular with the students. 学校严格的禁舞规定不受学生欢迎。 | 近义词:  unsympathetic to dancing, disapproving of dancing | 反义词: pro-dancing,  fond of dancing | 词性: adjective
