anaglyphical:/ˌænəˈɡlɪfɪkəl/
adjective
立体照片的，浮雕的
Relating to or resembling an anaglyph.  An anaglyph is a stereoscopic photograph or other image, usually printed in two colors (typically red and cyan), which appears three-dimensional when viewed through spectacles with lenses of corresponding colors.
The museum displayed several anaglyphical images of ancient ruins.  博物馆展出了几幅古代遗址的立体照片。
Near Synonyms: stereoscopic, 3D, three-dimensional
Antonyms: two-dimensional, flat
word type: adjective
