overgreatness:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪtnəs/ | n. | 过度的伟大; 过分的伟大 | Excessive greatness; greatness that is too much or overwhelming. | His overgreatness became his downfall, as others grew resentful of his perceived superiority. 他的过度伟大导致了他的垮台，因为其他人对他自认为的优越感感到不满。 | The weight of his overgreatness eventually crushed him. 他过度伟大的重担最终压垮了他。 | 近义词:excessiveness, immoderation, exorbitance | 反义词: insignificance, mediocrity, unimportance | 词性: noun

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "greatness" (noun)
