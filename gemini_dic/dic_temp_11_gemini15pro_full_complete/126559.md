nadir:/ˈneɪdɪr/ /ˈneɪdər/| n. |最低点；最底点；最糟糕的时刻|The nadir of his career was when he was fired from his dream job. 他事业的最低谷是被他梦寐以求的工作解雇的时候。|例句：The stock market reached its nadir in March. 股市在三月跌至谷底。|近义词：bottom, lowest point, rock bottom|反义词: zenith, peak, apex|词性: 名词

Etymology: From Arabic نظير naẓīr  "opposite, counterpart," originally meaning the point of the celestial sphere directly below the observer.
