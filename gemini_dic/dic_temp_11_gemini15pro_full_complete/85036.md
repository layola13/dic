harbor:/ˈhɑːrbər/ (US) /ˈhɑːbə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|庇护；怀有；藏匿；港口|To provide shelter or refuge; to hold a thought or feeling secretly; a place on the coast where ships may moor in shelter.|The fugitives were harbored by sympathetic villagers. 这些逃亡者受到了同情村民的庇护。|近义词：shelter, conceal, harbor, port|反义词：expose, reveal, evict|verb, noun

harbor:/ˈhɑːrbər/ (US) /ˈhɑːbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|港口|A sheltered place on the coast where ships can moor.|The harbor was filled with fishing boats. 港口里停满了渔船。|近义词：port, dock, marina|反义词：open sea|noun
