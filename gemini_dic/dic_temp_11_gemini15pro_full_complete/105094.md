kerneling:/ˈkɜːrnəlɪŋ/ | n. | 字距调整; 字偶距调整 | The adjustment of space between pairs of letters to improve the appearance and readability of text. |  This font looks much better with proper kerning.  这个字体经过适当的字距调整后看起来好多了。 |  Example: The designer adjusted the kerning between the 'W' and the 'a' for better visual balance. 设计师调整了“W”和“a”之间的字距以获得更好的视觉平衡。 | 近义词: letter-spacing, tracking | 反义词: N/A | noun
