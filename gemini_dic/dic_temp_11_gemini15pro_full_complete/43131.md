coyote:/kaɪˈoʊtiː/ /ˈkɔɪ.oʊt/| n. |郊狼，小狼|A small wolf-like animal native to North America.|例句：The coyote howled at the moon. 郊狼对着月亮嚎叫。

例句：Coyotes are known for their adaptability. 郊狼以适应性强而闻名。

近义词：jackal, wolf |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可考虑prey猎物) |词性:noun


coyote:/kaɪˈoʊtiː/ /ˈkɔɪ.oʊt/| adj. (俚语) |走私人口的，非法移民的|Relating to a person who smuggles people across borders, especially from Mexico to the United States.|例句：The coyote charged them a high price for the risky journey. 蛇头向他们收取了高昂的费用，让他们进行危险的旅程。

例句：The migrants relied on a coyote to get them across the border. 移民们依靠蛇头带他们越过边境。

近义词：smuggler, trafficker|反义词: (没有直接的反义词) |词性:adjective (informal)
