diphtheritic:/ˌdɪfθəˈrɪtɪk/
adjective
白喉的；类似白喉的
Relating to or characteristic of diphtheria, a serious bacterial infection typically affecting the mucous membranes of the nose and throat.
The child exhibited diphtheritic symptoms, including a sore throat and difficulty swallowing. 这孩子表现出白喉症状，包括喉咙痛和吞咽困难。
Near Synonyms: diphtherial, diphtheroid
Antonyms: healthy, well
词性: adjective
