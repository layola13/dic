anti-histamine:/ˌæntiˈhɪstəmɪn/ | n. |抗组胺药|A drug or other compound that inhibits the physiological effects of histamine. It is commonly used to treat allergies. |She took an anti-histamine for her hay fever. 她服用了抗组胺药来治疗花粉热。|Synonyms: allergy medication, anti-allergic |Antonyms: histamine agonist |noun


anti-histamine:/ˌæntiˈhɪstəmɪn/ | adj. |抗组胺的|Relating to or denoting a drug or other compound that inhibits the physiological effects of histamine. |He was prescribed anti-histamine cream for his rash. 他被开了抗组胺药膏来治疗皮疹。|Synonyms: anti-allergic |Antonyms: pro-histamine |adjective
