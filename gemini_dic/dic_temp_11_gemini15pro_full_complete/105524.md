kilostere:/ˈkɪləstɪər/| n. |千立方米，立方千米（木材体积单位）|A unit of volume equal to 1,000 cubic meters, used mainly for measuring timber.|The forestry industry uses kilosteres to measure large quantities of wood. 林业使用千立方米来计量大量的木材。|Synonym: cubic kilometer|Antonym: N/A|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From kilo- + stere, from Greek stereos "solid."
