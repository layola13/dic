objectist: /ˈɒbdʒɪktɪst/ | n. | 客观主义者；物体崇拜者 |  A person who believes in objectivism, a philosophical system developed by Ayn Rand, which emphasizes reason, individualism, and self-interest.  A person who worships or ascribes special significance to physical objects. | He's a staunch objectist and believes in the pursuit of rational self-interest. 他是一位坚定的客观主义者，相信追求理性的利己主义。 |  She collects antique dolls and some might consider her an objectist due to the reverence she holds for them. 她收集古董娃娃，有些人可能会认为她是一个物体崇拜者，因为她对这些娃娃非常崇敬。 | synonyms: materialist, egoist (in the context of Rand's philosophy) | antonyms: altruist, collectivist (in the context of Rand's philosophy) | noun
