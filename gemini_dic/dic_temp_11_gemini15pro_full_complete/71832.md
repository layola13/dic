Etymology->

From fleeting + -ness + -'s.  Fleeting comes from Middle English *fletynge, present participle of fleten (“to float, fly, flee”), from Old English flēotan (“to float, swim, flow”), from Proto-Germanic *fleutaną (“to float”), from Proto-Indo-European *plewd- (“to flow, float, swim”).
