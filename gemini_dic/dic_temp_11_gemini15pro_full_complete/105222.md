keystones:/ˈkiːˌstəʊnz/ (British) /ˈkiːˌstoʊnz/ (American)| n.|基石，拱心石，关键，要旨|A keystone is the central, wedge-shaped stone at the top of an arch, which locks the other stones in place.  It can also refer to something that is essential to the success or existence of something else. |The keystone of their defense is their goalkeeper.  他们的防守基石是他们的守门员。|synonyms: cornerstone, linchpin, centerpiece, foundation, core|antonyms: periphery, nonessential, unimportant|noun
