neocolonialists:/ˌniːoʊkəˈloʊniəlɪsts/
|n.|新殖民主义者|People who support or practice neocolonialism, the policy of using economic or political pressure to control or influence other countries, especially former dependencies.|Many critics argue that multinational corporations are acting as neocolonialists in developing countries.|Synonyms: imperialists, exploiters|Antonyms: anti-imperialists, liberators|Noun
