interunion:/ˌɪntərˈjuːnjən/
| adj.|工会间的，各工会之间的|Relating to or involving different labor unions.|例句：Interunion rivalry sometimes hinders effective collective bargaining. 工会间的竞争有时会阻碍有效的集体谈判。

例句：The interunion council met to discuss common concerns. 各工会理事会开会讨论共同关心的问题。
|近义词：interlabor, multiunion|反义词:intraunion|词性:形容词

Etymology:
inter- + union

inter-:  prefix meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally," from Latin inter (prep. and adv.) "between, among, in the midst of, within, during," from PIE *enter- "between, among" (source also of Sanskrit antar "between, within," Greek entera "intestines," Old Irish eter, Old Welsh ithr "between," Gothic undar, Old English under "under").

union: from Old French union, from Latin unionem (nominative unio) "unity, oneness, concord, a uniting, a pearl," from unus "one" (from PIE root *oi-no- "one, unique").
