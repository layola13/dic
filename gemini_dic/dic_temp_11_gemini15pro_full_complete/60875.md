elegy: /ˈɛlɪdʒi/ | n. | 挽歌，哀歌 | A poem of serious reflection, typically a lament for the dead. |  She wrote an elegy for her deceased father. 她为她已故的父亲写了一首挽歌。

| Example: His latest composition is an elegy for a lost friend.  他最新的作品是为一位失去的朋友而作的挽歌。 | Synonym: lament, dirge, requiem | Antonym: celebration, paean | noun
