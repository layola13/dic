interregent:/ˌɪntərˈriːdʒənt/
noun
摄政间,两个摄政期之间
a person who governs during the period between the death or abdication of a monarch and the accession or crowning of a successor.
The interregent oversaw the kingdom's affairs until the new king came of age.  摄政王在新国王成年之前负责王国的政务。
近义词: regent
反义词: subject, citizen
词性: n.


Etymology:
From Latin *interregnans*, present participle of *interregnare* ("to reign between"), from *inter-* ("between") + *regnare* ("to reign").
