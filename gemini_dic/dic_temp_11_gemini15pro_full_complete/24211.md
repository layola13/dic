bridge's:/ˈbrɪdʒɪz/| n.|桥的；桥梁的|It refers to something belonging to or associated with a bridge.|The bridge's structure is impressive.  这座桥的结构令人印象深刻。|近义词:of the bridge|反义词:None|词性:名词所有格

Etymology:
From Middle English *brigges*, from Old English *brycges*, genitive singular of *brycg* ("bridge").  The word ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *brugjo* and Proto-Indo-European *bʰruH-*.
