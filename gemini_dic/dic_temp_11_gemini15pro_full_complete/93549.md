imaginativeness:/ɪˌmædʒɪnətɪvnəs/| n. |想象力，创造力|The quality of being imaginative; the ability to form new ideas or images, especially in a creative or original way.|Her imaginativeness made her a gifted storyteller. 她的想象力使她成为一个有天赋的说书人。|近义词：creativity, ingenuity, inventiveness, originality, vision|反义词：dullness, unimaginativeness|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From imaginative + -ness.  "Imaginative" comes from Late Middle English: from medieval Latin imaginativus, from imaginari ‘to form an image,’ from imago, imagin- ‘image.’
