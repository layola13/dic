fighters:/ˈfaɪtərz/| n. |战士，战斗机，战斗人员|People who fight, either professionally (like boxers) or in a particular cause (like freedom fighters).|The firefighters bravely battled the blaze. 消防员勇敢地与大火搏斗。|
fighters:| n. |战斗机|Military aircraft designed for air-to-air combat.|The squadron of fighters intercepted the enemy bombers.  战斗机中队拦截了敌人的轰炸机。|
近义词：combatants, soldiers, warriors, warplanes, jets|反义词:pacifists, civilians|词性:noun


词源: From Middle English *fightere, from Old English *feohtere, *fihtere, agent noun from feohtian (“to fight”). Cognate with German Kämpfer.
