as-best-one-can:/æz ˈbɛst wʌn kæn/ | adv. | 尽力地，尽可能地 |  Means "to the best of one's ability," or "as well as one is able to, given the circumstances." It implies that there are limitations or challenges that might prevent perfect execution. | He repaired the broken vase as-best-one-can with glue and tape. 他用胶水和胶带尽力地修补了破碎的花瓶。 |  as best as possible, as well as possible, to the best of one's ability |  half-heartedly, carelessly | adverb
