half-frowningly:/ˌhɑːf ˈfraʊnɪŋli/
| adv. | 半皱眉地，略带不悦地 | With a slight frown; expressing mild displeasure or disapproval. | He looked at her half-frowningly, unsure of what to say. 他半皱眉地看着她，不知道该说什么。| She half-frowningly accepted the gift, clearly not thrilled with it. 她不太情愿地收下了礼物，显然对它并不感到兴奋。 | 近义词: disapprovingly, skeptically, doubtfully | 反义词: approvingly, enthusiastically, joyfully | 词性: 副词
