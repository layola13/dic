imparity:/ɪmˈpærəti/ | n. |不平等，不一致，奇数 |Lack of equality or similarity; difference.  Also, the state of being odd (not divisible by two).| The disparity in their ages was noticeable. 他们的年龄差异很明显。| The disparity between the rich and the poor is a serious problem. 贫富差距是一个严重的问题. | synonyms: inequality, difference, dissimilarity, disproportion, imbalance, variance, variation, unevenness, asymmetry; oddness | antonyms: equality, similarity, parity, evenness | noun


imparity:/ɪmˈpærəti/ | n. |奇数 |A number that is not divisible by two.| Seven is an disparity. 七是一个奇数。| The disparity of the sum of two odd numbers is always even. 两个奇数之和总是偶数. | synonyms: odd number | antonyms: even number | noun
