micro-evolutionary: /ˌmaɪkroʊˌɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri/ | adj. | 微进化的 | Relating to small-scale evolutionary changes within a species or population, typically over a relatively short period. | Example:  Micro-evolutionary changes can be observed in the development of antibiotic resistance in bacteria.  微进化的变化可以在细菌对抗生素耐药性的发展中观察到。

Example: The study focused on the micro-evolutionary processes driving adaptation to urban environments.  这项研究的重点是推动适应城市环境的微进化过程。| 近义词: intraspecific evolution | 反义词: macro-evolutionary | 词性: adjective
