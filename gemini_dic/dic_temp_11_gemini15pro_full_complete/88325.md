hippo:/ˈhɪpoʊ/ (US) /ˈhɪpəʊ/ (UK)| n.|河马|A large, thick-skinned, semiaquatic African mammal (Hippopotamus amphibius) with short legs and a large head and muzzle.|We saw a family of hippos wallowing in the mud. 我们看到一群河马在泥里打滚。|Synonyms: river horse, hippopotamus|Antonyms: None|Noun

hippo:/ˈhɪpoʊ/ (US) /ˈhɪpəʊ/ (UK)| n. (informal)|[俚语] 伪君子;虚伪的人|Someone who is outwardly very moral but whose behaviour shows they are not sincere.|He's such a hippo, preaching about honesty while cheating on his taxes. 他真是个伪君子，一边宣扬诚实，一边偷税漏税。|Synonyms: hypocrite, phony, fraud|Antonyms: sincere person, honest person|Noun
