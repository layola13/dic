lithophotography:/ˌlɪθəfəˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |石版摄影|A method of printing photographs using lithographic techniques, where the image is transferred from a stone or metal plate. |Example 1: Lithophotography was an important step in the development of photographic printing. 石版摄影是摄影印刷发展的重要一步。

Example 2:  He learned the intricate process of lithophotography from a master printer. 他从一位印刷大师那里学习了复杂的石版摄影工艺。|Synonym: photolithography |Antonym: N/A |noun
