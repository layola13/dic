bemoil:/bɪˈmɔɪl/
verb
使混乱；使烦恼；使焦急
to trouble or worry someone; to make someone confused or agitated
The unexpected news bemoiled her. 这突如其来的消息让她感到焦急。
The complex instructions completely bemoiled him.  这复杂的操作说明把他彻底搞糊涂了。
近义词: confuse, bewilder, disconcert, upset, agitate, perturb
反义词: calm, soothe, clarify, order
词性: verb
