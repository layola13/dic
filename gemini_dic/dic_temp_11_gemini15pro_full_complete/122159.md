mistrustfully:/mɪsˈtrʌstfəli/
adverb
不信任地，怀疑地
With doubt or suspicion.
She looked at him mistrustfully, unsure if he was telling the truth. 她怀疑地看着他，不确定他是否在说实话。
He eyed the stranger mistrustfully. 他不信任地盯着那个陌生人。

近义词:suspiciously, doubtfully, skeptically
反义词:trustingly, confidently, believingly
词性:adverb
