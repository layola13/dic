oppression:/əˈprɛʃən/| n.|压迫，压制，压抑|Prolonged cruel or unjust treatment or control.|The people lived under oppression for many years. 人民在压迫下生活了很多年。|近义词：tyranny, suppression, repression, persecution|反义词:freedom, liberty, liberation|noun

oppressive:/əˈprɛsɪv/| adj.|压迫的，压抑的，沉闷的|Causing discomfort by being excessive, intense, or difficult to endure.|The oppressive heat made it difficult to work. 闷热的天气使工作变得困难。|近义词：tyrannical, repressive, harsh, burdensome|反义词:freeing, liberating, relieving|adjective

oppressor:/əˈprɛsər/| n.|压迫者，压制者|A person or group that oppresses.|The people rose up against their oppressors. 人民起来反抗他们的压迫者。|近义词：tyrant, dictator, despot|反义词:liberator, emancipator|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *oppresser*, from Latin *opprimere* "press against, press down, suppress, overwhelm," from *ob* "against" (see ob-) + *premere* "to press" (see press (v.1)).
