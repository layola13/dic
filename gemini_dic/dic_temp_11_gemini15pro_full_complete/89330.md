homogenous:/ˌhoʊməˈdʒiːniəs/ /ˌhɒməˈdʒiːniəs/| adj.|同质的，同类的，均匀的|Of the same kind or nature; consisting of similar elements or parts.|The population of the village is remarkably homogenous. 这个村庄的人口构成非常同质。|近义词：uniform, similar, consistent, homogeneous|反义词：heterogeneous, diverse, varied|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin homogeneus, from Ancient Greek ὁμογενής (homogenḗs, “of the same race, of the same kind”), from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + γένος (génos, “race, kind”).
