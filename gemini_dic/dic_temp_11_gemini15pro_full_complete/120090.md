mim:/mɪm/ | n. | 模仿;模仿者;小丑 | An actor, especially a mime. |  She's a talented mim, able to convey a whole story without words. 她是一位才华横溢的哑剧演员，能够不用语言表达完整的故事。 | synonym: mime, impersonator | antonym: original | noun

mim:/mɪm/ | v. | 模仿 | To imitate (someone or their actions or words), typically in order to entertain or ridicule. | The child mimmed his father's gestures. 孩子模仿了父亲的手势。 | synonym: imitate, copy, ape | antonym: originate, create | verb
