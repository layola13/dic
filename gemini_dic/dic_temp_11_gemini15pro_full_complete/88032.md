highborn:/ˈhaɪbɔːrn/| adj.|出身高贵的，贵族出身的|Born into a family of noble rank; of noble parentage.  A person of noble birth or ancestry.|The highborn lady looked down on the commoners.|近义词：noble, aristocratic, patrician, blue-blooded|反义词：lowborn, common, plebeian|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Old English *hēahgeboren (attested only as hēahgeborene "nobly born"), from hēah "high" + geboren "born," past participle of beran "to bear."|
