brindled:/ˈbrɪnd(ə)ld/| adj.|有斑纹的，斑驳的|Having dark streaks or flecks on a lighter background.|The farmer owned a brindled cow.|近义词：streaked, mottled, tabby, spotted|反义词:solid, uniform|词性:形容词
Etymology: From brindle (n.), from brinded, past participle of brind (v.), "to make brown or tawny," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to brand (n.).
