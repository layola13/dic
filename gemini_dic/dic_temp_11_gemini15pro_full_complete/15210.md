backdates:/ˌbækˈdeɪts/| v. |回溯日期；追溯|To put an earlier date on (a document, cheque, etc.) than the actual date.  To predate.|The company backdated the contract to January 1st.  公司将合同的日期回溯到1月1日。|Synonyms: antedate, predate|Antonyms: postdate|verb

backdate:/ˈbækdeɪt/| n. |回溯日期|An earlier date put on a document than the actual date.|The backdate on the check was suspicious.  支票上的回溯日期很可疑。|Synonyms: antedate|Antonyms: postdate|noun
