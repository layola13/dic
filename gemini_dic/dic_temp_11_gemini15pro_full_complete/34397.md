classlessness:/ˈklɑːsləsnəs/
n.|无阶级性，无阶级状态|The state of lacking social classes or class distinctions.|The book explores the idea of classlessness in a utopian society. 这本书探讨了乌托邦社会中的无阶级概念。|近义词：equality, egalitarianism|反义词：classism, hierarchy|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From classless + -ness.  "Classless" itself comes from "class" (from Old French "classe" and ultimately Latin "classis") + the suffix "-less" (meaning "without").
