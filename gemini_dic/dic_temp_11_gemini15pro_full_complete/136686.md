nonsecular:/nɒnˈsɛkjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 世俗的，非宗教的 | Relating to worldly things or to things that are not regarded as religious, spiritual, or sacred; temporal. |  The school board voted to remove all nonsecular materials from the library. 学校董事会投票决定将所有世俗资料从图书馆移除。 |  The museum's collection included both religious and nonsecular art.  博物馆的收藏包括宗教和世俗艺术。 | Synonyms: worldly, temporal, earthly, profane, secular | Antonyms: religious, spiritual, sacred, holy, divine | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + secular.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "secular" comes from the Latin word "saecularis," meaning "of an age, occurring once in an age," later meaning "worldly."
