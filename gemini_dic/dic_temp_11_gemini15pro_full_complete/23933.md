breakwater: /ˈbreɪkˌwɔːtər/ (US) /ˈbreɪkˌwɔːtər/ (UK)| n. |防波堤，防浪堤|A structure built in the sea to protect a harbor or beach from the force of waves.|The breakwater provided a safe haven for the boats. 防波堤为船只提供了一个安全的避风港.|Synonyms: seawall, jetty, mole, groyne|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open sea")|Noun

Etymology: From "break" (reduce the force of) + "water" (waves).
