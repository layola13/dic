latticework: /ˈlætɪswɜːrk/ | n. | 格子细工，格子状结构 | openwork forming a network of crossing strips or bars. | The garden gate was made of intricate latticework. 花园大门是由复杂的格子细工制成的。| synonyms: fretwork, tracery, mesh, network, grid | antonyms: solid, mass | noun

Etymology->
From French *lattis* "lath work," from Old French *latta* "lath," and Germanic origin. Related to Old English *lætt* "lath."
