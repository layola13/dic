overaggravated: /ˌoʊvərˈæɡrəveɪtɪd/
| adj. | 过度加重的，过度恶化的 | Excessively aggravated; made worse than necessary.  It implies an unnecessary or disproportionate intensification of a negative situation. |  The already tense situation was overaggravated by the arrival of the police.  已经很紧张的局势因警察的到来而变得更加恶化。 |  exacerbated, worsened, inflamed |  ameliorated, improved, soothed | adjective
