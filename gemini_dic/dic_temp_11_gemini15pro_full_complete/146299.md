overfavors: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪvərz/
verb
偏袒；过分宠爱
To show excessive favoritism or kindness towards someone, often to the detriment of others.
He overfavors his youngest child, giving her everything she wants. 他过分宠爱最小的孩子，给她想要的一切。
The teacher was accused of overfavoring the athletes in her classes.  这位老师被指控在课堂上偏袒运动员。
近义词: pamper, coddle, spoil, indulge
反义词: neglect, ignore, slight
词性: verb
