cerris:/ˈsɛrɪs/| n.|土耳其栎，欧洲栓皮栎|A species of oak tree (Quercus cerris) native to southeastern Europe and Asia Minor, characterized by its hairy acorn cups.|The cerris oak is known for its distinctive acorns.|近义词: Turkey oak, Austrian oak|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From the Latin word "cerrus," meaning a kind of oak.
