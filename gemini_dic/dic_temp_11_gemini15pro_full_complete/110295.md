ligge: /ˈlɪɡə/ (Danish) | v. | 躺; 位于 | To lie down; to be situated. |  Jeg ligger på sofaen. (I am lying on the sofa.) |  Ligger kirken nær stationen? (Is the church located near the station?) | lie, be situated, be located | stand, sit | verb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Jeg ligger ofte på sofaen og læser. (I often lie on the sofa and read.)
Kirken ligger nær stationen. (The church is located near the station.)

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Jeg ligger og læser lige nu. (I am lying and reading right now. - This tense is not typically used with "ligge" in its meaning of "to be situated")
Han ligger og sover. (He is lying and sleeping.)


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Jeg har ligget på sofaen hele dagen. (I have been lying on the sofa all day.)
Bogen har ligget på bordet i flere dage. (The book has been lying on the table for several days.)

Jeg har ligget og læst hele eftermiddagen. (I have been lying and reading all afternoon.)
Han har ligget og sovet i flere timer. (He has been lying and sleeping for several hours.)
