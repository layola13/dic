noncorruptibly:/ˌnɒnkəˈrʌptɪbli/
adverb
不可腐蚀地，廉洁地
in a way that cannot be corrupted; in a way that is honest and has strong moral principles.
He served the public noncorruptibly for many years. 他廉洁地为公众服务了很多年。
近义词:incorruptibly, honestly, ethically
反义词:corruptly, dishonestly, unethically
词性:adverb

noncorruptible:/ˌnɒnkəˈrʌptɪbl/
adjective
不可腐蚀的，廉洁的
Not susceptible to corruption; honest and having strong moral principles.
She has a noncorruptible reputation. 她拥有廉洁的声誉。
近义词:incorruptible, honest, upright
反义词:corruptible, dishonest, venal
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + corruptible.  "Corruptible" comes from Late Latin corruptibilis, from Latin corrumpere "to destroy, spoil, corrupt," from com- "together" + rumpere "to break."  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
