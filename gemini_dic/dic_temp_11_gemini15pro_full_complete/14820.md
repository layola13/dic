axile:/ˈæksaɪl/
adjective
轴生的；与轴相关的
Situated or growing in the angle formed by a leaf or branch with the stem or axis from which it arises.
The axile placentation is found in lemon.  柠檬的胎座是轴生的。
The flowers have axile placentation. 这些花具有轴生胎座。
近义词:axial
反义词:terminal
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *axilis*, from *axis* ("axle, axis") + *-ilis* (adjective suffix).
