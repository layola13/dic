mell:/mel/| n. v.|气味, 嗅, 发出...的气味|A sweet or pleasant smell, often associated with fruit, flowers, or spices.  To smell something, typically with pleasure or interest; to emit a particular smell.|The air was filled with the sweet mell of ripe peaches. 空气中弥漫着成熟桃子的甜美气味。|近义词：aroma, scent, fragrance, odor|反义词:stench, stink|词性:名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mellen, from Old French meller (“to mix, meddle”), from Latin misceō (“I mix”).  The sense of "smell" likely developed from the idea of mixing or blending of scents.
