carrousel:/ˌkærəˈsɛl/| n.|旋转木马；行李传送带|A carrousel is a merry-go-round, a revolving circular platform with seats shaped like horses, other animals, or vehicles, on which people ride for amusement. It can also refer to a conveyor belt at an airport where passengers collect their luggage.|The children were thrilled to ride the carrousel at the fair. 孩子们在游乐场玩旋转木马玩得很开心。|The luggage carrousel was jammed with travelers waiting for their bags. 行李传送带挤满了等待行李的旅客。|近义词：merry-go-round, roundabout|反义词:|名词
