distillate:/ˈdɪstɪlɪt/ /ˈdɪstɪˌleɪt/| n.|蒸馏物，馏出物，精华|Something condensed from vapor produced by heating a substance, especially a purified liquid.  A substance extracted from another substance.|The whiskey is a distillate of fermented grain. 这种威士忌是发酵谷物的蒸馏物。|synonyms: essence, extract, concentrate, product, refinement |antonyms: mixture, solution, whole|noun


distillate:/ˈdɪstɪlɪt/ /ˈdɪstɪˌleɪt/| adj.|蒸馏的|Made by distilling.|Distillate water is used in laboratories. 蒸馏水用于实验室。|synonyms: purified, refined, extracted|antonyms: unrefined, crude, raw|adjective
