overmasterfully:/ˌoʊvərˈmæstərˌfʊli/
adverb
以压倒性的方式; 专横地; 强制地
in a supremely masterful manner; overwhelmingly and authoritatively.
The conductor overmasterfully led the orchestra through the complex symphony. 指挥家以极具驾驭力的方式带领乐队演奏了复杂的交响曲。
Near synonyms: overwhelmingly, decisively, compellingly, dominantly, imperiously
Antonyms: submissively, meekly, weakly
词性: 副词
