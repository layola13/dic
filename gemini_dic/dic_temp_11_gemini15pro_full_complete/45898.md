cutinize:/ˈkjuːtɪnaɪz/
verb
角质化；使角质化
to convert into cutin or keratin; to become like cutin or keratin. The epidermis cutinizes to form a protective layer. 表皮角质化形成保护层。
The cells on the surface of the leaf cutinize to prevent water loss. 叶片表面的细胞角质化以防止水分流失。
cornify, keratinize
soften
