bethump:/bɪˈθʌmp/
verb
重击，猛击
To strike heavily and repeatedly.
The giant bethumped the castle walls with his fists. 巨人用拳头猛击城堡的墙壁。
The waves bethumped the shore. 海浪拍打着海岸。
synonyms: pound, pummel, batter, thrash
antonyms: caress, stroke, tap
词性: 动词
