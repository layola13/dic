antileveling:/ˌæntiˈlɛvəlɪŋ/
adjective
反平等主义的，反对平均主义的
Opposing or discouraging leveling, especially social leveling; opposing equality.  Relating to or characterized by opposition to efforts to equalize things, especially social or economic conditions.
The antileveling policies of the government widened the gap between the rich and the poor. 政府的反平等政策加剧了贫富差距。
Near Synonyms:  elitist, hierarchical, inegalitarian, stratified
Antonyms: egalitarian, leveling, equalizing
词性: adjective
