mecha:/ˈmɛkə/ | n. | 机甲; 机械人 | A large, often humanoid fighting machine controlled by a pilot inside. It is a common trope in science fiction, particularly Japanese anime and manga. |  Example: The mecha's laser cannons blasted the enemy fortress. 机甲的激光炮轰炸了敌人的要塞。 | Example:  She piloted a giant mecha in the virtual reality game. 她在虚拟现实游戏中驾驶一个巨型机甲。 | 近义词: robot, giant robot, battlemech | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "human soldier") | 词性: noun

词源:
Borrowed from Japanese メカ (meka), abbreviation of メカニカル (mekanikaru, “mechanical”), from English *mechanical*.
