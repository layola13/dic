aggrandizement:/əˈɡrændɪzmənt/
n.|扩张；增大；强化|The act of increasing the power, status, or wealth of something or someone. |His insatiable desire for aggrandizement led him to conquer neighboring countries. 他对扩张的贪婪欲望导致他征服了邻国。|synonyms: enhancement, enlargement, augmentation, elevation, amplification|antonyms: diminution, reduction, decrease, lessening, weakening|noun

aggrandize:/əˈɡrændaɪz/
v.|增大，扩张；吹捧|To increase the power, status, or wealth of.|He sought to aggrandize himself by acquiring vast tracts of land. 他试图通过获取大片土地来提升自己的地位。|synonyms: enhance, enlarge, augment, elevate, amplify|antonyms: diminish, reduce, decrease, lessen, weaken|verb
