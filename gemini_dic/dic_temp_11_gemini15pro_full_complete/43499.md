craton:/ˈkreɪtɒn/ (UK), /ˈkreɪtɑːn/ (US)| n. |克拉通；稳定地块|A large, stable block of the earth's crust forming the nucleus of a continent.|The Canadian Shield is a large craton in North America. 加拿大地盾是北美的一个大型克拉通。|Synonyms: shield, platform|Antonyms: orogen|noun
