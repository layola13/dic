oblongly:/əˈblɒŋli/ /ɑːˈblɔːŋli/ | adv. | 呈长方形地，椭圆形地 | In a manner resembling an oblong; with a somewhat elongated or rectangular shape. | The leaves are oblongly shaped. 叶子呈长方形。|  The rug was oblongly placed in the center of the room.  地毯呈长方形摆放在房间中央。 | 近义词：lengthwise, longitudinally | 反义词: squarely, circularly | 词性: 副词

词源: From oblong + -ly.  Oblong from Latin oblongus "longer than broad," from ob "toward" + longus "long."
