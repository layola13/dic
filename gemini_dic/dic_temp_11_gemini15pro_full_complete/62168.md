encoders:/ɪnˈkoʊdərz/| n.|编码器|A device or program that converts data from one format to another, typically for transmission or storage.|The video encoders compressed the raw footage into a manageable file size. 视频编码器将原始素材压缩成可管理的文件大小。|近义词: coder, converter, translator|反义词: decoder|Noun
