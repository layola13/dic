manginess:/ˈmeɪndʒɪnəs/| n. |疥癣，污秽，肮脏|The state of being mangy; scabbiness and dirtiness, especially of an animal.|The dog's manginess was a clear sign of neglect. 这只狗的疥癣很明显是疏于照顾造成的。|近义词：scabies, mange, squalor, filthiness|反义词：cleanliness, hygiene|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mangy + -ness.  Mangy itself comes from the Old French *mangie (recorded as mangeure), from manger "to eat," referring to the itching and resulting skin damage caused by mites, leading to the animal's desire to scratch and "eat" its skin.
