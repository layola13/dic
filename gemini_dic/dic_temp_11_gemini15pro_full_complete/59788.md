ecotourism:/ˈiːkəʊtʊərɪzəm/| n. |生态旅游|Tourism directed toward exotic, often threatened, natural environments, especially to support conservation efforts and observe wildlife.|例句：Ecotourism is becoming increasingly popular as people seek more sustainable travel options. 生态旅游正变得越来越受欢迎，因为人们正在寻找更可持续的旅行选择。

例句：Costa Rica is a popular destination for ecotourism due to its rich biodiversity. 哥斯达黎加因其丰富的生物多样性而成为生态旅游的热门目的地。|近义词：sustainable tourism, green tourism, nature tourism|反义词:mass tourism|词性:noun
