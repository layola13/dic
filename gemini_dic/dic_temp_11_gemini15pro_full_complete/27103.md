californians: /ˌkælɪˈfɔːrniənz/
noun
加州人；加利福尼亚州人
People who live in or are from California.
Many Californians enjoy the beaches and warm weather. 许多加州人都喜欢沙滩和温暖的天气。
synonyms: Californians (no common single-word synonym), residents of California, people from California
antonyms: non-Californians
n.
