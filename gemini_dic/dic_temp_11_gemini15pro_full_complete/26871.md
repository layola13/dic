cajolingly: /kəˈdʒoʊlɪŋli/ | adv. | 诱哄地，甜言蜜语地 | in a way that persuades someone to do something by being nice to them or making promises to them | She smiled cajolingly at him, hoping he would agree to her request. 她诱哄地对他笑了笑，希望他会同意她的请求。| 近义词: persuasively, coaxingly, wheedlingly | 反义词: forcefully, threateningly | 词性: 副词

cajole: /kəˈdʒoʊl/ | vt. | 劝诱；哄骗 | persuade someone to do something by sustained coaxing or flattery | He cajoled her into going to the party with him. 他哄骗她和他一起去参加聚会。| 近义词: persuade, coax, wheedle | 反义词: force, threaten | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From French *cajoler*, perhaps from Old French *gaioler* "to chatter like a jay," from *gai* "jay," from Latin *gaius*.
