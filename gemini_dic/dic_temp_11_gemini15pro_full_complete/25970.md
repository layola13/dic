burr:/bɜːr/ (US) /bɜː(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|刺果，毛刺，粗糙的边缘，低沉的滚动声，|A rough or prickly seed case or flower head that clings to clothing and animal fur. Also, a thin ridge or rough edge left on metal by a cutting tool. As a verb, it means to make a whirring sound or to remove burrs from something.|

例句：The burrs stuck to my socks as I walked through the field.  我走过田野时，刺果粘在了我的袜子上。

例句：The machinist used a file to remove the burrs from the metal part.  机械师用锉刀去除金属零件上的毛刺。

近义词：prickle, barb, snag, whir, rough edge|反义词:smoothness|词性:noun, verb


burr:/bɜːr/ (US) /bɜː(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|方言，口音，|A whirring sound, especially in speech. It can also refer to a particular accent or dialect.|

例句：He spoke with a Scottish burr. 他说着一口苏格兰口音。

例句：The engine made a strange burring sound. 发动机发出一种奇怪的嗡嗡声。
