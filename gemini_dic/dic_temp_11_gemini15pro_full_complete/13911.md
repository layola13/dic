augustnesses:/ɔːˈɡʌstnəsɪz/
|n.|威严，庄严，崇高|The augustnesses of the royal family impressed everyone.  皇室的威严给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|
|n.|（复数）陛下（尊称皇室成员）|Their Augustnesses, the Emperor and Empress, will attend the ceremony. 陛下，天皇和皇后将出席仪式。|
|英文解析|Plural form of "augustness,"  derived from the adjective "august," meaning inspiring reverence or admiration; of supreme dignity or grandeur; majestic.  Used as a respectful term of address for royalty, particularly emperors and empresses.|
|例句|The augustnesses of the ancient temples filled the visitors with awe. 古老寺庙的庄严让游客们充满敬畏。|
|例句|Their Augustnesses graciously accepted the gifts from the delegation. 陛下们优雅地接受了代表团的礼物。|
|近义词|majesty, dignity, grandeur, solemnity, reverence|
|反义词|informality, commonness, ordinariness|
