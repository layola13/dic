gymnite:/ˈdʒɪmˌnaɪt/| n. |水镁石|A white, hydrous magnesium silicate mineral, Mg4Si6O15(OH)2·6H2O, occurring in fibrous or compact masses.  It is an alteration product of serpentine.|Gymnite is often found in association with serpentine deposits. 水镁石常与蛇纹石矿床共生。|Synonyms:  deweylite, sepiolite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
