gig:/ɡɪɡ/| n. vi.|演出；临时工作；轻便双轮马车；千兆字节|A live performance by a musician or group playing popular music, or a job, especially one that is temporary or that you do for a short period of time.|例句：The band is playing a gig in London next month.  这个乐队下个月将在伦敦演出。

|例句：He had a gig playing guitar at a wedding. 他有一份在婚礼上弹吉他的临时工作。|近义词：concert, performance, job, assignment|反义词: unemployment, permanence|词性:noun, verb

gig:/ɡɪɡ/| vi.|从事临时工作；<俚>嘲笑，愚弄|To work as a musician playing gigs, or to make fun of someone in a playful or unkind way.|例句：She gigged as a waitress while trying to become an actress.  她在努力成为一名演员的同时，做服务员的临时工作。

|例句：Don't gig me about my new haircut!  别拿我的新发型开玩笑！|近义词：work, perform, tease, mock|反义词: relax, praise|词性:verb
