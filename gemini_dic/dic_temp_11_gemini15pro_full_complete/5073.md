all-told:/ɔːlˈtɔʊld/| adv.|总共，合计|in total; altogether.  It refers to the final number or amount after everything has been counted or considered.|例句：All told, there were about fifty people present at the meeting.  总共有大约五十人出席了会议。

例句：The trip cost, all told, around $5,000.  这次旅行总共花费了大约5000美元。|近义词：altogether, in all, all in all, total|反义词:partially, incompletely|词性:副词
