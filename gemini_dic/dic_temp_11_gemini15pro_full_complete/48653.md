defenser:/dɪˈfɛnsər/ |n.|防御者，守卫者|A defenser is someone who protects or defends something or someone. |例句：He was a staunch defenser of human rights. 他是人权的坚定捍卫者。

例句：The city's defenders fought bravely against the invaders. 城市的守卫者勇敢地抵抗侵略者。


近义词：protector, guardian, champion, advocate|反义词：attacker, aggressor, opponent|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *defensor, from Latin dēfēnsor, from dēfendere (to defend).  Ultimately from de- (down, away) + fendere (to strike).
