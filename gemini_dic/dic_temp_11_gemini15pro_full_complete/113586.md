maccoboy: /məˈkɒbɔɪ/ | n. | 马科男孩（指牙买加种植和加工烟草的工人）| A Jamaican laborer employed in growing and curing tobacco. |  例句：The maccoboy worked tirelessly in the fields.  马科男孩不知疲倦地在田里工作。

例句：Maccoboys played a crucial role in Jamaica's tobacco industry. 马科男孩在牙买加的烟草业中发挥了至关重要的作用。

近义词：laborer, worker, farmhand | 反义词: employer, supervisor | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Spanish *macobo, from Taino *makabo.
