heathenesse:/ˈhiːðənɪsnɪs/ |n.|异教，不信教，异教徒|The state of being a heathen; paganism.  A lack of Christian or other religious belief. |  The missionaries sought to convert the heathenesse of the remote tribes.  传教士试图改变偏远部落的异教信仰。 |Synonyms: paganism, heathenism, idolatry, barbarism |Antonyms: Christianity, piety, devoutness| noun


heathenish:/ˈhiːðənɪʃ/ |adj.|异教的，不信教的，野蛮的|Characteristic of heathens; pagan.  Uncivilized; barbaric. | Their heathenish rituals involved animal sacrifice. 他们的异教仪式包括动物祭祀。  His table manners were considered quite heathenish. 他的餐桌礼仪被认为非常野蛮。 |Synonyms: pagan, barbarous, savage, uncivilized, irreligious |Antonyms: Christian, civilized, refined, pious, devout| adjective
