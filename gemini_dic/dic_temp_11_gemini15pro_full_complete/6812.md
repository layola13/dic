anarchist: /ˈænərkɪst/ (US), /ˈænəkɪst/ (UK)| n.|无政府主义者|A person who believes in or tries to bring about anarchy.  A person who rebels against any authority, established order, or conventional behavior.|例句：The anarchist called for the abolition of all forms of government.  这个无政府主义者呼吁废除一切形式的政府。

例句：She was labeled an anarchist because of her radical views. 由于她激进的观点，她被贴上了无政府主义者的标签。|近义词：rebel, revolutionary, insurgent, dissident|反义词：conformist, traditionalist|词性: noun


Etymology: From French *anarchiste*, from Ancient Greek *ἀναρχία* (anarkhia, “lack of a ruler, anarchy”), from *ἀν-* (an-, “without”) + *ἀρχή* (arkhē, “rule, authority, beginning”).
