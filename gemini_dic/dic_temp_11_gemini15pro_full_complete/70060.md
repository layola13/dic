fetwah: /ˈfæt.wɑː/ (US), /ˈfæt.wə/ (UK) | n. | 法特瓦，伊斯兰教令 | A legal opinion or decree issued by an Islamic religious leader or scholar. | The grand mufti issued a fetwah against smoking. 大穆夫提发布了禁止吸烟的教令。 |  The imam issued a fetwah permitting the marriage. 伊玛目颁发了一项允许结婚的法特瓦。| synonym: decree, ruling, edict, pronouncement | antonym:  (Due to the specific religious nature of a fetwah, a direct antonym is difficult to establish. One could perhaps consider "secular law" as a contrasting concept.) | noun
