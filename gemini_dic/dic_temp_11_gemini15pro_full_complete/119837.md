milden:/ˈmaɪldən/ | v. | 使缓和，使温和，减轻 | To make something less severe, harsh, or strong. | The government tried to milden the impact of the new tax. 政府试图减轻新税收的影响。| The warm sun mildened the cold air. 温暖的阳光使冷空气变得温和。 | 近义词: alleviate, soothe, moderate, lessen, temper | 反义词: aggravate, worsen, intensify, exacerbate | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *milden*, from Old English *mildnian* "to become mild," from *milde* "mild."
