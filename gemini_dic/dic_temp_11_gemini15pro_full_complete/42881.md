courtling: /ˈkɔːrtlɪŋ/
n.
求爱者；献殷勤的人
A person who courts someone; a suitor.
例句：The young courtling sent flowers to his beloved every day.  这位年轻的求爱者每天都给他的爱人送花。

例句：She had several courtlings vying for her attention.  有好几个追求者在争夺她的注意力。

近义词：suitor, admirer, wooer
反义词：rival, competitor


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *corteling, *curteling, equivalent to court + -ling.  Ultimately from Latin cohors (enclosure, retinue).
