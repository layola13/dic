coletit: /ˈkoʊlətɪt/| n.|颈圈，项圈|A close-fitting necklace or collar, typically made of lace, beads, or ribbon.|She wore a beautiful coletit made of pearls. 她戴着一个漂亮的珍珠颈圈。|近义词：necklace, collar, choker|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑loose necklace 松散的项链)|词性:noun


Etymology: From French *collerette*, diminutive of *collet*, meaning "collar," ultimately from Latin *collum*, "neck."
