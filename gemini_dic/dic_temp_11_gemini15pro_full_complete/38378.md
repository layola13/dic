conceptionalist:/kənˌsɛpʃənəlɪst/
n.|概念主义者；观念艺术家|An artist or writer who emphasizes the idea or concept behind the work, rather than the finished product itself.  A conceptualist prioritizes the intellectual or philosophical meaning over the aesthetic or material qualities. |The conceptionalist displayed a pile of bricks as his latest masterpiece, arguing that the concept of construction was the true art.  这位概念艺术家展示了一堆砖作为他的最新杰作，认为建造的概念才是真正的艺术。|Synonyms: conceptual artist, idea artist, minimalist|Antonyms: formalist, traditionalist|noun
