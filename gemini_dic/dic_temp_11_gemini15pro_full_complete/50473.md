depressedly:/dɪˈprɛsɪdli/ | adv. | 沮丧地，消沉地 | In a depressed manner; sadly; dejectedly. | She walked depressedly through the park after hearing the bad news. 她听到坏消息后沮丧地走过公园。|  He sighed depressedly and turned away. 他沮丧地叹了口气，转过身去。 | 近义词：sadly, glumly, dejectedly, despondently | 反义词：happily, cheerfully, joyfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  From the adjective "depressed" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Depressed" comes from the Latin "depressus," past participle of "deprimere" (to press down), from "de-" (down) + "premere" (to press).
