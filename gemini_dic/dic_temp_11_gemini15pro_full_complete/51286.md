detachable:/dɪˈtætʃəbl/| adj.|可拆卸的，可分开的|able to be detached or separated; removable|The hood of the jacket is detachable.  这件夹克的帽子是可拆卸的。|The keyboard is detachable from the tablet.  这个键盘可以从平板电脑上拆下来。|近义词：removable, separable, unattachable|反义词：fixed, permanent, attached|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from detach + -able.
