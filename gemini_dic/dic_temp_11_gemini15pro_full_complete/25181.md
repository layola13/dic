ETYMOLOGY->
From the merging of the names of two formerly separate cities, Buda and Pest, situated on opposite sides of the Danube River. Buda is of uncertain origin, possibly from a Slavic personal name or a word meaning "water". Pest likely derives from a Slavic word meaning "cave" or "oven," referring to a lime kiln in the area.
