covenanting:/ˈkʌvənəntɪŋ/
verb
立约，订立盟约
The act of making a formal agreement or promise.
例句：They were covenanting to protect the environment. 他们立约要保护环境。

例句：The two nations are covenanting for peace. 这两个国家正在缔结和平盟约。

近义词：pledging, promising, agreeing, contracting
反义词：reneging, breaking, violating
词性：verb (动名词)

covenant:/ˈkʌvənənt/
noun
契约，盟约
A formal agreement or promise.
例句：They made a covenant to never reveal the secret. 他们立约永远不泄露这个秘密。

例句：The ancient covenant bound the two tribes together.  古老的盟约将两个部落联系在一起。

近义词：agreement, contract, pledge, promise
反义词：disagreement, breach
词性：noun
