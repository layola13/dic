overimitate:/ˌoʊvərˈɪmɪteɪt/
verb
过度模仿
To copy or imitate someone or something excessively, often including unnecessary or irrelevant actions.
Children often overimitate the actions of adults, even when those actions are clearly unnecessary.  孩子们经常过度模仿成人的行为，即使这些行为显然是不必要的。
Monkeys were observed to overimitate the actions required to open a puzzle box.  观察到猴子过度模仿打开谜盒所需的动作。
近义词: ape, mimic, copy, emulate
反义词: ignore, neglect, disregard
词性:v.


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "imitate" (from Latin "imitari," meaning "to copy, portray")
