circumnutate:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnjuːteɪt/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈnuːteɪt/| v. |旋转运动，盘旋生长| (of a plant or a plant part) move in a revolving fashion.  The tip of the growing shoot circumnutates, tracing out an elliptical path.| The sunflower circumnutates as it grows, following the sun across the sky. 向日葵在生长过程中会旋转运动，跟随太阳穿过天空。|近义词：rotate, revolve, twirl|反义词:|词性:动词
