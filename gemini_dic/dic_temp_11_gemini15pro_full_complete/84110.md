half-justified:/ˌhɑːf ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 半对齐的，部分合理的 |  Aligned on one side (usually the left) but not the other, resulting in an uneven right margin. Also, partially justifiable or excusable. | The document was half-justified, looking somewhat unprofessional.  该文件是半对齐的，看起来有点不专业。 |  left-aligned, ragged right | fully justified, centered | adjective

half-justified:/ˌhɑːf ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪd/ | adj. |  部分合理的 |  Having some but not complete justification or excuse. | His actions were half-justified, given the provocation he faced. 他的行为情有可原，因为他受到了挑衅。 | excusable, understandable | inexcusable, unjustifiable | adjective
