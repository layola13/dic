nonmethodicalnesses:/ˌnɒnˌməθˈɒdɪkəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 非系统性；缺乏条理性；杂乱无章 |  The state or quality of being nonmethodical; lack of method or system; disorderliness.  |  His nonmethodicalnesses in handling the project led to delays and confusion. 他处理项目的非系统性导致了延误和混乱。 | synonyms: disorderliness, disorganization, haphazardness, randomness, lack of system | antonyms: methodicalness, orderliness, organization, systematization | noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + methodical (adjective meaning "done according to a systematic or regular procedure") + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality)
