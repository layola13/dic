cognation:/kɒɡˈneɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːɡˈneɪʃən/| n.|同源；同族关系；同族词|Relationship by descent from the same ancestor; words derived from the same root.|The English word "father" and the Latin word "pater" demonstrate cognation.|近义词：kinship, relationship, affinity, connection|反义词: unrelatedness, disassociation |noun

cognate:/ˈkɒɡneɪt/ /ˈkɑːɡneɪt/| adj. n.|同源的；同族的；同族词|Related by birth; descended from a common ancestor; (of a word) derived from the same root.|Spanish and Italian are cognate languages. |近义词：related, kindred, allied, similar|反义词: unrelated, dissimilar |adjective, noun
