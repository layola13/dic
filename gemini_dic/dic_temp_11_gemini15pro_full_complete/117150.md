meaningly:/ˈmiːnɪŋfəli/| adv.|有意义地，意味深长地|In a way that is significant or conveys a particular meaning.  It suggests an action or expression done with deliberate intention or purpose.|She smiled meaningfully at him. 她意味深长地对他笑了笑。|近义词：significantly, expressively, pointedly|反义词: aimlessly, vacantly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From meaning + -ly.  "Meaning" comes from Old English "mænan" (to mean, intend).
