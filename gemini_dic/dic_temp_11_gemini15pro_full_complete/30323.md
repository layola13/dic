censorship:/ˈsensərʃɪp/
n.|审查制度，审查|The censorship of the press prevented the truth from being reported. 新闻审查制度阻止了真相的报道。|
例句：Government censorship prevented the publication of the book. 政府审查阻止了这本书的出版。
例句：The film faced censorship due to its violent content.  这部电影因其暴力内容而面临审查。
近义词：suppression, restriction, control
反义词：freedom, openness, transparency
词性: noun

censorship:/ˈsensərʃɪp/
n.|审查|The censorship board cut several scenes from the movie. 审查委员会从电影中剪掉了几个场景。|
例句：Strict censorship was imposed during the war.  战争期间实行了严格的审查制度。
例句：The internet is subject to censorship in some countries.  在某些国家，互联网受到审查。
近义词：expurgation, redaction
反义词：liberty, permission
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "censura" (assessment, judgment, opinion), from "censere" (to assess, judge).
