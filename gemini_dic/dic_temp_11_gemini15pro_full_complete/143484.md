outgazes:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪzɪz/
verb
凝视胜过；以眼神压倒
to look at someone for a longer time than they can look at you, so that they look away
He outgazed his opponent, refusing to yield. 他以眼神压倒了他的对手，拒绝屈服。
近义词: stare down, outstare
反义词: avert one's gaze, look away
词性: v.
