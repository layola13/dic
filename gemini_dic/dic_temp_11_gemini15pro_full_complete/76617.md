gallooned:/ɡəˈluːnd/ | adj. |装饰着绸带的，（尤指像气球般）膨胀的 |Decorated with galloons (braids or ribbons); puffed out or swollen, especially like a balloon.|  The dress was gallooned with gold braid. 这条裙子用金色的穗带装饰。| 近义词：braided, ribboned, puffed, swollen | 反义词: plain, flat | 词性:形容词

词源:
From French *galonner*, from *galon* "braid, ribbon," perhaps ultimately of Germanic origin.
