out-of-state:/ˌaʊt əv ˈsteɪt/| adj.|外州的，州外的|Coming from or located outside a particular state.  Relating to or originating in a different state than the one being referred to.|例句：She is an out-of-state student. 她是一名外州的学生。

例句：Out-of-state tuition is higher. 外州的学费更高。|近义词：nonresident, foreign|反义词:in-state, resident|词性:adjective
