necromancy: /ˈnɛkrəˌmænsi/ | n. | 巫术，死灵法术 |  The practice of communicating with the dead, especially in order to predict the future or to influence the course of events.  It often involves rituals and spells. |  She was accused of practicing necromancy. 她被指控施行巫术。|  The ancient king consulted the necromancer to learn the outcome of the battle. 古代的国王咨询死灵法师，想知道战争的结果。 | Synonyms:  sorcery, witchcraft, black magic | Antonyms:  piety, holiness | noun
