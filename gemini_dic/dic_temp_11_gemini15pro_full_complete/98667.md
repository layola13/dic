insulars:/ˈɪnsjəlɚz/ | n. | 岛民；狭隘的人 | People who live on islands or who have a narrow, limited outlook. | The islanders maintained their traditional way of life. 岛民们保持着他们传统的生活方式。|  The insulars were suspicious of outsiders.  这些岛民对外部人员抱有怀疑态度。 | synonyms: islanders, provincials, narrow-minded people | antonyms: cosmopolitans, open-minded people | noun

insular:/ˈɪnsjəlɚ/ | adj. | 岛屿的；狭隘的 | Relating to or characteristic of an island; narrow or isolated in outlook or experience. |  The island community had an insular culture.  这个岛屿社区有一种insular文化。| The insular villagers rarely left their homes. 这些狭隘的村民很少离开他们的家。 | synonyms: isolated, narrow-minded, parochial | antonyms: cosmopolitan, broad-minded, open | adjective
