overfilter:/ˈoʊvərˌfɪltər/| v. |过度过滤|To filter excessively, often resulting in a loss of desirable qualities or characteristics.|The photographer overfiltered the image, making it look unnatural.  摄影师过度过滤了图像，使其看起来不自然。| The water was overfiltered and tasted bland. 水被过度过滤，尝起来淡而无味。 |overprocess, overrefine|underfilter|verb
overfilter:/ˈoʊvərˌfɪltər/| n. |过度过滤器 (较少用)|Something that overfilters.|  While not a common term, an "overfilter" could theoretically refer to a filter system designed to filter excessively. 虽然不是一个常用术语，“过度过滤器”理论上可以指设计用于过度过滤的过滤器系统。|  He designed an overfilter that removed all minerals from the water. 他设计了一个过度过滤器，可以去除水中的所有矿物质。 |overprocessor, overrefiner|underfilter|noun
