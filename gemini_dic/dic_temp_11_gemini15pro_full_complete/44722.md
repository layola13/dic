cruet:/ˈkruːɪt/| n.|调味瓶；盛调味品的容器|A small bottle for holding vinegar, oil, or other condiments. |She reached for the cruet of olive oil. 她伸手去拿橄榄油瓶。| The silver cruets gleamed on the dining table.  银制的调味瓶在餐桌上闪闪发光。 |近义词：bottle, container, vial|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 large container)|名词


Etymology:
From Old French *cruet* (small vessel), diminutive of *crue*, from Latin *crux* (pot, pitcher).
