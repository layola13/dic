dentistry: /ˈdɛntɪstri/ (US) /ˈdɛntɪstri/ (UK) | n. | 牙科；牙医学 | The science or practice of the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases of the teeth, gums, and related structures of the mouth. |  She is studying dentistry at university.  她正在大学学习牙医。 |  synonyms: dental medicine, odontology | antonyms: N/A | noun

From Late Latin *dentistria, from Latin dens (genitive dentis) "tooth" + -istry.
