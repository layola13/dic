darners:/ˈdɑːrnərz/
n.
缝补者；蜻蜓的一种
A person who darns socks or other clothes; a group of large, swift-flying dragonflies.
She is a skilled darners, mending all the holes in our socks. 她是一位熟练的缝补者，把我们袜子上的所有洞都补好了。
Darners are known for their agile flight.  蜻蜓以其敏捷的飞行而闻名。
近义词:mender, repairer (for person); dragonfly (for insect)
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:noun


darning:/ˈdɑːrnɪŋ/
n.
缝补；补过的部分
The act of mending holes in fabric; a mended place in fabric.
She spent the evening doing her darning. 她晚上都在缝缝补补。
The darning on the elbow of his sweater was almost invisible. 他毛衣肘部的补丁几乎看不见。
近义词:mending, patching, repair
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:noun


darn:/dɑːrn/
v.
缝补
To mend holes in fabric.
She darned the hole in her sock. 她把袜子上的洞补好了。
He carefully darned the tear in his jacket. 他仔细地缝补了夹克上的裂口。
近义词:mend, patch, repair, stitch
反义词:tear, rip
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dernen, from Old English *dernīan, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old Norse darna "to be hidden".  The meaning shifted from "to conceal by mending" to simply "to mend."
