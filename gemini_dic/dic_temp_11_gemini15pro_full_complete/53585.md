disburgeon:/dɪsˈbɜːrdʒən/
verb
萌芽，迅速发展（通常指负面事物）
To put forth buds; sprout. To expand or develop rapidly (usually used negatively, implying an undesirable or excessive growth).
The weeds disburgeoned in the neglected garden. 杂草在无人照料的花园里疯长。
The city's slums disburgeoned during the economic downturn. 经济衰退时期，城市的贫民窟迅速扩张。
近义词: proliferate, mushroom, burgeon, sprout, expand, grow rapidly
反义词: dwindle, shrink, contract, diminish, decrease, wither
词性: verb

disburgeon:/dɪsˈbɜːrdʒən/
noun
萌芽，迅速发展（通常指负面事物）
The act of burgeoning, especially in a negative or undesirable way.
The disburgeon of crime in the city was alarming. 城市犯罪的迅速蔓延令人担忧。
The recent disburgeon of online misinformation is a serious concern.  最近网络 misinformation 的泛滥是一个严重的问题。
近义词: proliferation, expansion, growth, increase
反义词: decline, decrease, reduction, shrinkage
词性: noun (rarely used)
