dearly:/ˈdɪrli/| adv.|昂贵地；深深地；亲切地|In a dear manner; with affection; at a high cost.  |She loved him dearly. 她深深地爱着他。|近义词：affectionately, fondly, expensively|反义词：cheaply, indifferently|词性:副词
词源：From Old English *dēorlīce (attested only as dēorlice), from dēore "dear, valuable" + -līce "-ly."
