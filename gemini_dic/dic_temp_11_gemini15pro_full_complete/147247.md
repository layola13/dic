overmelodiousness:/ˌōvərˌmelˈōdēəsnəs/
noun
过度旋律化；过分悦耳
Excessive melodiousness; an overabundance of melody.
The overmelodiousness of the music became cloying after a while. 这音乐过于旋律化，过了一会儿就变得腻味了。
近义词: saccharinity, sweetness
反义词: dissonance, harshness
词性: n.

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "melodious" (adj., from "melody" + "-ous") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
