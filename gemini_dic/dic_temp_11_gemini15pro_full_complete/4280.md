airwards:/ˈɛərwɔːrdz/
n.
空中奖项;无人机奖项
An international awards programme celebrating the best work with drones.  Recognising, rewarding and promoting positive drone use cases across industry, innovation and creativity.
The Airwards were established to recognize excellence in the drone industry.  空中奖项旨在表彰无人机行业的卓越成就。
Airwards has become a benchmark for drone innovation worldwide. 空中奖项已成为全球无人机创新的标杆。
synonyms: drone awards, unmanned aerial vehicle awards
