impregnatory:/ɪmˈprɛɡnəˌtɔːri/
adjective
浸渍的，使充满的
Making or tending to make pregnant; impregnating. Causing something to be filled with something else.
The impregnatory solution hardened the wood. 这种浸渍液使木材变硬了。
近义词: saturating, permeating, infusing
反义词: depleting, emptying


impregnate:/ˈɪmprɛɡneɪt/
verb
使怀孕，使充满，浸渍
(1) To make (a woman or female animal) pregnant. (2) To fill something with a substance.
(1) The queen was impregnated by the king. 女王被国王使怀孕了。
(2) The wood was impregnated with preservative. 木材浸渍了防腐剂。
近义词: fertilize, saturate, permeate
反义词: drain, empty

impregnation:/ˌɪmprɛɡˈneɪʃən/
noun
怀孕，浸渍，充满
The act of impregnating, or the state of being impregnated.
The impregnation of the wood with resin made it waterproof.  用树脂浸渍木材使其防水。
近义词: fertilization, saturation, permeation
反义词: depletion, emptying
