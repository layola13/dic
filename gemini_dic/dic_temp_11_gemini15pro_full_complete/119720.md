midst:/mɪdst/| n. |中间，当中|The flower bloomed in the midst of the desert.  这朵花在沙漠的中心绽放。|Example:  He stood in the midst of the crowd. 他站在人群当中。|Synonym: middle, center, heart|Antonym: edge, periphery, outside|Noun

midst:/mɪdst/| prep. |在…之中|She found peace in the midst of chaos.  她在混乱中找到了平静。|Example: In the midst of all the excitement, I forgot my keys. 在所有兴奋之中，我忘记了我的钥匙。|Synonym: among, amidst, within|Antonym: outside, beyond, without|Preposition
