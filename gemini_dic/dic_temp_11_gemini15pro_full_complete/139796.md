octene:/ˈɒktiːn/ /ˈɑːktiːn/| n. |辛烯|An alkene with the chemical formula C8H16. It is a hydrocarbon and a colorless liquid.  It has several isomers depending on the location of the double bond.|Octene is used in the production of plastics. 辛烯用于塑料生产。|Octene is a key component in some types of adhesives. 辛烯是某些类型粘合剂的关键成分。|近义词:alkene, olefin|反义词:alkane|词性:noun
