enthusiastic: /ɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/ | adj. | 热情的，热心的 | Showing intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval. | She was enthusiastic about the new project. 她对这个新项目充满热情。 |  He gave an enthusiastic speech. 他发表了一场热情洋溢的讲话。 | 近义词: eager, keen, ardent, passionate, avid | 反义词: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin enthusiasticus, from Greek enthousiastes, from enthousiazein 'be inspired or possessed by a god,' from en- 'in' + theos 'god.'
