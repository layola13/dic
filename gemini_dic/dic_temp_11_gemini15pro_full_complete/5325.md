allower:/əˈlaʊər/| n.|允许者；批准者|Someone who allows something; someone who gives permission.|The city council is the allower of building permits. 市议会是建筑许可的批准者。|例句：The allower of the event ensured everyone had a good time. 活动的批准者确保每个人都玩得开心。

例句：Only the allower can grant access to the restricted area. 只有批准者才能授予进入限制区域的权限。

近义词：granter, authorizer, permitter|反义词: forbidder, denier, prohibitor|词性:noun
