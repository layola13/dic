brokenheartedness:/ˈbroʊkənˌhɑːrtɪdnəs/| n. |心碎，悲痛|The state of being extremely sad because of a loss, especially the loss of love.|She suffered from intense brokenheartedness after her fiancé left her. 她未婚夫离开她后，她遭受了巨大的悲痛。|近义词：grief, sorrow, heartbreak, anguish|反义词：joy, happiness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From brokenhearted + -ness.  Brokenhearted comes from 'broken' + 'heart' + '-ed'.  The suffix '-ed' forms adjectives from nouns or verbs, and '-ness' forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
