castability:/ˌkæstəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可铸性；铸造性能 | The ease with which a metal or other material can be cast into a desired shape. | The castability of the alloy is excellent.  这种合金的可铸性非常好。| Synonyms: fluidity, pourability | Antonyms: uncastability (not a standard word), intractability | noun
