insurgency:/ɪnˈsɜːrdʒənsi/ | n. | 叛乱，暴动 | An organized rebellion aimed at overthrowing a constituted government through the use of subversion and armed conflict. | The government struggled to quell the insurgency in the southern provinces. 政府努力平息南部省份的叛乱。|  The insurgency threatened the stability of the region.  这场叛乱威胁着该地区的稳定。 |  rebellion, revolt, uprising |  obedience, submission | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insurgentia, from Latin insurgent-, insurgens, present participle of insurgere “to rise up,” from in- “against, upon, into” + surgere “to rise.”
