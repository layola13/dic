byssaceous: /bɪˈseɪʃəs/
adjective
丝状的；由细丝组成的；像棉絮的
Consisting of or resembling fine filaments or threads; like cotton or flax.
The byssaceous texture of the mushroom's cap made it difficult to clean. 这种蘑菇的菌盖有着丝状的质地，很难清洗。
The ancient tapestry had a byssaceous quality, delicate and easily torn.  这块古代挂毯有一种丝状的质感，很精致，容易撕破。
近义词: filamentous, fibrous, cottony, flossy
反义词: smooth, solid, compact
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *byssaceus, from byssus ("fine linen, cotton") + -aceous ("of the nature of, resembling"). Ultimately from Greek βύσσος (byssos, "fine linen, cotton").
