modulation:/ˌmɒdʒʊˈleɪʃn/ /ˌmɑːdʒəˈleɪʃn/| n.|调制；调节；变调|the act or process of changing the sound of your voice, or the quality of something, in order to create a particular effect|The violinist’s skillful modulation of the melody created a haunting atmosphere. 小提琴家娴熟的旋律调节营造了一种萦绕心头的氛围。

The modulation of light waves is essential for fiber optic communication. 光波的调制对于光纤通信至关重要。|近义词：variation, alteration, adjustment, modification|反义词: constancy, uniformity|词性:noun

modulation:/ˌmɒdʒʊˈleɪʃn/ /ˌmɑːdʒəˈleɪʃn/| n.|调幅；调频|in radio broadcasting, the process of adding information to an electronic signal that is being transmitted|The radio station uses frequency modulation (FM). 这家广播电台使用调频（FM）。

Amplitude modulation (AM) was an earlier form of radio broadcasting. 调幅（AM）是一种早期的广播形式。|近义词：tuning, adjusting|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
