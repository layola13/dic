macadam:/məˈkædəm/ | n. |碎石路;用碎石铺成的路面 | A road surface made with small broken stones. | The road was newly macadamed. 这条路新铺了碎石。 | synonyms: gravel road, crushed stone road | antonyms: paved road, asphalt road | noun

macadamize:/məˈkædəˌmaɪz/ | v. |用碎石铺路 | To cover (a road) with small broken stones. | They macadamized the road leading to the town.  他们用碎石铺设了通往城镇的道路。| synonyms: pave with macadam, gravel | antonyms: pave with asphalt, concrete | verb

Etymology: Named after John McAdam (1756–1836), a Scottish engineer who pioneered the method of road construction using broken stone.
