例句：The accusations of fraud were based on mere rumors and nonsubstantiation.  欺诈指控仅仅基于谣言和缺乏证据。|近义词：lack of proof, lack of evidence, unsubstantiated, unproven, unsupported|反义词:proof, evidence, substantiation, confirmation, validation|词性:noun
