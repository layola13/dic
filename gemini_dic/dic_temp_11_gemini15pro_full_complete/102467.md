jackrabbiting:/ˈdʒækˌræbɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 急速启动和停止；（尤指汽车）忽快忽慢地行驶 | Sudden and erratic acceleration and deceleration, especially of a vehicle. | The car was jackrabbiting through traffic, making it difficult for other drivers. 这辆车在车流中忽快忽慢地行驶，给其他司机带来了困难。| 近义词: jerky driving, erratic acceleration | 反义词: smooth driving, steady acceleration | 词性: 名词

词源:  This word is derived from the behavior of jackrabbits, which are known for their sudden bursts of speed and abrupt stops.  这个词来源于长耳大野兔的行为，它们以突然的快速奔跑和突然停止而闻名。
