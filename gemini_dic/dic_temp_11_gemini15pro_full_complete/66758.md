exoskeletons:/ˈeksoʊˌskelɪtənz/| n.|外骨骼|A rigid external covering for the body in some invertebrate animals, especially arthropods, providing both support and protection.|Lobsters, insects, and spiders all have exoskeletons.|Synonyms: shell, carapace, covering|Antonyms: endoskeleton|Noun
