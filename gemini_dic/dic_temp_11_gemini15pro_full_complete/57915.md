drillable:/ˈdrɪləbl/| adj.|可钻孔的，适合钻探的|Suitable for drilling or capable of being drilled.|例句：The rock formation is drillable.  这个岩层适合钻探。|例句：The material is not drillable with conventional equipment.  这种材料不能用常规设备钻孔。|近义词：penetrable, pierceable, borable|反义词：impenetrable, impervious|词性:形容词
词源:drill + -able. From "drill" (v., to bore a hole) + "-able" (suffix, capable of being).
