mazurka:/məˈzɜːrkə/ /məˈzʊərkə/| n. |玛祖卡舞曲；玛祖卡舞|A lively Polish folk dance in triple time, characterized by strong accents on the second or third beat.  It also refers to the music for this dance.|She danced a beautiful mazurka. 她跳了一支优美的玛祖卡舞曲。|近义词：polonaise, waltz|反义詞: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Polish *mazurek*, from Mazur 'Mazovia', a region of Poland.
