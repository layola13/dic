dorcahedron: /dɔːrkəˈhiːdrən/ | n. |十二面体|A polyhedron with twelve faces. Specifically, it often refers to a rhombic dodecahedron.  | A dorcahedron is a beautiful geometric shape. 十二面体是一个美丽的几何形状。| The mineral garnet often forms crystals in the shape of a dorcahedron. 石榴石矿物经常形成十二面体形状的晶体。 | 近义词: dodecahedron, polyhedron | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially sphere or amorphous shape) | 词性: noun
