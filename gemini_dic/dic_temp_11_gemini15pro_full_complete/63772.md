epics:/ˈɛpɪks/ | n. | 史诗; 壮举;  |A long poem, typically one derived from ancient oral tradition, narrating the deeds and adventures of heroic or legendary figures or the history of a nation.  A series of events considered to be particularly impressive or momentous.|  Homer's epics, the Iliad and the Odyssey, tell of the Trojan War. 荷马的史诗《伊利亚特》和《奥德赛》讲述了特洛伊战争的故事。| synonyms: saga, legend, chronicle | antonyms: anecdote, short story | noun

epic:/ˈɛpɪk/ | adj. | 史诗般的; 宏大的; |Heroic or grand in scale or character. | The journey across the mountains was an epic adventure.  穿越山脉的旅程是一次史诗般的冒险。 | synonyms: heroic, grand, monumental | antonyms: trivial, insignificant, minor | adjective
