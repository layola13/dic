frontierism:/ˌfrʌnˈtɪərɪzəm/
n.
开拓精神；拓荒主义；边疆主义
The spirit, ideology, or practice of settling and developing new frontiers, often associated with westward expansion in the United States.
The museum's exhibit explored the challenges and triumphs of frontierism in the 19th century.  博物馆的展览探索了19世纪拓荒主义的挑战和胜利。
expansionism, pioneering, colonialism
sedentary lifestyle, urbanism
noun
