coachability:/ˌkoʊtʃəˈbɪləti/| n.|可教练性；受教性|The quality of being open to and responsive to coaching or instruction.  Being able to be coached effectively.|Her coachability is one of her greatest strengths as an athlete. 她作为一名运动员，最大的优点之一就是她的可教练性。|Synonyms: receptiveness, teachability, trainability, malleability|Antonyms: unresponsiveness, resistance, stubbornness|noun
