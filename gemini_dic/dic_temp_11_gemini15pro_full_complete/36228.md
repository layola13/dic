cognatus:/kɒɡˈneɪtəs/ /kɑːɡˈneɪtəs/
adjective
同源的，同族的，同根的
From Latin cognātus (“related by blood, kindred”), from co- (“together”) + gnatus (“born”), perfect passive participle of nascor (“to be born”).
The English words "recognize" and "cognition" are cognate with "cognatus."  英语单词"recognize"和"cognition"与"cognatus"同源。
cognate, related, kindred
unrelated, dissimilar
