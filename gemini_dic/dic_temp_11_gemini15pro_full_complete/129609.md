non-conducting:/ˌnɒn kənˈdʌktɪŋ/ | adj. | 不导电的，非传导的 | Not able to conduct heat or electricity. |  Rubber is a non-conducting material. 橡胶是一种不导电的材料。 | The outer layer is a non-conducting plastic. 外层是不导电的塑料。 | 近义词: insulating, non-conductive | 反义词: conducting, conductive | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non < PIE *ne "not"
conducting (verb/adjective): from Latin conductus, past participle of conducere "to lead or bring together," from com- "together" + ducere "to lead."
