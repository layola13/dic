kufic:/ˈkufɪk/
adjective
库法的；库法体的
Relating to Kufa, a city in Iraq, or to a style of Arabic calligraphy developed there.
The manuscript was written in an elegant kufic script. 这份手稿是用优雅的库法体文字书写的。
Near synonyms:  calligraphic
Antonyms: N/A (style, not a quality with a direct opposite)
word type: adjective


kufic:/ˈkufɪk/
noun
库法体
An angular style of Arabic calligraphy developed in Kufa, Iraq.
Kufic is one of the oldest calligraphic forms of the Arabic script. 库法体是最古老的阿拉伯文字书法形式之一。
Near synonyms:  calligraphy, script
Antonyms: N/A (a type of script, not a quality with a direct opposite)
word type: noun


Etymology: From Kufa (الكوفة), a city in Iraq.  The Arabic name derives from كوف (kūf), meaning "gravel" or "pebbles," referring to the terrain.
