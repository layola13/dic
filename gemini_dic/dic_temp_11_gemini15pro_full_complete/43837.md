crepe:/kreɪp/ (US), /kreːp/ (UK)| n.| 薄饼，可丽饼 |A thin, light pancake. It can be served with sweet or savory fillings.| I had a delicious crepe filled with strawberries and cream. 我吃了一个美味的草莓奶油可丽饼。|Synonym: pancake, blini|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps thick pancake)|noun

crepe:/kreɪp/ (US), /kreːp/ (UK)| n.|绉绸，绉纱|A thin, crinkled fabric, typically of silk, wool, or rayon.|She wore a beautiful crepe dress to the party. 她穿了一件漂亮的绉纱连衣裙去参加聚会。|Synonym: chiffon, georgette|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps smooth fabric)|noun

Etymology: From French crêpe, from Old French crespe, from Latin crispus (“curled, wrinkled”).
