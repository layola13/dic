ostentatious:/ˌɑːstenˈteɪʃəs/ /ˌɒstenˈteɪʃəs/
adjective
炫耀的；卖弄的；招摇的
Characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to impress or attract notice.
例句：She wore an ostentatious diamond necklace. 她戴着一条炫耀的钻石项链。

例句：He drove an ostentatious sports car. 他开着一辆招摇的跑车。

近义词：showy, pretentious, flamboyant, flashy
反义词：modest, unassuming, understated, humble
词性: adjective
