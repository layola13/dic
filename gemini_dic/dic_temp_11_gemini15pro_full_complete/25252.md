buffer:/ˈbʌfər/ | n. v. |缓冲器，缓冲剂；起缓冲作用的人或物；（缓和冲击的）缓冲物|A buffer is something that prevents two things from harming each other by being between them.|The trees acted as a buffer against the strong winds. 这些树起到了抵御强风的作用。|近义词: cushion, shield, absorber |反义词: aggravator, intensifier |n.
buffer:/ˈbʌfər/ | v. |缓冲，减轻；保护|To buffer something is to protect it from harm by reducing the force of something else.|The government is trying to buffer the economy against the effects of the recession. 政府正在努力缓冲经济衰退的影响。|近义词: protect, cushion, shield |反义词: expose, worsen |v.
