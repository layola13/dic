microscope: /ˈmaɪkrəˌskoʊp/ (US) /ˈmaɪkrəskəʊp/ (UK)| n. |显微镜|An optical instrument used for viewing very small objects, typically magnified several hundred times.|Scientists use microscopes to study cells. 科学家用显微镜研究细胞。| The doctor examined the tissue sample under a microscope. 医生在显微镜下检查了组织样本。| synonyms: magnifier, lens | antonyms: telescope | noun
