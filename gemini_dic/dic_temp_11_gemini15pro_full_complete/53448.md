disapprobatory:/dɪsəˈprɒbətəri/ (US) /ˌdɪsəˈprɒbətri/ (UK)| adj.|不赞成的，表示反对的|Expressing disapproval; containing or conveying disapproval.|She gave him a disapprobatory look. 她不赞成地看了他一眼。|The teacher cast a disapprobatory glance at the noisy students. 老师不满地瞥了一眼吵闹的学生们。|近义词：disapproving, condemnatory, censorious|反义词：approving, commendatory, laudatory|词性:形容词

disapproval:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvəl/| n.|不赞成，反对|The feeling of not approving of something or someone.|Her disapproval of his behavior was obvious. 她对他行为的不满是显而易见的。|The plan met with widespread public disapproval.  这项计划遭到公众的广泛反对。|近义词：disapprobation, objection, condemnation|反义词：approval, acceptance, commendation|词性:名词

disapprove:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːv/| v.|不赞成，反对|To have or express an unfavorable opinion of.|I strongly disapprove of smoking. 我强烈反对吸烟。|He disapproved of her choice of friends. 他不赞成她选择的朋友。|近义词：object to, condemn, censure|反义词：approve of, commend, endorse|词性:动词
