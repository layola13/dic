cobalance:/koʊˈbæləns/| vt.|使钴平衡|To bring into or maintain a state of equilibrium with respect to cobalt.|The researchers are trying to cobalance the nutrients in the animal feed. 研究人员正试图平衡动物饲料中的营养成分，特别是钴含量。|近义词: stabilize, regulate|反义词: destabilize, deregulate|verb
