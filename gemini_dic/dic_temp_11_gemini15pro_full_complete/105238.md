khafre:/ˈkɑːfreɪ/| n. |卡夫拉（埃及第四王朝法老，胡夫之子）|Khafre, also known as Chephren, was an ancient Egyptian pharaoh of the 4th Dynasty during the Old Kingdom. He built the second largest pyramid at Giza.|Khafre's pyramid is slightly smaller than the Great Pyramid of Giza, built by his father, Khufu.|近义词:Chephren (another name for Khafre)|反义词:N/A|Noun
