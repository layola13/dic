overglaze:/ˈoʊvərˌɡleɪz/| adj. n.|釉上彩，釉上彩绘的|A type of ceramic decoration applied over a fired glaze.|The artist used bright overglaze enamels to decorate the vase. 艺术家用明亮的釉上彩瓷釉装饰花瓶。|近义词：on-glaze|反义词: underglaze|词性:形容词, 名词

overglaze:/ˈoʊvərˌɡleɪz/| v. |釉上彩绘|To decorate ceramics with colors applied over a fired glaze.|She overglazed the pottery with a delicate floral pattern.  她用精致的花卉图案为陶器上了釉上彩。|近义词：on-glaze|反义词: underglaze|词性:动词
