nonrevenger:/nɒn-rɪˈvɛndʒər/ | n. |非复仇者，不报复的人|Someone who does not seek revenge or retaliate for wrongs committed against them.  A person who chooses not to avenge themselves.|He is a nonrevenger, choosing forgiveness over retribution. 他是一个不报复的人，选择宽恕而不是报复。|近义词:forgiver, pacifist|反义词: avenger, retaliator |词性: noun

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "revenger" (from "revenge" - ultimately from Old French "revengier").  The word "revenge" itself traces back to the Latin "vindicare" meaning "to claim, avenge."
