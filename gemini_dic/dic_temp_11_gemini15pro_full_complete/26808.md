cage:/keɪdʒ/| n. vt.|笼子，囚禁|A structure of bars or wires in which birds or other animals are confined.|The canary was singing in its cage. 金丝雀在笼子里唱歌。|近义词：pen, enclosure, coop|反义词:freedom, liberty|noun
cage:/keɪdʒ/| vt.|把…关进笼子|To confine in a cage.|They caged the tiger. 他们把老虎关进了笼子。|近义词： imprison, confine, enclose|反义词:free, release, liberate|verb
