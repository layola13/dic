enantiopathy:/ɪˌnæntiˈɒpəθi/ |n.| 对抗疗法; 对抗病|A method of treating disease by producing effects opposite to those of the disease.|Enantiopathy is a traditional medical philosophy that believes in treating diseases with remedies that produce opposite effects to the symptoms. 对抗疗法是一种传统的医疗哲学，它主张用产生与症状相反效果的药物来治疗疾病。|Example 1:  Some practitioners believe enantiopathy is an effective method for treating certain illnesses. 一些医生认为对抗疗法是治疗某些疾病的有效方法。

Example 2: The concept of enantiopathy is central to some traditional medical systems. 对抗疗法的概念是某些传统医学体系的核心。|Synonym: allopathy, heteropathy|Antonym: homeopathy, isopathy|noun
