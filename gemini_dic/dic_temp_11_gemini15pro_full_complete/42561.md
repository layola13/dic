counterpace:/ˈkaʊntərpeɪs/| n.|对抗节奏，对应物，对应措施|An activity, plan, or device that is intended to counteract something undesirable. A measure taken to counter another's plans or actions.|The government introduced a counterpace to the rising inflation. 政府采取了应对措施以对抗不断上升的通货膨胀。|Near synonyms: countermeasure, countermove, antidote, riposte|Near antonyms:  inaction, encouragement, escalation|noun
