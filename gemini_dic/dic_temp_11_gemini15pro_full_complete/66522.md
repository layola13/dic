exhaustedly:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪdli/| adv.|精疲力竭地，疲惫不堪地|in a very tired way|She collapsed exhaustedly onto the sofa after the marathon. 她跑完马拉松后疲惫不堪地瘫倒在沙发上。| wearily, tiredly, fatiguedly | energetically, vigorously | adverb

Etymology->
From "exhausted" + "-ly". "Exhausted" comes from the Latin "exhaurire", meaning "to draw out completely", "to drain".
