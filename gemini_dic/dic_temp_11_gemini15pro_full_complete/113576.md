macc:/mæk/ | n. |  Media Access Control address (MAC address) | A unique identifier assigned to a network interface controller (NIC) for use as a network address in communications within a network segment. | Every network device has a unique MAC address. 每个网络设备都有一个唯一的MAC地址。|  Example: The technician needed the macc address of the computer to troubleshoot the network issue.  技术员需要这台电脑的MAC地址来解决网络问题。| synonym: MAC address, physical address, hardware address | antonym:  N/A (MAC addresses don't have opposites) | noun
