low-keying:/ˌloʊˈkiːɪŋ/
adjective
低调的；不张扬的
Playing down or minimizing; deliberately low-key.  Reducing the prominence or intensity of something.
例句：She wanted a low-key wedding, just close friends and family. 她想要一个低调的婚礼，只邀请亲朋好友。

例句：The celebration was very low-key, just a small dinner party. 庆祝活动非常低调，只是一个小型晚宴。

近义词：understated, subtle, quiet, restrained, subdued
反义词：ostentatious, flamboyant, showy, loud
词性:adjective
