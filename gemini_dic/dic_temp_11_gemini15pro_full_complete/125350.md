multilinguals:/ˌmʌltiˈlɪŋɡwəlz/ | n. | 多语者；使用多种语言的人 | People who can speak or understand multiple languages. | Many multilinguals find it easier to learn new languages. 许多多语者发现学习新语言更容易。| synonyms: polyglots, multilingual speakers | antonyms: monolinguals | noun

multilingual:/ˌmʌltiˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. | 多语的；使用多种语言的 | Able to speak or understand multiple languages. |  She is a multilingual individual, fluent in English, French, and Spanish. 她是一个多语种的人，精通英语、法语和西班牙语。| synonyms: polyglot, multilingual | antonyms: monolingual | adjective

Etymology:
From multi- (many) + lingual (relating to language).  The word "lingual" comes from Latin "lingua" meaning "tongue, language".
