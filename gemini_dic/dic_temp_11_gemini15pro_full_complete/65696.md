evennesses:/ˈiːvənɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
均匀性，平坦性，平稳性
The evenness of the playing field is crucial for a fair game.  比赛场地的平整度对于公平比赛至关重要。
The evenness of her breathing indicated she was asleep. 她呼吸的平稳表明她睡着了。
The evenness of the paint application gave the wall a professional look. 油漆涂抹的均匀性使墙壁看起来很专业。
Synonyms: uniformity, smoothness, regularity, consistency
Antonyms: unevenness, roughness, irregularity, inconsistency
