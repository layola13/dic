cryptography:/krɪpˈtɒɡrəfi/ /krɪpˈtɑːɡrəfi/| n. |密码学|The practice and study of techniques for secure communication in the presence of adversarial behavior.  It involves creating written or generated codes that allow information to be kept secret. |例句：Modern cryptography plays a vital role in ensuring online security. 现代密码学在保障网络安全方面起着至关重要的作用。

例句：He studied cryptography in college and now works for a cybersecurity firm. 他在大学学习密码学，现在在一家网络安全公司工作。|近义词：cryptology, code, cipher|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "plain text") |词性:noun
