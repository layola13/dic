detour:/ˈdiːtʊr/ /dɪˈtʊr/| n. vi. vt.|绕道，迂回；绕行的路|To detour is to take a longer route to avoid a problem on the usual route.|The road is closed, so we'll have to detour. 这条路封闭了，所以我们得绕道走。|近义词：bypass, divert, circumvent|反义词：go direct, go straight|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old French detourner "to turn aside," from de- "away" + tourner "to turn."
