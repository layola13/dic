ductilities:/ˌdʌkˈtɪlətiz/| n.|延展性，韧性，可塑性|The ductilities of various metals are important factors in engineering.  各种金属的延展性是工程中的重要因素。|例句：The ductilities of copper allow it to be easily drawn into wires. 铜的延展性使它很容易被拉成电线。

The material's ductilities make it suitable for a wide range of applications. 这种材料的延展性使其适用于广泛的应用。|近义词：malleability, flexibility, plasticity|反义词: brittleness, rigidity|词性: 名词 (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ductilitas*, from ductīlis (“ductile, easily led”), from dūcere (“to lead”).
