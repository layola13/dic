outflew:/ˌaʊtˈfluː/
verb
超过飞行；飞得比…远；胜过
to fly faster or further than something or someone else
The new plane outflew all its competitors. 这架新飞机的飞行速度超过了所有竞争对手。
surpassed, exceeded, outstripped
was outdone by, fell behind
