monographers:/məˈnɒɡrəfəz/
n.
专著作者；专题论文作者
Writers of monographs; writers of scholarly papers on a single specialized subject.
Several monographers have explored this topic in detail.  几位专著作者已经详细探讨了这个主题。
近义词: specialists, experts, scholars, writers
反义词: generalists, amateurs
词性: noun

monographer:/məˈnɒɡrəfər/
n.
专著作者；专题论文作者
A writer of a monograph; a writer of a scholarly paper on a single specialized subject.
The monographer spent years researching the history of this specific bird species.  这位专著作者花费数年研究了这种特定鸟类的历史。
近义词: specialist, expert, scholar, writer
反义词: generalist, amateur
词性: noun
