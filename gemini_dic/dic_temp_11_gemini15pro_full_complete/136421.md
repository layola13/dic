nonretraceable:/ˌnɒnrɪˈtreɪsəbl/ | adj. | 不可回溯的，无法追踪的 | Not able to be traced or followed back to its origin or source. |  The software uses a nonretraceable encryption algorithm, ensuring maximum security.  该软件使用不可回溯的加密算法，确保最大的安全性。

The killer used a nonretraceable phone to avoid being caught. 杀手使用无法追踪的电话以避免被抓。 | 近义词: untraceable, irreversible | 反义词: traceable, reversible | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (not) + retraceable (able to be retraced)  来自拉丁词根"trahere"，意思是"to draw or pull".  "re-" 前缀表示 "back" 或 "again".
