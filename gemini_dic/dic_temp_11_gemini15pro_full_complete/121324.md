misframing:/ˌmɪsˈfreɪmɪŋ/
noun
错误框架; 误导性框架; 歪曲; 曲解
Presenting or conceptualizing an issue or problem in a way that is inaccurate or misleading, leading to flawed understanding or solutions.
The misframing of the debate led to a unproductive discussion. 辩论的错误框架导致了无益的讨论。
The media's misframing of the event caused widespread public confusion. 媒体对事件的歪曲报道引起了公众的广泛困惑。
Synonyms: misrepresentation, distortion, mischaracterization
Antonyms: accurate representation, proper framing, clear depiction
n.
