negaters:/ˈnɛɡeɪtərz/
n.|否定者，否定词|People or things that negate something.  Those who deny or contradict. |The negaters in the debate skillfully refuted the opposition's points. 辩论中的否定者巧妙地反驳了对方的观点。|Near Synonyms: contradictors, dissenters, opponents, deniers|Antonyms: affirmers, supporters, proponents|noun
