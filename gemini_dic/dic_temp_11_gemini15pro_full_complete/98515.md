instant:/ˈɪnstənt/ /ˈɪnstnt/| n. adj.|瞬间，立即的|a very short period of time; immediate|例句：I'll be back in an instant. 我马上回来。|近义词：moment, second, immediate, instantaneous|反义词:delayed, gradual|词性:名词/形容词

instants:/ˈɪnstənts/ /ˈɪnstnts/| n. |瞬间，片刻|moments; very short periods of time|例句：The magic trick happened in mere instants.  魔术戏法就在瞬间发生。|近义词：moments, seconds|反义词:eons, ages|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "instant" (masculine), "instante" (feminine), from Latin "instantem" (nominative "instans"), present participle of "instare" "to stand near, press upon, urge, be at hand," from "in-" "on, upon" + "stare" "to stand."
