overgluts:/ˌoʊvərˈɡlʌts/ | v. n. | 过度供应，过剩 | to supply a market with more of a product than is needed or wanted; an excessive supply of something | The market is overglutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。| The current overglut of oil has driven prices down. 目前的石油供应过剩导致价格下跌。 |近义词：oversupply, surplus, glut |反义词: shortage, scarcity, deficit |词性:动词,名词
overglut:/ˌoʊvərˈɡlʌts/ | n. | 过剩，过度供应 | an excessive supply of something |  The overglut of information on the internet can be overwhelming. 互联网上信息的过剩可能会让人不知所措。 |  There's an overglut of housing in the area, which has depressed prices. 该地区住房供过于求，导致价格下跌。 |近义词：oversupply, surplus, glut |反义词: shortage, scarcity, deficit |词性:名词
