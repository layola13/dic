currency:/ˈkɜːrənsi/ (US) /ˈkʌrənsi/ (UK)| n.|货币；流通；流行|A system of money in general use in a particular country.|例句：The currency of the United States is the dollar. 美国的货币是美元。

例句：The euro is the currency of many European countries. 欧元是许多欧洲国家的货币。|近义词：money, cash, legal tender|反义词: N/A | Noun
