cross-border:/ˌkrɒsˈbɔːdə(r)/ /ˌkrɔːsˈbɔːrdər/| adj.|跨境的，跨界的|Involving or relating to two or more countries.|例句：Cross-border trade has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来跨境贸易大幅增长。|例句：The company specializes in cross-border mergers and acquisitions. 这家公司专门从事跨境并购。|近义词：international, transnational, global|反义词：domestic, national|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "cross" (meaning "to go across" or "extending across") + "border" (meaning "the edge or boundary of a country or region").  The word "cross" comes from Old Norse "kross," ultimately from Latin "crux" (cross).  The word "border" comes from Old French "bordure," from "bord" (edge).
