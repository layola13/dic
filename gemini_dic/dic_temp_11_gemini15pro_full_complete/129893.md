non-ionic:/ˌnɒn aɪˈɒnɪk/ /ˌnɑːn aɪˈɑːnɪk/
adjective
非离子型的
Not forming ions in solution.  A non-ionic substance does not dissociate into ions in solution.
Non-ionic detergents are less harsh than ionic ones. 非离子型洗涤剂比离子型洗涤剂更温和。
Non-ionic surfactants are used in many cleaning products. 非离子型表面活性剂用于许多清洁产品中。
synonyms: uncharged, neutral
antonyms: ionic, charged
词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
ionic (adjective): from ion + -ic.  Ion from Greek *ion "going," present participle of *ienai "to go."  Used by Faraday to describe charged particles moving in solution.
