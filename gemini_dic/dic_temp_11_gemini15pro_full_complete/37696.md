companioning:/kəmˈpænɪənɪŋ/
n.
陪伴；伴随；陪伴服务
The act of providing companionship or support to someone, often in a professional or therapeutic capacity.
She found great comfort in the companioning service offered by the hospice. 她从临终关怀机构提供的陪伴服务中找到了巨大的安慰。
companionship, support, attendance
neglect, abandonment
noun
