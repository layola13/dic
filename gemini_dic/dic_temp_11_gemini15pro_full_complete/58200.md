drudge:/drʌdʒ/| n. vi.| 苦工；做苦工的人；做单调乏味的工作|A person who does tedious, menial, or unpleasant work.|She felt like a drudge, constantly cleaning and cooking for her family. 她觉得自己像个苦力，不停地为家人做饭打扫。|近义词: toiler, laborer, menial, workhorse|反义词:idler, loafer|词性:noun, verb

drudge:/drʌdʒ/| vi.|做苦工；做单调乏味的工作|To work hard at tedious or menial tasks.|He drudged away at his homework all weekend. 他整个周末都在辛苦地做作业。|近义词: toil, slave, grind|反义词: relax, idle|词性:verb

词源: From Middle English druggen, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse draga (“to draw, drag”).
