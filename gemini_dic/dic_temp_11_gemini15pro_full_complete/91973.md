hypermyriorama:/ˌhaɪpərˌmɪriəˈrɑːmə/ | n. | 超万花筒; 可组合成各种画面的图片集 | A set of numerous pictorial plates which can be combined in various ways to form a large number of different pictures, scenes, or landscapes. | With the hypermyriorama, children could create endless imaginary worlds.  孩子们可以用超万花筒创造无尽的想象世界。|  panorama, diorama | N/A | noun

Etymology:
From hyper- + myriorama.  Hyper- is a prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessive," from Greek *hupér "over, above." Myriorama is from Greek *múrioi "ten thousand" + *hórama "sight, spectacle."
