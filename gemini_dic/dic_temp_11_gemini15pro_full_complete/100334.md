interspiration:/ˌɪntərspɪˈreɪʃən/| n.|相互激励，相互启发|The interspiration between the two artists led to a collaborative project.  两位艺术家之间的相互启发促成了一个合作项目。|例句：The interspiration between team members fostered a highly creative environment. 团队成员之间的相互激励营造了一个高度创造性的环境。

The interspiration of ideas from different cultures can lead to innovation. 来自不同文化的思想的相互启发可以带来创新。|近义词：interaction, collaboration, synergy, inspiration|反义词:isolation, separation|词性:名词

Etymology:  From Latin inter- (between, among) + spirare (to breathe).  Literally, "breathing between" or "breathing together," suggesting a reciprocal exchange of inspiration.
