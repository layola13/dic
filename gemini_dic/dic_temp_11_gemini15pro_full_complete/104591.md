kantianism:/ˈkæntiənɪzəm/
n.
康德主义
A system of philosophy based on the teachings of Immanuel Kant, especially his transcendental idealism and his categorical imperative.
Kantianism emphasizes the importance of reason and morality in understanding the world. 康德主义强调理性和道德在理解世界中的重要性。
Kantianism is a complex philosophy that requires careful study. 康德主义是一种需要仔细研究的复杂哲学。
近义词:transcendental idealism, critical philosophy
反义词:utilitarianism, empiricism
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Immanuel Kant (1724-1804), German philosopher + -ism.  源自德国哲学家伊曼努尔·康德 (1724-1804) 的名字 + -ism（主义）。
