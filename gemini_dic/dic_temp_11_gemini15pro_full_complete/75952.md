funiculate:/fjuːˈnɪkjʊleɪt/ /fəˈnɪkjələt/| adj.|索状的，绳状的|Resembling a rope or cord; consisting of small ropes or fibers.|The plant has funiculate roots that help it cling to rocks.  这种植物有绳状的根，帮助它 clinging to rocks.|近义词：ropelike, cordlike, stringy|反义词：smooth, flat|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *funiculus* (a small rope, diminutive of *funis* (rope)) + *-ate* (forming adjectives).
