divestment:/daɪˈvɛstmənt/ | n. | 撤资，剥离 | The act of selling off assets, such as stocks, bonds, or property.  It can also refer to the reduction of investments in certain areas or industries. |  The company announced its divestment of its coal mining operations. 该公司宣布剥离其煤矿业务。| synonyms: disposal, sale, liquidation | antonyms: investment, acquisition | noun
