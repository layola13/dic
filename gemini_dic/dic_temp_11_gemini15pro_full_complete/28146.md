capitulation:/kəˌpɪtʃʊˈleɪʃən/| n.|投降，屈服，让步|The capitulation of the enemy forces marked the end of the war. 敌军的投降标志着战争的结束。|The company's capitulation to the union's demands averted a strike. 公司对工会要求的让步避免了一场罢工。|近义词：surrender, submission, yielding|反义词：resistance, defiance|词性: noun

capitulation:/kəˌpɪtʃʊˈleɪʃən/| n.|（条款，协议等的）摘要，概要|The document provided a capitulation of the main points of the agreement.  该文件提供了协议要点的摘要。|He offered a brief capitulation of the events leading up to the crisis. 他简要概述了导致危机发生的事件。|近义词：summary, synopsis, recap|反义词：elaboration, expansion|词性: noun
