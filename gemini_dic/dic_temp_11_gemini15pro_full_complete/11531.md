arioso:/ˌɑːriˈoʊsoʊ/ /ˌæriˈoʊsoʊ/
n. adj. adv.|抒情的；咏叹调的；像咏叹调的|A passage in an opera or oratorio resembling an aria.  A piece of instrumental music resembling such a passage; melodious, lyrical.|The second movement is a lovely arioso for flute and harp. 第二乐章是长笛和竖琴演奏的优美抒情曲。|近义词：melodious, lyrical, cantabile|反义词：dissonant, jarring|词性: 名词, 形容词, 副词
Etymology: Italian, from aria 'air'.|来源于意大利语 aria，意为“空气”，“旋律”。
