outstole:/ˌaʊtˈstoʊl/
verb
超过，胜过（在偷窃方面）
surpassed in stealing; stole more than
Robin Hood outstole the Sheriff of Nottingham. 罗宾汉偷窃的财物比诺丁汉郡治安官多。
Synonyms: outdid, outstripped, surpassed
Antonyms:  lost to, was surpassed by
词性：动词
