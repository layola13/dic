massora:/məˈsɔːrə/
n.
马索拉文本，马索拉注释
The traditional body of critical text notes relating to the Hebrew Bible, its pronunciation, and accentuation.
The scribes who preserved and transmitted the Old Testament text from the 6th to the 10th century A.D. worked out a complex system of markings known as the Massora.  马索拉文本是公元6世纪到10世纪保存和传播旧约文本的抄写员制定的一套复杂的标记系统。

The Massora ensures the accurate transmission of the biblical text. 马索拉文本确保了圣经文本的准确传播。

近义词:textual criticism, annotation
反义词:None
词性:noun
