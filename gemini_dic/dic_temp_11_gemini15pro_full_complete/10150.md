apologizers:/əˈpɑləˌdʒaɪzərz/ | n. | 道歉者 | People who apologize; those who make apologies. |  Example 1: The apologizers offered sincere remorse for their actions. 道歉者对他们的行为表示真诚的悔恨。

Example 2:  A group of apologizers gathered to reconcile with those they had offended. 一群道歉者聚集在一起，与他们冒犯的人和解。 | Near Synonyms: penitents, contrite individuals | Antonyms: unrepentant individuals, offenders | Noun

PHRASE->
(由于apologizers是名词，很难组成常用词组，以下列出与"apologize"相关的词组)
1. apologize for something: 为某事道歉  (Example: He apologized for his rude behavior. 他为他的粗鲁行为道歉。)
2. apologize to someone: 向某人道歉 (Example: She apologized to her friend for being late. 她向她的朋友道歉迟到了。)
3. offer an apology:  提出道歉 (Example: The company offered an apology for the faulty product. 公司为有缺陷的产品道歉。)
4. accept an apology: 接受道歉 (Example: He accepted her apology and they made up. 他接受了她的道歉，他们和好了。)
5. owe someone an apology: 欠某人一个道歉 (Example: You owe him an apology for what you said. 你应该为你说的话向他道歉。)
