lonelier: /ˈləʊnliər/ (US) /ˈləʊnliə(r)/ (UK)| adj. |更孤独的，更寂寞的|Comparative form of lonely.  Feeling more isolated and alone than before or than someone else.| She felt lonelier than ever after her best friend moved away. 她最好的朋友搬走后，她感到比以往任何时候都更加孤独。|Near synonyms: more isolated, more desolate, more forlorn|Antonyms: less lonely, more sociable, more connected|adjective
