humdrum:/ˈhʌmˌdrʌm/
|adj.|单调的；乏味的|He led a humdrum existence. 他过着单调乏味的生活。|
|英文解析: Lacking excitement or variety; dull; monotonous.|例句：The meeting was humdrum and unproductive. 会议单调乏味，没有成果。|
|近义词：monotonous, dull, tedious, boring|
|反义词：exciting, thrilling, interesting, varied|
|词性：形容词|


词源:  From the reduplication of *hum* (onomatopoeic word suggesting a monotonous sound) + *drum* (also suggesting a monotonous sound).
