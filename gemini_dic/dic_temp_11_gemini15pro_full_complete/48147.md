decolonizing:/diːˈkɒlənaɪzɪŋ/
verb, adjective|去殖民化的，非殖民化的|The act of undoing colonialism, of freeing a country from being dependent on another country. It also refers to the process of changing people's thinking so they are no longer influenced by colonial ideas and attitudes.|Many countries in Africa and Asia underwent a process of decolonizing after World War II. 二战后，亚非许多国家都经历了非殖民化进程。|
synonyms: liberating, freeing, emancipating|
antonyms: colonizing, subjugating, imperializing|
