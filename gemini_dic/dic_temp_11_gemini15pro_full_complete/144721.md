oven-ready:/ˈʌvənˌredi/ | adj. | 可以直接烘烤的，准备充分的 |  Something that is oven-ready is fully prepared and needs only to be cooked in an oven.  It can also be used figuratively to describe something that is fully prepared and ready to be implemented or used. | The supermarket sells a range of oven-ready meals. 超市出售各种可以直接烘烤的餐食。|  The government presented an oven-ready Brexit deal. 政府提交了一份准备充分的脱欧协议。 |  pre-prepared, ready-made, prepared, complete | incomplete, unprepared | adjective
