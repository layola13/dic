collectivizes:/kəˈlɛktɪvaɪzɪz/
verb
集体化
To organize (something) on the basis of collectivism; to bring under collective ownership or control.
The government collectivized the farms. 政府将农场集体化了。
The farmers resisted the attempts to collectivize their land. 农民们抵制将他们的土地集体化的企图。
Synonyms: communalize, socialize, nationalize
Antonyms: privatize, individualize
詞性:v.
