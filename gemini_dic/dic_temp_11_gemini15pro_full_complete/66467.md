exequious:/ɪkˈsiːkwiəs/| adj.|谄媚的；奴从的；过分恭顺的|Excessively obedient or attentive; fawning.|例句：His exequious manner toward the CEO was obvious to everyone. 他对首席执行官谄媚的态度大家都很清楚。

例句：The exequious waiter hovered around the table, anticipating their every need.  那个奴颜婢膝的服务员在桌子旁转来转去，预料着他们的每一个需求。|近义词：servile, obsequious, fawning, sycophantic|反义词：independent, assertive, defiant|词性:形容词

词源:
Late Middle English: from Latin *exsequiosus, from exsequi ‘follow out, execute’ (see obsequious).
