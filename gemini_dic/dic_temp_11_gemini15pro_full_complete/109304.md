lendable:/ˈlɛndəbl/
adjective
可借出的；可贷款的
Suitable for lending; that can be lent.
The library has a wide selection of lendable books.  图书馆有很多可供外借的书籍。
This dress is lendable if you need it for the party.  如果你参加派对需要这件衣服，可以借给你。
Synonyms: loanable, borrowable
Antonyms: unlendable, non-lendable
词性: adjective
