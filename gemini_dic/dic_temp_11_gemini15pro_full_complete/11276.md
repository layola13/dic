arctic:/ˈɑːrktɪk/ (US) /ˈɑːktɪk/ (UK)| adj. n.|北极的，极寒的；北极|Relating to the regions around the North Pole.  The Arctic is a very cold and icy place.|The arctic fox has thick fur. 北极狐有厚厚的皮毛。|The Arctic is melting at an alarming rate. 北极正在以惊人的速度融化。|近义词：polar, frigid, glacial|反义词：tropical, equatorial|词性: adjective, noun

词源: From Late Middle English: from Old French artique, or via Latin from Greek arktikos ‘of the Great Bear’, from arktos ‘bear’ (also used to refer to the constellation).
