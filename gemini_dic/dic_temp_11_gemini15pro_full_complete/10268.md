appalachian:/ˌæpəˈleɪtʃən/ /ˌæpəˈlæʃən/| adj. n.|阿巴拉契亚的；阿巴拉契亚山脉|Relating to the Appalachian Mountains, a mountain range in eastern North America.  Also, a person from the Appalachian region.|例句：The Appalachian Trail is a popular hiking destination. 阿巴拉契亚步道是一个很受欢迎的徒步旅行目的地。

例句：Appalachian music is known for its distinctive sound. 阿巴拉契亚音乐以其独特的音色而闻名。|近义词：mountainous, highland (for the region)|反义词:coastal, lowland|词性:adjective, noun


Etymology->
From Appalachicola, a Native American tribe name, ultimately from *Apalachee, meaning "people on the other side."
