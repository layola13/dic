ovato:/oʊˈvɑːtoʊ/ /əʊˈvɑːtəʊ/
adjective
卵形的，椭圆形的
Having an oval shape, like an egg.
The leaves of the plant are ovato.  这种植物的叶子是卵形的。
The artist preferred canvases with an ovato shape.  这位艺术家偏爱椭圆形的画布。
近义词:oval, elliptical, ovate
反义词:round, circular, square
词性:adjective

ovato:/oʊˈvɑːtoʊ/ /əʊˈvɑːtəʊ/
noun
卵形
An oval shape. (Less common usage as a noun)
The ovato of the mirror was pleasing to the eye. 镜子的椭圆形令人赏心悦目。
The designer chose an ovato for the logo. 设计师为标志选择了椭圆形。
近义词:oval, ellipse
反义词:circle, square
词性:noun
