nondesirous:/ˌnɒndɪˈzaɪərəs/ | adj. | 不渴望的，不想要的 | Not desiring; not wanting. |  She was nondesirous of fame and fortune. 她不渴望名利。 |  unwilling, indifferent, apathetic | desirous, eager, keen | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + desirous.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "desirous" comes from the Old French "desiros," ultimately from the Latin "desiderare," meaning "to long for."
