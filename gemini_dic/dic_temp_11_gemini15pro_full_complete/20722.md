blame:/bleɪm/| n. vt.|责备，归咎；责任，过失|A statement or an act of assigning responsibility for a fault or wrong.|He blamed his brother for the broken window. 他把打破窗户的责任归咎于他的弟弟。|近义词：criticize, condemn, reproach, censure|反义词:praise, credit, exculpate|noun, verb
