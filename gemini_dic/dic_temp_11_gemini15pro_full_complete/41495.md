cornucopiate:/ˌkɔːrnjʊˈkoʊpiət/ /ˌkɔːrnjəˈkoʊpiət/
adjective
丰富的，充裕的，取之不尽的
Relating to or resembling a cornucopia; abundant, overflowing.
The Thanksgiving table was cornucopiate with delicious food. 感恩节的餐桌上摆满了美味佳肴。
The museum boasted a cornucopiate collection of ancient artifacts.  这家博物馆拥有丰富的古代文物收藏。
近义词: abundant, plentiful, copious, profuse, lavish
反义词: scarce, meager, scanty, sparse
词性: adjective

cornucopia:/ˌkɔːrnjʊˈkoʊpiə/ /ˌkɔːrnjəˈkoʊpiə/
noun
丰饶角；象征丰饶的事物
A horn of plenty, typically overflowing with fruit, flowers, and grain, symbolizing abundance and prosperity.
The painting depicted a cornucopia overflowing with fruits and vegetables. 这幅画描绘了一个装满水果和蔬菜的丰饶角。
The harvest festival featured a giant cornucopia as its centerpiece.  丰收节以一个巨大的丰饶角作为中心装饰。
近义词: abundance, plenty, bounty
反义词: scarcity, dearth, lack
词性: noun

词源: From Latin *cornū cōpiae* "horn of plenty", from *cornū* "horn" + *cōpiae* "plenty".
