hyperbolists:/haɪˈpɜːrbəlɪsts/
|n.|夸张者，吹牛者|People who habitually exaggerate; those who use hyperbole frequently.|The politician was known for being one of the great hyperbolists of his time.  这位政治家以他那个时代最伟大的夸张者之一而闻名。|Synonyms: exaggerators, embellishers, overstaters|Antonyms: understaters, minimizers|noun

hyperbole:/haɪˈpɜːrbəli/
|n.|夸张法，夸大之词|A figure of speech that uses extreme exaggeration to create emphasis or humor.| "I've told you a million times" is a common example of hyperbole. “我已经告诉你一百万次了”是夸张法的常见例子。|Synonyms: exaggeration, overstatement, magnification|Antonyms: understatement, litotes|noun
