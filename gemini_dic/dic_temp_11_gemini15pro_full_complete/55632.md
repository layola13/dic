ditches:/ˈdɪtʃɪz/
n.
沟渠;壕沟
A long narrow channel dug in the ground, typically used for drainage or irrigation.
The car swerved off the road and ended up in a ditch. 汽车偏离道路，最终掉进了一条沟里。
trenches, canals, gutters
ridges, mounds

词性:n.

ditches:/ˈdɪtʃɪz/
v.
抛弃; 丢弃 (第三人称单数)
To get rid of something or someone that is no longer wanted.
He ditched his old car for a new one. 他把旧车换成了新车。
abandon, discard, dump
keep, retain

词性:v.
