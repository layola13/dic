deescalates:/diːˈɛskəleɪts/
verb
缓和，降低，减轻
reduces the intensity of a conflict or potentially violent situation
The police officer deescalated the situation by speaking calmly to the protesters. 警察通过平静地与抗议者交谈缓和了局势。
The therapist helped deescalate the argument between the couple.  治疗师帮助缓和了这对夫妇之间的争吵。
synonyms: reduce, lessen, mitigate, alleviate, calm, soothe, pacify
antonyms: escalate, intensify, aggravate, worsen, exacerbate
