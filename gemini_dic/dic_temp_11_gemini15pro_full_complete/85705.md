havier:/ˈheɪviər/| adj.|更重的，更沉的|Comparative form of "heavy."  Means having more weight than something else.|The box is heavier than I expected. 这个箱子比我预料的要重。|近义词：weightier, more massive|反义词:lighter|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *hevier, *hevyere, comparative of hevy (“heavy”), from Old English hefigra, comparative of hefig (“heavy”).
