flanneled:/ˈflænəld/| adj.|法兰绒的；穿着法兰绒衣服的|Covered with or made of flannel.  Wearing clothes made of flannel.|例句：He wore a flanneled shirt. 他穿着一件法兰绒衬衫。|近义词：woolen, fleecy|反义词:cotton, silk|词性:形容词
词源: From flannel + -ed.  Flannel itself comes from Welsh *gwlanen, meaning "woollen article."
