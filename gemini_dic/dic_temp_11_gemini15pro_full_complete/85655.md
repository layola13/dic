haustellata:/ˌhɔːstəˈlɑːtə/ | n. | 吸口目（昆虫）| A large order of insects characterized by mouthparts adapted for sucking. | Example: Butterflies and moths belong to the order Haustellata. 蝴蝶和蛾子属于吸口目。| Example: The haustellata use their proboscis to feed on nectar. 吸口目昆虫用它们的喙吸食花蜜。|Synonym: None (It's a taxonomic classification)| Antonym: None (It's a taxonomic classification)| noun

Etymology: From Latin *haustellum* ("a little scoop, bucket"), diminutive of *haustrum* ("bucket, scoop") + -ata (New Latin taxonomic suffix to form order names).
