incubatory:/ˈɪŋkjʊbətɔːri/ /ˈɪŋkjʊbeɪtɔːri/| adj.|孵化的；潜伏的；培养的|Relating to incubation; the period between infection and the appearance of symptoms.  The process of keeping something, such as eggs, at the right temperature and humidity so that it develops.|The virus has an incubatory period of two to three weeks.|近义词：latent, dormant, gestational|反义词：active, manifest|词性:形容词
