nonconfining:/ˌnɒnkənˈfaɪnɪŋ/
adjective
非限制性的；不封闭的
Not limiting or restricting; allowing freedom of movement or action.
The horses roamed in a nonconfining pasture. 马儿在一片无拘无束的牧场上漫步。
Near synonyms: open, unrestricted, free, liberal
Antonyms: confining, restrictive, limiting, closed
词性: 形容词
