dyes:/daɪz/
noun
染料；染色剂
Substances used to color materials such as fabrics, hair, or plastics.
The artist used natural dyes to create vibrant colors in the tapestry.  艺术家用天然染料在挂毯上创作出鲜艳的色彩。
synonyms: colorants, pigments, stains
antonyms: bleaches
n.


dyes:/daɪz/
verb
染色
To give color to something using a dye.
She dyes her hair a different color every month. 她每个月都把头发染成不同的颜色。
synonyms: colors, tints, stains
antonyms: bleaches
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dēagian, from Proto-Germanic *daujaną (“to dye, color”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewh₂- (“to smoke, dust, darken”). Cognate with Dutch duigen, German taugen.  More at dew.
