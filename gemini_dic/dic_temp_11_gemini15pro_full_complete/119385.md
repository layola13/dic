microdensitometry: /ˌmaɪkroʊˌdɛnsɪˈtɑːmətri/ /ˌmaɪkroʊˌdɛnsɪˈtɒmətri/
| n. |显微光密度测定法；显微密度测定法| The measurement of the optical density of very small areas, such as spots on a photographic film, using a microdensitometer. | Microdensitometry was used to analyze the protein bands on the electrophoresis gel.  显微光密度测定法被用于分析电泳凝胶上的蛋白质条带。| Near synonyms: densitometry, optical density measurement | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
