demobilization: /ˌdiːˌmoʊbɪlɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 复员，遣散 | The process of releasing people from military service, especially at the end of a war. |  After World War II, demobilization of the troops took several months. 二战结束后，部队的复员工作持续了几个月。| synonyms: discharge, disbandment, release | antonyms: mobilization, enlistment, recruitment | noun

demobilize: /ˌdiːˈmoʊbɪlaɪz/ | v. | 复员，遣散 | To release from military service. | The government began to demobilize the army after the ceasefire. 停火后，政府开始遣散军队。| synonyms: discharge, disband, release | antonyms: mobilize, enlist, recruit | verb
