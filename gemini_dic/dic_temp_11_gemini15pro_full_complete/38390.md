conceptualist:/kənˈsɛptʃuəlɪst/
n.
概念主义者；观念艺术家
Someone who focuses on concepts and ideas rather than specific forms or appearances, especially in art.
Conceptual artists often use text, photography, or performance to explore ideas.  Many conceptual artists believe that the idea behind the artwork is more important than the finished object.
Sol LeWitt was a prominent conceptualist artist.  索尔·勒维特是一位杰出的概念主义艺术家。
The conceptualist argued that the true value of art lies in the concept, not the execution.  这位概念主义者认为，艺术的真正价值在于概念，而不是执行。
近义词: theorist, ideologue, idealist
反义词: pragmatist, realist
词性: noun
