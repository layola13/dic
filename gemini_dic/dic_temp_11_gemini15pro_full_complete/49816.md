demonstrably:/ˌdemənˈstreɪbli/ | adv. | 可论证地，明显地 | in a way that can be clearly shown to be true |  The claim is demonstrably false.  这个说法明显是错误的。 | demonstrably ->  clearly, evidently, obviously, patently, undeniably |  arguably, possibly, questionably | adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *demonstrabilis, from Latin demonstrō (“I point out, show, prove”) + -bilis.
