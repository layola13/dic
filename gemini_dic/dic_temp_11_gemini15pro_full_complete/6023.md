ameliorates:/əˈmiːliəreɪts/ | v. | 改善，改进 | to make something bad or unsatisfactory better |  The new policy ameliorates the negative effects of the previous one. 新政策改善了先前政策的负面影响。|  The medicine ameliorates the pain.  这种药减轻了疼痛。 | synonyms: improves, enhances, betters, upgrades | antonyms: worsens, exacerbates, deteriorates | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *melioratus, past participle of meliorare "to make better," from Latin melior "better."
