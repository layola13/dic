musashi:/muːˈsɑːʃi/ | n. |宫本武藏; 武藏(日本地名)| Miyamoto Musashi, a legendary Japanese swordsman, also known as Shinmen Takezō, Miyamoto Bennosuke, or by his Buddhist name, Niten Dōraku. Musashi is also the name of a former province of Japan, now part of Saitama and Tokyo prefectures. |  例句：Musashi is a celebrated figure in Japanese history. 宫本武藏是日本历史上著名的剑客。

例句：The book "Musashi" by Eiji Yoshikawa is a fictionalized account of his life. 吉川英治的小说《宫本武藏》是关于他人生的虚构故事。| 近义词：swordsman, samurai, warrior | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
