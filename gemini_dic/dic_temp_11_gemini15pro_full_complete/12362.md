ashing:/ˈæʃɪŋ/
n.
灰化；形成灰烬；灰烬
The process of burning something to ashes.
The ashing of the wood in the fireplace created a beautiful ember glow. 壁炉里木头的灰化过程产生了美丽的余烬光芒。

The ashing of the samples was completed in the laboratory.  样品的灰化在实验室完成。
incineration, burning
extinguishing, preserving
noun
