calcitic:/kælˈsɪtɪk/| adj.|方解石的；含方解石的|Composed of calcite; containing calcite.  Relating to or resembling calcite.|例句：The cave walls were covered in calcitic formations. 洞穴墙壁上覆盖着方解石构造。|例句：The soil sample contained a high percentage of calcitic material. 土壤样本中含有高比例的方解石物质。|近义词： calcareous|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms like siliceous or quartzose depending on the context)|词性: adjective
