mouths:/maʊðz/ /maʊθz/
|n.|嘴，口；入口，出口；河口|The plural form of "mouth".  It refers to the openings through which animals take in food and make sounds. It can also refer to the opening or entrance of something, like a cave or a river.|The horses had foamy mouths after their long run. 马匹长跑后嘴里都是泡沫。|The river flows into the sea at several mouths. 这条河流经几个河口流入大海。|synonyms: openings, orifices|antonyms: closures, exits|noun
