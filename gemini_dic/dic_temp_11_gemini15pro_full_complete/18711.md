bereavement: /bɪˈriːvmənt/| n.|丧失亲友，丧亲之痛|The death of her husband left her in a state of deep bereavement. 她丈夫的去世使她陷入了深深的悲痛之中。|例句：

Her recent bereavement has been very difficult for her.
她最近的丧亲之痛让她很难熬。

He offered his condolences on their bereavement.
他对他们的丧亲之痛表示哀悼。


|近义词：grief, mourning, loss, sorrow|反义词:joy, happiness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *berēafian "to deprive of, rob, dispossess," from be- + rēafian "to rob, plunder." The original meaning was closer to "robbery, deprivation," and the modern sense of "loss by death" developed later.
