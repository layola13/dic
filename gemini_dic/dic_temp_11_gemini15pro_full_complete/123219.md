monocot /ˈmɒnəkɒt/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəkɑːt/| n. |单子叶植物|A flowering plant with an embryo that bears a single cotyledon (seed leaf). Monocots also typically have leaves with parallel veins, scattered vascular bundles in the stem, the absence of a typical cambium, and an adventitious root system.|Example: Corn is a monocot, characterized by its single seed leaf and parallel leaf veins. 玉米是单子叶植物，其特征是具有单片子叶和并行的叶脉。

Example: Orchids and lilies are examples of monocots, showcasing the diversity of this plant group. 兰花和百合花是单子叶植物的例子，展示了这个植物群的多样性。|近义词: monocotyledon|反义词: dicot, dicotyledon|词性: noun
