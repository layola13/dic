liberating:/ˈlɪbəreɪtɪŋ/| adj.|解放的，使人自由的|Making one feel free from constraints or restrictions, giving a sense of relief and freedom.|例句：The experience of traveling alone can be incredibly liberating.  独自旅行的经历会让人感到无比自由。

例句：Breaking free from the toxic relationship was a liberating experience.  摆脱那段有害的关系是一种解脱的经历。|近义词：freeing, empowering, emancipating, releasing|反义词：restricting, confining, oppressive, stifling|词性:形容词
