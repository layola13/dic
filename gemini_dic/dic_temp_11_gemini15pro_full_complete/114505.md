maladjustment:/ˌmæləˈdʒʌstmənt/
|n.|失调，不适应|Inability to adapt oneself to the environment or circumstances.  The state of being maladjusted.|
|
例句：His maladjustment to school life made it difficult for him to learn. 他对学校生活的不适应使他学习困难。

例句：Social maladjustment can lead to isolation and loneliness.  社会不适应会导致孤立和孤独。

|
近义词：inadaptation, nonadaptation, unsuitability, incompatibility
|
反义词：adjustment, adaptation, suitability, compatibility
|
词性: noun
|
词源:  From mal- (badly) + adjustment.  Ultimately from Latin male (badly) and ad- (to) + justus (right, just).
|
