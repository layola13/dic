leve:/liːv/ | n. | 堤；堤岸；防波堤 | A bank or embankment built to prevent the overflow of a river or the encroachment of the sea. | Example: The levees held against the rising floodwaters.  堤坝挡住了上涨的洪水。| Synonym: embankment, dike, dyke | Antonym: ditch, trench | noun

leve:/liːv/ | v. | 征税；征收 | To impose or collect a tax, duty, or other assessment. | Example: The government levied a new tax on imported goods. 政府对进口商品征收了新的税。| Synonym: impose, assess, charge | Antonym: refund, remit | verb
