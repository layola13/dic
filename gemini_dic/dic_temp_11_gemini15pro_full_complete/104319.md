jut:/dʒʌt/
| n.|突出物；伸出部分|Something that juts out; a projection.|The jut of the rock provided a good view. 岩石的突出部分提供了一个很好的视野。|projection, overhang|recess, indentation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *jutten*, perhaps frequentative of *jetten* "to throw, cast," from Old North French *jetter* "to throw," from Late Latin *jactare*, frequentative of Latin *jacere* "to throw."
