fetishistic:/fɛtɪˈʃɪstɪk/
adjective
恋物的；迷信的；盲目崇拜的
Characterized by or relating to fetishism; excessively or irrationally devoted to something.
He has a fetishistic fascination with shoes. 他对鞋子有一种恋物癖式的迷恋。
Synonyms: obsessive, fixated, compulsive
Antonyms: indifferent, apathetic
词性: 形容词


fetish:/ˈfɛtɪʃ/
noun
恋物；迷信；盲目崇拜的对象
An inanimate object worshipped for its supposed magical powers or because it is considered to be inhabited by a spirit. Also, an excessive and irrational devotion or reverence for something.
He had a fetish for cleanliness. 他有洁癖。
Synonyms: obsession, fixation
Antonyms: indifference, apathy
词性: 名词
