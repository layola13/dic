bounce:/baʊns/| v. n.|弹跳，反弹；跳动；活力；(支票等)被拒付；|The child bounced the ball against the wall. 孩子把球往墙上扔，让它弹回来。|近义词：rebound, leap, spring, ricochet|反义词:fall, drop|词性:动词; 名词

词源:  From Middle English *bunsen, *bomsen, *bounsen (“to beat, bump, thump, knock”), likely from Anglo-Norman bondir, botir, from Old French bondir, boter (“to knock, push, beat, strike, thrust, butt”), from Frankish *bōtōn (“to beat”), from Proto-Germanic *bautaną (“to beat”). Cognate with Middle Dutch bōten (“to beat, strike, knock”), Old High German bōzan (“to beat, strike”). More at beat.
