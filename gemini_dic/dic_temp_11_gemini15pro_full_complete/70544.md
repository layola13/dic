filiety:/fɪˈliːɪti/ /fɪˈlaɪɪti/ | n. | 忠诚，忠实 |  Loyalty or faithfulness, especially to a lord or king.  Derived from Old French "fealté" and ultimately from Latin "fidelitas," meaning "faithfulness."  In modern usage, "fidelity" often refers to faithfulness in a broader sense, such as in marriage or to a cause or ideal. |  He swore filiety to the king. 他向国王宣誓效忠。|  The knight's filiety was unquestionable. 这位骑士的忠诚是毋庸置疑的。 | 近义词：loyalty, faithfulness, allegiance, devotion | 反义词：treachery, disloyalty, infidelity, perfidy | 词性：名词
