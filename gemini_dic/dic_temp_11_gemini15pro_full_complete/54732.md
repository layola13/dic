disparage:/dɪˈspærɪdʒ/| v. |贬低，诋毁，轻视|To speak of or treat slightingly; belittle:|He disparaged his competitor's products. 他贬低了竞争对手的产品。|The article disparaged the government's efforts to address the crisis. 这篇文章贬低了政府为解决危机所做的努力。|synonyms: belittle, denigrate, deprecate, detract from, minimize, underestimate, underrate|antonyms: praise, commend, extol, laud, compliment|verb

disparagement:/dɪˈspærɪdʒmənt/| n. |贬低，诋毁，轻蔑|The act of disparaging; belittlement:|Her constant disparagement of her colleagues created a hostile work environment. 她不断贬低同事，造成了敌对的工作环境。|He faced disparagement from critics who didn't understand his art. 他面临着那些不理解他艺术的评论家的贬低。|synonyms: belittlement, denigration, depreciation, detraction|antonyms: praise, commendation, extolment, laudation|noun
