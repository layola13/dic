anthill:/ˈænthɪl/| n. |蚁丘，蚁穴|A mound of earth, sand, pine needles, or other material formed by ants to house their colony.|The children were fascinated by the activity around the anthill. 孩子们被蚁丘周围的活动迷住了。|Synonyms: ant nest, ant colony, formicary|Antonyms: N/A|noun
