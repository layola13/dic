mascot's:/ˈmæskɒts/| n.|吉祥物(的)|The possessive form of "mascot," meaning belonging to a mascot.  It refers to something owned by or associated with a mascot.|The mascot's costume was torn after the game. 吉祥物的服装在比赛后被撕破了。|synonyms: emblem's, symbol's|antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "mascotte," meaning "lucky charm," originally from Provençal "mascoto," meaning "witchcraft," "sorcery."  Ultimately from "masca," meaning "witch," "mask."
