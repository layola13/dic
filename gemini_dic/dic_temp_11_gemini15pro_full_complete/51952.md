diacaustic:/ˌdaɪəˈkɔːstɪk/ |  adj. |焦散的| Having the properties of a caustic; burning or corrosive. | The diacaustic effect of the sun's rays can be seen on the bottom of a swimming pool.  太阳光线的焦散效应可以在游泳池底部看到。 | 近义词: caustic, burning| 反义词:  soothing, cooling |词性: adjective
