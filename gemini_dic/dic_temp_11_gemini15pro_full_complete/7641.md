annulling:/əˈnʌlɪŋ/
verb
废除；取消；宣告无效
The act of making something legally void.
The king annulled the marriage. 国王废除了这桩婚姻。
Synonyms: invalidating, nullifying, revoking, canceling, abolishing
Antonyms: validating, confirming, ratifying, upholding, establishing
verb
