biltong:/ˈbɪltɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɪltɔːŋ/ (US)| n. |牛肉干|A form of dried, cured meat that originated in Southern African countries. It is typically made from beef, but other meats like game can also be used. The meat is marinated in vinegar, spices, and salt, then air-dried. | I love snacking on biltong. 我喜欢吃牛肉干。|
|Synonyms: jerky, dried meat|
|Antonyms: N/A|
|noun|


bultong: This word seems to be a misspelling of "biltong". There is no officially recognized word "bultong" in English dictionaries.  The correct spelling is "biltong". Please refer to the entry for "biltong" above.
