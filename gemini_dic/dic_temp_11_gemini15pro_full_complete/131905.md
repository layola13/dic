noncontentiously:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɛnʃəsli/
adverb
无争议地，和平地
without argument or controversy; peacefully.
The matter was settled noncontentiously. 这件事和平解决了。
Synonym: amicably, peacefully, harmoniously
Antonym: contentiously, controversially, disputatiously
词性: 副词
