humanistics:/ˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪks/ | n. | 人文主义，人文学科 |  The study of human culture, especially its languages, literature, history, and philosophy.  |  She majored in humanistics in college. 她大学主修人文学科。 | synonyms: humanities, liberal arts | antonyms: sciences,  technical subjects | noun

humanistic:/ˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 人文主义的，人本主义的，人文精神的 | Relating to humanism or humanists; emphasizing human potential and values. | His approach to education is very humanistic. 他的教育方法非常人本主义。 | synonyms: humanitarian, compassionate, empathetic | antonyms: inhumane, unfeeling, scientific | adjective
