noninterpretative:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtɜːprɪtətɪv/ | adj. | 非解释性的，不带解释的 | Not providing interpretation or explanation; based on objective observation or description, without subjective analysis or evaluation. | The report provided a noninterpretative account of the events.  这份报告提供了对事件的非解释性描述。 | The scientist took noninterpretative measurements of the phenomenon.  科学家对这一现象进行了非解释性的测量。 | 近义词: descriptive, objective, factual | 反义词: interpretative, subjective, analytical | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "interpretative" (derived from "interpret," from Latin "interpretari" - to explain, expound).
