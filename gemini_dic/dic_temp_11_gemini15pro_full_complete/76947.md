gaps:/ɡæps/| n. |差距，缺口，空白，分歧|A gap is a space or opening in something, or the state of being open.|There's a wide gap between the rich and the poor. 贫富差距很大。|synonyms: opening, break, hole, chasm, rift|antonyms: closure, connection, bridge|noun

gaps:/ɡæps/| v. |使成缺口，裂开|(Of a surface or structure) to form an opening or crack.|The ice gaped, revealing the dark water below. 冰裂开了，露出了下面黑色的水。|synonyms: crack, split, open, divide|antonyms: close, seal, join|verb
