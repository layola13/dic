churrus:/ˈtʃʊrəs/ | n. |焦油大麻, 大麻脂|A resinous cannabis extract. It is typically dark brown and sticky.|Example 1: The police found a large quantity of churrus during the raid. 警方在突袭中发现了大量的焦油大麻。

Example 2: Churrus is often smoked in a chillum.  焦油大麻通常在一种叫做chillum的烟斗中吸食。|近义词：hashish, charas|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


词源:
From Hindi चरस (caras), from Sanskrit चरस् (caras, “hemp”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰers- (“to bristle”).
