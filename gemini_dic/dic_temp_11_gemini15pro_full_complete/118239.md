mercenarily:/ˌmɜːrsəˈnɛrəli/ | adv. | 唯利是图地；出于私利地 | In a mercenary way; motivated solely by personal gain. | He acted mercenarily, only interested in the financial rewards. 他唯利是图，只对经济回报感兴趣。 |  He married her mercenarily for her money. 他和她结婚是贪图她的钱财。| 近义词: avariciously, greedily, selfishly | 反义词: altruistically, selflessly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *mercenarius, from Latin mercennarius (“hired, earning wages”), from merces (“wages, pay, reward”), from merx (“merchandise”).
