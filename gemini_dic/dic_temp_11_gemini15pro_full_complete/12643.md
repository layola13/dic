assayers:/əˈseɪərz/
n.
化验员，鉴定员
People who examine and determine the content or quality of something, especially metals or ores.
The assayers tested the gold to determine its purity. 化验员测试了黄金以确定其纯度。
synonyms: testers, analysts, examiners
antonyms:  N/A (Lack of a direct antonym for a profession)
noun
