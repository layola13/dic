nonaccommodating:/ˌnɒnəˈkɒmədeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不迁就的，不乐于助人的
Unwilling to adapt or adjust to the needs or wishes of others; inflexible.
The nonaccommodating hotel staff refused to store our luggage after checkout.  这家不迁就的酒店员工拒绝在我们退房后存放行李。
He was so nonaccommodating that he wouldn't even move his car a few inches so I could get out of my parking space. 他非常不乐于助人，甚至不肯把他的车挪动几英寸，好让我从我的停车位里出来。
synonyms:inflexible, uncooperative, unhelpful, rigid, intractable
antonyms: accommodating, helpful, cooperative, flexible, adaptable
词性:形容词
