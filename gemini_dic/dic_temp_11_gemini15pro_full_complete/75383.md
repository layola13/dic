frowstiest:/ˈfraʊstiɪst/
adjective
最闷热的；最污浊的；最陈腐的
Most stale, stuffy, or musty.  Having the strongest unpleasant smell of something that has been shut up in a place that is not often aired.
The frowstiest room in the house was the attic, filled with old furniture and moth-eaten clothes.  房子里最闷的房间是阁楼，里面堆满了旧家具和蛀虫的衣服。
近义词:musty, stale, stuffy, airless, close
反义词:fresh, airy, ventilated

fusty:/ˈfʌsti/
adjective
闷热的；污浊的；陈腐的
Stale, stuffy, or musty.  Having an unpleasant smell of something that has been shut up in a place that is not often aired.
The library smelled fusty, like old books and dust. 图书馆闻起来很闷，像旧书和灰尘的味道。
近义词:musty, stale, stuffy, airless, close
反义词:fresh, airy, ventilated
