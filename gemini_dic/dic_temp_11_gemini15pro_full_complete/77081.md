garreteer:/ˌɡærəˈtɪər/| n.|阁楼诗人；穷作家；潦倒的文人|A writer, especially of poetry, of impoverished circumstances, often living in a garret. |例句：The garreteer poured his heart out in his melancholy verses.  这位阁楼诗人在他忧郁的诗句中倾诉衷肠。

例句：He lived the life of a garreteer, struggling to make ends meet with his meager earnings from writing. 他过着阁楼诗人的生活，靠微薄的写作收入勉强维持生计。|近义词：hack writer, struggling writer, impoverished writer|反义词: successful author, affluent writer|词性:noun
