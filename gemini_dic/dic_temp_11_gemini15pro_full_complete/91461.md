hylarchical:/ˌhaɪˈlɑːrkɪkəl/ | adj. |分层的，等级制的| Arranged in a hierarchy or according to levels of subordination. | Example 1: The company has a hylarchical structure, with the CEO at the top.  这家公司有一个等级森严的结构，首席执行官位于顶端。

Example 2: The hylarchical organization of the nervous system allows for complex processing of information. 神经系统的分层组织允许对信息进行复杂的处理。| 近义词: hierarchical, stratified, layered | 反义词: non-hierarchical, flat |词性:形容词
