chloroplast: /ˈklɔːrəˌplæst/ (US) /ˈklɔːrəˌplɑːst/ (UK)| n. |叶绿体|A plastid containing chlorophyll and in which photosynthesis takes place.  It is found in the cells of green plants and algae.|例句：Chloroplasts are the site of photosynthesis in plant cells. 叶绿体是植物细胞中进行光合作用的场所。

例句：The chloroplasts absorb sunlight to produce energy for the plant. 叶绿体吸收阳光为植物产生能量。|近义词: plastid |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology->
From chloro- + -plast.  Chloro- comes from the Greek "chloros" meaning "green." -Plast comes from the Greek "plastos" meaning "formed" or "molded."
