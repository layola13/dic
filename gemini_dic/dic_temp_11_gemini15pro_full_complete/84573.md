hammily:/ˈhæmɪli/| adv.|做作地，过火地，拙劣地|In a theatrical or exaggerated manner, often suggesting poor acting skills.|He delivered his lines hammily, overemphasizing every word. 他念台词念得很做作，每个字都过度强调。|例句：The villain laughed hammily, twirling his mustache.  反派做作地大笑，捻着他的胡子。

例句：She acted rather hammily in the school play. 她在学校的戏剧表演中演得有点过火。|近义词：theatrically, dramatically, exaggeratedly, affectedly, ostentatiously|反义词：subtly, naturally, realistically|词性:副词
