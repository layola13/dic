mercys:/ˈmɜːsiz/| n.|仁慈,怜悯,恩惠 (plural of mercy)|This word is the plural form of "mercy," meaning an act of kindness, compassion, or forgiveness, especially towards someone whom you have the power to punish or harm. It is rarely used in modern English. The singular form, "mercy," is much more common.|They begged for mercys from the king. 他们祈求国王的怜悯。(This sentence is grammatically incorrect and sounds archaic. It should be "They begged for mercy from the king.")|近义词：compassion, kindness, clemency, grace|反义词:cruelty, harshness|词性:noun
