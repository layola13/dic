brassicas:/ˈbræsɪkəz/
noun
芸苔属植物；十字花科蔬菜
Plants in the genus Brassica, which includes many important agricultural crops such as cabbage, cauliflower, and broccoli.
Brassicas are a good source of vitamins and minerals. 芸苔属植物是维生素和矿物质的良好来源。
Brassicas like kale and Brussels sprouts are known for their health benefits. 羽衣甘蓝和抱子甘蓝等芸苔属植物以其健康益处而闻名。
synonyms: cruciferous vegetables, cole crops
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with other plant families like Solanaceae or Legumes)
