levitates:/ˈlɛvɪteɪts/
verb
使… levitates (third-person singular simple present levitates, present participle levitating, simple past and past participle levitated)
To cause to rise and remain suspended in the air, especially by supernatural means.
The magician levitated the assistant above the stage.  魔术师把助手漂浮在舞台上方。
The monk, deep in meditation, appeared to levitate a few inches off the ground.  那位僧侣陷入了沉思，似乎漂浮在地面上几英寸。
近义词:float, hover, rise, ascend
反义词:sink, descend, fall, drop
词性:v.

levitate:/ˈlɛvɪteɪt/
verb
悬浮;飘浮
To rise or float in the air, especially as if by magic.
The ball appeared to levitate momentarily before falling back down. 球似乎在掉下来之前短暂地漂浮了一下。
In the dream, I was levitating above the city. 在梦中，我漂浮在城市上空。
近义词:float, hover, rise, ascend
反义词:sink, descend, fall, drop
词性:v.

词源: From Latin levitas ("lightness"), from levis ("light").
