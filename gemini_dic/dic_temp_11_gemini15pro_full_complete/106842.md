laborious:/ləˈbɔːriəs/| adj.|费力的，艰苦的，勤勉的|Requiring considerable time and effort.|例句：Writing a dictionary is a laborious task.  编写词典是一项艰苦的工作。|例句：The laborious journey took three days.  这趟艰苦的旅程花了三天时间。|近义词：arduous, strenuous, difficult, demanding, taxing|反义词：easy, simple, effortless|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *laborōsus*, from Latin labor (“work, toil, hardship”).
