didelphia:/daɪˈdɛlfiə/
noun
双孔类动物
A subclass of mammals, including marsupials, characterized by having two vaginas.
Didelphia are characterized by the presence of a pouch. 双孔类动物的特点是有育儿袋。

Didelphia includes kangaroos and opossums.  双孔类动物包括袋鼠和负鼠。

Synonym: Marsupialia
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, potentially placental mammals - Eutheria)
词性: noun
