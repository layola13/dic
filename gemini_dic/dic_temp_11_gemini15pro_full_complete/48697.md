defiantnesses:/dɪˈfaɪəntnəsɪz/
n.
反抗；挑衅；不服从
The state or quality of being defiant.  A plural form of defiantness.
His defiantnesses toward authority ultimately led to his expulsion. 他对权威的反抗最终导致他被开除。
The repeated defiantnesses of the child wore down the teacher's patience. 孩子屡次的反抗行为消磨了老师的耐心。
synonyms: rebelliousness, resistance, insubordination, disobedience, defiance
antonyms: obedience, submission, compliance, subservience
