it'll:/ˈɪtl/ (US), /ˈɪtl/ (UK)| contraction|它会（it will的缩写）|It's a contraction of "it will," used to express the simple future tense.|It'll be sunny tomorrow. 明天会是晴天。|近义词：it will|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider antonyms for the verb following "it'll," e.g., "it won't")|contraction

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
It'll rain later. 稍后会下雨。
It'll be a great party. 这将会是一个很棒的派对。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time next week, it'll be snowing. 下周这个时候，将会在下雪。
It'll be getting dark soon. 天很快就要黑了。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, it'll have been renovated. 到明年，它将已经翻新过了。
It'll have been ten years since we met. 到那时，我们将已经认识十年了。
