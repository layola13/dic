admins:/ˈædmɪnz/| n.|管理员（复数）|Plural form of "admin," short for "administrator." Refers to individuals with privileged access and control over a computer system, network, or online platform.|The admins banned several users for violating the terms of service. 管理员因违反服务条款而封禁了几个用户。|近义词：administrators, moderators, managers|反义词:users, members|noun
