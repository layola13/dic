brouhaha:/ˌbruːˈhɑːhɑː/ (US), /ˌbruːˈhɑːhɑː/ (UK)| n.|喧闹，骚动，吵闹|An uproar or commotion; a hubbub.|例句：The media created a brouhaha over the politician's remarks. 媒体对这位政客的言论大肆渲染，引起了轩然大波。

例句：There was a huge brouhaha when the wrong winner was announced.  宣布了错误的获胜者后，现场一片哗然。


|近义词：commotion, uproar, hubbub, fuss, furor|反义词:peace, quiet, calm, tranquility|词性:noun
