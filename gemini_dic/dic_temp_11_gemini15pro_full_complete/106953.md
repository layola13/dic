laciniolate:/ləˌsɪniˈoʊleɪt/ | adj. | 细裂的，边缘呈不规则深裂的 | Fringed or deeply cut, as the edge of a leaf or petal.  Characterized by narrow, irregular lobes or segments. | The laciniolate leaves of the dandelion are easily recognizable. 蒲公英的细裂叶很容易辨认。|  fringed, dissected, lobed, incised | entire, smooth | adjective
