backhandedness:/ˌbækˈhændɪdnəs/| n.|虚情假意；间接讽刺；反讽|The compliment seemed to have a certain backhandedness to it. 这句恭维话似乎有点儿虚情假意。|例句：He delivered a backhanded compliment about her outfit. 他拐弯抹角地讽刺了她的穿着。

例句：Her backhandedness made it difficult to know if she was being sincere. 她的讽刺让人很难知道她是否真诚。|近义词：sarcasm, irony, insincerity, double-dealing|反义词:sincerity, directness, honesty|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From backhanded + -ness.  Backhanded originally referred to a blow with the back of the hand, later extending to compliments or remarks that appear positive but have an implied negative meaning. The suffix -ness forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
