absolvable:/əbˈzɒlvəbl/ /əbˈsɑːlvəbl/
adjective
可赦免的; 可免除的
Capable of being absolved, forgiven, or excused.
His actions, though regrettable, were ultimately absolvable. 他的行为虽然令人遗憾，但最终还是可以原谅的。
The priest assured him that his sins were absolvable.  神父向他保证他的罪是可以赦免的。
近义词: forgivable, pardonable, excusable
反义词: unpardonable, unforgivable, inexcusable
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *absolvabilis, from Latin absolvere "to absolve"  (from ab- "from, away" + solvere "to loosen, release").
