liana:/liˈɑːnə/ /liˈænə/| n. | 藤本植物；攀缘植物 | A woody climbing plant that hangs from trees, especially in tropical rainforests. |  The monkeys swung from liana to liana. 猴子在藤蔓间荡来荡去。| The dense canopy was interwoven with lianas. 茂密的树冠交织着藤蔓。 | synonyms: vine, creeper, climber | antonyms: tree, shrub | noun
