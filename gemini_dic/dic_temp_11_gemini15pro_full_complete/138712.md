nulling:/ˈnʌlɪŋ/
n.|无效化，归零，调零|The process of reducing or eliminating a signal or value, often to zero.  This can apply to various fields, such as electronics, astronomy, and law.|The telescope uses a coronagraph for nulling the light from the star, allowing observation of fainter nearby objects. 望远镜使用日冕仪来消除来自恒星的光，从而可以观测附近较暗的物体。|synonyms: canceling, suppressing, neutralizing, zeroing|antonyms: amplifying, enhancing, increasing|noun
nulling:/ˈnʌlɪŋ/
v.|使无效，使归零，使调零|The act of making something null or void; reducing a value to zero.|The engineer nulled the signal to eliminate interference. 工程师使信号无效以消除干扰。|synonyms: canceling, suppressing, neutralizing, zeroing|antonyms: amplifying, enhancing, increasing|verb
