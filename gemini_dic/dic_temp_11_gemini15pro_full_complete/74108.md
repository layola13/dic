fouchyauchi: /fuːʃiˈaʊtʃi/ | adj. | 暴躁的，易怒的 | Easily annoyed and irritable; bad-tempered. |  Example: He gets very fouchyauchi when he's tired. 他累了的时候就会变得很暴躁。| Example:  Don't be so fouchyauchi! 别这么易怒！| 近义词: irritable, grumpy, touchy, cranky, testy | 反义词: patient, good-natured, amiable, easygoing | 词性: 形容词

词源:  This word likely originates from Scots or a similar dialect and is not standard English. It is possibly a blend of "fushionless" (meaning weak or ineffective) and "touchy." Its informal and dialectal nature makes a precise etymology difficult.
