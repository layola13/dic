coacher:/ˈkoʊtʃər/| n.|教练; 指导者|A person who trains or instructs someone in a particular skill or sport.  A coach can also be a mentor or guide in other areas of life.|My basketball coach is very strict. 我的篮球教练很严格。|The life coach helped me find my purpose.  这位人生教练帮助我找到了人生目标。|instructor, trainer, tutor, mentor, guide|student, pupil|noun
