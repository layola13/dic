mistressing:/ˈmɪstrɪsɪŋ/| n.|情妇；女主人；女教师；女校长|A woman who has a sexual relationship with a married man; a woman in a position of authority or control.|例句：He left his wife for his mistress. 他为了他的情妇离开了妻子。

例句：She was the mistress of the house. 她是这栋房子的女主人。

近义词：lover, paramour, concubine (for the first meaning); headmistress, principal (for the third and fourth meanings)|反义词：wife (for the first meaning)|词性: 名词


mistressing:/ˈmɪstrɪsɪŋ/| v. (mistress的现在分词)|支配；控制；精通|To be skilled in something; to have control or power over someone or something.|例句：She mistressed the art of negotiation. 她精通谈判的艺术。

例句：He mistressed his emotions. 他控制住了自己的情绪。

近义词：control, dominate, master|反义词：submit, surrender|词性: 动词
