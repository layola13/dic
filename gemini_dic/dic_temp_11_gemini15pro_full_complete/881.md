absurdism:/əbˈsɜːrdɪzəm/ /əbˈzɜːrdɪzəm/
n.
荒诞主义
The philosophical and literary doctrine that human beings live in an irrational, meaningless universe.
The play is a classic example of absurdism.  这出戏是荒诞主义的经典范例。

近义词: nihilism, existentialism
反义词: rationalism, meaningfulness
词性: noun
