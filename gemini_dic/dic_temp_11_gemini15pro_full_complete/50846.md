desecrators:/ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪtərz/
n.
亵渎者，污损者
Those who treat sacred things with disrespect or contempt; those who violate or profane something holy or revered.
The desecrators of the ancient temple were condemned by all. 那些亵渎古代寺庙的人受到了所有人的谴责。
synonyms: profaner, violator, defiler
antonyms: protector, guardian, reverer
noun


desecrate:/ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪt/
vt.
亵渎，污损
To treat (a sacred place or thing) with violent disrespect; violate.
The vandals desecrated the church by spray-painting graffiti on its walls. 故意破坏者在教堂墙壁上喷涂涂鸦，亵渎了教堂。
synonyms: profane, violate, defile
antonyms: consecrate, sanctify, venerate
verb
