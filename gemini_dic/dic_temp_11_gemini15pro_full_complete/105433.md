kid: /kɪd/ | v. | 开玩笑;戏弄;欺骗 | To tease or joke with someone. | Don't kid me! I know you're lying.  别骗我了! 我知道你在撒谎。| Example: He was just kidding when he said he was quitting. 他说他要辞职只是在开玩笑。 | Synonyms: tease, joke, fool, deceive | Antonyms: be serious, tell the truth | Verb
