half-annoyed:/ˌhæf əˈnɔɪd/
adjective
略感恼火的，有点生气的
Slightly irritated or annoyed; mildly displeased.
He gave a half-annoyed sigh. 他略带恼火地叹了口气。
She looked at him with a half-annoyed, half-amused expression. 她看着他，表情一半恼火一半好笑。
近义词: irritated, miffed, disgruntled, slightly annoyed
反义词: pleased, delighted, happy, content
词性: adjective

Etymology:
"half-" (Old English healf) + "annoyed" (from Old French anoier, to annoy)
