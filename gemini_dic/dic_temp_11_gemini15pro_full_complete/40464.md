contretemps:/ˈkɒntrətɒ̃/ (UK), /ˌkɒntrətɑːn/ (US)| n. |意外事件；不顺利；小事故|An unforeseen and unfortunate event; a hitch; a minor mishap.|例句：There was a slight contretemps over the seating arrangements.  座位安排上出了一点小意外。

例句：The meeting was delayed by a series of unfortunate contretemps. 会议由于一系列不幸的小事故而被推迟了。|近义词：mishap, setback, snag, hitch, difficulty, problem|反义词：success, boon, blessing|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *contretemps*, literally "against time". From *contre* ("against") + *temps* ("time").
