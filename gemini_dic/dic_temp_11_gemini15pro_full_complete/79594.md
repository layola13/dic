gloriation:/ˌɡlɔːriˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 颂扬，赞美，吹捧 | The act of glorifying or praising something or someone highly. |  The gloriation of violence in the media is a serious problem. 媒体对暴力的颂扬是一个严重的问题。| synonyms: praise, exaltation, extolment, laudation | antonyms: denigration, disparagement, belittlement | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gloriātiōn- (stem of gloriātiō ), from Latin gloriātus (past participle of gloriārī  "to boast, glory, take pride"), from glōria "glory, fame, renown."
