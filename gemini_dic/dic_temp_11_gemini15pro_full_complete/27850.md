canonistic:/kænəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 教会法规的，符合教会法的 | Relating to or based on canon law, the body of laws and regulations made by or adopted by ecclesiastical authority, especially for the government of the Christian organization. |  The canonistic rules regarding marriage were strictly enforced. 教会关于婚姻的法规被严格执行。|  The scholar presented a canonistic interpretation of the historical document.  这位学者对这份历史文献提出了教会法的解释。 | 近义词: canonical, ecclesiastical | 反义词: secular, lay | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *canonicus* (canonical) + -istic.
