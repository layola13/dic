disrobe:/dɪsˈroʊb/
verb
脱衣服，使脱光衣服
To remove one's clothes; to undress.
The king disrobed for his nightly bath. 国王脱下衣服准备进行夜间沐浴。
近义词:undress, strip, uncover
反义词:dress, clothe, robe
词性:v.

disrobe:/dɪsˈroʊb/
noun
脱衣服，脱光衣服的状态
The act of removing one's clothes.
Her disrobe was quick and efficient. 她脱衣服的动作迅速而有效率。
近义词:undressing
反义词:dressing
词性:n.
