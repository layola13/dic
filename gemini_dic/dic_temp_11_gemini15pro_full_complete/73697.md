forgiving:/fərˈɡɪvɪŋ/
adj.
宽容的，宽恕的
inclined to forgive; readily overlooking and excusing offenses.
She has a very forgiving nature. 她生性非常宽容。
近义词: lenient, merciful, tolerant
反义词: unforgiving, resentful, vindictive
词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb "forgive," which comes from Old English "forgiefan," meaning "to give, grant, allow; remit (a debt or punishment), pardon (an offense)."
