noninjuries:/ˌnɒnˈɪndʒəriːz/
noun
无伤害；无损伤
Absence of injuries; the state of being free from injuries.
The report confirmed noninjuries among the passengers. 报告确认乘客中无人受伤。
Synonyms: safety, wellness, health
Antonyms: injuries, harms, wounds
词性: 名词
