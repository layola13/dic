hairdos:/ˈhɛərdʊz/ (US), /ˈhɛəduːz/ (UK)| n.|发型，发式|A style in which hair is cut and arranged.  This word is a plural form of "hairdo."|She tried out several different hairdos before settling on one she liked. 她尝试了几种不同的发型，最后选定了一种自己喜欢的。|近义词：hairstyles, coiffures|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "unstyled hair")|noun
