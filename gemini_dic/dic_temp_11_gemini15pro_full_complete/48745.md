defining:/dɪˈfaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. |限定的，定义的; 最典型的，起决定性作用的| Giving the meaning of a word or expression; serving to identify or characterize; most important; crucial | The defining moment of the game was the final goal. 这场比赛的决定性时刻是最后一个进球。| synonyms: decisive, crucial, characteristic, essential | antonyms:  insignificant, unimportant, minor, trivial | adjective

defining:/dɪˈfaɪnɪŋ/ | v. |下定义，界定;  明确;  使…的轮廓分明| State or describe exactly the nature, scope, or meaning of; to show clearly the edge or shape of something |  The contract defines the terms of the agreement.  合同规定了协议的条款。| synonyms: specify, describe, explain, delineate, outline | antonyms: confuse, obscure, blur | verb (present participle of define)
