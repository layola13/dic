mismark:/ˌmɪsˈmɑːrk/ | vt. n. |错误地标记，标记错误；错误的标记|To mark incorrectly or with an error.  A mark that is incorrect. |例句：I mismarked the exam papers and had to regrade them. 我把试卷标记错了，不得不重新评分。|近义词：mislabel, blunder, err, mistake |反义词: mark correctly, label correctly |词性:动词,名词

mismark:/ˌmɪsˈmɑːrk/ | n. |错误的标记|A mark that is incorrect. |例句：The mismark on the product led to it being sold at a discounted price. 产品上的错误标记导致它以折扣价出售。|近义词：error, mistake, flaw, defect |反义词: correct mark |词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + mark (a sign or symbol).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss."  "Mark" comes from Old English "mearc" meaning "boundary, sign, mark."
