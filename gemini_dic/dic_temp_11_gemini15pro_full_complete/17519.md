beat-it:/ˌbiːt ˈɪt/ | n. | (俚语)逃跑，逃离 | An act of leaving or escaping quickly. This usage is less common than the phrasal verb. |  He made a beat-it when he saw the police coming.  他看到警察来了就逃跑了。 | escape, flight, getaway | arrival, stay | noun
