illegibilities:/ˌɪˌlɛdʒɪˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |难以辨认; 模糊不清|The illegibilities in the manuscript made it difficult to decipher. 手稿中的难以辨认之处使其难以破译。|例句：The doctor's handwriting was notorious for its illegibilities.  这位医生的笔迹以难以辨认而闻名。

例句：Due to the illegibilities in the contract, the agreement was nullified. 由于合同中难以辨认之处，协议被取消了。|近义词：unclearness, unreadability, obscurity|反义词: clarity, legibility, readability|词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源: From illegible (unable to be read) + -ity (forming nouns of quality or state) + -ies (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Latin in- (not) + legibilis (readable).
