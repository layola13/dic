misattributed:/ˌmɪsəˈtrɪbjʊtɪd/
adjective
错误归属的，被错误地归于…的
incorrectly attributed; assigned to the wrong person or source
The painting was misattributed to Rembrandt for many years. 这幅画被错误地归于伦勃朗多年。
The quote was misattributed to the wrong author. 这句话被错误地归于另一位作者。
synonyms: misassigned, wrongly attributed, falsely ascribed
antonyms: correctly attributed, properly assigned
adjective
