excerpt:/ˈeksɜːrpt/ | n. | 摘录；节选 | A passage or quotation taken or selected from a book, film, piece of music, etc. |  Example: An excerpt from her new thriller will appear in this weekend's magazine.  周末的杂志将刊登她新惊悚小说中的一个节选。| Synonym: extract, passage, selection, quotation | Antonym: whole, complete | noun


excerpt:/ɪkˈsɜːrpt/ | v. | 摘录；节选 | To take a passage or quotation from a book, film, piece of music, etc. | Example:  This article is excerpted from his new book. 这篇文章摘自他的新书。 | Synonym: extract, quote, select | Antonym: insert, include, whole| verb
