outpraying:/ˌaʊtˈpreɪɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的祈祷
Surpassing someone in prayer; praying longer or more fervently than another.
She believed she could outpray her rivals and secure God's favor. 她相信她可以通过比对手更虔诚的祈祷来获得上帝的眷顾。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词: be outdone, be surpassed
词性: verb (present participle)
