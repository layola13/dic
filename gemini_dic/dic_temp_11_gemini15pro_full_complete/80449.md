gong:/ɡɒŋ/ (UK) /ɡɔːŋ/ (US)| n.|锣|A percussion instrument, usually made of metal, that produces a resonant sound when struck.|The gong announced the start of the ceremony. 锣声宣布仪式开始。|synonyms: cymbal, bell|antonyms: silence, quiet|noun

gong:/ɡɒŋ/ (UK) /ɡɔːŋ/ (US)| n.|勋章，奖章 (figurative)|A medal or award, especially one given for bravery or achievement.|He received a gong for his services to the country. 他因对国家的贡献而获得一枚勋章。|synonyms: medal, award, honour|antonyms: dishonor, disgrace|noun
