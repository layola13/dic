induration:/ˌɪndʊˈreɪʃən/| n.|硬化，硬结|The process of hardening; the state of being hardened.  A hardened area, especially of skin or tissue.|Example 1: The doctor noticed an induration around the wound, a sign of infection. 医生注意到伤口周围有硬结，这是感染的迹象。

Example 2:  Exposure to the harsh weather caused induration of the skin.  暴露在恶劣天气下导致皮肤硬化。|Synonyms: hardening, sclerosis, firmness, consolidation|Antonyms: softening, yielding, flexibility|noun
