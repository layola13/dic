ETYMOLOGY->
From golden + -ness.  "Golden" comes from Old English "gylden,"  from Proto-Germanic "*gulþinaz," ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*ghel-," meaning "yellow, green, bright." The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
