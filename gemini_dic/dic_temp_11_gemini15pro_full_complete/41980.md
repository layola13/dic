cost-benefit:/ˌkɔːst ˈbɛnɪfɪt/ | adj. n.| 成本效益，利弊 |  Relating to or denoting an analysis of the cost effectiveness of a project or policy.  A cost-benefit analysis weighs the costs against the benefits. | Example:  The government conducted a cost-benefit analysis before building the new highway. 政府在修建新高速公路之前进行了成本效益分析。 | Synonym: cost-effectiveness,  return on investment (ROI)| Antonym: unprofitable, loss-making | adjective, noun

cost-benefit:/ˌkɔːst ˈbɛnɪfɪt/ | n.| 成本效益分析 | A systematic approach to estimating the strengths and weaknesses of alternatives used to determine options which provide the best approach to achieving benefits while preserving savings. | Example: A cost-benefit analysis showed that the project was not financially viable. 成本效益分析表明该项目在财务上不可行。 | Synonym: cost-effectiveness analysis, feasibility study| Antonym: N/A | noun
