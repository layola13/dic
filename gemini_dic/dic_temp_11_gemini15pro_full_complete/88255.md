hinged:/hɪndʒd/
adj.
铰链式的，用铰链连接的；取决于…的
attached with a hinge or hinges; depending entirely on
例句：The door is hinged on the left. 门的铰链在左边。

例句：The success of the project hinged on the funding. 这个项目的成功取决于资金。
近义词：jointed, pivoted, dependent
反义词：fixed, immobile
词性:形容词


hinge:/hɪndʒ/
n.
铰链
a piece of metal or other material that fastens two objects together, typically allowing one to swing open relative to the other
例句：The door hinge is rusty. 门铰链生锈了。

例句：The lid of the box is attached by a hinge. 盒子的盖子是用铰链连接的。
近义词：joint, pivot
反义词：N/A
词性:名词


hinge:/hɪndʒ/
v.
用铰链连接；依…而定
attach with or as if with a hinge; depend entirely on
例句：The gate hinges on a strong metal post. 大门用结实的金属柱子做铰链。

例句：The whole argument hinges on this one point. 整个论点都取决于这一点。
近义词：depend, pivot, turn
反义词：N/A
词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *henge, *hange, from Old English *henge, *hange (“hinge”), from Proto-Germanic *hangijaną (“to hang”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kenk- (“to turn, bend”). Cognate with Dutch heng (“hinge”), German Hänge (“hinge”), dialectal German Hange (“slope”), Swedish hänga (“to hang”), Old English hangian (“to hang”). Related to hang.
