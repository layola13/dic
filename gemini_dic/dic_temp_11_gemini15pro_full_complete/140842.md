on-the-bench:/ˌɒn ðə ˈbɛntʃ/ | adj. |  替补的，坐冷板凳的 | Out of action, not playing, especially in sports.  Relates to being a substitute player who is not currently participating in the game but is available if needed. It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who is not actively involved in a project or activity. |  The star quarterback was on-the-bench due to a shoulder injury.  明星四分卫因为肩伤坐在替补席上。 | synonyms: sidelined, benched, inactive, reserve | antonyms: active, playing, starting, in the game | adjective

on-the-bench:/ˌɒn ðə ˈbɛntʃ/ | n. |  替补队员的状态 | The state of being a substitute player.  | He spent most of the season on-the-bench. 他大部分赛季都坐在替补席上。 | synonyms: reserve status, bench status, inactivity | antonyms: active participation, starting position, playing status | noun
