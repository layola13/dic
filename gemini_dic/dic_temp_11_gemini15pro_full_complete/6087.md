americanises:/əˈmɛrɪkənaɪzɪz/
verb
使美国化
To cause to become American in character; to adopt or imbue with American characteristics.
McDonald's has americanised many aspects of global fast food culture. 麦当劳使全球快餐文化的许多方面美国化了。
The immigrant americanised his name to make it easier for people to pronounce.  这个移民为了让人们更容易发音而使他的名字美国化。
synonyms: Americanize, westernize
antonyms: localize, nationalize
词性:动词

americanises:/əˈmɛrɪkənaɪzɪz/
verb

To become American in character; to adopt American characteristics.
Many immigrants eventually americanise as they integrate into society.  许多移民在融入社会后最终美国化了。
He americanised quickly after moving to the United States.  他移居美国后很快就美国化了。
synonyms: assimilate, acculturate
antonyms:  retain (one's culture), preserve (one's heritage)
词性:动词
