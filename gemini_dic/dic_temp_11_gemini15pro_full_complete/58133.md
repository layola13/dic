dropwort: /ˈdrɒpwɜːrt/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːpwɜːrt/ (US) | n. | 水芹 | A poisonous plant of the parsley family (Oenanthe crocata) that grows in damp places and has clusters of small white flowers.  |  Ingestion of water dropwort can be fatal. 摄入水芹可能是致命的。 |  Be cautious when foraging near water, as water dropwort can be mistaken for edible plants.  在水边觅食时要小心，因为水芹很容易被误认为是可食用的植物。 | synonyms: water dropwort, hemlock water dropwort | antonyms: N/A | noun
