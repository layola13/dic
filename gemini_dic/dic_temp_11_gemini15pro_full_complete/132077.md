noncreativity:/ˌnɑnkriːeɪˈtɪvəti/
n.
缺乏创造力，无创造性
The state or quality of lacking creativity or originality.
His noncreativity made it difficult for him to come up with new ideas for the project.  他的缺乏创造力使他难以想出项目的新点子。
synonym: uncreativeness, unimaginativeness, barrenness, sterility
antonym: creativity, originality, innovation, ingenuity
noun
