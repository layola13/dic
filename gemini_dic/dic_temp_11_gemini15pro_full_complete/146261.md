overeye:/ˈoʊvərˌaɪ/| n.|眼罩，眼罩状物|A covering or shade for the eyes, often used to protect from light or dust.|She wore an overeye to block out the sunlight. 她戴着眼罩遮挡阳光。|近义词：eyeshade, blinder, visor|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "uncovered eyes")|noun

overeye:/ˈoʊvərˌaɪ/| adj.|眼睛上方的|Located above the eye.|The overeye area was bruised. 眼睛上方的区域淤青了。|近义词：supraorbital|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "suborbital")|adjective
