nonvibrator:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪbreɪtər/ | n. |非振动器，无振动装置|Something that does not vibrate.  A device or object that is specifically designed not to vibrate, or something that inherently lacks the capability to vibrate. |例句：The nonvibrator was crucial for the experiment's stability.  这个非振动器对于实验的稳定性至关重要。

例句：They designed a nonvibrator mounting system for the sensitive equipment. 他们为敏感设备设计了一个无振动安装系统。|近义词：stable platform, non-oscillating device |反义词: vibrator, oscillator |词性:名词
