outreaches:/ˈaʊtriːtʃɪz/
noun
外展服务；延伸活动；联络
an effort to bring services or information to people, especially people who are poor or live in a remote area
例句：The church's outreaches to the homeless have been very successful. 教堂对外展服务帮助无家可归者取得了巨大成功。

例句：The university has several outreaches designed to attract minority students. 这所大学有几个旨在吸引少数族裔学生的拓展项目。
近义词：programs, services, initiatives, extensions
反义词：limitations, restrictions
词性：n. (plural noun)
