byee:/baɪiː/
interjection
再见，拜拜
An informal way of saying goodbye.
例句：Byee, see you later! 再见，回头见！

例句：Byee, have a good day! 再见，祝你今天愉快！

近义词：goodbye, bye, ciao, later
反义词: hello, hi
词性：感叹词

词源:  "Byee" is a colloquial and informal shortening of "goodbye," which itself comes from "God be with ye," a contraction of "God be with you."  Over time, the phrase evolved from a blessing to a common farewell.
