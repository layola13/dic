embellish: /ɪmˈbɛlɪʃ/ | v. | 装饰，修饰，润色 | To make something more attractive or interesting by adding details, often ones that are not true. |  She embellished her story with details about her exciting travels. 她用关于她激动人心的旅行的细节润色了她的故事。| adorn, decorate, enhance, ornament |  simplify, understate | verb

embellished: /ɪmˈbɛlɪʃt/ | adj. | 装饰的，修饰的，润色的 |  Made more attractive or interesting by adding details. | The embellished dress was covered in sequins and beads.  这件装饰过的连衣裙上缀满了亮片和珠子。 | adorned, decorated, enhanced, ornamented | plain, simple, unembellished | adjective
