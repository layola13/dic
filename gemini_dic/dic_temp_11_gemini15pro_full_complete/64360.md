erato:/ˈɛrətoʊ/ | n. | 缪斯女神之一;爱情诗 | One of the nine Muses in Greek mythology, considered the Muse of lyric poetry, especially love poetry. | Erato is often depicted with a lyre.  厄拉托经常被描绘成拿着里拉琴的样子。| synonyms: Muse, inspirer | antonyms: None | Noun

erato:/ˈɛrətoʊ/ | n. | 爱情诗 |  A poem or song about love. | He wrote an erato to his beloved. 他为他的爱人写了一首爱情诗。| synonyms: love poem, love song, ode | antonyms: None | Noun
