falsify:/ˈfɔːlsɪfaɪ/ /ˈfɑːlsɪfaɪ/| n.| 伪造；篡改；歪曲 |The act of falsifying something|The scientist's reputation was ruined by the falsification of his data.  这位科学家的声誉因其数据的伪造而毁于一旦。|synonyms: forgery, counterfeiting, fabrication, misrepresentation|antonyms: verification, authentication, confirmation|noun
