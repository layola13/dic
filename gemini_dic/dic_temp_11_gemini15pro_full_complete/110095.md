licit:/ˈlɪsɪt/| adj.|合法的，正当的|Conforming to the law or rules; permitted.|例句：It is not licit to drive without a license.  无证驾驶是非法的。

例句：The police questioned the licit nature of the transaction. 警方质疑该交易的合法性。|近义词：legal, lawful, permissible, legitimate|反义词：illicit, illegal, unlawful, illegitimate|词性:形容词
