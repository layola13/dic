craftinesses:/ˈkræftiːnɪsɪz/
|n.| 狡猾；诡诈；熟练|The fox's craftinesses allowed it to outsmart the hunters. 狐狸的狡猾使它能够智胜猎人。|例句：His craftinesses were legendary in the business world.  他在商界的狡猾是传奇的。

His craftinesses eventually led to his downfall. 他的狡猾最终导致了他的垮台。

|近义词：cunning, guile, slyness, trickery, wiliness|反义词：honesty, forthrightness, sincerity, naiveté, artlessness|词性: 名词 (plural of craftiness)

Etymology: From crafty + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Crafty" comes from Middle English *crafti, from Old English cræftig "strong, powerful, skillful," from cræft "strength, skill, power."  The meaning shifted from "skillful" to "skillful in deception" over time.
