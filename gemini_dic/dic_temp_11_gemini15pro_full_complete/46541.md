dalf:/dɑlf/| n.| 法语深入研究文凭 |Diploma in Advanced French Language Studies (Diplôme Approfondi de Langue Française).  A diploma awarded by the French Ministry of Education to certify advanced French language proficiency.  It is the fourth level on the six-level scale of proficiency in French as a foreign language,  following DELF B2 and preceding DALF C2. |She passed the DALF C1 exam with flying colours. 她以优异的成绩通过了DALF C1考试。|Synonym: (In terms of level) DELF B2 (lower), DALF C2 (higher)|Antonym: None (as it's a specific qualification).|noun
