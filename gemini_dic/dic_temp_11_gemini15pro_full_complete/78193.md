geotag:/ˈdʒiːoʊtæɡ/ | n. |地理标记，地理标签 |A piece of data that identifies the geographical location where something, such as a photograph or video, was taken or recorded. | Example 1:  I added a geotag to my photo so my friends could see where I was when I took it.  我给我的照片添加了地理标记，这样我的朋友就能看到我拍照时在哪里。

Example 2: The geotag on the video showed that it was filmed in Central Park. 视频上的地理标签显示它是在中央公园拍摄的。 | Synonyms:  geolocation, geospatial tag, location tag | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
