levee:/ˈlɛvi/ (US), /ˈlɛviː/ (UK)| n.|堤坝，防洪堤|An embankment built to prevent the overflow of a river.|例句：The levee protected the town from the flood.  堤坝保护了小镇免受洪水侵袭。

例句：The Mississippi River levees are constantly being reinforced. 密西西比河的堤坝不断得到加固。|近义词：dike, embankment, barrier|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 breach (决口))|词性:noun
