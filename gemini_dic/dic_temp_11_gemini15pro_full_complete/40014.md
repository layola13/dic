consumability:/kənˌsjuːməˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可消耗性；易耗性 |The quality of being consumable; the fact of being designed to be used up and then replaced. | The consumability of office supplies like paper and ink is a significant expense. 办公用品如纸张和墨水的消耗性是一笔巨大的开支。| The high consumability of fast fashion contributes to environmental problems. 快时尚的高消耗性导致了环境问题。| synonyms: expendability, disposability | antonyms: durability, permanence | noun
