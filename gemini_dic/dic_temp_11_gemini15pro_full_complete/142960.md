outargue:/ˌaʊtˈɑːrɡjuː/
verb
胜过；驳倒
to argue more effectively than someone else
He outargued his opponents and won the debate. 他驳倒了对手，赢得了辩论。
近义词: outreason, defeat, best, conquer
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb
