interminability:/ɪnˌtɜːrmɪnəˈbɪləti/ /ˌɪntərmɪnəˈbɪlədi/| n. | 无休止，无限，没完没了 |The quality of being interminable; endlessness.|The interminability of the lecture made the students restless. 这场没完没了的讲座让学生们坐立不安。|近义词：endlessness, perpetuity, infinity|反义词：brevity, finiteness, shortness|词性:名词

interminable:/ɪnˈtɜːrmɪnəbl/ /ɪnˈtɜːrmɪnəbəl/| adj. | 无休止的，无限的，没完没了的| Seeming to be without end; lasting or seeming to last forever.|The journey seemed interminable. 这趟旅程似乎没有尽头。|近义词：endless, incessant, everlasting|反义词：brief, short, finite|词性:形容词


词源:
From Late Latin interminabilis, from in- "not" + terminabilis "limitable," from terminare "to limit," from terminus "end, limit."
