malacostomous: /ˌmæləˈkɒstəmʌs/ | adj. | 软口的，软嘴的 | Having a soft mouth or lips.  Relating to or characteristic of soft-mouthed fishes, especially those of the order Malacopterygii. |  Malacostomous fish are generally found in freshwater habitats. 软口鱼类通常发现于淡水栖息地。|  The scientist studied the malacostomous features of the fish specimen.  科学家研究了鱼类标本的软口特征。 | 近义词: soft-mouthed | 反义词: hard-mouthed | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Greek *malakos* "soft" + *stoma* "mouth."
