cerebroscopy:/ˌsɛrɪˈbrɒskəpi/ /ˌsɛrɪˈbrɑːskəpi/
noun
脑镜检查
Examination of the brain, typically through a small opening in the skull.
Neurosurgeons performed cerebroscopy to locate the tumor.  神经外科医生进行了脑镜检查以确定肿瘤的位置。
The cerebroscopy revealed no obvious abnormalities. 脑镜检查未发现明显异常。
近义词:cranioscopy
反义词:None
词性:n.
