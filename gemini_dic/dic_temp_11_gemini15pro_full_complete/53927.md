discoursing:/dɪˈskɔːrsɪŋ/
verb
讲述，论述
giving a speech or talking about a subject formally and in detail
He was discoursing on the merits of organic farming. 他正在论述有机农业的优点。
synonyms: talking, speaking, conversing, lecturing, holding forth, expatiating
antonyms: listening, being silent
