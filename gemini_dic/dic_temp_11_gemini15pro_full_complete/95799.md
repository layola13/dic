inconversant:/ˌɪnkənˈvɜːrsənt/ | adj. |不熟悉的，不精通的 | Not familiar or knowledgeable about something. | He was completely inconversant with the new software. 他对这款新软件一无所知。 |  She was inconversant with the local customs. 她对当地风俗不熟悉。 | 近义词: unfamiliar, ignorant, unacquainted, inexperienced | 反义词: familiar, conversant, knowledgeable, experienced | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *inconversāns, present participle of *inconversārī (“not to associate with”), from in- (“not”) + conversārī (“to associate with, keep company with”).
