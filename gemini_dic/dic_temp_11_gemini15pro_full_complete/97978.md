inoceramus:/ˌɪnoʊˈsɪərəməs/| n. |菊石|An extinct marine mollusk of the class Bivalvia, common in the Cretaceous period, having a pearly shell with a characteristic prismatic inner layer.  These fossils are often found in chalk deposits.|Example:  The paleontologist discovered a large inoceramus fossil embedded in the chalk cliff.  古生物学家在白垩质悬崖中发现了一个巨大的菊石化石。

Example: Inoceramus shells can vary greatly in size, some reaching over a meter in length. 菊石的壳大小差异很大，有些长度超过一米。|近义词:bivalve, clam (general terms)|反义词: N/A (extinct organism)|词性:noun
