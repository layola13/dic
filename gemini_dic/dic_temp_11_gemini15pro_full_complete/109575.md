let someone have it:/lɛt ˈsʌmwʌn hæv ɪt/ | v. | 严厉批评；狠狠惩罚；猛烈攻击 | To criticize someone severely; to punish someone harshly; to attack someone fiercely. |  例句：The coach really let the players have it after they lost the game. 教练在他们输掉比赛后狠狠地训斥了球员们。

例句：When I found out he lied to me, I let him have it. 当我发现他对我撒谎时，我把他狠狠地骂了一顿。| 近义词：criticize, scold, rebuke, reprimand, castigate, lambaste, attack, assault | 反义词：praise, commend, compliment | 词性：动词短语
