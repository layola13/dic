dissertations:/ˌdɪsəˈteɪʃənz/
n.
论文；专题论文；学术论文
A long research paper written for a university degree or diploma.
She's writing her dissertation on the history of feminist thought. 她正在撰写关于女权主义思想史的论文。
Her dissertation was on the impact of social media on political discourse. 她的论文是关于社交媒体对政治话语的影响。
thesis, treatise, study, paper, essay
monograph
noun
