outlier:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪər/| n.|异常值，离群值|A data point that differs significantly from other observations.|The data showed a few outliers that skewed the results. 数据显示有一些异常值使结果发生了偏差。|Synonyms: anomaly, deviation, aberration, exception, irregularity|Antonyms: norm, average, standard|Noun

outlier:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪər/| n.|局外人，与众不同的人|A person or thing situated away or detached from the main body or system.|She felt like an outlier in the group.  她在小组中感觉像个局外人。|Synonyms: outsider, nonconformist, maverick, individualist|Antonyms: insider, conformist|Noun
