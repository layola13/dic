jocosely:/dʒoʊˈkoʊsli/ | adv. | 诙谐地，滑稽地 | In a humorous or playful way; jocularly. | He jocosely remarked about the absurdity of the situation. 他诙谐地评论了局势的荒谬。|  He jocosely  mimicked his friend's  accent, causing everyone to laugh. 他滑稽地模仿了他朋友的口音，逗得大家都笑了。 | 近义词: playfully, humorously, jocularly, jokingly, waggishly | 反义词: seriously, solemnly, gravely | 词性: 副词

jocose:/dʒoʊˈkoʊs/ | adj. | 诙谐的，滑稽的 | Given to or characterized by joking; humorous or playful. | He has a jocose personality and is always making people laugh. 他性格诙谐，总是逗人发笑。 | She told a jocose story about her childhood. 她讲了一个关于她童年的滑稽故事。 | 近义词: humorous, playful, jocular, joking, waggish | 反义词: serious, solemn, grave | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late 16th century: from Latin jocosus, from jocus ‘jest’.
