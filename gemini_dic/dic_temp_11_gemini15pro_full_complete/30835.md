chablis:/ˈʃæbli/| n.|夏布利葡萄酒（一种干白葡萄酒）|A dry white wine made from Chardonnay grapes grown around Chablis, France.  It is known for its crisp acidity and mineral notes.|例句：We enjoyed a bottle of chilled Chablis with our seafood dinner. 我们晚餐喝了一瓶冰镇的夏布利葡萄酒搭配海鲜。

例句：Chablis is often considered one of the finest white wines in the world. 夏布利葡萄酒通常被认为是世界上最好的白葡萄酒之一。|近义词：white wine, Chardonnay|反义词:red wine|Noun
