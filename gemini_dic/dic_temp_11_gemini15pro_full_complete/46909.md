darkliest:/ˈdɑːrkɪst/ | adj. | 最黑暗的 |  Superlative form of dark; meaning most dark, most gloomy, or most evil. | The movie depicted the darkest days of the war.  这部电影描绘了战争中最黑暗的日子。| Synonyms: bleakest, gloomiest, grimmest | Antonyms: brightest, lightest, happiest | adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *derkest*, from Old English *deorcost*, superlative of *deorc* (“dark”).  The "-est" suffix is a common superlative ending in English.
