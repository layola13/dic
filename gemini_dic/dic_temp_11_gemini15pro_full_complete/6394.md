amphigory:/ˈæmfɪɡɔːri/ | n. |  荒诞的故事，滑稽的模仿作品，无稽之谈 | A nonsensical piece of writing or speech, often presented as if it were meaningful; a rigmarole. |  The poem was a pure amphigory, full of sound and fury, signifying nothing. 这首诗纯粹是无稽之谈，充满了喧嚣和愤怒，却毫无意义。 |  nonsense, gibberish, doggerel, balderdash | sense, meaning | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *amphigouri,* from Italian *anfìguri* (modern *anfiguri*), of unknown origin.  Possibly a playful alteration of *ambi-*, "around" + Greek *gyros*, "circle, ring."
