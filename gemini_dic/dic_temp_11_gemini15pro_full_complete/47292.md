de-expropriations:/ˌdiːɛksˌprəʊpriˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 收回；取消征用；归还 |The act of reversing an expropriation, returning property or assets that were previously seized by a government or other authority to their original owners. | The government announced a series of de-expropriations, returning land to farmers who had lost it during the previous regime. 政府宣布了一系列的收回行动，将土地归还给在前政权时期失去土地的农民。

The de-expropriations were seen as a key step towards reconciliation.  收回行动被视为迈向和解的关键一步。| 近义词: restitution, restoration, return | 反义词: expropriation, seizure, confiscation | 词性: 名词
