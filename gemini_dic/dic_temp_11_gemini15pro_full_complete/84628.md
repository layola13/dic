hand-down:/ˈhænd daʊn/| adj. n.|传下来的，祖传的; 馈赠，传家宝|Something passed down from an older generation.  A hand-down decision is one made by a higher court.|This ring is a hand-down from my grandmother. 这枚戒指是我祖母传下来的。|The judge's hand-down decision set a legal precedent. 法官的判决开创了法律先例。|inherited, bequeathed|modern, newly acquired|adjective, noun
hand-down:/ˈhænd daʊn/| v.|把…传下去|To give or leave something to someone younger than oneself.|She handed down her wedding dress to her daughter. 她把她的婚纱传给了她的女儿。|bequeath, pass on|keep, retain|verb
