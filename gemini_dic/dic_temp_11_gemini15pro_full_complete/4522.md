albuminosis:/ˌælbjʊmɪˈnoʊsɪs/
n.
蛋白尿；白蛋白尿
Presence of albumin in the urine, typically a symptom of kidney disease.
Example: The doctor suspected albuminuria after detecting albumin in the patient's urine sample. 医生在病人的尿液样本中检测到白蛋白后怀疑是蛋白尿。
Example: Albuminosis can be an early sign of diabetic nephropathy. 蛋白尿可能是糖尿病肾病的早期征兆。
Synonym: albuminuria, proteinuria
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's the presence of something)
