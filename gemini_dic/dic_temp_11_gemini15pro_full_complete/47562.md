debacchation:/ˌdiːbækˈkeɪʃən/ |n.| 狂欢，放荡，纵酒|An unrestrained indulgence in pleasure, especially drinking and revelry; an orgy. |The annual festival ended in several days of debacchation.  年度节日以几天的狂欢结束。|Synonyms: revelry, carousal, orgy, spree, bacchanalia|Antonyms: abstinence, sobriety, temperance|noun

debacchation:/ˌdiːbækˈkeɪʃən/ |n.| 放荡，堕落|A descent into a state of unrestrained revelry and indulgence. |His debacchation led to his financial ruin. 他的放荡导致了他的经济破产。|Synonyms: dissipation, depravity, profligacy|Antonyms: morality, virtue, decency|noun
