nutz: /nʌts/| n. |疯子；傻瓜；睾丸 (vulgar, slang) |A crazy or foolish person.  Also a vulgar slang term for testicles.|He's completely nutz for thinking he can win the lottery. 他认为自己能中彩票，简直疯了。|Synonyms: crazy, insane, mad, nuts, bonkers|Antonyms: sane, rational|Noun

nutz: /nʌts/| adj. |疯狂的；着迷的 (informal) | Enthusiastic about or obsessed with something.|She's nutz about that new band. 她对那个新乐队很着迷。|Synonyms: crazy, mad, enthusiastic, passionate|Antonyms: indifferent, apathetic|Adjective
