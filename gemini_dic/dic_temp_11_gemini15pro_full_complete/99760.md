intermedial:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiəl/
adjective
中间的；媒介的；处于中间阶段的
Existing or occurring between media.  Relating to or involving a relationship between different forms of media.
Example: The artist's work explored the intermedial space between painting and sculpture.  这位艺术家的作品探索了绘画和雕塑之间的媒介空间。

Example:  The performance was an intermedial experiment, combining live music, video projections, and spoken word. 这场表演是一个媒介实验，结合了现场音乐、视频投影和口语。
近义词: interactive, interdisciplinary, multimedia
反义词: separate, distinct, individual
词性: adjective
