inspissate:/ɪnˈspɪseɪt/ | v. | 使…变浓稠，浓缩；加浓，(使)变浓 |  The chef inspissated the sauce by reducing it over low heat. 厨师用小火慢炖来浓缩酱汁。|  The long drought inspissated the river, leaving only a trickle of water. 长期的干旱使河流变得浓稠，只留下涓涓细流。 | thicken, condense, concentrate | dilute, thin | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin inspissatus, past participle of inspissare "to thicken," from in- "in" + spissare "to thicken," from Latin spissus "thick."
