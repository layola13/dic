harsh:/hɑːrʃ/ (US) /hɑːʃ/ (UK)| adj.| 粗糙的，严厉的，刺耳的，严酷的|Unpleasant, unkind, cruel, or severe.|例句：The harsh winter left many people homeless. 严酷的冬天使许多人无家可归。|例句：The teacher's harsh criticism made the student cry. 老师严厉的批评使学生哭了。|近义词：severe, rigorous, austere, stringent, cruel, unkind|反义词：gentle, mild, kind, lenient, pleasant|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *harsch, from Old Norse *harskr (“sharp, bitter”), from Proto-Germanic *hariskaz (“gray-haired, old”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer- (“gray; old”).
