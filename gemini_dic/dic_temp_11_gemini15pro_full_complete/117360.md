mediastinal:/ˌmiːdiːæˈstaɪnəl/
adjective
纵隔的
Relating to the mediastinum, the central compartment of the chest cavity.
The doctor discovered a mediastinal mass on the X-ray. 医生在X光片上发现了一个纵隔肿块。
The patient underwent a mediastinal biopsy.  病人接受了纵隔活检。
近义词:  midthoracic (less precise)
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it refers to a specific anatomical location)
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Modern Latin *mediastinus, from Latin medius (“middle”) + -stinus (suffix forming adjectives).
