camber:/ˈkæmbər/ (US), /ˈkæmbə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|倾斜，翘曲；使...倾斜|A slightly convex or arched shape of a road or other horizontal surface.  The action of giving such a shape to something. |The road has a slight camber to allow rainwater to run off. 这条路略微倾斜以便雨水流走。|Synonyms:  slope, incline, curve, arch, bend |Antonyms:  flatness, levelness|noun
