counter-faller:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɔːlər/ | n. | 对抗者；反驳者；抵消因素 |  Someone or something that opposes or offsets the effect of something else. | The government's new policy acts as a counter-faller to rising inflation. 政府的新政策可以对抗通货膨胀。 |  The new safety measures acted as a counter-faller to the previous risks. 新的安全措施抵消了先前的风险。 | Synonyms: counteragent, offset, neutralizer | Antonyms:  reinforcement, catalyst,  exacerbator | noun
counter-faller:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɔːlər/ | adj. | 对抗的；反驳的；抵消的 | Having the effect of opposing or offsetting something else. |  The counter-faller force prevented the collapse of the bridge. 对抗力阻止了桥梁的倒塌。 | The counter-faller argument effectively challenged the prevailing theory. 反驳的论点有效地挑战了盛行的理论。 | Synonyms: opposing, counteracting, neutralizing | Antonyms: supporting, reinforcing, exacerbating | adjective
