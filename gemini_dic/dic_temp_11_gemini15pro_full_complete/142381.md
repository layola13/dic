orthoscopic:/ˌɔːθəˈskɒpɪk/ |adj.| 正视的，无畸变的 |Presenting an accurate image without distortion, especially of a lens or optical system.  Relating to or denoting a lens or lens system that produces an image free from distortion, especially in binoculars or telescopes.| The orthoscopic lens provided a clear and undistorted view of the landscape. 正视透镜提供了清晰无失真的景观视野。| The telescope's orthoscopic eyepiece allows for comfortable and accurate observations. 这台望远镜的正视目镜可以进行舒适而准确的观测。|  synonym: rectilinear, distortion-free | antonym: distorted, anamorphic | adjective
