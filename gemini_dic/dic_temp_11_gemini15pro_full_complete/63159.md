ensurer:/ɪnˈʃʊərər/| n.|确保者，保证人|Someone or something that ensures a particular outcome or result.  A person or thing that makes certain that something happens.|The company acted as an ensurer of quality control.  这家公司充当了质量控制的保证人。|Synonyms: guarantor, surety, underwriter|Antonyms:  neglecter, jeopardizer|noun
