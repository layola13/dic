nondisbursable:/ˌnɑːndɪsˈbɜːrsəbl/
adjective
不可支付的，不予拨付的
Not able to be disbursed or paid out.
The grant included a nondisbursable amount for administrative costs. 这笔拨款包含一笔不可支付的行政费用。
The scholarship's nondisbursable portion covered tuition, while the bursable portion covered living expenses. 奖学金的不可支付部分涵盖了学费，而可支付部分涵盖了生活费。
近义词: nonpayable, unexpendable
反义词: bursable, payable
词性: adjective
