nonfreedom:/nɒnˈfriːdəm/ | n. |缺乏自由，不自由|The absence or denial of freedom; the state of being unfree. |例句：The citizens lived in a state of nonfreedom, unable to express their opinions. 公民们生活在不自由的状态下，无法表达自己的意见。

例句：The regime enforced nonfreedom through strict censorship and surveillance. 该政权通过严格的审查和监视来强制实行不自由。|近义词：oppression, repression, captivity, bondage, subjugation |反义词：freedom, liberty, autonomy, independence |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + freedom.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not." "Freedom" comes from Old English "frēodōm," meaning "state of being free."
