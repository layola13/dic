interacting:/ˌɪntərˈæktɪŋ/
adjective
相互作用的，互相影响的
Engaging in reciprocal action or influence.
The interacting parts of the machine worked perfectly.  机器相互作用的部分完美地工作。
Interacting species in an ecosystem can influence each other's populations. 生态系统中相互作用的物种会影响彼此的种群数量。

近义词:  interrelated, connected, interdependent, communicating
反义词:  isolated, separate, independent, disconnected
词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "interact," which combines "inter-" (between, among) + "act" (to do, to perform).
