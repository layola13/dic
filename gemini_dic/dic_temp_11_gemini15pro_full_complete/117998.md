verb
纪念；使…永垂不朽
The monument memorializes the soldiers who died in the war. 这座纪念碑是为了纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵。
This poem memorializes the poet's love for his homeland. 这首诗歌颂了诗人对祖国的热爱。
Synonyms: commemorating, remembering, honoring
Antonyms: forgetting, ignoring, neglecting

memorialize: /məˈmɔːriəˌlaɪz/
verb
纪念；使…永垂不朽
They memorialized the event with a plaque. 他们用一块匾来纪念这件事。
The museum memorializes the history of the city.  这家博物馆记录了这座城市的历史。
Synonyms: commemorate, remember, honor
Antonyms: forget, ignore, neglect
词性: 动词(v.)


词源:
From late Middle English *memorialisen, from late Latin memoriālis, from Latin memoria (“memory”).
