odal:/ˈoʊdəl/| adj. n.|祖传的，祖传财产|Of or relating to inherited land, especially in Norway or other Scandinavian countries.  Relating to a type of freehold ownership of land in which ownership has belonged to a family for so long that it cannot be traced to any original grant.|My grandfather left me his odal land in Norway. 我的祖父把他在挪威的祖传土地留给了我。|近义词：inherited, ancestral, patrimonial|反义词：acquired|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology: From Norwegian *oðal* "inherited property," from Old Norse *óðal*. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *ōþalan*.
