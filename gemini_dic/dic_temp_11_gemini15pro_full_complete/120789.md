misarranged: /ˌmɪsəˈreɪndʒd/
adjective
排列错误的，顺序混乱的
placed in the wrong order or position; not arranged neatly or correctly.
The books were misarranged on the shelves.  书架上的书摆放得乱七八糟。
misplaced, disordered, jumbled, chaotic
arranged, organized, ordered
adjective
