fructose:/ˈfrʌktoʊs/ (US) /ˈfrʌktoʊz/ (UK)| n. |果糖|A simple sugar found in honey and fruit. It is a monosaccharide and an isomer of glucose.|Fructose is sweeter than glucose.|例句：
Honey contains a high percentage of fructose.  蜂蜜含有高比例的果糖。

Fruits like apples and grapes are natural sources of fructose.  像苹果和葡萄这样的水果是果糖的天然来源。

|近义词：levulose (technical term), fruit sugar|反义词: None (Fructose doesn't have a direct opposite.  Perhaps considering complex carbohydrates as a contrasting concept could be useful, but not a true antonym.)|词性:noun
