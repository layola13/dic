outsulk: /ˌaʊtˈsʌlk/
verb
超过…生闷气的时间；比…更能生闷气
To sulk longer or more effectively than someone else.
He outsulked her by a full hour after their argument.  在他们争吵之后，他比她多生闷气了整整一个小时。
近义词: outdo, surpass, beat
反义词: yield, submit
词性: verb
