high-pressuring:/haɪˈprɛʃərɪŋ/
|adj.|高压的，施加巨大压力的|Exerting or involving intense pressure, especially to force someone to do something.| The salesman used high-pressuring tactics to close the deal.  销售员使用了高压策略来完成交易。|Synonyms: coercive, forceful, insistent, pushy|Antonyms: gentle, lenient, relaxed, permissive|adjective


high-pressuring:/haɪˈprɛʃərɪŋ/
|v.|施加巨大压力|The act of exerting intense pressure, especially to force someone to do something.|They were high-pressuring him to sign the contract. 他们向他施加巨大压力，让他签合同。|Synonyms: coercing, forcing, pressurizing|Antonyms: persuading, encouraging, suggesting|verb
