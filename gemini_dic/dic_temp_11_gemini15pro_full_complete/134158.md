nonimpulsiveness:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpʌlsɪvnəs/
noun
非冲动性，不冲动
The quality of not being impulsive; the tendency to think before acting.
Her nonimpulsiveness served her well in the crisis. 她的不冲动在危机中对她很有帮助。
Synonyms:  self-control, restraint, patience, deliberation, composure, level-headedness
Antonyms: impulsiveness, recklessness, rashness, impetuousness, spontaneity
