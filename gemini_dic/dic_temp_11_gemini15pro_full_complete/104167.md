juniper:/ˈdʒuːnɪpər/| n.|杜松|An evergreen shrub or small tree with berrylike cones, which are used to flavor gin and some medicines.  |The air smelled of juniper and pine. 空气中弥漫着杜松和松树的气味。|Near synonyms: cedar, cypress|Antonym: None (Juniper is a specific type of plant, not a concept with a direct opposite.)|noun
