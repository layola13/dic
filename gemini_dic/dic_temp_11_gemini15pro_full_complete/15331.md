backspace:/ˈbækspeɪs/| n. v.|退格键，退格|A key on a computer keyboard that moves the cursor one position backward and deletes the preceding character.  退格键，用于将光标向后移动一个位置并删除前面的字符。|She hit the backspace key to correct the typo. 她按下退格键来纠正打字错误。|Synonyms: delete key, erase key|Antonyms:  N/A|noun
backspace:/ˈbækspeɪs/| v.|退格，删除|To use the backspace key. 使用退格键。|He backspaced over the mistake and typed the correct letter. 他用退格键删除了错误并输入了正确的字母。|Synonyms: delete, erase|Antonyms:  insert, type|verb
