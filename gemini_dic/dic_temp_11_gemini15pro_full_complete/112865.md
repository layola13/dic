lucratively: /ˈluːkrətɪvli/
adv.
获利地，有利可图地
in a way that produces a lot of money; profitably.
She invested her money lucratively in real estate. 她将资金投资于房地产，获利丰厚。
近义词: profitably, gainfully, advantageously
反义词: unprofitably, fruitlessly

词性: adverb

词源: From Late Latin *lucrativus, from lucrat-, past participle stem of lucrari "to gain, profit," from lucrum "profit, gain, wealth."
