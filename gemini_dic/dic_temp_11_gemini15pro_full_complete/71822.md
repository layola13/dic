flees:/fliːz/
verb
逃跑，逃离
third-person singular simple present indicative form of flee
The criminal flees the scene. 罪犯逃离了现场。
近义词：escapes, runs away, absconds
反义词：stays, remains, surrenders
词性：verb

flees:/fliːz/
noun
跳蚤 (plural of flea)
small, wingless, bloodsucking insects
The dog has flees. 这只狗有跳蚤。
近义词：parasites, vermin
反义词：(No direct antonym for a specific insect)
词性：noun (plural)

词源:
From Middle English *flien, from Old English flēon (“to flee, escape, vanish, avoid, shun”), from Proto-Germanic *fleuhaną (“to flee”), from Proto-Indo-European *plew- (“to flow, swim, flee”). Cognate with Scots flie (“to flee”), West Frisian flie (“to flee”), Dutch vlieden, vluchten (“to flee”), German fliehen (“to flee”), Danish flygte (“to flee, escape”). Related to flow.  The noun form "flees" (plural of flea) comes from Old English flēah.
