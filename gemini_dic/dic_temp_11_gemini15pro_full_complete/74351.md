fragrancy:/ˈfreɪɡrənsi/| n.|芬芳，香味，香气|A pleasant smell, often sweet or flowery.  A quality of being fragrant.|The air was filled with the fragrance of roses. 空气中弥漫着玫瑰花的香味。|近义词：aroma, scent, perfume, odor|反义词：stench, stink|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *fragrauncie*, borrowed from Old French *fragrancie*, from Medieval Latin *fragrantia*, from Latin *fragrāns* (“fragrant, sweet-smelling”), present participle of *fragrāre* (“to emit a sweet smell, be fragrant”), from *fragrum* (“strawberry”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰreg-* (“to break”).
