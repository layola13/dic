huzza:/həˈzɑː/ | int. n.|欢呼；欢呼声|A shout or cry of acclaim or encouragement. |The crowd let out a loud huzza when the team scored. 当球队得分时，人群发出震耳欲聋的欢呼声。| 近义词: cheer, hurrah, shout, acclaim | 反义词: boo, groan, hiss | 词性: interjection, noun
Etymology: From the imperative of *hussar*, "to hoist, lift, raise", also a source of *hoist*.
