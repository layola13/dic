behind bars:/bɪˈhaɪnd bɑːrz/| adj.|入狱，在监狱里；坐牢|Being in prison.  Literally, behind the bars of a prison cell.|He spent ten years behind bars for armed robbery. 他因持械抢劫入狱十年。|Synonyms: imprisoned, incarcerated, jailed, locked up|Antonyms: free, liberated, released|adjective

behind bars:/bɪˈhaɪnd bɑːrz/| n.|监狱|Prison.  Referring to the physical bars of a cell, representing imprisonment.|He's been living behind bars for most of his adult life.  他成年后大部分时间都在监狱里度过。|Synonyms: prison, jail, penitentiary|Antonyms: freedom, liberty|noun
