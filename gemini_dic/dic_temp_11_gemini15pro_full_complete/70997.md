firewire:/ˈfaɪə(r)waɪə(r)/| n.|火线，高速串行总线|A high-speed serial bus standard for connecting peripherals to a computer, especially audio and video equipment.|Example: I connected my camcorder to the computer using a FireWire cable. 我用火线连接了我的摄像机和电脑。|Example: FireWire offers faster data transfer rates than USB 1.1. 火线提供比USB 1.1更快的传输速率。|近义词：IEEE 1394, i.LINK|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps slower interfaces like USB 1.1 could be considered)|词性:noun
