bogberry:/ˈbɒɡˌbɛri/ /ˈbɑːɡˌberi/| n. |沼泽莓，越橘的一种|A type of cranberry that grows in bogs.  A dark-colored berry with a tart flavor, typically found in acidic bogs. |The bogberries were ripe for picking in late summer. 夏末，沼泽莓成熟了，可以采摘了。

|The tartness of the bogberry jam was a perfect complement to the rich cheese. 沼泽莓酱的酸味与浓郁的奶酪相得益彰。|近义词：cranberry, bilberry|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
