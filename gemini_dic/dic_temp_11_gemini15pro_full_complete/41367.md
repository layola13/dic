corklike:/ˈkɔːrklaɪk/
adj.
像软木的；软木似的
Resembling cork in texture, appearance, or lightness.
The bark of the tree was corklike and easy to peel.  这树的树皮像软木一样，很容易剥落。
The material felt corklike to the touch.  这种材料摸起来像软木一样。
近义词:  corky, buoyant
反义词:  dense, heavy
词性: adjective
