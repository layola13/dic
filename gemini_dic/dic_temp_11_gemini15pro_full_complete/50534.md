depulsory:/dɪˈpʌlsəri/
adjective
强制的，义务的
required by law or a rule; obligatory
例句：Attendance at school is compulsory for children of certain ages.  特定年龄的儿童必须上学。

例句：Military service is compulsory in many countries.  在许多国家，服兵役是强制性的。

近义词：mandatory, obligatory, required
反义词：optional, voluntary, elective
词性：adjective

词源：
From Late Latin *dēpulsōrius, from Latin dēpellere (“to drive away, avert”), from dē- (“from, away from”) + pellere (“to drive, push”).  The original sense was related to averting evil by ritual, hence "having the force of ritual obligation".
