manatee:/ˈmænətiː/| n.|海牛|A large, slow-moving aquatic mammal with a rounded tail fin, found in warm coastal waters and rivers.|例句：The manatee grazed peacefully on seagrass. 海牛平静地吃着海草。

例句：We saw a mother manatee with her calf. 我们看到了一只带着幼崽的海牛妈妈。|近义词：sea cow|反义词:None|词性:noun
Etymology: From Spanish *manatí*, from Taíno *manati*.
