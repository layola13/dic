frost:/frɒst/ (UK), /frɔːst/ (US)| n. v.| 霜；冰冻；严寒；冷漠 |A deposit of small white ice crystals formed on the ground or other surfaces when the temperature falls below freezing.|The car windows were covered in frost. 汽车车窗上覆盖着一层霜。|He gave me a frosty stare. 他冷冷地盯着我。|近义词: ice, freeze, coldness, chill|反义词: thaw, warmth|noun, verb
