microbiology: /ˌmaɪkrəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi/ (UK) /ˌmaɪkroʊbaɪˈɑːlədʒi/ (US) | n. | 微生物学 | The study of microorganisms, including bacteria, viruses, fungi, and algae. | Microbiology is a fascinating field of study. 微生物学是一个引人入胜的研究领域。| Microbiology plays a crucial role in understanding diseases. 微生物学在理解疾病方面起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: bacteriology（细菌学） (for a subset) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
micro- (small) + bio- (life) + -logy (study of)
