forgiven:/fərˈɡɪvn/
|adj.|被原谅的，宽恕的|Having been pardoned or absolved of an offense or debt.|I felt forgiven after apologizing. 道歉后我感觉被原谅了。|Synonyms: pardoned, excused, absolved|Antonyms: unforgiven, condemned, blamed
|词性:形容词|
