faunlike:/ˈfɔːnlaɪk/| adj.|像小鹿的，温顺的，优雅的|Resembling or characteristic of a fawn, especially in being gentle, graceful, or timid.|She had a faunlike grace and innocence. 她有一种小鹿般的优雅和天真。|Synonyms: graceful, elegant, delicate, gentle, timid, shy|Antonyms: clumsy, awkward, aggressive, bold|adjective
