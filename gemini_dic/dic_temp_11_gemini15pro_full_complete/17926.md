beethoven:/ˈbeɪthoʊvən/ (US) /ˈbeɪtˌhəʊvən/ (UK)| n.|贝多芬|Ludwig van Beethoven (1770-1827), a German composer and pianist. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest composers of all time.  His innovative compositions combined vocal and instrumental music, widening the scope of sonata, symphony, concerto and quartet.
