aerobaticses:/ˌɛərəˈbætɪksɪz/
|n.|特技飞行；空中特技|The air show featured breathtaking aerobaticses. 航空展上 menampilkan pertunjukan aerobatik yang menakjubkan.|
例句：The pilot performed daring aerobaticses in the sky. 飞行员在空中进行了大胆的特技飞行。
例句：The crowd was thrilled by the spectacular aerobaticses of the Blue Angels.  观众被蓝天使的壮观特技飞行所激动。
近义词：stunts, aerial acrobatics, maneuvers
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "straight and level flight")
词性:noun

词源：From aero- (relating to air) + Greek akrobatēs (acrobat).
