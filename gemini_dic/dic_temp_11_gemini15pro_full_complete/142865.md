out-lot:/ˈaʊtˌlɑːt/
n.
外围地块；独立地块
A parcel of land that is part of a subdivision but is separated from other lots, often used for commercial purposes or amenities.
The developer set aside an out-lot for a convenience store.  开发商预留了一块外围地块用于建造便利店。
The out-lot was designated for a community swimming pool. 这块独立地块被指定用于建造社区游泳池。
synonym:  peripheral lot, detached lot
antonym:  inner lot, main lot
noun
