colicky:/ˈkɒlɪki/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlɪki/ (US)| adj.| 绞痛的，患绞痛的|Relating to or resembling colic; suffering from or caused by colic.  Characterized by or causing severe pain in the abdomen or bowels.|The baby was colicky and cried for hours.  这个婴儿肚子绞痛，哭了几个小时。|Synonyms: spasmodic, griping, crampy|Antonyms: painless, comfortable|Adjective

colic:/ˈkɒlɪk/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlɪk/ (US)| n.| 绞痛|Severe pain in the abdomen or bowels.|The horse suffered from colic. 这匹马患了绞痛。|Synonyms: gripes, cramps, spasms|Antonyms: comfort, ease|Noun
