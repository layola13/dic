ego-tripped:/ˈiːɡoʊˌtrɪpt/ | adj. | 自我陶醉的，自负的 |  Experiencing or exhibiting an exaggerated sense of self-importance; excessively self-absorbed and focused on one's own ego.  Derived from "ego" (self) and "trip" (a journey, often a psychedelic one, characterized by altered perceptions). Implies a state of being overly focused on oneself, often to the point of delusion or detachment from reality. | He went on an ego trip after winning the award, bragging about it to everyone.  他获奖后就自我陶醉了，逢人就吹嘘。

He's so ego-tripped, he thinks everyone admires him. 他太自负了，以为每个人都崇拜他。| 近义词: narcissistic, self-absorbed, conceited, egoistic, vain | 反义词: humble, modest, self-effacing | 词性: 形容词
