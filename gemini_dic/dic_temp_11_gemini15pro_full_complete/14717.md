awareness:/əˈwɛːrnɪs/ | n. | 意识，认识，了解 | knowledge or perception of a situation or fact | Example: Public awareness of the problem is growing. 公众对这个问题的意识正在增强。 | Example: She had a keen awareness of his feelings. 她对他感觉的敏锐的洞察力。 | Synonyms: consciousness, realization, understanding, knowledge, perception | Antonyms: ignorance, unawareness | noun
