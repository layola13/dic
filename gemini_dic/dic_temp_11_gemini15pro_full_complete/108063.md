latent:/ˈleɪtnt/| adj.|潜在的，潜伏的，隐藏的|Present but not yet active, developed, or visible.|The virus can remain latent in the body for years. 病毒可以在体内潜伏多年。|近义词：dormant, inactive, hidden, potential, underlying|反义词：active, apparent, manifest, obvious|adjective

latent:/ˈleɪtnt/| n.|潜能，潜在力|Something that exists but is not active or cannot be seen.|He has a latent talent for music. 他有潜在的音乐天赋。|近义词：potential, capacity, possibility|反义词：actuality, reality|noun
