baby-talk:/ˈbeɪbi tɔːk/ (US), /ˈbeɪbi tɔːk/ (UK)| n.|婴儿语；儿语|A simplified form of speech used when talking to babies, characterized by high pitch, repetition, and simple vocabulary.|She often uses baby-talk with her little nephew. 她经常用儿语和她的小侄子说话。|近义词：child-directed speech, motherese|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps formal speech)|noun
