collusive:/kəˈluːsɪv/| adj.|共谋的，串通的|Involving or characterized by collusion; secretive and fraudulent.|例句：The collusive activities of the two companies were eventually exposed. 这两家公司的共谋活动最终被曝光。|例句：The price fixing was a result of collusive behavior by several leading manufacturers.  价格垄断是几家主要制造商串通行为的结果。|近义词：conspiratorial, clandestine, fraudulent, deceitful|反义词：honest, aboveboard, open, legitimate|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *collusivus, from Latin colludere "to play together, conspire," from com- "together" + ludere "to play."
