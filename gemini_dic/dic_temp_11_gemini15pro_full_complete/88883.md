hokey:/ˈhoʊki/| adj.|做作的，老套的，不自然的|contrived, artificial, overly sentimental, often in a way that is intended to be appealing but seems silly or insincere.  Something hokey can feel forced or clichéd.|The plot of the movie was incredibly hokey, full of predictable twists and unbelievable coincidences.  这部电影的情节非常老套，充满了可以预见的转折和令人难以置信的巧合。

Her dialogue was so hokey it was almost painful to listen to.  她的对话太做作了，听得人很难受。|近义词：corny, cheesy, schmaltzy, sentimental|反义词：genuine, sincere, authentic|词性:形容词
