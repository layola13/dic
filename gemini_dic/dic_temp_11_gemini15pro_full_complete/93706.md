imitatrix:/ˈɪmɪteɪtrɪks/
n.
女模仿者；女演员
a female imitator, especially an actress.
She was a brilliant imitatrix of famous actresses. 她是一位出色模仿著名女演员的人。
近义词:mimic, impersonator
反义词:none
词性:noun

imitatrix:/ˈɪmɪteɪtrɪks/
n.
女模仿艺术家
a female performer who imitates others, often for comedic effect.
The imitatrix had the audience in stitches with her impressions of politicians.  这位女模仿艺术家对政治人物的模仿让观众捧腹大笑。
近义词:impressionist, copycat
反义词:none
词性:noun
