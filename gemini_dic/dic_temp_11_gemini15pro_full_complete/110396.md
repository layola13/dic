liguliflorous:/ˌlɪɡjʊlɪˈflɔːrəs/
adj.

Having ligulate florets; belonging to the Liguliflorae.
例句：Dandelions are liguliflorous plants. 蒲公英是舌状花冠植物。

例句：The liguliflorous nature of the flower makes it easily recognizable.  这种花的舌状花冠特性使其易于辨认。
