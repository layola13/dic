auth:/ɔːθ/ | n. | 授权，认证 | Authorization or authentication, the process of verifying the identity of a user, device, or other entity. |  The auth system failed, locking several users out of their accounts.  授权系统出现故障，导致多名用户无法登录帐户。 | Synonyms: authentication, authorization, verification, validation | Antonyms: denial, rejection | noun

/ɔːθ/ | v. | 授权，认证 | To authorize or authenticate.  (This usage is less common but becoming more prevalent, especially in technical contexts.) |  The system will auth you using your fingerprint. 系统将使用你的指纹对你进行身份验证。 | Synonyms: authorize, authenticate, verify, validate | Antonyms: deny, reject | verb
