liabilities:/ˌlaɪəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|负债，债务|A company's liabilities are the sums of money which it owes.| The company's liabilities exceeded its assets.  这家公司的负债超过了它的资产。|Synonyms: debts, obligations |Antonyms: assets, equity| noun


liability:/ˌlaɪəˈbɪləti/| n.|责任，义务；负债；累赘|Legal liability for the accident rests with the driver.  这起事故的法律责任在于司机。|The company has liabilities of over $20 million. 这家公司负债超过2000万美元。 |His lack of social skills is a liability in his job. 他缺乏社交技巧，这对他来说是一个工作上的障碍。 |Synonyms: responsibility, accountability, debt, burden |Antonyms: asset, advantage| noun
