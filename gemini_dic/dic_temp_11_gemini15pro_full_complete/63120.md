enslave:/ɪnˈsleɪv/| vt.|奴役，使沦为奴隶|To make someone a slave; to deprive someone of freedom of choice or action.|The colonists enslaved many of the native people. 殖民者奴役了许多当地人。|Synonyms: subjugate, oppress, captive, subdue|Antonyms: liberate, free, emancipate|Verb

enslave:/ɪnˈsleɪv/| vt.|使沉溺，使着迷|To cause someone to become obsessed or completely dominated by something.|He was enslaved by his addiction to gambling. 他沉迷于赌博。|Synonyms: captivate, enthrall, addict|Antonyms: disenchant, liberate|Verb
