fuzzies:/ˈfʌziz/| n.|模糊的感觉；模糊的记忆；暖和舒适的感觉|a vague feeling of warmth and affection|例句：I get the fuzzies when I see pictures of my childhood. 我看到我童年的照片时，会感到暖暖的。

例句：She had the fuzzies after drinking hot chocolate. 喝了热巧克力后，她感到暖洋洋的。|近义词：warm feelings, good vibes, butterflies|反义词：negative feelings, chills, discomfort|词性:名词

词源:  Likely a playful pluralization of "fuzzy," which itself originates from "fuzz" (1570s), referring to loose, light fibers or fluff.  The sense of vagueness or indistinctness developed later.
