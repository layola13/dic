nasalizing:/ˈneɪzəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb|鼻音化|To make a sound nasal by lowering the soft palate so that air passes through the nose.|She is nasalizing her vowels.|近义词：nasalise|反义词:oralize
adjective|鼻音化的|Pronounced with air passing through the nose.|The nasalizing effect of a cold.|近义词：nasal|反义词:oral
