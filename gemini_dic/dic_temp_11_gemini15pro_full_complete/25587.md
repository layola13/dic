bumkin:/ˈbʌmkɪn/| n. |乡巴佬，土包子|A person from the countryside who is considered to be awkward and unsophisticated.|He's a real bumkin, he's never been to a big city before. 他真是个乡巴佬，以前从未去过大城市。|近义词：yokel, hick, rustic, peasant|反义词:sophisticate, cosmopolitan|noun

bumkin:/ˈbʌmkɪn/| n. |乡下人，粗人|Someone who is perceived as unsophisticated or socially awkward, often due to their rural background.|She felt like a bumkin at the fancy gala. 在奢华的晚会上，她觉得自己像个乡巴佬。|近义词：country bumpkin, yokel |反义词:city slicker|noun
