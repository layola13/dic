didelphic:/daɪˈdɛlfɪk/
adjective
双阴道的，有两个子宫的
Having two uteri and two vaginas.
Didelphic marsupials have two separate uteri and vaginas.  双阴道的有袋动物有两个独立的子宫和阴道。
The didelphic reproductive system is a characteristic feature of certain marsupials.  双阴道生殖系统是某些有袋动物的特征。
synonym: bitubular
antonym: monodelphic
词性: adjective
