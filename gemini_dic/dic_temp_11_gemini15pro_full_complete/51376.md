determinableness:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnəblənəs/ |n.|可确定性，可判定性|The determinableness of the outcome depends on several factors. 结果的可确定性取决于几个因素。|The lawyer questioned the determinableness of the evidence. 律师质疑证据的可判定性。|近义词:certainty, decidability, resolvability|反义词:indeterminacy, uncertainty, ambiguity|词性:名词

词源: From determine (v.) + -able (suffix) + -ness (suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *determinare*, from *de-* (completely) + *terminare* (to limit).
