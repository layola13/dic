efl:/iː ɛf ɛl/ |n.|English as a foreign language|English as a foreign language refers to the learning and teaching of English by speakers whose native language is not English.  EFL is often contrasted with ESL (English as a second language), where English is commonly spoken in the community.|She is studying EFL in Japan.  她在日本学习英语作为外语。 | synonym: ESOL (English for Speakers of Other Languages),  TESOL (Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages)| antonym: native language, mother tongue | noun
