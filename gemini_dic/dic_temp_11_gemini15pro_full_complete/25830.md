burglariously:/bɜːrˈɡlɛəriəsli/
adv.
入室盗窃地，非法闯入地
In a manner like a burglar; stealthily and illegally.
He entered the house burglariously, hoping to steal the jewels. 他偷偷摸摸地潜入房子，希望偷走珠宝。
The thief burglariously slipped into the museum at night. 小偷在夜里偷偷溜进了博物馆。
Synonyms: stealthily, furtively, surreptitiously, sneakily
Antonyms: openly, honestly, legally


Etymology:
From burglar + -ious + -ly.  "Burglar" comes from Old French "burglaire," meaning "housebreaker," ultimately from Germanic roots.
