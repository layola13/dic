moonlit:/ˈmuːnlɪt/| adj.|月光照耀的|Lit by the moon.  The scene was bathed in moonlit beauty.|例句：We walked along the moonlit beach. 我们沿着月光照耀的海滩散步。

例句：The moonlit garden was a place of magic and mystery. 月光下的花园是一个充满魔力与神秘的地方。|近义词：lunar, moon-drenched, moon-bright|反义词：dark, shadowy, unlit|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From moon + -lit,  "-lit" being a suffix meaning "illuminated by," derived from the past participle of the verb "light."
