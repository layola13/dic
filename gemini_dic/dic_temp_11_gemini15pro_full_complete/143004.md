outbelches:/ˌaʊtˈbɛltʃɪz/
verb
喷出;大声喷出
to erupt or eject (something, especially lava, gas, or smoke) noisily and forcefully.
The volcano outbelched lava and ash. 火山喷出熔岩和火山灰。
The dragon outbelched a plume of fire.  龙喷出一股火焰。
近义词:erupt, spew, belch, expel
反义词:absorb, inhale, ingest
