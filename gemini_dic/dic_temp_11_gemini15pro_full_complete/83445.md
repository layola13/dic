habitative:/ˈhæbɪtətɪv/
adjective
可居住的；适于居住的
Suitable for habitation; habitable.
The planet Mars is not currently habitative for humans. 火星目前不适合人类居住。
近义词：inhabitable, residential, livable
反义词：uninhabitable, unlivable
词性：adjective
词源：From Late Latin *habitativus, from Latin habitare (“to inhabit”) + -ivus.
