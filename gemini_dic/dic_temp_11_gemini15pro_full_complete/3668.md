agglutination:/əˌɡluːtɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |凝集，粘着|The process by which particles, such as red blood cells, clump together.|The agglutination of red blood cells is a key part of blood typing. 红细胞的凝集是血型鉴定的关键部分。|cohesion, adhesion, clumping|dispersion, separation|noun

agglutinate:/əˈɡluːtɪneɪt/| v. |凝集，粘合|To cause particles to clump together.|Certain antibodies can agglutinate red blood cells.  某些抗体会使红细胞凝集。|cluster, aggregate, bind|separate, disperse|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *agglutinatio, from Latin agglutinare "to glue to," from ad- "to" + glutinare "to glue," from gluten "glue."
