nvm:/ˌɛnˌviːˈɛm/| abbreviation |没关系；不要紧；算了|Short for "never mind." Used online or in text messages to indicate that something previously stated is no longer relevant or important.|例句：A: I thought we were meeting at 7. B: nvm, it's 8.  A：我以为我们7点见面。B：算了，是8点。|例句：I was going to complain about the service, but nvm, it's not worth it. 我本来要抱怨服务的，但是算了，不值得。|近义词：it's okay, forget it, no worries, don't worry about it|反义词:it matters|abbreviation
