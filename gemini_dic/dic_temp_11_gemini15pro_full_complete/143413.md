outflared:/ˌaʊtˈfleərd/
adjective
向外张开的，喇叭形的
spreading outward, resembling a flare
The outflared skirt flowed gracefully as she danced.  她跳舞时，喇叭形的裙子飘逸地舞动。
Her dress had an outflared hem. 她的裙子下摆是喇叭形的。
近义词: flared, bell-shaped, widening
反义词: tapered, narrow, constricted
词性: adjective
