condurrite:/kənˈdʌraɪt/| n. |砷铜矿|A naturally occurring mineral composed of arsenic and copper.  It is often found in association with other copper arsenides.|Condurrite is a rare mineral found in some copper deposits. 砷铜矿是一种罕见的矿物，存在于一些铜矿床中。|Synonyms: domeykite, algodonite |Antonyms: N/A|noun
