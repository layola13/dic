intercompany:/ˌɪntərˈkʌmpəni/ | adj. |公司间的，集团内部的|Occurring between or involving different companies of the same group.|  Intercompany transactions can be complex. 集团内部交易可能很复杂。| The intercompany loan helped the subsidiary stay afloat. 公司间的贷款帮助子公司维持运营。 |近义词：intragroup, intracorporate|反义词：external, outside|词性:形容词

词源:  "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among") + "company"
