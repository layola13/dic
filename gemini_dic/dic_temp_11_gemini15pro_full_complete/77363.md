gaugeably:/ˈɡɔːdʒəbli/ | adv. | 可测量地，可评估地 |  In a way that can be gauged or measured.  | The progress of the project is gaugeably improving. 项目的进展可以明显地看到正在改善。 | measurably, quantifiably, discernibly | immeasurably, imperceptibly | adverb

Etymology->
From gauge (v.) + -ably.  "Gauge" comes from Old North French "gauger," meaning "to measure the contents of a cask."
