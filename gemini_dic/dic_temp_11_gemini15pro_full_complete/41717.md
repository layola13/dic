corroboree: /kəˈrɒbəri/ (AU) /ˈkɔːrəˌbɔːri/ (US) | n. | 狂欢会，欢庆仪式；澳洲土著的仪式 | A ceremonial meeting of Australian Aborigines, often involving elaborate costumes, dancing, and singing. |  The tribe held a corroboree to celebrate the harvest.  部落举行狂欢会庆祝丰收。 |  The corroboree lasted for several days and nights.  狂欢会持续了好几个昼夜。 | synonyms: celebration, festival, ceremony, ritual | antonyms: wake, funeral | noun
