anti-platonisms: /ˌæntiˈpleɪtənɪzəmz/ | n. | 反柏拉图主义 |  Philosophical positions that oppose or reject the central tenets of Platonism, particularly the theory of Forms.  |  Example:  Anti-platonisms question the existence of an abstract realm of perfect ideals. 反柏拉图主义质疑完美理想的抽象领域的存在。

Example:  Modern anti-platonisms often focus on the implications of Plato's philosophy for ethics and politics. 现代反柏拉图主义通常关注柏拉图哲学对伦理和政治的影响。 | 近义词: nominalism, materialism | 反义词: Platonism, idealism | 词性: noun
