compulsivities:/kəmˌpʌlˈsɪvɪtiz/
|n.|强迫行为，强迫性|The state of being compulsive; a strong, often irresistible impulse to perform repetitive acts.|Her hand-washing rituals are just one of her many compulsivities.|近义词:obsessions, compulsions|反义词:control, restraint|词性:名词

Etymology: From compulsive + -ity.  Compulsive from compel + -ive. Compel from Latin compellere "to drive together, force, constrain," from com- "together" + pellere "to drive."
