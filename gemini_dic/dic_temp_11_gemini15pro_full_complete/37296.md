commandingnesses:/kəˈmɑːndɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
威严，气势; 掌控力; 指挥才能
The quality of being commanding; imperiousness or authoritativeness.
Her commandingness filled the room, silencing all dissent. 她的威严弥漫整个房间，让所有异议都消失了。
The CEO's commandingness was evident in every decision he made.  这位首席执行官的掌控力体现在他做出的每一个决定中。
authority, dominance, imperiousness
submissiveness, meekness, timidity
noun
