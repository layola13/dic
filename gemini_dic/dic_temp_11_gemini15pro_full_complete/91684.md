hypercolumn:/ˈhaɪpəˌkɑːləm/ | n. | 超柱 | A group of neurons in the visual cortex that are thought to represent a small region of the visual field and respond to all orientations and other features within that region.  | Example 1:  The researchers studied the activity of hypercolumns in the cat's visual cortex. 研究人员研究了猫的视觉皮层中超柱的活动。

Example 2: The concept of the hypercolumn is crucial for understanding how the brain processes visual information. 超柱的概念对于理解大脑如何处理视觉信息至关重要。 | Synonym:  cortical column, minicolumn (related, not exact synonyms) | Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a anatomical structure) | noun
