life-saving:/ˈlaɪfˌseɪvɪŋ/
adjective
救命的；救生的
designed to save or protect people's lives.
例句：The doctor's quick thinking proved to be life-saving.  医生的快速反应被证明是救命的。

例句：The life-saving equipment on the ship was regularly inspected. 船上的救生设备定期检查。

近义词：life-preserving, essential, crucial
反义词：life-threatening, fatal, deadly
词性：adjective
