omnispective:/ˌɒm.nɪˈspek.tɪv/
adjective
全视角的；全方位的
Having the ability to see everything; all-seeing.
From Latin *omnis* (all) + *spectus* (sight, view).  Relatively modern coinage, likely analogous to "perspective."
例句：The author attempted an omnispective narrative, showing the events from every character's point of view.  作者尝试了一种全视角的叙事，从每个角色的视角展现事件。

例句：With advanced surveillance technology, we are approaching an omnispective view of the city. 凭借先进的监控技术，我们正在接近对这座城市的全方位视角。
近义词：panoramic, all-seeing, comprehensive, universal
反义词：limited, restricted, narrow, myopic
词性：adjective
