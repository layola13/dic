He stared nonconvivially at the newcomer, making them feel unwelcome. 他不友好地盯着新来的人，让他们感到不受欢迎。 | 近义词：unsociably, unfriendly, inhospitably, coldly | 反义词：convivially, sociably, warmly, hospitably | 词性: 副词

词源: non- (not) + convivial (festive, sociable) + -ly (adverb suffix)
