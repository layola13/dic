cliquing: /ˈklɪkɪŋ/
n. |结党营私，小集团|The act of forming or joining a clique.  | There's a lot of cliquing going on in that office, making it difficult for newcomers to feel welcome. 办公室里有很多小集团，这让新来的人很难感到受欢迎。|近义词：ganging up, associating, socializing (in a closed group)|反义词：integrating, mingling, fraternizing|词性: 名词 (Gerund - 动名词)

cliquing: /ˈklɪkɪŋ/
v. |形成小集团，拉帮结派 (rarely used as a verb on its own) |Participating in the formation of a clique. | They were cliquing together and excluding everyone else. 他们聚在一起，排斥其他人。|近义词：ganging up, associating, socializing (in a closed group)|反义词：integrating, mingling, fraternizing|词性: 动词 (Present Participle)

词源:  From the noun "clique," borrowed from French "clique," meaning "latch, handle," figuratively a "small group of people."  Ultimately from Old French "cliquer," meaning "to click, clank."
