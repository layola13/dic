baleo:/ˈbeɪlioʊ/ | v. | 摇晃，使不稳定 | To cause something to sway or rock unsteadily, as if about to fall. | The strong winds baleoed the small boat.  强风使小船摇晃不定。|Synonyms: rock, sway, wobble, totter | Antonyms: stabilize, steady, secure | verb

baleo:/ˈbeɪlioʊ/ | v. | 使迷惑，使犹豫 | To make someone uncertain or hesitant.  | The sudden change of plans baleoed him. 突如其来的计划改变使他犹豫不决。 | Synonyms: confuse, bewilder, disconcert, unsettle | Antonyms: reassure, comfort, settle | verb
