calumniator:/kəˈlʌmniˌeɪtər/| n.|诽谤者，污蔑者|A person who makes false and malicious statements about someone. |The politician denounced his opponent as a calumniator who spread lies to damage his reputation.  这个政治家谴责他的对手是一个诽谤者，散布谎言来损害他的名誉。|Synonyms: slanderer, libeler, defamer, traducer|Antonyms: defender, advocate|Noun
