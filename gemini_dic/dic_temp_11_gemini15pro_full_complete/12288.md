ascidiarium:/ˌæsɪˈdɪəriəm/
n.
海鞘养殖场，海鞘缸
A container or enclosed area for keeping ascidians (sea squirts).
The marine biologist kept a small ascidiarium in her lab to study the creatures' reproductive cycle.  海洋生物学家在她的实验室里养了一个小的海鞘缸，用来研究这些生物的繁殖周期。
The ascidiarium housed various species of colorful sea squirts.  这个海鞘养殖场养殖了各种各样色彩鲜艳的海鞘。
近义词:aquarium, tank
词性:noun
