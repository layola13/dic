nonascendantly:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndəntli/
adverb|非上升地；非支配地|in a way that is not ascendant or dominant; not in an ascending or increasing order|The numbers were arranged nonascendantly, from highest to lowest.|近义词:descendingly, decreasingly|反义词:ascendantly, increasingly|词性:adverb
词源: non- (not) + ascendant (rising) + -ly (adverbial suffix)
