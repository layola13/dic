antlike:/ˈæntˌlaɪk/
adjective
像蚂蚁的；蚂蚁般的
Resembling an ant in appearance, activity, or behavior.
The workers moved in an antlike procession across the factory floor.  工人们像蚂蚁一样排着队穿过工厂车间。
Synonyms: industrious, diligent, tireless, busy
Antonyms: lazy, indolent, slothful
词性: 形容词
