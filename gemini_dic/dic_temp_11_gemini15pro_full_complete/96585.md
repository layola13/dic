inductances:/ɪnˈdʌktənsɪz/| n.|电感；电感量|The property of an electrical circuit by which a change in current induces an electromotive force.|The circuit has a high inductance.  这个电路的电感很高。|Near synonyms:  inductance (singular), self-inductance, impedance|Antonyms: capacitance|noun
