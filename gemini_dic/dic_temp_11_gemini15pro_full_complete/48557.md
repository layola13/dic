defamers:/dɪˈfeɪmərz/| n.|诽谤者，中伤者|People who defame someone; those who attack the reputation of another. |The defamers spread lies about the politician to ruin his career. 诽谤者散布关于这位政治家的谎言，以毁掉他的职业生涯。|synonyms: slanderers, libelers, calumniators, traducers |antonyms:  praisers, eulogizers| noun
defamers:/dɪˈfeɪmərz/| n.|诽谤者，中伤者|Individuals who make false and damaging statements about someone. |The journalist sued the defamers for their malicious accusations.  这位记者起诉了诽谤者，因为他们进行了恶意指控。|synonyms:  mudslingers, backbiters |antonyms:  supporters, advocates| noun
