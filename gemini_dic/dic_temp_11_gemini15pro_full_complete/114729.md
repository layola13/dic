malls:/mɔːlz/
n.
购物中心，商场
large enclosed shopping areas containing many different stores, restaurants, and other businesses.
We went to the malls to do some Christmas shopping. 我们去购物中心买圣诞礼物。
shopping centers, plazas, retail complexes
individual stores, local shops
