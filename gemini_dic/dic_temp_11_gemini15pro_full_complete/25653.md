bundling:/ˈbʌndəlɪŋ/| n.|捆绑，打包，归拢；（尤指美国早期的一种求爱风俗）同床共眠；搭售，捆绑销售|The bundling of products can often lead to cost savings for the consumer.  产品打包通常可以为消费者节省成本。|例句：The company offers bundling of its internet, phone, and TV services.  这家公司提供互联网、电话和电视服务的捆绑销售。

例句：The software comes bundled with a free antivirus program.  该软件捆绑了一个免费的杀毒程序。|近义词：packaging, combining, grouping, tying, binding|反义词:unbundling, separating, dividing|词性:noun
