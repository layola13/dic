nicknamer:/ˈnɪkˌneɪmər/
n.
给别人起绰号的人;  爱称者
A person who gives nicknames. One who uses nicknames or pet names.
He's a real nicknamer; everyone in the office has a nickname he came up with. 他真是个爱起绰号的人; 办公室里每个人都有一个他起的绰号。
My grandmother was a nicknamer, she had a special name for everyone in the family.  我祖母是个爱用昵称的人，她对家里的每个人都有一个特别的称呼。
synonym:  name-giver,  coiner of nicknames
antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider) formalist
