conventionalization:/kənˌvɛnʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |常规化，惯例化|The process of something becoming conventional or establishing conventions.  The act of making something conform to established conventions or rules. |The conventionalization of greetings in different cultures varies widely. 不同文化中问候语的常规化差异很大。| standardization, formalization, normalization | individualization, innovation | noun
etymology-> From conventionalize + -ation.  Conventionalize from convention + -al + -ize. Convention from Old French convencion, from Latin conventiō (“a coming together, meeting, agreement”), from conveniō (“I come together”), from con- (“together”) + veniō (“I come”).
