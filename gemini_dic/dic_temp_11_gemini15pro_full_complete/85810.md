hazards:/ˈhæzərdz/| n.|危险，危害，隐患|A hazard is something that is dangerous and likely to cause harm.|The busy road is a hazard to pedestrians. 这条繁忙的道路对行人来说很危险。|synonyms: dangers, risks, perils, threats|antonyms: safety, security|noun
