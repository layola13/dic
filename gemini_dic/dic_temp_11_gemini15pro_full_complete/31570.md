chasidism:/ˈxɑːsɪdɪzəm/ /ˈhɑːsɪdɪzəm/
n.
哈西德派，哈西德主义
A branch of Orthodox Judaism emphasizing mysticism, piety, and joy.
Chasidism is a Jewish religious movement that originated in Eastern Europe in the 18th century. 哈西德主义是18世纪起源于东欧的一个犹太教宗教运动。

The rabbi's teachings were rooted in Chasidism.  这位拉比的教义植根于哈西德主义。

近义词:Hasidism,  Jewish mysticism
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly secularism or assimilation)
词性:noun
