equiform:/ˈiːkwɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 同形的，全等的 | Having the same shape and size. | The two triangles are equiform. 这两个三角形全等。 |  Example: The two stamps are equiform, making it difficult to distinguish between them. 这两枚邮票形状相同，难以区分。

Example:  The architect designed two equiform buildings, one in London and one in New York.  这位建筑师设计了两座形状相同的建筑，一座在伦敦，一座在纽约。

| 近义词：congruent, identical, similar | 反义词：different, dissimilar | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin "aequi-" (equal) + "-formis" (shaped).
