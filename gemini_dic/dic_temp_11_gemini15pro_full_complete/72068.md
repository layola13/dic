flittermouse:/ˈflɪtərmaʊs/| n. |蝙蝠|An archaic or dialect term for a bat, referring to its erratic, fluttering flight. It literally means "fluttering mouse."|例句：The flittermouse darted through the twilight sky. 蝙蝠在暮色中飞掠而过。

例句：Old tales speak of flittermice haunting dark caves.  古老的故事讲述了蝙蝠出没于黑暗的洞穴。|近义词：bat|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
