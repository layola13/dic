instructively:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪvli/
adverb
有教育意义地；指导性地
in a way that provides useful information or ideas
The museum's exhibits are instructively labeled.  博物馆的展品都贴上了具有教育意义的标签。
The guide explained the history of the castle instructively. 导游生动地讲解了城堡的历史。

近义词:  informatively, educationally, enlighteningly, helpfully
反义词:  unhelpfully, uselessly, pointlessly
