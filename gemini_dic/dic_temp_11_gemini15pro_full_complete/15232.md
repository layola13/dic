backfilling:/ˈbækˌfɪlɪŋ/
n.|回填，回填土|The process of refilling an excavated area, such as a trench or foundation, with the material that was removed or with other suitable material.|例句：Backfilling is crucial after pipe installation to prevent ground collapse.  回填对于管道安装后防止地面塌陷至关重要。

例句：Proper backfilling around the foundation is necessary for stability.  在地基周围适当回填对于稳定性是必要的。|近义词：filling, refilling|反义词:excavating|词性:名词

backfilling:/ˈbækˌfɪlɪŋ/
v.|回填|The act of refilling an excavated area.|例句：They are backfilling the hole with gravel.  他们正在用砾石回填那个洞。

例句：The workers were backfilling the trench after laying the pipes.  工人们在铺设管道后正在回填沟渠。|近义词：filling, refilling|反义词:excavating|词性:动词
