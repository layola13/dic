mosquito:/məˈskiːtoʊ/ (US), /məˈskiːtəʊ/ (UK)| n. |蚊子|A small flying insect that bites people and animals and sucks their blood. Some types of mosquitoes spread diseases such as malaria.|
例句：
The mosquitoes were buzzing around my head all night.
蚊子整夜都在我头上嗡嗡叫。

Mosquitoes are a common pest in tropical climates.
蚊子是热带气候中常见的害虫。

近义词：gnat, midge|
反义词: (No direct antonym, but one might consider "predator" in the context of the food chain) |
词性: noun
