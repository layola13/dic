maladministered:/ˌmælədˈmɪnɪstərd/ | v. | 管理不善的；处理不当的 |  Managed or handled badly, inefficiently, or improperly, especially of official duties or public funds. | The funds were maladministered, leading to significant losses. 这笔资金管理不善，导致了重大损失。| The government was accused of maladministering the relief effort. 政府被指控对救灾工作处理不当。 | misused, mishandled, mismanaged | well-administered, properly managed | Verb
