missionate:/ˈmɪʃəneɪt/
verb
赋予使命；派遣；
to send someone on a mission.
例句：He was missionated to spread the word of God. 他被派遣去传播上帝的福音。

例句：The organization missionated him to Africa to provide medical aid. 该组织派遣他去非洲提供医疗援助。

近义词：send, dispatch, commission, assign, appoint
反义词：recall, withdraw

词性：v.

词源：From the Latin word "missionem" meaning "act of sending".
