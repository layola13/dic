amazingness:/əˈmeɪzɪŋnəs/
n.
令人惊叹的程度; 惊人之处
The quality of being amazing; the extent to which something is amazing.
The sheer amazingness of the Grand Canyon left me speechless. 大峡谷的壮丽景色让我 speechless。
Her performance showcased the amazingness of human potential. 她的表演展现了人类潜力的惊人之处。
Synonyms: wonder, marvelousness, awesomeness
Antonyms: mediocrity, ordinariness
词性: 名词
