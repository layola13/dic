biotechnologies:/ˌbaɪoʊtekˈnɑːlədʒiz/
noun
生物技术
The exploitation of biological processes for industrial and other purposes, especially the genetic manipulation of microorganisms for the production of antibiotics, hormones, etc.
Biotechnologies are rapidly changing the face of medicine. 生物技术正在迅速改变医学的面貌。
近义词: genetic engineering, genetic modification
反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 traditional methods)
词性: noun (plural)
