beaus:/boʊz/
n.
男朋友;情郎;追求者
A boyfriend or male admirer.
She has several beaus vying for her attention. 她有几个追求者在争夺她的注意。
Synonyms: boyfriend, sweetheart, admirer, suitor
Antonyms: enemy, rival
