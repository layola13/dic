commiserately:/kəˈmɪzəreɪtli/| adv.|同情地，怜悯地|In a way that shows or expresses sympathy or pity.|She looked at him commiserately. 她同情地看着他。|近义词：sympathetically, compassionately, pityingly|反义词：unsympathetically, heartlessly|词性:副词
commiserate:/kəˈmɪzəreɪt/| v.|同情，怜悯|Express or feel sympathy or pity.|She commiserated with him over his loss. 她对他的损失表示同情。|近义词：sympathize, condole, empathize|反义词：ignore, disregard|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *commiserari, from Latin com- ‘together’ + miserari ‘pity’.
