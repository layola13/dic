endue:/ɪnˈdjuː/ /ɪnˈduː/ | vt. | 赋予，授予，使具有 | To provide with a quality or power.  |  例句：Nature has endued her with great musical talent.  大自然赋予了她极高的音乐天赋。 | 近义词：bestow, endow, imbue, invest | 反义词：deprive, divest | verb

endue:/ɪnˈdjuː/ /ɪnˈduː/ | vt. |  穿上，披上 (古语) | To clothe or dress; array. (archaic) |  例句：He was endued in a suit of shining armor.  他身披闪亮的盔甲。(古语用法) | 近义词：clothe, dress, attire | 反义词：undress, disrobe | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old French *enduire, *enduer (“to lead into, put on, endow”), from Latin inducere (“to lead in, introduce, induce”).  Related to induct.  The sense of "clothe, invest" is now archaic.
