garbler:/ˈɡɑːrblər/| n.|扰乱者，模糊不清说话的人，加密器|A person or device that garbles something, especially speech or a message, making it unclear or unintelligible; a device that encodes information.|The garbler made it impossible to understand the original message.  扰乱者使得理解原始信息变得不可能。|Synonyms: distorter, scrambler, mangler, muddler|Antonyms: clarifier, elucidator|noun


garble:/ˈɡɑːrbl(ə)l/| vt.|断章取义，歪曲，混淆|To reproduce (a message, sound, or transmission) in a confused and distorted way.|He garbled the facts in his retelling of the story. 他在复述故事时歪曲了事实。|Synonyms: distort, twist, misrepresent, confuse, muddle|Antonyms: clarify, explain, elucidate|verb
