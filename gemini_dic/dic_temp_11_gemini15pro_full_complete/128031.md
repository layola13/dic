nerdier:/ˈnɜːrdiər (US) /ˈnɜːdiə(r) (UK)| adj.|更书呆子的，更怪咖的|Comparative form of "nerdy," meaning more characteristic of a nerd.  It implies a greater degree of intellectual interests, often in specialized or unconventional areas, combined with social awkwardness or unconventional behavior.| He's become even nerdier since he started collecting vintage computers.  他自从开始收集古董电脑后变得更书呆子气了。|  She has a nerdier sense of humor than most people. 她比大多数人更有书呆子式的幽默感。 | synonyms: geekier, dorkier | antonyms: cooler, hipper, more popular | adjective
