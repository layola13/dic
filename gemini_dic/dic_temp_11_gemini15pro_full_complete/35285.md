cluttering:/ˈklʌtərɪŋ/ | n. | 杂乱，混乱 | The act of filling a space or area with too many things, so that it looks untidy. |  The cluttering of the room made it hard to find anything. 房间里的杂乱使人很难找到任何东西。| synonyms: disorder, mess, chaos | antonyms: order, tidiness, neatness | noun

cluttering:/ˈklʌtərɪŋ/ | adj. | 杂乱的 | Filled with too many things, so that it looks untidy.  | Her desk was cluttering with papers and books. 她的桌子堆满了文件和书籍。 | synonyms: messy, disordered, chaotic | antonyms: tidy, neat, organized | adjective
