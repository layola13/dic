blackfoot:/ˈblækˌfʊt/ | n. adj.|黑脚; 黑脚族人; 黑脚病|A member of a North American Indian people originally inhabiting Montana and parts of adjacent Canada. Also: the Algonquian language of this people.  Also (as an adjective): of or relating to this people or their language.|例句：The Blackfoot tribe were known for their hunting skills. 黑脚部落以其狩猎技巧而闻名。
