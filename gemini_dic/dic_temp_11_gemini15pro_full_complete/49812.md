demonstrability:/ˌdemənˌstreɪˈbɪləti/ | n. |可证实性，可论证性| The demonstrability of a scientific theory is crucial for its acceptance.  A scientific theory's ability to be proven through observation or experiment. |The demonstrability of his claims was weak, relying more on anecdote than evidence. 他的说法的可证明性很弱，更多地依赖轶事而不是证据。|  provability, verifiability |  undemonstrability, unprovability | noun
