gymnocladus:/ˌdʒɪmˈnɒklədəs/ | n. |皂荚树；肥皂荚|A genus of deciduous trees of the legume family (Fabaceae), native to eastern North America and East Asia, having bipinnate leaves and greenish-white flowers in clusters.|例句：The Kentucky coffeetree (Gymnocladus dioicus) is a species of Gymnocladus.  肯塔基咖啡树（二歧皂荚）是皂荚树的一个品种。

例句：Gymnocladus chinensis is native to China.  中国皂荚原产于中国。|近义词：coffeetree, Kentucky coffeetree|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
