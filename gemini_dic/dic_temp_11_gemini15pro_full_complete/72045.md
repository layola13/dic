flirtationless:/flɜːrˈteɪʃən.ləs/
adjective
无调情的；不轻佻的；不开玩笑的
Without flirtation; serious or not playful.
She maintained a flirtationless demeanor throughout the professional conference. 她整个专业会议期间都保持着严肃而不轻佻的态度。
His flirtationless approach to dating made some people uncomfortable.  他这种不开玩笑的约会方式让一些人感到不舒服。
近义词:serious, unflirtatious, formal, reserved, demure
反义词:flirtatious, playful, coquettish, teasing
词性:adjective

Etymology:
flirtation (n.) + -less (suffix)

flirtation (n.): from flirt (v.) + -ation (suffix forming nouns of action or process)
flirt (v.): origin uncertain, possibly imitative of a quick, light movement

-less (suffix): Old English -lēas "free from, without," from Proto-Germanic *-lausaz, from PIE root *leu- "to loosen, divide, cut apart."
