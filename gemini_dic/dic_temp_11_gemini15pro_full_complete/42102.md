cots:/kɒts/ (UK), /kɑːts/ (US)| n.|简易床；折叠床；婴儿床|A cot is a small bed, especially one for a baby or child, or a light narrow bed that folds up for storage or transport.|The baby slept soundly in its cot.|婴儿在婴儿床里睡得很香。|synonyms: crib, cradle, camp bed|antonyms: bed, double bed|noun
