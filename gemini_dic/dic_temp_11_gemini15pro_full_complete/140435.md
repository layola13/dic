okeydokey:/ˌoʊkiˈdoʊki/| interj.|好的，没问题|An informal way of saying "okay."  Expresses agreement or acknowledgment.|例句：Okeydokey, I'll be there at six. 好的，我六点到那里。|近义词：okay, all right, sure|反义词: no, nope|词性: interjection
Etymology:  A playful and emphatic variant of "OK," which itself has disputed origins, possibly from "oll korrect" (all correct), a humorous misspelling popular in the 1830s.
