adjective

Resembling a dish in form; shallow and concave. Also, used to describe something unpleasant or undesirable, often likened to something one wouldn't want to eat.

近义词:unpleasant, disagreeable, distasteful, offensive, repellent
反义词:pleasant, agreeable, appealing, attractive
词性:adjective

词源: dish + like.  "Dish" referring to a shallow, concave vessel for food, and "like" indicating resemblance. The negative connotation likely developed through the association of a dish with unwanted leftovers or unappetizing food.
