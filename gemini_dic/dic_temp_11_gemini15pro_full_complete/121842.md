misrendering:/ˌmɪsˈrɛndərɪŋ/
noun
错误的诠释；误译；歪曲
an incorrect or misleading interpretation or translation of something
The article was full of misrenderings of the original text. 这篇文章充满了对原文的误译。
misinterpretation, mistranslation, distortion
accurate rendering, correct interpretation
n.
