freshwaters:/ˈfreʃˌwɔːtərz/ |n.|淡水，淡水水域|Of or relating to fresh water; living in or inhabiting fresh water.  指淡水或与淡水有关的；生活在或栖息于淡水中的。|Many freshwaters species are endangered due to pollution. 许多淡水物种因污染而濒临灭绝。|近义词：fresh water, inland water|反义词：saltwater, marine water|词性：名词

freshwater:/ˈfreʃˌwɔːtər/ |adj.|淡水的|Living in or related to fresh water. 生活在淡水中或与淡水有关的。|Freshwater fish are not able to survive in the ocean. 淡水鱼无法在海洋中生存。|近义词：fluvial, lacustrine|反义词：saltwater, marine|词性：形容词


词源:
Old English *ferscwater (attested only in compounds such as ferscwæterfisc ‘freshwater fish’), from fersc ‘fresh’ + wæter ‘water’.
