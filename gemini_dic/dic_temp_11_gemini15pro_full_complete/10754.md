apyrexial:/ˌeɪpaɪˈrɛksiəl/
adjective
无热的；退热的
Without fever; afebrile.
The patient is now apyrexial after taking the medication. 病人服药后已退烧。
近义词:afebrile, feverless
反义词:febrile, feverish
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From a- (without) + pyrexial (feverish), ultimately from Greek pyr (fire).
