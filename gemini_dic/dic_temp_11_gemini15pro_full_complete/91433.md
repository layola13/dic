hygiene:/ˈhaɪdʒiːn/| n.|卫生；保健法|Conditions or practices conducive to maintaining health and preventing disease, especially through cleanliness.|例句：Good hygiene is essential to prevent the spread of infection. 良好的卫生习惯对于防止感染传播至关重要。

例句：Dental hygiene is important for healthy teeth and gums.  牙齿卫生对于牙齿和牙龈的健康很重要。|近义词：cleanliness, sanitation, purity|反义词：dirtiness, filth, squalor|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hygiène*, from Greek *hygieinē (tekhnē)* "art of health," from *hygies* "healthy, sound," related to *Hygieia*, goddess of health.
