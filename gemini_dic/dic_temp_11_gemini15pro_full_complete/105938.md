klick:/klɪk/ | n. | 点击（声），咔嗒声 | A short, sharp sound, such as that made by a switch or a camera shutter. |  The camera made a soft klick as I took the picture. 相机在我拍照时发出轻微的咔嗒声。 | click, snap | silence, hush | noun

klick:/klɪk/ | v. | 点击，发出咔嗒声 | To make a short, sharp sound. |  The lock klicked shut. 锁咔嗒一声关上了。 | click, snap |  - | verb

Etymology:  From German *klicken*, of imitative origin.
