nonfacetiousness:/ˌnɒnˌfeɪˈsiːʃəsnəs/| n. |认真；严肃；不开玩笑|The nonfacetiousness of his tone surprised everyone.  他的语气严肃，让大家都感到惊讶。|Example:  Her nonfacetiousness about the matter was unsettling. 她对此事的认真态度令人不安。|Synonyms: seriousness, gravity, solemnity, earnestness|Antonyms: facetiousness, flippancy, levity|noun


Etymology->
From non- + facetiousness.  Facetious comes from the French *facetie*, from Latin *facetia* "jest, wit," ultimately from *facetus* "witty, elegant."
