depoliticization:/diːpəˈlɪtɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|去政治化，非政治化
|The act or process of removing something from the realm of politics, or of making it less political.|
|The depoliticization of education is a controversial issue.  教育的去政治化是一个有争议的问题。|
|The government is attempting the depoliticization of the police force. 政府正试图将警察部队去政治化。|
|neutralization, demilitarization|
|politicization|
|noun|
