cack-handed:/ˌkækˈhændɪd/
adjective
笨手笨脚的；左撇子的
clumsy or awkward, especially with the hands.  Sometimes also used to mean left-handed (although this usage is considered outdated and potentially offensive).
He made a cack-handed attempt to fix the shelf and ended up making it worse. 他笨手笨脚地试图修理架子，结果反而弄得更糟了。
Synonyms: clumsy, awkward, inept, maladroit, ham-fisted
Antonyms: skillful, dexterous, adept, adroit
