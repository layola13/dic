mats:/mæts/ | v. |(mat 的第三人称单数)|becomes entangled or intertwined into a thick mass; becomes felted.|Her hair matted with sweat. 她的头发被汗水浸湿后纠结在一起。|Synonyms: tangle, snarl, felt|Antonyms: untangle, comb, separate|verb
