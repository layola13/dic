granuliferous:/ˌɡrænjuˈlɪfərəs/ | adj. | 含颗粒的；颗粒状的 | Bearing or consisting of granules; granular. | The granuliferous texture of the rock made it difficult to climb.  这种岩石的颗粒状质地使攀登变得困难。| The soil is granuliferous and drains well. 土质颗粒状，排水良好。 | 近义词: granular, grainy | 反义词: smooth, fine | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin granulum (small grain) + -ferous (bearing).
