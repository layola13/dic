mangetout:/ˈmɑːnʒˌtuː/ (UK), /ˌmɑːnʒˈtuː/ (US)| n. |雪豆，甜豆|A flat, edible pea pod, eaten whole before the seeds inside develop fully.  It is also known as a snow pea. |例句：We had stir-fried mangetouts with garlic for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了蒜蓉炒雪豆。

例句：The mangetouts in the salad were crisp and delicious. 沙拉里的雪豆脆嫩可口。|近义词：snow pea, sugar snap pea|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
