inofficiously:/ˌɪnəˈfɪʃəsli/
adjective
不正式地;不亲切地;不友好地;违反自然义务地
Not done in an official capacity; informally.  Lacking in kindness or friendliness; unkind.  Contrary to natural duty or affection.
He inofficiously left his family out of his will. 他不顾亲情，没有在遗嘱中提及家人。
The letter was written inofficiously, lacking the usual formalities. 这封信写得非正式，缺少通常的礼节。
近义词: unkindly, unofficially, informally, improperly
反义词: kindly, officially, formally, dutifully
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *inofficiosus, from in- "not" + officium "duty, service."
