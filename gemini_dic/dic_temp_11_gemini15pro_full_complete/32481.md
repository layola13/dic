chincona: /ˌtʃɪŋˈkoʊnə/ /ˌtʃɪnˈkəʊnə/ | n. | 金鸡纳树；金鸡纳皮；奎宁 | A South American tree whose bark yields quinine, an alkaloid used to treat malaria. |  The bark of the chincona tree is a source of quinine. 金鸡纳树的树皮是奎宁的来源。

The explorers searched for chincona in the dense jungle. 探险家们在茂密的丛林中寻找金鸡纳树。 | 近义词：cinchona | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Spanish *quina-quina*, from Quechua *kina-kina*, "bark of fever tree".
