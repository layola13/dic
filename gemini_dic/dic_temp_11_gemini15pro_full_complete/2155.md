add-ons:/ˈæd ɑːnz/ (US) /ˈæd ɒnz/ (UK)
n.|附加组件，附加装置，插件|Something added, especially to an existing or principal thing to improve or enhance it.|This car has several add-ons, including a sunroof and heated seats.|这辆车有几个附加装置，包括天窗和加热座椅。
add-ons:|n.|附加物，附件|Supplementary items that can be added to a basic product or service, often at extra cost.|The software comes with several useful add-ons.|这款软件附带几个有用的附加组件。
add-ons:|n.|扩展功能|Features or functionalities that are added to a base product to enhance its capabilities.|Many add-ons are available for this web browser.|这个网页浏览器有很多可用的扩展功能。
add-ons:|n.|附赠品|Additional items given free or at a reduced price with a purchase.|The add-ons with this game include a poster and a soundtrack CD.|这个游戏附赠一张海报和一张原声音轨CD。


近义词:accessories, supplements, extensions, attachments
反义词:essentials, basics
词性:noun
