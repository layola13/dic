insue:/ɪnˈsuː/
verb
发生，接着发生；（因…而）产生
to happen after something else, especially as a result of it
例句：After the heavy rains, floods ensued.  暴雨过后，发生了洪水。

例句：Legal battles ensued after the company's bankruptcy. 公司破产后，随之而来的是法律诉讼。

近义词：follow, result, arise, stem
反义词：precede, cause
词性：v.
