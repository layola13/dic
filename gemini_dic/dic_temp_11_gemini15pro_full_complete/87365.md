heroisms:/ˈhɛrəʊɪzəmz/ | n. |英雄主义；英勇行为|Great acts of bravery or courage, often displayed in difficult or dangerous situations.  The plural form of heroism. |例句：The firefighters' heroisms saved countless lives. 消防员的英雄事迹拯救了无数生命。

例句：History books are filled with stories of heroisms in wartime. 历史书中充满了战争时期英雄事迹的故事。|近义词：bravery, courage, valor, gallantry |反义词:cowardice, timidity|词性:名词
