cricketers:/ˈkrɪkɪtərz/| n.|板球运动员|Players of the game of cricket.|The cricketers walked onto the field. 板球运动员走上球场。|近义词：athletes, sportsmen|反义词:(None specific, possibly non-cricketers)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From cricket (the sport) + -er (suffix denoting a person who performs an action) + -s (plural suffix).  The word "cricket" itself has uncertain origins, possibly deriving from Middle Dutch "krick(-e)" (stick) or Old French "criquet" (small stick).
