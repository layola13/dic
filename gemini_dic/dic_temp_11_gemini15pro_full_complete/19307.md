bibber:/ˈbɪbər/| n.|酒鬼；沉溺者；常饮者|A person who drinks alcoholic beverages frequently and to excess.  A person who is devoted to something.|例句：He was a notorious bibber, known for his capacity for ale. 他是个臭名昭著的酒鬼，以其海量闻名。

例句：She is a bibber of knowledge, constantly reading and learning. 她是个求知若渴的人，不断地阅读和学习。|近义词：drinker, tippler, imbiber, sot, drunkard|反义词:teetotaler, abstainer|词性:noun
Etymology->
From Middle English *biber*, from Old English *bibbere* (“drinker”), equivalent to *bibban* (“to drink”) + *-ere*. Cognate with Dutch *bijber* (“bee-eater”), German *Biber* (“beaver”). More at bib, beaver.
