cleans:/kliːnz/
verb
打扫；清洁；清除；洗；擦；使…干净
makes something tidy or free from dirt
She cleans the house every Saturday. 她每个星期六打扫房子。
tidies, washes, polishes
dirties, messes
verb

PHRASE->
clean up:  整理，清理；收拾干净   Clean up your room! 把你的房间收拾干净！
clean out:  把…打扫干净；清除；倾空  I need to clean out the refrigerator. 我需要清理一下冰箱。
clean off:  擦掉；清除  Clean off the table before dinner. 晚饭前把桌子擦干净。
spring clean: 春季大扫除  We're having a spring clean this weekend.  我们这周末要进行春季大扫除。
dry clean:  干洗  This dress needs to be dry cleaned. 这件衣服需要干洗。
