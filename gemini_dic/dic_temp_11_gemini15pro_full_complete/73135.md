foraging:/ˈfɔːrɪdʒɪŋ/ (US), /ˈfɒrɪdʒɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |觅食，搜寻食物|The act of searching for food or provisions.|Squirrels are foraging for nuts. 松鼠正在觅食坚果. |Synonyms: searching, hunting, scavenging, rummaging |Antonyms: planting, cultivating, farming |noun

forage:/ˈfɔːrɪdʒ/ (US), /ˈfɒrɪdʒ/ (UK)| v. |觅食，搜寻|To search for food or provisions.|The pigs foraged in the woods for acorns. 猪在树林里觅食橡子. |Synonyms: search, hunt, scavenge, rummage |Antonyms: plant, cultivate, farm |verb
