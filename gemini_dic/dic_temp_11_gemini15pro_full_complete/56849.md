dorsals:/ˈdɔːrsəlz/| n. |背部；背鳍；背侧的|Relating to or situated on the back, or upper surface, of an animal or plant; a dorsal fin, feather, etc.|The bird displayed its brightly colored dorsals during the mating ritual.  这只鸟在求偶仪式中展示了它颜色鲜艳的背部（羽毛）。|Synonyms: back, upper, rear|Antonyms: ventral, underside|Noun

dorsal:/ˈdɔːrsəl/| adj. |背部的；背侧的|Relating to or situated on the back, or upper surface, of an animal or plant.|The dorsal fin of a shark helps it to maintain balance.  鲨鱼的背鳍帮助它保持平衡。|Synonyms: back, upper, rear|Antonyms: ventral, underside|Adjective
