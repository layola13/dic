bereaver:/bɪˈriːvər/| n. | 丧亲者；使人丧失者 |Someone who deprives another of something valued, especially through death.  A person who causes someone to experience bereavement. |The bereaver was comforted by friends after the loss of his wife.| 近义词：mourner, griever | 反义词: comforter, consoler | 词性: noun

bereave:/bɪˈriːv/| vt. | 使丧失；使痛失（亲属等）|  (of a person) be deprived of a loved one through a profound absence, especially due to the loved one's death. | The accident bereaved her of her husband. 事故使她失去了丈夫。| 近义词：deprive, rob, strip | 反义词: provide, give | 词性: verb
