alimentary:/ˌælɪˈmentəri/ | adj. | 营养的；食物的；消化道的 | Relating to nourishment or nutrition; of or relating to the digestive tract. |  The alimentary canal is responsible for digesting food. 消化道负责消化食物。| Synonym: nutritional, digestive, nutritive | Antonym: non-nutritive | adjective

alimentary:/ˌælɪˈmentəri/ | n. | 滋养品；食物 | Nourishment; food. (This usage is less common) | The doctor prescribed alimentary supplements to improve his health. 医生开了营养补充剂来改善他的健康。 | Synonym: nourishment, food, sustenance | Antonym: starvation, deprivation | noun
