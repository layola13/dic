marauder: /məˈrɔːdər/ (US), /məˈrɔːdə(r)/ (UK)| n. |掠夺者；抢劫者；海盗|A person who roams in search of things to steal or people to attack.|例句：The marauders pillaged the village, taking everything of value. 掠夺者洗劫了村庄，带走了所有值钱的东西。

例句：Bands of marauders terrorized the countryside after the war. 战后，成群的掠夺者恐吓了乡村。|近义词：raider, plunderer, looter, pirate, brigand |反义词:protector, defender|词性:noun
