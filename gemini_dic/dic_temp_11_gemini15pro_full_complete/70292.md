fidge:/fɪdʒ/
verb|烦躁不安，坐立不安|To make small movements, especially of the hands and feet, through nervousness or impatience.|She fidgeted with her pen during the meeting.  她在会议期间不停地摆弄她的笔。|synonyms: fidget, twitch, squirm|antonyms: relax, be still|v.

fidge:/fɪdʒ/
noun|烦躁不安的人；坐立不安|A person who fidgets a lot.|He's such a fidge, he can never sit still. 他真是个坐立不安的人，从来都静不下来。|synonyms: restless person, squirmer|antonyms: calm person|n.
