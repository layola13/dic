erythrolitmin:/ɪˌrɪθrəˈlɪtmɪn/
noun
石蕊精，红靛
A complex weak acid extracted from certain lichens, forming the essential coloring matter of litmus.
Litmus paper turns blue in the presence of a base, because erythrolitmin is blue in alkaline solutions.  石蕊试纸在碱存在的情况下变蓝，因为石蕊精在碱性溶液中呈蓝色。
Erythrolitmin is the active ingredient in litmus paper that causes the color change. 石蕊精是石蕊试纸中引起颜色变化的活性成分。
Synonym: litmus
Antonym: N/A
