intelligentsia: /ɪnˌtɛlɪˈdʒɛntsɪə/ | n. |知识分子，知识阶层| A group of intellectuals and educated people who form an educated elite, often seen as influencing or leading public opinion and cultural trends.  | The intelligentsia often criticized the government's policies. 知识分子经常批评政府的政策。|  The Russian intelligentsia played a key role in the revolution. 俄罗斯知识分子在革命中扮演了关键角色。| synonyms: intellectuals, educated elite, literati | antonyms: masses, common people | noun
