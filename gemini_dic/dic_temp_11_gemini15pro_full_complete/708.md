absentaneous:/ˌæbsənˈteɪniəs/
adjective
缺席的；心不在焉的
Relating to or characterized by absence, especially habitually.  Often implies negligence or lack of attention.
He had an absentaneous air, as if his mind were elsewhere. 他一副心不在焉的样子，好像心在别处。
absent-minded, distracted, inattentive, preoccupied
present, attentive, focused, engaged
词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *absentaneus, from Latin absens, absent- ‘being away’ (from ab- ‘from’ + esse ‘to be’).
