irresistibly:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbli/| adv.|不可抗拒地|in a way that is impossible to resist; overwhelmingly.|Her smile was irresistibly charming. 她的笑容魅力无限，令人无法抗拒。|The warm, freshly baked bread smelled irresistibly good.  温暖的、刚出炉的面包闻起来香极了，让人无法抗拒。|近义词：overpoweringly, compellingly, unavoidably|反义词:resistably, avoidably|词性:副词
