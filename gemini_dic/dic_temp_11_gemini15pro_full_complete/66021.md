examples:/ɪɡˈzæmpəlz/| n.|例子，实例|Something that is typical of or serves to illustrate a whole class. |This is one of many examples of his generosity. 这是他慷慨大方的众多例子之一。|synonyms: instances, illustrations, cases, samples|antonyms: exceptions, anomalies|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English example, from Old French example, from Latin exemplum (“sample, model”), from eximere (“to take out, remove”), from ex (“out of”) + emere (“to take, buy”).
