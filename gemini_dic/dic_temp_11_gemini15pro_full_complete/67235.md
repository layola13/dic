expropriators:/ɪkˌsproʊpriˈeɪtərz/
n.
征用者；没收者； expropriator的复数
Those who seize property, often without compensation, especially for public use.
The expropriators seized the land for the construction of a new highway.  征用者为了修建一条新的高速公路而征用了这块土地。
The government acted as expropriators, taking control of the privately owned oil wells. 政府充当了征用者的角色，控制了私有的油井。
confiscators, seizers
owners, proprietors
noun
