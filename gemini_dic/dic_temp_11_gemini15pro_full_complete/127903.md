neolithic:/ˌniːəˈlɪθɪk/| adj. n.|新石器时代的，新石器时代|Relating to or denoting the later part of the Stone Age, characterized by the use of ground and polished stone tools, agriculture, and often pottery.|The Neolithic period marked a significant shift in human history.|Synonym: Stone Age (late), New Stone Age|Antonym: Paleolithic|adjective, noun

neolithic:/ˌniːəˈlɪθɪk/| adj. n.|新石器时代的，新石器时代|The Neolithic era saw the development of farming.|Synonym: agrarian|Antonym: hunter-gatherer|adjective, noun
