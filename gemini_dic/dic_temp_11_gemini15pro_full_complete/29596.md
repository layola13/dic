catechumen:/ˈkætɪˌkjuːmən/| n.|皈依者，慕道者|A person receiving instruction in the principles of Christianity before baptism.|例句：The catechumen attended classes every week to learn about the faith.  这位慕道者每周都去上课学习信仰。

例句：The priest welcomed the catechumens into the church.  神父欢迎这些皈依者加入教会。|近义词：convert, proselyte, neophyte|反义词:apostate|noun
