mischoose:/ˌmɪsˈtʃuːz/
verb
错误地选择；选错
to choose wrongly or make a bad choice
He mischose his career path and ended up in a job he hated. 他选错了职业道路，最终从事了一份他讨厌的工作。
She mischose her words and offended her friend. 她用词不当，冒犯了她的朋友。
近义词: select wrongly, choose poorly, make a bad choice, pick the wrong one
反义词: choose correctly, choose wisely, make a good choice, pick the right one
词性: verb

词源:
"mis-" (Old English *mis-, from Proto-Germanic *missa-, meaning "wrongly, astray, amiss") + "choose" (Old English cēosan, from Proto-Germanic *keusaną, meaning "to test, taste, choose").
