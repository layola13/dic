nonperformers:/ˌnɑːnpərˈfɔːrmərz/ | n. |不履行者，表现不佳者| People or things that do not perform well or produce the desired results. |The company is looking to replace its nonperformers.  这家公司正在寻找替代表现不佳的员工。|  The garden contained several nonperformers that needed to be removed. 花园里有一些没有长好的植物需要移除。|Synonyms: underachievers, failures, laggards | Antonyms: high-performers, achievers, stars | noun
