aromatization:/əˌroʊmətaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|芳香化，芳构化|The process of converting a non-aromatic compound into an aromatic one.  This often involves the formation of a benzene ring or other aromatic system.|The aromatization of cyclohexane produces benzene.  环己烷的芳香化产生苯。| The aromatization of gasoline improves its octane rating.  汽油的芳香化提高了它的辛烷值。|aromatization, dehydrogenation|hydrogenation, reduction|noun
