dividend: /ˈdɪvɪdend/ (US), /ˈdɪvɪdənd/ (UK)| n. |红利；股息；回报；被除数|A sum of money paid regularly (typically annually) by a company to its shareholders out of its profits (or reserves).  The number being divided. |Example 1: The company announced a dividend of $2 per share. 公司宣布每股派发2美元的股息。

Example 2:  The dividend from our investment was substantial. 我们投资的回报相当可观。

Example 3: In the equation 10 / 2 = 5, 10 is the dividend. 在等式 10 / 2 = 5 中，10是被除数。|Synonym: payout, return, yield, bonus, quotient |Antonym:  investment, debit, divisor|noun
