macos: /ˌmæk oʊˈɛs/ | n. | 苹果操作系统 |  macOS (previously Mac OS X and later OS X) is a series of proprietary graphical operating systems developed and marketed by Apple Inc. since 2001. It is the primary operating system for Apple's Mac computers. |  I'm using the latest version of macOS. 我正在使用最新版本的 macOS。 | Example: I upgraded my macOS to the latest version.  我将我的 macOS 升级到了最新版本。 | synonym: Apple operating system, Mac OS | antonym: Windows, Linux | noun
