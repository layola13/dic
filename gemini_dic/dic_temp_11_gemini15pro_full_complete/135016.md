nonnegligibly:/ˌnɒnˈnɛɡlɪdʒɪbli/| adv.|不可忽略地，显著地|Not negligibly; to a degree that is significant or cannot be ignored.|The error rate increased nonnegligibly after the software update.  软件更新后，错误率显著增加。|significantly, noticeably, appreciably, materially|negligibly, insignificantly, trivially|adverb

Etymology->
From non- + negligibly.  Negligibly comes from negligible + -ly. Negligible derives from the Latin *neglegere*, meaning "to neglect," plus the suffix -ible, meaning "capable of being."
