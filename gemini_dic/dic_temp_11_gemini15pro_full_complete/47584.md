debasers:/dɪˈbeɪsərz/
|n.|贬低者，降低者，败坏者
|People who debase something, such as currency or a person's reputation. Those who lower the quality, value, or character of something.|
|The debasers of the language were severely criticized by academics. 这些败坏语言的人受到了学者们的严厉批评。|
|corrupters, degraders, depreciators|
|elevators, enhancers, improvers|
|noun|


debase:/dɪˈbeɪs/
|v.|贬低，降低，败坏
|To reduce the quality, value, or character of something.|
|They debased the currency by adding lead to the coins. 他们通过在硬币中添加铅来降低货币的价值。|
|corrupt, degrade, depreciate|
|elevate, enhance, improve|
|verb|
