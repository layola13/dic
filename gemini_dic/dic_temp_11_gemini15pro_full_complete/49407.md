deliveress: /dɪˈlɪvərɪs/

A woman who delivers something.

例句：The deliveress from the restaurant arrived with our food. 餐厅的女递送员带着我们的食物来了。

近义词：delivery woman, delivery driver, courier
反义词: recipient, receiver
