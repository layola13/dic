graphing:/ˈɡræfɪŋ/ /ˈɡrɑːfɪŋ/| n. |作图，绘制图表|The process of creating a visual representation of data, typically on a coordinate plane.|Example: Graphing the results of the experiment showed a clear trend.  绘制实验结果的图表显示出一个明显的趋势。|Example:  Graphing calculators are allowed in this exam.  这次考试允许使用绘图计算器。|近义词：plotting, charting, diagraming|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "analyzing numerically")|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "graph" + "-ing."  "Graph" originates from the Greek "graphein," meaning "to write" or "to draw."
