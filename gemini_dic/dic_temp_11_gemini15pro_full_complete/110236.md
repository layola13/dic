lifelikenesses:/ˈlaɪflaɪknəsɪz/
noun
逼真；逼真的肖像；栩栩如生的复制品
The quality of appearing very similar to a real person or thing.  A very realistic likeness or image, especially of a person.
The wax museum was full of astonishing lifelikenesses of famous people.  这个蜡像馆里满是名人的逼真蜡像。
The artist captured her lifelikeness perfectly in the portrait.  艺术家在肖像画中完美地捕捉到了她的神态。
synonyms:  resemblance, similarity, likeness, verisimilitude, image, portrait
antonyms:  dissimilarity, difference, unlikeness, caricature
