hebraistic:/hiːˈbreɪ.ɪstɪk/ /hɪˈbreɪ.ɪstɪk/
adjective
希伯来式的；受希伯来语影响的
Characteristic of or resembling the Hebrew language, idiom, or culture.
His writing style is quite Hebraistic, reflecting his deep study of the Old Testament. 他的写作风格深受希伯来语的影响，反映了他对旧约的深入研究。
Near Synonyms:  Hebraic, Semitic
Antonyms:  Hellenistic, Greco-Roman
词性: adjective
