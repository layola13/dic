dorsal: /ˈdɔːrsəl/ (UK), /ˈdɔːrsəl/ (US)| adj. n.|背部的，背侧的；背鳍；背侧| Relating to the back or posterior part of a body, organ, or structure in animals or plants.| The dorsal fin of a shark cut through the water.  鲨鱼的背鳍划破水面。| dorsal fin of a dolphin cuts through the water. 海豚的背鳍划破水面。 |近义词：back, posterior|反义词：ventral, anterior|词性: adjective, noun
