hilal:/hɪˈlɑːl/| n.|新月，月牙，娥眉月|The hilal marks the beginning of the Islamic month.|新月标志着伊斯兰月份的开始。|The sighting of the hilal determines the start of Ramadan.|看到新月决定了斋月的开始。|近义词：crescent, new moon|反义词:full moon|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic هلال (hilāl, “crescent”), from the root ه ل ل (h-l-l, “to appear, become visible”).
