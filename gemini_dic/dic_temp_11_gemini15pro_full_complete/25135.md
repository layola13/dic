buckeyes:/ˈbʌkaɪz/ | n. | 七叶树果实；七叶树；俄亥俄州人 | A small, shiny, dark brown nut with a light tan patch that is the seed of the Ohio buckeye tree, the state tree of Ohio. Also, a nickname for residents of Ohio. |  Ohio State Buckeyes are known for their football team. 俄亥俄州立七叶树队以其橄榄球队而闻名。
