bowyer:/ˈboʊjər/| n.|弓箭制造者，制弓人|A person who makes bows.  A craftsman who makes and repairs bows and arrows.|The bowyer carefully selected a piece of yew wood for his next project. 制弓人仔细地挑选了一块紫杉木，用于他的下一个项目。|Synonyms: fletcher (sometimes used interchangeably, but technically a fletcher makes arrows), bowmaker|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
