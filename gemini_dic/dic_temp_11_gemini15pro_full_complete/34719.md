click-through:/ˈklɪkˌθruː/| n. adj.|点击率，点击通过率|The act of clicking on a hyperlink, especially in an online advertisement, to visit another website.|The banner ad had a surprisingly high click-through rate.  这幅横幅广告的点击率出奇地高。|近义词：click rate, CTR|反义词:|n.

click-through:/ˈklɪkˌθruː/| adj.|点击通路的，点击式的|Relating to or involving the act of clicking on a hyperlink.|This is a click-through agreement; you must accept the terms to proceed. 这是一个点击式协议；您必须接受条款才能继续。|近义词:|反义词:|adj.
