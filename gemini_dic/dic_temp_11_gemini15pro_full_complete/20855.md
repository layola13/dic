blatantly:/ˈbleɪtəntli/ | adv. | 公然地，明目张胆地 | in an open and unashamed manner | He blatantly lied about his whereabouts. 他公然撒谎说他的行踪。| 近义词: openly, obviously, conspicuously, flagrantly | 反义词: subtly, secretly, discreetly | 词性: 副词

词源: From blatant + -ly.  Blatant itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from Old French *blatir "to bleat," suggesting a loud, insistent sound, or perhaps related to Scots blate "bashful, shy," with a sense shift to "obtrusive."
