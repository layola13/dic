antitheology:/ˌæntiθiˈɒlədʒi/ | n. |反神学；反对神学|A system of thought or doctrine that opposes or denies the existence or nature of God as traditionally conceived.  It often critiques traditional theological concepts and may propose alternative explanations for the universe and human existence.| He considered himself an adherent of antitheology, rejecting the traditional notions of a divine creator. 他认为自己是一个反神学拥护者，拒绝传统的造物主概念。|Synonyms: atheism, irreligion, secularism, nonbelief |Antonyms: theology, religion, theism, faith | Noun
