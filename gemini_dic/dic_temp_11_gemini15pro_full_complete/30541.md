cephalopod:/ˈsefələpɒd/ (UK) /ˈsefələpɑːd/ (US)| n. |头足类动物|A marine mollusc of the class Cephalopoda, such as an octopus or squid, characterized by bilateral body symmetry, a prominent head, and a modification of the foot into a muscular siphon and sucker-bearing tentacles. |例句：Octopuses and squids are cephalopods. 章鱼和鱿鱼是头足类动物。

|例句：The giant squid is one of the largest cephalopods.  大王乌贼是最大的头足类动物之一。

|近义词：mollusk, invertebrate |反义词: vertebrate |词性:noun
