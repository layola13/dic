apparitions:/ˌæpəˈrɪʃənz/ | n. | 幽灵；幻影；鬼魂；出现 |  The sudden appearance of something, typically a ghost or ghostlike figure.  A supernatural appearance of a person or thing, especially a ghost; a specter or phantom; an act of appearing or becoming visible unexpectedly. |  He claimed to have seen apparitions in the old house. 他声称在那栋老房子里看到过幽灵。| synonyms: ghost, phantom, specter, wraith, vision, illusion | antonyms: reality, corporeality | noun

apparitions:/ˌæpəˈrɪʃənz/ | n. | 出現；顯現 | The act of becoming visible; appearance. | The apparitions of angels are mentioned in the Bible. 圣经中提到了天使的显现。| synonyms: appearance, manifestation, materialization | antonyms: disappearance, vanishing | noun
