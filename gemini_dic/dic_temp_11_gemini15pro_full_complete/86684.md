Etymology->
From hellish + -ness.  "Hellish" comes from "hell" (Old English hel, Proto-Germanic *haljō "underworld") + "-ish" (Old English -isc, Germanic *-iskaz "belonging to, having the character of").  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting state, quality, or condition.
