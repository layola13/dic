词源:  From Middle English *leken, from Old Norse leka (“to leak, drip, trickle”), from Proto-Germanic *lekōną (“to drip, leak”). Cognate with Danish lække (“to leak”), Swedish läcka (“to leak”), Icelandic leka (“to leak”).
