factorable:/ˈfæktərəbl/| adj.|可分解的，可因式分解的|Able to be factored or resolved into factors.  This often refers to mathematical expressions, particularly polynomials, that can be expressed as a product of simpler expressions.|The quadratic equation x² - 4 is factorable into (x-2)(x+2). 二次方程式 x² - 4 可因式分解为 (x-2)(x+2).|Synonyms: divisible, resolvable|Antonyms: irreducible, prime|adjective
