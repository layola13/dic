agnostic:/æɡˈnɑːstɪk/ /əɡˈnɑːstɪk/ | n. adj. |不可知论者；不可知论的 |A person who believes that nothing is known or can be known of the existence or nature of God or of anything beyond material phenomena; a person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God.  Relating to agnosticism or an agnostic. |例句：He's an agnostic who doesn't believe in any kind of deity. 他是一个不可知论者，不相信任何神灵。

|例句：She took an agnostic stance on the issue, preferring to wait for more evidence.  对于这个问题，她采取了不可知论的立场，宁愿等待更多证据。|近义词：skeptic, doubter, unbeliever |反义词：believer, devotee, theist |词性: 名词, 形容词
