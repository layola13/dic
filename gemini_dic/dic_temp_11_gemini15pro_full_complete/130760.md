nonamphibiously:/ˌnɒnæmˈfɪbiəsli/
adverb
非两栖地; 不像两栖动物那样
in a way that is not characteristic of amphibians; on land.
The turtle moved nonamphibiously across the sandy beach.  这只海龟不像两栖动物那样，在沙滩上移动。
The researchers observed the behavior of the normally aquatic creature as it moved nonamphibiously. 研究人员观察了这种通常生活在水中的生物在陆地上移动的行为。
近义词: terrestrially
反义词: amphibiously
词性: adverb
