gymkhanas:/dʒɪmˈkɑːnəz/
|n.|体育比赛，趣味运动会|A gymkhana is a sporting event comprising a series of contests, especially for horses and other animals, or vehicles.|The annual gymkhana is a highlight of the village calendar.  村里的年度趣味运动会是日历上的亮点。|The local riding school held a gymkhana for the children.  当地的骑马学校为孩子们举办了一场体育比赛。|Synonyms: competition, contest, meet, tournament|Antonyms: N/A
