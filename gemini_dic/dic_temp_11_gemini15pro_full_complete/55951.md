djerrid:/ˈdʒɛrɪd/| n.|标枪；骑马投枪游戏|A North African sport played on horseback, involving throwing a wooden javelin.  It also refers to the javelin itself.|The Berbers of North Africa are renowned for their skill in djerrid. 北非的柏柏尔人以其精湛的骑马投枪技术而闻名。

The djerrid tournament is a major cultural event.  骑马投枪比赛是一项重要的文化活动。|近义词：javelin, lance|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Arabic *jarīd* (جريد) meaning "palm-leaf rib," referring to the material from which the javelin was originally made.
