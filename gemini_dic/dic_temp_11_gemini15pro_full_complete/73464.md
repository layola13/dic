foreordinate:/fɔrˈɔːrdɪneɪt/
verb
预先注定；预定
to decree or ordain beforehand; predestine
例句：
Fate had foreordained their meeting.
命运预先注定他们的相遇。

Their victory was foreordained.
他们的胜利是命中注定的。

近义词：predestine, preordain, predetermine
反义词：random, chance, accidental
词性：verb


foreordained:/fɔrˈɔːrdeɪnd/
adjective
预先注定的
previously ordained or decreed by fate or a deity
例句：
He believed their meeting was foreordained.
他相信他们的相遇是命中注定的。

It seemed a foreordained conclusion.
这似乎是一个预先注定的结论。

近义词：predestined, preordained, predetermined
反义词：random, chance, accidental
词性：adjective
