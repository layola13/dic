distrainor:/dɪˈstreɪnər/| n.|扣押人，扣押财产者|A person who distrains (seizes property) to enforce the payment of a debt or obligation.|The distrainor seized the farmer's tractor for unpaid rent.|近义词：seizer, confiscator|反义词: debtor, payer|词性:noun

Etymology: From Anglo-French *destreinour, from destreindre "to constrain, distress," from Latin distringere.
