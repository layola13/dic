dining:/ˈdaɪnɪŋ/| n. adj.|用餐，进餐;餐厅|The act of eating dinner; a room used for dining.|We had a pleasant dining experience at the new restaurant. 我们在新餐厅享用了一顿愉快的晚餐。|近义词：eating, meal, repast|反义词：fasting, starving|noun, adjective


dining:/ˈdaɪnɪŋ/| adj.|用餐的|Used for or relating to dining.|The dining room table is beautifully set.  餐厅的桌子布置得很漂亮。|近义词：eating|反义词：N/A|adjective


词源:  From the Old French verb *disner* (modern French *dîner*), meaning "to dine," ultimately from the Vulgar Latin *disjūnāre*, meaning "to break one's fast," from the Latin prefix *dis-* meaning "apart" and *jējūnus* meaning "fasting."
