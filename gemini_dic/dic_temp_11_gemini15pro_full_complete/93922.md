immunized:/ˈɪmjʊnaɪzd/ | adj. |免疫的，已接种疫苗的 |Made resistant to infection, typically by inoculation. | Example:  The entire population has been immunized against polio.  全体人口都接种了脊髓灰质炎疫苗。| Synonyms: vaccinated, inoculated, protected | Antonyms: susceptible, vulnerable, unprotected | Word Type: Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From  "immunize" + "-ed".  "Immunize" comes from Late Latin "immunis" (exempt, free, not paying a share), from "in-" (not) + "munis" (performing services, obligated).
