guilts:/ɡɪlts/| n.|罪恶感，内疚|A feeling of having done wrong or failed in an obligation.|Overcome with guilts, she confessed to the crime.  被罪恶感淹没，她坦白了罪行。|近义词：remorse, shame, regret|反义词：pride, innocence|词性:名词

guilt:/ɡɪlt/| n.|罪行，内疚|The fact of having committed a specified or implied offense or crime.|He admitted his guilt. 他承认了自己的罪行。|近义词：culpability, responsibility|反义词：innocence|词性:名词
