arnica:/ˈɑːrnɪkə/ | n. | 山金车，山金车属植物 | A European flowering plant ( *Arnica montana* ) of the daisy family, whose dried flower heads and rhizomes are used in herbal medicine, especially in liniments or ointments to treat bruises and sprains.|  Arnica is often used to treat bruises. 山金车常用于治疗瘀伤。| Arnica cream can help with muscle soreness. 山金车膏可以缓解肌肉酸痛。| synonyms: leopard's bane, wolf's bane | antonyms: N/A | noun
