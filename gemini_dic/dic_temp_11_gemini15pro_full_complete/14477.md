avar:/ˈævər/| n.| 贪婪的人;守财奴|A greedy, miserly person; one who hoards wealth. |例句：The old miser was a notorious avar.  那个老守财奴是个臭名昭著的贪婪鬼。

例句：He was known throughout the town as an avar, unwilling to part with a single penny. 他在镇上是出了名的守财奴，一个子儿也不愿意花。|近义词：miser, skinflint, niggard, churl|反义词：philanthropist, altruist|词性: noun
