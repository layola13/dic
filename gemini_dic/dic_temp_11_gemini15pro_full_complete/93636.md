imboss:/ɪmˈbɒs/ /ɪmˈbɑːs/| v. n.|压印，凸印；使…浮雕；装饰|To decorate (a surface) with raised ornament. To carve, mold, or stamp a design on (a surface) so that it stands out in relief.|The company logo was embossed on the leather wallet. 公司的标志被压印在皮革钱包上.|近义词：engrave, stamp, carve, mold|反义词:flatten, smooth|verb

imboss:/ɪmˈbɒs/ /ɪmˈbɑːs/| n.|凸印，浮雕图案|A raised design or ornament.|The emboss on the stationery gave it a touch of elegance. 信纸上的浮雕图案使它显得优雅.|近义词：relief, ornamentation|反义词:indentation|noun
