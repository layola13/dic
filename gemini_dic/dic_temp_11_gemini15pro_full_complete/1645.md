acis:/ˈeɪsɪs/| n.|轴；坐标轴|A straight line through the center of an object, around which the object rotates or is symmetrically arranged.  It represents a reference line for measurement or alignment. |The Earth rotates on its acis. 地球绕其轴自转。| synonym: axis, axle | antonym: periphery, edge | noun
