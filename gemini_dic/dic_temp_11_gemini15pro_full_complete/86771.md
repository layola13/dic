helter-skelter:/ˈheltər ˈskeltər/| adv. adj. n.|慌乱地，杂乱的；混乱的；匆忙的|In a confused and disorderly way; hurriedly.|例句：People were running helter-skelter trying to escape the fire. 人们慌乱地四处奔逃，试图逃离火灾。|例句：The toys were scattered helter-skelter across the floor. 玩具散乱地扔在地板上。|近义词：haphazardly, pell-mell, disorderly|反义词：orderly, methodically|词性:副词，形容词，名词
词源: From the Middle English *helter-to-skelter*, perhaps from *helter* (to hang loosely) and *skelter* (to hasten, rush).  The original sense was probably of hanging loosely and flapping about while rushing.
