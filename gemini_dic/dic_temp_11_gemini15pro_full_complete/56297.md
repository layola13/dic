doggone:/dɒˈɡɒn/ (UK) /dɑːˈɡɔːn/ (US)| adj. interj.| 讨厌的；该死的；（表示惊讶、恼怒等）|A mild oath, used as a euphemism for "goddamn". It can function as an adjective or an interjection.|

例句：
That doggone dog keeps barking!
那该死的狗一直叫个不停！

It's doggone cold out here.
外面真冷得要命。


近义词：darned, blasted, confounded|
反义词: blessed, fortunate|
词性: adjective, interjection|
