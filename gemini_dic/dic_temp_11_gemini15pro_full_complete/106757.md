laager:/ˈlɑːɡər/ (US), /ˈlɑːɡə(r)/ (UK)| n.|营地，防御阵地|A camp or encampment, especially of circled wagons for defense.|The settlers formed a laager to protect themselves from attack. 定居者们组成防御阵地来保护自己免受攻击。|Synonyms: encampment, camp, stockade, corral|Antonyms: dispersal, scattering|noun

laager:/ˈlɑːɡər/ (US), /ˈlɑːɡə(r)/ (UK)| v.|扎营，组成防御阵地|To form or encamp in a laager.|The pioneers laagered their wagons at nightfall.  拓荒者们在夜幕降临的时候用篷车扎营。|Synonyms: encamp, camp, fortify|Antonyms: decamp, disperse|verb
