boydekin:/ˈbɔɪdəkɪn/| n. |小男孩，男孩儿|A diminutive or affectionate term for a boy, often used in Scotland or northern England.  It carries a sense of familiarity and sometimes slight condescension, similar to "laddie".|例句：The wee boydekin was playing in the garden. 小男孩在花园里玩耍。

例句：Look at that cheeky boydekin climbing the tree! 看看那个厚脸皮的小男孩爬树！|近义词：lad, laddie, boy, youngster|反义词:girl, lass|词性:noun
