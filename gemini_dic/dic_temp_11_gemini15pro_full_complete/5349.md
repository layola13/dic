allumette:/ˌæləˈmɛt/ | n. | 火柴, 小木棍 | A short, thin stick of wood tipped with a flammable mixture that ignites when struck against a rough surface. | He lit the candle with an allumette. 他用一根火柴点燃了蜡烛。 | synonym: match, lighter | antonym: extinguisher | noun
