overlearnednesses:/ˌoʊvərˈlɜːrndnɪsɪz/
n.
过度学习
The state of having learned something so thoroughly that it is automatic and difficult to change.
His overlearnedness of the piano piece made it difficult for him to adapt to a new interpretation.  他过度学习了那首钢琴曲，以至于很难适应新的诠释。
近义词：automaticity, ingrained habits
反义词：unfamiliarity, inexperience
词性：noun

overlearnedness:/ˌoʊvərˈlɜːrndnɪs/
n.

Her overlearnedness in typing allowed her to transcribe notes quickly and efficiently.  她对打字的过度学习使她能够快速有效地记录笔记。
近义词：rote learning, mastery
反义词：flexibility, adaptability
词性：noun


词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "learned" (past participle of "learn") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
