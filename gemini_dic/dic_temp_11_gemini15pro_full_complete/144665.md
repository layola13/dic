outwring:/ˌaʊtˈrɪŋ/

|v.|胜过，超过|To surpass or excel in wringing or extracting.|She outwrung her competitors in the competition. 她在比赛中彻底击败了她的竞争对手。|
近义词:extract, wring out, defeat, conquer, vanquish
反义词:lose, yield, surrender
词性:动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "wring" (verb meaning to squeeze or twist)
