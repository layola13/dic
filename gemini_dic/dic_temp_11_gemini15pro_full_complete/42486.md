countergage:/ˈkaʊntərɡeɪdʒ/ | n. |  反制措施；对抗策略；对策 | A countergage is an action taken to offset or counteract another action, especially in a competitive situation. | Example: The company launched a new advertising campaign as a countergage to its competitor's aggressive pricing strategy.  该公司发起了一场新的广告活动，以应对其竞争对手激进的定价策略。

Example:  His countergage to their accusations was a calm and reasoned explanation. 他对他们指控的反驳是冷静而合理的解释。 | Synonym: countermeasure, countermove, retaliation, riposte  | Antonym:  compliance, submission | noun
