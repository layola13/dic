baroscope:/ˈbærəˌskoʊp/| n.|气压计，气压指示器|An instrument that indicates variations in atmospheric pressure, often used to predict weather changes. It can also be a device for showing the weight or pressure of the air.| The baroscope registered a sudden drop in pressure, signaling the approaching storm. 气压计记录到压力突然下降，预示着暴风雨即将来临。|synonyms: barometer, aneroid barometer|antonyms: N/A|noun
