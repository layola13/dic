formalist:/ˈfɔːrməlɪst/ | n. adj. | 形式主义者；形式主义的 | Someone who adheres rigidly to prescribed forms or conventions, especially in art or literature. | Example: The artist was criticized by formalists for his unconventional use of color.  这位艺术家因其对色彩的非传统运用而受到形式主义者的批评。| Synonyms: conventionalist, traditionalist, purist | Antonyms: innovator, experimentalist, avant-gardist | noun

formalist:/ˈfɔːrməlɪst/ | adj. | 形式主义的 | Characterized by strict adherence to prescribed forms or conventions, especially in art or literature. | Example:  Her formalist approach to poetry emphasized rhyme and meter over emotional expression. 她对诗歌的形式主义方法强调韵律和格律，而不是情感表达。 | Synonyms: conventional, traditional, rigid | Antonyms: innovative, experimental, avant-garde | adjective
