crabbiness:/ˈkræbinəs/| n.|暴躁, 坏脾气|The state of being crabby; irritability.  A feeling of being cross and irritable.|His crabbiness this morning was due to lack of sleep. 他今天早上的暴躁是因为缺乏睡眠。|近义词：grumpiness, irritability, ill-temper, surliness|反义词：good humor, cheerfulness, pleasantness|词性:noun
词源:From crabbed + -iness.  Ultimately from the crab, referring to its perceived irritable nature.
