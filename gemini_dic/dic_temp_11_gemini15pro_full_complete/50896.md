desertion:/dɪˈzɜːʃən/ (US) /dɪˈzɜːʃn/ (UK)| n.|遗弃，抛弃；开小差；逃亡|The act of abandoning a person, cause, or place, especially in a way considered disloyal or irresponsible. |例句：His desertion of his family left them in dire straits. 他抛弃家人，使他们陷入困境。

例句：The soldier was court-martialed for desertion.  这名士兵因逃亡而被送上军事法庭。|近义词：abandonment, forsaking, relinquishment|反义词：loyalty, fidelity|词性:noun
