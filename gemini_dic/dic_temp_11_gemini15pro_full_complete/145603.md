overconscientiously: /ˌoʊvərˌkɑːnʃiˈɛnʃəsli/ | adv. | 过分认真地，过分谨慎地 |  In an excessively conscientious manner; with undue attention to detail or correctness.  | She completed the task overconscientiously, checking every tiny detail multiple times. 她过分认真地完成了任务，反复检查每一个小细节。| meticulously, scrupulously, excessively carefully | carelessly, negligently | adverb

词源:
From over- + conscientiously, from conscientious + -ly.  Conscientious from Latin conscientia "knowledge within oneself," from com- "with," + sciens, present participle of scire "to know."
