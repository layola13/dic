counterplayer:/ˈkaʊntərˌpleɪər/| n.| 对手; 对弈者 |A person who plays against another, especially in a game or sport; an opponent.|例句：He finally defeated his counterplayer in the chess tournament. 他最终在国际象棋比赛中击败了他的对手。|例句：She studied her counterplayer's moves carefully before making her own. 她仔细研究了对手的动作才开始下棋。|近义词：opponent, adversary, rival, competitor|反义词:ally, teammate, partner|noun
