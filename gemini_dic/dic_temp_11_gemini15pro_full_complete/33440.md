churlish:/ˈtʃɜːrlɪʃ/| adj.|粗鲁的，无礼的，脾气坏的|Rude and bad-tempered; surly.|例句：He dismissed her with a churlish grunt.  他粗暴地咕哝了一声把她打发走了。|例句：It would be churlish to refuse such a generous offer. 拒绝如此慷慨的提议会显得粗鲁无礼。|近义词：rude, impolite, surly, grumpy, ill-mannered, boorish|反义词：polite, courteous, gracious, affable|词性:形容词

churlishness:/ˈtʃɜːrlɪʃnəs/| n.|粗鲁，无礼，坏脾气|The quality of being rude, bad-tempered, or surly.|例句：His churlishness made it difficult to work with him. 他的粗鲁使得很难与他共事。|例句：She apologized for her churlishness earlier. 她为她先前的无礼道歉。|近义词：rudeness, impoliteness, surliness, grumpiness|反义词：politeness, courtesy, graciousness|词性:名词
