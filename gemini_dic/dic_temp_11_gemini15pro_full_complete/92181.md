hypersplenism:/ˌhaɪpərˈspliːnɪzəm/
n.
脾亢；脾功能亢进
Overactivity of the spleen, resulting in increased destruction of red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets.
The patient's low blood counts suggested hypersplenism.  病人的低血细胞计数表明他患有脾亢。
近义词:splenomegaly (though not always synonymous, as an enlarged spleen doesn't always lead to hypersplenism)
反义词:hyposplenism (though a direct opposite in terms of function, it is not a clinically used term as commonly as hypersplenism)
词性:noun
