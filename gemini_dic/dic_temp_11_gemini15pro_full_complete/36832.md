colonising: /ˈkɒlənaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzɪŋ/ (US)
| adj. |殖民的，拓殖的|Relating to the act of colonising.|The colonising forces established a new settlement. 殖民军队建立了一个新的定居点。|Synonyms: imperial, expansionist |Antonyms: indigenous, native|Adjective
