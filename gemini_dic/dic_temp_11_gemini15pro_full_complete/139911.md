odeling: /ˈjoʊdlɪŋ/ (also /ˈjɔːdlɪŋ/) | n. | ヨーデル、ヨーデル唱法 | A style of singing characterized by rapid changes in pitch between the chest register and falsetto, often associated with the Alps. |  He entertained the tourists with his impressive yodeling. 他用他令人印象深刻的ヨーデル唱法招待游客。 | synonyms: warbling, trilling | antonyms: monotone, droning | noun
