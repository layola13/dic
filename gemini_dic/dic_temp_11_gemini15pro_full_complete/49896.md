demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/| n.|异议，反对|An objection or hesitation.|Her demurs about the plan were ignored. 她对该计划的反对意见被忽视了。|Without demur, he accepted the assignment. 他毫不犹豫地接受了任务。|近义词：objection, protest, hesitation|反义词：acceptance, agreement, consent|noun
