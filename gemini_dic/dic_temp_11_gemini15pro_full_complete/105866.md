kithe:/kaɪð/
|v.|显露；表明；宣布；（苏格兰方言）使…众所周知|To make known, reveal, or show clearly.|He kithed his true feelings in his letter. 他在信中表明了他的真实感受。|近义词:reveal, disclose, show, manifest, declare|反义词:conceal, hide|词性:动词
词源:From Middle English *kythen, from Old English cȳðan (“to make known, show, manifest, declare, inform, announce, preach”), from Proto-Germanic *kunþijaną (“to make known”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵn̥h₂-tó- (“known”). Cognate with Old Frisian kytha (“to make known”), Old Saxon kūthian (“to make known”), Old High German chundōn (“to make known”), Old Norse kynna (“to make known, bear witness to”), Gothic (kunþjan, “to make known”). Related to uncouth.
