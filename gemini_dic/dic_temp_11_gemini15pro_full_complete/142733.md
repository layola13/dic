otolith:/ˈoʊtəlɪθ/ /ˈɒtəlɪθ/ | n. | 耳石 | A small calcareous concretion in the inner ear of vertebrates, involved in sensing gravity and acceleration. |  Fish use otoliths to sense their orientation in the water. 鱼利用耳石来感知它们在水中的方向。

The scientists studied the otoliths of the fish to determine its age. 科学家们研究了鱼的耳石来确定它的年龄。 | 近义词: statoconium | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
