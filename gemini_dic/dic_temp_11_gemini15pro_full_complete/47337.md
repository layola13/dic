de-shelled:/diːˈʃeld/
adjective
去壳的，脱壳的
Removed from its shell.
The de-shelled peanuts were ready for roasting. 去壳的花生已经准备好烘烤了。
近义词: shelled, husked
反义词: unshelled, in-shell
词性: adjective
