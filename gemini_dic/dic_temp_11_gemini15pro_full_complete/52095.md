diameter:/daɪˈæmɪtər/ | n. | 直径 | A straight line passing from side to side through the center of a body or figure, especially a circle or sphere. | The diameter of the circle is 10 cm.  圆的直径是10厘米。| synonyms: width, breadth | antonyms: radius | noun
