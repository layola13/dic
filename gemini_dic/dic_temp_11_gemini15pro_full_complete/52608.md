digestibility:/ˌdaɪˌdʒɛstɪˈbɪləti/
n.|消化率，可消化性|The digestibility of food varies depending on its composition.  食物的消化率因其成分而异。|
The experiment measured the digestibility of different types of protein.  该实验测量了不同类型蛋白质的消化率。|
近义词：assimilability, absorbability
反义词：indigestibility
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From digest + -ibility.  Digest comes from Latin digestus, past participle of digerere "to separate, distribute, arrange, digest," from dis- "apart" + gerere "to carry, bear."
