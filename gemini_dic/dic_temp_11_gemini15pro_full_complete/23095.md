bountifully:/ˈbaʊntɪfəli/| adv.|丰富地，慷慨地|in a bountiful way; generously.|The land bountifully provided for its people. 这片土地为其人民提供了丰富的资源。|例句：
The harvest was bountifully this year. 今年的收成很丰富。
She gave bountifully to the charity. 她慷慨地向慈善机构捐款。
|近义词：abundantly, copiously, generously, lavishly|反义词：sparsely, meagerly, stingily|词性:副词

词源:
From Middle English *bountifuli, from bountiful +‎ -ly.  Bountiful itself comes from  Middle English bountevous, from Anglo-Norman *bountive, from Old French bontivous (“bountiful, generous”).  Ultimately from Latin bonitas (“goodness”).
