imprimatur:/ˌɪmprɪˈmeɪtər/ | n. | 批准;许可;认可 | An official license to print or publish a book, especially one granted by the Roman Catholic Church.  A sign of approval or sanction. | The book received the imprimatur of the university press. 这本书获得了大学出版社的出版许可。| Synonyms: sanction, authorization, approval, endorsement, license, permission | Antonyms: disapproval, prohibition, ban, veto | noun
