metasilicic:/ˌmɛtəsɪˈlɪsɪk/| adj.|偏硅酸的|Relating to or containing metasilicic acid, which has the formula H2SiO3.  It's a term used in chemistry, particularly in mineralogy and geochemistry, to describe substances derived from or containing this acid.|Metasilicic acid is a key component in the formation of certain silicate minerals. 偏硅酸是某些硅酸盐矿物形成的关键成分。| The metasilicic rocks were analyzed for their mineral content. 对偏硅酸岩石进行了矿物含量分析。|silicic, silicate|non-silicic|adjective
