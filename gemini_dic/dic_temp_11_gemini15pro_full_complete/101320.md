inyanga:/ɪˈnjɑːŋɡə/ | n. | 伊南亚人；传统治疗师 | A traditional healer or diviner in Southern Africa, particularly among Nguni-speaking peoples.  | Example 1: The inyanga prepared a herbal remedy for the sick child. 伊南亚人给生病的孩子准备了草药。

Example 2:  People consulted the inyanga for advice on various matters. 人们向伊南亚人咨询各种事情。 | Synonym:  herbalist, diviner, healer, shaman | Antonym: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) doctor (in the Western medical sense) | Noun
