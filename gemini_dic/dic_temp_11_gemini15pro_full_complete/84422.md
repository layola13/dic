Hallstatt: /ˈhɔːlʃtæt/ (US) /ˈhælʃtæt/ (UK)| n. |哈尔施塔特|A village in Austria, known for its picturesque setting on Lake Hallstatt and its salt mine. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.| Tourists flock to Hallstatt for its stunning scenery. 游客们涌向哈尔施塔特欣赏其迷人的景色。

Hallstatt is famous for its centuries-old salt mine. 哈尔施塔特以其拥有数百年历史的盐矿而闻名。|近义词: village, town |反义词: city, metropolis |词性: noun
