noneligibly:/ˌnɒnˈɛlɪdʒɪbli/ | adv. |没有资格地，不合格地 | In a way that makes someone or something not eligible. | He was noneligibly discharged from the army due to a medical condition. 他因健康状况不合格地从军队退役。|  He voted noneligibly in the election, as he wasn't a registered voter. 他在选举中投了无效票，因为他不是登记选民。 | 近义词:  disqualifiedly, improperly | 反义词: eligibly, legitimately | 词性: 副词

词源:  noneligible (adjective) + -ly (adverb suffix).  "Noneligible" comes from "non-" (not) + "eligible" (qualified). "Eligible" derives from the Old French "eligable," ultimately from the Latin "eligere" (to choose).
