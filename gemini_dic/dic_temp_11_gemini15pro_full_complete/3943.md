agro-industry:/ˈæɡroʊˌɪndəstri/| n. |农业工业; 农产工业|The agro-industry plays a vital role in the country's economy. 农业工业在该国经济中扮演着至关重要的角色。| Agro-industry encompasses all activities involved in the production, processing, and distribution of agricultural products. 农业工业包括所有涉及农产品生产、加工和分配的活动。|近义词：agribusiness, agricultural industry|反义词: 无|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From *agro-* (combining form of *agri-* meaning "field, land," from Latin *ager*) + *industry*.
