boastless: /ˈboʊstləs/| adj.|不夸耀的，谦虚的|Without boasting; unassuming; modest.  Characterized by a lack of boasting or ostentation. |She lived a quiet, boastless life, content with her accomplishments but never feeling the need to shout them from the rooftops. 她过着平静、不夸耀的生活，对自己的成就感到满意，但从不觉得有必要大声宣扬。|近义词: humble, modest, unassuming, unpretentious|反义词: boastful, arrogant, conceited|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From boast + -less.  "Boast" comes from Middle English *bosten, probably from Anglo-Norman boster. "-less" is a common suffix meaning "without."
