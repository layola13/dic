for-the-birds:/ˌfɔːr ðə ˈbɜːrdz/ | adj. | 无聊的；无价值的；愚蠢的 | Uninteresting, worthless, or silly; something that is only of interest to birds and therefore not to humans. | That movie was for the birds. 那部电影真是无聊透顶。|  The lecture was so technical, it was for the birds.  那个讲座太专业了，听得我昏昏欲睡。 | 近义词: worthless, trivial, unimportant, inane, silly | 反义词: interesting, worthwhile, important, significant | 词性: 形容词

词源:
This idiom originated in the early 20th century, likely from the observation that birds often peck at discarded or insignificant things like horse droppings.  This led to the association of "for the birds" with something worthless or unimportant.
