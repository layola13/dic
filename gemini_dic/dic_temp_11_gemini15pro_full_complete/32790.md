choiceness:/ˈtʃɔɪsnɪs/| n.|精选；优良；精致|The choiceness of the ingredients contributed to the deliciousness of the meal.  食材的精选使得这顿饭非常美味。|近义词：excellence, quality, refinement, selectness|反义词：inferiority, poorness, crudeness|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *choisnes, from Old French choisine (“choice”), from choisir (“to choose”). Ultimately from Frankish *kausjan (“to test, choose, taste”), from Proto-Germanic *kausjaną (“to choose, test”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵews- (“to taste, choose”).
