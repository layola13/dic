ably:/ˈeɪbli/| adv.|能干地，巧妙地|In a competent and skillful manner; capably.|She handled the situation ably. 她巧妙地处理了这种情况。| 近义词: capably, competently, skillfully, adeptly, proficiently | 反义词: ineptly, clumsily, incompetently, poorly |词性:副词

词源:
From Middle English *habili, from Old French habile, from Latin habilis (“easy to handle, manageable, apt, fit, suitable”), from habēre (“to have, hold”).  The suffix -ly transforms the adjective "able" into the adverb "ably."
