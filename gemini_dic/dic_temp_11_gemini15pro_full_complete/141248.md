opacity: /oʊˈpæsɪti/ | n. |不透明度，浑浊度 |The quality of being opaque to radiant energy, especially light.  The degree to which something reduces the passage of light. |The opacity of the fog made driving difficult. 浓雾使驾驶困难。|Synonyms: opaqueness, cloudiness, nontransparency |Antonyms: transparency, clarity, translucence | noun
