gochi: /ˈɡoʊtʃiː/ | n. | 盛宴，美食，（尤指日本料理）|  A Japanese expression of gratitude for a delicious meal, similar to "bon appétit" or "Itadakimasu" before eating and "Gochisousama deshita" after eating. It can also refer to a feast or delicious food, especially Japanese cuisine. | Example 1:  After a wonderful sushi dinner, she said "Gochi!" to the chef.  在享用完一顿美味的寿司晚餐后，她对厨师说“Gochi！”。 Example 2:  He prepared a real gochi for his friends.  他为朋友们准备了一顿真正的盛宴。| Near Synonyms: feast, banquet, delicious food | Antonyms: meager meal, simple fare | noun
