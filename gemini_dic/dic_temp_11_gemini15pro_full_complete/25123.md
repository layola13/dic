buckboard:/ˈbʌkˌbɔːrd/
n.|四轮马车；无弹簧马车|A simple, open, horse-drawn carriage consisting of a long platform attached directly to the axles.|The pioneers crossed the plains in a buckboard.|近义词：wagon, carriage|反义词:car, automobile|词性:名词
Etymology: From buck + board.  "Buck" in this context likely refers to a frame or chassis, possibly influenced by the bucking motion experienced in such a springless vehicle. "Board" refers to the flat platform that forms the body of the carriage.
