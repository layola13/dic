nonalignments:/ˌnɒnəˈlaɪnmənts/ | n. |不结盟，不联盟|The state of not being formally aligned with or committed to any particular group, nation, or cause.  Absence of alignment or alliance. |例句：The country pursued a policy of nonalignment during the Cold War.  这个国家在冷战期间奉行不结盟政策。

例句：Many newly independent nations opted for nonalignment in the face of superpower rivalry.  面对超级大国的竞争，许多新独立的国家选择了不结盟。|近义词：neutrality, non-involvement, independence |反义词: alliance, alignment |词性:名词
