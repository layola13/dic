fictitiously: /fɪkˈtɪʃəsli/
adverb
虚构地，杜撰地
in a fictitious manner; not based on fact.
He fictitiously described himself as a doctor. 他虚构地把自己描述成一个医生。
The characters in the novel are entirely fictitious. 小说中的人物完全是虚构的。
近义词: imaginarily, falsely, untruly, spuriously
反义词: truly, factually, genuinely, really
词性: adverb

Etymology:  From Late Latin *fictitiosus, from Latin ficticius "artificial, counterfeit," from fingere "to shape, form, invent, feign."
