noninfectious:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɛkʃəs/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈfɛkʃəs/| adj.|非传染性的，非感染性的|Not capable of being transmitted through infection; not contagious.|The common cold is infectious, but arthritis is noninfectious.|近义词:noncommunicable, noncontagious|反义词:infectious, contagious|词性:形容词

noninfectious:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɛkʃəs/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈfɛkʃəs/| n.|非传染病|A disease that is not transmissible by infection.|Arthritis is a common noninfectious disease.|近义词:noncommunicable disease|反义词:infectious disease|词性:名词 (rarely used)
