abo's:/ˈæboʊz/
n.
土著居民（尤指澳大利亚土著居民）
(Abbreviation of) aborigines, especially of Australia.
例句：The abo's have lived on this land for thousands of years.  这些土著居民在这片土地上生活了数千年。

例句：The government is working to improve the living conditions of the abo's. 政府正在努力改善土著居民的生活条件。

近义词：Aborigines, indigenous people
反义词: None (No direct antonym for a group of people)
词性: Noun (Abbreviation)
