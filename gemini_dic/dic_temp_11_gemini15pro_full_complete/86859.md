hemigamous:/hɛˈmɪɡəməs/ | adj. | 半婚的 |  Relating to or characterized by hemigamy, a marriage system in which one sex (usually men) is permitted to have more than one spouse while the other sex is not. |  In some cultures, hemigamous practices are accepted. 在某些文化中，半婚制是被接受的。|  Polygamous, polyandrous | Monogamous | adjective
