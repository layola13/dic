backlink:/ˈbækˌlɪŋk/| n.|反向链接；导入链接|A backlink is a link from one website to another. Backlinks are also known as inbound links or incoming links.|Example: High-quality backlinks can improve a website's search engine ranking.  高质量的反向链接可以提升网站的搜索引擎排名。

Example:  The website earned several backlinks from reputable sources. 该网站从信誉良好的来源获得了几个反向链接。|近义词：inbound link, incoming link|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
