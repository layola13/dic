irresistableness:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbəlnəs/
n.
不可抗拒性；诱惑力
The irresistibleness of the chocolate cake made me break my diet.
巧克力蛋糕的诱惑力让我破了戒。
The irresistibleness of her smile captivated everyone in the room.
她那不可抗拒的微笑迷住了房间里的每一个人。

近义词:allure, charm, attraction, magnetism, temptation
反义词:repulsiveness, unattractiveness, averseness

词源: From irresistible + -ness.  Irresistible comes from the Late Latin irresistibilis, from in- "not" + resistere "to resist".
