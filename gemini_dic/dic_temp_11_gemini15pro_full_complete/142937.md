out-valued:/ˌaʊtˈvæljuːd/
|v.| 胜过；价值超过| to be worth more than something else|  Her company was out-valued by its competitors. 她的公司价值被竞争对手超越了.| surpass, exceed | be inferior to, be less valuable than| verb

out-valued:/ˌaʊtˈvæljuːd/
|adj.| 价值被超越的 | having a value surpassed by something else| His car became out-valued after the new model was released.  他的车在新车型发布后价值被超越了。| surpassed, exceeded |  inferior, less valuable | adjective
