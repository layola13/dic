gladship: /ˈɡlædʃɪp/
noun
高兴；喜悦；快乐
A feeling of great happiness and joy.
例句：Her gladship was evident in her radiant smile. 她的喜悦从她灿烂的笑容中显露无疑。

例句：He expressed his gladship at the news of his daughter's engagement. 他对女儿订婚的消息表示高兴。

近义词：joy, happiness, delight, elation, bliss
反义词：sadness, sorrow, grief, despair, misery
词性: 名词

词源:
"Gladship" is a combination of "glad" (meaning happy or pleased) and "-ship" (a suffix denoting a state or condition).  "Glad" comes from Old English "glæd," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*gladjaz." The suffix "-ship" also comes from Old English "-scipe."
