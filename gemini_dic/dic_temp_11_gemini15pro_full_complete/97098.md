infesters:/ɪnˈfɛstərz/
noun|侵扰者，寄生者|People or organisms that infest a place or a living being.|The infesters were quickly eradicated from the building.|近义词：pests, parasites, vermin|反义词: protectors, guardians|词性:n.
Etymology: From the verb "infest," which comes from the Old French "infester," derived from the Latin "infestare" meaning "to attack, harass."|词源
