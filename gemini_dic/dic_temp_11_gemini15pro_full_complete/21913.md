boers:/ˈbʊərz/ /ˈbɔːrz/ | n. | 布尔人 |  Descendants of Dutch settlers in southern Africa, especially farmers.  Historically, often used to refer specifically to those who participated in the Boer Wars against the British. |  The Boers established independent republics in the 19th century. 布尔人在19世纪建立了独立的共和国。

The Boers were skilled horsemen and marksmen. 布尔人是熟练的骑手和神枪手。 | 近义词: Afrikaners | 反义词:  (No direct antonym; perhaps British, but this is context-dependent) | 词性: Noun
