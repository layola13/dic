drily:/ˈdraɪli/| adv.|干燥地；冷淡地；枯燥无味地|In a dry manner; without emotion or enthusiasm.  Characterized by dryness, especially lacking moisture or emotional warmth.|She drily observed that his excuse was rather weak. 她冷淡地指出他的借口相当站不住脚。|Synonyms:  sharply, coldly, curtly, flatly, bluntly, matter-of-factly|Antonyms: warmly, emotionally, enthusiastically|词性: 副词

Etymology: From "dry" + "-ly."  "Dry" comes from Old English "dryge," of Germanic origin.
