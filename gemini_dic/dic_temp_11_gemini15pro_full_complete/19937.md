biodegradabilities:/ˌbaɪoʊˌdiːˌɡreɪdəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 生物降解性；生物可降解性 |  The capacity of materials to decompose into simpler substances through the action of microorganisms like bacteria and fungi. This process reduces environmental impact by allowing natural recycling of organic matter. |  Many companies are now focusing on the biodegradabilities of their packaging materials. 许多公司现在正专注于其包装材料的生物降解性。|  The new plastic boasts impressive biodegradabilities, breaking down within months in a compost heap.  这种新型塑料拥有令人印象深刻的生物降解性，在堆肥堆中几个月内就会分解。 |  decomposability, biodegradability (singular form) | non-biodegradability, persistence | noun
