everchanging:/ˌevərˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective
不断变化的；变化无常的
constantly changing; in constant flux
The fashion industry is everchanging, with new trends emerging every season.  时尚行业瞬息万变，每个季节都会出现新的潮流。
The political landscape is everchanging, making it difficult to predict the future. 政治格局瞬息万变，难以预测未来。
近义词:dynamic, fluid, volatile, mutable, inconstant
反义词:static, unchanging, constant, stable, permanent
词性:adjective

词源:
From ever + changing (present participle of change).  由ever（总是）和changing（change的现在分词）组成。
