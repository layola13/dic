coasteering:/ˈkəʊstɪərɪŋ/| n.| 海岸攀岩；海岸探险|An activity that involves traversing the intertidal zone of a rocky coastline on foot or by swimming, without the aid of boats or surfboards.  It typically includes scrambling, climbing, swimming, jumping, and exploring sea caves and other coastal features.|We went coasteering in Cornwall last summer. 我们去年夏天在康沃尔郡进行了海岸攀岩。|近义词：rock climbing, scrambling, sea-level traversing|反义词:|n.
