delegators:/ˈdɛlɪˌɡeɪtərz/| n.|代表；委托人；授权者|A person authorized to act as a representative for another or others, especially at a conference or meeting.|The delegators voted to accept the proposal. 代表们投票接受了这项提议。|Synonyms: representatives, envoys, commissioners, agents, deputies|Antonyms: recipients, receivers|Noun

delegator:/ˈdɛlɪˌɡeɪtər/| n.|代表；委托人；授权者|A person who delegates a task or responsibility to another.|The delegator assigned the project to his team. 委托人将项目分配给他的团队。|Synonyms: assigner, entrustor|Antonyms: assignee, trustee|Noun
