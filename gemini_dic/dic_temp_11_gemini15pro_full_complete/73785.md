formalizers:/ˈfɔːrməlaɪzərz/
noun
形式化者；规范化者
People or systems that establish clear rules or procedures.
The formalizers of the new policy ensured every detail was documented.  新的政策制定者确保每个细节都有记录。
The company’s formalizers created a strict protocol for handling customer complaints. 公司的程序制定者创建了一个严格的客户投诉处理方案。
codifiers, systematizers, structurers
informalizers, improvisers
n
