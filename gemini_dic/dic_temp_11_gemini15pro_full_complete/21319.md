bloodulf: /ˈblʌdˌʊlf/
n.
嗜血的人，残暴的人
A person who thirsts for blood; a cruel and bloodthirsty person.
He was a bloodulf, reveling in the violence he inflicted. 他是一个嗜血成性的人，陶醉于他施加的暴力。
The bloodulf rampaged through the village, leaving a trail of death and destruction.  这个嗜血的暴徒在村子里横冲直撞，留下了一片死亡和毁灭的痕迹。
近义词：savage, brute, fiend, monster
反义词：pacifist, humanitarian


词性: 名词 (Noun)

词源:  A compound of 'blood' and 'wolf'.  'Blood' refers to the literal or metaphorical desire for blood, while 'wolf' evokes the image of a predatory and ferocious animal.
