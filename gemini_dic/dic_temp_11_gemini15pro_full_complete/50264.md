depersonalize:/ˌdiːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪz/ /ˌdiːˈpɜːrsnəlaɪz/ |v. |使失去个性，使非人格化|To deprive of personality or individuality; to make impersonal.|The hospital gowns depersonalized the patients.  医院的病号服使病人失去了个性。|Synonyms: dehumanize, objectify, alienate|Antonyms: personalize, individualize|verb
depersonalization:/ˌdiːˌpɜːrsənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌdiːˌpɜːrsnələˈzeɪʃn/ |n. |非人格化，失去个性|The process of depersonalizing or the state of being depersonalized.|The depersonalization of customer service can be frustrating.  客户服务的非人格化令人沮丧。|Synonyms: dehumanization, objectification|Antonyms: personalization, individualization|noun
