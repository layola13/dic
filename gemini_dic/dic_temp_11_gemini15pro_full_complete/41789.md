corse:/kɔːrs/| n.| 尸体;诗歌(古语);|A body, especially a dead body (archaic or poetic).|例句：The knight lay slain, his corse upon the battlefield. 骑士被杀，他的尸体躺在战场上。

例句：The raven croaked above the silent corse.  乌鸦在寂静的尸体上空呱呱叫。|近义词：corpse, cadaver, body|反义词:living being|词性:名词
