ordinations:/ˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃənz/
|n.|授予圣职；任命；排序；排列；坐标|The act of ordaining someone into the Christian ministry; the process of conferring holy orders. Also, the arrangement or sequencing of things.|The ordinations of new priests took place in the cathedral. 新神父的任命仪式在大教堂举行。|Synonyms: appointments, installations, consecrations, investitures, arrangements, orderings|Antonyms: dismissals, removals, disarrangements, disorder|noun
