bakshis:/ˈbɑːkʃiːʃ/ /ˈbækʃiːʃ/
noun
小费，赏钱，贿赂
A tip, gratuity, or bribe, especially in South Asia and the Middle East.
Example 1: He gave the porter a small bakshis for carrying his bags.  他给了搬运工一小笔小费来搬运行李。

Example 2:  She offered a bakshis to the official to expedite the process. 她向官员提供贿赂以加快办理流程。

synonym
tip, gratuity, bribe, reward, recompense

antonym
penalty, fine, forfeiture

ETYMOLOGY->
From Persian بخشش (bakhshish), meaning "gift" or "forgiveness."  源自波斯语 بخشش (bakhshish)，意为“礼物”或“宽恕”。
