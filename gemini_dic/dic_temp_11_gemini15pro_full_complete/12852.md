assoilment:/əˈsɔɪlmənt/
n.
赦免；解除；释放；净罪
The act of absolving or freeing from sin or guilt; absolution.
He sought assoilment for his sins. 他寻求赦免自己的罪过。
近义词:absolution, forgiveness, pardon, release, exoneration
反义词:condemnation, conviction, incrimination, accusation
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French *assoilement, from assoillir (“to absolve”), from Latin absolvere (“to absolve”).
