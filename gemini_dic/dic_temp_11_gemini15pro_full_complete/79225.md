glare:/ɡlɛr/| v. n.| 怒视，瞪眼；发出刺眼的光|to look at someone or something angrily for a long time; to shine with a very strong light that is difficult to look at |He glared at her across the table. 他隔着桌子怒视着她。|The sun glared down from a cloudless sky. 太阳从万里无云的天空中发出刺眼的光芒。|synonyms: scowl, frown, glare at, stare, glower|antonyms: smile, beam|verb, noun
