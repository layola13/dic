aerobically:/ɛəˈroʊbɪkli/
adverb|有氧地；需氧地|In the presence of oxygen; using oxygen.  Relating to or involving aerobic respiration.|例句：Most living things respire aerobically. 大多数生物进行有氧呼吸。|例句：The bacteria grow aerobically. 这些细菌进行有氧生长。|近义词：with oxygen, in air|反义词:anaerobically|词性:副词
