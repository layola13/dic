garlicked:/ˈɡɑːrlɪkt/| adj.|涂有大蒜的，有大蒜味的|Flavored or seasoned with garlic.|The garlicked bread was delicious. 涂有大蒜的面包很美味。|Near synonyms: garlicky, garlic-flavored|Antonyms: bland, unflavored|adjective

garlicked:/ˈɡɑːrlɪkt/| adj.|充满大蒜的，弥漫着大蒜味的 (figurative, less common)|Overly infused with garlic; overwhelming with the presence or smell of garlic.  This meaning is often used figuratively. |The air was thick and garlicked after his cooking. 他做饭后，空气中弥漫着浓浓的大蒜味。|Near synonyms: pungent, overpowering|Antonyms: fresh, airy|adjective
