keened:/kiːnd/| v.|哀号，恸哭；渴望，热切希望|To wail in grief for a dead person; to express a keen desire or yearning for something.|She keened over the loss of her beloved pet. 她为失去心爱的宠物而哀号。|近义词：wailed, lamented, mourned, yearned, longed|反义词：rejoiced, celebrated|词性: 动词

词源: From the Old Irish *caine "to weep, lament," related to Old English cwanian "to mourn, lament" and Gothic kwainōn "to weep."
