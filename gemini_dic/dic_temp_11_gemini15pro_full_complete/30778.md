cestraciont:/sɛˈstreɪʃənt/ | n. | 异齿鲨 |  A type of shark belonging to the order Cestraciontiformes, characterized by heterodont dentition (different types of teeth). These sharks are also known as bullhead sharks or Port Jackson sharks. |  The Port Jackson shark is a well-known cestraciont.  港口杰克逊鲨是一种著名的异齿鲨。| synonym: bullhead shark, Port Jackson shark | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of shark exists) | noun
