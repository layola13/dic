localizers:/ˈloʊkəlaɪzərz/| n.|本地化人员，本地化器|People who adapt products or content to a specific local market.  They translate text, modify graphics, and adjust other aspects to make the product or content culturally appropriate and accessible to a particular audience.|The localizers ensured the software was available in multiple languages. 本地化人员确保该软件有多种语言版本。|translators, adapters|globalizers|noun

localizer:/ˈloʊkəlaɪzər/| n.|本地化器，定位器|A device or person that helps to determine or establish a location.  Can refer to a person involved in localization or a piece of equipment used for navigation or positioning.|The aircraft used a localizer to align with the runway. 飞机使用定位器与跑道对齐。|locator, positioner| n.
