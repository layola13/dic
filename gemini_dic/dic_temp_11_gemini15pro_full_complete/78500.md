ghat:/ɡɑːt/| n. |河边石阶，(尤指在印度教徒用作沐浴的)河坛; 山口，山路|A flight of steps leading down to a river, especially in India, used by bathers; a mountain pass.|例句： Pilgrims bathed in the ghat. 朝圣者在河坛沐浴。

例句：The ghats of the Ganges are sacred to Hindus. 恒河的河坛对印度教徒来说是神圣的。|近义词：steps, stairway, riverbank, landing|反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑  cliff  悬崖)|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi घाट (ghāṭ), from Sanskrit घट्ट (ghaṭṭa, “landing place, river bank”).
