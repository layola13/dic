duplicated:/ˈdjuːplɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. |复制的，重复的|Made as an exact copy of something else; identical.| 例句：The duplicated files take up too much space. 复制的文件占用了太多空间。|近义词：copied, cloned, replicated|反义词：original, unique|词性:形容词
