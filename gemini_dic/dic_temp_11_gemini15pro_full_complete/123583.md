montaging:/ˈmɒntɑːʒɪŋ/ /ˈmɑːntɑːʒɪŋ/
v.
制作蒙太奇；拼贴
creating a montage; assembling separate elements into a composite whole
The director is montaging the battle scenes for maximum impact. 导演正在将战斗场景剪辑成蒙太奇以达到最大的冲击力。
assembling, compiling, juxtaposing, composing, creating
