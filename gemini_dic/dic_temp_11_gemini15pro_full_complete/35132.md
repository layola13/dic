cloudlet:/ˈklaʊdlət/| n. |云端小站，微云|A small-scale cloud computing facility located close to the edge of the network, typically used to provide low-latency services to mobile devices and IoT devices.|Example 1: Cloudlets can improve the performance of mobile applications by processing data closer to the user. 云端小站可以通过在更靠近用户的位置处理数据来提高移动应用程序的性能。

Example 2:  The use of cloudlets is increasing with the growth of the Internet of Things. 随着物联网的发展，云端小站的使用也在增加。|近义词：edge server, micro data center, mobile edge computing (MEC) server|反义词: cloud, central server|词性:noun
