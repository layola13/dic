felt like:/fɛlt laɪk/
verb phrase
感觉像，像是
This fabric felt like silk.  这块布料摸起来像丝绸。
It felt like a dream. 这感觉像是一场梦。
近义词：seemed like, felt as if, had the feeling of
反义词: N/A
词性: verb phrase

Etymology: "Felt" comes from the Old English "felan," meaning "to feel." "Like" comes from the Old English "gelic," meaning "similar" or "alike."  The phrase combines the sensation of feeling with the comparison implied by "like."
