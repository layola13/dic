gynandrous:/ɡaɪˈnændrəs/ | adj. |雌雄同体的，雌雄同株的 | Having both male and female sexual organs in the same individual, as in some plants and animals. | Example 1:  Some species of snails are gynandrous, possessing both male and female reproductive organs. 有些蜗牛种类是雌雄同体的，同时拥有雄性和雌性生殖器官。

Example 2: The gynandrous flower has both stamens and pistils.  这种雌雄同体的花朵同时拥有雄蕊和雌蕊。 | Synonyms: hermaphroditic, monoecious | Antonyms: dioecious |词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Greek *gynē* (woman) + *anēr, andros* (man).
