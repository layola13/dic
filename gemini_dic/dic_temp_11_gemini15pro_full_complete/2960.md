adynamy:/ˌeɪˈdaɪnəmi/ | n. |无力；虚弱；衰弱|Lack of strength; weakness; debility.  A condition characterized by profound weakness and loss of energy. |  The patient's adynamy prevented him from even sitting up in bed. 病人的虚弱使他甚至无法在床上坐起来。 |  The adynamy following the surgery was expected but prolonged.  手术后的无力是预料之中的，但持续时间很长。| Near synonyms: weakness, debility, asthenia, lassitude | Antonyms: strength, vigor, energy | noun
