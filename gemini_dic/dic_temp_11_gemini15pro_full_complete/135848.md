nonprosaicnesses:/ˌnɒnproʊˈzeɪɪknəsɪz/
noun
非散文性；非平凡性；非寻常性
The state or quality of not being prosaic; imaginativeness, originality, unusualness.
His poetry is full of nonprosaicnesses, surprising the reader with unexpected imagery and metaphors. 他的诗歌充满了非同寻常之处，用意想不到的意象和比喻给读者带来惊喜。
Near Synonyms:  originality, imaginativeness, creativity, unconventionality, novelty, freshness, uniqueness, peculiarity, eccentricity,  unusualness, extraordinariness
Antonyms:  prosaicness, ordinariness, commonness, banality, dullness, staleness, conventionality, typicality
