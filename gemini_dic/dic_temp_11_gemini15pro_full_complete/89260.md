hominoid:/ˈhɒmɪnɔɪd/ /ˈhɑːmɪnɔɪd/| n. |类人猿，人科动物|A primate of the superfamily Hominoidea, such as a human, ape, or their extinct ancestors.  |Humans and chimpanzees are both hominoids. 人类和黑猩猩都是类人猿。|近义词：ape, primate|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-hominoid primate")|noun
