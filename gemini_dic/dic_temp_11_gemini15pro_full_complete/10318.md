appellant:/əˈpɛlənt/| n.|上诉人，申诉人|A person who appeals a decision, especially to a higher court.|The appellant argued that the lower court's decision was flawed. 上诉人辩称下级法院的判决有缺陷。|Example: The appellant filed a brief with the Supreme Court.  上诉人向最高法院提交了案情摘要。|Synonyms: petitioner, litigant, claimant|Antonyms: respondent, appellee|noun
