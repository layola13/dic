leafleted:/ˈliːflɪtɪd/
verb
散发传单；用传单宣传
distributed leaflets; advertised by distributing leaflets
The campaigners leafleted the neighborhood to raise awareness about the issue.  运动人士在社区散发传单，以提高人们对这个问题的认识。
The area was heavily leafleted before the election. 选举前，这个地区到处都是传单。
Synonym: pamphlet, hand out, distribute, circulate
Antonym: collect, gather
词性: 动词 (past participle)
