clip-cloped:/klɪpˈklɑːpt/ /klɪpˈklɒpt/
verb
发出“嗒嗒”声（尤指马蹄声）
The sound of a horse's hooves hitting a hard surface, such as a road or pavement.
The horse clip-clopped down the cobbled street.  马发出嗒嗒的蹄声沿着鹅卵石街道走下去。
The rhythmic clip-clopping of the horses' hooves echoed through the valley. 马蹄有节奏的嗒嗒声在山谷回荡。
近义词:clattered, trotted
反义词:(No direct antonym, but consider "galloped" for a contrasting sound)
词性:v.
