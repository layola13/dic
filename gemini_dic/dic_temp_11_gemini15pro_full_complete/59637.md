ecclesiology:/ɪˌkliːziˈɒlədʒi/
n.
教会学
The study of the Christian church, its nature, structure, and function.
Ecclesiology is a complex field encompassing historical, sociological, and theological perspectives. 教会学是一个涵盖历史、社会学和神学视角的复杂领域。
The book offers a comprehensive overview of Catholic ecclesiology. 这本书全面概述了天主教教会学。
synonyms: church studies, theology of the church
antonyms: secularism, atheism
noun
