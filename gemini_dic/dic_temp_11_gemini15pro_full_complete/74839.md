frenziedly:/ˈfren.ziːd.li/ | adv. | 狂热地，疯狂地 | In a frenzied manner; with wild excitement or agitation. |  He worked frenziedly to finish the project before the deadline.  他疯狂地工作以在截止日期前完成项目。 | Synonyms:  wildly, frantically, hysterically, manically | Antonyms: calmly, peacefully, quietly | 词性: 副词

frenzied:/ˈfren.ziːd/ | adj. | 狂热的，疯狂的 | Affected with frenzy; wildly excited or enthusiastic. | The crowd became frenzied when the band took the stage. 当乐队登上舞台时，人群变得疯狂起来。 | Synonyms: frantic, frenetic, wild, agitated | Antonyms: calm, peaceful, composed | 词性: 形容词

frenzy:/ˈfren.zi/ | n. | 狂热，疯狂 | A state of wild excitement or agitation. |  The shoppers were in a frenzy to grab the bargains. 购物者疯狂地抢购便宜货。 | Synonyms: madness, hysteria, delirium, rage | Antonyms: calmness, peace, tranquility | 词性: 名词
