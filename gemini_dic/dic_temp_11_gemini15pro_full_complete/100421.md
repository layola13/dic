intertinged:/ˌɪntərˈtɪndʒd/
adjective
混合的，交织的，渗透的
tinged or imbued with something different
Her joy was intertinged with sorrow. 她的喜悦中夹杂着悲伤。
Synonyms: imbued, infused, tinged, permeated, suffused
Antonyms: pure, unmixed, homogenous
词性: 形容词
