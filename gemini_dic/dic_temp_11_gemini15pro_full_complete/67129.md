exportability:/ˌɛkspɔːrtəˈbɪləti/
n.
可出口性，出口能力
The exportability of a product depends on various factors like quality, price, and demand.  一种产品的可出口性取决于质量、价格和需求等多种因素。
The government is trying to improve the exportability of agricultural products. 政府正在努力提高农产品的出口能力。
近义词:transferability, tradability
反义词:non-exportability
