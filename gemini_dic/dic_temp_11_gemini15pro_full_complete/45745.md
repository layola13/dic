curvy:/ˈkɜːvi/ /ˈkɝːvi/| adj.|弯曲的，曲线美的|Having a body with rounded, attractive curves.  Having or full of curves.|例句：She has a curvy figure. 她身材曲线优美。

例句：The road through the mountains was curvy and dangerous. 穿过山区的道路弯弯曲曲，十分危险。|近义词：shapely, voluptuous, sinuous, winding|反义词：straight, linear, thin|词性:形容词
