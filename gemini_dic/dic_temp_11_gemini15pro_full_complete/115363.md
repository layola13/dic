manoush:/mæˈnuːʃ/ | n. |  马努什（一种起源于法国的薄脆饼）| A thin, crispy flatbread, often topped with various ingredients, originating in France, typically associated with the Manouche people, a subgroup of the Roma/Sinti community. | Example: We enjoyed a delicious manoush topped with za'atar and cheese. 我们享用了一份美味的马努什，上面撒着香料和奶酪。| Example:  Street vendors sell manoush with a variety of toppings. 街头小贩出售各种配料的马努什。 | Synonym: flatbread, pita | Antonym: N/A | Noun
