midrash:/ˈmɪdrɑːʃ/ (US), /ˈmɪdræʃ/ (UK)| n.|米德拉什,  (犹太教)圣经注释|An ancient Jewish interpretation and commentary on the Hebrew scriptures, exploring the text's legal, ethical, and theological implications.|例句：The midrash offers a fascinating interpretation of the biblical story.  米德拉什对圣经故事提供了引人入胜的解释。

例句：Rabbis often use midrash to illuminate the meaning of scripture. 拉比经常使用米德拉什来阐明经文的含义。|近义词：commentary, exegesis, interpretation|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
