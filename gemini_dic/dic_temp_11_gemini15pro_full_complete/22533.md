booner:/ˈbuːnər/| n.|好运；意外收获；额外津贴|Something beneficial or fortunate; a windfall.|He got a booner when he found a $20 bill on the street. 他在街上捡到一张 20 美元的钞票，真是意外收获。|近义词：windfall, bonus, godsend|反义词：misfortune, loss|词性:名词

词源:
Possibly from Daniel Boone, the American frontiersman, suggesting a lucky find or stroke of good fortune. 也可能源于boon（恩惠，福利）加上-er后缀构成。
