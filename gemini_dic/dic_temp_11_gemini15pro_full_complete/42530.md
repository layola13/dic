countermanding:/ˌkaʊntərˈmændɪŋ/ | adj. | 反命令的，撤销的 | An order that countermands a previous order. | The general issued a countermanding order to stop the attack. 将军发布了反命令，停止进攻。 | 近义词: revoking, rescinding, canceling | 反义词: confirming, upholding, ratifying | 词性: 形容词

countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ | vt. |  撤销，取消（命令），收回 | To revoke (a command); to cancel or reverse (an order or instruction) |  He countermanded the order for more troops. 他取消了增兵的命令。 | 近义词: revoke, rescind, cancel, annul | 反义词: order, command, authorize, ratify | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *contremander, from contre- "against" + mander "to order," from Latin mandare "to entrust, order."
