misnamed:/ˌmɪsˈneɪmd/
adjective
命名错误的；叫错名字的
incorrectly named
The misnamed "Affordable Care Act" has actually made health insurance more expensive for many people.  错误命名的“平价医疗法案”实际上使许多人的医疗保险更加昂贵。
The street was misnamed after a historical figure who never actually lived there. 这条街以一个从未在那里居住过的历史人物的名字错误地命名。
近义词: wrongly named, inaccurately named
反义词: correctly named, aptly named
词性: adjective

misname:/ˌmɪsˈneɪm/
verb
给…错误命名；叫错…的名字
to give something or someone the wrong name
I misnamed him George when his name was actually John. 我把他错叫成乔治，而实际上他的名字是约翰。
The explorer misnamed the island, not realizing it had already been charted. 探险家给岛屿起了错误的名字，没有意识到它已经被绘制出来了。
近义词: miscall, mislabel
反义词: name correctly
词性: verb


词源:
From mis- + name.  由mis-（错误地）+ name（命名）组成。
