depressive:/dɪˈprɛsɪv/| adj. n.|抑郁的，令人沮丧的；抑郁症患者|Causing or tending to cause depression.  A person suffering from or susceptible to depression.|例句：The weather has been so depressive lately. 最近的天气真让人沮丧。|近义词：melancholy, gloomy, depressing, sad|反义词：cheerful, happy, joyful|adjective

depressive:/dɪˈprɛsɪv/| n.|抑郁症患者|A person suffering from or susceptible to depression.|例句：He is a depressive and needs professional help. 他是一位抑郁症患者，需要专业帮助。|近义词：sufferer|反义词：None|noun
