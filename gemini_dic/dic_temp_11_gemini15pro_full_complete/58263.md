drums:/drʌmz/ | n. |鼓; 鼓声; 鼓状物; 油桶|A percussion instrument consisting of a hollow cylinder or hemisphere covered with a tightly stretched membrane, played by striking with sticks or the hands.|The drummer kept a steady beat on the drums. 鼓手稳定地敲着鼓。|Synonyms: percussion, timpani, kettledrums|Antonyms: silence, quiet|noun

drums:/drʌmz/ | v. |击鼓; 咚咚地敲; 反复灌输;  |To play a drum or drums; to make a rhythmic sound by hitting something repeatedly.|He drummed his fingers on the table nervously. 他紧张地用手指敲着桌子。|Synonyms: tap, beat, rap|Antonyms: hush, mute|verb
