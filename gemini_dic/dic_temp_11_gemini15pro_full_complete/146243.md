overexpressivenesses: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈspreɪsɪvnɪsɪz/
| n. | 过度表达; 过分表现 | The state or quality of being excessively expressive, often to the point of being dramatic or exaggerated. | Her overexpressivenesses sometimes made her seem insincere. 她的过度表达有时让她显得不真诚。 |  The actor's overexpressivenesses bordered on caricature.  这位演员的过度表达近乎滑稽。 |  exaggeration, effusiveness, demonstrativeness |  understatement, reserve, restraint | noun

overexpressive: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈspreɪsɪv/
| adj. | 过度表达的; 过分表现的 |  Tending to express oneself excessively, often to the point of being dramatic or exaggerated. |  He's a bit overexpressive, but his heart is in the right place. 他有点过度表达，但他的心肠是好的。 | She gave an overexpressive performance that felt forced. 她表演得过于夸张，感觉很做作。| exaggerated, effusive, demonstrative | understated, reserved, restrained | adjective
