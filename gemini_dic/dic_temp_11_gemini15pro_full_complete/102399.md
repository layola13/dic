jaborine:/ˈdʒæbəriːn/ | n. |毒扁豆碱; 伪毒扁豆碱|An alkaloid obtained from jaborandi, isomeric with pilocarpine but having different physiological effects.|Jaborine can cause sweating and salivation. 毒扁豆碱会导致出汗和流涎。|Synonyms: pilocarpine, muscarine |Antonyms: atropine, scopolamine |noun
