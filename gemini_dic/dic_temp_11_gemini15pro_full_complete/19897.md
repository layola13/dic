binominal:/baɪˈnəʊmiəl/| adj. n.|二项式的；二项式|A binomial is an algebraic expression consisting of two terms connected by a plus sign or minus sign.  |Example: x + y is a binomial. x + y 是一个二项式。

|Example: The binomial theorem is a fundamental concept in algebra. 二项式定理是代数中的一个基本概念。|近义词: two-term|反义词: monomial, trinomial|词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *binominalis*, from Latin *bini* ("two each") + *nomin-, nomen* ("name").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₁nómn̥* (“name”).
