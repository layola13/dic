limiter:/ˈlɪmɪtər/| n.|限制器；限幅器；限流器|A device that limits something, such as the flow of current, the level of sound, or the speed of a vehicle.|The car has a speed limiter installed. 这辆车安装了限速器。|近义词：restrictor, regulator, controller|反义词:accelerator, enhancer|noun

limiter:/ˈlɪmɪtər/| n.|限制者；限定者|Someone or something that limits something.|He acts as a limiter on her spending habits. 他限制了她的消费习惯。|近义词：restrainer|反义词:promoter|noun
