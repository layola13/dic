co-occurring:/ˌkoʊ əˈkɜːrɪŋ/
adjective|共同出现的，同时发生的|happening or existing at the same time or in the same place|The study found several co-occurring risk factors for the disease. 这项研究发现了这种疾病的几个共同存在的风险因素。|Co-occurring symptoms often make diagnosis difficult. 同时出现的症状通常使诊断变得困难。|synonyms: concomitant, concurrent, simultaneous|antonyms: separate, independent|adjective
