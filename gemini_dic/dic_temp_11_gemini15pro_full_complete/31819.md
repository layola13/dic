checkup:/ˈtʃɛkʌp/ (US), /ˈtʃekʌp/ (UK)| n. |体检，检查，核查|A thorough examination, typically a medical one, to detect any problems.|例句：I have a yearly checkup with my doctor. 我每年都去看医生做体检。

例句：The mechanic gave the car a thorough checkup. 机械师对汽车进行了彻底的检查。

近义词：examination, inspection, review|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词
