interimistic:/ˌɪntərɪˈmɪstɪk/
adjective
临时的，过渡的
Existing or occurring for an interim or transitional period; temporary.
An interimistic government was put in place until elections could be held.  一个临时政府成立，直到选举能够举行。
The interimistic measures proved effective in stabilizing the market. 这些临时措施有效地稳定了市场。
近义词: provisional, temporary, interim, transitional
反义词: permanent, lasting
