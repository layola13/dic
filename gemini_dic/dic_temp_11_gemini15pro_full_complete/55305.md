dissolvability:/dɪˌzɒlvəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可溶性，溶解性|The dissolvability of sugar in water is well-known.  糖在水中的溶解性是众所周知的。|  The dissolvability of certain minerals depends on the pH of the solution.  某些矿物质的溶解度取决于溶液的pH值。 | solubility, solvability | insolubility, indissolubility | noun

Etymology->
From dissolve (from Latin dissolvere, from dis- "apart" + solvere "to loosen") + -ability (suffix forming nouns denoting quality or capacity).
