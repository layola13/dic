bedsit: /ˈbɛdsɪt/ (UK) /ˈbɛdˌsɪt/ (US)| n. |单间公寓；卧室兼起居室|A bedsit is a single room that serves as both a bedroom and a sitting room.|例句：She lived in a tiny bedsit in London. 她住在伦敦的一个小单间公寓里。

例句：The bedsit was cramped but affordable.  这单间公寓很拥挤，但价格实惠。
