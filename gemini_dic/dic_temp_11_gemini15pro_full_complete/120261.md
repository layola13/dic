mini-industry:/ˈmɪni ˌɪndəstri/| n. |小型产业，微型产业|A small-scale industry, often involving specialized products or services.|The craft beer scene has become a mini-industry in recent years. 近年来，精酿啤酒已经成为了一个小型产业。|例句：The local farmers market has spawned a mini-industry of artisan food producers. 当地的农贸市场催生了一个手工食品生产者的小型产业。

例句：The rise of blogging has created a mini-industry of online advertising and marketing. 博客的兴起创造了一个在线广告和营销的小型产业。|近义词：small business, cottage industry, niche market|反义词:large industry, major industry|词性:名词
