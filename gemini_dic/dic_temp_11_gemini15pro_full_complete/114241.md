PHRASE->

mail something to someone: 将某物寄给某人  Please mail this letter to Mr. Smith. 请把这封信寄给史密斯先生。
by mail: 通过邮件  I received the document by mail. 我通过邮件收到了文件。
airmail: 航空邮件  I sent the package by airmail. 我通过航空邮件寄送了包裹。
mass mailing: 大量邮寄  The company did a mass mailing of its new catalog. 这家公司大量邮寄了它的新目录。
