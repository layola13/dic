militaryism:/ˈmɪlɪtərɪzəm/| n. |军国主义|A belief or system that places strong emphasis on military power and values, often prioritizing military spending and preparedness over other social and economic needs. It can also refer to the glorification of military ideals and the influence of the military on civilian life.|The country's growing militaryism led to an arms race with its neighbors. 这个国家的军国主义日益增长，导致了与其邻国的军备竞赛。|militarization, bellicosity|pacifism, diplomacy|noun
