fit:/fɪt/ (UK) /fɪt/ (US)| adj. v. n.|合适的，健康的；使…适合；合身|A dress that fits well. 一件很合身的连衣裙。|The carpenter fitted the shelves. 木匠安装了架子。|The fit of the suit was perfect. 这套西装非常合身。|近义词：suitable, appropriate, healthy, strong;  match, adapt; suitability|反义词：unsuitable, inappropriate, unhealthy, weak; mismatch|词性:形容词,动词,名词

fit:/fɪt/ (UK) /fɪt/ (US)| adj.|健康的，强健的|He's very fit for his age. 就他的年龄而言，他非常健康。|She keeps fit by running every day. 她每天跑步来保持健康。|近义词：healthy, strong, well|反义词：unhealthy, weak, unfit|词性:形容词

fit:/fɪt/ (UK) /fɪt/ (US)| v.|使…适合，安装|The tailor fitted the suit to the customer. 裁缝为顾客修改西装使其合身。|We fitted new locks on all the doors. 我们在所有门上都安装了新锁。|近义词：adjust, adapt, install|反义词：remove|词性:动词

fit:/fɪt/ (UK) /fɪt/ (US)| n.|合身，痉挛，突发|The fit of the shoes was perfect. 鞋子非常合脚。|He suffered an epileptic fit. 他癫痫发作了。|A fit of laughter. 一阵大笑。|近义词：convulsion, spasm, outburst|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性:名词

PHRASE->

fit in: 融入，适应  He's trying to fit in with the new team. 他正努力融入新团队。
fit out: 装备，配备  They fitted out the ship for its voyage. 他们为航行装备了船只。
fit up:  诬陷，栽赃  He claimed he was fitted up by the police. 他声称他被警察诬陷了。
see fit: 认为合适  Do as you see fit. 你认为合适就怎么做。
fit as a fiddle: 非常健康  He's 80 years old but fit as a fiddle. 他已经80岁了，但身体非常健康。
