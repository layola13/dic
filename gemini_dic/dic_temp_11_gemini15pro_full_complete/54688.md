disorderly:/dɪsˈɔːrdərli/ /dɪsˈɔːr dɚ li/| adj.|混乱的，无序的，妨碍治安的|Lacking order or tidiness; unorganized or messy.  Behaving in a way that disrupts public order.|例句：The disorderly crowd surged forward. 混乱的人群向前涌去。

The police arrested him for disorderly conduct. 警察以扰乱治安罪逮捕了他。|近义词：chaotic, messy, untidy, unruly, rowdy|反义词：orderly, tidy, organized, neat, well-behaved|词性:形容词

disorderly:/dɪsˈɔːrdərli/ /dɪsˈɔːr dɚ li/| n.|扰乱治安的人; 违反纪律的人|A person who behaves in a disorderly way, causing disruption or disturbance.|例句：The police rounded up the disorderlies after the football match. 足球比赛结束后，警察围捕了滋事分子。

Several disorderlies were ejected from the bar. 几名扰乱秩序的人被赶出了酒吧。|近义词：troublemaker, hooligan, ruffian|反义词：law-abiding citizen|词性:名词
