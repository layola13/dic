cavorts:/kəˈvɔːrts/ | v. | 欢跃，嬉戏 | To leap or dance about excitedly; to prance; to frolic. | The lambs cavorted in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏。 |  The children cavorted on the beach, splashing and laughing. 孩子们在沙滩上嬉戏玩耍，溅起水花，欢声笑语。 | 近义词: frolic, gambol, prance, frisk | 反义词: mope, sulk | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From French *cavorte*, from Italian *cavorta*, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to *gavotta* "gavotte," a lively dance.
