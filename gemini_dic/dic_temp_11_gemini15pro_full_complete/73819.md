formerly:/ˈfɔːrmərli/| adv.|以前地，从前地|Previously; in the past.|She was formerly a teacher but now works in publishing. 她以前是老师，但现在在出版社工作。|例句：The building was formerly a school. 这栋楼以前是一所学校。|近义词：previously, once, earlier, before|反义词：currently, now, presently|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *formeli, from Old French *formelement (“formally, in due form”), from Medieval Latin formalis (“formal”), from Latin forma (“form, shape”). The sense shifted from "in a formal manner" to "in time past."
