multilobular:/ˌmʌltiˈlɒbjʊlə(r)/ /ˌmʌltiˈlɑːbjələr/ | adj. | 多叶的；多瓣的 | Having many lobes or lobules.  A lobe is a rounded projection or division, and a lobule is a small lobe.  This term is often used in a medical context to describe organs or tissues. | The doctor identified a multilobular tumor on the patient's lung. 医生在病人的肺部发现了一个多叶肿瘤。 | multilobed, multipartite | unilobular | adjective
