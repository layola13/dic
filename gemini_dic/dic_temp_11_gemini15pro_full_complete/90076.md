hostels:/ˈhɒstəlz/ (British), /ˈhɑːstəlz/ (American)| n.| 旅舍，招待所，廉价旅馆|A budget-oriented, shared-dorm accommodation that caters primarily to young travelers.|例句：We stayed in hostels during our backpacking trip through Europe. 我们在欧洲背包旅行期间住在旅舍。

例句：Hostels are a great way to meet other travelers.  旅舍是结识其他旅行者的好方法。


近义词：inns, budget hotels, backpacker lodges, dormitories|反义词:hotels, resorts, luxury accommodations|词性:noun
