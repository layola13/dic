contestingly:/kənˈtɛstɪŋli/
adv.
竞争地，争辩地
in a contesting manner; with contention or dispute
He contestingly argued his point, refusing to back down.  他争辩着自己的观点，拒绝让步。
The election was contestingly fought, with both sides accusing the other of fraud. 这场选举竞争激烈，双方都指责对方舞弊。
近义词: competitively, argumentatively, disputatiously
反义词: agreeably, harmoniously, peaceably
词性: adverb

词源: From contest + -ing + -ly.  Contest ultimately comes from the Latin "contestārī," meaning "to call to witness."
