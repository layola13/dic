memorializers:/məˈmɔːriəlaɪzərz/
n.
纪念者；追念者
People or entities that create or establish memorials.
The memorializers dedicated a plaque to the fallen soldiers. 纪念者们为阵亡的士兵献上了一块匾。
The memorializers commissioned a statue in his honor.  纪念者们委托制作了一座雕像来纪念他。
commemorators, celebrants
detractors, critics
noun
