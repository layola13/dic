The dancer held a freeze-framed pose, showcasing her incredible balance. 舞者保持着一个定格的姿势，展示了她惊人的平衡力。|近义词：frozen, still, static|反义词:moving, dynamic, fluid|词性:形容词

词源:  "Freeze" comes from Old French "fresier" meaning "to freeze." "Frame" comes from Old English "framian" meaning "to benefit, support, or make."  "Freeze-frame" is a compound word combining these two, literally meaning a frozen frame.
