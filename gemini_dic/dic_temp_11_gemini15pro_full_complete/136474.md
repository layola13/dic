nonrevolution:/ˌnɒnˌrɛvəˈluːʃən/ | n. | 非革命，不革命 | Absence of revolution; a state of no revolution. A situation where significant political or social change does not occur through a sudden, violent upheaval. | The country experienced a period of nonrevolution despite the economic hardship.  这个国家尽管经历了经济困难，但却没有发生革命。|  Near synonyms: stability, stagnation, quiescence | Antonyms: revolution, upheaval, revolt | noun
