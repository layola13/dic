deindustrialization:/diːɪnˌdʌstriələˈzeɪʃən/
n.
去工业化
The process by which an economy or area reduces industrial activity, especially heavy industry or manufacturing.
The city experienced a period of rapid deindustrialization in the 1980s.  这座城市在20世纪80年代经历了一段快速的去工业化时期。
Detroit's decline is often cited as an example of deindustrialization.  底特律的衰落经常被引用为去工业化的一个例子。
近义词:industrial decline, economic restructuring
反义词:industrialization, reindustrialization
