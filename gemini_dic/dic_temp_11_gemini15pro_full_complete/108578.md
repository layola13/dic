leachability:/ˌliːtʃəˈbɪləti/
n.
可浸出性，沥滤性
The capability of a substance to be dissolved and carried away by a percolating liquid, especially of a hazardous waste or its constituents.  某种物质被渗滤液体溶解和带走的可能性，尤指危险废物或其成分。
The leachability of the heavy metals from the soil was tested.  对土壤中重金属的可浸出性进行了测试。
Synonym: solubility, dissolvability
Antonym: insolubility, resistance to leaching
noun
