devastative:/ˈdevəsteɪtɪv/| adj.|毁灭性的，破坏性的|Causing widespread destruction or damage.|The hurricane had a devastative impact on the coastal communities.  飓风对沿海社区造成了毁灭性的影响。|例句：The fire had a devastative effect on the forest.  大火对森林造成了毁灭性的影响。

例句：The earthquake's impact was devastative. 地震的影响是毁灭性的。|近义词：destructive, ruinous, catastrophic|反义词：constructive, beneficial|词性:形容词


词源: From devastate + -ive.  Devastate comes from Latin *devastare, from de- "down, completely" + vastare "to lay waste."
