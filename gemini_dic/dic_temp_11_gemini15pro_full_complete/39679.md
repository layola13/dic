consider:/kənˈsɪdər/| v. | 考虑，认为，体谅 |To think carefully about something, typically before making a decision. |例句：I'm considering buying a new car. 我正在考虑买一辆新车。|近义词：think about, contemplate, ponder, regard, deem |反义词:ignore, disregard|verb

consider:/kənˈsɪdər/| v. | 考虑到，顾及 |To take something into account. |例句：We need to consider the cost of the project. 我们需要考虑到这个项目的成本。|近义词：take into account, factor in |反义词:disregard|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French considerer, from Latin considerare "to look at closely, observe, contemplate," literally "to observe the stars," from com- "together" (see com-) + sidus (genitive sideris) "star, constellation."
