disenchanter:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃæntər/ | n. |  使人清醒的人/事；扫兴的人/事 | Someone or something that disenchants someone; something that causes someone to stop believing in something or someone. | The harsh realities of war were a powerful disenchanter for many young idealists. 战争的残酷现实让许多年轻的理想主义者清醒了。| synonyms: disillusioner, spoiler, disappointment | antonyms: charmer, enchanter, delight | noun


disenchant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃænt/ | vt. | 使幻想破灭；使清醒 | To free from illusion or enchantment; to cause someone to stop believing in something or someone. | The experience disenchanted him with politics. 这段经历使他对政治幻想破灭。| synonyms: disillusion, disappoint, undeceive | antonyms: enchant, charm, captivate | verb
