clammy:/ˈklæmi/ | adj. |湿冷的；黏糊糊的 |Unpleasantly damp and sticky to the touch. |Her hands felt clammy and cold. 她的手摸起来又湿又冷。|例句：The walls of the cave were clammy and cold. 洞穴的墙壁湿冷。

例句：His forehead was clammy with sweat. 他的前额因为汗水而黏糊糊的。|近义词：damp, moist, sticky |反义词：dry, warm |词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *clæmig, from clām "clay, loam."  The original sense was "sticky like clay."
