dissociates:/dɪˈsoʊʃieɪts/ /dɪˈsəʊsieɪts/
verb
分离，分裂，脱离
breaks a connection or relationship between things, or becomes separate from something
The organization dissociated itself from the controversial group.  该组织宣布与这个有争议的团体脱离关系。
Sodium chloride dissociates into sodium and chloride ions in water. 氯化钠在水中分解成钠离子和氯离子。

近义词: separate, detach, disconnect, disunite
反义词: associate, connect, unite, combine
词性: verb


dissociation:/dɪˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən/ /dɪˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən/
noun
分离，分裂，脱离
the act of separating or the state of being separated
His dissociation from the group caused a lot of tension. 他与该组织的分离造成了很大的紧张气氛。
The dissociation of water into hydrogen and oxygen requires energy.  水分子分解成氢和氧需要能量。

近义词: separation, detachment, disconnection
反义词: association, connection, union
词性: noun
