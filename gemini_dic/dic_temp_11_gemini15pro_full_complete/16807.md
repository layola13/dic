baselessly:/ˈbeɪsləsli/
adv.
无根据地，无缘无故地
Without a basis in reason or fact.
He baselessly accused her of stealing. 他毫无根据地指责她偷窃。
近义词: groundlessly, unfoundedly, unjustifiably
反义词: justifiably, reasonably, with good reason
词性: 副词
