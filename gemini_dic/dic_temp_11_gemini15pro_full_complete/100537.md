interweave:/ˌɪntərˈwiːv/ (US), /ˌɪntəˈwiːv/ (UK)| v. |交织，穿插，编织|To blend or mix together; to connect closely, especially by weaving or intertwining.|The author skillfully interweaves historical facts with fictional narrative.  作者巧妙地将史实与虚构的叙事交织在一起。|The tapestry interweaves different colors and textures. 这幅挂毯交织了不同的颜色和纹理。|近义词：intertwine, interlace, blend, merge, combine|反义词：separate, disentangle, unravel|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *interweven*, from Old English *interwefan*, from *inter-* (between) + *wefan* (to weave).  Cognate with German *interweben*.
