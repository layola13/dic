leakiest: /ˈliːkiɪst/ | adj. | 漏水最多的，泄露最多的 | Having the most leaks or being most prone to leaking. | This is the leakiest boat I've ever been on. 这是我坐过的漏水最严重的船。 | The roof is the leakiest part of the house. 屋顶是房子里漏水最严重的地方。|近义词：most permeable, most porous |反义词: tightest, most impermeable |词性:形容词

词源:
From leak + -y + -est.  "Leak" comes from the Old Norse word "leka," meaning "to drip, to leak."  "-y" is a suffix that forms adjectives from nouns, indicating having the quality of, or full of. "-est" is a suffix forming the superlative degree of adjectives and adverbs.
