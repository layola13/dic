contenting:/kənˈtɛntɪŋ/
verb
使满足；使满意
satisfying or making someone satisfied
She contented herself with a single glass of wine. 她只喝了一杯葡萄酒就心满意足了。
近义词: satisfying, pleasing, gratifying
反义词: dissatisfying, displeasing


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *contenten*, from Old French *contenter* (modern French *contenir*), from Latin *contentus*, past participle of *continēre* (“to hold together, contain, restrain, confine, comprise, keep”), from *com-* (“together”) + *tenēre* (“to hold”).
