at-hand:/ætˈhænd/| adj.|手边的，近处的；即将发生的| readily available; close by; imminent|例句：The information you need is at hand. 你需要的信息就在手边。|近义词：available, accessible, near, close, approaching, imminent|反义词:distant, remote, far away|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *at hand, equivalent to at (preposition) + hand (noun).
