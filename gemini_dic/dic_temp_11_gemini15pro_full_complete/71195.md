fistula:/ˈfɪstʃʊlə/ (US), /ˈfɪstjʊlə/ (UK)| n. |瘘管；瘘|An abnormal passage or tube-like opening between two organs or between an organ and the surface of the body.|The patient developed a fistula after the surgery. 病人在手术后形成了一个瘘管。|Synonyms: channel, passage, tract|Antonyms: closure|Noun

fistula:/ˈfɪstʃʊlə/ (US), /ˈfɪstjʊlə/ (UK)| n. |通道；管道（植物）|A narrow tube-like passage in a plant.|Certain fungi form fistulas in their host plants. 某些真菌在其宿主植物中形成管道。|Synonyms: channel, duct|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
