nonpathologic:/ˌnɒnpæθəˈlɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 非病理性的；非病原的 | Not caused by disease; not indicative of disease.  |  Example: The doctor assured her that the lump was nonpathologic. 医生向她保证肿块是非病理性的。 | Example:  Nonpathologic changes in the body can occur due to aging.  身体的非病理性变化可能是由于衰老引起的。| Synonyms:  normal, healthy, benign, innocuous | Antonyms: pathologic, diseased, malignant | adjective

Etymology->
From non- + pathologic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Pathologic" comes from the Greek "pathos" (suffering, disease) + "-logos" (study, discourse).
