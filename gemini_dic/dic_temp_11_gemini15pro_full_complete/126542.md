nacker:/ˈnækə(r)/| n.| 屠马者；（尤指偷盗的）宰马商； knackered的缩写（表示“筋疲力尽的”）|A person who buys and slaughters worn-out horses, especially a dishonest dealer.  Also a shortened form of knackered (meaning exhausted).|例句：The old nacker came and took the lame horse away. 老屠马者来把跛马带走了。

例句：After that marathon, I'm absolutely knackered. 跑完马拉松后，我累垮了。|近义词：knackers (plural), horse butcher, horse slaughterer|反义词: 无|词性: 名词
