bugouts:/ˈbʌɡaʊts/
n.
紧急撤离；匆匆撤退；逃亡
A hurried departure; an escape.
The family made a bugout when the wildfire threatened their home.  这家人在野火威胁到他们的家时紧急撤离。
Preppers often have bugout bags ready in case of disaster.  准备者们通常会准备好逃生包以应对灾难。
escape, evacuation, flight
return
