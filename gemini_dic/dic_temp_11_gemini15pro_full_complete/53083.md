diphenyl:/daɪˈfɛnɪl/ /daɪˈfiːnəl/| n. |二苯基，联苯|A white crystalline hydrocarbon C12H10 consisting of two phenyl groups joined together. It is used in organic synthesis, as a heat-transfer agent, and in the manufacture of dyes, resins, and varnishes.|Diphenyl is commonly used in the manufacture of plastics. 二苯基常用于制造塑料。|Synonyms: biphenyl, phenylbenzene|Antonyms: None|noun

diphenyl:/daɪˈfɛnɪl/ /daɪˈfiːnəl/| adj. |二苯基的|Containing two phenyl groups.|Diphenyl ether is a colorless liquid. 二苯醚是一种无色液体。|Synonyms: biphenyl, phenylbenzene|Antonyms: None|adjective
