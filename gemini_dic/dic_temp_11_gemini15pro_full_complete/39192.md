congenialness:/kənˌdʒiːniˈæl.nəs/ |n.| 亲切；意气相投；适合性|The congenialness of the atmosphere made everyone feel welcome.  这种融洽的氛围让每个人都感到宾至如归。|例句：The congenialness of the group made the project a pleasure to work on.  团队的融洽使得这个项目开展起来非常愉快。

congenialness:/kənˌdʒiːniˈæl.nəs/|n.|和蔼可亲；性情相投；合宜|The congenialness of the climate suited her health.  这种宜人的气候对她的健康很有益。|例句：She appreciated the congenialness of her new colleagues. 她很欣赏新同事们的和蔼可亲。|近义词：agreeableness, affability, compatibility, pleasantness, suitability|反义词：unpleasantness, incompatibility, disagreeableness|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From congenial + -ness. Congenial comes from Late Latin congenialis "kindred, sympathetic," from com- "with, together" + genialis "of the same stock."
