dizzied:/ˈdɪzid/ | adj. | 头晕目眩的，感到困惑的 | Feeling lightheaded and unsteady, often caused by a spinning sensation; confused and disoriented. |  The roller coaster ride left me feeling completely dizzied.  过山车让我感到头晕目眩。

The sheer amount of information dizzied her.  大量的信息让她感到困惑。 | 近义词：lightheaded, giddy, faint, confused, bewildered | 反义词: steady, clearheaded, oriented | 词性:形容词

dizzy:/ˈdɪzi/ | adj. | 头晕目眩的，感到困惑的 | Feeling lightheaded and unsteady, often caused by a spinning sensation; confused and disoriented. |  The roller coaster ride left me feeling completely dizzy.  过山车让我感到头晕目眩。

The sheer amount of information made her dizzy.  大量的信息让她感到头晕。 | 近义词：lightheaded, giddy, faint, confused, bewildered | 反义词: steady, clearheaded, oriented | 词性:形容词


词源: From Old English *dysig, from Germanic *dusigaz, meaning "foolish, stupid."  The meaning shifted to "dizzy" in Middle English.
