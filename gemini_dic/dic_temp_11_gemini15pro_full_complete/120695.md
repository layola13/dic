misadvised:/ˌmɪsədˈvaɪzd/
adjective
中文翻译：不明智的；接受了错误建议的；被误导的
英文解析：Given bad advice; ill-advised.  Having received unwise or unsound advice.
例句：He made a misadvised investment that cost him dearly. 他做了一项不明智的投资，让他损失惨重。

例句：She was misadvised by her lawyer and lost the case.  她被她的律师误导了，输掉了官司。

近义词：ill-advised, unwise, imprudent, foolish
反义词：wise, prudent, judicious, well-advised
词性：adjective
