margent:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnt/| n.|边缘，页边空白，余地，差额，保证金|An edge; a border; a limit.  The blank space around the printed or written matter on a page.  An amount or degree by which a thing is won or falls short. A sum deposited with a broker to cover the risk of loss on a transaction.|The book had wide margins. 这本书有宽阔的页边空白。|Synonyms: border, edge, rim, verge, marginalia, leeway, room, space, allowance |Antonyms: center, middle, interior|noun


margent:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnt/| v. (obsolete)|加旁注于|To furnish with a margin; to enter in a margin. (obsolete)|He margented the page with notes. 他在页边空白处加了注释。(obsolete, rarely used)|Synonyms: annotate, comment, gloss|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for the verb form of margent)|verb
