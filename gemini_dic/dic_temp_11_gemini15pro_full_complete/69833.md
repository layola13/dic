While this phrase doesn't use "ferret,"  the imagery of a ferret asleep in its cage can be a humorous way to visualize someone "asleep at the switch".  Example:  "The security guard was asleep at the switch and the thieves got away with the jewels."  You could humorously say,  "He was as asleep as a ferret in its cage."
