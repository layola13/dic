effume:/ɪˈfjuːm/
verb
散发香气；冒烟
to give off fumes or vapor, especially with a pleasant smell.
The incense effumed a sweet fragrance.  香薰散发出一股甜香。
The volcano effumed a noxious gas. 火山散发出一股有毒气体。
近义词:emit, exhale, perfume
反义词:absorb, inhale
词性:v.

Etymology:
From Latin effumare, from ex- "out" + fumare "to smoke," from fumus "smoke."
