co-pilot:/ˈkoʊpaɪlət/ (US) /ˈkəʊpaɪlət/ (UK)| n.|副驾驶员；助手；领航员|A co-pilot is a pilot who helps the main pilot fly an aircraft.  A co-pilot can also refer to someone who assists another person in a task or project.|The co-pilot took over the controls when the captain became ill. 副驾驶在机长生病时接管了控制。

The CEO relies on his co-pilot to manage daily operations. 首席执行官依靠他的助手来管理日常运营。|近义词：assistant, helper, second in command|反义词: pilot (in the strict sense of being the primary pilot)|词性:noun
