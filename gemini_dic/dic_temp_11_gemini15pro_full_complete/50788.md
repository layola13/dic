descendant:/dɪˈsendənt/| n.|后裔，子孙，后代|A person who is descended from a particular ancestor.  A thing that has developed or evolved from something else.|例句：She is a descendant of Queen Victoria. 她是维多利亚女王的后裔。

例句：This programming language is a descendant of C.  这门编程语言是C语言的衍生。|近义词：offspring, progeny, successor, heir|反义词:ancestor, forebear|词性:noun
