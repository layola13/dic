circumjacent:/ˌsɜrkəmˈdʒeɪsənt/
adjective
周围的，邻近的
Surrounding; lying around.
The circumjacent fields were flooded after the heavy rain.  周围的田地在暴雨后被淹没了。
The king ruled over the city and all its circumjacent territories. 国王统治着这座城市及其周围的所有领土。

近义词: surrounding, neighboring, adjacent, adjoining
反义词: distant, remote
词性: adjective


词源:
From Latin circumjacentem, present participle of circumjacēre (“to lie around”), from circum (“around”) + jacēre (“to lie”).
