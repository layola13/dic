overbearingness:/ˌoʊvərˈberɪŋnɪs/ | n. |专横跋扈；傲慢；盛气凌人| The quality of being overbearing; arrogance or domineering behavior. |  His overbearingness alienated many of his colleagues. 他的专横跋扈疏远了许多同事。|  The CEO's overbearingness made it difficult for employees to share their ideas. 首席执行官的傲慢态度使员工难以分享他们的想法。 | synonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, domineeringness, imperiousness, high-handedness | antonyms: humility, modesty, meekness, submissiveness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From overbearing + -ness.  Overbearing itself comes from over- + bearing, with bearing meaning "demeanor" or "conduct."
