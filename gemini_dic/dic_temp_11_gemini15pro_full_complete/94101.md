impeachability:/ɪmˌpiːtʃəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可弹劾性；可指责性| The quality of being impeachable; liability to impeachment. | The president's lawyers argued against his impeachability. 总统的律师辩驳说他不应被弹劾。| synonym: liability, vulnerability | antonym: unimpeachability | noun

impeach:/ɪmˈpiːtʃ/ | v. | 弹劾；控告；质疑 | To charge (a public official) with misconduct in office before a competent tribunal.  To challenge the credibility or validity of. | The House of Representatives impeached the president. 众议院弹劾了总统。  The lawyer impeached the witness's testimony. 律师质疑证人的证词。| synonym: accuse, indict, question | antonym: exonerate, vindicate | verb
