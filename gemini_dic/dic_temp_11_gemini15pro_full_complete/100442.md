intertranspicuous:/ˌɪntərtrænsˈpɪkjuəs/
adjective
互相看得见的，透明的
mutually visible; transparent.  Allowing reciprocal view; such that whatever is on one side can be seen from the other, and vice versa.
例句：The glass walls of the office made it intertranspicuous, allowing everyone to see what everyone else was doing. 办公室的玻璃墙使其互相看得见，每个人都能看到其他人在做什么。

例句：The intertranspicuous nature of the aquarium allowed visitors to observe the marine life from all angles. 水族馆透明的特性使游客可以从各个角度观察海洋生物。
近义词：transparent, see-through, diaphanous, pellucid
反义词：opaque, obscure, hidden
词性：adjective
