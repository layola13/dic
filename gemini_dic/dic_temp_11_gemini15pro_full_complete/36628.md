collectivism: /kəˈlɛktɪvɪzəm/
n.
集体主义
A political or economic theory advocating collective control especially over production and distribution; also : a system marked by such control.  The principle or practice of prioritizing the needs and goals of a group over those of individuals.
例句：Collectivism is often contrasted with individualism. 集体主义常与个人主义对比。

例句：The kibbutz movement in Israel was founded on principles of collectivism.  以色列的基布兹运动建立在集体主义原则之上。
近义词：communalism, socialism, communism
反义词：individualism, libertarianism
词性：noun
