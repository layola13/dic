downscaling:/ˈdaʊnskeɪlɪŋ/
n.|缩减规模；降低规模；按比例缩小|The downscaling of the company led to many job losses. 该公司的规模缩减导致许多人失业。|synonyms: reduction, scaling back, downsizing|antonyms: upscaling, expansion, growth|noun

downscale:/ˈdaʊnskeɪl/
v.|缩小规模；降低规模；按比例缩小|The company decided to downscale its operations in Asia.  该公司决定缩减其在亚洲的业务。|synonyms: reduce, scale back, downsize|antonyms: upscale, expand, grow|verb
adj.|规模较小的；低档次的；面向低收入人群的|They're opening a new downscale supermarket aimed at budget shoppers. 他们正在开一家新的面向低收入人群的廉价超市。|synonyms: smaller, cheaper, budget, modest|antonyms: upscale, luxurious, premium, high-end|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
"down-" (prefix indicating reduction or decrease) + "scale" (from Old Norse "skal" - bowl, scale) + "-ing" (suffix forming present participle or noun)
