mogul:/ˈmoʊɡəl/| n.|大亨，巨头，显要人物|A powerful and influential person, especially in business or entertainment.|例句：He's a media mogul, owning several newspapers and television stations. 他是一位媒体大亨，拥有几家报纸和电视台。

例句：The Hollywood moguls were known for their lavish parties. 好莱坞的巨头们以其奢华的派对而闻名。|近义词：tycoon, magnate, baron, bigwig|反义词：nobody, underling|词性:noun
