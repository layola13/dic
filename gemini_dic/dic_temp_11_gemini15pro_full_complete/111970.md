lomentaceous:/ˌlɒmənˈteɪʃəs/ /ˌloʊmənˈteɪʃəs/
adjective
豆荚状的；分节的
Having a loment or loments; resembling a loment.  A loment is a type of fruit that breaks into one-seeded segments when mature.
The lomentaceous pods of the tick trefoil clung to my socks.  豆荚状的紫云英荚果粘在我的袜子上。

近义词:segmented, jointed, articulated
反义词:continuous, whole, unsegmented
词性:adjective
