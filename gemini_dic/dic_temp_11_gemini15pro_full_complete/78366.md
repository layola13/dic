gesticulate:/dʒɛˈstɪkjʊleɪt/
verb
做手势；用手势表达
make gestures, especially when speaking
The speaker gesticulated wildly, emphasizing his points. 演讲者疯狂地做着手势，强调他的观点。
近义词: gesture, signal, motion
反义词: be still, remain motionless
词性:v.

gesticulation:/dʒɛˌstɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/
noun
做手势；手势
the act of making gestures
Her animated gesticulations made her story even more engaging. 她生动的手势使她的故事更加引人入胜。
近义词: gesture, signaling, motioning
反义词: stillness, motionlessness
词性:n.
