jawline:/ˈdʒɔːlaɪn/ | n. | 下颌线，下巴轮廓 | The outline of the jaw. | He had a strong, chiseled jawline. 他有着棱角分明、轮廓清晰的下颌线。 |  She admired his sharp jawline. 她欣赏他清晰的下巴线条。| 近义词: jaw, chin | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From jaw + line.  "Jaw" comes from Old English "ceafl" meaning "jaw, jowl."  "Line" comes from Old French "ligne"  meaning "line, string."
