lra:/ˈɛl ɑːr ˈeɪ/ | abbreviation | 長期再授權協議 (Long-Run Average) | Long-Run Average, often used in economics and statistics to describe the average value of a variable over an extended period.  It helps to smooth out short-term fluctuations and reveal underlying trends. | The LRA of economic growth in the country is about 3%.  该国经济增长的长期平均值约为 3%。

The LRA for rainfall in this region is 25 inches per year. 该地区年降雨量的长期平均值为 25 英寸。| synonyms: long-term average, long-run mean | antonyms: short-run average, short-term average | n.
