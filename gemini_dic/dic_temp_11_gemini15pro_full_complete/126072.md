musths:/mʌsts/| n.|狂暴期，发情期|A period of heightened sexual activity and aggression in male elephants, characterized by a large secretion of a sticky fluid from the temporal glands.|例句：The bull elephant was in musth and extremely dangerous. 公象处于狂暴期，非常危险。

例句：During musths, elephants can be unpredictable and aggressive. 在狂暴期，大象的行为难以预测且具有攻击性。|近义词：rut, heat|反义词: docility, calmness|词性:名词

Etymology: From Hindi मस्त (mast, “intoxicated, in rut”), from Sanskrit मत्त (matta, “intoxicated, excited”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *medʰ- (“to get drunk, to be exhilarated”).
