hereto:/ˌhɪrˈtuː/ /ˌhɪrˈtuː/
adverb
至此，于此，关于此事
Relating to this document, matter, or subject.  Used in formal or legal contexts.
The parties hereto agree to the following terms.  双方在此同意以下条款。
The documents attached hereto form an integral part of this agreement.  附件构成本协议的组成部分。
近义词:herewith, herein, attached
反义词:thereto
词性:adverb
