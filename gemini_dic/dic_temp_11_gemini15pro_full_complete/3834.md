agonize:/ˈæɡənaɪz/| v. |感到极痛苦；苦苦思索|To suffer great pain or distress; to worry about something for a long time|She agonized over whether to tell him the truth. 她苦苦思索是否要告诉他真相。|Synonyms: worry, fret, struggle, suffer|Antonyms: relax, disregard, ignore|verb

agonize:/ˈæɡənaɪz/| v. |使极度痛苦|To cause to suffer agony|The thought of her child alone in the city agonized her. 让她孩子独自一人在城里让她感到极度痛苦。|Synonyms: torment, torture, distress|Antonyms: comfort, soothe, relieve|verb
