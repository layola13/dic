mantelletta:/ˌmæntəˈlɛtə/ | n. | 短披肩，披肩（尤指罗马天主教主教和高级教士所穿的）| A short cape or cloak, especially one worn by Roman Catholic prelates. | The bishop wore his mantelletta over his cassock. 主教在他的长袍外套上穿了短披肩。|  The cardinal's mantelletta was made of fine silk.  红衣主教的短披肩是用上等丝绸做的。 | synonyms: cape, cloak, mozzetta | antonyms: N/A | noun
