disabling:/dɪˈseɪblɪŋ/
adjective
使人衰弱的；使丧失能力的；令人难以忍受的
causing someone to be unable to do things easily or at all; causing serious difficulty or discomfort
例句：
The accident left him with a disabling injury.
这场事故使他留下了残疾。

She suffers from a disabling illness.
她患有一种使人丧失能力的疾病。


近义词：
crippling, incapacitating, debilitating
反义词：
enabling, empowering

词性
adjective
