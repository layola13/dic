andromeda:/ænˈdrɒmɪdə/ | n. | 仙女座；仙女座星系 | A large constellation in the northern hemisphere between Cassiopeia and Pegasus. It contains the Andromeda Galaxy. |  例句：The Andromeda galaxy is the closest major galaxy to our own Milky Way. 仙女座星系是我们银河系最近的主要星系。

例句：Andromeda is often depicted as a chained princess in Greek mythology.  在希腊神话中，仙女座经常被描绘成一个被锁链束缚的公主。| 近义词：galaxy (星系) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *Andromeda*, from Ancient Greek Ἀνδρομέδα (Androméda).  The name derives from  Ἀνήρ (Anḗr, “man”) + μέδω (médō, “I protect/rule”).
