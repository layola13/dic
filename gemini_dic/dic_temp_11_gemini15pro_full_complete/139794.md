octavo:/ɒkˈteɪvoʊ/ (US), /ɒkˈtɑːvəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|八开本；八开大小的|A size of book page resulting from folding each printed sheet into eight leaves (sixteen pages).  It also refers to a book made up of such pages.|This rare book is an octavo, making it a convenient size to hold. 这本珍本书是八开本的，拿起来很方便。|The octavo edition is more affordable than the quarto.  这本八开版的比四开版的更便宜。|近义词:book size, format|反义词:folio, quarto|词性:名词, 形容词
Etymology: From Latin *octāvus* (“eighth”), from *octō* (“eight”).
