craftsmanlike:/ˈkræftsmənˌlaɪk/| adj.|技艺精湛的，匠心独具的，精巧的|Resembling or characteristic of a skilled craftsperson; showing careful workmanship and attention to detail.|The furniture was crafted with craftsmanlike precision. 这件家具制作得非常精巧。|近义词：skillful, adept, expert, meticulous, precise|反义词：clumsy, amateurish, shoddy|词性:形容词

craftsman:/ˈkræftsmən/| n.|工匠，手艺人|A skilled worker who practices a craft.|My grandfather was a craftsman who made beautiful wooden toys. 我的祖父是一位手艺精湛的工匠，他制作漂亮的木制玩具。|近义词：artisan, artificer|反义词：amateur, novice|词性:名词

-like:/-laɪk/| suffix.|像…一样的，类似于…的|Used to form adjectives and adverbs meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," or "similar to."|The childlike innocence of her smile was captivating. 她孩子般天真的笑容很迷人。|近义词：similar to, resembling|反义词：unlike|词性:后缀

词源:
From "craftsman" + "-like." "Craftsman" comes from Old English "cræftsman," meaning "a man who practices a craft." "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives.
