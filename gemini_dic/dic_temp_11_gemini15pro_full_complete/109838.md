lexicologist:/ˌlɛksɪˈkɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌlɛksɪˈkɑːlədʒɪst/| n.|词汇学家；词典编纂者|A lexicologist studies words, their meanings, and how they are used in language. 词汇学家研究词汇、它们的含义以及它们在语言中的使用方式。|例句：The lexicologist dedicated her life to studying the etymology of words. 这位词汇学家毕生致力于研究词源。|例句：Lexicologists play a crucial role in compiling dictionaries. 词汇学家在编纂词典中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：linguist, philologist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek lexikos (relating to words) + -logist (one who studies). 源自希腊语 lexikos（与单词相关的）+ -logist（研究者）。
