dirges:/ˈdɜːrdʒɪz/
n.
挽歌；哀歌；悲歌
A dirge is a mournful song, piece of music, or poem, especially one expressing grief for the dead.
The choir sang a dirge for the fallen soldiers.  唱诗班为阵亡的士兵唱了一首挽歌。
The wind howled a mournful dirge through the trees. 风在树林中呼啸着，像一首悲伤的挽歌。
近义词:lament, elegy, requiem
反义词:celebration, anthem
词性:名词(plural)
