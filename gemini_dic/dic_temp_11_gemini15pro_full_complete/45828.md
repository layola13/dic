customises:/ˈkʌstəmaɪzɪz/
verb
定制，个性化
To modify something to suit a particular individual or task.
The company customises cars to meet customers' specifications. 公司根据客户的具体要求定制汽车。
personalizes, adapts, modifies, tailors
standardizes
