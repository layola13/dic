{italicised}  (Assuming the word is "what")

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pron. det. adv. int. | 什么，多么，何等 | Used to ask for information specifying something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ |  Synonyms: which, how, whatever | Antonyms: (No direct antonym for "what" as an interrogative pronoun) | pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? 她晚饭做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? 他整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie started? 电影开始前他们吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been talking about for so long? 他们一直在谈论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 你到年底会完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer? 到明年夏天他们会建成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
