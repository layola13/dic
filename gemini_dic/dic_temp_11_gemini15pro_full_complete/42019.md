It often involves detailed analysis of all expenses involved.|The costings for the new building project are still being finalized. 新建工程项目的成本核算仍在最后确定中。|Synonyms: calculations, estimations, budgeting, pricing|Antonyms: N/A (Costings is a process, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|noun
