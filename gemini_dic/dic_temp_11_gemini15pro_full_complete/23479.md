bragly: /ˈbræɡli/ | adj. | 吹嘘的，自夸的 | Given to bragging; boastful.  | He had a bragly manner that put people off. 他那种自吹自擂的态度让人反感。 | Example: She was rather bragly about her new car. 她对她的新车相当吹嘘。 | Synonyms: boastful, swaggering, vainglorious | Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From brag + -ly.  Brag itself likely comes from a Scandinavian source, possibly related to the Old Norse *braga, meaning "to shine, glitter."  The suffix -ly transforms the verb "brag" into an adjective.
