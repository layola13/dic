krill: /krɪl/ | n. |磷虾|Small, shrimp-like crustaceans, found in all oceans, forming a vital part of the food chain.|例句：Whales feed on massive quantities of krill.  鲸鱼以大量的磷虾为食。

例句：The krill population has declined due to overfishing. 由于过度捕捞，磷虾的数量已经减少。|近义词：plankton, crustacean |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific animal)|词性:noun
