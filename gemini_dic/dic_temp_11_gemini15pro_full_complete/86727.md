helmless:/ˈhɛlm.ləs/| adj.|无助的，没有方向的|Lacking a helm; without guidance or control; unable to manage.  Figuratively, it means feeling lost and directionless.|The helmless ship drifted aimlessly in the storm.  这艘无舵的船在暴风雨中漫无目的地漂流。|Synonyms: adrift, rudderless, lost, directionless, disoriented, bewildered|Antonyms: in control, directed, guided, confident, composed|词性:形容词
