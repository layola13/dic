词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *affrayen*, from Old French *esfreer* "to frighten," from *ex-* (out) + *frigus* (cold, shudder).  The sense evolved from causing someone to shudder from cold or fear to simply causing fear.
