militias:/məˈlɪʃəz/ | n. | 民兵组织；民兵队伍 | A body of citizens enrolled for military service, and called out periodically for drill but serving only in emergencies. | The militias were called up to defend the border. 民兵被召去保卫边境。| synonyms: armed forces,  irregular troops,  guerrilla force,  paramilitary force | antonyms: regular army, standing army | noun

militia:/məˈlɪʃə/ | n. | 民兵组织；民兵队伍 | A military force that is raised from the civil population to supplement a regular army in an emergency. | The state militia was activated during the flood. 州民兵在洪水期间被激活。| synonyms:  national guard, reserve forces,  citizen army | antonyms: regular army, standing army | noun
