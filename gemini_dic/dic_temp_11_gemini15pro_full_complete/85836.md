HBr:/ˌeɪtʃ biːˈɑːr/ | n. | 溴化氢;氢溴酸 | Hydrogen bromide is a diatomic molecule with the formula HBr. It is a colorless gas that dissolves in water, forming hydrobromic acid. | Hydrogen bromide is used in the manufacture of other bromides. 溴化氢用于制造其他溴化物。| synonyms: hydrogen bromide, anhydrous hydrobromic acid | antonyms: None | noun
