frustrated:/ˈfrʌstreɪtɪd/| adj.| 沮丧的，挫败的，失意的| Feeling or expressing distress and annoyance, especially because of inability to change or achieve something.| I felt frustrated when I couldn't solve the puzzle. 我无法解开谜题时感到很沮丧。

She was frustrated by the lack of progress.  她因缺乏进展而感到沮丧。

近义词：disappointed, discouraged, thwarted, defeated|反义词：satisfied, content, fulfilled|词性:形容词
