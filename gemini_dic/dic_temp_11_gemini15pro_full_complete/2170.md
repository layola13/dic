adderwort:/ˈædərˌwɜːrt/| n.| 蛇床，石龙芮|A European plant (*Persicaria bistorta*) of the knotweed family, with spikes of pink flowers and thick, twisted rootstocks.|The adderwort's root was thought to protect against adder bites. 蛇床的根被认为可以防止毒蛇咬伤。|Synonyms: bistort, snakeweed|Antonyms: None|Noun
