decamps:/dɪˈkæmps/
verb
撤营; 突然离开; 逃走
To leave suddenly or secretly, typically to avoid arrest or detection.
The suspect decamped to Mexico.  嫌疑犯逃到了墨西哥。
The circus decamped overnight.  马戏团连夜撤走了。
近义词: flee, escape, abscond, depart, leave
反义词: arrive, stay, remain
词性: verb
