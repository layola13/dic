diphyodont:/daɪˈfaɪəˌdɑːnt/ | adj. n. | 双齿型的，换牙的；双齿动物 | Having two successive sets of teeth, one succeeding the other. (Having a deciduous and a permanent dentition) | Most mammals are diphyodonts, meaning they have baby teeth and adult teeth. 大多数哺乳动物都是双齿动物，这意味着它们有乳牙和恒牙。|  Humans are diphyodont. 人类是双齿动物。 | synonyms: heterodont | antonyms: monophyodont | adjective, noun
