chorology:/kəˈrɒlədʒi/ /kɔːˈrɑːlədʒi/
n.
生物分布学；区域生态学
The study of the spatial distribution of organisms.
Chorology helps us understand why certain species are found in specific locations. 生物分布学帮助我们理解为什么某些物种出现在特定的地点。
The chorology of this rare plant is limited to a few mountain peaks. 这种稀有植物的分布仅限于几座山峰。
近义词: biogeography, phytogeography (for plants), zoogeography (for animals)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


Etymology:  From Greek *khōros* "space, region" + *-logia* "study of".
