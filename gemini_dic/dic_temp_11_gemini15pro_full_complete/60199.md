effigy:/ˈefɪdʒi/| n. |肖像，雕像；模拟像（尤指用于象征性惩罚的）|A crude figure representing a hated person. |例句：They burned an effigy of the president. 他们焚烧了总统的肖像。

例句：The protesters hung an effigy of the dictator. 抗议者悬挂了独裁者的模拟像。|近义词：image, likeness, figure, statue, dummy |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "original" in some contexts) |词性:noun
