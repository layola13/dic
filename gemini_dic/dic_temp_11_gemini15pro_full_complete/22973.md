bottone:/bɔˈtːoːne/ (Italian), /bəˈtoʊneɪ/ (English approximation)| n.|纽扣，按钮|(From Italian *bottone* meaning "bud," from Latin *bottō*, *buttō* "thick swelling, bud.") A small, usually round, disc or knob sewn onto clothing and used as a fastener, or a similar device used to activate something, such as an electrical switch.|She lost a button from her coat. 她外套上掉了一颗纽扣。

The button on the elevator was stuck. 电梯的按钮卡住了。

|近义词：fastener, switch, knob|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
