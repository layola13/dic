leptostraca:/ˌlɛptəˈstrɑːkə/| n. | 薄甲目 | An order of small, marine crustaceans characterized by a bivalved carapace enclosing the head and thorax, and a segmented abdomen ending in a caudal furca.  They are considered to be the most primitive living malacostracans. | Leptostraca are found in marine environments worldwide. 薄甲目在世界各地的海洋环境中都有发现。|  Leptostracans are an important part of the food chain. 薄甲目是食物链的重要组成部分。 | synonyms: Nebaliacea | antonyms: N/A | noun
