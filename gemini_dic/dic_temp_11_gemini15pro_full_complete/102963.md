jealousy: /ˈdʒeləsi/ | n. | 嫉妒，妒忌 | A feeling of resentment against someone because of their success, possessions, advantages, etc. | She was consumed by jealousy when she saw her rival's success. 她看到对手的成功，心中充满了嫉妒。|  envy, resentment, covetousness | admiration, goodwill | noun

jealoushood:  This word is not standard English. The correct word is "jealousy".
