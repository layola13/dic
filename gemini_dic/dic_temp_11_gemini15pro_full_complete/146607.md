overgrossly:/ˌoʊvərˈɡroʊsli/ | adv. | 过度地，极其地 | Excessively, to an excessive degree; in a way that is too large or too much.  It combines "over" (meaning excessively) and "grossly" (meaning in a vulgar or offensive manner, or excessively). While "grossly" can sometimes have negative connotations, "overgrossly" primarily emphasizes excessiveness. | The steak was overgrossly salted and inedible. 牛排盐放得太多了，不能吃。| The charity was overgrossly funded, raising concerns about mismanagement.  这个慈善机构资金过多，引发了对管理不善的担忧。 | 近义词: excessively, exorbitantly, unduly | 反义词: moderately, slightly, subtly | 词性: 副词
