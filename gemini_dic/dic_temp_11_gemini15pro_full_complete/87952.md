high-commissioner:/ˌhaɪ kəˈmɪʃənər/ | n. |高级专员|A diplomatic official of higher rank than an ambassador, typically representing one Commonwealth country in another. | The Australian High Commissioner to the United Kingdom attended the ceremony. | 澳大利亚驻英国高级专员出席了仪式。 |Synonym: ambassador, envoy, diplomat | Antonym: N/A | noun

high-commissioners:/ˌhaɪ kəˈmɪʃənərz/ | n. |高级专员们 (high-commissioner 的复数形式)|Plural form of high-commissioner.
