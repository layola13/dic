flavoured:/ˈfleɪvəd/ (US) /ˈfleɪvəd/ (UK)| adj.|加味的；有…味道的|Having a particular flavor added.|例句：The tea is flavoured with bergamot.  这茶是用佛手柑调味的。|近义词：flavored, seasoned, spiced|反义词:unflavored, bland, tasteless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From flavor + -ed.  "Flavor" comes from Old French *flaur, from Latin *flator, from flare "to blow." The "-ed" suffix is a common ending used to form adjectives from verbs or nouns, indicating a state or quality.
