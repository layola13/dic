arrestors:/əˈrɛstərz/
noun
避雷器；抑制器；阻尼器
A device used to protect electrical equipment from voltage surges, especially lightning strikes.  They divert excess voltage to the ground.
The power station is protected by lightning arrestors.  发电站装有避雷器。
Synonyms: surge protector, surge arrester, lightning arrester, voltage limiter
Antonyms: conductor
