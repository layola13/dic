outplaying:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪɪŋ/
verb
胜过，在游戏中击败
To perform better than another player or team in a game or sport.
He outplayed his opponent in the final set. 他在最后一局胜过了他的对手。
近义词: outmaneuver, surpass, outperform
反义词: underperform, lose to
词性: verb

反义词: be outsmarted, be outwitted
