condemningly:/kənˈdemɪŋli/
adverb
谴责地，批判地
in a way that strongly disapproves of someone or something.
He shook his head condemningly. 他谴责地摇了摇头。
Synonyms: disapprovingly, critically, censoriously
Antonyms: approvingly, supportively
词性: 副词
