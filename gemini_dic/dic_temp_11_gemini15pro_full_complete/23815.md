braziletto:/ˌbræzɪˈlɛtoʊ/
n.
巴西木；苏木
A reddish wood from several tropical trees, used in dyeing and cabinetmaking.
The artisan crafted a beautiful table from braziletto wood.  工匠用巴西木制作了一张漂亮的桌子。
Synonyms: brazilwood, Pernambuco wood
Antonyms: N/A
Noun


braziletto:/ˌbræzɪˈlɛtoʊ/
n.
苏木色；苏木染料
A red dye or pigment obtained from braziletto wood.
The artist used a rich braziletto dye to create the vibrant sunset.  艺术家用浓郁的苏木染料描绘了鲜艳的日落。
Synonyms: brazilwood dye
Antonyms: N/A
Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *brasileto*, diminutive of *brasil* ("Brazil"), from the color of embers (*brasa*) and ultimately from Medieval Latin *brasa* ("ember"). The wood, being reddish, was likened to the color of embers.
