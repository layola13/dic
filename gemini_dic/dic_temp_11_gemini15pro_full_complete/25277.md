buffoonery:/bəˈfuːnəri/
n.
滑稽可笑的行為；丑角的表演
Foolish or childish behavior.  Silly or ridiculous activities.
The crowd roared with laughter at his buffoonery.  观众被他滑稽的动作逗得哄堂大笑。
近义词：clowning, antics, tomfoolery, silliness, absurdity
反义词：seriousness, solemnity, dignity, gravity
词性: noun

Etymology:
From French "bouffonnerie," from "bouffon" (buffoon), from Italian "buffone" (clown, jester), possibly from "buffa" (jest, joke).  Ultimately of uncertain origin.
