overnormalities:/ˌoʊvərˌnɔːrˈmælɪtiz/ | n. | 过度正常化；过度规范化 |  The state of being excessively or rigidly normal, often to the point of appearing unnatural or artificial.  This can refer to behaviors, appearances, or social structures that adhere strictly to perceived norms, sometimes at the expense of individuality, creativity, or genuine human connection. | The community's overnormalities stifled any expression of individuality. 这个社区的过度规范扼杀了任何个性表达。 |  conformity, standardization, rigidity | eccentricity, deviance, nonconformity | noun
