browsable:/ˈbraʊzəbl/| adj.|可浏览的，可随意翻阅的|Suitable or easy to browse through.  Something that can be examined or perused casually. |例句：The online archive is easily browsable.  这个在线档案很容易浏览。

例句：The website has a browsable catalog of products. 该网站有一个可浏览的产品目录。|近义词：searchable, accessible, navigable, scannable|反义词：inaccessible, unsearchable, restricted|词性:形容词

词源: From "browse" (meaning to graze or feed on foliage, and later, to look through casually) + "-able" (suffix meaning capable of being). Ultimately from Old French "brouster" (to sprout or bud).
