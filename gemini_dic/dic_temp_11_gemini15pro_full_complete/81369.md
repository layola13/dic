grappler:/ˈɡræplər/| n.|格斗者；摔跤选手；抓住…的人|A person who grapples, especially in wrestling or in a fight.|The grappler quickly subdued his opponent.  这位格斗者迅速制服了他的对手。|Synonyms: wrestler, fighter, combatant|Antonyms: pacifist|noun

grapple:/ˈɡræpəl/| v.|格斗，搏斗；努力解决（难题）|To engage in a close fight or struggle, especially one involving wrestling or the use of holds; to struggle with or work hard to deal with or overcome (a difficulty or problem).|He grappled with the burglar. 他与窃贼搏斗。|Synonyms: wrestle, contend, struggle|Antonyms: surrender, yield|verb
