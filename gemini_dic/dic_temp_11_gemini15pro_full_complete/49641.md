demilitarising:/diːˈmɪlɪtəraɪzɪŋ/
verb
使非军事化，解除武装
The act of removing military forces, weapons, or fortifications from a region or country, or prohibiting their presence there.
The two countries agreed on demilitarising the border zone. 这两个国家同意将边境地区非军事化。
synonyms: disarming, neutralising
antonyms: militarising, arming
词性: 动词 (present participle)

demilitarisation:/diːˌmɪlɪtəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
非军事化，解除武装
The action or process of demilitarising a region or country.
The demilitarisation of the area was a key part of the peace agreement.  该地区的非军事化是和平协议的关键部分。
synonyms: disarmament, neutralisation
antonyms: militarisation, armament
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + military + -ise/-ize (suffix forming verbs) + -ing (present participle suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *militaris ("of soldiers").
