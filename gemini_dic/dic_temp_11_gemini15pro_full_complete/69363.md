federator:/ˈfɛdəˌreɪtər/| n.| 联合者，联邦主义者|A person or organization that unites separate states or groups into a federation or league.|The United Nations acts as a federator for many countries around the world. 联合国充当着世界许多国家的联合组织。|Synonyms: unifier, consolidator, league, alliance|Antonyms: divider, separator|noun

federate:/ˈfɛdəˌreɪt/ |v. | 联合，组成联邦|To unite separate states or groups into a federation or league.|The thirteen colonies federated to form the United States of America. 十三个殖民地联合起来组成了美利坚合众国。|Synonyms: unite, join, ally, combine|Antonyms: divide, separate, disunite|verb
