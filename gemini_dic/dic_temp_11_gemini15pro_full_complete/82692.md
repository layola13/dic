guey:/ɡweɪ/ | n. | 伙计，老兄，哥们 | A colloquial term used primarily in Mexican Spanish to refer to a man or guy. It can also be used as a general term of address, similar to "dude" or "man" in English. | Example: Oye, guey, ¿qué onda? = Hey, dude, what's up? | Synonyms: dude, man, pal, buddy, fella | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | Noun


guey:/ɡweɪ/ | adj. |  (Informal, often derogatory) foolish, stupid, clueless |  Used to describe someone as foolish, stupid or clueless. This usage can be considered offensive. | Example: ¡No seas guey! = Don't be stupid! | Synonyms: foolish, stupid, silly, dumb, clueless | Antonyms: smart, intelligent, wise | Adjective
