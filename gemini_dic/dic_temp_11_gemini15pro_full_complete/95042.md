inactivating:/ɪnˈæktɪveɪtɪŋ/
adjective
使不活跃的；使失效的；灭活的
making something inactive or no longer effective.
The virus is inactivated by heat.  热能使病毒失效。
The vaccine contains inactivated viruses. 疫苗包含灭活病毒。
synonyms: deactivating, neutralizing, disabling
antonyms: activating, enabling
词性: adjective


inactivate:/ɪnˈæktɪveɪt/
verb
使不活跃；使失效；灭活
to make something inactive or no longer effective.
The enzyme is inactivated at high temperatures. 高温会使酶失活。
The security system was inactivated during the power outage.  停电期间，安全系统失效了。
synonyms: deactivate, neutralize, disable
antonyms: activate, enable
词性: verb
