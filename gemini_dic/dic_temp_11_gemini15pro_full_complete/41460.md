corniferous:/kɔːrnɪˈfɛrəs/
adjective
含玉米的；产玉米的；[地质学]含角珊瑚的
containing or yielding corn; of or relating to the Corniferous period or formation of the Devonian period in eastern North America, characterized by horn corals
The corniferous limestone contains numerous fossils. 含角珊瑚的石灰岩包含大量的化石。
Near synonyms: corn-bearing, corn-producing, Devonian
Antonyms: barren, infertile
