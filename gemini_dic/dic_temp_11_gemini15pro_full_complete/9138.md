antinarrative:/ˈæntɪˈnærətɪv/| n.|反叙事，非叙事|A type of narrative that deliberately avoids the conventions of traditional storytelling, often rejecting chronological order, clear causality, or a single, unified perspective.  It often fragments the story, emphasizes ambiguity, and challenges the audience's expectations.|The film's antinarrative structure made it difficult to follow, but ultimately rewarding. 这部电影的反叙事结构让它很难理解，但最终却很有意义。|Synonyms: nonlinear narrative, fragmented narrative, experimental narrative|Antonyms: linear narrative, conventional narrative|noun
