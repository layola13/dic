compellable:/kəmˈpɛləbəl/ | adj. | 可强迫的，可迫使的 | Able to be compelled or forced to do something.  Subject to compulsion. | The witness was compellable to appear in court.  证人不得不/必须出庭作证。 | The defendant was compellable to provide a DNA sample. 被告不得不/必须提供DNA样本。 | synonyms:obligatory, mandatory, required, compulsory | antonyms:optional, voluntary, discretionary | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English compellable, from Anglo-Norman compellable, from Medieval Latin compellābilis, from Latin compellere (to compel).
