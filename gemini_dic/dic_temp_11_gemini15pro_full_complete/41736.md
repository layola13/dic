corrosion:/kəˈroʊʒən/ | n. | 腐蚀，侵蚀 | the process of destroying or damaging something slowly, especially by chemical action | The bridge's steel supports showed signs of corrosion. 桥的钢支架显示出腐蚀的迹象。 | synonyms: erosion, decay, deterioration | antonyms: preservation, protection | noun

corrosion:/kəˈrəʊʒən/ | n. | 腐蚀，侵蚀 | the process of destroying or damaging something slowly, especially by chemical action |  The salty air caused corrosion of the metal parts. 咸空气导致金属部件腐蚀。 | synonyms: rust, disintegration, decomposition | antonyms: strengthening, reinforcement | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *corrosiōn- (stem of corrosio ), from corrodere "to gnaw to pieces," from com- "together" + rodere "to gnaw."
