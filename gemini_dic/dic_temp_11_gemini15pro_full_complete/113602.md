macerate:/ˈmæsəreɪt/| v. |浸泡,软化|To soften or break down by soaking in a liquid.  The chef macerated the dried fruit in brandy overnight. 厨师将干果在白兰地中浸泡了一夜。|近义词：soak, steep, soften|反义词:harden, dry|verb
macerates:/ˈmæsəreɪts/| v. |浸泡,软化(第三人称单数)|Third-person singular simple present form of the verb "macerate". He macerates the fruit in wine before using it in the cake. 他在蛋糕中使用水果之前，先将其在葡萄酒中浸泡。|近义词：soaks, steeps, softens|反义词:hardens, dries|verb
