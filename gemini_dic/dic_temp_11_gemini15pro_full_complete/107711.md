lanthanite:/ˈlænθəˌnaɪt/| n.|镧石|A hydrated lanthanum carbonate mineral, typically occurring as pinkish, grayish, or yellowish earthy masses.  It is a source of lanthanum.|Lanthanite is often found in association with other rare earth minerals. 镧石常与其他稀土矿物伴生。|The museum's collection includes a beautiful specimen of lanthanite.  博物馆的收藏包括一件美丽的镧石标本。|Synonyms: none (as it's a specific mineral name) |Antonyms: N/A|noun
