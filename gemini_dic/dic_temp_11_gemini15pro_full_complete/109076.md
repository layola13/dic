legatees:/ˌlɛɡəˈtiːz/
n.
遗产继承人
A person or entity to whom a legacy is bequeathed.
The legatees will receive their inheritance after the reading of the will. 遗产继承人将在宣读遗嘱后获得他们的遗产。
beneficiaries, heirs, inheritors
executors, administrators
noun
