exploiting: /ɪkˈsplɔɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 利用; 剥削; 开发; | making full use of a resource or situation in a way considered unfair or selfish; using something in a way to get an advantage |  The company is accused of exploiting its workers by paying them low wages.  这家公司被指控剥削工人，支付低工资。

The company is exploiting new technology to increase productivity. 这家公司正在利用新技术来提高生产力。 | 近义词: utilizing, harnessing, taking advantage of, capitalizing on, abusing | 反义词:  squandering, neglecting, helping, supporting | 词性: 动词 (present participle of exploit)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *esploitier, from Latin explicitus "unfolded, set forth," past participle of explicare "to unfold, explain"
