neeps:/niːps/| n. |芜菁；瑞典芜菁 (尤指苏格兰英语)|A root vegetable, similar to a turnip, commonly eaten in Scotland.  Specifically, it refers to the white-fleshed varieties of turnip (Brassica rapa subsp. rapa).|例句：We had neeps and tatties for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了芜菁和土豆。

例句：Neeps are a staple in traditional Scottish cuisine. 芜菁是苏格兰传统美食中的主食。|近义词：turnip, swede (in some contexts)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps other root vegetables like carrots or parsnips could be considered contrasting options depending on the context)|词性:noun
