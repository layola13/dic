lazies:/ˈleɪziz/
n.
懒汉，懒鬼
People who are lazy; idlers.
Those lazies never do any work. 那些懒鬼从来不干活。
近义词: idlers, loafers, slackers
反义词: workers, busybodies
词性: 名词

词源:  From "lazy" + "-ies" (plural suffix often used informally).  Ultimately from Middle English *lazi*, of uncertain origin.
