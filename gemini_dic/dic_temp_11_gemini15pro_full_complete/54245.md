disgorgement:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒmənt/
n.|吐出；呕出；被迫交出，返还（非法所得）|The act of emptying out or releasing something, especially from the stomach or a container; the forced giving up of profits obtained illegally.|The volcano disgorged molten lava. 火山喷出了熔岩。|近义词：vomiting, expulsion, ejection, surrender, restitution|反义词：ingestion, retention, acquisition|词性:noun

disgorge:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒ/
v.|吐出；呕出；被迫交出，返还（非法所得）|To empty out or release something, especially from the stomach or a container; to be forced to give up profits obtained illegally.|The company was forced to disgorge its ill-gotten gains. 公司被迫交出非法所得。|近义词：vomit, expel, eject, surrender, return|反义词：ingest, retain, acquire|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "desgorger"  meaning "to clear the throat", from "des-" (meaning "out, away") + "gorger" (meaning "throat").  The word's meaning later expanded to include emptying anything out.
