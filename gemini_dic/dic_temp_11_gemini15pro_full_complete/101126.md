investigable:/ɪnˈvɛstɪɡəbl/| adj.|可调查的，可研究的|Able to be investigated; capable of being investigated.|The police said the death was investigable. 警方表示这起死亡事件是可以调查的。|Example: The cause of the fire is investigable. 火灾原因是可以调查的。|Synonyms: researchable, explorable, examinable, scrutable|Antonyms: unexaminable, inscrutable|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin investigabilis, from Latin investigare "to track, trace out," from in- "in, into" + vestigare "to track, trace," from vestigium "footprint, trace."
