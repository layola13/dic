etherish:/iˈθɪərɪʃ/
adjective
轻飘飘的，飘渺的；虚无缥缈的；超凡脱俗的
Light, airy, or delicate; intangible; unearthly.
She wore an etherish white gown that seemed to float around her. 她穿着一件飘逸的白色长袍，仿佛漂浮在她周围。
The music had an etherish quality that transported the listeners to another world. 这首音乐有一种空灵的特质，将听众带到了另一个世界。
近义词:airy, ethereal, delicate, ghostly, intangible, spiritual, otherworldly
反义词:earthy, substantial, concrete, material, physical
词性:adjective
