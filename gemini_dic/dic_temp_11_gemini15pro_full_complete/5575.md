altair: /ælˈtaɪər/ | n. | 牛郎星; 牵牛星 | A bright star in the constellation Aquila, the Eagle.  It is one of the closest stars visible to the naked eye. |例句：Altair is part of the Summer Triangle asterism. 牛郎星是夏季大三角星群的一部分。

例句：Altair is easily visible in the night sky during the summer months. 在夏季的夜空中很容易看到牛郎星。 | 近义词: Alpha Aquilae (正式名称), Atair (变体) | 反义词: None (恒星没有反义词) | 词性: noun
