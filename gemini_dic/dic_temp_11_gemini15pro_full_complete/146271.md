overfaithful: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪθfʊl/
adjective
过于忠诚的，过分忠实的
Excessively faithful or loyal, often to the point of being excessive or unnecessary.
She was overfaithful to her husband, even when he was clearly in the wrong. 她对她的丈夫过于忠诚，即使他明显是错的。
His overfaithful dog followed him everywhere, even to the bathroom. 他那只过于忠诚的狗跟着他到处走，甚至连浴室也不放过。
近义词: devoted, excessively loyal
反义词: unfaithful, disloyal
词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "faithful" (adjective meaning "loyal and steadfast")
