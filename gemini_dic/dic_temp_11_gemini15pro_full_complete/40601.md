convection:/kənˈvekʃən/
|n.|对流，传送，运流|The transfer of heat through a fluid (liquid or gas) caused by molecular motion.|Heat is transferred through the water by convection. 热量通过对流在水中传递。|近义词:transmission, transfer, advection|反义词:conduction, radiation|词性:名词
convections:/kənˈvekʃənz/
|n.|对流（convection的复数）|Multiple instances of the transfer of heat through a fluid (liquid or gas) caused by molecular motion.|The atmospheric convections caused the storm. 大气对流引发了风暴。|近义词:transmissions, transfers|反义词:conductions, radiations|词性:名词
