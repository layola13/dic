intermeshing:/ˌɪntərˈmɛʃɪŋ/
adjective
相互啮合的，互相交错的
(of parts of a machine or system) having teeth or other projections that fit into each other.
The gears are intermeshing smoothly. 齿轮平稳地啮合着。
近义词:interlocking, meshing, engaging
反义词:disengaging, separating
词性:adjective

intermesh:/ˌɪntərˈmɛʃ/
verb
(使)互相啮合，(使)互相交错
become or cause to become engaged or entangled with something else.
The cogs intermesh to drive the mechanism. 齿轮相互啮合以驱动机构。
近义词:interlock, mesh, engage
反义词:disengage, separate
词性:verb

intermesh:/ˌɪntərˈmɛʃ/
noun
相互啮合，互相交错
the act of intermeshing or the state of being intermeshed.
The intermesh of the gears ensures smooth operation. 齿轮的相互啮合确保了平稳运行。
近义词:interlocking, meshing, engagement
反义词:disengagement, separation
词性:noun
