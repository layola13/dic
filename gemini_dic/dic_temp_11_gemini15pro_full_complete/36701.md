colliquament:/ˌkɒlɪˈkwɑːmənt/ |n.|胚胎液，胚胎的原生质|The colliquament is the fluid surrounding the embryo in the amniotic sac. 胚胎液是羊膜囊中包围胚胎的液体。|The developing chick floats in the colliquament. 发育中的小鸡漂浮在胚胎液中。|近义词: amniotic fluid, embryonic fluid|反义词: None|Noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *colliquamentum, from Latin colliquāre (“to melt, dissolve, become liquid”), from com- + liquāre (“to liquefy”), from liquidus (“liquid”).
