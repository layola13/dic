noncounties:/ˌnɒnˈkaʊntiz/ | n. | 非县 | Areas or regions that are not designated as counties.  This typically refers to independent cities or other administrative divisions that exist outside the county system. |  Many large cities in the U.S. are noncounties, operating independently of any county government. 美国许多大城市都是非县，独立于任何县政府运作。

Noncounties often have their own police and fire departments. 非县通常拥有自己的警察和消防部门。 | near synonyms: independent cities, unincorporated areas | antonym: counties | noun
