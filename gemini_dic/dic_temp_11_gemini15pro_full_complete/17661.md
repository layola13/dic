beckoning:/ˈbekənɪŋ/| adj.| 诱人的；召唤的；吸引人的 | Appealing and inviting; attractive.  Making you want to go closer or do something. | The beckoning lights of the city called to her. 城市诱人的灯光在召唤着她。 | enticing, alluring, tempting, inviting | repellent, off-putting | adjective

beckon:/ˈbekən/| v.| 招手；示意；召唤 | To signal or summon, typically with a wave or nod. | He beckoned her over with a smile. 他笑着向她招手。| signal, summon, gesture, wave | repel, dismiss | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *beacnian "to make a sign, signal," related to beacon.  The original sense was "to make a sign by nodding or making a gesture with the finger or hand."
