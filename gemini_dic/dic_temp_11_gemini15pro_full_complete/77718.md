gemmiflorate:/ˌdʒɛmɪˈflɔːreɪt/
adjective
簇生的，芽状开花的
Bearing flowers in bud-like clusters.  Specifically, in botany, having the flower buds enveloped by imbricated bracts.
The gemmiflorate inflorescence of the plant was unique and captivating.  这种植物的簇生花序独特而迷人。
Synonyms: clustered, fasciculate
Antonyms: solitary, separate
adjective
