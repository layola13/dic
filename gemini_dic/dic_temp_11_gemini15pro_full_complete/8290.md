anti-Malthusianism:/ˌænti mælˈθuːziənɪzəm/ | n. | 反马尔萨斯主义 |  The belief that Thomas Robert Malthus's theory of population growth outstripping food supply is incorrect, or that it has been overcome by technological advancements.  |  Anti-Malthusianism gained traction in the late 20th century due to the Green Revolution.  反马尔萨斯主义在20世纪后期由于绿色革命而受到关注。| 近义词: Cornucopianism | 反义词: Malthusianism | 词性: 名词

anti-Malthusian:/ˌænti mælˈθuːziən/ | adj. | 反马尔萨斯主义的 | Relating to or characteristic of anti-Malthusianism; opposing Malthus's theory of population growth. |  He presented an anti-Malthusian argument for increased food production through technology. 他提出了一个通过技术增加粮食产量的反马尔萨斯主义论点。 | 近义词: Cornucopian | 反义词: Malthusian | 词性: 形容词
