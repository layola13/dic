The teacher sent a note home about the child's misbehavior in class. 老师因为孩子在课堂上的不当行为给家长发了通知。

The school has a zero-tolerance policy for misbehavior. 学校对不良行为采取零容忍政策。

|近义词：misconduct, wrongdoing, delinquency, bad behavior |反义词：good behavior, obedience |词性: noun
