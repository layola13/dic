fugleman:/ˈfjuːɡəlmən/| n.|领队，先导，带头人|A soldier, formerly one who acted as a guide or example in drill to others; hence, one who takes the lead in any movement or enterprise.|The CEO acted as the fugleman for the company's new initiative.|近义词：leader, pioneer, vanguard, frontman|反义词:follower|词性:noun

Etymology:
From German *Fügelmann* "wingman," from *Flügel* "wing" + *Mann* "man." Originally referred to a soldier who stood in front of a formation to demonstrate the movements to be performed during drill.
