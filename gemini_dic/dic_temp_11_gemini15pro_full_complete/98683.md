PHRASE->
add insult to injury = 雪上加霜
It was bad enough that he lost his job, but to add insult to injury, his car was stolen the next day. 他丢了工作已经够糟糕的了，但更糟糕的是，第二天他的车又被偷了。
swallow one's insults = 忍气吞声
He had to swallow his insults and pretend he didn't hear them. 他不得不忍气吞声，假装没听见那些侮辱。
hurl insults at someone = 对某人破口大骂
The protestors hurled insults at the police. 抗议者对警察破口大骂。
a grave insult = 严重的侮辱
His remarks were considered a grave insult to the entire community. 他的言论被认为是对整个社区的严重侮辱。
trade insults =  互相辱骂
The two politicians traded insults on live television. 两位政客在电视直播中互相辱骂。
