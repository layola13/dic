excavated:/ˈekskəveɪtɪd/| adj. |挖掘的，开凿的|Having been dug or hollowed out.  The archaeologists discovered an excavated tomb. 考古学家发现了一座被挖掘的坟墓。|近义词：unearthed, dug up, hollowed out|反义词:filled, buried|adjective

excavate:/ˈekskəveɪt/| v. |挖掘，开凿|To dig or hollow out earth, especially to find something.  They excavated the site for dinosaur fossils. 他们在遗址挖掘恐龙化石。|近义词：unearth, dig up, disinter|反义词:bury, fill|verb

词源: From Latin *excavātus*, past participle of *excavāre* "to hollow out," from *ex-* "out" + *cavāre* "to hollow," related to *cavus* "hollow."
