growthead:/ˈɡroʊθˌhɛd/
n.|增长头; 增长点|A leading sector, area, or product in terms of growth or development.  A person or group that promotes growth or development. |The tech industry is a significant growthead for the economy.|近义词:growth area, growth sector, driver of growth, engine of growth|反义词: declining sector, stagnant area|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
"growth" (from Old English "grōwþ," meaning "the process of growing") + "head" (from Old English "hēafod," meaning "head," referring to something leading or prominent). The term likely emerged by analogy to other compound words like "figurehead" or "spearhead."
