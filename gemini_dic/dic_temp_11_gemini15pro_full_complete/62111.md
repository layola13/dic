enchase:/ɪnˈtʃeɪs/ /ɛnˈtʃeɪs/
verb
镶嵌，嵌入；装饰
To decorate (something) by setting it with precious stones or other ornaments; to enclose in or as if in a frame.
The jeweler enchased the diamond in a gold setting. 珠宝商把钻石镶嵌在黄金底座上。

The silver locket was enchased with tiny pearls. 银质小盒子上镶嵌着小珍珠。

近义词: embed, inlay, ornament, adorn, embellish
反义词: extract, remove
词性: verb
