ghosted:/ˈɡoʊstɪd/| v. adj.| 忽視，突然消失，玩消失|To abruptly cease communication with someone (often a romantic partner) without explanation, like a ghost disappearing.|例句：He ghosted her after three dates.  他約會三次後就玩消失了。|例句：She felt ghosted and confused. 她感到被忽視，很困惑。|近义词：abandoned, ignored, ditched, jilted|反义词：stayed in contact, communicated, engaged|词性:动词, 形容词

ghosted:/ˈɡoʊstɪd/| adj.| 被忽視的，被玩消失的|Having been abruptly abandoned or ignored by someone, especially a romantic interest, without explanation.|例句：She felt ghosted after he stopped replying to her messages.  他停止回复她的信息后，她感觉被玩消失了。|例句：The ghosted employee never received a reason for their termination. 被玩消失的员工从未收到解雇的理由。|近义词：abandoned, ignored, ditched, jilted|反义词：appreciated, acknowledged, valued|词性:形容词
