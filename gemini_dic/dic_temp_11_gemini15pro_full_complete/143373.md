outfed: /ˌaʊtˈfɛd/
verb
喂得比...多; 吃得比...多
to feed more or better than another; to eat more than another.
The farmer outfed his neighbor's prize-winning pig.  这个农民把猪喂得比邻居的获奖猪还要肥。
He outfed everyone at the buffet. 他在自助餐上比任何人都吃得多。
Synonyms: out-eat, surpass in feeding
Antonyms: be out-eaten by, be surpassed in feeding
词性: 动词
