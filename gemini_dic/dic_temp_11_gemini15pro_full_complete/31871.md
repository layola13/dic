cheerleader:/ˈtʃɪrˌlidər/ (US), /ˈtʃɪəˌliːdə(r)/ (UK)| n.|啦啦队长|A person, usually a woman, who leads organized cheering at a sporting event, especially in American schools and colleges.  They perform routines, stunts, and chants to encourage the team and entertain the crowd.|The cheerleaders were doing backflips and high kicks. 啦啦队员们正在做后空翻和高踢腿。|synonyms: supporter, booster, rooter|antonyms: critic, detractor|noun
