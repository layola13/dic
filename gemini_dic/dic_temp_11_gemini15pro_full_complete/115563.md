maracanã:/ˌmærəkəˈnɐ̃/ | n. | 马拉卡纳体育场 |  A large football stadium in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, officially named Estádio Jornalista Mário Filho. It is one of the world's largest football stadiums. |  The Maracanã hosted the final match of the 2014 FIFA World Cup.  马拉卡纳体育场举办了2014年世界杯决赛。| synonym: stadium, arena | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "small field") | noun
