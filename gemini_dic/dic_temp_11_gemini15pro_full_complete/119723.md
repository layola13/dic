midstreams:/ˈmɪdstriːmz/
n.
中游, 中段, 中游产业
The processes, infrastructure, and activities involved in the processing, storing, transporting, and marketing of oil and natural gas after extraction and before refining or consumption.
Midstream companies play a crucial role in getting oil and gas from the wellhead to the consumer. 中游公司在将石油和天然气从井口输送到消费者手中扮演着至关重要的角色。
Midstream operations include pipelines, storage facilities, and processing plants. 中游业务包括管道、储存设施和加工厂。
midcourse, intermediary stages
upstream, downstream
noun
