oblique:/əˈbliːk/ /oʊˈbliːk/| adj. n.|倾斜的，间接的；斜体字，斜线|slanting or sloping; not straightforward or direct.  In printing: a slanting line (/) |The sunlight fell obliquely across the room. 阳光斜射穿过房间。|近义词：slanting, inclined, indirect, diagonal|反义词：straight, direct, perpendicular|adjective

oblique:/əˈbliːk/ /oʊˈbliːk/| n.|倾斜物，斜肌|something that is oblique, especially a slanting line or muscle. |The obliques are important muscles for core strength.  腹斜肌对核心力量很重要。|近义词：slant, slope|反义词：(n/a for this sense)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *obliquus, from Latin obliquus (“slanting, sideways, indirect”), from ob (“against, towards”) + liquis (“slanting”).
