grapple:/ˈɡræp(ə)l/ | n. v. | 抓斗，抓住；格斗，搏斗；努力克服，认真处理 |  n. An iron instrument used for grasping and holding fast to another vessel.  v. To seize another, as in wrestling; to contend in close fight.  He grappled with the problem for hours. 他几个小时都在努力解决这个问题。  The ship's grapple caught the sinking boat.  船上的抓斗抓住了正在下沉的小船。 | Synonyms: wrestle, contend, struggle, grip, clutch | Antonyms: release, surrender, yield | noun, verb
