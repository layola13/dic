bereave: /bɪˈriːv/ /bəˈriːv/
verb
剥夺，使丧失（尤指亲人）
To deprive and make desolate, especially by death.
She was bereaved of her husband in the war. 她在战争中失去了丈夫。
deprive, rob, strip
restore, give, endow
verb

bereaved: /bɪˈriːvd/ /bəˈriːvd/
adjective
失去亲人的，丧失亲人的
Deprived of something valuable or beloved; left alone and desolate, especially by the death of someone loved.
The bereaved family received many condolences.  这个失去亲人的家庭收到了很多慰问。
grieving, mourning, sorrowful
happy, joyous, cheerful
adjective
