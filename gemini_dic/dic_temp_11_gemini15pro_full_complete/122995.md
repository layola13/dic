monarchally:/məˈnɑːrkɪkli/ | adv. | 君主地，以君主的方式 | In a manner befitting a monarch; like a monarch.  | The king addressed the crowd monarchally, his voice booming with authority. 国王以君主的方式向人群讲话，他的声音充满权威。|  He waved his hand monarchally, dismissing the servant. 他像君主一样挥了挥手，打发走了仆人。| 近义词: regally, royally | 反义词: democratically, republically | 词性: 副词
词源: From monarch + -ally. Ultimately from the Greek monárkhēs.
