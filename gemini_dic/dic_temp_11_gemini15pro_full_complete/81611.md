greatnesses:/ˈɡreɪtnəsɪz/
noun
伟大, 卓越, 高尚
The quality of being great; eminence or distinction.
His greatness as a leader was undeniable. 他作为领袖的伟大是不可否认的。
Synonyms: eminence, distinction, magnitude, grandeur, excellence
Antonyms: insignificance, mediocrity, inferiority
词性:名词
