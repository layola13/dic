folioed:/ˈfoʊlioʊd/
adjective
页码编号的，有页码的
Numbered with folios; having folios.
The librarian carefully examined the folioed manuscript. 图书管理员仔细检查了这本有页码编号的手稿。
numbered, paginated
unnumbered

Etymology:
From folio + -ed.  "Folio" ultimately derives from the Latin word "folium" meaning "leaf".
