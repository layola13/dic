geomancer:/ˈdʒiːəˌmænsər/ | n. | 地卜师；风水师 | A person who practices geomancy; a diviner who interprets markings on the earth or by thrown earth. |  The geomancer advised the emperor on the best location for the new palace. 地卜师为皇帝建议了新宫殿的最佳位置。

The geomancer used a pendulum to locate underground water. 地卜师用摆锤来定位地下水。 | Synonym: diviner, soothsayer, augur | Antonym: scientist, skeptic | noun
