idealization:/ˌaɪdiːələˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|理想化；理想化的事物|The idealization of rural life is a common theme in literature.  乡村生活的理想化是文学中的一个常见主题。|The painting was an idealization of the natural world. 这幅画是对自然世界的理想化描绘。|近义词:glorification, romanticization, exaltation|反义词:vilification, denigration, disparagement|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin idealis "existing in idea," from Latin idea "idea" (from Greek idea "form, pattern," from idein "to see").  The suffix "-ization" denotes the process of making or becoming.
