blurbs:/blɜːrbz/
|n.|短评；推荐语；广告词|A short promotional description, as found on the back of a book.|The blurbs on the back of the book made it sound exciting.|synonyms: puff, plug, blurb, advertisement, notice, promotion|antonyms: criticism, pan, condemnation|noun

blurbs:/blɜːrbz/
|v.|写短评；作广告|To write or provide a blurb for.|The publisher blurbed the new novel as "a masterpiece."|synonyms: promote, advertise, publicize, hype, praise|antonyms: criticize, condemn, pan|verb
