augustans: /ɔːˈɡʌstənz/
noun
奥古斯都时代的人，奥古斯都时代的作家；具有奥古斯都时代风格的作家（或作品）
People or writers of the Augustan age (the period of Roman literature under the emperor Augustus,  traditionally dating from 43 BC to AD 14); writers or works in the style of this period.
The Augustans valued clarity, elegance, and wit in their writing. 奥古斯都时代的人重视作品中的清晰、优雅和机智。
Jonathan Swift is considered one of the greatest Augustans. 乔纳森·斯威夫特被认为是最伟大的奥古斯都时代作家之一。
近义词: neoclassicists, classicists
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "romantics" or writers of other periods as contrasting styles)
词性: n.

augustan: /ɔːˈɡʌstən/
adjective
奥古斯都时代的；具有奥古斯都时代风格的
Relating to or characteristic of the Augustan age; having the characteristics of the Augustan age, especially in literature, such as clarity, elegance, and wit.
The Augustan period was a golden age for Roman literature. 奥古斯都时代是罗马文学的黄金时代。
The poem reflects the Augustan ideals of order and harmony. 这首诗反映了奥古斯都时代秩序与和谐的理想。
近义词: neoclassical, classical
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "romantic" as a contrasting style)
词性: adj.
