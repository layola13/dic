awards: /əˈwɔːrdz/
n.
奖品，奖金；判决，裁定
Something given to a person or group in recognition of service, merit, achievement, etc.
She won several awards for her scientific research. 她因其科学研究获得了多个奖项。
synonyms: prize, honor, reward, accolade, distinction
antonyms: penalty, forfeiture, loss

awards: /əˈwɔːrdz/
v.
授予，给予；判给，裁定
To give something as an official payment, reward, or judgment.
The judges awarded the first prize to the young pianist.  评委们将一等奖授予了这位年轻的钢琴家。
synonyms: grant, bestow, confer, present
antonyms: withhold, deny, revoke
