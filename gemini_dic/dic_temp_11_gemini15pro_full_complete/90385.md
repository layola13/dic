howel:/ˈhaʊəl/| n. |哀号，恸哭; 嚎叫 |A long, mournful cry or wailing sound, typically expressing grief or pain. |The howel of the mourners filled the air. 哀悼者的恸哭声弥漫在空中。|近义词：wail, lament, cry, moan |反义词: laugh, chuckle |词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *houlen*, probably of imitative origin.  Related to howl (v.).
