overcare:/ˌoʊvərˈkɛr/ | v. | 过度关心，过分照顾 | To care for someone or something excessively, often to the point of being detrimental. | She overcared for her plants, watering them so much that they drowned. 她过度照料她的植物，浇水太多以至于它们淹死了。| He overcared for his children, stifling their independence. 他过分关心他的孩子，扼杀了他们的独立性。 | 近义词: overprotect, coddle, smother | 反义词: neglect, ignore | verb


overcare:/ˌoʊvərˈkɛr/ | n. | 过度关心，过分照顾 | Excessive care or concern, often to the detriment of the recipient. | Her overcare for her dog led to behavioral problems. 她对她的狗的过度关心导致了行为问题。 | The overcare he received as a child made him overly dependent on others. 他小时候受到的过度照顾使他过度依赖他人。| 近义词: overprotection, coddling | 反义词: neglect, indifference | noun
