ethically:/ˈeθɪkli/
adverb
伦理地；道德地
In a manner that conforms to accepted principles of right and wrong.
She acted ethically in reporting the incident to her supervisor. 她向主管报告了这件事，她的行为合乎道德。

ethically:/ˈeθɪkli/
adverb
合乎道德地; 凭良心
Doing what is right and fair.
He always strives to conduct his business ethically. 他总是努力按照道德规范开展业务。


ethically:/ˈeθɪkli/
adverb
合乎伦理地; 道义上
In a way that is morally acceptable.
It's not ethically justifiable to exploit workers in developing countries.  剥削发展中国家的工人不合乎道德。


ethically:/ˈeθɪkli/
adverb
有道德地
with good morals; with a sense of right and wrong
Ethically speaking, I don't think you can justify that.  从道德上讲，我认为你无法为那件事辩护。


ethically:/ˈeθɪkli/
adverb
合乎伦理道德地; 合乎职业道德地
According to principles of morality and ethics, especially professional ethics.
The doctor behaved ethically throughout the procedure.  医生在整个手术过程中都表现得合乎职业道德。
