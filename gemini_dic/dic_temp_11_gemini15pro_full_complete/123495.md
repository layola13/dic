monothalamous:/ˌmɒnəˈθæləmʌs/
adjective
单室的；单囊的
Having or consisting of only one chamber or cavity.
例句：The monothalamous foraminifera are a group of single-chambered organisms.  单室有孔虫是一组单腔生物。

例句：The researchers discovered a new species of monothalamous algae. 研究人员发现了一种新的单室藻类。
近义词：unicameral, unilocular
反义词：multilocular, polythalamous
词性：adjective
