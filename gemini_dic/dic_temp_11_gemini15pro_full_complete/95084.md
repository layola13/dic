inalienable:/ɪnˈeɪliənəbl/
adj.
不可剥夺的；不可分割的
not able to be taken away or given away by the possessor.
例句：
The Declaration of Independence of the United States lists life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness as inalienable rights.
美国《独立宣言》将生命、自由和追求幸福列为不可剥夺的权利。

Freedom of speech is an inalienable human right.
言论自由是一项不可剥夺的人权。

近义词：absolute, inherent, inviolable, unalienable
反义词：alienable, transferable
词性: adjective
