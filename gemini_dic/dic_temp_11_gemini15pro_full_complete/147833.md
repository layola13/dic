overpunished:/ˌoʊvərˈpʌnɪʃt/
adjective
过度惩罚的
Having been punished too severely or excessively.
The child felt overpunished for a minor mistake.  这个孩子因为一个小错误感到受到了过度惩罚。
He was overpunished for skipping school. 他因为逃学而受到了过重的惩罚。
Synonyms: excessively punished, unduly punished, disproportionately punished
Antonyms: underpunished, leniently punished
词性: adjective
