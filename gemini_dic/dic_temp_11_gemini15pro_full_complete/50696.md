dermestoid:/dɜːrˈmɛstɔɪd/ | adj. | 类似皮蠹的，皮蠹状的 |  Resembling or characteristic of dermestid beetles, which are known for their ability to digest keratin and other tough materials. | The dermestoid larvae were found infesting the museum's taxidermy collection.  这些类似皮蠹的幼虫被发现在博物馆的动物标本收藏中滋生。 |  Synonyms:  None specifically for dermestoid, but could use words like keratinolytic (keratin-digesting), scavenger-like. | Antonyms: None. | adjective

dermestoid:/dɜːrˈmɛstɔɪd/ | n. | 皮蠹状物 | Something that resembles a dermestid beetle or its characteristics. |  The unknown substance found on the bone had a dermestoid appearance. 在骨头上发现的未知物质具有皮蠹状外观。| Synonyms: None specifically. | Antonyms: None. | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "Dermestidae" (the family of dermestid beetles) + "-oid" (a suffix meaning resembling or having the form of).  Dermestidae comes from the Greek "derma" (skin) + "este" (to eat).
