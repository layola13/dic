nabbing:/ˈnæbɪŋ/
verb
逮捕，抓住，捉拿
The act of catching someone, especially a criminal.
Police are nabbing shoplifters all over the city. 警察正在全城抓捕商店扒手。
近义词: arresting, catching, apprehending, seizing
反义词: releasing, freeing
词性: Verb (Gerund or present participle of nab)

nabbing:/ˈnæbɪŋ/
noun
逮捕，抓住，捉拿
The act of catching someone, especially a criminal.
The nabbing of the suspect was caught on camera. 嫌疑犯被捕的画面被摄像头拍到了。
近义词: arrest, capture, apprehension
反义词: release
词性: Noun (Gerund)


词源:  From the verb "nab," which originated in the 18th century.  It's likely of Scandinavian origin, related to Swedish *nappa "to grasp, snatch."
