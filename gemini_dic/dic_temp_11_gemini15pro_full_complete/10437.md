applaudingly:/əˈplɔːdɪŋli/
adverb
赞赏地，称赞地
in a manner that expresses approval and praise.
The audience responded applaudingly to the performance. 观众对演出报以热烈的掌声。
Synonyms: approvingly, admiringly, commendably
Antonyms: disapprovingly, critically, scornfully
词性: 副词
