destructor:/dɪˈstrʌktər/| n. |析构函数；解构器；破坏者|A special method in object-oriented programming that is called when an object is destroyed or goes out of scope. It is used to release resources, such as memory, that were allocated by the object during its lifetime.|

例句：The destructor releases the memory allocated to the object.  析构函数释放分配给对象的内存。

例句：It's crucial to define a destructor to prevent memory leaks.  定义析构函数对于防止内存泄漏至关重要。

近义词：finalizer|反义词:constructor|词性:noun
