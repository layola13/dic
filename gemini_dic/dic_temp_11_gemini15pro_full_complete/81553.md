graylag:/ˈɡreɪlæɡ/| n.|灰雁|A large gray goose (Anser anser) native to Europe and Asia, the ancestor of most domestic geese.|例句：The graylag is a common sight in wetlands across Eurasia. 灰雁在欧亚大陆的湿地很常见。

例句：We observed a flock of graylag geese taking flight. 我们观察到一群灰雁起飞。|近义词：goose, wild goose|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From 'gray' + 'lag' (a dialectal word for 'goose'). The word 'lag' is likely of Scandinavian origin.
