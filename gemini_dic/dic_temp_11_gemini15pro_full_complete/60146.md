effectuate: /ɪˈfɛktʃueɪt/
verb
实施，实现，完成
To bring about; to cause to happen; to carry out; to accomplish.
The company effectuated the merger despite opposition from some shareholders.  公司不顾一些股东的反对，实现了合并。
The government finally effectuated the new policy. 政府最终实施了新政策。
Synonyms:  accomplish, achieve, execute, implement, realize, complete, fulfill
Antonyms:  hinder, obstruct, prevent, impede, thwart
词性: 动词
