artificious:/ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəs/| adj.|做作的，矫揉造作的，不自然的|Made or contrived in a way that seems artificial and intended to impress; lacking naturalness or spontaneity.|Her artificious laughter grated on my nerves. 她矫揉造作的笑声让我神经紧张。|Synonyms: affected, contrived, feigned, pretended, artificial, mannered|Antonyms: natural, genuine, sincere, unaffected, artless|adjective

artifice:/ˈɑːrtɪfɪs/| n.|诡计，欺骗，巧妙的办法|Clever or cunning devices or expedients, especially as used to trick or deceive others.|He won the game by artifice. 他靠诡计赢得了比赛。|Synonyms: trickery, deception, guile, cunning, stratagem|Antonyms: honesty, sincerity, forthrightness|noun
