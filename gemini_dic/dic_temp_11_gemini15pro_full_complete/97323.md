informational:/ˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃənl/
adjective
提供信息的；情报的；知识的
Providing information; relating to or characterized by facts about something.
The website is purely informational and does not sell any products.  这个网站纯粹提供信息，不售卖任何产品。
The brochure was very informational and helped me plan my trip. 这本小册子信息量很大，帮助我计划了我的旅行。
synonyms: informative, factual, instructive
antonyms: uninformative, misleading, deceptive
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From information + -al.  Ultimately from Latin informare (“to shape, train, instruct, educate”).
