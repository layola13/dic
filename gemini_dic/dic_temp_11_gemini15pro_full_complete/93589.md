imbalm:/ɪmˈbɑːm/ /ɪmˈbɔːm/ | vt. |  embalms, embalming, embalmed | 防腐，保存（尸体）| To treat (a corpse) with preservatives in order to prevent decay. | The Egyptians embalmed their pharaohs.  埃及人对他们的法老进行防腐处理。

The body was embalmed and placed in a tomb.  尸体经过防腐处理后被安放在坟墓里。 | 近义词：preserve, mummify | 反义词: decay, decompose | 动词
