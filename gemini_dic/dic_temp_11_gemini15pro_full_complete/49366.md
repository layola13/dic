deliquescent:/ˌdɛlɪˈkwɛsənt/
adjective
潮解的；易溶解的
(of a solid) becoming liquid by absorbing moisture from the air.
Example: Sodium hydroxide is a deliquescent substance and should be stored in airtight containers. 氢氧化钠是一种潮解性物质，应储存在密封容器中。

Example: The deliquescent nature of the salt made it unsuitable for use in humid environments.  这种盐的潮解性使其不适合在潮湿环境中使用。

近义词: hygroscopic, absorbent
反义词: anhydrous, non-hygroscopic
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin deliquescere "to melt away," from de- "down" + liquescere "to become liquid."
