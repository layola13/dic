corbeil:/ˈkɔːrbeɪl/| n. |柳条筐；篮子|A large wicker basket, typically with handles.|例句：The farmer filled the corbeil with freshly picked apples. 农民用柳条筐装满了刚摘的苹果。

例句：She carried a corbeil of bread to the market. 她提着一篮子面包去市场。|近义词：basket, hamper |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a physical object like a basket) |词性:noun
