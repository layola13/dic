mumbo-jumbo:/ˈmʌmbəʊ ˈdʒʌmbəʊ/
|n.|胡言乱语；莫名其妙的话；繁文缛节|Meaningless or unintelligible speech; gibberish; ritualistic nonsense.|The politician's speech was full of mumbo-jumbo that no one understood.  这位政客的演讲充满了没人能理解的胡言乱语。|The shaman performed a ritual filled with mumbo-jumbo.  萨满举行了一个充满繁文缛节的仪式。|近义词: gibberish, nonsense, balderdash, claptrap|反义词: sense, clarity|词性:名词
