hypomagnesaemic: /ˌhaɪpəʊˌmæɡnɪˈsiːmɪk/ | adj. | 低镁血症的 |  Characterized by or relating to an abnormally low concentration of magnesium in the blood.  |  The patient presented with hypomagnesaemic symptoms, including muscle tremors and weakness.  病人出现低镁血症症状，包括肌肉震颤和无力。 |  hypomagnesia, low magnesium | hypermagnesaemic | adjective


Etymology:

hypo- (Greek: under, beneath) + magnesi- (magnesium) + -emic (relating to the blood)
