duratives:/ˈdjʊərətɪvz/ /ˈdʊrətɪvz/| n. |持续体；持续语|A durative is a grammatical aspect that describes events or states that extend over a period of time.|Duratives emphasize the ongoing nature of an action or state, rather than its completion.|The lecture was a durative of three hours.  这场讲座持续了三个小时。|近义词：continuants,  prolonged events|反义词：punctual, instantaneous|noun
