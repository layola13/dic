例句：He studied gunnery at the military academy. 他在军事院校学习炮术。|近义词：artillery, ballistics|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gunnerye, from Old French *gunnerie, from gunner + -ie, ultimately from Late Latin canna "reed, cane," then "tube."
