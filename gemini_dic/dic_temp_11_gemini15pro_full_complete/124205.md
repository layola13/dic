mosh:/mɒʃ/ (UK) /mɑːʃ/ (US)| v. |激烈碰撞，在摇滚音乐会上猛烈撞击|To dance to rock music in a violent way, jumping up and down and often colliding with other dancers.|例句：The crowd moshed wildly in front of the stage.  人群在舞台前疯狂地碰撞。

例句：I got badly bruised when I was moshing at the concert.  我在音乐会上猛烈碰撞时受了重伤。

近义词：slam dance, body surf|反义词:waltz, slow dance|词性:动词


moshed:/mɒʃt/ (UK) /mɑːʃt/ (US) | v. (mosh的过去式和过去分词) |激烈碰撞，在摇滚音乐会上猛烈撞击（过去式）|The past tense and past participle of "mosh".|例句：We moshed all night at the rock concert. 我们在摇滚音乐会上整晚都在激烈碰撞。
