genitocrural:/ˌdʒɛnɪtoʊˈkrʊrəl/| adj.|生殖器-大腿的，腹股沟的|Relating to the genitalia and the thigh or groin.  Pertaining to the region of the genitals and the thigh. |The patient presented with genitocrural pain. 病人表现出腹股沟疼痛。|The genitocrural nerve provides sensation to the skin of the upper anterior thigh. 腹股沟神经为大腿前上部的皮肤提供感觉。|近义词:inguinal|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider areas distant from the groin, like acral)|词性:adjective
