oratorships:/ˈɔːrətərʃɪps/
n.
雄辩术；演讲才能；演说家身份
The skill or art of public speaking, especially in a formal and eloquent manner.  A group of speeches, or the position or role of an orator.
He honed his oratorships through years of practice. 他通过多年的练习磨练了他的演讲才能。
The competition showcased the oratorships of several promising young speakers. 比赛展示了几位有前途的年轻演讲者的演讲才能。
近义词: eloquence, rhetoric, public speaking, oratory
反义词: inarticulacy
