obreption:/əˈbrɛpʃən/
n.
窃取; 瞒骗获取
the act of obtaining something in a secretive or dishonest way.
He gained control of the company by obreption. 他通过瞒骗手段获得了公司的控制权。
近义词：subreption, deception, fraud, chicanery
反义词：honesty, forthrightness
词性：名词

词源:  From Latin *obreptionem, accusative singular of *obreptio, from obreptus, past participle of obrepere “to creep up on, steal upon,” from ob- “toward” + repere “to creep.”
