gratitude:/ˈɡrætɪtjuːd/
n.
感激之情，感谢
the quality of being thankful; readiness to show appreciation for and to return kindness.
She expressed her gratitude to him for his help. 她向他表达了感激之情，感谢他的帮助。

I felt a deep sense of gratitude for her kindness. 我对她的好意深感感激。


近义词:thankfulness, appreciation, gratefulness
反义词:ingratitude, resentment, thanklessness
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French gratitude, or from late Latin gratitudo, from Latin gratus ‘pleasing, thankful’.
