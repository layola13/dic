immatures:/ɪməˈtʊərz/ | n. |未成熟体，未成熟者| Young or undeveloped organisms or people. | The scientist studied the immatures of several butterfly species. 科学家研究了几种蝴蝶的未成熟体。| The farmer discarded the immatures from the harvest. 农民把未成熟的果实从收获中剔除。| 近义词: juveniles, youngsters, adolescents | 反义词: adults, matures | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *immaturus*, from Latin *in-* (not) + *maturus* (ripe, mature).
