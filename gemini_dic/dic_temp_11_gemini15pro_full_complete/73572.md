forestlike: /ˈfɒrɪstlaɪk/ (UK) /ˈfɔːrɪstlaɪk/ (US)| adj.|像森林的，森林似的|Resembling a forest; having the characteristics of a forest, such as dense trees, shade, and a sense of seclusion.|The garden was overgrown and forestlike.  花园杂草丛生，像森林一样。| The dense vegetation created a forestlike atmosphere. 茂密的植被营造出一种森林般的气氛。 |近义词：wooded, sylvan, forested|反义词：barren, treeless|词性: adjective


词源: forest + like.  "Forest" comes from Old French "forest" (ultimately from Latin "foris," meaning "outside").  "Like" comes from Old English "lic" and "gelic."
