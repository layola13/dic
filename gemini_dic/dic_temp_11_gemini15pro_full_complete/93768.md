immediatism:/ɪˈmiːdiətɪzəm/| n.|直接主义，立即行动主义|The doctrine or practice of emphasizing immediacy, especially in artistic representation or political action.  A focus on direct experience and action, often rejecting planning or traditional methods.  |Example: The group's immediatism led them to stage a protest without seeking official permits.  这个团体的直接主义导致他们在没有获得官方许可的情况下举行了抗议。

Example:  The artist's immediatism is reflected in the spontaneous brushstrokes and vibrant colors.  这位艺术家的直接主义体现在其自发的笔触和鲜艳的色彩中。|近义词：direct action, activism, spontaneity|反义词:gradualism, deliberation, planning|词性:noun
