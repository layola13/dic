lamellar:/ləˈmɛlər/ | adj. | 薄片的，层状的 | Arranged in thin plates or layers like scales or shells. |  The mushroom had a lamellar structure underneath its cap.  这种蘑菇的菌盖下面有层状结构。| layered, stratified, scaly | non-lamellar, homogenous | adjective

lamella:/ləˈmɛlə/ | n. | 薄片，薄层 | A thin plate, scale, or layer of bone, tissue, or other material. | The gills of a mushroom are composed of numerous lamellae.  蘑菇的菌褶由许多薄片组成。| layer, plate, scale |  N/A | noun

词源：From Latin *lamella*, diminutive of *lamina* ("thin plate, leaf, layer").
