afflictionless:/əˈflɪkʃən.ləs/
adjective
无苦恼的，无忧无虑的
Free from affliction; without pain, suffering, or distress.
She seemed entirely afflictionless, despite the hardships she had endured. 她似乎完全没有痛苦，尽管她经历了艰辛。
Near Synonyms: untroubled, carefree, unburdened, painless
Antonyms: afflicted, troubled, suffering, distressed
词性: 形容词
