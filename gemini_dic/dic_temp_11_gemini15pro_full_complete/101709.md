irrepleviable:/ˌɪrɪˈplɛviəbl/
adjective
不可收回的，无法追回的
Not capable of being replevied (replevin: a legal action to recover personal property wrongfully taken).  This refers to property that cannot be legally recovered through a writ of replevin, often because it has been sold or destroyed.
The stolen artwork, having been sold overseas, was deemed irrepleviable.  这件被盗的艺术品，因为已经在海外售出，被认为是无法追回的。
The data, corrupted beyond repair, was irrepleviable. 数据损坏无法修复，已经无法恢复了。
近义词:irrecoverable, unrecoverable, irretrievable
反义词:recoverable, retrievable
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + repleviable (capable of being replevied), from replevy + -able. Replevy comes from Anglo-French replevir, from re- (back, again) + plevir (to pledge).
