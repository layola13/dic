naturopathies:/ˌneɪtʃəˈrɒpəθiz/| n. |自然疗法|A system of alternative medicine based on the theory that diseases can be successfully treated or prevented without the use of drugs, by techniques such as control of diet, exercise, and massage.|She explored various naturopathies before finding a treatment that worked for her. 她尝试了各种自然疗法，才找到一种对她有效的疗法。|Synonyms: alternative medicine, holistic medicine, natural healing|Antonyms: conventional medicine, allopathy|Noun
