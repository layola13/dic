ligan:/ˈlɪɡən/| n. |配体|A molecule or ion that bonds to a central metal atom to form a coordination complex. |The heme group in hemoglobin contains a porphyrin ligan. 血红蛋白中的血红素基团包含一个卟啉配体。|Synonym: ligand, coordinating entity|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "free ion/molecule" in a specific context)|noun
