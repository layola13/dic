communique:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪ/ /kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪ/
n.
公报；官方声明；通讯
an official announcement or statement, especially one made to the media
The president issued a communique after the meeting. 总统在会后发表了一份公报。

The embassy issued a communique regarding the travel advisory. 大使馆发布了一份关于旅行建议的公报。


近义词：statement, announcement, bulletin, report
反义词：none (no direct opposite for a formal announcement)
词性：noun
