burkas:/ˈbɜːrkəz/ | n. | 布卡，罩袍 | A long, loose garment covering the whole body from head to toe, worn in public by some Muslim women. |  Many women choose to wear burkas as an expression of their religious beliefs. 许多妇女选择穿罩袍来表达她们的宗教信仰。

The burka is often seen in some Middle Eastern and South Asian countries. 在一些中东和南亚国家经常能看到罩袍。 | 近义词：niqab, veil, chador | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western clothing) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Urdu and Persian بُرقَع (burqaʻ), ultimately from Arabic بُرْقُع (burquʻ), meaning a face veil.
