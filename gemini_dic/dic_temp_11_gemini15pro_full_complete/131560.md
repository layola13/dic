noncommunicating:/ˌnɒnkəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不交流的；不传达的；不通的
Not communicating; not transmitting information or able to be traversed.
The patient was noncommunicating and unresponsive.  病人不交流也不回应。
The ventricles of the heart are noncommunicating. 心脏的几个心室互不相通。
Synonyms: uncommunicative, silent, taciturn, reserved, withdrawn
Antonyms: communicative, talkative, outgoing, expressive
词性: adjective
