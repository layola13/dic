limpidnesses:/lɪmˈpɪdnəsɪz/
n.
透明度；清澈度；明净度
The state or quality of being limpid; clarity and transparency.
The limpidnesses of the stream allowed us to see the colorful pebbles at the bottom.
溪水的清澈让我们能够看到底部五彩缤纷的鹅卵石。
Synonyms: clarity, transparency, pellucidity, lucidity
Antonyms: murkiness, cloudiness, opacity, turbidity
词性: 名词

limpid:/ˈlɪmpɪd/
adj.
透明的；清澈的；明净的
(of a liquid, especially water) completely clear and transparent.
(of a person's eyes) unclouded; clear.
(of a style or musical performance) clear and accessible.
The limpid waters of the Caribbean are truly breathtaking. 加勒比海清澈的海水真是令人叹为观止。
Her limpid eyes reflected the blue of the sky. 她清澈的眼睛映衬着天空的蓝色。
Synonyms: clear, transparent, crystalline, pellucid, lucid
Antonyms: murky, cloudy, opaque, turbid
词性: 形词
