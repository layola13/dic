hangman:/ˈhæŋmən/| n.|猜单词游戏|A word-guessing game where players try to guess a hidden word by suggesting letters, with each incorrect guess resulting in a part of a stick figure being drawn.  If the figure is completed before the word is guessed, the guesser loses. |We played hangman during the break.  我们在休息时间玩了猜单词游戏。|近义词：word game|反义词: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hangmann, from hangen "to hang" + mann "man".  The word literally means "hanging man".
