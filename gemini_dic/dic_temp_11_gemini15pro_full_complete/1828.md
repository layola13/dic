acrobatics: /ˌækrəˈbætɪks/ | n. | 特技；杂技 | The art or skill of performing acrobatic feats, such as balancing, tumbling, and aerial maneuvers. | The circus featured incredible acrobatics. 马戏团表演了精彩绝伦的杂技。 | 近义词：gymnastics, stunts | 反义词: stillness, inactivity | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *acrobatique*, from Greek *akrobatos* "walking on tiptoe, climbing aloft," from *akron* "summit, extremity" + *bainein* "to go, walk, step."
