bardine:/ˈbɑːrdiːn/| n.|巴丁鱼|A small freshwater fish of the carp family, common in Europe. It is similar to the dace but has a longer snout and a forked tail.|The bardine is a popular game fish in some parts of Europe. 巴丁鱼在欧洲某些地区是一种受欢迎的游钓鱼。|Synonyms: dace, bleak|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific fish species)|noun

Etymology->
From French *bardin*, diminutive of *barbe*, from Latin *barba* "beard" referring to the barbels (whisker-like sensory organs) of the fish.
