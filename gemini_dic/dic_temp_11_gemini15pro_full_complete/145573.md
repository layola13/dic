overcompliant:/ˌoʊvər.kəmˈplaɪ.ənt/ | adj. | 过分顺从的，过度遵从的 | Excessively compliant; obeying rules or requests too readily, often to one's own detriment. |  He was so overcompliant that he agreed to work overtime every day without extra pay. 他太顺从了，以至于同意每天加班却没有额外的报酬。|  She's overcompliant with her demanding boss, always putting his needs before her own. 她对苛刻的老板过度顺从，总是把他的需求放在自己的需求之前。 | 近义词: docile, submissive, acquiescent, obedient | 反义词: rebellious, defiant, assertive, noncompliant | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + compliant (adjective meaning "willing to comply")  由表示“过度”的前缀 over- 和形容词 compliant（意为“顺从的”）组合而成。
