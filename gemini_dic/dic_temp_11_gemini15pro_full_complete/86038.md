healthfulnesses:/ˈhɛlθfʊlnəsɪz/| n. |健康益处；有益健康|The healthfulnesses of a Mediterranean diet are well documented. 地中海饮食的健康益处已被充分证明。|例句：The healthfulnesses of regular exercise are numerous.  规律运动对健康有很多好处。

例句：She extolled the healthfulnesses of fresh fruits and vegetables. 她盛赞了新鲜水果和蔬菜的益处。|近义词：benefits, advantages, merits|反义词：unhealthiness, harms, detriments|词性:名词(plural)

词源: healthfulness (n.) + -es (plural suffix).  Ultimately from health (n.) + -ful (suffix) + -ness (suffix).
