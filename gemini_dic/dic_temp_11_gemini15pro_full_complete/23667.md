brangle:/ˈbræŋɡəl/| n. vi. vt.|争吵，吵闹；争论|A noisy quarrel or dispute.|例句：The children brangled over the last cookie. 孩子们为了最后一块饼干争吵。

例句：They were constantly brangling with their neighbours. 他们经常和邻居争吵。|近义词：quarrel, argue, squabble, wrangle, bicker|反义词：agree, concur, harmonize|词性:名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *branglen*, frequentative of unknown origin, perhaps related to Old French *branler* (“to shake, brandish”).
