nevadians:/nɪˈvædiənz/ /nɪˈveɪdiənz/
noun
内华达州人；内华达居民
People who live in or are from the state of Nevada.
Nevadians are known for their independent spirit. 内华达州人以其独立精神而闻名。
The Nevadians voted overwhelmingly in favor of the proposition. 内华达州人以压倒性多数投票赞成该提案。

synonyms:  Nevadans, residents of Nevada, citizens of Nevada
antonyms: non-Nevadians
