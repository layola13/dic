alkalinity:/ˌæl.kəˈlɪn.ɪ.ti/ | n. |碱度，碱性 |The capacity of water to neutralize acids. This capacity is primarily a function of the carbonate, bicarbonate, and hydroxide content of the water.|The alkalinity of the lake is decreasing due to acid rain.  湖泊的碱度由于酸雨正在下降。 |synonyms: basicity, basic capacity | antonyms: acidity | noun
