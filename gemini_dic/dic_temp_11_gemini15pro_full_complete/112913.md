ludwigite:/ˈlʌdwɪɡaɪt/ | n. |路德维希特（一种矿物）| A blackish brown mineral consisting of magnesium iron borate, occurring as fibrous masses in metamorphic limestone. | Ludwigite is found in contact metamorphic deposits. 路德维希特发现于接触变质矿床中。| Ludwigite is a relatively rare mineral. 路德维希特是一种相对稀有的矿物。 |  Synonyms: borate mineral (general), vonsenite (similar composition) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
