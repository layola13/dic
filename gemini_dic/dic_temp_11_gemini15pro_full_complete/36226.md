cognatic:/kɒɡˈnætɪk/ | adj. | 同源的；母系的 | Related by blood; descended from a common ancestor, especially through the female line. |  She traced her cognatic ancestry back to the 17th century. 她追溯母系祖先到17世纪。|  The inheritance laws favored cognatic descendants. 继承法偏向母系后裔。| synonyms: kindred, related, consanguineous, agnate (on the father's side) | antonyms: unrelated, non-cognate | adjective
