outsteaming:/ˌaʊtˈstiːmɪŋ/
adjective
向外喷出的，冒着蒸汽的
emitting steam outwards.
The outsteaming vents released a plume of hot air. 向外喷出的通风口释放出一股热气。
近义词: steaming, venting, exhaling
反义词: inhaling, absorbing


outsteaming:/ˌaʊtˈstiːmɪŋ/
noun
向外喷出的蒸汽
the act of emitting steam outwards.
The outsteaming from the geyser was impressive. 间歇泉喷出的蒸汽令人印象深刻。
近义词: emission, exhaust, exhalation
反义词: intake, inhalation
