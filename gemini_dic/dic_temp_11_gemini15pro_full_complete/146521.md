overgesticulated: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛstɪkjʊleɪtɪd/
verb (past tense)
做手势过度；夸张地做手势
Used to describe someone who made excessive hand gestures, often to the point of being distracting or theatrical.
He overgesticulated while telling the story, waving his arms wildly. 他讲故事时手势夸张，疯狂地挥舞着手臂。
The nervous presenter overgesticulated throughout his speech.  紧张的演讲者在整个演讲过程中都做手势过度。
Synonym: overacted, exaggerated
Antonym: understated, subtle
词性: 动词（过去式）
