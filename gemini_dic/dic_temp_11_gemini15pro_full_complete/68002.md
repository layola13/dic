factitiousness:/ˌfæktɪˈʃəsnəs/| n. |人为性；不自然；矫揉造作|The factitiousness of her emotions was apparent in her forced smile. 她的情感是人为的，在她强颜欢笑中显露无疑。|例句：The painting's factitiousness was evident in the clumsy brushstrokes. 这幅画的不自然之处体现在笨拙的笔触上。

The author's attempt at creating a sense of mystery felt strained and factitious. 作者试图营造一种神秘感，但却让人觉得牵强和造作。|近义词：artificiality, unnaturalness, contrivedness, affectation|反义词:genuineness, naturalness, authenticity|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *factitiosus*, from Latin *factitius* (“made by art, artificial”), from *factus*, past participle of *facere* (“to make, to do”).
