mottled:/ˈmɑːtld/ (US) /ˈmɒtld/ (UK)| adj.|斑驳的，杂色的|Marked with spots or smears of different colours.|The old wall was mottled with green and grey patches.  旧墙上布满了绿色和灰色的斑块。|The horse had a mottled coat. 这匹马的皮毛是杂色的。|spotted, dappled, speckled, blotched, variegated|uniform, solid, plain|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *motelen*, frequentative of *moten* (“to mark with spots”), probably from Old Norse *mota* (“to spot, stain”).  Related to mottle (verb).
