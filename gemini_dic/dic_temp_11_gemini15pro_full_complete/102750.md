ETYMOLOGY->
From jaunty + -ly.  Jaunty itself comes from the French gentil, meaning "noble, high-born," ultimately from Latin gentilis "of the same clan or family." The meaning shifted over time from referring to noble birth to a confident and stylish demeanor.
