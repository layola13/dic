implicativenesses:/ɪmplɪkətɪvnəsɪz/| n. |暗示性，含蓄性|The quality of being implicative; the state of containing or conveying implications.  The plural form of "implicativeness". |The implicativenesses of his remarks were not lost on the audience. 他的话里的暗示性没有被观众忽略。|近义词：implications, suggestions, nuances, connotations|反义词：explicitness, directness|noun
