exacerbation:/ɪɡˌzæsərˈbeɪʃən/ /ɛkˈsæsɚˌbeʃən/ | n. | 加剧，恶化 | An increase in the severity of a disease or its symptoms. |  The smoky conditions exacerbated her asthma. 烟雾弥漫的环境加剧了她的哮喘。| synonyms: aggravation, worsening, intensification | antonyms: improvement, amelioration, alleviation | noun

exacerbation:/ɪɡˌzæsərˈbeɪʃən/ /ɛkˈsæsɚˌbeʃən/ | n. | 激化，加剧（不好的局面） |  An increase in the bitterness or violence of something. | The president's comments only served to exacerbate the already tense situation. 总统的评论只会加剧本已紧张的局势。| synonyms: inflammation, aggravation | antonyms:  mitigation, appeasement | noun
