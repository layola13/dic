e-signature:/iːˈsɪɡnətʃər/ | n. | 电子签名 | An electronic signature, or e-signature, refers to data in electronic form which is logically associated with other data in electronic form and which is used by the signatory to sign. |  Example 1:  The contract was signed with an e-signature.  合同是用电子签名签署的。

Example 2: E-signatures are becoming increasingly common in business transactions. 电子签名在商业交易中越来越普遍。 |  Synonym: digital signature, electronic autograph | Antonym: handwritten signature, wet signature | noun
