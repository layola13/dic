nonsanguine:/ˌnɒnˈsæŋɡwɪn/ | adj. | 不乐观的，悲观的 | Not hopeful or optimistic; pessimistic. |  例句：He was nonsanguine about the team's chances of winning. 他对团队的获胜机会不乐观。

例句：The economic forecast was nonsanguine, predicting a recession. 经济预测不乐观，预言会出现衰退。

近义词：pessimistic, gloomy, despondent, unenthusiastic | 反义词：optimistic, sanguine, hopeful, cheerful | 词性：形容词

词源：From Latin *nonsanguineus*, from non- "not" + sanguineus "optimistic, cheerful" (literally "bloody," from sanguis "blood").  The original sense referred to someone not having a ruddy, healthy complexion, thus seen as lacking vigor and cheerfulness.
