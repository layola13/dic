muffineer:/ˈmʌfɪnɪər/| n. |松饼罐; 松饼撒粉器|A container with a perforated top for sprinkling sugar, etc., onto muffins or other food.  A small container with holes in the lid used for sprinkling powdered sugar or spices onto food, especially muffins.|She used the muffineer to dust powdered sugar over the warm muffins.|Synonym: sugar shaker, dredger|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sugar bowl" as it stores sugar instead of sprinkling it)|noun
