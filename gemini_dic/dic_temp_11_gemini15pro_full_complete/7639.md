annulled:/əˈnʌld/
verb
宣布无效；取消；废除
to declare officially that something is no longer legally valid
The marriage was annulled after just six months. 这段婚姻仅仅六个月后就被宣告无效了。
Their engagement was annulled. 他们的婚约被取消了。

synonyms:invalidate, nullify, abolish, cancel, repeal, revoke, rescind, void
antonyms:validate, ratify, confirm, uphold
