emetic:/ɪˈmɛtɪk/ | adj. n. |催吐的；催吐剂 |Causing vomiting; a medicine that causes vomiting. |The doctor prescribed an emetic to help the patient expel the poison. 医生给病人开了催吐剂以帮助排出毒物。|Synonyms: vomitive, nauseating |Antonyms: antiemetic |adjective, noun
emetic:/ɪˈmɛtɪk/ | n. |催吐剂 |A medicine or other substance that causes vomiting. |She was given an emetic after swallowing the poisonous berries. 她吞下有毒的浆果后被给予了催吐剂。|Synonyms: vomitive, nauseant |Antonyms: antiemetic |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin emetica, neuter plural of emeticus "causing vomiting," from Greek emetikos, from emeein "to vomit."
