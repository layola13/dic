fattily:/ˈfætlɪli/ | adv. | 肥胖地；油腻地；丰腴地 | In a way that involves or shows the presence of a large amount of fat. | The steak was cooked fattily, swimming in oil. 牛排油腻腻地煎着，浸在油里。 | 近义词：greasily, oilily, richly | 反义词：leanly, thinly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From fat (adjective) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The word "fat" comes from Old English "fæt," of Germanic origin, related to Dutch "vet" and German "Fett."
