objectivity:/ˌɒbdʒekˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌɑːbdʒekˈtɪvɪti/| n. |客观性；客观现实|The state or quality of being objective; lack of bias or prejudice.|The judge’s objectivity was questioned due to his personal relationship with the defendant.  法官的客观性因其与被告的私人关系而受到质疑。|Synonyms: impartiality, neutrality, detachment|Antonyms: subjectivity, bias, prejudice|noun

objectiveness:/əbˈdʒɛktɪvnəs/ /əbˈdʒɛktɪvnɪs/ |n.|客观性|The quality of being objective; based on facts rather than feelings or opinions.|He tried to maintain his objectiveness in evaluating the situation. 他试图在评估形势时保持客观。|Synonyms: impartiality, neutrality, detachment|Antonyms: subjectivity, bias, prejudice|noun
