depositing:/dɪˈpɒzɪtɪŋ/
v.
存放，储蓄
The act of putting something into a safe place, such as a bank account.
She is depositing her paycheck into her savings account. 她正在把她的薪水存入她的储蓄账户。
He is depositing the money in the bank. 他正在把钱存入银行。
synonyms: placing, lodging, storing, saving
antonyms: withdrawing, removing, taking out
