cancelations:/ˌkæn.səˈleɪ.ʃənz/ | n. | 取消，撤销 |The act of canceling something; an instance of being canceled. |Many airlines are offering refunds for cancellations due to the pandemic. 许多航空公司因疫情取消航班，并提供退款。|Synonyms: withdrawals, revocations, abolitions |Antonyms: confirmations, approvals |noun

cancelations:/ˌkæn.səˈleɪ.ʃənz/ | n. | 取消，撤销 | A decision to stop something that has already been arranged from happening. |There have been a lot of cancellations due to bad weather. 由于恶劣天气，已经有很多取消了。|Synonyms: abandonments, terminations, discontinuations |Antonyms: continuations, renewals |noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English cancellacion, from Latin cancellatio (“a crossing out”), from cancellare (“to cross out”), from cancelli (“lattice”).
