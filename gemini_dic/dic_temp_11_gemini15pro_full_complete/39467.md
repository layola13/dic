connivance:/kəˈnaɪvəns/
n.
共谋；默许；纵容
Secret or tacit consent to wrongdoing; collusion.
The crime was committed with the connivance of the security guard.  这起犯罪是在保安的纵容下进行的。
Synonyms: collusion, complicity, collaboration, conspiracy
Antonyms: opposition, resistance, interference, obstruction
noun


connivances:/kəˈnaɪvənsɪz/
n. (plural)
共谋；默许；纵容（复数）
Instances of secret or tacit consent to wrongdoing.
The investigation uncovered several connivances between government officials and private companies. 调查揭露了政府官员和私营公司之间的一些勾结。
Synonyms: collusions, complicacies, collaborations, conspiracies
Antonyms: oppositions, resistances, interferences, obstructions
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conniventia, from Latin connivēre "to close the eyes, wink at, overlook," from com- "together" (see com-) + nivēre "to wink, blink."
