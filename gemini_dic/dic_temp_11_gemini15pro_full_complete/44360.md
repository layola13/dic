cross-checked:/ˌkrɒsˈtʃekt/ (US) /ˌkrɒsˈtʃekt/ (UK)| adj. |交叉核对的，反复核实的|Verified by comparing with different sources or methods to ensure accuracy.|例句：The information has been cross-checked with several databases.  这些信息已经与几个数据库交叉核对过了。|例句：All the figures have been carefully cross-checked. 所有数字都经过仔细的交叉核对了。|近义词：verified, validated, confirmed|反义词：unverified, unchecked|词性:形容词

cross-check:/ˌkrɒsˈtʃek/ (US) /ˌkrɒsˈtʃek/ (UK)| v. |交叉核对，反复核实|To verify something by comparing it with different sources or methods.|例句：We need to cross-check these figures against last year's results. 我们需要将这些数字与去年的结果进行交叉核对。|例句：The police cross-checked the witness's statement with the CCTV footage. 警方将目击者的证词与闭路电视录像进行了交叉核对。|近义词：verify, validate, confirm|反义词：ignore, disregard|词性:动词
