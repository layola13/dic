baktun:/bɑːkˈtuːn/
n.
玛雅历中的一个周期，约394年
A period of 144,000 days (approximately 394 solar years) in the Maya Long Count calendar.
The end of the 13th baktun was interpreted by some as the end of the world in 2012.  第13个巴克顿的结束被一些人解释为2012年的世界末日。
The ancient Maya civilization meticulously tracked time using baktuns. 古玛雅文明使用巴克顿来一丝不苟地记录时间。
近义词:  cycle, period
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a short time period like "day" or "week" could be considered loosely antonymous in terms of scale)
词性: noun
