declinable:/dɪˈklaɪnəbl/| adj.|可变格的，可变化的|Able to be declined, as a noun or adjective, by inflection; capable of having different grammatical forms to express case, gender, number, tense, etc. |The adjective "beautiful" is declinable, changing to "beautifully" for the adverb form.|近义词：inflectable, variable|反义词：indeclinable, invariable|词性:adjective
