fleche:/fleɪʃ/ /flɛʃ/| n.|尖塔，尖顶，箭形符号|A slender spire, especially on a church roof, often of wood and covered with lead or copper.  A pointed, arrow-like architectural feature.|The cathedral boasts a magnificent fleche reaching towards the heavens.|近义词：spire, pinnacle, steeple|反义词:base, foundation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *flèche*, meaning "arrow," from Old French *flesche*, ultimately from Frankish *fliukka*.
