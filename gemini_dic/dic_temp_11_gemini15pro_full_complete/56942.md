dotterel:/ˈdɒtərəl (UK), ˈdɑːtərəl (US)| n.|鸻; 傻瓜; 易受骗的人|A small wading bird known for its tameness and easily being approached.  A gullible person; someone easily tricked.|例句：The dotterel is known for its charmingly naive behavior.  这种鸻以其天真烂漫的行为而闻名。

例句：He was such a dotterel that he fell for the oldest trick in the book. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然上了那老掉牙的当。|近义词：plover, dupe, gull|反义词: skeptic, cynic|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *doterel, likely of imitative origin, referring to the bird's perceived foolishness.
