maidishness:/ˈmeɪdɪʃnəs/
n.
少女气质；幼稚；天真烂漫
The quality of being like a young, unmarried woman; innocence or naivete.
She retained a certain maidishness despite being in her thirties. 尽管她已经三十多岁了，但她仍然保留着某种少女气质。
Synonyms: innocence, naivete, girlishness, youthfulness, immaturity
Antonyms: maturity, sophistication, worldliness
