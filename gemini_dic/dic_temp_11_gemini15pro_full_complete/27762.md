cannele:/kæˈnɛl/ | n. |可丽露; canelés (复数)|A small French pastry flavored with rum and vanilla, baked in a fluted, cylindrical mold. It has a soft custard center and a dark, caramelized crust.| I bought a box of cannelés from the bakery. 我从面包店买了一盒可丽露。| The cannelés were perfectly baked, with a crispy exterior and a soft interior. 这些可丽露烤得非常完美，外脆里嫩。| 近义词:custard cake, pastry | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *canelé*, from Gascon *canelat* "fluted," from *cana* "reed, cane."  The name refers to the fluted mold used to bake these pastries.
