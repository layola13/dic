malfunctioned: /ˌmælˈfʌŋkʃənd/
| v. | 故障；失灵；不起作用 |  failed to function or operate correctly. | The computer malfunctioned and lost all the data. 电脑发生故障，所有数据都丢失了。| synonyms: failed, broke down, crashed, glitched | antonyms: worked, functioned, operated | verb

malfunctioned: /ˌmælˈfʌŋkʃənd/
| adj. | 失灵的；故障的 | not functioning or operating correctly. | The malfunctioned engine caused the plane to crash. 发生故障的引擎导致飞机坠毁。 | synonyms: faulty, defective, broken | antonyms: working, functional, operational | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From mal- (badly) + function + -ed (past tense suffix).  The prefix "mal-" comes from French, ultimately from Latin "male" meaning "badly" or "ill". "Function" comes from Old French "function", ultimately from Latin "functio" meaning "performance, execution".
