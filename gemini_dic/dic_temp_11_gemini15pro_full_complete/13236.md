atavism: /ˈætəvɪzəm/ | n. | 返祖现象；隔代遗传 | The reappearance of a characteristic of an ancestor after it has been absent for several generations. |  The child's hairy back was considered an atavism, a throwback to some distant, ape-like ancestor.  这孩子的毛茸茸的背被认为是返祖现象，是某种遥远猿类祖先的特征重现。

The sudden reappearance of webbed feet in the family was considered an atavism.  这个家族中突然出现的蹼足被认为是一种返祖现象。

| 近义词：throwback, reversion | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly "modern trait" or "evolutionary novelty") | 词性: noun
