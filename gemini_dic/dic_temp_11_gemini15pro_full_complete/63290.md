enthuse: /ɪnˈθjuːz/ (US) /ɪnˈθjuːz/ (UK) | v. | 使热情，使充满激情 | To fill someone with enthusiasm or excitement. |  The coach enthused the team with his pep talk. 教练用他的激励讲话使队员们充满热情。

The new project enthused the entire department.  这个新项目让整个部门都充满了热情。


近义词: inspire, excite, motivate, stimulate
反义词: dampen, discourage, dispirit
词性: verb (动词)
