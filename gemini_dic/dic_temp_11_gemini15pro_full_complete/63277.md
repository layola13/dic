enthralling:/ɪnˈθrɔːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 迷人的，引人入胜的 |Capturing and holding the attention; fascinating. | The enthralling performance left the audience speechless.  引人入胜的表演让观众哑口无言。| synonyms: captivating, fascinating, mesmerizing, spellbinding | antonyms: boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + thrall (n.) meaning "slave," "bondage," ultimately from Old Norse þræll. The original sense was "to enslave," later evolving to mean "to captivate" or "to charm."
