mormonish:/ˈmɔːrmənɪʃ/ | adj. | 类似摩门教的；有点像摩门教徒的 | Resembling or characteristic of Mormons or Mormonism, often in a slightly negative or critical way. |  His lifestyle seemed rather mormonish, with a large family and strong community ties. 他的生活方式看起来有点像摩门教徒，有大家庭和牢固的社区联系。

| Example: Her mormonish views on marriage caused friction with her more liberal friends. 她类似摩门教的婚姻观与她更自由的朋友产生了摩擦。 | Synonym: Mormon-like, puritanical, conservative | Antonym: liberal, unconventional, worldly | Adjective
