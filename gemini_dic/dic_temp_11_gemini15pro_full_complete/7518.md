anneal:/əˈniːl/| v. |使退火，使韧化；使变坚韧 |To heat (metal or glass) and allow it to cool slowly, in order to remove internal stresses and toughen it.  Also, to strengthen or harden something, often figuratively. |The glass was annealed to make it more durable.  玻璃经过退火处理使其更加耐用。

The trials they faced annealed their relationship. 他们所面临的考验使他们的关系更加牢固。|近义词：temper, toughen, strengthen, harden|反义词：weaken, soften|词性:动词
