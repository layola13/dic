gristers:/ˈɡrɪstərz/ | n. |磨坊主，谷物加工商 | A person who grinds grain, especially a miller. | The local gristers provided flour for the entire community. 当地的磨坊主为整个社区提供面粉。 | 近义词: miller, grinder | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Middle English *grestes, plural of *griste, from Old English *griste, *grytste (“grinder”), agent noun from grindan (“to grind”).  Related to Dutch griester (“grinder”).
