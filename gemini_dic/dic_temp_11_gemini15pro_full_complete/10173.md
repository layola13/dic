apoplectic:/ˌæpəˈplɛktɪk/| adj.|中风的；盛怒的；狂怒的|Overcome with anger; extremely indignant.|例句：He was apoplectic when he heard the news. 他听到这个消息后勃然大怒。

例句：She was almost apoplectic with rage. 她气得几乎要发疯。|近义词：furious, enraged, livid, incandescent|反义词：calm, tranquil, peaceful|词性:形容词

apoplexy:/ˈæpəˌplɛksi/| n.|中风|Sudden loss of consciousness or bodily control, as from a stroke.|例句：His grandfather died of apoplexy. 他祖父死于中风。

例句：She suffered an attack of apoplexy. 她突发中风。|近义词：stroke, cerebral hemorrhage|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
