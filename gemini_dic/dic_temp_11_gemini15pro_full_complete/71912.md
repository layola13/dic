flexionless:/ˈflekʃənˌlɛs/| adj.|无屈折变化的，无词形变化的|Lacking inflection; uninflected.  Not changing form to express different grammatical functions.|The flexionless language relies on word order to convey meaning.|近义词:uninflected|反义词:inflected|词性:形容词
词源: From 'flexion' (bending, grammatical inflection) + '-less' (without). Ultimately from Latin 'flectere' (to bend).
