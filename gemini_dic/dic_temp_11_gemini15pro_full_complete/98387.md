insolency: /ˈɪnsələnsɪ/ (US), /ˈɪnsələnsɪ/ (UK) | n. |傲慢，无礼，粗鲁|  Rude and disrespectful behavior.  |  His insolency towards his teacher was unacceptable. 他对老师的无礼行为是不可接受的。 |  Example: The customer's insolency made the cashier furious.  顾客的傲慢无礼让收银员大发雷霆。| Synonyms:  rudeness, impudence, impertinence, disrespect, audacity | Antonyms: respect, courtesy, politeness, deference, humility | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insolentia, from Latin insolēns (insolent) + -ia.  Equivalent to in- (not) + solēns (accustomed, used to).  Originally meaning "unusual," the word's meaning shifted to "unaccustomed to proper behavior" and then to "rude."
