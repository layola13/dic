oscilloscope:/əˈsɪləskəʊp/ (UK), /əˈsɪləskoʊp/ (US)| n. |示波器|An electronic instrument that displays a visual representation of varying electrical voltages over time, typically on a screen. It is used to measure and analyze the waveform of electronic signals.|The engineer used an oscilloscope to analyze the signal from the sensor. 工程师用示波器分析传感器的信号。|Synonyms: scope, cathode-ray oscilloscope (CRO) |Antonyms: N/A |noun

oscilloscope:/əˈsɪləskəʊp/ (UK), /əˈsɪləskoʊp/ (US)| n. |示波器|An electronic instrument used for displaying and measuring the change in voltage of an electrical signal over time.|We used the oscilloscope to identify the source of the noise in the circuit. 我们用示波器识别电路中噪声的来源。|Synonyms: scope, CRO|Antonyms: N/A |noun
