matt:/mæt/| adj. n.|无光泽的，不光滑的；粗糙表面，无光表面|Having a dull or rough surface; not shiny or smooth. |The walls were painted a matt grey. 墙壁被漆成了暗灰色。|近义词:dull, flat, non-glossy, non-reflective|反义词:glossy, shiny, polished|adjective, noun

matt:/mæt/| n.|无光照片；无光纸；无光颜料；衬纸|A photograph or piece of paper with a matt surface; a matt paint or varnish; a border or mounting, especially of cardboard, for a picture. |The photo was printed on matt paper. 这张照片印在无光纸上。|近义词:matte (US spelling)|反义词:gloss|noun
