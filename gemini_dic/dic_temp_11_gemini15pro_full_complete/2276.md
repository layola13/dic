adenocarcinoma:/ˌædɪnoʊˌkɑːrsɪˈnoʊmə/| n. |腺癌|A malignant tumor formed from glandular tissue. It is a type of cancer that starts in glands that line the inside of many organs.|例句：Lung adenocarcinoma is a common type of lung cancer. 肺腺癌是一种常见的肺癌。

例句：She was diagnosed with adenocarcinoma of the colon. 她被诊断出患有结肠腺癌。|近义词：cancer, carcinoma, tumor, malignancy|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，健康的反义词是疾病/illness) |词性: noun
