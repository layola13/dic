cenas:/ˈseɪnəs/ (US), /ˈsɛnəs/ (UK)| n.|晚餐；正餐 (Especially in Spanish-speaking countries)|A formal evening meal, typically the main meal of the day.  Primarily used in Spanish and Portuguese.|"Vamos a cenar juntos esta noche." (Spanish) - "Let's have dinner together tonight."|近义词：dinner, supper, meal|反义词: breakfast, lunch|noun
