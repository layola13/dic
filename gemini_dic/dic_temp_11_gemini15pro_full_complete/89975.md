horsewhippers:/ˈhɔːrsˌwɪpərz/| n.| 鞭打者；用马鞭抽打的人|People who whip horses or others with a horsewhip.  Those who inflict punishment or enforce discipline by whipping, especially in a cruel or excessive manner. |The cruel horsewhippers were condemned by the public. 那些残忍的鞭打者受到了公众的谴责。|Near synonyms: floggers, scourgers|Antonyms: protector, defender|noun
