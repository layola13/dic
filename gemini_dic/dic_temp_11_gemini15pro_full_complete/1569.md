achingly:/ˈeɪkɪŋli/| adv.| 痛苦地，剧烈地，渴望地 |In a way that causes or expresses aching; painfully, intensely, yearningly.|  She looked at him achingly. 她痛苦地望着他。| 近义词: painfully, intensely, poignantly, yearningly | 反义词: easily, comfortably, indifferently | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ache + -ing + -ly.  "Ache" comes from Old English "acan," meaning "to ache, pain," which is related to Old High German "ahan" and Latin "aguere" (to be sharp, torment).  The suffix "-ing" forms a present participle, and "-ly" transforms the participle into an adverb.
