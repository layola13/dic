afflictingly:/əˈflɪktɪŋli/
adverb
痛苦地，折磨人地
In a manner that causes suffering or distress.
The disease afflictingly ravaged his body. 这种疾病痛苦地摧残着他的身体。
Synonyms: painfully, distressingly, agonizingly, excruciatingly
Antonyms: soothingly, comfortingly, pleasantly
词性: 副词
