convulsionist:/kənˈvʌlʃənɪst/
n.
抽搐者，痉挛者，装病者
A person who has convulsions; someone who suffers from or simulates convulsions.
The convulsionist fell to the floor, writhing in apparent agony.  抽搐者倒在地上，痛苦地扭动着身体。
The doctor suspected the patient was a convulsionist, faking the seizures for attention. 医生怀疑病人是个装病者，为了引起注意而假装癫痫发作。
近义词: sufferer, faker, malingerer
反义词: none (This word describes a condition, not a direct opposite.)
词性: noun

convulsive:/kənˈvʌlsɪv/
adj.
痉挛的，抽搐的，惊厥的
Characterized by or producing convulsions.
The child's convulsive coughing fit worried his parents. 孩子一阵阵痉挛性的咳嗽让他的父母很担心。
Her convulsive sobs racked her body. 她痉挛性的抽泣使她的身体颤抖。
近义词: spasmodic, jerky, fitful
反义词: smooth, steady, controlled
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convulsionista, from Latin convulsio ("a violent shaking, convulsion").  Ultimately from Latin convellere ("to pull violently, tear away"), from con- ("together, with") + vellere ("to pluck, pull").
