如果你是想了解疑问词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. adv.| 什么 | used to ask for information about something | 例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | 近义词: which | 反义词:  (What 没有直接的反义词) | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want? = 她想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing now? = 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? = 你学到了什么？
What has she eaten today? = 她今天吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about earlier? = 她刚才在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? = 会议前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by next year? = 到明年你会完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做这件事十年了？
What will she have been studying by graduation? = 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？
