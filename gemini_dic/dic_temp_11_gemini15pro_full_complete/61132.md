elongative:/ɪˈlɒŋɡətɪv/ |adj.|延长性的，伸长的|Tending to elongate; having the power of elongating.|例句：The elongative growth of plant stems is affected by light. 植物茎的伸长生长受光照影响。

例句：The snake's elongative body allows it to slither through tight spaces. 蛇细长的身体使它能够在狭窄的空间里滑行。|近义词：lengthening, extending, stretching, prolonging|反义词：shortening, contracting, compressing|词性：adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *ēlongātīvus, from Latin ēlongātus (past participle of ēlongāre ‘to lengthen’), from ē- (variant of ex- ‘out, from’) + longus ‘long’.
