overawful:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːfəl/| adj.|极其糟糕的，非常可怕的| An informal intensifier of awful, meaning extremely awful.  It emphasizes the negative quality being described.  It is not a standard English word and is considered colloquial.|例句：The traffic was overawful this morning. 今早的交通糟透了。

例句：That movie was overawful; I couldn't even finish watching it. 那部电影烂透了；我都没看完。|近义词：terrible, dreadful, horrible, atrocious|反义词: wonderful, excellent, fantastic|词性:adjective
