gallopers:/ˈɡæləpərz/| n.| 骑兵，快马，飞驰的马，疾驰者 |A group of riders, especially soldiers, moving at a gallop; horses moving at a gallop. |The gallopers raced across the field.  骑兵们飞驰过田野。

The gallopers thundered down the track. 飞驰的马匹沿着跑道轰鸣而过。 | 近义词: horsemen, cavalry, racers, chargers | 反义词:  pedestrians, walkers | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From the verb "gallop" + "-er" (agent suffix), ultimately from Old French "galoper." 源于动词 "gallop" 加上后缀 "-er" (表示行为者)，最终源自古法语 "galoper"。
