overdispersed:/ˌōvərˌdɪˈspɜːrst/ | adj. | 过度离散的 | Exhibiting greater variability than would be expected based on a given statistical model.  The data points are more spread out than predicted. | The overdispersed data suggested that the Poisson model was not appropriate.  这些过度离散的数据表明泊松模型并不适用。 | The distribution of infections was overdispersed across the region.  感染在该地区的分布过度分散。|近义词: overscattered, widely dispersed |反义词: underdispersed, clustered |词性: adjective

Etymology:
over- (prefix):  From Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi (meaning "over, above, beyond").  Indicates excess or surpassing.
disperse (verb): From Latin dispersus, past participle of dispergere ("to scatter"), from dis- ("apart, asunder") + spargere ("to scatter, sprinkle").
-ed (suffix): Forms the past participle and past tense of verbs, and also adjectives from nouns.
