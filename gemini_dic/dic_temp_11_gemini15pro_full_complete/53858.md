discontinuable:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuəbl/
adjective
可终止的，可停止的
able to be discontinued
The service proved unpopular and was therefore discontinuable. 这项服务不受欢迎，因此可以终止。
近义词: terminable, ceaseable, stoppable
反义词: continuous, perpetual, unending
词性: adjective


discontinuable:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuəbl/
词源:  From discontinue + -able. Discontinue comes from the Old French discontinuer, from dis- "apart, not" + continuer "to continue," from Latin continuare "to join together, connect, make continuous," from continuus "joined together, continuous."
