imperfection:/ˌɪmpərˈfekʃən/ | n. | 不完美之处；缺点 | Something that spoils the appearance or completeness of a thing. |  We all have our imperfections. 我们都有自己的缺点。 | synonyms: flaw, defect, blemish, fault, shortcoming, deficiency, weakness | antonyms: perfection, flawlessness, impeccability | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French imperfection, or from late Latin imperfectio(n-), from imperfectus ‘incomplete’ (see imperfect).
