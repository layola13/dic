editorializer: /ˌedɪˈtɔːriəlaɪzər/
n.
评论员，社论作者
A person who writes or expresses opinions, especially in an editorial.
The editorializer argued passionately for stronger environmental regulations.  这位评论员热情地呼吁加强环境监管。
The editorializer's bias was evident in his column.  这位评论员的偏见在他的专栏里显而易见。
synonyms: commentator, columnist, pundit, opinion writer
antonyms: reporter (a more neutral term)
noun
