fathom:/ˈfæðəm/ | n. v. | 英寻，理解，测量水深 | A fathom is a unit of length equal to six feet (1.8 meters), chiefly used in reference to the depth of water.  To fathom something means to understand it thoroughly. | The ship sank in over thirty fathoms of water. 这艘船沉没在三十多英寻深的水中。

He couldn't fathom her motives. 他无法理解她的动机。| synonyms: understand, comprehend, grasp, gauge | antonyms: misunderstand, misinterpret | noun, verb
