hasten:/ˈheɪsn/| v. |加速，催促，赶紧|to make something happen sooner or more quickly|例句：The bad news hastened his departure. 坏消息促使他早日离开。

例句：We hastened to the hospital. 我们赶紧去了医院。|近义词：accelerate, expedite, rush, hurry|反义词:delay, hinder, slow|词性:动词
