concussion:/kənˈkʌʃən/| n. |脑震荡；震动；强烈震动|A concussion is a temporary injury to the brain caused by a blow to the head. |例句：He suffered a concussion after falling off his bike. 他从自行车上摔下来后脑震荡了。

例句：The explosion caused a concussion that shattered nearby windows. 爆炸引起的震动震碎了附近的窗户。|近义词：impact, shock, trauma|反义词:stability, stillness|词性:noun

concussions:/kənˈkʌʃənz/| n. |脑震荡（复数）|The plural form of concussion. |例句：Multiple concussions can have long-term effects on the brain. 多次脑震荡会对大脑造成长期影响。

例句：The team has had a number of concussions this season.  这个队本赛季已经出现了多起脑震荡病例。|近义词：head injuries, traumas|反义词: (No direct antonym for the plural form)|词性:noun
