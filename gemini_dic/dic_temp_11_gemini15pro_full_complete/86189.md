heatedness:/ˈhiːtɪdnəs/
n.
激烈；热烈；兴奋
the state of being heated, either literally or figuratively.
The heatedness of the debate surprised everyone. 辩论的激烈程度让每个人都感到惊讶。
intensity, fervor, passion
calmness, coolness, tranquility
