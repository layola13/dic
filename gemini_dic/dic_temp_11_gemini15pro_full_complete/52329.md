dicotyledonous: /ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəs/ (US) /ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəs/ (UK)| adj.| 双子叶的| Having two cotyledons (seed leaves).|  Example: Roses are dicotyledonous plants. 玫瑰是双子叶植物。| Example:  Dicotyledonous trees often have broad leaves. 双子叶树木通常具有阔叶。| 近义词: dicot | 反义词: monocotyledonous | 词性: adjective

dicotyledon: /ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ (US) /ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ (UK)| n.| 双子叶植物| A plant having two cotyledons (seed leaves).| Example:  The garden is full of dicotyledons. 花园里满是双子叶植物。| Example:  A bean is a common example of a dicotyledon. 豆子是双子叶植物的一个常见例子。| 近义词: dicot | 反义词: monocotyledon | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From di- (two) + cotyledon (seed leaf) + -ous (adjective forming suffix).  Cotyledon ultimately comes from the Greek kotýlē, meaning "cup" or "bowl," referring to the shape of the seed leaf.
