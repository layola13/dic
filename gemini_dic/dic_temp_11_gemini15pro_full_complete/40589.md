convalescence:/ˌkɒnvəˈlesəns/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnvəˈlesəns/ (US)| n. |恢复期，康复期|The period of gradual recovery of health and strength after illness or injury. |例句：She is now in convalescence after her operation. 她现在术后处于康复期。

例句：He spent a month in convalescence at a seaside resort. 他在海滨度假胜地度过了一个月的康复期。

近义词：recovery, recuperation, rehabilitation|反义词: relapse, deterioration|词性:noun
