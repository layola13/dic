heppen:  This is not a standard English word.  It's likely a misspelling of "happen."  Therefore, the following analysis is for "happen."

/ˈhæpən/ (US) /ˈhæpən/ (UK)| v. |发生; 碰巧; 偶然|Something happens when it comes into existence, or occurs by chance.|例句：The accident happened so quickly. 事故发生得太快了。|近义词：occur, take place, transpire|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one might consider "prevent")|词性: verb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Accidents happen every day.  事故每天都在发生。
It often happens that I forget my keys. 我经常忘记带钥匙。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What is happening outside? 外面发生了什么事？
I think something strange is happening. 我想有什么奇怪的事情正在发生。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Has it happened yet?  已经发生了吗？
Something unexpected has happened. 发生了意想不到的事情。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I've been happening upon interesting articles lately.  我最近经常偶然发现一些有趣的文章。 (Note:  This usage is less common.  "Happening upon" is a phrasal verb meaning "finding by chance.")
She's been happening to be at the right place at the right time. 她一直很幸运地在正确的时间出现在正确的地点。(Note:  This usage is less common and might sound awkward.)

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The accident happened last night.  事故发生在昨晚。
I don't know what happened. 我不知道发生了什么事。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was watching TV when it happened.  事情发生时我在看电视。
They were talking when the phone rang. 他们正在说话时电话响了。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The accident had already happened before the police arrived. 事故在警察到达之前就已经发生了。
She had finished her work before I called. 在我打电话之前，她已经完成了工作。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been working on the project for months before they finally finished. 在最终完成之前，他们已经在这个项目上工作了几个月。
She had been waiting for an hour when the bus finally arrived. 公共汽车终于到达时，她已经等了一个小时。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I don't know what will happen next. 我不知道接下来会发生什么。
The meeting will happen tomorrow afternoon. 会议将于明天下午举行。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be working late when the game happens. 比赛进行时，我将工作到很晚。
They will be traveling to Europe when the conference happens. 会议召开时，他们将前往欧洲旅行。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, the event will have happened.  到下周，该事件将已经发生。
By the time you arrive, everything will have happened already. 等你到达的时候，一切都将已经发生。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, they will have been living in this house for ten years. 到明年，他们将已经在这所房子里住了十年。
By the time she graduates, she will have been studying medicine for six years. 到她毕业时，她将已经学了六年的医学。

虚拟语气:
If it were to happen, I would be prepared. 如果它发生了，我会做好准备。
I wish it hadn't happened. 我希望它没有发生。

被动语态 (While grammatically possible, "happen" is rarely used in the passive voice.)
It was made to happen.  它是被促成的。(This is a less common and somewhat awkward usage)
Things are happening that cannot be explained.  正在发生一些无法解释的事情。 (This is not truly passive; "things" are the subject acting)

疑问句:
What happened? 发生了什么事？
How did it happen? 它是如何发生的？

否定句:
Nothing happened. 什么也没发生。
It didn't happen the way I expected. 它并没有像我预期的那样发生。
