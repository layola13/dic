mirificent:/mɪˈrɪfɪsənt/| adj.|奇迹般的；惊人的；极好的|Extremely wonderful or marvelous; causing wonder or astonishment.|例句：The mirificent sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues. 奇妙的日落将天空染上了鲜艳的色彩。

例句：Her mirificent performance captivated the audience. 她精彩的表演吸引了观众。

近义词：wonderful, marvelous, amazing, extraordinary, splendid, magnificent|反义词：ordinary, mundane, unimpressive, terrible, awful|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *mirificus* (“wonderful, marvelous, causing wonder”), from *mirus* (“wonderful, strange”) + *-ficus* (suffix forming adjectives from verbs).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *smeyros* (“smiling, lovely”).
