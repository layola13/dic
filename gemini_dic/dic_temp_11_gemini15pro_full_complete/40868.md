cookbooks:/ˈkʊkbʊks/ | n. |食谱，烹饪书| A book containing recipes and instructions for preparing food. |  I have a collection of cookbooks from all over the world. 我收藏了来自世界各地的烹饪书。| synonyms: recipe books, culinary guides | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology: From "cook" (v.) + "book" (n.). "Cook" comes from Old English "coc" (noun), meaning "cook," from the verb "cocian," meaning "to cook." "Book" comes from Old English "boc," meaning "book, writing, document."
