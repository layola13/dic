capot:/ˈkæpoʊt/ (US), /ˈkæpɒt/ (UK)| n. |引擎盖；(尤指汽车的)车盖；(飞机的)座舱罩|A hinged cover for the engine of a motor vehicle or the cockpit of an aircraft. |例句：The mechanic opened the capot to check the engine. 技工打开引擎盖检查发动机。

例句：The pilot closed the capot before takeoff. 飞行员在起飞前关上了座舱罩。

|近义词：hood, bonnet (British English)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
