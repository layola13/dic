interamnian:/ˌɪntərˈæmniən/
adjective
两河之间的；美索不达米亚的
situated or dwelling between rivers; specifically : Mesopotamian
The interamnian plains were fertile.  两河之间的平原很肥沃。
近义词:Mesopotamian
反义词: (No specific antonym, consider using terms like "extra-Mesopotamian" or specifying a location outside Mesopotamia)
词性:adjective
