corroborate: /kəˈrɒbəreɪt/ (UK) /kəˈrɑːbəreɪt/ (US) | v. | 证实，确证 | To confirm or support a statement, theory, or finding with evidence. | The witness corroborated the victim's account of the robbery.  证人证实了受害者对抢劫案的描述。| synonyms: confirm, verify, substantiate, validate | antonyms: contradict, refute, deny, disprove | verb

corroborated: /kəˈrɒbəreɪtɪd/ (UK) /kəˈrɑːbəreɪtɪd/ (US)| adj. | 被证实的，得到确证的 | Supported or confirmed with evidence. | The police found corroborated evidence of the suspect's guilt. 警方发现了证实嫌疑人有罪的确凿证据。| synonyms: confirmed, verified, substantiated, validated | antonyms: contradicted, refuted, denied, disproven | adjective
