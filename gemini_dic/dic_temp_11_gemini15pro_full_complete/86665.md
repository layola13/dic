hellenistically:/ˌhɛlɪˈnɪstɪkəli/
adv.
希腊化地，希腊主义地
In a way that relates to or reflects the culture of ancient Greece, especially the period from the death of Alexander the Great to the Roman conquest.
The library was decorated hellenistically, with statues of Greek gods and goddesses.  图书馆以希腊化的风格装饰，摆放着希腊诸神和女神的雕像。
The city was planned hellenistically, with a grid pattern of streets and a central marketplace. 这座城市是按照希腊化的方式规划的，街道呈网格状，中心设有市场。
Synonym: Greek-like
Antonym: Roman-like
词性: 副词
