clam:/klæm/| n.|蛤，蛤蜊；沉默寡言的人|A type of shellfish with a hinged shell.  A person who is not talkative.|We dug for clams at the beach. 我们在海滩挖蛤蜊。|Synonyms: shellfish, bivalve|Antonyms: (for person) chatterbox, extrovert|noun

clams:/klæmz/| n.|蛤，蛤蜊（clam的复数形式）|Plural form of clam.  Refers to multiple clams.|The clams were delicious steamed with garlic. 这些蛤蜊用蒜蓉蒸非常美味。|Synonyms: shellfish, bivalves|Antonyms: (for person) chatterboxes, extroverts|noun
