decker:/ˈdɛkər/| n. | 甲板；双层公共汽车；双层床|A person or thing that decks, especially a ship's deckhand. Also (as a combining form) denoting a ship or other structure with a specified number of decks, as in double-decker. | The double-decker bus is a common sight in London.  双层巴士在伦敦很常见。| The sailor worked as a decker on the ship.  这名水手在船上做甲板水手。| synonyms: deckhand, sailor (for a ship's decker), double-decker (for a bus or bed), tiered | antonyms: N/A | noun
