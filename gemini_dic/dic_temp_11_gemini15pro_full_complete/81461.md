gratuitous:/ɡrəˈtjuːɪtəs/ | adj. | 无理由的，无端的；免费的；多余的 | given, done, or obtained without charge or payment; done without good reason; uncalled for | The film was criticized for its gratuitous violence. 这部电影因其无端的暴力而受到批评。|  Example: The movie contained a gratuitous amount of nudity.  这部电影包含了过多的裸露镜头。

| Synonyms: unnecessary, unwarranted, needless, unjustified, complimentary, free | Antonyms: necessary, essential, justified, warranted, paid |
