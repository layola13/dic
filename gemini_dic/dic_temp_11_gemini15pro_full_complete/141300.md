open-mindedness:/ˌoʊpənˈmaɪndɪdnəs/ | n. | 开放思想；心胸开阔 | The state of being receptive to new ideas or experiences; willingness to consider different perspectives. |  She approached the problem with open-mindedness and a willingness to learn. 她以开放的心态和学习的意愿来处理这个问题。| synonyms: receptiveness, tolerance, impartiality | antonyms: close-mindedness, intolerance, prejudice | noun
