incorporally:/ɪnˈkɔːrpərəli/
adjective
无实体地；非物质地；精神上地
not in bodily form; not materially; spiritually
He believed that angels existed incorporally, watching over humans. 他相信天使以无形的方式存在，守护着人类。
The concept of justice exists incorporally, guiding our laws and actions. 正义的概念以非物质的形式存在，指导着我们的法律和行为。
近义词:immaterially, spiritually, intangibly
反义词:physically, bodily, materially
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin incorporalis, from in- "not" + corporalis "bodily," from corpus "body."
