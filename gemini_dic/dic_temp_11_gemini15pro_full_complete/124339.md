mothy:/ˈmɒθi/ (UK), /ˈmɔːθi/ (US)| adj.| 蛾子似的；多蛾的；虫蛀的|Infested with moths or moth larvae; resembling a moth, especially in having a drab or speckled appearance.|The old house smelled musty and mothy.  这老房子闻起来又霉又像有蛾子。|The curtains were old and mothy. 窗帘又旧又满是虫蛀痕迹。|近义词：moth-eaten, insect-ridden|反义词：pristine, immaculate|词性:形容词
词源: From moth + -y.
