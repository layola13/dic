humbuggery:/ˈhʌmbʌɡəri/
noun
欺骗；谎言；胡说
Deceptive or false talk or behavior.
The salesman's promises were pure humbuggery, and I regret falling for them.  这个推销员的承诺纯粹是胡说八道，我很后悔上当受骗了。
He dismissed the claims as sheer humbuggery. 他把这些说法斥为纯粹的胡扯。
synonyms: deception, fraud, trickery, dishonesty, sham, pretense
antonyms: honesty, truthfulness, sincerity, genuineness
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From humbug + -ery.  Humbug itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from a slang term for a hoax or trick.
