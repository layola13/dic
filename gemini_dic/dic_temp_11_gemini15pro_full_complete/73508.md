foreseize:/fɔːrˈsiːz/
| vt.|预先抓住；先占；预先阻止；预先没收|To seize beforehand; to anticipate and prevent.|The government foreseized the criminal's assets before he could flee the country. 政府在他逃离该国之前预先没收了他的资产。|
|Synonyms: anticipate, preempt, forestall, prevent|
|Antonyms: allow, permit, facilitate|
|verb|

foreseize:/fɔːrˈsiːz/
| vt.|预先占有；抢先获得|To take possession of something before others can.|The company foreseized the opportunity to expand into new markets.  这家公司抢先获得了拓展新市场的机会。|
|Synonyms: seize, grasp, secure, obtain|
|Antonyms: lose, miss, forfeit|
|verb|
