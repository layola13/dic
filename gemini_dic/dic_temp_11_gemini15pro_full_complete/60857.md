electuary:/ɪˈlɛktʃʊəri/ /ɪˈlɛktuəri/
noun
糖膏剂；药糖浆
A medicinal paste consisting of a powder or other ingredient mixed with honey or syrup.
The physician prescribed an electuary to soothe the patient's sore throat.  医生给病人开了一种药糖浆来舒缓喉咙痛。
Synonyms: confection, medicinal paste, remedy
Antonyms: poison, toxin
