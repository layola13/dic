bipetalous:/baɪˈpɛtəlʌs/
adjective
二瓣的；有两枚花瓣的
Having two petals.
The rare orchid is bipetalous, a characteristic that distinguishes it from other species.  这种稀有的兰花是二瓣的，这一特征使其区别于其他品种。
This plant is characterized by its bipetalous flowers. 这种植物的特点是它的两瓣花。
synonym:dipetalous
antonym:polypetalous
adjective
