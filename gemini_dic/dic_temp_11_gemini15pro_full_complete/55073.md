disrelate:/ˌdɪsrɪˈleɪt/
verb
使…不相关；使…脱离关系
To deprive of or cease to have a relationship or connection.
The study disrelates income from education levels.  这项研究使收入与教育水平脱离关系。
The new policy disrelates performance from pay. 新政策使绩效与薪酬脱钩。
disconnect, detach, separate
relate, connect, associate
v.
