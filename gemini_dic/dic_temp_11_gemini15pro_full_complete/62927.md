enmeshed:/ɪnˈmeʃt/ |adj.| 纠缠的，卷入的 | Involved in something complicated or unpleasant that it is difficult to escape from. | The whales were enmeshed in fishing nets. 鲸鱼被渔网缠住了。| 近义词：entangled, trapped, involved | 反义词：free, extricated, uninvolved | 词性: 形容词

enmeshed:/ɪnˈmeʃt/ |v.| 缠住，卷入 | Cause to become entangled in something. |  His affairs were enmeshed with hers. 他的事情与她的事情纠缠在一起。 | 近义词：entangle, involve, trap | 反义词：free, extricate, disentangle | 词性: 动词

词源:  From en- + mesh.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "in" or "into," and "mesh" refers to the open spaces in a net.  Therefore, "enmeshed" literally means "in the meshes."
