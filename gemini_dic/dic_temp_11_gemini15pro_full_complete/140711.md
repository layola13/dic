omniety: /ɒmˈnaɪɪti/ | n. | 全知，无所不知 | The state of knowing everything. |  God is often associated with omniety. 上帝通常与全知联系在一起。 |  He aspired to a state of omniety, believing he could learn everything. 他渴望达到全知的状态，相信自己可以学到一切。 | 近义词: omniscience | 反义词: ignorance, nescience | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin omnis (“all”) + -ety (a suffix forming abstract nouns).  来自拉丁语 omnis（“所有”）+ -ety（构成抽象名词的后缀）。
