oversearch:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrtʃ/
verb
过度搜索；过度检索
To search excessively or too thoroughly, often to the point of diminishing returns or inefficiency.
The researcher oversearched the databases and ended up with thousands of irrelevant articles.  研究人员过度搜索了数据库，最终得到了数千篇不相关的文章。
They oversearched the internet for information, wasting valuable time. 他们在互联网上过度搜索信息，浪费了宝贵的时间。
近义词: over-research, overanalyze
反义词: undersearch, skim
词性: verb
