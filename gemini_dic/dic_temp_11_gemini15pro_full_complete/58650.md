dumbly:/ˈdʌmli/
adverb
愚蠢地；呆呆地；默默地
in a stupid or foolish manner; silently
She stared dumbly at the screen, unable to process the information. 她呆呆地盯着屏幕，无法处理信息。
He dumbly nodded, not understanding a word she said. 他傻傻地点了点头，一个字也没听懂她说的话。
近义词:stupidly, foolishly, silently, blankly
反义词:intelligently, wisely, eloquently
词性:副词
