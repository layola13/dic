lactant:/ˈlæktənt/| n.|乳汁分泌物；哺乳动物|A substance, such as lactose, that promotes lactation or stimulates milk production.  A mammal, especially while suckling. |The lactant secreted by the mammary glands provides essential nutrients for the newborn.  乳腺分泌的乳汁为新生儿提供必要的营养。 The lactant kangaroo joey stayed in its mother's pouch.  小袋鼠幼崽待在妈妈的育儿袋里。|近义词：suckling,  nursing infant|反义词: weanling (断奶的幼畜)|词性:noun
