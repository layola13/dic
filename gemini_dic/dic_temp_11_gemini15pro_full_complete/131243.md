noncarbolic:/ˌnɒnkɑːˈbɒlɪk/
adjective
非石炭酸的，不含石炭酸的
Not containing or derived from carbolic acid (phenol).
The wound was cleaned with a noncarbolic antiseptic.  伤口用非石炭酸的防腐剂清洗。
The hospital used noncarbolic soap.  这家医院使用不含石炭酸的肥皂。
近义词: nonphenolic
反义词: carbolic
