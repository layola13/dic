fore-alleged:/fɔːr-əˈlɛdʒd/| adj. |先前声称的，先前指控的|Claimed or asserted previously; previously alleged.  This refers to something that has been alleged earlier in a discussion, investigation, or legal proceeding.|The fore-alleged perpetrator was later found to be innocent. 先前声称的犯罪者后来被发现是无辜的。| The report addressed the fore-alleged financial irregularities. 报告探讨了先前指控的财务违规行为。|Synonyms: previously alleged, formerly claimed, earlier asserted|Antonyms: unclaimed, undenied, undisputed|adjective
