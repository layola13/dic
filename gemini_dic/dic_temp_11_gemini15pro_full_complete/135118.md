nonoccult:/ˌnɑːnəˈkʌlt/
adjective
非神秘的；不隐秘的；明显的
Not occult; not hidden from view or easily observable.
The doctor diagnosed a nonoccult gastrointestinal bleed. 医生诊断出明显的消化道出血。
Near Synonyms: overt, apparent, manifest, obvious, patent, unconcealed, evident, noticeable
Antonyms: occult, hidden, concealed, covert
词性: 形容词
