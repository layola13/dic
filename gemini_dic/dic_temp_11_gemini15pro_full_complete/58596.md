dull-witted:/ˌdʌlˈwɪtɪd/ | adj. | 愚钝的，迟钝的 | Lacking intelligence; slow to understand. | He made a dull-witted remark that offended everyone. 他说了一句愚蠢的话，冒犯了所有人。 |  She's not malicious, just a bit dull-witted. 她并非恶意，只是有点愚钝。 | 近义词：stupid, unintelligent, slow, obtuse | 反义词：intelligent, bright, sharp, clever | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From dull + witted.  "Dull" meaning lacking sharpness or vibrancy, and "witted" referring to mental sharpness or intelligence.
