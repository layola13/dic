entailer:/ɪnˈteɪlər/ | n. |  蕴涵者，必然结果 |Something that entails something else; a logically necessary consequence. | Building a new highway entails acquiring the necessary land.  修建一条新高速公路必然需要征用必要的土地。 | synonyms: consequence, result, implication, corollary | antonyms: cause, origin, antecedent | noun

entail:/ɪnˈteɪl/ | v. | 使需要，必然包含 | To involve something as a necessary or inevitable part or consequence. | This project will entail a lot of hard work. 这个项目将需要大量的辛勤工作。| synonyms: necessitate, involve, require, demand | antonyms: exclude, preclude, obviate | verb
