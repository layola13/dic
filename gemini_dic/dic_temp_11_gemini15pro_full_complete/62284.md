end in smoke:/ɛnd ɪn sməʊk/ | idiom | 以失败告终；化为泡影 | To result in failure; to come to nothing.  The project ended in smoke after the main investor pulled out. | 例句：All his plans ended in smoke. 他所有的计划都落空了。

例句：Their ambitious venture ended in smoke due to lack of funding.  他们雄心勃勃的企业由于缺乏资金而以失败告终。| 近义词：fail, fall through, come to nothing, fizzle out | 反义词：succeed, triumph, be successful | 词性：idiom (短语)
