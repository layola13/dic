overhardening:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdnɪŋ/
|n.| 过度硬化 |The overhardening of the steel made it brittle. 钢的过度硬化使其变脆。|
| |Excessive hardening, resulting in brittleness and reduced toughness.  指过度硬化，导致材料变脆，韧性降低。|
| |The overhardening of the metal caused it to crack under pressure.  金属的过度硬化导致其在压力下破裂。|
| |over-tempering, excessive hardening|
| |under-hardening, insufficient hardening|
|noun|
