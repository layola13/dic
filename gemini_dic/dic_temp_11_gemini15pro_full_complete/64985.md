estonians:/ɛˈstoʊniənz/
noun
爱沙尼亚人
People from Estonia, a country in Northern Europe.
Many Estonians speak both Estonian and Russian. 许多爱沙尼亚人既说爱沙尼亚语也说俄语。
近义词:citizens of Estonia, Estonian people
反义词:(None -  nationality doesn't have a direct opposite)
词性:n


Etymology:
From Estonia + -ian (suffix forming adjectives from nouns denoting places or regions).  Estonia itself is derived from the German name "Estland," ultimately from an ethnonym used by the people themselves.
