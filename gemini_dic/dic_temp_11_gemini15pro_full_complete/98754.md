intact:/ɪnˈtækt/| adj.|完整的，未受损的，原封不动的|Undamaged in any way; complete.|例句：The vase survived the fall intact. 花瓶摔落后仍然完好无损。|例句：He emerged from the scandal with his reputation intact. 他从丑闻中全身而退，名誉丝毫未损。|近义词：whole, complete, undamaged, unbroken, unimpaired|反义词：damaged, broken, destroyed, impaired|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *intactus*, from *in-* (not) + *tangere* (to touch).
