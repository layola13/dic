laborer:/ˈleɪbərər/ (US), /ˈleɪbərə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 劳动者，工人 |A person who does unskilled manual work for wages. |The laborers worked tirelessly in the hot sun. 工人们在烈日下不知疲倦地工作。|Synonyms: worker, workman, hand, navvy| Antonyms: employer, boss, manager| noun
