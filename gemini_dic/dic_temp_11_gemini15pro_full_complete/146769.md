overhypes:/ˌoʊvərˈhaɪps/| v.|过度炒作；过分宣传|To promote or publicize excessively, often to the point of creating unrealistic expectations.|The media overhyped the new phone, and it didn't live up to the expectations.|近义词：overrates, exaggerates, inflates|反义词: downplays, understates|词性:动词

overhypes:/ˌoʊvərˈhaɪps/| adj.|被过度炒作的|Excessively promoted or publicized, often to the point of creating unrealistic expectations.|The overhyped movie turned out to be a disappointment.|近义词：overrated, overblown|反义词: underrated, understated|词性:形容词


词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "hype" (from "hyperbole," meaning exaggeration).
