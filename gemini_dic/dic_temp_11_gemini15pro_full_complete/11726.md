aroideous:/ˌɛərɔɪdiəs/
adj.
天南星科植物的；类似天南星科植物的
Resembling or characteristic of plants of the Aroid family (Araceae), typically having a spathe and spadix.
The lush, aroideous foliage created a tropical feel in the greenhouse.  郁郁葱葱的天南星科植物叶子为温室营造了一种热带的感觉。
近义词:araceous
反义词: (No common antonym)
词性:adjective
