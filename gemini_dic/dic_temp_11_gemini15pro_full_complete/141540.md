The company's opportunisms in the market led to rapid growth, but also some questionable business practices. 公司在市场上的机会主义导致了快速增长，但也导致了一些有问题的商业行为。 | 近义词: exploitation, expediency, pragmatism | 反义词: principle, integrity, scrupulousness | 词性: noun (plural)
