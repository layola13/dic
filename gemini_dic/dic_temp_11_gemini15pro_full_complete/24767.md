brooks:/brʊks/ | n. | 小溪；溪流 | A small stream. |  The clear brooks tumbled down the mountainside. 清澈的小溪从山坡上流下来。| synonyms: streams, creeks, rivulets | antonyms: rivers, oceans | noun
