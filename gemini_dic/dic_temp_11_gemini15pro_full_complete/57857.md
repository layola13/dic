dretch:/dretʃ/
n.
卑鄙的人；可怜虫；堕落的人
A despicable or contemptible person; a wretch.
The dretch cowered in the corner, fearing the wrath of his master.  那个卑鄙的人蜷缩在角落里，害怕主人的愤怒。
He felt like a dretch after losing all his money in the gambling den.  在赌场输光所有钱后，他觉得自己像个可怜虫。

近义词: wretch, villain, scoundrel, miscreant, reprobate
反义词: saint, hero, paragon

词性: noun

词源:  From Middle English *drecche, from Old English *dreccan (“to trouble, vex, oppress”), from Proto-Germanic *drekkjaną (“to press, oppress”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrengʰ- (“to pull, drag, draw”). Cognate with Dutch dreg (“dreg, sediment”), German drücken (“to press, push, squeeze”), Icelandic drekka (“to drink”).  More at drag.
