antityphoid:/ˌæntiˈtaɪfɔɪd/ | adj. n. | 抗伤寒的；抗伤寒药（剂） | Preventing or counteracting typhoid fever.  Relating to a substance or medication used to prevent or treat typhoid fever. |  The doctor prescribed an antityphoid medication. 医生开了抗伤寒药。| Synonyms: antityphic | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "pro-typhoid" in a theoretical sense, meaning something that promotes typhoid, though this isn't a real word) | adjective, noun
