medallic:/məˈdælɪk/
adjective
奖牌的；似奖章的；适于制作奖牌的
Of or relating to medals; resembling a medal; suitable for making medals.
The collection included several medallic portraits of Roman emperors.  这批收藏品包括几幅罗马皇帝的奖章肖像。
synonyms: numismatic, commemorative, award-winning
antonyms: non-medallic, unawarded
