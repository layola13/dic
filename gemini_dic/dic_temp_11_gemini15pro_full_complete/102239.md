itemize:/ˈaɪtəmaɪz/ | v. | 列举；详细列出 | to list things separately, often including details about each one |  She itemized all the things she wanted to buy. 她详细列出了她想买的所有东西。

| The invoice itemizes all the goods delivered and their prices.  发票列出了所有交付的货物及其价格。 | list, detail, specify, enumerate | generalize | verb
