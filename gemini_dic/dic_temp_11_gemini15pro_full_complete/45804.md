custodianship:/kʌˈstoʊdiənʃɪp/
n.
监护权；保管；保管责任
The state of being responsible for and protecting something. The legal right to care for someone, especially a child, or to manage their financial affairs.
The museum has the custodianship of a vast collection of antique toys.  这家博物馆负责保管一大批古董玩具。
The court awarded custodianship of the children to their mother. 法院将孩子的监护权判给了他们的母亲。
近义词: guardianship, care, safekeeping, protection
反义词: neglect, abandonment

词性: noun
词源: From Latin custos (“guardian”) + -ship.
