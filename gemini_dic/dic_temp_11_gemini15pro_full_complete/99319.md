intercrusts:/ˈɪntərkrʌsts/
n.
硬壳之间的物质；地壳之间的物质
Material between crusts or layers, especially within the Earth's crust.
The geologists discovered rare minerals in the intercrusts of the tectonic plates.  地质学家在构造板块的夹层中发现了稀有矿物。
The intercrusts between the layers of rock contained fossilized remains. 岩层之间的夹层包含着化石遗骸。
近义词: layers, strata, fillings
反义词: surface, exterior
