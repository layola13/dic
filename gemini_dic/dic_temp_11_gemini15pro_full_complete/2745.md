adulteration:/əˌdʌltəˈreɪʃn/
n.
掺假，伪造，劣质化
the act of making something impure or weaker by adding something of poor quality
The adulteration of food products is a serious crime. 食品掺假是严重的犯罪行为。

近义词:debasement, contamination, dilution, sophistication
反义词:purification, refinement
