headhunted:/ˈhedˌhʌntɪd/ |adj.|猎头的，被猎头挖走的|Being approached by a recruiter for a job opportunity, often a senior or specialized role.  被招聘人员接洽工作机会，通常是高级或专业职位。|例句：She was headhunted by a rival company. 她被一家竞争对手公司挖走了。

例句：He felt flattered to be headhunted for the CEO position. 他对被猎头挖去担任CEO一职感到受宠若惊。|近义词：recruited, poached|反义词: N/A |词性: adjective

headhunt:/ˈhedˌhʌnt/ |v.|猎头，物色人才|To identify and approach suitable individuals for employment, typically in senior or specialized positions.  识别并接洽适合担任高级或专业职位的人才。|例句：Companies headhunt executives from competitors.  公司从竞争对手那里挖走高管。

例句：They are headhunting for a new marketing director.  他们正在物色一位新的营销总监。|近义词：recruit, poach|反义词: N/A |词性: verb
