americanized:/əˈmɛrɪkənaɪzd/
adjective
美国化的
Made or become American in character; assimilated to the customs and institutions of the United States.
The restaurant serves americanized Chinese food.  这家餐厅供应美式中餐。
The immigrants quickly became americanized. 这些移民很快就美国化了。
Near Synonyms: westernized, anglicized
Antonyms: traditional, indigenous
词性: 形容词
