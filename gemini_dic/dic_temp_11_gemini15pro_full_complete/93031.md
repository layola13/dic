idleby:/ˈaɪdəlˌbaɪ/ | n. | 闲散旁观者; 无所事事的人 | A person who is present but inactive, especially during a time of work or crisis.  |  The idleby stood watching the others work. 那些闲散旁观者站着看着别人干活。| 近义词: onlooker, bystander, lounger | 反义词: participant, worker, contributor | 词性: 名词

词源:  Compound word from "idle" (inactive) + "by" (near, beside).  Essentially meaning "idle by the side."
