intertranslatability:/ˌɪntərtrænsˌleɪtəˈbɪləti/| n.|互译性，可互译性|The intertranslatability of programming languages allows developers to easily port code from one platform to another. 编程语言的互译性使开发人员可以轻松地将代码从一个平台移植到另一个平台。|例句：The high intertranslatability between these two dialects makes it easy for speakers to understand each other. 这两种方言之间的高度互译性使得说话者很容易互相理解。

例句：The intertranslatability of the software ensures it can be used in multiple language settings.  这款软件的互译性确保它可以在多种语言环境中使用。

|近义词：translatability, convertibility, compatibility|反义词:incompatibility, untranslatability|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
inter- (prefix): between, among (from Latin)

late (verb): to carry, bring, bear (from Latin "latus")
-able (suffix): capable of, worthy of (from Latin "-abilis")
-ity (suffix): state or quality of being (from Latin "-itas")
