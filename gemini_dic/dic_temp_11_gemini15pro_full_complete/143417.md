outflashed:/ˌaʊtˈflæʃt/
verb
胜过，以更强的闪光压倒
surpassed in flashing; emitted a brighter flash than.
The lightning outflashed the fireworks. 闪电的光芒胜过了烟火。
outshone, eclipsed, outdazzled
