例句：Bubos are a characteristic symptom of the bubonic plague. 腹股沟淋巴结肿大是腺鼠疫的典型症状。|近义词：swollen lymph nodes,  |反义词:(No direct antonym exists, but the absence of bubos would indicate a lack of the associated condition.)|词性:noun (plural)
