larvae:/ˈlɑːrviː/
n.
幼虫
The immature, wingless, feeding stage of an insect that undergoes metamorphosis.
Mosquito larvae thrive in stagnant water.  蚊子幼虫在死水中 thrive。
近义词：grub, caterpillar, maggot
反义词: adult, imago
词性: 名词

larva:/ˈlɑːrvə/
n.
幼虫
The immature, wingless, feeding stage of an insect that undergoes metamorphosis.
The larva of a butterfly is called a caterpillar. 蝴蝶的幼虫叫做毛毛虫。
近义词：grub, caterpillar, maggot
反义词: adult, imago
词性: 名词


词源: From Latin *larva* "ghost, mask, evil spirit," of uncertain origin.  The biological sense developed in the 17th century, perhaps influenced by the notion of the insect being "masked" in its larval form.
