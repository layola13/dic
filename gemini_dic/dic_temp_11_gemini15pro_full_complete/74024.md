forwardly:/ˈfɔːrwərdli/| adv.|鲁莽地，冒失地；唐突地；率直地|In a bold and assertive manner, sometimes considered rude or presumptuous.  Moving or directed ahead.|She forwardly expressed her opinion, even though it contradicted the boss's view. 她唐突地表达了自己的观点，即使这与老板的观点相悖。|Synonyms: boldly, brashly, impudently, frankly, directly|Antonyms: timidly, shyly, reservedly, cautiously|词性:副词

forwardly:/ˈfɔːrwərdli/| adv.|向前地|Moving or directed ahead.|The troops marched forwardly towards the enemy lines. 部队向前朝着敌人的防线行进。|Synonyms: onward, ahead, forwards|Antonyms: backward, backwards|词性:副词
