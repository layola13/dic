overconservative:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsɜːrvətɪv/| adj.| 过于保守的，过度保守的|Excessively cautious and resistant to change; holding onto traditional values or methods too rigidly.|例句：His overconservative investment strategy resulted in missed opportunities. 他过于保守的投资策略导致错失良机。

例句：The overconservative dress code at the school prohibited even slightly colorful clothing.  学校过于保守的着装规定甚至禁止穿稍微鲜艳的衣服。|近义词：reactionary, die-hard, ultraconservative|反义词：progressive, liberal, radical|词性:形容词

词源: over- + conservative
over-(prefix):  excessively, too much
conservative (adjective): averse to change or innovation and holding traditional values.  From Latin conservativus, from conservare ‘preserve.’
