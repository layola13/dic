federacy:/ˈfɛdərəsi/| n.|联邦；同盟；联邦政府|A political entity characterized by a union of partially self-governing states or regions united by a central (federal) government.|The United States of America is a federacy. 美国是一个联邦制国家。| The Swiss federacy has a long history of neutrality.  瑞士联邦有着悠久的中立历史。|Synonyms: federation, confederation, union, alliance, league|Antonyms: empire, monarchy, dictatorship|Noun
