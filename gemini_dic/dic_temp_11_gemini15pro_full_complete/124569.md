mourner:/ˈmɔːrnər/ (US) /ˈmɔːnə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 哀悼者，送葬者 | A person who grieves or shows sorrow for someone who has died. |  She was a mourner at the funeral. 她是一位葬礼上的哀悼者。

The mourners gathered at the graveside. 哀悼者们聚集在墓旁。

近义词：grieving person, bereaved person | 反义词: celebrant | 词性: noun
