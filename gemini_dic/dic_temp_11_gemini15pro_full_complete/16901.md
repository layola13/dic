basisphenoid:/ˈbeɪsɪˌsfɪnɔɪd/| n. |基蝶骨|The basisphenoid is a bone in the skull base of vertebrates.|The basisphenoid forms part of the floor of the braincase.|近义词:presphenoid|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From basi- + spheno- + -oid.  Basi- comes from Greek *basis* meaning "base." Spheno- comes from Greek *sphēn* meaning "wedge." -oid is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "having the form of."  Therefore, basisphenoid literally means "wedge-shaped base."
