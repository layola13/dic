gulls:/ɡʌlz/ | n. |海鸥|Large seabirds with long wings and usually white or grey feathers.  They are often found near the coast.|例句：The gulls were circling overhead, looking for food. 海鸥在头顶盘旋，寻找食物。

例句：The beach was crowded with tourists and noisy gulls. 海滩上挤满了游客和吵闹的海鸥。|近义词：seabirds, seafowl |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用land birds 陆地鸟类) |词性:noun
