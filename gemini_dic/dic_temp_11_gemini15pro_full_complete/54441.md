disingenuously:/ˌdɪsɪnˈdʒɛnjuəsli/
adverb
不真诚地，不坦率地
in a way that is not sincere and hides something
He disingenuously claimed to know nothing about the matter. 他不真诚地声称对这件事一无所知。
近义词:dishonestly, deceptively, falsely
反义词:genuinely, sincerely, honestly
词性:adverb

disingenuous:/ˌdɪsɪnˈdʒɛnjuəs/
adjective
不真诚的，不坦率的
not candid or sincere, typically by pretending that one knows less about something than one really does
Her answer struck me as disingenuous. 她的回答让我觉得不真诚。
近义词:insincere, dishonest, deceitful
反义词:genuine, sincere, honest
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *disingenuus, from dis- "not" + ingenuus "frank, open".  The English word was formed from the Latin roots in the 16th century.
