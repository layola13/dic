kinkle:/ˈkɪŋkəl/| n. v.|扭结，卷曲；(使)起皱，(使)纠结|A kinkle is a small sharp bend or twist in something, especially a wire or thread.  To kinkle is to form a kinkle or kinkles; to become or cause something to become twisted or tangled.|The wire developed a kinkle, preventing the signal from passing through. 这根电线出现了一个扭结，阻止了信号通过。|近义词：twist, bend, curl, tangle|反义词:straighten|词性:名词, 动词

kinkle:/ˈkɪŋkəl/| v.| (使)起皱，(使)纠结|To kinkle is to become or cause something to become twisted or tangled, often forming small wrinkles or creases.|The ribbon kinkled when I tried to tie a bow.  当我试图系蝴蝶结时，丝带皱了起来。|近义词：crinkle, wrinkle, crumple|反义词:smooth, flatten|词性:动词
