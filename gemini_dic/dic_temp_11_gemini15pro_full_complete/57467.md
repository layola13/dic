draftiest:/ˈdræf.ti.ɪst/
adjective
通风最多的；最冷的
Most drafty; having the most drafts (currents of cold air).
The draftiest room in the house was the attic.  房子里通风最厉害的房间是阁楼。
Synonyms: airiest, chilliest, coldest, breeziest
Antonyms: warmest, stillest, air-tight
词性: 形容词
