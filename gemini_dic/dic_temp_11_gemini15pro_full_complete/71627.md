flatlined: /ˈflætˌlaɪnd/ | v. | 变平；失去活力；(尤指心跳)停止；死亡 | To become inactive or unchanging, often referring to a cessation of heartbeat and therefore death. | The patient's heart rate flatlined, and the doctors rushed to revive him. 病人的心跳停止了，医生们赶紧抢救他。 | The company's sales flatlined after the initial boom. 公司的销售额在最初的繁荣之后就停滞不前了。 |  近义词: stalled, stagnated, ceased, died | 反义词: surged, increased, revived | 词性: 动词

flatlined: /ˈflætˌlaɪnd/ | adj. | 平直的；停滞的；无生命的 | Inactive, unchanging, or lifeless. | The economy has been flatlined for the past few quarters. 过去几个季度经济一直停滞不前。 | The EEG showed a flatlined brainwave pattern, indicating brain death. 脑电图显示脑波呈平直状态，表明脑死亡。| 近义词: stagnant, inactive, lifeless | 反义词: active, dynamic, lively | 词性: 形容词
