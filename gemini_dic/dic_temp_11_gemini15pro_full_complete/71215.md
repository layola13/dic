fitchy:/ˈfɪtʃi/| adj.|尖的，(纹章学用语)尖底的|Pointed; especially (Heraldry) having the lower part sharpened to a point.|The shield was charged with a fitchy cross. 盾牌上饰有尖底十字架。|近义词：pointed, sharp, acute|反义词:blunt, rounded|词性:形容词
词源:From Old French *fichis, past participle of ficher (“to fix, stick”), from Frankish *fikkan, from Proto-Germanic *fikōną (“to stick, fix”). Cognate with Middle High German vichen (“to stick, fix”).  More at fix.
