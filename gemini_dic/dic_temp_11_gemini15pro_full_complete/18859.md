beseechings:/bɪˈsiːtʃɪŋz/
n.
恳求，乞求
earnest and humble requests or prayers
The king ignored the beseechings of his people. 国王无视了他的人民的恳求。
近义词：pleas, supplications, entreaties, petitions
反义词：demands, commands, orders
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:
From Middle English *besechyng, besekyng, from Old English bēseċung (“supplication”), equivalent to beseech + -ing.  Cognate with Old Saxon bisōkian (“to seek, beseech”), Old High German bisuochen (“to visit, seek, beseech”), German besuchen (“to visit”). The word ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *sōkijaną (“to seek”).
