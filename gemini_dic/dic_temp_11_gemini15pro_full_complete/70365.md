fieriest:/ˈfɪəriɪst/| adj.|最激烈的，最火爆的，最炽烈的|superlative adjective of fiery; most fiery.  Characterized by or displaying extreme passion, anger, or heat.|The fieriest debates were saved for last.  最激烈的辩论留到了最后。|近义词：hottest, most intense, most passionate, angriest|反义词：calmest, coolest, mildest|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *fieri, from Old French fier "proud, fierce, cruel, intense, strong, great", from Latin ferus "wild, untamed".  The "-est" suffix denotes the superlative form.
