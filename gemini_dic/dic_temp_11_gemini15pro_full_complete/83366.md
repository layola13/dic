n.
gyrolepis（旋鳞鱼）
An extinct genus of fish characterized by thick, rhomboidal scales.  A type of extinct fish with thick, diamond-shaped scales.
The fossil of Gyrolepis was found in Triassic rocks. 在三叠纪岩石中发现了旋鳞鱼的化石。
Gyrolepis is an important index fossil for the Triassic period. 旋鳞鱼是三叠纪的重要标准化石。
