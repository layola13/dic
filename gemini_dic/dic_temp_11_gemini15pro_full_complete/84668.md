handcloth:/ˈhændklɒθ/ (UK), /ˈhændklɔːθ/ (US)| n. |毛巾，洗脸巾|A small towel used for washing one's face or hands.|She used a warm handcloth to clean her face. 她用一条温热的毛巾擦脸。|Synonym: washcloth, facecloth, flannel (UK)|Antonym: N/A|noun
Etymology: From 'hand' + 'cloth', literally a cloth for the hands.  First recorded use in the late 16th century.
