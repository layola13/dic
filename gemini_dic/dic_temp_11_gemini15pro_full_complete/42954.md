covercle: /ˈkʌvərkl/ | n. | 盖子；罩子 | A lid or covering, especially for a container. | The silver covercle gleamed atop the tureen. 银色的盖子在汤碗上闪闪发光。|  The covercle of the pot rattled as the water boiled. 水烧开时，锅盖发出嘎嘎声。 | lid, cap, top | bottom | noun

Etymology:
From Old French *covercle, from Vulgar Latin *cooperculum, from Latin cooperculum (“lid, cover”), from cooperire (“to cover over”), from com- + operire (“to cover”).
