misencoding:/ˌmɪsɪnˈkoʊdɪŋ/ | n. | 编码错误，错误编码 | The misencoding of the data caused significant problems. 数据的错误编码导致了严重的问题。|  The misencoding of the file resulted in data loss.  文件的错误编码导致数据丢失。| 近义词: encoding error, incorrect encoding | 反义词: correct encoding | 词性: 名词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning wrong or bad) + "encoding" (from "encode," meaning to convert data into a coded form).  "Mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss." "Encode" ultimately derives from Latin "in-" (in, into) and "codex" (book, tablet).
