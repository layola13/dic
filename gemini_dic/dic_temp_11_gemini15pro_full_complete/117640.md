megathere:/ˈmɛɡəˌθɪər/ (US), /ˈmɛɡəˌθɪə/ (UK)| n. |大地懒|A large extinct ground sloth of the Pleistocene epoch, native to North and South America.|The museum displayed a complete megathere skeleton.  博物馆展出了一个完整的大地懒骨架。|Example: Scientists are studying the fossils of megatheres to understand their extinction. 科学家们正在研究大地懒的化石以了解它们的灭绝原因。|Synonym: giant ground sloth|Antonym: N/A|Noun
