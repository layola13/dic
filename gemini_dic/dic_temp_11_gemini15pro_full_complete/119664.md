middleweights:/ˈmɪdlˌweɪts/| n.| 中量级选手；中等重要的人（或事物）|A boxer who weighs between 160 and 168 pounds.  A person or thing of moderate importance or influence.|The fight was between two experienced middleweights. 这场比赛是两位经验丰富的重量级选手之间的对决。|Near synonyms:  mid-range, medium-sized, average|Antonyms: heavyweights, lightweights|noun
