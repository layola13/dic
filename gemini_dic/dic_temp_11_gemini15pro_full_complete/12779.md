assimilate:/əˈsɪməleɪt/ /əˈsɪmɪleɪt/| v. |吸收; 同化; 消化| to absorb and integrate (people or ideas) into a wider society or culture.|例句：Immigrants assimilate into the local culture. 移民融入当地文化。

例句：The body assimilates nutrients from food. 身体从食物中吸收营养。|近义词：absorb, integrate, incorporate, digest|反义词：reject, segregate, differentiate|词性：动词

assimilate:/əˈsɪməleɪt/ /əˈsɪmɪleɪt/| n. |被同化的事物; 被吸收的事物|something that has been assimilated.|例句：The new laws are assimilates from older traditions. 新法律是从旧传统中吸收的。

例句：The company's assimilates included new technologies and strategies. 该公司的吸收内容包括新技术和战略。|近义词：adoption, integration|反义词：rejection, segregation|词性：名词
