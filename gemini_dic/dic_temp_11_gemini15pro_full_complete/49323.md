delightless:/dɪˈlaɪtləs/
adjective
无乐趣的，不愉快的
Lacking delight or pleasure; joyless.
The delightless grey landscape stretched before them.  他们面前是一片毫无乐趣的灰色景象。
The funeral was a delightless affair. 葬礼是一个令人不愉快的场合。
近义词:joyless, cheerless, dreary, bleak, somber
反义词:delightful, joyous, cheerful, pleasant
词性:adjective

词源:
From delight + -less.  由delight(乐趣)和-less(无)组成。
