n.
结肠炎
Inflammation of the colon.
Example 1:  The doctor diagnosed him with colonitis after a series of tests.  医生经过一系列检查后诊断他患有结肠炎。

Example 2:  Her colonitis flared up after eating spicy food.  她吃了辛辣食物后结肠炎发作了。
Synonym: colitis
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider "colonic health")
