episodicity:/ˌɛpɪsəˈdɪsɪti/ | n. | 插曲性，偶发性，片段性 | The quality of occurring in episodes or separate incidents; not continuous or systematic. |  The episodicity of the rain made it difficult to plan outdoor activities.  这阵雨的断断续续使得很难计划户外活动。|  The artist's work is marked by its episodicity, jumping between different themes and styles. 这位艺术家的作品以其片段性为特征，在不同的主题和风格之间跳跃。 | discontinuity, sporadic nature,  intermittency | continuity, persistence, regularity | noun
