burbly:/ˈbɜːr.bli/ | adj. |汩汩作响的，多泡的|Making a bubbling sound; effervescent.  Characterized by a continuous murmuring sound. |The stream made a burbly sound as it flowed over the rocks.  小溪流过岩石发出汩汩的声音。| The baby gurgled and made burbly noises. 婴儿咯咯地笑着，发出汩汩的声音。 |近义词：gurgling, bubbling, effervescent |反义词:still, silent |词性:形容词

词源: From "burble" + "-y". "Burble" is likely imitative of the sound.
