anomia:/əˈnoʊmiə/| n.|失语症，命名性失语|A medical condition characterized by an inability to name objects or to recognize the written or spoken names of objects.|She suffered from anomia after the stroke and struggled to name everyday items. 她中风后患上了失语症，难以说出日常用品的名称。|近义词: aphasia|反义词: - |noun
