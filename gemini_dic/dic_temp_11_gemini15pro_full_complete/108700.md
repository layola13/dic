leamer:/ˈliːmər/ | n. |狐猴|A small, nocturnal primate with large eyes, native to Madagascar. | 例句：The lemur leaped from branch to branch. 狐猴在树枝间跳跃。 | 例句：We saw several different species of lemur at the zoo. 我们在动物园看到了几种不同的狐猴。 | 近义词：primate | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *lémur*, from Latin *lemures* "ghosts, spirits of the dead," perhaps from Proto-Italic *\lemōrēs*,  ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\lem-*. The name was given due to the nocturnal habits and large reflective eyes of lemurs.
