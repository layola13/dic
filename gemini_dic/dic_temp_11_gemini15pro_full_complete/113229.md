lusophone:/ˈluːsəˌfoʊn/ | adj. n.| 讲葡萄牙语的；讲葡萄牙语的人 |  Relating to the Portuguese language or to Portuguese-speaking countries or people. A person who speaks Portuguese, especially as a native language. |例句：Brazil is the largest lusophone country in the world. 巴西是世界上最大的葡语国家。|例句：The conference brought together lusophone writers from around the globe.  这次会议汇集了来自世界各地的葡语作家。|近义词：Portuguese-speaking|反义词: (No direct antonym, could use non-lusophone) |词性: adjective, noun
