disproportionableness:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənəblnəs/
n.
不相称；不成比例；不均衡
The quality of being disproportionate; lack of proportion.
The disproportionableness of her reaction surprised everyone. 她的反应如此过激，让每个人都感到惊讶。
近义词:disproportion, imbalance, inequality, disparity
反义词:proportion, balance, equality, symmetry
词性:名词

Etymology:
From disproportionate + -ness.  Disproportionate comes from dis- (not) + proportionate (proportional). Proportionate derives from Late Latin proportionatus, past participle of proportionare "to make proportionate," from Latin proportio "comparative relation, analogy," from pro "for" + portio "share, portion."
