detersion:/dɪˈtɜːrʒən/
n.
清洗；去垢；洗涤；清洁剂
the act of cleansing or washing; the state of being cleansed
例句：The detersion of the wound is crucial for preventing infection.  伤口清洗对于防止感染至关重要。

例句：Proper detersion of fruits and vegetables is important for food safety.  正确清洗水果和蔬菜对食品安全很重要。

近义词：cleansing, purification, washing, cleaning
反义词：contamination, soiling

词源：From Latin dētersiō (“a wiping off, cleansing”), from dētergeō (“I wipe off, cleanse”).
