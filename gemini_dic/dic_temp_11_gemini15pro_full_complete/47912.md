decentralize:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪz/ | v. |分散，去中心化 |To distribute the administrative powers or functions of (a central authority) over a less concentrated area.  To reorganize (a government, industry, etc) into smaller, more autonomous units.|The company decided to decentralize its operations and give more power to regional offices. 公司决定将其业务分散化，赋予区域办事处更多权力。|Synonyms: distribute, disperse, devolve |Antonyms: centralize, consolidate |verb

decentralization:/diːˌsɛntrələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 分散化，去中心化|The process of decentralizing an organization or government.|The decentralization of power led to greater regional autonomy. 权力下放导致更大的区域自治。|Synonyms: distribution, dispersal, devolution |Antonyms: centralization, consolidation |noun

decentralized:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 分散的，去中心化的 | Divided or distributed into more than one center.|The company has a decentralized structure, with offices all over the country.  这家公司结构分散，在全国各地都设有办事处。|Synonyms: distributed, dispersed |Antonyms: centralized, consolidated |adjective
