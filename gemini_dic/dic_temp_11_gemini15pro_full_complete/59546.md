eau:/oʊ/ (US) /əʊ/ (UK)| n.|水|Water.  Specifically, a type of water, often mineral water. From French "eau", meaning "water".|The restaurant offers a selection of fine eaux. 这家餐厅提供精选优质矿泉水。|Synonym: water, mineral water, |Antonym:  (No direct antonym for water itself, could consider aridity, dryness for the concept of lack of water)|noun
