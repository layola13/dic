cicuta:/saɪˈkjuːtə/ | n. | 毒芹；毒芹属植物 | A highly poisonous plant of the parsley family, especially the water hemlock. |  Cicuta is one of the most poisonous plants in North America. 毒芹是北美最毒的植物之一。

Cicuta poisoning can cause seizures and respiratory failure. 毒芹中毒会导致癫痫发作和呼吸衰竭。| 近义词: water hemlock, cowbane | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a poisonous plant, perhaps consider "edible plant" or a specific non-poisonous plant) | 词性: noun
