menar:/meˈnɑːr/ | v. | 意味着; 打算; 预示; 导致 | To have as a meaning; intend to convey. |  She didn't mean to hurt you. 她不是故意要伤害你。 |  signify, intend, portend, lead to | indicate, express, suggest | v.


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What does this word mean? =  这个词是什么意思？
This symbol means good luck. = 这个符号象征着好运。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you meaning by that? = 你那样说是什么意思？ (较少用)
What is he meaning to do? = 他打算做什么? (较少用，更常用 "What does he mean to do?")

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What has this experience meant to you? = 这段经历对你来说意味着什么？
I have always meant to visit Paris. = 我一直想去巴黎。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been meaning to call you. = 我一直想给你打电话。
She has been meaning to tell you the truth. = 她一直想告诉你真相。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did she mean by that remark? = 她那句话是什么意思？
I meant to tell you earlier. = 我本来想早点告诉你的。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was meaning to ask you about that. = 我正想问你这件事。(较少用)
He was meaning to leave when the phone rang. = 他正打算离开，这时电话响了。(较少用)

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had meant to finish the report before the meeting. = 我本来打算在会议前完成报告的。
She had meant to tell him the news, but she forgot. = 她本想告诉他这个消息，但她忘了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been meaning to visit her for ages. = 我一直想去看她，很久了。
He had been meaning to fix the car. = 他一直想修车。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will this decision mean for the future? = 这个决定对未来意味着什么？
I will mean to do it tomorrow. (较少用，更常用 "I will do it tomorrow" 或 "I intend to do it tomorrow")

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be meaning to ask him about it. (非常少用，更常用 "I will ask him about it")
She will be meaning to visit you soon. (非常少用，更常用 "She will visit you soon")

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, I will have meant to call you for a month. (不自然，更常用 "By next week, I will have intended to call you for a month.")
By then, she will have meant to finish the project. (不自然，更常用 "By then, she will have intended to finish the project.")

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been meaning to learn Spanish. (不自然)
By then, he will have been meaning to start his own business. (不自然)
