hindmost:/ˈhaɪndməʊst/ (UK) /ˈhaɪndmoʊst/ (US)| adj.|最后面的；最末尾的|Situated at the very back or end of something; furthest to the rear.|The hindmost car in the race was a bright red Ferrari.  比赛中最后一辆车是一辆鲜红色的法拉利。|
The ship's hindmost cabin offered stunning ocean views. 这艘船最后面的舱室可以欣赏到壮丽的海景。|
Synonyms: rearmost, last, final, ultimate, furthest|Antonyms: foremost, first, leading, front|adjective
