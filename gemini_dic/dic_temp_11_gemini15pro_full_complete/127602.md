needfully:/ˈniːdfəli/| adv.|必要地，需要地|If something is done needfully, it is done because it is necessary.|He needfully pointed out the flaws in her argument. 他必要地指出了她论证中的缺陷。|necessarily, essentially, indispensably|unnecessarily, needlessly|adverb
