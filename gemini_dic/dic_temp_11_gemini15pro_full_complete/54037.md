discusses:/dɪˈskʌsɪz/
verb
讨论，谈论
talks about something with another person or group of people
The committee discusses the proposal. 委员会讨论了这项提议。
近义词: talks over, debates, considers, examines
反义词: ignores, avoids, disregards
词性:v.


Etymology:
From Middle English *discussen, from Late Latin discussus, past participle of discutere (“to shake apart, scatter, disperse, break up, investigate, examine”), from dis- (“apart”) + quatere (“to shake”).
