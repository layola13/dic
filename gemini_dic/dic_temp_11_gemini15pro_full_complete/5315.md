allowableness:/əˈlaʊəbəlnəs/
n.
允许性；可允许性；正当性
The allowableness of the expense claim was questioned.  这笔费用报销的正当性受到了质疑。
The allowableness of such behavior is debatable. 这种行为的可允许性是有争议的。
permissibility, legitimacy, validity
unacceptability, impropriety
noun
