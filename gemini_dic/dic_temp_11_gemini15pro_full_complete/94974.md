in-the-black:/ɪn ðə blæk/
adjective
盈利；赢利；顺差
having money; profitable; not in debt
例句：The company has been in the black for the past three years.  这家公司过去三年一直盈利。

例句：After years of struggle, the business is finally in the black.  经过多年的奋斗，这家企业终于盈利了。

近义词：profitable, solvent, creditworthy
反义词：in the red, insolvent, bankrupt
词性：adjective
