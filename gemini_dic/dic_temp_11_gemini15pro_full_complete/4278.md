airway:/ˈeəˌweɪ/| n. |呼吸道；航线；航空公司|A passage for air into or out of the lungs. A designated route followed by aircraft.|例句：The smoke filled the airway, making it difficult to breathe. 烟雾充满了呼吸道，使呼吸困难。

例句：The airline opened a new airway between the two cities. 这家航空公司开通了两座城市之间的新航线。|近义词：air passage, respiratory tract, flight path, route|反义词:blockage (for respiratory sense)|词性:名词
