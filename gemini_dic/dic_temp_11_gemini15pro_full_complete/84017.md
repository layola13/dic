half-dried:/ˌhɑːfˈdraɪd/
adjective
半干的，未完全干燥的
Partially dried; not completely dry.
The half-dried laundry hung stiffly on the line.  半干的衣服僵硬地挂在绳子上。
The grapes were half-dried in the sun to make raisins. 葡萄在阳光下半干以制成葡萄干。
Near synonyms: partially dried, semi-dried
Antonyms: completely dried, desiccated, soaked
adjective
