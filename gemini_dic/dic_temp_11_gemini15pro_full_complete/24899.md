bruising:/ˈbruːzɪŋ/| adj. n.| 瘀伤的，令人沮丧的；瘀伤 |The bruising loss left the team demoralized. 这场惨痛的失利让球队士气低落。|Synonyms:  contusion,  injurious, damaging, harmful|Antonyms: healing, soothing, beneficial|adjective

bruising:/ˈbruːzɪŋ/| n.| 瘀伤 |The boxer had a nasty bruising around his eye.  拳击手的眼睛周围有一块严重的瘀伤。|Synonyms: contusion,  hematoma|Antonyms: healing|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bruise + -ing. Bruise comes from Old French bruisier, of uncertain origin.
