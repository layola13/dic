flapped:/flæpt/
verb
拍打，摆动，慌张
moved (something) up and down or back and forth
The bird flapped its wings and flew away. 鸟儿拍打着翅膀飞走了。
近义词:fluttered, waved, agitated
反义词:still, motionless
词性:v.

flapped:/flæpt/
adjective
(非正式) 慌张的，激动的
(informal) agitated, flustered
He got flapped when he heard the bad news. 他听到坏消息后慌了神。
近义词:flustered, agitated, panicked
反义词:calm, composed
词性:adj.
