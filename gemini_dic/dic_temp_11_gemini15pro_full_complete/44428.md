cross-posts:/ˈkrɒsˌpəʊsts/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsˌpoʊsts/ (US)| n. v.|交叉发布，跨平台发布|The act of posting the same message, link, or content to multiple online forums, social media platforms, or newsgroups.|She cross-posts all her Instagram photos to her Facebook page. 她把所有Instagram上的照片都交叉发布到她的Facebook页面上了。|He cross-posted the job ad to several websites. 他把招聘广告交叉发布到了几个网站上。|synonyms: syndicate, share, distribute, disseminate|antonyms: isolate, restrict, confine|noun, verb
