localizabilities:/ˌloʊkələˌzaɪzəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|可本地化性|The quality of something being capable of being localized (adapted to a particular place or market).  This often refers to software, websites, or other products that need to be adapted for different languages, cultures, or regions.|The localizabilities of the new software are impressive, supporting over 20 languages out of the box.|Near Synonyms: adaptability, translatability|Antonyms: non-adaptability, untranslatability|noun
