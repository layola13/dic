cenobitism:/ˈsɛnəbɪtɪzəm/
n.
修道院生活; 共住生活
The practice of living together in a religious community, especially in a monastery or convent.
Monastic life, characterized by communal living and adherence to a set of religious rules.
例句：Cenobitism offers a structured environment for spiritual growth.  修道院生活提供了一个结构化的环境以促进精神成长。

例句：The monks embraced cenobitism as a path to enlightenment.  僧侣们将修道院生活作为通往启蒙的道路。

近义词：monasticism, cloistered life, communal living
反义词：eremitism, anchoritism, seclusion
