mahout:/məˈhaʊt/| n. |驯象人，赶象人|A person who works with, rides, and cares for elephants, especially in India and Southeast Asia.|例句：The mahout guided the elephant through the dense jungle. 驯象人带领大象穿过茂密的丛林。

例句：The mahout skillfully controlled the elephant with verbal commands and gentle prods.  驯象人熟练地用口令轻拍控制着大象。|近义词：elephant driver, elephant keeper, elephant rider|反义词: None|词性: noun
