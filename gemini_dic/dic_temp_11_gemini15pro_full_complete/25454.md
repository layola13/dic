bulldogging:/ˈbʊldɒɡɪŋ/ | n. | 摔牛；(非正式)顽强的努力 | The act of wrestling a steer to the ground by seizing its horns and twisting its neck, or (informally) any persistent and determined effort. | Example: Bulldogging a steer is a dangerous rodeo sport. 摔牛是一项危险的竞技表演运动。

Example:  He showed real bulldogging in getting the project finished on time. 他在按时完成项目方面表现出了真正的顽强精神。 | 近义词: wrestling, grappling, persistence, tenacity | 反义词: giving up, quitting | 词性: 名词
