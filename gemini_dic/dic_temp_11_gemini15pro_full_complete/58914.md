durrie:/ˈdʌri/ | n. |粗毛地毯，毛毯；尤指南非或澳大利亚的|A heavy, woven floor covering, especially one made in southern Africa or Australia. |She bought a colorful durrie for her living room. 她为她的客厅买了一块色彩鲜艳的粗毛地毯。|Synonyms: rug, carpet, mat |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps bare floor) | noun
