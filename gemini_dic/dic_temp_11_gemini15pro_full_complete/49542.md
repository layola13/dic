demasculinized: /ˌdiːˈmæskjʊlənaɪzd/
| adj. | 去男性化的，被剥夺男性气质的 | Deprived of masculine characteristics or qualities; made less masculine. |  The advertisement portrayed a demasculinized image of men, showing them doing traditionally feminine tasks.  这则广告描绘了一个去男性化的男性形象，展示了他们正在做传统上女性化的工作。

The author argued that modern society is demasculinizing boys by discouraging traditional male roles. 作者认为现代社会通过阻止男孩扮演传统男性角色的方式，正在使他们去男性化。| 近义词: effeminate, unmasculine, weakened | 反义词: masculinized, strengthened, empowered | 词性: 形容词
