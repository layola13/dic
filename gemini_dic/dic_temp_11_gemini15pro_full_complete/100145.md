interracially:/ˌɪntərˈreɪʃəli/
adv.|跨种族地，不同种族之间地|Involving or occurring between people of different races.|例句：They were married interracially. 他们跨种族结婚了。|近义词：multiracially|反义词：intraracially|词性:副词
Etymology: inter- (between) + racial + -ly (adverbial suffix)
