multigauge:/ˈmʌltiɡeɪdʒ/
adjective
多规格的，多轨距的
designed or calibrated for more than one gauge or standard.
This multigauge railroad track can accommodate trains of different gauges. 这条多轨距的铁轨可以容纳不同轨距的火车。
multi-purpose, versatile
single-gauge, fixed-gauge
adjective
