outsainting:/ˌaʊtˈseɪntɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的圣洁；在圣洁方面胜过
Trying to appear more virtuous or morally superior than someone else.
She was outsainting him with her charitable works. 她用她的慈善事业胜过他的圣洁。
Synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel
Antonyms:  lag behind, fall short
词性: 动词
