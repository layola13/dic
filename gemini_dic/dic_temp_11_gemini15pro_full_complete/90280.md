houseleek: /ˈhaʊsliːk/ | n. |  长生草，瓦松 | A succulent plant of the genus *Sempervivum*, typically having rosettes of fleshy leaves and star-shaped pink or yellow flowers.  It is often grown on roofs and walls. |  Houseleeks are very drought-tolerant plants. 长生草是非常耐旱的植物。 |  Synonyms: hen and chickens, liveforever | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
