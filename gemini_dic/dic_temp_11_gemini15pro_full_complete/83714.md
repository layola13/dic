hagler:/ˈheɪɡlər/| n. |讨价还价者；经常争论的人|A person who argues persistently, especially about the cost of something; a hard bargainer.|He's a real haggler; he got the price down by 20%. 他真是个砍价高手，把价格压低了20%。|Synonyms: bargainer, negotiator, barterer|Antonyms: pushover, soft touch|noun
