gipser:/ˈɡɪpsər/ | n. | 灰泥匠，抹灰工 | A person who works with plaster, especially one who applies plaster to walls or ceilings. |  He hired a gipser to repair the cracked walls.  他雇了一个灰泥匠来修补破裂的墙壁。 | Synonyms: plasterer, stuccoer | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps demolition worker in some contexts) | noun
