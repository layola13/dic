discordantly:/dɪˈskɔːrdəntli/ | adv. |不和谐地，刺耳地 |  In a discordant manner; clashing or conflicting.  Characterized by a lack of harmony or agreement. | The instruments played discordantly, creating a cacophony of sound.  乐器演奏得很不和谐，发出刺耳的声音。|  harshly, jarringly, inharmoniously | harmoniously, agreeably, concordantly | adverb

discordant:/dɪˈskɔːrdənt/ | adj. |不和谐的，刺耳的 | Disagreeing or incongruous.  Conflicting or clashing.  Harsh and jarring in sound. | The discordant voices of the choir made the song unpleasant to listen to.  合唱团不和谐的声音使得这首歌听起来很刺耳。|  harsh, jarring, inharmonious, conflicting | harmonious, agreeable, concordant | adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *discordant-, from Latin discordans, present participle of discordare (“to disagree”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + cord- (“heart”).
