morphologic:/ˌmɔːrfəˈlɒdʒɪk/ /ˌmɔːrfəˈlɑːdʒɪk/ | adj. |  形态学的 | Relating to the forms of things, especially the forms of words in linguistics or the forms of living organisms in biology. |  The morphologic characteristics of the virus are unique.  这种病毒的形态特征是独一无二的。 | synonyms:  formal, structural, morphological | antonyms: functional, behavioral | 词性: adjective

morphology:/mɔːrˈfɒlədʒi/ /mɔːrˈfɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 形态学 | The study of the forms of things, in particular: 1. The study of the forms and structure of words in linguistics. 2. The study of the forms and structure of organisms in biology. | The morphology of the plant suggests it belongs to a new species.  这种植物的形态表明它属于一个新物种。 | synonyms: structure, form, anatomy, configuration | antonyms: N/A | 词性: noun
