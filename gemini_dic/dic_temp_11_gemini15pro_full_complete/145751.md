overcultivate: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌltɪveɪt/
verb
过度耕作；过度开发
to cultivate (land) excessively, leading to depletion of soil nutrients.
The farmers overcultivated the land, resulting in poor harvests in later years. 农民过度耕作土地，导致后来几年收成不好。
The constant need for higher yields led them to overcultivate the fields.  对更高产量的持续需求导致他们过度耕作田地。

overfarm, overuse
undercultivate, neglect
v.
