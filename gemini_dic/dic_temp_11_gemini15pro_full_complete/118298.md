mercurially:/mərˈkjʊəriəli/
| adv. |反复无常地，易变地；机智地，敏捷地|Characterized by rapid and unpredictable changeableness of mood.  Also, in a clever or intelligent way.|Her moods shifted mercurially, from cheerful excitement to sullen withdrawal. 她的情绪反复无常，从兴高采烈的兴奋到闷闷不乐的沉默。 He responded mercurially, with a witty retort that silenced his opponent. 他机智地回应，用一句妙语反驳使他的对手哑口无言。|Synonyms:  erratically, capriciously, fickly, volatilely, temperamentally;  nimbly, sharply, cleverly |Antonyms:  steadily, consistently, stably;  dully, sluggishly, stupidly |词性: 副词
