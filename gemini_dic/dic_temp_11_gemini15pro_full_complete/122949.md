momma:/ˈmɑːmə/ (US), /ˈmɒmə/ (UK)| n. |妈妈，母亲 (Informal, often used by children)|An informal and affectionate term for mother, often used by young children. It can also be used by adults in some dialects or as a term of endearment.|例句：Momma, can I have a cookie? 妈妈，我可以吃块饼干吗？

例句：He called out for his momma when he fell down. 他摔倒时喊着要妈妈。|近义词：mom, mommy, mother, mum (UK)|反义词: father, dad |词性: 名词
