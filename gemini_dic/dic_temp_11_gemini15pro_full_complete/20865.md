blatteration:/ˌblætəˈreɪʃən/
noun
胡说八道，废话，空话
Senseless, voluble talk or writing; blather.
He ignored their blatteration and continued with his work. 他无视他们的胡言乱语，继续工作。
近义词: blather, babble, prattle, gibberish, nonsense
反义词: sense, logic, reason
词性: noun

Etymology:
From blater (to chatter, prate), from Middle Dutch blateren, from Old French blatrer (to bleat). Ultimately of imitative origin.
