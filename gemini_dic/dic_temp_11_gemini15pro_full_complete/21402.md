blout: /blaʊt/
verb
炫耀，吹嘘
to brag or boast loudly and conspicuously
He blouted about his new car to everyone he met. 他逢人便吹嘘他的新车。
近义词：boast, brag, vaunt
反义词：downplay, minimize
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *blouten*, from Old Norse *bauta* "to strike, beat," related to Old English *bēatan* "to beat." The semantic shift from "to strike" to "to boast" is likely due to the image of striking one's chest in a display of pride.
