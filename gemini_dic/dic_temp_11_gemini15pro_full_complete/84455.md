haloing:/ˈheɪloʊɪŋ/ /ˈheɪləʊɪŋ/ | n. |晕轮，光晕效应|The act of perceiving a positive quality in one thing and allowing that perception to influence your opinions of other things related to the first.|The CEO's charisma created a haloing effect, leading employees to believe the company's products were superior even without evidence. 首席执行官的个人魅力营造了一种光环效应，导致员工即使没有证据也相信公司的产品很优秀。|近义词：bias, prejudice |反义词: objective assessment |词性:名词

haloing:/ˈheɪloʊɪŋ/ /ˈheɪləʊɪŋ/ | v. |使...晕轮，产生光晕效应|To cause or experience a halo effect.|His athletic achievements haloed his academic performance, leading teachers to overlook his shortcomings in class. 他的体育成就掩盖了他的学业表现，导致老师忽视了他在课堂上的不足。|近义词：glorify, idealize |反义词: criticize, scrutinize |词性:动词
