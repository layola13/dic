outswaggering: /ˌaʊtˈswæɡərɪŋ/
Verb
以更swagger的方式胜过；在swagger方面超过
Outdoing someone in swaggering; surpassing in swagger.
He outswaggered the other cowboys in the saloon. 他在酒吧里比其他牛仔更swagger。
Synonyms: outbragging, outboasting, outposing
Antonyms:  being humble, being modest
