allosauruses:/ˌæləˈsɔːrəsiz/ | n. |异特龙（复数）| Large, carnivorous dinosaurs that lived during the Late Jurassic period.  They are characterized by their powerful jaws, sharp teeth, and three-clawed hands. | Example: Allosauruses were apex predators of their time. 异特龙是它们那个时代的顶级掠食者。| Example:  The museum exhibit featured a complete skeleton of one of the largest allosauruses ever discovered.  博物馆展览展出了一具迄今为止发现的最大的异特龙之一的完整骨架。| 近义词: dinosaurs, theropods (更广义的近义词) | 反义词:  N/A (没有生物学上的反义词，可以考虑prey猎物作为生态学上的反义词) | Noun
