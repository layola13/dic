overgive:/ˌoʊvərˈɡɪv/
verb
过度给予；过分付出
to give too much of something
He overgave at the office, working late nights and weekends. 他在办公室里过度付出，经常加班到深夜和周末。
overspend, overdo
underspend, underdo
词性: 动词
