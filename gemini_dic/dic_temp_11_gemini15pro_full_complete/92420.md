hypolemniscus:/ˌhaɪpoʊlɛmˈnɪskəs/
n.
下丘系
A tract of nerve fibers in the brainstem that carries sensory information.
The hypolemniscus plays a crucial role in processing auditory stimuli. 下丘系在处理听觉刺激中起着至关重要的作用。
Synonyms: medial lemniscus (though not a perfect synonym, as they carry different sensory information)
Antonyms: N/A (Anatomical structures typically don't have direct antonyms)
