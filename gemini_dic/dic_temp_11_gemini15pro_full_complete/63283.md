enthroned:/ɪnˈθroʊnd/| adj. | 登基的， enthroned on a chair.|Placed in a position of authority or dignity, especially on a throne.  The newly enthroned king addressed his subjects. 新登基的国王向他的臣民讲话。|近义词：crowned, inaugurated, installed|反义词:dethroned, deposed|词性:形容词
enthrone:/ɪnˈθroʊn/| vt. | 使登基，使 enthrone the new king.|To place (someone) in a position of authority or dignity, especially on a throne.  The ceremony enthroned the new queen.  仪式使新女王登基。|近义词：crown, inaugurate, install|反义词:dethrone, depose|词性:动词
