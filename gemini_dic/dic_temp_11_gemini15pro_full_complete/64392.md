eremitish:/ˌerəˈmɪtɪʃ/ | adj. | 隐士般的，遁世的，孤僻的 | Like a hermit; solitary and withdrawn from society. |  He led an eremitish existence in the remote mountains. 他在偏远的山区过着隐居般的生活。

| Example: She has become quite eremitish since retiring.  她退休后变得相当孤僻。| Synonym: reclusive, secluded, solitary, withdrawn | Antonym: gregarious, sociable, outgoing | word property: adjective
