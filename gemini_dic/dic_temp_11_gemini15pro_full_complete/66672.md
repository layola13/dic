exocrines:/ˈɛksəkrɪnz/| n.|外分泌腺|Glands that secrete their products through ducts onto epithelial surfaces, such as the skin or the lining of the digestive tract.  These products are not hormones.|The pancreas has both endocrine and exocrine functions. 胰腺兼有内分泌和外分泌功能。|近义词:glandular epithelium|反义词:endocrines|词性:名词
