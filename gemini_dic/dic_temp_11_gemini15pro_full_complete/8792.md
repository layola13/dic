antielitism:/ˌæntiɪˈliːtɪzəm/
n.
反精英主义
opposition to elitism; the belief that everyone is equal and should have the same rights and opportunities, regardless of their social status, education, or wealth.
The candidate's antielitism resonated with working-class voters.  这位候选人的反精英主义主张引起了工薪阶层选民的共鸣。

近义词:populism, egalitarianism
反义词:elitism, snobbery
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix meaning "against") + elite + -ism (suffix forming nouns of action, state, or doctrine)
