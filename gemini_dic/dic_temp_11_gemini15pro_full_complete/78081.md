geodiferous:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈdɪfərəs/
adjective
含晶洞的；多晶洞的
Containing geodes or like geodes.  Bearing geodes.
The geodiferous basalt formations in the area attract geologists and rock hounds alike.  该地区的含晶洞玄武岩地层吸引了地质学家和岩石爱好者。
The cave was known for its geodiferous walls, sparkling with crystals. 这个洞穴以其布满晶洞的墙壁而闻名，墙壁上闪耀着水晶。
近义词: geode-bearing, crystal-lined
反义词: solid, uncrystallized
词性: adjective
词源: From *geo-* (earth) + *-diferous* (bearing), ultimately from Latin *ferre* (to bear).
