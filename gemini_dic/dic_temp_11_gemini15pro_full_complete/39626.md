consequences:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənsɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəkwɛnsɪz/ (US)| n. |结果，后果|a result or effect of an action or condition.|The consequences of her actions were severe. 她的行为后果很严重。|synonyms: results, outcomes, effects, repercussions|antonyms: causes, origins|noun

consequence:/ˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəkwɛns/ (US)| n. |结果，后果|a result or effect of an action or condition.|He ignored the warnings and suffered the consequences. 他无视警告，自食其果。|synonyms: result, outcome, effect, repercussion|antonym: cause, origin|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French consequence ("result, conclusion, importance"), from Latin consequentia ("a following together"), from consequentem (nominative consequens), present participle of consequi ("to follow after"), from con- ("together") + sequi ("to follow").
