adambulacral: /ˌædæmˈbjʊləkrəl/ | adj. n. | 步带的；管足沟的；步带骨板 | Relating to the ambulacra, the radial areas on the oral surface of echinoderms (like starfish and sea urchins) that bear the tube feet.  Also, one of the ossicles or plates supporting the ambulacra. | The starfish uses its adambulacral feet for locomotion. 海星用它的步带足来移动。|  The adambulacral spines of the sea urchin are modified for feeding. 海胆的步带棘被修改用于进食。 | Synonyms: ambulacral | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym exists, as this is a specific anatomical term) | adjective, noun
