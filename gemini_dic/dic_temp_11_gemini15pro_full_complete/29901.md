cautery: /ˈkɔːtəri/ (US) /ˈkɔːtəri/ (UK)| n.| 烧灼；烙术；烙器；烧灼剂 |The doctor used cautery to stop the bleeding. 医生用烧灼来止血。| Example: The surgeon used cautery to remove the wart.  外科医生用烧灼术去除了疣。|Synonyms:  cauterization, burning, searing|Antonyms:  cooling, freezing| Noun

cauterize: /ˈkɔːtəraɪz/ (US) /ˈkɔːtəraɪz/ (UK)| vt.| 烧灼；烙(伤口等)以止血或消毒|The wound was cauterized to prevent infection. 伤口被烧灼以防止感染。|Example:  They cauterized the blood vessel to stop the bleeding. 他们烧灼了血管来止血。|Synonyms: burn, sear, singe|Antonyms: cool, soothe| Verb
