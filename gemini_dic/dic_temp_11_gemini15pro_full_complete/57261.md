downmarket:/ˌdaʊnˈmɑːrkɪt/| adj. adv.|低档的，面向低端市场的；向下游销售；|Relating to or appealing to the cheaper end of the market; towards the cheaper end of the market.|例句：The company is planning to launch a downmarket version of its flagship product.  这家公司计划推出其旗舰产品的低端版本。|例句：They've had to reposition their products downmarket to attract more customers. 他们不得不将产品重新定位于低端市场以吸引更多顾客。|近义词：cheap, budget, inexpensive, low-end|反义词：upmarket, upscale, high-end, luxury|词性:形容词,副词
