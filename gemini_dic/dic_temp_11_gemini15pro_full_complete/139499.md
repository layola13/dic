obstructionistic:/əbˌstrʌkʃəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
阻挠的；妨碍的
Characterized by obstructionism; deliberately hindering or delaying progress.
The senator's obstructionistic tactics prevented the bill from passing.  参议员的阻挠策略阻止了法案的通过。
Synonyms: obstructive, hindering, delaying, impeding, thwarting
Antonyms: cooperative, helpful, facilitating, expediting
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From obstruction + -ist + -ic.  Obstruction comes from Latin *obstructionem, from obstruere "to build against, block up," from ob- "against" + struere "to pile, build."
