extirpate: /ˈɛkstərpeɪt/ | v. | 根除，消灭，彻底摧毁 | To destroy or remove completely; to root out. | The government is trying to extirpate corruption. 政府正在努力根除腐败。 |  The gardener worked diligently to extirpate the weeds from the flowerbed. 园丁努力工作，把花坛里的杂草全部清除掉。| 近义词: eradicate, eliminate, abolish, exterminate | 反义词: cultivate, foster, promote, encourage | 词性: 动词

extirpation: /ˌɛkstərˈpeɪʃən/ | n. | 根除，消灭 | The act of extirpating something. | The extirpation of smallpox was a major medical triumph. 根除天花是一项重大的医学成就。| The extirpation of invasive species is crucial for preserving biodiversity. 消灭入侵物种对于保护生物多样性至关重要。| 近义词: eradication, elimination, abolition | 反义词: cultivation, promotion | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *exstirpāre*, from *ex* (“out”) + *stirps* (“trunk, root, stock”).
