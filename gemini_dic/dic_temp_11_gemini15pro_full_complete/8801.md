antiempiricist:/ˌæntiɪmˈpɪrɪsɪst/| adj. n.|反经验主义的，反经验主义者|Opposing or denying the validity of empiricism (the theory that all knowledge is derived from sense-experience).|例句：The antiempiricist philosopher argued that reason, not experience, is the primary source of knowledge.  反经验主义哲学家认为，理性而不是经验是知识的主要来源。

例句：His antiempiricist stance led him to reject the scientific method. 他的反经验主义立场导致他拒绝科学方法。|近义词：rationalist, a priori, deductivist|反义词：empiricist, experimentalist|词性:adjective, noun
