cutter:/ˈkʌtər/ (US), /ˈkʌtə/ (UK)| n. |切割器，刀具；裁缝；切割机；快艇|A tool or device used for cutting.  A person or thing that cuts. A fast sailing boat.|例句：He used a glass cutter to score the window pane. 他用玻璃刀在窗格玻璃上划了个道子。

例句：The tailor is a skilled cutter. 这位裁缝是一位熟练的裁剪师。

近义词：knife, blade, shears, scissors, slicer, chopper, clipper, yacht, sailboat|反义词:joiner, welder|词性:noun

cutter:/ˈkʌtər/ (US), /ˈkʌtə/ (UK)| v. | 切割，剪切|To cut something.|例句：She cut the fabric with sharp scissors. 她用锋利的剪刀剪布料。

例句：He cut his finger while chopping vegetables. 他切菜时切到了手指。

近义词：slice, chop, sever, carve, trim, clip|反义词:join, weld|词性:verb
