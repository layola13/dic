flammiferous:/fləˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
火焰的；燃烧的；火焰似的
Bearing or producing flame; consisting of flame.
The flammiferous dragon breathed fire across the desolate landscape.  火焰喷射的巨龙在荒凉的土地上喷出火焰。
The magician conjured a flammiferous apparition that startled the audience. 魔术师变出一个火焰般的幽灵，吓了观众一跳。
近义词: fiery, flaming, incandescent, igneous
反义词: nonflammable, fireproof
