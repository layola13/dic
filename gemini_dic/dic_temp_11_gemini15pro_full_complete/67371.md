extensometer:/ɪkˈstenˈsɒmɪtər/ (US) /ɪkˈstenˈsɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |伸长计，引伸计|An instrument used to measure the elongation or extension of a material under stress. |The engineers used an extensometer to measure the strain on the bridge during load testing. 工程师们使用伸长计来测量桥梁在负载测试期间的应变。|Synonyms: strain gauge, dilatometer|Antonyms: compressor|noun
