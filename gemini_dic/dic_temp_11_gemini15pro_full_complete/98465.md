inspissate:/ɪnˈspɪseɪt/ /ɪnˈspɪsˌeɪt/ |v. |使浓稠，使变厚，使加浓；使（思想、文风等）凝练 |to make thicker or denser, especially by evaporation or dehydration; to make (a liquid) thicker by evaporation; to thicken or condense (language or style)
|The chef inspissated the sauce by reducing it on the stovetop. 厨师通过在炉子上慢慢熬煮来使酱汁变浓稠。

|The long, hot summer inspissated the syrup in the maple trees. 漫长炎热的夏天使枫树里的糖浆变得浓稠。


|近义词：thicken, condense, concentrate |反义词：thin, dilute, rarefy |词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin inspissātus, past participle of inspissāre (“to thicken”), from in- (“in, into”) + spissāre (“to thicken”), from Latin spissus (“thick, dense”).
