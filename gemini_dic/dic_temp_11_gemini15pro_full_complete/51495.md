detractiveness:/dɪˈtræktɪvnəs/
n.
减损性; 吸引力降低;  不吸引人
The quality of reducing the value, attractiveness, or desirability of something.
The detractiveness of the graffiti on the building lowered its property value.  建筑物上的涂鸦降低了它的房产价值。
近义词：unattractiveness, ugliness, homeliness
反义词：attractiveness, beauty, appeal
词性: 名词

detract:/dɪˈtrækt/
v.
贬低; 减损;
Reduce or take away from the worth or value of.
The loud music detracted from the enjoyment of the meal. 吵闹的音乐破坏了用餐的乐趣。
近义词：diminish, lessen, reduce
反义词：enhance, increase, augment
词性: 动词

detracting:/dɪˈtræktɪŋ/
adj.
贬低的; 有损的;
Reducing or taking away from the worth or value of.
His detracting comments about her appearance were unnecessary and hurtful. 他对她外貌的贬低性评论是不必要的，而且很伤人。
近义词：unfavorable, negative, critical
反义词：favorable, positive, complimentary
词性: 形容词


词源:  From Late Latin *dētractīvus, from Latin dētractus (past participle of dētrahere ‘draw away, remove’), from dē- ‘away from’ + trahere ‘to draw’.
