dynamometric:/ˌdaɪnəmɒˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 测力计的；测力计的 | relating to or using a dynamometer (an instrument for measuring force, torque, or power). |  A dynamometric test was carried out to measure the engine's output. 进行了一项测力计试验来测量发动机的输出功率。 |The dynamometric properties of the material were carefully analyzed. 对该材料的测力特性进行了仔细分析。 |近义词: force-measuring | 反义词: N/A | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From French *dynamométrique*, from Ancient Greek *dýnamis* (δύναμις, “power, force”) + *métron* (μέτρον, “measure”) + *-ikos* (-ικός, forming adjectives).
