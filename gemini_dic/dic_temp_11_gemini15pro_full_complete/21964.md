bogu:/ˈboʊɡuː/| n.|护具，剑道护具|A set of protective armor worn in kendo, consisting of a men (head protector), a do (chest protector), kote (gauntlets), and tare (hip protector).|Example:  He carefully put on his bogu before the kendo match. 他在剑道比赛前仔细地穿上了护具。

Example:  The bogu protected her from serious injury during the sparring session.  在练习赛中，护具保护她免受重伤。|近义词:armor, protective gear|反义词:(None -  lack of bogu implies vulnerability)|词性:noun
