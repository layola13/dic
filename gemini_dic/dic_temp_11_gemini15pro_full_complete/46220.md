cystidea:/sɪˈstɪdiə/
n.
海蕾纲（已灭绝）
An extinct class of echinoderms, related to blastoids and crinoids, that existed from the Middle Ordovician through the Late Devonian periods. They were characterized by a sac-like body enclosed in calcareous plates.
The cystidea fossils were found in the Ordovician strata.  在奥陶纪地层中发现了海蕾纲化石。
近义词:blastoids, crinoids
反义词:None (Since it's an extinct class, there's no direct opposite.)
词性:noun
