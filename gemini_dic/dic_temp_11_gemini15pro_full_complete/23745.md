bravado:/brəˈvɑːdoʊ/ (US) /brəˈvɑːdəʊ/ (UK)| n.|虚张声势；逞能；装腔作势|A show of boldness intended to impress or intimidate. |He displayed a false bravado that concealed his insecurity. 他表现出一种虚假的逞能，掩饰了他的不安全感。|例句：Despite his bravado, I knew he was scared. 尽管他装腔作势，我知道他很害怕。

例句：Her bravado was just a cover for her nervousness. 她的逞强只是为了掩盖她的紧张。


近义词：swagger, bluster, boastfulness, pretense, ostentation|反义词：timidity, shyness, modesty, humility|词性:名词

词源：From Italian *bravata*, meaning "bragging, boasting," from *bravo* "wild, courageous," of uncertain origin, perhaps from Latin *pravus* "crooked, depraved."
