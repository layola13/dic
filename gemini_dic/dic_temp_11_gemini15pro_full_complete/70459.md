figureless:/ˈfɪɡjərləs/
adjective
无图形的；无花纹的；朴素的
Lacking figures or patterns; plain.
The dress was figureless and simple, perfect for a casual outing. 这条裙子朴素无花纹，适合休闲郊游。
The walls were painted a figureless grey. 墙壁被漆成了朴素的灰色。
Synonyms: plain, simple, unadorned, undecorated
Antonyms: figured, patterned, ornate, decorated
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
figure (n.) + -less (suffix)
figure: from Old French *figure "shape, form, figure," from Latin figura "a shape, form, figure; kind, sort," from PIE root *dheigh- "to form, build."
-less: Old English -lēas "free from, without, deprived of," from Proto-Germanic *lausaz "loose, empty," from PIE root *leu- "to loosen, divide, cut apart."
