muce:/mjuːs/ | n. |霉菌;霉 |A furry growth of minute fungal hyphae, appearing typically in moist warm conditions, especially on food or other organic matter. | The bread had gone mouldy and was covered in green muce.  面包发霉了，上面长满了绿色的霉菌。| synonym: mold, mildew, fungus | antonym: cleanliness, sterility | noun
