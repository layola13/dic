discreetnesses:/dɪˈskriːtnəsɪz/
n.
谨慎；慎重；审慎
The quality of being discreet, especially in conduct or speech; circumspection.
She handled the sensitive situation with the utmost discreetness. 她以最大的谨慎处理了这个敏感的局面。
近义词: prudence, circumspection, tact, diplomacy, reserve, caution
反义词: indiscretion, carelessness, recklessness
词性: 名词 (plural)

discreet:/dɪˈskriːt/
adj.
谨慎的；慎重的；审慎的
Careful and circumspect in one's speech or actions, especially in order to avoid causing offense or attracting attention.
He is very discreet in his personal life. 他的私生活非常谨慎。
近义词: careful, cautious, circumspect, prudent, tactful, diplomatic
反义词: indiscreet, careless, reckless
词性: 形容词
