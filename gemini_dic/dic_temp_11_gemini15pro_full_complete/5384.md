ally:/ˈælaɪ/ (US) /ˈælaɪ/ (UK)| n.  v.|同盟者，盟友；结盟，与…联盟|A person, group, or nation that is associated with another for some common cause or purpose. |例句：The US and the UK were allies during World War II. 美国和英国在二战期间是盟友。|近义词：friend, partner, supporter|反义词:enemy, opponent, foe|noun
ally:/ˈælaɪ/ (US) /ˈælaɪ/ (UK)| v.|结盟，与…联盟|To unite or form a connection or relation between. |例句：Several smaller political parties allied to form a coalition government.  几个较小的政党联合起来组建了联合政府。|近义词：unite, join forces, combine|反义词:separate, divide, oppose|verb
