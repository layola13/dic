lloyds: /lɔɪdz/ | n. |  劳合社; 劳埃德船级社 |  A British insurance market and society of underwriters, known especially for insuring ships.  Also, a classification society (Lloyd's Register) that certifies the seaworthiness of ships. |  The ship was insured at Lloyds.  这艘船在劳埃德船级社投保了。 | synonyms:  insurance market, underwriters, maritime insurer | antonyms: N/A | noun
