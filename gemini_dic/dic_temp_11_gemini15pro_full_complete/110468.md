lilla:/ˈlɪlə/ | adj. | 淡紫色的 | Of a pale lilac colour. |  The walls were painted a soft lilla. 墙壁被漆成了柔和的淡紫色。 |  She wore a lilla dress to the party. 她穿着一件淡紫色的连衣裙去参加聚会。 | 近义词:lavender, mauve, violet | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑深色或对比色，例如) dark, black | 词性:形容词

词源: lilla (n.) "lilac blossom," 1778, from French lilas, from Arabic lilak, from Persian lilak "bluish," related to nilak "indigo." The adjective is recorded from 1873.
