nonabsolute:/ˌnɒnˈæbsəluːt/
adjective
非绝对的
not absolute; not complete or perfect; relative.
Her authority in the department is nonabsolute.  她在部门的权威不是绝对的。
near synonyms: qualified, conditional, restricted, limited
antonyms: absolute, unconditional, unrestricted, unlimited
词性: adjective
