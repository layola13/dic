enmarble:/ɪnˈmɑːrbl̩/
verb
使大理石化; 使僵硬; 使冷酷无情
To make something resemble marble, either in appearance or texture, or to make someone rigid or unfeeling.
The intense cold seemed to enmarble his features. 严寒似乎使他的容貌僵硬了。
The sculptor enmarbled the clay. 雕塑家把黏土变成了大理石状。
petrify, harden, fossilize
soften, melt, thaw
v.
