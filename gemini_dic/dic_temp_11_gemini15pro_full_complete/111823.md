logge:/lɒdʒ/ | n. |小屋，临时住所|A small, roughly built house or cabin, often made of logs.  A temporary dwelling or shelter. |例句：The loggers built a logge in the forest for the winter. 伐木工人在森林里建了一座小屋过冬。

例句：They found refuge in a small logge during the storm.  他们在暴风雨中在一个小木屋里找到了避难所。|近义词：cabin, hut, shed, shelter|反义词: mansion, palace|词性:noun


词源: Middle English logge, from Old French loge, from Frankish *laubja "shelter of foliage," from Proto-Germanic *laubjon, from *laubiz "leaf."
