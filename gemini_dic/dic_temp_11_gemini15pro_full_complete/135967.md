nonquantitativeness:/nɒnˈkwɒntɪtətɪvnəs/
|n.|非定量性；非数量性|The nonquantitativeness of the data makes it difficult to analyze statistically.  这些数据的非定量性使其难以进行统计分析。|This study highlights the nonquantitativeness of certain aspects of human experience. 这项研究强调了人类经验某些方面的非定量性。|近义词:qualitativeness, imprecision|反义词:quantitativeness, precision|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not; the opposite of
quantitative (adjective): relating to, measuring, or measured by the quantity of something rather than its quality.
-ness (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives, often indicating a state or quality.
