foramen:/fəˈreɪmən/| n. |孔, 穴|An opening, hole, or passage, especially in a bone.|The optic nerve passes through the optic foramen.|Synonyms: aperture, orifice, opening, hole|Antonyms: closure, blockage|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *foramen*, meaning "opening, hole,"  from *forare*, meaning "to bore, pierce."
