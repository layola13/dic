archangelic: /ˌɑːrkænˈdʒɛlɪk/ | adj. | 天使般的，如大天使的 | Resembling or befitting an archangel; of or relating to an archangel. |  Her voice was described as archangelic, pure and powerful. 她的声音被描述为天使般的，纯净而有力。

|例句：The painting depicted an archangelic figure with fiery wings. 这幅画描绘了一个长着火焰翅膀的天使形象。

|例句：He possessed an archangelic beauty, radiating light and grace. 他拥有天使般的美貌，散发着光芒和优雅。

|近义词：seraphic, celestial, divine, heavenly |反义词：demonic, infernal, hellish |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *archangelicus, from Greek arkhangelos (archangel) + -icus. Ultimately from Greek arkhi- (chief) + angelos (messenger).
