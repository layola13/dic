messaging:/ˈmɛsɪdʒɪŋ/
n.
消息传递，通讯
The act of sending and receiving messages, especially electronically.
Example: Instant messaging has become a popular way to communicate.  即时通讯已成为一种流行的沟通方式。

Example:  The company's internal messaging system is very efficient.  公司的内部通讯系统非常高效。
近义词：communication, correspondence
反义词：(No direct antonym, but consider) silence, non-communication
词性：noun
