distinguished:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt/| adj.|卓越的，著名的，杰出的|Successful, respected, and admired for qualities such as experience, skill, and achievement.|例句：He is a distinguished professor of law. 他是一位杰出的法学教授。|近义词：eminent, renowned, noted, celebrated, famous, illustrious|反义词：undistinguished, unknown, obscure, ordinary|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old French distinguer "to separate, distinguish," from Latin distinguere "to separate by pricking, mark by a puncture, discern clearly, distinguish," from dis- "apart" + stinguere "to prick, quench."
