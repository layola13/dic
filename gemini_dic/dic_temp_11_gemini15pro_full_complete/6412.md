amphistylic:/ˈæm.fɪˈstaɪ.lɪk/ | adj. | 双接型 |  (of the jaw suspension of vertebrates) in which the upper jaw is attached to the skull both directly and by the hyomandibular cartilage. | The shark exhibits an amphistylic jaw suspension.  鲨鱼展现出双接型的颌骨悬挂方式。 |  hyostylic | autostylic | adjective
