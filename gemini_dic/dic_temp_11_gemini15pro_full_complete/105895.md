kittenlike:/ˈkɪtnˌlaɪk/| adj.|像小猫一样的，温顺的|Resembling or characteristic of a kitten, especially in being small, playful, or cuddly.|The girl had a kittenlike quality, always curious and playful. 这个女孩有一种像小猫一样的气质，总是充满好奇心和爱玩耍。|近义词：catlike, feline, playful, cuddly, cute, adorable|反义词：fierce, aggressive, hostile|词性:形容词
