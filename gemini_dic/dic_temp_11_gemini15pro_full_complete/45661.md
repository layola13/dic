curtail:/kərˈteɪl/
verb
缩减，削减
reduce in extent or quantity; impose a restriction on.
Budget cuts have led to significant curtailments in the number of new projects. 预算削减导致新项目的数量大幅减少。

The pandemic curtailed international travel.疫情限制了国际旅行。

近义词:reduce, decrease, diminish, lessen, cut back, cut down, retrench
反义词:increase, expand, extend, lengthen, prolong, augment
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *curtailler, from curt "short" + -ailler (frequentative suffix).
