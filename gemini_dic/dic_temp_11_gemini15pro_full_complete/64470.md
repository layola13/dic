erlking:/ˈɜːrlkɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɜːlkɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|魔王，精灵王|A mythical creature in German folklore, often depicted as a malevolent spirit who lures children to their death.|The erlking reached out his hand, but the child clung to his father. 魔王伸出了手，但孩子紧紧地抱着他的父亲。|Synonyms: goblin, bogeyman, demon|Antonyms: angel, guardian angel|Noun
