fattest:/ˈfætɪst/ | adj. |最胖的|Superlative form of fat; having the most fat.  The fattest of the three bears sat in the biggest chair.|例句：He was the fattest cat I had ever seen. 他是我见过最胖的猫。

例句：She ate the fattest slice of cake. 她吃了最大块的蛋糕。|近义词：chubbiest, plumpest, heaviest|反义词：thinnest, slimmest, leanest|词性:形容词
词源：From Old English *fættest, superlative of fætt (“fat”). Cognate with Dutch vetst (“fattest”), German fettest (“fattest”).
