metapophysis:/ˌmetəˈpɒfɪsɪs/
noun
横突，后关节突
A bony process, especially the mamillary process of a lumbar vertebra, arising from the superior articular process of a vertebra and serving for muscle attachment.
The metapophysis of the lumbar vertebrae provides attachment for several back muscles. 腰椎的横突为几块背部肌肉提供附着点。
Synonyms: mamillary process, process, projection
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific anatomical feature)
