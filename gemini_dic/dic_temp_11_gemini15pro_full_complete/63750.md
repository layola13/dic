epicene:/ˈɛpɪsiːn/
n. adj.
雌雄同体的，阴阳人的；中性的
A noun or pronoun grammatically treated as masculine or feminine (or neuter) but designating an animate being of either sex. An epicene person.
例句：The word "author" is epicene. “author”这个词是中性的。

例句：In some languages, like English, most nouns are epicene, not specifically masculine or feminine. 在一些语言中，例如英语，大多数名词都是中性的，并非特指阳性或阴性。
近义词：unisex, androgynous, neuter
反义词：masculine, feminine
词性:名词，形容词

epicene:/ˈɛpɪsiːn/
词源：From Latin *epicoenus, from Ancient Greek ἐπίκοινος (epíkoinos, “shared in common, common to several”), from ἐπί (epí, “on, upon, at, by, near”) + κοινός (koinós, “common”).
