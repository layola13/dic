deride:/dɪˈraɪd/| v. |嘲笑，讥讽|To laugh at or make fun of someone or something in a way that shows you think they are stupid or of no value.|The critics derided his latest novel as sentimental drivel.  评论家嘲笑他的最新小说是感伤的废话。|Synonyms: mock, ridicule, scoff at, jeer at, taunt, sneer at|Antonyms: praise, commend, admire, respect|verb
