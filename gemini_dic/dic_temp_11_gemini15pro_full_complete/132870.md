nonelaborate:/ˌnɒnɪˈlæbərət/ /ˌnɑːnɪˈlæbərət/| adj.|简单的，不复杂的，不精细的|Not detailed, complicated, or excessive; simple.|The decorations were nonelaborate, but tasteful. 装饰很简单，但很有品味。|近义词：simple, basic, plain, unadorned, uncomplicated|反义词：elaborate, complex, detailed, intricate, sophisticated|词性:形容词

词源:  From non- + elaborate.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "elaborate" comes from the Latin "elaboratus," past participle of "elaborare," meaning "to work out."
