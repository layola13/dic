beadleship:/ˈbiːdəlʃɪp/
n.
the office or position of a beadle; the district under a beadle's authority.
职务；管辖区
The beadleship became vacant upon the old man's death.  老人的去世使得教区执事一职空缺。
His beadleship extended over several villages. 他的管辖范围涵盖了几个村庄。
Synonyms: position, office, post, job
Antonyms: unemployment
