hyoglossal:/ˌhaɪoʊˈɡlɒsəl/ | adj. | 舌骨舌的；舌骨舌肌的 | Relating to the hyoid bone and the tongue.  Specifically, it refers to the hyoglossus muscle, which originates from the hyoid bone and inserts into the side of the tongue. | The hyoglossal muscle plays a crucial role in tongue movement. |  Near synonyms:  lingual (related to the tongue) | Antonyms: None | adjective
