koeksister:/ˈkʊksɪstər/ (South African), /ˈkuːksɪstər/ (Dutch)| n. |油炸甜点；麻花|A traditional South African and Dutch sweet treat, similar to a doughnut, made of braided dough deep-fried and then soaked in syrup.|例句：I love eating koeksisters at the market. 我喜欢在市场上吃油炸甜点。

例句：My grandmother makes the best koeksisters. 我祖母做的油炸甜点是最好吃的。|近义词：doughnut, cruller|反义词: None|词性:noun
