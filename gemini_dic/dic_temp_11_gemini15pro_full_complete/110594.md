limitlessness:/ˈlɪmɪtləsnəs/
n.
无限；无界限；无量
The state or quality of being limitless.  The vastness and limitlessness of space.
She felt a sense of limitlessness as she gazed at the starry sky. 她凝视着星空，感到一种无限感。
The concept of limitlessness is often explored in science fiction. 无限的概念经常在科幻小说中被探讨。
近义词: boundlessness, infinity, endlessness
反义词: limitation, finiteness, boundedness
词性:名词
