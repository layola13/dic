overcomplacently: /ˌoʊvər kəmˈpleɪsntlɪ/
overcomplacently: adv. | 过于自满地，过分自得地 | in a manner that is excessively self-satisfied and unconcerned about potential problems. | He smiled overcomplacently, unaware of the impending danger. 他沾沾自喜地笑着，没有意识到即将到来的危险。| 近义词: smugly, complacently, self-satisfyingly | 反义词: anxiously, apprehensively, vigilantly |词性: 副词
