geocell:/ˈdʒiːoʊsɛl/
n.|地理单元，地理栅格|A geocell is a geographical area defined by a specific grid or tessellation system, often used in mapping, location-based services, and data analysis.|Geocells are used to optimize data storage and retrieval in location-based applications.|Synonym: grid cell, spatial cell|Antonym: N/A|Noun
