mous:/maʊs/ | n. |老鼠；鼠标；胆小如鼠的人|A small rodent with a pointed snout, small rounded ears, and a long, thin tail.  Also, a hand-held device used to control a computer's cursor. Can also refer to someone who is timid or shy.|例句：The mouse scurried across the floor. 老鼠在地板上 scurried across the floor.

例句：He moved the mouse to click on the icon. 他移动鼠标点击图标。|近义词：rodent, cursor controller, timid person (figurative)|反义词：brave person (figurative)|词性:noun
