overcatch:/ˈoʊvərˌkætʃ/| n. |过度捕捞；超额捕捞|The act of catching more fish than a population can sustain, leading to a decline in the population.|Overfishing and overcatch have depleted fish stocks in many areas. 过度捕捞和超额捕捞已经耗尽了许多地区的鱼类资源。|Synonyms: overfishing, excessive fishing|Antonyms: sustainable fishing|noun
