grecianizes:/ˈɡriːʃənaɪzɪz/
verb
使希腊化；赋予希腊风格；模仿希腊
to make Greek in character; to adopt or imitate Greek customs, style, or principles
The architect grecianized the design of the building by adding columns and pediments.  这位建筑师通过添加柱子和山形墙使建筑设计希腊化。
The museum's new exhibit grecianizes Roman sculptures, exploring the Hellenic influence on Roman art.  博物馆的新展览将罗马雕塑希腊化，探索希腊文化对罗马艺术的影响。
近义词: hellenizes, classicizes
反义词: romanizes, modernizes
词性:v


ETYMOLOGY->
From Grecian + -ize.  Grecian (meaning "Greek") comes from the Latin Graecianus, ultimately from the Greek Graikos.  The suffix -ize forms verbs meaning "to make or become like" or "to cause to conform to."
