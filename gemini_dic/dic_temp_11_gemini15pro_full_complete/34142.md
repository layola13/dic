claimless: /ˈkleɪmləs/
adjective
无主的；无人认领的；无人要求的
Without a claim, claimant, or owner; unclaimed.
The claimless luggage was donated to charity.  无人认领的行李被捐赠给了慈善机构。
The claimless property was auctioned off. 无人认领的财产被拍卖了。
近义词: unclaimed, abandoned, ownerless
反义词: claimed, owned
词性: adjective

词源: claim + -less
From claim (n.) + -less.  "Claim" comes from Old French "clamer" meaning "to call, cry out, proclaim."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
