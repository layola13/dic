frothier:/ˈfrɒθɪər (UK), ˈfrɑːθiər (US)| adj.|更加起泡的，更加轻浮的|More frothy; full of or covered with a mass of small bubbles.  Also, more frivolous or superficial.|The cappuccino was frothier than usual this morning. 今早的卡布奇诺比平时更起泡。|The party conversation was even frothier than last year's.  聚会的谈话比去年更加轻浮。|近义词：foamier, bubblier, flightier, frivolous|反义词：flat, serious, substantial|词性:形容词
词源:From froth + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  "Froth" comes from Old Norse *froða.
