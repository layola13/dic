green-around-the-gills:/ˌɡriːn əˈraʊnd ðə ɡɪlz/
adjective
面色苍白的，感到恶心的
Looking or feeling ill or nauseous, typically showing a pale or greenish complexion.
例句：He looked a little green-around-the-gills after the roller coaster ride.  他坐完过山车后脸色有点发青。

例句：The pregnant woman was feeling green-around-the-gills all morning.  这位孕妇整个早上都感到恶心。

近义词：pale, nauseous, queasy, sick, unwell
反义词：healthy, well, rosy, flushed

词性: adjective
