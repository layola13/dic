essayed:/ɛˈseɪd/ | v. | 尝试，试图 |  Attempted or tried to do something. | He essayed a smile, but it didn't quite reach his eyes. 他试图微笑，但笑容并没有到达他的眼底。|  She essayed to climb the mountain, but the weather turned bad. 她试图攀登那座山，但天气变坏了。 | attempted, tried, endeavored |  abandoned, forbore | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *essaier "to test, try," from Vulgar Latin *exagiare "to weigh," from Latin exagium "weighing, weight," from ex- "out" + agere "to drive, lead, act, do." The original sense was "to put to the test, try out."
