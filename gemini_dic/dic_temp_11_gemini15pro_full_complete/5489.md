aloofly:/əˈluːfli/ | adv. | 冷淡地，冷漠地 | In a manner that is distant, reserved, and uninterested. | She smiled aloofly and turned away. 她冷淡地笑了笑，转过身去。|  He aloofly dismissed their concerns. 他冷漠地不理会他们的担忧。 | 近义词: distantly, remotely, reservedly, unapproachably, coolly, indifferently | 反义词: warmly, cordially, affably, sociably | 词性: 副词

词源:
From aloof + -ly. Aloof comes from Middle English *alof, from a- "on" + loof "windward side of a ship".  Originally a nautical term referring to keeping a ship's sails to the wind, the sense of "at a distance" developed figuratively.
