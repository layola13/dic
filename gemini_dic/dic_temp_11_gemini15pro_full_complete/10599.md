apprised: /əˈpraɪzd/ | v. (past participle) | 通知，告知 | informed, made aware of something |  Example: I was apprised of the situation immediately. 我被立即告知了情况。

Example:  He kept me apprised of the latest developments. 他不断地告知我最新的进展。

近义词: informed, notified, advised, told
反义词: unaware, ignorant, uninformed
词性: verb (past participle - used with "be" or "get" to form the passive voice)

词源: From Old French *aprisier, from apris "learned, instructed," past participle of aprendre "to learn," from Latin apprehendere "to grasp, seize, understand."
