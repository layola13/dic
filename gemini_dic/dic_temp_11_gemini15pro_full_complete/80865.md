goutier:/ˈɡuːtieɪ/ | adj. |  美食的；讲究饮食的；有品位的 | Having or showing refined taste, especially in food or wine. |  He is known for his goutier palate and appreciation of fine dining. 他以其美食家的味蕾和对精致餐饮的欣赏而闻名。 |  refined, discerning, epicurean, gourmet  | unsophisticated, unrefined, uncultivated | adjective

词源:
From French *goutier*, from *gout* "taste," from Latin *gustus*.
