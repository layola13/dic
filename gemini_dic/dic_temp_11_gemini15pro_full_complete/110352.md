lightning:/ˈlaɪtnɪŋ/| n.|闪电|The sudden electrostatic discharge during an electrical storm between electrically charged regions of a cloud, between that cloud and another cloud, or between a cloud and the ground.|The lightning flashed across the sky. 闪电划过天空。|近义词：flash, bolt, spark|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 darkness, gloom  表示闪电的反面状态 - 黑暗)|词性:名词
