arsine:/ˈɑːrsiːn/ |n.|胂，砷化三氢|A colorless, flammable, extremely poisonous gas (AsH3) with a garlicky odor, formed by the action of acids on arsenides or by the reduction of arsenic compounds.|Exposure to arsine can cause severe health problems, even death. 暴露于胂中会导致严重的健康问题，甚至死亡。|Synonyms: arsenic hydride, hydrogen arsenide|Antonyms: None|Noun
