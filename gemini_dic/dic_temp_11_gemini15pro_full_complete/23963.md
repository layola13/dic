breaststroke: /ˈbrɛststroʊk/ (US) /ˈbrɛststrəʊk/ (UK)| n.|蛙泳|A swimming stroke in which the arms are moved forward, outward, and backward while the legs are kicked outward and then brought together.|He won the race using the breaststroke. 他用蛙泳赢得了比赛。|近义词: frog kick (informal)|反义词: backstroke, crawl, butterfly|noun

breaststroke: /ˈbrɛststroʊk/ (US) /ˈbrɛststrəʊk/ (UK)| v.|蛙泳|To swim using the breaststroke.|She breaststroked across the pool. 她蛙泳游过了泳池。|近义词: swim breaststroke|反义词: swim backstroke, swim crawl, swim butterfly|verb
