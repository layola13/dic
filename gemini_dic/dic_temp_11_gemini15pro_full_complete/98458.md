inspiredly:/ɪnˈspaɪərdli/
adverb
受到鼓舞地；灵感迸发地
In a manner showing or suggesting inspiration.
She played the piano inspiredly, her fingers dancing over the keys. 她充满激情地弹着钢琴，手指在琴键上飞舞。
He spoke inspiredly about his vision for the future. 他热情洋溢地讲述了他对未来的憧憬。
近义词: enthusiastically, passionately, fervently
反义词: listlessly, apathetically, halfheartedly
词性: 副词
