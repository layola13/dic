incommensurability:/ˌɪnkəmɛnʃərəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可通约性；不相称 |The state of being incommensurable; having no common measure or standard of comparison.  It refers to the inability to compare or judge two things by a single standard or measure, often because they are fundamentally different in nature or kind. | The incommensurability of their worldviews made communication difficult.  他们的世界观不可通约，使得沟通变得困难。 |  incomparability, disparity, difference, divergence | commensurability, compatibility, similarity | noun
incommensurable:/ˌɪnkəmɛnʃərəbl/ | adj. | 不可通约的；不相称的 | Not commensurable; having no common measure or standard of comparison, especially of different theories or values. |  His artistic vision was incommensurable with the commercial demands of the gallery.  他的艺术视野与画廊的商业需求不相称。 | incompatible, disparate, dissimilar, divergent | commensurable, compatible, similar | adjective
