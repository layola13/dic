misrelying:/ˌmɪsrɪˈlaɪɪŋ/
verb
错误地依赖；误信
relying incorrectly or mistakenly.
He misrelied on the information he found online and made a costly mistake. 他错误地依赖了他在网上找到的信息，犯了一个代价高昂的错误。
近义词:  mistrusting, doubting,  depending incorrectly
反义词:  trusting, believing, depending correctly
词性: verb (present participle)

misrely:/ˌmɪsrɪˈlaɪ/
verb
错误地依赖；误信
to rely or depend on something incorrectly or mistakenly.
I misrelied on the map and got completely lost. 我错误地依赖了地图，完全迷路了。
近义词:  mistrust, doubt, depend incorrectly
反义词:  trust, believe, depend correctly
词性: verb



词源:
"mis-" (prefix of Old English and Old French origin) meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "incorrectly" + "rely" (verb from late Middle English, from Old French "relier" meaning "to rally, bring together").  Therefore, "misrely" means to rely wrongly or incorrectly.
