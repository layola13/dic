fretwork:/ˈfrɛtwɜːrk/
noun
镂空细工，格子细工
Ornamental work consisting of interlaced designs, typically in wood.
The antique chair featured intricate fretwork along its back.  这把古董椅子的靠背上有复杂的镂空雕刻。
The guitar's soundhole was surrounded by delicate fretwork. 吉他的音孔周围环绕着精致的镂空细工。
synonyms: latticework, tracery, openwork, filigree
antonyms: solid, plain
