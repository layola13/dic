board:/bɔːrd/ (UK) /boːrd/ (US)| n. v.|板; 董事会; 甲板; 膳食; 上（船、飞机等）|A flat piece of wood or other stiff material, used for various purposes.  Also, a group of people who manage a company or organization, the side of a ship, providing meals, or the act of getting onto a ship, plane, etc.|

例句：
The notice was pinned to the notice board. 通知被钉在布告板上。

They replaced the rotten floor boards. 他们更换了腐烂的地板。


例句：
The board of directors met to discuss the budget. 董事会开会讨论预算。

She sits on the board of several charities. 她是几个慈善机构的董事会成员。


例句：
We walked along the deck, enjoying the fresh sea air. 我们沿着甲板散步，享受着新鲜的海风。

He slipped on the wet deck and fell. 他在湿漉漉的甲板上滑倒了。


例句：
Room and board are included in the price. 价格包括食宿。

The students pay for their board and lodging. 学生们支付他们的膳食和住宿费用。


例句：
Passengers are now boarding Flight 345 to London. 乘客们正在登机前往伦敦的345航班。

We boarded the train just as it was about to leave. 我们就在火车即将开动的时候上了车。
