auxiliary:/ɔːɡˈzɪliəri/ /ɑːɡˈzɪləri/ | adj. n.|辅助的，附属的；辅助者，助手|Providing supplementary or additional help and support.|例句：The hospital has an auxiliary power supply in case of a blackout.  医院有一个备用电源以防停电。|近义词：supporting, supplementary, additional, reserve, backup|反义词:primary, main, principal|adjective

auxiliary:/ɔːɡˈzɪliəri/ /ɑːɡˈzɪləri/ | n.|辅助者，助手|A person or thing providing supplementary or additional help and support.|例句：She works as an auxiliary nurse at the local hospital. 她在当地医院做助理护士。|近义词：assistant, helper, aide|反义词:leader, director|noun
