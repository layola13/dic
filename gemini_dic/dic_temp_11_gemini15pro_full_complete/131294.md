noncereals:/nɒnˈsɪəriəlz/
noun
非谷物，非粮食物
Crops other than cereals, such as legumes and vegetables, are classified as noncereals.
除谷物以外的作物，如豆类和蔬菜，都被归类为非谷物。
Synonyms: non-grain crops
Antonyms: cereals, grains
