foments:/foʊˈmɛnts/ | v. | 煽动，激起 | to encourage or stir up (negative feelings or activities) |  The politician's speeches fomented unrest in the crowd. 这位政治家的演讲煽动了人群中的不安。| 近义词：incite, instigate, provoke | 反义词：quell, suppress, allay | 词性: 动词

foments:/fəˈmɛnts/ | v. | 培养，促进 | Less commonly, to promote the growth or development of. |  The organization fomented artistic expression among young people. 该组织培养了年轻人的艺术表现力。| 近义词：foster, cultivate, nurture | 反义词：hinder, obstruct, impede | 词性: 动词
