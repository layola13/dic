gipsyism's:/ˈdʒɪpsiɪzəmz/ | n. |吉普赛人的习俗，吉普赛主义| The nomadic lifestyle and traditions of the Roma people are often referred to as gipsyism's.|  He studied the history and evolution of gipsyism's across Europe.| 近义词: Roma culture, Romani traditions | 反义词: settled lifestyle | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "gipsy" (now often considered offensive) + "-ism" (a suffix forming nouns denoting doctrines, beliefs, or practices).  "Gipsy" itself comes from the Middle English "gipcian," derived ultimately from the Greek "Aigyptios" (Egyptian), due to the mistaken belief that the Roma people originated in Egypt.
