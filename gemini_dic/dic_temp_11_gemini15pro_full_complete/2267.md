adept:/əˈdept/
adjective|熟练的，精通的|Highly skilled or proficient in something.|She is adept at playing the piano. 她钢琴弹得很熟练。|近义词：skilled, proficient, expert, masterful|反义词：clumsy, unskilled, inept|词性:adjective
adept:/əˈdept/
noun|专家，能手|A person who is skilled or proficient in something.|He is an adept in the art of negotiation. 他是谈判专家。|近义词：expert, specialist, professional, master|反义词：amateur, novice, beginner|词性:noun
