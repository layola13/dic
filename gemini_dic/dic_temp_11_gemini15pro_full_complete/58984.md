dutifulness:/ˌdjuːtɪfʊlnəs/
|n.|
|尽责；责任感；恭顺|
|The quality of being dutiful; conscientiousness in fulfilling obligations.|
|Her dutifulness to her parents was admirable. 她对父母的孝顺令人钦佩。|
|conscientiousness, responsibility, diligence, obedience|
|negligence, irresponsibility, disobedience|
|noun|


dutiful:/ˈdjuːtɪfʊl/
|adj.|
|尽责的；忠实的；恭顺的|
|Performing the duties expected or required; obedient and respectful.|
|He was a dutiful son who always cared for his aging parents. 他是一个孝顺的儿子，总是照顾年迈的父母。
She is a dutiful employee, always completing her tasks on time. 她是一位尽职的员工，总是按时完成任务。|
|responsible, conscientious, obedient, respectful|
|irresponsible, negligent, disobedient, disrespectful|
|adjective|
