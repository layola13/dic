outgambled: /ˌaʊtˈɡæmbəld/
verb
以赌博方式胜过, 赌赢
to defeat someone by being more skillful or lucky at gambling.
He outgambled his opponents at the poker table. 他在扑克桌上赌赢了对手。
近义词: outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb (past tense)
