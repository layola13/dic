balled-up:/ˌbɔːldˈʌp/ | adj. | 揉成一团的；蜷缩的；紧张的 | Crumpled or rolled tightly into a ball; also, tense and nervous. |  The student nervously crumpled his test into a balled-up mess.  这个学生紧张地把试卷揉成一团。 | synonyms: crumpled, bunched, screwed-up, tense, anxious | antonyms: smooth, flat, relaxed, calm | adjective

balled-up:/ˌbɔːldˈʌp/ | v. | 揉成球状；蜷缩 | To crumple or roll something tightly into a ball; to tense up. | He balled up his fists in anger. 他愤怒地握紧了拳头。 | synonyms: crumple, bunch, screw up, tense up | antonyms: smooth out, flatten, relax | verb
