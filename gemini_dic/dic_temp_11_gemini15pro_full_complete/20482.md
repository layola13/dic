bituminiferous:/ˌbɪtʃuːˈmɪnɪfərəs/
adjective
含沥青的；产沥青的
Containing or yielding bitumen.
The bituminiferous sands are a vast source of potential energy. 这些含沥青的砂岩是潜在的巨大能源。
The area is rich in bituminiferous shale. 这个地区富含含沥青的页岩。
Synonyms: asphalt-bearing, bituminous
Antonyms: non-bituminous


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bituminosus (bituminous) + -ferous (bearing).  "Bitumen" comes from Latin bitumen, which ultimately has Proto-Indo-European roots. "-ferous" comes from Latin -fer, meaning "bearing."
