benefactrix: /ˌbɛnɪˈfæktriks/
noun
女施主，女恩人
a woman who gives money or other help to a person or cause
例句：The benefactrix funded the construction of the new library.  这位女施主资助了新图书馆的建设。

例句：The hospital was built thanks to the generosity of a wealthy benefactrix.  这家医院的建成要感谢一位富有女施主的慷慨解囊。
近义词：patroness, donor
反义词: N/A (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑malefactor - 犯罪者)
词性: noun

bene-/beni- : good, well
-fact-: making, doing
-trix: female agent
