cheeped:/tʃiːpt/| v. |吱吱叫；唧唧叫|To make a short, high-pitched sound, typically of a young bird.|The baby birds cheeped in the nest.  雏鸟在巢里吱吱叫。|近义词：chirp, peep, twitter|反义词:squawk, shriek|动词
