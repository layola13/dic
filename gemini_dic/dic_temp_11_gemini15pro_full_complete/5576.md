PHRASE->
lay on the altar:  牺牲；献出  He laid his career on the altar of family. 他为了家庭牺牲了自己的事业。

approach the altar:  走向圣坛（通常指结婚）  The bride approached the altar with her father. 新娘和她的父亲一起走向圣坛。

lead to the altar:  与…结婚  He finally led her to the altar after a ten-year courtship.  经过十年的恋爱，他终于和她结婚了。

at the altar of:  为了…而牺牲  She sacrificed her happiness at the altar of duty. 她为了责任牺牲了自己的幸福。

altar boy/girl:  祭坛侍童  The altar boy helped the priest during the mass.  祭坛侍童在弥撒期间帮助神父。
