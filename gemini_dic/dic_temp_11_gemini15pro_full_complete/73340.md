forefence:/ˈfɔːrfɛns/| n.|前篱笆，前围栏|A fence at the front of a property.|The forefence needs repairing as some of the panels are broken. 前篱笆需要修理，因为有些板子坏了。|近义词：front fence, fence|反义词: back fence|词性:noun

Etymology:
fore- (Old English) meaning "before, in front" + fence (from Old French "fense" meaning "defense")
