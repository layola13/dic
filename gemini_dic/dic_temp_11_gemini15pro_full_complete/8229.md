anti-hero:/ˈæntiˌhɪəroʊ/ /ˈæntiˌhɪərəʊ/| n.|反英雄|A central character in a story, movie, or drama who lacks conventional heroic attributes.|Deadpool is a popular anti-hero. 死侍是一个受欢迎的反英雄。|Synonyms: protagonist, villain, rogue|Antonyms: hero, champion|Noun

anti-hero:/ˈæntiˌhɪəroʊ/ /ˈæntiˌhɪərəʊ/| n.|反主角|A main character who lacks traditional heroic qualities such as idealism, courage, or morality.|Walter White from Breaking Bad is a complex anti-hero.  《绝命毒师》中的沃尔特·怀特是一个复杂的反主角。|Synonyms: flawed protagonist, imperfect hero|Antonyms: hero, paragon|Noun
