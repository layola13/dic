contraster:/kənˈtræstər/ | n. | 对比者，形成对比的事物 |Something that contrasts with another, highlighting the differences. | The black dress served as a stark contraster to her bright white shoes.  这件黑色连衣裙与她亮白色的鞋子形成了鲜明的对比。| Synonyms: foil, antithesis | Antonyms: complement, parallel | noun

contraster:/kənˈtræstər/ | n. | 对比剂 | A substance used to enhance the visibility of structures or fluids in medical imaging. | The doctor injected a contraster to get a clearer image of the blood vessels. 医生注射了造影剂以便更清晰地看到血管图像。| Synonyms: contrast medium, contrast agent | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "obscuring agent") | noun
