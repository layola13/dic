boson:/ˈboʊzɒn/, /ˈboʊsɒn/| n. |玻色子|A subatomic particle, such as a photon, that has zero or integral spin and follows the statistical description given by S. N. Bose and Einstein.|Photons and gluons are examples of bosons.|Synonyms: elementary particle, subatomic particle|Antonyms: fermion|noun
