assyriological:/ˌæsɪriəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
亚述学的
Relating to Assyriology, the study of the language, history, and culture of ancient Assyria and Mesopotamia.
The museum houses an impressive collection of assyriological artifacts.  博物馆收藏了令人印象深刻的亚述学文物。
Near synonyms: Mesopotamian, Akkadian
No direct antonym.
adjective


Etymology:
From Assyria (referring to ancient Assyria) + -ological (suffix meaning "study of").  Ultimately derived from Akkadian Aššur.
