frijol:/friˈxoːl/ (US), /friˈhɔl/ (UK)| n. |豆子；菜豆|A bean, especially a kidney bean, grown and widely eaten in Latin America.|例句：Frijoles are a staple food in many Mexican dishes.  菜豆是许多墨西哥菜肴的主食。

例句：She cooked the frijoles with spices and onions. 她用香料和洋葱烹制了菜豆。|近义词：bean, kidney bean|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
