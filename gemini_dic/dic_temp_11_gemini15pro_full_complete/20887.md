blazoners:/ˈbleɪzənərz/
n.
纹章官；纹章描述者；吹嘘者
One who blazons coats of arms; a herald.  A person who proclaims or publishes widely.
The blazoners meticulously described the intricate details of the royal coat of arms. 纹章官一丝不苟地描述了皇家盾形纹章的复杂细节。

The company blazoners their new product as the best on the market.  该公司将他们的新产品吹嘘成市场上最好的。

Synonyms: heralds, announcers, proclaimers, trumpeters
Antonyms: concealers, suppressors
