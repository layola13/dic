disshadow:/dɪˈʃædoʊ/
verb
使失色；使相形见绌；使黯然无光
to cause something to seem less impressive or less important
The new skyscraper disshadows all the other buildings in the city.  这座新的摩天大楼使城市里的所有其他建筑都黯然失色。
近义词: eclipse, overshadow, outshine, surpass
反义词: highlight, emphasize, accentuate
词性: verb

disshadow:/dɪˈʃædoʊ/
动词

to cause something to seem less impressive or less important
