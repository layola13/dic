misted:/ˈmɪstɪd/| adj.|有雾的，模糊的，蒙上水汽的|Covered with mist; obscured by mist or condensation; made dim or indistinct.|The windows were misted over with condensation. 窗户上蒙上了一层水汽。|近义词：foggy, hazy, blurred, clouded|反义词：clear, bright|词性:形容词
词源: From mist (n.) + -ed, from Old English *mist "dark, misty, obscure," of unknown origin.
