misthink:/ˌmɪsˈθɪŋk/ | v. |  错误地认为；想错；误解 | To think incorrectly; to have an erroneous thought or belief. |  I misthought his intentions and wrongly accused him. 我误解了他的意图，错误地指责了他。

She misthought the time and arrived an hour late. 她弄错了时间，迟到了一个小时。


近义词: misunderstand, misinterpret, misconceive, misjudge | 反义词: understand, comprehend, grasp | 词性: 动词
