Etymology: From Middle English *fondlen, frequentative of fonden (“to be foolish; to act foolishly; to dote”), from Old English fondian (“to test, try; tempt”).  More at fond.


Synonyms: caress, stroke, pet, cuddle

Antonyms: abuse, neglect, mistreat
