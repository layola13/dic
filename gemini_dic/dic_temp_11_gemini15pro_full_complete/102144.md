一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing right now? = 你现在正在做什么？
What is he working on these days? = 他这些天在忙什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing since I last saw you? = 自从我上次见到你以来，你一直在做什么？
What has he been working on for the past few months? = 过去几个月他一直在做什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 在做出决定之前他们讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident occurred? = 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after you graduate? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2030? = 到2030年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么多久了？
