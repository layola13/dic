lift-off:/ˈlɪftɒf/ (UK) /ˈlɪftɔːf/ (US)| n.|发射，起飞|The moment of lift-off was breathtaking. 发射的那一刻令人叹为观止。|The rocket's lift-off was delayed due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，火箭的发射被推迟了。|近义词：launch, take-off, blast-off|反义词: landing, touchdown|词性:名词

Etymology:  Compound of "lift" and "off". "Lift" comes from Old Norse *lypta "to raise," and "off" from Old English of.  The combined meaning refers to the act of something being lifted off the ground.
