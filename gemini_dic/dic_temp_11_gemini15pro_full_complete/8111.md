anti-abortionist: /ˌæntiəˈbɔːʃənɪst/ | n. | 反堕胎者，反对堕胎的人 |  A person who opposes abortion. |  The anti-abortionist protested outside the clinic.  反堕胎者在诊所外抗议。|  pro-life advocate | abortion rights supporter, pro-choice advocate | noun


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
abortion (noun): the deliberate termination of a human pregnancy, most often performed during the first 28 weeks. (from Latin aboriri "to miscarry," from ab- "away" + oriri "to be born")
-ist (suffix):  forms agent nouns, denoting a person who performs a specified action or practises a specified art.
