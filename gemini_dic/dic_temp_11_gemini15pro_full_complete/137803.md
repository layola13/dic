nonveritable:/ˌnɒnvɛrɪtəbl/
adjective
非真实的，不真实的
Not genuinely or truly so; not factual; untrue.
The lawyer argued that the witness's testimony was nonveritable and should be disregarded.  律师辩称证人的证词不真实，应予忽略。
The movie presented a nonveritable account of historical events.  这部电影对历史事件的描述并不真实。
近义词:false, untrue, fictitious, spurious, fabricated, counterfeit
反义词:true, real, genuine, authentic, veritable
词性:adjective
