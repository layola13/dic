get-the-better-of:/ɡet ðə ˈbetər əv/
verb phrase
战胜，胜过，占上风
To defeat or overcome someone or something; to gain an advantage over someone or something.
He fought bravely, but his opponent eventually got the better of him. 他勇敢地战斗，但他的对手最终战胜了他。
Despite her best efforts, her nerves got the better of her during the presentation. 尽管她尽了最大努力，但在演讲过程中她的紧张情绪还是占据了上风。
Synonyms: overcome, defeat, conquer, triumph over, outwit, prevail over
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by, succumb to

get-the-better-of:/ɡet ðə ˈbetər əv/
verb phrase
控制；左右（尤指感情）
If a feeling gets the better of you, you cannot stop yourself from showing it or acting on it.
Curiosity got the better of her and she opened the letter.  好奇心战胜了她，她打开了信。
His anger got the better of him and he yelled at the driver. 他的怒火控制了他，他对着司机大喊大叫。
Synonyms:  overcome, overwhelm, control, dominate
Antonyms: control oneself, restrain oneself
