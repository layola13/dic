backstops:/ˈbækˌstɑːps/| n. v.|支持；后盾；防护网|A backstop is a measure taken to prevent something undesirable from happening.  It can also refer to a fence or screen placed behind a baseball catcher to stop wild pitches. |The government introduced regulations as a backstop against financial instability. 政府出台了防止金融不稳定的保障措施。|Synonyms: safeguard, precaution, safety net, support, protection|Antonyms: risk, danger, threat|noun
backstops:/ˈbækˌstɑːps/| v.|支持；担任后盾|To backstop is to provide support or a guarantee in case of failure.  In baseball, it means to play the position of backstop (catcher). |The central bank is backstopping the failing financial institution. 中央银行正在为这家濒临倒闭的金融机构提供支持。|Synonyms: support, reinforce, underpin, guarantee|Antonyms: undermine, weaken, sabotage|verb
