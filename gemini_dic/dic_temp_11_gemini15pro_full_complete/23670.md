brangling:/ˈbræŋɡlɪŋ/ | n. | 争吵，争论 | A noisy quarrel or dispute. |  Example 1: The brangling between the two families had gone on for generations.  两个家族之间的争吵已经持续了几代人。

Example 2:  The marketplace was filled with the brangling of merchants hawking their wares.  市场上充斥着小贩们兜售商品的喧闹声。 | Synonyms: quarrel, argument, dispute, wrangle, squabble, altercation | Antonyms: agreement, harmony, peace, accord | noun
