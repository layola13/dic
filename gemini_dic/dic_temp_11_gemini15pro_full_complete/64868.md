espringal:/ˈɛsprɪŋɡəl/| n.|巨型弩炮，弹射器|A large medieval crossbow or catapult used to hurl large bolts or stones.|The espringal hurled a massive stone at the castle walls. 巨型弩炮向城堡的城墙投掷了一块巨石。|Synonyms: catapult, trebuchet, ballista|Antonyms: N/A|noun

Etymology:
From Old French *espringale (Modern French *épringalle*), from Late Latin *springaldus*, from Medieval Greek *σπριγγάλδιον* (springáldion), diminutive of *σπρίγγος* (spríngos, “a spring”).  Ultimately from the same Proto-Germanic root as English *spring*.
