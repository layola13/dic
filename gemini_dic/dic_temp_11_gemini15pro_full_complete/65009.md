estrogen: /ˈɛstrədʒən/ (US), /ˈiːstrədʒən/ (UK)| n. |雌激素|A group of steroid hormones that promote the development and maintenance of female characteristics of the body.| During puberty, estrogen levels increase in girls, leading to the development of breasts and other secondary sexual characteristics. 青春期时，女孩的雌激素水平会上升，导致乳房和其他第二性征的发育。|  Estrogen therapy can help alleviate some of the symptoms of menopause. 雌激素疗法可以帮助缓解更年期的一些症状。| synonyms:  oestrogen, female hormone| antonyms: androgen| noun
