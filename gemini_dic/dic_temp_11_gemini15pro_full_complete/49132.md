deistical:/diːˈɪstɪkəl/| adj.| 自然神论的|Of or relating to deism, the belief in a God who created the universe but does not intervene in its affairs. |例句：He held a deistical view of the world, believing in a creator God who did not interfere with human affairs. 他持有一种自然神论的世界观，相信一个创造了宇宙但不干预其事务的造物主上帝。

例句：Her deistical beliefs led her to reject organized religion and embrace a more personal spirituality. 她的自然神论信仰使她拒绝有组织的宗教，并拥抱更个人的灵性。|近义词：rationalistic, philosophical|反义词：theistic, orthodox|词性: adjective

词源: From French *déistique*, from Latin *deus* (“god”).
