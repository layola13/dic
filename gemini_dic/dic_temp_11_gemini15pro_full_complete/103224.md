jettied:/ˈdʒɛtɪd/
adjective
突堤式的，建有突堤的
Built with or having jetties.  A coastline characterized by jetties.
The jettied harbor provided safe anchorage for the boats.  突堤式的港口为船只提供了安全的锚地。
近义词:piered, wharfed
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open" or "exposed" in the context of a coastline)
词性: adjective

jettied:/ˈdʒɛtɪd/
verb (past participle of 'jetty')
用突堤保护，突出
(Of a piece of land) extended into the water like a jetty.  Protected or supported by a jetty.
The coastline was jettied to prevent erosion.  海岸线用突堤加固以防止侵蚀。
近义词:protruded, extended
反义词: recessed, retracted
词性: verb (past participle)



词源:
From jetty + -ed.  "Jetty" originates from Old French "jetee," meaning "a throw," later evolving to describe a structure projecting into a body of water.
