exorhizal:/ˌɛksoʊˈraɪzəl/| adj.|外生的|Describing a type of mycorrhiza in which the fungal hyphae do not penetrate the root cells of the plant, but instead form a sheath around the root and grow between the cells of the root cortex.|The pine tree has an exorhizal association with certain fungi. 松树与某些真菌存在外生关系。|Synonyms: ectotrophic, ectomycorrhizal|Antonyms: endomycorrhizal, arbuscular|adjective
