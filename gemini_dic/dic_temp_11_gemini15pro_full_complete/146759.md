overhuntings: /ˌoʊvərˈhʌntɪŋz/
| n. |过度捕猎|Excessive hunting of a species, usually to the point of endangering its population.|The overhuntings of whales in the 19th century nearly drove them to extinction. 19世纪对鲸鱼的过度捕猎几乎使其灭绝。|Overexploitation, overfishing|Underhunting, conservation|noun
