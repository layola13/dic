lifebelts:/ˈlaɪfˌbelts/ | n. |救生圈; 救生带 |A ring-shaped inflatable device used to keep a person afloat in water. | The lifeboat was equipped with lifebelts for all passengers. 救生艇上为所有乘客配备了救生圈。 | The crew threw lifebelts to the people struggling in the water. 船员们向在水中挣扎的人们扔救生圈。 | synonyms: life preserver, lifebuoy, life ring, life jacket | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider:  anchor, weight) | noun
