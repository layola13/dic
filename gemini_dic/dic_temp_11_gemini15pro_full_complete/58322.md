drying:/ˈdraɪɪŋ/| adj. n.|干燥的，烘干；干燥，变干|The process of removing moisture.  The drying process takes several days.  干燥过程需要几天时间。|例句：The drying clothes flapped in the wind.  晾干的衣服在风中飘动。

The drying time for this paint is about two hours.  这种油漆的干燥时间约为两小时。|近义词：dehydrating, desiccation, airing|反义词：wetting, soaking, moistening|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Middle English *dryinge, derived from the verb dryen, from Old English dryġan (“to dry”), of Germanic origin.
