benthamite:/ˈbɛnθəˌmaɪt/| n. adj.|功利主义者；功利主义的|A follower of the philosophy of Jeremy Bentham, especially advocating utilitarianism (the greatest happiness for the greatest number).|例句：He was a staunch Benthamite, believing that the best policies were those that benefited the most people. 他是一位坚定的功利主义者，认为最好的政策是那些能造福最多人的政策。

例句：Benthamite principles influenced many social reforms in the 19th century. 功利主义原则影响了19世纪的许多社会改革。|近义词：utilitarian, pragmatist|反义词:altruist, idealist|词性:noun, adjective
