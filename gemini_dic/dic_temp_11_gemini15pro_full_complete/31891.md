cheeseburger:/ˈtʃiːzbɜːrɡər/
n.
芝士汉堡，干酪汉堡
A hamburger topped with melted cheese.
例句：I ordered a cheeseburger and fries. 我点了一个芝士汉堡和薯条。

例句：The cheeseburger was juicy and delicious.  这个芝士汉堡多汁又美味。

近义词：hamburger, burger
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially a salad or a healthier food option could be considered an opposite in terms of food choices)
词性:noun
