dissolvers:/dɪˈzɒlvəz/
noun|溶解器；溶解剂|A substance or apparatus that dissolves something.|The dissolvers broke down the complex chemicals into simpler substances.|近义词：solvents, dissolvants|反义词：precipitants, coagulants|词性:n.

dissolve:/dɪˈzɒlv/
verb|溶解；解散；消失|To become incorporated into a liquid so as to form a solution; to break up or dismiss (an assembly, partnership, etc.).|(From Latin dissolvere, from dis- ‘apart’ + solvere ‘loosen’.)|The sugar dissolved quickly in the hot water. 糖在热水中很快溶解了。 The parliament was dissolved after the scandal.  议会在丑闻后解散了。|近义词：melt, liquefy, disintegrate, disperse|反义词：solidify, congeal, assemble, convene|词性:v.
