double-book: /ˌdʌbəlˈbʊk/ (US), /ˌdʌbəlˈbʊk/ (UK)| vt.|重复预订；双重预订|To make two or more reservations for the same person or resource at the same time, often unintentionally.|I accidentally double-booked myself for two meetings on Friday afternoon. 我不小心把周五下午的两个会议重复预订了.|近义词：overbook, overlap|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: verb

double-book: /ˌdʌbəlˈbʊk/ (US), /ˌdʌbəlˈbʊk/ (UK)| n.|重复预订|An instance of making two reservations for the same person or resource at the same time.|The double-booking caused a lot of confusion. 重复预订造成了很多混乱。|近义词：overlapping reservation|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
