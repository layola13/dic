brutalists:/ˈbruːtəlɪsts/
n.|野兽派艺术家；粗暴的人|People who are brutalists often favor simple, functional designs and raw materials in architecture and art.  野兽派艺术家通常喜欢在建筑和艺术中使用简单的、功能性的设计和原材料。|The gallery showcased the works of several prominent brutalists.  这个画廊展出了几位著名野兽派艺术家的作品。|近义词:  hardliners, extremists|反义词:  moderates, traditionalists|词性: 名词

brutalist:/ˈbruːtəlɪst/
adj.|野兽派的；粗暴的|Brutalist architecture is characterized by its massive, blocky forms and exposed concrete surfaces. 野兽派建筑的特点是其巨大的块状形态和裸露的混凝土表面。|His brutalist approach to management alienated many employees. 他粗暴的管理方式疏远了许多员工。|近义词:  harsh, severe, austere|反义词:  gentle, refined, delicate|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From brutal (残酷的) + -ist (表示“……主义者”或“……的”)， ultimately from Latin brutus (“dull, senseless, irrational”). 源自brutal（残酷的）+ -ist（表示“……主义者”或“……的”），最终来自拉丁语brutus（“迟钝的、无知的、不合理的”）。
