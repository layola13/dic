kittiwakes:/ˈkɪtiweɪks/ | n. | 三趾鸥 | A type of gull that nests on cliffs and has black-tipped wings. |  例句：Thousands of kittiwakes nest on the cliffs each year. 每年有成千上万的三趾鸥在悬崖上筑巢。

例句：The kittiwakes circled overhead, searching for fish. 三趾鸥在头顶盘旋，寻找鱼群。| 近义词：gull, seabird | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑land bird陆地鸟类作为相对的概念) | 词性: noun
