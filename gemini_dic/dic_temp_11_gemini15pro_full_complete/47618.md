debenture:/dɪˈbɛntʃər/
|n.|债券；信用债券；无担保债券|A debenture is a type of bond or other debt instrument that is unsecured by collateral. Debentures are backed only by the creditworthiness and reputation of the issuer. |Example 1: The company issued debentures to raise capital for expansion.  该公司发行债券以筹集扩张资金。

Example 2: Investors were wary of purchasing debentures from the struggling company. 投资者对购买这家陷入困境的公司的债券持谨慎态度。|Synonym: bond, loan, note, obligation|Antonym: asset, equity|noun
