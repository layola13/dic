miszoned:/ˌmɪsˈzoʊnd/
adjective
错误划分的
incorrectly zoned; assigned to the wrong zone, especially in urban planning.
The property was miszoned as residential when it should have been commercial. 这处房产被错误地划分为住宅用地，而它应该是商业用地。
The miszoned land caused numerous problems for developers.  这块被错误划分的土地给开发商带来了许多问题。
近义词: incorrectly zoned, wrongly classified
反义词: correctly zoned, properly classified
词性: adjective

词源:
"mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "zoned" (past participle of "zone")
