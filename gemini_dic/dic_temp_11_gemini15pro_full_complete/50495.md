depressurize:/diːˈprɛʃəraɪz/ /diːˈpreʃəraɪz/
verb
减压; 释压
To release pressure from something that has been pressurized, such as an aircraft cabin or a pressure cooker.
The pilot began to depressurize the cabin as the plane descended.  飞行员随着飞机下降开始给机舱减压。
The pressure cooker depressurized with a loud hiss. 高压锅发出一声巨大的嘶嘶声后减压了。
synonyms: release pressure, decompress, vent
antonyms: pressurize, compress
词性: 动词
