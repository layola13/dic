fezziest:/ˈfɛzɪɪst/
adjective
最兴奋的；最热闹的
Most bubbly, lively, or exciting.
The fezziest party of the year was held at the governor's mansion.  今年最热闹的派对在州长官邸举行。
Synonyms: liveliest, bubbliest, most exciting, most vibrant
Antonyms: dullest, flattest, most boring
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From fizzy + -est.  Ultimately from fizz, of imitative origin.
