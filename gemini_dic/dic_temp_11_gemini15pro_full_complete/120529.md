miohippus:/ˈmaɪ.oʊˈhɪp.əs/| n. |中新马|An extinct genus of prehistoric horse, considered a transitional form between the earlier Mesohippus and the later Parahippus. It lived in North America during the Oligocene epoch.| Miohippus was slightly larger than Mesohippus and had a slightly longer face. 中新马比中马略大，脸也略长。| Miohippus fossils are commonly found in North American Oligocene deposits. 中新马化石常见于北美渐新世沉积物中。|近义词: Mesohippus, Parahippus (related genera)|反义词:  N/A (extinct animal, no direct opposite)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *μεῖον (meîon, “lesser”) + ἵππος (híppos, “horse”).  The name refers to its slightly smaller size compared to some later horse genera.
