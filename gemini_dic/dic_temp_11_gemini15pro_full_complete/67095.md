explorational:/ˌɛkspləˈreɪʃənəl/
adjective
探索性的，探究的
Relating to or involving exploration or investigation, especially to gain knowledge or experience.
The company is undertaking an explorational study of the market.  这家公司正在进行一项市场探索性研究。
The team went on an explorational mission to the Arctic.  这个团队前往北极进行了一次探索任务。
Synonyms: investigative, exploratory, probing, inquiring
Antonyms: conclusive, definitive, established
词性: 形容词
