idealizer:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzər/ | n. | 理想化者；完美化者 |  A person who idealizes someone or something; someone who represents another person or thing as perfect or better than in reality. | She was an idealizer, always seeing the best in everyone, even when they didn't deserve it. 她是一个理想化者，总是看到每个人最好的一面，即使他们不值得。

He was an idealizer of his childhood, remembering only the good times. 他把他童年理想化了，只记得美好的时光。


近义词: romanticizer, glorifier, optimist | 反义词: critic, cynic, realist | 词性: noun
