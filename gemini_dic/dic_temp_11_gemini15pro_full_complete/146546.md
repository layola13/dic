overgloominesses: /ˌoʊvərˈɡluːminəsɪz/
| n. |过度忧郁，极度沮丧|A state of excessive gloominess; multiple instances of extreme sadness or depression. |The constant rain and gray skies contributed to the overgloominesses of the townspeople.  连绵不断的雨和灰蒙蒙的天空加剧了镇民的极度沮丧。|近义词：despondencies, melancholies, depressions|反义词：joys, happinesses, elations
|词性: 名词(plural)
