obbligato:/ˌɑːblɪˈɡɑːtoʊ/ /ˌɒblɪˈɡɑːtəʊ/
| n. |(音乐)装饰音；必奏部分；义不容辞的事|A musical line or passage, often quite elaborate, that is added to a composition as an embellishment but which, unlike an ad lib, is written out in full and is an essential part of the piece. Something that is absolutely necessary or mandatory.  |The violinist played a beautiful obbligato.|近义词：indispensable, essential, required, mandatory, necessary|反义词：optional, unnecessary, inessential|noun
