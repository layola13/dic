cochin:/ˈkoʊtʃɪn/| n.|科钦（印度地名）;  一种大型家禽|A city and port on the Malabar Coast of southwest India. Also, a large breed of domestic fowl.|例句：We visited Cochin during our trip to India. 我们在印度之旅期间参观了科钦。

例句：My grandmother raises Cochins; they are such beautiful birds. 我祖母养科钦鸡；它们是如此美丽的鸟。|近义词：hen, chicken (referring to the fowl)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
