boatlike:/ˈboʊtlaɪk/| adj.|像船的，船形的|Resembling a boat in shape or form. |The vehicle had a boatlike hull, allowing it to easily navigate the flooded streets. 这辆车有一个像船一样的船体，使其能够轻松地在被洪水淹没的街道上行驶。|The canoe glided across the water with a boatlike motion. 这独木舟以像船一样的动作在水上滑行。|近义词：shiplike, nautical, marine|反义词:land-based, terrestrial|词性:形容词

Etymology: boat + like.  "Boat" comes from Old English "bāt", from Proto-Germanic "*baitaz". "Like" comes from Old English "gelīc", from Proto-Germanic "*galikaz".
