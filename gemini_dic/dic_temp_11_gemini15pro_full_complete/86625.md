helispherical:/ˌhɛlɪˈsfɛrɪkəl/
adjective
半球形的；螺旋球形的
Relating to or resembling a hemisphere; having the form of a spiral sphere.
This rare mineral forms helispherical crystals. 这种稀有矿物形成螺旋球形晶体。
The architect designed a helispherical dome for the new planetarium.  这位建筑师为新的天文馆设计了一个半球形的穹顶。
近义词: hemispherical, semiglobular
反义词: planar, flat
词性: adjective
