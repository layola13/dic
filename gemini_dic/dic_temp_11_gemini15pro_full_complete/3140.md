aestheticist: /ɛsˈθɛtɪsɪst/ /iːsˈθɛtɪsɪst/
n.
唯美主义者；耽于美感的人
A person who is devoted to aesthetic principles and the pursuit of beauty, especially in art, literature, and design.
Oscar Wilde was a famous aestheticist, known for his flamboyant style and emphasis on art for art's sake. 奥斯卡·王尔德是一位著名的唯美主义者，以其华丽的风格和强调为艺术而艺术而闻名。

The aestheticist decorated his home with exquisite art and furniture. 这位唯美主义者用精美的艺术品和家具装饰他的家。

近义词: connoisseur, art lover, dilettante
反义词: philistine, pragmatist
词性: noun
