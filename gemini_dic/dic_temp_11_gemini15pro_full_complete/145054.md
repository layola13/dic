overallegiance:/ˌoʊvərəˈliːdʒəns/ | n. | 过度忠诚；过分效忠 | Excessive loyalty or devotion, often to a fault.  It implies a degree of blind loyalty that may override other important considerations like ethics or reason. | His overallegiance to the company led him to cover up their illegal activities. 他对公司的过度忠诚导致他掩盖了他们的非法活动。| Example: The soldier's overallegiance to his commanding officer led him to question orders.  这个士兵对指挥官的过分忠诚使他开始质疑命令。| synonyms: excessive loyalty, blind devotion, undue allegiance, unquestioning obedience | antonyms: disloyalty, treachery, betrayal, insubordination | noun
