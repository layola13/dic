job:/dʒɒb/ (British) /dʒɑːb/ (American)| n.|工作，职业，任务|A regular activity performed in exchange for payment, especially as one's trade, occupation, or profession.|He's looking for a new job. 他正在寻找一份新工作。|Synonyms: occupation, profession, career, work, task, assignment|Antonyms: unemployment, leisure, idleness|noun

job:/dʒɒb/ (British) /dʒɑːb/ (American)| vt.|做零工，承包|To do odd jobs or piecework.|He jobbed around the country for a few years before settling down.  他在全国各地打了几年零工才安定下来。|Synonyms: freelance, contract|Antonyms: employ, hire|verb
