enchodus:/ˈɛŋkoʊdəs/| n. | 弓鳍鱼 | An extinct genus of ray-finned fish that lived from the Cretaceous to the Eocene epoch.  They are characterized by large, fang-like teeth. |  Enchodus fossils are commonly found in marine deposits.  弓鳍鱼化石常见于海洋沉积物中。|  Example:  The museum displayed a well-preserved Enchodus skull.  博物馆展出了一个保存完好的弓鳍鱼头骨。| 近义词: saber-toothed herring (colloquial),  extinct fish | 反义词: extant fish | 词性: noun
