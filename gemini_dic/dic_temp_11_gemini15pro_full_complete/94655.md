impressionless:/ɪmˈprɛʃənləs/| adj.|无表情的，冷漠的，无动于衷的|Showing no emotion or feeling; not easily impressed.|Her face remained impressionless throughout the entire ordeal. 她整场磨难中都面无表情。|近义词：apathetic, unemotional, indifferent, stoic, blank, expressionless|反义词：expressive, emotional, passionate, responsive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From im- (not) + impression + -less (without).  The word "impression" comes from the Latin "impressio," meaning a pressing into or upon.
