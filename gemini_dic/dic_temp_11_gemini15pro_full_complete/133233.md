nonexclusive:/ˌnɒnɪkˈskluːsɪv/
adjective
非独家的；非排他的
Not limited to one person or group; shared.  Permitting or existing under terms that do not exclude others from similar participation or action.
This is a nonexclusive agreement, meaning you can work with other companies. 这是一份非独家协议，意味着你可以与其他公司合作。
The restaurant has a nonexclusive contract with the supplier, allowing them to source ingredients from other vendors.  这家餐厅与供应商签订了非独家合同，允许他们从其他供应商处采购食材。
synonyms: nonspecific, general, open, unrestricted, shared
antonyms: exclusive, sole, restricted, limited, private
词性: adjective
