outglittering: /ˌaʊtˈɡlɪtərɪŋ/
Verb
胜过光辉，更加闪耀
Surpassing in glitter or brilliance; outshining.
Her dress outglittered all the others at the ball. 她的礼服在舞会上比其他所有人的都闪耀。
Near Synonyms: outshining, eclipsing, surpassing
Antonyms: dulling, dimming, obscuring
词性: Verb (动词)
