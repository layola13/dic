humanistically:/ˌhjuːmənˈɪstɪkli/
adv.
人文地，人道地
From a humanistic point of view, emphasizing human values and concerns.
例句：The issue was approached humanistically, focusing on the impact on individuals.  这个问题是从人道主义的角度来处理的，重点关注对个人的影响。

例句：She was educated humanistically, learning about philosophy, literature, and art. 她接受了人文主义教育，学习了哲学、文学和艺术。
近义词：humanely, compassionately, empathetically
反义词：inhumanely, cruelly, callously
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "humanist" + "-ic" + "-al" + "-ly".  "Humanist" comes from the Latin "humanus" (human) + "-ist". "-ic" and "-al" forms adjectives, and "-ly" forms adverbs.
