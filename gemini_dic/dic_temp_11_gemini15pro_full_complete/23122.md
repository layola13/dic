bourgeoisifications: /ˌbʊərʒwɑːzɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 中产阶级化，资产阶级化 | The process by which a neighborhood or area becomes more middle-class or upper-middle-class, often resulting in increased property values and the displacement of lower-income residents.  This involves changes in the character of a neighborhood through the influx of more affluent residents and businesses. |  The rapid bourgeoisifications of the neighborhood led to a surge in property prices. 这个社区迅速的中产阶级化导致房价飙升。

The bourgeoisifications of the city center has displaced many long-time residents. 市中心的中产阶级化使许多长期居民流离失所。 | 近义词: gentrification,  upscaling | 反义词: proletarianization, impoverishment | 词性: noun (plural)
