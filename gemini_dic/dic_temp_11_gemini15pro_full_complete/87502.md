heterochronism:/ˌhɛtərəˈkrɒnɪzəm/ | n. | 异时性，异时发生 |A developmental change in the timing or rate of events, leading to changes in size and shape. |Heterochronism can affect the rate of development of a body part relative to the body as a whole. 异时性会影响身体部位相对于整个身体的发育速度。| Heterochronism played a key role in the evolution of the human skull. 异时性在人类头骨的进化中起到了关键作用。 |近义词: heterochrony |反义词: isochronism |词性: noun
