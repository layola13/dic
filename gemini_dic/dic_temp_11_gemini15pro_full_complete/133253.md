nonexercises:/ˌnɑːnˈɛksərˌsaɪzɪz/ | n. | 非运动性活动 | Activities that are not considered exercise, but still contribute to calorie expenditure. |  People underestimate the calories burned through nonexercises like fidgeting or walking around the office. 人们低估了像坐立不安或在办公室走动等非运动活动所消耗的卡路里。| synonyms: non-exercise activity thermogenesis (NEAT), spontaneous physical activity | antonyms: exercises, workouts | noun

nonexercise:/ˌnɑːnˈɛksərˌsaɪz/ | adj. | 非运动的 | Not related to exercise. | The study focused on the nonexercise components of daily energy expenditure. 这项研究的重点是日常能量消耗的非运动组成部分。| synonyms: non-exercising, non-workout | antonyms: exercise, workout | adjective
