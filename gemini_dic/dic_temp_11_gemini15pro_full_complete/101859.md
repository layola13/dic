isagon:/ˈaɪsəɡɒn/ | n. | 等角多边形；正多边形 | A polygon whose angles are all equal. |  Example: A square is an isagon. 正方形是一个等角多边形。

Example: A regular pentagon is an isagon. 正五边形是一个等角多边形。| 近义词: equilateral polygon, regular polygon | 反义词: irregular polygon | 词性: noun
