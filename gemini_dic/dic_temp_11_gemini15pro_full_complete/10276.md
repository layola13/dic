appalment:/əˈpɔːlmənt/| n. |惊骇，恐慌|The appalment she felt after the accident was overwhelming.  事故发生后，她感到无比惊骇。|Etymology: From Middle English *appallen, from Old French apalir "to make pale," from a- "to" + palir "to grow pale."|近义词：horror, dismay, terror, fright|反义词：calm, composure, tranquility|noun
