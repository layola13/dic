amaryllidaceous:/ˌæmərɪlɪˈdeɪʃəs/
adj.
石蒜科的; 像石蒜的
Belonging to or resembling the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae), which includes plants like daffodils, snowdrops, and amaryllis.
Daffodils and snowdrops are amaryllidaceous plants.  水仙和雪花莲都是石蒜科植物。
The amaryllidaceous flowers were a vibrant splash of color in the garden.  石蒜科的花朵在花园里构成了一片鲜艳的色彩。
近义词: liliaceous (somewhat similar appearance)
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic classification)
词性: adjective
