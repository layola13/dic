carnauba: /kɑːrˈnɔːbə/ /kɑːrˈnaʊbə/
n.
巴西棕榈蜡，巴西蜡
A hard wax obtained from the leaves of a Brazilian palm tree (Copernicia prunifera), used in polishes, varnishes, and cosmetics.
Carnauba wax is used in car polish to give it a shine. 巴西棕榈蜡用于汽车抛光剂中，使其具有光泽。
Carnauba wax is harvested from the leaves of the carnauba palm. 巴西棕榈蜡是从巴西棕榈树的叶子上采集的。
近义词: palm wax
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of wax)

词性: noun
