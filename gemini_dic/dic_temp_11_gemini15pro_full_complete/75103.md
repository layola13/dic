fringier:/ˈfrɪndʒiər/
adjective
更边缘的，更另类的
more unconventional, more on the fringes of society or a particular group
Her fashion sense became increasingly fringier as she got older. 她的时尚感随着年龄的增长变得越来越另类。
Synonyms: unconventional, eccentric, offbeat, quirky, bohemian, avant-garde, alternative
Antonyms: conventional, mainstream, orthodox, traditional
词性: 形容词

fringe:/frɪndʒ/
noun
边缘，流苏
the outer or less important part of an area, group, or activity; a decorative border of hanging threads
They lived on the fringe of society. 他们生活在社会的边缘。
The curtains had a fringe along the bottom. 窗帘底部有一圈流苏。
Synonyms: edge, border, periphery, margin, rim, fringe benefit, perk
Antonyms: center, core, mainstream
词性: 名词
