imbibe:/ɪmˈbaɪb/ | v. | 吸收， imbibing, imbibed, imbibes | To absorb or assimilate something, especially information or ideas; to drink (alcohol). |  He imbibed a great deal of knowledge from his travels. 他从旅行中吸收了大量的知识。

She imbibed several glasses of wine at the party. 她在派对上喝了几杯酒。| 近义词: absorb, assimilate, drink, consume | 反义词: exude, emit, discharge | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin imbibere, from Latin in- + bibere "to drink".
