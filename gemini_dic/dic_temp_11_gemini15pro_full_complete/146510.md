overgeneralizes:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度概括，过分概括
Draws a conclusion about a whole group based on too few examples or insufficient evidence.
He overgeneralizes from a single bad experience and now avoids all dogs. 他因一次不好的经历就过度概括，现在躲避所有的狗。
Synonyms: stereotypes, generalizes excessively, makes sweeping statements
Antonyms: specifies, particularizes, qualifies, nuances
词性: 动词
