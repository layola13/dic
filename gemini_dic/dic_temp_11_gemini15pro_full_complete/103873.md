jubilant:/ˈdʒuːbɪlənt/| adj.|欢欣鼓舞的；喜气洋洋的；兴高采烈的|Feeling or expressing great happiness, especially due to a success.|The team was jubilant after winning the championship. 球队赢得冠军后欣喜若狂。|近义词：joyful, elated, triumphant, ecstatic|反义词：sad, dejected, miserable, despondent|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin jubilant-, jubilans, present participle of jubilare ‘to raise a shout of joy,’ from jubilum.
