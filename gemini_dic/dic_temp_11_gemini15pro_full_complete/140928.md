ondograph:/ˈɑːndəɡræf/ | n. |波形记录器；浪形记录器|An instrument for recording wave forms, especially variations in electric current.|The ondograph was used to analyze the fluctuating power output of the generator.|近义词:oscillograph, recorder|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:  From French *ondographe*, from *onde* (wave) + *-graphe* (writer, recorder).  Ultimately from Greek *onda* (wave) and *graphein* (to write).
