confer:/kənˈfɜːr/ (US) /kənˈfəːr/ (UK)| v.|授予，赋予; 商量，商议|To grant or bestow (a title, degree, benefit, or honor). To discuss something with someone else; consult; have discussions.|例句：The university conferred an honorary degree on the renowned author. 大学授予这位著名作家荣誉学位。

例句：The committee will confer to decide on the next course of action. 委员会将商议决定下一步行动。|近义词：grant, bestow, award, consult, discuss, deliberate|反义词：revoke, withhold, refuse|词性:动词
