admirableness:/ˌædmərəbəlnəs/
n.
令人钦佩的品质；值得赞赏
The quality of being admirable; worthiness of admiration.
Her admirableness shone through in her selfless acts of kindness. 她的无私的善举展现了她令人钦佩的品质。
The painting's admirableness lies in its intricate detail and vibrant colors. 这幅画的令人钦佩之处在于其复杂的细节和鲜艳的色彩。
synonyms: excellence, merit, virtue, worthiness
antonyms: awfulness, badness, deficiency, inadequacy
noun


Etymology:
From admirable + -ness.  Admirable comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin admirabilis, from admirari ‘to wonder at’.
