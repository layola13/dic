kasa:/ˈkɑːsə/ | n. |  茅屋; 草棚| A simple dwelling, often made of straw or other natural materials. Origin: From Japanese 傘 (kasa), extending its meaning from a covering to a simple shelter. |  The villagers lived in simple kasas. 村民们住在简陋的茅屋里。 | Synonyms: hut, shack, cabin | Antonyms: mansion, palace | Noun
