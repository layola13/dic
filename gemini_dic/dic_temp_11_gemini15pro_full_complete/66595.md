exhume:/ɪɡˈzjuːm/ /ɛksˈhjuːm/ | vt. | 挖掘；掘出（尤指尸体）| To dig out (something buried, especially a corpse) from the earth. | The body was exhumed for further investigation.  尸体被挖掘出来进行进一步调查。

The police exhumed the body to perform an autopsy. 警方挖出尸体进行尸检。

| 近义词：disinter, unearthor, dig up | 反义词：bury, inter | 词性：动词
