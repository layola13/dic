anagrams:/ˈænəɡræmz/| n. |字谜游戏，由相同字母组成的不同单词|A word or phrase formed by rearranging the letters of a different word or phrase, typically using all the original letters exactly once. |例句：Listen, chimney is an anagram of my niche. 听着，“chimney”是我的小众的字谜游戏。|例句：The word "silent" is an anagram of "listen".  单词 "silent" 是 "listen" 的字谜。|近义词：transposition, permutation, wordplay|反义词:|noun
