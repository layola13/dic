defended:/dɪˈfɛndɪd/
verb
已辩护的，已保卫的
past tense and past participle of "defend"  是动词"defend"的过去式和过去分词
He defended his actions in court. 他在法庭上为自己辩护。
近义词:protected, guarded, shielded, advocated for
反义词:attacked, assaulted, prosecuted
词性:verb

PHRASE->
defend against:  防御；抵抗  The soldiers defended the city against the enemy.  士兵们保卫城市抵抗敌人。
defend from:  保护…免受…  He defended his family from harm.  他保护他的家人免受伤害。
defend oneself:  为自己辩护  She had to defend herself against the accusations. 她不得不为自己辩护，驳斥那些指控。
defend a position: 捍卫一个立场  He strongly defended his position on the issue. 他坚决捍卫自己在这个问题上的立场。
defend a title: 卫冕冠军  She is defending her title at Wimbledon this year. 她今年在温布尔登卫冕她的冠军头衔。
