extraparochial:/ˌekstrəpəˈrəʊkiəl/
adjective
堂区外的；不受俗世影响的
Not belonging to or under the authority of any particular parish; outside the usual bounds of parish organization.  Figuratively, it can mean detached from the ordinary or mundane.
The extraparochial area was not subject to the same taxes as the rest of the town.  这个堂区外区域不像镇上的其他地方一样需要缴纳相同的税款。

The artist lived an extraparochial existence, unconcerned with the daily grind of most people. 这位艺术家过着一种超然物外的日子，不关心大多数人的日常琐事。

synonym:nonparochial, outlying, detached, unconventional
antonym:parochial, conventional, mainstream
词性:adjective
