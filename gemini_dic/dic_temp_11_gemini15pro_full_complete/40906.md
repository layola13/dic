coolant:/ˈkuːlənt/| n.|冷却剂，散热剂|A fluid used to reduce or regulate the temperature of a system, typically in a car engine or other machinery.|The car overheated because there was a leak in the coolant system. 汽车因为冷却系统泄漏而过热。|Synonyms: antifreeze, refrigerant|Antonyms: heating agent|noun
