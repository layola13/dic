interdictor:/ˈɪntərˌdɪktər/ | n. | 禁止者，阻碍者 |  A person or thing that prohibits or forbids something. | The court appointed an interdictor to manage the estate until the heir came of age. 法院指定了一名禁止者来管理遗产，直到继承人成年。| Synonyms: prohibitor, inhibitor, obstructor | Antonyms: facilitator, enabler | Noun

interdict:/ˌɪntərˈdɪkt/ | v. | 禁止，阻止 | To officially forbid or prohibit something. | The government interdicted the sale of alcohol to minors. 政府禁止向未成年人出售酒精。| Synonyms: prohibit, forbid, ban, bar | Antonyms: permit, allow, authorize | Verb
