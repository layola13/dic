overhair: /ˈoʊvərˌhɛr/ | n. |  覆盖毛，保护毛 | The overhair of a cat protects the softer undercoat from the elements.  猫的覆盖毛保护较柔软的底毛免受自然环境的影响。| Example: The dog's overhair was thick and glossy. 这只狗的覆盖毛浓密而有光泽。| Synonym: guard hair, topcoat | Antonym: undercoat, underfur | noun


overhair: /ˈoʊvərˌhɛr/ | n. | (艺术)覆盖的毛发 (绘画或雕塑中描绘的) | The artist meticulously rendered the overhair of the horse's mane. 艺术家一丝不苟地描绘了马鬃毛的覆盖毛。 | Example: The sculptor captured the texture of the lion's overhair.  雕塑家捕捉到了狮子覆盖毛的质感。| Synonym: outer hair, surface hair | Antonym: undercoat, underfur | noun
