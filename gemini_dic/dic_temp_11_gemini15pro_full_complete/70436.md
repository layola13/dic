figulated:/ˈfɪɡjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. |  塑造的，成形的 |  Formed into a definite shape; figured.  This word is archaic and rarely used. |  The artisan figulated the clay into a beautiful vase. 工匠把黏土塑造成一个漂亮的花瓶。 |  shaped, molded, formed |  formless, shapeless | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *figulatus, past participle of *figulare (“to shape, mold”), from Latin figulus (“potter”), from figus (“that which molds or shapes”).
