daybeds:/ˈdeɪbedz/
n.
沙发床，躺椅
A piece of furniture that can be used as both a sofa and a bed.
We bought a daybed for the guest room so it can be used as a sofa or a bed. 我们为客房买了一张沙发床，这样它既可以用作沙发，也可以用作床。
The daybed on the porch is perfect for an afternoon nap.  门廊上的躺椅非常适合午后小睡。
