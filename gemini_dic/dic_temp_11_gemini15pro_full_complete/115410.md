manteau:/ˈmæntoʊ/ (US), /ˈmæntoʊ/ (UK)| n. |大衣；覆盖物；掩饰；托词|A cloak or loose outer garment, often worn by women. Also, something that conceals or disguises something else.  Figuratively, it can refer to a pretext or excuse.|She wore a stylish manteau over her evening gown. 她穿着一件时髦的大衣套在晚礼服外面。

The project was a manteau for more sinister activities. 这个项目是更邪恶活动的掩饰。|近义词：cloak, cover, disguise, pretext, excuse|反义词: revelation, exposure|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "mantel," meaning "cloak" or "mantle," ultimately from Latin "mantellum," meaning "cloth, napkin, covering."
