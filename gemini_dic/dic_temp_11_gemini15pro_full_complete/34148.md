clairvoyance:/ˌklɛərˈvɔɪəns/| n. |透视力；洞察力|The ability to see things beyond the range of ordinary perception, or to know things without ordinary means.  The supposed faculty of perceiving things or events in the future or beyond normal sensory contact.|She claims to have the gift of clairvoyance. 她声称拥有透视的能力。|Near Synonyms:  precognition, second sight, foreknowledge, intuition, prescience|Antonyms:  ignorance, blindness, unawareness|noun
