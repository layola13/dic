cueing:/ˈkjuːɪŋ/ | n. |提示，暗示，线索 |The actor missed his cueing and stumbled over his lines.  这位演员错过了提示，念错了台词。|Example: The lighting cueing for the play was complex. 这部戏剧的灯光提示很复杂。|Synonyms: prompting, signaling, indicating |Antonyms:  miscuing, misleading | noun


cueing:/ˈkjuːɪŋ/ | v. |提示，暗示 |The director cued the actors to begin the scene. 导演提示演员开始表演。|Example:  She cued the band to start playing. 她示意乐队开始演奏。|Synonyms:  prompting, signaling, directing |Antonyms: ignoring, neglecting | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the noun "cue" (a signal, hint, or reminder), which itself likely originated from the letter "Q" marked on actors' scripts to indicate their entrances or lines.

PHRASE->
cue sb in (on sth):  向某人提供信息  Example: Can you cue me in on what's happening? 你能告诉我发生了什么事吗？
cue up:  准备播放  Example: Cue up the next song. 准备播放下一首歌。
take a cue from:  效仿，模仿  Example:  Take a cue from the experts.  向专家学习。
right on cue:  恰好在这个时候 Example: Right on cue, the phone rang.  电话铃声恰好在这个时候响了。
miss a cue:  错过提示 Example: The actor missed his cue and ruined the scene. 演员错过了提示，毁了这场戏。
