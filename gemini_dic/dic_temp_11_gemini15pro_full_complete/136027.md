nonrealization:/ˌnɒnriːəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌnɑːnriːələˈzeɪʃən/
n.
未实现，未实现的事物
The nonrealization of his dreams left him feeling empty. 他梦想的未实现让他感到空虚。
The project's nonrealization was due to a lack of funding.  该项目未能实现是因为缺乏资金。
non- + realization
noun
failure to realize or bring into being
The nonrealization of his ambitions frustrated him. 他未能实现自己的抱负，这让他感到沮丧。
Synonyms: non-fulfillment, failure
Antonyms: realization, fulfillment, achievement
