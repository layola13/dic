approof:/əˈpruːf/ | adj. | 批准的，认可的 |  Archaic form of "approved."  Meaning having received approval or sanction.  Rarely used in modern English. |  He gave his approof to the plan. 他批准了这个计划。(古英语用法，现代英语已很少使用) | 近义词: approved, sanctioned, authorized | 反义词: disapproved, rejected | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *appreven, from Old French approuver, from Latin approbare  ("to approve"). The suffix "-oof" is an archaic variant of "-al" or "-ed."
