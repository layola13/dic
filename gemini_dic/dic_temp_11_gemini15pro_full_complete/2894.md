advertising's:/ˈædvərtaɪzɪŋz/
noun
广告的；广告业的
Relating to advertising, or the business of advertising.
Advertising's impact on children is a serious concern.  广告对儿童的影响是一个严重的问题。
The advertising's message was clear and concise. 广告的讯息清晰而简洁。
Synonyms: promotional, publicity
Antonyms:  concealment, suppression
词性: 名词所有格


ETYMOLOGY->
From advertise + -ing + -'s.  "Advertise" comes from Old French "advertir" (to warn, inform), ultimately from Latin "advertere" (to turn towards). The "-ing" suffix forms a present participle or gerund, and the "'s" indicates possession.
