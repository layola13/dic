Example:  His brutishness was masked by a veneer of politeness. 他的粗暴被一层彬彬有礼的伪装所掩盖。|Synonyms: savagery, barbarity, brutality, cruelty, ferocity|Antonyms: gentleness, kindness, compassion, humanity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From brute + -ish + -ness.  Brute comes from the Old French brut, meaning "raw, unpolished, rough, coarse."
