awkward:/ˈɔːkwərd/ (US) /ˈɔːkwəd/ (UK)| adj.|尴尬的，笨拙的，棘手的|Causing difficulty; embarrassing or inconvenient; not graceful or skillful.|例句：I felt awkward when I spilled my drink on her. 我把饮料洒在她身上时感到很尴尬。|近义词：clumsy, uncomfortable, difficult, tricky|反义词:graceful, skillful, convenient, easy|词性:形容词

awkward:/ˈɔːkwərd/ (US) /ˈɔːkwəd/ (UK)| n.|笨拙的人; 尴尬的局面|A clumsy person; an embarrassing or difficult situation.|例句：There was an awkward silence after he told the joke. 他讲完笑话后，出现了尴尬的沉默。|近义词：clumsiness, difficulty|反义词:grace, skill, ease|词性:名词
