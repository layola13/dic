lapis-lazuli:/ˈlæpɪs ˈlæzjʊlaɪ/ /ˈlæpɪs ˈlæzəli/| n.|青金石|a deep blue semi-precious stone prized since antiquity for its intense color.  It is a rock composed primarily of lazurite, a sodium calcium aluminum silicate sulfur mineral.|The pharaoh's mask was inlaid with lapis lazuli. 法老的面具镶嵌着青金石。|Synonyms: lazurite, azure stone|Antonyms: N/A|noun
