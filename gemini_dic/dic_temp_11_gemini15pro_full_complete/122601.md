modernizable:/ˈmɒdənaɪzəbl/ (US) /ˈmɑːdərnaɪzəbl/| adj.|可现代化的|Able to be made modern or contemporary; capable of being updated or adapted to current standards or needs.|The factory's machinery is old but modernizable.  这家工厂的机器很旧，但是可以现代化。|The company invested in modernizable technology.  这家公司投资了可现代化的技术。|近义词：upgradable, adaptable, improvable|反义词：obsolete, outdated|词性:形容词

词源:
From modernize + -able.  Modernize itself comes from modern + -ize.  Modern ultimately derives from Late Latin modernus.
