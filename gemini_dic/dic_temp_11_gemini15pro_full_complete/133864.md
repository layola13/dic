nongratifyingly:/ˌnɒnˈɡrætɪfaɪɪŋli/
adverb
不令人满意地
in a way that does not give pleasure or satisfaction.
The experiment ended nongratifyingly, yielding no conclusive results.  这个实验结果不令人满意，没有得出任何结论性的结果。
He performed nongratifyingly in the competition, failing to meet his own expectations. 他在比赛中的表现不尽如人意，未能达到自己的期望。
近义词:unsatisfyingly, disappointingly, displeasingly
反义词:satisfyingly, pleasingly, gratifyingly
词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + gratifying (giving pleasure or satisfaction) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The root "gratify" comes from the Latin "gratificari," meaning "to please."
