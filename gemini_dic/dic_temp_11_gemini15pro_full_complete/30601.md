cerebralism: /ˈsɛrəbrəlɪzəm/ /səˈriːbrəlɪzəm/
n.
理性主义，知性主义, 过度强调智力的倾向
The philosophy that mental activity is the only or most important aspect of human existence.
He exhibited a certain cerebralism, preferring intellectual pursuits over physical activity. 他表现出某种理性主义，偏爱智力追求而不是体力活动。
intellectualism, rationalism
emotionalism, sentimentalism
