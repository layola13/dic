insociability:/ɪnˌsoʊʃəˈbɪləti/
n.
不善交际;孤僻
The state or quality of being unsociable; aversion to society.
His insociability made it difficult for him to make friends. 他的不善交际让他很难交到朋友。
近义词：unsociableness, aloofness, reclusiveness, standoffishness
反义词：sociability, gregariousness
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin īnsociābilis (“unsociable”) + -ity.  The Latin word is formed from in- (“not”) + sociābilis (“sociable”), from socius (“companion”) + -ābilis (“-able”).
