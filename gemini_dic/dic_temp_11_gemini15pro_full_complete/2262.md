adelopod:/ˈædɪləpɒd/ | n. |无足幼虫；蛆形幼虫|A larva lacking thoracic legs and abdominal prolegs, typically found in certain insect groups.  It is maggot-like in appearance. |The adelopod larva of the beetle burrowed into the wood.  甲虫的无足幼虫钻进了木头里。|Synonym: vermiform larva, apodous larva|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider "legged larva") |noun
