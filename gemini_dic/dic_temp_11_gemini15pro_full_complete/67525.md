extracurriculars: /ˌɛkstrəkəˈrɪkjələrz/ | n. | 课外活动 | Activities that are pursued in addition to the regular academic curriculum of school or college. |  She has a very impressive list of extracurriculars, including debate team, orchestra, and volunteering at the local hospital. 她有一份令人印象深刻的课外活动清单，包括辩论队、管弦乐队和在当地医院做志愿者。

| 近义词：activities, hobbies, pursuits | 反义词：academics, studies | 词性：名词

extracurricular: /ˌɛkstrəkəˈrɪkjələr/ | adj. | 课外的 | Outside the regular curriculum of school or college. |  She participated in many extracurricular activities in college. 她在大学里参加了许多课外活动。

| 近义词：outside, nonscholastic | 反义词：academic, scholastic | 词性：形容词
