altruistic:/ˌæltruˈɪstɪk/
adjective
利他的；无私的
Showing a disinterested and selfless concern for the well-being of others; unselfish.
例句：Her work for the charity was purely altruistic. 她为慈善机构工作纯粹是利他行为。

例句：Altruistic behavior is not always rewarded in our society. 在我们的社会里，利他行为并不总是得到回报。

近义词：selfless, benevolent, charitable, philanthropic, humanitarian
反义词：selfish, egoistic, self-centered
词性: adjective


altruism:/ˈæltruɪzəm/
noun
利他主义；无私
The belief in or practice of disinterested and selfless concern for the well-being of others.
例句：He was driven by altruism to help the homeless. 他出于利他主义帮助无家可归的人。

例句：True altruism expects nothing in return. 真正的利他主义不求回报。

近义词：selflessness, benevolence, charity, philanthropy, humanitarianism
反义词：selfishness, egoism, self-centeredness
词性: noun

altruistically:/ˌæltruˈɪstɪkli/
adverb
利他地；无私地
In an altruistic manner; unselfishly.
例句：She altruistically donated her kidney to her brother. 她无私地把自己的肾脏捐给了她的兄弟。

例句：He acted altruistically in rescuing the child from the burning building. 他舍己救人，从着火的大楼里救出了孩子。

近义词：selflessly, benevolently, charitably, philanthropically
反义词：selfishly, egoistically
词性: adverb


词源: From French *altruisme*, from Italian *altrui* "somebody else," from Latin *alteri huic* "to this other." Coined by French philosopher Auguste Comte.
