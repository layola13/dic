extracts:/ɪkˈstrækts/ (美式) /ɪkˈstræktz/ (英式)| v. | 提取，取出，摘录 | To remove or take out, especially by effort or force. |  He extracted several important figures from the report. 他从报告中提取了几个重要的数据。| synonyms: remove, take out, draw out, pull out, obtain, derive | antonyms: insert, inject, implant | verb
