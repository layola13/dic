heterogangliate:/ˌhɛtərəʊˈɡæŋɡlɪɪt/ /ˌhɛtəroʊˈɡæŋɡliɪt/
adjective
异神经节的
Having or relating to ganglia that are structurally different.  Relating to or possessing a nervous system in which the ganglia differ in structure and function.
The heterogangliate mollusks exhibit a more complex nervous system than their simpler counterparts. 异神经节的软体动物比结构简单的软体动物表现出更复杂的神经系统。
Certain species of snails are known to be heterogangliate.  某些蜗牛种类是已知的异神经节动物。
近义词: variable ganglion structure, differentiated ganglia
反义词: homogangliate
词性: adjective
