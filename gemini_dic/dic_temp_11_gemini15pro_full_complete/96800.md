inerrancy:/ɪˈnɛrənsi/| n.|无误性，绝对正确|The doctrine of inerrancy holds that the Bible is without error.  无误性学说认为圣经没有错误。|例句：The preacher emphasized the inerrancy of scripture.  牧师强调了圣经的无误性。

例句：Some scholars question the inerrancy of ancient texts. 一些学者质疑古代文献的无误性。|近义词：infallibility, accuracy, truth|反义词：fallibility, error, inaccuracy|词性:noun
