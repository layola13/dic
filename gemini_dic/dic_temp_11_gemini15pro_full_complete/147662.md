overpopulous:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːpjələs/| adj.|人口过多的，人口稠密的|Having too large a population.|The city is becoming increasingly overpopulated.  这个城市人口越来越稠密。|近义词：overcrowded, densely populated|反义词: sparsely populated, underpopulated|词性: adjective
Etymology:  From 'over-' (excessively) + 'populous' (having a large population).  'Populous' ultimately derives from the Latin 'populus' (people).
