allottable:/əˈlɒtəbl/| adj.|可分配的|Able to be allotted or assigned. |The company has a certain amount of allottable resources for each project.|近义词: assignable, distributable, apportionable|反义词: unallotable, indivisible|词性:形容词
词源: From allot + -able.  Allot comes from Old French *aloter "to divide by lots," from a- "to" + lot "portion, share."
