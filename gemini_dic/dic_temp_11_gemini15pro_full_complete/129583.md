ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + collaboration.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "lack of." "Collaboration" comes from the French "collaboration," from "collaborer," meaning "to work together," ultimately from the Latin "collaborare," from "com-" (together) + "laborare" (to work).
