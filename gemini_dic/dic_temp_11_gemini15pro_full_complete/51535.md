detur:/ˈdeɪtʊr/ | v. | let it be given; it is given |  It is a third-person singular present passive subjunctive form of the Latin verb *dō*, "I give". It indicates a command or concession. | Detur digniori. (Let it be given to the more worthy.) | 近义词: concedatur (let it be conceded), tribuatur (let it be attributed) | 反义词:  negatur (it is denied) | verb
