dictionary:/ˈdɪkʃəneri/ (US), /ˈdɪkʃənəri/ (UK)| n.|词典，字典|A book or electronic resource that lists the words of a language (typically in alphabetical order) and gives their meaning, or gives the equivalent words in a different language, often also providing information about pronunciation, origin, and usage.|I looked up the word in the dictionary.|近义词:lexicon, wordbook, glossary|反义词:None|noun
