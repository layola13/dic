hemmer:/ˈhɛmər/| n.|卷边器，缝边的人|A device or person that hems fabric.|She used a hemmer to finish the edge of the skirt. 她用卷边器缝好了裙子的边缘。|近义词：sewing machine, seamstress|反义词:|noun

hemmer:/ˈhɛmər/| v.|缝边，包围，限制|To fold and sew the edge of fabric; to surround or confine.|He hemmed the trousers to the correct length. 他把裤子缝到合适的长度。|近义词：sew, stitch, enclose, restrict|反义词:expand, liberate|verb
