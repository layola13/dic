eurocentricity:/ˌjʊərəʊˈsɛntrɪsɪti/ | n. |欧洲中心主义|The belief that European culture is superior to all other cultures and should be the standard by which all other cultures are judged.  Focusing on European culture or history to the exclusion of a wider world view.| Example: The history books displayed a marked eurocentricity, largely ignoring the contributions of non-European civilizations.  这些历史书籍展现出明显的欧洲中心主义，很大程度上忽略了非欧洲文明的贡献。

Example:  Her eurocentricity prevented her from fully appreciating the nuances of African art. 她的欧洲中心主义使她无法完全欣赏非洲艺术的细微之处。| 近义词: Western-centrism, Occidentalism | 反义词: multiculturalism, globalism | 词性: noun

Etymology: euro- (from Europe) + centric (from center) + -ity (noun-forming suffix)
