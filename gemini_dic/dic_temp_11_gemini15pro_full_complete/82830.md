gulaund:/ɡʊˈlɔːnd/ | n. | 沟渠; 水道; 下水道 |A ditch or channel, especially one carrying water; a drain. | The gulaund overflowed after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，沟渠溢出来了。|The farmer dug a gulaund to irrigate his fields. 农民挖了一条沟渠来灌溉他的田地。| synonyms: ditch, channel, trench, furrow, conduit | antonyms: ridge, mound | noun
