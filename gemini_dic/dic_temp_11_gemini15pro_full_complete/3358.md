affricative:/ˈæfrɪkətɪv/| n. adj.|塞擦音|A consonant sound that begins as a stop and releases as a fricative, such as the sounds represented by the letters "ch" and "j" in English.|Example: The "ch" sound in "church" is an affricative.  “church”中的“ch”音是一个塞擦音。|Example:  The "j" sound in "judge" is an affricative. “judge”中的“j”音是一个塞擦音。|近义词: affricate|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly a simple stop or fricative)|词性: noun, adjective
