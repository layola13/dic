admiral:/ˈædmərəl/ (US) /ˈædmɪrəl/ (UK)| n.|海军上将；舰队司令|A high-ranking officer in the navy.|Nelson was a famous British admiral. 纳尔逊是一位著名的英国海军上将。|commander, captain, chief|  |noun
Etymology->
From Old French *amirall, from Arabic أمير البحر (ʾamīr al-baḥr, “commander of the sea”). Compare Italian ammiraglio, Spanish almirante, Portuguese almirante.
