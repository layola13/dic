misprescribe:/ˌmɪsprɪˈskraɪb/
verb
错误地开处方；开错药
to prescribe medicine wrongly or in an unsuitable way
The doctor misprescribed antibiotics for a viral infection. 医生错误地给病毒感染开了抗生素。
misadminister, overprescribe
prescribe correctly
v.
