geophila:/dʒiːˈɒfɪlə/ | n. | 土生植物；地生植物 | A plant that grows in or on the ground.  It refers specifically to plants whose seeds germinate underground. | Example 1: Several species of *geophila* are found in the rainforest understory.  几种土生植物发现于雨林下层。

Example 2: The *geophila* thrived in the damp soil. 这种土生植物在潮湿的土壤中茁壮成长。 | 近义词: terrestrial plant, ground plant | 反义词: epiphyte, aquatic plant | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *gē* (earth) + *philos* (loving).
