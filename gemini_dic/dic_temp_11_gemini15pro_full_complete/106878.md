labyrinthial:/ˌlæbəˈrɪnθiən/ /ˌlæbəˈrɪnθiəl/
adjective
迷宫般的；复杂的；曲折的
Resembling a labyrinth; intricate and confusing.
例句：The building's labyrinthial corridors made it easy to get lost. 这座建筑迷宫般的走廊很容易让人迷路。

例句：The plot of the novel was labyrinthial, with numerous twists and turns. 这部小说的情节错综复杂，曲折离奇。

近义词：complex, intricate, convoluted, tortuous
反义词：simple, straightforward, clear
词性: adjective
