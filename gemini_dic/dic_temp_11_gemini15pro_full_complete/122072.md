mistful:/ˈmɪstfəl/| adj.|充满雾气的；迷蒙的；若有所思的；忧郁的|Filled with or characterized by mist; dreamy, pensive, or melancholic.|The mistful morning gave the landscape a mysterious air. 充满雾气的早晨给景色增添了神秘的气氛。|近义词：hazy, misty, dreamy, pensive, melancholic|反义词：clear, bright, cheerful|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mist + -ful.  "Mist" comes from Old English *mist, of Germanic origin. "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
