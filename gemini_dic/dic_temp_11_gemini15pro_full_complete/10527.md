appositely:/əˈpɒzɪtli/| adv.|适当地，贴切地|In a manner that is suitable and relevant to the situation.  Being highly relevant and appropriate.|She appositely quoted Shakespeare in her essay about the nature of tragedy. 她在她关于悲剧本质的文章中恰当地引用了莎士比亚的话。|近义词：appropriately, suitably, pertinently, relevantly|反义词：inappropriately, irrelevantly, unsuitably|词性:副词
词源: From Latin *appositus*, past participle of *apponere* ("to put near, apply"), from *ad* ("to") + *ponere* ("to put, place").
