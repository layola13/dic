agreeable-to:/əˈɡriːəbl tuː/
adjective
可接受的，同意的，适合的
willing to agree or consent to something; pleasing or acceptable
例句：The terms of the contract were agreeable to both parties.  合同条款双方都同意。

例句：We found a house with a price agreeable to our budget. 我们找到了一个价格符合我们预算的房子。

近义词：acceptable, amenable, consenting, compliant
反义词：unacceptable, disagreeable, opposed

词性: adjective
