humanising:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 人性化的；使更人道的 | Making something more humane or giving it human qualities. |  例句：The company is trying to humanise its image by focusing on its employees' stories. 公司正试图通过关注员工的故事来使其形象更加人性化。

例句：Humanising the workplace can lead to increased productivity and job satisfaction.  使工作场所人性化可以提高生产力和工作满意度。

近义词：humanitarian, compassionate, empathetic | 反义词：dehumanising, brutalising | 词性: adjective
