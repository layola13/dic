exuding:/ɪɡˈz(j)uːdɪŋ/
| adj. | 散发出；流露出；充分显露 | Displaying openly and strongly; radiating. |  The flowers were exuding a sweet fragrance.  这些花散发着甜甜的香味。| 近义词: radiating, emanating, emitting, displaying | 反义词: concealing, hiding, suppressing |词性:形容词

exude:/ɪɡˈz(j)uːd/
| v. | 散发；流露；渗出 | To discharge or emit (a liquid or gas, for example) slowly through pores or cuts; ooze. To display (an emotion or quality) strongly and openly. | The pine trees exuded a sticky sap. 松树渗出粘稠的树脂. He exuded confidence. 他浑身散发着自信。 | 近义词: radiate, emanate, emit, ooze | 反义词: absorb, conceal | 词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exsūdāre, from ex- 'out' + sūdāre 'to sweat'.
