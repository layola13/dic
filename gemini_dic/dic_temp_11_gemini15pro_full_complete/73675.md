forgetfully:/fərˈɡetfəli/| adv.|健忘地；疏忽地|In a forgetful manner; tending to forget.  Characterized by or exhibiting forgetfulness.|She forgetfully left her keys in the car. 她健忘地把钥匙忘在了车里。|近义词：absentmindedly, heedlessly, obliviously|反义词：mindfully, attentively|词性: 副词

词源：
From the adjective "forgetful" + the adverbial suffix "-ly". "Forgetful" comes from the verb "forget" (Old English *forgietan*) + the suffix "-ful".
