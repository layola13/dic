bescatter:/bɪˈskætər/
verb
散布，点缀
To scatter or sprinkle things over a surface.
The field was bescattered with wildflowers. 田野里点缀着野花。
The sky was bescattered with stars. 天空布满了星星。
synonyms: strew, scatter, sprinkle, dot
antonyms: gather, collect
词性: 动词
