daisy:/ˈdeɪzi/ | n. |极好的事物；|Something or someone excellent or outstanding.|That new sports car is a real daisy. 那辆新的跑车真是太棒了。|近义词：excellent, wonderful, outstanding |反义词: terrible, awful | Noun
