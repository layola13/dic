nonsymmetrical: /ˌnɒn.sɪˈmetrɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːn.səˈmetrɪkəl/ | adj. | 非对称的，不对称的 | Not symmetrical; lacking symmetry. |  The artist intentionally created a nonsymmetrical design. 艺术家特意创作了一个不对称的设计。

The building's nonsymmetrical facade made it stand out. 这栋建筑不对称的正面使其脱颖而出。 | 近义词: asymmetrical, dissymmetrical, irregular, uneven | 反义词: symmetrical, balanced, even, regular | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
symmetrical (adjective): From Late Latin symmetricalis, from Greek symmetros "of like measure," from syn- "together" + metron "measure."
