lucuma:/luˈkuːmə/ | n. |  卢库马果，蛋黄果 | A subtropical fruit native to the Andean valleys of Peru, Chile, and Ecuador with a dry, sweet flesh. |  Lucuma ice cream is popular in Peru.  卢库马冰淇淋在秘鲁很受欢迎。 | Synonyms: eggfruit, lucmo | Antonyms: N/A | noun

lucuma:/luˈkuːmə/ | n. |  卢库马粉 |  A powder made from dried lucuma fruit, used as a sweetener and flavoring. | I added lucuma powder to my smoothie for a caramel-like flavor. 我在我的冰沙里加了卢库马粉，以获得焦糖般的味道。 | Synonyms: lucuma powder, dried lucuma | Antonyms: N/A | noun
