cowriting:/ˈkoʊˌraɪtɪŋ/
n.
共同写作，合作写作
The process of writing something together with another person or people.
We did some cowriting on the new song. 我们一起合作创作了这首新歌。
collaboration, joint authorship
independent writing
noun
