depoliticization:/diːpəˌlɪtɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|去政治化; 非政治化|The removal of political influence or considerations from a situation or issue.  The process of making something less political.|The depoliticization of education is a complex issue. 教育的去政治化是一个复杂的问题.|neutralization, depoliticisation|politicization|noun
