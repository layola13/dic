nondeceptions:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɛpʃənz/
noun
非欺骗；真实；诚实
The absence of deception; truthfulness or honesty.
His campaign was marked by a refreshing lack of nondeceptions. 他的竞选活动以令人耳目一新的诚实而著称。
synonyms: truths, realities, honesties, sincerities
antonyms: deceptions, lies, falsehoods, fabrications
