newtons:/ˈnjuːtnz/ |n.|牛顿（力的单位）|The unit of force in the International System of Units (SI), equal to the force that produces an acceleration of one meter per second per second on a mass of one kilogram.|The apple fell to the earth due to newtons of force.  苹果由于牛顿的力落到地上。|近义词: N (symbol), unit of force|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
