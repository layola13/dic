geodesist:/ˌdʒiːˈɒdɪsɪst/
n.
大地测量学家
A specialist in geodesy, the science of accurately measuring and understanding the Earth's shape, size, and gravitational field.
The geodesist used GPS technology to measure the movement of tectonic plates.  这位大地测量学家使用GPS技术来测量构造板块的运动。

The geodesist calculated the exact coordinates of the summit. 这位大地测量学家计算出了山顶的确切坐标。

近义词: surveyor, cartographer
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "amateur mapper")
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *géodésiste*, from *géodésie* "geodesy",  from Greek *geōdaisia*  "division of the earth," from *gē* "earth" + *daiein* "to divide."
