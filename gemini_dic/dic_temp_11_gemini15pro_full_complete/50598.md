derbio:/ˈdɜːrbioʊ/ (US), /ˈdɜːbiəʊ/ (UK)| n. |德比; 同城德比; 重要比赛|A derby is a sporting event between two rival teams, usually from the same city or region.  It often carries a heightened sense of importance and rivalry.|The Manchester derby is always a passionate affair. 曼彻斯特德比总是充满激情。|Synonym: rivalry, contest, match|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "friendly match")|Noun
