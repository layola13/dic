blunging:/ˈblʌndʒɪŋ/
verb
混合，搅拌（尤指制陶业中将粘土与水混合）
The process of mixing clay with water to form slip, used in pottery making.
The workers were blunging the clay for hours to get the right consistency. 工人们搅拌粘土好几个小时以达到合适的稠度。
Synonyms: mixing, blending, stirring, agitating
Antonyms: separating, dividing, sorting
词性: 动词 (verb)
