anticonstitutionalism:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |违宪主义；反宪政主义|Opposition to or disregard for the principles or provisions of a constitution.  It refers to beliefs and actions that challenge the legitimacy or authority of a constitution.  |His anticonstitutionalism led him to openly defy the Supreme Court ruling. 他的反宪政主义导致他公然违抗最高法院的裁决。

The group's anticonstitutionalism posed a threat to the stability of the government.  该组织的反宪政主义对政府的稳定构成了威胁。|近义词：subversion, sedition, rebellion|反义词：constitutionalism, loyalty|词性: noun
