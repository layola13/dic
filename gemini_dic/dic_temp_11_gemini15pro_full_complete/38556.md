conclusiveness:/kənˈkluːsɪvnəs/
noun
结论性；决定性
The quality of being conclusive; putting an end to doubt or question.
The conclusiveness of the evidence led to a guilty verdict.  证据的结论性导致了有罪判决。
近义词: decisiveness, definitiveness, finality
反义词: inconclusiveness, ambiguity, uncertainty
词性: noun

conclusive:/kənˈkluːsɪv/
adjective
决定性的；最后的；确实的；确凿的
Serving to settle an issue; final.
The DNA evidence was conclusive. DNA证据是确凿的。
近义词: decisive, definitive, final
反义词: inconclusive, ambiguous, uncertain
词性: adjective

conclude:/kənˈkluːd/
verb
结束；推断出；断定
1. Bring (something) to an end. 2. Arrive at a judgment or opinion by reasoning.
The meeting concluded with a vote of thanks. 会议以表决致谢结束。
We concluded that he was guilty. 我们断定他有罪。
近义词: finish, end, deduce, infer
反义词: begin, start, commence
词性: verb
