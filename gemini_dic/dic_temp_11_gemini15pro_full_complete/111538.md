loathsomenesses:/ˈloʊðsəmnəsɪz/
n.
令人厌恶的事物；令人作呕的事物
The state or quality of being loathsome; extreme disgust.
The loathsomenesses of war are well documented. 战争的种种令人厌恶的事情都有详细的记载。
近义词:repulsiveness, vileness, disgustingness, offensiveness
反义词:pleasantness, attractiveness, agreeableness
词性:名词

loathsome:/ˈloʊðsəm/
adj.
令人作呕的；令人憎恶的；极不愉快的
Causing hatred or disgust; repulsive.
He found the idea of eating insects loathsome. 他觉得吃昆虫的想法很恶心。
近义词:repulsive, disgusting, abhorrent, detestable
反义词:pleasant, agreeable, attractive, delightful
词性:形容词
