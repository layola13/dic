brooms:/bruːmz/
n.
扫帚（复数）
a brush with a long handle, used for sweeping.
She grabbed the brooms and started sweeping the floor. 她抓起扫帚开始扫地。
近义词：brushes
反义词：(无直接反义词，可考虑mop 拖把, vacuum cleaner 吸尘器)
词性：名词
