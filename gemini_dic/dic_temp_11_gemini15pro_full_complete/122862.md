mollifiable:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪəbl/ | adj. | 可软化的，可缓和的 | Capable of being mollified; appeasable. |  The strict rules were mollifiable under certain circumstances. 在某些情况下，严格的规则是可以放宽的。 |  The king's anger seemed mollifiable with a sincere apology.  国王的愤怒似乎可以通过真诚的道歉来平息。 | 近义词: appeasable, placable, conciliable | 反义词: implacable, unyielding, inflexible | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin mollificabilis, from mollificare "to soften," from Latin mollis "soft" + facere "to make."
