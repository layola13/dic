fenny:/ˈfɛni/| adj.|沼泽的，有沼泽气味的|Marshy; boggy; having the odor of a fen.  Characterized by fens. |The air hung heavy and fenny around the stagnant pool.  空气沉闷， stagnant pool周围弥漫着沼泽的气味。|近义词：marshy, boggy, swampy, miry|反义词：dry, arid|词性:形容词
词源：From Old English *fenniġ, from fenn (“fen, marsh, bog”) + -iġ. Cognate with Dutch venig.
