inconsistency:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪstənsi/ | n. |不一致，矛盾，前后矛盾|lack of consistency or agreement; incompatibility| Her inconsistency makes it hard to trust her. 她的反复无常让人难以信任她。| The inconsistency between his words and actions was apparent. 他的言行不一很明显。| 近义词: contradiction, discrepancy, incongruity, variance | 反义词: consistency, agreement, harmony, uniformity | 词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin inconsistentia, from inconsistent- ‘not standing together’ (see inconsistent).
