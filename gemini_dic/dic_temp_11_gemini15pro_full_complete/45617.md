curse:/kɜːrs/ (US) /kɜːs/ (UK)| n. vt. vi.|诅咒，咒骂; 祸害，灾难; 骂人话|A solemn utterance intended to invoke a supernatural power to inflict harm or punishment on someone or something.  Also, a cause of misfortune or trouble.|She muttered a curse under her breath. 她低声咕哝了一句咒语。|Synonyms:  malediction, imprecation, anathema, hex, jinx|Antonyms: blessing, benediction|noun
