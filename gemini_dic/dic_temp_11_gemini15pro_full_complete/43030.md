cowerers:/ˈkaʊərərz/| n. |畏缩者，躲藏者|People who cower, showing fear and submissiveness.|The cowerers huddled in the corner during the storm. 暴风雨期间，畏缩的人们挤在角落里。|Synonyms:  timid souls, shrinking violets|Antonyms:  daredevils, heroes|Noun


cower:/ˈkaʊər/| v. |畏缩，躲藏|To crouch or shrink back in fear.|The dog cowered under the table during the fireworks.  烟花爆竹期间，狗躲在桌子底下畏缩着。|Synonyms: cringe, recoil, flinch|Antonyms:  brave, confront|Verb
