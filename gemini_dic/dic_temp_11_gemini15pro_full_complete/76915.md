gantlope:/ˈɡæntləʊp/ | n. | 鞭打，笞刑（尤指旧时军队中的惩罚）| A punishment, especially in a military context, in which the offender is forced to run between two rows of men who strike them with sticks or straps. | The soldier was sentenced to the gantlope for his insubordination.  这个士兵因不服从命令而被判处笞刑。| 近义词：flogging, whipping, lashing | 反义词: reward, praise | 词性: 名词

gantlope:/ˈɡæntləʊp/ | vt. | 对…施以鞭刑，笞打 | To subject someone to the punishment of the gantlope. | The deserter was gantloped before the entire regiment.  这名逃兵在全团面前被施以鞭刑。 | 近义词：flog, whip, lash | 反义词: pardon, forgive | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->

From Swedish *gatlopp*, from *gata* ("lane, street") + *lopp* ("run, course").  The punishment involved running a gauntlet, literally a passageway lined with men wielding sticks.
