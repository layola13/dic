leukemia: /luːˈkiːmiə/ | n. | 白血病 | A malignant progressive disease in which the bone marrow and other blood-forming organs produce increased numbers of immature or abnormal leukocytes. These suppress the production of normal blood cells, leading to anemia and other symptoms. |  My uncle was diagnosed with leukemia last year.  我叔叔去年被诊断出患有白血病。| Synonyms: cancer of the blood, blood cancer | Antonyms: health, wellness | noun
