esthetically:/ɛsˈθɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 美学地；审美地；雅致地 |  In a way that relates to beauty or appearance.  It describes how something looks or feels from an artistic perspective. | The room was aesthetically pleasing, with soft lighting and tasteful artwork. 房间布置得很美观，灯光柔和，艺术品也很有品味。|  artistically, beautifully, tastefully | uglily, displeasingly | adverb
