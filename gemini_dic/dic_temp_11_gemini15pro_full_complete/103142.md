jerriest:/ˈdʒɛriɪst/
adjective
最破旧的；最简陋的；最劣质的
Superlative form of jerry-built: most jerry-built.  Meaning constructed cheaply and poorly, often with inferior materials.
The jerriest shack on the block was finally demolished.  街区上最破旧的小屋终于被拆除了。
Near Synonyms:flimsiest, shakiest, rickety, ramshackle
Antonyms: sturdiest, soundest, most robust
词性:形容词
