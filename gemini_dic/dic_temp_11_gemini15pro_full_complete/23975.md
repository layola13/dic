breath-taking:/ˈbreθˌteɪkɪŋ/| adj.| 令人叹为观止的；激动人心的|Extremely exciting, beautiful, or surprising.|The view from the mountaintop was breath-taking. 从山顶望下去的景色令人叹为观止。|近义词：stunning, awesome, amazing, spectacular|反义词：dull, boring, unimpressive|形容词
