infantilisms:/ˈɪnfənˌtɪlɪzəmz/
n.
幼稚的言行；幼稚病
The persistence of infantilisms into adulthood can be a sign of developmental issues.  成年后仍然持续的幼稚行为可能是发育问题的迹象。
His speech was marked by infantilisms, such as baby talk and pouting. 他的讲话带有幼稚的特征，例如婴儿语和撅嘴。
近义词：childishness, immaturity, puerility
反义词：maturity, sophistication
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From infantile + -ism.  Infantile comes from Late Latin infantilis, from Latin infans (infant).  -ism is a suffix forming nouns meaning a characteristic behavior or quality.
