nonrecalcitrancies:/ˌnɒnˌrɪkælˈsɪtrənsiz/
noun
不顽固，顺从
lack of recalcitrance; the state of being compliant or yielding.
The nonrecalcitrancies of the students made it easy for the teacher to implement new classroom rules. 学生们的顺从使得老师很容易实施新的课堂规则。
Near Synonyms: compliance, obedience, acquiescence, submissiveness, docility
Antonyms: recalcitrance, defiance, resistance, disobedience, rebelliousness
