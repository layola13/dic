noncolor:/ˈnɒnˌkʌlə(r)/ | n. | 非彩色;素色;消色 |A color that lacks hue and saturation, such as white, black, or gray.| The artist preferred to work with noncolors, focusing on the interplay of light and shadow. 艺术家更喜欢使用非彩色，专注于光影的相互作用。| synonym: achromatic color, neutral color | antonym: color, chromatic color | noun

noncolor:/ˈnɒnˌkʌlə(r)/ | adj. | 非彩色的;素色的;消色的 |Having no hue; achromatic. | She chose a noncolor palette for her living room, creating a sense of calm and spaciousness. 她为客厅选择了非彩色的调色板，营造出一种平静和宽敞的感觉。| synonym: achromatic, neutral | antonym: colored, chromatic | adjective
