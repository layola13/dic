fabricating:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
制造，装配；捏造，伪造
the act of constructing something (as a piece of machinery)
例句：The company is fabricating parts for airplanes.  这家公司正在制造飞机零件。

例句：He was accused of fabricating evidence. 他被指控伪造证据。

近义词：manufacturing, producing, constructing, inventing, forging, falsifying
反义词：dismantling, destroying, demolishing

词性：v.


fabricate:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪt/
verb
制造，装配；捏造，伪造
to make up for the purpose of deception
例句：He fabricated a story about being robbed. 他编造了一个关于被抢劫的故事。

例句：The evidence was fabricated to make him look guilty.  证据是伪造的，目的是让他看起来有罪。

近义词：manufacture, produce, construct, invent, forge, falsify
反义词：dismantle, destroy, demolish

词性：v.


词源：From Latin *fabricātus*, past participle of *fabricāre* (“to build, construct, fashion, frame, forge”), from *fabrica* (“workshop, something skillfully produced”), from *faber* (“workman, craftsman, smith”).
