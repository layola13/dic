athwart:/əˈθwɔːrt/| prep. adv.|横跨，穿过，阻碍，反对|Preventing or hindering; contrary to one's wishes. |His plans ran athwart those of his superiors. 他的计划与上司的计划相冲突。|against, counter to, in opposition to|in accordance with, supporting|preposition, adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *on þweorh, from on "on" + þweorh "across, perverse, angry".  Related to "thwart".
