imager:/ˈɪmɪdʒər/ | n. | 成像器;  显像管;  描绘者;  想象者 | A device or person that produces images. |  The Hubble Space Telescope is a powerful imager that has captured stunning images of distant galaxies. 哈勃太空望远镜是一个强大的成像器，捕捉到了遥远星系的惊人图像。 | The artist was a skilled imager, able to create vivid pictures in the minds of his viewers.  这位艺术家是一位技艺精湛的描绘者，能够在观众心中创造生动的画面。 |  synonyms:  imaging device, scanner, visualizer, artist, painter, illustrator, depicter | antonyms:  None. | noun
