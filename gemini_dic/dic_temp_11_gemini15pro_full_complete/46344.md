dactyl: /ˈdæktɪl/ | n. | 指节，扬抑抑格 | A metrical foot consisting of one stressed syllable followed by two unstressed syllables. | Example: The word "poetry" is a dactyl.  “poetry”这个单词就是一个扬抑抑格。 | Example: Dactylic hexameter is a common meter in Greek and Latin epic poetry. 扬抑抑格六音步是希腊和拉丁史诗中常见的格律。 | Synonym:  finger, toe (in a broader sense) | Antonym: N/A | noun

dactylic: /dækˈtɪlɪk/ | adj. | 扬抑抑格的，指节的 |  Relating to or composed of dactyls. | Example:  The poem was written in dactylic hexameter. 这首诗是用扬抑抑格六音步写成的。 | Example:  The dactylic rhythm gives the poem a lilting quality. 扬抑抑格的节奏赋予了这首诗一种轻快的品质。| Synonym: N/A | Antonym: N/A | adjective
