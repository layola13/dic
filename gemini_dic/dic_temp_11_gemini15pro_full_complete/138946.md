nuttish:/ˈnʌtɪʃ/ | adj. | 疯狂的，古怪的；略带坚果味的 | Slightly crazy or eccentric; having a slightly nutty flavor. |  The old woman had a nuttish habit of collecting bottle caps. 这位老妇人有个古怪的习惯，喜欢收集瓶盖。 |  He made a nuttish suggestion about climbing the mountain in the dark. 他提出了一个疯狂的建议，要在黑暗中爬山。 | 近义词：crazy, eccentric, wacky, nutty, nutty, daft | 反义词：sane, sensible, rational | 词性:形容词

词源: From nut + -ish.  The sense of "crazy" developed from the earlier sense of "intoxicated," as if from eating too many nuts.
