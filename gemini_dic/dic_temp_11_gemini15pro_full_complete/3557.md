agamically:/ˌeɪˈɡæmɪkli/
adverb|无性地，无配子地|Without the involvement of gametes (sex cells) in reproduction.|Aphids reproduce agamically during the summer months. |蚜虫在夏季进行无性繁殖。|asexually, vegetatively|sexually
adverb|副词|无性地|
agamogenesis:/ˌeɪˌɡæmoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun|无性生殖|Reproduction without the fusion of gametes.|Agamogenesis is a common reproductive strategy in many invertebrates. |无性生殖是许多无脊椎动物常见的繁殖策略。|asexual reproduction|sexual reproduction
