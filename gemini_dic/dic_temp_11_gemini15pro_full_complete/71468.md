flamers:/ˈfleɪmərz/| n.|喷火器;火焰喷射器;煽动者|A person or thing that flames, especially a device that produces a stream of flame.  Also, a person who posts inflammatory messages online.|The soldiers used flamers to clear the bunkers. 士兵们使用喷火器清除掩体。|近义词：incendiaries, firebugs, agitators, provocateurs|反义词:peacemakers, mediators|noun
