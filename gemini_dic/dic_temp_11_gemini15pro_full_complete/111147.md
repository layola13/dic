lithe:/laɪð/| adj.|轻盈的，柔软的，灵活的| (of a person or their body) thin, supple, and graceful.|例句：The dancers were all lithe and elegant. 舞蹈演员们都轻盈优雅。

例句：The cat moved with a lithe grace. 猫咪的动作轻盈优雅。

近义词：supple, flexible, agile, nimble, graceful|反义词：stiff, rigid, clumsy, awkward|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *liðe "gentle, mild, meek," from Proto-Germanic *linthjaz "soft, yielding" (source also of Old Norse liðr "yielding, lenient," Gothic leiths "soft"). The meaning shifted in Middle English to "flexible, pliant," then to "agile, active" (early 15c.).
