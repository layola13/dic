blackwork:/ˈblækˌwɜːrk/| n.|黑色刺绣；黑色工作|A form of embroidery using black silk on white linen, typically with geometric patterns. Also, any work that is secret or sinister.|例句：She learned blackwork from her grandmother. 她从她祖母那里学了黑色刺绣。

例句：The government's involvement in blackwork operations has been exposed. 政府参与秘密行动的事情已经被曝光。|近义词：embroidery, needlework (for the craft); clandestine work, covert operations (for the secret work)|反义词：None (for the craft); overt operations, transparent work (for the secret work)|词性:noun
