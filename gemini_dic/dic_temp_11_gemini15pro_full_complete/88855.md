hoick:/hɔɪk/ | v. |猛拉；向上拽；（突然）抬起；提高（价格等） | To lift or pull something upwards suddenly and with effort. | He hoicked the heavy box onto the truck. 他把沉重的箱子猛地拽到了卡车上。

The landlord hoicked the rent up by 20%. 房东把租金一下子提高了20%。 | 近义词: jerk, yank, heave, boost, raise | 反义词: lower, drop | 词性: 动词
