carr:/kɑːr/ | n. | (沼泽的)低地, 湿地; (尤指)矿车; 姓氏卡尔 | A carr is a low-lying wetland, especially one that is peaty.  A carr can also refer to a cart used in mines, or the surname Carr. | The farmer drained the carr to create more pasture. 农民排干了沼泽地，以便创造更多的牧场。 | marsh, fen, bog, swamp |  (通常没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 upland - 高地) | noun

From Old Norse ǩǫrr, meaning "marsh," related to Danish kjær and Swedish kärr.
