nonencyclopedical:/ˌnɒnɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪkəl/
adjective
非百科全书式的
Not suitable for inclusion in an encyclopedia, typically because it is too specialized, trivial, or subjective.
This highly technical and nonencyclopedical information is not appropriate for a general audience. 这类高度技术性和非百科全书式的信息不适合普通读者。

近义词:unencyclopedic, specialized, niche, esoteric, trivial
反义词:encyclopedic, general, common, broad
