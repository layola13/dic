inveigh:/ɪnˈveɪ/
v.
痛斥，猛烈抨击
To attack verbally; denounce severely.
He inveighed against the government's policies. 他猛烈抨击政府的政策。
近义词: denounce, condemn, criticize, berate, lambaste
反义词: praise, commend, laud, extol
词性: 动词

invective:/ɪnˈvɛktɪv/
n.
辱骂，谩骂
Insulting, abusive, or highly critical language.
He hurled invective at the referee. 他对裁判破口大骂。
近义词: abuse, vituperation, diatribe, tirade, railing
反义词: praise, compliment, flattery
词性: 名词

词源:
From Latin *invectivus*, from *invectus*, past participle of *invehere* "to carry in, bring in, introduce," also "attack, assault," from *in-* "in, into, on, upon, against" + *vehere* "to carry."
