chairlift:/ˈtʃerˌlɪft/ | n. | 吊椅式索道，滑雪缆车 | A mechanical lift, usually consisting of a series of chairs suspended from a moving cable, used to transport skiers or sightseers up a mountain. |  We took the chairlift to the top of the mountain for a stunning view. 我们乘坐吊椅缆车到达山顶，欣赏壮丽的景色。

The chairlift offered breathtaking views of the valley below.  吊椅缆车提供了下面山谷的壮丽景色。


近义词：ski lift, gondola | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
