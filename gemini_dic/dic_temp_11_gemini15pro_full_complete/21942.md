bogging:/ˈbɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| v. |陷入泥沼;  停滞不前;  使陷入困境| Becoming stuck in mud or a similar soft substance; also, becoming stalled or slowed down. | The car got bogged down in the mud. 汽车陷入了泥潭。|  miring, stalling, hindering | advancing, expediting | verb

bogging:/ˈbɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| adj. | 令人沮丧的; 令人厌烦的 | Causing frustration or tedium, often due to slow progress or excessive complexity.  | The project became bogged down by bureaucratic procedures. 这个项目因官僚程序而陷入困境。| tedious, frustrating, cumbersome | exciting, stimulating | adjective
