ichthyography: /ˌɪkθiˈɒɡrəfi/
n.
鱼类志；鱼类图谱
A branch of zoology that deals with the description of fishes.
例句：The ichthyography of the region is still incomplete.  该地区的鱼类志仍未完成。

例句：He dedicated his life to the study of ichthyography. 他毕生致力于鱼类学研究。

近义词：fish study, piscatology
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, potentially "teratology" if focusing on the study of abnormalities)
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἰχθύς (ikhthús, “fish”) + -γραφία (-graphía, “writing, description”), from γράφω (gráphō, “I write”).
