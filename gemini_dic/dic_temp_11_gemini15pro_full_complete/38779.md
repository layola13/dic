conduciveness:/kənˈduːsɪvnəs/
n.
有利性；助长性；得宜
The quality of being conducive; the state of being helpful or favorable to something.
The quiet conduciveness of the library makes it a perfect place to study.  图书馆安静的环境使它成为学习的理想场所。
近义词: helpfulness, facilitation, suitability, advantageousness
反义词: unfavorableness, hindrance, obstruction, disadvantage
词性: noun

conducive:/kənˈduːsɪv/
adj.
有益的；有促进作用的；有助于…的
Making a certain situation or outcome likely or possible.
Regular exercise is conducive to good health. 经常锻炼有助于身体健康。
近义词: favorable, helpful, beneficial, advantageous
反义词: unfavorable, unhelpful, detrimental, disadvantageous
词性: adjective
