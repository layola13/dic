ferroso:/fɛˈroʊsoʊ/ /fɛˈrəʊsəʊ/
adjective
亚铁的；二价铁的
Containing or consisting of iron, especially in the divalent state.  Relating to or containing iron(II).
Iron(II) sulfate is a ferroso compound. 硫酸亚铁是一种亚铁化合物。
The solution turned deep blue, indicating the presence of ferroso ions. 溶液变成了深蓝色，表明存在亚铁离子。
近义词: ferrous
反义词: ferric
词性: adjective
