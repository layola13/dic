exoticists:/ɪɡˈzɑːtɪsɪsts/ | n. |异国情调爱好者；崇尚异国事物的人 | People who are fascinated by foreign cultures, customs, and products, often romanticizing or idealizing them. |  Many exoticists are drawn to the vibrant colors and intricate patterns of traditional Japanese clothing. 许多异国情调爱好者被日本传统服装鲜艳的色彩和复杂的图案所吸引。

|  The travel writer was accused of being an exoticist, portraying the local people as simplistic and naive.  这位旅行作家被指责为异国情调爱好者，把当地人描绘得过于简单和天真。 | 近义词:orientalist, romanticist | 反义词: nativist, localist | 词性:noun
