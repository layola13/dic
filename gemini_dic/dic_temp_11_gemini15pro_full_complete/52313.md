dickers:/ˈdɪkərz/| v.|讨价还价，争论；|To bargain or haggle, typically over a price or other terms of an agreement.|The two merchants dickered over the price of the rug. 两个商人就地毯的价格讨价还价。|近义词：bargain, haggle, negotiate|反义词:agree, concede|verb

dickers:/ˈdɪkərz/| n.|讨价还价，争论；(通常指琐碎的)交易|The act of bargaining or haggling, often over trivial matters.|After much dickering, they finally agreed on a price. 经过一番讨价还价，他们终于达成了价格协议。|近义词：bartering, negotiation, dealing|反义词:agreement, concession|noun
