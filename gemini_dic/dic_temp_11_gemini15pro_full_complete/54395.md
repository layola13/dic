disimmure:/ˌdɪsɪˈmjʊər/
verb
释放，解除禁锢
to release from confinement, especially from a walled-up enclosure
The prisoner was disimmured after twenty years.  这名囚犯在被禁锢二十年后获释。
liberate, release, free, extricate
