insusceptive:/ˌɪnsəˈseptɪv/
adjective
无感觉的；不能接受的；缺乏感受力的
Not susceptible; incapable of receiving; unimpressionable.
He seemed insusceptive to the beauty of the music, his face remaining impassive throughout the concert. 他似乎对音乐之美无动于衷，整场音乐会他的表情都毫无变化。
insusceptible, unresponsive, impervious, unreceptive
susceptible, receptive, responsive, sensitive
词性: 形容词
