olfactory:/ɒlˈfæktəri/ (US) /ɔːlˈfæktəri/ (UK)| adj.|嗅觉的；与嗅觉相关的|Relating to the sense of smell.|Dogs have a highly developed olfactory sense. 狗的嗅觉非常灵敏.|Synonyms: smelling, nasal, aromatic|Antonyms: anosmic|adjective

olfaction:/ɒlˈfækʃən/ (US) /ɔːlˈfækʃən/ (UK)| n.|嗅觉|The action or capacity of smelling; the sense of smell.|The olfaction of dogs is far superior to that of humans. 狗的嗅觉远胜于人类.|Synonyms: sense of smell, smelling|Antonyms: anosmia|noun
