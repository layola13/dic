metastannate:/ˌmetəˈstæneɪt/ | n. |偏锡酸盐|A salt containing the anion SnO₃²⁻.  It is derived from metastannic acid. |Sodium metastannate is used in the preparation of ceramic glazes.  偏锡酸钠用于制备陶瓷釉料。|Synonyms: tin(IV) oxide salt,  stannate |Antonyms: N/A |Noun
