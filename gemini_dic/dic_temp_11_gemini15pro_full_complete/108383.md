law-worthy:/ˈlɔːˈwɜːrði/
adjective
值得立法的；适合入法的
Fit to be made or recognized as law; deserving of legal protection or regulation.
例句：The committee deemed the proposal law-worthy and recommended it to the legislature. 委员会认为该提案值得立法，并将其推荐给立法机关。

例句：His actions, while morally questionable, were not considered law-worthy. 他的行为虽然在道德上值得怀疑，但并不被认为适合入法。
近义词：legitimate, legal, justifiable
反义词：illegal, illicit, unlawful
词性: adjective
