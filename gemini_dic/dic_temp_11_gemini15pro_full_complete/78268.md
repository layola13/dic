germanism:/ˈdʒɜːrmənɪzəm/ | /ˈdʒɜːrmənɪzəm/ | n. | 德语用法；德国习俗；日耳曼主义 | A word, expression, or idiom borrowed from the German language or characteristic of German.  A custom or belief characteristic of Germany or German people.  Devotion to or preference for Germany or German customs. |  His writing is full of germanisms. 他的写作充满了德语用法。| synonyms: German expression, German idiom, Teutonism | antonyms: Anglicism, Gallicism | noun
