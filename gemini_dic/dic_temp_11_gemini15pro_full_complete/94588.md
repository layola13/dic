impractical:/ɪmˈpræktɪkəl/| adj.|不切实际的，不实用的|Not workable or not suitable to be put into effect; not sensible or realistic.|例句：It's impractical to expect everyone to agree.  期望每个人都同意是不切实际的。

例句：The architect's design was beautiful but impractical.  这位建筑师的设计很漂亮，但不实用。

近义词：unrealistic, infeasible, unworkable, impossible|反义词:practical, realistic, feasible, workable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + practical.  "Practical" comes from Late Latin practicus "active, practical," from Greek praktikos "fit for action," from prassein "to do, act, perform."
