heathenly:/ˈhiːðənli/| adj. |异教的，野蛮的，不敬神的|Characteristic of heathens; pagan; irreligious; uncivilized or barbaric.|  His heathenly behavior shocked the church elders. 他的异教徒行为震惊了教会长老。|The warriors engaged in a heathenly ritual before battle. 战士们在战斗前举行了异教仪式。|近义词：pagan, ungodly, barbaric, irreligious, impious|反义词：religious, pious, godly, devout|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *hethenly, from Old English *hæþenlīċ, equivalent to heathen +‎ -ly.
