christianite:/ˈkrɪstʃəˌnaɪt/ | n. | 基督教信徒;  菲利普石 |A zeolite mineral, a hydrated potassium calcium aluminosilicate, occurring typically as white crystals. Also, a member of a Christian sect.|Christianites believed in pacifism and communal living. 基督教信徒相信和平主义和集体生活。 The geologist identified the sample as christianite. 地质学家将该样本鉴定为菲利普石。 | 近义词: Christian, believer, adherent (for a person); phillipsite (for the mineral) | 反义词: nonbeliever, atheist (for a person);  (no direct antonym for the mineral) | 词性: noun
