bewitchment:/bɪˈwɪtʃmənt/| n. |迷惑，着迷，施魔法|The state of being bewitched; enchantment.  The act of bewitching someone or something.|She was under his complete bewitchment. 她完全被他迷住了。|Synonyms: enchantment, fascination, captivation, spell, charm|Antonyms: disenchantment, repulsion, aversion|noun

bewitchment:/bɪˈwɪtʃmənt/| n. |施魔法|The act of casting a spell on someone or something.|The alleged bewitchment of the villagers caused widespread panic.  据称村民被施了魔法，引起了 widespread 恐慌。|Synonyms: hex, curse, jinx|Antonyms: blessing, benediction|noun
