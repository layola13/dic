overlooks:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊks/ | v. | 忽视；俯瞰；忽略 | fails to notice or consider something; has a view of from above | The report overlooks the significant role of teachers.  这份报告忽略了教师的重要作用。 | synonyms: misses, neglects, ignores, disregards | antonyms: notices, observes, heeds, acknowledges | verb

overlooks:/ˈoʊvərˌlʊks/ | n. | 高处；可俯瞰之处 | a place from which one can look down on something else |  The hotel has stunning overlooks of the valley. 这家酒店可以俯瞰山谷，景色迷人。| synonyms: vantage points, viewpoints | antonyms: low points, valleys | noun
