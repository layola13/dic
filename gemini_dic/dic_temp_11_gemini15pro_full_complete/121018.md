miscomfort:/dɪsˈkʌmfərt/| n. |不适，不舒服；不安，担忧|A feeling of slight pain or physical uneasiness, or of mental or emotional distress.|例句：The tight shoes caused him considerable discomfort.  这双紧的鞋子让他感到很不舒服。

例句：She felt a growing discomfort about the situation. 她对这种情况越来越感到不安。|近义词：unease, distress, malaise, hardship|反义词:comfort, ease, well-being|词性:名词
