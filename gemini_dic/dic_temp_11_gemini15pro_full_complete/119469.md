micrometre:/ˈmaɪkrəʊmiːtər/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊˌmiːtər/ (US)| n. |微米；测微计|A unit of length equal to one millionth of a metre.|The diameter of a human hair is about 75 micrometres.|Synonyms: micron, micrometer|Antonyms: kilometer, metre|noun


micrometres:/ˈmaɪkrəʊmiːtəz/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊˌmiːtərz/ (US)| n. |微米；测微计（复数）|Plural form of micrometre, a unit of length equal to one millionth of a metre. |The particles are only a few micrometres in size.|Synonyms: microns, micrometers|Antonyms: kilometers, metres|noun
