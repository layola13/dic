granularly:/ˈɡrænjʊləli/| adv.| 颗粒状地，详细地 |Characterized by a high level of detail; finely detailed.  Breaking down a complex topic into smaller, more manageable components.|The data was analyzed granularly to identify specific trends. 数据被详细地分析以识别特定的趋势。|We need to look at this issue more granularly to understand its root causes. 我们需要更详细地研究这个问题才能了解其根本原因。|近义词：in detail, meticulously, thoroughly, precisely|反义词：generally, broadly, superficially|词性:副词

词源: From "granular" + "-ly." "Granular" comes from Latin "granulum," meaning "little grain."
