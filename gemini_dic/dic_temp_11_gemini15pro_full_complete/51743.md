devolutions:/ˌdevəˈluːʃənz/ | n. |移交；转让（权力、责任等）；归还；退化|  The devolution of power from central government to local authorities. 中央政府向地方当局的权力移交。 |  例句：The devolution of power to the regions has led to greater autonomy. 权力下放到地区导致了更大的自治权。

例句：The devolution of responsibilities to lower levels of management is essential for efficient operation. 将责任下放到较低级别的管理层对于有效运作至关重要。


近义词：transfer, delegation, return, deterioration | 反义词: evolution, centralization, progress | 词性: 名词
