microbreweries:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈbruːəriz/ | n. |小型啤酒厂；微型酿酒厂|Small-scale breweries that produce limited quantities of beer, typically for local consumption. | There are several excellent microbreweries in this town. 这个镇上有几家很棒的小型啤酒厂。|  Many microbreweries offer tours and tastings. 许多小型啤酒厂提供参观和品尝服务。|Synonyms: craft breweries, brewpubs| Antonyms: macrobreweries, large breweries | noun

Etymology:  "micro-" + "brewery". "Micro-" is a combining form meaning "small," from Greek mikros. "Brewery" comes from "brew" (Old English breowan) + "-ery" (a suffix denoting a place where something is made).
