词源:
From the Greek name Ἑλένη (Helenē), probably meaning "torch" or "bright one".  The Spanish form, Ella, likely evolved through the French Hélène.

用法说明:
Primarily used as a female given name.  It can also be used as a pronoun in some dialects, though this is less common.
