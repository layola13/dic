internalising:/ɪnˈtɜːnəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ɪnˈtɜːrnəlaɪzɪŋ/| adj.|内化的，内在化的|The process of making attitudes or behavior part of one's nature.  将态度或行为变成自身性格一部分的过程。|She had internalized her parents' values. 她已经内化了父母的价值观。|synonyms: incorporating, integrating, assimilating|antonyms: externalising, expressing outwardly|adjective

internalise:/ɪnˈtɜːnəlaɪz/ /ɪnˈtɜːrnəlaɪz/| v.|内化|Make (attitudes or behavior) part of one's nature. 将（态度或行为）变成自身性格的一部分。|He had internalized the racism of the society he grew up in. 他已经内化了他成长环境中的社会种族主义。|synonyms: incorporate, integrate, assimilate|antonyms: externalise, express outwardly|verb
