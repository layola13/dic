emeralds:/ˈemərəldz/
|n.|祖母绿，绿宝石|A precious stone of rich green color.|She wore a necklace of sparkling emeralds. 她戴着一条闪闪发光的祖母绿项链。|Synonyms: gems, jewels|Antonyms: worthless stones|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *esmeralde*, from Latin *smaragdus*, from Greek *smáragdos* (of Semitic origin).
