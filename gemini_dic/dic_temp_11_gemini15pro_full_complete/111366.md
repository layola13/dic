liverless:/ˈlɪvərlɪs/| adj.| 无胆量的，怯懦的，没有肝脏的| Lacking courage; cowardly.  Also literally, having no liver. | He was so liverless he couldn't even ask her out. 他太胆小了，甚至不敢约她出去。| The liverless soldier abandoned his post.  这个胆小的士兵放弃了他的岗位。|Synonyms: cowardly, timid, spineless, gutless|Antonyms: brave, courageous, bold| adjective
