carbon-dated:/ˈkɑːrbən deɪtɪd/ | adj. | 碳测定年代的；碳年代测定的 | Determined or identified by carbon dating. |  The artifact was carbon-dated to be over 2000 years old. 这件文物经碳测定年代超过2000年。 | Synonyms: age-determined, radiocarbon-dated | Antonyms: undated | adjective

carbon-date:/ˈkɑːrbən deɪt/ | v. | 碳定年法测定…的年代 | To determine the age of something using carbon dating. | The archaeologists carbon-dated the ancient scrolls. 考古学家用碳定年法测定了古代卷轴的年代。| Synonyms: radiocarbon date | Antonyms: N/A | verb
