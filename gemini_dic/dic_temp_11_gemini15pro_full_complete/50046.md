denouement:/ˌdeɪnuːˈmɑːn/ (US), /deɪˈnuːmɒ̃/ (UK)| n. |结局，收场|The denouement of the play was unexpected and left the audience stunned.  这出戏的结局出人意料，让观众震惊不已。|The novel's denouement was both satisfying and heartbreaking. 这部小说的结局既令人满意又令人心碎。|近义词：conclusion, resolution, outcome, climax|反义词: beginning, start, introduction|词性: noun

Etymology: From French *dénouement*, from *dénouer* "to untie, unravel," from *de-* "un-, out" + *nouer* "to knot, tie."
