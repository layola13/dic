cill:/sɪl/ | n. | 窗台，门槛 |A shelf or slab, especially one built into a wall below a window. | The cat sat on the cill, watching the birds. 猫坐在窗台上，看着鸟儿。| 近义词: windowsill, ledge, sill | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Middle English *sille*, *syll*, from Old English *syll* (“threshold, groundsill, base, foundation”), from Proto-Germanic *sulją* (“sill, threshold”), from Proto-Indo-European *swel- (“to swell”). Cognate with West Frisian *syl* (“sill”), Dutch *dorpel* (“threshold”), German *Schwelle* (“threshold”), Swedish *syll* (“sill, sleeper”).
