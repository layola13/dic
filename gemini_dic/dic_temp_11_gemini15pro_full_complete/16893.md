basins:/ˈbeɪsɪnz/| n.|盆地；流域；盆|A basin is a large or small, natural or artificial hollow containing water.  A basin can also refer to a region drained by a river.|The Amazon basin is the largest in the world. 亚马逊盆地是世界上最大的盆地。

The farmer washed his hands in the basin.  农民在盆里洗了手。|近义词：bowl, sink, valley, depression|反义词:peak, summit|词性:noun


词源: From Old French *bacin (Modern French bassin), diminutive of Old French bac "basin, tub", probably from Vulgar Latin *baccinum, from Latin bacca "water vessel".
