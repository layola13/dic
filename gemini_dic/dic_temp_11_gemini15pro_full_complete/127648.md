needs:/niːdz/| n.  v. (third-person singular simple present *needs*, present participle *needing*, simple past and past participle *needed*)|需要，需求|A condition or situation in which something is required or wanted.|例句：He needs a new car. 他需要一辆新车。|近义词：requirements, necessities|反义词：wants, desires|词性:名词, 动词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He needs a new phone. 他需要一部新手机。
She needs more time to finish the project. 她需要更多时间来完成这个项目。

He is needing more support right now. 他现在需要更多的支持。
