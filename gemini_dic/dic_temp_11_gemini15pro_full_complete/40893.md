cookshop:/ˈkʊkʃɒp/ (UK), /ˈkʊkʃɑːp/ (US)| n. |小餐馆，熟食店|A small restaurant or shop selling cooked food, especially hot takeaway meals.|例句：We grabbed a quick lunch at the local cookshop. 我们在当地的小餐馆随便吃了顿午饭。

例句：The cookshop offers a variety of delicious pies and pasties. 这家熟食店提供各种美味的馅饼和肉馅饼。

近义词：eatery, cafe, snack bar, deli |反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) restaurant, supermarket |词性: noun
