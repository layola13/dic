antihemorrhagic:/ˌæntiˌhɛmərˈædʒɪk/
antihemorrhagic| adj. n.|止血的，止血药|Preventing or arresting hemorrhage.|The doctor prescribed an antihemorrhagic medication to control the bleeding. 医生开了止血药来控制出血。|Synonyms: styptic, hemostatic|Antonyms: hemorrhagic|adjective
antihemorrhagic:/ˌæntiˌhɛmərˈædʒɪk/
antihemorrhagic| n.|止血药|A substance that prevents or arrests hemorrhage.|The patient was given an antihemorrhagic to stop the internal bleeding.  病人被给予了一种止血药来阻止内出血。|Synonyms: styptic, hemostatic|Antonyms:  coagulant (Note: while both affect bleeding, they work differently.  Anticoagulants *prevent* clotting, while antihemorrhagics *promote* clotting or stop bleeding through other means.)|noun
