iatrochemist:/aɪˌætroʊˈkɛmɪst/ | n. | 医药化学家；炼金术士医生 | A person who practices iatrochemistry, a historical approach to medicine that sought to understand and treat diseases through chemical principles, often involving alchemy. | Paracelsus was a famous iatrochemist who believed that chemical remedies could cure diseases.  帕拉塞尔苏斯是一位著名的医药化学家，他相信化学疗法可以治愈疾病。| synonym: alchemist physician,  medical chemist | antonym:  modern physician, allopathic physician | noun
