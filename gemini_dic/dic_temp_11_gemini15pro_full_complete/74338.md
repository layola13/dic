fragmentarily:/ˌfræɡmənˈtɛrɪli/
adv.
片段地，零碎地
in a fragmentary manner; incompletely.
The information was received fragmentarily, making it difficult to understand the whole picture.  这些信息是零碎接收到的，很难理解全局。
The witness could only remember the events fragmentarily due to the trauma. 由于受到创伤，证人只能零星地记起事件的经过。
近义词: incompletely, partially, piecemeal, bit by bit
反义词: completely, wholly, entirely
词性: 副词
