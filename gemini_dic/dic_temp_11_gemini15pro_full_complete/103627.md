joistless:/ˈdʒɔɪstləs/
adjective
无托梁的，无桁架的
lacking joists (horizontal supporting beams in a floor or ceiling)
The joistless concrete slab provided a smooth, uninterrupted surface.  无托梁的混凝土板提供了一个光滑、连续的表面。
The architect designed a joistless roof system.  建筑师设计了一个无托梁的屋顶系统。
Near synonyms: beamles, supportless
Antonyms: joisted, supported
词性: adjective
