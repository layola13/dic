come-hither:/kʌmˈhɪðər/
adjective
诱惑的；勾引人的；性感的
suggestive of sexual invitation; alluring.
She gave him a come-hither look. 她向他抛了个媚眼。
近义词: alluring, seductive, inviting, tempting, provocative
反义词: uninviting, repulsive, off-putting
词性: adjective

come-hither:/kʌmˈhɪðər/
noun
媚眼；诱惑的眼神
a look or expression that invites sexual interest.
Her come-hither was obvious to everyone in the room. 房间里每个人都注意到了她的媚眼。
近义词:  leer, ogling, flirtatious glance
反义词:  glare, frown
词性: noun


词源:  From the phrase "come hither," meaning "come here," which acquired sexual connotations over time.  The earliest recorded usage of "come-hither" in its suggestive sense is from the 17th century.
