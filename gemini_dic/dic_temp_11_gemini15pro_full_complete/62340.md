epidemiology:/ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 流行病学 | The study of the distribution and determinants of health-related states or events in specified populations, and the application of this study to the control of health problems. | Epidemiology plays a crucial role in understanding and controlling outbreaks of infectious diseases. 流行病学在理解和控制传染病爆发方面起着至关重要的作用。

Epidemiology helped identify the source of the cholera outbreak. 流行病学帮助确定了霍乱爆发的源头。| 近义词: study of disease, public health | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
