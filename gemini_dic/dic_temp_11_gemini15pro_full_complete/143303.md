outdriving: /ˌaʊtˈdraɪvɪŋ/
verb
胜过驾驶；在驾驶方面超过
surpassing someone in driving skill or distance covered.
He was outdriving everyone on the track. 他在赛道上超越了所有人。
近义词: outpacing, surpassing
反义词: lagging behind
词性: 动词

outdriving: /ˌaʊtˈdraɪvɪŋ/
noun
胜过驾驶；在驾驶方面超过（的行为）
the act of surpassing someone in driving skill or distance covered.
His outdriving of the competition was impressive. 他在驾驶方面胜过对手，令人印象深刻。
近义词: outpacing, surpassing
反义词: lagging behind
词性: 名词


词源: "out-" (prefix meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "driving" (present participle of the verb "drive")
