ebbing:/ˈɛbɪŋ/
n.
退潮；衰退；减少
The ebbing of the tide revealed a hidden sandbar. 退潮露出了一个隐藏的沙洲。
The ebbing of his enthusiasm made him reconsider his career choice.  他热情消退，使他重新考虑自己的职业选择。
Recession, decline, waning, fading
Flowing, rising, increasing
