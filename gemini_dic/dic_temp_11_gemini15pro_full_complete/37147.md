come-to-blows:/kʌm tə bləʊz/ | phrasal verb | 打起来；发生冲突；|To begin fighting physically.  The argument escalated until the two men came to blows.|例句：They argued for hours and finally came to blows. 他们争论了几个小时，最后打了起来。

例句：The two families came to blows over a property dispute.  两个家庭因为财产纠纷打了起来。|近义词：fight, brawl, clash, scrap |反义词：reconcile, make peace |词性：动词短语
