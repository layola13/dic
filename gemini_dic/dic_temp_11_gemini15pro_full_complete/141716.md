oracularness:/ɔːˈrækjʊlɚnɪs/ |n.|神谕性，玄妙，神秘; the quality of being oracular; |Her pronouncements had an air of oracularness that left her followers in awe. 她的声明带有一种神谕般的语气，让她的追随者敬畏不已。|近义词：mystery, enigma, profundity, obscurity|反义词：clarity, perspicuity, obviousness|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From oracular + -ness.  "Oracular" comes from the Latin "oraculum" (oracle), meaning a divine utterance or the place where such utterances were given.  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
