leer:/lɪr/
verb
斜视，送秋波，不怀好意地看
to look at someone or something with a sideways glance that shows you are amused or have unkind thoughts about them
He leered at her across the table. 他隔着桌子不怀好意地看着她。
近义词: ogle, smirk, leer, glare
反义词: frown, scowl
词性: verb

leer:/lɪr/
noun
斜视，送秋波，不怀好意的眼神
an unpleasant look or smile at someone that shows you are amused or have unkind thoughts about them
He gave her a suggestive leer. 他向她投以暗示性的斜视。
近义词: smirk, ogle, glare
反义词: frown, scowl
词性: noun
