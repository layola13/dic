The correct spelling is *grateful*, not *greatful*.

grateful: /ˈɡreɪtfəl/ | adj. | 感激的，感谢的 | Feeling or showing an appreciation of kindness; thankful. | Example: I am grateful for your help. 我很感激你的帮助。| Example: She was grateful to him for being so kind. 她很感激他如此友善。 | Synonyms: thankful, appreciative, indebted | Antonyms: ungrateful, unappreciative | Word Type: Adjective

被动语态 (Not applicable to "grateful" as it is an adjective, not a verb. You can use "appreciated" instead.)
His help was greatly appreciated. 他的帮助受到了极大的赞赏。
Her efforts are being appreciated by everyone. 她的努力得到了大家的赞赏。



疑问句 (Grateful itself is not typically used in questions, but you can ask questions related to gratefulness)
