dystopianism:/dɪsˈtoʊpiənɪzəm/
n.
反乌托邦主义，反乌托邦思想
An imagined state or society in which there is great suffering or injustice, typically one that is totalitarian or post-apocalyptic.  A philosophy or genre of fiction that explores such societies.
The novel explores themes of dystopianism and social control. 这部小说探讨了反乌托邦主义和社会控制的主题。
近义词：anti-utopianism,  cacotopianism
反义词：utopianism
词性：名词

dystopian:/dɪsˈtoʊpiən/
adj.
反乌托邦的，反面乌托邦的
Relating to or denoting an imagined place or state in which everything is unpleasant or bad, typically a totalitarian or environmentally degraded one.
The film presents a dystopian vision of the future. 这部电影展现了对未来的反乌托邦式想象。
近义词：anti-utopian, cacotopian
反义词：utopian
词性：形容词
