lubricating:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪtɪŋ/| adj. |润滑的，起润滑作用的|applying a lubricant to something; making something slippery or smooth.|The mechanic is lubricating the engine. 机械师正在给发动机润滑。|The lubricating oil reduced friction. 润滑油减少了摩擦。|近义词：oiling, greasing, slicking|反义词：drying, roughening|词性:形容词

词源: From the Latin "lubricare" meaning "to make smooth or slippery."
