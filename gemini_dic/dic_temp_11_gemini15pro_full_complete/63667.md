epanorthosis:/ˌɛpənɔːrˈθoʊsɪs/ /ˌiːpənɔːrˈθoʊsɪs/
n.
正字法，修辞格，立刻纠正，自我更正
A figure of speech in which a speaker corrects or clarifies something they have just said, often to strengthen or soften the statement.
"I hate you—no, I don't hate you, I pity you."  我恨你——不，我不恨你，我可怜你。
Synonyms: correction, amendment, rectification, revision
Antonyms: misstatement, error, inaccuracy
noun
