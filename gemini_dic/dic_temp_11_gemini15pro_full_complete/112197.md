loony: /ˈluːni/
noun
疯子；傻瓜
A crazy or eccentric person.
He's a bit of a loony, but he's harmless. 他有点疯，但无害。
Synonyms: lunatic, madman, crazy person, eccentric
Antonyms: sane person, rational person
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From loony (adj.), from lunatic + -y.  Ultimately from Latin *lunaticus, from luna (“moon”). The belief was that changes in the moon caused intermittent insanity.
