foresights:/ˈfɔːrsaɪts/| n. |远见，先见之明，预见| the ability to predict or the action of predicting what will happen or be needed in the future.|  The company's lack of foresight resulted in significant financial losses. 该公司缺乏远见导致了重大经济损失。|
The architect's foresight in planning for future expansion saved the company millions.  这位建筑师在规划未来扩张方面的远见为公司节省了数百万美元。| synonyms: vision, prudence, anticipation, precaution | antonyms: shortsightedness, myopia, imprudence | noun

foresight:/ˈfɔːrsaɪt/| n. |深谋远虑| careful thought and planning for the future |  She had the foresight to book a hotel room in advance. 她很有远见地提前预订了酒店房间。| He demonstrated remarkable foresight in anticipating the market crash. 他在预测市场崩盘方面展现了非凡的远见。| synonyms:  forethought, planning, prudence | antonyms:  recklessness, impulsiveness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forsight*, equivalent to fore- +‎ sight. Cognate with Dutch voorzicht (“foresight, caution”), German Vorsicht (“caution”), Swedish försikt (“foresight, caution”).
