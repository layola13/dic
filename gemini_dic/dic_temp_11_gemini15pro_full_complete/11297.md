Ardennes:/ɑːrˈdɛn/ | n. | 阿登高地；阿登森林 | A heavily wooded and hilly region extending across SE Belgium, NE France, and Luxembourg.  It has been the scene of several important battles, especially in World War I and World War II.  | The Battle of the Bulge was fought in the Ardennes.  阿登战役发生在阿登高地。 |  The Ardennes forest is beautiful in the autumn. 秋天的阿登森林很美。 | synonyms: highlands, forest, woods | antonyms: plains, lowlands | noun
