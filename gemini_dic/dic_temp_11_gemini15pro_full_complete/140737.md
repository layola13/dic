omnipresence:/ˌɒm.nɪˈprez.əns/ | n. |无所不在；普遍存在 |The state of being everywhere at once or seeming to be everywhere at once. | His omnipresence was both reassuring and a little unnerving. 他的无所不在既令人安心，又有点令人不安。| The internet has led to the omnipresence of information. 互联网导致了信息的无所不在。 | ubiquity, pervasiveness, universality | absence, rarity | noun

omnipresent:/ˌɒm.nɪˈprez.ənt/ | adj. | 无所不在的；普遍存在的 | Present everywhere at the same time. | God is considered by many to be omnipresent. 许多人认为上帝是无所不在的。 | The omnipresent threat of war hung over the country. 战争的威胁笼罩着全国。 | ubiquitous, pervasive, universal | absent, rare | adjective
