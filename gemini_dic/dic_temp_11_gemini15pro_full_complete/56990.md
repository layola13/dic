double-dealer:/ˈdʌbəl ˈdiːlər/ | n. | 两面派，口是心非的人，欺诈者 | A person who acts deceptively and pretends to be loyal to one person or group while secretly working against them or for another. | He was exposed as a double-dealer, selling secrets to both sides of the conflict. 他被揭露是一个两面派，把秘密卖给了冲突双方。

| Example:  The double-dealer pretended to be his friend, but he was secretly plotting against him.  这个两面派假装是他的朋友，却在暗中密谋陷害他。

| Example: She gained notoriety as a political double-dealer, shifting alliances to suit her own ambitions. 她作为一名政治两面派而臭名昭著，为了自己的野心而改变立场。

| Synonyms: hypocrite, two-timer, traitor, deceiver, backstabber | Antonyms: loyalist, honest person, trustworthy individual | Noun
