frenemy:/ˈfrɛnəmi/| n.|亦敌亦友；表面朋友|A person who pretends to be your friend but is secretly an enemy or rival.|She's my frenemy - we act like friends, but she's always trying to outdo me. 她是我亦敌亦友的人——我们表现得像朋友，但她总是试图胜过我。|Synonyms: rival, competitor, opponent, adversary|Antonyms: friend, ally, supporter|noun

frenemy:/ˈfrɛnəmi/| n.|假朋友；表面朋友|Someone who acts like a friend but is secretly not one; a combination of the words friend and enemy.|He's such a frenemy; he always compliments my work but then criticizes it behind my back. 他真是个假朋友；他总是当面称赞我的工作，然后在背后批评它。|Synonyms: backstabber, two-faced person, hypocrite|Antonyms: true friend, confidant, soulmate|noun
