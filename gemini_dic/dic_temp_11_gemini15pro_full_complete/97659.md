inhibitor:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtər/| n.|抑制剂，抑制物|A substance that slows down or prevents a particular chemical reaction or other process, or that reduces the activity of a particular enzyme.|The drug acted as an inhibitor, preventing the virus from replicating.|synonyms: suppressant, repressor, retarder|antonyms: activator, catalyst, stimulant|noun

inhibit:/ɪnˈhɪbɪt/| vt.|抑制，约束|To prevent something from happening or make it happen less frequently or less effectively.|Fear can inhibit speech.|synonyms: restrain, curb, hinder, impede|antonyms: encourage, promote, stimulate|verb
