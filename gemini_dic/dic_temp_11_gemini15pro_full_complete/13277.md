atheisticalness:/ˌeɪθiˈɪstɪkəlnəs/
n.
无神论;不信神
The quality or state of being atheistic.
His atheisticalness was evident in his refusal to attend church.  他的无神论在他拒绝去教堂的表现中很明显。
