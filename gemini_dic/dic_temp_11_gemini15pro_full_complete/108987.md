left-handednesses:/ˌleftˈhændɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
左撇子(的狀態或性質)
The state or quality of being left-handed.
Her left-handedness made it difficult for her to use certain tools. 她的左撇子使她难以使用某些工具。
left-handedness, sinistrality
