nonpungencies:/ˌnɒnpʌndʒənsiz/| n. |温和；不辛辣；不刺激|The nonpungency of the dish made it suitable for children.  这道菜的温和使其适合儿童食用。|例句：She appreciated the nonpungency of the mild salsa. 她很欣赏这种淡味莎莎酱的温和。

The chef deliberately chose ingredients known for their nonpungencies.  这位厨师特意选择了以温和著称的食材。|近义词：mildness, blandness, insipidity|反义词: pungency, spiciness, sharpness|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "pungency" (the quality of having a sharp, strong taste or smell).  "Pungency" comes from the Latin "pungere" (to prick, sting).
