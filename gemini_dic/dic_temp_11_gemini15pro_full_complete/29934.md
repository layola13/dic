cavalryman: /ˈkævəlrimən/ (US) /ˈkævəlrimən/ (UK)| n. |骑兵; 马兵|A soldier who serves in a cavalry unit, traditionally fighting on horseback.|例句：The cavalryman charged into battle. 骑兵冲锋陷阵。

例句：The cavalryman's horse was shot from under him.  这名骑兵的马被射中了，他从马上摔了下来。

近义词：trooper, horseman, rider|反义词: infantryman, foot soldier|词性: noun
