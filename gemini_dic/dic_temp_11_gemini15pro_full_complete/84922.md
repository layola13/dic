hansard:/ˈhænzɑːrd/ | n. |议会记录，议会辩论记录|An official verbatim record of debates in a legislature, especially that of the British, Canadian, Australian, New Zealand, or other Commonwealth parliaments. | Example 1:  The minister's speech can be found in Hansard. 部长的演讲可以在议会记录中找到。

Example 2: Researchers often consult Hansard to understand the historical context of legislation. 研究人员经常查阅议会记录以了解立法的历史背景。|Synonyms: transcript, record, minutes, proceedings |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fiction," "fabrication" in a loose sense) |noun
