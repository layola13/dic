codesigned:/ˌkoʊdˈsaɪnd/ | adj. | 共同签署的，代码签名的 |  Digitally signed by multiple parties, typically involving a developer and a certifying authority, to guarantee authenticity and integrity. This is common practice for software distributed on platforms like iOS and macOS. | This app is codesigned by Apple and the developer. 这个应用程序由苹果和开发者共同签名。| The driver is codesigned by Microsoft and the hardware manufacturer. 该驱动程序由微软和硬件制造商共同签名。 | signed, certified | unsigned, uncertified | adjective
