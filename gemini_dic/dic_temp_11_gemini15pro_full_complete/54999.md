dispurvey:/dɪs.pɚˈveɪ/
verb
取消供应，停止供应
To deprive of provisions; to dismantle or deprovision.
The army was dispurveyed after the war ended.  战争结束后，军队停止了供应。
The castle was dispurveyed when the siege ended. 围攻结束后，城堡的物资供应被取消了。
近义词: deplete, strip, divest
反义词: supply, provision, equip
词性: verb

Etymology:
From dis- + purvey.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of". "Purvey" means to provide or supply.
