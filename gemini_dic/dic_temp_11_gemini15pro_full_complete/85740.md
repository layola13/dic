hawking:/ˈhɔːkɪŋ/| n. v.|兜售，叫卖；沿街叫卖；咳出(痰)|The act of selling goods by going from place to place, especially by shouting.  Also, the act of clearing the throat noisily.|He made a living hawking goods on the street. 他靠沿街叫卖货物为生。|近义词：peddling, vending, selling|反义词:buying, purchasing|词性:noun, verb
hawking:/ˈhɔːkɪŋ/|v.|（指鸟）捕捉；放鹰狩猎；咳出（痰等）|To hunt with a hawk. Also, to force something out of the throat by coughing.|The falconer was hawking in the fields. 猎鹰者在田野里放鹰狩猎。|近义词：hunting, chasing, expectorating|反义词: - |词性:verb
