devoices:/dɪˈvɔɪsɪz/
verb
剥夺…的发言权; 使…失声
to deprive of a voice, especially to deprive of power, influence, or effectiveness
The new law effectively devoices marginalized communities. 这项新法律实际上剥夺了边缘化社区的发言权。
synonyms: silence, mute, suppress, stifle
antonyms: empower, enable, give voice to
词性: 动词


devoices:/dɪˈvɔɪsɪz/
verb
(语音学)使…成为清音
(phonetics) to make (a sound) voiceless
The /p/ sound in "spot" is devoiced because it follows the /s/ sound. “spot”中的/p/音是清音，因为它跟在/s/音后面。
synonyms: unvoice
antonyms: voice
词性: 动词
