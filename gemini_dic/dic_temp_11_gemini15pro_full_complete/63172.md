entailable:/ɪnˈteɪləbl/
|adj.|可必然包含的，可推断的|Something that is entailable is logically or necessarily implied or involved by something else.|His argument entails that we should increase taxes. 他的论点意味着我们应该增税。|Synonyms: inferable, deducible, implicit|Antonyms: unrelated, independent|词性: 形容词
