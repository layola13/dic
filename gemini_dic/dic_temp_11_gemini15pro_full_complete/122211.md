misvalues:/ˌmɪsˈvæljuːz/
verb
错误估价，低估，看轻
To assess or appreciate something incorrectly, usually by undervaluing it.
She misvalues her own abilities and constantly undersells herself. 她低估了自己的能力，总是贬低自己。
The company misvalued the property and ended up losing money on the deal.  公司错误估价了这处房产，最终在交易中亏损了。
underestimate, undervalue, misjudge, miscalculate
overestimate, overvalue, appreciate, prize
v.
