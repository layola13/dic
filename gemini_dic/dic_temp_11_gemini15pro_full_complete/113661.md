machinist:/məˈʃiːnɪst/| n.|机械师，机工|A person who operates or repairs machinery, especially metal-working machines.|The machinist carefully crafted the metal part. 机械师精心制作了金属零件。|Synonyms: mechanic, operator, craftsman, artisan|Antonyms: amateur, novice|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From machine + -ist.  "Machine" comes from Old French "machine," ultimately from Latin "machina" (contrivance, engine, military machine), from Greek "mēkhanē" (device, means).  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who practices or is skilled in something.
