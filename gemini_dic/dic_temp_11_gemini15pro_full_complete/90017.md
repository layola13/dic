hosanna:/hɒˈzænə/ /hoʊˈzænə/ | n. int. | 和散那；欢呼声；赞美诗 | An exclamation of praise, adoration, or joy. Originally a Hebrew word meaning "save, we pray," it became an expression of praise and triumph. |  The crowd shouted "Hosanna!" as Jesus entered Jerusalem. 群众在耶稣进入耶路撒冷时高呼“和散那！” |  The choir sang hosannas to the newborn king.  唱诗班向新生的国王唱赞美诗。 | 近义词: praise, acclaim, cheer | 反义词: condemnation, denunciation | 词性:名词/感叹词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hosanna, from Ancient Greek ὡσαννά (hōsanná), from Hebrew הוֹשִׁיעָה נָּא (hōšîʿâ-nāʾ, literally “save, we pray”).
