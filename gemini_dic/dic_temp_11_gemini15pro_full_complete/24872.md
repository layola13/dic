PHRASE->
browse through = 浏览；翻阅
browse around = 随便看看
browse the web/internet = 上网浏览
browse a shop/store = 逛商店
browse a catalog =  浏览目录


词源：From Old French *brouster (attested in the derivative broust “young shoots, fodder”), from a Romance verb derived from Gaulish *brustia “sprouts” (compare Old Irish brús “fragments, crumbs”).


注意事项:  美式和英式发音相同 /braʊz/ 和 /braʊzɪz/
