homogeneal:/ˌhɒməˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. |同质的，均匀的 | Of the same kind or nature; uniform in composition or structure. | The scientist prepared a homogeneal solution for the experiment. 科学家为实验准备了均匀的溶液。 |  The mixture was homogeneal, with no visible clumps or variations. 这种混合物是均匀的，没有可见的团块或变化。 | 近义词：uniform, homogeneous, consistent | 反义词：heterogeneous, diverse, varied | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Late Latin homogeneus, from Greek homogenēs, from homos "same" + genos "kind, race."
