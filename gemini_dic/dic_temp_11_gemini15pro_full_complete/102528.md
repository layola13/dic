jadedly:/ˈdʒeɪdɪdli/ | adv. | 厌倦地，疲惫不堪地 |  In a manner exhibiting weariness and lack of enthusiasm, typically as a result of overexposure or excess. | She looked at the endless paperwork jadedly. 她厌倦地看着没完没了的文书工作。| Synonym: wearily, tiredly, listlessly, apathetically | Antonym: enthusiastically, eagerly, excitedly | 词性: 副词

jaded:/ˈdʒeɪdɪd/ | adj. | 厌倦的，疲惫不堪的 |  Tired, bored, or lacking enthusiasm, typically after having too much of something. | He became jaded with city life. 他对城市生活感到厌倦。 | Synonym: weary, tired, bored, cynical | Antonym: enthusiastic, eager, excited, fresh | 词性: 形容词
