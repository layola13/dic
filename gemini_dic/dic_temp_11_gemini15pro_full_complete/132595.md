nondilapidated: /ˌnɒndɪˈlæpɪdeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未破旧的，保存完好的 | Not dilapidated; in good repair. | The nondilapidated house stood proudly among the ruins. 这座保存完好的房子傲立于废墟之中。 |  The car, though old, was surprisingly nondilapidated. 虽然这辆车很旧，但它保存得非常好。| 近义词: well-maintained, sound, intact, pristine | 反义词: dilapidated, ramshackle, broken-down, run-down | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "dilapidated" (from Latin *dilapidatus*, past participle of *dilapidare* "to scatter like stones," from *dis-* "apart" + *lapidare* "to throw stones," from *lapis* "stone").
