decollectivized:/diːkəˈlɛktɪvaɪzd/
Verb
去集体化（的）
Converted from collective ownership to private ownership.
例句：The farms were decollectivized in the 1990s.  20世纪90年代，农场实行了去集体化。

例句：The decollectivized land was distributed among the farmers. 去集体化的土地分给了农民。

近义词：privatized, individualized
反义词：collectivized, nationalized
词性：verb (past participle)


decollectivize:/diːkəˈlɛktɪvaɪz/
Verb
去集体化
To abolish collective ownership or control, especially of farms or businesses.
例句：The government decided to decollectivize agriculture. 政府决定去集体化农业。

例句：Many countries decollectivized their economies after the fall of the Soviet Union.  苏联解体后，许多国家对其经济进行了去集体化。

近义词：privatize, individualize
反义词：collectivize, nationalize
词性：verb

decollectivization:/diːkəˌlɛktɪvaɪˈzeɪʃən/
Noun
去集体化
The process of abolishing collective ownership or control.
例句：The decollectivization of agriculture led to increased productivity.  农业去集体化导致了生产力的提高。

例句：The decollectivization of the economy was a complex and challenging process. 经济去集体化是一个复杂而具有挑战性的过程。

近义词：privatization, individualization
反义词：collectivization, nationalization
词性：noun
