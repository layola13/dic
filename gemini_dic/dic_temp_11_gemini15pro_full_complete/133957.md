nonhemolytic:/ˌnɑːnˌhiːməˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
非溶血性的
Not causing hemolysis; not involving the rupture or destruction of red blood cells.
The doctor confirmed that the reaction was nonhemolytic. 医生确认该反应是非溶血性的。
The patient presented with nonhemolytic jaundice.  病人表现出非溶血性黄疸。
Synonyms:  non-haemolytic (British spelling),  anhemolytic
Antonyms: hemolytic
词性: 形容词
