methodical:/məˈθɒdɪk(ə)l/ |adj.| 有条理的；井然有序的；一丝不苟的 |A methodical approach is essential for complex projects.  对于复杂的项目，有条理的方法至关重要。|例句：He is a methodical worker, always planning his day carefully. 他是一个有条理的工作者，总是仔细地计划他的一天。|近义词：systematic, organized, orderly |反义词：disorganized, chaotic, haphazard|词性: 形容词

词源:  From method + -ical + -ness.  Ultimately from Greek μέθοδος (méthodos, “way of teaching or going”).
