compensate:/ˈkɒmpənseɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpenseɪt/ (US)| v. |补偿，赔偿|to give something to someone in exchange for something that has been lost or damaged or for some inconvenience |The company compensated its employees for the extra hours they worked.  公司补偿了员工加班的时间。|synonyms: reimburse, recompense, indemnify, make amends for|antonyms: penalize, fine, deprive, forfeit|verb

compensate:/ˈkɒmpənseɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpenseɪt/ (US)| v. |抵消，弥补|to reduce or counteract the bad effect of something|Her enthusiasm compensates for her lack of experience. 她的热情弥补了她经验的不足。|synonyms: offset, counterbalance, make up for|antonyms: aggravate, worsen, exacerbate|verb
