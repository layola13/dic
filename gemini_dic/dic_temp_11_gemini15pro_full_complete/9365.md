antiputrefactive:/ˌæntiˌpjuːtrɪˈfæktɪv/
| adj. | 防腐的，抗腐败的 | Preventing or inhibiting putrefaction; antiseptic. |  Embalming fluid is an antiputrefactive agent.  防腐液是一种防腐剂。 | synonyms: antiseptic, preservative, disinfectant | antonyms: putrefactive, decaying, rotting | adjective

antiputrefactive:/ˌæntiˌpjuːtrɪˈfæktɪv/
| n. | 防腐剂 | A substance that prevents or inhibits putrefaction. | The scientist developed a new antiputrefactive for food preservation. 科学家开发了一种新的食品防腐剂。 | synonyms: preservative, antiseptic, disinfectant | antonyms:  putrefacient | noun


Etymology:
anti- (prefix) : against, opposite of (from Greek)
putrefactive (adjective): relating to or causing putrefaction (from Latin putrefacere,  "to make rotten")
-ive (suffix): forming adjectives, indicating tendency or function
