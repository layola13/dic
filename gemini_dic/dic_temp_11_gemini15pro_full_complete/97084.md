infertile:/ɪnˈfɜːrtaɪl/ /ɪnˈfɝːtəl/| adj.|不育的，不肥沃的|Not fertile; incapable of producing offspring (of people, animals, or plants); not able to sustain crops (of land).|例句：The couple discovered they were infertile after years of trying to conceive. 这对夫妇在尝试怀孕多年后发现他们不育。

例句：The infertile soil yielded a poor harvest.  贫瘠的土壤收成很差。|近义词：barren, sterile, unproductive|反义词：fertile, productive, fecund|词性:形容词
