bequeathable:/bɪˈkwiːðəbl/
adjective
可遗赠的
Able to be left to someone in a will.
My grandmother's house is bequeathable. 我祖母的房子是可以遗赠的。
He ensured that all his assets were bequeathable. 他确保他所有的资产都是可遗赠的。
近义词: inheritable, devisable, testable
反义词: non-bequeathable


词性: adjective

词源: From bequeath + -able.  "Bequeath" comes from Old English *bi-cweðan "to say, speak, utter," from "be-" (intensive prefix) + "cweðan" (to say).  "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being."
