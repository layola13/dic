monotone:/ˈmɒnətəʊn/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnətəʊn/ (US)| adjective, noun |单调的，单音调的；单调，单音调|Lacking in variation in tone or pitch.  Characterized by a tedious sameness or repetitiveness. |The lecturer's monotone voice made it difficult to stay awake. 演讲者单调的声音让人昏昏欲睡。|Synonyms: tedious, dull, boring, monotonous, unvaried |Antonyms: varied, diverse, interesting, exciting |adjective

monotone:/ˈmɒnətəʊn/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnətəʊn/ (US)| noun |单调，单音调|A sound or utterance that is unchanging in pitch. A tedious sameness or repetitiveness. |The steady monotone of the engine lulled him to sleep.  引擎稳定的单调声音使他昏昏欲睡。|Synonyms: drone, sameness, tedium |Antonyms: variety, diversity |noun
