noncognition:/ˌnɒnkɒɡˈnɪʃən/ | n. | 非认知 | The aspects of mental functioning not related to cognitive processes, such as personality, motivation, and emotional regulation. |  Noncognitive skills like perseverance are crucial for success. 非认知技能，例如毅力，对成功至关重要。| synonyms: affective domain, non-intellectual aspects | antonyms: cognition, intellectual abilities | noun
