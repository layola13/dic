outmans:/ˈaʊtmənz/
n.
歹徒，暴徒
a group of bandits or lawless people.
例句：The outmans robbed the stagecoach. 歹徒抢劫了驿马车。

例句：The sheriff formed a posse to hunt down the outmans. 警长组成了一队人马去追捕歹徒。
近义词：bandits, robbers, criminals, thugs, outlaws
反义词：lawmen, police, officers
词性：名词


词源:
Likely a variant of "outmen," which is derived from "out" (outside the law) + "men."  Meaning men living outside the law.
