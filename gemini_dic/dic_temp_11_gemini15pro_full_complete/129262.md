nodding:/ˈnɒdɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈnɑːdɪŋ/ (US)| n. adj.|点头，点头示意|the act of moving your head up and down, especially to show agreement or understanding|She sealed the deal with a firm nodding of her head. 她坚定地点了点头，最终达成了协议。|近义词：bowing, bobbing|反义词: shaking (one's head)|noun

nodding:/ˈnɒdɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈnɑːdɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|点头的|inclined to nod, especially in agreement or drowsiness|He gave a nodding acknowledgement of her presence. 他点头示意注意到她了。|近义词：agreeable, consenting|反义词: dissenting|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hnottian, frequentative of hnigan "to bend, bow," from Proto-Germanic *hnuganą, from PIE *kney- "to bend the knee, bow."
