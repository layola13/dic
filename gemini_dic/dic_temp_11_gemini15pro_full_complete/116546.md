mati:/ˈmɑːtiː/
noun
伙伴，朋友; (新西兰) 毛利人传统的问候语
a close friend or companion; (New Zealand) a traditional Māori greeting.
例句：He's been my mati for years. 他是我多年的伙伴。

例句：The group of matis gathered for a traditional ceremony.  这群伙伴聚在一起举行传统仪式。

近义词：friend, pal, buddy, comrade, companion
反义词：enemy, foe

词性: n.
