nonteachablenesses:/ˌnɒnˈtiːtʃəbəlˌnɛsɪz/
n.
不可教性；无法被教导的状态
The state or quality of being unteachable; inability to be taught.
His nonteachableness frustrated the tutor. 他的不可教性使导师感到沮丧。
non-educability, indocility, unteachableness, intractability
teachability, docility
noun
