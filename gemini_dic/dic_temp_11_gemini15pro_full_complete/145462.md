overcivilly:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪvɪlli/| adv.| 过分文明地，过分礼貌地，过分客气地 |Excessively or overly civilly; in a manner that is too polite or refined, often to the point of seeming insincere or affected. |He thanked her overcivilly, making her suspect his sincerity. 他过分客气地感谢她，让她怀疑他的真诚。|  She overcivilly inquired about his health, even though she didn't care. 即使她不在乎，她还是过分客气地询问了他的健康状况。 | 近义词: excessively politely, too formally, overly courteously | 反义词: rudely, impolitely, discourteously | 词性: 副词
