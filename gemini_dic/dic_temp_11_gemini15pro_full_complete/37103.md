comblessness:/ˈkoʊmləsnəs/| n.|无梳子状态；缺乏梳理|The state of being without a comb or not having combed one's hair.  This word is rarely used and generally considered humorous or informal.|His comblessness was apparent in his tangled, unruly hair. 他的头发乱蓬蓬的，一看就知道没梳过头。|Near synonyms: unkemptness, disheveledness|Near antonyms: tidiness, neatness|noun
