noninflectional:/ˌnɒnɪnˈflɛkʃənəl/ | adj. | 非屈折的，非活用 | Not undergoing inflection; not changing form to express different grammatical categories such as tense, person, number, case, gender, mood, or voice. | English has many noninflectional words, like "but" or "since." | 近义词: invariable, uninflected | 反义词: inflectional | 词性: adjective
