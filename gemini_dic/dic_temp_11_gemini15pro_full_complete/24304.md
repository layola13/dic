brightish:/ˈbraɪtɪʃ/| adj.|稍亮的，有点亮的|Moderately bright; somewhat bright.  It describes something that is bright, but not intensely so.|The room was brightish, even with the curtains drawn. 房间即使拉上了窗帘也还是有点亮。|Synonyms: fairly bright, moderately bright, somewhat bright|Antonyms: dim, dark, dull|adjective
