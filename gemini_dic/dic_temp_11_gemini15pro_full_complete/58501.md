duelist: /ˈduːəlɪst/ | n. |决斗者；决斗家| A person who fights a duel. |  The duelist drew his sword and saluted his opponent. 决斗者拔出剑向他的对手致敬。| The two duelists faced each other at dawn.  两个决斗者在黎明时分面对面站着。 | synonyms: combatant, fighter, contender | antonyms: pacifist, mediator | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *duelliste*, from duel + -iste. Ultimately from Latin *duellum* (war, fight).
