intertrafficked:/ˌɪntərˈtræfɪkt/ | v. |非法交易；进行人口贩卖|The gang intertrafficked women and children across international borders.  该团伙跨境贩卖妇女和儿童。| The authorities discovered a network that intertrafficked endangered species. 当局发现了一个贩卖濒危物种的网络。| 近义词：trafficked, smuggled, dealt | 反义词：protected, rescued | 词性：动词

词源:  "inter-" (between) + "traffic" (trade, commerce).  The prefix "inter-" indicates activity happening *between* or *among* entities, in this case, nations or groups. The word "traffic" originally referred to trade and commerce, but has evolved to also include illegal trade.
