friendliest:/ˈfrɛndliɪst/ | adj. | 最友好的 |  The superlative form of "friendly," meaning exhibiting the greatest degree of kindness, warmth, and approachability. | She's the friendliest person I know. 她是我认识的最友好的人。|  The friendliest dog in the park greeted everyone with a wagging tail. 公园里最友好的狗摇着尾巴迎接每个人。 |  kindest, warmest, most welcoming, most amiable | unkindest, coldest, unwelcoming, hostile | adjective
