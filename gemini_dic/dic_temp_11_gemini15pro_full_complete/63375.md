entomostracous:/ˌɛntoʊˈmɑːstrəkəs/| adj.| 甲壳纲的；属于昆虫类的；水蚤类的|Relating to or denoting small crustaceans, such as daphnia and copepods, that belong to the subclass Entomostrata (now mostly superseded).  They are mostly found in freshwater and marine environments.|Entomostracous crustaceans are an important food source for many fish. 甲壳纲动物是许多鱼类的重要食物来源。|Synonyms: microcrustacean, minute crustacean|Antonyms: malacostracan (although not a perfect antonym, it represents a contrasting group of crustaceans)|adjective
