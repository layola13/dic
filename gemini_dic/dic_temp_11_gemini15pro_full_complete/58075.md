drony:/ˈdroʊni/| adj.| 单调乏味的，沉闷的；懒惰的；迟钝的 | lacking in liveliness or interest; monotonous and tedious; sluggish and dull | The professor's droning lectures made it difficult for the students to stay awake.  教授单调乏味的讲课让学生们很难保持清醒。 | The traffic noise was a constant drone outside the window. 窗外的交通噪音持续不断，令人烦躁。 | synonyms: monotonous, tedious, dull, boring, lifeless, listless, lethargic, sluggish  | antonyms: lively, interesting, exciting, stimulating, energetic | adjective

drony:/ˈdroʊni/| n.|雄蜂；懒汉；寄生虫 | a male bee, especially a honeybee, which does no work; a lazy person; a parasite| The drone of the bees could be heard from the hive. 可以听到蜂巢里雄蜂的嗡嗡声。| He was considered a drone by his colleagues because he never seemed to do any work.  他的同事认为他是个懒汉，因为他似乎从来不做任何工作。| synonyms: idler, loafer, parasite, leech | antonyms: worker, busybody | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *drān, *drǣn (“male bee”), from Proto-Germanic *drāną (“drone bee”). Cognate with Old Norse dróni (“drone, male bee”). Related to Old English drēam (“joy, music, mirth”). The sense of "monotonous sound" developed from the sound made by a drone bee.
