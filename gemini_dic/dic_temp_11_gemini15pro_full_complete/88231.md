hindlimb:/ˈhaɪndˌlɪm/| n.|后肢|The kangaroo uses its powerful hindlimbs for hopping. 袋鼠用它强壮的后肢跳跃。|例句：The dog injured its hindlimb in the accident. 这只狗在事故中伤了后腿。

The cheetah's long, slender hindlimbs allow it to run at incredible speeds. 猎豹细长的后肢使它能以惊人的速度奔跑。|近义词：hind leg, back leg|反义词：forelimb, front leg|词性:noun

Etymology: From hind + limb.  "Hind" comes from Old English "hindan," meaning "behind,"  and "limb" comes from Old English "lim," meaning "joint, limb."
