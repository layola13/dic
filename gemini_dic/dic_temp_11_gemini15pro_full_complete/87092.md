her-ladyship:/hɜːr ˈleɪdiʃɪp/ | n. | 夫人；贵妇人；（尤指）太太； | A respectful or ironic term of address or reference to a woman of title or high social standing.  It can be used genuinely or sarcastically. | Example 1:  Her ladyship will be joining us for dinner this evening.  夫人今晚将和我们一起共进晚餐。

Example 2:  "Her ladyship" decided she didn't want to speak to the press after all.  “那位夫人”最终决定不想与媒体讲话。（讽刺语气）| Synonyms:  lady, madam, noblewoman | Antonyms:  commoner | noun
