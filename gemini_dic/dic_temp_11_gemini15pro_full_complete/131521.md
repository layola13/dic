noncomedogenic:/ˌnɑːnkɑːmədoʊˈdʒɛnɪk/
|adj.|
非粉刺性的；不会引起粉刺的
|Not likely to block pores and cause comedones (acne).|
|This moisturizer is noncomedogenic and suitable for sensitive skin. 这款保湿霜是非粉刺性的，适合敏感肌肤。|
|This foundation is labeled noncomedogenic, so it shouldn't cause breakouts.  这款粉底被标记为非粉刺性，所以它不应该引起痘痘。|
|non-acnegenic|
|comedogenic|
|adjective|
