candida:/ˈkændɪdə/ | n. |念珠菌|A yeast-like fungus, often part of the normal flora of the mouth, skin, intestinal tract, and vagina, that can cause infections, such as thrush, in people with weakened immune systems.|Example: Candida albicans is a common cause of yeast infections. 白色念珠菌是酵母菌感染的常见原因。|近义词: yeast, fungus |反义词: None |词性: noun
