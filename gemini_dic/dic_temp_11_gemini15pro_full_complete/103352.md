jilt: /dʒɪlt/
jilt: v. | 抛弃，甩掉（恋人）| To suddenly reject or abandon a lover. | She jilted him at the altar.  她在婚礼上抛弃了他。| 近义词：abandon, desert, dump | 反义词：marry, commit to | 词性: 动词

jilt: /dʒɪlt/
jilt: n. | 抛弃恋人的人 | A person, especially a woman, who abandons a lover. | He was a jilt, leaving her for another woman. 他是个负心汉，为了另一个女人离开了她。| 近义词：heartbreaker, cheat | 反义词：lover, fiancé(e) | 词性: 名词
