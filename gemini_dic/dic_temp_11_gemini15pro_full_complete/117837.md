mellin:/ˈmelɪn/| n. |梅林变换|A type of integral transform particularly useful in number theory and the study of special functions.  It transforms a function of a real variable to a function of a complex variable.|The Mellin transform of this function reveals its analytic properties.|近义词:Laplace transform, Fourier transform|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: Named after the Finnish mathematician Hjalmar Mellin (1854-1933).
