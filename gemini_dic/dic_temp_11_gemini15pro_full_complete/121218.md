misemphasizes:/ˌmɪsˈɛmfəˌsaɪzɪz/
|v.|错误地强调；轻描淡写；|To emphasize something incorrectly, or to give it less emphasis than it deserves.|The report misemphasizes the role of local communities in the project.|近义词: downplay, understate, de-emphasize |反义词: overemphasize, exaggerate, overstate|词性:verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrong" or "bad") + emphasize.
