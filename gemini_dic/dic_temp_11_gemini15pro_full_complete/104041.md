julep:/ˈdʒuːləp/| n. |朱丽酒; 薄荷酒; 止咳糖浆|A sweet flavored drink, often containing alcohol and mint.  Originally a medicinal drink from Persia, made with rose water and sugar. Later versions often include bourbon or other spirits, mint, sugar, and crushed ice.|  I sipped a refreshing mint julep on the porch. 我在门廊上啜饮了一杯清爽的薄荷朱丽酒。|  She made a non-alcoholic julep for the children. 她为孩子们做了一杯不含酒精的朱丽酒。|synonyms:cocktail, drink, beverage|antonyms: N/A|noun
