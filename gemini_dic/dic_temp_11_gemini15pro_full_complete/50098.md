dentigerous:/dɛnˈtɪdʒərəs/
adjective
含牙的；生牙的
Containing teeth, or having erupted teeth.
A dentigerous cyst is a fluid-filled sac that forms around the crown of an unerupted tooth. 含牙囊肿是一种充满液体的囊肿，它在未萌出的牙齿牙冠周围形成。
Dentigerous cysts are most commonly associated with impacted third molars. 含牙囊肿最常与阻生的第三磨牙相关。
Synonym: tooth-bearing
Antonym: edentulous
词性: 形容词
