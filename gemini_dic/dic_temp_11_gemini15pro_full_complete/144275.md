outsourced:/ˈaʊtsɔːrst/ | adj. v. (past participle) | 外包的；外购的 | obtained (goods or a service) from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source. |  Many companies outsource their IT operations to specialized firms. 许多公司将他们的IT业务外包给专业公司。 | The customer service department was outsourced to India. 客户服务部门被外包到印度。 | 近义词: subcontracted, contracted out, offshored | 反义词: insourced | 词性: adjective/verb (past participle)

outsource:/ˈaʊtsɔːrs/ | v. | 外包；外购 | obtain (goods or a service) from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source. | The company outsources its manufacturing to a factory in China.  这家公司将其制造业务外包给中国的一家工厂。 | They decided to outsource the cleaning services. 他们决定将清洁服务外包出去。 | 近义词: subcontract, contract out, offshore | 反义词: insource | 词性: verb
