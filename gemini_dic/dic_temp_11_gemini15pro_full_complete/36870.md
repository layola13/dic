colorant:/ˈkʌlərənt/| n.|着色剂，染料|A substance that adds or changes the color of something.  A substance used to color something.|例句：This food contains no artificial colorants.  这种食物不含人工色素。

例句：The artist used a natural colorant derived from plants.  这位艺术家使用了一种从植物中提取的天然色素。|近义词：dye, pigment, tint, color, stain|反义词:bleach, decolorant|词性:noun
