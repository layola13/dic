neuroptera:/njʊəˈrɒptərə/ | n. | 脉翅目昆虫 | An order of insects having four net-veined wings, including lacewings, antlions, and dobsonflies.  They are characterized by their large, membranous wings with a network of veins. | Example: Lacewings are beneficial neuropterans that prey on aphids. 草蛉是有益的脉翅目昆虫，它们捕食蚜虫。

Example:  The antlion larva digs a pit in the sand to trap unsuspecting prey. 蚁蛉幼虫在沙子里挖坑来诱捕毫无戒心的猎物。 | 近义词: (No direct synonyms for the taxonomic order) | 反义词: (No antonyms for a taxonomic order) | 词性: noun
