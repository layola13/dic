cringingly:/ˈkrɪndʒɪŋli/| adv.|  令人尴尬地；难为情地；忸怩地 |In a way that makes you feel embarrassed or uncomfortable. |She cringingly recounted the embarrassing story. 她难为情地讲述了那个尴尬的故事。|例句：He cringingly apologized for his mistake. 他尴尬地为自己的错误道歉。|近义词：awkwardly, embarrassingly, sheepishly|反义词：confidently, boldly|词性：副词
词源: From cringe + -ingly.  Cringe comes from Old English *crincan "to bend, yield, fall (in battle)," from Proto-Germanic *kringaną "to bend, turn, twist"
