meenie:/ˈmiːni/| n.| 小人物；无足轻重的人；小鱼；小东西 (Often used in children's rhymes or games.)|A meenie is a small or insignificant person or thing, often used in children's rhymes or games like "Eeny, meeny, miny, moe".  It doesn't have a precise definition outside of this context.|例句：Eeny, meeny, miny, moe, catch a tiger by the toe.  点兵点将，点到谁，谁就是倒霉鬼。
