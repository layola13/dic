coloristically:/ˌkʌlərˈɪstɪkli/
adv.|在色彩方面地，用色彩地|Relating to or characterized by color, especially in a striking or unusual way; in terms of color. | The film was coloristically rich, with vibrant hues and deep shadows. 这部电影色彩丰富，色调鲜艳，阴影深邃。| synonym:chromatically| antonym:monochromatically
词性:副词

coloristically:/ˌkʌlərˈɪstɪkli/
adj.|色彩的|Relating to or characterized by color, especially in a striking or unusual way. | The painting is coloristically interesting, with unusual juxtapositions of bright and dark shades. 这幅画在色彩上很有趣，明亮和黑暗的色调并置得不同寻常。| synonym:colorful, vibrant| antonym:drab, dull
