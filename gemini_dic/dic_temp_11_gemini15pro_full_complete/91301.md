hydromedusa:/ˌhaɪdrəməˈduːsə/ | n. |水螅水母|A free-swimming, umbrella-shaped jellyfish stage in the life cycle of some hydrozoans.  It reproduces sexually, producing gametes that fuse to form a planula larva, which then settles and develops into a polyp colony.| The hydromedusa pulsed gently through the water. 水螅水母在水中轻轻地搏动。| The hydromedusa released its gametes into the ocean current. 水螅水母将其配子释放到洋流中。|Synonyms: jellyfish, medusa | Antonyms: polyp | Noun
