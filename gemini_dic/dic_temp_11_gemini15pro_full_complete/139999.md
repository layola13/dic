odyssey:/ˈɒdəsi/ (UK) /ˈɑːdəsi/ (US)| n.|奥德赛；漫长而充满冒险的旅程|A long journey full of adventures, especially one marked by many changes of fortune.  From the Greek poet Homer's epic poem about Odysseus's ten-year journey home after the Trojan War.|His career as a diplomat has been a long and eventful odyssey.|journey, adventure, quest, pilgrimage|stay, idleness|noun
