hirudo:/hɪˈruːdoʊ/ /haɪˈrudəʊ/
n.
水蛭;蚂蟥
A bloodsucking aquatic or terrestrial worm, typically having a sucker at each end.
Leeches, such as Hirudo medicinalis, have been used in medicine for centuries.  水蛭，比如医蛭，几个世纪以来一直被用于医学。
Leeches are sometimes used to treat blood clots. 水蛭有时被用来治疗血栓。
近义词：leech
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider predator as a functional opposite)
词性: noun
