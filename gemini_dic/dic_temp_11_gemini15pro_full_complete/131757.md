noncongregative:/ˌnɒnkɒŋˈɡrɛɡətɪv/
adjective
非聚集的，不群居的
Not congregating; not assembling into a group or crowd.
The noncongregative nature of the species makes them difficult to study.  这个物种不群居的特性使它们难以研究。
Solitary animals often exhibit noncongregative behavior. 独居动物通常表现出非群居行为。
近义词: solitary, dispersed, scattered, isolated
反义词: gregarious, social, congregative
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- (not) + congregative (tending to congregate), from Latin congregare (to flock together), from con- (together) + grex (flock).
