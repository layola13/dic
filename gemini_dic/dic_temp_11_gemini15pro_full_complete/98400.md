insolvable:/ɪnˈsɒlvəbl/ /ɪnˈsɑːlvəbl/ | adj. | 不能解决的，无法解决的 | Not capable of being solved; admitting of no solution. |  例句：The problem seemed insolvable at first.  这个问题起初似乎无法解决。| 近义词：unsolvable, impossible, hopeless | 反义词：solvable, resolvable, possible | 词性：形容词

insolvable:/ɪnˈsɒlvəbl/ /ɪnˈsɑːlvəbl/ | adj. | 无可救药的，棘手的 | (of a person or their behaviour) impossible to reform or improve. |  例句：He has an insolvable drinking problem. 他有无法解决的酗酒问题。 | 近义词：incurable, irredeemable, incorrigible | 反义词：redeemable, reformable | 词性：形容词


Etymology:
From in- (not) + solvable (capable of being solved), from solve (find a solution to), from Latin solvere (to loosen, release, solve).
