memorializer:/məˈmɔːriəˌlaɪzər/
n.|纪念者; 提交请愿书的人|A person who creates or submits a memorial, especially a written statement of facts, requests, or complaints. |The memorializer presented a petition to the city council. 提交请愿书的人向市议会提交了一份请愿书。|Synonyms: petitioner, applicant, supplicant |Antonyms: opponent, objector
n.|词性

memorialize:/məˈmɔːriəˌlaɪz/
v.|纪念，缅怀；将…永载史册|To preserve the memory of someone or something, as in a ceremony or a written record. |They memorialized the fallen soldiers with a monument.  他们为阵亡的士兵建造了一座纪念碑以示纪念。|Synonyms: commemorate, remember, honor |Antonyms: forget, ignore, dishonor
v.|词性

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Late Latin *memorializare*, from Latin *memorialis* ("of memory"), from *memor* ("mindful").
