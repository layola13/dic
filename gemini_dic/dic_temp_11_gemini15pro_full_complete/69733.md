feracity:/fəˈræsɪti/
noun
凶猛;野性;勇猛;果敢;顽强
The quality of being fierce, or the state of being ferocious.
The feracity of the lion is legendary. 狮子的凶猛是传奇。
Her feracity in the courtroom was admirable.  她在法庭上的顽强令人钦佩。

synonyms: fierceness, ferociousness, aggressiveness, boldness, courage, tenacity, persistence
antonyms: timidity, meekness, cowardice, gentleness, passivity
