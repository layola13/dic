co-inherent:/ˌkoʊɪnˈhɪərənt/
adj.
共同固有的；内在关联的
Existing together and inseparable.  Two qualities or things are co-inherent if they cannot exist independently of each other.
例句：Freedom and responsibility are co-inherent. 自由和责任是内在关联的。

例句：Rights and duties are often seen as co-inherent. 权利和义务通常被认为是共同存在的。

近义词：intertwined, inseparable, inherent, intrinsic
反义词：separate, independent, extrinsic
词性：adjective

词源：
From co- + inherent.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "together" or "jointly." "Inherent" comes from the Latin "inhaerēre," meaning "to stick in" or "to be implanted."
