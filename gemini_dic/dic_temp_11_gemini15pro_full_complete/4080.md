ailan:/ˈeɪlən/ | n. | 臭椿 | A fast-growing, deciduous tree (Ailanthus altissima) native to China, but widely naturalized elsewhere, often considered invasive due to its suckering habit. It has pinnate leaves with a strong, unpleasant odor. | The ailan spread quickly, taking over the abandoned lot. 臭椿迅速蔓延，占据了废弃的空地。 | Example: The ailan trees grew tall and blocked the view. 这些臭椿树长得又高又挡住了视线。 | Synonyms: tree of heaven, Chinese sumac | Antonyms: N/A | noun
