foamy:/ˈfoʊmi/ (US) /ˈfəʊmi/ (UK)| adj.|泡沫的；起泡沫的|Covered in or resembling foam; frothy.|The beer had a thick, foamy head. 这啤酒上面有一层厚厚的泡沫。|近义词：frothy, bubbly, lathery|反义词:flat, still|词性:形容词
Etymology: From *foam* + *-y*.  *Foam* comes from Old English *fām*, from Proto-Germanic *faimaz*, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)пойmə-* (foam, froth). The suffix *-y* is a common adjective-forming suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
