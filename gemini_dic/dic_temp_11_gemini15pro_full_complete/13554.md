attemptive: /əˈtɛmptɪv/ | adj. | 企图的，试图的 | Making an attempt; trying. Characterized by or showing inclination to attempt something.  |  He made an attemptive smile, but it didn't quite reach his eyes. 他试图微笑，但并没有真正发自内心。 | 近义词: trying, endeavoring | 反义词: passive, inactive | 词性: 形容词

词源: From attempt + -ive.  "attempt" 源自拉丁语 "attentāre"，意为 "to try".  "-ive" 是一个形容词后缀，表示“有…倾向的；…的”。
