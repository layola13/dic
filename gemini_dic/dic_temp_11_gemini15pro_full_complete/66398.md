executors:/ˈɛksɪˌkjutərz/| n.|执行者，遗嘱执行人|People appointed to carry out the terms of a will. |The executors of his estate are selling his house. 他的遗产执行人正在出售他的房子。|近义词：administrator, trustee, enforcer|反义词: N/A|Noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *executour, from Anglo-Norman executur, Old French executeur (“executor”), and directly from Latin exsecūtor (“performer, executor”), agent noun from exsecūtus, past participle of exsequor (“I follow out, execute”).
