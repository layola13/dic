albedo:/ælˈbiːdoʊ/ | n. | 反照率 | The proportion of the incident light or radiation that is reflected by a surface, typically that of a planet or moon. | The fresh snow has a high albedo. 新鲜的雪有很高的反照率。 |  The desert sand has a low albedo. 沙漠的沙子反照率很低。 | synonyms: reflectivity, reflectance | antonyms: absorptivity, absorbance | noun
