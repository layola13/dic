aluminography:/əˌluːmɪˈnɒɡrəfi/
n.
铝版印刷术; 铝版印刷品
A process of printing from an aluminum plate.  It refers to both the process and the resulting prints.
Aluminography offers a unique aesthetic, different from traditional lithography. 铝版印刷提供了一种独特的审美，不同于传统的石版印刷。
Early 20th-century artists experimented with aluminography to achieve specific tonal effects.  20世纪初的艺术家尝试用铝版印刷来达到特定的色调效果。
近义词:lithography, metallography
反义词:(None -  No direct opposite for a specific printing process.)
词性:noun
