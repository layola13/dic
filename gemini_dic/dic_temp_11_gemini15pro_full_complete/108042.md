latch-onto:/ˈlætʃ ˈɒn tuː/ (US) /ˈlætʃ ˈɒn tuː/ (UK)| phrasal verb|抓住，理解，缠上|To grab hold of something, either physically or mentally.  To become attached to someone or something, often in a dependent or persistent way.|The child latched onto his mother's leg and refused to let go. 孩子紧紧抓住妈妈的腿，不肯放手。

The marketing team latched onto the new trend and developed a successful campaign. 营销团队抓住了新的趋势，并开展了一次成功的活动。|近义词：grasp, cling to, seize, understand, get, fixate on|反义词：release, let go, detach from|词性：动词短语
