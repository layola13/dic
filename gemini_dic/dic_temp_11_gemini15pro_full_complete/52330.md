dicotyledon: /ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ (UK) /ˌdaɪˌkɑːtɪˈliːdən/ (US)| n. |双子叶植物|A flowering plant with two embryonic seed leaves or cotyledons. Dicotyledons constitute the larger of the two great divisions of flowering plants.|例句：Roses and sunflowers are examples of dicotyledons. 玫瑰和向日葵是双子叶植物的例子。|例句：The dicotyledon's seed will split into two halves. 双子叶植物的种子会分裂成两半。|近义词：dicot|反义词: monocotyledon|词性: noun

dicotyledons: /ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdənz/ (UK) /ˌdaɪˌkɑːtɪˈliːdənz/ (US)| n. |双子叶植物|Plural form of dicotyledon, referring to multiple flowering plants with two embryonic seed leaves.|例句：Many common garden plants are dicotyledons. 许多常见的园林植物都是双子叶植物。|例句：Dicotyledons are characterized by their net-like veins in the leaves. 双子叶植物的特点是叶子上的网状叶脉。|近义词：dicots|反义词: monocotyledons|词性: noun
