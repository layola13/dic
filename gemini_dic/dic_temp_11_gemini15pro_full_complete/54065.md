diseased:/dɪˈziːzd/
adjective
患病的，有病的
Affected by a disease; ill.
The diseased tree had to be cut down.  这棵病树必须被砍掉。
diseased:/dɪˈziːzd/
adjective
病态的，不健康的 (比喻义)
Showing signs of moral or social decay.
He has a diseased mind. 他思想不正常。

近义词:sick, ill, ailing, unhealthy, morbid
反义词:healthy, well, sound

词性:adjective
