masculinity:/ˌmæskjʊˈlɪnɪti/
n.
男子气概，阳刚之气
Possessing qualities traditionally associated with men.
He felt pressure to prove his masculinity.  他感到有压力要证明自己的男子气概。
近义词：manliness, virility
反义词：femininity
词性：noun


masculinity:/ˌmæskjʊˈlɪnɪti/
n.
男性气质
The properties or qualities characteristic of being male; manliness.
Toxic masculinity can be harmful to both men and women.  有害的男性气质对男性和女性都有害。
近义词：maleness
反义词：femininity
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin masculinitas (“manhood”), from Latin masculinus (“male, masculine”), from masculus (“male”), diminutive of mas (“male”).
