nonpracticalnesses:/ˌnɒnpræktɪˈkælnəsɪz/
noun
不切实际之处；不实用性
the state or quality of being nonpractical; lack of practicality.
His nonpracticalnesses led to the project's failure. 他的不切实际导致了项目的失败。
Synonyms: impracticalities, unfeasibilities, uselessness, ineffectiveness
Antonyms: practicality, usefulness, effectiveness, feasibility
词性: 名词
