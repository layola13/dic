bonier:/ˈbəʊ.ni.ər/ | adj. | 更瘦骨嶙峋的 | Comparative adjective of bony: thinner, showing more bone structure. |  The old horse was looking much bonier after the harsh winter.  这匹老马在严冬过后看起来更加瘦骨嶙峋了。 |  The child's bonier face made her look older than she was.  孩子瘦削的脸让她看起来比实际年龄大。 | thinner, gaunt, skeletal, emaciated | fleshier, plumper, heavier | adjective

Etymology:
From bone + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bainam (bone).
