ill-treat:/ˌɪlˈtriːt/
verb
虐待，苛待
To treat someone cruelly or badly.
The animals were ill-treated by their owner. 这些动物受到主人的虐待。
He claimed he had been ill-treated by the police. 他声称他受到了警察的虐待。
mistreat, abuse, maltreat
