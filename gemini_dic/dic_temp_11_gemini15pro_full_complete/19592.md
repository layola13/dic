bigging:/ˈbɪɡɪŋ/
verb
吹嘘，夸大；使…显得重要；抬高身价
To exaggerate the importance of something; to make something seem bigger or more important than it actually is.
He was bigging up his role in the project.  他一直在夸大自己在这个项目中的作用。
She's always bigging herself up. 她总是自吹自擂。
boast, exaggerate, overstate, puff up
downplay, minimize, understate
v.
