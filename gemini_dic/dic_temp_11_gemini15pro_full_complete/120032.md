millionaires:/ˌmɪljəˈnɛərz/ | n. | 百万富翁（复数） | A person whose assets are worth at least one million units of a particular currency, such as dollars, euros, or pounds. |  Many millionaires live in this exclusive neighborhood. 许多百万富翁住在这个高档社区。| The company is owned by a group of foreign millionaires. 这家公司由一群外国百万富翁所有。 | synonyms: tycoon, magnate, plutocrat | antonyms: pauper, bankrupt | noun
