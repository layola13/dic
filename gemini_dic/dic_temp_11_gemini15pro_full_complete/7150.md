angelize:/ˈeɪndʒəlaɪz/ | v. |使像天使；使圣洁；净化；传播福音；宣扬基督教|To cause to resemble an angel; to purify; to convert to Christianity. |例句：The artist angelized the features of the Madonna in his painting.  这位艺术家在他的画作中使圣母玛利亚的容貌神圣化了。

例句：Missionaries traveled far and wide to angelize the indigenous people. 传教士们四处奔走，向土著居民传播福音。|近义词：purify, sanctify, evangelize, Christianize |反义词：corrupt, defile, demonize |词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *angelizare, from Latin angelus (“angel”) + -izare (equivalent to English -ize).
