outrage:/ˈaʊtreɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈaʊtreɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. vt.|愤怒，暴行；激怒|An act of extreme violence or viciousness; a feeling of righteous anger.|The murder of the child caused public outrage. 这起儿童谋杀案激起了公众的愤怒。|近义词：fury, indignation, wrath, resentment|反义词:calm, peace, pleasure|noun
outrage:/ˈaʊtreɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈaʊtreɪdʒ/ (UK)| vt.|激怒， outrage someone|To arouse intense anger, shock, or indignation in (someone).|The judge's decision outraged many people. 法官的判决激怒了很多人。|近义词：infuriate, enrage, incense|反义词:please, delight, soothe|verb
