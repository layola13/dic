advoyer:/ædˈvɔɪər/| n.| 送货员，运输代理人| A person who arranges for the transportation of goods.  This term is archaic and rarely used in modern English.  It originates from Anglo-Norman French. | The advoyer negotiated the safe passage of the merchant's goods through the dangerous mountain pass.  送货员通过危险的山路协商了商人货物安全通行。| 近义词: shipper, freight forwarder, carrier, transporter | 反义词: consignee, recipient | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Anglo-Norman French *advoier*, from Old French *advoer*, meaning "to direct towards".
