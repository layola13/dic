nonrefractivenesses:/ˌnɒnriːˈfræktɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
非折射性；不折射
The lack of a quality or capability of refracting light or other waves.
The nonrefractivenesses of the material made it unsuitable for lenses.  这种材料的非折射性使其不适合用于镜片。
Near synonyms:  opacity, nontransparency
Antonyms: refractiveness, transparency
noun
