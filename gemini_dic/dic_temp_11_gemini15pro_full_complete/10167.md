词源： From Late Latin *apophthegma, from Greek ἀπόφθεγμα (apóphtegma, “a terse, pointed saying”), from ἀποφθέγγεσθαι (apophthéngesthai, “to speak plainly”), from ἀπό (apó, “off, away from”) + φθέγγεσθαι (phthéngesthai, “to utter”).
