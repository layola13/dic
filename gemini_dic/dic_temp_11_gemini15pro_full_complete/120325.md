minify:/ˈmɪnɪfaɪ/| v. |使缩小，使简化|To reduce the size of (something, especially computer code or data) by eliminating unnecessary characters or other elements, without changing its functionality.|The developer minified the JavaScript file to improve website loading speed. 开发人员缩小了 JavaScript 文件以提高网站加载速度。|近义词:compress, condense, shrink, abridge, reduce|反义词:expand, enlarge, amplify, maximize|词性:动词

Etymology:
From mini- (small) + -fy (make or become).  The "-fy" suffix comes from the Latin facere "to make, do".
