butanoic:/bjuːˈtænoʊɪk/ | adj. | 丁酸的；酪酸的 | Relating to or derived from butane, especially by the addition of a carboxyl group.  It describes any substance containing or pertaining to butyric acid. | Butanoic acid has a strong, unpleasant odor. 丁酸有一种强烈难闻的气味。|  Butanoic acid is found in rancid butter.  丁酸存在于腐臭的黄油中。| 近义词: butyric | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From *butan-* (from *but-* (four) + *-ane* (suffix indicating an alkane)) + *-oic* (suffix indicating a carboxylic acid). Ultimately from Latin *butyrum* (butter) and Greek *-oeides* (resembling).
