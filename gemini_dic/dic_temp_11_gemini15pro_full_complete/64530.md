err:/ər/ |vi.|犯错，出错|To make a mistake; to be incorrect.| He erred in his calculations. 他计算时出错了。|Synonyms: mistake, blunder, slip, miscalculate|Antonyms: succeed, correct|Verb

err:/ɜːr/ |n.|错误，过失|A mistake or error.|  It was a minor err, but it had major consequences.  这是一个小错误，但后果很严重。|Synonyms: mistake, error, fault, blunder|Antonyms:  correctness, accuracy|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *errer*, from Latin *errare* "to wander, stray, roam, err, go wrong, mistake," related to *iter* "journey, road," from PIE root *ei-/*i- "to go."
