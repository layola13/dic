belied:/bɪˈlaɪd/
verb
掩饰；证明…为虚假；与…不符
To show to be false; contradict.
Her calm demeanor belied her inner turmoil. 她平静的举止掩饰了她内心的混乱。
Synonyms: contradict, mask, disguise
Antonyms: confirm, reveal, expose
词性: 动词
