conversationalists:/ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənəlɪsts/ /ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃənəlɪsts/
n.
健谈者，交谈者
People who enjoy and are skilled at engaging in conversations.
例句：The dinner party was a success, thanks to the lively conversationalists who kept the conversation flowing.  晚宴很成功，多亏了那些活跃的健谈者让谈话源源不断。

例句：She's one of the most engaging conversationalists I've ever met. 她是我见过的最迷人的健谈者之一。

近义词：talkers, communicators, speakers, chatters
反义词：introverts, recluses
词性：noun
