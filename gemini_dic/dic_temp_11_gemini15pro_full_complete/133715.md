nonfrosting:/ˌnɒnˈfrɒstɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈfrɔːstɪŋ/| adj.|不结霜的，防霜的|Not accumulating frost; resistant to frost formation.  This term describes something that either prevents frost from forming or doesn't accumulate frost itself.|This baker uses a special nonfrosting coating on their display cases.  这个面包师在他们的展示柜上使用了一种特殊的防霜涂层。|The nonfrosting windows are a great energy saver. 这些不结霜的窗户非常节能。|frost-resistant, frost-free|frosting|adjective
