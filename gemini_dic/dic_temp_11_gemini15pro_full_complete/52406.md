diddly:/ˈdɪdli/| adj. n.|微不足道的，少量的；琐碎的事物|A small or insignificant amount; something trivial or of little importance.|He doesn't have a diddly squat. 他什么也没有。|近义词：squat, nothing, zip, zilch|反义词:lots, much, a lot|词性：形容词，名词

词源:  Likely a playful alteration of "diddle," a word with various meanings including to cheat or swindle, and to waste time.  The reduplication reinforces the sense of smallness or insignificance.
