acanthopterygii:/ˌækænˌθɒptəˈrɪdʒiaɪ/
n.
棘鳍鱼纲
A large group of ray-finned fishes characterized by having bony, spiny rays in their fins.  This group includes most of the familiar bony fishes.
例句：Acanthopterygii are the most diverse group of fishes. 棘鳍鱼纲是最多样化的鱼类。

例句：The perch is a member of the Acanthopterygii. 河鲈是棘鳍鱼纲的成员。

近义词：bony fishes, teleosts
反义词: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ákantha* (thorn, spine) + *pterýgion* (little wing, fin).
