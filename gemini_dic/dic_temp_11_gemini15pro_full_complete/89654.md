hookwormy: /ˈhʊkwɜːrmi/ | adj. | 钩虫病的；似钩虫的；钩虫引起的；|Infested with or caused by hookworms; resembling a hookworm.  | The child's pale complexion and swollen belly suggested a hookwormy condition.  这个孩子脸色苍白，肚子肿胀，表明他可能感染了钩虫。 | The dog's hookwormy intestines were severely inflamed. 这只狗感染钩虫的肠子严重发炎。 | 近义词:  parasitized, infected | 反义词: healthy, uninfected | 词性: adjective


Etymology:  hook + worm + -y.  From "hookworm" (referring to the parasitic nematode) plus the adjectival suffix "-y."
