methenyl:/ˈmɛθənɪl/ | adj. | 甲炔的 |  Containing the methenyl group. |  Methenyl compounds are often unstable. 甲炔化合物通常不稳定。|  This reaction forms a methenyl bridge between the two molecules. 该反应在两个分子之间形成一个甲炔桥。 | 近义词: methine (less common)| 反义词: N/A | adjective
