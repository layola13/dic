impetrable:/ɪmˈpetrəbl/ | adj. | 不可穿透的；难以理解的；无动于衷的 | Not able to be penetrated, entered, or passed through; impossible to understand; not capable of being affected or moved. |  The fortress walls were impassable, offering an impenetrable defense against the enemy.  堡垒的城墙坚不可摧，为抵御敌人提供了不可逾越的防御。

The scientist's jargon was so impenetrable that even his colleagues struggled to understand him.  这位科学家的专业术语非常难懂，即使是他的同事也很难理解他。 | 近义词:impervious, impenetrable, unfathomable, inscrutable,  | 反义词:penetrable, permeable, understandable, comprehensible, susceptible | 词性:形容词
