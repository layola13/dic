caral:/ˈkærəl/| n.|古代城市，卡拉尔|Caral is an ancient city located in the Supe Valley in Peru. 卡拉尔是位于秘鲁苏佩山谷的一座古城。| Caral is considered one of the oldest civilizations in the Americas. 卡拉尔被认为是美洲最古老的文明之一。|近义词:city, ancient settlement|反义词:modern city|词性:名词

词源: The name "Caral" is thought to be derived from the Quechua word *qarall*, meaning "reed mat" or "reed basket," possibly referring to the materials used in the city's construction or the woven textiles produced there.

用法说明:
The word "caral" primarily refers to the specific archaeological site of Caral-Supe.  It is a proper noun and should be capitalized. It is not commonly used as a general term for ancient cities.
