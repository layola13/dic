cirrostrative:/ˌsɪroʊˈstreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 卷云状的，钩状云的 | Having the form of or resembling cirrus clouds; characterized by thin, wispy clouds. | The cirrostrative clouds painted the sunset sky. 卷云状的云彩描绘了日落的天空。 |  近义词: cirrous, wispy, feathery | 反义词: cumuliform, dense | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *cirrus* ("curl") + *-stratus* (layered).
