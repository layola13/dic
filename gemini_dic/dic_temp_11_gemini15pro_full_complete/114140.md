maharishi:/ˌmɑːhəˈrɪʃi/ | n. | 大师，圣人 | A great sage or spiritual leader, especially in Hinduism. From Sanskrit, mahā "great" + ṛṣi "sage". |  The Maharishi Mahesh Yogi popularized transcendental meditation in the West.  玛赫西·玛赫什·优济大师在西方推广了超觉静坐。| guru, sage, master, teacher | disciple, student | noun
