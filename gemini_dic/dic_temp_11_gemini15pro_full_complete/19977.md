biogas:/ˈbaɪoʊɡæs/| n. |沼气|A mixture of methane and carbon dioxide produced by the bacterial decomposition of organic matter and used as a fuel.|Biogas is a renewable energy source. 沼气是一种可再生能源。|Biogas is produced from anaerobic digestion of organic matter.  沼气是由有机物的厌氧消化产生的。|Synonyms: methane, natural gas|Antonyms: fossil fuel|noun
