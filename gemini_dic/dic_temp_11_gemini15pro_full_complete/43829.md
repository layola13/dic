creosol: /ˈkriːəsɒl/ (UK), /ˈkriːəsɔːl/ (US) | n. | 木馏油;甲酚;|A colorless, oily liquid with a smoky odor, obtained from wood tar and used as a disinfectant and antiseptic.  It is a methylphenol, isomeric with the cresols.| Creosol is a constituent of creosote. 木馏油是杂酚油的一种成分。| The creosol was used as a disinfectant.  木馏油被用作消毒剂。|Synonyms: methylguaiacol|Antonyms: None specifically.|Noun
