karmas:/ˈkɑːrməz/| n.|业力，因果报应|The concept of karmas suggests that actions have consequences.|例句：His bad karmas caught up with him. 他的恶报终于来了。|例句：She believes in the power of karmas. 她相信因果报应的力量。|近义词：fate, destiny, retribution, consequences|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 "grace", "forgiveness" 表示对业力的超越或解脱)|词性: 名词
