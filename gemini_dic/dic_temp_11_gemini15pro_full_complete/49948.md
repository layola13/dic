dendrologist:/dɛnˈdrɒlədʒɪst/
n.
树木学家
A scientist who studies trees, specifically their classification, growth, and other characteristics.
The dendrologist was able to identify the ancient oak by its unique bark pattern.  树木学家能够根据其独特的树皮纹理识别出这棵古老的橡树。
The dendrologist carefully examined the tree rings to determine its age. 树木学家仔细检查了树木的年轮来确定它的年龄。

近义词:
arborist, tree expert
反义词:
None

词性:
noun
