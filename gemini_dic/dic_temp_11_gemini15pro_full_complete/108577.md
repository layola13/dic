leachabilities:/ˌliːtʃəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |浸出性，沥滤性|The ability of a substance to be leached or dissolved out, especially by percolating water.|The leachabilities of heavy metals from the soil were tested. 从土壤中浸出重金属的能力进行了测试。|Synonyms: solubility, dissolvability|Antonyms: insolubility|noun
