focaccias:/foʊˈkɑːtʃəz/ /fəˈkɑːtʃəz/
noun
佛卡恰(一种意大利扁平面包)
A plural form of focaccia, a flat oven-baked Italian bread similar in style and texture to pizza doughs.  It may be topped with herbs, vegetables, or cheese.
We ordered a selection of focaccias with different toppings. 我们点了一些不同配料的佛卡恰。
The focaccias were served warm with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.  这些佛卡恰面包是温热的，配有橄榄油和香醋。
synonyms: flatbreads, Italian breads
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific type of bread)
