laid-over:/ˌleɪd ˈoʊvər/
adjective
中途停留的，延误的
Delayed or interrupted in a journey, especially by bad weather.
My flight was laid over in Chicago due to a blizzard. 我的航班因为暴风雪在芝加哥中途停留。
Synonyms: delayed, stranded, grounded
Antonyms: on-time, direct
词性: 形容词


laid-over:/ˌleɪd ˈoʊvər/
noun
中途停留
A temporary stop in a journey.
I had a long layover in Atlanta. 我在亚特兰大有一段很长的中途停留。
Synonyms: stopover, layover
Antonyms: direct flight, nonstop flight
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "lay over," meaning to make a temporary stop or stay. "Lay" in this sense comes from the Old English "lecgan," meaning "to cause to lie down."
