aplasia:/əˈpleɪʒə/ /eɪˈpleɪʒə/ | n. |发育不全，器官缺失|The absence or defective development of an organ or tissue. |例句：Aplasia of the thymus can lead to immune deficiency. 胸腺发育不全会导致免疫缺陷。

例句：Bone marrow aplasia can be a life-threatening condition. 骨髓发育不全可能危及生命。|近义词：hypoplasia, agenesis|反义词：hyperplasia|词性:名词


Etymology->
From Ancient Greek *ἀπλασία (aplasía)  "a not molding,"  from  ἀ- (a-)  "not" +  πλάσις (plásis) "a molding," from  πλάσσω (plássō)  "to mold, to form."
