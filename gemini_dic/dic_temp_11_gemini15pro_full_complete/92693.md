icebreakers:/ˈaɪsˌbreɪkərz/| n. |破冰游戏，破冰活动，破冰船|A game, activity, or device designed to relieve tension or formality, especially between strangers at a meeting or social gathering. Also refers to a ship designed to break through ice.|The facilitator started the workshop with a few icebreakers to get everyone comfortable.|近义词：warm-up, starter, energizer|反义词: N/A (No direct antonym)|Noun
