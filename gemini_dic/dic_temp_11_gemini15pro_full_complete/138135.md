northeastern:/ˌnɔːrθˈiːstərn/ |adj.|东北方的；来自东北方的|Situated in or coming from the northeast.|例句：She has a northeastern accent. 她操着东北口音。|例句：Northeastern winds brought heavy rain. 东北风带来了强降雨。|近义词：north-eastern|反义词：southwestern|词性：形容词

词源:  From north + eastern.  The word "north" traces back to Proto-Germanic *norþ, meaning "north," and ultimately to Proto-Indo-European *ner-, meaning "left, below." "Eastern" comes from Old English ēastern, meaning "eastern," related to ēast, "east."
