mealworms:/ˈmiːlwɜːrmz/ (US), /ˈmiːlwɜːmz/ (UK)| n. |黄粉虫|The larval form of the darkling beetle, often used as food for pets such as reptiles and birds, and increasingly for human consumption.|例句：I feed my lizard mealworms every other day. 我每隔一天喂我的蜥蜴黄粉虫。

例句：Mealworms are a good source of protein. 黄粉虫是良好的蛋白质来源。
