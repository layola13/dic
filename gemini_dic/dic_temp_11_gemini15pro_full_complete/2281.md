adenoids:/ˈædəˌnɔɪdz/| n. |腺样体|A mass of lymphoid tissue behind the nasal passages.  They are part of the immune system and help to fight infection, but can sometimes become enlarged and cause breathing problems.|The doctor recommended removing the child's adenoids. 医生建议切除孩子的腺样体。|Synonyms: pharyngeal tonsils|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but the absence of adenoids could be considered)|noun

adenoid:/ˈædəˌnɔɪd/| adj. |腺样体的|Relating to or resembling adenoids; also, (of a person's face) having a dull expression and partly open mouth, typically because of enlarged adenoids.|He had an adenoidal face and a nasal voice. 他面容呆滞，嘴巴半张，声音带鼻音。|Synonyms:  none|Antonyms:  none|adjective
