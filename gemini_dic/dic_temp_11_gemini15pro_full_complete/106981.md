litmus:/ˈlɪtməs/| n.|石蕊|A dye extracted from certain lichens that changes color according to the pH of the solution it is in, used as an indicator of acidity or alkalinity.|The scientist used litmus paper to test the acidity of the solution. 科学家用石蕊试纸测试溶液的酸度。|Synonyms: indicator, reagent|Antonyms: N/A|noun
