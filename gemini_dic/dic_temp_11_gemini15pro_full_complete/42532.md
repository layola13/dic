countermanifesto:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænɪˈfɛstoʊ/ | n. | 反宣言，反对声明 | A public declaration, usually in writing, that opposes or challenges the principles or claims of a previous manifesto. |  Example 1: The artist released a countermanifesto rejecting the traditional values of the art world. 这位艺术家发表了一份反宣言，反对艺术界的传统价值观。

Example 2: The political group issued a countermanifesto criticizing the government's new policies.  这个政治团体发表了一份反宣言，批评政府的新政策。 | Synonyms: rebuttal, refutation, denunciation | Antonyms: manifesto, declaration, proclamation | noun
