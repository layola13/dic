hyperemotively:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊtɪvli/
| adv. |过度情绪化地；极度情绪化地|In a manner characterized by excessive emotion or emotional expression.|She reacted hyperemotively to the news, bursting into tears and shouting. 她对这个消息反应过度情绪化，突然大哭大喊起来。|例句：He spoke hyperemotively about the loss of his childhood home. 他极其情绪激动地谈论着失去童年住所的事情。|近义词：overly emotionally, excessively emotionally, dramatically|反义词：calmly, stoically, apathetically|词性:副词
词源:  "hyper-" (Greek: over, excessive) + "emotive" (from "emotion") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
