corrector: /kəˈrɛktər/ (US), /kəˈrektə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 校正者，修正者，纠正者|A person or thing that corrects something, especially a person who marks mistakes in students' work. |例句：The spelling corrector in my word processor caught several errors. 我的文字处理器中的拼写校正器发现了一些错误。|例句：He works as a proof corrector for a publishing house. 他在一家出版社担任校对员。|近义词：editor, proofreader, reviewer, reviser|反义词：corruptor, damager|词性:noun
