aromaticities:/ˌærəməˈtɪsɪtiz/| n. |芳香性（复数）|The quality or state of being aromatic, often referring to the stability and special reactivity of cyclic, planar molecules with delocalized pi electrons, typically obeying Hückel's rule.|Benzene exhibits high aromaticity due to its delocalized pi electrons. 苯由于其离域的π电子而表现出高芳香性。|The aromaticities of these compounds were determined by NMR spectroscopy. 这些化合物的芳香性通过核磁共振波谱测定。|synonyms: aroma, fragrance, odor, scent|antonyms: stench, stink, odorlessness|noun
