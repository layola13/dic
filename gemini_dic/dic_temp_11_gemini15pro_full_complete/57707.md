dray:/dreɪ/
verb
运送，搬运
to transport (goods) by dray
例句：The farmer drayed his produce to market.  农民用马车把农产品运到市场。

例句：The company drayed the heavy equipment to the construction site. 公司用货车将重型设备运送到建筑工地。

近义词：haul, cart, transport
反义词：N/A
词性：v.

dray:/dreɪ/
noun
运货马车；货车
a low, strong cart or wagon without sides, used for heavy loads
例句：The dray was pulled by two large horses. 这辆货车由两匹大马拉着。

例句：The dray was filled with sacks of grain. 这辆马车装满了粮袋。

近义词：cart, wagon, truck
反义词：N/A
词性：n.

词源：From Old English *dræge  (a drawing, dragging), from  dragan (to draw, drag).  Related to draw.
