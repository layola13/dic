carefullest:/ˈkɛərfələst/ | adj. | 最小心的，最谨慎的 |  Superlative form of careful, meaning exhibiting the highest degree of care and attention.  While grammatically correct, it's considered archaic and less common than "most careful." | He was the carefullest driver I'd ever seen, always checking his mirrors and signaling well in advance. 他是我见过的最谨慎的司机，总是检查后视镜，并且提前发出信号。|  近义词: most careful, cautious, meticulous, scrupulous | 反义词: careless, reckless, negligent | 词性: 形容词
