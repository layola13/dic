caramelises:/ˈkærəməlaɪzɪz/
verb
焦糖化
(especially of sugar) change in colour and flavour when heated.  Turns brown and gives off a sweet, nutty smell.
The sugar caramelises and turns golden brown. 糖焦糖化变成金棕色。
Synonyms: brown, caramelize, glaze
Antonyms: bleach, whiten
v.
