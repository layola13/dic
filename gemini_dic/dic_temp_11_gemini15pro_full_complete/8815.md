antiepileptic:/ˌæntiˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk/| adj. n.|抗癫痫的；抗癫痫药|Preventing or reducing the frequency or severity of epileptic seizures; a medication used to treat epilepsy.|She takes antiepileptic medication to control her seizures. 她服用抗癫痫药物来控制癫痫发作。|近义词：anticonvulsant|反义词:proconvulsant|词性:形容词, 名词
antiepileptic:/ˌæntiˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk/| n.|抗癫痫药|A medication used to treat epilepsy.|The doctor prescribed a new antiepileptic for her. 医生给她开了一种新的抗癫痫药。|近义词：anticonvulsant|反义词:proconvulsant|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + epileptic.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek. "Epileptic" comes from the Greek word "epileptikos," meaning "seized upon," referring to the seizures characteristic of epilepsy.
