furcated:/ˈfɜːrkeɪtɪd/ /ˈfɝːkeɪtɪd/| adj.|分叉的，叉状的|Divided into branches or forks; forked.|The furcated tongue of a snake. 蛇的分叉舌头。|Synonyms: forked, branched, bifurcated|Antonyms: undivided, single, unbranched|adjective

furcate:/ˈfɜːrkeɪt/ /ˈfɝːkeɪt/| v. adj.|分叉，叉状的|Divide into two branches or forks.|The river furcates into two smaller streams. 这条河流分叉成两条小溪。|Synonyms: fork, branch, bifurcate|Antonyms: converge, unite, join|verb, adjective
