minestrone:/ˌmɪnɪˈstrəʊni/ (UK), /ˌmɪnɪˈstroʊni/ (US)| n. |蔬菜浓汤|A thick Italian soup containing vegetables, pasta, and often beans or meat.|例句：I ordered a bowl of minestrone for lunch. 我午餐点了一碗蔬菜浓汤。

例句：The minestrone was hearty and flavorful. 这蔬菜浓汤既丰盛又美味。|近义词：vegetable soup, Italian soup|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of soup)|noun
