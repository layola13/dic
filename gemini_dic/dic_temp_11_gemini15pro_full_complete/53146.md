diplomatism:/dɪˈploʊmətɪzəm/
n.
外交手腕，外交技巧
The skill of managing international relations, typically by a country's representatives abroad.
He showed great diplomatism in handling the crisis. 他在处理危机时展现了高超的外交手腕。
diplomacy, tact, statesmanship
aggression, belligerence
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *diplomatisme*, from *diplomate* ("diplomat") + *-isme*.  Ultimately from Greek *δίπλωμα* (*diploma*, “folded document”).
