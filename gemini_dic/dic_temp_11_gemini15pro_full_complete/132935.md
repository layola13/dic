nonembellishing:/ˌnɒnɪmˈbɛlɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
未加修饰的; 不加渲染的
Not embellished or decorated; plain and simple.
The report provided a nonembellishing account of the events.  这份报告对事件进行了未加渲染的描述。
Her nonembellishing style of writing was refreshing. 她不加修饰的写作风格令人耳目一新。
近义词: unembellished, unadorned, plain, simple, austere
反义词: embellished, ornate, decorated, elaborate, flowery
词性: adjective
