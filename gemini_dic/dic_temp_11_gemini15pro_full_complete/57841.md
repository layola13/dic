dressinesses:/ˈdrɛsɪnəsɪz/
noun
讲究的程度；衣着的正式程度
The state or quality of being dressed up or formally attired.
The various dressinesses of the guests ranged from casual to black tie.  客人们的衣着正式程度各不相同，从休闲到正式都有。
Near Synonyms: formalities, smartness, fanciness
Antonyms: casualness, informality
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dressiness + -es (plural suffix).  Dressiness itself comes from dressy (meaning "smartly or showily dressed") + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Dressy ultimately derives from dress (verb) + -y (adjective suffix).
