moussing:/ˈmaʊsɪŋ/ | n. |起泡沫，冒泡 |The act of forming or producing a mousse; the state of being covered with or resembling mousse. |The bartender was moussing the milk for the cappuccino.  调酒师正在为卡布奇诺打奶泡。|Near synonyms: foaming, frothing, bubbling |Antonyms: flattening, deflating | Noun

moussing:/ˈmaʊsɪŋ/ | adj. |起泡沫的，冒泡的 | Covered with or resembling mousse. | The moussing waves crashed against the shore. 泛着泡沫的波浪拍打着海岸。 |Near synonyms: foamy, frothy, bubbly |Antonyms: flat, still | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "mousser" (to foam, froth), derived from "mousse" (foam, froth), which ultimately comes from Latin "mulsa" (mead, honeyed wine).
