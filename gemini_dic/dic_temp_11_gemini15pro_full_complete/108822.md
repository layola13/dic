leaved:/liːvd/ | adj. | 有…叶的；叶状的 | Having leaves of a specified kind or number. |  The tree is broad-leaved. 这棵树是阔叶的。 | The plant has finely leaved stems. 这种植物的茎长着细小的叶子。 | leafy | leafless | adjective

Etymology:
From Old English *lēafed, from Proto-Germanic *laubiz, from Proto-Indo-European *leubʰ-. Cognate with German belaubt.
