discobolus:/dɪˈskɒbələs/ | n. | 铁饼运动员，掷铁饼者 |  A statue or representation of a discus thrower, especially the famous one by Myron.  It depicts an athlete in the midst of throwing a discus. | The museum's most prized possession was a Roman copy of the Discobolus.  博物馆最珍贵的藏品是罗马复制的《掷铁饼者》。| The Discobolus is a remarkable example of captured motion in sculpture.  《掷铁饼者》是雕塑中捕捉运动的非凡例子。 | synonyms: discus thrower, athlete | antonyms: spectator, viewer | noun
