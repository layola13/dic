iridiums:/ɪˈrɪdiəmz/
n.
铱（复数）
a hard, brittle, silvery-white transition metal of the platinum group, iridium is the second-densest metal (after osmium). It is highly resistant to corrosion.
例句：Iridiums are used in spark plugs because of their high melting point. 铱因其高熔点而用于火花塞。

例句：The scientists found traces of iridiums in the rock layer. 科学家们在岩层中发现了铱的痕迹。
近义词:platinum (in some applications), osmium (in terms of density)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical element)
词性:noun
