bubbler:/ˈbʌblər/ (US) /ˈbʌblə(r)/ (UK)| n.|饮水器; 汽水; 含酒精饮料|A fountain that dispenses drinking water. Also refers to a device for making bubbles in a liquid, or a carbonated beverage.|例句：The kids crowded around the bubbler on a hot day. 孩子们在大热天里挤在饮水器周围。

例句：He bought a strawberry bubbler from the corner store. 他在街角商店买了一瓶草莓汽水。|近义词：drinking fountain, water fountain (for drinking water dispenser), soda, pop (for carbonated beverage)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


bubbler:/ˈbʌblər/ (US) /ˈbʌblə(r)/ (UK)| n.|爱说话的人; 滔滔不绝的人|A person who talks a lot; a chatterbox.|例句：She's such a bubbler, always telling stories. 她真是个爱说话的人，总是讲故事。

例句：He was known as a bubbler in the office, constantly chatting with colleagues. 他在办公室里被称为健谈者，经常和同事聊天。|近义词：chatterbox, talker|反义词:quiet person, introvert|词性:noun
