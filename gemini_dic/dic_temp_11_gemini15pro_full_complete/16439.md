barefaced:/ˈbɛəfeɪst/ (US), /ˈbeəfeɪst/ (UK)| adj.|厚颜无耻的，公然的|Shamelessly open, impudent, or without concealment.|He told a barefaced lie. 他厚颜无耻地撒谎了。|Synonyms: audacious, brazen, shameless, impudent, boldfaced|Antonyms: ashamed, embarrassed, modest, shy|adjective

bareface:/ˈbɛəfeɪs/ (US), /ˈbeəfeɪs/ (UK)| n.|厚颜无耻的人|A shameless or impudent person.|He's a bareface for asking for a raise after that performance.  他在那样的表现后还要求加薪，真是厚颜无耻。|Synonyms: scoundrel, rogue, rascal|Antonyms: saint|noun
