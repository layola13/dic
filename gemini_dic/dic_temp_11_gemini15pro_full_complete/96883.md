inexpectable: /ˌɪnɪkˈspɛktəbl/
adjective
无法预料的，意料之外的
not expected; unforeseen.
His sudden resignation was completely inexpectable. 他的突然辞职完全出乎意料。
The team's victory was inexpectable given their recent poor form. 鉴于他们最近糟糕的状态，球队的胜利是出乎意料的。
近义词：unforeseen, unexpected, surprising, unanticipated
反义词：expected, predictable, foreseeable, anticipated
词性：adjective


词源:
From in- (not) + expectable (capable of being expected).  "Expectable" itself comes from the verb "expect" derived from Latin "expectare" (to look out for, await),  formed from "ex-" (out) + "spectare" (to look).
