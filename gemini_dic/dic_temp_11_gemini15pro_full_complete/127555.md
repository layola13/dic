necropsy: /ˈnɛkrɒpsi/ (US) /ˈnɛkrɒpsi/ (UK)| n. |验尸，尸体解剖|An examination of a dead body to determine the cause of death, similar to an autopsy but typically performed on animals.|The veterinarian performed a necropsy on the deceased horse. 兽医对死马进行了尸检。|近义词: autopsy, postmortem|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
