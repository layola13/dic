eelpout:/ˈiːlˌpaʊt/| n.| 黏鱼，七鳃鳗|A type of fish with a long, slender body resembling an eel, often found in cold, northern waters. It is known for its slime and sometimes considered a delicacy.|  The fisherman caught a large eelpout in the icy fjord.  渔夫在冰冷的峡湾里捕到了一条大黏鱼。|Synonyms: burbot, cusk, ling|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific fish species)|noun

eelpout:/ˈiːlˌpaʊt/| v. |板着脸，绷着脸|To sulk or look sullen; to pout.| He eelpouted all afternoon because he didn't get his way. 他整个下午都板着脸，因为他没有达到目的。|Synonyms: pout, sulk, frown|Antonyms: smile, grin, beam|verb
