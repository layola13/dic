corvettes:/kɔːrˈvets/ | n. | 小型快速战舰，轻巡洋舰 | Small, fast warships, especially one designed for escort duty. | The navy deployed several corvettes to patrol the coastal waters. 海军部署了几艘轻型护卫舰巡逻沿海水域。|  The corvette maneuvered swiftly through the narrow channel. 轻型护卫舰在狭窄的海峡中迅速航行。| synonyms: warships, frigates, destroyers | antonyms:  aircraft carriers, battleships | noun

词源:
From French "corvette," a small warship, from Dutch "korvet," ultimately from Latin "corbita," a slow-sailing cargo ship.
