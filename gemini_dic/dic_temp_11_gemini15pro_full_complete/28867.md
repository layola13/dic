carpetlike:/ˈkɑːrpɪtlaɪk/
adjective
像地毯的，像地毯一样覆盖的
Resembling or suggesting a carpet, typically in texture, thickness, or overall appearance.
The moss grew thick and carpetlike on the forest floor. 森林地面上的苔藓长得又厚又像地毯。
The field of wildflowers was carpetlike in its beauty.  野花田美丽得像地毯一样。
近义词:carpet-like, velvety, plush, soft, dense
反义词:sparse, thin, bare, rough, uneven
词性:adjective
