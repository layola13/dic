nonlugubriousness:/ˌnɒnlʊˈɡuːbriəsnəs/
noun
非忧郁;  快乐; 欢快
The state of not being lugubrious; cheerfulness.
Her nonlugubriousness was infectious, brightening the room with her smile.  她的快乐很有感染力，用她的笑容照亮了整个房间。
Synonyms: cheerfulness, happiness, merriment, gaiety, lightheartedness, joviality, mirth, levity
Antonyms: lugubriousness, sadness, gloom, melancholy, dejection, depression, sorrow, grief
