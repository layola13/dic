overbrightly: /ˌoʊvərˈbraɪtli/ | adv. | 过于明亮地，过分鲜亮地 |  In a way that is excessively bright or shining. It suggests an unnatural or artificial brightness. | She smiled overbrightly, trying to hide her nervousness. 她笑得过于灿烂，试图掩饰她的紧张。| The stage was lit overbrightly, washing out the actors' faces.  舞台灯光过于明亮，使演员的脸都看不清楚了。| 近义词: too brightly, excessively brightly, garishly | 反义词: dimly, faintly | 词性: 副词
