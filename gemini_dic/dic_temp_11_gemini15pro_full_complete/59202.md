dysphonia:/dɪsˈfoʊniə/ /dɪsˈfəʊniə/
n.|发音困难，失音|Difficulty in speaking, typically resulting from a physical disorder of the mouth, tongue, throat, or vocal cords.|The singer suffered from dysphonia after a severe cold.  这位歌手在重感冒后患上了发音困难。|例句：His dysphonia made it difficult for him to communicate. 他的发音困难使他难以沟通。|近义词：hoarseness, aphonia|反义词:euphonia|词性:noun
