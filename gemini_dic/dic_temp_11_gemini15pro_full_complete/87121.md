herbalist:/ˈɜːrbəlɪst/ (US) /ˈhɜːbəlɪst/ (UK)| n. |草药师；草药医生|A person who is expert in the use of herbs, especially as medicine. |例句：The herbalist recommended a tea made from chamomile and lavender for relaxation.  草药师推荐了一种用洋甘菊和薰衣草制成的茶来放松身心。

例句：She consulted an herbalist for a natural remedy for her insomnia. 她咨询了一位草药师，寻求治疗失眠的自然疗法。|近义词：botanist, herborist |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "conventional doctor" or "pharmacist" in certain contexts) |词性: Noun
