breachy:/ˈbriːtʃi/| adj.|易违反的，易破坏的；有缺口的|Prone to breaches or violations; having gaps or openings.|The old fence was breachy and the cattle kept getting out.  旧篱笆很容易被破坏，牛不断地跑出去。|Synonyms: leaky, permeable, porous, defective, insecure|Antonyms: secure, sound, intact, impermeable|adjective

breachy:/ˈbriːtʃi/| adj.|（指马）不服管束的，暴躁的|(of a horse) difficult to control; excitable.|The breachy mare threw her rider and galloped off.  那匹不服管束的母马把骑手摔了下来，飞奔而去。|Synonyms: unruly, unmanageable, disobedient, fractious, refractory|Antonyms: obedient, docile, manageable|adjective
