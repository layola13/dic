limberer:/ˈlɪmbərər/| n.|柔软体操运动员，使柔软的人或物|A person or thing that makes something or someone limber.  A person who performs limbering exercises.|The gymnast was a skilled limberer, preparing her body for the competition.|近义词：flexor, softener|反义词: stiffener|词性:noun

Etymology:  From the verb "limber" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Limber" comes from Middle English "limber," from Old English "limpian" (to soften, make flexible), related to Old High German "limpfan" (to soften flax).
