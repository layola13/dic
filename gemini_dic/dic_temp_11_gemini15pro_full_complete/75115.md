frisee: /friːˈzeɪ/ (US), /friːˈziː/ (UK)| n.| 菊苣，苦苣，皱叶莴苣|A type of endive with curly, frizzy leaves, often used in salads.|The frisee salad with poached egg was delicious. 菊苣沙拉配水煮蛋非常美味。|Example: The chef garnished the dish with fresh frisee.  厨师用新鲜的菊苣装饰这道菜。|Synonyms: endive, chicory, curly endive|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a type of vegetable)|Noun
