helps:/hɛlps/| v.| 帮助，援助，促进，有助于，救助| This medicine helps relieve the pain. 这种药有助于缓解疼痛。|近义词：assists, aids, supports|反义词: hinders, obstructs|词性:动词(第三人称单数)

词源:
From Middle English *helpen*, from Old English *helpan* (third-person singular present indicative *hilpþ*, past tense *healp*, past participle *holpen*), from Proto-Germanic *helpaną* (compare West Frisian *helpe*, Dutch *helpen*, German *helfen*), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel-p-*, from *ḱel-* (“to lean, incline, bend”).
