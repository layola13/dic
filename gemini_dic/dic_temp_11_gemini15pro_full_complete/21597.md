blunderbuss:/ˈblʌndərbʌs/
|n.|滑膛枪
|A short-barreled firearm with a flared muzzle, typically used in the 17th and 18th centuries.  一种枪管短、枪口喇叭状的火器，通常用于17和18世纪。|The coach guard defended the mail with a blunderbuss.  驿站守卫用一支滑膛枪保卫邮件。
He inherited an old blunderbuss from his grandfather. 他从祖父那里继承了一支旧滑膛枪。|近义词：musket, shotgun|反义词：rifle (although not a perfect opposite, it represents a later, more accurate firearm)|词性: noun

词源: From early Dutch *donderbus, from donder "thunder" + bus "gun, cannon".  来自早期荷兰语 *donderbus，由 donder “雷声” + bus “枪，大炮”组成。
