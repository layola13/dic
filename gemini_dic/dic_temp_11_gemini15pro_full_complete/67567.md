extrapolates:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪts/| v. |推断；外推|To infer or estimate by extending or projecting known information.|The scientist extrapolated the results of the study to the general population. 科学家将研究结果推断到普通人群。|近义词：infer, deduce, project, estimate, conjecture|反义词:interpolate|词性:动词

extrapolate:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪts/| v. |推断；外推|To infer something from known facts; to estimate by extending known information.|Based on current trends, we can extrapolate that the population will double in 20 years. 基于目前的趋势，我们可以推断人口将在20年内翻一番。|近义词：infer, deduce, project, predict|反义词:  N/A (no direct antonym, but "interpolate" represents an opposite process)|词性:动词

extrapolate a trend = 推断趋势
