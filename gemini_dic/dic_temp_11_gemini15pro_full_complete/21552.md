bluenesses: /ˈbluːnɪsɪz/
n.
蓝色的状态；蓝色区域；忧郁
The state or quality of being blue; areas of blue color; instances of sadness or depression.
The bluenesses of the sky were breathtaking. 天空的蓝色令人叹为观止。
Synonyms: blues, azure, cerulean, sapphires, depressions
Antonyms: yellows, redness, brightness, cheerfulness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "blueness," with the suffix "-es" forming the plural.  "Blueness" derives from "blue" + "-ness."  "Blue" traces back to Old French "bleu," ultimately from a Germanic source.  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns indicating a state or quality.
