assentingly:/əˈsɛntɪŋli/
adverb
赞同地，同意地
in a way that shows agreement or approval
She nodded assentingly. 她点头表示同意。
近义词: approvingly, concurringly, agreeably
反义词: dissentingly, objectingly, disagreeingly
词性: 副词

词源:
From assent + -ing + -ly.  Assent comes from the Old French assentir, from Latin assentire  ("to think the same, agree to"), from ad-  ("to, towards") + sentire  ("to feel, think").
