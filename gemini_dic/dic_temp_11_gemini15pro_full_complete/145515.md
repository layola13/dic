overcoated:/ˌoʊvərˈkoʊtɪd/ | adj. | 覆盖外衣的；涂层的 | Covered with an overcoat; coated on the outside. | The overcoated tablets dissolved slowly.  这些包衣的药片溶解得很慢。| The overcoated wires prevented short circuits. 包覆涂层的电线防止了短路。| 近义词: coated, covered | 反义词: bare, uncovered | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From "over" + "coat" + "-ed." "Over" indicates completely covering something, and "coat" refers to a layer or covering.  The "-ed" suffix transforms the verb "coat" into an adjective.
