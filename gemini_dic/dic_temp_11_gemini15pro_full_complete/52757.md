dilatations:/ˌdaɪləˈteɪʃənz/
n.
扩张，膨胀；扩大；散开
the act of becoming wider or larger; the state of being wide or large

The doctor observed the dilatations of the pupils in the patient's eyes.
医生观察了病人瞳孔的扩张。

The dilatations of the blood vessels can be a sign of a serious medical condition.
血管扩张可能是严重疾病的征兆。

近义词:expansions, enlargements, widenings
反义词:contractions, constrictions, narrowings
词性:noun (plural)


dilatation:/ˌdaɪləˈteɪʃən/
n.
扩张，膨胀；扩大；散开
the act of becoming wider or larger; the state of being wide or large

The dilatation of the pupils is a normal response to low light.
瞳孔扩张是对弱光的正常反应。

The patient underwent a dilatation and curettage procedure.
病人接受了扩张和刮宫手术。

近义词:expansion, enlargement, widening
反义词:contraction, constriction, narrowing
词性:noun


词源:
From Late Latin *dīlātātiō, from Latin dīlātātus, past participle of dīlātāre (“to spread out, extend, enlarge”), from dī- + lātus (“wide, broad”).
