infernality:/ˌɪnfərˈnælɪti/
noun
地狱般的状态; 可怕的性质
the quality of being infernal; hellishness.
The infernality of the prison conditions shocked the human rights inspectors.  监狱条件的可怕程度震惊了人权调查员。
Synonyms: hellishness, fiendishness, devilishness, diabolicality
Antonyms: heavenliness, divinity, blessedness
词性: 名词
