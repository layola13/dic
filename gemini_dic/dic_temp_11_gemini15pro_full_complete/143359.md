outfall:/ˈaʊtfɔːl/| n.|排水口，出水口，排放口|The place where water or waste liquid flows out from a pipe or channel.  The outfall of the sewage treatment plant is located several miles offshore.|例句：The city is monitoring the water quality near the sewage outfall.  该市正在监测污水排放口附近的水质。

例句：The factory's outfall pipe discharges waste into the river.  这家工厂的排放管将废物排放到河里。|近义词：outlet, discharge, exit|反义词: intake|词性:名词
