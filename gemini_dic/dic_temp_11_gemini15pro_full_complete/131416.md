nonclericals:/ˌnɑːnˈklɛrɪkəlz/
noun
非神职人员；非文职人员
People who are not members of the clergy or who do not work in clerical jobs.
The union represented both clericals and nonclericals.  该工会既代表神职人员也代表非神职人员。
The nonclericals went on strike for better pay. 非文职人员举行罢工，要求提高工资。
Synonyms: laypeople, seculars
Antonyms: clerics, clergy
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
