dakokuya:/dɑːkokuːjɑː/| n.| 打刻屋 (打卡屋) | A small, often cramped and poorly ventilated office space where workers, often freelancers or those in the gig economy, rent desks or small workstations on a short-term basis. These spaces are typically found in Japan and are associated with precarious employment. | Example 1: He works in a dakokuya near the station because he can't afford a proper office.  他因为负担不起一个合适的办公室，在车站附近的一个打卡屋工作。

Example 2: The dakokuya was filled with young people working on their laptops. 打卡屋里挤满了在笔记本电脑上工作的年轻人。 | Near synonyms: shared office, co-working space, business center (though these often imply higher quality and amenities) | Antonyms:  private office, corporate office | Noun
