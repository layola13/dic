collusory:/kəˈluːsəri/
adjective
串通的，共谋的
Involving or characterized by collusion; deceitful.
The collusory agreement between the two companies was designed to defraud consumers. 这两家公司之间的共谋协议旨在欺骗消费者。

The evidence suggests a collusory relationship between the defendant and the witness. 证据表明被告与证人之间存在串通关系。

Synonym: conspiratorial, fraudulent, deceitful, underhanded, clandestine
Antonym: honest, aboveboard, legitimate, forthright

词性: adjective
