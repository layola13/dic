hypothetist:/haɪˈpɑːθəsɪst/| n.|假设者，假说者|A person who makes hypotheses or proposes theories; someone who engages in hypothesizing. |例句：The hypothetist suggested a link between diet and the disease.  这位假设者提出了饮食和疾病之间的联系。

例句：The hypothetist's claims were met with skepticism.  这位假设者的说法遭到了怀疑。|近义词：theorist, speculator, conjecturer|反义词:experimenter, empiricist|词性:noun
