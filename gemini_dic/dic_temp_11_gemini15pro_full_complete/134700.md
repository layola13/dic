A plural form of nonmastery. |  His nonmasteries in certain software programs hindered his progress. 他在某些软件程序方面的未掌握阻碍了他的进步。| Synonyms: weaknesses, shortcomings, deficiencies, inabilities | Antonyms: strengths, proficiencies, expertise, skills | noun
