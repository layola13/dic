nonabidingness:/ˌnɒnəˈbaɪdɪŋnɪs/
noun
不服从，不遵守
The state or quality of not abiding by rules or laws.
His nonabidingness of the court order led to his arrest. 他不遵守法庭命令导致他被捕。
Near Synonyms: disobedience, noncompliance, recalcitrance, rebelliousness, defiance
Antonyms: obedience, compliance, conformity, submission
词性: 名词
