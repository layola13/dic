becloud:/bɪˈklaʊd/| vt.|使模糊，使昏暗，遮蔽|To obscure or confuse something, often with clouds or fog, but also metaphorically with emotions like doubt or fear.|例句：The fog beclouded the harbor, making it difficult for ships to enter. 浓雾笼罩着海港，使船只难以进入。

例句：His judgment was beclouded by anger. 他的判断力被愤怒蒙蔽了。|近义词：obscure, darken, confuse, obfuscate|反义词：clarify, illuminate, elucidate|词性:动词
