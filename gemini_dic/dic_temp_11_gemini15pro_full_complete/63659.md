epagogic:/ˌɛpəˈɡɒdʒɪk/ /ˌɛpəˈɡɑːdʒɪk/| adj.|教学法的；诱导的|Relating to or involving the methods of teaching; inductive.|The epagogic approach encourages students to discover principles for themselves.  这种教学法鼓励学生自己去发现规律。|近义词：didactic, instructive, pedagogical|反义词：None|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *epagōgikos*, from *epagein* "to lead on, induce," from *epi-* "upon" + *agein* "to lead."
