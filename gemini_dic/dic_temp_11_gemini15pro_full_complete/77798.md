generalizations:/ˌdʒɛnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 概括，归纳，普遍化 |  The process of forming general concepts by abstracting common properties from specific instances. |  His generalizations about women are offensive. 他对女性的概括性说法具有冒犯性。| synonyms: abstractions, summaries, conclusions | antonyms: specifics, details, particulars | noun

generalizations:/ˌdʒɛnərəlɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 普遍化，推广 | The act of applying a general rule or principle to a specific case. | The generalizations from this small study may not apply to the larger population.  这项小型研究的推广可能不适用于更大的人群。 | synonyms: applications, extensions | antonyms: restrictions, limitations | noun
