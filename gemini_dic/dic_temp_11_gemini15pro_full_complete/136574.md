nonsalutarily:/nɒnˌsæljʊˈtɛərɪli/
| adv. |不健康地，不利地| In a manner that is not beneficial to health or well-being.  The damp conditions affected him nonsalutarily. 潮湿的环境对他的健康不利。|例句：The policy affected the economy nonsalutarily. 这项政策对经济产生了不利影响。 The chemical spill nonsalutarily impacted the local ecosystem. 化学品泄漏对当地生态系统造成了不利影响。 |近义词：harmfully, detrimentally, unhealthily|反义词: beneficially, healthily, advantageously|词性:副词
词源: non- (not) + salutarily (in a healthy or beneficial way)  The word "salutarily" comes from the Latin "salūtāris," meaning "healthful."
