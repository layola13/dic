dvorak: /ˈdvɔːræk/ | n. | 德沃夏克键盘布局; 德沃夏克 (姓氏)|  A keyboard layout designed to be more efficient than QWERTY. Also, a surname, most famously associated with the composer Antonín Dvořák. | I switched to the Dvorak keyboard layout to improve my typing speed. 我改用德沃夏克键盘布局来提高我的打字速度。| Antonín Dvořák composed the famous New World Symphony. 安东宁·德沃夏克创作了著名的《新世界交响曲》。 | 近义词: (for keyboard layout) Colemak, Workman | 反义词: QWERTY | 词性: noun
