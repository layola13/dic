decentralises:/ˌdiːˈsɛntrəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 分散，去中心化 | To distribute the administrative powers or functions of (a central authority) over a less concentrated area or group.  | The company decentralised its operations, giving more autonomy to regional offices. 公司将业务分散，赋予区域办事处更大的自主权。 | The new CEO is decentralising control of the company. 新任首席执行官正在分散公司的控制权。 | synonyms: distribute, disperse, devolve, diffuse | antonyms: centralise, consolidate, concentrate | verb

decentralise:/ˌdiːˈsɛntrəlaɪz/ | v. | 分散，去中心化 | To distribute the administrative powers or functions of (a central authority) over a less concentrated area or group.  | Many companies are decentralising their operations. 许多公司正在分散其运营。| The government is decentralising power to local authorities. 政府正在将权力下放给地方当局。 | synonyms: distribute, disperse, devolve, diffuse | antonyms: centralise, consolidate, concentrate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + centralize, from central + -ize.  Ultimately from Latin centrum "center".
