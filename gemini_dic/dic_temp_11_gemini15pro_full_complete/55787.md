diverter:/daɪˈvɜːrtər/ (UK) /dəˈvɜːrtər/ (US)| n.| 转移器；分流器；导流板| something that diverts something else, especially a device for diverting the flow of a liquid or gas | The aircraft's thrust reverser acts as a diverter, redirecting engine exhaust forward.  这架飞机的反推力装置起作用就像一个导流板，将引擎排气向前重新导向。| synonyms: deflector, shunt, bypass | antonyms: concentrator, collector | noun


divert:/daɪˈvɜːrt/ (UK) /dəˈvɜːrt/ (US)| v.| 转移；使转向；使分心| to cause something or someone to change direction or course; to turn aside; to distract |  The heavy traffic diverted us onto a smaller road.  拥挤的交通使我们绕道到了一条小路上。 | synonyms: redirect, reroute, deflect, distract | antonyms: focus, concentrate, maintain, continue | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *diverten*, from Old French *divertir*, from Latin *dīvertere* (“to turn aside, turn away”), from *dī-* (“aside, away”) + *vertere* (“to turn”).
