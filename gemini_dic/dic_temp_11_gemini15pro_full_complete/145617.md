overconsiderately: /ˌoʊvərˌkənˈsɪdərɪtli/ | adv. | 过于体贴地，过分周到地 |  In an excessively thoughtful or considerate manner, often to the point of being unnecessary or burdensome.  It implies an excessive concern for the needs and feelings of others. | He overconsiderately offered to carry all her bags, even though she only had one small purse. 他过分殷勤地提出要帮她提所有的包，即使她只有一个钱包。|  Example: She overconsiderately avoided mentioning his recent job loss, fearing it would upset him. 她过分体贴地避免提及他最近失业的事，担心这会让他难过。| 近义词: excessively thoughtfully, overly solicitously | 反义词: inconsiderately, thoughtlessly | 词性: 副词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "considerate" (adjective meaning "thoughtful of others") + "-ly" (adverb suffix).
