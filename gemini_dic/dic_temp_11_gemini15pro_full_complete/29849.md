causative:/ˈkɔːzətɪv/| adj. n.| 使役的，原因的；使役动词，使役语态 |A causative verb or form expresses an action which is caused to happen by the subject.  A causative construction is a grammatical structure that expresses the idea of someone or something causing something else to happen.| He gave her a causative look. 他给了她一个意味深长的眼神。|近义词：causal, effective |反义词: preventative, inhibitory| adjective

causative:/ˈkɔːzətɪv/| n.| 使役动词，使役语态 |A causative verb or form expresses an action which is caused to happen by the subject.  A causative construction is a grammatical structure that expresses the idea of someone or something causing something else to happen.| The verb "make" is often used in causative constructions.  动词“make”经常用于使役结构。|近义词：- |反义词: -| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *causativus, from Latin causāt-, past participle stem of causāre "to plead a cause, allege," from causa "cause, reason."
