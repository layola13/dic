chapter:/ˈtʃæptər/ (US), /ˈtʃæptə/ (UK)| n.|章，篇，回；时期，阶段|A main division of a book, or a period in history or in a person's life. |例句：This is an important chapter in the history of our country. 这是我国历史上重要的一章。|例句：The next chapter of the book deals with the Civil War. 书的下一章讲述内战。|近义词：section, part, division, period, phase|反义词: N/A|noun
