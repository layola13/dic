myriacanthous:/ˌmɪriəˈkænθəs/
adjective
多刺的；多棘的
Having many spines or thorns.
The myriacanthous sea urchin is covered in sharp spines.  这只多刺的海胆全身长满了锋利的刺。
近义词:spinous, thorny, prickly
反义词:smooth, soft
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *myriad-* "ten thousand" + *acantha* "thorn, spine".
