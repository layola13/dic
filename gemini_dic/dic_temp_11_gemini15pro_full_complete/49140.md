deixis:/ˈdaɪksɪs/| n.|指示|Words or expressions that take their meaning relative to the time, place, and participants in the conversation, e.g., "here," "now," "I," "you."|I'm over *here* - come join me! 我在这边 - 过来和我一起吧！|The word "tomorrow" is an example of *deixis* because its meaning depends on the day it is uttered.  “明天”这个词是指示语的一个例子，因为它的含义取决于说话的当天。|reference, anaphora| - |noun

Simple Present:
I am here now. = 我现在在这里。
You are there now. = 你现在在那里。

Present Continuous:
I am standing here right now. = 我现在正站在这里。
She is sitting there at the moment. = 她现在正坐在那里。

Present Perfect:
I have been here before. = 我以前来过这里。
He has gone there many times. = 他去过那里很多次。

Present Perfect Continuous:
I have been waiting here for an hour. = 我在这里等了一个小时了。
They have been living there for years. = 他们已经在那里住了好几年了。

Simple Past:
I was there yesterday. = 我昨天在那里。
She was here last week. = 她上周在这里。

Past Continuous:
I was standing here when it happened. = 事情发生时我正站在这里。
He was living there at the time. = 那时他住在那里。

Past Perfect:
I had already been there before you arrived. = 你到达之前我已经去过那里了。
She had left here by the time I called. = 我打电话的时候她已经离开这里了。

Past Perfect Continuous:
I had been waiting here for hours before he showed up. = 在他出现之前，我已经在这里等了好几个小时了。
They had been living there for years before they moved. = 他们在搬家之前已经在那里住了好几年了。

Simple Future:
I will be there tomorrow. = 我明天会在那里。
She will come here next week. = 她下周会来这里。

Future Continuous:
I will be waiting here when you arrive. = 你到达时我会在这里等你。
He will be living there next year. = 明年他将住在那里。

Future Perfect:
I will have been here for a week by then. = 到那时我将在这里待一个星期了。
She will have left there by the time you get there. = 你到那里的时候她就已经离开那里了。

Future Perfect Continuous:
I will have been living here for two years by next month. = 到下个月我将在这里住了两年了。
They will have been studying there for five years by the time they graduate. = 到他们毕业的时候，他们将在那里学习了五年了。
