opiate:/ˈoʊpiət/ | n. adj.| 鸦片剂；麻醉剂；使人麻醉的东西 |A drug derived from opium.  Something that has a soothing or dulling effect. |She was given an opiate to relieve the pain. 她被给予鸦片剂来缓解疼痛。| The music was an opiate, soothing her anxieties. 音乐像麻醉剂一样, 舒缓了她的焦虑。 |Synonyms: narcotic, analgesic, painkiller, sedative |Antonyms: stimulant, invigorant |noun, adjective
