curtain-call: /ˈkɜːtn kɔːl/ (US) /ˈkɜːtən kɔːl/ (UK)| n. |谢幕；谢幕演出|The appearance of actors on stage after a performance to acknowledge the audience's applause.|  The actors took several curtain calls after the outstanding performance.  演员们在精彩的演出后多次谢幕。

The band returned for a curtain call after their final song.  乐队在最后一首歌结束后回到舞台谢幕。


近义词: encore, ovation | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "opening scene")| 词性: noun
