competed:/kəmˈpiːtɪd/
verb
竞争过，比赛过
past tense and past participle of "compete" - engaged in a contest or rivalry.
She competed in the Olympics. 她参加了奥运会比赛。
He competed against his brother for the top prize. 他和他的兄弟竞争最高奖项。
近义词: contended, vied, contested, rivaled
反义词: cooperated, collaborated, yielded
词性: verb
