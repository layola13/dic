contrastive:/kənˈtræstɪv/ | adj. | 对比的，对照的 |  Emphasizing or based on contrast; expressing or involving a comparison between two things. |  Contrastive analysis helps language learners understand the differences between their native language and the target language. 对比分析帮助语言学习者理解他们的母语和目标语言之间的差异。 | synonyms: comparative, differential, distinctive | antonyms: similar, alike, uniform | adjective

contrastive:/kənˈtræstɪv/ | adj. | 对比的，对照的 | Serving to compare and contrast; expressing or involving a comparison between two things. | She used a contrastive approach in her research to highlight the differences between the two groups.  她在研究中使用了对比方法来突出两组之间的差异。 | synonyms: comparative, relative, juxtapositional | antonyms: similar, alike, homogenous | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From contrast + -ive.  Contrast ultimately derives from the Latin "contra" (against) and "stare" (to stand).
