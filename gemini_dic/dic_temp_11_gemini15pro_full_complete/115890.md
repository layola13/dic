marmolite:/ˈmɑːrməˌlaɪt/| n. |叶蛇纹石|A massive, compact variety of serpentine, usually of a pale or dark green color, sometimes mottled or veined. It is easily cut and polished, and is used for ornamental purposes.|The fireplace was made of polished marmolite, a beautiful shade of deep green.|近义词：serpentine, verd antique|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider contrasting materials like granite or marble)|noun
