half-proven:/ˌhɑːfˈpruːvən/
adjective
半证实的，未完全证实的
Partially proven; not fully substantiated or confirmed.  The evidence is half-proven, so we need to investigate further.  证据只是半证实的，我们需要进一步调查。
half-demonstrated, partially substantiated, partly confirmed
fully proven, completely substantiated, unequivocally confirmed
adjective
