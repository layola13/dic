counterstroke:/ˈkaʊntərˌstrəʊk/ (US) /ˈkaʊntəˌstrəʊk/ (UK)| n.|反击，回击，对抗性措施|An action taken in opposition to another; a retaliatory move. |The government's counterstroke to the rebels' offensive was swift and decisive. 政府对叛军进攻的反击迅速而果断。

The boxer delivered a powerful counterstroke, knocking his opponent to the canvas.  拳击手使出一记强有力的反击，把对手击倒在拳台上。


|近义词：counterattack, riposte, retaliation|反义词:attack, offense|noun
