irrigated:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪtɪd/
adj.
灌溉的
Supplied with water by artificial means; watered.
The irrigated fields yielded a bountiful harvest. 灌溉的田地获得了丰收。
The farmer irrigated his crops regularly. 农民定期灌溉他的作物。
近义词:watered, drenched
反义词:arid, dry
词性:形容词

irrigate:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪt/
v.
灌溉
Supply water to (land or crops) to help growth, typically by means of channels.
Farmers irrigate their fields to ensure a good harvest.  农民灌溉田地以确保丰收。
The ancient Egyptians developed sophisticated methods to irrigate the land along the Nile. 古埃及人开发了复杂的方法来灌溉尼罗河沿岸的土地。
近义词:water, moisten
反义词:drain, dehydrate
词性:动词

Etymology:
From Latin irrigatus, past participle of irrigare "to water, refresh," from in- "in, into" + rigare "to water, moisten."
