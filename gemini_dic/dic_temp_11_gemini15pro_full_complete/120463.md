minority:/maɪˈnɔːrɪti/| n.|少数；少数民族；未成年|A smaller number or part, especially a number that is less than half the whole number; a group of people who differ racially or politically from a larger group of which it is a part; the state of being under the age of full legal responsibility.|The minority voted against the proposal. 少数人投票反对该提案。|Synonyms: few, small number|Antonyms: majority, most|noun
