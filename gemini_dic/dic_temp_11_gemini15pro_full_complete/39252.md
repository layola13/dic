congregation:/ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃn/| n.|集会，会众，教堂的会众|A congregation is a group of people assembled for religious worship. |The congregation sang hymns together. 教堂的会众一起唱赞美诗。|近义词：assembly, gathering, audience|反义词:dispersal, scattering|noun

congregate:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪt/| v.|聚集，集合|To congregate means to gather or assemble, especially in a large group. |People congregated in the town square. 人们聚集在城镇广场上。|近义词：assemble, gather, convene|反义词:disperse, scatter|verb
