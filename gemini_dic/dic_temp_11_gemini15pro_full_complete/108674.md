leagued:/liːɡd/
adjective
结盟的，同盟的
Joined together in a league or alliance.
The leagued nations fought against the common enemy. 结盟的国家共同对抗敌人。
近义词: allied, united, confederated, combined
反义词: divided, separate, disunited

词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *legued, *ligued, from Old French liegé, from Frankish *leidig, from Proto-Germanic *leudigaz (“belonging to the people, free”), from *leudiz (“people”) + *-igaz. Cognate with Old High German liuttag (“belonging to the people”), Old English lēode (“people”). More at lede, -ed.
