inductor:/ɪnˈdʌktər/| n. |电感器，感应器|A passive electronic component that stores energy in a magnetic field when electric current flows through it.|The inductor in the circuit helps to smooth out the voltage fluctuations. 电路中的电感器有助于平滑电压波动。|Synonyms: coil, choke, reactor|Antonyms: capacitor|noun
