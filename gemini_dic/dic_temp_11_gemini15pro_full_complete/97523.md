ingot:/ˈɪŋɡət/| n. |锭，块|A mass of metal, such as gold or silver, cast in a convenient shape for storage or transportation.|The gold ingots were locked away in the vault. 金锭被锁在金库里。|Example: The factory produces copper ingots for industrial use. 这家工厂生产用于工业的铜锭。|Synonyms: bar, block, lump, wedge|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
