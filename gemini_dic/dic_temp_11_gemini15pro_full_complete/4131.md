air-dropped:/ˈeərdrɒpt/ (UK) /ˈeərdrɑːpt/ (US)| v. adj.|空投，空降；通过空投发送|To deliver or send something, typically supplies or data, by dropping it from an aircraft or transmitting it wirelessly.|例句：The humanitarian aid was air-dropped to the refugees. 人道主义援助被空投给了难民。


例句：I air-dropped the photos to my friend's phone. 我把照片空投到我朋友的手机上。|近义词：parachuted, delivered, transmitted, beamed|反义词: picked up, uploaded|词性:动词(过去式和过去分词)
