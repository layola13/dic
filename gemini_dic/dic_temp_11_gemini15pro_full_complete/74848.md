frequentation:/ˌfriːkwənˈteɪʃən/ |ˌfriːkwɛnˈteɪʃən/
n.|常去；经常光顾；顾客人数|The frequentation of the museum has increased significantly this year. 今年博物馆的参观人数显著增加。|The restaurant's frequentation dropped after the health inspection.  卫生检查之后，这家餐厅的顾客人数下降了。|近义词：attendance, patronage, custom|反义词：absence, desertion|词性: noun

Etymology:
From French *fréquentation*, from *fréquenter* ("to frequent"), from Latin *frequentāre* ("to visit often, resort to, haunt, crowd, throng"), frequentative of *frequēns* ("crowded, numerous, often").
