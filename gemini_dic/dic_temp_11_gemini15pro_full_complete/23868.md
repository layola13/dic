bready:/ˈbredi/| adj.|像面包的；有面包味的|Resembling bread in texture or flavor. |The cake was dense and bready.  这蛋糕很瓷实，有面包味。

The bread was a little too bready for my taste.  这面包对我来说面包味太重了。|近义词：doughy, yeasty|反义词:crispy, crusty|词性:形容词

Etymology: From bread + -y.  源自 bread + -y.
