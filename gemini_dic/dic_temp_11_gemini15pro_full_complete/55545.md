distributors:/dɪˈstrɪbjətərz/
n.
分销商，经销商
a person or organization that supplies goods to shops and companies
例句：The company has a network of distributors across the country.  这家公司在全国拥有一张分销网络。

例句：They are the sole distributors of this product in the UK. 他们是该产品在英国的独家经销商。

近义词：supplier, vendor, dealer, agent
反义词：retailer, consumer

词性: noun
