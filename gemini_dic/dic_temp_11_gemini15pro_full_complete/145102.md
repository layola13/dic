overappreciative:/ˌoʊvərəˈpriːʃiətɪv/ | adj. | 过于欣赏的，过分感激的 | Showing or feeling excessive appreciation or gratitude.  It suggests a level of gratitude that is disproportionate to the action or gift received, and can sometimes be perceived as insincere or excessive. | He was so overappreciative of the small gift that it made me uncomfortable. 他对那份小礼物表现得过于感激，让我感到不舒服。|  She sent an overappreciative email after the interview, which might have seemed a bit much. 面试后，她发了一封过于感激的邮件，这可能看起来有点过头了。| 近义词: effusive, gushing, excessively grateful | 反义词: ungrateful, unappreciative | 词性: 形容词
