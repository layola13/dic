overconsideration:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃən/| n.|过度考虑，过分体贴|The act of considering something too much, often to the point of anxiety or inaction.  Excessive concern for the feelings or needs of others.|Her overconsideration for her guests meant she barely enjoyed the party herself. 她对客人的过分体贴意味着她自己几乎没有享受到聚会。|近义词：solicitude, overthinking, anxiety|反义词：inconsiderateness, thoughtlessness|词性:noun
