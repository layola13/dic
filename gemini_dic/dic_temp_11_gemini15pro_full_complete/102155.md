issuers:/ˈɪʃuərz/| n.|发行人，发行机构|An issuer is an entity that develops and registers securities for sale to the public.  发行人是一个开发和注册证券以向公众出售的实体。|The bond issuers are struggling to refinance their debt. 债券发行方正努力为其债务进行再融资。|synonyms: provider, supplier, distributor|antonyms: receiver, recipient|noun
