archegonium: /ˌɑːrkɪˈɡoʊniəm/ | n. | 颈卵器 |  The female reproductive organ in mosses, ferns, and most gymnosperms. It is a flask-shaped structure containing the egg cell. | The archegonium of the fern is located on the underside of the prothallus.  蕨类的颈卵器位于原叶体的下面。|  The moss archegonium is protected by a layer of cells.  苔藓的颈卵器被一层细胞保护着。 | synonym: female sex organ (in specific plants) | antonym: antheridium | noun
