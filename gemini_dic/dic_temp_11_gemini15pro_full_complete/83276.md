gymnotoka:/ˌdʒɪmnoʊˈtoʊkə/ | n. | 裸背鱼科 | A family of weakly electric fishes in the order Gymnotiformes, commonly known as the "naked back knifefishes". They are characterized by the absence of a dorsal fin and the presence of a long anal fin that extends along most of the body. | Gymnotoka are found in freshwater habitats in South America. 裸背鱼科鱼类发现于南美洲的淡水栖息地。| 近义词: knifefish (广义上的刀鱼) | 反义词:  None (没有明确的反义词) | 词性: noun
