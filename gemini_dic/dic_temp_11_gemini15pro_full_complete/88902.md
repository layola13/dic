holcomania:/ˌhɒlkəˈmeɪniə/ /ˌhoʊlkəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 疯狂购物症，购物狂 | An irresistible desire to buy articles, especially when unnecessary.  A compulsive shopper. | She suffers from holcomania and often maxes out her credit cards. 她患有购物狂，经常刷爆信用卡。|  Example:  His holcomania led him to buy three identical sweaters. 他的购物狂让他买了三件一模一样的毛衣。| Synonym: oniomania, compulsive shopping, shopping addiction | Antonym: frugality, thrift | Noun
