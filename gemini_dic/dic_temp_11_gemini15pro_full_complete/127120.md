native:/ˈneɪtɪv/ | adj. n.| 本地的，土著的；土著人 |Someone who was born in a particular place is a native of that place. |例句：He is a native of New York. 他是纽约人。

例句：English is not my native language. 英语不是我的母语。|近义词：indigenous, local, aboriginal|反义词：foreign, alien|词性:形容词,名词

native:/ˈneɪtɪv/ | n.| 本地人，土著居民 |A person born in a specific place. |例句：The natives were friendly and welcoming. 当地人友好而热情。

例句：She is a native of France. 她是法国人。|近义词：inhabitant, resident, local|反义词：foreigner, alien|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *natif* (Modern French natif), from Latin *nativus* "born, innate, natural," from *natus*, past participle of *nasci* "to be born."
