crevasse: /krɪˈvæs/ | n. | 裂缝，裂隙 | A deep open crack, especially in a glacier or ice sheet. |  The climbers had to be careful to avoid the crevasses in the glacier. 登山者必须小心避开冰川上的裂缝。| synonyms: fissure, chasm, crack, rift | antonyms: closure, seal | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *crevasse*, from Old French *crevace*, from *crever* "to break, burst," from Latin *crepare* "to crack, creak, rattle, break."
