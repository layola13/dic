laureates:/ˈlɔːriəts/ | n. | 获奖者；桂冠诗人 | Someone who has been awarded a very important prize or honour, especially the Nobel Prize.  Also, a poet officially appointed by a government or conferring institution, and traditionally crowned with a laurel wreath. | The Nobel laureates gathered in Stockholm. 诺贝尔奖获得者聚集在斯德哥尔摩。 | synonyms: winners, recipients, honorees, prizewinners (especially for Nobel Prize), poets laureate (for the official poet position) | antonyms: losers, failures | noun
