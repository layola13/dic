nutmeggy:/ˈnʌtˌmeɡi/ | adj. | 肉豆蔻味的；有肉豆蔻香气的 | Having the taste or fragrance of nutmeg. | The eggnog had a warm, nutmeggy flavor.  蛋酒有一种温暖的肉豆蔻香味。 | Synonyms: aromatic, spicy, fragrant | Antonyms: bland, tasteless | adjective

nutmeggy:/ˈnʌtˌmeɡi/ | adj. | （尤指颜色或气味）使人想起肉豆蔻的；略带棕色的 | (especially of a colour or smell) reminiscent of nutmeg; slightly brown. | The walls were painted a warm, nutmeggy hue. 墙壁被漆成了一种温暖的、略带棕色的色调。 | Synonyms: brownish, tan, beige | Antonyms: bright, vibrant | adjective
