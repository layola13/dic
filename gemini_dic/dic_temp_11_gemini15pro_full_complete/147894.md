overred:/ˌoʊvərˈrɛd/| adj.|过于红的，过熟的|Excessively red, often due to overripeness or excessive exposure to the sun.|The apples on the tree were overred and starting to rot. 树上的苹果过于红了，开始腐烂了。|The tomatoes were left on the vine too long and became overred. 西红柿在藤上留得太久了，变得过红了。|近义词：overripe, too red|反义词：underripe, green|词性:形容词
词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "red" (adjective).  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "red," indicating an excessive or extreme degree of redness.
