keratosa:/ˌkɛrəˈtoʊsə/| n. |角化病，角化症|A skin condition characterized by the formation of a horny layer or covering.|Keratosa can manifest in various forms, affecting different areas of the body. 角化病可以以各种形式表现出来，影响身体的不同部位。|Hyperkeratosis, keratosis| Leukoderma, hypopigmentation|noun
