narica:/nəˈriːkə/ | n. | 傻瓜，笨蛋 | A foolish or silly person. |  He acted like a narica and lost all his money. 他像个傻瓜一样，把所有的钱都输光了。

|近义词：fool, idiot, moron, imbecile, dolt, simpleton |反义词: genius, savant | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *narica*, possibly related to *nariz* ("nose"), implying someone who is easily led by the nose.  Alternatively, it might be connected to *narco* ("numb, dazed"), suggesting someone lacking in sense or awareness.  Its exact origin remains somewhat uncertain.
