bahai:/bɑˈhaɪ/ /bəˈhaɪ/| adj. n.|巴哈伊的；巴哈伊教徒|Relating to or a follower of Baha'i, a monotheistic religion emphasizing the spiritual unity of all humankind.|例句：He is a devout Bahai. 他是一位虔诚的巴哈伊教徒。|例句：She studies Bahai writings. 她研究巴哈伊教的著作。|近义词：Baha’i, Baha'ist|反义词：(None -  religions don't typically have direct antonyms)|词性:形容词, 名词


词源:
From Bahāʼullāh ( بهاءالله ), the title of Mírzá Ḥusayn-`Alí Núrí (1817–1892), Persian prophet and founder of the Baha’i Faith.  Bahāʼ (بهاء) means "glory" or "splendor" in Arabic.
