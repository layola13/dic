fastness:/ˈfæstnəs/| n.| 坚固性；牢固；不褪色；要塞 |The fastness of the dye was tested by washing the fabric.  这种染料的牢固度通过洗涤织物来测试。|The rebels retreated to their mountain fastness. 叛军撤退到他们的山区要塞。|近义词：strength, durability, security, fortress, stronghold|反义词：weakness, instability, insecurity|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *fastnes, from Old English fæstnes (“firmness, steadfastness”), from Proto-Germanic *fastiniz (“fastness”), derived from *fastuz (“fast”). Cognate with Scots fastness (“fastness”), West Frisian fastens (“fastness, firmness”), Dutch vastheid (“firmness, solidity”), German Festigkeit (“firmness, solidity”), Swedish fasthet (“firmness, solidity”).
