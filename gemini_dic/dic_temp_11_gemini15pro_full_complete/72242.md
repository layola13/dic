floridly:/ˈflɔːrɪdli/ /ˈflɒrɪdli/
adjective
华丽地；绚丽地；辞藻华丽地
Excessively ornate or elaborate in style or language; reddish or flushed in complexion.  Characterized by elaborate or colorful rhetoric.
She described the scene floridly, using a lot of extravagant language. 她用大量华丽的辞藻描述了这个场景。
His face was floridly red from the exertion. 他的脸因用力而涨得通红。
近义词：ornately, elaborately, flamboyantly, showily
反义词：simply, plainly, austerely, subtly
词性: adverb

词源: From Latin *flōridus*, from *flōrēre* "to blossom, flourish," from *flōs* "flower."
