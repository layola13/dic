high-ranking:/ˌhaɪˈræŋkɪŋ/| adj.|高级别的，高阶的|Having a high rank or position; important.|例句：She is a high-ranking official in the government. 她在政府中是一位高级官员。|例句：High-ranking officers attended the meeting. 高级军官出席了会议。|近义词：senior, top-level, prominent, important, influential|反义词：low-ranking, junior, subordinate|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"high" (OE hēah) + "ranking" (present participle of "rank," from OFr. rangier "to arrange, place in a row," from Frankish *hring "circle, ring")
