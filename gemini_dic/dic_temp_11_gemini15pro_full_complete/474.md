abnegation:/ˌæbnɪˈɡeɪʃn/
n.
放弃，克制，自制，舍己
the act of renouncing or rejecting something.
She demonstrated great abnegation by giving up her career to care for her sick mother. 她为了照顾生病的母亲放弃了自己的事业，表现出了极大的舍己精神。

The monk's life was one of abnegation, devoid of worldly pleasures.  这个僧侣的生活是克己的，没有任何世俗的享乐。


近义词:self-denial, renunciation, sacrifice, forbearance
反义词:indulgence, self-indulgence, gratification, acceptance
词性:noun
