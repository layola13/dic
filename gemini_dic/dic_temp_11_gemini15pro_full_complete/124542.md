Mountbatten: /ˈmaʊntˌbætən/ | n. | 蒙巴顿 | A surname, also the name of a British royal family branch.  It's a combination of the German names Battenberg and Mountbatten.  The Battenberg family anglicized their name to Mountbatten during World War I due to anti-German sentiment. | Lord Mountbatten was the last Viceroy of India.  蒙巴顿勋爵是最后一位印度总督。

Lord Louis Mountbatten was assassinated by the IRA.  路易斯·蒙巴顿勋爵被爱尔兰共和军暗杀。 | Synonym: (As a surname, no direct synonym) Perhaps "family name", "last name" | Antonym:  (As a surname, no direct antonym) | proper noun
