dourness:/ˈdaʊərnəs/ /ˈdʊrnəs/| n.| 严厉；阴郁；顽固；|The dourness of his expression made her uneasy. 他表情的严厉让她感到不安。|近义词：sternness, grimness, severity, sullenness|反义词：cheerfulness, geniality, affability|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *dour*, from Scottish Gaelic *dùr*, from Old Irish *dúr* (“hard, obstinate, sullen”), from Proto-Celtic *dūro- (“hard, firm”). Related to Welsh *duro* (“to harden, steel”).  The suffix "-ness" turns the adjective "dour" into the noun "dourness".
