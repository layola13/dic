horizonless:/həˈraɪzənləs/
adj.
无边无际的，无限的
Lacking a visible horizon; seemingly vast and boundless.
The desert stretched before them, a horizonless expanse of sand.  沙漠在他们面前延伸，一片无边无际的沙地。
The astronaut gazed at the horizonless expanse of space. 宇航员凝视着浩瀚无垠的太空。
近义词: boundless, limitless, infinite, vast, immense
反义词: limited, finite, bounded
词性: 形容词

词源:
From horizon + -less.  由 horizon（地平线）+ -less（无...的）组成.
