losable:/ˈluːzəbl/
adjective
可输的，可能丢失的
Capable of being lost.
This game is losable, but we still have a chance.  这场比赛可能会输，但我们还有机会。
The battle was losable from the start. 这场战斗从一开始就可能失败。
近义词: forfeitable, vulnerable
反义词: unlosable, certain (in the context of victory)
词性: adjective

Etymology:  From "lose" + "-able".  "Lose" comes from Old English "losian," meaning "to be lost, perish, destroy."  The suffix "-able" comes from Old French "-able" and Latin "-abilis," meaning "capable of being."
