allodialist:/ˌæləˈdaɪəlɪst/| n. |自主地主，完全所有权地主|A person who owns land free and clear of any superior landlord or feudal lord, having full and absolute ownership.|  Example 1:  The allodialist enjoyed complete control over his property, without owing any feudal duties.  完全所有权地主完全掌控自己的财产，无需履行任何封建义务。

Example 2:  In modern society, most landowners are essentially allodialists, although the concept of allodial title is rooted in history.  在现代社会，大多数土地所有者本质上都是自主地主，尽管自主所有权的概念源于历史。| 近义词:freeholder, independent landowner | 反义词:tenant, lessee | 词性:noun

词源: From French *allodialiste*, from Medieval Latin *allodialis*  "freehold", from Frankish *alōd* "full ownership".
