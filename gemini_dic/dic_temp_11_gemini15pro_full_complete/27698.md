candling:/ˈkændlɪŋ/| n.| 烛光；照蛋|The process of examining an egg by holding it to a light to see if it is fertile or contains a developing embryo. |例句：Candling is a traditional method used to check the quality of eggs.  照蛋是检查鸡蛋质量的传统方法。

例句：The farmer candled the eggs to see which ones were developing properly.  农民用照蛋法检查哪些鸡蛋发育正常。|近义词：testing, examining|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
