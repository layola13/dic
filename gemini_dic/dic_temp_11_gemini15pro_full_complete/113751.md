macrons:/ˈmeɪkrɒnz/ (US) /ˈmækrɒnz/ (UK)| n.|长音符号；长音符號|A diacritical mark placed above a vowel to indicate a long vowel sound, often represented as a horizontal line.  It is used in various languages, including Māori, Hawaiian, and Latin.|The word "māori" uses macrons to indicate the long vowel sounds.|近义词:long vowel marks, diacritics|反义词:breve (短音符号)|noun
