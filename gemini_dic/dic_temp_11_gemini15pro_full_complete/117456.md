meditator:/ˈmedɪteɪtər/| n.|冥想者，沉思者|A person who meditates, especially one who practices meditation regularly as a religious or spiritual exercise.|例句：The meditator sat in silence, focusing on their breath.  冥想者静静地坐着，专注于他们的呼吸。

例句：Experienced meditators can reach a deep state of relaxation.  经验丰富的冥想者可以达到深度放松的状态。|近义词：contemplator, thinker, ponderer, reflector|反义词:doer, actor|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *meditator, from Latin meditatus, past participle of meditari (“to meditate”).
