leachers:/ˈliːtʃərz/
n.
寄生虫; 榨取他人利益者; 水蛭; 
People who exploit others for their own gain, especially sexually or financially.  Those who take advantage of a system or resource without contributing. Also, literally, leeches (bloodsucking worms).
The leachers were banned from the website for downloading copyrighted material illegally. 那些榨取他人利益者因为非法下载受版权保护的资料而被禁止访问该网站。
Synonyms: exploiters, parasites, freeloaders, bloodsuckers
Antonyms: contributors, donors, benefactors
noun


leech:/liːtʃ/
n.
水蛭; 榨取他人利益者; 放血者; 
A bloodsucking worm.  Figuratively, someone who exploits others, especially for money.  Historically, also a physician who used leeches in medical practice.
The leech attached itself to my leg while I was swimming in the lake.  当我在湖里游泳时，水蛭附在了我的腿上。
Synonyms: parasite, bloodsucker, freeloader, exploiter
Antonyms: benefactor, donor, contributor
noun

leech:/liːtʃ/
v.
依附并榨取; 勒索; (用医蛭)吸血; 
To cling to and exploit someone, like a leech. To extort or drain resources from someone.  To apply leeches for medical purposes.
He leeched off his wealthy parents for years, refusing to get a job. 他多年来一直依赖他富有的父母，拒绝找工作。
Synonyms: exploit, sponge off, bleed dry, freeload
Antonyms: support, contribute, give
verb
