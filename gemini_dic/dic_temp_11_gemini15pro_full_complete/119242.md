mica:/ˈmaɪkə/| n.|云母|A shiny silicate mineral with a layered structure, found as minute scales in granite and other rocks, or as crystals. It is used as a thermal or electrical insulator.|Mica is used in the electronics industry. 云母用于电子工业。|近义词:isinglass,  vermiculite|反义词:None|noun
