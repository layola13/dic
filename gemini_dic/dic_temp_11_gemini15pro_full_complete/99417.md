interecclesiastical:/ˌɪntərɪˌkliːziˈæstɪkəl/
adjective
教会间的；不同教会之间的
Relating to relations between different churches or denominations.
The interecclesiastical dialogue fostered greater understanding between the two faiths.  教会间的对话促进了两种信仰之间更大的理解。
The conference addressed interecclesiastical relations in the region.  这次会议讨论了该区域不同教会之间的关系。
Synonym: interdenominational, interchurch
Antonym: intraecclesiastical, within a single church
词性: adjective
