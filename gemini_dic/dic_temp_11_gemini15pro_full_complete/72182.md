flophouses:/ˈflɑːphaʊzɪz/
noun
廉价旅馆，低级客栈
A cheap, run-down hotel or rooming house, often frequented by homeless or transient people.
He spent several weeks living in flophouses before finding a stable job. 他在找到一份稳定的工作之前，在廉价旅馆里住了几个星期。
The documentary explored the lives of people living in flophouses in the city. 这部纪录片探讨了生活在这个城市廉价旅馆里的人们的生活。

近义词:dives, fleabags, dosshouses
反义词:luxury hotels, five-star hotels
词性:noun
