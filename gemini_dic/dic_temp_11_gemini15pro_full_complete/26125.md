bustees:/bʌˈstiːz/
n.
贫民窟，棚户区
Slums, shantytowns, particularly those in India.
The bustees of Kolkata are overcrowded and lack basic sanitation. 加尔各答的贫民窟人满为患，缺乏基本的卫生设施。
slums, shantytowns
mansions, suburbs
