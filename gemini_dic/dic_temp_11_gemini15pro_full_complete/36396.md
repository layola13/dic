coinvestor:/ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛstər/| n.|共同投资者|A coinvestor is a person or entity that invests in the same venture as another investor, typically sharing the risks and rewards.|例句：Several coinvestors joined forces to fund the startup.  几个共同投资者联合起来为这家初创公司提供资金。

例句：The company sought coinvestors for its expansion project.  该公司为其扩张项目寻求共同投资者。|近义词：co-investor, joint investor, partner investor|反义词: sole investor|词性:noun
