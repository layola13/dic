kilog:/ˈkiːləʊɡ/| n. |千克，公斤|A unit of mass equal to 1000 grams.  It's the base unit of mass in the International System of Units (SI).|例句：The bag of potatoes weighs two kilogs. 这袋土豆重两公斤。

例句：Please give me one kilog of apples. 请给我一公斤苹果。


近义词：kilogram, kg |反义词: milligram, microgram|词性: noun
