bravado:/brəˈvɑːdoʊ/ (US) /brəˈvɑːdəʊ/ (UK)
n.|虚张声势，逞能|A show of bravery or defiance, often intended to impress or intimidate. |He displayed a false bravado that didn't fool anyone. 他表现出一种虚假的逞强，却没能骗过任何人。|
bravado:|n.|虚张声势，逞能|A swaggering show of boldness intended to impress or intimidate.|She faced the bullies with bravado, even though she was terrified. 即使她很害怕，她还是勇敢地面对恶霸。|
bravado:|近义词|swagger, bluster, boastfulness, braggadocio|
bravado:|反义词|humility, modesty, timidity, meekness|
bravado:|词性|noun|
bravado:|词源|From Italian *bravata*, meaning "bragging, boasting," from *bravo* "brave, wild," from Spanish *bravo*.|
