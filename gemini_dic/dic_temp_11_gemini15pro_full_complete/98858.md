intemperate:/ɪnˈtɛmpərət/ | adj. | (气候)恶劣的 | (of weather) harsh or extreme | The intemperate climate made farming difficult.  恶劣的气候使得耕作困难。| synonyms: harsh, severe, extreme, inclement, wild, stormy, tempestuous, turbulent, violent | antonyms: mild, temperate, clement | adjective
