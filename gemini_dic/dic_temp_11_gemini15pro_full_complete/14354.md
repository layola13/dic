autopathic:/ˌɔːtəˈpæθɪk/ | adj. | 自体疗法的；自体病的 |  Relating to or involving autopathy, a system of medicine in which a disease is treated by doses of the products of the disease itself. |  Autopathic remedies are sometimes used in alternative medicine.  自体疗法有时用于替代医学。 | synonyms: isopathic, homeopathic | antonyms: allopathic, conventional | adjective

autopathic:/ˌɔːtəˈpæθɪk/ | n. | 自体疗法患者；自体病患者 |  A person who practices or undergoes autopathy. | He considers himself an autopathic, relying on his body's own ability to heal. 他认为自己是一个自体疗法者，依靠自身的身体自愈能力。 | synonyms: isopathist, homeopathist | antonyms: allopathist, conventional doctor | noun
