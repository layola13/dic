overbuild: /ˌoʊvərˈbɪld/ | v. | 过度建设；建造过多 | To construct too many buildings in an area or to add too many stories to a building. |  They overbuilt the area with cheap apartments. 他们在该地区过度建造了廉价公寓。 | The developer overbuilt the site, creating a glut of office space. 开发商过度开发了这块地，造成了办公空间过剩。 | 近义词: overdevelop | 反义词: underbuild | 词性: 动词

overbuild: /ˈoʊvərˌbɪld/ | n. | 过度建设；建造过多 | The excessive construction of buildings in an area. | The overbuild of the city center has led to traffic congestion. 市中心的过度建设导致了交通拥堵。 | There's significant overbuild of retail space in the town.  这个镇上的零售空间严重过剩。| 近义词: overdevelopment | 反义词: underdevelopment | 词性: 名词
