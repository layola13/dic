dry-cleanable: /ˌdraɪˈkliːnəb(ə)l/
adjective
可干洗的
Able to be cleaned by dry cleaning.
This silk dress is dry-cleanable. 这件丝绸连衣裙可以干洗。
The coat is expensive, but fortunately it's dry-cleanable.  这件外套很贵，但幸运的是它可以干洗。
近义词: washable, launderable
反义词: non-dry-cleanable, hand-wash only
