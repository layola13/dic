coalface:/ˈkoʊlfeɪs/ (US), /ˈkəʊlfeɪs/ (UK)| n. |煤炭工作面；（喻）艰苦劳动的场所|The area of a coal mine where coal is cut from the seam. Figuratively, it refers to any place where the most challenging or demanding work is done. |例句：He spent years working at the coalface.  他在煤矿工作面工作了多年。

例句：Ministers need to understand the problems faced by teachers at the coalface of education. 部长们需要了解教育第一线教师面临的问题。|近义词：front line, trenches, thick of it |反义词:headquarters, office|noun
