coeditor:/koʊˈɛdɪtər/| n. |共同编辑；合作编辑；合编者|A person who edits something jointly with another or others.|Example: She and her husband are coeditors of the journal. 她和她的丈夫是这本杂志的共同编辑。|Example: The coeditors worked tirelessly to meet the deadline.  这些合作编辑不知疲倦地工作以赶上截止日期。|近义词：joint editor, collaborator|反义词:|n.
