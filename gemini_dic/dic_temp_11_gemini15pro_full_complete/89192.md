homeroom:/ˈhoʊmˌruːm/ (US), /ˈhəʊmˌruːm/ (UK)| n.| 班级会议；导师时间；导师办公室|A classroom where pupils of a particular class or form register at the beginning and end of the school day, often used for other administrative or counselling purposes.|例句：Students must report to their homeroom every morning. 学生们每天早上必须到他们的班级会议室报到。

例句：My homeroom teacher is very supportive. 我的班主任老师非常支持我。

|近义词：classroom, form room, advisory period|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
