fettling:/ˈfɛtlɪŋ/
noun
整理；修整；清理；喷砂清理铸件毛刺
The process of removing excess material, such as sand, metal, or imperfections, from a casting or other object.
The fettling shop is where the castings are cleaned and smoothed.  铸件清理车间是铸件被清理和平滑的地方。
After casting, the metal undergoes a fettling process to remove excess material.  铸造后，金属要经过一个修整过程以去除多余的材料。
finishing, trimming, cleaning, smoothing
roughing
