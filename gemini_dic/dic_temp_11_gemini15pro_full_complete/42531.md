countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ | v. n. | 撤销，取消；相反的命令 | to revoke or cancel (a command, order, etc.); an order revoking a previous one | The general countermanded the attack. 将军取消了进攻。| synonyms: revoke, rescind, cancel, annul, repeal | antonyms: order, command, authorize, approve | verb

countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ | n. | 相反的命令 | an order revoking a previous one |  The supply shipment was halted by a countermand from headquarters.  总部下达了相反的命令，停止了物资运输。| synonyms: revocation, cancellation | antonyms: order, command, authorization, approval | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *contremander, from contre- "against" + mander "to order." Ultimately from Latin mandare "to entrust, order."
