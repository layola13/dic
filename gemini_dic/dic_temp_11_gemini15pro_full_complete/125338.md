multilaterally:/ˌmʌltɪˈlætərəli/| adv.|多边地，多方面地|Involving more than two nations or parties.  Performed by or affecting several parties at once.|例句：The issue was discussed multilaterally by the member states.  成员国对这个问题进行了多边讨论。|例句：They agreed to pursue the matter multilaterally. 他们同意多边地处理此事。|近义词：jointly, collectively, together|反义词:bilaterally, unilaterally|词性:adverb

Etymology->
From multi- (many) + lateral (sided) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many."  "Lateral" comes from Latin "latus" meaning "side."
