overdazzled: /ˌoʊvərˈdæzəld/ | adj. | 目眩的，眼花缭乱的 |  Blinded or confused by too much light or brilliance; overwhelmed by an excess of something dazzling. | The overdazzled audience gasped at the spectacular fireworks display.  烟火表演壮观无比，令眼花缭乱的观众惊叹不已。|  The child, overdazzled by the sheer number of toys, couldn't decide which one to choose.  孩子被琳琅满目的玩具弄得眼花缭乱，不知道该选哪一个。 | Synonyms: dazzled, bewildered, overwhelmed, stunned | Antonyms: unimpressed, unfazed, indifferent | adjective
