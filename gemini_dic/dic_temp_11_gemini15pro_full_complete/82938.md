gumshoed:/ˈɡʌmʃuːd/| v. | 暗中侦察，秘密调查 | To investigate or follow someone discreetly and persistently, like a detective. | The reporter gumshoed the politician, hoping to uncover a scandal. 记者暗中调查这位政治家，希望能发现丑闻。| Synonyms: snoop, pry, investigate, shadow | Antonyms: ignore, neglect | Verb

gumshoeing:/ˈɡʌmʃuːɪŋ/ | n. | 暗中侦察，秘密调查 | The act of investigating or following someone discreetly and persistently. |  His gumshoeing led him to a surprising discovery. 他的秘密调查让他有了惊人的发现。| Synonyms: snooping, prying, investigation, shadowing | Antonyms: ignorance, neglect | Noun

词源：来源于20世纪初美国俚语，指侦探穿的橡胶底鞋，因为这种鞋走起路来悄无声息，适合秘密行动。后来逐渐演变为动词，指像侦探一样秘密调查。
