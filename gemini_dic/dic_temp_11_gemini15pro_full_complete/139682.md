occursion:  This is not a standard English word.  The correct word is **occurrence**.

occurrence: /əˈkʌrəns/ | n. |发生；出现；事件；发生的事情|An instance of something occurring or happening. |例句：The occurrence of earthquakes in this region is frequent.  这个地区地震频发。

例句：It was a strange occurrence. 这是一件奇怪的事情。

近义词：incident, event, happening, phenomenon |反义词: nonoccurrence, absence
词性: noun
