marigold: /ˈmærɪɡoʊld/ (US) /ˈmærɪɡɒld/ (UK)| n. |万寿菊；金盏花|A common garden plant with bright yellow or orange flowers.|例句：The marigolds in her garden were a vibrant splash of color. 她花园里的万寿菊是一片鲜艳的色彩。

例句：She planted marigolds to deter pests in her vegetable patch. 她种植万寿菊以阻止菜地里的害虫。|近义词：calendula (specifically Pot Marigold), flower, blossom|反义词: (No direct antonym for a flower name, could consider concepts like weed or blight in specific contexts)|词性:noun
