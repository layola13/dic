namby-pambiness:/ˈnæmbi ˈpæmbinəs/| n.|幼稚；柔弱；娇气|The state or quality of being namby-pamby; weak, sentimental, and ineffectual character or behavior. |His namby-pambiness irritated her. 他的娇气让她恼火。|近义词：sentimentality, effeminacy, weakness, feebleness|反义词：strength, firmness, decisiveness|词性:名词

词源:
From namby-pamby, a reduplication of the given name Ambrose, used derisively for a weak or effeminate person, especially a man.
