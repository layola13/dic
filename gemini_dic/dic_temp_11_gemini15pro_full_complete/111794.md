loftlike:/ˈlɔːftlaɪk/
adjective
像阁楼一样的
Resembling or suggestive of a loft, typically characterized by high ceilings, open space, and industrial features.
The apartment has a loftlike feel with its exposed brick walls and large windows. 这间公寓有着裸露的砖墙和大窗户，有一种阁楼般的感觉。
airy, spacious, open-plan, industrial-style
cramped, small, closed-in
