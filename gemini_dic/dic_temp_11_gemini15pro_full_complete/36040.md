codle:/ˈkɒdl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːdl/ (US)| v. n.|娇养；溺爱；慢煮|To treat someone very well and protect them too much, or to cook something slowly in liquid just below boiling point.|She coddled her youngest son. 她溺爱她的小儿子。|The eggs were coddled for four minutes. 鸡蛋煮了四分钟。|近义词：pamper, indulge, spoil, baby, cosset|反义词：neglect, ignore|词性：动词, 名词
