awninged:/ˈɔːnɪŋd/
adjective
有遮阳篷的，装有遮阳篷的
Having an awning or awnings.
The awninged patio provided a welcome shade from the afternoon sun.  带遮阳篷的露台提供了下午阳光下的阴凉。
The cafe had awninged tables on the sidewalk.  咖啡馆的人行道上摆放着带遮阳篷的桌子。
近义词:covered, sheltered
反义词:exposed, uncovered
词性:adjective

词源: From awning + -ed.  "Awning" comes from the obsolete French word "auvent" meaning "sailcloth stretched over a doorway or window."
