emulous: /ˈɛmjʊləs/ | adj. | 竞争的；好胜的；求胜心切的 | desiring or ambitious to equal or surpass another; motivated by a spirit of rivalry |  He was emulous of his brother's success. 他对哥哥的成功感到很羡慕，并渴望超越他。 | synonyms: ambitious, aspiring, competitive, envious | antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, unambitious | adjective

emulous: /ˈɛmjʊləs/ | adj. | 仿效的；模仿的 | imitative | The young writer's style was clearly emulous of Hemingway.  这位年轻作家的风格明显是在模仿海明威。 | synonyms: imitative, copying | antonyms: original, innovative | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin aemulus (“rivaling, emulous”), from aemulari (“to rival”).
