long-sighted:/ˌlɒŋˈsaɪtɪd/ /ˌlɔːŋˈsaɪtɪd/| adj.|远视的；有远见的|Able to see things clearly that are far away but not things that are close; showing foresight.|例句：She's long-sighted and needs glasses to read. 她远视，需要戴眼镜才能阅读。|近义词：far-sighted, prescient, visionary|反义词：short-sighted, myopic, nearsighted|词性:形容词
long-sighted:/ˌlɒŋˈsaɪtɪd/ /ˌlɔːŋˈsaɪtɪd/| n.|远视的人|A person who can see distant objects more clearly than near ones.|例句：He's a long-sighted and can see the mountains clearly. 他是个远视眼，能清楚地看到远处的山。|近义词：far-sighted person|反义词：short-sighted person, myopic person|词性:名词

词源:  From long + sighted.  "Sighted" comes from the Old English "gesīht" meaning "sight, vision."
