overarousals:/ˌoʊvərəˈraʊzəlz/| n. |过度兴奋，过度唤醒|A state of excessive physiological and psychological arousal, often associated with conditions like PTSD and insomnia.  It can manifest as anxiety, panic attacks, or difficulty sleeping.|  Night terrors and sleepwalking are common signs of overarousals in children. 夜惊和梦游是儿童过度兴奋的常见迹象。|  He experienced overarousals after the traumatic event.  他在创伤事件后经历了过度兴奋。|近义词：hyperexcitability, overstimulation|反义词：calm, relaxation|词性: Noun (plural)

词源: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "arousal" (noun from "arouse," meaning to awaken or excite).  "Arouse" comes from the Middle English "arousen," from Anglo-French "arouser," ultimately from Latin "ad-" (to) + "rogare" (to ask).
