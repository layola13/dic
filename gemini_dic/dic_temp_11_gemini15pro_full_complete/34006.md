cited: /ˈsaɪtɪd/
verb
引用，引证
to mention something as proof for a theory or as a reason why something has happened
The judge cited a previous case to support his argument. 法官引用了以前的案例来支持他的论点。
synonyms: quoted, referenced, mentioned
antonyms: ignored, omitted
词性: 动词


Etymology:
From Latin citatus, past participle of citare "to summon, urge, call, put in sudden motion, rouse," frequentative of ciere "to set in motion, stir, shake, excite."
