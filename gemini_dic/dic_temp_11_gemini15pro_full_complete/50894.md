desertifying:/dɪˈzɜːrtɪfaɪɪŋ/
v.
使沙漠化; 使(土地)变成沙漠
The process of desertifying fertile land can have devastating consequences.  肥沃的土地沙漠化进程会造成毁灭性的后果。
The unsustainable farming practices are desertifying the region. 不可持续的耕作方式正在使该地区沙漠化。
近义词:aridifying,  desiccating
反义词:reforesting,  irrigating
词性:动词(verb)

desert:/ˈdezərt/
n. 沙漠
vt. 遗弃；放弃
vi. 擅离职守；逃亡
The Sahara Desert is vast and unforgiving. 撒哈拉沙漠广阔无垠，环境恶劣。
He deserted his family and fled the country. 他抛弃了家人，逃离了这个国家。
近义词:wasteland,  barren
反义词:oasis,  paradise
词性:名词(noun), 动词(verb)
