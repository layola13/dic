drongo: /ˈdrɒŋɡəʊ/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːŋɡoʊ/ (US) | n. | 鴗| A songbird found in Africa, Asia, and Australia, known for its intelligence and sometimes aggressive behavior. It's also used informally to refer to a fool or a stupid person. | The drongo mimicked the calls of other birds perfectly.  鴗完美地模仿了其他鸟类的叫声。

The guide pointed out a spangled drongo perched on a branch.  导游指着栖息在树枝上的一只闪亮的鴗。| Synonym: fool, idiot, simpleton (informal) | Antonym: genius, clever person | noun
