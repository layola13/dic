overofficiousness:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɪʃəsnəs/| n. | 过于殷勤；多管闲事 | Excessive officiousness; being too eager to offer unwanted help or advice. | Example: His overofficiousness annoyed the other guests, who felt he was interfering. 他的多管闲事惹恼了其他客人，他们觉得他很爱管闲事。

Example:  The overofficiousness of the waiter made them feel uncomfortable. 服务员过分的殷勤让他们感到不舒服。| 近义词：intrusiveness, meddlesomeness, interference | 反义词:  reserve, discretion, aloofness | 词性: noun
