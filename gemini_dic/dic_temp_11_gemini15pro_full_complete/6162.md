amigo:/əˈmiːɡoʊ/ (US), /əˈmiːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. |朋友，伙伴|A friend or companion, especially a male friend.  This word is borrowed from Spanish.|He met his amigo at the cafe for a coffee. 他和他的朋友在咖啡馆见了面喝咖啡。|Synonyms: friend, pal, buddy, mate, companion|Antonyms: enemy, foe|Noun
