dacoit:/dəˈkɔɪt/| n.|土匪，强盗|A member of a band of armed robbers, especially in India or Burma (Myanmar).|例句：The dacoits terrorized the villages, stealing livestock and valuables. 土匪恐吓村庄，偷窃牲畜和贵重物品。

例句：Stories of dacoit bravery and cunning are part of the folklore of the region.  关于土匪勇敢和狡猾的故事是该地区民间传说的一部分。|近义词：bandit, robber, outlaw, brigand|反义词:lawman, policeman|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi  डाकू (ḍākū), from Sanskrit  दस्यु (dasyu), meaning "robber," "enemy."
