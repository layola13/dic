disinterestedness:/dɪsˈɪntrɪstɪdnəs/
|n.| 公正无私；客观；不偏不倚 |The judge showed complete disinterestedness in the case.  法官对此案表现出完全的公正无私。| The scientist approached the experiment with disinterestedness, ensuring objective results.  科学家以客观的态度进行实验，确保结果的客观性。| impartiality, objectivity, neutrality | bias, partiality, prejudice | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (not) + interested + -ness (forming nouns of quality or state).  The prefix "dis-" negates the meaning of "interested," leading to the sense of being unbiased.
