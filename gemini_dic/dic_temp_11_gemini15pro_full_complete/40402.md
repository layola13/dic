contraindicated:/ˌkɑːntrəˈɪndɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. |禁忌的，不适宜的 |Not recommended or safe to use (in a particular situation, typically due to a patient's medical condition). | Example: The drug is contraindicated in patients with liver disease.  这种药物对肝病患者是禁忌的。| Example:  Surgery is contraindicated in patients with severe heart conditions.  对于有严重心脏病的患者，手术是禁忌的。| Synonym: inadvisable, unadvisable, not recommended, proscribed, prohibited | Antonym: indicated, advisable, recommended | adjective
