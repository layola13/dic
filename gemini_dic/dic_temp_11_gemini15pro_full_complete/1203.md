accommodable:/əˈkɒmədeɪbəl/
adjective
可容纳的；可适应的；可调和的
(of someone's character or nature) easily altered to suit other people's needs or desires; adaptable.
例句：She has a very accommodable nature. 她性格随和。

例句：The schedule is accommodable to our needs.  这个时间表可以根据我们的需要进行调整。

近义词：adaptable, flexible, accommodating, obliging, compliant
反义词：inflexible, rigid, unaccommodating, stubborn
词性: adjective


accommodating:/əˈkɒmədeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
乐于助人的；肯通融的；随和的
(of a person) willing to fit in with someone's wishes or needs.
例句：He was very accommodating and allowed us to check in early. 他非常通融，允许我们提前入住。

例句：The hotel staff were very accommodating and helpful. 酒店员工非常乐于助人。

近义词：helpful, obliging, cooperative, considerate, kind
反义词：unhelpful, inconsiderate, uncooperative
词性: adjective


词源:
From late 16th century: from accommodate + -able.
