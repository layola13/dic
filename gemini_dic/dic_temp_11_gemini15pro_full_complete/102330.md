ivorytype: /ˈaɪvəriˌtaɪp/ | n. |  象牙版画；象牙版画印刷术 | A photographic print made on a thin sheet of ivory, popular in the mid-19th century.  |  Example: The museum displayed a collection of delicate ivorytypes from the Victorian era.  博物馆展出了一系列维多利亚时代的精美象牙版画。

Example:  Ivorytypes were prized for their sharpness and detail. 象牙版画因其清晰度和细节而备受珍视。 | Synonym:  photograph, print, daguerreotype, ambrotype, tintype | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps) negative, digital image | noun
