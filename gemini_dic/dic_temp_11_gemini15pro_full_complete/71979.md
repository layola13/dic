flimsinesses:/ˈflɪmzɪnɪsɪz/
n.
脆弱, 薄弱, 肤浅
the state or quality of being flimsy; weakness or instability.
The flimsinesses of the evidence made it difficult to prove the case.  这些证据的薄弱使得难以证明这个案件。
frailty, weakness, instability, fragility
strength, robustness, solidity, sturdiness
noun
