egrimony:/ˈɛɡrɪmɔni/ | n. | 悲痛，悲伤；痛苦 |  Intense grief or sorrow, usually caused by bereavement. |  She was overcome with egrimony after the death of her husband. 她丈夫去世后，她悲痛欲绝。| Synonyms: grief, sorrow, bereavement, distress | Antonyms: joy, happiness, elation | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *egrimonie* (modern French *acrimonie*), from Latin *aegrimonia* "grief, sorrow, vexation, affliction," from *aeger* "sick, ill, distressed."
