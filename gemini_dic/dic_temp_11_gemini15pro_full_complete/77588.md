gee:/dʒiː/ | int. | 哎呀; (表示惊讶、赞叹、不耐烦等) | An exclamation expressing surprise, admiration, or impatience. | Gee, that's a big cake! 哎呀，这蛋糕真大! | 近义词: wow, gosh, my goodness | 反义词:  (interjections generally don't have antonyms) | 词性:感叹词

gee:/dʒiː/ | n. | (旧) 马; (用于命令马向右转) | (dated) A horse; (used as a command to a horse to turn right). | The old farmer yelled "Gee!" to his horse. 老农夫对他的马大喊“驾！”(向右转) | 近义词: horse (as a noun) | 反义词: haw (as a command to turn left) | 词性:名词
