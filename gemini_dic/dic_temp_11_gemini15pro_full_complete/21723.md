boarer:/ˈbɔːrər/| n.|生育者，生产者；令人厌烦的人|A boarer is someone or something that produces or yields something, often in abundance. It can also refer to someone who is tedious or boring. |The farmer prized his boarer sows for their large litters. |Synonyms: producer, bearer, creator, generator; bore, dullard|Antonyms: consumer, destroyer; entertainer|noun
