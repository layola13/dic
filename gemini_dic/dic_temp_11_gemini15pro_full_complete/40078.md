|n.|传染性，感染性|The contagiousnesses of several diseases were discussed at the conference. 会议上讨论了几种疾病的传染性。|
|The high contagiousness of the new virus strain is alarming.  这种新病毒株的高传染性令人担忧。|
|Synonyms: infectiousness, communicability|
|Antonyms: non-contagiousness, resistance|
|noun|


Etymology:
From contagious + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *contagiosus* ("touching,") from *contingere* ("to touch closely").
