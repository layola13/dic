anniversary: /ˌænɪˈvɜːsəri/ (US) /ˌænɪˈvɜːs(ə)ri/ (UK)| n. |周年纪念日|The date on which an event took place in a previous year; the day on which a special event is celebrated each year.|例句：Today is their tenth wedding anniversary. 今天是他们结婚十周年纪念日。

例句：The company is celebrating its 25th anniversary this year.  这家公司今年庆祝其成立25周年。


近义词：celebration, commemoration, jubilee|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
