flectionless:/ˈflɛkʃənˌləs/| adj.|无屈折的，不变形的|Lacking inflection; not changing form. |The flectionless words in Chinese grammar pose challenges for learners accustomed to inflected languages.  汉语语法中不变形的词对习惯于屈折语的学习者来说构成挑战。|The robot's flectionless voice made it sound unnatural.  机器人的不变形的声音使它听起来不自然。|invariable, unchanging, uninflected|inflected, variable, changing|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From *flection* (a variant spelling of *inflection*) + *-less*.  *Inflection* comes from Late Latin *inflexio*, from Latin *inflectere* "to bend, curve," from *in-* "in, into" + *flectere* "to bend." The suffix *-less* is an Old English suffix meaning "without."
