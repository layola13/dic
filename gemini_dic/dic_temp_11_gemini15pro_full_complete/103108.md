jerid:/ˈdʒɛrɪd/| n.|标枪，掷枪 (尤指北非的一种轻标枪)|A light wooden javelin used in North Africa, especially in equestrian exercises.|The skilled horseman hurled the jerid with great accuracy.  熟练的骑手以极高的精准度投掷标枪。|Synonyms: javelin, lance, spear|Antonyms: shield, armor|noun

jerid:/ˈdʒɛrɪd/| n.|马上枪术比赛|A game or sport involving the throwing of jerids from horseback.|Jerid was a popular pastime among the Berber tribes. 马上枪术比赛是柏柏尔部落中流行的娱乐活动。|Synonyms: equestrian game, mounted sport|Antonyms: N/A|noun
