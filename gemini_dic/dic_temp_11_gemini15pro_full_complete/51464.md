detonators: /ˈdɛtəneɪtərz/ (US), /ˈdɛtəneɪtəz/ (UK) | n. | 雷管; 起爆器 | A device used to trigger an explosion, typically by causing a small explosion that in turn detonates a larger explosive charge. | The miners carefully placed the detonators in the holes drilled into the rock face. 矿工们小心翼翼地将雷管放入钻入岩壁的孔中。

The demolition crew attached detonators to the support beams of the old building.  拆迁人员将雷管连接到旧建筑的支撑梁上。

| 近义词: fuse, primer, trigger | 反义词:  None (Detonators initiate explosions; there isn't a direct opposite for this action.) | 词性: Noun
