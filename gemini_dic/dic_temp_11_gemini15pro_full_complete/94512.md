importuner:/ˌɪmpɔːrˈtjuːnər/ | n. | 纠缠不休的人；不断请求的人 | A person who makes repeated or persistent requests, especially in a way that is annoying or troublesome. | He was known as an importuner, always bothering people for favors. 他总是缠着别人帮忙，被认为是一个纠缠不休的人。|  The importuner kept asking for money, even after being repeatedly refused.  即使被反复拒绝，这个纠缠不休的人仍然不断地要钱。 | synonyms: beggar, pest, solicitant, supplicant | antonyms: benefactor, donor, giver | noun
