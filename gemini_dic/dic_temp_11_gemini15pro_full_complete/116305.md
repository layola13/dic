mast:/mæst/ | n. |桅杆；天线杆；旗杆；坚果 |A tall, vertical pole or spar on a ship or boat, used to support sails, flags, or other equipment. Also, a tall metal tower used to support an antenna for radio, television, or mobile phone signals, or a similar structure for a flagpole. Also, the edible nuts of forest trees such as beech and oak. |The ship's mast was broken in the storm. 这艘船的桅杆在暴风雨中断了。| synonym: pole, spar, antenna, aerial | antonym:  (no direct antonym, perhaps base, foundation) | noun


mast:/mæst/ | n. | (尤指猪、牛的) 果实, 坚果 | The nuts of forest trees (especially beech and oak) that have fallen to the ground, used as food for pigs and other animals. | The pigs foraged for mast in the woods. 猪在树林里觅食坚果。| synonym: nuts, acorns, beechnuts | antonym: (no direct antonym) | noun
