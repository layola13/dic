isohalsine:/ˌaɪsoʊˈhælˌsaɪn/ | n. |等盐线；等盐度线|A line on a map or chart connecting points of equal salinity.  It represents areas in a body of water with the same salt concentration.|Isohalsines are drawn on oceanographic charts to illustrate the distribution of salinity. 等盐线绘制在海洋学图表上，以说明盐度的分布。

The isohalsines show a distinct pattern of increasing salinity towards the equator. 等盐线显示出盐度向赤道递增的明显模式。|近义词: isohaline (adjective form)|反义词: None|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From iso- (Greek: "equal") + hals- (Greek: "salt") + -ine (suffix forming nouns).
