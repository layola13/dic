beechier:/ˈbiːtʃɪər/ | adj. | 更像山毛榉的；山毛榉更多的 |  More resembling a beech tree, especially in having smoother bark or denser foliage.  | The second tree was beechier, with smoother, grey bark. 第二棵树更像山毛榉，树皮更光滑，呈灰色。|  The north side of the hill was beechier than the south.  山北侧比南侧山毛榉树更多。| 近义词:  None (This is a comparative adjective, synonyms would depend on the context) | 反义词: None (Comparative adjective) | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From beech + -ier (comparative suffix).  源自 beech（山毛榉） + -ier（比较级后缀）。
