isotropy:/aɪˈsɒtrəpi/ |n.| 各向同性；等向性 | The property of being identical in all directions.  A material with isotropy exhibits the same properties regardless of the direction of measurement. | The universe, on a large scale, exhibits isotropy.  宇宙在大尺度上表现出各向同性。

The isotropy of the material makes it ideal for this application. 这种材料的各向同性使其非常适合这种应用。| synonym: uniformity, homogeneity | antonym: anisotropy |noun
