crudded:/ˈkrʌdɪd/
verb
弄脏, 使污秽; 涂满油脂; 包上油脂;
covered with or made up of crud
例句：The engine was crudded with oil. 引擎上沾满了油。

例句：His boots were crudded with mud. 他的靴子上沾满了泥。

近义词：dirty, soiled, grimy, filthy
反义词：clean, spotless, pristine
词性：v.
