dejeuner:/deʒøne/ | n. | 午餐 |  The midday meal, lunch.  Derived from the Old French disner, meaning "to break one's fast," related to the modern French dîner (dinner).  Originally the main meal of the day, its meaning shifted as dinner became more prominent. | We had a delightful déjeuner at a charming bistro. 我们在一家迷人的小酒馆吃了一顿愉快的午餐。| synonym: lunch, midday meal | antonym: dinner, supper | noun
