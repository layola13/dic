babblers:/ˈbæblərz/ (US), /ˈbæbləz/ (UK)| n.| 喋喋不休的人，饶舌的人；（鸟类）噪鹛|A person who talks incessantly and foolishly; a bird of the Timaliidae family, known for their vocalizations.|The babblers in the office made it difficult to concentrate. 办公室里喋喋不休的人让我很难集中注意力。|Synonyms: chatterboxes, gossips|Antonyms: quiet people, introverts|noun
