mechanizing:/ˈmɛkəˌnaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使机械化, 机械化
The process of introducing machines or automatic devices to replace manual labor or animal power.
例句：The company is mechanizing its production line to increase efficiency.  这家公司正在使其生产线机械化以提高效率。

例句：Mechanizing agriculture led to increased crop yields.  农业机械化导致作物产量增加。

近义词：automating, modernizing
反义词：manualizing

词性:v.
