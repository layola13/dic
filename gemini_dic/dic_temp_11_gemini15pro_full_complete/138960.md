/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) |pron.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something.|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|Which, who |None|Pronoun

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she study at university? = 她在大学学习什么专业？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she bought at the market? = 她在市场买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开前做了什么？
What had they eaten before I arrived? = 在我到达之前他们吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 在她换专业之前她一直在学习什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will she bring to the picnic? = 她会带什么去野餐？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 到今天结束时你将完成什么？
What will she have achieved by next year? = 到明年她将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
