overfaintness: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪntnəs/
noun
过度虚弱, 过度昏厥
An excessive state of faintness or weakness.
She experienced a sudden overfaintness and had to sit down. 她突然感到过度虚弱，不得不坐下。
The heat and humidity contributed to her overfaintness.  高温和潮湿导致她过度虚弱。
近义词: weakness, faintness, dizziness, lightheadedness
反义词: strength, vigor, robustness
词性: noun

Etymology:
"Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "faintness" (from "faint" (Old French faint, feint, from Latin fingere "to form, shape, invent, feign") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)).
