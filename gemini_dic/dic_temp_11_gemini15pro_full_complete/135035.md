nonnihilism:/ˌnɒnˈnaɪɪlɪzəm/| n. |非虚无主义|The philosophy that not all values are baseless and that existence is not meaningless.  It rejects nihilism. |After years of cynicism, she embraced nonnihilism, finding meaning in human connection and the pursuit of knowledge.|近义词：existentialism, optimism, humanism|反义词:nihilism, pessimism|名词
