metapectin:/ˈmetəˌpektɪn/ | n. | 变性果胶 | A modified form of pectin, usually produced by chemical treatment, resulting in altered properties such as solubility and gelling capacity. | Example: Metapectin is often used in food processing as a thickening agent.  变性果胶通常在食品加工中用作增稠剂。

Example: The addition of metapectin improved the texture of the jam. 添加变性果胶改善了果酱的质地。 | 近义词: modified pectin, treated pectin | 反义词: native pectin, protopectin | 词性: noun
