nonsuperimposable:/ˌnɒnˌsuːpərɪmˈpəʊzəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˌsuːpərɪmˈpoʊzəbl/
adjective
不可重叠的
Not able to be superimposed.  Two objects are nonsuperimposable if one cannot be placed on top of the other so that all parts coincide.
The two mirror image forms of a chiral molecule are nonsuperimposable.  手性分子的两种镜像形式是不可重叠的。
Near synonyms: asymmetric, chiral
Antonyms: superimposable, achiral
词性: adjective
