hamming:/ˈhæmɪŋ/ | n. | 海明码，汉明码 | A method for detecting and correcting errors in data transmission.  It involves adding extra bits to a message to identify and correct single-bit errors. |  Example: Hamming codes are used in computer memory and data storage devices. 海明码用于计算机内存和数据存储设备中。

Example: The use of Hamming codes ensures data integrity during transmission. 使用海明码可确保传输过程中的数据完整性。

| 近义词: error correction code, ECC | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


hamming distance:/ˈhæmɪŋ ˈdɪstəns/ | n. | 海明距离 | The number of positions at which the corresponding symbols are different in two strings of equal length. It is used in information theory, coding theory, and cryptography. | Example: The Hamming distance between "karolin" and "kathrin" is 3.  “karolin”和“kathrin”之间的海明距离是3。

Example: Hamming distance is a crucial concept in error detection and correction. 海明距离是错误检测和纠正中的一个关键概念。


| 近义词: edit distance | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
