neothalamus: /ˌniːoʊˈθæləməs/ | n. | 新丘脑 |  The neothalamus is a part of the thalamus, a structure in the brain that relays sensory and motor signals to the cerebral cortex. It is involved in higher-level processing of information. | The neothalamus plays a crucial role in cognitive functions. 新丘脑在认知功能中起着至关重要的作用。| The damage to the neothalamus can result in sensory deficits. 对新丘脑的损害会导致感觉缺陷。| synonyms: dorsal thalamus | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, as it is a specific brain structure) | noun
