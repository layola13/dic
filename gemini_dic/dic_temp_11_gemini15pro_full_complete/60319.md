egocentric:/ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. | 自我中心的；以自我为中心的 | Thinking only of oneself, without regard for the feelings or desires of others; self-centered. | He was so egocentric that he couldn't see things from anyone else's perspective. 他太以自我为中心了，以至于无法从别人的角度看待事物。 |  She has an egocentric worldview, believing she's the center of the universe. 她有一个以自我为中心的世界观，认为自己是宇宙的中心。 | 近义词: self-centered, narcissistic, self-absorbed | 反义词: altruistic, selfless, other-centered | 词性: adjective

egotistical:/ˌiːɡoʊˈtɪstɪkl/ | adj. | 自负的；自大的 | Excessively conceited or absorbed in oneself; self-important. |  His egotistical behavior alienated his friends. 他自负的行为疏远了他的朋友。 | The egotistical actor constantly boasted about his awards.  这位自负的演员不断吹嘘他的奖项。| 近义词: conceited, arrogant, vain | 反义词: humble, modest, unassuming | 词性: adjective


词源:  From the Latin ego ("I") + Greek kentron ("center").
