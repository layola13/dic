auen:/ˈaʊən/ | n. | 水边草地，河边草地 |  Low-lying meadow, especially by a river. |  The cattle grazed peacefully in the auen. 牛群在河边草地上平静地吃草。 | synonym: meadow, pasture, water-meadow | antonym: upland, hill | noun

词源: From German *Auen*, plural of *Aue* "water meadow, river meadow, island," from Proto-Germanic *awjō* "island, land near water."
