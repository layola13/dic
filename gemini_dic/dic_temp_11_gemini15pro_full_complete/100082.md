interpollinates:/ˌɪntərˈpɒləneɪts/
verb
异花授粉，互相授粉
To transfer pollen from one flower to another, especially between different plants or species.
The bees interpollinate the flowers in the garden. 蜜蜂给花园里的花朵异花授粉。
近义词: cross-pollinate, hybridize
反义词: self-pollinate
词性:v.
