benevolently:/bəˈnevələntli/
adverb
仁慈地；善意地；乐善好施地
in a kind and well-meaning way
The king smiled benevolently upon his subjects. 国王仁慈地对他的臣民微笑。
kindly, charitably, generously
malevolently, cruelly, harshly
词性: 副词
