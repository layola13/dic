nonnegations:/ˌnɒnɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. |非否定; 非否定式|  Statements or expressions that do not contain negations (words like "not," "never," "no").  It often refers to mathematical expressions that exclude negative values. |  The theorem holds for all nonnegations.  该定理适用于所有非否定式。| synonyms: affirmations, positive statements | antonyms: negations, denials | noun
