hackest:/ˈhækɪst/| v.|你非法入侵 (第二人称单数现在时)|This form is archaic and rarely used.  It's the second person singular present tense form of the verb "hack," meaning you are currently engaging in hacking.  It carries a sense of illicit access to computer systems.|Thou hackest the king's servers! 你非法入侵国王的服务器！(古英语用法，非常罕见)|近义词:intrude, breach, crack|反义词:protect, secure|词性:动词

PHRASE->
(由于"hackest"是古英语，很少用于现代英语，因此以下搭配均使用"hack")
hack into = 入侵
hack around =  乱搞，瞎弄
hack (something) together =  草草拼凑
hack (something) off =  砍掉
hack (one's way through) =  开辟道路


注意事项:

“hackest” 极其罕见，一般只会在模仿古英语或极少数方言中出现。 在现代英语中，用 “hack” 的其他形式代替它会更自然。
