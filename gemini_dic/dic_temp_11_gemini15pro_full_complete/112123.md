longulite:/ˈlɒŋɡjʊlaɪt/| n.|长柱状矿物（尤指锥辉石）|A mineral, especially augite, occurring in long prismatic crystals.|The geologist identified the longulite as a form of augite. 地质学家将这种长柱状矿物鉴定为锥辉石的一种。|Near synonyms: prism, crystal|Antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct opposite for a specific mineral formation)|noun
