gibibit:/ˈɡɪbiˌbɪt/| n.|吉比特|A unit of information equal to 2^30 (1,073,741,824) bits, using the binary prefix gibi.  It is often used to measure computer memory or storage capacity.|My new hard drive has a capacity of one gibibit.|近义词：gigabyte (often used interchangeably, but technically different)|反义词: N/A|noun
