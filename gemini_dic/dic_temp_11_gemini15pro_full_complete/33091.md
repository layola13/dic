christcross:/ˈkrɪsˌkrɒs/ /ˈkrɪsˌkrɔːs/ | n. adj. adv.| 十字交叉;纵横交错 |A pattern of intersecting lines forming squares or diamonds; marked with crossing lines. | The architect designed a christcross pattern on the building's facade. 建筑师在大楼的正面设计了一个十字交叉的图案。 | The children played christcross on the playground. 孩子们在操场上玩画格子跳的游戏。 |  近义词: crisscross, crosswise, intersecting | 反义词: parallel, straight | 词性: 名词, 形容词, 副词

Etymology:  From Christ + cross, referring to the marking of a cross, particularly in religious contexts.  Over time, the term broadened to include any intersecting lines forming a cross-like shape.
