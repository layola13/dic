negligence:/ˈneɡlɪdʒəns/| n.|疏忽，过失|The accident was caused by his negligence.  事故是由于他的疏忽造成的。|Failure to take proper care in doing something.|例句：His negligence resulted in a serious accident. 他的疏忽导致了一起严重的事故。

例句：The doctor was sued for medical negligence.  这个医生因医疗事故被起诉了。

近义词：carelessness, neglect, inattention, dereliction|反义词：carefulness, diligence, attention|词性：名词
