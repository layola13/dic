inunctuosity:/ɪnˌʌŋktʃuˈɑːsəti/ | n. |油腻；油滑；奉承；油嘴滑舌 |  Excessive oiliness or greasiness, especially in speech or manner; excessive flattery or insincerity. | His inunctuosity made it difficult to trust him. 他的油嘴滑舌让人很难相信他。 | 近义词:unctuousness, oleaginousness, smarm, slickness | 反义词: sincerity, genuineness, forthrightness | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inunctuositas, from Latin inunctus (“anointed, smeared”), past participle of inungere (“to anoint, smear”), from in- (“in, on”) + ungere (“to anoint”).
