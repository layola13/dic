half-syllabled:/ˌhɑːf ˈsɪləbəld/
adjective
半音节的;含糊不清的
uttered or spoken in a hesitant or unclear way, as if only half of each syllable is pronounced.
He muttered a half-syllabled apology. 他含糊不清地咕哝了一句道歉。
近义词: mumbled, muttered, inarticulate
反义词: articulate, clear, distinct
词性: adjective
