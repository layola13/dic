intermediate:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiət/ | adj. n.| 中间的，中级的；中间体，媒介物 | Lying or situated between two extremes, especially in time, space, or degree. Also, a person who acts as a link between people or things. | He enrolled in an intermediate English course. 他报名参加了一个中级英语课程。| synonyms: middle, medium, average, halfway | antonyms: extreme, initial, final | adjective

intermediate:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiət/ | n. | 中间体，媒介物 | A person who acts as a link between people or things, or a substance formed during a chemical reaction that is not the final product. | The reaction produces several intermediates. 该反应产生了几种中间体。| synonyms: mediator, intermediary, go-between | antonyms: N/A | noun
