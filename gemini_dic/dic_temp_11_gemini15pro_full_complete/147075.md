overliberal:/ˌoʊvərˈlɪbərəl/| adj.| 过于自由主义的；过分开明的|Excessively liberal; favoring extensive social and political reforms.|例句：His overliberal views alienated many of the more conservative members of the party. 他的过分自由主义的观点疏远了党内许多较为保守的成员。

例句：Some critics considered the government's policies overliberal and fiscally irresponsible. 一些批评家认为政府的政策过于自由，财政上不负责任。|近义词：ultra-liberal, radical, progressive|反义词：conservative, reactionary, right-wing|词性:形容词
