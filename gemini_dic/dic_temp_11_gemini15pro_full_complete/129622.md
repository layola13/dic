non-consequential:/ˌnɒnˌkɒnsɪˈkwenʃəl/| adj.|无关紧要的，不重要的|Not important or significant, having no consequence.|Her opinion on the matter was deemed non-consequential.|近义词:insignificant, unimportant, trivial, inconsequential|反义词:significant, important, consequential|词性:形容词
词源: From non- + consequential.  "Consequential" comes from Late Latin *consequentia*, from Latin consequi "to follow," from com- "together" + sequi "to follow."
