multigenic:/ˌmʌltɪˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 多基因的 | Relating to or determined by several different genes. | Example: Height is a multigenic trait, influenced by many genes. 身高是一个多基因性状，受许多基因的影响。| Example: Multigenic diseases are more complex to study than single-gene disorders. 多基因疾病比单基因疾病研究起来更复杂。 | Near synonyms: polygenic, pleiotropic | Antonym: monogenic | word type: adjective
