dactylopterous:/ˌdæktɪˈlɒptərəs/
adjective
指翼鱼的；有指状鳍的
Having finger-like fins or wings.
例句：The dactylopterous fish glided along the ocean floor.  指翼鱼沿着海底滑翔。

例句：Scientists are studying the unique swimming mechanism of dactylopterous species. 科学家正在研究指翼鱼物种独特的游泳机制。

近义词:finger-finned
反义词:(None -  lacks a direct antonym;  one might consider fish *without* specialized fins)
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
