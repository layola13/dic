jaywalkers:/ˈdʒeɪˌwɔːkərz/| n. |乱穿马路的人，行人违章者|A person who crosses a street carelessly or illegally, especially by not using a crosswalk.|Jaywalkers risk their lives and the safety of drivers. 乱穿马路的人危及自身生命和他人的安全。|Near synonyms: pedestrian violators, scofflaws|Antonym: law-abiding pedestrian|noun
