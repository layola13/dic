misemployment:/ˌmɪsɪmˈplɔɪmənt/
|n.|
|错位就业，未能充分发挥才能的职业|
|The state of being employed in a job that does not make full use of one's skills and abilities.|
|Many graduates are facing misemployment due to the tough job market. 许多毕业生由于就业市场不景气而面临着错位就业。|

|He felt a sense of misemployment in his current role. 他感觉自己在目前的职位上没有充分发挥自己的才能。|

|近义词: underemployment, improper placement|
|反义词: suitable employment, proper placement|
|词性: noun|
