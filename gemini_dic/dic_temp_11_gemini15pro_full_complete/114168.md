mahomentanize: /məˈhoʊmɪntənaɪz/
verb
使...短暂存在；使...稍纵即逝
to make something last only a very short time; to make something fleeting.
The artist attempted to mahomentanize the fleeting beauty of a sunset in his painting.  这位艺术家试图在他的画作中捕捉日落稍纵即逝的美。
The fireworks mahomentanized the night sky with bursts of color.  烟花使夜空短暂地绽放出 bursts of color。
近义词: ephemeralize
反义词: eternalize, perpetuate
词性: verb
