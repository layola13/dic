abatised:/əˈbeɪtɪzd/
adjective
降低了的，减弱了的
Reduced in intensity or degree; lessened.
The storm has abatised, and the sun is starting to peek through the clouds.  暴风雨减弱了，太阳开始从云层中探出头来。
The pain in his leg had abatised considerably after taking the medication. 服药后，他腿上的疼痛明显减轻了。
近义词: diminished, lessened, reduced, subsided, mitigated
反义词: increased, intensified, aggravated, worsened
词性: adjective

词源:  From the French *abatir* "to cast down, demolish," from *a-* (from Latin *ad-*) "to" + *batir* "to build."  The English word "abate" retains this sense of lowering or reducing.  "Abatised" is the past participle form of "abate," used adjectivally.
