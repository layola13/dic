disvelop: /dɪsˈvɛləp/
verb
(This word is likely a misspelling of "develop")
发展，开发，培养，冲洗（照片）
To grow or cause to grow and become more mature, advanced, or elaborate.
例句：
She worked hard to develop her skills.  她努力提升自己的技能。

He developed the film in the darkroom. 他在暗室里冲洗了胶卷。

近义词：grow, evolve, progress, mature, expand, advance, cultivate, enhance
反义词：decline, regress, deteriorate, worsen, shrink, diminish, stunt
