evaginate:/iˈvædʒɪneɪt/
verb
外翻，翻出
to turn something inside out so that the inner surface becomes the outer surface; to cause something to do this.
The lining of the uterus evaginates after delivery of the placenta. 子宫内膜在胎盘娩出后外翻。
The petals of the flower evaginated as it bloomed. 花朵盛开时，花瓣向外翻卷。
