half-shut:/ˌhɑːf ˈʃʌt/ /ˌhæf ˈʃʌt/| adj.| 半闭的，半掩的 | Partly closed, but not completely. | The curtains were half-shut, letting in a sliver of light. 窗帘半掩着，透进一丝光线。|  She kept her eyes half-shut, pretending to be asleep. 她半闭着眼睛，假装睡着了。 | Near synonyms:  ajar, partly open | Antonyms: wide open, fully open | word property: adjective
