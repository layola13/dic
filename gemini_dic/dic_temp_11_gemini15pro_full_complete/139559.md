obtusely:/əbˈtjuːsli/ /ɑːbˈtuːsli/
adverb
迟钝地；愚笨地；不敏锐地
In a manner that is slow to understand or perceive; lacking sharpness or quickness of intellect.  It can also describe an angle greater than 90 degrees but less than 180 degrees in geometry. However, this usage is less common in general language.
He obtusely missed the obvious clues in the mystery novel. 他愚笨地错过了推理小说中明显的线索。
The detective obtusely overlooked the crucial piece of evidence.  侦探愚笨地忽略了关键的证据。
Synonym: stupidly, dully, insensitively, bluntly
Antonym: sharply, keenly, astutely, perceptively
词性: 副词
