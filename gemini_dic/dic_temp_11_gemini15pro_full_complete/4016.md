ahistoricity:/ˌeɪhɪstəˈrɪsɪti/
n.
非历史性；脱离历史；无视历史
The quality of being without historical basis or context.
The film was criticized for its ahistoricity, blending costumes and events from different centuries. 这部电影因其非历史性而受到批评，它混合了不同世纪的服装和事件。
lack of historical perspective, anachronism
historical accuracy, historicity
noun
