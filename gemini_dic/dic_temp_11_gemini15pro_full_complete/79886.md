gnathonic:/nɑːˈθɑːnɪk/| adj.|阿谀奉承的，谄媚的|Given to or characterized by flattery; sycophantic.  Excessively flattering to someone of higher social standing in order to gain favor.|例句：His gnathonic praise of the CEO was embarrassing to witness.  他谄媚地赞扬CEO，让人尴尬不已。

例句：The gnathonic courtier bowed low before the king. 谄媚的朝臣在国王面前深深地鞠了一躬。|近义词：sycophantic, fawning, flattering, obsequious, servile|反义词：frank, candid, sincere, forthright|词性:adjective

词源：From Greek gnathos "jaw" + -ic.  Originally referred to a parasite that attaches itself to the jaws of fish.  The meaning shifted to "flatterer" because flatterers were seen as clinging to those in power.
