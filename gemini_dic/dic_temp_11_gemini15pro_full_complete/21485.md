blubbered:/ˈblʌbərd/| v.|哭诉，抽泣|To cry noisily and uncontrollably; to weep with loud sobs.|The child blubbered when he lost his toy. 孩子丢了玩具后哭了起来。|近义词：sobbed, cried, wept, wailed|反义词:laughed, chuckled|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English  *blobberen*, of imitative origin.  Related to Dutch  *blobberen*, German  *blubbern*.  The sense of "to speak indistinctly while weeping" arose around the 17th century.
