lanciform:/ˈlænsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
矛形的，披针形的
Shaped like a lance or spearhead; tapering to a point at each end.
The lanciform leaves of the willow tree drooped gracefully. 柳树披针形的叶子优雅地垂下。
Synonyms: lanceolate, spear-shaped, fusiform
Antonyms: ovate, rotund, circular
adjective
