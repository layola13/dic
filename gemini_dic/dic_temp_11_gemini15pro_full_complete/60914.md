elephantiasis:/ˌɛlɪfənˈtaɪəsɪs/| n.|象皮病|A chronic condition characterized by the thickening and enlargement of body tissues, especially in the limbs and genitals, caused by obstruction of the lymphatic vessels.|Elephantiasis can be a debilitating disease. 象皮病可能是一种使人衰弱的疾病。|Synonyms: lymphatic filariasis, filariasis|Antonyms: None (This is a medical condition; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|noun
