defi:/ˈdiːfaɪ/ /dəˈfaɪ/| n. |去中心化金融（Decentralized Finance）|Decentralized finance (DeFi) is an emerging financial technology that aims to remove intermediaries, such as banks and other financial companies, from financial transactions.  It uses cryptocurrency and blockchain technology to create a more transparent and accessible financial system.|Example 1: DeFi is changing the way we think about finance. 去中心化金融正在改变我们对金融的思考方式。

Example 2: Many believe DeFi offers a more equitable financial system. 许多人认为去中心化金融提供了一个更公平的金融体系。|近义词：decentralized finance|反义词: centralized finance|词性:名词
