despecificate:/diːspiˈsɪfɪkeɪt/
v.
去除特性；使不具体；概括化
To remove specific characteristics or details from; to generalize.
The report despecificated the findings to protect the privacy of the participants.  这份报告概括了调查结果以保护参与者的隐私。
The artist despecificated the figures in the painting, making them more universal.  艺术家将画中的人物概括化，使它们更具普遍性。
近义词: generalize, abstract, summarize
反义词: specify, detail, particularize
词性:动词
