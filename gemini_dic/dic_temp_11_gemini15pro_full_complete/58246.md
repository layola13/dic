drumbeats:/ˈdrʌmˌbiːts/ /ˈdrʌmˌbits/
noun
鼓声；鼓点
The rhythmic drumbeats filled the air.  有节奏的鼓声充满了空气。
The drumbeats quickened as the dancers approached the stage.  随着舞者走近舞台，鼓声加快了。
synonyms: drumming, percussion, rhythm, pulse
antonyms: silence, quiet
n.
