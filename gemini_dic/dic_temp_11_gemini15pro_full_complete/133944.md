nonheading:/nɒnˈhɛdɪŋ/ | adj. | 无标题的，非标题的 | Not having a heading or title. Used to describe something, typically a document or section of text, that lacks a title or heading. |  The email was nonheading, making it difficult to identify the subject. 这封邮件没有标题，很难识别主题。| The document was nonheading and therefore difficult to file. 这份文件没有标题，因此难以归档。 | untitled, headerless | headed, titled | adjective
