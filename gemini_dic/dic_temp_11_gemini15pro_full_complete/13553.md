attempting:/əˈtɛmptɪŋ/ | adj. | 尝试的；企图的；诱人的 | Trying to do something, especially something difficult. Appealing and inviting. | She made an attempting smile. 她勉强笑了笑。| Synonyms: trying, endeavoring, alluring, tempting | Antonyms: easy, effortless, unappealing, repulsive | Word Class: Adjective

attempting:/əˈtɛmptɪŋ/ | v. | 尝试；企图 | The present participle of "attempt," meaning to try to do something, especially something difficult. | He is attempting to climb Mount Everest. 他正尝试攀登珠穆朗玛峰。| Synonyms: trying, endeavoring | Antonyms: abstaining, avoiding | Word Class: Verb (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *attempten, from Old French attempter, from Latin attemptare (“to try, test, attack, tempt”), frequentative of Latin temptare (“to handle, touch, feel, try, test, tempt”).
