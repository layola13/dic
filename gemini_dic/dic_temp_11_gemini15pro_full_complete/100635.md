intolerable:/ɪnˈtɒlərəbl/ (UK), /ɪnˈtɑːlərəbl/ (US)| adj.|无法忍受的，不能容忍的|Not able to be tolerated or endured; unbearable.|The heat was intolerable, so we had to stay indoors.  天气热得无法忍受，所以我们不得不待在室内。|Synonyms: unbearable, insufferable, unendurable|Antonyms: tolerable, bearable, acceptable|adjective
