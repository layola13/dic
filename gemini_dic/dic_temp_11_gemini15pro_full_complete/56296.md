doggo:/ˈdɒɡəʊ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːɡoʊ/ (US)| n.|狗狗（可爱的说法）|An informal and affectionate way to refer to a dog, often used online and in social media. It implies a sense of cuteness and playfulness.|例句：Look at that cute doggo! 看看那只可爱的狗狗！

例句：My doggo loves belly rubs. 我的狗狗喜欢被揉肚子。

近义词：pup, puppy, pooch, doggie|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a playful term)|词性: Noun
