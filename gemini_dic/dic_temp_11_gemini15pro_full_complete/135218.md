nonowning:/nɒnˈəʊnɪŋ/ | adj. | 非拥有的，不拥有所有权的 | Not having or involving ownership.  Referring to a relationship where one entity does not possess or control another.  Often used in programming contexts to describe references or pointers that do not imply ownership of the referenced object. |  `The nonowning pointer simply refers to the data; it doesn't control its lifetime.` 非拥有的指针只是引用数据，它不控制数据的存在周期。

`Nonowning references are useful for avoiding memory leaks.` 非拥有的引用对于避免内存泄漏很有用。 | 近义词:  non-possessory, unowned | 反义词: owning, possessive | 词性: adjective
