montaine:/mɒnˈteɪn/ | adj. | 山的；多山的；山区的 | Of or relating to mountains; mountainous. |  Example 1: The montaine region is known for its breathtaking views.  山区以其壮丽的景色而闻名。

Example 2:  Montaine air is often crisp and clean. 山区的空气通常清新干净。 | 近义词: mountainous, alpine, highland | 反义词: lowland, flat | 词性: adjective


词源: From French *montain*, from Old French *montagne* (“mountain”), from Vulgar Latin *montanea*, from Latin *montanus* (“of a mountain”), from *mons*, *montis* (“mountain”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*mentis* (“mountain”).
