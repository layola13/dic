oversanguinenesses:/ˌōvərˈsæŋɡwɪnɪsnɪsɪz/
|n.| 过分乐观，过度自信 |
|The state of being excessively sanguine; undue optimism or confidence.|
|His oversanguineness about the company's future led to some poor investment decisions. 他对公司未来的过度乐观导致了一些糟糕的投资决策。|
|Optimism, hopefulness, confidence|
|Pessimism, cynicism, doubt|
|noun|
