avis:/ˈeɪvɪs/ (US), /ˈævɪs/ (UK)| n.| 通知，意见，鸟 | An opinion, view, or judgment.  Specifically, in legal contexts, it can refer to a formal notice. It also means "bird" in Latin. | The judge issued an avis that the trial would be postponed. 法官发出通知，审判将被推迟。 | synonyms: opinion, view, judgment, notice, announcement | antonyms:  None (for the primary meaning of opinion/notice).  | noun
