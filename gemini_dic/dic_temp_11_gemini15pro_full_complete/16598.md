baroka:/bəˈroʊkə/
adjective
巴洛克式的
Relating to or characteristic of a style of European architecture, music, and art of the 17th and 18th centuries that followed mannerism and is characterized by ornate detail, curves, and contrast.
The palace is a masterpiece of baroka architecture. 这座宫殿是巴洛克建筑的杰作。
近义词:ornate, elaborate, flamboyant
反义词:simple, plain, austere
词性:adjective
