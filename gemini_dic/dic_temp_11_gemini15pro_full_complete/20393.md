bitchiest: /ˈbɪtʃiɪst/ | adj. | 最刻薄的，最恶毒的 | Superlative form of "bitchy," meaning extremely malicious, spiteful, or unpleasant.  It describes someone who displays these qualities to the highest degree. |  She was the bitchiest girl in school, always spreading rumors.  她总是散布谣言，是学校里最刻薄的女孩。

| Her bitchiest comments were always directed at her rivals. 她最恶毒的评论总是针对她的竞争对手。 | 近义词: cattiest, nastiest, meanest | 反义词: kindest, nicest, sweetest | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "bitch" (female dog) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-est" (superlative suffix).  The original meaning referred to a bad-tempered female dog, and the meaning extended to describe a spiteful or unpleasant woman.
