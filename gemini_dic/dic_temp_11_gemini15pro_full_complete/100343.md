intersqueezing:/ˌɪntərˈskwiːzɪŋ/
|v.|相互挤压，挤入…之间|The rocks were intersqueezed between the layers of clay. 岩石被挤压在粘土层之间。|例句：He intersqueezed himself between two large men on the crowded bus. 他在拥挤的公交车上把自己挤在两个大个子男人之间。

例句：The different colored clays were intersqueezing, creating a marbled effect. 不同颜色的粘土相互挤压，形成大理石效果。|近义词：compress, squeeze, wedge, jam, pack|反义词：separate, expand, loosen|词性：动词
