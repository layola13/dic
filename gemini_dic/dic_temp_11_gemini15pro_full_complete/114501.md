maladaptive:/ˌmæləˈdæptɪv/ | adj. | 适应不良的，不适应的 | Not providing adequate or appropriate adjustment to the environment or situation. |  His maladaptive behavior led to his dismissal from the company.  他的适应不良行为导致他被公司解雇。 |  Maladaptive coping mechanisms can worsen stress.  不适当的应对机制会加剧压力。 | 近义词: dysfunctional, counterproductive, unsuitable, inappropriate | 反义词: adaptive, suitable, appropriate | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
mal- (bad, ill) + adaptive (capable of adapting)
From Latin *malus* (bad) + ad- (to) + aptus (fit)
