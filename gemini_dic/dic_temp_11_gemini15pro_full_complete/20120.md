biparous:/ˈbɪpərəs/| adj.|双生的，一次产两子的|Producing two offspring at a birth.|例句：The biparous sheep gave birth to two healthy lambs. 这只双产的绵羊生了两只健康的羊羔。

例句：Biparous reproduction is common in some species of primates.  双胎繁殖在某些灵长类物种中很常见。


近义词：twin-bearing, dichorionic|反义词: uniparous, multiparous|词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *biparus ("producing two at a birth"), from bi- ("two") + parere ("to bring forth, bear").
