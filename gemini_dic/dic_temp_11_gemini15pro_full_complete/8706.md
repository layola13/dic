anticritically:/ˌæntiˈkrɪtɪkli/
adverb
反批判地；不加批判地
In an uncritical manner; without criticizing.
He accepted the information anticritically, without questioning its source. 他不加批判地接受了这些信息，没有质疑其来源。
She listened anticritically to the speaker, absorbing everything he said. 她不加批判地听着演讲者，吸收了他所说的一切。
近义词: uncritically, passively, blindly, indiscriminately
反义词: critically, skeptically, analytically, discerningly
词性: adverb

词源:
Prefix "anti-" (against, opposed to) + "critically" (in a critical manner).  "Critically" comes from "critical," which ultimately derives from the Greek word "kritikos" (able to judge).
