fabricatress:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪtrɪs/
n.
女制造者，女编造者
A woman who fabricates; a woman who invents or makes something.
She was a skilled fabricatress of intricate jewelry. 她是一位技艺精湛的复杂珠宝制作者。
近义词: maker, producer, inventor, creator
反义词: destroyer
