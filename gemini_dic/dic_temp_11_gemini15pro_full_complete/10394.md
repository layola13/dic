appendiculate:/əˌpɛndɪˈkjʊleɪt/ | adj. | 附属的；有附器的；有小附属物的 | Having small appendages or appendices.  Characterized by appendages. |  Many deep-sea creatures are appendiculate, with strange, bioluminescent growths. 许多深海生物都有附属物，身上长着奇异的、发光的赘生物。

The appendiculate leaves of the plant helped it cling to the rocky surface.  这种植物的附器状叶片帮助它附着在岩石表面。 | 近义词: appendaged, appendant | 反义词: smooth, unadorned | 词性: adjective

appendiculate:/əˌpɛndɪˈkjʊlɪt/ | n. |  有附器动物 | An animal possessing appendages. |  The study focused on the feeding habits of marine appendiculates. 这项研究的重点是海洋有附器动物的摄食习性。 | 近义词: N/A (As a noun, it's a specific biological term) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源:
Late 18th century: from Latin appendiculatus, from appendicula ‘small appendage’ (diminutive of appendix).
