eulachon:/ˈjuːləkɒn/ (US), /ˈjuːləkən/ (UK)| n. | 尤拉肯油鱼; 烛鱼 |A small anadromous smelt (Thaleichthys pacificus) of the North Pacific, valued for its oil and delicate flesh.  It is also known as the oolachan, candlefish, and hooligan.|The eulachon run is a vital food source for many First Nations communities. 尤拉肯鱼洄游是许多原住民社区的重要食物来源。

The eulachon were so plentiful that early explorers said you could almost walk across the river on their backs. 早期探险家说，尤拉肯鱼数量众多，你几乎可以踩着它们的背过河。|近义词：smelt, candlefish, oolachan, hooligan|反义词: None|词性:noun
