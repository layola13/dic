megastructural:/ˌmɛɡəˈstrʌktʃərəl/| adj.| 巨型结构的，特大结构的|Relating to a megastructure, an extremely large artificial object.  It describes something related to or characteristic of a megastructure.  Megastructures are hypothetical constructions of immense size, far surpassing any current human-built structures.|The Dyson sphere is a classic example of a megastructural concept. 戴森球是巨型结构概念的经典例子。|Near synonyms: massive, immense, gigantic, colossal|Near antonyms: small, tiny, miniature|adjective
