nonhyperbolical:/ˌnɒnˌhaɪpəˈbɒlɪkəl/ | adj. | 非双曲线的，不夸张的 | Not exaggerated; literal or realistic.  Characterized by the absence of hyperbole or exaggeration. | The witness gave a nonhyperbolical account of the events. 目击者对事件进行了不夸张的描述。 |  The scientist presented a nonhyperbolical explanation of the phenomenon. 科学家对这种现象给出了不夸张的解释。 | 近义词: literal, realistic, factual, unembellished, understated | 反义词: hyperbolic, exaggerated, overstated, inflated | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "hyperbolical" (from Greek "hyperbole" meaning "excess," "overcasting").
