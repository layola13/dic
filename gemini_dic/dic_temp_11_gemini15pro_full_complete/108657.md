leaflessness:/ˈliːfləsnəs/
n.
无叶状态；光秃秃
The state of being without leaves.
The leaflessness of the trees in winter gives the landscape a stark beauty. 冬天树木的无叶状态赋予了风景一种荒凉的美。

The leaflessness of the branches made the tree look dead. 光秃秃的树枝使这棵树看起来像死了一样。

近义词: bareness, nudity
反义词: leafiness, foliage
词性: 名词
