forgot:/fərˈɡɒt/ (UK) /fərˈɡɑːt/ (US)| v. |忘记|Past tense of *forget*, meaning to fail to remember something.|I forgot my keys at home. 我把钥匙忘在家里了。|近义词：misremembered, overlooked|反义词：remembered|词性:动词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I forget things easily. 我很容易忘记事情。
He forgets his glasses all the time.  他总是忘记他的眼镜。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am forgetting everything these days. 我这几天什么都忘记。
He is forgetting his manners. 他正在忘记他的礼貌。 (比喻用法)

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have forgotten what I wanted to say. 我忘记了我想要说什么。
She has forgotten her password. 她忘记了她的密码。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been forgetting things a lot lately. 我最近经常忘记事情。
He has been forgetting to take his medication. 他最近一直忘记吃药。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I forgot to call her yesterday. 我昨天忘记给她打电话了。
He forgot his wallet. 他忘记了他的钱包。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was forgetting all about my worries. 我正在忘记所有烦恼。
He was forgetting his lines in the play.  他在忘记戏里的台词。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had forgotten all about our appointment. 我完全忘记了我们的约会。
She had forgotten to bring her umbrella. 她忘记带伞了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been forgetting to water the plants. 我一直忘记给植物浇水。
He had been forgetting his responsibilities. 他一直忘记他的责任。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will forget all about this soon. 我很快就会忘记这一切。
He will forget to buy milk. 他会忘记买牛奶。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be forgetting all about this by tomorrow.  到明天我就会忘记这一切。
He will be forgetting his troubles soon enough. 他很快就会忘记他的烦恼。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have forgotten all about this by next week. 到下周我就会忘记这一切。
He will have forgotten his password by then. 到那时他会忘记他的密码。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
(This tense is rarely used with "forget" in a literal sense, but here is a hypothetical example)
By the time I'm eighty, I will have been forgetting things for decades. 到我八十岁的时候，我忘记事情已经几十年了。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't forgotten my wallet, I would have bought that dress. 如果我没有忘记我的钱包，我就买了那条裙子。
I wish I hadn't forgotten her birthday. 我希望我没有忘记她的生日。

被动语态:
(Forget is rarely used in the passive voice)


疑问句:
Did you forget your phone? 你忘记带手机了吗？
Have you forgotten our agreement? 你忘记了我们的约定吗？


否定句:
I didn't forget your birthday. 我没有忘记你的生日。
She hasn't forgotten our promise. 她没有忘记我们的承诺。


PHRASE->
forget about it = 算了吧/别在意
forget it = 算了/休想
forget me not = 勿忘我
forget oneself =  失态；忘记自己的身份
forget the time =  忘记时间


词源:
From Old English *forġietan*, from *for-* (indicating loss or destruction) + *ġietan* (to get).  The original sense was "to lose one's hold on something."
