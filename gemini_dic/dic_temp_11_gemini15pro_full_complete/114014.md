magmatically:/mæɡˈmætɪkli/
adjective
岩浆地；岩浆性地
formed from or by magma; related to magma.
The rocks were formed magmatically. 这些岩石是岩浆形成的。
The area is characterized by magmatically active volcanoes. 这个地区以岩浆活动频繁的火山为特征。
Synonyms: igneous, volcanic
Antonyms: sedimentary, metamorphic
词性: 形容词
