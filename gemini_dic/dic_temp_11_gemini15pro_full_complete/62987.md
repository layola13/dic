词源:
From Middle English *enom, enem*, from Old French *enem*, *enemi*, from Latin *inimīcus* (“enemy, unfriendly, hostile”), from *in- (“not”) + amīcus* (“friend, friendly, loving”). Doublet of *enemy*.
