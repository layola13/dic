dressmakers:/ˈdrɛsˌmeɪkərz/
n.
裁缝师（尤指女装裁缝）; 女装裁缝;
A person who makes women's clothes, especially dresses.
The dressmakers worked tirelessly to finish the wedding gown. 那些裁缝师不知疲倦地工作，以完成那件婚纱。
