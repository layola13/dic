luxury:/ˈlʌkʃəri/ /ˈlʌɡʒəri/| n. adj.|奢侈，豪华；奢侈品|Something that is expensive and enjoyable but not essential.|She lives a life of luxury. 她过着奢侈的生活。|近义词：opulence, extravagance, richness|反义词：necessity, poverty, austerity|noun

luxury:/ˈlʌkʃəri/ /ˈlʌɡʒəri/| adj.|奢华的，豪华的|Providing great comfort and pleasure, especially when involving great expense.|They stayed in a luxury hotel. 他们住在一个豪华酒店。|近义词：luxurious, opulent, lavish|反义词：basic, simple, austere|adjective
