aground:/əˈɡraʊnd/
adjective
搁浅的；触礁的
Touching or stuck on the bottom in shallow water.
The ship ran aground on a reef. 这艘船在礁石上搁浅了。
近义词: stranded, beached, grounded
反义词: afloat
词性: adjective


aground:/əˈɡraʊnd/
adverb
搁浅地；触礁地
In a position of being aground.
The boat drifted aground. 船漂流搁浅了。
近义词: ashore
反义词: afloat
词性: adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From a- + ground.  "A-" is a prefix meaning "on" or "in".  "Ground" refers to the bottom of a body of water.
