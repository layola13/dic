koan:/ˈkoʊɑːn/| n.|公案, 禅宗谜题|A paradoxical anecdote or riddle, used in Zen Buddhism to demonstrate the inadequacy of logical reasoning and to provoke enlightenment.|The Zen master posed a koan to his students, hoping to guide them towards enlightenment.|近义词：riddle, puzzle, enigma, paradox|反义词:solution, explanation|noun
