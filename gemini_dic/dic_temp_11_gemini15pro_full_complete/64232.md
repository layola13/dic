equinoctial:/ˌiːkwɪˈnɒkʃəl/ /ˌiːkwəˈnɑːkʃəl/
adjective|昼夜平分的；二分点的；暴风雨的|Happening at or near the time of an equinox.  与春分或秋分时间相符或相近的。|The equinoctial gales blew fiercely. 二分点的暴风雨刮得很猛烈。|近义词：equatorial|反义词：solstitial
noun|二分点；二分圈；暴风雨|Either of the two times of the year when the sun crosses the celestial equator, when day and night are of approximately equal length (about September 22 and March 20).  一年中太阳两次穿过天球赤道的时间，此时白天和黑夜的长度大致相等（大约在9月22日和3月20日）。|The spring equinoctial is in March. 春分在三月。|近义词：equinox|反义词：solstice
