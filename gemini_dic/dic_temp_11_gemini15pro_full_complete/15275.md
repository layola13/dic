backlashing:/ˈbæklæʃɪŋ/
n.|强烈抵制，反对，反冲|A strong negative reaction by a large number of people, especially to a social or political change.|The government is facing a backlashing from voters over its new tax policy. 政府正面临选民对其新税收政策的强烈反对。|repercussion, resistance, reaction|acceptance, support|noun
backlashing:/ˈbæklæʃɪŋ/
v.|强烈抵制，反对，反冲（进行时）|Reacting strongly and negatively, typically to a social or political change.|The community is backlashing against the proposed development. 社区正在强烈反对拟议中的开发项目。|resisting, opposing, reacting|accepting, supporting|verb
