envidia: /enˈviðja/ (Spanish), /ˈenviːdiə/ (English pronunciation of the Spanish word)| n.| 羡慕，嫉妒 |A feeling of discontented or resentful longing aroused by someone else's possessions, qualities, or luck. | She couldn't hide her envidia of her friend's new car. 她无法掩饰自己对朋友新车的羡慕。|  Envidia corroded their friendship. 嫉妒腐蚀了他们的友谊。 |Synonyms: jealousy, covetousness, envy | Antonyms: admiration, goodwill, generosity | noun
