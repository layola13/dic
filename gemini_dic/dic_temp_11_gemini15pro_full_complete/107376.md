lamentably:/ˈlæməntəbli/| adv.|遗憾地，可惜地，悲哀地|Sadly, regrettably, unfortunately.  It expresses sorrow or regret about something.|The project was lamentably underfunded.  这个项目资金不足，令人遗憾。|Synonyms: regrettably, sadly, unfortunately, deplorably, woefully|Antonyms: fortunately, luckily, happily, joyfully|Adverb
