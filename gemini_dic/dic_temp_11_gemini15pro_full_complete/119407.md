microfiche: /ˈmaɪkrəʊfiːʃ/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊfiːʃ/ (US)| n.|缩微胶片|A flat piece of film containing microphotographs of the pages of a newspaper, catalogue, or book.|The library has back issues of the newspaper on microfiche.|近义词: microfilm|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hard copy")|noun

microfiches: /ˈmaɪkrəʊfiːʃɪz/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊfiːʃɪz/ (US)| n.|缩微胶片（复数）|Plural form of microfiche, referring to multiple flat pieces of film containing microphotographs.|The archives hold thousands of microfiches containing historical documents.|近义词: microfilms|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hard copies")|noun
