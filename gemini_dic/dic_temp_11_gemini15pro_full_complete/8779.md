antiecclesiastically:/ˌæntiɪˌkliːziˈæstɪkəli/
adv. | 反教会地，反对教会地 |  In a manner opposed to the church or its principles. |  He spoke antiecclesiastically about the church's stance on social issues. 他就教会在社会问题上的立场发表了反教会的言论。 |  irreverently, profanely, secularly |  religiously, piously, devoutly | adverb

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek)
ecclesiastic (adjective/noun): relating to the church (from Greek "ekklesia" meaning "church")
-al (suffix): forming adjectives from nouns
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
