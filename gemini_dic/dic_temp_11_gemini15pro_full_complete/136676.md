nonsecret:/ˈnɑːnˈsiːkrət/ /ˈnɒnˈsiːkrɪt/| adj.|非秘密的，公开的|Not secret; open.  Not kept hidden from others. |The information is nonsecret and available to the public.  该信息不是秘密，可供公众查阅。|The meeting minutes are nonsecret. 会议记录不是秘密。| 近义词: public, open, overt, disclosed|反义词: secret, confidential, classified, hidden|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non-, from ne (“not”) + oinom (“one”).  Meaning "not."
secret (noun/adjective): from Old French secret, from Latin sēcrētus (“set apart, hidden”), past participle of sēcernere (“to separate, distinguish, set apart”), from sē- (“apart”) + cernere (“to distinguish, separate”).
