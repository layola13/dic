overmellownesses: /ˌoʊvərˈmɛloʊnɪsɪz/
|n.| 过熟, 过于成熟的状态; (尤指水果)软得过分; (比喻)过于伤感或多愁善感 |The overmellownesses of the peaches made them unsuitable for pie.  桃子熟过头了，不适合做馅饼。| Overmellowness can sometimes indicate decay in fruit. 过熟有时表明水果腐烂。 |例句：The overmellownesses of the late summer fruits attracted swarms of insects. 夏末水果的过度成熟吸引了成群的昆虫。|例句：He disliked the overmellownesses of the sentimental movie, finding it excessively mawkish. 他不喜欢这部感伤电影的过度煽情，觉得它过于伤感。 |近义词：ripeness, softness, mushiness, sentimentality|反义词：firmness, tartness, hardness, unsentimentality|词性: noun


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "mellow" (adjective meaning "soft, ripe, sweet, gentle") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
