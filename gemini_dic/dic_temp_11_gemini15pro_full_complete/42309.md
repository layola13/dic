counter-penalty:/ˈkaʊntər ˈpɛnəlti/ | n. |反惩罚；对抗性处罚| A penalty imposed in response to a penalty imposed by another party.  It's essentially a retaliatory measure in a context where penalties or sanctions are being exchanged. | The government imposed counter-penalties on imported goods after the other country raised tariffs on their exports. 政府在另一个国家提高对其出口产品的关税后，对进口商品实施了反惩罚措施。

The company faced counter-penalties after it breached the contract.  这家公司在违反合同后，面临反惩罚。 | synonyms: retaliatory penalty, counter-sanction, countermeasure | antonyms:  concession, compromise | noun
