cigarette:/ˌsɪɡəˈrɛt/ | n. | 香烟，卷烟 | A thin cylinder of finely cut tobacco rolled in paper for smoking. |  He lit a cigarette. 他点燃了一支香烟。 | synonyms: cig, fag (informal), butt (informal) | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cigarette*, diminutive of *cigare* (cigar). Ultimately from Spanish *cigarro*.
