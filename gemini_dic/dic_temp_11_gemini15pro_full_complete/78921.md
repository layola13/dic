gipsyism:/ˈdʒɪpsiɪzəm/ | n. |吉卜赛主义；流浪癖；吉卜赛人的生活方式|The state or condition of being a Gypsy, or the practices and customs associated with Gypsies.  A nomadic way of life, often characterized by freedom and a close connection to nature.  |He embraced gipsyism and travelled the world. 他拥抱了吉卜赛式的生活，周游世界。|Synonym: nomadism, wanderlust|Antonym: sedentariness, stability|noun
