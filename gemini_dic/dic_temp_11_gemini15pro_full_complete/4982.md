alkaloid:/ˈælkəlɔɪd/| n.|生物碱|Any of a class of nitrogenous organic compounds of plant origin that have pronounced physiological actions on humans.  Many alkaloids are used in medicine or as drugs, including morphine, quinine, nicotine, and caffeine.|Morphine is a powerful alkaloid used to relieve pain. 吗啡是一种强效的生物碱，用于缓解疼痛。|Synonyms:  base, organic base|Antonyms: acid|noun
