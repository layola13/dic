egilops:/ˈɛdʒɪlɒps/ | n. |山羊草, 泪囊炎|A genus of Eurasian grasses (tribe Triticeae) that includes the goat grasses and is closely related to wheat, some kinds being noxious weeds in grainfields.|The farmer found egilops growing in his wheat field. 农民发现他的麦田里长着山羊草。| 近义词: goat grass, Aegilops | 反义词: N/A | Noun
