ectopia: /ɛkˈtoʊpiə/ /ɪkˈtəʊpiə/| n. |异位，错位|The displacement of an organ or body part from its normal location. |The doctor diagnosed an ectopia of the lens in his left eye.  医生诊断出他左眼晶状体异位。|Synonyms: displacement, malposition, abnormal location|Antonyms: normal position, orthotopia|noun

ectopic: /ɛkˈtɒpɪk/ /ɪkˈtɑːpɪk/| adj. |异位的|Located in an abnormal place or position. |She had an ectopic pregnancy. 她发生了宫外孕。|Synonyms: displaced, misplaced, abnormally located|Antonyms: normal, orthotopic|adjective
