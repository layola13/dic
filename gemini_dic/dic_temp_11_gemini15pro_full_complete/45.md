aaaghh:/ˈɑːxən/ /ˈæɡ/ | int. |啊;呀;（表示疼痛、惊讶、烦恼、厌恶等）|An exclamation expressing sudden pain, surprise, annoyance, or disgust. | Example: "Aaaghh! I stubbed my toe!"  啊！我的脚趾撞到了！

Example: "Aaaghh! That spider is huge!" 啊！那只蜘蛛好大！

近义词：ouch, yikes, eek, ugh, oh

反义词: (Interjections typically don't have direct antonyms, but words expressing pleasure or contentment could be considered contrasting, such as: "Mmm," "Ahh," "Yay")

词性: interjection
