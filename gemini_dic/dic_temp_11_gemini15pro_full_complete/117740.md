melanochroic:/ˌmɛlənəˈkrəʊɪk/| adj.|黑色素的，深色的|Having dark pigmentation or relating to dark pigmentation.  Refers to individuals or populations with darker skin coloration due to increased melanin production.|Melanochroic populations are more common in regions with high UV radiation. 黑色素沉着的人群在紫外线辐射高的地区更为常见。|The melanochroic feathers of the bird provided excellent camouflage. 这只鸟的深色羽毛提供了极佳的伪装。|近义词：dark-skinned, pigmented, swarthy|反义词：leucochroic, fair-skinned|词性: adjective
