intercommunity:/ˌɪntərˈkəˈmjuːnɪti/
adjective
社区间的，跨社区的
Existing or occurring between different communities.
The intercommunity rivalry between the two towns has been going on for decades.  这两个城镇之间的社区竞争已经持续了几十年了。
Near synonyms: inter-community, cross-community
Antonyms: intracommunity
词性: adjective
