copartner:/ˈkoʊˌpɑːrtnər/ | n. | 合伙人，共同合作者 | A person who shares in a business undertaking with another or others. |  My copartner and I started this business ten years ago. 我和我的合伙人十年前创办了这家公司。|  
copartner:/ˈkoʊˌpɑːrtnər/ | n. | 共犯，同谋 | A person with whom one shares guilt. | The police arrested the thief and his copartner. 警察逮捕了小偷和他的同伙。 | Synonyms: partner, associate, colleague, collaborator | Antonyms: opponent, adversary, competitor | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + partner.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "together," "jointly," or "mutually." "Partner" comes from Old French "parçonier" meaning "sharer, participant."
