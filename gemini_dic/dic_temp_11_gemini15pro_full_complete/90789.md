humouring: /ˈhjuːməɹɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhjuːmərɪŋ/ (US) | v. | 迎合；迁就；顺着…的意思 | Indulging or complying with someone's mood or whims, often to appease them or avoid conflict. |  She was humouring her little brother by playing his silly game. 她陪着弟弟玩他那愚蠢的游戏，以迁就他。 |  Example: He was humouring his boss by pretending to agree with his absurd ideas. 他假装同意老板荒谬的想法，以迎合他。 | Synonyms: indulging, appeasing, accommodating, pandering to | Antonyms: opposing, contradicting, defying | Verb
