ergograph:/ˈɜːrɡəɡræf/ | n. | 测力计；测力器 | An instrument for measuring and recording the work done by a muscle or group of muscles, especially by the fingers. |  The researcher used an ergograph to measure the subject's finger flexion strength. 研究人员使用测力计测量受试者手指的屈曲强度。

The ergograph recorded a decrease in muscle performance over time. 测力计记录了肌肉性能随时间的下降。| 近义词: dynamometer | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *ergon* (work) + *graphein* (to write).
