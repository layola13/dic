abort:/əˈbɔːrt/ (US) /əˈbɔːt/ (UK)| v. n.|中止，流产，夭折|To stop a process or activity before it is completed; to terminate a pregnancy prematurely.|例句：The mission was aborted due to bad weather.  任务因恶劣天气而中止。|近义词：cancel, terminate, quit, stop, miscarry|反义词:continue, proceed, complete|verb
abort:/əˈbɔːrt/ (US) /əˈbɔːt/ (UK)| n.|中止，流产|The act of aborting something, especially a pregnancy or mission.|例句：The pilot had to perform an emergency abort. 飞行员不得不进行紧急中止。|近义词：termination, cessation, miscarriage|反义词:continuation, completion|noun

词源：From Latin *abortus*, past participle of *aborīrī* "to miscarry, pass away, disappear," from *ab* "away, from" + *orīrī* "to arise, be born."
