geotracking:/ˈdʒiːoʊˌtrækɪŋ/
noun
地理追踪，地理定位
The process of tracking the location of something or someone using GPS or other similar technologies.  It involves recording and monitoring the movement of a person, animal, or object.
The company uses geotracking to monitor its delivery fleet. 该公司使用地理追踪来监控其送货车队。
Geotracking allowed researchers to study the migration patterns of the whales. 地理追踪使研究人员能够研究鲸鱼的迁徙模式。
geolocation, location tracking, position tracking
