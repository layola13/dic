obturator:/ˈɒbtjʊˌreɪtər/ (US) /ˈɒbtʃʊˌreɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|闭孔器，(照相机的)快门，塞子|A muscle, membrane, or other structure that closes or covers an opening in the body.  A device used to close an opening, such as a camera shutter or a plug for a test tube.|

例句：
The surgeon repaired the patient's obturator hernia.  外科医生修复了病人的闭孔疝。

The camera's obturator malfunctioned, causing overexposure.  相机的快门发生故障，导致曝光过度。


近义词：plug, stopper, cover, shutter|
反义词:opener|
词性:noun|

obturate:/ˈɒbtjʊˌreɪt/ (US) /ˈɒbtʃʊˌreɪt/ (UK)| v.|封闭，堵塞|To close or block an opening or passage.|

例句：
The blood clot obturated the artery, causing a stroke.  血块阻塞了动脉，导致中风。

He used a piece of cloth to obturate the leak in the pipe. 他用一块布堵住了管道上的漏洞。


近义词：close, block, occlude, obstruct|
反义词:open|
词性:verb|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *obturator, from Latin obturare (“to stop up, close”), from ob- (“against, toward”) + turare (“to stop up”).
