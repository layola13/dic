bungert:/ˈbʊŋɡərt/| n.|果园，果树园|A bungert is a type of orchard, especially common in the Rhineland region of Germany, that is traditionally used for growing fruit trees and often also includes a small vineyard and vegetable garden.|We visited a beautiful bungert in the Moselle valley, filled with apple, pear, and plum trees. 我们参观了摩泽尔山谷一个美丽的果园，里面种满了苹果树、梨树和李子树。|Synonym: orchard, fruit garden|Antonym: wasteland|noun
