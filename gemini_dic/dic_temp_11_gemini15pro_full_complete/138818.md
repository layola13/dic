nunciature: /ˈnʌnʃiətʃər/ | n. | 教廷大使馆；教廷大使的职位 | The Pope's nunciature in London is located near the Houses of Parliament.  教皇驻伦敦的教廷大使馆位于议会大厦附近。|  A permanent diplomatic mission of the Holy See, headed by a nuncio. 教廷的常驻外交使团，由教廷大使领导。 |例句：

The nunciature issued a statement condemning the violence.
教廷大使馆发表声明谴责暴力行为。

The new nuncio presented his credentials to the president.
新任教廷大使向总统递交了国书。

| 近义词：embassy, legation | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *nūnciātūra, from Latin nūntiātus (“announced, reported”), past participle of nūntiō (“I announce, report”), from nūntius (“messenger, message”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱnew- (“to scratch, rub”).
