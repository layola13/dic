canceling: /ˈkænsəlɪŋ/ | v. | 取消，撤销 | The act of stopping something that has already been arranged or planned. |  They are canceling the concert due to the storm. 他们因为暴风雨取消了音乐会。| synonyms: abandoning, revoking, withdrawing | antonyms: approving, confirming, establishing | verb (present participle)

canceling: /ˈkænsəlɪŋ/ | n. | 取消，撤销 | The act or process of canceling something. | The canceling of the flight caused a lot of inconvenience. 航班取消造成很多不便。| synonyms: abolition, annulment, revocation | antonyms: approval, confirmation, establishment | noun (gerund)
