diatonic:/ˌdaɪəˈtɒnɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈtɑːnɪk/| adj.| 全音阶的，自然音阶的 | Relating to or consisting of the notes, intervals, or chords natural to the prevailing key without chromatic alteration.| The diatonic scale of C major contains no sharps or flats. C大调的全音阶不包含升降号。|近义词：heptatonic (referring specifically to seven-note scales), natural|反义词：chromatic, non-diatonic|词性: adjective

diatonic:/ˌdaɪəˈtɒnɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈtɑːnɪk/| n.| 全音阶|A diatonic scale or system.|  He played a simple diatonic melody. 他演奏了一段简单的全音阶旋律。|近义词：heptatonic scale (specifically a seven-note diatonic scale)|反义词：chromatic scale|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *dia* ("through") + *tonikos* ("of or pertaining to tones").
