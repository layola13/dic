nonorganization:/ˌnɒnɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 非组织，缺乏组织 | Absence of organization; lack of systematic arrangement or structure. | The nonorganization of the event led to chaos. 活动缺乏组织导致一片混乱。| The nonorganization of his files made it difficult to find anything.  他的文件缺乏组织，很难找到任何东西。 | 近义词: disorder, disorganization, chaos, mess | 反义词: organization, order, structure, system | 词性: noun

词源: non- + organization.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "lack of." "Organization" comes from the Old French "organizacion" and ultimately from the Latin "organizare," meaning "to furnish with organs."
