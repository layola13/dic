frescade:/frɛˈskɑːdeɪ/ | n. | 清凉饮料，壁画 | A refreshing drink, often made with fruit juice and sugar, served cold; a fresco painting. |  We sipped frescades on the patio. 我们在露台上啜饮着清凉饮料。 The artist unveiled a stunning frescade depicting a mythological scene.  这位艺术家揭幕了一幅描绘神话场景的精美壁画。 | 近义词: cooler, beverage, refresher, mural | 反义词: (For drink) hot drink, (for painting) oil painting | 词性: noun
