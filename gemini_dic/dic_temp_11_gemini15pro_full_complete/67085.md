exploiter:/ɪkˈsplɔɪtər/| n.|剥削者，利用者|Someone who takes unfair advantage of a situation or person for their own benefit.|The exploiter took advantage of the workers' desperation for jobs and paid them very low wages. 剥削者利用工人急于找工作的心理，支付给他们非常低的工资。|近义词：user, abuser, profiteer|反义词: benefactor, philanthropist|noun
