creepy-crawly:/ˈkriːpi ˈkrɔːli/ (US), /ˈkriːpi ˈkrɔːli/ (UK)| n. | 爬虫；令人毛骨悚然的生物 |A creepy-crawly is a small insect or other creature that gives some people a feeling of fear or disgust.  It is an informal word.|  Example:  She screamed when she saw a creepy-crawly in the bathtub. 她看到浴缸里有只爬虫，尖叫起来。| Example: He hates creepy-crawlies of all kinds. 他讨厌各种爬虫。| Synonym: insect, bug, critter | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider larger animals or pleasant creatures) | Noun

creepy-crawly:/ˈkriːpi ˈkrɔːli/ (US), /ˈkriːpi ˈkrɔːli/ (UK)| adj. | 令人毛骨悚然的; 蠕动的 |  Causing a feeling of fear and disgust; crawling. This usage is less common than the noun form. | Example: The dark, abandoned house had a creepy-crawly atmosphere. 这栋黑暗废弃的房子有一种令人毛骨悚然的气氛。 | Example: I felt a creepy-crawly sensation on my arm and brushed it off. 我感觉胳膊上有什么东西在爬，就把它拂掉了。| Synonym: creepy, spooky, chilling | Antonym: comforting, reassuring | Adjective
