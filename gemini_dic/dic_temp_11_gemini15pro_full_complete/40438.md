contrasted:/kənˈtrɑːstɪd/ | adj. | 对比的，对照的 |  Differing strikingly; dissimilar.  Having been compared or juxtaposed to show differences. | The artist contrasted the light and dark shades expertly in the painting. 艺术家在画作中巧妙地对比了明暗色调。|  The simple village life was sharply contrasted with the hustle and bustle of the city. 简单的乡村生活与城市的喧嚣形成了鲜明的对比。| synonyms: compared, differentiated, juxtaposed, opposed | antonyms: similar, alike, uniform, homogeneous | adjective
