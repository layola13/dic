follow up:  跟进  I will follow up on this matter tomorrow. 我明天会跟进这件事。
follow through:  完成；贯彻到底  He needs to follow through on his promises. 他需要兑现他的承诺。
follow suit:  跟着做；照着做  When one store lowered its prices, the others followed suit. 当一家商店降价时，其他商店也跟着降价。
as follows:  如下  The rules are as follows: no smoking, no drinking, no littering. 规则如下：禁止吸烟，禁止饮酒，禁止乱扔垃圾。
