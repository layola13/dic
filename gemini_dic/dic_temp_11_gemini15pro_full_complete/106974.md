词源：From Middle English *lakken, *lackin (“to be wanting, to fail”), from Middle Dutch lacken (“to be deficient, to be wanting”), from Old Dutch *lakon (“to jump, to leap, to fail”), from Proto-Germanic *lakōną (“to jump, to spring, to fail, to be lacking”). Cognate with Scots lack (“to want, to need, to be without”), West Frisian lakke (“to fail, to err”), German dialectal laken (“to be lacking, to be failing”). Related to leak.
