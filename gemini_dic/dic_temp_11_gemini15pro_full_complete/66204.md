excitable:/ɪkˈsaɪtəbl/ | adj. | 易激动的，兴奋的 | Easily aroused to excitement; readily stirred emotionally. |  She has a very excitable puppy that jumps at everything. 她有一只很容易兴奋的小狗，对什么都跳来跳去。

The excitable child couldn't sit still during the movie.  这个好动的小孩在电影放映期间根本坐不住。| 近义词：nervous, sensitive, high-strung, temperamental | 反义词：calm, composed, placid, serene | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Late Latin *excitabilis, from Latin excitare (“to rouse, excite”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + citare (“to summon, rouse, excite”).
