askari: /æˈskɑːri/ (US), /ˈæskəri/ (UK)| n.|士兵，当地士兵，(尤指东非的)土兵|A native soldier in East Africa, especially in the former armies of European colonial powers.|Example 1: The askari stood guard outside the colonial administrator's office.  殖民地行政官办公室外站着一名土兵。

Example 2:  Many askaris were recruited from local tribes. 许多士兵是从当地部落招募的。|Synonyms: soldier, trooper, guardsman, native soldier|Antonyms: civilian|noun
