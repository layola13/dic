bungler:/ˈbʌŋɡlər/| n.|笨拙的人，搞砸事情的人|A person who makes mistakes often or is clumsy in their actions. This individual often messes things up due to incompetence or carelessness.|He's such a bungler in the kitchen; he's always breaking dishes. 他在厨房里真是个笨手笨脚的人，总是打破碗碟。

She was a bungler when it came to DIY projects.  她在DIY项目上是个十足的笨蛋。|近义词：blunderer, incompetent, klutz, duffer|反义词：expert, professional, adept|词性:名词(singular)


词源: From the verb "bungle," which likely originated from the Norwegian word "bungla" meaning "to work clumsily."
