anthologies:/ænˈθɑːlədʒiz/
n.
选集，文集
A collection of selected literary pieces or passages or other creative works, such as poems, short stories, plays, or musical pieces.
This anthology includes some of the best poems of the 20th century. 这本文集收录了一些20世纪的最佳诗歌。
collections, compilations
singles, individual works
noun
