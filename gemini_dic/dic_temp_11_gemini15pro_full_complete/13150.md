asymptotic:/ˌæsɪmˈtɒtɪk/ (US) /ˌæsɪmpˈtɑːtɪk/ (UK)| adj.|渐近的；渐近线的|Approaching a value or curve arbitrarily closely but never reaching it.|The graph of the function is asymptotic to the x-axis.  该函数的图像与x轴渐近。|near, approaching, converging|diverging, separating|adjective

asymptote:/ˈæsɪmptəʊt/ (US) /ˈæsɪmptəʊt/ (UK)| n.|渐近线|A line that a curve approaches, as it heads towards infinity, but never reaches.|The x-axis is an asymptote to the curve. x轴是这条曲线的渐近线。|limit|none|noun
