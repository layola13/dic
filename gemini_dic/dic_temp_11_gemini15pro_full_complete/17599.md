beautifulness:/ˈbjuːtɪflnəs/
n.
美丽，漂亮
The quality of being beautiful; attractiveness.
Her paintings capture the beautifulness of the natural world. 她的画捕捉到了自然世界的美丽。
近义词：beauty, loveliness, attractiveness, prettiness
反义词：ugliness, unattractiveness, homeliness
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From beautiful + -ness.  "Beautiful" comes from Middle English *beautevous, from Anglo-French *beauteus, from Old French beauteus "beautiful," from beauté "beauty," from Late Latin bellitatem (nominative bellitas) "beauty," from Latin bellus "beautiful, handsome, fine," of uncertain origin. The suffix "-ness" is a native English suffix forming nouns expressing a state, quality, or condition.
