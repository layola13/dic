hero-worshiped:/ˈhɪəroʊ ˈwɜːʃɪpt/ | adj. | 英雄崇拜的，对…盲目崇拜的 | Admiring someone excessively and treating them like a hero.  |  例句：The young boy hero-worshipped the famous basketball player.  这个小男孩对那位著名的篮球运动员非常崇拜。| 近义词：idolized, lionized | 反义词：disdainful, scornful | 词性：形容词
