idiophanous: /ɪˌdiˈɒfənəs/ | adj. | 透明的，半透明的 | (of minerals) Partially or imperfectly transparent; translucent.  Appearing transparent or translucent. | The idiophanous wings of the butterfly shimmered in the sunlight. 蝴蝶半透明的翅膀在阳光下闪烁。| The mineral quartz can sometimes be idiophanous. 石英矿有时是半透明的。 | 近义词: translucent, diaphanous, sheer, transparent | 反义词: opaque | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Greek *ἰδιοφανής (idiophanēs, “appearing peculiar, peculiar in appearance”), from ἴδιος (ídios, “one's own, peculiar, private”) + φαίνω (phaínō, “I bring to light, I show”).
