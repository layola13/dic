misrelating:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪtɪŋ/
verb
错误地联系；不恰当地联系
showing or creating a wrong or inappropriate relationship between things
He was misrelating cause and effect. 他错误地联系了因果关系。
misinterpreting, misrepresenting, distorting
correctly relating, accurately connecting
v.
