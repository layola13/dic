hernial:/ˈhɜːniəl/| adj.|疝气的；与疝有关的|Relating to or characterized by a hernia.  A hernia is a condition in which an organ or tissue protrudes through an opening in the muscle or tissue that holds it in place.|The doctor diagnosed a hernial protrusion. 医生诊断出疝气突出。|Synonyms: ruptured, protruded|Antonyms: intact, normal|adjective


hernia:/ˈhɜːniə/| n.|疝气|A condition in which part of an organ is displaced and protrudes through the wall of the cavity containing it.|He developed a hernia after lifting heavy weights. 他举重后得了疝气。|Synonyms: rupture, protrusion|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "normal anatomy")|noun
