expediment:/ɪkˈspedɪmənt/| n.|障碍，阻碍；累赘；麻烦事|something that makes it difficult to do or achieve something|例句：The lack of funding was a major impediment to the project. 资金不足是该项目的主要障碍。

例句：Her constant interruptions were an impediment to my work. 她不断的打扰阻碍了我的工作。|近义词：obstacle, hindrance, obstruction, barrier|反义词：aid, help, assistance|词性:noun
