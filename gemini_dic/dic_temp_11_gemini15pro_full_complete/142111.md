orienting:/ˈɔːriˌɛntɪŋ/ /ˈɔːrɪˌɛntɪŋ/ | v. | 确定方向，使适应，使熟悉 | Determining one's position or relation to surroundings; aligning or positioning something; familiarizing someone with new surroundings or circumstances. | Example: The compass is useful for orienting yourself in unfamiliar terrain. 指南针在不熟悉的地形中很有用，可以帮你确定方向。

orienting:/ˈɔːriˌɛntɪŋ/ /ˈɔːrɪˌɛntɪŋ/ | adj. | 定向的，适应的 | Serving to orient or familiarize. | Example: The introductory lecture was orienting for the new students.  介绍性的讲座帮助新生熟悉环境。

近义词: adapting, adjusting, familiarizing, positioning, aligning
反义词: disorienting, confusing
词性:  v. / adj.
词源: From Latin *oriens, orientis, present participle of oriri (“to rise”). Related to orient.
