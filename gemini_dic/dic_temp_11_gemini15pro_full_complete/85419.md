harvestless:/ˈhɑːrvɪstlɪs/| adj.|无收成的，歉收的|Without a harvest; unproductive.|The farmer experienced a harvestless year due to the drought. 由于干旱，农民经历了一个歉收的年份。|近义词：barren, unproductive, infertile|反义词:fruitful, fertile, productive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "harvest" (Old English *hærfest, from Proto-Germanic *harbistaz) + "-less" (Old English -lēas).
