energizer:/ˈenərˌdʒaɪzər/| n.|激励者；赋能者；兴奋剂； energizing activity|Something or someone that gives energy and enthusiasm.  |The coach gave an inspiring speech that acted as an energizer for the team. 教练发表了一场鼓舞人心的演讲，对团队起到了激励作用。|synonyms: stimulant, motivator, invigorator, boost, tonic|antonyms: depressant, downer, enervator|noun

energizers:/ˈenərˌdʒaɪzərz/| n.|激励者们；赋能者们；兴奋剂们；energizing activities|Plural form of "energizer," referring to multiple people or things that provide energy and enthusiasm. |Team-building activities can be effective energizers for employees. 团队建设活动可以有效地激励员工。|synonyms: stimulants, motivators, invigorators, boosts, tonics|antonyms: depressants, downers, enervators|noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From energize + -er.  Energize comes from energy + -ize.  Energy ultimately derives from the Greek "energeia" meaning activity, operation.
