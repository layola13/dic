barrable:/ˈbærəbl/| adj.|可闩住的，可阻挡的|Able to be barred; capable of being obstructed or blocked.|The door was barrable, providing extra security. 这扇门可以闩上，提供额外的安全保障。|Example 2:  The river was barrable with a dam. 这条河可以用大坝拦住。|近义词：lockable, securable, obstructable|反义词: open, accessible|词性:adjective
词源: From "bar" (verb, meaning to obstruct or fasten with a bar) + "-able" (suffix, meaning capable of being). 源自动词“bar”（意为用杆阻挡或固定）+后缀“-able”（意为能够）。
