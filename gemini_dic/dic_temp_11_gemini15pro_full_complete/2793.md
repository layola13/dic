advanceman: /ədˈvænsmən/ | n. | 先遣人员，预先安排者 | A person sent ahead to make arrangements for a visit by an important person, a performance, etc. |  The advanceman had already booked the hotel rooms and arranged for the press conference.  先遣人员已经预订了酒店房间并安排了新闻发布会。 | Synonyms: forerunner, precursor, herald | Antonyms: follower | noun

advanceman: /ədˈvænsmən/ | n. |  宣传员 | A person employed to publicize and promote a person, organization, or cause. | The advanceman distributed flyers and spoke to local media to generate interest in the upcoming event. 宣传员分发了传单并与当地媒体交谈，以引起人们对即将举行的活动的兴趣。 | Synonyms: publicist, promoter | Antonyms: detractor | noun
