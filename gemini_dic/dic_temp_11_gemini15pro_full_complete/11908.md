arrowroot:/ˈærəˌruːt/| n. | 竹芋粉，藕粉 | A starch obtained from the rhizomes of various tropical American plants. |  She used arrowroot to thicken the sauce. 她用竹芋粉使酱汁变稠。| 近义词: cornstarch, tapioca starch | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "wheat flour" as a contrasting thickening agent) | noun

arrowroot:/ˈærəˌruːt/| n. | 竹芋 | Any of various tropical American plants of the genus *Maranta*, from which arrowroot starch is obtained. | The arrowroot plant has large, ornamental leaves. 竹芋的叶子很大，很具有观赏性。 | 近义词: (No direct synonym for the plant itself, other Maranta species could be considered) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | noun
