Koblenz: /ˈkoʊblɛnts/ (US), /ˈkɔːblɛnts/ (UK)| n. | 科布伦茨 | A city in Germany situated at the confluence of the Rhine and Moselle rivers. | Koblenz is a beautiful city with a rich history. 科布伦茨是一座拥有丰富历史的美丽城市。| Koblenz is known for its Deutsches Eck, where the two rivers meet.  科布伦茨以其德意志之角而闻名，那里是两条河流的交汇处。| synonyms: city, municipality | antonyms: countryside, rural area | noun
