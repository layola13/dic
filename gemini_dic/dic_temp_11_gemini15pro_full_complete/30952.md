chaise:/ˈʃɛz/ (French pronunciation)| n.|轻便马车;  敞篷马车|A light open horse-drawn carriage, usually with a folding hood.|We took a leisurely ride through the park in a chaise. 我们乘坐一辆轻便马车悠闲地穿过公园。
