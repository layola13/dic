alibied:/ˈælɪbaɪd/| v.| 提供不在场证明；为...辩解| To have provided an alibi for someone or something; to have offered an excuse or justification.| He alibied his absence by claiming he was at a conference. 他声称自己在参加会议，以此作为缺席的不在场证明。| The company alibied the poor sales figures by blaming the economy.  公司将糟糕的销售数据归咎于经济不景气，以此为借口。|Synonyms: excused, justified, vindicated|Antonyms: accused, blamed, incriminated|verb
