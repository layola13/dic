incanous:/ɪnˈkeɪnəs/| adj.|灰白的，头发花白的|Covered with short, fine, whitish or grayish hairs or down, hoary.  Having a grayish-white appearance, as from age or weathering. |例句：The incanous old man sat on the park bench. 灰白头发的老人坐在公园的长椅上。

例句：The incanous leaves of the plant shimmered in the sunlight.  植物灰白的叶子在阳光下闪烁。|近义词：hoary, gray, greyish, grizzled, silvery|反义词：youthful, dark, black|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *incanus, from in- (“in, on”) + canus (“white, gray”).
