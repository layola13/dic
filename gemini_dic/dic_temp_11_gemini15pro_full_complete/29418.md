catadrome:/ˈkætəˌdroʊm/| n. |逆行;洄游;下溯|A migration of fish, such as eels, down rivers to the sea for spawning.|Salmon are anadromous, while eels are catadromous, migrating in the opposite direction. 鲑鱼是溯河洄游的，而鳗鱼是降河洄游的，迁移方向相反。|近义词：downstream migration|反义词: anadrome|词性:名词
