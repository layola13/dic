cofferer:/ˈkɒfərə(r)/ (US: /ˈkɔːfərər/) | n. | 金库保管员；司库；财政大臣 | a person who is in charge of a coffer (a strongbox or chest for storing valuables, especially money); a treasurer | The king's cofferer was responsible for collecting taxes. 国王的司库负责收税。| synonyms: treasurer, bursar, cashier | antonyms: debtor, borrower | noun


cofferer:/ˈkɒfərə(r)/ (US: /ˈkɔːfərər/) | n. | 保管贵重物品的人 | a person who keeps or guards valuable things | The museum's cofferer carefully cataloged the new artifacts. 博物馆的保管员仔细地将新文物编入目录。| synonyms: custodian, guardian, keeper | antonyms: thief, robber | noun
