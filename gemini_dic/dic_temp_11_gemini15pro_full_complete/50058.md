dense:/dɛns/| adj.|密集的，稠密的，浓厚的，愚钝的|Closely compacted in substance; crowded close together.|The forest was dense with trees. 森林里树木茂密。|近义词：thick, compact, concentrated, crowded, stupid|反义词：sparse, thin, light, transparent, intelligent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *densus* "thick, crowded, compact," perhaps related to *condensus* "thickened, condensed."
