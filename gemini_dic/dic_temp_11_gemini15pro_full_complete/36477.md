coleaders:/ˈkoʊˌliːdərz/
n.
共同领导人，共同领袖
people who share the responsibility of leading a group, organization, or project.
例句：The coleaders of the project presented their findings to the board. 项目的共同领导人向董事会提交了他们的调查结果。

例句：The two coleaders have very different leadership styles.  两位共同领导人的领导风格截然不同。

近义词：co-leaders, joint leaders, shared leaders
反义词：follower, subordinate
词性：名词
