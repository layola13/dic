out-boxes:/ˈaʊtˌbɑːksɪz/
|n.| 发件箱；已发送邮件|A place, either physical or virtual, where copies of letters or emails that have been sent are kept.|I always check my out-boxes to make sure the email was sent successfully. 我总是检查我的发件箱以确保电子邮件已成功发送。|Synonyms: sent folder, sent items, sent mail|Antonyms: in-boxes, inbox|noun
