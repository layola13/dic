cageful:/ˈkeɪdʒfʊl/ | n. | 一笼（人）；一笼的量 | A cage completely filled with something, typically people in a restricted or unpleasant situation. | The refugees were huddled together like a cageful of frightened animals.  难民们像一笼受惊的动物一样挤在一起。 | near synonyms: a crowd of (in a negative context), a group of (in a negative context) | antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider "liberated group") | noun
