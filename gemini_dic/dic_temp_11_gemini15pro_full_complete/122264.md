mitigable:/ˈmɪtɪɡəbl/
adjective
可减轻的，可缓和的
Capable of being mitigated or lessened in severity or intensity.
The risks associated with this project are mitigable with proper planning.  这个项目相关的风险可以通过适当的规划来减轻。
The environmental damage is mitigable if action is taken now. 如果现在采取行动，环境损害是可以减轻的。
近义词: reducible, alleviatable, lessenable
反义词: unmitigable, unavoidable, inescapable
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin mitigabilis, from mitigare "to soften, alleviate," from mitis "soft, mild" + -igare (frequentative suffix).
