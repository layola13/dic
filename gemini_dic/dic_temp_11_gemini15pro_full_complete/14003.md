auriphrygiate:/ɔːrɪˈfrɪdʒieɪt/
adjective
绣金边的，用金线绣的
Decorated with gold embroidery or needlework.
The queen wore an auriphrygiate gown. 女王穿着一件绣金边的礼服。

近义词:gold-embroidered, gold-worked, ornate, embellished
反义词:plain, simple, unembellished
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *auriphrygium, from aurum "gold" + Phrygium "Phrygian," referring to the richly embroidered cloth produced in Phrygia.
