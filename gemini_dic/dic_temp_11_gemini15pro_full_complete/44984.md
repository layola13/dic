crystallizable:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzəbl/
adjective
可结晶的
Able to be crystallized; capable of forming crystals.
The solution is crystallizable under the right conditions. 在适当的条件下，这种溶液可以结晶。
This substance is highly crystallizable. 这种物质很容易结晶。
近义词:crystallizing, crystalline
反义词:amorphous, noncrystalline
词性:adjective

crystallize:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪz/
verb
结晶，使结晶；使具体化，使明确
(of a substance) form crystals or cause a substance to form crystals.  Make (something) definite or precise.
The salt crystallized as the water evaporated. 随着水分蒸发，盐结晶了。
His ideas crystallized into a solid proposal. 他的想法具体化成了一份完整的提案。
近义词:solidify, congeal, concrete
反义词:melt, dissolve, liquefy
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cristalisable*, from cristalliser (see crystallize) + -able.
