hagiographer:/ˌhæɡiˈɒɡrəfər/ | n. |圣徒传记作者；吹捧者|A writer of saints' lives; a biographer who idealizes or idolizes the subject.|The hagiographer portrayed the leader as a flawless visionary.  这位圣徒传记作者将这位领导人描绘成一个完美无瑕的远见卓识者。|Synonyms: biographer, eulogist, panegyrist|Antonyms: critic, detractor|noun

hagiographist:/ˌhæɡiˈɒɡrəfɪst/ | n. |圣徒传记作者；吹捧者|A writer of saints' lives; a biographer who idealizes or idolizes the subject.|The hagiographist glossed over the leader's flaws and focused solely on his achievements.  这位圣徒传记作者掩盖了领导人的缺点，只关注他的成就。|Synonyms: biographer, eulogist, panegyrist|Antonyms: critic, detractor|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
