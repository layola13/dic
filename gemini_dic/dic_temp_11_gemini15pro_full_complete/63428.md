entraps:/ɪnˈtræps/| v. |诱捕，使陷入圈套，使落入陷阱|To catch someone in a trap, either literally or figuratively.  To cause someone to become involved in a difficult or compromising situation, often by deceit or trickery.|The hunter entrapped the rabbit in a snare. 猎人用陷阱诱捕了兔子。|Synonyms: ensnare, trap, snare, catch, tangle, enmesh|Antonyms: release, free, liberate|verb
