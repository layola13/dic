boredom:/ˈbɔːrdəm/ (US) /ˈbɔːdəm/ (UK)| n.|无聊，厌倦|The state of being weary and restless through lack of interest.|She suffered from acute boredom during the long flight. 她在这漫长的飞行中感到极其无聊。|
近义词：ennui, tedium, monotony, weariness|反义词：interest, excitement, engagement, amusement|词性:名词


Etymology:
From *bore* + *-dom*,  *bore* comes from Old English *borian* "to pierce, perforate," related to German *bohren* "to drill." The suffix *-dom* forms abstract nouns of state, condition, or domain.
