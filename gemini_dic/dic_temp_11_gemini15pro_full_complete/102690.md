jammers:/ˈdʒæmərz/| n.|干扰器；干扰者；阻塞者；卡纸|A device that transmits radio signals to block, jam, or interfere with authorized radio communications.|The military uses jammers to disrupt enemy communications.  军方使用干扰器来干扰敌人的通讯。|The prison installed jammers to prevent inmates from using cell phones. 监狱安装了干扰器，以防止囚犯使用手机。|近义词：blockers, disruptors, inhibitors|反义词：amplifiers, boosters|词性:名词


词源: From the verb "jam" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Jam" in the sense of "to block or obstruct" dates back to the 14th century.
