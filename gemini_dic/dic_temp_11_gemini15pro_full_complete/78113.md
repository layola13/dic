geologist:/dʒiˈɒlədʒɪst/ /dʒiˈɑːlədʒɪst/| n.|地质学家|A scientist who studies the earth, its structure, and its history.  A person who is an expert in geology.|The geologist examined the rock formations to determine their age.| 近义词:earth scientist, geoscientist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From geology + -ist. Geology comes from the Greek words gē (earth) and -logia (study of).
