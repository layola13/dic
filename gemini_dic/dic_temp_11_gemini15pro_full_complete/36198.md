cogent:/ˈkoʊdʒənt/| adj.|令人信服的；有说服力的| (of an argument or case) clear, logical, and convincing.|Her cogent arguments persuaded the jury.|近义词:convincing, persuasive, compelling, strong, forceful, effective, telling, valid, sound, weighty, impactful|反义词:unconvincing, weak, ineffective, invalid, unsound, specious|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *cogentem*, present participle of *cogere* "to compel, collect, bring together," from *co-* "together" + *agere* "to drive, lead, act."
