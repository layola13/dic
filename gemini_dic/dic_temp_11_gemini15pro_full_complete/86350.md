hecticness:/ˈhɛktɪknəs/
noun
忙碌；紧张
The state of being hectic; a busy and frantic activity.
The hecticness of modern life can be overwhelming.  现代生活的忙碌可能会让人难以承受。
近义词: frenzy, bustle, chaos, rush
反义词: calmness, tranquility, peace, quiet
词性: noun

Etymology:  From hectic + -ness. Hectic from Greek *hektikós "habitual," from *héxis "habit, state," related to ekhein "to have, hold."
