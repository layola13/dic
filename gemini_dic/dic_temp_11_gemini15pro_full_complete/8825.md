antievolutionism:/ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪzəm/
n.
反进化论
The belief that the theory of evolution is false and should be opposed.
Antievolutionism has been a recurring theme in some religious and social circles.  反进化论在一些宗教和社会圈子里一直是一个反复出现的主题。
Creationism is a common form of antievolutionism. 创造论是反进化论的一种常见形式。
近义词:creationism
反义词:evolutionism
词性:noun
