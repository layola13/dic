endogenous:/ɛnˈdɒdʒɪnəs/ /ɛnˈdɑːdʒənəs/| adj.|内源的，内生的|Originating or produced within an organism, tissue, or cell.|例句：Endogenous hormones are produced by the body itself.  体内激素是由身体自身产生的。

例句：The endogenous growth theory emphasizes innovation as a key driver of economic growth. 内生增长理论强调创新是经济增长的关键驱动力。|近义词：internal, inherent, innate|反义词：exogenous, external|词性:adjective

词源: From Greek *éndon* "within" + *-genēs* "produced, born."
