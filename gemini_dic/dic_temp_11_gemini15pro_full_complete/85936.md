headlongness:/ˈhɛdˌlɔŋnɪs/| n. |鲁莽，轻率，仓促|The quality or state of being headlong; rashness; impetuosity.|He jumped into the project with a certain headlongness that worried his colleagues. 他一头扎进这个项目，其鲁莽程度让他的同事们感到担忧。|Synonyms: recklessness, impetuosity, rashness, precipitateness, haste, foolhardiness|Antonyms: caution, prudence, deliberation, circumspection|noun

headlong:/ˈhɛdˌlɔŋ/| adj. adv. |轻率的，鲁莽的；头朝前的|Done with the head foremost; rashly; hastily.|She ran headlong into the crowd. 她一头扎进了人群。|Synonyms: rashly, recklessly, hastily, precipitately|Antonyms: cautiously, prudently, deliberately|adjective, adverb
