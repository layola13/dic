craggedly:/ˈkræɡɪdli/ | adv. |崎岖地，陡峭地 |Characterized by or full of crags; ruggedly. |The path wound craggedly up the mountainside. 这条小路崎岖地蜿蜒上山。|近义词：roughly, ruggedly, unevenly |反义词：smoothly, evenly |词性:副词

词源: From craggy + -ly.  Ultimately from the Scottish Gaelic word *creag*, meaning "rock."
