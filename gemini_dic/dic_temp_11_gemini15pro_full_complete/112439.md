lotus:/ˈloʊtəs/
n.
莲花
An aquatic plant with large, showy flowers and flat, rounded leaves.  Often symbolic of purity and enlightenment in Eastern religions.
The lotus flower is a symbol of purity. 莲花是纯洁的象征。
synonyms: water lily, nenuphar
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a flower, perhaps consider concepts like decay, wilting)
