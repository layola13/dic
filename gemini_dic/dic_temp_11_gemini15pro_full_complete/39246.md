congree:/kɒŋˈɡriː/ /kɑːŋˈɡriː/
verb
一致; 符合; 融洽
To agree; to accord; to be in harmony.
例句：Their views on the project congred with ours. 他们对这个项目的看法与我们一致。

例句：The witness's testimony congred with the physical evidence. 目击者的证词与物证相符。

近义词：agree, accord, harmonize, coincide, conform
反义词：disagree, differ, clash, conflict
词性：verb (动词)
