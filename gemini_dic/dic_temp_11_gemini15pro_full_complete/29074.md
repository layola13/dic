carvene:/ˈkɑːrviːn/| n.|蒎烯；香芹烯|A colorless liquid hydrocarbon, C10H16, found in caraway oil and used in perfumes and flavorings. It is a limonene isomer.|Carvene is used to give a distinct aroma to certain perfumes.|近义词：limonene, terpene|反义词:|noun
