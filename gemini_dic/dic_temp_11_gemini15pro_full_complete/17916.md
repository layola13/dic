bees:/biːz/
n.
蜜蜂（复数）
Plural form of "bee," a flying insect known for producing honey and beeswax.
The bees were buzzing around the hive. 蜜蜂在蜂巢周围嗡嗡作响。
The orchard relies on bees for pollination. 果园依靠蜜蜂授粉。
近义词：honeybees, pollinators
反义词: 无
词性: 名词
