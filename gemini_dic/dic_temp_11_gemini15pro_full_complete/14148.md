authorizable:/ˈɔːθəˌraɪzəbl/
adjective
可授权的
able to be authorized.
例句：The contract contains several authorizable clauses.  合同包含几个可授权的条款。

例句：Only specific personnel have authorizable access to the secure server. 只有特定人员拥有可授权访问安全服务器的权限。

近义词：approvable, permissible, sanctionable
反义词：unauthorizable, forbidden, prohibited
词性: adjective

词源:
From authorize + -able. Authorize comes from Old French auctoriser, from Latin auctorizare "to authorize," from auctor "author."
