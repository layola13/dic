heteroecious:/ˌhɛtərˈiːʃəs/
adjective
异株的
(of rust fungi) requiring two different host species to complete its life cycle.
The heteroecious rust fungus requires both wheat and barberry to complete its life cycle.  这种异株锈菌需要小麦和小檗才能完成其生命周期。
The wheat leaf rust is a heteroecious fungus. 小麦叶锈病是一种异株真菌。
synonyms:  autoecious (antonym)
antonyms:  autoecious
adjective
