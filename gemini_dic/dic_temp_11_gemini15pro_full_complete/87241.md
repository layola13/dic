heretification:/ˌhɛrɪtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |异端化，宣布…为异端|The act of declaring something or someone heretical.|The heretification of Galileo by the church stunted scientific progress for years. 教会将伽利略定为异端，多年来阻碍了科学的进步。|近义词：condemnation, denunciation|反义词：sanctification, canonization|词性:名词

词源: From Medieval Latin *haeretificatio*, from Late Latin haereticus (“heretical”) + -ficatio. Ultimately from Ancient Greek αἵρεσις (haíresis, “choice, sect”).
