hotpress:/ˈhɒtˌprɛs/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtˌprɛs/ (US)| n. v.|热压机，热压；用热压机压|A machine used for pressing materials using heat and pressure.  Also, the act of using such a machine.|The vinyl record was made using a hotpress for a superior finish.|Synonyms: press, mangle|Antonyms: (For the verb) cool, air dry|noun, verb

hot-press:/ˈhɒtˌprɛs/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtˌprɛs/ (US)| adj.|热压的|Made or finished using a hotpress.|The hot-press paper had a smooth, glossy surface.|Synonyms: pressed, calendered|Antonyms: unpressed, uncalendered|adjective
