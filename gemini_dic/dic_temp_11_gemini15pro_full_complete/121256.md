misestimating:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪtɪŋ/
verb
错误估计；估错
Estimating something incorrectly.
He misevaluated the situation, leading to a series of bad decisions. 他错误地评估了情况，导致了一系列糟糕的决定。
misjudging, undervaluing, overestimating
correctly estimating, accurately assessing
v.
