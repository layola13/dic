happiest:/ˈhæpiɪst/ | adj. | 最幸福的 |  Superlative form of "happy," meaning experiencing the greatest degree of joy or contentment. |  She had the happiest birthday of her life. 她度过了她一生中最幸福的生日。|  This is the happiest I've ever been. 这是我迄今为止最幸福的时刻。 | 近义词: most joyful, most cheerful, most content | 反义词: saddest, most miserable, most unhappy | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *happieste, superlative of happy, from Old Norse *happ, meaning "luck, chance, fortune."
