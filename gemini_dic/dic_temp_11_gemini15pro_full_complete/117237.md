mebibit:/ˈmɛbɪbɪt/| n. |兆比特|A mebibit is a unit of information or computer storage equal to 2^20 megabits or 1,048,576 megabits.  It is a member of a set of binary prefixes defined by the International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC).|My new hard drive has a capacity of several mebibits.|近义词: Mebit|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|词性:noun
