firstperson:/ˈfɜːst ˈpɜːsn/ /ˈfɝːst ˈpɝːsn/
|adj. n.|第一人称|Referring to the grammatical person used by a speaker to refer to themselves or a group including themselves, or to a narrative perspective from the speaker's point of view. |She wrote the story in the first person. 她以第一人称写了这个故事。|近义词：personal, subjective|反义词:third-person, second-person|词性:形容词，名词
