columbic: /kəˈlʌmbɪk/ | adj. | 库仑的；关于库仑力的 | Relating to Coulomb's law, which describes the electrostatic force of attraction or repulsion between charged particles. | The columbic interaction between the ions is strong. 离子间的库仑相互作用很强。|  The columbic force keeps the electrons bound to the nucleus. 库仑力使电子束缚在原子核周围。 | 近义词: electrostatic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider concepts like "gravitational" in specific contexts) | 词性: adjective

词源: From the surname of Charles-Augustin de Coulomb (1736-1806), French physicist who formulated Coulomb's law.
