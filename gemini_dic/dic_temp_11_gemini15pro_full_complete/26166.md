butanes:/ˈbjuːteɪnz/ | n. | 丁烷 |  A flammable hydrocarbon gas of the alkane series, existing in two isomeric forms, n-butane and isobutane, used in bottled gas. |  Butanes are commonly used as fuels. 丁烷通常用作燃料。| Butane is stored in liquid form in cylinders. 丁烷以液态储存在钢瓶中。| synonyms: alkane, hydrocarbon | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a chemical compound) | noun
