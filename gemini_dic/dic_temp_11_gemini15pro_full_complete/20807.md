blasphemes:/blæsˈfiːmz/| v.|亵渎，辱骂(神明)，| speaks irreverently about God or sacred things. | He blasphemes against God. 他亵渎了上帝。|  synonyms: curses, reviles, profanes | antonyms: reveres, worships | verb (third-person singular simple present blasphemes, present participle blaspheming, simple past and past participle blasphemed)


blasphemy:/ˈblæsfəmi/| n.|亵渎，渎神，|  the act or offense of speaking sacrilegiously about God or sacred things; profane talk. |  His words were considered blasphemy. 他的话被认为是亵渎神明的。 | synonyms: sacrilege, profanity, irreverence | antonyms: reverence, piety | noun (plural blasphemies)


词源:
From Middle English *blasfemen, from Old French blasfemer, from Late Latin blasphemare, from Ancient Greek βλασφημεῖν (blasphēmeîn, “to speak evil of, slander”), from βλάπτειν (bláptein, “to hurt, harm”) + φήμη (phḗmē, “speech, utterance, rumor, report”).
