elitists:/ɪˈliːtɪsts/ | n. |精英主义者；杰出人物；上层人士|A person who believes that a society or system should be led by an elite; a member of such an elite.| The elitists in the art world often dismiss popular art forms.  艺术界的精英主义者常常对流行的艺术形式不屑一顾。 |  近义词: snobs, aristocrats, highbrows | 反义词: populists, egalitarians | 词性: 名词

elitist:/ɪˈliːtɪst/ | adj. |精英主义的；优秀人才的|Relating to or supporting the belief that a society or system should be led by an elite.|  The elitist attitude of the university's admissions committee drew criticism. 这所大学招生委员会的精英主义态度招致了批评。 | 近义词: exclusive, snobbish, aristocratic | 反义词: egalitarian, democratic, populist | 词性: 形容词
