gesticulate:/dʒɛˈstɪkjʊleɪt/
verb
做手势；用手势表达
To make gestures, especially when speaking, to emphasize one's words or to express an idea or feeling.
She gesticulated wildly as she described the accident. 她描述事故时疯狂地做着手势。
The speaker gesticulated to emphasize his point. 演讲者做手势来强调他的观点。
synonym: gesture, signal, motion, wave
antonym: be still, freeze
词性: 动词
