archaeologies:/ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒiz/
n.
考古学（archaeology 的复数形式）
the study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and physical remains.
Archaeologies from different regions reveal unique cultural practices.  不同地区的考古学揭示了独特的文化习俗。
近义词:excavations, studies
反义词:(No direct antonym, potentially "fabrications" in a context discussing historical accuracy)
词性:Noun
