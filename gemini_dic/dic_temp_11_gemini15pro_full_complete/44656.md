cru:/kruː/| n.| 心脏；核心；关键；精髓|The cru of the matter is that we don't have enough money. 这件事的核心是我们没有足够的钱。|Example: The cru of his argument was that education is the key to success.  他论证的核心是教育是成功的关键。

Example:  Understanding the cru of the problem is essential to finding a solution. 了解问题的核心对于找到解决方案至关重要。

|Synonym: core, heart, essence, gist, kernel|Antonym: periphery, exterior, surface|noun
