lampblack:/ˈlæmpˌblæk/| n. |灯黑，炭黑|A fine black soot produced by the incomplete burning of oil, resin, or other organic substances, used as a pigment.|The artist used lampblack to create a deep, rich shadow in the painting. 艺术家用灯黑在画中创造了一个深邃而丰富的阴影。|例句：The ink was made from lampblack and gum arabic.  这种墨水是由灯黑和阿拉伯树胶制成的。


例句：Lampblack is a traditional pigment used in inks and paints. 灯黑是一种用于油墨和油漆的传统颜料。|近义词：carbon black, soot|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider white pigments like titanium dioxide)|词性:noun
