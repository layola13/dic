over-discuss:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskʌs/| v. |过度讨论，过分讨论|To discuss something excessively, beyond what is necessary or productive.|We over-discussed the issue and ended up going in circles. 我们过度讨论了这个问题，结果一直在兜圈子。|The team over-discussed the project, leading to analysis paralysis.  团队过度讨论了这个项目，导致分析瘫痪。|近义词：overanalyze, belabor|反义词: under-discuss (not a common word), skim over|词性:动词
