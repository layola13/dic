axunge:/ˈæksʌndʒ/ | n. | 猪油；猪脂 | Animal fat, especially lard.  It is a semi-solid white fat product obtained by rendering the fatty tissue of a pig.  Historically used in cooking and for medicinal purposes. |  The recipe called for a tablespoon of axunge.  这食谱需要一汤匙猪油。| Synonyms: lard, grease, fat | Antonyms: lean (adj.) | noun
