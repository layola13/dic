misplaced: /ˌmɪsˈpleɪst/ | adj. | 放错地方的；不合适的；记错的 | not put in the correct or usual place; directed towards someone or something unsuitable | I have misplaced my keys again. 我又把钥匙放错地方了。| synonyms: lost, mislaid, out of place | antonyms: found, in place | adjective

misplaced: /ˌmɪsˈpleɪst/ |  v. |  放错地方 | simple past tense and past participle of misplace | I misplaced my wallet and couldn't find it anywhere. 我把钱包放错了地方，怎么也找不到。 | synonyms: mislay, lose | antonyms: find, locate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + placed, past participle of place.  The prefix "mis-" signifies "wrong" or "badly."  Therefore, "misplaced" literally means "wrongly placed."
