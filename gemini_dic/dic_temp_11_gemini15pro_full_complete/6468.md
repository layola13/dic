ampulla:/æmˈpʌlə/ | n. | 安瓿；壶腹 |A dilated saclike cavity.  A small, sealed glass container that holds a sterile solution for injection. | The doctor broke the ampulla containing the vaccine. 医生打破了装着疫苗的安瓿。| synonyms: vial, ampoule | antonyms: None | noun

ampulla:/æmˈpʌlə/ | n. | 壶腹 | An anatomical sac or cavity, especially one shaped like a Roman ampulla. A localized dilation of a canal or duct, especially of the semicircular canals of the ear. | The ampulla of Vater is where the pancreatic duct and common bile duct enter the duodenum. 壶腹是胰管和胆总管进入十二指肠的地方。| synonyms: sac, cavity | antonyms: None | noun
