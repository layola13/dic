keep-a-stiff-upper-lip:/kiːp ə stɪf ˈʌpər lɪp/ | idiom | 保持坚忍，不动声色 | To remain calm and unemotional, especially during a difficult situation.  To not show your feelings when you are upset. | Example: Despite the devastating news, she kept a stiff upper lip and carried on. 尽管听到了毁灭性的消息，她仍然保持坚忍，继续前进。

Example: He was very disappointed not to get the job, but he kept a stiff upper lip. 他对没有得到这份工作感到非常失望，但他不动声色。 | 近义词: stay calm, remain composed, keep one's composure, not show one's feelings | 反义词: lose control, break down, get emotional, show one's feelings | idiom
