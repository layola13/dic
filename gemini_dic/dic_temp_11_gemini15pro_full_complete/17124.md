batterer:/ˈbætərər/| n.|殴打者，施暴者|A person who batters another, especially one who beats a spouse or partner.|The police arrested the batterer after he assaulted his wife. 警方在施暴者殴打妻子后逮捕了他。|例句：The batterer was sentenced to five years in prison. 施暴者被判处五年监禁。

例句：She was afraid of her batterer and sought refuge at a shelter. 她害怕她的施暴者，并在庇护所寻求庇护。|近义词：abuser, assailant, attacker|反义词:victim|词性:noun
