gingers:/ˈdʒɪndʒərz/| n.|姜; 生姜; 姜黄色; 有活力的人|A pungent, spicy rhizome, the root of the ginger plant, used in cooking and medicine.  Also, a yellowish-brown or reddish-brown color, like that of ginger root. Can also refer to a person with red or yellowish-brown hair, or a lively, spirited individual.|例句：She added fresh gingers to the stir-fry. 她往炒菜里加了鲜姜。

例句：He has a shock of gingers hair. 他有一头火红的姜黄色头发。|近义词：ginger root, spice, pep, vigor|反义词:lethargy, apathy|词性:noun
