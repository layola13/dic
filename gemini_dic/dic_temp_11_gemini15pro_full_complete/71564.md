flasher:/ˈflæʃər/ (US) /ˈflæʃə/ (UK)| n.|暴露狂；闪光灯；转向灯|A person, especially a man, who exposes their genitals in public.  A device that flashes light, especially a light on a vehicle that indicates it is turning.|The police arrested a flasher in the park. 警方在公园逮捕了一名暴露狂。|The car's flasher wasn't working. 汽车的转向灯坏了。|Synonyms: exhibitionist (for the person), blinker (for the light), indicator (for the light)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for the person)  (For the light) constant light|noun

flasher:/ˈflæʃər/ (US) /ˈflæʃə/ (UK)| n.|闪光装置; 闪烁器|A device that produces flashes of light.|The photographer used a flasher to illuminate the dark room. 摄影师用闪光灯照亮了黑暗的房间。|Synonyms: strobe, flash|Antonyms: continuous light|noun
