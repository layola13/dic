brocaded:/brəˈkeɪdɪd/
adjective
织锦的; 锦缎的
Woven with a raised design; made of brocade.  Decorated with or as if with brocade.
例句：She wore a brocaded gown to the ball. 她穿着一件织锦长袍去参加舞会。

例句：The walls were covered in brocaded silk. 墙壁上覆盖着织锦绸缎。
近义词：embroidered, figured, ornamented, patterned
反义词：plain, simple, unadorned
词性：adjective

brocade:/brəˈkeɪd/
noun
织锦，锦缎
A rich fabric woven with a raised design, typically with gold or silver thread.
例句：The cushions were made of a beautiful brocade.  这些垫子是用漂亮的织锦做的。

例句：The queen wore a gown of gold brocade.  女王穿着一件金锦长袍。
近义词：tapestry, damask
反义词：None
词性：noun

词源: From Italian *broccato*, past participle of *broccare* "to decorate with studs," from *brocco* "small nail, tack, sprout."
