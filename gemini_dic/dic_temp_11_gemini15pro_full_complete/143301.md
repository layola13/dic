outdriven:/ˌaʊtˈdrɪvn/
verb
超越，胜过，以更大的动力驱使
surpassed in driving; propelled with greater force.
He was outdriven by his competitor in the final lap of the race. 他在比赛的最后一圈被他的对手超越了。
近义词: surpassed, outpaced, overtaken
反义词: outdone, defeated
词性:Verb (past participle)

outdriven:/ˌaʊtˈdrɪvn/
adjective
被超越的，被胜过的
surpassed or defeated in driving.
The outdriven team was disappointed with their performance. 被超越的队伍对他们的表现感到失望。
近义词: defeated, conquered
反义词: victorious, winning
词性:Adjective
