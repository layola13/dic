misinformation:/ˌmɪsɪnfərˈmeɪʃən/| n. |错误信息；虚假信息|False or inaccurate information, especially that which is intended to deceive.|例句：The spread of misinformation online is a serious problem. 网上错误信息的传播是一个严重的问题。

例句：They were deliberately fed misinformation. 他们被故意灌输了错误信息。


近义词：disinformation, falsehood, untruth, lie, fallacy|反义词：truth, accuracy, fact, correctness|词性：名词
