broken:/ˈbrəʊkən/ (UK) /ˈbroʊkən/ (US)| adj.|破碎的；损坏的；折断的；不完整的；中断的；不流畅的；违背的；破产的；（精神上）崩溃的；（身体）垮掉的|Something that is broken is damaged and separated into pieces. It can also refer to something that is not functioning correctly, interrupted, or violated. In a figurative sense, it can describe someone who is emotionally or physically devastated, or financially ruined.|例句：The vase is broken. 花瓶碎了。

例句：He has a broken leg. 他的腿断了。
