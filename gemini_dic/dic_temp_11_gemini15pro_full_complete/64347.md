eraser:/ɪˈreɪsər/ (US), /ɪˈreɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|橡皮擦；板擦|A tool used to remove marks made by pencil, pen, or chalk.|例句：Can I borrow your eraser? 我可以借你的橡皮擦吗？

例句：The teacher used an eraser to clean the blackboard. 老师用板擦擦干净了黑板。

近义词：rubber (UK), wiper|反义词:pencil, pen, chalk|词性:noun
