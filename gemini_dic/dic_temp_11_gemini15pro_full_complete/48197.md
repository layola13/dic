decompressions:/diːkəmˈprɛʃənz/
|n.|减压，解压|The act or process of decreasing the pressure of a gas or the pressure on a body or substance.  The divers ascended slowly to allow for gradual decompression. 潜水员缓慢上升以便逐渐减压。|
|近义词:release, reduction, alleviation|
|反义词:compression, pressurization|
|词性:名词|

decompression:/diːkəmˈprɛʃənz/
|n.|（计算机）解压缩|The process of expanding compressed data back to its original size.  The file needs to be decompressed before you can use it.  这个文件需要解压缩后才能使用。|
|近义词:expansion, extraction|
|反义词:compression|
|词性:名词|
