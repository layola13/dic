cotransduced:/ˌkoʊtrænsˈdjuːst/
| adj. |共同转导的|Transferred together into a cell, typically by viral vectors carrying multiple genes of interest.|
例句：The cells were cotransduced with lentiviral vectors expressing GFP and the gene of interest. 细胞用表达GFP和目的基因的慢病毒载体共同转导。

例句：Cotransduction frequency was assessed by analyzing the percentage of cells expressing both markers. 通过分析表达两种标记的细胞的百分比来评估共同转导频率。
|近义词：co-transfected, simultaneously transduced|
|反义词:individually transduced|
|词性:adjective|
