leave-taking: /ˈliːvˌteɪkɪŋ/ (British), /ˈliːvˌteɪkɪŋ/ (American)| n.|告别，告辞|The leave-taking was brief and awkward. 告别简短而尴尬。|例句：After a tearful leave-taking, she boarded the train.  在泪流满面的告别之后，她登上了火车。

例句：He made his leave-taking as quickly as possible. 他尽快地告辞了。

近义词：farewell, departure, goodbye|反义词：arrival, greeting, welcome|词性:名词

词源：From "leave" (Old English *lēofan "to allow, grant permission, leave behind") + "taking" (from "take" - Old Norse taka "to grasp, seize, lay hold of").  The compound noun signifies the act of taking one's leave.
