off-licensee:/ˌɒfˈlaɪsənˈsiː/ (UK) /ˌɔːfˈlaɪsənˈsiː/ (US)| n.|持有执照的非酒吧酒类零售商|A person or business holding a license to sell alcoholic beverages for consumption off the premises.|例句：The off-licensee was required to close at 10 pm. 这家非酒吧酒类零售商被要求晚上10点关门。

例句：I bought a bottle of wine from the local off-licensee. 我从当地的非酒吧酒类零售商那里买了一瓶葡萄酒。

近义词：liquor store, bottle shop (especially Australian), wine merchant|反义词:pub, bar|词性:名词
