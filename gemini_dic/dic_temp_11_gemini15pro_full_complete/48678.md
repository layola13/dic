defero:/dɪˈfɜːroʊ/ (US), /dɪˈfɛroʊ/ (UK)| v. |推迟，延期；把…提交（给），委托（给）；给予，献出；尊敬，服从|To postpone or delay something; to submit or entrust something to someone; to offer or give something; to show respect or deference to someone.|The meeting has been deferred to next week. 会议已推迟到下周。|Synonyms: postpone, delay, adjourn, submit, entrust, offer, give, respect, defer|Antonyms: expedite, hasten, advance, withdraw, withhold, disrespect, disobey|verb
