acquist:/ækˈkwɪst/ |n.|获得物，获得，收购|Something acquired or gained, especially an addition to a collection.  It can also refer to the act of acquiring something.|例句：The museum's latest acquist is a rare painting by Van Gogh.  博物馆最新的藏品是一幅梵高的稀有画作。

例句：The company's recent acquist of a smaller rival strengthened its market position. 公司最近收购了一家规模较小的竞争对手，加强了其市场地位。|近义词：acquisition, purchase, gain|反义词:loss, forfeiture|词性:noun
Etymology: From Italian *acquisto*, derived from the Latin *acquisitum*, the past participle of *acquiro*  "to acquire," itself compounded from *ad-* "to" + *quaero* "to seek, gain."
