innkeeper:/ˈɪnˌkiːpər/| n.|旅店老板，店主|A person who owns or manages an inn.|The innkeeper welcomed us with a warm smile.  旅店老板热情地微笑着欢迎我们。|例句：The innkeeper offered us a comfortable room for the night.  旅店老板给我们提供了一间舒适的房间过夜。|近义词：host, hotelier, publican|反义词:guest, customer|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *innekepere*, equivalent to *inn* + *keeper*.  Equivalent to Dutch *herbergier*, German *Herbergsvater*, *Herbergsmutter*.
