footnoting:/ˈfʊtnəʊtɪŋ/| n.|脚注；加脚注|The act of adding footnotes to a text.  The process of providing supplementary information or citations at the bottom of a page.|The academic paper included extensive footnoting to support its claims. 这篇学术论文包含大量的脚注来支持其论点。|近义词：annotation, referencing, citation|反义词:|名词
