gentil:/ʒɑ̃.ti/ (French), /dʒenˈtiːl/ (anglicized)| adj.|亲切的；和蔼的；有礼貌的|Showing kindness and courtesy; agreeable.|She was a very gentil hostess, making sure everyone felt welcome. 她是一位非常和蔼的女主人，确保每个人都感到宾至如归。|例句：He greeted me with a gentil smile. 他以亲切的微笑迎接我。|近义词：kind, courteous, polite, gracious, amiable|反义词：rude, unkind, impolite, discourteous|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French gentil, from Latin gentilis "of the same clan or family," from gens "clan, race, nation, family."  The meaning shifted from "noble, of noble birth" to "courteous, refined" in both French and English.
