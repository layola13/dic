engineering:/ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/
n.|工程；工程学|The application of science and mathematics to develop useful structures and machines.| He studied engineering at university. 他在大学学习工程学。
近义词:technology, construction
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) destruction, dismantling
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "engin" (skill, trick, machine), from Latin "ingenium" (natural talent, invention).  The meaning shifted to include the construction of military engines, and later broadened to encompass civilian applications of science and technology.
