expectorate: /ɪkˈspɛktəreɪt/ | v. | 吐痰，咳出 | To cough or spit out phlegm from the throat or lungs. |  He expectorated a large wad of phlegm into the tissue. 他把一大团痰吐进了纸巾里。

The doctor asked him to expectorate into the cup. 医生让他把痰吐到杯子里。


| 近义词: cough up, spit, hawk | 反义词: swallow, ingest | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin expectorare ("to clear the breast, drive out from the chest"), from ex- ("out") + pector- (stem of pectus "breast") + -are (infinitive suffix).
