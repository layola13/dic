eurosceptic: /ˌjʊərəˈskɛptɪk/ /ˌjʊroʊˈskɛptɪk/
noun, adjective|欧洲怀疑论者；对欧盟持怀疑态度的|A person who is opposed to increasing the powers of the European Union, or to the idea of European political unification.  Also used as an adjective to describe such a person or their views.|例句：He is a well-known eurosceptic. 他是一位著名的欧洲怀疑论者。|例句：The eurosceptic wing of the party is gaining influence. 该党欧洲怀疑派正在获得影响力。|近义词：anti-European, EU-critical|反义词：pro-European, Europhile|词性: noun, adjective
