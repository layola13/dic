etiolin: /ˈiːtiəʊlɪn/ | n. | 乙醇啉 | A yellow crystalline substance found in etiolated plant tissues, especially in seedlings grown in the dark.  It is a type of chlorophyll precursor. |  Etiolin accumulates in plants deprived of light.  植物缺乏光照时会积累乙醇啉。| The researchers measured the etiolin content in the dark-grown seedlings.  研究人员测量了在黑暗中生长的幼苗中的乙醇啉含量。 | synonyms: protochlorophyll, chlorophyll precursor | antonyms: chlorophyll | noun
