deal someone in:/diːl ˈsʌmwʌn ɪn/| phrasal verb |把某人拉入伙；让某人参与|To include someone in a plan, activity, or business deal.|例句：They decided to deal her in on the project. 他们决定让她参与这个项目。|近义词：include, involve, let in on|反义词：exclude, leave out|词性：动词短语
