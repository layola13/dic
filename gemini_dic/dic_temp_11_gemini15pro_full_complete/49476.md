delusional:/dɪˈluːʒənəl/
adjective
妄想的，错觉的
Characterized by or based on delusion.
He had delusional beliefs about being a superhero. 他妄想自己是超级英雄。

She suffers from delusional disorder. 她患有妄想症。

近义词: psychotic, hallucinatory, deranged, mad, insane
反义词: rational, sane, lucid, realistic

词性: adjective
