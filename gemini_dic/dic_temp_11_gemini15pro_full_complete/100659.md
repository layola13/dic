intoner:/ɪnˈtəʊnər/ (UK) /ɪnˈtoʊnər/ (US)| n.|调音器, 音调器|A device used to tune musical instruments to the correct pitch.|例句：The guitarist used an electronic intoner to tune his guitar. 吉他手用电子调音器给他的吉他调音。

例句：She relies on a tuning fork as an intoner for her violin. 她依靠音叉作为她小提琴的调音器。|近义词：tuner, pitch pipe|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "intone" + "-er". "Intone" comes from Latin "intonare"  (to thunder, to speak in a loud voice), from "in-" (in, on) + "tonare" (to thunder). The "-er" suffix denotes an instrument or agent.
