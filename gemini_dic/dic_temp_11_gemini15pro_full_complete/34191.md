clamp-down-on:/ˈklæmp daʊn ɑːn/ | phrasal verb |压制；取缔；禁止；打击| To suppress or restrict something firmly. | The government announced a clamp-down on illegal immigration. 政府宣布要打击非法移民。|
The school initiated a clamp-down on cheating. 学校开始严厉打击作弊行为。| synonyms: suppression, crackdown, curb, restriction | antonyms: allowance, permission, liberalization | verb phrase
