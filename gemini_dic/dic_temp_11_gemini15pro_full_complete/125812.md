murmured: /ˈmɜːmərd/ (UK) /ˈmɜːrmərd/ (US) | v. | 低声说，咕哝 | to say something in a low, soft, or indistinct voice | He murmured an apology. 他低声说了句道歉。| whispered, muttered, mumbled | shouted, yelled | verb

murmur: /ˈmɜːmə(r)/ (UK) /ˈmɜːrmər/ (US)| n. | 低声细语，喃喃声；抱怨声 | a low, continuous sound, like the sound of a stream or the wind; a complaint uttered in a low and indistinct tone |  The murmur of the crowd grew louder.  人群的低语声越来越大。 | whisper, rumble, grumble | shout, yell | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *murmurer*, from Latin murmurare "to murmur, mutter, roar," of imitative origin.
