geckos:/ˈɡekoz/ | n. |壁虎|Small lizards, often found in warm climates, known for their ability to climb smooth surfaces.|Geckos are nocturnal reptiles. 壁虎是夜行动物。|
Geckos can climb walls and ceilings. 壁虎可以在墙壁和天花板上爬行。|
Synonyms: lizards|Antonyms: None|Noun
