filefish:/ˈfaɪlfɪʃ/| n. | 棘鱼；皮剥鲀 |A type of tropical fish with a leathery skin and a rough, file-like texture.| The filefish uses its long snout to probe for food in crevices.  棘鱼用它长长的鼻子在缝隙中探寻食物。|近义词：leatherjacket, foolfish |反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider fish with smooth scales like a tuna)|noun
