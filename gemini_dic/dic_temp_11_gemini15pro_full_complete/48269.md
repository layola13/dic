decortication:/dɪˌkɔːrtɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
去除皮质;剥皮;去壳
The surgical removal of the cortex or outer layer of an organ or structure, especially of the brain, kidney, or lung.
The decortication of the lung helped improve the patient's breathing.  去肺皮质手术改善了病人的呼吸。
exfoliation, peeling, husking
covering, encasing
noun


decorticate:/dɪˈkɔːrtɪkeɪt/
vt.
去除…的皮层;剥…的皮;去…的壳
To remove the cortex or outer layer from (an organ, seed, etc.).
The machine decorticates the rice grains efficiently.  这台机器能高效地去除米粒的壳。
