lobscouse:/ˈlɒbskɔːs/ (UK) /ˈlɑːbskɔːs/ (US)| n.|炖牛肉，水手杂烩|A stew of meat, vegetables, and hardtack (ship's biscuit). It is a traditional dish of Liverpool, England.|例句：My grandmother makes a delicious lobscouse. 我祖母做的炖牛肉非常美味。

例句：Lobscouse was a common meal for sailors in the 19th century.  炖牛肉是19世纪水手们的常见食物。|近义词：stew, hash|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps delicate cuisine) |词性:noun
