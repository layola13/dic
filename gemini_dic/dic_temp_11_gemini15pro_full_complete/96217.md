indexless:/ˈɪndɛkslɪs/| adj.|无索引的，没有索引的|Lacking an index; not indexed.  This makes searching through the large document indexless and thus very time-consuming.  这使得在大型文档中搜索没有索引，因此非常耗时。|近义词:unindexed, non-indexed|反义词:indexed|词性:形容词
