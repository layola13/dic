flossification:/flɒsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
noun
绒毛化，起毛，棉絮化
The process of forming floss or becoming like floss; the state of being flossified.
The flossification of the cotton candy made it look like a cloud. 棉花糖的绒毛化使它看起来像一朵云。
近义词:fluffing, fuzzing
反义词:compacting, solidifying
词性:n.

Etymology:  From floss + -ification.  "Floss" itself comes from Old French "flosche" meaning "silk flock".
