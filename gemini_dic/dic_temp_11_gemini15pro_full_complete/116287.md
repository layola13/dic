massicot:/ˈmæsɪkɒt/ (US) /ˈmæsɪkət/ (UK)| n.|密陀僧，黄色的氧化铅|A yellow pigment, lead monoxide (PbO), used in painting and as a drier in certain oils and varnishes. It was also used as a medicine in ancient times.|The artist used massicot to create a vibrant yellow in the painting.|Synonyms: lead monoxide, yellow lead oxide|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|noun
