akira:/əˈkɪrə/| n. |明，亮，聪明；阿基拉（人名）|A Japanese name meaning "bright" or "intelligent". It is also the title of a famous cyberpunk manga and anime film.  It's derived from the Japanese words  明 (aki) meaning "bright, clear" and 良 (ra) meaning "good, excellent."|Akira is a popular name in Japan.  阿基拉在日本是一个很受欢迎的名字。| The film Akira is a cyberpunk classic. 电影《阿基拉》是一部赛博朋克经典之作。|近义词：bright, intelligent, clever, smart|反义词：dull, unintelligent|词性:noun
