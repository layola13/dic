dragonish:/ˈdrægənɪʃ/ | adj. | 像龙的；凶猛的；暴戾的 | Resembling or suggestive of a dragon, often in behavior; fierce; cruel. | The old woman had a dragonish look in her eyes when she was angered.  老妇人生气时，眼里露出凶狠的目光。 |  The CEO's dragonish attitude intimidated the employees.  首席执行官的暴戾态度吓坏了员工。 | 近义词: draconian, fierce, ferocious, cruel, tyrannical | 反义词: gentle, kind, meek, docile | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From *dragon* + *-ish*.  The suffix *-ish* forms adjectives meaning "somewhat like" or "having the quality of".
