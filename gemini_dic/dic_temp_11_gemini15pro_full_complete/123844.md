moralistic:/ˌmɔːrəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 说教的，道学的 | characterized by or expressive of a concern with morality, especially of a narrow or conventional kind.  | He gave me a moralistic lecture on the dangers of drinking. 他就喝酒的危害对我进行了一番道学气的说教。 | 近义词：preachy, sanctimonious, self-righteous | 反义词：amoral, nonmoral, unconventional | 词性: adjective

moralistic:/ˌmɔːrəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 道德色彩的 | Having or showing strong opinions about what is right behavior and what is wrong behavior. |  She adopted a moralistic tone when she spoke about the importance of honesty.  当她谈到诚实的重要性时，她采取了一种道德主义的语气。 | 近义词：didactic, judgmental | 反义词：non-judgmental, permissive | 词性: adjective
