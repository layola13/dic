aniest:/ˈeɪniɪst/ | adj. | 最...的；最渴望...的 |  Superlative form of *any*.  It's archaic and rarely used, expressing the highest degree of a quality among a group.  This word is considered nonstandard and should be avoided in formal writing. | He was the aniest for praise of all the students. 他是所有学生中最渴望得到表扬的。 | 近义词: keenest, most eager, most desirous | 反义词: least eager, least interested | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *ani*, from Old English *ænig*
