micrometrical: /ˌmaɪkrəˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/
|adj.|
微米的；精密的；极其细致的|
Relating to small measurements, typically on the scale of a micrometer; extremely precise and detailed.|
例句：The micrometrical adjustments on the instrument allowed for very fine tuning.  仪器上的微米级调整允许非常精细的调谐。

例句：His micrometrical analysis of the poem revealed subtle shifts in rhythm and meter. 他对这首诗的细致分析揭示了节奏和韵律的微妙变化。
|近义词：precise, minute, detailed, meticulous|
|反义词：imprecise, inaccurate, coarse, rough|
词性: adjective
