mistranscribed: /ˌmɪstrænˈskraɪbd/ | adj. | 抄写错误的，誊写错误的 | Incorrectly transcribed; written down wrongly.  Mistranscribed words can lead to confusion and miscommunication. 抄写错误的单词会导致混淆和沟通不畅。| The court reporter mistranscribed the witness's testimony. 法庭记录员错误地记录了证人的证词。 | Synonyms: misspelled, miswritten, inaccurate, erroneous | Antonyms: correctly transcribed, accurate, correct | adjective
