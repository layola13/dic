endaspidean:/ˌɛndəˈspaɪdiən/ | adj. | 内生骨的，内生骨骼的 | Relating to or denoting animals having an internal skeleton. |  Endaspidean vertebrates include mammals, birds, and reptiles. 内生骨脊椎动物包括哺乳动物、鸟类和爬行动物。|  The endaspidean structure provides support and protection for internal organs. 内生骨结构为内脏提供支撑和保护。 | 近义词: vertebrate, endoskeletal | 反义词: exoskeletal, invertebrate | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Greek *ἔνδον (éndon, “within”) + ἀσπίς (aspís, “shield”) + -ean.  由希腊语 *ἔνδον (éndon, “在...之内”) + ἀσπίς (aspís, “盾”) + -ean 组成。
