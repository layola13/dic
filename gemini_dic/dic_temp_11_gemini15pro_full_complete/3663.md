agglomerative:/əˈɡlɒmərətɪv/
adjective
聚集的，凝聚的
Characterized by the process of collecting or gathering into a mass or ball.
The agglomerative properties of the dust particles made them clump together. 这些尘埃颗粒的聚集特性使它们凝结在一起。
synonyms: clustering, aggregative, cumulative, collective
antonyms: dispersive, scattering, distributive
词性: adjective


agglomerate:/əˈɡlɒməreɪt/
verb
聚集，凝聚
To gather into a mass or ball.
The snowflakes agglomerated into a giant snowball. 雪花聚集成了一个巨大的雪球。
synonyms: amass, accumulate, congregate, coalesce
antonyms: disperse, scatter, separate
词性: verb


agglomerate:/əˈɡlɒmərət/
noun
聚集体，凝聚物
A mass or collection of things gathered together.
The rock was an agglomerate of various minerals. 这块岩石是由各种矿物聚集而成的。
synonyms: cluster, aggregation, collection, mass
antonyms: dispersion, scattering
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *agglomeratus*, past participle of *agglomerare* "to wind into a ball," from *ad-* "to" + *glomus* "ball."
