PHRASE->
immix with (与…混合):  immix the flour with the sugar. 将面粉与糖混合。
immix thoroughly (彻底混合):  immix the ingredients thoroughly before baking. 烘烤前彻底混合配料。
immix into (混合到…中): immix the spices into the sauce. 将香料混合到酱汁中。
immix well (充分混合):  immix the batter well to avoid lumps.  充分混合面糊以避免结块。
immix evenly (均匀地混合):  immix the colors evenly to create a consistent shade. 均匀地混合颜色以创建一致的阴影。

词源: From Latin immiscere, from in- 'into' + miscere 'to mix'.
