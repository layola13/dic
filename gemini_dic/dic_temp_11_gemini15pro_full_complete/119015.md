methodistical:/ˌmɛθəˈdɪstɪkəl/| adj.|循序渐进的，有条理的，墨守成规的|Characterized by order and method; systematic.  Relating to or characteristic of Methodism.|He has a very methodical approach to his work, always planning each step carefully. 他工作很有条理，总是仔细地计划每一步。|近义词：systematic, orderly, organized|反义词：chaotic, disorganized, random|词性:形容词
词源: Late 18th century: from method + -ical.  Originally applied derogatorily to Methodists, because of the perceived regularity of their religious practices.
