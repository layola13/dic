It emphasizes the use of such language in a particular situation.| He swore expletively at the driver who cut him off.  他咒骂着那个超他车的司机。|  She shouted expletively when she stubbed her toe.  她踢到脚趾时破口大骂。 | synonym: profanely, obscenely, abusively | antonym: politely, respectfully | adverb
