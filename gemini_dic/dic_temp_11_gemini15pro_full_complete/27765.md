cannelure:/ˈkænəlʊr/ | n. | 沟槽，凹槽；（子弹或炮弹上的）环形槽 | A groove or indentation, especially a circumferential groove on a bullet or cartridge case. | The cannelure on the bullet helps it to grip the rifling of the barrel. 子弹上的环形槽有助于它抓住枪管的膛线。 | groove, indentation, channel | protrusion, bump | noun
