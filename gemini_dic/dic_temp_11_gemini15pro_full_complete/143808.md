outpaint:/ˈaʊtpeɪnt/| v. |外绘; 扩展绘制|To extend an existing image by generating new content beyond its original boundaries, often seamlessly blending the new with the old.|The AI can outpaint images, creating realistic extensions of the scene.  人工智能可以外绘图像，创造逼真的场景扩展。|近义词：extend, expand, extrapolate|反义词: crop, trim, reduce|词性:动词

词源:  "Out-" (meaning beyond or outside) + "paint" (meaning to create a picture).  A relatively recent term popularized with the advent of AI image generation. 由"out-"（表示超出或外部）和"paint"（表示创建图片）组成。一个相对较新的术语，随着AI图像生成的出现而流行起来。
