defogged:/diˈfɒɡd/ /diˈfɑːɡd/
verb
已清除雾气的，已去雾的
Cleared of fog; made free of fog.
The pilot defogged the windshield before takeoff. 飞行员在起飞前清除了挡风玻璃上的雾气。
Synonyms: cleared, clarified
Antonyms: fogged, obscured
词性: 动词
