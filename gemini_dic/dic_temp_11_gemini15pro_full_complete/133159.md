词源：
"Nonethnologically" is formed by adding the prefixes "non-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of") and "ethno-" (relating to ethnicity or culture) to the root "logical" (relating to logic or reason), and the suffix "-ly" (forming an adverb).  "Ethnology" itself comes from the Greek words "ethnos" (nation, people) and "logos" (study). “非民族学地”是由前缀“non-”（意思是“不”或“与…相反”）和“ethno-”（与民族或文化有关）添加到词根“logical”（与逻辑或推理有关），以及后缀“-ly”（构成副词）而形成的。“Ethnology”本身来自希腊语“ethnos”（民族，人民）和“logos”（研究）。
