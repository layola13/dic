geoduck:/ˈɡuːiˌdʌk/ | n. | 象拔蚌 |  A very large, burrowing clam of the Pacific coast of North America. Its shell is much smaller than its long, fleshy siphon. |  The geoduck is prized for its savory flavor and unique texture. 象拔蚌因其美味和独特的口感而备受推崇。| synonym: king clam, Pacific geoduck | antonym: None | noun
