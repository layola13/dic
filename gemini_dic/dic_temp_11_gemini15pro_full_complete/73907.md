forsooth:/fɔːrˈsuːθ/
adverb
真的；确实（古语，现用于讽刺）
indeed (archaic, now used ironically)
"Forsooth, I am no knight errant." 真的，我不是个游侠骑士。
Synonyms: truly, indeed, verily
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it's an archaic intensifier.  Consider words like "falsely," "untruly" as antonyms to the implied meaning.)
Part of Speech: adverb

Etymology: From Old English *for sōþe ("for truth"), from for ("for") + sōþ ("truth").
