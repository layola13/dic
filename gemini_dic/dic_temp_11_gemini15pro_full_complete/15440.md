bad-copy:/ˌbæd ˈkɑːpi/ | n. | 劣质副本；次品；不好的复制品 | A bad copy is a reproduction of something that is of poor quality, often due to errors in the copying process or inferior materials. |  The print I received was a bad copy, blurry and faded. 我收到的印刷品是劣质副本，模糊且褪色。

The pirated DVD was a bad copy, with distorted sound and pixelation.  这张盗版DVD是劣质副本，声音失真，像素化严重。 | synonyms: inferior copy, flawed reproduction, poor replica, duplicate of poor quality | antonyms: good copy, perfect replica, high-quality reproduction | noun
