out-tell:/ˌaʊtˈtel/
verb
胜过；讲述胜过；比……讲得好
to tell a story better than someone else
He out-told all the other storytellers at the festival. 他在节日上比其他所有讲故事的人都讲得好。
surpass in storytelling
No one could out-tell her grandmother when it came to fairy tales.  说到童话故事，没有人能比她祖母讲得更好。
excel, outdo, surpass, outmatch
-
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + tell.
