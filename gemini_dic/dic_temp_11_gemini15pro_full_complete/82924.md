gumming:/ˈɡʌmɪŋ/| v.|涂胶，用胶粘，分泌树胶，使…粘稠|The label was gumming up the printer. 标签把打印机弄粘住了。|近义词：sticking, gluing, adhering|反义词: ungluing, detaching|词性:动词(verb)
gumming:/ˈɡʌmɪŋ/| n.|牙龈，树胶|The baby is teething and its gums are sore. 宝宝正在长牙，牙龈很疼。|近义词：gingiva|反义词: None|词性:名词(noun)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gōma (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *gumō, from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰeyh₁- (“to yawn, gape”). Cognate with West Frisian gom (“gum”), Dutch gom (“gum”), German Gaumen (“palate”). More at yawn.  The verb sense derives from the noun.
