nonremedies:/ˌnɒnˈrɛmədiz/ | n. | 无救济措施；无法补救的事情 |  Things for which there are no remedies; actions or situations that cannot be rectified or cured. |  The court ruled that the plaintiff had no claim for damages, as the harm suffered fell under the category of nonremedies. 法院裁定原告没有损害赔偿的请求权，因为其所遭受的损害属于无法补救的情况。| Near synonyms: irremediable situations, incurable problems, uncorrectable issues | Antonyms: remedies, solutions, cures | noun
