infantilization:/ɪnˌfæn.tɪ.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/
n.
幼稚化；婴儿化；将…视为幼儿
The act of treating someone as if they were a child, making them seem less mature or capable.
The infantilization of women in advertising is a common complaint.  广告中对女性的幼稚化是一个普遍的抱怨。
infantilisation (British English)
childishness, immaturity, puerility
maturity, adulthood, sophistication
noun
