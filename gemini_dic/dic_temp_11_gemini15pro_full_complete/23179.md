bowdlerised:/ˈboʊdləraɪzd/ /ˈbaʊdləraɪzd/| adj.|删节的，净化过的|Edited or censored to remove anything considered offensive or inappropriate.  The play was bowdlerised for television. 这部戏剧为了电视播出而进行了删节。|近义词：censored, expurgated, sanitized|反义词：unabridged, unexpurgated|词性:形容词

bowdlerise:/ˈboʊdləraɪz/ /ˈbaʊdləraɪz/| vt.|删节，净化|To edit or censor a text or play by removing parts considered offensive or inappropriate. They bowdlerised the book to make it suitable for children. 他们删节了这本书以使其适合儿童阅读。|近义词：censor, expurgate, sanitize|反义词：retain, preserve|词性:动词

bowdlerisation:/ˌboʊdləraɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌbaʊdləraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|删节，净化|The act of editing or censoring a text or play by removing parts considered offensive or inappropriate. The bowdlerisation of the play removed all references to sex and violence.  这部戏剧的删节去掉了所有关于性和暴力的内容。|近义词：censorship, expurgation, sanitization|反义词：preservation, retention|词性:名词
