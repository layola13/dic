falk:/fɔːk/ | n. |猎鹰；隼|A diurnal bird of prey with long pointed wings and a notched beak, typically catching prey by swooping from a height.|The falk soared high above the mountains. 猎鹰在群山上空翱翔。|Synonym: falcon, hawk |Antonym: prey |noun

falk:/fɔːk/ | v. (rare, archaic) |捕捉；猎杀（尤指用猎鹰）|To hunt with a falcon; to hawk.|He would falk and hunt all day long. 他会整天用猎鹰捕猎。|Synonym: hawk, hunt |Antonym: protect, release |verb
