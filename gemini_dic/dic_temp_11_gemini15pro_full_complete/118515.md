mesocephalic:/ˌmɛsoʊˈsɛfəlɪk/ | adj. | 中头型的 | Having a head of medium proportions; specifically : having a cephalic index between 75 and 80. |  The skull found at the archaeological site was determined to be mesocephalic.  考古遗址发现的头骨被确定为中头型的。 | Synonyms: mesocephalous | Antonyms: brachycephalic, dolichocephalic | adjective

mesocephalic:/ˌmɛsoʊˈsɛfəlɪk/ | adj. | 中脑的 | Relating to the midbrain. | Damage to the mesocephalic area can result in severe motor impairments. 对中脑区域的损伤会导致严重的运动障碍。 | Synonyms: midbrain | Antonyms: N/A | adjective
