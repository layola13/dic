bibliotheke:/ˌbɪbliəˈθiːkiː/ /ˌbɪbliəˈθɛkə/ | n. | 图书馆，藏书室 | A library or collection of books.  This word is more commonly seen in its derived forms, like "library" in English. It represents the original Greek term. |  The ancient bibliotheke contained scrolls of papyrus and parchment.  古代的藏书室里收藏着莎草纸和羊皮纸卷轴。

The king's private bibliotheke held countless volumes on history and philosophy.  国王的私人藏书室里收藏了无数关于历史和哲学的书籍。 | 近义词: library, archive | 反义词: None (lack of a direct antonym - perhaps "empty room" in a very specific context) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek βιβλιοθήκη (bibliotheke), from βιβλίον (biblion, “book”) + θήκη (theke, “case, box”).
