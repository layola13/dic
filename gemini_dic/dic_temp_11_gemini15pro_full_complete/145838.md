overdeliciousness:/ˈoʊvərˌdɪˈlɪʃəsnəs/ |n.|极度美味，非常美味|A state of being extremely delicious, beyond simply delicious. |The overdeliciousness of the chocolate cake made me want to eat the whole thing. 这块巧克力蛋糕的极致美味让我想要把它全部吃掉。|Near synonyms: exquisite deliciousness, supreme deliciousness, heavenly deliciousness|Antonyms: blandness, unappetizingness|Noun

Etymology: "Over-" (Old English *ofer, meaning "above, beyond") + "delicious" (from Old French delicieus, from Latin deliciosus "delightful," from deliciae "pleasures, delights") + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), forming nouns of state or quality)
