half-disposed:/ˌhæf dɪˈspoʊzd/
adj.
有点倾向于；略微有意于
somewhat inclined or willing; partially disposed
He was half-disposed to agree with her. 他有点倾向于同意她的观点。
Near synonyms: partially inclined, somewhat willing, slightly disposed
Antonyms: completely unwilling, totally opposed
词性: 形容词
