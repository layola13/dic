brrr:/brɜːr/ (US) /brɜː/ (UK)| interjection | 寒冷的声音；打冷颤的声音 | An exclamation used to express that one is cold. |  It's freezing out here! Brrrr!  外面太冷了！ бррр！ | 近义词: shiver, shudder | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider words expressing warmth like "ahh" or "mmm") | 词性: interjection
