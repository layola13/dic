outbaking:/ˈaʊtbeɪkɪŋ/
verb
比…烤得更好;在烘焙方面胜过
Surpassing someone in baking skills.
She outbaked her mother with her prize-winning apple pie. 她用她那屡获殊荣的苹果派在烘焙方面胜过她母亲。
近义词: outdo, surpass
反义词: be outdone by

词性:v.
