hypogyn:/ˈhaɪpəˌdʒaɪn/ /ˈhaɪpoʊˌdʒaɪn/
adjective
下位的，子房上位花的
(of a flower) having the other floral parts inserted below the gynoecium, the ovary being superior.
The flower is hypogynous, meaning the sepals, petals, and stamens are attached below the ovary.  这朵花是下位花的，意味着萼片、花瓣和雄蕊都着生于子房下方。
The botanist identified the plant by its hypogynous flowers.  植物学家通过其下位花识别了这种植物。

近义词:superior (referring to ovary position)
反义词:epigynous, perigynous
词性:adjective
