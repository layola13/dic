nuncupatory:/nʌŋˈkjuːpətɔːri/
adjective
口头的；口述的
Of or relating to a declaration or a will made orally, especially just before death.
例句：He made a nuncupatory will on his deathbed. 他在临终前立了口头遗嘱。

例句：Nuncupatory contracts are generally not enforceable for large transactions.  口头合同通常对大额交易不具有强制执行力。


synonym:oral, verbal, spoken
antonym:written
词性:adjective
