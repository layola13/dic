nothingarianism:/ˈnʌθɪŋɛəriənɪzəm/
n.
虚无主义；一无所是论
The belief that nothing is true, or that nothing deserves to be believed.  
He embraced nothingarianism after experiencing a series of personal tragedies.  在经历了一系列个人悲剧之后，他接受了虚无主义。
近义词：nihilism, skepticism
反义词：belief, faith
词性：noun
