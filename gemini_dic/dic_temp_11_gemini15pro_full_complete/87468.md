hessian:/ˈhɛʃən/| adj.|粗麻布制的；黑森傭兵的|Made of hessian; relating to Hesse in Germany.|She carried her belongings in a hessian bag.|Synonyms: burlap, jute|Antonyms: silken, satiny|adjective
