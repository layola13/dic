blitzed:/blɪtst/| adj. v.|喝醉的，烂醉如泥的；快速突袭，闪电战|From German Blitzkrieg "lightning war," from Blitz "lightning" + Krieg "war."  Originally referring to a sudden military attack, it later evolved to describe a state of extreme intoxication.|例句：After a night of bar hopping, he was completely blitzed.  在酒吧里逛了一晚上后，他完全喝醉了。|近义词：drunk, intoxicated, hammered, plastered, wasted|反义词:sober|词性:形容词/动词

It can refer to a rapid military attack or, figuratively, to completing something quickly and intensely.|例句：The team blitzed through the project and finished it ahead of schedule.  团队快速完成了项目，提前完成了进度。|近义词：attack, raid, storm, rush, speed through|反义词:dawdle|词性:动词
