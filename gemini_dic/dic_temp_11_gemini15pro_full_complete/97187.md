infixion:/ɪnˈflɪkʃən/ | n. | 变形，词尾变化 | The process of adding affixes to a word stem to create different grammatical forms, such as tenses, plurals, or case endings. | Example: The word "walks" is formed by the infixion of "-s" to the base form "walk."  走路这个词是通过在词根walk上添加-s变形而来的。| Example:  The infixion "-ed" indicates past tense in many English verbs.  "-ed"词尾变化在很多英语动词中表示过去式。 | Synonyms: inflection, affixation | Antonyms: derivation | noun
