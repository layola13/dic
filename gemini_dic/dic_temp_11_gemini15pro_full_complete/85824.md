haziest:/ˈheɪziɪst/| adj.|最朦胧的，最模糊的|Superlative form of hazy: most hazy.  It means covered by or filled with haze, making things difficult to see clearly. |The haziest days of summer are often the hottest. 夏天最朦胧的日子通常也是最热的日子。|The view from the mountaintop was haziest in the afternoon. 从山顶望下去，下午的景色最为朦胧。|近义词：foggiest, mistiest, murkiest|反义词：clearest, sharpest|词性:形容词
