non-porous:/ˌnɒnˈpɔːrəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɔːrəs/| adj.|无孔的，不透气的|Not allowing liquid or air to pass through.|This material is non-porous and waterproof. 这种材料无孔且防水。|Synonyms: impermeable, airtight, sealed, impervious|Antonyms: porous, permeable, pervious|adjective

non-porous:/ˌnɒnˈpɔːrəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɔːrəs/| n.|无孔物质|A substance that does not allow liquid or air to pass through. (Less common usage as a noun)|The non-porous nature of the container kept the contents dry.  这个容器的无孔特性使内容物保持干燥。|Synonyms: impermeable material, airtight material|Antonyms: porous material, permeable material|noun
