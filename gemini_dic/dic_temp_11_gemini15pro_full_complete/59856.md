ectozoon:/ˈɛktəʊˌzoʊən/
n.
外寄生虫
An ectozoon is a parasite that lives on the outside of its host.
Fleas and ticks are common ectozoa that can infest pets. 跳蚤和蜱虫是常见的体外寄生虫，可以感染宠物。
Lice are another example of an ectozoon that can affect humans.  虱子是另一种可以影响人类的体外寄生虫。
