beriberi:/ˌberɪˈberɪ/ | n. | 脚气病 | A disease caused by a deficiency of thiamine (vitamin B1), characterized by weakness, pain in the limbs, and heart failure. |  He suffered from beriberi due to a poor diet. 他因饮食不良患上了脚气病。|  Synonym: thiamine deficiency | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a disease, could consider "health" in a broad sense) | noun
