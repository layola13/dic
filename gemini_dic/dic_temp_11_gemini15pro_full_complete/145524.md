overcoloring: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌlərɪŋ/
noun
过度着色，上色过重
The act of applying too much color, often resulting in a loss of detail or a muddy appearance.
The artist ruined the painting by overcoloring. 艺术家因为过度着色毁了这幅画。
近义词: overpainting
反义词: undercoloring
词性: n.

overcoloring: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌlərɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度着色，上色过重
Applying too much color to something.
He's overcoloring that area of the drawing. 他给图画的那块区域上色过重了。
近义词: overpainting
反义词: undercoloring
词性: v.
