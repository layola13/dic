hypidiomorphic:/ˌhaɪpɪdioʊˈmɔːrfɪk/ | adj. | 半自形 | Partially euhedral; used to describe mineral grains in an igneous rock that show some crystal faces but are not completely bounded by crystal faces. | The plagioclase crystals in this thin section are hypidiomorphic.  这薄片中的斜长石晶体是半自形的。|  subhedral | anhedral, allotriomorphic | adjective
