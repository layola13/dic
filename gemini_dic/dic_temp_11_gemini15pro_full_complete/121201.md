mise:/miːz/ | n. | 下注，赌注;  支出，费用; (尤指舞台剧的) 布景，舞台布置; (法语)放置，安置 | An amount of money put up as a stake in gambling. The staging of a play, including scenery, costumes, and lighting. |  He increased his mise to five hundred dollars. 他把赌注增加到五百美元。 The mise-en-scène of the play was breathtaking. 这部戏的舞台布置令人叹为观止。 | 近义词: stake, wager, bet, outlay, expenditure, setting, staging | 反义词: winnings, gain, income | 词性: 名词
