hideth:/ˈhaɪdɪθ/| v.|隐藏，掩藏|Archaic third-person singular present form of hide. It means "conceals" or "puts out of sight."|He hideth his treasure where no one can find it. 他把他的宝藏藏在无人能找到的地方。|近义词：conceals, covers, masks, obscures|反义词：reveals, exposes, shows|词性:动词

虚拟语气:
If he hideth the truth, he will regret it. 如果他隐瞒真相，他会后悔的。 (Note: Using "hideth" in the conditional is highly archaic and unnatural in modern English.  "If he hides the truth..." is preferable.)
I wish she hideth her feelings better. 我希望她能更好地隐藏自己的感情。(Note: As above, this is highly archaic. "I wish she hid her feelings better" or "I wish she would hide her feelings better" are preferable.)
