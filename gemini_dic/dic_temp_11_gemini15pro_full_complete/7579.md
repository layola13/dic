annotators:/ˈænəteɪtərz/
n.
注释者，注解者
People who add notes or comments to a text or image, providing explanations or additional information.
The annotators carefully examined the ancient manuscript, adding notes about its historical context.  注释者仔细检查了古代手稿，添加了关于其历史背景的注释。
The image annotators labeled each object in the photograph to train the AI model.  图像注释者标记了照片中的每个对象以训练 AI 模型。
synonyms: commentators, interpreters, explainers, analysts
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps consider) obfuscators, misinterpreters
noun
