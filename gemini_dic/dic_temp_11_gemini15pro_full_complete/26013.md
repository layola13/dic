busbar:/ˈbʌsbɑːr/ (US), /ˈbʌsbɑː/ (UK)| n. |汇流排，母线|A heavy, rigid metallic conductor, typically copper or aluminum, used to distribute electrical power within a switchboard, distribution board, substation, or other electrical apparatus.|The main busbar in the substation carries a high voltage.|近义词：conductor, power rail|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps insulator)|noun
