deconsecrating:/ˌdiːkɒnˈsɪkreɪtɪŋ/
verb
取消…的神圣性；使…不再神圣
The act of depriving something of its sacred character.
The church was deconsecrated and turned into a community center.  这座教堂被取消了神圣性，变成了一个社区中心。
The city council voted to deconsecrate the old cemetery. 市议会投票决定取消旧墓地的神圣性。
synonyms:desecrating, profaning
antonyms:consecrating, sanctifying
