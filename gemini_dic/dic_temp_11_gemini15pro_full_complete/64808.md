esoteric:/ˌesəˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. | 秘传的；深奥的；难理解的 | Intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with a specialized knowledge or interest. |  The group held esoteric discussions about ancient philosophies.  这个小组就古代哲学进行了深奥的讨论。 | synonyms: abstruse, arcane, recondite, obscure | antonyms: exoteric, common, simple, accessible | adjective

esoterical:/ˌesəˈtɛrɪkəl/ | adj. | 秘传的；深奥的；难理解的 |  Relating to or characterized by esotericism.  | He delved into esoterical studies of medieval alchemy. 他深入研究了中世纪炼金术的深奥学问。 | synonyms: abstruse, arcane, recondite, obscure | antonyms: exoteric, common, simple, accessible | adjective
