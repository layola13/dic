gravitating:/ˈɡrævɪteɪtɪŋ/
v.
移动；被吸引
moving towards something; being attracted to something
Young people are gravitating towards city centres.  年轻人正逐渐被大城市中心所吸引。
tend, incline, drift
resist, avoid
verb

gravitate:/ˈɡrævɪteɪt/
v.
被吸引到；受引力作用而运动
to be attracted to or move toward something
People tend to gravitate toward others who share their interests. 人们往往会被与自己有共同兴趣的人所吸引。
be drawn to, be attracted to, incline towards
repel, push away
verb
