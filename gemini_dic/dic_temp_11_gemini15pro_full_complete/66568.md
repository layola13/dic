exhibitory:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtəri/ /ɛɡˈzɪbətɔːri/
adjective
展览的；展出的；具有刺激性的
serving to exhibit or show; stimulating or exciting
The museum's exhibitory space is quite large. 博物馆的展出空间非常大。
The drug has an exhibitory effect on the nervous system. 这种药物对神经系统有刺激作用。
近义词:  display, exhibition, stimulating, exciting
反义词: inhibitory, suppressive
词性: adjective
