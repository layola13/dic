eagerer:/ˈiːɡərər/ | adj. | 更渴望的，更热切的 | Comparative form of "eager," meaning more enthusiastic and interested. | She was eagerer than ever to start the new project. 她比以往任何时候都更渴望开始这个新项目。 |  He was eagerer to please his boss than his colleagues. 他比他的同事更渴望取悦他的老板。| 近义词: keener, more enthusiastic, more avid | 反义词: less eager, apathetic, indifferent | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *eger, from Old French eigre, aigre "sharp, keen, sour," from Latin acer "sharp, keen, biting, pungent, eager."
