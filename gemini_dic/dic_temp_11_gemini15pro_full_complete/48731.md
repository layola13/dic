defile:/dɪˈfaɪl/| v. |玷污，亵渎，败坏；(尤指大量人)通过狭窄地方|To make something dirty or impure, especially something sacred; to march in a single file, typically through a narrow space.|The vandals defiled the church by spraying graffiti on the altar. 这些故意破坏者在圣坛上喷涂涂鸦，亵渎了教堂。|synonyms: corrupt, pollute, contaminate, desecrate, tarnish, sully, taint|antonyms: purify, cleanse, sanctify|verb

defile:/ˈdiːfaɪl/| n. |狭窄的山谷，峡谷|A narrow passage, especially between mountains; a gorge.|The army marched through the defile. 军队穿过峡谷行进。|synonyms: gorge, canyon, pass, ravine|antonyms: plain, open field|noun
