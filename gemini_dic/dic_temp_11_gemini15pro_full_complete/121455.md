misjoined:/ˌmɪsˈdʒɔɪnd/
adjective
错误连接的，连接不当的
incorrectly joined or connected
The plumber misjoined the pipes, causing a leak. 水管工错误地连接了管道，导致漏水。
The two halves of the sculpture were misjoined, making it look lopsided.  雕塑的两半连接不当，使它看起来歪斜。
近义词: misconnected, improperly joined
反义词: correctly joined, properly connected
词性: adjective

词源:
From mis- (expressing wrongness or error) + joined (past participle of join).
