associating:/əˈsoʊsieɪtɪŋ/
v. |交往，联系；联想|
Present participle of "associate".  The act of connecting things in one's mind or spending time with someone. |
He is associating his childhood with feelings of happiness. 他把他的童年与快乐的感觉联系在一起。

She is associating with a group of artists. 她正在与一群艺术家交往。

近义词:connecting, linking, joining, mingling, socializing
反义词:separating, disconnecting, isolating
词性:Verb (present participle)
