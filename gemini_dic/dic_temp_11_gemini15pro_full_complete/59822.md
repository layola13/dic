ecthlipsis:/ɪkˈθlɪpsɪs/ | n. |省略；脱落；（尤指诗歌中）省略音节或字母|The omission of a sound or syllable when speaking, as in "I'm" for "I am".  This is also a rhetorical figure involving the omission of a word or words readily implied by the context.|I’d rather go home now. (ecthlipsis of "I would") 我宁愿现在回家。|近义词：elision, omission|反义词：inclusion, addition|词性: noun
