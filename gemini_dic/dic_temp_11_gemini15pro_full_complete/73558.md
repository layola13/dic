forestalling:/fɔːrˈstɔːlɪŋ/
verb
抢先阻止, 预先阻止
preventing something from happening by acting in advance.
The company forestalled a hostile takeover bid by acquiring a competitor.  该公司通过收购竞争对手，抢先阻止了恶意收购的企图。
synonyms: preventing, preempting, anticipating, averting
antonyms: allowing, permitting, facilitating
v.

forestall:/fɔːrˈstɔːl/
verb
抢先阻止, 预先阻止
act in advance of (someone) in order to prevent them from doing something.
They forestalled the criticism by offering a public apology. 他们通过公开道歉，抢先阻止了批评。
synonyms: preempt, anticipate
antonyms: allow, permit
