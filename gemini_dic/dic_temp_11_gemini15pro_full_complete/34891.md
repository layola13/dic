clippingly:/ˈklɪpɪŋli/| adv.|简短地，简洁地|In a clipped or concise manner; curtly.  Derived from the verb "clip" meaning to cut short.|She answered clippingly, "No." 她简短地回答说：“不”。|Synonyms: curtly, tersely, abruptly, briefly, concisely|Antonyms: verbosely, lengthily, elaborately|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "clipping" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Clipping" comes from the verb "clip," which has its origins in Old Norse "klippa."
