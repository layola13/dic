lunet:/luːˈnɛt/| n.|月牙形, 小月牙, 弧形|A crescent-shaped figure or object, especially an architectural opening or recess in a vault or dome.  A small crescent-shaped area or space.|The lunet above the doorway was filled with stained glass.  门口上方的月牙形空间装满了彩色玻璃。|Synonyms: crescent, demilune, half-moon|Antonyms: circle, sphere|noun

Etymology->
From French *lunette*, diminutive of *lune* (“moon”), from Latin *lūna* (“moon”).
