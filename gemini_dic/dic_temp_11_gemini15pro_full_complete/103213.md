jetsam:/ˈdʒɛtsəm/ | n. |  (海上)投弃的货物；被海浪冲上岸的货物；废弃物；无用之物 | Goods thrown overboard from a ship to lighten it in an emergency, especially such goods when washed ashore. Also, discarded odds and ends; useless stuff. | The storm washed ashore a strange collection of jetsam, including a child's toy and a tattered life jacket.  暴风雨把一堆奇怪的物品冲上了岸，包括一个儿童玩具和一件破烂的救生衣。| synonyms: flotsam, debris, wreckage, refuse | antonyms: valuables, treasure | noun
