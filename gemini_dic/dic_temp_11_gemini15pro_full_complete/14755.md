awfulnesses:/ˈɔːflnəsɪz/
n.
糟糕的事情；可怕的事情；令人厌恶的事情
The sheer awfulnesses of war are difficult to comprehend.  战争的残酷程度难以理解。
The book detailed the awfulnesses of life in the prison camps. 这本书详细描述了集中营生活的可怕之处。
Synonyms: horrors, atrocities, terrors, nightmares
Antonyms: wonders, beauties, delights, marvels
