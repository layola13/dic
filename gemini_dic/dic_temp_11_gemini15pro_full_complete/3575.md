agaric:/ˈæɡərɪk, əˈɡærɪk/| n. |蘑菇（尤指伞菌科的）|A fleshy spore-bearing fruiting body of a fungus, typically in the form of a cap atop a stem, especially one consisting of the gills on the underside of the cap.|例句：The field was dotted with white agarics.  田野上点缀着白色的蘑菇。

例句：He identified the mushroom as a common field agaric. 他认出这种蘑菇是一种常见的田野伞菌。|近义词：mushroom, toadstool|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something inedible like "poisonous fungus")|词性:noun
