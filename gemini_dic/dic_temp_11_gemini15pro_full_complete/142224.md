ornithoscelida:/ˌɔːrnɪθəˈsɛlɪdə/ | n. |鸟臀目| A group of dinosaurs characterized by a pelvic structure superficially similar to that of birds.  This group traditionally included ornithopods and thyreophorans, but its validity is now widely questioned. | Ornithoscelida was once considered a major dinosaur group. 鸟臀目曾被认为是一个主要的恐龙类群。| The classification of Ornithoscelida is still debated among paleontologists.  关于鸟臀目的分类，古生物学家仍在争论。| 近义词:  (formerly) Dinosauria | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
