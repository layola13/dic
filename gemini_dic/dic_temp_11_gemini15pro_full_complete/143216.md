outcull:/ˈaʊtkʌl/ |v. |剔除；淘汰| To select and remove (animals, especially livestock) from a group, typically because they are inferior or undesirable.| The farmer outculled the weakest lambs from the flock.  农民从羊群中剔除了最弱的小羊。|例句：The rancher outculled the sickly cattle to prevent the spread of disease.  牧场主剔除了病牛以防止疾病传播。

例句：They outculled several trees from the orchard that weren't producing fruit.  他们从果园里剔除了几棵不结果的树。|近义词：cull, weed out, eliminate, remove|反义词：include, keep, retain|词性：动词
