demotist: /ˈdɛməˌtɪst/ | n. |平民主义者；民众主义者| A person who believes in or advocates for democracy, especially direct democracy, or who champions the rights and interests of ordinary people.  |  The demotist argued for greater public involvement in government decision-making.  这位平民主义者主张公众更多地参与政府决策。| synonyms: populist, egalitarian, democrat | antonyms: elitist, aristocrat, oligarch | noun
