arena's:/əˈriːnəz/
noun
竞技场的（所有格）
possessive form of arena, meaning belonging to an arena.  It refers to something owned by or associated with a place of public contest or assembly.
The arena's roar was deafening. 竞技场的喧嚣震耳欲聋。
The arena's lighting was impressive.  竞技场的灯光令人印象深刻。
Synonyms: stadium's, coliseum's, amphitheater's
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a possessive, could consider concepts like "private room's," "home's")
词性: 名词所有格
