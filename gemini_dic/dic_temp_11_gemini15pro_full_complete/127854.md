nenia:/ˈniːniə/ /ˈninɪə/
noun
挽歌；葬歌；哀悼歌
A song or poem expressing grief or sorrow, especially for the dead.
She composed a nenia for her deceased grandmother. 她为她已故的祖母创作了一首挽歌。
The nenia was sung by a chorus of mourners.  这首挽歌是由一群哀悼者合唱的。
synonyms: lament, elegy, dirge, requiem
antonyms: celebration, paean, hymn
