cloche:/klɒʃ/ (UK) /kloʊʃ/ (US)| n.|钟形罩，玻璃罩，（女式）钟形帽|A transparent glass or plastic cover used to protect plants from frost or wind, or a woman's close-fitting, bell-shaped hat.|例句：The gardener placed cloches over the seedlings to protect them from the frost. 园丁在幼苗上放置钟形罩以保护它们免受霜冻。

例句：She wore a stylish cloche hat to the races. 她戴着一顶时尚的钟形帽去参加比赛。|近义词：bell jar, cover, dome, hat|反义词:|noun
