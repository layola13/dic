He bowingly accepted the criticism. 他俯首帖耳地接受了批评。
She bowingly followed the orders. 她唯命是从地服从命令。

She bowingly apologized for the inconvenience.  她低声下气地为带来的不便道歉。
He bowingly presented the gift to the king. 他毕恭毕敬地将礼物呈献给国王。
