Göbekli Tepe: /ɡøbekli ˈtepe/ | n. | 哥贝克力石阵 |  A Neolithic archaeological site in southeastern Turkey, considered the world's oldest known megalithic structure. |  Example: Göbekli Tepe predates pottery and agriculture. 哥贝克力石阵早于陶器和农业出现。 |  Synonym:  megalithic site, prehistoric site | Antonym: modern structure | Noun
