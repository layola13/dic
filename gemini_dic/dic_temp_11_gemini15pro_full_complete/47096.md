daubry:/ˈdɔːbri/| n.|粗劣的画作; 拙劣的涂抹;  粗制滥造的东西|A poor or crude painting or piece of writing; something poorly or cheaply made. |The painting he presented was nothing more than a daubry. 他拿出的画只不过是一幅拙劣之作。|近义词：smear, smudge, botch|反义词:masterpiece, work of art|词性:名词

Etymology: From French *daubrée*, from *dauber* "to plaster, coat," from Latin *dealbare* "to whitewash," from *de-* "down, completely" + *albare* "to whiten."
