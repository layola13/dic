inaugural: /ɪˈnɔːɡjərəl/ (US), /ɪˈnɔːɡjʊrəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|就职的，开幕的；就职典礼|Marking the beginning of an institution, activity, or period; a ceremony or speech marking such a beginning.|The inaugural flight of the new aircraft was a success. 新飞机的首航非常成功。|近义词：initial, first, opening, introductory|反义词：final, closing, concluding|词性: adjective, noun

inaugural: /ɪˈnɔːɡjərəl/ (US), /ɪˈnɔːɡjʊrəl/ (UK)| n.|就职典礼|A ceremony or speech marking the beginning of an institution, activity, or period.|The president's inaugural address was inspiring.  总统的就职演说令人鼓舞。|近义词：initiation, commencement, opening ceremony|反义词：closing ceremony, farewell|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inauguralis*, from Latin inaugurare "to take omens from the flight of birds; to consecrate, install in office," from in- "in, into" + augurare "to predict, foretell," from augur "soothsayer, diviner."
