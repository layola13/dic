noncommendableness:/ˌnɑːnkəmɛndəblnəs/
n.
不可称道, 不值得推荐
lack of commendableness; the state or quality of not being worthy of praise or recommendation.
His noncommendableness made him unsuitable for the position. 他的不可称道使他不适合这个职位。
近义词:undesirability, unworthiness, disreputability
反义词:commendableness, worthiness, merit
词性:noun
