counterstock:/ˈkaʊntərstɑːk/| n.|对冲股票，平衡股票，对立股票|A stock or group of stocks bought or sold to offset a position in another stock or group of stocks, thus reducing the risk of loss. |Example 1: He bought counterstock in a competing company to hedge his bets.  他购买了竞争公司的对冲股票以对冲风险。

Example 2: The investor used counterstock to minimize the potential losses from his main investment. 该投资者使用对冲股票来最大限度地减少其主要投资的潜在损失。|Synonym: hedge, offset, counterbalance|Antonym:  primary investment, core holding|noun
