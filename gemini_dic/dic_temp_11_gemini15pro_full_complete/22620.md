boozily:/ˈbuːzɪli/| adv.|酩酊大醉地，酒醉醺醺地|In a manner affected by alcohol; drunkenly.|He sang boozily at the karaoke bar. 他在卡拉OK酒吧里醉醺醺地唱歌。|例句：She laughed boozily and stumbled into the room. 她醉醺醺地笑着，跌跌撞撞地走进房间。|近义词：drunkenly, tipsily|反义词：soberly|词性:副词

词源: From booze + -ily.  Booze comes from Middle Dutch *būsen.
