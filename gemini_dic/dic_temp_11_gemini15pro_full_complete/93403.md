illiteracy:/ɪˈlɪtərəsi/ | n.|文盲，不识字|The inability to read or write.|例句：Illiteracy is a major problem in many parts of the world. 文盲是世界许多地方的主要问题。|例句：The organization is working to eradicate illiteracy. 该组织正在努力消除文盲。|近义词：unletteredness|反义词：literacy|词性：名词

词源: From Latin *illitteratus*, from in- ‘not’ + litteratus ‘lettered’.
