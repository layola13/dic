overflying:/ˌoʊvərˈflaɪɪŋ/| adj.  v. (present participle of overfly)|飞越的，从…上空飞过的|flying over something, especially a country or area of land|例句：The overflying aircraft was identified as a commercial airliner.  这架飞越的飞机被确定为一架商用客机。|例句：Regulations for overflying military airspace are very strict. 飞越军事空域的规定非常严格。|近义词：flying over, passing over|反义词:landing, grounded|词性:形容词, 动词(现在分词)

词源：From "over" + "flying" (present participle of "fly").  "Over" indicates location above, and "fly" signifies movement through the air.
