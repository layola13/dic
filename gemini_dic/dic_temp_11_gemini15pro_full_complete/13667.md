attracting:/əˈtræktɪŋ/
|v.|吸引，引诱|Drawing or pulling something towards itself; causing interest or pleasure.|The colorful flowers are attracting bees. 这些鲜艳的花朵正在吸引蜜蜂。|近义词：alluring, charming, enticing, fascinating, captivating|反义词：repelling, deterring, disgusting|词性：动词现在分词
词源：From Latin *attrahere*, from *ad-* "to" + *trahere* "to draw, pull".
