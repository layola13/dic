e-toll:/ˈiːtəʊl/ | n. | 电子收费 | Electronic toll collection, typically using transponders or license plate recognition to automatically charge drivers for using a toll road or bridge. | Example: The e-toll system saves time and reduces traffic congestion. 电子收费系统节省时间并减少交通拥堵。| Example:  I paid the e-toll online after my trip. 我在旅行结束后在网上支付了电子通行费。| Synonym: electronic toll collection, automated toll collection | Antonym: manual toll collection | noun
