incorruption:/ˌɪnkəˈrʌpʃən/
n.
 incorruptibility, honesty
The state of not being corrupted; freedom from decay or degeneration, especially of a body after death.
His incorruption and integrity were admired by all. 他廉洁正直的品格受到所有人的钦佩。
integrity, probity, honesty, righteousness
corruption, depravity, dishonesty, immorality
noun
