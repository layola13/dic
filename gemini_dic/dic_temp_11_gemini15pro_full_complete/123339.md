monolingual:/ˌmɒnəˈlɪŋɡwəl/ /ˌmɑːnəˈlɪŋɡwəl/| adj. |单语的；只用一种语言的|Speaking or using only one language.  Able to speak only one language.|She grew up in a monolingual household. 她在一个单语家庭长大。|近义词:unilingual|反义词:bilingual, multilingual|词性:形容词
Etymology: From mono- (one) + lingual (relating to language). Ultimately from Greek monos (alone, single) and Latin lingua (tongue, language).
