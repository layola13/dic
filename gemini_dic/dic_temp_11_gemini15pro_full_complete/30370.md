centerfold:/ˈsɛntərfoʊld/| n.|插页，中央图片，(尤指杂志)裸体照片|A large picture printed across the center pages of a magazine, often of a sexually suggestive nature.|Example: The magazine's centerfold this month featured a scantily clad model. 这本杂志本月的插页刊登了一个衣着暴露的模特。

Example: He carefully removed the centerfold from the magazine and pinned it to his wall. 他小心翼翼地从杂志上取下插页，并把它钉在墙上。

|近义词：pinup, playmate|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "cover")|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From center + fold.  "Fold" here refers to the way a large sheet of paper is folded in the center to create the magazine's pages. The term gained its association with nudity in the mid-20th century with the rise of magazines like Playboy.
