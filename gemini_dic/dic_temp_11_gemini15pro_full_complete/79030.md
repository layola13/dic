give-away:/ˈɡɪvəˌweɪ/ (US), /ˈɡɪvəweɪ/ (UK)| n. adj.|赠品，泄露，暴露|Something that is given away free, especially as a prize or promotion; an unintentional action or remark that reveals a secret.|The radio station was having a give-away for a new car.  这家广播电台正在举行赠送新车的活动。|Synonyms: freebie, premium, giveaway, prize, gift|Antonyms: purchase, sale|noun

give-away:/ˈɡɪvəˌweɪ/ (US), /ˈɡɪvəweɪ/ (UK)| adj.|暴露的，泄露的|Revealing unintentionally, betraying.|His nervous laugh was a dead give-away. 他紧张的笑声完全暴露了他的秘密。|Synonyms: revealing, telltale, incriminating|Antonyms: concealing, hiding|adjective
