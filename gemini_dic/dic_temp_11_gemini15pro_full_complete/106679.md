kvetches:/ˈkvɛtʃɪz/ | n. | 抱怨，牢骚 | A complaint, especially a chronic or petty one. |  Her constant kvetches about the weather are getting tiresome. 她不断抱怨天气，真是烦人。 | Synonyms: complaint, grumble, whine | Antonyms: praise, compliment | Noun

词源： From Yiddish קװעטשן (kvetshn), from Middle High German quetschen (“to squeeze, pinch, crush”).


注意事项:  kvetches 主要用于美式英语，尤其是在非正式场合。  虽然 kvetches 可以作名词，但动词形式更为常见。
