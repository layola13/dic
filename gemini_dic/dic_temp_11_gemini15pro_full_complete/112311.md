lordliness:/ˈlɔːrdlɪnɪs/
n.
傲慢，尊贵，威严
The state or quality of being lordly; haughtiness, dignity, majesty.
He behaved with great lordliness. 他举止非常傲慢。
Synonyms: haughtiness, arrogance, superciliousness, pomposity, grandeur, majesty, nobility
Antonyms: humility, modesty, humbleness
