midgarth: /ˈmɪdɡɑːrθ/
n.
人间；中土世界
The world inhabited by humans; the earth.
The hero returned from his adventures to the familiar comforts of midgarth.  英雄结束了冒险，回到了熟悉舒适的人间。
Midgarth, in Norse mythology, was the realm of men. 在北欧神话中，中土世界是人类的领域。
synonym: Earth, world, human world, mortal realm
antonym: heavens, underworld, afterlife, otherworld
