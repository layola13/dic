micrometeorites:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmiːtiəraɪts/ | n. | 微陨石 | Extremely small meteorites, typically less than a millimeter in size, that constantly bombard Earth. |  Micrometeorites are so tiny they often go unnoticed. 微陨石非常微小，经常被忽视。| Synonyms: cosmic dust, space dust | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

micrometeorite:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmiːtiəraɪt/ | n. | 微陨石 | A single particle of a micrometeorite. | A single micrometeorite can hold clues about the early solar system. 一颗微陨石可以提供关于早期太阳系的线索。 | Synonyms: cosmic dust particle, space dust particle | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
