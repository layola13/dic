onomantic:/ˌɒnəˈmæntɪk/ | adj. |  预言的，与名字相关的 | Relating to divination by names.  Derived from the Greek words "onoma" (name) and "manteia" (divination). It refers to the practice of predicting the future or determining character based on a person's name. |  Some people believe in onomantic practices, thinking that names hold a hidden power.  有些人相信名字占卜，认为名字蕴含着隐藏的力量。 |  nominative, name-based |  - | adjective
Etymology: From Greek ὄνομα (ónoma, “name”) + μαντεία (manteía, “divination”).
