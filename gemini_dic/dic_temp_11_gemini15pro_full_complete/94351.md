impiousness:/ɪmˈpaɪəsnəs/| n. |不虔诚，不敬，邪恶|Lack of piety or reverence, especially for God; wickedness.|His impiousness shocked the devout villagers. 他的不敬震惊了虔诚的村民们。|Near Synonyms: irreverence, ungodliness, blasphemy, profanity, sacrilege|Near Antonyms: piety, devoutness, reverence, holiness|noun

impious:/ˈɪmpiəs/| adj. |不虔诚的，不敬的，邪恶的|Not showing respect or reverence, especially for God; wicked.|It was considered impious to question the priest's authority.  质疑神父的权威被认为是不敬的。|Near Synonyms: irreverent, ungodly, blasphemous, profane, sacrilegious|Near Antonyms: pious, devout, reverent, holy|adjective
