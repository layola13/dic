illustrable:/ˈɪləstreɪtəbl/
adjective
可图解的，可说明的
Capable of being illustrated; possible to explain or demonstrate with examples.
This principle is easily illustrable by a simple diagram.  这个原理很容易用一个简单的图表来说明。
The complex process is illustrable with a flowchart.  这个复杂的过程可以用流程图来说明。
近义词: demonstrable, explicable, explainable
反义词: inexplicable, obscure, unclear
词性: adjective

词源:
From illustrate + -able.  来自 illustrate (说明，图解) + -able (可...的)
