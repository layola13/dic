appropriator:/əˈproʊpriˌeɪtər/ | n. | 占用者，拨款者 |Someone who takes something for their own use, typically without permission or right. | The appropriator of the funds was eventually caught and prosecuted.  资金占用者最终被抓获并被起诉。 |  近义词: usurper, seizer, confiscator | 反义词: donor, contributor | 词性: noun

appropriator:/əˈproʊpriˌeɪtər/ | n. | 拨款者 |Someone who officially allocates money for a particular purpose. | The city council is the appropriator of public funds for infrastructure projects. 市议会是基础设施项目公共资金的拨款者。| 近义词: funder, financier | 反义词: recipient, beneficiary | 词性: noun
