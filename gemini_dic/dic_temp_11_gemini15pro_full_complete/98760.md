intagliated: /ɪnˈtæliˌeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 雕刻的，刻花的 | Engraved or carved into a surface, especially as a design. | The intagliated design on the ring was exquisite.  戒指上雕刻的图案非常精美。 |  The artist meticulously intagliated the design into the silver plate. 艺术家一丝不苟地将图案雕刻在银盘上。 | 近义词: engraved, carved, incised | 反义词: raised, embossed | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Italian *intagliare*, from *in-* ‘in’ + *tagliare* ‘to cut’.
