favonian:/fəˈvoʊniən/| adj.|温和的，温暖的，西风的|Of or relating to the west wind; mild and warm.|The favonian breeze gently stirred the leaves. 温和的西风轻轻地吹动着树叶。|近义词：balmy, mild, gentle, zephyrlike|反义词：harsh, cold, biting, frigid|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *favonius* "west wind," from *favere* "to favor." The west wind was considered a gentle, favorable wind in ancient Rome.
