moggie: /ˈmɒɡi/ (British English) | n. | 家猫，猫咪 | A cat, especially a domestic cat.  |  Example: My neighbour's moggie likes to sleep in my garden.  我的邻居家的猫喜欢在我家花园里睡觉。

| Example: The stray moggie was eventually adopted by a kind family. 这只流浪猫最终被一个好心的家庭收养了。 | Synonym: cat, feline, kitty, puss | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for an animal.  Could consider "dog" as a common contrasting pet.) | Noun
