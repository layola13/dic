emmetropic: /ˌɛmɪˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 正视的，屈光正常的 | Having normal vision; not needing corrective lenses. |  Example 1:  Her eyes were tested and found to be emmetropic. 她的眼睛经过测试，发现是正视的。

Example 2:  An emmetropic eye can focus images clearly on the retina.  正视眼可以清晰地将图像聚焦在视网膜上。 | 近义词: normal-sighted | 反义词: ametropic, myopic, hyperopic | 词性: adjective

emmetropia: /ˌɛmɪˈtrəʊpɪə/ | n. | 正视，屈光正常 | The state of having normal vision. | Example 1:  The ophthalmologist confirmed her emmetropia. 眼科医生证实了她是正视眼。

Example 2: Achieving emmetropia is the goal of refractive surgery.  达到正视是屈光手术的目标。 | 近义词: normal vision | 反义词: ametropia, myopia, hyperopia | 词性: noun
