game-changing:/ˈɡeɪmˌtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective
改变游戏规则的; 具有颠覆性的
Having a significant and disruptive impact on the existing situation or market.  A game-changing innovation alters the landscape and often establishes a new standard.
The invention of the smartphone was a game-changing moment in the history of technology.  智能手机的发明是科技史上一个改变游戏规则的时刻。

The development of the internet has been a game-changing technology for communication. 互联网的发展是一项改变了沟通方式的颠覆性技术。


近义词:revolutionary, transformative, groundbreaking, disruptive, innovative
反义词:insignificant, minor, trivial, conventional, traditional
词性:adjective
