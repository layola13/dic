accessory: /əkˈsesəri/ (US) /əkˈsesəri/ (UK)| n. adj. |配件，附件；附属的，次要的|A thing that can be added to something else in order to make it more useful, attractive, or effective.  A supplementary or subordinate fact or detail.|She wore a simple black dress with a colorful scarf as an accessory. 她穿着一件简单的黑色连衣裙，搭配一条色彩鲜艳的围巾作为配饰。|Synonyms:  adjunct, attachment, component, addition, supplement|Antonyms: essential, necessary, integral, fundamental|noun, adjective
