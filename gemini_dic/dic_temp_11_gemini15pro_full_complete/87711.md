hexastich:/ˈhɛksəstɪk/ | n. | 六行诗; 六句诗 | A poem or stanza of six lines. |  Example: The poet concluded his thoughts with a poignant hexastich. 这位诗人以一首感人的六行诗结束了他的思考。

Example: She wrote a hexastich for her friend's birthday. 她为朋友的生日写了一首六行诗。 | Near Synonyms: sestet, sixain | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific verse form) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hexastichos*, from *hexa-* "six" + *stichos* "line, row, verse."
