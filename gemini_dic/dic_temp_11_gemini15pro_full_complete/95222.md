inbred:/ˈɪnbrɛd/| adj.|近亲繁殖的；天生的；根深蒂固的|Bred from closely related individuals, especially over many generations; Innate or ingrained.|例句：The problems of inbred communities. 近亲繁殖群落的问题。|近义词：inborn, congenital, inherent, innate|反义词：outbred, acquired|词性：形容词
inbred:/ˈɪnbrɛd/| n.|近亲繁殖的动植物|An animal or plant produced by inbreeding.|例句：The prize-winning dog was an inbred. 这条获奖的狗是近亲繁殖的。|近义词：--|反义词：--|词性：名词
