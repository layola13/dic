objectification: /əbˌdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 物化，客体化 | The act of treating a person as an object, especially a sexual object, reducing them to their physical attributes and ignoring their personality or dignity. |  The advertisement's objectification of women was met with widespread criticism.  该广告对女性的物化遭到了广泛批评。

The objectification of women in media is a serious issue.  媒体中对女性的物化是一个严重的问题。 | 近义词：dehumanization, depersonalization | 反义词：humanization, personalization | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin objectificātiō, from objectificātus, past participle of objectificāre  "to present to the mind, make objective," from ob- "toward" + jectus, past participle of jacere "to throw".
