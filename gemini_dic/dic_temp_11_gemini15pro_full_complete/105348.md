kickass:/ˈkɪkæs/| adj. informal| 极好的，很棒的；厉害的| exceptionally good; excellent. | That concert was kickass! 那场音乐会真是太棒了！|  That's a kickass guitar you've got there! 你那把吉他真是太酷了！|近义词：awesome, excellent, amazing, fantastic, superb|反义词：awful, terrible, lousy|词性:形容词

词源:
kick (v.) + ass (n.).  "Kick ass" originally meant to beat up or defeat someone thoroughly. Over time, it evolved to become an intensifier, eventually settling as an adjective meaning "excellent."
