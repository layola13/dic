epigee:/ˈɛpɪdʒiː/| n. |近地点|The point in the orbit of the moon or a satellite at which it is nearest to the earth.|The satellite reached its epigee at 04:00 GMT.  卫星在格林威治标准时间4:00到达近地点。|近义词:periapsis|反义词:apogee|词性:名词
