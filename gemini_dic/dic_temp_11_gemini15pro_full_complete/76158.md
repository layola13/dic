fusilier:/ˌfjuːzɪˈlɪər/ /ˌfjuːsəˈlɪr/| n.|火枪手；轻步兵|A soldier armed with a fusil (a light musket).  Historically, fusiliers were specially trained infantrymen.  Now, the term is largely historical or ceremonial.|The fusiliers stood in formation, ready for inspection.  火枪手们排好队形，准备接受检阅。|Synonyms: infantryman, rifleman, soldier, trooper|Antonyms: civilian|noun
