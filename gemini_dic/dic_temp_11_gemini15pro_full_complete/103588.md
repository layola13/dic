join issue:/ˌdʒɔɪn ˈɪʃuː/ | v. | 争论；就…展开辩论 | To begin to formally disagree or argue about something. | The two parties joined issue over the proposed tax cuts. 双方就拟议的减税方案展开了辩论。

The candidates joined issue on the question of healthcare reform. 候选人们就医疗改革问题展开了辩论。

Synonyms: disagree, dispute, contend, take issue, clash, lock horns

Antonyms: agree, concur, concede

Etymology: From Anglo-French *joindre issue*, from joindre "to join" + issue "outcome, exit, a point in question".  Meaning shifted from "to reach a point of decision or contention" to simply "to contend."

Word Type: Verb (Phrasal Verb)
