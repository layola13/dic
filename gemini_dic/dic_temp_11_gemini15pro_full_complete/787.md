absorbednesses:/əbˈzɔːrbdnəsəz/
|n.|全神贯注，专注|The state of being absorbed; preoccupation.|He worked with such absorbedness that he didn't notice the time.|近义词:  preoccupation, engrossment, immersion, captivation|反义词: distraction, inattention, heedlessness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From absorbed + -ness.  "Absorbed" comes from the Latin "absorbere," meaning "to swallow up."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
