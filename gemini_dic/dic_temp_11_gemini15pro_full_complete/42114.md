cotter:/ˈkɑːtər/ (US), /ˈkɒtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|楔子，销，栓|A cotter is a metal fastener, typically a split pin or wedge, used to secure two parts together.|The cotter pin held the wheel securely on the axle.  车轴上的楔子将车轮牢牢固定住。|Synonyms: pin, wedge, key, fastener|Antonyms: N/A|noun

cotter:/ˈkɑːtər/ (US), /ˈkɒtə(r)/ (UK)| v.|用楔子固定|To fasten or secure something with a cotter.|He cottered the joint with a steel pin. 他用钢销将接头楔住。|Synonyms: fasten, secure, pin, wedge|Antonyms: loosen, unfasten, detach|verb
