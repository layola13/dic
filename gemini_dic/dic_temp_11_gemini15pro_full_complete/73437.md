foremean:/fɔːrˈmiːn/
adjective
预兆的；预示的
signifying beforehand; presignificant; serving as an omen or a presage
The dark clouds foremean a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。
近义词:presage, portend, foreshadow, augur
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective

foremean:/fɔːrˈmiːn/
verb
预示；预兆
to suggest or indicate beforehand; portend
The raven's appearance foremeans ill fortune.  乌鸦的出现预示着厄运。
近义词:presage, portend, foreshadow, augur
反义词:N/A
词性:verb
