outstreet:/ˈaʊtˌstriːt/
n.
郊区街道；偏僻街道
A street in the outlying districts of a town or city.
We drove down a quiet outstreet. 我们沿着一条安静的郊区街道行驶。
近义词: backstreet, side street
反义词: main street, high street
词性: 名词
