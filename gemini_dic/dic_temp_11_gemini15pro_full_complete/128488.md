newspapers:/ˈnuːzˌpeɪpərz/ (US), /ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpəz/ (UK)| n.|报纸|Printed publications containing news, articles, and advertisements.|I read several newspapers every morning to stay informed. 我每天早上都会读几份报纸以保持消息灵通。|近义词：journals, periodicals, gazettes|反义词: None|Noun

Etymology:
From "news" + "paper". "News" itself is likely a contraction of "new things".
