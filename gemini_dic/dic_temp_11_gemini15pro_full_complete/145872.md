overdescriptively:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskrɪptɪvli/
adverb|过度描述地，过于详细地|Characterized by the use of excessive description, providing more detail than necessary or helpful; too minutely detailed.|The novel was overdescriptively written, making it tedious to read. 这部小说写得过于详细，读起来很乏味。|近义词：verbosely, elaborately, exhaustively|反义词：concisely, succinctly, briefly|词性: adverb
Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "descriptive" (from Late Latin descriptivus, from Latin describere "to write down, copy") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
