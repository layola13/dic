epistome:/ˈɛpɪstoʊm/| n. |上唇；口上板；唇基|A plate or shield-like structure covering the mouthparts of certain invertebrates, such as arachnids and some crustaceans.  In insects, it refers to the clypeus, a part of the insect's "face" just above the mouth. |The spider's epistome was clearly visible under the microscope. 这只蜘蛛的口上板在显微镜下清晰可见。|Synonyms: clypeus (in insects), rostrum |Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms exist due to the specific anatomical nature of the term)|noun
