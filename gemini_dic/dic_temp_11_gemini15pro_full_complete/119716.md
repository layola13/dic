midships:/ˈmɪdʃɪps/ | adv. adj. n.|在船中部；在正中央|In or toward the middle of a ship's length.|The captain ordered the crew to assemble midships. 船长命令船员在船中部集合。|The midships section of the ship was damaged in the storm. 船的中部在风暴中受损。|近义词：amidships, center, middle|反义词：fore, aft|词性: 副词，形容词，名词

Etymology: From Middle English *middeschippes*, from *midde* (middle) + *schippes* (genitive plural of *schip* - ship).  Essentially meaning "in the middle of the ships" (where "ships" referred to the structure of a ship's sides).
