nebulas:/ˈnɛbjʊləz/ /ˈnebjələz/ | n. |星云;星云状的|A cloud of gas and dust in outer space, visible in the night sky either as an indistinct bright patch or as a dark silhouette against other luminous matter. |例句：The Horsehead Nebula is a dark nebula in the constellation Orion. 马头星云是猎户座中的一个暗星云。

例句：Astronomers study nebulas to learn about the formation of stars. 天文学家研究星云以了解恒星的形成。|近义词：clouds (in a general sense), galaxies (sometimes)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "clear sky" in a specific observational context) |词性: Noun (plural)

nebula: /ˈnɛbjʊlə/ /ˈnebjələ/ | n. |星云;星云状的|A cloud of gas and dust in outer space, visible in the night sky either as an indistinct bright patch or as a dark silhouette against other luminous matter. |例句：The Orion Nebula is a diffuse nebula situated south of Orion's Belt. 猎户座星云是一个弥漫星云，位于猎户座腰带的南部。

例句：Some nebulae are the birthplaces of stars. 一些星云是恒星的诞生地。|近义词：clouds (in a general sense), galaxies (sometimes)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "clear sky" in a specific observational context) |词性: Noun (singular)
