nonpliableness:/ˌnɒnˈplaɪəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
不顺从; 固执; 倔强; 不易弯曲
Lack of pliableness; inflexibility or stubbornness.
His nonpliableness made negotiations difficult. 他的不妥协使得谈判变得困难。
近义词: inflexibility, rigidity, stubbornness, obstinacy
反义词: flexibility, pliancy, docility, compliance
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From non- (not) + pliable (easily bent) + -ness (forming nouns of quality).  Pliable ultimately comes from Latin *plicabilis, from plicare (to fold).
