deepfake: /ˈdiːpfeɪk/ | n. adj.|深度伪造，深度造假|A video, photo, or audio recording that has been manipulated using artificial intelligence to create a realistic but fabricated representation of someone saying or doing something they did not actually say or do.| The politician denied the video was real, claiming it was a deepfake designed to damage his reputation.  这位政治家否认视频是真的，声称这是一个旨在损害他声誉的深度伪造视频。| Deepfake technology can create incredibly realistic videos. 深度伪造技术可以制作出非常逼真的视频。|synonyms:  synthetic media,  AI-generated media,  fake video/audio/photo| antonyms: authentic media, real footage, genuine recording | noun, adjective
