词源:

"Nonbreach" and its plural form "nonbreaches" are formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the noun "breach."  "Breach" itself comes from the Old French word "breche," meaning "a break or gap."  Therefore, "nonbreach" literally means "not a break" or "not a violation."
