grumbler:/ˈɡrʌmblər/ (US), /ˈɡrʌmblə(r)/ (UK)| n. |抱怨者，发牢骚的人|A person who complains habitually, often about petty things.|He's a chronic grumbler, always finding something to whine about. 他是一个惯于抱怨的人，总是能找到一些事情来抱怨。|The old grumbler was unhappy about everything.  这个老爱抱怨的人对一切都感到不满。|complainer, whiner, malcontent|optimist, enthusiast|noun
