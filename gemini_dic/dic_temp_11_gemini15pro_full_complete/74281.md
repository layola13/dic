frabbit:/ˈfræbɪt/| n.|碎布; 破布; 无价值的东西|A frabbit is a small piece of cloth, especially one that is torn or frayed. It can also refer to something considered worthless or insignificant.|The tailor swept up the frabbits from the floor. 裁缝把地上的碎布扫了起来。|He dismissed the idea as a mere frabbit. 他认为这个想法毫无价值。|近义词：rag, scrap, shred|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 "whole cloth" 表示完整的布料)|词性:名词
