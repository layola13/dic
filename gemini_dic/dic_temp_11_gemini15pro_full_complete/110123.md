lidded:/ˈlɪdɪd/| adj.|有盖的，带盖的|Having a lid or cover.  Often used in combination, e.g., "heavy-lidded eyes."|She carried a small, lidded basket. 她提着一个带盖的小篮子。|The sleepy cat had heavy-lidded eyes.  这只困倦的猫眼皮沉重。|近义词：covered, capped|反义词：uncovered, open|词性:形容词

lidded:/ˈlɪdɪd/| adj.|（眼睛）半闭的，沉重的|Having eyelids that appear heavy, often due to tiredness or sleepiness.|He looked at her with heavy-lidded eyes. 他用沉重的眼皮看着她。|The old dog lay dozing, its eyes lidded. 老狗躺着打盹，眼睛半闭着。|近义词：droopy, heavy|反义词：wide-open, alert|词性:形容词
