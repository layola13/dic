nigrosine:/ˈnaɪɡrəˌsiːn/ /ˈnɪɡrəˌsin/| n.|苯胺黑，黑染料|A synthetic black dye, derived from aniline, used in microscopy and as a colorant in various applications.|例句：Nigrosine is commonly used to stain nerve tissues for microscopic examination. 苯胺黑常用于染色神经组织以便显微镜检查。

例句：The artist used nigrosine to create a deep black ink for his calligraphy.这位艺术家用苯胺黑为他创作的书法作品调制了一种深黑色的墨水。|近义词：aniline black, black dye|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
