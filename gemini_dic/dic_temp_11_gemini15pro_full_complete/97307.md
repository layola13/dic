informal:/ɪnˈfɔːrml/ (US) /ɪnˈfɔːməl/ (UK)| adj.|非正式的，不拘礼节的|Characterized by a casual, relaxed, and familiar style or manner; not formal or official.|We had an informal meeting at the coffee shop. 我们在咖啡店进行了一次非正式的会面。|Synonyms: casual, relaxed, unofficial, familiar|Antonyms: formal, official, ceremonious|adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + formal.  The word "formal" ultimately derives from the Latin "forma" (form, shape).
