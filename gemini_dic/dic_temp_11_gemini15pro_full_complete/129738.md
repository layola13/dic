non-equitable:/nɒnˈɛkwɪtəbl/ | adj. |不公平的，不公正的 | Not fair or just; unequal. |  The non-equitable distribution of resources led to social unrest. 资源分配不公导致了社会动荡。|  The current healthcare system is non-equitable, favoring the wealthy.  目前的医疗体系不公平，偏袒富人。 | 近义词: unfair, unjust, biased, discriminatory, prejudiced | 反义词: equitable, fair, just, impartial | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
equitable (adjective): from Old French "equitable,"  from Latin "aequitabilis," from "aequitas" meaning "equality, fairness."
