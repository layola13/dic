minnesinger:/ˈmɪnəˌsɪŋər/| n. |吟唱诗人，恋歌歌手|A medieval German lyric poet who composed and sang love songs, often about chivalry and courtly love.|The minnesinger wandered from castle to castle, entertaining the nobility with his songs. 恋歌歌手在城堡间游走，用歌声娱乐贵族。|近义词：troubadour, bard, poet|反义词:|noun
