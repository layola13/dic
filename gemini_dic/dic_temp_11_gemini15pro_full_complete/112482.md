lounged:/laʊndʒd/
verb
休息; 闲逛;  (懒洋洋地) 躺着, 倚靠
past tense and past participle of lounge
She lounged by the pool all afternoon. 她整个下午都躺在游泳池边。

近义词:relaxed, reclined, rested, idled
反义词:worked, hurried, rushed
词性:verb (past tense/past participle)
