blindfold:/ˈblaɪndˌfoʊld/ (US) /ˈblaɪndfəʊld/ (UK)| n. v. adj.|眼罩，蒙眼，蒙住眼睛的|A piece of cloth tied over someone's eyes to prevent them from seeing.  To cover someone's eyes with a blindfold.  Having one's eyes covered with a blindfold.|

孩子们玩蒙眼捉人游戏。

她被蒙上眼睛，带进了房间。
