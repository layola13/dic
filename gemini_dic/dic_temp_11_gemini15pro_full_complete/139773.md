octamerous: /ɒkˈtæmərəs/
adjective
八基数的；八部的；八数的
Having eight parts, especially of a flower with eight petals, sepals, etc. or a mollusc with eight arms.
The octamerous flower is a beautiful example of nature's symmetry.  这朵八基数的花是大自然对称性的一个美丽例子。

The botanist identified the rare octamerous specimen.  植物学家识别出了这种罕见的八基数标本。


synonym: eight-parted, eight-membered
antonym: (No direct antonym, consider specifying a different number of parts, e.g., tetramerous, pentamerous)
word type: adjective
