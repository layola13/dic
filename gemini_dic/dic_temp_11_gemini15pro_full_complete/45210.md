gullible:/ˈɡʌləbl/ | adj. | 易受骗的；轻信的 | Easily persuaded to believe something; credulous. |  He was so gullible he believed the email promising him a million dollars. 他太容易受骗了，竟然相信那封承诺给他一百万美元的电子邮件。

She's so gullible, she'll believe anything. 她太容易上当了，什么都信。


| 近义词：credulous, naive, trusting | 反义词：skeptical, cynical, suspicious | 词性: adjective
