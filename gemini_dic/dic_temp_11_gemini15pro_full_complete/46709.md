danceable:/ˈdɑːnsəbl/ (US), /ˈdɑːnsəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可跳舞的；适合跳舞的|Suitable for dancing; easy to dance to.|The music was upbeat and danceable.  这音乐欢快且适合跳舞。|The band played a danceable tune. 乐队演奏了一支适合跳舞的曲子。|近义词：rhythmic, groovy, catchy|反义词：undanceable, awkward, stiff|词性: adjective
