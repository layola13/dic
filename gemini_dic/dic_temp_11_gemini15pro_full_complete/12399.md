asiaticism: /ˌeɪʒiˈætɪsɪzəm/ /ˌeɪʃiˈætɪsɪzəm/
|n.|亚洲主义|A political ideology that promotes the unity and solidarity of Asian peoples and nations against Western influence.  It encompasses various forms, from advocating cultural exchange and economic cooperation to more aggressive assertions of Asian superiority and the rejection of Western values.| Japan's wartime rhetoric often employed Asiaticism to justify its expansionist policies. 日本的战时言论经常使用亚洲主义来为其扩张政策辩护。|Near Synonyms: Pan-Asianism, Asian nationalism|Antonyms: Occidentalism, Westernization|Noun


asiatic: /ˌeɪʒiˈætɪk/ /ˌeɪʃiˈætɪk/
|adj.|亚洲的|Relating to or characteristic of Asia or its people.  Can sometimes be considered outdated or even offensive due to its broad generalization and potential for stereotyping.|The museum has a fascinating collection of Asiatic art.  这家博物馆收藏了引人入胜的亚洲艺术品。|Near Synonyms: Asian|Antonyms: Western, European, Occidental|Adjective
