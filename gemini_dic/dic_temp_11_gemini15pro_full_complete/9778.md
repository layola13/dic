antozone:/ˈæntɪˌzoʊn/ |n.|反臭氧，过氧化氢 |A hypothetical form of ozone, O₃, considered as having a negatively charged oxygen atom; hydrogen peroxide.  Considered by 19th-century scientists to be a constituent of healthy air. |The scientists of the time believed antozone was beneficial, even essential for healthy air.  当时的科学家认为反臭氧是有益的，甚至是健康空气所必需的。|Synonyms:  peroxide (specifically hydrogen peroxide) |Antonyms:  ozone (in the context of 19th-century theories) |noun
