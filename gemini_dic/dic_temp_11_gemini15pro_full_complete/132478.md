nonderogatorilyness:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɒɡətɔːrɪlɪnɪs/ | n. |  无贬义; 不贬损 | The state or quality of being nonderogatory.  It implies an absence of any intention to belittle or disparage. | He spoke of his former employer with a surprising nonderogatorilyness, given their acrimonious parting. 他谈到他以前雇主时，竟然没有贬低之意，考虑到他们不欢而散，这真是令人惊讶。 | 近义词: neutrality, objectivity, impartiality | 反义词: disparagement, denigration, derogatoriness | 词性: 名词

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + derogatory (adjective meaning "showing a critical or disrespectful attitude") + -ly (adverb suffix) + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives)
