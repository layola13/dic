idioelectric:/ˌɪdioʊɪˈlɛktrik/| adj.|特异电性的，自身电性的|Pertaining to the electrical properties unique to a specific substance or tissue, especially in living organisms. This often refers to the ability of certain tissues, like muscle, to generate their own electrical activity.|The idioelectric properties of the heart muscle allow it to contract rhythmically. 心肌的特异电性使其能够有节奏地收缩。|近义词：bioelectric|反义词:|adjective
