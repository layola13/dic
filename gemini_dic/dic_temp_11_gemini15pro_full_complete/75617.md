fugue:/fjuːɡ/
n.
赋格; 轮唱; 神经错乱
A contrapuntal composition in which a short melody or subject is introduced by one part and successively taken up by others and developed by interweaving the parts. A state of loss of awareness of one's identity, often coupled with flight from one's usual environment.

Fugue state is a rare psychiatric disorder characterized by reversible amnesia for personal identity, including the memories, personality, and other identifying characteristics of individuality. The state can last days, weeks, months, or even longer.

近义词: canon, round, amnesia
反义词: lucidity, awareness
词性: 名词


fuguer:/ˈfjuːɡər/
n.
创作赋格曲的人; 逃亡者; 神经错乱者
A person who composes fugues. A person who runs away or escapes. A person in a fugue state.

The fuguer disappeared from his home without any warning, leaving his family worried and confused.

近义词: composer, runaway, amnesiac
反义词: resident, returnee
词性: 名词
