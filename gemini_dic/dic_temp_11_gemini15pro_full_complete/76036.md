furl:/fɜːrl/| v. n.|卷起，收拢； furlough 休假|To roll or fold up and secure neatly, as a sail or a flag.  Also, to dismiss from service, especially to grant a leave of absence to.|The captain ordered the sailors to furl the sails. 船长命令水手们收起帆。|近义词：fold, roll up, stow, unfurl|反义词: unfurl, unroll, spread|verb, noun


furl:/fɜːrl/| n.|卷，折叠；休假|The act of furling something, or the state of being furled.|The furl of the sails signaled the end of the voyage. 帆的卷起标志着航行的结束。|近义词：fold, roll|反义词: unfolding, unrolling|noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English furlen, from Old French ferler "to furl," of uncertain origin, possibly from Frankish *ferlingan.
