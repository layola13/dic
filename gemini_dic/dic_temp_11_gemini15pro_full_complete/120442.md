noun
明尼苏达人
People who live in or are from the state of Minnesota.
Many Minnesotans enjoy spending time outdoors, especially in the summer. 许多明尼苏达人喜欢在户外活动，尤其是在夏天。
Minnesotans are known for their "Minnesota nice" demeanor.  明尼苏达人以他们的“明尼苏达式友好”举止而闻名。
Synonyms:  Minnesotan residents, Minnesota natives
