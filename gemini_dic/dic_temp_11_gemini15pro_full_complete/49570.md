dementedly:/dɪˈmɛntɪdli/
adverb
疯狂地；精神错乱地
In a way that suggests someone is affected by dementia; madly, insanely.
He laughed dementedly, startling the other passengers on the bus. 他疯狂地大笑，吓到了公交车上的其他乘客。
He paced dementedly around the room. 他在房间里疯狂地踱步。
Synonyms: insanely, madly, crazily, wildly, frantically, hysterically
Antonyms: sanely, rationally, calmly, reasonably
词性: 副词
