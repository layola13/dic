一般现在时 (Simple Present):
All's quiet on the western front.  西部战线一片寂静。
All's fair in love and war.  情场如战场，一切皆公平。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Not applicable.  "All's" is a static expression and doesn't fit naturally in the continuous tense.


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Not applicable. Similar to the continuous tense, "all's" doesn't typically work with the perfect aspect.


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Not applicable.  Same reasoning as above.
