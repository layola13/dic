anarchy:/ˈænərki/| n.|无政府状态，混乱，无秩序|A state of disorder due to absence or nonrecognition of authority.|The country descended into anarchy after the coup. 政变后，这个国家陷入了无政府状态。|Synonyms: chaos, disorder, lawlessness, turmoil, pandemonium|Antonyms: order, law, government, control|noun

anarchic:/əˈnɑːrkɪk/| adj.|无政府的，无秩序的，混乱的|Resembling anarchy; lacking any rules or order.|The classroom was anarchic without a teacher. 没有老师，教室里一片混乱。|Synonyms: chaotic, lawless, unruly, disorderly|Antonyms: orderly, lawful, organized|adjective
