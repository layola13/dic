joylessly:/ˈdʒɔɪləsli/ | adv. | 无乐趣地，沉闷地 | Without joy; in a way that is not enjoyable or happy. | She stared joylessly out the window at the gray sky. 她闷闷不乐地望着窗外灰蒙蒙的天空。| She ate her dinner joylessly, pushing the food around her plate. 她无乐趣地吃着晚餐，把食物在盘子里拨来拨去。 | 近义词: gloomily, sadly, morosely, dourly | 反义词: joyfully, happily, cheerfully | 词性: 副词

词源: joyless (adjective) + -ly (adverb suffix).  "Joyless" comes from "joy" (from Old French "joie," ultimately from Latin "gaudia," plural of "gaudium" meaning "joy") + "-less" (a suffix meaning "without").
