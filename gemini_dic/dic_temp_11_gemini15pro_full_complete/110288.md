ligature:/ˈlɪɡətʃər/ /ˈlɪɡətʃʊr/ | n. |  连字；捆绑物；结扎线 | something that binds or ties; a character consisting of two or more joined letters; a thread, wire, etc., used in surgery, especially to tie up a bleeding artery |  The two letters are joined together by a ligature. 这两个字母用连字连接在一起。 | synonyms: bond, tie, link, connection, ligature | antonyms: separation, detachment | noun

ligature:/ˈlɪɡətʃər/ /ˈlɪɡətʃʊr/ | v. | 捆绑；结扎 | to tie or bind something tightly; to tie up a bleeding artery | The doctor ligatured the bleeding artery. 医生结扎了出血的动脉。 | synonyms: bind, tie, fasten, secure | antonyms: untie, loosen, release | verb
