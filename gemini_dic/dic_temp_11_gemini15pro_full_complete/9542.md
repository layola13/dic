antisentimental:/ˌæntiˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəl/
|adj.|反感伤的，反对感伤主义的|Opposed to or rejecting sentimentality; unsentimental.|He took an antisentimental view of the war, focusing on the brutal realities rather than romanticized notions of heroism. 他对战争采取了反感伤主义的观点，关注残酷的现实，而不是英雄主义的浪漫主义观念。|
|例句：Her antisentimental approach to life was refreshing. 她对生活的反感伤态度令人耳目一新。|
|例句：He gave an antisentimental account of his childhood. 他讲述了一个不带感情色彩的童年故事。|
|近义词：unsentimental, hard-boiled, cynical, pragmatic|
|反义词：sentimental, emotional, mawkish, romantic|
|词性: adjective|

antisentimentalism:/ˌæntiˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəlɪzəm/
|n.|反感伤主义|Opposition to or rejection of sentimentality.|Antisentimentalism was a key element of modernist literature. 反感伤主义是现代主义文学的一个关键元素。|
|例句：The film was a study in antisentimentalism. 这部电影是对反感伤主义的研究。|
|例句：Her antisentimentalism was often mistaken for coldness. 她的反感伤主义常被误认为是冷漠。|
|近义词：cynicism, pragmatism, realism|
|反义词：sentimentality, romanticism, emotionalism|
|词性: noun|
