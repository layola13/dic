dietic:/daɪˈɛtɪk/
adjective
饮食的；与饮食有关的
Relating to diet or to the regulation of diet.
例句：
The dietic restrictions were difficult to follow at first.  一开始很难遵守饮食限制。

She consulted a dietic specialist for help with her weight loss. 她咨询了一位饮食专家以帮助她减肥。

反义词: None
