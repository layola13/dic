hypersthene:/ˈhaɪpərˌsθiːn/| n. |紫苏辉石|A rock-forming mineral belonging to the pyroxene group, containing iron and magnesium.|The geologist identified the sample as hypersthene.  地质学家鉴定该样本为紫苏辉石。|Hypersthene is a common component in certain igneous and metamorphic rocks. 紫苏辉石是某些火成岩和变质岩的常见成分。|近义词: enstatite, bronzite|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From Greek *hyper* (over, above) + *sthenos* (strength), referring to its greater hardness than amphibole, a mineral with which it was often confused.
