metagenome:/ˈmɛtəˌdʒiːnoʊm/| n. |宏基因组|The collective genomes of the microorganisms that reside in an environmental niche.  It represents the genetic potential of a community of organisms.| Researchers analyzed the metagenome of the soil to identify the microbes present. 研究人员分析了土壤的宏基因组以鉴定存在的微生物。|近义词: microbiome (sometimes used interchangeably but technically refers specifically to the genomes of the microbiota), environmental DNA |反义词:  None (no single antonym exists)|Noun

metagenome:/ˈmɛtəˌdʒiːnoʊm/| n. |宏基因组|The entire genetic material present in a given environment, typically from a mixed community of organisms.|Scientists are studying the gut metagenome to understand its role in human health. 科学家正在研究肠道宏基因组以了解其在人类健康中的作用。|近义词: microbiome, environmental DNA|反义词: None|Noun
