aldermanship:/ˈɔːldərmənʃɪp/
n.
市議員的職位；市議員身份
The position or office of an alderman.
He held the aldermanship for ten years. 他担任了十年的市議員。
近义词:council membership,  municipal office
反义词:None
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ealdormann-scipe, from ealdorman (alderman) + -scipe (ship, state, condition).
