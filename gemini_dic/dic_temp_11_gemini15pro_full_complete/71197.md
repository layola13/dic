fistular:/ˈfɪstjʊlər/ | adj. | 管状的；瘘管的；空心的 | Relating to, of the nature of, or having fistulas; hollow like a pipe. | The doctor diagnosed a fistular tract in the patient's abdomen. 医生诊断出病人腹部有瘘管。 | The bone had a fistular structure. 这块骨头具有管状结构。 | 近义词: tubular, hollow | 反义词: solid | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *fistularis, from Latin fistula (“pipe, tube”).
