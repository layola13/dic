invaluable:/ɪnˈvæljuəbl/
adjective
无价的，非常宝贵的
too valuable to measure; priceless
Her advice has been invaluable to me. 她的建议对我来说非常宝贵。
近义词：priceless, precious, indispensable, essential
反义词：worthless, valueless, useless
词性: adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + valuable.  Originally meant "not able to be valued" because the value was too high to be estimated.  Over time, the meaning shifted to "extremely valuable."
