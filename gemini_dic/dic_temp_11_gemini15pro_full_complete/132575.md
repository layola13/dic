nondieting:/ˌnɒnˈdaɪətɪŋ/ | adj. | 非节食的，不控制饮食的 |  Relating to or advocating an approach to weight management that does not involve restrictive diets.  It emphasizes healthy eating habits and body acceptance rather than strict calorie counting or food restrictions. |  She adopted a nondieting approach to eating, focusing on nutritious foods and mindful eating practices. 她采取了一种非节食的饮食方法，注重营养食物和正念饮食实践。

She's written a book about nondieting and body positivity. 她写了一本关于非节食和身体积极性的书。 | 近义词: intuitive eating, mindful eating | 反义词: dieting, restrictive eating | 词性: 形容词
