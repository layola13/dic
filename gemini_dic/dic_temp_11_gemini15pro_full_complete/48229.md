deconstructive: /ˌdiːkənˈstrʌktɪv/ | adj. | 解构的，解构主义的 |  Characterized by deconstruction, a philosophical approach that analyzes texts and other cultural phenomena by exposing their underlying assumptions and contradictions. |  Her deconstructive analysis of the novel revealed its hidden biases. 她对这部小说的解构主义分析揭示了其隐藏的偏见。 | Synonyms: analytical, critical, subversive | Antonyms: constructive, synthetic, unifying | adjective

deconstruct: /ˌdiːkənˈstrʌkt/ | v. | 解构，拆解 | To analyze (a text or other cultural artifact) by exposing its underlying assumptions and contradictions. | The professor deconstructed the poem, revealing its complex layers of meaning. 教授解构了这首诗，揭示了其复杂的含义。 | Synonyms: analyze, dissect, dismantle | Antonyms: construct, synthesize, build | verb
