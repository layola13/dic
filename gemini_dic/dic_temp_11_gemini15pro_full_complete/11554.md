aristocratical:/ˌærɪstəˈkrætɪk(ə)l/
adjective
贵族的；贵族式的
Of, relating to, or characteristic of aristocracy.
The family lived in an aristocratical mansion.  这个家族住在一座贵族式的豪宅里。
She has an aristocratical air about her. 她身上有一种贵族气质。
近义词：noble, aristocratic, highborn, patrician, blue-blooded
反义词：plebeian, common, working-class
词性：adjective

词源：
From French *aristocratique*, from Greek *aristokratikos*, from *aristokratia* "rule of the best."
