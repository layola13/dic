desirably:/dɪˈzaɪərəbli/
adverb
理想地，合意地
in a way that is desired or considered to be good or pleasant
It is desirably located near the town centre. 它理想地坐落在靠近市中心的位置。
The property is desirably situated close to schools and shops. 这处房产位置优越，靠近学校和商店。

Synonyms: preferably, ideally, advantageously, appropriately
Antonyms: undesirably, unsuitably, inappropriately
词性: 副词
