cadets:/kəˈdɛts/| n. |学员，学生军官|A person, especially a young person, who is training to become an officer in the armed forces or police.|The army cadets were marching in formation. 陆军学员们正在列队行进。|Example: The naval cadets spent the summer on a training ship.  海军学员们在训练舰上度过了夏天。|Synonyms: trainee, student, recruit|Antonyms: veteran, graduate, expert|Noun

cadet:/kəˈdɛts/| n. |（尤指年轻的）见习生，实习生|A young person who is training for a particular job or profession, especially in business or industry.|The company takes on a number of engineering cadets each year.  这家公司每年都会招收一些工程学徒。|Example: She started her career as a cadet journalist on a local newspaper.  她以地方报纸的实习记者开始了她的职业生涯。|Synonyms: trainee, apprentice, intern|Antonyms: professional, expert, veteran|Noun
