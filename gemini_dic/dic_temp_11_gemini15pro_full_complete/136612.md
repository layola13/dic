nonsatires:/nɒnˈsætaɪəz/
noun
非讽刺作品
works that are not satires, lacking the characteristics of satire such as irony, sarcasm, or ridicule.
The library's collection includes numerous nonsatires alongside its satirical works.  图书馆的馆藏包括大量非讽刺作品以及讽刺作品。
His recent publications are mostly nonsatires, focusing on realistic portrayals of everyday life.  他最近的出版物大多是非讽刺作品，侧重于对日常生活的现实描绘。
Synonyms:  non-satirical works, serious works, earnest works
Antonyms: satires, lampoons, parodies
