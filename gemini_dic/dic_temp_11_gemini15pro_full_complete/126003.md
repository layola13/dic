muslinet:/ˈmʌzlɪnɛt/| n.|薄纱，细棉布|A fine, thin cotton fabric, often used for curtains or clothing.|She wore a dress of delicate muslinet. 她穿着一件精致的薄纱连衣裙。|近义词: gauze, voile, chiffon|反义词: canvas, denim|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mousselinette*, diminutive of *mousseline* ("muslin"),  from Italian *mussolina*,  from *Mosul* (a city in Iraq where the fabric was originally made).
