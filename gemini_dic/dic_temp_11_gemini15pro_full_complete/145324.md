overbrowned: /ˌoʊvərˈbraʊnd/
adjective
烤焦的，烤得过久的
cooked for too long until excessively brown
The cookies were overbrowned and tasted a bit burnt.  饼干烤焦了，尝起来有点糊味。
The toast was slightly overbrowned, but still edible.  吐司有点烤焦了，但还能吃。
近义词: burnt, scorched, overcooked
反义词: undercooked, underdone
词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "brown" (verb/adjective)

"over-" 表示"过度"、"超过"。
"brown" 作动词表示"变成棕色"，作形容词表示"棕色的"。
