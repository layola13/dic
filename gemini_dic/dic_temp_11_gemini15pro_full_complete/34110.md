一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want for lunch? 她午餐想吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching? 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用那笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? 你一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm yesterday? 昨天晚上8点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 开会时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？

What will you do this weekend? 你这周末要做什么？
