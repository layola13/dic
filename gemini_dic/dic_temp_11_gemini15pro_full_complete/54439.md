disingenuity:/ˌdɪsɪndʒəˈnjuːɪti/
n.|不真诚，不坦率，狡猾|Lack of honesty or sincerity; insincerity.|Her disingenuity was obvious when she pretended not to know about the surprise party.|近义词: dishonesty, deceit, duplicity, guile, cunning|反义词: honesty, sincerity, candor, frankness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *disingenuitas, from disingenuus "insincere," from dis- "not" + ingenuus "frank, open."
