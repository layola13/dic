nonmenial:/ˌnɒnˈmiːniəl/
adjective
非体力劳动的；脑力劳动的
Not menial or requiring little skill; not servile.  Relating to or denoting work that involves the use of the mind rather than physical effort.
He took a nonmenial job after graduating from college. 他大学毕业后从事了一份非体力劳动的工作。
近义词: professional, skilled, white-collar, intellectual
反义词: menial, unskilled, blue-collar, manual
词性: adjective
