follow-through:/ˈfɒləʊ θruː/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊ θruː/ (US)| n.|后续行动，完成；(击球后的)随挥动作|The project failed due to a lack of follow-through.  这个项目由于缺乏后续行动而失败了。|例句：His follow-through on the golf swing was perfect. 他高尔夫挥杆的随挥动作很完美。|近义词：completion, execution, follow-up|反义词：inaction, neglect|词性:名词

follow-through:/ˈfɒləʊ θruː/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊ θruː/ (US)| v.|完成，贯彻到底|He promised to help, but he didn't follow through. 他答应帮忙，但没有做到。|例句：She needs to follow through on her promises. 她需要兑现她的承诺。|近义词：complete, fulfill, execute|反义词：abandon, quit|词性:动词
