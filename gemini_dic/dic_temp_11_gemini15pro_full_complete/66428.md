exegete:/ˈɛksɪdʒiːt/| n.|注释者，解释者|One who explains or interprets, especially a biblical text.|The renowned exegete offered a compelling interpretation of the ancient scripture.  这位著名的注释者对古代经文作出了令人信服的解释。|近义词：interpreter, commentator, analyst, expounder|反义词:misinterpreter, distorter|noun

exegete:/ˈɛksɪdʒiːt/| v.|解释，注释|To interpret or explain, especially a biblical text.|He exegeted the passage in detail, revealing its hidden meanings. 他详细解释了这段文字，揭示了其隐藏的含义。|近义词：interpret, explain, expound, analyze, comment on|反义词:misinterpret, distort|verb
