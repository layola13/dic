midtone:/ˈmɪdˌtoʊn/| n. | 中音; 中间色调 |A tone, color, or shade that is neither very light nor very dark; intermediate between extremes.|The artist used a variety of midtones to create a sense of depth. 艺术家使用了各种中间色调来营造一种深度感。|Synonyms: neutral, intermediate|Antonyms: highlight, shadow|noun
