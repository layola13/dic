over-fixes:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪksɪz/
verb
过度修复；矫枉过正
The mechanic over-fixed my car and now it's worse than before.  机械师过度修理了我的车，现在比以前更糟糕了。
He over-fixes his mistakes and ends up creating more problems. 他过度纠正自己的错误，最终制造了更多问题。
Synonym: overcompensate, overdo, over-repair
Antonym: under-fix, neglect
