isobars:/ˈaɪsəʊbɑːz/ /ˈaɪsəbɑːrz/| n.|等压线|Lines on a weather map connecting points having the same atmospheric pressure.|Isobars on the map showed a large area of high pressure.|Synonyms: contour lines (of equal pressure)|Antonyms: None|Noun

isobar:/ˈaɪsəʊbɑːr/ /ˈaɪsəbɑːr/| n.|同量异位素|One of two or more atoms with the same atomic weight but different atomic numbers.|Carbon-14 and nitrogen-14 are isobars.|Synonyms: nuclide|Antonyms: None|Noun
