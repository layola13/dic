nestier:/ˈnɛstiər/ | adj. | 更爱筑巢的，更恋家的 | More inclined to build or stay in a nest; more home-loving. | The nestier robins began building earlier in the spring.  更恋家的知更鸟在春天更早开始筑巢。 |  近义词：domestic, homebody | 反义词: adventurous, outgoing | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "nest" + "-ier" (comparative suffix). 源自“nest（巢）” + “-ier（比较级后缀）”。
