antibacterial:/ˌæntibaækˈtɪəriəl/
adjective
抗菌的；抗菌性的
Destroying or inhibiting the growth of bacteria.
This handwash contains antibacterial agents.  这种洗手液含有抗菌剂。
近义词:antimicrobial, antiseptic
反义词:bacteriogenic


antibacterial:/ˌæntibaækˈtɪəriəl/
noun
抗菌剂；抗菌药
A substance that destroys or inhibits the growth of bacteria.
Many common household cleaners contain antibacterials. 许多常见的家用清洁剂都含有抗菌剂。
近义词:antibiotic, disinfectant
反义词:None


词性: 可以做形容词和名词
