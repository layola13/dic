finally:/ˈfaɪnəli/| adv.|最后，终于|After a long wait, we finally received our visas.  漫长的等待之后，我们终于收到了签证。|例句：Finally, the rain stopped. 最后，雨停了。|近义词：eventually, ultimately, at last|反义词:initially, firstly|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *finalie, from Old French *finalment, from Late Latin fīnāliter, from Latin fīnālis (“of or belonging to the end”) + -iter (adverbial suffix). Equivalent to final +‎ -ly.
