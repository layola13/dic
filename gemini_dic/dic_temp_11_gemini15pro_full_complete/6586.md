anacanths: /ˈænəkænθs/
n.
棘鱼类；棘鳍总目鱼类
Acanthopterygii, or the ray-finned fishes.  A large group of bony fishes characterized by having the anterior rays of their dorsal and anal fins stiff and sharp, forming spines.
Anacanths are a group of fish known for their spiny fins. 棘鱼类是一组以棘鳍而闻名的鱼类。
Fossil anacanths have been found in ancient lakebeds.  在古老的湖床中发现了棘鱼类化石。
近义词: spiny-finned fishes, acanthopterygians
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps soft-rayed fishes as a contrasting group)
词性: noun
