bristling:/ˈbrɪslɪŋ/| adj.|竖立的，发怒的，充满的|Covered with or characterized by bristles; showing anger or indignation; filled with something specified.|例句：The cat, bristling with anger, arched its back. 那只猫怒气冲冲，弓起了背。|近义词：prickly, spiky, indignant, furious|反义词:calm, smooth, peaceful|词性:形容词
bristle:/ˈbrɪsl/| n. vi.|短而硬的毛，发怒，竖起|A short, stiff hair; to react in an angry or offended manner; to stand upright.|例句：The bristles on the brush are made of nylon. 刷子上的毛是用尼龙做的。|近义词：hair, fiber, bristle (v.)|反义词:smooth|词性:名词,动词
