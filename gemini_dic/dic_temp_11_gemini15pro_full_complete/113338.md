luxuriantly:/lʌɡˈʒʊəriəntli/
adverb
繁茂地，茂盛地；丰富地；奢华地
Growing or occurring in abundance;  lush;  profuse;  ostentatiously rich.
The vines grew luxuriantly, covering the entire wall. 葡萄藤蔓生长茂盛，覆盖了整面墙。
Synonyms: profusely, abundantly, copiously, lavishly, richly
Antonyms: sparsely, meagerly, poorly, simply
词性: 副词

luxuriant:/lʌɡˈʒʊəriənt/
adjective
繁茂的，茂盛的；丰富的；奢华的
Growing or occurring in abundance; lush; profuse; ostentatiously rich.
The jungle had luxuriant vegetation.  丛林里有茂密的植被。
Synonyms: lush, abundant, copious, lavish, rich
Antonyms: sparse, meager, poor, simple
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Latin *lūxuriāns*, present participle of *lūxuriāre* (“to be luxuriant, to abound”), from *lūxuria* (“luxury, abundance”).
