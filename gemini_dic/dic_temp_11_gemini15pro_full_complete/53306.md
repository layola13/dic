disability:/ˌdɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 伤残，残疾；无力；无资格 |A physical or mental condition that limits a person's movements, senses, or activities.|  She has a learning disability. 她有学习障碍。 | 近义词：impairment, handicap, incapacity | 反义词：ability, capability | 词性: 名词

disability:/ˌdɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 伤残，残疾；无力；无资格 | The state of being unable to do something because of a physical or mental problem. | He has a disability that prevents him from walking.  他因残疾而无法行走。| 近义词：infirmity, ailment | 反义词：health, fitness | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: disabilite, from Old French desabilité, from des- (expressing negation) + able + -ity.
