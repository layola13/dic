dandelion: /ˈdændɪlaɪən/ (US) /ˈdændɪlaɪən/ (UK)| n. |蒲公英|A common wild plant with a bright yellow flower that turns into a soft white ball of seeds which are easily blown away by the wind. |The field was covered in dandelions.|近义词：blowball, clocks|反义词: N/A|名词

dandelion: /ˈdændɪlaɪən/ (US) /ˈdændɪlaɪən/ (UK)| adj. |蒲公英似的，像蒲公英的 (较少用)|Resembling a dandelion, especially in color or shape.  |She wore a dandelion-yellow dress.|近义词：yellow, golden|反义词: N/A|形容词
