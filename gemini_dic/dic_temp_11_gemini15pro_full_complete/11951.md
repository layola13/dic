art's:/ɑːrts/ | n. |艺术的；艺术家的；艺术作品的|The possessive form of "art," indicating ownership or association.  It means "belonging to art" or "of art." |例句：The art's impact on society is profound.艺术对社会的影响是深远的。

例句：The gallery showcased the art's beauty. 画廊展示了艺术的美。|近义词：artistic, creative |反义词:inartistic, uncreative |词性:possessive adjective/pronoun


Etymology:
From Middle English *artes, from Old French arts, plural of art, from Latin artem, accusative singular of ars (“art, skill, craft”). The possessive form developed naturally in English along with other possessive forms.
