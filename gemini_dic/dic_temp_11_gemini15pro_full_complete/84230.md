half-seriously:/ˌhæf ˈsɪəriəsli/
|adv.|半开玩笑地，半认真地|Partly seriously and partly not seriously; not entirely seriously. |He said half-seriously that he might retire next year. 他半开玩笑地说他明年可能退休。|She half-seriously suggested they elope. 她半开玩笑地建议他们私奔。|
|近义词: partly seriously, not entirely seriously, jokingly, lightly|反义词: seriously, whole-heartedly, earnestly|词性: 副词
