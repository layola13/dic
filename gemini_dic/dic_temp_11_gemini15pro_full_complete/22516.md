boominess:/ˈbuːminəs/| n.|低沉的轰鸣声；嗡嗡声；(声音)浑浊|A quality of sound reproduction characterized by excessive emphasis of low frequencies, resulting in a muddy or indistinct sound.|The boominess of the bass made it difficult to hear the other instruments. 低音的轰鸣声使其他乐器难以被听到。|近义词：resonance, rumble, thump|反义词：clarity, sharpness|词性:名词

Etymology:  From "booming" + "-ness". "Boom" itself comes from Middle Dutch *bōmen, imitative.
