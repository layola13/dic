factualness:/ˈfæktʃuəlˌnɪs/
n.
事实性；真实性
The factualness of the report was questioned.  这份报告的真实性受到了质疑。
The historian emphasized the factualness of the events described.  历史学家强调了所描述事件的真实性。
近义词:truthfulness, accuracy, verity, authenticity
反义词:falseness, inaccuracy, fiction, fabrication
词性:noun

factualnesses:/ˈfæktʃuəlˌnɪsnɪsɪz/
n. (plural)
事实性；真实性（复数）
Rarely used.  很少使用。
The various factualnesses of the different accounts made it difficult to determine what truly happened.  不同描述的各种真实性使得难以确定真正发生了什么。  (This example is contrived, as the singular form "factualness" would be more natural.)
近义词:truthfulnesses, accuracies, verities, authenticities (These plural forms are also rare.)
反义词:falsenesses, inaccuracies, fictions, fabrications (These plural forms are also rare.)
词性:noun
