co-sovereignty:/koʊˈsɒvrɪnti/| n. |共同主权，共治|The sharing of governmental power over a single territory by two or more entities.|The agreement established co-sovereignty over the island. 该协议确立了对该岛的共同主权。|Synonyms: joint sovereignty, shared sovereignty|Antonyms: sole sovereignty, absolute sovereignty|noun


co-sovereign:/koʊˈsɒvrɪn/| adj. |共同主权的，共治的|Sharing governmental power over a single territory.|The two countries are co-sovereign over the disputed territory. 这两个国家对有争议的领土拥有共同主权。|Synonyms: jointly sovereign, shared sovereign|Antonyms: solely sovereign, absolutely sovereign|adjective
