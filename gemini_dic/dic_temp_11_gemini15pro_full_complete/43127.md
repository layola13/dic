coyishnesses:/ˈkɔɪɪʃnɪsɪz/
n.
害羞，腼腆(复数)
The quality of being coy, especially in a playful or affected way; a deliberate attempt to appear shy or modest.
Her coyishnesses were clearly designed to attract attention. 她故作害羞的样子显然是为了吸引别人的注意。
近义词：shyness, bashfulness, modesty, demureness, coquetry
反义词：boldness, brashness, confidence, assertiveness
词性: noun (plural)


coyishness:/ˈkɔɪɪʃnɪs/
n.
害羞，腼腆
The quality of being coy, especially in a playful or affected way; a deliberate attempt to appear shy or modest.
She showed a certain coyishness when asked about her boyfriend. 当被问及她的男朋友时，她表现出某种害羞。
近义词：shyness, bashfulness, modesty, demureness, coquetry
反义词：boldness, brashness, confidence, assertiveness
词性: noun

coy:/kɔɪ/
adj.
害羞的，腼腆的；忸怩作态的
Making a pretense of shyness or modesty that is intended to be alluring.
She gave him a coy smile. 她给了他一个害羞的微笑。
近义词：shy, bashful, modest, demure, coquettish
反义词：bold, brash, confident, assertive
词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *coi, from Old French coi (“quiet, still”), from Latin quietus.
