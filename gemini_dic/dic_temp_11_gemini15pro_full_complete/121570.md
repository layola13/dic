misnavigating:/ˌmɪsnævɪɡeɪtɪŋ/
|v.|错误导航，导航错误|Navigating incorrectly, leading to a wrong destination or path.|
He admitted misnavigating and ending up in the wrong city. 他承认导航错误，结果到了错误的城市。

The ship captain was accused of misnavigating and causing the accident.  船长被指控航行错误导致事故发生。
|近义词：straying, going astray, losing one's way, taking a wrong turn|反义词：navigating correctly, following the right path|词性: 动词(present participle)
