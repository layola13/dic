bipunctual:/baɪˈpʌŋktʃuəl/
adjective
有两个点的
Having two points or dots.
The bipunctual ladybug has two distinct spots on its back. 这只双点瓢虫背上有两个明显的斑点。

近义词: two-spotted, double-pointed
反义词: unspotted, single-pointed
词性: adjective
