discourage:/dɪˈskɜːrɪdʒ/ /dɪˈskʌrɪdʒ/| vt.|使气馁，使沮丧，劝阻|To cause (someone) to lose confidence or enthusiasm; to try to prevent something|The coach's harsh words discouraged the young player. 教练严厉的话使这位年轻球员感到气馁。

The heavy rain discouraged us from going out.  大雨阻止了我们外出。|近义词：deter, dissuade, demoralize|反义词: encourage, hearten, inspire|词性:动词
