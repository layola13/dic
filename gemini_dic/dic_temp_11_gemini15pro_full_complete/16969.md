bastardises:/ˈbæstərdaɪzɪz/
verb
使杂化；使品质变坏；窜改；歪曲
corrupts, debased, or makes impure, especially by mixing with something that is considered inferior.
The movie bastardised the classic novel. 这部电影歪曲了这部经典小说。
synonyms:debase, corrupt, contaminate, adulterate, defile, spoil, pervert, warp, distort, twist, misrepresent, falsify
antonyms:purify, refine, improve, enhance
词性:v.
