earlily:/ˈɜːrlili/ | adv. | 早期地; 过早地 | In a time relatively early. | They arrived earlily for the meeting. 他们很早就到了会议现场。 |  The family earlily recognized the child's talent.  这家人很早就发现了这个孩子的天赋。 | 近义词: prematurely, early | 反义词: late, belatedly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "early" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Early" comes from Old English "ærlice," meaning "early."
