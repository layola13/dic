grampus:/ˈɡræmpəs/| n.|逆戟鲸，虎鲸；海豚（旧称）|A toothed whale of the dolphin family, with a rounded snout and a tall dorsal fin. It is typically black and white. |例句：The grampus surfaced, its blowhole spouting a plume of water. 逆戟鲸浮出水面，它的气孔喷出一股水柱。

例句：Sailors used to call orcas grampuses.  水手过去常常把逆戟鲸叫做海豚。|近义词：orca, killer whale, dolphin (archaic)|反义词:|词性:noun
