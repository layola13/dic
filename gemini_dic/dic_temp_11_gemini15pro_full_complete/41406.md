corneas:/ˈkɔːrniəz/
n.
角膜（复数）
The transparent outer covering of the eye that refracts light and protects the eye.  眼睛透明的外层，用于折射光线并保护眼睛。
The surgeon transplanted the corneas.  外科医生移植了角膜。
