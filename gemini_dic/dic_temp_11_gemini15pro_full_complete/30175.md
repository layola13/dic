celection: /sɪˈlɛkʃən/
n.|挑选, 选择, 精选集|The process of carefully choosing something from a group of things; a group of things that have been carefully chosen. |She has a wide selection of clothes in her wardrobe. 她的衣柜里有各种各样的衣服。|selection, choice, option|elimination, rejection|noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *selectionem, from Latin selectus (past participle of seligere "to choose apart"), from se- "apart" + legere "to choose, gather, read".
