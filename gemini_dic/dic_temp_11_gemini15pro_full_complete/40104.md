contango:/kɒnˈtæŋɡəʊ/ (UK) /kənˈtæŋɡoʊ/ (US)| n. |期货溢价（现货价格低于期货价格）|A situation in the futures market where the price of a commodity for future delivery is higher than the spot price. This typically occurs when there are costs associated with holding the physical commodity, such as storage or financing costs.|Example 1: The contango in the oil market reflects the high cost of storing crude oil. 石油市场的期货溢价反映了原油的高储存成本。

Example 2:  Investors can profit from contango by buying the commodity at the spot price and selling futures contracts. 投资者可以通过以现货价格购买商品并出售期货合约来从期货溢价中获利。|近义词: premium|反义词: backwardation, normal backwardation|词性:noun
