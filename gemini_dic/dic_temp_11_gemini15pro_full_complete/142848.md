out-gloom: /ˌaʊtˈɡluːm/
verb
使更显阴郁；使黯然失色
to make something seem gloomy or dark by comparison
The dark clouds out-gloomed the already gray landscape.  乌云使原本灰色的风景更加阴郁。
The setting sun out-gloomed the vibrant colors of the flowers. 落日使鲜艳的花朵黯然失色。
Synonyms: overshadow, eclipse, darken
Antonyms: brighten, illuminate, highlight
词性: 动词
