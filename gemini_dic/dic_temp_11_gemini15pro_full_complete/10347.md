appeasing:/əˈpiːzɪŋ/
adjective
安抚的，抚慰的
pacifying or conciliatory.
例句：His appeasing manner calmed the angry customer. 他的安抚态度让愤怒的顾客平静了下来。

例句：The government's appeasing policy towards the aggressors proved to be a mistake. 政府对侵略者的绥靖政策被证明是一个错误。

近义词：conciliatory, placatory, soothing, mollifying, pacifying
反义词：provoking, aggravating, antagonizing, inciting

词性: adjective
