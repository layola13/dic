overfertility:/ˌoʊvər.fərˈtɪl.ə.ti/ | n. | 过度生育，生育力过剩 | The state of being excessively fertile, often leading to overpopulation.  It refers to a situation where a population produces more offspring than can be sustainably supported by the available resources. |  Overfertility in some regions contributes to poverty and environmental degradation. 一些地区的过度生育导致了贫困和环境恶化。| synonyms: hyperfertility, excessive fertility | antonyms: infertility, subfertility | noun
