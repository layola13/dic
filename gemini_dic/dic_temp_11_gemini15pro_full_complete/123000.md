monarchical:/məˈnɑːrkɪkəl/
adjective
君主制的；君主政体的
Of or relating to a monarch or monarchy. Characterized by or favoring monarchy.
例句：The United Kingdom is a constitutional monarchical state. 英国是一个君主立宪制国家。

例句：He holds monarchical views on government. 他对政府持有君主制观点。

synonym: regal, royal, kingly, queenly, sovereign, imperial
antonym: republican, democratic, egalitarian
词性: adjective
