outdesigning:/ˌaʊtdɪˈzaɪnɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的设计; 设计上更胜一筹
surpassing in design; designing something superior to another design
The new iPhone is outdesigning all its competitors. 新款iPhone在设计上胜过所有竞争对手。
近义词: outperforming, surpassing, excelling
反义词: underdesigning, lagging behind

词性: Verb (动词)
英文解析: Present participle of the verb "outdesign" which means to surpass or excel in design; to design something that is superior to another design in terms of aesthetics, functionality, or innovation.
中文翻译: (动词现在分词)设计上胜过;在设计上更胜一筹
