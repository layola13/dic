darnedest:/ˈdɑːrnɪdɪst/| adj.|最奇怪的；最出乎意料的；最令人惊奇的|An intensifier used with adjectives like strangest, oddest, funniest, etc. It emphasizes the surprising or unusual nature of something.  It originates from "darned," a euphemism for "damned," combined with "-est" (superlative suffix).|That's the darnedest thing I've ever heard!  那是我听过的最奇怪的事情！

He had the darnedest excuse for being late. 他编了个最离谱的迟到理由。|近义词：strangest, oddest, funniest, craziest|反义词:normalest, usualest (though these are not commonly used)|词性:adjective
