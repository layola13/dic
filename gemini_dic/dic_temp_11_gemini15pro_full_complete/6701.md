analyze:/ˈænəˌlaɪz/ | v. |分析，解析|To examine something methodically and in detail, typically in order to explain and interpret it.|例句：We need to analyze the data before we can draw any conclusions. 我们需要先分析数据才能得出任何结论。

例句：The scientist analyzed the chemical composition of the rock. 科学家分析了岩石的化学成分。|近义词：examine, investigate, study, scrutinize, assess, evaluate|反义词：synthesize, combine, ignore|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *analysare, from Ancient Greek ἀναλύειν (analúein, “to undo, resolve”), from ἀνά (aná, “up”) + λύειν (lúein, “to loosen”).
