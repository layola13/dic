dopey:/ˈdəʊpi/| adj.|昏昏沉沉的；愚蠢的；迟钝的|Slow-witted, sluggish, or sleepy, often due to the effects of drugs or alcohol.  Suggests a mild and harmless form of stupidity.|She felt dopey after the long flight.  长途飞行后她感到昏昏沉沉的。|Synonyms: drowsy, groggy, foolish, silly, dazed, stupefied|Antonyms: alert, intelligent, sharp, clever, bright|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *doop* (meaning "sauce, dipping"), related to *doopen* (meaning "to dip, baptize").  The original sense referred to a thick sauce or gravy. The meaning shifted to "stupid" in the early 20th century, possibly influenced by the slang use of "dope" to refer to drugs.
