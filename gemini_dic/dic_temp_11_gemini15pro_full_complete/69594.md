felucca:/fəˈluːkə/ |n.|三角帆船|A type of small boat with a triangular sail, used in the eastern Mediterranean.|The felucca glided across the water. 三角帆船滑过水面。|近义词：lateen-rigged boat, sailboat|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *feluca*, from Arabic *fulk* (فُلك), meaning "ship." Ultimately from Ancient Greek *phólos* (φόλος), meaning  "ship, warship."
