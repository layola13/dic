nutant:/ˈnjuːtənt/ | adj. | 下垂的，低垂的 | Drooping, hanging downward, typically of a plant part such as a flower or leaf. | The plant's nutant flowers were a delicate shade of pink.  这种植物下垂的花朵是淡粉色的。|  The snowdrops stood with nutant heads. 雪花莲低垂着头。 | synonyms: drooping, pendulous, nodding, cernuous | antonyms: erect, upright | adjective
