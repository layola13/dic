glares:/ɡlɛərz/| n. |怒视，瞪眼；刺眼的光|A glare is a long, angry look.  Glares can also refer to strong, unpleasant lights.|She gave him a furious glare. 她狠狠地瞪了他一眼。

The sun's glare reflected off the water. 阳光的刺眼光芒反射在水面上。|近义词：scowls, frowns, glares (v.), stares,  intense light, bright light|反义词: glances, glimpses, dim light|词性:noun


glares:/ɡlɛərz/| v. |怒视，瞪眼；发出刺眼的光|To glare is to look at someone angrily for a long time. It can also mean to shine with a strong, unpleasant light.|He glared at her across the table. 他隔着桌子怒视着她。
