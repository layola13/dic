fifteenths:/fɪfˈtiːnθs/
noun
十五分之一
One of fifteen equal parts of a whole.
He ate two fifteenths of the pizza. 他吃了披萨的十五分之二。
近义词: one fifteenth (singular)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

fifteenths:/fɪfˈtiːnθs/
adjective
第十五的
Relating to the position in a sequence of fifteen.
This is the fifteenth time I've told you! 这是我第十五次告诉你了!
近义词: fifteenth (when referring to a single item in a sequence)
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective
