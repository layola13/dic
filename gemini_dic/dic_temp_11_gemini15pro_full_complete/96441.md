indistinguished:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt/

平凡的，不杰出的，无特色的
Lacking distinction; not easily noticed or recognized; unremarkable.
His career was long but indistinguished.  他的职业生涯很长，但并不杰出。
