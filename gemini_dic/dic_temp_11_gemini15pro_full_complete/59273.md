eagerly:/ˈiːɡərli/| adv.|热切地，渴望地|In a way that shows great enthusiasm and impatience.|She eagerly awaited his arrival. 她热切地等待着他的到来。|Example: The children eagerly opened their presents. 孩子们热切地打开了他们的礼物。|Synonyms: enthusiastically, keenly, avidly|Antonyms: apathetically, indifferently|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *egerly, from eger (“eager”) + -ly, from Old English -līce (“-ly”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *agiz (“sharp, pointed”).
