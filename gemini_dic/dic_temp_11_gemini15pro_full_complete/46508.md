daisied:/ˈdeɪzid/| adj.|雏菊花似的，饰以雏菊的|Covered or decorated with daisies.|The field was daisied and bright. 田野里开满了雏菊，一片光明。|近义词：flowery, floral|反义词：bare, barren|词性:形容词

词源: From daisy + -ed.  Ultimately from Old English dægesēage "day's eye".
