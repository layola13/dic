kingfish:/ˈkɪŋˌfɪʃ/| n.| 黄鰤/石首鱼/大眼鰤/无鳔石首鱼 |A large marine food and game fish found in warm seas. It is known for its firm, white flesh.|We caught a huge kingfish on our fishing trip. 我们在钓鱼之旅中捕获了一条巨大的黄鰤。|近义词：mackerel, tuna|反义词: None|noun


kingfish:/ˈkɪŋˌfɪʃ/| n.| 重要人物/领袖/大亨|A person of considerable power or influence in a particular field.|He's the kingfish of the local political scene. 他是当地政坛的要人。|近义词：leader, boss, magnate|反义词:follower, subordinate|noun
