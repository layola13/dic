advisement:/ədˈvaɪzmənt/| n.| 劝告，忠告，建议；考虑，审议|The matter is under advisement.  这件事正在审议中。|例句：The case is now under advisement.  这个案件现在正在审议中。

例句：After careful advisement, he decided to accept the offer.  经过仔细考虑后，他决定接受这个提议。|近义词：advice, counsel, deliberation, consideration|反义词:inattention, neglect|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *avisement, *avysement, from Anglo-Norman avisement, from aviser (“to advise”) + -ment.
