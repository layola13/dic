confuse:/kənˈfjuːz/ (US) /kənˈfjuːz/ (UK)| vt.|混淆，弄错|To think wrongly that one thing is another.|I always confuse her with her sister. 我总是把她和她姐姐弄混。|synonyms: mix up, mistake|antonyms: distinguish, differentiate|verb
