decanter:/dɪˈkæntər/
noun
醒酒器
A decorative glass bottle used to serve wine or other alcoholic beverages, typically after they have been decanted (poured from another container to remove sediment).
He poured the wine into a crystal decanter. 他把酒倒进一个水晶醒酒器里。
近义词:carafe, bottle
反义词:None
词性:Noun
