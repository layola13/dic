metagrammatism:/ˌmɛtəˈɡræmətɪzəm/
|n.|换语症，语法错乱|A speech disorder characterized by the inappropriate substitution of one grammatical form for another (e.g., singular for plural, present for past tense).|The patient's metagrammatism made it difficult to understand what he was trying to say. 病人的换语症使人们很难理解他想表达的意思。|Metagrammatism can be a symptom of certain neurological disorders.  换语症可能是某些神经系统疾病的症状。|近义词:paragrammatism, grammatical error, dysgrammatism|反义词:grammatical correctness|词性:noun
