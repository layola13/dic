foggiest:/ˈfɒɡiɪst/ (UK) /ˈfɑːɡiɪst/ (US)| adj.|最模糊的，最不清楚的|Superlative adjective of foggy.  Meaning extremely unclear, vague, or hazy, often referring to understanding or memory.|I haven't the foggiest idea what you're talking about. 我一点儿也不知道你在说什么。|Synonyms: vaguest, haziest, murkiest, unclear, obscure|Antonyms: clearest, sharpest, distinct, obvious|adjective
