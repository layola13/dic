multitudinosity:/ˌmʌltɪtjuːdɪˈnɒsɪti/ /ˌmʌltɪtuːdɪˈnɑːsɪti/| n. |众多，大量，群众众多|The state or quality of being multitudinous; vastness of number.|The multitudinosity of stars in the night sky is awe-inspiring. 夜空中繁星的众多令人敬畏。|Near synonyms: multitude, abundance, profusion, numerousness, |Antonyms: scarcity, paucity, fewness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *multitudinōsitās, from Latin multitūdō (“multitude”) + -ōsitās (“-osity”).  Equivalent to multitude +‎ -osity.
