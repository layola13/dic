outdrove:/aʊtˈdroʊv/
verb
胜过，在驾驶方面超过
drove someone out of something by driving better than them.
He outdrove all the other competitors in the race and won easily. 他在比赛中胜过所有其他选手，轻松获胜。
Synonyms: surpassed, excelled, beat, triumphed over
Antonyms: lost to, was defeated by
