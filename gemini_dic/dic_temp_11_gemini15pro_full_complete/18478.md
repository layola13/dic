benched:/bɛntʃt/| v. adj.|使坐冷板凳，被搁置，下放到替补席|To be removed from a starting position in a team, usually in sports, or to be kept out of action or participation in something.|例句：The coach benched him for the rest of the game. 教练让他在剩下的比赛中坐冷板凳。|近义词：sideline, replace, substitute, remove|反义词:start, include, play|词性:动词/形容词
benched:/bɛntʃt/| adj.|被搁置的，被放在一边的|Kept out of action, participation, or consideration; set aside.|例句：The project has been benched due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目已被搁置。|近义词：shelved, suspended, postponed, inactive|反义词:active, ongoing, current|词性:形容词
