comitia:/kəˈmɪʃiə/ | n. |选举，投票| Elections or voting in ancient Rome. | The comitia were a crucial part of the Roman Republic.  选举是罗马共和国的重要组成部分。 |  Candidates would often campaign vigorously before the comitia. 候选人通常会在选举前积极进行竞选活动。 | Synonyms: election, vote, poll | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "appointment" or "selection") | Noun
