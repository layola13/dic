delicatessen:/ˌdɛlɪkəˈtɛsən/
|n.|熟食，美味佳肴|A prepared food, typically high-quality or considered a delicacy.|The delicatessen offered a wide variety of imported cheeses. 这家熟食店提供各种各样的进口奶酪。|synonyms: delicacy, gourmet food, fine food|antonyms: junk food, fast food
|noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Delikatessen*, plural of *Delikatesse*, from French *délicatesse* "delicacy", from *délicat* "delicate".
