brickleness:/ˈbrɪk(ə)lnəs/
noun
脆性；易碎性
The quality of being brittle; easily broken or cracked.
The brickleness of the material made it unsuitable for the project.  这种材料的脆性使其不适合该项目。

The archaeologist handled the ancient pottery with extreme care due to its brickleness. 考古学家由于古代陶器的脆性，小心翼翼地处理它。

近义词: fragility, brittleness, friability
反义词: toughness, resilience, durability
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From brittle + -ness.  Brittle comes from Old English *bryttian "to break," from Proto-Germanic *breutan, from Proto-Indo-European *bhreu- "to break, crack."
