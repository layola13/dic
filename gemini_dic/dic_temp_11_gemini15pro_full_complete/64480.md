erminois:/ˈɜːmɪnwɑː/ /ɜːrmɪnwɑː/
adjective
金黄色的;  饰有貂皮纹的
A tincture of gold strewn with black ermine spots.  一种散布着黑色貂皮斑点的金色。
The shield is erminois, a black cross flory. 盾牌是金黄色的，饰有一个黑色的花形十字架。
近义词:golden, yellow, aurous (referring to the color aspect)
反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for a heraldic tincture)
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *erminois, diminutive of ermine. Ultimately from Germanic *harminaz (whence also German Hermelin), of uncertain origin, possibly from a name of the Armenian rat (which is also the source of "Armenian").
