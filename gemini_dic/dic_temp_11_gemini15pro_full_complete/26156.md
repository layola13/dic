busying:/ˈbɪziɪŋ/| v. | 使忙碌，使奔走 |  keeping someone occupied or engaged in activity. |  He was busying himself with preparations for the party. 他正忙着准备派对。|  occupying, engaging |  idling, relaxing | verb
Etymology: From busy + -ing (present participle suffix).  The word "busy" comes from Old English *bisig.
