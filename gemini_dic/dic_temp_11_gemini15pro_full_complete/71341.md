flabellinerved:/ˌflæbɛlɪˈnɜːvd/
adjective
扇状脉的
Having veins that radiate from a common point, like a fan.
The flabellinerved leaves of the ginkgo tree are unique and easily recognizable.  银杏树的扇形脉叶子独特且易于识别。
The botanist identified the plant by its flabellinerved leaves.  植物学家通过扇形脉的叶子识别了这种植物。
近义词:fan-shaped, palmately veined
反义词:pinnately veined
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *flabellum* (fan) + -inerved (having nerves or veins).  源自拉丁语 *flabellum*（扇子）+ -inerved（有神经或脉络）。
