morale:/məˈrɑːl/ /məˈræl/ | n. |士气，斗志，精神面貌 |The state of the spirits of a person or group as exhibited by confidence, cheerfulness, discipline, and willingness to perform assigned tasks.  |The team's morale was low after they lost three games in a row. 球队连输三场后士气低落。|Synonyms: spirit, confidence, enthusiasm |Antonyms: despair, discouragement, apathy |noun
