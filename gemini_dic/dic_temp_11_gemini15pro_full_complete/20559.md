blackballer: /ˈblækˌbɔːlər/ | n. | 阻止他人成功的人；排球者 | A person who prevents someone else from achieving success, often through malicious actions or ostracism. |  He was accused of being a blackballer after he spread rumors about his competitor. 他被指控散布关于竞争对手的谣言，是一个排球者。 | Synonyms: saboteur, obstructor, underminer | Antonyms: supporter, advocate, helper | noun

blackball: /ˈblækˌbɔːl/ | v. | 阻止；排斥 | To prevent someone from joining a group or achieving success, often through malicious actions or ostracism. | The committee blackballed her application because of her past misconduct. 委员会因为她过去的不当行为而否决了她的申请。 | Synonyms: veto, reject, exclude | Antonyms: accept, approve, include | verb
