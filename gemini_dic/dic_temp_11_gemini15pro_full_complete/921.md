abuts:/əˈbʌts/
verb
毗邻；邻接；紧靠
borders upon; adjoins
例句：Their property abuts onto a nature reserve. 他们的房产紧邻一个自然保护区。

例句：The building abuts a busy street. 这栋建筑物毗邻一条繁忙的街道。

近义词：adjoins, borders, neighbors, touches
反义词：separates, detaches


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French abouter "to join end to end, to place end to end," from a- "to" + bout "end."
