effemination:/ɪˌfɛmɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|柔弱,女性化; 娘化|The process of making someone or something appear or behave more feminine; a loss of strength and masculinity.|His effemination was apparent in his delicate gestures and high-pitched voice. 他的女性化表现在他纤细的手势和尖细的嗓音上。|近义词：feminization, weakness, softness|反义词：masculinity, virility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin effeminātiōn- (stem of effeminātiō), from effēmināre ‘make effeminate’ + -tiōn- -tion.
