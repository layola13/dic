overfervently:/ˌōvərˈfɜːrvəntlɪ/
adverb
过度热情地
with excessive fervor; too fervently.
He praised her overfervently, making her uncomfortable. 他过度热情地赞扬她，让她感到不舒服。
Synonyms: excessively zealously, too passionately, overenthusiastically
Antonyms: apathetically, indifferently, halfheartedly
词性: 副词


overfervent:/ˌōvərˈfɜːrvənt/
adjective
过度热情的
excessively fervent; too fervent.
Her overfervent support for the cause alienated some people. 她对这项事业过度热情的支持疏远了一些人。
Synonyms: excessively zealous, too passionate, overenthusiastically
Antonyms: apathetically, indifferent, unenthusiastic
词性: 形容词


fervent:/ˈfɜːrvənt/
adjective
热情的；热烈的
having or displaying a passionate intensity.
He has a fervent belief in his religion. 他对自己的宗教有着热烈的信仰。
Synonyms: passionate, ardent, zealous
Antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic
词性: 形容词


词源:
From Late Latin *fervent-, from Latin fervere "to boil, glow."  The English word "fervent" arrived via Old French.  The prefix "over-" simply intensifies the meaning, indicating an excess of fervor.
