mawmish:/ˈmɔːmɪʃ/| adj.|感伤的，多愁善感的|Sentimental, often to an excessive degree. Characterized by or exhibiting mawkishness.  Evoking or expressing a sickly sentimentality. |She wrote a mawmish poem about the death of her cat. 她写了一首关于她猫的死亡的感伤的诗。| The film was criticized for its mawmish portrayal of love. 这部电影因其对爱情的感伤描写而受到批评。 |近义词：sentimental, cloying, saccharine, schmaltzy |反义词：unsentimental, cynical, hard-boiled|词性:形容词

词源:  From *maw*, an obsolete word meaning "stomach" + *-ish*. Originally referred to a sickly or nauseated feeling, later extended to overly sentimental feelings that could be considered similarly sickening.
