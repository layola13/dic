collectivized:/kəˈlɛktɪvaɪzd/| v. |集体化的，集体的|To organize (something) on the basis of collectivism; to bring under collective ownership or control.|The farms were collectivized by the government. 政府将农场集体化了。|Synonyms: communalized, nationalized, socialized|Antonyms: privatized, individualized|verb

collectivized:/kəˈlɛktɪvaɪzd/| adj. |集体化的，集体的|Organized on the basis of collectivism; owned or controlled collectively.|The collectivized farms struggled to meet production quotas. 集体化的农场难以达到生产配额。|Synonyms: communal, collective, joint|Antonyms: private, individual|adjective
