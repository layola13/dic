mobilities:/moʊˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.|移动性，流动性|The ability to move or be moved freely and easily.  The quality or state of being mobile.|例句：Increased social mobilities have led to a more diverse population in urban areas.  社会流动性的增加导致城市地区人口更加多样化。

例句：The new wheelchairs offer greater mobility for users. 新轮椅为使用者提供了更大的灵活性。|近义词：flexibility, adaptability, portability, maneuverability|反义词：immobility, rigidity, inflexibility|词性:名词
