decemlocular: /ˌdesəmˈlɒkjʊlə(r)/
adjective
十室的；有十个室的
Having ten locules (compartments within an organ such as an ovary or fruit).
The botanist identified a decemlocular ovary in the newly discovered plant species.  植物学家在新发现的植物物种中发现了一个十室的子房。
The decemlocular fruit contained numerous seeds.  这个十室的果实包含许多种子。
Near Synonyms: plurilocular, multilocular
Antonyms: unilocular, bilocular
Word Origin: From Latin *decem* (ten) + *locularis* (having compartments).
