contemporariness:/kənˌtɛmpəˈrɛrɪnɪs/| n. |当代性，同时代性|The state or quality of being contemporary; existence at the same time as another or others.|The exhibition skillfully captured the contemporariness of the artists' work.  这个展览巧妙地捕捉到了艺术家作品的当代性。|Synonyms:  modernity, currency, presentness, up-to-dateness|Antonyms:  antiquity, datedness, obsolescence, anachronism|noun
