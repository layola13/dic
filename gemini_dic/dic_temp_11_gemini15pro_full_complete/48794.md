deflector:/dɪˈflektər/ | n. |偏转器，导流板|Something that deflects, especially a plate or surface for deflecting air, liquid, particles, etc. |The car's deflectors help to reduce wind noise. 汽车的导流板有助于减少风噪声。| The airplane's wing deflectors aid in controlling lift. 飞机的机翼导流板有助于控制升力。 | synonyms: baffle, shield, guard, deflective device | antonyms: conductor, conduit | noun

deflectors:/dɪˈflektərz/ | n. |偏转器，导流板（复数）|Plural form of deflector. |The deflectors on the hood protect the windshield from insects. 引擎盖上的导流板保护挡风玻璃免受昆虫撞击。 |  The air conditioning deflectors direct the airflow. 空调导流板引导气流方向。| synonyms: baffles, shields, guards | antonyms: conductors, conduits | noun
