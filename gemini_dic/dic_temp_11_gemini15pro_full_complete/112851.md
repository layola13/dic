luckiness:/ˈlʌkɪnɪs/
n.
幸运；好运
The state of being lucky; good fortune.
He attributed his success to skill and hard work rather than luckiness.  他将自己的成功归因于技能和努力工作，而不是运气。
She felt a surge of luckiness when she found a $20 bill on the sidewalk.  她在人行道上发现一张 20 美元的钞票时感到一阵幸运。
近义词：fortune, good luck, serendipity
反义词：misfortune, bad luck, unluckiness
词性: 名词

词源：From lucky + -ness.  来自 lucky（幸运的）+ -ness（名词后缀，表示状态或性质）。
