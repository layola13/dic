avail-themselves-of:/əˈveɪl ðɛmˈsɛlvz ɒv/| v.|利用，使用|to make use of something; to take advantage of an opportunity|Many employees availed themselves of the company's training program. 许多员工都利用了公司的培训项目。|The students availed themselves of the library's resources. 学生们利用了图书馆的资源。|近义词：utilize, make use of, take advantage of|反义词: ignore, neglect|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avaler (to be of value, to profit), from Latin ad valere (to be worth).  The reflexive form "avail oneself of" emerged later, emphasizing the act of taking or seizing the opportunity or resource.
