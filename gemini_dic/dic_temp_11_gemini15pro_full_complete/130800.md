nonantagonistically:/ˌnɒnænˌtæɡəˈnɪstɪkli/
adverb
非对抗性地
in a way that is not antagonistic; cooperatively.  Not characterized by or resulting from antagonism or opposition.
The two groups worked together nonantagonistically to achieve a common goal.  这两个团体非对抗性地合作以实现共同目标。
The issue was resolved nonantagonistically through mediation.  这个问题通过调解以非对抗性的方式得到了解决。
Synonym: cooperatively, harmoniously, amicably, peacefully
Antonym: antagonistically, hostilely, aggressively, combatively
词性: 副词
