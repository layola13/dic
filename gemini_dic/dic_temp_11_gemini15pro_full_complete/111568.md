lobeline: /ˈloʊbəliːn/ | n. | 洛贝林 |  A crystalline alkaloid, C₂₂H₂₇NO₂, obtained from Indian tobacco (Lobelia inflata) and used as a respiratory stimulant.  It has similar effects to nicotine. |  Lobeline is sometimes used to help people quit smoking.  洛贝林有时被用来帮助人们戒烟。| synonyms:  none listed (pharmacological agents are rarely synonymous, they may have similar effects) | antonyms: respiratory depressant | noun
