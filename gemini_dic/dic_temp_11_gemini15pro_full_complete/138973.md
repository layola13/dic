nyctitropism: /nɪkˈtɪtrəˌpɪzəm/
n.
向夜性，趋暗性
The tendency of certain plants to turn their leaves or flowers towards darkness.
The nyctitropism of the prayer plant's leaves is fascinating to watch.  含羞草叶子的向夜性非常迷人。
