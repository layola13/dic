lachrymose: /ˈlækrɪməʊs/ (UK), /ˈlækrɪmoʊs/ (US) | adj. | 泪流满面的，催人泪下的 | Given to weeping; tearful; mournful. Characterized by or causing tears or weeping.  |  The film's lachrymose ending left the audience sobbing.  这部电影催人泪下的结局让观众哭泣。|  She delivered a lachrymose speech about the loss of her beloved pet. 她发表了一篇关于失去爱宠的催人泪下的演讲。 | 近义词: tearful, weepy, mournful, doleful | 反义词: cheerful, joyous, happy | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Late Latin *lacrimosus, from Latin lacrima (“tear”).
