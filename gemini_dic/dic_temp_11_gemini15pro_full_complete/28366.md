caravette:/ˌkærəˈvɛt/| n.|轻便小型旅行车，小型货车|A small, light van or caravan, especially one used for camping. | We rented a caravette for our camping trip. 我们租了一辆轻型旅行车去露营。|The family enjoyed their holiday traveling in their caravette. 这家人开着他们的轻型旅行车享受假期。|近义词：camper van, minivan, caravan|反义词：lorry, truck|词性：名词

词源：From French *caravette*, diminutive of *caravane*, from Italian *carovana*. Ultimately from Persian *kārvān*.
