fruitarian: /fruːˈteəriən/ | n. | 果食者；水果主义者 | A person who eats only fruit. |  She's a strict fruitarian and doesn't eat any vegetables, grains, or nuts. 她是一个严格的果食者，不吃任何蔬菜、谷物或坚果。

| Example:  My friend is a fruitarian and believes it's the healthiest way to eat. 我的朋友是一位果食者，并且相信这是最健康的饮食方式。


| Synonym:  vegan, vegetarian (partially) | Antonym:  carnivore, omnivore | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fruit + -arian (a suffix forming nouns and adjectives denoting adherence to a particular kind of diet or way of life).  Ultimately from Latin fructus "fruit, enjoyment, produce, profit."
