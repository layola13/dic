out-of-running:/ˌaʊt əv ˈrʌnɪŋ/
adjective
出局的，没有获胜机会的
No longer able to win or succeed in a competition or election.
After losing the primary, she was out of the running for the presidency.  在输掉初选后，她失去了竞选总统的资格。
The injury put him out of the running for the championship.  这次受伤使他失去了争夺冠军的机会。
out of contention, eliminated, disqualified
in the running, contending
adjective
