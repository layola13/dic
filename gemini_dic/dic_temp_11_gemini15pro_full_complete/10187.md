aporias:/əˈpɔːriəz/ /eɪˈpɔːriəz/
n.
疑惑；难题；僵局
An aporia is an expression of real or pretended doubt or uncertainty especially for rhetorical effect.  It can also refer to a logical impasse or paradox, a point at which a text or theory reaches an irresolvable internal contradiction or an impasse.
The philosopher explored the aporia of free will versus determinism. 哲学家探讨了自由意志与决定论的难题。
近义词：dilemma, puzzle, paradox, quandary, impasse
反义词：solution, answer, certainty
词性：名词
