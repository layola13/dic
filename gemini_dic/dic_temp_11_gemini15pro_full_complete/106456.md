kotow:/ˌkaʊˈtaʊ/ /kəˈtoʊ/ | n. vi.|叩头，磕头；卑躬屈膝；唯命是从|act of kneeling and touching the ground with the forehead in worship or submission; to behave in an excessively subservient manner|例句：
He refused to kotow to the emperor.
他拒绝向皇帝叩头。

The workers were expected to kotow to the boss.
工人们被期望对老板卑躬屈膝。

|近义词：bow, grovel, fawn, kowtow|反义词： defy, resist, disobey|词性: noun, verb
