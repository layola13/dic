idian:/ɪˈdiən/ | n. |习语，成语; 方言,土语|A group of words established by usage as having a meaning not deducible from those of the individual words (e.g. over the moon, see the light). A form of language peculiar to a particular region or group.| He used a common idian in his speech. 他在演讲中使用了一个常见的成语。|Synonym: expression, idiom, locution, phrase, saying | Antonym: standard language, formal language
词性: 名词
