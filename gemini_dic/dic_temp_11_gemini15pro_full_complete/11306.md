ardipithecus:/ˌɑːrdɪˈpɪθɪkəs/ | n. |始祖地猿，地猿属| An extinct primate genus, considered a very early hominin and potentially ancestral to Australopithecus.  It lived in Ethiopia about 4.4 million years ago. |  Ardipithecus ramidus is a crucial find for understanding human evolution.  始祖地猿拉米达种是了解人类进化过程中的一个关键发现。 |  Ardipithecus kadabba is an older species of Ardipithecus.  始祖地猿卡达巴种是更古老的地猿种类。| synonym: hominin, early human ancestor | antonym:  (no direct antonym, perhaps modern human - Homo sapiens) | noun
