aptitude:/ˈæptɪtjuːd/
n.
才能，天资，资质
A natural ability to do something.
She showed a great aptitude for learning languages. 她展现出学习语言的极佳天资。

aptitude:/ˈæptɪtjuːd/
n.
倾向，习性
A tendency to do something.
He had an aptitude for getting into trouble. 他有惹麻烦的倾向。

aptitude:/ˈæptɪtjuːd/
n.
适合性
Suitability or fitness.
The aptitude test measures your aptitude for the job. 适职测试衡量你对这份工作的适合性。


Etymology:
From Late Latin *aptitūdō, from Latin aptus (“fit, suitable”) + -itūdō, equivalent to apt + -itude.

Synonyms:
talent, gift, skill, ability, faculty, capacity, knack, flair, bent, inclination, propensity, predisposition

Antonyms:
inability, ineptitude, incompetence, lack
