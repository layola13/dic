defiatory:/dɪˈfaɪəˌtɔːri/
adj.
挑衅的，蔑视的
Showing open resistance or bold disobedience.
She gave him a defiant look. 她挑衅地看了他一眼。
近义词：rebellious, resistant, challenging, provocative
反义词：submissive, obedient, compliant, respectful
词性：形容词

defiatory:/dɪˈfaɪəˌtɔːri/
adj.
目中无人的
Characterized by defiance; boldly resistant or challenging.
His defiant attitude angered the judge. 他目中无人的态度激怒了法官。
近义词：insolent, impudent, audacious
反义词：deferential, respectful, humble
词性：形容词
