cliffier:/ˈklɪfiər/
adjective
更险峻的，悬崖更多的
Characterized by having more or steeper cliffs.
The coastline became cliffier as we traveled north. 我们向北走时，海岸线变得更加陡峭。
The western side of the island is much cliffier than the east. 岛的西侧比东侧悬崖更多。
Synonyms: steeper, craggy, precipitous
Antonyms: flatter, gentler, sloping
词性: 形容词
