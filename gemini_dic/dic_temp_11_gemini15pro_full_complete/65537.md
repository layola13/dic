euthanasiasts:/ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒɪəsts/
n.
安乐死实施者，支持安乐死的人
People who perform or advocate euthanasia, the act of painlessly ending the life of someone suffering from an incurable and painful disease.
The group of euthanasiasts lobbied for changes in the law.  这群安乐死实施者游说修改法律。
