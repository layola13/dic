overharvests:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrvɪsts/ | n. |过度捕捞；过度采伐|The act of harvesting a renewable resource to the point of diminishing returns.  It can apply to plants, animals, or other natural resources.|Overharvesting of fish has led to a decline in their population. 过度捕捞鱼类导致其数量下降。|Synonyms: overexploitation, depletion |Antonyms: conservation, sustainable harvesting|noun


overharvests:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrvɪsts/ | v. |过度捕捞；过度采伐|To harvest a renewable resource beyond its sustainable yield.|They overharvested the timber, leading to deforestation.  他们过度采伐木材，导致森林砍伐。|Synonyms: overexploit, deplete |Antonyms: conserve, sustain|verb
