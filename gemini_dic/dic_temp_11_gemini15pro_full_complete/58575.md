dulciana:/ˌdʌlsiˈænə/ /ˌdʌlʃiˈɑːnə/| n. |杜尔恰纳琴，一种文艺复兴时期的键盘乐器|A dulciana is a Renaissance keyboard instrument, a quiet type of harpsichord with a delicate, sweet tone.  It is known for its soft and expressive sound, suitable for intimate settings. It typically has two manuals (keyboards) and a single set of strings.|例句：The dulciana's delicate tone filled the small chapel. 杜尔恰纳琴柔和的音调充满了小教堂。

例句：She played a mournful melody on the dulciana. 她在杜尔恰纳琴上演奏了一曲悲伤的旋律。|近义词：harpsichord, virginal|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps forte-piano, due to its dynamic range)|词性:noun
