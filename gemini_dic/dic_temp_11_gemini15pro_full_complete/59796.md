ecotypically:/ˌiːkoʊˈtɪpɪkli/ | adv. |生态型地，生态型地 | In a way that is typical of an ecotype.  Characterized by the adaptations of a specific ecotype. | The plant ecotypically thrives in shady, moist environments.  这种植物生态型地生长在阴暗潮湿的环境中。| 近义词: characteristically, typically | 反义词: atypically | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "ecotype" + "-ically."  "Ecotype" comes from "eco-" (from Greek *oikos* meaning "house, dwelling, environment") + "type."  "-ically" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
