accounted:/əˈkaʊntɪd/
|v.|被认为是；被解释为；（数量上）占；对…负有责任|Explained; considered; comprised; responsible for.|The poor weather accounted for the small crowd. 天气不好是人少的原因。|近义词：explained, considered, attributed, responsible|反义词：unexplained, disregarded, irresponsible|词性:动词

accounted:/əˈkaʊntɪd/
|adj.|已解释的；已说明的|Explained; given an explanation or justification for.|The missing money is now fully accounted for.  丢失的钱现在已经全部得到了解释。|近义词：explained, justified, clarified|反义词：unexplained, unjustified, unclear|词性:形容词
