ae:/iː/ | interj. | 啊 | An exclamation of surprise, joy, or pain. | "Ae! I won the lottery!" 啊！我中彩票了！| Synonym: oh, ah | Antonym: (No direct antonym for an exclamation) | Interjection


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you see? = 你看到了什么？
What does she eat for breakfast? = 她早餐吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are they watching on TV? = 他们在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been studying at university? = 她一直在大学里学什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say to him? = 你对他说了什么？
What did she buy at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会时穿了什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病之前吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? = 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will she wear to the wedding? = 她会穿什么去参加婚礼？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? = 她下学期会学什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have learned by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将学到什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying for by the time she gets her PhD? = 到她获得博士学位时，她将已经学习什么多久了？



疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what he's saying. = 她不明白他在说什么。


虚拟语气:
If I knew what he wanted, I would give it to him. = 如果我知道他想要什么，我会给他的。
I wish I knew what the answer was. = 我希望我知道答案是什么。


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What the hell! = 搞什么鬼！
what if = 如果...会怎么样？
what for = 为了什么？
what about = ...怎么样？


注意事项:  ae本身作为一个独立的单词使用场景较少，更多情况下作为词根出现。 以上例句大多用what进行举例，因为what更贴近题意中要求的what的用法。
