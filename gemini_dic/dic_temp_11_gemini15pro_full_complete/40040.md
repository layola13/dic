consummately:/ˈkɒnsʌmətli/ /kənˈsʌmətli/| adv.|完美地；精湛地；彻底地|In a way that is complete and perfect; supremely.|She played the piece consummately. 她完美地演奏了这首曲子。|perfectly, expertly, flawlessly|imperfectly, poorly, inadequately|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *consummatus (past participle of consummare, "to sum up, complete, perfect"), from Latin con- ("together") + summa ("sum, total").
