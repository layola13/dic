gymnorhinal:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈraɪnəl/| adj.| 嗅内皮层的，与嗅内皮层相关的 | Relating to the entorhinal cortex, a part of the brain involved in memory and navigation. | The gymnorhinal cortex plays a crucial role in spatial navigation. 嗅内皮层在空间导航中起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词:entorhinal | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek *gumnos* (naked, bare) + *rhis* (nose) + -al (suffix forming adjectives).  The term refers to the entorhinal cortex, which appears relatively smooth ("naked") compared to other cortical areas.
