driftlessness:/ˈdrɪftləsnəs/
n.
无漂移；未受冰川作用
The state of being unaffected by glacial drift.  This refers to an area that was not covered by glaciers during the last ice age.
The Driftless Area is known for its unique topography. 无漂移区以其独特的地形而闻名。
The region's driftlessness has resulted in a unique ecosystem. 该地区的无漂移状态形成了一个独特的生态系统。
