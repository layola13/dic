indoctrinate:/ɪnˈdɑːktrɪneɪt/ /ɪnˈdɒktrɪneɪt/| vt.|灌输；教导；向…灌输|to teach someone to fully accept the ideas, opinions, and beliefs of a particular group and to not consider other ideas, opinions, and beliefs|The army indoctrinates new recruits to obey orders without question.  军队向新兵灌输要无条件服从命令。

They have been indoctrinated from an early age with their parents' political beliefs.  他们从小就被灌输了父母的政治信仰。|近义词：brainwash, propagandize, inculcate|反义词:educate, enlighten|词性:动词

indoctrinated:/ɪnˈdɑːktrɪneɪtɪd/ /ɪnˈdɒktrɪneɪtɪd/| adj.|被灌输的；受…影响的|having been taught a particular set of beliefs or ideas so thoroughly that you do not question them|He was indoctrinated with communist ideology from an early age. 他从小就被灌输了共产主义思想。

Many young people are indoctrinated with consumerism through advertising. 许多年轻人通过广告被灌输了消费主义思想。|近义词：brainwashed, programmed, conditioned|反义词:open-minded, independent|词性:形容词
