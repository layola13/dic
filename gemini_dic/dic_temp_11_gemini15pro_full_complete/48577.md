defeat:/dɪˈfiːt/ (US) /dɪˈfiːt/ (UK)| n. vt.|失败，击败|An act of defeating or being defeated; The state of being defeated.|例句：The team suffered a crushing defeat.  这个队伍遭受了惨败。|近义词：loss, failure, overthrow, conquer, vanquish|反义词:victory, triumph, win|词性:名词/动词
defeat:/dɪˈfiːt/ (US) /dɪˈfiːt/ (UK)| vt.|击败，战胜|To win a victory over (someone or something) in a battle, game, or contest.|例句：They defeated the enemy in a decisive battle. 他们在决定性战役中击败了敌人。|近义词：conquer, vanquish, overcome, beat, subdue|反义词:lose to, surrender to, yield to|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *defete*, *desfeite* "defeat, undoing, destruction," from *desfaire* "undo, destroy," from *des-* (from Latin *dis-* "apart, away") + *faire* "to do" (from Latin *facere* "to make, do").
