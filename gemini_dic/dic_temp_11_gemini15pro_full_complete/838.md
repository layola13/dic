abstractedly:/æbˈstræktɪdli/
adverb
抽象地；心不在焉地
in a way that shows you are not paying attention to what is happening around you because you are thinking about something else.
She smiled abstractedly, lost in her own thoughts. 她心不在焉地笑了笑，沉浸在自己的思绪中。
He nodded abstractedly, clearly not listening to what I was saying. 他心不在焉地点点头，显然没在听我说话。

近义词: absentmindedly, vaguely, distantly, inattentively, dreamily
反义词: attentively, carefully, thoughtfully, consciously

词源: From the adjective "abstract" + "-ed" + "-ly". "Abstract" comes from Latin "abstractus", past participle of "abstrahere" (to draw away), from "abs-" (away) + "trahere" (to draw).
