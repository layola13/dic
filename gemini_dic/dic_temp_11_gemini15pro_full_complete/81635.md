greedily: /ˈɡriːdəli/ | adv. | 贪婪地，贪心地 | In a way that shows a strong desire for more food, money, possessions, power, etc., than is necessary or reasonable. | He greedily ate all the cookies. 他贪婪地吃掉了所有的饼干。| The child greedily grabbed the toy. 孩子贪婪地抓住了玩具。| 近义词: avariciously, covetously, rapaciously | 反义词: generously, selflessly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Middle English *grediliche*, equivalent to greedy +‎ -ly.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grēdigaz*.
