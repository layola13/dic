citric acid:/ˈsɪtrɪk ˈæsɪd/| n.|柠檬酸|A weak organic acid that occurs naturally in citrus fruits. It is widely used as a flavoring and preservative in food and beverages.|Lemonade contains citric acid. 柠檬水含有柠檬酸。|Synonyms: sour salt,  |Antonyms: alkali, base|noun


citric acids: /ˈsɪtrɪk ˈæsɪdz/ |n.| 柠檬酸(复数，通常指不同种类的柠檬酸或柠檬酸盐)|Plural form of citric acid, often referring to various forms or salts of citric acid.|Citric acids are used in various industrial applications. 不同种类的柠檬酸在各种工业应用中都有使用。|Synonyms: citrates (when referring to salts)|Antonyms: alkalis, bases|noun
