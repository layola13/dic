intangible:/ɪnˈtændʒəbl/ | adj. n. |无形的，难以捉摸的；非物质资产|Unable to be touched or grasped; not having physical presence.|例句：The benefits of a good education are often intangible. 良好教育的益处往往是无形的。|近义词：impalpable, incorporeal, abstract|反义词：tangible, concrete, material|词性:形容词

intangible:/ɪnˈtændʒəbl/ | n. |无形资产|Something that cannot be touched or grasped; a non-physical asset.|例句：Goodwill is an intangible asset of a company. 商誉是公司的无形资产。|近义词：non-physical asset|反义词：tangible asset|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intangibilis, from in- "not" + tangibilis "tangible," from Latin tangere "to touch."
