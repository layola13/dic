overculls: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌlz/ | n. | 过度剔除，过度淘汰 | The act of culling too many animals or items, resulting in a smaller than desired population or quantity. |  The overculls of deer in the park have led to an imbalance in the ecosystem. 公园里鹿的过度剔除导致了生态系统失衡。| synonyms: over-thinning, excessive culling | antonyms: under-culling, insufficient culling | noun
