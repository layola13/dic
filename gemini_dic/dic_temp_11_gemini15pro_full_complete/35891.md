coconut:/ˈkoʊkənʌt/ (US) /ˈkəʊkənʌt/ (UK)| n.|椰子|The hard-shelled fruit of the coconut palm, containing white flesh and a milky liquid.|例句：I love the taste of fresh coconut. 我喜欢新鲜椰子的味道。

例句：We drank coconut milk straight from the coconut. 我们直接从椰子里喝椰奶。

近义词：cocoanut (archaic)|反义词:None|词性:noun
