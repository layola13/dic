nongrain:/ˈnɑːnɡreɪn/| n. |非谷物，非粮作物|A crop that is not a cereal grain, such as soybeans, potatoes, or cotton.|Example: Farmers are increasingly diversifying their crops to include nongrains like lentils and chickpeas. 农民们正越来越多地将他们的作物多样化，包括小扁豆和鹰嘴豆等非谷物。|Synonym: noncereal|Antonym: grain, cereal|noun
