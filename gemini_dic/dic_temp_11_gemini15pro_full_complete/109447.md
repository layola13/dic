leper:/ˈlepər/ (US), /ˈleprə/ (UK)| n.|麻风病人；受排斥的人；|A person who has leprosy, a chronic infectious disease that affects the skin, nerves, and mucous membranes. Also used figuratively to describe someone who is ostracized or shunned.|例句：In the past, lepers were often ostracized from society. 过去，麻风病人经常被社会排斥。

例句：He felt like a leper, avoided by everyone because of the rumors.  他感觉自己像个麻风病人，因为谣言而被所有人回避。|近义词：outcast, pariah|反义词:|noun
