hornblende:/ˈhɔːrnˌblend/ (US) /ˈhɔːnˌblend/ (UK)| n. |角闪石|A common rock-forming mineral, a complex inosilicate series of the amphibole group. It is a dark, usually black or dark green mineral found in igneous and metamorphic rocks.|Hornblende is a common component of granite. 角闪石是花岗岩的常见成分。

The hornblende in this rock sample is particularly well-formed. 这块岩石样本中的角闪石形态特别好。|近义词：amphibole|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)
词性:noun
