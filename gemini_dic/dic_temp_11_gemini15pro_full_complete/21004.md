blessedly:/ˈblesɪdli/| adv.|幸运地，幸福地，谢天谢地|In a blessed manner; fortunately; thankfully.  It describes something happening in a way that brings relief, peace, or happiness.|Blessedly, the rain stopped before the picnic.  谢天谢地，野餐前雨停了。|Synonyms: fortunately, luckily, thankfully, mercifully|Antonyms: unfortunately, unluckily, regrettably|adverb
词源->
From "blessed" + "-ly." "Blessed" comes from Old English "blēdsian," meaning "to consecrate with blood," later evolving to mean "to make holy, happy, or fortunate." The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs.
