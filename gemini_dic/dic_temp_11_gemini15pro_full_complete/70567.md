filled:/fɪld/| adj.| 充满的，填满的 |Something that is filled is full of something. | The room was filled with smoke. 房间里充满了烟雾。| full, replete, crammed | empty, vacant | adjective

filled:/fɪld/| v.| 填充，装满（fill的过去式和过去分词）| The past tense and past participle of fill. |  I filled the glass with water. 我把杯子装满了水。 | loaded, packed, stuffed | emptied, depleted | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *filled, *fylden, *ifilled, from Old English fyllan (“to fill, make full, satisfy, fulfill”), from Proto-Germanic *fulljaną (“to fill”), from Proto-Indo-European *pl̥h₁- (“to fill”). Cognate with West Frisian folle (“to fill”), Dutch vullen (“to fill”), German füllen (“to fill”), Danish fylde (“to fill”), Swedish fylla (“to fill”).
