boisterous:/ˈbɔɪstərəs/| adj.|喧闹的；狂暴的；充满活力的|Full of noisy enthusiasm and energy; rowdy.|The party was a boisterous affair with loud music and lots of dancing. 派对很热闹，音乐震耳欲聋，很多人在跳舞。|Synonyms: lively, energetic, rowdy, rambunctious|Antonyms: quiet, calm, peaceful|adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *boistous, *boystous, from Anglo-Norman boistous, alteration of Old French boisteus (“lame, crippled, clumsy”), of uncertain origin, probably from a Germanic source.
