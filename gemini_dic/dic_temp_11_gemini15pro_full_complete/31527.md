chartaceous:/tʃɑːrˈteɪʃəs/
adj.
纸质的；像纸的
Resembling paper in texture; papery.  Thin and flexible, like paper.
The mushroom had a chartaceous cap. 这种蘑菇的菌盖像纸一样。
The dried leaves felt chartaceous. 干枯的叶子摸起来像纸一样。
Synonyms: papery, papyraceous, thin, flexible
Antonyms: thick, rigid, stiff
adjective
