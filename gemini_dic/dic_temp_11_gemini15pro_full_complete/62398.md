endodermal:/ˌɛndəˈdɜːrməl/| adj.|内胚层的|Of or relating to the endoderm, the innermost layer of cells or tissue of an embryo in early development.  The endoderm develops into the lining of the digestive tract and other internal organs.|The endodermal cells differentiate into various tissues. 内胚层细胞分化成各种组织。|The scientist studied the endodermal development of the frog embryo.  科学家研究了青蛙胚胎的内胚层发育。|近义词:entodermal, hypoblastic|反义词:ectodermal, mesodermal|词性:形容词
endoderm:/ˈɛndəˌdɜːrm/| n.|内胚层|The innermost of the three primary germ layers of the embryo, developing into the lining of the digestive tract and other internal organs.|The endoderm forms the lining of the gut. 内胚层形成肠道内壁。|The development of the endoderm is crucial for the formation of vital organs. 内胚层的发育对于重要器官的形成至关重要。|近义词:entoderm, hypoblast|反义词:ectoderm, mesoderm|词性:名词
