chaps:/tʃæps/ | n. | 牛仔皮套褲；家伙（尤指男人）| Leather leggings worn over trousers by cowboys to protect their legs. Also, an informal term for men, especially friends. |  The cowboys wore chaps to protect their legs from thorns. 牛仔们穿着皮套褲以保护他们的腿免受荆棘的伤害。|Synonyms: leggings, fellows, guys | Antonyms: None | Noun

chaps:/tʃæps/ | n. | (英国非正式)下颚；嘴 | (chiefly British informal) Jaws; mouth | He had a bit of food stuck to his chaps. 他嘴上沾了点食物。| Synonyms: jaws, mouth | Antonyms: None | Noun
