incurvation:/ˌɪnkɜːrˈveɪʃən/ | n. |弯曲，曲率 |The act of curving or bending inwards; the state of being curved inwards. |The incurvation of the spine can lead to serious health problems. 脊柱弯曲会导致严重的健康问题。|The artist emphasized the gentle incurvation of the hills in his painting.  这位艺术家在他的画中强调了山丘柔和的曲线。|近义词: curvature, bend, curve |反义词: straightness |词性: noun

词源: From Latin *incurvātus*, past participle of *incurvāre* (“to bend, curve”), from *in-* (“in, into”) + *curvāre* (“to curve, bend”), from *curvus* (“curved, bent”).
