clavigerous:/kləˈvɪdʒərəs/
adjective
持钥匙的；掌管钥匙的
bearing or holding keys, especially as a symbol of office or authority.
The clavigerous guard unlocked the ancient gate.  持钥匙的守卫打开了古老的大门。
Synonyms: key-bearing, key-holding
Antonyms: keyless
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *clāviger* (key-bearing), from *clāvis* (key) + *-ger* (carrying, bearing).  The *-ger* suffix is related to the verb *gerō* (I carry, I bear).
