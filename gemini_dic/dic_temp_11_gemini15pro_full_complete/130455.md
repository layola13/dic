nonabsorbent:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɔːbənt/ /ˌnɑːnəbˈsɔːrbənt/| adj.|非吸收性的，不透水的|Not able to absorb liquids or other substances.|This raincoat is made of a nonabsorbent material. 这件雨衣是用不吸水的材料做的。|The table has a nonabsorbent surface, making it easy to clean. 这张桌子表面不吸水，很容易清洁。|近义词：impermeable, waterproof, water-resistant|反义词: absorbent, permeable, porous|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- + absorbent.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "absorbent" comes from the Latin "absorbēre," meaning "to swallow up."
