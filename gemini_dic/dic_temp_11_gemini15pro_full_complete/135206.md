nonosmotic:/ˌnɒnɒzˈmɒtɪk/
adjective
非渗透的
Not pertaining to or involving osmosis; not osmotic.
The transport of these ions is nonosmotic. 这些离子的运输是非渗透性的。
近义词:non-osmotic
反义词:osmotic
词性:adjective

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + osmotic (from Greek ōsmos "pushing, thrusting," related to ōthein "to push")
