gibbered:/ˈdʒɪbərd/| v.|胡言乱语，喋喋不休|to speak quickly and in a way that is difficult to understand, especially because you are very frightened or excited|He gibbered incoherently about the monster he had seen. 他语无伦次地说着他看到的怪物。|近义词：babble, chatter, ramble|反义词: articulate, enunciate|动词

gibbering:/ˈdʒɪbərɪŋ/ | adj. |语无伦次的，喋喋不休的|speaking quickly and in a way that is difficult to understand|The gibbering madman was taken to the hospital. 语无伦次的疯子被送往医院。|近义词：babbling, chattering, rambling|反义词: articulate, coherent|形容词
