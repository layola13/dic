gilse:/ɡɪls/ | n. | 母鲑鱼(产卵后返回淡水) | A female salmon that has returned to fresh water to spawn. | The gilse were plentiful in the river this year. 今年河里的母鲑鱼很多。| 近义词:salmon, spawner | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑male salmon雄性鲑鱼) | 词性:名词

Etymology: From Scottish Gaelic *gealasaich*, diminutive of *gealas* (“brightness, whiteness”), referring to the silvery appearance of the fish.
