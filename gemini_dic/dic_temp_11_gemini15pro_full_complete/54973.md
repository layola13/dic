disproportionality: /ˌdɪsprəˌpɔːrʃəˈnælɪti/ | n. | 不成比例，不相称 |  The condition of being out of proportion; a lack of balance or equality between things. | There is a disproportionality between the number of male and female students in the engineering program. 工程专业男女学生人数不成比例。|  imbalance, disparity, inequality | proportionality, balance, parity | noun

disproportionate: /ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənət/ | adj. | 不成比例的，不相称的 | Too large or too small in comparison with something else; not having the correct relationship in size, amount, importance, etc. | The punishment was disproportionate to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不成比例。|  excessive, immoderate, uneven | proportionate, balanced, commensurate | adjective
