minded:/ˈmaɪndɪd/| adj.|有…倾向的；介意…的；注意…的|Having a specified kind of mind or disposition; inclined or disposed in a specified way.|例句：She is very career-minded. 她很有事业心。

例句：He's not politically minded. 他对政治不感兴趣。|近义词：inclined, disposed, oriented|反义词：uninterested, indifferent|词性:形容词
