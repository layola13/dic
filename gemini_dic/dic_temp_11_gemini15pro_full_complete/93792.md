immeritous: /ˌɪmɛrɪtəs/
adjective
无功受禄的; 不应得的
undeserved; not merited
He received immeritous praise for the project. 他因这个项目受到了不应得的赞扬。
The award was considered immeritous by many. 许多人认为这个奖项是无功受禄的。
近义词: undeserved, unwarranted, unmerited
反义词: deserved, earned, merited
词性: adjective
词源:  From Latin *immeritus, from in- "not" + meritus "deserved."
