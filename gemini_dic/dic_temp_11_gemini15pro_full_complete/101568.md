ironwood:/ˈaɪərnˌwʊd/| n. |铁木（指各种质地坚硬的树木）|Any of various trees having exceptionally hard wood.  It refers to a variety of tree species known for their dense, hard wood.|例句：The furniture was made of ironwood and extremely heavy. 这件家具是用铁木做的，非常重。

例句：Ironwood trees are slow-growing but highly valued for their durability. 铁木生长缓慢，但因其耐久性而备受重视。|近义词：hardwood|反义词:softwood|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *īsenwudu (attested only in compounds like īsenwudu-beam "ironwood beam"), from īsen "iron" + wudu "wood."  The name reflects the density and hardness of the wood, which is comparable to iron.
