applique:/əˈplɪkeɪ/ /ˈæplɪkeɪ/
n.|贴花，补花；装饰品|A decorative piece of material sewn or stuck onto another larger piece.|She decorated her jeans with colorful appliques.|近义词:patch, ornament, embellishment|反义词:None|Noun
applique:/əˈplɪkeɪ/ /ˈæplɪkeɪ/
vt.|缝贴，贴上|To sew or stick a decorative piece of material onto another larger piece.|She appliqued flowers onto the quilt.|近义词:attach, affix, sew on|反义词:detach, remove|Verb
