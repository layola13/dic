eldercare: /ˈɛldərˌkɛr/| n.|老年护理，老年人照护|The increasing elderly population requires more comprehensive eldercare services.  人口老龄化加剧需要更全面的老年护理服务。|Eldercare is a growing industry as the population ages. 随着人口老龄化，老年护理是一个不断发展的行业。|近义词：geriatric care, senior care|反义词: child care,  pediatric care|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From elder + care.  "Elder" meaning older and "care" meaning the provision of what is necessary for the health, welfare, maintenance, and protection of someone or something.
