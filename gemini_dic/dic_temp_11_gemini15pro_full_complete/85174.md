harebrained:/ˈhɛːrbreɪnd/ (US) /ˈheəbreɪnd/ (UK)| adj.|轻率的；鲁莽的；不考虑后果的|Foolish and impractical.  Originating from the belief that hares were easily startled and therefore not very intelligent.  Literally, having the brain of a hare.|That harebrained scheme of yours is bound to fail.  你那轻率的计划注定要失败。|Synonyms: foolish, reckless, rash, impulsive, ill-conceived, ill-advised, madcap, cockamamie|Antonyms: sensible, prudent, wise, cautious, level-headed|adjective
