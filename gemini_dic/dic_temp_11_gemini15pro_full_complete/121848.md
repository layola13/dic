misreporters:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrtərz/ | n. | 误报者，错误报道者 |  People who report something incorrectly, either through error or intentionally.  These could be journalists, witnesses, or anyone relaying information inaccurately. | The misreporters spread false information about the incident. 误报者传播了关于该事件的虚假信息。| The misreporters were criticized for their inaccurate reporting. 这些误报者因其不准确的报道而受到批评。 | synonyms: fabricators, distorters, perverters | antonyms: accurate reporters, truth-tellers | noun
