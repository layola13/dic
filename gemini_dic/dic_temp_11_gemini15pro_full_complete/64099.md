eponymy:/ɪˈpɒnɪmi/ /əˈpɑːnɪmi/| n.|命名法；名字的由来；用人名命名|The name of the disease, Parkinson's disease, is an eponymy, named after the physician James Parkinson.|例句：The eponymy of Alzheimer's disease is derived from the name of Alois Alzheimer, who first described the condition. 阿尔茨海默病的命名源于首次描述该病症的阿洛伊斯·阿尔茨海默的名字。

例句：Many anatomical structures have eponymies based on the names of the scientists who discovered them. 许多解剖结构的名称都源于发现它们的科学家的名字。|近义词：name derivation, nomenclature|反义词: None|词性: noun
