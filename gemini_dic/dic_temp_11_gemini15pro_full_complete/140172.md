ETYMOLOGY->

From offensive + -ness + -es.  "Offensive" traces back to Old French "offensif," from Latin "offensus," past participle of "offendere" meaning "to strike against, offend."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality. "-es" is a plural suffix.
