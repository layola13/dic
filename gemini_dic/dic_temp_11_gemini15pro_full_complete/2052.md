acupuncture: /ˈækjʊpʌŋktʃər/ | n. | 针灸 | A system of complementary medicine that involves pricking the skin or tissues with needles, used to alleviate pain and to treat various physical, mental, and emotional conditions. |  Example: Acupuncture is often used to treat back pain. 针灸常用于治疗背痛。

Example: She tried acupuncture to relieve her migraines. 她尝试针灸来缓解偏头痛。 | 近义词: acupressure (穴位按压) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps conventional medicine or Western medicine) | 词性: noun
