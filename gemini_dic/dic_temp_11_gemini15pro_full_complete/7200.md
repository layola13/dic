angle:/ˈæŋɡəl/ (US), /ˈæŋɡl/ (UK)| n. |角，角度，观点，立场|A figure formed by two lines diverging from a common point.  The space between two intersecting lines or surfaces at or close to the point where they meet.|例句：The two walls met at an odd angle.  两堵墙以一个奇怪的角度相交。|近义词：corner, bend, perspective, viewpoint|反义词:straight|noun


angle:/ˈæŋɡəl/ (US), /ˈæŋɡl/ (UK)| v. |钓鱼，谋取，使带有角度|To fish with a rod and line. To present or try to obtain something in a particular way.|例句：He angled for a promotion. 他谋求晋升。|近义词：fish, scheme, maneuver|反义词:|verb
