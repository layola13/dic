fausse:/fos/ (French)| adj.|假的，错误的，人造的|False, artificial, not genuine.  Derived from the Old French word "fals," ultimately from the Latin "falsus."  Used primarily in contexts related to jewelry or gemstones, indicating imitation or non-genuine materials.  Can also be used more generally to mean incorrect or misleading.|例句：She was wearing a fausse pearl necklace. 她戴着一条人造珍珠项链。

例句：The painting was discovered to be a fausse. 这幅画被发现是赝品。|近义词：fake, artificial, imitation, counterfeit, spurious, ersatz|反义词：genuine, real, authentic|词性:adjective
