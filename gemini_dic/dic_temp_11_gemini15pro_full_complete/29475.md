cataplectic:/ˌkætəˈplektɪk/ | adj. | 猝倒的，猝倒症的 | Relating to or characterized by cataplexy, a sudden loss of muscle tone often triggered by strong emotions. |  He experienced a cataplectic attack when he laughed too hard. 他大笑的时候出现了猝倒症发作。| 近义词: akinetic,atonic | 反义词: tonic, spastic | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *kataplēxis* "a striking down," from *kata-* "down" + *plēxis* "a stroke."
