eserine:/ˈɛsəriːn/| n.|依色林,毒扁豆碱|A parasympathomimetic alkaloid, C₁₅H₂₁N₃O₂, obtained from the Calabar bean, used as a miotic and to treat glaucoma. It inhibits acetylcholinesterase.|Eserine is used to treat glaucoma. 依色林用于治疗青光眼。|Synonyms: physostigmine|Antonyms: atropine|noun
