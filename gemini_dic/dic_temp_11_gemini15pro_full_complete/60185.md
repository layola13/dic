efficaciousness:/ˌefɪˈkeɪʃəsnəs/| n. | 功效，有效性 |The quality of being efficacious; effectiveness.  The power to produce a desired effect. | The efficaciousness of the new drug was quickly apparent.  这种新药的疗效很快显现出来。 | synonyms: effectiveness, potency, strength, efficacy | antonyms: ineffectiveness, impotence, weakness, inefficacy | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *efficacitas, from Latin efficax ("effective") + -itas. Equivalent to efficacious +‎ -ness.
