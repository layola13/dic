fictitious:/fɪkˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
虚构的，编造的
created by imagination; not real or true; made up
The characters in the novel are entirely fictitious.  小说中的人物完全是虚构的。
The story was based on fictitious events.  这个故事是基于虚构的事件。
synonyms: fabricated, invented, imaginary, made-up, untrue, false
antonyms: real, actual, true, genuine, factual
词性: 形容词
