hypothecate:/haɪˈpɑθɪkeɪt/| v. |抵押，担保|To pledge something (usually property) as security for a loan without giving up possession of it.|例句：He hypothecated his house to secure the business loan. 他抵押了他的房子以获得商业贷款。

例句：The farmer hypothecated his land to get a loan for new equipment.  农民抵押了他的土地以获得新设备的贷款。|近义词：pledge, mortgage, collateralize|反义词: redeem, reclaim|词性: 动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *hypothecāre, from Latin hypotheca (“pledge, security”), from Ancient Greek ὑποθήκη (hupothḗkē, “deposit, pledge, security”).
