abasements:/əˈbeɪsmənts/
n.
屈辱，贬低，降低
the act of humbling oneself or lowering one's status or dignity; a state of being humbled or lowered.
The king suffered many abasements after being captured by the enemy. 国王被敌人俘虏后遭受了许多屈辱。
His abasement before the emperor was complete. 他在皇帝面前彻底卑躬屈膝。
近义词:humiliations, degradations, submissions
反义词:exaltations, elevations
