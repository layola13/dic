beplumed:/bɪˈpluːmd/
adjective
装饰着羽毛的；戴着羽毛的
adorned or decorated with feathers, often in a showy or elaborate way
The knight's helmet was beplumed with bright red feathers.  这位骑士的头盔上装饰着鲜红色的羽毛。
The beplumed dancer moved gracefully across the stage.  这位戴着羽毛装饰的舞者优雅地在舞台上移动。
近义词: feathered, plumed, adorned
反义词: bare, unadorned
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + plumed, past participle of plume (v.) "to decorate with feathers."  The prefix "be-" adds intensity or completeness to the verb.
