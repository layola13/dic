entertains:/ˌɛntərˈteɪnz/ | v. |  娱乐；招待；使有兴趣；怀有；考虑 |  provides amusement or enjoyment for (someone).  | The comedian entertains the audience with his jokes.  喜剧演员用他的笑话娱乐观众。 |  amuses, diverts, interests | bores, wearies | verb

entertain:/ˌɛntərˈteɪn/ | v. | 娱乐；招待；使有兴趣；怀有；考虑 | considers (a possibility or proposal). |  She entertained the idea of moving to the countryside. 她考虑搬到乡下的想法。 | consider, contemplate, harbor | dismiss, reject | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French entretenir, from entre- (“between, among”) + tenir (“to hold”), from Latin tenēre.
