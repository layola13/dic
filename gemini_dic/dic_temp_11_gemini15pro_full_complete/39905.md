constitutive:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːtɪv/ /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːtɪv/| adj.|构成的；组成的；基本的；本质的；制定的；创立的|Forming a part of the inherent nature of something; basic or essential.|例句：Language is constitutive of identity. 语言构成身份认同。|例句：The constitutive elements of the universe are still a mystery. 宇宙的基本组成元素仍然是个谜。|近义词：essential, basic, fundamental, inherent, integral|反义词：nonessential, incidental|词性:形容词

constitutive:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːtɪv/ /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːtɪv/| n.|要素；组成成分|A constituent part or element of something.|例句：The constitutive of the drug needs further analysis.  这种药物的成分需要进一步分析。|例句：Identifying the constitutives of a successful team is crucial.  确定一个成功团队的组成要素至关重要。|近义词：component, element, ingredient, constituent|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
