antimoral:/ˌæntiˈmɔːrəl/ | adj. | 反道德的，非道德的 | Opposing or indifferent to morality; neither moral nor immoral.  It describes behavior or beliefs that are not concerned with whether something is right or wrong.  It is distinct from *immoral*, which means actively opposing morality. | His antimoral stance shocked many people who believed in traditional values. 他反道德的立场震惊了许多笃信传统价值观的人。|  He lived an antimoral life, unconcerned with the concepts of right and wrong. 他过着一种非道德的生活，对是非对错的概念漠不关心。| 近义词: amoral, nonmoral, unmoral | 反义词: moral, ethical | 词性: 形容词
