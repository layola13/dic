clogs:/klɑːɡz/ /klɒɡz/
n.|木屐，障碍，阻塞物|A type of shoe made of wood, or with a wooden sole. Also, something that blocks or impedes progress or flow.|She wore bright red clogs. 她穿着一双鲜红色的木屐。|近义词：shoes, slippers, obstacles, hindrances|反义词:aids, assistance
词性:名词

clogs:/klɑːɡz/ /klɒɡz/
v.|阻塞，堵塞|To block or become blocked.|The drain was clogged with hair. 下水道被头发堵塞了。|近义词：block, obstruct, jam|反义词:clear, unblock
词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *cloggen*, from Old Norse *klakki* (“block of wood, log”), from Proto-Germanic *klukkaz* (“lump, block”). Cognate with Icelandic *klakki* (“block of ice”), Swedish *klaka* (“piece of ice, icicle”).
