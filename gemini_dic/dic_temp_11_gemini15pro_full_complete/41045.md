copied:/ˈkɒpɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpid/ (US)| adj. |复制的；抄写的|Something that has been copied is a reproduction of an original.|The teacher confiscated the copied exam papers. 老师没收了抄袭的试卷。|近义词：duplicated, replicated, reproduced|反义词：original, unique|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "copy," which comes from Old French "copier" (to copy, transcribe), ultimately from Latin "copia" (abundance, plenty).  The "-ed" suffix transforms the verb into a past participle adjective.
