bequeathals:/bɪˈkwiːðəlz/
n.
遗产；遗赠物；遗赠
Something that is bequeathed; a legacy.
His will made several specific bequeathals to his children. 他的遗嘱中特别提到要给他的孩子们一些遗产。
legacy, inheritance, endowment
donation
noun
