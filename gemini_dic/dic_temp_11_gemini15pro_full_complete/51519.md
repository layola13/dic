detrite:/dɪˈtraɪtəs/
n.|碎屑，残渣|Waste or debris of any kind, especially that resulting from disintegration or wearing away; fragments produced by rubbing away. |The ocean floor is covered in a layer of detritus.|近义词: debris, waste, remains, sediment, rubble|反义词: wholeness, entirety|词性: noun
Etymology: From Latin *dētrītus*, past participle of *dēterere* "to rub away, wear away," from *dē-* "away" + *terere* "to rub, grind."
