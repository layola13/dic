outweaves:/ˌaʊtˈwiːvz/
verb
胜过编织，比……编织得更好
surpasses in weaving; weaves more intricately than
The skilled artisan outweaves his apprentices with intricate designs.  这位技艺精湛的工匠以复杂的图案胜过他的学徒。
The spider outweaves its web with incredible speed and precision.  蜘蛛以惊人的速度和精度编织它的网。
近义词:surpass, excel, outdo
反义词:fail, lose, underperform
词性:v.

Etymology:
From out- + weave.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding.
