hamble:/ˈhæmbəl/ | adj. | (古语) 跛的；残废的 | (Archaic) Lame; crippled. |  The hamble beggar limped down the street. 跛脚的乞丐一瘸一拐地走在街上。| Synonyms: lame, crippled, disabled | Antonyms: able-bodied, healthy, sound | adjective
