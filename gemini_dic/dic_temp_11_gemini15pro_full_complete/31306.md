chaplets:/ˈtʃæplɪts/
n.
花环，念珠
A string of beads used in praying, especially in the Roman Catholic Church; a wreath or garland for the head.
She wore a chaplet of flowers in her hair. 她头上戴着花环。
The priest held his chaplets and began to pray. 神父拿着念珠开始祈祷。
