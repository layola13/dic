omphalocele:/ˈɒmfələˌsiːl/ (US), /ˈɒmfələˌsiːl/ (UK)| n. |脐膨出|A birth defect in which the infant's intestine or other abdominal organs stick outside of the belly button. |The baby was born with an omphalocele, requiring immediate surgery.  这个婴儿出生时患有脐膨出，需要立即进行手术。|Synonyms: exomphalos|Antonyms: None|Noun
