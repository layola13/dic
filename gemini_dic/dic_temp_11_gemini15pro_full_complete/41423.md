cornering:/ˈkɔːrnərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkɔːnərɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |转弯; 垄断; 囤积居奇|The act of going around a corner, especially in a vehicle; the act of gaining control of a large supply of something, often to manipulate the price.|例句：His cornering was impeccable on the racetrack. 他在赛道上的转弯技巧无可挑剔。|近义词：turning, rounding, monopolizing, hoarding|反义词:straightening|noun

cornering:/ˈkɔːrnərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkɔːnərɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |转弯的|Relating to the act of cornering.|例句：The car's cornering ability is impressive. 这辆车的转弯性能令人印象深刻。|近义词：turning|反义词:straightening|adjective
