inequality:/ɪnɪˈkwɒləti/ (US) /ˌɪnɪˈkwɑːləti/ (UK)| n.|不平等，不均等；不等式|Lack of equality; disparity.  A mathematical expression which shows that two quantities are not equal.|The report highlighted the inequality of access to healthcare.  这份报告强调了医疗保健机会的不平等。|近义词：disparity, imbalance, difference, unevenness, asymmetry|反义词：equality, parity, balance, uniformity|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Middle English: from Old French inequalité or late Latin inaequalitas, from Latin inaequalis ‘unequal’.
