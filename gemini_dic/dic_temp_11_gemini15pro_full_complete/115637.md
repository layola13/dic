marcid:/ˈmɑːrsɪd/ | adj. | 枯萎的，憔悴的 | Withered; shriveled; lacking vitality or freshness.  | The marcid flowers drooped in the vase.  枯萎的花朵在花瓶里低垂着。 |  The marcid leaves fell from the dying tree. 枯萎的叶子从垂死的树上掉落。 | synonyms: withered, shriveled, wilted, faded, drooping, limp, flaccid, decayed, stale | antonyms: fresh, vibrant, blooming, healthy, turgid | adjective
