niche:/nɪtʃ/ /niːʃ/| n.| 壁龛；合适的位置；商机；小生态环境 |A shallow recess, especially one in a wall to display a statue or other ornament.  A comfortable or suitable position in life or employment. A specialized segment of the market for a particular kind of product or service.| She found her niche in life as a teacher. 她找到了自己的人生定位——当老师。| synonyms: alcove, nook, cranny, recess, slot, place, position, role, job, vocation, calling, métier, opportunity, market, segment | antonyms:  general, mainstream| noun

niche:/nɪtʃ/ /niːʃ/| adj.|  (of a product or service) appealing to a small, specialized section of the population| Denoting or relating to products, services, or interests that appeal to a small, specialized section of the population.|  They cater to a niche market of collectors. 他们迎合了一个收藏家的小众市场。| synonyms: specialized, select, exclusive, limited | antonyms:  mass-market, mainstream, general| adjective
