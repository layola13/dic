beaded:/ˈbiːdɪd/ /ˈbidɪd/
adjective
有珠子的；饰以珠子的
Decorated or covered with beads.
The dress was beaded with pearls. 这件礼服饰有珍珠。
近义词:pearled, jeweled, ornamented
反义词:plain, unadorned
词性:adjective

beaded:/ˈbiːdɪd/ /ˈbidɪd/
verb (used with object), beaded, beading.
用珠子装饰
To decorate or cover with beads.
She beaded the neckline of her dress. 她在连衣裙的领口饰以珠子。
近义词:decorate, adorn, embellish
反义词:strip, bare
词性:verb
