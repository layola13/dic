auripigment:/ɔːˈrɪpɪɡmənt/| n. |雄黄；雌黄；orpiment|A bright yellow mineral, arsenic trisulfide (As2S3), used as a pigment.|The artist used auripigment to create a vibrant yellow in the painting.|近义词:orpiment, arsenic trisulfide, King's yellow|反义词: None (no direct antonym for a specific mineral)|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French *or piment, from Medieval Latin *aurumpigmentum ("gold pigment"), from Latin aurum ("gold") + pigmentum ("pigment").  The "aurum" part likely refers to the color, not the presence of actual gold.
