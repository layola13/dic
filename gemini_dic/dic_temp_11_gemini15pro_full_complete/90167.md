hotspots:/ˈhɒtspɒts/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtspɑːts/ (US)| n.|热点; 热区; 热点地区; 热门景点|A place of more than usual interest, activity, or popularity.|Tourists flocked to the hotspots of the city.  游客涌向城市热点地区。|The café is a popular hotspot for students. 这家咖啡馆是学生们的热门聚集地。|synonyms: focal point, hub, center, hotbed, popular spot|antonyms: backwater, dead zone|noun

hotspot:/ˈhɒtspɒts/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtspɑːts/ (US)| n.|(计算机)热点|A wireless access point, typically in a public place like a coffee shop or airport.|The airport offers free Wi-Fi hotspots for travelers. 机场为旅客提供免费的无线网络热点。|I connected to the hotel's hotspot to check my email. 我连接到酒店的热点来查看我的电子邮件。|synonyms: Wi-Fi hotspot, access point, wireless network|antonyms: offline, disconnected|noun
