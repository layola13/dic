crosshatch:/ˈkrɒshætʃ/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːshætʃ/ (US)| n. v.|交叉影线，交叉排线|A pattern of closely spaced parallel lines that cross each other, often at right angles, used for shading or decoration.  |The artist used crosshatching to create a sense of depth in the drawing. 艺术家用交叉影线在画作中营造出一种深度感。| The architect’s plans were covered in crosshatching. 建筑师的图纸上布满了交叉影线。| synonyms: cross-hatching, hatching| antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "solid fill" or "blank") |noun, verb

crosshatch:/ˈkrɒshætʃ/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːshætʃ/ (US)| v.|用交叉影线绘制|To mark or shade with crosshatches. |He crosshatched the area on the map to indicate the forest. 他在地图上用交叉影线标示出森林区域。| She carefully crosshatched the metal surface. 她小心地在金属表面画上交叉影线。| synonyms: cross-hatching, shade, hatch| antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider "erase" or "clear")|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From cross- + hatch (v.).  The noun is from the verb.  "Hatch" in this context refers to the marking of lines.
