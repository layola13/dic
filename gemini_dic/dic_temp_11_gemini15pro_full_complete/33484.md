chymiferous:/ˌkaɪmɪˈfɪərəs/ | adj. | 产乳糜的；含乳糜的 | Containing chyle, a milky fluid consisting of lymph and emulsified fats formed in the small intestine during digestion and absorbed by the lacteals. | The chymiferous vessels carry chyle from the intestines. 乳糜管将乳糜从肠道输送出去。|  Synonyms: chyle-bearing, lacteal | Antonyms: achylous | adjective
