cover-up:/ˈkʌvər ʌp/ (US) /ˈkʌvə(r) ʌp/ (UK)| n. |掩盖；隐瞒；遮掩物|An attempt to hide something dishonest or illegal.|例句：The government tried to cover up the scandal. 政府试图掩盖这丑闻。|近义词：concealment, disguise, whitewash|反义词：disclosure, revelation|noun
cover-up:/ˈkʌvər ʌp/ (US) /ˈkʌvə(r) ʌp/ (UK)| v. |掩盖；隐瞒|To prevent something dishonest or illegal from becoming known.|例句：They tried to cover up their mistakes. 他们试图掩盖他们的错误。|近义词：conceal, hide, suppress|反义词：reveal, expose|verb
