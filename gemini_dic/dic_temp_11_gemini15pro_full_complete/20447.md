bitterish:/ˈbɪtərɪʃ/ | adj. | 微苦的，有点苦的 | Slightly bitter; having a somewhat bitter taste. | The coffee was a little bitterish this morning. 今早的咖啡有点苦。|  The grapefruit had a bitterish tang.  这柚子有点苦味。 | 近义词: slightly bitter, acrid, tart | 反义词: sweet, sugary | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From 'bitter' + '-ish' (suffix indicating a lesser degree of the quality).  'Bitter' comes from Old English 'biter', from Proto-Germanic '*bitraz', ultimately from Proto-Indo-European '*bheid-'. '-ish' is a native English suffix.
