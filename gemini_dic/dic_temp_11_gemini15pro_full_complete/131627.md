noncompoundable:/ˌnɒnkɒmˈpaʊndəbl/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːmˈpaʊndəbl/ | adj. | 不可复合的，不能与其他罪行合并处罚的 | Not capable of being compounded or settled; (of a criminal offense) so serious that it cannot be legally settled by agreement between the parties involved, but must be pursued through the courts. |  The judge ruled that the offense was noncompoundable and the case had to proceed to trial. 法官裁定该罪行不可复合，案件必须进行审判。

The severity of the crime made it noncompoundable.  罪行的严重性使其不可复合。 | 近义词: uncompromisable, irreconcilable | 反义词: compoundable, compromisable | 词性: adjective
