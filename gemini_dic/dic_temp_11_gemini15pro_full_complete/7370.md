animadvert:/ˌænɪmædˈvɜːrt/
verb
批评；非难；评论
To criticize or censure unfavorably; to comment critically on.
The teacher animadverted on the student's lack of effort. 老师批评了这个学生缺乏努力。
The critic animadverted severely on the play's poor plot.  评论家严厉地批评了这部戏糟糕的情节。
近义词: criticize, censure, rebuke, reprimand, denounce
反义词: praise, commend, laud, extol
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin animadvertere ("to turn one's mind to, attend to, consider, notice, perceive, remark upon"), from animus ("mind, spirit, courage") + advertere ("to turn to"), from ad- ("to, towards") + vertere ("to turn").
