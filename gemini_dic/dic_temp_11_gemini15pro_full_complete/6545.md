amylogen:/ˈæmɪləʊdʒɛn/| n.|淀粉生成素；淀粉原|A substance which forms starch, especially a starch-forming granule in a plant cell.|Amylogen is stored in the plastids of plant cells.|淀粉生成素储存在植物细胞的质体中。|Synonyms: starch former, starch precursor|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, potentially enzymes that break down starch)|Noun
