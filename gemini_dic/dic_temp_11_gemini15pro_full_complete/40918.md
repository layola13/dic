coolibah:/ˈkuːlɪbɑː/
noun
桉树的一种,尤指生长在澳大利亚的树种
A type of eucalyptus tree, especially one growing in Australia.
The coolibah tree provides shade in the scorching Australian outback.  酷利巴树为炎热的澳大利亚内陆地区提供阴凉。
Coolibah trees are highly drought-tolerant.  酷利巴树非常耐旱。
synonym: eucalyptus, gum tree
