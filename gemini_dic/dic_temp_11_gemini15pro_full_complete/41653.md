correi:/kɔˈreɪ/ /kəˈreɪ/| n.| 勘误表，正误表| A list of errors in a printed work, together with their corrections. | Example: The first edition of the book contained a correi listing the misspellings. 第一版书包含一个勘误表，列出了拼写错误。

Example:  A separate correi was issued to address the inaccuracies in the map.  另外发布了一份勘误表以解决地图中的错误。| 近义词: errata, corrigenda | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
