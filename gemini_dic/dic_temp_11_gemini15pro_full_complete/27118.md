calipee:/ˈkælɪpiː/
noun
印花棉布，卡里布印花棉布
A lightweight cotton fabric, often brightly printed, typically used for summer clothing.
Her summer dress was made of a light calipee. 她的夏装是用轻薄的印花棉布做的。
近义词: calico, chintz, cotton
反义词: None (lacks a direct opposite, could consider heavy fabrics like wool or leather)
词性: n.

calipee:/ˈkælɪpiː/
verb (rare)
用印花棉布装饰
To decorate or cover with calipee fabric.
They calipeed the chairs with bright floral patterns. 他们用鲜艳的花卉图案的印花棉布装饰椅子。
近义词: decorate, cover, adorn
反义词: strip, uncover
词性: v.
