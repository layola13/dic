bleachability:/ˌbliːtʃəˈbɪləti/
n.|可漂白性|The quality of being able to be bleached or whitened.  The ease with which a material can be bleached.|This fabric has excellent bleachability.  这种织物具有优异的可漂白性。|synonyms:  whitening potential, decolorizing potential|antonyms:  non-bleachable, colorfastness|noun
