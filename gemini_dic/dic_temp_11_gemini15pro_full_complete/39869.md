constellate:/ˈkɑːnstəleɪt/ /ˈkɒnstəleɪt/| v. |聚集，形成星座，组成 |To form into a cluster or group; to gather together.  The stars constellated in the night sky. 星星在夜空中形成了星座。|近义词：cluster, gather, assemble, congregate |反义词: disperse, scatter |词性:动词
constellate:/ˈkɑːnstəleɪt/ /ˈkɒnstəleɪt/| adj. |布满星星的，像星座一样的 |Resembling a constellation; studded with stars. The ceiling was constellated with tiny lights. 天花板上布满了小灯，就像星星一样。|近义词：starry, star-studded |反义词: - |词性:形容词
