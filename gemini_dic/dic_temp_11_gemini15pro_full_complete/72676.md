foaminesses:/ˈfoʊmɪnɪsɪz/
noun
泡沫状，起泡沫的性质
the quality of being foamy; the state of producing or containing foam
The foaminesses of the beer were impressive, with a thick head forming on top.  啤酒的泡沫很丰富，顶部形成了厚厚的一层泡沫。
Near synonyms: frothiness, effervescence
Near antonyms: flatness, stillness
词性: noun
