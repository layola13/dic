noun
醉酒状态；酗酒行为
The plural form of "booziness," referring to multiple instances or degrees of the state of being boozy.  It suggests an atmosphere or environment characterized by excessive drinking or the effects of alcohol.
The party was marked by several boozinesses, with guests becoming increasingly intoxicated throughout the evening.
Synonyms: drunkennesses, intoxications
Antonyms: sobriety, temperance
