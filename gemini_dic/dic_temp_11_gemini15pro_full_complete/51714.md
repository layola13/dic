devising:/dɪˈvaɪzɪŋ/
v.
设计; 构思; 发明; 策划;
Present participle of devise.  The act of creating or inventing something new.
They are devising a new marketing strategy. 他们正在制定新的营销策略。
Engineers are devising new ways to generate energy. 工程师们正在设计新的能源生产方法。
近义词:  creating, inventing, planning, formulating,  scheming,  concocting,  engineering
反义词:  copying, imitating,  following
词性: verb (present participle)
