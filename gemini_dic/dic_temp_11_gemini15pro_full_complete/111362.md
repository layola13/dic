liveried: /ˈlɪvərid/ | adj. | 穿制服的，穿着 livery 的 | Dressed in a livery; wearing a distinctive uniform. | The liveried chauffeur opened the door of the limousine. 穿制服的司机打开了豪华轿车的门。| The hotel employed liveried doormen. 这家酒店雇佣了穿制服的门卫。 | 近义词：uniformed, clad | 反义词: plain-clothed, civilian | 词性: adjective

词源: From the noun "livery," which comes from Old French "livrée," meaning "delivery" or "allowance of food and clothing."  Originally referred to the distinctive clothing given to servants or retainers of a noble household.
