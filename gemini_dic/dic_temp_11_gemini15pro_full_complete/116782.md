maven:/ˈmeɪvən/| n.|专家，内行|A person who is experienced or knowledgeable in a particular field.|例句：She's a maven in the fashion industry. 她在时尚界是个专家。

例句：He's a marketing maven, always coming up with new ideas. 他是一位营销专家，总是能想出新点子。|近义词：expert, specialist, authority, connoisseur, aficionado|反义词：amateur, novice, beginner|词性：名词

词源: From Yiddish meyvn, from Hebrew mevin 'one who understands'.
