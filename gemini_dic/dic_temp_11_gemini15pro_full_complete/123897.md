mordanted:/ˈmɔːrdəntɪd/| adj.| 媒染过的；被腐蚀的；辛辣的；尖刻的|Treated with a mordant; fixed (as color) by a mordant.  Figuratively, biting or sarcastic. | The mordanted fabric held the dye brilliantly.  媒染过的织物使染料保持鲜艳。| 近义词：fixed, etched, caustic, acrid, biting, sarcastic | 反义词: unfixed, bland, mild, gentle |词性:形容词


mordanted:/ˈmɔːrdəntɪd/| v.| 媒染；使固定（颜色）；腐蚀；使痛苦| To treat with a mordant; to fix (a color) with a mordant. | The artist mordanted the silk before applying the natural dyes.  艺术家在使用天然染料之前先对丝绸进行了媒染。| 近义词：fix, etch, corrode | 反义词: release, loosen |词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mordant*, present participle of *mordre* "to bite," from Latin *mordēre* "to bite."  The sense of "biting" or "sarcastic" is a figurative extension of the chemical meaning.
