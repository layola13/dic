flysheet:/ˈflaɪʃiːt/| n.|防雨布，防水布；帐单；传单|A piece of waterproof material used as a secondary outer covering on a tent, to protect it from rain.|We used a flysheet to protect our tent from the heavy rain. 我们用防雨布保护我们的帐篷免受大雨淋湿。|Synonyms: tarp, rain sheet, groundsheet|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "inner tent")|Noun
