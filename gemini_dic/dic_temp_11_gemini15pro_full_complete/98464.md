inspirit:/ɪnˈspɪrɪt/
verb
鼓舞，激励
to fill (someone) with courage, spirit, or hope
例句：The coach's speech inspirited the team before the big game. 教练的讲话在比赛前鼓舞了球队。

例句：The news of the victory inspirited the soldiers to fight on. 胜利的消息鼓舞了士兵们继续战斗。

近义词：encourage, motivate, inspire, uplift, hearten
反义词：discourage, dispirit, demoralize
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin inspirare "to breathe or blow into," figuratively "to inspire, influence," from in- "in, into" + spirare "to breathe."  The figurative sense of "animate, excite, encourage" developed in English in the late 16th century.
