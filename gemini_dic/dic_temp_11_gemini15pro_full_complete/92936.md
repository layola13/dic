identify:/aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ/ | v. | 识别，认出，确定 | To recognize somebody/something and be able to say who or what they are. |  The police officer identified the suspect from a photograph. 警察通过照片认出了嫌疑人。

identify:/aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ/ | v. | 发现，找到 | To find or discover something. |  Scientists have identified a new species of frog. 科学家们发现了一个新的蛙类物种。

identify:/aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ/ | v. | 使等同于；把…看成一样 | To consider somebody/something to be the same as somebody/something else. | Many people identify happiness with wealth. 许多人把幸福等同于财富。


近义词: recognize, pinpoint, determine, distinguish
反义词: confuse, mistake, misidentify
词性: verb (动词)

词源: Late 16th century: from late Latin identificare, from ident- ‘the same’ + -ificare (from facere ‘to make’).

identify with:  认同，感同身受 | I identify with the struggles of working mothers. 我对职业母亲的挣扎感同身受。
