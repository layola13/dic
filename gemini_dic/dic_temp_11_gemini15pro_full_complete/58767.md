dunk:/dʌŋk/| v. n. |扣篮，浸泡|To shoot a basketball by leaping high and throwing the ball down through the hoop, or to dip something into liquid.|He dunked the biscuit in his tea. 他把饼干浸泡在茶里。

He dunked the ball with impressive force. 他以惊人的力量扣篮。|近义词：dip, submerge, immerse (for dipping), slam (for basketball)|反义词: withdraw, remove|词性:动词, 名词


dunks:/dʌŋks/| n. |扣篮（复数）|Plural form of "dunk," referring to multiple instances of dunking a basketball.|He scored several dunks in the game. 他在比赛中完成了几次扣篮。|近义词：slams|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


词源:  From Middle Dutch *dunken "to dip," related to Old English *dyncan "to dip."
