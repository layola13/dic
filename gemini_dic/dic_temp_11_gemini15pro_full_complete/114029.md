magnateships:/ˈmæɡnətˌʃɪps/ | n. |大亨的地位；巨头的地位| The position or status of a magnate, often used in the plural.  A magnate is a person of great influence, importance, or power, especially in business or industry. |  The decline of traditional magnateships in the steel industry. 钢铁行业传统巨头地位的衰落。| synonyms: tycooneries, mogulships,  baronships (in a specific industry), leadership, dominance | antonyms: powerlessness, insignificance, subordination | noun
