overcommend: /ˌoʊvər kəˈmend/ | vt. | 过度称赞，过分表扬 | To praise excessively or unduly. | He overcommended her performance, making it seem better than it actually was. 他过分称赞了她的表演，使它看起来比实际情况要好得多。|  The manager overcommended the new employee, setting unrealistic expectations for his future performance.  经理过分表扬了新员工，对他未来的表现设定了不切实际的期望。| 近义词: overpraise, exaggerate, laud excessively | 反义词: underpraise, criticize, downplay | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "commend" (verb meaning "to praise")
