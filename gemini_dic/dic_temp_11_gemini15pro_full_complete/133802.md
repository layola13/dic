nongerminating:/ˌnɒnˈdʒɜːmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非发芽的，不发芽的
Not germinating; unable to sprout or grow.
The nongerminating seeds were discarded.  这些不发芽的种子被丢弃了。
The experiment focused on the reasons for the nongerminating condition of the seeds.  这个实验的重点是研究这些种子不发芽的原因。
Synonyms: dormant, inert, inactive
Antonyms: germinating, sprouting, growing
