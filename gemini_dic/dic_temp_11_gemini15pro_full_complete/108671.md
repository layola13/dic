leafy:/ˈliːfi/| adj.|多叶的；叶茂盛的；|Covered with leaves; having many leaves.|例句：The leafy trees provided welcome shade on a hot day.  炎热的天气里，枝繁叶茂的树木提供了宜人的阴凉。

例句：We walked down a leafy lane. 我们沿着一条绿树成荫的小路走着。|近义词：foliated, lush, verdant|反义词：bare, leafless|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lēafī*, from *lēaf* (leaf) + *-ī* (adjectival suffix).  Cognate with German *laubig*.
