charta:/ˈtʃɑːrtə/| n.|宪章，特许状|A formal written statement of the rights of a particular group of people, or of principles that should govern a particular area of activity.  A document granting certain specified rights, powers, privileges, or functions from the sovereign power of a state to an individual, corporation, city, etc.|例句：The Magna Carta is a famous historical charta.  大宪章是一部著名的历史性宪章。

例句：The company received a royal charta to establish a trading post.  该公司获得了建立贸易站的皇家特许状。|近义词：charter, constitution, statute|反义词:None|词性:noun
