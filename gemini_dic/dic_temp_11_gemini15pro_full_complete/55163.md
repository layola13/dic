dissembler:/dɪˈsɛmblər/| n. |伪君子，掩饰者|A person who conceals their true motives, feelings, or beliefs.|He was a dissembler, pretending to be their friend while secretly plotting against them. 他是个伪君子，假装是他们的朋友，却在暗中密谋反对他们。|近义词：hypocrite, pretender, liar, deceiver|反义词：honest person, truthful person|词性:noun

dissemble:/dɪˈsɛmbəl/| v. |掩饰，假装|To conceal one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs.|She dissembled her anger with a forced smile. 她强颜欢笑以掩饰她的愤怒。|近义词：feign, pretend, mask, disguise|反义词：reveal, expose, be honest|词性:verb
