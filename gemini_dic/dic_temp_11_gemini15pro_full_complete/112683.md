lowkey:/ˌloʊˈkiː/| adj. adv.|低调的，低调地|Not intended to attract a lot of attention; restrained or subdued.  Origin:  From hip-hop slang, likely a blend of "low" and "key."|例句：They had a lowkey wedding ceremony with only close family and friends. 他们举行了一个低调的婚礼，只邀请了亲朋好友。

例句：She wanted to keep the party lowkey, so she only invited a few people. 她想让派对保持低调，所以只邀请了几个人。|近义词：subtle, understated, quiet, discreet, chill|反义词：flashy, ostentatious, loud, flamboyant|词性: adjective, adverb
