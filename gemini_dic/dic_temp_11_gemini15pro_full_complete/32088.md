chessel:/ˈtʃɪzəl/ (US), /ˈtʃɪzəl/ (UK)| v.|凿；雕刻；骗取|To shape or cut something with a chisel; to obtain something by dishonest means.|She chiselled a small piece of wood from the block. 她从木块上凿下一小块木头.  He tried to chisel me out of my money. 他试图骗走我的钱。|近义词：carve, sculpt, swindle, cheat|反义词: N/A|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French cisel, from Vulgar Latin *cisellum, diminutive of Latin caesum, past participle of caedere "to cut."
