infeoffment:/ɪnˈfiːfmənt/ | n. |  封地；授予封地；封地证书 | The act of granting a fief or fee; the instrument or deed by which it is conveyed; the fief or fee itself. |  The king granted infeoffment of the land to his loyal knight. 国王将这块土地封给了他忠诚的骑士。 | 近义词: enfeoffment, investiture | 反义词: dispossession, divestiture | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Old French *infeffement, from infeffer "to enfeoff," from en- "in" + fief "fief." Ultimately from Latin feudum.
