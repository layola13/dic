dolloped:/ˈdɒləpt/ (UK) /ˈdɑːləpt/ (US)| v. |舀，盛，放；（随意地）大量给予|To serve or place (a soft or sticky substance) in a large, shapeless mass.|She dolloped a large spoonful of whipped cream onto the pie. 她往馅饼上舀了一大勺生奶油。|Near synonyms:  spooned, plopped, ladled, heaped|Antonyms:  removed, scraped, skimmed|verb

dollop:/ˈdɒləp/ (UK) /ˈdɑːləp/ (US)| n. |一团，一勺，一堆|A large, shapeless mass of a soft or sticky substance.|A dollop of sour cream goes well with this dish.  这道菜配上一团酸奶油味道会更好。|Near synonyms:  spoonful, blob, lump, mound|Antonyms:  crumb, speck, sliver|noun
