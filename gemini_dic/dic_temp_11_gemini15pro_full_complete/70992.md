firewalls:/ˈfaɪərwɔːlz/ (US), /ˈfaɪəwɔːlz/ (UK)| n.|防火墙|A system designed to prevent unauthorized access to or from a private network.|Example: The company installed firewalls to protect its data from hackers. 公司安装了防火墙来保护其数据免受黑客攻击。|Example:  Strong firewalls are essential for online security.  强大的防火墙对于在线安全至关重要。|近义词：security system, network protection|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps) vulnerability, open access|Noun
