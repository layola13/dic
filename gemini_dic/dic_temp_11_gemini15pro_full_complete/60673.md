electrepeter: /ɪˌlɛktriˈpiːtər/ | n. | 电复读机; 电覆诵机 | A device that electronically records and repeats sounds, typically used for language learning. |  Example: The student used an electrepeter to practice his pronunciation.  这个学生用电复读机练习发音。 | Example:  Electrepeters were popular before digital language learning tools became common. 在数字语言学习工具普及之前，电复读机很受欢迎。 | Synonyms:  language learning device, audio repeater, sound recorder | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider: silent reading, textbook) | noun
