bessie:/ˈbɛsi/| n.|贝西（女子名）|A diminutive of Elizabeth, meaning "God is my oath". It is often used as a name for cows, especially in children's literature.|Bessie the cow jumped over the moon. 贝西这头牛跳过了月亮。|近义词：Liz, Beth, Betty (as diminutives of Elizabeth); Cow (if referring to an animal)|反义词: N/A|noun
