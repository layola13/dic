loom:/luːm/
n.|织布机|A machine for weaving cloth.|The weaver sat at her loom.|近义词: weaving machine|反义词: None|词性: 名词

loom:/luːm/
vi.|隐约出现；逼近|To appear large and indistinct.|Dark clouds loomed on the horizon.|近义词: emerge, appear, threaten|反义词: recede, disappear|词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *geloma, "tool, implement," of Germanic origin. The sense of "loom" as a weaving machine developed later.
