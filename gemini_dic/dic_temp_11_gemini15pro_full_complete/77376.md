gaunter:/ˈɡɔːntər/ | adj. | 更瘦的，更憔悴的 |  (comparative of gaunt) thinner and more haggard than usual, especially from suffering, hunger, or age. | He looked gaunter than the last time I saw him. 他看起来比我上次见到他时更憔悴了。| synonyms: thinner, leaner, skinnier, more emaciated, more haggard | antonyms: fatter, plumper, heavier, healthier | adjective
