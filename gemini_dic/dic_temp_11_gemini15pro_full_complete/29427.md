catalectic: /ˌkætəˈlɛktɪk/
catalectic: adj.
catalectic: 缺音节的，诗行结尾缺少一个或多个音节的
catalectic:  A catalectic line of verse is missing one or more syllables at the end, especially in comparison to the normal or complete line.
catalectic: The final line of the poem was catalectic, giving it an abrupt, unfinished feel.
catalectic: 近义词: truncated, incomplete
catalectic: 反义词: acatalectic, complete
