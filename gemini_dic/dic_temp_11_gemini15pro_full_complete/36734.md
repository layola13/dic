colloidality:/ˌkɒlɔɪˈdælɪti/ | n. | 胶体性，胶态 | The state or quality of being a colloid.  A colloid is a mixture in which one substance of microscopically dispersed insoluble particles is suspended throughout another substance. | The colloidality of the mixture was determined by light scattering experiments. 混合物的胶体性是通过光散射实验确定的。 | synonyms: colloidal state, colloidal nature | antonyms: non-colloidal state | noun
