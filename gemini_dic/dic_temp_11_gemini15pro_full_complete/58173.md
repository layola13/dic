drownage:/ˈdraʊnɪdʒ/ | n. |溺死，溺毙 | Death caused by drowning. | The coroner attributed the death to drownage.  验尸官将死因归结为溺水。| 近义词: drowning, asphyxiation | 反义词: survival, resuscitation | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From drown + -age.  From Middle English *drounen, from Old Norse *drukkna (“to drown”), from Proto-Germanic *drunknaną (“to sink, drown”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)treg- (“to turn, twist”).
