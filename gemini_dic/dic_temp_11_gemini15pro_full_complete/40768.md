convincednesses:/kənˈvɪnstnɪsɪz/
|n.|确信，深信；坚信不疑（通常用复数形式）|The state of being convinced; firm belief.  A collection or aggregation of instances of being convinced.|He spoke with such conviction and convincednesses that everyone believed him. 他说话如此坚定，充满自信，让每个人都相信了他。|近义词：certainties, convictions, assurances|反义词：doubts, uncertainties|词性:名词

词源:
From convincedness + -es (plural suffix).  Convincedness itself comes from convinced (past participle of convince) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin convincere "to overcome, conquer, convict."
