muscogees:/məˈskoʊɡiz/ | n. | 马斯科吉人；克里克人 | A member of a Native American people formerly inhabiting Alabama and Georgia, now living mainly in Oklahoma.  A Muskogean language spoken by the Muscogee people. | The Muscogees were forced to relocate to Oklahoma during the Trail of Tears. 马斯科吉人在“血泪之路”期间被迫迁移到俄克拉荷马州。| The Muscogees have a rich cultural heritage. 马斯科吉人有着丰富的文化遗产。 | synonyms: Creek, Muskogee (singular), Mvskoke | antonyms: N/A | noun
