bloater:/ˈbloʊtər/| n.| 膨胀物，臃肿物；（尤指腌制熏干的）鲱鱼|A bloater is something that has become swollen or bloated. It can also refer to a type of smoked herring.|The software had become a bloater, full of unnecessary features.|近义词：pufferfish, swellfish, distended, bloated|反义词: deflated, shrunk|词性: noun
bloat:/bloʊt/| v.  n.|（使）膨胀，肿胀；|To bloat is to swell or puff up, typically due to gas or fluid. As a noun, it refers to the state of being bloated.|The gas made my stomach bloat.|近义词：swell, distend, inflate|反义词: deflate, shrink|词性: verb, noun
