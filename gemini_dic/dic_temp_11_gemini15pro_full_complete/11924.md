arsenate:/ˈɑːrsənɪt/ |ˈɑːrsnɪt/ | n. |砷酸盐；砷酸酯|A salt or ester of arsenic acid. |The arsenate is a toxic substance. 这种砷酸盐是一种有毒物质。|Arsenate contaminated the groundwater. 砷酸盐污染了地下水。|近义词: arsenic compound |反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially a reducing agent in a chemical context)|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *arséniate*, from arsenic + -ate. Ultimately from Old French arsenic, from Medieval Latin arsenicum, from Arabic *al-zarnik* (الزرنيخ), from Persian *zarnik* (زرنيخ, “orpiment, gold paint”), from *zar* (زر, “gold”) + *nik* (نيك, “good”).
