over-talked:/ˌoʊvərˈtɔːkt/| adj.|说得过多的，过度讨论的|Having talked too much or excessively, often to the point of being tedious or overwhelming.  The issue was over-talked and no clear conclusion was reached.|例句：The meeting was over-talked and unproductive. 会议讨论过度，没有成果。

例句：I felt over-talked and exhausted after the conference. 会议结束后，我觉得说得太多，筋疲力尽。|近义词：overdiscussed, overanalyzed|反义词：undiscussed, unexplored|词性:形容词
