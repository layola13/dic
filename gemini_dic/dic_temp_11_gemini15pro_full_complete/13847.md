auger:/ˈɔːɡər/ (US) /ˈɔːɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. |螺旋钻,  钻孔机|A tool with a helical bit for boring holes, especially in wood or ice. |例句：He used an auger to drill a hole for the fence post. 他用螺旋钻钻了一个洞来立栅栏柱子。

例句：The ice auger made quick work of creating a fishing hole. 冰钻很快地钻出了一个钓鱼洞。|近义词：drill, borer, gimlet|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
