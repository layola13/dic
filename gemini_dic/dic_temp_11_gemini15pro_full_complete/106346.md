kokama:/koʊˈkɑːmə/ | n. | 科卡马人；科卡马语 |  An indigenous people and their language from the Amazon region of Brazil. | The Kokama people live along the Solimões River. 科卡马人生活在索利蒙伊斯河沿岸。| Kokama is a Tupian language. 科卡马语是图皮语系的一种语言。 | synonyms: (for people) indigenous group, tribe; (for language) Tupian language | antonyms: N/A | noun

kokama:/koʊˈkɑːmə/ | adj. | 科卡马人的；科卡马语的 |  Relating to the Kokama people or their language. |  The Kokama culture is rich in traditions. 科卡马文化富有传统。 | The Kokama language is endangered. 科卡马语濒临灭绝。| synonyms: indigenous, tribal | antonyms: N/A | adjective
