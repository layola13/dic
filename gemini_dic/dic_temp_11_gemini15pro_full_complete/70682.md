finalises:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
完成；最终确定；定稿
puts the finishing touches to a plan, agreement, etc.
The contract was finalised last week.  上周合同最终敲定。
synonyms: complete, conclude, settle
antonyms: begin, start, commence
词性: 动词(verb)
