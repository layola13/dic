gypper's:/ˈɡɪpərz/| n.|骗子, 敲诈勒索者(的)|A gypper is someone who cheats or swindles others, often through deceptive practices. Gypper's is the possessive form, referring to something belonging to a gypper.|That's the gypper's car. 那是那个骗子的车。|Synonyms: swindler's, cheat's, con artist's, fraudster's|Antonyms: honest person's, philanthropist's|noun
