coproducing:/ˌkəʊprəˈdjuːsɪŋ/
verb
共同制作；联合制作
The film was coproduced by France and Italy. 这部电影是由法国和意大利联合制作的。
coproducing derives from the prefix "co-" (meaning "together" or "jointly") and the verb "produce" (meaning "to create or manufacture").  The prefix "co-" alters the meaning of "produce" to indicate a joint effort in creation or manufacturing.
例句：
They are coproducing a new play. 他们正在联合制作一部新戏。

The two companies are coproducing a new television series. 这两家公司正在联合制作一部新的电视剧。

近义词：collaborating, joint producing
反义词：solely producing
