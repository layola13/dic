kerosines:/ˈkɛrəˌsiːnz/
n.
煤油（通常用 kerosene，kerosines 较少用）
A thin, clear liquid oil used as a fuel in jet engines and some lamps.  Kerosines is a less common plural form.
The old lamp used kerosines.  这盏旧灯使用煤油。(更常见的说法是 The old lamp used kerosene.)
Synonym: kerosene, paraffin, fuel oil
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific fuel)
Noun
