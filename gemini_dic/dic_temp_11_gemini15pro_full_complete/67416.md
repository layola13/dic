externs:/ˈɛkstɜːrnz/ | n. |外部变量；外部程序| Variables or functions defined outside the current module or program. |  The program relies on several externs declared in a separate header file.  该程序依赖于在单独的头文件中声明的几个外部变量。 | synonyms: external variables, global variables | antonyms: local variables | noun

externs:/ˈɛkstɜːrnz/ | adj. |外部的| Relating to something outside the current module or program. | This function calls an extern subroutine. 这个函数调用一个外部子程序。| synonyms: external, outside | antonyms: internal, inside | adjective
