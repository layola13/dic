free-form:/ˈfriːfɔːrm/| adj.|自由格式的，非正式的|Not following a strict or formal structure; allowing for variation and improvisation. |The artist preferred free-form sculpture, unbound by traditional rules.  这位艺术家更喜欢自由形式的雕塑，不受传统规则的束缚。|例句：The workshop encouraged free-form discussion among the participants. 研讨会鼓励参与者之间自由讨论。|近义词：unstructured, informal, open-ended|反义词:structured, formal, rigid|词性:形容词

free-form:/ˈfriːfɔːrm/| n.|自由格式|Something that is free-form, such as a piece of art or a type of writing. |The architect's design was a free-form structure, unlike anything she'd created before.  这位建筑师的设计是一个自由形式的结构，与她以前创作的任何东西都不一样。|例句：The poem was a free-form expression of her emotions. 这首诗是她情感的自由表达。|近义词：improvisation, ad-lib|反义词:structure, framework|词性:名词
