maxfps:/mæks ɛf piː ɛs/ |n.| 最大帧率 |Maximum Frames Per Second. It refers to the highest number of frames that a graphics card, software, or display can produce or process per second.  It impacts the smoothness and visual quality of motion in videos and games.| The game's maxfps is capped at 60.  这款游戏的最大帧率限制在60帧。 | synonyms: frame rate limit, maximum frame rate | antonyms: minfps (minimum frames per second) | noun
