amiabler:/ˈeɪmiəblər/ | adj. | 更和蔼的，更友善的 |  Comparative form of amiable: more amiable. Having or displaying a friendly and pleasant manner. |  She's become much amiabler since she started her new job. 她自从开始新工作后变得更加和蔼可亲了。|  He was always amiabler after a good meal.  他饱餐一顿后总是更加和蔼。| 近义词: friendlier, more agreeable, more affable, more cordial | 反义词: less amiable, unfriendlier, less agreeable, more hostile | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Middle French amiable, from Late Latin amicabilis (“friendly”), from Latin amicus (“friend”) + -abilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning "able to be," "worthy of being," "tending to," "having the quality of").
