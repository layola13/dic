mis-joins:/ˌmɪsˈdʒɔɪnz/
noun
错误连接，连接错误
incorrectly joining parts, especially of data in a database.
The database suffered from several mis-joins, leading to inaccurate reports. 数据库中存在一些错误连接，导致报告不准确。
mismatches, incorrect linkages
correct joins, accurate linkages
