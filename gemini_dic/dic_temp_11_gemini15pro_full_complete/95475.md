incito:/ɪnˈsaɪtoʊ/ /ɪnˈsiːtoʊ/
verb
煽动，激起，激励
to encourage or urge (someone) to do something, especially something wrong or violent.
The politician's fiery speech incited the crowd to riot.  这位政客慷慨激昂的演讲煽动了群众暴乱。
The propaganda was designed to incite hatred against the enemy. 这份宣传旨在煽动对敌人的仇恨。
近义词:provoke, stimulate, instigate, goad, encourage
反义词:deter, dissuade, pacify, calm, soothe
词性:v.

incito:/ɪnˈsaɪtoʊ/ /ɪnˈsiːtoʊ/
词源: From Latin incitō (“I urge on, rouse, excite”), frequentative of inciō (“I begin, commence, undertake”), from in (“in, on, upon”) + cieō (“I set in motion, stir, rouse”).
词性:v.
