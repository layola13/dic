nitrosalicylic:/ˌnaɪtroʊˌsælɪˈsɪlɪk/ | adj. |硝基水杨酸的；含硝基水杨酸的 | Containing or relating to nitrosalicylic acid, a compound used in analytical chemistry, especially for determining reducing sugars. | Nitrosalicylic acid reagent is used to measure the enzyme activity. 硝基水杨酸试剂用于测量酶活性。 | 近义词: (No direct synonyms, but related terms include) 3,5-dinitrosalicylic acid (DNS), DNSA | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

nitrosalicylic acid:/ˌnaɪtroʊˌsælɪˈsɪlɪk ˈæsɪd/ | n. | 硝基水杨酸 | A yellow crystalline solid used as a reagent for detecting reducing sugars.  Specifically, 3,5-dinitrosalicylic acid is commonly used.  | The nitrosalicylic acid assay is a common method for measuring enzyme activity. 硝基水杨酸测定法是一种常用的酶活性测量方法。 | 近义词: DNS, DNSA, 3,5-dinitrosalicylic acid | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
