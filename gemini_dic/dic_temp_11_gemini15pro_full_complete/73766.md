formaldehyde:/fɔːrˈmældaɪhaɪd/ /fɔːrˈmæl.də.haɪd/
n.|甲醛|A colorless pungent gas in solution made by oxidizing methanol. It is used as a disinfectant and preservative, and in making synthetic resins.|Formaldehyde is used in the manufacture of many industrial products.|近义词:methanal|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
