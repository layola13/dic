outstagger:/ˌaʊtˈstæɡər/ | v. | 超越，胜过，（尤指在耐力方面）比…坚持更久 | To surpass or outdo, especially in endurance. | The marathon runner outstaggered his opponents and won the race. 马拉松选手超越了他的对手，赢得了比赛。| The team outstaggered all expectations and made it to the finals.  这个团队超出了所有人的预期，进入了决赛。 | 近义词: outlast, surpass, outdo, overcome | 反义词: succumb, yield, surrender | 词性: 动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "stagger" (verb meaning "to walk or move unsteadily, as if about to fall").  The combination creates the sense of exceeding someone in stamina, especially when both parties are near exhaustion, like staggering.
