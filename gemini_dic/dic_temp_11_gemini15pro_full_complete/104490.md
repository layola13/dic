kakuei: /kɑːkuːeɪ/ | n. |  角榮 (Kakuei Tanaka, a former Prime Minister of Japan) |  Refers to Kakuei Tanaka (田中角榮), who served as Prime Minister of Japan from 1972 to 1974.  He is a controversial figure known for his rapid economic policies and involvement in the Lockheed bribery scandals. | He was often referred to simply as "Kakuei" by those who knew him.  田中角榮常被熟人簡稱為“角榮”。| Synonym: Tanaka Kakuei | Antonym: N/A | proper noun
