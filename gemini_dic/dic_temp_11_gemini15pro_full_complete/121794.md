misrecords:/ˌmɪsrɪˈkɔːrdz/
|v. (third-person singular simple present misrecords, present participle misrecording, simple past and past participle misrecorded)|错误记录；记错|To record something incorrectly, either by writing it down wrong or by inputting it incorrectly into a system.|The clerk misrecorded the customer's order, leading to a delay in delivery. 店员记错了顾客的订单，导致交货延迟。|misenters, misreports, misstates|correctly records, accurately documents|verb

misrecord:/ˌmɪsrɪˈkɔːrd/
|n.|错误记录|An instance of recording something incorrectly.|The misrecord in the database caused a significant accounting error. 数据库中的错误记录导致了一个严重的会计错误。|error, mistake, inaccuracy|correct record, accurate entry|noun
