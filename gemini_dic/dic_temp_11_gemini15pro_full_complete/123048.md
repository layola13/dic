monesia:/məˈniːʒə/ /məˈniːziə/| n. |莫尼西娅树皮提取物；莫尼西娅|An extract from the bark of the monesia tree, used medicinally, especially as an astringent and antiseptic. |Monesia is used in some traditional medicines to treat diarrhea. 莫尼西娅在一些传统药物中用于治疗腹泻。|Monesia bark extract has shown promising results in laboratory studies for its antimicrobial properties. 莫尼西娅树皮提取物在其抗菌特性方面在实验室研究中显示出有希望的结果。|近义词: astringent, antiseptic |反义词: None |词性:noun
