ergonomically:/ˌɜːrɡəˈnɒmɪkli/ | adv. | 符合人体工程学地 | Designed or arranged to be efficient and comfortable for the user. |  This chair is ergonomically designed to support your back. 这把椅子符合人体工程学设计，可以支撑你的背部。 | The keyboard is ergonomically positioned to minimize strain on your wrists.  这款键盘符合人体工学定位，可最大限度地减少手腕的压力。| 近义词: comfortably, efficiently, user-friendly | 反义词: uncomfortably, inefficiently, awkwardly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "ergonomics" + "-ically".  "Ergonomics" comes from Greek "ergon" (work) + "nomos" (law).
