hyperrationally:/ˌhaɪpərˈræʃənəli/ | adv. | 超理性地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive or exaggerated rationality, often to the point of neglecting or dismissing emotions, intuition, or other non-logical factors.  It implies an overly analytical approach to decision-making. | He hyperrationally analyzed every possible outcome before making a simple decision. 他在做一个简单的决定之前，超理性地分析了每一个可能的结果。|  He hyperrationally calculated the risks and benefits of every option, ignoring his gut feeling. 他超理性地计算了每个选项的风险和收益，忽略了他的直觉。 | 近义词: excessively rationally, over-analytically | 反义词: irrationally, emotionally | 词性: 副词
