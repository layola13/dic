habituate:/həˈbɪtʃueɪt/ /həˈbɪtʃəˌweɪt/| vt.|使习惯于|to accustom by frequent repetition or prolonged exposure|The soldiers were habituated to long marches.|近义词: accustom, inure, adapt, condition|反义词: unaccustomed|词性:动词

habituate:/həˈbɪtʃueɪt/ /həˈbɪtʃəˌweɪt/| vi.|习惯于|to become habituated|She soon habituated to the climate.|近义词: acclimatize, adjust|反义词: unaccustomed|词性:动词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin *habituatus, past participle of habituare, from habitus ‘condition, appearance, dress’ (see habit).
