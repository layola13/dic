amphictyonic:/æmfɪkˈtaɪənɪk/
adjective
古希腊城邦联盟的; 宗教联盟的
Relating to an amphictyony, an ancient Greek association of neighboring states for the protection of a religious sanctuary.
The amphictyonic council met regularly to discuss matters of mutual interest.  古希腊城邦联盟议会定期开会讨论共同关心的问题。

The Delphic Amphictyony was one of the most powerful in ancient Greece.  德尔斐联盟是古希腊最有权势的联盟之一。

Synonym: federal, allied, confederate
Antonym: independent, separate, disunited
词性: adjective
