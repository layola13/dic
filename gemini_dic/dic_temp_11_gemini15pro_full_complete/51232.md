destroying:/dɪˈstrɔɪɪŋ/
v.
摧毁，毁灭
the process of damaging something so badly that it cannot be used or no longer exists.
The fire was destroying the forest. 大火正在摧毁森林。
demolishing, ruining, wrecking
preserving, creating
