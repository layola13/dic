blinds:/blaɪndz/
n.
窗帘；百叶窗；遮光物
Window coverings, typically thin slats of wood, metal, or plastic, that can be adjusted to control light and privacy.
She closed the blinds to block out the afternoon sun. 她拉上百叶窗以遮挡下午的阳光。

近义词：shades, shutters, curtains
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) open window, uncovered window
词性: 名词


blinds:/blaɪndz/
v.
使失明；使目眩；使失去判断力
To deprive of sight, dazzle, or deprive of judgment or understanding.
The sudden flash of light blinded him momentarily. 突如其来的闪光使他暂时失明。

近义词：dazzles, obscures
反义词:enlighten, illuminate
词性: 动词


词源: From Old English *blindsian, frequentative of *blindan "to make blind," from Proto-Germanic *blindjanan, from *blindaz "blind."
