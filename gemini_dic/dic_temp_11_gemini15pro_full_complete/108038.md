latch:/lætʃ/ | n. v. |闩锁，门闩；抓住，锁住 |A fastening or lock consisting of a bar that fits into a notch or slot and is secured with a catch, or a spring-loaded bolt operated by a handle or key.  To fasten or secure something with a latch.|  She lifted the latch and opened the gate. 她 подняла засов и открыла калитку.  He latched the door securely before leaving. 他走之前牢牢地锁上了门。 |近义词：bolt, lock, clasp, fastener, secure |反义词: unlatch, open, release |词性: noun, verb

latch:/lætʃ/ | v. |抓住，锁住 |To grab or attach oneself to something firmly. To become firmly attached or embedded.|The baby latched onto its mother's finger. 婴儿紧紧抓住母亲的手指。 The idea latched onto his mind and wouldn't let go. 这个想法牢牢地印在他的脑海里，挥之不去。|近义词：grasp, grip, cling, fasten |反义词: release, unlatch, detach |词性: verb
