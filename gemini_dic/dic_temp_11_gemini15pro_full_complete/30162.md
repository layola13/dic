celebrates: /ˈsɛləˌbreɪts/ (US) /ˈsɛlɪbreɪts/ (UK)| v. |庆祝，庆贺| marks a special occasion with festivities or other activity. |例句：The city celebrates its founding day every year.  这个城市每年都庆祝它的建市日。

例句：We celebrated our anniversary with a romantic dinner. 我们用一顿浪漫的晚餐庆祝了我们的结婚纪念日。


近义词：commemorates, observes, honors|反义词:mourns, grieves|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *celebratus, past participle of celebrare "to frequent, honor, praise," from Latin celeber "frequented, populous, famous, renowned," of uncertain origin.
