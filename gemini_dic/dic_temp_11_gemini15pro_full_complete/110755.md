lingoes:/ˈlɪŋɡoʊz/ | n. | 灵格斯；语言；行话 | Lingoes is a dictionary and translation software. 灵格斯是一款词典和翻译软件。| Lingoes supports many languages. 灵格斯支持多种语言。 | 近义词: dictionary, translator, lexicon | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  Likely a playful alteration of "lingo," which refers to a specialized vocabulary or jargon.  "Lingo" itself comes from Italian *lingua* meaning "tongue, language," ultimately from Latin *lingua*.
