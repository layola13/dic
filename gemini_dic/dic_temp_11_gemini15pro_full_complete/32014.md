cheque:/tʃek/ (British English), /tʃɛk/ (American English)| n.|支票|A written order to a bank to pay a stated sum from the drawer's account.|例句：Could you cash this cheque for me, please?  可以帮我兑现这张支票吗？

例句：I wrote him a cheque for £500. 我给他开了一张500英镑的支票。

近义词：check (American English), draft, money order|反义词: cash|词性:noun
