iron-duke: /ˈaɪən djuːk/
noun
铁公爵（指惠灵顿公爵）
nickname of Arthur Wellesley, 1st Duke of Wellington, known for his firmness and determination, likened to iron.
The Iron Duke led his troops to victory at Waterloo. 铁公爵率领他的部队在滑铁卢战役中取得了胜利。
Synonyms: Duke of Wellington, Arthur Wellesley
Antonyms: N/A
