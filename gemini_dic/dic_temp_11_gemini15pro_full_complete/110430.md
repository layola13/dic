likelier:/ˈlaɪkliər/ | adj. | 更可能的 | More likely; more probable. | It's likelier that she'll arrive tomorrow than today. 她明天比今天来的可能性更大。|  It is likelier that the rain will stop soon. 雨很快就会停的可能性更大。 | 近义词:more probable, more likely | 反义词:less likely, less probable | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *liklier, comparative of likely; equivalent to likely + -er.  Ultimately from Old Norse líkligr (“likely”).
