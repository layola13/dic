non-lutheran:/ˌnɒn ˈluːθərən/ | /ˌnɑːn ˈluːθərən/
adj. n.|非路德教的；非路德教徒|Not pertaining to or characteristic of Lutheranism or its adherents.  A person who is not a Lutheran.|
The church welcomed both Lutheran and non-Lutheran members.  这个教堂欢迎路德教徒和非路德教徒成员。
He identified as a non-Lutheran Christian. 他认为自己是非路德派的基督徒。
|近义词:non-protestant, non-denominational|反义词:Lutheran|词性:形容词，名词
