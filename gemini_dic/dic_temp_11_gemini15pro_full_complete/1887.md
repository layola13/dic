acrotomous:/əˈkrɒtəməs/| adj.|顶生的，顶端的|Growing from the apex or tip, especially of a fruit.|The acrotomous growth of the plant was unusual.  这种植物的顶端生长很不寻常。|近义词：apical, terminal|反义词：basal|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Greek *ákros* (topmost, outermost) + *-tomos* (cutting, cut).
