exhaling:/ɛksˈheɪlɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
呼气; 呼吸出
the process of breathing out air from the lungs.
She was exhaling slowly, trying to calm down. 她慢慢地呼气，试图让自己平静下来。
The yoga instructor told us to focus on our inhaling and exhaling. 瑜伽教练告诉我们专注于吸气和呼气。
近义词:breathing out,  expiring
反义词:inhaling, inspiring
词性:v.
