microphonous:/maɪˈkrɒfənəs/ | adj. | 传声的，声音放大的 |  Amplifying sound; transmitting sound with increased volume.  Used to describe something that amplifies or transmits sound well, often referring to spaces or materials that carry sound easily. | The hall was strangely microphonous, every whisper could be heard at the back.  这个大厅出奇地传声，每个低语在后面都能听到。

The domed ceiling made the room microphonous.  穹顶天花板使房间传声。 | 近义词: resonant, amplifying, sound-carrying | 反义词: soundproof, sound-absorbing | 词性: adjective
