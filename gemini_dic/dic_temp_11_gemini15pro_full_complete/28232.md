capstan:/ˈkæpstən/ (US), /ˈkæpstn/ (UK)| n.|绞盘；起锚机|A capstan is a rotating drum or cylinder with a vertical axis, used for winding rope or cable, particularly in nautical settings. It provides leverage for tasks such as raising anchors or mooring ships.|The sailors used the capstan to raise the anchor.|Synonyms: winch, windlass|Antonyms: N/A|noun
