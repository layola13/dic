kvetcher:/ˈkvɛtʃər/ | n. | 抱怨者，爱发牢骚的人 | A person who complains habitually, especially about petty matters. | My aunt is a real kvetcher; she complains about everything. 我阿姨真是个爱抱怨的人；她对什么都抱怨。|  He's such a kvetcher! He never has anything positive to say. 他真是个爱抱怨的人！他从来没有什么积极的话要说。| complainer, whiner, grumbler |  optimist, enthusiast | noun
