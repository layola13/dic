champignon:/ʃæmˈpɪnjən/, /ˌʃɑːmpɪˈnjɔːn/ | n. |蘑菇，香菇 | An edible fungus, typically white, with a stalk and domed cap.  |例句：I added sliced champignons to the pasta sauce. 我在意大利面酱里加了切片的香菇。

例句：The dish was garnished with sautéed champignons. 这道菜用炒香菇装饰。|近义词：mushroom, fungus |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology->
From French *champignon*, ultimately from Late Latin *campinolius* "of the fields," from *campus* "field."
