eusebian: /juːˈsiːbiən/
adjective
尤西比乌斯的；尤西比乌斯派的
Relating to Eusebius of Caesarea, a 4th-century bishop and historian of the Christian Church.
Eusebius's writings are a valuable source of early Christian history. 尤西比乌斯的著作是早期基督教历史的宝贵资料。
His approach to church history was very Eusebian, focusing on the triumph of Christianity. 他对教会历史的研究方法非常具有尤西比乌斯的风格，侧重于基督教的胜利。
Near Synonyms:  patristic, early Christian
Antonyms:  non-Christian, pagan
词性: adjective
