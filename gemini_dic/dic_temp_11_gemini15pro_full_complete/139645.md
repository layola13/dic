occulting:/əˈkʌltɪŋ/
verb/adjective
掩蔽，掩星的
covering or hiding from view; eclipsing.
The moon is occulting the star. 月球正在遮蔽这颗恒星.
synonyms: eclipsing, covering, hiding, obscuring, blocking
antonyms: revealing, uncovering, exposing
verb

occulting:/əˈkʌltɪŋ/
adjective
掩蔽的，掩星的
relating to the act of occulting.
An occulting light is used for navigation.  掩星灯用于导航。
synonyms: eclipsing, covering, hiding, obscuring, blocking
antonyms: revealing, uncovering, exposing
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *occultare*, frequentative of occulere "to cover over, conceal," from ob "over" + celare "to hide."
