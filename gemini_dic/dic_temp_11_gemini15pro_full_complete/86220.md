heathered:/ˈheðərd/| adj.|石南花色的，掺杂的，混色的|Having a mixed or mottled coloring, often resembling the colors of heather.  Originally referring to yarn or fabric woven with differently colored strands, especially shades of grey and brown. Now also used more generally to describe anything with this type of coloring.|例句：She wore a heathered grey sweater. 她穿着一件石南灰色的毛衣。

例句：The walls were painted a heathered beige. 墙壁被漆成了掺杂米色的颜色。|近义词：mottled, variegated, flecked, mingled|反义词：solid, uniform, monochrome|词性: adjective

词源: From heather + -ed.  Heather refers to a type of low-growing shrub with small purplish-pink flowers.  The term “heathered” originally described yarn or fabric made from a mixture of different colored wool or fibers, often including the naturally occurring shades of heather plants.
