creatinine: /kriˈætɪnɪn/ | n. |肌酐|A waste product produced by muscles and filtered from the blood by the kidneys. High levels of creatinine in the blood can indicate kidney problems.| Blood tests showed elevated creatinine levels. 血液测试显示肌酐水平升高。|Synonyms:  None (as it's a specific chemical compound)| Antonyms: None| Noun
