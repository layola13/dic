detainer:/dɪˈteɪnər/
n.|扣押; 拘留令; 扣留者|A writ authorizing the further detention of a person already in custody.|The police issued a detainer for the suspect.|近义词：warrant, hold, custody|反义词:release|词性:名词

detainer:/dɪˈteɪnər/
n.|扣押物; 阻碍物|Something that detains or holds back.|The fallen tree acted as a detainer, blocking the road.|近义词：obstacle, impediment, barrier|反义词:aid, assistance|词性:名词
