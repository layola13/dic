eudemonistical:/ˌjuːdɪmɒˈnɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 幸福主义的；快乐主义的 | Of or relating to eudemonism; based on the pursuit of happiness and well-being.  |  He held a eudemonistical view of morality, believing that actions are right if they promote happiness. 他持有幸福主义的道德观，认为如果行为能促进幸福，那么这些行为就是正确的。| 近义词: hedonistic, utilitarian | 反义词: ascetic | 词性: 形容词

eudemonism:/ˌjuːdɪˈmɒnɪzəm/ | n. | 幸福主义；快乐主义 |  A system of ethics that bases morality on the pursuit of happiness or well-being. |  Eudemonism emphasizes the importance of living a fulfilling life. 幸福主义强调过一种充实生活的重要性。| 近义词: hedonism, utilitarianism | 反义词: asceticism | 词性: 名词
