nonrurally:/nɒnˈrʊərəli/
adverb|非农村地，在城市地|Not in a rural area; in or characteristic of a city or town.|Many young people are choosing to live nonrurally for better job opportunities. 许多年轻人为了更好的工作机会选择在城市生活。|Example:  Access to healthcare is generally better nonrurally.  在城市，医疗保健的可及性通常更好。|Synonyms: urbanly, in town, in the city|Antonyms: rurally, in the country, in the countryside
Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "rural" (from Latin "ruralis" meaning "of the countryside") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
