bunchily:/ˈbʌntʃɪli/
adjective
成束地，成群地
Growing or occurring in bunches; clustered together.
The flowers grew bunchily along the path.  这些花沿着小路成簇地生长。
Synonyms: clustered, clumped, grouped
Antonyms: scattered, dispersed, spread out
词性: 形容词
