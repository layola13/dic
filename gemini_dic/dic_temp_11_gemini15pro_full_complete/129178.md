nobbily:/ˈnɒbɪli/| adv.|傲慢地，显贵地|In a snobbish or aristocratic manner, showing off wealth or social status.|He dressed nobbily in a tailored suit and expensive shoes. 他穿着剪裁考究的西装和昂贵的鞋子，一副贵族派头。|Synonyms: aristocratically, haughtily, snobbishly, pretentiously|Antonyms: humbly, modestly, simply|Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From *nob* (meaning a person of wealth or high social standing) + *-ly* (adverbial suffix).  The word "nob" likely originated as a shortened form of "nobleman."
