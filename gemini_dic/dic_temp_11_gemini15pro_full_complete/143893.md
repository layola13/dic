outpouring:/ˈaʊtpɔːrɪŋ/
n.
涌现；倾泻；流露
a sudden release of strong feelings
There was an outpouring of grief after the princess's death. 公主去世后，人们悲痛万分。
effusion, overflow, outburst, surge
holding back, suppression
noun
