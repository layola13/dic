madelenes:/ˈmædəleɪnz/ | n. |玛德琳蛋糕 |Small, shell-shaped sponge cakes, often flavored with lemon and made with almond flour.  They are associated with the French author Marcel Proust and his novel "Remembrance of Things Past". |  I baked a batch of madelenes for the afternoon tea. 我为下午茶烤了一批玛德琳蛋糕。


| Example:  Dipping a madeleine into his tea, he was suddenly transported back to his childhood.  他把一块玛德琳蛋糕浸入茶中，突然回到了童年。 | Synonym:  shell cakes, petits fours (somewhat) | Antonym: N/A | Noun
