monastically:/məˈnæstɪkli/
adverb
禁欲地，像修道士一样地
in a monastic manner; like a monk.
He lived monastically, devoting himself to prayer and study. 他过着禁欲的生活，致力于祈祷和学习。
The artist lived monastically, isolating himself from the outside world.  这位艺术家过着像修道士般的生活，把自己与外界隔绝开来。
近义词: ascetically, austerely, simply
反义词: luxuriously, extravagantly, hedonistically
词性: adverb

monastic:/məˈnæstɪk/
adjective
修道院的，僧侣的
relating to monks, nuns, or monasteries; resembling or suggestive of monks or their way of life.
The monks lived a monastic life of prayer and contemplation.  僧侣们过着祈祷和沉思的修道院生活。
The old building had a monastic atmosphere.  这栋老建筑有一种修道院的氛围。
近义词: cloistered, conventual, religious
反义词: secular, worldly
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin monasticus, from Greek monastikos "solitary," from monastes "monk," from monazein "to live alone," from monos "alone."
