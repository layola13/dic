congregativeness:/kɑːŋɡrɛɡəˈtɪvnəs/
n.
群居性，聚集性
The tendency or inclination to congregate; the quality of being congregative.
The congregativeness of the species is evident in their large flocks.  这个物种的群居性体现在它们庞大的群体中。
近义词：sociability, gregariousness
反义词：solitariness, unsociability
词性：名词

congregative:/ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪtɪv/
adj.
群居的，聚集的
Tending to congregate; assembling.
Pigeons are congregative birds. 鸽子是群居的鸟类。
近义词：social, gregarious
反义词：solitary, antisocial
词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *congregativus, from Latin congregatus (past participle of congregare "to collect into a flock"), from com- "together" + grex "flock."
