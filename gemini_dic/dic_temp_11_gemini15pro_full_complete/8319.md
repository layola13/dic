anti-parliament:/ˌæntiˈpɑːləmənt/ | adj. n.| 反议会的，反议会组织；| Opposed to or hostile towards parliaments or parliamentary government.  A group or movement opposed to parliamentary government. |  The anti-parliament sentiment is growing. 反议会情绪正在增长。 | The anti-parliament staged a protest. 反议会组织举行了抗议。| synonyms: anti-democratic, counter-parliamentary | antonyms: pro-parliament, parliamentary | adjective, noun
