cocounsel: /ˌkoʊˈkaʊnsəl/ | n. | 共同辩护律师；协同律师 | A lawyer who assists the principal attorney in a case. |  The experienced lawyer served as cocounsel in the high-profile trial.  经验丰富的律师在备受瞩目的审判中担任协同律师。 | Synonyms: associate counsel, assisting counsel | Antonyms: opposing counsel | noun

cocounsel: /ˌkoʊˈkaʊnsəl/ | v. | 共同辩护；担任协同律师 | To act as cocounsel in a legal case. |  She agreed to cocounsel with him on the case. 她同意与他共同处理此案。 | Synonyms: assist, collaborate, work together | Antonyms: oppose, work against | verb
