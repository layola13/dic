aumery:/ˈɔːməri/| n.|储物柜，衣柜；特别是教堂里的圣器室|A small room or cupboard used for storing things, especially vestments or sacred objects in a church. | The priest retrieved the chalice from the aumery.  神父从圣器室取回了圣杯。|Synonyms: cupboard, closet, locker, vestry|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "open space" in a metaphorical sense)|Noun
