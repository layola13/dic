ferule:/ˈfɛruːl/ (US), /ˈfɛrʊl/ (UK)| n. v.|戒尺；金属环；握柄；管夹|A rod, cane, or flat piece of wood used for punishing children, especially by striking them on the hand; a metal ring or cap placed around a cane or tool handle to prevent splitting or wear.|
The teacher used a ferule to discipline unruly students. 老师用戒尺管教不守规矩的学生。
He strengthened the handle of his axe with a metal ferule. 他用金属环加固了斧头的把手。|
Synonyms: rod, cane, stick, ruler (for punishment); ring, cap, band (for tools)|
Antonyms: reward, praise (for punishment)|noun
ferule:/ˈfɛruːl/ (US), /ˈfɛrʊl/ (UK)|v.|用戒尺打|To punish with a ferule.|
The headmaster feruled the boy for his disobedience. 校长用戒尺惩罚了这个男孩的违抗行为。
In the old days, teachers would ferule students for talking in class. 在过去，老师会用戒尺打上课讲话的学生。|
Synonyms: cane, beat, strike, punish|
Antonyms: reward, praise|verb
