octoate: /ˈɒktəʊeɪt/ /ˈɑːktəʊeɪt/
noun
辛酸盐；2-乙基己酸盐;  辛酸酯
a salt or ester of octanoic acid (formerly called caprylic acid).
例句：Lead octoate is used as a catalyst in polyurethane production. 辛酸铅在聚氨酯生产中用作催化剂。

例句：Metal octoates are often used as drying agents in paints and varnishes. 金属辛酸盐常在油漆和清漆中用作干燥剂。
近义词: caprylate (especially for the salt or ester of the n-octanoic acid isomer)
反义词: None (There isn't a direct antonym for a specific chemical compound like octoate)
词性: n.
