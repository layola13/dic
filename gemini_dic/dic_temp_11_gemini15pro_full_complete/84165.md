half-plucked:/ˌhɑːfˈplʌkt/
adjective
半拔的; 拔了一半的
partially plucked; having some, but not all, feathers or hairs removed.
The half-plucked chicken lay on the counter, waiting to be prepared.  那只拔了一半毛的鸡躺在柜台上，等待被处理。
The half-plucked eyebrows gave her a surprised look.  她那半拔的眉毛让她看起来很惊讶。
partially removed, incompletely plucked
fully plucked, unplucked
adjective
