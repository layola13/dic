drawbridge:/ˈdrɔːbrɪdʒ/| n.|吊桥|A bridge that can be raised or lowered to prevent or allow passage, typically over a moat, harbor entrance, or other waterway.|The knight lowered the drawbridge to allow the king's carriage to enter the castle. 骑士放下吊桥，让国王的马车进入城堡。|The drawbridge was raised to prevent the enemy from crossing. 吊桥升起，防止敌人过河。|Synonyms: bascule bridge, lifting bridge|Antonyms: fixed bridge|noun
