insightfulness:/ˈɪnsaɪtflnəs/| n.|洞察力；富有洞察力|The insightfulness of her analysis impressed everyone. 她的分析的洞察力给每个人留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：perspicacity, acuity, discernment|反义词：obtuseness, shallowness|词性: 名词

词源:  From insightful + -ness.  Insightful comes from insight, which originated from the Old English  *insīht, meaning "inner sight, mental vision."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
