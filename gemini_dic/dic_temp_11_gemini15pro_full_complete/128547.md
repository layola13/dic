NGOs:/ˌɛnˌdʒiˈoʊz/
|n.|非政府组织|Non-governmental organizations; organizations that operate independently of any government, typically one whose purpose is to address a social or political issue.|Many NGOs are working to improve access to clean water in developing countries. 许多非政府组织正在努力改善发展中国家的清洁水供应。|
|例句：Several NGOs are providing aid to the refugees.  一些非政府组织正在向难民提供援助。|
|例句：The NGOs played a crucial role in the disaster relief effort.  这些非政府组织在灾难救援工作中发挥了至关重要的作用。|
|近义词：non-profit organizations, charities, voluntary organizations|
|反义词：governmental organizations|
|词性：名词|
