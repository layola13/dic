lorate:/ˈlɔːreɪt/
adjective
带状的，条形的
shaped like a strap or ribbon; strap-shaped.
The lorate leaves of the eelgrass swayed gently in the current.  海草的带状叶子在水流中轻轻摇曳。
近义词: strap-shaped, ribbon-like, linear
反义词: ovate, circular, orbicular
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *lōrātus, from lōrum (“thong, strap”).
