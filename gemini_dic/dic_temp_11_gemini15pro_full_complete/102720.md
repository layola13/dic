jangles:/ˈdʒæŋɡlz/ (US), /ˈdʒæŋɡəlz/ (UK)| v. n.| 刺耳地发出叮当声；发出刺耳的金属撞击声；(叮当声)使烦躁；争吵，吵嘴|A verb that describes a harsh metallic ringing sound, or the act of making such a sound. It can also be used as a noun referring to this sound or a state of nervousness or irritation.  As a noun, it can also mean a noisy quarrel.|The bracelets on her wrist jangled as she moved. 她手腕上的手镯随着她的移动发出叮当声。

The constant jangling of the phone made me jumpy.  电话不断地响，让我烦躁不安。

The keys jangled in his pocket. 他口袋里的钥匙叮当作响。

The children's constant bickering jangled my nerves. 孩子们不停地争吵，让我心烦意乱。

The spurs of the cowboy jangled as he walked. 牛仔走动时，马刺发出叮当声。


|近义词：clatter, jingle, clang, rattle, clash, grate, jar, annoy, irritate |反义词: soothe, calm, quiet|词性: verb, noun
