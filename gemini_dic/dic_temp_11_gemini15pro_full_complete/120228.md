mineworker:/ˈmaɪnˌwɜːrkər/ (US) /ˈmaɪnˌwɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|矿工|A person who works in a mine, extracting minerals such as coal, ore, or precious stones.|The mineworkers went on strike for better pay and safer working conditions. 矿工们为了争取更高的工资和更安全的工作条件而举行了罢工。|近义词：miner, collier (especially for coal miners)|反义词:None|Noun
