escapologist:/ɪˈskeɪpɒlədʒɪst/ /ɛˈskeɪpɑːlədʒɪst/
n.
逃脱艺术家；擅长从束缚中逃脱的人
A performer who specializes in escaping from restraints.
例句：The escapologist wowed the audience by freeing himself from a locked safe submerged in water.  逃脱艺术家通过从沉入水中的保险箱中逃脱出来，让观众惊叹不已。

例句：Harry Houdini was a famous escapologist known for his daring feats. 哈里·胡迪尼是一位著名的逃脱艺术家，以其大胆的壮举而闻名。

近义词：escape artist
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "captor" or "jailer" in a specific context)
