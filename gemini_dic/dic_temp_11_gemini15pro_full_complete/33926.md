cirrhosed:/sɪˈroʊst/| adj.|肝硬化的|Affected with cirrhosis of the liver; hardened, as by cirrhosis.  (受肝硬化影响的；硬化的，如肝硬化)|His liver was cirrhosed due to years of heavy drinking. 他的肝脏由于多年酗酒而硬化。|The patient's cirrhosed liver was failing.  病人的肝硬化正在恶化。|Synonyms:  hardened, sclerotic, fibrous|Antonyms: healthy, normal, undamaged|adjective
