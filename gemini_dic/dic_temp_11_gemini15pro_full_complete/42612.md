counterraiding:/ˈkaʊntərˌreɪdɪŋ/
noun
反突袭，反劫掠
An act of raiding in retaliation for a previous raid.
The army launched a counterraiding operation against the enemy camp. 军队对敌营发动了反突袭行动。
Synonyms: counterattack, retaliation, reprisal
Antonyms: defense, retreat
词性: 名词
