hyperorthodoxies:/ˌhaɪpərˈɔːrθədɑːksiz/
|n.|极端正统观念，极端正统的教条|Extreme or excessive adherence to orthodox beliefs or practices, often to a degree considered fanatical or rigid.|The hyperorthodoxies of the sect discouraged any form of independent thought.  这个教派的极端正统观念压制了任何形式的独立思考。|
|The political landscape was dominated by conflicting hyperorthodoxies. 政治格局被相互冲突的极端正统观念所主导。|synonyms: dogmatism, fundamentalism, rigidity|antonyms: liberalism, heterodoxy, unorthodoxy|noun|
