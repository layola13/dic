carvery: /ˈkɑːvəri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːrvəri/ (US)| n. |雕刻店，切肉店；(尤指在餐馆或酒店供应的)切肉，烤肉|A carvery is a restaurant or a part of a restaurant where cooked meat is carved for you.  A carvery can also refer to the selection of cooked meats available.|例句：The hotel has a popular Sunday carvery. 这家酒店的周日烤肉很受欢迎。

例句：We went to the carvery for lunch. 我们去烤肉店吃午饭。|近义词：buffet, cafeteria, deli|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From the verb "carve" + "-ery" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a place of business or activity).  "Carve" comes from the Old English "ceorfan," meaning "to cut."
