nonuniformist:/ˌnɒnˈjuːnɪfɔːmɪst/ | n. | 非均变论者 | A person who believes that geological changes have occurred suddenly and catastrophically rather than gradually and uniformly. |  Example 1: The nonuniformist argued that the canyon was formed by a sudden, massive flood. 非均变论者认为峡谷是由突发的巨大洪水形成的。

Example 2:  His nonuniformist theories were met with skepticism by the established geological community. 他的非均变论理论受到了已确立的地质学界的质疑。 | 近义词: catastrophist | 反义词: uniformitarian, gradualist | 词性: noun

nonuniform:/ˌnɒnˈjuːnɪfɔːm/ | adj. | 非均匀的，不一致的 | Not uniform; varying or irregular. | Example 1: The nonuniform distribution of resources created economic disparities. 资源分配不均造成了经济差距。

Example 2: The painting had a nonuniform texture, with some areas smooth and others rough. 这幅画的纹理不均匀，有些地方光滑，有些地方粗糙。 | 近义词: uneven, irregular, varying, heterogeneous | 反义词: uniform, consistent, homogeneous | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
uniform (adjective/noun): from Late Latin uniformis, from uni- "one" + forma "form"
-ist (suffix):  forms agent nouns or nouns of adherence to a doctrine or system
