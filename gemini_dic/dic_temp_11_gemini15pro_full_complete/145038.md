overages:/ˈoʊvərɪdʒɪz/
n.
超额，超支，超出部分
amounts over and above an expected or allowed amount or number.
The overages on her phone bill resulted in a hefty charge.  她的电话账单超额导致了一笔巨额费用。
Surplus, excess, extra amount
Shortage, deficit, shortfall
noun
