epithet:/ˈɛpɪθɛt/ | n. | 称号，绰号，修饰语 | An adjective or descriptive phrase expressing a quality characteristic of the person or thing mentioned. | Homer's epithet for the dawn is "rosy-fingered."  荷马对黎明的称号是“玫瑰指的”。

The athlete earned the epithet "The Rocket" for his speed.  这位运动员因其速度而获得了“火箭”的称号。 | synonyms: appellation, sobriquet, nickname, moniker, label, tag, designation | antonyms: misnomer, anonymity | noun
