aikido:/aɪˈkiːdoʊ/ | n. | 合气道 | A Japanese martial art that uses throws and joint locks to subdue an opponent, emphasizing harmony and non-resistance. |  Example 1: She's been practicing aikido for five years. 她练习合气道已经五年了。

Example 2: Aikido focuses on redirecting an attacker's energy rather than meeting force with force. 合气道注重引导攻击者的能量，而不是以力抗力。 | 近义词: martial art, jiu-jitsu | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
