induce:/ɪnˈd(j)uːs/ (US) /ɪnˈdjuːs/ (UK)| vt.| 诱导，引起，导致，劝说 |To lead or move by persuasion or influence; to bring about or give rise to.|The advertisement induced me to buy the product. 这则广告诱使我购买了该产品.|Synonyms: persuade, convince, cause, prompt, stimulate, bring about|Antonyms: deter, dissuade, prevent, hinder|verb
