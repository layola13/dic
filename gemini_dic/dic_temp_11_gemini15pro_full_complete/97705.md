inimically:/ɪˈnɪmɪk(ə)li/
adverb
敌意地，怀敌意地
in a hostile or unfriendly manner
例句：He stared inimically at his rival. 他敌意地盯着他的对手。

例句：She spoke inimically about her former employer. 她带着敌意谈论她以前的雇主。

近义词：hostilely, antagonistically, unfriendlily, malevolently
反义词：amicably, friendly, kindly

词性：adverb

词源：From Late Latin *inimicalis, from Latin inimicus (“unfriendly, hostile”)
