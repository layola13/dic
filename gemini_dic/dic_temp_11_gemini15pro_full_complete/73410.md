foreknowable:/fɔːrˈnoʊəbl/
adjective
可预知的，可预见的
able to be known in advance.
例句：The future is not always foreknowable. 未来并非总是可预见的。
例句：Some events are foreknowable, while others are not. 有些事件是可以预见的，而有些则不是。
近义词：predictable, foreseeable, anticipated
反义词：unforeseeable, unpredictable, unknown
词性: adjective
