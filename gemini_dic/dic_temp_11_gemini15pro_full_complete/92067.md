hyperprophetic:/ˌhaɪpərprəˈfɛtɪk/| adj.|过度预言的，极端预言的|Excessively prophetic; characterized by excessive or exaggerated prophecy.  It describes something that goes beyond typical prophecy, often with an emphasis on doom and gloom or extreme interpretations.|例句：His hyperprophetic pronouncements about the end of the world were met with skepticism. 他关于世界末日的极端预言遭到了质疑。

例句：The book offered a hyperprophetic vision of a dystopian future, filled with technological control and societal collapse. 这本书提供了一个反乌托邦未来的极端预言式愿景，充满了技术控制和社会崩溃。|近义词：apocalyptic, millenarian, doomsday|反义词：realistic, pragmatic|词性:adjective
