darksome:/ˈdɑːrksəm/| adj.|黑暗的，阴暗的|Gloomy, dark; suggestive of gloom.  Characterized by darkness; dimly lit.|The forest path was darksome and forbidding. 森林小径阴暗而令人生畏。|近义词：dark, gloomy, dim, murky, shadowy|反义词：bright, light, sunny|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *derksom, equivalent to dark +‎ -some.
