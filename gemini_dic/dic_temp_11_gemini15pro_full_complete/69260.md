featherbone:/ˈfeðərbəʊn/ (US) /ˈfeðərbəʊn/ (UK)| n. |鲸须；鲸骨；用鲸须做的紧身胸衣|A flexible, lightweight material made from baleen (whalebone) used in corsets and other garments in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.  It provided structure and support without the rigidity of traditional boning.|Her grandmother's wedding dress had featherbone stays. 她祖母的结婚礼服有鲸骨支撑。|Synonyms: baleen, whalebone, corset stay|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "steel boning" as a contrasting material)|Noun
