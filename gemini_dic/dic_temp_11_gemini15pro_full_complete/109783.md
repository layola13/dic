levities: /ˈlɛvɪtiz/
n.
轻浮；轻率；不严肃的行为
behavior or comments that are lighthearted and not serious
The speaker's levities were inappropriate for the solemn occasion. 演讲者的轻浮言行与庄严的场合不符。

近义词: frivolity, flippancy, lightheartedness, gaiety, amusement
反义词: seriousness, gravity, solemnity, sobriety

词性: noun (plural)
词源: From Latin *levitas, from levis "light".
