disestimation:/ˌdɪsɛstɪˈmeɪʃən/
n.
低估；轻视；贬低
An unfairly low opinion of someone or something; undervaluing.
The disestimation of her abilities led to her being overlooked for the promotion.  她能力被低估，导致她在晋升中被忽视。
The artist suffered from a disestimation of his own talent.  这位艺术家低估了自己的才能。
Synonyms: undervaluation, underestimation, depreciation, belittlement
Antonyms: overestimation, appreciation, valuation
noun
