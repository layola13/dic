dotty:/ˈdɒti/ (UK) /ˈdɑːti/ (US)| adj.|古怪的；疯狂的；过分喜爱的；有点疯狂的|eccentric or slightly mad; excessively fond of someone or something|She's become quite dotty lately, always talking to her plants. 她最近变得很古怪，总是跟她的植物说话。|He's dotty about his new car. 他对他的新车爱不释手。|近义词：eccentric, crazy, foolish, infatuated|反义词：sane, sensible|词性:形容词

dotty:/ˈdɒti/ (UK) /ˈdɑːti/ (US)| n.|傻瓜；怪人|a silly or eccentric person (often used playfully)|Oh, you dotty!  哦，你这傻瓜！|近义词：fool, eccentric|反义词：genius|词性:名词
