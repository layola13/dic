hanap:/ˈhɑːnɑːp/| n.|寻找，搜索 (Tagalog)|A Tagalog word meaning "search," "look for," or "seek."|Example:  Naghahanap ako ng trabaho. (I am looking for a job.)

Example:  Hanapin mo ang susi. (Look for the key.)|Synonyms: seek, search, look for|Antonyms: ignore, neglect|Noun
