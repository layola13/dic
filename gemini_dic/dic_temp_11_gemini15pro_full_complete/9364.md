antipuritans:/ˌæntiˈpjʊərɪtənz/
| n. |反清教徒|People opposed to the Puritans, their beliefs, or their practices.  They often favored more traditional religious practices and a less strict moral code.|The antipuritans believed the Puritans were too extreme in their religious views.|近义词：nonconformist, dissenter|反义词:Puritan|Noun
