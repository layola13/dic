fats:/fæts/
n.
脂肪
A natural oily or greasy substance occurring in animal bodies, especially when deposited as a layer under the skin or around certain organs.
Eating too many fats can lead to health problems.
吃太多脂肪会导致健康问题。

lipids, oils
carbohydrates, proteins
noun


fat:/fæt/
n.
脂肪
The same as fats.
The fat in the meat adds flavor.
肉里的脂肪增加了味道。

grease, lard
lean meat
noun

fat:/fæt/
v.
养肥
To make fat or fatter; to feed an animal for slaughter.
They fattened the pig for the market.
他们把猪养肥了上市。

feed, nourish, plump
starve, thin

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fætt (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *fatto-, from Proto-Indo-European *peyH-  "to swell".
