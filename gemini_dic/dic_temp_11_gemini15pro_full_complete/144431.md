outswagger:/ˌaʊtˈswæɡər/
verb
以更嚣张的态度胜过; 比…更 swagger
He tried to outswagger the policeman, but it didn't work.  他试图比警察更嚣张，但没有成功。
She outswaggered everyone at the party with her flamboyant outfit. 她以其华丽的服装在派对上比所有人都更招摇。
近义词: outdo, surpass, outshine
反义词: be outdone, be surpassed
词性: verb
