nondiscordant:/ˌnɒndɪˈskɔːrdənt/
adjective
不矛盾的，一致的，和谐的
Not disagreeing; harmonious; concordant.
The results of the two studies were nondiscordant, both showing a positive correlation. 这两项研究的结果是一致的，都显示出正相关。
Synonyms: harmonious, agreeable, concordant, consistent, compatible
Antonyms: discordant, conflicting, disagreeing, incompatible
词性: 形容词

词源：
From non- + discordant.  Discordant itself comes from Late Latin discordant-, discordans, present participle of discordare "to disagree," from dis- "apart" + cord-, cor "heart". Therefore, nondiscordant literally means "not disagreeing in heart."
