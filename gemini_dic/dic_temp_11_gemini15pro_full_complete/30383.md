centifidous:/ˌsɛntɪˈfaɪdəs/
adjective
多足的；百足的
Having many feet or legs; resembling a centipede.
Centifidous insects scurried across the forest floor. 多足昆虫在森林地面上疾走。
Near synonyms: multiped, myriapod
Antonyms: apodal, footless
word type: adjective
