crapple:/ˈkræpəl/| n. | 劣质苹果产品；蹩脚货；废物 | A derogatory term for Apple products, especially when perceived as overpriced or poorly designed.  It combines "crap" (meaning something of poor quality) and "Apple". |  "He thinks the new iPhone is a total crapple." 他认为新款iPhone完全是垃圾。 | 近义词:junk, garbage, lemon | 反义词:masterpiece, gem | 词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
Blend of "crap" and "Apple".  First appeared in the early 21st century with the rise of Apple's consumer electronics dominance.  Reflects a critical perspective on the brand and its products.
