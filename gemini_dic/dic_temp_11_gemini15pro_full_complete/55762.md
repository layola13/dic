diversifiable:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪəbl/
adjective
可分散的，可多样化的
able to be diversified or varied.  Making investments in different areas can create a diversifiable portfolio that reduces risk.
Example:  A diversifiable portfolio protects you from losses in any one particular sector.  一个多元化的投资组合可以保护你免受任何特定行业的损失。

Example:  The company's income streams are highly diversifiable, reducing their reliance on any single product.  该公司的收入来源高度多样化，减少了对任何单一产品的依赖。

Synonym:  varied, adaptable, flexible
Antonym:  concentrated, specialized, inflexible
词性: 形容词
