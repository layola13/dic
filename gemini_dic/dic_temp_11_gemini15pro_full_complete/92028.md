hyperpathetic:/ˌhaɪpərˈpæθɛtɪk/ | adj. |过度敏感的；过度反应的|Excessively or abnormally sensitive to stimuli, especially to sensory stimuli.|
The hyperpathetic patient flinched at the slightest touch.  这个过度敏感的病人轻轻一碰就畏缩了。

He had a hyperpathetic response to the medication. 他对药物的反应过度敏感。


近义词:hypersensitive, oversensitive, touchy |
反义词:insensitive, impassive, stoic |
词性:adjective


词源:
From Greek *hyperpathes* "suffering excessively," from *hyper-* "over, beyond" + *pathos* "suffering, feeling"
