disgraceful:/dɪsˈɡreɪsfl/
adjective
可耻的；丢脸的；不光彩的
Bringing the company into disrepute through his actions was disgraceful.  他的行为使公司名誉扫地，真是可耻。
His behavior was absolutely disgraceful. 他的行为绝对是丢脸的。
近义词: shameful, dishonorable, discreditable, ignominious, deplorable
反义词: honorable, respectable, admirable, commendable
词性: adjective
词源: From dis- (expressing negation) + graceful.  由dis-（表示否定）+ graceful（优雅的）组成。
