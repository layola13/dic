backhander:/ˈbækˌhændər/ (UK), /ˈbækˌhændər/ (US)| n.|贿赂；回扣；不正当交易；反手击球|A bribe or illicit payment; a hit made with the back of the hand. |He offered the official a backhander to secure the contract. 他向官员行贿以确保拿到合同。|Synonyms: bribe, kickback, payoff, sweetener, under-the-table payment|Antonyms: honest payment, legitimate transaction|Noun

backhander:/ˈbækˌhændər/ (UK), /ˈbækˌhændər/ (US)| n.|反手击球|A hit made with the back of the hand, especially in tennis or badminton. |He won the point with a powerful backhander. 他以一记强有力的反手击球赢得了这一分。|Synonyms: backhand stroke, backhand shot|Antonyms: forehand|Noun

词源： back + hand + -er.  由“back”（背面）和“hand”（手）组成，加上后缀“-er”表示动作或工具。最初指用手背击球，后来引申为“贿赂”，因为贿赂通常是偷偷摸摸地进行的，就像用手背传递东西一样。
