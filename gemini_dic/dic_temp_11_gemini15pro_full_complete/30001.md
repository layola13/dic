cavorted:/kəˈvɔːrtɪd/
verb
雀跃；嬉戏
jumped or danced around excitedly
The lambs cavorted in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏。
近义词: frolicked, gambolled, pranced, romped
反义词: trudged, slouched, moped

词性: v.

词源: From the Italian *cavortare*, frequentative of *cavolare*, from *cavolo* 'a stalk' (of cabbage, etc.), hence 'to leap, curvet'.
