associatory:/əˈsoʊʃiəˌtɔːri/ /əˈsəʊʃiətəri/
| adj.|联想的，结合的|Relating to or based on association of ideas or things.|例句：Associatory learning is a key component of cognitive development. 联想学习是认知发展的一个关键组成部分。

例句：The associatory cortex plays a crucial role in higher-level thinking. 联想皮层在高级思维中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：connected, related, linked|反义词：unrelated, disconnected, separate|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *associatorius, from Latin associare (“to join, unite”) + -ory.
