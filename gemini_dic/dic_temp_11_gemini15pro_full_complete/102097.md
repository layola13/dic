isospore:/ˈaɪsɵspɔːr/
n.
等孢子
A spore of a cryptogam, such as a fern or alga, that is of one kind and size and develops into a gametophyte that produces both male and female gametes.
Isospores are characteristic of most ferns. 等孢子是大多数蕨类植物的特征。
Isospores germinate to produce bisexual gametophytes. 等孢子萌发产生双性配子体。
synonym: homospore
antonym: heterospore
noun
