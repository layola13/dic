hypersentimentally:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəli/ | adv. | 过分感伤地，过度多愁善感地 | In a way that is excessively or exaggeratedly sentimental. | She reacted hypersentimentally to the old photograph, bursting into tears at the sight of it. 她看到那张老照片，反应过度感伤，一下子就哭了。| Example: He spoke hypersentimentally about his childhood, making everyone roll their eyes. 他过度感伤地谈论自己的童年，让每个人都翻白眼。| 近义词: mawkishly, over-sentimentally, cloyingly | 反义词: pragmatically, realistically, unsentimentally | 词性: 副词

词源:
"hyper-" (prefix): from Greek "hyper" (over, above, beyond)
"sentiment": from French "sentiment" (feeling, opinion), from Latin "sentire" (to feel)
"-al" (suffix): forming adjectives from nouns
"-ly" (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
