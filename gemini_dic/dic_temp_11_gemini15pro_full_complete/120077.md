milter:/ˈmɪltər/| n.|邮件过滤器|A mail filter is a program that processes email messages as they are being sent or received.  It can be used to perform a variety of functions, such as blocking spam, scanning for viruses, and modifying message headers.|The milter intercepted the spam before it reached my inbox. 这个邮件过滤器在我的垃圾邮件到达收件箱之前就拦截了它。|Synonyms: mail filter, email filter|Antonyms: None|noun
