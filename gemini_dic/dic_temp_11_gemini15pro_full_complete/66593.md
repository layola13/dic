exhume:/ɪɡˈzjuːm/ /ɛksˈhjuːm/
verb
掘出；挖出
To dig out (something buried, especially a corpse) from the earth.
The body was exhumed for further examination.  尸体被挖出来进行进一步检查。
The archaeologist exhumed the ancient artifacts. 考古学家挖掘出了古代文物。

近义词:unearth, disinter, dig up
反义词:bury, inter
词性:verb
