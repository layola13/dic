lastingly:/ˈlɑːstɪŋli/ /ˈlæstɪŋli/
adv.
持久地，永久地
in a way that lasts for a long time or forever.
His work has lastingly influenced the field of psychology. 他的工作对心理学领域产生了持久的影响。
The experience lastingly affected her perspective on life. 这段经历永久地改变了她的人生观。

近义词:permanently, enduringly, perpetually
反义词:temporarily, briefly, fleetingly
词性:副词

词源:
From "lasting" + "-ly".  "Lasting" comes from the Middle English "lastinge," from Old English "læstende," present participle of "læstan" (to last, endure). The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives.
