PHRASE->
Not commonly used in phrases.  It's primarily an adverb describing a state of being.  It’s more common to use "lonely" with phrases.  For example:
a lonely heart  一颗孤独的心
a lonely road 一条孤寂的道路
a lonely existence  孤独的存在
to feel lonely 感到孤独
to be lonely 孤独


词源：From lone + -ly (2).  Ultimately from Old English *ānlic (compare German einlich). More at one, -ly.
