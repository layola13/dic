decollectivizes:/diːkəˈlɛktɪvaɪzɪz/
verb
取消集体化；解散集体农庄
To reverse the process of collectivization, typically referring to the dismantling of collective farms and returning land to private ownership.
The government decollectivized agriculture in the 1990s, returning land to individual farmers. 政府在20世纪90年代取消了农业集体化，将土地归还给个体农民。
The reforms decollectivized farming and allowed for private enterprise. 改革取消了农业集体化，允许私营企业。
近义词: privatize, individualize
反义词: collectivize, nationalize
词性: verb

Etymology:
de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + collectivize (from collective + -ize)
