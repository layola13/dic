cenobitical:/ˌsɛnəˈbɪtɪkəl/
adjective
修道院的；集体生活的
Living in a community, especially under religious rule.
The monks lived a cenobitical life, sharing meals and prayers.  修道士们过着集体生活，一起吃饭和祈祷。

The cenobitical order required all members to reside within the monastery walls. 这个修道院的规则要求所有成员都住在修道院的墙内。
synonym: monastic, conventual, cloistered
antonym: eremitical, solitary, anchoritic
word type: adjective
