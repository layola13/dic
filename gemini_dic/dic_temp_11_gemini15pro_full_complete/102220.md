itchily:/ˈɪtʃɪli/ | adv. | 发痒地，渴望地 | In a manner that causes or suggests an itching sensation; restlessly or impatiently. |  He scratched his arm itchily.  他痒得抓了抓胳膊。| Synonyms: restlessly, impatiently, uneasily | Antonyms: calmly, patiently, contentedly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From itchy + -ly. Itchy comes from Old English *iccig.
