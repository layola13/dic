gome:/ɡoʊm/| n.| 欺骗；诡计；（尤指）商业欺诈 | An instance of deception, trickery, or swindling, especially in business dealings. | He lost all his savings in a gome involving fake investments. 他在一次涉及虚假投资的骗局中损失了所有积蓄。 | 近义词：fraud, scam, deception, trickery | 反义词：honesty, integrity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Yiddish גנבֿענען (ganvenen, “to steal”), from גנבֿ (ganev, “thief”). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *knabô ("boy, knave, servant").
