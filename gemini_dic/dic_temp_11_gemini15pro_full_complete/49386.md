delirium:/dɪˈlɪəriəm/| n.| 谵妄，神志不清|A state of mental confusion and excitement characterized by disorientation for time and place, often with hallucinations, incoherent speech, and a fluctuating level of consciousness.|The patient experienced delirium due to a high fever.|近义词：confusion, madness, insanity|反义词：lucidity, sanity|名词
