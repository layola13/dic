crook:/krʊk/| n. |骗子，罪犯，弯曲的东西|A person who is dishonest or a criminal.  Also, a bend or curve, especially in a stick or stream.|例句：The crooks robbed the bank. 骗子抢劫了银行。

The shepherd used his crook to guide the sheep. 牧羊人用他的弯杖引导羊群。|近义词：thief, robber, criminal, swindler, con artist, bend, curve|反义词:police officer, law enforcement, straight|词性:noun

crook:/krʊk/| v. |弯曲，使成钩形|To bend or curve something.|例句：He crooked his finger to beckon her closer. 他弯曲手指示意她靠近。

She crooked her arm around his. 她把胳膊弯曲绕在他的胳膊上。|近义词：bend, curve, hook|反义词:straighten|词性:verb
