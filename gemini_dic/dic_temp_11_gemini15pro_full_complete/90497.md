huckstered:/ˈhʌkstərd/| v.|兜售，叫卖（尤指廉价或劣质商品）；散布，宣传（尤指政治观点）|to promote or sell something, especially aggressively or noisily|He huckstered his wares on the street corner. 他在街角兜售他的商品。|Synonyms: peddle, hawk, tout|Antonyms: buy, purchase|verb

huckster:/ˈhʌkstər/| n.|小贩，叫卖者；（尤指政治观点的）兜售者，散布者|a person who sells small items, either door-to-door or from a stall or small store|The huckster was known for his loud voice and persuasive sales pitch.  这个小贩以他洪亮的声音和有说服力的推销技巧而闻名。|Synonyms: peddler, hawker, vendor|Antonyms: buyer, customer|noun
