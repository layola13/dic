inexpansible:/ˌɪnɪkˈspænsəbl/
adjective
不能膨胀的;不能扩张的;不可伸展的
not able to be expanded.
The metal framework was rigid and inexpansible. 金属框架坚硬且不可伸缩。
近义词:inflexible, rigid, unyielding
反义词:expansible, flexible, elastic
词性:adjective
