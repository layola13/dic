flackeries:/ˈflækəriːz/
n.
吹捧；吹嘘；公关活动；虚伪的奉承
Excessive or insincere praise or publicity, especially of a promotional nature.
The endless flackeries surrounding the new movie made it seem overhyped.  这部新电影周围无休止的吹捧让它看起来被过度炒作了。
近义词: hype, publicity, promotion, puffery
反义词: criticism, condemnation
词性: noun (plural)
