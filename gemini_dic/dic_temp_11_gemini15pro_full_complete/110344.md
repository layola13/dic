lighting's:/ˈlaɪtɪŋz/
noun
照明，灯光
The possessive form of "lighting," referring to something that belongs to or is associated with lighting.
The lighting's intensity was adjusted for the scene.  舞台的灯光强度根据场景进行了调整。
synonyms: illumination, luminance, radiance
antonyms: darkness, obscurity


词性: noun
中文翻译:照明的, 灯光的 (所有格)
英文解析:The possessive form of "lighting," indicating ownership or association.  It refers to a quality or attribute of the lighting itself.
例句:
The lighting's effect on the mood was significant. 灯光对气氛的影响很显著。

The lighting's color temperature was too warm for the photograph.  照片的灯光色温太暖了。

近义词: illumination's, luminance's (less common)
反义词: darkness's, obscurity's (less common, often metaphorical)
