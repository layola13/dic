inarticulate:/ˌɪnɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/ | adj. |表达不清的，不善言辞的|Unable to express oneself clearly or fluently.|He was so shocked he became inarticulate. 他震惊得说不出话来。|Synonyms: speechless, tongue-tied, dumb, mute|Antonyms: articulate, eloquent, fluent|adjective

inarticulate:/ˌɪnɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/ | adj. |含糊不清的，不清晰的|(of sounds) not uttered or formed clearly.|She made an inarticulate sound of protest. 她发出含糊不清的抗议声。|Synonyms: indistinct, unclear, muffled|Antonyms: clear, distinct, articulate|adjective
