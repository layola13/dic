immensest:/ɪˈmɛnsɪst/ | adj. | 最广阔的，最巨大的 |  Superlative form of immense: extremely large or great, especially in scale or degree. | The immensest ocean on Earth is the Pacific. 地球上最广阔的海洋是太平洋。|  The universe is an immensest expanse. 宇宙是一片浩瀚无垠的区域。| 近义词：largest, hugest, vastest, greatest | 反义词：smallest, tiniest, minutest | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *immensus*, from in- "not" + mensus "measured" (past participle of metiri "to measure").
