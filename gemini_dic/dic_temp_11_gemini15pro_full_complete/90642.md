humanized:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzd/| adj.|人性化的，人道的|Made more humane or human; given human qualities or characteristics.  Characterized by kindness, sympathy, or consideration for humans or animals.|The company developed a humanized version of the software, making it more user-friendly.|近义词：compassionate, humane, sympathetic, kind, gentle|反义词：inhumane, cruel, brutal|词性:形容词
词源: From humanize + -ed.  Humanize comes from the Latin word "humanus" meaning human.
