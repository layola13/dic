anti-political:/ˌænti pəˈlɪtɪkəl/
adjective
反政治的; 不关心政治的; 厌恶政治的
Opposed to or averse to politics; not interested in politics.
例句：He has a very anti-political stance. 他有着非常反政治的立场。

例句：Many young people today are anti-political. 现在的很多年轻人对政治不感兴趣。

近义词：apolitical, non-political
反义词：political, pro-political
词性：adjective
