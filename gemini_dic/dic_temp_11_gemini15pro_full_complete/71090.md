fish-farms:/ˈfɪʃ fɑːrmz/ | n. | 养鱼场 |  A place where fish are raised commercially in enclosures such as ponds, tanks, or cages in rivers or the sea. | Salmon are often raised in fish-farms. 鲑鱼通常在养鱼场饲养。|  The fish-farms provide a sustainable source of protein. 这些养鱼场提供了可持续的蛋白质来源。 | synonyms: aquaculture, fish hatchery, pisciculture | antonyms: wild fishery | noun
