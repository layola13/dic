objectifier: /əbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪər/
noun
物化者；将…视为客体的人
Someone who objectifies another person, treating them as a thing rather than a person with feelings and agency.
She felt objectified by the way he stared at her. 她觉得他盯着她的方式让她感觉自己被物化了。
objectifying (as an adjective), objectification (as a noun)
humanizer
