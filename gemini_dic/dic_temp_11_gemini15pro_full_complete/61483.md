embreathement:/ɪmˈbriːðmənt/| n.| 呼吸; 注入精神; 渗透 |The embreathement of new life into the old traditions. 将新的生命注入旧的传统中。|例句：The artist's work was an embreathement of his passion for nature.  这位艺术家的作品是他对自然热情的一种呼吸。

例句：Her poetry is an embreathement of hope and resilience. 她的诗歌充满了希望和韧性。|近义词：inspiration, infusion, permeation|反义词:  suffocation, suppression|词性: 名词

词源: From  "em-" (in, into) + "breathe" + "-ment" (forming nouns of action or result).
