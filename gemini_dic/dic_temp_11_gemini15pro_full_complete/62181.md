encomiastic:/ˌɛnkəʊmiˈæstɪk/ | adj. | 赞美的，颂扬的 | Praising; eulogistic.  Expressing or giving high praise. |  The encomiastic speech praised the retiring CEO's vision and leadership.  这位退休CEO的赞美之词赞扬了他的远见卓识和领导才能。 |  The valedictorian delivered an encomiastic address.  毕业生代表发表了热情洋溢的演讲。 | laudatory, complimentary, panegyrical | critical, derogatory | adjective
