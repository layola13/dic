aisle: /aɪl/ | n. | 过道，通道 | A passage between rows of seats in a building such as a church or theater, an airplane, or between rows of shelves in a supermarket. |  例句：Please keep the aisle clear. 请保持过道畅通。 | 近义词：passage, walkway, corridor | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 wall, barrier) | 词性: noun

aisle: /aɪl/ | n. | (尤指教堂的)侧廊 |  A part of a church divided from the nave by pillars.  | 例句：The bride walked down the aisle.  新娘沿着教堂的侧廊走过来。| 近义词：nave, wing (在特定语境下) | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: noun
