nonrecipients:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈsɪp.i.ənts/ | n. | 非接受者；未收到者 | People who do not receive something, such as a benefit, payment, or communication. |  The nonrecipients of the scholarship were disappointed. 没有获得奖学金的人很失望。|  The email was sent to all staff, but some nonrecipients reported not seeing it. 这封邮件发送给了所有员工，但一些未收到邮件的人报告说没有看到它。| synonyms: non-beneficiaries, excluded parties | antonyms: recipients, beneficiaries | noun
