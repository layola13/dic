irreligiously:/ˌɪrɪˈlɪdʒəsli/
adv.
不敬地，亵渎地
In a way that lacks reverence or respect for religion or religious matters.
He spoke irreligiously about the sacred texts. 他不敬地谈论神圣的经文。
Synonyms: profanely, impiously, blasphemously
Antonyms: reverently, piously, devoutly
词性: 副词
