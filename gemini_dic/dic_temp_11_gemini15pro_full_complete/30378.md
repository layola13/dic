centesimo:/tʃenˈteɪzɪmoʊ/ /tʃenˈtɛzɪmoʊ/
noun
分，分币（意大利、梵蒂冈等国的货币单位）
One hundredth of a lira (Italian currency) or euro (Vatican City currency).
A centesimo is a small unit of currency. 分币是一种小的货币单位。
The price was 5,000 lire and 50 centesimi. 价格是5000里拉和50分。
Synonyms: cent, penny
Antonyms: euro, dollar, lira (larger denominations)
n.
