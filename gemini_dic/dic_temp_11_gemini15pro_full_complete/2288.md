adenophyllous:/ˌædɪnəˈfɪləs/
adjective
腺叶的
Having leaves crowded together, as in a gland.  Characterized by having glands on the leaves.
The adenophyllous plant secreted a sticky resin.  这种腺叶植物分泌出一种粘稠的树脂。
近义词:glandular
反义词:eglandular
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἀδήν (adḗn, "gland") + *φύλλον (phúllon, "leaf").
