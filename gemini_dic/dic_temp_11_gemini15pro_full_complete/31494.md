charmingly:/ˈtʃɑːrmɪŋli/ | adv. | 迷人地，可爱地 | In a way that is pleasant, attractive, and likeable. | She smiled charmingly. 她迷人地笑了笑。|  He charmingly offered to help. 他热情地主动帮忙。| 近义词: attractively, delightfully, pleasantly, gracefully, winsomely | 反义词: unattractively, displeasingly, offensively | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From charm + -ing + -ly.  "Charm" ultimately comes from the Latin "carmen" meaning "song, verse, incantation, charm."
