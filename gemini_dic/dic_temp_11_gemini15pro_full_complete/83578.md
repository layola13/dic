haematinometric:/ˌhiːmətɪnəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
血色素测定的
Relating to the measurement of haematin (an oxidized form of heme, the iron-containing part of hemoglobin).
Haematinometric methods are used to determine the concentration of hemoglobin in blood.
This study uses a haematinometric method to assess iron deficiency.
近义词:  hemoglobinometric, colorimetric (related to color measurement, often used for hemoglobin)
反义词:  None (specific antonyms are unlikely given the technical nature of the word)
词性: adjective


haematinometer:/ˌhiːmətɪnˈɒmɪtər/
noun
血色素计
An instrument used to measure the concentration of haematin, and thus indirectly hemoglobin, in blood.
The haematinometer provided a quick and relatively accurate measurement of hemoglobin levels.
The doctor used a haematinometer to check for anemia.
近义词: hemoglobinometer, hemometer
反义词: None (specific antonyms are unlikely given the technical nature of the word)
词性: noun


Etymology:
haematin-: from Greek haima, haimat- ‘blood’
-metric: from Greek metron 'measure'
-meter: from Greek metron 'measure'
