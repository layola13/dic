cocoonery:/kəˈkuːnəri/
noun
茧房; 安乐窝; 逃避现实的状态
a place or state of seclusion or isolation, like a cocoon.
例句：He retreated into his cocoonery, refusing to face the world. 他躲进自己的安乐窝，不愿面对世界。

例句：Her apartment, filled with books and plants, became her cocoonery during the pandemic.  在疫情期间，她那充满书籍和植物的公寓成了她的避风港。

近义词：retreat, sanctuary, refuge, seclusion
反义词：exposure, worldliness
词性: noun
