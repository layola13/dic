epileptoid:/ˈɛpɪˌlɛptɔɪd/| adj. n.|癫痫样的，类癫痫的；癫痫样人格|Resembling epilepsy or a seizure; characteristic of epilepsy.  Also, (as a noun) a person with an epileptoid personality.|He exhibited epileptoid behavior after the head injury.|近义词：convulsive, ictal|反义词: None|词性: adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From epi- (upon, over) + Greek *lēptikos (seizing, grasping) + -oid (resembling).  Ultimately from Greek *lēpsis (a seizing).
