circumcursation: /ˌsɜːrkəmkɜːrˈseɪʃən/ /ˌsɝːkəmkɝːˈseɪʃən/
noun
迂回，绕圈子，冗长
the use of many words where fewer would do, especially in a deliberate attempt to be vague or evasive.
His circumcursation of the issue made it difficult to understand his true position.  他绕着圈子谈论这个问题，让人难以理解他的真实立场。
The lawyer's circumcursation seemed designed to confuse the jury. 律师的冗长陈述似乎故意要迷惑陪审团。
synonyms: circumlocution, periphrasis, verbosity, prolixity
antonyms: conciseness, brevity, succinctness
