leftward:/ˈleftwərd/| adj. adv.|向左的，向左地|Moving or facing toward the left side.|The car drifted leftward. 汽车向左漂移。|近义词：left, toward the left|反义词:rightward, to the right|词性:形容词, 副词


Etymology:
From "left" + "-ward" (suffix indicating direction). "Left" ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *luftus meaning "weak, useless" (the left hand traditionally being considered weaker). "-ward" comes from Old English "-weard."
