obfirmate:/ˈɒbfɜːmeɪt/| vt.|硬化，使顽固|To harden or make stubborn; to strengthen or confirm (a resolve, belief, etc.).|He obfirmated his resolve to climb the mountain despite the risks. 他不顾风险，更加坚定了要爬山的决心。|近义词: harden, strengthen, reinforce, solidify|反义词: soften, weaken|词性:动词
词源: From Latin obfirmare (to strengthen, make firm), from ob- (towards, against) + firmare (to make firm), from firmus (firm).
