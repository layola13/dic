catfishing:/ˈkætˌfɪʃɪŋ/
n.
网络钓鱼；假扮他人身份进行网络诈骗
The act of creating a fake online persona to trick someone into a relationship or other interaction.
He was a victim of catfishing; the woman he'd been talking to online for a year turned out to be a man in another country. 他是网络钓鱼的受害者；和他网上聊了一年的女人原来是另一个国家的男人。
She was accused of catfishing after creating a fake profile to harass her ex-boyfriend.  她被指控网络钓鱼，因为她创建了一个虚假个人资料来骚扰她的前男友。
近义词：online deception, internet fraud, cyberstalking
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "honest online interaction")
