estrade:/ɛˈstrɑːd/ /ɪˈstreɪd/| n.|讲台，演奏台，高台|a raised platform, especially one from which speeches are made or music is performed|The conductor took his place on the estrade and raised his baton. 指挥家在演奏台上就位，举起了指挥棒。|Synonyms: platform, podium, dais, stage|Antonyms: floor, ground|noun
