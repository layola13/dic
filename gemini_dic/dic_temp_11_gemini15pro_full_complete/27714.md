cane:/keɪn/| n.| 拐杖；茎；藤条|A stick used as an aid in walking or hiking, typically made of wood and with a curved handle.|He leaned heavily on his cane as he walked. 他走路时沉重地倚着拐杖。|近义词：stick, staff, walking stick|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑crutch作为功能上的对比)|noun

cane:/keɪn/| v.| 用藤条打|To beat with a cane.|The schoolmaster caned the disobedient boy. 校长用藤条打了那个不听话的男孩。|近义词：flog, whip, lash|反义词:(没有直接的反义词)|verb
