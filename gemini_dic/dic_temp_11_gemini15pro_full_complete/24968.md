brusque:/brʌsk/ | adj. |  唐突的，鲁莽的；（言语）生硬的；（行为）粗率的；（气候）寒冷刺骨的 | Abrupt or offhand in speech or manner. | "She asked a few brusque questions." 她问了几个唐突的问题。| Synonyms: abrupt, curt, blunt, short, sharp, terse, gruff, rude, impolite, discourteous, unceremonious, peremptory | Antonyms: polite, courteous, gentle, kind | adjective


Etymology:
From French *brusque*, from Italian *brusco* "sour, harsh, rough," perhaps from Late Latin *bruscus* "butcher's broom" (a prickly shrub), or of imitative origin.
