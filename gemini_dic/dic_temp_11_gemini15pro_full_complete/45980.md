cyanobacteria:/saɪˌænoʊbækˈtɪəriə/ | n. |蓝细菌，蓝绿藻 | Photosynthetic bacteria that contain chlorophyll and other pigments and are found in various aquatic habitats.  They are sometimes considered algae. | Example 1: Cyanobacteria blooms can be toxic to humans and animals. 蓝细菌大量繁殖会对人类和动物造成毒害。

Example 2:  Scientists believe cyanobacteria were among the first organisms to produce oxygen on Earth. 科学家认为蓝细菌是最早在地球上产生氧气的生物之一。| 近义词: blue-green algae | 反义词: N/A | Noun
