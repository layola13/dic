-alogy: /ˈælədʒi/
suffix
-学，-论，表示“学科”，“论述”
A suffix used to form nouns meaning "the study of," "theory of," or "practice of."
Biology is the study of living organisms. 生物学是研究生物体的学科。
Mineralogy is the study of minerals. 矿物学是研究矿物的学科。
