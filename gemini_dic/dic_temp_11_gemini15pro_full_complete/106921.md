lacertus: /ləˈsɜːrtəs/ /læˈsɜːrtəs/ | n. | 上臂的肌肉；臂；力量 | The large muscle in the upper arm.  It refers to the muscular part of the arm, especially the upper arm, often associated with strength and power. |  He flexed his lacertus, showing off his impressive muscles. 他弯曲了他的上臂肌肉，炫耀着他令人印象深刻的肌肉。| The blacksmith's powerful lacertus allowed him to swing the hammer with ease. 铁匠强壮的上臂使他能够轻松地挥动锤子。 | synonyms: biceps, muscle, brawn, sinew | antonyms: weakness, frailty | noun
