gley:/ɡleɪ/ | n. |潜育层，灰黏土| A waterlogged soil layer that develops a characteristic bluish-gray or greenish-gray color due to the reduction of iron and other elements under anaerobic conditions. | The gley horizon in the soil profile indicated poor drainage. 土壤剖面中的潜育层表明排水不良。| The presence of gley suggests the area is prone to flooding.  潜育层的出现表明该地区容易发生洪水。| synonyms: glei, hydromorphic soil | antonyms: well-drained soil | noun
