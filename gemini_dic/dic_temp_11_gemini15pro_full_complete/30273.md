cemental:/sɪˈmɛntəl/| adj.| 水泥的；胶结的 |Relating to or resembling cement; adhesive. |The cemental properties of the substance made it ideal for bonding. 这种物质的胶结特性使其成为理想的粘合剂。| Example:  The cemental layer of the tooth is important for its stability. 牙齿的粘合层对其稳定性很重要。|Synonyms: adhesive, binding, cohesive|Antonyms: separating, detaching|adjective

cemental:/sɪˈmɛntəl/| n.| 牙骨质 |A specialized calcified substance covering the root of a tooth. |The dentist examined the cemental area of the tooth for damage. 牙医检查了牙齿的牙骨质区域是否有损伤。| Example:  Loss of cemental attachment can lead to tooth loss. 牙骨质附着丧失会导致牙齿脱落。|Synonyms: cementum|Antonyms: N/A|noun
