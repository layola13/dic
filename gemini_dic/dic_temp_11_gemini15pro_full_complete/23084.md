boundlessnesses:/ˈbaʊndləsnɪsɪz/
|n.|无限；无边无际|The boundlessnesses of the universe inspire awe and wonder. 宇宙的浩瀚无垠激发了敬畏和惊奇。|Boundlessnesses refer to the state or quality of being boundless or limitless, often used in the plural to emphasize the vastness or multiple aspects of something without limits.  It signifies a lack of boundaries or constraints, extending infinitely in various dimensions or aspects.|The poet attempted to capture the boundlessnesses of human imagination. 这位诗人试图捕捉人类想象力的无限性。|近义词：immensities, infinities, vastnesses|反义词：limitations, finitudes|词性: 名词

词源:  From boundless + -ness + -es.  Boundless itself comes from bound (limit) + -less (without).
