hybridizers:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzərz/
n.
杂交者，杂交育种者
People who create hybrids, often referring to plants or animals.  This could be through controlled breeding or genetic modification.
Luther Burbank was one of the most famous hybridizers of the 20th century.  路德·伯班克是20世纪最著名的杂交育种者之一。

The hybridizers are working on a new strain of drought-resistant wheat.  这些杂交育种者正在研究一种新的抗旱小麦品种。

breeders, crossbreeders
