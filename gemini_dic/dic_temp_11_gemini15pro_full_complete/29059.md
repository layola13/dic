caruncular: /kəˈrʌŋkjʊlər/
adjective
肉阜的；小肉瘤的
Relating to, or having the form of, a caruncle. Small, fleshy outgrowth.
The caruncular area around the bird's eye was bright red. 这只鸟眼睛周围的肉阜区域呈鲜红色。
caruncular swelling of the urethra 尿道肉阜状肿胀
Near synonyms: fleshy, meaty, pulpy
Antonyms: smooth, flat

caruncle: /ˈkærʌŋkl/
noun
肉阜；小肉瘤
A small, fleshy outgrowth; a wattle.  Specifically, in anatomy and zoology, a small, fleshy protuberance, often red or brightly colored.
The turkey's caruncle wobbled as it gobbled. 火鸡的肉垂随着它的叫声而晃动。
Near synonyms: wattle, comb, crest
Antonyms: N/A (lack of a specific antonym; one might use terms like "smooth area" in specific contexts)
词性: 名词
