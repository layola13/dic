anti-critical:/ˌæntiˈkrɪtɪkəl/
adjective
反批评的；反对批评的；不加批评的
Opposed to or discouraging criticism; uncritical.
The government's anti-critical stance stifled public discourse. 政府的反批评立场扼杀了公众讨论。
His anti-critical attitude towards the project made it difficult to identify its flaws. 他对该项目不加批评的态度使得很难发现其缺陷。
synonyms: uncritical, noncritical, accepting, tolerant, unjudging, undiscriminating
antonyms: critical, analytical, discerning, judgmental
词性: 形容词
