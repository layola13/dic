fumigant:/ˈfjuːmɪɡənt/
n.|熏蒸剂；熏蒸消毒剂|A chemical substance used in the form of a gas or vapor to destroy pests, such as insects, rodents, or fungi.|The warehouse was treated with a fumigant to eliminate insects.|近义词:pesticide, insecticide, disinfectant|反义词:|词性:名词
