inapprehension:/ˌɪnˌæprɪˈhɛnʃən/| n. |不安，忧虑，恐惧|A feeling of anxiety or dread about something that might happen.|She waited for the results with inapprehension. 她焦急地等待着结果。|Synonyms: anxiety, worry, dread, fear, unease, apprehension, foreboding, misgiving, disquiet, perturbation, solicitude, trepidation|Antonyms: calmness, composure, tranquility, serenity, peace of mind|noun
