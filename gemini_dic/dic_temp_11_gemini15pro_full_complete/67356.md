extensibleness:/ɪkˌstɛnsɪbəlnəs/| n. |可扩展性，延展性|The quality of being extensive or capable of being extended.  The capacity to be enlarged or stretched.|The extensibleness of the rubber band allowed it to fit around the large package.|近义词：expandability, stretchability, flexibility|反义词: inflexibility, rigidity|词性:noun
Etymology: From "extensible" + "-ness". "Extensible" comes from Late Latin "extensibilis", from Latin "extendere" (to stretch out), from "ex-" (out) + "tendere" (to stretch).
