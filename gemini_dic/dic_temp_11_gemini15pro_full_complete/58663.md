dumfounding:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪŋ/
adjective
令人震惊的；使人目瞪口呆的
Extremely surprising or shocking; astonishing.
Her sudden resignation was absolutely dumfounding. 她突然辞职绝对令人震惊。
The magician's tricks were dumfounding. 魔术师的戏法令人叹为观止。
近义词:astonishing, astounding, bewildering, shocking, stunning
反义词:unsurprising, expected, predictable
词性:adjective
词源:From dumbfound, from dumb (unable to speak) + confound (to confuse and surprise).
