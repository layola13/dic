incomprehensiblenesses:/ɪnˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪblənəsɪz/ /ɪnˌkɑːmprɪˈhensəblənəsɪz/
n.
不可理解性；费解性
The incomprehensibleness of the ancient text baffled the scholars.  古代文本的费解性难倒了学者们。
His motives remain wrapped in incomprehensibleness. 他的动机仍然笼罩在神秘之中。
近义词:unintelligibility, impenetrability, obscurity
反义词:clarity, comprehensibility, understandability
词性:名词

incomprehensible:/ɪnˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪbl/ /ɪnˌkɑːmprɪˈhensəbl/
adj.
不可理解的；费解的
The instructions were completely incomprehensible.  这些指示完全无法理解。
His behavior is incomprehensible to me. 他的行为让我无法理解。
近义词:unintelligible, impenetrable, obscure
反义词:clear, comprehensible, understandable
词性:形容词

comprehensible:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪbl/ /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhensəbl/
adj.
可理解的；容易理解的
The explanation was clear and comprehensible.  解释清晰易懂。
The book is written in a clear and comprehensible style. 这本书的写作风格清晰易懂。
近义词:understandable, intelligible, clear
反义词:incomprehensible, unintelligible, obscure
词性:形容词

comprehend:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhend/ /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhend/
v.
理解；领会
I couldn't comprehend what he was saying. 我无法理解他在说什么。
She fully comprehended the seriousness of the situation. 她完全理解了情况的严重性。
近义词:understand, grasp, perceive
反义词:misunderstand, misinterpret
词性:动词
