dihedron:/daɪˈhiːdrən/ | n. | 二面角; 二面体 |A figure formed by two intersecting planes or half-planes. A dihedron is characterized by its dihedral angle, the angle between its two faces.| The dihedral angle between the two wings of the airplane is crucial for stability.  飞机两翼之间的二面角对稳定性至关重要。| synonym: dihedral angle | antonym: N/A | noun

dihedral:/daɪˈhiːdrəl/ | adj. | 二面的 |Having two plane faces or sides.  Relating to a dihedron or dihedral angle.| The dihedral symmetry of the crystal is evident in its structure. 晶体的二面体对称性在其结构中很明显。 | synonym: two-sided, bilateral | antonym: N/A | adjective
