monocycle:/ˈmɒnəʊˌsaɪkəl/ (US: /ˈmɑːnəˌsaɪkəl/) | n. |独轮车|A cycle with only one wheel.|He rode the monocycle across the park. 他骑着独轮车穿过公园。|Synonym: unicycle|Antonym: bicycle|noun

monocyclic:/ˌmɒnəʊˈsaɪklɪk/ (US: /ˌmɑːnəˈsaɪklɪk/) | adj. |单环的；单周期的|Having a single cycle or recurring at intervals of a single cycle.|The monocyclic plant blooms only once a year. 这种单周期植物一年只开一次花。|Synonym: single-cycle|Antonym: polycyclic|adjective
