hosannaing:/hoʊˈzænəɪŋ/
| n.|欢呼，赞美，歌颂|The act of praising or expressing adoration, especially in a religious context.  Derived from the Hebrew word "hoshanna," originally meaning "save us now," it has evolved to express joyful praise and adoration.|The crowd was hosannaing the returning hero.| 近义词: praising, acclaiming, lauding, extolling|反义词: condemning, criticizing, denouncing|词性:Noun (Gerund or present participle of the verb "to hosanna")

hosanna:/hoʊˈzænə/
| n. int. vt.|和散那，欢呼，赞美|An exclamation of praise and adoration, especially in a religious context.
