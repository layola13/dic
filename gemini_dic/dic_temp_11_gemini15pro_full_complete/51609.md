devata:/dɪˈvɑːtə/  /deɪˈvɑːtɑː/
noun
神，神灵
A deity; a god or goddess, especially in Hinduism.
The temple was dedicated to a powerful devata.  这座寺庙供奉着一个强大的神灵。
Devata are often invoked for protection and blessings.  人们经常祈求神灵保佑和赐福。
Synonym: deity, god, goddess
Antonym: demon, devil
