baronetage:/ˌbærənɪtɪdʒ/
n.
男爵爵位；男爵名册
The rank of baronet; the collective body of baronets.
The baronetage is a hereditary rank of honour. 男爵爵位是一种世袭的荣誉称号。
The complete baronetage of England. 英格兰完整的男爵名册。
synonyms: peerage, nobility, aristocracy
antonyms: commonalty, peasantry


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *baronnettage*, from *baronnet* (baronet) + *-age*.
