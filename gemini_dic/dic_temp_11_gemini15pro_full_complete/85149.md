hardscapes:/ˈhɑːrdskeɪps/
n.
硬景观，硬质景观
Non-living elements of landscaping, such as paved areas, retaining walls, and fences.
The hardscapes of the garden included a brick patio, a stone walkway, and a wooden fence.  花园的硬景观包括一个砖砌露台、一条石头走道和一个木栅栏。
近义词：hard landscaping
反义词：softscapes

词性: noun
