cervelat:/sərˈvɛlət/ /sərˈvəˌlɑː/ | n. |香肠，萨拉米香肠|A type of highly seasoned smoked sausage, often made of beef and pork.  It's similar to salami. |例句：We had cervelat and cheese for lunch. 我们午餐吃了香肠和奶酪。

例句：The cervelat was sliced thinly and served with crackers.  香肠被切成薄片，与饼干一起食用。|近义词：salami, sausage|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cervelas*, ultimately from Italian *cervellata*, from *cervello* ("brain"), because it was originally made with brains.
