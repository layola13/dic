anomalistic: /ˌænəm.əˈlɪs.tɪk/ | adj. |  异常的，反常的 |  Deviating from the common rule; irregular; abnormal.  Relating to or characterized by anomaly. Often used in astronomy to describe orbits that are not perfectly elliptical due to perturbations. | The comet's anomalistic period is slightly different from its sidereal period. 这颗彗星的反常周期与其恒星周期略有不同。 | The anomalistic precession of the Moon's orbit is a complex phenomenon. 月球轨道的异常进动是一个复杂的现象。 | 近义词: aberrant, atypical, deviant, irregular, unusual | 反义词: normal, regular, typical, standard | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *anomalisticus, from Greek ἀνώμαλος (anōmalos, “uneven, irregular”) + -istic.
