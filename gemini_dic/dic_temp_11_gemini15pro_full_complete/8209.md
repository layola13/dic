anti-european:/ˌæntiˌjʊərəˈpiːən/
adjective
反欧洲的；敌视欧洲的
Opposed to European integration or policies, or hostile towards Europe or Europeans.
His anti-European sentiments were well known. 他反欧洲的情绪是众所周知的。
Synonyms: Eurosceptic, Europhobic
Antonyms: pro-European, Europhile
词性: 形容词


anti-european:/ˌæntiˌjʊərəˈpiːən/
noun
反欧洲主义者
A person who is opposed to European integration or policies.
The anti-Europeans are gaining ground in some countries.  在一些国家，反欧洲主义者正在得势。
Synonyms: Eurosceptic, Europhobe
Antonyms: pro-European, Europhile
词性: 名词
