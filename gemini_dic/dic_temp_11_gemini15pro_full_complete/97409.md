infringement: /ɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt/ | n. | 侵犯，违反 |  The act of breaking the terms of a law, agreement, etc.; violation. | Downloading copyrighted movies without permission is an infringement of copyright.  未经许可下载受版权保护的电影是侵犯版权的行为。 | synonyms: violation, breach, transgression, contravention | antonyms: compliance, obedience, observance | noun

infringement: /ɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt/ | n. | 侵害，侵权 | The action of limiting or undermining something. |  The construction of the new highway was seen as an infringement on their peaceful way of life. 新公路的建设被视为对他们平静生活方式的侵犯。 | synonyms: encroachment, intrusion, trespass | antonyms: respect, upholding | noun
