diverless:/ˈdaɪvərləs/| adj.|无潜水员的；不需要潜水员的|Characterized by the absence of divers; not requiring divers.  It refers to underwater operations or activities that can be conducted without the physical presence of a diver.|例句：Diverless underwater inspection techniques are becoming increasingly popular. 无潜水员的水下检测技术正变得越来越流行。

例句：The diverless system allows for repairs to be carried out at greater depths.  无潜水员系统允许在更深的深度进行维修。|近义词：unmanned, remotely operated, automated|反义词:manned, diver-operated|词性:形容词
