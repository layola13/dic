ferruginous:/fəˈruːdʒɪnəs/
adjective
铁锈色的；含铁的
containing or consisting of iron oxide; resembling iron rust in color
The soil in this area is ferruginous. 这个地区的土壤是铁锈色的。
The stream flowed over ferruginous rocks. 小溪流过铁锈色的岩石。
近义词:rusty, iron-rich, iron-bearing, ochreous
反义词:non-ferruginous
