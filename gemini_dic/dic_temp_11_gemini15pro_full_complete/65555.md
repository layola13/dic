euthyneura:/juːˈθɪnjʊrə/ | n. | 真后腮亚纲 | A large and diverse subclass of Gastropoda mollusks, characterized by having their mantle cavity oriented anteriorly and their nervous system untorted.  They include pulmonates (land snails and slugs) and opisthobranchs (sea slugs, sea hares, etc.). |  Euthyneura is a significant group within the Gastropoda. 真后腮亚纲是腹足纲中的一个重要类群。|  Opisthobranchia, Pulmonata |  Streptoneura(扭神经亚纲, 已弃用, 但可以表示概念上的对立面) | noun
