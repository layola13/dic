coquettish:/kɒˈkɛtɪʃ/ /koʊˈkɛtɪʃ/| adj.|卖弄风情的；娇媚的；轻佻的|Characteristic of a coquette; flirtatious.  Behaving in such a way as to suggest a playful sexual attraction; flirtatious.|She gave him a coquettish glance. 她娇媚地瞥了他一眼。|近义词：flirtatious, playful, teasing, alluring|反义词：modest, demure, reserved|词性:adjective
Etymology: From French *coquet*, diminutive of *coq* (cock, rooster).  Originally referred to a woman who trifles with men's affections.
