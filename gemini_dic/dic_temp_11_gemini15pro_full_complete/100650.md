intombment: /ɪnˈtuːmment/ | n. | 入葬，埋葬；安葬 | The act of placing a body in a tomb, especially a large vault.  |  The king's intombment was a grand ceremony.  国王的葬礼是一场盛大的仪式。| 近义词: burial, interment | 反义词: exhumation | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin intombamentum, from intombare "to place in a tomb," from in- "in" + tomba "tomb."
