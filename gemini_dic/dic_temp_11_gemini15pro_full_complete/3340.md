affranchise:/əˈfræntʃaɪz/
verb
解放，赋予公民权
To grant freedom to; to give voting rights.
例句：The Nineteenth Amendment affranchised women in the United States.  美国第十九修正案赋予了妇女选举权。

例句：The slaves were affranchised after the Civil War. 内战后奴隶们被解放了。

synonym: liberate, enfranchise, free, empower
antonym: enslave, disenfranchise, oppress, subjugate
词性: verb
