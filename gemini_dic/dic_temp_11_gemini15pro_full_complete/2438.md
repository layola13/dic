adjudicator:/əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtər/| n.|裁决者，裁判员，评判员|A person who makes a formal judgment or decision about something.|The adjudicator listened carefully to both sides of the argument before making her decision. 裁判员在作出决定之前仔细听取了双方的论点。|Synonyms: judge, arbiter, umpire, referee|Antonyms: defendant, plaintiff|noun
