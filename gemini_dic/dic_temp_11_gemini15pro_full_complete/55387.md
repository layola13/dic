distemperment:/dɪsˈtɛmpəmənt/ | n. | 精神错乱，疾病，失调 | A state of being disordered or unbalanced, often referring to a disturbed mental or emotional state, or a physical ailment.  |  The dog's distemperment made him snappy and unpredictable. 这只狗的疾病使它暴躁且难以预测。 | Synonyms: ailment, sickness, malady, disorder, instability, derangement | Antonyms: health, wellness, stability, balance, order | noun

distemperment:/dɪsˈtɛmpəmənt/ | n. | (绘画) 颜色调和不良 |  A poor or faulty blending of colors in painting. | The distemperment of the colors in the painting made it appear muddy. 这幅画的颜色调和不良，使它看起来很浑浊。 | Synonyms: imbalance, discordance | Antonyms: harmony, balance | noun
