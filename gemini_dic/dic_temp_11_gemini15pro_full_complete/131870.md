nonconsumptiveness:/ˌnɒnkənˈsʌmptɪvnəs/ | n. |非消耗性，非消费性 |The nonconsumptiveness of the resource means it can be used repeatedly without being depleted.  这种资源的非消耗性意味着它可以重复使用而不会枯竭。|  This digital product boasts nonconsumptiveness, allowing unlimited access and usage.  这款数字产品以其非消耗性而自豪，允许无限访问和使用。 | 近义词:  inexhaustibility, undepletability | 反义词: consumability, depletability | 词性: noun

Etymology: non- + consumptive + -ness

"Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."

"Consumptive" is an adjective relating to consumption, the act of using up a resource. It derives from the Latin "consumere," meaning "to consume, use up, or waste."

"-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
