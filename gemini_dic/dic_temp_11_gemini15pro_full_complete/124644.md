mouthily:/ˈmaʊθɪli/ | adv. | 口头上地；夸夸其谈地；饶舌地 | In a manner characterized by excessive talking, often boastful or indiscreet. | He boasted mouthily about his accomplishments. 他夸夸其谈地吹嘘自己的成就。 | 近义词：garrulously, loquaciously, volubly | 反义词: taciturnly, reticently | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From mouth (n.) + -ily (adv. suffix).
