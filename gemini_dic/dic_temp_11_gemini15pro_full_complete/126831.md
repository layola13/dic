naphthalidine:/ˈnæfθəlɪdiːn/| n.|萘啶，萘胺|A crystalline aromatic hydrocarbon, C10H8, analogous to quinoline and containing a nitrogen atom in place of one of the CH groups of naphthalene. It is the parent substance of numerous dyes.|Naphthalidine derivatives are being investigated for their potential use in organic semiconductors.|近义词:naphthylamine|反义词:None|noun
