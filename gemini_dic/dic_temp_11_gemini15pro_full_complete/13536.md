attempt:/əˈtempt/ |vt.| 尝试，试图；攻击|To try to do something, especially something difficult. |He attempted to escape from prison. 他试图越狱。| try, endeavor, strive | desist, abandon | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *atempter, from Latin attemptare "to try, test, attack, tempt," frequentative of Latin temptare "to handle, touch, feel, try, test, tempt," from PIE root *tem- "to cut."
