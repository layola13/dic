misalliance:/ˌmɪs.əˈlaɪ.əns/ | n. | 不合适的婚姻，门不当户不对的婚姻 | A marriage between people who are unsuitable for each other, especially because of differences in social status. |  例句：Their marriage was considered a misalliance because of the vast difference in their social backgrounds. 他们的婚姻被认为是不合适的，因为他们的社会背景差异巨大。

例句：The play explores the complexities of a misalliance between a wealthy businessman and a working-class woman.  这戏剧探讨了一个富商和一个工人阶级妇女之间不匹配婚姻的复杂性。


近义词：bad match, unsuitable marriage, mésalliance | 反义词：good match, suitable marriage | 词性：名词
