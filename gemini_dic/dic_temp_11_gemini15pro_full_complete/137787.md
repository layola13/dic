nonvenal:/nɒnˈviːnəl/
adjective
不腐败的，廉洁的，公正的
Not susceptible to bribery; incorruptible.
The judge was known for his nonvenal character and unwavering commitment to justice.  这位法官以其廉洁的品格和对正义的坚定承诺而闻名。
She sought a nonvenal politician to represent her district. 她寻找一位廉洁的政治家来代表她所在的地区。
近义词:incorruptible, honest, upright, impartial
反义词:venal, corrupt, dishonest, bribable
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *non venalis*, from non "not" + venalis "for sale, venal".
