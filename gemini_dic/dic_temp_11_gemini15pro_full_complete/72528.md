flushness:/ˈflʌʃnɪs/
n.
平整；齐平；同高
The flushness of the surface was checked with a spirit level.
用水平仪检查了表面的平整度。

The carpenter ensured the flushness of the door with the frame.
木匠确保门与门框齐平。

Evenness; levelness.
平坦；水平。

The flushness of the two surfaces makes them appear seamless.
两个表面的齐平使它们看起来天衣无缝。

Near synonyms: levelness, evenness, smoothness
近义词：水平，均匀，光滑

Antonyms: unevenness, roughness, bumpiness
反义词：不平坦，粗糙，凹凸不平

词性: noun
