imaginist:/ɪˈmædʒɪnɪst/| n. adj.|意象派诗人，意象派的|A member of a school of poetry that flourished in England and the United States between 1912 and 1917, aiming at clarity of expression through the use of precise images.  A poet or writer who uses precise imagery.|Ezra Pound was a prominent imaginist.  埃兹拉·庞德是一位著名的意象派诗人。|The poem is written in a characteristically imaginist style. 这首诗是用典型的意象派风格写的。|synonyms: imagist, symbolist|antonyms: realist, naturalist|noun, adjective
