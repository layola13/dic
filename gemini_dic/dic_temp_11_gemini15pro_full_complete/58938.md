dust:/dʌst/ | n. v. | 灰尘，尘土；粉末 | Fine, dry powder consisting of tiny particles of earth or waste matter. |  The furniture was covered in dust. 家具上满是灰尘。| dirt, grime, powder | cleanliness | noun
dust:/dʌst/ | v. |  掸掉灰尘；撒（粉末） | Remove dust from a surface or sprinkle with a powder. |  She dusted the shelves carefully. 她仔细地掸了掸架子上的灰尘。| clean, wipe, sprinkle | soil | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dūst, from Proto-Germanic *dunstaz (“vapor, dust”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewh₂- (“to rise, fly up, smoke, dust”). Cognate with Dutch duist (“dust cloud, mist”), German Dunst (“vapor, steam”).
