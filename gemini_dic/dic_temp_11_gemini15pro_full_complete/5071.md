all-ticket:/ɔːlˈtɪkɪt/ | adj. | 全票入场的；只有持票者才能入场的 |  A system where everyone attending an event must have a pre-purchased ticket, no tickets are sold at the door. |  The concert was all-ticket, so we had to book online in advance. 这场音乐会是全票入场的，所以我们必须提前在网上预订。| Synonym: sold out, pre-sold | Antonym: open admission, general admission | adjective
