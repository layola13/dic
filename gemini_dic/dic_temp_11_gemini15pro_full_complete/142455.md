oscilloscopically:/ˌɒsɪləˈskɒpɪkli/ | adv. | 用示波器地；示波地 | In a manner relating to or using an oscilloscope; by means of an oscilloscope. | The signal was analyzed oscilloscopically to determine its frequency.  用示波器分析信号以确定其频率。 | 近义词: with an oscilloscope, using an oscilloscope | 反义词: None | 词性: 副词

词源: From oscilloscope + -ically. Oscilloscope comes from  oscillate (from Latin oscillāre ‘swing’) + -scope (from Greek skopeîn ‘look at’).
