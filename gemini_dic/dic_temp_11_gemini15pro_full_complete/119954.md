milled:/mɪld/
adjective
碾磨的，磨碎的
ground or crushed in a mill.
The baker used milled flour to make the bread. 面包师用碾磨的面粉做面包。
milled:/mɪld/
verb
碾磨，磨碎
past tense and past participle of "mill".
The grain was milled into flour. 谷物被磨成面粉.
近义词: ground, crushed, pulverized
反义词: whole, unprocessed
词性: adjective, verb
词源: From Middle English *millen, from Old English mylen (“to mill”), from Proto-Germanic *milnōną (“to grind, crush”), from Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (“to grind, crush”). Cognate with Dutch malen (“to grind, paint”), German mahlen (“to grind”), Swedish mala (“to grind”).
