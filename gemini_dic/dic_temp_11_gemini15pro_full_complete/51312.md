detainable:/dɪˈteɪnəbl/
adjective
可拘留的；可扣留的
able to be detained.
The police said the suspect was detainable. 警方说嫌疑人可以被拘留。
The goods were detainable by customs. 海关可以扣留这些货物。

近义词:arrestable, confinable
反义词:releasable

词性:adjective
词源:From detain + -able.  Detain comes from Old French detener, from Latin detinēre "to hold back, keep back," from de- "away, from" + tenēre "to hold."
