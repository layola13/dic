monometer:/məˈnɒmɪtər/ |/məˈnɑːmɪtər/| n. |单音步，单音节诗|A verse or line consisting of a single metrical unit (a foot).|The poem was written entirely in monometers, creating a stark and rhythmic effect. 这首诗完全是用单音步写成的，创造了一种鲜明而有节奏的效果。|  The monometer intensified the emotional impact of each word.  这个单音步增强了每个词的情感冲击力。 | synonym: single foot | antonym:  polymeter| noun
