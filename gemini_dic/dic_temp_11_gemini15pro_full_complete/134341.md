nonintellectual:/ˌnɒnɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl/
adjective
非智力的；不需动脑筋的
Not involving or requiring much intelligence.
He prefers nonintellectual pursuits, like sports and video games. 他更喜欢非智力型的追求，比如体育和电子游戏。
Synonyms: unintellectual, non-academic, simple, unscholarly
Antonyms: intellectual, academic, complex, scholarly
词性: 形容词


nonintellectual:/ˌnɒnɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl/
noun
非知识分子
A person who is not interested in intellectual pursuits.
The club attracted a mix of intellectuals and nonintellectuals.  这个俱乐部吸引了知识分子和非知识分子的混合人群。
Synonyms: philistine, lowbrow
Antonyms: intellectual, highbrow
词性: 名词
