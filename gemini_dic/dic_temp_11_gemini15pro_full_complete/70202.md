fibrolite:/ˈfaɪbrəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 纤维石，硅线石 |A mineral form of aluminum silicate, Al2SiO5, occurring in fibrous masses, often as a component of metamorphic rocks. | Fibrolite is a common constituent of high-grade metamorphic rocks. 纤维石是高级变质岩的常见成分。|  Fibrolite can be used as a refractory material. 纤维石可以用作耐火材料。 |Synonyms: sillimanite, bucholzite | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *fibr(o)-*  "fiber" + *-lite* "stone, mineral."  Ultimately from Latin *fibra* "fiber" and Greek *lithos* "stone."
