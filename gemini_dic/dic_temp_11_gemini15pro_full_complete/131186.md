nonbudding:/ˌnɒnˈbʌdɪŋ/
adjective
非芽殖的，不发芽的
Not producing buds; relating to or characterized by a process of reproduction that does not involve budding.
Nonbudding yeast strains were used in the experiment to control for growth patterns.  实验中使用了非芽殖酵母菌株来控制生长模式。
The researchers studied the nonbudding reproduction process of the bacteria. 研究人员研究了这种细菌的非芽殖繁殖过程。
近义词: non-germinating, non-sprouting
反义词: budding, germinating
词性: adjective
