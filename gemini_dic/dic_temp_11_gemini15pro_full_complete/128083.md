nervous:/ˈnɜːrvəs/ (US) /ˈnɜːvəs/ (UK)| adj.|紧张的，焦虑的，神经质的|Easily agitated or alarmed; tending to worry.|例句：I'm nervous about the upcoming exam. 我对即将到来的考试感到紧张。|近义词：anxious, apprehensive, uneasy, tense|反义词：calm, relaxed, composed, confident|词性:形容词

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French *nervus, from medieval Latin nervosus, from Latin nervus ‘sinew, nerve’.
