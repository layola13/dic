delineation:/dɪˌlɪnɪˈeɪʃn/
n.|描绘，勾勒，轮廓|The artist's skillful delineations of the human form were breathtaking.  这位艺术家对人体形态的巧妙描绘令人叹为观止。|例句：The report provides a clear delineation of the company's financial situation.  这份报告清晰地描绘了公司的财务状况。|近义词：description, portrayal, outline, sketch, depiction|反义词：obscuration, blurring|词性：名词

delineation:/dɪˌlɪnɪˈeɪʃn/
n.|界限，分界线|The treaty established a clear delineation between the two countries. 该条约在两国之间建立了明确的界限。|例句：The delineation of responsibilities between the departments was ambiguous. 各部门之间的职责划分不明确。|近义词：demarcation, boundary, division|反义词：fusion, integration|词性：名词


词源:  From Latin *dēlineātiō*, from dēlineāre (“to sketch, trace out”), from dē- (“from, down from, away, out”) + lineāre (“to make a line”), from līnea (“line”).
