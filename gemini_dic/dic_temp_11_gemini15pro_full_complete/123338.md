monolatry:/ˈmɒnəˌleɪtri/ /ˈmɑːnəˌleɪtri/
n.
一神崇拜; 敬奉唯一之神，但不否认其他神存在
The worship of one god without denying the existence of others.
Ancient Israelites practiced a form of monolatry, worshipping Yahweh but acknowledging the existence of other gods.  古代以色列人实行一种一神崇拜的形式，崇拜耶和华，但承认其他神灵的存在。
The king demanded monolatry, declaring his god supreme while tolerating the worship of other deities. 国王要求一神崇拜，宣布他的神是至高无上的，同时容忍对其他神灵的崇拜。

近义词:henotheism
反义词:polytheism, atheism
词性:noun

词源:
From Greek monos "single" + latreia "worship."  源自希腊语 monos “单个” + latreia “崇拜”。
