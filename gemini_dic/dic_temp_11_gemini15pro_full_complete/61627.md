emigrational:/ˌemɪˈɡreɪʃənəl/
adjective
移民的; 移居国外的
Relating to emigration; of or pertaining to the act of leaving one's country to settle in another.
The emigrational wave from Ireland to America during the potato famine was immense.  爱尔兰在马铃薯饥荒期间向美国的移民潮非常巨大。
The family faced many emigrational challenges. 这个家庭面临许多移民挑战。
近义词: migratory, expatriate
反义词: immigrational, repatriation
词性: adjective

词源:  From emigration + -al.  Emigration ultimately derives from the Latin ex "out of" + migrare "to migrate."
