flowerinesses:/ˈflaʊərɪnəsɪz/
noun
华丽的辞藻；辞藻的繁复
the quality of being flowery, or using too many complicated words
His prose is known for its flowerinesses. 他的散文以其华丽的辞藻而闻名。
近义词:ornateness, elaborateness, flamboyance
反义词:simplicity, plainness
词性:n.


Etymology:
From floweriness + -es (plural suffix). Floweriness is derived from flowery (full of flowers or flowery language) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality). Flowery comes from flower + -y (suffix forming adjectives).
