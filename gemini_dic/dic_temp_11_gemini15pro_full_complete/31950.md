chelonian: /kɪˈloʊniən/ | adj. n. |龟类的，乌龟；甲鱼|Relating to or resembling a turtle or tortoise.  A turtle or tortoise.|例句：The Galapagos Islands are famous for their chelonian inhabitants. 加拉帕戈斯群岛以其龟类居民而闻名。|例句：The chelonian slowly made its way across the road. 那只乌龟慢慢地穿过马路。|近义词：turtle, tortoise, testudine |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *chelonianus, from Greek χελώνη (chelōnē, “tortoise, turtle”) + -anus.
