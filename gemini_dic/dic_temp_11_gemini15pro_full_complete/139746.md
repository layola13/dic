ocker:/ˈɒkə(r)/ (AU) /ˈɑːkər/ (US)| n. |澳洲佬；粗俗的澳洲人|A person who is regarded as being typically Australian, often associated with stereotypical characteristics such as boisterousness, masculinity, and a working-class background.  Sometimes used pejoratively. |He's a real ocker, always talking about footy and beer. 他是个典型的澳洲佬，总是谈论澳式足球和啤酒。|近义词：bogan, Aussie, bloke|反义词:sophisticate, refined person|noun

ocker:/ˈɒkə(r)/ (AU) /ˈɑːkər/ (US)| adj. |粗俗的；典型的澳大利亚的|Characteristic of or relating to an ocker. |He had a broad ocker accent. 他操着一口浓重的澳洲口音。|近义词：Australian, bogan, unsophisticated|反义词:sophisticated, refined|adjective
