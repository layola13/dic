nonnegligence:/ˌnɑːnˈnɛɡlɪdʒəns/
n.
无过失；免责；无疏忽
The finding of nonnegligence means the company is not liable for the damages.  无过失的裁决意味着公司无需对损害赔偿负责。
Absence of negligence; due care. 没有过失；应有的注意。
The report concluded that there was nonnegligence on the part of the driver. 报告的结论是司机方面没有过失。
Synonyms: due care, diligence, carefulness
Antonyms: negligence, carelessness, neglect
noun
