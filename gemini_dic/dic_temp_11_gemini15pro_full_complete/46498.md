dairy:/ˈdeəri/ (US), /ˈdɛəri/ (UK)| n. adj.|乳制品；牛奶场；乳品店；乳品业|A place where milk and cream are stored and milk products are made, or a business that sells these products.  Can also be used as an adjective to describe products made from milk.|

例句：
We buy our milk from the local dairy. 我们从当地的奶场买牛奶。

She works at a dairy farm. 她在一个奶牛场工作。

近义词：creamery, milk plant|
反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|
词性：名词, 形容词
