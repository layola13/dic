nonsubstantiveness:/ˌnɒnsəbˈstænʃɪvnəs/
|n.|非实体性；无实质内容；空洞性|The nonsubstantiveness of the argument made it easy to refute.  这个论点的空洞性使其很容易被反驳。|The report was criticized for its nonsubstantiveness and lack of concrete proposals.  这份报告因其缺乏实质内容和具体建议而受到批评。|近义词:insignificance, immateriality, emptiness, hollowness|反义词:substantiveness, substance, materiality|词性:名词
