audiotape:/ˈɔːdiəʊteɪp/ (UK) /ˈɔːdioʊteɪp/ (US)| n. |录音带；磁带|A magnetic tape for recording sound.|例句：I found an old audiotape of my grandfather singing. 我找到了一盘我祖父唱歌的旧录音带。

例句：The interview was recorded on audiotape. 采访是用录音带录制的。

近义词：cassette, tape recording|反义词: None|词性: noun
