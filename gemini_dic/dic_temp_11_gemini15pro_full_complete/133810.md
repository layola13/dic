nonglacially:/ˌnɒnˈɡleɪʃəli/ | adv. | 非冰川地；非冰期地 |  Not related to, caused by, or occurring during a glacial period.  Relating to processes or features that are not of glacial origin. | The valley was formed nonglacially, by the erosive action of the river.  这个山谷是由河流的侵蚀作用而非冰川作用形成的。 | Synonym: fluvially, interglacially | Antonym: glacially | adverb
