multiaxial:/ˌmʌltiˈæksiəl/
adjective
多轴的
Having or relating to multiple axes; involving several axes, especially several axes of a crystal or a coordinate system.
The psychiatrist diagnosed her with a multiaxial disorder. 精神科医生诊断她患有多轴障碍。
multifaceted, polyhedral
uniaxial, single-axis
