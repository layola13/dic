brontosaurian:/ˌbrɒntəˈsɔːriən/| adj. n.|雷龙的；像雷龙一样巨大的；迟钝笨拙的人|Of or relating to the Brontosaurus; resembling a Brontosaurus, especially in size or slowness.|例句：The brontosaurian skeleton dominated the museum hall.  雷龙骨架占据了博物馆大厅的主导地位。|例句：His brontosaurian movements made him seem clumsy. 他像雷龙一样迟钝的动作使他显得笨拙。|近义词：gigantic, enormous, colossal, massive, huge, immense|反义词:tiny, small, minuscule|词性:形容词，名词

brontosaurian:/ˌbrɒntəˈsɔːriən/| n.|巨大的动物；迟钝笨拙的人|A large, slow-moving animal or person.|例句：The CEO, a known brontosaurian, finally agreed to the changes.  这位首席执行官，一个众所周知的迟钝笨拙的人，最终同意了这些改变。|例句：Metaphorically, the bureaucracy can be seen as a brontosaurian.  比喻地说，官僚机构可以被看作是一只雷龙。|近义词：dinosaur, behemoth, leviathan|反义词:none|词性:名词


词源:  From *Brontosaurus* + *-ian*.  *Brontosaurus* comes from Greek *brontē* (thunder) + *sauros* (lizard).
