causal:/ˈkɔːzl/| adj.|因果的，原因的|Relating to, or acting as a cause; relating to cause and effect. |The study demonstrated a causal link between smoking and lung cancer. 这项研究证明了吸烟和肺癌之间的因果关系。|Smoking is a causal factor in the development of lung cancer. 吸烟是导致肺癌的一个原因。|Synonyms: causative, generating, originative|Antonyms: resultant, resulting, consequential|adjective
