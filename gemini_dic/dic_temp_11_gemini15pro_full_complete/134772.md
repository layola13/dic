nonmetallic:/ˌnɒn.məˈtæl.ɪk/ /ˌnɑːn.məˈtæl.ɪk/| adj.|非金属的|Not having the characteristics of a metal.|例句：Sulfur is a nonmetallic element. 硫是一种非金属元素。|例句：She prefers nonmetallic jewelry. 她更喜欢非金属珠宝。|近义词：non-metal|反义词:metallic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + metallic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Metallic" refers to the properties or appearance of metals.
