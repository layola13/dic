diodes:/ˈdaɪəʊdz/ (UK), /ˈdaɪoʊdz/ (US)| n. |二极管|A semiconductor device with two terminals, typically allowing the flow of current in one direction only.|The radio uses several diodes. 这台收音机使用了多个二极管。|近义词：rectifier (整流器 - 功能近似)|反义词: N/A (二极管没有直接的反义词)|noun
