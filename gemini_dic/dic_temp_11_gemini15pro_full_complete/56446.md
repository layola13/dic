dolmenic:/dɒlˈmɛnɪk/
adjective
巨石墓的；巨石文化的
relating to or resembling a dolmen.
The site contained several dolmenic structures, indicating a prehistoric burial ground.  该遗址包含几个巨石墓结构，表明这是一个史前墓地。
The archaeologist specialized in dolmenic architecture.  这位考古学家专门研究巨石墓建筑。
近义词: megalithic
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, consider "modern" or "contemporary" in some contexts)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *dolmenique*, from Breton *dolmen* (table stone) + *-ic*. Ultimately from *taol* (table) and *men* (stone).
