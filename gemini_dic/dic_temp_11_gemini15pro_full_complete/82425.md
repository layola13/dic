gruesomest:/ˈɡruːsəməst/ | adj. | 最可怕的，最令人毛骨悚然的 | Superlative form of gruesome; causing repulsion or horror; extremely unpleasant. | The gruesomest scene in the movie was the aftermath of the battle.  这部电影中最可怕的一幕是战斗的后果。|  horrific, terrifying, ghastly, macabre | pleasant, delightful, agreeable | adjective

Etymology:  From Middle English *grewsome, from Old Norse grūsome, from grū "horror, dread" + -some "-some."
