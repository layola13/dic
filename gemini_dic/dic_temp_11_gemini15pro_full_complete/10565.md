appreciative: /əˈpriːʃətɪv/ | adj. | 感激的，欣赏的 | Feeling or showing gratitude or pleasure. | She was very appreciative of our help. 她非常感谢我们的帮助。 |  I am appreciative of your kindness. 我很感激你的好意。 | grateful, thankful, admiring | ungrateful, unthankful | adjective

appreciate: /əˈpriːʃieɪt/ | v. | 欣赏；感激；增值 | To recognize the full worth of; to be grateful for (something). To increase in value. | I appreciate your help. 我感谢你的帮助。| The value of the house has appreciated over the years. 房子的价值这些年来一直在增长。 | value, cherish, acknowledge |  disregard, ignore, depreciate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin appreciativus, from Latin appretiatus (past participle of appretiare “to set a price to, value”), from ad- “to” + pretium “price.”
