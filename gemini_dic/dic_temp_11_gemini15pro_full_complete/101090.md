inverisimilitude:/ˌɪnvɛrɪsɪˈmɪlɪˌtjuːd/
|n.|不像真实；不似真；貌似真实|The play's inverisimilitude made it difficult to connect with the characters.  这出戏的不真实感让人难以与剧中人物产生共鸣。|The movie's plot relied heavily on inverisimilitude, featuring talking animals and magical occurrences.  这部电影的情节严重依赖于虚构，以会说话的动物和魔法事件为特色。|近义词：unreality, implausibility, fiction|反义词：verisimilitude, realism, plausibility|词性:名词

Etymology->
From Late Latin *inuerisimilitūdō*, from in- + uerisimilitūdō (“likeness to truth, probability”), from uerisimilis (“like the truth, probable”) + -tūdō (suffix forming abstract nouns).  Ultimately from Latin uērus (“true”).
