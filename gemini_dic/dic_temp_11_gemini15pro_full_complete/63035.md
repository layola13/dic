enrapturedly:/ɪnˈræptʃərdli/
adverb
狂喜地，着迷地
in a manner filled with great delight or enchantment.
She listened enrapturedly to the music. 她听得如痴如醉。
近义词:ecstatically, rapturously, blissfully
反义词:apathetically, indifferently, boredly
词性:副词

词源:
From enraptured + -ly. Enraptured from en- (expressing intensification) + raptured (past participle of rapture). Rapture from Latin raptura (“a carrying off, abduction, snatching away”), from raptus, perfect passive participle of rapio (“I seize, snatch, carry off”).
