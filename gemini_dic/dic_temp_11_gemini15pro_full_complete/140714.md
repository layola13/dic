omnific:/ɒmˈnɪfɪk/| adj.|无所不能的；全能的|Having unlimited power; all-powerful.|例句：The omnific power of the creator. 造物主的无所不能的力量。|例句：An omnific deity. 一位全能的神。|近义词：almighty, omnipotent, all-powerful|反义词：powerless, impotent, weak|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *omnifex* (“all-making”), from *omni-* (“all”) + *-fex* (“maker”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁-* (“to put, place, set”).
