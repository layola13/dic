non-recoverable: /ˌnɒn rɪˈkʌvərəbl/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˈkʌvərəbl/ | adj. | 不可恢复的，无法挽回的 | Not able to be recovered, regained, or rectified. | The data loss was non-recoverable, and we had to start the project from scratch. 数据丢失是不可恢复的，我们不得不从头开始这个项目。 | The damage to the environment is non-recoverable. 对环境的损害是不可恢复的。|近义词：irreversible, irreparable, permanent |反义词：recoverable, reversible, reparable |词性: 形容词

non-recoverable: 词源来自 non- (not) + recoverable.  Recoverable 源自拉丁语 recuperare "to recover,"  字面意思是 "to get back."
