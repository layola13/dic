fablers:/ˈfeɪblərz/
n.
寓言家；讲故事的人
People who tell fables, especially those who invent them.
The old fablers would gather the children around the fire and tell tales of mythical creatures.  老寓言家们会把孩子们聚集在火堆旁，讲述神话生物的故事。
The fablers of ancient Greece passed down stories that are still told today. 古希腊的寓言家们流传下来的故事至今仍在讲述。
storytellers, mythmakers, raconteurs
liars, deceivers
noun
