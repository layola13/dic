oppresses:/əˈprɛsɪz/
verb
压制，压迫
to govern or rule cruelly or unjustly; to burden spiritually or mentally
The dictator oppresses the people with his iron fist.  独裁者用铁腕压制人民。
Synonyms: subjugate, suppress, tyrannize, domineer
Antonyms: liberate, free, empower, support
词性: 动词
