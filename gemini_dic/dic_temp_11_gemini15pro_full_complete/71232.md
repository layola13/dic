fitted:/ˈfɪtɪd/ | adj. |合适的，安装好的，配备的|Made to conform to the shape of something; equipped or furnished.|The tailor made a fitted suit for him.  裁缝为他做了一套合身的西装。|近义词:suitable, appropriate, equipped, furnished|反义词:loose, ill-fitting, unequipped|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fitten*, from Old English *fietan* (“to arrange, adorn”), from Proto-Germanic *fetijaną* (“to adorn”), from Proto-Indo-European *peth₂-* (“to put, place, arrange”).  Related to *fit* (verb).
