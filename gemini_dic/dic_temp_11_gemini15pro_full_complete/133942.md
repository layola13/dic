nonhazardousness:/ˌnɒnˈhæzərdəsnəs/
noun
无害性；非危险性
The nonhazardousness of the material was confirmed by the lab results.  该材料的无害性已由实验室结果证实。
The report emphasized the nonhazardousness of the new cleaning product. 报告强调了这种新型清洁产品的无害性。
Synonyms: safety, harmlessness, innocuousness
Antonyms: dangerousness, hazardousness, perilousness
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): from Latin, meaning "not"
hazardous: from hazard, ultimately from Arabic az-zahr ("the die"), referring to games of chance.
-ness (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
