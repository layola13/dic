houseboating:/ˈhaʊsˌboʊtɪŋ/
n.
houseboating(水上住宿)
The recreational activity of living or traveling on a houseboat.
We spent our summer vacation houseboating on Lake Powell. 我们在鲍威尔湖上度过了我们的水上住宿的暑假。

近义词：boating, cruising, sailing
反义词：(None specific - could be land-based vacationing)
词性: noun
