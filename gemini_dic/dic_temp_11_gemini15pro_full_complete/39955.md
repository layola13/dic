construct:/kənˈstrʌkt/ | v. |建造，构造；创立|To build or create something, typically a large structure, or to form something such as a sentence, argument, or system by combining parts.|The workers constructed a new bridge over the river. 工人们在河上建造了一座新桥。|synonyms: build, erect, establish, create, form, fabricate, assemble|antonyms: demolish, destroy, dismantle|verb

construct:/ˈkɒnstrʌkt/ | n. |概念，构想；解释；建造物|An idea or theory containing various conceptual elements, typically one considered to be subjective and not based on empirical evidence.|That's an interesting construct, but is there any evidence to support it? 这是一个有趣的构想，但是有任何证据支持它吗？|synonyms: concept, idea, notion, theory, model|antonyms: reality, fact, truth|noun
