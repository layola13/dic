externalism: /ɪkˈstɜːnəlɪzəm/ | n. | 外在主义 | The view that factors external to the mind can play a role in determining the content of mental states. | Hilary Putnam's famous Twin Earth thought experiment is a classic argument for externalism.  希拉里·普特南著名的孪生地球思想实验是支持外在主义的经典论证。| external factors, outside influences | internalism | noun

externalism: /ɪkˈstɜːnəlɪzəm/ | n. | 外在主义 |  In social sciences, it refers to theories that emphasize the importance of social and environmental factors in shaping individual behavior and beliefs. |  Externalism suggests that social structures and cultural norms heavily influence a person's development. 外在主义认为社会结构和文化规范对一个人的发展有很大的影响。 |  social constructivism, environmental determinism | individualism | noun
