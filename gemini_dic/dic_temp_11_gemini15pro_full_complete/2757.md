adultery:/əˈdʌltəri/
n.
通奸，婚外情
Voluntary sexual intercourse between a married person and a person who is not their spouse.
He was accused of adultery. 他被指控通奸。
近义词：infidelity, unfaithfulness, affair
反义词：fidelity, faithfulness
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *avowtrie*, from Old French *avulterie*, from Late Latin *adulterium*, from Latin *adulterare* (“to corrupt, defile, commit adultery”), from *ad* (“to”) + *alter* (“other”).
