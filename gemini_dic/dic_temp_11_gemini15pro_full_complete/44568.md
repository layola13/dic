crouch:/kraʊtʃ/| v. n.|蹲伏，蜷缩；弯腰|To bend low with the limbs close to the body, especially to avoid being seen or to prepare for an action like jumping or attacking.|The cat crouched low in the grass, ready to pounce. 猫低伏在草丛中，准备突袭。|近义词：squat, stoop, bend|反义词:stand, rise|词性:动词/名词
crouch:/kraʊtʃ/| n.|蹲伏的姿势|The act of crouching.|He adopted a crouch behind the wall. 他在墙后采取了蹲伏的姿势。|近义词：squatting position|反义词:standing position|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *crouchir, from Vulgar Latin *cocoricare, frequentative of Latin cubare "to lie down".
