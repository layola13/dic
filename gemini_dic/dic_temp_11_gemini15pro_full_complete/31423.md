charioteer:/ˌtʃɛrɪəˈtɪr/ | n. |战车驭者；驾驶员；领导者|A person who drives a chariot; someone who is in charge and guides something. |The charioteer skillfully maneuvered his chariot around the track.  战车驭者熟练地驾驶着他的战车绕着赛道行驶。

The CEO is seen as the charioteer of the company, leading it through challenging times.  这位首席执行官被视为公司的领导者，带领公司度过充满挑战的时期。| 近义词: driver, pilot, guide, leader | 反义词: follower, passenger | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Late Middle English *chariotere*, from Old French *charretier* “cart driver,” from *charrete* “cart,” from Vulgar Latin *carra*, from Gaulish *karros* “wagon, cart”.  The meaning shifted from "cart driver" to "chariot driver" likely due to the more glamorous and heroic image associated with chariots.
