badly:/ˈbædli/ | adv. | 坏地，严重地，非常地 | In a bad manner; poorly; to a great extent; seriously. | He played the piano badly. 他钢琴弹得很糟糕。 | poorly, terribly, awfully, severely | well, skillfully | adverb

badly:/ˈbædli/ | adj. | 坏的，严重的 (主要用于英式英语，美式英语中较少用作形容词) | In a bad condition; seriously.  | He was badly injured in the accident. 他在事故中受了重伤。(英式英语) | seriously injured, gravely ill | well, healthy, slightly injured | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *badlice, from bad + -lice (an adverbial suffix).
