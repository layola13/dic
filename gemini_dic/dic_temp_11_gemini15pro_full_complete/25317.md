bugled:/ˈbjuːɡəld/ |v. |吹号；（尤指军队）发出号声|To sound a bugle.  The soldier bugled reveille at dawn.|例句：The cavalry bugled a retreat.  骑兵吹响了撤退号。

例句：The officer bugled the troops to advance. 军官吹号命令部队前进。


|近义词：trumpeted, sounded, blew|反义词:(No direct antonym, consider "silenced")|词性:动词

词源:
From bugle (n.), from Old French bugle "wild ox, buffalo," also a hunting horn made from its horn, from Vulgar Latin *buculus, diminutive of Latin bos "ox, cow." The verb sense is from the 17th century.
