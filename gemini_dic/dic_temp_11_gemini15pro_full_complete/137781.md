nonvegetativeness:/ˌnɒnˌvɛdʒɪˈteɪtɪvnəs/
|n.|非植物性，无生命状态|The state of not exhibiting the characteristics of life associated with plants, such as growth, reproduction, and response to stimuli.  Essentially, it refers to a state of inactivity or lack of vital functions typically observed in living organisms, especially plants. It can also imply a lack of consciousness or mental activity in humans or animals.|The prolonged nonvegetativeness of the patient concerned the doctors. 病人长时间的无生命状态让医生们担忧。|Near synonyms: lifelessness, inactivity, dormancy, unconsciousness|Near antonyms: vitality, life, consciousness, activity|noun
