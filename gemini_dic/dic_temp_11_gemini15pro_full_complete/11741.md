aromaticity:/ˌærəməˈtɪsəti/ | n. | 芳香性 | The property of a molecule having a cyclic structure with delocalized pi electrons, giving it unusual stability and a characteristic odor. | Benzene exhibits high aromaticity due to its ring structure and delocalized electrons. 苯由于其环状结构和离域电子而表现出高芳香性。 | synonyms: fragrance, aroma, scent | antonyms: odorlessness, stench | noun
