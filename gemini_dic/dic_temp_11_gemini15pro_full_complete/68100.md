fady:/ˈfeɪdi/| adj.| 过时的；褪色的；微弱的；不重要的| weak or faint; lacking strength or intensity; not important or significant | The photographs had become fady and yellowed with age. 照片已经褪色泛黄，显得老旧了。|近义词：pale, faint, weak, faded, insipid, vapid|反义词：strong, vibrant, bright, important, significant|词性: 形容词

词源: From the French word "fade," meaning "insipid, tasteless," from the Latin "vapidus," meaning "having lost its flavor."
