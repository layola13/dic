inbarge:/ɪnˈbɑːrdʒ/ | vt. |  装入驳船; 用驳船运载 | To load onto or transport by barge. | They inbarged the cargo for shipment down the river. 他们把货物装上驳船，沿河运送。|  The company inbarged the coal for transport to the power plant. 公司用驳船将煤炭运送到发电厂。 |Synonyms: ship, transport, convey | Antonyms: unload, disembark | verb
