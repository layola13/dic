co-inheritance:/ˌkoʊɪnˈhɛrɪtəns/ | n. |共同继承，共遗传| The simultaneous inheritance of two or more characteristics from a parent or ancestor.  This term is often used in genetics and computer science (specifically in object-oriented programming). |  In some species, co-inheritance of certain genes can lead to specific traits. 在某些物种中，某些基因的共同继承会导致特定的性状。

Co-inheritance can also refer to the legal concept of multiple parties inheriting the same property. 共同继承也可以指多个当事人继承同一财产的法律概念。 | synonyms:  joint inheritance, simultaneous inheritance | antonyms: N/A | noun
