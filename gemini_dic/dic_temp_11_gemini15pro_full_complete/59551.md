eavesdrop:/ˈiːvzdrɒp/ (US) /ˈiːvzdrɒp/ (UK)| v. |偷听，窃听|To secretly listen to a private conversation.|例句：She eavesdropped on their conversation from behind the curtain. 她从窗帘后面偷听了他们的谈话。

例句：He was caught eavesdropping on his neighbor's phone calls. 他被抓到偷听邻居的电话。

近义词：listen in, overhear, snoop|反义词: - |词性:动词
