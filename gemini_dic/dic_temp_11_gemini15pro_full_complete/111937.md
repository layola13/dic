loller:/ˈlɒlə(r)/ /ˈlɑːlər/ | n. |傻笑的人；游手好闲的人；流浪汉|A person who lolls about idly; a lounger; a vagrant.  A person, especially a young one, who laughs a lot.|例句：The lollers were hanging around the street corner.  这些游手好闲的人在街角闲逛。

例句：He's such a loller, always giggling about something. 他真是个爱傻笑的人，总是咯咯地笑个不停。|近义词：idler, loafer, lounger, slacker, goof-off, giggle, chuckle|反义词:worker, busybody|词性:名词
