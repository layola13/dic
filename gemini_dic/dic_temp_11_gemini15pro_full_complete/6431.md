ampicillin:/ˌæmpɪˈsɪlɪn/| n.|氨苄青霉素；氨苄西林|A semisynthetic penicillin derivative, C16H19N3O4S, that is effective against a broad range of gram-positive and gram-negative bacteria and is used in the treatment of various infections, such as gonorrhea and urinary tract infections.|The doctor prescribed ampicillin for her ear infection.|近义词：amoxicillin, penicillin|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a specific antibiotic)|词性:noun
