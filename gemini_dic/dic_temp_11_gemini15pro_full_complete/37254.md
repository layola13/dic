comically:/ˈkɒmɪkli/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmɪkli/ (US)| adv.|滑稽地，可笑地|In a way that is funny, especially in a strange or unexpected way.|He tripped and fell comically. 他滑稽地绊倒了。|The situation was comically absurd.  这种情况荒谬得可笑。|近义词：humorously, amusingly, ludicrously|反义词：seriously, solemnly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From late Middle English *comical* + *-ly*.  *Comical* comes from Late Latin *comicus*, from Latin *cōmicus*, from Greek *κωμικός* (kōmikós), relating to comedy.
