naphthacene:/ˈnæfθəsiːn/| n.|萘嵌苯，并四苯|A crystalline aromatic hydrocarbon, C18H12, analogous to anthracene and obtained from coal tar.|Naphthacene is a polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbon. 萘嵌苯是一种多环芳烃。|Naphthacene derivatives are being investigated for use in organic field-effect transistors.  萘嵌苯衍生物正被研究用于有机场效应晶体管。|Synonym: tetracene|Antonym: None|noun
