nip-away:/nɪp əˈweɪ/| phrasal verb | 逐渐减少，一点点地去掉 | To gradually remove or reduce something, bit by bit. |  The sculptor carefully nipped away at the excess clay. 雕塑家小心翼翼地一点一点去掉多余的黏土。

|例句：He's been nipping away at the problem for weeks, but hasn't found a solution yet.  他几周来一直在努力解决这个问题，但还没有找到解决方案。

|例句：The company has been nipping away at its debt, slowly but surely. 公司一直在慢慢地减少债务。

|近义词：chip away, erode, whittle down |反义词：augment, increase, build up |词性：动词短语
