backspacers:/ˈbækˌspeɪsərz/| n. |退格键使用者；使用退格键的人|People who use the backspace key, often to correct typing errors.  This term can sometimes have a slightly negative connotation, implying excessive correction or indecisiveness.|He's such a backspacer, he takes forever to write a simple email. 他真是个退格键狂魔，写个简单的邮件都要花很长时间。|近义词:  deleters, editors, revisers|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider)  confident typists,  fast writers|词性: Noun
