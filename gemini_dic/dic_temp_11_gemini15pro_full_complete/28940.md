carrotier:/kɑːrɔtjeɪ/ (French pronunciation, approximate English: /kærɒtˈɪeɪ/)
n.|胡萝卜种植者；胡萝卜商人|A person who grows or sells carrots. |He is a carrotier in the local market. 他是当地市场上的一个胡萝卜商。|Synonyms: carrot farmer, carrot seller|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
Etymology: From French "carotte" (carrot) + "-ier" (a suffix denoting a person who deals with something).
