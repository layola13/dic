myopia:/maɪˈoʊpiə/
n.
近视
nearsightedness; lack of foresight
例句：His myopia prevented him from seeing the distant mountains. 他的近视使他无法看到远处的山脉。

例句：The government's myopia in not investing in education will have long-term consequences. 政府在教育方面缺乏远见的投资将产生长期后果。

近义词：nearsightedness, shortsightedness
反义词：hyperopia, farsightedness
词性：名词


myopic:/maɪˈɑːpɪk/
adj.
近视的;缺乏远见的
lacking foresight or intellectual insight
例句：The company's myopic focus on short-term profits led to its eventual downfall.  该公司目光短浅地注重短期利润，最终导致其垮台。

例句：Her myopic view of the situation prevented her from seeing the bigger picture. 她对形势的短视使她无法看到全局。
近义词：shortsighted, narrow-minded, unthinking
反义词：farsighted, visionary, prescient
词性：形容词
