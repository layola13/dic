cornification:/ˌkɔːrnɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ |n.|角化；角质化|The process of forming a horny substance; the conversion of epithelium into keratin or horn.  The development of keratinized tissue, such as skin, hair and nails. |Excessive cornification can lead to dry, flaky skin. 角质化过度会导致皮肤干燥脱屑。|The cornification of the epidermis is essential for its protective function. 表皮的角质化对其保护功能至关重要。|
近义词: keratinization,  keratinisation |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
