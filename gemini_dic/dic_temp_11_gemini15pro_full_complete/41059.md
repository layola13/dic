copium:/ˈkoʊpiəm/| n.|精神胜利法; 自我欺骗;  |A coping mechanism to delude oneself into believing something is true, when they know it is not, to protect their own feelings.|He's clearly lost the election, but he's clinging to copium, claiming voter fraud. 他显然已经输掉了选举，但他却沉迷于精神胜利法，声称选举舞弊。|近义词:self-deception, denial, wishful thinking|反义词:acceptance, realism, facing reality|词性:名词

词源:
A portmanteau of "cope" and "opium."  It originated in online gaming communities, particularly around 2018, to describe the behavior of clinging to unlikely hopes or justifications for failure.  It suggests a numbing or intoxicating effect similar to that of opium, allowing someone to avoid the pain of reality.
