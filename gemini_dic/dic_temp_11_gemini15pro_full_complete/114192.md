maidan:/maɪˈdɑːn/| n. |广场，露天场地，(尤指城镇中心的)广场|A large open space in a town or city, especially one used for public gatherings or events; used especially in countries of the former Soviet Union.|The protesters gathered in the maidan to voice their dissent. 抗议者聚集在广场上表达他们的异议。|Synonyms: square, plaza, public square|Antonyms: enclosure, confined space|Noun
