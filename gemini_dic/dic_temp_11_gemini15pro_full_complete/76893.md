gangway:/ˈɡæŋweɪ/ | n. |通道，舷梯，（人群中的）通路|A raised platform or passageway, often with steps, used for boarding or leaving a ship or aircraft. Also, a clear passageway through a crowd. |例句：The passengers boarded the ship via the gangway.  乘客通过舷梯登上了船。

例句：Please make a gangway for the paramedics. 请为医护人员让出一条通道。|近义词：passage, aisle, walkway, corridor |反义词: barrier, obstruction |词性: noun

gangway:/ˈɡæŋweɪ/ | int. |让路，借光|Used to politely request someone to move aside to allow passage. |例句：Gangway! Coming through with the stretcher!  借光！担架来了！

例句：Gangway, please, I need to get to the door.  请让一下，我需要到门口去。|近义词：excuse me, pardon me |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: interjection
