bridewell:/ˈbraɪdwɛl/| n.|拘留所，监狱；教养院|A house of correction; a prison. Originally a palace in London converted into a prison. Now, often used to refer to any jail or place of detention.|He was sent to the bridewell for vagrancy. 他因流浪被送进了拘留所。|Synonyms: jail, prison, lockup, detention center|Antonyms: freedom, liberty|noun

bridewell:/ˈbraɪdwɛl/| n.|教养院，感化院|A place of correction or rehabilitation, especially for young offenders.|The bridewell was intended to reform petty criminals. 这所教养院旨在改造轻罪犯。|Synonyms: reformatory, correctional facility|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "mainstream school")|noun
