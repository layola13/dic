apteral:/ˈæptərəl/
adjective
无翼的；无柱廊的
Without wings; (of a building) without columns or a colonnade.
The temple was apteral, a stark contrast to the grand, columned structures surrounding it. 这座寺庙没有柱廊，与周围宏伟的柱状建筑形成鲜明对比。
The architect's design featured an apteral façade, a bold choice for a public building.  建筑师的设计以无柱廊的立面为特色，这是公共建筑的大胆选择。

近义词:wingless, columnless
反义词:winged, columned
词性:adjective

Etymology: From Greek *ἀ- (a-, “not”) + *πτερόν (pterón, “wing, feather”) or *πτερόν (pterón) + *-ālis, whence Latin *apterālis.
