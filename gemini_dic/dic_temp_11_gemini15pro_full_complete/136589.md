nonsanctimoniousness:/ˌnɒnˌsæŋktɪˈməʊniəsnəs/
noun
不虚伪；真诚；坦率
The quality of not being sanctimonious; sincerity.  Lack of pretense of holiness or religious superiority.
He spoke with a refreshing nonsanctimoniousness about his own struggles with faith. 他以一种令人耳目一新的真诚谈论了他自己在信仰上的挣扎。
