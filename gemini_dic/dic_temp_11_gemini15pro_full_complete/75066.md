frighting:/ˈfraɪtnɪŋ/| adj.|令人恐惧的，骇人的|Causing fear or alarm.|The movie was so frightening I couldn't sleep. 这部电影太吓人了，我睡不着。|近义词:scary, terrifying, horrifying|反义词:comforting, reassuring|词性:形容词

frighting:/ˈfraɪtnɪŋ/| n.|惊吓|The act of frightening someone.|The sudden noise gave her a frighting. 突然的噪音吓了她一跳。 |近义词:scaring, terrifying|反义词:comforting, reassuring|词性:名词 (较少使用)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *frightenen*, from Old English *fryhtnian* (“to frighten, terrify”), from *fryhto* (“fright, fear”) + *-nian* (suffix forming verbs). Cognate with West Frisian *frichtsje* (“to frighten”), Dutch *verschrikken* (“to be frightened”), German *furcht* (“fear”), *fürchten* (“to fear”).
