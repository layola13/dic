apprenticement: /əˈprɛntɪsmənt/ | n. | 学徒期，学徒身份 | The state or period of being an apprentice.  A system of training a new generation of practitioners of a trade or profession with on-the-job training and often some accompanying study (classroom work and reading). |  He served his apprenticement as a carpenter.  他当木匠学徒期满。| synonyms: apprenticeship, traineeship, internship, novitiate | antonyms: mastership, expertise, professionalism | noun

apprenticement: /əˈprɛntɪsmənt/ | n. | 学徒制 | A system of training a new generation of practitioners of a trade or profession with on-the-job training. | The company has a strong apprenticement program. 这家公司有很强的学徒培训计划。 | synonyms: apprenticeship scheme, training program | antonyms:  independent study, self-taught | noun
