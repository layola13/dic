miracles:/ˈmɪrəkəlz/ (US), /ˈmɪrəkəlz/ (UK)| n. |奇迹；令人惊奇的人（或事）|An event that appears inexplicable by the laws of nature and so is attributed to a supernatural cause; something very surprising or remarkable.|例句：The doctor said her recovery was a miracle. 医生说她的康复是个奇迹。

例句：It's a miracle that no one was hurt.  真是奇迹，没有人受伤。


近义词：wonder, marvel, phenomenon|反义词：disaster, calamity|词性:noun
