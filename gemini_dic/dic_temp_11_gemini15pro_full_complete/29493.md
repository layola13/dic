catapulting:/ˈkætəˌpʌltɪŋ/ | v. | 投射，发射，使…突然成功 | The novel catapulted her to fame. 这部小说使她一跃成名。| synonyms: launching, projecting, propelling, boosting | antonyms: hindering, obstructing, slowing, decelerating | Verb

catapult:/ˈkætəˌpʌlt/ | n. | 弹弓，投石器 |  The soldiers used a catapult to launch rocks at the castle walls. 士兵们用弹弓向城堡的城墙发射石块。| synonyms: slingshot, trebuchet | antonyms: N/A | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
