mandating:/ˈmænˌdeɪtɪŋ/| v.|授权；强制执行；命令|The law mandates that all children attend school. 法律规定所有儿童必须上学。|近义词：ordering, requiring, directing, prescribing, imposing|反义词：forbidding, prohibiting, banning|verb (present participle)

mandate:/ˈmænˌdeɪt/| n. vt.|授权；命令；指令|The government has a mandate to reduce poverty. 政府有责任减少贫困。|近义词：order, command, directive, instruction, decree|反义词：prohibition, ban|noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mandatum* "something entrusted to one's care," noun use of neuter past participle of *mandare* "to entrust, order," from *manus* "hand" + *dare* "to give."
