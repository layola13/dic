cowalker:/ˈkoʊˌwɔːkər/| n.|同事，共同工作者|A person who walks with another, especially for exercise or companionship; a colleague.|例句：My coworker and I often go for walks during our lunch break. 我和我的同事经常在午休时间去散步。

例句：She carpooled with a coworker to save on gas. 她和一位同事拼车以节省汽油。|近义词：colleague, associate, workmate|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用competitor竞争对手, 或rival对手，但语境不同)|词性:noun
