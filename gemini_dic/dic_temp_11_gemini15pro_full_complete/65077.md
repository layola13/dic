ethan:/ˈiːθeɪn/ | n. | 乙烷 | A colorless, odorless, gaseous alkane, C₂H₆, that occurs as a constituent of natural gas and is used chiefly as a fuel and in the manufacture of other organic chemicals. |  Ethan is the second simplest alkane after methane. 乙烷是继甲烷之后第二简单的烷烃。| synonym: dimethyl | antonym: N/A | noun
