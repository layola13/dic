illnesses:/ˈɪlnɪsɪz/
noun
疾病（复数）
A disease or period of sickness affecting the body or mind.
Many common illnesses can be prevented with proper hygiene.  许多常见疾病可以通过适当的卫生习惯来预防。
He suffered from a series of childhood illnesses. 他小时候患过一系列疾病。
近义词: diseases, sicknesses, ailments, disorders
反义词: health, wellness
词性: noun
