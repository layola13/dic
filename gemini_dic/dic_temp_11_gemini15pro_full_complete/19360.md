bibliopolic:/ˌbɪbliəˈpɒlɪk/
n.
selling books; of or relating to booksellers
a person who buys or sells books, especially rare or used books.
The bibliopolic carefully examined the first edition for any imperfections.  这位书商仔细检查了这本初版书，看看有没有任何瑕疵。
Synonyms: bookseller, book dealer, book merchant
Antonyms: book buyer, customer, reader
noun

bibliopolic:/ˌbɪbliəˈpɒlɪk/
adj.
of or relating to the selling of books
relating to booksellers or the book trade.
He had a bibliopolic interest in the history of printing. 他对印刷史有着书商般的兴趣，尤其关注其商业方面。
Synonyms: bookselling, bookish
Antonyms: non-bookish, non-literary
adjective
