bated:/ˈbeɪtɪd/| adj.|屏息的，抑制的|Reduced or moderated in intensity, often due to anxiety or anticipation.  It literally means having abated or lessened, especially one's breath.|She waited with bated breath for the results of the competition. 她屏息等待着比赛结果。|近义词：suppressed, restrained, hushed|反义词：unrestrained, uninhibited|词性:形容词
