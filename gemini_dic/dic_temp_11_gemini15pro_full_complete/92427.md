hyponastic: /ˌhaɪpəˈnæstɪk/ | adj. | 向下弯曲的，下垂的 |  Relating to or characterized by hyponasty, the upward bending of the lower surface of a plant part (such as a leaf) caused by greater growth on the lower side. | The hyponastic response of the plant's leaves was evident after the heavy rain.  植物的叶子在大雨后表现出明显的向下弯曲的反应。| The hyponastic leaves drooped towards the ground. 向下弯曲的叶子向地面低垂。 | 近义词: drooping, pendant | 反义词: epinastic | 词性: adjective
