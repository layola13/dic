countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ |ˌkaʊntərˈmɑːnd/
v. |撤销，取消（命令）| To revoke or cancel (a command or order). |The general countermanded the attack after realizing the enemy was stronger than anticipated. 将军意识到敌人比预期的要强大后，取消了进攻。| revoke, rescind, cancel, override | confirm, endorse, ratify, approve | verb

countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ |ˌkaʊntərˈmɑːnd/
n. |取消命令，撤销| An order revoking a previous command. |The captain received a countermand from headquarters just before launching the missiles.  舰长在发射导弹前接到了总部的取消命令。| revocation, cancellation, repeal | confirmation, ratification | noun
