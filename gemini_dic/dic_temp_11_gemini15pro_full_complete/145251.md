overblind:/ˈoʊvərblaɪnd/| adj.|过度盲目的|Excessively uncritical; too willing to believe or accept something without question or analysis.  Derived from "over-" (excessively) and "blind" (unable or unwilling to see or understand).|He was overblind to her faults and idealized her. 他对她缺点视而不见，把她理想化了。|The overblind trust he placed in his business partner led to his financial ruin. 他对他生意伙伴过度盲目的信任导致了他的经济破产。|近义词: naive, gullible, credulous, unsuspecting|反义词: discerning, skeptical, critical, cautious|词性:形容词
