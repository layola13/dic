flotten:/ˈflɒtən/ (UK), /ˈflɑːtn̩/ (US)| n.|舰队，船队|A group of ships, especially warships, operating together under one command.|The German flotten sailed towards the English Channel.|近义词：fleet, armada, navy|反义词: (No direct antonym for a group of ships.  Perhaps "individual ships" or "scattered vessels")|词性:noun

词源: From German "Flotte," meaning "fleet."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *flotōn, meaning "a floating, a swimming," related to "float."
