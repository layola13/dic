leprechaun: /ˈlɛprəkɔːn/ (US), /ˈlɛprɪkɔːn/ (UK)| n. |小妖精，矮妖|A small, mischievous sprite in Irish folklore, typically depicted as a bearded man, often wearing a coat and hat, who guards a hidden pot of gold at the end of the rainbow.|例句：Legend says that if you catch a leprechaun, it will grant you three wishes.  传说如果你抓住一个小妖精，它会满足你三个愿望。

例句：The children were searching for leprechauns at the end of the rainbow. 孩子们在彩虹的尽头寻找小妖精。|近义词：sprite, fairy, pixie|反义词: giant |词性: noun
