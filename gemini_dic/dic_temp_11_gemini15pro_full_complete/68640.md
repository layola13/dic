faquir:/fəˈkɪər/ | n. |托钵僧，苦行僧 | A Muslim or Hindu religious ascetic who lives by begging.  A holy man, especially one who performs feats of endurance. |  Example 1: The faquir sat cross-legged on a bed of nails.  托钵僧盘腿坐在钉床上。

Example 2:  The faquir claimed to have miraculous healing powers.  这个苦行僧声称拥有神奇的治疗能力。| Synonym: ascetic, mendicant, mystic | Antonym: materialist, hedonist | noun
