amentiform:/əˈmɛntɪfɔːrm/| adj.|葇荑花的，柔荑花序状的|Resembling a catkin; shaped like a catkin, a spike of unisexual flowers.  A slender, cylindrical inflorescence, with sessile flowers along a central axis, as in the willow, alder, and birch.|The tree has amentiform flowers. 这棵树有柔荑花序状的花。|Synonyms: catkin-like, amentaceous|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could consider terms like "radiate" for flowers with a different structure) |adjective
