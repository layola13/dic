malaise:/mæˈleɪz/| n. |不适， malaise;不舒服，uneasiness;社会弊病，social ills|A general feeling of discomfort, illness, or unease whose exact cause is difficult to identify.|例句：A sense of malaise has settled over the country. 一种不安的情绪笼罩着全国。

例句：He complained of generalized malaise and fatigue. 他抱怨全身不适和疲劳。|近义词：unease, discomfort, sickness, illness, lethargy|反义词：well-being, comfort, energy|词性:名词
