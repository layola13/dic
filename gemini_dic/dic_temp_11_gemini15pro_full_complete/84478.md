halter:/ˈhɔːltər/ (US) /ˈhɒːltə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. |缰绳；笼头；（女装的）露背上衣|A rope or strap with a noose at one end, used for leading or tying up an animal.  A woman's top, tied around the neck, leaving the back and shoulders bare. |例句：The horse was led by a halter.  这匹马被缰绳牵着。


例句：She wore a brightly colored halter top to the beach. 她穿着一件颜色鲜艳的露背上衣去了海滩。|近义词：lead, bridle (for animal halter);  tank top, crop top (for clothing halter)|反义词: N/A|词性:名词,动词


halter:/ˈhɔːltər/ (US) /ˈhɒːltə(r)/ (UK)|v. |用缰绳拴住；突然停止|To stop something suddenly or abruptly.|例句：The project was abruptly haltered due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目突然停止了。


例句：He haltered the horse to the fence post. 他把马拴在篱笆柱上。|近义词：stop, cease, halt|反义词:continue, proceed|词性:动词
