aggregately:/ˈæɡrɪɡɪtli/
adverb|总体地，合计地|In a manner that combines all parts or elements; taken as a whole.|例句：Aggregately, the company's profits increased by 10%. 总体而言，公司的利润增长了10%。|例句：The small donations aggregately amounted to a substantial sum. 小额捐款合计起来数目可观。|近义词：collectively, combined, overall, in total, altogether|反义词：individually, separately, singly|词性: 副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *aggregatus*, past participle of aggregare "to add to," from ad- "to" + gregare "to herd, flock," from grex "flock, herd."
