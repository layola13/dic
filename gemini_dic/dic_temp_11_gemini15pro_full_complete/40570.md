contuse:/kənˈtjuːz/ /kənˈtuːs/ | v. | 瘀伤，挫伤 | To injure (a part of the body) by bruising without breaking the skin. |  He contused his knee playing football. 他踢足球时擦伤了膝盖。

The boxer's blows contused his opponent's ribs. 拳击手的重击使对手的肋骨挫伤。

| 近义词：bruise, injure | 反义词: heal, mend | 词性: 动词

contusion:/kənˈtjuːʒən/ /kənˈtuːʒn/ | n. | 瘀伤，挫伤 | An injury to a part of the body without breaking the skin, causing discoloration and pain. | The fall resulted in a severe contusion on her arm. 摔倒导致她手臂严重瘀伤。

He suffered multiple contusions in the car accident. 他在车祸中遭受了多处挫伤。

| 近义词：bruise, injury | 反义词: healing | 词性: 名词
