despondent:/dɪˈspɒndənt/ /dɪˈspɑːndənt/| adj.|沮丧的，失望的，灰心的| Feeling or showing profound hopelessness and discouragement.|例句：He felt despondent after failing the exam.  他考试不及格后感到沮丧。

例句：She was despondent over the loss of her job. 她因为失去工作而感到沮丧。|近义词：dejected, discouraged, disheartened, depressed, downcast|反义词：cheerful, optimistic, hopeful|词性:形容词
