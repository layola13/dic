orthoclastic:/ˌɔːrθəˈklæstɪk/ | adj. | 正交碎裂的，直角解理的 | Breaking at right angles to a crystallographic plane, especially at right angles to the main axis of a crystal. |  Orthoclastic cleavage is a characteristic feature of feldspar minerals. 正长石的解理是长石矿物的一个特征。| synonyms: rectangular, right-angled | antonyms: inclined, oblique | adjective
