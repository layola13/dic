gallopingly:/ˈɡæləpɪŋli/| adv.|飞快地，急速地|In a galloping manner; at a gallop.  Describes something increasing or advancing very rapidly.|例句：The disease spread gallopingly through the population. 这种疾病在人群中迅速蔓延。|近义词：rapidly, swiftly, quickly, speedily, hastily, expeditiously|反义词：slowly, gradually, leisurely|词性:副词
