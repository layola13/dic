consecutive:/kənˈsekjətɪv/| adj.|连续的，连贯的|Following one after another in a continuous series or order; successive.|例句：He won three consecutive championships. 他赢得了三连冠。|例句：The team achieved five consecutive victories.  这个队伍取得了五连胜。|近义词：successive, sequential, uninterrupted|反义词:non-consecutive, intermittent, sporadic|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin consecutivus, from consequi ‘follow closely’, from con- ‘together’ + sequi ‘follow’.
