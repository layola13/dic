ingrapple:/ɪnˈɡræpəl/ | v. | 搏斗，格斗；全力对付，努力克服 | To engage in a close fight or struggle, especially one that involves grappling; to wrestle or contend with (a problem or difficulty). |  The two wrestlers ingrappled fiercely in the ring. 两名摔跤手在擂台上激烈地搏斗。

The government is ingrappling with the problem of rising inflation. 政府正在努力克服通货膨胀加剧的问题。 | 近义词：grapple, wrestle, contend, struggle | 反义词：yield, surrender, give up | 词性：动词

词源：From in- + grapple.  "In-" is a prefix meaning "in, into, on, upon, within". "Grapple" comes from the Old French word "graper" meaning "to seize, grasp".
