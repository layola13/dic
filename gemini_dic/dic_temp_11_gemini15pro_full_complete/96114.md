indelibility:/ˌɪndɪˈlɪbɪlɪti/
n.
不可磨灭性，难以忘怀
The quality of being indelible; impossible to remove, erase, or wash away.
The indelibility of the memory haunted him. 这段难以忘怀的记忆一直困扰着他。
Synonyms: permanence, durability, lastingness
Antonyms: ephemerality, transience, temporariness
noun
