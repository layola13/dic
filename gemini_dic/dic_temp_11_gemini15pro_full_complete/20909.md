bleakest:/ˈbliːkɪst/ | adj. | 最荒凉的，最凄凉的，最黯淡的 |  Superlative form of bleak: most bleak. Characterized by a lack of warmth, hope, or cheerfulness. | The bleakest winter I ever experienced was in Alaska. 我经历过的最荒凉的冬天是在阿拉斯加。| synonyms: dreariest, most desolate, most dismal, most grim, most cheerless | antonyms: brightest, most cheerful, most hopeful, most promising | adjective
