convalesced:/ˌkɒnvəˈlest/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlest/ | v. | 康复，渐愈 |To recover health and strength gradually after sickness or injury. |  After her surgery, she convalesced at home for several weeks. 她手术后在家休养了几周。

She slowly convalesced from a serious illness. 她慢慢地从一场重病中康复。| 近义词: recovered, recuperated, improved, healed | 反义词: deteriorated, worsened, declined | 词性: 动词
