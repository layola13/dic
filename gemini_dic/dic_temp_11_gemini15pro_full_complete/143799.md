outoffices:/ˈaʊtˌɔːfɪsɪz/
n.
附属建筑；外屋；办公楼以外的办公室
A building or buildings away from a main office building, used for extra office space.
Our company has several outoffices located throughout the city.  我们公司在整个城市都有几处附属办公室。
The accounting department is housed in one of the outoffices. 会计部门设在其中一间外屋。
synonyms: annex, branch office, satellite office
antonyms: main office, headquarters
词性: 名词
