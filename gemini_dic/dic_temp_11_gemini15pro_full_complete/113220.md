lushest:/ˈlʌʃɪst/| adj.|最茂盛的；最繁茂的；最葱郁的|Superlative form of lush: most lush. Characterized by abundant, healthy growth of vegetation.|The garden was at its lushest in June.  六月里花园里草木最为繁茂。|The Amazon rainforest is one of the lushest places on earth. 亚马逊雨林是地球上最葱郁的地方之一。|Synonyms:  most luxuriant, most verdant, most prolific, richest|Antonyms:  barren, sparse, arid, desolate|adjective

lush:/lʌʃ/| adj.|茂盛的；繁茂的；葱郁的|Characterized by abundant, healthy growth of vegetation.|The valley was filled with lush green vegetation. 山谷里长满了茂盛的绿色植被。|The hotel had lush carpets and comfortable furniture.  这家酒店铺着豪华的地毯，家具也很舒适。|Synonyms:  luxuriant, verdant, abundant, rich|Antonyms:  barren, sparse, arid, desolate|adjective
