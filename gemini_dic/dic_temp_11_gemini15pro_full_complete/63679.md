epe:/ˈiːpiː/
noun|外部保护设备（External Protective Equipment）|Equipment designed to protect someone or something from external hazards.|Workers handling hazardous materials must wear appropriate EPE.  处理危险材料的工人必须穿戴合适的外部防护设备。|近义词:PPE (Personal Protective Equipment), safety gear|反义词:N/A|n.
epe:/ˈiːpiː/
abbreviation|每股收益（Earnings Per Share）|A company's net profit divided by the number of common shares outstanding. It indicates the profitability of a company.|The company's EPE increased significantly this quarter. 该公司本季度的每股收益大幅增长。|近义词:profit per share|反义词:loss per share|n.
