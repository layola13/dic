nonfelonious:/ˌnɑːnˈfɛˈloʊniəs/
adjective
非重罪的
Not constituting or involving a felony; of a lesser offense than a felony.
The defendant was charged with a nonfelonious misdemeanor.  被告被指控犯有不构成重罪的轻罪。
He committed a nonfelonious act. 他犯了一个非重罪行为。
synonym:misdemeanor, petty
antonym:felonious
词性:形容词
