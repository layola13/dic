circulating:/ˈsɜːrkjʊleɪtɪŋ/ /ˈsɝːkjəleɪtɪŋ/| adj. |循环的，流通的|moving continuously or freely through a closed system or area; passing from person to person or place to place|The circulating library is popular in the town. 城镇里的流通图书馆很受欢迎。|  New coins are now circulating.  新的硬币正在流通。|近义词：flowing, rotating, spreading, distributing|反义词：stagnant, static|adjective
