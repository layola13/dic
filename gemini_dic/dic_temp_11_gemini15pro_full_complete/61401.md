emblemize:/ˈembləmaɪz/| v. | 象征，代表 | to be a symbol of something; to represent something | The dove emblemizes peace. 鸽子象征和平。| The lion emblemizes courage and strength. 狮子象征着勇气和力量。 | synonyms: symbolize, represent, typify, embody | antonyms: misrepresent, belie | verb

emblemize:/ˈembləmaɪz/| v. | 体现，使具体化 | to make something concrete or visible |  His actions emblemize the spirit of generosity. 他的行为体现了慷慨的精神。 |  Her artwork emblemizes the struggles of her community. 她的作品体现了她所在社区的奋斗。 | synonyms: embody, personify, exemplify | antonyms: abstract, obscure | verb
