gooeynesses:/ˈɡuːinəsɪz/
gooeynesses|n.|粘糊糊的东西；粘性|The gooeynesses of the melted marshmallows made it difficult to eat. 融化的棉花糖的粘性使它难以食用。|
gooeynesses|近义词：stickiness, gumminess|反义词：dryness, firmness|名词

gooey:/ˈɡuːi/
gooey|adj.|粘糊糊的；粘性的|The cake was too gooey for my liking.  这个蛋糕对我来说太粘了。|
gooey|近义词：sticky, gummy, viscous|反义词：dry, firm, hard|形容词

-ness:/nəs/
