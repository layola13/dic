novitiate:/nəˈvɪʃɪɪt/ /noʊˈvɪʃɪət/| n.|见习期，修道院，新手|the period of being a novice, especially in a religious order, or the place where novices live.|She entered the novitiate at the age of 18. 她18岁进入修道院见习。|Synonyms: apprenticeship, probation, traineeship|Antonyms: experience, expertise|noun

novitiate:/nəˈvɪʃɪɪt/ /noʊˈvɪʃɪət/| n.|新手，初学者|a person who is new to a situation or activity and is still learning|He's a novitiate in the world of finance. 他是金融界的新手。|Synonyms: beginner, novice, learner, tyro|Antonyms: expert, veteran, professional|noun
