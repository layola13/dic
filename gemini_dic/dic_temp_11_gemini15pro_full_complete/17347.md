beachheads: /ˈbiːtʃhɛdz/ | n. |滩头阵地，立足点|A secure initial position that has been gained (especially in military operations) and can be used for further advancement.  A foothold; a strong position from which further progress can be made. |The troops established a beachhead and began to advance inland. 部队建立了滩头阵地，开始向内陆推进。| The company's new software product gave them a beachhead in the competitive market.  这家公司的新软件产品使他们在竞争激烈的市场中获得了一席之地。| 近义词: foothold, base, bridgehead, starting point | 反义词:  (no direct opposite, consider words like "retreat," "loss," or "defeat") | 词性: noun
