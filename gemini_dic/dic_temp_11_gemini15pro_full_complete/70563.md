fill-the-bill:/fɪl ðə bɪl/
verb phrase
满足要求；符合需要；胜任
to be suitable for a particular purpose or need
This inexpensive wine perfectly fills the bill for our party.  这种廉价的葡萄酒完全符合我们聚会的需要。
synonyms: meet the requirements, serve the purpose, be suitable, be adequate, suffice, do the trick, fit the bill (also fill the bill)
antonyms:  fall short, be inadequate, be insufficient
