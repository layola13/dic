abomination:/əˌbɒmɪˈneɪʃn/ /əˌbɑːmɪˈneɪʃn/| n.|憎恶, 讨厌的东西, 令人憎恶的事物|Something that causes disgust or hatred; a vile, shameful, or detestable action, condition, habit, etc.|The cruel treatment of animals is an abomination.  虐待动物令人憎恶。|Synonyms: atrocity, evil, outrage, anathema, detestation|Antonyms: delight, love, pleasure|noun


abomination:/əˌbɒmɪˈneɪʃn/ /əˌbɑːmɪˈneɪʃn/| n.|可憎之人, 令人厌恶的人|A person who is loathsome or disgusting.|He considered the traitor an abomination. 他认为叛徒是令人憎恶的人。|Synonyms: monster, villain, wretch|Antonyms: hero, saint, angel|noun
