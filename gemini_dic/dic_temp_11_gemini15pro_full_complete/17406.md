beam:/biːm/| n. v.| 横梁，光束，笑容；照射，发射，眉开眼笑 |A beam is a long, thick piece of wood, metal, or concrete, especially one used to support weight in a building or other structure.  A beam of light is a line of light that shines from a bright object.  A beam is also a wide, happy smile.|The sunlight beamed through the window. 阳光透过窗户照射进来。|近义词：ray, girder, smile, grin|反义词：frown, scowl|noun, verb
