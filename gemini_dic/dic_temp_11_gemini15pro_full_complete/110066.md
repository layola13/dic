lichened:/ˈlaɪkənd/ /ˈlɪtʃənd/ | adj. | 覆盖着地衣的；像地衣的 | Covered with or resembling lichens. | The old stone wall was lichened and crumbling.  古老的石墙覆盖着地衣，正在 crumbling. | 近义词: mossy, overgrown | 反义词: bare, clean | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From lichen + -ed.  "Lichen" comes from the Greek word "leikhēn" meaning "to lick," referring to the way some lichens appear to adhere to surfaces.
