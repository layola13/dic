overmeasure:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛʒər/| n. vt.|过量，过度测量；额外的量|An amount over and above a standard measure.  Giving more than is due or expected.|The baker gave us an overmeasure of flour.|近义词：excess, surplus, overabundance|反义词: shortage, deficit, undermeasure|n.
overmeasure:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛʒər/| vt.|过量供应；过度测量|To give or measure more than is due or expected.|The shopkeeper overmeasured the fabric, giving us extra material.|近义词：overfill, oversupply|反义词: underfill, undersupply|v.
