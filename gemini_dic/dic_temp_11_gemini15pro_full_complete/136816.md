nonshrinking:/ˌnɒnˈʃrɪŋkɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈʃrɪŋkɪŋ/
adjective
不缩水的；防缩的
Resistant to shrinking, especially after washing.
This shirt is made of nonshrinking fabric. 这件衬衫是用不缩水布料做的。
The label promised nonshrinking performance. 标签承诺防缩水。
近义词:preshrunk, washable, durable
反义词:shrinking, shrinkingable
词性:adjective
