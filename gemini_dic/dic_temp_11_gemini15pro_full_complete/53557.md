disbarred:/dɪsˈbɑːrd/ | v. |取消律师资格|To expel a lawyer from the bar, preventing them from practicing law. |The lawyer was disbarred for misconduct.  这名律师因行为不端被取消了律师资格。|Synonyms: disenfranchise, expel, remove|Antonyms: admit, reinstate, license|Verb

disbarred:/dɪsˈbɑːrd/ | adj. |被取消律师资格的|Having been expelled from the legal profession. |The disbarred lawyer now works as a consultant.  这位被取消律师资格的律师现在担任顾问。|Synonyms: expelled, unlicensed|Antonyms: licensed, admitted|Adjective
