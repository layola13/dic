overcontribution:/ˌoʊvərˌkɑːntrɪˈbjuːʃən/
n.
过度捐献，超额捐款
An amount contributed in excess of a prescribed limit.  The act of contributing too much.
He made an overcontribution to his retirement account and had to withdraw some of the funds. 他向自己的退休账户缴款过多，不得不提取部分资金。
Near Synonyms: excess contribution, surplus contribution
Antonyms: undercontribution, insufficient contribution
noun
