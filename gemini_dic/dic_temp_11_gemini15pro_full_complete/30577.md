ceratospongiae:/ˌsɛrətoʊˈspʌndʒiaɪ/ | n. | 角质海绵纲 | A class of sponges (Porifera) characterized by a skeleton made of spongin fibers with or without siliceous spicules, but never containing six-rayed siliceous spicules. |  Ceratospongiae includes the bath sponges. 角质海绵纲包括沐浴海绵。| synonym: Demospongiae (in some classifications) | antonym:  (No clear antonym, as these are classifications within a larger group.  Could potentially list other sponge classes like Hexactinellida or Calcarea if contrast is needed) | noun
