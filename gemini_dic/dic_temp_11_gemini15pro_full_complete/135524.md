Example:  While he is known for his plays, he also wrote several nonplays during his career.  虽然他以戏剧闻名，但他在其职业生涯中也写过几部非戏剧作品。 |Synonyms: non-dramatic works, other literary forms| Antonyms: plays, dramas| noun
