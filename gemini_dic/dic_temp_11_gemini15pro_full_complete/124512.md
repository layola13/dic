mounds:/maʊndz/
| n. |土堆，堆；大量，许多|A rounded mass of earth, sand, or other material; a large quantity or pile of something.|The archaeologists excavated several burial mounds. 考古学家挖掘了几个墓葬土堆。|Near synonyms: heap, pile, stack, accumulation|Near antonyms: hollow, pit, depression|noun

mounds:/maʊndz/
| v. |堆积，积聚|(Of something soft or loose) form into a rounded heap.|The snow mounded up against the wall. 雪堆积在墙边。|Near synonyms: heap, pile, stack|Near antonyms: scatter, disperse|verb
