amphoteric:/ˌæmfəˈtɛrɪk/
adjective
两性的；兼性的；酸碱两性的
(of a substance) able to react either as an acid or a base.
Aluminum oxide is amphoteric. 氧化铝是两性的。
synonyms: ambivalent, dual
antonyms: unambiguous, unequivocal
词性: adjective

amphoteric:/ˌæmfəˈtɛrɪk/
adjective
两性的；兼性的；酸碱两性的
Able to act as both an acid and a base.  This typically describes metal oxides or hydroxides that can react with both acids and bases to form salts.
Water can act as an amphoteric substance. 水可以作为两性物质。
synonyms: amphiprotic (more specific to proton transfer)
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but a substance could be specifically acidic or basic.)
词性: adjective
