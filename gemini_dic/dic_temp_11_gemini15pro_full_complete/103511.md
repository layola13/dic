jocular:/ˈdʒɒkjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈdʒɑːkjələr/| adj.|滑稽的，诙谐的，爱开玩笑的|Given to or characterized by joking; humorous or playful.|He made a jocular remark about the weather. 他对天气开了个玩笑。|近义词：humorous, playful, witty, jesting, facetious|反义词：serious, solemn, grave|词性:形容词
词源:Late 16th century: from Latin jocularis, from jocus ‘jest’.
