bifurcated: /ˈbaɪfərkeɪtɪd/ /ˈbaɪfɜːrkeɪtɪd/
adjective
分叉的，分两路的
divided into two branches or forks.
The river bifurcated into two smaller streams. 这条河流分叉成两条小溪。

The bifurcated road led to two different towns. 这条分叉的道路通向两个不同的城镇。


近义词: forked, branched, divided
反义词: unified, joined, merged
词性: adjective
