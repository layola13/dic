intervene:/ˌɪntərˈviːn/
intervene|v.|介入，干预，调解|to become involved in a situation in order to improve it or prevent it from getting worse|The central bank intervened in the currency markets to try to stabilize the exchange rate. 中央银行干预货币市场，试图稳定汇率。|interfere, mediate, step in|refrain, abstain|verb

intervened:/ˌɪntərˈviːnd/
intervened|v. (past tense)|介入了，干预了，调解了|past tense of "intervene"|The teacher intervened when the two boys started fighting.  老师在两个男孩开始打架时进行了干预。|interfered, mediated, stepped in|refrained, abstained|verb
