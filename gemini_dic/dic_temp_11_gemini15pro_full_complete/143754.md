outmanoeuvred: /ˌaʊtməˈnuːvərd/ (UK) /ˌaʊtməˈnuːvərd/ (US)
verb
以策略胜过，智胜
to defeat someone by being more clever or skillful in your plans and methods
例句：The politician outmanoeuvred his opponents and won the election. 这位政治家以策略胜过他的对手，赢得了选举。

例句：The company was outmanoeuvred by its competitors in the race to develop new technology.  这家公司在开发新技术的竞争中被其竞争对手以策略胜过。

近义词：outwit, outsmart, surpass, defeat, overcome
反义词：be outwitted, be outsmarted, lose to

词性: verb
