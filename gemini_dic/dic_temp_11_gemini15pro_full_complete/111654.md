/ˈloʊkeɪtər/ (US) /ləʊˈkeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 定位器；探测器；搜索器|A device used to determine the position of something.|The GPS locator helped us find our lost dog.  全球定位系统定位器帮助我们找到了丢失的狗。|synonyms: finder, detector, sensor, tracker|antonyms: displacer, loser|noun


/ˈloʊkeɪtər/ (US) /ləʊˈkeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 定位程序；存储单元|In computer science, a locator refers to an address or pointer that identifies a specific data element or resource.|The locator pointed to the exact location of the file on the server. 定位程序指向服务器上文件的准确位置。|synonyms: address, pointer, reference, identifier|antonyms: N/A|noun


Etymology: From the verb "locate" + "-or,"  ultimately from Latin "locare" (to place).
