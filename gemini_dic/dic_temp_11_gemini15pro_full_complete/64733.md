eschew:/ɪsˈtʃuː/
verb
避开；回避；避免
deliberately avoid using or doing something
She eschews publicity and avoids social media. 她回避公众关注，避免使用社交媒体。
He eschews all forms of violence. 他避免一切形式的暴力。
近义词：avoid, shun, abstain from, refrain from, forgo
反义词：embrace, indulge in, welcome
词性：动词

eschew:/ɪsˈtʃuː/
Etymology: From Old French *eschiver, from Frankish *skiuhan "to shy away from, dread," from Proto-Germanic *skeukhwōną "to be startled, shy away." Related to shy.
