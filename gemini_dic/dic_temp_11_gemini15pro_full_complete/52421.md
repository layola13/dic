didjeridoo: /ˌdɪdʒəriˈduː/ (also /ˌdɪdʒəriˈduː/) | n. | 迪吉里杜管 | A wind instrument developed by Indigenous Australians of northern Australia, made from a length of eucalyptus branch or bamboo that has been hollowed out by termites. It is played by blowing into one end while vibrating the lips, producing a continuous drone with rhythmic variations. |  He played a haunting melody on the didjeridoo. 他用迪吉里杜管演奏了一首萦绕心头的曲子。 |  The didjeridoo's deep drone resonated through the valley. 迪吉里杜管的低沉嗡嗡声在山谷中回荡。 | 近义词: drone pipe, Aboriginal trumpet | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
