grene:/ɡriːn/| adj.|绿色的；未成熟的；缺乏经验的|Of the color between blue and yellow in the spectrum; colored like grass or emeralds.  Also, inexperienced or immature. |例句：The grene leaves rustled in the wind. 绿叶在风中沙沙作响。

例句：He was but a grene lad, fresh from the farm. 他只是个刚从农场来的青涩小伙子。|近义词：verdant, leafy, immature, inexperienced|反义词：ripe, mature, experienced, brown, yellow|词性:形容词
词源: Middle English: from Old English grēne, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch groen and German grün.
