key:/kiː/| n. adj.|钥匙，关键，秘诀，（键盘上的）键，答案，图例|A key is a piece of metal that has been cut into a special shape so that it can be used for locking or unlocking a door, starting a car, etc.  A key can also refer to something crucial or essential, a solution, or an explanation.|例句：This is the key to the front door. 这是前门的钥匙。|近义词：clue, solution, answer, secret, code, button|反义词:lock, problem, mystery|词性:名词, 形容词

key:/kiː/| adj.|关键的，重要的，主要的|Something that is key is of crucial importance.|例句：She played a key role in the negotiations. 她在谈判中起了关键作用。|近义词：crucial, critical, essential, vital, important, central|反义词:minor, unimportant, insignificant, trivial, peripheral|词性:形容词
