fructuary:/ˈfruːktʃuəri/| n. adj.|受益人；享有收益权的|A person who receives the income or profits from property or an investment, but does not own the property or investment itself.  A fructuary interest is the right to enjoy the fruits of something owned by another person.|She is the fructuary of her late husband's estate. 她是她已故丈夫遗产的受益人。|Synonyms: beneficiary, recipient, heir |Antonyms: grantor, settler|noun

fructuary:/ˈfruːktʃuəri/| adj.|享有收益权的；结果实的|Producing or yielding fruit; relating to the enjoyment of profits or benefits.|He has a fructuary interest in the trust. 他在信托中享有收益权。|Synonyms: fruitful, productive, profitable|Antonyms: barren, unproductive|adjective
