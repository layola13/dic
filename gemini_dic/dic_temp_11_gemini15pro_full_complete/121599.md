misopinion:/ˌmɪsəˈpɪnjən/ | n. | 错误的观点，误解 | An incorrect or mistaken opinion or belief. | He had a misopinion about her intentions, thinking she was trying to sabotage him when she was actually trying to help. 他误解了她的意图，认为她试图破坏他，而实际上她是在试图帮助他。| Example:  It's a common misopinion that all snakes are venomous.  许多人误以为所有的蛇都有毒。|Synonyms: misconception, fallacy, delusion, misapprehension| Antonyms:  correct opinion, accurate understanding, truth, fact | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + opinion (from Old French opinion, from Latin opiniō, meaning "opinion, conjecture, fancy").
