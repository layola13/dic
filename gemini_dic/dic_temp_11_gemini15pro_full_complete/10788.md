aquarius: /əˈkwɛəriəs/ | n. | 水瓶座（人），水瓶座（星座） | A person born under the sign of Aquarius (between January 20 and February 18), or the constellation itself. |例句：She is an Aquarius, so she's very creative. 她是一个水瓶座，所以她很有创造力。 |例句：Aquarius is an air sign. 水瓶座是风象星座。|近义词: (for people) nonconformist, individualist | 反义词: (no direct antonym for a zodiac sign) | 词性: noun


词源:  From Latin *aquārius* "water carrier," from *aqua* "water."  The constellation was named this because it appears in the sky during the rainy season.
