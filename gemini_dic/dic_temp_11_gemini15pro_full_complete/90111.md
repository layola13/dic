hot-dog:/ˈhɒtˌdɒɡ/ (British), /ˈhɑːtˌdɔːɡ/ (American)| n.|热狗|A frankfurter, especially one served in a long split roll.|例句：I ate a hot-dog for lunch. 我午饭吃了个热狗。|近义词：frankfurter, wiener, |反义词:|noun

hot-dog:/ˈhɒtˌdɒɡ/ (British), /ˈhɑːtˌdɔːɡ/ (American)| v. |炫耀（尤指体育运动）|To perform elaborate maneuvers or stunts, especially in a sport.|例句：He was hot-dogging on his skateboard. 他在滑板上炫技。|近义词：show off, grandstand|反义词:|verb
