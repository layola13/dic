coursework:/ˈkɔːrswɜːrk/ (US) /ˈkɔːswɜːk/ (UK)
noun
课业；课程作业
written or practical work done by a student during a course of study, and usually assessed to count towards a final mark or grade.
She's struggling to keep up with her coursework. 她正努力跟上她的课业。
I have a lot of coursework to complete before the end of the semester.  我这学期结束前有很多课业要完成。

近义词: schoolwork, studies, assignments, homework
反义词: leisure, recreation
词性: n.
