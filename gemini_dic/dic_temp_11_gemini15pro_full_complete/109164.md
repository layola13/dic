legitimatenesses:/ləˌdʒɪtɪmɪtnəsɪz/
|n.|合法性；正统性|The state of being legitimate or lawful.  The quality or fact of being genuine and conforming to recognized standards. |The legitimatenesses of their claims were questioned. 他们的声明的合法性受到了质疑。|Synonyms: validity, lawfulness, legality, authenticity, genuineness|Antonyms: illegality, illegitimacy, invalidity, spuriousness|noun
