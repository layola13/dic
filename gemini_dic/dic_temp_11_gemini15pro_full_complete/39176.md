congealing:/kənˈdʒiːlɪŋ/
verb
凝固；冻结
The fat congealed on the top of the soup.  汤上面的脂肪凝固了。
The blood congealed around the wound.  血液在伤口周围凝结。
近义词: solidifying, thickening, hardening, setting
反义词: melting, liquefying, thawing
词性: verb (present participle)

congeal:/kənˈdʒiːl/
verb
凝固；冻结
The gravy began to congeal as it cooled. 肉汁冷却后开始凝结。
Fear congealed his blood.  恐惧使他的血液凝固。
近义词: solidify, thicken, harden, set
反义词: melt, liquefy, thaw
词性: verb


Etymology: From Old French congeler, from Latin congelare ("to freeze, harden"), from com- ("together") + gelare ("to freeze").
