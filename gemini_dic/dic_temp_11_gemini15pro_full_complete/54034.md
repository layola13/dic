discussed:/dɪˈskʌst/| v.|讨论，谈论|  past tense and past participle of "discuss"|We discussed the problem at length. 我们详细讨论了这个问题。|Synonyms: debated, deliberated, considered|Antonyms: ignored, avoided|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *discussen*, from Latin *discussus*, past participle of *discutere* ("to shake apart, disperse, examine"), from *dis-* ("apart") + *quatere* ("to shake").
