frailer:/ˈfreɪlər/ | adj. | 更虚弱的；更脆弱的 | (comparative of frail) Weaker and more delicate; more easily broken or damaged. |  The old woman grew frailer with each passing day. 老妇人一天天变得更加虚弱。| synonyms: weaker, feebler, more delicate, more fragile | antonyms: stronger, healthier, more robust |  adjective

frailer:/ˈfreɪlər/ | n. | 脆弱的人；易碎的东西 | (noun form of frail, though less common) A person who is frail or something that is easily broken. |  The earthquake left many frailers homeless and injured. 地震使许多体弱者无家可归，受伤。| synonyms: weakling, invalid | antonyms: strongman, powerhouse | noun
