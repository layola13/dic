metering:/ˈmiːtərɪŋ/
n.
计量；测量；分配
The process of measuring and recording the amount of something used, especially electricity, gas, or water.
Smart metering systems can help consumers track their energy usage.  智能计量系统可以帮助消费者跟踪他们的能源使用情况。
measurement, gauging, quantification
allocation, rationing, distribution
noun
