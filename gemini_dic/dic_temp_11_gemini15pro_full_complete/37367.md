commensurably:/kəˈmɛnʃərəbli/ | adv. | 相称地，相当地 | In a way that is in proportion to something else; proportionately. | The rewards were distributed commensurably with the effort put in.  奖励与付出的努力成比例地分配。| 近义词: proportionately, correspondingly, proportionally, relatively, comparably | 反义词: disproportionately, inadequately, insufficiently | 词性: 副词

commensurate:/kəˈmɛnʃərət/ | adj. | 相称的，相当的 | Corresponding in size or degree; in proportion. | Her salary is commensurate with her experience. 她的薪水与她的经验相称。| 近义词: proportionate, corresponding, equivalent, appropriate, suitable | 反义词: disproportionate, inadequate, insufficient | 词性: 形容词


词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin commensuratus ‘measured together’, from com- ‘together’ + mensurare ‘to measure’.
