gyreful:/ˈdʒaɪərfl/
adjective
旋转的，回旋的
Characterized by gyres or circular motions; whirling.
例句：The gyreful dance of the leaves in the wind was mesmerizing.  风中旋转的树叶舞动着，令人着迷。

例句：The gyreful motion of the planets around the sun is a constant reminder of the vastness of space. 行星绕太阳旋转的运动不断提醒着我们太空的浩瀚。
近义词：whirling, swirling, rotating, circling
反义词：static, still, motionless
词性: adjective

词源:  From "gyre" (a circular or spiral motion) + "-ful" (suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by").  "Gyre" itself comes from the Greek word "gyros," meaning "circle" or "ring."
