decussately:/ˌdɛkəˈseɪtli/ | adv. | 交叉地，十字形地 |  arranged in the form of an X, often referring to anatomical structures that cross each other. | The optic nerves decussate at the optic chiasm.  视神经在视交叉处交叉。| 近义词: crisscross, intersect | 反义词: parallel | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Latin *decussat-*, past participle stem of *decusso*,  "to divide crosswise," from *decussis* "a Roman coin of ten *asses*," thus marked with an X.
