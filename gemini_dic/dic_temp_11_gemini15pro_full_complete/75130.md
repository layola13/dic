friskiest:/ˈfrɪskiɪst/
adjective
最活泼的
Most playful and energetic.
The friskiest puppy in the litter was chasing its tail.  这窝里最活泼的小狗正在追逐自己的尾巴。
synonyms: liveliest, most energetic, most playful, most spirited
antonyms: quietest, calmest, most subdued
词性: 形容词
