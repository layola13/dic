distribute:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːt/ (US) /dɪˈstrɪbjuːt/ (UK)| v. |分发，分配，散布|To divide something among several or many people or to spread or scatter something over an area.|The company distributes its products nationwide.  这家公司在全国范围内分销其产品。|The teacher distributed the exam papers to the students. 老师把试卷分发给学生。|synonyms: allocate, apportion, dispense, disseminate|antonyms: collect, gather, hoard|verb
