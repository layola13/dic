consigning:/kənˈsaɪnɪŋ/
verb
交付；托运；寄售
delivering something to a person's custody, typically in order for it to be sold
The owner is consigning the paintings to Sotheby’s.  这位主人正在把这些画寄售给苏富比拍卖行。
synonyms: delivering, transferring, committing, entrusting
antonyms: receiving, accepting, retaining, withholding
verb

consign:/kənˈsaɪn/
verb
交付；托运；寄售
assign; commit decisively or permanently
She consigned the letter to the flames. 她把信投入火中。
synonyms: assign, commit, relegate, banish
antonyms: retrieve, reclaim, rescue
