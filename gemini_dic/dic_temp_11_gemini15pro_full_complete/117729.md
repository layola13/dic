melanic:/məˈlænɪk/
adjective
黑色素的;黑化的
Characterized by the presence of melanin, especially in abnormally large amounts.
The melanic form of the peppered moth became more common during the Industrial Revolution.  工业革命期间，胡椒蛾的黑化形态变得更加普遍。
近义词:dark, black, pigmented
反义词:leucistic, albino
词性:adjective

melanic:/məˈlænɪk/
noun
黑化个体
An individual with an unusually dark coloration due to melanin.
The researchers observed a higher frequency of melanics in polluted areas. 研究人员在污染地区观察到更高频率的黑化个体。
近义词:dark form, black variant
反义词:light form, pale variant
词性:noun
