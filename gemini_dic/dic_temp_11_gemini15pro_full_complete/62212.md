encouraging:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
令人鼓舞的；鼓励的
giving someone support or confidence; making someone more likely to do something
Her encouraging words boosted my confidence. 她鼓舞人心的话增强了我的信心。
近义词:positive, supportive, inspiring, motivating
反义词:discouraging, disheartening, demotivating
词性:adjective

词源:
From the verb "encourage," which comes from Old French "encoragier," meaning "to make strong, hearten."  The prefix "en-" intensifies the meaning, and "-courage" comes from Latin "cor" meaning "heart."
