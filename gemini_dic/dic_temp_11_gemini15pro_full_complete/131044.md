nonavoidablenesses:/ˌnɒnəˈvɔɪdəblnəsɪz/
|n.|不可避免性|The state or quality of being unavoidable; inevitability.|The nonavoidablenesses of aging are something we all must face. 我们都必须面对衰老的不可避免性。|近义词: inevitability, unavoidability, certainty|反义词: avoidability, preventability|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + avoidable + -ness.  "Avoidable" comes from the Old French "avoider" meaning "to empty, clear out," from the prefix "es-" (out) + "vuider" (to empty).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives.
