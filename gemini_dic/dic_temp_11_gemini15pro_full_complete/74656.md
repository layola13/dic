free-up:/friː ˈʌp/| phrasal verb |释放，腾出|To make something available for use by removing it from its current use or by getting rid of things that are no longer needed.|I need to free up some space on my hard drive. 我需要在我的硬盘上腾出一些空间。|Example:  Decluttering your closet can free up a lot of space.  整理你的衣橱可以腾出很多空间。|Synonyms: release, liberate, clear, empty |Antonyms: occupy, fill, clog|verb phrase
