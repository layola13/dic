discreditability:/dɪsˌkrɛdɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可信性，不名誉，声名狼藉
The state or quality of being discreditable; something that brings discredit.
His discreditability made it difficult for him to find work. 他的不名誉让他很难找到工作。
近义词:disgrace, ignominy, disrepute, shame
反义词:credibility, honor, respectability

词性:noun

词源: From discreditable + -ity.  Discreditable itself comes from discredit + -able. Discredit ultimately derives from the Latin dis- (not) + credere (to believe).
