logistics:/ləˈdʒɪstɪks/ /loʊˈdʒɪstɪks/
noun
物流；后勤；逻辑学
The detailed organization and implementation of a complex operation.  The commercial activity of transporting goods to customers.
例句：The logistics of the military campaign were complex. 军事行动的后勤保障非常复杂。

例句：The company specializes in the logistics of transporting fresh produce. 这家公司专门从事新鲜农产品的运输物流。
近义词：organization, planning, supply chain management, distribution
反义词：N/A
词性：noun


etymology: From French *logistique*, from *loger* "to lodge", ultimately from Frankish *lauban* "to shelter." Originally referred to the art of quartering troops.
