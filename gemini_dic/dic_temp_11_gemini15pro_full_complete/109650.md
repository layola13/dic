leucaniline:/ˌluːkəˈnɪlɪn/ |n.|无色碱;还原 Fuchsine|A colorless triamine, C19H19N3, the leuco base of rosaniline, obtained by reduction of rosaniline.  It forms colorless crystals, melting at 100°C.  On oxidation, it is converted back to rosaniline.|Leucaniline is an important intermediate in the synthesis of certain dyes. 无色碱是某些染料合成中的重要中间体。|Synonym: triaminotriphenylmethane|Antonym: rosaniline|noun
