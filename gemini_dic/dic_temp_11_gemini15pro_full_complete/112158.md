look-ins:/ˈlʊkɪnz/| n.|短暂访问；(尤指电视)简短露面；短暂参与|A brief visit or appearance, especially a brief appearance on television or a short period of participation in something.|例句：He only had a few look-ins during the game. 他在比赛中只有几次短暂的出场。

例句：The local news program gave the event a few look-ins. 当地新闻节目对该事件进行了简短的报道。|近义词：visits, glimpses, cameos|反义词:extended stays, long appearances|词性:名词
