annihilative:/əˈnaɪəˌleɪtɪv/| adj.|毁灭性的，歼灭性的|Causing destruction or complete annihilation; extremely destructive.|The annihilative power of nuclear weapons is a constant threat to world peace. 核武器的毁灭性力量是对世界和平的持续威胁。|The hurricane had an annihilative effect on the small coastal town. 飓风对这个沿海小镇造成了毁灭性的影响。|近义词：destructive, devastating, ruinous, cataclysmic|反义词：creative, constructive, beneficial|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin annihilativus, from Latin annihilatus (past participle of annihilare "to reduce to nothing"), from ad- "to" + nihil "nothing".
