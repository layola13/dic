dandyishly:/ˈdændɪɪʃli/ | adv. | 花花公子般地，纨绔子弟般地 | In a manner characteristic of a dandy; elegantly or stylishly, often with a touch of flamboyance or affectation. | He strolled dandyishly down the street, twirling his cane. 他沿着街道花花公子般地漫步，旋转着手杖。| Near synonyms:  foppishly, stylishly, smartly, nattily, rakishly | Antonyms: sloppily, carelessly, plainly, unstylishly |词性: 副词

dandy:/ˈdændi/ | n. | 花花公子，纨绔子弟 | A man unduly devoted to style, smartness, and fashion in dress and appearance. | He was a dandy, always impeccably dressed. 他是个花花公子，总是穿着无可挑剔。 | Near synonyms: fop, beau, coxcomb | Antonyms: slob | 词性: 名词
