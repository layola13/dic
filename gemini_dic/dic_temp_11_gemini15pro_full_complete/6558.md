abyss:/əˈbɪs/|n.|深渊，无底洞；地狱；极大的痛苦|A deep, immeasurable space, gulf, or cavity; anything profound, unfathomable, or infinite.|She stared into the abyss below.| 近义词: chasm, gorge, void, gulf | 反义词: summit, peak | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle French *abisme*, from Late Latin *abyssus*, from Ancient Greek *ἄβυσσος (ábyssos)* meaning "bottomless," from *ἀ- (a-)*  "without" + *βυσσός (byssós)*  "bottom, depth."
