courses:/ˈkɔːrsɪz/ (US), /ˈkɔːsɪz/ (UK)| n. |课程; 路线; 一道菜|A series of lessons or lectures on a particular subject. Also, the route or direction something moves in, or a part of a meal.|

例句：I'm taking three courses this semester. 我这学期上了三门课。

例句：The ship altered its course to avoid the storm.  这艘船改变了航线以避开风暴。

例句：The main course was roast beef. 主菜是烤牛肉。

近义词：classes, lessons, subjects, curriculum, path, route, direction, dish

反义词:| (No direct antonym, depends on the context. For "path," maybe "detour")

词性: noun
