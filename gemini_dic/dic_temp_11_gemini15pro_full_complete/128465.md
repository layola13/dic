newsily:/ˈnjuːzɪli/ | adv. | 好管闲事地，爱传闲话地 | In a manner characteristic of someone who is eager to spread news, often gossip. |  She newsily told everyone about the argument she'd overheard. 她到处跟人说她偷听到的争吵。| 近义词: gossipmongeringly, chattily, officiously | 反义词: discreetly, privately, reservedly | 词性: 副词
