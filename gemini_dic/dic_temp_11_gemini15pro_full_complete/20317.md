bisexuals:/baɪˈsɛkʃuəlz/
noun
双性恋者
People who are sexually attracted to both men and women.
Many bisexuals feel marginalized by both the heterosexual and homosexual communities.  许多双性恋者感到被异性恋和同性恋群体边缘化。
Bisexuals often face unique challenges in a society that frequently assumes everyone is either straight or gay.  双性恋者在一个经常假设每个人要么是异性恋要么是同性恋的社会中，往往面临独特的挑战。
synonyms: non-monosexuals, ambisexuals, pansexuals
antonyms: heterosexuals, homosexuals
