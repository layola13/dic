greatness:/ˈɡreɪtnəs/| n.|伟大；巨大；重要性|The quality of being great; eminence or distinction.|His paintings achieved greatness in his later years. 他的绘画在他晚年达到了巅峰。|近义词：magnitude, grandeur, eminence, distinction|反义词：insignificance, mediocrity, inferiority|词性:名词

词源：From Middle English *gretnes*, from Old English *grēatnes*, equivalent to *great* + *-ness*. Cognate with Scots *gritness* (“greatness”).
