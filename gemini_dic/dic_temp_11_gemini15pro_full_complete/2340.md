adhesions:/ədˈhiːʒənz/
|n.|粘连；黏附；|The surgery was complicated by adhesions from a previous operation.  这次手术因先前手术造成的粘连而变得复杂。|
|n.|坚持；忠诚；|His strong political adhesions prevented him from compromising. 他强烈的政治立场使他无法妥协。|
|Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin adhaesio(n-), from the verb adhaerere (see adhere).|
|Synonyms:  (for medical sense) scar tissue, bands, connections. (for non-medical sense) attachment, loyalty, allegiance, fidelity|
|Antonyms: (for non-medical sense) disloyalty, unfaithfulness, detachment|
|Word type: Noun|
