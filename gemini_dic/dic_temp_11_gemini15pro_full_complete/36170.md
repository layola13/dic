coffered:/ˈkɒfəd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːfərd/ (US)| adj.| 镶嵌的，方格状的，(天花板)格状的| Having sunken panels or coffers, resembling a series of recessed squares or rectangles.| The ceiling of the library was coffered and ornate.  图书馆的天花板是格子状的，装饰华丽。|Synonyms: recessed, paneled, inlaid| Antonyms: flat, smooth| adjective
