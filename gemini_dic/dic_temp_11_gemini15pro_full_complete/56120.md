doctrinally: /dɒkˈtraɪnəli/ /dɑːkˈtraɪnəli/
| adv. |教义上地；学理上地|In a way that relates to a doctrine or doctrines; according to doctrine.|The statement was doctrinally unsound.  这个说法在教义上是站不住脚的。| The Pope is doctrinally infallible. 教宗在教义上是不会错的。|Synonym: dogmatically, theoretically|Antonym: practically, empirically|词性: 副词
