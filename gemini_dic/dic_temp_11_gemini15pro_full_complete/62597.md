enfeloned:/ɪnˈfɛl·ənd/
verb
使成为重罪犯；宣布…为重罪
To make something a felony (a serious crime).  To declare guilty of a felony.
The king enfeloned anyone who spoke against him. 国王把任何反对他的人都定为重罪犯。
Synonym:incriminate, indict
Antonym:exonerate, acquit
词性:动词
