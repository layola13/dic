interadaptions:/ˌɪntərˌædæpˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |相互适应|  The reciprocal adaptations of two or more species or parts to one another.  It refers to the evolutionary process where two or more species influence each other's adaptations over time. | The interadaptions between the bee and the flower are fascinating. 蜜蜂和花朵之间的相互适应令人着迷。| The complex interadaptions within the ecosystem maintain its balance. 生态系统内复杂的相互适应维持着它的平衡。 | synonyms: coadaptation, coevolution | antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider maladaptation, incompatibility) | noun
