manoscope:/ˈmænəˌskoʊp/| n.|压力计，压力示波器|An instrument for measuring and recording variations in pressure, especially in the intake manifold of an internal combustion engine.|The mechanic used a manoscope to diagnose the engine problem. 机械师用压力计诊断发动机问题。|Example: The manoscope showed a significant drop in pressure, indicating a leak in the system. 压力计显示压力显著下降，表明系统中存在泄漏。|Synonyms: pressure gauge, manometer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
