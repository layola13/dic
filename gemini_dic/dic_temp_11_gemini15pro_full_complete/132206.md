nondebater:/ˌnɒndɪˈbeɪtər/ | n. |非辩论者，不参加辩论的人 | Someone who does not participate in debates. | He's a nondebater, preferring to observe rather than argue. 他是一个非辩论者，宁愿观察也不愿争论。|  She identified as a nondebater and felt uncomfortable in the competitive environment. 她认为自己是一个非辩论者，在这个竞争激烈的环境中感到不舒服。 | 近义词: nonparticipant, observer | 反义词: debater, arguer | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "debater" (from the verb "debate," ultimately from Old French "debatre" meaning "to fight, struggle, contend").
