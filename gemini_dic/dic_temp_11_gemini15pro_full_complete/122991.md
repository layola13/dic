monandry:/məˈnændri/ /moʊˈnændri/
|n.|一雄一雌；一夫一妻制（植物学）;一雄蕊；单雄蕊（植物学）|The condition or practice of having only one husband or male mate at one time (zoology); having only one stamen (botany).|Monandry is rare in most animal species.  大多数动物物种中，一夫一妻制很罕见。|Monandry is a characteristic of certain plant families. 单雄蕊是某些植物科的特征。|近义词:monogamy (in the zoological sense)|反义词:polyandry, polygamy|词性:noun
