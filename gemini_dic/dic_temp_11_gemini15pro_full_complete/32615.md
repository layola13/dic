chirping:/ˈtʃɜːrpɪŋ/ | n. | 唧唧喳喳声；啁啾声 | the short, sharp high-pitched sounds made by small birds or insects | The chirping of the crickets filled the night air. 蟋蟀的鸣叫声充满了夜空。 | 近义词:twittering, peeping | 反义词: silence | 词性:名词

chirping:/ˈtʃɜːrpɪŋ/ | adj. | 吱吱喳喳的；啁啾的 | making short, sharp high-pitched sounds | The chirping birds woke me up early this morning. 吱吱喳喳的鸟儿一大早就把我吵醒了。| 近义词:twittering, peeping | 反义词: silent | 词性:形容词


chirp:/tʃɜːrp/ | v. | 唧唧喳喳地叫；啁啾 | to make short, sharp high-pitched sounds | The sparrows were chirping merrily in the trees. 麻雀在树上欢快地啁啾。| 近义词:twitter, peep | 反义词: be silent | 词性:动词


词源:  Imitative origin, echoing the sound.  (源于模仿声音)
