mortgaged:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒd/| adj.|已抵押的|Having a mortgage on (a property); used as security for a loan.|例句：The house is mortgaged to the bank. 这房子抵押给了银行。|例句：They are struggling to pay their mortgaged home. 他们正努力偿还抵押贷款的房子。|近义词：pledged, secured|反义词:unmortgaged, free and clear|词性:形容词

mortgaging:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒɪŋ/|v.|抵押|The act of using property as security for a loan.|例句：He considered mortgaging his house to start a business. 他考虑抵押房子创业。|例句：Mortgaging their future for short-term gains is not a wise decision.  为了短期利益抵押他们的未来不是明智的决定。|近义词：pledging, securing|反义词:redeeming, paying off|词性:动词
