neglectful:/nɪˈɡlɛktfəl/| adj.|疏忽的，玩忽职守的|Failing to care for properly.|The neglectful landlord allowed the building to fall into disrepair.  疏忽大意的房东任由房屋破败不堪。|近义词：careless, negligent, remiss, lax, inattentive|反义词：attentive, careful, conscientious, diligent, mindful|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *neglectus*, past participle of *neglegere* "disregard, neglect," from *nec* "not" + *legere* "gather, choose, read."
