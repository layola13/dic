hardier:/ˈhɑːrdiər/ (US), /ˈhɑːdiə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更强壮的，更坚强的，更耐寒的|More robust, stronger, and more able to survive difficult conditions, especially cold weather.|These hardier plants can withstand frost. 这些更耐寒的植物能抵御霜冻。|Synonyms: tougher, stronger, more robust, more resilient|Antonyms: weaker, more fragile, more delicate|adjective

hardier:/ˈhɑːrdiər/ (US), /ˈhɑːdiə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更大胆的，更勇敢的|More bold or daring.|The hardier explorers ventured further into the unknown. 更大胆的探险家们进一步冒险进入未知领域。|Synonyms: bolder, more daring, more courageous|Antonyms: more timid, more cautious, more fearful|adjective
