euhemerism: /juːˈhiːmərɪzəm/ | n. | 世俗化，理性化神话|The interpretation of myths as traditional accounts of historical persons and events.|Many scholars see euhemerism in the portrayal of King Arthur as a real historical figure. 许多学者将亚瑟王描绘成一个真实的历史人物，视其为世俗化的例子。|Example:  The theory of euhemerism suggests that many gods were originally real people whose deeds became exaggerated over time. 世俗化理论认为，许多神最初都是真实存在的人，他们的行为随着时间的推移而被夸大。|Synonyms:  rationalization, demystification, historicization|Antonyms:  mythification, deification, apotheosis|noun
