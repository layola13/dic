Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner tonight? = 他今晚在做什么晚餐？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before the party? = 在派对之前她吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 在她换专业之前，她一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will she bring to the picnic? = 她会带什么去野餐？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be wearing to the wedding? = 她婚礼上会穿什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have accomplished by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have finished reading by next month? = 到下个月她将读完什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习了什么多久？
