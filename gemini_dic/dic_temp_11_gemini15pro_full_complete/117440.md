meditate: /ˈmedɪteɪt/ (US), /ˈmedɪteɪt/ (UK) | v. | 冥想，沉思，思考 |  to think deeply about something; to focus one's mind for a period of time, in silence or with the aid of chanting, for religious or spiritual purposes or as a method of relaxation. |  She meditates for an hour every morning. 她每天早上冥想一个小时。|  He meditated on the meaning of life. 他沉思生命的意义。 | synonyms: contemplate, ponder, reflect, muse, ruminate | antonyms:  ignore, neglect, disregard | verb
