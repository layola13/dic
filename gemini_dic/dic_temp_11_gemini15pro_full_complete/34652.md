cleptomania:/ˌkleptəˈmeɪniə/| n. |盗窃癖，偷窃狂|An impulse control disorder characterized by a recurrent failure to resist urges to steal items that are not needed for personal use or monetary value.|She was diagnosed with kleptomania after being caught stealing a lipstick she didn't even need.|近义词:theft, stealing, shoplifting|反义词:honesty|词性:noun
