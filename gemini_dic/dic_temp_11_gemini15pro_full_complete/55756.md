diverse:/dɪˈvɜːrs/ (US) /daɪˈvɜːs/ (UK)| adj.|不同的，多种多样的，形形色色的|Showing a great deal of variety; of different kinds or forms.|例句：The city has a diverse population. 这个城市人口构成十分多元。|近义词：varied, different, multiple, various|反义词：similar, uniform, homogeneous|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *diversus*, past participle of *divertere* "to turn aside, turn in different directions," from *dis-* "apart, asunder" + *vertere* "to turn."
