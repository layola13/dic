cutesier:/ˈkjuːt.si.ər/ /ˈkjuː.t̬i.ər/
adjective
更可爱的
comparative form of *cute*: more cute
例句：My new puppy is even cutesier than my last one. 我的新小狗比上一只更可爱了。
例句：That kitten is cutesier with its little bow. 那只小猫戴着小蝴蝶结更可爱了。
近义词：more adorable, more charming, sweeter
反义词：uglier, less attractive, homelier
词性: 形容词
