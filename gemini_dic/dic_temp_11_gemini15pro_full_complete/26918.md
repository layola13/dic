calamist:/ˈkæləmɪst/| n.|诽谤者，造谣者|A person who utters calumnies; a slanderer or detractor.|He was known as a calamist, always spreading rumors and lies about his rivals. 他被称为诽谤者，总是散布关于竞争对手的谣言和谎言。|近义词：slanderer, detractor, libeler, defamer|反义词：advocate, supporter|词性: noun

calamist:/ˈkæləmɪst/| n.|灾难预言者，悲观主义者|A person who predicts disaster; a pessimist.|The calamist predicted the end of the world was near. 这个灾难预言者预测世界末日即将来临。|近义词：pessimist, doomsayer, alarmist|反义词：optimist|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin calamistēs, from Latin calumnia (“false accusation, malicious misrepresentation”), from calvi (“to deceive”).
