heavenlier:/ˈhevnliər/ | adj. |更天堂般的，更神圣的|More heavenly; resembling heaven more closely. | The music was even heavenlier than I remembered.  这音乐比我记忆中的还要天籁。|近义词：divine, celestial, ethereal, sublime |反义词:earthly, mundane, hellish |词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *hevenlier, comparative of hevenli (“heavenly”), equivalent to heavenly + -er (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Old English heofonlic.
