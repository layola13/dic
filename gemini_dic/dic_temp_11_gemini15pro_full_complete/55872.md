divisibility:/dɪˌvɪzɪˈbɪləti/| n. |可除性，可分性|The property of being divisible by a number.  The divisibility of 12 by 3 means that 12 can be divided by 3 with no remainder.|The divisibility of 100 by 25 is obvious. 100除以25的整除性是显而易见的。|近义词：factorability |反义词: indivisibility|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *divisibilitee*, from Late Latin *dīvīsībilis* (divisible) + *-itās* (-ity).
