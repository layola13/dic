overrestrain:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstreɪn/
verb
过度限制，过度约束
to restrain too much; to control or limit someone or something too much
The child was so overrestrained by his parents that he became withdrawn.  这孩子被他父母过度约束，变得孤僻了。
The government's overrestrained economic policies stifled growth. 政府过度限制的经济政策扼杀了经济增长。

近义词: restrict, curb, inhibit, suppress
反义词: encourage, liberate, free, unleash
词性: verb
