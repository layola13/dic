chewier:/ˈtʃuː.i.ər/ (US), /ˈtʃuː.ɪ.ə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更有嚼劲的，更耐嚼的|Comparative form of chewy: more chewy.  Describes something that requires more effort to chew than something else.|This steak is much chewier than the last one I had. 这块牛排比我上次吃的那块更有嚼劲。|The jerky was surprisingly chewier than I expected.  这肉干比我想象中更有嚼劲。|近义词：tougher, firmer, denser|反义词：tenderer, softer, less chewy|词性:形容词

词源: From *chew* + *-y* (adjective-forming suffix) + *-er* (comparative suffix). *Chew* comes from Old English *cēowan*.
