erg:/ɜːrɡ/
n.
尔格（物理学单位）
A unit of work or energy, equal to one dyne acting through a distance of one centimeter.
The explosion released 10^15 ergs of energy.  这次爆炸释放了10^15尔格的能量。
近义词:joule
反义词:(No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)
词性:noun


erg:/ɜːrɡ/
n.
沙丘
A broad, flat area of desert covered with wind-swept sand.
The camel trekked across the vast erg.  骆驼跋涉穿过广阔的沙丘。
近义词:dune, sand sea
反义词:(No direct antonym for a geographical feature)
词性:noun
