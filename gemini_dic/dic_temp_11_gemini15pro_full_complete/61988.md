enactors:/ɪˈnæktərz/ | n. | 颁布者；实施者；扮演者 |  People who enact something, meaning they put it into practice, perform it, or make it happen. | The enactors of the new law were praised for their foresight. 新法律的颁布者因其远见而受到赞扬。|  The enactors of the play brought the characters to life. 戏剧的演员赋予了角色生命。|Synonyms: performers, implementers, legislators | Antonyms: observers, spectators | Noun
