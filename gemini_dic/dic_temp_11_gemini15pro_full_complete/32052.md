cherry-like: /ˈtʃɛriˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像樱桃的，樱桃似的 | Resembling a cherry in shape, color, or taste.  | The candies were small and round, cherry-like in appearance.  这些糖果又小又圆，外观像樱桃。 |  The berries were small and cherry-like. 这些浆果小巧，像樱桃一样。 | synonyms: cherry-red, cerise, ruby, crimson | antonyms: unlike a cherry, dissimilar to a cherry | adjective
