enhance:/ɪnˈhæns/ /ɛnˈhæns/ |v.|增强，提高，改进|to increase or further improve the quality, value, or extent of something|例句：The new software enhances the performance of the computer.  这套新软件增强了计算机的性能。

例句：Regular exercise can enhance your physical and mental well-being.  规律的运动可以增强你的身心健康。|近义词：improve, boost, strengthen, augment, upgrade|反义词:diminish, reduce, weaken, impair, degrade|词性:动词
