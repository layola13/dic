microscopial: /ˌmaɪkrəˈskɒpɪkəl/ (US) /ˌmaɪkrəˈskɒpɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|微观的；极小的|Relating to or involving a microscope; extremely small, so as to be visible only with a microscope.|The bacteria are microscopial and cannot be seen with the naked eye. 这些细菌是微观的，肉眼看不到。|The dust mites are microscopial creatures that live in our homes.  尘螨是生活在我们家中的微小生物。|近义词: minute, tiny, minuscule|反义词: macroscopic, huge, gigantic|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *microscopique*  and directly from Modern Latin *microscopius* "microscopic," from Greek *mikros* "small" + *-skopios*  "watching."
