dactylet:/ˈdæktɪlɪt/| n.|指小节, daktylos 指状物; 指章|A dactyl is a metrical foot consisting of one long syllable followed by two short syllables, or of one stressed syllable followed by two unstressed syllables, as in the word "poetry".  A dactylet is a small, finger-like object or formation.|The geologist pointed to the dactylet formations on the cave wall. 地质学家指着洞壁上指状的构造。|Synonyms: dactyl (in the geological sense), fingerling (can sometimes be used for small finger-like objects)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
