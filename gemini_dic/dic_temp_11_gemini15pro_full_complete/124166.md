morula:/ˈmɒrʊlə/ (UK) /ˈmɔːrʊlə/ (US)| n. |桑椹胚|A solid ball of cells resulting from the cleavage of a fertilized ovum, and from which a blastula is formed.|The morula stage is crucial for the development of the embryo. 桑椹胚阶段对胚胎发育至关重要.|Synonyms:  blastomere (early stage) |Antonyms:  blastocyst (later stage)|noun
