cicada:/sɪˈkeɪdə/ /sɪˈkɑːdə/| n. |蝉|A large homopterous insect with long transparent wings, the male of which produces a loud, shrill sound by vibrating two membranes on its abdomen.|The cicadas buzzed loudly in the summer heat.  夏天的酷暑中，蝉鸣声声。|Synonyms:  locust (imprecise) |Antonyms: None (no direct opposite for an insect)|noun
