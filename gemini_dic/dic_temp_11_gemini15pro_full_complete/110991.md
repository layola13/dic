liquidizing:/ˈlɪkwɪdaɪzɪŋ/
v.
液化；溶解；清算；消灭
Turning something into a liquid state; converting assets into cash; eliminating.
She was liquidizing the frozen fruit for a smoothie. 她正在把冷冻水果液化做冰沙。
The company is liquidizing its assets to pay off debts. 公司正在清算资产以偿还债务。
Synonyms: liquefying, dissolving, melting, cashing in, eliminating, wiping out
Antonyms: solidifying, freezing, accumulating
Verb
