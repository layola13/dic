broil:/brɔɪl/| v. |烧烤，烤炙|To cook by direct exposure to radiant heat, as over a grill or under an oven broiler.|例句：I broiled the chicken for dinner. 我烤了鸡肉做晚餐。

例句：The steak was broiled to perfection. 牛排烤得恰到好处。|近义词：grill, roast, bake|反义词: boil, simmer, stew|词性：动词
词源：Middle English broilen, from Old French bruillir, of Germanic origin; akin to Old High German brātan to roast
