glumal: /ˈɡluːməl/
noun|肾小球|A tuft of capillaries situated within a Bowman's capsule at the beginning of a nephron in the kidney.|The glumeral filters waste products from the blood.|近义词: glomerulus|反义词:  (No direct antonym exists.  Consider terms related to other parts of the nephron, like "renal tubule")|词性:n.
