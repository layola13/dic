memoirs:/ˈmɛmwɑːrz/ (US) /ˈmɛmwɑːz/ (UK)
n.
回忆录；自传
A historical account or biography written from personal knowledge or special sources.
Her memoirs offer a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the rich and famous. 她的回忆录提供了对富人和名人生活的一个迷人的一瞥。
synonyms: autobiography, reminiscences, life story, chronicle
antonyms: fiction, fantasy
