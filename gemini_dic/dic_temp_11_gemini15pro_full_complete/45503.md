curds:/kɜːrdz/| n. |凝乳，豆腐|The solid portion of milk that separates from the whey when milk is coagulated, typically by adding rennet or an acid.|例句：She added lemon juice to the milk to form curds. 她往牛奶里加柠檬汁做凝乳。

例句：Cheese is made from curds. 奶酪是由凝乳制成的。|近义词：coagulum, clabber|反义词:whey|noun
