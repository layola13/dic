outshone:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊn/ /ˌaʊtˈʃɒn/
verb
胜过，使黯然失色
surpassed in splendor or excellence; eclipsed.
例句：Her performance outshone all the others. 她的表演胜过所有其他人。
例句：The new model completely outshone the older versions. 新型号完全使旧版本黯然失色。
近义词：excelled, surpassed, eclipsed, outdid, overshadowed
反义词：failed, paled, dimmed
词性：v.
