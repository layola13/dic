disciplinarian:/ˌdɪsəplɪˈnɛəriən/ | n. | 纪律严明的人；执行纪律者 | A person who believes in or practices firm discipline. |  The new teacher was a strict disciplinarian and the students quickly learned to follow the rules. 新来的老师是一个严格的纪律执行者，学生们很快就学会了遵守规则。| synonyms: enforcer, martinet, stickler, taskmaster | antonyms: pushover, softy | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *disciplinaire*, from Latin *disciplina* ("instruction, knowledge, learning, military discipline")
