gift-wrap:/ˈɡɪftˌræp/| v. n.|礼物包装|To wrap a gift in decorative paper and ribbon.|I need to gift-wrap this present for my friend's birthday. 我需要为朋友的生日礼物包装一下。|近义词：wrap, package|反义词:unwrap|词性:动词/名词
gift-wrap:/ˈɡɪftˌræp/| n.|礼物包装纸|Decorative paper used for wrapping gifts.|I bought some beautiful gift-wrap for the holidays. 我为假期买了一些漂亮的包装纸。|近义词：wrapping paper|反义词:None|词性:名词

词源:  Compound word formed from "gift" and "wrap." "Gift" comes from Old Norse "gipt" meaning "gift, dowry." "Wrap" comes from Old English "wræppan" meaning "to cover, clothe, embrace."
