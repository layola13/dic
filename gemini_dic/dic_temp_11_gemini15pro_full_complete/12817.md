assists:/əˈsɪsts/| n.  |助攻；协助；帮助|An action that helps another person to do something or helps something to happen.  |例句：He had 3 goals and 2 assists in the game. 他在这场比赛中进了3个球，还有2次助攻。

例句：The software provides online assists to users.  这款软件为用户提供在线帮助。 |近义词：helps, aids, supports|反义词:hinders, obstructs|noun

assist:/əˈsɪst/| vt. |帮助；协助；援助|To help someone to do something. |例句：He assisted her in finding a job. 他帮她找工作。

例句：The organization assists refugees with food and shelter. 该组织为难民提供食物和住所。|近义词：help, aid, support|反义词:hinder, obstruct|verb
