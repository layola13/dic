cordate:/ˈkɔːrdeɪt/ /ˈkɔːrdət/| adj.|心形的；心脏形的|Shaped like a heart, with a pointed base and rounded lobes.|The leaves of the linden tree are cordate. 菩提树的叶子是心形的。|Synonyms: heart-shaped, cordiform|Antonyms: obcordate, obovate|adjective

cordate:/ˈkɔːrdeɪt/ /ˈkɔːrdət/| n.|心形物|Something shaped like a heart.|The valentine was a simple cordate.  这个情人节礼物是一个简单的心形物。|Synonyms: heart shape|Antonyms: N/A|noun
