complimentingly:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentɪŋli/ | adv. | 赞美地，恭维地 | In a manner that expresses praise or admiration. | She spoke complimentingly about his work, praising his attention to detail. 她赞美了他的工作，称赞他注重细节。| He smiled complimentingly at her new dress. 他对她的新裙子报以赞赏的微笑。 | 近义词：flatteringly, approvingly, admiringly | 反义词：critically, disparagingly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "complimentary" + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Complimentary" comes from the noun "compliment," which ultimately derives from the Italian word "complimento," meaning "fulfillment of requirements, act of civility."
