hydroxyl:/haɪˈdrɒksɪl/ /haɪˈdrɑːksɪl/| n. |羟基; 氢氧基|A hydroxyl is a chemical group consisting of one oxygen atom and one hydrogen atom bonded together.  It is often represented as -OH.  |Water (H₂O) contains a hydroxyl group. 水（H₂O）含有一个羟基。

The hydroxyl radical is highly reactive. 羟基自由基反应活性很高。|近义词：hydroxy group|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
