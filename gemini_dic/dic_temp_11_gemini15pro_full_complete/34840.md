clinical:/ˈklɪnɪkəl/| adj.|临床的；诊所的；冷静客观的；简朴的|Relating to the observation and treatment of actual patients rather than theoretical or laboratory studies.|例句：The clinical trial showed promising results. 临床试验显示出有希望的结果。|近义词：medical, practical, objective, analytical|反义词：theoretical, subjective, emotional|词性:形容词
clinical:/ˈklɪnɪkəl/| n.|临床实习；临床教学|A session of supervised practical medical training.|例句：Medical students are required to complete a certain number of clinicals. 医学生被要求完成一定数量的临床实习。|近义词：practicum, internship, residency|反义词：theory, lecture|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *clinique*, from Greek *klīnikē* (art) meaning "bedside manner," from *klīnē* meaning "bed, couch."
