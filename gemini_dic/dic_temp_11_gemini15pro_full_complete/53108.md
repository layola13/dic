diphthong:/ˈdɪfθɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈdɪpθɔːŋ/ (US)| n. |双元音|A sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves towards another (as in coin, loud, and side).|The word "loud" has a diphthong.|近义词: vowel glide, vowel combination|反义词: monophthong|词性: noun
