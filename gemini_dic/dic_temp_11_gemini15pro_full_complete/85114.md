harder:/ˈhɑːrdər/ (US), /ˈhɑːdə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更难的，更努力的，更坚硬的|Comparative form of "hard."  It means to a greater extent in difficulty, effort, or firmness.|The exam was harder than I expected. 考试比我预期的要难。|近义词：more difficult, tougher, more arduous|反义词:easier, softer, simpler|adjective
