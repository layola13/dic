alveolar:/ælˈviːələr/ /ælˈviːəlɑːr/ | adj. n.| 肺泡的；齿槽的 |Relating to or resembling alveoli (tiny air sacs in the lungs where the exchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide takes place). Also refers to sounds produced by placing the tongue against the alveolar ridge (the ridge behind the upper teeth).| The alveolar sacs are crucial for respiration. 肺泡囊对于呼吸至关重要。 | The "t" sound in "top" is an alveolar consonant. “top”中的“t”音是齿槽辅音。| synonyms: pulmonary (for lung-related meaning), dental (for teeth-related meaning) | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, depends on context) | adjective, noun
