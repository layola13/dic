half-insinuated:/ˌhæf ɪnˈsɪnjuːeɪtɪd/
adjective
半暗示的，隐约流露的
Implied or suggested indirectly and subtly, not fully expressed.  It describes something that is communicated in a way that is only partially clear or direct, often with a suggestive or hinting tone.
Her half-insinuated criticisms stung more than outright accusations. 她半遮半掩的批评比直接的指责更伤人。
He gave a half-insinuated threat about revealing her secret. 他隐约威胁要揭露她的秘密。

近义词:implied, suggested, hinted, intimated
反义词:explicit, stated, direct, overt
词性:adjective
