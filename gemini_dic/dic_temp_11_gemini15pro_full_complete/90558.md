hugging:/ˈhʌɡɪŋ/| n. |拥抱，搂抱|The act of holding someone close to your body with your arms, especially to show affection.|She gave her daughter a long, comforting hug. 她给了她女儿一个长时间的安慰的拥抱。|近义词：embracing, cuddling, squeezing|反义词：repelling, pushing away|词性:名词 (gerund)
hug:/hʌɡ/| v. |拥抱，搂抱|To hold someone close to your body with your arms, especially to show affection.|They hugged each other tightly. 他们紧紧地拥抱在一起。|近义词：embrace, cuddle, squeeze|反义词：repel, push away|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *huggen*, perhaps of Scandinavian origin, related to Norwegian *hugga* (“to comfort, cherish”), or from Low German *huggen* (“to crouch”).
