guardingly:/ˈɡɑːrdɪŋli/
adverb|谨慎地，提防地|in a cautious and reserved manner, especially so as to avoid revealing information or committing oneself.|He spoke guardingly about his future plans. 他对未来的计划说得谨慎。|carefully, cautiously, warily|openly, frankly, freely|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "guarding" + "-ly". "Guarding" comes from the verb "guard," which originates from Old French "garder" meaning "to protect, keep, watch."  The suffix "-ly" is used to form adverbs.
