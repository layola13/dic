grassless:/ˈɡrɑːslɪs/
adj.
无草的；寸草不生的
lacking grass; having no grass
The desert landscape was barren and grassless.  这片沙漠景观荒芜而寸草不生。
The harsh winds had left the hills grassless.  凛冽的风吹得山上寸草不生。
近义词:bare, barren, denuded
反义词:grassy, verdant
词性:形容词
