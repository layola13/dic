misadapted:/ˌmɪsəˈdæptɪd/
adjective
不适应的，适应不良的
not properly adapted; poorly suited to a particular purpose or situation.
The polar bear was misadapted to the warm climate.  这只北极熊不适应温暖的气候。
近义词:maladapted, ill-adapted, unsuited, unfit
反义词:adapted, suited, fit, well-adapted
词性:adjective
词源:mis- (badly) + adapted (adjusted to suit a purpose or situation)
