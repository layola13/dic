minatorily:/ˈdɒmɪnətərɪli/ | adv. | 专横地，盛气凌人地 | In a dominating or overbearing manner; imperiously. | He spoke minatorily, silencing all dissent. 他专横地说着，压制了所有异议。|  He dismissed her minatorily, not even bothering to hear her explanation. 他专横地把她打发走了，甚至懒得听她的解释。 |  Synonym: domineeringly, imperiously, overbearingly, authoritatively | Antonym: submissively, humbly, meekly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Latin *dominātōrius*, from dominātus, past participle of dominārī ‘to rule’, from dominus ‘lord, master’.
