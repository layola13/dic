outblossoming:/ˌaʊtˈblɑsəmɪŋ/
|adj.|盛开的，繁花似锦的|Surpassing in blossoming; blossoming profusely.  Exceeding in beauty or excellence.|The outblossoming garden was a riot of color. 盛开的花园色彩缤纷。|近义词：flourishing, blooming, thriving|反义词：withering, fading, declining|词性:形容词
词源：out- + blossoming. "out-" prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing." "blossoming" from "blossom," meaning "to produce or yield blossoms."
