anthropocentrically: /ˌænθrəpəʊˈsɛntrɪkli/ | adv. | 以人类为中心地 | In a way that regards humankind as the central or most important element of existence, especially as opposed to God or animals. |  Many environmentalists argue that our anthropocentrically biased view of the world is the root cause of ecological destruction.  许多环保主义者认为，我们以人类为中心的偏见是生态破坏的根本原因。 | Example 2:  She anthropocentrically assumed that the animals were there solely for human benefit. 她以人类为中心的假设认为，这些动物的存在完全是为了人类的利益。 | 近义词: human-centered, egocentrically | 反义词: biocentrically, ecocentrically | 词性: 副词

词源:
anthropo- (Greek) = human
centric (Latin) = center
-ally (suffix) = in a ... manner
