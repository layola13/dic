marginals:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəlz/ | n. | 边际值，边缘群体，边缘席位 |Items written in the margins of a book; people existing at the margins of society. | The marginals in the election are likely to determine the outcome. 选举中边缘席位可能会决定结果。 | synonyms: fringes, periphery, insignificant items, minor details | antonyms: core, center, mainstream, important elements | noun

marginals:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəlz/ | adj. | 边缘的，微不足道的 |Relating to or situated at the edge or margin of something; barely profitable or worthwhile. | The marginal cost of producing one more unit is very low. 生产再多一个单位的边际成本很低。 | synonyms: peripheral, borderline, insignificant, minor | antonyms: central, core, significant, major | adjective
