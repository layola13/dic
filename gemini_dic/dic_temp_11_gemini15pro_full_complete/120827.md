misauthorized:/ˌmɪsˈɔːθəraɪzd/
adjective
未经授权的，错误授权的
Having been given authorization mistakenly or improperly; authorized by the wrong person or for the wrong purpose.
The misauthorized transaction resulted in a significant loss for the company. 这笔未经授权的交易给公司造成了重大损失。
The clerk misauthorized the return and the customer received a full refund even though the item was damaged.  店员错误地授权了退货，即使商品已损坏，顾客也获得了全额退款。

近义词:unauthorized, wrongly authorized
反义词:authorized, approved
词性:adjective
