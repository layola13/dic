gyrogonite:/ˈdʒaɪrəˌɡoʊnaɪt/| n. |轮藻化石，石果|A small, calcareous, spiral fossil fruit of certain charophyte algae, often found in freshwater deposits.|Gyrogonites are useful for dating and correlating sedimentary rocks.|近义词: charophyte fossil, stonewort fossil|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology: From Greek *gyros* "ring, circle" + *gonos* "offspring, seed".
