chalybean: /kəˈlɪbiən/ | adj. | 钢铁般的；坚硬的；（古）指与钢铁有关的 | Made of or resembling steel; (archaic) relating to steel. | The chalybean warrior wore armor that gleamed in the sunlight.  钢铁般的战士穿着在阳光下闪闪发光的盔甲。 | The ancient blacksmith possessed chalybean strength. 古代铁匠拥有钢铁般的力量。| 近义词: steely, iron, hard, firm | 反义词: soft, weak, pliable | 词性: 形容词

chalybeate: /kəˈlɪbiət/ | n. | 铁质矿泉水；含铁的 | A mineral spring containing iron salts; containing iron. |  The doctor recommended drinking chalybeate water for her anemia. 医生建议她喝铁质矿泉水来治疗贫血。 | The spa was famous for its chalybeate springs.  这个温泉以其含铁的泉水而闻名。 | 近义词: ferruginous | 反义词:  (无明确反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *chalybeus, from Greek khalyps (χάλυψ), "steel," related to Khalybēs (Χάλυβες), an ancient people of the Black Sea region famed for their ironworking.  源自拉丁语 *chalybeus，来自希腊语 khalyps (χάλυψ), "钢铁," 与 Khalybēs (Χάλυβες) 有关， Khalybēs 是黑海地区的一个古代民族，以其铁制品而闻名。
