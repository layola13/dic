decrumbed:/diːˈkrʌmd/
|v. |去除面包屑|To remove breadcrumbs from something, typically after frying or baking.|The chef decrumbed the fish before serving it.  厨师在上菜前去除了鱼上的面包屑。|例句：She carefully decrumbed the countertop after making the sandwiches. 她做完三明治后仔细地清除了台面上的面包屑。|近义词：clean, clear, remove|反义词:cover, coat|词性:动词

词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning "remove") + "crumb" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  Derived from the noun "crumb" which has origins in Old English "cruma."
