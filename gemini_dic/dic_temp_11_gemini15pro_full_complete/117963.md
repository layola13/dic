memetic:/mɪˈmɛtɪk/ /miːˈmetɪk/
adjective
模因的; 文化基因的
Relating to memes, which are ideas, beliefs, or practices that spread from person to person within a culture.
The memetic spread of the viral video was astonishing. 这段病毒视频的模因传播速度惊人。
Near Synonyms:viral, contagious, spreading
Antonyms:localized, isolated, contained
Word Origin: From "meme," coined by Richard Dawkins, from Greek "mimema," meaning "something imitated."
