discrepant:/dɪˈskrepənt/
adjective
不同的，不一致的，有差异的
Containing or showing a difference; disagreeing.
The two reports offered discrepant accounts of the accident.  两份报告对事故的描述不一致。
近义词:inconsistent, conflicting, differing, divergent, varying
反义词:consistent, concordant, similar, identical
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *discrepāns*, present participle of *discrepāre* (“to sound differently, disagree”), from *dis-* + *crepāre* (“to rattle, creak, crack”).
