epileptic:/ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk/ | adj.  n. |癫痫的；患癫痫症的；癫痫患者 |Relating to or affected by epilepsy, a neurological disorder characterized by recurrent seizures.  A person who has epilepsy. |例句：He has epileptic seizures. 他有癫痫发作。|近义词：convulsive, seizure-prone |反义词: healthy, non-epileptic |词性:形容词, 名词

epileptical:/ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk(ə)l/ | adj. |癫痫的；患癫痫症的 |Relating to or characteristic of epilepsy. |例句：Epileptical seizures can be frightening to witness. 目睹癫痫发作可能会很可怕。|近义词：epileptic, convulsive |反义词: healthy, non-epileptic |词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *épileptique*, from Late Latin *epilepticus*, from Greek *ἐπιληπτικός (epilēptikos)*, from *ἐπιλαμβάνειν (epilambanein, “to seize, attack”)*.
