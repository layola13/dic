equanimity:/ˌekwəˈnɪməti/ /ˌiːkwəˈnɪməti/| n. | 平静；镇定；沉着 | Mental calmness, composure, and evenness of temper, especially in a difficult situation. |例句：She accepted both the good and the bad with equanimity. 她平静地接受了好与坏。|例句：He maintained his equanimity despite the chaos around him. 尽管周围一片混乱，他仍然保持镇定。|近义词：composure, calmness, tranquility, serenity, poise |反义词：anxiety, agitation, panic, distress, hysteria |词性:noun

equanimous:/ˌekwəˈnɪməs/ /ˌiːkwəˈnɪməs/| adj. | 平静的；镇定的；沉着的 | Having or showing mental calmness, composure, and evenness of temper, especially in a difficult situation. |例句：He remained equanimous in the face of danger. 面对危险，他保持镇定。|例句：Her equanimous response to the crisis impressed everyone.  她在危机面前的镇定反应给每个人都留下了深刻印象。|近义词：calm, composed, tranquil, serene, poised |反义词：anxious, agitated, panicked, distressed, hysterical|词性:adjective
