extuberancy:/ɪɡˈzjuːbərənsi/| n. |热情洋溢；兴高采烈；活力充沛|The state of being exuberant; overflowing abundance; lavishness.|Her exuberance and enthusiasm were infectious. 她的热情和活力很有感染力。|Synonyms: ebullience, buoyancy, elation, exhilaration|Antonyms: apathy, dullness, gloom, listlessness|noun


extuberant:/ɪɡˈzjuːbərənt/| adj. |热情洋溢的；兴高采烈的；充满活力的|Filled with or characterized by a lively energy and excitement.|The child was exuberant when he opened his birthday present.  当孩子打开生日礼物时，他欣喜若狂。|Synonyms: lively, enthusiastic, energetic, high-spirited|Antonyms: subdued, restrained, quiet, listless|adjective
