ETYMOLOGY->
The origin of "fuddy-duddy" is uncertain, but it's thought to be imitative, possibly related to "fud" (an old word for backside).  "Fuddy" likely arose as a shortened form of "fuddy-duddy".  The reduplication in "fuddy-duddy" emphasizes the sense of fussiness and old-fashionedness.
