outbleeding: /aʊtˈbliːdɪŋ/
|n.|外出血；体外出血|The process of bleeding, especially profusely, from a wound or orifice.|The soldier suffered severe outbleeding from a gunshot wound.  这名士兵枪伤导致严重外出血。|近义词: hemorrhage, bleeding|反义词: clotting, hemostasis|词性: 名词

Etymology:
"out-" (Old English *ūt*, from Proto-Germanic *ūt-) + "bleeding" (Middle English *bleden*, from Old English *blēdan*)
