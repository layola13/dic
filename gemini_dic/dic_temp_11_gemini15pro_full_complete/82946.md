gunarchy:/ˈɡʌnɑːrki/
n.
枪支统治;枪支泛滥的社会;枪权至上
A social system or state of affairs where gun ownership and use are widespread and largely unregulated, often leading to violence and a sense of lawlessness.
The escalating gun violence in some cities suggests a state of gunarchy. 一些城市不断升级的枪支暴力暗示着枪支统治的状态。
Gunarchy is a growing concern in many countries. 枪支泛滥是许多国家日益关注的问题。
synonyms: gun culture, gun proliferation, gun violence, lawlessness, anarchy
antonyms: gun control, peace, order, security, stability
