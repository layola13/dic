calcography:/kælˈkɒɡrəfi/ | n. |  铜版雕刻术；铜版画 | The art of engraving on copper or other metal plates, from which prints are made.  |  The museum has a fine collection of 18th-century calcography.  这家博物馆收藏了精美的 18 世纪铜版画。 | 近义词: engraving, etching | 反义词:  None (No direct opposite for a specific artistic technique) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *calcographie*, from Italian *calcografia*, from Ancient Greek *χαλκός* (*khalkós* - copper) +  *-γραφία* (*-graphía* - writing, drawing).
