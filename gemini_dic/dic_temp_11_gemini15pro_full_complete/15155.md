backhoe: /ˈbækˌhoʊ/ | n. |反铲挖掘机；挖掘装载机|A piece of excavating equipment consisting of a digging bucket on the end of a jointed arm mounted on the back of a tractor.  It is typically used for digging trenches, foundations, and other earthmoving tasks.| The backhoe was used to dig the foundation for the new house.  反铲挖掘机被用来挖掘新房子的地基。|Synonyms: excavator, digger |Antonyms: N/A | Noun

backhoe: /ˈbækˌhoʊ/ | v. |用反铲挖掘机挖掘|To excavate or dig using a backhoe.| They backhoed a trench for the new water line.  他们用反铲挖掘机挖了一条沟渠来铺设新的水管。|Synonyms: excavate, dig |Antonyms: fill, bury | Verb
