blastoid:/ˈblæstɔɪd/
n.|类海蕾，海蕾类动物|An extinct echinoderm of the class Blastoidea, related to crinoids and cystoids, having a stalk, a budlike body, and five ambulacra.|The museum's collection includes several well-preserved blastoids from the Paleozoic era.  博物馆的收藏包括几个保存完好的来自古生代的海蕾类动物。|Synonyms: blastoid echinoderm|Antonyms: None|noun

blastoid:/ˈblæstɔɪd/
adj.|类海蕾的|Resembling or characteristic of a blastoid.|The blastoid fossil was easily identifiable due to its distinctive five-fold symmetry. 这块类海蕾化石很容易辨认，因为它具有独特的五重对称性。|Synonyms: blastoidian|Antonyms: None|adjective
