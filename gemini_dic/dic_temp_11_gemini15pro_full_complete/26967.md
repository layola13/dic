calcifying:/ˈkælsɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
钙化的，硬化的
Becoming hardened by the depositing of calcium salts.
例句：The calcifying arteries reduced blood flow.  钙化的动脉减少了血液流动。

例句：The doctor discovered calcifying tissue in the patient's lungs. 医生在病人的肺部发现了钙化组织。

近义词：hardening, ossifying
反义词: softening, decalcifying
词性: adjective
