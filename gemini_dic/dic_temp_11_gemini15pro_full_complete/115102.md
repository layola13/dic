manganiferous:/ˌmæŋɡəˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
含锰的；锰质的
Containing or yielding manganese.
The soil in this region is manganiferous.  这个地区的土壤含锰。
Near the volcano, they found manganiferous rocks. 在火山附近，他们发现了含锰的岩石。
Synonyms: manganese-bearing, manganese-rich
Antonyms: manganese-deficient, manganese-poor
词性: 形容词
