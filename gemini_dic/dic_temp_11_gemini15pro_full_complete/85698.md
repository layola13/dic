havenward:/ˈheɪvənwərd/| adv. |朝着避难所的方向，向天堂的方向| Towards a haven; towards a place of safety or refuge, often with spiritual connotations of heaven.|例句：She looked havenward, seeking solace in prayer. 她仰望天堂，在祈祷中寻求慰藉。|例句：The ship sailed havenward, seeking shelter from the storm.  这艘船驶向避风港，寻求躲避暴风雨的庇护。|近义词：heavenward, homeward, toward safety|反义词: away, outward|词性:副词
