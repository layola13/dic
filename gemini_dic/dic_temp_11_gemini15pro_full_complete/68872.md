fastback:/ˈfæstˌbæk/| n. adj.|快背式; 有快背式车顶的|A car with a roof that slopes down to the rear at a single, continuous curve.|例句：He drove a classic fastback sports car. 他开着一辆经典的快背式跑车。

例句：The fastback design gives the car a sporty look. 快背式设计赋予了这款车运动感的外观。|近义词：sports car, coupe|反义词:sedan, hatchback|词性:名词, 形容词
