epitaphing: /ˈɛpɪˌtæfɪŋ/
verb
题写墓志铭; 为...撰写墓志铭
The act of writing or carving an epitaph.
He was epitaphing his grandmother's tombstone. 他正在为他祖母的墓碑题写墓志铭。
She spent hours epitaphing the monument. 她花了几个小时为纪念碑题写碑文。
