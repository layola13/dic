irascibility:/ɪˌræsɪˈbɪləti/
noun
易怒，暴躁
the quality of being easily made angry
His irascibility was well known, so people avoided provoking him. 他脾气暴躁是出了名的，所以人们都避免惹他生气。

近义词:irritability, touchiness, testiness, peevishness, cantankerousness
反义词:patience, forbearance, tolerance, good humor, calmness
词性:noun
