enjoyment:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪmənt/ | n. | 享受，乐趣 |The state of taking pleasure in something. | She found great enjoyment in reading. 她从阅读中获得了极大的乐趣。|  近义词: pleasure, delight, satisfaction, gratification | 反义词: displeasure, dissatisfaction, misery | 词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French enjoiement, from enjoier ‘give joy to’, based on Latin gaudere ‘rejoice’.
