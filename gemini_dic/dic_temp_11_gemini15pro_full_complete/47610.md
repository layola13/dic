debauchery:/dɪˈbɔːtʃəri/
n.
放荡，纵情酒色
excessive indulgence in pleasure or sensuality
He lived a life of debauchery. 他过着放荡的生活。

近义词: dissipation, profligacy, licentiousness, corruption, depravity
反义词: abstinence, moderation, restraint, virtue, purity
词性: 名词
