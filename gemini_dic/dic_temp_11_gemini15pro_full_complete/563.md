abortionists: /əˈbɔːrʃənɪsts/
n.
堕胎者，施行堕胎手术的人
A person who performs abortions, especially illegally or unethically.
例句：
The state legislature passed a law requiring abortionists to have admitting privileges at a local hospital.
州立法机关通过了一项法律，要求堕胎者在当地医院拥有住院特权。

Protesters gathered outside the clinic, shouting at the abortionists.
抗议者聚集在诊所外，对着堕胎者大喊。

近义词：abortion provider, abortion doctor
反义词: pro-lifer (反堕胎人士)
词性: 名词

词源: abortion (堕胎) + -ist (表示人)
