autoimmunity:/ˌɔːtəʊɪˈmjuːnɪti/ | n. |自身免疫性|A condition in which the body's immune system mistakenly attacks its own healthy tissues and organs.|  Autoimmunity is the underlying cause of many diseases, including rheumatoid arthritis. 自身免疫性是许多疾病的根本原因，包括类风湿性关节炎。|synonyms:  self-immunity| antonyms:  tolerance, immunodeficiency| noun
