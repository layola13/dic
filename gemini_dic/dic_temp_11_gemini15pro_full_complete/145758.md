overcunning:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌnɪŋ/ | adj. | 过于狡猾的，诡计多端的 | Excessively cunning or clever; too clever for one's own good.  Characterized by excessive or deceptive cleverness. | He was overcunning in his dealings and ended up alienating everyone. 他在交易中过于狡猾，最终疏远了所有人。| The overcunning fox was eventually caught in his own trap. 那只过于狡猾的狐狸最终落入了自己的陷阱。 | 近义词: crafty, wily, sly, devious, scheming | 反义词: naive, ingenuous, artless, guileless | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Middle English *overconnyng, from Old English *ofercunnig, equivalent to over- + cunning.
