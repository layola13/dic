ghettoization:/ˌɡɛtəʊɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |隔离；少数民族聚居区化；贫民区化 | The process of confining a particular group of people to a certain area, often with negative social and economic consequences.  This can be enforced by law or by societal pressures. | The ghettoization of certain neighborhoods has led to increased poverty and crime.  某些街区的贫民窟化导致了贫困和犯罪的增加。 | segregation, isolation, marginalization | integration, inclusion | noun

ghettoize:/ˈɡɛtəʊaɪz/ | vt. |使隔离；使集中居住（尤指在贫民区）| To restrict or confine a particular group of people to a certain area, often implying negative social and economic consequences. |  Policies that ghettoize minority groups perpetuate inequality. 使少数群体集中居住的政策会使不平等永久化。| segregate, isolate, marginalize | integrate, include | verb
