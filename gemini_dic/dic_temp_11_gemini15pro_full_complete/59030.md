dwarfish: /ˈdwɔːrfɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈdwɔːrfɪʃ/ (US) | adj. | 矮小的，侏儒的 |  Like a dwarf; small in stature; stunted in growth.  Resembling or characteristic of a dwarf, especially in being abnormally small. |  The bonsai tree, with its dwarfish proportions, was a perfect miniature of its larger cousin. 盆景树，以其矮小的比例，是其较大表亲的完美缩影。|  The child's dwarfish stature was a result of a rare genetic condition.  这个孩子矮小的身材是一种罕见遗传病的结果。 | Synonyms: diminutive, miniature, stunted, undersized, pygmy | Antonyms: giant, enormous, huge, gigantic, colossal | word type: adjective
