lardlike:/ˈlɑːrdlaɪk/
adjective
像猪油的;肥胖的
Resembling lard; fat.
His face was lardlike and pale. 他的脸像猪油一样，苍白无力。
近义词：fatty, greasy, obese, flabby
反义词：lean, thin, slender

词性: adjective
