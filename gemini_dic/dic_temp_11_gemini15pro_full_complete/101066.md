inventional:/ɪnˈvɛnʃənəl/| adj.|发明的；有发明才能的；善于发明的|Of or relating to invention; characterized by invention.  Pertaining to or showing inventive talent. |  He has a very inventional mind and is always coming up with new ideas. 他有着非常强的发明才能，总是能想出新点子。|近义词：inventive, ingenious, creative|反义词：uninventive, uncreative|词性:形容词
词源: Late 16th century; earliest use found in Edmund Spenser (c1552–1599), poet. From invention + -al.
