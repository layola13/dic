nanosecond:/ˈnænoʊˌsɛkənd/ /ˈnænəʊˌsɛkənd/| n.|纳秒；十亿分之一秒|A nanosecond is one billionth of a second.  It's an extremely short period of time.|Light travels about one foot in a nanosecond. 光在一纳秒内传播约一英尺。

The computer can perform calculations in nanoseconds. 这台计算机可以在纳秒内进行计算。|近义词:millimicrosecond|反义词:(No direct opposite, but could use larger units of time like "second", "minute", "hour")|词性:noun
