limitarian:/lɪmɪˈtɛəriən/
n.
限制性饮食者；力求减少动物制品消费的人
A person who consumes animal products, but in significantly reduced quantities, typically for ethical or environmental reasons.
Example:  As a limitarian, she tries to eat mostly plant-based meals, only occasionally indulging in meat or dairy. 作为一名限制性饮食者，她尽量以植物性食物为主，偶尔才会吃肉或奶制品。

Example: He became a limitarian after learning about the environmental impact of animal agriculture. 在了解了畜牧业对环境的影响后，他成了一名限制性饮食者。

近义词:reducetarian, flexitarian
反义词:carnivore

词性:noun
