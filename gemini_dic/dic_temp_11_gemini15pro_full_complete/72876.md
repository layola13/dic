例句：The plant is remarkably folliful for its size.  这株植物就其大小而言，叶子茂盛得出奇。

近义词：leafy, lush, verdant|反义词:bare, leafless|词性:形容词

词源:  From Latin *folli- (related to *folium "leaf") + -ful (suffix meaning "full of").
