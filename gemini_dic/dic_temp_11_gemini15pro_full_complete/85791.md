haythorn:/ˈheɪθɔːrn/| n. |山楂; 山楂树|A thorny shrub or small tree of the rose family, with white, pink, or red blossoms and small dark red fruits (haws).|The haythorn blossomed in May. 五月里山楂树开花了。|Synonym: hawthorn, whitethorn|Antonym: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑一些非同科的树木，例如pine 松树)|noun
