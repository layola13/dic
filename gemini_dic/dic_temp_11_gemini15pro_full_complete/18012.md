begging:/ˈbeɡɪŋ/| n. v.|乞讨，恳求|The act of asking for something (especially money or food) in a humble way.  The act of earnestly requesting something.|例句：She was begging him to stay. 她恳求他留下。|近义词：pleading, imploring, entreating, supplicating|反义词：demanding, commanding|词性: noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English beggen, from Old North French begard (“religious beggar”), from Frankish *beggāri (“beggar”).
