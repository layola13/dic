honestest:/ˈɒnɪstɪst/ | adj. | 最诚实的 |  Although grammatically correct as the superlative of "honest", "honestest" is rarely used in modern English.  "Most honest" is the preferred form. |  He was the honestest man I ever knew. (archaic) 他是我认识的最诚实的人（古语）。|  Synonyms: most honest, truthful, sincere, upright | Antonyms: most dishonest, deceitful, lying, corrupt | adjective
