chesterlite: /ˈtʃɛstərˌlaɪt/
n.
切斯特岩
A variety of microcline feldspar, typically white or pale in color, often found in pegmatites.
Chesterlite is a mineral found in pegmatites. 切斯特岩是一种在伟晶岩中发现的矿物。
This specimen of chesterlite exhibits a beautiful pearly luster. 这块切斯特岩标本展现出美丽的珍珠光泽。
Synonyms: microcline, feldspar
Antonyms: N/A
