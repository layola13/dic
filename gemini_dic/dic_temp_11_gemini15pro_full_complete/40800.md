convoker:/kənˈvoʊkər/| n.|召集人，召集者|A person or thing that convokes, especially a meeting or assembly.  The convoker of the meeting announced the agenda. 会议的召集人宣布了议程。|近义词：organizer, convener, summoner|反义词: attendee, participant|noun
