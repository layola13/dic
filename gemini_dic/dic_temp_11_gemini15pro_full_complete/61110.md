elodian:/ɪˈloʊdiən/| n. |伊乐藻；伊乐藻属植物|A submerged aquatic plant (Elodea canadensis) with translucent leaves, native to North America but naturalized elsewhere, often a troublesome weed.|The elodian choked the pond, making it difficult for fish to swim. 伊乐藻阻塞了池塘，使鱼难以游动。|近义词：waterweed, pondweed|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps terrestrial plant)|Noun
