cutoff:/ˈkʌtˌɔːf/ (US), /ˈkʌtɒf/ (UK)| n. adj.|截止; 切断; 分界线|A point or limit beyond which something is no longer applicable or possible.|The application cutoff date is October 1st. 申请截止日期是10月1日。| deadline, limit | continuation |noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb phrase "cut off," where "cut" means to sever or interrupt and "off" indicates separation or disconnection.
