biologists:/baɪˈɒlədʒɪsts/ /baɪˈɑːlədʒɪsts/| n.|生物学家|A scientist who studies living organisms.|Biologists are studying the effects of climate change on marine life. 生物学家正在研究气候变化对海洋生物的影响。|Biologists discovered a new species of frog in the Amazon rainforest.  生物学家在亚马逊雨林中发现了一种新的青蛙。|scientists, researchers, naturalists| - |noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From biology + -ist.  Biology comes from Greek bios "life" + -logy "study of".
