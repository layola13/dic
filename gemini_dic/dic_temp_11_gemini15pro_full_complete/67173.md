expostulatory:/ɪkˈspɒstjʊlətəri/ /ɪkˈspɑːstʃʊlətɔːri/
adjective
劝诫的，告诫的， remonstrative, containing or expressing expostulation
Containing strong remonstrance or urging.
Her expostulatory tone made it clear she wasn't happy with the decision.  她责备的语气表明她对这个决定很不满意。
近义词: remonstrative, reproachful, admonitory, rebuking
反义词: approving, commending, laudatory, praising
词性: adjective
