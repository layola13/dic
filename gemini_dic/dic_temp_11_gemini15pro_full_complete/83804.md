hairsplitter:/ˈhɛərˌsplɪtər/| n.|吹毛求疵的人；钻牛角尖的人|Someone who makes unnecessarily fine distinctions; a person who argues about insignificant details.|He's such a hairsplitter, always arguing about the smallest things. 他真是个吹毛求疵的人，总是为鸡毛蒜皮的小事争论不休。|Synonyms: quibbler, nitpicker, pedant|Antonyms: generalist, pragmatist|noun
