febrifugal:/ˈfebrɪfjuːɡəl/
adjective
解热的；退热的
Reducing fever; antipyretic.
Aspirin is a common febrifugal medicine. 阿司匹林是一种常见的退烧药。

近义词:antipyretic, antifebrile
反义词:pyrogenic

词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *febrifugus*, from *febris* ("fever") + *fugare* ("to put to flight").  Related to *febrifuge*.
