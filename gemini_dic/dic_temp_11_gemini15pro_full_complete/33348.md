chummiest:/ˈtʃʌmiɪst/
adjective
最亲密的，最友好的
Superlative form of chummy: most chummy.  Meaning having a close and friendly relationship.
They were the chummiest couple I'd ever met. 他们是我见过最亲密的一对。
closest, friendliest, thickest
distant, aloof, unfriendly
