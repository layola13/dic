cryptographists:/krɪpˈtɒɡrəfɪsts/ | n. |密码学家；密码研究者|A person who studies or practices cryptography, the art and science of secure communication in the presence of adversaries.|Cryptographists are working to develop more secure encryption algorithms. 密码学家正在努力开发更安全的加密算法。|Synonyms: codebreakers, cipher experts, security specialists|Antonyms: None|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From cryptography + -ist.  Cryptography comes from Greek kryptós "hidden" + -graphia "writing."
