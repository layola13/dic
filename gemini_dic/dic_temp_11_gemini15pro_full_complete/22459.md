bookless:/ˈbʊklɪs/
adjective
无书的；没有书的
Without books; lacking books.
The family was so poor they were practically bookless.  这个家庭很穷，几乎没有书。
He grew up in a bookless household. 他在一个没有书的家庭中长大。
synonyms:  unlettered, illiterate, uneducated
antonyms:  bookish, literate, learned
词性: 形容词
