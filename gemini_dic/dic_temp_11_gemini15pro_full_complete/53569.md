disbelievers:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːvərz/
n.
不信者，怀疑者
People who do not believe something, especially a religious doctrine.
Many disbelievers questioned the claims of the new prophet. 许多不信者质疑这位新先知的断言。
synonyms: skeptics, doubters, cynics, unbelievers
antonyms: believers, followers, adherents
词性: 名词

disbeliever:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːvər/
n.
不信者，怀疑者
A person who does not believe something, especially a religious doctrine.
The disbeliever scoffed at the idea of miracles.  这个不信者嘲笑奇迹的想法。
synonyms: skeptic, doubter, cynic, unbeliever
antonyms: believer, follower, adherent
词性: 名词
