flatbed:/ˈflætˌbed/ (US) /ˈflætbed/ (UK)| n. adj.|平板卡车；平板拖车；平板式的|A truck or trailer with a flat platform for carrying goods, without sides or a roof.  A flatbed scanner or printer has a flat surface on which you put the paper or document.|The furniture was delivered on a flatbed truck. 家具是用平板卡车运来的。|Synonyms: platform truck, low-loader, trailer |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps enclosed truck)|noun, adjective

flatbed:/ˈflætˌbed/ (US) /ˈflætbed/ (UK)| adj.|平板式的|Having a flat surface, especially for carrying goods or scanning/printing.|He bought a flatbed scanner for his home office. 他为他的家庭办公室买了一台平板扫描仪。|Synonyms: flat, level, planar|Antonyms:  inclined, sloped|adjective
