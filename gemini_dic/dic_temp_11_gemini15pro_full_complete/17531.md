beatable:/ˈbiːtəbl/| adj.|可击败的，可战胜的|Able to be defeated or overcome.|例句：The champion is beatable, but it will be a tough match. 这位冠军是可以被打败的，但这将会是一场艰难的比赛。|近义词：defeatable, conquerable, vulnerable|反义词：unbeatable, invincible|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "beat" (verb) + "-able" (suffix). The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "fit to be," or "worthy of being." "Beat" comes from Old English *bēatan.
