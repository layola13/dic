misattributing:/ˌmɪsəˈtrɪbjuːtɪŋ/
verb
错误地归因于；张冠李戴
incorrectly attributing something to someone or something else
He misattributed the quote to Shakespeare, when it was actually by Marlowe. 他把这句话错误地归因于莎士比亚，实际上是马洛写的。
misascribe, miscredit
correctly attribute, properly credit
v.
