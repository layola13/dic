homeland:/ˈhoʊmlænd/ (US) /ˈhəʊmlænd/ (UK)| n.|祖国；故乡|One's native land; the country where one was born or where one's ancestors lived.|She returned to her homeland after many years abroad. 她多年在国外后回到了祖国。|Synonyms: native land, birthplace, motherland, fatherland|Antonyms: foreign land, abroad|noun

homeland:/ˈhoʊmlænd/ (US) /ˈhəʊmlænd/ (UK)| n.|故土；家乡|The place where one's roots are; a place where one feels a sense of belonging.|The valley was his homeland, and he longed to return.  这个山谷是他的故乡，他渴望回到那里。|Synonyms: home, native land, birthplace|Antonyms: foreign land, abroad|noun
