interinstitutionally:/ˌɪntərˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənəli/
adverb
机构间地
in a way that involves two or more institutions
The project is being managed interinstitutionally.  这个项目是由多个机构共同管理的。
The universities collaborate interinstitutionally on research projects.  这些大学在研究项目上进行机构间的合作。
近义词: collaboratively, jointly, cooperatively
反义词: independently, separately
词性: adverb

词源:
inter- (between) + institution + -al + -ly
