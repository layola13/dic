madreporaria:/ˌmædrɪpəˈrɛəriə/ | n. |  madreporarian, stony corals, reef-building corals |A large order of stony corals characterized by having a porous skeleton.| Madreporaria are the primary builders of coral reefs.  石珊瑚是珊瑚礁的主要建造者。| Synonyms: Scleractinia, stony corals | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps soft corals or non-reef building corals could be considered contrasting groups) | Noun


madreporite:/ˌmædrɪpəˈraɪt/ | n. | madreporite |The porous plate on the aboral surface of an echinoderm, through which water enters the water-vascular system.| The madreporite is crucial for the echinoderm's hydraulic system.  筛板对于棘皮动物的液压系统至关重要。| Synonyms: sieve plate | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | Noun
