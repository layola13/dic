corpuscularity:/kɔːrˌpʌskjʊˈlærɪti/
n.
微粒性，粒子性
The corpuscularity of light was a major topic of debate in the 17th century.  17世纪，光的微粒性是一个主要的争论话题。
The theory emphasizes the corpuscularity of matter. 该理论强调物质的粒子性。
Synonyms: granularity, particularity
Antonyms: continuity, wholeness
noun
