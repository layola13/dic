guesstimation:/ˌɡɛstɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 估算，估计 | An estimate based on a combination of guesswork and calculation. |  I made a guesstimation of how many people would attend the party. 我粗略估计了一下会有多少人参加聚会。|  estimate, approximation, rough calculation | precise calculation, accurate measurement | noun

guesstimate:/ˈɡɛstɪmeɪt/ | v. | 估算，估计 | To form an estimate based on guesswork and calculation. | We can only guesstimate the total cost at this stage. 在这个阶段，我们只能粗略估计总成本。| estimate, approximate,  surmise | calculate precisely, measure accurately | verb


Etymology:
A blend of *guess* and *estimation*.  First recorded in the mid-20th century.
