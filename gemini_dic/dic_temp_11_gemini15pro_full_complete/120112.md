mimical:/ˈmɪmɪk(ə)l/
adjective
模仿的;滑稽的
Imitative; characterized by or exhibiting mimicry.
Her mimical performance of the teacher was hilarious. 她模仿老师的表演滑稽极了。
近义词: imitative, mimetic, apish, mocking
反义词: original, genuine, authentic
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *mimicus, from Latin mimus (“mime, mimic”) + -icus.
