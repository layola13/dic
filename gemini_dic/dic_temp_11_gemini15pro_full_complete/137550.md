nontransgressive:/ˌnɑːntrænsˈɡrɛsɪv/
adjective
非越界的；不违反的；不逾矩的
Not exceeding or violating accepted limits or boundaries; not transgressive.
Her art is surprisingly nontransgressive, dealing with traditional themes in a conventional style. 她的艺术令人惊讶地不越界，以传统的方式处理传统主题。

The novel's plot is remarkably nontransgressive, adhering to familiar tropes and avoiding controversial subjects. 这部小说的情节非常不越界，坚持熟悉的比喻，避免有争议的话题。

synonym: conventional, orthodox, traditional, conformist, compliant
antonym: transgressive, rebellious, radical, unconventional, defiant
词性: 形容词
