essentiate:/ɪˈsɛnʃɪeɪt/ | n. | 本质; 要素 | The essential element or characteristic. | The essentiate of the argument was a plea for greater understanding. 这场争论的本质是呼吁加深理解。 | synonyms: essence, core, gist, heart | antonyms: periphery, exterior, surface | noun
