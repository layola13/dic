interrepulsions:/ˌɪntərɪˈpʌlʃənz/| n. |相互排斥，互相排斥|The interrepulsions between the electrons keep them apart.|电子之间的相互排斥使它们保持分离。|近义词：repulsions, mutual repulsions|反义词:attractions|词性:名词

Etymology:
From inter- (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + repulsion (from Latin repulsio, meaning "a pushing back").
