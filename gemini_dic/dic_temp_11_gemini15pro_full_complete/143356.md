outfaced: /ˌaʊtˈfeɪst/
verb
战胜；使无地自容；厚着脸皮对待
to stare down or intimidate someone until they give in
He outfaced his opponent in the staring contest. 他在瞪眼比赛中战胜了对手。
stare down, intimidate, cow
yield, submit, give in
