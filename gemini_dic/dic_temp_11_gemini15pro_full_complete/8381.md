anti-reduction:/ˌænti rɪˈdʌkʃən/ | adj. | 反还原论的，反对还原论的 | Opposing or resisting reductionism, the philosophical position that complex systems can be fully understood by breaking them down into their simpler components. |  Anti-reductionist approaches to consciousness emphasize the emergent properties of the whole system. 反还原论的意识研究方法强调整个系统的涌现性。

Anti-reductionist theories argue that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts. 反还原论理论认为整体大于部分之和。 | 近义词: holistic, emergent | 反义词: reductionist | 词性: adjective
