ketching:/ˈkɛtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 吸引人的；迷人的；有感染力的 | Catching describes something that is easily spread from one person to another, typically referring to illnesses or emotions. | Her enthusiasm was catching. 她的热情很有感染力。|  The flu is a catching illness. 流感是一种传染性疾病。 | 近义词: infectious, contagious, captivating | 反义词: repellent, off-putting | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "catch,"  meaning to seize or take hold of. The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a present participle, which can function as an adjective.  Ultimately from Old North French cachier.
