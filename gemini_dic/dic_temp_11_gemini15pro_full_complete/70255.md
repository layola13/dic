fictionary:/ˈfɪkʃəˌnɛri/ | n. | 虚构词典，虚构词汇集 | A collection of invented or fictional words, often associated with a particular fictional universe or author.  It's not a standard English word but a neologism. |  The author included a fictionary at the back of the book to explain the made-up language of her fantasy world.  作者在书的末尾附上了一本虚构词典，用来解释她幻想世界中自造的语言。 | 近义词: glossary, lexicon (for real words), vocabulary | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Likely a portmanteau of "fiction" + "dictionary". "Fiction" comes from the Latin "fictio," meaning "a shaping, a feigning." "Dictionary" originates from the Medieval Latin "dictionarium," meaning "a collection of words and phrases."
