laconize:/ˈlækənaɪz/| v.|简洁地说，简而言之|to express oneself concisely; to speak in a brief or terse manner|She laconized her lengthy explanation into a single sentence. 她把冗长的解释简化为一句话。|He laconized his response to a simple "yes." 他言简意赅地用一个“是”字回答。|近义词：condense, abbreviate, summarize|反义词:elaborate, expatiate, expand|词性:动词
