fruitier:/ˈfruːtiər/| adj.|更水果味的；果香更浓郁的|Having a stronger or more noticeable taste or smell of fruit.  More like fruit than something else.|例句：This wine is fruitier than the last one we tried. 这款酒比我们上次尝的那款果味更浓。

例句：The new variety of strawberry is fruitier and sweeter.  这个新品种的草莓果味更浓郁，也更甜。|近义词：more flavorful, richer, sweeter, tangier|反义词：less flavorful, bland, tart, sour|词性:形容词
Etymology: From fruit + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "fructus" (fruit).
