enclitic:/ɪnˈklɪtɪk/| adj. n.|粘附词，后接成分|A word or particle that grammatically functions as a word but is phonologically bound to the preceding word, like "-n't" in "didn't".  It leans on the preceding word for pronunciation. |Example: The word "-n't" in "isn't" is an enclitic.  "isn't"中的 "-n't" 是一个粘附词。|近义词：suffix, affix|反义词: proclitic|词性: adjective, noun

enclitic:/ɪnˈklɪtɪk/| adj. n.|后接的，后接成分|Relating to or being an enclitic. |Example:  The pronoun " 'em" (them) is often used enclitically. 代词 "'em" (them) 常用作后接成分。|近义词：dependent, bound|反义词: independent, free|词性: adjective
