ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chiterling*, from Old English *ċetel*, from Proto-Germanic *ketilaz* (meaning "kettle, cauldron").  The connection likely arises from the resemblance of the intestines to a coiled kettle. The diminutive suffix *-ling* was added later.
