callowy:/ˈkæloʊi/| adj.|长满老茧的；硬结的；无情的|Having calluses; hardened; insensitive.|His callowy hands showed years of hard manual labor.  他那双长满老茧的手表明他多年从事繁重的体力劳动。|The CEO's callowy response to the layoffs showed a lack of empathy. 首席执行官对裁员的冷漠回应表明他缺乏同理心。|近义词：calloused, hardened, insensitive, thick-skinned|反义词：soft, tender, sensitive, compassionate|词性: 形容词
Etymology->
From callus + -y.  Callus comes from Latin callum "hard skin."
