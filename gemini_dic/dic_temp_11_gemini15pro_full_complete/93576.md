imam:/ɪˈmɑːm/ | n. | 伊玛目，阿訇 | A Muslim religious leader, often the leader of congregational prayer in a mosque. |  The imam led the Friday prayers. 伊玛目带领了周五的祈祷。

The imam delivered a sermon about the importance of charity. 伊玛目发表了一篇关于慈善重要性的布道。 | 近义词：prayer leader, cleric, Muslim leader | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic إمام (ʾimām, “leader, example, model”), from أَمَّ (ʾamma, “he preceded, he led the way”).
