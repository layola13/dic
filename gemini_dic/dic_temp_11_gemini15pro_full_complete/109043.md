legal-tender:/ˈliːɡl ˈtɛndər/ | n. |法定货币；法币|Currency that must be accepted by law in settlement of debts.|The US dollar is legal tender in the United States. 美元是美国的合法货币。|Example:  Shopkeepers are obliged to accept legal tender.  店主有义务接受法定货币。|Synonyms: legal currency, lawful money |Antonyms: counterfeit money, fake currency |noun
