imbroglio:/ɪmˈbroʊljoʊ/ /ɪmˈbrəʊliəʊ/
n.
混乱；纠葛；纷乱
a confused and complicated situation or affair
例句：The political imbroglio threatened to destabilize the entire region. 政治上的纠纷有可能使整个地区动荡不安。

例句：He found himself caught in an imbroglio of conflicting loyalties. 他发现自己陷入了忠诚冲突的困境。

近义词：muddle, tangle, predicament, entanglement, complication
反义词：order, clarity, simplicity
词性：名词
