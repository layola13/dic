flavorful:/ˈfleɪvərfəl/| adj.|美味的；有味道的|Having a strong and pleasant taste.|The soup was rich and flavorful. 这汤味道浓郁鲜美。|近义词：tasty, delicious, savory, palatable|反义词：bland, tasteless, flavorless|词性:形容词
Etymology: From flavor + -ful.  "Flavor" comes from Old French *flaur, from Latin *flator "a blowing, blast," from flare "to blow."  The suffix "-ful" means "full of" or "characterized by."
