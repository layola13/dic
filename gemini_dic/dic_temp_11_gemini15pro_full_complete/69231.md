fearsomenesses:/ˈfɪərsəmnəsɪz/
n.
令人恐惧的性质；可怕之处
The quality of inspiring fear or awe; terribleness.
The fearsomenesses of the deep ocean are still largely unknown. 深海的可怕之处在很大程度上仍然是未知的。
fearsomeness, terribleness, dreadfulness, awfulness
pleasantness, attractiveness, beauty

Etymology:
From fearsome + -ness.  Fearsome from fear + -some.  Fear from Old English *fǣr, from Proto-Germanic *feraz. -some from Old English -sum. -ness from Old English -nes(s).
