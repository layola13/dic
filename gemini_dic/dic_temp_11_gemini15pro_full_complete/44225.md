crocketing: /ˈkrɒkɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːkɪtɪŋ/ (US)
|n.|  装饰性尖顶, 尖顶装饰,  |A series of projecting ornaments, usually of foliage, formed at regular intervals along the sloping edge of a gable, buttress, spire, or other architectural member.|The elaborate crocketing on the church spire was a testament to the skill of the stonemasons. 教堂尖顶上精巧的尖顶装饰证明了石匠的技艺。|Synonyms: ornamentation, decoration, embellishment|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun
