blockbuster:/ˈblɒkbʌstər/ (UK) /ˈblɑːkbʌstər/ (US)| n. | 大片；轰动一时的事物；畅销书 |A thing of great power or size, especially a film, book, or other product that is a great commercial success. |例句：The movie was a blockbuster, breaking box office records. 这部电影是一部大片，打破了票房纪录。

例句：Her new novel is expected to be a summer blockbuster.  她的小说预计会成为夏季的畅销书。

近义词：hit, smash, triumph, sensation |反义词:flop, failure|词性:名词
