outbellowed:/ˌaʊtˈbɛloʊd/
verb
大声吼叫，胜过…的吼叫
to shout louder than someone or something else
The captain outbellowed the storm, urging his crew to keep working. 船长的声音盖过了风暴的呼啸，敦促他的船员继续工作。
Synonyms: outshout, drown out
Antonyms: whisper, murmur
词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + bellow.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing in the action of the verb. "Bellow" originates from Old English "bellan," meaning "to roar," related to Old Norse "belja."
