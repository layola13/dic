CEFR:/ˈsiːˌɛfˈɑːr/ /ˈseɪfɑːr/ | n. |欧洲共同语言参考标准 (Common European Framework of Reference for Languages)|  A descriptive scale used to measure language proficiency in learners of foreign languages across Europe and beyond.  It describes what a learner can do at each level of proficiency in terms of reading, writing, listening, and speaking. | The student's English level is B2 according to the CEFR.  这个学生的英语水平根据欧洲共同语言参考标准是B2级。 | synonyms: language proficiency scale, language framework | antonyms: N/A | noun
