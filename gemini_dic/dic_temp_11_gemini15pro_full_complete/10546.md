appraisers:/əˈpreɪzərz/
n.
估价员，鉴定人
People who estimate the value of something, especially for official purposes.
The appraisers valued the painting at $1 million. 估价员对这幅画的估价为100万美元。
近义词: assessors, valuers, evaluators
反义词:  N/A
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old French word "apreisier" meaning "to appreciate, to value," which in turn comes from the Latin "appretiare" meaning "to set a price to." The suffix "-er" denotes a person who performs the action of the verb.
