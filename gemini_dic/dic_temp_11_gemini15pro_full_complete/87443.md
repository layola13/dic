hesitatingly:/ˈhezɪteɪtɪŋli/| adv.|犹豫地，迟疑地|In a way that shows hesitation or uncertainty.|She hesitatingly agreed to go with him. 她犹豫地同意和他一起去。|近义词：tentatively, cautiously, reluctantly|反义词：confidently, decisively, readily|词性: 副词
Etymology: From the verb "hesitate" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Hesitate" comes from the Latin "haesitare" meaning "to stick fast, remain fixed".
