homonymous:/hɒˈmɒnɪməs/ /həˈmɑːnɪməs/| adj.|同名的，同音异义的|Having the same name as another, or (of words) having the same pronunciation as another but a different meaning, origin, or spelling.|The two girls, both named Sarah, were homonymous. 这两个女孩都叫莎拉，是同名的。|The words "too" and "two" are homonymous.  “too”和“two”这两个词是同音异义词。|近义词：homonymic, namesake|反义词：antonymous, heteronymous|词性:adjective
