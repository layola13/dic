nonmanipulative:/ˌnɒn.məˈnɪpjʊ.lə.tɪv/ | adj. | 非操控性的，非操纵性的 | Not manipulative; not influencing or controlling someone unfairly or unscrupulously. | She appreciated his nonmanipulative approach to the situation. 她很欣赏他处理这种情况的非操控性方式。 |  He gave her some nonmanipulative advice. 他给了她一些非操控性的建议。 | 近义词: honest, straightforward, genuine, authentic, sincere | 反义词: manipulative, controlling, scheming, devious, exploitative | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not". This prefix negates the word it attaches to.
manipulative (adjective): from manipulate (verb) + -ive (suffix forming adjectives).  Manipulate comes from Latin *manipulus "handful, bundle", ultimately from manus "hand" + *ple- "to fill".
