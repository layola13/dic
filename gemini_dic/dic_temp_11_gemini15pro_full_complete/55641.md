ditheism:/ˈdaɪθiːɪzəm/
n.
二神教，二神论
The belief in two gods, especially two equal and opposing gods.
Ditheism posits two equally powerful deities, often in conflict with each other.  二神教假设存在两个同样强大的神，他们经常相互冲突。
Ancient Zoroastrianism is sometimes described as a form of ditheism.  古代琐罗亚斯德教有时被描述为一种二神论的形式。
近义词:dualism, bitheism
反义词:monotheism, polytheism
词性:noun
