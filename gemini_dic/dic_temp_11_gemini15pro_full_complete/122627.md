/ˈmɒdɪfaɪ/ | /ˈmɑːdɪfaɪ/ | v. | 修改，更改，修饰 | To make partial or minor changes to something, typically so as to improve it or to make it less extreme. |  Example: The company modified its policy to allow more flexible working hours. 公司修改了政策，允许更灵活的工作时间。 | Example:  We had to modify our plans because of the bad weather. 因为天气不好，我们不得不修改计划。 | Synonyms: alter, change, adjust, adapt, amend | Antonyms: maintain, preserve, keep, retain | verb
