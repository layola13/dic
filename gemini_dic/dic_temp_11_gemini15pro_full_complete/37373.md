commensurations:/kəˌmɛnʃəˈreɪʃənz/
| n. | 吊唁，慰问 | expressions of sympathy and sorrow for someone, especially on the death of a loved one. |  We offer our sincere commensurations to the family on their loss. 我们向这个家庭表示诚挚的慰问。| synonyms: condolences, sympathies, commiserations | antonyms: congratulations, felicitations | noun
commensurate:/kəˈmɛnʃərət/
| adj. | 相称的，相当的 | corresponding in size or degree; in proportion. | Her salary is commensurate with her experience. 她的薪水与她的经验相称。| synonyms: proportionate, corresponding, equivalent, comparable | antonyms: disproportionate, unequal, mismatched | adjective
