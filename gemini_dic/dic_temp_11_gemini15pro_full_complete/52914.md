dimple:/ˈdɪmpl/ /ˈdɪmpəl/| n.|酒窝；凹痕；涟漪|A small hollow place, especially one that appears on a person's cheek when they smile, or on the surface of something.|She has a dimple in her left cheek. 她的左脸颊上有一个酒窝。|近义词：indentation, hollow, depression|反义词:bulge, protuberance|noun
