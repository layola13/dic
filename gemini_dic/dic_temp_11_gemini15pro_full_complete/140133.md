off-the-map:/ˌɒf ðə ˈmæp/ (US) /ˌɒf ðə ˈmæp/ (UK)| adj.|偏远的，人迹罕至的；鲜为人知的；非同寻常的|Remote, isolated, far from civilization; unknown or uncharted; unusual or unconventional.|例句：We stayed in an off-the-map village in the Himalayas. 我们在喜马拉雅山脉的一个偏远村庄里住了一段时间。|例句：The restaurant served off-the-map cuisine. 这家餐厅供应非同寻常的菜肴。|近义词：remote, isolated, obscure, unconventional, unusual|反义词：well-known, popular, common, conventional|词性:形容词

词源:  The phrase literally refers to places not marked on a map, hence unknown or unexplored. It has figuratively extended to describe anything unconventional or unusual.
