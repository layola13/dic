margin:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪn/ (US), /ˈmɑːdʒɪn/ (UK)| n. | 边缘；利润；余地；保证金 |an edge or border; the difference between the cost price and the selling price of an item; a spare amount or measure or degree allowed or given for contingencies or special situations;  a sum deposited with a broker to cover the risk of loss on a transaction or account | The book has wide margins. 这本书的页边很宽。| synonyms: border, edge, rim, perimeter, profit, leeway, allowance | antonyms: center, middle, loss, deficit | noun

margin:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪn/ (US), /ˈmɑːdʒɪn/ (UK)| v. |加旁注；留边 | to provide with a margin; to furnish with an explanatory note in the margin | He margined the paper with notes. 他在纸的边缘加了注释。| synonyms: annotate, comment, gloss | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the verb form) | verb
