cachexia:/kəˈkɛksiə/| n. |恶病质，体质衰弱|A condition of physical wasting away due to the loss of weight and muscle mass that occurs in patients with diseases such as advanced cancer or AIDS.|Example 1: The patient with advanced cancer suffered from severe cachexia.  晚期癌症患者患有严重的恶病质。

Example 2:  Cachexia can be a debilitating side effect of chronic illnesses. 恶病质可能是慢性疾病的衰弱副作用。|Synonym: wasting, atrophy, emaciation|Antonym: robustness, vigor, health|noun
