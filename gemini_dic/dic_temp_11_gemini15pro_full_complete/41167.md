copycat:/ˈkɒpɪkæt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiːkæt/ (US)| n. adj.|模仿者，抄袭者；模仿的，抄袭的|A person who copies another person's behavior, work, etc., especially to annoy or compete with them; relating to or involving the copying of another person's behavior, work, etc.|例句：He was accused of being a copycat. 他被指责为抄袭者。|近义词：imitator, mimic, impersonator|反义词：original, innovator|词性：名词，形容词
