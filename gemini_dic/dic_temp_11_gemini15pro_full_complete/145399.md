overcautious:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəs/| adj.|过于谨慎的，过分小心的|Excessively cautious; too careful and unwilling to take risks.|例句：He was overcautious in his investments, missing out on some profitable opportunities. 他在投资方面过于谨慎，错过了一些盈利的机会。

例句：Her overcautious nature prevented her from experiencing new things. 她过于谨慎的天性使她无法体验新事物。|近义词：careful, prudent, cautious, wary, circumspect|反义词：reckless, careless, impulsive, rash, imprudent|词性:形容词
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "cautious" (from Latin "cautus," meaning "careful")
