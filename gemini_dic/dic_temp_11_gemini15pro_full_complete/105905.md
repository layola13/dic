kitty-corner:/ˈkɪtiˌkɔːrnər/ /ˈkɪdiˌkɔːrnər/
adjective, adverb|斜对角的，对角线上的|Located diagonally opposite.  Something situated diagonally across from something else, especially across a street or intersection.|The drugstore is kitty-corner from the bank.|Synonyms: diagonally opposite, catty-corner, cater-cornered|Antonyms: adjacent, neighboring|adjective, adverb
