caramel: /ˈkærəməl/ (US) /ˈkærəmel/ (UK)| n. adj.|焦糖；焦糖色的|A light brown confection made by heating sugar, or a substance made by heating sugar until it turns brown.  Can also be used as an adjective to describe the color or flavor.|例句：I love caramel apples. 我喜欢焦糖苹果。

例句：She has caramel-colored hair. 她有一头焦糖色的头发。|近义词：toffee, butterscotch|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, adjective

Etymology: From Middle French *caramelle*, from Spanish *caramelo*, probably from Late Latin *cannamella*, "sugar cane", from Latin *canna* "reed, cane" + *mella* "honey".
