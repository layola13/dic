nombles:/ˈnʌmbəlz/| n. |内脏，尤指鹿的内脏；可食用动物内脏（尤指鹿、牛或猪）|The nombles were considered a delicacy in medieval times.  在中世纪，内脏被认为是一种美味佳肴。|Synonyms: offal, entrails, viscera|Antonyms: None (No direct opposite for a specific type of food)|noun
Etymology: From Old French *nombles*, plural of *nomble*, from Latin *lumulus*, diminutive of *lumbus* "loin".|
