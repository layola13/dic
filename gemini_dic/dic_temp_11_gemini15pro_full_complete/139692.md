oceania:/ˌoʊʃiˈeɪniə/ | n. |大洋洲|A geographic region comprising Melanesia, Micronesia, Polynesia, Australasia, and sometimes the Malay Archipelago. |例句：Oceania is a vast and diverse region. 大洋洲是一个广阔而多样的地区。

例句：Many unique cultures thrive in Oceania. 许多独特的文化在大洋洲繁荣发展。|近义词：Australasia, South Pacific |反义词:None |词性:noun
