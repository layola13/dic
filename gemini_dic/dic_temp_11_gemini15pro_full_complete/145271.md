overbooking: /ˌoʊvərˈbʊkɪŋ/ | n. | 超额预订 | The practice of selling more tickets or reservations than are actually available, in the expectation that some people will cancel. | The airline apologized for the overbooking and offered compensation to the affected passengers. 航空公司为超额预订道歉，并向受影响的乘客提供赔偿。 | 近义词: overselling | 反义词: underbooking | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "booking" (noun referring to the act of reserving something)
