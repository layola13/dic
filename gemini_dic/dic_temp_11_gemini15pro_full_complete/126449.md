mystify:/ˈmɪstɪfaɪ/| v. |使迷惑，使神秘化|To confuse or puzzle someone completely; to make something difficult to understand.|The magician's tricks mystified the audience. 魔术师的戏法使观众迷惑不解。|

The disappearance of the plane mystified investigators. 飞机的失踪让调查人员感到困惑。

|近义词：bewilder, perplex, puzzle, baffle, confound|反义词：clarify, enlighten, explain|词性：动词
