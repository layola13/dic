impropriatrix:/ɪmˌprəʊpriˈeɪtrɪks/
n.
女业主；女所有人；女经营者
A female owner or proprietor, especially of a business or property.
She was the impropriatrix of a successful bakery. 她是一家成功的面包店的女老板。
The impropriatrix of the estate welcomed her guests warmly.  这位庄园的女主人热情地欢迎她的客人。
近义词：owner, proprietor, proprietress
反义词：tenant, renter
词性：noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *impropriatrix, from Latin improprius (“not one's own”) + -atrix (feminine agent suffix).  The masculine form is impropriator.
