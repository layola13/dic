PHRASE->
in all likelihood = 十有八九; 很可能
The project will, in all likelihood, be completed on time. 这个项目很可能会按时完成。

There is every likelihood that... = ...很有可能
There is every likelihood that the price will go up. 价格很有可能会上涨。

increase the likelihood of... = 增加...的可能性
Regular exercise increases the likelihood of living a longer, healthier life. 规律的运动会增加活得更长寿、更健康的可能性。

reduce the likelihood of... = 降低...的可能性
Wearing a seatbelt reduces the likelihood of serious injury in a car accident. 系安全带可以降低车祸中严重受伤的可能性。

assess the likelihood of... = 评估...的可能性
We need to assess the likelihood of success before we invest. 在投资之前，我们需要评估成功的可能性。
