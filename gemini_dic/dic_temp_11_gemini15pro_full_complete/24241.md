bridlers:/ˈbraɪdlərz/| n.|控制者；约束者；勒住缰绳的人|People who bridle something, often used figuratively to describe those who restrain or control something.  |The bridlers of free speech sought to suppress dissenting voices.|近义词：restrainers, controllers, inhibitors|反义词:liberators, emancipators|Noun
