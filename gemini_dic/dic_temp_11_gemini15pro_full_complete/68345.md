fall-through-the-cracks:/fɔːl θruː ðə kræks/
idiom
被遗忘；被忽视；未被注意到
To be overlooked or neglected, especially of something important that deserves attention.
Many small businesses have fallen through the cracks during the pandemic and received no financial assistance. 许多小企业在大流行期间被忽视，没有得到任何财政援助。
be overlooked, be neglected, be forgotten, slip through the net
be noticed, be taken care of
