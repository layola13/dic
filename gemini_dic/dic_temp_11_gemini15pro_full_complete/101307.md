inwardly:/ˈɪnwərdli/
adverb
内心深处地；秘密地
Towards the inside; privately, secretly.
She smiled outwardly, but inwardly she was furious. 她表面上笑了，但内心却很愤怒。
近义词:internally, privately, secretly
反义词:outwardly, openly, publicly
词性:副词

inward:/ˈɪnwərd/
adjective
向内的；内心的
Directed or proceeding toward the inside; inner.
The inward curve of the bay sheltered the boats. 海湾向内的曲线为船只提供了庇护。
近义词:internal, inner
反义词:outward, external
词性:形容词

词源:
From Old English *inneweard (unattested), from inne "within" + -weard "-ward."  Cognate with Old Frisian innwerd, Old Saxon innward, Old High German inn(a)wart, Old Norse inn varða.
