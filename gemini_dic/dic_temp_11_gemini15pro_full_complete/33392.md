chuprassy:/tʃʌˈprɑːsi/ | n. | 茶房，办公室勤杂人员 | An office attendant or messenger, especially in India.  Derived from Hindi and Urdu.  Historically, a chuprassy performed various tasks like serving tea, delivering messages, and running errands.  |  The chuprassy brought us tea and biscuits. 茶房给我们送来了茶和饼干。| The chuprassy delivered the important documents. 茶房递送了重要的文件。 | Synonyms: peon, orderly, messenger | Antonyms: manager, executive | Noun
