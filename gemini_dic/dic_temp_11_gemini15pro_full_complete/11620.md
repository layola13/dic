armageddon:/ˌɑːrməˈɡɛdən/ | n. | 世界末日；大决战 |A catastrophic or decisive conflict, often viewed as ultimately destructive.  The term originates from the biblical book of Revelation, referring to the final battle between good and evil. | The film depicted a nuclear armageddon. 这部电影描绘了一场核战争导致的世界末日。| 近义词：apocalypse, cataclysm, doomsday | 反义词: peace, tranquility | noun
