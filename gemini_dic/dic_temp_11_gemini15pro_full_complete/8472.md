antiasthmatics:/ˌæntiæsˈmætɪks/ | n. | 抗哮喘药，平喘药 |  Medications used to prevent or treat asthma attacks.  These drugs work by relaxing the muscles in the airways, reducing inflammation, or blocking the effects of substances that trigger asthma symptoms. | Example: The doctor prescribed antiasthmatics to help control his breathing. 医生开了抗哮喘药来帮助他控制呼吸。| Example:  She carries her antiasthmatics with her at all times. 她随时都带着她的抗哮喘药。 | Synonyms: asthma medications, bronchodilators, corticosteroids | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but potentially: asthma triggers) | noun
