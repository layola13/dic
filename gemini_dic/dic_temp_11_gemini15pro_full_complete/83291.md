gynander:/ˌɡaɪˈnændər/ | n. |雌雄嵌体，雌雄同体|An organism, especially an insect, that exhibits both male and female characteristics in different parts of its body. | Example 1: The researchers discovered a rare gynander butterfly, with one wing displaying male coloration and the other female. 研究人员发现了一只罕见的雌雄嵌体蝴蝶，它的一个翅膀呈现雄性颜色，另一个翅膀呈现雌性颜色。

Example 2: Gynandromorphism can occur in various species, creating gynanders with a mosaic of sexual characteristics. 雌雄同体现象可以发生在不同的物种中，产生具有性别特征镶嵌的雌雄嵌体。 | Near synonyms: hermaphrodite, intersex | Antonym: unisexual | noun
