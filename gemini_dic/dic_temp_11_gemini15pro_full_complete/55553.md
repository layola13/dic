distringas:/dɪˈstrɪŋɡəs/
noun
扣押令，扣留令
A legal writ compelling attendance in court or the seizure of property.
例句：The court issued a distringas against the defendant's assets. 法院对被告的资产发出了扣押令。

例句：A distringas was used to compel the witness to appear in court.  扣押令被用来强制证人出庭。

近义词：writ, warrant, summons, seizure
反义词：release

词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *destreindre, from Latin distringere "to draw tight, bind fast," from dis- "apart" + stringere "to draw tight."
