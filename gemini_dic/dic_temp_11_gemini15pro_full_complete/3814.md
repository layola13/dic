agnosticisms: /æɡˈnɑːstɪˌsɪzəmz/
n. |不可知论 |The doctrines or practices of agnostics. |He explored various agnosticisms before settling on his own beliefs. 他在确立自己的信仰之前，探索了各种不可知论。|近义词: skepticism, unbelief |反义词: faith, belief |词性: 名词

Etymology: From agnostic + -ism (suffix forming nouns of action, state, condition, doctrine). Agnostic is from a- (not) + gnostic (knowing), from Greek *gnōstos, verbal adjective of gignōskein (to know).
