eductor:/ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtər/ (US), /ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtə/ (UK)| n.|教育家；教育者|A person who educates; a teacher, instructor, or professor.|例句：He was a respected educator who influenced generations of students. 他是一位受人尊敬的教育家，影响了几代学生。

例句：The educators at the conference discussed new teaching methods. 在会议上，教育工作者们讨论了新的教学方法。

近义词：teacher, instructor, tutor, professor, mentor|反义词：student, pupil|词性：noun


eductor:/ɪˈdʌktər/ (US), /ɪˈdʌktə/ (UK)| n.|喷射泵；引射器|A device that uses a fluid under pressure to draw in and transport another fluid.|例句：The eductor was used to pump water from the well. 这台喷射泵用来从井里抽水。

例句：An eductor is often used in industrial processes to mix liquids.  喷射器常用于工业过程中混合液体。

近义词：injector, ejector, jet pump|反义词：N/A|词性：noun
