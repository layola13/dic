chiefage:/ˈtʃiːfɪdʒ/| n.|酋长身份/酋长统治时期|The chiefage lasted for two decades. 酋长统治持续了二十年。|例句：His chiefage was marked by prosperity. 他的酋长统治时期以繁荣为标志。

The tribe thrived during her chiefage. 在她的酋长统治期间，部落繁荣昌盛。|近义词：leadership, reign, rule|反义词: subordination, subjection|词性:名词

Etymology:  "chief" (from Old French "chef," ultimately from Latin "caput" meaning "head") + "-age" (a suffix forming nouns denoting status, rank, or period of office).
