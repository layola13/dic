cercaria:/sərˈkɛəriə/| n. |尾蚴|The larval form of a parasitic fluke (trematode worm), typically possessing a tail and capable of swimming freely in water. It is a stage in the fluke's life cycle, following the sporocyst or redia stage, and preceding entry into the final host or an intermediate host.|The cercaria of Schistosoma penetrates human skin.  血吸虫的尾蚴会穿透人体皮肤。

The pond water was teeming with cercariae.  池塘水里充满了尾蚴。|近义词:larva|反义词:|noun
