caterwaul:/ˌkætərˈwɔːl/| vi. n.| 尖叫；猫叫春；|To make a loud, harsh, howling cry, like that of a cat in heat.|The cats caterwauled outside my window all night, keeping me awake. 猫整夜都在我的窗外嚎叫，吵得我睡不着。|近义词：shriek, howl, wail, scream|反义词:whisper, murmur|Verb

caterwaul:/ˌkætərˈwɔːl/| n.| 猫叫春的声音；尖叫声；|A loud, harsh, howling cry.|The caterwaul of the band was unbearable.  乐队的演奏声简直是噪音，让人难以忍受。|近义词：shriek, howl, wail, scream|反义词:whisper, murmur|Noun
