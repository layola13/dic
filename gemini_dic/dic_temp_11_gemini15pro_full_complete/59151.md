dynastical:/daɪˈnæstɪkəl/ | adj. |王朝的；世袭的| Relating to a dynasty or line of hereditary rulers. | The dynastical struggles weakened the empire. 王朝斗争削弱了帝国。| The museum houses a collection of dynastical artifacts from the Ming Dynasty.  这家博物馆收藏了明朝的王朝文物。 | 近义词:hereditary, ancestral | 反义词:elected, democratic | 词性:形容词

词源: From dynasty + -al.  Dynasty comes from the Greek word dynastēs, meaning "lord" or "ruler."
