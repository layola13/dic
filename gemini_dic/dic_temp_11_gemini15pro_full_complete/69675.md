fenderless:/ˈfɛndələs/| adj.|无挡泥板的，没有挡泥板的|Lacking fenders (the protective metal or plastic covers over the wheels of a vehicle).|例句：He rode a fenderless bicycle through the muddy puddles. 他骑着一辆没有挡泥板的自行车穿过泥泞的水坑。

例句：The fenderless jeep looked rugged and ready for off-road adventures.  这辆没有挡泥板的吉普车看起来很坚固，随时可以进行越野探险。|近义词：unprotected, exposed|反义词：fendered, protected|词性:形容词
