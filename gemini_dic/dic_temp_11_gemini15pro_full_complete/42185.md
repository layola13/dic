coulisses:/kuːˈliːsɪz/| n. |幕后，后台；（舞台两侧的）侧幕|A space immediately offstage, especially one used by actors when they are not visible to the audience.|The secrets of the political coulisses are now revealed in this new book. 这本新书揭示了政治幕后的秘密。|synonyms: wings, backstage, behind the scenes|antonyms: onstage, center stage|noun

coulisse:/kuːˈliːs/| n. |（舞台两侧的）侧幕；幕后，后台；（尤指政治或外交的）秘密活动|A flat piece of scenery at the side of a stage, used with others like it to create an effect of depth or to mask the wings;  The activities that happen behind the scenes in politics, business, etc.|The president was heavily involved in the political coulisse. 总统深度参与了政治幕后活动。|synonyms: wings, backstage, behind the scenes|antonyms: onstage, center stage|noun
