maligner:/məˈlaɪnər/ | n. | 诽谤者，恶意中伤者 | A person who speaks harmful untruths about someone. |  He was accused of being a maligner of the company's reputation.  他被指控恶意中伤公司的声誉。 | synonym: slanderer, defamer, libeler | antonym:  supporter, advocate | noun

malign:/məˈlaɪn/ | v. | 诽谤，污蔑；中伤 | To speak about someone in a spitefully critical manner. |  She maligned her ex-husband to anyone who would listen. 她向任何愿意听的人诽谤她的前夫。| synonym: slander, defame, libel, disparage | antonym: praise, commend, extol | verb

malign:/məˈlaɪn/ | adj. | 有害的，恶意的 | Evil in nature or effect; malevolent. | The tumor was found to be malign.  这个肿瘤被发现是恶性的。 | synonym: harmful, evil, malevolent, pernicious, cancerous | antonym: benign, beneficial, harmless | adjective
