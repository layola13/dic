chaser:/ˈtʃeɪsər/| n.|追逐者；猎人；驱逐舰；（餐后）烈酒|A person or thing that chases.  A drink of a milder beverage taken after a strong alcoholic drink.|The police car was in hot pursuit of the chaser. 警车正在紧追不舍地追捕追逐者。|He ordered a whiskey chaser after his beer. 他在喝完啤酒后又要了一杯威士忌。|Synonyms: pursuer, hunter, follower|Antonyms: prey, quarry|noun

chaser:/ˈtʃeɪsər/| n.|（餐后）烈酒|A drink of a milder beverage taken after a strong alcoholic drink.|He took a beer chaser after his shot of tequila. 他在喝完一杯龙舌兰酒后，喝了一杯啤酒作为chaser。|Synonyms: follow-up drink, rinse|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
