holding-point:/ˈhəʊldɪŋ pɔɪnt/| n.|集结待命点；临时停顿点；保持点|A designated location where troops, vehicles, or other resources are temporarily stationed or assembled, often while awaiting further orders or developments.  It can also refer to a point in a process where progress is temporarily halted.|The refugees reached a holding-point just outside the city.  难民们到达了城外的一个集结待命点。

The project reached a holding-point while waiting for funding.  这个项目在等待资金到位期间暂时停顿了。|synonyms: staging area, assembly point, rendezvous point, waystation, checkpoint|antonyms: destination, terminus, final point|noun
