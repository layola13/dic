balker:/ˈbɔːkər/ (US), /ˈbɔːlkə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 阻挠者，拒绝者，畏缩不前的人|A person who refuses to proceed or cooperate; someone who hesitates or stops short of doing something.|The horse was a balker and refused to jump the fence. 这匹马很倔，拒绝跳过栅栏。|Synonyms: resister, objector, nonconformist, refusenik|Antonyms: cooperator, conformist, participant|noun

balk:/bɔːk/ (US), /bɔːlk/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|障碍，阻碍；突然止步；畏缩；阻止，妨碍|1. (n.) A hindrance or obstacle. 2. (vi.) To stop short and refuse to proceed. 3. (vt.) To hinder or thwart.|1. (n.)  The new regulations proved to be a major balk to progress. 新的规章制度被证明是进步的主要障碍。 2. (vi.) The horse balked at the last jump. 马在最后一个障碍前突然止步。 3. (vt.)  His plans were balked by unexpected difficulties. 他的计划因意外的困难而受阻。|Synonyms: (n.) obstacle, impediment, obstruction; (v.) hesitate, recoil, frustrate|Antonyms: (n.) aid, assistance; (v.) facilitate, aid, abet|noun, verb intransitive, verb transitive
