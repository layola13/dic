eclectically:/ɪˈklɛktɪkli/
adverb
兼收并蓄地，博采众长地
In a manner that derives ideas, style, or taste from a broad and diverse range of sources.
She decorated her apartment eclectically, with a mix of antique and modern furniture. 她兼收并蓄地装饰了她的公寓，混合了古董和现代家具。
synonym: diversely, broadly, comprehensively
antonym: narrowly, restrictively, homogeneously
词性: 副词
