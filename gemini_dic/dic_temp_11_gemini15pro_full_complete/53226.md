directions:/dəˈrɛkʃənz/ /daɪˈrɛkʃənz/ | n. |方向，指示，说明 | Instructions or guidance on how to do something or get somewhere. | Follow the directions on the package.  按照包装上的说明操作。| synonyms: instructions, guidance, orders | antonyms: misdirection, confusion | noun

directions:/dəˈrɛkʃənz/ /daɪˈrɛkʃənz/ | n. |方位 | The position or point to or from which something is directed. | The house faces in a southerly direction. 房子朝南。 | synonyms: bearing, course, heading | antonyms: location, position | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *direction*  (Modern French direction), from Latin *directionem* (nominative *directio*), noun of action from past-participle stem of *dirigere* "to direct," from *dis-* "apart" + *regere* "to guide, rule."
