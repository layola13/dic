commercialise:/kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪz/ (UK), /kəˈmɜːrʃəlaɪz/ (US)| v.| 商业化，商品化|To develop something and make it available for sale; to exploit something for business or profit.|例句：The university has been criticized for commercialising its research. 这所大学因将其研究成果商业化而受到批评。|近义词：marketise, merchandise, monetise|反义词:decommercialize|词性:动词

commercialise:/kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪz/ (UK), /kəˈmɜːrʃəlaɪz/ (US)| n.| 商业化|The process of making something available for sale; the exploitation of something for business or profit.|例句：The commercialisation of Christmas has become excessive. 圣诞节的商业化已经变得过分了。|近义词：marketing, merchandising, monetization|反义词:decommercialization|词性:名词
