anti-bible:/ˌæntiˈbaɪbl/| adj. n.|反圣经的，反对圣经的；与圣经教义相悖的书籍|Something that is opposed to or contradicts the teachings of the Bible.  It can also refer to a publication that criticizes or challenges the Bible.|例句：His views were considered anti-bible by some religious groups.  他的一些宗教团体认为他的观点是反圣经的。

例句：Some scholars consider certain Gnostic texts to be anti-bibles. 一些学者认为某些诺斯替教的文献是反圣经的。|近义词：blasphemous, sacrilegious, heretical, profane|反义词：biblical, scriptural, pious, devout|词性: 形容词，名词
