dry: /draɪ/
dry: adj. | 干燥的; 干旱的; 无雨的; 口渴的; | Free from moisture or liquid; not wet or moist. | The ground was dry and cracked.  地面干燥开裂。|  arid, dehydrated, parched | wet, damp, moist | adjective
dry: v. | (使)变干; (使)干燥; | To become dry; to make something dry. |  Hang the clothes to dry.  把衣服挂起来晾干。 | dehydrate, desiccate, parch |  moisten, dampen, soak | verb
dry: n. |  干旱(时期或地区); | A period or place without rain. | The long dry killed the crops. 长时间的干旱使庄稼枯死了。 | drought, aridity | wetness, humidity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *drȳge (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *draugiz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrewgʰ- (“dry”). Cognate with Old Saxon drōgi, Old Frisian drūge, Dutch droog, Old High German trugi, German trocken, Old Norse draugr (“dry”).
