kennel:/ˈkɛn(ə)l/| n. |狗窝，犬舍|A small shelter or cage for a dog.|例句：My dog sleeps in his kennel at night. 我的狗晚上睡在它的狗窝里。

kennel:/ˈkɛn(ə)l/| n. |(尤指繁殖或训练用) 犬舍；养犬场|A building or enclosure where dogs are bred, trained, or boarded.|例句：We left our dog at the kennel while we were on vacation. 我们度假时把狗寄养在犬舍。

kennel:/ˈkɛn(ə)l/| vt. |把…关进狗窝|To put or keep (a dog) in a kennel.|例句：We kennelled our dog while we were away. 我们外出时把狗关进了狗窝。


Etymology: From Old French *kenil, from Medieval Latin canile, from Latin canis (“dog”).

Synonyms: doghouse, cage, shelter, pound, cattery (for cats)

Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) freedom, outdoors
