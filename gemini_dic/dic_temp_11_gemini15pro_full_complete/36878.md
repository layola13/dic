coloratura:/ˌkʌlərəˈtʊrə/ /ˌkʌlərəˈtʊərə/| n.|花腔；炫技的演唱风格；花腔女高音|A rapid melodic passage, with a lot of ornamentation, requiring great vocal agility.  A soprano specializing in such passages.|The opera singer's coloratura was breathtaking.  这位歌剧演唱家的花腔令人叹为观止。|近义词：ornamentation, embellishment, flourish|反义词:simplicity|词性:noun

coloraturas:/ˌkʌlərəˈtʊrəz/ /ˌkʌlərəˈtʊərəz/| n.|花腔（复数）；炫技的演唱风格（复数）；花腔女高音（复数）|Plural form of coloratura, referring to multiple instances of rapid melodic passages, ornamentation, or singers specializing in them.|The coloraturas in Mozart's operas are notoriously difficult. 莫扎特歌剧中的花腔是出了名的难唱。  She is known for her dazzling coloraturas. 她以其令人眼花缭乱的花腔而闻名。|近义词：ornamentations, embellishments, flourishes|反义词:simplicities|词性:noun
