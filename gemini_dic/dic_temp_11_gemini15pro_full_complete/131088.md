nonbeneficial:/ˌnɒnbɛnɪˈfɪʃəl/| adj.|无益的，不利的|Not advantageous or helpful; producing no benefits.|例句：The policy change proved to be nonbeneficial to the economy. 这项政策的改变证明对经济没有益处。|近义词：detrimental, harmful, disadvantageous, unproductive, unfavorable|反义词: beneficial, advantageous, helpful, profitable, productive|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + beneficial (from Latin *bene facere* "to do good")
