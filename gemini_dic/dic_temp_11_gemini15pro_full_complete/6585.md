anacanthous:/ˌænəˈkænθəs/
adj.
无棘的，无刺的
Lacking spines or thorns; spineless.
The anacanthous fish, unlike its spiny relatives, navigated the coral reef with ease.  这种无棘的鱼，与其多刺的亲戚不同，可以轻松地在珊瑚礁中穿梭。
The botanist identified the plant as an anacanthous variety of rose.  植物学家将这种植物鉴定为一种无刺的玫瑰品种。
近义词:smooth, spineless
反义词:spiny, thorny, prickly
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *an-* (without) + *akantha* (thorn, spine).
