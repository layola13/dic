insistive:/ɪnˈsɪstɪv/| adj.|坚持的，执着的|Continuing to demand something; persistent.|例句：The child's insistive demands for a new toy finally wore down his parents.  孩子坚持要一个新玩具，最终他的父母妥协了。

例句：She was insistive that she had seen him at the party. 她坚持说她在派对上见过他。|近义词：persistent, insistent, tenacious|反义词：yielding, submissive, compliant|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *insistyfe*, from late Latin *insistīvus*, from Latin *insistō* (“I stand upon, press upon, pursue, persist”), from *in-* (“in, on”) + *sistō* (“I stand”).
