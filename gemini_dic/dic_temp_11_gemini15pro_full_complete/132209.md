nondebilitating:/ˌnɒndɪˈbɪlɪteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非衰弱性的；不使人虚弱的
Not causing weakness or debilitation.
She suffered a nondebilitating illness that allowed her to continue working. 她患了一种不使人虚弱的疾病，可以继续工作。
The injury, while painful, was nondebilitating. 虽然受伤很疼，但不至于使人虚弱。
Synonyms: mild, moderate, non-incapacitating
Antonyms: debilitating, weakening, incapacitating
词性: 形容词
