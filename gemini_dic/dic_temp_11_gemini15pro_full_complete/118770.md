metallize:/ˈmɛtəlaɪz/ | v. | 金属化;镀金属 | To coat or cover with metal. |  The plastic parts were metallized to give them a shiny finish. 塑料零件镀了金属以使其具有光泽。| synonyms: plate, coat, cover | antonyms: strip, remove | verb

metallize:/ˈmɛtəlaɪz/ | v. | 使...具有金属特性 | To give something the properties of a metal. |  The process metallizes the surface of the material, making it conductive.  这个过程使材料表面金属化，使其具有导电性。| synonyms:  impregnate, infuse | antonyms: demetallize | verb
