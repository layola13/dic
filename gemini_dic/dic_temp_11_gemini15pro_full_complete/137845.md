nonviewers:/ˌnɒnˈvjuːərz/
n.
非观看者，没收看电视节目的人
People who do not watch a particular television program or television in general.
The advertising campaign failed to reach nonviewers. 这场广告宣传活动未能触及到非观看者。
近义词：non-watchers
反义词：viewers, audience
词性：名词
