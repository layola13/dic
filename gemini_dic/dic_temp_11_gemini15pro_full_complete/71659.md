flattery:/ˈflætəri/| n.|奉承；恭维；谄媚|Excessive and insincere praise, given especially to further one's own interests.|She used flattery to get what she wanted from him. 她用奉承来从他那里得到她想要的东西。|He was immune to flattery. 他对奉承免疫。|近义词：praise, adulation, commendation, compliments|反义词：criticism, condemnation, reproach|noun
