chevrette:/ʃəˈvrɛt/| n.|小山羊皮革，小山羊肉|A soft, supple leather made from goatskin, often used for gloves or bookbindings.  It can also refer to the meat of a young goat. |This luxurious bag is crafted from the finest chevrette.  这个奢华的包包是由最好的小山羊皮制成的。|The chef prepared a delicious chevrette roast.  厨师准备了一道美味的烤小山羊肉。|近义词：kidskin, goatskin, kid (for meat)|反义词:|noun
