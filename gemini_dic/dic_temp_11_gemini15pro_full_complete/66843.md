expatriation:/ˌɛksˌpeɪtriˈeɪʃən/ |n.| 外籍化，移居国外；放弃国籍 |The expatriation of wealthy individuals can have a significant impact on a country's economy. 富人移居国外会对一个国家的经济产生重大影响。|  Expatriation can be a difficult process, both emotionally and logistically. 移居国外在情感上和后勤上都是一个艰难的过程。| emigration, exile, relocation | repatriation, immigration | noun
etymology->
From French *expatriation*, from *expatrier* (“to expatriate”), from Latin *ex* (“out of”) + *patria* (“native land, fatherland”).
