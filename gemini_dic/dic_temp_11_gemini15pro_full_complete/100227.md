interrogatorily:/ˌɪn.təˈrɒɡ.ə.tɪv.li/ /ˌɪnˌter.əˈɡɑː.tɔr.əl.i/
| adv. | 质疑地，疑问地 | In a manner that expresses or suggests a question; questioningly. | "Is that really true?" she asked interrogatorily. “那真是真的吗？”她质疑地问道。|近义词：questioningly, inquiringly, searchingly |反义词:affirmatively, declaratively |词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From interrogative + -ly.  Interrogative comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin interrogativus, from interrogare ‘ask’, from inter- ‘between’ + rogare ‘ask’.
