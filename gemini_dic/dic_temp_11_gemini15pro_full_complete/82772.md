guillotine: /ˈɡɪlətiːn/ (US), /ˈɡiː.ə.tiːn/ (UK) | n. |断头台；切纸机|A machine with a heavy blade sliding vertically in grooves, used for beheading people.| The guillotine was a symbol of the French Revolution. 断头台是法国大革命的象征。| The printer uses a guillotine to cut the paper. 印刷厂用切纸机裁纸。| synonym: beheader, executioner (for the device used for beheading), cutter (for paper guillotine) | antonym: N/A | noun

guillotines: /ˈɡɪlətiːnz/ (US), /ˈɡiː.ə.tiːnz/ (UK) | n. |断头台（复数）；切纸机（复数）|Plural form of "guillotine," referring to more than one such device.|  Several guillotines were used during the Reign of Terror.  恐怖统治时期使用了数台断头台。| The printing house has two guillotines for different paper sizes.  这家印刷厂有两台用于不同纸张尺寸的切纸机。| synonym: beheaders, executioners (for devices used for beheading), cutters (for paper guillotines) | antonym: N/A | noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From French *guillotine*, named after Joseph-Ignace Guillotin, a French physician who proposed its use, although he did not invent it.  Ultimately from Guillaume, a personal name.
