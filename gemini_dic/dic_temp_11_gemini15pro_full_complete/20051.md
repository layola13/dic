biophysically:/ˌbaɪoʊˈfɪzɪkəli/
adverb
生物物理地
Relating to biophysics or employing the principles of biophysics.  Characterized or explained in terms of biophysics.
The problem was analyzed biophysically, considering the molecular interactions and forces involved.  这个问题从生物物理学角度进行了分析，考虑了所涉及的分子相互作用和力。
The ion channels were biophysically characterized using patch-clamp techniques.  使用膜片钳技术对离子通道进行了生物物理学表征。
近义词:physically, biologically
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider conceptually opposite approaches like "chemically" or "metaphysically")
词性:adverb
