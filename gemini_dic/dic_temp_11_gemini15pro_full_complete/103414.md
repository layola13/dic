jitterbug: /ˈdʒɪtərbʌɡ/ (US), /ˈdʒɪtəbʌɡ/ (UK)| n. vi.|吉特巴舞；跳吉特巴舞的人；紧张不安的人|A fast, acrobatic dance popular in the 1940s, performed to swing music. Also refers to a person who dances the jitterbug or someone who is nervous or restless.|例句：They danced the jitterbug all night. 他们整晚都在跳吉特巴舞。

例句：He's a real jitterbug before a performance. 他在演出前总是非常紧张。|近义词：nervous person, fidget,  swing dance, Lindy Hop|反义词:calm person, relaxed person|词性:noun, verb
