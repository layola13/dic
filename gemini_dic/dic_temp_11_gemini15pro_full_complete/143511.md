outgoer:/ˈaʊtˌɡoʊər/
n.|外出者；好交际的人|A person who is outgoing and sociable; someone who enjoys going out and meeting people.|She's a real outgoer and loves parties.|近义词：extrovert, social butterfly|反义词：introvert, homebody|词性:名词
Etymology: out- + -goer (from go)
