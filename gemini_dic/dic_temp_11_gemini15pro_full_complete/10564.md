appreciation:/əˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən/| n. |欣赏，感激，感谢，增值|An understanding of the good qualities of something; an expression of gratitude; an increase in value.|例句：She expressed her appreciation for their hard work. 她对他们的辛勤工作表示感谢。

例句：The painting has increased in appreciation over the years. 这幅画多年来一直在增值。|近义词：gratitude, thanks, recognition, increase, rise|反义词:depreciation, criticism, disapproval|词性:noun
