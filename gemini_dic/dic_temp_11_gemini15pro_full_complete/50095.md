dentiferous:/dɛnˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
有齿的；具齿的
Bearing teeth, toothed.
The dentiferous jaw of the shark is intimidating.  鲨鱼长满牙齿的下颚令人生畏。
The fossilized skull was clearly dentiferous. 这块颅骨化石明显有牙齿。
synonyms: toothed, dentate
antonyms: toothless, edentulous
