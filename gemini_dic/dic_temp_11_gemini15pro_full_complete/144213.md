outskate:/ˌaʊtˈskeɪt/
verb
胜过滑冰，滑冰技术胜过
To skate better or faster than someone else.
He easily outskated his opponent in the race. 他在比赛中轻松地胜过了对手。
surpass in skating, skate better than
outskate the competition 在滑冰比赛中胜出
She outskated everyone else and won the gold medal. 她滑冰技术胜过所有人，赢得了金牌。
outperform, outdo, beat
be outskated by
