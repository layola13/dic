backboned:/ˈbækˌboʊnd/ | adj. | 有脊椎的；有骨气的；坚定的 | Having a backbone; resolute or courageous. |例句：Backboned animals include mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, and fish. 有脊椎动物包括哺乳动物、鸟类、爬行动物、两栖动物和鱼类。

例句：She showed a backboned determination to succeed despite the obstacles. 尽管困难重重，她表现出坚定的决心要取得成功。|近义词：vertebrate, resolute, courageous, determined |反义词：invertebrate, spineless, cowardly |词性:形容词

词源:
From back + bone + -ed.  "Back" refers to the spine, and "bone" refers to the skeletal structure.  The suffix "-ed" forms an adjective indicating possession of the quality described by the preceding elements.
