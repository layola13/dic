deadborn:/ˈdedbɔːrn/| adj.|死胎的，夭折的；失败的|Born dead; not alive at birth. Also, unsuccessful; not developing or surviving.|例句：The baby was sadly deadborn.  这个婴儿很不幸地是个死胎。

例句：Their plans for a new business were deadborn.  他们新业务的计划失败了。|近义词：stillborn, abortive, unsuccessful|反义词：live, successful|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old English *dēadboran (unattested), from dēad "dead" + boran "to bear."  来自古英语 *dēadboran（未经证实的），由 dēad “死的” + boran “生育”组成。
