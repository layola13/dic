noncompetitor:/ˌnɑːn kəmˈpɛtɪtər/ | /ˌnɒn kəmˈpɛtɪtə(r)/ | n. | 非竞争者；非参赛者 |  A person or entity that is not involved in a competition or does not compete with others in a particular market or field. |  The small bookstore thrived because it focused on rare books and didn't consider large online retailers as noncompetitors. 这家小型书店生意兴隆，因为它专注于稀有书籍，并且不把大型在线零售商视为非竞争对手。

The local bakery considered the supermarket a noncompetitor because they focused on different types of baked goods.  这家本地面包店认为超市是非竞争对手，因为他们专注于不同类型的烘焙食品。


| 近义词: nonparticipant, outsider | 反义词: competitor, rival | 词性: noun
