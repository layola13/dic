misreforms:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɔːrmz/
noun
错误的改革；不当的改革
Reforms that have unintended negative consequences or are poorly implemented.
The government's misreforms in the education sector led to a decline in standards. 政府在教育部门的错误改革导致了标准的下降。
Near Synonyms: misguided reforms, flawed reforms, counterproductive reforms
Antonyms: successful reforms, effective reforms, beneficial reforms
词性: 名词
