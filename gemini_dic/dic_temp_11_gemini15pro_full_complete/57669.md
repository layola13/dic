drawfiling:/ˈdrɔːˌfaɪlɪŋ/
noun
抽锉; 拉锉
The act or process of smoothing or finishing a metal surface using a drawfile.
The mechanic used drawfiling to smooth the rough edges of the part.  机械师用拉锉来磨平零件的粗糙边缘。
smoothing, finishing
roughing, filing (as a general term)
noun
