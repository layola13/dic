half-woven:/ˌhɑːfˈwoʊvən/
half-woven| adj.|半编织的；未完成编织的|Partially woven; not completely finished being woven. The process of weaving is only partly done.|The tapestry was half-woven, its vibrant threads telling an unfinished story. 这幅挂毯只完成了一半的编织，其鲜艳的丝线讲述着一个未完成的故事。|partly woven, unfinished, incomplete|finished, complete|adjective
half-woven:/ˌhɑːfˈwoʊvən/
half-woven| adj.|比喻：未完成的，不完整的|Figuratively used to describe something incomplete or unfinished.|Her plans were still half-woven, leaving much room for change. 她的计划仍未完成，还有很大的修改空间。|undeveloped, rudimentary, in progress|developed, complete, finalized|adjective
