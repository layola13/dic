burrows:/ˈbʌroʊz/ (US) /ˈbʌrəʊz/ (UK)| n. v.|洞穴，地道；挖洞，挖地道|A burrow is a hole or tunnel dug in the ground by an animal such as a rabbit, fox, or mole, to live in.|The rabbits disappeared into their burrows. 兔子消失在它们的洞穴里。|近义词：hole, tunnel, den, lair|反义词: mound, hill|noun

burrows:/ˈbʌroʊz/ (US) /ˈbʌrəʊz/ (UK)| v.|挖洞，挖地道；钻进，钻研|To burrow is to dig a hole or tunnel in the ground, or to move through something as if digging.|The mole burrowed deep into the earth. 鼹鼠在地里挖了很深的洞。|近义词：dig, tunnel, excavate|反义词: unearth, uncover|verb
