jacobsen:/ˈdʒeɪkəbsən/| n.|雅各布森|A surname of Scandinavian origin, derived from the given name Jacob.  It can also refer to the Jacobsen syndrome, a rare congenital disorder.|例句：Mr. Jacobsen is a renowned architect. 雅各布森先生是一位著名的建筑师。

例句：Jacobsen syndrome is caused by a deletion on chromosome 11. 雅各布森综合征是由11号染色体缺失引起的。|近义词: (For the surname - no direct synonyms, as it's a proper noun.  For the syndrome, perhaps "11q deletion disorder")|反义词: (None)|词性:Noun
