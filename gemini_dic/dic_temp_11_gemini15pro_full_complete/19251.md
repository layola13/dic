bhaskara: /bɑːˈskɑːrə/ | n. |婆什迦罗，婆什迦罗第二|Bhaskara (also known as Bhaskara II or Bhaskaracharya) was a 12th-century Indian mathematician and astronomer. He is known for his significant contributions to algebra, arithmetic, trigonometry, and calculus. | Bhaskara wrote the Siddhanta Shiromani, a major treatise on mathematical astronomy. 婆什迦罗写了《历算书论》，这是一部关于数理天文学的重要著作。|  Bhaskara's work on indeterminate equations was groundbreaking. 婆什迦罗在不定方程方面的工作是开创性的。| synonym: mathematician, astronomer | antonym: N/A | noun
