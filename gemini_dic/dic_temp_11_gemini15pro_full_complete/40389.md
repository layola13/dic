contradistinct:/ˌkɒntrədɪˈstɪŋkt/ /ˌkɑːntrədɪˈstɪŋkt/
adjective
截然不同的，对比鲜明的
Clearly distinguished or contrasted.

The two approaches are contradistinct. 这两种方法截然不同。

His views are contradistinct from hers. 他的观点与她的截然不同。

近义词: distinct, different, contrasting, disparate, dissimilar
反义词: similar, alike, identical, same

词性: adjective
