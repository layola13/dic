cavalryman:/ˈkævəlrimən/| n. |骑兵, 马兵|A soldier who serves in the cavalry (a military unit that fights on horseback).|例句：The cavalryman charged across the field. 骑兵冲过战场。

例句：The cavalrymen were skilled horsemen. 这些骑兵都是骑术精湛的骑手。|近义词：trooper, horseman, rider|反义词:infantryman, foot soldier|词性:noun
