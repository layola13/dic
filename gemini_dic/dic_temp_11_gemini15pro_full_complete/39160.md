conga:/ˈkɒŋɡə/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡə/ (US)| n. vi.|康茄舞，康茄鼓|A Cuban dance performed by a large group of people moving in a long, winding line. Also refers to the tall, narrow drum used to play the music for this dance.|例句：The conga line snaked its way through the party. 康茄舞的队伍像蛇一样穿过派对。

例句：He played a lively rhythm on the conga. 他在康茄鼓上演奏着活泼的节奏。|近义词：dance, drum|反义词: stillness, silence|词性:名词, 动词
