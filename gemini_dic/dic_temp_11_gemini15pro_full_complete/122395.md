Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他靠什么谋生？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done to help? 他做了什么来帮忙？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been studying at university? 她在大学里一直在学什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? 她参加聚会穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? 你离开之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? 警察到达之前发生了什么事？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted? 我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? 她下学期会学什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have learned by the time she finishes the course?  到她完成课程的时候，她将学到什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
