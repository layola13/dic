balalaika:/ˌbæləˈlaɪkə/| n.|巴拉莱卡琴|A Russian stringed musical instrument of the lute family with a triangular body.|He played a lively tune on his balalaika. 他用他的巴拉莱卡琴演奏了一首活泼的曲子。|近义词:domra, lute|反义词:None|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian балалайка (balalajka), of imitative origin.
