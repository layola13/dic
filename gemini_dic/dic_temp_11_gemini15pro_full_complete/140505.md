oldster:/ˈoʊldstər/| n. |老人，长者|An informal word for an elderly person.  It is often used humorously or affectionately.|例句：The oldster regaled us with stories of his youth.  这位老人给我们讲述了他年轻时的故事。

例句：A group of oldsters were playing checkers in the park. 一群老人家在公园里下棋。|近义词：senior citizen, elderly person, senior, veteran |反义词: youngster, youth, child |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From old + -ster (a suffix forming nouns denoting persons, often with a derogatory or humorous connotation).  The word "youngster" is formed with the same suffix.
