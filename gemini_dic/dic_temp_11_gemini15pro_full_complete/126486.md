myths:/mɪθs/| n.|神话，虚构的故事|A traditional story, especially one concerning the early history of a people or explaining some natural or social phenomenon, and typically involving supernatural beings or events. |例句：Ancient Greek myths are full of gods and goddesses. 古希腊神话充满了神和女神。

例句：Many myths have a basis in historical events. 许多神话都以历史事件为基础。|近义词：legends, fables, folktales, lore|反义词：facts, realities, truths|词性:noun
