governance:/ˈɡʌvərnəns/ /ˈɡʌvər.nəns/| n. |治理，管理，统治|The process of governing, especially the control and direction exercised by a government or other ruling body.|The company's governance was questioned after the scandal.  这家公司的管理在丑闻曝光后受到了质疑。|synonyms: administration, management, control, rule, authority|antonyms: misrule, anarchy, chaos|noun
