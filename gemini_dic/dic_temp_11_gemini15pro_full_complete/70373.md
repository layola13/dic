fife:/faɪf/
n.
横笛; Fife
A small high-pitched transverse flute similar to a piccolo, used especially in military bands.
The shrill sound of the fife pierced the air.  横笛尖锐的声音划破长空。
近义词:flute, piccolo
反义词: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument)
