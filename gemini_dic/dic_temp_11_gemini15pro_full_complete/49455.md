deltoid: /ˈdɛltɔɪd/ | n. adj. | 三角肌; 三角形的; |A large triangular muscle covering the shoulder joint and serving to abduct and flex and extend and rotate the arm. | The deltoid muscle is responsible for lifting the arm. 三角肌负责抬起手臂。| The shape of the building is deltoid.  这座建筑的形状是三角形的。|Synonyms: triangular, delta-shaped | Antonyms: circular, round | noun, adjective

deltidium: /dɛlˈtɪdiəm/ | n. | 三角肌 | The deltoid muscle, particularly its fleshy part. |  Injury to the deltidium can make lifting difficult.  三角肌损伤会使抬举变得困难。| He strained his deltidium lifting weights. 他举重时拉伤了三角肌。 | Synonyms: deltoid muscle | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific muscle) | noun
