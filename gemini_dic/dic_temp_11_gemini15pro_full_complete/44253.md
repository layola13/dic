crofting: /ˈkrɒftɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈkrɑːftɪŋ/ (US) | n. | 小农场耕作；小佃农耕作 | The practice of working a small farm, especially in the Scottish Highlands and Islands.  Crofting often involves a combination of small-scale agriculture, fishing, and weaving. | Crofting is a way of life in the Highlands.  在高地，小农场耕作是一种生活方式。

Crofting communities are often tightly knit. 小农场耕作社区通常联系紧密。 | 近义词: smallholding, subsistence farming | 反义词: intensive farming, agribusiness | 词性: 名词
