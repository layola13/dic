chamois:/ˈʃæmi/ (US), /ˈʃæmwɑː/ (UK)| n. | 羚羊, 麂皮, 鹿皮|A type of goat-antelope with short hooked horns, found in mountainous areas of Europe. Also, a soft, pliable leather originally made from the skin of this animal, now often from sheepskin or other material.|例句：The chamois nimbly leaped across the rocks. 羚羊灵活地跳过岩石。

例句：She used a chamois to polish the car. 她用一块麂皮擦拭汽车。|近义词：antelope, mountain goat, suede, leather|反义词: None|词性:noun


chamois:/ˈʃæmi/ (US), /ˈʃæmwɑː/ (UK)| adj. |麂皮色的|Having a yellowish-beige color.|例句：She wore chamois-colored gloves. 她戴着麂皮色的手套。

例句：The walls were painted a soft chamois. 墙壁被涂成了柔和的麂皮色。|近义词：buff, beige, yellowish-brown|反义词: None|词性:adjective
