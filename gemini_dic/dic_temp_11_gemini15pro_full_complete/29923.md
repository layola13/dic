cavalierish:/ˌkævəˈlɪərɪʃ/| adj.|傲慢的；漫不经心的；轻佻的|Showing a lack of proper concern; offhand.  Marked by or given to arrogant disregard of others.|He gave a cavalierish wave of his hand, dismissing my concerns. 他傲慢地挥了挥手，对我的担忧不屑一顾。|Synonyms: arrogant, haughty, supercilious, disdainful, condescending, offhand|Antonyms: humble, respectful, considerate, courteous|adjective
