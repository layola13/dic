impulsive:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪv/ | adj. | 冲动的，易冲动的 | Acting or done without forethought. |  She made an impulsive decision to quit her job. 她一时冲动决定辞掉工作。

He's impulsive and often regrets things he's said. 他很冲动，经常后悔自己说过的话。

近义词：rash, impetuous, spontaneous, hasty | 反义词：cautious, deliberate, thoughtful, considered | 词性：形容词

词源：Late 16th century: from late Latin impulsivus, from impuls- ‘driven’, from the verb impellere (see impel).
