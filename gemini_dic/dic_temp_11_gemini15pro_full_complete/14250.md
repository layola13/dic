autodidact: /ˌɔːtəˈdaɪdækt/ | n. | 自学者 | A person who has taught themself a subject or skill without formal education or a teacher. |  He was a brilliant autodidact, having mastered several languages on his own. 他是一位才华横溢的自学者，自学了多门外语。


Lincoln was largely an autodidact, educating himself through voracious reading. 林肯很大程度上是个自学者，通过大量的阅读来教育自己。


近义词: self-taught person, self-learner | 反义词: formally educated person | 词性: noun
