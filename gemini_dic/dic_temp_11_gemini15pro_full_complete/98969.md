inter-layering:/ˌɪntərˈleɪərɪŋ/
n.|交错层叠，夹层|The process of placing layers of different materials between or among other layers.|Geological formations often exhibit inter-layering of sedimentary rocks and volcanic ash. 地质构造经常呈现出沉积岩和火山灰的交错层叠。|The cake featured inter-layering of sponge cake and cream.  这个蛋糕的特点是海绵蛋糕和奶油的交错层叠。|stratification, layering|N/A|noun
