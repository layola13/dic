nonitemizers:/ˌnɑːnˈaɪtəˌmaɪzərz/
|n.| 非逐项扣除者 |People who choose the standard deduction instead of itemizing deductions on their tax returns.  These individuals do not list specific deductions like charitable contributions or medical expenses.| Nonitemizers often find the standard deduction simpler and more beneficial than itemizing. |  Most taxpayers are now nonitemizers due to the increased standard deduction. | 近义词: standard deduction filers | 反义词: itemizers | 词性: 名词
