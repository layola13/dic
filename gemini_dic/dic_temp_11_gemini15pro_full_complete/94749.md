improvisation:/ˌɪmprəvaɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 即兴创作，即兴表演 | The act of creating or performing something spontaneously, without preparation. |  The musician's improvisation on the piano was breathtaking.  钢琴家即兴的钢琴演奏令人叹为观止。| synonyms: ad-libbing, extemporization, off-the-cuff | antonyms: preparation, rehearsal | noun

improvise:/ˈɪmprəvaɪz/ | v. | 即兴创作，即兴表演 | To create or perform something spontaneously, without preparation. |  He had to improvise a speech when the speaker failed to show up.  当演讲者没有出现时，他不得不即兴发表演讲。| synonyms: ad-lib, extemporize, wing it | antonyms: prepare, rehearse | verb
