kahneman:/ˈkɑːnəmən/ | n. | 卡尼曼 |  Refers to Daniel Kahneman, an Israeli-American psychologist and economist notable for his work on the psychology of judgment and decision-making, as well as behavioral economics. He was awarded the 2002 Nobel Memorial Prize in Economic Sciences. | Daniel Kahneman's work challenged traditional economic theory. 丹尼尔·卡尼曼的工作挑战了传统的经济理论。| synonym:  psychologist, economist, Nobel laureate | antonym: N/A | noun
