nonrulings:/nɒnˈruːlɪŋz/

nonruling:/nɒnˈruːlɪŋ/
|adj.| 非裁决的；非判例的 |Describing something that does not constitute a legal ruling or precedent.| The lawyer argued that the judge's comment was a nonruling statement. 律师辩称法官的评论并非裁决性声明。|Synonyms: non-precedential, non-binding |Antonyms: ruling, binding | adjective
