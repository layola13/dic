erectilities: /ɪˌrɛkˈtɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 勃起功能，勃起能力 | The ability to achieve and maintain an erection. 词源: From "erectile" + "-ities" (suffix forming plural nouns). "Erectile" comes from Late Latin *erectilis, from Latin erectus (past participle of erigere "to erect"), from ex- "out" + regere "to direct, guide, rule."
