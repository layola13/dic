bastes:/beɪsts/| v.| 浇油(烤肉时); 粗缝; 痛打, 责骂 |to moisten (roasting meat) with pan juices, fat, or other liquid; to sew with long, loose stitches; to criticize or reprimand severely |She bastes the turkey every thirty minutes. 她每三十分钟就给火鸡浇一次油。|近义词：(for moistening) drizzle, brush,  (for sewing) tack, stitch, (for criticizing) lambaste, castigate |反义词: (for moistening) dry, (for sewing) unstitch, (for criticizing) praise, commend|词性: 动词

baste:/beɪst/| n.| 粗缝; 浇油 | a loose stitch used to hold fabric temporarily in place; the act of moistening roasting meat |The baste held the seam together until she could sew it properly.  粗缝把接缝固定在一起，直到她能把它缝好。|近义词：(for sewing) tacking stitch, (for moistening) drizzling |反义词: (for sewing) permanent stitch|词性: 名词
