nonsaturation:/ˌnɒnˌsætʃəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 不饱和；非饱和 |The state of being unsaturated, especially of an organic compound containing double or triple bonds between carbon atoms.| The nonsaturation of the fatty acids is important for their fluidity. 脂肪酸的不饱和度对其流动性很重要。| unsaturation, insaturation | saturation | noun
