ha'p'orth:/ˈheɪpəθ/ /ˈheɪpwərθ/| n.|少量，很少的钱|A small amount, especially a small amount of something that can be bought.|例句：I'll buy a ha'p'orth of milk. 我会买少量的牛奶。|例句：It won't make a ha'p'orth of difference. 这不会有什么区别。|近义词：bit, little, trifle|反义词：lot, much|词性:名词

词源:
Contraction of *halfpennyworth*,  the amount that a halfpenny would buy.  The halfpenny ceased to be legal tender in 1984.
