glossata:/ɡlɒˈseɪtə/ | n. | 舌鞘亚纲 | A large subclass of insects, including moths and butterflies, characterized by having a coiled proboscis formed from modified maxillae. | Example:  The glossata are known for their diverse wing patterns. 舌鞘亚纲以其多样化的翅膀图案而闻名。

Example:  Butterflies and moths belong to the Glossata. 蝴蝶和飞蛾属于舌鞘亚纲。 | 近义词: Lepidoptera (although Glossata is a subclass within Lepidoptera) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
