gulled:/ɡʌld/
verb
欺骗，愚弄
deceived or tricked, especially in a foolish or naive way
He was gulled into believing that he had won the lottery. 他被骗了，以为自己中了彩票。
近义词:deceived, tricked, duped, cheated, swindled, hoodwinked, fooled
反义词:disabused, enlightened, undeceived
词性:verb
