bubbly:/ˈbʌbli/ (US), /ˈbʌbli/ (UK)| adj. n.|起泡的，活泼的；香槟酒；汽水|Full of bubbles; lively and cheerful; sparkling wine (often champagne).|例句：The champagne was bubbly and delicious.  香槟酒气泡丰富，美味可口。

例句：She has a bubbly personality. 她性格活泼开朗。|近义词：effervescent, sparkling, vivacious, champagne, fizzy drink|反义词：flat, still, dull, lifeless|词性: adjective, noun

bubbly:/ˈbʌbli/ (US), /ˈbʌbli/ (UK)| n.|香槟酒；汽水；起泡的液体|Sparkling wine, typically champagne; a fizzy or carbonated drink.|例句：We celebrated with a bottle of bubbly. 我们开了一瓶香槟庆祝。

例句：She poured herself a glass of bubbly. 她给自己倒了一杯汽水。|近义词：champagne, sparkling wine, fizzy drink|反义词：-|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the noun "bubble" + the suffix "-y," indicating a characteristic or quality. The word "bubble" itself comes from Middle English "bobel," of imitative origin, echoing the sound of bubbling.
