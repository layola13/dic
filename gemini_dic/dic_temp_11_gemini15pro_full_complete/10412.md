appetite:/ˈæpɪtaɪt/| n.|食欲，胃口；欲望；爱好|A natural desire to satisfy a bodily need, especially for food.|She has a healthy appetite. 她胃口很好。|Synonyms: hunger, craving, desire, longing|Antonyms: aversion, distaste, disgust|noun

appetite:/ˈæpɪtaɪt/| n.|欲望；爱好|A strong desire for something.|He has an appetite for adventure. 他有冒险的欲望。|Synonyms: desire, passion, yearning, craving|Antonyms: indifference, apathy|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French apetit, from Latin appetitus (“desire, craving, longing, appetite”), from appetere (“to strive after, desire”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + petere (“to seek, aim at, go to, attack, demand”).
