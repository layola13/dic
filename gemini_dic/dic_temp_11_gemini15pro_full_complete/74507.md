frappe:/fræp/ | n. |冰沙，加冰的饮料|A blended iced drink, often made with coffee, milk, and flavorings.  It can also refer to any type of drink chilled with ice. |例句：I ordered a caramel frappe on a hot day.  我在大热天点了一杯焦糖冰沙。

例句：In some regions, "frappe" simply means any drink served with ice. 在某些地区，“frappe”仅仅指任何加冰的饮料。|近义词：smoothie, milkshake, iced coffee, blended drink |反义词: hot drink, hot coffee |词性:noun

frappe:/fræp/ | v. |（使）冰冻，用冰镇|To chill a drink with ice.  In some contexts, it can also mean to cover with ice or to freeze. |例句：She frapped the juice before serving it. 她在上果汁之前先冰镇了一下。

例句：The sudden cold snap frapped the lake over. 突如其来的寒流使湖面结冰。|近义词：chill, ice, cool |反义词: heat, warm |词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *frapper*, meaning "to strike, hit, or knock".  The sense of chilling a drink likely comes from the idea of hitting or shaking it with ice.
