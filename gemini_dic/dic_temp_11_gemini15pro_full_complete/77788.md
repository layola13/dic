generalist:/ˈdʒɛnərəlɪst/| n.|通才，多面手|A person competent in several different fields or activities.|She's a generalist, able to contribute to many different areas of the project. 她是一个多面手，能够为项目的许多不同领域做出贡献。|synonyms: all-rounder, polymath, versatile person|antonyms: specialist, expert|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From general + -ist.  "General" comes from Old French "general"  ultimately from Latin "generalis" meaning "relating to all, of a whole class."  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who practices or is skilled in something.
