overfaithfully:/ˌoʊvərˈfeɪθfəli/
| adv. | 过分忠实地，过于虔诚地 | Performing duties with excessive zeal and adherence, sometimes to the point of being detrimental or unnecessary. | He followed the instructions overfaithfully, missing the spirit of the law in his adherence to the letter. 他过分忠实地执行了指示，执着于字面意思而忽略了法律的精神。| 近义词: religiously, scrupulously, meticulously | 反义词: carelessly, negligently | 词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "faithfully" (adverb from "faithful," derived from "faith" + "-ful").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "faithfully," indicating an excess or extreme degree of faithfulness.
