midbrains:/ˈmɪdbreɪnz/ | n. | 中脑 | The midbrain, also called the mesencephalon, is the topmost part of the brainstem, the connection between the brain and the spinal cord. It is responsible for various functions including vision, hearing, motor control, sleep/wake, arousal (alertness), and temperature regulation. | The doctor explained that damage to the midbrains can affect eye movement. 医生解释说中脑损伤会影响眼球运动。 | The midbrain plays a crucial role in relaying visual and auditory information. 中脑在传递视觉和听觉信息方面起着至关重要的作用。 | synonyms: mesencephalon | antonyms: N/A | noun
