lamellicornia:/ˌlæməlɪˈkɔːrniə/
noun
金龟子类
A large group of beetles characterized by their clubbed antennae, which have plate-like segments that can be opened like a fan.
The lamellicornia include scarab beetles and stag beetles. 金龟子类包括圣甲虫和锹形虫。
近义词: scarabaeoidea
反义词: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group)
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin lamella (“thin plate”) +‎ cornu (“horn”).
