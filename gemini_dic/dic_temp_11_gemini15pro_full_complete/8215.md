anti-freudian:/ˌænti-ˈfrɔɪdiən/
adjective
反弗洛伊德的
Opposed to or rejecting the psychoanalytic theories of Sigmund Freud.
His anti-Freudian stance made him unpopular amongst his colleagues. 他反弗洛伊德的立场使他在同事中不受欢迎。
Near synonyms:  non-Freudian, post-Freudian
Antonyms:  Freudian, psychoanalytic
詞性: adjective
