naturalisms:/ˈnætʃrəlɪzəmz/
n.
自然主义（复数）
A style and theory of representation based on the accurate depiction of detail.  The belief that everything arises from natural properties and causes, and supernatural or spiritual explanations are excluded or discounted.
Emile Zola's novels are a prime example of literary naturalism.  埃米尔·左拉的小说是文学自然主义的典范。
realism, verisimilitude
idealism, spiritualism
noun
