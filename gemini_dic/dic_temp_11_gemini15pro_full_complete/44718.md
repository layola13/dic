cruelty:/ˈkruːəlti/| n.|残忍，残酷；残酷的行为|The cruelty of the dictator shocked the world.  独裁者的残忍震惊了世界。|近义词：brutality, inhumanity, savagery|反义词:kindness, compassion, mercy|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crualte*, borrowed from Old French *crualte*, from Late Latin *crūdēlitās*, from Latin *crūdus* ("raw, unripe, bloody, cruel") + *-tās* (forming nouns of quality).
