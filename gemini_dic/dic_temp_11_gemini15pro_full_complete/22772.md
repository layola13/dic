borstal: /ˈbɔːrstəl/ (UK) | n. | 少年犯教养院 | A type of youth detention center in Britain (historically).  A borstal was designed to rehabilitate young offenders through training and education.  This term is largely outdated and no longer used officially. | He was sent to borstal for three years for car theft. 他因偷车被送进少年犯教养院三年。| Synonym: reformatory, youth detention center | Antonym:  None (The concept doesn't have a direct opposite, perhaps "freedom" or "liberty" in a broad sense.) | noun


borstal: /ˈbɔːrstəl/ (UK) | adj. | 少年犯教养院的 | Relating to a borstal. |  He received a borstal sentence. 他被判处送入少年犯教养院。 | Synonym: reformatory | Antonym: None | adjective
