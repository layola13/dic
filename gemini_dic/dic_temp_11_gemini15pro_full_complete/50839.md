desecrater:/ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪtər/| n. |亵渎者，污损圣物者|A person who desecrates; someone who violates or treats something sacred with irreverence or contempt.|The desecrater spray-painted graffiti on the church walls. 教堂墙上的涂鸦是亵渎者的行为。|近义词：vandal, profaner, defiler|反义词：protector, guardian|词性：名词

desecrate: /ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪt/ | vt. |亵渎，污损|To violate or treat (something sacred) with irreverence or contempt; to damage or pollute (something holy or respected).|The vandals desecrated the cemetery by knocking over tombstones. 故意破坏公物者推倒墓碑，亵渎了墓地。|近义词：profane, defile, violate|反义词：consecrate, sanctify|词性：动词

词源: From Late Latin *desecrātus, past participle of desecrāre  "to unconsecrate," from de- "un-, remove" + sacrāre "to consecrate, make sacred," from sacer "sacred."
