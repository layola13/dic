nonprovisionary:/ˌnɒnprəˈvɪʒənəri/
adjective
非临时性的，最终的，不可更改的
Relating to something that is final and cannot be changed or revoked.
The court issued a nonprovisionary injunction against the company. 法院对该公司发布了最终禁令。
non-provisional, final, conclusive, absolute, definitive, unconditional
provisional, temporary, interim, conditional
