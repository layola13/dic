fracture:/ˈfræktʃər/ (US) /ˈfræktʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|骨折；断裂；裂缝；破裂|A fracture is a break or crack in a bone or other hard material.  As a verb, it means to cause something to break or crack.|
The X-ray revealed a hairline fracture in her wrist.  X光显示她手腕有一条细小的裂缝。
He fractured his leg in a skiing accident.  他在一次滑雪事故中摔断了腿。
|近义词：break, crack, rupture, split|反义词：mend, repair, heal|词性: noun, verb


Etymology:
From Late Middle English *fracturen*, from Latin fractūra (“a breach, break”), from frangere (“to break”).
