mudguard:/ˈmʌdɡɑːrd/ (US) /ˈmʌdɡɑːd/ (UK)| n. |挡泥板；泥瓦|A curved piece of metal or other material fitted above the wheel of a vehicle, especially a bicycle or motorcycle, to protect the rider or the vehicle from mud or water thrown up by the wheels.|
My bike needs a new mudguard; the old one is broken. 我的自行车需要一个新的挡泥板，旧的坏了。

The car's mudguards were caked in mud after the off-road drive.  越野行驶后，汽车的挡泥板沾满了泥。

近义词：fender, splash guard|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
