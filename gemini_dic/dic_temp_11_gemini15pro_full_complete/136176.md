nonreinforcements:/ˌnɒnˌriːɪnˈfɔːsmənts/ | n. |  不强化，无强化 | Absence of reinforcement, meaning the lack of any consequence (positive or negative) following a behavior, which often leads to the extinction of that behavior.  | The nonreinforcements led to a decrease in the frequency of the child's tantrums.  孩子没有得到强化，导致他发脾气的频率降低了。|  Synonyms:  non-reward, absence of reinforcement | Antonyms: reinforcements, rewards | noun
