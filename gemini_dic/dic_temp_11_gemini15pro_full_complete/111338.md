lived-in:/ˈlɪvdɪn/| adj.|居住过的；有人住的；舒适随便的| Appearing comfortable and informal; as if inhabited for a long time.  Showing signs of occupancy. |The house had a lived-in feel, with cozy furniture and family photos on the walls. 这房子有一种有人住过的感觉，舒适的家具和墙上的家庭照片。|近义词：homely, comfortable, cozy, inhabited, occupied |反义词:uninhabited, vacant, empty, sterile, pristine|词性:形容词
