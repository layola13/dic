microexpression:/ˈmaɪkroʊɪkˌsprɛʃən/| n. |微表情|A very brief, involuntary facial expression shown on the face of humans according to emotions experienced. They usually occur in high-stakes situations, where people have something to lose or gain.|He studied her face carefully, trying to detect any microexpressions that might betray her true feelings. 他仔细地观察她的脸，试图发现任何可能暴露她真实感受的微表情。|
|Example: Even though she tried to appear calm, a microexpression of fear flickered across her face. 即使她试图表现得平静，一丝恐惧的微表情还是在她脸上闪过。|Synonyms: tell, facial expression, involuntary expression |Antonyms:  macroexpression (not a commonly used term), conscious expression|noun
