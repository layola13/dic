describing: /dɪˈskraɪbɪŋ/ | adj. | 描述的，描写的 |  present participle of "describe," meaning to give an account in words of (someone or something), including relevant characteristics, qualities, or events. | The book contains a describing passage of the battle. 这本书包含一段描述战斗的文字。 |近义词：depicting, portraying, representing, characterizing | 反义词：obscuring, concealing | 词性: adjective

describing: /dɪˈskraɪbɪŋ/ | n. | 描述，描写 |  A gerund form of "describe," acting as a noun, referring to the act of describing. | Describing the scene vividly brought it to life for the readers.  生动地描述场景使读者眼前一亮。 | 近义词：depiction, portrayal, representation, characterization | 反义词：obscuring, concealing | 词性: noun (gerund)
