craftspeople:/ˈkræftsˌpiːpəl/ (US), /ˈkrɑːftsˌpiːpəl/ (UK)| n.|工匠；手艺人|People who make beautiful or practical objects using their skills with their hands.|The market featured local craftspeople selling handmade jewelry and pottery.  这个市场以当地的手工艺人出售手工制作的珠宝和陶器为特色。|Synonyms: artisans, craftspersons, makers, smiths|Antonyms: amateurs, novices|noun
