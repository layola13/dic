daboia:/dəˈbɔɪə/| n. |蝰蛇；锁蛇|A venomous viper (Vipera russellii) of southern Asia. It is responsible for causing the majority of snakebite deaths in its range due to its widespread distribution, aggressive nature, and potent venom.|例句：The daboia is a highly venomous snake found in India. 锁蛇是一种在印度发现的剧毒蛇。

例句：Daboia envenomation can cause severe bleeding and tissue damage.  锁蛇的毒液会导致严重的出血和组织损伤。|近义词：Russell's viper, chain viper|反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for an animal species)|词性: noun
