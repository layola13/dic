nonevidential:/ˌnɒnɛvɪˈdɛnʃəl/
adjective
非证据的，无证据的
Not based on or supported by evidence; not serving as evidence.
The lawyer dismissed the testimony as nonevidential. 律师认为证词不具证据效力，将其驳回。

The detective found the witness's statement nonevidential and pursued other leads.  侦探发现证人的陈述不构成证据，于是继续追查其他线索。

synonym: unsubstantiated, unproven, unsupported
antonym: evidential, proven, substantiated
词性: adjective
