cobia:/ˈkobiə/ (US), /ˈkəʊbiə/ (UK)| n.|军曹鱼，黑鲯|A large, pelagic fish (Rachycentron canadum) found in warm waters worldwide, prized for its firm, white flesh.|例句：We caught a huge cobia while deep-sea fishing. 我们在深海钓鱼时钓到了一条巨大的军曹鱼。

例句：Cobia is a popular game fish because of its strength and fighting ability. 军曹鱼因其力量和战斗力而成为一种受欢迎的游钓鱼。|近义词：black kingfish, lemonfish, prodigal son, crabeater|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
