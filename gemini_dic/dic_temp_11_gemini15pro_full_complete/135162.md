nonopinionated:/nɒnəˈpɪnjəneɪtɪd/| adj.|没有主见的，不固执己见的|Not having strong opinions; open to different ideas and perspectives.  This allows for flexibility and adaptability in various situations, fostering collaboration and understanding.|  Example: The platform offers a nonopinionated framework, allowing developers to use their preferred tools and libraries. 这个平台提供了一个没有主见的框架，允许开发人员使用他们喜欢的工具和库。| Example:  She appreciated his nonopinionated approach to the project, as it allowed for a more democratic decision-making process. 她很欣赏他对这个项目不固执己见的做法，因为它允许更民主的决策过程。| 近义词: open-minded, impartial, neutral, objective| 反义词: opinionated, dogmatic, biased, prejudiced|词性:形容词
