minorities:/maɪˈnɔːrətiz/ | n. | 少数民族；少数群体；少数派 |A relatively small group of people, especially one commonly discriminated against in a community, society, or nation, differing from others in race, religion, language, or political persuasion.  |  Ethnic minorities often face discrimination. 少数民族经常面临歧视。| synonyms: minority groups, marginalized groups, underrepresented groups | antonyms: majority, mainstream | noun

minorities:/maɪˈnɔːrətiz/ | n. | 少数；少部分 | The smaller number or part, especially a number or part representing less than half of the whole. | Only a minority of students voted in the election. 只有少数学生参加了投票。| synonyms: small number, fraction, handful | antonyms: majority, bulk | noun
