diverted:/daɪˈvɜːrtɪd/ /dɪˈvɜːrtɪd/| adj. |转移的，改道的，被转向的|Turned aside from a course or direction; entertained.|例句：The flight was diverted to another airport due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，航班改飞往另一个机场。|近义词：rerouted, redirected, distracted, amused|反义词：undisturbed, uninterrupted|词性:形容词

divert:/daɪˈvɜːrt/ /dɪˈvɜːrt/| vt. |转移，使转向，使分心，娱乐|To turn aside from a course or direction; to draw the attention of someone or something from one thing to another; to amuse or entertain.|例句：The police diverted traffic around the accident. 警察将交通改道绕过事故现场。|近义词：reroute, redirect, distract, amuse|反义词：maintain, focus|词性:动词

词源：From Old French *divertir, from Latin dīvertere (“to turn aside, divert”), from dis- (“apart, aside”) + vertere (“to turn”).
