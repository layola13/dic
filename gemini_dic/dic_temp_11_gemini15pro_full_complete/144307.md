outsport:/ˌaʊtˈspɔːrt/
verb
在体育运动中胜过，比…更优秀
To surpass or defeat in a sporting activity.
He outsported his rivals in the race. 他在比赛中胜过对手。
She easily outsported the other competitors. 她轻松地胜过其他参赛者。
近义词: outplay, outperform, beat, defeat, conquer
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb

outsport:/ˌaʊtˈspɔːrt/
词源: out- + sport
"out-" prefix 表示“超过，胜过”
"sport" 指“体育运动”
