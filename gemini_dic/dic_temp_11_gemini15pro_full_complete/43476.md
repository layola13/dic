crassly:/ˈkræsli/| adv.|粗俗地；愚钝地；粗鲁地|In a crass or insensitive manner; without refinement or intelligence.  Showing no understanding or sensitivity.|He crassly insulted her in front of everyone. 他当着所有人的面粗鲁地侮辱了她。|Synonyms: crudely, coarsely, insensitively, vulgarly, stupidly|Antonyms: subtly, sensitively, tactfully, politely, intelligently|adverb
