giddying:/ˈɡɪdiɪŋ/| adj.|令人眩晕的，令人眼花缭乱的|Causing dizziness; exciting or exhilarating to the point of disorientation.|The fast pace of city life can be giddying at times. 城市生活的快节奏有时会令人眼花缭乱。|Synonyms: dizzying, intoxicating, exhilarating|Antonyms: calming, soothing, steadying|adjective

giddying:/ˈɡɪdiɪŋ/| adj.|轻率的，不稳重的|Characterized by irresponsible frivolity.|Their giddying spending habits eventually led to financial trouble. 他们轻率的消费习惯最终导致了经济困难。|Synonyms: frivolous, flighty, irresponsible|Antonyms: serious, responsible, cautious|adjective
