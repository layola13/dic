incommutability:/ˌɪnkəmjuːtəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可改变性，不变性|The quality or state of being unable to be changed or altered.|The incommutability of the laws of physics is a fundamental principle. 物理定律的不可改变性是一个基本原则。|Synonyms: immutability, unchangeableness, permanence, fixity|Antonyms: mutability, changeability, variability, alterability|noun
