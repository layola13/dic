danaide:/ˈdænəˌaɪd/ /ˈdeɪnəˌaɪd/| n. |达娜伊得斯（希腊神话人物），徒劳无功的人|A Danaide in Greek mythology was one of the 50 daughters of Danaus, king of Argos.  They were condemned to eternally fill a bottomless vessel with water as punishment for murdering their husbands on their wedding night.  Figuratively, a danaide refers to someone engaged in a futile or endless task.|She felt like a danaide, constantly cleaning a house that never stayed clean. 她感觉自己就像一个达娜伊得斯，不停地打扫永远也干净不了的房子。|近义词：Sisyphean, futile, pointless|反义词:productive, fruitful|词性:noun
