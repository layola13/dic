blackeners:/ˈblækənərz/| n.|涂黑剂；抹黑者；使变黑之物|Substances or individuals that blacken something.  They can refer to physical substances like shoe polish or metaphorical agents that tarnish reputations.|The newspaper article, full of unsubstantiated accusations, served as a blackener of the politician's character. 这篇报纸文章充满了未经证实的指控，败坏了这位政治家的名声。|Synonyms: darkeners, obscurers|Antonyms: whiteners, brighteners|noun
