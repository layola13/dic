disassociate:/dɪsəˈsoʊʃieɪt/ | v. | 分离，脱离，不联系 | To separate or disconnect from something, especially emotionally or mentally. |  He tried to disassociate himself from his past. 他试图和他的过去划清界限。| detach, disconnect, separate, withdraw | associate, connect, join, unite | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + associate, from Latin associare "to join with," from ad- "to" + sociare "to unite," from socius "companion."
