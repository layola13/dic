aphthae: /ˈæfθiː/ (US), /ˈæfθiː/ (UK)| n. |口腔溃疡|Small, shallow sores that develop on the soft tissues in your mouth or at the base of your gums.  They can make eating and talking uncomfortable. They are not contagious.|

例句：
I have painful aphthae on my tongue.
我的舌头上长了疼痛的口腔溃疡。

The doctor diagnosed her with aphthae.
医生诊断她患有口腔溃疡。


近义词：canker sores, mouth ulcers|
反义词:  None (Aphthae describes a specific condition, it doesn't have a direct opposite)|
词性: noun
