angina:/ænˈdʒaɪnə/ /ænˈdʒiːnə/| n. |心绞痛|A condition marked by severe pain in the chest, often also spreading to the shoulders, arms, and neck, caused by an inadequate blood supply to the heart.|He was rushed to the hospital with severe angina.|Synonyms: chest pain, heart pain|Antonyms: comfort, ease|noun

angina:/ænˈdʒaɪnə/ /ænˈdʒiːnə/| n. |咽峡炎|Inflammation of the throat, especially the fauces and tonsils.|The doctor diagnosed her with angina.|Synonyms: sore throat, pharyngitis|Antonyms: health, wellness|noun
