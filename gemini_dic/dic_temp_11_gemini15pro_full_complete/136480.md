nonrhetorically:/ˌnɒnriˈtɒrikəli/ /ˌnɑːnriˈtɔːrɪkli/
adverb
非修辞地；不带修辞色彩地；就字面意义而言
Literally, without using rhetorical devices.  Focusing on the literal meaning and avoiding figures of speech.
He stated nonrhetorically that he was leaving the company. 他直截了当地说他要离开公司。
She nonrhetorically described the accident scene. 她如实地描述了事故现场。
literally, plainly, directly
figuratively, rhetorically
