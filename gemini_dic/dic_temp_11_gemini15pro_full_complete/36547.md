collagenous:/kəˈlædʒɪnəs/
adj.
胶原的；含胶原的
Relating to or consisting of collagen.
Collagenous fibers provide structural support to tissues. 胶原纤维为组织提供结构支撑。
The dermis is a collagenous layer of skin. 真皮是皮肤的胶原层。
近义词:connective
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "non-collagenous")
词性:adjective
