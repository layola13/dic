Doubs: /duː/ | n. | 杜河 | A river in eastern France and western Switzerland, a tributary of the Saône. | The Doubs River flows through a picturesque valley. 杜河蜿蜒流过风景如画的山谷。| 近义词: river, tributary | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Gaulish *Dubis, meaning "black, dark," possibly referring to the color of its water.  Related to Old Irish dub ("dark, black"), Welsh dwfn ("deep"), and Breton du ("black").
