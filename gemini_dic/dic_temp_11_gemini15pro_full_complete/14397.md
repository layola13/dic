autosuggestible:/ˌɔːtoʊsəˈdʒɛstəbəl/
adjective
易受暗示的; 易被自我暗示的
Easily influenced by suggestion, especially self-suggestion.
Example: Hypnosis works best on autosuggestible people.  催眠对易受暗示的人最有效。
Example:  He was so autosuggestible that he convinced himself he was ill. 他很容易受自我暗示的影响，以至于说服自己生病了。
Near Synonyms: suggestible, impressionable, susceptible, gullible
Near Antonyms: resistant, skeptical, critical
word type: adjective
