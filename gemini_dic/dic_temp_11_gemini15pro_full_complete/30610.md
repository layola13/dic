cerebrifugal:/ˌsɛrɪˈbrɪfjuːɡəl/
adjective
离脑的；自大脑向外的
Directed or proceeding away from the brain.
Cerebrifugal axons conduct impulses away from the brain. 离脑轴突将冲动从大脑传导出去。
Cerebrifugal pathways carry information from the brain to the periphery. 离脑通路将信息从大脑传递到外周。
近义词: efferent
反义词: cerebripetal
词性: adjective
