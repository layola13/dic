arbitrariness:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtrerɪnəs/
n.|任意性；武断；专横|The quality of being based on random choice or personal whim, rather than any reason or system.|The arbitrariness of the decision angered the employees.  这个决定的武断性激怒了员工。|capriciousness, randomness, wilfulness, despotism|reasonableness, fairness, objectivity|noun
arbitrarily:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtrerɪli/
adv.|任意地；武断地；专横地|In a random manner, without any reason or system.|The rules were applied arbitrarily. 规则被随意应用。|randomly, capriciously, desultorily|systematically, rationally, objectively|adverb
arbitrary:/ˈɑːrbɪtreri/
adj.|任意的；武断的；专横的|Based on random choice or personal whim, rather than any reason or system.|The choice of the winner seemed completely arbitrary.  获胜者的选择似乎完全是任意的。|capricious, random, whimsical, despotic|reasonable, fair, objective, reasoned|adjective
