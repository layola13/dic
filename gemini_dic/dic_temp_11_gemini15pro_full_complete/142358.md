orthopaedics: /ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪks/ /ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪks/ | n. | 骨科（学） |  The branch of medicine dealing with the correction of deformities of bones or muscles.  It comes from the Greek words "orthos" (straight) and "pais" (child), originally referring to the correction of deformities in children. |  She specializes in orthopaedics and treats a lot of sports injuries. 她专攻骨科，治疗很多运动损伤。| synonyms:  orthopedic surgery, bone surgery | antonyms: N/A | noun

orthopedic: /ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪk/ /ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪk/ | adj. | 骨科的 | Relating to the branch of medicine dealing with the correction of deformities of bones or muscles. | He needs orthopedic shoes to support his ankles. 他需要矫形鞋来支撑脚踝。| synonyms: bone, skeletal | antonyms: N/A | adjective
