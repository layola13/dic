escribed:/ɪˈskraɪbd/ | adj. | 外接的，外切的 |  Drawn outside a figure, touching each side or side produced.  Relating to an escribed circle. |  The escribed circle of a triangle is tangent to one side and the extensions of the other two sides. 三角的外接圆与一边相切，与另外两边的延长线相切。| circumscribed, exscribed | inscribed | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exscribere* "to write out," from *ex-* "out" + *scribere* "to write."  The mathematical sense developed from the idea of a circle drawn outside a figure.
