cathedralic:/kəˌθiːdrəlˈɪk/
adjective
大教堂的；主教座堂的；大教堂似的
Resembling or characteristic of a cathedral; stately and magnificent.
The cathedralic grandeur of the building was awe-inspiring.  这栋建筑的大教堂般的宏伟令人敬畏。
The organ music filled the cathedralic space.  风琴音乐充满了大教堂般宽阔的空间。
近义词:grand, majestic, imposing, stately
反义词:humble, modest, simple, unpretentious
词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin *cathedralicus, from cathedra ("seat, chair," especially "bishop's throne"), from Ancient Greek καθέδρα (kathédra).
