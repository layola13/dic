contractee:/ˌkɒntrækˈtiː/
n.
承包人；订约人
A person who has entered into a contract, especially one who is employed to do work under contract.
The contractee is responsible for completing the project by the deadline.  承包人负责在截止日期前完成项目。
The company hired several contractees to work on the new software. 公司雇佣了几个承包商来开发新软件。
近义词:contractor, employee, worker
反义词:employer
词性:noun
