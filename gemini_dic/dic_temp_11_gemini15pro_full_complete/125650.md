mummified:/ˈmʌmɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 木乃伊化的，干瘪的 | Preserved by embalming and wrapping as a mummy; shriveled. | The archaeologists discovered a mummified cat in the ancient Egyptian tomb.  考古学家在古埃及陵墓中发现了一只木乃伊化的猫。| The extreme heat mummified the fruit left on the counter.  极端的高温使留在柜台上的水果干瘪了。 | Synonyms: embalmed, preserved, desiccated, withered | Antonyms: fresh, living, moist, plump | word type: adjective

mummy:/ˈmʌmi/ | n. | 木乃伊 | A body embalmed and dried after death, as was the ancient Egyptian custom. | The museum exhibit featured several mummies from different cultures.  博物馆的展览展出了来自不同文化的几具木乃伊。 |  Synonyms: cadaver, corpse | Antonyms: living being | word type: noun
