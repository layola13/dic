non-hero:/ˌnɒnˈhɪərəʊ/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈhɪroʊ/ (US)| n.|非英雄;普通人;反英雄|A person who lacks the qualities traditionally associated with a hero, such as courage, bravery, or noble intentions. It can also refer to an ordinary person or a protagonist who displays flaws or acts in morally ambiguous ways.|He's the non-hero of this story, a regular guy caught in extraordinary circumstances. 他是这个故事里的非英雄，一个陷入非凡境遇的普通人。|The film explores the concept of the non-hero, a protagonist who is flawed and relatable.  这部电影探讨了非英雄的概念，一个有缺陷且让人产生共鸣的主角。|Synonyms: ordinary person, average Joe, anti-hero, unconventional hero|Antonyms: hero, champion, idol|Noun
