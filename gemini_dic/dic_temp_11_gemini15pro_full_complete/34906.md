cliquishness:/ˈkliːkɪʃnəs/| n.|小集团性，排外性|The tendency to form or associate in small, exclusive groups.|Her cliquishness made it difficult for new people to join the group. 她的排外性使新成员难以融入这个团体。|Example: The cliquishness of the club made it difficult for outsiders to join.  俱乐部的排外性使得外人难以加入。

Example:  The team suffered because of the cliquishness of some of the players.  由于一些球员的小集团主义，整个团队受到了影响。|Synonyms: exclusivity, clannishness, insularity|Antonyms: inclusiveness, openness, gregariousness|noun
