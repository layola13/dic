loaf's:/loʊfs/
n.
一个面包的；一条面包的
Possession form of "loaf," indicating ownership or belonging.
That's half of the loaf's size. 那是半个面包的大小。
The baker accidentally burned the loaf's crust. 面包师不小心烤焦了面包皮。
synonyms: loaf’s (no direct synonyms for possessive forms, consider "of the loaf," "belonging to the loaf")
