mangonism:/ˈmæŋɡoʊnɪzəm/| n. |性虐待狂，施虐狂|A psychological disorder characterized by an obsessive desire to inflict pain, humiliation, or suffering on others, often involving sexual gratification.  A form of sadism specifically related to inflicting pain or humiliation.|

例句：His mangonism manifested in his cruel treatment of animals. 他对动物的残忍虐待表现出他的施虐狂倾向。

例句：The detective suspected the killer's mangonism was the motive for the crime. 侦探怀疑凶手的性虐待狂是犯罪动机。


近义词：sadism, cruelty, brutality|
反义词: masochism, altruism|
词性: noun|

词源：From Late Latin *mangonismus, from mangonizare "to polish, to torture, to trick," from Greek manganon "war machine, rack, trick."|
