crash-lands:/ˈkræʃ lændz/
verb
坠毁着陆
To land roughly or suddenly, typically due to damage or malfunction.
The pilot skillfully crash-landed the damaged plane in a field.  飞行员熟练地将受损的飞机迫降在一块田地里。
The spacecraft crash-landed on Mars after a system failure.  航天器因系统故障在火星上坠毁着陆。
Synonyms: crash, ditch, force-land
Antonyms: land smoothly, take off
词性: 动词


crash-landing:/ˈkræʃ ˌlændɪŋ/
noun
坠毁着陆
An instance of landing roughly or suddenly, typically due to damage or malfunction.
The crash-landing of the plane was captured on video. 飞机的坠毁着陆被视频记录了下来。
The pilot managed a successful crash-landing despite the engine failure. 尽管发动机出现故障，飞行员还是成功地进行了坠毁着陆。
Synonyms: crash, ditching, forced landing
Antonyms: smooth landing, takeoff
词性: 名词
