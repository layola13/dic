evolvent:/ˈiːvəˌlɒnt/ | n. |  渐开线；展开线；渐伸线 | A curve traced by a point on a straight line as the line rolls without slipping along a fixed curve (especially a circle). | The gear teeth are designed based on the evolvent curve.  齿轮齿的设计是基于渐开线曲线。| involute | N/A | noun

evolvent:/ˈiːvəˌlɒnt/ | adj. |  展开的；渐开的 |  Unrolling or unfolding. |  The evolvent surface of the gear. 齿轮的渐开线表面。 | developed, unfolded, unrolled | involuted | adjective
