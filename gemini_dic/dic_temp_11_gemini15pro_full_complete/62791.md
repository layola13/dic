engrosser:/ɪnˈɡroʊsər/
n.
囤积者；垄断者；专心致志于…的人
A person or organization that buys up large quantities of goods, especially food, in order to control the price and make a profit; a person who is completely absorbed in something.
The engrossers bought up all the grain, causing a shortage in the market.  这些囤积者买断了所有的粮食，导致市场短缺。
He was such an engrosser of his work that he often forgot to eat. 他如此专注于他的工作，以至于经常忘记吃饭。
近义词:monopolist, hoarder, absorber
反义词:distributor, sharer
词性:名词
