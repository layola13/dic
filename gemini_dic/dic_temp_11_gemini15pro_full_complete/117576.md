megaliths: /ˈmɛɡəlɪθs/ | n. | 巨石阵, 巨石建筑 | A large prehistoric stone monument, typically one formed by a single massive stone or several stones placed together. |  Stonehenge is perhaps the most famous example of megaliths. 巨石阵也许是巨石建筑最著名的例子。|  The purpose of these megaliths remains a mystery. 这些巨石建筑的用途仍然是个谜。 | synonyms: monolith, henge, dolmen, menhir | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "small stones" in a specific context) | noun
