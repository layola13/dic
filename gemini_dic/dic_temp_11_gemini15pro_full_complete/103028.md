jejunum:/dʒɪˈdʒuːnəm/
n.
空肠
The middle portion of the small intestine, between the duodenum and the ileum.
Digestion and absorption of nutrients occur in the jejunum.  空肠是营养物质消化和吸收的场所。
The doctor found a blockage in the patient's jejunum. 医生发现病人的空肠有阻塞。
Synonym: midgut
Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific anatomical part)
