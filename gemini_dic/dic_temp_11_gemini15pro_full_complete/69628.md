feminist: /ˈfɛmɪnɪst/ (US), /ˈfɛmɪnɪst/ (UK)| n. adj.| 女权主义者；女权主义的|A person who supports the belief that women should have the same rights and opportunities as men.  Relating to or supporting feminism.|例句：She is a strong feminist and advocates for equal pay. 她是一位坚定的女权主义者，倡导同工同酬。|近义词：women's rights advocate, suffragist, egalitarian|反义词：anti-feminist, sexist|词性: noun, adjective

feminist: /ˈfɛmɪnɪst/ (US), /ˈfɛmɪnɪst/ (UK)| adj.| 女权主义的|Relating to or supporting feminism.|例句：She took a feminist perspective on the issue.  她从女权主义的角度看待这个问题。|近义词：pro-feminist, egalitarian|反义词：anti-feminist, sexist|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *féministe*, from *féminin* (“feminine”) + *-iste* (suffix forming agent nouns and adjectives). Ultimately from Latin *fēmina* (“woman”).
