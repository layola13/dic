appositionally:/ˌæpəˈzɪʃənəli/
adv.
同位地，同位语地
placed in apposition; as an apposition.  Placed side by side, often with a comma, semicolon, or colon, in order to explain or modify.
My friend, John, is a doctor. 我的朋友，约翰，是一个医生。
John, my friend, is a doctor.  约翰，我的朋友，是一个医生。
近义词: juxtaposedly, alongside
反义词: separately, disjointedly
词性: adverb
