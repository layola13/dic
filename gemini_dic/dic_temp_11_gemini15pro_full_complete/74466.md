frankensteining:/ˈfræŋkənˌstaɪnɪŋ/
|v.|（像弗兰肯斯坦一样）拼凑，粗制滥造|Creating something by combining disparate parts, often with a negative connotation of being unnatural or poorly made.|The director was accused of frankensteining the movie together from scenes of other films.|近义词：cobbling together, patching up, improvising|反义词： crafting, refining, perfecting|词性:动词

词源:
Derived from the name of Victor Frankenstein, the fictional scientist in Mary Shelley's novel *Frankenstein*, who created a monster from body parts of the deceased.
