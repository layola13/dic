hyperdrive:/ˈhaɪpərˌdraɪv/| n.|超光速引擎；超速驱动|A fictional propulsion system that allows spacecraft to travel faster than light.|The Millennium Falcon made the Kessel Run in less than twelve parsecs thanks to its hyperdrive. 千年隼号凭借其超光速引擎在不到十二秒差距的时间内完成了 Kessel Run。|例句：The hyperdrive malfunctioned, leaving them stranded in space. 超光速引擎发生故障，使他们滞留在太空中。

例句：Without a functioning hyperdrive, interstellar travel would be impossible. 没有功能正常的超光速引擎，星际旅行是不可能的。|近义词：warp drive, jump drive|反义词:None|词性:noun
