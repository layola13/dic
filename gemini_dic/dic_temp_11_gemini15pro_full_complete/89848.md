hornpipe:/ˈhɔːrnpaɪp/ (US) /ˈhɔːnpaɪp/ (UK)| n. |角笛舞; 角笛舞曲|A lively dance, traditionally popular with sailors, or the music for this dance.  Originally played on a hornpipe (a type of wind instrument).|The sailors danced a hornpipe on the deck. 水手们在甲板上跳起了角笛舞。|The band played a lively hornpipe. 乐队演奏了一首活泼的角笛舞曲。|近义词：jig, reel|反义词: - |名词
