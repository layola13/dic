hyperclimaxes:/ˌhaɪpərˈklaɪməksiːz/
|n.| (rhetoric)  层进式高潮; 超级高潮 |A sequence of climaxes, each more intense than the preceding one, leading to the ultimate culmination.  A series of escalating climaxes in a narrative or dramatic work. | The author employed a series of hyperclimaxes to build suspense throughout the novel.  作者在整部小说中运用了一系列层进式高潮来营造悬念。| climax, escalation|anticlimax, decline| noun
