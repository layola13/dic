communicable:/kəˈmjuːnɪkəbl/ /kəˈmjuːnɪkəbəl/
adjective
可传播的；传染的
Able to be transmitted from one person to another, typically by direct contact.
Measles is a highly communicable disease. 麻疹是一种高度传染的疾病。
The virus is highly communicable through airborne droplets. 这种病毒通过空气飞沫传播性极强。
近义词: contagious, infectious, transmissible
反义词: non-communicable, non-contagious
词性: adjective
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin communicabilis, from communicare ‘share, communicate’ (see communicate).
