bepommel:/bɪˈpɒməl/
verb
痛打，猛击
To beat or strike with repeated blows, especially with a pommel (the knob on the hilt of a sword or dagger).
The knight bepummeled his opponent with the pommel of his sword.  骑士用剑柄猛击他的对手。
beat, pummel, thrash
soothe, caress
v.
