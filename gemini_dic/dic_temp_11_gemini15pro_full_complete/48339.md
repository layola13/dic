decriminalizing: /diːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使合法化；非刑事化
The act of removing or reducing the criminal penalties associated with a particular action or behavior.
Portugal decriminalized drug use in 2001. 葡萄牙在2001年将吸毒非刑事化。
They are considering decriminalizing certain minor offenses. 他们正在考虑将某些轻微违法行为非刑事化。
synonyms: legalizing, legitimizing, authorizing
antonyms: criminalizing, prohibiting, banning
