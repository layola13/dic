overhomely: /ˌoʊvərˈhoʊmli/ | adj. | 过于朴素的，过于简单的；过分家常的 | Excessively plain or simple; too suggestive of home life, often to the point of being unattractive or uninteresting. | Her overhomely dress made her look out of place at the gala. 她过于朴素的着装让她在晚会上显得格格不入。|  The restaurant's decor was overhomely, bordering on shabby. 这家餐馆的装潢过于家常，近乎寒酸。 | 近义词: plain, simple, homely, unpretentious, dowdy | 反义词: stylish, fashionable, elegant, sophisticated, chic | 词性: 形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "homely" (adjective meaning "characteristic of home life").  "Homely" itself comes from Old English "hāmlic," meaning "belonging to the home."
