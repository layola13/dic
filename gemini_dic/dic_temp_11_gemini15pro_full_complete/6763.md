anamnesis:/ˌænæmˈniːsɪs/ | n. |回忆；回忆录；(尤指医疗)病历；既往症|The doctor took a thorough anamnesis from the patient before making a diagnosis. 医生在做出诊断之前详细询问了病人的病史。|例句：A detailed anamnesis is crucial for accurate diagnosis.  详细的病史对于准确诊断至关重要。

例句：The patient's anamnesis revealed a history of heart disease. 病人的病史显示他有心脏病史。|近义词：history, record, recollection, medical history|反义词: - |noun
