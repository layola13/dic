approvednesses:/əˈpruːvɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
批准；认可；赞同
The state of being approved; approvals.  This word is very rarely used.
His approvednesses for the project were finally given. 他对这个项目的批准最终被给予了。
近义词:approvals, sanctions, endorsements
反义词:disapprovals, rejections, condemnations
词性:名词

Etymology:
From approved + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives, expressing state, quality, or condition).  "Approved" itself comes from the verb "approve," which originates from the Old French "aprover," and ultimately from the Latin "approbare" (to prove, test, approve).
