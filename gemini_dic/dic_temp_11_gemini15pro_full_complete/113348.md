luxuriously: /lʌɡˈʒʊəriəsli/ | adv. | 奢侈地，豪华地 |  In a rich and lavish manner; with great comfort and extravagance.  Characterized by luxury or opulence. | She sighed contentedly, stretching out luxuriously on the soft sofa. 她心满意足地叹了口气，舒舒服服地躺在柔软的沙发上。|  The hotel suite was luxuriously appointed with silk drapes and antique furniture. 酒店套房布置豪华，配有丝绸窗帘和古董家具。| 近义词：opulently, lavishly, sumptuously, extravagantly | 反义词：simply, plainly, austerely, modestly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From the Latin word "luxuria" meaning "excess, abundance," which became the Old French "luxurie" and then the English "luxury."  The adverbial form "luxuriously" is formed by adding the suffix "-ously."
