crapshoot:/ˈkræpʃuːt/| n.| 赌博，冒险，碰运气的事情|A situation in which the outcome is very uncertain; a gamble. |例句：Starting a new business can be a real crapshoot.  创业就像是一场赌博。

例句：The election is a crapshoot, with neither candidate having a clear advantage.  这次选举胜负难料，两位候选人都没有明显的优势。|近义词：gamble, risk, lottery, long shot|反义词:sure thing, certainty|词性:名词
