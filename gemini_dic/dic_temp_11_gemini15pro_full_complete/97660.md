inhibitor:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtər/| n. |抑制剂，抑制物|A substance that slows down or prevents a particular chemical reaction or other process, or that reduces the activity of a particular enzyme.|Enzyme inhibitors can be used to treat various diseases. 酶抑制剂可以用来治疗各种疾病。|synonyms: suppressant, repressor, retarder|antonyms: activator, catalyst, stimulant|noun

inhibitors:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtərz/| n. |抑制剂，抑制物（inhibitor 的复数形式）|The plural form of inhibitor, referring to multiple substances that slow down or prevent chemical reactions or processes.|Many enzyme inhibitors are used in medicine. 许多酶抑制剂用于医学。|synonyms: suppressants, repressors, retarders|antonyms: activators, catalysts, stimulants|noun
