ETYMOLOGY->
From dairy + woman. "Dairy" comes from Middle English *deirie*, from Old French *deyerie* meaning "dairy," ultimately from Old Norse *deyja* meaning "female servant, milkmaid." "Woman" comes from Old English *wīfmann*, meaning "female human being."
