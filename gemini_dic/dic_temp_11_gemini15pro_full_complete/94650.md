impressionisms:/ɪmˈprɛʃənɪzəmz/
n.
印象主义（作品），印象派（作品）
The style or movement of Impressionism, characterized by a concern with depicting the visual impression of the moment, especially in terms of the shifting effect of light and color.
Her paintings are characterized by vibrant colors and loose brushstrokes, typical of Impressionism. 她的画作以鲜艳的色彩和奔放的笔触为特征，是典型的印象派风格。
synonyms: Impressionist works, Impressionist art
antonyms: Realism, Classicism
noun
