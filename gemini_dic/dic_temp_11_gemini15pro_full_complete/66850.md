expectancies: /ɪkˈspektənsiz/
n.
预期；期望；期待值
The state of thinking or hoping that something, especially something pleasant, will happen or be the case.
Their expectancies about the future were very high. 他们对未来的期望值很高。
Synonyms: hopes, anticipations
Antonyms: despair, hopelessness
