intraparietal:/ˌɪntrəpəˈraɪɪtəl/| adj.|顶骨内的，顶叶内的|Located within the parietal lobe of the brain, which is involved in processing sensory information, spatial awareness, and navigation.|The intraparietal sulcus plays a crucial role in visually guided reaching and grasping. 顶内沟在视觉引导的伸手和抓取中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词: parietal|反义词: extraparietal (not commonly used)|词性: adjective

Etymology:
intra- (Latin): within, inside
parietal (Latin parietalis, from paries "wall"): relating to the wall of a body cavity
