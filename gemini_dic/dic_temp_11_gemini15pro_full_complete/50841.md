desecrates:/ˈdɛsɪkreɪts/
verb
亵渎，玷污
To treat (a sacred place or thing) with violent disrespect; violate.
The vandals desecrated the church by spray-painting graffiti on the altar.  那些故意破坏公物者在祭坛上喷涂涂鸦，亵渎了教堂。
desecrate:  v.
玷污, 亵渎
The soldiers desecrated the temple. 士兵们亵渎了这座寺庙。


synonyms: profanes, violates, defiles
antonyms:  consecrates, sanctifies, reveres
词性: 动词
