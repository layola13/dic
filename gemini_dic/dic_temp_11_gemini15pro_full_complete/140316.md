oglio: /ˈɔʎo/ | n. | oil, olive oil | A greasy liquid obtained from olives, nuts, seeds, or other sources, used in cooking, cosmetics, and other products. |  I use olive oil in my salad dressing. 我在我的沙拉酱里用橄榄油。| synonyms: lubricant, grease, fat | antonyms: water, solid | noun

Etymology: From Italian *oglio*, from Latin *oleum*, ultimately from Ancient Greek *ἔλαιον (élaion)*, meaning "olive oil."
