maladies:/ˈmælədiz/ | n. | 疾病，弊病 | diseases or ailments, especially those that are chronic or serious |  The country suffers from a host of social and economic maladies. 这个国家遭受着一系列社会和经济弊病。 | 近义词: illnesses, diseases, ailments, disorders, afflictions | 反义词: health, wellness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Old French *maladie "illness, disease," from malade "ill, sick," from Latin male habitus "in a bad condition, badly kept," from male "badly, ill" + habitus "condition."
