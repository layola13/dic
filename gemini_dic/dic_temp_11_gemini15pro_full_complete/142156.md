horizon:/həˈraɪzn/ | n. | 地平线；眼界；范围 | The sun dipped below the horizon. 太阳落到了地平线以下。|  The company needs to expand its horizons.  这家公司需要拓展视野。 | synonyms: skyline, vista, outlook, perspective | antonyms: nadir | noun

词源：From Old French *orizon (Modern French horizon), from Late Latin horizontem, from Ancient Greek ὁρίζων (horízōn, “limiting”), present participle of ὁρίζω (horízō, “I limit, define, separate”), from ὅρος (hóros, “boundary, limit, landmark”).
