gobbledegook:/ˈɡɒbldiɡuːk/ (US) /ˈɡɒbldɪɡuːk/ (UK)| n. |胡言乱语；官样文章；晦涩难懂的话|Unintelligible jargon; nonsensical or overly complex language. |The contract was filled with legal gobbledegook.  合同里充斥着法律术语。|Synonyms: jargon, gibberish, nonsense, doublespeak, mumbo jumbo|Antonyms: clarity, plain English, straightforwardness|noun
