adulate:/ˈædjʊleɪt/| v. |奉承；谄媚；过分称赞|To praise or admire excessively or servilely. |例句：He adulates her constantly, hoping for a promotion. 他不停地奉承她，希望能升职。

例句：The fans adulates their favorite singers. 粉丝们狂热地追捧他们最喜欢的歌手。

近义词：flatter, fawn (on/over), praise excessively, overpraise, worship, idolize|反义词：criticize, condemn, disparage|词性：动词
