arresters:/əˈrɛstərz/
noun
避雷器；阻止器；制动器
A device used to protect electrical equipment from voltage surges, especially lightning strikes.  It limits the voltage across an electrical device by diverting or absorbing excess current.
The arresters on the power lines protected the transformers from the lightning strike. 电力线上的避雷器保护变压器免受雷击。
synonyms: surge protector, lightning arrester, surge diverter
antonyms: conductor, amplifier
