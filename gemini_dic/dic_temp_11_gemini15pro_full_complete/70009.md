fetid:/ˈfetɪd/ /ˈfiːtɪd/| adj.|恶臭的，腐臭的|Having an offensive smell; stinking.|The fetid air of the swamp made it difficult to breathe. 沼泽的恶臭空气令人难以呼吸。|Synonyms: foul, putrid, rank, noisome, malodorous|Antonyms: fragrant, sweet-smelling, aromatic|adjective

Etymology->
From Latin *fetidus*, from *fētēre* "to stink".
