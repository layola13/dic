moribund:/ˈmɔːrɪbʌnd/ | adj. | 垂死的，临终的；即将结束的 |  In a dying state; near death; about to die. Also, in a state of inactivity or obsolescence; near an end. | The moribund patient was barely clinging to life.  垂死的病人几乎无法维持生命。 | The moribund economy showed no signs of recovery.  垂死的经济没有复苏的迹象。 | 近义词: dying, expiring, failing, stagnant | 反义词: thriving, flourishing, vibrant, healthy | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *moribundus*, present participle of *morī* (“to die”).
