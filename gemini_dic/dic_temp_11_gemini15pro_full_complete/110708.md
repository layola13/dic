linebacker: /ˈlaɪnˌbækər/ | n. | 中后卫，线卫 | A defensive position in American football, usually positioned behind the defensive linemen and in front of the defensive backs.  The linebacker's responsibilities include tackling ball carriers, covering receivers, and rushing the passer. |  The linebacker made a crucial interception. 中后卫完成了一次关键的拦截。

The linebacker blitzed the quarterback.  线卫突袭了四分卫。| 近义词: defender, tackler | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "offensive player") | 词性: noun
