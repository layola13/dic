accommodatingly: /əˈkɒmədeɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 乐于助人地，通融地 | In a way that is willing to fit in with someone's wishes or needs. | She accommodatingly offered to drive us to the airport. 她乐于助人地提出开车送我们去机场。|  The hotel staff accommodatingly changed our room when we complained about the noise. 当我们抱怨噪音时，酒店工作人员通融地为我们换了房间。| 近义词: obligingly, helpfully, kindly, graciously | 反义词: unhelpfully, inconsiderately | 词性: 副词

词源:  From the verb "accommodate," which comes from the Latin "accommodare," meaning "to make fit, adapt."  The "-ing" suffix creates the present participle, and the "-ly" suffix transforms it into an adverb.
