manchineel:/mænʃəˈniːl/ | n. |  马疯树，毒番石榴 | An evergreen tree (Hippomane mancinella) of the spurge family, native to Florida, the Caribbean, and parts of Central and South America.  It has milky sap that is extremely poisonous and irritating to the skin. Its small apple-like fruit is also toxic. |  The manchineel tree is considered one of the most dangerous trees in the world. 马疯树被认为是世界上最危险的树之一。|  The manchineel's fruit can cause severe blistering and burning if ingested. 如果摄入马疯树的果实，会导致严重的起泡和灼伤。 | Synonyms: beach apple (though this can be misleading), poison guava | Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific tree species. Could use "safe tree" or "harmless tree" for contrasting meaning) | Noun
