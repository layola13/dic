invitor:/ɪnˈvaɪtər/ | n. |邀请人；召集人|A person who invites someone to something. |例句：The invitor of the party welcomed each guest personally.  聚会的邀请人亲自迎接每位客人。

例句：As the invitor, she felt responsible for the success of the event. 作为邀请人，她觉得有责任确保活动的成功。|近义词：host, hostess |反义词: guest, invitee |词性: noun

Etymology: From the Latin word "invitare" meaning "to invite". The suffix "-or" denotes the agent or person performing the action.
