anti-patriot:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriət/ |n.|反爱国者，不爱国的人|Someone who is opposed to their own country, especially one who betrays it.| He was branded an anti-patriot for protesting the war. 他因为抗议战争而被打上了反爱国者的烙印。|Synonyms: traitor, dissident, defector|Antonyms: patriot, nationalist|Noun


anti-patriot:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriət/ |adj.|反爱国的，不爱国的|Opposed to one's own country, especially in a way that betrays it.| His anti-patriot sentiments led him to leak classified information. 他的反爱国情绪导致他泄露了机密信息。|Synonyms: treacherous, unpatriotic, disloyal|Antonyms: patriotic, loyal|Adjective
