beadle:/ˈbiːdl/ | n. |  小吏；执法吏；教堂司事 | A minor parish official who performs various civic duties, such as keeping order or acting as a messenger. Historically, a beadle also acted as a crier or usher.  |  The beadle led the procession into the church. 教堂司事带领队伍进入教堂。|  The beadle kept a close eye on the crowd during the festival. 节日期间，执法吏密切关注着人群。 | Synonyms: officer, attendant, official, warden, verger | Antonyms:  lawbreaker, criminal | noun
