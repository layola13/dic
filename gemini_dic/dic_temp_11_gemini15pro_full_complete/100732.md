intransgressible:/ˌɪntrænsˈɡrɛsɪbl/| adj.|不可逾越的，不可侵犯的|Not able to be transgressed or violated; inviolable.|例句：The intransgressible laws of nature. 不可逾越的自然法则。|例句：An intransgressible moral code. 不可侵犯的道德准则。|近义词：inviolable, unbreakable, impassable|反义词：transgressible, violable, breakable|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *intransgressibilis, from Latin in- "not" + transgressus, past participle of transgredi "to step across, go beyond," from trans- "across" + gradi "to step, walk."
