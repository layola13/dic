copresidents:/ˌkoʊˈprɛzɪdənts/ | n. | 共同主席；联合主席 | Two or more individuals who share the duties and responsibilities of a president. |  The company has two copresidents who share the leadership responsibilities.  这家公司有两名共同主席，共同承担领导责任。 | The copresidents signed the agreement together. 联合主席共同签署了协议。 | synonyms: co-chairs, joint presidents | antonyms: subordinate, vice president | noun
