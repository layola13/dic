defragmentation:/diːˌfræɡmənˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 磁盘碎片整理，碎片整理 | The process of reorganizing files on a hard drive so that they are stored contiguously, improving performance. | After defragmentation, my computer runs much faster. 磁盘碎片整理后，我的电脑运行速度快多了。 |  synonyms: consolidation, optimization | antonyms: fragmentation | noun
