afterthoughts:/ˈæftərθɔːts/| n.|事后想法，后来的想法，补充的想法|An afterthought is something that you think of or mention later, after you have finished dealing with the main subject.|I added a brief thank you as an afterthought. 我事后又简短地加了一句感谢。|synonyms: reconsideration, second thought, hindsight|antonyms: forethought, premeditation|noun

afterthought:/ˈæftərθɔːts/| n.|事后想法，后来的想法，补充的想法|Something that is added or done later, often as a supplement to something else that is regarded as more important.|The patio was clearly an afterthought.露台显然是后来才加建的。|synonyms: addendum, supplement, appendix|antonyms: original plan, primary design|noun
