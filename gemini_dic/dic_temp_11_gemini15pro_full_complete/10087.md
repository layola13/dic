apod:/ˈæpɒd/ (UK) /ˈeɪpɑːd/ (US)| n. |无足动物；无肢动物|An animal without legs or feet, especially an invertebrate. |例句：The earthworm is an apod. 蚯蚓是一种无足动物。

例句：Some lizards have evolved into apods. 一些蜥蜴进化成了无足动物。|近义词：legless animal, limbless animal|反义词: tetrapod (四足动物)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀ- (a-, “without”) + ποδ- (pod-, “foot”).
