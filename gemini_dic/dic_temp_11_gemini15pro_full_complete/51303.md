ETYMOLOGY->
From detailed + -ness.  "Detailed" comes from the Old French "detaillier" meaning "to cut in pieces," from "de-" (down, from) + "tailler" (to cut).  The "-ness" suffix transforms an adjective into a noun denoting a state or quality.
