gritty:/ˈɡrɪti/| adj.|坚韧不拔的；有砂砾的；粗糙的|Showing courage and resolve in a demanding situation; containing or resembling grit; coarse in texture.|The team showed a gritty performance in the face of adversity.  这支队伍在逆境中表现出坚韧不拔的精神。|The beach was covered in gritty sand.  海滩上覆盖着粗糙的沙子。|近义词：courageous, determined, resolute, sandy, grainy, coarse|反义词：cowardly, weak, smooth, fine|词性:形容词

词源: From *grit* (small hard particles) + *-y* (forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *greutaną* (to grind).
