cirrhosis: /sɪˈroʊsɪs/ (US), /sɪˈrəʊsɪs/ (UK)| n. |肝硬化|A chronic disease of the liver marked by degeneration of cells, inflammation, and fibrous thickening of tissue. It is typically a result of alcoholism or hepatitis.|例句：The patient's cirrhosis was caused by years of heavy drinking.  这位病人的肝硬化是由多年的酗酒引起的。

例句：Cirrhosis of the liver is a serious condition that can lead to liver failure. 肝硬化是一种严重的疾病，可导致肝衰竭。


近义词：liver damage, hepatic fibrosis|反义词: healthy liver, normal liver function|词性: noun
