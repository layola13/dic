hobbler:/ˈhɑːblər/ (US), /ˈhɒblər/ (UK)| n.| 修补匠；笨手笨脚的工人；做…做得不好的人；业余爱好者|A person who mends or repairs things clumsily; a person who does something poorly or inefficiently; a person who pursues a hobby.|He's a bit of a hobbler when it comes to DIY.  他在DIY方面有点笨手笨脚。|Synonyms: tinkerer, amateur, dabbler, hobbyist|Antonyms: professional, expert|noun
