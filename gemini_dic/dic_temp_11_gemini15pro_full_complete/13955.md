auralities:/ɔːrˈælɪtiz/
n.
听觉特性；可听性；音质
The qualities of sound; the perceptible aspects of sound.
The composer explored the auralities of different instruments.  这位作曲家探索了不同乐器的听觉特性。
The auralities of the recording were distorted.  录音的音质失真了。
Synonyms: sounds, acoustics, tones, timbres
Antonyms: silence, quiet
noun
