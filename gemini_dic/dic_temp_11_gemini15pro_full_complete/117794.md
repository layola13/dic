melilite:/ˈmɛləˌlaɪt/ | n. |黄长石|A mineral of the melilite group, a sorosilicate of calcium, sodium, aluminium, magnesium, and iron, occurring as yellowish or brownish tetragonal crystals in igneous rocks. |The melilite in this rock sample is unusually clear. 这块岩石样本中的黄长石异常透明。|Synonyms:  gehlenite, åkermanite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
