identism: /aɪˈdɛntɪzəm/
n.|认同主义，身份认同政治|A political approach that focuses on the experiences and perspectives of people based on their shared identities, such as race, gender, or sexual orientation. It often emphasizes the importance of recognizing and addressing systemic inequalities and discrimination.|例句：Identism can be a powerful force for social change. 认同主义可以成为社会变革的强大力量。

例句：Critics argue that identism can be divisive and lead to a focus on differences rather than common ground. 批评人士认为，认同主义可能造成分裂，并导致人们关注差异而不是共同点。|近义词：identity politics,  social justice activism|反义词：universalism, colorblindness|词性: noun
