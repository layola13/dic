appealers:/əˈpiːlərz/
noun
上诉人，申诉人
Those who make an appeal; people who formally request that a decision be changed.
The appealers argued that the judge's decision was unfair. 上诉人辩称法官的判决不公。
The appealers hoped the higher court would overturn the verdict.  上诉人希望高等法院能推翻原判。
synonyms: petitioners, applicants, appellants
antonyms: respondents, defendants
n.
