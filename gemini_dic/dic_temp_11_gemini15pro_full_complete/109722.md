levelism:/ˈlɛvəlɪzəm/
n.
平均主义;平等主义
The theory or practice of seeking equality, especially in the distribution of wealth and power.
He advocated for levelism in education, believing all children should have equal opportunities. 他提倡教育平等主义，认为所有儿童都应该有平等的机会。
synonyms: egalitarianism, equality, fairness
antonyms: elitism, hierarchy, inequality
noun
