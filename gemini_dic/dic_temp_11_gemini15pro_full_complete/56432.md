dollishness:/ˈdɑːlɪʃnəs/ /ˈdɒlɪʃnəs/| n.|幼稚; 娃娃气; 像玩具娃娃的特性|The quality of resembling or behaving like a doll, often in a childish or innocent way.  Characterized by a delicate, pretty, or slightly artificial appearance and manner. |She maintained a certain dollishness throughout her life, even into adulthood. 她一生都保持着某种娃娃气，即使到了成年也是如此。|Her dollishness, with her wide eyes and innocent smile, made her seem younger than she was.  她那双大眼睛和天真的笑容，让她看起来比实际年龄更小。|近义词：childishness, naiveté, innocence, immaturity, artlessness|反义词：maturity, sophistication, worldliness|词性:noun
