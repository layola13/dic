fathomed:/ˈfæðəmd/ | v. | 理解；了解；探测(深度) |  To understand something completely; to measure the depth of water. |  I couldn't fathom his motives. 我无法理解他的动机。| Synonyms: understand, comprehend, grasp, gauge | Antonyms: misunderstand, misinterpret | verb

fathomable:/ˈfæðəməbl/ | adj. | 可理解的；可测量的 | Capable of being understood; capable of being measured. |  Her behavior was perfectly fathomable. 她的行为完全可以理解。 | Synonyms: understandable, comprehensible, graspable | Antonyms: unfathomable, incomprehensible | adjective

fathomless:/ˈfæðəmləs/ | adj. | 深不可测的；无底的；难以理解的 | Too deep to be measured; impossible to understand. |  The ocean's depths are fathomless. 海洋的深度是深不可测的。 | Synonyms: unfathomable, immeasurable, bottomless | Antonyms: fathomable, measurable, shallow | adjective
