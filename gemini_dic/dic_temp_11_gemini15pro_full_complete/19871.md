binges:/ˈbɪndʒɪz/| n.| 放纵，狂欢；狂热行为|A period of excessive indulgence in an activity, especially eating, drinking, or taking drugs.|She went on a shopping binge and spent all her savings. 她疯狂购物，花光了所有积蓄。|近义词：sprees, excesses, indulgences|反义词: moderation, abstinence| noun

binges:/ˈbɪndʒɪz/| v. (binges, binging, binged)|  放纵，狂欢；狂热地做|To indulge in an activity, especially eating, drinking, or taking drugs, excessively.|He binged on ice cream after the breakup.  分手后他狂吃冰淇淋。|近义词：overindulge, gorge, revel|反义词: abstain, moderate| verb
