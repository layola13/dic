left-out:/ˌleft ˈaʊt/| adj.|被遗漏的；被冷落的|Feeling left out is a common experience, especially in new social situations.  被冷落是一种常见的体验，尤其是在新的社交场合。|例句：The shy child felt left out at the birthday party.  这个害羞的孩子在生日聚会上感到被冷落。

left-out:/ˌleft ˈaʊt/| adj.|被排除在外的|He felt left out when his friends went to the concert without him. 当他的朋友们没有带他去音乐会时，他感到被排除在外。|近义词：excluded, omitted, neglected, overlooked|反义词:included, involved|词性:形容词
