martyrdom:/ˈmɑːrtərdəm/ | n. |殉难，牺牲|The state of being a martyr; the suffering or death of a martyr. |Joan of Arc was burned at the stake, achieving martyrdom for her faith.  圣女贞德被火刑处死，为她的信仰殉难。|  synonyms: sacrifice, immolation, self-sacrifice | antonyms: survival, deliverance | noun

martyrdom:/ˈmɑːrtərdəm/ | n. |（为事业或信仰的）巨大痛苦，折磨|Great suffering or anguish endured for a cause or belief. |He endured years of professional martyrdom before finally achieving recognition. 他在最终获得认可之前，忍受了多年的职业折磨。 | synonyms: ordeal, torment, suffering | antonyms: comfort, ease, pleasure | noun
