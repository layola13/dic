acutangular:/ˌæˌkjuːˈtæŋɡjʊlər/ | adj. | 锐角的 | Having three acute angles (less than 90 degrees).  A triangle where all angles are less than 90 degrees is acutangular. |  The architect designed a building with an acutangular roof.  这位建筑师设计了一座带有锐角屋顶的建筑。

The crystal structure is acutangular, giving it a unique appearance. 这种晶体结构是锐角的，赋予它独特的外观。| 近义词: acute-angled | 反义词: obtuse-angled, right-angled | 词性: 形容词
