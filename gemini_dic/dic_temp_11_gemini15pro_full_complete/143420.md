outflatter: /aʊtˈflætər/ | v. | 过分奉承，胜过…的奉承 | To flatter someone more excessively than another person does. |  He outflattered all the other suitors vying for the heiress's attention. 他对那位女继承人的奉承远远超过了其他追求者。|  She outflattered her rivals to gain the favor of the boss. 她为了获得老板的青睐，对竞争对手极尽奉承之能事。 | 近义词: outdo, surpass, eclipse | 反义词: understate, downplay | 词性: 动词

词源: out- + flatter

out-:  prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing," "external," "outside," "away from," "thoroughly," "completely." 表示“超过”、“胜过”、“外部”、“外面”、“远离”、“彻底”、“完全”。

flatter:  from Old Norse *flatra "to stroke, caress," from Proto-Germanic *flatojanan (source also of Old English floterian "be agitated on the surface," Middle Dutch vlotteren "to float").  来自古挪威语*flatra“抚摸，爱抚”，源于原始日耳曼语*flatojanan（也是古英语floterian“在表面上激动”、中古荷兰语vlotteren“漂浮”的来源）。
