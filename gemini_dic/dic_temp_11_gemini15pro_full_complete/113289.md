lutescent:/luːˈtesənt/
adjective
浅黄色的，略带黄色的
Becoming yellowish; pale yellow.
The lutescent leaves of the dying tree signaled the arrival of autumn. 秋天临近，垂死的树长出了淡黄色的叶子。

The fungus developed a lutescent hue as it matured.  随着成熟，真菌呈现出浅黄色。

yellowish, pale yellow, straw-colored
