dissertate:/ˈdɪsərˌteɪt/| v.| 详细论述，撰写论文 |to speak or write formally and at length about a subject| He disserted at length on the history of the region. 他详细论述了该地区的历史。|近义词：discuss, expound, elaborate|反义词:summarize, condense|词性:动词
dissertation:/ˌdɪsərˈteɪʃən/| n.| 专题论文，学位论文| a long essay on a particular subject, especially one written as a requirement for the degree of doctor of philosophy.|  She wrote her dissertation on the poetry of Sylvia Plath. 她写了一篇关于西尔维娅·普拉斯诗歌的学位论文。|近义词：thesis, treatise, essay|反义词:outline, abstract|词性:名词
