gizmo:/ˈɡɪzməʊ/ (US) /ˈɡɪzməʊ/ (UK)| n.|小玩意儿；小装置；小机械|a gadget, especially a mechanical one whose name is unknown or forgotten|例句：He showed me a new gizmo he'd bought for his car. 他给我看他为他的车买的新玩意儿。

例句：This gizmo is supposed to slice vegetables quickly.  这个小装置应该可以快速切蔬菜。

近义词：gadget, widget, contraption, device, apparatus|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "basic tool" or "simple implement")|词性:noun
