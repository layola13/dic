osteodystrophy: /ˌɒstiəʊˈdɪstrəfi/ /ˌɑːstioʊˈdɪstrəfi/
| n. | 骨营养不良 | Defective bone formation or calcification, frequently associated with disturbances in calcium and phosphorus metabolism and often caused by chronic kidney disease. | Renal osteodystrophy is a common complication of chronic kidney disease. 肾性骨营养不良是慢性肾病的常见并发症。|  近义词: osteopathy, bone disease | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "healthy bone development") | 词性: noun
