billeting:/ˈbɪlɪtɪŋ/
n.
提供住宿; 驻扎; 宿舍
The act of assigning lodging to soldiers.  The provision of lodging. Accommodation provided in this way.
The soldiers were billeted in the town hall. 士兵们被安排住在市政厅。
The billeting of troops in private homes caused resentment among the locals.  军队驻扎在私人住宅中引起了当地居民的不满。
lodging, accommodation, housing
