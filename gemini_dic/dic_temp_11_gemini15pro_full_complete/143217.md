outcull:/ˌaʊtˈkʌl/
|v.|剔除，淘汰|To remove or select something, typically something inferior or undesirable, from a larger group.|The farmer outculled the weakest lambs from the flock. 农民从羊群中剔除了最弱的小羊。|近义词: cull, reject, eliminate, weed out|反义词: select, choose, retain|词性: 动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "out, away") + "cull" (verb meaning "to select from a large quantity; to choose; to pick").  The "out-" prefix emphasizes the removal aspect of the selection.
