mathematic:/ˌmæθəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 数学的；精确的 | Relating to or of the nature of mathematics. |  Mathematics is a complex subject. 数学是一门复杂的学科。 |  This calculation requires mathematical precision.  这个计算需要数学上的精确性。| 近义词: numerical, quantitative | 反义词: imprecise, inaccurate | 词性: 形容词

mathematics:/ˌmæθəˈmætɪks/ | n. | 数学 | The abstract science of number, quantity, and space. | Mathematics is the queen of sciences. 数学是科学女王。 | She excels in mathematics. 她擅长数学。 | 近义词:  arithmetic, calculation | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词


词源：From Late Latin *mathematica, from neuter plural of Late Latin mathematicus “mathematical,” from Greek mathematikos “disposed to learn,” from manthanein “to learn.”
