nonsubjects:/ˈnɒnˈsʌbdʒɪkts/ |n. |非主题，非科目|People who are nonsubjects in a particular discussion or field of study are not directly involved or affected.  Those who are nonsubjects do not have a say in the matter.  It can also refer to topics or areas that are not the main focus of attention or study.| The decision about the new curriculum only affects students; parents and teachers are nonsubjects in this matter.  | 近义词: outsiders, bystanders, irrelevant parties | 反义词: subjects, participants, stakeholders | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "subjects" (plural noun meaning "topics of study, discussion, or consideration; or people under the authority or control of another").
