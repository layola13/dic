mournful:/ˈmɔːrnfʊl/ (US) /ˈmɔːnfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|悲哀的，悲伤的|Feeling or expressing sorrow or grief.|例句：The mournful sound of the bagpipes filled the air.  风笛悲哀的声音弥漫在空气中。

例句：She wore a mournful expression throughout the funeral.  在整个葬礼过程中，她都带着悲伤的表情。

近义词：sad, sorrowful, grieving, doleful, plaintive|反义词：happy, joyful, cheerful, celebratory|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *mornful, from Old English *mōrnfull, equivalent to mōrn (“mourning”) +‎ -full (“-ful”).
