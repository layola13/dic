lothario:/ləˈθɑːrioʊ/
noun
花花公子，浪荡子
a man who behaves selfishly and irresponsibly in his sexual relationships with women
例句：He was known as a bit of a lothario in his younger days. 他年轻时有点花花公子的名声。

例句：The play features a charming but unscrupulous lothario who seduces and abandons several women.  这部戏剧的主角是一位迷人但肆无忌惮的花花公子，他勾引并抛弃了几个女人。

近义词：womanizer, philanderer, playboy, rake, seducer
反义词：faithful husband, loyal partner
词性: n.

词源: From the character Lothario in Nicholas Rowe's play *The Fair Penitent* (1703), adapted from *The Impious in Love*, a play by the Spanish dramatist Felipe Godínez. Lothario in the play is a charming but unscrupulous seducer.
