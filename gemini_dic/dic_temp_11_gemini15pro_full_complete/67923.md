facelift: /ˈfeɪslɪft/ (US) /ˈfeɪslɪft/ (UK)
n.
面部拉皮手术；翻新，改进
A cosmetic surgical operation to remove wrinkles and other signs of aging from the face; an act of improving the appearance of something, especially a building.
例句：She had a facelift to look younger. 她做了面部拉皮手术，让自己看起来更年轻。

例句：The hotel underwent a major facelift. 这家酒店进行了大规模翻新。

近义词：renovation, improvement, overhaul
反义词：decline, deterioration
词性: 名词

facelift: /ˈfeɪslɪft/ (US) /ˈfeɪslɪft/ (UK)
v.
给…做面部拉皮手术；翻新，改进
To give a facelift to something.
例句：They're planning to facelift the entire building. 他们计划翻新整栋楼。

例句：The surgeon facelifted her to remove the wrinkles. 外科医生给她做了面部拉皮手术去皱。

近义词：renovate, improve, overhaul
反义词：damage, neglect
词性: 动词
