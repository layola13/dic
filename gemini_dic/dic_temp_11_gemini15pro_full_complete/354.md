abide-by:/əˈbaɪd baɪ/| v.|遵守，遵循|To accept or follow a rule, decision, or agreement.|例句：Both parties agreed to abide by the judge's decision. 双方同意遵守法官的裁决。|例句：They promised to abide by the terms of the contract. 他们承诺遵守合同条款。|近义词：observe, comply with, follow, adhere to|反义词：disobey, violate, breach, ignore|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abiden bi*, from *abiden* (to abide) + *bi* (by).  *Abiden* is from Old English *ābīdan* (to wait, remain, endure), from *ā-* (intensive prefix) + *bīdan* (to wait).
