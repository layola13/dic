mousebutton:/ˈmaʊs ˌbʌtn/| n.|鼠标按键|A button on a computer mouse that is pressed to perform a specific action.|例句：I clicked the left mouse button to open the file. 我点击鼠标左键打开了文件。

例句：The right mouse button usually brings up a context menu. 右键通常会弹出上下文菜单。|近义词：mouse key|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
