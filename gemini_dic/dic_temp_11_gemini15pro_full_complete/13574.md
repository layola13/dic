attendingly:/əˈtɛndɪŋli/| adv.|专心地，聚精会神地|In a manner that suggests one is paying close attention.  It implies attentiveness and careful observation.|She listened attendingly to the speaker, absorbing every word. 她专心地听着演讲者讲话，吸收着每一个字。|近义词：attentively, carefully, heedfully|反义词：inattentively, carelessly, distractedly|词性:副词
词源: From attend + -ing + -ly. Attend comes from the Latin attendere, meaning "to stretch toward, give heed to."
