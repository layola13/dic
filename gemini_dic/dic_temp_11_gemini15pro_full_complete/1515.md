acetonuria:/ˌæsɪtəˈnjʊəriə/
n.
酮尿症
The presence of acetone bodies in the urine.
Example: The doctor diagnosed acetonuria after analyzing the patient's urine sample. 医生在分析病人的尿样后诊断出酮尿症。

Example: Acetonuria can be a sign of diabetes. 酮尿症可能是糖尿病的征兆。

近义词: ketonuria
反义词: (None, absence of a condition doesn't have a specific antonym)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From aceto- + -uria.  Aceto- comes from Latin acētum ("vinegar"), referring to the smell of acetone, which is similar to vinegar.  -uria is a suffix denoting a condition of the urine.
