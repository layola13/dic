keratode:/ˈkɛrətəˌdoʊd/| n. |角膜刀|A surgical knife used for making incisions in the cornea.|例句：The ophthalmologist used a keratode to perform the corneal incision. 眼科医生使用角膜刀进行角膜切开术。

例句：A diamond keratode is often preferred for its precision. 金刚石角膜刀因其精确性而常被首选。|近义词：corneal knife, corneal trephine|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kerato- (horn, cornea) + -tome (cutting instrument).
