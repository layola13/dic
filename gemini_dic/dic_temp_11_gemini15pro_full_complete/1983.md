actor:/ˈæktər/ (US), /ˈæktə(r)/ (UK)| n.|演员|A person who performs in plays, movies, or television shows.|例句：He is a famous actor. 他是一位著名的演员。

例句：The actor delivered a powerful performance. 这位演员的表演很有感染力。

近义词：performer, player, artist, thespian|反义词: audience, spectator|词性:noun
