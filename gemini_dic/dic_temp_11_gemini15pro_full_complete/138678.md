nugatory:/ˈnjuːɡətɔːri/| adj.|无价值的，无效的，琐碎的|Of no value or importance; trifling; insignificant.  This word often describes laws, agreements, or actions that are ineffective or have no practical impact.|The treaty became nugatory after the war broke out.  该条约在战争爆发后变得无效。|Synonyms: worthless, futile, trivial, inconsequential, ineffective|Antonyms: valuable, significant, effective, important|adjective

Etymology->
From Latin *nugatorius*, from *nugari* "to trifle, jest," from *nugae* "jests, trifles."
