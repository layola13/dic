manuscriptal:/ˌmænjuˈskrɪptəl/
adjective
手写的，原稿的
of, relating to, or resembling a manuscript
The manuscriptal evidence suggests the poem was written in the 18th century. 手稿证据表明这首诗写于18世纪。
近义词:handwritten, hand-scribed
反义词:printed, typed
词性:adjective
