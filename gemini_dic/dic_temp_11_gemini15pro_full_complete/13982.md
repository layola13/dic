aurichalceous: /ˌɔːrɪˈkælsiəs/ | adj. | 金黄色的，铜黄色的 | Made of or resembling aurichalc, a legendary metal, typically described as golden or brass-like. |  The aurichalceous glow of the sunset filled the sky. 夕阳的金黄色光芒充满了天空。 | The statue had an aurichalceous sheen.  这座雕像闪耀着金黄色的光泽。 | 近义词: golden, brassy, bronze, copper-colored | 反义词: silvery, dull | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *aurichalceus, from Ancient Greek ὀρειχάλκειος (oreikhálkeios), from ὀρείχαλκος (oreíkhalkos, “mountain copper, brass”).
