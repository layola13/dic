intervocalic:/ˌɪntərvoʊˈkælɪk/ | adj. |元音间的，位于两个元音之间的|Placed between vowels.  Referring to a sound, typically a consonant, that occurs between two vowels.|The /t/ in "better" is intervocalic.|近义词: medial (referring to sounds within a word) |反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider initial or final in terms of sound position within a word) |词性: adjective
