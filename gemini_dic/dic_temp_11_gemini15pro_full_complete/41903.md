cosmic: /ˈkɒzmɪk/ (UK), /ˈkɑːzmɪk/ (US) | adj. |宇宙的；广大无边的| Relating to the universe or cosmos, especially in its vastness and mystery. |例句：Cosmic rays bombard the Earth's atmosphere. 宇宙射线轰击地球大气层。|例句：The sheer scale of the universe makes us feel insignificant in the cosmic scheme of things. 宇宙的巨大规模让我们在宇宙的宏伟计划中感到微不足道。| 近义词：universal, global, vast, immense | 反义词：terrestrial, earthly | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cosmique*, from Late Latin *cosmicus*, from Greek *kosmikos* "of the universe," from *kosmos* "order, good order, orderly arrangement," later "the world, the universe."
