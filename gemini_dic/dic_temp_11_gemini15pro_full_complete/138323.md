noter:/ˈnoʊtər/ (US) /ˈnəʊtər/ (UK)| n.|记录者，做笔记的人|A person who takes notes, especially one who makes a record of the proceedings of a meeting, etc. |The noter meticulously recorded every detail of the meeting.  记录员一丝不苟地记录了会议的每一个细节。|Synonyms: recorder, scribe, clerk|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "forgetful person" or "inattentive listener")|Noun
