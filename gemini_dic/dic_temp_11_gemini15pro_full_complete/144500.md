outtoil:/ˌaʊtˈtɔɪl/
verb
超越；在劳动上胜过
to work harder than someone else
例句：She outtoiled everyone on the team and became the top salesperson. 她比团队中的任何人都更努力，成为了顶尖的销售人员。

例句：He was determined to outtoil his competitors and win the contract. 他决心比他的竞争对手更努力，赢得合同。

近义词：outwork, surpass, excel
反义词：lag behind, underperform
