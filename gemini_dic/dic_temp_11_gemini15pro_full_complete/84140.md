half-mummified:/ˌhæf ˈmʌmɪfaɪd/
adj.
半木乃伊化的
partially mummified; preserved in a state between fresh and fully mummified.
The explorers found a half-mummified body in the tomb. 探险家们在墓穴中发现了一具半木乃伊化的尸体。
Near synonyms: partially mummified, semi-mummified
Antonyms: fresh, decomposed, putrefied
词性: adjective
