complaisance:/kəmˈpleɪsns/ | n. | 殷勤；柔顺；彬彬有礼；讨好; | A disposition to please or comply; affability. |  She treated everyone with complaisance. 她待人彬彬有礼。| synonyms: obligingness, courtesy, affability, civility, politeness, graciousness | antonyms: rudeness, impoliteness, incivility, discourtesy, ungraciousness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French complaisance, from complaisant (“obliging”), from Old French, from complaire (“to please”), from Latin complacēre, from com- (“with”) + placēre (“to please”).
