discoing: /ˈdɪskoʊɪŋ/
verb
迪斯科舞，跳迪斯科
Engaging in the activity of disco dancing.
例句：They were discoing the night away. 他们在跳迪斯科舞度过夜晚。

例句：He was discoing under the flashing lights. 他在闪烁的灯光下跳迪斯科。

近义词：dancing, grooving, boogying
反义词：(No direct opposite, perhaps relaxing, sitting)
词性：v. (present participle of disco)

词源：From the noun "disco" (short for discotheque), which originated in the 1960s referring to a type of nightclub playing recorded dance music, particularly music with a strong four-on-the-floor beat. The verb "to disco" emerged subsequently.
