mellowed:/ˈmɛloʊd/| adj.|柔和的，成熟的，醇厚的|Having become softer and more gentle; less harsh or intense.  Also, pleasantly ripe or sweet, as fruit.|The cheese has mellowed nicely over time.  这奶酪随着时间的推移变得醇厚了。|近义词:softened, matured, ripened, mild, gentle|反义词:harsh, immature, unripe, sour, bitter|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *mellowen*, from Old English *mearu* ("soft, tender, delicate, sweet").
