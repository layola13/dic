hymnography:/hɪmˈnɒɡrəfi/
n.
赞美诗学; 赞美诗集
The study or writing of hymns.  A collection of hymns.
The professor specialized in the hymnography of the early church.  教授专门研究早期教会的赞美诗学。
The new hymnography included several modern compositions. 新的赞美诗集包含了几首现代作品。
近义词: hymnology
反义词: (No direct antonym exists)
词性: noun
