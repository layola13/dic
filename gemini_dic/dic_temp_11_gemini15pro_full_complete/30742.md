cesarean:/siˈzɛəriən/ (US), /sɪˈzeəriən/ (UK)| n. adj.|剖腹产；剖腹产的|A surgical operation for delivering a child by cutting through the wall of the abdomen and the uterus. |例句：She had a cesarean section with her first child. 她生第一个孩子时做了剖腹产手术。|近义词：C-section, Cesarean section, Cesarean delivery|反义词: vaginal delivery, natural childbirth|词性:名词, 形容词


cesarean:/siˈzɛəriən/ (US), /sɪˈzeəriən/ (UK)| adj.|剖腹产的|Relating to or delivered by cesarean section. |例句：The baby was a cesarean birth.  这个婴儿是剖腹产出生的。|近义词：C-section, Cesarean|反义词: vaginal, natural|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin caesareus (“of Caesar”), from Caesar.  The Roman emperor Julius Caesar is said to have been delivered this way.  The surgical procedure predates Caesar.
