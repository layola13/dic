inmost:/ˈɪnməʊst/ (US) /ˈɪnmoʊst/| adj.|最深处的；最内心的；最秘密的|Situated furthest within; most intimate or secret. |例句：He revealed his inmost thoughts to his diary. 他在日记中吐露了他最深处的想法。

例句：She shared her inmost fears with her best friend. 她和她最好的朋友分享了她内心最深处的恐惧。|近义词：innermost, deepest, profoundest|反义词：outermost, superficial, external|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *innemest, superlative of inne "within," from Proto-Germanic *innam- "innermost," from PIE root *en "in."
