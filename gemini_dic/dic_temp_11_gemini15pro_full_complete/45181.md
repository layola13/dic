cuirass:/kwɪˈræs/| n. |胸甲|A piece of armor covering the torso, typically made of metal and consisting of a breastplate and backplate fastened together.|The knight polished his cuirass until it gleamed in the sunlight. 骑士把他的胸甲擦得锃亮，在阳光下闪闪发光。|The museum displayed a Roman cuirass from the first century.  博物馆展出了一件公元一世纪的罗马胸甲。|近义词：armor, breastplate|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "clothing" in a very broad sense)|noun
