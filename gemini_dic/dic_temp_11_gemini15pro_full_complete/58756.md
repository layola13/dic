dungyard: /ˈdʌŋjɑːrd/
n.
粪场，堆肥场
A place where dung is collected and stored, often for use as manure.
The farmer spread the dung from the dungyard onto his fields.  农民把粪场里的粪肥撒到田里。
synonyms: manure heap, compost heap
antonyms: N/A
