bachelorism: /ˈbætʃələrɪzəm/
n.
单身主义，独身生活
The state or condition of being a bachelor; the system of living as a bachelor.  
He enjoyed his bachelorism, free from the responsibilities of family life. 他享受着单身生活，没有家庭生活的责任。
近义词：singlehood, celibacy
反义词：marriage, matrimony
词性：名词

bachelorism: /ˈbætʃələrɪzəm/
n.
单身汉的生活方式，单身汉习气
The habits or lifestyle associated with being a bachelor.
His apartment was a testament to his bachelorism - messy, with takeout containers everywhere. 他的公寓证明了他的单身汉习气——凌乱不堪，到处都是外卖盒。
近义词：single life
反义词：married life
词性：名词
