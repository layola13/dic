cemented:/sɪˈmɛntɪd/
adjective
巩固的，加强的；粘合的
Made stronger; firmly established; bonded together.
The bricks were cemented together.  这些砖被粘合在一起。
近义词: consolidated, strengthened, fixed, bonded, adhered
反义词: weakened, loosened, separated, detached
词性: adjective

cemented:/sɪˈmɛntɪd/
verb
巩固，加强；粘合
To make something stronger; to firmly establish something; to bond something together.
Their shared experience cemented their friendship. 他们的共同经历巩固了他们的友谊。
近义词: consolidate, strengthen, fix, bond, adhere
反义词: weaken, loosen, separate, detach
词性: verb
