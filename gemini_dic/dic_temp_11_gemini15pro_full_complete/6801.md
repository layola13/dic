anapophysis:/ænəˈpɒfɪsɪs/
n.
横突, 副突
A bony outgrowth or process, especially a transverse process on a vertebra of a lower vertebrate, that arises from a centrum and is often fused with a rib.
The anapophysis of the lumbar vertebrae provides attachment for certain muscles and ligaments.  腰椎的横突为某些肌肉和韧带提供附着点。
Synonyms: process, outgrowth, projection
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but possibly:  depression, indentation)
noun
