girdler:/ˈɡɜːrdlər/| n.|束带者；腰带制造者；环剥虫|A person or thing that girdles, especially a kind of beetle larva that girdles trees.|The girdler had damaged several trees in the orchard.|近义词：belt maker, cincher, engraver (for insects)|反义词:|noun

girdler:/ˈɡɜːrdlər/| n.|环剥者|Someone who removes a ring of bark from a tree, causing it to die.|The girdler was trying to clear a path through the dense forest.|近义词：logger, forester (in some contexts)|反义词:|noun
