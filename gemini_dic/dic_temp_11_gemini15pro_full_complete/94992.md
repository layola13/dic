in-the-wind:/ɪn ðə wɪnd/ | adj. |  即将发生；正在酝酿；在流传中 | Something that is 'in the wind' is about to happen, is being planned or discussed secretly, or is being rumored. |  There's a big change in the wind.  重大变革即将发生。|Synonyms: impending, forthcoming, brewing, rumored, circulating | Antonyms: settled, established, definite, confirmed | adjective

in-the-wind:/ɪn ðə wɪnd/ | adj. |  飘忽不定；随风飘荡 |  Used to describe something that is moving freely in the wind. (Less common usage related to the idiom.) | The leaves were in the wind. 树叶随风飘荡。 | Synonyms:  drifting, airborne, afloat | Antonyms: grounded, still, motionless | adjective
