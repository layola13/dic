outbribed:/aʊtˈbraɪbd/
verb
用贿赂胜过
to bribe someone more effectively than another person
He outbribed his competitors to secure the contract. 他用贿赂胜过竞争对手，从而拿到了合同。

近义词: outbid, surpass in bribery
反义词: be outbribed

词性: v.
