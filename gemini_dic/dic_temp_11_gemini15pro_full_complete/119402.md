microfelsitic:/ˌmaɪkroʊfɛlˈsɪtɪk/| adj.|微霏细岩的，显微霏细结构的|Composed of extremely fine-grained crystals, too small to be distinguished with an ordinary microscope, characteristic of felsites and some other igneous rocks.|The rock sample exhibited a microfelsitic texture, indicating rapid cooling.|Near synonyms: aphanitic, cryptocrystalline|Antonym: phaneritic|adjective
