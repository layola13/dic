anchorlike: /ˈæŋkərˌlaɪk/
adjective
像锚一样的，坚定的
Resembling an anchor; firm, steadfast.
The old sailor had an anchorlike grip on the rope.  这位老水手紧紧地抓住绳子，像锚一样牢固。
His anchorlike faith sustained him through difficult times. 他像锚一样坚定的信念支撑他度过了艰难时期。
近义词: firm, steadfast, unwavering, resolute
反义词: weak, unstable, wavering, irresolute
词性: adjective

词源:  A compound word formed from "anchor" + "like". "Anchor" comes from Old English * ancor, from Latin ancora, from Greek ankura. "Like" comes from Old English gelic.
