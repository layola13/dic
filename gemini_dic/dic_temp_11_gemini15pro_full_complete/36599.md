colleagued:/ˈkɒliːɡd/ | adj. |  成为同事的；有同事的；由同事组成的 | Having colleagues; associated with colleagues. | My colleagued friend helped me with the project.  我的同事朋友帮助我完成了这个项目。 | Near Synonyms:  collaborative, joint, shared, combined | Antonyms:  independent, solo, unaffiliated | adjective

Etymology:

From *colleague* + *-ed*.  *Colleague* comes from the French *collègue*, ultimately from the Latin *collēga* "partner in office," from *com-* "with" + *lēga* "one sent as envoy, deputy, legate," related to *lēgāre* "to depute, bequeath."
