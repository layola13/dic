accipitres: /ækˈsɪpɪtriːz/
noun
猛禽
Birds of prey; diurnal birds of prey such as eagles, hawks, vultures, and falcons.
例句：Accipitres are known for their sharp talons and keen eyesight. 猛禽以其锋利的爪子和敏锐的视力而闻名。

例句：The accipitres soared high above the mountains. 猛禽在群山上空翱翔。

近义词：raptors, birds of prey
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider prey animals like rodents or songbirds)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Latin *accipiter*, meaning "hawk," from *accipere* "to seize," from *ad- "to" + *capere* "to take."
