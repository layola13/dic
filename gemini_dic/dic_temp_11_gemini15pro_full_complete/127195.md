naturist:/ˈneɪtʃərɪst/| n.|自然主义者；裸体主义者|A person who believes in nudism or naturism.  A person who studies natural history, especially plants and animals.|例句：Some naturists believe that being naked is a healthy and natural way to live.  一些自然主义者认为裸体是一种健康自然的生活方式。

例句：He is a keen naturist and enjoys observing wildlife in its natural habitat. 他是一位热心的自然主义者，喜欢观察野生动物在其自然栖息地。|近义词：nudist, naturalist|反义词:|noun
