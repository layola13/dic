dockyard:/ˈdɒkjɑːd/ (UK), /ˈdɑːkjɑːrd/ (US)| n.|船坞，造船厂|A place where ships are built or repaired.|The city's dockyards were busy with activity. 这座城市的船坞一片繁忙景象。|近义词：shipyard, naval yard|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "dock" (an enclosed area of water in a port for loading, unloading, and repairing ships) + "yard" (an enclosed area of ground). The word "dock" ultimately comes from Middle Dutch "docke."
