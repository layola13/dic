attends:/əˈtɛndz/
verb
出席；参加；照顾；护理
be present at (an event, meeting, or gathering).  Give heed (to); take care of.
He attends class regularly.  他定期上课。
The nurse attends to the patient's needs. 护士照顾病人的需要。
Synonyms:  participate in, be present at, go to, frequent, look after, care for, minister to
Antonyms:  absent, miss, neglect, ignore
词性: 动词
