cratonic:/krəˈtɑːnɪk/
adjective
克拉通的；稳定大陆块的
Relating to or characteristic of a craton, a stable and ancient part of the Earth's continental lithosphere.
The cratonic core of the continent has remained relatively undisturbed for billions of years.  这块大陆的克拉通核心数十亿年来保持相对稳定。
The researchers studied the cratonic rocks to learn more about the early history of the Earth. 研究人员研究了克拉通岩石，以了解更多关于地球早期历史的信息。
Synonyms: stable, ancient, continental
Antonyms: unstable, young, oceanic
word class: adjective
