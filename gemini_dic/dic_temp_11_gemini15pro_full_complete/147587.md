overpicture:/ˈoʊvərˌpɪktʃər/| n. |叠印画面，画外音|An image superimposed on another, often used in film or television; or a narrative or commentary overlaid on a scene.|The director used an overpicture of a flock of birds to symbolize freedom. 导演用鸟群的叠印画面来象征自由. | synonym:superimposition, overlay | antonym: background, underlay | noun

overpicture:/ˌoʊvərˈpɪktʃər/| v. |叠印，配画外音|To superimpose an image or sound over another.|The film overpictured scenes of war with images of children playing. 这部电影将战争场景与儿童玩耍的画面叠印在一起. |synonym:superimpose, overlay | antonym:  underlay | verb
