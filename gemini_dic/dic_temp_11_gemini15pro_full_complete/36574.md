collaret:/ˈkɒləret/ (UK) /ˈkɑːləret/ (US)| n. |项圈；颈圈；（尤指为女性设计的）装饰性颈圈|A collaret is a close-fitting necklace or decorative band worn around the neck, especially by women. |She wore a beautiful diamond collaret. 她戴着一个漂亮的钻石颈圈。

The queen's collaret was made of pearls and emeralds. 女王的项圈由珍珠和祖母绿制成。

|近义词：necklace, choker, torque|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly loose necklace/chain)|词性: noun
