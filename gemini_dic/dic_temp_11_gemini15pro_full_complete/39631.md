consequent-to:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt tuː/ (US) /ˈkɑːnsɪkwent tuː/| adj. prep.|作为…的结果，由于…|Resulting from something; as a result of something.  It's a more formal way of saying "as a result of" or "because of".|The road closures consequent to the flooding caused significant delays. 由于洪水导致的道路封闭造成了严重的延误。|The increase in tourism, consequent to the new advertising campaign, boosted the local economy. 由于新的广告宣传活动，旅游业的增长促进了当地经济。|Synonyms:  resulting from, due to, because of, as a result of, owing to, in consequence of|Antonyms:  irrespective of, regardless of|word type: adjective, preposition
