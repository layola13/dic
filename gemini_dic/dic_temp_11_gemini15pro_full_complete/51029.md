deslums:/diːˈslʌm/
verb
改造贫民窟；清除贫民窟
To clear slums; to improve the housing and living conditions of a slum area.
The government launched a program to deslum the city. 政府启动了一项改造城市贫民窟的计划。
The council is attempting to deslum the area by building new housing. 市议会正试图通过建造新住房来改造该地区的贫民窟。
