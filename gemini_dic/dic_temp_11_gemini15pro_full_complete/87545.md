heterologous:/ˌhɛtərˈɒləɡəs/ /ˌhɛtərˈɑːləɡəs/
adjective
异源的，不同的
Derived from Greek *héteros* "other" + *lógos* "relation."  Refers to something derived from a different species or source.
Heterologous expression of a protein means producing it in a different organism from the one where it is naturally found. 异源表达一种蛋白质意味着在一个不同于其自然存在的生物体中产生它。

The researchers used a heterologous expression system to produce the protein. 研究人员使用异源表达系统来生产这种蛋白质。

近义词:different, dissimilar, foreign, unrelated
反义词:homologous, similar, related, identical
词性:adjective
