adapted:/əˈdæptɪd/ | adj. | 适应的，改编的 | Suitable for a particular purpose or situation, especially after modification. | The play was adapted from a novel.  这个剧本是由小说改编的。| 近义词: modified, adjusted, altered, converted | 反义词: unadapted, original, unmodified | 词性: 形容词

adapted:/əˈdæptɪd/ | v. | （使）适应，改编 |  To make something suitable for a new use or purpose; to modify. | He adapted the novel for the screen. 他将这部小说改编成了电影剧本。 | 近义词: adjust, modify, alter, convert | 反义词: retain, preserve | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adaptāre*, from *ad-* (“to, toward”) + *aptāre* (“to fit”).
