attending:/əˈtɛndɪŋ/
attending|v.|参加，出席；照料，护理；伴随|
attending|adj.|出席的；伴随的；护理的|
attending|n.|出席；侍候；护理|

attending (verb):  The act of being present at an event or providing care or service to someone.  Derived from Old French "atendre" meaning "to wait, expect, attend."  Evolved into Middle English "attenden" meaning "to pay attention to, wait upon."

attending (adjective):  Descriptive of someone who is present at an event or providing care.

attending (noun):  (less common) The act of being present or providing care.



例句：
He is attending the conference.
他正在参加会议。

The nurse is attending to the patient.
护士正在照料病人。

近义词 (verb):  participating, present,  caring for, ministering to
近义词 (adjective): present, participating

反义词 (verb):  absent, neglecting, ignoring
反义词 (adjective): absent, non-attending


词性: Verb (primarily), Adjective, Noun (less common)
