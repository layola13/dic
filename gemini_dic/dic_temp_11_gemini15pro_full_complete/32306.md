chiffony:/ˈʃɪfəni/ | adj. | 薄而透明的；轻飘飘的 |  Resembling chiffon in being thin and translucent; light and airy. |  The dress was made of a chiffony material that flowed beautifully. 这件连衣裙是用一种轻飘飘的薄纱面料制成的，非常飘逸。 |  diaphanous, sheer, gauzy |  thick, heavy, opaque | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From chiffon + -y. Chiffon itself comes from French, meaning "rag, cloth."
