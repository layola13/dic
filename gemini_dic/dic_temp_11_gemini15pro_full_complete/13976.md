aureola:/ɔːˈriːələ/ |ɔˈriːələ/
n.|光环，日冕，晕|A bright circle of light or color, especially a radiant light around the head or body of a sacred personage in religious paintings. Also, a diffuse luminous glow surrounding a bright object.|The saint was depicted with a golden aureola around his head. 圣人被描绘成头部周围有一圈金色的光环。|synonyms: halo, nimbus, corona, glory, aura|antonyms: darkness, gloom|noun

aureolas:/ɔːˈriːəliːz/ |ɔˈriːəliːz/
n.|光环，日冕，晕（aureola的复数形式）|The plural form of aureola, referring to multiple bright circles of light.|The angels in the painting all had bright aureolas. 画中的天使都有明亮的光环。|synonyms: halos, nimbuses, coronae, glories, auras|antonyms: darknesses, glooms|noun
