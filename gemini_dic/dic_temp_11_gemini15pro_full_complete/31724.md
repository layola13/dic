cheapen:/ˈtʃiːpən/
verb
使…便宜；降低…的价值；贬低
to make something seem less valuable or important
Mass production has cheapened the image of handcrafted furniture.  大规模生产降低了手工家具的形象。

近义词:devalue, depreciate, downgrade, lower
反义词:enhance, increase, raise, upgrade
词性:v.
