headliner:/ˈhedˌlaɪnər/| n.|主要演员；头条新闻；领衔者|A performer or act that is the star attraction of a show or event.  The main story in a newspaper or broadcast.|例句：The headliner for tonight's concert is a Grammy-winning artist. 今晚音乐会的主角是一位格莱美奖得主。

例句：The headliner in the newspaper was about the local elections. 报纸的头条是关于地方选举的。|近义词：star, lead, main act, top billing, main story, leading story|反义词:supporting act, minor story|词性:noun
