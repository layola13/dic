fencelike:/ˈfɛnsˌlaɪk/
adjective
像栅栏的; 类似篱笆的
Resembling a fence in appearance or function.
The bushes grew thick and fencelike, creating a natural barrier.  灌木丛长得又密又像篱笆，形成了一道天然屏障。
The soldiers formed a fencelike barricade with their shields. 士兵们用盾牌筑起一道像篱笆一样的路障。
similar, barrier-like, enclosing
open, unobstructed
adjective
