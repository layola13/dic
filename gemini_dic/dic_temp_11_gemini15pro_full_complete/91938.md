hypermetaphorical:/ˌhaɪpərˌmɛtəˈfɔːrɪkəl/ | adj. | 超级隐喻的 |  Extending or exaggerating the use of metaphor; using metaphors within metaphors.  It refers to a metaphor that is highly complex, layered, or self-consciously elaborate. |  Her poetry is densely hypermetaphorical, making it a challenge to decipher. 她的诗歌充满了复杂的超级隐喻，难以理解。

The novel's hypermetaphorical language creates a dreamlike atmosphere. 这部小说的超级隐喻语言营造了一种梦幻般的氛围。 | 近义词:  overly metaphorical, excessively figurative, elaborately metaphorical | 反义词: literal, straightforward, plain | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
metaphorical (adjective): from metaphor, from Greek metaphora "a transfer," from metapherein "to transfer, carry over," from meta- "over, across" + pherein "to carry, bear"
