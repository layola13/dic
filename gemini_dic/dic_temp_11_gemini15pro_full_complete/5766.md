amalgamated:/əˈmælɡəmeɪtɪd/
adjective
合并的，融合的
Combined or united, especially through merging companies or organizations.
The company amalgamated with several smaller firms to create a larger corporation. 这家公司与几家小公司合并，创建了一个更大的公司。
amalgamated:/əˈmælɡəmeɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)
合并，融合
To combine or unite to form one organization or structure.
Several local businesses have been amalgamated to form a single large company. 几家当地企业合并成了一家大公司。

Synonyms: combined, merged, united, consolidated, blended, integrated
Antonyms: separated, divided, split, disunited, segregated
Word type: adjective, verb (past participle)
