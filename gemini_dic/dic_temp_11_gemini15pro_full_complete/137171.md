nonsuccessional:/ˌnɒn.səkˈseʃ.ən.əl/ | adj. | 非演替的，非连续的 | Not relating to or involved in ecological succession; not following in a regular series. |  The disturbance created a nonsuccessional environment, preventing the usual plant communities from developing.  这种干扰创造了一个非演替的环境，阻止了通常植物群落的发展。| Example: The volcanic eruption left the area nonsuccessional. 火山爆发使该地区变成了非演替状态。 | Synonyms:  asynchronous, nonsequential, disrupted | Antonyms: successional, sequential, seral | word type: adjective
