ferryman:/ˈfɛrimən/ | n. | 渡船夫；摆渡人 | A person who operates a ferry, conveying passengers or goods across a river or other body of water. | The ferryman took us across the river in his small boat. 渡船夫用他的小船把我们渡过了河。 | The ferryman expertly navigated the treacherous currents. 渡船夫熟练地驾驭着险恶的水流。 |  Synonyms: boatman, oarsman | Antonyms: passenger | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
