eighteen-wheeler:/ˌeɪˈtiːn ˈwiːlər/ | n. | 十八轮卡车；牵引式挂车 | A large truck with a trailer having a total of eighteen wheels (ten on the tractor and eight on the trailer). |  Example 1: The eighteen-wheeler jackknifed on the icy highway. 十八轮卡车在结冰的高速公路上折叠起来了。

Example 2:  Eighteen-wheelers are a common sight on interstate highways. 十八轮卡车在州际公路上很常见。 | 近义词:semi-truck, tractor-trailer, big rig | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "car" or "small truck" in certain contexts) | 词性: noun
