imperviously:/ɪmˈpɜːrviəsli/
|adv.|无动于衷地，不受影响地，不能渗透地|Not allowing fluid to pass through.  Not affected by pain or distress.|The fortress walls stood imperviously against the enemy attack.  堡垒的城墙坚不可摧地抵御着敌人的进攻。|Synonyms: impenetrably, impermeably, invulnerably|Antonyms: permeably, vulnerably, susceptibly
|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *imperviosus, from Latin in- (“not”) + pervius (“pervious”).
