gutlessness:/ˈɡʌtləsnəs/| n.|缺乏勇气；怯懦；胆怯|Lack of courage; cowardice; timidity. |He showed surprising gutlessness in the face of danger.  面对危险，他表现出令人惊讶的胆怯。|
gutlessness:/ˈɡʌtləsnəs/| n.|懦弱；无胆量|Cowardice; lack of guts.|Her gutlessness prevented her from speaking up. 她的胆怯使她不敢开口说话。|
gutlessness:/ˈɡʌtləsnəs/| n.|没有胆量；缺乏魄力|Lack of nerve; lack of boldness.|The gutlessness of the politicians was appalling. 这些政客的懦弱令人震惊。|


Etymology: gutlessness is derived from "gutless," which is formed by combining "gut" (referring to intestines, often associated with courage) and the suffix "-less" (meaning without).  The word "gut" in this sense originates from the Old English word "guttas."
