allonge:/əˈlɒndʒ/ | n. | 附加纸；加页 | A slip of paper attached to a bill of exchange or promissory note, on which to write endorsements when there is not sufficient space on the bill itself. | Example:  The bill of exchange required an allonge to accommodate all the signatures of the endorsers.  汇票需要附加纸来容纳所有背书人的签名。

Example:  The allonge was carefully attached to the promissory note.  附加页被小心地贴在期票上。 | Synonyms:  rider, annex, attachment | Antonyms: N/A | noun
