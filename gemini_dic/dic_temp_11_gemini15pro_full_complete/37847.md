competitions:/ˌkɒmpəˈtɪʃənz/ (UK) /ˌkɑːmpəˈtɪʃənz/ (US)| n. |竞赛，比赛|A situation in which someone is trying to win something or be more successful than someone else.|例句：The school holds several competitions throughout the year. 学校一年举办好几场比赛。

例句：She entered the photography competition and won first prize. 她参加了摄影比赛并获得了一等奖。

近义词：contests, tournaments, races, games|反义词：cooperation, collaboration|词性：名词
