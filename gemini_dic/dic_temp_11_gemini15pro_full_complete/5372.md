alluvial:/əˈluːviəl/| adj.|冲积的，淤积的|Relating to or derived from alluvium; deposited by running water.|The farmer's land was rich with alluvial soil, perfect for growing crops.  农民的土地富含冲积土，非常适合种植庄稼。|例句：The alluvial plain is fertile. 冲积平原很肥沃。|近义词：sedimentary, deposited, fluvial|反义词： igneous, volcanic|词性：形容词
alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/| n.|冲积层，冲积土|A deposit of clay, silt, sand, and gravel left by flowing streams in a river valley or delta, typically producing fertile soil.|The river deposited a thick layer of alluvium along its banks. 河流沿岸沉积了厚厚一层冲积土。|例句：The alluvium made the land very fertile. 冲积层使土地非常肥沃。|近义词：sediment, deposit, silt|反义词： bedrock|词性：名词

PHRASE->
alluvial fan 冲积扇

alluvial soil 冲积土
alluvial deposit 冲积物
alluvial valley 冲积谷

词源: From Latin *alluvialis, from alluvius (“wash, flood”), from alluere (“to wash against”), from ad- (“to, toward”) + luere (“to wash”).

注意事项:  冲积层通常指由河流沉积的物质，而沉积物是一个更广泛的术语，可以指由任何自然力（如风、冰川或水）沉积的物质。
