intervaried:/ˌɪntərˈvɛərid/
adjective
相互不同的，多样化的
Varied among themselves; diversified.  Consisting of different kinds or sorts.  Marked by diversity or variety.
The landscape was intervaried with hills, forests, and rivers.  这片土地上丘陵、森林和河流交错，景色多样。
The program included an intervaried selection of musical pieces.  该节目包含了各种各样的音乐作品。
近义词: diverse, varied, assorted, miscellaneous, heterogeneous
反义词: uniform, homogeneous, similar, consistent
词性: adjective

词源:
From inter- + varied, past participle of vary.  "Inter-" signifies "between" or "among," while "vary" means "to change" or "to differ." Thus, "intervaried" describes something that is varied between its different parts or elements.
