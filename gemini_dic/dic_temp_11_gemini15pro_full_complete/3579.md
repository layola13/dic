aghast:/əˈɡæst/
adjective
惊骇的，吓呆的
Filled with horror or shock.
She stood aghast at the news of the accident. 她听到事故的消息惊呆了。
The world watched aghast as the towers collapsed. 当塔楼倒塌时，全世界都惊恐地看着。

近义词:horrified, shocked, stunned, appalled, dumbfounded
反义词:calm, composed, unfazed

词性:adjective

词源:From Middle English *agast, from Old English *āgǣstan (“to terrify, frighten”), from ā- (“intensive prefix”) + gǣstan (“to frighten”). More at aghast.
