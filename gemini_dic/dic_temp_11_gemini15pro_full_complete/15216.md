backdropped:/ˈbækdrɒpt/ (UK) /ˈbækdrɑːpt/ (US)| v. |作为...的背景|To provide a backdrop for something means to be the background against which it happens or is seen.|The mountains backdropped the peaceful village. 群山是宁静村庄的背景。|The stage was backdropped with a starry night scene. 舞台的背景是繁星点点的夜空。|synonyms: background, scenery, setting|antonyms: foreground|verb

backdropped:/ˈbækdrɒpt/ (UK) /ˈbækdrɑːpt/ (US)| adj. |以...为背景的|Having something as a backdrop.|The backdropped stage looked magnificent.  以...为背景的舞台看起来很壮观。|The backdropped mountains provided a stunning view. 以群山为背景的景色令人叹为观止。|synonyms: backgrounded|antonyms: foregrounded|adjective
