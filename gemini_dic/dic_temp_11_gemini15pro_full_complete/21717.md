boardsailing:/ˈbɔːrdˌseɪlɪŋ/ /ˈbɔrdˌseɪlɪŋ/
n.
帆板运动，风帆冲浪
The sport of riding on a sailboard.
He enjoys boardsailing in the summer.  他夏天喜欢玩帆板运动。

近义词：windsurfing
反义词：(No specific antonym, could be considered the opposite of other water sports like kayaking, swimming, etc.)
词性：noun
