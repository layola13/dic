outflourish: /ˌaʊtˈflɜːrɪʃ/
verb
胜过，繁荣过，比...更繁荣
to flourish more than; surpass in flourishing; to grow or develop more successfully than someone or something else.
The new company outflourished its competitors in the first year. 这家新公司在第一年就超过了竞争对手。
Her garden outflourished all the others in the neighborhood. 她的花园比邻居家的所有花园都繁荣。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel, exceed
反义词: lag behind, fall behind, decline, wither

词性: v.

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "flourish" (from Old French "floriss-" meaning "to blossom, prosper").
