outinvent:/ˌaʊtɪnˈvɛnt/| v. | 比…更具创造力；发明出比…更好的东西 | To invent something superior to or more ingenious than something else. |  Tesla aimed to outinvent Edison in the field of electricity. 特斯拉的目标是在电力领域超越爱迪生。

| Elon Musk's companies are constantly trying to outinvent the competition.  埃隆·马斯克的公司一直在试图比竞争对手更有创造力。 | 近义词：surpass, outdo, eclipse | 反义词:  be surpassed, be outdone |词性:动词
