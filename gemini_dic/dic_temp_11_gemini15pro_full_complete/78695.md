gigafactories:/ˈɡɪɡəˌfæktəriz/| n. | 超级工厂 |  A very large factory, especially one manufacturing batteries or electric vehicles.  It combines several production stages under one roof, aimed at achieving economies of scale. | Tesla is building several gigafactories around the world. 特斯拉正在世界各地建造几座超级工厂。| synonyms: megafactory, large-scale factory | antonyms: small factory, workshop | noun
