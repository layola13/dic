overdrive:/ˈoʊvərdraɪv/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈdraɪv/ (UK)| n. v.|超速行驶，过度驱动；加倍努力|Driving in overdrive can wear out your engine faster. 超速行驶会更快地磨损你的引擎。|近义词：accelerate, speed up, intensify|反义词：slow down, decelerate|词性:名词/动词

overdrives:/ˈoʊvərdraɪvz/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈdraɪvz/ (UK)| v. (third-person singular simple present)|超速行驶，过度驱动；加倍努力|He often overdrives his employees, leading to burnout. 他经常过度驱使他的员工，导致他们精疲力竭。|近义词：overworks, pushes too hard|反义词：underutilizes, goes easy on|词性:动词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "drive" (v. to operate a vehicle)
