misclassed: /ˌmɪsˈklɑːst/ | /ˌmɪsˈklæst/
adjective | 错误分类的；归类错误的 | Incorrectly classified or categorized. | The librarian misclassed the biography as fiction.  图书管理员把传记错归类为小说。| The museum had misclassed the ancient artifact. 博物馆把这件古代文物归错了类。 | miscategorized, misfiled, wrongly classified | correctly classified, accurately categorized
词性

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + class (meaning "to categorize" or "to assign to a class").  The prefix "mis-" alters the meaning of "class" to indicate an incorrect or erroneous classification.
