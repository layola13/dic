expatiate:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪt/ /ɛkˈspeɪʃɪˌeɪt/ | vi. | 详述，细说 | To speak or write at length or in detail. | She expatiated on the benefits of a vegetarian diet. 她详细阐述了素食的好处。

| He expatiated on his travels in Europe. 他详细讲述了他在欧洲的旅行。 | 近义词：elaborate, expand, descant, discourse, dilate | 反义词：summarize, condense, abbreviate | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *expatiatus, past participle of expatiari (“to wander, digress”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + spatiari (“to walk”), from spatium (“space”).
