aquaculture:/ˈækwəkʌltʃər/ | n. |水产养殖 |The farming of aquatic organisms such as fish, crustaceans, molluscs and aquatic plants.  Involves intervening in the rearing process to enhance production, such as regular stocking, feeding, and protecting from predators. |Salmon aquaculture is a major industry in Norway. 挪威的三文鱼水产养殖是一个主要产业。| 近义词: fish farming, mariculture | 反义词: wild fishing | noun
