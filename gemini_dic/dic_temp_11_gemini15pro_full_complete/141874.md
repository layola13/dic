orchil:/ˈɔːrkɪl/ | n. |  染料；紫色染料；地衣 | A purplish dye obtained from certain lichens. |  The ancient Britons used orchil to dye their clothes. 古代英国人用地衣染料来染衣服。| synonyms: dye, pigment, colorant | antonyms: bleach | noun
