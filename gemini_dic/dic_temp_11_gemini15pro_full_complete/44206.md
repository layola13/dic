crocetin: /kroʊˈsiːtɪn/ | n. | 藏红花素，藏红花酸 | A carotenoid dicarboxylic acid, C20H24O4, that forms the glycoside crocin, the coloring matter of saffron.  | Crocetin is a natural food coloring. 藏红花素是一种天然食用色素。| Crocetin has shown promising results in some studies on cognitive function.  在一些关于认知功能的研究中，藏红花素显示出有希望的结果。 | 近义词: saffron extract, carotenoid | 反义词: N/A | noun
