misgrowth:/mɪsˈɡroʊθ/
noun
畸形生长，发育不良
Abnormal or faulty growth; a malformation.
The botanist studied the misgrowth on the tree, trying to determine the cause.  植物学家研究了树上的畸形生长，试图确定其原因。
The child's misgrowth was a cause for concern for his parents.  孩子的发育不良让他的父母很担心。
Synonyms: malformation, deformity, abnormality
Antonyms: normal growth, healthy development
