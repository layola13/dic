fely:/ˈfiːli/ | adj. | 猫一样的; 像猫的; 狡猾的 | Resembling or suggestive of a cat, especially in being sly or stealthy. |  The thief had a fely cunning about him.  那个贼身上有一种猫一样的狡猾。 |  catlike, feline |  doglike, canine | adjective

Etymology: From "fe-" (a prefix denoting "related to cats," derived from "feline") + "-ly" (a suffix indicating resemblance or manner).
