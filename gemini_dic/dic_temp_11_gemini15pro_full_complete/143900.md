outpracticed:/ˌaʊtˈpræktɪst/
verb
通过更多的练习胜过，练习得比...更好
To surpass or defeat someone through greater practice or training.
The young pianist outpracticed his rivals and won the competition.  这位年轻的钢琴家通过比对手更多的练习赢得了比赛。
He outpracticed everyone in the orchestra and became the lead violinist. 他比管弦乐队的每个人练习得都多，成为了首席小提琴手。
近义词:outperform, outdo, surpass
反义词:underperform
