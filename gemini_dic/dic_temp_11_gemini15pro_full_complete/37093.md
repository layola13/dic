combinedly:/kəmˈbaɪnɪdli/
adv.
共同地，联合地
Acting combinedly, they managed to lift the heavy object.  共同行动，他们设法抬起了重物。
The companies combinedly control a large share of the market. 这些公司共同控制了市场的大部分份额。
Synonym: jointly, together, collectively
Antonym: separately, individually
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From combine (v.) + -ed + -ly.  "Combine" comes from Old French "combiner," from Latin "combinare" meaning "to join two by two."
