nonmeditativeness:/ˌnɑnˌmedɪˈtæɪtɪvnəs/
|n.| 非冥想状态；不沉思的状态 |The state of not being meditative; absence of contemplation or reflection.|  Her nonmeditativeness made it difficult for her to focus during yoga. 她不沉思的状态使她很难在瑜伽中集中注意力。| 近义词: restlessness, distraction, unreflectiveness | 反义词: meditativeness, contemplation, reflectiveness | 词性: noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
meditativeness (noun): the state of being meditative
