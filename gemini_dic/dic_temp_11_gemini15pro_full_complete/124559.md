mountings:/ˈmaʊntɪŋz/
n.
安装;底座;衬托物;镶嵌;（尤指宝石的）底座;（照片等的）装裱
the act of fixing something firmly and securely in position.  Also refers to the supports, settings, or framework used for this purpose, particularly for gems, photographs, or other displayed items.
The diamond had beautiful gold mountings. 这颗钻石镶嵌在美丽的黄金底座上。
Synonyms: settings, frames, supports, fixtures, bases
Antonyms: detachments, removals

mountings:/ˈmaʊntɪŋz/
n.
（照片等的）装裱
the presentation or framing of a photograph or other artwork.
The photograph's mountings enhanced its beauty. 照片的装裱提升了它的美感。
Synonyms: framing, presentation, matting
Antonyms:  N/A
