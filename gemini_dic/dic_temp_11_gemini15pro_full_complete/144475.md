outthreaten:/ˌaʊtˈθrɛtn/
verb
恐吓; 比…更具威胁性; 超越…的威胁
to threaten more than someone or something else
例句：He outthreatened his rivals and won the election. 他恐吓了他的对手，赢得了选举。

例句：The new weapons system outthreatens anything our enemies possess.  新的武器系统比我们敌人拥有的任何武器都更具威胁性。

近义词：intimidate, menace, cow
反义词：protect, defend, reassure
词性：v.
