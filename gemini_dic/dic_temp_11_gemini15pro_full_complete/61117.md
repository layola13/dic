elohistic: /ˌɛloʊˈhɪstɪk/
adjective
以“Elohim”指称上帝的; 用Elohim指称上帝的
Relating to or designating the passages of the Hebrew Bible in which the Hebrew word Elohim is used for God.  The Elohistic source is one of the hypothesized sources of the Pentateuch.
The Elohistic source portrays God as transcendent and majestic, often communicating through dreams and visions. 上帝以超然和威严的形象出现，经常通过梦和异象进行交流。
The scholar argued that the elohistic text predates the Yahwistic one.  学者认为以“Elohim”指称上帝的文本早于以“Yahweh”指称上帝的文本。
近义词: Priestly (祭司的) (sometimes considered overlapping)
反义词: Yahwistic (用耶和华指称上帝的)
