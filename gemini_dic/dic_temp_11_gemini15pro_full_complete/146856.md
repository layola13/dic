overindulgently:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌləntli/
adverb
过度放纵地；过分纵容地
in a way that allows someone to have or do too much of something enjoyable, especially food or drink
He smiled overindulgently at his grandson. 他对孙子过分溺爱地笑了笑。
Synonyms: excessively, immoderately, intemperately
Antonyms: moderately, abstemiously, temperately
词性: 副词
