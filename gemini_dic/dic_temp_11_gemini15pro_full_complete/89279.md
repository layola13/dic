homodemic:/ˌhɒməˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 同区域结婚的；同区域通婚的 | Relating to or characterized by marriage within a defined population group, typically a locality or social class. |  The study found a high rate of homodemic marriage in the rural community. 这项研究发现，在农村社区中，同区域结婚的比率很高。| Homodemic marriages were common in the past due to limited travel options. 过去由于交通不便，同区域通婚很常见。 |近义词：endogamous | 反义词：exogamous | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From homo- (Greek, "same") + -demic (from Greek demos, "people, population")
