ceremonial: /ˌserəˈməʊniəl/ (UK) /ˌserəˈmoʊniəl/ (US)| adj. n.| 仪式的；礼仪的；正式的；礼仪；仪式 | relating to or used in a ceremony, especially one that is formal or religious; a formal event performed on a special occasion | The ceremonial opening of the new school was attended by the mayor. 新学校的开幕式由市长主持。 | formal, ritualistic, conventional | informal, casual | adjective, noun

ceremonial: /ˌserəˈməʊniəl/ (UK) /ˌserəˈmoʊniəl/ (US)| n. |仪式；典礼| a system of rules governing behavior or etiquette |  The coronation is a grand ceremonial. 加冕典礼是一场盛大的仪式。 | rite, ritual, observance | informality | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *ceremonial*, from Late Latin *ceremoniālis*, from Latin *caerimōnia* ("religious ceremony, sacred rite").
