fogyisms:/ˈfoʊɡiˌɪzəmz/
n.
守旧思想；过时观念
Outdated or conventional attitudes and opinions, especially those characteristic of old people.
His fogyisms about women in the workplace made him unpopular with his younger colleagues. 他对职场女性的守旧思想让他不受年轻同事的欢迎。
近义词:conservatism, old-fashionedness, traditionalism
反义词:modernism, progressivism
