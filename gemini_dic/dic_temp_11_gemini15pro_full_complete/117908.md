melt-away:/mɛlt əˈweɪ/
verb
消失; 融化; 逐渐减少
gradually disappear or dissipate, often as if by melting
The snow began to melt away as the sun came out.  太阳出来后，雪开始融化消失。
His anger slowly melted away as she apologized.  在她道歉后，他的怒气慢慢消散了。
近义词:disappear, vanish, fade, dissolve
反义词:appear, materialize, solidify
词性:verb (vi.)


melt-away:/mɛlt əˈweɪ/
adjective
(rarely used)  描述融化消失的
describing something that melts away
The melt-away effect of the morning mist was beautiful. 清晨薄雾的消散景象很美。 (This usage is less common)
近义词:vanishing, disappearing, fading
反义词:appearing, materializing, solidifying
词性:adjective
