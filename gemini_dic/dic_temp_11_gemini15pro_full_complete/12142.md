arunachal:/ˌərʊˈnɑːtʃəl/ |adj. n.|阿鲁纳恰尔的，阿鲁纳恰尔邦|Arunachal, derived from Sanskrit, means "land of the dawn-lit mountains."  It primarily refers to Arunachal Pradesh, a state in Northeast India. It can be used as an adjective or as part of the proper noun.|例句：Arunachal Pradesh is known for its biodiversity. 阿鲁纳恰尔邦以其生物多样性而闻名。

例句：The Arunachal macaque is a primate found in the region. 阿鲁纳恰尔猕猴是该地区的一种灵长类动物。|近义词：(As a place name, no direct synonym)  Perhaps "Northeast Indian" in some contexts.|反义词: N/A |词性: adjective, noun (as part of a proper noun)
