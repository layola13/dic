overproves:/ˌoʊvərˈpruːvz/| v. |过度证明；证明过多|To prove something to an excessive degree, often to the point of undermining the argument.  The lawyer overproved his case, boring the jury with unnecessary details.|例句：He overproved his point and lost the audience's attention. 他过度证明自己的观点，失去了听众的注意力。

例句：By providing too much evidence, she actually overproved her case and made it seem less believable.  她提供了太多的证据，实际上过度证明了她的观点，反而让它看起来不那么可信了。|近义词：overstate, overargue|反义词: understate|词性:动词
