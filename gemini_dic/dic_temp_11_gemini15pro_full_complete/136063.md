nonreceptive:/ˌnɒnrɪˈseptɪv/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈseptɪv/| adj.|不接受的，不感受的，无反应的|Not receptive; unresponsive; not willing or able to receive information or ideas.|The patient was nonreceptive to the new medication.  这个病人对新药没有反应。|The audience was surprisingly nonreceptive to his jokes.  观众对他的笑话 surprisingly 没有反应。|Synonyms: unreceptive, unresponsive, resistant, impervious, impassive|Antonyms: receptive, responsive, open, susceptible, sensitive|Adjective
