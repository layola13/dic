non-entry:/ˈnɒn ˈentri/ /nɑːn ˈentri/ | adj. n.|禁止进入的，非进入式的；禁止进入, 无权进入|A non-entry sign was posted on the door. 门上贴着禁止进入的标志。|The non-entry of unauthorized personnel is strictly enforced.  严禁未经授权的人员进入。|近义词: restricted, prohibited, closed, inaccessible |反义词: open, accessible, permitted, entry |词性: 形容词, 名词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "entry" (the act of entering).  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not."  "Entry" comes from the Old French word "entrée," ultimately derived from the Latin "intrare" (to enter).
