disintegratory:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡrətɔːri/
adjective
崩解的，瓦解的
Causing or tending to cause disintegration; causing something to break up into small parts or components.
The disintegratory forces of the explosion shattered the building. 爆炸的崩解力量震碎了大楼。
The report detailed the disintegratory effects of the new policy on the community. 报告详细说明了新政策对社区的瓦解性影响。
近义词: disruptive, destructive, divisive
反义词: unifying, constructive, cohesive
词性: adjective

词源: From disintegrate + -ory.  Disintegrate comes from the Latin dis- "apart" + integrare "to make whole," from integer "whole, complete."
