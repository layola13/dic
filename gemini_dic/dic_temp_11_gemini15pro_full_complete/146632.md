overhappily:/ˌoʊvərˈhæpɪli/
adverb|过于快乐地；非常幸福地|Excessively happily; more happily than is reasonable or necessary.|She laughed overhappily, a little too shrill. 她笑得过于开心，有点太刺耳了。|He overhappily accepted the invitation. 他非常高兴地接受了邀请。|近义词: ecstatically, blissfully, joyfully|反义词: unhappily, sadly, miserably|词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "happily" (from "happy" + "-ly"). "Happy" comes from Middle English *happi, from Old Norse *happ "luck, chance," related to the verb *happen.
