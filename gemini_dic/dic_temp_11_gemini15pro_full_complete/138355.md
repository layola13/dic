notidanian:/nəʊtɪˈdeɪniən/ | adj. n. | 六鳃鲨目的；六鳃鲨科的 | Relating to or belonging to the Notidanidae, a family of sharks characterized by six or seven gill slits.  Also, a member of this family. |  例句：The notidanian shark is an ancient species. 六鳃鲨是一种古老的物种。

例句：The cow shark is a notidanian. 牛鲨是六鳃鲨科的一种。| 近义词：hexanchiform (more technical) | 反义词：N/A | 词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  *Notidanus* (the genus name), from Greek *nōtos* (back) + *idanus* (strong, vigorous).  The genus name likely refers to the strong, prominent back of these sharks.
