knittle:/ˈnɪt(ə)l/
n.
一小段编织物; 一小段绳子; 少量
a small piece of knitting, string, etc.
She had a knittle of wool in her pocket. 她口袋里有一小团毛线。
近义词:bit, piece, snippet, fragment
反义词:lot, abundance
词性:名词


knittle:/ˈnɪt(ə)l/
v.
编织; 打结
to knit; to tie knots
She knittled a small blanket for the baby. 她为婴儿织了一条小毯子。
近义词:knit, weave, tie, knot
反义词:unravel, untie
词性:动词
