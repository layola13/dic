addict:/ˈædɪkt/ (US) /ˈædɪkt/ (UK)| n.  v. |瘾君子；对…入迷的人；沉溺于…的人|a person who is addicted to a particular substance, typically an illegal drug.  To cause (someone or oneself) to become dependent on something (especially a narcotic drug).|He's a video game addict. 他是个电子游戏迷。 |She was addicted to heroin. 她染上了海洛因毒瘾。|近义词:devotee, enthusiast, fanatic, fiend, junkie (n.); hook (v.)|反义词:moderate (n. & v.)|词性:名词，动词
