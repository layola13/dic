by-product:/ˈbaɪˌprɒdʌkt/ (UK) /ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkt/ (US)| n.| 副产品；副作用 |Something produced in addition to the main product of a process. |例句：
One of the by-products of burning coal is carbon dioxide.
燃烧煤炭的副产品之一是二氧化碳。

Wood chips are a by-product of the lumber industry.
木屑是木材工业的副产品。

近义词：side effect, spin-off, consequence, result|反义词: main product, primary product|词性:noun
