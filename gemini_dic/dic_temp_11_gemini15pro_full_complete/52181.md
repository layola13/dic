diarian:/daɪˈɛəriən/ | n. |日记作者；记日记的人|A person who keeps a diary.|She's a meticulous diarian, recording every detail of her day. 她是一位一丝不苟的日记作者，记录着每天的每一个细节。|例句：He was a prolific diarian, filling notebooks with his thoughts and observations. 他是一位多产的日记作者，用他的想法和观察填满了笔记本。

例句：Anne Frank's diary reveals her to be a perceptive and articulate diarian. 安妮·弗兰克的日记展现了她是一位敏锐而善于表达的日记作者。|近义词：journalist, writer, recorder|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who doesn't record anything) |词性: noun
