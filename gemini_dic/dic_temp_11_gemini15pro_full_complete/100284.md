intersegments:/ˈɪntərˌsɛɡmənts/
noun
节间；体节间的部分
The intersegments of an insect's body allow for flexibility and movement.  昆虫身体的节间部分允许灵活性和运动。
The robot's flexibility comes from the intersegments in its arms.  这个机器人的灵活性来自于它手臂上的节间部分。
近义词: sections, segments, divisions
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly consider "joint" as a related but opposite concept in some contexts)
词性:n.
