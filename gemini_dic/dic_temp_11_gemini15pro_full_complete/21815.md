bocal:/boʊˈkɑːl/ | n. |  烧杯；广口瓶；（乐器）吹口 | A wide-mouthed glass jar or beaker, often used in laboratories or for preserving food. Also, the mouthpiece of a wind instrument. | The chemist stored the chemicals in a bocal. 化学家把化学药品储存在广口瓶里。 |The musician adjusted the bocal of his bassoon. 音乐家调整了他巴松管的吹口。| synonyms: jar, beaker, container, mouthpiece | antonyms: lid, stopper | noun
