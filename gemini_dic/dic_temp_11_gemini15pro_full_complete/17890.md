been-around:/bɪn əˈraʊnd/| adj.| 经验丰富的；老练的；见过世面的；久经世故的；世故的；老于世故的| experienced; worldly-wise; having been to many places and seen many things| He's been around – he knows how to handle these situations. 他经验丰富 – 他知道如何处理这些情况。|近义词：experienced, sophisticated, worldly, knowledgeable|反义词:naive, inexperienced, unsophisticated|词性:形容词

词源:  "been" (过去分词 of "be") + "around" (adv. - in or to many different places).  The combination suggests someone who has existed or been present in various places and situations, implying experience and worldliness.
