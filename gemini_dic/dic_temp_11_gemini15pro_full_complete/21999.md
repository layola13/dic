boilerless:/ˈbɔɪlərlɪs/
adjective
无锅炉的，不用锅炉的
Lacking a boiler; not requiring a boiler.
This new boilerless heating system is very efficient.  这种新型的无锅炉供暖系统非常高效。
The boilerless office building utilizes geothermal energy. 这栋无锅炉的办公楼利用地热能。
near synonyms: boiler-free, tankless
antonyms: boiler-equipped, boiler-using
word class: adjective
