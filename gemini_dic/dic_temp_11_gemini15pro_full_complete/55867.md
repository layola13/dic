divinization:/ˌdɪvɪnɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|神化，神化作用|The divinization of Roman emperors was a common practice. 罗马皇帝的神化是一种常见的做法。|The artist's work explored the divinization of nature.  这位艺术家的作品探索了对自然的崇拜。|近义词：deification, apotheosis|反义词：humanization, secularization|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *divinizatio*, from Latin divinizare (“to make divine”), from divinus (“divine”).  Ultimately from *divus (“god”).
