commodus:/ˈkɒmədəs/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmədəs/ (US)| adj.|方便的；舒适的；宽敞的|Suitable; convenient; comfortable; spacious.  Derived from Latin commodus "suitable, convenient, proper, fitting, agreeable," from com- "together" (see com-) + modus "measure, manner" (see mode (n.)).|The apartment was commodus, with plenty of space for everyone. 这间公寓很宽敞，每个人都有足够的空间。|The new car is very commodus, with plenty of legroom.  这辆新车很舒适，腿部空间很宽敞。|Synonyms: convenient, comfortable, spacious, suitable, agreeable, handy, useful|Antonyms: inconvenient, uncomfortable, cramped, unsuitable, awkward|adjective
