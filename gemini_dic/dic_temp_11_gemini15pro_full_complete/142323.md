orthodoxy: /ˈɔːθədɒksi/ (UK) /ˈɔːrθədɑːksi/ (US)| n. |正统；正统观念；正统做法|Authorized or generally accepted theory, doctrine, or practice.|例句：The church insists on strict orthodoxy in its teachings. 教会坚持其教义的严格正统。|例句：He challenged the prevailing political orthodoxy. 他挑战了盛行的政治正统观念。|近义词：doctrine, dogma, tenet, belief, creed|反义词：heterodoxy, heresy, unorthodoxy|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *orthodoxia*, from Ancient Greek ὀρθοδοξία (orthodoxía, “right opinion, right teaching”), from ὀρθός (orthós, “straight, right, true”) + δόξα (dóxa, “opinion, glory”).
