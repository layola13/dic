dabble:/ˈdæbəl/| v. |涉猎，浅尝，戏水 |To casually engage in an activity without serious intent or commitment; to splash playfully in water. |He dabbled in painting, but never produced any masterpieces. 他涉猎过绘画，但从未创作出任何杰作。

She dabbled her toes in the cool stream. 她把脚趾伸进凉爽的溪流里戏水。|近义词：tinker, dip, dab, paddle, splash |反义词：specialize, master, immerse, delve |词性:动词

dabble:/ˈdæbəl/| n. |少量，少许 |A small amount or superficial involvement. |He had a dabble in the stock market. 他少量涉足了股票市场。|近义词：smattering, bit, taste |反义词：abundance, profusion, immersion |词性:名词
