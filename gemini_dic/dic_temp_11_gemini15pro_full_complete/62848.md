enjoiners:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪnərz/
n.
禁令者，禁止者，命令者
People who obtain or issue injunctions; those who command or prohibit something.
The enjoiners prevented the company from releasing the new product.  禁令者阻止了公司发布新产品。
The enjoiners insisted that the construction be halted immediately.  命令者坚持要求立即停止施工。
Synonyms: prohibitors, commanders, orderers
Antonyms: permitters, authorizers
noun
