arrowheaded:/ˈærəʊˌhɛdɪd/| adj.|箭头形的，楔形的|Shaped like an arrowhead; cuneiform. |The arrowheaded inscription on the stone was difficult to decipher.  石头上的楔形文字难以辨认。|The arrowheaded leaves of the plant were a distinctive feature. 这种植物的箭头形叶子是一个显著特征。|近义词：cuneiform, sagittal, pointed|反义词: rounded, blunt|词性:形容词

词源:  From arrow + headed.  "Headed" acts as a suffix meaning "having a head of a specific kind."  The word "arrowhead" itself refers to the pointed tip of an arrow. The term has been extended to describe any object shaped like this tip.
