copresident:/ˌkoʊˈprɛzɪdənt/| n.|共同主席，共同总统|A person who shares the position and duties of president with another person.|Example: The company has two copresidents who share the responsibilities of leadership. 这家公司有两名共同总裁，共同承担领导责任。
Example:  The copresidents worked together to guide the organization through a difficult period. 两位共同主席共同努力带领该组织度过了困难时期。|近义词：co-chairman, joint president|反义词:|名词
