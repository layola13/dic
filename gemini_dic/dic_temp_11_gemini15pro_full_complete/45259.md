cultivability:/ˌkʌltɪvəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可耕性，可培养性 | The cultivability of land depends on various factors like soil type, climate, and water availability.  土地的可耕性取决于土壤类型、气候和水资源等多种因素。 | The cultivability of a new strain of bacteria is crucial for its use in research.  一种新细菌菌株的可培养性对其在研究中的应用至关重要。 | synonyms: arability, fertility, tilth | antonyms: barrenness, infertility, sterility | noun

cultivable:/ˈkʌltɪvəbl/ | adj. | 可耕种的，可培养的 | The land is cultivable and suitable for growing crops. 这块土地可以耕种，适合种植农作物。 |  This type of bacteria is cultivable in a laboratory setting. 这种细菌可以在实验室环境下培养。 | synonyms: arable, fertile, tillable | antonyms: barren, infertile, sterile | adjective
