nongeometrical:/ˌnɒnˌdʒiːəˈmetrɪkəl/
adjective
非几何的
Not pertaining to or according to geometry; not geometrical.  Lacking geometric form or properties.
The artist's work focused on nongeometrical shapes and organic forms.  这位艺术家的作品专注于非几何形状和有机形式。
His understanding of the concept was nongeometrical, relying more on intuition. 他对这个概念的理解是非几何的，更多地依赖于直觉。
synonyms: nongeometric, ungeometrical, informal
antonyms: geometrical, geometric
词性: adjective
