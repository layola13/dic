bibliophilism:/ˌbɪblɪəˈfɪlɪzəm/
n.
藏书癖，爱书癖
A love of books, especially for their physical attributes, such as binding and typeface.
He inherited his father's bibliophilism and amassed a huge library. 他继承了父亲的藏书癖，积累了大量的藏书。
bibliomania, book-collecting
bibliophobia
noun
