hulled:/hʌld/| adj.|去壳的；去皮的|Having had the hulls removed.  Removed from the hull, pod, or shell.|例句：Hulled barley is easier to digest. 去壳的大麦更容易消化。|例句：We bought hulled sunflower seeds for the birds. 我们为鸟儿买了去壳的葵花籽。|近义词：shelled, husked, dehusked|反义词：unhulled, unshelled|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "hull," which comes from Middle English *hullen*, *hollen*, from Old English *hyllan* "to cover," related to *helan* "to conceal."  The original sense was to cover or enclose, as in a ship's hull.  The meaning shifted to removing the outer covering of seeds or fruits.
