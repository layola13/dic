anteriorness:/ænˌtɪəriərnɪs/
n.|前部, 前面, 前性|The state or quality of being anterior; location towards the front.|The anteriorness of the frontal lobe allows it to process higher-level cognitive functions.|近义词：frontality, forwardness|反义词：posteriority|词性:名词

Etymology:
From anterior + -ness.  "Anterior" comes from the Latin "anterior," comparative of "ante" meaning "before" or "in front."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing state or quality.
