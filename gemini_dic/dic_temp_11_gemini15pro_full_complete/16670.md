barratry: /ˈbærətri/ (US) /ˈbærɪtri/ (UK) | n. | 欺诈性诉讼；船长或船员的欺诈或不法行为；煽动诉讼 | The fraudulent exciting or maintaining of lawsuits or quarrels.  Also, any act committed by the master or mariners of a vessel tending to their prejudice, such as running away with the ship, willfully destroying her, or embezzling any part of her cargo. | The lawyer was accused of barratry for filing frivolous lawsuits.  该律师因提起无意义的诉讼而被指控犯有欺诈性诉讼罪。

The captain was charged with barratry after he intentionally grounded the ship. 船长因故意将船搁浅而被控犯有不法行为罪。 | 近义词: fraud, deceit, chicanery, litigation, malpractice (in maritime context) | 反义词:  honesty, integrity, good faith | 词性: 名词
