ineffectualness:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktʃuːəlnəs/| n. |无效；无能；徒劳无功|The ineffectualness of the policy was clear to everyone.  这个政策的无效性对每个人来说都很明显。|例句：The ineffectualness of their attempts to stop the fire was heartbreaking. 他们试图阻止火灾的努力徒劳无功，令人心碎。

The ineffectualness of the medication led to a worsening of his condition.  药物的无效导致他的病情恶化。|近义词：futility, uselessness, impotence, inefficacy|反义词：effectiveness, efficacy, potency|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ineffectual + -ness.  Ineffectual comes from in- (not) + effectual (producing a desired effect). Effectual ultimately derives from the Latin word "effectus" (effect).
