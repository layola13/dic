germane:/dʒɜːrˈmeɪn/| adj.|相关的，贴切的|Relevant to a subject under consideration.|例句：Her remarks were not germane to the discussion. 她的言论与讨论无关。

例句：The lawyer argued that the evidence was germane to the case. 律师认为证据与案件相关。|近义词：relevant, pertinent, applicable, apposite|反义词：irrelevant, extraneous, inappropriate|词性：形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *germanus* "having the same parents, full, own," from *germen* "sprout, bud, germ." Originally meaning "having the same parents," the sense evolved to "closely akin" and then to "relevant."
