chook:/tʃʊk/| n.|鸡，(尤指供食用的)母鸡|A chook is a common term for a chicken, especially one raised for meat. |例句：The farmer went out to feed the chooks. 农民出去喂鸡了。

例句：We're having roast chook for dinner. 我们晚餐吃烤鸡。|近义词：chicken, hen (母鸡), fowl (家禽)|反义词: rooster (公鸡) (在性别上相反)|词性:名词
