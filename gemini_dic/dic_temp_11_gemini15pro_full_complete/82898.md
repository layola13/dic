gum-up:/ɡʌm ˈʌp/
verb
堵塞；使粘住；使运转不灵
to cause something to become sticky and unable to work properly
The works were completely gummed up by the snow.  暴雪使工程完全瘫痪了。
近义词: clog, jam, obstruct, foul
反义词: clear, unclog, free
词性: verb

gum-up:/ɡʌm ˈʌp/
adjective
粘糊糊的；运转不灵的
sticky and not working properly
The mechanism had become gummed up with grease. 机械装置被油脂弄得粘糊糊的，运转不灵了。
近义词: sticky, clogged, jammed
反义词: clear, smooth, functioning
词性: adjective


词源:  "gum" (referring to a sticky substance) + "up" (indicating a negative effect or stoppage)
