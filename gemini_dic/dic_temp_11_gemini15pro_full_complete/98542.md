instill:/ɪnˈstɪl/| vt.|灌输，逐渐 instill，使充满|To gradually introduce (an idea, feeling, or attitude) into someone's mind, so that it has a strong influence on the way that person thinks or behaves.|例句：The coach instilled a sense of discipline in his players. 教练向他的球员灌输纪律感。

例句：Parents try to instill good manners in their children. 父母努力给孩子灌输良好的礼仪。|近义词：implant, inculcate, infuse, introduce, inspire|反义词：eradicate, remove, uproot|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "instillare" meaning "to drip in," from "in-" (in, into) + "stillare" (to drip).
