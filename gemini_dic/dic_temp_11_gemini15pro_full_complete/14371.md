autopsy: /ˈɔːtɑpsi/ | n. | 验尸，尸体解剖 |A postmortem examination to discover the cause of death or the extent of disease.| The autopsy revealed that the victim had died of a heart attack. 验尸结果显示受害者死于心脏病发作。 | synonyms: postmortem, necropsy | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin autopsia, from Ancient Greek αὐτοψία (autopsía, “seeing with one's own eyes”), from αὐτός (autós, “self”) + ὄψις (ópsis, “sight”).
