nonacoustically:/ˌnɒnəˈkuːstɪkli/
adjective
非声学地
not involving or relating to sound or acoustics.
The information was transmitted nonacoustically, using light signals.  信息以非声学方式传输，使用的是光信号。
Data can be transferred nonacoustically via optical cables.  数据可以通过光缆非声学地传输。
Near synonyms: silently, inaudibly, noiselessly
Antonyms: acoustically, audibly, sonorously
word class: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
acoustic (adjective): From Greek akoustikos "of or for hearing," from akouein "to hear."
-ally (suffix): Forming adverbs from adjectives.
