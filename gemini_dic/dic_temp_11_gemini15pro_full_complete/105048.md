keratin: /ˈkerətɪn/ (US), /ˈkerətɪn/ (UK)| n. |角蛋白|A fibrous protein forming the main structural constituent of hair, feathers, hoofs, claws, horns, etc.|例句：Keratin is the key structural material making up the outer layer of human skin. 角蛋白是构成人类皮肤外层的关键结构材料。

例句：The strength of your nails depends on the amount of keratin they contain. 你指甲的强度取决于它们所含的角蛋白量。


近义词：protein, scleroprotein|反义词: None|词性: Noun
