legibility:/ˌledʒəˈbɪləti/| n.|易读性，清晰度|The quality of being easy to read; clear enough to be read easily.|The legibility of her handwriting is very poor. 她的字迹很难辨认。|Synonyms: readability, clarity, decipherability|Antonyms: illegibility, unintelligibility|noun

legible:/ˈledʒəbl/| adj.|清晰的，易读的|Clear enough to be read easily.|The document was barely legible. 这份文件几乎难以辨认。|Synonyms: readable, clear, decipherable|Antonyms: illegible, unreadable|adjective
