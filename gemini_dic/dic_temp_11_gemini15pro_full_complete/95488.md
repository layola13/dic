adjective
嵌入的，被包围的
Enclosed or surrounded, as within a cavity or by a foreign tissue.  Embedded deeply in something.
The foreign body was inclavated within the muscle tissue.  异物被嵌入肌肉组织中。
The splinter was inclavated so deeply that surgery was required to remove it.  碎片嵌入太深，需要手术才能取出。
近义词:embedded, encased, enclosed, surrounded
反义词:exposed, superficial, free, detached
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inclavatus, from Latin in- (“in”) + clavus (“nail, spike”).  Literally "nailed in," hence "fixed firmly," "embedded."
