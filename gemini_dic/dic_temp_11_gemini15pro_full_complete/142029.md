organogen:/ɔːrˈɡænədʒɛn/
n.
器官原基，器官形成素
A substance that promotes the development of an organ or tissue.
Organogens are essential for normal embryonic development.  器官原基对正常的胚胎发育至关重要。
The scientists identified several organogens in the developing plant. 科学家们在发育中的植物中鉴定出几种器官形成素。
Synonyms: primordium, blastema
Antonyms: N/A
Noun
