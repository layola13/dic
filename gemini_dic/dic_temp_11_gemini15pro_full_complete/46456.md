daguerreotypy:/dəˈɡɛrətaɪpi/
n.
银版照相法；银版照片
a photograph taken by an early photographic process employing an iodine-sensitized silvered plate and mercury vapor.
The museum has a fascinating collection of early daguerreotypies.  这家博物馆收藏了引人入胜的早期银版照片。
近义词:photograph, image
反义词:None.
词性:noun

daguerreotype:/dəˈɡɛrətaɪp/
n.
银版照片
a photograph taken by this process.
This daguerreotype captures the likeness of a young woman in the 1850s. 这张银版照片捕捉了一位19世纪50年代年轻女子的肖像。
近义词:photograph, picture
反义词:None.
词性:noun


daguerreotype:/dəˈɡɛrətaɪp/
vt.
用银版照相法拍摄
to photograph by this process.
The photographer daguerreotyped the family in front of their home. 摄影师用银版照相法拍摄了这家人在自家门前的照片.
近义词:photograph
反义词:None.
词性:verb


词源:  Named after Louis-Jacques-Mandé Daguerre, the French artist and inventor who developed the process in the 1830s.
