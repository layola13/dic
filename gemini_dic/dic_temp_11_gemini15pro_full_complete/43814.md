creole:/ˈkriːoʊl/ (US) /ˈkriːɒl/ (UK)| n. adj.|克里奥尔语；克里奥尔人；克里奥尔人的；混合的|A language that has developed from a mixture of different languages.  A person of mixed European and black descent, especially in the Caribbean.  Relating to or denoting a Creole language or people.  A style of cooking originating in Louisiana, based on French and Spanish cuisine with influences from other cultures.|例句：The creole language is a fascinating blend of French, African, and Spanish influences.  这种克里奥尔语融合了法语、非洲语和西班牙语的影响，非常引人入胜。

例句：She is a creole from Martinique. 她是一位来自马提尼克岛的克里奥尔人。|近义词：pidgin, dialect, hybrid|反义词:pure, unmixed|词性:名词，形容词
