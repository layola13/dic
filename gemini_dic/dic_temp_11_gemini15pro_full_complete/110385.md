lignone:/ˈlɪɡnoʊn/| n.|木质素，木素|A complex polymer found in the cell walls of many plants, making them rigid and resistant to decay. It is a byproduct of the paper pulping process.|Lignone is a major component of wood. 木质素是木材的主要成分。

Lignone can be used as a biofuel. 木质素可以用作生物燃料。|近义词：lignin|反义词: None|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lignōsus* ("woody"), from *lignum* ("wood").
