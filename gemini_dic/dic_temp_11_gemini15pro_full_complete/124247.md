mossy:/ˈmɔːsi/ (US), /ˈmɒsi/ (UK)| adj.| 苔藓的；长满苔藓的|Covered with moss; resembling moss.|The old stone wall was mossy and damp.  古老的石墙长满了苔藓，潮湿不堪。|The mossy forest floor was soft underfoot.  长满苔藓的森林地面踩上去很柔软。|近义词：moss-covered, moss-grown|反义词: bare, clean|词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mossi*, from Old English *mosig* (“mossy”), equivalent to *moss* + *-y*. Cognate with Scots *mossy* (“mossy”), West Frisian *mosich* (“mossy”), Dutch *mossig* (“mossy”), German *moosig* (“mossy”).
