apronless:/ˈeɪprənlɪs/
adj.
没有围裙的；不穿围裙的
Without an apron.
The chef, apronless and relaxed, greeted the guests.  这位厨师，没穿围裙，很放松地向客人们打招呼。
The apronless baker dusted flour off his hands.  没穿围裙的面包师掸了掸手上的面粉。
近义词:  bare, uncovered (in the context of not wearing an apron)
反义词: aproned
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From apron + -less.  "Apron" comes from Old French "naperon" (meaning a small tablecloth), which was mistakenly divided as "a napron" in Middle English.  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking."
