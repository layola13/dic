finites:/ˈfaɪnaɪts/| n.| 有限的事物；限定动词|Things that are finite; verbs that are marked for person, number, and tense.|The finites of the verb are marked for tense. 限定动词有时间标记。|近义词:limited, bounded|反义词:infinites, unlimited|词性:名词(通常用复数形式)

Etymology:  From Late Middle English *finiten, from Latin *fīnītus (“limited, bounded”), past participle of fīnīre (“to limit, bound, finish”), from fīnis (“end, limit, boundary”).
