homing:/ˈhoʊmɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhəʊmɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.| 归巢的，自动引导的 | relating to or describing the ability of an animal or device to find its way back to its starting point automatically | The homing pigeon carried the message back to headquarters.  这只信鸽把信息带回了总部。| synonyms:  navigating, tracking, guiding | antonyms:  lost, wandering, straying| adjective


homing:/ˈhoʊmɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhəʊmɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 归巢，自动引导 | the act of returning to a starting point, especially automatically | The homing instinct of salmon is remarkable. 鲑鱼的归巢本能非常了不起。| synonyms: return, navigation, guidance | antonyms: departure, straying, wandering| noun
