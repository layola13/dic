arthur:/ˈɑːrθər/| n. |亚瑟|A traditionally masculine given name of Celtic origin, meaning "bear-like" or "noble; courageous".  It is associated with the legendary King Arthur of Britain. |King Arthur is a prominent figure in British folklore.  亚瑟王是英国民间传说中的杰出人物。|Arthur is a common name in many English-speaking countries.  亚瑟是许多英语国家常见的姓名。|近义词:Art, Artie|反义词:N/A|noun
