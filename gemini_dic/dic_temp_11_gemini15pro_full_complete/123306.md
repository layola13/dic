monoglyceride:/ˌmɒnəʊˈɡlɪsərʌɪd/ /ˌmɑːnəˈɡlɪsəˌraɪd/| n. |单甘油酯；甘油一酸酯|A glyceride formed by the esterification of one hydroxyl group of glycerol with a fatty acid.|Monoglycerides are often used as emulsifiers in food. 单甘油酯常在食品中用作乳化剂。|Synonyms: monoacylglycerol|Antonyms: triglyceride|Noun
