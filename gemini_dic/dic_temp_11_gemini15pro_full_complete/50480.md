depression:/dɪˈprɛʃən/ /dɪˈprɛʃn/| n.| 沮丧，抑郁，萧条，洼地 |A prolonged feeling of helplessness, hopelessness, and sadness.  A period of low general economic activity.|She suffered from severe depression after the loss of her job. 她失业后患上了严重的抑郁症. The country is in a deep economic depression. 国家正处于严重的经济萧条之中.|近义词：melancholy, gloom, sadness, recession, slump|反义词：happiness, joy, elation, boom, prosperity|词性:noun
