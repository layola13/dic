enemata:/ˈenɪmətə/
n.
灌肠剂；灌肠法
Solutions introduced into the rectum and colon.
The doctor prescribed enemata to relieve the patient's constipation. 医生开了灌肠剂来缓解病人的便秘。
Synonyms: clyster, injection
Antonyms: N/A
