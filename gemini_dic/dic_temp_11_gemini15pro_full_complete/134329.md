noninsulating: /ˌnɒnˈɪnsjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非绝缘的 | Not insulating; not preventing the passage of heat, electricity, or sound. |  The noninsulating material allowed the heat to escape.  非绝缘材料使热量散失。

The noninsulating windows made the house drafty in the winter.  非绝缘的窗户使房子在冬天通风。 | 近义词: conductive, non-isolating | 反义词: insulating, isolating | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "insulating" (from "insulate," from Latin "insula" meaning "island," figuratively "something detached").  The word implies the absence of the quality of being insulated or separated, especially regarding the flow of heat, electricity, or sound.
