impediment:/ɪmˈpɛdɪmənt/| n. |阻碍，障碍，妨碍|Something that hinders or obstructs progress or movement.|The heavy traffic proved to be a major impediment to our journey.  大交通对我们的旅程造成了很大的阻碍。|Synonyms: obstacle, hindrance, obstruction, barrier, hurdle |Antonyms: advantage, aid, assistance, help|noun

impediment:/ɪmˈpɛdɪmənt/| n. |(言语)障碍，缺陷|A speech defect, such as a stammer or lisp.|His impediment made it difficult for him to give public speeches. 他的言语障碍使他难以发表公开演讲。|Synonyms: speech defect, stammer, lisp, stutter |Antonyms: fluency, eloquence|noun
