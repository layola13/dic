nowadays:/ˈnaʊədeɪz/| adv.|如今，现在|At present, in the present time.|Nowadays, people are more aware of the importance of a healthy diet. 如今，人们更加意识到健康饮食的重要性。|Nowadays, many people prefer to shop online. 现在，许多人更喜欢网上购物。|近义词：currently, presently, these days|反义词:formerly, previously|词性:副词

词源: From "now" + "a days," meaning "on days now."  The "a" is a weakened form of "on." Over time, "nowadays" became a single word.
