glitziness:/ˈɡlɪtsɪnəs/
noun
浮华；炫目；奢华
The quality of being glitzy; ostentatious glamour and showiness.
The glitziness of Hollywood attracts many young people. 好莱坞的浮华吸引了很多年轻人。

The actress toned down the glitziness of her outfit for the charity event.  这位女演员为了慈善活动降低了她服装的浮华程度。

近义词:glamour, flashiness, showiness, ostentation, glitter
反义词:simplicity, modesty, understatement, plainness
词性:noun
