melastoma: /ˌmɛləˈstoʊmə/ | n. | 野牡丹; 野牡丹属植物 | A genus of tropical shrubs or small trees of the family Melastomataceae, having large purple or pink flowers and edible berries. |  例句：The melastoma bloomed vibrantly in the humid greenhouse.  野牡丹在潮湿的温室里开得鲜艳。

| 例句：Several species of melastoma are cultivated as ornamental plants. 有几种野牡丹被栽培为观赏植物。 | 近义词：Tibouchina (a related genus) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *melan* (μέλαν) "black, dark" + *stoma* (στόμα) "mouth," referring to the black stain left on the mouth after eating the fruit of some species.
