country: /ˈkʌntri/ (US) /ˈkʌntri/ (UK)| n. |国家，乡村，故乡|A nation with its own government, occupying a particular territory.  A rural area.|例句：My country is China. 我的国家是中国。

例句：I grew up in the country. 我在乡下长大。|近义词：nation, land, state, rural area|反义词:city, urban area|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old French *cuntrée "region, territory," from Vulgar Latin *(terra) contrata "(land) lying opposite," from Latin contra "against, opposite."
