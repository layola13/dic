nonpatentabilities:/ˌnɒnˌpeɪtənˈtəˈbɪlɪtiz/
|n.| 不可专利性|The state or quality of not being patentable; things or characteristics that cannot be patented.|The lawyer explained the nonpatentabilities of abstract ideas.|近义词: unpatentability|反义词: patentability|词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Latin "non" meaning "not"
patent (root):  Latin "patens, patentis" meaning "lying open, evident, public"
-able (suffix):  Makes the root word an adjective meaning "capable of being"
-ity (suffix):  Transforms the adjective into a noun denoting a state or quality
