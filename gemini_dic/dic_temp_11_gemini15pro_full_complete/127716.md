neglectors:/nɪˈɡlɛktərz/| n.| 忽视者；疏忽者；玩忽职守者 |  People who fail to care for someone or something that they are responsible for. | The neglectors of the property allowed it to fall into disrepair. 这些房产的疏忽者任由其失修。 | synonyms: derelict, slacker, shirker | antonyms: caretaker, guardian, custodian | noun


neglect:/nɪˈɡlɛkt/| n. vt.| 疏忽，忽略；忽视，疏漏；玩忽职守| The state of being uncared for or unattended to. To fail to care for properly. |  He neglected his studies and failed the exam. 他荒废了学业，考试不及格。| synonyms: disregard, ignore, overlook | antonyms: cherish, nurture, attend | noun/verb
