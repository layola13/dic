employable:/ɪmˈplɔɪəbl/ | adj. | 可雇佣的；适于就业的 | Suitable for being employed; having the skills, experience, or qualities that employers are looking for. |  She gained valuable work experience that made her more employable. 她获得了宝贵的可以提升她就业能力的工作经验。 |  Example:  With his excellent communication skills, he is highly employable.  由于他优秀的沟通能力，他非常容易找到工作。| Synonyms: hirable, qualified, suitable, eligible | Antonyms: unemployable, ineligible, unsuitable | adjective
