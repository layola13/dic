ellipsoid:/ɪˈlɪpsɔɪd/ | n. |椭球体；椭圆体 |A three-dimensional shape resembling a stretched-out sphere, all plane sections of which are ellipses or circles. | The Earth is not a perfect sphere, but rather an ellipsoid. 地球不是一个完美的球体，而是一个椭球体。|  The rugby ball is shaped like a prolate ellipsoid.  橄榄球的形状像一个长椭球体。 | synonyms: spheroid, oval | antonyms: sphere, cube | noun

ellipsoidal:/ɪˌlɪpˈsɔɪdəl/ | adj. |椭球的；椭圆体的 |Having the form of an ellipsoid; egg-shaped. | The artist created an ellipsoidal sculpture.  艺术家创作了一个椭球形的雕塑。 | The bacteria had an ellipsoidal shape when viewed under the microscope. 在显微镜下观察，这种细菌呈椭圆形。 | synonyms: oval, ovoid, elliptic | antonyms: spherical, cubic | adjective
