basketball: /ˈbæskɪtbɔːl/ (US) /ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl/ (UK)| n.| 篮球 |A game played between two teams of five players in which each team tries to score by throwing a ball through a hoop (= a ring and net) fixed above each end of the court. Also, the ball used in this game.| He plays basketball every weekend. 他每个周末都打篮球。|近义词: hoops (informal)|反义词:  (No direct antonym for the sport itself.  Could consider opposing sports like baseball or soccer if contrasting activities are needed.)|词性: noun
