ineffableness:/ɪnˈɛfəbəlnəs/ | n. | 不可言喻；难以表达 | The quality of being too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words. | The ineffableness of the experience left her speechless. 这种体验的难以言喻让她哑口无言。 | synonyms: inexpressibility, indescribability, unutterability | antonyms: expressiveness, describability, articulateness | noun

ineffable:/ɪnˈɛfəbəl/ | adj. | 不可言喻的；难以表达的 | Too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words. |  The beauty of the sunset was ineffable.  日落的美丽难以言喻。| synonyms: inexpressible, indescribable, unutterable | antonyms: expressible, describable, utterable | adjective
