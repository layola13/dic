incurrence:/ɪnˈkɜːrəns/| n. |招致，引发，蒙受，承担|The act of incurring something, such as a debt, a cost, or a penalty.  The state of having liabilities or expenses.|例句：The incurrence of heavy debts led to the company's bankruptcy. 承担巨额债务导致了这家公司的破产。

例句：The company reported an incurrence of significant operating expenses in the last quarter.  该公司报告称，上一季度发生了巨额运营支出。|近义词：acquisition, assumption, obtaining, taking on|反义词:avoidance, evasion|词性:noun
