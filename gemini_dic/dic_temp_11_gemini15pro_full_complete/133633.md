nonforests:/ˈnɒnˌfɒrɪsts/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˌfɔːrɪsts/
n.
非森林地区
Areas that are not classified as forests, typically lacking the tree cover and ecological characteristics of a forest.
Nonforests cover a vast area of the planet, including grasslands, deserts, and urban areas.  非森林地区覆盖了地球上的广阔区域，包括草原、沙漠和城市地区。
Nonforests can include agricultural land, developed areas, and barren land. 非森林可以包括农业用地、发达地区和贫瘠的土地。
Synonym: non-forested areas, non-woodlands
Antonym: forests, woodlands
noun
