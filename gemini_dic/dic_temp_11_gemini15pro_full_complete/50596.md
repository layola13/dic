deray:/dɪˈreɪ/| n. vt.| 耽搁，延迟，妨碍；使脱轨 |A situation causing delay; an act of delaying something; to cause something to be delayed or to run late; to obstruct or hinder the progress of something. | The train was derayed by the heavy snow. 火车因大雪而延误。| 近义词：delay, hinder, obstruct, impede | 反义词：expedite, hasten, accelerate | 词性:名词,动词


Etymology: From Old French *desreier, from des- "dis-" + reier "to arrange, array".
