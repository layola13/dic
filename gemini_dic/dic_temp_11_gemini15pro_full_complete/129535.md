non-biblical:/ˌnɒnˈbɪblɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈbɪblɪkəl/
adjective
非圣经的; 世俗的
Not found in or not relating to the Bible.
Example:  The story has non-biblical origins.  这个故事起源于圣经之外。

Example:  He explored non-biblical texts for inspiration. 他探索非圣经文本以寻求灵感。


secular, worldly, profane
scriptural, biblical, religious
