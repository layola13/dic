damselflies:/ˈdæmzəlˌflaɪz/| n. |豆娘；蟌|A group of insects similar to dragonflies but smaller and with slenderer bodies. They typically hold their wings together above their bodies when at rest.|例句：The damselflies flitted among the reeds by the pond. 豆娘在池塘边的芦苇丛中飞来飞去。

例句：I watched a pair of damselflies mating in the air. 我看到一对豆娘在空中交配。

|近义词：dragonflies|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
