anticoagulant:/ˌæntiːkoʊˈæɡjələnt/| n. |抗凝剂，抗凝血剂|A substance that prevents or reduces the coagulation (clotting) of blood.|The doctor prescribed an anticoagulant to prevent blood clots. 医生开了抗凝药以防止血凝块。|Synonyms: blood thinner, antithrombotic|Antonyms: coagulant, procoagulant|noun


anticoagulant:/ˌæntiːkoʊˈæɡjələnt/| adj. |抗凝的，抗凝血的|Having the effect of preventing or reducing the coagulation of blood.|Anticoagulant therapy is often used after surgery. 抗凝治疗常用于手术后。|Synonyms: antithrombotic|Antonyms: coagulant, procoagulant|adjective
