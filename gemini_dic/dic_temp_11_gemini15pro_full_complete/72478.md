ETYMOLOGY->
From *fluoro- + borate.  "Fluoro-" indicates the presence of fluorine, and "borate" refers to a salt of boric acid or containing the anion BO₃³⁻ or a similar anion containing boron and oxygen.  In the case of fluoborate, the boron is bonded to four fluorine atoms instead of oxygen.
