nonprotective:/ˌnɒnprəˈtɛktɪv/ | adj. |非保护性的，不具保护作用的 | Not providing protection or shielding from harm or danger. |  Wearing nonprotective clothing in the sun can lead to sunburn. 在阳光下穿不具保护作用的衣服会导致晒伤。

The nonprotective coating on the metal caused it to rust quickly.  金属上不具保护作用的涂层导致它很快生锈。| 近义词: vulnerable, exposed, undefended | 反义词: protective, shielding, safeguarding | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + protective (adjective from protect, from Latin protectus, past participle of protegere "to cover in front, protect," from pro- "in front" + tegere "to cover").
