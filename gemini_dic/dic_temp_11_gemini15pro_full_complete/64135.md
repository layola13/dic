equableness:/ˈiːkwəbəlnəs/
n.
平静；均匀；稳定
The equableness of his temper made him a good mediator.
他性情平和，这使他成为一名优秀的调解员。
Evenness, steadiness, stability, uniformity, consistency
Variability, unevenness, instability, inconsistency
noun

equable:/ˈiːkwəbəl/
adj.
稳定的；平静的；均匀的
He has an equable temperament.
他性情平和。
Even, steady, stable, uniform, consistent
Variable, uneven, unstable, inconsistent
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *equable, from Late Latin aequabilis (“equal, uniform”), from Latin aequus (“equal, level, fair”) + -abilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning “capable of being,” “able to be,” “worthy of being,” “tending to,” “likely to”).
