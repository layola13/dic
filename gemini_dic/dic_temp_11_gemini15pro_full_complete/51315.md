detainee:/ˌdiːteɪˈniː/
n.
被拘留者，被扣押者
A person held in custody, especially for political reasons.
The detainee was held without charge for several weeks.  这名被拘留者未经指控被关押了数周。
Near synonyms: prisoner, captive, inmate
Opposite:  freeman, liberator
noun
