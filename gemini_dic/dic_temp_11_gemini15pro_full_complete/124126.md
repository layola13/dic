mortgageor:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒɔːr/ (US) /ˈmɔːɡɪdʒɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|抵押人，按揭人|The mortgageor is responsible for making timely payments on the loan. 抵押人有责任按时偿还贷款。|例句：The mortgageor defaulted on their loan payments. 抵押人拖欠了贷款。

The mortgageor signed the documents transferring ownership of the property. 抵押人签署了转移财产所有权的文件。|近义词：borrower, debtor|反义词：mortgagee, lender|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mortgageor, from mortgager ("to mortgage"), from mort ("dead") + gage ("pledge").  The idea is that the property is a "dead pledge" until the loan is repaid.
