exocardial:/ˌɛksoʊˈkɑːrdiəl/
adjective
心外膜的；位于心外膜外的
Located outside the heart, specifically outside the myocardium (the heart muscle).
The exocardial surface of the heart is covered by the pericardium. 心脏的心外膜表面被心包膜覆盖。
The doctor identified an exocardial lesion. 医生发现了一个心外膜病灶。
近义词:subepicardial
反义词:endocardial
词性:adjective
