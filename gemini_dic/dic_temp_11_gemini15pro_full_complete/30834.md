chabasite:/ˈkæbəˌsaɪt/ /ˈtʃæbəˌsaɪt/
n.
菱沸石
A group of tectosilicate minerals within the zeolite family. Chabazite occurs as a series of minerals, rather than a single mineral, and varies in silicon-aluminum ratio.
Chabazite is often used as a molecular sieve due to its porous structure. 菱沸石由于其多孔结构，常被用作分子筛。
Chabazite crystals are typically rhombohedral and can be white, pink, or colorless. 菱沸石晶体通常呈菱面体，可以是白色、粉红色或无色。
近义词:zeolite (沸石)
