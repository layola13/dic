extraversion:/ˌekstrəˈvɜːrʒən/
noun
外向性，外向性格
The act, state, or habit of being predominantly concerned with obtaining gratification from what is outside the self.
Her extraversion makes her a natural leader. 她的外向性格使她成为天生的领导者。
近义词：sociability, gregariousness
反义词：introversion
词性：n.

noun
外向性（复数）
Plural form of extraversion, referring to multiple instances or types of outward-focused behavior.
The study explored the different extraversions manifested in various cultures. 这项研究探讨了不同文化中表现出的不同类型的性格外向。
近义词：sociabilities, gregariousnesses (less common)
反义词：introversions
词性：n.
