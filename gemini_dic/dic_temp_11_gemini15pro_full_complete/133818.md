nonglobular:/ˌnɒnˈɡlɒbjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 非球形的，非球状的 | Not globular; not spherical or rounded. |  The protein has a nonglobular structure, which contributes to its fibrous nature.  这种蛋白质具有非球形结构，这有助于其纤维状性质。

The artist created a sculpture with nonglobular shapes, challenging traditional aesthetics.  这位艺术家创作了一个具有非球状形状的雕塑，挑战了传统的审美观。| Near synonyms: nonspherical, amorphous, irregular | Antonyms: globular, spherical, rounded | adjective
