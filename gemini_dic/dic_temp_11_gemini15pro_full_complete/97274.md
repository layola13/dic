influencer:/ˈɪnfluənsər/| n. |影响者；有影响力的人|A person who affects or changes the way that other people behave, for example by making recommendations about things they should buy or fashion they should wear.|Many teenagers trust influencers more than traditional celebrities. 许多青少年比相信传统名人更相信网红。|The influencer posted a video about the new phone.  这个网红发布了一个关于新款手机的视频。|近义词：leader, trendsetter, authority, opinion leader|反义词:follower|noun
