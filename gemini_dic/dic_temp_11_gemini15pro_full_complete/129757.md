non-executive:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv/
| adj. n.|非执行的；非执行董事|Not involved in the day-to-day management of a company, organization, etc., but responsible for making decisions about the way it is run.|He is a non-executive director of the company. 他是这家公司的非执行董事。|Synonym: independent, advisory|Antonym: executive|adjective

non-executive:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv/
| n.|非执行董事|A member of a company's board of directors who is not involved in the day-to-day management of the company.|The non-executives on the board provide valuable independent oversight. 董事会中的非执行董事提供了宝贵的独立监督。|Synonym: independent director, outside director|Antonym: executive director|noun
