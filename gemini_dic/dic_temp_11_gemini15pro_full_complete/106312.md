kobellite:/ˈkoʊbəˌlaɪt/| n.|针硫铋铅矿|A mineral species, lead bismuth sulfide, Pb2(Bi,Sb)2S5, that occurs as metallic, radiating, acicular crystals.|The kobellite sample exhibited a distinct metallic luster. 这块针硫铋铅矿样品展现出明显的金属光泽。|Synonyms:cosalite, bismuthinite|Antonyms: None|noun
