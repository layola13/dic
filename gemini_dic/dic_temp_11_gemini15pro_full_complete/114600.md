malebranchism:/ˈmeɪlbrɑːŋʃɪzəm/
|n.|
|马勒伯朗士主义; occasionalism; occasionalism|
|The philosophical doctrine of Malebranche, which holds that God is the only true cause, and that what we call secondary causes are only occasions on which God acts.  It denies any causal efficacy to created things, asserting that they are merely occasions for God's intervention.|
|Malebranchism posits that God is the direct cause of all events, including human actions. 马勒伯朗士主义认为上帝是所有事件的直接原因，包括人类行为。|

|The theory of occasionalism, as espoused in Malebranchism, is a complex theological concept.  正如马勒伯朗士主义所拥护的那样，偶然主义理论是一个复杂的神学概念。|
|近义词: occasionalism|
|反义词: (No direct antonym, but contrasting philosophies could include determinism, materialism, or pantheism)|
