nonsuppressivenesses:/ˌnɒnsəˈprɛsɪvnəsnɪsɪz/ | n. | 非抑制性；非压抑性 |  The state or quality of not being suppressive, typically referring to the immune system's inability to suppress or control the growth or activity of something (e.g., a virus, cancer cells).  It signifies a lack of control or regulation.  | The patient's nonsuppressivenesses regarding the viral infection led to rapid disease progression. 病人对病毒感染的非抑制性导致疾病迅速发展。 |  The multiple nonsuppressivenesses observed in the patient's immune response were alarming. 在病人免疫反应中观察到的多重非抑制性令人担忧。 | Near synonyms:  lack of control, inability to suppress, unresponsiveness | Antonyms:  suppression, control, regulation | Noun
