noncriticizing:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɪtɪsaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
不加批评的；不挑剔的
Not criticizing; accepting without judgment.
She adopted a noncriticizing attitude towards her son's artwork. 她对儿子的艺术作品采取了不加批评的态度。
He listened with a noncriticizing ear. 他带着不加评判的态度倾听。
近义词: accepting, tolerant, supportive, understanding, nonjudgmental
反义词: critical, judgmental, disapproving, censorious
