clavellated:/ˈklævəˌleɪtɪd/
adjective
成棒状的，有小棒的
Furnished or armed with small clubs or clavules.  Shaped like a small club.
The antennae of the insect are clavellated, giving it a distinctive appearance.  这种昆虫的触角成棒状，赋予它独特的外观。
Near synonyms: club-shaped, clubbed
Antonyms: smooth, unadorned
词性: adjective
