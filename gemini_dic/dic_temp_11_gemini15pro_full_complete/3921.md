agriculturist:/ˈæɡrɪˌkʌltʃərɪst/
n.
农学家；农业工作者
A person who is skilled in or practices agriculture.
例句：The agriculturist advised the farmers on the best time to plant their crops.  这位农学家建议农民们最佳的播种时间。

例句：She is a renowned agriculturist known for her research on sustainable farming practices. 她是一位著名的农学家，以其对可持续农业实践的研究而闻名。
近义词：farmer, grower, cultivator
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *agricultor, from Latin agri cultura ("cultivation of the field"), from ager ("field") + cultura ("cultivation").
