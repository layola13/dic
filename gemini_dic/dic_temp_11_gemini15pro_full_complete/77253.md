gastronomically: /ˌɡæstrəˈnɒmɪkli/ | adv. | 美食地；讲究地；从美食角度 |  In a way that relates to the art and practice of good eating. |  The meal was gastronomically exquisite. 这顿饭精致美味。| 近义词: culinarily, epicureanly | 反义词: plainly, simply | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From French *gastronomique*, from *gastronomie* (“gastronomy”) + *-ique* (“-ic”), ultimately from Ancient Greek *γαστήρ* (*gastḗr*, “belly, stomach”) + *νόμος* (*nómos*, “law, custom”).
