departeds: /dɪˈpɑːrtɪdz/ | n. |死者，逝者|  This word is not standard English.  While "departed" can be used as an adjective (e.g., "our departed friends"),  "departeds" as a plural noun is grammatically incorrect.  The correct way to refer to multiple deceased people is "the departed" or "those who have departed". | *Incorrect usage:* The departeds will be remembered.| Near Synonyms: the deceased, the dead | Antonyms: the living | Noun
