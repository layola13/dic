frangulinic:/fræŋˈɡjʊlɪnɪk/ | adj. |弗angula酸的，鼠李酸的 | Relating to or derived from frangulin, a glycoside found in the bark of certain shrubs, especially the alder buckthorn (Frangula alnus), and used as a laxative.  |  Frangulinic acid is a hydrolysis product of frangulin. 鼠李酸是弗angula的一种水解产物。 |Synonyms:  emodinic (somewhat similar in chemical structure and effect)  | Antonyms:  None | Adjective
