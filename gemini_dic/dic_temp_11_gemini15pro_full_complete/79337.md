glee-clubs:/ˈɡliː klʌbz/
n.
合唱团，欢乐合唱团
A glee club is a group of people who sing together for pleasure, typically performing choral music.
Our college has a very active glee club.  我们大学有一个非常活跃的合唱团。
The glee club performed a medley of Broadway hits. 合唱团表演了百老汇热门歌曲的串烧。
近义词:choir, chorus
反义词: (无直接的反义词，可以考虑用soloist 独唱者)
词性:noun
