extracapsular:/ˌekstrəkæpsjʊlər/
adjective
囊外的；包膜外的
Located or occurring outside a capsule, especially the capsule of a joint or organ.
The surgeon performed an extracapsular cataract extraction.  外科医生进行了囊外白内障摘除术。
Extracapsular fractures are more common than intracapsular fractures. 囊外骨折比囊内骨折更常见。

Synonyms: extracapsular
Antonyms: intracapsular
