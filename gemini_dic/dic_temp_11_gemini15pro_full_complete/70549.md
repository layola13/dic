filigraned:/ˈfɪlɪɡreɪnd/
adjective
饰以金银丝细工的；精细复杂的
decorated with delicate and intricate ornamentation (typically made of gold or silver wire)
The antique box was filigreed with silver.  古董盒子上饰有银丝。
The lawyer presented a filigreed argument. 律师提出了一个精细复杂的论点。
Synonyms: ornate, intricate, delicate, elaborate
Antonyms: plain, simple, unadorned
词性: adjective
