accuse-of:/əˈkjuːz ʌv/| vt.|指控，控告|To blame someone for something; to charge someone with wrongdoing.  To say that someone has done something wrong or illegal.|The police accused him of theft. 警方指控他盗窃。|Synonyms: blame, charge, indict, incriminate|Antonyms: defend, exonerate, acquit|verb
