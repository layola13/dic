mothering:/ˈmʌðərɪŋ/| n. adj.|母性的，养育，溺爱|The act of caring for someone as a mother would, providing nurture, comfort, and protection.|She felt a surge of mothering towards the lost child. 她对这个迷路的孩子涌起一股母性之情。|近义词：nurturing, caring, protective|反义词：neglecting, abandoning|词性:名词,形容词

Etymology: From "mother" (Old English "mōdor") + "-ing" (suffix forming nouns from verbs, indicating the action or process).
