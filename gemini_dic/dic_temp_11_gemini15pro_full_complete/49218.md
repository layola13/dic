delectus: /dɪˈlɛktəs/ /diːˈlɛktəs/
noun
选择, 挑选, 精选
A careful selection or choice of the best or most desirable.
The anthology is a delectus of the finest poems of the 20th century.  这本选集是20世纪最佳诗歌的精选。
synonyms: selection, choice, anthology, collection, compilation
antonyms: rejection, exclusion, omission
n.
