bordar:/bɔrˈðar/ (Spanish), /bɔːrˈdɑːr/ (English approximation)| v. |绣边，镶边；接近，毗邻|To embroider, to edge or border something, or to be close to or bordering on something (often a quality or concept).|She bordered the tablecloth with delicate lace. 她给桌布绣上了精致的花边。

His behavior bordered on rudeness. 他的行为近乎粗鲁。|近义词：edge, trim, border (as a noun), adjoin, verge on|反义词: (For bordering on)  be far from, be nowhere near|verb
