escuage:/ˈɛskjʊɪdʒ/ | n. | 盾牌钱，免役税 | A monetary payment made in lieu of military service, especially during the feudal era. |  In medieval England, escuage was a common way for knights to avoid personal military service. 在中世纪的英格兰，盾牌钱是骑士避免个人兵役的常见方式。| synonyms: scutage, shield money | antonyms: military service | noun
