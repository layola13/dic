collybist:/ˈkɒlɪbɪst/
noun
货币兑换商
a money changer, especially one dealing in foreign currencies
In medieval times, collybists played a crucial role in facilitating international trade. 在中世纪，货币兑换商在促进国际贸易方面发挥了至关重要的作用。
Synonyms: money changer, exchange broker
Antonyms: client, customer
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *collybiste*, from Late Latin *collȳbista*, from Latin *collȳba* ("small coin, money"). Ultimately from Greek *κόλλυβος* (kóllybos, “small coin”).
