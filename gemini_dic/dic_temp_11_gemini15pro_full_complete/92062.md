hyperprolactinaemic:/ˌhaɪpəˌproʊlæktɪˈniːmɪk/ | adj. | 高泌乳素血症的 |  Characterized by or relating to hyperprolactinaemia, a condition in which there is an abnormally high level of prolactin in the blood. | Hyperprolactinaemic patients may experience infertility.  高泌乳素血症患者可能会经历不孕。| Hyperprolactinemic | Normo-prolactinaemic | adjective

Etymology:
* hyper- (Greek):  "over, above, beyond"
* prolactin (Greek, Latin):  "pro-" (before, forth) + "lac" (milk)  referring to the hormone's role in milk production.
* -aemia (Greek): "blood condition"
* -ic (Greek): forming adjectives
