corked:/kɔːrkt/ | adj. | 软木塞味的；（因软木塞污染）变味的；无生气的；沉闷的 | Affected with cork taint, a musty flavor or odor in wine caused by a contaminated cork; lacking vitality or spirit; dull. | This wine tastes corked. 这酒有软木塞味。| 近义词：musty, stale, flat, lifeless | 反义词：fresh, lively, vibrant | 词性: 形容词

corked:/kɔːrkt/ | v. | 用软木塞塞住 | To seal with a cork. | He corked the bottle of wine. 他用软木塞塞住了葡萄酒瓶。| 近义词：seal, stopper, plug | 反义词：uncork, open | 词性: 动词
