bittock:/ˈbɪtək/
noun
小块; 少量; 一点儿
A small piece or amount of something.
He ate a bittock of cheese. 他吃了一小块奶酪。
Synonyms: morsel, piece, fragment, scrap, bit
Antonyms: lot, whole, abundance
词性: 名词
