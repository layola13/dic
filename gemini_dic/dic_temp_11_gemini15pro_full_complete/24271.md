brigade:/brɪˈɡeɪd/| n.|旅，队，组|A group of people organized for a particular purpose, especially soldiers or firefighters.|例句：The fire brigade arrived quickly at the scene. 消防队迅速赶到现场。

例句：A cleaning brigade was organized to tidy up the park.  一个清洁队被组织起来清理公园。|近义词：team, group, troop, unit, corps|反义词:|noun
