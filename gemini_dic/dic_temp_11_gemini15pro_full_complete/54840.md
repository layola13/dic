displaceable:/dɪsˈpleɪsəbl/
adjective
可替代的; 可置换的; 可移动的
Able to be moved or put in a different place.  Something that can be displaced or replaced by something else.
The refugees are living in displaceable tents. 这些难民住在可移动的帐篷里。
The battery is displaceable and can be easily changed.  这块电池是可拆卸的，可以轻松更换。
synonyms: removable, replaceable, portable, mobile
antonyms: fixed, permanent, immovable
词性: adjective
