isochromatic:/ˌaɪsəʊkrəˈmætɪk/
adjective
等色的，同色的
Having the same color or shade.  Relating to or denoting the subjective equality of brightness between two colors of different hues.
The isochromatic lines on the map indicated areas of equal elevation.  地图上的等色线表示海拔相同的区域。
Synonyms:  monochromatic, homochromatic
Antonyms:  polychromatic, heterochromatic
词性: adjective
