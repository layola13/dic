mitigating:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪtɪŋ/| adj.| 减轻的，缓和的 | making something less harmful, serious, or painful | The judge took his mitigating circumstances into account when sentencing him. 法官在量刑时考虑了他的减轻处罚情节。|近义词：alleviating, lessening, reducing, extenuating |反义词: aggravating, exacerbating |词性:形容词
词源: From Latin mitigatus, past participle of mitigare "to soften, make mild," from mitis "soft, mild" + -igare (related to agere "to do, drive, act").
