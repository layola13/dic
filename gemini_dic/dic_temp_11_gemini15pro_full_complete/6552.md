amylometer:/ˌæmɪˈlɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌæmɪˈlɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|淀粉测定仪；糊化仪|An instrument used to measure the viscosity of starch paste, which indicates the enzyme activity of alpha-amylase.  It is used to determine the starch quality and suitability for various industrial applications.|The amylometer showed a rapid increase in viscosity, indicating high enzyme activity.|Synonyms: viscometer (for general viscosity measurement)|Antonyms: None|noun

amylometer:/ˌæmɪˈlɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌæmɪˈlɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|淀粉品质测定器|A device used to measure the thickening power of starch.|The baker used an amylometer to ensure the consistency of his bread dough.|Synonyms: Consistometer|Antonyms: None|noun
