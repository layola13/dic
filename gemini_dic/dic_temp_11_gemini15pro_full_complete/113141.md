lunk:/lʌŋk/ | n. | 笨蛋，傻瓜；大块头（通常指笨拙的）| a clumsy, stupid person; a large, awkward person |  He called me a lunk because I tripped over the rug. 他叫我笨蛋，因为我被地毯绊倒了。| synonyms: oaf, dolt, klutz, lummox | antonyms: genius, whiz, expert | noun

lunks:/lʌŋks/ | n. | 笨蛋们，傻瓜们；大块头们 | plural form of "lunk" | Those lunks dropped the piano down the stairs.  那些笨蛋把钢琴从楼梯上摔下来了。 | synonyms: oafs, dolts, klutzes, lummoxes | antonyms: geniuses, whizzes, experts | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Possibly from dialectal *lunk, "to move heavily and clumsily," or related to lump.
