columbium:/kəˈlʌmbiəm/| n.|铌|A metallic chemical element, atomic number 41, with symbol Cb (now officially niobium). It is a soft, grey, ductile transition metal, which is often found associated with tantalum.  It was originally called columbium, after the mineral columbite, from which it was extracted.|Columbium was once used in the production of certain types of steel. 铌曾用于某些类型钢的生产。|Synonym: niobium|Antonym: N/A|noun
