kangarooer:/ˌkæŋɡəˈruːər/
n.
袋鼠獵人;捕捉袋鼠的人
A person who hunts or traps kangaroos.
He was a skilled kangarooer, known for his ability to track the animals. 他是一位熟练的袋鼠猎人，以追踪动物的能力而闻名。
近义词: kangaroo hunter, trapper
反义词: kangaroo protector, conservationist
词性: 名词
