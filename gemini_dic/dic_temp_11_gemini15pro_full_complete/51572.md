deuthydroguret: /djuːtɪˈhaɪdrəɡjʊərɛt/ | n. |氘化物; 重氢化物 | A compound containing deuterium.  A compound in which one or more hydrogen atoms have been replaced by deuterium. | Example:  Lithium deuteride is a deuterohydride of lithium. 氘化锂是锂的氘化物。

Example:  The synthesis of the deuterohydride was challenging.  合成这种氘化物很有挑战性。 | 近义词: deuteride | 反义词: hydride | 词性: noun
