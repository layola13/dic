briner:/ˈbraɪnər/| n.|盐水缸，盐水池，腌制者|A container filled with brine, used for pickling or preserving food; a person who brines food.|The chef placed the turkey in the briner overnight. 厨师把火鸡放在盐水缸里浸泡了一夜。|近义词：pickler, curer|反义词:|n.

brine:/braɪn/| n. vt.|盐水，海水；用盐水浸泡|Salt water, especially a strong solution used for pickling or preserving food; to soak or preserve in brine.|The olives were packed in brine. 橄榄浸泡在盐水中。  She brined the chicken before roasting it. 她烤鸡前先用盐水浸泡。|近义词：saltwater, seawater (for the noun); pickle, cure (for the verb)|反义词: freshwater (for the noun)|n. vt.
