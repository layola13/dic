communed:/kəˈmjuːnd/ |vi.|交流；共享；领圣餐|To feel a deep sense of connection and understanding with someone or something, often in a spiritual or emotional way.  Also means to receive Holy Communion.|例句：She communed with nature, finding peace in the quiet forest.  她在与大自然交流中，在宁静的森林里找到了平静。|近义词：connect, share, commune, partake|反义词:disconnect, isolate|verb
