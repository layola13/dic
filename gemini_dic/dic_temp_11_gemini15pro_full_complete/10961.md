arbitraging:/ˈɑːrbɪtrɑːʒɪŋ/ | n. | 套利，套汇 | The simultaneous buying and selling of securities, currency, or commodities in different markets or in derivative forms in order to take advantage of differing prices for the same asset. |  He made a fortune by arbitraging between the London and New York stock exchanges. 他通过在伦敦和纽约证券交易所之间进行套利赚了一大笔钱。| synonyms: profiting from price differences, speculation, trading | antonyms: investing, long-term holding | noun

arbitrage:/ˈɑːrbɪtrɑːʒ/ | n. v. | 套利，套汇 |  The simultaneous buying and selling of securities, currency, or commodities in different markets or in derivative forms in order to take advantage of differing prices for the same asset.  | He plans to arbitrage the price difference between gold in London and New York.  他计划利用伦敦和纽约黄金的价差进行套利。 | synonyms: exploit price differences, speculate, trade | antonyms: invest, hold long-term | noun, verb
