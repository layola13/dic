clear-sightednesses: /ˌklɪərˈsaɪtɪdnɪsɪz/ | n. | 清明, 洞察力,远见卓识 | The state or quality of being clear-sighted; the ability to see clearly or understand situations and problems well.  |  His clear-sightednesses allowed him to anticipate the market crash. 他的远见让他预料到了市场崩盘。 |  perspicacity, acuity, insight, vision | short-sightedness, myopia, obtuseness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From clear-sighted + -ness.  "clear-sighted"  itself comes from "clear" (Old French cler, from Latin clarus) + "sighted" (Old English gesīhted).
