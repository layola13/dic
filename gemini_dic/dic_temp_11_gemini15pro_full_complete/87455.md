hesperidene:/ˌhɛspəˈrɪdiːn/ | n. |橙皮苷，橙皮烯|A colorless, crystalline hydrocarbon, C10H16, found in the essential oils of citrus fruits.| Hesperidene is a major component of orange oil. 橙皮苷是橙油的主要成分。| The refreshing scent of hesperidene filled the air. 空气中弥漫着橙皮苷清爽的香味。|Synonyms: limonene, d-limonene| Antonyms: None| noun
