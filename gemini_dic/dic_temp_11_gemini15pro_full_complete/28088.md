capillary:/ˈkæpɪˌleri/ (US), /kəˈpɪləri/ (UK)| adj. n.|毛细血管的；毛状的；毛细管；|A capillary is an extremely narrow blood vessel.|The capillaries are the smallest of the blood vessels.|近义词：minute, fine, hairlike|反义词:large, thick|词性:形容词, 名词
capillature:/ˈkæpɪlətʃər/ (US), /kəˈpɪlətʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|毛细血管系统；毛细作用；|The capillature is the network of capillaries in a particular area or organ.|The drug is absorbed into the capillature.|近义词：capillary network, capillary system|反义词: (None -  lacks a direct antonym)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *capillātūra, from Latin capillāris (“hair-like”), from capillus (“hair”).
