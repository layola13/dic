man:/mæn/ | n. | 男人；人类；人；丈夫；（国际象棋的）兵；（某些牌戏的）一人 | A male human adult.  A human being of either sex. A piece used in various board games, often representing a soldier or other male figure. | Example: That man is tall. 那个男人很高。| Example: All men are created equal. 人人生而平等。 | Synonyms: male, human, person, husband, guy, fellow, chap | Antonyms: woman, female | Noun


man:/mæn/ | vt. | 为…配备人员；操纵；使振作 | To supply with men, especially for service or defense. To strengthen or fortify (something, such as a wall) with men. | Example: They manned the barricades. 他们驻守在街垒。| Example: The ship was manned by an experienced crew. 这艘船配备了经验丰富的船员。| Synonyms: staff, operate, crew | Antonyms: unman, depopulate | Verb
