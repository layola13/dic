daggiest:/ˈdæɡiɪst/| adj.|最土的，最过时的|Superlative form of "daggy," meaning unfashionable, outdated, or socially awkward.  It describes something or someone that is extremely out of style or embarrassing. | Example 1: That's the daggiest sweater I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最土的毛衣。

Example 2: He wore the daggiest clothes to the party. 他穿着最过时的衣服去参加了派对。|Synonyms: unfashionable, outdated, frumpy, dowdy, unstylish, naff (UK)|Antonyms: fashionable, stylish, trendy, chic, cool|adjective

Etymology->
From Australian slang "dag," meaning a piece of dried dung hanging from a sheep's fleece. The term later evolved to mean unfashionable or socially awkward, likely due to the association of dags with unkemptness and rural life.
