marginal:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnl/ | adj. | 边缘的，微小的，不重要的 | relating to or situated at the edge or margin of something; small and not very important | The marginal cost of producing another unit is relatively low.  生产额外一个单位的边际成本相对较低。| synonyms: peripheral, borderline, insignificant, minimal | antonyms: central, core, significant, major | adjective

marginal:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnl/ | n. | 边缘地区，边缘席位 | an area near a border or edge, or a seat in parliament held by a small majority |  The company operates in the marginal areas of the market. 这家公司在市场的边缘地带运营。 | synonyms: periphery, border, fringe | antonyms: center, core | noun
