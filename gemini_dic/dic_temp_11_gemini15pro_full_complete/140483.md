old-womanishness:/ˌoʊld ˈwʊmənɪʃnəs/ | n. | 老态龙钟,  女人气,  婆婆妈妈 | The state of having qualities or characteristics considered typical of an old woman, often used in a disapproving way, suggesting fussiness, timidity, or conservatism. | Her old-womanishness prevented her from trying anything new. 她老气横秋的性格使她不敢尝试任何新事物。| 近义词: fussiness, timidity, conservatism, prudishness | 反义词: boldness, daring, adventurousness | 词性: noun

词源: From "old woman" + "-ish" (forming adjectives meaning "like" or "having the qualities of") + "-ness" (forming nouns from adjectives).
