countered:/ˈkaʊntərd/| v.|反击，对抗，应对|To meet or oppose, especially in response to something.|The company countered the negative publicity with a series of positive advertisements.  公司以一系列正面广告来反击负面宣传。|
countered:/ˈkaʊntərd/| v.|反驳，提出相反意见|To offer in opposition.|He countered her argument with a witty remark. 他用一句机智的话反驳了她的论点。|
countered:/ˈkaʊntərd/| adj.|相反的，相对的|(Less common) Opposite or contrary.|The countered force was strong. 相反的力量很强。|近义词: opposed, resisted, confronted, rebutted, contradicted|反义词: supported, agreed, yielded|词性: Verb (primarily), Adjective (less common)
