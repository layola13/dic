breaststroke:/ˈbrɛstˌstrəʊk/ (US) /ˈbrɛstˌstrəʊk/ (UK)| n.|蛙泳|A swimming stroke performed on the chest, with both arms moving symmetrically outward and backward while the legs are tucked in and then kicked out in a propulsive motion.|He won the race using the breaststroke. 他用蛙泳赢得了比赛。|近义词: breaststroke swimming |反义词: crawl, backstroke|noun


breaststrokes:/ˈbrɛstˌstrəʊks/ (US) /ˈbrɛstˌstrəʊks/ (UK)| n.|蛙泳(复数形式，指多次蛙泳动作或不同种类的蛙泳)|Multiple instances or variations of the breaststroke swimming technique. |She practiced several different breaststrokes to improve her speed. 她练习了几种不同的蛙泳以提高速度。|近义词: breaststroke variations |反义词: crawl strokes, backstrokes|noun
