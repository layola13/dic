ETYMOLOGY->
From Medieval Latin *alcohol, from Arabic الكحل (al-kuḥl, “kohl, powdered antimony”), referring to a fine powder produced by sublimation. The term was later used to refer to any sublimated substance, then any liquid obtained by distillation, including wine spirits, and finally restricted to ethanol.
