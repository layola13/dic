comforter:/ˈkʌmfərtər/ (US) /ˈkʌmfətə(r)/ (UK)| n.|安慰者；被子；棉被|A person or thing that comforts; a thick, padded quilt or bedspread. |例句：
The soft comforter kept her warm all night.  柔软的被子让她整夜都很温暖。

She found a comforter in her best friend during a difficult time. 在困难时期，她从她最好的朋友那里找到了安慰。

|近义词：quilt, duvet, blanket, consoler |反义词:  distress, discomfort |词性: noun
