discontinuing:/dɪskənˈtɪnjuɪŋ/
Verb
停止，中断
The act of ending something, usually a regular activity or process.
The company is discontinuing production of this model.  该公司将停止生产这款型号。
They are discontinuing the old software. 他们将停止使用旧软件。
Synonyms: ceasing, halting, abandoning, terminating, stopping
Antonyms: continuing, starting, commencing, initiating
