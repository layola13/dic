churners:/ˈtʃɜːrnərz/
n.
频繁更换服务提供商的用户；流失客户
Customers who frequently change service providers; those who contribute to customer churn.
Telecommunication companies are trying to reduce churners by offering better deals. 电信公司正在努力通过提供更好的优惠来减少客户流失。
High churners cost businesses a lot of money.  高流失率的客户会让企业损失很多钱。
synonyms: switchers, defectors
antonyms: loyal customers
noun
