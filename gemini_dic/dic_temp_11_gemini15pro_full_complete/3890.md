agreeably:/əˈɡriːəbli/ | adv. | 欣然地，愉快地；适宜地 | In a pleasing or agreeable manner; pleasantly; suitably. | The evening passed agreeably.  晚上过得很愉快。| 近义词: pleasantly, nicely, amiably, genially | 反义词: unpleasantly, disagreeably | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *agreable, from Anglo-Norman agreable, from Old French agreable, from agreer (“to please”), from Late Latin *aggrātus, from Latin grātus (“pleasing, agreeable”) + ad- (“to”).
