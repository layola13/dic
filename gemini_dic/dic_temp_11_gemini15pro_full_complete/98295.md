insider:/ɪnˈsaɪdər/ (US), /ɪnˈsaɪdə/ (UK)| n. |知情人，内部人员|A person who has special knowledge or access to confidential information within a group or organization.|The insider leaked crucial information to the press. 知情人士向媒体泄露了关键信息。|Synonyms: informant, confidant, expert|Antonyms: outsider, stranger|Noun


insider:/ɪnˈsaɪdər/ (US), /ɪnˈsaɪdə/ (UK)| adj. |内部的，秘密的|Relating to or characteristic of an insider.|Insider trading is illegal. 内幕交易是非法的。|Synonyms: internal, confidential, private|Antonyms: external, public, open|Adjective
