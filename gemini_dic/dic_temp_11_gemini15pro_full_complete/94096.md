词源: From Late Latin *impavāre, from in- + pāvāre (“to pave”), from Latin pavīre (“to beat, ram down”), from pāvīmentum (“a hard, rammed-down floor”).


注意事项:  While "impave" exists and is a legitimate word, it is significantly less common than "pave". In almost all cases, "pave" is the preferred and more natural choice.  Using "impave" might sound overly formal or even archaic.
