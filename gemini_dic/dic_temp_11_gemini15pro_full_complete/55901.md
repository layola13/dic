divorces: /dɪˈvɔːrsɪz/ | n. | 离婚（复数）| The legal dissolution of a marriage.  The ending of a marriage by a legal process. |  Many divorces occur due to irreconcilable differences. 许多离婚是由于不可调和的分歧造成的。

The rate of divorces has increased in recent years. 近年来离婚率有所上升。 | 近义词：separations, dissolutions | 反义词：marriages, unions | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Old French *divorcer, from Latin *divortiare “to separate, go different ways,” from divertere “to turn aside, turn away,” from dis- “apart” + vertere “to turn.”
