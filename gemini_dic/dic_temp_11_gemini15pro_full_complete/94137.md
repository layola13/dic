impedimenta:/ɪmˌpɛdɪˈmɛntə/
n.
辎重，累赘，障碍
baggage or other things that make movement difficult
The hikers sent their impedimenta up the mountain by mule.  徒步旅行者用骡子把他们的辎重送上山。

近义词: baggage, luggage, encumbrance, burden, obstacle, hindrance
反义词: assistance, aid, help

词性: 名词 (Noun)

词源:  Late Middle English: from Latin *impedamentum, from impedire ‘impede,’ from in- ‘in, into’ + pes, ped- ‘foot.’
