apnoeic:/æpˈniːɪk/ | adj. | 呼吸暂停的，窒息的 | Relating to or characterized by apnea (temporary cessation of breathing). | The patient experienced an apnoeic episode during surgery. 病人在手术期间经历了一次呼吸暂停。|  Near synonyms: asphyxial, suffocative | Antonyms: breathing, respiring | adjective

apnea:/ˈæpniə/ | n. | 呼吸暂停 | Temporary cessation of breathing, especially during sleep. | Sleep apnea can be a serious health concern. 睡眠呼吸暂停可能是一个严重的健康问题。 | Near synonyms: asphyxia, suffocation | Antonyms: breathing, respiration | noun
