boatswain:/ˈboʊsən/ (US) /ˈbəʊsn/ (UK)| n.| 水手长；甲板长|A petty officer on a ship who is in charge of the deck crew and responsible for maintaining equipment.  This person pipes orders using a boatswain's whistle.|The boatswain piped the crew to quarters. 水手长用哨子吹集合信号让船员们到住处集合。|Synonyms: deck officer, bosun|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps subordinate sailor)|Noun
