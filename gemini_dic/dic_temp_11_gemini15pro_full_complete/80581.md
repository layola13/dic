goofily: /ˈɡuːfɪli/ | adv. | 愚蠢地，傻乎乎地 | In a silly or foolish manner. | He grinned goofily at the camera. 他对着镜头傻乎乎地咧嘴一笑。|  He danced goofily around the room. 他傻乎乎地在房间里跳舞。 | 近义词：foolishly, sillily, absurdly | 反义词：seriously, sensibly, wisely | 词性: 副词

goofy: /ˈɡuːfi/ | adj. | 愚蠢的，傻的 | Silly or foolish. | He had a goofy grin on his face.  他脸上带着傻乎乎的笑容。| It was a goofy movie, but we laughed a lot. 这是一部傻乎乎的电影，但我们笑了很多。 | 近义词：silly, foolish, absurd | 反义词：serious, sensible, wise | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Likely a variant of "goff" or "gawf," meaning "fool" or "clown," which dates back to the 19th century.  "Goofy" as an adjective appeared first, with "goofily" as the adverbial form following later.
