non-Semite:/ˌnɒnˈsɛmaɪt/ | n. | 非闪米特人 | A person who is not a Semite (a member of any of the peoples who speak or spoke a Semitic language, such as Hebrew, Arabic, or Aramaic). |  Example:  He argued that the policy was unfairly targeting non-Semites. 他认为这项政策不公平地针对非闪米特人。

| Example:  The community included both Semites and non-Semites.  这个社群包括闪米特人和非闪米特人。 | Synonym: Gentile | Antonym: Semite | Noun

non-Semitic:/ˌnɒn səˈmɪtɪk/ | adj. | 非闪米特的 | Not Semitic; not of or relating to the Semites or their languages. | Example: The museum houses a collection of non-Semitic artifacts.  这家博物馆收藏了一系列非闪族文物。

| Example:  They studied both Semitic and non-Semitic languages.  他们研究了闪米特语和非闪米特语。 | Synonym: Gentile | Antonym: Semitic | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From "non-" (not) + "Semite" (a member of a group of peoples of Southwest Asia including Arabs and Jews).  "Semite" ultimately derives from the biblical name "Shem," one of the sons of Noah.
