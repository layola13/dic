doominess:/ˈduːmɪnɪs/| n.|厄运；注定失败；悲观情绪|A state of being doomed, or appearing likely to have a negative outcome; a feeling of impending misfortune or disaster.|The doominess of the situation was palpable.  这种局势的厄运感显而易见。|The film's doominess permeated the entire cinema. 这部电影的悲观气氛弥漫了整个电影院。|近义词：gloom, pessimism, bleakness, despair|反义词：optimism, hope, cheerfulness|词性:名词

词源: From "doom" (Old English *dōm "judgment, decree, destiny") + "-iness" (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality or state).
