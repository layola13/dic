jamesonite:/ˈdʒeɪmzəˌnaɪt/| n. |硫锑铅矿|A dark gray metallic mineral, a lead antimony sulfide (Pb4FeSb6S14), that is an ore of antimony. |The jamesonite formed veinlets in the quartz.|近义词：stibnite, boulangerite|反义词: N/A|noun
