offside:/ˌɒfˈsaɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈsaɪd/| adjective, noun, adverb|越位的，越位位置，在越位位置|In soccer, a player is offside if they are nearer to their opponent's goal line than both the ball and the second-to-last opponent when the ball is played to them.  In general, it means being in an unfavorable or disadvantageous position.|例句：The striker was clearly offside when he received the pass.  那名前锋接球时明显越位了。|近义词：illegal, ineligible|反义词：onside|词性：adjective, noun, adverb

offside:/ˌɒfˈsaɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈsaɪd/|noun|越位，越位位置|The position of being offside in a game, especially soccer.|例句：The referee blew the whistle for offside. 裁判吹哨判罚越位。|近义词：invalid position|反义词：onside position|词性：noun

offside:/ˌɒfˈsaɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈsaɪd/|adverb|在越位位置|In an offside position.|例句：The player strayed offside.  这名球员跑到了越位位置。|近义词：illegally positioned|反义词：onside|词性：adverb
