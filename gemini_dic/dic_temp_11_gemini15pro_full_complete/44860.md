crutched:/krʌtʃt/
adjective
拄着拐杖的；用拐杖支撑的
supported by a crutch or crutches.
He crutched his way across the room. 他拄着拐杖走过房间。
近义词：supported, propped
反义词：unsupported

crutch:/krʌtʃ/
noun
拐杖
a staff or support used by injured or infirm persons to assist them in walking
She had to use crutches after the accident. 事故发生后她不得不使用拐杖。
近义词：support, prop
反义词：none

verb
用拐杖支撑
to support with a crutch
He crutched his injured leg. 他用拐杖支撑着他受伤的腿。
近义词：support, prop
反义词：none
