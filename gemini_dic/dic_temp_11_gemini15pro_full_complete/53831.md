disconsecrate:/ˌdɪskɒnˈsɪkreɪt/ /ˌdɪskɑːnˈseɪkreɪt/| vt.| 撤销圣职；使不再神圣；亵渎|To deprive (a consecrated thing or place) of its sacred character; to treat (something sacred) with disrespect.|The altar was disconsecrated after the church closed. 教堂关闭后，祭坛被撤销了圣职。| The vandals disconsecrated the cemetery by spray-painting graffiti on the tombstones. 故意破坏者在墓碑上喷涂涂鸦，亵渎了墓地。|synonyms: desecrate, profane, defile|antonyms: consecrate, sanctify, hallow|verb
