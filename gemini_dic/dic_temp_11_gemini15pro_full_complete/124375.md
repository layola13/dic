motivelessly:/ˈmoʊtɪvləsli/ | adv. | 无动机地，无缘无故地 | Without a motive; for no apparent reason. | He motivelessly attacked a stranger on the street. 他无缘无故地袭击了街上的一个陌生人。|  She motivelessly quit her job. 她无缘无故地辞掉了工作。| 近义词: aimlessly, pointlessly, senselessly, wantonly | 反义词: purposefully, deliberately, intentionally | 词性: 副词

词源:
From motive + -less + -ly.  "Motive" comes from Old French "motif" ultimately from Latin "movēre" meaning "to move." The suffix "-less" indicates a lack of something, and "-ly" forms adverbs.
