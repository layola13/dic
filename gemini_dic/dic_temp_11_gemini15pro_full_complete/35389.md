co-operationist:/kəʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃənɪst/
n.
合作者，合作主义者
A person who believes in or practices co-operation.
例句：The co-operationist movement gained momentum in the early 20th century.  合作主义运动在20世纪早期获得了发展势头。

例句：He was a staunch co-operationist, believing in the power of collective action. 他是一位坚定的合作主义者，相信集体行动的力量。

近义词：collaborator, partner, ally
反义词：opponent, antagonist
词性: noun

Etymology:
From co-operation + -ist.  Co-operation comes from co- + operation. Operation derives from Latin operari "to work."
