condom:/ˈkɒndɒm/ (UK), /ˈkɑːndəm/ (US)| n. |避孕套，安全套|A thin rubber sheath worn on the penis during sexual intercourse to prevent pregnancy or the spread of sexually transmitted diseases.|Example:  Condoms are an effective method of birth control.  避孕套是一种有效的避孕方法。

Example: Using condoms helps protect against STIs. 使用避孕套有助于预防性传播疾病。|近义词：prophylactic, rubber|反义词: None (Condoms don't have a direct antonym.  The absence of their use might be implied by "unprotected sex," but this isn't a single word antonym.)|词性:noun
