breadroot:/ˈbredˌruːt/| n. |面包根，土茯苓|A starchy root of a North American prairie plant (Psoralea esculenta) used as food by Native Americans.  It's also known as prairie turnip, tipsin, or Indian breadroot.|The Native Americans traditionally harvested breadroot in the fall.|近义词：tipsin, prairie turnip, Indian potato|反义词: None|Noun
