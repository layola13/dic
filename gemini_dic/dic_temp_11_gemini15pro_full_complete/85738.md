hawkeye:/ˈhɔːkaɪ/| n.|鹰眼; 敏锐的目光; 神射手;|A hawkeye is a person with excellent eyesight, especially someone who is skilled at noticing small details. It also refers to a keen observer.  The term is derived from the bird of prey known for its sharp vision. It can also be used in the context of a nickname or code name, notably for the Marvel Comics character Clint Barton. | Hawkeye spotted the tiny error in the document. 鹰眼发现了文件中微小的错误。| 近义词:sharp eye, keen observer, eagle eye, marksman|反义词:blind, unobservant|词性:名词
