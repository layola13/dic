impregnable:/ɪmˈprɛɡnəbəl/| adj.|坚不可摧的；无法攻克的；不受影响的|Unable to be captured or broken into.  Not capable of being overcome or refuted.|例句：The fortress was built on a high cliff, making it virtually impregnable.  这个堡垒建在高悬崖上，几乎坚不可摧。

例句：His arguments were so well-reasoned as to be impregnable. 他的论证如此有理有据，以至于无懈可击。|近义词：invincible, unassailable, unconquerable|反义词：vulnerable, weak, defenseless|词性:形容词
