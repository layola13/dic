dalal:/dɑːˈlɑːl/ | n. | 中间人，经纪人，掮客 | A person who acts as an intermediary, especially between buyers and sellers; a broker. |  He worked as a dalal in the stock market. 他在股票市场做经纪人。| synonyms: broker, agent, middleman, intermediary, go-between | antonyms: buyer, seller, client, customer | noun

dalal:/dɑːˈlɑːl/ | v. | 做中间人，掮客 | To act as a dalal; to broker a deal. | He dalaled the sale of the property. 他为这处房产的出售做中间人。| synonyms: broker, mediate, negotiate, arrange, intermediate | antonyms: obstruct, hinder, impede | verb
