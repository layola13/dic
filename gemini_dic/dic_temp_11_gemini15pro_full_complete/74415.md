franchised:/ˈfræntʃaɪzd/
|adj.|特许的，获得特许经营权的|Granted a franchise; authorized to sell or distribute a company's goods or services in a particular area.|The franchised dealer sells only Ford cars.|近义词：licensed, authorized, accredited|反义词：unfranchised, independent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the Old French word "franchir" meaning "to free," later evolving to "franchise" referring to a privilege or exemption granted by a sovereign. The "-ed" suffix transforms it into an adjective indicating the state of having been granted a franchise.
