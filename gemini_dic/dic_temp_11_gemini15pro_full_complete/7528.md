annex's:/ˈænɛksɪz/
noun
附件的；附属建筑物的
belonging to or associated with an annex (a building added to or adjoining a larger one).
The annex's roof was leaking. 附属建筑的屋顶漏水了。
Synonyms:  appendix's, adjunct's, addition's
Antonyms: main building's, core's

Etymology:
From Late Latin *annexus, past participle of annectere "to tie or bind to," from ad- "to" + nectere "to bind."
