indistinguishably:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbli/
| adv. | 无法区分地，难以辨别地 | in a way that makes it impossible to distinguish between two or more things | The twins were dressed so similarly that they were indistinguishably. 这对双胞胎穿着如此相似，以至于无法区分。 | The two fabrics are virtually indistinguishable in quality.  这两种布料的质量几乎无法区分。| synonym: identically, similarly, alike | antonym: distinctly, differently, separately | adverb

indistinguishable:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/
| adj. | 无法区分的，难以辨别的 | not able to be identified as different or distinct | The two shades of blue are almost indistinguishable. 这两种蓝色几乎无法区分。 |  Her accent is indistinguishable from a native speaker's. 她的口音与母语人士的口音无法区分。 | synonym: identical, similar, alike | antonym: distinct, different, separate | adjective
