edged:/ɛdʒd/ | adj. | 有边的，锋利的，尖锐的，略带…的 | Having a sharp edge or border; slightly tinged with a specified quality or feeling. | The knife had an edged blade. 这把刀有锋利的刀刃。 | 近义词：sharp, keen, pointed, bordered, tinged | 反义词：blunt, dull, rounded | 词性:形容词

edged:/ɛdʒd/ | v. | 缓缓移动，给…加边 | (past tense and past participle of *edge*) Moved gradually or cautiously; furnished with an edge or border. | He edged closer to the door. 他慢慢地靠近门。 | 近义词：inched, sidled, crept, bordered | 反义词：rushed, darted | 词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ecg* (noun) and *ecgian* (verb), meaning "edge" and "to sharpen," respectively.  Related to German *Ecke* (corner).
