inculpate:/ˈɪnkʌlpeɪt/ | vt. | inculpate | to incriminate; to charge with a crime; to blame | The witness's testimony seemed to inculpate the defendant. 目击者的证词似乎将被告定罪。| synonyms: incriminate, accuse, blame | antonyms: exonerate, acquit, vindicate | verb
