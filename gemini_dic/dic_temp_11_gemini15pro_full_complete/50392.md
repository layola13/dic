depose:/dɪˈpoʊz/ /dɪˈpəʊz/ | v. | 免职；罢免；作证 | To remove someone from a position of power, especially a monarch or other ruler; to testify or give evidence, especially in a legal context. | The king was deposed by his own son. 国王被他自己的儿子罢黜了。

The witness deposed that she had seen the defendant at the scene of the crime.  证人作证说她看到被告在犯罪现场。| 近义词：oust, dethrone, remove, testify | 反义词：install, enthrone | verb

deposed:/dɪˈpoʊzd/ /dɪˈpəʊzd/ | adj. | 被罢免的 | Removed from a position of power. | The deposed king lived the rest of his life in exile. 被废黜的国王在流亡中度过了余生。 | 近义词：ousted, dethroned, former | 反义词：reigning, incumbent | adjective
