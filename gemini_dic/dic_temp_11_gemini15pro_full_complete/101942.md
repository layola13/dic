ismaelian:/ˌɪsmɑːˈɪliən/ |adj. n.|伊斯玛仪派的，伊斯玛仪派信徒|Relating to or a follower of Ismailism, a branch of Shia Islam.|例句：He is an Ismaili Muslim. 他是一位伊斯玛仪派穆斯林。|例句：The Ismailian community is known for its philanthropic work. 伊斯玛仪派社群以其慈善工作而闻名。|近义词：Shia Muslim, Nizari|反义词：Sunni Muslim|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology:
From Ismail + -ian. Ismail refers to Ismail ibn Jafar, the seventh Imam, whose followers formed the Ismaili branch of Shia Islam.
