infighters:/ˈɪnˌfaɪtərz/ | n. | 内讧者，内斗者 |  A person who engages in infighting, typically within an organization or political party.  | The party was weakened by infighters who cared more about personal power than the party's success. 该党被那些更关心个人权力而不是党的成功的内讧者削弱了。 | synonyms: dissidents, rivals, factions,  | antonyms: collaborators, allies, teammates | noun

infighter:/ˈɪnˌfaɪtər/ | n. | (拳击)近战拳击手 | A boxer who fights at close range. |  The infighter relentlessly attacked his opponent, landing a series of powerful body blows.  这位近战拳击手不断攻击他的对手，进行了一系列强有力的身体打击。 | synonyms: brawler, slugger | antonyms: out-boxer, counterpuncher | noun
