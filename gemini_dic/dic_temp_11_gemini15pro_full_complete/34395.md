classism:/ˈklæsɪzəm/| n.|阶级歧视，阶级主义|Prejudice against or in favor of people belonging to a particular social class.|例句：Classism is a pervasive issue in many societies. 阶级歧视在许多社会中是一个普遍存在的问题。

例句：She faced classism when she applied for the job because of her background.  她申请这份工作时因为她的背景而面临阶级歧视。|近义词：elitism, snobbery, prejudice, discrimination|反义词：egalitarianism, equality|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From class + -ism.  "class" denoting social group, combined with "-ism" which forms nouns signifying a doctrine, belief, or practice.
