nonefficient:/ˌnɒnɪˈfɪʃənt/
adjective
效率低下的，无效率的
not efficient; wasteful of time, energy, or materials.
The nonefficient use of energy resources is a major concern. 能源资源的低效利用是一个主要问题。
The old heating system was nonefficient and costly.  旧的供暖系统效率低下且成本高昂。
近义词:inefficient, wasteful, unproductive
反义词:efficient, productive, effective
词性:adjective

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "efficient" (from Latin efficiens, present participle of efficere "to work out, accomplish," from ex- "out" + facere "to do, make")
