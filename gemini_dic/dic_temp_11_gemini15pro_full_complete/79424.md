glisten:/ˈɡlɪsən/| vi.| 闪耀，闪光|To shine with a soft reflected light, often due to a wet or smooth surface.  |The dew made the spiderwebs glisten in the morning sun.  晨露使蜘蛛网在早晨的阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：sparkle, glitter, shimmer, gleam|反义词：dull|词性：动词
