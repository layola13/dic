gunrunners:/ˈɡʌnˌrʌnərz/
noun
军火走私犯；非法贩卖武器者
People who smuggle or illegally sell guns.
The gunrunners were caught trying to cross the border. 这些军火走私犯在试图越境时被抓获。
Synonyms: arms traffickers, weapons smugglers
Antonyms: peacekeepers, diplomats
词性: 名词
