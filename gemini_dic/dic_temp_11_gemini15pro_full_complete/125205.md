multicircuits:/ˌmʌltiˈsɜːrkɪts/ | n. | 多电路，复式电路 |  A system or device containing multiple circuits.  It refers to an arrangement where several circuits are integrated or operate together. | The multicircuits in this device allow for complex signal processing.  这个设备中的多电路允许进行复杂的信号处理。| 近义词: multi-circuit system, complex circuitry | 反义词: single circuit, simple circuit |  noun
