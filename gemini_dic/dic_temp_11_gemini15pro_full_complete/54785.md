dispensaries:/dɪˈspɛnsəriːz/
n.
药房，诊疗所
A place where medicine or medical treatment is dispensed.
Many people rely on local dispensaries for their prescriptions. 许多人依靠当地的药房来配药。
Free medical care is available at the dispensary.  在诊疗所可以获得免费医疗。
pharmacy, clinic, health center
