jerrymander: /ˈdʒɛriˌmændər/ (US), /ˈdʒɛriˌmændə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|不公正地划分选区；操纵选区|To divide a voting area into election districts in a way that gives one political party an unfair advantage.|The opposition party accused the government of jerrymandering the electoral districts to maintain its power. 反对党指责政府操纵选区以维持其权力。| The state legislature gerrymandered the districts to favor the incumbent. 州立法机关为了偏袒现任者而重新划分选区。|Synonyms: manipulate, rig, redistrict (unfairly)|Antonyms: reapportion (fairly), redistribute (equitably)|Noun, Verb
