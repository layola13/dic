fifthly:/ˈfɪfθli/
adverb
第五地，第五
In the fifth place; as the fifth point.
Firstly, we need to secure funding. Secondly, we need to hire staff. Thirdly, we need to find a suitable location. Fourthly, we need to develop a marketing strategy. Fifthly, we need to launch the product.
