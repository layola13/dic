outrank:/ˌaʊtˈræŋk/ | v. | 超越，级别高于 | to have a higher rank or position than someone or something |  The new general outranks all the other officers. 新来的将军级别高于所有其他军官。|  She quickly outranked her colleagues. 她很快级别就超过了她的同事们。| 近义词: surpass, exceed, outstrip, top | 反义词:  be outranked by, be subordinate to | 词性: 动词
