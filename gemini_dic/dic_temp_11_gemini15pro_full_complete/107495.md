lancet: /ˈlænsɪt/ (US) /ˈlɑːnsɪt/ (UK)| n.|柳叶刀；手术刀；尖顶窗|A small, pointed, two-edged surgical knife, often with a double edge, used for making incisions.  Also, a pointed, arched window.|The surgeon used a lancet to make the incision.|Synonym: scalpel, knife|Antonym: blunt (instrument)|noun
