outreasoning:/ˌaʊtˈriːzənɪŋ/
|v.|以理服人；胜过…的推理；|To defeat or surpass someone in reasoning or argumentation.|He outreasons his opponents with his calm logic. 他以冷静的逻辑推理战胜了对手。|近义词：outarguing, outsmarting, besting|反义词：losing an argument, being persuaded|词性:动词

词源：out- + reasoning.  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "surpassing" or "exceeding". "Reasoning" refers to the action of thinking about something in a logical, sensible way.
