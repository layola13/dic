overprolific: /ˌōvərprəˈlɪfɪk/ | adj. | 过于多产的，过度繁殖的 | Producing offspring or fruit in excessive abundance.  | The overprolific rabbit population quickly overwhelmed the island's resources. 兔子数量过多，迅速耗尽了岛上的资源。|  The apple tree was overprolific this year, yielding more apples than we could possibly eat. 今年苹果树产量过高，结的苹果比我们能吃的还要多。 | 近义词: fecund, fruitful, productive, abundant | 反义词: barren, infertile, unproductive, scarce | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From *over-* + *prolific*. *Over-* is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." *Prolific* comes from the Latin *proles* (offspring) and *facere* (to make).
