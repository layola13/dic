cognati:/kɒɡˈnɑːti/ /kɑːɡˈneɪti/
n.
同源词
Words that have a common etymological origin.
English "father" and Latin "pater" are cognates.
英语“father”和拉丁语“pater”是同源词。
近义词：related words, derived words
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly unrelated words)
词性: noun
