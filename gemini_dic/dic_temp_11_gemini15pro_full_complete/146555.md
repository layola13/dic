overgoverned:/ˌoʊvərˈɡʌvərnd/
adjective
过度管理的；管制过多的
Ruled or managed with too much control or too many regulations.
The city is overgoverned, with too many bureaucratic procedures hindering progress. 这个城市管理过度，过多的官僚程序阻碍了发展。
The company became overgoverned and inflexible, stifling innovation.  这家公司变得管理过度，缺乏灵活性，扼杀了创新。
Synonyms: overregulated, overmanaged, excessively controlled
Antonyms: undergoverned, unregulated, laissez-faire
词性: adjective
