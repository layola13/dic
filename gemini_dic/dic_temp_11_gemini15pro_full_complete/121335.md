misgendering:/ˌmɪsˈdʒɛndərɪŋ/ | n. |  错误地称呼性别 | The act of referring to someone using a pronoun or term that does not correctly reflect their gender identity. |  He apologized for misgendering his colleague. 他为错误地称呼了他同事的性别而道歉。 | Misgendering can be hurtful and disrespectful. 错误地称呼性别可能具有伤害性和不尊重性。 |  Near Synonyms: mislabeling, misidentifying | Antonyms: respecting pronouns, affirming gender | noun

misgender:/ˌmɪsˈdʒɛndər/ | vt. |  错误地称呼性别 | To refer to someone using a pronoun or term that does not correctly reflect their gender identity. | I accidentally misgendered the customer and felt terrible about it. 我不小心错误地称呼了顾客的性别，对此感到非常糟糕。 |  The actor was misgendered in several media articles.  这位演员在几篇媒体文章中被错误地称呼了性别。 | Near Synonyms: mislabel, misidentify | Antonyms: respect pronouns, affirm gender | verb
