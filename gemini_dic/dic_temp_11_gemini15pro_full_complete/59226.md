dystrophy:/ˈdɪstrəfi/| n.|肌营养不良|A progressive degeneration of a tissue, especially of muscle, characterized by weakness, wasting, and loss of function.  It is often inherited.|Muscular dystrophy is a debilitating disease. 肌营养不良是一种使人衰弱的疾病.|Synonyms: degeneration, atrophy, wasting|Antonyms: growth, development|noun
