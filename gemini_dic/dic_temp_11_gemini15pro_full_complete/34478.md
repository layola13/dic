clayed:/kleɪd/
v. |用黏土处理|To cover or treat with clay.|The potter clayed the wheel before starting to work. 陶工在开始工作前用黏土处理了转轮。|近义词：daubed, smeared, plastered|反义词: cleaned, scraped|动词

clayed:/kleɪd/
adj. |黏土的，涂有黏土的|Covered or treated with clay.|The clayed field held the moisture well.  这片涂有黏土的田地很好地保持了水分。|近义词：earthen, clayey|反义词: clean, bare|形容词
