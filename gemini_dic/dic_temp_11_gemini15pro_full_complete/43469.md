crass:/kræs/
| adj. | 粗鲁的；愚钝的；非常的 | insensitive; stupid; without refinement |  He made a crass remark about her weight. 他对她的体重说了一句粗鲁的话。| insensitive, stupid, vulgar, crude, coarse, uncouth, oafish, boorish | refined, sophisticated, sensitive, subtle, discerning | adjective

crassness:/ˈkræsnɪs/
| n. | 粗鲁；愚钝 | The crassness of his behavior shocked everyone. 他粗鲁的行为震惊了所有人。| insensitivity, stupidity, vulgarity, crudity, coarseness | refinement, sophistication, sensitivity, subtlety | noun


Etymology:
From Latin *crassus* "thick, fat, dense," from Proto-Italic *krassos*, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱr̥h₂-nós* "thick." The semantic shift from "thick" to "insensitive" likely occurred through the notion of being "thick-headed" or "slow-witted."
