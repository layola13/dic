epitomator:/ɪˈpɪtəmeɪtər/
n.
概括者；摘要器；象征者
Someone or something that epitomizes something.  A person or device that creates an epitome or summary.
The website serves as an epitomator of current events, condensing complex news stories into digestible summaries.  This AI program is an excellent epitomator, able to summarize lengthy documents quickly and accurately.
近义词:summarizer, abstracter, encapsulator
反义词:expander, elaborator
词性:名词

epitomize:/ɪˈpɪtəmaɪz/
v.
成为…的缩影；概括；集中体现
To be a perfect example of something; to embody the essential characteristics of something.
Her work epitomizes the struggles of immigrant families. 她的作品集中体现了移民家庭的奋斗。 The new law epitomizes the government's commitment to environmental protection. 这项新法律体现了政府对环境保护的承诺。
近义词:represent, embody, typify, exemplify
反义词:misrepresent, belie
词性:动词

epitome:/ɪˈpɪtəmi/
n.
缩影；摘要；象征；典范
A person or thing that is a perfect example of a particular quality or type. A summary of a written work; an abstract.
He is the epitome of kindness. 他是善良的典范。 This poem is the epitome of Romantic literature. 这首诗是浪漫主义文学的缩影。
近义词:personification, embodiment, quintessence, archetype
反义词:antithesis
词性:名词
