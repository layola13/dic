agreeance: /əˈɡriːəns/
noun
同意，一致
The state of being in agreement; concord or harmony.
We are in agreeance on this matter. 我们在这个问题上意见一致。
synonyms: agreement, concord, harmony, accord, consensus
antonyms: disagreement, discord, disharmony, conflict, dissent
词性: 名词

词源: From agree + -ance.  Ultimately from Latin grātus "pleasing".
