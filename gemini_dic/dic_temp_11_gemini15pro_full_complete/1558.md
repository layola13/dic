achiever:/əˈtʃiːvər/| n. |成功者，获得者|Someone who achieves something, especially something difficult or impressive. |She is a high achiever who always strives for excellence. 她是一位追求卓越的高成就者。

He was an academic achiever throughout his school years. 他在整个学生时代都是一名学业优异者。|近义词：winner, victor, champion, success, high-flyer |反义词:failure, loser, underachiever|词性:noun
