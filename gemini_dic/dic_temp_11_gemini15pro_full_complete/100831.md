introductorily:/ˌɪntrəˈdʌktərɪli/
adverb
介绍性地，引论性地
Serving as an introduction; preliminary.
The topic was introduced introductorily in the first chapter.  这个主题在第一章进行了介绍性的阐述。
The chairman introductorily explained the agenda. 主席介绍性地解释了议程。
Near Synonyms: briefly, preliminarily, initially, shortly
Antonyms: comprehensively, thoroughly, exhaustively
词性: 副词
