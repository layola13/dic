deathful:/ˈdɛθfəl/| adj.|致命的，死亡的|Causing or likely to cause death; deadly.  Marked by numerous deaths.|The Black Death was a deathful plague. 黑死病是一场致命的瘟疫。|近义词：deadly, lethal, fatal, mortal|反义词：life-giving, vital, harmless|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *dethful, equivalent to death +‎ -ful.
