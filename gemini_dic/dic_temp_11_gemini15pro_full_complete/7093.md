anesthetises:/ˌænəsˈθɛtʌɪzɪz/ /ˌænɪsˈθɛtʌɪzɪz/
|v.|麻醉|To administer an anesthetic to (a person or animal), especially so as to induce a loss of consciousness.|The surgeon anesthetised the patient before the operation. 外科医生在手术前给病人麻醉了。|Synonyms: numb, desensitize|Antonyms: stimulate, sensitize|verb

anesthetizes:/ˌænəsˈθɛtʌɪzɪz/ /ˌænɪsˈθɛtʌɪzɪz/
|v.|麻醉|To deprive of feeling or awareness.|The dentist anesthetized my gums before extracting the tooth. 牙医在拔牙之前麻醉了我的牙龈。|Synonyms: numb, desensitize|Antonyms: stimulate, sensitize|verb
