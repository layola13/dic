counterthreat:/ˈkaʊntərˌθret/| n.|反威胁，对抗威胁|A counterthreat is an action or statement made in response to a threat, intended to deter the threatening party. |The government issued a counterthreat in response to the terrorist demands. 政府对恐怖分子的要求发出了反威胁。|Near synonyms: countermeasure, retaliation, deterrent|Near antonyms: concession, appeasement|noun


counterthreat:/ˈkaʊntərˌθret/| adj.|反威胁的，对抗威胁的|Counterthreat measures are designed to neutralize or mitigate potential threats. |The military developed counterthreat capabilities to address emerging cyber warfare tactics.  军方发展了反威胁能力以应对新兴的网络战策略。|Near synonyms: defensive, protective, preemptive|Near antonyms: offensive, aggressive|adjective
