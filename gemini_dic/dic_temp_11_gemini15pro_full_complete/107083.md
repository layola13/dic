lade:/leɪd/ | v. | 装载，装货；（用勺子）盛；大量给予； | to load (a ship, vehicle, etc.) with cargo or passengers; to load something, such as food, with a spoon or ladle; to burden (someone) with something, usually something unwelcome | The workers laded the ship with cargo. 工人们把货物装上船。| synonyms: load, freight, burden, charge | antonyms: unload, discharge | verb

lade:/leɪd/ | n. | 水道，河道 (古语，罕用) | a ditch, trench, or drain, especially one carrying water |  The old lade ran through the fields. 旧的水道穿过田野。| synonyms: channel, ditch, trench | antonyms:  N/A | noun

PHRASE->
lade down with:  使…负担过重  He was laden down with packages. 他被包裹压得喘不过气来。
lade out:  大量给予  She laded out compliments to everyone. 她对每个人都大加赞赏。（这个用法比较古老）
lade up: 装满，装载  They laded up the wagon with hay. 他们把干草装满了货车。
lade with:  装满…  The ship was laden with spices.  这艘船装满了香料。
heavy laden:  负载沉重的  The heavy laden truck crawled up the hill. 负载沉重的卡车缓慢地爬上山坡。
