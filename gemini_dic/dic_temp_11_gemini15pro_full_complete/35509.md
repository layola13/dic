coadministrations: /ˌkoʊədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃənz/ | n. | 共同管理，共同执行，共同给药 | The act of administering two or more drugs or therapies together. |  The coadministration of these two drugs can lead to serious side effects. 这两种药物的共同给药会导致严重的副作用。

The study investigated the coadministration of vitamins and minerals. 这项研究调查了维生素和矿物质的共同服用。 | 近义词：joint administration, concurrent administration | 反义词：separate administration | 词性: 名词


词源: co- (together) + administration (管理，执行)
