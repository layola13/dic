creeler:/ˈkriːlər/ | n. |整经工；整经架；(纺织用)经轴架| A person or machine that prepares yarn for weaving by winding it onto spools or bobbins, or the frame or rack that holds these spools or bobbins. | The creeler expertly loaded the bobbins onto the frame. 整经工熟练地将筒子装到框架上。| The creeler was full of yarn ready for weaving. 整经架上装满了准备织布的纱线。 | synonyms: warper, beamer, bobbin winder | antonyms: unwinder | noun
