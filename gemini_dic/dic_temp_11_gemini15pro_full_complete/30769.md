cesspipe:/ˈsɛspaɪp/
n.
污水管，粪池管道
A pipe that carries sewage.
The cesspipe was blocked, causing unpleasant odors. 污水管堵塞了，散发出难闻的气味。

The old cesspipe needs to be replaced.  旧的污水管需要更换。
近义词：sewer pipe, drainpipe, waste pipe
反义词: None.


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cess" (meaning sewage, from Middle French "cesse") + "pipe".
