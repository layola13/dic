lachrymae:/ˈlækrɪmiː/
n.
眼泪; 悲哀; 哀歌
Tears; sorrow; a mournful piece of music.
Mozart's Requiem in D minor contains the moving "Lacrimosa" movement. 莫扎特的D小调安魂曲包含动人的“落泪”乐章。
近义词:tears, weeping, grief, sorrow
反义词:joy, happiness
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin *lachryma*, "tear".  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dakru-, *draku- "tear". Related to Greek δάκρυ (dákry), "tear".
