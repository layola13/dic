inulin:/ˈɪnjʊlɪn/ /ˈɪnjuːlɪn/
n.
菊粉，菊糖
A type of soluble fiber found in many plants.  It is a polysaccharide composed of fructose units.
Inulin is often added to processed foods to increase fiber content. 菊粉常被添加到加工食品中以增加纤维含量。
Many plants store inulin as a source of energy. 许多植物储存菊粉作为能量来源。

近义词:fructan, oligofructose
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound)
词性:noun
