overbitterness:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪtərnəs/| n.|过度苦涩，极度怨恨|A state of extreme bitterness or resentment.|His overbitterness towards his former employer was palpable. 他对他前雇主的极度怨恨显而易见。|The overbitterness of the coffee made it undrinkable. 咖啡太苦了，没法喝。|近义词: resentment, rancor, acrimony|反义词: forgiveness, acceptance|词性: noun

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "bitterness" (from "bitter" (Old English biter "sharp, biting, cutting; painful, grievous," from Proto-Germanic *bitraz "to bite," from PIE root *bheid- "to split, cleave, bite") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
