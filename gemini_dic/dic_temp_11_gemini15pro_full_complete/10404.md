appertainance:/əˌpɜːrˈteɪnəns/
n.|附属物，附属品；从属关系|Something that belongs or relates to something else; the state of belonging to something. |The garden and all its appertainances were sold together.|近义词：accessory, adjunct, appurtenance|反义词: detachment, separation|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *appertenaunce, from Old French apertenance, from apertenir (“to appertain”), from Latin ad + pertinēre (“to pertain”).
