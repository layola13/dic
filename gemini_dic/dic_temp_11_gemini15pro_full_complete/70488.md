filament:/ˈfɪləmənt/| n.|细丝，灯丝|A slender threadlike object or fiber, especially one found in animal or plant structures or used in certain technologies like light bulbs.|例句：The spider spun a silken filament.  蜘蛛吐出一条丝状的细丝。

例句：The light bulb filament glowed brightly.  灯泡的灯丝发出明亮的光。|近义词：fiber, strand, thread|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

filament:/ˈfɪləmənt/| n.| Filamentous structure|丝状结构|A structure resembling a filament or composed of filaments.|例句：The fungus formed a complex filamentous structure.  真菌形成了一个复杂的丝状结构。

例句：The neuron's axon is a long filamentous structure.  神经元的轴突是一个长的丝状结构。|近义词：fibril, threadlike structure|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
