cudgelling:/ˈkʌdʒəlɪŋ/| n. | 痛打，反复思考 |The act of hitting someone or something repeatedly with a cudgel; the act of thinking hard about something.|He resorted to cudgelling the donkey to make it move. 他用棍棒反复抽打驴子，想让它动起来。|  She spent hours cudgelling her brains, trying to remember where she'd put the keys. 她绞尽脑汁想了好几个小时，试图记起她把钥匙放在哪里了。|近义词：beating, thrashing, pondering, racking (one's brains)|反义词: praising, ignoring|词性:noun

cudgel:/ˈkʌdʒəl/| n. vt. |棍棒，用棍棒打|A short, thick stick used as a weapon; to beat with a cudgel.|The farmer used a cudgel to defend himself against the wolves. 农夫用棍棒保护自己免受狼的袭击。| The protesters were cudgelled by the police. 抗议者被警察用棍棒殴打。|近义词：club, stick, beat, bludgeon|反义词: caress, protect|词性: noun, verb
