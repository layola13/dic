hepatology:/ˌhɛpəˈtɒlədʒi/ /ˌhiːpəˈtɑːlədʒi/| n. |肝脏病学|The study of the liver, gallbladder, biliary tree, and pancreas, and the management of their disorders.|例句：He specialized in hepatology after medical school. 他医学院毕业后专攻肝脏病学。

例句：The hepatology department at the hospital is renowned for its research. 这家医院的肝病科以其研究而闻名。|近义词：liver medicine|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek  ἧπαρ (hêpar, “liver”) + -λογία (-logia, “study of”).
