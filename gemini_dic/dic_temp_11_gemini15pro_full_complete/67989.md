factional:/ˈfækʃənəl/| adj.|派系的，派别斗争的|Characterized by internal conflict and division within a group or organization.  Relating to factions.|The factional fighting within the party threatened to tear it apart.  党内派系斗争有可能使其分裂。|近义词：sectarian, divisive, partisan|反义词：united, cohesive|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *factionalis*, from Latin *factio* ("a making, a doing, a faction, party"), from *factus*, past participle of *facere* ("to do, to make").  The sense of "internal conflict" arose from the actions and divisions created by factions.
