confinedness:/kənˈfaɪndnəs/
n.
限制；封闭；狭窄
The state of being confined or limited.
The confinedness of the small apartment made her feel claustrophobic.  这间小公寓的狭小让她感到幽闭恐惧症。
Her injury resulted in a period of confinedness to her bed. 她的伤势导致她卧床一段时间。
近义词: restriction, constraint, limitation, imprisonment, confinement
反义词: freedom, liberty, openness, spaciousness
词性: 名词

词源: From confine + -ness.  Confine 源于拉丁语 confinis，意为"having a common boundary," from com- "together" + finis "boundary, end."
