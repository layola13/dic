additivity:/ˌædɪˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 可加性，加合性 | The property of a function or process where the result of the combination of multiple inputs is equal to the sum of the results of each input applied individually. | The principle of superposition relies on the additivity of the system's response to multiple stimuli.  叠加原理依赖于系统对多重刺激的响应的可加性。|  Linearity, cumulativity |  Subtractivity, non-linearity | noun
