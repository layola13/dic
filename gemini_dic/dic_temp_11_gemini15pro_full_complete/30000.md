cavort:/kəˈvɔːrt/
verb
欢跃，嬉戏
To leap or dance about in a lively and excited way; to prance.
The children cavorted in the playground. 孩子们在操场上嬉戏。
The lambs cavorted in the spring meadow. 羊羔在春天的草地上欢蹦乱跳。
近义词:frolic, gambol, frisk, prance, romp
反义词:mope, sulk
词性:v.

cavort:/kəˈvɔːrt/
noun
欢跃，嬉戏
An act of leaping or dancing about in a lively and excited way.
The children's cavorts in the park were delightful to watch.  孩子们在公园里欢快的嬉戏玩耍真让人赏心悦目。
The dolphin's cavorts delighted the onlookers.  海豚的嬉戏使旁观者高兴。
近义词:frolic, gambol, frisk, prance, romp
反义词:inactivity, stillness
词性:n.
