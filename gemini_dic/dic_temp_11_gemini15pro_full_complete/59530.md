eat-out:/ˌiːt ˈaʊt/ | n. |外出就餐|The act of eating a meal at a restaurant or other establishment, rather than at home.| We decided to have an eat-out tonight because I didn't feel like cooking.  我们今晚决定出去吃饭，因为我不想做饭。|Synonyms: dining out, eating out|Antonyms: eating in, home-cooked meal|noun

eat-out:/ˌiːt ˈaʊt/ | v. |外出就餐|To eat a meal at a restaurant or other establishment, rather than at home. | We eat out about twice a week. 我们每周大约出去吃两次饭。|Synonyms: dine out, eat at a restaurant|Antonyms: eat in, cook at home|verb
