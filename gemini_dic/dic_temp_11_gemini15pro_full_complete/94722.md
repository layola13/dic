impropriator:/ɪmˈproʊprieɪtər/ | n. | 侵占教会产业者；世俗教士 | A layman who receives the revenues of a benefice.  A secular owner of church property or its income, especially one who had acquired it by impropriation. | The impropriator of the local church owned much of the surrounding land.  当地教会的侵占者拥有周围的大部分土地。 | Synonyms: appropriator, usurper | Antonyms: donor, benefactor | noun
