dynamics:/daɪˈnæmɪks/ | n. |力度变化; 强弱变化 | variations in loudness or intensity | The dynamics of the symphony were breathtaking. 这部交响曲的强弱变化令人叹为观止。| synonyms: volume changes, intensity variations | antonyms: monotony, flatness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dynamicus, from Greek dynamikos "powerful," from dynamis "power," from dynasthai "to be able, to have power."
