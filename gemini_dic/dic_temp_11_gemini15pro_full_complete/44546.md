crotaline:/ˈkrɒtəlaɪn/ /ˈkrɑːtəlaɪn/
adjective
响尾蛇的；蝮蛇的
Relating to or resembling pit vipers, especially rattlesnakes.
Crotaline snakes are venomous. 响尾蛇是有毒的。
The crotaline venom is hemotoxic. 响尾蛇的毒液是血液毒素。
Synonym:pit viper
Antonym:nonvenomous
词性:adjective
