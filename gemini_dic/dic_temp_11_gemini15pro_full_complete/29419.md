catadromous: /kəˈtædrəməs/
adjective
溯河产卵的；降河产卵的
Migrating down rivers to the sea to spawn.
European eels are catadromous, spending their adult lives in fresh water but migrating to the Sargasso Sea to breed. 欧洲鳗鱼是溯河产卵的，它们的成年生活在淡水中度过，但会迁徙到马尾藻海繁殖。
The catadromous fish began its journey downstream to the ocean. 溯河产卵的鱼开始了它向下游到海洋的旅程。
近义词: ocean-spawning
反义词: anadromous (溯河洄游的)
词性: adj.
