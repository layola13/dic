demoralizer: /dɪˈmɔːrəlaɪzər/ | n. | 使人泄气的人或事; 败坏士气的人或事 |  A person or thing that demoralizes; something that reduces morale. | The team's string of losses was a real demoralizer. 球队的连败真是令人沮丧。| 近义词: discouragement, deterrent, depressant | 反义词: encouragement, inspiration, motivator | 词性: 名词

demoralize: /dɪˈmɔːrəlaɪz/ | v. | 使士气低落; 使泄气; 使 demoralized | To deprive of spirit or confidence; discourage. | Constant criticism can demoralize even the most enthusiastic employees.  不断的批评即使是最热情的员工也会感到沮丧。 | 近义词: dishearten, discourage, depress | 反义词: encourage, hearten, inspire | 词性: 动词
