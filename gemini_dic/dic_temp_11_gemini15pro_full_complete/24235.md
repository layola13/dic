bridies:/ˈbraɪdiz/| n. |肉馅饼，肉饼 (尤指苏格兰的一种)|A savory pastry, typically filled with minced meat, onions, and spices, and often baked or fried.  Especially common in Scotland. |例句：The bridies were hot and delicious, straight from the oven. 肉馅饼热腾腾的，刚从烤箱里出来，非常美味。

例句：She bought a couple of bridies for lunch. 她买了几个肉馅饼当午餐。|近义词：pasties, pies, turnovers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "salad" if contrasting a heavy meal with a light one) |词性:noun
