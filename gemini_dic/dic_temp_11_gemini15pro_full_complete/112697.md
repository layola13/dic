lowlinesses:/ˈloʊlinɪsɪz/
noun
卑微，谦逊，低微
The state or quality of being lowly; humility; meekness.
He spoke of the lowlinesses of his birth. 他谈到了他出身的卑微。
近义词: humility, meekness, humbleness, modesty
反义词: arrogance, pride, haughtiness
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *lowlinesse*, from Old English *lāwlīcnes* (“humility”), equivalent to lowly +‎ -ness.
