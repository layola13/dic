homologize:/həˈmɒlədʒaɪz/ /həˈmɑːlədʒaɪz/| vt.|使相同；使同源；认可；批准|To make something similar or consistent; to officially approve something.|The government has homologized the new car safety standards. 政府已经批准了新的汽车安全标准。|The two versions of the software have been homologized. 这两个版本的软件已经统一了。|synonyms: standardize, normalize, ratify, approve|antonyms: differentiate, distinguish, reject, disapprove|verb

homologization:/həˌmɒlədʒaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /həˌmɑːlədʒaɪˈzeɪʃn/| n.|相同化；同源化；认可；批准|The act of making something similar or consistent; the official approval of something.|The homologation of the new car safety standards will save lives. 新汽车安全标准的批准将拯救生命。|The homologation process took several months. 认证过程花了几个月的时间。|synonyms: standardization, normalization, ratification, approval|antonyms: differentiation, distinction, rejection, disapproval|noun
