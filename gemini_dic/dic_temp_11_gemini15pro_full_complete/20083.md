bioscience:/ˈbaɪəʊsaɪəns/ (UK) /ˈbaɪoʊsaɪəns/ (US)| n.|生物科学|The study of living organisms, including their structure, function, development, origin, evolution, and distribution.  It encompasses various disciplines like biology, botany, zoology, and ecology.|例句：Advances in bioscience are leading to new medical treatments. 生物科学的进步正在带来新的医疗方法。|例句：She's pursuing a career in bioscience. 她正在追求生物科学领域的职业。|近义词：life science, biology|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- + science.  "Bio-" comes from the Greek "bios," meaning "life." "Science" comes from the Latin "scientia," meaning "knowledge."
