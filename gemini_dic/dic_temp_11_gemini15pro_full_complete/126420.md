mysterious:/mɪˈstɪəriəs/| adj.|神秘的，难以理解的|Difficult or impossible to understand, explain, or identify.|The old house had a mysterious atmosphere. 这栋老房子有一种神秘的气氛。|近义词：enigmatic, puzzling, strange, obscure, secret|反义词：obvious, clear, transparent, known, familiar|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mysteriosus, from Latin mysterium ("mystery, secret rite"), from Ancient Greek μυστήριον (mustḗrion, “secret rite, mystery”).
