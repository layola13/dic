korrigum:/ˈkɒrɪɡəm/ | n. | 柯瑞甘羚,  红额羚 |A reddish-brown antelope (Damaliscus lunatus korrigum) of western and central Africa, with backward-curving horns.  It is also called topi. | The korrigum grazed peacefully on the savanna.  柯瑞甘羚在热带草原上平静地吃草。|  The korrigum population has declined due to habitat loss. 柯瑞甘羚的数量因栖息地丧失而减少。| 近义词：topi, tiang (related subspecies) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
