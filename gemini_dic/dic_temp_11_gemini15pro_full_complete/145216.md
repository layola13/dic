overbear:/ˌoʊvərˈber/ | v. | 压倒，制服，专横 | To overwhelm or dominate by superior force or authority; to be overbearing. |  The loud music overbore our conversation.  喧闹的音乐盖过了我们的谈话。

The company's CEO overbore any opposition to his plans.  这家公司的首席执行官压制了任何反对他的计划的声音。| 近义词: overpower, subdue, dominate, overwhelm, browbeat | 反义词: submit, yield, acquiesce | 词性: 动词


overbearing: /ˌoʊvərˈberɪŋ/ | adj. | 专横的，傲慢的，盛气凌人的 |  Domineering, arrogant, and bossy. | He had an overbearing personality that made it difficult to work with him. 他专横的个性让人很难和他共事。

She found his overbearing manner offensive. 她觉得他傲慢的态度令人反感。 | 近义词: domineering, arrogant, haughty, imperious, dictatorial | 反义词: humble, modest, meek, submissive | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Middle English *overberen, from Old English oferberan (“to bear over, carry over, cover, protect, oppress”), from ofer (“over”) + beran (“to bear”).
