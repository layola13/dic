dripper:/ˈdrɪpər/| n. |滴漏，滴水器，点滴器|A device that dispenses liquid by dripping it slowly.  A person who drips something.|例句：The coffee dripper made a soothing sound as it brewed. 咖啡滴漏在煮咖啡时发出舒缓的声音。
