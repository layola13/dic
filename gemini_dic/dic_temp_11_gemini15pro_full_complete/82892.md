gulpy:/ˈɡʌlpi/| adj.|大口的；一口气吞下的；紧张的；胆怯的|Characterized by gulping, especially from nervousness or fear.  Tending to swallow large amounts quickly.|She took a gulpy breath before starting her presentation. 她开始演讲前紧张地深吸了一口气。|近义词：nervous, anxious, apprehensive, hesitant|反义词：calm, relaxed, composed|词性:形容词
