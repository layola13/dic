far-fetched:/ˌfɑːrˈfetʃt/ | adj. |牵强的；难以置信的；不切实际的 | improbable; strained; unconvincing |  Example: The plot of the movie was so far-fetched that I couldn't suspend my disbelief.  这部电影的情节太牵强了，我无法相信。

Example: His explanation for being late was rather far-fetched. 他迟到的解释相当牵强。

近义词: improbable, implausible, unlikely, unbelievable, incredible, dubious, questionable, unconvincing, strained, forced, labored, artificial, contrived, flimsy, thin, weak, tenuous

反义词: credible, believable, plausible, likely, probable, realistic, convincing, down-to-earth

词性: adjective
