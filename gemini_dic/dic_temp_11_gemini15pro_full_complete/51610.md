deve:/ˈdeɪv/| v. |(古语或方言) thrive, prosper; ought to; must |To deve is an archaic or dialectal form meaning to thrive or prosper. It can also function as an archaic form of "ought to" or "must".|He deve do better in school. (He must do better in school.) 他必须在学校里做得更好。|The crops did deve in the fertile valley. (The crops thrived in the fertile valley.)  庄稼在肥沃的山谷里茁壮成长。|近义词：thrive, flourish, prosper, ought to, must|反义词:fail, decline, wither, shouldn't, mustn't|词性:verb
