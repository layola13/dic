overshoots:/ˌoʊvərˈʃuːts/ /ˌəʊvəˈʃuːts/| v. n.| 超过，越过，过冲 | The missile overshot its target. 导弹超过了目标。| Synonyms: exceed, surpass, overreach | Antonyms: undershoot, fall short | verb

overshoots:/ˌoʊvərˈʃuːts/ /ˌəʊvəˈʃuːts/| n. | 超过，过冲 (量) | The overshoot in the budget was significant. 预算超支很严重。| Synonyms: excess, surplus | Antonyms: deficit, shortfall | noun

Etymology: From over + shoot.  "Over" implies going beyond a limit, and "shoot" originally meant to move quickly or suddenly.
