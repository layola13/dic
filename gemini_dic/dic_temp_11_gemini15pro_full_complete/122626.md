modif:/ˈmɒdɪf/ | v. | 修改，更改 | To make partial or minor changes to something, typically so as to improve it or to make it less extreme. |  Example: We'll modif the software to make it more user-friendly. 我们将修改软件，使其更加用户友好。 | Example:  The architect modified his design to accommodate the client's wishes. 建筑师修改了他的设计以满足客户的愿望。 | Synonyms: alter, adjust, amend, adapt | Antonyms: maintain, preserve, keep, retain | Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *modifien, from Old French modifier, from Latin modificāre (“to limit, restrain, measure”), from modus (“measure, limit”) + -ficāre (a suffix forming verbs from adjectives), from facere (“to make, do”).
