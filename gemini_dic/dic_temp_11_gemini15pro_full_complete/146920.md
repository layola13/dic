overintellectualized: /ˌoʊvərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlaɪzd/
adjective
过度理智化的；过于学术化的
characterized by and given to excessive intellectual activity
The theory is dense and overintellectualized, making it difficult for the average reader to understand.  这个理论过于深奥和学术化，让普通读者难以理解。

His approach to the problem was overintellectualized and lacked practical application. 他解决问题的方法过于理性化，缺乏实际应用。

Synonym: overanalyzed, overthought, abstract, esoteric
Antonym: simple, straightforward, pragmatic, intuitive
词性: 形容词
