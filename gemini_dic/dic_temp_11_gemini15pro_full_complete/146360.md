overfixing: /ˌoʊvərˈfɪksɪŋ/
verb
过度修复；过度关注
To excessively focus on fixing or solving a problem, often to the detriment of the overall situation or relationship.
例句：He was overfixing the car, replacing parts that didn't need to be replaced. 他过度修理汽车，更换了不需要更换的零件。

例句：By overfixing her child's problems, she inadvertently hindered his ability to develop coping mechanisms.  由于过度关注解决孩子的问题，她无意中阻碍了他发展应对机制的能力。

近义词: overdoing, meddling, interfering
反义词: neglecting, ignoring
词性: verb (动词)


overfixing: /ˌoʊvərˈfɪksɪŋ/
noun
过度修复；过度关注
The act of overfixing something.
例句：Her overfixing of the situation only made things worse. 她对情况的过度关注反而使事情变得更糟。

例句：The therapist warned against overfixing in relationships, emphasizing the importance of allowing individuals to solve their own problems.  治疗师警告不要在人际关系中过度修复，强调允许个人解决自己问题的重要性。


近义词: overdoing, meddling, interference
反义词: neglect, ignorance
词性: noun (名词)
