gossiper:/ˈɡɒsɪpə(r)/ (US) /ˈɡɑːsɪpər/ (UK)| n. |流言蜚语者；爱说闲话的人|A person who habitually spreads rumors or talks about the private matters of others. |She's known as a gossiper, so be careful what you tell her. 她以爱说闲话闻名，所以要注意你告诉她什么。|Synonyms: rumormonger, scandalmonger, busybody|Antonyms: confidant, discreet person|noun

gossipy:/ˈɡɒsɪpi/ (US) /ˈɡɑːsəpi/ (UK)| adj. |爱说闲话的；充满流言蜚语的|Given to or characterized by gossip. |It was a gossipy little town where everyone knew everyone else's business.  这是一个爱说闲话的小镇，每个人都知道别人的事。|Synonyms: chatty, talkative (but with a negative connotation)|Antonyms: discreet, reserved|adjective
