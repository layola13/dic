mulier:/ˈmuːliɛr/ | n. | woman, wife |A Latin word meaning "woman" or "wife."  It is the origin of many words in Romance languages related to women. | The ancient Romans revered the *mulier* as the heart of the family. 古罗马人尊崇妇女为家庭的核心。|  The *mulier* played a vital role in Roman society.  这位妇女在罗马社会中扮演着至关重要的角色。| synonyms: femina, uxor (Latin) woman, wife (English)| antonyms: vir (Latin) man, husband (English)| noun
