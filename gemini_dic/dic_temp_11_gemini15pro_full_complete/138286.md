notary:/ˈnoʊtəri/ (US) /ˈnəʊtəri/ (UK)| n.|公证人；公证员|A public official authorized to witness signatures and certify documents.|Example:  The notary public stamped and signed the document. 公证人在文件上盖章签名。

Example: You'll need to get this affidavit notarized. 你需要对这份宣誓书进行公证。|Synonyms:  notary public,  certified signing agent|Antonyms: N/A|noun

notary:/ˈnoʊtəri/ (US) /ˈnəʊtəri/ (UK)| adj.|公证的|Relating to or involving a notary.|Example:  The notary seal validated the contract. 公证印章使合同生效。

Example:  She provided notary services for the community. 她为社区提供公证服务。|Synonyms: notarized, attested|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
