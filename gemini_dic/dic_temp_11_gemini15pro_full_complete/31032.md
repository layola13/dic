chamat:/ʃɑːˈmɑːt/ | n. | 欺骗，诡计，骗局 | A trick, hoax, or deception. |  The magician's chamat amazed the audience. 魔术师的骗局让观众惊叹不已。| synonyms: trick, hoax, ruse, deception, fraud, artifice, wile, stratagem | antonyms: honesty, truth, sincerity | noun

chamat:/ʃɑːˈmɑːt/ | v. | 欺骗，哄骗 | To trick or deceive someone. | The con man chamated the elderly woman out of her life savings. 骗子骗走了老妇人的毕生积蓄。| synonyms: trick, deceive, dupe, hoodwink, swindle, defraud, cheat | antonyms: be honest, tell the truth | verb
