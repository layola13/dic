circumduction:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈdʌkʃən/ | n. |环转，绕环运动|The circular movement of a limb or eye.  It combines flexion, extension, abduction, and adduction in a sequence.|The pitcher warmed up his shoulder with slow circumductions. 投手用缓慢的环转运动来热身肩膀。|近义词：rotation, gyration |反义词: immobility, fixation |noun
