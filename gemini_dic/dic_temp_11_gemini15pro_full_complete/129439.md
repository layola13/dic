non-abelian:/ˌnɒn əˈbiːliən/ | adj. | 非阿贝尔的 |  Referring to a group, operation, or other mathematical structure where the order in which operations are performed affects the result. In simpler terms, a *b* is not equal to *b* *a*. This contrasts with abelian structures, where the order of operations does not matter. | The rotation group SO(3) is a classic example of a non-abelian group. 三维旋转群 SO(3) 是非阿贝尔群的一个典型例子。

Non-abelian gauge theories play a crucial role in the Standard Model of particle physics. 非阿贝尔规范理论在粒子物理学的标准模型中起着至关重要的作用。 | Near Antonyms: abelian, commutative | Antonyms:  abelian | adjective
