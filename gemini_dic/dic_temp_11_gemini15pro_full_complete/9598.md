antisoporifics:/ˌæntiˌsɒpəˈrɪfɪks/ /ˌæntiˌsɑːpəˈrɪfɪks/
noun
抗睡剂，兴奋剂
Substances that prevent or counteract sleepiness.
The doctor prescribed antisoporifics to help the patient stay awake during the day. 医生给病人开了抗睡剂，以帮助他在白天保持清醒。
synonyms: stimulants, wakefulness-promoting agents
antonyms: soporifics, hypnotics, sedatives
