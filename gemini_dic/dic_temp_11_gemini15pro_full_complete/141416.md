ophite:/ˈoʊfaɪt/
noun
蛇纹石
A rock composed largely of serpentine, often containing chromite or magnetite.
Ophites are commonly found in areas with a history of volcanic activity. 蛇纹石通常在有火山活动历史的地区被发现。
