arachnidium:/ərækˈnɪdiəm/ | n. | 蜘蛛网，蛛丝 | A silken thread or web spun by a spider. |  The spider skillfully constructed its arachnidium to catch unsuspecting prey. 蜘蛛巧妙地构建了它的蛛网来捕捉毫无戒心的猎物。| synonym: cobweb, spiderweb | antonym: N/A | noun

arachnidium:/ərækˈnɪdiəm/ | n. | (蘑菇的)蛛网状菌幕 | A cobweb-like partial veil covering the gills of some immature mushrooms. | The arachnidium on the young mushroom eventually disintegrated as it matured.  年轻蘑菇上的蛛网状菌幕最终随着它的成熟而瓦解。 | synonym: cortina | antonym: N/A | noun
