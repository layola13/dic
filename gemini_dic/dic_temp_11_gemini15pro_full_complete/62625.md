enforcedly:/ɪnˈfɔːrsɪdli/
adv.
强制地，强迫地
By force; compulsorily.
The new regulations were enforcedly implemented despite public opposition.  新的规章制度不顾公众的反对，强制执行。
The curfew was enforcedly imposed on the city after the riots. 宵禁在骚乱后强制实施于该城市。
Synonyms: compulsorily, forcibly, perforce
Antonyms: voluntarily, willingly, optionally
词性: 副词
