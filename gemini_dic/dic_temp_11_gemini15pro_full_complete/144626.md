outwished:/ˌaʊtˈwɪʃt/
verb
胜过…的愿望；比…更渴望
To have wished for something more intensely or for a longer period than someone else.
She outwished him for the promotion, having coveted it for years. 她比他更渴望升职，觊觎这个职位已经好几年了。
近义词: outdesire, surpass in wishing
反义词: be less desirous, be outdone in wishing
词性: verb
