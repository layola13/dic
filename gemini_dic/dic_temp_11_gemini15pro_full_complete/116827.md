maxilla:/mækˈsɪlə/ (US), /mækˈsɪlə/ (UK)| n.|上颌骨; 上颌|The maxilla is the bone that forms the upper jaw in vertebrates. 上颌骨是构成脊椎动物上颌的骨头。|The maxilla helps to shape the face and supports the teeth. 上颌骨有助于塑造脸型并支撑牙齿。|近义词: jawbone|反义词: mandible (下颌骨)|词性:noun

maxillas:/mækˈsɪliː/ (US), /mækˈsɪliː/ (UK)| n.|上颌骨（复数）|The maxillas of birds are adapted for various feeding strategies. 鸟类的上颌骨适应各种摄食策略。|Injuries to the maxillas can be serious and require surgery.  上颌骨受伤可能很严重，需要手术。|近义词: jawbones|反义词: mandibles (下颌骨的复数)|词性:noun
