dulocracy: /djuːˈlɒkrəsi/ /duˈlɑːkrəsi/
n.
暴君统治; 奴隶统治; 残暴统治
A government by slaves or by the mob; oppressive government.
The reign of the brutal king was a true dulocracy, marked by fear and suffering.  这个残暴国王的统治是真正的暴政，充满了恐惧和苦难。
Dulocracy is not a form of government anyone would desire. 没有人会想要暴政这种政府形式。
Synonyms: tyranny, despotism, oppression, dictatorship
Antonyms: democracy, republic, self-government
