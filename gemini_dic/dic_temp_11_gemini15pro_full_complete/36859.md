colophon: /ˈkɒləfən/ (UK), /ˈkɑːləfɑːn/ (US) | n. | 出版商标志；版权页；书籍的末页 | An inscription placed at the end of a book or manuscript, often containing facts about its production. |  The colophon listed the date of printing and the name of the printer. 版权页列出了印刷日期和印刷商的名字。 | synonym: imprint, printer's mark, device | antonym: preface, foreword | noun

colophons: /ˈkɒləfɒnz/ (UK), /ˈkɑːləfɑːnz/ (US) | n. | 出版商标志（复数）；版权页（复数）；书籍的末页（复数）| The plural form of colophon, referring to multiple inscriptions at the end of books or manuscripts. | The colophons in these medieval manuscripts often contain fascinating details about the scribes and illuminators. 这些中世纪手稿中的版权页通常包含有关抄写员和照明师的引人入胜的细节。| synonym: imprints, printer's marks, devices | antonym: prefaces, forewords | noun
