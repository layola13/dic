agminated: /ˈæɡmɪˌneɪtɪd/
adjective
聚集的，密集的
clustered together; densely packed
The agminated glands in the skin secrete sweat. 皮肤中的聚集腺体会分泌汗液。
The flowers were agminated at the tip of the stem. 花朵密集地生长在茎的顶端。
近义词: clustered, aggregated, congregated, concentrated
反义词: scattered, dispersed, diffused

词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *agminatus, past participle of agminare (“to form into a squadron or troop”), from agmen (“a marching column, train, troop, flock, multitude”).
