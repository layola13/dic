drivebolt:/ˈdraɪvˌboʊlt/
noun
驱动螺栓;传动螺栓
A drivebolt is a type of fastener used in construction, typically made of hardened steel and designed to be driven into wood or concrete.
The contractor used drivebolts to secure the frame of the house. 承包商用驱动螺栓固定房屋框架。
Synonyms:  anchor bolt, fastener
Antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a physical object)
