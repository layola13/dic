overcapable:/ˌoʊvərˈkeɪpəbəl/
adj.
过分能干的，能力过剩的
Having more than enough capability; excessively capable.
She felt overcapable for the entry-level position, like her skills were being wasted. 她觉得自己的能力超过了入门级职位，就像她的技能被浪费了一样。
He’s overcapable and often takes on too much responsibility. 他能力过剩，经常承担过多的责任。
近义词: overqualified, overskilled
反义词: incompetent, incapable
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessively) + "capable" (having the ability, fitness, or quality necessary to do or achieve a specified thing).  "Capable" ultimately derives from the Latin "capere" (to take, seize, hold).
