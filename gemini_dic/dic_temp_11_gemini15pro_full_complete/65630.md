evangelistic:/ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 福音的；传福音的；热衷于某事的 | Of or relating to evangelism; marked by ardent or zealous enthusiasm for a cause. |  She has a very evangelistic approach to her vegetarianism. 她对素食主义采取了一种非常热衷的态度。|  Example: His evangelistic fervor inspired many. 他的传教热情鼓舞了很多人。| Synonyms: missionary, zealous, ardent, fervent, passionate | Antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | adjective

evangelistically:/ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 以传福音的方式；热心地 | In an evangelistic manner; with ardent or zealous enthusiasm. | He spoke evangelistically about the benefits of organic farming.  他热情地讲述了有机农业的好处。| Example: She promoted her new business evangelistically. 她热情地推广她的新业务。| Synonyms: zealously, ardently, fervently, passionately | Antonyms: apathetically, indifferently | adverb
