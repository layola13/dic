capucine:/kæpjuːˈsiːn/ |n.|金莲花；旱金莲； капуцин (kaputsin - Russian)|A plant with brightly colored, usually red, orange, or yellow, flowers. It is often grown in gardens.  Also, a brownish color resembling the color of a Capuchin monk's habit.|The capucine flowers cascaded down the side of the pot.|近义词：nasturtium (often used interchangeably),  cress (for some edible varieties)|反义词: N/A|noun


capucine:/kæpjuːˈsiːn/ |adj.|旱金莲色的； капуциновый (kaputsinovyy - Russian) |Of a brownish color resembling the color of a Capuchin monk's habit. |She wore a capucine dress.|近义词：brownish, reddish-brown|反义词: N/A|adjective
