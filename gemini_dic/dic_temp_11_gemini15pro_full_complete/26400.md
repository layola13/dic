byard:/ˈbaɪərd/| n.|畜栏，围场|An enclosed area for keeping livestock, especially cattle.|The farmer kept his cows in the byard. 农民把他的牛关在畜栏里。|近义词：barnyard, corral, enclosure, pen|反义词: open field|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *biȝerd*, from Old English *byrggeard* (“enclosure, courtyard”), equivalent to *burh* (“borough, fortified place”) + *geard* (“yard, enclosure”). Cognate with Dutch *bergplaats* (“storehouse”).
