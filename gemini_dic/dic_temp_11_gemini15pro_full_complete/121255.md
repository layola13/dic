misestimates:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪts/ | v.  n. |错误估计，低估，高估|To misestimate something means to judge it incorrectly or inaccurately.|He misestimated the cost of the project and ran out of money. 他错误估计了项目的成本，结果资金不足。|Synonyms: underestimate, overestimate, miscalculate, misjudge|Antonyms: estimate correctly, judge accurately|verb

misestimate:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmət/ | n. |错误的估计|A misestimate is an inaccurate or incorrect judgment or calculation.|Her misestimate of the distance led them to run out of gas. 她对距离的错误估计导致他们汽油耗尽。|Synonyms: underestimation, overestimation, miscalculation|Antonyms: accurate estimation, correct judgment|noun
