forgers:/ˈfɔːrdʒərz/| n.|伪造者；锻工|A person who makes or sells forged documents, signatures, or other items.|The forgers were caught trying to pass off counterfeit money. 伪造者试图使用假币时被抓获。|近义词：counterfeiters, fakers, imitators|反义词:authenticators, verifiers|noun

forgers:/ˈfɔːrdʒərz/| n.|锻工|A person who shapes metal by heating it in a forge and hammering it.|The blacksmith, a skilled forger, crafted a beautiful gate.  这位铁匠是一位技艺精湛的锻工，打造了一扇漂亮的门。|近义词：blacksmiths, metalsmiths|反义词: N/A|noun
