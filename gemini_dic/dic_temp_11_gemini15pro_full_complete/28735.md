carnalmindedness:/ˈkɑːrnlˈmaɪndɪdnɪs/| n. | 肉体思想; 世俗思想; 肉欲; |  A preoccupation with the physical and sensual desires, often to the exclusion of spiritual or intellectual matters.  It signifies a mindset focused on gratifying base instincts and appetites. |  His carnalmindedness led him down a path of self-destruction. 他的肉欲导致他走向自我毁灭的道路。|  sensuality, lasciviousness, lustfulness, worldliness | spirituality, piety, asceticism | noun
