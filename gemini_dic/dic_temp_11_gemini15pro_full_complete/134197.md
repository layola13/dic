nonincreasing:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkriːsɪŋ/| adj.|非增的，不递增的|Not increasing; remaining constant or decreasing.  A nonincreasing sequence of numbers is one where each subsequent number is less than or equal to the previous number.|The company's profits have been nonincreasing for the past three quarters.|近义词:non-increasing, decreasing, monotonic decreasing|反义词:increasing, rising|词性:adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non*, meaning "not"
increasing (present participle of increase): from Late Middle English encresen, from Old French encreistre, from Latin increscere "to grow in, increase," from in- "in" + crescere "to grow"
