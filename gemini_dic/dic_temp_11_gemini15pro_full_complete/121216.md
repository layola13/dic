misemphasize:/ˌmɪsˈɛmfəˌsaɪz/
|v.|错误地强调；轻描淡写；不够重视|To emphasize wrongly or insufficiently; to understate; to downplay.|He misemphasized the importance of regular exercise in maintaining good health. 他低估了经常锻炼对保持健康的重要性。|The report misemphasizes the negative impacts of the proposed policy. 该报告淡化了拟议政策的负面影响。|近义词: downplay, understate, de-emphasize|反义词: emphasize, overemphasize, exaggerate|词性:动词
词源:  "mis-" (wrongly) + "emphasize"
