non-conscious:/nɒnˈkɒnʃəs/ (UK) /nɑːnˈkɑːnʃəs/ (US)| adj.|无意识的，非意识的|Not consciously aware of something, but still influenced by it; relating to mental processes that are not accessible to consciousness.|His non-conscious biases affected his decision-making. 他无意识的偏见影响了他的决策。|Synonyms: unconscious, subconscious, involuntary|Antonyms: conscious, aware, deliberate|Adjective


non-conscious:/nɒnˈkɒnʃəs/ (UK) /nɑːnˈkɑːnʃəs/ (US)| n.|无意识|The part of the mind that is not accessible to conscious awareness.|Researchers are exploring the impact of the non-conscious on behavior. 研究人员正在探索无意识对行为的影响。|Synonyms: subconscious, unconscious mind|Antonyms: consciousness, conscious mind|Noun
