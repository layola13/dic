drawbridges:/ˈdrɔːbrɪdʒɪz/
n.
吊桥
A bridge that can be raised and lowered to allow ships to pass underneath or to prevent access.
The drawbridges were raised to prevent the enemy from crossing the moat. 吊桥升了起来，阻止敌人越过护城河。

近义词:bascule bridge, swing bridge
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "fixed bridge")
词性:noun (plural)
