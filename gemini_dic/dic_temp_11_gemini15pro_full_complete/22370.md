booby-trap: /ˈbuːbi træp/ | n. | 陷阱，诱杀装置 | A device or setup designed to harm or surprise someone unexpectedly, often disguised as something harmless. | The soldiers were warned about booby traps hidden throughout the abandoned village. 士兵们被警告废弃村庄里到处都藏有诱杀装置。

The tomb raider narrowly avoided a booby trap triggered by a tripwire.  盗墓者勉强躲过了一个由绊索触发的陷阱。 | 近义词: trap, snare, pitfall | 反义词: safeguard, protection | 词性: noun
