labiodental:/ˌleɪbiəˈdɛnt(ə)l/
adjective
唇齿音的
Relating to the lips and teeth; formed by the lips and teeth.
The sounds /f/ and /v/ are labiodental. /f/和/v/是唇齿音。
近义词:dentilabial
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider specifying a different place of articulation like velar or glottal)
词性:adjective
