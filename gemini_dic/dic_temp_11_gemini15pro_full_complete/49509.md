demagogueries: /ˌdɛməˈɡɒɡəriːz/ /ˌdɛməˈɡɑːɡəriːz/
| n. |煽动性言论，蛊惑人心的言辞 |  inflammatory rhetoric or appeals designed to manipulate the emotions of an audience, often by exploiting prejudices or fears |  His campaign was filled with demagogueries about immigrants stealing jobs. 他的竞选活动充斥着关于移民抢走工作的煽动性言论。| synonyms: rabble-rousing, incendiary rhetoric, appeals to prejudice | antonyms: reasoned discourse, logical argument, factual presentation | noun (plural)
