outmeasure:/ˌaʊtˈmeʒər/
verb
超过，胜过
to surpass in measurement; to exceed in size, extent, or degree.
Our team outmeasured theirs by a significant margin.  我们的团队在很大程度上超过了他们的团队。
The new stadium outmeasures the old one in capacity. 新体育场的容量超过了旧体育场。
surpass, exceed, outstrip, outperform
fall short of, be inferior to
v.
