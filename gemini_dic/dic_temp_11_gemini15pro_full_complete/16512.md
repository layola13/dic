barite:/ˈbɛəˌraɪt/ | n. |重晶石|A dense, white, or colorless mineral, barium sulfate (BaSO4), used as a source of barium and in paints, X-ray diagnostics, and glassmaking. | Barite is commonly used as a weighting agent in drilling fluids. 重晶石通常用作钻井液中的加重剂。

Barite deposits are often found in sedimentary rocks. 重晶石矿床经常在沉积岩中被发现。|近义词:barytes|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Baryt*, from Ancient Greek βαρύς (barús, “heavy”) + -ite.
