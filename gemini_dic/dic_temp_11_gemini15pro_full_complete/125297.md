multifunctionality:/ˌmʌltiˌfʌŋkʃəˈnæləti/ | n. | 多功能性；多功能状态 | The multifunctionality of this device makes it a great value.  这个设备的多功能性使其物超所值。 |例句：This new smartphone boasts impressive multifunctionality, serving as a phone, camera, GPS, and much more. 这款新的智能手机拥有令人印象深刻的多功能性，可以用作电话、相机、GPS等等。

例句：The multifunctionality of the software allows it to be used in various departments across the company. 该软件的多功能性使其可以在公司各个部门使用。| 近义词：versatility, adaptability, flexibility | 反义词：specialization, limited function, single-purpose | 词性: noun
