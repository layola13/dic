allowables:/əˈlaʊəbəlz/
| n. |允许额；容许量；可扣除的费用|Amounts that are permitted, especially in accounting or regulations.  Things that are considered acceptable or permissible.|The company's allowables for travel expenses are clearly outlined in the employee handbook. 该公司在员工手册中明确列出了差旅费的允许额。|Synonyms:  permitted expenses, deductible expenses, allowances|Antonyms: disallowables, disallowed expenses|noun

allowable:/əˈlaʊəbəl/
| adj. |可允许的；容许的；正当的|That which is permissible or acceptable.|Wearing jeans is allowable on Fridays. 周五可以穿牛仔裤。|Synonyms: permissible, acceptable, legitimate, admissible|Antonyms: unacceptable, impermissible, prohibited, forbidden|adjective
