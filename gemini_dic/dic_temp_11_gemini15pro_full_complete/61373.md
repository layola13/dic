embittering:/ɪmˈbɪtərɪŋ/
adjective
使痛苦的，使怨恨的
Causing someone to feel bitter or resentful.
The constant criticism had an embittering effect on him.
不断的批评使他感到痛苦。
Her betrayal left him with an embittering sense of resentment.
她的背叛让他充满了怨恨。
近义词:resentful, acrimonious, bitter
反义词:sweetening, conciliatory, soothing
词性:adjective

embitter:/ɪmˈbɪtər/
verb
使痛苦，使怨恨
To cause someone to feel bitter or resentful.
The loss of his job embittered him.
失去工作使他很痛苦。
Years of hardship had embittered her.
多年的苦难使她变得怨恨。
近义词:sour, poison, anger
反义词:sweeten, please, gratify
词性:verb
