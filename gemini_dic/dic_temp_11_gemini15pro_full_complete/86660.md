hellenics:/hɛˈlɛnɪks/
noun
希腊文化；希腊研究；希腊精神
The study of ancient Greek culture, language, and literature.  (对古希腊文化、语言和文学的研究。)
He's fascinated by Hellenics and spends hours reading about Greek mythology. 他对希腊文化着迷，花费数小时阅读希腊神话。
近义词:Greek studies,  Hellenism
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-Hellenic" or referring to a specific other culture like "Roman")
词性:n.


hellenic:/hɛˈlɛnɪk/
adjective
希腊的；希腊文化的
Relating to ancient Greece or its language or culture. (与古希腊或其语言或文化相关的。)
The museum has a remarkable collection of Hellenic art.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的希腊艺术品。
近义词:Greek
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "non-Hellenic" or referring to a specific other culture like "Roman")
词性:adj.
