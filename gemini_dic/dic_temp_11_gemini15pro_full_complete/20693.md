bladefish:/ˈbleɪdfɪʃ/| n. |刀鱼，剃刀鱼|A fish with a compressed, knife-like body shape.  These fish are often found in tropical or subtropical waters and are known for their agility and sharp appearance.|  The bladefish darted through the coral reef. 刀鱼飞快地穿过珊瑚礁。| synonyms: razorfish, knifefish | antonyms: N/A | noun
