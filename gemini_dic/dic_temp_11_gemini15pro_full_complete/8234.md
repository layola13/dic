anti-idealism:/ˌænti aɪˈdiːəlɪzəm/ | n. |反理想主义| A doctrine or philosophical position opposed to idealism. It rejects the belief that reality is fundamentally mental or spiritual in nature and instead emphasizes the importance of material things or objective reality.| His anti-idealism led him to believe that pursuing utopian visions was futile. 他的反理想主义让他相信追求乌托邦式的愿景是徒劳的。| The architect's anti-idealism was reflected in his preference for functional designs over aesthetically pleasing ones.  这位建筑师的反理想主义体现在他偏爱功能性设计而非美观的设计。|近义词: materialism, realism, pragmatism |反义词: idealism |词性: noun
