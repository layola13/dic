monoptote:/ˈmɒnəptəʊt/ | n. |单数同形词，单数不变格词|A noun that has only one case form.  It does not change its form in different grammatical contexts. |例句：The word "sheep" is a monoptote; it is both singular and plural.  单词 "sheep" 是一个单数同形词；它的单复数形式相同。

例句：The Latin word "fas" (divine law) is a monoptote. 拉丁语单词 "fas"（神法）是一个单数同形词。|近义词：indeclinable noun|反义词：declined noun|词性: noun
