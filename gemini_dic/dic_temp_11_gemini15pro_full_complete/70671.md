finagle:/fɪˈneɪɡl/| v.|欺骗；哄骗；（用计谋）获得；|To obtain something by devious or dishonest means.  To achieve something by trickery or manipulation. |She finagled a free upgrade to first class. 她骗取了免费升舱到头等舱的机会。|He finagled his way into the exclusive club. 他耍手段进入了那个高级俱乐部。|synonyms: wangle, manipulate, scheme, contrive, swindle|antonyms: earn, deserve, be honest|verb
