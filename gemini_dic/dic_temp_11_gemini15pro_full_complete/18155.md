being-as:/ˈbiːɪŋ æz/ | conjunction | 既然；因为 | Used as a conjunction meaning "since" or "because".  It is considered informal or dialectal in Standard English. | Being as it's raining, we'll stay inside. 既然下雨，我们就待在室内。 |  Since, because, seeing that |  Although, though | conjunction

seeing that:/ˈsiːɪŋ ðæt/ | conjunction | 既然；因为 | Used to introduce a reason or cause, similar to "because" or "since".  | Seeing that it's late, we should go home. 既然已经晚了，我们应该回家了。 | Since, because, as, being that | Although, though | conjunction
