disburse:/dɪsˈbɜːs/ | v. |支付，支出，分配| to pay out (money), especially from a fund; to distribute | The government disbursed funds for the disaster relief effort. 政府拨款用于救灾工作。| synonyms: pay out, distribute, expend, allocate | antonyms: collect, receive, gather | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desborser, from des- (apart, away) + bourse (purse). Ultimately from Late Latin bursa (leather pouch).
