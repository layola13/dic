leeching:/ˈliːtʃɪŋ/
n.|水蛭疗法；榨取；吸血|The practice of applying leeches to the skin.  The act of exploiting or draining resources or advantage from someone or something.|Doctors used leeching to treat a variety of ailments. 医生用水蛭疗法治疗各种疾病。|
synonyms: exploiting, draining, bleeding, parasitizing|antonyms: giving, contributing, supporting|noun

leeching:/ˈliːtʃɪŋ/
v.|用水蛭治疗；依附并榨取|Applying leeches medicinally.  Exploiting or draining resources or advantage from someone or something.|They leeched the toxins from his blood. 他们用水蛭吸出了他血液中的毒素。|
synonyms: exploiting, draining, bleeding, parasitizing|antonyms: giving, contributing, supporting|verb
