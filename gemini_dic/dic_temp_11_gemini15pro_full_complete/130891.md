nonarrogances: /ˌnɒnˈærəɡənsɪz/
noun
非傲慢；谦逊
Lack of arrogance; humility.  The state of not being arrogant.
His nonarrogance and willingness to learn impressed everyone. 他的谦逊和求知欲给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
Synonyms: humility, modesty, humbleness
Antonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, pride, conceit
