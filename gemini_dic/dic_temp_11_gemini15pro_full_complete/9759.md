antoeci:/ænˈtiːsi/ | n. | 对趾人；相对而居的人 | People who live at the same latitude but on opposite meridians, so that their noon and midnight occur at the same time but their days begin twelve hours apart. |  Antoeci experience similar seasons but opposite times of day. 对趾人经历相似的季节，但时间相反。|  The antoeci of a location experience the same length of day. 一个地方的对趾人经历相同的白昼长度。| synonym: antipodes (loosely), counter-dwellers | antonym: perioeci | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἀντίοικοι (antioikoi), from ἀντί (anti, "opposite") + οἶκος (oikos, "house").
