depressions:/dɪˈprɛʃənz/ | n. | 沮丧，抑郁，萧条 |  A state of feeling very unhappy and without hope; a period of reduced economic activity. |  She suffered from periods of deep depression. 她患有严重的抑郁症。 The country is in a deep economic depression.  这个国家正处于严重的经济萧条之中。 | synonyms: sadness, gloom, melancholy, slump, recession | antonyms: happiness, joy, elation, boom, prosperity | noun

depression:/dɪˈprɛʃən/ | n. | 凹陷，洼地 | A hollow or indentation in a surface. | The meteorite left a large depression in the ground. 陨石在地面上留下了一个大坑。  There was a noticeable depression in the sand where the child had been playing. 孩子玩耍的地方，沙地上有一个明显的凹陷。 | synonyms: hollow, dip, pit, cavity | antonyms:  bulge, protuberance, elevation | noun
