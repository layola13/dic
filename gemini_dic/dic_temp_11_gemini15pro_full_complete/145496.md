overclog:/ˌoʊvərˈklɒɡ/ /ˌoʊvərˈklɑːɡ/ | v. | 堵塞；阻塞；超载 | To overload or congest something, causing it to function poorly or inefficiently. | The pipes were overclogged with hair and debris. 水管被头发和杂物堵塞了。

The server was overclogged with traffic and crashed. 服务器因流量过载而崩溃了。| 近义词：clog, block, obstruct, congest | 反义词: clear, unclog | 词性:动词

overclogs:/ˌoʊvərˈklɒɡz/ /ˌoʊvərˈklɑːɡz/ | n. | 阻塞物；堵塞 | Something that overloads or congests, causing a blockage or inefficiency.  (This noun form is less common than the verb.) | The overclogs in the drain caused the sink to overflow.  排水沟里的阻塞物导致水槽溢出。 | 近义词：blockages, obstructions | 反义词:  clearances | 词性:名词
