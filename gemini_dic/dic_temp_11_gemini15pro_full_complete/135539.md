Relating to a system or society where only one ideology or perspective is dominant or tolerated. | The regime maintained a nonpluralistic approach to governance, suppressing all dissenting voices. 这个政权维持着非多元化的治理方式，压制所有异议。 | The community's nonpluralistic mindset hindered any meaningful cultural exchange.  这个社群非多元化的思维方式阻碍了任何有意义的文化交流。 | 近义词: monolithic, uniform, homogeneous, totalitarian | 反义词: pluralistic, diverse, multicultural, heterogeneous | 词性: 形容词
