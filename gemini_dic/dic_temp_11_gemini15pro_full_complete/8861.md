antifeminisms:/ˈæntiˈfɛmɪnɪzəmz/
n.
反女权主义
Opposition to feminism; ideologies and movements that oppose the advancement of women's rights and equality.
The rise of antifeminisms in online spaces is a concerning trend.  反女权主义在网络空间的兴起是一个令人担忧的趋势。
The book analyzed various strands of antifeminisms throughout history. 这本书分析了历史上各种反女权主义的脉络。
近义词:misogyny, sexism
反义词:feminism, pro-feminism
词性:noun
