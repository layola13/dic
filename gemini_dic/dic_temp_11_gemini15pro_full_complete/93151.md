igniferous:/ɪɡˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
产火的；能点火的
producing or capable of producing fire.
例句：The igniferous rocks spewed from the volcano. 火山喷出了燃烧的岩石。
例句：The campers carefully built an igniferous pile of wood. 露营者小心翼翼地堆起一堆易燃的木柴。
近义词：flammable, incendiary, combustible
反义词：nonflammable, fire-resistant
词性：adjective
