gratified:/ˈɡrætɪfaɪd/| adj.|满足的，高兴的|Pleased and satisfied.|例句：I was gratified to hear of your success. 听到你的成功我很高兴。|例句：She was gratified by the positive feedback she received.  她对收到的积极反馈感到满意。|近义词：pleased, satisfied, content, fulfilled|反义词：disappointed, dissatisfied, disgruntled|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin gratificari "to oblige, to do a favor," from gratus "pleasing, agreeable, thankful" + -ficari (verbal suffix).
