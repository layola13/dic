enleven:/ɪnˈlɪvən/ |v. |使充满活力，使生动；使快活|To make something more lively, interesting, or enjoyable.|The performance was enlivened by the comedian's jokes.  演出因喜剧演员的笑话而更加生动。|
近义词:animate, invigorate, stimulate, energize|反义词:bore, dull, tire|词性:动词

词源:
From en- + life + -en.  "En-" acts as a prefix meaning "to put into or into the condition of," while "-en" is a suffix that forms verbs meaning "to make or become."  Therefore, "enliven" literally means "to put into life."
