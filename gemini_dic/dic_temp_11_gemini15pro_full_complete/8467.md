antiarthritis:/ˌæntiɑːrˈθraɪtɪs/
adjective
抗关节炎的
Used to describe drugs or treatments that relieve or prevent arthritis.
Example: She takes antiarthritis medication daily. 她每天服用抗关节炎药物。
Example:  He's looking for an antiarthritis diet. 他正在寻找一种抗关节炎的饮食。
Synonym:  antiarthritic
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider) arthritis-causing, arthritis-promoting
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposing (from Greek "anti-")
arthritis (noun): inflammation of the joints (from Greek "arthron" meaning joint + "-itis" meaning inflammation)
