bitternut:/ˈbɪtərˌnʌt/ | n. |苦山核桃；苦山核桃树|A type of hickory tree (Carya cordiformis) known for its bitter nuts and bright yellow fall foliage. Also refers to the nut itself.  |  The squirrels avoided the bitternuts, preferring the sweeter acorns. 松鼠不吃苦山核桃，更喜欢甜橡子。| synonym:  swamp hickory | antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific tree species, but perhaps) sweet nut tree | noun
bitternut:/ˈbɪtərˌnʌt/ | adj. |苦味的；苦涩的|Having a bitter taste, like that of a butternut.| The bitternut flavor of the pie was unexpected and unpleasant.  这馅饼的苦山核桃味出乎意料，而且令人不快。| synonym: acrid, bitter | antonym: sweet | adjective
