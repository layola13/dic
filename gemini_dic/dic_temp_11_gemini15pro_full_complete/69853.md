ferrocalcite:/ˌfɛrəˈkælsaɪt/ | n. | 铁方解石 | A mineral consisting of a mixed crystal of calcite and siderite (iron carbonate). It is a variety of calcite containing iron. | The cave formations were composed primarily of ferrocalcite, giving them a yellowish tint.  这些洞穴构造主要由铁方解石组成，呈现出淡黄色。| Near synonyms:  calcite, siderite, ankerite | Antonym: None (Specific chemical compositions don't typically have antonyms) | noun
