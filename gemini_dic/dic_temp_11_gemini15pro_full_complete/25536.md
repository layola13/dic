bullshitting: /ˈbʊlʃɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 胡说八道，瞎扯，忽悠 | Talking nonsense, often to deceive or impress; engaging in empty or pretentious talk. | He was bullshitting about his accomplishments. 他在吹嘘他的成就。|  He's just bullshitting you, don't believe him. 他只是在忽悠你，别相信他。 | 近义词: lying, exaggerating, fabricating, bluffing | 反义词: being truthful, being honest | 词性: 动词 (present participle of "bullshit")

bullshit: /ˈbʊlʃɪt/ | n. | 废话，胡说，胡扯 |  Nonsense, lies, or empty talk. | That's a load of bullshit!  那是一派胡言！| Don't give me that bullshit.  别跟我胡扯。| 近义词: nonsense, rubbish, lies, claptrap | 反义词: truth, facts | 词性: 名词
