enslavers:/ɪnˈsleɪvərz/
n.|奴役者，使人沦为奴隶的人|People who enslave others, forcing them into servitude.|The enslavers profited greatly from the forced labor of those they held captive. 奴隶主从他们囚禁的人的强迫劳动中获利颇丰。|The enslavers treated their captives with unspeakable cruelty. 奴隶主以难以形容的残忍对待他们的俘虏。|synonyms: slaveholders, slave masters, oppressors|antonyms: liberators, emancipators
