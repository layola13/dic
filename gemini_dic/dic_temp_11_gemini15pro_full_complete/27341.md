cambering:/ˈkæmbərɪŋ/
verb
拱起，弯曲，成弧形
giving something a slightly curved or arched shape
The bridge is cambering slightly under the weight of the traffic.  桥在交通的重压下略微拱起。
近义词:arching, curving, bending
反义词:straightening, flattening
词性:v.


cambered:/ˈkæmbərd/
adjective
拱形的，弯曲的
slightly curved or arched
The road is cambered to allow rainwater to drain off. 道路是拱形的，以便雨水流走。
近义词:arched, curved, bent
反义词:straight, flat
词性:adj.
