misbrew:/ˌmɪsˈbruː/
verb
酿坏，调制失败，搞砸
to brew (beer, etc.) badly; to make a bad mixture of something.
The witch misbrewed the potion and turned the prince into a frog. 女巫把药水酿坏了，把王子变成了青蛙。
The bartender misbrewed the cocktail and it tasted awful. 调酒师把鸡尾酒调坏了，味道很糟糕。
mismanage, bungle, spoil, ruin
brew correctly, prepare well
