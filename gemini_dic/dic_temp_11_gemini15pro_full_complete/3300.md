afflicter:/əˈflɪktər/| n.|折磨者，使痛苦的人|Someone or something that causes pain or suffering.|The drought was a cruel afflicter of the land. 干旱是这片土地残酷的折磨者。|近义词：tormentor, scourge, bane|反义词:comforter, reliever, savior|词性:noun
Etymology: From the verb "afflict," which comes from the Latin "affligere," meaning "to strike down, dash to the ground, weaken, damage, humble, distress." The suffix "-er" denotes the agent or doer of the action.
