misassignments:/ˌmɪs əˈsaɪnmənts/ | n. | 错误分配；分配错误 | The report highlighted several misassignments of funds. 报告强调了资金的几处分配错误。|  Incorrect assignments, wrong allocations | The misassignments of tasks led to project delays.  任务分配错误导致项目延误。| 近义词: misallocations, errors, blunders | 反义词: correct assignments, proper allocations | 词性: 名词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly") + "assignment" (from assign, from Old French assigner, from Latin assignare "to mark out, allot")
