hematuria:/ˌhɪːməˈtʊəriə/ /ˌheməˈtʊriə/| n. |血尿|The presence of blood in the urine.|例句：The doctor suspected a kidney stone after the patient reported hematuria. 医生在病人报告血尿后怀疑是肾结石。

例句：Microscopic hematuria can be a sign of various underlying conditions.  镜下血尿可能是各种潜在疾病的征兆。|近义词：blood in urine|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider the absence of a condition: anuria -  无尿)|词性:noun
