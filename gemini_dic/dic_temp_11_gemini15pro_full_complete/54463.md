disintegrating:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
瓦解，解体，崩溃
breaking up into small parts or pieces; ceasing to exist as an integrated whole.
The old book was disintegrating in my hands. 这本旧书在我手里都快散架了。
Near synonyms: crumbling, decaying, falling apart
Antonyms: integrating, uniting, solidifying
verb
