exhausted:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪd/ /ɪɡˈzɑːstɪd/| adj.|筋疲力尽的，疲惫不堪的|Drained of all energy; extremely tired.|例句：After the marathon, I was completely exhausted. 马拉松结束后，我筋疲力尽。|近义词：tired, weary, fatigued, drained, spent|反义词:energetic, refreshed, invigorated|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exhaustus*, past participle of *exhaurire* "to drain out, draw out completely," from *ex-* "out" + *haurire* "to draw, drain."
