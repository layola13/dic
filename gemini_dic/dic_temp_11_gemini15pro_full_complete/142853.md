out-greening:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnɪŋ/
verb
胜过环保;在环保方面做得更好
surpassing someone in environmental consciousness or practices; becoming more environmentally friendly than others
Tesla is out-greening its competitors with its focus on sustainable energy. 特斯拉专注于可持续能源，在环保方面胜过其竞争对手。
The city is out-greening its neighbours by implementing a comprehensive recycling program. 该市通过实施全面的回收计划，在环保方面胜过其邻近城市。
synonyms: outperforming environmentally, exceeding in green practices
antonyms: lagging behind environmentally, less environmentally conscious
词性: 动词
