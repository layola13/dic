amentaceous:/ˌæmənˈteɪʃəs/
adjective
柳絮状的；有柔荑花序的
Bearing catkins; resembling a catkin.
The amentaceous flowers of the willow tree are a sign of spring. 柳树的柔荑花序状的花朵是春天的标志。
The hazel tree has long, amentaceous flowers. 榛树有着长长的柔荑花序状的花朵。
Synonyms: catkin-bearing, catkinate
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but one could consider: flowerless, acatkinate - though these aren't perfect opposites)
词性: adjective
