microsthenic: /ˌmaɪkrəsˈθɛnɪk/
adjective
体型中等强壮的；体格适中的
Having a body build of average muscular development and strength.
Example:  Microsthenic individuals are generally considered to have a balanced physique. 体型中等强壮的人通常被认为拥有均衡的体格。

Example:  He had a microsthenic build, neither overly muscular nor thin. 他的体格适中，既不太肌肉发达也不瘦弱。

近义词: mesomorphic, average build
反义词: asthenic, hypersthenic
词性: adjective
