finicking:/ˈfɪnɪkɪŋ/| adj.|挑剔的，吹毛求疵的|Excessively meticulous or fastidious about details; fussy.|例句：He was finicking about the placement of the silverware. 他对银器的摆放很挑剔。|近义词：fussy, fastidious, picky, particular, meticulous, choosy, exacting|反义词：easygoing, undemanding, tolerant|词性:形容词
finicking:/ˈfɪnɪkɪŋ/| n.|吹毛求疵，过于讲究细节|The act of being finicky.|例句：All that finicking over the flower arrangements was unnecessary. 所有那些对插花的吹毛求疵都是不必要的。|近义词：fussiness, fastidiousness, pickiness|反义词：tolerance, acceptance|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From earlier *finick, perhaps related to Low German fniken “to fidget, trifle,” or perhaps a variant of *fenek, from fen “marsh” (describing unsteady, shifting ground).  The original meaning was "to fidget, trifle."  The sense of "to be overly fastidious" developed later.
