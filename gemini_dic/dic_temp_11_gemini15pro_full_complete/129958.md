non-mediterraneans:/ˌnɒnˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniənz/
noun
非地中海地区的人；非地中海民族
People or ethnic groups who are not from or native to the Mediterranean region.
Many non-mediterraneans find it difficult to adjust to the siesta culture. 许多非地中海地区的人发现很难适应午睡文化。
Non-mediterraneans often have a lower tolerance for extremely hot weather.  非地中海地区的人通常对酷热的天气耐受性较低。
Synonyms: non-Mediterranean people, outsiders
Antonyms: Mediterraneans, Mediterranean people
n.
