overdomesticates:/ˌoʊvərdoʊˈmɛstɪkeɪts/ | v. | 过度驯化 | To domesticate (an animal or plant) to an excessive degree, often resulting in a loss of wild characteristics and dependence on humans.  |  Modern farming practices have overdomesticated many breeds of chicken, making them less resilient to disease. 现代农业实践过度驯化了许多品种的鸡，使它们对疾病的抵抗力降低。 | over-domesticate |  under-domesticate | verb


overdomesticated:/ˌoʊvərdoʊˈmɛstɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度驯化的 |  Domesticated to an excessive degree. | The overdomesticated dog breeds often suffer from health problems.  过度驯化的犬种经常遭受健康问题。| overbred, over-refined | wild, feral | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + domesticate.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Domesticate" comes from the Latin word "domesticus," meaning "belonging to the house."
