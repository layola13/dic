gloominess:/ˈɡluːminəs/| n. |忧郁；阴暗；沮丧|A state of darkness or sadness, often referring to a place or a person's mood. |例句：The gloominess of the weather matched her mood. 天气的阴沉和她忧郁的心情相符。

例句：The gloominess of the forest made him uneasy. 森林的阴暗让他感到不安。|近义词：darkness, sadness, depression, melancholy, dimness|反义词：brightness, cheerfulness, happiness, light|词性:名词

gloomy:/ˈɡluːmi/| adj. |阴暗的；忧郁的；沮丧的|Dark or poorly lit; depressing or pessimistic.|例句：The room was gloomy and damp. 房间阴暗潮湿。

例句：He had a gloomy outlook on life. 他的人生观很悲观。|近义词：dark, sad, depressed, pessimistic, somber|反义词：bright, cheerful, happy, optimistic, light|词性:形容词
