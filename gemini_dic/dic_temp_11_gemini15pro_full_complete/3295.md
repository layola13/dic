afflatus:/əˈfleɪtəs/
noun
神圣的启示，灵感，神的吹气
A divine creative impulse or inspiration.
例句：The artist claimed that his afflatus came directly from the gods.  这位艺术家声称他的灵感直接来自众神。

例句：She felt an afflatus to write a novel about her experiences. 她感到一种冲动，想写一本关于她经历的小说。

近义词：inspiration, revelation, insight, impulse
反义词：lack of inspiration, dullness
词性:n.
