backgrounds:/ˈbækɡraʊndz/ | n. | 背景，经历 | The setting or situation surrounding an event, person, or thing; a person's origin, education, experience, etc. |  She comes from a privileged background. 她出身于一个优越的家庭。| synonyms: backdrop, setting, context, history, origin | antonyms: foreground | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From back + ground.  "Ground" in this sense means the surface or area behind the main subject of a picture.  The metaphorical meaning of "circumstances or past events affecting someone or something" developed later.
