lithocarp:/ˈlɪθəˌkɑːrp/| n. |石果; 核果; 硬核果|A fruit with a stony pericarp, such as a peach or cherry.|Peaches and plums are examples of lithocarps. 桃子和李子都是核果的例子。|Synonyms: stone fruit, drupe|Antonyms: berry, pome|noun
