martyrologist:/ˌmɑːrtəˈrɒlədʒɪst/| n. |殉道者学者；殉道史编纂者|A scholar who studies or writes about martyrs. | Foxe's Book of Martyrs was compiled by a Protestant martyrologist. | 近义词:hagiographer| 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *martyrologium*, from Greek *martyrologion*, from *martyr* (witness) + *-logos* (collecting, speaking).
