high-sounding:/ˌhaɪˈsaʊndɪŋ/
adjective
华而不实的，夸大的
pompous or pretentious, especially in the use of language.
He delivered a high-sounding speech about patriotism, but his actions didn't match his words. 他发表了一篇关于爱国主义的华而不实的演讲，但他的行动与他的言辞并不相符。

high-sounding:/ˌhaɪˈsaʊndɪŋ/
adjective
(尤指语言)浮夸的，言过其实的
(especially of language) pompous or pretentious.
She used a lot of high-sounding jargon in her presentation, but I don't think she really understood the topic. 她在演讲中使用了许多华而不实的术语，但我认为她并不真正理解这个主题。


近义词:pompous, pretentious, grandiose, bombastic, inflated
反义词:simple, modest, unpretentious, down-to-earth


词源:
From high + sounding, referring to words that sound impressive but lack real meaning. 源自 high + sounding，指的是听起来令人印象深刻但缺乏真正意义的词语。
