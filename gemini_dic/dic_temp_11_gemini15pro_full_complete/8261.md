anti-institutionally:/ˌæntiˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃənəli/
adjective
反体制地，反机构地
opposed to established institutions or the way they operate
His art was seen as a form of anti-institutional protest. 他的艺术被视为一种反体制抗议的形式。
The group's anti-institutional stance attracted many young followers.  该组织的反体制立场吸引了许多年轻追随者。

近义词:nonconformist, rebellious, maverick, iconoclastic, dissident, unconventional, unorthodox
反义词:conformist, conventional, orthodox, establishment, institutional
词性:adjective
