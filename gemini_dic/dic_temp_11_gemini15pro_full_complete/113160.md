lupin:/ˈluːpɪn/ | n. | 羽扇豆，羽扇豆的种子 |  A plant of the pea family, with deeply divided leaves and tall colorful flower spikes. The seeds of some kinds of lupin can be eaten. | I have a recipe for lupin flour bread. 我有一个羽扇豆粉面包的食谱。


Lupin seeds are a good source of protein. 羽扇豆种子是蛋白质的良好来源。 | 近义词: lupine | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑其他植物种类，例如 roses 玫瑰) | 词性: noun


词源：From Latin *lupinus, from lupus (“wolf”).  The name may refer to the plant's supposed ability to deplete the soil of nutrients, as if it were "wolfing" them down.
