deputator: /ˈdɛpjʊˌteɪtər/ | n. | 代理人，代表，副手 | A person appointed to act for or represent another; a deputy. | The deputator handled the negotiations in the chairman's absence.  代理人在主席缺席的情况下处理了谈判。 | Synonyms: deputy, representative, substitute, agent, surrogate | Antonyms: principal, leader, head | noun

deputator: /ˈdɛpjʊˌteɪtər/ | n. | 委派者，任命者 | A person who deputes someone else to act on their behalf. | The deputator chose a reliable individual to manage the project. 委派者选择了一个可靠的人来管理这个项目。 | Synonyms: appointer, delegator, commissioner | Antonyms: recipient, appointee, delegate | noun
