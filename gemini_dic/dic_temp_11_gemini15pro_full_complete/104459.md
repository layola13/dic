kaique:/kaɪˈiːk/| n. |小帆船，轻舟|A small sailboat or canoe-like boat, typically used in South America and especially Brazil.  It's often used for fishing or transport on rivers and coastal waters.|The fisherman paddled his caique down the river. 渔夫划着他的小帆船顺流而下。|Synonyms: canoe, skiff, dinghy|Antonyms: ship, yacht|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *caíque*, from Turkish *kayık* "boat, skiff," perhaps ultimately of Turkic or Mongolic origin.
