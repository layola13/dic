hypnogenic:/ˌhɪpnəˈdʒɛnɪk/ /ˌhɪpnəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
催眠的; 诱导入睡的; 导致睡眠的
Tending to produce hypnosis or sleep.
例句：The soft music had a hypnogenic effect.  轻柔的音乐有催眠的效果。

例句：The rhythmic rocking of the train had a hypnogenic effect on the passengers. 火车有节奏的摇晃对乘客有催眠作用。

近义词：soporific, somnolent, hypnotic
反义词：stimulating, invigorating
词性: adjective
