committeeisms:/kəˈmɪtiːɪzəmz/
|n.|委员体；委员会的习气；官样文章|The report was filled with committeeisms, making it difficult to understand the actual findings. 这份报告充满了委员体的语言，很难理解实际的发现。|例句：The document was riddled with committeeisms, obscuring the true meaning.  这份文件充斥着官样文章，掩盖了真正的含义。

例句：She tried to avoid committeeisms in her writing, opting for clear and concise language. 她尽量避免在写作中使用委员体，选择清晰简洁的语言。|近义词：jargon, officialese, bureaucratese|反义词：plain language, clear expression|词性:名词


词源: committee (委员会) + -ism (主义，习气)
