non-tidal:/ˌnɒnˈtaɪdəl/
adjective
非潮汐的
Not relating to or influenced by tides.
The non-tidal currents are driven by wind and density differences. 非潮汐洋流是由风和密度差异驱动的。
The non-tidal wetlands are important habitats for many species. 非潮汐湿地是许多物种的重要栖息地。
近义词:non-periodic
反义词:tidal
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
non-:  prefix meaning "not" from Latin non
tidal: relating to tides, from tide + -al
