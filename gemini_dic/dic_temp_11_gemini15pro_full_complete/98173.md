inscription:/ɪnˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. |题词，铭文，刻印|A piece of writing inscribed on a surface, such as stone or metal. | The inscription on the tombstone was barely legible. 墓碑上的铭文几乎难以辨认。| inscription on the wall 墙上的铭文 | synonym: lettering, engraving, carving | antonym: erasure, deletion | noun

inscription:/ɪnˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. |登记，注册，入会|The act of inscribing or recording.  |  His inscription in the club roster was dated last week. 他在俱乐部名册上的登记日期是上周。| club inscription 俱乐部入会 | synonym: enrollment, registration | antonym: withdrawal, cancellation | noun
