overfruitfulness: /ˌoʊvərˈfruːtfʊlnəs/
| n. | 过度结果实; 过度丰产; 过度生育 | The state of producing too much fruit.  Excessive fertility.  Can also be used metaphorically to describe an overabundance of something. |  The apple tree's overfruitfulness led to smaller, less flavorful apples this year. 今年苹果树结的果实过多，导致苹果个头小，味道淡。 | 近义词: overabundance, plethora, profusion, superabundance | 反义词: scarcity, dearth, barrenness | 词性: noun

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer) + "fruit" (Old French fruit, from Latin fructus) + "-ful" (Old English -ful) + "-ness" (Old English -nes)
