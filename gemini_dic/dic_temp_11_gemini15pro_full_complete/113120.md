lungfishes:/ˈlʌŋfɪʃɪz/
n.
肺鱼
A type of fish that can breathe both air and water.
Lungfishes are known for their ability to survive in drought conditions by burrowing into mud. 肺鱼以其在干旱条件下钻入泥土中生存的能力而闻名。
Lungfishes are ancient fish that have existed for millions of years. 肺鱼是存在了数百万年的古老鱼类。
Synonyms: Dipnoi
Antonyms: None
