nonpenetrability:/ˌnɒnpɛnɪtrəˈbɪlɪti/ /ˌnɑːnpɛnɪtrəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可穿透性，无法渗透性|The quality or state of being nonpenetrable; impenetrability.  The inability of two bodies to occupy the same space at the same time.|The nonpenetrability of matter is a fundamental principle in physics. 物质的不可穿透性是物理学中的一个基本原理。

The nonpenetrability of the fortress walls ensured its safety.  堡垒城墙的不可穿透性确保了它的安全。|近义词：impenetrability, impermeability,  inaccessibility|反义词：permeability, penetrability, porosity|词性:noun
