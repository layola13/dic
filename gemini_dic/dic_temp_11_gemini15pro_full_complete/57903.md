driftpin:/ˈdrɪftpɪn/| n.| 销钉，定位销，顶针 |A pin, usually tapered, used to align parts during assembly or to drive out another pin. |The mechanic used a driftpin to align the holes before inserting the bolt. 机械师在插入螺栓前用定位销对准孔。|The driftpin held the two pieces of metal together perfectly. 这个销钉完美地将两块金属固定在一起。| synonyms: dowel pin, locating pin, alignment pin | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fastener" in a broad sense since it prevents movement rather than enabling alignment) | noun
