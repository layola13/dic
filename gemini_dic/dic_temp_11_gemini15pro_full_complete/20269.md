birthrate:/ˈbɜːθreɪt/| n.|出生率|The number of births in a given population over a specific period, usually expressed as a ratio of live births per 1,000 people per year.|The country's birthrate has been declining steadily for the past decade.  这个国家的出生率在过去十年里一直在稳步下降。|synonyms: fertility rate, natality|antonyms: mortality rate, death rate|noun
