earthpea:/ˈɜːrθˌpiː/
n.
花生;落花生
A plant native to South America that produces pods containing edible seeds. These seeds are also called peanuts.
例句：I love to eat roasted earthpeas. 我喜欢吃烤花生。

例句：Earthpeas are a good source of protein. 花生是良好的蛋白质来源。

近义词：peanut, groundnut
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词)
词性: 名词
