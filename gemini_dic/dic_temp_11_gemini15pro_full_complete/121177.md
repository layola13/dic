misdirectedness:/ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛktɪdnəs/
|n.|误导，方向错误|The misdirectedness of the marketing campaign led to disappointing sales. 营销活动的误导导致销售额令人失望。|The letter's misdirectedness meant it reached the recipient a week late. 信件的投递错误导致收件人晚了一周才收到。|近义词：misguidance, misdirection|反义词: guidance, direction|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From misdirect + -ed + -ness.  "Misdirect" comes from "mis-" (wrongly) + "direct" (to guide).  "-ed" forms the past participle (and sometimes adjective form) of the verb, and "-ness" creates the noun form indicating a state or quality.
