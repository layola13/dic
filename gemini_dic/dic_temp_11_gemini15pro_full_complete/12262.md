ascented:/ækˈsɛntɪd/ | adj. | 带有口音的；加重音符号的 | Having a noticeable accent; marked with an accent mark. | Her accented English revealed her French origins. 她带有口音的英语暴露了她的法国血统。| The accented syllable is the second one.  重读音节是第二个。 | 近义词：toned, stressed, pronounced | 反义词：unaccented, flat | 词性: adjective

词源: From accent + -ed.  来源于 accent (口音) + -ed (形容词后缀).
