gradability:/ˌɡreɪdəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可分级性，等级性 | The quality of an adjective or adverb that can be expressed in different degrees (e.g., very big, bigger, biggest).  |  The gradability of adjectives allows us to express different levels of intensity. 形容词的可分级性使我们能够表达不同程度的强度。 |  The gradability of "hot" is clearly demonstrated by words like "hotter" and "hottest".  “hot”的可分级性可以通过“hotter”和“hottest”等词清楚地证明。 |  scalability, variability | ungradability | noun
