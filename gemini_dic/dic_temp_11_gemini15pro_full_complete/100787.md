intrepidity:/ˌɪn.trəˈpɪd.ə.ti/ | n. | 无畏；勇敢 | Resolute fearlessness, especially in the face of danger. |  She showed remarkable intrepidity in the face of the raging fire. 她面对熊熊大火表现出了非凡的勇气。 |  courage, bravery, boldness, fearlessness, daring, valor | cowardice, timidity, fearfulness, apprehension | noun

intrepid:/ɪnˈtrep.ɪd/ | adj. | 无畏的；勇敢的 |  Fearless; adventurous (often used for rhetorical or humorous effect). | The intrepid explorers ventured into the uncharted jungle. 无畏的探险家们冒险进入了未知的丛林。| brave, courageous, fearless, daring, bold, valiant | cowardly, timid, fearful, apprehensive | adjective
