deservingly:/dɪˈzɜːvɪŋli/
adv.
应得地，理所当然地
in a manner that is deserved; justly.
He was deservingly praised for his bravery. 他因勇敢而理所当然地受到称赞。
近义词: justly, rightfully, appropriately
反义词: undeservedly, unfairly, inappropriately
词性: 副词 (adverb)
词源: From deserve + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *deservire "to serve well," from de- "completely" + servire "to serve."

PHRASE->
deservingly so = 理所当然地；名副其实地
He was praised for his work, and deservingly so. 他因其工作受到赞扬，这是理所当然的。
receive something deservingly = 应得地收到某物
She deservingly received the award for her bravery. 她应得地获得了表彰她勇敢的奖项。
be deservingly punished = 受到应有的惩罚
The criminal was deservingly punished for his crimes.  罪犯因其罪行受到了应有的惩罚。
be deservingly rewarded =  获得应得的奖励
He was deservingly rewarded for his honesty. 他因诚实而获得了应得的奖励。
