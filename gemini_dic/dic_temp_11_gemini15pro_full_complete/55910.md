divulgation:/ˌdaɪvʌlˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 揭露；泄露；公开 | the act of making something known, especially something that was previously secret or unknown |  The divulgation of confidential information led to a scandal.  泄露机密信息导致了一场丑闻。 | synonyms: disclosure, revelation, exposure, unveiling | antonyms: concealment, suppression, secrecy | noun

divulgation:/ˌdaɪvʌlˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 公布；发表 | the act of making something public or available to people | The divulgation of the company's financial results was delayed. 该公司财务业绩的公布被推迟了。| synonyms: publication, announcement, proclamation | antonyms: withholding, suppression | noun
