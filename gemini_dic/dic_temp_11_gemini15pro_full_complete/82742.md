guilded:/ˈɡɪldɪd/| adj.|镀金的，金色的，富丽堂皇的，虚饰的|Covered with gold or a gold-colored substance; having a showy or deceptively attractive appearance.|The guilded cage of Hollywood fame can be a trap for young actors. 好莱坞名望的镀金笼对年轻演员来说可能是个陷阱。|近义词：golden, ornate, embellished, ostentatious|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From gild (v.) + -ed.  "Gild" comes from Old English "gyldan," meaning "to cover with gold."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*gulþijaną," related to "gold."
