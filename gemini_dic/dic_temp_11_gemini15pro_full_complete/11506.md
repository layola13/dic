aridnesses:/əˈrɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
干旱，干燥
The state or quality of being arid; dryness.
The aridnesses of the desert made it difficult for plants to grow. 沙漠的干旱使得植物难以生长。

aridnesses:/əˈrɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
乏味，枯燥
Lack of interest or excitement; dullness.
The aridnesses of the lecture made it hard to stay awake. 演讲的枯燥乏味让人昏昏欲睡。

近义词:dryness, barrenness, drought, aridity, sterility
反义词:humidity, moisture, wetness, lushness, fertility
词性:noun
