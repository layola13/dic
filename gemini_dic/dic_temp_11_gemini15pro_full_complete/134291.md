noninherited:/ˌnɒnɪnˈherɪtɪd/ | adj. | 非遗传的，非继承的 | Not inherited from parents or ancestors; acquired during one's lifetime. | Example:  A suntan is a noninherited characteristic. 晒黑的肤色是非遗传特征。| Example:  His love for music is noninherited, his parents are tone-deaf. 他对音乐的热爱并非遗传，他的父母都是音痴。 | Synonyms: acquired, non-hereditary | Antonyms: inherited, hereditary, genetic | adjective
