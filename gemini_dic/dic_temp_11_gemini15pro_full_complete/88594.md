hitter:/ˈhɪtər/ | n. | 打击者；安打者；<非正式>成功者 | someone who hits something, especially someone who plays baseball and is good at hitting the ball; someone who achieves something impressive |  例句：He's a powerful hitter and can knock the ball out of the park. 他是一个强力击球手，可以把球打出球场。

例句：She's a real hitter in the business world. 她在商界是一位真正的成功人士。 | 近义词：batsman, batter, striker | 反义词：pitcher | 词性：名词

hitter:/ˈhɪtər/ | n. | <非正式>批评者；攻击者 | someone who criticizes or attacks someone or something | 例句：The president's a hitter when it comes to dealing with the opposition. 总统在对付反对派时是一个强硬的攻击者。

例句：He's known as a hitter in the political arena. 他在政治舞台上以攻击性强而闻名。 | 近义词：critic, attacker, assailant | 反义词：supporter, defender | 词性：名词
