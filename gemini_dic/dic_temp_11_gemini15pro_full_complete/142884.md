out-of-the-blue:/ˌaʊt əv ðə ˈbluː/
adjective
突然地；意外地
Happening suddenly and unexpectedly.
例句：The job offer came completely out of the blue. 这份工作机会完全是突然出现的。

例句：One day, out of the blue, she announced she was leaving. 有一天，她突然宣布要离开。

近义词：unexpectedly, suddenly, abruptly
反义词：predictably, expectedly
词性: adjective
