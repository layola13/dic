outdesigned:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈzaɪnd/
verb
在设计上胜过
To have created a superior design to something else; to have designed something that surpasses another design in functionality, aesthetics, or other aspects.
The new phone has completely outdesigned its competitors.  这款新手机在设计上完全胜过了它的竞争对手。
The architect outdesigned all the other submissions for the new museum.  这位建筑师的设计胜过了所有其他提交的新博物馆方案。
surpass, outstrip, outperform
outperform, eclipse
