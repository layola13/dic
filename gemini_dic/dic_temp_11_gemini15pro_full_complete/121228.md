misencodes:/ˌmɪsɪnˈkoʊdz/ | v. | 编码错误，误编码 | To encode incorrectly. This often happens in digital systems where data is represented by a series of bits. A misencode can lead to data corruption or misinterpretation. | The software misencoded the file, resulting in a corrupted image.  该软件对文件进行了错误编码，导致图像损坏。

The message was misencoded, causing the recipient to receive gibberish.  信息编码错误，导致接收者收到乱码。 | 近义词: misrepresents, garbles, corrupts | 反义词: encodes correctly | 词性：动词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "encode" (verb meaning "to convert into a coded form").  "mis-" 前缀（意思是“错误地”或“糟糕地”）+ “encode” 动词（意思是“转换成编码形式”）。
