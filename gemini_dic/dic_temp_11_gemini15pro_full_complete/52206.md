diastole:/daɪˈæstəli/ /daɪˈæstəʊli/| n. | 心脏舒张；舒张期 |The period of the heartbeat when the heart relaxes and allows the chambers to fill with blood. |The doctor measured his systole and diastole. 医生测量了他的收缩压和舒张压。| synonym: relaxation (of the heart) | antonym: systole | noun

diastolic:/ˌdaɪəˈstɒlɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈstɑːlɪk/| adj. | 心脏舒张的；舒张期的 |Relating to the diastole of the heart. |Diastolic blood pressure is the pressure in the arteries when the heart rests between beats. 舒张压是指心脏在两次心跳之间休息时动脉中的压力。| synonym: relaxation (of the heart)  | antonym: systolic | adjective
