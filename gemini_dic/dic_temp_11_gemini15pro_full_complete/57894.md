drifter:/ˈdrɪftər/| n.|漂流者，流浪汉，游民|A person who moves from place to place without a permanent home or job; something that drifts.|He was a drifter, moving from town to town in search of work. 他是一个流浪汉，为了寻找工作而辗转于各个城镇之间。|Synonyms: nomad, wanderer, vagabond, roamer|Antonyms: settler, resident, stay-at-home|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From drift (v.) + -er (agent suffix).  Drift itself comes from Old Norse *drifa, meaning "to drive, push."
