allophylian:/ˌæləˈfɪliən/ | adj. | 外族通婚的；喜爱异族文化的 | Showing a sexual attraction to or preference for people of a different race or ethnic group; having a strong affinity for a culture other than one's own. |  Example: His allophylian tendencies led him to marry someone from a different cultural background.  他外族通婚的倾向让他娶了一个来自不同文化背景的人。

| Example: Her allophylian interests manifested in her extensive travels and study of foreign languages. 她对异族文化的兴趣体现在她广泛的旅行和对外语的学习中。 | 近义词: xenophile | 反义词: xenophobe | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek ἄλλος (állos, “other”) + φίλος (phílos, “loving, dear, friend”).
