murram:/ˈmʌrəm/| n. |红土，风化土，砖红壤|A hard reddish earth used for road surfaces, especially in East Africa. |例句：The murram road was dusty and bumpy.  这条红土路尘土飞扬，坑坑洼洼。

例句：They used murram to build the foundation of the house. 他们用红土建造房屋的地基。

近义词：laterite, hardpan|反义词:|词性:noun

词源:  From Swahili *muram*.
