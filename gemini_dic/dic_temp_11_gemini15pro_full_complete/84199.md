half-regained:/ˌhæf riːˈɡeɪnd/
adj.
一半恢复的，部分恢复的
partially recovered or restored
He felt a sense of half-regained confidence after the pep talk.  他在鼓舞人心的讲话后感到信心部分恢复了。
The team's half-regained strength wasn't enough to win the game. 球队恢复的部分力量不足以赢得比赛。
近义词:partially restored, partly recovered
反义词:fully regained, completely lost
词性:形容词

词源:
"half-" (Old English healf) + "regained" (past participle of "regain," from "re-" (Latin, again) + "gain" (Old North French gaigner, to gain))
