desilted:/diːˈsɪltɪd/
Verb
去除淤泥的，清除淤泥后的
Cleared of silt; having had silt removed.
The harbor was desilted to allow larger ships to enter. 港口被清除了淤泥，以便更大的船只进入。
近义词: dredged, cleaned, cleared
反义词: silted, clogged
词性:  Past Participle (verb)


desilt:/diːˈsɪlt/
Verb
清除淤泥
To remove silt from.
They are desilting the riverbed to improve water flow.  他们正在清除河床的淤泥以改善水流。
近义词: dredge, clear, excavate
反义词: silt up
词性: verb
