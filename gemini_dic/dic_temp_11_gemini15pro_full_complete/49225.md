delegate: /ˈdɛlɪɡət/ (US) /ˈdɛlɪɡeɪt/ (UK)| n. vt.|代表，委托|A person sent or authorized to represent others, in particular an elected representative sent to a conference.|The conference was attended by delegates from over 100 countries.  来自100多个国家的代表出席了会议。| synonyms: representative, envoy, commissioner, agent, deputy, ambassador, emissary | antonyms: constituent, principal | noun

delegate: /ˈdɛlɪɡeɪt/ (US) /ˈdɛlɪɡeɪt/ (UK)| vt.|委派，授权|Entrust (a task or responsibility) to another person, typically one who is less senior than oneself.|He delegated the task of interviewing new candidates to his assistant. 他把面试新候选人的任务委托给了他的助手。 | synonyms: assign, entrust, commit, transfer, consign, devolve | antonyms: retain, keep, withhold | verb
