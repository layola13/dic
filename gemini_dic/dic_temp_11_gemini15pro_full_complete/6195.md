ammite:/ˈæmaɪt/
n.
炸药的一种
A type of explosive.
Ammonite was used in some early forms of dynamite.  氨炸药曾用于早期的一些炸药。
近义词: explosive, dynamite
反义词: None (lack of direct antonym for a specific type of explosive)
词性: noun

词源:  From ammonia + -ite (chemical suffix). 源自 ammonia（氨）+ -ite（化学后缀）。
