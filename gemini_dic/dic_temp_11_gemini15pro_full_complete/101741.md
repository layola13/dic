irresoluble:/ˌɪrɪˈzɒljʊbəl/ (US) /ˌɪrɪˈsɒljʊbəl/ (UK)| adj.|无法解决的，不溶的|Not capable of being resolved or dissolved.|例句：The problem proved to be irresoluble.  这个问题被证明是无法解决的。

例句：The two countries have an irresoluble conflict. 这两个国家之间存在着不可调和的冲突。

近义词：insoluble, unsolvable, intractable|反义词: soluble, resolvable, solvable|词性:形容词

irresolvable:/ˌɪrɪˈzɒlvəbəl/ (US) /ˌɪrɪˈsɒlvəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|无法解决的|Not capable of being resolved.| This word is less common than "irresoluble" and is generally considered a variant spelling.  It carries the same meaning. | 例句：They faced an irresolvable dilemma. 他们面临着一个无法解决的困境。

例句：The irresolvable differences led to the breakup of the partnership. 无法解决的分歧导致了合作关系的破裂。
