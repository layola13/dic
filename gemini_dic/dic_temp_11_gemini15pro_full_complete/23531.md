braindamaged:/ˈbreɪnˌdæmɪdʒd/
adjective
脑损伤的
Having lasting damage to the brain.  This damage can affect cognitive function, motor skills, behavior, and other aspects of a person's life.
The braindamaged patient struggled with basic motor skills. 脑损伤的病人难以进行基本的运动技能训练。
The accident left him braindamaged. 事故使他脑部受损。
Synonyms: brain-injured, mentally impaired, neurologically damaged
Antonyms: healthy, unimpaired, sound
词性: 形容词
