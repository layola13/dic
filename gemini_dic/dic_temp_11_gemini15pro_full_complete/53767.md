discomforting:/dɪsˈkʌmfərtɪŋ/
adjective
令人不安的；不舒服的
Causing anxiety or unease.
The silence after his question was discomforting. 他提问后的一片沉默令人不安。
The room was painted a discomforting shade of green. 房间被漆成一种令人不舒服的绿色。
近义词: unsettling, disturbing, troubling, unnerving
反义词: comforting, reassuring, soothing
词性: adjective

discomfort:/dɪsˈkʌmfərt/
noun
不舒服；不安
A feeling of slight pain or physical uneasiness.
She shifted in her seat, trying to relieve the discomfort in her back. 她在座位上挪了挪，试图缓解背部的酸痛。
He felt a growing discomfort about the situation. 他对情况感到越来越不安。
近义词: unease, malaise, distress
反义词: comfort, ease, well-being
词性: noun

词源:  From dis- (expressing negation) + comfort.  The verb "discomfort" is older, dating back to the 14th century, meaning "to make uneasy." The adjective "discomforting" is a later development.
