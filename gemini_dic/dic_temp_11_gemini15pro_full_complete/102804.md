jargoneer:/ˌdʒɑːrɡəˈnɪr/ | n. |行话使用者；满口术语的人| A person who uses jargon excessively; someone who speaks or writes using technical or specialized language that is difficult for others to understand. |例句：The jargoneer lost his audience with his complex explanations. 这个满口术语的人用他复杂的解释让听众失去了兴趣。

例句：The academic paper, filled with jargon, was difficult for the layman to understand.  这篇学术论文充满了术语，外行人很难理解。|近义词：specialist, technician, expert, professional, connoisseur |反义词:layman, amateur, novice|词性:noun
