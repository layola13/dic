eugenic:/juːˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
优生的；优生学的
relating to or fitted for the production of good offspring, especially in the human race.
Eugenics was a popular social philosophy in the early 20th century. 优生学在20世纪早期是一种流行的社会哲学。
Eugenics has a dark history associated with forced sterilizations. 优生学有一段与强制绝育相关的黑暗历史。
近义词:  genetic, hereditary
反义词: dysgenic
词性: adjective
