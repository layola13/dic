malfunction:/mælˈfʌŋkʃən/
| n. vi.|故障，失灵|A malfunction is a failure to function or work properly. |The printer malfunctioned and printed the document with streaks. 打印机发生故障，打印出来的文件有条纹。|
| | |The car's engine malfunctioned on the highway. 汽车的发动机在高速公路上发生故障。|
| | |synonyms: failure, breakdown, glitch, error, defect|
| | |antonyms: working, functioning, operating, performing|

ETYMOLOGY->
From mal- (badly, wrongly) + function.  "Mal-" comes from the Latin word "male," meaning "badly" or "ill." "Function" comes from the Latin word "functio," meaning "performance" or "execution."
