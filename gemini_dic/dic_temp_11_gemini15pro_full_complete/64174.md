equating:/ɪˈkweɪtɪŋ/
verb
使相等；等同
The act of considering one thing to be the same as or equivalent to another.
Many people equate wealth with happiness. 许多人把财富等同于幸福。
Synonyms:  comparing, likening, paralleling, associating
Antonyms: differentiating, distinguishing, contrasting
verb

equate:/ɪˈkweɪt/
verb
使相等；等同
To consider one thing to be the same as or equivalent to another.
He equates criticism with personal attacks. 他把批评等同于人身攻击。
Synonyms: compare, liken, parallel, associate
Antonyms: differentiate, distinguish, contrast
