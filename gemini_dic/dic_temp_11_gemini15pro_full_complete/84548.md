hammer-away:/ˈhæmər əˈweɪ/
verb phrase
反复强调；坚持不懈地做
to repeat something many times or to work at something persistently
He hammered away at the importance of education. 他反复强调教育的重要性。
The team hammered away at the project until it was finished. 团队坚持不懈地做这个项目，直到完成。
persist, persevere, plug away, keep at it
give up, quit
