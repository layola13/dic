institutist: /ˌɪnstɪˈtjuːtɪst/
n.
拥护制度者，制度主义者
A person who advocates for or adheres to institutionalism.
The institutist argued that strong institutions are key to a stable society.  这位制度主义者认为，强大的制度是社会稳定的关键。
Synonyms: institutionalist, conventionalist
Antonyms:  nonconformist, rebel, iconoclast
noun
