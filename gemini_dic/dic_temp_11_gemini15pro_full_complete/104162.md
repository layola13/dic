junina:/ʒuˈninə/ |adj. n.|六月节的；六月节|Relating to or characteristic of the Brazilian festivities of Festa Junina, typically celebrated in June.  It can also refer to the festivities themselves.  These festivities often involve bonfires, traditional food, music, and dance.|例句：The junina celebrations are a vibrant part of Brazilian culture.  六月节的庆祝活动是巴西文化中充满活力的一部分。

例句：She wore a colorful junina dress to the party. 她穿着色彩鲜艳的六月节服装去参加派对。|近义词：Festa Junina (which is the actual name of the festival) |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective, noun
