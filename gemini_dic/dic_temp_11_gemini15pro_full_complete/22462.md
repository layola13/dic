booklike:/ˈbʊklaɪk/
adj.
像书的；书本似的
Resembling a book in form or appearance.
The artifact was small and booklike, bound in leather.  这件文物很小，像书一样，用皮革装订。
The tablet computer, despite its electronic nature, had a surprisingly booklike feel.  这款平板电脑虽然是电子产品，但手感却出奇地像书。
近义词：bookish
反义词: None
