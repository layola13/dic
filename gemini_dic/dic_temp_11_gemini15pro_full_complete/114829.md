mammal-like:/ˈmæməlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像哺乳动物的；类似哺乳动物的|Resembling or characteristic of a mammal.  This refers to features or traits that are similar to those found in mammals.|The ancient reptile had mammal-like teeth.  这种古老的爬行动物有类似哺乳动物的牙齿。|Near synonyms:  mammalian, therapsid|Antonym:  reptilian|Word type: adjective

mammal-like:/ˈmæməlˌlaɪk/| n.|类似哺乳动物的动物|An animal that resembles a mammal, often referring to extinct synapsids.|The cynodont was a mammal-like reptile.  犬齿龙是一种类似哺乳动物的爬行动物。|Near synonyms:  proto-mammal, mammalian ancestor|Antonym:  reptile|Word type: noun
