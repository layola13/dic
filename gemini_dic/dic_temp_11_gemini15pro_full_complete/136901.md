nonsolicitous:/ˌnɑːnsəˈlɪsɪtəs/
adjective
不关心的，不操心的，漠不关心的
Not showing care or concern; indifferent.
She was nonsolicitous about the outcome of the election. 她对选举结果漠不关心。
synonyms: unconcerned, indifferent, apathetic, uninterested, detached, aloof, uncaring, careless, heedless, regardless, nonchalant
antonyms: solicitous, concerned, caring, attentive, mindful
