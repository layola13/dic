bilabials:/baɪˈleɪbiəlz/| n.|双唇音|Consonant sounds produced using both lips.  These sounds are made by obstructing or constricting airflow at the lips.|The sounds /p/, /b/, and /m/ are bilabials.  /p/、/b/和/m/都是双唇音。|近义词:labials|反义词:|noun
