outsail:/ˌaʊtˈseɪl/
verb
胜过航行，在航行中超过
to sail faster or better than another ship or sailor
The newer yachts easily outsailed the older ones. 新型游艇轻松胜过旧型游艇。
近义词: outstrip, surpass, beat
反义词: lag behind, trail
