changeful: /ˈtʃeɪndʒfəl/ | adj. | 易变的，多变的 | Subject to frequent change; variable. | The weather has been very changeful recently, switching from sunshine to rain in an instant. 最近天气变化无常，瞬间从晴天转为雨天。

The political landscape is changeful, with new alliances forming and dissolving all the time.  政治格局瞬息万变，新的联盟不断形成和瓦解。

| 近义词: variable, fickle, mutable, erratic, unstable, volatile | 反义词: constant, stable, steady, unchanging, invariable | 词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Middle English *chaungeful*, equivalent to *change* + *-ful*.  The word "change" itself ultimately comes from the Latin "cambiare" meaning "to barter, exchange."
