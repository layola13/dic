minicyclones:/ˈmɪniˌsaɪkloʊnz/| n. |小型气旋，迷你旋风|A small-scale atmospheric vortex, resembling a cyclone but smaller in size and often short-lived. These are often observed over warm water bodies or deserts. |Minicyclones formed over the lake, creating dust devils on the shore. 小型气旋在湖面上形成，在岸边制造了尘卷风。

|Minicyclones can be hazardous to small aircraft. 小型气旋对小型飞机来说是危险的。|近义词：dust devils, whirlwinds, vortices|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "calm," "stillness")|词性:noun
