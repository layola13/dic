codefendant:/ˌkoʊdɪˈfɛndənt/| n.|共同被告|A person who is charged with a crime along with another person or other people.|例句：The codefendants were tried together. 共同被告一起受审。|例句：She and her codefendant pleaded not guilty. 她和她的共同被告拒不认罪。|近义词：fellow defendant|反义词:plaintiff, prosecutor|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
"co-" (together, joint) + "defendant" (a person sued in a court of law)
