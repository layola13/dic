cev:/dʒɛv/| n.|土耳其烤肉|Turkish grilled or roasted meat, often lamb or beef, cooked on a vertical rotisserie. It is usually served in a pita or other flatbread, or on a plate with various accompaniments.|Example: I had a delicious cevapi for lunch. 我午餐吃了一份美味的土耳其烤肉。|Example: The cev was seasoned perfectly and cooked to a tender perfection. 这份烤肉调味完美，烤得鲜嫩可口。|近义词：kebab, shawarma, gyro|反义词:|noun
