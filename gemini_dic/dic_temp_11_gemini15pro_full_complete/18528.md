benefactrice: /ˌbɛnɪˈfækt.rɪs/
n.
女施主，女恩人
A woman who confers a benefit; a female benefactor.
She was a benefactrice of the arts, donating generously to the museum. 她是一位艺术赞助人，慷慨地向博物馆捐款。
The hospital was built thanks to a generous benefactrice. 这家医院的建成多亏了一位慷慨的女捐助者。
近义词: patroness, donor
反义词: malefactor
词性: noun
