non-intelligence:/ˌnɒn ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/ | n. | 非情报；非智力；无智力 | Lack of intelligence; the state of not being intelligent. |  The test measures non-intelligence factors such as emotional stability.  这项测试测量的是非智力因素，例如情绪稳定性。 |  The algorithm filters out non-intelligence signals from the data. 该算法从数据中过滤掉非情报信号。 | 近义词: unintelligence, stupidity, ignorance | 反义词: intelligence, intellect, smartness | 词性: 名词

词源:
"non-" is a prefix meaning "not," from Latin.  "Intelligence" comes from Old French "intelligence," ultimately from Latin "intelligentia," meaning "understanding, knowledge, power of discerning."
