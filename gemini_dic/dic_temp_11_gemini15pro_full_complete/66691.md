exogyra:/ˌɛksˈɒdʒɪrə/ | n. | 牡蛎；外卷牡蛎 | An extinct oyster of the genus Exogyra, having a spirally coiled shell with the lower valve strongly convex.  It's a type of fossil oyster characterized by its thick, often curved or spiraled shell. |  Exogyra fossils are common in Cretaceous rocks. 外卷牡蛎化石在白垩纪岩石中很常见。|  The museum displayed a large Exogyra costata fossil. 博物馆展出了一块巨大的肋骨外卷牡蛎化石。| synonyms: oyster, gryphaea | antonyms: N/A | noun
