cangue:/kæŋ/ | n. |枷, 颈枷 |A heavy wooden frame worn around the neck as a punishment, used especially in China. | The cangue was a common punishment in ancient China for petty criminals.  古代中国，枷是用于轻罪犯的常见刑罚。|The prisoner was forced to wear the cangue for a week. 囚犯被迫戴枷一周。|Synonyms: yoke, stocks, pillory | Antonyms: freedom, liberty | noun
