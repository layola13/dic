jack:/dʒæk/| v.|抬起；顶起；提高（价格等）|To lift or raise something, often with a jack; to increase sharply (price, etc.)|例句：They jacked up the price of gas. 他们提高了汽油价格。|近义词：raise, lift, hoist, boost|反义词:lower, decrease|verb
