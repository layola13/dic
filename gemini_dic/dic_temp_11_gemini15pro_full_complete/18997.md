bestowments:/bɪˈstoʊmənts/
|n.| 赋予，赠与；捐赠物|Something bestowed, a gift or donation.  Specifically, natural talents or qualities.|The university was grateful for the bestowments of its alumni. 这所大学对校友们的捐赠表示感谢。|Near synonyms: gifts, donations, endowments, benefactions|Antonyms: deprivations, confiscations|Noun
