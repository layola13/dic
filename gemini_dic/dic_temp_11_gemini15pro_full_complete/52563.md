diffused:/dɪˈfjuːzd/| adj.|弥漫的，扩散的，散开的|Spread out over a large area; not concentrated.|The light was diffused by the fog. 光线被雾散射了。|近义词：spread, dispersed, scattered, disseminated|反义词：concentrated, focused, localized|adjective

diffused:/dɪˈfjuːzd/| adj.|不清楚的，朦胧的，不清晰的|Not clear or distinct; vague.|The photograph was diffused and out of focus. 照片模糊不清，焦点不对。|近义词：indistinct, vague, blurry, hazy|反义词：clear, distinct, sharp, focused|adjective
