demoralised:/dɪˈmɒrəlaɪzd/ /dɪˈmɔːrəlaɪzd/
adjective
泄气的，沮丧的，士气低落的
Having lost confidence or hope; disheartened.
The team was demoralised after losing the championship game.  这支队伍在输掉冠军赛后士气低落。
The constant criticism left him feeling demoralised.  不断的批评让他感到沮丧。
synonym:discouraged, disheartened, dejected, despondent
antonym:encouraged, heartened, optimistic, motivated
词性:adjective


demoralize:/dɪˈmɒrəlaɪz/ /dɪˈmɔːrəlaɪz/
verb
使泄气，使沮丧，使士气低落
To cause (someone) to lose confidence or hope; dispirit.
The loss demoralized the entire team.  这场失利使整个团队士气低落。
His harsh words demoralized her. 他的刻薄的话让她很沮丧。
synonym:discourage, dishearten, dispirit
antonym:encourage, hearten, inspire
词性:verb
