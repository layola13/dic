disableness:/dɪsˈeɪblɪdnəs/
n.
残疾，无能，无力
The state or condition of being disabled; incapacity.
His disableness prevented him from climbing the stairs. 他的残疾使他无法爬楼梯。
近义词: disability, impairment, incapacity, handicap
反义词: ability, capability, competence
