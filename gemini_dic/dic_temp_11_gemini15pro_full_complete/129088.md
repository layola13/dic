nitrose:/ˈnaɪtrəs/ /ˈnaɪtroʊs/ | adj.| 硝基的，含氮的; nitrous acid or its salts, especially potassium nitrite, used in curing meat. | relating to or containing nitrogen, especially in a combined state with oxygen. Nitrose gases are formed in some industrial processes.  |  The nitrose fumes were overpowering.  硝基烟雾很浓。 | synonyms: nitric, nitrogenous, nitrogen-bearing | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "oxygen-bearing" or "denitrified" depending on context) | word type: adjective

Sodium nitrite is used as a food preservative and an antidote to cyanide poisoning. |  The use of nitrites in processed meats is controversial.  在加工肉类中使用亚硝酸盐是有争议的。 | synonyms:  (Specific nitrites like sodium nitrite, potassium nitrite) | antonyms: (No direct antonyms) | word type: noun
