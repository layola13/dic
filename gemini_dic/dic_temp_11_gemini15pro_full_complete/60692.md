electrifier:/ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪər/| n.|电气化者，充电器|A person or thing that electrifies something. electrify:/ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪ/| v.|使电气化，使兴奋，使激动|To charge with electricity, or to thrill or excite.|The news electrified the nation. 这条新闻震惊了全国。|近义詞:excite, thrill, stimulate, energize, galvanize|反义詞:bore, depress, calm, soothe|verb
