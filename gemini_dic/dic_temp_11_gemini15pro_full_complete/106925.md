lacewings:/ˈleɪswɪŋz/
n.
草蛉
insects of the order Neuroptera, characterized by their delicate, lace-like wings.
Lacewings are beneficial insects that help control garden pests. 草蛉是有益的昆虫，可以帮助控制花园害虫。

Lacewings are often found in gardens and fields. 草蛉经常在花园和田野里被发现。

近义词: aphid lion (specifically the larvae), neuropteran
反义词: pest (in a general sense, as lacewings are predators of pests)
词性: noun
