foray:/ˈfɔːreɪ/ (US), /ˈfɒreɪ/ (UK)| n. vi.|突袭，袭击；涉足，初次尝试|A sudden attack or incursion into enemy territory, especially one made for the purpose of obtaining something; a brief and often adventurous excursion into a new field or area of activity.|例句：The troops made a foray into enemy territory. 部队突袭了敌方领土。
