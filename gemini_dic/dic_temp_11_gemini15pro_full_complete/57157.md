dowdiest: /ˈdaʊdiɪst/
adjective
最寒酸的，最土气的
(superlative of) dowdy:  Dressed in an unattractive or old-fashioned way; shabby.  Having a dull or uninteresting appearance.
例句：She wore the dowdiest dress to the party. 她穿着最土气的衣服去参加了派对。
例句：He looked dowdiest in that old suit. 他穿着那套旧西装看起来最寒酸。
近义词：shabbiest, frumpiest, drab, unfashionable
反义词：smartest, stylish, fashionable, chic
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From dowdy + -est (superlative suffix). Dowdy is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "dowd," a term for an elderly, shabbily dressed woman.
