finnikin:/ˈfɪnɪkɪn/| adj.|讲究的，挑剔的，过分讲究的|Excessively particular or fastidious, especially in matters of taste or dress; fussy.|She's so finnikin about her food, it drives me crazy. 她对食物太挑剔了，快把我逼疯了。|He's a finnikin dresser, always concerned with the smallest details. 他穿衣打扮很讲究，总是关注最细微的细节。|近义词：fussy, fastidious, particular, picky, choosy|反义词：unfussy, easygoing, undemanding|词性:形容词

词源:
Likely a variant of *finikin*, itself of uncertain origin. Possibly related to *fine* (adj.), referring to a refined or delicate sensibility, or perhaps influenced by Dutch *fijn* "fine, delicate."
