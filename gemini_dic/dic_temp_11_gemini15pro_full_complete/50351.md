depolished:/diːˈpɒlɪʃt/
adj.
失去光泽的，磨光的
Having had the polish removed; matte.
The depolished granite countertop gave the kitchen a rustic look.  失去光泽的花岗岩台面给厨房带来了一种乡村气息。
近义词: unpolished, matte, dull, frosted
反义词: polished, glossy, shiny
词性:形容词

depolish:/diːˈpɒlɪʃ/
v.
去除光泽，使失去光泽
To remove the polish from; make matte.
The craftsman deliberately depolished the metal to create a vintage effect.  工匠特意去除了金属的光泽，以营造复古效果。
近义词: dull, matte, tarnish
反义词: polish, shine, gloss, burnish
词性:动词
