manipulate:/məˈnɪpjuleɪt/ /məˈnɪpjəleɪt/| vt.|操纵，控制，处理|to control or influence something or someone, often in a clever or dishonest way; to skillfully handle or operate something|He manipulated the stock market for his own profit. 他操纵股市为自己牟利。|synonyms: control, influence, exploit, maneuver, operate, handle|antonyms: release, liberate, free, neglect|verb

manipulate:/məˈnɪpjuleɪt/ /məˈnɪpjəleɪt/| adj.|可操纵的|able to be manipulated|The controls on this machine are quite easy to manipulate. 这台机器上的控制装置很容易操作。|synonyms: controllable, maneuverable, operable|antonyms: fixed, immovable, unmanageable|adjective
