nonqualifying:/nɒnˈkwɒlɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
不合格的，没有资格的
Not meeting the requirements or criteria for something.
The nonqualifying rounds of the tournament were held last week.  上周举行了锦标赛的非资格赛。
He made a nonqualifying contribution to his retirement plan. 他向他的退休计划缴纳了一笔不符合条件的款项。
近义词: ineligible, unacceptable, disqualifying
反义词: qualifying, eligible, acceptable
词性: adjective
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + qualifying (present participle of the verb "qualify")
