climacteric:/klaɪˈmæk.tər.ɪk/ | adj. n. |更年期; 突变期; 关键时期 |A period of significant change or transition, especially referring to the menopause in women or a corresponding period in men. It can also refer to a critical stage or turning point in the development of something.|

例句：She experienced the usual climacteric symptoms. 她经历了常见的更年期症状。

例句：The company is at a climacteric in its history. 这家公司正处于其历史上的一个关键时期。

词源： From Late Latin climactericus, from Greek klimakterikos "of or pertaining to a critical period in human life," from klimax (genitive klimaktos) "ladder, rung of a ladder; critical point in sickness, the climacteric year in life."
