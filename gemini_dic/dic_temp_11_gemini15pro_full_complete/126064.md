muster-in: /ˈmʌstər ɪn/ (British) /ˈmʌstər ɪn/ (American)
verb
正式入伍；召集
to officially enroll someone into the armed forces.
The recruits were mustered in at the army base.  新兵在军事基地正式入伍。
The regiment was mustered in for the upcoming war.  该军团为了即将到来的战争而集结。
synonyms: enroll, enlist, induct, conscript
antonyms: discharge, demobilize, muster-out
verb

muster-in: /ˈmʌstər ɪn/ (British) /ˈmʌstər ɪn/ (American)
noun
入伍；召集
the act of officially enrolling someone into the armed forces.
The muster-in ceremony was held on the parade ground.  入伍仪式在阅兵场上举行。
The muster-in of new troops boosted the army's morale.  新兵的入伍鼓舞了军队的士气。
synonyms: enrollment, enlistment, induction, conscription
antonyms: discharge, demobilization, muster-out
