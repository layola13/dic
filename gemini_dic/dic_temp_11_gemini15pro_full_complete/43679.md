credentialing:/krɪˈdɛnʃəlɪŋ/
n.
资格认证；资格审查
the process of checking someone's qualifications and experience to make sure they are suitable for a particular job or course.
例句：The credentialing process for doctors is very rigorous.  医生资格认证的过程非常严格。

例句：The organization handles the credentialing of all its members. 该组织负责所有成员的资格认证。
近义词：certification, accreditation, licensing
反义词：disqualification
词性：名词
