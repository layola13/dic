havoc:/ˈhævək/| n.|浩劫，大破坏，混乱| widespread destruction and disorder: The storm wreaked havoc on the coast.  这场暴风雨对海岸造成了严重的破坏。|例句：The earthquake caused havoc in the city. 地震给这座城市带来了浩劫。

例句：The virus wreaked havoc on the computer system. 病毒对计算机系统造成了严重破坏。|近义词：devastation, destruction, chaos, mayhem|反义词：order, peace, calm|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old French *havoc* "pillaging, devastation," from the exclamation *havok* "cry of attack," of uncertain origin.
