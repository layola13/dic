dexa:/ˈdɛksə/
prefix|前缀|A prefix meaning "ten," used in the metric system.|Used in words like "decagram" (ten grams) and "decaliter" (ten liters).|A decagram is a unit of mass equal to ten grams.|近义词: deca-|反义词: deci-|词性: prefix
