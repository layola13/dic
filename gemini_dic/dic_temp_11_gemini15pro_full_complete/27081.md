calibrates:/ˈkælɪbreɪts/| v.|校准，标定|to check or adjust a measuring instrument to ensure its accuracy. ETYMOLOGY->
From French calibrer, from Italian calibrare,  from Arabic qālib "mold," perhaps influenced by Late Latin calibrare "to measure the bore of a gun."
