mastabah:/ˈmæstəbɑː/ /ˈmɑːstəbɑː/
|n.|梯形金字塔，平顶金字塔|A flat-roofed, rectangular structure with sloping sides, built over a subterranean burial chamber, especially in ancient Egypt.|The pharaoh's mastaba was smaller than the later pyramids. 法老的梯形金字塔比后来的金字塔小。|近义词: tomb, burial chamber|反义词: None|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic مصطبة (maṣṭaba), meaning "bench".
