north-easterly: /ˌnɔːrθˈiːstərli/ /ˌnɔːrθˈiːstərli/
adjective
东北方的；从东北方来的
Coming from the northeast; situated in or towards the northeast.
The north-easterly wind brought cold air. 东北风带来了冷空气。
The ship sailed in a north-easterly direction. 船向东北方向航行。
Synonyms: northeastern
Antonyms: south-westerly, south-western
词性: adjective
