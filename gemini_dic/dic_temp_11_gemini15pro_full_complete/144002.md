outreaching:/ˌaʊtˈriːtʃɪŋ/
adjective
外展的；延伸的；积极联系的
making a special effort to help or give advice to people, especially people who live in the poorer areas of a town or city
例句：The library has an outreach program for children in the community. 图书馆有一个面向社区儿童的外展项目。

例句：The church's outreach ministry provides food and clothing to the homeless. 教堂的外展事工为无家可归者提供食物和衣服。

近义词：expansive, extending, proactive, community-based
反义词：insular, withdrawn, inward-looking
词性：adjective


outreach:/ˈaʊtriːtʃ/
noun
外展；延伸；积极联系
the activity of an organization that provides services to people in the community, especially those who cannot or are unlikely to come to an office, a hospital, etc. for help
例句：The hospital does a lot of outreach work with elderly people in the community. 这家医院为社区老年人做了很多外展工作。

例句：The organization's outreach efforts are focused on helping at-risk youth. 该组织的外展工作重点是帮助有风险的青年。
近义词：community service, social work, public service, assistance
反义词：neglect, indifference
词性：noun


词源:  "outreach" comes from the verb "to outreach," which is formed by combining the prefix "out-" (meaning "beyond" or "further") with the verb "reach" (meaning "to extend to" or "to make contact with").  The earliest recorded use of "outreach" as a verb is from the 14th century.
