correligionist:/ˌkɔːrɪˈlɪdʒənɪst/ /ˌkɑːrɪˈlɪdʒənɪst/
n.
教友；同教者
A person who shares the same religion as another.
Example: He greeted his correligionists at the temple.  他在寺庙里向他的教友们问好。

Example:  She found comfort and support among her correligionists after the disaster. 灾难过后，她在教友中找到了安慰和支持。

Synonym:  co-religionist, fellow believer, coreligionary
Antonym:  nonbeliever, infidel, heretic
词性: noun
