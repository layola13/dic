breezinesses:/ˈbriːzinəsɪz/
noun
轻快，活泼，轻松愉快
The state of being breezy; airiness.  A light and cheerful quality.
The breezinesses of her personality made her popular. 她性格的轻快活泼使她很受欢迎。
Synonyms: airiness, lightness, freshness, liveliness, cheerfulness, levity
Antonyms: seriousness, heaviness, gloominess, dullness
词性: 名词
