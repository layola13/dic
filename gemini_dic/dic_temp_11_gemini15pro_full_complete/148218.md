overshrunk:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrʌŋk/ | adj. | 过度缩水的，缩水过度的 |  Having shrunk excessively, usually due to washing or drying at too high a temperature.  |  This sweater is overshrunk; I can't even get it over my head.  这件毛衣缩水太厉害了，我连头都套不进去。 |Synonyms: shrunken, too small, undersized| Antonyms: oversized, baggy, loose| adjective

overshrunk:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrʌŋk/ | v. | (使)过度缩水 | Past participle of *overshrink*.  Having been shrunk excessively, usually due to washing or drying at too high a temperature.  | I've overshrunk my favorite shirt in the dryer. 我把最喜欢的衬衫在烘干机里烘缩水了。 | Synonyms: shrunken, shrunk | Antonyms: stretched, expanded | verb
