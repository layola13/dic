ecocidal:/ˈiːkəʊˌsaɪdəl/ /ˈekəʊˌsaɪdəl/| adj.|生态灭绝的，对生态造成毁灭性破坏的|Relating to or involving ecocide; destructive to the environment.  |例句：The use of such powerful pesticides is ecocidal.  使用如此强力的杀虫剂会造成生态灭绝。


例句：The company's ecocidal practices led to the destruction of the local rainforest. 该公司的生态灭绝行为导致了当地雨林的破坏。


近义词：environmentally destructive, ecologically damaging, ruinous, devastating|反义词:eco-friendly, environmentally sustainable|词性:adjective
