Morgoth:/ˈmɔːrɡɒθ/| n. |魔苟斯|The primary antagonist in J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium, a fallen Vala and the original Dark Lord.  He is the embodiment of evil in Middle-earth, responsible for much of its suffering and corruption.|例句：Morgoth, once a Vala of great power, rebelled against the other Valar and sought to dominate Middle-earth. 魔苟斯，曾经是一位拥有强大力量的维拉，反抗了其他维拉，并试图统治中土世界。

例句：The Elves fought valiantly against Morgoth and his armies of Orcs. 精灵们英勇地对抗魔苟斯和他的兽人军队。|近义词：Dark Lord, the Enemy|反义词:None (No direct opposite in Tolkien's mythology, although good forces oppose him)|词性:noun
