contester's:/kənˈtɛstərz/
noun
竞争者, 争辩者, 质疑者
belonging to or associated with a contester (someone who disputes something or enters a contest)
The contester's argument was well-researched.  竞争者的论点经过了充分的研究。
synonyms: disputant's, challenger's, opponent's
antonyms: supporter's, advocate's
n.
