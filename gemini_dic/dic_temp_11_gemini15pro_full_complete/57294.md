downsitting:/ˈdaʊnˌsɪtɪŋ/
noun
静坐; 冥想; 坐下来
The act of sitting down.  It can also refer to a period of quiet contemplation or meditation, especially in a seated posture.
He found peace in downsitting and reflection. 他在静坐和反思中找到了平静。
synonyms: sitting, settling, seating, meditation, contemplation
antonyms: standing, rising, moving, activity
