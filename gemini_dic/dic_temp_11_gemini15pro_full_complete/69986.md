fetal:/ˈfiːt(ə)l/ (US), /ˈfiːtəl/ (UK)| adj.|胎儿的；胎的|Relating to a fetus; of or occurring in a fetus.|例句：Fetal development is a complex process. 胎儿发育是一个复杂的过程。

例句：The doctor monitored the fetal heartbeat. 医生监测了胎儿的心跳。|近义词：embryonic, unborn|反义词：postnatal, newborn|词性：形容词

词源：From Late Latin *fetalis*, from Latin fetus (“offspring, pregnancy”).
