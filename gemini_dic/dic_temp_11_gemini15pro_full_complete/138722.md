number:/ˈnʌmbər/ (US) /ˈnʌmbə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. adj.|数字，号码；数量；麻木；|A number of people attended the meeting. 许多人参加了会议。|近义词：numeral, figure, quantity, sum, total|反义词: few, minority|词性:名词, 动词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *nombre*, from Latin numerus (“number, quantity”), related to Greek νέμω (némō, “I distribute, assign”).
