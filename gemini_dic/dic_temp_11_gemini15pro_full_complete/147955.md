overreservedness:/ˌoʊvərˌrɪˈzɜːrvdnəs/
|n.|过度拘谨，过分矜持|The state of being excessively reserved or restrained in manner or behavior.  It implies a reluctance to express oneself freely or openly, often due to shyness, formality, or a desire to avoid attention.|Her overreservedness made it difficult for her to make new friends. 她过分的矜持让她很难结交新朋友。| reserve, reticence, inhibition, modesty|extroversion, openness, boldness, gregariousness|noun
