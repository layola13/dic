lepidosauria:/ˌlɛpɪdoʊˈsɔːriə/| n. |鳞龙亚纲|A subclass of reptiles, distinguished by characteristics such as overlapping scales and a transverse cloacal slit.  It includes lizards, snakes, and the tuatara.|例句：Lepidosauria is a diverse group containing over 10,000 species.  鳞龙亚纲是一个包含超过10,000个物种的多样化群体。

例句：The tuatara is the only surviving member of the order Rhynchocephalia within Lepidosauria.  楔齿蜥是鳞龙亚纲喙头目中唯一幸存的成员。|近义词：Squamata (partially),  Rhynchocephalia (partially)|反义词: None (as it's a taxonomic classification)|词性:noun
