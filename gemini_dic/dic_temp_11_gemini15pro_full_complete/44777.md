crumpling:/ˈkrʌmplɪŋ/ | n. | 弄皱，压皱 |The act of crushing something into wrinkles or folds. |He balled up the paper, crumpling it tightly in his fist. 他把纸揉成一团，紧紧地握在手里。|  The crumpling of the aluminum can was surprisingly loud.  铝罐被压扁的声音出奇地大。| synonyms: crushing, wrinkling, creasing | antonyms: smoothing, flattening, ironing | Noun

crumpling:/ˈkrʌmplɪŋ/ | adj. |  易皱的；正在被弄皱的 | Tending to wrinkle or crease easily; in the process of being wrinkled or creased. |  The crumpling fabric was difficult to work with. 这种易皱的布料很难加工。|  The sound of crumpling leaves filled the autumn air.  秋风中充满了树叶沙沙作响的声音。| synonyms: wrinkling, creasing, foldable | antonyms: smooth, flat, ironed | Adjective
