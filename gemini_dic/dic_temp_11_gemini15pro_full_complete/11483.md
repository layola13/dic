arhythmic:/əˈrɪðmɪk/
adjective
无节律的，心律失常的
Lacking rhythm or regularity, especially in relation to the heartbeat.
His arhythmic heartbeat concerned the doctors. 他的心律不齐让医生很担心。
近义词:irregular, erratic
反义词:rhythmic, regular
词性:adjective

arhythmical:/əˈrɪðmɪkəl/
adjective
无节律的，心律失常的
Lacking rhythm or regularity, especially in relation to the heartbeat. (Less common than "arhythmic")
The arhythmical pulse suggested a serious heart condition.  不规则的脉搏暗示着一种严重的心脏疾病。
近义词:irregular, erratic
反义词:rhythmic, regular
词性:adjective


词源:
From a- (not) + rhythmic, from Greek rhythmos (rhythm).
