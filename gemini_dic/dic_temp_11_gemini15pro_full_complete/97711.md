inimitability:/ɪnˌɪmɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.
inimitable quality; the state of being impossible to imitate or copy.
无法模仿；独特性；不可比拟
Her inimitability as a dancer made her a legend. 她作为一名舞蹈演员的独特性使她成为一个传奇。

近义词:unmatchedness, peerlessness, singularity, uniqueness, irreplaceability
反义词:ordinariness, commonness, typicality

词性:noun

词源:From Late Latin *inimitabilis "inimitable," from in- "not" + imitabilis "imitable"  (from imitari "to imitate"). The suffix -ity transforms the adjective into a noun denoting a state or quality.
