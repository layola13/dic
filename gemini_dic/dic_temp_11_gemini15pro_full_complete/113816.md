madame:/ˈmædəm/ (US), /ˈmædəm/ (UK)| n.|女士，夫人；太太|A polite term of address for a woman, originally used for a woman of high rank or authority.  Used especially in shops by assistants to female customers.|例句：Madame, may I help you? 女士，有什么可以帮您的吗？|近义词：Mrs., lady, ma'am|反义词: sir, mister|noun
