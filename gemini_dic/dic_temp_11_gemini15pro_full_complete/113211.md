luscious:/ˈlʌʃəs/| adj.|美味的，甘美的；诱人的，性感的|Highly pleasing to the taste or smell; richly satisfying; sensually attractive.|例句：The cake was so luscious, I couldn't resist having another piece.  蛋糕太美味了，我忍不住又吃了一块。

例句：She wore a luscious red dress that turned heads wherever she went. 她穿着一件性感的红色连衣裙，无论走到哪里都引人注目。|近义词：delicious, delectable, tempting, seductive, alluring|反义词：unappetizing, bland, repulsive|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *luscious*, from Old French *luscios* "delicious," probably from Latin *luxuria* "luxury, abundance," from *luxus* "excess, luxury."
