glooming:/ˈɡluːmɪŋ/| n.|  昏暗，忧郁 | the state of being dark or depressing | The glooming of the weather reflected his mood.  天气阴沉反映了他的心情。| synonyms: darkness, gloominess, depression | antonyms: brightness, cheerfulness | noun
