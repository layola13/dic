garde:/ɡɑːrd/ | n. | 警卫，守卫，防护装置 | A person or group of people who protect something or someone. | The king had a loyal garde to protect him. 国王有一支忠诚的卫队保护他。|  The museum's security garde patrols the galleries. 博物馆的保安人员在展厅巡逻。 | synonyms: guard, sentinel, watchman | antonyms: attacker, assailant | noun

garde:/ɡɑːrd/ | n. | (法语) 看守，保管 |  (French) Act of guarding or keeping. |  The documents were under strict garde. 文件受到严密保管。 | The jewels were placed in the bank's garde. 珠宝被存放在银行的保管库中。 | synonyms: safekeeping, custody, protection | antonyms: neglect, abandonment | noun
