enthrone: /ɪnˈθroʊn/ | v. |  登基；加冕；使…居于最高地位 |  The new king was enthroned in a lavish ceremony. 新国王在一个奢华的仪式上登基。 | 例句：They enthroned him as their leader. 他们拥立他为他们的领袖。 | 近义词：crown, install, invest | 反义词：dethrone, remove | 词性: 动词

词源: From en- + throne.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "to put into or onto."  Therefore, "enthrone" literally means "to put onto a throne."
