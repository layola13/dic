outfeasting:/ˌaʊtˈfiːstɪŋ/
| n. | 盛宴；宴饮；胜过…的宴饮 | The outfeasting and revelry continued for days. 盛宴和狂欢持续了好几天。 |  The king's outfeasting was legendary. 国王的盛宴是传奇性的。| 近义词：feasting, banqueting, carousing | 反义词：fasting, abstinence | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From "out-" (meaning to surpass or exceed) + "feasting" (the act of having a feast).  The "out-" prefix indicates surpassing or exceeding in the act of feasting.
