glitters:/ˈɡlɪtərz/| v. n.|闪耀，闪烁；闪光物| shines with many small, bright flashes of reflected light; small, sparkling objects| The diamond glitters in the sunlight. 钻石在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：sparkles, shines, glimmers|反义词：dims, dulls|词性:动词(第三人称单数)

glitters:/ˈɡlɪtərz/| n.|闪光物；金属片|small, sparkling objects; pieces of glittering material|She sprinkled glitters on her painting. 她在她的画上撒了一些闪光粉。|近义词：sequins, sparkles, spangles|反义词：none|词性:名词(复数)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *glitren, frequentative of *gliteren, from Old English *glit(e)nian, from Proto-Germanic *glitinōną, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰleyt- (“to shine, gleam”). Cognate with Scots glitter (“to glitter”), West Frisian glitterje (“to glitter”), Dutch glitteren (“to glitter”), German glitzern (“to glitter”), Swedish glittra (“to glitter”).
