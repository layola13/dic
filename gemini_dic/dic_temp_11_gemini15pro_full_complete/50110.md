denting:/ˈdɛntɪŋ/
noun
凹陷，产生凹痕
the act of making a dent in something
例句：The car suffered some minor denting in the accident. 这辆汽车在事故中有一些轻微的凹痕。

例句：The denting on the refrigerator door was noticeable. 冰箱门上的凹痕很明显。

近义词：indentation, depression, concavity
反义词：protrusion, bulge

verb
(dent的现在分词)
使凹陷，造成凹痕
making a dent in something
例句：The hail was denting the cars parked outside.  冰雹把停在外面的汽车砸出了凹痕。

例句：Dropping the toolbox was denting the wooden floor.  掉落的工具箱把木地板砸出了凹痕。

近义词：indenting, depressing
反义词：protruding, bulging
