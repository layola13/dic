encompassing:/ɪnˈkʌmpəsɪŋ/
adjective
包罗万象的，涵盖的
including everything or having a wide range
The book encompasses the history of the Roman Empire. 这本书涵盖了罗马帝国的历史。
The new law encompasses a wide range of environmental issues. 新法律涵盖了广泛的环境问题。

近义词:comprehensive, inclusive, all-embracing, wide-ranging
反义词:limited, narrow, restricted
词性:adjective
