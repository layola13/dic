facaded:/fəˈsɑːdɪd/
| adj. |有门面的，有虚假的表面的|Having a facade; giving a deceptive outward appearance. |The old building was facaded with modern materials, hiding its true age. 这座老建筑用现代材料装饰了门面，掩盖了它的真实年龄。|The politician presented a facaded image of sincerity, concealing his true intentions. 这位政客展现出一副真诚的表面，掩盖了他的真实意图。|近义词:fake, artificial, superficial, deceptive, misleading|反义词:genuine, authentic, real, true|词性:形容词

词源:
From French façade (“front of a building”), from Italian facciata, from faccia (“face”), from Vulgar Latin *facia, from Latin facies (“face, form, appearance”).
