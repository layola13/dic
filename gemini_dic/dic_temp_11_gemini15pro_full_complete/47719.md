decadency:/ˈdɛkədənsi/ /dɪˈkeɪdənsi/| n. |堕落，衰败；颓废|A state of moral or cultural decline or decay.  A period of decline or decay in a society, art form, etc.|The city was filled with a sense of decadency and despair. 这个城市充满了颓废和绝望的气息。

The decadency of the Roman Empire is a well-studied historical phenomenon.  罗马帝国的衰落是一个被广泛研究的历史现象。|近义词：decline, decay, degeneration, corruption, dissipation|反义词：growth, development, progress, improvement, virtue|词性: noun
