nomadic:/noʊˈmædɪk/ /nəˈmædɪk/| adj.|游牧的；流浪的|Relating to nomads; wandering from place to place.|The nomadic tribes moved their herds to fresh pastures each season.  游牧部落每个季节都把他们的牲畜迁徙到新的牧场。|
Synonyms: wandering, itinerant, roving, migratory, peripatetic|
Antonyms: settled, sedentary, stationary, fixed|
Word origin: From Late Latin *nomadicus, from Greek nomas (genitive nomados) "roaming, wandering, pastoral," literally "one who lets cattle graze," from nemein "to pasture, graze."|
词性: 形容词
