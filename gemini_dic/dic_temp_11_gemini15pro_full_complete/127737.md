negotiant:/nɪˈɡoʊʃiənt/ /nɪˈɡəʊʃiənt/
n.
商人，谈判者，协商者
A person who negotiates, especially in business or commerce; a trader.
例句：The wine negotiant traveled to France to sample the latest vintage.  这位葡萄酒商前往法国品尝最新的年份葡萄酒。

例句：As a skilled negotiant, she secured a favorable deal for her company.  作为一名熟练的谈判者，她为公司争取到了一笔有利的交易。

近义词：trader, merchant, dealer, broker, negotiator
反义词: customer, buyer, client
词性: noun
