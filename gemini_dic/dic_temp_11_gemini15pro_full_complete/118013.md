memorize:/ˈmɛməˌraɪz/ (US), /ˈmɛmərʌɪz/ (UK)| vt.|记忆，背诵|To commit something to memory; to learn something by heart.|I'm trying to memorize this poem. 我正在努力背诵这首诗。|
近义词:learn, remember, commit to memory, internalize|
反义词:forget|
词性:动词


Etymology:
From Late Latin memorizare, from Latin memor "mindful of."
