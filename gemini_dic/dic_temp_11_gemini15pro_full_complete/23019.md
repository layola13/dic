boulder:/ˈboʊldər/ (US) /ˈbəʊldə(r)/ (UK)| n.|巨石；漂石|A large rock, typically one that has been worn smooth by erosion.|例句：The hikers scrambled over the boulders.  徒步旅行者们爬过巨石。

例句：A massive boulder blocked the road. 一块巨大的巨石挡住了道路。

|近义词：rock, stone|反义词:pebble, gravel|词性:名词
