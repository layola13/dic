lopsided:/ˌlɒpˈsaɪdɪd/ /ˌlɑːpˈsaɪdɪd/| adj.|不平衡的，倾向一方的，歪的|Uneven or disproportionately heavy on one side.|例句：The painting hung lopsided on the wall.  这幅画歪挂在墙上。

例句：The election resulted in a lopsided victory for the incumbent.  选举结果是现任者以压倒性优势获胜。

近义词：asymmetrical, unbalanced, uneven, irregular, skewed|反义词：balanced, symmetrical, even, uniform|词性:形容词

lopsidedness:/ˌlɒpˈsaɪdɪdnəs/ /ˌlɑːpˈsaɪdɪdnəs/| n.|不平衡，不对称|The state of being lopsided.|例句：The lopsidedness of the score reflected the difference in skill between the two teams.  比分的不平衡反映了两队之间技术上的差距。

例句：The lopsidedness of the agreement was immediately apparent. 协议的不平衡性显而易见。


近义词：asymmetry, imbalance, unevenness|反义词：balance, symmetry, evenness|词性:名词


词源:  From lop + -sided. Lop meaning "to hang down limply," suggesting something drooping or heavier on one side.
