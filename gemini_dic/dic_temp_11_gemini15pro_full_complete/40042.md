consummate:/ˈkɑːnsəmət/ /ˈkɒnsəmət/| vt. adj.|完成；使完美；圆房|to make (something) complete or perfect; to fulfill|例句：The deal was consummated with a handshake. 这笔交易以握手成交。|例句：Their happiness was consummated with the birth of their first child.  他们的第一个孩子的出生使他们的幸福达到了顶点。|近义词：complete, finalize, perfect, fulfill, achieve|反义词:begin, start, ruin, destroy|词性:动词

consummate:/ˈkɑːnsəmət/ /ˈkɒnsəmət/| adj.|完美的；圆满的；技艺高超的|perfect or complete in every respect|例句：He's a consummate professional. 他是一位技艺高超的专业人士。|例句：She played the piece with consummate skill. 她以完美的技巧演奏了这首曲子。|近义词：perfect, complete, supreme, ultimate, accomplished|反义词:imperfect, incomplete, flawed|词性:形容词
