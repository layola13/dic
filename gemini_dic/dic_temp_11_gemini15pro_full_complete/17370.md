beading:/ˈbiːdɪŋ/| n.|串珠, 珠饰, 珠子细工|Small, decorative balls threaded onto string or wire, or the activity of threading them.|She wore a necklace with intricate beading. 她戴着一条做工复杂的串珠项链。|近义词:jewellery, ornamentation, decoration|反义词:(无直接反义词，可考虑 plainness, simplicity)|词性:noun

beading:/ˈbiːdɪŋ/| n.|(液体表面的)珠状, 水珠|Small drops of liquid forming on a surface.|Beading of condensation formed on the cold window. 冷凝的水珠在冰冷的窗户上形成。|近义词:droplets, beads|反义词:(无直接反义词)|词性:noun
