investors:/ɪnˈvɛstərz/ | n. |投资者|People or organizations that put money into a project or enterprise with the expectation of making a profit.|例句：The investors are expecting a high return on their investment. 投资者期望他们的投资能获得高回报。|例句：Many investors are interested in the new technology company. 许多投资者对这家新科技公司感兴趣。|近义词：stockholders, shareholders, financiers, backers|反义词：borrowers, debtors|词性: 名词
