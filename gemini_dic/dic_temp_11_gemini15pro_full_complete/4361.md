alabaster: /ˈæləˌbæstər/ /ˈæləˌbɑːstər/ | n. adj. |雪花石膏; 雪花石膏制的 |A fine-grained, translucent form of gypsum, often white and used for carving. Also, something made of this material, or having its qualities (smooth, pale).|The museum displayed a collection of alabaster statues.  博物馆展出了一批雪花石膏雕像。| synonym: gypsum, marble, chalk | antonym: granite, obsidian | noun

alabaster: /ˈæləˌbæstər/ /ˈæləˌbɑːstər/ | adj. |光滑的; 苍白的; 雪白的 | Smooth and white like alabaster. | Her skin was alabaster and flawless. 她的皮肤像雪花石膏一样光滑无瑕。| synonym: pale, white, smooth | antonym: dark, rough, tanned | adjective
