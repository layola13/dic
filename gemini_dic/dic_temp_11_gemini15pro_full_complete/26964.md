calcifies:/ˈkælsɪfaɪz/
verb
钙化
To harden by depositing calcium salts.  The process by which tissue becomes hardened by the deposition of calcium salts.
例句：Over time, the cartilage calcifies and becomes bone. 随着时间的推移，软骨会钙化并变成骨骼。

例句：The arterial plaque calcifies, making the arteries less flexible.  动脉斑块钙化，使动脉弹性降低。
近义词：harden, ossify, petrify
反义词：soften, dissolve
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *calcificāre, from calc-, calx (“lime, limestone”) + -ficāre,  -fy.
