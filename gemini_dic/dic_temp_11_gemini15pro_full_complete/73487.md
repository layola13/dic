forers:/ˈfɔːrərz/
n.
先行者，先驱者
those who come or act before others, precursors
The forers of the internet age could not have imagined the impact it would have on society.  互联网时代的先行者们无法想象它对社会的影响。

近义词: pioneers, predecessors, precursors, vanguards
反义词: followers, successors, laggards

词性: noun (plural)

词源: "forer" + "-s" (plural suffix). "Forer" comes from the Middle English "forere," from Old English "fore," meaning "before."
