nondiscrimination:/ˌnɒndɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 不歧视，无歧视 | The absence of discrimination; the practice of treating all people equally, regardless of their race, sex, religion, etc.  |  The company has a strict policy of nondiscrimination in hiring and promotion.  这家公司在招聘和晋升方面有严格的非歧视政策。 |  equality, fairness, impartiality | discrimination, prejudice, bias | noun
