liberalize:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪz/| v. |使自由化，放宽限制|To make something less strict or controlled, allowing more freedom.|The government decided to liberalize the trade laws to boost the economy. 政府决定放宽贸易法以促进经济发展.| synonyms: loosen, relax, deregulate, ease, open up | antonyms: restrict, tighten, regulate, control|verb
liberalization:/ˌlɪbərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |自由化，放宽限制|The act or process of making something less strict or controlled.|The liberalization of trade policies has led to increased global competition. 贸易政策的自由化导致全球竞争加剧.| synonyms: deregulation, relaxation, easing | antonyms: restriction, tightening, regulation|noun
