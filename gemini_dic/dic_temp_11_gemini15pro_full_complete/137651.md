nonunderstanding:/ˌnɒnʌndəˈstændɪŋ/ | adj. | 不理解的，缺乏理解的 | Lacking understanding; not understanding. | He had a nonunderstanding look on his face when I explained the complex theory. 当我解释这个复杂的理论时，他脸上露出了一副不理解的表情。|  She displayed a nonunderstanding attitude towards the cultural differences. 她对文化差异表现出不理解的态度。 | 近义词: uncomprehending, insensitive, oblivious | 反义词: understanding, comprehending, empathetic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "understanding" (from "understand," from Old English "understandan" - to grasp the meaning of).
