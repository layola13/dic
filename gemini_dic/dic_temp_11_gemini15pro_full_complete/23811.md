brazier:/ˈbreɪʒər/| n.|火盆，炭盆|A metal container that holds burning coals, used for heating or cooking.|The blacksmith used a brazier to heat the metal. 铁匠用火盆加热金属。|The family huddled around the brazier for warmth. 全家围坐在火盆旁取暖。|近义词:fire pit, barbeque, grill|反义词:|noun


brazier:/ˈbreɪʒər/| n.|胸罩|A woman's undergarment that supports the breasts.|She bought a new brazier. 她买了一件新胸罩。 |The brazier was too tight. 胸罩太紧了。|近义词:bra|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *brasier*, from *braise* "burning coals," from Frankish *brasa*, from Proto-Germanic *brasō* "fire, burning."  (For the "bra" meaning, it's a shortened form of "brassiere.")
