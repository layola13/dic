jargonesque:/ˌdʒɑːrɡəˈnɛsk/ | adj. | 行话式的；术语化的 | Resembling or characteristic of jargon; technical terminology that is difficult for the layperson to understand. |  Her jargonesque language made it difficult for anyone outside the field to understand her presentation. 她的行话式语言让该领域之外的人很难理解她的演讲。|  Example: The lawyer's jargonesque explanation left the client confused.  律师的行话式解释让客户感到困惑。| Synonyms: technical, specialized, esoteric, obscure | Antonyms: simple, clear, straightforward, plain | adjective
