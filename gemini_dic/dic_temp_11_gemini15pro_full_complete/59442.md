earthward:/ˈɜːθwərd/ /ˈɝːθwɚd/| adv. adj.|向地球；朝向地面|towards the earth; in the direction of the earth|The spacecraft began its earthward descent.  宇宙飞船开始向地球下降。|
The apple fell earthward from the tree. 苹果从树上掉落到地面。|
Synonyms: down, downwards, groundward|
Antonyms: upward, skyward|
Adverb


earthward:/ˈɜːθwərd/ /ˈɝːθwɚd/| adj.|向地球的；朝向地面的|directed or moving towards the earth|The earthward slope of the mountain was steep. 山面向地球一侧的斜坡很陡。|
The earthward pull of gravity kept us grounded. 地球引力使我们保持在地面上。|
Synonyms: descending, falling|
Antonyms: ascending, rising|
