communalizes:/kəˈmjuːnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使公有化；使共有；使集体化
To make something communal; to transfer something to communal ownership or control; to give something the qualities of communal life.
The government communalized the farmland. 政府将农田公有化了。
The new housing development communalized the swimming pool and playground. 新的住宅区将游泳池和游乐场公有化了。
Synonyms: collectivize, socialize, share
Antonyms: privatize, individualize
词性:v.
