beagle:/ˈbiːɡ(ə)l/| n.|小猎犬，比格犬|A small hound with short legs and long ears, bred for hunting hares and rabbits.|例句：The beagle sniffed the ground, following the scent of the rabbit. 小猎犬嗅着地面，追踪兔子的气味。

例句：My beagle loves to chase squirrels in the park. 我的小猎犬喜欢在公园里追逐松鼠。|近义词：hound, dog|反义词:cat (并非严格意义上的反义词，仅作为不同种类的动物)|词性:noun
