campaigned:/kæmˈpeɪnd/
verb
参加竞选，开展活动
engaged in a campaign
例句：They campaigned vigorously for their candidate.  他们积极地为他们的候选人助选。

例句：She campaigned against the proposed new law. 她反对提议的新法律，并为此开展了活动。

近义词：advocated, canvassed, lobbied, promoted, pushed
反义词：opposed

词性:v.


词源：From Old French *campaigner (attested in the form campaignier "live in the fields, be in a military camp"), ultimately from Late Latin campania "open country," from Latin campus "plain, field of battle."
