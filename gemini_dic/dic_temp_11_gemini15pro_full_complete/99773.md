intermediation:/ˌɪntərˌmiːdiˈeɪʃn/| n.|中间人服务，中介，调解|The act of mediating between two parties or groups; the process of intervening between two or more parties to settle a dispute or facilitate an agreement.|The bank acted as an intermediation in the merger.|Synonyms: mediation, intervention, arbitration, negotiation|Antonyms: non-intervention, inaction|noun
