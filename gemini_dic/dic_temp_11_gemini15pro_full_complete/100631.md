intitule:/ɛ̃.ti.ty.le/ (French), /ɪnˈtɪtjuːl/ or /ɪnˈtaɪtl/ (English)| n. | 标题，名称，题目 |  A title or heading, especially of a legal document, musical composition, or work of art.  Derived from French "intituler" (to entitle), from Latin "intitulare" (to give a title to).| The intitule of the painting is "Starry Night". 这幅画的标题是“星夜”。 | synonym: title, heading, caption | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "body" or "content" in contrast to "title") | noun
