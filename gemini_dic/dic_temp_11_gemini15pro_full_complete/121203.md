miseased:/ˌmɪsˈiːzd/
adjective
患病的，不健康的
Affected by disease; ill.
The miseased animal was quarantined. 这只患病的动物被隔离了。
近义词：sick, ill, diseased, ailing
反义词：healthy, well
词性：adjective
词源：From mis- (badly) + eased (comfortable, at ease).  Ultimately from Old French aise.
