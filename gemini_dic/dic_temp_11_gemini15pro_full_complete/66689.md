exogenously:/ɛkˈsɑːdʒɪnəsli/
adverb
外生地，外源性地
Originating from outside a system; externally caused.
The price of oil is determined exogenously by global markets. 石油价格是由全球市场外生地决定的。
The variable was introduced exogenously into the model.  这个变量是从外部引入到模型中的。
近义词:externally, outwardly
反义词:endogenously, internally
词性:adverb

词源:
From exo- (outside) + -genous (producing or originating) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Greek exo (outside) and genos (birth, race, kind).
