monetarian: /ˌmʌnɪˈtɛəriən/ | adj. n. | 货币的；货币主义的；货币主义者 | Relating to money or the monetary system; a believer in monetarism (the theory that the supply of money is the chief determinant of economic activity). |  Monetarian policies often focus on controlling inflation. 货币主义政策通常侧重于控制通货膨胀。 | The monetarian argued that controlling the money supply was key to economic stability.  这位货币主义者认为，控制货币供应是经济稳定的关键。| 近义词: monetary, fiscal (related to finance) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps) Keynesian (representing a different economic theory) | 词性: adjective, noun
