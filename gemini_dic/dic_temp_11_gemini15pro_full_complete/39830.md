conspiratorially:/kənˌspɪrəˈtɔːriəli/
adverb
阴谋地，秘密地
in a way that suggests a secret plan or agreement
The two men exchanged glances conspiratorially.  这两个男人交换了一下眼神，像是密谋什么。
They whispered conspiratorially in the corner. 他们在角落里低声密语，像是在密谋。
Synonyms:secretly, furtively, clandestinely, privately, covertly
Antonyms:openly, publicly, frankly


Etymology:
From Late Latin conspiratorius + -ly.  From conspirare "to agree together, combine, plot," from com- "together" (see com-) + spirare "to breathe" (see spirit (n.)).
