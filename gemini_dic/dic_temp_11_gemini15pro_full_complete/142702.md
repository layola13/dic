others:/ˈʌðərz/| pronoun|其他的人或物|People or things that are not the one or ones already mentioned or known about.|Some people like coffee, others prefer tea.  有些人喜欢咖啡，其他人喜欢茶。|synonyms: the rest, the remainder|antonyms: these, those|pronoun

others:/ˈʌðərz/| noun|其他人；其他物|People or things other than those already mentioned.|She cares more about others than herself. 她关心别人胜过关心自己。|synonyms: the rest, the remainder|antonyms: these, those|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ōðre (plural), from Proto-Germanic *anþeraz. Cognate with Old Saxon ōðre, Old Frisian āthere, Old High German andere, Old Norse aðrir, Gothic anþarai.
