cadency:/ˈkeɪdnsɪ/ (US), /ˈkeɪdnsi/ (UK)| n.|节奏; 韵律; 抑扬顿挫|A rhythmic flow of sounds or words.  The rise and fall of the voice in speaking.|The poet's cadency was soothing and hypnotic.  这位诗人的抑扬顿挫令人舒缓而催眠。|Synonyms: rhythm, flow, lilt, inflection, intonation|Antonyms: monotony, droning|noun
