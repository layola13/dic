dulcetly:/ˈdʌlsɪtli/| adv. |甜地；悦耳地；温柔地|In a sweet and soothing manner; melodiously.|She sang dulcetly, her voice a gentle caress to the ears. 她唱得很甜美，她的声音温柔地抚慰着耳朵。|例句：The birds sang dulcetly in the morning. 清晨鸟儿婉转地歌唱。|近义词：sweetly, melodiously, soothingly, gently|反义词：harshly, discordantly, stridently|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *dulciter, from Latin dulcis (“sweet”).
