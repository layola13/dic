|n.|授予贵族称号；使高贵|The act of ennobling someone or something; the quality of being ennobled. |His ennoblement to the peerage was a great honor. 他被授予贵族爵位是一项极大的荣誉。|Synonyms: elevation, exaltation, dignifying|Antonyms: degradation, debasement|noun


ennoblement:/ɪˈnoʊblmənt/
|n.|使高尚；使崇高|The act of making something or someone noble or of higher status. |The queen bestowed an ennoblement upon the brave knight. 女王将贵族称号授予这位勇敢的骑士。|Synonyms:  uplifting, refinement|Antonyms: corruption, degradation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ennoblement*, from ennoblir “to ennoble,”  from en- “in, on” + noble “noble.” Ultimately from Latin nobilis "noble."
