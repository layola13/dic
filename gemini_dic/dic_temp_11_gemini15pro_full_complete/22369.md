booby's:/ˈbuːbiz/| n.|笨蛋的；傻瓜的；呆子的；鲣鸟的|Something that belongs to a booby (a foolish person or a type of seabird).  Refers to the possessive form of "booby."|例句：Don't listen to him; that's just booby's talk.  别听他的，那只是傻瓜的胡言乱语。

例句：The booby's nest was precariously perched on the cliff edge. 鲣鸟的巢危险地坐落在悬崖边缘。|近义词：fool's, idiot's, dunce's (for person); gannet's (for bird)|反义词:genius's, sage's|词性:Possessive noun
