leptynite:/ˈlɛptɪnaɪt/| n. |细粒麻粒岩|A fine-grained metamorphic rock consisting mainly of quartz and feldspar, typically formed by the metamorphism of sedimentary rocks.|The geologist identified the sample as leptynite due to its quartz and feldspar composition. 地质学家根据其石英和长石成分将样品鉴定为细粒麻粒岩。|Synonym: granulite|Antonym: N/A|noun
