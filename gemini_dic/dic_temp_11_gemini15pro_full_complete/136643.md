nonschooling:/ˈnɒnˌskuːlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌskuːlɪŋ/ (UK)
n.|非学校教育|The practice of educating children outside of the traditional school system, often at home, focusing on self-directed learning and real-world experiences.|Many families are choosing nonschooling as an alternative to traditional education. 许多家庭选择非学校教育作为传统教育的替代方案。|
近义词:homeschooling, unschooling, self-directed learning
反义词:schooling, traditional education
词性:名词
