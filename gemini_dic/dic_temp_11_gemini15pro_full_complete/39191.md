congenially:/kənˈdʒiːniəli/ | adv. | 融洽地，友好地，合得来地 | In a pleasant, friendly, and agreeable manner; agreeably. | We chatted congenially about old times. 我们愉快地聊起了往事。|  The host and hostess entertained their guests congenially. 主人和女主人热情友好地招待了他们的客人。 | 近义词: agreeably, amiably, pleasantly, genially, affably | 反义词: unpleasantly, disagreeably, inhospitably | 词性: 副词

congenial:/kənˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. | 意气相投的，合得来的；适宜的，宜人的 | (of a person) pleasant and friendly; (of a thing) pleasant or agreeable because suited to one's taste or inclination. |  She found him to be a congenial companion. 她发现他是一个意气相投的伴侣。| The department provides a congenial atmosphere for research.  这个系提供了一个适宜研究的氛围。 | 近义词: agreeable, pleasant, suitable, compatible, kindred | 反义词: uncongenial, disagreeable, unsuitable, incompatible | 词性: 形容词
