buzzardet: /ˌbʌzɑːrˈdɛt/ | n. |  秃鹰雏鸟；毛脚鵟雏鸟 | A young buzzard, especially one still in the nest. | The buzzardet was still too young to fly.  这只秃鹰雏鸟还太小，不能飞翔。| 近义词: chick, fledgling | 反义词: adult (buzzard) | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Middle English *busardet, from Old French busaret, diminutive of busard (“buzzard”).
