durational:/djʊˈreɪʃənl/ |adj.|持续的，持续期间的|Relating to or concerned with duration.  It describes something that lasts for a particular length of time.|The durational aspect of the music was emphasized by the long, sustained notes. 这段音乐的持续性方面通过长而持续的音符得到了强调。|近义词：lasting, enduring, prolonged, continuous|反义词：brief, short, momentary|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *dūrātiō*  "a hardening, continuance," from *dūrāre* "to harden, last, endure," from *dūrus* "hard."  The "-al" suffix turns it into an adjective.
