believe:/bɪˈliːv/ (US) /bɪˈliːv/ (UK)| v.|相信，认为；信仰|To accept something as true or real; to have faith in something or someone.|I believe you. 我相信你。|synonyms: trust, accept, credit, have faith in|antonyms: doubt, disbelieve, distrust|verb

believe:/bɪˈliːv/ (US) /bɪˈliːv/ (UK)| n.|相信，信仰；信念|An acceptance that something is true or real; a firmly held opinion or conviction.|His belief in God is unshakeable. 他对上帝的信仰坚定不移。|synonyms: faith, trust, conviction|antonyms: doubt, disbelief, skepticism|noun
