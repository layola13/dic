foralite: /ˈfɔːrəlaɪt/
noun
镁橄榄石
An orthorhombic mineral consisting of magnesium iron silicate, (Mg,Fe)2SiO4, occurring widely in igneous and metamorphic rocks and in meteorites.
Foralite is a common component of mafic and ultramafic igneous rocks. 镁橄榄石是镁铁质和超镁铁质火成岩的常见成分。
Foralite is often found in meteorites. 镁橄榄石经常在陨石中发现。
Synonyms: olivine, peridot
Antonyms: None (Foralite is a specific mineral, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
