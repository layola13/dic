hoisting:/ˈhɔɪstɪŋ/| n.|变量提升|In JavaScript, hoisting is the process whereby the interpreter appears to move the declaration of functions, variables or classes to the top of their scope, prior to execution of the code.|The variable `x` is hoisted in this example: `console.log(x); var x = 5;` |近义词：lifting, raising|反义词:lowering|词性:noun
