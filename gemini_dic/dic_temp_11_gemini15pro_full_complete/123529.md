monotremata:/ˈmɒnəˌtriːməˌtə/ | n. | 单孔目动物 | An order of primitive egg-laying mammals comprising the echidnas and the platypus, characterized by a single cloacal opening for the urogenital and digestive systems. |  The platypus and the echidnas are the only surviving members of the Monotremata.  鸭嘴兽和针鼹是单孔目仅存的成员。 | synonyms: Prototheria | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic order) | noun
