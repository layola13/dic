autochange:/ˈɔːtəʊˌtʃeɪndʒ/ | n. | 自动更改，自动切换 |  The process of something changing automatically, often due to a pre-set condition or trigger. | The autochange feature on my phone switches to data when the Wi-Fi signal is weak. 我的手机上的自动切换功能会在 Wi-Fi 信号弱时切换到数据网络。 | synonyms: automatic change, automatic switch, automatic transition, autoswitch | antonyms: manual change, manual switch, manual transition | noun


/ˌɔːtəʊˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 自动更改的，自动切换的 |  Describing something that changes automatically. | The autochanging headlights adjust to the brightness of the surroundings. 自动切换的大灯会根据周围环境的亮度进行调整。 | synonyms: self-adjusting, self-regulating, adaptive | antonyms: manual, non-automatic | adjective
