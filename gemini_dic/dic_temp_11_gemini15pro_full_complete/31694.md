chauvinisms: /ˈʃoʊvɪnɪzəmz/
n.|沙文主义，盲目爱国主义| Excessive or prejudiced loyalty or devotion to one's own group, cause, gender, or nation. | Example:  His chauvinisms about his alma mater are well known.  他对母校的盲目热爱是出了名的。| Example:  Cultural chauvinisms can lead to misunderstandings and conflict. 文化沙文主义会导致误解和冲突。 |Synonyms:  bigotry, prejudice, fanaticism, jingoism | Antonyms:  tolerance, open-mindedness, cosmopolitanism | Noun
