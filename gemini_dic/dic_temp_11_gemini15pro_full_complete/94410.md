implicative:/ɪmˈplɪkətɪv/| adj.|蕴含的，含蓄的，必然导致的|Suggesting something indirectly; carrying or involving an implication. |Her silence was implicative of her guilt. 她的沉默暗示了她的罪行。|近义词：suggestive, connotative, inferential|反义词：explicit, direct|词性:形容词

Etymology: From the Late Latin *implicātīvus*, from Latin implicare "to involve, entangle," from in- "in" + plicare "to fold."
