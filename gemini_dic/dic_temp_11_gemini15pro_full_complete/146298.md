overfavoring: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪvərɪŋ/
|v.|过分偏爱，过度偏袒|Showing excessive favoritism towards someone or something.|The coach was accused of overfavoring his star player. 教练被指责过分偏袒他的明星球员。|例句：The policy overfavors large corporations. 这项政策过度偏袒大公司。|近义词：偏袒 favoring, showing partiality, prioritizing, biasing|反义词：公平对待 treating fairly, being impartial, neglecting|词性:动词
