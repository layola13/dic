nonpliabilities:/ˌnɒnplaɪəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 无责任，无义务，无债务 |  Absence of liabilities; things for which one is not liable or responsible.  This word is less commonly used than "assets" or "liabilities" individually, and usually appears in legal or financial contexts when specifically discussing the absence of obligations. | The company's nonpliabilities were clearly outlined in the contract.  该公司在合同中明确列出了其无责任义务。|  The lawyer emphasized the nonpliabilities of his client in the case.  律师强调了他的客户在本案中没有责任。 | Near Synonyms:  exemptions, immunities | Antonyms: liabilities, obligations | noun
