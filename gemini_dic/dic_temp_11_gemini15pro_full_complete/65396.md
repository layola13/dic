euphemistical: /ˌjuːfəˈmɪstɪk(ə)l/ | adj. |委婉的| Relating to or expressed in euphemism; a mild or indirect word or expression substituted for one considered to be too harsh or blunt when referring to something unpleasant or embarrassing. |  She used a euphemistical phrase to describe his death, saying he had "passed away." 她用委婉的措辞来描述他的死亡，说他“去世了”。 |  She gave a euphemistical account of the incident to avoid upsetting her mother. 为了避免让她母亲难过，她对这件事的描述很委婉。|  indirect, polite, understated, delicate, softened |  blunt, direct, frank, harsh, indelicate | adjective
