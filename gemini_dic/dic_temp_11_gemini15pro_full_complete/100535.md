intervolve:/ˌɪntərˈvɒlv/ |v.|互相缠绕，相互关联|To be involved or entwined with each other; to mutually involve or engage.|The destinies of the two families were intervolved. 这两个家族的命运相互纠缠。|近义词：intertwine, interweave, interlace, connect, relate|反义词：separate, disconnect, disengage|词性:动词

词源:
From Latin *intervolvere, from inter- (“between”) + volvere (“to roll”).
