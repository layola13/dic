overseriousnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈsɪəriəsnəsɪz/
noun
过度严肃；过分认真
The state of being excessively serious.
His overseriousnesses made him seem unfriendly. 他过分的严肃让他看起来很不友好。
Her overseriousnesses about the rules alienated her colleagues. 她对规则的过分认真疏远了她的同事。
近义词: solemnity, gravity, seriousness
反义词: lightheartedness, frivolity, levity
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
From over- + seriousness + -es (plural suffix).  "Over-" is a prefix indicating "too much" or "excessive". "Seriousness" is derived from "serious," which ultimately comes from the Latin word "serius" meaning "grave" or "weighty."  The "-es" ending marks the plural form.
