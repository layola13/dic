instigatrix: /ˈɪnstɪˌɡeɪtrɪks/
noun
女煽动者，女教唆犯
A woman who instigates or incites to action, especially to something evil.
例句：The instigatrix of the riot was never identified.  这场骚乱的煽动者从未被确认。

例句：She was the instigatrix of the whole affair. 她就是整件事的始作俑者。

近义词：agitator, inciter, provocateur, firebrand
反义词：peacemaker, mediator, conciliator
词性：noun
