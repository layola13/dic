criosphinx:/ˈkraɪoʊsfɪŋks/| n.|冰狮身人面像|A mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head of a ram, often depicted in ice sculptures or associated with cold, icy environments.  It's a blend of "cryo-" (relating to cold) and "sphinx".|例句：The criosphinx guarded the entrance to the ice palace. 冰狮身人面像守卫着冰宫的入口。

例句：Legends speak of a criosphinx that roams the frozen wastes. 传说有一只冰狮身人面像在冰冻荒原上游荡。|近义词：ice sculpture, mythical creature|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fire creature")|词性:noun
