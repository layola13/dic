imide:/ˈɪmaɪd/ | n. |酰亚胺| A nitrogenous organic compound containing the bivalent radical >C=NH<, derived from ammonia or a primary amine by replacement of two hydrogen atoms by a bivalent nonacidic organic radical. | Phthalimide is an important chemical intermediate. 邻苯二甲酰亚胺是一种重要的化工中间体。 | synonym:  dicarboximide | antonym: N/A | noun
