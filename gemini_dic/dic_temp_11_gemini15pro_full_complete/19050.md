bethlemite:/ˈbɛθləˌmaɪt/| adj.|精神错乱的；疯狂的|Insane; mad.|His bethlemite ravings disturbed the neighbors.|Synonyms: insane, mad, lunatic, demented|Antonyms: sane, rational|adjective
