gharara:/ɡəˈrɑːrə/
n.
traditional South Asian women's garment; a wide, flared, pleated pair of trousers
A gharara is a type of traditional women's clothing worn in the Indian subcontinent, particularly in the Awadh region of India and Pakistan. It is a pair of wide-legged trousers that are very full and flared, often featuring elaborate pleats and embroidery.
She wore a beautiful gharara to the wedding. 她穿着一件漂亮的裙裤去参加婚礼。
The gharara swirled around her as she danced.  当她跳舞时，裙裤在她周围旋转。
synonyms: sharara, lehenga, skirt
antonyms: trousers, pants
noun
