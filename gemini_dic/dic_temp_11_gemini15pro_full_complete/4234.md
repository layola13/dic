airlock:/ˈɛərlɑːk/ (US), /ˈeəˌlɒk/ (UK)| n.|气闸；气密室|An airtight chamber, typically between two areas of differing pressure, that allows passage between them without significant loss of pressure.|例句：The astronauts cycled through the airlock before stepping onto the lunar surface. 宇航员在踏上月球表面之前先通过了气闸。

例句：The hospital's operating room is accessed through an airlock to maintain sterility. 医院的手术室通过气闸进入以保持无菌状态。
