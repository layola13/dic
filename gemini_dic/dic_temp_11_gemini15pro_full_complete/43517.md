craving:/ˈkreɪvɪŋ/| n.|渴望，热望|A powerful desire for something.|I have a craving for chocolate. 我很想吃巧克力。|Synonyms: desire, yearning, longing, hunger|Antonyms: aversion, dislike, indifference|noun

crave:/kreɪv/| vt.|渴望，热望|To have a strong desire for something.|I crave a quiet evening at home. 我渴望在家享受一个安静的夜晚。|Synonyms: desire, yearn for, long for|Antonyms: dislike, hate, abhor|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *crafian (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *krabōną (“to ask, demand”), from Proto-Indo-European *grebʰ- (“to seize, take, grab”). Cognate with Old Norse krefja (“to demand”).
