cross-contaminate:/ˌkrɒs kənˈtæmɪneɪt/ /ˌkrɑːs kənˈtæmɪneɪt/ | v. | 交叉污染 | To unintentionally transfer bacteria or other harmful substances from one surface or object to another, especially food. |  Raw meat can cross-contaminate cooked food if they are stored together. 生肉如果和熟食放在一起，会交叉污染熟食。| synonyms: contaminate, pollute, infect, taint | antonyms: sterilize, sanitize, purify | verb

cross-contamination:/ˌkrɒs kənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃən/ /ˌkrɑːs kənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 交叉污染 | The process by which bacteria or other microorganisms are unintentionally transferred from one substance or object to another, with harmful effect. |  Proper food handling can prevent cross-contamination. 正确处理食物可以防止交叉污染。| synonyms: contamination, pollution, infection | antonyms: sterilization, sanitization, purification | noun
