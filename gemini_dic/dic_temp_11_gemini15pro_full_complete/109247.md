leitmotif:/ˈlaɪtmoʊˌtiːf/ /ˈlaɪtməʊtiːf/| n.|主导动机；主题；中心思想|A recurrent theme throughout a musical or literary composition, associated with a particular person, idea, or situation.|The leitmotif of the opera is love and loss. 这部歌剧的主题是爱与失去。|Synonyms: theme, motif, subject, topic, idea, concept|Antonyms: variation, digression|noun
