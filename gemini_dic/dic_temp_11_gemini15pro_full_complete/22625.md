bop:/bɑːp/| n. v.|敲击，轻打；流行音乐|A light blow.  To hit lightly. A type of jazz music, especially popular in the 1940s and 1950s.|He gave her a playful bop on the nose. 他开玩笑地轻拍了一下她的鼻子。|She bopped him on the head with a newspaper. 她用报纸敲了一下他的头。|That new song is a real bop! 那首新歌真不错！|近义词：hit, tap, strike, jazz, tune|反义词：(For the verb) caress, stroke (For the noun - music genre) classical music|词性：名词，动词
