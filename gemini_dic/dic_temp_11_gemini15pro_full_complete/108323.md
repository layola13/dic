laureate: /ˈlɔːriət/ | n. adj. |桂冠诗人，获奖者；佩戴桂冠的|A person honored for achievement in an art or science, especially one awarded a prize or medal for writing.  Also means crowned with laurel.|The Nobel laureate gave a moving speech. 诺贝尔奖获得者发表了感人的演讲。|Synonyms: victor, champion, winner, prizewinner|Antonyms: loser, failure |noun, adjective
