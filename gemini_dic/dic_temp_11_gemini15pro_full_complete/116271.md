massasauga:/ˌmæsəˈsɔːɡə/ | n. |响尾蛇（Massasauga rattlesnake）|A small, venomous rattlesnake (Sistrurus catenatus) found in the eastern and midwestern United States and adjacent areas of Canada. |例句：The massasauga is a shy snake and will usually only bite if provoked.  响尾蛇是一种害羞的蛇，通常只有在被激怒时才会咬人。

|例句：Massasaugas prefer wet habitats like marshes and swamps. 响尾蛇喜欢沼泽和湿地等潮湿的栖息地。|近义词：rattlesnake, viper|反义词: N/A (No direct opposite for a specific snake species) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ojibwe(Ojibwa) *maza'saga' , meaning "great river mouth" or "grassy opening."  This likely refers to the snake's habitat.
