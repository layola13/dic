astraddle:/əˈstræd(ə)l/
adjective
跨着，骑着，横跨
With legs wide apart and balanced on either side of something.
例句：She sat astraddle the motorcycle. 她跨坐在摩托车上。

例句：The town is astraddle the border.  这个城镇横跨边界。
近义词：across, astride, over, spanning, straddling
反义词：alongside, beside


adverb
跨着，骑着，横跨
In a position with legs wide apart on either side of something.
例句：He sat astraddle on the fence. 他跨坐在篱笆上。

例句：A small bridge lay astraddle the stream. 一座小桥横跨在小溪上。
近义词：across, astride, over, spanning, straddling
反义词：alongside, beside


词性：adjective, adverb
