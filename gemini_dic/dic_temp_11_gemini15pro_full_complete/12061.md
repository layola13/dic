artifactual:/ˌɑːrtɪˈfæktʃuəl/ | adj. | 人工的；人为造成的；因人为因素产生的 |  Relating to or caused by an artifact, especially in scientific or technical contexts.  This often means something is a result of the process used and not a reflection of the real situation. | The observed increase in temperature was artifactual, due to a faulty sensor.  观察到的温度升高是人为造成的，原因是传感器故障。 | The blurry image was artifactual, a result of the compression algorithm used. 模糊的图像是人为造成的，是所使用的压缩算法的结果。 |  artificial, spurious, factitious | genuine, authentic, real | adjective
