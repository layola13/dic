downshifted:/ˈdaʊnˌʃɪftɪd/| adj.|降档的，放慢生活节奏的|Having changed to a lower gear; reduced in scope or intensity; simplified; adopted a less stressful or demanding lifestyle. |例句：They downshifted to a simpler life in the countryside. 他们放慢生活节奏，搬到乡下过上了简朴的生活。

例句：He downshifted his career to spend more time with his family.  他为了有更多时间陪伴家人而降低了职业要求。

近义词：scaled back, simplified, decelerated|反义词:upshifted, accelerated|词性:形容词
