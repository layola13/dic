dowager:/ˈdaʊədʒər/| n.|寡妇，遗孀（尤指拥有亡夫财产或头衔的）；贵妇人|A woman who holds some title or property from her deceased husband, especially the widow of a king, duke, etc.  A dignified elderly woman.|The dowager queen still held considerable influence.  太后仍然拥有相当大的影响力。|近义词：widow, matron|反义词: bachelor (applicable only in the sense of an unmarried person)|noun
