alloquy:/ˈæləkwɪ/
n.
正式的讲话；演说；谈话
a formal speech, address, or conversation
The president delivered a stirring alloquy to the nation. 总统向全国发表了激动人心的演说。
近义词：address, speech, discourse, oration
反义词：silence, quiet
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *alloquium, from Latin alloquī (“to speak to”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + loquī (“to speak”).
