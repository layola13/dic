ethnologies:/ˌɛθˈnɒlədʒiz/
n.
民族志学；人种学
The scientific study of the origin, distribution, and characteristics of human racial groups.  The study of the characteristics of various peoples and the differences and relationships between them.
例句：He is specializing in ethnologies at the university. 他在大学专攻民族学。

例句：Her research in ethnologies took her to remote villages in Africa. 她的民族学研究把她带到了非洲偏远的村庄。

近义词：anthropology, ethnography
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
