insanitary:/ɪnˈsænɪteri/ | adj. |不卫生的，不健康的 | Not sanitary; unclean; likely to cause disease. | The insanitary conditions in the refugee camp led to an outbreak of cholera.  难民营不卫生的条件导致了霍乱的爆发。| 近义词: unhygienic, unhealthy, contaminated, polluted | 反义词: sanitary, hygienic, clean, sterile | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + sanitary (relating to health or hygiene).  "Sanitary" ultimately comes from the Latin word "sanitas" meaning "health."
