greasepaint:/ˈɡriːspeɪnt/ /ˈɡriːsˌpeɪnt/| n. |油彩；化妆油|A thick oily makeup used by actors, especially on stage. |例句：The actors applied greasepaint to their faces before the performance. 演员们在演出前往脸上涂抹油彩。

例句：He removed the greasepaint with cold cream after the show. 演出结束后，他用冷霜卸掉了油彩。|近义词：makeup, stage makeup|反义词: None|词性:noun


greasepaints:/ˈɡriːspeɪnts/ /ˈɡriːsˌpeɪnts/| n. |油彩（复数）|The plural form of greasepaint. |例句：The greasepaints were brightly colored and designed for stage lighting. 这些油彩颜色鲜艳，是为舞台灯光设计的。

例句：She carefully organized her greasepaints in a dedicated makeup box. 她小心翼翼地把她油彩在一个专门的化妆盒里整理好。|近义词：makeups, stage makeups|反义词: None|词性:noun


词源:  From "grease" + "paint."  Refers to the oily or greasy texture of the makeup.
