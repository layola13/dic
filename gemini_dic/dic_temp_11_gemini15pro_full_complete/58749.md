dunghill:/ˈdʌŋhɪl/| n.|粪堆，污秽的地方，卑贱的地位|A pile of dung; a manure heap. Figuratively, a place of degradation or moral filth.  |The king was reduced from his throne to a dunghill. 国王从王位沦落到卑贱的地位。|近义词：muckheap, manure heap, midden, compost heap|反义词:palace, throne|词性:名词
