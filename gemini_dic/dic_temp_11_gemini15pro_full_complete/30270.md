cembalo:/ˈtʃɛmbəloʊ/ (US), /ˈtʃɛmbəloʊ/ (UK)| n. |大键琴|A keyboard instrument resembling a grand piano but having strings that are plucked instead of struck by hammers.|He played a beautiful piece on the cembalo. 他在大键琴上演奏了一首优美的曲子。|Synonym: harpsichord|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps piano as a contrasting instrument)|noun
