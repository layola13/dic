chomped:/tʃɒmpt/ (UK) /tʃɑːmpt/ (US)| adj. | (非正式)被咬过的；被嚼过的 | Having been bitten or chewed. | The chomped end of the carrot looked ragged. 胡萝卜被咬过的那一头看起来很粗糙。| synonyms: bitten, chewed, gnawed | antonyms: whole, untouched | adjective
