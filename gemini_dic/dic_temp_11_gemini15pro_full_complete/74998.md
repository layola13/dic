friedest:/ˈfriːdəst/| adj.|最平静的，最宁静的|This is the superlative form of "fried" (peaceful) which isn't a standard English word. It likely comes from German "friedlich" (peaceful) with the superlative suffix "-est".  It means "most peaceful" or "most tranquil".  It's not common in standard English.|*Example (constructed as it's not standard English):  The friedest place I know is the mountaintop. 我知道的最平静的地方是山顶。 *Example (constructed as it's not standard English): Of all the lakes, this one is the friedest. 在所有的湖泊中，这个是最宁静的。|近义词：most peaceful, most tranquil, calmest, serenest|反义词：most turbulent, most chaotic, most restless|词性:形容词
