impracticalnesses: /ɪmˈpræktɪkəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 不切实际之处；不实用性 | The impracticalnesses of the plan soon became apparent. 该计划的不切实际之处很快就显现出来了。|  The many impracticalnesses of the proposal led to its rejection.  这个提案的诸多不切实际之处导致它被否决。 | impracticality, unfeasibility | practicality, feasibility | noun


Etymology:
From impractical + -ness.  Impractical comes from in- (not) + practical. "Practical" traces back to Late Latin practicus "active, practical," from Greek praktikos "fit for action," from prassein "to do, act, perform."
