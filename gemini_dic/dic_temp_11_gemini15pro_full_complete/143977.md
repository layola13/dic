outrager:/ˈaʊtˌreɪdʒər/| n.|暴徒，施暴者，令人愤慨的人或事|A person who commits an outrage; someone who grossly insults or injures another person or thing. |The outrageous act of vandalism committed by the outragers shocked the community. 暴徒犯下的令人发指的破坏行为震惊了整个社区。|近义词：villain, offender, wrongdoer|反义词:protector, defender|词性:名词

词源: From Old French outrage "excess, violence," from outre "beyond" (from Latin ultra "beyond") + -age.
