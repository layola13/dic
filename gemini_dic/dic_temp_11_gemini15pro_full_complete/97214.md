inflated: /ɪnˈfleɪtɪd/
adjective
膨胀的，夸张的，通货膨胀的
Filled with air or gas; exaggerated; increased abnormally, especially prices.
The inflated balloon floated across the room.  膨胀的气球飘过房间。

The company's profits were inflated by dubious accounting practices.  该公司的利润因可疑的会计行为而被夸大。

The inflated prices of houses are making it difficult for first-time buyers.  房价飞涨，首次购房者难以负担。


synonym: exaggerated, enlarged, swollen, pumped-up, overblown
antonym: deflated, reduced, shrunken, minimized, understated
词性: adjective
