absoluter:/ˈæbsəˌluːtər/
adj.|更绝对的，更完全的|Something that is absoluter is even more absolute than something else that is already considered absolute. This is a somewhat unusual usage, as "absolute" already implies completeness or totality.|This rule is absoluter than the previous one, allowing no exceptions whatsoever.|近义词:more complete, more total, more utter|反义词:less complete, less total, less utter|词性:形容词
