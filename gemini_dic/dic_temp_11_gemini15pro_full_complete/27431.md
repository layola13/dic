camisole:/ˈkæmɪˌsoʊl/ (US), /ˈkæmɪsɒl/ (UK)| n.|女式背心；吊带衫|A woman's sleeveless undergarment, typically having thin straps and made of silk or nylon, and often worn under transparent blouses.  It can also be worn as a top by itself.|She wore a lace camisole under her sheer blouse. 她穿着一件蕾丝吊带背心在透明衬衫里面。

She wore a silk camisole as a top on a hot summer day. 在炎热的夏天，她穿着一件丝绸吊带衫作为上衣。|近义词：undershirt, tank top, vest|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly outerwear)|词性:noun
