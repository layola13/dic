genericnesses:/dʒəˈnɛrɪknɪsɪz/ | n. | 普遍性；一般性；通俗性 | The state or quality of being generic; lack of distinguishing features or qualities. | The genericnesses of the products made them unappealing to consumers.  这些产品的普遍性使它们对消费者没有吸引力。 | Example: The genericnesses of the plot made the movie predictable.  情节的通俗性使这部电影很容易预测。 | Synonyms: generality, commonality, ordinariness | Antonyms: uniqueness, distinctiveness, individuality | noun
