effeminize:/ɪˈfɛmɪnaɪz/| vt.|使女性化，使柔弱|To make someone or something more feminine in qualities, appearance, or manner.  It can also refer to making something more suitable or appealing to women.|He accused his political opponents of trying to effeminize the military. 他指责他的政敌试图使军队女性化。|The marketing campaign seemed to effeminize the product to appeal to a wider audience.  这次营销活动似乎是为了吸引更广泛的受众而使产品女性化。|synonyms: feminize, soften, weaken|antonyms: masculinize, strengthen, harden|verb
