nonrecyclable:/ˌnɒnriːˈsaɪkləbl/ | /ˌnɑːnriːˈsaɪkləbl/ | adj. | 不可回收的 | Not able to be recycled or processed for reuse. |  Many plastic bags are nonrecyclable and end up in landfills. 许多塑料袋是不可回收的，最终被填埋。|  These single-use coffee pods are nonrecyclable, which is bad for the environment.  这些一次性咖啡胶囊不可回收，对环境有害。 | Synonyms: non-reusable, disposable, unrecoverable | Antonyms: recyclable, reusable, recoverable | adjective
