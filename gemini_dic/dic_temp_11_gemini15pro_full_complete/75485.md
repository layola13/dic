frustratory:/ˈfrʌstreɪtəri/ /ˈfrʌstrətɔːri/
adjective
令人沮丧的
Causing frustration; tending to frustrate.
It's frustrating when you can't find the information you need. 当你找不到你需要的信息时，这很令人沮丧。
The lack of progress is frustrating.  缺乏进展令人沮丧。

近义词:disappointing, annoying, exasperating, vexing
反义词:satisfying, gratifying, rewarding
词性:adjective
