liveries:/ˈlɪv(ə)riz/
noun
制服；彩 livery；（尤指公司或组织成员穿的）特殊制服；（旧时）仆人的制服；（比赛用车的）装饰涂装
A livery is a special uniform worn by servants or officials, or special colours used on vehicles, especially public transport vehicles.

The chauffeur wore a smart dark green livery.  司机穿着整洁的深绿色制服。

All the buses and taxis were painted in the same livery. 所有的公共汽车和出租车都涂上了相同的颜色。

synonyms:uniform, costume, attire, garb, outfit
antonyms:plainclothes, mufti

noun
（动物，尤指马的）毛色；（鸟的）羽毛
The natural or characteristic coloring, especially of a mammal or bird.

The horse's livery was a dappled grey. 这匹马的毛色是斑驳的灰色。

synonyms: coat, plumage, coloration
antonyms: N/A


词性: noun
