alkaloids:/ˈælkəlɔɪdz/ | n. | 生物碱 | Any of a class of nitrogenous organic compounds of plant origin that have pronounced physiological actions on humans. |  Many alkaloids, such as morphine and nicotine, are used in medicine. 许多生物碱，例如吗啡和尼古丁，被用于医药。| synonyms: bases, nitrogenous compounds | antonyms: acids | noun
