emigrated: /ˈɛmɪɡreɪtɪd/ | v. | 迁移, 移居国外 | past tense and past participle of emigrate, meaning to leave one's own country in order to settle permanently in another. | My grandparents emigrated from Italy to the United States in 1920. 我的祖父母在1920年从意大利移民到美国。|  They emigrated to escape the war. 他们移居国外以逃避战争。 | synonyms: migrated, relocated, resettled | antonyms: immigrated, returned, repatriated | verb
