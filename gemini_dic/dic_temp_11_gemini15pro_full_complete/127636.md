needless:/ˈniːdləs/
adj.
不必要的，无用的
Not needed; unnecessary.
It is needless to say that we were all disappointed.  不用说，我们都很失望。
近义词：unnecessary, superfluous, redundant
反义词：necessary, essential, required
词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *nedles, from Old English nēadlēas (“without compulsion, involuntary”), equivalent to need +‎ -less.  Cognate with Dutch noodloos, German notlos.
