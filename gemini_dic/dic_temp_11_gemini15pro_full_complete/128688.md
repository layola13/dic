nidary:/ˈnɪdəri/ | n. | 鸟巢，特指鹰巢 | A nest of a bird of prey, especially an eagle. | The climbers spotted a nidary high on the cliff face.  登山者在悬崖峭壁上发现了一个鹰巢。 | 近义词: aerie, eyrie | 反义词: None (no single antonym for a specific bird's nest) | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *nidery, *niderie, from Old French nidiere, from nid ("nest"), from Latin nīdus ("nest").
