degelling:/diːˈdʒɛlɪŋ/
n.|脱胶，去胶|The process of removing gelling agents from a substance.  This can refer to reversing the gelation process, or physically removing gelled material.|例句：The degelling process was crucial for the experiment's success.  脱胶过程对实验的成功至关重要。

例句：Degelling of the agar allowed the researchers to study the individual components.  琼脂的脱胶使研究人员能够研究单个成分。|近义词：liquefaction, melting, dissolving|反义词：gelling, solidifying, setting|词性:名词

degelling:/diːˈdʒɛlɪŋ/
v.|脱胶，去胶|To remove gelling agents from a substance, reversing or breaking down the gel structure.|例句：The scientists degelled the sample by heating it. 科学家们通过加热样品来脱胶。

例句：The enzyme degelled the pectin, making the juice less viscous.  这种酶使果胶脱胶，从而降低了果汁的粘度。|近义词：liquefy, melt, dissolve|反义词：gel, solidify, set|词性:动词
