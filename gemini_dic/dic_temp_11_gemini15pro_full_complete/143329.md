outechoing:/ˌaʊtˈekəʊɪŋ/
|adj.|回响的，胜过回响的；压倒性的，盖过其他声音的|surpassing or exceeding in sound; overpowering or drowning out other sounds|The outechoing roar of the crowd filled the stadium. 震耳欲聋的欢呼声充满了体育场。|近义词：resounding, deafening, overwhelming|反义词：faint, quiet, subdued|词性:形容词
词源:  "outechoing" is formed from the prefix "out-" (meaning "exceeding" or "surpassing") and the verb "echoing" (meaning "producing an echo").  It suggests a sound so powerful that it surpasses a typical echo.
