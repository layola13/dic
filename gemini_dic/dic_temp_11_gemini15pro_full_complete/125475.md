multipointed:/ˈmʌltiˌpɔɪntɪd/
adj.
多尖的，多点的
Having many points or tips.
The multipointed star shone brightly in the night sky.  多尖的星星在夜空中闪耀。
Near Antonyms: rounded, blunt, smooth
Antonyms: single-pointed
