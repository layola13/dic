domiciles:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪlz/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməsaɪlz/ (US)| n. |住所，住处|A person's domicile is the place where they live or have their permanent home.  A person can only have one domicile at a time.|Many retirees establish their domiciles in Florida for the warm weather. 许多退休人员为了温暖的天气在佛罗里达州定居。|Synonyms: residence, abode, dwelling, home|Antonyms:  None (Domicile implies permanence; a true antonym would imply impermanence or lack of a fixed home)|Noun

domicile:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməsaɪl/ (US)| v. |使定居，(合法地)确定住所|To legally establish someone's residence or home.|The company domiciled its new headquarters in Delaware. 该公司将其新总部设在特拉华州。|Synonyms:  establish residence, settle|Antonyms: relocate, emigrate|Verb
