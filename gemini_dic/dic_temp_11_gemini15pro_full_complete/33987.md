citation:/saɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 引用，引证；传票，罚单 | a quotation from or reference to a book, paper, or author, especially in a scholarly work.  A summons to appear in court. |  例句：The student's essay included several citations from Shakespeare.  学生的论文中引用了莎士比亚的几句话。

例句：He received a citation for speeding. 他收到一张超速驾驶的罚单。 | 近义词：quotation, reference, summons | 反义词：N/A | 词性: noun


词源:  Late Middle English: from Latin *citatio(n-), from the verb citare ‘summon, urge’.
