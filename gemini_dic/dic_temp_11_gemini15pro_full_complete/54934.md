dispositor:/dɪˈspɒzɪtər/ /dɪˈspɑːzɪtər/ | n. | 支配星 |The planet that rules the sign in which another planet is placed, in astrology.  | Example: Mars is in Taurus, and Venus, the ruler of Taurus, is in Gemini. So Venus is the dispositor of Mars. 火星在金牛座，而金星是金牛座的守护星，位于双子座。因此，金星是火星的支配星。| Example:  The dispositor of the Sun in this chart is Mercury.  在这张星盘中，太阳的支配星是水星。 | Synonym: ruler, lord | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific astrological term) | noun
