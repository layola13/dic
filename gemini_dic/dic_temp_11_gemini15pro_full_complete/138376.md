notobranchiata:/ˌnoʊtəbræŋkiˈɑːtə/| n.| 背鳃类，后鳃类 |A subclass of Gastropoda mollusks characterized by having the gills situated posterior to the heart.  These are sea slugs, often with elaborate and colorful external gills.| Notobranchiata include many species of colorful sea slugs found in tropical waters. 背鳃类包括许多在热带水域发现的色彩鲜艳的海蛞蝓。|近义词:Opisthobranchia (older classification)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Prosobranchia as a contrasting subclass of gastropods) | 词性: noun
