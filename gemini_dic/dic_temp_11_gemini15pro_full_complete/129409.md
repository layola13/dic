nominalizing:/ˈnɑːmɪnəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 名词化；名词化过程 | The process of forming a noun from a verb or adjective. |  Example: The nominalizing of the verb "run" creates the noun "runner." 把动词“run”名词化就形成了名词“runner”。| 近义词: nominalization | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "verbalizing") | 词性: noun
