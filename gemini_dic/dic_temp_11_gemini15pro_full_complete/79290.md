glaive:/ɡleɪv/| n.|戟; 长矛; |A glaive is a type of pole weapon, a long pole with a single-edged blade at the end. |The knight wielded his glaive with skill and precision. 骑士熟练而精准地挥舞着他的戟。| The museum displayed a collection of medieval glaives. 博物馆展出了一系列中世纪的戟。|Synonyms: spear, lance, halberd|Antonyms: shield, armor|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *glaive*, from Late Latin *glavia*, of Celtic origin.
