guia:/ˈɡiːə/ | n. | guide, guidebook, handbook, manual | a person who leads or directs other people on a journey; a book, document, or other publication that provides information or instruction on a particular subject | Example: The guia led us through the dense forest.  导游带领我们穿过茂密的森林。| Example: I consulted the guia before assembling the furniture. 我在组装家具之前查阅了指南。|Synonyms: guide, escort, conductor, handbook, manual | Antonyms: follower | Noun
