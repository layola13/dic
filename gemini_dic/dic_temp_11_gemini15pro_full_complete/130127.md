non-realism:/nɒnˈriːəlɪzəm/ | n. | 非现实主义 |  A genre or style in art, literature, film, etc., that does not attempt to represent reality accurately.  It often involves elements of fantasy, magic, surrealism, or the grotesque. |  Example: Magical realism, a type of non-realism, often blends fantastical elements with everyday life. 魔幻现实主义，一种非现实主义，经常将幻想元素与日常生活相融合。

Example: The play's non-realism was evident in its use of abstract sets and symbolic costumes.  这出戏的非现实主义体现在它对抽象布景和象征性服装的使用上。 | Synonyms: fantasy, surrealism, idealism, romanticism | Antonyms: realism, naturalism | noun
