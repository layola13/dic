conjointly:/kənˈdʒɔɪntli/| adv.|共同地，联合地|Together, in conjunction with; in combination.|The two companies worked conjointly on the project.  两家公司在这个项目上共同合作。|The report was prepared conjointly by the two departments. 这份报告是由两个部门联合准备的。|近义词：together, jointly, collectively, in conjunction, combinedly|反义词:separately, individually, independently|词性:副词

Etymology->

From Late Latin *conjunctus*, from Latin conjungere (“to join together”), from con- (“together”) + jungere (“to join”).
