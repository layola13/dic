gutty:/ˈɡʌti/| n.| (旧) 羊肠线高尔夫球|A golf ball made of gutta-percha.  |He used a gutty to play a round of golf. 他用羊肠线高尔夫球打了一轮高尔夫。|Synonyms: gutta-percha ball |Antonyms: modern golf ball| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gut + -y.  The noun sense refers to gutta-percha, a type of latex used to make golf balls.  The adjective sense developed from the toughness associated with animal intestines (guts).
