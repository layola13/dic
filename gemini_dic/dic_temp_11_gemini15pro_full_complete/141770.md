orational:/ɔːˈreɪʃənəl/| adj.| 演说的，演讲的；口头的 |Of, relating to, or characterized by oratory; suitable for oratory.  Fit for or resembling an oration. |His orational skills impressed the audience. 他的演讲技巧给观众留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：rhetorical, eloquent, spoken|反义词：written, taciturn|词性:adjective
词源: From Latin *orationalis*, from *oratio* ("speech, oration").
