doar: /doˈar/ (Brazilian Portuguese)
verb
to give, to donate, to bestow
This verb is used primarily in Brazilian Portuguese.  It means "to give," "to donate," or "to bestow." It's similar to the Spanish verb "dar."
Example: Eu quero doar dinheiro para a caridade. (I want to donate money to charity.)

Example: Ela doou seu tempo para ajudar os necessitados. (She donated her time to help those in need.)

Synonym: dar (Portuguese), conceder (Portuguese - more formal),  donate (English), give (English)
Antonym: receber (Portuguese - to receive), tomar (Portuguese - to take), take (English)
word class: verb
