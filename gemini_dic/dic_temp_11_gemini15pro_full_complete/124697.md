mowable:/ˈmoʊəbl/| adj.|可修剪的，可刈割的|Suitable for mowing; capable of being mowed.  Able to be cut with a lawnmower or other cutting implement.|The grass in the park is mowable now. 公园里的草现在可以修剪了。|Synonyms: cuttable, trimmable|Antonyms: unmowable, overgrown|adjective
