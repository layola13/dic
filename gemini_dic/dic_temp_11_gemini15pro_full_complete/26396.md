by-the-way:/baɪ ðə ˈweɪ/| adv.|顺便说一下，顺便提一下|Used to introduce a remark or subject unconnected with what was previously being talked about.|By the way, have you seen John lately? 顺便问一下，你最近见过约翰吗？|Incidentally, parenthetically|N/A|adverb
