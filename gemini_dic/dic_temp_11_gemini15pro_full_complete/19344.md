bibliomaniac: /ˌbɪbliəˈmeɪniæk/ | n. | 书籍狂；藏书癖 | A person who has an excessive fondness for collecting books, often regardless of their content or value. | He was a bibliomaniac, his house overflowing with stacks of books. 他是个藏书癖，他的房子里堆满了书。 | bibliophile, bookworm | book hater | noun
