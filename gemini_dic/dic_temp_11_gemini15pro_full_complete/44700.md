crudest:/ˈkruːdɪst/| adj.|最粗糙的；最粗俗的；最简陋的|Most basic and undeveloped; lacking refinement or subtlety; offensively coarse or vulgar.|His jokes were crude and often offensive. 他的笑话很粗俗，经常带有冒犯性。|The tools they used were of the crudest kind. 他们使用的工具是最简陋的那种。|Synonyms: primitive, rudimentary, unrefined, vulgar, coarse|Antonyms: refined, sophisticated, polished, elegant, subtle|adjective
