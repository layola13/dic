detesters:/dɪˈtestərz/
n.
憎恨者；厌恶者
People who strongly dislike or hate something or someone.
The new law had many detractors and detesters. 这项新法律有很多批评者和反对者。

近义词:haters, loathers, scorners, despisers
反义词:admirers, lovers, supporters, fans
词性:noun (plural)

detest:/dɪˈtest/
v.
憎恶；厌恶
To feel intense dislike or hatred for.
I detest hypocrisy. 我憎恨虚伪。

近义词:hate, loathe, abhor, despise
反义词:love, admire, adore, cherish
词性:verb
