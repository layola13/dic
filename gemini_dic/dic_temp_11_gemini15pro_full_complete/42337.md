counteracted:/ˌkaʊntərˈæktɪd/
verb
抵消，对抗
to act against something in order to reduce its force or neutralize it.
The medicine counteracted the poison.  这种药抵消了毒性。
The government's spending cuts were counteracted by increased consumer borrowing. 政府的削减开支被消费者借贷的增加所抵消。
neutralize, offset, negate
augment, enhance, reinforce
