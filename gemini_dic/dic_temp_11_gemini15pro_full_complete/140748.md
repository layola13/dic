omnisexuality:/ˌɒmniˌsɛkʃʊˈælɪti/ | n. | 全性恋 | Attraction to people of all genders and sexes.  It acknowledges and appreciates the full spectrum of gender identity and expression. |  Example 1:  "Omnisexuality is about being attracted to someone regardless of their gender." 全性恋是指被某人吸引，而不管其性别如何。

Example 2: "They identify as omnisexual and are open to relationships with people of all genders." 他们认同自己是全性恋，并对与所有性别的人建立关系持开放态度。 | Near Synonyms: pansexuality, polysexuality | Antonyms: monosexuality | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From omni- (Latin, "all") + sexual + -ity. The prefix "omni" emphasizes attraction to *all* genders, acknowledging the nuances and complexities of gender beyond the binary.
