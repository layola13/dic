goldwynism:/ˈɡoʊldwɪnɪzəm/ | n. |高德温式妙语(一种无意中造成的幽默表达，通常是逻辑混乱或用词不当造成的)| A humorous remark, especially one made unintentionally, usually through a confused logic or inappropriate use of words.
