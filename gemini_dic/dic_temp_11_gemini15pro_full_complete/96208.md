indexation:/ˌɪndɛkˈseɪʃən/| n.|指数化|The act of linking a value, such as wages or prices, to an index to adjust for inflation or other changes.|例句：Indexation of wages helps protect employees from the erosion of purchasing power caused by inflation. 工资指数化有助于保护雇员的购买力免受通货膨胀的影响。

例句：The government introduced indexation of pensions to keep pace with the rising cost of living. 政府引入了养老金指数化，以跟上不断上涨的生活成本。|近义词：adjustment, linking, scaling|反义词:stagnation, fixed|词性:noun
