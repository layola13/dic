ampersand:/ˈæmpərsænd/| n.|和号，& |A symbol (&) representing the word *and*.|例句：The company name is Smith & Jones. 公司名称是Smith & Jones。|例句：Use an ampersand between the two names.  在两个名字之间用一个“&”符号。|近义词：and|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for a symbol.)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrase "and per se and," meaning "and [the character &] by itself is and."  This phrase, used when reciting the alphabet, eventually became slurred and shortened to "ampersand."
