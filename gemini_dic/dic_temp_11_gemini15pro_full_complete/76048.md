furloughing:/ˈfɜːrloʊɪŋ/ | n. | 休假，暂时解雇 |The act of placing an employee on furlough.  This involves a temporary, unpaid leave of absence, typically due to economic reasons or other circumstances beyond the employee's control.| Many companies resorted to furloughing staff during the pandemic. 许多公司在疫情期间采取了让员工休假的措施。| synonyms: laying off, suspending, benching | antonyms: hiring, employing, reinstating | noun

furlough:/ˈfɜːrloʊ/ | n. vt.| 休假，暂时解雇 |A temporary leave of absence from work, often unpaid, typically due to economic conditions or other special circumstances. As a verb, it means to place someone on furlough.|  The company furloughed 100 workers. 公司让100名工人休假。 He was furloughed for three months. 他被休假了三个月。 | synonyms: layoff, suspension, leave of absence | antonyms: employment, hiring | noun, verb
