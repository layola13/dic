honorific:/ˌɒnəˈrɪfɪk/ /ˌɑːnəˈrɪfɪks/ | adj. |表示尊敬的 | Conferring or conveying honor. |  The title "Doctor" is an honorific. “博士”是一个尊称。| synonyms: respectful, deferential, courteous | antonyms: disrespectful, derogatory | adjective
