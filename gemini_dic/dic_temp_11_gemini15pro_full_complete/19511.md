biennial:/baɪˈɛnɪəl/ /baɪˈɛniəl/| adj. n.|两年一次的；两年生的|Happening every two years; lasting for two years.  A plant that lives for two years. |

例句：
The biennial conference attracts scholars from all over the world.
这每两年一次的会议吸引了来自世界各地的学者。

例句：
Carrots are biennial plants.
胡萝卜是二年生植物。


近义词：two-yearly|反义词:annual, perennial|词性:adjective, noun


biennial:/baɪˈɛnɪəl/ /baɪˈɛniəl/| n.|两年一次发生的事件；二年生植物|An event that happens every two years; a plant that lives for two years.|

例句：
The flower show is a biennial.
花展是两年一次的。

例句：
Many common garden flowers are biennials.
许多常见的园林花卉都是二年生植物。

近义词：two-year event, two-year plant|反义词:annual, perennial|词性:noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *biennalis, from Latin bi- "two" + annus "year."
