equality:/ɪˈkwɒləti/ /iˈkwɑːləti/| n. |平等，均等|The state of being equal, especially in status, rights, and opportunities.|例句：They are fighting for equality of opportunity. 他们正在为机会均等而奋斗。

例句：The principle of equality before the law is fundamental. 法律面前人人平等的原则是根本性的。

近义词：parity, equivalence, uniformity, sameness|反义词:inequality, disparity, difference, bias|词性:noun
