gaudiest:/ˈɡɔːdiɪst/
adjective
最花哨的；最俗丽的
superlative adjective of gaudy: extravagantly bright or showy, typically so as to be tasteless.
例句：She wore the gaudiest dress at the party. 她在聚会上穿着最花哨的裙子。

例句：The carnival was a riot of gaudiest costumes. 嘉年华上满是花里胡哨的服装。

近义词：flashiest, showiest, loudest, tackiest
反义词：plainest, simplest, subtlest, understated
词性：adjective
