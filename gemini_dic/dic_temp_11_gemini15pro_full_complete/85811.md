haze:/heɪz/ /heiz/ | n. v. | 薄雾，霾; 模糊，迷糊 |  Atmospheric moisture, dust, smoke, and other dry particles that obscure visibility.  As a verb, it means to become hazy or to subject someone to hazing (initiation rituals). |  A thick haze hung over the city. 城市上空笼罩着浓厚的雾霾。| synonyms: mist, fog, smog, obscurity | antonyms: clarity, clearness | noun

haze:/heɪz/ /heiz/ | v. | 使朦胧，使模糊不清；戏弄，欺凌(尤指新生) | To make or become hazy; to subject to hazing. |  The hot weather hazed the distant hills. 炎热的天气使远处的山峦变得朦胧。| synonyms:  obscure, blur, mist over | antonyms: clarify, clear | verb
