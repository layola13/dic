His kiddishnesses were amusing at first, but they soon grew tiresome. 他幼稚的行为起初很有趣，但很快就变得令人厌烦了。
synonyms: immaturity, childishness, silliness, puerility, frivolity
antonyms: maturity, seriousness, sophistication, wisdom
