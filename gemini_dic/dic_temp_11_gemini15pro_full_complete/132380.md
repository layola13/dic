nondelusive:/ˌnɑːndɪˈluːsɪv/
adjective
非妄想的；不虚妄的
Not based on or characterized by delusion; realistic or grounded in reality.
Her nondelusive thinking allowed her to assess the situation accurately. 她不妄想的思维方式让她能够准确地评估形势。
The report presented a nondelusive picture of the company's financial situation. 这份报告呈现了公司财务状况的真实情况。
Synonyms: realistic, rational, sane, lucid, clear-headed, grounded
Antonyms: delusional, unrealistic, irrational, insane, psychotic, ungrounded
词性: 形容词
