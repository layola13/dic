dependable:/dɪˈpɛndəbl/ | adj. | 可靠的，可信赖的 | Someone or something that is dependable is reliable and can be trusted to do what you expect or need. |  She's a dependable friend. 她是一个可靠的朋友。


My car is old but dependable. 我的车很旧但很可靠。


近义词:reliable, trustworthy, steadfast | 反义词:unreliable, untrustworthy, fickle | 词性:形容词


词源: From "depend" (依赖) + "-able" (能够…的), ultimately from Latin "dependere" (to hang down).
