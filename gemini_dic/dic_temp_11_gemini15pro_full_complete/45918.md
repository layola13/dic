cutson: /ˈkʌtsən/ | n. | (非正式)儿子，孩子 (尤指男孩) | A term of endearment for a son, often used informally.  This word is likely a blend of "cut" (possibly referring to a younger version) and "son". Its etymology is uncertain and it is not a standard English word. |  "Hey there, cutson, how's it going?"  嘿，小子，最近怎么样？ | 近义词: son, boy, kid, lad | 反义词: daughter | 词性: 名词
