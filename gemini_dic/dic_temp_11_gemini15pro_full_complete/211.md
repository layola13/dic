abbreviated: /əˈbriːvieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 简略的，缩写的 | Shortened; made briefer, especially by omitting letters or words. |  Example: The meeting minutes were abbreviated to save time. 会议记录为了节省时间而被简略了。

Example:  He used an abbreviated form of his name. 他使用了自己名字的缩写形式。

Synonym: shortened, condensed, abridged, compressed

Antonym: lengthened, extended, expanded, unabridged

词性: adjective


词源: From Latin *abbreviatus*, past participle of *abbreviare* "to shorten," from *ab-* "from, away" + *brevis* "short."
