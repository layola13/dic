nonmonistic:/ˌnɒnˈmɒnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非一元论的 | Not monistic; characterized by the belief that reality is not composed of a single substance or principle. |  His philosophy is decidedly nonmonistic, embracing a plurality of fundamental elements. 他的哲学明显是非一元论的，包含了多种基本元素。|  Her nonmonistic worldview allows for both the material and the spiritual. 她非一元论的世界观允许物质和精神的存在。 | pluralistic, dualistic | monistic | adjective
