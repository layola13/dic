drowned:/draʊnd/| v. adj.|淹死，溺死；浸透，淹没|to die under water because you are unable to breathe; to kill someone in this way; to completely cover something in liquid|例句：The man drowned in the river. 那个人在河里淹死了。|近义词：submerged, inundated, suffocated (v.); soaked, drenched (adj.)|反义词：rescued, saved (v.); dry (adj.)|词性:动词, 形容词
drowned:/draʊnd/| adj.|淹死的，溺死的|covered with or full of too much of a liquid; having died by drowning|例句：The drowned rat struggled to shore. 那只溺水的耗子挣扎着游向岸边。|近义词：soaked, drenched, saturated|反义词：dry|词性:形容词
