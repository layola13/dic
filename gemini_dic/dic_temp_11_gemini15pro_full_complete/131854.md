nonconstructive:/ˌnɑːnkənˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
无建设性的，没有帮助的
Not helpful or likely to produce a good result.
His criticism was totally nonconstructive. 他的批评完全没有建设性。
Synonyms: unhelpful, unproductive, negative, useless, detrimental
Antonyms: constructive, helpful, productive, useful, beneficial
词性: 形容词
