discerpibility:/dɪˌsɜːrnɪˈbɪləti/
n.
可辨别性, 分辨能力
The quality of being discernible; discernibleness.  The capability of being discerned or distinguished.
The discerpibility of the subtle differences between the two wines impressed the sommelier. 这两种葡萄酒之间细微差别的可辨别性给侍酒师留下了深刻的印象。

近义词:distinguishability, perceptibility, differentiability
反义词:indistinguishability, imperceptibility
