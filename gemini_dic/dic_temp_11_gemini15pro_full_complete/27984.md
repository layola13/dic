canvases:/ˈkænvəsɪz/
n.
油画布; 帆布;
The artist prepared several canvases for his next series of paintings.  这位艺术家为他的下一系列绘画准备了几块画布。
The sails of the ship were made of strong canvas.  船的帆是用结实的帆布做的。
近义词: fabrics, cloths
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用表示不同材质的词，例如：silk, plastic)
词性: 名词 (plural of canvas)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French canevas, from Late Latin cannabis "hemp," from Greek kannabis. Originally referring to hemp cloth, the meaning broadened to include other types of strong cloth and then to the prepared surface used for painting.
