baybars:/ˈbeɪbɑːrz/| n.|拜巴尔斯|Baybars, also spelled Baibars, was a Mamluk sultan of Egypt and Syria. He reigned from 1260 to 1277.  He is known for his military victories against the Crusaders and the Mongols.|Baybars led the Mamluks to victory at the Battle of Ain Jalut. 拜巴尔斯带领马穆鲁克军队在艾因贾鲁特战役中取得了胜利。|Baybars consolidated Mamluk power in the region. 拜巴尔斯巩固了马穆鲁克在该地区的势力。| synonyms: sultan, ruler, leader| antonyms: subject, follower|noun
