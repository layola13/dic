napoleonic:/nəˌpōˌlēˈɑːnɪk/
adjective
拿破仑的；拿破仑时代的；像拿破仑的
Relating to or characteristic of Napoleon Bonaparte or his reign.
Napoleon's ambition for conquest resulted in Napoleonic Wars across Europe. 拿破仑的征服野心导致了席卷欧洲的拿破仑战争。

The Napoleonic Code had a lasting impact on legal systems.  拿破仑法典对法律体系产生了持久的影响。

synonym: imperial, authoritarian, dictatorial
antonym: democratic, egalitarian
Etymology: From Napoleon + -ic.  源自 Napoleon + -ic。
