dipaschal:/dɪˈpæskəl/| adj.|逾越节后的，复活节后的|Relating to the period after Passover or Easter.  Derived from Greek "dia" (through, after) and "pascha" (Passover).|The dipaschal celebrations continued for a week. 逾越节后的庆祝活动持续了一周。|The dipaschal lamb was traditionally eaten on the first Sunday after Easter.  复活节后的第一个星期天传统上要吃逾越节羔羊。|近义词：post-paschal, post-Easter|反义词：pre-paschal, pre-Easter|词性:adjective
