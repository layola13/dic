insurmountable:/ɪnsərˈmaʊntəbl/
adjective
不可克服的
Too great to be overcome.  Presenting a difficulty or obstacle that cannot be overcome.
The team faced insurmountable odds in the championship game.  该队在冠军赛中面临着不可克服的困难。
The challenge seemed insurmountable at first, but they persevered.  起初，挑战似乎不可逾越，但他们坚持了下来。
近义词: insuperable, unconquerable, invincible
反义词: surmountable, conquerable, vincible
词性: adjective
词源: From in- "not" + surmount + -able. Surmount comes from Old French surmonter "to rise above," from sur- "over" + monter "to mount, climb."
