exchanging:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/
v.|交换，交流|The act of giving something to someone and receiving something else in return.  The process of sharing information, ideas, or feelings.|They were exchanging gifts. 他们在交换礼物。|Synonyms: trading, swapping, bartering|Antonyms: keeping, retaining|verb

exchanging:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/
adj.|交换的，交流的|Relating to or involving the act of exchanging.|They had an exchanging glances. 他们交换了一下眼神。|Synonyms: reciprocal, mutual|Antonyms: one-sided, unilateral|adjective
