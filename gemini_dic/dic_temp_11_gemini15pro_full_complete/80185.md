godheads:/ˈɡɒdhedz/ /ˈɡɑːdhedz/
n.
神性，神格; 神;
The state or nature of being divine; a god or goddess.  A representation of a god, especially in the form of a sculptured bust or head.
例句：The ancient Egyptians believed in the godheads of many different gods and goddesses. 古埃及人信仰许多不同的神灵的神性。

例句：The museum displayed godheads of various Roman deities.  博物馆展出了各种罗马神灵的雕像头像。
近义词：divinity, deity, god, goddess
反义词：mortal, human
词性：名词
