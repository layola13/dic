brackishness:/ˈbrækɪʃnəs/| n.|咸度；微咸；|The brackishness of the water made it undrinkable.  水的微咸使其无法饮用。|近义词：salinity, saltiness|反义词：freshness|词性：名词
Etymology: From brackish + -ness.  Brackish comes from Middle Dutch *brak "salty," related to Old English bræc "breaking."  The connection likely refers to the slightly salty taste of water where fresh and salt water mix, a "broken" salinity.
