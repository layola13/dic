inconsequentially:/ˌɪnkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəli/ /ˌɪnkɑːnsɪˈkwɛnʃəli/| adv.|无关紧要地，无足轻重地|In a manner that lacks importance or significance; trivially.|He spoke inconsequentially about the weather while we waited for the news. 他漫不经心地谈论着天气，而我们则在等待消息。|She dismissed the incident inconsequentially. 她轻描淡写地把这件事带过了。|近义词：trivially, insignificantly, unimportantly, negligibly|反义词：significantly, importantly, consequentially|词性: 副词
