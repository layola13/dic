gumma:/ˈɡʌmə/| n. |树胶肿，梅毒瘤|A gumma is a soft, non-cancerous growth that can occur in the body as a late manifestation of syphilis. It is a characteristic lesion of tertiary syphilis.|The patient presented with a gumma on his leg, a sign of late-stage syphilis.  这个病人的腿上出现了一个树胶肿，这是晚期梅毒的迹象。|Synonyms: lesion, node, tumor|Antonyms: health, wellness|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin gumma, from Greek kommi "gum," referring to the gummy consistency of the lesion.
