jezabels:/ˈdʒɛzəˌbɛlz/ | n. | 耶洗别（复数）|  A plural form of Jezebel, referring to multiple women perceived as wicked, shameless, or morally unrestrained, often in a controlling or manipulative manner.  It alludes to the biblical figure Jezebel, the wife of King Ahab of Israel. |  The town gossips acted like a pack of jezabels, spreading rumors about everyone.  小镇上的那些长舌妇像一群耶洗别一样，到处散播谣言。 |  The jezabels of the office were always trying to undermine their colleagues. 办公室里的那些耶洗别总是试图暗中破坏她们同事的名誉。 |  harlots, vixens, temptresses | virtuous women, saints | noun
