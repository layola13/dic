counterquestions:/ˈkaʊntərˌkwɛstʃənz/| n.|反诘；反问|A question asked in response to another question, often to avoid answering the original question or to gain more information.|例句：When asked about his alibi, he responded with a series of counterquestions. 当被问及他的不在场证明时，他用一系列反问来回应。

例句：She skillfully used counterquestions to control the flow of the conversation. 她巧妙地运用反问来控制谈话的走向。|近义词：cross-questions, return questions|反义词:answers, responses|词性:名词

词源: counter- (prefix meaning "against" or "in opposition to") + question (n.)
