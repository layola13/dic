emblemmed:/ɪmˈblɛmd/
adjective
饰有徽章的，印有标志的
Marked or decorated with an emblem; symbolic.
The ambassador's car was emblemmed with the country's flag. 大使的汽车上饰有该国国旗。
The knights wore emblemmed surcoats. 骑士们穿着饰有徽章的罩衣。
近义词:badged, crested, decorated, marked
反义词:plain, undecorated
词性:adjective
