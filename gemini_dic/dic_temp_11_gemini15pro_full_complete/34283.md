claret-red:/ˈklærɪt ˈred/ | adj. | 紫红色，酒红色 | A dark purplish-red colour, the colour of claret wine. |  The curtains were a rich claret-red. 窗帘是浓郁的酒红色。 | 近义词: burgundy, wine-red, maroon | 反义词: pale, light | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "claret" (a type of red wine) + "red" (the color). "Claret" comes from the Old French "claret," meaning "clear, bright," later used to describe a light red wine.
