earnests:/ˈɜːrnɪsts/| n. |定金；保证金；认真；诚挚|Something given as a pledge of the fulfillment of a contract or the sincerity of an intention.  A token of what is to come; a down payment.|He gave her a ring as an earnest of his intentions. 他送给她一枚戒指作为他诚意的保证。|近义词：deposit, pledge, guarantee, down payment, seriousness, sincerity|反义词：frivolity, insincerity|noun
