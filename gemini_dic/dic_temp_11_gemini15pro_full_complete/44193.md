croaking:/ˈkroʊkɪŋ/ /ˈkrəʊkɪŋ/
n.|呱呱叫声|the deep hoarse sound made by a frog or a crow|The croaking of the frogs filled the night air. 青蛙的呱呱叫声充满了夜空。|近义词：cawing, rasping|反义词:chirping, tweeting|词性:名词
croaking:/ˈkroʊkɪŋ/ /ˈkrəʊkɪŋ/
adj.|发出呱呱叫声的|making a deep hoarse sound like a frog or a crow|A croaking raven perched on the branch. 一只发出呱呱叫声的渡鸦栖息在树枝上。|近义词：raucous, hoarse|反义词:melodious, sweet|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *croken*, present participle of *croken* (“to croak”), from Old Norse *kráka* (“crow, raven”), from Proto-Germanic *krakon* (“to croak”), from Proto-Indo-European *ger- (“to cry, call out”).
