endangered: /ɪnˈdeɪndʒərd/ | adj. | 濒危的 |  At risk of extinction; seriously threatened with extinction.  |  The giant panda is an endangered species.  大熊猫是一种濒危物种。 | The Amur leopard is critically endangered.  远东豹处于极度濒危状态。| synonyms: threatened, imperiled, vulnerable, at risk | antonyms: safe, secure, thriving, flourishing | adjective

词源:
From en- (使成为，置于...之中) + danger (危险) + -ed (形容词后缀，表示“处于...状态的”)
