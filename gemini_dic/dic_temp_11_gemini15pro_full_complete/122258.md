mithgarthr: /mɪθˈɡɑːrθr/
noun
米斯加斯尔（中土世界地名）
A fictional location in J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium, specifically mentioned in connection with the dwarves.  It's often interpreted as a general term for the dwellings of the dwarves, especially those in the East.
The dwarves of Mithgarthr were renowned for their craftsmanship. 米斯加斯尔的矮人以其精湛的工艺而闻名。
The location of Mithgarthr is not precisely defined in Tolkien's writings. 米斯加斯尔的位置在托尔金的著作中没有精确定义。
Synonym:  Dwarf-holds,  Dwarf-realms
Antonym:  (No direct antonym,  perhaps lands of other races like elves or men)
词性: 名词
