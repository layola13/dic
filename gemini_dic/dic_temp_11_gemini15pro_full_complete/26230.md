butterinesses:/ˈbʌtərɪnɪsɪz/
noun
油腻感；奶油味；黄油味
The quality of being buttery; resembling or suggesting butter in richness, smoothness, or flavor.
The butterinesses of the pastries were overwhelming, leaving a greasy film in my mouth.  这些糕点的油腻感太重了，在我的嘴里留下一层油脂。
The various butterinesses of the cheeses were sampled by the judges.  评委们品尝了各种奶酪的不同奶油味。
richness, smoothness, creaminess
greasiness, dryness
