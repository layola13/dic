granges:/ɡreɪn(dʒ)ɪz/ | n. | 谷仓，农场，农庄 | A group of farm buildings.  Often used in the plural. | The farmer stored the hay in his granges. 农民把干草储存在他的谷仓里。| The old granges were converted into luxury apartments.  那些旧农庄被改建成了豪华公寓。 | 近义词: barns, farms, farmsteads | 反义词:  (No direct opposite, perhaps city, urban area) | Noun
