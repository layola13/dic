cornicing:/ˈkɔːrnɪsɪŋ/ | n. | 装檐口，飞檐，檐口装饰 | The act or process of fitting or constructing a cornice.  The decorative molding along the top of a wall or building. | The cornicing in that old house is very elaborate. 那栋老房子的檐口装饰非常精致。 | 近义词: molding, trim | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From the Old French "cornise," ultimately from the Latin "cornix" (crow), perhaps referring to the beak-like projection of a cornice.
