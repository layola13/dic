gelsemine:/ˈdʒɛlsɪmiːn/
noun
钩吻素
A highly toxic alkaloid found in the gelsemium plant.
Gelsemine can cause respiratory paralysis in high doses. 钩吻素大剂量服用会导致呼吸麻痹。
Gelsemine is a potent neurotoxin. 钩吻素是一种强效神经毒素。

synonym: alkaloid, neurotoxin
