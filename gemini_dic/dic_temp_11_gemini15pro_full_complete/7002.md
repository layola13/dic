androgynies: /ænˈdrɒdʒɪniz/ /ænˈdrɑːdʒɪniz/
n.
雌雄同体；阴阳人；双性人；男女皆宜的风格
The state or quality of being androgynous; having both masculine and feminine characteristics.
Fashion designers are increasingly promoting androgyny in their clothing lines.  时装设计师越来越多地在他们的服装系列中推广中性风格。
David Bowie cultivated a sense of androgyny in his stage persona. 大卫·鲍伊在他的舞台角色中培养了一种雌雄同体的意味。
Synonyms: unisexuality, hermaphroditism, bisexuality, ambiguity
Antonyms: masculinity, femininity
noun
