outpoison:/aʊtˈpɔɪzən/
verb
以毒攻毒；毒害更甚
to poison someone or something more than another person or thing has already poisoned them or it.
例句：The spy outpoisoned his enemy.  这个间谍比他的敌人更狠毒。

例句：The company tried to outpoison the competition by releasing a more toxic product. 这家公司试图通过发布毒性更强的产品来在毒性方面胜过竞争对手。
近义词：outvenom, surpass in poisoning
反义词：cure, heal
词性：v.
