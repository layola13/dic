cherry: /ˈtʃeri/ (US) /ˈtʃɛri/ (UK)| n.|樱桃；樱桃树；樱桃色；处女膜|A small, round, soft red or yellow fruit with a single hard stone in the middle.  A tree that bears cherries. A bright red colour. |例句：The cherry pie was delicious. 樱桃派真好吃。

The cherry blossoms are beautiful in spring. 春天樱花很美。|近义词：fruit, berry|反义词: (No direct antonym for the fruit.  Could consider "vegetable" as a broad contrast.)|noun


cherry: /ˈtʃeri/ (US) /ˈtʃɛri/ (UK)| adj.|樱桃色的；鲜红色的|Bright red in colour. |例句：She wore a cherry-red lipstick. 她涂了樱桃红色的口红。

He drove a cherry-red sports car. 他开了一辆樱桃红色的跑车。|近义词：crimson, scarlet|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider colours like "pale," "dull," or specific contrasting colours like "teal" or "olive.")|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old North French *cherise (French cerise), from Vulgar Latin *ceresia, cerasia, from Latin cerasus “cherry tree,” from Ancient Greek kerasos.
