offender:/əˈfendər/| n.|罪犯，冒犯者|A person who commits an illegal act or breaks a rule or law.  Someone who causes you to feel upset, annoyed, or resentful.|The offender was sentenced to five years in prison. 罪犯被判五年徒刑。|Synonyms: culprit, criminal, delinquent, wrongdoer|Antonyms: victim, law-abider|noun
