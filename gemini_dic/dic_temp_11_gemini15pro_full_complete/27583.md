canaro:/kəˈnɑːroʊ/
n.
Canary seed; a mixture of seeds fed to caged birds, especially canaries.
Canary seed, or a mixture of seeds for caged birds.  It's derived from the Italian word "canarino," meaning canary.
The pet store sells a special canaro blend for finches. 这家宠物店出售一种特殊的雀类混合饲料。
Synonyms: birdseed, seed mix
Antonyms: None (lacks a direct opposite)
