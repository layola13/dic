eldritch:/ˈɛldrɪtʃ/| adj.|怪异的，奇异的，令人毛骨悚然的|Strange and eerie; suggestive of the supernatural; unearthly and sinister.|例句：The eldritch glow of the swamp made us uneasy. 沼泽怪异的光芒让我们感到不安。|近义词：weird, uncanny, eerie, otherworldly, supernatural, bizarre|反义词：normal, ordinary, mundane|词性:形容词
词源：From Middle English *elriche, from Old English *ylfe, *ælfe (“elf, fairy, incubus”) + *rīċe (“kingdom, realm, domain”).  Essentially meaning "of the realm of elves or fairies," implying a supernatural or otherworldly quality.
