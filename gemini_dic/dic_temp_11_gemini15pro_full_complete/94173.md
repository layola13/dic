impennous:/ɪmˈpɛnəs/
adjective
无翼的；没有羽毛的
lacking wings or feathers; wingless.
The impennous bird was unable to fly. 这只没有翅膀的鸟无法飞行。
近义词:wingless, flightless
反义词:winged, feathered
词性:adjective

impennous:/ɪmˈpɛnəs/
adjective
飞不起来的
unable to fly.
The newly hatched chicks were impennous and relied on their parents for food. 刚孵出的小鸡没有羽毛，无法飞行，依靠父母喂食。
近义词:wingless, flightless
