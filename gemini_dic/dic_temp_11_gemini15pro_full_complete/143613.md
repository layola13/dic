outissued:/ˌaʊtˈɪʃud/
verb
发行过多；过度发放
having issued more of something (such as currency or stock) than is warranted or safe
The company outissued shares, diluting the value of existing holdings.  这家公司过度发行股票，稀释了现有股份的价值。
oversupply, overissue
undersupply
词性：动词
