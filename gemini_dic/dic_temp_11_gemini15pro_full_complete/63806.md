epiderm:/ˈepɪdɜːrm/ (US), /ˈepɪdɜːm/ (UK)| n.|表皮，外皮|The outermost layer of skin or cells.|The sunburn only affected the epiderm. 晒伤只影响了表皮。|Synonyms: cuticle, skin, covering|Antonyms: hypoderm, dermis|Noun

epidermal:/ˌepɪˈdɜːrməl/ (US), /ˌepɪˈdɜːməl/ (UK)| adj.|表皮的|Relating to the epidermis.|Epidermal cells are constantly being shed and replaced. 表皮细胞不断脱落和更新。|Synonyms: cutaneous, dermal|Antonyms: subcutaneous, hypodermal|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *epi-* meaning "upon, on" and *derma* meaning "skin".
