outmanipulate:/ˌaʊtməˈnɪpjuleɪt/| v. |胜过；以策略胜过；巧妙地操纵 |to manipulate (someone) more effectively than they manipulate others; to defeat or win an advantage over someone by being more skillful or clever at using manipulation. |  She outmanipulated her rivals and secured the top position in the company. 她以计谋胜过对手，确保了在公司的最高职位。

|She outmanipulated him in the negotiations and got a much better deal. 她在谈判中比他技高一筹，获得了更好的协议。 | 近义词: outmaneuver, outwit, outslick, outsmart | 反义词: be outmaneuvered, be outwitted |词性:动词
