interradius:/ˌɪntərˈreɪdiəs/
n.|间辐，辐间|The area between two radii of a circle or sphere, especially in biology, the region between the ambulacral areas of an echinoderm.|The interradius of the starfish contained small spines.|近义词:interradial zone|反义词:radius|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin *inter* (between) + *radius* (ray, spoke).
