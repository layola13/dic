nominee:/ˌnɒmɪˈniː/ /ˌnɑːmɪˈniː/
n.
被提名者；候选人
A person who is proposed or formally entered as a candidate for an office or as a recipient of a prize or honor.
The Academy Award nominees were announced this morning. 奥斯卡奖提名名单今天上午公布了。

The nominees for best picture are all excellent films. 最佳影片的提名影片都很优秀。


近义词:candidate, contender, applicant
反义词:non-nominee (not a standard word, but expresses the opposite concept)
词性:noun
