PHRASE->
churn out: 大量生产  The factory churns out thousands of cars each day.  这家工厂每天生产数千辆汽车。
churn rate: 流失率  The company is trying to reduce its customer churn rate.  公司正在努力降低客户流失率。
churn up: 搅起，激起  The boat's propeller churned up the mud.  船的螺旋桨搅起了泥浆。
emotional churning: 情绪波动  He was experiencing a lot of emotional churning after the breakup.  分手后，他经历了很多情绪波动。
churn something over: 仔细考虑  She churned the idea over in her mind.  她在心里反复思考这个想法。


词源：来源于古英语 *ċyrin (未证实), 与荷兰语 kernen 和德语 kirnen 同源，意为“搅动，凝固”。
