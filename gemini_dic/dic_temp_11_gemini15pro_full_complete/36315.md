coholder:/ˈkoʊˌhəʊldər/| n. |共同持有者，共同所有人|A joint holder, especially of an office, property, or title.|Example 1: John and Mary are coholders of the property.  约翰和玛丽是这处房产的共同所有人。

Example 2:  The two sisters are coholders of the family business.  这两姐妹是家族企业的共同所有人。|Synonyms: joint owner, joint tenant, co-owner, sharer|Antonyms: sole owner, sole proprietor|noun
