nonmarkets:/ˌnɒnˈmɑːrkɪts/ | n. | 非市场 |  Institutions and interactions that operate outside of traditional market mechanisms, often involving government, social norms, or other non-monetary exchanges.  Relating to or characteristic of activities not governed by market forces. |  Example: Nonmarkets play a significant role in environmental regulation.  非市场在环境监管中扮演着重要角色。

Example:  Understanding nonmarkets is crucial for businesses operating in regulated industries. 了解非市场对于在受监管行业运营的企业至关重要。 |  Synonyms: non-market institutions, public sector, civil society, informal economy | Antonyms: markets, private sector, free market | noun
