organotrophic:/ɔːrɡænəˈtrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 有机营养的 | Obtaining energy from the oxidation of organic compounds. | Example: Many bacteria are organotrophic, meaning they derive energy from breaking down organic matter. 许多细菌是有机营养的，这意味着它们通过分解有机物获得能量。 | Example: Organotrophic organisms play a vital role in nutrient cycling in ecosystems.  有机营养生物在生态系统的营养循环中起着至关重要的作用。 | Synonym: chemoorganotrophic, heterotrophic | Antonym: lithotrophic, autotrophic | Adjective
