easterliness:/ˈiːstərlɪnɪs/
noun
东风；偏东方向；东向性
The prevailing wind has an easterliness. 盛行风偏东。
The ship's course was corrected for easterliness. 船的航向修正了东向偏差。
Synonyms: eastward tendency, eastward direction, east wind
Antonyms: westerliness
词性: 名词
