malacopterygious:/ˌmæləˌkɒptəˈrɪdʒiəs/| adj.|软鳍鱼类的，辐鳍鱼类的|Having soft, articulated fin rays, characteristic of most bony fishes.|例句：Malacopterygious fishes are the largest group of vertebrates. 软鳍鱼类是最大的脊椎动物类群。

例句：The trout is a malacopterygious fish found in freshwater streams. 鳟鱼是一种在淡水溪流中发现的软鳍鱼。|近义词：soft-finned|反义词: acanthopterygian (spiny-rayed)|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *malakos* (soft) + *pterygion* (fin, little wing)
