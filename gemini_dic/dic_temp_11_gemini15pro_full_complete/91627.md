hyperbarically:/ˌhaɪpərˈbærɪkli/| adv.|高压地，在高压下|in a hyperbaric environment; at a pressure greater than normal atmospheric pressure.  Under conditions of high pressure.|The wound was treated hyperbarically.  伤口在高压下治疗。|近义词: under pressure, pressurized|反义词: hypobarically (at low pressure)|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From hyperbaric + -ally.  Hyperbaric from hyper- (above, excessive) + baric (relating to pressure).  Ultimately from Greek *ὑπέρ (hupér, “over, above”) and βάρος (báros, “weight”).
