naturalising:/ˈnætʃrəlaɪzɪŋ/
v.
使归化；移植；吸收
The process of becoming a citizen of a country that you were not born in.  Also, adapting (a plant or animal) to a new environment.
Many immigrants are naturalising as British citizens. 许多移民正在归化为英国公民。
The botanist successfully naturalised the exotic plant species.  这位植物学家成功地将外来植物物种移植到本地。
近义词:acclimatizing, adapting, assimilating
反义词:repatriating, alienating
词性:动词

naturalization:/ˌnætʃrələˈzeɪʃən/
n.
归化；移植；吸收
The process of becoming a citizen of a country that you were not born in. Also, the adaptation of a plant or animal to a new environment.
She applied for naturalization after living in the country for five years.  在这个国家生活了五年后，她申请了归化。
The naturalization of the species was remarkably swift.  这个物种的适应速度非常快。
近义词:acclimatization, adaptation, assimilation
反义词:repatriation, alienation
词性:名词


词源: From the Latin word "naturalis" meaning "natural," with the suffixes "-ize" and "-ing" or "-ation" added to form the verb and noun forms respectively.
