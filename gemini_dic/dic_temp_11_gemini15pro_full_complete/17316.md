bdellomorpha:/ˌbdɛləˈmɔːrfə/| n. |蛭形轮虫类|A class of rotifers characterized by their ability to survive desiccation and reproduce asexually. They are also known for their long-term evolutionary success without sexual reproduction.|Bdellomorpha are microscopic animals found in freshwater habitats. 蛭形轮虫类是微小的动物，发现于淡水栖息地。|Near synonyms: rotifers, bdelloid rotifers|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
