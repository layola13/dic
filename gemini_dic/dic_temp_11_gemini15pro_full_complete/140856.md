on-the-heels-of:/ɑn ðə hiːlz əv/| prepositional phrase |紧随其后；紧接着|closely following something; soon after something|例句：The second disaster followed on the heels of the first. 第二场灾难紧随第一场灾难之后。|近义词：following, after, shortly after, close behind, in the wake of|反义词:preceding, before, long after|词性:介词短语
