diagrammable:/ˈdaɪəˌɡræməbl/| adj.|可绘制成图表的，可图解的|Something that is diagrammable can be represented or explained using a diagram.  It often refers to sentences or concepts that can be visually broken down to illustrate their structure or relationships.|The sentence "The cat sat on the mat" is easily diagrammable.  这只猫坐在垫子上，这句话很容易用图表表示。|Synonyms:  representable, illustratable, chartable|Antonyms:  abstract, incomprehensible, unanalyzable|Word Type: Adjective
