derivatively:/dɪˈrɪvətɪvli/
adv.
衍生地；派生地；非独创地
in a way that is derived from something else; not original
The music is, for the most part, derivatively dull. 这部音乐作品大部分都是抄袭来的，很枯燥乏味。
近义词: unoriginally, secondarily, imitatively
反义词: originally, innovatively, creatively
词性: 副词

derivatively:/dɪˈrɪvətɪvli/
adjective
衍生的；派生的；非独创的
Characteristic of something derived; unoriginal.
His paintings are rather derivative. 他的画作颇为缺乏创意。
近义词: unoriginal, secondary, imitative
反义词: original, innovative, creative
词性: 形容词


词源:  From derivative + -ly.  Derivative itself comes from Late Latin *derivativus, from Latin derivare "to draw off, divert," from de- "off" + rivus "stream."
