libertarian:/ˌlɪbərˈtɛəriən/ | adj. n. | 自由意志主义的；自由意志主义者 |  One who advocates maximizing individual rights and minimizing the role of the state. |  He's a libertarian who believes in minimal government intervention. 他是一位自由意志主义者，信奉最小限度的政府干预。 |  adj:  individualistic, independent | adj: authoritarian, totalitarian | adjective, noun

libertarian:/ˌlɪbərˈtɛəriən/ | n. | 自由意志主义者 | A person who believes in the doctrine of free will. |  She's a libertarian who believes in individual freedom above all else. 她是一位自由意志主义者，认为个人自由高于一切。 |  n: individualist, free thinker | n: statist, authoritarian | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *libertaire*, from Latin *lībertās* ("freedom") + *-ārius*.
