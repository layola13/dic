nonrebellious:/ˌnɒnrɪˈbɛliəs/ | /ˌnɑːnrɪˈbɛliəs/ | adj. | 不叛逆的，顺从的 | Not rebellious; obedient and compliant. |  Example: The nonrebellious students quietly followed the teacher's instructions.  不叛逆的学生安静地听从老师的指示。

Example:  Her nonrebellious nature made her a favorite with the more traditional teachers. 她不叛逆的天性使她成为较传统老师的宠儿。


近义词: obedient, compliant, docile, submissive, conforming
反义词: rebellious, defiant, insubordinate, disobedient, unruly
词性: adjective
