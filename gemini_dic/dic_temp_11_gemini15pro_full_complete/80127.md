gobshite: /ˈɡɒbʃaɪt/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜，白痴 (offensive)|A highly offensive term used to describe someone considered stupid or foolish.  This word is considered vulgar and should be avoided in polite conversation.| "That gobshite left the keys in the car!" 那个白痴把钥匙留在车里了！ | 近义词：idiot, moron, fool, imbecile, jerk, twit | 反义词: genius, intelligent person (avoid direct antonyms due to offensive nature) | 词性: noun
