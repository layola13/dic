llbs:/ˈel biːˈɛs/ | n. | pounds (weight) |  Abbreviation for pounds, a unit of weight.  Derived from the Latin word "libra" (meaning scales or balance).  Commonly used informally, especially in online contexts. | I need 10 llbs of potatoes. 我需要10磅土豆。|  Example: I bought 5 llbs of apples at the market. 我在市场买了5磅苹果。| synonym: lb, lbs | antonym: kg, kilogram | abbreviation
