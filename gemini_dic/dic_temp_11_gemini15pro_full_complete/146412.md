overfond:/ˌoʊvərˈfɑːnd/| adj.| 过于喜爱;溺爱;|Excessively fond; too affectionate or indulgent.|She's overfond of her cat, treating it like a human child. 她太溺爱她的猫了，把它当作人类小孩一样对待。|He's overfond of sweets, which is probably why he has so many cavities. 他太喜欢吃甜食了，这可能就是他有很多蛀牙的原因。|近义词：doting, indulgent, besotted|反义词：indifferent, detached, uncaring|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From 'over' (meaning "excessive") + 'fond' (meaning "affectionate").  The word 'fond' ultimately derives from the Old English 'fondian,' meaning "to try, test, tempt."  The sense of affection developed later.
