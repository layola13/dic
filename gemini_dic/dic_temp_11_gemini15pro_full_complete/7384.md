animalization:/ˌænɪməlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 动物化；兽性化 |The process of making something or someone seem like an animal, or of giving something or someone animal qualities or characteristics.  This can refer to literal transformations, metaphorical descriptions, or degrading treatment that reduces a person to animal-like status. |  His portrayal of the character emphasized its animalization, highlighting the primal instincts beneath the civilized veneer. 他对这个角色的刻画强调了它的兽性，突出了文明外表下的原始本能。

The animalization of refugees in the media is a dangerous trend that dehumanizes vulnerable individuals.  媒体对难民的动物化描述是一个危险的趋势，它剥夺了弱势群体的尊严。 | synonyms: brutalization, dehumanization, degradation | antonyms: humanization, elevation, ennoblement | noun
