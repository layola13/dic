asphodel:/ˈæsfədɛl/ | n. | 阿福花，日光兰；（希腊神话）阴间的植物 |A plant of the genus Asphodelus, having long, narrow leaves and clusters of small, white or yellow flowers. In Greek mythology, it was associated with the underworld and the Elysian Fields.|  The fields were covered in asphodel.  田野里开满了日光兰。 | Synonyms: daffodil, narcissus | Antonyms: N/A | noun

asphodel:/ˈæsfədɛl/ | adj. |  像阿福花的; 苍白的 |Resembling or characteristic of the asphodel plant, often in reference to a pale or ghostly color.| His face was asphodel in the moonlight. 月光下，他的脸苍白得像日光兰。| Synonyms: pale, wan, ghostly | Antonyms: vibrant, colorful | adjective
