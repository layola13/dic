hausse:/ˈhaʊs/ (US), /hɔːs/ (UK)| n.| 涨价，上涨，上升; (尤指物价)飞涨|A general increase in prices, especially a rapid or substantial increase.|Example 1:  The recent economic boom has led to a significant hausse in property prices. 最近的经济繁荣导致了房地产价格的显著上涨。

Example 2: The stock market experienced a sudden hausse after the positive earnings report.  在积极的收益报告发布后，股市经历了突然上涨。|近义词：increase, rise, surge, upswing, upturn|反义词: decline, decrease, fall, drop, downturn|词性: noun

词源: From French "hausse," literally "rise," from "hausser" "to raise," from Frankish "*hausijan," from Proto-Germanic "*hausijaną."
