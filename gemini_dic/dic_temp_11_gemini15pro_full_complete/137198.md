nonsuit:/ˈnɒnˌsuːt/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌsjuːt/ (UK)| n. v. |不起诉；驳回诉讼；败诉|The judge granted the defendant's motion for a nonsuit. 法官批准了被告的驳回诉讼动议。|例句：The plaintiff was nonsuited for failing to appear in court. 原告因未出庭而被驳回诉讼。|近义词：dismissal, rejection|反义词:prosecution, conviction|词性:名词,动词
Etymology: From Anglo-French *nonsuite, from non- "not" + suite "following, pursuit," ultimately from Latin sequi "to follow."
