assuage: /əˈsweɪdʒ/ /əˈswedʒ/ | v. | 平息，缓和，减轻 | To make (an unpleasant feeling) less intense. | The medicine assuaged his pain.  这药减轻了他的疼痛。 |  He tried to assuage her grief. 他试图抚慰她的悲伤。 | synonyms: relieve, ease, alleviate, mitigate, soothe, mollify, allay | antonyms: aggravate, exacerbate, intensify | verb
