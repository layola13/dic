map:/mæp/ | n. vt. |地图；示意图；绘制地图；详细计划|A diagrammatic representation of an area of land or sea showing physical features, cities, roads, etc.  |She unfolded the map and traced the route with her finger. 她打开地图，用手指沿着路线描摹。| synonyms: chart, plan, diagram | antonyms:  globe (arguably, as a 3D representation) | noun

map:/mæp/ | vt. |绘制地图；详细计划；映射|To represent something on a map; to plan something in detail. |Scientists are mapping the human genome. 科学家们正在绘制人类基因组图。| synonyms: chart, plan, outline | antonyms:  (no direct antonym for the verb form) | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "mappemonde" meaning "map of the world," shortened to "mappe,"  ultimately from Latin "mappa" meaning "napkin, cloth."  The original sense was a representation drawn on cloth.
