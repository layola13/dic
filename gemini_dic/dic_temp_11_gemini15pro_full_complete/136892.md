nonsocially:/nɒnˈsəʊʃəli/
adv.
非社交地，不善交际地
Not in a social manner; not sociably.
He dined nonsocially, preferring to eat alone. 他非社交性地用餐，更喜欢独自一人吃。
近义词: unsociably, asocially
反义词: sociably
词性: 副词

nonsocially:/nɒnˈsəʊʃəli/
adv.
不合群地
In a way that avoids or excludes social interaction.
She behaved nonsocially, ignoring everyone at the party.  她在派对上表现得不合群，无视了所有人。
近义词: unsociably, reclusively
反义词: sociably, gregariously
词性: 副词
