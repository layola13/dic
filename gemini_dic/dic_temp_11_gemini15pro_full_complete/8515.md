anticapitalists:/ˌæntiˈkæpɪtəlɪsts/ | n. | 反资本主义者；反资本主义分子 | People who oppose capitalism. |  The anticapitalists protested the new trade agreement. 反资本主义者抗议新的贸易协定。 | Synonyms: radicals, socialists, communists, revolutionaries | Antonyms: capitalists, conservatives | Noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) + capitalist + -s (plural suffix)

*   anti-: from Greek *anti, meaning "against," "opposite of"
*   capitalist: from capital + -ist, ultimately from Latin *caput, meaning "head," referring to principal sum of money
*   -s: plural marker
