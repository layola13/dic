intends:/ɪnˈtɛndz/| v.|打算，计划，意图|He intends to study abroad next year. 他打算明年出国留学。|近义词：plan, aim, mean, propose|反义词: avoid, neglect, abandon|verb

Etymology->
From Old French entendre "to intend, understand, hear," from Latin intendere "to stretch out, extend, strain, exert, direct one's attention," from in- "toward" + tendere "to stretch." The meaning "to have in mind as a purpose" developed in French and was adopted in English.
