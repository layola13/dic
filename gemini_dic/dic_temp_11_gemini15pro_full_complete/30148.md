celadon:/ˈsɛləˌdɑːn/| n. adj.|青瓷，青瓷色|A pale green colour, typically seen in Chinese pottery.|The vase was a beautiful celadon, reminiscent of jade.|近义词：jade green, seafoam green, mint green|反义词:crimson, scarlet|词性:名词，形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *céladon*, from the name of Céladon, a character in Honoré d'Urfé's pastoral romance *L'Astrée* (1607–27), who wore pale green ribbons.
