outbidders:/ˌaʊtˈbɪdər/
| n. |出价更高者；胜过他人出价者|Someone who offers a higher price than someone else at an auction or in a competitive bidding situation.|The outbidders were disappointed when they lost the auction. 出价更高者在拍卖中失利时感到失望。|Synonyms: higher bidder, winning bidder|Antonyms: underbidder, losing bidder|Noun

outbid:/ˌaʊtˈbɪd/
| vt. |出价高于（某人）；胜过…的出价|To offer a higher price than someone else in an auction or other competitive bidding situation.|She outbid all the other buyers for the antique vase. 她出价高于所有其他买家，买下了那只古董花瓶。|Synonyms: overbid, top someone's bid|Antonyms: underbid|Verb
