non-chronological: /ˌnɒnˌkrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | /ˌnɑːnˌkrɑːnəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 非按时间顺序的 | Not arranged in the order in which events happened. |

The film uses a non-chronological narrative structure, jumping between different periods of the protagonist's life. | 这部电影采用非时间顺序的叙事结构，在主人公人生的不同时期之间跳跃。

The photographs were displayed in a non-chronological order, creating a sense of disorientation. | 照片以非时间顺序排列，营造出一种迷失方向的感觉。


近义词: anachronic, out of sequence |
反义词: chronological, sequential |
词性: adjective |

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non < PIE *ne "not"
chronological (adjective): from Greek khronologikos, from khronos "time" + -logos "collecting"
