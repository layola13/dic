defenced:/dɪˈfɛnst/ | v. | 防御，保卫 |  Past tense and past participle of "defend".  It means to protect from attack or harm. | The city was well defenced against invaders. 这座城市防御入侵者很坚固。| 近义词: protected, guarded, shielded | 反义词: attacked, assaulted | 词性: 动词

defenced:/dɪˈfɛnst/ | adj. | 设防的 | Protected or fortified. Less common than "defended." |  The defenced city held off the siege. 这座设防的城市抵挡住了围攻。| 近义词: fortified, protected | 反义词: undefended, vulnerable | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Middle English *defensen, from Old French defense, from Latin dēfēnsa (“protection, defense”), from dēfendere (“to ward off, defend”), from dē (“away from”) + fendere (“to strike, push”).
