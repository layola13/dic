inter-coordinated:/ˌɪntərkoʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. |相互协调的，互相配合的 |  Arranged to work together smoothly and efficiently.  Derived from "inter-" (between) + "coordinated" (organized to work together). | The inter-coordinated efforts of the various departments ensured the project's success. 各个部门相互协调的努力确保了项目的成功。 |  interconnected, integrated, synchronized | disjointed, fragmented | adjective
