condoner:/kənˈdoʊnər/| n.|宽恕者；容忍者；原谅者|Someone who condones something.  A person who overlooks or forgives an offense, usually a serious one.|The condoner of the crime was never brought to justice.  包庇罪犯的人从未被绳之以法。|Near Synonyms: excuser, apologist, forgiver, pardoner|Antonyms: accuser, critic, denouncer|noun
