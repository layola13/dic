angiography: /ˌændʒiˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. |血管造影术|A medical imaging technique used to visualize the inside of blood vessels and organs, particularly arteries, veins, and the heart chambers. It involves injecting a radiopaque contrast agent into the blood vessels and then taking X-ray images. |The doctor recommended a coronary angiography to check for blockages in the heart arteries. 医生建议做冠状动脉造影来检查心脏动脉是否有阻塞。|
|Synonyms: arteriography, venography|Antonyms: None|Noun
