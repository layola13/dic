cryptogamous: /krɪpˈtɒɡəməs/ /krɪpˈtɑːɡəməs/ | adj. | 隐花植物的，隐花的 | Relating to or belonging to the Cryptogamia, a large group of plants (including ferns, mosses, algae, and fungi) that reproduce by spores and do not have flowers or seeds. |  Example:  Ferns and mosses are cryptogamous plants. 蕨类植物和苔藓植物是隐花植物。

Example:  The study of cryptogamous plants is an important part of botany. 对隐花植物的研究是植物学的重要组成部分。


近义词: spore-bearing, flowerless | 反义词:  phanerogamous (显花植物的) | 词性: adjective
