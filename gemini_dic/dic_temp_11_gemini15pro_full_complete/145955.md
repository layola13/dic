overdistant:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪstənt/
adjective
过于遥远的；关系疏远的；冷淡的
Excessively distant, either physically or emotionally.
例句：Their relationship had become overdistant due to years of living apart.  由于多年的分居，他们的关系变得疏远了。

例句：He maintained an overdistant demeanor, making it difficult to connect with him. 他保持着一种过于冷淡的态度，让人很难与他建立联系。

近义词：remote, aloof, detached, standoffish, reserved
反义词：close, intimate, warm, friendly, approachable
词性: adjective
