cathodic:/kəˈθɒdɪk/ (US) /kəˈθɑːdɪk/ (UK)| adj.|阴极的；负极的|Relating to or occurring at the cathode of an electrochemical cell or electron tube.|The cathodic reaction in this battery produces hydrogen gas.  这个电池中的阴极反应产生氢气。|The metal object was coated using a cathodic process.  这个金属物体是用阴极工艺涂层的。|Synonyms: negative (electrode), anodic (opposite in function, refers to the anode)|Antonyms: anodic|adjective
