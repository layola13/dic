mimetical:/mɪˈmɛtɪk(ə)l/
adj.
模仿的; 拟态的
Characterized by, exhibiting, or of the nature of mimicry.
The chameleon's mimetical abilities allow it to blend in with its surroundings. 变色龙的模仿能力使其能够与周围环境融为一体。
近义词: imitative, mimic, aping
反义词: original, genuine
词性: adjective
