nooks:/nʊks/
|n.|角落，隐蔽处|A quiet secluded or obscure corner.  A small recess, typically in a wall.|例句：The cat curled up in one of the nooks of the sofa. 猫蜷缩在沙发的角落里。

例句：We found a cozy nook in the library to study. 我们在图书馆里找到了一个舒适的角落学习。
|近义词：corners, recesses, crannies, niches|反义词:centers, openings, exposures|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *nok, from Old English *noc (attested in compounds such as ēstnoc (“corner between east and north”)), from Proto-Germanic *nukkaz (“corner, angle”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)neug- (“to bend, turn”).
