orbicula: /ɔrˈbɪkjʊlə/ | n. | 小环; 小盘 | A small disk, or a structure resembling a small disk. | The orbicula is a small calcareous plate in some brachiopods. 小环是某些腕足动物体内的一个小型钙质板。| The archaeologist discovered an ancient orbicula. 考古学家发现了一个古老的小盘。 | synonyms: disk, plate, lamina | antonyms: sphere, globe | noun

orbicular: /ɔrˈbɪkjʊlər/ | adj. | 圆形的; 环形的 | Circular or disk-shaped. | The flower has orbicular petals. 这朵花有圆形的花瓣。 | The artist painted an orbicular design.  艺术家画了一个圆形图案。 | synonyms: circular, round, spherical, disk-shaped | antonyms: linear, rectangular, square | adjective
