actinometric:/ˌæktɪnəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
光化测定的，光能测量的
Relating to or determined by actinometry; employed in or based upon the measurement of the intensity of radiation, especially of sunlight.
The actinometric measurements were crucial for understanding the rate of photosynthesis.  光化测量对于理解光合作用速率至关重要。
The scientist used an actinometric instrument to measure the sun's energy.  科学家使用光化测量仪器来测量太阳的能量。
近义词: radiometric
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

Etymology:  From actino- + -metric.  Actino- comes from the Greek "aktinos" meaning "ray". -metric relates to measurement.
