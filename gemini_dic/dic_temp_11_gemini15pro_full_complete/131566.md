noncommunicativenesses:/nɒnkəˈmjuːnɪkətɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
不愿沟通；不爱说话；沉默寡言
The state of being unwilling to communicate or share information.
His noncommunicativenesses made it difficult to understand his needs. 他不爱说话，很难理解他的需求。
Her noncommunicativenesses worried her family. 她沉默寡言让她的家人很担心。
近义词: reticence, silence, uncommunicativeness
反义词:  communicativeness, loquacity, talkativeness
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
From non- + communicative + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Communicative" comes from Late Latin communicativus, from communicare "to share, impart," from communis "common."
