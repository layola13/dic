evacuative:/ɪˈvækjʊeɪtɪv/
adjective
排泄的; 蒸发的; 放出的
Relating to or causing evacuation, especially the removal of moisture or vapor.
The evacuative cooling system helps regulate the temperature.  蒸发冷却系统有助于调节温度。
The process uses an evacuative method to remove excess water.  这个过程使用蒸发的方法去除多余的水分。
近义词:expelling, discharging, emissive
反义词:retentive, absorbing
词性:adjective
