juglone: /ˈdʒʌɡloʊn/ | n. | 胡桃醌；核桃素 | A toxic chemical compound, 5-hydroxy-1,4-naphthalenedione, found in the leaves, roots, and bark of walnut trees. It inhibits the growth of other plants nearby, a phenomenon known as allelopathy. | The juglone from the black walnut tree stunted the growth of the tomatoes planted nearby. 黑胡桃树分泌的胡桃醌抑制了附近种植的西红柿的生长。 | 近义词: allelochemical | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, as it's a specific chemical compound.  One could consider "growth stimulant" in the context of its effect on other plants) | 词性: noun
