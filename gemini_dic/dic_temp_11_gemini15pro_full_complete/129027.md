NITAAC: /ˈnaɪtæk/ | n. | 国家跨部门采购中心 (National Interagency Acquisition Center) |  A centralized, best-in-class contracting solution that offers streamlined procurement for IT commodities, advisory and assistance services, and IT security.  It provides access to pre-vetted contract holders offering a wide range of IT products and services. |  Example: Agencies can utilize NITAAC to quickly acquire needed IT solutions.  各机构可以利用NITAAC快速获得所需的IT解决方案。| Synonym:  CIO-SP3, SEWP | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but potentially "traditional procurement") | Noun
