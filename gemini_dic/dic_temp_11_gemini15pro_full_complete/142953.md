outacted: /ˌaʊtˈæktɪd/
verb
胜过…的演技；表演得比…好
To perform better than someone else in a play or film.
He was completely outacted by his co-star. 他完全被他的搭档的演技比下去了。
Synonyms: outperformed, surpassed, eclipsed
Antonyms: underperformed
词性: 动词
