outparish:/ˈaʊtpærɪʃ/
noun
外教区; 郊区教区
An outparish is a part of a parish that is geographically separate from the main part, often remote or rural.
The clergyman had to travel miles to conduct services in the outparish.  牧师必须长途跋涉到外教区主持礼拜。
remote parish, outlying district
central parish, inner city parish
n.
