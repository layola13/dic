intensification:/ɪnˌtɛnsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|加强，强化，加剧|the act of making something stronger or more extreme|The intensification of the conflict led to war. 冲突的加剧导致了战争。|Synonyms: strengthening, escalation, amplification, augmentation|Antonyms: weakening, reduction, decrease|noun

intensify:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪ/| v.|加强，增强，加剧|to become or make something greater, stronger, or more extreme|The heat intensified as the day wore on. 随着时间的推移，天气越来越热。|Synonyms: strengthen, escalate, amplify, augment|Antonyms: weaken, reduce, decrease|verb
