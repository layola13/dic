boviform:/ˈboʊvɪfɔːrm/
adjective
牛形的；似牛的
Resembling an ox or cow in form or shape.
The boviform creature lumbered across the field.  这头牛形的生物缓缓穿过田野。
The artist sculpted a boviform statue.  艺术家雕刻了一尊牛形雕像。
近义词:ox-like, cow-like
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider antonyms for "ox" or "cow" such as "small," "weak," etc.)
词性:adjective
词源: From Latin "bos, bovis" (ox, cow) + "-form" (shaped like)
