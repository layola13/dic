drawlatch:/ˈdrɔːlætʃ/| n.|拉簧锁, 簧插销|A spring-loaded latch or bolt, often used on doors or gates, that automatically fastens when closed.|The gate swung shut and the drawlatch clicked into place. 大门砰地关上了，拉簧锁咔哒一声锁上了。|近义词：latch, bolt, catch|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "unlocked mechanism")|Noun
