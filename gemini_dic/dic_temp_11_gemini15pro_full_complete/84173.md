half-proletarian:/ˌhæfˌproʊlɪˈtɛəriən/
| n. adj.| 半无产者，半无产阶级的 |  A person who belongs to the half-proletariat, a social class between the proletariat and the peasantry or petty bourgeoisie, often having some land or other means of production but also relying on wage labor. |  The half-proletarians often struggled to make ends meet, relying on both farming and occasional factory work. 半无产者常常难以维持生计，依靠耕作和偶尔的工厂工作。| semi-proletarian, marginal worker | bourgeois, capitalist | noun, adjective
