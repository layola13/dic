ellipticity:/ɪˌlɪpˈtɪsɪti/| n. |椭圆度，椭圆率|The degree to which a shape deviates from being perfectly circular or spherical.  A measure of how elongated an ellipse is.|The ellipticity of the Earth's orbit is small, meaning it's almost a perfect circle.|Synonyms: eccentricity, oblateness|Antonyms: circularity, sphericity|noun

ellipticity:/ɪˌlɪpˈtɪsɪti/| n. |椭圆率（光学）|A measure of the polarization of light, specifically the ratio of the minor and major axes of the polarization ellipse.|The ellipticity of the laser beam was measured using a polarimeter.|Synonyms: polarization ratio|Antonyms: N/A|noun
