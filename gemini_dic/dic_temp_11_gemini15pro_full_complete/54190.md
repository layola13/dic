disestablishment:/dɪsɪˈstæblɪʃmənt/
n.
撤销官方地位; 解除建制
The act of depriving a church or other religious organization of its official status or privileges.
The disestablishment of the Church of England is a recurring topic of debate.  英国教会的撤销官方地位是一个反复出现的辩论话题。
The disestablishment of the monarchy led to the formation of a republic.  君主制的废除导致了共和国的成立。
近义词：abolition, termination, annulment
反义词：establishment, founding, institution
词性：名词
