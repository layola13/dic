audited:/ˈɔːdɪtɪd/| adj.|审计过的，已审核的|Having been officially examined and verified.  The financial records were audited by an independent firm.|例句：The audited accounts showed a significant profit.  审计后的账目显示出可观的利润。

例句：The company's audited financial statements were released to the public.  公司审计后的财务报表已向公众发布。|近义词：examined, checked, verified, reviewed|反义词:unaudited|词性:形容词
