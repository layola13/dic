longimanous:/ˌlɒŋɪˈmeɪnəs/ | adj. | 长手的，长臂的 | Having long hands or arms. |  The longimanous gibbon swung effortlessly through the trees.  长臂猿轻松地在树间荡来荡去。 | Synonyms: long-armed, long-handed | Antonyms: short-armed, short-handed | adjective

Etymology: From Latin *longimanus*, from *longus* (long) + *manus* (hand).
