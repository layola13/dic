feignedness:/ˈfeɪndnɪs/
n.
假装；虚伪
the quality or state of being feigned; pretense.
Her feignedness was obvious to everyone in the room. 房间里每个人都看穿了她的假装。
近义词：pretense, artificiality, insincerity, dissimulation
反义词：genuineness, sincerity, authenticity, candor
词性: 名词

feign:/feɪn/
v.
假装；装作
to give a false appearance of : simulate
He feigned illness to avoid going to school. 他装病不去上学。
近义词：pretend, simulate, sham, affect
反义词：be honest, be genuine
词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *feinednes, derived from Old French feint, past participle of feindre “to feign, pretend,” from Latin fingere "to shape, form, invent, feign."
