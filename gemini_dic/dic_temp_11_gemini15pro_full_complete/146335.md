overfertilizes: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrtəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度施肥
Applies too much fertilizer to (soil or plants).
The gardener overfertilizes his roses, causing them to wilt. 园丁给他的玫瑰施肥过多，导致它们枯萎。
She overfertilized her lawn, and now it's burnt. 她给草坪施肥过多，现在烧焦了。
