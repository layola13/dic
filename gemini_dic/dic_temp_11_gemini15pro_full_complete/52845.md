dimensionality:/ˌdɪmɛnʃəˈnælɪti/ | n. | 维度；次元 | The quality of having a particular number of dimensions.  It refers to the number of independent variables or parameters needed to specify a point or state within a given space or system. |  A square has two dimensions, while a cube has three. 正方形是二维的，而立方体是三维的。


The dimensionality of the problem made it difficult to solve. 这个问题的维度使其难以解决。


近义词: dimension, size, magnitude | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider concepts like "lack of dimension," "formlessness," or "simplicity") | 词性: noun
