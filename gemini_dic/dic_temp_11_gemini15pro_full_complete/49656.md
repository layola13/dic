demirelievo:/ˌdɛmi rɪˈliːvoʊ/ /ˌdɛmi rɪˈliːvəʊ/
n.|半浮雕|Sculptural relief in which the figures project slightly from the background. Figures in demirelievo appear to be raised about half their circumference from the background.|The artist specialized in demirelievo carvings of mythological scenes.  这位艺术家擅长神话场景的半浮雕雕刻。|bas-relief, low relief|high relief|noun
