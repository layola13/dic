deshambo:/deɪˈʃæmboʊ/ /dəˈʃæmboʊ/
noun
剪刀石头布（非正式）
A game similar to "Rock, Paper, Scissors," where "rock" crushes "scissors," "scissors" cuts "paper," and "paper" covers "rock."  Often used informally and regionally, especially in the Pacific Northwest of the United States.
例句：Let's settle this with a game of deshambo.  我们用剪刀石头布来解决这个问题吧。

例句：I always choose rock in deshambo. 我玩剪刀石头布的时候总是出石头。

近义词：Rock, Paper, Scissors; roshambo
反义词：(None)
词性:n.
