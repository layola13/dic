denuding:/dɪˈnjuːdɪŋ/
| v. |剥夺，使裸露|The process of stripping something bare, especially of vegetation or natural resources.  The logging company is denuding the forest of its ancient trees.|例句：The flood denuded the land, leaving only bare rock. 洪水冲走了土地上的植被，只留下了裸露的岩石。

例句：Overgrazing is denuding the grasslands of their protective cover. 过度放牧正在剥夺草原的保护层。|近义词：stripping, baring, uncovering, exposing|反义词：covering, clothing, protecting|词性：动词
