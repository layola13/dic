albeda: /ælˈbiːdə/ | n. | 反照率 | The proportion of the incident light or radiation that is reflected by a surface, typically that of a planet or moon. | The albedo of fresh snow is high, reflecting most sunlight back into space. 新鲜雪的反照率很高，将大部分阳光反射回太空。| synonyms: reflectivity, reflectance | antonyms: absorptivity | noun
