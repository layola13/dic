outwater:/ˈaʊtwɔːtər/
noun
排水沟，出水口
A channel or conduit for carrying water away from a structure or area.
Rainwater flows through the outwater and into the street. 雨水流过排水沟，流到街上。
Synonyms: drain, outlet, spillway, waterway
Antonyms: intake, inlet
