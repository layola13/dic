equicrural:/ˌiːkwɪˈkrʊərəl/| adj.|等边的，等腰的|Having equal legs or sides, especially (of a triangle) having two equal sides.|An equicrural triangle has two sides of equal length.|近义词：isosceles|反义词：scalene|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *aequicruris*, from aequi- 'equal' + cruris genitive of crus 'leg'.
