balling:/ˈbɔːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 极好的，非常棒的；（非正式）哭泣的 | extremely good, excellent; (informal) crying heavily |  例句：That concert was balling! 那场音乐会真是太棒了！| 近义词：awesome, excellent, fantastic, amazing, great, superb, wonderful, brilliant | 反义词：terrible, awful, horrible, dreadful | 词性: 形容词

balling:/ˈbɔːlɪŋ/ | v. |  (非正式) 哭泣 | (informal) crying heavily | 例句：She was balling her eyes out after the breakup. 分手后她哭得很伤心。 | 近义词：crying, weeping, sobbing | 反义词：laughing, rejoicing | 词性: 动词


词源: balling (adj.) likely originates from African American Vernacular English (AAVE).  Its meaning of "excellent" may be related to the idea of "having a ball," meaning having a great time.  balling (v.) is simply the present participle of the verb "to ball," which in this context means "to cry."
