cultivars:/ˈkʌltɪˌvɑːrz/
n.
栽培品种
A plant variety that has been produced in cultivation by selective breeding.
This nursery specializes in growing rare cultivars of roses.  这家苗圃专门种植稀有品种的玫瑰。
synonyms: variety, strain, breed, hybrid
antonyms: wild type, native species
noun
