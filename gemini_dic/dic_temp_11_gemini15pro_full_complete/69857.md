ferromagnetic:/ˌfɛroʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|铁磁的|Of or relating to a substance or body capable of being magnetized by an external magnetic field and of retaining magnetism when the field is removed.|Iron, nickel, and cobalt are ferromagnetic materials. 铁、镍和钴是铁磁性材料。|Synonyms: magnetic, magnetizable|Antonyms: diamagnetic, paramagnetic|adjective

ferromagnetic:/ˌfɛroʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk/| n.|铁磁体|A substance or body that is ferromagnetic.|The ferromagnetic was attracted to the magnet.  这个铁磁体被磁铁吸引了。|Synonyms: magnet|Antonyms: non-magnet|noun
