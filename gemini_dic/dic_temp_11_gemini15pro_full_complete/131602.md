noncomplacent:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpleɪs(ə)nt/ | adj. | 不自满的，不满足的 | Not complacent; not satisfied with the current situation and eager to improve. |  The noncomplacent athlete continued to train even after winning the championship.  这位不自满的运动员即使在赢得冠军后也继续训练。

The team remained noncomplacent despite their early successes. 尽管早期取得了成功，团队仍然没有自满。

| 近义词:  dissatisfied, restless, driven, ambitious, eager | 反义词: complacent, satisfied, content, smug | 词性: adjective
