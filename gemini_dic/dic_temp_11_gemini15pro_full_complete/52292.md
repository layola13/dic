dichromats:/ˈdaɪkrəʊmæts/ | n. | 二色视者；二色性色觉者 | People or animals who have dichromacy, a form of color blindness in which one or more of the three basic color mechanisms are absent or not functioning.  They have only two types of functioning cone cells in their eyes. | Dichromats are unable to distinguish as many colors as people with normal vision. 二色视者无法像视力正常的人一样区分那么多颜色。|  Dogs are dichromats and see the world in shades of blue and yellow. 狗是二色视者，它们眼中的世界是蓝色和黄色的。 | Near synonyms: color-blind individuals,  people with color vision deficiency | Antonyms: trichromats, tetrachromats | noun

dichromat:/ˈdaɪkrəʊmæt/ | n. | 重铬酸盐 | A salt containing the divalent anion Cr₂O₇²⁻. | Potassium dichromate is a strong oxidizing agent. 重铬酸钾是一种强氧化剂。| Sodium dichromate is commonly used in leather tanning. 重铬酸钠通常用于皮革鞣制。 | Near synonyms: bichromate | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
