austerely:/ɔːˈstɪrli/ | adv. | 严厉地，严格地，简朴地 | In a stern and serious manner; without excess or ornamentation. | She looked at him austerely. 她严厉地看了他一眼。| severely, strictly, simply | leniently, luxuriously | adverb

austerity:/ɔːˈstɛrɪti/ | n. | 严厉，严格，简朴 | Sternness or severity of manner or attitude; extreme plainness and simplicity of style or appearance; conditions of enforced or extreme economy. | The austerity of the wartime years was difficult for everyone. 战时岁月的艰苦对每个人来说都很艰难。| severity, strictness, simplicity | leniency, luxury | noun
