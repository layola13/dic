bandaged:/ˈbændɪdʒd/| adj.|包扎的，绑着绷带的|Having a bandage or bandages applied.|Her bandaged arm was in a sling. 她包扎着的手臂用吊带吊着。|近义词：dressed, covered, bound|反义词:unbandaged, exposed|adjective

bandage:/ˈbændɪdʒ/| n. vt.|绷带；用绷带包扎|A strip of material used to bind a wound; to wrap a bandage around a wound.|The nurse bandaged his injured leg.  护士用绷带包扎了他受伤的腿。|近义词：dressing, wrap, bind|反义词: uncover, expose|noun, verb
