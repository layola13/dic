blazoner:/ˈbleɪzənər/ | n. |纹章官，纹章描述者；宣扬者|A person who designs or describes coats of arms; someone who proclaims or publicizes widely.|The blazoner carefully described the intricate details of the family crest. 纹章官仔细地描述了家族徽章的复杂细节。| The company hired a blazoner to spread the news of their new product. 公司聘请了一位宣扬者来传播他们新产品的消息。|Synonyms: herald, announcer, proclaimer|Antonyms: silencer, suppressor|noun
