impairing:/ɪmˈpɛərɪŋ/
| adj. | 损害的，削弱的 | Weakening or damaging something, especially a faculty or function. |  The illness was impairing her ability to walk. 这种疾病正在削弱她行走的能力。| detrimental, harmful, damaging | beneficial, helpful | adjective
