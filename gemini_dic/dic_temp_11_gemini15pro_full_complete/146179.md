overexcite:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsaɪt/| v. |过度兴奋，过分激动|To cause (someone) to become overly excited or agitated.|The loud music and flashing lights overexcited the children. 喧闹的音乐和闪烁的灯光让孩子们过度兴奋。|Synonyms: agitate, stimulate, inflame|Antonyms: calm, soothe, tranquilize|verb

overexcited:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsaɪtɪd/| adj. |过度兴奋的，过分激动的|Excessively or unduly excited.|The children were overexcited about going to the amusement park. 孩子们对去游乐园感到非常兴奋。|Synonyms: thrilled, exhilarated, stimulated|Antonyms: calm, relaxed, composed|adjective
