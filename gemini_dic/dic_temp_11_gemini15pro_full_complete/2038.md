aculeated:/əˈkjuːlieɪtɪd/
adjective
尖刺的，有刺的
Having a sharp point; prickly.
例句：The aculeated leaves of the holly bush can be quite painful. 冬青灌木丛带刺的叶子可能会很疼。

例句：Many cacti are aculeated, protecting themselves from herbivores. 许多仙人掌都有刺，以保护自己免受食草动物的侵害。

近义词：prickly, thorny, spiny, barbed
反义词：smooth, soft

词性: adjective

词源: From Latin aculeatus, past participle of aculeare (“to furnish with prickles”), from aculeus (“a prickle, sting”).
