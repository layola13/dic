kirchner:/ˈkɪrçnər/ | n. | 基什内尔 |  Referring to either Néstor Kirchner (1950-2010) or Cristina Fernández de Kirchner, prominent Argentine politicians.  Néstor Kirchner served as President of Argentina from 2003 to 2007, and Cristina Fernández de Kirchner, his wife, served as President from 2007 to 2015 and again from 2019 to 2023.  The term can also refer to their political era, often referred to as Kirchnerism. |  The Kirchner years saw significant social and economic changes in Argentina. |  近义词: Kirchnerism (when referring to their political ideology),  Argentine politicians (a broader category) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could potentially list opposing political figures or ideologies depending on context) | noun
