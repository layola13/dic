misdirect:/ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛkt/ /ˌmɪsdaɪˈrekt/
verb
误导，指错方向
to send (someone or something) to the wrong place or in the wrong direction
The letter was misdirected and never reached its destination. 这封信被寄错了地址，从未到达目的地。
misguide, mislead
guide, direct
词性: 动词

misdirect:/ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛkt/ /ˌmɪsdaɪˈrekt/
noun
误导
the act of misdirecting
The package's misdirection caused a significant delay. 包裹的投递错误导致了严重的延误。
misguidance
guidance
词性: 名词
