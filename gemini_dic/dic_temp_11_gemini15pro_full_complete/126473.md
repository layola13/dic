mythologically:/ˌmɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb
神话地；神话学地
In a way that relates to mythology; from the point of view of mythology.
The hero was mythologically linked to the sun god.  这位英雄在神话中与太阳神有关联。
The story is mythologically significant.  这个故事在神话学上意义重大。
近义词:allegorically, symbolically, fabulously
反义词:historically, factually, realistically
词性:adverb

词源:
From mythology + -ally.  来自 mythology（神话）+ -ally（副词后缀）。
