duratively:/ˈdjʊərətɪvli/ | adv. |持续地，持久地|In a durative manner; over a period of time.  It describes actions or states that continue for a noticeable duration. |The pain persisted duratively for several hours. 疼痛持续了好几个小时。|近义词: continuously, persistently, constantly, enduringly |反义词: briefly, momentarily, transiently |词性:副词
词源: From "durative" + "-ly." "Durative" comes from Late Latin "dūrātīvus," from Latin "dūrāre" (to harden, last, endure), from "dūrus" (hard).
