flocculence:/ˈflɒkjʊləns/ (US) /ˈflɑːkjələns/ (UK)| n. |絮凝，絮状，绒毛状|The state of being flocculent; a cloud-like mass of loosely aggregated particles.  A soft, woolly, or fluffy appearance or texture.|The flocculence of the clouds made them look like cotton candy.|近义词：fluffiness, wooliness, cloudiness, aggregation|反义词: clarity, smoothness, compactness|词性: noun

flocculent:/ˈflɒkjʊlənt/ (US) /ˈflɑːkjələnt/ (UK)| adj. |絮凝的，絮状的，绒毛状的|Having a fluffy or woolly appearance; resembling a loose mass of aggregated particles.|The flocculent precipitate formed slowly in the beaker.|近义词：fluffy, woolly, cloudy, fleecy|反义词: clear, smooth, compact|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *flocculentus*, from *floccus* ("tuft of wool, flock"), related to *vellus* ("fleece").  The ending *-ulentus* forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "having the quality of."
