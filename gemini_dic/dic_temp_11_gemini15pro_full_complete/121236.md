miserableness:/ˈmɪzərəb(ə)lnəs/| n.|悲惨，痛苦，可怜|The miserableness of their living conditions was appalling. 他们的生活条件之悲惨令人震惊。|例句：The refugees' miserableness was evident in their gaunt faces and tattered clothes.  难民们憔悴的面容和破烂的衣服显露出了他们的悲惨处境。

例句：He couldn't bear the miserableness of being alone any longer. 他再也无法忍受孤独的痛苦了。|近义词：misery, wretchedness, unhappiness, desolation|反义词: happiness, joy, bliss|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *miserablenesse, from miserable +‎ -ness. Equivalent to miserable +‎ -ness.
