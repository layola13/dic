outwaiting:/ˌaʊtˈweɪtɪŋ/
verb
比…等待更久; 比…熬得更久
waiting out someone or something means waiting longer than them or it.
He outwaited his rivals and eventually became CEO. 他比他的竞争对手熬得更久，最终成为了首席执行官。
Synonyms: outlast, outstay
Antonyms: surrender, give in
词性: 动词
