jacking:/ˈdʒækɪŋ/| n. |顶起，千斤顶，抢劫 |The act of lifting with a jack. Also, a slang term for stealing, especially carjacking. |They were jacking up the car to change a tire. 他们正在用千斤顶顶起汽车来换轮胎。  The police reported a rise in carjackings. 警方报告称劫车案有所上升。|近义词：lifting, boosting, robbing |反义词: lowering |词性:名词/动名词
