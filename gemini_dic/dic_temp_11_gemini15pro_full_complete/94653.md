impressionistically:/ɪmˈprɛʃənɪstɪkli/
adv.
印象主义地；给人印象深刻地
In a way that resembles or suggests the style of Impressionism, especially by emphasizing overall impression rather than accurate representation of detail.
She described the scene impressionistically, capturing the feeling of the moment rather than specific details. 她印象式地描述了这个场景，捕捉了那一刻的感觉而不是具体的细节。
The painting was done impressionistically, with short brushstrokes and vibrant colors. 这幅画是用印象派的风格完成的，笔触短促，色彩鲜艳。

Synonyms: suggestively, evocatively, atmospherically
Antonyms: realistically, precisely, accurately
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From impressionist + -ic + -ally.  Impressionist derives from French impressionniste, from impression "impression," ultimately from Latin impressio "a pressing into, imprint," from imprimere "to press into," from in- "in" + premere "to press."
