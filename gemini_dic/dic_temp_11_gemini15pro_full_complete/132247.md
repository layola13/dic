nondecisivenesses:/ˌnɒndɪˈsaɪsɪvnɪsɪz/
noun
优柔寡断，犹豫不决
The state or quality of being unable to make decisions.
His nondecisivenesses cost him the promotion. 他的优柔寡断让他失去了晋升的机会。
indecision, hesitancy, vacillation
decisiveness, resoluteness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + decisiveness.  Decisiveness comes from decisive, from Late Latin dēcīsus, past participle of dēcīdere (to decide), from de- (down, from) + caedere (to cut).
