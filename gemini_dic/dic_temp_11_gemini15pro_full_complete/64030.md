epithelial:/ˌɛpɪˈθiːliəl/ | adj. | 上皮的，上皮组织的 | Relating to or consisting of epithelium. | The epithelial cells lining the lungs are crucial for gas exchange. 肺部内壁的上皮细胞对于气体交换至关重要。 |  近义词: epidermal | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "connective tissue" in some contexts) | 词性: adjective

epithelium:/ˌɛpɪˈθiːliəm/ | n. | 上皮，上皮组织 | The thin tissue forming the outer layer of a body's surface and lining the alimentary canal and other hollow structures. |  The epithelium of the skin protects the body from the external environment.  皮肤的上皮保护身体免受外部环境的侵害。| 近义词:  lining, tissue | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
