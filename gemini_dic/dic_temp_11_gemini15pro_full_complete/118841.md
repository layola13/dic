ETYMOLOGY->
From metaphorical + -ness.  Metaphorical derives from metaphor, which comes from the Greek μεταφορά (metaphorá), meaning "transfer," from μετα- (meta-), "over, across" + φέρειν (pherein), "to carry, bear."  The suffix -ness forms nouns indicating a state, quality, or condition.
