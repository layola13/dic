intertissued:/ˌɪntərˈtɪʃuːd/
adjective
交织的，交错的
woven together, intermingled
例句：The tapestry was intertissued with threads of gold and silver. 这幅挂毯用金线和银线交织而成。

例句：Her life was intertissued with moments of joy and sorrow. 她的生活交织着快乐和悲伤的时刻。

近义词：interwoven, intertwined, interlaced
反义词：separated, disentangled
词性：adjective

词源：
From inter- + tissue + -ed.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Tissue" refers to a woven fabric or a network of cells. "-ed" is a suffix that forms adjectives from verbs. Therefore, "intertissued" literally means "woven between" or "woven among."
