judaist:/ˈdʒuːdeɪɪst/ | n. |犹太教徒；犹太主义者|A person who practices or adheres to Judaism.  |例句：He is a devout Judaist and observes all the religious holidays. 他是一位虔诚的犹太教徒，并遵守所有的宗教节日。

例句：She identified as a Judaist, although she didn't attend synagogue regularly. 她认为自己是犹太教徒，尽管她不经常去犹太教堂。|近义词：Jew, Hebrew, Israelite |反义词:Gentile |词性:noun
