hemiplegy: /ˌhɛmɪˈpliːdʒi/ (US), /ˌhɛmɪˈpliːdʒi/ (UK)| n. |偏瘫，半身不遂|Paralysis of one side of the body, typically as a result of stroke affecting the opposite side of the brain.|After the stroke, she suffered from hemiplegy and could not move her left arm and leg.  中风后，她患上了偏瘫，无法移动左臂和左腿。

The hemiplegy affected his right side, making it difficult for him to write. 偏瘫影响了他的右侧身体，使他难以书写。


近义词: paralysis, paresis|反义词: none|词性: noun
