containerized:/kənˈteɪnəraɪzd/
adj.
集装箱化的，容器化的
packed and transported in a container, especially for shipping
例句：Containerized shipping has revolutionized international trade. 集装箱运输彻底改变了国际贸易。

例句：The containerized cargo was easily loaded onto the ship. 集装箱货物很容易装上船。

近义词：packaged, boxed, crated
反义词：unpackaged, loose, bulk
词性: adjective


词源:
From container + -ize + -ed.  "Container" itself comes from "contain," which ultimately derives from the Latin "continere," meaning "to hold together."
