grouchiness:/ˈɡraʊtʃinəs/| n.|脾气暴躁，抱怨，牢骚|A state of being irritable and complaining; bad temper.|He woke up this morning with a terrible case of grouchiness. 他今天早上醒来脾气非常暴躁。|近义词：grumpiness, irritability, crankiness, surliness|反义词：cheerfulness, good humor, happiness|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From grouchy + -ness.  Grouchy itself comes from the verb "grouse" (to complain), which has uncertain origins, possibly related to the Old French word "groucher" (to grumble).
