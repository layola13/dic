dissuades:/dɪˈsweɪdz/
verb
劝阻，阻止
To persuade someone not to take a particular course of action.
His father dissuaded him from joining the army.  他的父亲劝他不要参军。
近义词:deter, discourage, advise against
反义词:persuade, encourage, urge
词性:v.

dissuades:/dɪˈsweɪdz/
Etymology: From Latin dissuadere, from dis- "away" + suadere "to advise."
词源：源自拉丁语 dissuadere，由 dis- “离开” + suadere “建议” 组成。
