oppressiveness:/əˈprɛsɪvnəs/
|n.|压抑，沉闷，高压|The oppressiveness of the humid heat was stifling.  潮湿的暑热令人窒息。|近义词：tyranny, despotism, repression, severity, harshness|反义词：freedom, liberty, leniency, gentleness|词性:名词

oppressive:/əˈprɛsɪv/
|adj.|压抑的，沉闷的，高压的|The atmosphere in the room was oppressive. 房间里的气氛很压抑。|近义词：tyrannical, despotic, repressive, severe, harsh|反义词：free, liberating, lenient, gentle|词性:形容词

oppress:/əˈprɛs/
|v.|压迫，压制，使烦恼|The government oppressed the people with high taxes. 政府用高额税收压迫人民。|近义词：subjugate, suppress, repress, tyrannize|反义词：liberate, free, empower|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *oppressīvus, from Latin oppressus (past participle of opprimere “to press against, suppress”), from ob- (“against”) + premere (“to press”).
