kissingcrust:/ˈkɪsɪŋ krʌst/
n.|接吻壳，连底壳|The bottom crust of a double-crust pie that bubbles up and touches (or "kisses") the top crust during baking.|例句：The pie had a perfect kissingcrust, showing it was baked just right. 这个派的连底壳烤得恰到好处。|近义词：bottom crust, pie crust|反义词:|名词
