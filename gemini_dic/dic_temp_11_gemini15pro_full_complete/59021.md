dwalin:/ˈdwɔːlɪn/| n. |德瓦林|A dwarf in J.R.R. Tolkien's The Hobbit, one of the thirteen dwarves who accompany Bilbo Baggins on his journey to the Lonely Mountain.  Dwalin is Balin's younger brother and is known for his blue beard and gold belt. The name likely comes from Old Norse mythology.|Dwalin played his viola and sang a song about gold. 德瓦林演奏了他的中提琴，唱了一首关于黄金的歌。|近义词:dwarf, companion|反义词:elf, enemy|词性:noun
