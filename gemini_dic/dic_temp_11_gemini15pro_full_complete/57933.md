drinkability:/ˌdrɪŋkəˈbɪləti/ | n. |适饮性，易饮性|The quality of a beverage that makes it pleasant and easy to drink.  This encompasses factors like smoothness, flavor balance, and lack of harshness. | This wine has excellent drinkability.  这款酒非常易饮。|近义词：palatability, potability |反义词:  unpalatability |词性:noun

drinkabilities:/ˌdrɪŋkəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |适饮性（复数）|The plural form of "drinkability," referring to the qualities of multiple beverages that make them pleasant and easy to drink. |The drinkabilities of these beers vary greatly. 这些啤酒的适饮性差异很大。|近义词：palatabilities, potabilities |反义词: unpalatabilities|词性:noun
