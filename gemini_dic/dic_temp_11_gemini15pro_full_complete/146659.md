overhastily:/ˌoʊvərˈheɪstɪli/| adv.|过于匆忙地，草率地|Done or acting too quickly, without sufficient thought or care. |He overhastily agreed to the terms of the contract and later regretted it. 他过于草率地同意了合同条款，后来后悔了。|She overhastily packed her bags and forgot her passport. 她匆忙收拾行李，忘记了护照。|近义词：hurriedly, rashly, precipitately|反义词：carefully, deliberately, thoughtfully|词性: 副词
