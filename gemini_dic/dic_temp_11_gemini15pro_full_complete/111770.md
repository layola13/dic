lodging:/ˈlɒdʒɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈlɑːdʒɪŋ/ (US)| n. |住宿，寄宿（lodgings的单数形式，不常用）|The act of providing or the state of being accommodated with temporary housing.|He found lodging at a nearby inn. 他在附近的小旅馆找到了住处。|Synonyms: accommodation, shelter|Antonyms: eviction, expulsion|noun
