kain:/keɪn/ | n. | 凯恩（人名）; (苏格兰)租金，税金；(古)杀人，凶杀 | A male given name, derived from the surname, which is of uncertain origin, possibly from a Gaelic word meaning "tribute" or "rent."  Can also refer to the biblical Cain, who murdered his brother Abel. |  例句：Cain is a popular name in Scotland.  凯恩在苏格兰是一个很受欢迎的名字。

例句：He refused to pay the kain demanded by the laird. 他拒绝支付地主索要的租金。 | 近义词：(For the "rent" meaning) tribute, levy, tax | 反义词: (For the "rent" meaning)  None.  (For the "biblical meaning) redemption, forgiveness | 词性: noun
