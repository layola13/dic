dayflower:/ˈdeɪˌflaʊər/| n. |鸭跖草，鸭跖草属植物|A plant of the genus Commelina, typically having blue flowers.  These plants are common weeds in many areas.|例句：The dayflower bloomed brightly in the morning sun.  鸭跖草在早晨的阳光下灿烂地开着花。

例句：Dayflowers are known for their delicate blue petals. 鸭跖草以其精致的蓝色花瓣而闻名。|近义词：Asiatic dayflower, spiderwort|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
