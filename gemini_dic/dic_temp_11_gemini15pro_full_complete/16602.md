barometer:/bəˈrɒmɪtər/ (UK) /bəˈrɑːmɪtər/ (US)| n.|气压计；晴雨表；指标|An instrument measuring atmospheric pressure, used especially in forecasting the weather and determining altitude.|The barometer is falling, which indicates that rain is coming. 气压计正在下降，这表明要下雨了。|synonyms: gauge, indicator, measure, index|antonyms: N/A|noun

barometric:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ (UK) /ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ (US)| adj.|气压的；晴雨表的|Relating to or measured by a barometer.|Barometric pressure is a good indicator of weather changes. 气压是天气变化的良好指标。|synonyms: atmospheric|antonyms: N/A|adjective
