granites:/ˈɡrænɪts/ | n. |花岗岩；坚毅；冷酷无情|A very hard, crystalline, igneous rock, consisting mainly of quartz, feldspar, and mica, used extensively in building.|The kitchen countertops were made of polished granite. 厨房的台面是用抛光花岗岩做的。|Synonyms:stone, rock|Antonyms: softness, fragility|Noun
