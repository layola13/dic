haggish:/ˈhæɡɪʃ/| adj.|像巫婆的；丑陋的；脾气坏的|Resembling or characteristic of a hag; ugly and bad-tempered.|The old woman had a haggish appearance, with a hooked nose and warts on her chin.  这个老妇人有着巫婆般的外貌，鹰钩鼻，下巴上还有疣。|Synonyms: witchlike, shrewish, unpleasant, repulsive|Antonyms: beautiful, attractive, pleasant, agreeable|adjective

haggish:/ˈhæɡɪʃ/| adj.|吝啬的；贪婪的|Miserly; greedy.|She was known for her haggish behavior, always hoarding her money and refusing to share. 她因其吝啬的行为而闻名，总是囤积金钱，拒绝分享。|Synonyms: miserly, greedy, stingy, parsimonious|Antonyms: generous, charitable, altruistic|adjective
