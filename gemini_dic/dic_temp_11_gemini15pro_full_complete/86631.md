helixes:/ˈhiːlɪksiːz/ |n.|螺旋，螺旋线|A helix is a spiral shape, like a corkscrew or a spring.  Helixes is the plural form of helix. |DNA molecules form a double helix. DNA分子形成双螺旋结构。|The bacteria are helix-shaped. 这些细菌呈螺旋状。| synonyms: spirals, coils, whorls| antonyms: lines, straights | noun

helixes:/ˈhiːlɪksiːz/ |n.|螺旋桨|Helixes can also refer to the rotating blades of a helicopter or propeller (less common).|The helicopter's helixes whirred loudly. 直升机的螺旋桨呼呼作响 (不太常见用法)。|The ship's helixes churned the water. 船的螺旋桨搅动着水。(不太常见用法)。|synonyms: propellers, rotors| antonyms: N/A | noun
