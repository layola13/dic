batch:/bætʃ/| n.| 一批，一炉，一组 |A quantity or number of things considered as a group or produced at one time.| The baker made a large batch of cookies.  面包师烤了一大炉饼干。| synonyms: group, lot, set | antonyms: individual, single | noun

batch:/bætʃ/| vt.| 分批处理 | To process or deal with (something) in batches.| The data is batched and processed overnight.  数据被分批处理，通宵进行。| synonyms: group, bundle | antonyms: separate, individualize | verb
