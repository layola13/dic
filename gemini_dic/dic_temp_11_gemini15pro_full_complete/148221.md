oversights:/ˈoʊvərˌsaɪts/ | n. |疏忽，失察|An oversight is a mistake made because of a failure to notice something.| Due to an oversight, I forgot to attach the file to the email. 由于疏忽，我忘记把文件附加到电子邮件上了。 |synonyms: lapse, error, mistake, blunder, slip, omission, failure, neglect | antonyms: attention, care, diligence, meticulousness| noun


oversight:/ˈoʊvərˌsaɪt/ | n. |监督，监管|Oversight is the act of watching or directing something.| The committee has oversight of the project. 委员会负责监督这个项目。| synonyms: supervision, management, control, inspection, monitoring | antonyms: neglect, disregard | noun
