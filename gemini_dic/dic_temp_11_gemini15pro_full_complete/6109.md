amerinds:/əˈmɛrɪndz/
|n.|美洲印第安人|A term used to refer to the indigenous peoples of the Americas.  Now largely considered outdated and potentially offensive due to its association with colonialism.  It is generally preferred to use more specific and respectful terms like "Native American," "Indigenous peoples of the Americas," or the names of specific tribes.|Many older texts refer to the amerinds of North America.  许多古老的文献提到了北美的美洲印第安人。|近义词：Native Americans, American Indians, Indigenous peoples of the Americas|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
