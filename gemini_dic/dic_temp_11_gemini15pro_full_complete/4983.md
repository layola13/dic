alkaloidal:/ˌæl.kəˈlɔɪ.dəl/ | adj. |生物碱的；似生物碱的 |  Having the properties of or containing alkaloids. | The chemist analyzed the alkaloidal content of the sample. 化学家分析了样品中生物碱的含量。 | Synonyms: alkaloid-like, alkaloid-containing | Antonyms: non-alkaloidal | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From alkaloid + -al.  Alkaloid comes from alkali (meaning "ashes of a plant") + -oid (meaning "resembling").  Ultimately from Arabic *al-qalī* "ashes of saltwort".
