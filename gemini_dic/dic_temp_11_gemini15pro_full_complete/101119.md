invertin:/ɪnˈvɜːrtɪn/ | n. |转化酶| An enzyme that catalyzes the hydrolysis of sucrose into glucose and fructose.  Also called invertase. | Example 1: Invertin is used in the food industry to produce invert sugar.  转化酶在食品工业中用于生产转化糖。

Example 2: The activity of invertin can be affected by temperature and pH.  转化酶的活性会受到温度和pH值的影响。 | Synonym: invertase, sucrase | Antonym: None | noun
