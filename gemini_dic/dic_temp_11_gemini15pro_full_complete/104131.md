ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French joncade ‘a rush mat’, later denoting a dish served on rushes, from jonc ‘rush’. The original sense ‘sweet dish of cream, cheese, etc.’ gave rise to the current sense of a festive occasion.
