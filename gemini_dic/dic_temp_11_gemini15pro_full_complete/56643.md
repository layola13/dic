donnish:/ˈdɒnɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːnɪʃ/ (US)| adj.|学究气的；书呆子气的；教授风范的|Resembling or characteristic of a university don; pedantic, scholarly, bookish.|He had a donnish air about him, always correcting people's grammar. 他身上有一种学究气，总是纠正别人的语法。|近义词: academic, scholarly, learned, erudite, bookish, pedantic|反义词: unscholarly, ignorant, philistine|词性: adjective

词源: From *don* (n.) + *-ish* (suffix).  The word "don" originally referred to a head or fellow of a college at Oxford or Cambridge.
