multibusiness:/ˌmʌltiˈbɪznɪs/| adj.| 多业务的，多元化经营的 | Relating to or operating several different businesses. |  Example:  A multibusiness company often faces challenges in managing diverse portfolios.  一家多元化经营的公司常常面临管理多样化投资组合的挑战。

Example: The multibusiness strategy allows the company to spread risk across different sectors. 多元化经营战略使公司能够分散不同行业的风险。 | Near Synonyms: diversified, conglomerate, multi-industry | Opposite: specialized, single-business | Adjective
