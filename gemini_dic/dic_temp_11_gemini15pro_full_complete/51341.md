detectional:/dɪˈtɛkʃənəl/
adjective
检测的; 与检测相关的
Relating to or involving detection.
The detectional capabilities of the new radar system are impressive.  新的雷达系统的探测能力令人印象深刻。
The study focused on the detectional limits of the instrument. 这项研究的重点是该仪器的检测极限。
近义词:detective, sensing, perceptive
反义词:unperceptive, oblivious
词性:adjective

词源:  Derived from "detection" + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives). "Detection" comes from the Late Latin "detectio," from the Latin verb "detegere," meaning "to uncover, discover."
