gargoyle: /ˈɡɑːrɡɔɪl/ | n. |滴水嘴兽，怪兽状雕饰|A carved stone grotesque, typically projecting from the gutter of a building, designed to convey rainwater clear of a wall.|The cathedral is adorned with numerous gargoyles.  大教堂装饰着许多滴水嘴兽。|Example:  Rainwater poured from the gargoyle's mouth. 雨水从滴水嘴兽的嘴里倾泻而出。|Synonyms: grotesque, chimera |Antonyms: N/A |noun
