foreshadow:/fɔːrˈʃædoʊ/ (US) /fɔːˈʃædəʊ/ (UK)
|v.|预示，预兆|to indicate or suggest something that will happen in the future|The dark clouds foreshadowed a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。|
|v.|预示，预兆|Her early interest in airplanes foreshadowed her later career as a pilot. 她早期对飞机的兴趣预示着她后来成为一名飞行员。|
|近义词:|presage, portend, forebode, augur|
|反义词:|belie|
|词性:|verb|
