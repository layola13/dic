lamm:/læm/ | n. |羔羊, 小羊, 幼畜|A young sheep, especially one less than one year old or without permanent teeth.  A young animal of certain other species, such as the goat. An innocent, gentle, or weak person.|例句：The lamm frolicked in the meadow. 小羊在草地上嬉戏。


例句：He is a lamm led to the slaughter. 他像待宰的羔羊一样无辜。|近义词：lambkin, ewe lamb|反义词: ram, adult sheep |词性: noun
