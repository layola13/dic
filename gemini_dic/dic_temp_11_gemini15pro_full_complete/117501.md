meech:/miːtʃ/
verb
秘密会面; 偷偷摸摸地见面; 
to have a secret meeting
例句：They meeched in the back room to avoid being seen. 他们为了不被看见在后屋偷偷摸摸地见面。

例句：The lovers would meech at a secluded spot in the park. 这对恋人会在公园一个僻静的地方幽会。

近义词：meet clandestinely, rendezvous secretly
反义词：meet openly, gather publicly
词性: verb
