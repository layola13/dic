anticonsumers:/ˌæntiˈkənˈsuːmərz/ | n. |反消费者主义者；抵制消费主义的人| People who actively resist consumerism, often by reducing their consumption, reusing goods, and advocating for ethical and sustainable practices. |  Example: Anticonsumers often choose to buy less and repair more. 反消费主义者通常选择少买多修。

Example:  Many anticonsumers are concerned about the environmental impact of excessive consumption. 许多反消费主义者担心过度消费对环境的影响。| 近义词:  minimalists, conscious consumers, ethical consumers | 反义词:  consumerists, materialists | 词性: 名词
