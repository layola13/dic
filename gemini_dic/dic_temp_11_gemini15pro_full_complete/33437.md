churlishness:/ˈtʃɜːrlɪʃnəs/
n.
粗鲁；无礼；粗野
rudeness; ill-mannered behavior
His churlishness embarrassed his wife at the party. 他的粗鲁在聚会上让他的妻子感到尴尬。
The waiter's churlishness made the dining experience unpleasant. 服务员的粗鲁使得用餐体验很不愉快。
近义词：rudeness, incivility, impoliteness, surliness, boorishness
反义词：politeness, courtesy, civility, graciousness
词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *churlischnes, equivalent to churlish +‎ -ness.  "Churlish" itself comes from Old English ceorlisc "of or pertaining to a churl," with "churl" originally meaning a peasant or freeman of the lowest rank. Over time, the term took on negative connotations of rudeness and bad manners.
