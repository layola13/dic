例句：She spent her allowance on frivolous kickshaws. 她把零用钱都花在了轻佻的小玩意儿上。|近义词：trifles, baubles, gewgaws, trinkets|反义词: necessities, essentials|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *kikeshawe*, likely a playful reduplication based on 'kickshoe' (a light shoe or slipper), suggesting something frivolous or dainty.
