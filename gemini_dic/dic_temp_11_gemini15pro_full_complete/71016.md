firmament:/ˈfɜːrməmənt/ (US), /ˈfɜːməmənt/ (UK)| n. |苍穹；天空；天球|The sky, especially when regarded as a solid arch or vault.|例句：The stars twinkled in the vast firmament. 浩瀚的苍穹中，星星闪烁。


例句：They gazed up at the firmament, marveling at its beauty. 他们凝视着天空，惊叹于它的美丽。


近义词：sky, heavens, vault, canopy|反义词:ground, earth|词性:noun

词源：From Old French *firmament (Modern French firmament), from Late Latin firmamentum "a strengthening, support, prop," also "sky," from Latin firmare "to make firm, strengthen, support," from firmus "firm, strong."  The notion is of the sky as a solid vault.
