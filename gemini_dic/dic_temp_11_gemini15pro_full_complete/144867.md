over-rated:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪtɪd/ |adj.|被高估的，评价过高的|Considered to be better or more important than it really is.|The restaurant is overrated in my opinion. 在我看来，这家餐馆被高估了。|overvalued, overestimated|undervalued, underestimated|adjective
