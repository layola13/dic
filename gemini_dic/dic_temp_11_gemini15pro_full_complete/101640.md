irrefragability:/ˌɪrɪˌfræɡəˈbɪləti/
n.|不可反驳性，不容置疑|The lawyer presented arguments of such irrefragability that the jury had no choice but to acquit. 律师提出的论点如此不容置疑，陪审团别无选择，只能宣告无罪。|例句：The irrefragability of the evidence led to a swift conviction.  证据确凿，迅速定罪。|近义词：indisputability, incontestability, irrefutability|反义词：dubiousness, questionability, disputability|词性:名词

irrefragable:/ˌɪrɪˈfræɡəbl/
adj.|不可反驳的，不容置疑的|His logic was irrefragable, leaving no room for argument. 他的逻辑无懈可击，不容置疑。|例句：She presented irrefragable proof of her innocence. 她提供了无可辩驳的证据证明自己无辜。|近义词：incontestable, indisputable, undeniable|反义词：dubious, questionable, disputable|词性:形容词
