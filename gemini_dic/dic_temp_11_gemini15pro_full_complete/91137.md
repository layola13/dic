hybridization:/ˌhaɪbrɪdəˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌhaɪbrɪdɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |杂交，混合，杂化|The process of combining different varieties of plants or animals to produce hybrids; the process of combining different things to create something new.|Scientists are studying the hybridization of different species of corn. 科学家正在研究不同玉米品种的杂交。|synonyms: crossbreeding, interbreeding, mixing, blending|antonyms: separation, segregation|noun
