nonconnectivity:/ˌnɑːnkɒnɛkˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 非连接性，不连通性 | The state of not being connected, especially in a network or system. Lack of connection or communication. | The nonconnectivity of the remote village made communication difficult.  这个偏远村庄的不连通性使得通讯很困难。 |  The network experienced nonconnectivity issues due to a server failure. 由于服务器故障，网络出现了不连通问题。 | synonyms: disconnection, isolation, separation | antonyms: connection, connectivity, linkage | noun
