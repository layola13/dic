PHRASE->

crystallise out: 析出  The salt crystallised out of the solution. 盐从溶液中析出。

crystallise into:  形成; 具体化为  His vague ideas eventually crystallised into a concrete plan. 他模糊的想法最终形成了一个具体的计划。

crystallise around:  围绕…形成; 以…为中心  The movement crystallised around the issue of equal rights.  这场运动以平等权利问题为中心展开。

crystallise one's thoughts:  整理思绪  She needed time to crystallise her thoughts before making a decision.  她需要时间整理思绪，然后再做决定。

a crystallising moment:  关键时刻；决定性时刻  The election was a crystallising moment in the nation's history.  这次选举是国家历史上的一个关键时刻。
