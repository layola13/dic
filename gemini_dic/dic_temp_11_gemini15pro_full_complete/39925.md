constrict:/kənˈstrɪkt/| v. |收缩，压缩，限制|To make something narrower or tighter, or to become narrower or tighter; to limit or restrict something.|The drug constricts the blood vessels. 这种药物会使血管收缩。|Synonyms: compress, squeeze, tighten, narrow, restrict|Antonyms: expand, dilate, widen, broaden|verb

constrict:/kənˈstrɪkt/| v. |约束，束缚|To limit or restrict someone's actions or freedom.|Fear constricted her throat.  恐惧让她喉咙发紧。|Synonyms: restrain, confine, inhibit, limit|Antonyms: liberate, free, release|verb
