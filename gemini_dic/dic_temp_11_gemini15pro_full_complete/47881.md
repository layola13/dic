decembrist:/dɪˈsembrɪst/
n.
十二月党人
a member of a group of Russian revolutionaries who led an unsuccessful uprising in December 1825.
The Decembrists were exiled to Siberia after their failed revolt. 十二月党人在起义失败后被流放到西伯利亚。
synonyms: revolutionary, rebel, insurgent
antonyms: loyalist, conformist
noun
