commemorative: /kəˈmɛməˌreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 纪念的，纪念性的 | Serving to commemorate or mark by some ceremony or observation. |  This commemorative stamp was issued for the Queen's Jubilee. 这枚纪念邮票是为女王登基禧年发行的。

The museum houses a commemorative exhibition of the artist's work.  博物馆举办了这位艺术家作品的纪念展。 | 近义词：memorial, celebratory, remembrance | 反义词:  forgettable | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin commemorativus, from commemorare "to call to mind, remember," from com- "together" + memorare "to mention, relate," from memor "mindful."
