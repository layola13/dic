deselects:/ˌdiːsəˈlekts/| v. |取消选择，撤销选择| To reverse the selection of; to remove from a selected group or category. |例句：He deselected the file by clicking on it again. 他再次点击文件取消了选择。

例句：She deselected several items from her online shopping cart. 她从她的在线购物车中取消选择了几个商品。|近义词：uncheck, unselect, dechoose |反义词: select, choose |词性:动词
