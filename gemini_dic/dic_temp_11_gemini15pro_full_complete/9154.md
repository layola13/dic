antinaturally:/ˌæntɪˈnætʃərəli/
| adv. |违反自然地；反常地；|Acting in a way that goes against what is considered natural or normal.|He behaved antinaturally, showing no emotion after the tragedy. 他表现得很反常，在悲剧发生后没有流露出任何情感。|Synonyms: unnaturally, abnormally, perversely|Antonyms: naturally, normally|Word type: Adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against, opposite) + naturally (in a natural way).  The prefix "anti-" comes from Greek, and "naturally" comes from Late Middle English.
