nonperceptibly:/ˌnɒnpərˈsɛptɪbli/ /ˌnɑːnpərˈsɛptɪbli/
adverb
不易察觉地
In a way that is too small or subtle to be noticed.
The light dimmed nonperceptibly. 光线不知不觉地变暗了。
The poison worked nonperceptibly. 毒药不知不觉地发挥了作用。

近义词:imperceptibly, subtly, gradually, slightly
反义词:perceptibly, noticeably, obviously, clearly
词性:adverb

词源:
From non- + perceptible + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Perceptible" comes from the Latin "perceptus," the past participle of "percipere," meaning "to perceive" or "to grasp." "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
