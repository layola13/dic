inane:/ɪˈneɪn/ | adj. | 空洞的；愚蠢的；无意义的 | Lacking sense, substance, or significance; silly or stupid. |  Her inane comments added nothing to the conversation. 她的空洞评论对谈话没有任何帮助。 |  The movie's plot was inane and predictable.  这部电影的情节空洞且可预料。 | 近义词：silly, foolish, stupid, vapid, empty, meaningless | 反义词：sensible, intelligent, meaningful, profound | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late 16th century: from Latin *inanis, from in- ‘not’ + -anis (related to annus ‘year’). The original sense was ‘empty, void’, later ‘useless, futile’.
