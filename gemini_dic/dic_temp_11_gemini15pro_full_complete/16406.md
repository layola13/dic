barcodes:/ˈbɑːrˌkoʊdz/ /ˈbɑːrkoʊdz/| n.|条形码|A machine-readable code in the form of numbers and a pattern of parallel lines of varying widths, printed on and identifying a product.|The cashier scanned the barcodes on all the items.|Synonyms: UPC, Universal Product Code, scan code|Antonyms: None|Noun

barcodes:/ˈbɑːrˌkoʊdz/ /ˈbɑːrkoʊdz/| n.|条形码|A method of representing data in a visual, machine-readable form.|Barcodes are used extensively in retail and logistics.|Synonyms:  bar code, Universal Product Code, UPC|Antonyms: None|Noun
