halation:/ˌheɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. |晕影，光晕|A blurring or spreading of light around a bright image on a photographic plate or film, or a similar effect on a television screen. | The old photo had a halation around the streetlights. 这张老照片的路灯周围有晕影。| 近义词：glow, aura, halo | 反义词: clarity, sharpness | noun
