hexagons:/ˈhɛksəɡɑːnz/
n.
六边形；六角形
A plane figure with six straight sides and angles.
Bees build their honeycombs in a structure of hexagons. 蜜蜂把它们的蜂巢建造成六边形的结构。
The bathroom floor was tiled with small hexagons. 浴室的地板是用小的六边形瓷砖铺成的。
