organ:/ˈɔːrɡən (UK), ˈɔːrɡən (US)| n.|器官;机构;风琴;新闻媒介|A part of the body of a person, plant, or animal that performs a particular function. Also, a large musical instrument with pipes that produce sound when air is blown through them by electrical power; a group of people who work together for a particular purpose.|

The heart is a vital organ. 心脏是一个重要的器官。

The church organ filled the air with music. 教堂的管风琴使空中充满了音乐。

The newspaper is an organ of public opinion. 报纸是公众舆论的工具。

近义词:instrument, part, component, body, medium, means
反义词:whole
词性:noun
