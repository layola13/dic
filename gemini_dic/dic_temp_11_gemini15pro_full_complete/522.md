abomasum:/ˌæboʊˈmeɪsəm/| n. |皱胃|The fourth and final stomach compartment in ruminant animals (such as cows and sheep), where the main part of digestion occurs.|The abomasum is responsible for the secretion of rennet, which helps in the coagulation of milk. 皱胃负责分泌凝乳酶，有助于牛奶的凝固。|The vet found a blockage in the cow's abomasum. 兽医发现牛的皱胃有阻塞。|Synonyms: maw, stomach (in a broader sense)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
