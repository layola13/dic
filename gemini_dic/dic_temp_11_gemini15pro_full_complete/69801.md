fernlike:/ˈfɜːrnlaɪk/| adj.|像蕨类植物的；蕨类植物似的|Resembling or characteristic of a fern, especially in having delicate, feathery fronds.|The plant had delicate, fernlike leaves. 这种植物有纤细的、像蕨类植物一样的叶子。|The fernlike pattern on the fabric was intricate and beautiful. 布料上蕨类植物似的图案复杂而美丽。|近义词：feathery, plumy, frondlike|反义词: (No clear opposite, perhaps "solid" or "compact" in reference to leaf shape)|词性:形容词
