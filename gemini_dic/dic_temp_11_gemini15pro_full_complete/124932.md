N/A - "Morir" is intransitive and doesn't have a passive voice.

注意事项:

This verb is conjugated in the second person singular, which can sound direct and even harsh in some contexts.  Be mindful of the situation and relationship with the person you are speaking to before using this form.  It is more common to use a more formal or less direct way to talk about death.
