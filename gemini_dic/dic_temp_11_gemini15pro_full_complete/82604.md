guardroom:/ˈɡɑːrdruːm/ | /ˈɡɑːrdrʊm/
n.| 警卫室；卫兵室 | A room used by guards, especially in a castle, prison, or other large building. | The prisoner was taken to the guardroom. 囚犯被带到了警卫室。| security room, guardhouse |  None | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "guard" + "room".  "Guard" comes from Old French "garde" (meaning "to protect, watch over"), ultimately from Frankish "*wardan". "Room" comes from Old English "rūm" (meaning "space, place").
