hurdlework:/ˈhɜːrdlˌwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈhɜːdəlˌwɜːk/ (UK)| n. |跨栏赛跑；障碍赛跑；跨栏运动|A form of running race in which runners must jump over a series of hurdles. |Example 1: He excels at hurdlework and has won several medals. 他擅长跨栏，并赢得了几枚奖牌。

Example 2:  Hurdlework requires agility and speed. 跨栏需要敏捷性和速度。|近义词：hurdling, hurdle race, steeplechase |反义词: (No direct opposite, possibly long-distance running, marathon) |词性: noun
