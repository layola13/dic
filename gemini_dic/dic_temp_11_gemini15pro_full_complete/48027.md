decisiveness:/dɪˈsaɪsɪvnəs/
|n.|果断，决断力
|The quality of being able to make decisions quickly and effectively.  能够快速有效地做出决定的品质。
|Her decisiveness in a crisis was admirable.  她在危机中的果断令人钦佩。
|determination, resolution, firmness
|hesitation, indecision, vacillation
|noun
