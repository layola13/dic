discomfort:/dɪsˈkʌmfərt/| n. |不适，不舒服；不安|A feeling of slight pain or physical uneasiness.|例句：The tight shoes caused him considerable discomfort. 这双紧的鞋子让他感到很不舒服。|近义词：unease, distress, pain|反义词:comfort, ease|名词

discomfort:/dɪsˈkʌmfərt/| vt. |使不舒服；使不安|To make someone feel slightly worried or uncertain.|例句：The news of the layoffs discomforted the employees. 裁员的消息让员工们感到不安。|近义词：disturb, upset, worry|反义词:comfort, reassure|动词
