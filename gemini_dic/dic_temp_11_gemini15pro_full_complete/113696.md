maclurea:/məˈklʊəriə/| n. |大角石|An extinct genus of large, coiled, marine mollusks belonging to the Gastropoda class, characterized by their massive, low-spired shells. These creatures thrived in the Ordovician period.|Maclurea were common in the shallow seas of the Ordovician.  大角石在奥陶纪的浅海中很常见。

Maclurea fossils can be found in North America and Europe.  在北美和欧洲都能找到大角石化石。|近义词:gastropods (more general),  腹足纲动物（更普遍）|反义词: N/A (extinct genus)|词性:noun
