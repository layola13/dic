disinure:/ˌdɪsɪˈnjʊər/ | vt. | 使习惯于（令人不快或痛苦的事物）; 使不再害怕;  | To accustom someone to something unpleasant or painful, so that they no longer find it uncomfortable or upsetting. |  The soldiers were disinured to the horrors of war.  这些士兵对战争的恐怖已经麻木了。

| 近义词: harden, inure, habituate | 反义词: sensitize | 词性: 动词


disinure:/ˌdɪsɪˈnjʊər/ | vt. | 使失去热情; 使不再关心;  | To cause someone to lose their enthusiasm or concern for something. | The constant criticism had disinured him to praise.  不断的批评使他对赞扬无动于衷了。

| 近义词: disillusion, disenchant | 反义词: inspire, enthuse | 词性: 动词
