odometry: /oʊˈdɒmətri/ (US) /əʊˈdɒmɪtri/ (UK) | n. | 测程法；里程计读数 | The process of determining the position and orientation of a mobile robot by measuring the rotation of its wheels or tracks.  It uses sensors to measure the distance traveled by the robot and uses this information to estimate its current location. |  The robot's odometry indicated it had traveled 10 meters.  机器人的里程计显示它已经行驶了 10 米。


The Mars rover uses odometry to navigate the Martian surface. 火星探测器使用测程法在火星表面导航。| synonyms: dead reckoning, mileage | antonyms: N/A | noun
