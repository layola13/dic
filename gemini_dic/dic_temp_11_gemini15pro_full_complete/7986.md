anthologising:/ˌænˈθɒlədʒaɪzɪŋ/ /ˌænˈθɑːlədʒaɪzɪŋ/| v.|将作品选入文集；编选文集|The process of selecting and compiling literary works, or extracts from them, into an anthology.|He is anthologising the best poems of the 21st century. 他正在编选21世纪的最佳诗歌。|Synonyms: compiling, collecting, selecting|Antonyms: excluding, omitting|Verb
