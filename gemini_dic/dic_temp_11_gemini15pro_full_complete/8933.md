antihierarchies:/ˌæntiˈhaɪərɑːrkiːz/
|n.| 反等级制度;非等级制度;反等级结构|A system or organization that actively opposes or avoids traditional hierarchical structures, promoting equality and shared power.|The antihierarchies of some online communities allow for more open communication.  一些在线社区的反等级制度允许更开放的沟通。|synonyms: egalitarianism, non-hierarchical structures, flat organizations|antonyms: hierarchies, stratified systems, authoritarianism|noun
