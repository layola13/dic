compression:/kəmˈprɛʃən/ | n. |压缩，浓缩，压迫|The compression of the gas reduced its volume. 气体的压缩减少了它的体积。|例句：Data compression helps save storage space. 数据压缩有助于节省存储空间。|例句：The compression bandage helped reduce the swelling.  加压绷带帮助减轻了肿胀。|近义词：condensation, compaction, constriction |反义词: expansion, decompression, release|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compressionem, from Latin compress-, past participle stem of comprimere "to press together," from com- "together" + premere "to press."
