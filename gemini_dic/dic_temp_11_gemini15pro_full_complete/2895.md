advertisings:/ˈædvərtaɪzɪŋz/ | n. | 广告 (复数形式，罕用) | The act or practice of calling public attention to one's product, service, need, etc., especially by paid announcements in newspapers and magazines, over radio or television, on billboards, etc.  Although grammatically correct, "advertisings" is rarely used; "advertising" is typically used even when referring to multiple advertising campaigns or instances. |  Example 1: The company invested heavily in its advertisings. (Less common usage) 公司在其广告上投入巨资。

Example 2:  The various advertisings for the new product were very effective. (Less common usage)  新产品的各种广告非常有效。| Synonym: advertising, promotion, publicity | Antonym:  concealment, suppression | noun
