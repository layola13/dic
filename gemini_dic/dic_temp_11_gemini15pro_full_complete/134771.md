nonmetal:/ˈnɒnˌmɛtl/ (UK) /ˈnɑːnˌmɛtl/ (US)| n.|非金属|An element that doesn't have the characteristics of a metal (such as hardness, luster, or the ability to conduct heat or electricity).  A nonmetal is typically brittle and may be a solid, liquid, or gas at room temperature.|Oxygen is a nonmetal essential for life. 氧气是一种对生命至关重要的非金属。|Synonyms: non-metal, metalloid (in some contexts)|Antonyms: metal|noun
