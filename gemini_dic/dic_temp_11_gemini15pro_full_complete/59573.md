ebonicses:/ɛˈbɑːnɪksiːz/| n. |非裔美国人英语的多种形式|Multiple forms of African American Vernacular English (AAVE), sometimes considered a dialect of American English.|例句：Researchers are studying the different ebonicses spoken across the United States. 研究人员正在研究美国各地不同的非裔美国人英语。

例句：The evolution of ebonicses reflects the diverse cultural experiences of African Americans. 非裔美国人英语的演变反映了非裔美国人不同的文化经历。|近义词：AAVEs, Black English Vernaculars|反义词:Standard American English|词性:名词

词源:  From Ebonics (itself a blend of ebony and phonics) + -es (plural suffix).
