dioecian:/daɪˈiːʃən/ |adj.|雌雄异株的；雌雄异体的|Having male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals.|Holly trees are dioecian, meaning that some trees have only male flowers and others have only female flowers. 冬青树是雌雄异株的，这意味着有些树只有雄花，而另一些树只有雌花。|Synonyms: unisexual, gonochoric|Antonyms: monoecious, hermaphroditic|Adjective

dioecious:/daɪˈiːʃəs/ |adj.|雌雄异株的；雌雄异体的|Having male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals.|The dioecious nature of the ginkgo tree means you need both a male and a female tree to produce seeds. 银杏树的雌雄异株特性意味着你需要雄树和雌树才能产生种子。|Synonyms: unisexual, gonochoric|Antonyms: monoecious, hermaphroditic|Adjective


Etymology: From Greek *di- "two" + *oikos "house".  Literally "two houses", referring to the separate male and female plants.
