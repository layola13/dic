antireduction:/ˌænti rɪˈdʌkʃən/
| n. | 反还原论 | The philosophical position that a complex system is not simply the sum of its parts and cannot be fully explained by reducing it to its individual components. |  Antireductionism argues against explaining complex phenomena solely in terms of simpler ones.  反还原论反对仅仅用更简单的现象来解释复杂现象。|  The study embraced an antireductionist approach, emphasizing the interplay of various factors.  这项研究采用了反还原论的方法，强调各种因素的相互作用。 | holism, organicism | reductionism | noun
