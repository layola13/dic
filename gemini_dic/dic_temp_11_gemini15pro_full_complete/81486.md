gravelled:/ˈɡrævəld/ /ˈɡrævld/| adj.|铺满碎石的；沙哑的（嗓音）|Covered with gravel; (of a voice) hoarse or rough-sounding.|The path was gravelled, making it difficult to walk on. 这条小路铺满了碎石，很难行走。|近义词：gritty, gravelly, stony|反义词:smooth, paved|词性:形容词
gravelled:/ˈɡrævəld/ /ˈɡrævld/| v.|用碎石铺；使（嗓音）沙哑|(past tense and past participle of gravel) To cover with gravel; to make (a voice) hoarse or rough-sounding.|His voice was gravelled by years of smoking. 多年的吸烟使他的嗓音沙哑。|近义词：roughen|反义词:smooth|词性:动词
