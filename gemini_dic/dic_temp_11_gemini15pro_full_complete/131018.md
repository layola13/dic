nonaugmentatives:/nɒnɔːɡˈmɛntətɪvz/ | n. |非增义词，非增强词 |Words that do not express increased size, intensity, or force.  They are the standard or base forms of words, without any suffixes or modifications that indicate augmentation. |  These nonaugmentatives provide a neutral description of the objects. 这些非增义词对事物进行了中性的描述。| synonyms: base forms, standard forms | antonyms: augmentatives, intensifiers | noun
