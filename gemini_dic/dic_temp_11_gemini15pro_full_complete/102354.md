izard:/ˈɪzərd/ | n. |蜥蜴 |A reptile with rough, scaly skin, four legs, and a long tapering tail. |The lizard darted across the hot sand. 蜥蜴飞快地穿过滚烫的沙子。|Synonyms: reptile, gecko, skink |Antonyms: mammal, bird |noun


izard:/ˈɪzərd/ | adj. (obsolete, rare) |敏捷的，灵活的 |Nimble, agile. |He moved with izard-like agility. 他像蜥蜴一样敏捷地移动着。|Synonyms: agile, nimble, quick |Antonyms: clumsy, slow |adjective
