clucks:/klʌks/
| n. vi.|咯咯声，咯咯叫|The sound a hen makes, especially after laying an egg.|The hen clucked happily after laying a large brown egg. 母鸡下了一个大大的棕色蛋后，高兴地咯咯叫了起来。|近义词：cackles, chuckles|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑silence, quiet)|名词, 动词
