compromised:/ˈkɒmprəmaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|妥协的，受损的，危害的|weakened or damaged; exposed to suspicion or danger|例句：The security system was compromised.  安全系统受到了损害。|近义词：jeopardized, endangered, weakened, undermined|反义词：secure, safe, protected|词性:形容词

compromised:/ˈkɒmprəmaɪzɪŋ/ (UK)  /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|有损名誉的，尴尬的|causing damage to someone's reputation| 例句：Photographs in compromising positions were published in the newspaper. 报纸上刊登了有损名誉的照片。|近义词：embarrassing, incriminating, damaging|反义词：flattering, commendable|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin compromissus, past participle of compromittere "to promise mutually, to submit to arbitration," from com- "together" + promittere "to promise"
