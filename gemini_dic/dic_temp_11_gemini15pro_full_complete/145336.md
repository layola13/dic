overbrutalizes:/ˌoʊvərˈbruːtəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度残暴对待
To treat with excessive brutality; to brutalize beyond what is considered reasonable or necessary.
The dictator's regime overbrutalized the protesting citizens, resorting to inhumane torture methods.  独裁者的政权过度残暴地对待抗议的公民，采取了不人道的酷刑。
The prison guards overbrutalized the inmates, inflicting unnecessary pain and suffering. 监狱看守过度残暴地对待囚犯，给他们造成不必要的痛苦和折磨。
Synonyms: maltreat, mistreat, abuse, torture
Antonyms: protect, cherish, nurture
词性: 动词 (verb)
