lirella:/laɪˈrɛlə/ | n. | 小犁沟，细沟，(地衣)子囊盘盖 |A small furrow, channel, or groove, especially one on the surface of a lichen apothecium.|The lirella on the lichen were clearly visible under magnification. 在放大镜下，地衣上的小犁沟清晰可见。|近义词：furrow, groove, channel|反义词：ridge|词性: 名词
