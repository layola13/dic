galvanoscopic:/ˌɡæl.və.nəˈskɒp.ɪk/| adj.|电流显示的，验电的|Relating to a galvanoscope; indicating the presence and direction of a small electric current. |The galvanoscopic deflection indicated a weak current. 电流显示偏转表明电流微弱。|Synonyms: electrometric, galvanometric|Antonyms:  non-conductive, insulating|adjective
