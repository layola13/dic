cuckolded:/ˈkʌkəldɪd/| adj.|戴绿帽的，妻子与人通奸的|A man whose wife has had sex with another man, especially without the husband's knowledge or consent.|例句：He felt cuckolded and humiliated when he discovered his wife's affair. 他发现妻子有外遇后感到自己被戴了绿帽子，倍感羞辱。

例句：The play explored the themes of jealousy and betrayal in a cuckolded husband. 这部戏剧探讨了一个被戴绿帽子的丈夫的嫉妒和背叛的主题。|近义词：cheated on, betrayed, dishonored|反义词:faithful, loyal|词性:形容词

cuckold:/ˈkʌkəld/| n. vt.|戴绿帽的男子；使戴绿帽子|A man whose wife is sexually unfaithful; to make a cuckold of (a man).|例句：He was a cuckold, unaware of his wife's infidelity. 他被戴了绿帽子，却对妻子的不忠一无所知。

例句：She cuckolded her husband with his best friend. 她和丈夫最好的朋友通奸，给丈夫戴了绿帽子。|近义词：cheat on, betray|反义词: be faithful to|词性:名词，动词
