million:/ˈmɪljən/ |n.|百万|A cardinal number equal to one thousand thousand (1,000,000).| There are millions of stars in the sky. 天空中有数百万颗星星。| The lottery winner received millions of dollars. 彩票中奖者获得了数百万美元。 |近义词: myriad, countless|反义词: few, handful|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *million* (modern French *million*), from Old Italian *milione*, augmentative of *mille* "thousand," from Latin *mille*.
