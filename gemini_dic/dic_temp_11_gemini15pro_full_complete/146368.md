overfloat:/ˈoʊvərˌfloʊt/| n. v.|过剩，溢出，超额发行|An amount in excess of what is needed or usual; an overflow.  As a verb, it means to issue an excessive amount of (currency, stocks, etc.) or to flood or overflow.|

例句：
The overfloat of shares on the market depressed prices.
市场上股票的超额发行压低了价格。

The river overfloated its banks after the heavy rain.
暴雨过后，河水溢出了河岸。


近义词：excess, surplus, overflow, glut, inundate|
反义词: shortage, deficit, scarcity|
词性: noun, verb|
