allegories: /ˈæləˌɡɔːriz/ | n. | 寓言，讽喻 | A story, poem, or picture that can be interpreted to reveal a hidden meaning, typically a moral or political one. |  The novel is full of allegories about the corrupting influence of power. 这部小说充满了关于权力腐败影响的寓言。

The Faerie Queene is a complex allegory of the virtues and vices. 仙后是关于美德和恶行的复杂寓言。


| 近义词: fable, parable, metaphor, symbol | 反义词: literal interpretation | 词性: noun
