coulomb:/ˈkuːlɒm/, /ˈkuːlɑːm/ | n. | 库仑 | The SI unit of electric charge, equal to the quantity of electricity conveyed in one second by a current of one ampere. | A lightning bolt can deliver a charge of several coulombs.  一道闪电可以传递几库仑的电荷。 |The battery stores a charge of 1000 coulombs.  这块电池储存了1000库仑的电荷。 | synonyms: unit of charge | antonyms: N/A | noun
