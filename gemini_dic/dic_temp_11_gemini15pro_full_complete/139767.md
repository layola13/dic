octagynous:/ɒkˈtædʒɪnəs/
adjective
八雌蕊的；有八个雌蕊的
Having eight pistils (the female reproductive part of a flower).
The octagynous flower attracted the attention of the botanist.  八雌蕊的花引起了植物学家的注意。
The newly discovered species was identified as octagynous.  新发现的物种被确定为八雌蕊的。
Near Synonyms:  octamerous (having parts in eights),  octogyne (a plant with eight pistils)
