muler:/ˈmjuːlər/ (US), /ˈmjuːlə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 骡夫；赶骡子的人；走私贩|A person who drives mules or uses them to transport goods; a person who smuggles something. |He worked as a muler, transporting goods across the mountains. 他在山里当骡夫，运送货物。

The police caught the muler trying to smuggle drugs across the border. 警察抓住了试图跨境走私毒品的骡夫。 |近义词：mule driver, smuggler, trafficker|反义词: None| noun
