mediated:/ˈmiːdieɪtɪd/
adjective
居中的，调解的，斡旋的
brought about by or involving an intermediary or mediating agent.
例句：The dispute was settled through mediated negotiation. 争议通过调解谈判得到解决。

例句：The two countries established a mediated communication channel.  两国建立了调解沟通渠道。

近义词：arbitrated, negotiated, facilitated, brokered
反义词：direct, unmediated, immediate

词性: adjective
