clad:/klæd/
adjective
穿…衣服的；覆盖…的
covered
例句：
He was clad in a black suit. 他穿着一套黑色西装。

The mountains were clad in snow. 山脉被白雪覆盖。

近义词：dressed, clothed, covered
反义词：naked, bare
词性：形容词


Etymology:
From Old English *clædd, past participle of clēoðan  "to clothe," from Proto-Germanic *klæþjanan, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)klei- "to stick, adhere."  Related to cleave (v.). The same root produced German kleid "dress, garment."
