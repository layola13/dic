biometric:/ˌbaɪəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. n.|生物特征的，生物统计的；生物识别技术；生物测量学|Relating to or involving the application of statistical analysis to biological data.  Also,  referring to methods of identifying someone based on physical characteristics. |例句：Biometric authentication is becoming increasingly popular. 生物识别身份验证正变得越来越流行。

Biometric scanners are used for security purposes. 生物识别扫描仪用于安全目的。|近义词：biological, physiological|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun


biometrics:/ˌbaɪəˈmɛtrɪks/ | n. |生物识别技术；生物统计学|The science and technology of measuring and analyzing biological data.  Also, the measurement and analysis of unique physical or behavioral characteristics (such as fingerprint or voice patterns) especially as a means of verifying personal identity. |例句：Biometrics offers a more secure way to access your accounts. 生物识别技术提供了一种更安全的帐户访问方式。

The use of biometrics is growing in the workplace. 生物识别技术在工作场所的使用正在增长。|近义词：biometry|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
