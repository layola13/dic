fidejussor:/ˌfɪdeɪˈdʒʌsər/ /ˌfaɪdeɪˈdʒʊər/| n. |保证人，担保人|A person who acts as surety for another; one who becomes bound for the payment of a debt or performance of an obligation of another person.|例句：The fidejussor agreed to cover the loan if the borrower defaulted.  保证人同意如果借款人违约则偿还贷款。

例句：The court held the fidejussor liable for the debt. 法院认定保证人对债务负有责任。|近义词：guarantor, surety, sponsor|反义词: debtor, obligee |词性: noun
