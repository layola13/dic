antiliberalisms:/ˌæntiˈlɪbərəlɪzəmz/
|n.|反自由主义|Doctrines or practices opposed to liberal principles, often emphasizing tradition, authority, or collective identity over individual rights and freedoms.  It encompasses a range of ideologies, including conservatism, authoritarianism, and certain forms of nationalism.|The rise of antiliberalisms in the 20th century led to various forms of political extremism. 20世纪反自由主义的兴起导致了各种形式的政治极端主义。|近义词: illiberalism, authoritarianism, conservatism|反义词: liberalism, progressivism|noun
