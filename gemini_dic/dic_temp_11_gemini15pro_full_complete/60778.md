electromagnetically:/ɪˌlɛktroʊmæɡˈnɛtɪkli/
adverb
电磁地
In a way that relates to electromagnetism; by means of electromagnetism.
The particles are electromagnetically charged. 这些粒子带电磁性。
The door is locked electromagnetically. 这扇门是用电磁锁锁上的。


Synonyms: magnetically
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "mechanically," "manually," or describing a specific non-electromagnetic force)
词性: adverb (副词)
