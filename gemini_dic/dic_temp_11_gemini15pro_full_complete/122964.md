monacid:/ˌmɒnˈæsɪd/ /ˌmɑːnˈæsɪd/
adjective
一元的；单酸的
Containing only one replaceable hydrogen atom.  A monacid base is one containing only one hydroxyl group.
Monobasic; having only one acidic hydrogen atom per molecule.
例句：A monacid base contains only one hydroxyl group. 一元碱只含有一个羟基。

例句：Phosphoric acid can act as a monacid, diacid or triacid. 磷酸可以作为一元酸、二元酸或三元酸。
近义词：monobasic, monovalent
反义词：diacid, triacid, polyacid
词性：adjective
