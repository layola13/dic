leaked: /liːkt/ | v. | 泄露，渗漏 | to allow a secret or private matter to become known | The confidential document was leaked to the press. 机密文件被泄露给了媒体。| The pipe is leaking. 水管正在漏水。 | synonyms: disclosed, revealed, divulged, emitted, escaped | antonyms: concealed, hidden, contained, plugged, sealed | verb

leaked: /liːkt/ | adj. | 泄露的，渗漏的 | having been disclosed or revealed; allowing a substance to escape | The leaked information caused a scandal. 泄露的信息引发了丑闻。 | The leaked oil polluted the river. 泄露的石油污染了河流。| synonyms: disclosed, revealed, divulged,  porous, permeable | antonyms: concealed, hidden, contained,  impermeable, sealed| adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English leken (“to leak, drip”), from Old Norse leka (“to leak, drip”), from Proto-Germanic *lekōną (“to drip, leak”), from Proto-Indo-European *lēg- (“to drip, leak”).
