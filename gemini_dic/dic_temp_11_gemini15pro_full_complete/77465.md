gayety:/ˈɡeɪəti/ /ˈɡeɪɪti/| n.|快乐，欢乐，高兴|A feeling of cheerfulness and merriment. |例句：The festival was filled with music and gayety. 节日里充满了音乐和歌声，一片欢乐景象。

例句：Her infectious gayety brightened the room. 她富有感染力的快乐照亮了整个房间。

近义词：mirth, merriment, cheerfulness, jollity, festivity, glee|反义词: sadness, gloom, melancholy, dejection|词性:noun

词源: From Old French "gaiete," from "gai" (joyful). Ultimately from Frankish "*gahi," meaning "joyful" or "quick."
