maidan:/maɪˈdɑːn/ | n. |广场，露天市场，公共集会场所|A large open space in a town or city, used as a market or for public gatherings. Often used in reference to Eastern European cities.|例句：The protesters gathered in the maidan to voice their dissent. 抗议者聚集在广场上表达他们的异议。

例句：The maidan was bustling with activity, merchants selling their wares and children playing. 广场上熙熙攘攘，商贩们叫卖着他们的商品，孩子们嬉戏玩耍。|近义词：square, plaza, public space|反义词: enclosed space |词性:noun
