expressionist:/ɪkˈspreʃənɪst/| n. adj.|表现主义者，表现主义的|A person who favors or practices expressionism, especially in art, literature, or music.  An adherent or practitioner of expressionism. characterized by the use of strong colors, simplified forms, and exaggeration or distortion to express emotions. |例句：The painting is a classic example of German Expressionist art. 这幅画是德国表现主义艺术的典型例子。

|例句：She's an expressionist dancer known for her intense performances. 她是一位表现主义舞者，以其强烈的表演而闻名。|近义词：artist, creative, avant-garde|反义词：traditionalist, classicist|词性: noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From German Expressionismus, from Expression,  equivalent to express + -ion + -ismus -ism. Ultimately from Latin expressus, past participle of exprimere to press out.
