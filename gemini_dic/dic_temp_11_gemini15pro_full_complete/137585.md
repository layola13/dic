nontraversable:/ˌnɒntrəˈvɜːsəbl/ /ˌnɑːntrəˈvɜːrsəbl/| adj.|不可穿越的，无法通行的|Not able to be traversed or crossed; impassable.|例句：The dense jungle was nontraversable.  茂密的丛林无法通行。|近义词：impassable, inaccessible, impenetrable|反义词：traversable, passable, accessible|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- + traversable.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "traversable" comes from the French "traverser" meaning "to cross."
