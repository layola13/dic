antiskeptics:/ˌæntiˈskɛptɪks/
|n.|反怀疑论者|Those who oppose or argue against skepticism are called antiskeptics.  那些反对或驳斥怀疑论的人被称为反怀疑论者。| Antiskeptics believe that knowledge is attainable. 反怀疑论者认为知识是可以获得的。|近义词:believer, dogmatist|反义词:skeptic, doubter|词性:noun

antiskeptical:/ˌæntiˈskɛptɪkəl/
|adj.|反怀疑论的|His antiskeptical stance was clear in his writings. 他的反怀疑论立场在他的著作中很明确。|The professor presented an antiskeptical argument.  教授提出了一个反怀疑论的论点。|近义词:confident, trusting|反义词:skeptical, doubting|词性:adjective

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "against," "opposite of"
skeptic: from Greek "skeptikos," meaning "inquiring," "reflective"
