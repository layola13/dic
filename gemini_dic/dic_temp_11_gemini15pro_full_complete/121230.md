misenter:/mɪsˈɛntər/| v. |错入；放错地方；写错|To enter wrongly or put in a wrong place, especially in writing. | He misentered the data into the spreadsheet, causing a significant error. 他把数据错输入了电子表格，导致了一个严重的错误。

She misentered the address and the package was delivered to the wrong house. 她写错了地址，包裹被送到了错误的房子里。|近义词：misspell, miswrite, mistype|反义词: enter correctly|词性:动词
