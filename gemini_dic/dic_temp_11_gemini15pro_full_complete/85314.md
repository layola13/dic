harp-on:/ˈhɑːrp ɑːn/ | phrasal verb | 喋喋不休；反复唠叨 | To talk or complain about something repeatedly. |  He keeps harping on about how much money he lost. 他一直在唠叨他损失了多少钱。

He's always harping on about his ex-girlfriend. 他总是喋喋不休地谈论他的前女友。

近义词: dwell on, go on about,  fixate on | 反义词: let go, move on, forget about | 词性: 动词短语
