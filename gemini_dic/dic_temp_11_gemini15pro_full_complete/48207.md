decongestant:/ˌdiːkənˈdʒɛstənt/ | n. | 解充血药；减轻充血的药物 | A medication that provides relief from nasal congestion. |  I took a decongestant to relieve my stuffy nose. 我服用了解充血药来缓解鼻塞。

decongestant:/ˌdiːkənˈdʒɛstənt/ | adj. | 解充血的；减轻充血的 |  Having the property of relieving congestion. |  This nasal spray has a decongestant effect.  这种鼻喷剂有解充血的作用。


Synonym: |  anticongestant, nasal decongestant,  |
Antonym: |  None (The opposite would be something that *causes* congestion.) |
Word type: | Noun, Adjective |
