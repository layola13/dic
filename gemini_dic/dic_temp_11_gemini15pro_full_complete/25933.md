burnous:/ˈbɜːrnʊs/ | n. |  伯努斯，一种带兜帽的长斗篷 | A long, loose hooded cloak worn by Arabs and Berbers. |  He wore a burnous that shielded him from the desert sun. 他穿着一件伯努斯，遮挡着沙漠的阳光。|  cloak, cape, mantle |  None | noun
Etymology->
From French *burnous*, from Arabic *burnūs* برنس. Ultimately from Late Latin *byrrus*, a hooded cloak of coarse material.
