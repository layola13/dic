accursed:/əˈkɜːsɪd/ /əˈkɜːrst/| n.|被诅咒的人或物|A person or thing that is cursed.|The villagers feared the accursed object found in the ruins. 村民们害怕在废墟中发现的被诅咒的物品。|Synonyms: pariah, outcast|Antonyms: saint|noun
