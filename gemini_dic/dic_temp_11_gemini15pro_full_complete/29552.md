catchier:/ˈkætʃɪər/| adj.|更吸引人的，更动听的，更容易记住的|More likely to attract attention or be remembered, often due to a pleasing melody or clever wording.|The new jingle is catchier than the old one.  这首新的广告歌比旧的更朗朗上口。|近义词：memorable, appealing, infectious|反义词：forgettable, dull, unappealing|词性:形容词
Etymology: From 'catchy' + '-er' (comparative suffix). 'Catchy' originates from 'catch', meaning to seize or attract.
