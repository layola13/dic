cage's:/keɪdʒɪz/
noun
笼子的；鸟笼的
belonging to or associated with a cage.
The cage's door was left open.  笼子的门开着。
近义词: aviary's, enclosure's
反义词: freedom, liberty
词性: possessive noun (名词所有格)


cage:/keɪdʒ/
noun, verb
名词: 笼子；鸟笼；监狱
动词: 把…关进笼子；禁锢
From Old French, from Latin cavea "cavity, cage."
名词例句: The bird escaped its cage.  这只鸟逃出了笼子。
动词例句:  They caged the tiger. 他们把老虎关进了笼子。
近义词: (n.) pen, enclosure, jail; (v.) confine, imprison
反义词: (n.) freedom, liberty; (v.) release, free
词性: noun, verb
