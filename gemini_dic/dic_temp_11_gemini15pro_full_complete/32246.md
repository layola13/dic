chickling:/ˈtʃɪklɪŋ/ | n. | 小鸡；雏鸡；乳臭未干的小孩 | A young chicken or a very young child. |  The farmer carefully gathered the chicklings into the coop.  农民小心翼翼地把小鸡都赶进了鸡舍。

The guide referred to the new recruits as chicklings.  导游把新兵称为乳臭未干的小孩。 | 近义词：chick, fledgling, nestling | 反义词：adult, grown-up | 词性：名词

词源: From "chick" + "-ling" (a diminutive suffix).  来自 "chick"（小鸡） + "-ling" （表示小的后缀）。
