hypogeum:/ˈhaɪpəˈdʒiːəm/ |n.|地下墓穴，地下室|An underground chamber, often used as a tomb or for religious purposes.  A subterranean structure, especially one used for burial in ancient times.|The archaeologist discovered a hypogeum containing several well-preserved sarcophagi. 考古学家发现了一个地下墓穴，里面有几个保存完好的石棺。|Synonyms: crypt, vault, catacomb|Antonyms: surface, aboveground|noun
