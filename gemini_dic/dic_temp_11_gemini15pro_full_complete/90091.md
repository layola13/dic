hosting:/ˈhoʊstɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhəʊstɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |托管，主持，主办|the activity of providing the computer equipment and software necessary for a website or other online service to be available on the Internet|例句：The company offers web hosting services. 这家公司提供网站托管服务。

例句：They are hosting a party for their anniversary. 他们在为他们的周年纪念举办一个派对。|近义词：accommodation, lodging (for physical hosting); managing, organizing (for event hosting); providing, supplying (for web hosting)|反义词: N/A |词性: noun


hosting:/ˈhoʊstɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhəʊstɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |主持的，主办的|acting as host |例句：The hosting provider is responsible for server maintenance. 主机提供商负责服务器维护。

例句：The hosting country welcomed the athletes. 主办国欢迎运动员们。|近义词：organizing, managing|反义词: N/A |词性: adjective (less common)
