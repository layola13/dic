dangering:/ˈdeɪndʒərɪŋ/
|v.|危及，使处于危险之中|Putting oneself in harm's way, exposing to risk or peril.|例句：The rising floodwaters are endangering the lives of the villagers. 上涨的洪水正危及村民的生命。|例句：His reckless driving is endangering everyone on the road. 他鲁莽的驾驶正危及路上每个人的安全。|近义词：imperiling, jeopardizing, compromising|反义词：protecting, safeguarding|词性：动词的现在分词形式，用作形容词

dangering:/ˈdeɪndʒərɪŋ/
|adj.|危险的，造成危险的|Presenting a threat or hazard; causing danger or risk.|例句：The crumbling bridge is in a endangering state.  摇摇欲坠的桥梁处于危险的状态。|例句：The factory's toxic waste is endangering the local environment. 工厂的有毒废物正在危及当地环境。|近义词：hazardous, perilous, risky|反义词：safe, secure|词性：形容词
