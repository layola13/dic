itinerant:/aɪˈtɪnərənt/| adj. |巡回的，流动的|Traveling from place to place, especially for work.|例句：The itinerant preacher moved from town to town spreading his message.  这位巡回传教士辗转于各个城镇传播他的信息。

例句：An itinerant lifestyle can be exciting but also lonely.  巡回的生活方式可能令人兴奋，但也可能孤独。|近义词：nomadic, wandering, traveling, peripatetic|反义词：settled, stationary, resident|词性：形容词

itinerant:/aɪˈtɪnərənt/| n. |巡回者，流动者|A person who travels from place to place, especially for work.|例句：Itinerants often find work as farm laborers during harvest season.  流动工人在收获季节经常找到农场工人的工作。

例句：The itinerants set up camp by the river. 流浪者在河边扎营。|近义词：wanderer, nomad, traveler|反义词：resident, settler|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *itinerantem, present participle of itinerari "to journey," from Latin iter "journey, road."
