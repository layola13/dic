defacer:/dɪˈfeɪsər/| n.|污损者，涂鸦者|A person who defaces something, especially by writing or drawing on it. |The defacer was caught on camera spraying graffiti on the wall.  涂鸦者被摄像机拍到在墙上喷涂涂鸦。|近义词：vandal, graffiti artist|反义词：restorer, conservator|词性: noun

Etymology:
From deface + -er.  From the verb "deface," meaning to spoil the surface or appearance of something, which comes from the Old French "desfacer"  (to disfigure, destroy).
