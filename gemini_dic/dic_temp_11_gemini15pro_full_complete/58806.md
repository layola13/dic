duograph: /ˈdjuːəɡrɑːf/ /ˈduːəɡræf/ | n. | 双字母; 双字位; 双元音 | A group of two successive letters representing a single sound or phoneme.  | The "ph" in "phone" is a duograph. "phone"中的"ph"是一个双字母。 | 近义词: digraph | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Late Latin *digraphus, from Ancient Greek δίγραφος (dígraphos, “written twice”), from  δίς (dís, “twice”) + γράφω (gráphō, “to write”).
