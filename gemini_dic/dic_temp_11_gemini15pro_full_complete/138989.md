nymphetic:/nɪmˈfɛtɪk/| adj.|仙女般的，像小仙女的；性感的，诱人的 (尤指年轻女孩) |Having the qualities of a nymph, especially in being attractive and sexually desirable (typically of a young girl).  |例句：She had a nymphetic grace and beauty. 她有一种仙女般的优雅和美丽。|例句:  He was drawn to her nymphetic charm. 他被她像小仙女般的魅力所吸引。|近义词：elfin, fairy-like, ethereal, graceful, alluring, seductive|反义词：haggard, unattractive, homely|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From French *nymphétique*, from Latin *nympha* (nymph) + *-icus* (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Greek *νύμφη* (nýmphē).
