ahmes:/ˈɑːmɛs/| n.|阿梅斯|Ahmes, also known as Ahmose, was an Egyptian scribe who lived during the Second Intermediate Period (c. 1650-1550 BC). He is known for copying the Rhind Mathematical Papyrus, one of the oldest known mathematical texts.|Ahmes is best known for his work on the Rhind Papyrus. |近义词:N/A (人名通常没有近义词)|反义词:N/A (人名通常没有反义词)|词性:noun (名词)
