nonphilosophers:/ˌnɒn fɪˈlɒsəfəz/ /ˌnɑːn fɪˈlɑːsəfərz/
noun
非哲学家
People who are not philosophers; those who do not study or specialize in philosophy.
Nonphilosophers often find the subject of metaphysics confusing. 非哲学家经常发现形而上学这个主题令人困惑。
Many nonphilosophers have contributed to ethical discussions. 许多非哲学家对伦理讨论做出了贡献。
synonyms:laypeople, non-specialists
antonyms:philosophers, thinkers
n.
