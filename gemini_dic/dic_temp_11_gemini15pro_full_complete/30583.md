cercal:/ˈsɜːrkəl/| adj.|尾须的，尾须状的|Of or relating to cerci, which are paired appendages at the end of the abdomen of some insects.|The cercal sensory system of crickets allows them to detect minute air currents. 蟋蟀的尾须感觉系统使它们能够探测到微小的气流。|近义词: caudal, appendicular|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider terms like "cephalic" or "anterior" for opposite location)|词性: adjective
