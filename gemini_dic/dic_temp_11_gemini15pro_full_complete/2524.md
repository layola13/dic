admirabilities:/ˌædmərəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.|令人钦佩的品质；优点；长处|The admirabilities of her character were evident to everyone. 她的优秀品质每个人都显而易见。|例句：His courage and determination are two of his many admirabilities. 他的勇气和决心是他众多优点中的两个。|近义词：virtues, merits, strengths, excellences|反义词：faults, weaknesses, shortcomings|词性:名词
