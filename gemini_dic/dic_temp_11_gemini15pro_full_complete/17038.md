bathed:/beɪðd/ /bɑːθt/| v. adj.|沐浴，浸泡，沉浸，充满|Past tense and past participle of bathe.  Covered, immersed, or suffused with something.|例句：The valley was bathed in sunlight. 山谷沐浴在阳光中。|近义词：soaked, drenched, immersed, steeped, covered|反义词:dry, parched|词性:动词, 形容词
词源：From Old English *baþian, from Proto-Germanic *baþōną (“to bathe”), from Proto-Indo-European *bheh₂- (“to warm”). Cognate with Old Norse baða (“to bathe”), Old High German badon (“to bathe”), German baden (“to bathe”).
