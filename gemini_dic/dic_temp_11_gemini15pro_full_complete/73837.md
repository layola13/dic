formless:/ˈfɔːmləs/| adj.|无形的，无定形的，散漫的|Lacking definite form or shape; shapeless.  Without clear structure or organization.|The formless fears of the night haunted her. 夜晚无形的恐惧萦绕着她。|Synonyms: shapeless, amorphous, nebulous, vague, undefined|Antonyms: shaped, formed, definite, structured, organized|adjective
