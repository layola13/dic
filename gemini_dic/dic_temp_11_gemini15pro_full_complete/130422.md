nonabiding:/ˌnɒnəˈbaɪdɪŋ/
adjective
不遵守的，不持久的
Not abiding; not obeying or enduring.
The nonabiding nature of his promises led to distrust. 他不遵守承诺的本性导致了不信任。
Near Synonyms: disobedient, rebellious, unruly, lawless, noncompliant, insubordinate, defiant, recalcitrant, contumacious, refractory
Antonyms: obedient, compliant, law-abiding, abiding

词性: adjective
