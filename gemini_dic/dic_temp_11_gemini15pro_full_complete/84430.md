ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin hallucinatio (“a wandering of the mind, error”), from Latin hallucinari (“to wander in mind, talk idly, dream”), from alucinari (“to wander in mind”), from Greek ἀλύω (alúō, “I wander”).  来自晚期拉丁语 hallucinatio（“精神错乱，错误”），来自拉丁语 hallucinari（“精神错乱，胡言乱语，做梦”），来自 alucinari（“精神错乱”），来自希腊语 ἀλύω (alúō，“我徘徊”)。
