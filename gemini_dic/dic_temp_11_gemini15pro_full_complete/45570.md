curliest:/ˈkɜːrliɪst/| adj.|最卷曲的|Superlative form of curly: most curly.  Having the most curls or waves.|Her hair was the curliest I had ever seen. 她的头发是我见过的最卷曲的。|近义词：waviest, kinkiest|反义词:straightest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *curli, from Old English cyrlu (“curly, curled”), from Proto-Germanic *krulijaz, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to turn, bend”).
