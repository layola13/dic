corrasion:/kəˈreɪʒən/ | n. | 磨损，冲蚀，侵蚀 |  The process of wearing away a surface by the action of other matter, such as water, wind, or ice carrying sediment. | The canyon walls show clear signs of corrasion from wind and sand. 峡谷壁上显示出明显的风沙磨损痕迹。 | The Grand Canyon is a testament to the power of corrasion over millions of years.  大峡谷证明了数百万年来侵蚀的力量。 | 近义词：erosion, abrasion, attrition | 反义词：accretion, deposition | 词性：名词
