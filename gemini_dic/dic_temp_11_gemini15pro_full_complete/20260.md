birthed:/bɜːrθt/
v.
分娩，生产；使诞生；产生
gave birth to; brought into existence; created
例句：She birthed a healthy baby boy. 她生了一个健康的男婴。

例句：The project birthed several successful spin-off companies. 这个项目催生了几个成功的衍生公司。

近义词：bore, delivered, produced, generated, spawned
反义词：killed, destroyed

词性：动词 (verb)


词源：From birth + -ed.  The verb "birth" itself comes from Old English "byrþ" meaning "birth, origin".
