cagier:/ˈkɑːʒeɪ/ (US), /ˈkæʒɪə/ (UK)| n. |记账员，出纳员|A person who keeps financial records for a business or organization.  A cashier. Though less common now, sometimes refers specifically to a cashier in a gambling establishment.|The cagier at the casino quickly counted out the winnings.  赌场里的出纳员快速地数出了赢款。|Synonyms: cashier, teller, treasurer|Antonyms: customer, client|noun
