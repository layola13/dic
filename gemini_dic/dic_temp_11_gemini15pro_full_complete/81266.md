granivores:/ˈɡrænɪvɔːrz/| n. |食谷动物；谷食性动物|Animals that primarily eat seeds and grain.|Squirrels and mice are common granivores. 松鼠和老鼠是常见的食谷动物。|Many desert rodents are granivores, relying on seeds for sustenance. 许多沙漠啮齿动物是食谷动物，依靠种子维持生命。|近义词：seed-eaters, grain-eating animals|反义词:carnivores, herbivores|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Latin *granum* (grain) + *vorare* (to devour).
