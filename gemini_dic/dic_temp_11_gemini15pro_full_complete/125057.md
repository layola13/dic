muley:/ˈmjuːli/ /ˈmʌli/ | adj. n. | 无角的；阉过的；无角的动物（尤指公鹿）| Having no horns, or having had its horns removed; (especially of a male deer) having no horns. | The muley deer grazed peacefully in the meadow.  那只无角的鹿在草地上平静地吃草。|近义词: hornless, polled, dehorned |反义词: horned, antlered |词性:形容词/名词

muley:/ˈmjuːli/ /ˈmʌli/ | n. | 无角的动物；无角公鹿| A hornless animal, especially a male deer.  |  The hunter spotted a large muley hiding in the bushes. 猎人发现一只大型无角鹿藏在灌木丛中。|近义词: hornless deer, polled deer |反义词: stag, buck |词性:名词


词源: From obsolete *muly, probably from Middle English *moyley, from Old French moil "hornless," perhaps from a Germanic source.
