destructively:/dɪˈstrʌktɪvli/
adverb
破坏性地，毁灭性地
in a way that causes destruction or damage.
The hurricane destructively swept through the coastal towns.  飓风破坏性地席卷了沿海城镇。

The fire spread destructively through the forest. 大火破坏性地蔓延了整个森林。

Synonym:ruinously, catastrophically, devastatingly
Antonym:constructively, beneficially, helpfully
词性:副词
