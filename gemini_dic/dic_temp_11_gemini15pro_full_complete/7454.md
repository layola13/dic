anisometric:/ˌænˌaɪsoʊˈmɛtrɪk/
|adj.|
不等轴的，各向异性的|
Having unequal dimensions or measurements, especially in crystallography or ophthalmology.  Referring to crystals, it means having axes of unequal length. In ophthalmology, it describes eyes with significantly different refractive power.|

例句：
The anisometric crystals refracted the light in unusual patterns.
这些不等轴的晶体以不寻常的模式折射光线。

The patient exhibited anisometropia, with one eye significantly more nearsighted than the other.
该患者表现出屈光参差，一只眼睛比另一只眼睛近视得多。


近义词：unequal, asymmetrical, disproportionate
反义词：isometric, symmetrical, proportionate
词性: adjective
