offhandish:/ˌɒfˈhændɪʃ/ /ˌɔːfˈhændɪʃ/| adj.|随便的，唐突的，漫不经心的|Showing little interest or enthusiasm; casual and slightly rude.|He gave an offhandish reply, clearly not taking my question seriously. 他漫不经心地回答，显然没有认真对待我的问题。|例句：She dismissed my suggestion with an offhandish wave of her hand. 她漫不经心地挥了挥手，否定了我的建议。

例句：His offhandish manner made him seem uninterested in the project. 他漫不经心的态度让他看起来对这个项目不感兴趣。|近义词：casual, curt, dismissive, brusque, aloof|反义词：considerate, thoughtful, polite, courteous, engaged|词性:形容词
