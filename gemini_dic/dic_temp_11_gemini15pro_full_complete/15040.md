babushka:/bəˈbʊʃkə/ | n. |  бабушка (俄语), 围巾，头巾 | An elderly woman; a headscarf tied under the chin, typically worn by elderly women. | My babushka always makes the best borscht. 我的祖母总是做最好吃的罗宋汤。 | She wore a colorful babushka to keep her head warm. 她戴了一条色彩鲜艳的头巾保暖。 | 近义词: grandmother, headscarf | 反义词: (grandmother) grandchild | 词性: noun


babushka:/bəˈbʊʃkə/ | n. |  俄罗斯套娃 | A set of wooden dolls of decreasing size placed one inside another. |  The child loved playing with her set of babushka dolls.  这个孩子喜欢玩她的俄罗斯套娃。 |  He bought a beautifully painted babushka as a souvenir. 他买了一个画得很漂亮的俄罗斯套娃作为纪念品。 | 近义词: nested doll, matryoshka doll | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
