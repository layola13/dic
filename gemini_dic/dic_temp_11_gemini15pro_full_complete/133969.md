nonheroical:/ˌnɒnhɪˈrəʊɪkəl/
adjective
非英雄的；不英勇的
Not heroic; lacking the characteristics of a hero; unheroic.
His nonheroical actions during the crisis disappointed many.  他在危机期间不英勇的行为令许多人失望。
The soldier's nonheroical retreat was criticized by his superiors.  这名士兵不英勇的撤退受到了上级的批评。
Synonyms: unheroic, cowardly, pusillanimous, craven
Antonyms: heroic, brave, courageous, valiant
word type: adjective
