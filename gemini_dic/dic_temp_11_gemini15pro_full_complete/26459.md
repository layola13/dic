byte's:/baɪts/| n.|字节的|The possessive form of "byte," indicating ownership or association.  It signifies that something belongs to or is related to a byte.  While grammatically correct, it's less common than using "of a byte" or rephrasing the sentence.  Usually used in technical contexts.|Example: The byte's value is 255. 字节的值是255。|近义词: (No direct synonym for a possessive.  Alternatives depend on context: of a byte, per byte)|反义词: (No antonym for a possessive)|词性:Possessive Noun
