chimaera:/kaɪˈmɪərə/ /kɪˈmɪərə/| n. |嵌合体；虚构的怪物；幻想；妄想|A fire-breathing female monster with a lion's head, a goat's body, and a serpent's tail.  A thing that is hoped or wished for but in fact is illusory or impossible to achieve.|例句：The chimaera of economic stability. 经济稳定的幻想。|例句：She was pursuing a chimaera. 她正在追求一个妄想。|近义词：illusion, fantasy, delusion|反义词：reality|词性：名词

chimerical:/kaɪˈmerɪkəl/ /kɪˈmerɪkəl/| adj. |空想的；妄想的|Wildly fanciful or imaginative.|例句：A chimerical scheme for getting rich.  一个致富的妄想计划。|例句：His dreams of becoming a rock star were chimerical. 他想成为摇滚明星的梦想是不切实际的。|近义词：illusory, fanciful, unrealistic|反义词：realistic, practical|词性：形容词
