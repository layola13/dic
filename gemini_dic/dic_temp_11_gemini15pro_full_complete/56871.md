dos:/dɒs/ (UK) /dɑːs/ (US)| n. |磁盘操作系统（Disk Operating System）|A computer operating system that was popular in the 1980s and early 1990s, especially MS-DOS.  It is characterized by a command-line interface. | Many older computer games were designed to run on DOS. 许多老电脑游戏被设计成在DOS系统上运行。|Synonym: operating system|Antonym: N/A|noun
