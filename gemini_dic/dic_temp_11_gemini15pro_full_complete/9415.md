antiradiation:/ˌæntiˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən/
adjective
防辐射的
Designed to protect against or reduce the harmful effects of radiation.
例句：
The workers wore antiradiation suits. 工人们穿着防辐射服。

例句：
Antiradiation medication can help mitigate the effects of radiation exposure.  防辐射药物可以帮助减轻辐射暴露的影响。


近义词：radioprotective, radiation-proof, radiation-resistant
反义词：radioactive


词性
adjective

词源
anti- (prefix meaning "against") + radiation (noun)
