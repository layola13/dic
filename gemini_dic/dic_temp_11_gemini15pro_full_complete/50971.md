desilverize:/diːˈsɪlvəraɪz/
verb
去除银
To remove silver from.
The process desilverizes the lead ore. 这个过程去除铅矿石中的银。

The photographer desilverized the used fixing solution to recover the silver. 摄影师从用过的定影液中提取银。
近义词: extract silver, remove silver
反义词: silver-plate, silver

词性: verb (动词)
