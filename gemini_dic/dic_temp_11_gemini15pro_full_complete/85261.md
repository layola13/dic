harmonical:/hɑːrˈmɒnɪkəl/ /hɑːrˈmɑːnɪkl/| adj.|和谐的，调和的|Relating to harmony; concordant; musical.  Characterized by harmony of sound; pleasing to the ear.|The harmonical sounds of the orchestra filled the hall.  管弦乐队的和谐之声充满了大厅。|近义词：harmonic, melodious, euphonious|反义词：disharmonious, discordant|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *harmonicālis, from Latin harmonicus (“harmonic”) + -ālis.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek ἁρμονικός (harmonikos).
