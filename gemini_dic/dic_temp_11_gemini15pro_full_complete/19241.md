bhai:/ˈbaɪ/| n.|兄弟，哥们，老兄 (主要用于南亚文化中)|A term of endearment and respect for a brother or close male friend, primarily used in South Asian cultures.  It signifies a bond of brotherhood and camaraderie.|Example:  My bhai, always there for me. 我的兄弟，永远支持我。|Synonym: brother, dude, pal, mate|Antonym: enemy, foe|noun

bhai:/ˈbaɪ/| int.|哎，喂 (表示惊讶、同意或疑问)|An interjection expressing surprise, agreement, or questioning, primarily used in South Asian cultures.|Example: Bhai, what a surprise! 哎，真是个惊喜！|Synonym: wow, hey, right|Antonym: (No direct antonym for an interjection)|interjection
