gul:/ɡʌl/| n.|轻信的人，容易受骗的人|A gul is a person who is easily tricked or fooled. |例句：He's such a gul, he believed everything she said. 他真是个容易受骗的人，她说什么他都信。

例句：The con artist easily took advantage of the gul. 骗子很容易就占了这个轻信的人的便宜。|近义词：sucker, dupe, chump, fool|反义词:skeptic, cynic|词性:noun
