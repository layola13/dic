hockamore:/ˈhɑːkəmɔːr/| n.|马笼头，牛鼻绳|A rope or strap used to tie a horse's head to its leg, or to tie a cow's head to its foreleg, to prevent it from straying.|The farmer used a hockamore to keep the young colt from wandering off. 农民用马笼头防止小马驹走失。|Synonyms: hobble, fetter|Antonyms: unleash, free|noun
