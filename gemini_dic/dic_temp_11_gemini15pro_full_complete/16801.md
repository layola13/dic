baseborn:/ˈbeɪsbɔːrn/
adjective
出身低微的，私生的
born of low parentage; illegitimate.
He was mocked for being baseborn, his father unknown. 他因出身低微，父亲不详而被人嘲笑。
The baseborn child was never truly accepted by the royal family.  这个私生子从未被王室真正接受。
近义词:lowborn, illegitimate, bastard
反义词:noble, legitimate, highborn
词性:adjective

词源:
From base (lowly) + born.  The word dates back to Middle English.
