outfigured:/ˌaʊtˈfɪɡərd/
verb
胜过，智胜
surpassed in figuring or calculating; defeated in a contest of skill or cunning
He outfigured his opponent in the chess match. 他在棋赛中智胜了对手。
Synonyms: outwit, outsmart, outmaneuver
Antonyms: be outwitted, be outsmarted
