disvaluation:/dɪsˌvæljuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 贬值，低估 |  The act of lowering or reducing the value or importance of something. | The disvaluation of the currency led to economic instability. 货币贬值导致经济不稳定。| synonyms: devaluation, depreciation, undervaluation, belittling | antonyms: appreciation, valuation, increase, enhancement | noun

disvalue:/dɪsˈvæljuː/ | vt. | 贬低，轻视 | To consider or treat as having little value or importance. |  He tends to disvalue the opinions of others. 他倾向于轻视别人的意见。| synonyms: undervalue, underestimate, depreciate, belittle | antonyms: value, appreciate, overestimate, esteem | verb
