operosity:/ˌɒpəˈrɒsɪti/ /ˌɑːpəˈrɑːsɪti/
n.
慷慨，大方，乐善好施
The quality of being generous or liberal in giving.
Her operosity is well known throughout the community.  她在社区里以慷慨大方而闻名。
He showed great operosity in donating to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐款，表现出极大的慷慨。
近义词：generosity, liberality, munificence, bounty
反义词：stinginess, meanness, parsimony, avarice
词性：名词

词源: From Latin *operositas*, from operosus (“active, busy, laborious”), from opus (“work”).  源自拉丁语 *operositas*，来自 operosus（“活跃的，忙碌的，勤劳的”），来自 opus（“工作”）。
