accommodational: /əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃənəl/
|adj.|
|住宿的；适应的；迁就的|
|Relating to accommodation, or designed to accommodate;  adapting or making adjustments to suit needs or circumstances.|

|The hotel offered accommodational facilities for families with young children.  这家酒店为有小孩的家庭提供住宿设施。|

|The company adopted an accommodational approach to meet the diverse needs of its employees. 公司采取了一种适应性方法来满足员工的多样化需求。|


|近义词: adaptive, flexible, accommodating|
|反义词: inflexible, rigid, unaccommodating|
|词性: adjective|
