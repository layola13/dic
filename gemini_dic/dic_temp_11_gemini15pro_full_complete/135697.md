nonpreservation:/ˌnɑːnprɛzərˈveɪʃən/ |n.| 未保存，未保护，不保存 |The nonpreservation of these historical documents is a great loss. 这些历史文献没有被保存下来是一个巨大的损失。| The nonpreservation of the rainforest will lead to devastating consequences.  对雨林的未保护将会导致毁灭性的后果。 | 近义词: neglect, lack of preservation, failure to preserve | 反义词: preservation, conservation, protection | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
preservation (noun): from Late Middle English preservacioun, from Old French preservacion, from Latin praeservatio(n-), from praeservare "to preserve."
