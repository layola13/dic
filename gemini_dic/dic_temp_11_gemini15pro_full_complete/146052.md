overdrynesses:/ˌoʊvərˈdraɪnɪsɪz/
noun
过度干燥
The state of being excessively dry.
The overdrynesses of her skin caused discomfort.  她的皮肤过度干燥导致不适。
近义词: aridity, desiccation
反义词: moistness, humidity
词性: noun


Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "dry" (Old English drīge, from Proto-Germanic *draugiz, meaning "dry") + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), a suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
