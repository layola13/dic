hatlessness:/ˈhæt.ləs.nəs/
n.
无帽状态; 不戴帽子的状态
The state of being without a hat.
His hatlessness in the blizzard was a foolish choice. 他在暴风雪中不戴帽子是个愚蠢的选择。
Near synonyms: bareheadedness, uncovered head
Opposite: wearing a hat
