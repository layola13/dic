caricature: /ˈkærɪkətʃər/ (US), /ˈkærɪkətʃʊə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|漫画；讽刺画；夸张的描述；滑稽模仿|A picture, description, or imitation of a person or thing in which certain striking characteristics are exaggerated in order to create a comic or grotesque effect.|The politician was easily identified by the caricature of him in the newspaper.  这位政治家很容易从报纸上对他的漫画中被认出来。|He caricatured the president's mannerisms perfectly. 他完美地模仿了总统的举止。|synonyms: cartoon, parody, satire, burlesque, lampoon|antonyms: portrait, likeness|noun, verb
