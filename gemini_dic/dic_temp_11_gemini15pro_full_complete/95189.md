inaugurative: /ɪˈnɔːɡjərətɪv/ /ɪˈnɑːɡjərətɪv/
adjective
就职的，开幕的
Marking the beginning of an institution, activity, or period of office; relating to an inauguration.
The president's inaugural address was inspiring. 总统的就职演说是鼓舞人心的。
The museum held an inaugurative exhibition.  博物馆举办了开幕展览。
Synonyms:  opening, introductory, initial, commencing, beginning, inception
Antonyms:  closing, concluding, final, ending
词性: adjective
