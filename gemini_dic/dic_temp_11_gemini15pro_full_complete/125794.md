murine:/ˈmjʊəraɪn/| adj.|鼠的，鼠科动物的|Relating to or characteristic of mice or rats.  Of or relating to the family Muridae (the largest family of rodents, containing over 700 species, including rats and mice).|例句：The study used a murine model to investigate the disease.  这项研究使用鼠模型来研究这种疾病。

例句：Murine leukemia viruses are common in laboratory mice.  鼠白血病病毒在实验室小鼠中很常见。|近义词：ratlike, mouselike, rodent|反义词:(No direct antonym, could consider "human" in specific contexts)|词性:adjective
