bitmapping:/ˈbɪtmæpɪŋ/| n.|位图映射，位图化|The process of representing images or characters as a pattern of dots (bits) on a screen or printer.  将图像或字符表示为屏幕或打印机上点（位）的模式的过程。|Example: Bitmapping is essential for displaying images on a computer screen. 位图映射对于在计算机屏幕上显示图像至关重要。

Example: The printer uses bitmapping technology to produce high-quality output.  这台打印机使用位图映射技术来产生高质量的输出。|近义词：rasterization, pixelization|反义词：vectorization|词性：noun
