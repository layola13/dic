manically:/məˈnɪkəli/ | adv. | 狂躁地，疯狂地 | In a wild, enthusiastic, or excited way, typically characterized by uncontrolled or excessive activity. | He paced the room manically. 他疯狂地在房间里踱步。| She laughed manically. 她狂笑不止。 | 近义词：frantically, hysterically, wildly, excitedly, furiously | 反义词：calmly, peacefully, serenely | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "manic" + "-ally" (adverbial suffix). "Manic" derives from the Greek "manikos," meaning "mad, insane," related to "mania."
