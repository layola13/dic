donga:/ˈdɒŋɡə/| n. |峡谷，沟壑；(南非俚语) 城镇，郊区|A deep ravine or gully, especially one with a stream. (South African slang) A town or suburb. |The hikers followed the donga down to the river.  徒步旅行者沿着峡谷走到河边。| 近义词：ravine, gully, gorge, canyon (formal), kloof (South African) |反义词: ridge, peak|名词

donga:/ˈdɒŋɡə/| n. |(南非俚语)  麻烦，困境 |(South African slang) Trouble, difficulty. |He got himself into a bit of a donga. 他陷入了一点麻烦。|近义词：trouble, difficulty, predicament, mess|反义词: solution, ease|名词
