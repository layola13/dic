cryology:/kraɪˈɒlədʒi/
n.
低温学
The study of low temperatures, their production, and their effects on matter.
Cryology plays a crucial role in understanding the behavior of materials at extremely low temperatures.  低温学在理解极低温下材料的行为方面起着至关重要的作用。
Cryology is a fascinating field with applications in various scientific disciplines. 低温学是一个引人入胜的领域，在各种科学学科中都有应用。
近义词:low-temperature physics
反义词:thermology
