careen:/kəˈriːn/
Verb
倾斜，(尤指车辆)歪歪斜斜地疾驶，使倾斜
To lean, sway, or tip to one side while in motion (especially of a vehicle); to cause to lean or tip over.
The car careened around the corner, nearly hitting a pedestrian. 汽车歪歪斜斜地驶过拐角，差点撞到一个行人。
Synonyms: tilt, lean, lurch, sway, tip
Antonyms: straighten, stabilize, right
词性: 动词

careen:/kəˈriːn/
Verb
(船只)倾侧，(船)为清理或修理而倾侧
(of a ship) to turn (a ship) on its side for cleaning or repair.
The ship was careened on the beach for repairs to its hull. 这艘船被倾斜在沙滩上修理船体。
Synonyms: heel, list
Antonyms: right
词性: 动词
