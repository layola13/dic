mur:/mɜːr/ (British) /mɝːr/ (American)| n.|低沉的抱怨声，咕哝声；(尤指)不满的低语|A low, indistinct, continuous sound, typically of complaint or dissatisfaction; a muttered complaint, especially one expressing dissatisfaction.|The crowd responded with a mur of disapproval. 人群发出不满的低语声。|Synonyms: mutter, murmur, grumble, whisper|Antonyms: shout, yell, proclaim|noun
