hexaphyllous:/ˌheksəˈfɪləs/
adjective
六叶的；有六片花瓣（或萼片）的
Having six petals or six leaves in a whorl.
The hexaphyllous flower attracted the bee.  这朵六瓣的花吸引了蜜蜂。
Synonyms: six-petaled, six-leaved (in a whorl)
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could use terms like "few-petaled" or specify a different number of petals/leaves like "five-petaled/pentaphyllous")
Etymology: From Greek *hexa- (six) + *phyllon (leaf)
