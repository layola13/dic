nonrealists:/ˌnɒnˈriːəlɪsts/ | n. |非现实主义者；不切实际的人|People who do not base their beliefs or actions on what is actually happening or possible.  They may be idealistic, utopian, or simply impractical.| Example: The nonrealists in the group kept suggesting impossible solutions.  小组里那些不切实际的人不断提出不可能的解决方案。
| Example:  He was labeled a nonrealist for believing world peace could be achieved overnight.  他因为相信世界和平可以一夜之间实现而被贴上了不切实际的标签。| Synonyms: idealists, dreamers, utopians, fantasists | Antonyms: realists, pragmatists | Noun
