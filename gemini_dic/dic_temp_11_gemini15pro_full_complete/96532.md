indoles:/ˈɪndoʊlz/ | n. |吲哚|A class of aromatic heterocyclic organic compounds containing a benzene ring fused to a pyrrole ring.  They are found in many natural products and are important building blocks in organic chemistry. | Tryptophan, an essential amino acid, is an indole derivative. 色氨酸，一种必需氨基酸，是一种吲哚衍生物。| Indoles are responsible for the characteristic fecal odor. 吲哚是粪便特有气味的原因。 | synonyms:  benzopyrroles | antonyms:  None | noun
