overinclination: /ˌoʊvərɪnklɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
过度倾向，过度倾斜
An excessive inclination or tendency towards something.
The cyclist's overinclination on the sharp turn caused him to crash.  这位骑自行车的人在急转弯时过度倾斜导致他摔倒了。
Overinclination to agree with authority can stifle critical thinking.  过度倾向于同意权威会扼杀批判性思维。
Synonyms: propensity, predisposition, tendency, leaning
Antonyms: aversion, disinclination, resistance
