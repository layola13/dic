flabbiest:/ˈflæbiɪst/| adj.|最松弛的，最肥胖的|Superlative form of flabby: most flabby.  Meaning having soft, loose flesh; not firm.  It can refer to physical condition or, metaphorically, to a lack of strength or effectiveness.|His arms were flabbiest after he stopped working out.  他停止锻炼后，胳膊变得最松弛了。|The flabbiest excuse I've ever heard! 我听过的最站不住脚的借口！|Synonyms: softest, loosest, flaccid,  limp|Antonyms: firmest, tightest, toned, muscular|adjective
