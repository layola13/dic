kilted:/ˈkɪltɪd/
adjective
穿着苏格兰短裙的；褶裥状的
Wearing a kilt; pleated.
例句：
He looked handsome in his kilted attire.
他穿着苏格兰短裙的服装，看起来很英俊。

The kilted dancers performed with energy and grace.
穿着褶裥裙的舞者充满活力和优雅地表演。

近义词：pleated, skirted
反义词：unpleated, trousered
词性：adjective

词源：From *kilt* + *-ed*. *Kilt* is of uncertain origin, perhaps from a Scandinavian source related to Old Norse *kjilt* "tuck up," from *kjalta* "to lap over."
