creak:/kriːk/ /kriːk/| vi.|嘎吱作响|To make a harsh, grating sound, typically of wooden or metal objects rubbing together.|The door creaked open. 门嘎吱一声打开了。|近义词：squeak, groan, grate|反义词:silence|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *creken, *criken, from Old English *crēcian (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *krekōną (“to make a noise, creak”), from Proto-Indo-European *g(ʷ)erHg- (“to swallow, resound”). Cognate with Middle Dutch crijken (“to creak”), German kreischen (“to shriek, screech”).
