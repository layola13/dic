motifs:/moʊˈtiːfs/ (US), /məʊˈtiːfs/ (UK)| n. |主题; 中心思想; 基调; 装饰图案; 花纹|A recurring subject, theme, idea, especially in a literary, artistic, or musical work.  A distinctive and recurring form, shape, or figure in a design.|The wallpaper has a floral motif.  壁纸上有花的图案。 Recurring motifs of despair and alienation permeate the novel.  绝望和疏离的反复出现的主题贯穿了整部小说。|themes, patterns, designs, figures, elements, images, ideas, subjects|antonyms: N/A (Motifs represent recurring elements, the opposite would imply a lack of recurrence, which isn't a single word.)|noun
