mediates:/ˈmiːdieɪts/| v. | 调解，斡旋；传递，传导 | acts as an intermediary between two parties to effect an agreement or reconciliation; transmits or conveys something | The lawyer mediates disputes between landlords and tenants.  律师调解房东和租户之间的纠纷。 | synonyms: arbitrates, intercedes, negotiates, conveys, transmits | antonyms:  agitates, instigates, hinders | verb
