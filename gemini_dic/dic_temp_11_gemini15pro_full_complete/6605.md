anaclastic:/ˌænəˈklæstɪk/
adjective
变形表面的;折射的
Relating to or denoting refraction by an uneven surface.  Specifically referring to lenses or mirrors that have an uneven surface, designed to produce a specific type of refraction.
An anaclastic curve is used in the design of certain lenses.  变形表面曲线用于某些透镜的设计。
The artist used an anaclastic mirror to create a distorted reflection.  这位艺术家使用变形镜来创造扭曲的反射。
近义词:refractive, astigmatic
反义词:regular, smooth
词性:adjective
