imperatively:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪvli/| adv.|命令式地，强制地，必须地|In a commanding or authoritative manner; necessarily.|例句：The general spoke imperatively to his troops. 将军用命令的口吻对他的部队讲话。|例句：It is imperatively necessary that we act now. 我们现在必须采取行动。|近义词：peremptorily, urgently, necessarily, essentially|反义词:optionally, unnecessarily|词性:副词

词源: From Late Latin *imperativus*, from Latin imperare "to command," from in- "in" + parare "to prepare."  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *pere- (1) "to lead across."
