endognath:/ˈɛndoʊɡnæθ/ | n. | 内颚类动物 | An arthropod of a group characterized by having mouthparts enclosed within the head, as in insects, myriapods, and arachnids. |  Endognaths are a diverse group of arthropods. 内颚类动物是种类繁多的节肢动物群。| synonym:  entognatha (less common) | antonym: ectognath | noun
