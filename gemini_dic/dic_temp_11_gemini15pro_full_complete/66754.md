exortive:/ɪɡˈzɔːrtɪv/| adj.|驱逐的，逐出的；倾向于驱逐的|Tending to drive out or expel.  Relating to or causing expulsion, especially of evil spirits.|The shaman performed an exortive ritual to cleanse the village of malevolent spirits.  萨满进行了驱邪仪式，以清除村庄里的恶灵。|近义词：expulsive, purgative, cleansing|反义词：attractive, inviting|词性:adjective

Etymology: From Latin *exortīvus*, from *exortus*, past participle of *exorīrī* "to rise, appear, begin".  Ultimately from *ex-* "out" + *orīrī* "to rise, originate".
