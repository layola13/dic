ginger:/ˈdʒɪndʒər/ (US) /ˈdʒɪndʒə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.|姜；姜黄色；精力；活力|A pungent, spicy rhizome, the common spice; a light yellowish-orange to moderate or strong reddish-orange color; spirit or vigor.|She added fresh ginger to the stir-fry. 她往炒菜里加了鲜姜。|近义词：pep, zest, spice|反义词:apathy, lethargy|noun

ginger:/ˈdʒɪndʒər/ (US) /ˈdʒɪndʒə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|姜黄色的；淡红色的|Of a light yellowish-orange to moderate or strong reddish-orange color.|The cat had ginger fur. 这只猫的毛是姜黄色的。|近义词：reddish, yellowish|反义词:brunette, dark|adjective
