irresistible:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbl/ /ˌɪrɪˈzɪstɪbl/ | adj. | 不可抗拒的；无法抵挡的 | Too attractive and tempting to be resisted. |  例句：The chocolate cake was irresistible.  巧克力蛋糕令人无法抗拒。

例句：She gave him an irresistible smile. 她给了他一个令人无法抗拒的微笑。

近义词：compelling, alluring, enticing, tempting | 反义词：resistible, unappealing, unattractive | 词性：形容词

irresistibles:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəblz/ /ˌɪrɪˈzɪstɪblz/ | n. | 无法抗拒的东西（通常指复数）| Things that are too attractive and tempting to be resisted. | 例句：The shop window was full of irresistibles.  商店橱窗里摆满了令人无法抗拒的商品。

例句：The new spring fashions are simply irresistibles.  新的春季时装简直让人无法抗拒。

近义词：temptations, allurements | 反义词：/ | 词性：名词
