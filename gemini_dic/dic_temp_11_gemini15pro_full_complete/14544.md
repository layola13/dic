aversion:/əˈvɜːrʒən/
n.
厌恶，反感
a strong dislike or disinclination.
He had a deep aversion to getting up early. 他非常讨厌早起。
disinclination, dislike, distaste, antipathy, abhorrence, loathing
liking, fondness, preference, inclination
noun
