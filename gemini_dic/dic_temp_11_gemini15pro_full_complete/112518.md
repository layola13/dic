louvre:/ˈluːvər/ (US) /ˈluːvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|百叶窗；通风窗；天窗|A set of sloping slats fixed in a frame, used to admit air and light while excluding rain and the sun. Also, a domed structure with openings, often used as a roof or ceiling.|The louvres on the roof were open to let in the cool night air. 屋顶上的百叶窗打开了，让凉爽的夜风进来。|synonyms: shutter, blind, vent, ventilator|antonyms: solid wall, closed window|noun
