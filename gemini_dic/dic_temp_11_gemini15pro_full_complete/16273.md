baptises:/bæpˈtaɪzɪz/
verb
施洗；命名
administers baptism to; christens.
John the Baptist baptised Jesus in the River Jordan.  施洗约翰在约旦河为耶稣施洗。
christens, names
--- (no antonym applicable)
verb

baptize:/bæpˈtaɪz/
verb
施洗；命名
administers baptism to; christens.
They baptized their child Sarah. 他们给孩子施洗，取名莎拉。
christen, name
--- (no antonym applicable)
