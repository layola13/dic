interwreathing:/ˌɪntərˈriːðɪŋ/
adjective
相互缠绕的，交织的
The definition of interwreathing is twisting or twining together; intertwining.  Two vines interwreathing around each other.
例句：The lovers walked hand in hand, their fingers interwreathing. 恋人们手牵着手走着，手指相互缠绕。

例句：The intricate design featured interwreathing vines and flowers.  这复杂的设计以交织的藤蔓和花朵为特色。
近义词：intertwined, interwoven, interlaced
反义词：separated, disentangled
词性：adjective

interwreathing:/ˌɪntərˈriːðɪŋ/
noun
相互缠绕，交织
The act of twisting or twining together.
例句：The interwreathing of the branches created a natural archway. 树枝的交织形成了一个天然的拱门。

例句：The artist captured the interwreathing of light and shadow in his painting.  艺术家在他的画作中捕捉到了光影的交织。
近义词：intertwining, interweaving, interlacing
反义词：separation, disentanglement
词性：noun
