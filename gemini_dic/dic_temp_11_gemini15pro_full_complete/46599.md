damasken:/ˈdæməskən/| v. |镶嵌，装饰（尤指用大马士革钢）|To decorate with a pattern, usually of inlaid metal, especially damascus steel.|The craftsman damaskened the sword with gold and silver.|近义词：inlay, adorn, embellish, decorate|反义词:strip, bare|词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle French *damasquiner*, from Old French *damasquin*, from *damas*, meaning "Damascus".  Damascus steel, originating in Damascus, Syria, was known for its distinctive patterns, which led to the verb meaning to decorate in a similar style.
