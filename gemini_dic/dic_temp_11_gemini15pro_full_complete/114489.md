malacopterygian:/ˌmæləˈkɒptərɪdʒɪən/ /ˌmæləˈkɑːptəˌrɪdʒiən/
noun|软鳍鱼类|A fish of the superclass Malacopterygii, characterized by soft, segmented fin rays.|The trout is a malacopterygian.|Synonyms: soft-finned fish|Antonyms: acanthopterygian (spiny-rayed fish)|n.
