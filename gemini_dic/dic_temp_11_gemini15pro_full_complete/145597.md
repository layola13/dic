overcondensing:/ˌoʊvərˈkənˌdɛnsɪŋ/
verb
过度压缩，过度浓缩
reducing the volume of something, such as steam or data, to an excessive degree
The engineer warned against overcondensing the steam, as it could damage the engine.  工程师警告不要过度压缩蒸汽，因为这会损坏发动机。
The algorithm was overcondensing the data, leading to a loss of important information. 该算法过度压缩了数据，导致重要信息丢失。
