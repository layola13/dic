beggary:/ˈbɛɡəri/| n.|乞讨；贫穷；乞丐身份|The state of being a beggar; extreme poverty; the practice of begging.|He lived a life of beggary on the streets. 他在街上过着乞讨的生活。|近义词：poverty, destitution, mendicancy|反义词：wealth, affluence|词性:noun
Etymology: From Middle English *beggerie*, from Old French *begarie*. Ultimately from *beggar* + *-y*.
