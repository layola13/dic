ETYMOLOGY->
From bass + -ist.  "Bass" ultimately comes from the Italian "basso" meaning "low".  "-ist" is a suffix used to form nouns indicating a person who performs a particular activity or plays a particular instrument.

SYNONYMS->
bass player (for bass guitar)
double bass player (for double bass)
low voice singer (for singing)


ANTONYMS->
(No direct antonyms for a person playing an instrument. One could consider higher-pitched instruments as a contrasting concept, e.g., soprano, tenor.)
