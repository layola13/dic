aurae:/ˈɔːriː/
noun
光环，气氛，气象
a distinctive atmosphere surrounding a given source.
The painting has an aura of mystery. 这幅画有一种神秘的气氛。
synonyms: atmosphere, ambience, air, feel, mood, vibe, quality, character, spirit, emanation, radiation
antonyms: blandness, dullness
n.

aurae:/ˈɔːriː/
noun
(尤指圣人、神灵头上的)光环，光轮
(especially in paintings, etc.) a bright light that can be seen around the head of a holy person or an angel
Angels are traditionally depicted with aurae/aura surrounding their heads.  天使传统上被描绘成头部周围有光环。
synonyms: halo, nimbus, aureole, corona, glory, radiance
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: shadow, darkness)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin aura ("breeze, air"). The plural form is either aurae (Classical Latin) or auras (Late Latin and English).
