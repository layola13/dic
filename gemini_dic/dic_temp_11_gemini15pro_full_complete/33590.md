cimbal:/ˈtʃɪmbəl/| n.|扬琴，匈牙利的一种弦乐器|A cimbalom is a stringed musical instrument of the dulcimer family, with a trapezoidal soundboard. It has strings struck with hammers.|The band's music featured a cimbalom, giving it a unique sound. 乐队的音乐以扬琴为特色，赋予了它独特的声音。|Synonyms: dulcimer, hammered dulcimer|Antonyms: None|noun
