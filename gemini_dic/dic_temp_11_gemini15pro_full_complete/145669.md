overcontrolled: /ˌoʊvər kənˈtroʊld/ | adj. | 过度控制的，控制过度的 |  Excessively controlled, often referring to emotions or behavior, implying an unhealthy or restrictive level of self-regulation. | He seemed overcontrolled, never showing any emotion. 他看起来控制过度，从不显露任何情绪。 |  She had an overcontrolled personality, always keeping her feelings bottled up. 她性格过度压抑，总是把自己的感受憋在心里。 | 近义词: repressed, inhibited, restrained | 反义词: uninhibited, expressive, impulsive | 词性: 形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "controlled" (past participle of the verb "control")
