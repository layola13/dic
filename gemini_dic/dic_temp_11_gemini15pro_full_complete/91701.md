hyperconscientiousness:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɒnʃiˈɛnʃəsnəs/ | n. | 过度认真; 过度尽责; 过度良心不安 |  The state of being excessively conscientious, often to the point of scrupulosity or anxiety. It involves an extreme focus on doing things perfectly and adhering strictly to rules and moral principles.  |  Her hyperconscientiousness made it difficult for her to delegate tasks. 她的过度认真使得她难以委派任务。 |  meticulousness, scrupulousness, conscientiousness | carelessness, negligence | noun
