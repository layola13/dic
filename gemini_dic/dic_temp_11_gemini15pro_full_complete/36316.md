coholder:/ˈkəʊˌhəʊldər/ (US) /ˈkoʊˌhoʊldər/| n.|共同持有者，共同继承人，联名持有人|A person who holds something jointly with another or others, such as property, a title, or an office.|例句：The coholders of the patent will share the profits equally. 专利共同持有人将平分利润。

例句：They are coholders of the world record. 他们是这项世界纪录的共同保持者。|近义词：joint owner, joint tenant, co-owner|反义词:sole owner, sole proprietor|词性:noun


coholders:/ˈkəʊˌhəʊldərz/ (US) /ˈkoʊˌhoʊldərz/| n.|共同持有者们，共同继承人们，联名持有人们|Plural form of coholder.|例句：The coholders of the property were notified of the sale. 房产的共同持有人被告知了出售事宜。

例句：All coholders must agree to the terms of the contract. 所有共同持有人都必须同意合同条款。|近义词：joint owners, joint tenants, co-owners|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
