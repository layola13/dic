nonpurulent:/ˌnɒnˈpjʊərələnt/ /ˌnɑːnˈpjʊrələnt/| adj.|无脓的|Not containing or discharging pus; not suppurative.|The wound was clean and nonpurulent.  伤口干净且无脓。|The doctor confirmed the rash was nonpurulent. 医生确认皮疹没有化脓。|近义词：non-suppurative, sterile, clean|反义词: purulent, suppurative|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + purulent.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "purulent" comes from the Latin word "purulentus," meaning "full of pus," from "pus" (pus) + "-lent" (full of).
