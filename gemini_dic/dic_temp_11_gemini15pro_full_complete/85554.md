hatching:/ˈhætʃɪŋ/| n. v.|孵化，孵出; 策划，筹划|The hatching of the eggs took several weeks.  蛋的孵化持续了几个星期。  The villains were hatching a plot to overthrow the government.  坏人们正在策划一个推翻政府的阴谋。|例句：The chicks are hatching. 小鸡正在孵化。 The scientists are hatching a plan to save the endangered species. 科学家们正在制定一项拯救濒危物种的计划。|近义词：incubation, brooding, plotting, scheming|反义词：none|词性:名词, 动词
Etymology: From Middle English *hacchen, from Old English *hæċċan, from Proto-Germanic *hakkōną (“to hatch, hack”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to cut”).
