demobilises:/ˌdiːˈməʊbəlaɪzɪz/
verb
复员，遣散（尤指战时）
discharges (someone) from military service.
The government demobilised the troops after the war.  政府在战后遣散了军队。
synonyms: discharge, release, dismiss
antonyms: enlist, recruit, mobilize
词性: 动词
