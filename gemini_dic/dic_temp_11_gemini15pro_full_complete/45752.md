cushiest:/ˈkʊʃiɪst/ | adj. | 最舒适的，最安逸的 |  Superlative form of "cushioned," meaning offering the most padding, comfort, and ease.  It often implies a situation or job that is very easy and undemanding. |  He landed the cushiest job in the company. 他得到了公司里最轻松的工作。 |  comfortable, cozy, soft, plush, luxurious |  uncomfortable, hard, austere, demanding, difficult | adjective
