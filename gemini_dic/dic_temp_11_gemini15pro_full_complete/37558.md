commorancy:/ˈkɒmərənsi/ | n. | 居住；住所 |A period of residence; a dwelling place. |He established his commorancy in a small cottage by the sea. 他在海边的一间小别墅里安顿下来。|例句：The family took up their commorancy in a new town.  这个家庭在一个新的城镇定居下来。

例句：Her commorancy was a grand estate, passed down through generations. 她的住所是一座宏伟的庄园，世代相传。

近义词：residence, dwelling, abode, domicile | 反义词： N/A | 词性：noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *commorantia, from Latin commorans, present participle of commorari "to reside, stay, sojourn," from com- "together" + morari "to delay, tarry."
