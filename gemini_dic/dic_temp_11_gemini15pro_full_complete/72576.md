flexibility:/ˌflɛksəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 灵活性，柔韧性，适应性 | The ability to be easily modified or adapted to different conditions. |  Yoga increases flexibility.  瑜伽可以增强柔韧性。 | synonym: adaptability, elasticity, pliancy | antonym: rigidity, inflexibility, stiffness | noun

flexibility:/ˌflɛksəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 灵活性，柔韧性，适应性 | The quality of being easily bent or shaped without breaking. |  The flexibility of the material allows it to be molded into various shapes.  这种材料的柔韧性使它可以被塑造成各种形状。 | synonym: suppleness, plasticity, malleability | antonym: brittleness, fragility, hardness | noun
