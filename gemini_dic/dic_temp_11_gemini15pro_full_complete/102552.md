jaganatha:/ˌdʒʌɡəˈnɑːθə/| n.| 札格纳特，主宰世界者，（比喻）不可抗拒的强大力量|A form of Krishna, worshipped especially in Puri, Odisha, India, and often regarded as a destructive force.|The festival of Jagannatha is celebrated with great pomp and show.  札格纳特节庆祝活动盛大而隆重。|The car festival of Jagannatha attracts millions of devotees. 札格纳特的车节吸引了数百万信徒。|近义词：Krishna, Lord, deity|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Sanskrit जगन्नाथ (jagannātha), from जगत् (jagat, “world, universe”) + नाथ (nātha, “lord, protector”). Literally "lord of the universe".
