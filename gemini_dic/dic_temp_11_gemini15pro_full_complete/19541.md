biforine: /baɪˈfɔːraɪn/
noun
双叉形, 两叉的, 具有两个开口的
having two openings or forks; forked.  Relating to or resembling a biforine, a type of two-pronged, often perforated pendant worn in the late Roman and early medieval periods.
The archaeologist unearthed a delicate biforine pendant. 考古学家出土了一个精致的双叉形吊坠。
near synonyms: forked, bifurcate, two-pronged
antonyms: single, unified, unbranched
n.
