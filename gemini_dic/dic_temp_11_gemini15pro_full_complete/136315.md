nonrequirable:/ˌnɒnrɪˈkwaɪərəbəl/
adjective
非必需的，不需要的
Not necessary or required; able to be dispensed with.
This feature is nonrequirable and can be deactivated.  这个功能是非必需的，可以停用。
The extra paperwork is nonrequirable and just adds to the bureaucratic burden. 这些额外的文书工作是不必要的，只会增加官僚负担。
synonyms: unnecessary, optional, dispensable, inessential
antonyms: necessary, required, essential, mandatory
词性: 形容词
