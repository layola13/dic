nonpervasively:/ˌnɒnpərˈveɪsɪvli/
adv.
非普遍地，非渗透地
Not in a pervasive manner; not spreading throughout.
The disease manifested nonpervasively, affecting only specific areas of the body.  这种疾病表现得并不普遍，只影响身体的特定区域。
The new technology was adopted nonpervasively, with many companies choosing to stick with older systems. 这项新技术并未得到普遍采用，许多公司选择继续使用旧系统。
近义词:locally, partially, selectively
反义词:pervasively, ubiquitously, widely
词性:副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
pervasive (adjective): from Latin pervadere "to pass through, pervade," from per- "through" + vadere "to go"
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
