alcoranist:/ælˈkɔːrənɪst/
n.
信奉古兰经者；伊斯兰教徒
A believer in the Quran; a Muslim.
He was raised as an Alcoranist, adhering to the teachings of Islam. 他从小被培养成一名伊斯兰教徒，坚持伊斯兰教的教义。

近义词: Muslim, Moslem, Islamic follower, believer in Islam
反义词: non-Muslim, infidel (in some contexts, considered offensive)
词性: noun
