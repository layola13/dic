hash-out:/hæʃ ˈaʊt/| phrasal verb | 充分讨论；详细讨论；争论解决 | to discuss something thoroughly, especially in order to reach an agreement or settle a dispute |  We need to hash out the details of the contract before we sign. 我们需要在签合同之前充分讨论合同的细节。

| Example: They hashed out their differences and finally reached a compromise. 他们详细讨论了他们的分歧，最终达成了妥协。| Synonyms: thrash out, iron out, work out, talk through, hammer out | Antonyms: avoid, ignore, gloss over | verb phrase


hash-out:/hæʃ ˈaʊt/| n. | （充分讨论后达成的）协议，结果 | the agreement or result that is reached after a thorough discussion | The hash-out from the meeting was that we would postpone the project.  会议充分讨论后的结果是我们将会推迟这个项目。| Synonyms: agreement, settlement, resolution, outcome, conclusion | Antonyms: disagreement, discord, impasse | noun
