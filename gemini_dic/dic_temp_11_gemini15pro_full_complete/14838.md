axis:/ˈæksɪs/| n. |轴，轴线；中心线；坐标轴；对称轴|A straight line about which a body or geometric object rotates or may be supposed to rotate. |The earth rotates on its axis. 地球绕其轴自转。| synonym: axle, shaft, pivot | antonym: periphery, edge | noun


axis:/ˈæksɪs/| n. |（政治上的）联盟，轴心|A group of countries, organizations, or people that form an alliance. |The Axis powers in World War II were Germany, Italy, and Japan.  第二次世界大战中的轴心国是德国、意大利和日本。| synonym: alliance, coalition, bloc | antonym: opposition, enemy | noun
