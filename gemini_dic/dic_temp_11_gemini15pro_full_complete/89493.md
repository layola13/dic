honeypot:/ˈhʌnipɒt/ (US) /ˈhʌnipɔt/ (UK)| n.|蜜罐；诱饵；圈套|A honeypot is a computer system or network segment designed to attract and trap attackers, allowing security professionals to study their methods and gather intelligence.|The company set up a honeypot to lure hackers and learn about their tactics.  这家公司设置了一个蜜罐来引诱黑客并了解他们的策略。|Synonyms: trap, lure, bait, decoy|Antonyms: shield, protection, defense|noun


honeypot:/ˈhʌnipɒt/ (US) /ˈhʌnipɔt/ (UK)| v.|引诱；欺骗|To entice or deceive someone with something attractive.|The scammers used a fake lottery to honeypot unsuspecting victims. 骗子用假的彩票来引诱毫无戒心的受害者。|Synonyms: lure, entice, tempt, decoy|Antonyms: repel, deter, discourage|verb
