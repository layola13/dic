gnathopod:/ˈnæθəpɒd/ (US) /ˈnæθəpɑːd/ (UK)| n. |颚足|An appendage modified for seizing and manipulating food, found in some crustaceans, particularly amphipods.|Amphipods use their gnathopods for grasping food. 片脚类动物用它们的颚足抓取食物。|近义词:appendage, claw|反义词: None|noun
