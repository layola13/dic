anophyte: /ˈænəˌfaɪt/
n.
一年生植物
a plant that completes its life cycle, from germination to the production of seed, within one year
The common dandelion is an example of an anophyte. 蒲公英是一年生植物的例子。
近义词: annual
反义词: perennial
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  *ános (ἄνος, “year”) + *phytón (φυτόν, “plant”)
