nephritical:/nɪˈfrɪtɪkəl/
adjective
肾炎的;与肾炎有关的
Relating to or affected by nephritis (inflammation of the kidneys).
Nephritis is an inflammation of the kidneys. The two forms are acute nephritis, which develops suddenly, and chronic nephritis, which develops slowly.
肾炎是肾脏的炎症。有两种形式：急性肾炎，突然发作；慢性肾炎，缓慢发展。

The patient exhibited nephritical syndrome, including proteinuria and edema.
该患者表现出肾病综合征，包括蛋白尿和水肿。

Synonyms: renal, kidney-related
Antonyms: healthy (kidney)
