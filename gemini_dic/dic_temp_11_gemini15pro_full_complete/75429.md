fruited:/ˈfruːtɪd/
adjective
结果实的；产水果的
Bearing or having produced fruit.
The branches were fruited with ripe apples.  树枝上结满了成熟的苹果。
近义词:fruit-bearing, fruit-laden
反义词:barren, unfruitful
词性:adjective

词源: From fruit + -ed.  来自fruit (水果) + -ed (形容词后缀，表示“具有…的”)
