discommendation:/dɪsˌkɑːmɛnˈdeɪʃən/
n.|谴责，不推荐，反对|An expression of disapproval; blaming or criticizing someone or something; the act of not recommending something.|The mayor’s public discommendation of the police chief led to his resignation. 市长公开谴责警察局长，导致他辞职。|Synonyms: condemnation, censure, disapproval, denunciation|Antonyms: recommendation, praise, approval, endorsement|noun

discommend:/ˌdɪskəˈmɛnd/
vt.|谴责，不推荐|To express disapproval of; to advise against.|I discommend this course of action as it is likely to fail. 我不推荐这种做法，因为它很可能会失败。|Synonyms: condemn, censure, deprecate, criticize|Antonyms: recommend, praise, endorse, approve|verb
