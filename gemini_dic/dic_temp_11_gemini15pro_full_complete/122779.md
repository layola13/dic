molar-tooth:/ˈmoʊlər tuːθ/ | adj. n. | 磨牙状的，臼齿状的；臼齿 | Resembling a molar tooth; a molar tooth.  | The fossil had a distinctive molar-tooth shape. 这块化石有一个独特的臼齿形状。|  The child's molar-tooth was loose.  这个孩子的臼齿松动了。| Synonym: molar | Antonym: incisor | adjective, noun

Etymology:  From "molar" (referring to a grinding tooth) + "tooth." "Molar" ultimately derives from Latin "molaris," meaning "grinding," related to "mola," meaning "millstone."
