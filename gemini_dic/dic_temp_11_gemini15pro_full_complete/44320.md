crop out:/krɒp aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 露出，显露；（图像）裁剪掉 | To appear or become visible unexpectedly; to remove unwanted parts of an image. | The bedrock crops out at the surface. 基岩露出地面。| synonyms: emerge, appear, surface, stick out, protrude | antonyms: disappear, vanish, recede, sink, submerge | verb

crop out:/krɒp aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 裁剪掉 | To remove (something) from a photograph or other image by cropping. |  I cropped out the background of the photo. 我裁剪掉了照片的背景. | synonyms: cut out, trim, clip, exclude | antonyms: include, add, insert | verb
