allhallowmas: /ɔːlˈhæləʊməs/ (UK) /ɔːlˈhæloʊmæs/ (US)| n. |万圣节前夕; 诸圣日前夕|All Hallows' Eve, the evening before All Saints' Day (November 1st). It is a Christian festival, but its celebrations have pagan roots.|例句：We carved pumpkins for Allhallowmas. 我们为万圣节前夕雕刻南瓜。|例句：Allhallowmas is a time for remembering the saints. 万圣节前夕是纪念圣徒的日子。|近义词：Halloween, All Hallows' Eve|反义词: None|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ealra hālgena mæsse "mass of all saints," from ealra (genitive plural of eall "all") + hālgena (genitive plural of hālig "holy") + mæsse "mass".  Over time, the name was shortened to All Hallows' Mass, then Allhallowmas.
