nonfollowing:/nɒnˈfɒləʊɪŋ/ | adj. | 非后续的，不跟随的 | Not following; specifically, (of a day) not the one immediately after another specified day.  A nonfollowing day is any day *except* the one immediately after. | The sale is valid on Saturday and any nonfollowing weekday.  该促销活动在周六和任何非后续工作日有效。

The museum is closed on Mondays and the nonfollowing Tuesday. 博物馆在周一和接下来的周二以外的任何一天都开放。 | 近义词: subsequent (often used in negative contexts, e.g., "not subsequent"), succeeding (but implies a sequence),  | 反义词: following, succeeding, next | 词性: adjective
