mare:/mɛər/| n. |母马；母驴；梦魇|A female horse or donkey. A bad or frightening dream.|例句：The mare gave birth to a healthy foal. 这匹母马生了一匹健康的小马驹。

例句：He woke up in a cold sweat after a terrible mare. 他做了一个可怕的噩梦，醒来时一身冷汗。|近义词：nightmare (for the dream sense), female horse (for the animal sense)|反义词: stallion (male horse)|词性:noun
