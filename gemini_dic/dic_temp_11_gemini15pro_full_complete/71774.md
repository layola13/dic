fleasome: /ˈfliːsəm/ | n. | 跳蚤市场；旧货市场；廉价市场 | A market where inexpensive goods, often secondhand, are sold. |  I found a great vintage dress at the fleasome. 我在跳蚤市场找到了一件很棒的复古连衣裙。 |  We're going to the fleasome this weekend to look for bargains.  我们这个周末要去跳蚤市场淘便宜货。 | 近义词: flea market, swap meet, rummage sale | 反义词: department store, boutique | 词性: 名词


词源:  A blend of "flea" and "some," suggesting a place where one might find items infested with fleas, or simply inexpensive and miscellaneous items like those found at a flea market.  由"flea"(跳蚤)和"some"(一些)混合而成，暗示一个可能找到跳蚤出没的物品的地方，或者仅仅是像跳蚤市场上找到的那些廉价的、各种各样的物品。
