decry:/dɪˈkraɪ/ | v. |公开谴责；强烈批评 | to criticize something publicly and strongly | The government's economic policies were widely decried. 政府的经济政策受到了广泛谴责。|  The new law was decried by human rights groups.  这项新法律遭到了人权组织的谴责。 |  condemn, denounce, criticize, censure | praise, commend, laud | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French descrier "cry down, blame, speak ill of," from des- "down" + crier "to cry."
