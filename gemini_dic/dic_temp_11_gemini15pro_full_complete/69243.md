feasta:/ˈfɛstə/ | n. | 盛宴，节日，庆祝 | A feast; a celebration; a festival. |例句：The village held a feasta to celebrate the harvest. 村庄举行盛宴庆祝丰收。

例句：The wedding feasta lasted for three days. 婚礼盛宴持续了三天。| 近义词：celebration, festival, banquet, feast | 反义词：famine, dearth | 词性：名词
