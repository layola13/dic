anoil:/əˈnɔɪl/
|vt.|涂油，上油|To apply oil to something; to lubricate or treat with oil.|He anoils the hinges of the gate every spring. 他每年春天都会给大门铰链上油。|近义词: lubricate, oil, grease|反义词: dry|词性:动词

词源:
From a- (Old English) + oil (Old French oile, from Latin oleum)
