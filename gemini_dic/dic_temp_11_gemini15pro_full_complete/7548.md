annihilable:/əˈnaɪəˌleɪbəl/
adjective
可消灭的；可毁灭的
Capable of being annihilated; destructible.
The dictator believed his enemies were annihilable and sought to eliminate all opposition. 独裁者认为他的敌人是可以被消灭的，并试图消除所有反对派。
The annihilable evidence was quickly destroyed.  这些可以被销毁的证据很快就被销毁了。
近义词: destructible, eradicable, extinguishable
反义词: indestructible, eternal, imperishable
词性: adjective

annihilate:/əˈnaɪəˌleɪt/
verb
消灭；毁灭
To destroy completely; to reduce to nothing.
The bomb annihilated the entire city. 炸弹摧毁了整个城市。
The company was annihilated by the competition.  这家公司被竞争对手彻底击败了。
近义词: destroy, obliterate, decimate
反义词: create, build, establish
词性: verb

annihilation:/əˌnaɪəˈleɪʃən/
noun
消灭，毁灭
The act of annihilating or the state of being annihilated; total destruction.
The annihilation of the city left only ruins. 城市的毁灭只留下了废墟。
The prospect of nuclear annihilation is terrifying. 核毁灭的前景令人恐惧。
近义词: destruction, obliteration, decimation
反义词: creation, construction, establishment
词性: noun


词源:
From Late Latin annihilare "reduce to nothing," from Latin ad "to" (see ad-) + nihil "nothing" (see nil).
