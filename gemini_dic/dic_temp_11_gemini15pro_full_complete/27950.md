cantonalisms:/kænˈtɒnəlɪzəmz/
|n.|地方主义，州主义|The rise of cantonalisms threatened the unity of the nation. 地方主义的兴起威胁着国家的统一。|English explanation:  Features of language, customs, or attitudes specific to a canton (a subdivision of a country, especially Switzerland).  It often implies a focus on local interests and sometimes resistance to central authority.|Example:  Switzerland has a rich history of cantonalisms, reflected in its diverse languages and traditions. 瑞士有着丰富的州主义历史，体现在其多样化的语言和传统。|Synonym: regionalisms, localisms|Antonym: nationalism, centralism|noun
