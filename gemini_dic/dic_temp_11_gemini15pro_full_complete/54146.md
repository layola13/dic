disengages:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪz/
|v.|脱离，解开，退出|to release or detach something; to become free from something|The soldier disengaged the safety catch on his rifle. 士兵解开了步枪上的保险栓。|detach, disconnect, withdraw, release|engage, connect, attach|verb

disengaged:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒd/
|adj.|脱离的，解开的，空闲的，不感兴趣的|not emotionally involved or interested; detached|He seemed disengaged from the conversation, staring out the window. 他似乎对谈话不感兴趣，凝视着窗外。|detached, aloof, withdrawn, uninvolved|engaged, involved, interested|adjective
