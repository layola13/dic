nonparallelism:/ˌnɒnˈpærəlelɪzəm/ | n. | 非平行，不平行性 | Lack of parallelism; absence of parallel structure.  This refers to grammatical or rhetorical constructions where elements that should be similar in structure are not. | The sentence "She enjoys swimming, biking, and to run" demonstrates nonparallelism; it should be "She enjoys swimming, biking, and running." | 她喜欢游泳、骑自行车和跑步。这个句子体现了非平行结构；它应该改为“她喜欢游泳、骑自行车和跑步”。 | 近义词: asymmetry, disparity, unevenness | 反义词: parallelism, balance, symmetry | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + parallel + -ism (suffix forming nouns denoting a doctrine, belief, or practice)
