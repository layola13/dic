jello:/ˈdʒɛloʊ/| n.|果冻，胶状物|A sweet, brightly colored food made from gelatin.  It is usually eaten as a dessert.|例句：The children love to eat jello. 孩子们喜欢吃果冻。|近义词：jelly, gelatin dessert|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "solid food")|noun
