cambrian:/ˈkæmbriən/| adj. n.|寒武纪的；寒武纪;|Of, relating to, or denoting the first period in the Paleozoic era, between the PreCambrian and Ordovician periods, lasting from about 542 to 488 million years ago.|The Cambrian explosion saw a rapid diversification of life forms. 寒武纪大爆发见证了生命形式的快速多样化。|Near synonyms: Paleozoic, early Paleozoic|Antonym: PreCambrian|adjective

cambrian:/ˈkæmbriən/| n.|寒武纪|The first period in the Paleozoic era, between the PreCambrian and Ordovician periods, lasting from about 542 to 488 million years ago.|Trilobites were abundant during the Cambrian period.  三叶虫在寒武纪时期非常丰富。|Near synonyms: Paleozoic era, early Paleozoic|Antonym: PreCambrian|noun
