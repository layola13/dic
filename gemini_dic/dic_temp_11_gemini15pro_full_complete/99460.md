interfacing:/ˈɪntərˌfeɪsɪŋ/ | n. |接口; 界面; 交互;  连接; (尤指) 计算机接口 |The process of connecting two or more systems or devices so that they can exchange information.  It also refers to the point of interaction or communication between two systems, especially a computer and its user or peripherals. |The software provides a user-friendly interfacing with the database.  该软件提供了用户友好的数据库接口。|Synonyms: interface, connection, link, interaction |Antonyms: disconnection, separation| noun

interfacing:/ˈɪntərˌfeɪsɪŋ/ | adj. |交互的; 连接的 |Serving as a connection between systems or devices.  |Interfacing modules allow different types of equipment to be connected. 交互模块允许不同类型的设备连接。|Synonyms: connecting, linking, interactive |Antonyms: disconnecting, separating| adjective
