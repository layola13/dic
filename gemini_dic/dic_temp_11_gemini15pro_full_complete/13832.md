auditoriums:/ˌɔːdɪˈtɔːriəmz/
n.
礼堂；观众席
A large room or building used for meetings, lectures, concerts, etc., and equipped with seating for an audience.
The university has two large auditoriums. 这所大学有两个大礼堂。

The concert will be held in the school auditorium. 这场音乐会将在学校礼堂举行。

近义词: halls, theaters, venues
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其功能相反的场所，例如：closet)
词性: 名词
