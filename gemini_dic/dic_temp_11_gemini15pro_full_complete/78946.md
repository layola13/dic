girdled: /ˈɡɜːrdld/ (US) /ˈɡɜːdld/ (UK)| adj. v. (past participle of girdle)|被环绕的，束缚的|Encircled or bound with or as if with a belt or girdle.  The island was girdled by a coral reef.  |例句：The knight girdled his sword. 骑士佩上他的剑。

例句：The town was girdled by walls.  这个城镇被城墙环绕。|近义词：encircled, surrounded, encompassed, ringed|反义词：unbound, free, open|词性:形容词, 动词(过去分词)
Etymology: From Old English *gyrdan, from Proto-Germanic *gurdjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *gherdh-. Related to "girth" and "yard".
