inbreaking:/ˈɪnbreɪkɪŋ/
adjective
入侵的，闯入的
An act of entering a building illegally, typically to commit a crime.
The inbreaking tide flooded the coastal village. 入侵的潮水淹没了沿海村庄。
近义词：intruding, invasive, encroaching
反义词：retreating, withdrawing
词性: adjective

词源:  From "in-" (into) + "breaking" (the act of breaking in).  Relatively modern formation.
