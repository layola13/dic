into:/ˈɪntuː/ /ˈɪntə/
preposition
到…里；进入…之内；成为…状况；深入…之中
expressing movement or action with the result that someone or something becomes enclosed or surrounded by something else.
She walked into the room. 她走进了房间。
近义词:to, toward, inside
反义词:out of
词性:介词
