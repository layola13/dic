gentrifiers:/ˈdʒɛntrɪfaɪərz/
|n.|中产阶级化推动者；高档化推动者|People who move into a working-class neighbourhood, causing property values to rise and often displacing the original residents.  那些搬进工人阶级社区，导致房产价值上涨，并经常迫使原住民搬迁的人。|The gentrifiers renovated the old houses, but many long-time residents were priced out of the neighborhood.  高档化推动者翻新了老房子，但许多长期居民因房价上涨而被迫搬离该社区。|近义词:  urban pioneers,  developers,  renovators|反义词:  displaced residents,  original inhabitants|词性: Noun (Plural)

gentrifier:/ˈdʒɛntrɪfaɪər/
|n.|中产阶级化推动者；高档化推动者|A person who moves into a working-class neighbourhood, causing property values to rise and often displacing the original residents.  搬进工人阶级社区，导致房产价值上涨，并经常迫使原住民搬迁的人。|The arrival of the gentrifier led to an increase in coffee shops and art galleries.  高档化推动者的到来导致咖啡店和美术馆的增加。|近义词:  urban pioneer,  developer,  renovator|反义词:  displaced resident,  original inhabitant|词性: Noun (Singular)

词源:  From the noun "gentry" (上流社会；绅士阶层) + "-fier" (表示“使…化”的后缀).
