homatropine:/hoʊˈmætrəˌpin/ | n. |后马托品|An anticholinergic drug used to dilate the pupil of the eye.|Homatropine is used to dilate the pupil for eye examinations. 后马托品用于散瞳以进行眼科检查。|The ophthalmologist used homatropine drops to dilate the patient's pupils. 眼科医生使用后马托品滴眼液来扩张病人的瞳孔。|Synonyms: atropine, scopolamine | Antonyms: pilocarpine, physostigmine | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From homo- + atropine, reflecting its chemical similarity to atropine.  Atropine derives from Atropa belladonna, the deadly nightshade plant,  "Atropa" coming from Atropos, one of the three Fates in Greek mythology, whose task was to cut the thread of life.
