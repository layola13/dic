cinematographers:/ˌsɪnəməˈtɒɡrəfərz/ (US) /ˌsɪnəməˈtɒɡrəfəz/ (UK)| n. |电影摄影师|A person who is an expert in the art of cinematography, responsible for the camera and lighting choices that shape the visual style of a film.|例句：The cinematographers of that movie did a fantastic job creating a sense of unease. 那部电影的摄影师在营造不安感方面做得非常出色。

cinematographers:| n. |电影摄影师|Someone who photographs movies; director of photography.|例句：Roger Deakins is one of the most acclaimed cinematographers of our time. 罗杰·迪金斯是我们这个时代最受赞誉的电影摄影师之一。|近义词：director of photography, cameraman, DP|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
