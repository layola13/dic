inorganic:/ˌɪnɔːrˈɡænɪk/ | adj. |无机的，非有机的| Not derived from living matter; composed of matter other than plant or animal. |  例句：Rocks are inorganic materials. 岩石是无机物。

例句：Inorganic chemistry is the study of the properties and reactions of inorganic compounds. 无机化学是对无机化合物的性质和反应的研究。| 近义词：non-organic, mineral | 反义词：organic | 词性：形容词
