lyencephalous:/laɪɛnˈsɛfələs/| adj.|无脑畸形的|Characterized by the absence of a brain, especially the cerebrum and cerebellum.  This is a serious birth defect that is usually fatal. |Example: The lyencephalous fetus was not viable.  无脑畸形的胎儿无法存活。

Example: Lyencephaly is a severe neural tube defect. 无脑畸形是一种严重的神经管缺陷。|近义词：anencephalic|反义词: N/A (The opposite would imply having a normal brain, which isn't a specific medical term.)|词性: adjective
