myrrh:/mɜːr/ (US) /mɜː(r)/ (UK)| n.| 没药（一种芳香的树脂）|A fragrant gum resin obtained from certain trees and used in perfumes, incense, and medicines. |The Magi brought gifts of gold, frankincense, and myrrh.  三位智者带来了黄金、乳香和没药的礼物。

The ancient Egyptians used myrrh in embalming. 古埃及人用没药来防腐。|近义词:frankincense, balm|反义词: None|词性:noun
