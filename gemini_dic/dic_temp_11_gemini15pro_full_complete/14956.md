b-lister:/ˈbiː lɪstər/ | n. | 二线明星；二流演员 | A person who is famous or successful but less so than the most famous or successful people.  A celebrity of the second tier. |  She's a b-lister in Hollywood, but she's still quite well-known.  她在好莱坞是二线明星，但她仍然很有名。|  minor celebrity, second-tier star | A-lister, superstar | Noun
