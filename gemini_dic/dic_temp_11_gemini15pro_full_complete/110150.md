lied: /laɪd/
verb
撒谎；(lie 的过去式和过去分词)
told an untruth; said something that was not true
He lied about his age. 他谎报了年龄。
近义词: fibbed, prevaricated, deceived
反义词: told the truth
词性: verb (动词)

lied: /laɪd/
verb
躺下；平卧；处于…状态；位于；展现；(lie 的过去式和过去分词)
(of a person or animal) be in or assume a horizontal or resting position on a supporting surface
The dog lied down in the shade. 狗躺在阴凉处。
近义词: reclined, rested, lay
反义词: stood, sat
词性: verb (动词)
