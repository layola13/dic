myriological:/ˌmɪriəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
无数的，万种的; 关于无数事物的
Relating to myriology, the study of a vast number of things or a vast range of subjects.
Myriological research encompasses a vast amount of data.  无数的研究所包含了大量的数据。
The library's myriological collection covers a vast range of topics.  图书馆浩瀚的馆藏涵盖了无数的主题。
近义词: innumerable, countless, vast, extensive, comprehensive
反义词: limited, restricted, few, scarce
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *myrios* (μύριος) "ten thousand, countless" + *-logos* (-λογος) "study of".
