junco:/ˈdʒʌŋkoʊ/ (US), /ˈdʒʌŋkəʊ/ (UK)| n.|暗眼灯草鹀|A small North American finch (bird) with a short conical bill, typically dark gray or brown above and white below.  It is common in open woodlands and gardens.|I saw a junco hopping around in my garden. 我看到一只暗眼灯草鹀在我的花园里跳来跳去。|近义词：sparrow, finch|反义词: (No direct opposite for a specific bird species)|noun
