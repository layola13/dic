happens:/ˈhæpənz/| v.|发生，碰巧，偶然发生|Something unexpected happened. 发生了意想不到的事情。|近义词：occurs, takes place, transpires|反义词:ceases, stops|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *happenen*, from Old Norse *happna* (“to happen, befall”), from *happ* (“chance, good luck”).  The noun *happ* is related to the adjective *happy*.
