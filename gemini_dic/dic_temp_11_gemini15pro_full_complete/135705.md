nonprevalent:/nɒnˈprɛvələnt/| adj.|不普遍的，不流行的，很少见的|Not prevalent; uncommon; infrequent. |This disease is nonprevalent in developed countries. 这种疾病在发达国家并不普遍。|The custom of arranged marriages is nonprevalent in Western societies. 包办婚姻的习俗在西方社会不普遍。|近义词：uncommon, rare, infrequent, unusual, scarce|反义词：prevalent, common, widespread, frequent, usual|词性:形容词
词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "prevalent" (from Latin "praevalēre" meaning "to be very strong or powerful," from "prae-" meaning "before, pre-" + "valēre" meaning "to be strong").
