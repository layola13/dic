longicornia:/ˌlɒŋɪˈkɔːniə/ | n. |天牛科|A family of beetles characterized by their exceptionally long antennae, often longer than their bodies.  They are also known as long-horned beetles.|  Example: The entomologist specialized in the study of longicornia. 这位昆虫学家专门研究天牛科。 | Example:  Many species of longicornia are considered pests because their larvae bore into wood. 许多种类的长角甲虫被认为是害虫，因为它们的幼虫会钻进木头。|  Synonym: Cerambycidae | Antonym: N/A | Noun
