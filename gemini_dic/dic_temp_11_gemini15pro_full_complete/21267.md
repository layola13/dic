bloodhound:/ˈblʌdhaʊnd/| n.|猎犬（一种嗅觉灵敏的犬种）|A large hound of a breed with a wrinkled face and drooping ears, known for its keen sense of smell and used in tracking.|The police used bloodhounds to track the escaped convict. 警方用猎犬追踪逃犯。|近义词：hound, tracker dog|反义词: N/A|名词

词源:  From Middle English *blodhounde, from Old English blōdhund (“bloodhound, sleuth-hound, a dog that hunts by scent and can follow a trail of blood”), equivalent to blōd (“blood”) + hund (“hound”).  来自中古英语 *blodhounde，源自古英语 blōdhund（“猎犬，追踪犬，一种通过气味狩猎并能追踪血迹的狗”），相当于 blōd（“血”）+ hund（“猎犬”）。
