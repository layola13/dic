clapsticks:/ˈklæpstɪks/ | n. | 击板，拍板 |A pair of sticks, usually wooden, that are clapped together rhythmically to create a percussive sound. They are often used in music, dance, and storytelling. | The dancer used clapsticks to accentuate the rhythm of the music. 舞蹈家用拍板强调音乐的节奏。 |  The children were entertained by the storyteller's use of clapsticks. 孩子们被讲故事的人使用拍板的方式逗乐了。 | synonyms: rhythm sticks, claves, music sticks | antonyms: N/A | noun
