antenna:/ænˈtɛnə/ (US), /ænˈtiːnə/ (UK)| n. |天线；触角|A device for sending or receiving radio waves; a feeler on the head of an insect, crab, etc.|The car radio antenna was broken. 汽车的无线电天线坏了。|The butterfly fluttered its antennae. 蝴蝶拍打着它的触角。|synonyms: aerial, feeler|antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "receiver" in a specific context)|noun
