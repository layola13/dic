noblesse:/noʊˈblɛs/ | n. |贵族,  高贵,  贵族阶级| The quality of being noble in character, mind, or rank.  High social standing. |  The noblesse of his character was evident in his selfless acts. 他的高贵品质体现在他无私的行为中。 |synonyms: aristocracy, nobility, gentry | antonyms: commonalty, peasantry | noun

noblesse oblige:/noʊˈblɛs əˈbliːʒ/ | n. | 貴族義務| The inferred responsibility of privileged people to act with generosity and nobility toward those less privileged. |  Noblesse oblige demands that he use his wealth to help others.  贵族义务要求他用自己的财富去帮助他人。 | synonyms: social responsibility, moral obligation | antonyms: selfishness, greed | noun
