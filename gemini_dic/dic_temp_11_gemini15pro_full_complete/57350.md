dowse: /daʊz/ | v. | 探测；用占卜杖探测水源；寻找地下水 | to search for underground water or minerals using a divining rod or similar instrument |  He dowsed for water on the property. 他在这片地上探测水源。| synonyms: divine, water-witch | antonyms:  (no direct antonyms, could consider "excavate" as a contrasting method of finding water) | verb

dowse: /daʊz/ | n. | 探测；探测水源 | the act of searching for underground water or minerals using a divining rod or similar instrument | The dowse revealed a potential well site. 探测显示了一个可能的井址。| synonyms: divining, water-witching | antonyms: (no direct antonyms) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dowsen*, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old High German tūsan "to make a noise, to rush, to gush," referring to the sound of underground water.
