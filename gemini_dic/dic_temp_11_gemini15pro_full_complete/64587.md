eruciform:/ˌɛrʊˈsɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 蚕形的，毛虫状的 | Resembling a caterpillar; having the shape of a larva. |  The eruciform larva of the butterfly eventually transforms into a winged adult. 蝴蝶的毛虫状幼虫最终会变成带翅膀的成虫。| Synonyms: caterpillar-like, larval, vermiform | Antonyms:  imago, adult (referring to insect life stages) | adjective
