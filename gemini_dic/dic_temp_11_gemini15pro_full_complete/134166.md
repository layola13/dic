nonimputativeness:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpjuːtətɪvnəs/
|n.| 非归罪性；非可归因性 |The nonimputativeness of the evidence meant the defendant could not be held responsible.  证据的非归罪性意味着被告不用承担责任。|  The principle of nonimputativeness protects individuals from being blamed for actions they did not commit. 非归罪性原则保护个人免于因他们没有犯下的行为而受到指责。 |  responsibility, accountability |  imputability, culpability | noun
