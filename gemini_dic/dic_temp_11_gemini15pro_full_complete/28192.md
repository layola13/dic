capriccio:/kəˈpriːtʃoʊ/ (US), /kəˈprɪtʃioʊ/ (UK)| n.|奇想，随想曲，反复无常|A sudden, impulsive, and seemingly unmotivated notion or action; a whimsical idea or unpredictable change of mind.  A piece of music, usually lively and free in form.|
He painted a landscape on a whim, a sudden capriccio that surprised even himself. 他一时兴起画了一幅风景画，这突如其来的奇想甚至让他自己也感到惊讶。

The orchestra played a delightful capriccio by Vivaldi.  管弦乐队演奏了维瓦尔第一首令人愉快的随想曲。

|近义词：whim, fancy, impulse, notion|反义词：plan, calculation, deliberation|词性: noun
