circuit-breaker:/ˈsɜːrkɪt breɪkər/ | n. | 断路器; 熔断机制 | A safety device that interrupts the flow of electricity in a circuit when an overload occurs. | The circuit breaker tripped and cut off the power to the kitchen. 断路器跳闸了，切断了厨房的电源。 | synonyms: fuse, safety switch | antonyms: conductor | noun

circuit-breaker:/ˈsɜːrkɪt breɪkər/ | n. | （股票交易中的）熔断机制 | A regulatory measure that temporarily halts trading on a stock exchange when prices fall sharply. | The circuit breaker was triggered after the stock market plunged 10%. 股市暴跌10%后，熔断机制被触发。| synonyms: trading halt, market curb | antonyms: continuous trading | noun
