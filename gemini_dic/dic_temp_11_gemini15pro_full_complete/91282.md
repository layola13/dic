hydrography:/haɪˈdrɒɡrəfi/ (US) /haɪˈdrɑːɡrəfi/ (UK)| n.|水文地理学；水道测量学|The science that deals with the measurement and description of the physical features of bodies of water, especially navigable waters, and the compilation of such information for the production of nautical charts.|例句：The hydrography of the area is complex, with numerous shoals and reefs. 该地区的水文地理情况复杂，有许多浅滩和礁石。

例句：Accurate hydrography is essential for safe navigation. 准确的水文测量对于安全航行至关重要。|近义词：hydrology, bathymetry|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
