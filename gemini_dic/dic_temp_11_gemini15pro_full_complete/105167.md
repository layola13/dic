keuper:/ˈkɔɪpər/| n. |考珀（三叠纪时期欧洲的一个地质层）|A European lithostratigraphic unit (a sequence of rock strata) deposited during the latter part of the Triassic period. It is named after a region in Southern Germany.  The Keuper is characterized by evaporites, mudstones, and sandstones, often brightly colored.|The Keuper marl is a distinctive reddish-brown color. 考珀泥灰岩是一种独特的红棕色。

The fossils found in the Keuper strata provide valuable insights into the Triassic ecosystem.  在考珀地层中发现的化石为了解三叠纪生态系统提供了宝贵的线索。|近义词:Triassic strata (更广义)|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑其他地质时期，例如Jurassic)|词性:名词
