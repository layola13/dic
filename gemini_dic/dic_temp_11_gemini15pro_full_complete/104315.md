justnesses:/ˈdʒʌstnɪsɪz/
noun
正义, 正确性，公正性
The quality of being just; fairness, righteousness, equitableness.
His justnesses in dealing with the dispute was praised by all. 他处理争端的公正性受到了所有人的赞扬。
Synonyms: righteousness, fairness, equitableness, justice, impartiality
Antonyms: injustice, unfairness, bias, partiality
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *justnes*, from Old French *justesse*  (Modern French *justesse*), from Late Latin *justitia*, from Latin *justus* ("just, righteous").
