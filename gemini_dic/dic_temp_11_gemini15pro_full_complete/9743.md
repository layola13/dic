antivivisectionists:/ˌæntiˌvɪvɪˈsɛkʃənɪsts/ | n. |反活体解剖者，反对活体解剖的人|People who oppose vivisection, the practice of performing operations on live animals for scientific research.|例句：Antivivisectionists protested outside the laboratory. 反活体解剖者在实验室外抗议。|例句：The antivivisectionists argued that the research was cruel and unnecessary. 反活体解剖者认为这项研究既残忍又不必要。|近义词：animal rights activists, animal welfare advocates|反义词：pro-vivisectionists (this term is less common and often considered pejorative), supporters of animal research|词性: 名词
