overobese:/ˌōvərōˈbiːs/| adj.|极度肥胖的，过度肥胖的|Excessively obese; extremely overweight.  Characterized by a body mass index (BMI) of 40 or higher.|He was diagnosed as overobese and advised to seek medical help. 他被诊断为极度肥胖，并被建议寻求医疗帮助。|近义词：morbidly obese, extremely obese, severely obese|反义词:underweight, thin, slim|词性:形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "obese"
"over-"  from Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi (meaning "over, above, beyond")
"obese" from Latin obesus (meaning "fat, stout"), past participle of obedere (meaning "to devour, eat away"), from ob- (meaning "towards, against") + edere (meaning "to eat")
