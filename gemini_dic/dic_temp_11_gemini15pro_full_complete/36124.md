coes:/koʊz/ | n. | (通常复数)co-enzyme的缩写 | Co-enzymes are organic molecules that are required by certain enzymes to carry out catalysis. |  These coes are essential for proper enzyme function. 这些辅酶对酶的正常功能至关重要。 | 近义词: coenzymes | 反义词: 无 | 词性: 名词缩写

/koʊz/ | v. | (第三人称单数)co-enzyme的缩写，通常不用作动词 | Co-enzymes are organic molecules that are required by certain enzymes to carry out catalysis. |  The enzyme coes with the substrate.  这个酶与底物共同作用。(这种用法不常见且不规范，建议使用co-operates 或 interacts) | 近义词:  (如果指共同作用) co-operates, interacts | 反义词: 无 | 词性: 动词缩写 (不常用)
