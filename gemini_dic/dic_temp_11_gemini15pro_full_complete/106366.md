komatiite:/ˈkoʊmətiaɪt/| n. |科马提岩|An ultramafic volcanic rock, rich in magnesium and low in silica, thought to have formed from very high-temperature lavas early in Earth's history.|Komatiites are found in ancient Archaean cratons. 科马提岩发现于古老的太古代克拉通中。|Komatiite flows are evidence of a much hotter mantle in the early Earth. 科马提岩流是早期地球地幔温度更高的证据。|近义词:peridotite, picrite |反义词: (No direct antonym as it's a specific rock type)
词性: noun
