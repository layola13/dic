fiscals:/ˈfɪskəlz/ | adj. n. |财政的；财政年度的；财政官员（fiscal的名词复数）| relating to government revenue, especially taxes | The government's fiscal year begins in April. 政府的财政年度从四月开始。| synonyms: financial, budgetary, monetary | antonyms: non-fiscal | adjective

fiscals:/ˈfɪskəlz/ | n. |财政官员（fiscal的名词复数）| officials who deal with government finances | The fiscals are meeting to discuss the budget. 财政官员们正在开会讨论预算。| synonyms: treasurers, finance officers | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fiscalis, from Latin fiscus "state treasury, money basket," of uncertain origin.
