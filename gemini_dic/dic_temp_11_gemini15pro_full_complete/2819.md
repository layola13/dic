adventitious:/ˌædvenˈtɪʃəs/| adj.|偶然的，外来的，非固有的|appearing or occurring accidentally or unexpectedly, not inherent or essential|例句：The plant's adventitious roots grew from the stem. 这种植物的不定根从茎上长出来。|例句：The adventitious encounter led to a lifelong friendship. 这次偶然的相遇促成了一段终生的友谊。|近义词：accidental, incidental, fortuitous, extrinsic|反义词：inherent, intrinsic, essential|词性:形容词

adventitious:/ˌædvenˈtɪʃəs/| adj.|（医学）后天的，获得性的|(medical) acquired, not congenital|例句：Adventitious deafness can be caused by exposure to loud noises. 后天性耳聋可能是由于暴露于巨大的噪音中引起的。|例句：The patient developed an adventitious infection after surgery. 病人在手术后出现了获得性感染。|近义词：acquired, secondary|反义词：congenital, primary|词性:形容词


词源:  From Latin *adventitius, from adventus (“arrival”), from advenīre (“to arrive”), from ad- (“to”) + venīre (“to come”).
