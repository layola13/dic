creaser's:/ˈkriːsər z/
noun
折痕制造者; 压褶工;  (尤指) 熨衣工
A person or device that creates creases, especially in fabric.
The creaser's iron left a sharp line down the front of the trousers.  熨衣工的熨斗在裤子前面留下了一条清晰的线条。
Synonyms: ironer, presser
Antonyms: smoother
