jointless:/ˈdʒɔɪntləs/
adjective
无缝的，无连接的，连续的
Without joints; seamless or continuous.
The artist created a jointless sculpture from a single piece of clay. 艺术家用一块黏土创作了一个无缝的雕塑。
The fabric is jointless, making it ideal for swimwear. 这种布料是无缝的，非常适合做泳衣。
seamless, continuous, unbroken
jointed, segmented, connected
