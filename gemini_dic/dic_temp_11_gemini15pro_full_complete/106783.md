labellum:/ləˈbɛləm/ /ləˈbeɪləm/ | n. |唇瓣；下唇|The modified petal of an orchid flower, often differing markedly in size, shape, and color from the other petals and sepals.  It serves as a landing platform for pollinating insects.|The bee landed on the orchid's labellum. 蜜蜂落在了兰花的唇瓣上。|Synonyms: lip, petal |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider other flower parts like sepal or petal if contrasting) | noun

labellum:/ləˈbɛləm/ /ləˈbeɪləm/ | n. |小唇|A small lip or lip-like part.|The labellum of the insect was crucial for feeding.  这种昆虫的小唇对进食至关重要。|Synonyms: ligule, labium |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun
