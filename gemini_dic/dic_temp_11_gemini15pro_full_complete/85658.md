haustorium:/hɔːˈstɔːriəm/
n.
吸器
A specialized organ of a parasitic plant or fungus that penetrates the host's tissues and absorbs nutrients.
The haustorium of the dodder plant penetrates the stem of its host.  菟丝子的吸器穿透了宿主的茎。
haustoria (plural)
n.
吸器（复数）
The haustoria of the fungus draw nutrients from the tree's roots. 真菌的吸器从树的根部吸取营养。

近义词:absorptive organ, sucker
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider  "root" in the context of a non-parasitic plant)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin haustor "drawer, drinker", from haurire "to draw, drink".
