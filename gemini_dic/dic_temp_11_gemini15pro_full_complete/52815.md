diluent:/ˈdɪljʊənt/ | n. | 稀释剂，冲淡剂 | A substance used to dilute something, especially a paint, varnish, or other liquid. | The artist used a special diluent to thin the oil paint.  艺术家用一种特殊的稀释剂来稀释油画颜料。 | synonyms: thinner, solvent, weakening agent | antonyms:  concentrate, thickener | noun
