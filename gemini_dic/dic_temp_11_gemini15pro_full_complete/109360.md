leno:/ˈliːnoʊ/
n.|莱诺织物，绞经纱组织|A kind of open weave fabric, often used for curtains, in which pairs of warp threads are twisted around each other to hold the filling threads in place.|The curtains were made of a delicate leno weave.|近义词:gauze, mesh|反义词:None|词性:名词
