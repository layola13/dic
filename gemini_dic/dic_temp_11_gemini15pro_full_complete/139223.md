objurgation:/ˌɒbdʒɜːˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 斥责，责骂 | A harsh rebuke or reprimand. |  例句：The teacher's objurgation silenced the unruly students. 老师的斥责让不守规矩的学生安静了下来。

例句：He faced severe objurgation for his careless mistake. 他因为粗心的错误而面临严厉的斥责。

近义词：rebuke, reprimand, reproach, scolding, castigation | 反义词：praise, commendation, compliment | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin objurgare ("to chide, rebuke, blame"), from ob- ("against") + jurgare ("to scold, quarrel").
