loafers: /ˈloʊfərz/ (US) /ˈləʊfəz/ (UK)| n.|乐福鞋，懒汉鞋|A type of slip-on shoe, often made of leather or suede, with a low heel and no laces or buckles.|He wore a pair of brown loafers to the party. 他穿着一双棕色乐福鞋去参加聚会。|Synonyms: slip-ons, flats|Antonyms: boots, lace-up shoes|Noun

loafers: /ˈloʊfərz/ (US) /ˈləʊfəz/ (UK)| n.|游手好闲的人|People who avoid work or responsibility.|He called them a bunch of loafers. 他称他们是一群游手好闲的人。|Synonyms: idlers, slackers|Antonyms: workers, hard workers|Noun
