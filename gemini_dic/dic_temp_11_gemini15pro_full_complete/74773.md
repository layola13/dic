freeze:/friːz/| v. n.| 结冰，冻结，冷冻，僵住 | To change from a liquid to a solid state by loss of heat; to become rigid or immobile, especially from fear or shock. | The lake freezes over in winter. 冬天湖面会结冰。| synonyms: chill, congeal, solidify, halt | antonyms: thaw, melt, liquefy | verb
freeze:/friːz/| n.| 冻结，冰冻期 | A period of very cold weather. |  There's a freeze warning tonight. 今晚有霜冻警报。| synonyms: frost, cold snap | antonyms: thaw | noun
