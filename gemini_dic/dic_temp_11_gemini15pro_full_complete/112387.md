losses:/ˈlɒsɪz/ (UK) /ˈlɔːsɪz/ (US)| n.|损失，亏损|The company suffered heavy losses last year.  公司去年遭受了严重的损失。|
例句：The company reported losses of $10 million.  该公司报告损失了1千万美元。

例句：Her death was a great loss to the family. 她的去世对家庭来说是一个巨大的损失。


近义词：defeats, setbacks, damages, detriments|反义词:gains, profits, wins|词性:名词


词源：From Middle English *losses, plural of los, from Old French los, from perdre “to lose,” from Latin perdō (per- “through, away” + dō “give”).
