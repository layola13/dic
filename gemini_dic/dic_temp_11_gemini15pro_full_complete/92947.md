ideograms:/ˈaɪdɪəɡræmz/ |n.| 表意文字;表意符号 |A written symbol that represents an idea or object directly rather than a particular word or speech sound, as in Chinese script.|Chinese characters are ideograms. 汉字是表意文字。| The ancient Egyptians used ideograms in their writing system. 古埃及人在他们的书写系统中使用表意文字。| synonyms:  logograms, symbols, glyphs |antonyms: alphabets, phonetic scripts | noun
