miscreancy: /ˈmɪskriːənsi/
n.
恶行；不端行为
An instance of miscreant behavior; a misdeed or wicked act.
His miscreancies finally landed him in jail. 他的恶行最终使他入狱。

罪行；过失
A villainous or criminal act.
The king punished the knight for his miscreancy. 国王因骑士的罪行而惩罚了他。


近义词: wrongdoing, misconduct, villainy, wickedness, iniquity, transgression
