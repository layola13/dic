nonpsychoanalytical:/ˌnɒnˌsaɪkoʊˌænəˈlɪtɪkəl/
adjective
非精神分析的
Not pertaining to or based on psychoanalysis; rejecting or opposing the methods or principles of psychoanalysis.
His approach to therapy is nonpsychoanalytical, focusing on behavioral modification rather than exploring childhood experiences.  他采用的治疗方法是非精神分析式的，侧重于行为矫正而不是探索童年经历。
The study took a nonpsychoanalytical approach, focusing on observable behaviors. 这项研究采用了一种非精神分析的方法，侧重于可观察的行为。
Synonyms: non-Freudian, behavioral, cognitive
Antonyms: psychoanalytical, Freudian
词性: adjective
