coenogamy:/siːˈnɒɡəmi/ | n. | 共同交配，群婚 | The fertilization of a single egg by multiple sperm, or the fusion of multiple gametes.  The state or practice of communal marriage. |  Coenogamy is observed in some primitive organisms.  一些原始生物中可以观察到群婚现象。|  Synonyms: communal marriage, group marriage | Antonyms: monogamy | Noun
