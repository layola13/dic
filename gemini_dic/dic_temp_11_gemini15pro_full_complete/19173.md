bewildered:/bɪˈwɪldərd/| adj.|困惑的，不知所措的|Confused and uncertain; perplexed.|例句：She looked bewildered when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息时看起来很困惑。|近义词：confused, perplexed, puzzled, disoriented|反义词：clear-headed, oriented, composed|词性:形容词
词源:From be- + wilder (“to lead astray, confuse”), from wild + -er.
