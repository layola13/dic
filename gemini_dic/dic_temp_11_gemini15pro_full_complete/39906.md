constitutively:/kənˈstɪtjuːtɪvli/
| adv. |组成地；本质地；结构上地|In a constitutive manner; in a way that establishes something or is essential to it. |This protein is constitutively expressed in heart tissue. 这种蛋白质在心脏组织中组成性表达。|
constitutively:/kənˈstɪtjuːtɪvli/
| adv. |天生；固有地|By inherent nature; intrinsically. |Some people are constitutively more optimistic than others. 有些人天生就比其他人更乐观。|
constitutively:/kənˈstɪtjuːtɪvli/
| adv. |构成地| In a way that forms a part or element of something. | The board is constitutively made up of elected members.  董事会由民选成员构成。|
constitutively:/kənˈstɪtjuːtɪvli/
| adv. | | |The enzyme is constitutively active, meaning it's always working. 这种酶具有组成型活性，意味着它一直在工作。|
近义词: inherently, intrinsically, naturally, essentially
反义词: conditionally, inducibly
词性: adverb

词源: From constitute + -ive + -ly.  "Constitute" comes from Latin *constitutus*, the past participle of *constituere* "to set up, establish," from *con-* "together" + *statuere* "to set, place."
