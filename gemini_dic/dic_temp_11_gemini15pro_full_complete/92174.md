hyperspatiality:/ˌhaɪpərˌspeɪʃiˈælɪti/ | n. |超空间性，高维空间性 | The state or quality of being hyperspatial; relating to or involving hyperspace, a space of more than three dimensions. |  The concept of hyperspatiality is often explored in science fiction, allowing for faster-than-light travel.  超空间性的概念经常在科幻小说中被探讨，允许比光速更快的旅行。 | synonyms: multidimensionality, higher-dimensionality | antonyms: two-dimensionality, three-dimensionality | noun
