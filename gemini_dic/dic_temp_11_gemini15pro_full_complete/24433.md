bristletail:/ˈbrɪs(ə)lˌteɪl/
n.
衣鱼，笔尾虫
A small, wingless insect with three long bristles at the end of its abdomen.  It is often found in houses, feeding on starchy materials.
Bristletails are ancient insects that have been around for millions of years. 衣鱼是古老的昆虫，已经存在了数百万年。
I found a bristletail in my bathroom. 我在浴室里发现了一只衣鱼。
近义词:silverfish (though not all bristletails are silverfish), firebrat (for the species Thermobia domestica)
反义词: None (as it is a specific type of insect)
词性:Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bristle + tail.  "Bristle" refers to the stiff hairs, and "tail" refers to their posterior appendages.
