flaunty:/ˈflɔːnti/ | adj. |炫耀的，卖弄的 |Ostentatiously or conspicuously stylish; showy and intended to attract notice.  Characterized by showing off, especially in a confident and provocative way. | She wore a flaunty dress to the party, hoping to catch everyone's attention. 她穿着一件炫耀的连衣裙去参加派对，希望能吸引所有人的注意。 |Synonyms: ostentatious, showy, flashy, flamboyant, extravagant, pretentious | Antonyms: modest, understated, unassuming, discreet, humble, simple | adjective

flaunty:/ˈflɔːnti/ | adj. |招摇的 | Displaying something (especially oneself) in a showy or boastful way. | He flauntyly displayed his expensive new watch. 他招摇地展示他昂贵的新手表。 |Synonyms:  ostentatious, showy, flashy, flamboyant, extravagant, pretentious | Antonyms: modest, understated, unassuming, discreet, humble, simple | adjective
