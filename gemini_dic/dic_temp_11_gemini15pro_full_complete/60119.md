effacing:/ɪˈfeɪsɪŋ/
adjective
抹去的，谦逊的，不引人注目的
Making oneself inconspicuous; modest.
He has an effacing manner. 他举止谦逊。
近义词:modest, humble, self-effacing, unassuming, retiring
反义词:assertive, bold, confident, immodest, arrogant
词性:adjective

efface:/ɪˈfeɪs/
verb
擦掉，抹去
Erase (a mark) from a surface.
The inscription had been effaced by weathering.  铭文因风化而被抹去了。
近义词:erase, obliterate, expunge, delete, remove
反义词:preserve, enhance, emphasize, highlight
词性:verb

词源:
From Old French *effacer, from e(x)- ‘out’ + face ‘face.’
