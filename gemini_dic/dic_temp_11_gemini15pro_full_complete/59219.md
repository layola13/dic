dystopian:/dɪˈstəʊpiən/ (UK) /dɪˈstoʊpiən/ (US)
adjective
反乌托邦的；反面乌托邦的
Relating to or denoting an imagined place or state in which everything is unpleasant or bad, typically a totalitarian or environmentally degraded one.
The novel presents a dystopian vision of the future. 这部小说展现了对未来反乌托邦的构想。

近义词: apocalyptic, nightmarish, bleak, oppressive
反义词: utopian, ideal, idyllic
词性: adjective
