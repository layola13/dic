madreporic:/ˌmædrəˈpɔːrɪk/
adjective| madreporic | madrepore的，多孔珊瑚的 | Relating to or resembling a madrepore, a type of stony coral with a porous structure. | The madreporic plate is a crucial part of the starfish's water vascular system.  海星的筛板是其水管系统的重要组成部分。| porous, perforated | solid, impermeable
Etymology: From French *madréporique*, from *madrépore* (“madrepora, madrepore”), from Italian *madrepora*, from Latin *mater* (“mother”) + *porus* (“pore”).
word type: adjective
