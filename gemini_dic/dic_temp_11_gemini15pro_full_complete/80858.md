gourmandize: /ˈɡɔːrməndaɪz/ | v. | 暴饮暴食；贪吃 | To indulge in good food; to eat greedily or excessively. | He gourmandized on the buffet, trying every dish. 他在自助餐上暴饮暴食，每道菜都尝了一遍。|  He gourmandized his way through the holiday season, gaining ten pounds.  他整个假期都在大吃大喝，体重增加了十磅。 | 近义词: overeat, gorge, binge, indulge | 反义词: diet, fast, abstain | 词性: 动词

gourmandize: /ˈɡɔːrməndaɪz/ | n. | 暴饮暴食；贪吃 | The act of indulging in good food; excessive eating. |  His gourmandize led to health problems. 他的暴饮暴食导致了健康问题。 | Her weekend of gourmandize left her feeling sluggish. 她周末的暴饮暴食让她感到懒洋洋的。 | 近义词: gluttony, overeating,  binging | 反义词: abstinence, moderation | 词性: 名词


词源: From French *gourmandiser*, from *gourmand* (gourmet, gourmand).
