heterochromia: /ˌhɛtərəˈkroʊmiə/ /ˌhɛtərəʊˈkrəʊmɪə/ | n. | 异色症 | A condition in which the irises of the eyes are different colors, or one iris has different colors. |  David Bowie's striking appearance was partly due to his heterochromia. 大卫·鲍伊引人注目的外貌部分原因是他的异色症。| synonyms:  anisochromia, dichromatism | antonyms: None | noun

heterochromia iridis: /ˌhɛtərəˈkroʊmiə ˈɪrɪdɪs/ /ˌhɛtərəʊˈkrəʊmɪə ˈɪrɪdɪs/ | n. | 虹膜异色症 | A specific type of heterochromia affecting the iris of the eye. |  Heterochromia iridis can be complete, with each iris a different color, or sectoral, where parts of one iris are different colors. 虹膜异色症可以是完全的，每个虹膜颜色不同，也可以是扇形的，其中一个虹膜的部分颜色不同。| synonyms:  anisochromia iridis, dichromatism iridis | antonyms: None | noun
