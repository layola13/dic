nonexploitations:/ˌnɒnˌɛksplɔɪˈteɪʃənz/
noun
非剥削; 不剥削
Lack of exploitation or the act of refraining from exploiting something or someone.  It refers to a situation where resources, people, or situations are not used unfairly or unjustly for personal gain.
The company prides itself on its nonexploitation of its workers, providing fair wages and safe working conditions.  这家公司以不剥削工人为荣，提供公平的工资和安全的工作条件。
Near Synonyms:  fairness, justice, equity, respect, ethical treatment
Antonyms: exploitation, abuse, misuse, oppression, manipulation
