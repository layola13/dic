congregated:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪtɪd/ (US)| v. |聚集的，集合的|gathered together in a group or crowd; assembled. |例句：The students congregated in the hall for the assembly. 学生们聚集在大厅里参加集会。|近义词：assembled, gathered, clustered, amassed|反义词：dispersed, scattered|词性：动词(过去分词)

congregate:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (US)| v. |聚集，集合|come together in a group or crowd; assemble. |例句：Birds congregate on the wires in the evening.  傍晚时分，鸟儿们聚集在电线上。|近义词：assemble, gather, cluster, amass|反义词：disperse, scatter|词性：动词


词源:  From Latin congregatus, past participle of congregare "to collect into a flock," from com- "together" (see com-) + gregare "to flock," from grex (genitive gregis) "flock, herd."
