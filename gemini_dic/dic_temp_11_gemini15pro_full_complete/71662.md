flatting:/ˈflætɪŋ/
n.
合租；合住公寓
The act of sharing an apartment or house with others.
例句：Flatting with friends can be a lot of fun.  和朋友合租公寓会很有趣。

例句：She found flatting cheaper than living alone. 她发现合租比自己住便宜。

近义词：sharing, cohabiting, rooming
反义词：living alone

词性: noun

词源：From the noun "flat" (apartment) + "-ing" (present participle suffix, used here to form a gerund).  来自名词“flat”（公寓）+“-ing”（现在分词后缀，此处用于构成动名词）。
