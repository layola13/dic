aconite:/ˈækəˌnaɪt/ (US), /ˈækənaɪt/ (UK)| n.|乌头，附子|A poisonous plant of the genus Aconitum, also known as monkshood or wolfsbane. Its extracts have been used medicinally but are highly toxic.|The aconite is a beautiful but deadly flower. 乌头是一种美丽但致命的花。|The gardener warned us not to touch the aconite. 园丁警告我们不要碰乌头。|Synonyms: monkshood, wolfsbane, leopard's bane|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a plant, could consider "antidote" in a medicinal context)|noun
