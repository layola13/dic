dioritic:/ˌdaɪəˈrɪtɪk/
adjective
闪长岩的；含闪长岩的
Relating to, resembling, or containing diorite, a grey to dark grey intermediate intrusive igneous rock.
Diorite is a dioritic rock.  闪长岩是一种闪长岩岩石。
The geologist identified the sample as dioritic. 地质学家将该样本鉴定为闪长岩的。
Synonyms: igneous, plutonic
Antonyms: sedimentary, metamorphic
词性: adjective
