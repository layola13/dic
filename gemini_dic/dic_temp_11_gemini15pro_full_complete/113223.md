lushness:/ˈlʌʃnəs/
n.
葱郁，茂盛；丰富，华丽
The state of being lush; abundant growth.
The garden was notable for its lushness.  这个花园以其葱郁而闻名。
abundance, richness, luxuriance, profusion
barrenness, sparseness, aridity
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From lush + -ness.
