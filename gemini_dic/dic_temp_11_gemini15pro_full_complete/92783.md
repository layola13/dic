icily:/ˈaɪsɪli/| adv.|冰冷地；冷淡地|In a cold and unfriendly manner; without any warmth or emotion.|She looked at him icily. 她冷冷地望着他。|近义词：coldly, frostily, frigidly|反义词：warmly, kindly|词性:副词
Etymology: From icy + -ly.  "Icy" comes from the Old English "isig," meaning "icy," from "is," meaning "ice."
