horizontal:/ˌhɔːrɪˈzɒntl/ (British), /ˌhɔːrɪˈzɑːntl/ (American)| adj.|水平的；地平线的|Parallel to the horizon; flat and level.|例句：The carpenter checked the horizontal beam to make sure it was straight. 木匠检查了水平横梁，确保它是直的。|例句：Draw a horizontal line across the middle of the page. 在页面中间画一条水平线。|近义词：level, flat, even|反义词：vertical, perpendicular, upright|词性：形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *horizontālis, from Latin horizontem (nominative horizōn), from Ancient Greek ὁρίζων (horízōn, “boundary, horizon”), present participle of ὁρίζω (horízō, “I define, limit”), from ὅρος (hóros, “boundary, limit”).
