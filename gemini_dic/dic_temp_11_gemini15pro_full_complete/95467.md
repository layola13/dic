incited:/ɪnˈsaɪtɪd/
verb
煽动，激起
past participle of incite
The politician's speech incited the crowd to riot.  这位政治家的演讲煽动了人群暴乱。
Near Synonyms:  provoked, instigated, spurred, goaded
Antonyms:  soothed, calmed, pacified, appeased
词性: 动词(过去分词)

incite:/ɪnˈsaɪt/
verb
煽动，激起
to encourage someone to do or feel something unpleasant or violent
The article was intended to incite hatred against the government. 这篇文章旨在煽动对政府的仇恨。
Near Synonyms:  provoke, instigate, spur, goad
Antonyms:  soothe, calm, pacify, appease
词性: 动词
