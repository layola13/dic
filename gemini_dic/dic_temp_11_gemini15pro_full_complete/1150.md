accipenser:/ækɪˈpɛnsər/ | n. |鱘魚|A genus of fish that includes the sturgeon.  These fish are known for their bony plates and roe (caviar).|例句：The accipenser is prized for its caviar.  这种鲟鱼因其鱼子酱而备受珍视。

例句：Several species of accipenser are endangered.  几种鲟鱼物种濒临灭绝。|近义词：sturgeon|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *accipēnser*, from *accipiter* (“hawk”) + an unknown second element, perhaps related to *pēnsum* (“task, weight”).  The connection to "hawk" is uncertain, but may refer to the fish's pointed snout or predatory nature.
