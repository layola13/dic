overbreeds:/ˌoʊvərˈbriːdz/
verb
过度繁殖，过量育种
To breed (animals) excessively, often leading to undesirable traits or health problems.
The farmer overbred his rabbits, resulting in many weak and sickly offspring.  农民过度繁殖他的兔子，导致许多后代虚弱多病。
overproduces, overcrowds
underbreeds
v.
