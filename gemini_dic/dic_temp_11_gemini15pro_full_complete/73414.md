forel:/ˈfɔːrəl/| n.|红点鲑;湖红点鲑;|A species of freshwater fish in the salmon family, typically with red spots.  A type of trout, usually found in lakes. |例句：The fisherman caught a large forel in the lake. 渔夫在湖里钓到一条大红点鲑。

例句：Forel are popular game fish because of their fight and flavor. 红点鲑因其挣扎的力度和美味而成为受欢迎的游钓鱼。|近义词：trout, char|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish species) |词性:noun
