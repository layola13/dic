cofeatured:/koʊˈfiːtʃərd/
adjective
共同出演的，共同作为主角的
appearing together in a leading or prominent role; jointly featured.
The two actors cofeatured in the Broadway play. 这两位演员共同主演了这部百老汇戏剧。
近义词: joint, shared, combined
反义词: separate, individual, solo
词性: adjective

词源:
co- (prefix) + featured (past participle of feature)

co-: from Latin *com, meaning "with, together"
feature: from Old French feture "fashion, form, appearance," from Latin factura "a making, a doing, work," from past participle stem of facere "to make, do."
