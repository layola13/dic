decemvirate:/ˌdɛsɪmˈvɪrət/ | n. |十人执政委员会；十人团 |A body of ten men holding power, especially the Roman commission of ten appointed to codify the laws in the 5th century BC.| The decemvirate ruled Rome with an iron fist. 十人执政委员会以铁腕统治罗马。| synonym: commission, council, board | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "monarchy" or "dictatorship" as contrasting forms of government) | noun

decemvir:/dɪˈsɛmvər/ | n. |十人执政委员会的成员 | A member of a decemvirate. | Each decemvir held considerable power. 每位十人执政委员会的成员都掌握着相当大的权力。| synonym: commissioner, councilor, board member | antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
