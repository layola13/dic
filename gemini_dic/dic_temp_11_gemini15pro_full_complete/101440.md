irascible:/ɪˈræsɪbəl/ /ɪˈræsəbəl/
adjective
易怒的，暴躁的
Having or showing a tendency to be easily angered.
He's known for his irascible temper and often yells at his employees. 他以脾气暴躁闻名，经常对员工大吼大叫。
The irascible old man shouted at the children playing in his yard.  那个脾气暴躁的老人对着在他院子里玩耍的孩子们大喊大叫。
近义词：irritable, grumpy, cantankerous, choleric, short-tempered
反义词：calm, patient, good-natured, affable
词性: adjective
