nonreductive:/ˌnɒnrɪˈdʌktɪv/ | adj. | 非还原性的，非简化的 | Not involving or characterized by reduction; preserving the original or essential characteristics. |  The artist employed a nonreductive approach, incorporating every element of the original sketch into the final painting.  艺术家采用了一种非还原性的方法，将原始草图的每个元素都融入到最终的绘画中。 | The museum adopted a nonreductive strategy for preserving the ancient artifacts.  博物馆采用了一种非还原性的策略来保存古代文物。| 近义词: holistic, comprehensive, integrative | 反义词: reductive, simplistic, minimalist | 词性: adjective

词源:  From non- + reductive.  "Reductive" comes from the Latin "reducere" meaning "to lead back."  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning of "reductive."
