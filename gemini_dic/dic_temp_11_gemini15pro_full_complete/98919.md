intentional:/ɪnˈtɛnʃənəl/ | adj. | 故意的，蓄意的 | Done with purpose; deliberate. | She gave him an intentional push. 她故意推了他一把。|  He made an intentional error to test the system. 他故意弄了个错误来测试系统。| 近义词: deliberate, purposeful, conscious, voluntary | 反义词: accidental, unintentional, involuntary | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *intentionalis, from Latin intentio ‘stretching out, purpose’ (see intention).
