climb:/klaɪm/| v. n.|攀登，爬；上升，增长|To go up something using your feet and often your hands; to increase in level, amount, etc.|He climbed the stairs slowly. 他慢慢地爬上楼梯。|近义词：ascend, mount, scale|反义词:descend, fall|词性:动词, 名词

climb:/klaɪm/| n.|攀登，爬；上升，增长|An act of climbing; a place where you can climb|It was a difficult climb to the top of the mountain. 到达山顶是一次艰难的攀登。|近义词：ascent, scaling|反义词:descent|词性:名词

词源: From Old English *climban (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *klimban, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)klembh- (“to lean, bend, limp”). Cognate with Dutch klimmen, German klimmen, Swedish klämma.  Related to clump.
