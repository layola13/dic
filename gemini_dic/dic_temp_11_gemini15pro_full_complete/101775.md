irrevealable:/ˌɪrɪˈviːləbl/
adjective
不可揭露的，无法透露的
Not able to be revealed or disclosed; too private or sacred to be known.
Her innermost thoughts and feelings were irrevealable. 她内心深处的想法和感受是无法透露的。

The secrets of the ancient society are irrevealable.  古代社会的秘密是不可揭露的.

synonym: undisclosed, unutterable, incommunicable, private, secret
antonym: revealable, disclosable, public, open

词性: adjective
