fud:/fʌd/| n.| 恐惧、不确定性和怀疑 (Fear, Uncertainty, and Doubt) |Information intended to spread fear, uncertainty, and doubt, especially to discourage investment or adoption of something new.  散布旨在传播恐惧、不确定性和怀疑的信息，尤指为阻止投资或采用新事物。| There's a lot of fud being spread about the new cryptocurrency. 关于这种新的加密货币有很多的负面消息正在传播。 | synonyms: disinformation, misinformation, propaganda, scare tactics | antonyms: facts, truth, certainty, assurance | noun

fud:/fʌd/| v. (informal)|散播不实信息以制造恐慌|To spread fear, uncertainty, and doubt, often through misinformation.  散布恐惧、不确定性和怀疑，通常通过错误信息。|  They're trying to fud the competition by spreading rumors. 他们试图通过散布谣言来扰乱竞争。 | synonyms:  disinform, mislead, deceive | antonyms:  inform, enlighten, clarify | verb
