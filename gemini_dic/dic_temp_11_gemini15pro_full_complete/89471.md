honeycombing:/ˈhʌnikəʊmɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhʌnikomiŋ/ (US)| n.| 蜂窝状，空洞形成|The formation of cavities or a pattern of cavities within a tissue or material, resembling a honeycomb.|The X-ray showed extensive honeycombing in the patient's lungs.  X光显示病人肺部有广泛的蜂窝状病变。|Synonyms: cavitation, pitting, erosion|Antonyms: solidity, compactness|noun

honeycombed:/ˈhʌnikəʊmd/ (UK) /ˈhʌnikomd/ (US)| adj.| 蜂窝状的，多孔的|Riddle with cavities, like a honeycomb.|The old wall was honeycombed with holes.  老墙布满了洞，像蜂窝一样。|Synonyms: porous, pitted, cavernous|Antonyms: solid, compact|adjective
