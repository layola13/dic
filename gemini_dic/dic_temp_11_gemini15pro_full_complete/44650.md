croze: /kroʊz/ | n. |填字游戏; 克漏字 |A cloze test, also cloze deletion test or occlusion test, is an exercise, test, or assessment consisting of a portion of text with certain words removed (cloze text), where the participant is asked to replace the missing words. |The teacher gave the students a croze test to assess their reading comprehension. 老师给学生们做了一次克漏字测试，以评估他们的阅读理解能力。| synonyms: fill-in-the-blank, gap-fill | antonyms: N/A | noun
