delouser:/diːˈlaʊzər/ | n. | 除虱器，灭虱剂 | A substance or device used to kill lice. | The soldier used a delouser to get rid of the lice in his clothes. 士兵用除虱剂清除衣服上的虱子。 |  The family used a special delouser shampoo to treat head lice. 这家人用一种特殊的除虱洗发水来治疗头虱。| synonyms: insecticide, pesticide, lice comb | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps lice attractant, but not commonly used) | noun

delouse:/diːˈlaʊs/ | v. | 除虱，清除虱子 | To rid someone or something of lice. |  The nurse deloused the patient's hair. 护士为病人清除头发上的虱子。 |  They deloused the entire house after the infestation.  虫害发生后，他们对整个房子进行了除虱。| synonyms: disinfest, cleanse, sanitize | antonyms: infest | verb
