endodermis:/ˈɛndəʊdɜːrmɪs/| n. | 内皮层 | The innermost layer of the cortex in plant roots and some stems, surrounding the vascular cylinder.  It regulates the passage of water and minerals into the vascular tissues. | The endodermis plays a crucial role in controlling nutrient uptake. 内皮层在控制养分吸收方面起着至关重要的作用。

The endodermis is characterized by the Casparian strip. 内皮层的特征是凯氏带。 | 近义词: inner layer,  | 反义词: epidermis | 词性: noun
