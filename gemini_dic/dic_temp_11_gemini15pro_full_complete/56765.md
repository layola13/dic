dopamine:/ˈdoʊpəmiːn/ (US) /ˈdəʊpəmiːn/ (UK)| n.|多巴胺|A neurotransmitter associated with pleasure, motivation, and reward. It plays a crucial role in various brain functions, including movement, memory, and mood.|例句：Dopamine is released when we experience something pleasurable. 当我们经历快乐的事情时，多巴胺就会释放。|近义词: neurotransmitter, hormone|反义词: (No direct antonym, but dopamine antagonists block its effects)|noun
