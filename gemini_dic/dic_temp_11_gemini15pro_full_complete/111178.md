lithoglyphic:/ˌlɪθəˈɡlɪfɪk/
adjective

The artist specialized in lithoglyphic portraits.  这位艺术家专攻石刻肖像。

lithoglyphy:/lɪˈθɒɡlɪfi/
noun
石刻术；在石头上雕刻
The art of engraving on stone.
Lithoglyphy was a common practice in ancient Egypt.  石刻是古埃及的常见做法。
The artist's skill in lithoglyphy was unmatched.  这位艺术家的石刻技艺无与伦比。
近义词:petroglyphy, rock carving, inscription
