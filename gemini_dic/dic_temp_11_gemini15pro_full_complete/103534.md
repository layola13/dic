ETYMOLOGY->
The origin of "jog" is uncertain.  One theory suggests it may be related to "shog," a Middle English word meaning "to shake or jolt."  Another possibility is that it is a 16th-century variant of "dodge."  The modern sense of "to run slowly" emerged in the 19th century.
