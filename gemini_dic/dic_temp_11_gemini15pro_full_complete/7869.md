antecedently:/ˌæn.tɪˈsiː.dənt.li/
adverb|先前地，预先地|previously, earlier, beforehand; in advance.  Refers to something happening before something else, often in a causal or logical relationship.|He had antecedently agreed to the terms of the contract. 他先前同意了合同条款。|
synonyms: previously, beforehand, earlier, prior to, in advance
antonyms: subsequently, afterward, later
part of speech: adverb

antecedently:/ˌæn.tɪˈsiː.dənt.li/
adverb|先前地，预先地|Having been or existed before something else, especially in a way that influences or causes that thing. |The problems were antecedently addressed in the previous meeting. 这些问题在之前的会议上已经预先讨论过了。|
synonyms:  aforementioned, foregoing, preceding, former
antonyms:  subsequent, following, succeeding, latter
part of speech: adverb
