nontensility:/ˌnɒntɛnˈsɪləti/
noun
不可拉伸性; 非张力
The lack of ability to be stretched or extended; the inability to withstand tension.
The nontensility of the material made it unsuitable for bungee jumping. 这种材料的不可拉伸性使其不适合用于蹦极跳。
Near synonyms: inextensibility, rigidity, stiffness
Antonyms: tensility, elasticity, stretchability
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + tensile (capable of being stretched) + -ity (forming abstract nouns of quality).  Tensile ultimately derives from the Latin "tendere" (to stretch).
