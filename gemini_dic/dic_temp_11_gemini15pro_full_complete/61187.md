elumbated: /iːˈlʌmbeɪtɪd/
adjective
无力下腰的，腰部僵硬的
weakened or exhausted in the loins.  Having lost flexibility in the lower back. Characterized by an inability to bend at the waist.
例句：After the long hike, he felt elumbated and could barely stand up straight.  长途跋涉之后，他感到腰部僵硬，几乎站不直。

例句：The elderly woman became increasingly elumbated as her arthritis worsened.  随着关节炎的恶化，这位老妇人越来越无法弯腰了。

近义词：stiff, inflexible, rigid, debilitated
反义词：flexible, supple, limber
词性：adjective
