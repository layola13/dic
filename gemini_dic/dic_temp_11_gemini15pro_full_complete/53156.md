diplostemony:/dɪplɒˈstɛməni/ | n. | 双雄蕊, 二强雄蕊 |  The condition in which a flower has twice as many stamens as petals, with the stamens arranged in two whorls. | The flower exhibits diplostemony, with two sets of stamens. 这朵花展现出双雄蕊的特征，有两组雄蕊。| synonym:  none (highly specific botanical term) | antonym: none (highly specific botanical term)| noun
