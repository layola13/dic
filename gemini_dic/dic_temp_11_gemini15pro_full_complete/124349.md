motionbuilder:/ˈməʊʃənˌbɪldər/| n.|动作构建器|A 3D character animation software produced by Autodesk. It is used for creating, editing, and animating 3D characters and other objects. It is often used in film, television, and video game production.|Example: The animators used MotionBuilder to create realistic character movements for the film.|近义词:character animation software, 3D animation software|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
motion (n.) + builder (n.)

motion (n.):  from Old French *mocien (modern French motion), from Latin motionem (nominative motio) "a moving, motion, gesture," noun of action from past-participle stem of movere "to move" 

builder (n.): from build (v.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting an agent or one who performs an action). build (v.) Old English byldan "to build, construct, erect," from Proto-Germanic *bildan "to build"
