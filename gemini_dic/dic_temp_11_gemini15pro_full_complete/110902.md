lipases:/ˈlaɪpeɪsiz/ /ˈlɪpeɪsiːz/| n.|脂肪酶|Enzymes that catalyze the hydrolysis of fats (lipids) into fatty acids and glycerol.  They play a crucial role in digestion and other biological processes.|Pancreatic lipases are essential for digesting fats in the small intestine. 胰脂肪酶对于小肠消化脂肪至关重要。

Lipases are used in various industrial applications, such as the production of detergents and biofuels.  脂肪酶用于各种工业应用，例如洗涤剂和生物燃料的生产。|近义词:esterases|反义词:(No direct antonym, but inhibitors block lipase activity)|词性:noun
