outlier:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪər/ | n. | 异常值，离群值 |  Something that is situated away from or classed differently than a main or related body.  A person or thing differing from all other members of a particular group or set. |  The data point was considered an outlier and removed from the analysis.  这个数据点被认为是异常值，并从分析中移除。 | Synonyms: anomaly, deviation, aberration, exception, irregularity | Antonyms: norm, standard, average | noun
