information:/ˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃn/ | n. |信息，资料，情报| Facts provided or learned about something or someone. |例句：The website provides a lot of information about local businesses.  这个网站提供了很多关于当地企业的信息。

例句：She gave me some useful information about the job. 她给了我一些关于这份工作的有用信息。|近义词：data, facts, intelligence, news |反义词: disinformation, misinformation |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French informacion, from Latin informatio (“formation, conception, teaching”),  from informare ("to shape, train, instruct, educate"), from in- (“in, into”) + formare (“to form, shape”).
