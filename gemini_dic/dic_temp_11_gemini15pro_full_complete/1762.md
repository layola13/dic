acquisitions:/ˌækwɪˈzɪʃənz/
n.|获得，收购，获得物|The company made several acquisitions last year to expand its market share.  该公司去年进行了几次收购以扩大其市场份额。|例句：The museum's latest acquisition is a rare 17th-century painting.  博物馆最近的收购是一幅罕见的17世纪的画作。

例句：His acquisition of knowledge through reading is impressive. 他通过阅读获取知识的能力令人印象深刻。|近义词：purchases, mergers, takeovers|反义词:divestments, disposals|词性:noun
