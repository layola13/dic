disspirit:/dɪˈspɪrɪt/
verb
使沮丧，使气馁
To discourage or depress; to dampen the spirits of.
The team was dispirited by their unexpected loss.  该队因意外失利而感到沮丧。
The constant rain dispirited the campers.  连绵不断的雨使露营者感到沮丧。
synonym: demoralize, deject, discourage
antonym: encourage, hearten, inspire
词性: 动词
