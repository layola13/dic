avoyer:/ævˈɔɪər/ | n. | 稅務官；收稅員 | An officer who collects tolls or taxes, especially in France or Switzerland. |  The avoyer oversaw the collection of taxes from the local merchants. 稅務官監督當地商人的稅收。| synonym: tax collector, revenue officer | antonym: taxpayer | noun

avoyer:/ævˈɔɪər/ | n. | 市长；市政官 | A chief magistrate in some Swiss cantons. | The avoyer addressed the citizens during the annual festival. 市长在年度节日期间向市民发表讲话。| synonym: mayor, magistrate | antonym: citizen, resident | noun
