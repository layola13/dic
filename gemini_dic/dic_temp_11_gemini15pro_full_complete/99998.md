interpealed:/ˌɪntərˈpiːld/ | v. |  撤销；废除（法令、判决等） | To revoke or rescind a law, decree, or sentence. |  The king interpealed the unjust law. 国王废除了那条不公正的法律。| repeal, revoke, rescind, annul, overturn | enact, establish, uphold | verb
Etymology: From Late Latin *interpellare, from Latin inter- + appellare "to address, appeal to".
