offbeatness:/ˌɔːfˈbiːtnəs/ | n. | 非传统；不落俗套；反常 |The quality of being unusual and different from most people or things. | Her offbeatness made her stand out from the crowd. 她的不落俗套使她从人群中脱颖而出。|  The film's offbeatness and quirky humor appealed to a niche audience. 这部电影的反常和古怪幽默吸引了一群小众观众。 | synonyms: unconventionality, eccentricity, quirkiness, idiosyncrasy | antonyms: conventionality, normality, typicality | noun

offbeat:/ˌɔːfˈbiːt/ | adj. | 非传统的；不落俗套的；反常的 | Unusual and different from most people or things. |  She has a very offbeat sense of humor. 她有一种非常不落俗套的幽默感。 | They went to an offbeat little restaurant. 他们去了一家非传统的小餐馆。 | synonyms: unconventional, eccentric, quirky, idiosyncratic | antonyms: conventional, normal, typical | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From off-beat (adjective), meaning "away from the beat," referring to music or rhythm.  "Off" indicating deviation or separation, and "beat" referring to the regular pulse in music. The suffix "-ness" turns the adjective into a noun denoting a state or quality.
