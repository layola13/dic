decompartmentalizations: /ˌdiːkəmˌpɑːrtˌmɛntələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 去隔间化；去部门化；消除界限 | The process of breaking down barriers between different departments or groups within an organization or system, or between different aspects of one's life. | The decompartmentalization of education allows students to explore connections between different subjects. 教育的去隔间化让学生能够探索不同科目之间的联系。

The decompartmentalization of government agencies is intended to improve efficiency and communication. 政府机构的去部门化旨在提高效率和沟通。| 近义词: integration, unification, blending, merging | 反义词: compartmentalization, segregation, division, separation | 词性: noun
