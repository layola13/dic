cosying:/ˈkoʊziɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkəʊziɪŋ/ (UK)| v. |使舒适；窝在...里；|Making oneself or something else warm and comfortable, often in a small or enclosed space.  It can also imply becoming friendly or intimate with someone.|例句：They were cosying up by the fire. 他们在炉火旁偎依取暖。|例句：She was cosying up to the boss, hoping for a promotion. 她为了升职巴结老板。|近义词：snuggling, cuddling, nestling, getting comfortable|反义词:distancing, separating|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the adjective "cosy" + "-ing" suffix, indicating the act of making cosy. "Cosy" itself likely originated from Scots "cozie," of uncertain origin.
