gully:/ˈɡʌli/| n.|沟壑，溪谷，排水沟|A deep ditch or channel worn in the earth by running water. |The heavy rain carved a gully in the hillside. 大雨在山坡上冲出了一条沟壑。|近义词：ravine, ditch, channel, gorge|反义词:ridge, peak|noun
gully:/ˈɡʌli/| vt.|使成沟壑，侵蚀成沟|To erode or wear away, forming a gully. |Heavy rains gullied the fields.  暴雨把田地冲成了沟壑。|近义词：erode, channel, furrow|反义词:fill, level|verb
