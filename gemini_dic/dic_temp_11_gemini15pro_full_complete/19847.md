binder:/ˈbaɪndər/| n.|装订机；活页夹；粘合剂；捆绑者|A binder is a cover for holding loose sheets of paper together.  装订机是一种用于将散页纸固定在一起的封面。|The teacher used a binder to organize her student's work. 老师用活页夹整理学生的作业。|近义词：folder, portfolio, notebook|反义词: loose leaf|noun

binder:/ˈbaɪndər/| n.|粘合剂|A binder is a substance that makes things stick together. 粘合剂是一种使物体粘在一起的物质。|The binder in the paint helps it adhere to the wall. 油漆中的粘合剂有助于其粘附在墙上。|近义词：adhesive, glue|反义词: solvent|noun
