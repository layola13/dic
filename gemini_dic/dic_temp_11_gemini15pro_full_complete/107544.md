landleaper:/ˈlændˌliːpər/ | n. | 陆地跳跃者；陆地上的跳跃者 |  A creature that leaps or jumps on land. It often refers to terrestrial animals known for their jumping ability, such as certain frogs, insects, or even small mammals.  | The agile landleaper easily cleared the fallen log.  敏捷的陆地跳跃者轻松越过了倒下的原木。|  jumper, leaper, hopper | burrower, crawler | noun
