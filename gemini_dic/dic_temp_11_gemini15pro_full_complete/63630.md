eolian:/iːˈoʊliən/
adjective
风的；风成的；风蚀的
relating to or arising from the action of the wind.
The eolian sands sculpted the rocks into fantastic shapes. 风蚀的沙子将岩石雕刻成奇特的形状。
Synonyms: windswept, wind-blown, airy
Antonyms: sheltered, protected
词性: 形容词


eolians:/iːˈoʊliənz/
noun
风成物；风神埃俄罗斯
(usually initial capital letter) Aeolus, god of the winds in Greek mythology, or the winds themselves personified. Also, things relating to Aeolus, the winds, or wind-produced effects, such as musical instruments.
The archaeologist studied the eolians deposited by ancient storms. 考古学家研究了古代风暴沉积的风成物。
Synonyms: wind deposits, dunes
Antonyms: (For Aeolus as god of winds) None.  (For wind deposits)  None direct antonym, but could consider "alluvial deposits" (water deposits).
词性: 名词
