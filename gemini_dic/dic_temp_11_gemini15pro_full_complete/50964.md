designs:/dɪˈzaɪnz/| n. |设计；图案；构思；意图|plans or drawings produced to show the look and function or workings of a building, garment, or other object before it is built or made; a decorative pattern|例句：The architect showed us the designs for the new building.  建筑师向我们展示了新建筑的设计图。

例句：Her dress designs are very original. 她的服装设计非常新颖。|近义词：plans, blueprints, drawings, patterns, schemes, intentions|反义词：n/a|词性:noun


designs:/dɪˈzaɪnz/| v. |设计；构思；计划；预定|decide upon the look and functioning of (a building, garment, or other object), typically by making a detailed drawing of it; do or plan (something) with a specific purpose in mind|例句：She designs clothes for children. 她设计童装。

例句：This course is designed to help students improve their writing skills.  这门课程旨在帮助学生提高写作能力。|近义词：plan, create, develop, intend|反义词：n/a|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "deisigner" (to designate, mark out), from Latin "designare" (to mark out, designate), from "de-" (out, from) + "signare" (to mark).
