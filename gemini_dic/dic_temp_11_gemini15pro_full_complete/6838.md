anathema: /əˈnæθəmə/ | n. | 诅咒，令人厌恶的人或事 | something or someone that one vehemently dislikes. A formal curse by a pope or a council of the church, excommunicating a person or denouncing a doctrine. |  The very idea of working on a Sunday was anathema to him.  星期天工作对他来说简直是不可接受的。| synonyms: abomination, curse, bane, execration | antonyms: blessing, benediction | noun

anathema: /əˈnæθəmə/ | n. | 被诅咒的人或事物 | a person or thing formally denounced or excommunicated by a religious authority. | The heretic was declared anathema by the church.  异教徒被教会宣布为被诅咒的人。 | synonyms: outcast, pariah, persona non grata | antonyms: saint, idol | noun
