moonstruck:/ˈmuːnˌstrʌk/| adj.|着迷的，神魂颠倒的；月光照射的| Affected by the moon, especially in the sense of being romantically sentimental, whimsical, or slightly mad.|She had a moonstruck look in her eyes after he proposed. 他求婚后，她眼里充满了着迷的神情。|Synonyms: lovesick, infatuated, dreamy, whimsical|Antonyms: rational, sensible, down-to-earth|adjective

moonstruck:/ˈmuːnˌstrʌk/| adj.|月光照射的|Exposed to the light of the moon.|The moonstruck beach was beautiful. 月光照耀下的海滩很美。|Synonyms: moonlit|Antonyms: shadowed, dark|adjective
