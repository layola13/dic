cloisonné:/ˌklɔɪzəˈneɪ/ /ˌklɔɪzəˈniː/ | n. adj.|景泰蓝；景泰蓝制的|Enamelware in which colored areas are separated by thin metal strips, usually of gold or silver, soldered to a metal base.  A technique of enameling in which the enamel is contained within wires or thin strips of metal soldered to a metal base.|The vase was made of exquisite cloisonné.  这个花瓶是用精美的景泰蓝制成的。|The cloisonné jewelry box was a treasured family heirloom.  这个景泰蓝首饰盒是珍贵的传家宝。|Synonyms: enamelwork, enamel |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the object itself; perhaps "plain metalwork" for contrast)|noun, adjective
