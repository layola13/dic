eleatic:/ˌɛliˈætɪk/
adjective
埃利亚学派的；埃利亚的
Relating to Elea, a Greek colony in southern Italy, or to the Eleatic school of philosophy founded by Parmenides.
Eleatic philosophy denies the reality of change and plurality.  埃利亚哲学否认变化和多元性的真实性。
Parmenides was the founder of the Eleatic school. 巴门尼德是埃利亚学派的创始人。
Synonyms:  Parmenidean, monistic
Antonyms:  pluralistic, Heraclitean
