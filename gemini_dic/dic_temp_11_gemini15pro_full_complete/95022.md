inaccessible:/ɪnəkˈsɛsəbəl/
|adj.| 不可及的；难接近的；难理解的|The summit was inaccessible due to the heavy snow. 由于大雪，山顶无法到达。|
| |The complex scientific jargon made the article inaccessible to the average reader. 复杂的科学术语使普通读者无法理解这篇文章。|
| |Synonym: remote, impassable, unapproachable, incomprehensible|
| |Antonym: accessible, approachable, near, comprehensible|
|adjective|
