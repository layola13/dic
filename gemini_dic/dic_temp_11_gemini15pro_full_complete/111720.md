locomotively:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊtɪvli/| adv.|像火车头一样；用火车头；以机车牵引|In a manner resembling a locomotive; by means of a locomotive. |The train moved locomotively along the tracks. 火车像火车头一样沿着轨道行驶。|近义词：powerfully, forcefully|反义词：weakly, feebly|词性:副词
locomotive:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊtɪv/| n. adj.|火车头；机车；运动的，移动的|A powered rail vehicle used for pulling trains; relating to locomotion; self-propelled. |The locomotive pulled a long line of freight cars. 火车头拉着一长列货车。|近义词：engine, train|反义词：stationary, immobile|词性:名词/形容词

词源: From Latin *loco motivus* (“place moving”), from *locus* (“place”) + *motivus* (“moving”).
