misconstruct:/ˌmɪskənˈstrʌkt/ | n. | 误解；错误的解释 | A wrong or inaccurate interpretation or understanding. | His misconstruct of the situation led to further problems. 他对情况的误解导致了进一步的问题。 | Synonyms: misinterpretation, misunderstanding, misapprehension, distortion | Antonyms: understanding, comprehension, correct interpretation | noun
