bicyanide:/ˌbaɪˈsaɪəˌnaɪd/ | n. | 双氰化物，氰化物 |A compound containing two cyanide groups. It often refers to the cyanide anion (CN-) when two of them are associated with a metal cation (e.g., mercury(II) bicyanide, Hg(CN)2).| Mercury(II) bicyanide is a highly toxic substance.  氰化汞是一种剧毒物质。| Potassium bicyanide is used in electroplating. 氰化钾用于电镀。 | cyanide, dicyanide |  | noun
