outshriek: /ˌaʊtˈʃriːk/ | v. |  发出比…更大的喊叫声；胜过…的喊叫 | to shout louder than someone or something else |  She outshrieked the other fans at the concert. 她演唱会上喊叫的声音盖过了其他歌迷。 | 近义词：outcry, outshout, yell, scream | 反义词: whisper, murmur | 词性: 动词

词源: out- + shriek.  "Out-" prefix meaning surpassing or exceeding. "Shriek" meaning a loud, sharp cry.
