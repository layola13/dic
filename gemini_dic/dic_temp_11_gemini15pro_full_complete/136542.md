nonrustic:/ˌnɑːnˈrʌstɪk/ | adj. | 非乡村的，非质朴的 | Not rustic; sophisticated, elegant, or refined. |  She wore a nonrustic, elegant gown to the gala. 她穿着非乡村风格的优雅礼服参加了晚会。| The interior design was decidedly nonrustic, featuring sleek lines and modern art.  室内设计明显是非乡村风格的，以流畅的线条和现代艺术为特色。 | 近义词: sophisticated, urbane, polished, refined | 反义词: rustic, rural, simple, unsophisticated | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: non- + rustic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "rustic" refers to a rural or simple style.
