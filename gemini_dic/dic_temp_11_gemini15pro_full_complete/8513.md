anticapitalism:/ˌæntiˈkæpɪtəlɪzəm/
n.|反资本主义|Opposition to capitalism. It encompasses a range of ideologies and movements that criticize and seek to replace capitalism with alternative economic systems, often emphasizing social ownership and democratic control of the means of production.|Many young people are drawn to anticapitalism as a solution to perceived economic inequalities.|近义词：socialism, communism, anarchism|反义词:capitalism|词性:noun
