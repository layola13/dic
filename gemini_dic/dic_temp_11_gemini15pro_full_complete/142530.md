ossifies:/ˈɑːsɪfaɪz/| v. |骨化；硬化；僵化|To become bone; to harden or become rigid, like bone; to become inflexible or fixed.|例句：The cartilage gradually ossifies into bone. 软骨逐渐骨化成骨。|例句：His political views ossified as he got older. 他的政治观点随着年龄的增长而僵化。|近义词：harden, solidify, rigidify, fossilize|反义词:soften,liquify,flexibilize|词性:动词
