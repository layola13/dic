adjourn:/əˈdʒɜːrn/ /əˈdʒɝːn/ | v. | 暂停，休会，延期 | To suspend a meeting or session to a later time or another place. | The judge adjourned the trial until the following week. 法官将审判延期到下周。

The meeting was adjourned due to lack of quorum.  由于缺乏法定人数，会议休会了。| 近义词：postpone, suspend, recess, defer | 反义词： convene, commence, open | 词性: 动词
