endostoma:/ɛndoʊˈstoʊmə/| n.|内口;|An internal opening or orifice. Specifically, in zoology, the inner or true mouth in certain invertebrates, as distinguished from the oral opening of the buccal cavity.|The endostoma of the snail is hidden within its head.|近义词:aperture, orifice|反义词:|noun
