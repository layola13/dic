hypoplastron:/ˈhaɪpəʊˌplæstrɒn/ (US) /ˈhaɪpəʊˌplæstrən/ (UK)| n. |腹甲（龟类）|The hypoplastron is a bony plate that forms part of the plastron, the ventral (belly) side of the shell of a turtle or tortoise.|The hypoplastron of this fossil is remarkably well-preserved. 这块化石的腹甲保存得非常好。|Synonym: plastral plate|Antonym: carapace|noun
