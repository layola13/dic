instiller:/ɪnˈstɪlər/ | n. |灌输者；鼓吹者|Someone who instills a particular feeling, idea, or principle into someone else. |例句：She was the instiller of strong moral values in her children. 她向她的孩子们灌输了强烈的道德价值。

例句：He is seen as an instiller of hope in the community. 他被视为社区希望的灌输者。|近义词：inculcator, implanter, infuser |反义词：extractor, remover |词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "instill," which comes from the Latin word "instillare," meaning "to drip in."  "In-" means "in" and "stillare" means "to drip."
