dialight:/ˈdaɪəˌlaɪt/| n.|透光板，透光材料，日光灯|A translucent panel, especially one used in architecture, or a material that transmits light. Also, a fluorescent lamp.|The dialight panels in the roof allowed natural light to illuminate the space. 屋顶上的透光板让自然光照亮了空间。|The architect used dialight to create a sense of openness.  建筑师使用透光材料来营造一种开放感。|synonyms: translucent panel, skylight, light panel|antonyms: opaque material, solid panel|noun
