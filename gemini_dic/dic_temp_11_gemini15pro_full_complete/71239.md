fitting:/ˈfɪtɪŋ/| adj. n.|合适的，恰当的；装配，配件|Something that is fitting is suitable or appropriate. A fitting is a small part that you connect to something.|例句：The dress was a fitting tribute to her elegance. 这条裙子是对她优雅气质的恰如其分的赞美。|近义词：suitable, appropriate, proper, apt, relevant, convenient, fitting, befitting|反义词：inappropriate, unsuitable, improper, unfitting|词性:形容词

fitting:/ˈfɪtɪŋ/| n.|装配，安装；配件，零件|The act of putting something together, or a small part that is used to connect things.|例句：The fitting of the new kitchen will take two days. 新厨房的安装将需要两天时间。|近义词：installation, assembly, attachment, connection|反义词：disassembly, removal|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fittian, from fit (suitable, proper). Related to Old Norse fitja (to knit, weave), and German fügen (to join).  The noun sense developed from the verb.
