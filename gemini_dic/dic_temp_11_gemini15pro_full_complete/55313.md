dissolvents:/dɪˈsɒlvənts/ | n. | 溶剂；溶解物 | Substances that dissolve other substances. |  Many industrial cleaners contain harsh dissolvents. 许多工业清洁剂含有刺激性强的溶剂。| synonyms: solvents, diluents | antonyms: solutes, precipitants | noun

dissolvent:/dɪˈsɒlvənt/ | adj. | 溶解的；有溶解力的 | Having the power of dissolving; causing solution. | A dissolvent liquid can break down certain materials.  一种溶解性液体可以分解某些物质。| synonyms: solvent, dissolving | antonyms: insoluble, non-solvent | adjective
