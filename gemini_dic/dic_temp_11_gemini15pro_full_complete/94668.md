impreventable:/ˌɪmprɪˈvɛntəbl/
adjective
不可避免的，无法阻止的
Not able to be prevented.
The accident was impreventable due to the icy conditions. 由于路面结冰，事故无法避免。
The judge ruled that the death was an impreventable accident. 法官裁定这起死亡是不可避免的事故。
近义词: unavoidable, inevitable, inescapable
反义词: preventable, avoidable, stoppable
词性: adjective

词源: im- (not) + preventable (able to be prevented)  Ultimately from Latin *praevenire* "to come before, anticipate".
