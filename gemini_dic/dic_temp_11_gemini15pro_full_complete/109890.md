liaising:/liˈeɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
联络，联系
acting as a link between people or groups
例句：
The company is liaising with local businesses to promote the new product.
该公司正在与当地企业联系，以推广新产品。

She's been liaising with the community group about the proposed changes.
她一直在与社区团体就拟议的变更进行联系。

近义词：
coordinating, collaborating, communicating, networking, connecting
反义词：
isolating, separating, disconnecting

词性:
v.
