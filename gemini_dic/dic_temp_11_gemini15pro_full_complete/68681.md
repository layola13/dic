farcicality:/fɑːrˈsɪkəlɪti/| n. |滑稽；荒唐；闹剧 |The farcicality of the situation was apparent to everyone.  情况的滑稽之处每个人都显而易见。|The play was criticized for its sheer farcicality. 这部戏剧因其纯粹的闹剧而受到批评。|近义词：absurdity, ludicrousness, ridiculousness |反义词：seriousness, solemnity |词性:noun

Etymology:
From farce (n.) + -ical (adj. suffix) + -ity (n. suffix).  Ultimately from Latin farcire "to stuff."
