micas: /ˈmaɪkəz/| n. |云母（的复数形式）|A group of hydrous potassium, aluminum silicate minerals. They readily separate into very thin, tough, often transparent, and elastic sheets.|We can see glittering micas in the granite. 我们可以在花岗岩中看到闪闪发光的云母。|Synonyms:  vermiculite (in some contexts), phlogopite (for specific types), biotite (for specific types)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
