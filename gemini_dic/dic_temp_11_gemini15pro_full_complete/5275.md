allopath:/ˈæləpæθ/| n.|对抗疗法医师；对抗疗法信奉者|A practitioner of conventional medicine; a believer in or advocate of allopathy.|例句：The allopath prescribed antibiotics for the infection.  对抗疗法医师开了抗生素来治疗感染。|例句：Some people prefer alternative medicine to seeing an allopath.  有些人更喜欢替代医学而不是去看对抗疗法医师。|近义词：conventional doctor, traditional physician|反义词：homeopath, naturopath|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek ἄλλος (állos, “other, different”) + πάθος (páthos, “suffering, disease”).  Coined by Samuel Hahnemann, the founder of homeopathy, to contrast conventional medicine with his own system.
