misregulate:/ˌmɪsˈrɛɡjʊleɪt/| v. |错误地管制，调节失当|To regulate badly or incorrectly.|The government misregulated the banking industry, leading to the financial crisis.|mismanage, mishandle|properly regulate|Verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + regulate (to control or direct by a rule, principle, or method).  The word "regulate" comes from the Latin word "regulare," meaning "to control by rule, direct."
