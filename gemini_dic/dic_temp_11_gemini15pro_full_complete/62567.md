energized:/ˈenərdʒaɪzd/| adj.|充满活力的，精力充沛的|Having been given energy; invigorated.|例句：I felt energized after a good night's sleep. 我睡了一夜好觉后感到精力充沛。|近义词：invigorated, stimulated, revitalized, refreshed|反义词：tired, exhausted, drained, lethargic|词性:形容词

Etymology: From energize + -ed.  Energize comes from energy + -ize. Energy ultimately derives from the Greek word "energeia" meaning "activity, operation."
