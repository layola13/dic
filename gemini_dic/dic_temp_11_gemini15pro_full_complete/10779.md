aquapuncture: /ˈækwəˌpʌŋktʃər/
noun
水针疗法
A form of therapy similar to acupuncture, but using injections of liquid substances (often water) at acupuncture points instead of needles.
Aquapuncture is often used to treat musculoskeletal pain. 水针疗法常用于治疗肌肉骨骼疼痛。
Aquapuncture can be used to treat a variety of ailments. 水针疗法可以用来治疗各种疾病。
Synonyms: acupuncture injection, aqua-acupuncture, water acupuncture
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider contrasting therapies like massage, chiropractic)
