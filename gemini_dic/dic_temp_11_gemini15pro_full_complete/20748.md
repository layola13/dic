blanching:/ˈblæntʃɪŋ/ /ˈblɑːntʃɪŋ/| n. v.|漂白，变白；(尤指用沸水)烫，焯|The process of briefly immersing food in boiling water, usually to inactivate enzymes, reduce bitterness, or preserve color.  It's also used to loosen skins for peeling.|例句：Blanching the vegetables helps to retain their vibrant color.  焯蔬菜有助于保持它们鲜艳的颜色。

例句：The blanching of her face showed how frightened she was. 她脸色发白，表明她有多害怕。|近义词：whitening, paling, scalding, parboiling|反义词:darkening, browning|词性:noun, verb

词源: From Old French "blanchir" (to whiten), from "blanc" (white). Ultimately from Frankish *blank (white, shining), from Proto-Germanic *blankaz.
