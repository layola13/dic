inculcate:/ɪnˈkʌlkeɪt/
verb
灌输,谆谆教诲
To instill (an attitude, idea, or habit) by persistent instruction.
The teacher inculcated a love of learning in her students. 老师向学生灌输热爱学习的精神。
instill, implant, ingrain, impress, fix
eradicate, remove, extinguish
v.
