homages:/ˈhɒmɪdʒɪz/ /ˈɑːmɪdʒɪz/
noun
敬意，致敬；(尤指对名人的)公开致敬，崇敬
public tributes of respect, especially to famous people
The film pays homage to the classic Hollywood musicals. 这部电影向经典的好莱坞音乐剧致敬。
tributes, respects, salutations
disrespects, insults
n.
