bad-assed:/ˌbædˈæst/ | adj. |  厉害的，强悍的，令人敬畏的 |  (Slang) Tough, uncompromising, and intimidating; exceptionally cool or impressive.  Originates from "bad ass," referring to a stubborn or difficult donkey/mule. Evolved to describe someone difficult to handle and later someone admirable for their strength and skill. |  That guitarist is bad-assed.  那个吉他手太厉害了。|  Synonyms: awesome, badass, cool, tough, formidable, impressive, kick-ass, wicked | Antonyms: weak, timid, cowardly | adjective

bad-ass:/ˈbædæs/ | n. |  厉害的人，狠角色 | (Slang)  A tough, uncompromising, and intimidating person; someone exceptionally cool or impressive.  | He's a real bad-ass on the basketball court. 他在篮球场上是个狠角色。 | Synonyms: tough guy, hotshot, maverick | Antonyms: weakling, coward | noun
