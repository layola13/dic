intrinsically:/ɪnˈtrɪnzɪk(ə)li/ /ɪnˈtrɪnsɪkli/
adjective
内在地；本质地；固有地
Belonging naturally; essential.
例句：
The value of a work of art is intrinsically linked to its creator.  一件艺术品的价值与其创作者有着内在的联系。

Creativity is intrinsically linked to risk-taking. 创造力与冒险精神有着内在的联系。


近义词：inherently, essentially, naturally, fundamentally
反义词：extrinsically, superficially
词性: adjective
