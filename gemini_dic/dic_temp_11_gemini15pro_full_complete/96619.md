indulgential: /ɪnˈdʌldʒɛnʃəl/
adjective
放纵的，纵容的
Characterized by or showing indulgence; lenient; permissive.
He gave his children an indulgential smile. 他对孩子们露出了纵容的微笑。

近义词: lenient, permissive, forgiving, tolerant, indulgent
反义词: strict, severe, harsh, unforgiving, intolerant
词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *indulgentialis, from indulgentia (“indulgence”) + -alis (suffix forming adjectives).
