culminate:/ˈkʌlmɪneɪt/| v. |达到顶点；达到高潮；告终| to reach the highest point of development; to end, especially after gradually increasing in force or intensity|例句：The celebration culminated in a spectacular fireworks display. 庆祝活动以一场壮观的烟火表演达到高潮。

|例句：Her years of research culminated in the publication of her groundbreaking book. 她多年的研究最终出版了她的开创性著作。|近义词：climax, peak, conclude, end|反义词:begin, start, commence|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin culminatus, past participle of culminare "to crown, top, bring to a point," from culmen "top, summit."
