illicitous:/ɪˈlɪsɪtəs/
adjective
非法，不正当的
Forbidden by law, rules, or custom.
例句：The illicitous trade of drugs. 非法毒品交易。
例句：He was involved in an illicitous affair. 他卷入了一段不正当的恋情。
近义词：illegal, unlawful, illegitimate, prohibited, forbidden
反义词：legal, lawful, legitimate, permitted, allowed
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *illicitus*, from in- ‘not’ + licitus ‘lawful’.
