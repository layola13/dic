maligners:/məˈlaɪnərz/ | n. | 诽谤者，恶意中伤者 | People who malign others, spreading false and damaging information about them. | The maligners spread rumors about her to damage her reputation. 那些诽谤者散布关于她的谣言，以损害她的名誉。|  The politician's maligners launched a smear campaign against him. 这位政治家的恶意中伤者对他发起了一场诽谤运动。 | synonyms: slanderers, detractors, libelers, backbiters | antonyms:  supporters, admirers, defenders | noun


malign:/məˈlaɪn/ | v. | 诽谤，污蔑，中伤 | To speak or write harmful untruths about someone or something. |  She was maligned by her rivals who were jealous of her success.  她的竞争对手嫉妒她的成功，对她进行了诽谤。| He maligned the company's products in an online review. 他在网上评论中诽谤了该公司的产品。| synonyms: defame, slander, libel, vilify, denigrate | antonyms: praise, commend, extol, laud | verb
