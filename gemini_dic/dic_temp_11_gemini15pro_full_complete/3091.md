aerophone:/ˈɛəroʊfoʊn/| n. | 气鸣乐器 |A musical instrument that produces sound by means of vibrating air.  This includes woodwinds, brass, and free reed instruments. | The clarinet is an aerophone, producing sound through the vibration of air within its cylindrical body.  单簧管是一种气鸣乐器，通过其圆柱形管体内的空气振动产生声音。|The saxophone is also an aerophone. 萨克斯也是一种气鸣乐器。| synonym:wind instrument | antonym:idiophone, membranophone | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + φωνή (phōnḗ, “sound”).
