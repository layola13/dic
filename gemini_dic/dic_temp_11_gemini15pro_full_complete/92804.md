iconicism:/aɪˌkɒnɪsɪzəm/ | n. |图像性；肖像性；图符性 |The resemblance between a sign and its object, as in the case of a photograph or a diagram.  | The iconicism of the painting, with its realistic depiction of a bowl of fruit, was striking. 这幅画的图像性十分引人注目，它逼真地描绘了一碗水果。 | resemblance, similarity, likeness | abstraction, symbolism | noun

iconicity:/aɪˌkɒnɪsɪti/ | n. |图像性；肖像性；图符性 | The property of being iconic, or resembling the thing it represents. | The iconicity of emojis makes them easily understood across cultures.  表情符号的图像性使它们易于跨文化理解。 | iconicism, resemblance, similarity | abstraction, symbolism | noun
