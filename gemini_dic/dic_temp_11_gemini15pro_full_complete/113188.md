lurg:/lɜːɡ/ | n. |  (非正式)一种不明确的疾病，通常指感冒或流感 | An undefined illness, often a cold or the flu.  | I've come down with a terrible lurg. 我得了重感冒。 |  近义词: bug, illness, sickness, ailment | 反义词: health, wellness | 词性: 名词

词源:  The origin of "lurg" is uncertain. It's thought to be imitative, possibly echoing the sound of coughing or sneezing, or related to the word "lurgy," which has similar meanings. It first appeared in print in the mid-20th century.
