greensward:/ˈɡriːnzˌwɔːrd/ /ˈɡriːnˌswɔːrd/
greensward|n.|绿茵；草地；草坪|A stretch of green, grassy land; a lawn or turf.|The picnickers spread their blanket on the greensward. 野餐的人在草地上铺开了毯子。|近义词：lawn, turf, grass, meadow|反义词:barren, wasteland|词性:名词
greensward| | |From Middle English *grenswharde, from Old English grēneswearde (“greensward”), equivalent to green + sward.| | | |
