assuetude:/əˈswiːtjuːd/ /əˈsuːɪtuːd/
|n.|习惯，习性；长期从事|Custom; habitual practice, especially of something difficult or requiring discipline.|Years of assuetude to a harsh climate had hardened the men. 多年的对恶劣气候的适应使这些人变得坚强。|近义词: custom, habit, practice, habituation, acclimation|反义词: unfamiliarity, inexperience|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *assuetudo*, from Latin assuetus (“accustomed”), past participle of assuescere, from ad- (“to”) + suescere (“to accustom, to become used to”).
