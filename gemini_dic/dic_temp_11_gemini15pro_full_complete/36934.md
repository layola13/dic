colostrum:/kəˈlɑːstrəm/| n.|初乳|The first milk produced by a mammal after giving birth, rich in antibodies and nutrients.|例句：Colostrum is crucial for the newborn's immune system. 初乳对于新生儿的免疫系统至关重要。

例句：The farmer collected the colostrum from the cow immediately after the calf was born.  农夫在小牛出生后立即收集了母牛的初乳。|近义词：first milk, beestings|反义词:None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *colostrum*, from colō ("I cultivate, inhabit, till, tend, cherish, foster, nurse, support") (ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“to cover, conceal, save”)).
