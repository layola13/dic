fuga:/ˈfjuːɡə/ /ˈfuːɡɑː/ | n. |赋格曲; 遁走; |A musical form in which a theme or subject is introduced by one voice or instrument and is imitated by others, each in turn.  A flight, escape, or running away.| 例句：The composer skillfully crafted a complex fuga. 作曲家巧妙地创作了一首复杂的赋格曲。

例句：His sudden fuga left everyone bewildered. 他的突然逃跑让每个人都感到困惑。 |近义词：flight, escape, decampment (for the sense of running away) |反义词: arrival, return (for the sense of running away)|词性:noun
