gigantomachy:/ˌdʒaɪɡæntəˈmæki/| n.| 巨人之战，巨人族之战|A struggle or conflict of titans or giants; any great struggle or conflict.|The gigantomachy depicted in Greek mythology is a fierce battle between the Gods and the Giants. 希腊神话中描绘的巨人之战是众神与巨人之间的一场激战。|近义词：battle, war, struggle, conflict|反义词:peace, truce|noun
