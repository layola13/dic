abstractive:/æbˈstræktɪv/| adj.|抽象的，摘要的|Characterized by the process of abstracting; considering something theoretically or separately from something else.  Dealing with a subject in a theoretical or generalized way; not concrete.|Researchers used an abstractive summarization technique to condense the lengthy research paper into a concise abstract. 研究人员使用抽象摘要技术将冗长的研究论文浓缩成简洁的摘要。|Synonyms: theoretical, conceptual, notional, intellectual, philosophical, speculative|Antonyms: concrete, tangible, factual, literal|adjective
