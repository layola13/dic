outinvents:/ˌaʊtɪnˈvɛnts/
verb
胜过发明；在发明方面超越
To invent something superior to or more advanced than something else.
Tesla outinvented Edison in many ways. 特斯拉在很多方面都比爱迪生更有发明创造力。
surpass in inventing, outdo in invention
be outinvented by
