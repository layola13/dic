lackland:/ˈlæklənd/| adj. n.|缺少土地的；无地的；无立锥之地的；无土地者|Lacking land; landless.  A person without land.|He was a lackland peasant, forced to work for others. 他是一个无地的农民，被迫为别人工作。|The lackland farmers migrated west in search of opportunity. 无地的农民向西迁移寻找机会。|近义词：landless, dispossessed, propertyless|反义词：landed, propertied|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology:
"lack" (Old English *lacu "fault, defect," from Proto-Germanic *lakō) + "land" (Old English land "ground, soil, territory")
