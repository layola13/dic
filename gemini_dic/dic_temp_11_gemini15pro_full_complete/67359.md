extensional:/ɪkˈstɛnʃənl/
adjective
外延的；延展的
Denoting or relating to the actual, particular, or concrete objects referred to by a term, rather than the general concept or properties implied.  Focusing on the members of a set or class rather than on their common properties.
例句：An extensional definition lists all the members of the class denoted by the term.  外延定义列出了该术语所指类的所有成员。

例句：Her extensional approach to the problem focused on the specific cases rather than the abstract theory. 她用外延的方法来处理这个问题，重点关注具体案例而不是抽象理论。
近义词：denotative, explicit, specific
反义词：intensional, implicit, general
词性: adjective
