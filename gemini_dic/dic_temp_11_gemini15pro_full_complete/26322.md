buyout:/ˈbaɪaʊt/| n.|买断; 收购; 接管|The management team secured a buyout of the company. 管理团队获得了公司的收购权。|例句：The company announced a management buyout.  公司宣布了管理层收购。

例句：They offered him a buyout package to leave the company. 他们向他提供了一份收购方案，让他离开公司。|近义词：acquisition, takeover, purchase|反义词: sale|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "buy" + "out". "Buy" comes from Old English "bycgan" meaning "to buy, purchase, redeem," from Proto-Germanic "*bugjanan." "Out" comes from Old English "ūt," meaning "out, outside," from Proto-Germanic "*ut," from Proto-Indo-European "*ud-," meaning "out, up, away."
