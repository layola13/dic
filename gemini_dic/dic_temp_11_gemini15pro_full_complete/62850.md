enjoinment:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪmənt/
n.|享受，乐趣；命令，禁止|The act of enjoying something; An order or prohibition.
|例句：She found great enjoyment in reading. 她从阅读中获得了很大的乐趣。

|例句：The court's enjoinenment prevented the company from selling the product. 法院的禁令阻止了公司销售该产品。
|近义词：pleasure, delight, satisfaction, injunction, prohibition|反义词：displeasure, dissatisfaction, allowance, permission|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English enjoinement, from Old French enjoignement, from enjoindre (“to enjoin”), from Latin iniungere (“to join into, to impose upon”).  The sense of "prohibition" developed in law from the use of injunctions to command or prohibit specific actions.
