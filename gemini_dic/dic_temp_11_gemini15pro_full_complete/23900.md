breakbeat:/ˈbreɪkbiːt/| n.|碎拍;断拍|A breakbeat is a sampled drum beat, often used in hip hop, jungle, and other electronic music genres. It's characterized by its syncopated rhythm and often features a distinctive drum break or fill.|例句：The DJ's set was full of infectious breakbeats.  DJ的演出充满了感染力的碎拍。

例句：Breakbeat music originated in the 1970s. 碎拍音乐起源于20世纪70年代。|近义词：drum break, beat, rhythm|反义词:melody, harmony|词性:noun
