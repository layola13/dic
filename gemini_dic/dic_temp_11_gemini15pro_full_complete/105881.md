kitschy:/ˈkɪtʃi/| adj.|俗气的；庸俗的；媚俗的|Characterized by excessive garishness and sentimentality, typically of popular or mass-produced art or design.  Often considered in bad taste but sometimes appreciated in an ironic or knowing way.  Derives from the German word "Kitsch," meaning trash or junk.|例句：Her apartment was filled with kitschy souvenirs from her travels. 她的公寓里摆满了旅行带回来的俗气纪念品。

例句：The restaurant's decor was a bizarre mix of kitschy Americana and faux-French elegance. 这家餐厅的装饰是庸俗的美式风格和仿法式优雅的古怪组合。|近义词：tacky, gaudy, garish, cheesy, campy|反义词：tasteful, elegant, sophisticated, refined|词性:adjective
