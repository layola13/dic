atomism:/ˈætəmɪzəm/ | n. |原子论；原子主义| The theory that all of reality and all objects in the universe are composed of very small, indivisible and indestructible building blocks known as atoms, or that reality is composed of multiple discrete constituents.|  Democritus and Leucippus were the first to propose the theory of atomism. 德谟克利特和留基伯是第一个提出原子论的人。

Atomism influenced the development of modern physics. 原子论影响了现代物理学的发展。| 近义词: reductionism, | 反义词: holism | 词性: noun
