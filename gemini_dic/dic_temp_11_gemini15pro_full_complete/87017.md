hentai:/ˈhɛntaɪ/| adj. n.|变态的，性变态的；色情动漫，变态色情作品|A Japanese term for pornographic anime and manga, often involving fantastical or bizarre sexual acts. It can also be used as an adjective to describe something as perverse or sexually deviant.|例句：He's really into hentai. 他真的很喜欢色情动漫。|例句：Some people find hentai offensive. 有些人觉得色情动漫很冒犯。|近义词：pornographic, perverse, erotic|反义词：wholesome, decent|词性:adjective, noun

词源: From Japanese 変態 (hentai), literally "transformation," "metamorphosis," "abnormality," "perversion."  From 変 (hen, “change, strange”) +‎ 態 (tai, “form, appearance”).
