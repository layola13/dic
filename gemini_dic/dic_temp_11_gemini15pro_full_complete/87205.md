hereditament:/ˌhɛrɪˈdɪtəm(ə)nt/
n.
可继承财产；遗产
Property that can be inherited; any property, whether real or personal, which can be inherited.
The old manor house was a valuable hereditament passed down through generations. 这座古老的庄园是代代相传的宝贵遗产。

Hereditaments can include both corporeal and incorporeal property. 遗产可以包括有形和无形财产。

Synonyms: inheritance, legacy, patrimony
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider concepts like liability, debt)
Noun
