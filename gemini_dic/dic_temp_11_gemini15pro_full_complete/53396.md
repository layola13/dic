disallow:/ˌdɪsəˈlaʊ/
v.|不允许，不准，驳回|The judge disallowed the evidence. 法官不接受该证据。|English explanation: To refuse to allow or accept something; to reject or prohibit.|例句：The company disallowed the employee's request for a raise. 公司拒绝了员工加薪的请求。

例句：The referee disallowed the goal because of a foul.  裁判因犯规而判进球无效。
|近义词：reject, refuse, prohibit, forbid|反义词：allow, permit, accept|词性:verb
