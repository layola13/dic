capable:/ˈkeɪpəbl/ | adj. | 有能力的，能干的 | Having the skill or qualities necessary to do something. | She is a capable lawyer. 她是一位能干的律师。|  He is capable of finishing the project on time. 他有能力按时完成这个项目。 | 近义词: competent, able, skilled, proficient | 反义词: incapable, incompetent, unable, unskilled | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *capabilis*, from Latin capere "to take, seize, hold, grasp, comprehend."  The notion is of "being able to grasp or hold (mentally or physically)."
