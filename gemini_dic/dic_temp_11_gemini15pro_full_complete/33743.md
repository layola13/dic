circuitously:/sɜːrˈkjuːɪtəsli/
adverb
迂回地，绕圈子地
in a way that is not direct
He answered the question circuitously, never quite getting to the point. 他迂回地回答了这个问题，始终没有说到点子上。

The path wound circuitously through the forest. 这条小路蜿蜒曲折地穿过森林。

indirectly, tortuously, windingly
directly, straightforwardly
词性: 副词
