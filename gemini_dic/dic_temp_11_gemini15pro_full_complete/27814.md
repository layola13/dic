cannoting:/kəˈnuːɪŋ/ /kæˈnuːɪŋ/
n.
激流独木舟运动；激流皮划艇运动
The sport of travelling in a canoe, especially down fast-flowing rivers.
We went cannoting down the Zambezi River. 我们去赞比西河玩了激流独木舟。
近义词:white-water canoeing, kayaking
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "flatwater canoeing")
词性:noun
