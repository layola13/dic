airinesses:/ˈɛərɪnɪsɪz/
n.
轻盈；空灵；通风良好
The quality of being airy; light and delicate; open to the air.
The room was filled with an airiness that made her feel at peace.  房间里充满了轻盈的空气，让她感到平静。
The airinesses of the curtains gave the room a romantic feel. 窗帘的轻盈飘逸给房间增添了浪漫的气息。
lightness, delicacy, etherealness
heaviness, denseness, stuffiness
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From airy + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Airy comes from Old English *ærig, from ær "air."
