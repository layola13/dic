jagless:/ˈdʒæɡləs/
adjective
无锯齿的；光滑的
Smooth, without jags or notches.
The knife had a jagless blade. 这把刀的刀刃很光滑。
近义词:smooth, even, seamless
反义词:jagged, serrated, notched
词性:adjective

词源: "jagless" comes from the noun "jag" (a sharp, irregular projection) + the suffix "-less" (meaning "without").  The word "jag" has uncertain origins, possibly from a Scandinavian source.
