click's:/klɪks/| n.|点击次数，点击量|A click is the act of pressing a button on a mouse or other pointing device.  It often refers to the number of times a link or advertisement is clicked on a website.|The website received thousands of clicks in the first hour.  这个网站在第一个小时就收到了数千次点击。|近义词：hits, visits|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially "ignores")|noun
