flaxiest:/ˈflæksiɪst/| adj.|最柔软的，最易弯曲的|Superlative form of "flaxy," meaning resembling flax in its flexibility.  It describes something that bends or sways very easily.|The flaxiest willow branches dipped low over the stream.  最柔软的柳树枝低垂在溪流上。|The gymnast was incredibly flaxiest.  这个体操运动员非常灵活。|近义词：supplest, most pliable, most flexible, limpest|反义词:stiffest, rigidest, firmest|词性:形容词
词源: From flax + -y + -est.  Flax refers to a plant with long, flexible fibers used for making linen. The suffix "-y" forms adjectives, and "-est" creates the superlative form.
