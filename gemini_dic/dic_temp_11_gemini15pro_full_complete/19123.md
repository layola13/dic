betwixt:/bɪˈtwɪkst/| prep. adv.|在…之间；介于…之间|Between.  It's an older, more literary word.|The house is betwixt the river and the road. 这房子位于河流和道路之间。|近义词：between, amid, amongst|反义词:outside, beyond|词性:介词, 副词
词源:From Old English *betweox, from *be "by" + *tweon "two".  Related to "tween."
