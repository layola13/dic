ignoblest:/ɪɡˈnoʊblɪst/
adjective
最卑鄙的，最不光彩的
most base or dishonorable; mean
例句：He committed the ignoblest act of betrayal. 他犯下了最卑鄙的背叛行为。

例句：The ignoblest motives drove him to commit the crime. 最卑劣的动机驱使他犯下了罪行。

近义词：basest, meanest, vilest, lowest, most contemptible
反义词：noblest, highest, most honorable
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *ignobilis* (not noble), from *in-* (not) + *nobilis* (noble).
