domeykite:/ˈdoʊmaɪkaɪt/| n. | 多迈克矿 |A silver-white to steel-gray mineral, a copper arsenide (Cu3As) that is an important ore of copper. | Domeykite is a valuable ore mineral found in copper deposits. 多迈克矿是一种在铜矿床中发现的有价值的矿石矿物。| Domeykite can tarnish to a yellowish or iridescent hue upon exposure to air. 多迈克矿暴露在空气中会变成淡黄色或彩虹色。|近义词: algodonite, whitneyite (other copper arsenides) |反义词: N/A (Minerals don't typically have antonyms) | 词性: noun
