disoblige:/ˌdɪsəˈblaɪdʒ/ | vt. | 怠慢，使不快，拒绝给予…的帮助 | to refuse to do something that someone has asked you to do, especially when this causes problems for them | I was sorry to disoblige him, but I couldn't agree to his request. 我很抱歉怠慢了他，但我不能同意他的要求。 | Synonyms: offend, slight, snub, refuse, decline | Antonyms: oblige, accommodate, assist | verb

disoblige:/ˌdɪsəˈblaɪdʒ/ | vt. | 违背（某人的意愿）| to go against someone's wishes. | He disobliged his family by refusing to go to college. 他违背了家人的意愿，拒绝上大学。| Synonyms: defy, disobey, contradict | Antonyms: obey, comply, conform | verb
