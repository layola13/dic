offer:/ˈɒfə(r)/ (British), /ˈɔːfər/ (American)|v.|提供；提议；出价|She offered to help me with my homework. 她主动提出帮我做作业。|propose, suggest, tender|refuse, decline, reject|verb

Etymology: From Middle English *offren, *offrien, from Old French offrir, from Latin offerre (“to offer, present, furnish”), from ob- (“towards”) + ferre (“to bear, carry”).
