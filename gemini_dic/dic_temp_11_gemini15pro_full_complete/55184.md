dissentation:/ˌdɪsəˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 论文；异议；争论 | A long piece of writing on a particular subject, especially one that is done for a higher university degree;  the expression of disagreement with a statement or opinion.|  Example: She is writing her dissertation on the impact of social media on political discourse. 她正在撰写关于社交媒体对政治话语影响的论文。| Synonyms: thesis, treatise, discourse, argument | Antonyms: agreement, concord, harmony | noun

dissentation:/ˌdɪsəˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 争论，意见分歧 | The holding or expression of opinions different from those commonly or officially held. | Example: There was much dissent among the faculty about the new curriculum.  教员们对新课程有很多不同意见。 | Synonyms: disagreement, difference of opinion, discord, disunity, | Antonyms: agreement, concord, harmony | noun
