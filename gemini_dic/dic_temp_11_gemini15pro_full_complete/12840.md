associationist:/əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃənɪst/| n. adj.|联想主义者，联想主义的|A believer in or advocate of associationism (the theory that mental processes, especially learning, are based on the association of ideas).|例句：The associationist believed that all knowledge is acquired through experience.  这位联想主义者相信所有知识都是通过经验获得的。

例句：Associationist psychology dominated the 19th century. 联想主义心理学在19世纪占据主导地位。|近义词：empiricist, behaviorist|反义词：nativist, rationalist|词性: noun, adjective

Etymology:  From association + -ist.  "Association" ultimately comes from the Latin "associare," meaning "to join to, unite, attach."
