bait:/beɪt/| n. vt.|诱饵，饵；引诱|Something used to attract and catch something else, often an animal or fish.  It can also be used figuratively to refer to something used to entice someone into a trap or trick.|The fisherman used worms as bait. 渔夫用蠕虫作诱饵。|Synonyms: lure, decoy, enticement|Antonyms: deterrent, repellent|noun
bait:/beɪt/| vt.|放饵于；折磨；激怒|To put bait on a hook or trap; to torment or tease someone.|He baited the hook with a worm. 他用蠕虫作诱饵。|Synonyms: lure, entice, taunt, provoke|Antonyms: soothe, placate|verb
