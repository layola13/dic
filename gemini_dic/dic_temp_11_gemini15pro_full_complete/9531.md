antisemitic:/ˌæntiːsəˈmɪtɪk/
adjective
反犹太主义的；反犹太人的
Hostile to or prejudiced against Jews.
Her comments were seen as antisemitic. 她的评论被视为反犹太主义。
anti-Jewish, anti-Semitic
philosemitic, pro-Semitic
adjective

antisemitism:/ˌæntiˈsɛmɪtɪzəm/
noun
反犹太主义
Hostility to or prejudice against Jews.
Antisemitism is on the rise in Europe. 欧洲的反犹太主义正在抬头。
anti-Jewishness, anti-Semitism
philosemitism
noun
