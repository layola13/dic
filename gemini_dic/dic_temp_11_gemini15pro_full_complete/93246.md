iive: /aɪv/
adjective
活的; 生动的; 实况转播的; 充满活力的
alive; living; not dead.  A live broadcast.  A lively and vibrant performance.
The band played a live concert.  乐队举办了一场现场音乐会。
We watched a live broadcast of the football game. 我们观看了这场足球比赛的现场直播。
近义词: living, alive, vital, vibrant
反义词: dead, inanimate, dull
词性: adjective

iive: /aɪv/
verb (非标准用法, likely a misspelling of "live")
居住; 生存; 过着…的生活
To reside in a place; to experience life in a certain way.  (This usage is non-standard and likely a misspelling.  The correct spelling is "live".)
He iives in a small town. (incorrect spelling) 他住在一个小镇上。(拼写错误)
They iive a simple life. (incorrect spelling) 他们过着简朴的生活。(拼写错误)
近义词: reside, dwell, inhabit, exist
反义词: die, perish
词性: verb (non-standard)

词源:  Likely a misspelling of "live," which comes from Old English *libban, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch leven and German leben.
