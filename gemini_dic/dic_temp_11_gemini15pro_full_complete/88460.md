histohaematin:/ˌhɪstoʊˈhiːmətɪn/ | n. |细胞色素| A respiratory pigment found in the cells of many organisms, now known as cytochrome.  It plays a crucial role in cellular respiration. |  Histohaematin was originally thought to be a single substance but was later found to be a complex of cytochromes. 细胞色素最初被认为是一种单一物质，但后来发现它是一种细胞色素的复合物。| Synonym: cytochrome | Antonym: N/A | noun
