joyous:/ˈdʒɔɪəs/| adj.|快乐的；高兴的；充满喜悦的|Full of joy; very happy.|例句：The atmosphere was joyous and festive. 气氛欢乐而喜庆。|例句：They celebrated their victory with joyous abandon. 他们尽情地庆祝胜利。|近义词：happy, joyful, cheerful, merry, blithe|反义词：sad, sorrowful, unhappy, miserable|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *joious, from Old French joius, from Late Latin gaudiosus (“joyful”), from Latin gaudium (“joy”), from gaudere (“to rejoice”).
