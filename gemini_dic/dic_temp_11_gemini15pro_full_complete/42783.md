couped:/kuːpt/
adjective
被切断的，截断的
Cut off squarely or abruptly; severed.  Used in heraldry to describe a part of a charge represented as cut off cleanly.
The lion rampant couped at the shoulders was the symbol of the family.  这只肩膀被切断的跃立狮子是这个家族的象征。
Near Synonyms:  truncated, severed, cut off
Antonyms:  whole, complete, intact
词性: 形容词
