apology:/əˈpɒlədʒi/ (UK), /əˈpɑːlədʒi/ (US)| n.|道歉，歉意|An expression of regret for having caused someone pain or offense. |例句：He offered a sincere apology for his behavior. 他为自己的行为真诚地道歉。

例句：She accepted his apology and they made up. 她接受了他的道歉，他们和好了。

近义词：excuse, pardon, amends|反义词：offense, insult|词性：noun
