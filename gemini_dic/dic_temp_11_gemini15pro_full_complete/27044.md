calendarer:/ˈkælɪndərər/| n.| 轧光机操作工，整理工|A worker who operates a calender, a machine used to smooth and finish cloth, paper, or other materials.|The calendarer carefully adjusted the rollers to achieve the desired finish on the fabric.|近义词：finisher, operator|反义词:|noun

calendarer:/ˈkælɪndərər/| n.| 轧光机|A machine used to smooth and finish cloth, paper, or other materials by pressing them between rollers.|The factory invested in a new calendarer to improve the quality of its paper products.|近义词：calender, rolling machine|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From calender (v.) + -er (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Latin cylindrus "cylinder."
