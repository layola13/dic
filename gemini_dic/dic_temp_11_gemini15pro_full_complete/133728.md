nonfugitively:/ˌnɒnˈfjuːdʒɪtɪvli/
adverb
非逃亡地；非短暂地
In a manner that is not fleeting or temporary; permanently; in a way that does not evade capture or avoid detection.
The suspect was apprehended nonfugitively, remaining in his residence until the police arrived.  嫌疑犯被非逃亡地逮捕，一直待在他的住所直到警察到来。
He addressed the issue nonfugitively, providing a detailed and permanent solution. 他非短暂地解决了这个问题，提供了一个详细而永久的解决方案。
近义词: permanently, enduringly, lastingly
反义词: fugitively, transiently, temporarily
词性: adverb
