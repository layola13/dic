meagerly:/ˈmiːɡərli/ | adv. | 贫乏地，不足地，少量地 | In a way that is small in quantity and poor in quality; sparsely. |  The food was meagerly portioned. 食物份量很少。| Synonyms:  scantily, sparingly, sparsely, thinly, inadequately | Antonyms:  abundantly, amply, copiously, generously, lavishly | 词性: 副词
