nonacoustic:/ˌnɒnəˈkuːstɪk/
adjective
非声学的；非原声的
Not producing or involving sound waves, especially not used in or produced by the transmission of sound waves.
The electric guitar relies on nonacoustic means to amplify its sound. 电吉它依靠非原声方式放大声音。
近义词: electronic, electric
反义词: acoustic
词性: adjective
