confiners:/kənˈfaɪnərz/
n.
限制者；约束者
People or things that confine or restrict something or someone.
The confiners of the prisoners were strict and unforgiving.  囚犯的限制者非常严格，毫不留情。
High walls acted as confiners, preventing the animals from escaping. 高墙充当了限制物，防止动物逃跑。

近义词: restrictors, limiters, jailers, guardians
反义词: liberators, emancipators
词性: 名词 (plural)
