covetingly: /ˈkʌvɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 贪婪地，渴望地 | In a manner that expresses a strong desire to possess something belonging to another. | She looked covetingly at her friend's new car. 她贪婪地看着她朋友的新车。 | Examples:
He stared covetingly at the expensive watch in the shop window.  他贪婪地盯着商店橱窗里那块昂贵的手表。

She eyed the diamond necklace covetingly. 她渴望地注视着那条钻石项链。


| 近义词：enviously, jealously, greedily, longingly | 反义词：generously, disinterestedly | 词性：副词
