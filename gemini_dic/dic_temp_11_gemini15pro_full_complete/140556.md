solid:/ˈsɑːlɪd/ /ˈsɒlɪd/| adj. n.|固体的；坚固的；可靠的；实心的；立体的；纯的；连续的；一致的；结实的；【名】固体；立体图形|A solid is something that is firm and hard and keeps its shape. solid:/ˈsɑːlɪd/ /ˈsɒlɪd/| n.|固体；立体图形|A solid is a substance or object that is firm and stable in shape; not liquid or fluid.|Ice is water in its solid state. 冰是水的固态。|近义词:object, substance|反义词:liquid, gas|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
