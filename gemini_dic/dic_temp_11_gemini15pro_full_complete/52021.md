diaheliotropic:/ˌdaɪəˌhiːliəˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 向日性的，背日性的 |  Turning or orienting away from the sun.  It describes the movement of a plant or plant part in relation to the sun, specifically moving *away* from it.  Contrast with "heliotropic" which describes movement *towards* the sun. | The leaves of some desert plants are diaheliotropic to minimize water loss from excessive sun exposure.  沙漠中一些植物的叶子具有背日性，以最大限度地减少因过度日晒而造成的水分流失。|  apheliotropic |  heliotropic | adjective
