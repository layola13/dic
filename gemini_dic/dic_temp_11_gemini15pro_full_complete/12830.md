associated: /əˈsoʊʃieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 关联的，相关的；联合的；（与不愉快事物）有关联的 | connected with something else; joined with another person or group; involving something bad or unpleasant |  He is associated with several charities. 他与几个慈善机构有关联。| synonyms: connected, related, linked, affiliated, allied | antonyms: unconnected, unrelated, separate, independent | adjective

associated: /əˈsoʊsieɪt/ | v. | 联系，关联；交往，结交 | to connect someone or something in your mind with someone or something else; to spend time with someone, especially someone that other people do not approve of | I don't want to be associated with drugs. 我不想和毒品沾边。| synonyms: connect, relate, link, combine, join | antonyms: separate, disconnect, disassociate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *associātus*, past participle of *associāre*  "to join, unite," from *ad-* "to" + *socius* "companion, ally."
