ooelogical:/ˌoʊəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 卵石学的，与卵石研究相关的 | Relating to the study of bird eggs, their formation, and their nests. |  Birdwatchers often use oological data to identify different species.  鸟类观察者经常使用卵石学数据来识别不同的物种。 |  Example: The museum's oological collection contains eggs from all over the world.  博物馆的卵石收藏包含来自世界各地的鸟蛋。| Near Synonyms: ornithological | Antonyms: N/A | Word Type: Adjective
