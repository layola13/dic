meddler: /ˈmedlər/ (US), /ˈmedlə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 爱管闲事的人，干预者 | A person who interferes in other people's affairs without being asked or needed. | She's such a meddler; she always has to stick her nose in everyone's business. 她真是个爱管闲事的人；她总是把鼻子伸到每个人的事情里。

She earned a reputation as a meddler by offering unsolicited advice to her colleagues. 她因为向同事提供不请自来的建议而赢得了爱管闲事的名声。


Synonyms: busybody, interferer, intruder, interloper
Antonyms:  non-interventionalist, bystander

词性: noun
