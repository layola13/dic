flopped:/flɑːpt/| v.|失败，砸锅；扑通倒下；(因精疲力竭而)重重地坐下(或躺下)|To fall, collapse, or land heavily or clumsily; to be completely unsuccessful; to fail.|The play flopped on Broadway.  这出戏在百老汇演出失败了。| The exhausted runner flopped onto the grass.  精疲力竭的跑步者扑通一声倒在草地上。|近义词：failed, bombed, collapsed, slumped|反义词:succeeded, thrived|词性:动词

词源:  From the imitative verb *flop*, meaning "to fall or strike with a flapping sound".

flop down = 重重地坐下或躺下

flop over = 翻倒; 倾覆

a complete flop = 完全的失败
