lignitiferous:/lɪɡˈnɪtɪfərəs/
adj.
含褐煤的；产褐煤的
Containing or yielding lignite; bearing lignite.
The lignitiferous strata provided a valuable source of fuel. 这些褐煤地层提供了宝贵的燃料来源。
The expedition discovered a lignitiferous deposit in the valley. 探险队在山谷中发现了一处褐煤矿床。
lignite-bearing, coal-bearing
N/A
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin lignum (wood) + -i- + -fer (bearing) + -ous (having the quality of).  Essentially meaning "wood-bearing," but specifically referring to the type of wood that has become lignite.
