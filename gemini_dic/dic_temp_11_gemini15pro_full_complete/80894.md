governmentalizing: /ˌɡʌvərnˈmɛntəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使政府化；使官僚化
The process of making something more governmental, often involving increased bureaucracy, regulation, and control by the state.
The opposition party accused the ruling party of governmentalizing the healthcare system. 反对党指责执政党将医疗保健系统官僚化。
The increasing governmentalizing of education has led to a decline in innovation. 教育日益官僚化导致创新下降。
bureaucratizing, nationalizing
privatizing
