erects:/ɪˈrɛkts/| v.| 建立，竖立，使直立| builds, constructs, sets up something vertically. |The construction crew erects the skyscraper in record time. 建筑工人以创纪录的速度建造了这座摩天大楼。| The soldier erects a makeshift flagpole. 士兵竖起了一个临时旗杆。| synonyms: build, construct, raise, establish, assemble | antonyms: demolish, destroy, dismantle, tear down | verb

erect:/ɪˈrɛkt/| adj.| 直立的，垂直的，竖起的 | upright, vertical, not leaning or slouching. |He stood erect, shoulders back, and head held high. 他站得笔直，肩膀向后，头高高抬起。| The cat's ears were erect, alert to every sound. 猫的耳朵竖了起来，对每一个声音都保持警惕。| synonyms: upright, vertical, straight, perpendicular | antonyms: drooping, slouching, horizontal, leaning | adjective
