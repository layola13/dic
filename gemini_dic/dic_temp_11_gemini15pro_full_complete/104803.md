KDKA:/ˌkeɪdiːˈkeɪˈeɪ/ | n. | KDKA (a radio station) | KDKA is a Class A clear-channel AM radio station licensed to Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. It is owned by Audacy, Inc. and broadcasts on 1020 kHz with a power of 50,000 watts. | KDKA is known as the world's first commercial radio station. KDKA是世界上第一个商业广播电台。 |  Near Synonyms: radio station, broadcaster | Antonyms: N/A | noun
