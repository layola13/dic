arithmometer:/ˌærɪθˈmɒmɪtər/ (US: /ˌærɪθˈmɑːmɪtər/) | n. | 算术机，机械计算器 | A mechanical calculating device that could perform basic arithmetic operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. |  Charles Babbage's Difference Engine, a precursor to the computer, could be considered a complex arithmometer.  查尔斯·巴贝奇的差分机，计算机的先驱，可以被认为是一种复杂的算术机。

The arithmometer was a crucial tool for businesses and scientists before the advent of electronic calculators. 在电子计算器出现之前，算术机是企业和科学家的重要工具。

近义词: calculator, calculating machine | 反义词: abacus | 词性: noun
