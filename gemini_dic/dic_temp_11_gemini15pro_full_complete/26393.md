by-the-bye:/ˌbaɪ ðə ˈbaɪ/
| adv.|顺便提一句，附带说说|Used to introduce a remark or point not directly connected with what has just been said.  It is a somewhat archaic or literary term. |By the bye, have you seen my keys?  顺便问一下，你看到我的钥匙了吗？|近义词：incidentally, by the way|反义词:purposely, intentionally
|词性:副词

by-the-bye:/ˌbaɪ ðə ˈbaɪ/
| n.|An incidental remark; a digression.|A casual aside in conversation.|He added, by the bye, that he would be late. 他顺便说一句，他会迟到。|近义词：aside, digression|反义词:main point, central theme
|词性:名词

PHRASE->
Not commonly used in phrases. Its standalone adverbial/noun form is the primary usage.  However, one could contrive examples like:

"Oh, by the bye, what's up?" (combining with another phrase) 哦，顺便问一下，怎么了？
"That's interesting, by the bye." (used for emphasis)  那很有趣，顺便说一句.
"Just a thought, by the bye." (introducing a thought)  只是一个想法，顺便一提。
"Let me add this, by the bye." (introducing an addition)  让我补充一下，顺便说一句。
"By the bye, what about dinner?" (transitioning to a new topic)  顺便问一下，晚饭怎么样？


词源： From Middle English *bi the weie (“by the way”), equivalent to by +‎ the +‎ way.  Over time, it evolved to "by the bye."
