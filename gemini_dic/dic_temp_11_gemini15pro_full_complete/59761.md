economised:/iˈkɒnəmaɪzd/ (UK) /iˈkɑːnəmaɪzd/ (US)| v. | 节约，节省 |  To avoid waste or extravagance; to use sparingly or frugally. | They economised on fuel by travelling by bus. 他们为了省油选择乘坐巴士出行。|  We had to economise on heating because of the high cost of energy. 由于能源成本高昂，我们不得不节省暖气。|  save, conserve, cut back, retrench |  waste, squander, splurge | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *économiser*, from *économie* (“economy”) + *-iser* (suffix forming verbs).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek *οἰκονομία* (oikonomía, “household management”).
