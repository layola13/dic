logarithm:/ˈlɔːɡərɪðəm/ /ˈlɑːɡərɪðəm/| n. |对数|A quantity representing the power to which a fixed number (the base) must be raised to produce a given number.|The logarithm of 100 to the base 10 is 2.  100以10为底的对数是2。|近义词: exponent, power |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *logarithmus, from Ancient Greek λόγος (lógos, “word, reason, ratio”) + ἀριθμός (arithmós, “number”).
