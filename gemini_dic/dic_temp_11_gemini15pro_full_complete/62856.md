enjoyably:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪəbli/| adv.|愉快地，享受地|In a way that gives pleasure or enjoyment. |We spent the evening enjoyably with friends. 我们和朋友们愉快地度过了一个晚上。|She sang enjoyably at the concert. 她在音乐会上唱得很尽兴。|近义词：pleasantly, delightfully, happily|反义词：unpleasantly, miserably, drearily|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From enjoyable + -ly. Enjoyable is from enjoy + -able. Enjoy comes from Old French enjoir, from en- "in" + joir "to rejoice," from Latin gaudere.
