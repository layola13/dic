eliminates:/ɪˈlɪmɪneɪts/| v. |消除，排除，淘汰|removes or gets rid of something completely.  The new policy eliminates the need for paperwork. 新政策消除了对文书工作的需求。|例句：The company eliminated several positions to save money. 公司为了省钱裁撤了几个职位。

例句：She eliminated dairy from her diet. 她从饮食中去除了乳制品。


近义词：remove, eradicate, abolish, get rid of|反义词:create, add, introduce, keep|词性:动词
