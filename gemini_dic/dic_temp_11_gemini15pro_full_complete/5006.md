all-americas:/ˌɔːl əˈmɛrɪkəz/
| adj. |全美明星的；全美洲的|A team of outstanding athletes chosen as the best in their sport in a particular year, typically in college or high school sports in the US.|例句：He was named to the all-Americas team. 他被选入全美明星队。|例句：She's an all-Americas basketball player. 她是一位全美明星篮球运动员。|近义词：all-star, top-ranked|反义词:none|词性:adjective
词源: all + Americas,  'all' meaning encompassing the entire group, and 'Americas' referring to North and South America, or specifically the US depending on context.  Originally used to denote a pan-American ideal, now typically used in a sporting context within the US.
