cylindroid:/ˈsɪlɪndrɔɪd/
noun
圆柱体；类圆柱体
a surface or solid resembling a cylinder.
The artist sculpted a cylindroid figure from clay.  艺术家用粘土雕刻了一个类圆柱体的人物。
The engine part was roughly cylindroid in shape.  这个引擎部件的形状大致呈圆柱形。
Synonyms: cylindrical, columnar
Antonyms: spherical, cubical
