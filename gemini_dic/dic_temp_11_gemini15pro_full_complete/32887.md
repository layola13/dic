chondroganoidea:/ˌkɒndrəɡeɪnɔɪdiə/
n.
软骨硬鳞鱼类
a group of extinct fishes characterized by a cartilaginous skeleton and ganoid scales
The Chondroganoidea flourished in the Devonian period. 软骨硬鳞鱼类在泥盆纪时期繁盛。

英文解析：An extinct group of primitive fishes, typically from the Devonian period, characterized by a cartilaginous skeleton and ganoid scales, a type of thick, shiny scale.
