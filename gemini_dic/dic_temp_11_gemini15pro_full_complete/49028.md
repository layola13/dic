degreaser:/diːˈɡriːzər/
noun
除油剂，去油剂
A substance used to remove grease.
Use a degreaser to clean the oven. 用除油剂清洁烤箱。
近义词：cleaner, solvent, detergent
反义词:  N/A
词性: noun

degrease:/diːˈɡriːs/
verb
去除油脂
To remove grease from something.
Degrease the engine before repainting it.  给发动机除油后再重新喷漆。
近义词：clean, scour
反义词: grease, oil
词性: verb
