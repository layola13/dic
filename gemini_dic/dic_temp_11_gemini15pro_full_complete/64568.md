errorlessly:/ˈɛrərˌlɛsli/ | adv. | 无误地，完美地 | Without any errors; perfectly. | She performed the piece errorlessly, not missing a single note. 她完美地演奏了这首曲子，一个音符都没错。|  Example 2: He completed the complex task errorlessly, impressing his superiors. 他完美地完成了这项复杂的任务，给他的上司留下了深刻的印象。 | 近义词: flawlessly, impeccably, perfectly | 反义词: faultily, imperfectly, erroneously | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "error" (from Old French "errer," meaning "to wander, stray," ultimately from Latin "errare") + "-less" (meaning "without") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
