entodermal:/ˌɛntəˈdɜːrməl/ | adj. | 内胚层的 | Of or relating to the entoderm (the innermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo). | The entodermal cells differentiate into the lining of the digestive tract and its associated organs. 内胚层细胞分化成消化道及其相关器官的内壁。|  The respiratory system develops from an entodermal outpocketing of the foregut. 呼吸系统由前肠的内胚层向外突出而发育而来。 | Synonyms: endodermal, hypoblastic | Antonyms: ectodermal, mesodermal | adjective
