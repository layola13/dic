manequin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/ /ˈmænəˌkɪn/ | n. |人体模型；时装模特儿|A life-sized dummy used to display clothes.  A person who models clothes. | The dress looked stunning on the mannequin. 这条裙子在人体模型上看起来美极了。

The mannequin stood perfectly still, showcasing the designer's latest creation.  时装模特儿一动不动地站着，展示着设计师的最新作品。 | 近义词:dummy, model, lay figure| 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "real person" in certain contexts) | 词性:noun
