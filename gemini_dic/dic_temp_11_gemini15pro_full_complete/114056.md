magnetisms: /ˈmæɡnɪtɪzəmz/
| n. |磁性，吸引力；磁学|The magnetism of the Earth protects us from harmful solar radiation.  地球的磁性保护我们免受有害的太阳辐射。| Magnetism is the force exerted by magnets when they attract or repel each other. 磁性是磁铁相互吸引或排斥时施加的力。| 近义词：attraction, allure, pull, charm | 反义词：repulsion | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *magnetismus, from Latin magnes (“magnet, lodestone”) + -ismus.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek μαγνῆτις λίθος (magnētis lithos, “Magnesian stone”). Magnesia was a region in ancient Thessaly, Greece.
