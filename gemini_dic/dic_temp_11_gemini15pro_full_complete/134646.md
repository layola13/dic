nonmalignant:/ˌnɒn.məˈlɪɡ.nənt/
adjective
非恶性的
Not cancerous; benign.
The doctor assured her that the lump was nonmalignant. 医生向她保证肿块是良性的。
近义词:benign, harmless, innocent
反义词:malignant, cancerous
词性:adjective
