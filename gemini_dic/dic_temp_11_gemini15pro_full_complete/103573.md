It often carries a negative connotation, implying that the person is trying to quickly gain recognition or benefit from something that others have worked on for longer.|  Those johnnies-come-lately think they can just waltz in and take over. 这些新来的人认为他们可以轻而易举地进来接管一切。| Synonyms: newcomer, novice, upstart, latecomer | Antonyms: veteran, old-timer, expert | noun
