gigantism: /ˈdʒaɪɡənˌtɪzəm/ | n. |巨大；庞大 | The sheer gigantism of the project overwhelmed the team.  项目的巨大规模让团队不堪重负。| synonyms: immensity, vastness | antonyms: smallness, tininess | noun
