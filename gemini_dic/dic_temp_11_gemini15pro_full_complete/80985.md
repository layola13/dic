gradation:/ɡrəˈdeɪʃn/ | n. | 渐变；等级；层次；阶段 | a series of gradual changes, or a stage in this series.  A process taking place gradually, in stages; the passing of one tint or shade of colour to another. | The artist used subtle gradations of color in the painting. 艺术家在画作中使用了微妙的色彩渐变。|  The report detailed the gradations of rank within the company. 报告详细说明了公司内部的等级层次。 | synonyms: progression, sequence, scale, series, spectrum | antonyms: abruptness, suddenness | noun
