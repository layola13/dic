ascendent:/əˈsɛndənt/| adj. n.|上升的，占优势的；优势，支配地位|Rising, increasing in power or influence; a position of dominance or controlling influence.|例句：His ascendent star in the political arena is undeniable. 他在政治舞台上日益上升的影响力是不可否认的。|例句：The company is in the ascendent, gaining market share rapidly. 这家公司正处于上升期，市场份额迅速增长。|近义词：rising, dominant, prevailing, increasing|反义词：declining, waning, descending, subordinate|词性:形容词,名词
ascendant:/əˈsɛndənt/| n.| 占星学中的上升星座|The zodiacal sign rising above the eastern horizon at the time of someone's birth.|例句：Her ascendant is in Leo, which explains her fiery personality. 她的上升星座是狮子座，这解释了她火热的性格。|例句：Knowing your ascendant can provide insights into your outward personality. 了解你的上升星座可以让你洞察你的外在性格。|近义词：rising sign|反义词：descendant|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *ascendent*, from Latin *ascendēns*, present participle of *ascendō* (“I climb, mount, ascend”), from *ad* (“to, towards”) + *scandō* (“I climb”).
