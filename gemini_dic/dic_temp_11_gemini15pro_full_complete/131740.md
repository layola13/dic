nonconfiscations:/ˌnɑːnkɑːnfɪˈskeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非没收，不没收 | The government's policy of nonconfiscations reassured investors. |  The treaty guaranteed the nonconfiscations of foreign investments. | 近义词: non-seizure, non-expropriation | 反义词: confiscations, seizures, expropriations | 词性: 名词


Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
confiscation (noun): from Late Latin confiscationem (nominative confiscatio),  from confiscare "to confiscate," from com- "together" + fiscus "imperial treasury"
