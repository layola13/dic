alutaceous: /ˌæljuˈteɪʃəs/
adjective
泥土色的；似泥土的；粘土状的
Of the color of, resembling, or containing clay.
The alutaceous soil was difficult to dig.  泥土色的土壤很难挖。
The archaeologist carefully examined the alutaceous fragments of pottery. 考古学家仔细检查了陶器的粘土状碎片。
近义词: clayey, earthy, earthen
反义词:  None (Lack of a direct antonym for a color/texture descriptor)
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *alutaceus*, from *aluta* "soft leather," perhaps because of the smooth texture of worked clay.
