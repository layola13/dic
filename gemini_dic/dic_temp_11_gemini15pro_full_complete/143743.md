outmaneuver:/ˌaʊtməˈnuːvər/ | /ˌaʊtməˈnuːvər/ | v. | 智胜，以策略取胜 | To defeat or gain an advantage over someone by being more clever or skillful in your plans or actions. |  The company outmaneuvered its competitors by releasing a new product first. 这家公司通过率先发布新产品战胜了竞争对手。

The general outmaneuvered the enemy and forced them to retreat. 将军以计谋战胜了敌人，迫使他们撤退。 | 近义词：outwit, outsmart, surpass, circumvent | 反义词：be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted | 动词
