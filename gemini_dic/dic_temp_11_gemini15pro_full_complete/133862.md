nongratifications:/ˌnɒnˌɡrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
lack of gratifications; absence of rewards or pleasure.
中文翻译：缺乏满足感；没有奖励或乐趣。
英文解析：The state of not being gratified; lack of satisfaction or pleasure.  It refers to the absence of things that typically bring pleasure, satisfaction, or a sense of reward.
例句：The nongratifications of the job led to high employee turnover.  这份工作缺乏满足感导致员工流动率很高。

例句：He experienced a deep sense of nongratifications after the project failed. 项目失败后，他感到深深的挫败感。
近义词：disappointments, frustrations, dissatisfaction, discouragement
反义词：gratifications, satisfaction, fulfillment, pleasure
词性：noun
