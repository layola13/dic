misadaptations:/ˌmɪsædæpˈteɪʃənz/| n. | 适应不良，不适应|Traits or behaviors that were once adaptive but have become disadvantageous in a changed environment. |The polar bear's thick fur, once an adaptation to icy conditions, is becoming a misadaptation in a warming Arctic. 北极熊厚厚的皮毛，曾经是对冰雪环境的适应，在变暖的北极正成为一种适应不良。

The company's rigid hierarchical structure, once a source of strength, is now a misadaptation in a rapidly changing market.  该公司僵化的等级结构，曾经是力量的源泉，现在在快速变化的市场中是一种适应不良。|近义词: maladaptations,  inaptitudes |反义词: adaptations |词性: noun
