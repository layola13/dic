look-for:/lʊk fɔːr/ | phrasal verb | 寻找，寻求 | To try to find something or someone. | I'm looking for my keys. 我正在找我的钥匙。

|近义词：search for, seek, hunt for |反义词: ignore, neglect |词性:动词短语

look-for:/lʊk fɔːr/ | phrasal verb | 期待，盼望(较不常见) | To expect something to happen. | We're looking for a positive outcome. 我们期待一个积极的结果。

|近义词：expect, anticipate |反义词: dread |词性:动词短语
