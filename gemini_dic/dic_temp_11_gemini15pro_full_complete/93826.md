immigratory:/ˈɪmɪɡrətɔːri/
adjective
移民的；迁徙的
Relating to immigration; tending to immigrate.
The government implemented new immigration policies affecting migratory birds. 政府实施了新的移民政策，影响了候鸟。
Migratory birds fly south for the winter. 候鸟飞往南方过冬。
synonyms: migrant, mobile, wandering, nomadic
antonyms: settled, resident, native, indigenous
词性: adjective
