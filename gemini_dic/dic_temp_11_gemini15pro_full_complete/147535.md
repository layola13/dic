overpatriotically: /ˌoʊvərˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪkli/ /ˌəʊvəˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪkli/
| adv. | 过度爱国地 | Excessively patriotic; with too much patriotism. |  He spoke overpatriotically about the nation's history, ignoring its flaws. 他过度爱国地谈论国家的历史，忽略了它的缺点。| Synonyms: excessively patriotic, ultranationalistically, jingoistically | Antonyms: unpatriotically, disloyally | 词性: 副词
