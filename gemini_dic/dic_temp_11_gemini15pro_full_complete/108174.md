latte:/ˈlɑːteɪ/ (US), /ˈlɑːteɪ/ (UK)| n.|拿铁咖啡|A coffee beverage made with espresso and steamed milk, often topped with a thin layer of foam. It's derived from the Italian "caffè latte" which literally means "coffee milk".| I ordered a latte at the coffee shop. 我在咖啡店点了一杯拿铁。

I prefer a latte to a cappuccino. 我比起卡布奇诺更喜欢拿铁。|近义词：caffe latte, coffee|反义词:|noun
