macadamization: /məˈkæd.ə.maɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (US) /məˌkæd.ə.maɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (UK)| n. | 碎石路面；用碎石铺路| The process of constructing a road surface using layers of compacted broken stone, as developed by John Loudon McAdam. | The macadamization of the road greatly improved travel times.  这条路的碎石铺设极大地缩短了旅行时间。 |Synonyms: paving, surfacing, road construction | Antonyms: deterioration, neglect | noun

macadamize: /məˈkæd.ə.maɪz/ (US) /məˈkæd.ə.maɪz/ (UK)| vt. | 用碎石铺路| To construct or pave (a road) with layers of compacted broken stone. |  They macadamized the road to make it more durable.  他们用碎石铺路以使其更耐用。|Synonyms: pave, surface | Antonyms: dig up, excavate | verb
