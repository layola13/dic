let the cat out of the bag:/lɛt ðə kæt aʊt əv ðə bæɡ/| idiom |泄露秘密；说漏嘴|To reveal a secret unintentionally.|例句：He let the cat out of the bag about the surprise party. 他不小心泄露了惊喜派对的秘密。

例句：Try not to let the cat out of the bag about the present.  尽量别把礼物的事说漏嘴了。

近义词：reveal a secret, spill the beans, give the game away|反义词: keep a secret, conceal, hide|词性:idiom
