highly:/ˈhaɪli/| adv.|高度地，非常，极；高价地|Used to emphasize the degree or extent of something.|He is a highly skilled musician. 他是一位技艺高超的音乐家。|近义词：extremely, very, exceptionally, remarkably|反义词：slightly, moderately, barely|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *highly*, from *high* + *-ly*.
