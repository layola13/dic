inextricable:/ˌɪnɪkˈstrɪkəbl/ | adj. | 无法解脱的，纠缠不清的，难解的 | The two climbers were roped together in an inextricable tangle. 两名登山者被绳子缠在一起，无法解脱。| 近义词: inseparable, tangled, involved | 反义词: separable, simple, easy | 词性: 形容词

词源：
From Late Latin inextricabilis, from in- (“not”) + extricabilis (“that can be disentangled”), from extricare (“to disentangle”), from ex- (“out of”) + tricae (“perplexities, hindrances”).
