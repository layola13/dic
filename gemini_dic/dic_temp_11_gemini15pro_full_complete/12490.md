aspergillus:/æsˈpɜːrˌdʒɪləs/| n. |曲霉菌属|A genus of common molds (fungi) found in various environments, some species of which can cause infections (aspergillosis) in humans and animals, especially those with weakened immune systems.|The doctor diagnosed him with an aspergillus infection in his lungs. 医生诊断他的肺部感染了曲霉菌。|近义词:mold, fungus|反义词:None (aspergillus is a specific genus, not a concept with an opposite)|noun
