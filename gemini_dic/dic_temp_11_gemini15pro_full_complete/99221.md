intercolonizations:/ˌɪntərkɑːlənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ /ˌɪntəkɒlənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |相互殖民化|The act of multiple groups or entities colonizing each other or each other's territories.|例句：The intercolonizations between European powers in the 17th century shaped the political landscape of the world. 17世纪欧洲列强之间的相互殖民化塑造了世界政治格局。

例句：The complex intercolonizations between various indigenous tribes in pre-colonial America are a subject of ongoing research.  前殖民时期美洲各土著部落之间复杂的相互殖民化是正在进行的研究课题。|近义词：mutual colonization, reciprocal colonization|反义词:decolonization|词性:名词
