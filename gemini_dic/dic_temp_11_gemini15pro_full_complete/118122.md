menopome:/ˈmɛnəˌpoʊm/ | n. | 美西螈，墨西哥钝口螈 | A large, permanently aquatic salamander (Necturus maculosus) native to lakes and streams of Mexico and the eastern United States, having bushy external gills and underdeveloped limbs.  It is also known as an axolotl in its larval form. |  例句：The menopome is a fascinating creature that can regenerate lost limbs.  美西螈是一种迷人的生物，可以再生失去的肢体。

例句：Researchers study the menopome for its remarkable regenerative abilities. 研究人员研究美西螈是为了了解其非凡的再生能力。 | 近义词：axolotl (in larval form), mudpuppy, waterdog | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
