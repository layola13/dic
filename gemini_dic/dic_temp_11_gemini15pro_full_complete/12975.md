asthmatically:/æsˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 喘息地；气喘吁吁地 | In a way that suggests someone is having difficulty breathing, typically due to asthma. | She breathed asthmatically after running up the stairs. 她跑上楼梯后喘息着。|  Example: The old dog panted asthmatically in the summer heat. 老狗在夏天的酷暑中气喘吁吁地喘着气。| Synonyms: wheezily, breathlessly, gaspingly | Antonyms: easily, smoothly, calmly |词性:副词
