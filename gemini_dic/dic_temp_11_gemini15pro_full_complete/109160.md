legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/ | adj. |合法的，正当的，合理的|Allowed by law; reasonable and acceptable|例句：The lawyer questioned whether the contract was legitimate.  律师质疑该合同是否合法。|近义词：legal, valid, lawful, justifiable |反义词: illegal, unlawful, illegitimate |词性:形容词
legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/ | v. |使合法化，宣布…为合法|To make something legal or acceptable|例句：The government legitimized the use of medical marijuana. 政府将医用大麻的使用合法化。|近义词：legalize, validate, authorize |反义词: outlaw, ban, prohibit |词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French legitime, from Latin legitimus ‘lawful’, from lex, leg- ‘law’.
