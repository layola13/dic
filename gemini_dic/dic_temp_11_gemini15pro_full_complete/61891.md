emptiness:/ˈɛmptɪnəs/| n.|空虚，空旷，空洞|A feeling of lack of purpose or meaning in life.  A state of being empty or unfilled.|例句：The emptiness of the vast desert was both awe-inspiring and frightening.  广阔沙漠的空旷既令人敬畏又令人恐惧。

例句：She felt a deep emptiness after her children left home.  孩子们离家后，她感到深深的空虚。|近义词：void, hollowness, vacuum, vacancy|反义词:fullness, abundance, plenitude|词性:noun
