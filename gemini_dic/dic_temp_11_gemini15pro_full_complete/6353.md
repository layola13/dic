amphibian:/æmˈfɪbiən/ | n. adj. | 两栖动物，水陆两栖的 | An animal that lives both on land and in water. |例句：Frogs and salamanders are amphibians. 青蛙和蝾螈是两栖动物。

amphibian:/æmˈfɪbiən/ | n. adj. | 两栖动物，水陆两栖的 |  Relating to or characteristic of an amphibian. | 例句：Amphibian vehicles can travel on both land and water. 水陆两栖车辆可以在陆地和水上行驶。

amphibian:/æmˈfɪbiən/ | n. adj. | 两栖动物，水陆两栖的 |  Suitable for use on both land and water. | 例句：The military uses amphibian aircraft. 军方使用水陆两用飞机。

amphibian:/æmˈfɪbiən/ | n. adj. | 两栖动物，水陆两栖的 |  (of a military operation) involving forces landed from the sea. | 例句：The D-Day landings were a major amphibian operation.  诺曼底登陆是一次重要的两栖作战。

近义词：frog, toad, newt, salamander (as nouns), semiaquatic (as adjective)
反义词：terrestrial, aquatic
词性：noun, adjective

词源：From Late Latin amphibius, from Ancient Greek ἀμφίβιος (amphíbios, “living a double life”), from ἀμφί (amphí, “on both sides”) + βίος (bíos, “life”).
