liroconite: /laɪˈrɒkənaɪt/
noun
铜铝砷酸盐矿物, 水砷铝铜矿
A hydrated copper aluminium arsenate mineral, usually bluish-green in color, occurring as crystals in oxidized copper deposits.
Liroconite is a rare secondary mineral found in oxidized copper deposits. 水砷铝铜矿是一种罕见的次生矿物，发现于氧化铜矿床中。
Liroconite crystals are often beautifully formed and highly prized by collectors. 水砷铝铜矿晶体通常形态优美，深受收藏家珍视。
Synonyms: none (highly specific mineral name)
Antonyms: N/A (not applicable to minerals)
词性: noun
