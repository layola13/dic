de-numbers:/diːˈnʌmbərz/| v. |去除数字|To remove numbers from something, often a text or document.  This can involve removing page numbers, numbered lists, or any other numerical identifiers.|例句：The editor de-numbered the manuscript before sending it to the reviewers. 编辑在将稿件发送给审稿人之前删除了稿件中的页码。

例句：The software can de-number files, making them easier to organize.  这个软件可以去除文件中的数字，使它们更容易整理。|近义词：remove numbers, strip numbers|反义词: number, enumerate |词性:动词
