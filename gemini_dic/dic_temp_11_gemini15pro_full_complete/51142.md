despoil:/dɪˈspɔɪl/
verb
掠夺，剥夺，破坏
to steal or violently remove valuable possessions from a place; to make a place less attractive by damaging or destroying it.
The invaders despoiled the city of its treasures. 侵略者掠夺了这座城市的宝藏。
The countryside has been despoiled by pollution. 乡村已经被污染破坏了。
synonym:pillage, plunder, rob, loot, ransack, ravage, devastate
antonym:protect, preserve, enhance, restore
词性:动词


despoil:/dɪˈspɔɪl/
noun
掠夺物，战利品 (通常用复数 despoils)
The despoils of war.  战争的掠夺物。
synonym:loot, spoils, plunder, booty
antonym:donation, offering
词性:名词
