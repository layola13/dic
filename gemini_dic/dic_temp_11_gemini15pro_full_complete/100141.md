interpunction:/ˌɪntərˈpʌŋkʃən/ | n. | 标点符号 |The act of punctuating; the state of being punctuated.  The use of punctuation marks in writing. | Correct interpunction is essential for clear writing. 正确的标点符号对于清晰的写作至关重要。| synonym: punctuation | antonym: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *interpunctionem (nominative interpunctio), noun of action from past-participle stem of interpunctuare "to point between," from inter "between" + punctuare "to prick, point, dot"
