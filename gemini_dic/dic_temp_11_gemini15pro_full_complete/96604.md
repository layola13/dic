inductorium:/ɪnˈdʌktɔːriəm/ | n. | 感应圈，电感器 | An apparatus for generating a high voltage by electromagnetic induction, especially a Ruhmkorff coil.  A device historically used to generate high voltages for experiments and early medical applications. It is a type of induction coil. |  The scientist used an inductorium to study electrical discharges.  科学家使用感应圈来研究放电现象。 |  induction coil, Ruhmkorff coil |  - | noun
