nonconjunctions:/ˌnɑn kənˈdʒʌŋkʃənz/ | n. | 非连词 |Words that are not conjunctions, meaning they do not connect words, phrases, or clauses. |  The words "dog" and "cat" are nonconjunctions.  “狗”和“猫”这两个词不是连词。| Near synonyms: nonconnectives | Antonyms: conjunctions | Noun
