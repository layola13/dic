holiest:/ˈhoʊliɪst/| adj.|最神圣的，最圣洁的|Most sacred; most holy.|Jerusalem is considered the holiest city by many religions. 耶路撒冷被许多宗教视为最神圣的城市.|The holiest day in the Christian calendar is Easter Sunday. 基督教日历中最神圣的日子是复活节星期天.|近义词：sacred, divine, blessed|反义词：unholy, profane, wicked|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old English *hāligest, superlative of hālig ("holy"), from Proto-Germanic *hailagaz ("holy, healthy, whole"), from Proto-Indo-European *kailo- ("whole, healthy, of good omen").  The "-est" suffix is the superlative ending.
