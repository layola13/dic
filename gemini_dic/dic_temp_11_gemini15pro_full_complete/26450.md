byssiferous:/bɪˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
产丝的，生丝的
Producing bissus (fine silky filaments secreted by certain mollusks, especially mussels, used to attach themselves to rocks, etc.).
Mussels are byssiferous mollusks, using their byssus to anchor themselves to rocks.  贻贝是产丝的软体动物，用它们的丝足附着在岩石上。
The byssiferous glands of the mussel secrete the strong, silky threads. 贻贝的产丝腺分泌出坚韧的丝线。
近义词: silk-producing, filamentous
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly consider "smooth-shelled" for mollusks lacking byssus)
词性: adjective
