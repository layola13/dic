cottised:/ˈkɒtɪsaɪzd/
adjective
带小盾的，侧面有小盾的
Having small shields placed along the sides of a larger shield, particularly in heraldry.
The cottised cross on the knight's shield marked him as a member of a specific order. 骑士盾牌上的带小盾十字架标志着他属于某个特定骑士团。
近义词: flanked, bordered
反义词: plain, unadorned
词性: adjective

词源:
From the French "coticé," past participle of "coticer," meaning "to furnish with cottices" or "little ribs/sides." Ultimately from "cote" meaning "rib" or "side."
