barge:/bɑːrdʒ/ (US) /bɑːdʒ/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|驳船；大型游船|A flat-bottomed boat for carrying freight, typically on canals and rivers, or a similar large boat.  A large, clumsy boat.|The coal barge moved slowly down the river. 这艘煤炭驳船沿着河缓慢地向下游移动。|synonyms: lighter, scow, boat|antonyms: airplane, car|noun
barge:/bɑːrdʒ/ (US) /bɑːdʒ/ (UK)| vi. vt.|闯入；冲撞；撞|To move forcefully or roughly; to interrupt rudely or intrusively.|He barged into the meeting without knocking. 他没敲门就闯进了会议室。|synonyms:  intrude, interrupt, burst in,  push|antonyms: sneak, tiptoe|verb
