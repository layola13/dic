coadjutor:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒuːtər/| n.|助手，副手，助理主教|A coadjutor is an assistant or helper, especially in the church, such as an assistant bishop.|The bishop appointed a coadjutor to help him with his duties. 主教任命了一位助手来帮助他履行职责。|Synonyms: assistant, helper, aide, deputy, auxiliary|Antonyms: opponent, adversary|noun

coadjutor:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒuːtər/| adj.|辅助的，协助的|Serving as a coadjutor or assistant.|He served as coadjutor bishop for five years before becoming the diocesan bishop. 他担任助理主教五年后才成为教区主教。|Synonyms: assisting, auxiliary, supporting|Antonyms: opposing, hindering|adjective
