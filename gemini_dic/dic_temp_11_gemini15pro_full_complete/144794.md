over-flowable:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊəbəl/
adjective
可溢出的，可满溢的
able to overflow or be overflowed; capable of exceeding capacity and spilling over.
The cup was so full that it was over-flowable.  杯子太满了，都要溢出来了。
The river is over-flowable during the rainy season. 雨季时，这条河很容易泛滥。
近义词: overflowing, inundable, floodable
反义词: contained, limited
