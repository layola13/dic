conversely:/ˈkɒnvɜːsli/ /kənˈvɜːrsli/| adv.|相反地，反过来说|Introducing a statement or idea that reverses one that has just been made or referred to.|He was regarded as a hero, conversely, his brother was seen as a traitor. 他被视为英雄，相反，他的兄弟被视为叛徒。|Conversely, the drug could be used to save lives. 相反地，这种药物可以用来拯救生命。|近义词：on the other hand, in contrast, contrarily|反义词：similarly, likewise|词性:副词
