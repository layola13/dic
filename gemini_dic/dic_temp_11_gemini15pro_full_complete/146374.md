overflooded:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌdɪd/| adj.|被淹没的，泛滥的|Overwhelmed or inundated with something, especially water or an emotion. Filled beyond capacity.|The river overflooded its banks after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，河水漫过了河岸。|The market is overflooded with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。|近义词：inundated, overwhelmed, swamped|反义词：dry, arid|词性:形容词

overflooded:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌdɪd/| v.|（使）泛滥，（使）充满|Past participle of "overflood". To flood excessively; inundate.|The town was overflooded after the dam burst.  大坝决堤后，小镇被淹没了。|My inbox is constantly overflooded with spam emails. 我的收件箱总是被垃圾邮件淹没。|近义词：inundate, overwhelm, swamped|反义词：drain, dry|词性:动词
