neophyte:/ˈniːəˌfaɪt/| n.|新手，初学者；新皈依者|A person who is new to a subject or skill; a recent convert to a religion.|例句：The neophyte writer struggled with writer's block.  这位新手作家与写作障碍作斗争。

例句：He was a neophyte in the world of finance. 他是金融界的新手。|近义词：beginner, novice, tyro, rookie, newcomer, convert, proselyte|反义词：expert, veteran, professional|noun
