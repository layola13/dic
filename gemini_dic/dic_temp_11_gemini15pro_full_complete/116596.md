matriculation: /məˌtrɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/ /məˌtrɪkjəˈleɪʃn/
n.
入学，注册; 入学考试
The act of enrolling or being enrolled in a college or university.  The examination or other qualifications required for matriculation.

例句：
Her matriculation at the university was delayed by a year.
她上大学的时间推迟了一年。

Matriculation exams are crucial for entry into this program.
入学考试对于进入该项目至关重要。

近义词：enrollment, registration, admission
反义词：withdrawal, graduation

词性: noun


词源:  From Late Latin *matriculatio, from matricula "register, roll," diminutive of matrix "list, register."
