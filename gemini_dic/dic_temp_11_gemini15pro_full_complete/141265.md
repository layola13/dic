opalotype:/ˈoʊpəlˌtaɪp/| n.| 蛋白石照片，蛋白石印相|A photographic image on glass backed with an opaque white substance to simulate the appearance of an opal.|This opalotype captures the beauty of the Victorian era. 这张蛋白石照片捕捉到了维多利亚时代的美丽。|Near synonyms: ambrotype, ferrotype|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific photographic process)|noun
