mongal:/ˈmɒŋɡəl/ | n. | 蒙古人；蒙古马；笨蛋；傻瓜 | A person of Mongolian descent; a Mongolian horse; (offensive, derogatory) a stupid or foolish person. |  He rode a sturdy mongal across the steppe. 他骑着一匹强壮的蒙古马穿越草原。


| 近义词:  Mongolian (for person or horse), idiot, fool, moron (for derogatory meaning) | 反义词: genius (for derogatory meaning) | 词性: noun


mongal (derogatory): /ˈmɒŋɡəl/ | n. | 笨蛋；傻瓜 |  (offensive, derogatory) A stupid or foolish person.  | Don't be a mongal! Think before you act.  别犯傻！三思而后行。


| 近义词: idiot, fool, moron, imbecile | 反义词: genius, intellectual | 词性: noun
