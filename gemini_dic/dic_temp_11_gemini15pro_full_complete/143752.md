outmanning: /ˌaʊtˈmænɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
以人数取胜；在人数上超过
defeating or surpassing in numbers of men.
The home team was outmanned but not outfought. 主队人数上处于劣势，但斗志昂扬。
Synonyms: outnumber, surpass, exceed
Antonyms: outnumbered, undermanned
词性：动词(现在分词)
