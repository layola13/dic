antienvironmental:/ˌæntiɪnˌvaɪrənˈmentl/
adjective
反对环境保护的；破坏环境的
Opposed to environmental protection or causing environmental damage.
The company's antienvironmental policies led to significant pollution. 该公司的反环保政策导致了严重的污染。
The factory's antienvironmental practices have damaged the local ecosystem. 这家工厂的反环保行为破坏了当地的生态系统。
Synonyms:  environmentally destructive, eco-destructive, anti-green
Antonyms:  environmentally friendly, pro-environment, green, eco-friendly
词性: 形容词
