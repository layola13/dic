irreflective:/ˌɪrɪˈflektɪv/
adjective
无反射的；不反射的；不反省的；缺乏思考的
not reflective; without reflection.  Lacking in consideration or thought.
He stared with an irreflective gaze. 他茫然地凝视着。
近义词: thoughtless, unthinking, heedless, careless, unreflective
反义词: reflective, thoughtful, contemplative
词性: adjective

irreflective:/ˌɪrɪˈflektɪv/
adjective
无思虑的
not given to reflection; thoughtless
His irreflective comments hurt her feelings. 他不经意的评论伤害了她的感情。
近义词: thoughtless, inconsiderate, rash, impulsive
反义词: thoughtful, considerate, reflective
词性: adjective
