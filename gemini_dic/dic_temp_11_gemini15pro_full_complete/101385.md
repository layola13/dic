iota's:/ˈaɪoʊtəz/
noun
极少量；一点儿
A very small amount; a bit.
He doesn't have an iota of common sense. 他一点常识都没有。
Synonyms: bit, speck, trace, scintilla
Antonyms: abundance, lot, plenty
词性: 名词
