noncontemporaneousnesses:/ˌnɒnkənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəsnəsɪz/
noun
不同时性；非同时代性
The state or quality of not being contemporaneous; not existing or occurring at the same time.
The noncontemporaneousnesses of the documents made it difficult to establish a clear timeline of events.  这些文件非同时期性使得难以建立清晰的事件时间表。
The historian noted the noncontemporaneousnesses of the sources, which complicated his analysis.  历史学家注意到资料来源的非同时代性，这使得他的分析变得复杂。
近义词: asynchrony, non-simultaneity
反义词: contemporaneity, simultaneity
词性: noun
