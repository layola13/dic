overmodernized: /ˌoʊvərˈmɒdərnɪzd/ /ˌoʊvərˈmɑːdərnaɪzd/
adjective
过度现代化的
Having been modernized to an excessive or unnecessary degree.  Modernized beyond what is practical or desirable.
The city center felt sterile and overmodernized, lacking the charm of its historical past.  这个市中心感觉毫无生气，过度现代化，缺乏其历史过去的魅力。

The kitchen was overmodernized with gadgets she never used.  厨房里装满了她从未使用过的现代化小玩意。
Synonyms: overdeveloped, excessively modernized
Antonyms: underdeveloped, antiquated, outdated
词性: adjective
