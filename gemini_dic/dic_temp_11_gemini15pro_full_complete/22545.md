boor:/bʊr/| n. | 粗野的人，乡巴佬 | A rude, insensitive, and clumsy person. | 例句：He acted like a complete boor at the dinner party, insulting the host and spilling wine on the guests.  他在晚宴上表现得像个十足的粗人，侮辱主人，把酒洒在客人身上。| 例句：The boor pushed his way to the front of the line, ignoring the glares of those he inconvenienced. 这个粗鲁的人挤到队伍的最前面，无视那些被他打扰的人的怒视。| 近义词：lout, churl, oaf, yahoo, barbarian | 反义词：gentleman, sophisticate | 词性: 名词
