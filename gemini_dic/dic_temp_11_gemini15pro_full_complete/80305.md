gold:/ɡoʊld/ (US) /ɡəʊld/ (UK)| n. adj.|黄金，金色的|A precious yellow metal, highly valued for its beauty, resistance to corrosion, and rarity. Also used to describe things having the color of gold.|例句：The ring is made of pure gold.  这枚戒指是由纯金制成的。

例句：She won a gold medal at the Olympics.  她在奥运会上赢得了一枚金牌。|近义词：aureate, golden|反义词:silver, platinum|词性:名词, 形容词
