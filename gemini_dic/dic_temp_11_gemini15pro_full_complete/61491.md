embroidered: /ɪmˈbrɔɪdərd/ (US) /ɪmˈbrɔɪdəd/ (UK)
adjective
绣花的；刺绣的
Decorated with needlework.
例句：She wore an embroidered blouse. 她穿着一件绣花衬衫。

例句：The tablecloth was embroidered with delicate flowers.  桌布上绣着精致的花朵。

近义词：decorated, ornamented, stitched
反义词：plain, unembellished
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *embroidier, from en- "in, on" + broider "to embroider," from broder, from Germanic *brozjan "to prick, prod."
