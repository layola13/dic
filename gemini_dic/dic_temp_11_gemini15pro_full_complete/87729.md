hexadecimal:/ˌheksəˈdesɪməl/| adj. n.|十六进制的，十六进制数|Relating to or based on the number 16.  A number system with a base of 16.|The computer stored the number in hexadecimal format.|近义词：base-16, sexadecimal|反义词:decimal, binary|词性:adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hexa- (Greek, "six") + decimal (Latin, "ten").  Essentially, six and ten together make sixteen, the base of this system.
