clonky:/ˈklʌŋki/| adj.|笨重的；粗陋的；运行不畅的|Making a loud, dull sound, as of heavy metal objects hitting each other; awkward, clumsy, or unwieldy.  Often used to describe machinery or technology that is outdated or inefficient.|The car's engine made a clonky noise. 汽车的发动机发出哐啷哐啷的声音。|The website design is a bit clonky.  这个网站的设计有点笨拙。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, clunky, cumbersome, rickety|反义词：smooth, sleek, efficient, streamlined|词性:形容词
