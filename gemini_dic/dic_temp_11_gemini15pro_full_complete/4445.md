阿尔巴隆加
An ancient city of Latium, traditionally founded by Ascanius, son of Aeneas. It was the birthplace of Romulus and Remus, the legendary founders of Rome.
According to legend, Alba Longa was destroyed by Rome.  阿尔巴隆加据传被罗马摧毁。
Alba Longa was the leading city of the Latin League.  阿尔巴隆加是拉丁同盟的主要城市。
