divinable:/dɪˈvaɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可预言的，可推测的 | Able to be guessed or understood by intuition or insight. |  Her intentions were easily divinable from her behavior. 她的意图很容易从她的行为中推测出来。 | synonyms: predictable, foreseeable, discernible, perceptible | antonyms: unpredictable, unforeseeable, indiscernible, imperceptible | word type: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *divinable, from Old French devinable, from deviner (to divine), from Latin divinare (to foretell, predict, prophesy), from divinus (divine), from divus (god).
