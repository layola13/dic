autochthonousnesses:/ˌɔːtɒkˈθɒnəsnəsɪz/ | n. |本土性，土著性| The state or quality of being autochthonous; the property of being indigenous. |  The autochthonousnesses of the various tribes in the region are well documented.  该地区各部落的本土性都有详细的记录。 |Synonym: indigeneity, aboriginality | Antonym: foreignness, alienness | noun

autochthonous:/ˌɔːˈtɒkθənəs/ | adj. |本土的，土著的|  (of a plant or animal) indigenous rather than descended from migrants or introduced from elsewhere. | The island's autochthonous species are threatened by invasive ones.  该岛的本土物种受到入侵物种的威胁。 | Synonym: indigenous, native, aboriginal | Antonym: foreign, alien, introduced, exotic | adjective
