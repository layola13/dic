grapple:/ˈɡræpəl/ (US) /ˈɡræpəl/ (UK)| n. v. |抓住；格斗；钩爪；抓斗|A struggle or contest in which the participants attempt to seize or hold each other.|The climber used a grapple to ascend the cliff face. 攀岩者用抓钩爬上悬崖面。|Synonyms: wrestle, contend, clutch, grip|Antonyms: release, free, surrender|noun
grapple:/ˈɡræpəl/ (US) /ˈɡræpəl/ (UK)| v. |格斗，搏斗；抓住；努力克服|To engage in a close fight or struggle without weapons; to try hard to deal with or understand something difficult.|He grappled with the burglar. 他与窃贼扭打起来。|Synonyms: wrestle, contend, clutch, grip|Antonyms: release, free, surrender|verb
