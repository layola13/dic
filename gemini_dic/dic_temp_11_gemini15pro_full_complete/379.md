ability's:/əˈbɪlətiz/| n.|能力，技能（ability的名词所有格）|It's a testament to her ability's power. 这证明了她的能力很强。|近义词：capability's, talent's, skill's|反义词：inability's, weakness's|词性:名词所有格

词源:
From Late Middle English: from Old French habilité, or from late Latin habilitatem, both from Latin habilis ‘apt’, from habere ‘to have’.
