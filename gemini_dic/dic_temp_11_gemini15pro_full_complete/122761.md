moisturizers:/ˈmɔɪstʃəˌraɪzərz/
noun
保湿霜，润肤霜
Creams or lotions designed to add or restore moisture to the skin.
I use a variety of moisturizers to keep my skin hydrated. 我使用各种保湿霜来保持皮肤水分。
Many people apply moisturizers after showering. 很多人在淋浴后涂抹保湿霜。
Synonyms: emollients, hydrators, creams, lotions
Antonyms: drying agents, desiccants
