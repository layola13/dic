flabbergast:/ˈflæbərɡæst/| vt.|使大吃一惊，使目瞪口呆|To surprise or astonish someone so greatly that they are speechless or bewildered.|The news completely flabbergasted me. 这消息让我大吃一惊。|synonyms: astound, amaze, shock, stun, startle, dumbfound|antonyms: expect, anticipate|verb

flabbergasted:/ˌflæbərˈɡæstɪd/| adj.|大吃一惊的，目瞪口呆的|Extremely surprised or astonished.|I was flabbergasted by his sudden confession. 我被他突然的坦白惊呆了。|synonyms: astonished, amazed, shocked, stunned, startled, dumbfounded|antonyms: unsurprised, unexcited|adjective
