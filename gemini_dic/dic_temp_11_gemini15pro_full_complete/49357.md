delint:/diːˈlɪnt/| v. |去除绒毛，去除代码中的警告或错误|To remove lint, fluff, or small pieces of fiber from something. In computer programming, it refers to removing warnings, stylistic errors, or minor bugs from source code.|I need to delint my sweater.  我需要清理一下我的毛衣。

The programmer used a tool to delint the code. 程序员使用工具来清理代码。|近义词：clean, tidy, remove, debug|反义词:mess up, pollute|词性:动词

词源:  "de-" (prefix indicating removal) + "lint" (noun referring to small fibers or fluff).  "lint" 最初指的是从亚麻布上收集的短纤维。后来，在计算机编程中，"lint" 指的是源代码中的小错误或警告，因此 "delint" 就指去除这些错误或警告。
