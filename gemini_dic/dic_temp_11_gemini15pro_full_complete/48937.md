defunct:/dɪˈfʌŋkt/
adjective
失效的， defunct
No longer existing or functioning.
例句：The company is now defunct.  这家公司现在已经倒闭了。

例句：The organization’s charter has been defunct for over a decade.  该组织的章程已失效十多年了。

近义词：extinct, obsolete, expired, bygone, vanished
反义词： extant, existing, functioning, operating, current
词性: adjective


词源: Late Middle English: from Latin defunctus ‘having finished’, past participle of defungi ‘perform, discharge (a duty)’.
