craves:/kreɪvz/| v.|渴望，热望|Third-person singular present tense form of crave.  It means to have an intense desire for something.|She craves chocolate. 她渴望巧克力。|He craves attention. 他渴望得到关注。|近义词：desires, yearns for, longs for, hankers after|反义词：dislikes, abhors, loathes|词性:动词
