observatories:/əbˈzɜːrvətɔːriz/| n.|天文台；气象台；观测站|A place or building equipped with telescopes and other scientific instruments for observing astronomical, meteorological, or other natural phenomena. |例句：Many observatories are located on mountaintops to minimize atmospheric interference. 许多天文台都位于山顶，以尽量减少大气干扰。

例句：The observatory tracked the trajectory of the newly discovered comet.  天文台追踪了新发现的彗星的轨迹。

近义词：astronomical observatories, meteorological stations, monitoring stations|反义词:|词性:noun
