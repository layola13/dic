antifilibuster:/ˌæntiˈfɪlɪbʌstər/
adjective|反冗长辩论的，阻止阻挠议事的|Opposed to the use of the filibuster, a tactic used in legislative bodies to delay or block a vote. |The senator was a staunch antifilibuster advocate, believing it hindered progress.  这位参议员是坚定的反冗长辩论的倡导者，认为它阻碍了进步。|近义词：anti-obstructionist|反义词：pro-filibuster|词性: adjective


antifilibuster:/ˌæntiˈfɪlɪbʌstər/
noun|反冗长辩论者，阻止阻挠议事者|A person who opposes the use of the filibuster. |The antifilibusters argued that the tactic was being abused. 反冗长辩论者认为这种策略被滥用了。|近义词：anti-obstructionist|反义词：filibusterer|词性: noun
