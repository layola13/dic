hyperimmunization:/ˌhaɪpərɪmjənɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 超免疫 | The process of inducing an enhanced immune response, typically by administering repeated doses of an antigen.  |  The doctor recommended hyperimmunization for the travelers going to a high-risk area.  医生建议前往高风险地区的旅行者进行超免疫。 | synonyms: hyperimmunisation, superimmunization | antonyms: immunosuppression | noun
