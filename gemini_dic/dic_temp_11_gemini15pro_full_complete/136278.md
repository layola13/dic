nonreplicate: /nɒnˈrɛplɪkeɪt/ (US) /nɒnˈrɛplɪkeɪt/ (UK)| adj. vi. | 非复制的，不复制的 | Not replicating; not reproducing.  Unable or designed not to replicate. | The nonreplicate study showed inconsistent results. 这项非重复性研究显示结果不一致。| The virus was engineered to be nonreplicate. 该病毒被设计成无法复制。| 近义词: non-duplicating, nonreproducing | 反义词: replicate, reproduce | 词性: adjective, intransitive verb

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "replicate" (from Latin "replicare," meaning "to fold back, repeat")
