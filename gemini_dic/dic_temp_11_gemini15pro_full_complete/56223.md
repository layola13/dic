doer:/ˈduːər/| n. | 实干家，行动者 |  A person who acts rather than merely talking or thinking. |  He's a doer, not a talker. 他是一个实干家，而不是空谈家。| 近义词: actor, performer, worker, operator | 反义词: thinker, planner, dreamer, idler | noun

doer:/ˈduːər/| n. | 行事者 |  A person who habitually does a specified thing. |  She's a doer of good deeds. 她是一个做好事的人。 | 近义词: practitioner, executor | 反义词: abstainer | noun
