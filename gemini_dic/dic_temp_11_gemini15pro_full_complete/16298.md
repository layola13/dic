bar-hop:/ˈbɑːr hɑːp/| vi.|酒吧间串游，流连于各个酒吧|To go from one bar to another, typically drinking alcohol at each one.|例句：They decided to bar-hop through the city center. 他们决定在市中心逛酒吧。

例句：We spent the evening bar-hopping and met some interesting people. 我们晚上逛了几个酒吧，遇到了一些有趣的人。|近义词：pub-crawl|反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "staying in")|词性：verb
