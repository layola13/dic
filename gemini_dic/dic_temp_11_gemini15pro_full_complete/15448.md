bad-tempered:/ˌbædˈtempəd/ | adj. | 脾气暴躁的，易怒的 | easily angered; irritable | He's very bad-tempered in the mornings. 他早上脾气很暴躁。| irritable, grumpy, irascible, cross, surly | good-natured, easygoing, affable, amiable | adjective

bad-tempered:/ˌbædˈtempəd/ | adj. | 脾气暴躁的，易怒的 | having or showing a tendency to be easily annoyed or made angry | She gets bad-tempered when she's tired. 她累了就容易发脾气。| cranky, touchy, short-tempered | patient, tolerant | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From bad + tempered. "Tempered" in this sense means "having a particular state of mind or feelings," relating to the tempering of metal, which changes its properties.
