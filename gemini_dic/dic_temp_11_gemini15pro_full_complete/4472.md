albertans:/ælˈbɜːrtənz/
noun
阿尔伯塔省人
People who live in or originate from Alberta, a province in Canada.
Many Albertans are employed in the oil and gas industry. 许多阿尔伯塔省人在石油和天然气行业工作。
synonyms: Albertan people, residents of Alberta
antonyms: non-Albertans
