furrowers:/ˈfɜːroʊərz/
noun
犁沟者；耕耘者
People or things that make furrows (long, narrow trenches or grooves) in the ground.
The furrowers had prepared the fields for planting. 犁沟者已经准备好田地播种了。
farmers, cultivators, plowmen
pavers, smoothers
