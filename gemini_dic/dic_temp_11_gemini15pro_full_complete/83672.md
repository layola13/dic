hafts:/hæfts/| n.|柄，把；(尤指刀、斧、剑等的)柄，把儿；部分|The axe had been driven into the haft. 斧头已装上柄。|例句：The knight grasped the haft of his sword. 骑士握住剑柄。

例句：The hafts of the knives were made of polished wood. 这些刀的柄是用抛光木头做的。|近义词：handles, hilts (especially for swords/daggers), grips|反义词: blade (对比刀刃和刀柄)|词性:名词

hafts:/hæfts/| v.|给…装柄|He hafted the axe head securely. 他把斧头牢牢地装在柄上。|例句：The craftsman carefully hafted the new blade. 工匠小心翼翼地给新刀刃装上柄。

例句：She hafted the spear with a strong piece of wood. 她用一块结实的木头给矛装上柄。|近义词：attach, fix, fit|反义词: detach, remove|词性:动词


词源:
From Old English *hæft* "handle, haft," from Proto-Germanic *haftjaz. Related to Old Norse *heft* "handle," and ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kap-/*keh₂p- "to grasp."
