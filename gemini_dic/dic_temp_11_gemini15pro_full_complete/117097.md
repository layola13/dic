meal:/miːl/| n. |餐，一顿饭|A quantity of food eaten at one time.|We had a delicious meal at the restaurant. 我们在餐馆吃了一顿美味的饭。|近义词：dinner, repast, feast|反义词:snack|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mǣl (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *mālō (“time, point of time, fixed time, assembly, speech, agreement, contract, meal”), from Proto-Indo-European *mol- (“to assemble”). Cognate with Old Frisian mēl (“time, occasion, meal”), Old Saxon māla (“time, speech”), Old High German māla (“time, assembly, court session”), Old Norse mál (“speech, language, matter, case, time, occasion”).
