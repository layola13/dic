haubergeon:/ˈhɔːbərdʒən/| n. |锁子甲, 短锁子甲|A short coat of mail, typically sleeveless, worn over a gambeson or other armor. |The knight donned his haubergeon before riding into battle. 骑士在骑马出战前穿上了他的锁子甲。|Synonyms: mail shirt, byrnie, chainmail|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "civilian clothing")|noun
