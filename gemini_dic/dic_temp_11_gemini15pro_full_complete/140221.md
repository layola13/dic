officiate:/əˈfɪʃieɪt/
verb
主持；执行；担任裁判
to perform a ceremony, especially a religious one; to act as an official in charge of something, especially a sporting event
The archbishop officiated at the royal wedding.  大主教主持了皇室婚礼。
The referee officiated the game fairly. 裁判公平地主持了比赛。
近义词: preside, referee, administer, conduct, direct
反义词: None
词性: verb
