feudally:/ˈfjuːdəli/ | adv. | 封建地 | In a feudal manner; characteristic of feudalism. | The land was organized feudally, with lords and serfs.  这片土地是以封建方式组织的，有领主和农奴。| The peasants were treated feudally.  农民们受到了封建式的对待。 |  近义词: archaically, hierarchically | 反义词: democratically, egalitarianly | 词性: 副词

词源: From *feudal* + *-ly*. *Feudal* comes from Old French *feodal*, from Medieval Latin *feodalis*, from *feodum* "fief" (see *fief*).
