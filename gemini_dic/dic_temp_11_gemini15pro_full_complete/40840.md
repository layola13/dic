convulsiveness:/kənˈvʌlsɪvnəs/| n.|抽搐，痉挛，惊厥|The state of being convulsive; characterized by or subject to convulsions.|The convulsiveness of his laughter was unsettling. 他笑得抽搐的样子令人不安。|近义词：spasticity, fitfulness, jerkiness|反义词：smoothness, stillness, calmness|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *convulsīvus, from Latin convulsus (past participle of convellere, "to pull violently together"), from con- ("together") + vellere ("to pluck, pull").  The "-ness" suffix transforms the adjective "convulsive" into the noun "convulsiveness."
