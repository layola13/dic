initialing:/ɪˈnɪʃəlɪŋ/ | n. | 草签，首字母缩写 | The act of marking or signing with initials. |  Example: The initialing of the contract signifies agreement to the terms. 合同的草签表示同意条款。

Example: Initialing each page of the document prevents fraudulent insertion of pages.  在文件的每一页上草签可以防止有人欺诈性地插入页面。| 近义词: signing, endorsing | 反义词: N/A | noun
