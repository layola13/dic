curtailers:/kərˈteɪlərz/
n.
缩减者；限制者；削减者
People or things that curtail something.  Those who reduce or limit something.
The budget curtailers were unpopular. 预算削减者不受欢迎。
近义词: restrictors, reducers, limiters
反义词: expanders, increasers, augmenters
词性: 名词(plural)

curtail:/kərˈteɪl/
v.
缩减；限制；削减
To reduce in extent or quantity; to shorten.
The government curtailed spending on education. 政府削减了教育支出。
近义词: reduce, restrict, shorten, decrease, diminish
反义词: increase, expand, extend, lengthen, augment
词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *curtailler, from curtal "shortened, docked," from Latin curtus "short."
