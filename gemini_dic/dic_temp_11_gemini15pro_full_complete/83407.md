habendum:/həˈbɛndəm/
n.|财产让与证书|The clause in a deed, lease, or other conveyance that defines the extent of the interest or rights granted; the "to have and to hold" clause.|The habendum clause in the deed specified that the property was to be used for residential purposes only.|近义词:clause, provision, stipulation|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

词源: From Anglo-French *habendum et tenendum* "to have and to hold," the initial words of the habendum clause in a deed. Ultimately from Latin *habendum* (gerund of *habēre* "to have") + *tenendum* (gerund of *tenēre* "to hold").
