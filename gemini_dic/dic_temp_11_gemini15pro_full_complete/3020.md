aerie:/ˈɛəri/ /ˈɪəri/| n.|高处的巢；鹰巢；高处房屋|A lofty nest, especially of a bird of prey such as an eagle or hawk, typically built on a cliff or other high place.  A house or other dwelling situated in a high or elevated place.|The eagle returned to its aerie.  这只鹰回到了它的巢穴。|近义词：nest, eyrie, perch|反义词:ground|名词
