excecation: /ˌeksɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
切除；切开
The surgical excecation of the tumor was successful.  肿瘤的切除手术很成功。
The excecation of the infected tissue prevented the spread of the infection. 切除受感染的组织阻止了感染的蔓延。
近义词: excision, removal, resection
反义词: implantation, insertion, addition
词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *excecatio, from Latin excecare “to blind, make blind,” from ex- "out" + caecare "to blind."  The medical meaning developed from the idea of "cutting out."
