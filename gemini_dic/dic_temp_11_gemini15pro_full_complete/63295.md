enthusiasms:/ɪnˈθuːziæzəmz/| n. |热情，热忱，热衷的事物|A feeling of energetic interest in a particular subject or activity and an eagerness to be involved in it.|She has a lot of enthusiasms, but they don't last long. 她有很多热情，但都持续不久。|The students showed great enthusiasm for the project. 学生们对这个项目表现出了极大的热情。|近义词：passion, zeal, fervor, eagerness, excitement|反义词：apathy, indifference, disinterest|词性:名词
