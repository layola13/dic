consensually:/kənˈsɛnʃuəli/| adv.|同意地，经双方同意地|In a way that is agreed to by the people involved; by mutual consent.|The land was consensually sold. 这块地是双方同意出售的。|例句：The decision was made consensually by all members of the team.  团队所有成员一致同意做出这个决定。

例句：They consensually agreed to end their business partnership. 他们双方同意终止业务合作关系。|近义词：mutually, jointly, by agreement, with consent|反义词：forcibly, against one's will, without consent|词性:副词
