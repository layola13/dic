disproportionateness:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənətnəs/
|n.|不相称；不成比例；不均衡|The disproportionateness of the punishment to the crime caused public outcry.  对罪行处以不相称的惩罚引起了公众的强烈抗议。|The study revealed the disproportionateness of resource allocation across the region. 这项研究揭示了该区域资源分配的不均衡。|近义词：imbalance, disparity, inequality, asymmetry|反义词：proportionality, balance, symmetry|词性: noun

disproportionate:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənɪt/
|adj.|不相称的；不成比例的；不均衡的|The punishment was disproportionate to the crime. 惩罚与罪行不相称。|He has a disproportionately large head. 他的头大得不成比例。|近义词：unequal, uneven, lopsided, excessive|反义词：proportionate, balanced, symmetrical|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + proportionate + -ness.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Proportionate" comes from the Latin "proportionatus," past participle of "proportionare," meaning "to make proportionate." "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
