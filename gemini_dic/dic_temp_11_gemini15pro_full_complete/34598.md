cleavable:/ˈkliːvəbl/| adj.|可裂解的，可分裂的|Able to be cleaved or split.|The molecule is cleavable by specific enzymes.  这个分子可以被特定的酶裂解。|例句：The diamond is cleavable along certain planes.  钻石可以沿着某些平面裂开。|近义词：splittable, divisible, separable|反义词：unbreakable, indivisible|词性:形容词
cleavable:/ˈkliːvəbl/| adj.|可劈开的|Able to be cleaved or split.|The wood is cleavable with an axe. 这块木头可以用斧头劈开。|例句：This type of rock is easily cleavable. 这种岩石很容易劈开。|近义词：fissile, fissible|反义词：unsplittable|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From cleave (v.) + -able.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kleubaną (“to cleave”).
