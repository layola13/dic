incarnated: /ɪnˈkɑːrneɪtɪd/ /ɪnˈkɑːrneɪtəd/
adjective
化身的，体现的
Embodied in flesh; personified.  Having a bodily, especially a human, form.
例句：
The devil incarnate. = 恶魔的化身。

God incarnate. =  上帝的化身。


近义词：embodied, personified, materialized, manifested
反义词：disembodied, spiritual, ethereal
词性: adjective
