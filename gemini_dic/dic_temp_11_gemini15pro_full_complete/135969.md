nonrabbinical:/ˌnɑːnˌræˈbɪnɪkəl/
adjective
非拉比的；非犹太教律法学者的
Not pertaining to, characteristic of, or adhering to the interpretations and traditions of the rabbis.
The book presented a nonrabbinical interpretation of the Torah.  这本书提出了对律法书的非拉比式解释。
This custom is a nonrabbinical tradition.  这个习俗是非拉比的传统。
synonyms: non-talmudic, secular, lay
antonyms: rabbinical, talmudic
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + rabbinical.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Rabbinical" refers to the rabbis, Jewish scholars and teachers of the Torah.
