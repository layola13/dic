ablators:/əˈbleɪtərz/| n.| 烧蚀器；消融器；切除器|A device or substance that removes material from a surface by ablation (the removal of material by melting, vaporization, or other erosive processes).|The spacecraft used ablators to protect it from the intense heat of atmospheric re-entry.  航天器使用烧蚀器来保护它免受大气层重返的强烈热量的影响。

Ablators are crucial for protecting the nose cone of a rocket. 烧蚀器对于保护火箭的鼻锥至关重要。|近义词：ablative materials, thermal protection systems|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "constructive materials")|词性:noun
