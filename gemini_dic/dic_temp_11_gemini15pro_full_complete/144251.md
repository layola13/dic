outsnatch: /aʊtˈsnætʃ/
verb
攫取；抢夺
To snatch something away from someone else; to grab something quickly and forcefully from another's grasp.
He out snatched the ball from the opponent just before the goal line. 他在球门线前从对手手中抢走了球。
The eagle outsnatched the fish from the osprey's talons.  老鹰从鱼鹰的爪子里抢走了鱼。
近义词: seize, grab, wrest, snatch
反义词: give, offer, relinquish, yield
词性: verb
