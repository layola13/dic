buttress:/ˈbʌtrɪs/| n. vt.|支撑，扶壁；支持物；加固物|A buttress is a structure built against a wall to support it.|The cathedral walls are supported by huge stone buttresses. 教堂的墙壁由巨大的石质扶壁支撑。|The government buttressed its position by releasing new economic figures. 政府通过发布新的经济数据来加强其立场。|Synonyms: support, prop, reinforce, strengthen|Antonyms: weaken, undermine|noun, verb


buttress:/ˈbʌtrɪs/| v.|支持，加强|To buttress something means to support or strengthen it.|The lawyer buttressed his argument with legal precedents.  律师用法律先例来支持他的论点。|His claims were buttressed by the testimony of several witnesses.  他的说法得到了几个证人的证词的支持。|Synonyms: support, prop, reinforce, strengthen|Antonyms: weaken, undermine|verb
