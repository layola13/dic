oversensitizing:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪˌtaɪzɪŋ/| v.| 过度敏感化；使过分敏感| The constant criticism was oversensitizing him to any remarks about his work.  不断的批评使他对任何关于他工作的评论都过度敏感。| He worried that the training might be oversensitizing the dogs to certain sounds. 他担心这种训练可能会使狗对某些声音过度敏感。|近义词: hypersensitizing|反义词: desensitizing|词性: 动词(gerund or present participle)

oversensitive:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/| adj.| 过度敏感的；神经质的| She's oversensitive to criticism. 她对批评过度敏感。| He's oversensitive about his weight. 他对自己的体重过度敏感。|近义词: touchy, hypersensitive|反义词: insensitive, thick-skinned|词性: 形容词

词源:  From over- + sensitizing, the present participle of sensitize.  Sensitize itself comes from  sensitive + -ize.  Ultimately from Latin *sensitīvus, from sentire "to feel."
