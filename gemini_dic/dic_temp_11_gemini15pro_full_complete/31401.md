chargehands:/ˈtʃɑːrdʒhændz/
| n. | 工头，领班 | A chargehand is a worker who is in charge of a small group of other workers.  A chargehand is responsible for supervising a small team of workers. | The chargehand told the team to start work on the new project. 领班告诉团队开始新项目的工作。

The chargehand ensured everyone was following safety procedures. 领班确保每个人都遵守安全规程。 | synonyms: foreman, supervisor, team leader, crew chief | antonyms: worker, subordinate, employee | noun
