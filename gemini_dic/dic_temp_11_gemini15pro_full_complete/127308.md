navy-blue:/ˈneɪvi bluː/| adj.|海军蓝|A dark blue colour, the colour of a navy uniform.|She wore a navy-blue dress to the wedding. 她穿了一件海军蓝的连衣裙去参加婚礼。|近义词：dark blue, indigo, sapphire|反义词:light blue, pale blue|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "navy" (referring to the British Royal Navy) + "blue".  The term became associated with the dark blue color of the British naval uniforms, adopted in 1748.
