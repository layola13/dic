bituminizes:/bɪˈtʃuːmənaɪzɪz/ /bɪˈtuːmənaɪzɪz/
Verb
涂沥青；使成为沥青状
To cover or treat with bitumen or asphalt.
The workers bituminized the road surface.  工人们给路面涂上了沥青。
The intense heat bituminized the organic matter.  强烈的热量使有机物变成了沥青状。
近义词: asphalt, tar, pave
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "remove bitumen/asphalt")
词性: verb
