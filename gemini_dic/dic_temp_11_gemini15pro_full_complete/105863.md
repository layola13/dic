kitful: /ˈkɪtfʊl/
noun
满满一袋; 成套工具
A kitful is a bag full of a particular kind of thing, typically tools or equipment.
He carried a kitful of tools with him. 他随身带着满满一袋工具。
Synonyms: bagful, packful
Antonyms: empty bag, empty pack
词性: 名词
