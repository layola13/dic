electorally:/ɪˈlɛktərəli/| adv.|选举地，在选举方面|In a way that relates to elections.  This word describes actions, outcomes, or situations considered in the context of elections or electoral processes.| The party performed poorly electorally.  该党在选举中表现不佳。|  The new policy is electorally advantageous. 这项新政策在选举方面是有利的。|synonym:  politically, by election |antonym: non-electorally (not a standard word, but conveys the opposite meaning) |词性: adverb
