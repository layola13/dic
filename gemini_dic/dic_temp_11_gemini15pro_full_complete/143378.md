outfenced:/ˌaʊtˈfɛnst/
verb
胜过；以击剑战胜；以篱笆围住
defeated in a fencing match; enclosed or surrounded with a fence.
He was easily outfenced by his opponent. 他轻易地被对手以击剑击败。
The garden was outfenced to keep out rabbits. 花园用篱笆围起来以防兔子进入。
