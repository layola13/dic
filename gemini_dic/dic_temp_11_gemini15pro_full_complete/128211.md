neurobiotactic: /ˌnʊəroʊˌbaɪoʊˈtæktɪk/
adjective
神经趋向性的
Relating to or exhibiting neurobiotaxis, the tendency of developing or regenerating nerve fibers to grow toward their normal target or destination.
Neurobiotactic cues guide the axons of developing neurons.  神经趋向性线索引导发育中神经元的轴突。
The neurobiotactic process is crucial for the proper wiring of the nervous system. 神经趋向性过程对于神经系统的正确连接至关重要。
近义词: neurotropic
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps one could consider terms like "neurorepulsive")
词性: adjective
