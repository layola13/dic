earnestly:/ˈɜːrnɪstli/ (US) /ˈɜːnɪstli/ (UK)| adv.|认真地，诚挚地|In a serious and sincere manner, showing deep sincerity and conviction.|She listened earnestly to his explanation. 她认真地听着他的解释。|He earnestly desired to make amends for his past mistakes. 他真诚地渴望弥补过去的错误。|近义词：sincerely, seriously, gravely, solemnly|反义词：frivolously, lightly, carelessly|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *ernesly, equivalent to earnest +‎ -ly.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *arnustaz (“seriousness, zeal”).  Cognate with Old Frisian ernustli, Old Saxon ernustlīko, Old High German arnustlīhho, German ernstlich.
