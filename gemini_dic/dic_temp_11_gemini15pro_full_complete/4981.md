alkalizer: /ˈælkəˌlaɪzər/ | n. | 碱化剂；抗酸剂 | A substance that reduces acidity, especially in the body. | Example 1: She takes an alkalizer to relieve her heartburn. 她服用抗酸剂来缓解胃灼热。

Example 2:  The gardener added an alkalizer to the soil to balance the pH. 园丁在土壤中添加了碱化剂以平衡pH值。 | Synonyms: antacid, neutralizer, base | Antonyms: acidifier, acid | Noun
