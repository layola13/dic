inveighing:/ɪnˈveɪɪŋ/
| v. | 痛斥，猛烈抨击 | speaking or writing bitterly about something or someone |  He was inveighing against the evils of modern society. 他在痛斥现代社会的弊病。 | synonyms: denounce, condemn, criticize, rail against | antonyms: praise, commend, laud, extol | verb
