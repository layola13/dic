insouciance:/ɪnˈsuːsiəns/| n.|无忧无虑；漫不经心；漠不关心|Lack of concern; carefree indifference.  A feeling of not worrying about anything.|She met the news of her job loss with surprising insouciance.|Synonyms: nonchalance, indifference, unconcern, lightheartedness, carelessness|Antonyms: anxiety, worry, concern, solicitude, nervousness|noun
