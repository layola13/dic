lackadaisical: /ˌlækəˈdeɪzɪkəl/ | adj. | 懒散的，无精打采的 | Lacking enthusiasm and determination; carelessly lazy. |  He had a lackadaisical approach to his work, which often resulted in mistakes. 他对工作懒散的态度经常导致错误。|  The team's lackadaisical performance cost them the game. 球队无精打采的表现让他们输掉了比赛。 | Synonyms: listless, lethargic, apathetic, indolent, sluggish | Antonyms: energetic, enthusiastic, motivated, diligent, vigorous | adjective

Etymology:
From lackaday (expressing mild dismay or regret) + -ical (forming adjectives).  "Lackaday" is an archaic exclamation, a shortening of "alack the day."
