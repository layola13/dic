chartreuse:/ʃɑːrˈtruːz/ /ʃɑːrˈtuːs/
| n. adj.| 黄绿色; 查特勒酒; |A vibrant yellowish-green colour, similar to the colour of Chartreuse liqueur.  Can be used as a noun to refer to the colour itself, or as an adjective to describe something of that colour.|
The walls were painted a striking chartreuse. 墙壁被漆成了醒目的黄绿色。
She wore a chartreuse dress to the party. 她穿了一件黄绿色的连衣裙去参加聚会。
|近义词：lime green, yellowish-green|反义词: maroon, burgundy|词性: noun, adjective
