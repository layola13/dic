formalises:/ˈfɔːməlaɪzɪz/
verb
使正式化；正式确定
makes something official or sets it out in a definite form.
The agreement was formalised in a written contract. 该协议以书面合同的形式正式确定。
The new regulations formalise the existing practices. 新规章使现有的做法正式化。
Synonyms: codify, systematize, regularize, standardize
Antonyms: informalize
词性: 动词
