incorporator:/ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtər/ |n.| 公司创办人；注册登记人 | A person who incorporates a company or organization.  A person who applies for and receives a corporate charter from a state on behalf of a company. | Example 1: The incorporator signed the articles of incorporation. 公司创办人签署了公司章程。

Example 2: The state requires the incorporator to provide certain information. 州政府要求公司注册登记人提供某些信息。 | Synonym: founder, organizer, charter member | Antonym: N/A | Noun
