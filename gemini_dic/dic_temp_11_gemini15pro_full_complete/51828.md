dewired:/diˈwaɪərd/
adj.
去除线路的，拆除线路的，切断线路的
Having had wires or wiring removed.  The bomb was dewired, rendering it harmless. 炸弹的线路被拆除了，使其变得无害。
The building was dewired before demolition.  这栋楼在拆除之前被切断了线路。
近义词: disconnected, dismantled
反义词: wired, connected
词性: 形容词

词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + "wired" (past participle of "wire," meaning "to install wires")
