cryonically:/kraɪˈɒnɪkli/
adverb
低温地; 冷冻地
in a way that involves or relates to cryonics, the practice of freezing the body of a person who has died, in the hope that it may be possible in the future to bring them back to life
例句：His body was cryonically preserved. 他的遗体被低温保存。
例句：She hopes to be cryonically frozen after death. 她希望死后被冷冻保存。
近义词:  by freezing, at low temperatures
反义词:  (no direct antonym, consider) at room temperature, naturally
词性: adverb
