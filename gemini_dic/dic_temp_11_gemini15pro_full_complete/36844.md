colonizers:/ˈkɑːlənaɪzərz/ | n. |殖民者|People who establish a colony or colonies in a new territory.|The early colonizers faced many hardships. 早期的殖民者面临许多困难。|The colonizers exploited the resources of the new land. 殖民者开发了新土地的资源。|近义词：settlers, pioneers|反义词:indigenous people, natives|词性:noun

colonizer:/ˈkɑːlənaɪzər/ | n. |殖民者|A person who settles in a new region or establishes control over it, often displacing or subjugating the indigenous population.|European colonizers arrived in the Americas in the 15th century. 欧洲殖民者在15世纪抵达美洲。|The colonizer imposed their own laws and customs on the local people. 殖民者把他们自己的法律和习俗强加于当地人民。|近义词：settler, pioneer, imperialist|反义词:native, indigenous person|词性:noun
