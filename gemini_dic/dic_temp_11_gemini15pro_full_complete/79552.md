glomeration:/ɡləˌmərˈeɪʃən/
n.
聚集，凝聚；glomerate
A large cluster of something, especially buildings or businesses.  A mass formed by gathering or mingling things together.
The city is a glomeration of various cultures and ethnicities.  这座城市是各种文化和种族的聚集地。
The urban glomeration includes the city center and surrounding suburbs.  这个都市圈包括市中心和周围的郊区。
cluster, aggregation, collection, concentration, mass
dispersion, scattering
