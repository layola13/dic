chamade:/ʃəˈmɑːd/ | n. | 喇叭声；（尤指）停战或投降的信号；恳求，哀求 | A signal, especially by drum or trumpet, for a parley or surrender.  A fervent appeal or entreaty. | The besieged fortress sounded a chamade, indicating their willingness to negotiate.  被围困的堡垒吹响了停战号，表示他们愿意谈判。 |  近义词:  parley, truce, appeal, plea, entreaty | 反义词: attack, assault | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From French *chamade*, from Spanish *llamada* "call", from *llamar* "to call," ultimately from Latin *clamāre* "to shout."
