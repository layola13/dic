holometabolic:/ˌhɒləʊmɛtəˈbɒlɪk/ | adj. | 全变态的 | Undergoing complete metamorphosis, i.e., having a larval stage morphologically distinct from the adult, as in butterflies, moths, beetles, and flies. | Butterflies are holometabolic insects. 蝴蝶是全变态昆虫。| The beetle undergoes holometabolic development. 甲虫经历全变态发育。| 近义词: complete metamorphosis | 反义词: hemimetabolic | 词性: adjective

词源:
From holo- + metabolic.  Holo- comes from Greek hólos "whole, entire, complete."  Metabolic comes from Greek metabole "change."
