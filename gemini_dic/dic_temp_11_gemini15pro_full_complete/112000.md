loner:/ˈloʊnər/ (US) /ˈləʊnə(r)/ (UK)| n. |独来独往的人，喜欢独处的人|A person who prefers to be alone and avoids the company of others.  A person who is often alone and does not have many friends. |He was always a loner, preferring his own company to that of others. 他一直是个独来独往的人，喜欢自己一个人待着，胜过与他人相处。

She's a bit of a loner and doesn't really socialize with her colleagues. 她有点独来独往，不太与同事们交往。|近义词：solitary, recluse, introvert|反义词：extrovert, social butterfly|词性: 名词

词源: lone (adj.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting persons)  From Middle English lone, from Old English ānlica “only, solitary,” from ān “one.” The suffix -er forms agent nouns.
