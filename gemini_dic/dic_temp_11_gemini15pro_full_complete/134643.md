nonmalignancies:/ˌnɒn.məˈlɪɡ.nən.siz/
n.
非恶性肿瘤；良性肿瘤
Medical conditions or growths that are not cancerous and do not spread to other parts of the body.
The doctor assured her that the lump was one of the nonmalignancies. 医生向她保证肿块是良性的。
近义词: benign tumors, benign growths
反义词: malignancies, cancers
词性: noun (plural)


nonmalignancy:/ˌnɒn.məˈlɪɡ.nən.si/
n.
非恶性；良性
The state or quality of not being malignant; benignity.
The biopsy confirmed the nonmalignancy of the tumor. 活检证实了肿瘤的良性。
近义词: benignity, innocence
反义词: malignancy, cancerousness
词性: noun (singular)
