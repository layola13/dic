lapidarian:/ˌlæpɪˈdɛəriən/
adjective
简洁的；刻板的；言简意赅的
Relating to the inscription or engraving of stones; concise, terse, or severe in style or expression.
His lapidarian prose style perfectly suited the subject matter. 他的简洁的散文风格非常适合这个主题。

The inscription on the tombstone was lapidarian in its brevity. 墓碑上的铭文简洁扼要。

近义词:concise, terse, succinct, pithy, epigrammatic, brief, compact, curt, incisive
反义词:verbose, prolix, wordy, lengthy, rambling, diffuse, long-winded, circumlocutory

词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lapidarius* "of stone," from *lapis* "stone."
