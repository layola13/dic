outpursued:/ˌaʊtpərˈsuːd/
verb
超越追赶，胜过
To pursue something or someone beyond a certain point or limit; to surpass in pursuing.
He outpursued his rivals in the race and won by a large margin. 他在比赛中超越了对手，并以很大的优势获胜。
Synonyms: outstrip, outrun, surpass, exceed
Antonyms: lag behind, trail, fall behind
词性: 动词
