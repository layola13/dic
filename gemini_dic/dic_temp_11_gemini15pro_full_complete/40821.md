convoyed:/kənˈvɔɪd/
verb
护送，陪送
acted as an escort for (someone or something), especially for protection.
The troops convoyed the refugees to the border.  部队护送难民到边境。
The ship was convoyed across the Atlantic. 这艘船被护航穿过大西洋。
synonyms: escorted, accompanied, guarded, protected, shepherded
antonyms: abandoned, deserted, forsaken

词性: v. (verb, 动词)
