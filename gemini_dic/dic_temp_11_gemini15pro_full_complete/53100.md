diphthongising:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡaɪzɪŋ/ /daɪˈfθɔːŋɡaɪzɪŋ/
verb
双元音化
The process of making a single vowel sound into a diphthong.
The Great Vowel Shift in English involved a lot of diphthongising of previously pure vowels.  英语中的元音大推移涉及到许多先前纯元音的双元音化。
This dialect is known for diphthongising its long vowels.  这种方言以其长元音双元音化而闻名。
近义词:  vocalizing
反义词:  monophthongizing
词性:  v.
