bejape: /bɪˈdʒeɪp/
verb
欺骗，愚弄
to deceive or trick someone, often in a playful or mischievous way
He bejaped his sister into believing there was a monster under her bed. 他欺骗他的妹妹，让她相信她床底下有怪物。
trick, deceive, fool, hoax, dupe
