nondepressing:/ˌnɑːndɪˈprɛsɪŋ/ | adj. | 非令人沮丧的，不抑郁的 | Not causing or characterized by depression; uplifting or cheerful. |  The bright, sunny room was nondepressing. 明亮、阳光充足的房间并不令人沮丧。|  The movie was surprisingly nondepressing despite its serious subject matter. 尽管这部电影主题严肃，但出人意料地并不令人沮丧。 | Synonyms: uplifting, cheering, encouraging, positive, optimistic | Antonyms: depressing, gloomy, disheartening, discouraging, pessimistic | adjective

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + depressing (present participle of depress, from Latin depressus, past participle of deprimere "to press down").
