cowpoke:/ˈkaʊpoʊk/| n. |牛仔；牧童|A cowpoke is an informal term for a cowboy, someone who herds and tends cattle.|The cowpoke rode his horse across the dusty plains. 牛仔骑着马穿过尘土飞扬的平原。|近义词：cowboy, cowhand, rancher, herdsman|反义词: None (This is an occupational term, not a descriptive one with a direct opposite).|noun
