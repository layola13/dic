approval: /əˈpruːvəl/ | n. | 批准，认可，同意 | the belief that someone or something is good or acceptable | The project has now received approval from the government.  该项目现已获得政府批准。| 近义词: consent, acceptance, sanction, ratification | 反义词: disapproval, rejection, condemnation | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin approbatio(n-), from approbare ‘approve’.
