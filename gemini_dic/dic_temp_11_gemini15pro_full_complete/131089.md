nonbeneficially:/ˌnɒnbɛnɪˈfɪʃəli/
adverb
非有利地，无益地
in a way that does not produce any benefits or advantages.
The funds were used nonbeneficially, resulting in a loss for the investors. 资金的使用没有带来任何好处，反而给投资者造成了损失。
The policy change affected the company nonbeneficially. 这项政策变化对公司产生了不利影响。
近义词: detrimentally, harmfully, disadvantageously
反义词: beneficially, advantageously, profitably
词性: adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of
beneficial (adjective): producing good results or effects
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
