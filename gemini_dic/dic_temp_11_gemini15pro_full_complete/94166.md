impenitency: /ɪmˈpɛnɪtənsi/
impenitency| n.|不悔悟，顽固，执迷不悟|The impenitency of the criminal shocked the judge. |例句：His impenitency made it difficult to forgive him. 他的顽固不化使得很难原谅他。

例句：The preacher spoke of the dangers of impenitency. 牧师谈到了顽固不化的危险。

近义词：obduracy, hardness, intransigence, recalcitrance
反义词：remorse, penitence, contrition
词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *impaenitentia, from im- 'not' + paenitentia 'repentance'.
