inferno:/ɪnˈfɜːrnoʊ/ (US), /ɪnˈfɜːnəʊ/ (UK)| n. |地狱；火海；阴间|A large fire that is dangerously out of control.|The whole city was an inferno last night after the earthquake.|近义词：blaze, conflagration, firestorm|反义词:paradise, heaven|词性:名词
Etymology: From Italian *inferno*, literally “hell,” from Latin *īnfernus*  "below, underground, of the lower world," from *īnferus*,  comparative of *īnfra* "below."
