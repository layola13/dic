dadas:/ˈdɑːdɑːz/ |n. |达达主义（艺术）|A European art movement that flourished between 1916 and 1923. Dadaism rejected reason and logic, prizing nonsense, irrationality, and intuition. | The dada movement emerged as a reaction to the horrors of World War I. 达达运动是对第一次世界大战的恐怖的一种反应。|  The dadaists' embrace of absurdity challenged traditional artistic conventions. 达达主义者对荒谬的拥护挑战了传统的艺术惯例。|  Synonyms: Dadaism | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional art") | noun
