foreword:/ˈfɔːrwɜːrd/ (US) /ˈfɔːwəd/ (UK)| n.|前言，序言|A short introductory section of a book, typically written by someone other than the author.|Example:  The foreword of the biography was written by a close friend of the subject.  传记的序言是由这位主人公的一位密友撰写的。

Example: I skipped the foreword and went straight to the first chapter. 我跳过了前言，直接看第一章。|近义词：preface, introduction, prologue|反义词: afterword, epilogue|词性:noun
