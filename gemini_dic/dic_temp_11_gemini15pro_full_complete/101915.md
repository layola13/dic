Can also be used as an adjective to describe something related to this ideology. |  He's been described as an Islamist militant. 他被描述为一名伊斯兰武装分子。|  The Islamist group claimed responsibility for the attack.  这个伊斯兰组织声称对这次袭击负责。 |Synonyms: Islamic fundamentalist, jihadist (often used pejoratively),  | Antonyms: secularist, moderate Muslim | noun, adjective
