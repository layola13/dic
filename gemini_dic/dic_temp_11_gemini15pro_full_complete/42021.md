costively:/ˈkɒstɪvli/| adv.|便秘地；迟缓地；吝啬地|In a manner characterized by constipation; slowly and with difficulty; reluctantly or stingily.|He spoke costively, as if each word were a precious gem.|近义词：reluctantly, stingily, sparingly, grudgingly|反义词：freely, generously, readily|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *costifly, from Old French costif (“constipated”), from Medieval Latin cōnstīpātīvus, from Latin cōnstīpātus (“pressed closely together”), past participle of cōnstīpō (“I press or crowd together”), from com- (“together”) + stīpō (“I press, pack, or cram”).
