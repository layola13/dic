defocuses:/diːˈfəʊkəsɪz/
|v.|使…散焦，使…模糊；分散(注意力等)|To cause something to lose focus or clarity, either literally (as with a lens) or figuratively (as with attention).|The photographer defocuses the background to emphasize the subject. 摄影师虚化了背景以突出主体。|blurs, softens, obscures|focuses, sharpens, clarifies|verb
