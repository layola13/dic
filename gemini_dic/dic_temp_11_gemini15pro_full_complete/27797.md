cannonaded:/ˌkænəˈneɪdɪd/
verb
炮轰，猛烈攻击
bombarded heavily with cannon fire, or figuratively, with something else (e.g., questions, criticism)
The fort was cannonaded for three days before surrendering.  堡垒被炮轰了三天后才投降。
The speaker was cannonaded with questions from the audience. 演讲者被观众的提问轰炸。
bombard, shell, batter, blitz
--- (no clear antonym, perhaps "protected," "defended")
