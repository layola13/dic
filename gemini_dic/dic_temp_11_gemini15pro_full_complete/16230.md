banquetings:/ˈbæŋkwɪtɪŋz/
noun
宴会, 宴请
The act of feasting; a series of feasts.  Specifically, elaborate and often ceremonious meals for numerous people.
The king held numerous banquetings during the holiday season. 国王在节日期间举行了多次宴会。
近义词: feasting, celebrating, entertaining
反义词: fasting, starving
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
From Middle English *banquetynge*, from Anglo-Norman *banqueter*, from Old French *banquet*, diminutive of *banc* ("bench, table").  Originally referring to a small bench or table for refreshments, the meaning extended to the refreshments themselves and ultimately to elaborate feasts.
