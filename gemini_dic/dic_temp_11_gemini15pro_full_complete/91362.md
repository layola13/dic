hydrosorbic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈsɔːrbɪk/ | adj. | 氢山梨酸的；氢化的山梨酸 |  Relating to or derived from sorbic acid by hydrogenation. | Food preservatives often contain hydrosorbic acid.  食品防腐剂通常含有氢山梨酸。|  Synonyms: hexenoic acid, 2-propenyl acrylic acid. | Antonyms: None commonly used. | Adjective
