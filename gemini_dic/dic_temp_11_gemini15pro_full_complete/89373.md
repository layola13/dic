homoorgan:/ˌhoʊmoʊˈɔːrɡən/ | n. | 同种器官 | An organ transplanted between genetically identical individuals (identical twins or clones).  Derived from "homo-" (same) and "organ". |  Identical twins can receive homoorgan transplants without the risk of rejection. 同卵双胞胎可以接受同种器官移植，而不会有排斥反应。|  Isograft | Allograft, xenograft | noun
