onerously:/ˈəʊnərəsli/ /ˈɑːnərəsli/| adv.|沉重地；繁重地；艰巨地|in a way that involves a lot of effort and difficulty|例句：The regulations impact onerously on small businesses. 这些规章制度沉重地影响着小型企业。|例句：He was onerously burdened with debts. 他背负着沉重的债务。|近义词：burdensomely, heavily, oppressively|反义词: lightly, easily|词性:副词

词源:
From Late Latin *onerosus, from Latin onerosus (“burdensome, troublesome”), from onus (“burden, load”).
