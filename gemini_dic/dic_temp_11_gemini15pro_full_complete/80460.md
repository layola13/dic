gonidial:/ɡɒˈnɪdiəl/ /ɡəˈnaɪdiəl/| adj.|生殖的，芽体的|Relating to or producing gonidia; asexually reproductive.|Gonidial cells are crucial for the lichen's propagation. 芽体细胞对地衣的繁殖至关重要。|近义词: reproductive, asexual |反义词: sexual|adjective


gonidium:/ɡəˈnɪdiəm/ /ɡɑːˈnɪdiəm/| n.|芽体，无性繁殖细胞|An asexual reproductive cell, especially in algae, fungi, and lichens.|The gonidium is dispersed and grows into a new organism. 芽体被散布并生长成新的有机体。|近义词: spore, propagule|反义词: gamete|noun
