high-graded:/haɪˈɡreɪdɪd/
verb
提炼高品位矿石;  挑选出最好的; 优中选优
To remove the richest ore from a mine or the best items from a collection, leaving the remainder of lower quality.
The mine was high-graded during the gold rush, leaving only low-grade ore behind.  淘金热期间，矿山被提炼了高品位矿石，只剩下低品位矿石。
The art collection was high-graded over the years, with the best pieces being sold off. 多年来，艺术收藏品被优中选优，最好的作品都被卖掉了。
近义词: cherry-pick, cream, skim
反义词: dilute, downgrade
