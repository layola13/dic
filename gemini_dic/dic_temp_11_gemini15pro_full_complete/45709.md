curvature:/ˈkɜːrvətʃər/| n. |曲率，弯曲，曲度|The curvature of the lens affects the focus of the image.  镜片的曲率会影响图像的焦距。|例句：The road has a sharp curvature. 这条路弯度很大。  The curvature of the earth is not readily apparent. 地球的曲率不容易察觉。|近义词：bend, curve, arc, bow|反义词:straightness, flatness|词性:noun

curvative:/ˈkɜːrvətɪv/| adj. |弯曲的，有曲线的| Its curvative shape allows it to fit snugly. 它的弯曲形状使它能够紧密贴合。 |例句：The artist employed curvative lines in his drawing.  这位艺术家在他的画中使用了曲线。 The building has a distinctive curvative design. 这栋建筑有一个独特的曲线设计。|近义词：curved, bending, bowed|反义词:straight, linear|词性:adjective
