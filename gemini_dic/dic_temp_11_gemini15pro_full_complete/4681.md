alepidote:/eɪˈlɛpɪdoʊt/ | adj. | 无鳞片的；(指岩石)不含鳞片状矿物的 | Lacking scales or lepidote structures, especially referring to rocks that do not contain scaly minerals like mica.  | The alepidote rhododendrons are easier to maintain than the lepidote varieties.  无鳞片的杜鹃花比有鳞片的品种更容易维护。| Synonyms: scaleless, non-lepidote | Antonyms: lepidote, scaly | adjective


alepidote:/eɪˈlɛpɪdoʊt/ | n. | 无鳞片植物/动物 | A plant or animal lacking scales or lepidote structures. |  Some species of earthworms are considered alepidotes.  一些种类的蚯蚓被认为是无鳞片的。 | Synonyms: scaleless creature/plant | Antonyms: lepidote organism | noun
