honestnesses:/ˈɑːnɪstnɪsɪz/
n.
诚实，正直
the quality of being honest
例句：He was admired for his honesty and integrity. 他因诚实和正直而受人钦佩。

例句：Her honestnesses shone through in her every action. 她的诚实体现在她的一举一动中。
近义词：integrity, uprightness, probity, sincerity, truthfulness
反义词：dishonesty, deceit, duplicity
词性: 名词


词源:  From Middle English *oneste, borrowed from Old French oneste, honeste, from Latin honestus (“honorable, respectable, dignified”), from honos (“honor, reputation, office”).
