confederationisms:/kənˌfɛdəˈreɪʃənɪzəmz/ | n. | 邦联主义 |  Theories or systems of political organization based on the principle of confederation, emphasizing the autonomy of individual units within a larger alliance or union. | The rise of confederationisms in the 19th century reflected a desire for greater regional autonomy. 19世纪邦联主义的兴起反映了人们对更大区域自治的渴望。|  federalisms, regionalisms | centralism, unitarianism | noun
