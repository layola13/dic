delightingly:/dɪˈlaɪtfʊli/| adv.|令人愉快地，欢快地|In a way that gives great pleasure; charmingly.|She sang delightfully. 她唱得令人愉快。|近义词：charmingly, pleasantly, wonderfully, joyfully|反义词：unpleasantly, disagreeably|词性:副词

词源: From delight + -ing + -ly.  "Delight" comes from the Old French "deleiter," from Latin "delectare," meaning "to allure, entice, charm."
