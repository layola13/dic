implored:/ɪmˈplɔːrd/| v. |恳求，哀求|To beg someone earnestly or desperately to do something.|She implored him to stay. 她恳求他留下。|He implored the judge for mercy. 他恳求法官开恩。|近义词：begged, pleaded, beseeched|反义词：demanded, commanded|词性:动词

implore:/ɪmˈplɔːr/| v. |恳求，哀求|From Old French *implorer, from Latin implorare "to call upon, invoke, beseech," from in- "on, upon" + plorare "to weep, cry out."|The stranded traveler implored passing cars for help.  受困的旅行者恳求过往的车辆帮忙。|The defendant implored the jury to believe his story.  被告恳求陪审团相信他的故事。|近义词：beg, plead, beseech, entreat|反义词：demand, command|词性:动词
