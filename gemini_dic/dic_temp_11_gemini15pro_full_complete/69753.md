ferguson:/ˈfɜːrɡəsən/ | n. |弗格森|A Scottish surname, also a place name in Scotland and the United States.  It is most commonly associated with Sir Alex Ferguson, the former manager of Manchester United football club.  It gained further notoriety in 2014 after the shooting of Michael Brown in Ferguson, Missouri, which sparked widespread protests about racial injustice and police brutality.|例句：Sir Alex Ferguson managed Manchester United for over 25 years.  弗格森爵士执教曼联超过25年。
