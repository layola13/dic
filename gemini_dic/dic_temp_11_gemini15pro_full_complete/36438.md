colature:/ˈkɒlətʃə(r)/ /ˈkɑːlətʃər/
n.
滤液；滤出物
The liquid that has passed through a filter.
The colature obtained from the coffee grounds was rich and flavorful. 从咖啡渣中滤出的滤液浓郁而 flavorful。
filtrate, effluent, leachate
