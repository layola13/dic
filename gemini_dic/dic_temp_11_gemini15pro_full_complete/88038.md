highbrowisms: /ˈhaɪˌbraʊɪzəmz/
noun
高雅文化；自命不凡的言论；高调言论
Pretentious, intellectual language or ideas often used to impress others.
He peppered his conversation with highbrowisms, hoping to impress his new colleagues. 他在谈话中穿插着高雅的言辞，希望能给新同事留下深刻印象。
synonyms: intellectualism, pretentiousness, pedantry, snobbishness
antonyms: simplicity, plainness, unpretentiousness, modesty
n.
