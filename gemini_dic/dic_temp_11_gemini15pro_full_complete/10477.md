applicative:/əˈplɪkətɪv/| adj.|适用的，可应用的|Relating to or involving application; that can be applied.|This research has applicative value in the field of medicine. 这项研究在医学领域具有应用价值。|近义词:applicable, relevant, pertinent, practical|反义词:inapplicable, irrelevant, impractical|词性:adjective
applicative:/əˈplɪkətɪv/| n.|应用函子 (functional programming)|In functional programming, an applicative functor is a higher-order type constructor that allows applying a function wrapped in a functor to values wrapped in the same functor.|The applicative allows sequencing computations within a context. 应用函子允许在一个上下文中对计算进行排序。|近义词:functor, monad|反义词:None (specialized term)|词性:noun
