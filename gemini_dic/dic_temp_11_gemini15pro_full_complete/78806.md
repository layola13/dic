gimbals: /ˈɡɪmbəlz/ | n. | 万向节; 平衡环; 常复数 |A device consisting of two or three concentric rings or hoops so pivoted that an object mounted on the innermost ring can remain level or oriented in a specific direction, regardless of the motion of the supporting structure. | The gyroscope is mounted in gimbals so that it can rotate freely in any direction.  陀螺仪安装在万向节中，以便它可以沿任何方向自由旋转。

The camera stabilizer uses gimbals to keep the footage steady.  相机稳定器使用万向节来保持画面稳定。 | synonyms:  pivot, swivel, bearing, mount, support | antonyms: fixed, immobile | noun


gimbals: /ˈɡɪmbəlz/ | v. | 用平衡环装置; 使装上平衡环 | To mount (something) on gimbals.  | They gimbaled the compass to keep it level. 他们用平衡环安装了指南针以保持其水平。

The telescope was gimbaled to allow for precise tracking of celestial objects.  望远镜装有万向节，可以精确跟踪天体。| synonyms:  suspend, balance, stabilize, mount  | antonyms:  fix, secure | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gimmal, from Old French gemel, gemeau (“twin”), from Latin gemellus (“twin”).  The original gimbals were often made with twinned rings.
