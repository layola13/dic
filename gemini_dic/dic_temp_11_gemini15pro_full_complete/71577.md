flashings:/ˈflæʃɪŋz/
n.
(金属板的)泛光, 闪光;  防水盖片; (屋顶、烟囱等的)遮雨板
Pieces of metal used to prevent water from entering the junction of a roof with another surface.

The flashings around the chimney need to be replaced.
烟囱周围的防水板需要更换。

The builder installed new lead flashings around the dormer window.
建筑工人给老虎窗周围安装了新的铅制防水盖片。


Synonyms: flashing (singular), weatherproofing, cladding
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider concepts like leaks, gaps, exposed)
词性: noun


flashing:/ˈflæʃɪŋ/
n.
(金属板的)泛光, 闪光
A sudden bright light.

I saw a flashing in the distance, perhaps lightning.
我看到远处有闪光，可能是闪电。


Synonyms: gleam, glimmer, sparkle
Antonyms: dullness
词性: noun


flashing:/ˈflæʃɪŋ/
adj.
闪光的, 闪烁的
Shining brightly in quick flashes.

The flashing lights of the police car caught my attention.
警车的闪光灯引起了我的注意。

She wore a dress with flashing sequins.
她穿着一件缀着闪亮亮片的连衣裙。


Synonyms: sparkling, glittering, twinkling
Antonyms: dull, dim
词性: adjective
