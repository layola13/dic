crosspollination:/ˌkrɒsˌpɒləˈneɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌkrɔːsˌpɑːləˈneɪʃn/ (US)| n. |异花授粉；交叉传粉；（思想、文化等的）相互影响|The crosspollination of ideas between different cultures can lead to exciting new developments. 不同文化之间思想的相互影响可以带来令人兴奋的新发展。|例句：The crosspollination of ideas between the two companies resulted in a highly innovative product.  两家公司思想的相互影响造就了一款高度创新的产品。

例句：Crosspollination is essential for many fruit trees to bear fruit. 异花授粉对于许多果树的结实至关重要。|近义词：cross-fertilization, hybridization|反义词: self-pollination|词性:noun
