counterscale:/ˈkaʊntərˌskeɪl/| n.| 对称物，平衡力，对比物| something that has an equal but opposite effect to something else; a contrasting element that produces a balance or modifies an impression | The bright colors of the flowers were a welcome counterscale to the drab walls.  这些鲜艳的花朵与单调的墙壁形成了令人愉悦的对比。 |近义词：counterbalance, offset, antithesis |反义词: similarity, likeness | noun

counterscale:/ˈkaʊntərˌskeɪl/| v.| 平衡，抵消 | to act as a counterbalance to something; to offset or compensate for something | His natural exuberance is counterscaled by his wife's quiet reserve. 他天生的热情奔放与他妻子安静的矜持相互平衡。 | 近义词：counterbalance, offset, compensate | 反义词: enhance, amplify | verb
