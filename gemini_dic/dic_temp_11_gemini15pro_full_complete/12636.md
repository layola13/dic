assaulters:/əˈsɔːltərz/
n.
攻击者；袭击者

The assaulters were apprehended by the police. 攻击者被警察逮捕了。
Several assaulters were caught on camera. 几个攻击者被摄像头拍到了。
attackers, assailants
victims, defenders
