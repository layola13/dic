natural:/ˈnætʃrəl/ (US) /ˈnætʃ(ə)rəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|自然的；天然的；正常的；天生的；不做作的|Existing in or caused by nature; not made or caused by humankind.  Innate; inherent.|例句：It's natural to feel nervous before an exam.  考试前感到紧张是很自然的。|近义词：normal, regular, typical, innate, inherent|反义词：artificial, unnatural, synthetic|词性:形容词,名词

natural:/ˈnætʃrəl/ (US) /ˈnætʃ(ə)rəl/ (UK)| n.|天生具有某种才能的人|A person who is naturally skilled or talented in something.|例句：He's a natural at playing the piano. 他天生就是弹钢琴的料。|近义词：talent, gifted person|反义词:beginner, amateur|词性:名词
