cream-of-the-crop:/kriːm əv ðə krɒp/ |n.|精英；佼佼者；最佳人选|The graduates of this program are the cream of the crop. 这个项目的毕业生都是精英。|Synonym: best of the best, elite, pick of the bunch|Antonym: dregs, riffraff|Noun

cream-of-the-crop:/kriːm əv ðə krɒp/ |adj.|最好的；最优秀的|These strawberries are cream of the crop. 这些草莓是最好的。|Synonym: top-notch, first-rate, premium|Antonym: inferior, second-rate, substandard|Adjective

词源: This idiom alludes to the cream rising to the top of milk.  The best is similarly thought to rise to the top of any group or class.  这个习语指的是奶油上升到牛奶的顶部。同样，最好的人也被认为会上升到任何群体或阶级的顶部。
