misdeclared:/ˌmɪsdɪˈkleərd/ | adj. | 申报错误的，虚报的 | Incorrectly or falsely declared, especially in relation to goods or income for official purposes (such as customs or taxes). | The customs officials seized the goods because they were misdeclared. 海关官员扣押了这些货物，因为它们申报不实。|  The businessman was fined for misdeclaring his income.  这个商人因虚报收入而被罚款。 | misstated, falsified | correctly declared, truthfully declared | adjective
