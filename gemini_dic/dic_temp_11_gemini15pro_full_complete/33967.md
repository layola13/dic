cisleithan:/sɪsˈlaɪθən/
adjective
adj. 莱塔尼亚的；内莱塔尼亚的
Relating to the part of Austria-Hungary west of the Leitha River, comprising Austria proper, Bohemia, Moravia, and Austrian Silesia.
The cisleithan government struggled to maintain control amidst rising nationalist tensions. 内莱塔尼亚政府在民族主义紧张局势日益加剧的情况下努力维持控制。
The cisleithan provinces were more industrially developed than the transleithan ones. 内莱塔尼亚的省份比外莱塔尼亚的省份工业化程度更高。
Synonym: Austrian
Antonym: Transleithan
词性: 形容词
