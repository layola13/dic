consistent:/kənˈsɪstənt/| adj.|一致的，始终如一的，连续的|Acting or behaving in the same way over time, especially so as to be fair or accurate.  Remaining the same in behaviour, attitudes, or qualities.|She has been a consistent supporter of the arts. 她一直是艺术的坚定支持者。|synonyms:  steady, regular, uniform, stable, unchanging, invariable, unvarying|antonyms: inconsistent, erratic, fluctuating, changeable, variable|adjective

consistent:/kənˈsɪstənt/| adj.|相符的，吻合的，符合的|Agreeing with something; compatible. In agreement with other facts or with previous statements or beliefs.|His story is consistent with the police report. 他的说法与警方报告一致。|synonyms: compatible, congruous, consonant, in agreement, in accordance, corresponding, tallying|antonyms: inconsistent, incompatible, incongruous, conflicting, contradictory|adjective
