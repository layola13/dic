illiberalness:/ɪˈlɪbərəlnəs/
|n.|偏狭; 不开明; 心胸狭窄|The state or quality of being illiberal; narrow-mindedness or intolerance.|His illiberalness prevented him from accepting new ideas. 他的狭隘使他无法接受新思想。|近义词: intolerance, narrow-mindedness, bigotry, prejudice|反义词: tolerance, open-mindedness, liberality|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From illiberal + -ness.  Illiberal comes from in- (not) + liberal (generous, open-minded).  The root of "liberal" traces back to Latin "liber" (free).
