brachyceral:/brækɪˈsɪrəl/
adjective
短头颅的
Having a short broad head, especially a skull that is shorter than it is wide.
Brachycephalic breeds, like Bulldogs, are prone to breathing problems.  像斗牛犬这样的短头颅犬种容易出现呼吸问题。
近义词:short-nosed, short-muzzled
反义词:dolichocephalic
词性:adjective
