deviators:/ˈdiːvieɪtərz/
n.
偏差者，偏离者，背离者
Those who deviate from a norm, standard, or expectation.
The deviators in the study showed a marked difference in their responses.  这项研究中的偏差者在他们的反应中表现出明显的差异。
The deviators from the party line were quickly silenced.  偏离党的路线的人很快就被噤声了。
synonyms: nonconformists, mavericks, rebels, outliers, eccentrics
antonyms: conformists, followers, traditionalists
noun

deviate:/ˈdiːvieɪt/
v.
偏离，背离
To stray from a norm, course, or principle.
The plane deviated from its planned flight path due to bad weather.  由于恶劣天气，飞机偏离了预定的航线。
He never deviated from his strict vegetarian diet.  他从未偏离过他严格的素食饮食。
synonyms: stray, diverge, digress, veer
antonyms: conform, adhere, comply
verb
