axite:/ˈæksaɪt/| n.|斧石|A mineral of the pyroxene group, typically pale green or greyish-green in color. It is a silicate of magnesium, iron, and calcium.|The geologist identified the sample as axite.|近义词:diopside, augite|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek 'axinē' (axe) + '-ite' (suffix indicating a mineral).  Named for its typical cleavages, which resemble the blade of an axe.
