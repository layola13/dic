entomotomist:/ˌɛntəˈmɒtəmɪst/
n.
昆虫解剖学家
A person who dissects or studies the anatomy of insects.
The entomotomist carefully examined the insect's wing structure under the microscope.  昆虫解剖学家在显微镜下仔细检查了昆虫的翅膀结构。
近义词: insect anatomist
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

Etymology:
From entomo- (relating to insects) + -tomist (one who cuts or dissects).  Entomo- comes from the Greek éntomon "insect" and -tomist comes from the Greek tomē "a cutting."
