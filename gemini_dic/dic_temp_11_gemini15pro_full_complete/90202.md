hound:/haʊnd/| n. vt.|猎犬，追捕|A dog of a breed used for hunting, especially one able to track by scent.  A person who relentlessly pursues someone or something.|The police hounded the suspect across the country. 警察在全国范围内追捕嫌疑犯。|近义词：dog, tracker, pursue, hunt|反义词:prey, quarry|noun, verb
