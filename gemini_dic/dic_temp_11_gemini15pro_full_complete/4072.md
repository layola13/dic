aiguillette:/ˌeɪɡwɪˈlɛt/ | n. | aiguillette (美式), aiguillette (英式) |  A braided cord or string with metal tips, often ornamental, worn on uniforms or as part of a costume.  A pointed, needle-like piece of meat, especially of beef. | The general's uniform was adorned with gold aiguillettes. 将军的制服上装饰着金色的肩章。  | The chef prepared aiguillettes of beef tenderloin. 厨师准备了牛里脊肉。 | 近义词: cord, braid, lace (for the ornamental cord); strip, sliver (for the meat) | 反义词: None | noun
