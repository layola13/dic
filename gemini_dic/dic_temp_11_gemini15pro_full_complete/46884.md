daringness:/ˈdɛərɪŋnɪs/
n.
大胆；勇敢；胆量
The quality or state of being daring; boldness.
His daringness impressed everyone. 他的胆量让每个人都印象深刻。

近义词: audacity, boldness, bravery, courage, fearlessness, intrepidity, nerve, pluck, valor
反义词: timidity, cowardice, fearfulness, apprehension

词性: noun

词源: From daring + -ness.  Daring itself comes from the verb "dare," from Old English *durran, from Proto-Germanic *durzaną, meaning "to dare."
