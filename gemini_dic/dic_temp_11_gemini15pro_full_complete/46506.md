daises:/ˈdeɪziz/ | n. |雏菊|A common European wild flower with a yellow centre and white petals, similar to the oxeye daisy but smaller.  A type of flower with a round yellow centre and many white petals. |She picked a bunch of daises from the field. 她从田野里摘了一束雏菊。| The field was covered in white daises. 这片田野上开满了白色的雏菊。| synonyms: daisy, oxeye daisy, marguerite | antonyms: N/A | noun
