|n.|铹（一种人工合成的放射性化学元素）|A synthetic radioactive chemical element with the symbol Lr and atomic number 103.  It is named after Ernest Lawrence, inventor of the cyclotron.|Lawrencium is a highly radioactive element and is not found in nature. 铹是一种高放射性元素，在自然界中不存在。

Lawrencium isotopes have very short half-lives. 铹同位素的半衰期很短。|近义词: (无，因其特指)|反义词: (无，因其特指)|词性: noun
