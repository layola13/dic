accidentally: /ˌæksɪˈdɛntəli/ | adv. | 偶然地，意外地 | Happening by chance or unexpectedly; not planned or intended. | I accidentally deleted the file. 我不小心删除了文件。 |  I accidentally bumped into her at the grocery store. 我在杂货店不小心撞到了她。 | 近义词: unintentionally, by chance, inadvertently | 反义词: deliberately, intentionally, on purpose | 词性: 副词

词源: From the noun "accident" + "-al" (forming adjectives) + "-ly" (forming adverbs). Ultimately from Latin *accidens*, present participle of *accidere* "to happen".
