inflammableness:/ɪnˌflæməˈbɪlnəs/| n. |易燃性；易激动性|The quality of being easily set on fire or excited; flammability. |The inflammableness of the dry leaves made them a fire hazard. 干燥树叶的易燃性使它们存在火灾隐患。|Synonyms: flammability, combustibility|Antonyms: nonflammability, incombustibility|Noun
