caballeria:/ˌkɑːbəˈjɛriːə/ | n. |骑兵，骑士精神|A body of soldiers who serve on horseback, or the principles and practices of chivalry.|  The general led the caballeria into battle. 将军率领骑兵投入战斗。| The caballeria charged across the field. 骑兵冲过战场。 | 近义词: cavalry, horsemen, knights | 反义词: infantry, foot soldiers | 词性: noun
词源: From Spanish caballería, from caballero (“horseman, knight”), from Late Latin caballārius (“horseman”), from caballus (“horse”). Ultimately from Gaulish caballos.
