affirmative:/əˈfɜːmətɪv/| n.|肯定；赞成；肯定词|A word or statement expressing agreement or consent. |He answered in the affirmative. 他给予了肯定的答复。|Synonyms: yes, agreement, consent |Antonyms: negative, refusal|noun
