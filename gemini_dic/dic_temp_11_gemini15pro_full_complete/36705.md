colliquefaction:/ˌlɪkwɪˈfækʃən/| n. |液化，熔化|The process of making or becoming liquid.|The earthquake caused soil liquefaction, which led to the collapse of several buildings. 地震造成了土壤液化，导致几座建筑物倒塌。|近义词：melting, dissolving, thawing|反义词:solidification, freezing|noun
