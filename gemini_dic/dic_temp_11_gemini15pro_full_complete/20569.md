blackbird:/ˈblækˌbɜːrd/ (US) /ˈblækˌbɜːd/ (UK)| n.|乌鸫，黑鸟|A common European thrush (Turdus merula) the male of which is black with a yellow bill and a yellow ring around the eye, and the female of which is brown.|例句：A blackbird sang sweetly in the tree.  一只乌鸫在树上甜美地歌唱。|近义词：thrush, starling|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
blackbird:/ˈblækˌbɜːrd/ (US) /ˈblækˌbɜːd/ (UK)| n.|黑鸟（泛指）|Any of various birds having predominantly black plumage.|例句：Several different species are called blackbirds.  有几种不同的鸟类被称为黑鸟。|近义词：crow, raven|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
