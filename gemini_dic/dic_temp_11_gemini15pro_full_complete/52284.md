dichroic:/daɪˈkroʊɪk/ | adj. | 二色的，双色的，多向色的 | Exhibiting two colors, especially of transmitted light when viewed from different angles or by transmitted and reflected light. | The dichroic glass in the window shimmered with different colors as the sun moved across the sky.  窗户上的二向色玻璃随着太阳在天空中移动而闪烁着不同的颜色。| Synonyms: iridescent, opalescent | Antonyms: monochromatic | adjective


dichroic:/daɪˈkroʊɪk/ | n. | 二色性物质，二向色性材料 | A dichroic substance or material. | The artist used a dichroic filter to create a unique effect in their photograph.  艺术家使用二向色滤光片在他们的照片中创造了独特的艺术效果。| Synonyms: dichroic material, dichroic filter | Antonyms: N/A | noun
