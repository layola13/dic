blessed:/ˈblesɪd/ /ˈblest/ | adj.|神圣的，受祝福的，幸福的，该死的|Made holy; consecrated. Bringing happiness and peace. Used ironically or humorously to mean annoying or inconvenient.|例句：The priest blessed the bread and wine.  神父祝福了面包和葡萄酒。

例句：They were blessed with two healthy children. 他们有幸拥有两个健康的孩子。

近义词：sacred, holy, fortunate, lucky, blissful |反义词：cursed, damned, unfortunate, unhappy|词性:形容词

blessed:/ˈblesɪd/ /ˈblest/ | n.|受祝福的人|A person who is blessed.|例句：The blessed are those who mourn, for they will be comforted.  哀恸的人有福了，因为他们必得安慰。

例句：Count your blessings.  要珍惜你所拥有的一切。

近义词：saint |反义词：sinner|词性:名词
