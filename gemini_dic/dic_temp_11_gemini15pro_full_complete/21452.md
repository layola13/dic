blowout:/ˈbloʊaʊt/ (US) /ˈbləʊaʊt/ (UK)| n.| (非正式) 争吵;  口角| A blowout in this sense refers to a loud, angry argument. | We had a huge blowout about the holiday plans. 我们在假期计划上大吵了一架。
