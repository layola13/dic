faxing:/ˈfæksɪŋ/
n.|传真; 传真发送|the process of sending a document electronically over a phone line|I spent the morning faxing contracts to our clients. 我早上都在忙着把合同传真给客户。|telecopying, transmitting|printing
v.|传真|to send (a document) by fax|Please fax me the report as soon as possible. 请尽快把报告传真给我。|telecopy, transmit|receive, print
词性: 动名词/现在分词
