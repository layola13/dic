endive:/ˈɛndaɪv/ (US), /ˈɛndɪv/ (UK)| n.| 菊苣，苦苣|A leafy vegetable with a slightly bitter flavor, of which there are several varieties including frisée and escarole. It is often used in salads.|例句：I added some endive to my salad for a bit of bitterness. 我在我的沙拉里加了一些菊苣，增添一些苦味。

例句：The chef prepared a delicious dish featuring roasted endive and walnuts.  厨师准备了一道美味的烤菊苣核桃菜。|近义词：chicory, escarole, frisée|反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of vegetable)|词性:noun
