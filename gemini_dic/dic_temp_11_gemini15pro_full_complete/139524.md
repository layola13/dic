obtaining:/əbˈteɪnɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
获得，得到
The process of getting or acquiring something.
She is obtaining her degree in biology this year. 她今年将获得生物学学位。
They are obtaining the necessary permits for the construction. 他们正在获得建造所需的许可证。
synonyms: acquiring, gaining, procuring, earning, achieving
antonyms: losing, forfeiting, relinquishing
词性: 动词(现在分词)
