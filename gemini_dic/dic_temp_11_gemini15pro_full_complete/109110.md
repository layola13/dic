leggiero:/ledˈdʒɛːroʊ/ /lɛdˈdʒɛːroʊ/
adjective
轻快的，轻巧的
lightly, delicately, in a light and agile manner.  This Italian musical term indicates a light and rapid style of performance.
The pianist played the passage leggiero, making it sound almost ethereal. 钢琴家轻快地演奏了这段乐句，使它听起来几乎像空灵的。
The music called for a leggiero touch, a delicate dance of the fingers on the keys.  这段音乐需要轻巧的触键，手指在琴键上轻盈地舞动。
近义词:light, delicate, airy, nimble, swift
反义词:heavy, ponderous, slow
词性:adjective
词源:Italian, from *leggiero, from Latin *leviarius, from levis 'light'
