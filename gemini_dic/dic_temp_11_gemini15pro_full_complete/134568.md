nonlocalized:/ˌnɒnˈləʊkəlaɪzd/ /ˌnɑːnˈloʊkəˌlaɪzd/
adjective
非局部的，非定域的
Not confined or restricted to a particular location; spread out or dispersed.
The pain was nonlocalized, making it difficult to diagnose.  这种疼痛是非局部的，难以诊断。
Electrons in a metal are nonlocalized and can move freely throughout the material. 金属中的电子是非局域性的，可以在整个材料中自由移动。
近义词: diffuse, dispersed, spread out, widespread
反义词: localized, concentrated, focal, confined
词性: adjective
