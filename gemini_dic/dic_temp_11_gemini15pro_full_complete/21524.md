bluebeard:/ˈbluːbɪərd/| n.|蓝胡子, 凶残的丈夫|A man who marries and kills one wife after another.  A wife-killer.  Often refers to a man who has had several wives or partners, suggesting a sinister or dangerous personality.|例句：The story of Bluebeard is a cautionary tale about the dangers of unchecked power. 蓝胡子的故事是一个关于不受约束的权力之危险的警示故事。

例句：He gained a reputation as a bluebeard after his third wife mysteriously disappeared.  在他第三任妻子神秘失踪后，他获得了蓝胡子的名声。|近义词：wife-killer, tyrant, abuser|反义词:protector, guardian|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the French "Barbe bleue," referring to the fairytale character.  The fairytale likely has roots in earlier legends and historical figures, including Gilles de Rais, a 15th-century French nobleman and serial killer.
