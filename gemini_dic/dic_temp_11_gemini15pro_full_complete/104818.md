keckling:/ˈkɛklɪŋ/| n. vi.|咯咯笑；尖声笑|The act of laughing in a shrill, broken manner, often suggesting mockery or derision.|The old women were keckling like hens over some private joke. 老妇们像母鸡一样咯咯地笑着，谈论着私下的笑话。|近义词：cackling, giggling, chortling|反义词: sobbing, crying|词性:noun, verb

keckle:/ˈkɛkəl/| vi.|咯咯笑；尖声笑|To laugh in a shrill, broken manner, often suggesting mockery or derision.|He keckled with delight at his own joke. 他为自己开的玩笑高兴地咯咯笑了起来。|近义词：cackle, giggle, chortle|反义词: sob, cry|词性:verb

词源: From Middle English *kekelen, frequentative of keken (“to cackle”), of imitative origin.
