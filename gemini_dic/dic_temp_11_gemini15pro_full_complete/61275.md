emasculator:/ɪˈmæskjʊˌleɪtər/ | n. | 去势钳，阉割器 | A surgical instrument used for castration. |  例句：The veterinarian used an emasculator to castrate the bull. 兽医用去势钳阉割了公牛。

例句：The emasculator was sterilized before the procedure. 去势钳在手术前进行了消毒。| 近义词：castrator, gelder | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

emasculate:/ɪˈmæskjʊleɪt/ | vt. | 阉割；使柔弱；删节；削弱 | 1. To deprive of strength or vigor; weaken. 2. To castrate. 3. To edit (a text) so as to remove parts considered offensive. | 例句：The new law emasculated the power of the governor. 新法律削弱了州长的权力。

例句：Critics claimed the censorship had emasculated the play. 批评家声称审查制度已经阉割了这部戏剧。| 近义词：weaken, debilitate, enfeeble, castrate, neuter, expurgate | 反义词: strengthen, empower | 词性: verb


词源:

From Late Latin emasculatus, past participle of emasculare "to castrate, weaken," from Latin e- "out" + masculus "male."
