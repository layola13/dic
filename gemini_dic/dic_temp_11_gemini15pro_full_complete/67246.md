expunction:/ɪkˈspʌŋkʃən/| n.|删除；抹去；涂掉|The act of erasing or removing something completely, especially from a written record.|He requested the expunction of his criminal record. 他要求删除他的犯罪记录。|Synonyms: deletion, erasure, obliteration|Antonyms: addition, inclusion, insertion|noun

expunction:/ɪkˈspʌŋkʃən/| n.|消除；忘却|The act of eliminating something from one's memory or consciousness.|She sought expunction of the painful memories. 她试图忘却痛苦的回忆。|Synonyms: elimination, removal|Antonyms: remembrance, retention|noun
