falling-away:/ˈfɔːlɪŋ əˈweɪ/ | n. | 衰退，脱落，背离 | a gradual decrease or decline; the act of abandoning a belief, cause, or group |  The falling-away of subscribers has worried the magazine's publishers. 订阅用户数量的下降让杂志出版商感到担忧。| synonyms: decline, desertion, abandonment | antonyms: increase, growth, adherence | noun

falling-away:/ˈfɔːlɪŋ əˈweɪ/ | adj. | 衰退的，脱落的 | characterized by a gradual decrease or decline | The falling-away support for the political party led to its defeat.  对该政党日渐衰退的支持导致了它的失败。| synonyms: declining, diminishing | antonyms: increasing, growing | adjective
