indicative:/ɪnˈdɪkətɪv/| adj. n.|指示的，象征的；陈述语气|Serving as a sign or indication of something; relating to the mood of a verb that expresses factual statements.|Her smile was indicative of her good mood. 她的笑容表明她心情很好.|近义词：suggestive, representative, symbolic, denotative|反义词：unrevealing, concealing|词性:形容词

indicative:/ɪnˈdɪkətɪv/| n.|陈述语气|The mood of a verb that expresses factual statements.|The indicative mood is used for most ordinary sentences. 陈述语气用于大多数普通句子。|近义词：(没有直接的近义词，因为这是语法术语)|反义词：(没有直接的反义词，因为这是语法术语)|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin indicativus, from indicare ‘indicate’.
