lagophthalmos:/ˌlæɡɒfˈθælmɒs/ (US) /ˌlæɡɒfˈθælməs/ (UK)| n.|兔眼症，眼睑闭合不全|Lagophthalmos is a condition in which the eyelids cannot fully close.|The patient suffered from lagophthalmos after the surgery. 手术后病人患上了兔眼症。|Synonyms: lagophthalmia, inability to close eyelids|Antonyms: blepharospasm (眼睑痉挛)|noun
