fisherfolk:/ˈfɪʃərfəʊk/ (UK) /ˈfɪʃərfolk/ (US)| n.|渔民；渔夫（总称，尤指作为一个群体或社区）|People who fish, especially as a community or as a way of life.  A collective term, often emphasizing the traditional or community aspect of fishing.|例句：The fisherfolk rely on the sea for their livelihood. 渔民依靠大海维持生计。

例句：The local fisherfolk protested against the new fishing regulations. 当地的渔民抗议新的捕鱼规定。

近义词：fishermen, fishers, anglers|反义词: None (No direct opposite)|词性: Noun
