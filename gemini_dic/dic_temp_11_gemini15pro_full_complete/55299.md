noun
放荡；淫乱
The state of being dissolute; debauchery.  Immorality; looseness in morals.
His dissolutenesses were well known throughout the city. 他在全城的放荡行为是出了名的。

词性: 名词(plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dissolutenesse, from Late Latin dissolūtus (“loose, unrestrained”), past participle of dissolvere (“to loosen, dissolve”) + -ness. Equivalent to dissolute +‎ -ness.
