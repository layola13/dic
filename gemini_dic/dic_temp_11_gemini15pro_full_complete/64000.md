epistolic: /ˌɛpɪˈstɒlɪk/ /ˌɛpɪˈstɑːlɪk/
adjective
书信体的；书信式的
Relating to or written in the form of letters.
例句：
The novel is written in an epistolary style.
这本小说是以书信体写成的。

Her epistolary novel was a bestseller.
她那部书信体小说成了畅销书。

近义词：lettered, correspondence-based
反义词：non-epistolary

词性: adjective
