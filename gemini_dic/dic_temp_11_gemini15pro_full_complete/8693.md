anticrack:/ˌæntiˈkræk/ | adj. | 防裂的，抗裂的 | Designed to prevent cracking or resist the effects of cracking. | This paint has anticrack properties, making it ideal for exterior walls.  这种油漆具有抗裂特性，是外墙的理想选择。| The builder used anticrack mortar for the foundation. 建筑工人使用抗裂砂浆做地基。 | 近义词: crack-resistant, crackproof | 反义词: brittle, fragile | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "anti-" (prefix meaning against) + "crack" (noun referring to a line of breakage).  "Anti-" 源自希腊语 "anti"，意思是“反对”或“相反”。 "Crack" 的起源可以追溯到古英语 "cracian"，意思是“发出尖锐的爆裂声”。
