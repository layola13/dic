defix:/ˈdiːfɪks/| v. |使固定，牢固地附着；施以魔法|To attach firmly; to cast a spell on something or someone, often with malicious intent.|The witch defixed a curse upon the kingdom. 女巫对王国施加了诅咒。|近义词：attach, fasten, bind, curse, hex|反义词:detach, loosen, unbind, bless|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēfīgere* (to fasten down, fix, transfix), from *dē-* (down, completely) + *fīgere* (to fix, fasten).
