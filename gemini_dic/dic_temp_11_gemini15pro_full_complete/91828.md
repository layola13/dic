hypergenetically:/ˌhaɪpərjəˈnɛtɪkli/
adjective
超遗传地
In a manner related to or influenced by hypergenesis, which refers to evolutionary changes above the species level.
The development of altruism has been explained hypergenetically, focusing on the benefits to the group rather than the individual.
近义词:  macroevolutionarily
反义词:  microevolutionarily
词性:  adjective

hypergenetically:/ˌhaɪpərjəˈnɛtɪkli/
adverb
超遗传地
Referring to something occurring or developing through hypergenesis, evolutionary changes above the species level.
The emergence of complex social structures in certain insect colonies could be interpreted hypergenetically.
近义词: macroevolutionarily
反义词: microevolutionarily
词性: adverb
