epizooty:/ˌɛpɪzoʊˈəti/ | n. | 动物流行病，瘟疫 | An epizootic is an epidemic in non-human animals.  It is analogous to an epidemic in humans. | The epizooty swept through the cattle ranches, decimating the herds.  这场瘟疫席卷了牧场，大量牲畜死亡。| 近义词：epizootic, epidemic (when referring to humans), outbreak, plague | 反义词: (None - the lack of an epizooty would simply be the absence of widespread disease) | 词性: noun

epizooty:/ˌɛpɪzoʊˈəti/ | adj. |  (Rare) Of or relating to an epizooty. | The epizooty conditions led to the quarantine of the entire farm. 这种动物流行病的情况导致整个农场被隔离。 | 近义词：epidemic, contagious | 反义词: (None -  the lack of epizooty qualities would simply be the absence of widespread disease characteristics) | 词性: adjective (rare usage)
