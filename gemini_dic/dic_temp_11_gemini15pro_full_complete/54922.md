disposers:/dɪˈspəʊzəz/
n.
处理者；处置者；支配者；（垃圾）处理器
People who have the power or authority to decide what to do with something or someone.  Specifically, it often refers to garbage disposals in kitchens.
The city council are the disposers of public funds. 市议会是公共资金的支配者。
Modern kitchens are often equipped with garbage disposers. 现代厨房通常配备垃圾处理器。
近义词:managers, administrators, controllers, handlers, garbage disposals
反义词:recipients, receivers
词性:名词(plural)

dispose:/dɪˈspəʊz/
v.
处理；处置；安排；使倾向于
To get rid of something, especially by throwing it away. Also, to arrange things or people in a particular way or position, or to make someone feel a particular way towards someone or something.
The company disposed of its toxic waste safely. 公司安全地处置了有毒废物。
His cheerful manner disposed me to like him. 他开朗的举止使我倾向于喜欢他。
近义词:discard, eliminate, arrange, incline
反义词:keep, retain, disorder
词性:动词

disposal:/dɪˈspəʊzl/
n.
处理；处置；支配；清理
The act of getting rid of something, or the way in which something is arranged.
The safe disposal of nuclear waste is a major problem. 安全处理核废料是一个主要问题。
The company put a computer at my disposal. 公司提供了一台电脑供我使用。
近义词:removal, clearance, arrangement, control
反义词:retention, acquisition
词性:名词
