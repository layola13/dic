outgazed:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪzd/
verb
以眼神胜过，盯得...不敢对视
looked at someone for longer than they could bear, until they looked away
He outgazed his opponent in a staring contest. 他在瞪眼比赛中以眼神胜过对手。
outstare, outface
-
verb
词源：out- + gaze
