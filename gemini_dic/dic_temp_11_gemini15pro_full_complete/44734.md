cruller:/ˈkrʌlər/ | n. |油炸圈饼；麻花|A small, twisted, fried cake, often topped with icing or sugar.|例句：I love the crispy texture of a freshly made cruller. 我喜欢新鲜出炉的油炸圈饼酥脆的口感。

例句：The bakery sells a variety of crullers, including glazed, powdered sugar, and chocolate. 这家面包店出售各种各样的油炸圈饼，包括糖霜、糖粉和巧克力口味的。|近义词：doughnut, friedcake |反义词: None |词性:noun

Etymology: From Dutch *kruller*, diminutive of *krul* (“curl”).  Relates to the twisted shape of the pastry.
