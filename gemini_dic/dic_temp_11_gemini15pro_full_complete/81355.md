graphophone:/ˈɡræfəˌfoʊn/| n. |留声机；早期型号的留声机|A graphophone is an early type of phonograph that used wax cylinders for recording and playback.  留声机是一种早期的留声机，使用蜡筒进行录制和播放。|例句：The inventor demonstrated the graphophone to a fascinated audience.  发明家向一群着迷的观众展示了留声机。

例句：The graphophone played a scratchy recording of a popular song. 留声机播放了一首流行歌曲的沙哑录音。|近义词：phonograph, record player|反义词: None|词性: noun
