nonmonastic:/ˌnɒnməˈnæstɪk/ | /ˌnɑːnməˈnæstɪk/ | adj. | 世俗的，非修道院的 | Not related to or characteristic of a monastery or monastic life.  |  Example: He preferred a nonmonastic life, choosing to live among the people rather than secluded in a monastery. 他更喜欢世俗的生活，选择住在人群中，而不是隐居在修道院里。

| Example: She pursued a nonmonastic path, dedicating herself to social work instead of religious contemplation.  她追求世俗的道路，投身于社会工作而不是宗教沉思。| Synonym: secular, worldly, lay | Antonym: monastic, cloistered, religious | adjective
