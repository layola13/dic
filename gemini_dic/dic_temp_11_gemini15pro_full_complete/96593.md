induction:/ɪnˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 就职; 入门; 归纳;  | The process of introducing someone to a new job, skill, organization, etc.; the process of discovering a general principle from particular facts or instances. |  The induction of new members will take place in September. 九月份将举行新成员的入职仪式。| synonyms: initiation, onboarding, inauguration, inference, generalization | antonyms: deduction, conclusion, discharge, dismissal | noun

induction:/ɪnˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 感应 | The production of an electric or magnetic state by the proximity (without contact) of an electrified or magnetized body. | The cooker uses induction heating. 这个炉子使用感应加热。 | synonyms: stimulation, generation, excitation | antonyms: none | noun
