nonarchitecturally:/ˌnɒnˌɑːrkɪˈtektʃərəli/
adjective
非建筑地；非建筑学地
Not in an architectural manner; without regard to architectural principles.
The building was nonarchitecturally designed, focusing purely on functionality.  这栋建筑的设计并非基于建筑学，纯粹注重功能性。
The space was divided nonarchitecturally, resulting in an awkward layout. 这个空间的划分没有建筑学的考量，导致布局很别扭。
Synonyms:  infunctionally,  unconventionally,  pragmatically
Antonyms:  architecturally,  aesthetically,  artistically
词性: adjective
