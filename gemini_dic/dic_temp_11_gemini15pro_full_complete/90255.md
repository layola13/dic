housebreakings: /ˈhaʊsˌbreɪkɪŋz/
noun
入室盗窃(复数)
The act of illegally entering a house or other building with the intent to commit a crime, especially theft.
There have been a series of housebreakings in the neighborhood recently. 最近附近发生了一系列入室盗窃案。
近义词: burglaries, robberies
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 "security measures" 安全措施)
词性: noun
