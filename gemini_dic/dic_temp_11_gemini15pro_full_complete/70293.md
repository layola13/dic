fidget:/ˈfɪdʒɪt/| v. n.|坐立不安，烦躁；烦躁不安的人或物|To make small movements, especially of the hands and feet, through nervousness or impatience.  A person who fidgets a lot.|The children were fidgeting in their seats.  孩子们在座位上坐立不安。|近义词：squirm, wriggle, twitch|反义词：relax, be still|词性:verb, noun
fidgety:/ˈfɪdʒɪti/| adj.|烦躁的，坐立不安的|Restless and uneasy.|The long wait made him fidgety.  漫长的等待让他烦躁不安。|近义词：restless, impatient|反义词：calm, patient|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *figeten*, frequentative of *fike*, of uncertain origin.
