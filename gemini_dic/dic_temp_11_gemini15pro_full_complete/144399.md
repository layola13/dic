outstripping: /ˌaʊtˈstrɪpɪŋ/
verb
超过，胜过
exceeding or surpassing someone or something in performance, achievement, or growth.
China's economic growth is outstripping that of many developed nations. 中国的经济增长超过了许多发达国家。
The demand for new housing is outstripping supply. 新房的需求超过了供应。
synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, outdoing, eclipsing, overtaking, transcending
antonyms: lagging behind, falling behind, trailing
