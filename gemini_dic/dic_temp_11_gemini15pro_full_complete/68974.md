fatidic:/fəˈtɪdɪk/| adj.|预言性的，命运注定的|Of or relating to fate or proceeding from fate; prophetic.  Foretelling or foreshadowing evil or tragic developments.|例句：The queen made a fatidic prophecy about the kingdom's downfall. 女王对王国的覆灭做出了一个预言性的预言。|例句：A fatidic sense of doom pervaded the gathering. 一种不祥的厄运感弥漫在聚会上。|近义词：prophetic, ominous, portentous|反义词：auspicious, fortunate|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fatidicus*, from fatum (“fate”) + -dicus (“-speaking”).
