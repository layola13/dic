engraves:/ɪnˈɡreɪvz/
verb
雕刻；铭刻
cuts or carves a design, inscription, etc., into a hard surface such as stone, metal, or wood.
The jeweler engraves the ring with the couple's initials. 珠宝商在戒指上刻上这对新人的首字母。
inscribes, etches, carves
erases, obliterates
v.
