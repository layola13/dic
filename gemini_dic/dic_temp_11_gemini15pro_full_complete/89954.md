horsepower:/ˈhɔːrsˌpaʊər/ (US) /ˈhɔːsˌpaʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.|马力|A unit of power equal to 550 foot-pounds per second (745.7 watts).|例句：The engine produces 200 horsepower. 这台发动机产生200马力。|近义词：power, capacity, wattage|反义词: weakness|noun
