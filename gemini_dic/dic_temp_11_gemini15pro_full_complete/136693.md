nonsedentary:/nɒnˈsɛdənˌtɛri/ | adj. | 非久坐的，活动的 | Not sedentary; characterized by or requiring much movement or activity. |  Example:  A nonsedentary lifestyle is important for maintaining good health.  保持非久坐的生活方式对维持健康很重要。

Example:  She has a nonsedentary job that requires her to be on her feet all day.  她从事一份需要整天站立的非久坐工作。| 近义词: active, mobile, dynamic | 反义词: sedentary, inactive, static | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "sedentary" (from Latin "sedentarius," meaning "sitting").
