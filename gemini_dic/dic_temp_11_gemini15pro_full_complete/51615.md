developability:/dɪˌvɛləpəˈbɪləti/| n.|可开发性，发展性|The developability of the land is high, making it a prime location for new housing. 这块土地的可开发性很高，使其成为新建住宅的主要地点。|The software has excellent developability, allowing for easy customization and expansion.  这款软件具有出色的可开发性，可以轻松定制和扩展。|近义词：potential, feasibility, capacity|反义词:inviability, impossibility|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From develop (v.) + -ability (suffix).  Develop comes from Old French desveloper, meaning "to unfold, unwrap."  The suffix -ability denotes the quality or state of being able to be done.  Therefore, developability refers to the capacity for something to be developed or the ease with which it can be developed.
