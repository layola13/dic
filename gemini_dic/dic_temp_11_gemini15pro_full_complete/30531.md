cephalization:/ˌsefələˈzeɪʃən/| n.|头部化，头部集中|The evolutionary trend towards the concentration of sensory organs and nervous control at the anterior end of the body, forming a head and brain.  头部器官和神经控制系统集中于身体前端、形成头部和大脑的进化趋势。|Cephalization is a key feature of vertebrates.  头部化是脊椎动物的一个关键特征。|
|近义词:concentration of neural tissue, development of a head|反义词:|词性:noun

cephalization:/ˌsefələˈzeɪʃən/| n.|头部化，头部集中|The increasing complexity of the head and brain over evolutionary time. 随着进化时间的推移，头部和大脑的复杂性不断增加。|The degree of cephalization is often used as a measure of an animal's evolutionary advancement. 头部化的程度通常被用作衡量动物进化程度的标准。|
