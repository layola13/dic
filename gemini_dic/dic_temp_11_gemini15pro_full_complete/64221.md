equilibrium:/ˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəm/ /ˌek wəˈlɪbriəm/| n. |平衡，均衡|A state in which opposing forces or influences are balanced.|The country's economy has finally reached a state of equilibrium.  这个国家的经济终于达到了平衡状态。|synonyms: balance, stability, symmetry|antonyms: imbalance, instability, asymmetry|noun

equilibrium:/ˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəm/ /ˌek wəˈlɪbriəm/| n. |平静，镇静|A state of mental or emotional calmness, composure, and evenness.|Despite the chaos around her, she maintained her equilibrium. 尽管周围一片混乱，她仍然保持镇静。|synonyms: composure, calmness, poise|antonyms: agitation, anxiety, perturbation|noun
