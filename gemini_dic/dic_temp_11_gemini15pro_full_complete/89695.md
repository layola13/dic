hootingly:/ˈhuːtɪŋli/ | adv. | 嘲笑地，喧闹地 | In a way that expresses loud amusement or scorn, often with hooting sounds. |  He laughed hootingly at the comedian's joke. 他对着喜剧演员的笑话大声嘲笑。|  She dismissed his suggestion hootingly. 她嘲笑地否定了他的建议。 | synonym: derisively, scornfully, mockingly | antonym: respectfully, seriously | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "hoot" (verb) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Hoot" originates from imitative origin, referring to the sound of an owl or a loud shout of disapproval or laughter.
