hemisphere:/ˈhemɪsfɪr/ | n. | 半球 | One of the halves of the earth as divided by the equator or a meridian. | The northern hemisphere experiences summer when the southern hemisphere experiences winter.  北半球在南半球经历冬季时经历夏季。 | globe, side | -- | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hēmisphaerium, from Ancient Greek ἡμισφαίριον (hēmisphaírion), from ἡμι- (hēmi-, “half”) + σφαῖρα (sphaîra, “ball, globe”).
