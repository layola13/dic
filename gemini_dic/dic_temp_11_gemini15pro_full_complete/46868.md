darer:/ˈdɛərər/| n.| 敢于冒险的人；挑战者|Someone who dares; a bold adventurer.|例句：He was a darer, always willing to try new things. 他是个敢于冒险的人，总是愿意尝试新事物。

例句：She proved herself a darer by skydiving from a plane. 她通过从飞机上跳伞证明了自己是个勇敢的人。|近义词：adventurer, risk-taker, daredevil|反义词：coward, timid person|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "dare" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Dare" comes from Old English "durran" meaning "to dare, to venture."
