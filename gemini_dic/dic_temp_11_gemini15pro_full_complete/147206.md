overmanages:/ˌoʊvərˈmænɪdʒɪz/
verb
过度管理，管理过细
to manage too much; to control or regulate too closely or too much
The company overmanages its employees, stifling creativity and innovation.  这家公司过度管理员工，扼杀了创造力和创新。
He overmanages his children, scheduling every minute of their day. 他对孩子的管理过于细致，把他们每天的每一分钟都安排得满满当当。
micromanage, overcontrol
undermanage
v.
