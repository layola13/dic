napus:/ˈneɪpəs/| n.|芜菁, 油菜|A genus of herbaceous plants in the mustard family (Brassicaceae), including the rape plant and rutabaga.  It is cultivated for its seeds, which yield oil, and for its leaves, which are used as fodder.|例句：Napus is an important oilseed crop. 油菜是一种重要的油料作物。

例句：The napus field was in full bloom. 油菜田盛开了。|近义词：rape, canola|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
