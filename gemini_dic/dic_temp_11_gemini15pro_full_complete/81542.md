grayback:/ˈɡreɪˌbæk/| n.|灰背虱; (尤指美国内战时期) 南方邦联士兵; 灰鲸|A louse, especially one infesting the body; (especially during the American Civil War) a Confederate soldier; a gray whale.|例句：The soldiers were plagued by graybacks during the long campaign.  这些士兵在漫长的战役中饱受虱子的折磨。

例句：He was a grayback fighting for the Confederacy. 他是一名南方邦联士兵。|近义词：louse, Confederate, gray whale|反义词:Union soldier (specifically for the Confederate meaning)|词性:noun


grayback:/ˈɡreɪˌbæk/| n.|灰鲸|A baleen whale of the North Pacific, having gray mottled skin.|例句：We saw a grayback breaching off the coast of California. 我们看到一只灰鲸在加利福尼亚海岸附近跃出水面。

例句：Graybacks migrate thousands of miles each year. 灰鲸每年迁徙数千英里。|近义词：gray whale, Eschrichtius robustus|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


词源：Compound of gray + back.  灰色+背部。 指这种生物或人的颜色和特征。
