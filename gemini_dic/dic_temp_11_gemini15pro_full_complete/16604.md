barometer:/bəˈrɒmɪtər (UK), bəˈrɑːmɪtər (US)/| n.| 气压计；晴雨表；指标 | An instrument measuring atmospheric pressure, used especially in forecasting the weather and determining altitude.  It is also used figuratively to refer to something that indicates change or trends. | The barometer is falling, which suggests rain is coming. 气压计正在下降，这表明要下雨了。| synonym: gauge, indicator, measure | antonym: N/A | noun

barometric:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk (UK), ˌbɛrəˈmɛtrɪk (US)/| adj.| 气压的；晴雨表的 | Relating to or measured by a barometer. | Barometric pressure is a key factor in weather forecasting. 气压是天气预报的关键因素。| synonym: atmospheric | antonym: N/A | adjective
