legislatorially:/ˌlɛdʒɪsˈleɪʃərəli/
adjective
以立法方式
in a legislative manner; by means of legislation
The issue was handled legislatorially.  这个问题是以立法方式处理的。
The matter was resolved legislatorially through the passing of a new law. 这件事是通过一项新法律的通过以立法方式解决的。
近义词:  legally, by law, through legislation
反义词:  executively, judicially, informally
词性: adverb
