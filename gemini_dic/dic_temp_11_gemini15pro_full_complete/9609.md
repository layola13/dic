antispiritual:/ˌæntiˈspɪrɪtʃuəl/
adjective
反精神的，反对精神的，非精神上的
opposed to or hostile to spirituality or religion; not spiritual.
His antispiritual attitude made him unpopular with the religious community.  他反精神的态度让他在宗教团体中不受欢迎。
The government's policies were seen as antispiritual by many. 许多人认为政府的政策是反精神的。
近义词: irreligious, secular, materialistic, worldly
反义词: spiritual, religious, devout, pious
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + spiritual.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Spiritual" refers to matters of the spirit or soul.
