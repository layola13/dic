corporeality:/ˌkɔːrpəˈriːælɪti/ /ˌkɔːrpəˈriːələti/
noun
物质性；肉体性；形体存在
The state of having a physical body; material existence.
例句：The artist explored themes of corporeality and spirituality in her work.  这位艺术家在她的作品中探索了物质性和精神性的主题。

例句：He struggled with the corporeality of his illness, finding it hard to accept his physical limitations. 他与疾病的肉体性作斗争，发现很难接受自己的身体局限性。

近义词：physicality, embodiment, materiality, tangibility
反义词：immateriality, spirituality, incorporeality
词性:n.
