forearmed:/fɔːrˈɑːrmd/
adjective
有准备的；预先准备好的
Prepared in advance; equipped beforehand.
例句：Forewarned is forearmed.  凡事预则立。

例句：Being forearmed with the knowledge of potential problems, they were able to avoid any major setbacks.  由于预先了解了潜在的问题，他们避免了任何重大的挫折。

近义词：prepared, ready, equipped, braced
反义词：unprepared, vulnerable, defenseless
词性：adjective
