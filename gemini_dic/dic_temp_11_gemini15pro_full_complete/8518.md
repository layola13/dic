anticaste:/ˌæntiˈkɑːst/
adjective
反种姓的；反对种姓制度的
Opposed to the caste system.
India's anticaste movement has a long history.  印度的反种姓运动有着悠久的历史。
synonyms:  anti-casteist, egalitarian
antonyms: casteist, pro-caste
adjective
