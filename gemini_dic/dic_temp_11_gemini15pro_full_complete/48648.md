defenselessly:/dɪˈfɛnsləsli/
adverb|无助地，无力抵抗地|Without any means of defending oneself; in a vulnerable manner.|The small animal cowered defenselessly as the predator approached. 小动物在捕食者靠近时无助地畏缩着。|
The child stood defenselessly in the face of the bully's taunts.  孩子面对恶霸的嘲讽，无力地站着。|
Synonyms: vulnerably, helplessly, powerlessly|Antonyms: protectively, securely, powerfully|词性: 副词
