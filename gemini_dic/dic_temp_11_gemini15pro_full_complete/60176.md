effervescent:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsənt/ | adj. | 兴奋的；活跃的；冒泡的 | Vivacious and enthusiastic; giving off bubbles; fizzy. |  The champagne was effervescent and bubbly. 香槟酒气泡翻腾。| 近义词: bubbly, sparkling, fizzy, lively, vivacious | 反义词: flat, dull, listless | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *effervescent-*, present participle of *effervescere* "to boil up, boil over," from *ex-* "out" + *fervescere* "to begin to boil, grow hot," from *fervere* "to boil, glow."
