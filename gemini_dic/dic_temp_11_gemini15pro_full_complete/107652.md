languidness:/ˈlæŋɡwɪdnəs/ | n. | 慵懒；倦怠；没精打采 |The state of being languid; lack of energy or vitality. | She moved with a certain languidness, as if the heat had sapped all her energy. 她行动有些慵懒，好像酷暑耗尽了她的全部精力。

The languidness of the afternoon made it difficult to concentrate. 下午的慵懒让人难以集中注意力。| 近义词：lassitude, lethargy, listlessness, inertia, sluggishness | 反义词：energy, vigor, liveliness, activity | 词性: 名词
