faster:/ˈfæstər/ (US), /ˈfɑːstə(r)/ (UK)| adj. adv.|更快的，更快地|Comparative form of "fast."  Indicates a greater speed or rate than something else.|He runs faster than his brother. 他跑得比他哥哥快。|近义词：quicker, more rapidly, speedily|反义词：slower, more slowly|词性: 形容词/副词

Etymology: From Middle English *fastere*, comparative of *fast*, from Old English *fæst*  (firm, fixed, strong, fast), from Proto-Germanic *fastuz* (firm, fast), from Proto-Indo-European *pastós* (fixed).
