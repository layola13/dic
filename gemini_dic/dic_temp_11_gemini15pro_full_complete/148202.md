overshoot:/ˌoʊvərˈʃuːt/ /ˌəʊvəˈʃuːt/| v. n.| 超过，超出；飞越；过冲|The plane overshot the runway. 飞机冲出了跑道。|近义词：exceed, surpass, overreach|反义词：undershoot, fall short|词性:动词,名词

overshoot:/ˌoʊvərˈʃuːt/ /ˌəʊvəˈʃuːt/| n.| 超过量，过冲|The stock market experienced a significant overshoot before correcting. 股市在回调之前经历了显著的超调。|近义词：excess, surplus, overage|反义词：deficit, shortfall|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over + shoot.  "Over" signifies "beyond" or "in excess," while "shoot" originally meant "to move quickly or suddenly."  Thus, "overshoot" literally means to shoot or go beyond a target or limit.
