max-out:/ˌmæks ˈaʊt/ | phrasal verb | 达到极限；最大化；刷爆（信用卡）| To reach the limit of something, especially a credit card or loan.  To use all of something available. | I maxed out my credit card buying Christmas presents. 我为了买圣诞礼物刷爆了信用卡。| synonyms: reach the limit, exhaust, use up, deplete | antonyms: conserve, save, preserve | verb

max-out:/ˌmæks ˈaʊt/ | noun | 最大值；极限 | The maximum amount or limit of something. | The max-out on this loan is $10,000. 这笔贷款的最高限额是1万美元。| synonyms: limit, maximum, ceiling | antonyms: minimum, bottom | noun
