marginalized:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəlaɪzd/ | adj. |边缘化的，被排斥的| Treated as insignificant or peripheral.|例句：Many indigenous communities feel marginalized by mainstream society. 许多原住民社区感到被主流社会边缘化。

例句：The report highlights the difficulties faced by marginalized groups in accessing healthcare. 该报告强调了边缘化群体在获得医疗保健方面所面临的困难。|近义词：excluded, peripheral, disadvantaged, voiceless, dispossessed, ostracized|反义词：central, mainstream, included, privileged, empowered|词性：形容词


词源:
From margin (边缘) + -al (形容词后缀) + -ize (动词后缀，使...) + -ed (过去分词后缀)
