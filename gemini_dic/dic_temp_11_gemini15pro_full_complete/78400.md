get-down:/ɡet daʊn/| phrasal verb |下来；开始认真工作；使沮丧；写下；吞下|To descend or cause to descend. To begin to work seriously. To depress or demoralize. To write something down. get-down:/ɡɛt daʊn/| noun | 沮丧; 令人沮丧的事; 令人扫兴的事 | A state of depression or low spirits. Something that is depressing or disheartening.|例句：He's been in a real get-down since he lost his job. 他自从失业以来一直很沮丧。 |近义词：depression, gloom, blues|反义词:elation, happiness, joy|词性:名词
