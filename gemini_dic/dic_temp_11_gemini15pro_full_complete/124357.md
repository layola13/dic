motionlessness:/ˈmoʊʃənləsnəs/| n. |静止，不动|The motionlessness of the air was oppressive. 空气静止不动，令人感到压抑。|例句：The motionlessness of the deer allowed the hunter to take a perfect shot.  鹿的一动不动让猎人找到了绝佳的射击机会。

The complete motionlessness of the figure was unnerving.  那个人一动不动的样子令人不安。|近义词：stillness, immobility, inactivity|反义词：movement, motion, activity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From motion + -less + -ness.  "Motion" comes from Old French "mocien" and ultimately from Latin "motio," meaning "a moving."  The suffix "-less" indicates a lack of something, and "-ness" turns an adjective into a noun.
