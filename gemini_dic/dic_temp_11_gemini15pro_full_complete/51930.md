diabetes insipidus:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz ɪnˈsɪpɪdəs/ | n. | 尿崩症 | A disorder of the pituitary gland characterized by excessive thirst and excretion of large amounts of diluted urine, caused by a deficiency of vasopressin (antidiuretic hormone). |  Diabetes insipidus can be treated with synthetic vasopressin. 尿崩症可以用合成血管加压素治疗。|近义词: polyuria, polydipsia (symptoms) | 反义词:  N/A (It's a disease, not a descriptive term) | 词性: noun

diabetes insipidus:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz ɪnˈsɪpɪdəs/ | n. | 尿崩症 | A disorder characterized by excessive thirst and frequent urination, but unlike diabetes mellitus, it's not related to high blood sugar.
