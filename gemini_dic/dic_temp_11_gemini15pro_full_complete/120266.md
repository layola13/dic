mini-roundabout:/ˈmɪni ˈraʊndəbaʊt/| n.|小型环岛，迷你环形交叉路口|A small roundabout, often marked by a painted circle on the road, where drivers must give way to traffic approaching from the right.|Drivers approaching a mini-roundabout should slow down and give way to traffic from the right.  驾驶员接近小型环岛时应减速并让路给来自右侧的车辆。|synonyms: traffic circle, rotary, small roundabout|antonyms: intersection, crossroads|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
mini- (prefix): from Latin *minimus, superlative of parvus "small."  Meaning "small, miniature."
roundabout (n.): from "round about," describing the circular path.
