boyau:/ˈbɔɪ.oʊ/ (US), /ˈbɔɪ.əʊ/ (UK)| n.|坑道，地道；（尤指第一次世界大战中的）交通壕|A narrow, winding trench or communication passage, especially one used in World War I.|例句：The soldiers huddled in the boyau, seeking shelter from the shelling. 士兵们挤在交通壕里，躲避炮击。

例句：The boyau provided a vital link between the front line and the rear. 交通壕提供了前线和后方之间的重要连接。|近义词：trench, tunnel, passage, dugout|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly: open field, surface)|词性:noun
