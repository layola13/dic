habilatory:/həˈbɪləˌtɔːri/
adjective
康复的，复原的
Helping or intended to help someone to improve their health and become able to do the things they did before they were ill or injured.
The patient underwent habilatory therapy to regain lost function. 病人接受了康复治疗以恢复失去的功能。

habilatory:/həˈbɪləˌtɔːri/
adjective
使能够的，使胜任的
Making someone able to do something.
The program provides habilatory services to individuals with developmental disabilities. 该项目为发育障碍人士提供使其能够胜任的服务。


Etymology: From Late Latin *habilitatorius, from Latin habilito "make able," from habilis "apt, skillful," from habeo "to have, hold."

Synonyms: restorative, rehabilitative, remedial, therapeutic, curative
Antonyms: debilitating, disabling, impairing
