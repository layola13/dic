abundant:/əˈbʌndənt/| adj.|丰富的，大量的，充足的|Present in great quantity; more than adequate.|例句：The country is abundant in natural resources. 这个国家自然资源丰富。|近义词：plentiful, copious, ample, profuse|反义词：scarce, meager, sparse|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *abundant-(em)*, present participle of *abundare* "to overflow, abound," from *ab* "from" (see ab-) + *undare* "to surge, overflow," from *unda* "wave."
