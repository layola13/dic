moraine:/məˈreɪn/| n. |冰碛|A mass of rocks and sediment carried down and deposited by a glacier.|The hikers struggled across the rugged moraine.  徒步旅行者艰难地穿过崎岖的冰碛。|
|Synonyms:  glacial drift, till, debris|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bedrock" in some contexts)|Noun|

moraine:/məˈreɪn/| n. |冰碛|A ridge, mound, or other distinct accumulation of unsorted rock material deposited by a glacier.|The valley was dotted with moraines left behind by the retreating glacier.  山谷里点缀着冰川消退后留下的冰碛。|Synonyms:  glacial deposit, glacial till|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|Noun|
