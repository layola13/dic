heterodoxly:/ˌhɛtərəˈdɒksli/ | adv. | 异端地，非正统地 | In a heterodox or unconventional manner; contrary to established beliefs or doctrines. | He heterodoxly challenged the prevailing scientific theories. 他非正统地挑战了盛行的科学理论。|  He lived heterodoxly, rejecting societal norms.  他过着非正统的生活，拒绝社会规范。 |Synonyms: unconventionally, unorthodoxly, dissidently, heretically | Antonyms: orthodoxly, conventionally, traditionally |词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  ἕτερος (héteros, “other, different”) +  δόξα (dóxa, “opinion, belief”).
