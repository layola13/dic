nonmigratory:/ˌnɒnˈmaɪɡrətəri/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈmaɪɡrətri/ (UK)| adj.|非迁徙的，不迁徙的|Not migratory; not migrating.  Referring to animals that do not migrate seasonally.|例句：Nonmigratory birds stay in the same region year-round. 非迁徙的鸟类一年四季都待在同一个地区。|近义词：resident, sedentary|反义词:migratory, nomadic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + migratory.  "Migratory" comes from Late Latin "migratorius," from "migrat-," past participle stem of "migrare"  meaning "to migrate." "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."
