fly-driver:/ˈflaɪ ˈdraɪvər/ | n. | 代驾司机；飞行驾驶员（较少用） | A person who is hired to drive someone else's car, often because the owner is unable or unwilling to drive themselves, especially after consuming alcohol.  Sometimes also refers to a pilot, but this usage is less common. | Example 1: They called a fly-driver to take them home after the party.  他们聚会后叫了代驾司机送他们回家。

Example 2:  He works as a fly-driver on weekends to earn extra money. 他周末做代驾司机赚外快。| Near Synonyms: designated driver, chauffeur, driver | Opposite: passenger, rider | Noun
