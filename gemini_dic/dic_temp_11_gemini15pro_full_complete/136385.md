nonrestrained:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈstreɪnd/ | adj. | 无拘无束的，不受限制的 | Not restrained; free from restraint or limitations. | The children ran nonrestrained through the fields. 孩子们无拘无束地在田野里奔跑。|  The nonrestrained growth of the weeds choked the flowers. 杂草不受限制地生长，扼杀了花朵。 | 近义词: unrestrained, unfettered, free | 反义词: restrained, restricted, limited | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + restrained (past participle of restrain, from Old French "restreindre," from Latin "restringere" - "to bind back, hold in").
