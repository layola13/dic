astrodome: /ˈæstrəˌdoʊm/ | n. | 天文馆，太空穹顶 | A large dome-shaped structure, typically with a transparent roof, used for astronomical observations or as part of a spacecraft.  A transparent dome, especially one forming part of a spacecraft, offering a wide view. |  The astrodome housed a powerful telescope.  天文馆里有一台强大的望远镜。
