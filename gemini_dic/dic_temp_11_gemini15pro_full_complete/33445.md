churnability:/ˌtʃɜːrnəˈbɪləti/
n.
客户流失率，客户流失倾向
The rate at which customers stop using a service or product.
The company is trying to reduce churn by offering better customer service.  这家公司正试图通过提供更好的客户服务来降低客户流失率。
High churn rates can be a problem for businesses. 高客户流失率对企业来说可能是个问题。
attrition rate, turnover rate
retention rate, loyalty
noun
