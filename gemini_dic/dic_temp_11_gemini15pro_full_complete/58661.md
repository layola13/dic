dumfounded:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪd/
adjective
惊呆的，目瞪口呆的
So amazed or confused that one is unable to speak; bewildered.
She was dumbfounded by the news. 她被这个消息惊呆了。

The sudden appearance of a ghost left him dumbfounded. 突然出现的鬼魂让他目瞪口呆。

近义词: astonished, speechless, stunned, bewildered, flabbergasted
反义词: unsurprised, calm, composed

词性: adjective
