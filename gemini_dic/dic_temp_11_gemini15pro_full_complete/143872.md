outpoise:/ˌaʊtˈpɔɪz/
verb
超过，胜过，在…方面强过
to outweigh or surpass in weight, importance, or influence
Her confidence outpoised her nervousness during the presentation. 她在演讲中，自信战胜了紧张。
outweigh, surpass, exceed
lag behind, fall short of
v.
