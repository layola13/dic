mamzer:/ˈmɑːmzər/| n. |私生子，杂种，坏蛋，讨厌鬼|A child born from certain forbidden relationships (specifically, from a relationship between a married Jewish woman and a man she is not married to, or from incest or adultery), or, more generally, a despicable person.|例句：He was called a mamzer for his cruel actions. 他因为残忍的行为被称为杂种。

例句：In the old days, a mamzer faced significant social stigma. 在过去，私生子面临着巨大的社会耻辱。|近义词：bastard, illegitimate child, scoundrel, villain|反义词:legitimate child|词性:noun
