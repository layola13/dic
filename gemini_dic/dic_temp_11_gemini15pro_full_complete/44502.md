crosshatching:/ˈkrɒsˌhætʃɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsˌhætʃɪŋ/ (US)| n.|交叉影线，交叉排线|The technique of using intersecting sets of parallel lines to create shading or texture in a drawing or engraving.|The artist used crosshatching to create a sense of depth in the portrait.  这位艺术家用交叉影线在肖像画中营造出一种深度感。|近义词:hatching, shading|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider terms like "blending" or "smooth shading")|noun
