curiously:/ˈkjʊəriəsli/| adv.|好奇地，奇怪地|In a curious manner; with curiosity.  Showing interest or inquisitiveness.  Strangely or unusually.|例句：She looked at him curiously. 她好奇地看着他。|近义词：inquisitively, strangely, oddly, unusually|反义词:indifferently, apathetically|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *curiously*, from Old French *curios*, from Latin *cūriōsus* (“careful, inquisitive, elaborate”), from *cūra* (“care, concern, attention”).
