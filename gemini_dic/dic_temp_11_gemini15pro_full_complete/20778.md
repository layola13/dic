blanketless: /ˈblæŋkɪtləs/
adjective
无毯的；没有毯子的
Without a blanket.
The blanketless child shivered in the cold.  没有毯子的小孩在寒冷中瑟瑟发抖。
Near synonyms: uncovered, exposed
Antonyms: blanketed, covered
词性: 形容词
