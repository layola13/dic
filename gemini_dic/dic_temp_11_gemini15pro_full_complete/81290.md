grants:/ɡrænts/ | v. | 授予；同意；承认 | Agree to give or allow (something requested) to someone. | The judge granted the defendant bail. 法官准予被告保释。| synonyms: allows, permits, bestows | antonyms: denies, refuses, withholds | verb
