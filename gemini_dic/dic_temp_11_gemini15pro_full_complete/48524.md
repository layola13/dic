deescalations:/ˌdiːɛskəˈleɪʃənz/
noun
缓和；降级；减缓
The act of reducing the intensity or seriousness of something, such as a conflict or crisis.
The de-escalation of the conflict was welcomed by all parties. 冲突的缓和受到各方的欢迎。
The company is implementing de-escalation strategies to manage customer complaints. 公司正在实施降级策略来管理客户投诉。
Synonyms: reduction, lessening, mitigation, alleviation, abatement
Antonyms: escalation, intensification, aggravation
