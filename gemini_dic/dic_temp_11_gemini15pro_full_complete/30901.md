chagrining:/tʃəˈɡriːnɪŋ/| adj.|令人懊恼的，令人失望的|Causing annoyance or disappointment.|It was chagrining to realize that I had made such a silly mistake. 意识到自己犯了如此愚蠢的错误，真是令人懊恼。|近义词:annoying, disappointing, frustrating, vexing|反义词:pleasing, gratifying, satisfying|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "chagrin," from Old French "chagreiner," of uncertain origin, possibly related to "grin" in the sense of showing one's teeth in anger or displeasure.
