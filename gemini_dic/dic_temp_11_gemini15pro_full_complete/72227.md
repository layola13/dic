floriated: /ˈflɔːrieɪtɪd/
adjective
繁花的；华丽的；装饰华丽的
Ornamented with flowers or floral designs; flowery in style or language.
The wedding cake was floriated with intricate sugar flowers. 婚礼蛋糕上装饰着复杂的糖花。
The writer's floriated prose was difficult to understand.  这位作家的华丽散文难以理解。
近义词: ornate, embellished, flowery, elaborate
反义词: plain, simple, unadorned
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *floriatus, past participle of *floriare "to flower, blossom," from flor-, stem of flos "flower."
