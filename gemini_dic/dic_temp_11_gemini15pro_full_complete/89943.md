horseman:/ˈhɔːrsmən/ (US) /ˈhɔːsmən/ (UK)| n. |骑马的人；骑手；马术师|A person who rides a horse, especially someone skilled in horsemanship.|例句：The horseman galloped across the field.  骑手飞驰穿过田野。

例句：He was a skilled horseman, able to control his mount with ease. 他是一位技艺娴熟的骑手，能够轻松地驾驭他的坐骑。

近义词：rider, equestrian, jockey|反义词:pedestrian|词性:noun
