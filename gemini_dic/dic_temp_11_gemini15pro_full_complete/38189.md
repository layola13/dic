compulsive:/kəmˈpʌlsɪv/| adj.|强迫性的，难以抑制的|Resulting from or characterized by compulsion; irresistible.|He's a compulsive gambler. 他是个嗜赌成瘾的人。|近义词: obsessive, uncontrollable, involuntary|反义词: controlled, voluntary, restrained|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late 16th century: from French compulsif, -ive, or modern Latin compulsus (past participle of compellere ‘compel’).
