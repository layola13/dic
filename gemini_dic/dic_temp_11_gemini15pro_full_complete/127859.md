neoblast:/ˈniːəʊblæst/ | n. |成胚细胞，再生细胞|An undifferentiated cell, typically found in invertebrates, that is capable of developing into other cell types and plays a role in regeneration.|Neoblasts are essential for planarian regeneration. 成胚细胞对涡虫的再生至关重要。|Synonyms: regeneration cell, undifferentiated cell|Antonyms: differentiated cell|noun
