interdependencies:/ˌɪntərdiˈpɛndənsiz/| n. |相互依存，相互依赖|The complex interdependencies of the global economy make it vulnerable to shocks. 全球经济复杂的相互依存关系使其容易受到冲击。|例句：The interdependencies between the various departments of the company are essential for its smooth operation. 公司各部门之间的相互依赖对其顺利运作至关重要。

例句：Understanding the interdependencies of ecosystems is crucial for conservation efforts. 了解生态系统的相互依存关系对于保护工作至关重要。|近义词：interconnections, interrelationships, correlations|反义词: independence, autonomy|词性:名词
