alehouses:/ˈeɪlˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
酒馆，啤酒屋
A place where ale (beer) is sold and consumed.
The weary travelers stopped at one of the many alehouses along the road.  疲惫的旅行者在路边的众多酒馆中的一家停了下来。
pubs, taverns, bars
