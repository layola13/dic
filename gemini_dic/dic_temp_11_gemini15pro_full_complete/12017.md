arthropoda:/ɑːrˈθrɒpədə/
|n.|节肢动物门|A phylum of invertebrate animals that includes insects, arachnids, crustaceans, and myriapods, characterized by jointed appendages, a segmented body, and an exoskeleton.|例句：Arthropoda is the largest phylum in the animal kingdom. 节肢动物门是动物界中最大的门。

|例句：Insects, spiders, and crabs all belong to the phylum Arthropoda. 昆虫、蜘蛛和螃蟹都属于节肢动物门。
|近义词：invertebrate, arthropod|反义词:|n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἄρθρον (árthron, “joint”) + πούς (poús, “foot”).
