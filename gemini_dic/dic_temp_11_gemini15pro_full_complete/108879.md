lectors: /ˈlɛktərz/
noun
讲师，朗读者
People who give lectures or read aloud, especially in a church service or academic setting.
The lectors for the conference were highly regarded in their fields.  会议的讲师在各自的领域都享有很高的声誉。
The lector read a passage from the Bible.  朗读者读了一段圣经。
synonyms: readers, speakers, presenters, instructors
antonyms: listeners, audience, students
n.
