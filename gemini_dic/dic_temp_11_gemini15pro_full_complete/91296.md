hydrolytically:/ˌhaɪdrəˈlɪtɪkli/| adv. | 水解地 | In a manner that involves hydrolysis, the chemical breakdown of a compound due to reaction with water. | The peptide bonds in proteins can be hydrolytically cleaved by enzymes. 蛋白质中的肽键可以通过酶水解裂解。| The substance is hydrolytically unstable in alkaline solutions. 该物质在碱性溶液中水解不稳定。 | 近义词: by hydrolysis | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider terms like "synthetically," "dehydration," depending on the context) | 词性: 副词
