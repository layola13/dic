orthoconic:/ˌɔːrθəˈkɒnɪk/
adjective
正圆锥形的，直锥形的
Having the form of a straight cone; having a circular base with elements that intersect at a single point on the perpendicular axis through the center of the base.
The nautilus shell is an example of an orthoconic spiral.  鹦鹉螺壳是正圆锥螺旋的一个例子。

The orthoconic projection is used in cartography. 正圆锥投影用于制图学。
近义词:conical
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "curved," "spiral" for contrasting shapes)
词性: adjective
