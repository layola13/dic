implode:/ɪmˈploʊd/| v. | 内爆；崩溃；瓦解 | To collapse violently inward. | The old building imploded during demolition. 这栋老建筑在拆除过程中内爆了。| The submarine imploded under the immense pressure of the deep ocean. 潜水艇在深海巨大的压力下内爆了。|  近义词：collapse, crumble, disintegrate | 反义词: explode, burst | verb

implode:/ɪmˈploʊd/| v. |(比喻) 崩溃，垮掉 | To break down or fall apart suddenly. |  His carefully constructed plans imploded when funding was withdrawn.  当资金被撤回时，他精心制定的计划破灭了。| The company imploded due to internal conflicts.  由于内部冲突，这家公司垮掉了。| 近义词：fall apart, break down, collapse | 反义词: flourish, thrive | verb
