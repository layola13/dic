afterburners: /ˈæftərbɜːrnərz/ (US) /ˈɑːftərbɜːnəz/ (UK)| n. |加力燃烧室；复燃器|A device on a jet engine that burns extra fuel to increase thrust.|The pilot engaged the afterburners for a rapid ascent. 飞行员启动加力燃烧室以便快速爬升。|Example: The fighter jet roared as the afterburners kicked in.  战斗机在加力燃烧室启动时发出轰鸣声。|Synonyms: reheat, augmenter|Antonyms: N/A|noun
