multihued:/ˌmʌltiˈhjuːd/ | adj. | 多色的，五彩缤纷的 | Having many different colors or hues. | The butterfly's wings were multihued, flashing iridescent blues and greens. 蝴蝶的翅膀五彩缤纷，闪烁着彩虹般的蓝色和绿色。 | The artist painted a multihued sunset over the mountains. 艺术家在山上画了一个五彩缤纷的日落。| 近义词: multicolored, polychromatic, varicolored | 反义词: monochrome, monochromatic | 词性: 形容词

词源: "multi-" (many) + "hued" (colored).  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many." "Hue" comes from Old English "hiw" meaning "form, appearance, color."
