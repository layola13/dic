nondisciplinary: /ˌnɒndɪsəˈplɪnəri/ (British) /ˌnɑːndɪsəˈplɪneri/ (American)| adj.| 非学科的，跨学科的| Not related to a particular academic discipline; interdisciplinary. |  Example: The university offers a nondisciplinary course exploring the intersection of art and science. 这所大学开设了一门非学科课程，探索艺术与科学的交叉点。

Example:  The conference brought together scholars from various fields for a nondisciplinary discussion of climate change.  这次会议汇集了来自各个领域的学者，就气候变化进行了一场跨学科的讨论。


|近义词: interdisciplinary, cross-disciplinary, multidisciplinary|反义词: disciplinary, subject-specific|词性: adjective
