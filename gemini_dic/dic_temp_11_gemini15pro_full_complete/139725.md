ochlocracy: /ɒklɒkrəsi/ | n. |暴民统治；众愚政治 | Government by the mob; mob rule.  A situation where a mob or crowd exercises political power or control, often characterized by chaos and disregard for established laws or procedures. | The French Revolution, in its most radical phase, descended into ochlocracy. 法国大革命在其最激进的阶段沦为了暴民统治。

The historian warned that unchecked populism could lead to ochlocracy. 历史学家警告说，不受约束的民粹主义可能导致暴民统治。| 近义词: mobocracy, anarchy | 反义词: aristocracy, meritocracy | 词性: noun
