eightieths:/ˈeɪtɪɪθs/
noun
八十分之一
One of eighty equal parts of something.
He ate seven eightieths of the pizza. 他吃了披萨的八十分之七。
近义词: one-eightieth (singular)
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider multiples like eighty, one hundred sixtieths, etc.)
词性:n.
