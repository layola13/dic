herbicidal:/ˌhɜːrbɪˈsaɪdəl/
adjective
除草的；杀草的
Relating to or denoting herbicides; used to destroy unwanted plants.
The herbicidal action of the chemical effectively eliminated the weeds. 这种化学物质的除草作用有效地清除了杂草。
synonyms: weed-killing, phytocidal
antonyms: growth-promoting, fertilizing
word type: adjective
