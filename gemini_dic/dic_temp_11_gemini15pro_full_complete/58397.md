dubieties:/duːˈbaɪətiz/| n.|疑惑；疑问；不确定|doubts; uncertainties.  A feeling of uncertainty or lack of conviction.|His mind was filled with dubieties about the future of the company.|近义词：doubts, uncertainties, misgivings, reservations|反义词：certainties, convictions, assurances|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *dubiete, from Old French dubiete, from Late Latin dubietatem, from Latin dubietas (“doubt, uncertainty”), from dubius (“doubtful”).
