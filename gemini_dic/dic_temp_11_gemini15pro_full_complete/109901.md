liar:/ˈlaɪər/ (US), /ˈlaɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.|说谎者，骗子|A person who tells lies. |He's a liar and a cheat. 他是个骗子，也是个老千。|
synonyms: prevaricator, fabricator, fibber, storyteller|
antonyms: truth-teller, honest person|
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lier, from Old English lēogere (“liar”), from Proto-Germanic *leugarijaz (“liar”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewgʰ- (“to lie, deceive”). Cognate with German Lügner (“liar”), Dutch leugenaar (“liar”).
