hagiographer:/ˌhæɡiˈɒɡrəfər/ |/ˌheɪdʒiˈɑːɡrəfər/| n. |圣徒传记作者；吹捧者|A person who writes biographies of saints, often idealizing or exaggerating their virtues.  A writer who greatly praises someone, often uncritically.|The hagiographer portrayed the politician as a saintly figure, ignoring all his flaws.  这位圣徒传记作者将这位政治家描绘成一个圣人般的人物，忽略了他所有的缺点。| biographer, eulogist, panegyrist | critic, detractor | noun
