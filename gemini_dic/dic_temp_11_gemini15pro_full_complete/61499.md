embroil: /ɪmˈbrɔɪl/ | vt. | 卷入，纠缠 | to involve someone deeply in an argument, conflict, or difficult situation | The two countries were embroiled in a bitter dispute over territory. 这两个国家卷入了一场关于领土的激烈争端。| The company was embroiled in a legal battle with its competitors. 这家公司陷入了与竞争对手的法律纠纷中。 |  synonyms: involve, entangle, enmesh | antonyms: extricate, disentangle | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *embröillier* "to confuse, perplex, entangle," from *en-* "in" + *bröillier* "to stir, mix," from *broil* "a piece, fragment."
