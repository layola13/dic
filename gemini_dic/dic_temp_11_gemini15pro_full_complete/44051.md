cringers:/ˈkrɪndʒərz/| n.|令人尴尬的人或事物；尴尬的时刻|People who post those videos are serious cringers. 发布这些视频的人真是让人尴尬。|近义词：embarrassing people/things, awkward moments|反义词:  comfortable people/things, graceful moments|词性:名词

Etymology:  From the verb "cringe" (meaning to shrink back in fear or embarrassment), with the suffix "-er" added to denote a person or thing that causes the feeling of cringing.  "Cringe" itself comes from the Old English "cringan."
