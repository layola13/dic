ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English holihoc, from Old English holihoc, where holi is "holy" and hoc comes from Old French hoque, meaning "mallow".  The "holy" part likely refers to its purported use in treating illnesses, possibly brought back from the Holy Land by Crusaders.
