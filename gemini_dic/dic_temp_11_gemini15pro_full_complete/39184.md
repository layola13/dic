congeneric:/ˌkɒndʒɪˈnɛrɪk/ /ˌkɑːndʒəˈnɛrɪk/| adj.|同属的；同类的；同族的|Belonging to the same genus or kind.|例句：The congeneric species exhibited similar behaviors.  同属的物种表现出相似的行为。|近义词：related, akin, similar, cognate|反义词：unrelated, dissimilar, different|词性:形容词

congeneric:/ˌkɒndʒɪˈnɛrɪk/ /ˌkɑːndʒəˈnɛrɪk/| n.|同类；同属生物|An organism belonging to the same genus as another.|例句：The researchers compared the DNA of several congenerics. 研究人员比较了几种同类生物的DNA。|近义词：relative, kinsman|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
