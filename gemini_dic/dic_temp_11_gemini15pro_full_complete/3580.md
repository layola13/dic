agastric:/eɪˈɡæstrɪk/
adjective
无胃的；缺乏胃的
Lacking a stomach or digestive function.
The agastric organism absorbs nutrients directly through its skin.  这种无胃生物直接通过皮肤吸收营养。
The parasite, being agastric, relies on its host for digestion. 由于这种寄生虫没有胃，它依靠宿主来消化。
近义词: stomachless
反义词: gastric
词性: adjective
