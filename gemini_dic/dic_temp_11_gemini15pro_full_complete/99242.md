intercommonage:/ˌɪntərˈkɒmənɪdʒ/
n.
公共牧场；共有地
Land held in common by several communities or townships.
The farmers grazed their cattle on the intercommonage. 农民们在公共牧场上放牧他们的牛。
synonyms: common land, common ground, commons
antonyms: private land, private property
noun
