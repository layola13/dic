gimbal:/ˈɡɪmbəl/| n. |万向节，平衡环|A device that allows an object to remain horizontal even as its support moves, typically consisting of a ring or rings within which the object is free to rotate.|The camera was mounted on a gimbal to keep it steady during the bumpy ride.  摄像机安装在万向节上，以便在颠簸的行驶过程中保持稳定。|近义词:stabilizer, mount|反义词:fixed support|词性:名词

gimbal:/ˈɡɪmbəl/| v. |用万向节支撑，使平衡|To support or suspend with a gimbal.|The ship's compass was gimbaled to ensure accuracy. 船上的罗盘用万向节支撑以确保准确性。|近义词:balance, stabilize|反义词:fix, secure|词性:动词
