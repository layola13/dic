non-didactic:/ˌnɒn daɪˈdæktɪk/ | adj. | 非教诲的，非说教的 | Not intended to teach a lesson or moral.  Focusing on entertainment or aesthetic value rather than instruction. | The movie was non-didactic, focusing more on entertainment than on delivering a message.  这部电影是非教诲性的，更注重娱乐而不是传达信息。| The museum's non-didactic approach allowed visitors to explore and interpret the exhibits freely. 博物馆的非教诲性方法允许游客自由探索和解读展品。 |  non-instructive, informal, entertaining | didactic, preachy, moralizing | adjective
