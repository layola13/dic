buxomness:/ˈbʌksəmnɪs/| n. |丰满，健壮 |The state or quality of being buxom; full-figured and healthy-looking.  It often refers to a woman's figure, implying a full bosom and hips. |Her buxomness was evident in the tight-fitting dress. 她的丰满在紧身裙下显露无疑。|Synonyms: plumpness, full-figuredness, shapeliness, voluptuousness|Antonyms: slenderness, thinness, slimness|noun
