overpiteousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈpɪtiəsnəsɪz/
| n. | 过度怜悯，过分同情 | Excessive pity or compassion.  A state of being excessively or unduly piteous. | His overpiteousnesses towards the criminal was unsettling. 他对罪犯的过度怜悯令人不安。 | 近义词: oversensitivity, excessive compassion | 反义词:  hardness, ruthlessness | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *ubari, meaning "over, above") + "piteous" (from Old French piteus, from Latin pietosus "full of piety, devout, merciful," from pietas "piety, dutifulness") + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), a suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
