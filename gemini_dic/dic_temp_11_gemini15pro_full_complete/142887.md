out-of-the-loop:/ˌaʊt əv ðə ˈluːp/
adjective
脱离圈子；不知情；不了解情况
Not informed about something; unaware of recent developments.
例句：I've been out-of-the-loop on this project for a while. 我有一段时间没有参与这个项目，所以不太了解情况。

例句：He felt out-of-the-loop after being away on vacation.  度假回来后，他感觉自己与工作脱节了。

近义词：uninformed, unaware, in the dark, out of touch
反义词：informed, in the loop, up to date, in the know
词性: adjective

词源:  This phrase originates from the world of electrical circuits, where a "loop" refers to a closed circuit.  Being "out of the loop" meant being disconnected from the flow of information or power.  This technical term was then figuratively applied to social and informational contexts.
