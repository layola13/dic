gauche:/ɡoʊʃ/ (US), /ɡəʊʃ/ (UK)| adj.|笨拙的，不圆滑的，缺乏社交技巧的|Lacking social grace; awkward and clumsy.|例句：His gauche table manners embarrassed his date. 他的笨拙的餐桌礼仪让他的约会对象很尴尬。|例句：She made a gauche remark about his weight. 她对他体重说了一句不得体的话。|近义词：awkward, clumsy, inept, tactless|反义词：graceful, elegant, suave, sophisticated|词性:形容词
词源:From French gauche 'left, awkward', ultimately from Frankish *walkan 'to walk, roll'. The original sense was 'left-handed', then 'awkward' (from the traditional association of left-handedness with clumsiness).
