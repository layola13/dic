nonemigrant:/ˌnɒnˈemɪɡrənt/ | n. adj. |非移民;非移民的|A person who enters a country temporarily, usually for a specific purpose such as work, study, or tourism, and does not intend to establish permanent residence.|例句：The nonemigrant visa allows him to work in the US for two years.  这个非移民签证允许他在美国工作两年。

例句：She entered the country as a nonemigrant student. 她以非移民学生的身份进入该国。


|近义词：visitor, alien, temporary resident|反义词:immigrant, emigrant, permanent resident|词性:名词，形容词
