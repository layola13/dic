cookie:/ˈkʊki/ (US), /ˈkʊki/ (UK)| n.|小甜饼；(计算机)浏览器缓存|A small, flat, sweet cake.  A small file stored on your computer by a website you visit.|例句：I baked some chocolate chip cookies. 我烤了一些巧克力曲奇饼干。
