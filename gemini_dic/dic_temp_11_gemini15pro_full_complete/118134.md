mensches:/ˈmɛnʃəs/| n.|人；(尤指)正派的人；体面的人|A person of integrity and compassion.  A good, decent person.|例句：He's a real mensches, always willing to help others. 他真是个好人，总是乐于助人。

例句：She's one of the few mensches in this cutthroat business.  在这个残酷的行业里，她是为数不多的几个正派人之一。|近义词：good person, decent person, humanitarian, mensch (singular)|反义词：bad person, scoundrel, villain|词性:noun

词源:
From Yiddish מענטש (mentsh), from Middle High German mensch, from Old High German mennisco, from Proto-Germanic *mannuskaz, diminutive of *mannō (“man”). Cognate with German Mensch, Dutch mens.
