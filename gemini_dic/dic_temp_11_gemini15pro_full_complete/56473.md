doltish:/ˈdoʊltɪʃ/| adj.|愚蠢的，笨拙的|Lacking intelligence or good sense; stupid.  Characterized by or revealing stupidity.|He made a doltish remark that offended everyone in the room. 他说了一句愚蠢的话，冒犯了房间里的每个人。|近义词：stupid, foolish, unintelligent, obtuse, dense|反义词：intelligent, clever, smart, bright, astute|词性:形容词
词源: From *dolt* + *-ish*.  *Dolt* comes from Middle English *dolte*, probably from Low German *dolt*, related to *dul* "dull, stupid".
