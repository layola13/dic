jeggings: /ˈdʒɛɡɪŋz/
n.
紧身牛仔裤
Tight-fitting stretch pants that resemble jeans.
She wore a pair of jeggings and a tunic top. 她穿了一条紧身牛仔裤和一件束腰外衣。

近义词: skinny jeans, leggings
反义词: wide-leg jeans, baggy pants
词性: noun
