bito: /ˈbiːtoʊ/ | n. | 比托（一种加密货币）| A cryptocurrency. | Example: I invested some money in bito. 我投资了一些钱在比托上。 | Example: The price of bito fluctuated wildly today. 比托的价格今天波动很大。| Synonym: cryptocurrency, digital currency, virtual currency | Antonym: fiat currency, physical currency | Noun

Etymology:  "Bito" is a portmanteau of "Bitcoin" and "ETF" (exchange-traded fund). It refers specifically to the ProShares Bitcoin Strategy ETF, the first Bitcoin-linked ETF listed on the New York Stock Exchange.  It derives its name from being linked to Bitcoin.
