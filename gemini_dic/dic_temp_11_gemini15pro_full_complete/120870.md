misbestows:/ˌmɪsbɪˈstoʊz/
verb
错误地给予；滥用；浪费
To give something, especially love, affection, or praise, to an unworthy recipient or for an unworthy purpose.
He misbestowed his affections on a woman who only wanted his money. 他把感情错付在一个只想要他钱的女人身上。
misapply, misuse, squander, waste
bestow correctly, use wisely
词性: 动词
