drifter:/ˈdrɪftər/| n.|漂泊者，流浪汉，游民；漂流物|A person who moves from place to place without a permanent home or job; something that drifts.|He was a drifter, moving from town to town, taking odd jobs. 他是一个漂泊者，从一个城镇搬到另一个城镇，做着零散的工作。|synonyms: vagrant, wanderer, nomad, rover, hobo|antonyms: settler, resident, stay-at-home|noun

drifter:/ˈdrɪftər/| n.|漂流物；漂浮物|Something that drifts, such as a piece of wood or ice floating on water.|The beach was littered with drifters carried in by the tide. 海滩上到处是被潮水带来的漂流物。|synonyms: flotsam, jetsam|antonyms: anchored object|noun
