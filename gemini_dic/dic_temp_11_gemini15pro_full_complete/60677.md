electric-blues:/ɪˈlɛktrɪk bluːz/| n.|电蓝调|A subgenre of blues music characterized by the use of electric instruments, particularly the electric guitar.|例句：I love listening to electric blues. 我喜欢听电蓝调。

例句：The band played a soulful electric blues set. 乐队演奏了一组深情的电蓝调。|近义词：Chicago blues, blues rock|反义词:acoustic blues|词性:noun
