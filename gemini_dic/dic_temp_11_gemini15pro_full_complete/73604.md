一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He lives here forever.  他永远住在这里。
The sun shines forever. 太阳永远照耀着。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is staying here forever. 她将永远待在这里。
He's working on this project forever, it seems.  他似乎永远都在做这个项目。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has lived here forever. 他一直住在这里。
They have been friends forever. 他们一直是朋友。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
She has been working on this project forever. 她一直都在做这个项目。
He has been living here forever, seemingly. 他似乎一直都住在这里。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He lived there forever.  他一直住在那里。
They promised to be friends forever. 他们承诺永远是朋友。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He was living there forever, it seemed. 他似乎一直都住在那里。
She was working on that painting forever. 她一直在画那幅画。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had lived there forever before he moved. 他搬家之前一直住在那里。
They had been friends forever before the argument.  在争吵之前他们一直是朋友。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
She had been working on that project forever before it was cancelled.  项目取消前，她一直都在做这个项目。
He had been living there forever, seemingly, before he disappeared.  在他消失之前，他似乎一直都住在那里。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will live here forever. 他将永远住在这里。
They will be together forever. 他们将永远在一起。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
He will be living here forever. 他将永远住在这里。
This time next year, she will be travelling the world forever. 明年这个时候，她将永远在环游世界。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, they will have been married forever. 到明年，他们结婚就很久了。(夸张用法)
By the time the project is finished, she will have been working on it forever.  等到项目完成时，她做这个项目就很久了。(夸张用法)

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By 2030, he will have been living here forever. 到2030年，他住在这里就很久了。(夸张用法)
By the time she retires, she will have been working at the company forever. 到她退休时，她在这个公司工作就很久了。(夸张用法)

虚拟语气:
If I could live forever, I would travel the world. 如果我能永生，我会环游世界。
I wish I could stay young forever. 我希望我能永远年轻。

被动语态: (forever本身是副词，没有被动语态)

疑问句:
Will they be together forever? 他们会永远在一起吗？
Has she been living here forever? 她一直住在这里吗？

否定句:
They won't be together forever.  他们不会永远在一起。
She hasn't lived here forever. 她没有一直住在这里。
