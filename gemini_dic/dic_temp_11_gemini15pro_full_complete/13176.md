at-ease:/ætˈiz/
adjective
放松的，自在的
relaxed and comfortable; free from worry or awkwardness
The soldiers were told to stand at ease. 士兵们被告知稍息。
近义词：relaxed, comfortable, informal
反义词：tense, formal, uneasy
词性: adjective


at-ease:/ətˈiz/
noun
稍息姿势
a posture in which a soldier stands relaxed but attentive, weight balanced on both feet, heels together, and hands clasped behind the back
The sergeant called the platoon to attention, then at ease. 军士命令排成队列的士兵立正，然后稍息。
近义词：(No direct equivalent, but describes a state similar to "rest")
反义词：attention
词性: noun


词源:  From the military command "Stand at ease!", dating back to the 17th century.  "Ease" in this context refers to a lessening of strict posture.
