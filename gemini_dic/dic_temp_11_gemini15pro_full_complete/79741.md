glumaceous:/ɡluːˈmeɪʃəs/| adj.|颖状的，具颖的|Relating to, resembling, or having glumes (the chaffy bracts enclosing the spikelet of a grass).|The glumaceous flowers are characteristic of grasses.|近义词：chaffy, bracteate|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) non-glumaceous (although not commonly used)|词性: adjective
