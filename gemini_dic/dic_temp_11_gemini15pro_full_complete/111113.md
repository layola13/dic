literalized:/ˈlɪtərəlaɪzd/ | adj. |字面化的，逐字的|Made literal; interpreted literally.|The poem was literalized in the film adaptation, losing some of its symbolic power. 这首诗在电影改编中被字面化了，失去了它的一些象征意义。|The director literalized the metaphor, showing a character actually turning into a tree. 导演将这个比喻字面化了，展示了一个角色真的变成了一棵树。| 近义词: verbatim, exact, precise| 反义词: figurative, metaphorical, symbolic | 词性: 形容词

literalize:/ˈlɪtərəlaɪz/ | v. |使字面化，逐字解释|To make literal; to interpret literally.|The director literalized the metaphor, showing a character actually turning into a tree. 导演将这个比喻字面化了，展示了一个角色真的变成了一棵树。|The scriptwriter literalized the idiom, which made the dialogue sound unnatural. 编剧把这个习语字面化了，使对话听起来很不自然。|近义词: interpret literally, translate verbatim | 反义词: interpret figuratively, symbolize| 词性: 动词

词源: From literal + -ize.  "Literal" comes from Late Latin *litteralis*, from Latin littera "letter."
