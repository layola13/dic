anapaests:/ˈænəˌpests/ | n. |抑抑扬格，短短长格|A metrical foot in poetry consisting of two unstressed syllables followed by a stressed syllable.  |Example:  The word "understand" is an anapaest. “understand”这个单词就是一个抑抑扬格。

Example:  The Assyrian came down like the wolf on the fold, And his cohorts were gleaming in purple and gold; And the sheen of their spears was like stars on the sea, When the blue wave rolls nightly on deep Galilee. (Lord Byron, "The Destruction of Sennacherib")  亚述人像狼扑羊群一样下来了，他的队伍在紫色和金色的光芒中闪耀；他们长矛的光辉就像海上的星星，当蓝色的波浪每夜在深深的加利利海上翻滚时。(拜伦勋爵，《西拿基立的毁灭》)|近义词:tribrach (less common) |反义词:dactyl |词性:noun
