essaying:/ɛˈseɪɪŋ/
v.
尝试；试验；试图；创作
trying to do something, especially something difficult; attempting or trying to produce a piece of writing or a work of art.
He was essaying a smile. 他试图挤出一个微笑。
She is essaying a novel. 她正在创作一部小说。
近义词:attempting, trying, endeavoring, undertaking
反义词:abandoning, neglecting, giving up
词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *assaien, from Old French assaier, from Vulgar Latin *exagiare, from Latin exagium (“weighing, balance”), from exigō (“weigh accurately, examine, test”), from ex (“out”) + agō (“drive, lead, act”).
