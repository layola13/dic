irrepresentable:/ˌɪrɛprɪˈzɛntəbl/
adjective
无法代表的；不可描述的；难以表达的
not able to be represented or described.
Her grief was so profound, so irrepresentable, that she couldn't even speak. 她悲伤如此深切，如此难以言表，以至于她一句话都说不出来。
The sheer scale of the universe is irrepresentable to the human mind. 宇宙的浩瀚规模是人类思维无法想象的。
Synonyms: indefinable, indescribable, inexpressible, unutterable
Antonyms: definable, describable, expressible, representable
词性: 形容词
