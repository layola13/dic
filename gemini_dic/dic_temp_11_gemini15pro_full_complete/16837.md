bashfuller:/ˈbæʃfələr/ | adj. | 更害羞的，更腼腆的 |  Comparative form of bashful: more bashful.  It describes someone who exhibits a greater degree of shyness or timidity than another person or than they did previously. | She was always bashful as a child, but she's even bashfuller now. 她小时候总是很害羞，但现在更害羞了。|  The new student was bashfuller than his brother.  这个新学生比他哥哥更害羞。 | 近义词: shyer, more timid, more reserved | 反义词: bolder, more outgoing, more confident | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *baschfuler, from Old English *bescēamfullere, comparative of bescēamfull (“bashful”).  The word "bashful" ultimately derives from the verb "abash," meaning "to make ashamed or embarrassed."
