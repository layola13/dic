nonconfederation:/ˌnɒnkənˈfɛdəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 非联邦; 未加入联邦的状态 |  The state of not being part of a confederation; absence of a confederation.  A situation where entities choose not to form or join a confederation. |  The nonconfederation of the independent states led to numerous trade disputes. 独立国家未组成联邦导致了大量的贸易争端。| Their continued nonconfederation made them vulnerable to outside threats.  他们持续不结盟的状态使他们容易受到外部威胁。 | Near synonyms: disunity, separateness | Antonyms: confederation, union, alliance | noun
