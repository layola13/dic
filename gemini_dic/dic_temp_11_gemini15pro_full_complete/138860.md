nurseries:/ˈnɜːrsəriːz/ (US) /ˈnɜːsəriz/ (UK)
n.|托儿所；苗圃|A place where young children are cared for while their parents are at work, or a place where young plants are grown for sale or for planting elsewhere. |The nurseries are full of playful toddlers. 这些托儿所里满是嬉戏玩耍的幼儿。|synonyms: crèche, daycare, kindergarten, garden center, plant nursery|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly "school" for older children, or "wasteland" for the opposite of a plant nursery)
词性: 名词(plural)
