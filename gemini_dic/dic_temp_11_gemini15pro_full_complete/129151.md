no-hopers:/ˌnəʊˈhəʊpəz/
|n.|没有希望的人；失败者；毫无前途的人|People who are considered unlikely to succeed or improve their situation.  Those deemed to have no chance of success or a positive future.|They were labelled as no-hopers by the school system. 他们被学校系统贴上了无可救药的标签。|Synonyms: losers, failures, deadbeats, underdogs (sometimes)|Antonyms: high-fliers, achievers, winners|Noun

no-hoper:/ˌnəʊˈhəʊpə/
