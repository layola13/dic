noninfringement:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt/
|n.|不侵权|The act of not violating a law, right, or agreement, especially relating to intellectual property.|The company ensured the noninfringement of its competitors' patents.|近义词:compliance, observance|反义词:infringement, violation|词性:名词
Etymology:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + infringement (from Old French *enfreindre, "to break," from Latin infringere, "to break into, weaken")
