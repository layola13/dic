noncondimental:/ˌnɒnkɒndɪˈmentəl/ | adj. | 非调味品的，不加佐料的 | Not used as a condiment; not serving as a condiment.  A noncondimental approach to cooking focuses on the natural flavors of ingredients. |  She prefers a noncondimental diet, avoiding sauces and spices. 她喜欢不加佐料的饮食，避免酱汁和香料。| 近义词: plain, unseasoned, bland | 反义词: condimental, seasoned, spicy | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "condiment" (from Old French "condiment," from Latin "condimentum," meaning "spice, seasoning") + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives)
