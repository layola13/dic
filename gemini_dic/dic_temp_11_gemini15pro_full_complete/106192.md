knocks:/nɒks/ (UK) /nɑːks/ (US)| n. v.|敲击声，敲门声；打击；批评；（发动机）爆震声；敲，击，打|A sudden short sound made by something hitting a hard surface.  To hit something firmly, especially to try to break it, open it, or make it move.|例句：There was a knock at the door. 有人敲门。|近义词：rap, tap, bang, hit, strike|反义词: (For the verb) protect, defend|词性:noun, verb

knocks:/nɒks/ (UK) /nɑːks/ (US)| n. |敲击声，敲门声；打击|Repeated sharp blows.  A misfortune or setback.|例句：He took a few knocks to the head during the fight. 在打斗中，他的头部被击中了几次。|近义词：blows, hits, setbacks|反义词: (For the noun - setback) success, triumph|词性:noun
