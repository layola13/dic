incorrect: /ɪnkəˈrɛkt/ | adj. | 不正确的，错误的 | Not correct; wrong or not accurate. |  The answer you gave is incorrect. 你给的答案是不正确的。

The information in the article was factually incorrect. 文章中的信息实际上是不正确的。

近义词: wrong, erroneous, inaccurate, false, mistaken | 反义词: correct, right, accurate, true


词性: adjective


词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin incorrectus, from in- ‘not’ + correctus (see correct).
