atrocha:/ɑːˈtroʊtʃə/ /əˈtroʊtʃə/ | n. | 小路，捷径，小道 | A narrow path or trail, especially one used by pack animals or hikers. |  We took a scenic atrocha through the mountains. 我们走了一条穿过山脉的风景优美的小路。|Synonyms: path, trail, track, lane | Antonyms: highway, road, expressway | noun

atrocha:/ɑːˈtroʊtʃə/ /əˈtroʊtʃə/ | n. | (尤指拉美国家)不正当手段，非法途径 | An illegal or improper method or procedure. | He used an atrocha to get the permit approved. 他用不正当手段获得了许可证的批准。 | Synonyms: shortcut, workaround, dodge, ruse | Antonyms: proper channels, legitimate means | noun
