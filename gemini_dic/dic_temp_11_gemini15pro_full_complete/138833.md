nunneries: /ˈnʌnəriz/
noun
女修道院（nunnery 的复数）
A building or group of buildings where nuns live and work.
The nuns lived a quiet life in the nunneries.  修女们在女修道院过着平静的生活。

The nunneries provided sanctuary for women seeking refuge.  女修道院为寻求庇护的妇女提供了避难所。

Synonyms: convents, monasteries, abbeys
Antonyms: brothels, dens of iniquity
