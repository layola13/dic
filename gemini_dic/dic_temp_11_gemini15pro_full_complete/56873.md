dosa:/ˈdoʊsə/ (US), /ˈdəʊsə/ (UK)| n.|多萨饼，一种南印度薄饼|A thin, crispy crepe made from fermented batter of ground rice and lentils, a staple food in South India.|例句：I had a delicious dosa for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一份美味的多萨饼。

例句：The restaurant serves a variety of dosas with different fillings. 这家餐厅供应各种馅料的多萨饼。|近义词：crepe, pancake|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something thick and bread-like)|词性:noun
