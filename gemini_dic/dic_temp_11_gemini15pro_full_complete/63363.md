entomologically:/ˌɛntəməˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb
昆虫学地；从昆虫学的角度
From the perspective of entomology; in a way relating to the study of insects.
The specimen was entomologically examined.  该样本进行了昆虫学检验。
The artist rendered the butterfly wings entomologically accurate.  这位艺术家以昆虫学角度精确地描绘了蝴蝶翅膀。
近义词:  insect-related,  from an insect perspective
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider) non-entomologically, generally
