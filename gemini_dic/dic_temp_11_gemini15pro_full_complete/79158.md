glama:/ˈɡlɑːmə/ | n. | 魅力，魔力 |  Glamour, enchantment, allure.  It refers to a mysterious and fascinating quality that attracts people.  | The actress possessed a certain glama that captivated audiences.  这位女演员拥有一种迷人的魅力，吸引了观众。 | 近义词: charm, allure, magnetism, charisma | 反义词: dullness, drabness, ordinariness | 词性: 名词

词源:  Shortened from "glamour," itself derived from "grammar" via Scots.  In the Middle Ages, "grammar" referred to any kind of learning, including magic and occult practices.  The association with magic eventually led to the word's current meaning of enchantment and allure.
