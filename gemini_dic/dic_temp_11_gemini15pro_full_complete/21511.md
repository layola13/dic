blue-babies:/ˈbluː ˌbeɪbiz/ | n. | 蓝婴，紫绀婴儿 |Infants with a bluish discoloration of the skin due to inadequate oxygenation of the blood, often caused by congenital heart defects.|  The doctor diagnosed the newborn as one of the blue-babies due to a heart defect.  医生诊断这个新生儿因为心脏缺陷是蓝婴。| Synonym: cyanotic infants | Antonym: healthy infants | noun
