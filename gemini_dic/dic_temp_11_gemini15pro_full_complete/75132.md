friskiness:/ˈfrɪskɪnɪs/| n.|活泼，嬉闹，欢快|Playfulness and a lively, lighthearted manner.|The puppy's friskiness was infectious, making everyone laugh.|近义词：playfulness, liveliness, exuberance, cheerfulness, sprightliness|反义词：sluggishness, lethargy, apathy, dullness|词性:noun

Etymology:
From frisky + -ness.  Frisky itself comes from the Middle English *frisken*, likely of Scandinavian origin.
