outrage:/ˈaʊtreɪdʒ/| n. vt.|愤怒，暴行；激怒|An act of extreme violence or viciousness.  A feeling of righteous anger.|The murder of innocent civilians is an outrage.  谋杀无辜平民是一种暴行。|近义词：fury, indignation, wrath, affront, offense|反义词:calm, peace, pleasure, delight|词性:名词,动词

outrage:/ˈaʊtreɪdʒ/| vt.|激怒，使震怒|To arouse intense anger, resentment, or indignation in.|The judge's decision outraged many people. 法官的判决激怒了很多人。|近义词：infuriate, enrage, incense, anger, provoke|反义词:please, delight, soothe, calm|词性:动词
