circulatory:/ˌsɜːrkjəˈleɪtəri/ (US), /ˌsɜːkjʊˈleɪtəri/ (UK)| adj.|循环的，循环系统的|Relating to circulation or the circulatory system, which is responsible for transporting blood and other fluids throughout the body.|The circulatory system is essential for delivering oxygen and nutrients to the body's tissues.|近义词：circulating, vascular|反义词: stagnant, blocked|词性: adjective

circulatorious:/ˌsɜːrkjəˈlɔːriəs/ (US), /ˌsɜːkjʊˈlɔːriəs/ (UK)| adj.|迂回的，绕圈子的|Characterized by a roundabout or indirect course or manner; circuitous.  This word is less common than "circuitous." |His circulatorious explanation confused everyone.|近义词：circuitous, indirect, rambling|反义词: direct, straightforward|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *circulatorius, from Latin circulator (“peddler, hawker”), from circulor (“go about, wander”), from circulus (“circle”), diminutive of circus (“circle, ring”).
