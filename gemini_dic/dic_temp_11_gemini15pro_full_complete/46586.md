damaging:/ˈdæmɪdʒɪŋ/| adj.|有害的，破坏性的|Causing or likely to cause harm or damage.|The report detailed the damaging effects of pollution on the environment. 这份报告详细说明了污染对环境的破坏性影响。|例句：The storm caused damaging floods.  暴风雨造成了破坏性的洪水。

例句：Spreading false rumors can be incredibly damaging to someone's reputation. 散布谣言对一个人的声誉会造成极大的损害。|近义词：harmful, detrimental, injurious, destructive|反义词：beneficial, helpful, constructive|词性:形容词
