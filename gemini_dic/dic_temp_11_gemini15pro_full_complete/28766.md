carnivoral: /kɑːrˈnɪvərəl/ | adj. | 食肉的；肉食性的 | Relating to or characteristic of a carnivore; meat-eating. | Lions are carnivoral animals. 狮子是食肉动物。|  A carnivoral diet consists mainly of meat. 肉食性饮食主要由肉类组成。 | 近义词: carnivorous, predacious | 反义词: herbivorous | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin *carnivoralis, from carnivorus (“flesh-eating”) + -alis (adjective suffix). Ultimately from caro, carn- (“flesh”) + vorare (“to devour”).
