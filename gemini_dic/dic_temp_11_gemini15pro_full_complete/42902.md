cousinliness:/ˈkʌzənlɪnɪs/
n.
表亲间的情谊；亲切友好
The state or quality of being cousinly; friendliness or kindness, especially as shown between cousins.
He greeted me with a surprising cousinliness, considering we hadn't seen each other in years.  他以令人惊讶的亲切友好迎接我，考虑到我们已经多年没见了。
近义词：amiability, affability, cordiality, friendliness
反义词：hostility, unfriendliness, coldness


ETYMOLOGY->
From cousinly + -ness.  Cousinly from cousin + -ly.  Cousin from Old French cosin, from Latin consobrinus.
