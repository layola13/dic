flanneling:/ˈflænəlɪŋ/| n. | 敷衍；空话；含糊其辞 |The politician's speech was full of flannel, offering no concrete solutions. 这位政治家的演讲充满了空话，没有提供任何具体的解决方案。|例句：He was just flanneling to avoid answering the tough question. 他只是在含糊其辞以避免回答这个棘手的问题。|近义词：equivocation, prevarication, hedging, waffling |反义词:directness, honesty, candor|noun

flannel:/ˈflænəl/| v. |  敷衍；用空话搪塞 |He flanneled his way through the interview, hoping they wouldn't notice his lack of experience. 他在面试中含糊其辞，希望他们不会注意到他缺乏经验。|例句：Stop flanneling me and tell me the truth! 别再敷衍我了，告诉我真相！|近义词：equivocate, prevaricate, hedge, waffle |反义词:be direct, be honest, be forthright|verb
