broker-dealers:/ˈbroʊkər ˈdiːlərz/ | n. | 经纪自营商 | A broker-dealer is a firm that buys and sells securities for its own account and also executes trades for its clients. |  Many large financial institutions act as broker-dealers. 许多大型金融机构充当经纪自营商。|  Broker-dealers are regulated by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). 经纪自营商受证券交易委员会 (SEC) 的监管。| synonyms: securities firm, brokerage firm, investment bank | antonyms: N/A | noun
