Example 2:  Many branches of Chassidism exist today, each with its own Rebbe. 如今存在许多哈西德主义分支，每个分支都有自己的拉比。|近义词：Hasidism, Hasidic Judaism|反义词: None (lacks a direct antonym)|词性: Noun
