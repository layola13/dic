faffed:/fæft/
verb
浪费时间，闲荡，瞎忙
spend time idly or inefficiently
例句：I faffed about all afternoon and got nothing done. 我整个下午都在瞎忙，什么也没做。
例句：Stop faffing and get ready! 别磨蹭了，赶紧准备!
近义词：dawdle, mess around, procrastinate, potter
反义词：hurry, work diligently
词性:v.
