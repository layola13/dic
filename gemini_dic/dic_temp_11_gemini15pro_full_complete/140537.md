oleoptene:/ˈōlēˌäpˌtēn/ | n. |油树脂的液态部分；油树脂中不挥发的液态部分|The oleoptene is the liquid portion of an oleoresin, which doesn't volatilize.|The oleoptene of copaiba balsam is used in microscopy.  古巴香脂的油树脂部分用于显微镜检查。|Synonyms: essential oil, eleoptene |Antonyms: stearoptene|Noun
