barrens:/ˈbærənz/| n. |荒地，贫瘠之地|A large, flat area of land that is infertile, desolate, and typically treeless.|The pioneers struggled to survive in the barrens of the American West.  拓荒者们在美国西部的荒原上挣扎求生。

|Synonyms: wasteland, desert, wilderness, badlands|Antonyms:  oasis, fertile land, grassland|Noun


barren:/ˈbærən/| adj. |贫瘠的，不毛的；不结果实的；无益的；空洞的|Too poor to produce much or any vegetation; (of a plant or tree) not producing fruit or seed; showing no results or achievements; (of a place or building) bleak and lifeless.|The barren landscape stretched for miles. 贫瘠的景象绵延数英里。

The apple tree was barren this year. 今年这棵苹果树没有结出果实。

|Synonyms: infertile, unproductive, sterile, arid, desolate|Antonyms: fertile, fruitful, productive, lush|Adjective
