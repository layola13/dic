midge:/mɪdʒ/| n. |蠓，蚊蚋|A small two-winged fly, especially one that bites or causes irritation.|例句：The midges were swarming around our heads as we walked by the lake. 我们沿着湖边走的时候，蠓虫在我们头上成群地飞舞。

例句：She swatted at the midges buzzing around her face. 她拍打着在她脸上嗡嗡作响的蠓虫。|近义词：gnat, mosquito|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
