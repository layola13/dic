nietzsche: /ˈniːtʃə, ˈniːtsʃə/ | n. | 尼采 | German philosopher Friedrich Nietzsche (1844-1900), known for his critical texts on religion, morality, contemporary culture, philosophy, and science, using a distinctive German language style and displaying a fondness for aphorism. | Nietzsche's philosophy had a profound impact on 20th-century thought. 尼采的哲学对20世纪的思想产生了深远的影响。 |  Synonyms: philosopher, thinker, intellectual | Antonyms: follower, disciple | noun
