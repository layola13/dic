flashbulb:/ˈflæʃbʌlb/ | n. | 闪光灯泡；闪光灯；（尤指）记忆犹新、清晰如昨的往事 | A flashbulb is a bulb that produces a bright flash of light for taking photographs, especially in conditions where there is not much natural light. It can also refer to a vivid and detailed memory of an emotionally significant event. |  I remember the moment vividly, like a flashbulb going off in my mind. 我清晰地记得那一刻，就像脑海中闪现出一个闪光灯泡。 | synonym: flash, photoflash, flashgun, vivid memory, searing memory | antonym: dim light, vague memory | noun

flashbulb:/ˈflæʃbʌlb/ | adj. | （记忆）清晰的，鲜明的 |  Flashbulb memories are usually very detailed and vivid.  闪光灯记忆通常非常详细和生动。| synonym: vivid, graphic, distinct, clear | antonym: vague, hazy, fuzzy, indistinct | adjective
