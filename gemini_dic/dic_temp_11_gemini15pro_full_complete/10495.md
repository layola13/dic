apply:/əˈplaɪ/ /əˈplaɪ/| v. |申请；应用；运用；涂抹；适用|to request something, usually officially, especially in writing or by sending in a form; to put or spread something on a surface; to make something relevant or suitable|例句：I will apply for a visa tomorrow. 我明天将申请签证。|近义词：request, use, utilize, administer, pertain|反义词:ignore, neglect|词性:verb

apply:/əˈplaɪ/ /əˈplaɪ/| n. |应用；应用程序；涂抹|an act of applying something; a computer program designed for a particular purpose; an act of putting or spreading something on a surface|例句：The apply of new technology has improved efficiency. 新技术的应用提高了效率。|近义词：application, use, utilization|反义词: removal, discontinuation |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English applien, aplien, from Old French aplier, from Latin applicāre (“to fold, fasten to, attach, join, add, direct (one's attention) to”), from ad- (“to”) + plicāre (“to fold”).
