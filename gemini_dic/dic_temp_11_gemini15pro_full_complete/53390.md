disagreer:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːər/| n.|不同意者，反对者|A person who disagrees or holds a contrary opinion.|He's a known disagreer and always challenges the status quo. 他是一个著名的反对者，总是挑战现状。|近义词：dissenter, objector, opponent|反义词： supporter, proponent|词性:noun
Etymology:  From disagree + -er.  Disagree comes from dis- (expressing negation) + agree (from Old French agreer, ultimately from Latin gratus "pleasing").
