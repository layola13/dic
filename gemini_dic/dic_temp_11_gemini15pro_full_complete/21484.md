blubber:/ˈblʌbər/| n. vi.|鲸脂；（海豹等海兽的）脂肪；哭泣|The whale's blubber provides insulation and buoyancy. 鲸鱼的鲸脂提供隔热和浮力。|例句：The harpoon struck deep into the whale's blubber. 鱼叉深深地刺入了鲸鱼的鲸脂。|例句：The baby seal had a thick layer of blubber to keep it warm. 小海豹有一层厚厚的鲸脂来保暖。|近义词：fat, lipid|反义词:leanness|词性:名词,动词

blubber:/ˈblʌbər/| vi.|大声哭；哭诉|He blubbered like a baby when he heard the bad news. 他听到坏消息后像个婴儿一样哭了起来。|例句：Stop blubbering and tell me what happened! 别哭了，告诉我发生了什么事！|例句：She blubbered something about losing her purse. 她哭哭啼啼地说她丢了钱包。|近义词：cry, weep, sob|反义词:laugh|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *blubberen, frequentative of *blubben (“to swell, bubble”).  Related to Dutch blob (“bubble, blister”), German Blobbe (“jellyfish”).  The sense of "whale fat" arose from its oily, semi-liquid nature.
