overmobilize: /ˌoʊvərˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过度动员，过度调动 | To mobilize more resources, especially troops or personnel, than are necessary or appropriate. | The country overmobilized for the war, leaving its economy weakened. 这个国家为战争过度动员，导致经济衰弱。| The general overmobilized his troops, exhausting them before the battle even began. 将军过度动员了他的部队，使他们在战斗开始之前就精疲力竭了。 | 近义词：overdeploy, overextend | 反义词：undermobilize, underdeploy | 词性：动词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, too much") + "mobilize" (from French "mobiliser," from "mobile" meaning "movable")
