overimports:/ˈoʊvərɪmpɔːrts/
n.
过度进口
The act of importing more goods than a country needs or can consume domestically, often leading to trade imbalances.
Excessive importation of goods.
例句：Overimports can negatively impact domestic industries. 过度进口会对国内产业造成负面影响。

例句：The country's overimports contributed to a large trade deficit. 该国的过度进口导致了巨额贸易逆差。

近义词：excess imports, surplus imports
反义词: underexports, underimports
词性: noun


overimport:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpɔːrt/
v.
过度进口
To import more goods than a country needs or can consume domestically.
To import excessively.
例句：Some countries overimport agricultural products. 一些国家过度进口农产品。

例句：They overimported luxury goods, leading to a trade imbalance. 他们过度进口奢侈品，导致贸易失衡。

近义词：import excessively
反义词: underexport, underimport
词性: verb


词源：
"Over-" (prefix) + "import" (verb/noun).  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much." "Import" derives from the Latin "importare," meaning "to bring in."
