impiteous:/ɪmˈpɪtiəs/
adjective
无情的，冷酷的
lacking pity or compassion; merciless.
例句：The impiteous judge sentenced the young man to life in prison.  无情的法官判处这名年轻人终身监禁。

例句：The impiteous storm raged for days, showing no mercy to the coastal towns.  无情的暴风雨肆虐了数日，对沿海城镇毫不留情。

近义词：ruthless, merciless, cruel, heartless, callous
反义词：compassionate, merciful, kind, sympathetic

词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *impietosus, from in- "not" + pietas "piety, duty".
