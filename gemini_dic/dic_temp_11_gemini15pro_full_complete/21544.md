bluefish:/ˈbluːfɪʃ/| n.|蓝点马鲛|A marine fish (Pomatomus saltatrix) that is bluish gray above and silvery white below, found in temperate and tropical waters worldwide and valued as a food and game fish.|We caught a large bluefish off the coast. 我们在海岸附近捕获了一条大蓝点马鲛。|近义词：tailor, snapper|反义词:/(none for a specific fish)/|noun
