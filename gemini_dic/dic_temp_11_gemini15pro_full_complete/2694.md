adroiter:/əˈdrɔɪtər/ | adj. | 更灵巧的，更熟练的 | More skillful and dexterous; more expert. | He was an adroiter craftsman than his brother, able to carve intricate designs with ease. 他比他兄弟更灵巧的工匠，能够轻松雕刻复杂的图案。 |  skillful, dexterous, adept, proficient, expert | clumsy, awkward, inept, unskilled | adjective

Etymology:
From Middle French *adroiteur* (unattested), from adroit + -eur, ultimately from Latin *dexter* (right, skillful). The comparative form is formed by adding "-er."
