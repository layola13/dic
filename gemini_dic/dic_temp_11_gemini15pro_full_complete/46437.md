dagoba:/dəˈɡoʊbə/ /ˈdɑːɡəʊbə/| n. |佛塔，舍利塔，窣堵坡|A dome-shaped structure erected over Buddhist relics or a sacred object.|例句：The ancient dagoba stood on a hill overlooking the valley.  古老的佛塔矗立在可以俯瞰山谷的小山上。

例句： Pilgrims circumambulate the dagoba as a sign of respect. 朝圣者绕着佛塔行走以示尊敬。|近义词：stupa, shrine, temple|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Sinhalese *dāgäba*, from Pali *dhātugabbha* (“relic chamber”), from Sanskrit *dhātugarbha* (“womb/container of relics”), from *dhātu* (“relic, element”) + *garbha* (“womb, interior”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰedʰ- (“to join, fit”).
