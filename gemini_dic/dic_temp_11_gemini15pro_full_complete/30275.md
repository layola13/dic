cementations:/ˌsiːmɛnˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |胶结作用；胶结物|The process in which clastic sediments are converted into consolidated rock by the precipitation of mineral cement in the pore spaces between particles. Also, the cementing material itself.| The geologist studied the cementations of the sandstone. 地质学家研究了砂岩的胶结作用。|近义词：consolidation, lithification |反义词: erosion, weathering |词性:noun

cementations:/ˌsiːmɛnˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |胶结物|The materials deposited between the grains of sediment during cementation.| The iron oxide cementations gave the rock a reddish hue. 氧化铁胶结物使岩石呈现微红色。|近义词：cement, binding agent |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
