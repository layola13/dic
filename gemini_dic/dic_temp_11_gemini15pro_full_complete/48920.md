defrosts:/diːˈfrɒsts/ (UK), /diːˈfrɔːsts/ (US)| v. |除霜，解冻，融化| To remove frost or ice from something, or to become free of frost or ice. |例句：She defrosts the freezer every six months. 她每六个月给冰箱除一次霜。

例句：The windshield defrosted quickly in the sun.  挡风玻璃在阳光下很快就除霜了。|近义词：thaws, melts, unfreezes|反义词：freezes|词性：动词

defrost:/diːˈfrɒst/ (UK), /diːˈfrɔːsts/ (US)| n. |除霜，解冻|The action or process of removing frost or ice from something. |例句：The defrost cycle takes about 30 minutes. 除霜周期大约需要30分钟。

例句：Put the chicken in the fridge for a slow defrost. 把鸡肉放在冰箱里慢慢解冻。|近义词：thawing, melting|反义词：freezing|词性：名词
