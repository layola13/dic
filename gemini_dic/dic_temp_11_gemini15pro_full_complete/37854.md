competitor:/kəmˈpɛtɪtər/ (US), /kəmˈpɛtɪtə/ (UK)| n. |竞争者，对手|A person, team, or company that competes against others.|例句：Our main competitor is a company called Acme. 我们的主要竞争对手是一家叫Acme的公司。

例句：She finished ahead of her closest competitors. 她领先于她最接近的竞争对手完成了比赛。

近义词：rival, opponent, challenger|反义词：ally, partner, collaborator|词性：名词
