dashedly: /ˈdæʃɪdli/ | adv. | 猛冲地，急速地 | In a manner characterized by sudden, energetic movement or action; with dash. | He dashedly ran across the street to catch the bus. 他猛冲过马路去赶公共汽车。 |  She dashedly signed the document without reading it. 她没读就飞快地签了文件。 | 近义词: hurriedly, swiftly, rapidly, hastily | 反义词: slowly, leisurely, cautiously | 词性: 副词

词源: From dash + -ly.  "Dash" originates from a Scandinavian source, possibly Old Norse *daska "to strike, slap."
