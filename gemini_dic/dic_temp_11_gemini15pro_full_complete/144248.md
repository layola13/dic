outsmiled:/ˌaʊtˈsmaɪld/
verb
胜过…的笑容; 以笑容胜过
outdo someone in smiling.
She outsmiled him, her radiant joy infectious. 她以灿烂的笑容胜过他，她由衷的喜悦极具感染力。

She outsmiled everyone in the room, making them feel instantly at ease. 她的笑容胜过房间里的所有人，让他们立刻感到轻松自在。

近义词: outshone, outcharmed
反义词: frowned at, scowled at
词性: verb
