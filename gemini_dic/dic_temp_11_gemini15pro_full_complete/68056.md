facular:/ˈfæk.jʊ.lər/| adj.|光斑的；耀斑的|Relating to or characteristic of a facula, a bright region on the sun's surface.|The astronomers observed a facular region near the sunspot.|近义词：bright, luminous, radiant|反义词：dark, dim, obscure|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *facularis, from Latin facula (“little torch, small light”), diminutive of fax (“torch, lamp”).
