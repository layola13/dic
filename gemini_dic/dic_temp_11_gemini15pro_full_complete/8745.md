antidevelopment:/ˌænti dɪˈvɛləpmənt/
n.|反发展，反开发|Opposition to planned growth, especially economic growth, typically motivated by a concern for environmental protection or social equity.|The local community voiced strong antidevelopment sentiments, fearing the proposed resort would destroy the natural habitat.当地社区表达了强烈的反发展情绪，担心拟议的度假村会破坏自然栖息地。|近义词：preservation, conservation|反义词：development, progress|词性:名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
development (noun): the process of developing or being developed (from develop, from Old French desveloper, from des- "un-" + voloper "to wrap")
