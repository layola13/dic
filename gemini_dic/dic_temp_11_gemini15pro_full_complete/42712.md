counterturn:/ˈkaʊntərˌtɜːrn/ /ˈkaʊntərˌtɝːn/| n.|反击，反制，逆转|An unexpected action taken in opposition to another action.  A move or series of moves made in response to an opponent's maneuver.  A reversal of fortune or situation.|The boxer's counterturn caught his opponent off guard and led to a knockout.  拳击手的反击让他的对手措手不及，导致了击倒。

The politician's counterturn involved exposing his rival's corruption.  这位政治家的反击包括揭露对手的腐败。|近义词：countermove, counterattack, riposte, retaliation|反义词:initiative, attack|词性:名词
