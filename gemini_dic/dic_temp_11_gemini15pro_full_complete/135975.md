nonradiances:/ˌnɑːnˈreɪdiənsɪz/
|n.|无辐射，非辐射|The absence of emitted radiation; the state of not radiating.|The satellite measures nonradiances at specific wavelengths to determine atmospheric composition. 卫星测量特定波长的非辐射来确定大气成分。|
|例句：The researchers studied the nonradiances in the deep ocean. 研究人员研究了深海中的非辐射。|
|例句：Understanding nonradiances is crucial for accurate climate modeling. 理解非辐射对于准确的气候建模至关重要。|
|近义词：non-emission, absence of radiation|
|反义词：radiances, emission|
|词性：名词|
