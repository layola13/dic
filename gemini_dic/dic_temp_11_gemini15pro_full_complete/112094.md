longitude:/ˈlɒndʒɪtjuːd/ (UK), /ˈlɑːndʒɪtuːd/ (US)| n.|经度|The angular distance of a place east or west of the Greenwich meridian, or west of the standard meridian of a celestial object, usually expressed in degrees and minutes.|The longitude of Beijing is approximately 116 degrees east. 北京的经度大约是东经116度。|近义词:meridian|反义词:latitude|词性:noun

latitude:/ˈlætɪtjuːd/ (UK), /ˈlætɪtuːd/ (US)| n.|纬度|The angular distance of a place north or south of the earth's equator, or of a celestial object north or south of the celestial equator, usually expressed in degrees and minutes.|The latitude of London is about 51 degrees north. 伦敦的纬度大约是北纬51度。|近义词:parallel|反义词:longitude|词性:noun
