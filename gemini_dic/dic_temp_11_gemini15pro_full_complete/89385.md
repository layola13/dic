homophily:/ˌhɒməˈfɪli/ /ˌhɑːməˈfɪli/ | n. |同质性，同类相吸|The tendency of individuals to associate and bond with similar others.|Birds of a feather flock together, which demonstrates homophily in social groups.|近义词：assortativity, similarity, likeness|反义词:heterophily, diversity|noun
