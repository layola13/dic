bewail:/bɪˈweɪl/
verb
恸哭；悲叹；哀悼
express great sadness or disappointment about something.
She bewailed the loss of her beloved pet. 她为失去心爱的宠物而悲痛不已。
The community bewailed the closure of the local library. 社区居民为当地图书馆的关闭而哀叹。
lament, mourn, grieve
rejoice, celebrate
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + wail.  "Be-" acts as an intensifier.  "Wail" comes from Old Norse *væla "to cry aloud, lament."
