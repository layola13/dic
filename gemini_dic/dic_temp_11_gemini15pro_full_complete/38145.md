compressible:/kəmˈprɛsəbl/ | adj. | 可压缩的 | Capable of being compressed or made more compact. | Air is a compressible fluid.  空气是一种可压缩流体。

The sponge is compressible. 海绵是可压缩的。 | 近义词:  squeezable, reducible, condensable | 反义词: incompressible, rigid, inflexible | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compressibilis, from Latin compress-, past participle stem of comprimere "to compress" + -ibilis "-ible".
