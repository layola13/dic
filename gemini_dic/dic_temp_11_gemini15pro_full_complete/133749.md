nongalvanized:/ˌnɒnˈɡælvənaɪzd/
adjective
未镀锌的
Not coated with zinc for protection against rust.
The nongalvanized steel quickly began to rust in the damp environment.  未镀锌的钢材在潮湿的环境中很快开始生锈。
The builder accidentally used nongalvanized nails, which corroded within months.  建筑工人不小心使用了未镀锌的钉子，几个月内就腐蚀了。
近义词:uncoated, unprotected
反义词:galvanized, zinc-coated
词性:adjective
