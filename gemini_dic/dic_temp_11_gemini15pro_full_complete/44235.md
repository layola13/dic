crocodiling: /ˈkrɒkəˌdaɪlɪŋ/ | n. | 鳄鱼式游泳; 鳄鱼行为 (指像鳄鱼一样静静地潜伏等待时机) | The act of swimming like a crocodile, or behaving like a crocodile by lying in wait for an opportunity. |  He was crocodiling in the shallows, waiting for a fish to swim by. 他像鳄鱼一样潜伏在浅滩中，等待鱼游过来。| 近义词: lurking, stalking | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) fleeing, retreating | 词性: noun


Etymology: From crocodile + -ing (present participle suffix).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek κροκόδειλος (krokódeilos).
