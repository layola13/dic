off-white:/ˈɔːfˈwaɪt/ | adj. | 灰白色，米白色 | A color that is a very pale tint of white, often with a slight yellowish or grayish tinge. |  例句：She wore an off-white dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件灰白色的连衣裙去参加婚礼。| 近义词：cream, ivory, eggshell | 反义词：bright white, pure white | 词性: adjective

off-white:/ˈɔːfˈwaɪt/ | n. | 灰白色，米白色 | The color off-white. | 例句：The walls were painted in an off-white. 墙壁被漆成灰白色。 | 近义词：cream, ivory, eggshell | 反义词：bright white, pure white | 词性: noun
