direptitious:/ˌdaɪrəpˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
偷偷摸摸的，暗中的
Characterized by stealth; sly and secretive.
He made a direptitious attempt to steal the cookies from the jar. 他偷偷摸摸地试图从罐子里偷饼干。
The spy gained entry through a direptitious maneuver.  这名间谍通过秘密行动潜入。
近义词: clandestine, furtive, surreptitious, stealthy
反义词: overt, open, obvious
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *dīreptitius, from dīreptus (“snatched away, plundered”), perfect passive participle of dīripere (“to snatch away, plunder”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + rapere (“to snatch, seize”).
