deployabilities:/dɪˌplɔɪəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|可部署性，部署能力|The deployabilities of the new software are impressive.  这款新软件的部署能力令人印象深刻。|例句：The system boasts improved deployabilities, allowing for quicker setup and configuration.  该系统拥有改进的部署能力，允许更快的设置和配置。

The company prioritizes deployabilities when developing new products. 该公司在开发新产品时优先考虑部署能力。|近义词：adaptability, flexibility, portability|反义词: inflexibility, rigidity|词性:noun (plural)

词源:  From deploy + -able + -ity.  Deploy comes from Old French despleier, meaning "to unfold."
