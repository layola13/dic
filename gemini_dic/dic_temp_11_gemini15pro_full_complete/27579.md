canard:/kəˈnɑːrd/ /kəˈnɑːrd/ | n. |谣言；假新闻；误传|A false or unfounded rumor or story, especially one deliberately fabricated to deceive. | The story about the company merging was a complete canard.  这个关于公司合并的故事完全是谣言。| Synonyms: rumor, falsehood, fabrication, hoax, lie | Antonyms: truth, fact | noun

canard:/kəˈnɑːrd/ /kəˈnɑːrd/ | n. |（飞机的）鸭式翼，前翼|A small winglike projection attached to the fuselage of an aircraft ahead of the main wing. | The Wright brothers' 1903 Flyer featured a canard.  莱特兄弟1903年的飞行器以鸭式翼为特色。| Synonyms: foreplane, forward wing | Antonyms: tailplane | noun
