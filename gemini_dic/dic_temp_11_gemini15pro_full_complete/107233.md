lairdliest:/ˈlɛərdlɪɪst/ | adj. | 最像地主的；最具地主风范的 |  Superlative form of "lairdly," meaning most resembling or characteristic of a laird (a landed proprietor in Scotland). |  He had the lairdliest air about him, surveying his vast estate.  他以最具地主风范的气派，巡视着自己广阔的庄园。 |  aristocratic, lordly, grand, stately, dignified | humble, modest, unassuming | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Scottish "laird" (landowner) + "-ly" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-est" (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Old Norse "leir" (clay, loam).
