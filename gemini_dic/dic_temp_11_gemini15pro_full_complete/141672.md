optionless:/ˈɒpʃənləs/| adj.|无选择的，没有其他选择的|Having no options or alternatives; without any other choice.|例句：Feeling optionless, she accepted the only job offer she received.  感到别无选择，她接受了唯一收到的工作机会。

例句：The villagers were optionless and had to evacuate due to the impending flood. 由于即将到来的洪水，村民们别无选择，只能撤离。|近义词：helpless, restricted, limited, trapped|反义词:free, resourceful, empowered|词性:adjective
