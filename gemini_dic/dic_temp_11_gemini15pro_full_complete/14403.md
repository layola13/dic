autosuggestive:/ˌɔːtəʊsəˈdʒestɪv/ | adj. | 自我暗示的；心理暗示的 | Producing or tending to produce ideas or feelings in oneself; self-induced. |  Example 1: His illness was thought to be autosuggestive, brought on by his own anxieties. 他的疾病被认为是自我暗示引起的，源于他自身的焦虑。

Example 2: The power of autosuggestive therapy is often underestimated.  自我暗示疗法的威力常常被低估。 | Near synonyms: self-induced, psychosomatic, subjective | Antonyms: objective, external | word property: adjective
