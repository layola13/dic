friendlier:/ˈfrɛndliər/| adj.|更友好的，更亲切的|The comparative form of "friendly," meaning showing kindness and a pleasant disposition to a greater degree.|She was much friendlier after she had a cup of coffee. 她喝了一杯咖啡后变得更友好了。|  My new neighbor is friendlier than my old one. 我的新邻居比我的老邻居更友好。|Synonyms: more amiable, more affable, more cordial|Antonyms: less friendly, more hostile, more aloof|adjective
