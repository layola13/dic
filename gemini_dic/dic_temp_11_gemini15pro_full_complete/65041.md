etch:/etʃ/ | v. | 蚀刻；铭记 | To cut into a surface, especially metal or glass, by means of acid; to impress deeply on the memory. |  The artist etched the design onto the glass plate. 艺术家把设计蚀刻在玻璃板上。

The memory of that day is etched in my mind. 那一天的记忆深深地铭刻在我的脑海里。 | 近义词: engrave, carve, inscribe | 反义词: erase, obliterate | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle Dutch etsen, frequentative of eten "to eat."
