hangmanship:/ˈhæŋmənˌʃɪp/
noun
绞刑技巧; 上吊技巧;  (尤指)行刑技巧
The hangmanship was swift and efficient. / 行刑技巧快速而有效。
skill in hanging, especially in executions
He studied the hangmanship of the old west. / 他研究了旧西部的绞刑技巧。
近义词: execution, hanging
反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可以考虑 reprieve, pardon)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "hangman" (executioner) + "-ship" (suffix denoting skill or art).  "Hangman" comes from Old English "hangmann," literally "hanging man."
