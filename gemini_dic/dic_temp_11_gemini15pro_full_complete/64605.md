erupting:/ɪˈrʌptɪŋ/
verb
爆发，喷发
sudden outburst or release, typically of a volcano, emotions, or a conflict.
The volcano is erupting. 火山正在爆发。
近义词:exploding, bursting, blowing up
反义词:dormant, quiet, inactive
词性:v.
