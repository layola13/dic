hydrosphere:/ˈhaɪdrəsfɪr/ | n. | 水圈 | All the waters on the earth's surface, such as lakes and seas, and sometimes also the water over the earth's surface, such as clouds. | The hydrosphere is essential for life on Earth.  地球上的水圈对生命至关重要。| The Earth's hydrosphere includes all forms of water. 地球的水圈包括所有形态的水。 | synonym:  water sphere | antonym: lithosphere | noun

Etymology: From hydro- + sphere.  Hydro- comes from Greek *hudōr* "water". Sphere comes from Greek *sphaira* "ball, globe".
