blindsiding:/ˈblaɪndˌsaɪdɪŋ/| v. |突袭；出其不意地打击；使措手不及|The news of his resignation completely blindsided us. 他辞职的消息完全让我们措手不及。|例句：The company blindsided its competitors with the new product launch. 这家公司用新产品发布打了竞争对手一个措手不及。

例句：She was blindsided by the sudden change in plans.  计划的突然改变让她措手不及。

近义词：ambushing, surprising, shocking|反义词:forewarning, informing|词性：动词

blindside:/ˈblaɪndˌsaɪd/| n. |盲侧；弱点|The attack came from his blindside. 攻击来自他的盲侧。|例句：He was tackled from his blindside. 他被从盲侧拦截。

例句：Everyone has a blindside, a weakness that can be exploited. 每个人都有一个盲点，一个可以被利用的弱点。

近义词：weakness, vulnerability|反义词:strength, advantage|词性：名词
