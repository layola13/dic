concupiscibleness:/kənˌkjuːpɪˈsɪbəlnəs/
n.
贪欲，好色
The state or quality of being concupiscible; strong sexual desire.
His concupiscibleness led him down a dark path. 他的贪欲使他走上了一条不归路。
近义词：lust, lechery, lasciviousness, sensuality
反义词：chastity, purity, abstinence
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concupiscibilis, from Latin concupiscere "to long for, covet," from com- "together" + cupiscere "to desire."
