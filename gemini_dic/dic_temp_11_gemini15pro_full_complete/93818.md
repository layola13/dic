immigrate: /ˈɪmɪɡreɪt/ (US) /ˈɪmɪɡreɪt/ (UK)| v. |移民; 移居入境|To come to live permanently in a foreign country.|Many people immigrated to the United States in the 19th century. 许多人在19世纪移民到美国。|
Example:  My grandparents immigrated to Canada from Italy. 我的祖父母从意大利移民到加拿大。
|Synonyms: relocate, resettle, migrate to|Antonyms: emigrate|verb


emigrate: /ˈemɪɡreɪt/ (US) /ˈemɪɡreɪt/ (UK)| v. | 移居国外; 移民|To leave one's own country in order to settle permanently in another.|Millions of Irish emigrated to America during the potato famine.  在马铃薯饥荒期间，数百万爱尔兰人移民到美国。|
Example: He emigrated from Russia to escape the political unrest. 他为了躲避政治动荡而从俄罗斯移民。|Synonyms: migrate, relocate|Antonyms: immigrate|verb
