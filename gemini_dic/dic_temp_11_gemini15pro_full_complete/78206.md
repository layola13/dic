gephyrean:/ˌdʒɛfɪˈriːən/| adj. n.|星虫类的；星虫动物|Relating to or belonging to the Gephyrea, a group of marine worms.  A worm of the phylum Gephyrea.|例句：The gephyrean worm burrowed into the sand. 星虫钻进了沙子里。

Gephyreans are found in marine environments worldwide. 星虫动物在世界各地的海洋环境中都能找到。|近义词：sipunculid, echiuran|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
Etymology:  From Modern Latin *Gephyrea*, from Greek *gē* "earth" + *phyrein* "to plant, grow."  Originally believed to be related to plants due to their sedentary lifestyle.
