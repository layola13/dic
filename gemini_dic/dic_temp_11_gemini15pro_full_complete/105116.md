kersin: /ˈkɜːrsɪn/ | n. | 角蛋白;角素 | A fibrous protein forming the main structural constituent of hair, feathers, hoofs, claws, horns, etc. | Example 1: Keratin is a key component of human hair and nails. 角蛋白是人类头发和指甲的关键组成部分。

Example 2:  The strength of a bird's beak comes from keratin. 鸟喙的强度来自角蛋白。| Synonym: scleroprotein | Antonym: N/A | Noun
