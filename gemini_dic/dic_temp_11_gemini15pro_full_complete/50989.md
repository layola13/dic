desireless:/dɪˈzaɪərlɪs/
adjective
无欲的；没有欲望的；淡泊的
Free from desire; not desiring or craving anything.
He lived a desireless life, seeking only inner peace. 他过着无欲无求的生活，只追求内心的平静。
Near synonyms: indifferent, apathetic, unambitious, detached
Antonyms: desirous, ambitious, greedy, craving
词性: 形容词
