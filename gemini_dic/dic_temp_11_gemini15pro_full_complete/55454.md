distinguishedly:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪdli/| adv.|显著地，杰出地，出色地|In a way that sets someone or something apart as special or superior.  Characterized by excellence, distinction, or eminence.|She dressed distinguishedly in a tailored suit. 她穿着一套剪裁得体的西装，显得十分出众。|Synonyms: notably, remarkably, exceptionally, prominently, eminently|Antonyms: ordinarily, commonly, unexceptionally, inconspicuously|Adverb
