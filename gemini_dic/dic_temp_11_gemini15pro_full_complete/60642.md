electee:/ɪˌlɛkˈtiː/
n.
当选者，被选中的人
a person who has been elected to an office or position but has not yet formally taken on the role
The electee will begin his term in January.  这位当选者将于一月份开始他的任期。
Synonyms: successful candidate, winner, victor
Antonyms: loser, defeated candidate
词性: 名词
