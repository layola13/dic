adriatic:/ˌeɪdriˈætɪk/ | adj. n.|亚得里亚海的；亚得里亚海|Relating to the Adriatic Sea, a body of water separating the Italian Peninsula from the Balkan peninsula.  The Adriatic itself. |例句：We sailed across the Adriatic Sea. 我们航行穿过亚得里亚海。

例句：The Adriatic coast is known for its beautiful beaches. 亚得里亚海海岸以其美丽的海滩而闻名。|近义词：Mediterranean (for the general region) |反义词:None|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology: From Latin *Adriaticus*, from *Adria*, the name of a town on the coast. Ultimately from Illyrian *adur* meaning "water" or "sea".
