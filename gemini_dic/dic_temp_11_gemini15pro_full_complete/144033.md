outrhyming:/ˌaʊtˈraɪmɪŋ/
verb
超越韵律；以更复杂的韵律压倒
surpassing in rhyming; outdoing in the use of rhyme
Shakespeare outrhymed all his contemporaries.  莎士比亚的韵律运用超过了他所有的同时代人。

近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
