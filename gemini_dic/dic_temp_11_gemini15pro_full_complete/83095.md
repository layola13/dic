gusseted:/ˈɡʌsɪtɪd/
adjective
有三角形插布的；有衬料的
Having a gusset or gussets. A gusset is a piece of fabric inserted into a seam to add width or reduce stress from tight-fitting clothing.
The bag is gusseted at the sides for extra capacity.  这个包的侧面有三角形插布，可以增加容量。
The gussets under the arms allow for greater freedom of movement. 腋下的三角形插布可以提供更大的活动自由。
近义词: expanded, widened, reinforced
反义词: narrow, tight, constricted
词性: adjective

gusseted:/ˈɡʌsɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)
装上三角形插布；装上衬料
Fitted with a gusset or gussets.
The dress was gusseted at the waist. 这件连衣裙的腰部装有三角形插布。
The backpack is cleverly gusseted to expand its capacity.  这个背包巧妙地装有三角形插布以扩展其容量。
近义词: expanded, widened, reinforced
反义词: narrow, tight, constricted
词性: verb (past participle)


词源: From "gusset" (n.) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives and past participles). "Gusset" itself comes from Old French "gosset" (a piece of armor protecting the armpit), perhaps of Germanic origin.
