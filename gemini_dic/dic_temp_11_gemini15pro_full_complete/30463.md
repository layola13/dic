centrifugally:/ˌsɛntrɪˈfjuːɡəli/ /ˌsɛnˈtrɪfɪɡəli/
adverb
离心地
Moving or tending to move away from a center.
The water is spun centrifugally until it separates from the clothes. 水被离心旋转直到与衣服分离。
The rotating blades fling the water centrifugally. 旋转的叶片将水甩向四周。

近义词:outward, outwardly
反义词:centripetally
词性:adverb

centrifuge:/ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/
noun
离心机
A machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate fluids of different densities or liquids from solids.
The blood samples were spun in a centrifuge. 血样在离心机中旋转。
A centrifuge is used to separate the cream from the milk. 离心机用于将奶油从牛奶中分离出来。
近义词:separator, extractor
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

centrifuge:/ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/
verb
用离心机分离
To rotate something at high speed in a centrifuge.
The technician centrifuged the blood samples. 技术人员用离心机分离了血样。
The mixture was centrifuged to separate the components. 混合物被离心分离以分离其成分。
近义词:spin, whirl, rotate
反义词:N/A
词性:verb
