noncontradictories:/ˌnɒnkɒntrəˈdɪktəriz/ |n.| 非矛盾命题；不互相矛盾的事物 |Statements or things that are not contradictory to each other, meaning they can both be true at the same time.| The two witnesses provided noncontradictory accounts of the accident. 两名目击者提供的关于事故的描述并不矛盾。| synonyms: compatibles, consistencies | antonyms: contradictories, inconsistencies | noun

noncontradictory:/ˌnɒnkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ |adj.| 不矛盾的；一致的 |Not contradictory; consistent.|  His explanation was noncontradictory to the evidence. 他的解释与证据并不矛盾。| synonyms: consistent, compatible, harmonious | antonyms: contradictory, inconsistent, conflicting | adjective
