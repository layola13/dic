midshipman: /ˈmɪdʃɪpmən/ (US) /ˈmɪdʃɪpmən/ (UK)| n. |海军学员；见习军官|A student at a naval academy training to be an officer. |例句：The midshipmen were training on the ship. 海军学员们正在船上训练。

例句：He was a midshipman at the Naval Academy. 他是海军学院的一名学员。

近义词：naval cadet, officer trainee|反义词: admiral, captain |词性: noun

midshipmen: /ˈmɪdʃɪpmən/ (US) /ˈmɪdʃɪpmən/ (UK)| n. |海军学员；见习军官(复数)|Plural form of midshipman. |例句：The midshipmen were given their orders. 海军学员们接到了命令。

例句：Several midshipmen were promoted to ensign. 几名海军学员被提升为少尉。

近义词：naval cadets, officer trainees|反义词: admirals, captains |词性: noun
