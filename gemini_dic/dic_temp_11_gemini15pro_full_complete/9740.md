antiviruses:/ˈæntiˌvaɪrəsɪz/
noun
杀毒软件；抗病毒软件
Software designed to detect and remove computer viruses.
I regularly update my antiviruses to protect my computer.  我定期更新我的杀毒软件以保护我的电脑。
Antivirus software is essential for online security.  杀毒软件对于在线安全至关重要。

近义词:antivirus software, virus protection, malware protection
反义词:virus, malware

词性:noun (plural)
词源:anti- (against) + virus
