henware:/ˈhenwɛər/
n.
陶器; 炊具; 厨房用具
Earthenware or stoneware pottery, especially for cooking.
This antique shop specializes in henware and other kitchen collectibles. 这家古董店专营陶器和其他厨房收藏品。
近义词:cookware, pottery, earthenware
反义词: (无明确反义词)
词性:名词

词源:  "Hen" in this context likely refers to domestic fowl, and "ware" denotes manufactured goods.  Henware originally referred to sturdy pottery suitable for everyday use, including cooking, much like a hen's practical role in providing eggs and meat.  Over time, it has become a more general term for kitchen pottery.
