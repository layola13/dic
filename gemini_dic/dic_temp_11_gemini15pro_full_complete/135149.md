nonomission:/ˌnɒnəˈmɪʃən/
n.|非排放; 不排放|The state of not emitting something, especially pollutants.|例句：The company achieved nonomission of greenhouse gases.  这家公司实现了温室气体零排放。|近义词：non-emission, zero emission|反义词:emission, discharge|词性:名词
Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "emission" (from Latin "emissionem," meaning "a sending out"). "Non-" negates the action of emitting.
