beaver:/ˈbiːvər/ (US), /ˈbiːvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|海狸；勤奋工作的人|A large semiaquatic rodent with webbed hind feet and a broad, flat tail, native to North America, Europe, and Asia.  Known for building dams and lodges.|The beavers built a dam across the stream. 海狸在溪流上筑起了一座水坝。|近义词：worker, builder|反义词:idler, loafer|noun
