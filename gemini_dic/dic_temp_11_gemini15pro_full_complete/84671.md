handcrafting:/ˈhændˌkræftɪŋ/ |n.|手工制作；手工艺|The process of making something by hand, skillfully and with attention to detail.  This often involves traditional techniques and results in unique, high-quality items.| She took up handcrafting as a hobby during the pandemic, creating beautiful jewelry and home decor.|Synonyms:  craftwork, handiwork, artisanship|Antonyms: mass production, factory production|Noun

handcrafted:/ˌhændˈkræftɪd/ |adj.|手工制作的；精工细作的|Made skillfully by hand, not by machine. Implies high quality and attention to detail.|  The handcrafted wooden toys were much more expensive than the plastic ones, but their quality was undeniable.|Synonyms: handmade, artisan-made, hand-wrought|Antonyms: machine-made, mass-produced|Adjective
