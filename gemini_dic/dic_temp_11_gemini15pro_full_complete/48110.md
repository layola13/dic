declining:/dɪˈklaɪnɪŋ/
adjective
下降的，衰退的
decreasing; becoming smaller, fewer, or weaker
The company's profits have been declining for the past three years.  这家公司的利润在过去三年里一直在下降。
The declining birth rate is a concern for many countries.  出生率下降是许多国家关心的问题。
synonyms: decreasing, diminishing, dwindling, falling, sinking, weakening
antonyms: increasing, rising, growing, improving, strengthening
词性: 形容词


declining:/dɪˈklaɪnɪŋ/
verb (present participle of decline)
下降，衰退，谢绝
decreasing; becoming smaller, fewer, or weaker; refusing politely
He is declining to comment on the allegations. 他拒绝对这些指控发表评论。
The number of students taking Latin is declining.  学习拉丁语的学生人数正在减少。
synonyms: refusing, rejecting, turning down; decreasing, diminishing, dwindling
antonyms: accepting, agreeing; increasing, rising, growing
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English declinen, from Old French decliner, from Latin declīnāre (“to bend down, turn aside”), from de- (“down, away”) + clīnāre (“to bend, incline”).
