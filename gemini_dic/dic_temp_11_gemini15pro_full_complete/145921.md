overdiluted:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈluːtɪd/
adjective
过度稀释的
having been diluted too much; excessively diluted
The overdiluted paint barely covered the wall. 这过度稀释的油漆几乎遮不住墙。
The chemist realized he had overdiluted the solution and needed to start again. 化学家意识到他把溶液过度稀释了，需要重新开始。
synonyms: weakened, thin, watery
antonyms: concentrated, strong, thick
词性: 形容词
