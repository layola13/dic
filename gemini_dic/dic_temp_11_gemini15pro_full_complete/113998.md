magistrality:/ˌmædʒɪˈstrælɪti/| n. |权威性；专横；威严|The quality of being magisterial; authority; domineering manner.|He spoke with a certain magistrality that silenced the room. 他说话带着某种权威性，让整个房间都安静了下来。|Synonyms: authority, dominance, ascendancy, imperiousness, command|Antonyms: subservience, humility, modesty|noun

magisterial:/ˌmædʒɪˈstiəriəl/| adj. |权威的；专横的；威严的|Having or showing great authority; domineering; majestic.|Her magisterial presence commanded respect. 她威严的仪态令人肃然起敬。|Synonyms: authoritative, commanding, imperious, august, dignified|Antonyms: humble, meek, subservient|adjective
