oversilent:/ˌoʊvərˈsaɪlənt/| adj.| 过于沉默的，过分安静的 | Excessively silent; more silent than is usual or expected.  Characterized by an unusual or excessive lack of sound or speech. |  He was oversilent during the meeting, which made everyone uncomfortable. 他在会议期间过于沉默，这使得每个人都感到不舒服。|  The library was oversilent, making it difficult to concentrate. 图书馆里太安静了，让人难以集中注意力。 | quiet, reserved, taciturn, uncommunicative |  talkative, loquacious, garrulous | adjective
