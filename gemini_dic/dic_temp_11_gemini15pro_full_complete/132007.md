noncorrection:/ˌnɑːnkəˈrɛkʃən/ | n. | 不改正，不纠正 | The policy of noncorrection led to a decline in writing skills.  这种不纠正的政策导致了写作能力的下降。|  The teacher's noncorrection of the student's grammar mistakes was surprising. 老师没有纠正学生的语法错误，令人惊讶。 | 近义词: non-intervention, inaction | 反义词: correction, amendment | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + correction (from Latin corrigere, "to correct")
