ambidextrousnesses:/ˌæmbɪdɛkˈstrʌsnəsɪz/
n.
雙手靈巧；雙手俱利；左右開弓
The quality or state of being ambidextrous.  The ability to use both hands equally well.
Her ambidextrousness was an advantage in playing musical instruments. 她的雙手靈巧在演奏樂器方面是一個優勢。
ambidexterity, two-handedness
one-handedness, awkwardness
noun
