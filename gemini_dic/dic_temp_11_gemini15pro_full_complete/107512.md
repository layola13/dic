land-up:/lænd ˈʌp/ | phrasal verb | 结果是; 最终处于 | To end in a particular place or situation, often unexpectedly or after a series of events. |  We set off for London, but we landed up in Birmingham. 我们出发去伦敦，但最终却到了伯明翰。 | Synonyms: end up, wind up, finish up | Antonyms: set out, begin, start | phrasal verb

land-up:/lænd ˈʌp/ | phrasal verb |  使陷入（困境） | To cause someone to be in a difficult or unpleasant situation. | His reckless spending landed him up in serious debt. 他鲁莽的花费使他陷入了严重的债务危机。| Synonyms: involve, embroil, entangle | Antonyms: extricate, free, liberate | phrasal verb
