oligist: /ˈɑːlɪɡɪst/ /ˈɒlɪdʒɪst/
n.
寡头政治支持者；少数统治的拥护者
A person who believes in or supports oligarchy (rule by a small group).
The oligist argued that a small group of experts could govern more effectively than a large, unwieldy democracy.  这位寡头政治支持者认为一小群专家比一个庞大而笨拙的民主政体更能有效地治理国家。
近义词: authoritarian, elitist
反义词: democrat, egalitarian
词性: noun

oligist: /ˈɑːlɪɡɪst/ /ˈɒlɪdʒɪst/
n.
寡头政治成员; 寡头

The oligists controlled the country's resources and suppressed dissent. 这些寡头控制着国家的资源并压制异议。
近义词: autocrat, plutocrat
反义词: commoner, citizen
词性: noun
