eal:/iːl/
n.|鳗鱼|A snake-like fish with smooth skin.|We caught an eel in the river. 我们在河里捉到了一条鳗鱼。|近义词:moray, conger|反义词:None|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æll, from Proto-Germanic *ēlaz, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂él- (“brown, yellow”). Cognate with Old Frisian ēl, Dutch aal, German Aal, Old Norse áll.
