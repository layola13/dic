artefactual: /ˌɑːtɪˈfæktʃuəl/ | adj. | 人造的；人为的；非自然的 | Relating to or resulting from human workmanship or action rather than a natural process. |  The increase in temperature was artefactual, caused by the faulty thermometer.  温度的升高是人为的，是由有故障的温度计造成的。 |  The scratches on the fossil were artefactual, not part of the original specimen.  化石上的划痕是人为的，并非原始标本的一部分。 |  artificial, man-made, synthetic | natural, genuine, real | adjective
