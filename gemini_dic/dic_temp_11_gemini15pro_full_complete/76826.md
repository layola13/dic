gander:/ˈɡændər/| n.|雄鹅；一看，一瞥|A male goose.  Also used informally to mean "a look" or "a quick glance".|例句：The old gander led his flock across the field. 老雄鹅带领鹅群穿过田野。

例句：I took a quick gander at the newspaper headlines. 我快速浏览了一下报纸的标题。|近义词：goose (for the bird), look, glance, peek|反义词:goose (female)|noun
