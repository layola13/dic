hinging:/ˈhɪndʒɪŋ/
v.
取决于，以…为转移
depending entirely on
The success of the project hinges on the cooperation of all parties.  这个项目的成功取决于各方的合作。
hinge on/upon something
近义词:depending, contingent, reliant
反义词:independent
词性:动词

hinging:/ˈhɪndʒɪŋ/
adj.
枢纽的，关键的
pivotal, crucial
The hinging moment of the game came in the final seconds.  比赛的关键时刻出现在最后几秒钟。
近义词:pivotal, crucial, central, key
反义词:unimportant, insignificant
词性:形容词
