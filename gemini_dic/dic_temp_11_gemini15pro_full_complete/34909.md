词源:
"Clique" comes from French "clique," meaning "latch, snap," figuratively a "knuckle," and thus a "gang."  The "-ism" suffix transforms it into a noun denoting a practice or system.  “clique”来源于法语“clique”，意思是“闩锁、按扣”，比喻为“指关节”，因此是“帮派”。“-ism”后缀将其转换为表示实践或制度的名词。
