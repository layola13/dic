miscopying: /ˌmɪsˈkɒpiɪŋ/ /ˌmɪsˈkɑːpiɪŋ/
noun
错误抄写，抄写错误
the act of copying something incorrectly
The miscopying of a single digit in the account number caused the payment to be delayed.  账号中一个数字的抄写错误导致付款延迟。
近义词: miswriting, transcription error, copying error
反义词: accurate copying, correct transcription
词性: noun

miscopy: /ˌmɪsˈkɒpi/ /ˌmɪsˈkɑːpi/
verb
抄错，错误地抄写
to copy something incorrectly
I miscopied the phone number and couldn't reach him. 我抄错了电话号码，联系不上他。
近义词: miswrite, transcribe incorrectly
反义词: copy correctly, transcribe accurately
词性: verb
