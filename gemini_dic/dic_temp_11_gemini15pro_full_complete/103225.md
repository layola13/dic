jettier:/ˈdʒɛtɪər/
adjective
更黑的，更像墨水的; 更突出的，更明显的
darker and more like jet; more prominent or pronounced.
The mountains appeared jettier against the pale sky. |群山在苍白的天空的映衬下显得更加墨黑。
The shadows grew jettier as the sun began to set. |随着太阳开始落山，阴影变得更加浓重。
近义词:blacker, darker, inkier, prominent, pronounced
反义词:lighter, paler, fainter
词性:adjective

词源:
From jet + -ier (comparative suffix).  Jet itself comes from Old French *jaet (modern French jaiet), from Latin gagates, from Greek gagatēs, relating to Gágai, a town in Lycia, Asia Minor, where the substance was found.
