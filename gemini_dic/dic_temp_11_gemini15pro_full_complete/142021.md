organized: /ˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/ (US) /ˈɔːɡənaɪzd/ (UK)| adj.| 有条理的，有组织的，井井有条的 | arranged or structured in a systematic way |  She's a very organized person. 她是一个非常有条理的人。|  The event was well organized and ran smoothly.  这次活动组织得很好，进行得很顺利。 | 近义词: structured, ordered, methodical, systematic, efficient, tidy | 反义词: disorganized, messy, chaotic, cluttered | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "organize," which comes from the French "organiser," ultimately from the Late Latin "organizare" meaning "to furnish with organs," from the Latin "organum" meaning "instrument, tool, organ."
