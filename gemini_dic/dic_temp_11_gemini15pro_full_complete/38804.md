conducts:/kənˈdʌkts/| v.|传导，引导，指挥，进行，表现|The metal conducts electricity. 这种金属导电。|近义词：leads, guides, directs, manages, performs|反义词:resists, obstructs|verb

conducts:/ˈkɒndʌkts/| n.|行为，举止|His conduct was unacceptable. 他的行为令人无法接受。|近义词：behavior, demeanor|反义词:misconduct|noun


词源: From Late Latin conductus, past participle of conducere "to lead or bring together," from com- "together" + ducere "to lead."
