echauguette:/eʃoɡɛt/| n.|瞭望台，观察哨|A watchtower or lookout post, especially one with a projecting structure.|From the top of the echauguette, the guard could see for miles. 从瞭望塔顶上，卫兵可以看到几英里外的地方。|Synonyms: watchtower, lookout, observation post|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider  hidden place, concealed area) |Noun
