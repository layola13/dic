broomed:/bruːmd/
verb
打扫；席卷；（快速地）清除；
cleaned with a broom; swept across quickly and powerfully.
例句：The stable boy broomed the floor. 马童扫了地板。
例句：The wind broomed the leaves into a pile. 风把树叶扫成一堆。
近义词：swept, cleaned, cleared
反义词：scattered, littered
词性：v.

broomed:/bruːmd/
adjective
被扫过的；被清除的
swept clean; cleared out
例句：The floor was broomed clean. 地板被打扫干净了。
例句：The streets were broomed after the parade. 游行结束后街道被清扫干净了。
近义词：swept, cleaned, cleared
反义词：dirty, messy
词性：adj.
