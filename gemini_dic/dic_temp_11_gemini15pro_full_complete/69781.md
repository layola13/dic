fermentative:/fərˈmɛntətɪv/
adjective
发酵的
Relating to or involving fermentation, the chemical breakdown of a substance by bacteria, yeasts, or other microorganisms, typically involving effervescence and the giving off of heat.
The fermentative process is used to produce yogurt.  发酵过程用于生产酸奶。
Kimchi is a traditional Korean fermentative food.  泡菜是韩国传统的fermented食物。
synonym:fermenting, zymogenic
antonym:non-fermentative
词性:adjective
