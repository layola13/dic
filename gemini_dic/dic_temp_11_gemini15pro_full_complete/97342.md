infortunate: /ɪnˈfɔːrtʃənət/ (British) /ɪnˈfɔːrtʃənɪt/ (American)| adj. |不幸的，倒霉的|Unlucky; experiencing misfortune.  Characterized by bad luck.| He was unfortunate to lose his job just before Christmas. 他很不幸在圣诞节前丢了工作。|  She made an unfortunate remark that offended everyone. 她无意中说了一句冒犯所有人的话。|近义词: unlucky, hapless, ill-fated, wretched|反义词: fortunate, lucky, blessed|词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *infortunatus*, from in- "not" + fortunatus "fortunate" (from fortuna "fortune").
