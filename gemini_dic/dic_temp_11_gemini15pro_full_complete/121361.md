misground:/mɪsˈɡraʊnd/
n.
误解，分歧，争论点
A misunderstanding or point of disagreement. 
例句：Their misground stemmed from a difference in interpretation of the contract. 他们的分歧源于对合同的不同解释。

例句：The main misground between the two parties was the issue of compensation. 双方主要的分歧在于赔偿问题。

近义词：misunderstanding, disagreement, dispute, contention
反义词：agreement, accord, harmony, concord
词性: 名词
