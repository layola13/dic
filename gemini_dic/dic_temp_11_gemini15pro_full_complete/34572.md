clearer:/ˈklɪərər/ (US), /ˈklɪərə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更清晰的，更明白的|Comparative form of "clear," meaning more transparent, more easily understood, or more evident.|The explanation is clearer now. 现在解释更清楚了。|The photo is clearer than the previous one. 这张照片比上一张更清晰。|近义词：more distinct, more transparent, more obvious, more evident, more understandable|反义词：vaguer, more obscure, more ambiguous, less distinct, murkier|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *clerer, comparative of cler, from Old French cler, from Latin clārus (“clear, bright, loud”).
