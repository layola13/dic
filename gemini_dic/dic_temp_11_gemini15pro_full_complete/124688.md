moviegoer: /ˈmuːviɡəʊər/ (British) /ˈmuːviɡoʊər/ (American)| n. |电影观众，常看电影的人 |A person who goes to the cinema frequently.| Example:  The moviegoer was thrilled with the new action film.  这位电影观众对这部新的动作片感到兴奋。

Example: Moviegoers lined up for hours to see the premiere.  电影观众排了好几个小时的队才看到首映。| 近义词: film fan, cinemagoer, movie buff | 反义词:  (No direct opposite, perhaps someone who rarely or never goes to movies) | 词性: noun
