overballast:/ˈoʊvərˈbæləst/ | v. | 过度压载，加压载过多 | To add excessive ballast to (a ship or other vessel). | The ship was overballasted, making it sluggish and difficult to maneuver. 这艘船过度压载，使其行动迟缓且难以操纵。| The captain ordered the crew to remove some of the ballast because the ship was overballasted. 船长命令船员移除一些压载物，因为船过度压载了。 | overload, overburden | underballast | Verb
