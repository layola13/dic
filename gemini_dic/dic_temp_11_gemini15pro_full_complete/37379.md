commentary:/ˈkɒmənt(ə)ri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmənteri/ (US)| n.|评论，解说，注释|A series of notes explaining or interpreting something; an expression of opinions or offering of explanations about an event or situation.|The sports commentator gave a thrilling account of the race.  体育解说员对比赛进行了激动人心的描述。|Synonyms: explanation, interpretation, analysis, exposition, review, critique |Antonyms: silence, taciturnity|noun
