enclaving:/ɪnˈkleɪvɪŋ/ | n. |包围；形成飞地；嵌入|The act of surrounding and isolating a territory or group of people within a larger territory.|The enclaving of the rebel-held city caused a humanitarian crisis. 包围叛军控制的城市造成了一场人道主义危机。|近义词：enclosing, surrounding, isolating|反义词：integrating, merging, uniting|v.

enclave:/ˈɛnˌkleɪv/ | n. |飞地；被包围的领土；孤立的群体|A portion of territory within or surrounded by a larger territory whose inhabitants are culturally or ethnically distinct.|The ethnic enclave provided a sense of community for the immigrants. 这个少数民族聚居区为移民提供了一种社区感。|近义词：territory, region, district|反义词：mainland, surrounding area|n.
