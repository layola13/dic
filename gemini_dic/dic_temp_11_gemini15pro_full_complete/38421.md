concertative:/kənˈsɜːtətɪv/
adjective
协同的；共同完成的；一致的
acting together; done in combination or unison.
The concertative efforts of the team led to their victory.  团队的协同努力使他们取得了胜利。
The musicians delivered a concertative performance, blending their instruments seamlessly.  音乐家们进行了协同表演，将他们的乐器完美地融合在一起。
近义词: collaborative, cooperative, joint, combined, united
反义词: individual, separate, independent, solo
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin concertat-, past participle stem of concertare "to contend, dispute, fight, concert, arrange," frequentative of concertare "to join together, unite," from com- "together" (see com-) + certare "to contend" (see certain).
