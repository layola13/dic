antirationalist:/ˌæntiˈræʃənəlɪst/
noun|反理性主义者|Someone who opposes or rejects rationalism. |He's a self-proclaimed antirationalist, believing intuition is superior to logic. 他自称是反理性主义者，认为直觉优于逻辑。|
近义词:irrationalist, obscurantist|反义词:rationalist, logician|词性:n.

antirationalist:/ˌæntiˈræʃənəlɪst/
adjective|反理性主义的|Opposed to or rejecting rationalism. |Her antirationalist stance made it difficult to have a productive debate. 她反理性主义的立场使得很难进行富有成效的辩论。|
近义词:irrational, illogical|反义词:rational, logical|词性:adj.
