mistrustingly:/mɪsˈtrʌstɪŋli/
adverb
不信任地，怀疑地
With suspicion or lack of trust.
She looked at him mistrustingly, unsure of his motives. 她怀疑地看着他，不确定他的动机。
近义词:suspiciously, doubtfully, skeptically
反义词:trustingly, confidently, believingly
词性:adverb

词源:
From mistrusting + -ly. Mistrusting is the present participle of mistrust, from mis- + trust.
