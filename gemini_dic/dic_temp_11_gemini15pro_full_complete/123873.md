moratorium:/ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəm/ | n. | 暂停，延期，中止 | A temporary prohibition of an activity. |  The government announced a moratorium on new construction in the area. 政府宣布暂停该地区的新建工程。

The bank declared a moratorium on loan repayments. 银行宣布暂停偿还贷款。| 近义词：suspension, postponement, delay, halt, freeze | 反义词：resumption, continuation, commencement | 词性: 名词
