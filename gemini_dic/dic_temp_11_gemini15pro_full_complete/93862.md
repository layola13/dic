immoderancy:/ɪˈmɒdərənsi/| n. |不节制，过度，无节制|Lack of moderation or restraint; excess.|His immoderancy in eating led to health problems. 他饮食无度导致了健康问题。|近义词：excess, intemperance, overindulgence|反义词:moderation, temperance, restraint|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *immoderantia, from immoderant- (stem of immoderans) not moderate + -ia -y.  Im- is a Latin negative prefix. Moderant- comes from the Latin moderārī "to moderate, restrain."
