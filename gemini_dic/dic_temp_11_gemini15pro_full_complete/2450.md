adjuration:/ˌædʒʊˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 恳求；命令；誓言 | An earnest request or command; the act of urging someone solemnly or earnestly. | The king's adjuration to his people to remain calm during the crisis was met with widespread fear. 国王恳求人民在危机期间保持冷静，但却遭到了普遍的恐惧。 | Synonyms: appeal, plea, entreaty, exhortation, injunction, command | Antonyms: dissuasion, discouragement | noun

adjure:/əˈdʒʊr/ | v. |  恳求；命令；起誓 | To urge earnestly or solemnly; to command under oath or penalty. | I adjure you to tell the truth. 我恳求你说实话。| Synonyms: beseech, implore, entreat, exhort, command | Antonyms: dissuade, discourage | verb
