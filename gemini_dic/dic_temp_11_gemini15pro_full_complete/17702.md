bedaub:/bɪˈdɔːb/
verb
涂抹；弄脏；玷污
to smear or cover with something sticky or messy
The child bedaubed the wall with paint. 孩子把墙上涂满了颜料。
近义词: smear, daub, besmirch, soil
反义词: clean, cleanse
词性: verb

bedaub:/bɪˈdɔːb/
词源:  From be- + daub, from Old French dauber "to plaster, whitewash."
英文解析: To cover or smear with a sticky or messy substance; to stain or soil.
例句: The children bedaubed themselves with mud while playing in the garden.  孩子们在花园里玩耍时把自己弄得满身是泥。

例句:  He inadvertently bedaubed his shirt with ketchup. 他不小心把番茄酱弄到了衬衫上。


近义词: smear, smudge, daub, cover, coat
反义词: clean, cleanse, purify
词性: verb
