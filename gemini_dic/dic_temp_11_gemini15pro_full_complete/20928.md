bleary-eyedness:/ˈblɪəri aɪdnɪs/ | n. | 睡眼惺忪，视线模糊 | The state of having bleary eyes, typically as a result of lack of sleep or tiredness. |  After a long flight, she suffered from terrible bleary-eyedness.  经过长途飞行后，她双眼昏花。| Synonyms: sleepiness, drowsiness, fatigue | Antonyms: alertness, wakefulness | noun

bleary-eyed:/ˈblɪəri aɪd/ | adj. | 睡眼惺忪的，视线模糊的 | Having eyes that are red or watery and vision that is not clear, typically as a result of lack of sleep or tiredness. | He was bleary-eyed from studying all night. 他熬夜学习，双眼昏花。| Synonyms: sleepy, drowsy, tired | Antonyms: alert, awake | adjective
