hooves:/huːvz/ | n. |蹄子；（尤指马等动物的）蹄|The horse's hooves clattered on the cobblestones. 马的蹄子在鹅卵石路上发出嗒嗒的响声。|Hooves is the plural form of hoof, the hard covering on the foot of an ungulate mammal such as a horse, cow, or deer.  It comes from Old English *hōf, of Germanic origin, related to Dutch hoef and German Huf. |The farrier trimmed the horse's hooves.  蹄铁匠修剪了马的蹄子。|Synonyms: feet (in the context of ungulates),  |Antonyms: N/A | noun
