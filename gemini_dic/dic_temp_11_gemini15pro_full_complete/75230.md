frolic:/ˈfrɒlɪk/ (US) /ˈfrɑːlɪk/ (UK)| n. vi. vt. adj.|嬉戏，玩闹；嬉戏的，欢乐的|An act of playful behavior, especially by children or animals; to behave playfully; playful and merry.|例句：The lambs frolicked in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏。|近义词：play, romp, gambol, caper|反义词：mope, sulk|词性:名词,动词,形容词
词源：From Middle Dutch wrolijk, vrolijk "joyful, merry," from Proto-Germanic *wrolijks "happy, blessed," from *wrolikaz "glory, bliss."
