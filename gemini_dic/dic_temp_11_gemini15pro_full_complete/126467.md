mythographer:/mɪˈθɒɡrəfər/ (US) /mɪˈθɑːɡrəfər/ | n. |神话作者，神话收集者|A person who writes or compiles myths.  A scholar who studies myths.| Ovid was a Roman mythographer. 奥维德是一位罗马神话作者.|  mythologist, myth writer |  N/A | noun
Etymology->
From Ancient Greek *μυθογράφος (muthográphos, “myth-writer”), from *μῦθος (mûthos, “myth”) + *γράφω (gráphō, “I write”).
