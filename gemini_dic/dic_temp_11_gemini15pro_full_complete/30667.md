ceroon:/səˈruːn/| n.|包装箱，大包，包裹 (尤指粗麻布包裹的干货)|A large bale or package, especially of dried goods wrapped in coarse cloth or burlap.|The traders brought ceroons of spices and silks.|近义词：bale, package, bundle|反义词:|名词

Etymology: From Spanish *serón*, ultimately from Latin *sīra*, a large pannier or basket.
