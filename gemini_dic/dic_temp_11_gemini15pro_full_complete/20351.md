bismuthiferous:/bɪzˈmʌθɪfərəs/
adjective
含铋的；产铋的
Containing or yielding bismuth.
The mine is known for its bismuthiferous deposits.  这个矿以其含铋矿床而闻名。
The bismuthiferous ore was sent for refining.  这批含铋的矿石被送去提炼。
Synonyms: bismuth-bearing, bismuth-rich
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but one could use terms like "bismuth-deficient" or "bismuth-free")
词性: adjective
