ordonnant:/ɔʁ.dɔ.nɑ̃/
adjective
ordering, directing, commanding
This is the ordonnant principle of the system. 这是系统的指导原则。
近义词: directing, commanding, prescribing
反义词: disorganized, chaotic
词性: adjective

词源: From Old French ordonant, present participle of ordoner, from Latin ordinare "to set in order, arrange, regulate," from ordo "row, rank, order."
