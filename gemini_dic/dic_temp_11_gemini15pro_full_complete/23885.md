break-the-back-of:/breɪk ðə bæk əv/ | idiom |  打破…的脊梁；完成…最艰难的部分；克服主要困难 | To complete the most difficult part of a task or project, making the remainder easier to accomplish. | The team worked all weekend to break the back of the project.  团队整个周末都在工作，以完成项目最艰难的部分。| synonyms: make a dent in, overcome the worst of, get over the hump, turn the corner | antonyms: prolong, exacerbate, worsen | idiom
