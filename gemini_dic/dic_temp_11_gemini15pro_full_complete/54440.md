disingenuous:/ˌdɪsɪnˈdʒɛnjuəs/ | adj. | 不真诚的，不坦率的，虚伪的 | Not candid or sincere, typically by pretending that one knows less about something than one really does. |  He gave a disingenuous answer to the question, pretending he didn't know the truth. 他对这个问题给出了一个不真诚的答案，假装他不知道真相。

She offered a disingenuous apology, clearly not meaning a word of it. 她虚伪地道歉，显然没有半点诚意。

近义词: insincere, dishonest, deceitful, duplicitous, mendacious | 反义词: sincere, honest, candid, frank, forthright | 词性: adjective

词源: Late 16th century: from dis- (expressing negation) + ingenuous.
