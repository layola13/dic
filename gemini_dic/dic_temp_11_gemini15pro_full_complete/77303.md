gatelike:/ˈɡeɪtˌlaɪk/
adjective
像门的；似闸门的
Resembling a gate in form or function.
The entrance to the castle was gatelike, with two large wooden doors.  城堡的入口像门一样，有两扇巨大的木门。
The narrow passage between the rocks was gatelike. 岩石间的狭窄通道像闸门一样。
similar, resembling, doorlike, portal-like
