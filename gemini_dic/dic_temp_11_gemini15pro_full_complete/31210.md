chank: /tʃæŋk/ | n. | 厚块，大块；贝壳碎片 | A thick, solid piece of something; a fragment of a seashell. | He took a large chank of cheese. 他拿了一大块奶酪。|  The beach was littered with chanks of seashells. 海滩上散落着贝壳碎片。 | synonyms: chunk, lump, hunk, piece, fragment | antonyms: sliver, crumb, speck | noun
