demagog:/ˈdeməɡɒɡ/ (UK), /ˈdeməɡɑːɡ/ (US)| n. |煽动者，蛊惑民心的政客|A political leader who seeks support by appealing to popular desires and prejudices rather than by using rational argument.| Hitler was a demagog who exploited people's fears and prejudices. 希特勒是一个利用人们的恐惧和偏见煽动民心的煽动者。

The senator's demagogic rhetoric incited violence among his supporters.  这位参议员煽动性的言辞煽动他的支持者实施暴力。

|近义词：rabble-rouser, agitator, firebrand|反义词：statesman, conciliator|词性: noun
