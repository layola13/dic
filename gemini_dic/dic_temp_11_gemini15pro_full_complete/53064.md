dioramic:/ˌdaɪəˈræmɪk/ | n. |透景画；立体模型| A diorama; a miniature three-dimensional scene. | The dioramic of the rainforest was incredibly detailed.  这个热带雨林的立体模型非常细致。| synonyms: diorama, model, miniature | antonyms: N/A | noun
