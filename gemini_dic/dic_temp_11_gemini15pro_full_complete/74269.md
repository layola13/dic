foxy:/ˈfɒksi/ (UK) /ˈfɑːksi/ (US)| adj.|狡猾的，奸诈的；性感的，有魅力的|Attractive, alluring, and slightly mischievous or cunning.  Can refer to physical attractiveness, particularly with a sly or playful quality, or to cunning intelligence.|She had a foxy look in her eyes. 她眼神里透着一股狡黠。|近义词：cunning, sly, attractive, seductive, alluring|反义词：naive, innocent, unattractive|词性:形容词
词源: From fox + -y.  Originally referred to the cunning of a fox, and later extended to describe attractive people perceived as clever and slightly mischievous.
