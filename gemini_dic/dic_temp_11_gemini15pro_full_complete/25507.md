bullying:/ˈbʊliɪŋ/| n.|欺凌，霸凌|The act of intimidating or harming someone weaker or more vulnerable.|He was a victim of bullying at school. 他在学校里是欺凌的受害者。|
synonyms: intimidation, harassment, persecution, victimization|
antonyms: supporting, protecting, defending|noun


bullying:/ˈbʊliɪŋ/| adj.|欺凌的，霸凌的|Relating to or characteristic of a bully.|He displayed bullying behavior. 他表现出欺凌行为。|
synonyms: intimidating, harassing|
antonyms: supportive, protective|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bully + -ing.
