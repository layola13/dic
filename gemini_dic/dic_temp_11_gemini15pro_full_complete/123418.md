monoplastic:/ˌmɒnəˈplæstɪk/ | adj. |单型的，单塑性的| Formed from a single type of plastic material.  Relating to or involving only one form or kind.| The toy was made of a monoplastic material, making it easier to recycle. 这个玩具是由单一塑性材料制成的，使其更容易回收。| 近义词: homogeneous, uniform | 反义词: heterogeneous, multi-plastic (not commonly used) |词性: 形容词
