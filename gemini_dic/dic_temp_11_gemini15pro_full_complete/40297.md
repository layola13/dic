continuousness:/kənˌtɪnjuːəsnəs/| n. |连续性，持续性|The state or quality of being continuous; uninterrupted extension in space, time, or sequence.|The continuousness of the rain made it difficult to go outside.  连绵不断的雨使得外出变得困难。|近义词：continuity, persistence, permanence|反义词：discontinuity, interruption, impermanence|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From continuous + -ness. Continuous from Late Middle English continuouse, from Late Latin continuus, from Latin continēre (“to hold together”), from com- (“together”) + tenēre (“to hold”).
