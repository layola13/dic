bechic:/ˈbiːkɪk/ | adj. | 止咳的；镇咳的 | Relieving or suppressing coughs; cough-relieving. | This lozenge is bechic and can help alleviate your cough.  这种含片有止咳作用，可以帮助缓解你的咳嗽。 | synonyms:  antitussive, pectoral | antonyms:  tussigenic,  expectorant | adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *bēchicus, from Greek βηχικός (bēchikós, “of or for a cough”), from βήξ (bēx, “cough”).
