necrologic: /ˌnɛkrəˈlɑːdʒɪk/ | adj. | 死亡的，讣告的 | Relating to or constituting an obituary; obituary. |  The necrologic notice was brief but heartfelt.  讣告简短却饱含深情。 | Synonyms: obituary, funereal, elegiac | Antonyms: celebratory, vital, living | adjective

necrology: /nɪˈkrɒlədʒi/ | n. | 讣告；死亡名单；讣闻录 | A list of deaths, especially one published annually; an obituary. | The church published a necrology of its members who had died that year. 教堂公布了一份当年去世教友的讣闻录。 | Synonyms: obituary, death list, mortality list | Antonyms: birth register, registry of births | noun
