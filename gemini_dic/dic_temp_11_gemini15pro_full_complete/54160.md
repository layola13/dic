disentanglements:/dɪsɪnˈtæŋɡlmənts/ | n. | 解开；理清；摆脱 | The act of disentangling something, or the state of being disentangled.  The process of separating things that have become twisted or knotted together.  Also refers to freeing oneself from a difficult or complicated situation. | After hours of work, she finally achieved the disentanglement of the fishing nets.  几个小时的工作后，她终于把渔网解开了。| synonyms: unraveling, untangling, extrication, separation, resolution | antonyms: entanglement, complication, confusion | noun
