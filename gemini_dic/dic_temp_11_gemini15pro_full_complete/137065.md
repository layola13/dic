nonstratified: /ˌnɒnˈstrætɪfaɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈstrætɪfaɪd/
adjective
非分层的，未分层的
Not divided or arranged into strata or layers.
The soil sample was nonstratified, suggesting it had been recently disturbed.  土壤样本是非分层的，表明它最近被扰动过。
The researcher analyzed the nonstratified sample of participants.  研究人员分析了未分层的参与者样本。
近义词: unstratified, homogeneous
反义词: stratified, layered
词性: adjective
