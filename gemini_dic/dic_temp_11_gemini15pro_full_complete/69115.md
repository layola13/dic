faver:/ˈfeɪvər/| n.| 恩惠；帮助；喜爱；偏袒 |A kind action or a helpful act; showing preference for someone or something.| Could you do me a favor and close the window? 你能帮我个忙关上窗户吗? |近义词：kindness, help, support, preference |反义词：disfavor, opposition |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *faveur, from Latin favor ("favor, goodwill, kindness, inclination, partiality"), from faveō ("I favor, am well-disposed towards").
