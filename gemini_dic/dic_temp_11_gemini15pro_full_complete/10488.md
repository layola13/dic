applique:/əˈpliːk/ /ˈæplɪkeɪ/| n. v.|贴花，补花；贴花工艺；贴饰|An applique is a decoration made of one material sewn or stuck onto another. |
例句：She decorated the dress with a beautiful floral applique. 她用漂亮的贴花装饰了连衣裙。

例句：The artist appliqued colorful patches onto the denim jacket.  这位艺术家在牛仔夹克上贴上了彩色的补丁。|近义词：patch, inlay, ornamentation|反义词: N/A|词性:noun, verb


applique:/əˈpliːk/ /ˈæplɪkeɪ/| v.|缝贴，贴上|To applique something means to sew or stick it onto something else as a decoration. |
例句：She appliqued the lace onto the collar of the blouse. 她把蕾丝贴在衬衫领子上。

例句：He appliqued a patch onto the ripped jeans. 他在破牛仔裤上贴了一块补丁。|近义词：attach, affix, sew on|反义词: detach, remove|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *appliquer*, "to apply," from Latin *applicare*, from *ad-* ("to") + *plicare* ("to fold").  The sense of "ornamentation applied to another surface" developed in the 18th century.
