fahr:/ˈfɑːrənˌhaɪt/ | adj. |华氏的|Relating to, based on, or conforming to a thermometric scale on which under standard atmospheric pressure the boiling point of water is at 212 degrees above the zero of the scale, the freezing point is at 32 degrees above zero, and the interval between the freezing and boiling points is divided into 180 equal degrees.|The temperature is 80 degrees Fahr. 温度是80华氏度。|近义词:Celsius, centigrade|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词
