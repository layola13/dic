overlapped:/ˌoʊvərˈlæpt/| adj. v. |重叠的，交叠的；（时间上）部分同时发生的|extend over and cover part of the same area; coincide partly in time|例句：The shingles on the roof overlapped. 屋顶上的瓦片相互重叠。|例句：Our vacations overlapped by a couple of days. 我们的假期重叠了几天。|近义词：coincide, overlap, intersect|反义词:separate, distinct|词性:形容词，动词

overlap:/ˌoʊvərˈlæp/| n. v. |重叠；交叠；部分相同|the amount by which two things or activities cover the same area or time|例句：There is considerable overlap between the two subjects. 这两个学科有很多重叠之处。|例句：The roof tiles overlap each other. 屋顶瓦片相互叠盖。|近义词：coincidence, intersection, convergence|反义词:separation, divergence|词性:名词，动词
