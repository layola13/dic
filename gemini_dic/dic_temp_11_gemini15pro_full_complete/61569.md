emending:/ɪˈmɛndɪŋ/
verb
修改；校订；改进
Correcting or improving a text or other work by making alterations or additions.
The editor is emending the manuscript before publication. 编辑正在出版前校订稿件。
近义词: correcting, revising, amending, improving
反义词: worsening, corrupting, damaging
