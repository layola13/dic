haycock:/ˈheɪkɒk/ (UK) /ˈheɪkɑːk/ (US)| n. |干草堆，草垛|A conical or rounded pile of hay. |例句：The farmer built a large haycock in the field. 农民在地里堆了一个大干草堆。

例句：The children played hide-and-seek amongst the haycocks. 孩子们在草垛间玩捉迷藏游戏。|近义词：haystack, rick|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *heycok, from Old English *hēacōc (attested only as a surname Hēacōc), from hēg (“hay”) + cōc (“cook, heap”). Cognate with Middle Dutch hooicook (“haycock”), German Heuhaufen (“haycock”).
