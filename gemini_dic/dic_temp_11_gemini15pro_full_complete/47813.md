decarbonizes:/diːˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪz/
verb
减少碳排放
to reduce or remove carbon, especially carbon dioxide, from something
例句：The company is investing heavily in technologies that decarbonize its operations.  这家公司正在大力投资使其运营脱碳的技术。

例句：Many countries are striving to decarbonize their economies to mitigate climate change. 许多国家正在努力使其经济脱碳以减缓气候变化。
近义词：reduce emissions, decrease carbon footprint, go green
反义词：increase emissions, pollute
