headhunts:/ˈhedˌhʌnts/| n. v. |猎头|The act of seeking out and recruiting highly skilled individuals for executive or other high-level positions.  |  Example:  The company engaged in aggressive headhunting to fill the vacant CEO position.  这家公司积极猎头以填补空缺的首席执行官职位。|Synonym: recruit, poach, scout|Antonym: dismiss, fire|noun
headhunts:/ˈhedˌhʌnts/| v. |猎头|To seek out and recruit (someone) for a position, typically a senior one. | Example:  They were headhunted by a rival firm.  他们被一家竞争对手公司挖走了。|Synonym: recruit, poach, scout|Antonym: dismiss, fire|verb
