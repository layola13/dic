koso:/ˈkoʊsoʊ/| n.| 控诉，告发 |An accusation, especially in a public setting. It's also derived from Japanese.  |The koso against the corrupt official led to his resignation. 对腐败官员的控诉导致他辞职。|近义词：accusation, charge, allegation, denunciation |反义詞: defense, vindication|词性:名词
