dodos:/ˈdoʊdoʊz/
n.
渡渡鸟（复数）
Extinct flightless bird that formerly inhabited the island of Mauritius.  It was a large, clumsy bird with a heavy beak.
The dodos were hunted to extinction. 渡渡鸟被猎杀至灭绝。

近义词：none (since it's extinct, there are no truly equivalent species)  (因为渡渡鸟已经灭绝，没有真正意义上的同类物种)
反义词：none
词性：noun
