nondelinquent:/ˌnɑːndɪˈlɪŋkwənt/ | adj. | 未拖欠的，不欠债的；无不良行为的 | Not delinquent; having fulfilled all obligations; not exhibiting socially unacceptable behavior. |  The bank reported him as a nondelinquent borrower.  银行报告说他是一位没有拖欠贷款的借款人。 | Synonyms: responsible, compliant, law-abiding, upstanding | Antonyms: delinquent, overdue, rebellious, criminal | adjective

nondelinquent:/ˌnɑːndɪˈlɪŋkwənt/ | n. | 无不良行为的人 | A person who is not delinquent. | The majority of the students in the school are nondelinquents. 学校里大多数学生都没有不良行为。 | Synonyms: conformist, good citizen | Antonyms: delinquent, offender, lawbreaker | noun
