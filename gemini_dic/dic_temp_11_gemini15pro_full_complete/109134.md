legions:/ˈliːdʒənz/
n.
军团，众多，大批
A large group of soldiers, or a vast number of people or things.
The Roman legions conquered much of Europe.  罗马军团征服了欧洲的大部分地区。
Caesar commanded vast legions of soldiers. 凯撒指挥着庞大的军团。
近义词:army, multitude, host, throng, horde
反义词:few, handful
