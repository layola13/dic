carrancha:/kəˈrɑːntʃə/ | adj. | 干瘪的; 枯萎的 | Dried-up; withered.  | The carrancha landscape stretched before them.  干枯的景象展现在他们面前。| synonym: desiccated, withered, shriveled | antonym: lush, vibrant | adjective
