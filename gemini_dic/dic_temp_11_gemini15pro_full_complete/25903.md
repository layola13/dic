burn:/bɜːrn/ (British), /bɝːn/ (American)| v. n. adj.|燃烧，烧伤，烧灼|To be on fire, to destroy or damage by fire, a mark or injury caused by fire, heat, or acid.|例句：The fire burned brightly. 火烧得很旺。|近义词：blaze, scorch, singe, char, ignite, combust|反义词：extinguish, douse, quench|词性:动词

burn:/bɜːrn/ (British), /bɝːn/ (American)| n.|烧伤，灼伤|An injury caused by fire, heat, or acid.|例句：He suffered severe burns in the accident. 他在事故中受了严重的烧伤。|近义词：scorch, sear|反义词：(none direct)|词性:名词

burn:/bɜːrn/ (British), /bɝːn/ (American)| adj.|烧着的，燃烧的|On fire.|例句：A burn smell filled the air. 空气中弥漫着烧焦的味道。(Here, "burn" functions as an attributive noun modifying "smell")|近义词：burning, fiery|反义词：(none direct)|词性:形容词
