coincider:/ˌkoʊɪnˈsaɪdər/| n.|巧合者，同时发生的事物|A person or thing that coincides with another.  Two events coinciding in time.|例句：The two conferences are coinciders, happening on the same weekend. 这两个会议恰好同时发生，都在同一个周末。

例句：The rise in crime was a coincider with the economic downturn.  犯罪率的上升与经济衰退同时发生。|近义词：correspondent, contemporary|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
