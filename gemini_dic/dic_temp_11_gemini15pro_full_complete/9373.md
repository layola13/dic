antiquarianism:/ˌæn.tɪˈkwɛə.ri.ə.nɪ.zəm/| n. |古物研究；古董癖；考古癖|The study or collection of antiquities.  A devotion to or interest in the past, especially its customs, art, and architecture.| He had a passion for antiquarianism, spending hours in dusty bookshops. 他对古物研究充满热情，在满是灰尘的书店里一待就是几个小时。|Synonyms: archaism,  antiquity, classicism|Antonyms: modernism, futurism|noun

antiquarian:/ˌæn.tɪˈkwɛə.ri.ən/| adj. n. |古文物的；古董的；收藏古董的| Relating to or dealing in antiques or rare books.
