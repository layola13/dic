bescorn:/bɪˈskɔːrn/
verb
轻蔑，嘲笑，鄙视
To treat with contempt or disdain; to mock or scorn.
He bescorned their efforts to make peace. 他对他们为和平所做的努力嗤之以鼻。
近义词: deride, mock, scoff, disdain, scorn, contempt
反义词: admire, respect, praise, esteem
词性: verb

词源:
From be- + scorn.  The prefix "be-" intensifies the meaning of the verb "scorn".
