capably:/ˈkeɪpəbli/| adv.|能干地，有能力地|Having the ability and skill to do something well.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed.|She capably handled the difficult situation. 她能干地处理了这个棘手的局面。|近义词：competently, efficiently, effectively, skillfully|反义词：incompetently, clumsily, ineptly|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "capable" + "-ly." "Capable" comes from the Late Latin "capabilis," meaning "receptive, able to grasp," from "capere" meaning "to take, seize."
