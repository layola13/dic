concubinacy:/kɒnˈkjuːbɪnɪsi/ /kɑːnˈkjuːbɪnəsi/
n.
姘居；妾侍(身份或制度)
The state of being a concubine; the practice of keeping a concubine.
The emperor's concubinage consisted of hundreds of women.  皇帝的後宮佳麗有數百人。
concubine, cohabitation
marriage, monogamy
noun

concubine:/ˈkɒŋkjʊbaɪn/ /ˈkɑːŋkjʊbaɪn/
n.
妾；姘妇；情妇
A woman who lives with a man but has lower status than his wife or wives.
In ancient China, emperors often had many concubines. 在古代中国，皇帝通常有很多妃妾。
mistress, paramour
wife
noun
