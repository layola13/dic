crunchy:/ˈkrʌntʃi/| adj.|嘎嘣脆的|Pleasantly crisp and brittle.|These chips are perfectly crunchy. 这些薯片非常脆。|Synonyms: crisp, brittle, firm|Antonyms: soft, soggy, mushy|adjective
