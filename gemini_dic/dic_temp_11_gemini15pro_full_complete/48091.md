declaw:/dɪˈklɔː/
verb
去爪
to remove the claws of (an animal, such as a cat)
Example:  The vet declawed the kitten.  兽医给小猫做了去爪手术。

Example:  They considered declawing their cat to protect their furniture. 他们考虑给猫去爪以保护家具。

近义词:  onychectomy
反义词:  None (The opposite action would be to surgically attach claws, which is not a common practice.)
词性:  verb
