gormand:/ˈɡɔːrmənd/ /ˈɡɔːrmɑːnd/| n.|贪吃的人；暴食者|A person who eats greedily or to excess; a glutton.|He was a renowned gormand, capable of devouring an entire roast chicken in one sitting. 他是个著名的贪吃鬼，能一口气吞下一整只烤鸡。|近义词：glutton, gourmand, epicure, gormandizer|反义词: ascetic, abstainer|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *gormand*, from Old French *gourmant*. Ultimately from Frankish *gurmant*, from *gurma* "throat, gullet".
