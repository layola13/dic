nonsynonymous:/ˌnɒn-sɪˈnɒnɪməs/ /ˌnɑːn-sɪˈnɑːnɪməs/| adj.|非同义的|Not having the same meaning; (of a mutation) resulting in a codon that specifies a different amino acid and hence potentially altering the function of the protein.|A nonsynonymous mutation in the gene can lead to a change in the protein's structure. 基因中的非同义突变会导致蛋白质结构的改变。|Synonyms: different, dissimilar, distinct|Antonyms: synonymous, identical, same|adjective
