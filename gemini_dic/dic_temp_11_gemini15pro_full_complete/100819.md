introduce:/ˌɪntrəˈd(j)uːs/ (US) /ˌɪntrəˈdʒuːs/ (UK)| vt. |介绍；引进；提出；采用|To make (someone) known to another by name and often other information, or to bring (something, such as a product, bill, or idea) into existence or use.|例句：I'd like to introduce my friend, John. 我想介绍一下我的朋友，约翰。

例句：The company introduced a new product line last year.  这家公司去年推出了一条新的产品线。

近义词：present, familiarize, launch, implement|反义词:remove, withdraw|词性:动词
