insightful:/ˈɪnsaɪtfəl/| adj.|有洞察力的；富有见地的|Having or showing an accurate and deep understanding; perceptive.|Her insightful comments helped us understand the problem. 她富有见地的评论帮助我们理解了这个问题。|近义词：perceptive, astute, discerning, insightful |反义词：unperceptive, obtuse, shallow|形容词

词源: From insight + -ful.  "insight" comes from the Old English "insīeht", meaning "inner sight" or "deep understanding."
