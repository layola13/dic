monogamic:/ˌmɒnəˈɡæmɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈɡæmɪk/| adj.|一夫一妻制的，单配偶制的|Practicing or involving marriage with only one person at a time.|Swans are known for their monogamic relationships. 天鹅以其一夫一妻制的关系而闻名。|Synonyms: single-mate, uniparous|Antonyms: polygamous, promiscuous|adjective

monogamy:/məˈnɒɡəmi/ /məˈnɑːɡəmi/| n.|一夫一妻制，单配偶制|The practice or state of being married to one person at a time.|Monogamy is the most common form of marriage in Western cultures. 一夫一妻制是西方文化中最常见的婚姻形式。|Synonyms: single-mate, uniparous|Antonyms: polygamy, promiscuity|noun
