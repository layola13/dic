gethsemane:/ɡɛθˈsɛməni/| n.|客西马尼|A garden at the foot of the Mount of Olives, where Jesus went with his disciples after the Last Supper. It is traditionally regarded as the place where Jesus was arrested before his crucifixion.|Jesus prayed in Gethsemane before his arrest. 耶稣在被捕前在客西马尼祷告。|近义词:garden, olive grove|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
