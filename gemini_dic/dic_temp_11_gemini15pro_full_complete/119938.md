milksopism:/ˈmɪlksɒpɪzəm/ | n. |懦弱，优柔寡断|The state or quality of being a milksop; effeminacy; lack of courage or firmness. | His milksopism prevented him from standing up to the bully. 他的懦弱使他不敢反抗恶霸。| 近义词:cowardice, timidity, pusillanimity, spinelessness | 反义词:bravery, courage, fortitude, boldness | 词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From milksop (n.) + -ism.  Milksop (n.) comes from milk + sop, referring to bread soaked in milk, considered a weak food for infants and invalids.  Therefore, a milksop is someone considered weak and unmanly.
