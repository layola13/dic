barrier:/ˈbæriər/ (US) /ˈbæriə(r)/ (UK)| n.|障碍，屏障，栅栏|something that prevents people or things from moving from one place to another, or from doing something|The language barrier made communication difficult. 语言障碍使沟通变得困难。|Synonyms: obstacle, hurdle, impediment, obstruction|Antonyms: opening, access, passage|noun

barrier:| n.|隔阂，障碍|a circumstance or obstacle that keeps people or things apart or prevents communication or progress|There are many barriers to overcome in order to achieve success. 要想取得成功，有许多障碍需要克服。|Synonyms: hurdle, obstacle, impediment|Antonyms: aid, assistance, help|noun

barrier:| n.|关卡，检票口|a structure or gate across a road or path, especially one used to control traffic or restrict access|They lifted the barrier and let the car through. 他们抬起了关卡，让汽车通过。|Synonyms: gate, barricade, fence|Antonyms: entrance, opening|noun
