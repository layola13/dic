lordlily:/ˈlɔːrdˌlɪli/ | n. | 君主百合 | A type of lily, possibly a poetic or invented name, evoking a sense of regal beauty and stature. It doesn't correspond to a recognized botanical name. |  The poet described a field of lordlilies, their white petals gleaming in the moonlight.  诗人描述了一片君主百合，它们白色的花瓣在月光下闪闪发光。 | 近义词：lily, regal lily | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific flower) | 词性:noun

lordlily:/ˈlɔːrdˌlɪli/ | adj. | 高贵的，如君主般的（通常用于修饰百合花）| Used poetically or figuratively to describe something, usually a lily, as majestic and noble like a lord.  |  The lordlily white flowers stood tall and proud. 高贵洁白的百合花傲然挺立。 | 近义词：regal, majestic, stately | 反义词:humble, common | 词性:adjective
