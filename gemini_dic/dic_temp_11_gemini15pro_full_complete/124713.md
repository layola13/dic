moxy:/ˈmɑːksi/ | n. | 魄力，勇气，胆量 | Skill and determination; nerve; audacity.  A combination of skill, courage, and determination. | She had the moxy to stand up to the bully. 她有勇气对抗恶霸。 |  近义词: nerve, guts, chutzpah, grit, backbone | 反义词: timidity, cowardice, fear | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Yiddish  *moxie* (energy, courage, pep), perhaps ultimately from Hebrew  *m’ots* (strength, energy, force, courage)
