dropsicalness:/drɒˈsɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 水肿; 浮肿 | The condition of being dropsical; excessive accumulation of fluid in the body tissues. |  His dropsicalness was a symptom of his failing heart. 他的水肿是心力衰竭的症状。 |  edema, swelling, bloating | dehydration, leanness | noun

Etymology:
From dropsical + -ness.  Dropsical comes from Late Latin hydropicus, from Greek hydrōpikos, from hydrōps "dropsy," from hydōr "water" + ōps "appearance."
