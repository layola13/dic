leanly:/ˈlinli/| adv.|精瘦地，简洁地，贫乏地|Without any excess; sparingly or meagerly.  Characterized by a lack of fat; thin and wiry.  Containing little or no fat.|He ate leanly, mostly vegetables and fish. 他吃得很清淡，主要是蔬菜和鱼。 The company was run leanly, with a minimal staff.  这家公司精简运营，员工很少。|近义词：thinly, sparingly, meagerly, simply|反义词：richly, lavishly, extravagantly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From lean (adjective) + -ly (adverb suffix).  The adjective "lean" comes from Old English "hlæne," meaning "thin, meager," from Proto-Germanic "*khlinaz,"  related to Old High German "hlīni" (lean, thin).
