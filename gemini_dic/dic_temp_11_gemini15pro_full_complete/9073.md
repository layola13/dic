antimilitarists:/ˌæntiˈmɪlətərɪsts/
n.
反军事主义者
People who oppose war and military activities.
The antimilitarists protested the government's decision to increase military spending. 反军事主义者抗议政府增加军费的决定。
The group of antimilitarists organized a peace march. 这群反军事主义者组织了一场和平游行。
近义词:pacifists, peace activists
反义词:militarists, warmongers
词性: Noun


antimilitarist:/ˌæntiˈmɪlətərɪst/
adj.
反军事的
Opposed to war and military activities.
She holds antimilitarist views. 她持有反军事的观点。
The article presented an antimilitarist perspective on the conflict. 这篇文章提出了关于冲突的反军事观点。
近义词:pacifistic
反义词:militaristic, pro-war
词性: Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix) + militarist.
"anti-" comes from Greek, meaning "against" or "opposite".
"militarist" comes from "military" + "-ist" (suffix forming agent nouns).  "Military" ultimately derives from Latin "militaris" meaning "of soldiers, of warfare".
