acceleratory:/əkˈsɛləˌrɔːri/ /əkˈsɛləˌtɔːri/
adjective
加速的，促进的
Serving to accelerate; accelerating. Increasing the speed of something.
The government is implementing acceleratory policies to boost the economy. 政府正在实施加速政策以刺激经济。
New technologies have had an acceleratory effect on globalization. 新技术对全球化产生了加速作用。

近义词:speeding, quickening, hastening, expediting
反义词:decelerating, slowing, hindering, impeding
词性:adjective
