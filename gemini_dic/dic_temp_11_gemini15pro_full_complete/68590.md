fantad:/ˈfæntæd/ | n. |幻想；幻觉；空想|A product of imagination; a fantasy. | He had a fantad of winning the lottery. 他幻想中了彩票。 | synonyms: fantasy, delusion, illusion, hallucination, dream, vision | antonyms: reality, truth | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Likely a shortened and altered form of "fantasy," from Old French "fantasie," ultimately from Greek "phantasia" meaning "appearance, imagination."
