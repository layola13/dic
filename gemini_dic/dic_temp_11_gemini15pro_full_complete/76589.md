gallinaceous:/ˌɡælɪˈneɪʃəs/| adj.|鸡形的；类似鸡的|Relating to or resembling fowl of the order Galliformes, such as chickens, turkeys, and pheasants.|例句：Guinea fowl are gallinaceous birds often raised for meat.  珍珠鸡是经常被饲养用作肉食的鸡形目鸟类。

例句：The farm specialized in raising gallinaceous poultry. 这家农场专门饲养鸡形目家禽。|近义词：avian, fowl-like|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider non-gallinaceous)|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gallināceus*, from *gallīna* ("hen") + *-āceus* (suffix forming adjectives).
