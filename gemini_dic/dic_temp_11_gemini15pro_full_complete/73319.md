forecourts:/ˈfɔːrkɔːrts/ (UK), /ˈfɔːrkɔːrts/ (US)| n. |前院，广场，加油站前的空地|An open area in front of a large building or petrol/gas station. |The hotel has a large forecourt with a fountain.  这家酒店有一个带喷泉的大前院。

The petrol station's forecourt was crowded with cars. 加油站前的空地挤满了汽车。|近义词：courtyard, plaza, apron |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "backyard" in some contexts) |词性: noun
