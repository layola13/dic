heartily:/ˈhɑːrtɪli/ | adv. | 衷心地，热诚地，|With genuine warmth and enthusiasm; sincerely.  |例句：He thanked her heartily for her help. 他衷心感谢她的帮助。|近义词：warmly, sincerely, enthusiastically|反义词：coldly, apathetically|词性:副词

heartily:/ˈhɑːrtɪli/ | adv. | 丰盛地，大量地|In a hearty manner; vigorously; to a great extent. |例句：They ate heartily after the long hike. 长途跋涉后，他们饱餐了一顿。|近义词：abundantly, copiously|反义词：sparsely, meagerly|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hertely*, from Old English *heortlice* (“heartly, heartily, bravely, courageously, willingly, earnestly”), equivalent to *heart* + *-ly*. Cognate with German *herzlich* (“cordial”).
