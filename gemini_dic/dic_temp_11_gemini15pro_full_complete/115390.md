manslaughtered:/ˈmænˌslɔːtərd/ | v. |被过失杀害的 |Killed unlawfully but without malice aforethought.  | The driver was manslaughtered in the accident.  司机在事故中被过失杀害。| Synonyms: killed, slain | Antonyms: saved, rescued | verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *manslaghter, equivalent to man +‎ slaughter.  Compare Old English mansliht, manslæht (“manslaughter”).  Surface analysis is man +‎ slaughter.
