discuss:/dɪˈskʌs/ | v. | 商量，商讨 | To consider or examine by argument, comment, etc.; talk over or write about, especially to explore solutions; debate. | They discussed the matter for hours. 他们讨论了几个小时。| synonyms: negotiate, bargain, arrange | antonyms: disregard, dismiss | verb
