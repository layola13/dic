haemapoietic:/ˌhiːməpɔɪˈɛtɪk/ /ˌheməpɔɪˈɛtɪk/| adj.|造血的；生血的|Relating to or involved in the formation of blood cells.|例句：Bone marrow is the primary haemapoietic tissue in adult humans.  成年人骨髓是主要的造血组织。

例句：Haemapoietic stem cells are responsible for the constant replenishment of blood cells throughout life. 造血干细胞负责在人的一生中不断补充血细胞。|近义词：hematopoietic, blood-forming|反义词:|adjective
