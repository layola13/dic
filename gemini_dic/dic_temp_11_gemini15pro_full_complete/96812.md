inertly:/ɪˈnɜːrtli/
adverb
惰性地;迟钝地;不活泼地
in a way that is not active or lively.
The gas hung inertly in the air. 这种气体惰性地悬浮在空中。
Synonyms: passively, inactively, sluggishly, listlessly
Antonyms: actively, energetically, dynamically
词性: 副词


inert:/ɪˈnɜːrt/
adjective
惰性的，不活泼的
lacking the ability or strength to move, or not moving
He lay inert on the floor. 他一动不动地躺在地板上。
Synonyms: inactive, passive, motionless, immobile
Antonyms: active, dynamic, energetic, lively
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Latin *inert-, from iners “unskilled, inactive,” from in- “not” + ars “skill, art”
