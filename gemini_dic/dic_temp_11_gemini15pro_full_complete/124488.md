mouldies:/ˈməʊldiz/ | n. | 发霉的东西；发霉的食物 | Things that are mouldy, especially food. |  The bread had gone bad and was covered in mouldies.  面包坏了，上面长满了霉菌。 | synonyms: mould, mildew, fungus | antonyms: fresh, clean | noun

mouldy:/ˈməʊldi/ | adj. | 发霉的；陈腐的 | Covered with or smelling of mould; stale. | The cheese was mouldy and had to be thrown away. 奶酪发霉了，不得不扔掉。| synonyms: musty, mildewed, fusty | antonyms: fresh, clean | adjective
