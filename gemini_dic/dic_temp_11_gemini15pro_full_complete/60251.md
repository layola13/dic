effuse:/ɪˈfjuːz/| v.|流出；散发；倾诉；|To flow out freely; to emanate; to express (feelings, etc.) freely and openly.|The flowers effused a sweet fragrance. 这些花散发出甜甜的香味.|近义词：exude, emanate, pour out, gush, radiate|反义词:contain, suppress, withhold|词性:动词
effuse:/ɪˈfjuːz/| n.|流出；溢出|The act of effusing or that which is effused.|The effuse of emotion after the victory was palpable. 胜利后情绪的流露是显而易见的。|近义词：outpouring, outflow, discharge|反义词:suppression, containment|词性:名词

词源: From Latin effusus, past participle of effundere "to pour out," from ex- "out" + fundere "to pour."
