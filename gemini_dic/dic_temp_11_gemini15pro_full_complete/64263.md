equipondious:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɒndɪəs/ /ˌiːkwɪˈpɑːndɪəs/
adjective
等重的；相等的
of equal weight; equivalent
例句：The two objects are equipondious. 这两个物体重量相等。

例句：The equipondious arguments presented by both sides made it difficult for the judge to reach a verdict. 双方提出的等效论据使法官难以作出裁决。

近义词：equal, equivalent, commensurate
反义词：unequal, disparate, different
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *aequipondiōsus, from aequi- "equal" + pondus "weight."
