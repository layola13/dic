obstructers:/əbˈstrʌktərz/
noun
阻碍者，妨碍者
People or things that obstruct or block something.
The obstructers parked their cars across the driveway, preventing anyone from entering. 阻碍者把他们的车停在了车道上，阻止任何人进入。

The fallen tree was a major obstructer to traffic. 倒下的树是交通的主要障碍。

Synonyms: hinderers, blockers, impediments, obstacles
Antonyms: facilitators, enablers, helpers, supporters
词性: 名词
