bizarreness:/bɪˈzɑːrnəs/
noun
奇异；古怪；怪诞
The quality of being very strange or unusual.
The sheer bizarreness of the situation was astonishing.  这种情况的怪诞程度令人震惊。
Synonyms: strangeness, oddity, peculiarity, eccentricity, outlandishness
Antonyms: normality, ordinariness, usualness, commonality
词性: 名词

bizarre:/bɪˈzɑːr/
adjective
奇异的；古怪的；怪诞的
Very strange or unusual.
She had a bizarre dream last night. 她昨晚做了一个怪诞的梦。
Synonyms: strange, odd, peculiar, eccentric, outlandish
Antonyms: normal, ordinary, usual, common
词性: 形容词
