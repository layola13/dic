forming:/ˈfɔːrmɪŋ/| v. |形成，构成，塑造|The clouds are forming into interesting shapes. 云朵正在形成有趣的形状。|例句：The children are forming a circle. 孩子们正在围成一个圈。|近义词：creating, developing, shaping, making, composing|反义词：destroying, dismantling, dissolving|Verb

Etymology: From Middle English *formen*, from Old French *former*, from Latin *formāre* (“to form, shape, build, fashion, create, arrange, imagine”), from *forma* (“form, shape, figure, appearance, beauty”).
