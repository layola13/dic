conny:/ˈkɒni/ (UK) /ˈkɑːni/ (US)| n. |兔子; (尤指)野兔|A rabbit, especially a wild one. This word is primarily used in British English, particularly in dialects.|Example:  I saw a conny darting across the field. 我看到一只野兔飞快地穿过田野。
Example: The farmer caught a conny in his snare.  农夫在他的陷阱里抓到了一只野兔。|近义词: rabbit, hare|反义词:  (No direct antonym for an animal)|词性: noun
