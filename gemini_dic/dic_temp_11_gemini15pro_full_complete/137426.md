nontheist:/ˌnɒnˈθiːɪst/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈθiːɪst/ (UK)| n.|无神论者|A person who does not believe in the existence of God or gods.|例句：He identified himself as a nontheist.  他自称是无神论者。

例句：She explained her nontheist worldview. 她解释了她的无神论世界观。|近义词：atheist, disbeliever, infidel, skeptic|反义词：theist, believer|词性: 名词

nontheistic:/ˌnɒnθiˈɪstɪk/ (US) /ˌnɒnθiˈɪstɪk/ (UK)| adj.|无神论的|Relating to or characteristic of nontheism; not believing in God or gods.|例句：He holds a nontheistic belief system. 他持有无神论的信仰体系。

例句：Her perspective on life is nontheistic. 她的人生观是无神论的。|近义词：atheistic, unbelieving|反义词：theistic, believing|词性: 形容词
