bleeding-heart:/ˈbliːdɪŋ hɑːrt/ | adj. n. | 多愁善感的（人）；悲天悯人的（人）|  Someone who is excessively sympathetic towards those who are perceived as suffering or disadvantaged.  Derived from the bleeding-heart flower, whose shape resembles a heart with a drop of blood. |  She's such a bleeding-heart, always getting upset about stray animals. 她真是个多愁善感的人，总是为流浪动物感到难过。|  He donated a large sum of money to the charity, earning him the label of a bleeding-heart liberal.  他向慈善机构捐了一大笔钱，这让他赢得了“悲天悯人的自由主义者”的称号。| 近义词：compassionate, empathetic, sentimental | 反义词：callous, uncaring, apathetic | 词性: 形容词，名词

bleeding-heart:/ˈbliːdɪŋ hɑːrt/ | n. |荷包牡丹 |  A garden plant with heart-shaped flowers, typically pink and white, that resemble a heart with a drop of blood. | The bleeding-hearts in her garden were in full bloom. 她花园里的荷包牡丹开满了花。 | She planted bleeding-hearts along the border of the flowerbed.  她在花坛的边缘种了荷包牡丹。 | 近义词：Dicentra spectabilis (scientific name) | 反义词：N/A | 词性: 名词
