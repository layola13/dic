aggrandizer:/əˈɡrændaɪzər/
n.
夸大者；吹牛者；自我吹嘘者
A person who exaggerates or makes something appear greater than it actually is.
He was a self-aggrandizer, always boasting about his achievements.  他是一个喜欢自我吹嘘的人，总是吹嘘自己的成就。
The politician was accused of being an aggrandizer, inflating his accomplishments for personal gain.  这位政客被指责夸大其词，为了个人利益夸大自己的成就。

Synonyms: boaster, braggart, show-off
Antonyms: self-deprecator, minimizer
词性: 名词
