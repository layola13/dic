dairyman:/ˈdɛəriˌmæn/ | n. | 奶农；乳品商人 | A person who works in or owns a dairy farm or business, dealing with the production and sale of milk and other dairy products. |  The dairyman milks the cows twice a day.  奶农每天挤两次牛奶。

The dairyman delivers fresh milk to the local shops. 奶农将新鲜牛奶送到当地商店。 | 近义词：dairy farmer, milkman | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From dairy + -man. Dairy comes from Middle English deyerie, from Old English deyrie "female dairy servant," from deye "dairymaid," of uncertain origin. -man is a common suffix denoting a person who performs a particular activity or holds a particular occupation.
