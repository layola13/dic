detentions:/dɪˈtɛnʃənz/
n.
拘留，扣留，延迟
The act of detaining someone or something.
The school principal imposed several detentions for disruptive behavior. 校长对扰乱秩序的行为处以了几次拘留。
After several detentions, he finally started to behave in class. 在几次拘留之后，他终于开始在课堂上守规矩了。
confinement, imprisonment, custody
release, liberation
noun
