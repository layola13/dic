cogency:/ˈkoʊdʒənsi/| n. |说服力，信服力|The cogency of his argument convinced the jury. |例句：The cogency of her plea swayed the judge. 她恳求的说服力打动了法官。

例句：His argument lacked cogency and failed to persuade the audience.  他的论证缺乏说服力，未能说服听众。|近义词：persuasiveness, potency, validity, strength|反义词：weakness, invalidity, fallacy|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin cogentia, from cogent- ‘compelling’, from the verb cogere (see coerce).
