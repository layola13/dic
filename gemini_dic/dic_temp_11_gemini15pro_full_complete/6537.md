amyl: /ˈæmɪl/ | n. |戊基; 淀粉| A univalent radical, C5H11, of the paraffins, existing in several isomeric forms.  It is the characteristic radical of amyl alcohol and other amyl compounds, and enters into a large number of organic substances. | Amyl acetate is commonly used as a solvent. 戊酸戊酯通常用作溶剂。| 近义词: pentyl | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

amyl: /ˈæmɪl/ | adj. |戊基的; 含淀粉的|  Relating to or derived from amyl alcohol; containing the amyl radical. | Amyl nitrite is a vasodilator. 亚硝酸戊酯是一种血管扩张剂。| 近义词: pentylic | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
