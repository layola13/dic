isomeric:/ˌaɪsəˈmɛrɪk/ | adj. | 同分异构的 | Relating to or exhibiting isomerism; having the same chemical composition but different structural forms. |  Glucose and fructose are isomeric sugars. 葡萄糖和果糖是同分异构糖。| isomeric compounds are molecules with the same molecular formula but different structural formulas. 同分异构化合物是具有相同分子式但不同结构式的分子。 | synonyms: isomerous | antonyms: non-isomeric | adjective
