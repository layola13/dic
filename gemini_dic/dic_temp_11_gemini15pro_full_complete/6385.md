amphigean:/æmˈfɪdʒiən/
adj.
两栖的；陆水两栖的
Living or occurring both on land and in water.
Amphigean species can survive in both terrestrial and aquatic environments.  两栖物种可以在陆地和水生环境中生存。
Salamanders are often considered amphigean.  蝾螈通常被认为是两栖的。
近义词: amphibious
反义词: terrestrial, aquatic
词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek amphi- (“on both sides, around”) + gaia (“earth”).
