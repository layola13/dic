heroics:/hɪˈroʊɪks/| n. |英雄行为；英勇事迹|Great bravery or daring action, especially in a difficult situation.  |例句：The firefighters' heroics saved many lives. 消防员的英勇行为挽救了许多生命。

例句：Her heroics during the flood were truly remarkable.  她在洪水期间的英勇行为确实非凡。|近义词：bravery, courage, valor, daring, heroism |反义词: cowardice, timidity |词性:noun
