feature:/ˈfiːtʃər/ (US), /ˈfiːtʃə/ (UK)| n. v.| 特色，特征；容貌；特写；主演|A distinctive attribute or aspect of something; a prominent aspect of the face; a special article or program highlighting a particular person, place, or topic; to prominently display or present something.|

例句：
The car's best feature is its fuel efficiency. 这辆车的最大特点是省油。

This magazine features an article on the latest fashion trends. 这本杂志刊登了一篇关于最新时尚潮流的文章。


近义词：characteristic, trait, attribute, quality, aspect, highlight
反义词: flaw, defect, shortcoming
词性: noun, verb


feature:/ˈfiːtʃər/ (US), /ˈfiːtʃə/ (UK)| v.| 以...为特色；主演|To prominently display or present something; to have as a prominent performer or ingredient.|

例句：
The film features a young and talented cast.  这部电影由年轻而有才华的演员主演。

The restaurant features a variety of local dishes. 这家餐厅以各种当地菜肴为特色。



近义词：highlight, showcase, star, spotlight
反义词: exclude, omit
词性: verb
