decidability:/dɪˌsaɪdəˈbɪləti/| n. |可判定性|The quality of being decidable; the property of a problem or question for which an effective method exists to determine the answer.|The decidability of the halting problem is a fundamental concept in computer science.  停机问题的可判定性是计算机科学中的一个基本概念。|synonyms: solvability, computability|antonyms: undecidability, insolvability|noun

decidability:/dɪˌsaɪdəˈbɪləti/| n. |可决定性|The quality of a question or problem that can be definitively answered or solved.|The decidability of the theorem remains a topic of ongoing research.  该定理的可判定性仍然是一个持续研究的课题。|synonyms: definability, resolvability|antonyms: indeterminacy, undecidability|noun
