overassess:/ˌoʊvərəˈses/ | v. | 过度评估；过高估计 | To assess something too highly or too thoroughly. | The company overassessed the value of its assets.  这家公司高估了其资产的价值。

The teacher overassessed the students' abilities. 老师高估了学生的能力。 | 近义词: overestimate, overvalue | 反义词: underestimate, undervalue | 词性: 动词
