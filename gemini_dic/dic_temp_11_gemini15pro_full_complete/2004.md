actuality:/ˌæktʃuˈæləti/ | n. |现实，实际情况；现状|The actuality of the situation was far worse than they had imagined. 现实情况比他们想象的要糟糕得多。|The movie blurs the line between actuality and fiction. 这部电影模糊了现实和虚构之间的界限。|近义词：reality, fact, truth |反义词：fantasy, illusion, fiction |词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin actualitas, from Latin actualis (“active, practical”), from actus (“an act, action, performance, a doing”), from agere (“to do, act, drive”).
