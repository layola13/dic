fulling:/ˈfʊlɪŋ/| n.| 缩绒；洗呢；漂洗|The process of cleansing, shrinking, and thickening woven cloth, especially wool, by moisture, heat, and pressure.  将织物，特别是羊毛，通过湿气、热量和压力进行清洁、收缩和加厚的过程。|The craftsman specialized in the fulling of woolen cloth.  这位工匠专门从事羊毛织物的缩绒。|Synonyms: felting, milling|Antonyms: stretching, expanding|noun
