obscured:/əbˈskjʊərd/
adjective
模糊的，昏暗的，晦涩的
Hidden, unclear, or difficult to understand.
The moon was obscured by clouds. 月亮被云遮住了。
synonyms: hidden, veiled, clouded, indistinct, ambiguous, vague
antonyms: clear, obvious, distinct, transparent, evident
词性: adjective


obscured:/əbˈskjʊərd/
verb
使模糊，使昏暗，使晦涩
To hide or make unclear.
The fog obscured the view. 雾遮蔽了视线。
synonyms: hide, conceal, cover, veil, mask, shroud
antonyms: reveal, uncover, expose, clarify, illuminate
词性: verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *obscuren, from Latin obscūrāre (“to darken, obscure”), from obscūrus (“dark, obscure, indistinct”).
