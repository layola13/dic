Chittagong: /ˌtʃɪtəˈɡɒŋ/ (UK), /ˌtʃɪtəˈɡɔːŋ/ (US) | n. |吉大港|A major port city and the commercial center of southeastern Bangladesh, located on the Karnaphuli River.|He visited Chittagong on his business trip to Bangladesh. 他在去孟加拉国的商务旅行中访问了吉大港。|Synonyms: Chattogram (official name), port city|Antonyms: N/A|noun

Etymology:
The origin of the name "Chittagong" is uncertain. Several theories exist, including derivations from Bengali, Arabic, and Persian.  The official name is now Chattogram (চট্টগ্রাম), reflecting the local pronunciation.
