canoeists:/kəˈnuːɪsts/
n.
独木舟运动员；划独木舟的人
A person who paddles a canoe.
The canoeists paddled down the river. 独木舟运动员沿着河向下划。
Canoeists enjoyed the calm waters of the lake.  独木舟运动员享受着湖面平静的水域。
kayakers, paddlers
non-canoeists (This is not a standard word, but expresses the opposite idea.)
noun
