boogieing:/ˈbuːɡiɪŋ/ | v. | 跳布吉舞 | Dancing to boogie music, a style of blues and jazz music characterized by a strong, rhythmic beat. |  They were boogieing all night long. 他们整晚都在跳布吉舞。| 近义词：dancing, grooving | 反义词: standing still, sitting | verb

boogie:/ˈbuːɡi/ | n. | 布吉舞 | A style of blues and jazz music with a strong, rhythmic beat.  | The band played a lively boogie. 乐队演奏了一曲活泼的布吉舞曲。| 近义词：blues, jazz | 反义词: - | noun

词性
verb

词源
From the term "boogie-woogie", a style of blues piano playing.
