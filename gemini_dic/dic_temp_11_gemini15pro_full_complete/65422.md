ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek εὐφορία (euphoría, “easy bearing, fertility”), from εὔφορος (euphoros, “bearing well, fertile”), from εὖ (eu, “well”) + φέρω (pherō, “I bear, carry”).
