integrator:/ˈɪntɪɡreɪtər/| n.|积分器；集成商；整合者|A device or person that integrates.  Something that combines parts into a whole.|例句：The electronic integrator calculates the area under the curve. 电子积分器计算曲线下的面积。

例句：The company acts as an integrator of different software systems.  这家公司充当不同软件系统的集成商。|近义词：combiner, unifier, assembler, consolidator|反义词:separator, divider|noun
