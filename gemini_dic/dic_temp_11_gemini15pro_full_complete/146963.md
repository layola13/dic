overissue:/ˈoʊvərɪʃuː/ /ˈəʊvərɪʃuː/| n. v. |过度发行；过量发行；超额发行|The act of issuing more of something, especially currency or stock, than is authorized or desirable.  An excessive amount of something issued.|

例句：
The overissue of currency can lead to inflation.
货币过度发行会导致通货膨胀。

The company's overissue of stock diluted the value of existing shares.
该公司股票的过度发行稀释了现有股票的价值。

近义词：overproduction, glut, surplus, excess
反义词：undersupply, shortage, scarcity
词性: noun, verb
