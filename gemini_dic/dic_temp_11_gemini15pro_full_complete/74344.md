fragmenting:/ˈfræɡmɛntɪŋ/
verb
分裂，破碎
breaking or causing something to break into small separate parts
The empire began fragmenting into small independent states.  这个帝国开始分裂成许多小的独立国家。
The report shows a society fragmenting along racial lines. 这份报告显示一个社会正沿着种族界限分裂。
Synonyms: disintegrating, splitting, breaking apart, shattering
Antonyms: uniting, merging, consolidating, joining
