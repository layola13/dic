counterflory:/ˈkaʊntərˌflɔːri/ | n. | 反花饰，对饰，反衬花饰 | An architectural ornament, especially in Gothic architecture,  representing flowers or leaves turned downwards, usually placed in alternation with upright ornaments. | The intricate counterflory along the arch added a touch of elegance to the cathedral.  教堂拱门上复杂的反花饰增添了一丝优雅。| The craftsman meticulously carved the counterflory on the wooden panel.  工匠一丝不苟地在木板上雕刻反花饰。 | Synonyms: counterdecoration, contrasting ornament | Antonyms:  main ornament, principal decoration | Noun
