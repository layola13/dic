extrapolative:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪtɪv/| adj.|外推的，推断的|Based on extending a trend or data set beyond the area where it is known or has been measured; conjectural; inferential.|例句：The company's extrapolative predictions about future market share proved to be overly optimistic.  该公司对未来市场份额的外推预测被证明过于乐观。

例句：Making extrapolative judgments about the entire population based on a small sample size can be misleading.  根据小样本量对整个人口做出推断性判断可能会产生误导。|近义词：conjectural, inferential, predictive, projected|反义词：interpolative, measured, observed|词性:adjective
