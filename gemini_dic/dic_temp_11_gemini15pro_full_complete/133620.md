nonforbearances: /ˌnɑːnfɔːrˈbɛərənsɪz/ | n. | 不宽容；不耐心；不忍耐；不 forbearance 的状态或行为 |  The bank's nonforbearances on late payments led to many foreclosures.  这家银行对逾期付款的不宽容导致许多房屋被取消抵押品赎回权。 | 近义词: intolerance, impatience, severity | 反义词: forbearance, patience, leniency | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "forbearance" (from Old French "forberance," from "forberer" - to endure, tolerate).  "Non-" 前缀表示“不”，"forbearance" 来自古法语 "forberance"，源自 "forberer" - 忍受，容忍。
