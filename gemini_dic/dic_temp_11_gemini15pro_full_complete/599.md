abox:/əˈbɒks/| adv.| 在船上；上船；在火车上；上火车 | aboard a ship or train; in or into a ship or train | The passengers went abox the train.  乘客们上了火车。 | 近义词: aboard, on board | 反义词: ashore, disembarked | 词性: 副词

abox:/əˈbɒks/| prep.| 在…上；到…上 | on or into (a ship, train, etc.) |  They climbed abox the ship. 他们登上了船。 | 近义词: aboard, on board | 反义词: ashore, off | 词性: 介词

abox (词源): From a- + box (n.), originally meaning "in a box," later generalized to any kind of enclosure, especially a ship or train.

注意事项:
abox 作为介词和副词，意义相近，用法略有不同。介词后面需要跟名词，而副词则不需要。  在现代英语中，aboard 更常用。
