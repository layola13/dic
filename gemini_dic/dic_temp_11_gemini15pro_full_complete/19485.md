bidding's:/ˈbɪdɪŋz/
noun
出价；投标；命令；邀请
The process of offering prices for something, especially at an auction. Also, the act of commanding or inviting.
The bidding's started at $10,000.  拍卖起价为1万美元。
The king issued his bidding's to the court. 国王向宫廷发出了他的命令。
synonyms: offers, commands, instructions, invitations
antonyms: requests, pleas, supplications
