optionalize:/ˌɒpʃənəlaɪz/ | v. | 使成为可选 | To make something optional; to allow something to be chosen or not chosen. |  The company decided to optionalize the retirement plan. 公司决定将退休计划改为可选。|  You can optionalize the extra features on the car. 你可以选择汽车上的额外功能。| synonyms: make optional, allow as an option, offer as a choice | antonyms: make mandatory, require, compel | verb
