despairfully:/dɪˈspeərfəli/
adverb|绝望地；失望地|In a way that shows or expresses despair. |She looked at him despairfully. 她绝望地望着他。|Near Synonyms: hopelessly, despondently, dejectedly|Near Antonyms: hopefully, optimistically
Etymology: From despair + -ful + -ly.  Ultimately from the Latin dēspērāre ("to give up hope").
