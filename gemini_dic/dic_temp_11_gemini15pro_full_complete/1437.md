ace-up-my-sleeve:/ˌeɪs ˌʌp maɪ ˈsliːv/
noun
王牌；秘密武器；锦囊妙计
A secret advantage or resource that can be used when needed.
I had an ace up my sleeve – I knew the CEO personally. 我有一个秘密武器——我私下认识首席执行官。
Synonyms: secret weapon, hidden advantage, trump card
Antonyms: disadvantage, weakness
词性: 名词短语
