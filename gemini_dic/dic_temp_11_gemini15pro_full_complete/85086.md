hardbake:/ˈhɑːrdbɪːk/ /ˈhɑːrdbeɪk/| n. v.|硬糖果；杏仁饼干；严厉批评；|A hard, brittle candy made of caramelized sugar and nuts, especially almonds.  (n.) To criticize harshly. (v.)|例句：She gave him a real hardbake for arriving late. 她因为他迟到狠狠地批评了他。

The bakery is famous for its delicious hardbake. 这家面包店以其美味的杏仁饼干而闻名。|近义词：(n.)brittle, toffee, almond roca,  (v.)berate, scold, reprimand|反义词:(v.)praise, commend|词性:名词，动词

hardbake:/ˈhɑːrdbɪːk/ /ˈhɑːrdbeɪk/| adj.|坚硬的；不妥协的；|Uncompromising; inflexible.|例句：He has a hardbake attitude towards negotiations. 他在谈判中态度强硬，不妥协。

The hardbake clay was difficult to mold.  这坚硬的粘土很难塑形。|近义词：inflexible, unyielding, rigid, firm|反义词:flexible, yielding, soft|词性:形容词
