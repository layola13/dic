legalities:/liːˈɡælɪtiz/
n.
合法性，法律程序，法律手续
The legal aspects of a matter; the legal requirements.
例句：
The legalities of the contract are complex.  合同的法律程序很复杂。

例句：
We need to ensure all the legalities are covered before we proceed.  我们需要确保在进行之前所有法律手续都已完成。

近义词：legal aspects, legal requirements, legal formalities
反义词：illegalities
词性：名词
