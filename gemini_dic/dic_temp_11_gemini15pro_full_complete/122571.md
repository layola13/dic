moderation:/ˌmɒdəˈreɪʃən/ /ˌmɑːdəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |适度；节制；缓和|The key to a healthy lifestyle is moderation in all things.  健康生活方式的关键在于凡事适度。|例句：He advocated moderation in all things.  他提倡凡事都要适度。|近义词：temperance, restraint, control|反义词：excess, immoderation|词性:名词

moderation:/ˌmɒdəˈreɪʃən/ /ˌmɑːdəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |主持；仲裁|The debate was under the moderation of a neutral panel. 辩论由一个中立小组主持。|例句：The teacher acted as a moderation in the student dispute. 老师在学生纠纷中充当了调解人。|近义词：arbitration, mediation|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English moderacion, from Old French moderacion, from Latin moderātiō (“a regulating, governing”), from moderātus (“moderate, kept within bounds”), past participle of moderārī (“to moderate, regulate, restrain”), from modus (“measure, manner, mode”).
