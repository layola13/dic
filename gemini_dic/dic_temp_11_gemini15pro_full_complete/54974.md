disproportionally:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənəli/
| adv. |不成比例地，不相称地|In a way that is out of proportion; excessively.|例句：Minority groups are disproportionately affected by unemployment. 失业对少数群体的影响尤为严重。|近义词：unequally, unevenly, unfairly|反义词：proportionally, equally, evenly|词性：副词

disproportionate:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənət/
| adj. |不成比例的，不相称的|Too large or too small in comparison with something else.|例句：The punishment was disproportionate to the crime. 惩罚与罪行不相称。|近义词：unequal, uneven, excessive|反义词：proportionate, equal, balanced|词性：形容词

词源:  From dis- + proportionate, from Latin proportionatus, past participle of proportionare "to proportion," from proportio "proportion, analogy," from pro "for" + portio "portion, share."  The prefix dis- adds the sense of "not" or "opposite of."
