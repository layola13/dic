gutted:/ˈɡʌtɪd/ | adj. | 非常失望的，沮丧的；被毁坏的，掏空的 | Extremely disappointed, devastated; destroyed or emptied inside. |  I was absolutely gutted when my team lost the final. 我当我的队伍输掉决赛时，我非常失望。

The fire gutted the building, leaving only the outer walls standing. 大火烧毁了大楼，只剩下外墙矗立着。| 近义词：devastated, heartbroken, disappointed, destroyed, ruined | 反义词：delighted, thrilled, happy, intact | 词性:形容词

gutted:/ˈɡʌtɪd/ | v. | 掏空，取出内脏；摧毁…的内部 | To remove the internal organs of; to destroy the inside of something. |  They gutted the fish before cooking it. 他们在烹饪之前把鱼的内脏掏空了。

The rioters gutted several stores in the city center. 暴徒们洗劫了市中心的几家商店。 | 近义词：eviscerate, disembowel, destroy, devastate | 反义词：repair, rebuild, restore | 词性:动词
