ectozooen:/ˌɛktoʊzoʊˈɛn/| n. |外寄生动物|An ectozooen is a parasitic animal that lives on the exterior of its host.|例句：Ticks and lice are examples of ectozooen. 蜱虫和虱子是外寄生动物的例子。|例句：The ectozooen caused severe skin irritation to the host.  这种外寄生动物引起了宿主的严重皮肤刺激。|近义词：ectoparasite|反义词:endoparasite|词性:noun


词源:
From ecto- + zooen, from Greek zōion "animal."
