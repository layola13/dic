boasting: /ˈboʊstɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbəʊstɪŋ/ (UK)| n. & adj.| 自夸，夸耀；值得夸耀的 | the act of talking about oneself or one's achievements with excessive pride; something that a person or place is proud to have |  He was boasting about his new car. 他一直在吹嘘他的新车。 | synonyms: bragging, crowing, vaunting | antonyms: modesty, humility | noun

boasting: /ˈboʊstɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbəʊstɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |值得夸耀的| having something to be proud of | The town is boasting a new sports center.  这个镇以拥有一座新的体育中心而自豪。| synonyms: proud, pleased | antonyms: ashamed, embarrassed | adjective
