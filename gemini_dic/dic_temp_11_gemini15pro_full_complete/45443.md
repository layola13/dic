curacy:/ˈkjʊərəsi/| n.|准确性，精确性|The curacy of the data is crucial for the experiment's success.  数据的准确性对于实验的成功至关重要。|例句：The clockmaker prides himself on the curacy of his timepieces. 这位钟表匠以他的钟表的精确性而自豪。

例句：The curacy of her aim ensured she hit the bullseye. 她瞄准的精确性确保她正中靶心。|近义词：accuracy, precision, exactness|反义词:inaccuracy, imprecision, vagueness|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *curatia, from Latin cura (“care, attention”).
