osteopath:/ˈɒstiəpæθ (UK) / ˈɑːstiəpæθ (US)| n.|骨病医生，整骨医生|A practitioner of osteopathy, a system of complementary medicine involving the treatment of medical disorders through the manipulation and massage of the skeleton and musculature.|例句：I went to see an osteopath for my back pain. 我去看骨病医生治疗背痛。|例句：The osteopath manipulated my neck to relieve the tension. 整骨医生按摩我的脖子以缓解紧张。|近义词：chiropractor, physical therapist|反义词:|noun
