notary:/ˈnoʊtəri/ (US), /ˈnəʊtəri/ (UK)| n.|公证人；公证员|A public official authorized to witness signatures, certify documents, and perform other official acts.|Example: The notary verified the signatures on the contract. 公证员核实了合同上的签名。

Example:  You need to find a notary public to notarize this document. 你需要找个公证人来公证这份文件。

|近义词：notary public,  clerk, official|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


notaries:/ˈnoʊtəriz/ (US), /ˈnəʊtəriz/ (UK)| n.|公证人（复数）|Plural form of "notary," referring to multiple notaries public.|Example: The notaries were busy authenticating the documents.  公证员们正忙着验证文件。

Example: Several notaries are available at the courthouse.  法院有几位公证员。
