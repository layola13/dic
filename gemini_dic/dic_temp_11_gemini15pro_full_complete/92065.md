hyperpromiscuousness:/ˌhaɪpərˌprɑːmɪˈskjuːəsnəs/ | n. |过度滥交，极度滥交|The state of engaging in numerous casual sexual encounters with different partners.  It implies a degree of indiscriminateness or lack of selectivity in choosing partners, often exceeding what is considered socially acceptable.|Studies suggest a correlation between childhood trauma and hyperpromiscuousness in some individuals. 研究表明，童年创伤与某些人的过度滥交之间存在关联。|synonyms:  promiscuity, wantonness, licentiousness |antonyms: chastity, fidelity, monogamy | noun
