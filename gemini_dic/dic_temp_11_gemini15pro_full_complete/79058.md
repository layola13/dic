giver:/ˈɡɪvər/| n.|给予者，赠予者|A person who gives something, especially habitually.|例句：He is a generous giver to charity. 他是一个慷慨的慈善捐赠者。

例句：She's known as a giver, always putting others' needs before her own. 她以乐于助人而闻名，总是把别人的需求放在自己之前。


近义词：donor, contributor, benefactor|反义词:taker, receiver|词性:noun
