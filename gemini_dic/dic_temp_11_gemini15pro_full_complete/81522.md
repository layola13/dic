gravitas:/ˈɡrævɪtɑːs/ (US), /ˈɡrævɪtæs/ (UK)| n.| 庄重，严肃，威严| Dignity, seriousness, or solemnity of manner.|  The judge spoke with gravitas, conveying the seriousness of the situation. 法官讲话庄重，传达了情况的严重性。

The historian recorded the events with appropriate gravitas.  这位历史学家以恰如其分的庄重记录了这些事件。


|近义词：seriousness, solemnity, dignity, weight, importance, gravity|反义词：frivolity, levity, flippancy|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gravitas* "weight, heaviness, seriousness," from *gravis* "heavy."  源自拉丁语 *gravitas*，意为“重量、沉重、严肃”，来自 *gravis*，意为“重的”。
