datura:/dəˈtʃʊərə/ /dəˈtʊrə/ | n. |曼陀罗，洋金花|A genus of poisonous plants with large, trumpet-shaped flowers, belonging to the nightshade family. Several species are cultivated for their ornamental flowers, but they are all highly toxic. |例句：The datura plant is known for its beautiful but dangerous flowers. 曼陀罗植物以其美丽但危险的花朵而闻名。

例句：He accidentally ingested some datura and became seriously ill. 他不小心摄入了一些曼陀罗，病得很重。


|近义词：jimsonweed, thorn-apple|反义词:|n.
