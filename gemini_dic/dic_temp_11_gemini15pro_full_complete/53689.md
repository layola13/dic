disciplinedly:/ˈdɪsəplɪndli/ | adv. | 纪律严明地，训练有素地 | In a controlled and ordered way, showing a high level of self-discipline. |  She worked disciplinedly, focusing on one task at a time. 她工作很有条理，一次只专注于一项任务。|  He trained disciplinedly for the marathon, sticking to a strict schedule. 他为了马拉松进行了严格的训练，坚持着严格的计划。 | 近义词: rigorously, methodically, systematically | 反义词: haphazardly, erratically, carelessly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From "disciplined" + "-ly". "Disciplined" comes from the Latin word "disciplina" meaning "instruction, knowledge".
