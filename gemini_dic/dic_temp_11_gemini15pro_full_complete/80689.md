gorge:/ɡɔːrdʒ/| n.|峡谷；咽喉；暴食|A deep, narrow valley with steep sides, often with a stream running through it.  Also refers to the throat or the act of eating greedily. |We hiked through the magnificent gorge. 我们徒步穿越了壮丽的峡谷。|近义词：canyon, ravine, chasm, gulch|反义词:plain, plateau|noun

gorges:/ˈɡɔːrdʒɪz/| v. (third-person singular simple present gorges, present participle gorging, simple past gorged, past participle gorged)|狼吞虎咽；塞满|To eat greedily and to excess; to stuff or cram full.|He gorged himself on the buffet. 他在自助餐上大吃特吃。|近义词：stuff, cram, devour, guzzle|反义词:nibble, peck|verb
