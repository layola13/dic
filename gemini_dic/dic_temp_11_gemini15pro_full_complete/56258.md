dogcatcher:/ˈdɒɡˌkætʃə(r)/ (US) /ˈdɒɡˌkætʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. |捕犬员；动物控制员|A person employed to catch stray dogs.|The dogcatcher took the lost poodle to the animal shelter. 捕犬员把那只走失的贵宾犬带到了动物收容所。|Synonyms: animal control officer, pound keeper|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From dog + catcher.  "Catcher" comes from the verb "catch," which has roots in Old North French "cachier" and ultimately Latin "captiare," meaning "to seize."
