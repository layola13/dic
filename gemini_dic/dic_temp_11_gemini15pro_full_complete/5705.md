alumni: /əˈlʌmnaɪ/ (plural), /əˈlʌmnəs/ (singular masculine), /əˈlʌmnə/ (singular feminine)
noun
毕业生；校友
A graduate or former student of a particular school, college, or university.
例句：The alumni of Harvard University are a powerful network. 哈佛大学的校友是一个强大的网络。

例句：The university invited its alumni to the graduation ceremony. 大学邀请了校友参加毕业典礼。

近义词：graduate, former student
反义词:current student
词性:noun
