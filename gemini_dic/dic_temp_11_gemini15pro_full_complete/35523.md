coagulable:/koʊˈæɡjələbəl/
adjective
可凝结的
Capable of being coagulated; tending to coagulate.
The doctor tested to see if the patient's blood was coagulable. 医生测试以查看病人的血液是否可凝结。
近义词: clottable, congealable
反义词: incoagulable, noncoagulable
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin coagulābilis, from coagulāre (to curdle, congeal) + -bilis (capable of being).
