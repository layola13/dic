antemural:/ˌæntɪˈmjʊərəl/| n. adj.|防御工事，外围防御; 防御性的|A defensive wall or other protective barrier placed before the main wall of a fortified place.  Something serving as a defense or safeguard.|例句：The antemural of the castle was breached by the enemy's siege weapons. 敌人的攻城武器攻破了城堡的外围防御工事。

例句：The city's strong economic policies served as an antemural against the recession. 这座城市强有力的经济政策成为了抵御经济衰退的防御工事。|近义词：rampart, bulwark, barrier, defense, safeguard|反义词: vulnerability, weakness|词性:名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *antemuralis, from Latin ante ("before") + murus ("wall").
