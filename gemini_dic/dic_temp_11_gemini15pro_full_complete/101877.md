ischiatic:/ɪskiˈætɪk/ | adj. | 坐骨的，坐骨神经的 | Relating to the ischium (the lower and back part of the hip bone). | The ischiatic nerve is the largest nerve in the body. 坐骨神经是人体最大的神经。 | The patient complained of ischiatic pain. 病人抱怨坐骨疼痛。 | 近义词: sciatic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *ischiaticus, from Greek iskhiadikos, from iskhio-, combining form of iskhion "hip joint, hip."
