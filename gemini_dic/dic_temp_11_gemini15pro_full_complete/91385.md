hydrotherapies:/ˌhaɪdrəˈθɛrəpiz/
| n. |水疗法|The treatment of disease or injury using water.  It encompasses a broad range of approaches and techniques, including  whirlpools, underwater massage, and aquatic exercise.|She underwent a series of hydrotherapies to help with her arthritis. 她接受了一系列的水疗法来治疗她的关节炎。|Synonyms: water therapy, balneotherapy, aquatic therapy|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "dry needling," "land-based exercise")|noun
