minibibliography:/ˌmɪniˌbɪbliˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. |小型参考书目，简要参考书目 |A minibibliography is a short list of books, articles, or other sources related to a particular topic.  It is often included at the end of a paper or chapter. |  This chapter concludes with a minibibliography on the topic of artificial intelligence.  本章最后附有一个关于人工智能主题的简要参考书目。| synonyms: short bibliography, select bibliography, brief bibliography | antonyms: comprehensive bibliography, extensive bibliography | noun
