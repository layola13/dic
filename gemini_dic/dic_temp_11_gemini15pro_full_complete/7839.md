antapoplectic:/ˌæntɪˌæpəˈplɛktɪk/ | adj. | 预防中风的，缓解中风的 | Good for preventing or relieving apoplexy (stroke). |  Example: The doctor prescribed antapoplectic medication to the patient. 医生给病人开了预防中风的药。

Example:  He believed a daily walk was antapoplectic. 他认为每天散步可以预防中风。 | 近义词: anti-apoplectic, stroke-preventing | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek "anti-" (against) +  "apoplexia" (stroke).
