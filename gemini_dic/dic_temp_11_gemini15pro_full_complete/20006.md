biological:/ˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | /ˌbaɪəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 生物的；生物学的 | Relating to biology or living organisms. |  Biological research is crucial for understanding life. 生物学研究对于理解生命至关重要。|  The effects of the chemical were purely biological. 这种化学物质的影响纯粹是生物性的。| 近义词: organic, living | 反义词: inorganic, non-biological | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- (life) + -logical (relating to the study of). The term "biology" itself comes from the Greek words "bios" (life) and "logos" (study).
