augurous:/ˈɔːɡərəs/ | adj. | 吉兆的，预示好运的 | Suggesting a positive and successful future. |  The augurous meeting between the two leaders suggested a new era of peace.  两国领导人之间预示好兆的会面表明和平的新时代即将到来。

| 近义词：promising, favorable, auspicious, propitious | 反义词：ominous, inauspicious, unfavorable, sinister | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *augurōsus*, from *augur* (soothsayer, diviner) + *-ōsus* (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *awg-/*aug- (to increase, strengthen).
