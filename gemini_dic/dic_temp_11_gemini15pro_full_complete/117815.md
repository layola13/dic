melissylene:/ˌmɛlɪˈsaɪliːn/ | n. | 蜜蜂烯，香蜂草烯 | A bicyclic monoterpene, C10H16, found in the oils of several plants, especially lemon balm (Melissa officinalis). It is used in perfumery. |  Melissylene is a key component of lemon balm oil, giving it a characteristic lemony scent. 蜜蜂烯是香蜂草油的关键成分，赋予其特有的柠檬香味。 | The perfumer used melissylene to create a fresh, citrusy fragrance. 调香师使用蜜蜂烯来创造一种清新、柑橘味的香水。 | synonyms:  lemon balm oil component, bicyclic monoterpene | antonyms: N/A | noun
