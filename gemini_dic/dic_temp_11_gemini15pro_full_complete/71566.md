flash:/flæʃ/ | n. v. |闪光，闪现；(想法) 突然出现；闪光灯；一瞬间；(尤指愤怒的) 闪现；(装饰用的) 闪光物 | A flash of lightning lit up the sky. 一道闪电照亮了天空。|近义词：gleam, flicker, glimpse, burst|反义词：dim, fade|词性:名词；动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *flasa "to rush, dash," related to Old English *flæscian "to strip flesh from." The original sense was likely related to a sudden movement or burst.
