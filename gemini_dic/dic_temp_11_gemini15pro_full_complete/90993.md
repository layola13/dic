hurtless:/ˈhɜːrtlɪs/| adj.|无害的，不伤人的|Harmless; causing no pain or injury.  Not intended to cause offense or upset.|The comment was meant to be hurtless, but she still took offense. 这句话本意是无害的，但她仍然生气了。|近义词：harmless, innocuous, inoffensive|反义词：harmful, hurtful, injurious|词性:形容词
词源: From hurt + -less.  Essentially meaning "without hurt."
