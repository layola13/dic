hypnotherapist:/ˌhɪpˈnəθɛrəpɪst/ | n. |催眠治疗师|A therapist who uses hypnosis to treat medical or psychological disorders. |例句：The hypnotherapist helped me quit smoking.  催眠治疗师帮助我戒烟了。

例句：I'm seeing a hypnotherapist for my anxiety. 我正在看一位催眠治疗师治疗我的焦虑。|近义词： hypnotist |反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性: noun


Etymology->
From hypno- + therapist.  Hypno- comes from Greek hypnos "sleep." Therapist comes from Greek therapeutes "attendant, servant," later "one who takes care of the sick."
