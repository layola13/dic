burster:/ˈbɜːrstər/ (US) /ˈbɜːstə(r)/ (UK)| n.|爆裂物；爆破器；突发事件；（尤指工作或活动）一阵短时间的爆发|Something that bursts or causes something to burst.  A device that bursts or explodes. A sudden, brief period of intense activity, especially of work or effort.|The fireworks display ended with a loud burster.  烟火表演以一声巨响的爆裂物结束。|synonyms:exploder, detonator, cracker, blaster|antonyms:dud, fuse (when referring to a malfunctioning explosive)|noun
