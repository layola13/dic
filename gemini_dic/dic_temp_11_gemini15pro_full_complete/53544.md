disavowment:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊmənt/ | n. |否认，拒绝承认，放弃|The government issued a disavowment of the ambassador's remarks. 政府发表声明，否认了大使的言论。|例句：His disavowment of the debt left his family in financial ruin. 他拒不承认债务，使他的家庭陷入经济困境。

例句：The company issued a public disavowment of the employee's actions. 公司公开声明否认该员工的行为。

近义词：denial, rejection, repudiation, renunciation|反义词：acknowledgment, acceptance, admission, affirmation|词性：名词
