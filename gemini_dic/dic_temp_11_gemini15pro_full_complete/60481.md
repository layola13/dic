ejectment:/ɪˈdʒɛktmənt/| n. |驱逐，逐出；收回（房产）|The act of ejecting; expulsion. The legal process to recover possession of real property. |The landlord sought ejectment of the tenants for non-payment of rent. 房东因租户未缴纳租金而寻求将其驱逐。|The family faced ejectment from their home. 这家人面临着被赶出家门的困境。|近义词：eviction, expulsion, ouster|反义词: admittance, inclusion|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin ējectāmentum, from ēject-, past participle stem of ēicere "to throw out," from ex- "out" + jacere "to throw."
