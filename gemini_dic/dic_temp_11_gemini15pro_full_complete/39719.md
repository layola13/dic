consignments:/kənˈsaɪnmənts/
n.
货物; 寄售品
a batch of goods destined for or delivered to someone.
The company received a large consignment of new computers.  这家公司收到了一大批新的电脑。
We've just had a new consignment of furniture. 我们刚收到一批新家具。
近义词：shipment, delivery, batch, load
反义词:  N/A
词性: 名词
