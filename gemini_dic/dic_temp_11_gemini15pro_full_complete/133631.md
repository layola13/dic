nonforest:/ˌnɒnˈfɒrɪst/ | n. | 非森林 |  Land that is not classified as forest, typically lacking a certain density and height of trees.  |  Example:  The nonforest area was used for agriculture.  非森林地区用于农业。

Example:  The researchers studied the biodiversity of both forest and nonforest habitats. 研究人员研究了森林和非森林栖息地的生物多样性。  | 近义词:  non-timbered, unwooded, treeless, open land | 反义词: forest, woodland, timbered | 词性: noun
