forgivableness:/fərˈɡɪvəblnəs/| n. |可原谅性，宽恕|The forgivableness of her mistake depends on the context. 她的错误是否可以原谅取决于具体情况。|例句：The forgivableness of his actions is debatable.  他的行为是否值得原谅还有待商榷。

例句：He admired her forgivableness and compassion. 他钦佩她的宽宏大量和同情心。|近义词：pardonableness, excusableness|反义词: unforgivableness, unpardonableness|词性:名词

Etymology: From 'forgive' + '-able' + '-ness'.  'Forgive' comes from Old English 'forgiefan', meaning "to give, grant, bestow; to give up, remit (a debt, sin, etc.)."  '-able' is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of, fit for, tending to, given to." '-ness' is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, expressing a state or quality.
