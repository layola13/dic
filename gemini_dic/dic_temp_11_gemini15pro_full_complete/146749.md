overhumanizes:/ˌoʊvərˈhjuːmənaɪzɪz/
verb
过分人性化，过分赋予人性
attributes human qualities or emotions to something that isn't human, to an excessive degree.
The film overhumanizes the robots, making them seem more like people than machines.  这部电影过分人性化了机器人，使它们看起来更像人而不是机器。
anthropomorphizes, personifies
dehumanizes
