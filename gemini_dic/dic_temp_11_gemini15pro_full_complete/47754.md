decalogist:/dɪˈkælədʒɪst/
n.
十诫专家，精通摩西十诫的人
A person who is an expert in the Decalogue (the Ten Commandments).
The decalogist explained the nuances of the seventh commandment. 十诫专家解释了第七诫的细微差别。
近义词: theologian, biblical scholar
反义词: None
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *dekalogos* (ten words, the Ten Commandments) + *-ist* (one who practices or is an expert in).  *deka-* (ten) + *logos* (word, speech).
