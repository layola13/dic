go-missing:/ɡəʊ ˈmɪsɪŋ/
verb phrase
失踪，下落不明
To become lost or disappear; to be unaccounted for.
My keys have gone missing. 我的钥匙不见了。
The hiker went missing in the mountains.  这名徒步旅行者在山中失踪了。
近义词: disappear, vanish, get lost
反义词: appear, be found
词性: verb phrase
