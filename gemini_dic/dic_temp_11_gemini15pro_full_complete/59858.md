ectropion:/ɛkˈtroʊpiən/
noun
外翻；睑外翻
the eversion of an edge or margin, as of an eyelid
Ectropion is a condition in which the eyelid turns outwards. 睑外翻是一种眼睑向外翻的疾病。
The lower eyelid ectropion caused excessive tearing. 下眼睑外翻导致过度流泪。
近义词: eversion
反义词: entropion
词性: n.
