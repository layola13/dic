nonebulliently:/ˌnɒnɪˈbʌliəntli/ | adv. | 不兴高采烈地，沉闷地 |  In a manner lacking enthusiasm or exuberance; not bubbly or lively.  It describes a state of being quiet, subdued, or lacking in energy. | He accepted the news nonebulliently, his face showing no sign of joy. 他毫无热情地接受了这个消息，脸上没有一丝喜悦。| The team nonebulliently walked off the field after their defeat.  球队在失败后闷闷不乐地走下了赛场。 | 近义词: listlessly, apathetically, dully, lifelessly | 反义词: enthusiastically, excitedly, joyfully, exuberantly | 词性: 副词

词源:  "Nonebulliently" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "ebullient" (meaning "overflowing with enthusiasm") and the adverbial suffix "-ly."  "Ebullient" itself comes from the Latin "ebullire," meaning "to boil over."
