ephemera: /ɪˈfɛmərə/ | n. | 短暂存在的事物;蜉蝣; ephemera | things that exist or are used or enjoyed for only a short time. |  Old concert tickets and brochures are ephemera. 旧的音乐会门票和小册子都是些转瞬即逝的东西。 | synonyms: transience, short-lived items, memorabilia | antonyms: memorabilia, keepsakes, permanent things | noun

ephemeral: /ɪˈfɛmərəl/ | adj. | 短暂的;朝生暮死的 | lasting for a very short time. |  Mayflies are ephemeral insects, living only for a day.  蜉蝣是生命短暂的昆虫，只活一天。| synonyms: transient, fleeting, short-lived | antonyms: permanent, enduring, lasting | adjective
