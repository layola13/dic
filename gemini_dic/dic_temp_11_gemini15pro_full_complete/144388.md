outstricken:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪkən/
adjective
受灾的，受折磨的
severely affected by something, especially by disease or misfortune
例句：The outstricken community received aid from around the world. 受灾社区收到了来自世界各地的援助。

例句：The outstricken family struggled to cope with their loss.  这个饱受打击的家庭努力应对他们的损失。

近义词：afflicted, stricken, devastated
反义词：healthy, thriving


词性: adjective

词源：out- + stricken (past participle of strike).  "Out-" intensifies the meaning of "stricken," implying a more severe or widespread affliction.
