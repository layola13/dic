fascinators:/ˈfæsɪneɪtərz/
n.
头饰；女用小礼帽；令人着迷的人或物
A fascinator is a decorative headpiece, typically a small, elaborate ornament attached to a band or clip, worn by women at formal occasions.  It can also refer to something or someone that fascinates.
She wore a striking fascinator to the wedding. 她戴着一顶引人注目的头饰参加婚礼。

Her intelligence and wit make her a true fascinator. 她的智慧和机智使她成为一个真正令人着迷的人。

Synonyms: headpiece, ornament, decoration, charmer, enchanter
Antonyms: bore
