intertwined:/ˌɪntərˈtwaɪnd/| adj.|交织的，缠绕的，密切相关的|twisted together or closely connected so as to be difficult to separate|例句：Their destinies are intertwined. 他们的命运紧密相连。|例句：The branches of the trees were intertwined, forming a natural canopy. 树枝交错缠绕，形成了一个天然的树冠。|近义词：interwoven, entangled, connected, linked|反义词：separate, disentangled, unconnected|词性:形容词

词源:
From inter- + twine.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "twine" means to twist together.
