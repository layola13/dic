compendious:/kəmˈpendiəs/| adj.|简明的，扼要的；全面的，包罗万象的|Containing or presenting much information concisely.|This compendious volume provides a comprehensive overview of the subject. 这本简明的书对该主题进行了全面的概述。|近义词：concise, succinct, summary, brief, comprehensive, thorough|反义词：prolix, verbose, lengthy, rambling|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin compendiosus, from compendium (see compendium).
