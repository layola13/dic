coacting:/koʊˈæktɪŋ/
adjective
共同作用的，互相影响的
acting together or in concert
The coacting forces of wind and rain eroded the cliff face. 风雨共同作用侵蚀了悬崖表面。
近义词: interacting, synergistic, cooperating
反义词: opposing, antagonistic
词性: adjective

coact:/koʊˈækt/
verb
共同行动，互相影响
to act together or in concert
The two drugs coact to produce a stronger effect.  这两种药物共同作用产生更强的效果。
近义词: interact, cooperate, collaborate
反义词: oppose, counteract
词性: verb
