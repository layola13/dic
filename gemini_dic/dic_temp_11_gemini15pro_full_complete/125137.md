multi-position:/ˌmʌlti pəˈzɪʃən/ | adj. | 多位置的，多功能的 | Able to be used in or adapt to multiple positions or configurations. | This multi-position chair can be adjusted for sitting, reclining, or even lying down.  这把多功能椅子可以调节成坐姿、斜躺甚至平躺的姿势。|  versatile, adjustable, adaptable, flexible | fixed, single-position | adjective

multi-position:/ˌmʌlti pəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 多位置，多功能 | A state or quality of having multiple positions or functions.  |  The multi-position functionality of this tool makes it a valuable addition to any workshop.  这个工具的多功能性使其成为任何车间的重要补充。 | versatility, adjustability, adaptability, flexibility | fixity, single-function | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much") + "position" (from Old French "posicion" and ultimately from Latin "positio" meaning "a placing, situation").
