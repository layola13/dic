anti-imperialists:/ˌæntiɪmˈpɪəriəlɪsts/| n.|反帝国主义者|People who oppose imperialism, the policy of extending a country's power and influence through diplomacy or military force.|例句：The anti-imperialists protested the country's military actions overseas. 反帝国主义者抗议该国在海外的军事行动。

例句：Many anti-imperialists believed that colonialism was morally wrong. 许多反帝国主义者认为殖民主义在道德上是错误的。|近义词：nationalists, activists, revolutionaries|反义词:imperialists, colonialists|词性:noun (plural)


anti-imperialist:/ˌæntiɪmˈpɪəriəlɪst/| adj.|反帝国主义的|Opposed to imperialism.|例句：The anti-imperialist movement gained momentum in the early 20th century. 反帝国主义运动在20世纪初获得了动力。

例句：They held an anti-imperialist rally to voice their opposition to the war. 他们举行了一场反帝国主义集会，表达他们对战争的反对。|近义词：nationalistic, anti-colonial|反义词:imperialist, colonial|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + imperialist.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek "anti." "Imperialist" comes from "imperial," relating to an empire, ultimately from Latin "imperium," meaning "command" or "power."
