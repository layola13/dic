ETYMOLOGY->
From "ice" + "skate" (n.), from Dutch "schaats," probably related to Old French "eschasse" (stilt).  由“ice”（冰）和“skate”（冰鞋）组成，“skate”来自荷兰语“schaats”，可能与古法语“eschasse”（高跷）有关。
