equitably:/ˈɛkwɪtəbli/
adverb|公平地，公正地|in a fair and impartial manner|例句：The resources were distributed equitably among the team members. 资源在团队成员之间公平分配。|近义词：fairly, justly, impartially|反义词:unfairly, unjustly, biasedly|词性:副词
equitably:|词源: From Latin *aequitabilis*, from aequitas ("equality, fairness") + -abilis ("-able").|
