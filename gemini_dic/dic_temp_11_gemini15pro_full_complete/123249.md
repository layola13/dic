monocytes:/ˈmɒnəˌsaɪts/ (US) /ˈmɒnəʊˌsaɪts/ (UK)| n.|单核细胞|A type of white blood cell that is part of the immune system and helps fight infection.  They are the largest type of leukocyte and can differentiate into macrophages and dendritic cells.|A high monocyte count can indicate infection or inflammation. 单核细胞计数高可能表明感染或炎症。|leukocytes, white blood cells|erythrocytes (red blood cells)|noun
词性:noun
