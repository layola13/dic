contrarian:/kənˈtreəriən/ | n. adj. | 反向投资者；逆向思维者；持相反意见者 | a person who opposes or rejects popular opinion or established practice. |  He's a contrarian who always takes the opposite view. 他是一个反向投资者，总是持相反的观点。 | synonyms: nonconformist, dissenter, maverick, iconoclast | antonyms: conformist, follower, traditionalist | noun

contrarian:/kənˈtreəriən/ | adj. |  反向的；逆向思维的；持相反意见的 | opposing or rejecting popular opinion or established practice. | He has a contrarian investment strategy, buying when others are selling. 他有一个反向投资策略，在别人卖出的时候买入。 | synonyms: dissenting, opposing, unconventional | antonyms: conforming, conventional, orthodox | adjective
