funnel:/ˈfʌn(ə)l/| n. v. |漏斗；漏斗状物；烟囱|A cone-shaped utensil with a tube at the apex for conducting liquid or other substances through a small opening.|Use a funnel to pour the oil into the engine. 用漏斗把油倒进发动机里。|近义词：channel, conduit, tube|反义词: outlet, opening|名词
funnel:/ˈfʌn(ə)l/| v. |（使）流经狭窄空间；汇集；集中|To move or be moved through a narrow opening or space, as into a funnel.|The crowd funneled into the stadium.  人群涌入体育场。|近义词：channel, direct, guide|反义词: disperse, scatter|动词
