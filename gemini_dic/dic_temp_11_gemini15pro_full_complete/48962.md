degenerate:/dɪˈdʒɛn.ə.reɪt/ /dɪˈdʒɛn.ɚ.ət/ | adj. vi. n.|退化的，堕落的；退化，恶化；堕落者|A degenerate gambler lost his family's savings. 一个堕落的赌徒输光了家里的积蓄。|近义词：decayed, deteriorated, corrupt, depraved|反义词：noble, virtuous, moral, upright|词性：形容词

degenerate:/dɪˈdʒɛn.ə.reɪt/ /dɪˈdʒɛn.ɚ.ət/ | vi.|退化，恶化|His health degenerated quickly after the diagnosis. 诊断后他的健康迅速恶化。|近义词：decline, worsen, deteriorate|反义词：improve, ameliorate, recover|词性：动词

degenerate:/dɪˈdʒɛn.ə.reɪt/ /dɪˈdʒɛn.ɚ.ət/ | n.|堕落者|He was considered a degenerate by his community. 他被他的社区认为是一个堕落者。|近义词：reprobate, scoundrel, miscreant|反义词：gentleman, saint, paragon|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin degeneratus, past participle of degenerare "to depart from one's kind, deteriorate," from de- "from" + genus "race, kind, stock."
