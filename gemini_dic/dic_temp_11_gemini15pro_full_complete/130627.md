nonadministrant:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪnɪstrənt/
noun
非管理人员；非行政人员
a person who is not an administrator.
The nonadministrant staff members were not privy to the company's financial decisions. 非管理人员没有参与公司的财务决策。
近义词: non-manager, staff member, employee
反义词: administrator, manager, executive
词性: noun

nonadministrant:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪnɪstrənt/
adjective
非管理的；非行政的
not related to administration.
The nonadministrant duties included cleaning and maintenance. 非管理职责包括清洁和维护。
近义词: non-managerial, non-executive
反义词: administrative, managerial, executive
词性: adjective
