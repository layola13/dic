inconsequent:/ɪnˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ | adj. | 不合逻辑的，不一致的，前后矛盾的 | Not following logically from or agreeing with what has gone before.  |  His argument was rambling and inconsequent. 他的论点杂乱无章，前后矛盾。 | synonyms: illogical, inconsistent, contradictory, incompatible, conflicting, discordant, discrepant | antonyms: consistent, logical, coherent | adjective

inconsequent:/ˌɪnˈkɒnsɪkwent/ | adj. | 无关紧要的，不重要的 | Not important or significant. | The details are inconsequent to the main issue. 这些细节对主要问题无关紧要。 | synonyms: unimportant, insignificant, trivial, trifling, negligible, immaterial, irrelevant | antonyms: important, significant, relevant | adjective
