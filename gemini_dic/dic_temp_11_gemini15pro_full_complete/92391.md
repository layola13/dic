hypocycloid:/ˈhaɪpəʊˌsaɪklɔɪd/ | n. | 内摆线 | A plane curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle rolling internally on the circumference of a fixed circle. |  The spirograph can draw beautiful hypocycloids.  这台万花尺可以画出美丽的内摆线。|  近义词: roulette (更广义的轮线) | 反义词: epicycloid (外摆线) | noun
