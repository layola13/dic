bellyband: /ˈbeliˌbænd/| n. | 腹带；束腹带；腰带；(书的)腰封 | A piece of material worn around the belly, either for support or warmth.  A band of paper wrapped around a book, often containing promotional information. |  The pregnant woman wore a bellyband to support her growing abdomen. 孕妇戴着腹带以支撑她日益增大的腹部。

The publisher used a bellyband to highlight the book's awards. 出版商用腰封来突出这本书的奖项。 | 近义词：band, girdle, waistband, cummerbund | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词
