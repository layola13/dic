brown-bagged: /ˈbraʊnˌbæɡd/
| adj. |自带午餐的；自带酒水的|Bringing one's own lunch, typically in a brown paper bag, to work or school.  It also refers to bringing one's own alcoholic beverages to an event or establishment.|
例句：
She brown-bagged it to work every day to save money. 她每天自带午餐上班以省钱。

He brown-bagged a six-pack to the party. 他自带了六罐啤酒去派对。

|近义词：pack a lunch, BYOB (Bring Your Own Booze/Beverage),|反义词: eat out, dine out |
词性: 形容词
