orotundity:/ˌɔːrəˈtʌndɪti/
n.
（声音、风格等的）洪亮，雄浑；（语言）夸张，夸大其词
Pompous or inflated language; the quality of being bombastic.
The senator's orotundity made some listeners believe that he was speaking from the heart, but others suspected that he was merely being theatrical.  参议员雄浑的语气使一些听众相信他是发自内心的，但其他人怀疑他只是在演戏。
The orotundity of the language in the epic poem added to its grandeur. 史诗中夸张的语言增添了它的雄伟气势。
近义词:bombast, grandiloquence, pomposity, magniloquence
反义词:simplicity, plainness, conciseness, understatement
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ōrōtundus* (round-mouthed, full-sounding), from *ōs* (mouth) + *rotundus* (round).
