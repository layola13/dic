lanthanums:/ˈlænθənəmz/ | n. |镧（元素符号La）的复数形式|The plural form of lanthanum, a chemical element with the symbol La and atomic number 57. It is a soft, silvery-white metal that tarnishes rapidly when exposed to air. Lanthanums are part of the lanthanide series of elements.| Lanthanums are used in some types of batteries. 镧被用在某些类型的电池中。 |Synonyms: (for lanthanum singular) rare earth element, lanthanide |Antonyms: None (elements do not have antonyms)| noun
