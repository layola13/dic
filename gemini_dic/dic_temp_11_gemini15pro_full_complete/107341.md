lambswool:/ˈlæmzˌwʊl/| n. |羊羔毛，羊羔毛制品|Lambswool is the first wool shorn from a lamb, typically softer and finer than wool from adult sheep.  It's often used for making high-quality clothing. |This sweater is made of lambswool. 这件毛衣是羊羔毛做的。|
The baby blanket is made of soft lambswool. 这婴儿毯是由柔软的羊羔毛制成的。|Synonyms: fleece, wool|Antonyms: synthetic fiber|noun
