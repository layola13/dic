clavicle: /ˈklævɪkəl/ | n. | 锁骨 | The clavicle, or collarbone, is a long bone that connects the sternum (breastbone) to the scapula (shoulder blade). |  The doctor examined his clavicle after the fall. 医生在他摔倒后检查了他的锁骨。|  The cyclist fractured his clavicle in the crash.  骑自行车的人在撞车事故中摔断了锁骨。 | synonyms: collarbone | antonyms: N/A | noun

clavin: /ˈklævɪn/ | n. |棒状体，锁骨素 (在某些文献中也指锁骨, 但更常见的是指棒状体, 一种生物碱) | Clavin is an alkaloid isolated from the fungus *Aspergillus clavatus*. It's known for its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. | Clavin has shown promising results in treating certain bacterial infections. 棒状体在治疗某些细菌感染方面显示出良好的效果。 |  Researchers are studying the potential of clavin as a new antibiotic. 研究人员正在研究棒状体作为一种新抗生素的潜力。 | synonyms:  (for alkaloid meaning)  patulin,  (If referring to bone) collarbone, clavicle | antonyms: N/A | noun
