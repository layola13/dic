focusses:/ˈfəʊkəsɪz/ /ˈfoʊkəsɪz/| v.| 集中；关注；聚焦|The photographer focussed the camera on the flower. 摄影师把相机对准了花。|例句：The company is focussed on expanding its market share.  这家公司专注于扩大市场份额。|近义词：concentrates, centers, zeroes in|反义词:distracts, ignores|词性:动词

focusses:/ˈfəʊkəsɪz/ /ˈfoʊkəsɪz/| n.| 焦点；中心点 (较少用，常用复数形式 foci)|The main focus of the meeting was the budget. 会议的重点是预算。|例句：The earthquake's focus was 10 miles underground. 地震的震源在地下10英里处。|近义词：center, core, focal point|反义词:periphery, margin|词性:名词

PHRASE->
focus on = 集中于
focus one's attention on = 集中注意力于
bring something into focus = 使某事成为焦点
out of focus = 失去焦点; 模糊
main focus = 主要焦点


词源: From Latin focus "hearth, fireplace"


注意事项:
*focusses 是 focus 的英式拼写变体，美式拼写为 focuses。两种拼写都被广泛接受。
*focus 作为名词时，复数形式可以是 focuses 或 foci。focusing 是 focus 的动名词形式。
