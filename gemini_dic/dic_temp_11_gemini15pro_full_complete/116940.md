maze:/meɪz/| n.|迷宫，困惑，错综复杂的事物|A complex network of paths or passages, often designed as a puzzle or recreational activity. A confusing or difficult situation or problem.|The old house was a maze of corridors and staircases. 这座老房子走廊和楼梯错综复杂，像个迷宫。|Synonyms: labyrinth, network, tangle, web|Antonyms: order, simplicity|noun

maze:/meɪz/| vt.|使迷惑，使混乱|To bewilder or confuse someone.|The complicated instructions mazed him. 复杂的操作说明把他弄糊涂了。|Synonyms: bewilder, confuse, puzzle, confound|Antonyms: clarify, enlighten|verb
