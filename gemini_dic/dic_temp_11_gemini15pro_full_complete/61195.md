elutriation:/ɪˌluːtriˈeɪʃən/
n.|淘析；洗涤；漂净|The process of separating particles based on their size, shape, and density by washing them with a stream of liquid or gas.|The archaeologist used elutriation to separate the tiny artifacts from the soil. 考古学家使用淘析法从土壤中分离出微小的文物。|separation, purification, decantation|contamination, pollution|noun
