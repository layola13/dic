intactly:/ɪnˈtæktli/| adv.|完整地，未受损地|in a way that is not damaged or impaired in any way.  It emphasizes the completeness and perfect condition of something.|The package arrived intactly, despite the rough handling. 包裹完好无损地到达了，尽管处理得很粗暴。|近义词：completely, entirely, perfectly, wholly|反义词：partially, incompletely, brokenly|词性:副词
词源: From intact + -ly. Intact from Latin intactus "untouched," from in- "not" + tactus "touched," past participle of tangere "to touch."
