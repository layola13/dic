cold-sweats:/ˌkoʊld ˈswɛts/ | n. |冷汗|A state of profuse sweating, typically accompanied by a feeling of coldness, often caused by fear, anxiety, or illness. |  I woke up in cold sweats after having a nightmare. 我做了个噩梦，醒来时出了一身冷汗。|  The thought of public speaking gives me cold sweats.  一想到要公开演讲，我就出了一身冷汗。 | synonyms: clammy skin, night sweats, diaphoresis | antonyms: dry skin, composure | noun
