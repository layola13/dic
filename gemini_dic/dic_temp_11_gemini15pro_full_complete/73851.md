formulaically: /ˌfɔːrmjəˈleɪɪkli/ /ˌfɔːrmjəˈleɪɪkli/ | adv. | 刻板地，公式化地 | In a formulaic way; according to a formula or set procedure.  Characterized by or conforming to a formula or set procedure. | The politician gave a formulaic speech that lacked any real passion.  这位政客发表了一场刻板的演讲，缺乏任何真正的激情。

The student's essay was formulaic and unoriginal.  这个学生的文章刻板且缺乏 originality。 | 近义词: routinely, mechanically, rigidly, conventionally | 反义词: creatively, originally, imaginatively, uniquely | 词性: 副词
