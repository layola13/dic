iconologies:/ˌaɪkənˈɒlədʒiz/
n.
图像学，图像志，象征主义研究
The study of the meaning of images, symbols, and allegorical figures, especially in art.
His latest book delves into the iconologies of Renaissance painting.  他的新书深入探讨了文艺复兴时期绘画的图像学。
Near Synonyms: symbolism, iconography, semiotics
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "literalism")
noun
