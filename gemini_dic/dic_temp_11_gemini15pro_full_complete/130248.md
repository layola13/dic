non-spatially:/nɒnˈspeɪʃəli/ | adv. | 非空间地，不涉及空间地 | Not in a way that relates to space or position. |  The data was analyzed non-spatially, focusing on temporal trends.  数据以非空间方式分析，重点关注时间趋势。 | Example:  The information was organized non-spatially, categorized by topic instead of location. 信息以非空间方式组织，按主题而不是位置分类。| 近义词: nonspatially, aspatially | 反义词: spatially | 词性: 副词
