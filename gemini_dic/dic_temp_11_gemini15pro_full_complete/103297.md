jiber:/ˈdʒaɪbər/ | n. |嘲讽者；嘲弄；奚落|A person who jeers or taunts; a mocking or sarcastic remark.| He's a constant jiber, always making fun of others. 他总是嘲讽别人，是个不停地奚落人的家伙。|Synonyms: mocker, scoffer, taunter |Antonyms: admirer, praiser| noun

jibe:/dʒaɪb/ | vi. vt. |嘲笑；与…一致；相符；(船帆)改变方向| To make a mocking or sarcastic remark; to agree or be consistent; (of a sail) to shift from one side of a vessel to the other. | His story didn't jibe with the facts. 他的故事与事实不符。 The captain ordered the crew to jibe the sails. 船长命令船员调整帆的方向。|Synonyms: mock, taunt, correspond, agree |Antonyms: praise, compliment, disagree| verb
