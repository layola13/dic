inattention's:/ˌɪnəˈtɛnʃənz/
n.|不注意，疏忽|The state of not paying attention to something. The possessive form of "inattention".|His inattention's caused the accident.| 近义词：negligence, carelessness, disregard| 反义词：attention, heedfulness, mindfulness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inattentionem, accusative singular of *inatentio (“inattention”), from in- (“not”) + attentio (“attention”).  Attentio comes from attendere (“to attend”), from ad- (“to”) + tendere (“to stretch”).
