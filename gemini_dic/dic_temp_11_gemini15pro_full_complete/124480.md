mouldboard:/ˈmoʊldbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈməʊldbɔːrd/ (UK)| n. |犁壁; 翻土板|A curved metal plate on a plough that turns over the soil.|The farmer adjusted the mouldboard on his plough. 农民调整了犁上的犁壁。|Synonyms: plowshare, share|Antonyms: N/A|noun

mouldboard:/ˈmoʊldbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈməʊldbɔːrd/ (UK)| adj. |犁壁的; 翻土板的 |Relating to or characteristic of a mouldboard. |  He uses a mouldboard plough on his farm. 他在他的农场使用犁壁犁。|Synonyms: N/A|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *moldbrede*, equivalent to mold (noun) +‎ board.  The "mold" refers to the earth or soil.
