overbearing:/ˌoʊvərˈberɪŋ/ | adj. | 专横的，傲慢的，压倒一切的 |  Domineering, bossy, and arrogant, tending to dictate to others and assert control in a way that makes others feel small or insignificant.  It can also describe something overwhelmingly large or powerful. |  He had an overbearing father who always dictated his career choices. 他有一个专横的父亲，总是支配他的职业选择。

The overbearing heat made it difficult to work outdoors.  酷热难耐，使户外工作变得困难。 |  domineering, autocratic, dictatorial, tyrannical, imperious, high-handed, bossy, arrogant, haughty, presumptuous |  humble, modest, meek, unassuming, submissive, compliant | adjective


overbear:/ˌoʊvərˈber/ | v. | 压倒，制服，镇压 | To overwhelm or overcome by superior force, authority, or influence. | His forceful personality tends to overbear any opposition. 他强势的性格往往会压倒任何反对意见。

The sheer volume of evidence overbore the defense's arguments. 大量的证据压倒了辩方的论点。 | subdue, suppress, overwhelm, overcome, dominate, crush | yield, submit | verb
