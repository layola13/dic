nonaerated:/ˌnɒnˈeəreɪtɪd/
adj.
无气的，不充气的
Not containing air or gas, especially carbon dioxide.
The nonaerated water tasted flat. 这不充气的水尝起来很淡。
近义词:still, flat
反义词:aerated, carbonated, fizzy
词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix): not, without  非，没有
aerated (adjective): containing air or gas, especially carbon dioxide  充气的，含气的
