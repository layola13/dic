aweigh:/əˈweɪ/
adjective
已起锚的，(尤指船锚)刚离开水底的
(of an anchor) just clear of the bottom.
The anchor is aweigh.  锚已起航。
近义词: off the bottom, raised
反义词: at anchor, grounded
词性: adjective

Etymology:  From a- + weigh.  Originally referred to the moment when an anchor was lifted clear of the seabed. The "a-" prefix indicates "on" or "in a particular state."  "Weigh" in this context comes from an older meaning related to lifting or raising.
