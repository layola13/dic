asilt:/əˈsɪlt/| adj.|淤塞的，充满泥沙的|Covered or filled with silt; choked with mud or sediment.|The harbor was asilt, making navigation difficult. 港口淤塞，航行困难。|近义词：silted, muddy, clogged|反义词:clear, unobstructed|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From a- (Old English) meaning "on" + silt (noun).  The word "silt" itself comes from Old Norse *sylt, meaning "mud, mire."
