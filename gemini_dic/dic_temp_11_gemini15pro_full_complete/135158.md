nonoperable:/ˌnɒnˈɒpərəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈɑːpərəbl/
adjective
不能运作的；无法操作的；无法动手术的
Not able to be operated or used; (of a medical condition) not suitable to be treated by surgery.
The equipment is nonoperable due to a power outage.  由于停电，设备无法运行。
The tumor was deemed nonoperable due to its location.  由于肿瘤的位置，它被认为是无法手术的。
近义词: inoperable, unusable, dysfunctional
反义词: operable, functional, working
词性: adjective
