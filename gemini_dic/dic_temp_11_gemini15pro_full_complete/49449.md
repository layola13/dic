delta:/ˈdeltə/ | n. | 三角洲；德尔塔（希腊字母表第四个字母）；变化；差额 | A triangular tract of sediment deposited at the mouth of a river, typically where it diverges into several outlets.  The fourth letter of the Greek alphabet (Δ, δ). A change or difference. | The Nile delta is a fertile agricultural region. 尼罗河三角洲是一片肥沃的农业区。The delta of the stock price was significant. 股票价格的变化很大。 | synonyms: change, difference, variation, shift, fluctuation | antonyms: stability, constancy | noun

delta:/ˈdeltə/ | adj. | 三角洲的；与德尔塔相关的 | Relating to or shaped like a delta. | The delta region is prone to flooding. 三角洲地区容易发生洪水。| synonyms: triangular, alluvial | antonyms: N/A | adjective
