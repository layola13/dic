averroism:/əˈvɛroʊɪzəm/
n.
阿威罗伊主义
The philosophical system of Averroes, characterized by the belief in the unity of the intellect for all humankind and the compatibility of faith and reason.
Averroism influenced many medieval thinkers. 阿威罗伊主义影响了许多中世纪的思想家。
近义词:Averroës's philosophy, rationalism
反义词:mysticism, irrationalism
词性:noun
