nonunited:/ˌnɒnjuːˈnaɪtɪd/
adjective
不团结的，不统一的
not united; not joined together.
The nonunited tribes were easily conquered.  不团结的部落很容易被征服。
The political parties remained nonunited on the key issues. 各政党在关键问题上仍然没有达成一致。
近义词:divided, disunited, separate, fragmented
反义词:united, unified, joint, combined
词性:adjective

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "united" (joined together)
