airtightness:/ˈɛəˌtaɪtnəs/| n.|密封性，气密性|The airtightness of the container is crucial for preserving the food.  这个容器的密封性对于保存食物至关重要。|The new windows improved the airtightness of the house, reducing drafts and energy loss.  新窗户提高了房屋的密封性，减少了穿堂风和能量损失。|近义词：seal, impermeability|反义词:leakiness, porosity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From air + tight + -ness.  Air meaning the gases in the atmosphere. Tight meaning firmly fixed or closed. -ness is a suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality.
