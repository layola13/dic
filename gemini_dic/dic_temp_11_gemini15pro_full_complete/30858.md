chador:/ˈtʃædɔːr/ (US), /ˈtʃɑːdɔːr/ (UK)| n.|罩袍，查朵|A large piece of cloth worn by some Muslim women, especially in Iran, that covers the body from head to feet, leaving only the face exposed.|She wore a black chador that concealed her figure.|Synonyms: veil, hijab, burqa, niqab|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider) Western clothing, revealing clothing|Noun
