half-refined:/ˌhæf rɪˈfaɪnd/
adjective
半精炼的
Partially refined; not fully processed or purified.
The half-refined sugar still contained some impurities. 半精炼的糖仍然含有一些杂质。
The factory produced half-refined oil. 这家工厂生产半精炼油。
nearly refined, partly refined
fully refined, completely refined
