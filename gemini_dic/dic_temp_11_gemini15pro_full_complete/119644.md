middle-tone:/ˈmɪdlˌtoʊn/ (US), /ˈmɪdlˌtəʊn/ (UK)| adj.| 中音的；中等音调的|A middle-tone is a tone that is neither high nor low in pitch.  It refers to a range of frequencies in the middle of the audible spectrum.|The singer's voice had a rich, middle-tone quality.  这位歌手的声音音色丰富，音调适中。|mid-range, medium-pitched|high-pitched, low-pitched|adjective
