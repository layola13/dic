centrifugal: /sɛnˈtrɪfjʊɡəl/ | adj. | 离心的 | Moving or tending to move away from a center. | The centrifugal force pushes the objects outwards when the wheel spins. |  轮子旋转时，离心力将物体向外推。 | 近义词: efferent, outward | 反义词: centripetal, inward | 词性: adjective

centrifugal: /sɛnˈtrɪfjʊɡəl/ | n. | 离心机 | A machine that separates substances by spinning them at high speed. | The lab uses a centrifugal to separate blood components. |  实验室使用离心机分离血液成分。 | 近义词: centrifuge, separator | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *centrum* (center) + *fugere* (to flee).
