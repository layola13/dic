collider:/kəˈlaɪdər/| n.|对撞机；碰撞器|A machine that accelerates particles to high speeds and then causes them to collide.  |例句：The Large Hadron Collider is the world's largest and most powerful particle collider. 大型强子对撞机是世界上最大、功能最强的粒子对撞机。

例句：Scientists use colliders to study the fundamental constituents of matter. 科学家使用对撞机来研究物质的基本组成部分。|近义词：accelerator|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
