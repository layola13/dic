cybersquatter:/ˈsaɪbərˌskwɑːtər/| n. |域名抢注者，网络蟑螂|Someone who registers an Internet domain name (often a well-known company or brand name) in the hope of selling it to its rightful owner at a profit.|The cybersquatter was asking for $10,000 for the domain name. 这个域名抢注者对这个域名索价1万美元。|近义词：domain squatter|反义词: legitimate domain owner| noun
