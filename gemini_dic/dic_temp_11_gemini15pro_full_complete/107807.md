lapstone:/ˈlæpˌstoʊn/| n.|补鞋匠的锤子，敲鞋石|A lapstone is a stone or metal block held in the lap and used as an anvil by shoemakers in hammering and shaping leather.|The cobbler used a lapstone to repair the worn-out sole.  补鞋匠用敲鞋石修补磨损的鞋底。|synonyms: anvil, last|antonyms: None|noun
