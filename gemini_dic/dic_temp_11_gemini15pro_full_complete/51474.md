detox:/ˈdiːtɒks/ (UK) /ˈdiːtɑːks/ (US)| n. v. | 解毒；戒瘾（期）；排毒；净化 | A process or period of time in which one abstains from or rids the body of toxic or unhealthy substances; detoxification. | She went on a detox diet to cleanse her system. 她进行了排毒饮食来清洁身体系统。 |  synonyms: cleanse, purify, purge | antonyms: contaminate, pollute | noun
