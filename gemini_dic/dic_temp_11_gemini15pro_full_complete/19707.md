bilingualism: /baɪˈlɪŋɡwəlɪzəm/ | n. | 双语能力；双语现象 | The ability to use two languages fluently.  The practice of using two languages. |  Many countries promote bilingualism in their education systems. 许多国家在其教育体系中推广双语教育。|  Bilingualism is becoming increasingly important in a globalized world. 在全球化的世界中，双语能力变得越来越重要。 | multilingualism, biculturalism | monolingualism | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + lingual (relating to language) + -ism (forming nouns denoting doctrines, beliefs, practices, or characteristics).  Ultimately from Latin *bi- (two) + lingua (tongue, language).
