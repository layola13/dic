bbses:/ˈbiː biː ɛsɪz/ | n. | 网络论坛 (复数形式) |  Plural form of BBS, which stands for Bulletin Board System.  A BBS is a computer system running software that allows users to connect and log in via a terminal program. Once logged in, they can perform functions such as uploading and downloading software and data, reading news and bulletins, and exchanging messages with other users through email, public message boards, and direct chatting.  While largely supplanted by the World Wide Web, some BBSes still exist. |  He frequented several bbses in the early 90s. 他在90年代初经常光顾几个BBS。|  Forums, message boards, online communities |  (No clear antonym for a plural noun like this) | noun
