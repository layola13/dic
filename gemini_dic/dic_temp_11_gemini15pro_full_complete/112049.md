longbow:/ˈlɒŋboʊ/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋboʊ/ (US)| n. |长弓|A type of tall bow drawn by hand and shooting a long feathered arrow.  A powerful weapon used in medieval warfare, especially by English archers.|The English longbow proved decisive at the Battle of Agincourt.| 近义词:bow,  | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern firearms as a contrasting weapon)|noun
