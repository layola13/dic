combatting:/kəmˈbætɪŋ/
verb
打击，对抗
present participle of combat, meaning to take action to reduce or prevent something bad or undesirable.
The government is combatting inflation. 政府正在对抗通货膨胀。
They are combatting the spread of the disease. 他们正在努力控制疾病的蔓延。
近义词: fighting, opposing, resisting, tackling
反义词: supporting, aiding, promoting, encouraging
词性: verb (present participle)

combat:/ˈkɒmbæt/ (British) /ˈkɑːmbæt/ (American)
noun
战斗，搏斗
a fight or contest between individuals or groups.
The soldiers were engaged in fierce combat. 士兵们进行了激烈的战斗。
Hand-to-hand combat is a dangerous activity. 肉搏战是一项危险的活动。
近义词: fight, battle, struggle, conflict
反义词: peace, truce, harmony, agreement
词性: noun

combat:/ˈkɒmbæt/ (British) /ˈkɑːmbæt/ (American)
verb
与…战斗，打击
to fight or struggle against.
The firefighters worked tirelessly to combat the blaze. 消防队员不知疲倦地与大火搏斗。
We must combat poverty and inequality. 我们必须与贫困和不平等作斗争。
近义词: fight, battle, oppose, resist
反义词: support, aid, promote, encourage
词性: verb

词源: From Old French combatre, from com- "together" + batre "to beat".
