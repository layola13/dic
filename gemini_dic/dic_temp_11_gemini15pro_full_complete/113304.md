luthers:/ˈluːθərz/| n.|路德们；路德派信徒|Followers or adherents of Martin Luther's teachings, especially during the Reformation.  It can also refer to things named after Martin Luther.|例句：The Luthers challenged the authority of the Pope. 路德的追随者们挑战了教皇的权威。

例句：Many Luthers were persecuted for their beliefs. 许多路德派信徒因其信仰而受到迫害。|近义词：Lutherans, Protestants, Reformers|反义词：Catholics|词性:名词


词源:
From the surname of Martin Luther (1483-1546), the German theologian and reformer who initiated the Protestant Reformation.  The suffix "-s" indicates a plural form, denoting a group of people associated with Luther.
