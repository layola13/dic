bulldoze: /ˈbʊldoʊz/ (US) /ˈbʊldəʊz/ (UK) | v. | 推土；摧毁；胁迫 | to clear land or demolish buildings using a bulldozer; to force someone into doing something | The construction crew bulldozed the old building to make way for the new shopping mall. 建筑队用推土机推倒了旧楼，以便为新的购物中心腾出地方。

The government bulldozed the legislation through parliament despite public opposition. 政府不顾公众的反对，强行通过了这项立法。 | 近义词: demolish, flatten, level, coerce, intimidate | 反义词: construct, build, protect | 词性: 动词
