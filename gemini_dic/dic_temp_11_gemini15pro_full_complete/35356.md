co-head:/koʊˈhɛd/ | n. |共同领导，共同负责人|A person who shares the leadership of an organization or a department with one or more other people. |  Example: The company appointed two co-heads to lead the marketing department.  公司任命了两位共同负责人来领导市场部。

Example:  The film had two co-heads of animation, each responsible for different aspects of the project.  这部电影有两个动画共同负责人，各自负责项目的不同方面。| 近义词: co-leader, joint head, co-director | 反义词: subordinate, assistant | 词性: 名词
