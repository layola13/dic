chronotropic:/ˌkrɒnəˈtrɒpɪk/ /ˌkroʊnəˈtroʊpɪk/| adj.|变时性的，变速的|Affecting the rate of time or speed, particularly relating to the heart rate.|Chronotropic drugs can increase or decrease the heart rate. 变时性药物可以增加或减少心率。|Synonyms: chronotrophic|Antonyms: None|adjective

chronotropic:/ˌkrɒnəˈtrɒpɪk/ /ˌkroʊnəˈtroʊpɪk/| n.|变时性药物|A chronotropic agent, especially a drug that affects the heart rate.|The doctor prescribed a negative chronotropic drug to slow the patient's rapid heart rate. 医生开了负性变时药物来减缓病人的快速心率。|Synonyms: chronotrophic agent|Antonyms: None|noun
