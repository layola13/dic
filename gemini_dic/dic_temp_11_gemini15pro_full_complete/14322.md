automatism:/ɔːˈtɒmətɪzəm/ (US) /ɔːˈtɑːmətɪzəm/| n.|自动症；无意识行为|The act of doing something without being aware of it, often because of a medical condition.|He claimed his actions were due to automatism brought on by sleepwalking. 他声称他的行为是由于梦游引起的自动症。|Synonyms: unconsciousness, involuntary action, reflex|Antonyms: consciousness, volition, deliberate action|noun

automatism:/ɔːˈtɒmətɪzəm/ (US) /ɔːˈtɑːmətɪzəm/| n.|（艺术）自动主义|A technique in art and writing that emphasizes spontaneity and the subconscious.|The artist employed automatism to create unpredictable and surprising works.  这位艺术家运用自动主义来创作不可预测和令人惊讶的作品。|Synonyms: spontaneity, improvisation, free association|Antonyms: deliberation, planning, control|noun
