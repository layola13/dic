ameliorated: /əˈmiːliəreɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)
改善的，改进的
made something better; improved
The new policy ameliorated the working conditions in the factory. 新政策改善了工厂的工作条件。
Her pain was ameliorated by the medication.  药物减轻了她的疼痛。
Synonyms: improved, enhanced, bettered, upgraded, refined
Antonyms: worsened, aggravated, deteriorated, exacerbated
verb
