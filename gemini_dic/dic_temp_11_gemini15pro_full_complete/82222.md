grottoes:/ˈɡrɑːtoʊz/ /ˈɡrɒtəʊz/
noun
洞穴，石窟
A grotto is a small cave, especially an artificial one in a park or garden.
We visited the beautiful grottoes at the edge of the lake. 我们参观了湖边的美丽洞穴。
caves, caverns
