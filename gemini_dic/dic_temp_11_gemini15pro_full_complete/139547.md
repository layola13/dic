obtrusive:/əbˈtruːsɪv/| adj.|突兀的；冒昧的；强加于人的|Noticeably unwelcome or intrusive.  Pushing oneself or one's ideas forward or noticeable in a way that is unwelcome.|The advertisements were very obtrusive and spoiled the look of the website. 这些广告非常突兀，破坏了网站的美观。| synonyms: intrusive, conspicuous, noticeable, prominent, blatant | antonyms: inconspicuous, unobtrusive, subtle, discreet | adjective

obtrude:/əbˈtruːd/| v.|打扰；闯入；突出；强加|Become noticeable in an unwelcome or intrusive way; impose or force (something) on someone in an unwelcome way.|I don't want to obtrude upon her privacy. 我不想打扰她的隐私。| synonyms: impose, intrude, interfere, meddle | antonyms: withdraw, recede, retract | verb
