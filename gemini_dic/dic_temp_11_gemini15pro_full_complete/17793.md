bedraggled:/bɪˈdræɡəld/
adjective
邋遢的，凌乱的
Limp and soiled, as with rain or mud.
The bedraggled hikers finally reached the cabin.  筋疲力尽、浑身泥泞的徒步旅行者终于到达了小屋。
The bedraggled kitten looked pathetic in the pouring rain.  在倾盆大雨中，那只湿漉漉的小猫看起来很可怜。
近义词:disheveled, unkempt, soaked, drenched
反义词:tidy, neat, groomed, pristine
词性:adjective
