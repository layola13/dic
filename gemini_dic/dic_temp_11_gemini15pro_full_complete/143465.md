outgabbled:/ˌaʊtˈɡæbəld/
verb
胜过(某人)的饶舌；比(某人)更健谈
To talk more loquaciously or rapidly than; talk someone down.
He outgabbled his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中说得比对手快，胜过了对手。
近义词: outtalk, outspeak, outrhetoric
反义词: be quiet, listen
