butterbur:/ˈbʌtərbɜːr/ (US), /ˈbʌtəbɜː(r)/ (UK)| n. |蜂斗菜；款冬|A perennial plant (Petasites hybridus) of the daisy family, native to Europe and Asia, having large rhubarb-like leaves and pinkish-purple flowers. It is used in herbal medicine. |Butterbur is traditionally used to treat migraines.  蜂斗菜传统上用于治疗偏头痛。|Synonyms: coltsfoot, sweet coltsfoot|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
