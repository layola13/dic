ill-fitting:/ˌɪlˈfɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不合身的；不合适的
not fitting well; too loose or too tight
The suit was ill-fitting and made him look awkward.  这套西装不合身，让他看起来很笨拙。
She felt uncomfortable in the ill-fitting dress.  她穿着不合身的连衣裙感到很不舒服。
近义词: tight, loose, unsuitable, inappropriate
反义词: well-fitting, suitable, appropriate, fitting
词性: adjective
