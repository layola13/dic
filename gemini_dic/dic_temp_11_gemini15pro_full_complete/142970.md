outbabbling:/ˌaʊtˈbæblɪŋ/
| v. | 比...说得更多，说得更流畅; 压倒(某人的声音);  |  Outbabbling his opponent in the debate, the politician won the crowd's favor.  这位政治家在辩论中说得比对手更多，赢得了观众的支持。 | 近义词: outtalking, outspeaking | 反义词: silenced, hushed | verb


outbabbling:/ˌaʊtˈbæblɪŋ/
| adj. |  滔滔不绝的; 口若悬河的; | The outbabbling brook created a soothing melody. 滔滔不绝的小溪创造出舒缓的旋律。 | 近义词: garrulous, loquacious | 反义词: quiet, silent | adjective
