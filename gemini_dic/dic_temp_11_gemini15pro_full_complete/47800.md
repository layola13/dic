decapodous: /dɪˈkæpədəs/ | adj. | 十足的，有十条腿的 | Having ten legs or feet, characteristic of decapod crustaceans. |  Many commercially important crustaceans are decapodous, such as crabs and lobsters. 许多重要的商业甲壳类动物是十足目的，例如螃蟹和龙虾。|  Decapodous crustaceans are a vital part of the marine ecosystem. 十足目甲壳类动物是海洋生态系统的重要组成部分。 | synonymous: ten-footed, ten-legged | antonymous: (No direct antonym, could use descriptions like "few-legged" or names of specific groups with different numbers of legs, such as "hexapod") | adjective
