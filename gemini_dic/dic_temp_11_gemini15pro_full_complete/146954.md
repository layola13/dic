overinvests: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛsts/ | v. | 过度投资 | Invests too much (time, money, energy, etc.) in something. | The company overinvested in new technology and neglected its core business.  这家公司在新技术上过度投资，而忽略了其核心业务。| The government overinvested in infrastructure projects. 政府在基础设施项目上过度投资了。 | synonyms: overspends, overcommits | antonyms: underspends, underinvests | verb
