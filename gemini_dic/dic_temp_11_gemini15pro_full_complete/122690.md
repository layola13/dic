moesogothic: /ˌmoʊsoʊˈɡɒθɪk/ | adj. | 萌系哥特 | A subgenre of gothic fashion and aesthetic characterized by a combination of gothic elements with cute and childlike elements, often featuring pastel colors, stuffed animals, and anime-inspired imagery. |  She wore a moesogothic outfit, complete with a black lace dress, pink hair bows, and a Hello Kitty backpack. 她穿着一套萌系哥特服装，包括一件黑色蕾丝连衣裙、粉红色发夹和一个Hello Kitty背包。 |  Synonyms: pastel goth, kawaii goth | Antonyms: traditional goth, romantic goth | Adjective
