consecutive:/kənˈsekjətɪv/| adj.|连续的，连贯的|following one after another in a continuous series or order; successive|He won three consecutive championships. 他赢得了三连冠。|She was absent for five consecutive days. 她连续五天缺席。|近义词：successive, sequential, uninterrupted|反义词：nonconsecutive, intermittent, sporadic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *consecutivus, from Latin consecutus, past participle of consequi (“to follow closely”), from com- (“together”) + sequi (“to follow”).
