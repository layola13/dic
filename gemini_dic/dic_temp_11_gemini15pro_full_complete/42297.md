counter-ion:/ˈkaʊntər ˈaɪən/ /ˈkaʊntər aɪˌɑːn/ | n. |反离子，抗衡离子| An ion with a charge opposite to that of another ion.  It maintains electrical neutrality in a solution or compound.  |

例句：
The sodium ion is the counter-ion to the chloride ion in sodium chloride.
氯化钠中，钠离子是氯离子的反离子。

In an ionic polymer, the counter-ions balance the charge of the polymer backbone.
在离子聚合物中，反离子平衡聚合物骨架的电荷。

近义词：gegenion |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
