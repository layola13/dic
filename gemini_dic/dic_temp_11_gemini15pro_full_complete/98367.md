insistency:/ɪnˈsɪstənsi/| n.|坚持，坚决，持续|The lawyer's insistency finally persuaded the judge to reconsider the case. 律师的坚持最终说服了法官重新考虑这个案子。|例句：His insistency on the point irritated her. 他对这一点的坚持让她很恼火。

例句：The insistency of the salesman finally wore her down.  销售员的坚持不懈最终让她屈服了。|近义词：persistence, perseverance, tenacity|反义词:hesitation, vacillation|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin insistentia, from insistere ‘persist’ (see insist).
