allowances:/əˈlaʊənsɪz/
| n. |津贴，补贴；限额；折扣；允许；体谅|A sum of money paid regularly to a person, often to cover particular expenses; the act of allowing something; consideration for extenuating circumstances. |The company offers generous travel allowances to its employees. 公司为员工提供丰厚的差旅津贴。|近义词：stipend, payment, grant, concession, permission|反义词：disallowance, prohibition, restriction|词性:名词(plural)

allowances:/əˈlaʊənsɪz/
| n. |零花钱|Money given regularly, typically to a child.|My parents give me a weekly allowance for chores. 我父母每周给我零花钱做家务。|近义词：pocket money, spending money|反义词：None|词性:名词(plural)
