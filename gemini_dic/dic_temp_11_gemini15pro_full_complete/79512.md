globiferous:/ˌɡloʊˈbɪfərəs/
adjective
球状的；多球的；产球的
Bearing globes or globules.  Resembling a globe or globule. Producing globes or globules.
The globiferous seaweed bobbed gently in the tide.  这种球状的海藻在潮水中轻轻摇摆。
globular, spherical, round, orbicular
flat, planar
