fibrousnesses:/ˈfaɪbrəsnəsɪz/
n.
纤维性；纤维状
The state or quality of being fibrous.
The fibrousnesses of the plant's roots allowed it to cling to the rocky terrain.  这种植物根部的纤维性使其能够 clinging to the rocky terrain.
近义词:  fibrosity
反义词: smoothness, softness
