overnumerously:/ˌoʊvərˈnuːmərəsli/
|adv.|过多地，人数过多地|being more numerous than is required or desired; excessively.|The lifeboats were overnumerously filled with panicked passengers.|近义词: excessively, redundantly, superfluously|反义词: insufficiently, sparsely, meagerly|词性: 副词
词源:  From "over" (meaning "excessive") + "numerous" (meaning "great in number") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
