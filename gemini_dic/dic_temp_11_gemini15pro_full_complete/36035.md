codiscoverers:/ˌkoʊdɪˈskʌvərərz/| n. |共同发现者|People who discover something together.  Those who share the credit for a discovery.| Marie Curie and her husband, Pierre, were codiscoverers of radium and polonium.|synonyms: co-inventors, collaborators, joint discoverers |antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "rival researchers," "independent discoverers")|noun
