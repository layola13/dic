deformer:/dɪˈfɔːrmər/| n.|变形器，变形者|A thing or agent that causes deformation. It can be a physical force, a tool, software, or even an abstract concept.|The blacksmith used a hammer as a deformer to shape the metal.  铁匠用锤子作为变形器来塑造金属。|Synonyms: distorter, warper|Antonyms: preserver, maintainer|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "deform" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Deform" comes from the Old French "deformer,"  and ultimately from the Latin "deformare,"  meaning "to disfigure, distort."
