godhead:/ˈɡɒdhed/ /ˈɡɑːdhed/| n. |神性，神格，神位|The state of being God; divine nature or essence.  The Godhead is often used in theological discussions about the nature of God, especially in Christianity and other monotheistic religions.|例句：They believed in the godhead of their king. 他们相信他们国王的神性。

例句：The concept of the Trinity explores the godhead in Christianity.  三位一体的概念探讨了基督教中的神性。|近义词：divinity, deity, godliness|反义词:mortality, humanity|词性:noun
