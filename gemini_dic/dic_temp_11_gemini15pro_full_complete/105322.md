kiboshing:/ˈkaɪbɒʃɪŋ/
| n. |阻止，禁止，中断|The act of putting a stop to something; suppressing or preventing something from happening.|They put the kibosh on our plans for a party. 他们阻止了我们的派对计划。|Synonyms: halting, stopping, suppressing, preventing|Antonyms: starting, initiating, encouraging, promoting|gerund/present participle

kibosh:/ˈkaɪbɒʃ/
| n. |阻止，禁止，中断|An abrupt end; something that serves to suppress or check something.|The rain put the kibosh on our picnic.  雨水阻止了我们的野餐。|Synonyms: veto, stop, check, suppression|Antonyms: start, initiation, encouragement, promotion|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
The origin of "kibosh" is uncertain, but it's thought to be possibly from Irish *cie bais* 'cap of death' or from Shelta or Yiddish.
