antireflectively:/ˌæntiɹɪˈflɛktɪvli/
|adv.|抗反射地|In a manner that reduces or prevents reflection of light or other radiation.|The lens was coated antireflectively to minimize glare.  镜片经过抗反射涂层处理，以最大程度地减少眩光。| 近义词: nonreflectively | 反义词: reflectively | 词性: 副词


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): from Greek *anti, meaning "against," "opposite of"
reflect (verb): from Latin reflectere, meaning "to bend back," "to turn back"
-ive (suffix): forms adjectives
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
