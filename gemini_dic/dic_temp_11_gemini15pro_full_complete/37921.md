completable:/kəmˈpliːtəbl/ | adj. | 可完成的，可补充的 | Capable of being completed or finished. | The project is complex but completable within the given timeframe.  这个项目很复杂，但在规定的时间内可以完成。| The puzzle is completable in under an hour. 这个拼图可以在一小时内完成。 |  近义词: achievable, doable, feasible, finishable | 反义词: impossible, unachievable, unfinished | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "complete" + "-able".  "Complete" comes from the Latin "complētus," past participle of "complēre" (to fill up, complete), from "com-" (together) + "plēre" (to fill). "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin, meaning "capable of being."
