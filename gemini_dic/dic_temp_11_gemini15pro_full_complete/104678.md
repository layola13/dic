kart:/kɑːrt/ | n. | 卡丁车；小型赛车 | A small, low-slung motor vehicle, typically used for racing on indoor or outdoor tracks. |  He won the kart race. 他赢得了卡丁车比赛。

kart:/kɑːrt/ | n. | 手推车；购物推车 | A small vehicle with two or four wheels, used for carrying loads. |  I put my groceries in the shopping kart. 我把我的杂货放进了购物车里。


Etymology: Shortened from "go-kart," likely influenced by the word "cart."

Synonyms: go-cart, buggy, trolley (for shopping kart)

Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a physical object like a kart. Perhaps "large vehicle" could be considered a conceptual antonym.)
