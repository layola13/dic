noncrimes:/ˈnɒnkraɪmz/ (US) /ˈnɒnkraɪmz/ (UK)| n. |非罪行，非犯罪行为|Actions or behaviors that are not considered criminal offenses by law.|Many behaviors once considered deviant are now seen as noncrimes. 许多曾经被认为是 deviant 的行为现在被视为非罪行。|Synonyms: non-offenses, lawful acts|Antonyms: crimes, offenses|noun
