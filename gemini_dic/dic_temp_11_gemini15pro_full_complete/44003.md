criminalizes:/ˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使…成为犯罪；宣布…为非法
makes something illegal; declares something a crime
The law criminalizes certain types of speech. 这项法律将某些类型的言论定为犯罪。
synonyms: outlaws, prohibits, bans
antonyms: legalizes, permits, allows
词性: 动词
