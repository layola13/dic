certificatory:/ˌsɜːrtɪˈfɪkətɔːri/ | adj. | 证明的；授予证书的 | Relating to or serving as a certificate; certifying. |  This document is certificatory of your completion of the course.  这份文件证明你完成了课程。| 近义词: certifying, validating, attesting | 反义词: invalidating, nullifying | 词性: 形容词

certificatory:/ˌsɜːrtɪˈfɪkətɔːri/ | n. | 证明文件；证书 | A document that serves as a certificate. | The certificatory was proof of his qualifications. 这份证明文件是他资格的证明。 | 近义词: certificate, document, certification | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "rejection" or "denial" in context) | 词性: 名词
