intercorrelating:/ˌɪntərkɔːrəˈleɪtɪŋ/
verb
相互关联
Showing a mutual relationship or connection.  The process or state of being correlated with each other.
The study found that several factors are intercorrelating, influencing overall health outcomes. 这项研究发现，有几个因素相互关联，影响着整体健康结果。
Intercorrelating variables can make it difficult to isolate the cause of a phenomenon.  相互关联的变量会使我们难以分离出某种现象的成因。
Synonyms:  interrelated, interconnected, interacting, interdependent
Antonyms: independent, unrelated, disconnected
