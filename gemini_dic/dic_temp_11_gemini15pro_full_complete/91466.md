hylobate:/ˈhaɪləˌbeɪt/ | n. |  长臂猿 | An ape of the family Hylobatide, characterized by long arms and no tail, found in Southeast Asia. |  The hylobate swung effortlessly through the trees.  长臂猿在树林间轻松地荡来荡去。 | 近义词：gibbon | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *hýlobates*, "tree-walker," from *hýlē* "wood, forest" + *batēs* "one that walks."
