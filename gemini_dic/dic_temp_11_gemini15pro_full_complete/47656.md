debosh:/dɪˈbɔːʃ/ | v. | 放荡，寻欢作乐，纵情酒色 | To indulge in excessive pleasure, especially of a sensual kind; to corrupt or seduce from virtue. |  He deboshed himself with wine and women. 他沉溺于酒色。| 近义词: carouse, revel, indulge, dissipate | 反义词: abstain, moderate, behave | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Middle French *desbaucher*, from des- "away, from" + bauche "workshop, beam, joist". Originally meant "to lead astray from work".
