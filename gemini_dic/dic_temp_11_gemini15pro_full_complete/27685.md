candlemas: /ˈkændlməs/ | n. | 圣烛节 | A Christian holy day celebrated on February 2nd, commemorating the presentation of Jesus in the temple.  It marks the end of the Christmas-Epiphany season in some Western liturgical churches. | Example 1: Candlemas is a traditional time for making pancakes. 圣烛节是传统的制作煎饼的时间。

Example 2: Many churches hold special services on Candlemas. 许多教堂在圣烛节举行特别仪式。 | 近义词: Feast of the Presentation of the Lord, Purification of the Virgin Mary | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *Candelmæsse, from candel "candle" + mæsse "mass".  The name refers to the blessing of candles on this day, symbolizing Christ as the light of the world.
