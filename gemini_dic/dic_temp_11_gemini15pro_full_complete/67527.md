extraditable:/ɛkˈstrædɪtəbl/
adjective
可引渡的
Subject to extradition; liable to be surrendered to another jurisdiction, especially under the terms of a treaty.
The suspect is extraditable to the United States.  这个嫌疑犯可以引渡到美国。
He is not extraditable under the current treaty.  根据现行条约，他不能被引渡。
synonyms: returnable, deportable
antonyms: non-extraditable

词性: adjective
