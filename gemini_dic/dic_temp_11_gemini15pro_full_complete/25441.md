bullan:/ˈbʊlən/ | n. | 公牛; (尤指未阉割的) | A bullan is a young bull, especially one that has not been castrated. |  The farmer brought his prize bullan to the market. 农民把他最好的小公牛带到市场。| synonyms: bullock, steer | antonyms: cow, heifer | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bullon*, diminutive of *bulle* (“bull”), from Old English *bula* (“bull”), from Proto-Germanic *bulô* (“bull”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰew- (“to roar, bellow”). Cognate with Scots *bull*, West Frisian *bolle*, Dutch *bul*, German *Bulle*, Danish *bul*, Swedish *bulle*, Icelandic *boli*.
