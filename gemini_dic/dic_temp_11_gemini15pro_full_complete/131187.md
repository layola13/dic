nonbuddings:/ˌnɒnˈbʌdɪŋz/
n.
非萌芽，未萌发; 无性繁殖的生物体（通常指不通过出芽方式繁殖）
Organisms or parts of organisms that are not budding, or reproducing asexually by budding.  This term often refers to organisms that reproduce through other means like binary fission or spores.
Nonbudding hydra were observed in the experiment.  实验中观察到未萌发的螅。
Near synonyms: non-germinating, non-sprouting
Antonyms: budding, germinating, sprouting
noun
