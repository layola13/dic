overgreedy: /ˌoʊvərˈɡriːdi/ | adj. | 过于贪婪的，贪得无厌的 | Excessively greedy; wanting more than is reasonable or necessary.  Formed by the prefix "over-" (meaning excessively) and the adjective "greedy". | He became overgreedy and lost everything he had worked for. 他变得贪得无厌，失去了他努力奋斗的一切。|  The overgreedy corporation exploited its workers. 这家贪婪的公司剥削了它的工人。 | avaricious, rapacious, covetous | generous, selfless | adjective
