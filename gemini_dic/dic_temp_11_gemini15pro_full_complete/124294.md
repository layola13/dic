词源：
"Mother-of-pearl" comes from the fact that the material is produced by mollusks, specifically oysters and other shellfish, which are considered the "mothers" of the pearls they sometimes also create.  “珍珠母”这个词来源于这种材料是由软体动物，特别是牡蛎和其他贝类产生的，它们被认为是它们有时也会创造的珍珠的“母亲”。
