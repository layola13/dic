inexhalable:/ˌɪnɛksˈheɪləbəl/
adj.
不能呼出的；不能吐露的
Unable to be exhaled or breathed out; (figuratively) unable to be uttered or expressed.
The scientist studied the inexhalable gases trapped in the ancient ice core.  科学家研究了困在古代冰芯中无法呼出的气体。
His grief was so profound, it felt like an inexhalable weight on his chest.  他的悲伤如此深沉，感觉就像胸口压着一块无法吐露的重物。
近义词:incommunicable, unspeakable, unutterable
反义词:exhalable, expressible, utterable
词性:adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + exhalable (capable of being exhaled), from exhale (from Latin exhalare "to breathe out").
