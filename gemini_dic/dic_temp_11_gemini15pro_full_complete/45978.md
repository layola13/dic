cyanite:/ˈsaɪənaɪt/ | n. | 蓝晶石 | A triclinic mineral, aluminum silicate (Al2SiO5), commonly blue, also gray, green, black, or colorless, used as a refractory. |  Cyanite is a polymorph of andalusite and sillimanite. 蓝晶石是红柱石和矽线石的多晶型物。

Cyanite is often found in metamorphic rocks. 蓝晶石常发现于变质岩中。| 近义词: disthene | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) | 词性: noun
