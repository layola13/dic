outrhyme:/ˌaʊtˈraɪm/ | v. n. | 超越韵律; 外韵; 外韵诗 | An instance of rhyming on a stressed syllable beyond the last stressed syllable of a line.  A word at the end of one line that rhymes with a word within the following line. | "The *outrhyme* gave the poem a unique musicality." 外韵赋予了这首诗独特的音乐性。 | synonyms: extended rhyme | antonyms: internal rhyme, perfect rhyme | noun
