calf-like:/ˈkɑːfˌlaɪk/
adjective
小牛般的；像小牛的
Resembling a calf, especially in being young, awkward, or clumsy.  Having qualities (like innocence, awkward grace, etc.) that are associated with a young calf.
The newborn foal took calf-like steps on its wobbly legs.  这匹新生的马驹用它摇晃的腿迈着小牛般的步伐。
The young dancer, though talented, had a calf-like awkwardness on stage.  这位年轻的舞者虽然很有天赋，但在舞台上却有着小牛般的笨拙。
近义词:awkward, clumsy, gangly, ungainly
反义词:graceful, elegant, coordinated
词性:adjective
