leeway:/ˈliːweɪ/| n.| 余地；自由活动的空间；允许的偏差；（船）偏航|The design allows very little leeway for change. 这个设计几乎没有修改的余地。|近义词：latitude, margin, flexibility, space, room|反义词：restriction, limitation|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle Dutch *līwai, from lī "leeward" + wai "way".  The original nautical meaning referred to the sideways drift of a ship to leeward.  This sense broadened metaphorically to the current meaning.
