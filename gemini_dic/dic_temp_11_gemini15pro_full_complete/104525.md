kalki: /ˈkʌlki/ | n. | 毁灭者，第十化身 | The tenth and final avatar of the Hindu god Vishnu, who is prophesied to appear at the end of the Kali Yuga, the current age of decline, to restore order and righteousness. | Example:  Many Hindus believe that Kalki will arrive riding a white horse and wielding a flaming sword. 许多印度教徒相信卡尔基会骑着白马，挥舞着燃烧的剑到来。

Example: The prophecy of Kalki offers hope for a future free from suffering. 卡尔基的预言为摆脱苦难的未来提供了希望。| 近义词: avatar, incarnation | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Sanskrit कल्कि (kalki), meaning "white horse," "destruction," or "time."  It's derived from the root *kal, meaning "to calculate, count."
