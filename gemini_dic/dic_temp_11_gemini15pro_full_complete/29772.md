cattily:/ˈkætɪli/| adv.|刻薄地，恶意地|In a spiteful or mean-spirited way, often with the intention to hurt or offend.|She cattily remarked on his weight gain. 她刻薄地评论了他的体重增加。|例句：He cattily dismissed her suggestions as impractical. 他恶意地驳回了她的建议，认为不切实际。|近义词：spitefully, maliciously, nastily, bitingly, meanly|反义词：kindly, generously, charitably|词性：副词
