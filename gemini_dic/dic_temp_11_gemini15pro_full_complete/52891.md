diminish:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃ/| v. |减少，减小，降低|To make or become less; reduce in size, extent, or range.|The supply of food diminished as the winter progressed.  随着冬季的推进，食物供应减少了。|synonyms: decrease, reduce, lessen, dwindle, shrink|antonyms: increase, enlarge, expand, augment, amplify|verb
diminished:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃt/| adj. |减少的，减小的|Made smaller or less; reduced.|He felt diminished in her presence. 在她面前，他感到自己矮了一截。|synonyms: reduced, lessened, decreased, smaller, weaker|antonyms: increased, enlarged, expanded, greater, stronger|adjective
