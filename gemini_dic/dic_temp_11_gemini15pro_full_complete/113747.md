macromeric:/ˌmæk.rəˈmɛr.ɪk/ | adj. | 大分子的，巨分子的 | Relating to or consisting of macromeres.  A macromere is a larger blastomere resulting from the unequal cleavage of a fertilized ovum. |  The macromeric cells are located at the vegetal pole of the embryo. 巨分子的细胞位于胚胎的植物极。 |  Macromeric segmentation is characteristic of certain mollusks.  大分子的分割是某些软体动物的特征。 | synonyms: large, big (when referring to cell size), megaloblastic | antonyms: micromeric | adjective
