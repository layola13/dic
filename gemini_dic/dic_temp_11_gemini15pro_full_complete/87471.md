hest:/hest/ | n. | 命令，指示，嘱咐 |  An archaic or dialectal word meaning command or behest. |  "He gave the hest to his men." 他给他的手下下达了命令。 | 近义词: command, order, instruction, behest | 反义词: request, plea | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old English *hæs, from Proto-Germanic *haisiz. Cognate with Old Norse heit (promise, vow), Gothic haidus (manner, fashion).
