exurgent:/ɪkˈsɜːrdʒənt/| adj.|上升的，涌现的，勃发的|Rising or bursting forth with force; surging.  Characterized by a sudden increase or rapid growth.|The exurgent flames leaped from the burning building.  熊熊烈焰从燃烧的建筑中喷涌而出。|近义词：emerging, burgeoning, surging, erupting, ascendant|反义词：declining, waning, diminishing, subsiding|词性:形容词
词源: Latin exsurgēns, exsurgent-, present participle of exsurgere to rise up, from ex- + surgere to rise
