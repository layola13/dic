beguiling:/bɪˈɡaɪlɪŋ/
adjective
迷人的，诱人的
Charming or enchanting, often in a deceptive way.
Her beguiling smile made him forget all his worries. 她迷人的微笑让他忘记了所有的烦恼。
synonyms: enchanting, captivating, alluring, charming, tempting, seductive
antonyms: repellent, repulsive, unappealing, off-putting
词性:形容词

beguiling:/bɪˈɡaɪlɪŋ/
adjective
具有欺骗性的
Having the power to deceive or mislead.
The salesman had a beguiling manner that made people trust him, even though he was lying.  这个销售员有一种欺骗性的态度，让人们相信他，即使他在撒谎。
synonyms: deceptive, misleading, deceitful, treacherous
antonyms: honest, truthful, straightforward
词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *begylen*, from Old English *begalian* ("to bewitch, fascinate"), from *be-* (intensifier) + *galian* ("to enchant, conjure"). Related to Old Norse *galdr* ("magic, incantation").
