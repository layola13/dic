lymphated:/lɪmˈfætɪd/
adjective
淋巴的；淋巴肿大的
Affected by lymphedema; swollen with lymph.
The lymphated arm was twice the size of the other.  淋巴肿胀的手臂是另一只手臂的两倍大。
The surgeon carefully examined the lymphated tissue.  外科医生仔细检查了淋巴肿大的组织。
近义词: swollen, edematous
反义词: normal, healthy
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From lymph + -ated.  Lymph comes from Latin *lympha, meaning "water."  The suffix "-ated" means "affected by or provided with."
