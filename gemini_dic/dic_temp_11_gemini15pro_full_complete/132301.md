nondefiantness:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪəntnəs/ | n. | 不反抗; 温顺; 顺从 | The state or quality of being nondefiant; lack of defiance.  It implies a passive acceptance of authority or a situation, often without protest or resistance. |  His nondefiantness made him an easy target for bullies.  他毫不反抗的态度使他很容易成为恶霸的目标。|  The dog's nondefiantness was evident in its lowered tail and ears.  这只狗的顺从表现在它低垂的尾巴和耳朵上。 |  obedience, submissiveness, compliance, acquiescence | defiance, resistance, rebelliousness | noun
