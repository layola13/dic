disyoke:/dɪsˈjoʊk/ | v. | 解除束缚，卸轭，分离 | to free from a yoke; to separate or detach |  The farmer disyoked the oxen at the end of the day.  农民在一天结束时给牛解了轭。| synonyms: unyoke, detach, separate, disconnect | antonyms: yoke, connect, join, unite | verb

disyoke:/dɪsˈjoʊk/ | v. |  脱离，摆脱（束缚、控制等） | to free from something that restricts or burdens |  He disyoked himself from the responsibilities of the family business. 他摆脱了家族企业的责任。| synonyms: liberate, free, release, extricate | antonyms:  burden, restrain, confine, restrict | verb
