absurdity:/əbˈsɜːrdəti/ /æbˈsɜːrdəti/| n. |荒谬；荒诞；荒谬的事情|The absurdity of the situation was apparent to everyone.  这种情况的荒谬性显而易见。|近义词：nonsense, foolishness, illogicality, irrationality|反义词:sense, logic, rationality|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *absurditas, from Latin absurdus (“out of tune, discordant, irrational, silly”), from ab- (“from, away from”) + surdus (“deaf, dull, mute”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *swer- (“to sound, speak”).
