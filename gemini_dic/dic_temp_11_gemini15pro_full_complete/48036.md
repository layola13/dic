decivilizing: /diːˈsɪvɪˌlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使丧失文明的，使野蛮化的
making someone or something less civilized
例句：The decivilizing effects of war are devastating. 战争使人丧失文明的影响是毁灭性的。

例句：Exposure to extreme violence can have a decivilizing effect on individuals. 接触极端暴力会对个人产生使之野蛮化的影响。

近义词：barbarizing, brutalizing, degrading, corrupting
反义词：civilizing, refining, cultivating, enlightening
词性：adjective
