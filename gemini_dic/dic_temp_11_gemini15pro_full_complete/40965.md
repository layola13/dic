cooperators:/koʊˈɑːpəreɪtərz/
n.
合作者，合作人
People who work together towards a common goal or purpose.
The cooperators in the project shared the workload and the credit.  这个项目的合作者们分担了工作量和荣誉。
collaborators, partners, associates
competitors, rivals, opponents
noun
