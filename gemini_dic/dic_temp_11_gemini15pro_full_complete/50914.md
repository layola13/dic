deserving:/dɪˈzɜːrvɪŋ/ | adj. | 值得的，应得的 | Worthy of reward or praise; having good qualities that merit something. |  She is a deserving recipient of the award. 她是一位 deserving 的获奖者。| 近义词: worthy, meritorious, entitled | 反义词: undeserving, unworthy | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Old French *deservir, present participle of deservir (“to deserve”), from Latin dēservīre (“to serve diligently, deserve”), from dē (“fully, intently”) + servīre (“to serve”).
