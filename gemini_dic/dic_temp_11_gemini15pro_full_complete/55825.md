divided:/dɪˈvaɪdɪd/
adjective
分裂的，分开的
separated into parts or pieces.
The country was deeply divided on the issue of gun control.  这个国家在枪支管制问题上存在严重分歧。
The cake was divided into eight equal slices. 蛋糕被分成了八等份。
近义词: separated, split, fractured
反义词: united, joined, whole
词性: adjective


divided:/dɪˈvaɪdɪd/
verb
分开，划分
separate or be separated into parts or groups.
He divided the money equally among his children. 他把钱平分给他的孩子们。
The river divides the city into two distinct areas. 这条河将城市分成两个截然不同的区域。
近义词: separate, split, distribute
反义词: unite, join, combine
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *divīdere*, present active infinitive of *dīvidō* (“I divide, separate, distribute”), from *dis-* + *vidō* (“I separate”).
