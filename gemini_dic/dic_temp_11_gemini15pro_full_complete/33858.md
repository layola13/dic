circumscissile:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈsɪsɪl/ | adj. | 横裂的 | Splitting or opening along a transverse circular line, like a lid. |  The fruit of the plant is circumscissile, opening like a little pot with a lid. 这种植物的果实是横裂的，像一个小罐子带着盖子一样打开。| Example 2:  The circumscissile capsule released its seeds when fully ripe.  成熟的横裂蒴果释放出种子。| synonym: dehiscent | antonym: indehiscent | adjective
