catstitch:/ˈkætˌstɪtʃ/| n.| 猫步缝纫，锁边缝纫，十字形缝纫|A type of stitch resembling the tracks of a cat, used for lightly tacking together fabrics that are to be permanently joined later or for closing a wound temporarily.|She used a catstitch to temporarily hold the two pieces of fabric together. 她用猫步缝纫法将两块布料临时缝合在一起。|Synonyms: tacking stitch, basting stitch|Antonyms: permanent stitch|noun
