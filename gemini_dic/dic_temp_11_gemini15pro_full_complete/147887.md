overready:/ˌoʊvərˈrɛdi/| adj.|过分准备的；过早的；性急的|Too prepared or eager; premature; hasty.|He was overready to criticize others, which made him unpopular. 他总是过早地批评别人，这让他很不受欢迎。|The troops were overready for the battle and launched a premature attack. 部队对战斗准备过度，发动了过早的攻击。|近义词：premature, eager, hasty, impetuous|反义词：unprepared, hesitant, cautious|词性:形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "ready" (adjective).  From Old English "ræde" meaning "prepared."
