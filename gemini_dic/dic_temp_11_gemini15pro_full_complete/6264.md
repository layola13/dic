amomum:/ˈæməməm/ | n. |豆蔻|A genus of plants in the ginger family, Zingiberaceae, native to Asia, Africa, and Australia.  The fruits of some species are used as spices, such as cardamom.|

例句：
Amomum is a valuable spice in many Asian cuisines.
豆蔻是许多亚洲菜肴中的珍贵香料。

Amomum villosum is commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine.
砂仁（一种豆蔻）常用于中医。


近义词：cardamom (specifically refers to the spice derived from some Amomum species), ginger (broadly related)
反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for a plant genus)
词性: noun
