backdate:/ˌbækˈdeɪt/| v.|回溯日期，追溯既往|To put an earlier date on (a document, cheque, etc.) than the actual date.|The cheque was backdated to January 1st. 这张支票被回溯到1月1日。|近义词：anachronize, postdate|反义词: predate|词性:动词

backdate:/ˈbækdeɪt/| n.|回溯日期|An instance of putting an earlier date on a document than the actual date.|The backdate on the contract raised some eyebrows. 合同上的回溯日期引起了一些人的注意。|近义词：retroactive date|反义词: future date|词性:名词
