fidalgo:/fɪˈdælɡoʊ/
n.
贵族（尤指葡萄牙或西班牙的）
A nobleman, especially of Portuguese or Spanish descent.
He was a fidalgo, proud of his lineage. 他是一位贵族，以自己的血统为荣。
nobleman, gentleman, aristocrat
commoner, peasant
noun
