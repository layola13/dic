hennish:/ˈhɛnɪʃ/| adj.|像母鸡的；胆怯的；婆婆妈妈的|Resembling a hen, especially in being timid or fussy.|例句：He had a rather hennish way of flapping his hands when he got excited. 他激动时拍打双手的方式有点像母鸡。|近义词：timid, cowardly, fussy, nervous, apprehensive|反义词：brave, bold, courageous, daring|词性：形容词

hennish:/ˈhɛnɪʃ/| adj.|女性化的；适于女性的|Characteristic of or suitable for a woman, sometimes in a derogatory sense.|例句：The decor was rather hennish, with lots of floral patterns and pastel colours.  这儿的装饰非常女性化，有很多花卉图案和柔和的颜色。|近义词：feminine, womanly|反义词：masculine, manly|词性：形容词
