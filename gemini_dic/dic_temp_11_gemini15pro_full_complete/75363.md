froths:/frɒθs/, /frɑːθs/| n. v.| 泡沫，起泡沫 |A mass of small bubbles formed on or in liquid, typically by agitation or fermentation. (As a verb) To produce or emit froth.| The beer froths when poured. 啤酒倒出来时会起泡沫。|近义词：foam, bubbles, lather|反义词: liquid, flat|词性:noun, verb
Etymology: From Old English *frōþ, from Proto-Germanic *frōþaz. Cognate with Dutch fraad (“froth”), German Fraß (“food for animals”), Old Norse froða (“froth”).
