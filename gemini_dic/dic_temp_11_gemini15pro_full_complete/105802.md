kissability:/ˌkɪsəˈbɪləti/
n.|可吻性，令人想亲吻的程度|The quality of being appealing enough to kiss; attractiveness.|She has a certain kissability about her. 她有一种让人想亲吻的魅力。|近义词：attractiveness, appeal, charm|反义词：unattractiveness, repulsion|词性:名词

Etymology:
kiss (n.) + -able (suffix) + -ity (suffix)

kiss (n.) Old English *cyssan "to kiss," of imitative origin.
-able (suffix) Middle English -able, -ible, from Old French -able and directly from Latin -abilis.
-ity (suffix) from Old French and Latin; Old French -ité, from Latin -itas, -itatis.
