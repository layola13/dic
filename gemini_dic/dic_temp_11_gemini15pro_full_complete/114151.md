mahayanists:/ˌmɑːhɑːˈjɑːnɪsts/
n.
大乘佛教徒
Followers of Mahayana Buddhism, one of the two major traditions of Buddhism.
The Mahayanists believe in the bodhisattva ideal. 大乘佛教徒相信菩萨理想。
Many Mahayanists strive to achieve enlightenment for the benefit of all beings. 许多大乘佛教徒努力证悟，以利益一切众生。
Synonyms: Mahayana Buddhists
Antonyms: Theravadins, Hinayanists
noun
