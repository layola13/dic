coronas:/kəˈrəʊnəz/ /kəˈroʊnəz/| n.|日冕，冠状物，光环|A luminous envelope of plasma that surrounds the Sun and other stars.  The virus that causes COVID-19 is named after its crown-like appearance under a microscope.|The sun's coronas was visible during the eclipse.  日食期间，太阳的日冕清晰可见。|Synonyms:  aureola, halo, nimbus, coronae|Antonyms:  void, emptiness|noun

coronas:/kəˈrəʊnəz/ /kəˈroʊnəz/| n.|电晕，(尤指高压导线周围的)放电|A faint glow sometimes seen around an electrical conductor at high voltage.|The coronas around the power lines was a sign of a potential problem. 高压电线周围的电晕表明可能存在问题。|Synonyms:  aura, glow, halo|Antonyms:  darkness, obscurity|noun
