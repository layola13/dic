ambulatorily:/ˌæmbjʊləˈtɔːrɪli/
adverb
以步行方式地；无需卧床地
in a way that involves walking around or moving about
例句：The patient was ambulatorily treated.  这位病人接受了无需卧床的治疗。

例句：She was able to move ambulatorily after the surgery.  手术后她能够走动了。

近义词：on foot, walking, mobilizing
反义词: bedridden, immobile
词性: adverb
