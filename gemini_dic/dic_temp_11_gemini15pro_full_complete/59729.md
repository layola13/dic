ecmascript:/ˈɛkməskrɪpt/| n. |ECMAScript，(一种脚本语言标准)|A standardized scripting language specification, most commonly used for client-side scripting on the World Wide Web.  It is best known as the language on which JavaScript is based. |Example: ECMAScript 6 introduced many new features to the language.  ECMAScript 6为该语言引入了许多新特性。|近义词:JavaScript|反义词:(无)|词性:noun
