fluidize:/ˈfluːɪdaɪz/| v. |流化，使…流化|To make a substance, especially a solid, behave like a fluid by suspending it in a gas or liquid.|The catalyst is fluidized by the upward flow of gas.  催化剂被向上流动的气体流化。|The process fluidizes the coal for easier transport. 这个过程使煤流化以便于运输。|近义词：liquefy, suspend|反义词：solidify|词性:动词
fluidization:/ˌfluːɪdaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |流化，流态化|The process of making a substance fluid, especially by suspending it in a gas or liquid.|Fluidization technology is widely used in the chemical industry. 流化技术在化工行业得到广泛应用。|The fluidization of the sand created a quicksand-like effect. 沙子的流化产生了类似流沙的效果。|近义词：liquefaction, suspension|反义词：solidification|词性:名词

词源:  From fluid + -ize.  "Fluid" comes from Latin "fluidus," meaning "flowing."
