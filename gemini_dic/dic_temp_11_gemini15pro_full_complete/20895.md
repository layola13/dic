blea:/bliː/
n.
the inner bark of a tree, especially of the oak, formerly ground up and used in tanning.
树的内层树皮，尤指橡树皮，以前磨碎后用于鞣革。
Oak blea was a key ingredient in traditional tanning methods. 橡树皮是传统鞣革方法中的关键成分。
近义词:tanbark
反义词: (None - lacks a direct opposite)
词性:noun


词源: From Old English *blēa, from Proto-Germanic *blaijaną. Cognate with Old Norse bleyja (“to cover”). Related to Old English blǣc (“pale, wan, livid”).
