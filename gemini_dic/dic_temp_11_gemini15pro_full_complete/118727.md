Relating to or involving metageometry, which is the study of geometries that differ from Euclidean geometry, especially non-Euclidean geometries.
Metageometrical investigations explored the implications of abandoning Euclid's parallel postulate. 元几何的研究探索了放弃欧几里得平行公设的含义。
