fizzled:/ˈfɪzəld/ | v. | 失败；落空；逐渐消失 |To end or fail in a weak or disappointing way. | The party fizzled out when the music stopped.  聚会随着音乐的停止而逐渐冷清下来。|近义词：failed, flopped, petered out | 反义词：succeeded, thrived | verb

fizzled:/ˈfɪzəld/ | adj. |  失败的；落空的 (less common) | Having ended or failed in a weak or disappointing way.  |  His grand plans for the business ended up fizzled. 他雄心勃勃的商业计划最终以失败告终。|近义词：failed, unsuccessful|反义词：successful, thriving | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
Likely imitative of the sound of a fizzing drink going flat, or a fire sputtering out.  Possibly related to the Middle Low German *fisseln "to break wind silently."
