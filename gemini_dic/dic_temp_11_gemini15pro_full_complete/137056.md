nonstooping:/nɒnˈstuːpɪŋ/ | adj. | 不屈不挠的，不弯腰的 | Not stooping; remaining upright; also, continuing without stopping or pausing.  |  The nonstooping spirit of the marathon runners inspired the crowd.  马拉松运动员不屈不挠的精神鼓舞了人群。 |  She maintained a nonstooping posture despite her age. 尽管年事已高，她仍然保持着挺拔的姿势。 | 近义词:  unyielding, resolute, persistent, continuous,  uninterrupted | 反义词: yielding, stooping, intermittent, halting | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "stooping" (present participle of "stoop," from Old English "stūpian," meaning "to bend down").
