carnality:/kɑːrˈnælɪti/
n.
肉体；肉欲；淫荡
The state of being concerned with or preoccupied by physical, especially sexual, needs and activities.
He indulged in carnality. 他沉溺于肉欲。
近义词：lust, sensuality, fleshiness
反义词：spirituality, chastity, abstinence
词性：名词

carnality:/kɑːrˈnælɪti/
n.
肉体关系；性关系
Sexual intercourse. (Often used in legal or religious contexts.)
The scriptures condemn carnality outside of marriage. 圣经谴责婚外性行为。
近义词：intercourse, copulation
反义词：abstinence, chastity
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin carnalitas "fleshiness," from Latin carnalis "of the flesh," from caro, carnis "flesh"  + -ity.
