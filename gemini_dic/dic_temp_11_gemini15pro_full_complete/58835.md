duplex:/ˈduːpleks/ (US) /ˈdjuːpleks/ (UK)| n. adj.|双工的，复式的，双重的；双联式房屋|A duplex is a building divided into two separate dwelling units, either side-by-side or stacked one above the other. It can also refer to something having two principal parts or elements.|The family lived in a duplex, sharing a wall with their neighbors. 这家人住在一个双联式房屋里，与邻居共用一堵墙。|Synonyms: two-family house, semi-detached|Antonyms: single-family home|n. adj.

duplex:/ˈduːpleks/ (US) /ˈdjuːpleks/ (UK)| adj.|双工的，双向的|Duplex communication allows information to flow in both directions simultaneously.|The duplex printer could print on both sides of the paper. 这台双面打印机可以在纸的两面打印。|Synonyms: two-way, double|Antonyms: simplex, one-way|adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *duplex* "twofold, double," from *duo* "two" + *plicō* "to fold."
