outqueried:/ˌaʊtˈkwɪərid/
verb
胜过提问；在提问中胜过
To surpass or defeat someone in questioning or argument, especially by asking more insightful or pertinent questions.
The experienced lawyer outqueried the young witness, revealing inconsistencies in his testimony.  经验丰富的律师在提问中胜过了年轻的证人，揭露了他证词中的矛盾之处。
近义词: outwit, outsmart, outmaneuver
反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted
