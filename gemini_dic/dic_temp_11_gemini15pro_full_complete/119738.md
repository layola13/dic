midweekly:/ˌmɪdˈwiːkli/| adj.|一周中期的；周三的|Happening or done in the middle of the week.|例句：The midweekly meeting was postponed.  一周中期的会议被推迟了。|近义词：midweek|反义词:weekend|词性:形容词

midweekly:/ˌmɪdˈwiːkli/| n.|周三报纸|A newspaper published in the middle of the week, typically on a Wednesday.|例句：I always read the midweekly for local news. 我总是阅读周三的报纸来获取本地新闻。|近义词：midweek paper|反义词:weekend edition|词性:名词
