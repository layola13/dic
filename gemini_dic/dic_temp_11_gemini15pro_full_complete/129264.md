noddingly:/ˈnɑːdɪŋli/| adv.|点头地，赞许地|In a manner that involves nodding one's head, often to indicate agreement, understanding, or encouragement.|She noddedly agreed with the proposal. 她点头表示同意这个提议。|近义词：agreeably, approvingly, affirmatively|反义词：disapprovingly, negatively|词性:副词

Etymology: From "nodding" (present participle of "nod") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Nod" comes from Old English *hnodian.
