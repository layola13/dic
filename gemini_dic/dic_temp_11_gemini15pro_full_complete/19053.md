bethrall:/bɪˈθrɔːl/| vt.| 迷住，吸引，使着迷|To capture the attention and interest of someone completely; to fascinate.|The magician's performance completely bethralled the audience.  魔术师的表演完全迷住了观众。

The beautiful sunset bethralled us all.  美丽的日落使我们所有人都着迷。|近义词：captivate, enchant, fascinate, mesmerize|反义词：bore, repel, disgust|词性:动词

词源:
From be- + thrall.  Be- is an intensifying prefix. Thrall originally meant "slave" or "servant," so bethrall literally means "to enslave" or "to hold in bondage," which evolved into the figurative sense of captivating someone's attention.
