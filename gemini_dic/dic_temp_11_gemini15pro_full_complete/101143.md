investment:/ɪnˈvɛstmənt/ (US) /ɪnˈvɛstmənt/ (UK)| n.|投资; 投资额; 投入|The act of putting money, effort, time, etc. into something to make a profit or get an advantage, or the money, effort, time, etc. used.|

例句：
He made a large investment in real estate.
他投资了大量的房地产。

例句：
Education is an investment in the future.
教育是对未来的投资。

近义词：expenditure, outlay, spending, contribution
反义词：divestment, withdrawal
词性：noun
