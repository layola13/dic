factoidal:/fækˈtɔɪdəl/
adjective
事实性的；貌似真实的；伪造的
Resembling or having the characteristics of a fact, but not necessarily true.  Presented as factual, but actually of questionable truth.
例句：Many factoidal stories are circulating on social media. 许多貌似真实的故事在社交媒体上流传。
例句：The article was full of factoidal assertions that couldn't be verified. 这篇文章充满了无法证实的貌似真实的主张。
近义词：fact-like, pseudo-factual, spurious
反义词：factual, true, genuine
词性: adjective

词源：
From fact + -oidal, influenced by anecdotal.  The suffix "-oidal" means "resembling" or "having the form of."
