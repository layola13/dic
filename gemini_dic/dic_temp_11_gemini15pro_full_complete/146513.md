overgenerosity:/ˌoʊvərˌdʒɛnəˈrɒsəti/ | n. | 过度慷慨，过分大方 | The state of being overgenerous; excessive generosity.  It can sometimes lead to financial difficulties or be taken advantage of. | His overgenerosity left him with little money to pay his own bills. 他的过度慷慨使他几乎没有钱支付自己的账单。|  Synonyms: lavishness, prodigality, extravagance | Antonyms: stinginess, miserliness, parsimony | noun
