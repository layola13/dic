nonregressively:/ˌnɑːnriˈɡrɛsɪvli/
adverb|非退化地，不退步地|In a way that does not regress or decline; progressively.|The system is designed to learn nonregressively, meaning it retains previously acquired knowledge while adding new information.|近义词:progressively, forward, onward|反义词:regressively, backward, declining|词性:adverb
Etymology:  "non-" (not) + "regressively" (from "regress," ultimately from Latin "regressus," past participle of "regredi" - to go back).
