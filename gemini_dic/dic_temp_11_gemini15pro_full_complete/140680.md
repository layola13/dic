ominate:/ˈɒmɪneɪt/ /ˈɑːmɪneɪt/| v.|预兆，预示；(不祥地) 出现|To indicate or foreshadow, especially something negative or inauspicious; to loom or appear in a menacing way.|例句：Dark clouds ominated a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。

例句：The tower ominated the landscape. 这座塔在景观中显得阴森森的。|近义词：forebode, portend, presage, augur|反义词：N/A|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ominari*, present active infinitive of *ominor* ("foretell, prophesy, predict, point out"), from *omen* ("omen, portent").
