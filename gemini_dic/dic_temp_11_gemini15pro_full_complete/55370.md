distastefully:/dɪsˈteɪstfəli/
adverb
令人厌恶地，讨厌地
in a way that shows a lack of good taste and consideration for other people's feelings.
She distastefully mocked his accent. 她令人厌恶地嘲笑他的口音。
Synonyms: offensively, objectionably, vulgarly, crudely, indelicately
Antonyms: tastefully, respectfully, politely, pleasantly, agreeably
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + tasteful + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin gustus "taste."
