hobnobbing:/ˈhɒbnɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbnɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| n. | 交往，亲密来往，(尤指)与地位较高的人交往 | The act of spending time with someone, especially someone who is important or famous, in a friendly way. | He was hobnobbing with royalty at the garden party. 他在园游会上与皇室成员亲密来往。| Synonyms: socializing, mingling, fraternizing, associating | Antonyms: isolating, avoiding, shunning | noun
