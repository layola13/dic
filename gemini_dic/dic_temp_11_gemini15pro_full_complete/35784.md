cockatoo:/ˌkɒkəˈtuː/ (UK), /ˌkɑːkəˈtuː/ (US)| n. |凤头鹦鹉|A parrot with a large, brightly coloured crest and curved beak, native to Australia and surrounding islands.|例句：The cockatoo squawked loudly from its perch.  凤头鹦鹉从栖木上大声鸣叫。

例句：We saw a flock of sulphur-crested cockatoos flying overhead. 我们看到一群硫冠凤头鹦鹉从头顶飞过。

|近义词：parrot|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
