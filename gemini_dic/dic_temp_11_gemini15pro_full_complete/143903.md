outpraise:/ˌaʊtˈpreɪz/
verb
超越赞美; 过度赞美
To praise excessively or more than someone or something else.
He outpraised all the other speakers at the conference. 他在会议上对所有其他发言人的赞扬都超过了他们。
She outpraised her rival's product, highlighting its flaws. 她过度赞扬了竞争对手的产品，突出了它的缺陷（反讽）。
近义词:laud, extol, overpraise
反义词:criticize, condemn, disparage
词性:v.
