indentors:/ɪnˈdɛntərz/| n.|缩进器；缩进工具；压头|A tool or device used to create indentations, especially in metal or other hard materials.  They are also used in typesetting and word processing to control the spacing at the beginning of a line of text.|The blacksmith used special indentors to create a decorative pattern on the iron gate. 铁匠用特殊的压头在铁门上创造了一个装饰图案。|The typesetter adjusted the indentors to create a hanging indent for the bibliography. 排字员调整了缩进器，为参考书目创建了一个悬挂缩进。|synonyms:  indenter,  notcher,  impressor|antonyms:  protruder,  extender|noun
