ontogenic:/ˌɒntəˈdʒɛnɪk/ |adj.|个体发生的，个体发育的|Relating to ontogeny, the development of an individual organism from its earliest stage to maturity.|The researchers studied the ontogenic changes in the brain structure of mice. 研究人员研究了小鼠大脑结构的个体发育变化。|Synonyms: developmental, maturational|Antonyms: phylogenetic|adjective
