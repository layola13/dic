cullionly:/ˈkʌliəni/| adj.|卑鄙的，恶棍的，懦弱的|A mean, base, or cowardly person; a scoundrel.|He called his opponent a cullionly knave.|近义词：villainous, dastardly, craven|反义词：noble, honorable, courageous|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *culȝon, from culle (“testicle”) + -on, a diminutive suffix.  Originally referred to a man castrated as punishment, hence a base or despicable person.
