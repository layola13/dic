bergmote:/ˈbɜːrməʊt/
n.
矿工大会，矿工法庭
a meeting or court of miners; a miners' assembly.
The bergmote met to discuss safety regulations. 矿工大会开会讨论安全规章。
The bergmote levied fines for unsafe practices. 矿工法庭对不安全的操作处以罚款。
synonyms: miners' assembly, miners' meeting, miners' court
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "individual miner" as opposed to a collective)
noun
