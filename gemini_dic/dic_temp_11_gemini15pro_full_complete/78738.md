giglet: /ˈɡɪɡlət/ | n. | 轻佻女子，爱傻笑的女孩 | A frivolous, flighty, or giggling girl. | She was known as a giglet in her youth, always laughing and flitting about. 她年轻时被称为轻佻女子，总是笑着四处乱窜。 |  Synonyms: flirt, coquette, minx | Antonyms: prude, matron | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *giglen, frequentative of giggen  "to giggle," of uncertain origin.
