anisomeric:/ˌænɪsoʊˈmɛrɪk/ | adj. | 异构的（指异头物）| Relating to or being anomers; exhibiting anomerism. | The anisomeric forms of glucose differ in the configuration at the anomeric carbon. 葡萄糖的异构体在异头碳的构型上有所不同。| Example 2: The two anisomeric forms of the sugar interconvert in solution.  这种糖的两种异头异构体在溶液中可以相互转化。 | synonyms: anomeric | antonyms: N/A | adjective
