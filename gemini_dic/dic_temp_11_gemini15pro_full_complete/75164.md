fritzed-out:/ˈfrɪtstˌaʊt/
adjective
烧坏的；故障的；精疲力竭的
Broken, malfunctioning, or exhausted, especially due to overuse or stress related to technology or electronic devices.  Often implies a sense of being overwhelmed and unable to function properly.
My old laptop is completely fritzed-out; it won't even turn on. 我的旧笔记本电脑完全坏了，甚至无法开机。
After working 12 hours straight on the computer, I felt completely fritzed-out. 在电脑前连续工作了 12 个小时后，我感到筋疲力尽。
近义词: burned-out, exhausted, fried, broken, malfunctioning
反义词: functional, working, energetic, rested
词性: adjective
