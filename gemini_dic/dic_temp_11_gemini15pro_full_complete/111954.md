loltun:/loʊlˈtuːn/| n. |洛尔通（玛雅遗址）|A Mayan archaeological site located in the Yucatán Peninsula of Mexico, known for its impressive cave system and historical significance.  It features a large "cenote" or sinkhole, and evidence of human habitation dating back to the Classic period.| We visited the Loltun Caves during our trip to the Yucatan. 我们在尤卡坦半岛旅行期间参观了洛尔通洞穴。|近义词:Mayan ruins, archaeological site|反义词: (None -  a proper noun referring to a specific place doesn't typically have antonyms)|词性:noun
