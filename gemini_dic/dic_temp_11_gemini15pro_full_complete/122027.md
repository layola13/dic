missuccess: /ˌmɪsˈsəkˈsɛs/
n.
lack of success; failure
The project was a missuccess due to poor planning.  这个项目由于计划不周而失败了。
The new product launch was a missuccess and resulted in significant financial losses. 新产品的发布是一个失败，导致了巨大的经济损失。
近义词: failure, flop, debacle, fiasco
反义词: success, triumph, achievement
词性: noun


Etymology:  A blend of "miss" (meaning failure or lack of) and "success".  This word is not standard English and considered informal or even nonstandard.  It is formed by analogy with words like "misfortune" or "mishap."
