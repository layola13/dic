bamboozle:/bæmˈbuːzl/ | v. | 欺骗，蒙蔽，迷惑 | To trick or deceive someone, often in a playful or mischievous way. | He bamboozled his little brother into giving him his candy. 他哄骗他弟弟把糖果给了他。

bamboozle:/bæmˈbuːzl/ | n. | 欺骗，蒙蔽 | An act of tricking or deceiving someone.  |  The whole affair was a bamboozle to get him out of the country.  整个事件都是为了把他骗出国而设的局。


bamboozle:/bæmˈbuːzl/ | adj. |  (非正式) 令人困惑的，欺骗性的 | Confusing or deceptive (informal usage, less common). | The instructions were so bamboozling that I couldn't assemble the furniture.  说明书太让人迷惑了，我根本组装不了家具。

近义词: deceive, trick, hoodwink, mislead, dupe, confuse, bewilder
反义词: enlighten, clarify, explain
词性:  verb (primarily), noun (less common), adjective (informal, rare)

词源:  Origin uncertain; possibly from the dialectal English verb *bam* (to hoax, cheat) + *foozle* (to bungle, deceive).
