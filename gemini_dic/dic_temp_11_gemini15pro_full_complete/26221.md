butterfly:/ˈbʌtərˌflaɪ/ (US) /ˈbʌtəflaɪ/ (UK)| n. |蝴蝶|An insect with large, often brightly coloured wings.|例句：A butterfly landed on the flower. 一只蝴蝶落在了花上。

例句：The children chased butterflies in the garden. 孩子们在花园里追逐蝴蝶。|近义词：moth|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *buterfleoge*, equivalent to butter +‎ fly.  The reason for the name is uncertain, but theories include the insect's excrement resembling butter, or that butterflies were active when butter was being churned, or that the color of some species resembled butter.
