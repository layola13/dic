librar:/ˈlaɪbrɑːr/ (US), /ˈlaɪbrər/ (UK)| n.|图书馆|A building or room containing collections of books, periodicals, and sometimes films and recorded music for people to read, borrow, or refer to.|She often studies in the library. 她经常在图书馆学习。|近义词:athenaeum, book repository|反义词: N/A|Noun
