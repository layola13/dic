ichneumonides:/ɪknjuːˈmɒnɪdiːz/ | n. |姬蜂科昆虫| A large and important family of parasitic wasps (Ichneumonidae), whose larvae feed on or in other insects, especially caterpillars.  These wasps play a crucial role in controlling insect populations.| The ichneumonides are beneficial to gardeners as they control pest populations.| 近义词: parasitic wasps, ichneumon flies | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "pest insects" as their prey) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From New Latin *Ichneumonides*, plural of *Ichneumon*, from Greek *ikhneumōn* "ichneumon," literally "tracker, hunter," from *ikhneuein* "to track, hunt."
