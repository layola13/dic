devotionals: /dɪˈvoʊʃənəlz/ | n. | 虔诚的读物；灵修材料 | Short readings or reflections, often with a religious or spiritual focus, designed to inspire contemplation and prayer. |  I read my devotionals every morning to start my day with a sense of peace. 我每天早上读灵修材料，以平和的心态开始新的一天。

| Example: The pastor wrote daily devotionals for his congregation. 牧师为他的会众撰写每日灵修材料。 | Synonym: meditations, reflections, prayers | Antonym: distractions, frivolities | Noun

devotional: /dɪˈvoʊʃənl/ | adj. | 虔诚的；献身的 | Characterized by or expressing devotion. |  She has a devotional attitude towards her work. 她对工作非常投入。

| Example: He sang a devotional hymn. 他唱了一首虔诚的赞美诗。 | Synonym: pious, devout, religious | Antonym: irreverent, impious | Adjective


Etymology: From Late Middle English *devociounal, from Old French devocional, from Late Latin devotionālis, from Latin devotiō (“dedication, consecration”), from devotus (“devoted”).
