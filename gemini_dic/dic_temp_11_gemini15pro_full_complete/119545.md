microsite: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌsaɪt/ | n. | 小型网站，微型网站 | A small website, typically dedicated to a particular product, campaign, or topic, that exists independently of a company's main website. |  Example 1: The company launched a microsite specifically for its new line of athletic wear.  该公司专门为其新的运动服系列推出了一个小型网站。

Example 2:  The marketing team created a microsite to promote the upcoming conference.  营销团队创建了一个小型网站来宣传即将召开的会议。 | 近义词: minisite, landing page | 反义词:  main website, corporate website | 词性: 名词
