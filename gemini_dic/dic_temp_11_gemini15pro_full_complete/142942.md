out-waved:/ˌaʊtˈweɪvd/
verb
胜过(在挥手致意方面)；挥舞(手臂等)超过
surpass in waving; wave (arms, etc.) more vigorously than
The enthusiastic fans out-waved everyone else at the concert.  热情的歌迷在音乐会上挥舞得比其他人都厉害。
Near Antonyms: be outdone, lose to
Synonyms: surpass, exceed
