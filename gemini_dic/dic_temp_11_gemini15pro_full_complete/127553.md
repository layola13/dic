necropsied: /ˈnɛkrɑpsiːd/ /ˈniːkrɒpsiːd/
verb (past tense)
尸检过的；被解剖的
examined (a dead body or plant) to discover the cause of death or disease.
The animal was necropsied to determine the cause of death.  这只动物被解剖以确定死因。
The necropsied tissues revealed the presence of a tumor.  解剖后的组织显示存在肿瘤。
Synonyms:  autopsy, dissected, examined post-mortem
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "living," "viable," or "untouched" in certain contexts)
verb
