fibrously:/ˈfaɪbrəsli/
adv.
纤维状地；成纤维状地
composed of or resembling fibers; in a fibrous manner
The meat was tough and fibrously textured.  这肉很硬，纤维很粗。
近义词: stringily, filamentously
反义词: smoothly, homogenously
词性: 副词

fibrous:/ˈfaɪbrəs/
adj.
纤维的；纤维状的；含纤维的
containing or consisting of fibers
Celery is a fibrous vegetable.  芹菜是一种富含纤维的蔬菜。
近义词: stringy, filamentous
反义词: smooth, nonfibrous
词性: 形容词

fiber/fibre: /ˈfaɪbər/ (US) /ˈfaɪbə/ (UK)
n.
纤维；光纤；（性格、道德等的）品质；基本要素
a thread or filament from which a vegetable tissue, mineral substance, or textile is formed; a slender elongated threadlike structure
Plants are made up of fibers. 植物是由纤维构成的。
近义词: filament, thread
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider concepts like "whole," "mass")
词性: 名词


词源:
From Latin *fibrosus, from fibra (“fiber”)
