homonymic: /ˌhɒməˈnɪmɪk/ (UK) /ˌhɑːməˈnɪmɪk/ (US)| adj.|同音的，同名的|Having the same name or pronunciation as another word but a different meaning.|The words "right" and "write" are homonymic.|近义词：homonymous|反义词：heteronymic|词性:形容词

homonym: /ˈhɒmənɪm/ (UK) /ˈhɑːmənɪm/ (US)| n.|同音异义词|A word that is spelled and pronounced the same as another word but has a different meaning.|The words "bat" (the animal) and "bat" (used in baseball) are homonyms.|近义词：homophone, homograph|反义词：heteronym|词性:名词
