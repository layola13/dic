oilskin:/ˈɔɪlskɪn/| n. adj.|油布；油布雨衣|A waterproof cloth made by treating cotton or linen with oil or wax.  A garment made from this cloth. |例句：The fisherman wore an oilskin to protect himself from the rain. 渔夫穿了一件油布雨衣来挡雨。

例句：He kept his maps safe in an oilskin pouch. 他把地图安全地放在一个油布袋里。|近义词：waterproof, raincoat, slicker|反义词: absorbent|词性:名词, 形容词
