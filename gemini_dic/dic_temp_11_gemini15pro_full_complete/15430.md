bacterium:/bækˈtɪəriəm/| n. |细菌|A single-celled microscopic organism, some of which cause disease.|例句：The bacterium E. coli can cause food poisoning. 大肠杆菌会导致食物中毒。

例句：Scientists are studying the bacterium to understand its resistance to antibiotics. 科学家正在研究这种细菌以了解其对抗生素的耐药性。

|近义词：microbe, germ|反义词: None (bacteria don't have direct opposites, perhaps "multicellular organism" could be considered a contrasting concept)|词性: Noun
