changqing:/tʃɑːŋˈtʃɪŋ/| adj. n.|常青的，常绿植物，长青| Evergreen; referring to plants that retain green leaves throughout the year. Also used as a name or to symbolize enduring vitality. |The changqing pine trees stood tall against the winter sky. 常青的松树高高耸立在冬日的天空下。|The Changqing Gardens are famous for their lush greenery. 长青花园以其郁郁葱葱的绿色植物而闻名。|近义词：evergreen, perennial|反义词: deciduous, ephemeral|词性: adjective, noun


词源:  From Chinese 长青 (chángqīng), meaning "evergreen" or "long-lasting green."  长 (cháng) means "long," and 青 (qīng) means "green" or "youthful."
