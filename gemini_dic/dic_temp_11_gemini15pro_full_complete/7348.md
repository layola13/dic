anicut:/ˈænɪkʌt/
n.
拦河坝, 小型水坝, 灌溉坝
A low dam built across a stream to raise the level of water for irrigation.
The anicut diverted water into the fields for the rice paddies.  拦河坝将水引入稻田。
The farmers relied on the anicut for their crops.  农民依靠拦河坝灌溉农作物。
近义词: weir, dam, barrage
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "natural waterway")


ETYMOLOGY->
From Tamil  "anakkattu"  meaning "dam, embankment".
