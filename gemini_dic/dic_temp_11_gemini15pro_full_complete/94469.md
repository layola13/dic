imponderableness:/ɪmˈpɒndərəblənəs/ /ɪmˈpɑːndərəblənəs/| n. | 不可衡量性；难以理解的事物；无重量 |The state or quality of being imponderable; something that is difficult or impossible to assess or understand. |  The imponderableness of her sudden decision left everyone baffled.  她突然的决定令人费解，让每个人都感到困惑。 | 近义词：inscrutability, mystery, enigma, uncertainty | 反义词：certainty, clarity, obviousness | 词性: noun

imponderable:/ɪmˈpɒndərəbl/ /ɪmˈpɑːndərəbl/| adj. | 不可衡量的；难以理解的；无重量的 | Difficult or impossible to assess or understand. | The future is full of imponderable factors. 未来充满了不可估量的因素。| 近义词：inscrutable, unfathomable, immeasurable | 反义词：measurable, calculable, understandable | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
