noncomic:/nɑːnˈkɑːmɪk/
adjective
非喜剧的；严肃的
Not relating to or characteristic of comedy; serious.
His plays are known for their noncomic themes. 他的戏剧以其非喜剧主题而闻名。
近义词：serious, grave, somber
反义词：comic, humorous, funny
词性：adjective
