non-distinctive:/ˌnɒn dɪˈstɪŋktɪv/ | adj. | 非区别性的，非显著的 | Not serving to distinguish; not characteristic or individualizing. | The sound change was non-distinctive, so it didn't create a new phoneme.  这个语音变化是非区别性的，因此它没有创造新的音素。 |  The color variations were non-distinctive and difficult to perceive. 这些颜色变化不明显，难以察觉。 | Synonyms: insignificant, unremarkable, common, ordinary, generic | Antonyms: distinctive, characteristic, unique, remarkable, significant | adjective
