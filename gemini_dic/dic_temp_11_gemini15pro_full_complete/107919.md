larviform: /ˈlɑːrvɪfɔːrm/ (US), /ˈlɑːvɪfɔːm/ (UK)| adj.| 幼虫状的，像幼虫的|Resembling or shaped like a larva. |The larviform creature wriggled in the mud.  这像幼虫的生物在泥里蠕动。|The parasite has a larviform stage in its life cycle. 这种寄生虫在其生命周期中有一个幼虫阶段。|近义词：larval, grub-like, worm-like|反义词: adult, mature|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin *larva* ("ghost, mask, larva") + *-formis* ("-shaped").
