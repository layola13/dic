chaffery:/ˈtʃæfəri/
noun
玩笑; 戏弄;  打趣
Light, playful banter or teasing.
例句：He greeted her with some good-natured chaffery. 他用一些善意的玩笑来问候她。

例句：The friends engaged in friendly chaffery throughout the evening. 朋友们整晚都在友好地互相开玩笑。

近义词：banter, teasing, raillery, badinage
反义词：seriousness, solemnity

词性: n.
