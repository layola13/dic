ametabolic:/ˌeɪmɛtəˈbɒlɪk/
adjective
无代谢的，非代谢的
Relating to or characterized by ametabolism; not involving metabolism.  Lacking or failing to exhibit metabolism.
例句：Certain viruses are considered ametabolic because they lack the machinery for generating energy.  某些病毒被认为是无代谢的，因为它们缺乏产生能量的机制。

例句：Ametabolic organisms rely entirely on host cells for their survival. 无代谢生物完全依赖宿主细胞生存。
近义词：non-metabolic
反义词：metabolic
词性：adjective
