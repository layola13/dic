bazooka:/bəˈzuːkə/| n.|火箭筒，火箭炮|A portable rocket launcher used against tanks and other armored vehicles.|The soldier fired the bazooka at the enemy tank. 士兵用火箭筒攻击敌人的坦克。|Synonyms: rocket launcher, anti-tank weapon|Antonyms: N/A|noun

bazookas:/bəˈzuːkəz/| n.|火箭筒，火箭炮（复数）|Plural form of bazooka, referring to multiple portable rocket launchers.|The soldiers were armed with bazookas and grenades. 士兵们装备了火箭筒和手榴弹。|Synonyms: rocket launchers, anti-tank weapons|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the musical instrument called a "bazooka" played by American comedian Bob Burns in the 1930s, which resembled the weapon in shape.
