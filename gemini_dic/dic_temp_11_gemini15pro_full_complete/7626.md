annular:/ˈæn.jʊ.lər/ | adj. |环形的，环状的 |Shaped like a ring; of or forming a ring. |例句：An annular eclipse occurs when the Moon covers the Sun's center, leaving a visible ring of fire around the Moon's edges.  日环食发生在月亮遮住太阳中心时，在月球边缘留下一个可见的火环。

例句：The machine uses an annular cutter to create perfect circles in the metal. 这台机器使用环形刀具在金属上切割出完美的圆形。|近义词：ring-shaped, circular, ringlike |反义词: linear, straight |词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *annularis, from Latin anulus (“ring”) + -aris.
