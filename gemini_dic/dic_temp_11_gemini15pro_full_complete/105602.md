kindredship:/ˈkɪndrɪdʃɪp/| n.|亲属关系；血缘关系；相似性；同类关系|A feeling of closeness and understanding that exists between people. |例句：I felt a deep sense of kinship with the other volunteers. 我和其他志愿者感到了一种深深的亲属关系。

例句：There is a strong kinship between music and language.  音乐和语言之间有着很强的相似性。|近义词：affinity, relationship, connection, similarity, likeness, resemblance|反义词：alienation, estrangement, distance|词性:名词
