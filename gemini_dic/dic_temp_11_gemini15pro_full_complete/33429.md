churchwomen: /ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɪmɪn/
noun
教堂妇女；教会妇女
Women who are active members of a church.
The churchwomen organized a bake sale to raise money for the new roof. 教堂妇女组织了一个烘焙义卖来为新屋顶筹款。
The churchwomen gathered for their weekly Bible study. 教会的妇女们聚集在一起进行每周的圣经学习。
Synonyms: parishioners, congregants, female members of the church
Antonyms: None (There isn't a direct opposite of a churchwoman)
