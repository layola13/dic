cancelled:/ˈkænsəld/ (US) /ˈkænsld/ (UK)| v.|取消，撤销|to officially end or stop something before it has finished or before it was meant to happen.|例句：I cancelled my subscription to the magazine. 我取消了我的杂志订阅。|近义词：abort, abandon, withdraw, revoke|反义词:approve, confirm, validate|词性:动词过去式/过去分词

词源: From the Old French word "canceler,"  from Medieval Latin "cancellare," meaning "to cross out with lines," ultimately from Latin "cancelli," meaning "lattice."
