corncob-pipes:/ˈkɔːrnˌkɑːb paɪps/ | n. | 玉米芯烟斗 | A pipe for smoking, made from the dried cob of a corn plant.  The central cylindrical core of an ear of corn, after the kernels have been removed, can be fashioned into a simple smoking pipe. |  He lit his corncob-pipe and sat on the porch. 他点燃玉米芯烟斗，坐在门廊上。 |  pipe, tobacco pipe | cigarette, cigar | noun
