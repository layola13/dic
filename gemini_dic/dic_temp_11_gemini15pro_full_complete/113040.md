luminiferous:/ˌluːmɪˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
发光的，传播光的
transmitting or producing light.
The luminiferous ether was a hypothetical medium postulated in the 19th century to carry light waves. 19世纪假设的光以太是一种用来传播光波的假想介质。
The sun is a luminiferous body. 太阳是一个发光体。
近义词:radiant, luminous, bright
反义词:dark, opaque
