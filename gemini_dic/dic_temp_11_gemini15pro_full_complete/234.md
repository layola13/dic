abdicate: /ˈæbdɪˌkeɪt/ (US) /ˈæbdɪkeɪt/ (UK)| v. |放弃（权力、责任等）；退位|To formally renounce or relinquish a position of power or responsibility.|The king abdicated his throne. 国王退位了。|He abdicated all responsibility for the project. 他放弃了对该项目的所有责任。|近义词：resign, relinquish, renounce, step down|反义词：assume, usurp, seize|词性:动词
词源：From Latin abdicatus, past participle of abdicare (“to disown, renounce, reject”), from ab- (“away from”) + dicare (“to proclaim, dedicate”).
