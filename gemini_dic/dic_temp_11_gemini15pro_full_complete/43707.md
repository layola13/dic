creditress: /ˈkrɛdɪtrɪs/
noun
女债权人；女性债主
A woman to whom money is owed.
例句：The creditress demanded full payment of the debt. 女债权人要求全额偿还债务。

例句：The business owed a significant sum to the creditress.  这家公司欠了女债权人一大笔钱。

近义词：creditor, lender
反义词：debtor, borrower
词性：n.


Etymology: From Old French *creditrice, from Latin crēditrīx (“female lender”), from crēdere (“to believe, trust, lend”).  The -ess suffix denotes a female.
