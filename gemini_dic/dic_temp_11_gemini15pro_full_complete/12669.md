assembly's:/əˈsɛmbliz/| n.|集会的，装配的|The assembly's decision was final. 大会作出的决定是最终决定。|例句：The assembly's decision to raise taxes was unpopular.  大会提高税收的决定不受欢迎。

The school assembly's theme this week is kindness. 本周学校集会的主题是善良。|近义词：gathering's, meeting's, convention's, congress's|反义词:dispersal's, separation's|词性:名词所有格

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "assemblee,"  from "assembler" (to assemble), from Latin "ad" (to) + "simul" (together). The possessive form is created by adding 's.
