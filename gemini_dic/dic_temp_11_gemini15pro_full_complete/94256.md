impermanently: /ɪmˈpɜːrmənəntli/ | adv. | 非永久地，暂时地 | Not permanently; for a limited time only. | The exhibition was impermanently housed in the old school building.  这个展览临时设在旧校舍里。|  The refugees were impermanently settled in tents.  难民们临时住在帐篷里。 | 近义词: temporarily, briefly, fleetingly | 反义词: permanently, eternally, lastingly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "im-" (not) + "permanent" (lasting) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Permanent" comes from Latin "permanens," present participle of "permanere" (to continue, endure), from "per-" (through) + "manere" (to remain).
