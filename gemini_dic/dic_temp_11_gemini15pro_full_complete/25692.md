bunker mentality:/ˈbʌŋkər mɛnˈtælɪti/ | n. |掩体心态；闭关自守的心态；|A defensive, isolated, and often paranoid attitude, as if one were in a protective bunker, characterized by distrust, fear of the outside world, and a refusal to consider alternative perspectives.| The company developed a bunker mentality after the hostile takeover attempt.  这家公司在经历恶意收购企图后，形成了一种掩体心态。|Synonyms: siege mentality, defensiveness, parochialism, insularity, narrow-mindedness|Antonyms: open-mindedness, receptiveness, cosmopolitanism| noun


bunker mentalities:/ˈbʌŋkər mɛnˈtælɪtiz/ | n. |掩体心态（复数）| Multiple instances of a defensive, isolated, and often paranoid attitude. |  The various departments within the organization developed their own bunker mentalities, hindering collaboration.  该组织内部的各个部门都形成了各自的掩体心态，阻碍了合作。 |Synonyms: siege mentalities, defensivenesses, parochialisms, insularities, narrow-mindedness|Antonyms: open-mindedness, receptiveness, cosmopolitanism| noun
