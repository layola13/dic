fumingly:/ˈfjuːmɪŋli/
adverb
愤怒地，生气地
showing anger, especially by sending out smoke or fumes
He stomped off fumingly. 他怒气冲冲地离开了。
近义词：angrily, furiously, wrathfully
反义词：calmly, peacefully
词性：副词

词源：From fume + -ing + -ly.  "Fume" comes from the Old French "fum," which in turn derives from the Latin "fumus" meaning "smoke."
