colley:/ˈkɒli/ (UK), /ˈkɑːli/ (US)| n.|柯利牧羊犬; 苏格兰牧羊犬|A breed of herding dog, originating in Scotland, known for its intelligence and agility.  Commonly referred to as a Collie.|例句：The border colley expertly herded the sheep. 边境牧羊犬熟练地放牧羊群。

例句：Lassie, a famous fictional colley, was known for her loyalty.  著名的虚构牧羊犬莱西以其忠诚而闻名。|近义词：sheepdog, herding dog|反义词: (No direct antonym for a breed of dog)|词性:noun
