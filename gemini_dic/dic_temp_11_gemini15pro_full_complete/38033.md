componentry:/kəmˈpoʊnənˌtri/| n.|组件；部件；零件|The quality of the bicycle's componentry is excellent. 这辆自行车的部件质量非常好。|The company specializes in manufacturing high-end componentry for racing cars. 这家公司专门生产赛车的高端部件。|近义词：components, parts, elements|反义词: whole, entirety|词性:noun
Etymology: From component + -ry.  "Component" comes from the Late Latin "compōnēns", present participle of "compōnere" (to put together), from "com-" (together) + "pōnere" (to put, place).  The suffix "-ry" forms nouns denoting a collection or group of things, a place where something is done, or a state or condition.
