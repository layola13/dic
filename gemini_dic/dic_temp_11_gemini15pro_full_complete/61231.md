emaculation: /ɪˌmækjʊˈleɪʃən/
n.
去除污点；使洁净；使无瑕疵
The act of removing spots or stains; the state of being spotless or immaculate.
The emaculation of the old painting revealed vibrant colors underneath.  这幅古老绘画的去污露出了下面鲜艳的色彩。
近义词: cleansing, purification, cleaning
反义词: staining, soiling, defilement
词性: 名词

emaculation: /ɪˌmækjʊˈleɪʃən/
v.
去除污点；使洁净
To remove spots or stains; to make spotless or immaculate.
The restorer carefully emaculated the antique vase. 修复师小心翼翼地清洁了古董花瓶。
近义词: cleanse, purify, clean
反义词: stain, soil, defile
词性: 动词


词源:  From Late Latin *emaculatus, past participle of emaculare "to remove spots," from Latin e- "out" + macula "spot."
