non-metal:/ˈnɒnˌmɛtl/ (UK) /ˈnɑːnˌmɛtl/ (US)| n. |非金属|An element that doesn't have the characteristics of a metal (such as hardness, luster, or the ability to conduct heat or electricity).|例句：Oxygen is a non-metal. 氧气是一种非金属。|近义词：nonmetallic element|反义词:metal|noun

non-metallic:/ˌnɒn.məˈtæl.ɪk/ (UK) /ˌnɑːn.məˈtæl.ɪk/ (US)| adj. |非金属的|Not having the properties of a metal.|例句：Sulfur is a non-metallic element. 硫是一种非金属元素。|近义词：non-metal|反义词:metallic|adjective
