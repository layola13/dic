depressomotor:/dɪˌprɛsoʊˈmoʊtər/
adjective
抑制运动的; 降低运动机能的
Relating to or causing decreased motor activity or function.
Certain drugs have a depressomotor effect, slowing down physical reactions. 某些药物具有抑制运动的作用，会减缓身体反应。
The patient exhibited depressomotor symptoms after the surgery. 病人在手术后表现出运动机能抑制的症状。
近义词:hypokinetic,  akinetic
反义词:stimulatory
