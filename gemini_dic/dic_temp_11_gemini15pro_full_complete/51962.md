diacodium:/daɪəˈkoʊdiəm/
n.
止咳糖浆；罂粟糖浆
A syrup containing poppy heads, formerly used as a sedative and mild narcotic, especially for children.
Diacodium was a common remedy for coughs in the 19th century.  19世纪，罂粟糖浆是治疗咳嗽的常用药物。
Synonyms: syrup, opiate, anodyne, narcotic
Antonyms: stimulant
Etymology: From Late Latin diacodion, from Greek diā kōdeíōn (poppy heads), from diá (through) + kōdeíā (poppy head).
Noun
