cambistry: /ˈkæmbɪstri/| n. |换钱，兑换；汇兑业务|The study or practice of exchanging money, especially of different countries.|  Ancient cambistry involved complex calculations due to the varying weights and purity of different coins. 古代的汇兑业务涉及复杂的计算，因为不同硬币的重量和纯度各不相同。

Cambistry played a vital role in international trade before the advent of modern banking. 在现代银行业出现之前，汇兑在国际贸易中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：exchange, money changing|反义词: None|词性: noun
Etymology: From Old French *cambiste (“money changer”), from Italian cambista, from cambiare (“to exchange”), of uncertain origin.
