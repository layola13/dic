behooved:/bɪˈhuːvd/| v.|理应，应该，有必要|It behooves us to be careful. 我们有必要小心谨慎。|近义词：ought, should, befit, become|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以用should not, ought not 表示相反意思)|verb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
It behooves everyone to follow the rules.  每个人都应该遵守规则。
It behooves the government to address this issue.  政府有必要解决这个问题。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
It is behoving us to consider all options carefully. (用法不常见) 我们正在仔细考虑所有选项是应该的。
It is behoving them to act responsibly. (用法不常见)  他们有责任采取负责任的行动。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
It has behoved us to remain silent until now.  到目前为止，我们有必要保持沉默。
It has behoved them to work together on this project.  他们一直有必要在这个项目上合作。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous): (极少使用)


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
It behooved him to speak the truth. 他有必要说出真相。
It behooved them to act quickly. 他们有必要迅速采取行动。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous): (极少使用)


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
It had behoved them to warn us of the danger.  他们本来有必要警告我们危险。
It had behooved him to be more cautious.  他本来应该更加谨慎。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous): (极少使用)


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
It will behoove us to be prepared.  我们有必要做好准备。
It will behoove them to cooperate.  他们有必要合作。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous): (极少使用)


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
It will have behoved us to make a decision by then.  到那时，我们有必要做出决定。
It will have behoved them to take action before the deadline.  到截止日期前，他们有必要采取行动。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous): (极少使用)


虚拟语气:
If it behooved me to apologize, I would.  如果我有必要道歉，我会的。
It would have behoved him to be more considerate.  他本应该更体贴一些。


被动语态: (behooved 不用于被动语态)


疑问句:  (behooved 极少用于疑问句)


否定句:
It did not behoove him to be so rude.  他不应该这么粗鲁。
It will not behoove us to ignore the problem.  我们不应该忽视这个问题。
