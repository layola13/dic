codeveloper:/ˌkoʊdɪˈvɛləpər/| n.|共同开发者，合作开发者|A person who develops something jointly with another or others.|Example:  She worked with him as a codeveloper on the project. 她和他作为共同开发者一起参与这个项目。
Example: The codevelopers presented their new app at the conference. 共同开发者们在会议上展示了他们的新应用程序。|近义词：collaborator, partner, joint developer|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly competitor or sole developer)|词性: noun
