flask:/flɑːsk/ /flæsk/| n.|烧瓶；细颈瓶；酒瓶|A flask is a bottle, usually a small one with a narrow neck, that is used to hold liquids.|He filled the flask with water. 他用水灌满了烧瓶。|Synonyms: bottle, container, vessel|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object like a flask)|noun

flask:/flɑːsk/ /flæsk/| n.|一小口（尤指烈性酒）|A flask can also refer to the amount of liquid held in a flask, especially a small amount of spirits.|He took a flask of whisky to warm him up. 他喝了一小口威士忌暖暖身子。|Synonyms: shot, nip, swig|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a quantity)|noun
