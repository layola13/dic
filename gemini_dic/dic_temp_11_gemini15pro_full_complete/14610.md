adjective | 无生命的；缺乏活力的；不生动有趣的 | lacking life, spirit, or animation; inanimate |  Her avit performance disappointed the audience. 她毫无生气的表演让观众失望。 | lifeless, dull, inanimate | lively, animated, vibrant | adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *avītus, from avīre (“to long for, crave, desire”), from avis (“bird, omen”).  The original sense seems to have been "eager," hence "inspired," with the current sense arising through the negative prefix a-.
