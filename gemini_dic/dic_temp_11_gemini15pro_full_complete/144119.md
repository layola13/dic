outscoring:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːrɪŋ/
verb
超过…的得分
The act of scoring more points than an opponent in a game or competition.
The Lakers were outscoring their opponents by an average of 10 points a game. 湖人队平均每场比赛比对手多得10分。
近义词: outpointing, beating, defeating
反义词: being outscored, losing to
词性: Verb (present participle)
