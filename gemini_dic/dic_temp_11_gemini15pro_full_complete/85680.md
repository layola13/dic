have-a-short-fuse:/hæv ə ʃɔːrt fjuːz/ | idiom | 易怒，脾气暴躁 |  To have a quick temper; to get angry easily. This idiom refers to the short length of a fuse on explosives, implying a quick explosion of anger. | My boss has a short fuse, so be careful what you say to him. 我的老板脾气很暴躁，所以说话要小心点。| synonyms: quick-tempered, irascible, irritable, touchy | antonyms: even-tempered, patient, calm | idiom
