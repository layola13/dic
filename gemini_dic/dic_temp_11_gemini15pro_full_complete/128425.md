Also, refers to the province of Newfoundland and Labrador in Canada.|例句：My uncle has a Newfoundland dog. 我叔叔有一只纽芬兰犬。

例句：Newfoundland and Labrador is the easternmost province of Canada. 纽芬兰和拉布拉多是加拿大最东端的省份。|近义词：Newfoundland dog (for the dog breed); Labrador (for the province - often used together)|反义词:None|词性:noun, adjective
