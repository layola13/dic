kaiten:/ˈkaɪtɛn/| n.|开店; 开业; 开张; (日语, 指旋转寿司店)|A kaiten sushi restaurant is a conveyor belt sushi restaurant, where plates with different kinds of sushi are placed on a rotating conveyor belt that winds through the restaurant. |I love eating at the kaiten sushi restaurant. 我喜欢在旋转寿司店吃饭。|The new kaiten sushi bar is very popular. 新开的旋转寿司店很受欢迎。|Synonyms: conveyor belt sushi, rotating sushi|Antonyms: N/A|noun
