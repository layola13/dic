inauguration:/ɪˌnɔːɡjəˈreɪʃən/ |n.| 就职典礼；落成典礼；开幕式|The act of formally beginning a new operation or office, especially that of a president. |The inauguration of the new president was a grand affair. 新总统的就职典礼是一场盛大的活动。|
inauguration:/ɪˌnɔːɡjəˈreɪʃən/ |n.| 开创；开始|The beginning of something new. |The inauguration of the new program was met with enthusiasm. 新项目的启动受到了热烈的欢迎。|
inauguration:/ɪˌnɔːɡjəˈreɪʃən/ |n.| 就职；就任|The formal induction into office. |His inauguration as mayor took place last week. 他上周就任市长。|


近义词: commencement, initiation, opening, launch
反义词: conclusion, termination, ending
词性: noun


词源: From Late Latin *inauguratio, from Latin inaugurare (“to take omens from the flight of birds; to install in office”), from in- + augurare (“to augur, foretell; to consecrate by augury”), from augur (“augur, diviner”), possibly from avis (“bird”) + -gur, related to garrire (“to chatter”).
