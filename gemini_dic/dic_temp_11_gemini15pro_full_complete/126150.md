mutinying:/ˈmjuːtɪnaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 叛变，哗变 | Engaging in mutiny; refusing to obey orders and attempting to take control from authority. | The sailors were mutinying against the cruel captain.  水手们正在反抗残暴的船长。| Synonyms: rebelling, revolting, rising up | Antonyms: obeying, complying, submitting | Verb

mutiny:/ˈmjuːtɪni/ | n. | 叛乱，兵变 | Open rebellion against constituted authority, especially by soldiers or sailors against their officers. | The mutiny was quickly suppressed by the loyal troops.  兵变很快就被忠诚的部队镇压了。| Synonyms: rebellion, revolt, uprising | Antonyms: obedience, compliance, submission | Noun
