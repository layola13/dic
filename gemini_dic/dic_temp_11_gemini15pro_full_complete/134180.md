nonincidents:/ˌnɒnˈɪnsɪdənts/ | n. | 非事件；小事；未遂事件 | An event or situation that is not considered serious or significant enough to be classified as an incident.  It often refers to something that could have been an incident but was prevented or resolved quickly. |  The police reported a few nonincidents during the festival.  警方报告节日里发生了几起小事。| Near misses, uneventful occurrences | incidents, accidents, emergencies | noun
