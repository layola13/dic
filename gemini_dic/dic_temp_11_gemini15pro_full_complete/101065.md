1. The telephone was a groundbreaking invention.  电话是一项突破性的发明。
2. Necessity is the mother of invention. 需要是发明之母。
| Synonyms: discovery, innovation, creation, contrivance | Antonyms: imitation, copy,  reality, truth | noun
