freshet:/ˈfrɛʃɪt/| n.|春汛，涨水；清新之物|A sudden overflow of a stream caused by heavy rain or melting snow.  A rush of fresh water.|The spring freshet flooded the valley. 春汛淹没了山谷。|Synonyms: flood, spate, surge, inundation|Antonyms: drought, dryness|noun
