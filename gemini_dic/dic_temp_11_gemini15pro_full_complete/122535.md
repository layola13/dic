modest:/ˈmɑːdɪst/ (US) /ˈmɒdɪst/ (UK)| adj.|谦虚的，谦逊的；适度的，不太大的；端庄的，朴素的|Not excessive or extreme; moderate. Having or showing a moderate or humble estimate of one's merits, importance, etc.  Unpretentious, not showy.|She wore a modest dress to the church. 她穿着一件朴素的连衣裙去了教堂。|Synonyms: humble, unassuming, unpretentious, moderate, simple, plain|Antonyms: arrogant, boastful, immodest, extravagant, ostentatious|adjective
