guerre:/ɡɛʁ/| n. |战争，战争状态|A state of armed conflict between different nations or states or different groups within a nation or state.|France was at guerre with England. 法国与英国交战。|近义词：war, conflict, battle, hostility|反义词：peace, truce, armistice|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old French *guerre, from Frankish *werra, from Proto-Germanic *werzō ("confusion, turmoil"). Cognate with Old High German werra ("quarrel, confusion, strife, war"). Doublet of werre.
