nontumultuously:/ˌnɒnˌtʌmʌlˈtʃuːəsli/
adverb
平静地；安静地；不起骚动地
Without tumult; peacefully and quietly.
The crowd dispersed nontumultuously after the concert.  音乐会结束后，人群平静地散开了。
The river flowed nontumultuously through the valley.  河流平静地流过山谷。
近义词: calmly, peacefully, quietly, serenely
反义词: tumultuously, boisterously, riotously
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + tumultuously.  Tumultuously comes from tumult (meaning "commotion, uproar") + -ously (adverbial suffix).  Tumult ultimately derives from the Latin word tumultus.
