PHRASE->
Let's face it = 面对现实吧
Let's get this straight = 让我们把话说清楚

Let's get started = 让我们开始吧
Let's see = 让我们看看


注意事项:

"Let's" is always followed by the base form of the verb.

While "let's" is a contraction of "let us," they aren't always interchangeable.  "Let us" can be used in more formal contexts and can also mean "allow us to."  "Let's" is specifically for making suggestions.
