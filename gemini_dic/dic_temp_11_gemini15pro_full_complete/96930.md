inexpugnably:/ˌɪnɪkˈspʌɡnəbli/
adverb
无法攻克地，坚不可摧地
in a way that cannot be captured or defeated
The fortress was inexpugnably defended.  这座堡垒被坚不可摧地防守着。
The castle stood inexpugnably on the hilltop. 这座城堡屹立在山顶，坚不可摧。
近义词: invulnerably, impregnably, unconquerably
反义词: vulnerably, weakly
词性: adverb
