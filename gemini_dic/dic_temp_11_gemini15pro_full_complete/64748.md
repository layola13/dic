escout:/ɪˈskaʊt/ |n. |侦察员，侦察兵；|A person sent out to obtain information, especially a soldier or scout sent out to reconnoitre. |例句：The escout reported back to the general. 侦察员向将军报告了情况。

例句：The team sent an escout ahead to check the terrain. 队伍派了一名侦察兵到前面去查看地形。|近义词：scout, spy, reconnaissance, |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
