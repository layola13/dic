corporealism:/kɔːrˈpɔːriəlɪzəm/
n.
物质主义; 肉体主义; 唯体论
The belief that physical matter is the only reality.  The doctrine that attributes materiality or substantiality to God or spirits.
He rejected corporealism and embraced a more spiritual worldview. 他拒绝了物质主义，转而接受了一种更精神的世界观。
Corporealism emphasizes the physical and tangible aspects of existence.  唯体论强调存在的物质和有形方面。
近义词:materialism, physicalism, somatism
反义词:spiritualism, idealism
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *corporeālis, from Latin corpus (“body”) + -ālis.  The suffix -ism denotes a doctrine, belief, or theory.
