lordosis:/lɔːrˈdoʊsɪs/
noun
脊柱前凸，腰椎前弯
An inward curvature of the spine.  Specifically, an excessive inward curvature of the lumbar spine.
Lordosis is often seen in pregnant women. 怀孕妇女常见脊柱前凸。
The gymnast's lordosis was very pronounced.  体操运动员的腰椎前凸非常明显。
近义词:swayback, hyperlordosis
反义词:kyphosis
词性:noun
