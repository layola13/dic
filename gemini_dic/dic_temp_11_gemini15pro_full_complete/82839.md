gulf:/ɡʌlf/ | n. | 海湾；深渊；分歧 | a large area of sea that is partly surrounded by land; a deep division or difference between people, groups, or things | The Persian Gulf is a major shipping route. 波斯湾是一条主要的航运路线。| bay, inlet, cove | chasm, abyss | noun


gulf:/ɡʌlf/ | vt. | 吞噬；吞没 | (of water) to flow over and cover (something) completely | The rising tide gulfed the small island. 上涨的潮水吞没了小岛。 | engulf, swallow, inundate | expose, reveal | verb
