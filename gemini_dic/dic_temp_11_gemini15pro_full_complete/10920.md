araneoidea:/əˌreɪniˈɔɪdiə/ | n. | 园蛛总科 | A superfamily of spiders, including orb-weavers and other related families.  It encompasses a diverse group of spiders known for their web-spinning abilities. |  The Araneoidea are a large and diverse group, containing many familiar spiders. 园蛛总科是一个庞大而多样化的群体，包含许多我们熟悉的蜘蛛。|  Scientists discovered a new species belonging to the Araneoidea superfamily. 科学家们发现了一个属于园蛛总科的新物种。 | synonyms: orb-weaver spiders (in part),  | antonyms: None | noun
