monarchist:/ˈmɒnəkɪst/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnərkɪst/ (US)| n.|君主主义者；保皇党人|A person who supports the principle of monarchy or believes in having a monarch.|例句：The monarchist faction plotted to restore the king to the throne. 保皇党派密谋使国王复位。

例句：She is a staunch monarchist and believes in the divine right of kings. 她是一个坚定的君主主义者，相信君权神授。


近义词：royalist, conservative (in some contexts)|反义词：republican, anti-monarchist|词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *monarchiste*, from monarque (“monarch”) + -iste (“-ist”).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek μονάρχης (monárkhēs, “sole ruler”).
