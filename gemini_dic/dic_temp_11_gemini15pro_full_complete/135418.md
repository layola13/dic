nonperpetuation:/ˌnɒnpərˌpetʃuˈeɪʃən/
noun
非永存，不延续
The nonperpetuation of harmful stereotypes is a key goal of social justice movements.  消除有害的刻板印象的非延续性是社会正义运动的一个关键目标。
The museum is dedicated to the nonperpetuation of violence.  这家博物馆致力于非暴力延续。
Synonym: cessation, discontinuation, termination
Antonym: perpetuation, continuation,  preservation
