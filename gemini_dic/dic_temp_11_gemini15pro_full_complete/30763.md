accessible:/əkˈsesəbl/ | adj. | 可接近的，可进入的；易理解的；可获得的 |  Easy to reach, enter, or obtain; easy to understand or appreciate. | The museum is accessible to wheelchair users.  博物馆方便轮椅使用者进入。| The information is readily accessible online.  这些信息很容易在网上找到。| synonyms: reachable, attainable, available, comprehensible | antonyms: inaccessible, unattainable, unavailable, incomprehensible | word type: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin accessibilis (“approachable”), from Latin accedere (“to approach”) + -ibilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning “capable of, worthy of, tending to”).  Latin accedere is from ad- (“to, towards”) + cedere (“to go, yield”).
