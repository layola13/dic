multiples:/ˈmʌltɪplz/| n.|倍数；多份|A quantity containing something a specified number of times.|The store ordered multiples of the popular toy to keep up with demand.  这家商店订购了多份这款受欢迎的玩具，以满足需求。|synonyms: products, factors|antonyms: divisors, fractions|noun


multiples:/ˈmʌltɪplz/| adj.|多重的；多种的|Having or involving many parts, elements, or features.|The company faced multiples challenges during the economic downturn.  这家公司在经济低迷时期面临多重挑战。|synonyms: numerous, manifold|antonyms: single, singular|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English multiple, from Old French multiple, from Latin multiplus (“manifold, containing many times over”), from multus (“much, many”) + -plus (“fold”).
