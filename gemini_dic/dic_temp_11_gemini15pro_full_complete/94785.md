impugn:/ɪmˈpjuːn/ | vt. | 指责，质疑，抨击 | To challenge the truth, validity, or honesty of (a statement or motive); call into question. |  The politician's integrity was impugned by his opponent.  这位政治家的正直受到了对手的质疑。

|  She impugned his motives for donating to the charity. 她质疑他向慈善机构捐款的动机。 |  challenge, question, dispute, attack, criticize | defend, support, uphold, endorse | verb
