footies:/ˈfʊtiz/| n.|婴儿连体衣; 脚套; (尤指婴儿或儿童穿的)连袜裤|One-piece garments for babies or young children that cover the feet, often made of soft, warm material like fleece.  They are sometimes called sleepsuits or sleepers.  They can also refer to socks or slippers designed for babies or toddlers. |The baby looks cozy in his footies.|近义词：sleepers, pajamas, onesies|反义词:(None - No direct opposite for a garment type)|Noun
