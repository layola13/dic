holdings:/ˈhoʊldɪŋz/| n.|持有物，持股，财产|Something owned, especially land, shares, or other investments.|例句：The company's holdings include real estate and stocks. 这家公司的资产包括房地产和股票。|例句：His holdings in the company have increased significantly. 他在公司的持股大幅增加。|近义词：assets, possessions, investments|反义词:liabilities, debts|noun

holdings:/ˈhoʊldɪŋz/| n.|（通常指土地）保有量，保有地，（尤指农场或庄园）所有地|Land, especially a farm or estate, held by an owner or tenant.|例句：The family's holdings extended for miles. 这个家族的土地绵延数英里。|例句：The farmer worked tirelessly on his small holdings. 这位农民不知疲倦地在他的小块土地上劳作。|近义词：property, estate, land|反义词: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "to hold,"  from Old English *healdan "to hold, keep, guard, retain,"  of Germanic origin, related to Dutch houden and German halten.  The noun form developed to refer to things held or possessed.
