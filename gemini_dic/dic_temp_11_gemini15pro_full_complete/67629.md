extremis:/ɪn ɛksˈtreɪmɪs/ /ɪn ɛkˈstriːmɪs/| adverb, noun (treated as singular)|在紧急关头，在危急时刻|in a situation of extreme danger or difficulty, especially when death is likely|例句：He confessed when he found himself in extremis. 当他发现自己处于危急关头时，他坦白了。|近义词：dire straits, crisis, emergency, peril|反义词:safety, security|词性:adverb, noun

Etymology: From Latin *in extremis* "at the point of death," literally "in the outermost things."
