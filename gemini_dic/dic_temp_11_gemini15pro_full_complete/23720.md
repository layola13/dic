brassiness:/ˈbræsɪnɪs/
n.
铜色；黄铜色；粗俗；厚脸皮
the quality of being like brass in colour or sound; loud, vulgar, and insensitive behaviour
Her brassiness and confidence captivated the audience. 她的厚脸皮和自信吸引了观众。
近义词：vulgarity, boldness, gaudiness, flashiness
反义词：subtlety, refinement, modesty, humility
词性：noun
