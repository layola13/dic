ademption:/əˈdɛmpʃən/ | n. | 撤销遗赠；失效；取消 | The extinction or withdrawal of a legacy, by an act of the testator equivalent to revocation, as by disposal of the subject of the bequest, without express revocation of the bequest itself. |  The sale of the house constituted an ademption of the bequest in the will.  这栋房子的出售构成了对遗嘱中遗赠的撤销。 | synonyms: revocation, cancellation, annulment, withdrawal, rescission | antonyms: bequest, inheritance | noun
