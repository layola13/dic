humanitarian:/hjuːˌmænɪˈtɛəriən/ | adj. n. |人道主义的；人道主义者 |Concerned with or seeking to promote human welfare. |例句：The organization provides humanitarian aid to refugees.  这个组织向难民提供人道主义援助。|例句：He was a true humanitarian, always helping those in need. 他是一位真正的人道主义者，总是帮助那些需要帮助的人。|近义词：philanthropic, charitable, benevolent |反义词：inhumane, cruel, merciless |词性: 形容词, 名词
Etymology: Late 18th century: from humanity + -arian.
