cressy:/ˈkrɛsi/| adj.|水芹味的；生长着水芹的；像水芹的|Having a pungent, peppery taste like watercress; abounding with watercress; resembling watercress.|The air was fresh and cressy. 空气清新，带着水芹的香味。|Synonyms: peppery, pungent, tangy|Antonyms: bland, mild|Adjective
