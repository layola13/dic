apyrous:/ˈæpɪrəs/
adjective
耐火的; 不燃的
Resistant to fire; incombustible.
例句：Apyrous materials are used in the construction of furnaces. 耐火材料用于建造熔炉。

例句：The apyrous qualities of asbestos made it a popular building material in the past. 石棉的耐火特性使其在过去成为一种流行的建筑材料。

近义词：fireproof, fire-resistant, nonflammable, incombustible
反义词：flammable, combustible, inflammable
词性：adjective

词源：From Greek *ἄπυρος* (apyros), from *ἀ-* (a-, *not*) + *πῦρ* (pyr, *fire*).
