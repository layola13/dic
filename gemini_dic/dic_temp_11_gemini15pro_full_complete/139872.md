oculist: /ˈɒkjəlɪst/ (UK) /ˈɑːkjəlɪst/ (US)| n. |眼科医生|A doctor who specializes in the treatment of diseases and disorders of the eye.| Example:  The oculist prescribed new glasses for her.  她去看眼科医生，医生给她开了新眼镜的处方。

Example: My grandmother sees an oculist regularly to check her glaucoma. 我祖母定期去看眼科医生检查青光眼。

反义词: None
