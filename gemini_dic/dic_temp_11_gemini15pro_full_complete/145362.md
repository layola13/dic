overburns:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrnz/
verb
过度燃烧; 烧过头
to burn something too much
The toast overburns easily in this toaster.  这台烤面包机很容易把面包烤焦。

近义词: scorch, char
反义词: undercook

词性: verb
