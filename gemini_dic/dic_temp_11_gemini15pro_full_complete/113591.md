macedoine:/ˌmæsɪˈdwɑːn/ | n. | 马其顿沙拉，水果什锦，蔬菜丁 | A mixture of diced fruits or vegetables, often in a sweet sauce or dressing.  Originally referring to a mixture of different peoples or things, alluding to the diverse population of ancient Macedonia. | Example 1: For dessert, we had a refreshing macedoine of summer fruits.  餐后甜点，我们享用了一份清爽的夏季水果什锦。

Example 2: The chef prepared a colorful macedoine of vegetables as a garnish.  厨师准备了一份色彩缤纷的蔬菜丁作为装饰。| 近义词：medley, mixture, assortment, potpourri | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) single ingredient, plain | 词性: noun
