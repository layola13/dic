affreight:/əˈfreɪt/
verb
租船；包租（船舶或其一部分）
to hire (a ship or part of a ship) for the carriage of goods
The company affreighted a vessel to transport the cargo.  这家公司租了一艘船来运输货物。

affreight:/əˈfreɪt/
verb
运送（货物）；为…支付运费
to transport (goods) by ship; pay the freight for
They affreighted the goods to Europe. 他们将货物运往欧洲。

近义词: charter, hire, lease
反义词: None

词性: verb
