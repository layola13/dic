antifreezes:/ˈæntifriːzɪz/
noun
防冻剂（复数）
A liquid, typically one based on ethylene glycol, added to the water in a vehicle's radiator to prevent it from freezing.
I bought some antifreezes for my car before winter. 我在冬天来临前为我的车买了一些防冻剂。
The garage stocks a range of antifreezes suitable for all vehicles.  这家修车厂备有各种适合所有车辆的防冻剂。
synonyms: coolant,  cryoprotectant
antonyms: pro-freeze (not a commonly used term)
