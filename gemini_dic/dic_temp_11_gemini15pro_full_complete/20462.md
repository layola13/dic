bitterweed:/ˈbɪtərˌwiːd/| n.|苦苣菜，苦味杂草|A plant, typically of the genus *Helenium*, having a bitter taste and often considered a weed.  It can be toxic to livestock.|The cows avoided the bitterweed in the pasture.|近义词：ragwort, sneezeweed, yellow star thistle|反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant, could use beneficial plant, forage)|noun
