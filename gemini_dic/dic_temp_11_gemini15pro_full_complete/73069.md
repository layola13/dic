footracing:/ˈfʊtreɪsɪŋ/
n.
赛跑，竞走
The act or sport of racing on foot.
He enjoys footracing and participates in marathons regularly. 他喜欢跑步，经常参加马拉松比赛。
近义词：running, sprinting, racing
反义词：walking, strolling
