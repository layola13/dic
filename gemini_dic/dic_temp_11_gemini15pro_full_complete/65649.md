evaporated:/ɪˈvæpəreɪtɪd/
verb
蒸发的；消失的
converted into vapor; disappeared; vanished.
The water evaporated quickly in the hot sun.  太阳底下水蒸发得很快。

近义词:vanished, dissipated, disappeared
反义词:condensed, solidified, materialized
词性:verb
