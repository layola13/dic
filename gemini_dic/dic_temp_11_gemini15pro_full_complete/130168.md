non-revealing:/ˌnɒn rɪˈviːlɪŋ/ |adj.|不透露信息的，不泄露的|Not revealing private or sensitive information; discreet.|She wore a non-revealing dress to the business meeting. 她穿了一件不暴露的连衣裙去参加商务会议。|The magician's tricks were non-revealing of his methods. 魔术师的戏法并没有透露他的方法。|近义词: discreet, concealing, uninformative, guarded|反义词: revealing, transparent, explicit|词性: 形容词


Etymology:
"non-" (prefix) from Latin *nōn* meaning "not"
"revealing" from the verb "reveal" which comes from Old French *reveler* meaning "to unveil, disclose." Ultimately from Latin *revelare* meaning "to unveil, reveal."
