currying: /ˈkɜːriɪŋ/ | n. | 函数柯里化 |  A technique in functional programming where a function that takes multiple arguments is transformed into a sequence of functions, each taking a single argument.  |  Currying allows for partial application of functions. 柯里化允许函数的部分应用。 |  partial application, closure | N/A | noun
