cremates:/krɪˈmeɪts/
verb
火化，焚烧（尸体）
reduces (a body) to ashes by burning.
The local crematorium cremates several bodies a day. 当地的火葬场每天火化几具尸体。
cremates:/krɪˈmeɪts/
v.
火化
burn (a corpse) to ashes
They cremated his body according to his wishes. 他们按照他的遗愿火化了他的遗体。
cremates:/krɪˈmeɪts/
近义词: incinerates, burns
反义词: buries, inters
