joey:/ˈdʒoʊi/| n. |幼袋鼠; 小动物(尤指袋鼠);  孩子，年轻人|A young kangaroo.  A baby animal, especially a kangaroo.  A child or young person (informal).|The joey stayed close to its mother's pouch. 幼袋鼠紧紧地待在妈妈的育儿袋里。|近义词：kangaroo, baby, kid, youngster|反义词:adult, grown-up|词性:noun

词源:  Shortened form of "Joseph", then applied to young kangaroos and other marsupials, perhaps because they are seen as clinging to their mothers like a child to a parent.
