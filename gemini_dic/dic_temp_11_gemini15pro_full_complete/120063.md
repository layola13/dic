millwright:/ˈmɪlˌrɪt/| n.| 磨坊工；机械修理工；安装工|A skilled worker who installs, maintains, and repairs machinery, especially in a factory or mill. |The millwright quickly diagnosed the problem with the conveyor belt. 磨坊工很快诊断出传送带的问题。

The millwright is responsible for keeping all the machinery in the factory running smoothly.  机械修理工负责保持工厂里所有机器的平稳运行。|Synonym: mechanic, machinist, technician, engineer|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "saboteur" in a negative context)|noun
