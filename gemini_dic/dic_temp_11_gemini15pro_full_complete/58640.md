dumbfounded:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪd/ | adj. | 惊呆的，目瞪口呆的 | So amazed or astonished that one is unable to speak. |  The news left her completely dumbfounded.  这个消息让她完全惊呆了。 | astonished, speechless, stunned, bewildered | unsurprised, unfazed | adjective

dumbfounded:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪd/ | adj. | 惊愕的，说不出话的 |  So surprised and confused that you cannot speak. | He was dumbfounded by the accusation. 他被指控惊得说不出话来。 | shocked, amazed, astounded |  calm, composed | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From dumb (unable to speak) + confound (to confuse and surprise).  The original meaning was "made speechless by confusion".
