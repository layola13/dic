Having or showing intellect; intellectual.  Possessing or characterized by high intellect.
例句：He was a highly intellected man, well-versed in philosophy and literature. 他是一个知识渊博的人，精通哲学和文学。

例句：The intellected conversation stimulated everyone present.  这场充满智慧的对话让所有在场的人都感到兴奋。
近义词：intelligent, intellectual, brainy, smart, bright
反义词：unintelligent, stupid, foolish, simpleminded
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *intellectus*, past participle of intellegere ("to understand"), from inter ("between") + legere ("to choose, gather, read").  The "-ed" suffix transforms the noun "intellect" into an adjective.
