hurly-burly:/ˈhɜːli ˈbɜːli/ /ˈhɝːli ˈbɝːli/
n.
喧闹; 骚乱; 混乱;
A state of commotion or confusion.
The marketplace was a scene of hurly-burly. 市场上一片混乱。

近义词:commotion, tumult, uproar, chaos, pandemonium
反义词:peace, quiet, tranquility, order, calm
词性:名词
