ghostlily:/ˈɡoʊstˌlɪli/| n. |鬼百合，幽靈百合|A type of lily, usually white or pale in color, often associated with ghosts, death, or mourning due to its appearance and symbolism.  It may refer to several different species depending on the region, including some species of *Hymenocallis* (spider lilies).|The ghostlily bloomed eerily in the moonlight. 鬼百合在月光下诡异地盛开。|The old graveyard was dotted with ghostlilies.  古老的墓地里点缀着鬼百合。|近义词：spider lily, death lily, resurrection lily|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
