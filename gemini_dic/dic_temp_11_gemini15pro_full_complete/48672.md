deference:/ˈdefərəns/ /ˈdɛfərəns/| n. |尊重，顺从，敬重|respectful submission or yielding to the judgment, opinion, will, etc., of another.|He showed deference to his elders. 他对长辈表示尊重。|近义词：respect, reverence, courtesy, submission, compliance|反义词：disrespect, defiance, disobedience|noun

deferences:/ˈdefərənsɪz/ /ˈdɛfərənsɪz/| n. |尊重，顺从，敬重（deference的复数形式，较少使用）|respectful submissions or yieldings to the judgment, opinion, will, etc., of another.|He showed many deferences to his elders throughout his life. 他一生中多次对长辈表示尊重。（这个例句略有不自然，因为deferences的复数形式在实际应用中比较少见）|近义词：respects, reverences, courtesies, submissions, compliances|反义词：disrespects, defiances, disobediences|noun
