malwares:/ˈmælweərz/ | n. |恶意软件| Software programs designed to damage or do other unwanted actions on a computer system. |  My computer is running very slowly, I think it might be infected with malwares. 我的电脑运行非常慢，我想它可能感染了恶意软件。|
malwares:/ˈmælweərz/ | n. |恶意软件| Malwares can steal your personal information, corrupt your files, and even control your computer remotely. 恶意软件可以窃取您的个人信息，损坏您的文件，甚至可以远程控制您的计算机。|
malwares:/ˈmælweərz/ | n. |恶意软件|近义词：viruses, spyware, adware, ransomware|反义词：antivirus, security software|词性: 名词
