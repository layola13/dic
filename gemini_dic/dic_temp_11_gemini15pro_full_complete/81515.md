gravidness:/ˈɡrævɪdnəs/| n. |怀孕，妊娠|The state of being pregnant; pregnancy.  The gravidness of the queen bee was evident in her swollen abdomen.|例句：The mare's gravidness was obvious in her enlarged belly. 母马的怀孕从她鼓起的肚子就能看出来。

例句：Her gravidness made her more susceptible to fatigue. 她的怀孕使她更容易感到疲劳。|近义词：pregnancy, gestation|反义词:non-pregnancy, barrenness|词性:noun
词源:From Late Latin *graviditas, from Latin gravidus (“pregnant, heavy, full”), from gravis (“heavy”).
