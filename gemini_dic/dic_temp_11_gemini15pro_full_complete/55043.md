disquietous:/dɪsˈkwaɪətəs/| adj.|令人不安的，焦虑的|Causing anxiety or uneasiness; disturbing.|The disquietous news of the impending storm made everyone nervous. 即将到来的暴风雨的令人不安的消息让每个人都感到紧张。|近义词：unsettling, disturbing, troubling, worrisome, alarming|反义词：calming, soothing, reassuring, peaceful, tranquil|词性:形容词
词源: From disquiet + -ous.  Disquiet comes from the Old French *desquiete, from des- (expressing negation) + quiet (quiet).
