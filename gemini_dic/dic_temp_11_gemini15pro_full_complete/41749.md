corrugation:/ˌkɔːrəˈɡeɪʃn/ | n. | 波纹；褶皱；起皱 |  The process of forming wrinkles or grooves in a material, or the resulting wrinkles or grooves themselves.  | The corrugation of the cardboard provides extra strength.  纸板的波纹提供了额外的强度。 | synonyms: furrow, wrinkle, groove, ridge, crimp | antonyms: smoothness, flatness | noun


corrugated:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 波纹状的；有褶皱的 |  Shaped into alternating ridges and grooves. |  Corrugated iron is commonly used for roofing. 波纹铁皮通常用于屋顶。 | synonyms: wrinkled, grooved, furrowed, ridged | antonyms: smooth, flat | adjective


Etymology:
From Latin "corrugatus," past participle of "corrugare" (to wrinkle), from "com-" (together) + "ruga" (wrinkle).
