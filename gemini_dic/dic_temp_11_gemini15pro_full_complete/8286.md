anti-locking:/ˈæntiˌlɒkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈæntiˌlɑːkɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|防抱死的，防锁死的|Preventing something from locking, especially the wheels of a vehicle during braking.|The car is equipped with anti-locking brakes. 这辆车配备了防抱死刹车系统。|Synonyms: non-locking, anti-skid|Antonyms: locking|adjective


Etymology->

From anti- + locking, the present participle of lock.
