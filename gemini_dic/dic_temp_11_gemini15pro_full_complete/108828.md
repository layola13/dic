leavenous:/ˈlɛvənəs/| adj.|发酵的；有酵的；使人不安分的；使人兴奋的|Pertaining to or resembling leaven; containing leaven; causing fermentation; tending to excite or agitate. |The leavenous bread rose beautifully.  发酵的面包长得很好。 The leavenous atmosphere in the room was palpable. 房间里不安的气氛很明显。|近义词：fermentative, yeasty, exciting, agitating|反义词：unleavened, calming, soothing|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From Middle English *levenous, from Old French *levineus, from Late Latin levāmineus (“of or pertaining to leaven”), from Latin levāmen (“leaven, yeast”), from levō (“to lift, raise”).
