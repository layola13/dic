floppy:/ˈflɒpi/ (UK) /ˈflɑːpi/ (US)| adj. n.|松软的；软盘|Something that is floppy is soft and hangs loosely rather than being stiff.  A floppy disk is a small flat disk enclosed in a plastic case, used for storing computer data. |The dog has floppy ears. 这只狗的耳朵耷拉着。|近义词：limp, flexible, droopy|反义词：stiff, rigid|形容词
