banquette:/bæŋˈkɛt/ | n. |长椅，宴会 |A long upholstered bench, often found in restaurants or along a wall.  It can also refer to a feast or banquet.|  The banquette in the corner offered a cozy spot to enjoy a meal.  角落里的长椅提供了一个舒适的用餐地点。| synonym: bench, seat, sofa | antonym: chair (arguably - banquette implies a continuous seating arrangement unlike individual chairs)| noun

banquette:/bæŋˈkɛt/ | n. |宴会 |A feast or banquet, although this usage is less common in modern English.|The king held a lavish banquette for his guests. 国王为他的客人们举办了盛大的宴会。 |synonym: feast, banquet, dinner | antonym: snack, fast | noun
