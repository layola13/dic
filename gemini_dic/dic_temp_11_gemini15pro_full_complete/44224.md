crocketed:/ˈkrɒkɪtɪd/ /ˈkrɑːkɪtɪd/| adj.|钩状的，有钩饰的，球状的|Decorated with crockets, small, hook-like ornaments, often found on Gothic architecture. Also, shaped like a croquet ball. |The gable ends were crocketed and topped with finials. 山墙的末端饰有钩饰，顶部有顶尖饰。

The crocketed spires of the cathedral reached towards the sky.  教堂钩状的尖顶伸向天空。|近义词：ornamented, decorated, adorned|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性:形容词
