angelot:/ˈeɪndʒəlɒt/ | n. |小天使，一种古老的弦乐器|A small lute or rebec, popular in the 16th and 17th centuries.  It was similar to a violin but held upright and played with a bow. |例句：The angelot produced a delicate, sweet sound, often used in consort music.  小天使发出一种精致甜美的声音，常用于合奏音乐。


例句：He played a mournful tune on his angelot. 他用他的小天使演奏了一首悲伤的曲子。 |近义词：rebec, lute, viol |反义词: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) |词性:noun
