mindlessnesses:/ˈmaɪndləsnɪsɪz/
n.
心不在焉，无意识，不留心（的状态）
The state of being mindless; lack of attention or awareness.
He ate the entire bag of chips in a fit of mindlessness. 他心不在焉地吃完了整袋薯片。
近义词：inattention, absent-mindedness, thoughtlessness, heedlessness
反义词：mindfulness, awareness, attention, alertness
词性：名词（复数）

Etymology:
From mindless + -ness.  Mindless itself comes from mind + -less.  The suffix "-less" means "without."  The suffix "-ness" turns an adjective into a noun, indicating a state or quality.
