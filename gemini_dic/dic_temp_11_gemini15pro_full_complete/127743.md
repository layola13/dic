negotiations:/nɪˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃənz/ /nɪˌɡəʊʃiˈeɪʃənz/| n. |谈判，协商|A discussion aimed at reaching an agreement.|The negotiations between the two countries lasted for months.  两国之间的谈判持续了数月。|synonyms: talks, discussions, deliberations|antonyms: disputes, arguments|noun

negotiation:/nɪˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃən/ /nɪˌɡəʊʃiˈeɪʃən/| n. |谈判，协商|The process of discussing something with someone in order to reach an agreement.|The negotiation of the treaty took several years.  该条约的谈判花了数年时间。|synonyms: bargaining, haggling, dealing|antonyms: dictation, imposition|noun

negotiate:/nɪˈɡoʊʃiˌeɪt/ /nɪˈɡəʊʃiˌeɪt/| v. |谈判，协商|To try to reach an agreement or compromise by discussion with others.|They negotiated a peace treaty. 他们谈判达成了一项和平条约。|synonyms: bargain, haggle, deal|antonyms: dictate, impose|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *negociacion, from Late Latin negōtiātiō (“a doing business, trade”), from Latin negōtior (“I do business”), from negōtium (“business”), from nec (“not”) + ōtium (“leisure”).
