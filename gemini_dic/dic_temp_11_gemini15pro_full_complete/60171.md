effervescence:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsəns/ | n. | 兴奋，活跃，冒泡 | The state of being bubbly or fizzy; vivacity and enthusiasm. | The effervescence of the crowd was contagious.  人群的兴奋情绪很有感染力。| synonyms: fizz, sparkle, bubbliness, vivacity, ebullience | antonyms: flatness, dullness, apathy, lethargy | noun


effervescent:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsənt/ | adj. | 兴奋的，活跃的，冒泡的 | Vivacious and enthusiastic; giving off bubbles. | Her effervescent personality brightened the room. 她活泼的性格照亮了整个房间。| synonyms: bubbly, sparkling, fizzy, lively, enthusiastic | antonyms: flat, dull, apathetic, lethargic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *effervescentia, from Latin effervescere "to boil up, ferment," from ex- "out" + fervescere "to begin to boil, grow hot," from fervere "to boil, glow."
