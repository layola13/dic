depositor:/dɪˈpɒzɪtər/ (US) /dɪˈpɒzɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |存款人；储户|A person who puts money into a bank account.|Example: The bank sent a statement to each depositor.  银行向每位储户发送了一份对账单。|Example:  As a depositor, you have the right to withdraw your funds. 作为储户，您有权提取您的资金。|Synonym:  saver, investor, contributor|Antonym: borrower,  withdrawer|noun
