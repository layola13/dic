boxed-out:/ˈbɑːkst aʊt/| adj.| 被卡位的，被挡出的，被挤出的 | Prevented from getting something (like a rebound in basketball) because someone else is in a better position. | The smaller player was completely boxed out and couldn't get the rebound.  那个较矮的球员完全被卡住了，抢不到篮板球。| 近义词: shut out, excluded, blocked | 反义词:  unopposed, free |词性: 形容词

boxed-out:/ˈbɑːkst aʊt/| adj.| 处于不利地位的，被边缘化的 |  Put in a disadvantageous position; marginalized. |  He felt boxed out of the promotion process because he hadn't gone to the right schools. 他觉得自己在晋升过程中处于不利地位，因为他没有上过合适的学校。 | 近义词: sidelined, marginalized, disadvantaged | 反义词:  advantaged, favored, privileged |词性: 形容词
