biodegrades:/ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdz/| v.|生物降解|Breaks down or decomposes naturally as a result of the action of living organisms, typically microorganisms.| Example: The new packaging biodegrades quickly, reducing landfill waste.  这种新包装能快速生物降解，减少垃圾填埋场的浪费。|Synonym: decomposes, rots, decays|Antonym: persists, remains, endures|Verb

biodegradable:/ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdəbl/| adj.|可生物降解的|Capable of being decomposed by bacteria or other living organisms.|Example:  Biodegradable plastics are becoming increasingly popular. 可生物降解的塑料正变得越来越流行。|Synonym: compostable, decomposable|Antonym: non-biodegradable, persistent|Adjective
