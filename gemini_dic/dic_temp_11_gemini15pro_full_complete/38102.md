compounded:/kəmˈpaʊndɪd/ | adj. | 复合的；加重的；混合的 | Made worse by the combination of two or more problems or factors; formed of two or more parts/elements. |  The problem was compounded by the bad weather. 这个问题因恶劣天气而更加严重。 |  aggravated, worsened, intensified |  alleviated, mitigated | adjective

compounded:/kəmˈpaʊndɪd/ | v. | 混合；加重；恶化 | To make something bad worse by adding to it. To calculate and add interest to a sum of money. To form a word from two or more words. |  His financial problems were compounded by his gambling habit. 他的赌博习惯加剧了他的经济问题。 | worsen, exacerbate, aggravate | improve, alleviate | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *componere* "to put together," from *com-* "together" + *ponere* "to put, place."
