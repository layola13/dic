barbotine:/ˌbɑːrˈbəʊtiːn/ | n. | 泥浆,  化妆土 | A thin slip of clay used for decorating pottery or for joining pieces together. |  The artist used barbotine to create a raised design on the vase. 艺术家用泥浆在花瓶上创作了一个凸起的图案。 | Synonyms: slip, liquid clay | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "dry clay" in a practical sense) | noun

barbotine:/ˌbɑːrˈbəʊtiːn/ | n. | (尤指用于烹饪的)鱼, 泥鳅 | A type of eel, especially one used for cooking. | The chef prepared a delicious dish of barbotine.  厨师准备了一道美味的泥鳅菜肴。 | Synonyms: eel, loach | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
