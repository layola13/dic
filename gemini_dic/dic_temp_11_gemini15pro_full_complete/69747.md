ferengi:/fəˈrɛŋɡi/
n.
Ferengi, a fictional extraterrestrial race
Ferengi are a humanoid species from the Alpha Quadrant in the Star Trek universe, known for their shrewd business practices and love of profit.
Example: The Ferengi attempted to trade for the technology, but their offer was rejected.  费伦吉人试图用这项技术进行交易，但他们的提议被拒绝了。

Example:  Quark, a Ferengi, ran a bar on Deep Space Nine.  夸克，一个费伦吉人，在深空九号上经营一家酒吧。
近义词:  profiteer, merchant (in some contexts)
反义词:  philanthropist (in some contexts)
词性: noun
