orthology:/ɔːrˈθɒlədʒi/ (US) /ɔːˈθɒlədʒi/ (UK)
n.|直系同源|The study of orthologous genes provides insights into evolutionary relationships. |Orthology refers to the relationship between genes that diverged after a speciation event.|The researchers studied the orthology of the two genes to determine their common ancestor.
近义词: homology (a broader term)
反义词: paralogy
词性: noun
