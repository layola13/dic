foreadvise:/fɔːrədˈvaɪz/
verb
预先通知，事先告知
To inform or notify in advance.
The shipping company foreadvised the customer of the arrival date.  货运公司提前通知了客户到达日期。
We foreadvised the hotel of our late arrival. 我们提前通知了酒店我们晚到。
近义词: prewarn, notify, inform
反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用 withhold information - 隐瞒信息)
词性: verb (动词)

词源: fore- (prefix meaning "before") + advise (verb meaning "to give counsel to; to offer an opinion or suggestion as worthy or expedient to be followed")
