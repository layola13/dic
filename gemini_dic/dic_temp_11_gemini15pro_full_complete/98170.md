inscribes:/ɪnˈskraɪbz/
verb
刻，题写，铭记
To write, carve, or engrave (words or symbols) on something, especially as a formal or permanent record.  Also, to draw (a figure) within another so that their boundaries touch but do not intersect.
The jeweler inscribed the ring with their initials. 珠宝商在戒指上刻上了他们的姓名首字母。

The architect inscribed the circle within a square. 建筑师将圆画在正方形内接。

engrave, carve, etch, imprint
erase, delete, obliterate
