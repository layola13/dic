ashanti: /əˈʃænti/ | n. adj.| 阿散蒂（人）；阿散蒂人的；阿散蒂语| A member of a people living mainly in Ghana and speaking the Twi language; the language of the Ashanti people; relating to the Ashanti people or their language. |  Example:  The Ashanti people are known for their rich culture. 阿散蒂人以其丰富的文化而闻名。

Example:  He bought an Ashanti stool at the market. 他在市场上买了一个阿散蒂凳子。| 近义词: Akan (more general term) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun, adjective


Etymology: From the Twi name for themselves.
