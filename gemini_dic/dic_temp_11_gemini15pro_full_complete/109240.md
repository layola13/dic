leisurely:/ˈliːʒərli/ /ˈleʒərli/| adj. adv.|悠闲的，从容的；悠闲地，从容地|Acting or done without hurry; unhurried.|She strolled leisurely through the park. 她悠闲地在公园里漫步。|近义词：relaxed, unhurried, slow, easygoing|反义词：hurried, rushed, hasty|词性: 形容词/副词

词源: From leisure + -ly.  "Leisure" comes from Old French "leisir" meaning "permission, opportunity," ultimately from Latin "licēre" meaning "to be permitted."
