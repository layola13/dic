administer:/ədˈmɪnɪstər/
verb
管理，执行，给予
to manage or direct; to give or apply (something, such as a medicine or an oath)
The nurse administered the medication to the patient.  护士给病人服了药。
manage, execute, dispense
neglect, withhold
v.
