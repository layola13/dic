compassionate:/kəmˈpæʃənət/ | adj. | 富有同情心的，表示怜悯的 | Having or showing sympathy and concern for others. | She was a compassionate nurse who always put her patients first. 她是一位富有同情心的护士，总是把病人放在第一位。|  He felt compassionate towards the homeless man. 他对这个无家可归的人感到同情。 | 近义词：sympathetic, empathetic, kind, caring | 反义词：cruel, callous, indifferent, uncaring | 词性: adjective | 词源: Late Middle English: from Old French *compassionat, from compassion
