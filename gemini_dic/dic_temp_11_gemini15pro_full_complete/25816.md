burgher:/ˈbɜːrɡər/ (US), /ˈbɜːɡə/ (UK)| n.|市民，公民；中产阶级|A citizen of a town or city, typically a member of the middle class.|例句：The burghers of the town voted for a new mayor.  该镇的市民投票选举了一位新市长。

例句：Many burghers felt the new tax was unfair. 许多市民觉得新税收不公平。|近义词：citizen, townsman, bourgeois|反义词: peasant, aristocrat|noun
