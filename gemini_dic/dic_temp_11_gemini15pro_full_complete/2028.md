现实性；实际活动；事实
The state of being actual; activity; actuality.
His actuosity made him a valuable asset to the team.  他的务实性使他成为团队的宝贵资产。
The actuosity of the situation demanded immediate action.  情况的紧迫性需要立即采取行动。

词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *actuositas, from Latin actuosus (“active, energetic”), from actus (“act, action”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of, prone to").
