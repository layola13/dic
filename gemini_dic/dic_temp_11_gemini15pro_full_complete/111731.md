locule:/ˈlɑːkjuːl/ /ˈlɒkjuːl/| n. |小室，房室，腔室|A small cavity or compartment within a larger structure, especially in a plant ovary or an anther containing pollen.|The locules of the ovary contain the ovules. 子房的小室包含胚珠。|近义词：cavity, chamber, compartment|反义词:|noun
