mansa:/ˈmænsə/| n.| 曼萨（西非古代帝国统治者的称号）|A title of a ruler of an ancient empire in West Africa, especially in the Mali Empire.| Mansa Musa was the most famous mansa of the Mali Empire. 曼萨·穆萨是马里帝国最著名的曼萨。| king, emperor, sovereign, ruler | subject, commoner | noun

Etymology:
From Mandinka *mansa (“king, emperor”), ultimately from Proto-Mande-Vie *mansa (“master, king, chief”).
