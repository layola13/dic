conspirator:/kənˈspɪrətər/| n. |阴谋者，共谋者，同谋者|A person who takes part in a conspiracy; someone who plots with others, especially against the law or against a person in authority.|The conspirators were arrested before they could carry out their plan. 阴谋者在实施计划之前就被逮捕了。|Synonyms: plotter, schemer, intriguer, collaborator|Antonyms: loyalist, opponent, adversary|noun

conspirator:/kənˈspɪrətər/| n. |阴谋家，共犯，密谋者|Someone who is involved in a secret plan to do something harmful or illegal.|The conspirators met in secret to discuss their plan to overthrow the government.  阴谋家们秘密会面，讨论他们推翻政府的计划。|Synonyms:  ringleader,  abettor,  accomplice|Antonyms:  informant, whistleblower|noun
