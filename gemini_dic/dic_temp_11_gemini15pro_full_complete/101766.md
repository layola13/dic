irretentive:/ˌɪrɪˈtɛntɪv/
adjective
健忘的，记忆力差的
not able to retain or remember things; forgetful
Her irretentive mind made it difficult for her to learn new vocabulary. 她健忘的头脑使她很难学习新词汇。
He was so irretentive that he often forgot where he had parked his car. 他太健忘了，经常忘记把车停在哪里了。
近义词:forgetful, unretentive, oblivious
反义词:retentive, mindful
