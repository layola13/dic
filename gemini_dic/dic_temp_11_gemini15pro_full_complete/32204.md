chicana:/tʃɪˈkɑːnə/ | n. |奇卡纳（墨西哥裔美国女性）|A woman of Mexican descent living in the United States, especially one who is politically or culturally active.  Often used to assert ethnic pride and identity.|例句：The chicana artist painted a mural celebrating her heritage.  这位奇卡纳艺术家画了一幅壁画来庆祝她的传统。

例句：The conference focused on the experiences of chicanas in higher education.  这次会议的重点是奇卡纳在高等教育中的经历。|近义词：Latina, Hispanic, Mexican American|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it refers to a specific cultural identity) |词性:noun
