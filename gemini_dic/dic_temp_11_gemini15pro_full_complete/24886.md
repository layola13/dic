brucite:/ˈbruːsaɪt/| n.|水镁石|A white, pearly mineral consisting of magnesium hydroxide, Mg(OH)₂. It is formed by alteration of periclase and other magnesium minerals.|Brucite is often found in serpentine deposits. 水镁石经常在蛇纹岩矿床中发现。|Brucite occurs as tabular crystals or foliated masses. 水镁石以板状晶体或叶状块体形式出现。|Synonyms: magnesium hydroxide, nemalite (fibrous variety)|Antonyms: None|noun
