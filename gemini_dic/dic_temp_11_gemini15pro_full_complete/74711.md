freemen:/ˈfriːmən/| n. |自由民；公民；享有特权的人|A person who is not a slave or serf.  A full citizen of a city or borough.|The freemen of the city elected the mayor.  这座城市的自由民选举了市长。|Synonyms: citizen, burgess, commoner|Antonyms: slave, serf|noun
freemans: /ˈfriːmənz/ | n. | 自由民；公民；享有特权的人（freeman的复数形式） |  The plural form of "freeman," referring to multiple individuals who are not slaves or serfs and hold certain rights and privileges. | The freemans of the guild held a special meeting.  该行会的自由民举行了一次特别会议。 | Synonyms: citizens, burgesses, commoners| Antonyms: slaves, serfs | noun
