kazoo:/kəˈzuː/
noun|卡祖笛|A small, toy-like musical instrument that modifies the sound of the user's humming voice.|例句：He played a lively tune on the kazoo. 他用卡祖笛演奏了一首活泼的曲子。|例句：The children were entertained by the kazoo performance. 孩子们被卡祖笛的表演逗乐了。|近义词：mirliton, membranophone|反义词: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument)|n.
