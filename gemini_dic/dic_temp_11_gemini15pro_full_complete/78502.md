ghauts:/ɡɔːts/
n.
(尤指印度的) 高止山脉；(印度) 河边的石阶
Mountain passes or ranges; especially the Western and Eastern Ghats of India; steps on a riverbank in India.
The Western Ghats are a biodiversity hotspot. 西高止山脉是一个生物多样性热点地区。
Pilgrims bathed on the ghauts of the Ganges. 朝圣者在恒河边的石阶上沐浴。
近义词:mountains, ranges, steps, stairways
反义词:plains
词性:名词
