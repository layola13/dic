over-sufficient:/ˌoʊvər səˈfɪʃənt/
adjective
过剩的，过多的
more than sufficient; excessive
The over-sufficient supply of food led to waste.  食物供应过剩导致浪费。
The charity received an over-sufficient amount of donations.  该慈善机构收到了过多的捐款。
近义词: excessive, superfluous, redundant
反义词: insufficient, inadequate, deficient
词性: adjective
词源: From over + sufficient.  Over- is a prefix meaning "above," "beyond," "excessively," and sufficient comes from the Latin sufficientem, meaning "being enough."
