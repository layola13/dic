anidiomatic:/ˌænɪdiəˈmætɪk/
adj.
不合习惯用法的，不地道的
not typical of the natural way in which someone speaks or writes a language
His French was grammatically correct, but somewhat anidiomatic. 他的法语语法正确，但有点不地道。
近义词:unidiomatic, unnatural, awkward, stilted
反义词:idiomatic, natural, fluent
词性:adjective
