ovaritis:/ˌoʊvəˈraɪtɪs/| n. |卵巢炎|Inflammation of one or both ovaries.|She was diagnosed with ovaritis after experiencing severe pelvic pain. 她经历了严重的骨盆疼痛后被诊断出患有卵巢炎。|Synonym: oophoritis|Antonym: None|Noun

Etymology:  From Modern Latin *ovaritis*, from Latin *ovarium* (ovary) + *-itis* (inflammation).
