misrepresentation:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪzɛnˈteɪʃən/| n.| 歪曲；虚假陈述；误传|The advertisement was a gross misrepresentation of the facts.  这则广告严重歪曲了事实。|Synonyms: distortion, falsification, misstatement|Antonyms: accurate representation, truth, honesty|noun

misrepresent:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛnt/| vt.| 歪曲；不如实地叙述；误传|He claimed that the company had misrepresented its financial position. 他声称该公司不如实地陈述了其财务状况。|Synonyms: distort, falsify, misstate|Antonyms: represent accurately, portray truthfully|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + represent (to present).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old French "mes-" from Latin "minus" (less). "Represent" comes from Latin "repraesentare" (to present again, to exhibit).
