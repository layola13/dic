break-up:/ˈbreɪkʌp/| n.| 分手，破裂，分解，瓦解|The band's break-up was a shock to their fans. 乐队的解散对他们的粉丝来说是一个巨大的冲击。|近义词：split, separation, disintegration, collapse|反义词：union, reconciliation, merger|词性:名词
break-up:/ˈbreɪk ʌp/| v.| 打碎，拆散，分解，结束(关系)|They decided to break up their partnership. 他们决定结束合作关系。|近义词：separate, dissolve, disintegrate, demolish|反义词：unite, join, combine|词性:动词

词源: From the phrasal verb "break up," composed of the verb "break" (Old English *brecan) and the adverb/preposition "up" (Old English up).  The noun form developed later, referring to the result of the action of breaking up.
