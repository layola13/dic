inductometer:/ɪnˌdʌkˈtɒmɪtər/ | n. |电感计，感应系数计 |An instrument for measuring inductance.  It typically consists of a calibrated variable inductor that can be compared against an unknown inductance. | The engineer used an inductometer to measure the inductance of the coil. 工程师使用电感计测量线圈的电感。|Synonyms: inductance meter, variometer |Antonyms: N/A | noun
