aquatint:/ˈækwətɪnt/ | n. | 腐蚀铜版；腐蚀铜版画；水彩画 | A method of etching that produces a tonal effect, rather than lines, on a printed image. | The artist used aquatint to create a misty atmosphere in the background of the print.  艺术家用腐蚀铜版法在版画的背景中营造出朦胧的氛围。 | etching, engraving |  lithography (sometimes) | noun
aquatint:/ˈækwətɪnt/ | vt. | 用腐蚀铜版法制作(版画) | To engrave by means of aquatint. | He aquatinted the copper plate to produce a soft, grainy texture. 他用腐蚀铜版法在铜板上制作出柔和的颗粒状纹理。 | etch, engrave |  - | verb
