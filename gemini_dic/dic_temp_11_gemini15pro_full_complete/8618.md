anticolonialism: /ˌænti kəˈloʊniəlɪzəm/ | n. | 反殖民主义 | The opposition to colonial rule and the belief in the right of colonized peoples to self-determination. |  The rise of anticolonialism after World War II led to the independence of many nations.  二战后反殖民主义的兴起导致了许多国家的独立。

| 近义词: decolonization, independence, self-determination | 反义词: colonialism, imperialism | 词性: noun
