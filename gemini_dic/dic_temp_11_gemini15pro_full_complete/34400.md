classman:/ˈklɑːsmən/ | n. | 同班男生; 低年级男生 | A male student in the same class as another, especially one in a lower year. | He was a classman of mine at Harvard. 他是我在哈佛的同班同学（他比我低年级）。 | Synonyms: schoolmate, classmate | Antonyms: upperclassman | noun
