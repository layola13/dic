opalescence:/ˌoʊpəˈlɛsəns/ | n. |乳光，蛋白光，晕彩|A milky iridescence, like that of an opal.  The quality of reflecting light and displaying varying colors. |The opalescence of the pearl was breathtaking. 这颗珍珠的晕彩令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: iridescence, shimmer, sheen, luster|Antonyms: dullness, flatness|Noun


Etymology->
From French *opalescence*, from *opale* (“opal”) + *-escence* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality or state), from Latin *opalēs* (“opal”), from Greek *ὀπάλλιος* (opállios, “opal”).
