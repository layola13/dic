infographic:/ˌɪnfəˈɡræfɪk/ | n. | 信息图；信息图表 | A visual representation of information or data, e.g. as a chart or diagram. | Example 1: The infographic clearly explained the complex data.  信息图清楚地解释了复杂的数据。

Example 2:  She created an infographic to summarize the key findings of the report. 她创建了一个信息图表来总结报告的主要发现。| Synonyms: data visualization, chart, diagram, visual | Antonyms: text, essay, report | noun
