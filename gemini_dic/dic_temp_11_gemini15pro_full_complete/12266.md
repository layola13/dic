ascertainably:/ˌæsərˈteɪnəbli/
adv.
可确定地，可查明地
in a way that can be ascertained; verifiably.
例句：The facts are not ascertainably true. 这些事实的真实性无法确定。

例句：The cause of the fire was not ascertainably determined. 火灾的原因未能查明。

近义词：verifiably, demonstrably, provably
反义词：uncertainly, doubtfully, questionably
词性：副词
