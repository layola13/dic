backs: /bæks/ | n. | 背部；后面；支持；后退；背书；（纸牌游戏中的）最后几张牌 | The plural form of "back".  Refers to the rear surface of the human body, the reverse side of something, a position of support, the act of moving backward, endorsement or support, or the last remaining cards in a card game. | He felt a sharp pain in his lower back. 他感到下背部一阵剧痛。| Synonyms: rears, spines, supports, reverses, endorsements | Antonyms: fronts, forefronts, oppositions | noun

backs: /bæks/ | v. | 支持；后退；背书；下赌注于；位于…的后面 | Third-person singular present tense of "back". Refers to the act of supporting someone or something, moving backward, endorsing something, betting on something, or being located behind something. | She backs her local football team. 她支持她当地的足球队。 | Synonyms: supports, endorses, champions, reverses, retreats | Antonyms: opposes, obstructs, hinders, advances | verb
