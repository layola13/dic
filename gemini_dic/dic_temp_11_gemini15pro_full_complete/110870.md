lionises:/ˈlaɪənaɪzɪz/
verb
崇拜；把…捧为名流
to treat (someone) as a very important and famous person
The media lionises athletes. 媒体把运动员捧为名流。
Synonyms:  idolize,  exalt,  glorify,  celebrate,  worship
Antonyms:  criticize,  condemn,  denounce,  disparage
词性:v.
