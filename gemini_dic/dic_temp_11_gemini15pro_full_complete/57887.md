driest:/ˈdraɪɪst/| adj.|最干燥的|Superlative form of dry.  Having the least moisture or rainfall.  Used to describe something that has the absolute lowest level of wetness compared to other things.|The Atacama Desert is one of the driest places on earth. 阿塔卡马沙漠是地球上最干燥的地方之一。

The driest month of the year is usually July.  一年中最干燥的月份通常是七月。|近义词：arid, parched, dehydrated|反义词：wettest, dampest, soggiest|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *drygeste (unattested), superlative of dryge "dry," from Proto-Germanic *drugaz.  Related to Old Norse draugr "dry log," Dutch droog, German trocken. The -est suffix is a common superlative ending in English.
