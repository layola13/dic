Fulahs:/ˈfuːləz/ | n. | 富拉尼人 |  A member of a primarily Muslim people scattered throughout West Africa, from Lake Chad to the Atlantic coast. They traditionally are pastoral nomads but many now live in towns. Also called Fulani, Fulah. | The Fulahs are known for their cattle herding. 富拉尼人以放牧牛群而闻名。|  The Fulahs migrated across West Africa. 富拉尼人迁移到西非各地。 | Synonyms: Fulani, Peul | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
