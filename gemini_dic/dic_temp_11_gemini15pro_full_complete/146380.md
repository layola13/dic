overfloridnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈflɔːrɪdnəsɪz/
|n.| 过花，过于华丽 |  The quality or state of being overflorid; excessive ornateness.  |  His prose is marked by overfloridnesses of style. 他的散文以风格过于华丽为特点。 |  ornateness, extravagance, flamboyance |  simplicity, plainness | noun

overflorid:/ˌoʊvərˈflɔːrɪd/
|adj.| 过于华丽的，过分修饰的 | Excessively ornate or flowery in style or language. | The overflorid decorations in the room were overwhelming. 房间里过于华丽的装饰让人感到压抑。 |  flowery, ornate, flamboyant  | simple, plain, understated | adjective
