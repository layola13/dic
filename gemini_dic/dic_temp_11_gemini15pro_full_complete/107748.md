lapdog:/ˈlæpˌdɒɡ/ (UK), /ˈlæpˌdɔːɡ/ (US)| n. |哈巴狗，奉承者|A small dog kept as a pet and often held in the lap.  Figuratively, a person who is subservient and fawning, especially to someone in power. |She carried her little lapdog everywhere she went. 她把她的小哈巴狗带到任何地方。 He's nothing but the CEO's lapdog. 他只不过是首席执行官的走狗。|近义词：toy dog, pampered pet, sycophant, toady|反义词：independent person, critic|词性：名词
