duyoung: /duːjʌŋ/ | n. |儒艮|A large, herbivorous marine mammal related to the manatee, found in warm coastal waters of the Indian and western Pacific Oceans.|The dugong grazed peacefully on the seagrass. 儒艮平静地在海草上吃草。|Synonym: sea cow|Antonym: (No direct antonym for an animal species, could consider terrestrial herbivores)|Noun


Etymology:
From Malay *duyung*, ultimately from Sanskrit *dugong*, meaning "lady of the sea".
