connectednesses:/kəˈnɛktɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
连接性；关联性；联系
The state of being connected or interconnected.
The internet has increased the connectedness of the world. 互联网增强了世界的连接性。

The conference fostered a sense of connectedness among the participants.  这次会议促进了与会者之间的联系感。
近义词：connection, linkage, relatedness, interdependence
反义词：isolation, separation, disconnection
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From connectedness, from connect + -ed + -ness.  Connect ultimately derives from the Latin "connectere," meaning "to bind together."
