micrography: /maɪˈkrɒɡrəfi/ /maɪˈkrɑːɡrəfi/
n.
显微照相术；显微摄影；微型书法；微型书写
the art or practice of writing or drawing in extremely small characters; the production of extremely small writing, images, or photographs.

Micrography is used to create incredibly detailed images of small objects. 显微照相术用于创建小物体的极其详细的图像。

He was fascinated by micrography and spent hours creating miniature works of art. 他对微型书法很着迷，花了几个小时创作微型艺术作品。

近义词: photomicrography, microphotography
反义词: macrography
词性: noun
