fronds:/frɑːndz/
n.
叶子（尤指蕨类植物、棕榈树等的）;复数
The large, divided leaves of ferns, palms, and some other plants.
The fern unfurled its delicate fronds.  蕨类植物展开了它精致的叶子。
foliage, leaves
branches, roots
noun
