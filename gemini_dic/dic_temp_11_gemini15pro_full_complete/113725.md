macrocystis: /ˌmækroʊˈsɪstɪs/| n. |巨藻属；巨藻|A genus of large brown kelps, including the giant kelp (Macrocystis pyrifera), found in cool coastal waters.  These kelps form extensive underwater forests that provide habitat for a variety of marine life.|例句：Macrocystis pyrifera is the largest species of kelp. 巨藻是最大的海藻种类。

|例句：The Macrocystis forest provides a rich habitat for many fish. 巨藻林为许多鱼类提供了丰富的栖息地。|近义词：kelp, seaweed|反义词: None|词性: noun
