arid:/ˈærɪd/ | adj. | 干旱的；贫瘠的；枯燥的 | (of land or a climate) having little or no rain; too dry or barren to support vegetation.  Lacking in interest, excitement, or meaning. | The desert landscape was arid and desolate.  沙漠景观干燥荒凉。 The lecture was arid and tedious. 演讲枯燥乏味。 | synonyms: dry, barren, parched, desert, waterless, infertile, unproductive, sterile, desiccated, dehydrated | antonyms: fertile, lush, moist, humid, wet, damp | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *aridus*, from *ārēre* "to be dry, parched, burn."
