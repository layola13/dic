anticensoriously:/ˌæntiˌsɛnsɔːriəsli/
adverb
反审查地，不加审查地
Without censorship or criticism; in a manner that avoids censorship or refraining from censoring.
He spoke anticensoriously about the government's policies. 他毫不避讳地谈论了政府的政策。
Synonyms: freely, openly, unreservedly, frankly
Antonyms: censoriously, restrictively, cautiously, guardedly
Word type: Adverb
