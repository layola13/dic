mucronate:/ˈmjuːkrəneɪt/| adj.| 骤尖的，具短尖的| Abruptly terminated by a short, sharp point; tipped with a mucro. | The leaf tip is mucronate.  叶尖是骤尖的。| The Holly leaves are dark green, glossy, and mucronate. 冬青树的叶子是深绿色的，有光泽的，并且有短尖。| 近义词: cuspidate, acute, pointed | 反义词: obtuse, rounded | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mucrōnātus, past participle of mucrōnāre (“to sharpen, to point”), from mucrō (“a sharp point, edge”).
