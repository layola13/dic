fuguists:/ˈfjuːɡɪsts/
n.
河豚鱼爱好者；吃河豚鱼的人
A person who enjoys eating fugu, or pufferfish, despite the potential risks.
Fuguists appreciate the unique taste and texture of fugu, despite its potential toxicity.  河豚鱼爱好者欣赏河豚鱼独特的口味和质地，尽管它有潜在的毒性。

Many fuguists travel to Japan to experience the thrill of eating this delicacy. 许多河豚鱼爱好者前往日本体验吃这种美味的刺激。
Synonym: fugu connoisseurs, pufferfish enthusiasts
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps) people who avoid risky foods, cautious eaters
词性: noun
