affirmations:/ˌæfərˈmeɪʃənz/| n.|肯定；断言；确认|Positive statements that can help you to challenge and overcome self-sabotaging and negative thoughts.|She repeats positive affirmations every morning to boost her self-esteem. 她每天早上重复积极的肯定语来增强她的自尊。|synonyms: assertions, declarations, pronouncements|antonyms: denials, negations|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *affirmacioun*, from Old French affirmation, from Latin affirmatiō, affirmātiōnem, from affirmare (to affirm).
