deghosted:/diːˈɡoʊstɪd/
adjective
去除了重影的；去除了鬼影的
Removed or reduced ghosting (visual artifacts) in an image or display.
The new monitor boasts deghosted images, providing a much clearer picture.  这台新显示器拥有去鬼影图像技术，提供更清晰的画面。
The deghosted image was significantly sharper. 去除了重影的图像明显更加清晰。
synonym:  clarified, sharpened, improved (in image quality)
antonym:  ghostly, blurred
词性: 形容词
