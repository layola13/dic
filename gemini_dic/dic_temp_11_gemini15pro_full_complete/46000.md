cyberbully:/ˈsaɪbərˌbʊli/| n. v.|网络欺凌者（名词）, 网络欺凌（动词）|Someone who uses electronic communication to bully a person, typically by sending messages of an intimidating or threatening nature.  (使用电子通讯工具欺凌他人的人，通常以发送恐吓或威胁性质的信息的方式。)|The teenager was cyberbullied relentlessly by anonymous classmates.  这个青少年被匿名同学无情地网络欺凌。|He was accused of cyberbullying his ex-girlfriend. 他被指控网络欺凌他的前女友。|近义词：harass, intimidate, troll|反义词:support, encourage|词性:名词, 动词
