dispersive:/dɪˈspɜːrsɪv/| adj.|分散的，弥散的，散射的|Tending to disperse or cause dispersion; spreading widely.|The dispersive properties of the prism split the white light into a rainbow. 棱镜的色散特性将白光分成彩虹。|synonyms: scattering, diffusing, spreading, dissipating|antonyms: concentrating, focusing, converging, collecting|adjective

dispersive:/dɪˈspɜːrsɪv/| adj.|色散的|Relating to or causing dispersion of light or other electromagnetic radiation.|The dispersive medium caused the different wavelengths of light to travel at different speeds. 色散介质导致不同波长的光以不同的速度传播。|synonyms: refractive, prismatic|antonyms: non-dispersive|adjective
