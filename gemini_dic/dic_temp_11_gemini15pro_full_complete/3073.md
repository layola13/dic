Example 2:  She consulted a practitioner of aeromancy to understand the meaning of the strange cloud formations. 她咨询了一位气象占卜师来理解奇特云彩形状的含义。|近义词：divination, augury |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + -mancy (divination).
