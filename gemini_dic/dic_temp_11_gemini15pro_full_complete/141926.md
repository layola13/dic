ordinarily:/ˌɔːrdɪˈnɛrɪli/ (US), /ˌɔːdɪˈnərɪli/ (UK)
adverb
通常地，一般地
Usually; under normal conditions.
She ordinarily gets up at 7 am. 她通常早上7点起床。

He doesn't ordinarily wear a suit to work. 他通常不穿西装上班。

usually, normally, typically, generally
exceptionally, rarely, unusually, infrequently
词性: 副词
