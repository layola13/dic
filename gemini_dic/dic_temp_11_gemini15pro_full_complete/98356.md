insistence:/ɪnˈsɪstəns/| n.|坚持，坚决，强调|The firm act of demanding something; the quality of being insistent; persistent and unwavering demand.|His insistence on punctuality was well known. 他对守时的坚持是众所周知的。|近义词：persistence, perseverance, tenacity|反义词:acquiescence, yielding|noun

insistence:/ɪnˈsɪstəns/| n.|坚持，坚决，强调|An insistent and persistent demand.|Her insistence that we stay for dinner was flattering. 她坚持让我们留下吃饭，这很令人受宠若惊。|近义词：demand, requirement|反义词:waiver|noun
