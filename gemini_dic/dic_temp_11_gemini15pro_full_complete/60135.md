effectless:/ɪˈfɛktləs/
adjective
无效的；不起作用的；没有影响的
Having no effect; not producing the desired or intended result.
His protests were completely effectless. 他的抗议完全无效。
The medicine proved effectless against the virus. 这种药证明对病毒无效。
近义词:futile, ineffective, useless, vain
反义词:effective, potent, powerful
词性:adjective
