consistory:/kənˈsɪstəri/ | n. |宗教会议；教会法院；枢密院|A formal meeting of church officials or advisors, especially in the Roman Catholic Church, or a court or council of officials. |The Pope announced the new cardinals at a consistory. 教皇在宗教会议上宣布了新的枢机主教。| The consistory met to discuss the church's finances. 教会法院开会讨论教会的财政状况。|Synonyms: council, assembly, synod, tribunal |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "dispersal," "dissolution") |Noun

consistory:/kənˈsɪstəri/ | adj. |（古）一致的，协调的|(Archaic) Consistent; harmonious. | Their actions were consistory with their beliefs. 他们的行为与他们的信仰一致。(Archaic usage) | Synonyms: consistent, harmonious, congruent | Antonyms: inconsistent, discordant, incongruous | Adjective
