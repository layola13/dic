nonmigrations:/ˌnɒnmaɪˈɡreɪʃənz/
n.|非迁移，不迁移|The nonmigrations of certain bird species are influenced by the availability of food sources. 某些鸟类的非迁移受食物来源的影响。|
nonmigrations:|Absence of migration; lack of movement from one region to another.  Nonmigrations can be observed in some populations of birds that have adapted to year-round food availability in a specific area.|The researchers studied the nonmigrations of the local deer population. 研究人员研究了当地鹿群的非迁移现象。|
近义词:sedentariness, immobility, residence|
反义词:migrations, movements, relocation|
词性:noun
