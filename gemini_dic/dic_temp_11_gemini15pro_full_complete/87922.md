hierological:/ˌhaɪərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
与圣物有关的；神圣的；祭司的
Relating to sacred writings or hieroglyphics; pertaining to priests or sacred matters.
The ancient Egyptians used a hierological script for religious texts. 古埃及人使用一种神圣的文字来书写宗教文本。

The museum houses a collection of hierological papyri.  博物馆收藏了一批神圣的纸莎草纸。
sacred, priestly, religious
secular, profane
adjective
