inhaling:/ɪnˈheɪlɪŋ/
verb
吸入，吸气
the act of breathing in
例句：She was inhaling the fresh mountain air. 她正在呼吸新鲜的山间空气。

例句：He was inhaling deeply, trying to calm down. 他深深地吸气，试图让自己平静下来。

近义词：breathing in, respiring
反义词：exhaling
词性：动词 (present participle of inhale)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inhalare, from in- "in" + halare "to breathe".
