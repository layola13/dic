lapel:/ləˈpel/| n. | 翻领；（西服上衣的）驳头 |The part of a garment, especially a coat or jacket, that is folded back from the front opening and forms a continuation of the collar.| He adjusted his lapel before addressing the crowd. 他在向人群讲话前整理了一下他的翻领。| synonyms: collar, facing | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
