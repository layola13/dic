offensive:/əˈfɛnsɪv/ | adj. n.| 冒犯的；攻击性的；进攻的 | causing someone to feel resentful, upset, or annoyed; actively aggressive; relating to or designed for attack |  His comments were deeply offensive to many people. 他的言论冒犯了很多人。| synonyms: insulting, abusive, obnoxious, aggressive, attacking | antonyms: inoffensive, harmless, defensive, pleasant, agreeable | adjective

offensive:/əˈfɛnsɪv/ | n.| 进攻；攻势 | an aggressive military operation | The army launched a major offensive against the enemy. 军队对敌人发起了大规模进攻。| synonyms: attack, assault, onslaught, campaign | antonyms: defense, retreat, withdrawal | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *offensif, from Late Latin offensivus (“striking against”), from Latin offendere (“to strike against, offend”).
