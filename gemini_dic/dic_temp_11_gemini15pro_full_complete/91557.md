hyper-normality's:/ˌhaɪpər-nɔːrˈmælətiz/ | n. | 超常态 |  A state of exaggerated normality, often appearing artificial or surreal due to excessive adherence to perceived norms. It's a noun formed by adding the prefix "hyper-" (meaning excessive) to "normality" and then adding the possessive suffix "'s". |  The pristine lawns and eerily silent streets gave the suburb an unsettling air of hyper-normality's.  这片郊区原始的草坪和异常安静的街道给人一种令人不安的超常态感。|  artificial normality, exaggerated normalcy, simulated normalcy  | unnaturalness, abnormality, eccentricity | noun
