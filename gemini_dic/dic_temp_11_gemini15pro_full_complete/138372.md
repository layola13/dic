notionist:/ˈnoʊʃənɪst/ | n. | 理論家；空想家；观念论者 | Someone who holds or promotes a particular notion or set of notions, especially theoretical or impractical ones. | He was dismissed as a mere notionist by his more pragmatic colleagues. 他被他那些更务实的同事们斥为一个纯粹的空想家。 |  近义词: theorist, ideologue, visionary | 反义词: pragmatist, realist | 词性: noun

notion:/ˈnoʊʃən/ | n. | 观念；想法；概念 |  A conception of or belief about something. | I have a notion that he's not telling us the whole truth. 我觉得他没有告诉我们全部真相。 | 近义词: idea, concept, belief | 反义词: fact, reality | 词性: noun

-ist: /-ɪst/ | suffix | ...主义者；...者；从事...的人 | A suffix forming nouns denoting a person who practices or believes in a particular thing, or holds a particular office or position. |  A socialist.  社会主义者。| 近义词: -er, -ian | 反义词: None | 词性: suffix
