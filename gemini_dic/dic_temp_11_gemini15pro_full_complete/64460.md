eristical:/ɪˈrɪstɪk(ə)l/
adjective
争论的，好辩的
Characterized by or involving disputation or controversy, often with the intention of winning rather than reaching the truth.
He engaged in eristical arguments, more concerned with winning the debate than finding the truth. 他参与了争论性的辩论，更关心赢得辩论而不是寻找真相。
近义词：argumentative, contentious, disputatious, polemical
反义词：conciliatory, agreeable, cooperative
词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek *eristikos*, from *eris* "strife."
