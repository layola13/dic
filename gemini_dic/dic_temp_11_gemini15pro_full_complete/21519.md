blue-on-blue:/ˌbluː ɒn ˈbluː/| adj.|自相残杀的；友军误击的|An attack on one's own forces or allies, especially by fire from one's own side.  Originating from military contexts where friendly forces mistakenly engage each other, often due to difficulties in identification.  The term evokes the image of blue representing friendly forces on military maps and displays.|例句：The blue-on-blue incident resulted in several casualties. 友军误击事件造成数人伤亡。

例句：The inquiry focused on preventing future blue-on-blue engagements. 调查的重点是防止未来再次发生友军误击。|近义词：friendly fire, fratricidal fire|反义词:enemy fire, hostile fire|词性:形容词
