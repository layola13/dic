misestimation:/ˌmɪsˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃən/
n.
误判；错误估计
An incorrect estimation of something.
The project's failure was due to a misestimation of the costs involved.  这个项目的失败是由于对相关成本的错误估计。
miscalculation, underestimation, overestimation
correct estimation, accurate assessment
noun
