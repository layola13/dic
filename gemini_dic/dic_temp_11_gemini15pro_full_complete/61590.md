emery:/ˈeməri/ (US), /ˈɛməri/ (UK)| n.|金刚砂，刚玉粉|A very hard black or gray mineral substance, corundum, used as an abrasive.|The carpenter used emery cloth to smooth the wood. 木匠用金刚砂布打磨木头。|Synonyms: abrasive, corundum, grinding powder|Antonyms: lubricant|noun

emeries:/ˈeməriz/ (US), /ˈɛməriz/ (UK)| n.|金刚砂，刚玉粉（emery的复数形式）|The plural form of emery, referring to multiple types or quantities of the abrasive mineral.|He stocked various grades of emeries for different polishing tasks. 他储备了各种等级的金刚砂，用于不同的抛光任务。|Synonyms: abrasives, corundums, grinding powders|Antonyms: lubricants|noun
