cooperatively:/koʊˈɑːpərətɪvli/
adverb
合作地；协作地
in a way that involves mutual assistance in working toward a common goal.
The team worked cooperatively to complete the project.  团队合作完成了这个项目。
The countries agreed to cooperatively manage the shared resources. 这些国家同意合作管理共享资源。
近义词:jointly, collaboratively, together, mutually
反义词:independently, separately, alone, individually
词性:adverb

词源:
From cooperate + -ive + -ly.  Cooperate comes from co- (together) + operate (to work).
