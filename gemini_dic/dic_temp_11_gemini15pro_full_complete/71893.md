flew:/fluː/
verb
飞, 飞行(fly的过去式)
past tense of fly.  It indicates the action of flying happened in the past.
He flew to London yesterday. 他昨天飞去了伦敦。
The bird flew away. 鸟飞走了。
近义词:soared, glided
反义词:landed, descended
词性:verb
