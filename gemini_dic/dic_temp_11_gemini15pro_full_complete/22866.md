botanicals:/bəˈtænɪkəlz/
|n.|植物；植物性药材；植物标本|Substances obtained from plants, used especially in medicinal or cosmetic products.|Many skincare products contain natural botanicals. 许多护肤品含有天然植物成分。|Synonyms: plants, herbs, flora|Antonyms: synthetics, chemicals|noun


botanical:/bəˈtænɪkəl/
|adj.|植物学的；与植物有关的|Relating to plants or the study of plants.|She has a keen interest in botanical research. 她对植物学研究有浓厚的兴趣。|Synonyms: plant-based, floral, herbal|Antonyms: synthetic, artificial, non-biological|adjective
