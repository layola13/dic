at-instance-of:/æt ˈɪnstəns ɒv/ | prepositional phrase | 在...的实例 |  Refers to a specific occurrence or example of something. It highlights a particular point in time or a specific case where something happens or exists. |  At the instance of the CEO, the project was approved. 在首席执行官的指示下，该项目获得批准。 |  At that instance, the lights went out. 在那一刻，灯灭了。 | synonyms: upon the occurrence of, at the point of,  in the case of | antonyms: generally, usually, typically | phrase
