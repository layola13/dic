aneurysmally:/ˌænjuˈrɪzməli/
adv.
像动脉瘤一样地；异常膨大地；危险地
in a way that resembles or suggests an aneurysm; abnormally or dangerously
His blood pressure rose aneurysmally. 他的血压异常升高。
近义词: dangerously, precariously, alarmingly
反义词: safely, securely, stably
词性: 副词

词源:
From aneurysm + -al + -ly.  Aneurysm comes from the Greek "aneurysma," meaning "a widening."
