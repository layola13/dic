fallowist:/ˈfæləʊɪst/| n.|追随者；盲从者|Someone who follows a particular belief, practice, or leader, especially uncritically or without independent thought.  A follower, often implying a lack of originality or critical thinking.|例句：He's just a fallowist, never questioning the leader's decisions. 他只是一个盲从者，从不质疑领导的决定。

例句：The party was full of fallowists, all echoing the same political slogans.  这个派对充满了追随者，都在重复着相同的政治口号。|近义词：follower, disciple, adherent, imitator|反义词：leader, innovator, pioneer, maverick|词性: noun
