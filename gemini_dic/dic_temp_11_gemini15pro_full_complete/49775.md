demonical and demoniacal are essentially synonyms, though demoniacal tends to emphasize a more frenzied or violently possessed state.

demonial:/dɪˈməʊniəl/ | adj. | 恶魔的; 恶魔似的 | Of or relating to a demon or demons. |  The ancient text described demonial possessions and exorcisms. 这部古代文献描述了恶魔附身和驱魔仪式。

They believed the storm was a demonial manifestation. 他们认为这场风暴是恶魔的显现。 | 近义词: demonic, demoniacal, devilish, satanic, infernal, fiendish | 反义词: angelic, divine, heavenly | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Late Latin *daemonialis, from Latin daemon "demon" + -alis (adjectival suffix).  Ultimately from Greek daimon "divinity, spirit."
