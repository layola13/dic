overfreedom:/ˈoʊvərˌfriːdəm/
n.
过度自由
Excessive freedom; a state where freedom is so extensive that it becomes detrimental or paralyzing.
Some argue that too many choices can lead to overfreedom and a sense of paralysis.  有些人认为过多的选择会导致过度自由和瘫痪感。
Overfreedom can be overwhelming, making it difficult to make decisions. 过度的自由可能会让人不知所措，难以做出决定。
synonyms: excess of freedom, unlimited freedom, boundless liberty
antonyms: restriction, constraint, limitation
noun
