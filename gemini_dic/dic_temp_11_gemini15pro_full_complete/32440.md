chimerically:/kaɪˈmɛrɪk(ə)li/| adv.|异想天开地，荒诞地|In a way that is wildly fanciful or imaginative; unrealistically.|He chimerically believed he could fly to the moon using only balloons. 他异想天开地认为只用气球就能飞到月球。|近义词：fancifully, imaginatively, unrealistically, delusionally|反义词：realistically, practically, pragmatically|词性:副词
