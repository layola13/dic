bellyacher:/ˈbɛliˌeɪkər/ (US), /ˈbɛliˌeɪkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |抱怨者，牢骚满腹的人|A person who complains, especially habitually. |例句：He's a constant bellyacher, always finding something to gripe about. 他总是抱怨个不停，总能找到一些事情来抱怨。

例句：The boss is tired of listening to the bellyachers and wants them to focus on solutions. 老板厌倦了听抱怨，希望他们专注于解决方案。

近义词：complainer, whiner, griper, grouch|反义词: optimist, enthusiast |词性: noun
