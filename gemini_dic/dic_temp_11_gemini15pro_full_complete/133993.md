nonhit:/ˌnɒnˈhɪt/ | n. | 未击中；未命中；（棒球）未安打 | A batted ball that does not become a hit.  A base runner who advances after a nonhit is not credited with a stolen base. | The batter swung mightily but it was a nonhit.  击球手用力挥棒，但没有击中。 | 近义词: miss, failure | 反义词: hit, success | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "hit"
