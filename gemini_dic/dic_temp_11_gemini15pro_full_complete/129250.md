nocument:/ˈnoʊkjʊmənt/| n.|无证件者，无身份者|A person without legal documentation or proof of identity.  This word is not standard English and is likely a neologism. It seems to be formed by analogy with "document" and the prefix "no-".|Example: The government is trying to help nocuments gain legal status. 政府正在努力帮助无证件者获得合法身份。|Example:  Many nocuments face significant challenges in accessing basic services. 许多无证件者在获得基本服务方面面临重大挑战。|近义词：undocumented immigrant, illegal alien (note that this term is considered offensive by many)|反义词:documented immigrant, citizen|词性:noun
