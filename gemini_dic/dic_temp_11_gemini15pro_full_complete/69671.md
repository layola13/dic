fender:/ˈfɛndər/ (US), /ˈfɛndə/ (UK)| n.|挡泥板，防护板；（乐器）共鸣板|A part of a vehicle, such as a car, bicycle, or boat, that arches over the wheel and prevents mud, water, and stones from being thrown up.|The car's fender was dented in the accident. 汽车的挡泥板在事故中凹陷了。|Synonyms: mudguard, wing (UK)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

fender:/ˈfɛndər/ (US), /ˈfɛndə/ (UK)| v.|防御，保护；挡开|To ward off or defend against something.|He used his arms to fender off the blows. 他用胳膊挡开了打击。|Synonyms: defend, protect, shield|Antonyms: attack, expose|verb
