oversized:/ˈoʊvərˌsaɪzd/| adj.|特大号的，超大的|Larger than the usual or standard size. |She bought an oversized sweater. 她买了一件特大号的毛衣。|近义词：large, big, huge, giant, enormous|反义词:small, tiny, petite|词性:形容词
词源:  From "over-" (meaning "excessively") + "sized" (meaning "having a particular size").
