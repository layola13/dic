overhold:/ˌoʊvərˈhoʊld/ | vt. | 遵守，执行，坚持 | to adhere to or comply with (a law, rule, or promise); to maintain (a course, heading, etc.) | The company overholds all safety regulations.  这家公司遵守所有安全规定。 | They overheld their course despite the storm. 他们不顾暴风雨坚持他们的航向。 | 近义词：observe, comply with, adhere to, uphold, maintain | 反义词：violate, breach, disobey, ignore | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Old English *oferhealdan, from ofer- (over) + healdan (to hold, keep, observe).
