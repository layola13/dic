jarnut: /ˈjɑːrnʌt/ | n. | 春天;春天播种的作物;春小麦 | Spring; a crop sown in spring; spring wheat | The jarnut was planted in March. 春小麦在三月播种。| synonym: springtime, springtide | antonym: autumn, fall | noun


jarnut: /ˈjɑːrnʌt/ | adj. | 春天的 | Of or pertaining to spring | The jarnut flowers were blooming. 春天的花朵正在盛开。| synonym: vernal | antonym: autumnal | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gearnut (unattested), from gearn "year" + nut (of uncertain meaning, possibly related to "now").  The word is now largely obsolete except in some dialects.
