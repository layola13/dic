mesonephros:/ˈmɛzoʊˌnɛfrɒs/ |n.| 中肾|The mesonephros is a type of kidney that functions in vertebrate embryos. 中肾是脊椎动物胚胎中的一种肾脏。| The mesonephros develops after the pronephros and before the metanephros. 中肾在原肾之后、后肾之前发育。|Synonym: Wolffian body|Antonym: None|Noun

mesonephros:/ˌmɛsəˈnɛfrɒs/ |n.| 中肾|The mesonephros plays a crucial role in the development of the urogenital system. 中肾在泌尿生殖系统发育中起着至关重要的作用。|  In some fish and amphibians, the mesonephros persists as the functional kidney in adults. 在一些鱼类和两栖动物中，中肾作为功能性肾脏一直存在于成体中。 |Synonym: mid-kidney|Antonym: None|Noun
