opalescent:/ˌoʊpəˈlɛsənt/ | adj. |乳白色的，乳白的，蛋白石色的|Exhibiting a play of colors like that of an opal; iridescent.|The vase had an opalescent glaze that shimmered in the light.  这个花瓶上有一层乳白色的釉，在光线下闪闪发光。|Synonyms: iridescent, pearly, shimmering, nacreous|Antonyms: dull, matte, monochrome|adjective

opalescence:/ˌoʊpəˈlɛsəns/ | n. |乳白，乳光|The quality or state of being opalescent.|The opalescence of the pearl was captivating. 珍珠的乳白色光泽令人着迷。|Synonyms: iridescence, shimmer, pearliness|Antonyms: dullness, flatness|noun
