liposuction: /ˈlɪpəʊˌsʌkʃən/ (UK) /ˈlaɪpoʊˌsʌkʃən/ (US)| n. | 抽脂术 | The surgical removal of fat beneath the skin with the aid of suction. |  She's considering liposuction to remove excess fat from her thighs. 她正在考虑通过抽脂手术来去除大腿上多余的脂肪。| synonyms: lipectomy, liposculpture | antonyms: fat grafting, lipofilling | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *lipo-*,  "fat" (from Greek *lipos*) + *suction* (from Latin *suctus*, past participle of *sugere*, "to suck").
