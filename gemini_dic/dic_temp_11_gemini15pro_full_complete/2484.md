adlai:/ˈædˌlaɪ/| n.|阿德莱，阿德莱·史蒂文森（人名）|Adlai refers to either Adlai Ewing Stevenson I (1835–1914) who served as the 23rd Vice President of the United States or his grandson, Adlai Ewing Stevenson II (1900–1965), who served as the 31st Governor of Illinois and was the Democratic Party's nominee for president in 1952 and 1956.|Adlai Stevenson was a prominent figure in American politics. 阿德莱·史蒂文森是美国政坛的杰出人物。|近义词:Stevenson|反义词:None|词性:noun

adlai:/ˈædˌlaɪ/| n.|乔布斯泪，一种谷物|Adlai is a type of grain, also known as Job's tears, that is cultivated for its edible seeds.|Adlai is a nutritious grain that can be cooked like rice.  乔布斯泪是一种营养丰富的谷物，可以像米饭一样煮。|近义词:Job's tears, coix seed|反义词:None|词性:noun
