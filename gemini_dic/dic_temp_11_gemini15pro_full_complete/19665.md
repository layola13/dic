bilaterality:/ˌbaɪˌlætəˈrælɪti/
n.|双边性，双侧性|The state or quality of being bilateral; involving two sides, parties, or countries.|例句：The bilaterality of the agreement ensured both nations benefited.  该协议的双边性确保了两国都能受益。

例句：Bilaterality is essential for a strong and lasting partnership. 双边性对于建立牢固和持久的伙伴关系至关重要。|近义词：reciprocity, mutuality|反义词：unilaterality|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + lateral (of or pertaining to the side) + -ity (forming nouns of quality or state)
