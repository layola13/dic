antisuffrage:/ˌæntiˈsʌfrɪdʒ/ | n. | 反对妇女参政; 反对妇女选举权 | Opposition to the extension of suffrage (the right to vote), especially to women. | The antisuffrage movement actively campaigned against giving women the right to vote. 反对妇女参政运动积极开展反对赋予妇女投票权的活动。| The National Association Opposed to Woman Suffrage was a powerful antisuffrage organization. 全国反对妇女参政协会是一个强大的反妇女参政组织。 | 近义词: antifeminist | 反义词: pro-suffrage, suffragist | 词性: 名词
