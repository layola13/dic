godzilla:/ɡɒdˈzɪlə/ /ɡɑːdˈzɪlə/| n. |哥斯拉|A giant, monstrous, fictional creature that destroys cities, originating from Japanese cinema. It is often seen as a metaphor for the destructive power of nature or technology.|Godzilla destroyed the city. 哥斯拉摧毁了这座城市。|Synonyms: monster, kaiju, beast|Antonyms: hero, savior|noun
