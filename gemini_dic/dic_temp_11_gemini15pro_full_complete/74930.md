frettingly:/ˈfrɛtɪŋli/
adverb|焦躁地，不安地|In a manner characterized by worry and anxiety.  Showing or expressing worry.|She paced frettingly around the room, waiting for the news. 她焦躁地在房间里踱来踱去，等待着消息。|nervously, anxiously, worriedly|calmly, serenely|词性: 副词

frettingly:/ˈfrɛtɪŋli/
词源|From fret (verb) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverb suffix). Ultimately from Old English *fretan "to devour, consume," related to Old Norse freta "to gnaw, fret."|词源解析：来源于动词fret（烦躁，焦急）加上现在分词后缀-ing和副词后缀-ly。最终源于古英语*fretan“吞噬，消耗”，与古诺尔斯语freta“啃，烦躁”相关。
