existences:/ɪɡˈzɪstənsɪz/
|n.|存在；生存；生活；生活方式|The state of being or living. The way in which someone lives.|例句：The struggle for existence is a constant theme in his novels.  生存斗争是他小说中永恒的主题。

|例句：They led a peaceful existence in the countryside. 他们在乡下过着平静的生活。|近义词：being, life, survival, presence|反义词：nonexistence, nothingness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English existence, from Old French existence, from Late Latin existentia, from Latin existent- ‘existing’, present participle of existere ‘exist’, from ex- ‘out’ + sistere ‘stand’.
