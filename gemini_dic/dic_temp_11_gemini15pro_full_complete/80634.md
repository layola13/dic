goosewinged:/ˈɡuːswɪŋd/
adjective
鹅翼形的；（指帆船）侧风航行的
(of a sailboat) sailing with the wind abeam; having sails set on opposite sides.  Resembling the spread wings of a goose in flight.
The goosewinged sloop sliced through the waves.  鹅翼形的单桅帆船破浪前进。
Near synonyms:  wing and wing, by the wind
Antonyms:  head to wind, close-hauled
