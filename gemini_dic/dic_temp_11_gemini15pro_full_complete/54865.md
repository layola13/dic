displeasedly:/dɪsˈpliːzɪdli/
adverb
不悦地，生气地
In a manner showing displeasure or annoyance.
She frowned displeasedly at the mess. 她不悦地皱着眉头看着这乱七八糟的样子。
He tossed the paper aside displeasedly. 他不高兴地把报纸扔到一边。
近义词: angrily, crossly, grumpily, sullenly, moodily
反义词: happily, cheerfully, gladly, pleasantly, contentedly
词性: adverb

词源:
From displeased + -ly.  Displeased itself comes from dis- (expressing negation) + pleased (from Old French plesir, meaning "to please").
