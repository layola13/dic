doublespeakers:/ˈdʌblˌspikərz/
noun
复数名词: 口是心非的人；两面派；双面人
Someone who says one thing and does another; a hypocrite.
例句：Politicians are often accused of being doublespeakers, promising one thing during campaigns and doing another once elected. 政客们经常被指责是两面派，在竞选期间承诺一件事，一旦当选就做另一件事。

例句：He's such a doublespeaker! He told me he supported my project, but then he voted against it. 他真是个两面派！他告诉我他支持我的项目，但后来却投了反对票。

近义词：hypocrite, two-faced person, liar, deceiver
反义词：honest person, straightforward person
词性：n.
