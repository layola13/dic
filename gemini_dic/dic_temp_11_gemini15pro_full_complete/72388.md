fluctuate:/ˈflʌktʃueɪt/ (US), /ˈflʌktʃʊeɪt/ (UK)| v. |波动，起伏，变动|To change continually; to rise and fall irregularly.|The price of gold has been fluctuating wildly this year. 今年黄金价格波动剧烈。|Synonyms: vary, oscillate, waver, change, shift|Antonyms: stabilize, remain constant, stay fixed|verb

fluctuation:/ˌflʌktʃuˈeɪʃən/ (US), /ˌflʌktʃʊˈeɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |波动，起伏，变动|An irregular rising and falling in number or amount; a variation.|There have been significant fluctuations in the stock market recently. 最近股票市场出现了显著的波动。|Synonyms: variation, oscillation, instability, change, shift|Antonyms: stability, constancy, fixedness|noun
