hypothetical:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
假设的，假定的
Based on or serving as a hypothesis; supposed.
It's a hypothetical question, but what would you do if you won the lottery? 这是一个假设性的问题，但如果你中了彩票你会怎么做？
theoretical, supposed, assumed, conjectural
actual, real, factual, proven
adjective
