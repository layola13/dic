neurodivergent:/ˌnʊərəʊdaɪˈvɜːdʒənt/ /ˌnʊroʊdaɪˈvɜːrdʒənt/
adjective
神经多样化的；神经分歧的
Differing in mental or neurological function from what is considered typical or normal.  Relating to a person who has conditions such as autism spectrum disorders, ADHD, dyslexia, etc.
Example: The school offers support programs for neurodivergent students.  这所学校为神经多样化的学生提供支持项目。

Example:  Many neurodivergent individuals have unique strengths and talents. 许多神经多样化的个体拥有独特的优势和才能。

Synonym: neuroatypical, neurodiverse
Antonym: neurotypical
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
neuro- (prefix): relating to nerves or the nervous system, from Greek "neuron" (nerve)
divergent (adjective): tending to be different or develop in different directions, from Latin "divergere" (to turn aside, deviate)
