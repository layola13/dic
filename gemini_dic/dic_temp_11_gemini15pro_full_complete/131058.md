nonbanishments:/nɒnˈbænɪʃmənts/
noun
非放逐；未被驱逐的状态
The state of not being banished or exiled.
The king declared nonbanishments for all political prisoners. 国王宣布所有政治犯都不会被流放。
Their nonbanishments were conditional upon good behavior. 他们的不被驱逐是有条件的，取决于他们的良好行为。
Synonyms: pardons, reprieves, amnesties
Antonyms: banishments, exiles, deportations
