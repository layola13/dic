circumspective:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈspektɪv/ | adj. | 谨慎的，慎重的，考虑周全的 | Careful to consider all circumstances and possible consequences; cautious. | She was circumspect in her choice of words, aware of the delicate situation. 她措辞谨慎，意识到情况微妙。

The lawyer was circumspect in his advice, warning his client of the potential risks. 律师在提出建议时非常谨慎，警告他的客户潜在的风险。| 近义词：cautious, prudent, wary, discreet | 反义词：reckless, impulsive, careless, rash | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin circumspectus, past participle of circumspicere (“to look around, be cautious”), from circum (“around”) + specere (“to look”).
