go-along:/ɡəʊ əˈlɒŋ/ /ɡoʊ əˈlɔːŋ/| phrasal verb | 同意，赞同；合作；进展；陪同前往 | To agree with someone or something; to cooperate; to make progress; to accompany someone to a place. |例句：I decided to go along with their plan. 我决定同意他们的计划。|近义词：agree, cooperate, concur, accompany, proceed |反义词：disagree, oppose, resist, object |词性: 动词短语

go-along:/ɡəʊ əˈlɒŋ/ /ɡoʊ əˈlɔːŋ/| n. |（非正式）附属物，附加物|Something that is included with something else, often unexpectedly. |例句：The job came with a few go-alongs, like a company car and a phone. 这份工作有一些附加条件，比如一辆公司配车和一部电话。|近义词：addition, extra, supplement |反义词：N/A |词性: 名词
