modernday:/ˈmɒdəndeɪ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərn deɪ/ (US)| adj.|当代的，现代的|Of the present time; contemporary.|例句：Modernday technology is constantly evolving. 当代科技不断发展。|例句：Modernday artists often explore new and unconventional themes. 当代艺术家经常探索新的和非传统的主题。|近义词：contemporary, current, present-day|反义词：ancient, old-fashioned, outdated|词性:形容词

词源:  Compound of "modern" and "day." "Modern" comes from Old French "moderne," ultimately from Latin "modernus," meaning "just now, recent."  "Day" comes from Old English "dæg."
