geode:/ˈdʒiːoʊd/| n.|晶洞； geodes(复数)|A hollow rock inside which crystals have grown.|例句：The geodes were filled with sparkling amethyst crystals. 晶洞里充满了闪耀的紫水晶晶体。

例句：He collected geodes of various sizes and shapes. 他收集了各种大小和形状的晶洞。|近义词：vug, cavity, hollow|反义词:solid|词性:noun
