impedances:/ɪmˈpiːdənsɪz/| n. |阻抗（impedance的复数）|The total impedance of a circuit is the combined effect of resistance, capacitance, and inductance.|The impedances of the speakers need to match the amplifier.|近义词：resistance, reactance|反义词：admittance, conductance|词性:名词

词源: From *impedire* "to hinder, impede" (from *in-* "in, on" + *pes-* "foot") + *-ance*.
