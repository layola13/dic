nonentomologic:/ˌnɒnˌɛntəməˈlɒdʒɪk/
adjective
非昆虫学的
Not pertaining to entomology; not connected with the study of insects.
This research is nonentomologic, focusing on the behavior of primates. 这项研究不是昆虫学的，重点是灵长类动物的行为。
non-entomological
Not related to insects.
Her research interests are nonentomologic; she studies marine mammals. 她的研究兴趣与昆虫无关；她研究海洋哺乳动物。
Synonyms: non-insectological
Antonyms: entomologic, insectological
词性: adjective
