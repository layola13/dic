antipatriarchs:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriɑːrks/
n.
反父权制者，反对父权制的人
People who oppose patriarchy, a social system in which men hold primary power and predominate in roles of political leadership, moral authority, social privilege and control of property.
The antipatriarchs protested against the traditional gender roles. 反父权制者抗议传统的性别角色。
The conference was attended by many feminists and antipatriarchs. 许多女权主义者和反父权制者参加了这次会议。

近义词:feminists, egalitarians
反义词:patriarchs, traditionalists
词性:noun
