integrative:/ˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪv/| adj.|综合的，一体化的|Combining or coordinating separate elements so as to provide a harmonious, interrelated whole.|例句：An integrative approach to medicine treats the whole person, not just the disease.  综合疗法治疗的是整个人，而不仅仅是疾病。|例句：The company is adopting a more integrative strategy to streamline its operations.  这家公司正在采用更具整合性的战略来精简其运营。|近义词：holistic, comprehensive, unified, combined|反义词：divisive, fragmented, separate|词性:形容词
词源: From integrate + -ive.  From Latin integratus, past participle of integrare "to make whole," from integer "whole, untouched."
