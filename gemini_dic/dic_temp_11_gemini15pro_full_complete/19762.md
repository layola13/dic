billiards:/ˈbɪljərdz/ (US), /ˈbɪliədz/ (UK)| n. |台球；桌球；撞球|A game played on a rectangular table covered with green cloth, in which two or more balls are struck with a cue.|He enjoys playing billiards in his spare time. 他喜欢在空闲时间打台球。|Synonyms: pool, snooker|Antonyms: None|Noun
