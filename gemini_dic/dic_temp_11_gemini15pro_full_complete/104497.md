kalashnikov:/kəˈlæʃnɪkɒf/ /kəˈlæʃnɪˌkɔːf/ | n. | 卡拉什尼科夫冲锋枪；AK-47|A type of automatic assault rifle, originally designed by Mikhail Kalashnikov.  It is known for its simple design, durability, and widespread use.| The soldier carried a Kalashnikov.  这个士兵携带了一把卡拉什尼科夫冲锋枪。|Synonyms: AK-47, assault rifle, automatic rifle |Antonyms: peace (abstract opposite)|noun

Etymology: Named after its designer, Mikhail Kalashnikov (Михаил Калашников).
