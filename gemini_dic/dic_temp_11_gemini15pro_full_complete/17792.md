bedraggled:/bɪˈdræɡəld/
adjective
邋遢的，拖泥带水的
dirty and dishevelled, as from being dragged in the mud or wet
The bedraggled campers finally made it back to their campsite after getting lost in the woods.  那些浑身泥泞的露营者在树林里迷路后终于回到了营地。
近义词: dishevelled, unkempt, messy, mud-spattered
反义词: neat, tidy, groomed, immaculate
词性: adjective
