incastellated:/ɪnˈkæsəleɪtɪd/
adjective
筑有城堡的；像城堡一样的；城堡式的
Fortified with or as if with a castle.  Resembling a castle in form or structure.
The incastellated mansion stood on a hill overlooking the valley.  这座城堡式的宅邸矗立在俯瞰山谷的小山上。
The incastellated city walls provided a strong defense.  这座城市筑有城堡的城墙提供了坚固的防御。
近义词: fortified, castellated, embattled
反义词: unfortified, undefended
词性: adjective

incastellate:/ɪnˈkæsəleɪt/
verb
筑城堡于；使成城堡状
To fortify with or as if with a castle.
The king incastellated the town to protect it from invaders. 国王在城镇周围修建城堡以抵御侵略者。
They incastellated themselves in the old fortress.  他们把自己围困在古老的堡垒中。
近义词: fortify, strengthen
反义词: dismantle, weaken
词性: verb

词源:
From Late Latin *incastellatus, past participle of *incastellare "to fortify with a castle," from in- "in" + castellum "castle."
