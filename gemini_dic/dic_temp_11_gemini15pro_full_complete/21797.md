bobcats:/ˈbɑːbkæts/ | n. |山猫，猞猁|A North American wild cat (Lynx rufus) with a short tail, typically reddish brown with dark spots and bars.|例句：Bobcats are elusive and rarely seen. 山猫行踪不定，很少被人看到。

例句：The bobcat stalked its prey through the undergrowth. 山猫在灌木丛中跟踪它的猎物。|近义词：lynx, wildcat|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑domestic cat家猫作为相对的概念)|词性: noun
