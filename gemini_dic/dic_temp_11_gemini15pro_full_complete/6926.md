ancipital:/ænˈsɪpɪtəl/
adjective
两头的，两端的，双刃的
Having two edges or sharp points.
The ancipital sword was deadly in close combat.  这把双刃剑在近身格斗中是致命的。
The plant's leaves had an ancipital structure. 这种植物的叶子具有两端的结构。
近义词: two-edged, double-edged, bilateral
反义词: single-edged, unilateral
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *anci- "two-edged" + -ceps, -cipitis "head".  来自拉丁语 *anci- “双刃的” + -ceps, -cipitis “头”。
