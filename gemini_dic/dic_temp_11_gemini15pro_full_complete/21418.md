blow-horn: /ˈbloʊhɔːrn/ (US), /ˈbləʊhɔːn/ (UK)
n.|扩音器；喇叭|A portable, often hand-held, device that amplifies the voice or other sounds. |The coach used a blow-horn to direct the players during the game. 教练在比赛期间用扩音器指挥球员。|
synonyms:megaphone, loudhailer, bullhorn
antonyms:microphone (partially, as it receives sound rather than amplifying it)
词性:noun

blow-horn: /ˈbloʊhɔːrn/ (US), /ˈbləʊhɔːn/ (UK)
v.|用扩音器喊|To shout or announce something using a blow-horn.|He blow-horned the instructions to the crowd. 他用扩音器向人群喊指示。
synonyms:announce, proclaim, broadcast
antonyms:whisper, murmur
词性:verb


词源:  Compound word from "blow" (referring to the action of blowing air) and "horn" (referring to the horn-shaped device).
