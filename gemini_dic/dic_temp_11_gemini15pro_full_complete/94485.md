importable:/ɪmˈpɔːrtəbl/ | adj. |可进口的，可输入的| Able to be imported; suitable for import. |  Example 1:  Many foreign fruits are importable to this country. 许多外国水果可以进口到这个国家。

Example 2: The software makes data importable from various sources.  这款软件可以从各种来源导入数据。|Synonyms: admissible,  allowable,  permitted |Antonyms: non-importable, inadmissible, prohibited |词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From import + -able.  "Import" comes from the Latin "importare," meaning "to bring in."  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or suitability.
