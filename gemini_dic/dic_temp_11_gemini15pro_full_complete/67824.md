eyewitnesses:/ˈaɪwɪtnɪsɪz/| n. |目击者；见证人|A person who has personally seen an event and can give a first-hand account of it.|The eyewitnesses to the robbery gave conflicting accounts of what happened.  抢劫案的目击者对发生的事情说法不一。|synonyms: observer, onlooker, witness, spectator|antonyms:  none (The concept doesn't have a direct opposite.)|noun
