counterculture:/ˈkaʊntərˌkʌltʃər/| n. |反文化|A way of life and set of attitudes opposed to or at variance with the prevailing social norm.|The hippie movement of the 1960s is a prime example of a counterculture.|Synonyms: subculture, underground, alternative culture|Antonyms: mainstream culture, dominant culture|Noun
