orthopnea:/ɔːrˈθɒpniːə/ /ɔːrˈθɑːpniːə/
noun
呼吸困难;端坐呼吸
Shortness of breath (dyspnea) that occurs when lying flat, causing the person to have to sleep propped up in bed or sitting in a chair.
The patient complained of orthopnea and had to sleep sitting up.  病人抱怨呼吸困难，不得不坐着睡觉。
近义词:dyspnea
反义词:(no direct antonym, consider eupnea - normal breathing)
词性:n.
