ebrillade:/ˌebrɪˈlɑːd/
noun
炫耀武力; 炫耀;  炫耀武力(尤指挥舞武器)
A flamboyant and often gratuitous display of martial skill or weaponry, especially with the intent of intimidation or showing off.
The knight's ebrillade with his sword impressed the ladies of the court. 这位骑士炫耀剑术，给宫廷里的女士们留下了深刻的印象。
Synonyms: flourish, display, brandishing, show, parade
Antonyms: concealment, hiding, modesty, understatement
