coprincipal:/koʊˈprɪnsəpəl/
| adj. n.| 共同负责人，共同委托人 |A coprincipal is a person or entity that shares equal responsibility or authority with another in a particular undertaking, especially in a loan or other financial agreement.| John and Mary are coprincipals on the mortgage.  约翰和玛丽是抵押贷款的共同负责人。|The bank listed both partners as coprincipals on the business loan.  银行将两位合伙人都列为商业贷款的共同委托人。|近义词：joint principal, co-obligor|反义词:|adjective, noun
