eponyme:/ˈɛpəˌnɪm/ | n. | 名词 |同名人，命名来源|A person after whom a discovery, invention, place, etc., is named or thought to be named. |  Example:  The sandwich is an eponym, named after the Earl of Sandwich. 三明治是一个同名词，以三明治伯爵命名。

Example:  Alzheimer's disease is an eponym named after Alois Alzheimer. 阿尔茨海默病是以阿洛伊斯·阿尔茨海默的名字命名的。 | 近义词: namesake | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
