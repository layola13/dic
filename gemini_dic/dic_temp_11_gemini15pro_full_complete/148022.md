overroast: /ˌoʊvərˈroʊst/ | v. adj.| 烤过头，烤过火 | to roast (something) for too long| I overroasted the chicken and it was dry. 我把鸡烤过头了，很干。| The overroasted vegetables were inedible. 烤过头的蔬菜不能吃了。 |近义词：burn, scorch, char |反义词: undercook, underroast |词性: 动词 & 形容词


Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "roast" (Middle English rosten, from Old French rostir, of Germanic origin).
