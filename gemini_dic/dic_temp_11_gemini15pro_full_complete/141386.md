operculum:/oʊˈpɜːrkjʊləm/ /əˈpɜːrkjʊləm/
n.
盖, 覆盖物, 鳃盖
A lid or covering, especially one on a plant or animal.  Specifically, the bony flap covering and protecting the gills of fishes.
The operculum of the snail helps it to retain moisture.  蜗牛的盖有助于保持水分。

The fish opened and closed its operculum as it breathed.  鱼呼吸时打开和关闭它的鳃盖。

近义词:covering, lid, flap, plate
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "opening")
词性:noun


operculum:/oʊˈpɜːrkjʊləm/ /əˈpɜːrkjʊləm/
n.
(植物学)蒴盖
The lid-like covering of a moss capsule or other plant structure.
The operculum of the moss capsule detached, releasing the spores. 苔藓蒴果的盖子脱落，释放出孢子。

The botanist carefully examined the operculum of the seed pod. 植物学家仔细检查了种荚的盖子。


近义词:cap, lid
反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:noun
