heterotopia:/ˌhɛtərəˈtoʊpiə/ | n. | 异位，异位症 | The presence of a particular tissue or organ in an abnormal anatomical location.  |  Example: Gray matter heterotopia is a neurological disorder characterized by misplaced gray matter in the brain.  大脑灰质异位是一种以大脑灰质错位为特征的神经系统疾病。

Example:  The doctor discovered a heterotopia in the patient's liver during the ultrasound. 医生在超声检查中发现了病人肝脏中的异位组织。 | Synonym: ectopia, displacement, malposition | Antonym: orthotopia | noun


heterotopism:/ˌhɛtərəˈtoʊpɪzəm/ | n. | 异位性，异位现象 | The condition or state of being heterotopic; the displacement of an organ or tissue from its normal position. | Example: Heterotopism of the pancreas can sometimes cause digestive problems. 胰腺异位有时会导致消化问题。

Example: The study investigated the causes of heterotopism in embryonic development.  这项研究调查了胚胎发育过程中异位的原因。| Synonym: ectopia, displacement, malposition | Antonym: orthotopia | noun
