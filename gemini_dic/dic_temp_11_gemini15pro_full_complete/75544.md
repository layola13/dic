fuchi:/ˈfuːtʃiː/
n.
fuchi (刀剑装具)
The fuchi is a decorative metal collar or ferrule between the tsuka (hilt) and the habaki (blade collar) of a Japanese sword.
The fuchi on this katana is decorated with a gold inlay of a dragon. 这把武士刀的缘头用金子镶嵌了一条龙。
