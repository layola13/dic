faxable:/ˈfæksəbl/
adjective
可传真的
able to be sent by fax.
This document is faxable.  这份文件可以传真。
The contract is faxable, so please send it over as soon as possible.  合同可以传真，请尽快发过来。
近义词:transmittable
反义词:non-faxable
词性:adjective
