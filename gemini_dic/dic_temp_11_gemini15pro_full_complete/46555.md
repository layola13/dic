dalliers:/ˈdæliərz/| n.| 闲荡者，拖延者 | People who dawdle or delay, especially those who linger or procrastinate. | The dalliers missed the bus because they were too busy chatting. 闲荡者错过了公共汽车，因为他们忙于聊天。| 近义词：lingerers, loiterers, procrastinators, idlers | 反义词：hustlers, go-getters | Noun

dallied:/ˈdælid/ | v. | 闲荡，玩耍，拖延 | To waste time or delay, especially by lingering or procrastinating. | He dallied on the way to school and ended up being late. 他在上学的路上闲逛，结果迟到了。 | 近义词：linger, loiter, procrastinate, dawdle | 反义词：hurry, hasten | Verb

dallying:/ˈdæliɪŋ/ | n. | 闲荡，玩耍，拖延  | The act of wasting time or delaying. | His dallying cost him the opportunity. 他的拖延使他失去了这个机会。 | 近义词：lingering, loitering, procrastinating, dawdling | 反义词：hurrying, hastening | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *dalier, frequentative of daler "to chat," of uncertain origin.
