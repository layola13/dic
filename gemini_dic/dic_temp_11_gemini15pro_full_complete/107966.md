laser-scan:/ˈleɪzər skæn/ | n. | 激光扫描 | The process of using a laser beam to systematically sweep over an object or area to collect data about its shape, size, or other characteristics.  |  The researchers used laser-scan technology to create a 3D model of the ancient artifact. 研究人员使用激光扫描技术创建了古代文物的 3D 模型。 | synonyms: laser ranging, lidar scanning | antonyms: N/A | noun

laser-scan:/ˈleɪzər skæn/ | v. | 激光扫描 | To use a laser beam to systematically sweep over an object or area to collect data. |  They laser-scanned the entire building to create a detailed floor plan.  他们用激光扫描了整栋建筑，以创建详细的平面图。 | synonyms: laser range, lidar scan | antonyms: N/A | verb
