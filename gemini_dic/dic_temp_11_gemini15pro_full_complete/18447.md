bemeet: /biːmiːt/
verb
相遇；相识；邂逅
To encounter or meet someone, especially unexpectedly or after a period of time.
例句：I bemet my old friend at the conference. 我在会议上遇到了我的老朋友。

例句：We bemet by chance at the airport. 我们在机场偶然相遇。

近义词：encounter, meet, run into, come across
反义词：avoid, miss, part
词性：v.


词源：
"bemeet" is not a standard English word. It likely arises from a blend of "be" and "meet," aiming to express the concept of encountering someone. While "meet" sufficiently covers this meaning, adding "be" is redundant and grammatically incorrect.
