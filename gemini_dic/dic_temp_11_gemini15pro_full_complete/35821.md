cockiness:/ˈkɒkɪnɪs/ (US) /ˈkɑːkinɪs/| n. |自负，骄傲自大|The quality of being cocky; arrogance or overconfidence.|His cockiness irritated many of his colleagues.|近义词：arrogance, swagger, presumption, conceitedness|反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence|词性: noun

词源: From cocky + -ness.  "Cocky" originally referred to a rooster's strutting and confident demeanor, derived from "cock" (male chicken).  The "-ness" suffix transforms the adjective "cocky" into the noun "cockiness."
