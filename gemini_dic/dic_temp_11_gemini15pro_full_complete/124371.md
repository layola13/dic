motive:/ˈmoʊtɪv/ (US) /ˈməʊtɪv/ (UK)| n. | 动机，目的，主题 | Something that causes a person to act in a particular way; a reason for doing something. |  The police are still trying to establish a motive for the murder. 警方仍在试图确定谋杀的动机。 | synonyms: reason, purpose, incentive, impetus | antonyms: discouragement, deterrent | noun

motive:/ˈmoʊtɪv/ (US) /ˈməʊtɪv/ (UK)| adj. |  促使…的，激发…的 | Producing motion or action.  (Less common usage) | The motive power of the engine was steam.  这台发动机的动力是蒸汽。 | synonyms: driving, activating, impelling | antonyms: hindering, obstructing | adjective
