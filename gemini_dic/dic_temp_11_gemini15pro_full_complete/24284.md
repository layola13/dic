brigandine:/ˈbrɪɡənˌdaɪn/| n.|锁子甲， бригантина (brigantina - Russian)|A piece of medieval armor consisting of small metal plates riveted to fabric or leather.|The knight wore a brigandine under his surcoat for added protection. 骑士在外衣下穿了一件锁子甲以加强保护。|Synonyms: coat of mail, hauberk, byrnie|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "civilian clothing")|Noun
