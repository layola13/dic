nontimbered:/ˌnɒnˈtɪmbəd/
adjective
未砍伐的；非木材的
Not covered with timber trees; not forested.  Relating to forest products other than timber.
The nontimbered areas were used for grazing.  未砍伐的区域用于放牧。
The study focused on the sustainable harvesting of nontimbered forest products.  这项研究的重点是可持续地获取非木材的森林产品。
Synonyms:  untimbered,  treeless,  open, clear
Antonyms:  timbered, forested, wooded
词性: adjective
