onlooker:/ˈɒnˌlʊkər/ (UK), /ˈɑːnˌlʊkər/ (US)| n. |旁观者，观众|A person who watches something that is happening but is not involved in it.|Many onlookers gathered at the scene of the accident. 许多旁观者聚集在事故现场。|
The onlookers cheered as the parade passed by.  当游行队伍经过时，旁观者们欢呼起来。
|Synonyms: spectator, observer, witness, bystander|Antonyms: participant, player|noun
