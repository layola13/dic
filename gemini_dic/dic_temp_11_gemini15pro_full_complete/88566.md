hitchhikes:/ˈhɪtʃhaɪks/ | v. | 搭便车旅行；免费搭车 | To travel by asking for free rides from passing vehicles. | He hitchhiked across Europe last summer.  他去年夏天搭便车游遍了欧洲。| synonyms: thumb a ride, bum a ride | antonyms: drive, fly, walk | verb

hitchhike:/ˈhɪtʃhaɪk/ | n. | 搭便车旅行 | A journey made by hitchhiking. |  She went on a hitchhike across the country. 她搭便车环游全国。| synonyms: free ride, lift | antonyms: paid transportation | noun
