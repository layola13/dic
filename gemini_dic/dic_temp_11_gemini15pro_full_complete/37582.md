commutative:/kəˈmjuːtətɪv/ | adj. | 交换的，可交换的 |  Relating to a binary operation in which the order of the operands does not affect the result.  |  Addition is commutative: 2 + 3 = 3 + 2. 加法是可交换的：2 + 3 = 3 + 2. | synonyms: interchangeable, permutable | antonyms: noncommutative, non-interchangeable | word type: adjective
