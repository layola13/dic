confess:/kənˈfɛs/ | v. | 承认，坦白，忏悔 | To admit or acknowledge something reluctantly, typically a fault or wrongdoing. | I must confess, I didn't do my homework. 我必须承认，我没有做作业。 | admit, acknowledge, own up | deny, conceal | verb
