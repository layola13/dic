maldanian: /mælˈdeɪniən/ | adj. n. | 马尔丹尼亚的；马尔丹科的；马尔丹科动物 | Relating to or belonging to the Maldanidae, a family of marine polychaete worms.  Also, a member of this family. |  例句：Maldanian worms are found in marine environments worldwide. 马尔丹蠕虫遍布全球的海洋环境。

例句：The study focused on the feeding habits of maldanian polychaetes. 这项研究的重点是马尔丹多毛类的摄食习性。 | 近义词：polychaete, bamboo worm (for some species) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  Named after Maldane, a genus within the Maldanidae family.  The genus name likely derives from a mythological figure or place, but the precise origin is obscure.
