dressily:/ˈdrɛsɪli/| adv.|衣着华丽地，衣着讲究地|In a dressy manner; smartly or elegantly.  Wearing clothes suitable for a formal occasion.|She was dressily attired for the gala. 她为了晚会盛装打扮。|近义词：smartly, elegantly, fashionably, stylishly|反义词：casually, plainly, simply|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From dress + -ly.  "Dress" comes from Old French "dresser" meaning "to straighten, arrange, prepare".
