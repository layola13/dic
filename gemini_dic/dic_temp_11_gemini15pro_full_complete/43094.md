cowslipping:/ˈkaʊslɪpɪŋ/
n.
報春花盛開;長滿報春花的
The activity or time of cowslips being in bloom.  Cowslipping typically refers to the spring season when these flowers are prevalent.
The meadows were ablaze with cowslipping. 草地上一片报春花盛开。
近义词:blooming, flowering
反义词:wilting, fading
词性:名詞

cowslipping:/ˈkaʊslɪpɪŋ/
adj.
開滿報春花的
Covered with or abundant in cowslips.
The cowslipping meadows were a beautiful sight. 開滿報春花的草地是一道美丽的風景。
近义词:blooming, flowering
反义词:barren, desolate
词性:形容詞

词源:  From "cowslip" (the flower) + "-ing" (suffix indicating an action or process).  "Cowslip" itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from "cow slop" referring to cow dung, perhaps because the plant was thought to grow in pastures where cows had grazed.
