abortive:/əˈbɔːrtɪv/
adjective
失败的，夭折的
Failing to produce the intended result.
例句：After several abortive attempts to start a business, he finally succeeded.  经过几次创业失败后，他终于成功了。

例句：The abortive coup attempt resulted in many arrests.  那次失败的政变导致许多人被捕。

近义词：unsuccessful, failed, fruitless, futile
反义词：successful, fruitful, productive
词性：adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin abortivus, from abort- ‘miscarry’, from ab- ‘away, from’ + oriri ‘arise, be born’.
