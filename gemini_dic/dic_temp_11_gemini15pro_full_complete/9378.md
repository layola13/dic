antiquarks:/ˈæntikwɔːrks/ | n. | 反夸克 |  A subatomic particle that is the antiparticle of a quark.  Antiquarks have the same mass and spin as their corresponding quarks, but opposite charge and other quantum numbers. |  Scientists study antiquarks to understand the fundamental nature of matter. 科学家研究反夸克以了解物质的基本性质。|  Antimatter is composed of antiquarks. 反物质由反夸克组成。 | synonyms: antiparticle | antonyms: quark | noun
