annexable:/əˈnɛksəbl/
adjective
可吞并的; 可附加的
Capable of being annexed; able to be added or attached.
The small kingdom was annexable by its larger neighbor. 这个小王国可以被它更大的邻居吞并。
The document includes an annexable appendix.  该文件包含一个可附加的附录。
synonyms: attachable, appendable, incorporable, addible
antonyms: unattachable, independent, separate
词性: adjective
