laminating:/ˈlæmɪneɪtɪŋ/
n.|层压，叠片，覆膜|The process of applying a thin layer of protective material to a surface. |The laminating of the documents prevented them from tearing. 文件的覆膜防止了它们被撕裂。|coating, covering|removal|noun

laminate:/ˈlæmɪneɪt/
v.|层压，叠片，覆膜|To apply a thin layer of protective material to a surface. |I need to laminate these posters so they last longer. 我需要把这些海报覆膜，这样它们就能保存更久。|coat, cover|remove, strip|verb

laminate:/ˈlæmɪneɪt/
n.|层压材料，层压板|A material made by bonding together thin layers of material. |The table is made of laminate. 这张桌子是由层压板制成的。|veneer, plywood|solid wood|noun

laminated:/ˈlæmɪneɪtɪd/
adj.|层压的，叠片的，覆膜的|Covered with a thin layer of protective material. |The laminated floor is easy to clean. 覆膜地板很容易清洁。|coated, covered|bare, uncovered|adjective
