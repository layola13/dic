heckle:/ˈhɛkəl/| v. n.|起哄，诘问； heckling (n.)|to interrupt a public speaker with derisive or aggressive comments or questions.|The comedian was heckled by a drunk audience member.  那个喜剧演员被一个喝醉的观众起哄。|The politician tried to ignore the heckling from the crowd. 这位政治家试图无视人群的诘问。|近义词：jeer, taunt, interrupt, badger|反义词:support, praise, encourage|词性:动词, 名词

词源: From Middle Scots *hekelen, of uncertain origin.  Possibly a frequentative of *hek, an imitation of the sound of clearing one's throat.
