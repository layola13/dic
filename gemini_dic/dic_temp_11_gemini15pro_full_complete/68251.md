faithfully:/ˈfeɪθfəli/| adv.|忠实地，忠诚地，如实地|In a faithful manner; in a way that is loyal and true to someone or something.  Showing accuracy or exactness; precisely.|She faithfully attended every meeting. 她忠实地参加了每次会议。|近义词：loyally, truthfully, accurately, diligently, conscientiously|反义词：disloyally, untruthfully, inaccurately, negligently|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *feithfulli, equivalent to faithful +‎ -ly.
