miniatures:/ˈmɪnɪtʃərz/ |n.|微缩模型；缩图；微型画；微型人（或物）|A miniature version of something is much smaller than the usual size.  A miniature is a very small painting, usually of a person.| She collects miniatures of vintage cars. 她收藏老爷车的微缩模型。| synonyms: model, replica, reduction,  diminutive | antonyms: enlargement, magnification | noun
miniatures:/ˈmɪnɪtʃərz/ |adj.|微型的；缩小的|Extremely small.|  She bought a miniature tea set for her dollhouse. 她为她的玩具屋买了一套微型茶具。| synonyms: tiny, small, minute, diminutive | antonyms: giant, huge, enormous | adjective
