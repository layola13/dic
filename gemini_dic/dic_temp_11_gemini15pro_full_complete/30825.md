cha-cha-chaed:/ˌtʃɑː tʃɑː tʃɑːd/| v.|跳恰恰舞|danced the cha-cha-cha; performed the cha-cha-cha.  Derived from the name of the dance itself, "cha-cha-chá."|例句：They cha-cha-chaed the night away. 他们跳了一整晚的恰恰。|近义词：danced, swayed, grooved|反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "stood still")|词性:动词
