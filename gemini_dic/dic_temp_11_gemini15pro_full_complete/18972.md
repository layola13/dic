bestiaries:/ˈbɛstiəriːz/
n.
动物寓言集，怪兽图鉴
A collection of descriptions of real and fabled animals, often illustrated, and typically giving symbolic interpretations of the animals' behavior.
Medieval bestiaries were filled with descriptions of both real and mythical creatures. 中世纪的动物寓言集里充满了对真实和神话生物的描述。
compendiums, collections, catalogues
(No direct antonym exists, but perhaps "realistic fauna guides")
noun
