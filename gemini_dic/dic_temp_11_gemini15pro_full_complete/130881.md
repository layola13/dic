nonarmament:/ˌnɒnˈɑːməmənt/
noun
裁军，解除武装
The absence of armament; the reduction or abolition of military weapons.
The group advocated for complete nonarmament as a path to world peace.  这个团体主张全面裁军，以此作为通往世界和平的道路。
The policy of nonarmament was debated fiercely in the parliament.  裁军政策在议会中引发了激烈的辩论。
Synonyms: disarmament, demilitarization
Antonyms: armament, militarization
