fustian:/ˈfʌstiən/| n. adj.|浮夸的语言；粗斜纹布|Pompous or inflated language; a type of heavy twilled cotton cloth.|His speech was full of fustian and empty promises. 他的演讲充满了浮夸的语言和空洞的承诺。|近义词：bombast, rant, claptrap, verbose, pompous|反义词：conciseness, simplicity, sincerity|noun
fustian:/ˈfʌstiən/| adj.|夸大的；虚夸的|Characterized by pretentious or inflated language.|The politician's fustian rhetoric failed to impress the voters.  这位政客浮夸的言辞未能打动选民。|近义词：bombastic, inflated, pompous|反义词：simple, unpretentious, down-to-earth|adjective
