martha:/ˈmɑːrθə/ (US), /ˈmɑːθə/ (UK)| n. |玛莎|A female given name, of Aramaic origin, meaning "lady" or "mistress".  It is a biblical name, borne by the sister of Lazarus and Mary of Bethany.|Martha is a common name in many Western countries. 玛莎在许多西方国家是一个常见的名字。|Mary, Elizabeth, Sarah|None|noun
