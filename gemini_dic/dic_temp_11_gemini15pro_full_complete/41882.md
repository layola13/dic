cosherer:/ˈkəʊʃərər/| n.|犹太教律法监督员；持戒严格的犹太人|A person who observes the Jewish dietary laws or who supervises the preparation of food in accordance with those laws. |例句：The caterer hired a cosherer to oversee the preparation of the meal for the Bar Mitzvah.  宴会承办商聘请了一位犹太教律法监督员来监督犹太成年礼餐的准备工作。

例句：My grandmother was a strict cosherer, never mixing meat and dairy. 我的祖母是一位严格遵守犹太教饮食戒律的人，从不混用肉类和奶制品。|近义词：kashrut observer, mashgiach|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who disregards kashrut)|词性: noun
