berm:/bɜːrm/| n.|边缘；路肩；护堤；矮墙|A raised bank or wall, typically made of earth or gravel, alongside a road, railroad track, or canal, often serving as a protective barrier or path.|The cyclists rode along the berm of the canal.  骑自行车的人沿着运河的边缘骑行。|The soldiers built a berm to protect their camp. 士兵们修筑了一个矮墙来保护他们的营地。|Synonyms: bank, embankment, levee, shoulder|Antonyms: ditch, trench|noun
