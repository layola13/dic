chalkest:/ˈtʃɔːkɪst/
verb|用粉笔写或画; 用白垩覆盖|You chalkest the lines on the court before the game. 你在比赛前用粉笔在球场上画线。| 近义词: write with chalk, draw with chalk| 反义词: erase, wipe |词性: 动词


Etymology: From Middle English *chalken, from Old English *cealcian (“to chalk, mark with chalk”), from cealc (“chalk”).
