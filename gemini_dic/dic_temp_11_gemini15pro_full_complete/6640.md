anaesthetises:/ænˈiːsθətaɪzɪz/ /əˈnɛsθəˌtaɪzɪz/
verb
麻醉
To administer an anesthetic to (a person or animal), especially so as to induce a loss of sensation.
The vet anaesthetised the cat before the operation. 兽医在手术前给猫麻醉了。
Synonyms: numb, drug, sedate
Antonyms: stimulate, awaken
词性: 动词(verb)
