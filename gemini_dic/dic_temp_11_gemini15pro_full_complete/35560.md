词源：
From French *coalition*, from Latin *coālitiō* (“union, alliance”), from *coālitus*, past participle of *coālēscō* (“grow together, unite”), from *co-* (com-, “together”) + *alēscō* (“grow, be nourished”). Related to *adolescent*.
