gating:/ˈɡeɪtɪŋ/| n. |门控，选通，控制|The process of controlling the flow of something, such as information or electricity.|Example: The gating of ion channels is essential for nerve impulse transmission. 离子通道的门控对神经冲动的传递至关重要。|Example:  The software allows for precise gating of the audio signal.  这个软件允许对音频信号进行精确的门控。|Synonyms: controlling, regulating, modulating|Antonyms: opening, releasing|noun


gating:/ˈɡeɪtɪŋ/| adj. |门控的，选通的|Relating to or involving a gate or gating.|Example: Gating circuits are used in electronics. 门控电路用于电子学中。|Example:  The researchers studied the gating mechanism of the protein. 研究人员研究了蛋白质的门控机制。|Synonyms: controlled, regulated |Antonyms: uncontrolled, unregulated|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From gate + -ing.  The word "gate" itself comes from Old English "geat" meaning "gate, opening, passage."
