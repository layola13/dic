angus:/ˈæŋɡəs/| n.|安格斯牛（一种肉牛）|A breed of hornless, black beef cattle originating in Scotland.|例句：The Angus steak was cooked to perfection. 安格斯牛排做得非常完美。

例句：Angus cattle are known for their high-quality marbling. 安格斯牛以其高质量的大理石纹理而闻名。|近义词：beef cattle, steer|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑dairy cattle 奶牛)|词性:noun
