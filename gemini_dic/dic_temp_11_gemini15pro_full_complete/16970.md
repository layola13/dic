bastardising:/ˈbæstərdaɪzɪŋ/
verb
篡改，歪曲，劣化
corrupting or debased something, typically an artistic work or other product of human endeavor, by altering or adapting it in a crude or tasteless way.
The film bastardised the original novel. 这部电影歪曲了原著小说。
synonyms: corrupting, debasing, degrading, perverting, distorting, adulterating, contaminating, spoiling, ruining, wrecking, defiling
antonyms: preserving, improving, enhancing
verb

被篡改的，被歪曲的，被劣化的
