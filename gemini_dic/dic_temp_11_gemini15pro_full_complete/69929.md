fervidness:/ˈfɜːrvɪdnəs/| n.|热情; 热烈; 炽热|The fervidness of his belief was inspiring.  他的信仰的热情令人鼓舞。|例句：The fervidness of her love was evident in every letter she wrote. 她写的每一封信都流露出她炽热的爱情。|近义词：passion, ardor, zeal, intensity|反义词：apathy, indifference, coldness|词性:名词

fervid:/ˈfɜːrvɪd/| adj.|热情的; 热烈的; 炽热的|He delivered a fervid speech about the importance of education. 他发表了关于教育重要性的热情洋溢的演讲。|例句：She has a fervid imagination. 她有着丰富的想象力。|近义词：passionate, ardent, zealous, intense|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, cold|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fervidus, from Latin fervere "to boil, glow," from PIE root *bhreu- "to boil, bubble, effervesce." The noun fervidness is formed by adding the suffix -ness.
