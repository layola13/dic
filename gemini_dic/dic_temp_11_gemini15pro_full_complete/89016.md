hollowness:/ˈhɒləʊnəs/ (UK), /ˈhɑːloʊnəs/ (US)| n.|空虚，空洞，空心|A state of being empty inside, either physically or emotionally; a lack of substance or meaning.|The hollowness of the tree trunk surprised him.  树干的空心让他感到惊讶。|The hollowness in her voice betrayed her grief. 她空洞的声音暴露了她的悲伤。|近义词：emptiness, void, vacuum, vacancy|反义词: fullness, solidity, substance|词性:noun
