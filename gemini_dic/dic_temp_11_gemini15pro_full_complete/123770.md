moor:/mʊər/ (UK) /mɔːr/ (US)| n.| 沼地，荒野|A tract of open uncultivated upland, typically covered with heather.|The hikers got lost on the moors.  徒行者在沼地上迷路了。|Synonyms: heath, wasteland, bog|Antonyms: meadow, grassland|noun


moor:/mʊər/ (UK) /mɔːr/ (US)| v.| 停泊(船)|To secure (a vessel) in a particular place, as by cables and anchors or by lines.|We moored the boat to the dock. 我们把船停泊在码头。|Synonyms: dock, berth, anchor|Antonyms: unmoor, cast off|verb
