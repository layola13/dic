nonlyricalnesses:/ˌnɒnˈlɪrɪkəlnəsɪz/
n.|非抒情性；缺乏抒情性|The nonlyricalnesses of the report made it difficult to connect with emotionally. 这份报告缺乏抒情性，难以产生情感共鸣。|The nonlyricalnesses of his prose contrasted sharply with the poetic nature of his earlier works.  他的散文的非抒情性与他早期作品的诗意形成了鲜明的对比。|近义词:prosaicness, matter-of-factness, unpoeticalness|反义词:lyricism, poeticalness|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix) + lyrical + -ness (suffix)
"non-"  表示否定，"lyrical" 指的是抒情的，"-ness" 将形容词转换为名词，表示状态或性质。
