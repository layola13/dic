dramatization:/ˌdræmətaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌdrɑːmətaɪˈzeɪʃn/
n.
戏剧化；改编成戏剧；夸张
The act of dramatizing something; a dramatic representation or adaptation.
The dramatization of the novel for television was very successful.  这部小说改编成电视剧非常成功。

近义词:adaptation, enactment, performance, portrayal, presentation, production, rendition, version
反义词:nondescriptness
