abjudication:/əˌdʒuːdɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
裁决，判决，剥夺
The formal giving or pronouncing of a judgment or decree in a court of law; also, the judgment given.  A judicial decision or sentence. The act of depriving someone of rights or property by judicial decision.
The abjudication of the case took several months due to its complexity.  这个案件的判决由于其复杂性而花费了几个月的时间。

The abjudication declared him bankrupt. 法院的判决宣布他破产。

近义词:judgment, decree, ruling, decision, sentence, adjudication
反义词:indecision, acquittal, pardon

词性:noun

词源:Late Middle English: from Latin abjudicatio(n-), from the verb abjudicare, from ab- ‘away from’ + judicare ‘to judge’.
