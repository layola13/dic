laciniate: /ləˈsɪnieɪt/ | adj. | 撕裂的，边缘细裂的，深裂的 | Divided into narrow, irregular lobes or segments; fringed. |  The laciniate leaves of the dandelion are easily recognizable. 蒲公英的撕裂状叶子很容易辨认。|  The fern's laciniate fronds created a delicate pattern. 蕨类植物深裂的叶子构成了一种精致的图案。|  fringed, jagged, serrated, incised | entire, smooth | adjective
