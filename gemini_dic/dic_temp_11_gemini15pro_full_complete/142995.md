outbawls:/ˌaʊtˈbɔːlz/
verb
大声哭喊，胜过哭
cry louder than; surpass in crying
She outbawled her brother when their mother refused to buy them candy. 她大声哭喊，胜过她弟弟，因为他们的母亲拒绝给他们买糖果。
outcry, outshout, outwail

v.
