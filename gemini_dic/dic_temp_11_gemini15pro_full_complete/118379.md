merle:/mɜːl/| n. adj.| Merle, 梅尔, 斑点，杂色的|A dark bluish-gray or mottled coloring, as of the coat of a bird or a dog.  Also, an animal with such coloring.  Derived from the Old French "merle" meaning "blackbird."|The breeder specializes in merle Australian Shepherds.  这个饲养员专门饲养梅尔澳洲牧羊犬。|近义词：mottled, dappled, piebald, skewbald|反义词: solid-colored|词性:名词, 形容词
