dodge-out:/ˈdɑːdʒ aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 躲避，逃避，溜走 | To leave or exit a place quickly and discreetly, often to avoid something or someone. | He dodged out of the meeting before the boss could ask him any questions. 他在老板问他问题之前就溜出了会议。| 近义词：slip out, sneak out, duck out | 反义词: enter, confront | 词性: 动词短语

dodge:/dɑːdʒ/ | v. | 躲避，闪避 | To avoid something by moving quickly. |  He dodged the ball thrown at him. 他躲开了扔向他的球。 | 近义词: evade, avoid, elude | 反义词: confront, face | 词性: 动词

out:/aʊt/ | adv., adj., prep., n. | 出去，外面的，在外 | Outside, not in; away from home, work, etc. |  He went out for a walk. 他出去散步了。| 近义词: outside, away | 反义词: in, inside | 词性: 副词，形容词，介词，名词
