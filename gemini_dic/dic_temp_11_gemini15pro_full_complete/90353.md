hovelling:/ˈhʌvəlɪŋ/ | v. | (尤指鸟类)翱翔；盘旋，徘徊；(直升机)悬停 | The helicopter hovelled over the landing pad. 直升机在停机坪上空盘旋。|近义词：hovering, soaring, lingering|反义词:  diving, plunging | 词性: 动名词 (present participle of the verb "hovel")


词源: "Hovelling" comes from the verb "hovel," which originally meant "to dwell in a small, mean dwelling." The sense of "hovering" developed from the idea of staying in one place, like a bird over its nest.  The word is now mostly replaced by "hovering."
