impenetrableness:/ɪmˌpenɪtrəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
不能穿透；不可理解；难以接近
the state of not allowing anything to pass through
The impenetrableness of the jungle made it difficult to explore.  这片丛林的难以穿透性使得它难以被探索。
impenetrability, impermeability, opacity
permeability, penetrability, transparency
noun
