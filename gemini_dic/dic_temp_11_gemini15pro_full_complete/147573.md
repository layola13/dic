overpersecuted:/ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrsɪˌkjuːtɪd/
|adj.|过度迫害的，受迫害过度的|
|Meaning: Persecuted to an excessive degree.  Subjected to more persecution than is warranted or just.|
|Example 1: The overpersecuted religious minority finally found refuge in a neighboring country.  这个受迫害过度的宗教少数群体最终在邻国找到了避难所。|
|Example 2:  Feeling overpersecuted by the media, the celebrity retreated from public life. 感觉被媒体过度迫害，这位名人退出了公众生活。|
|近义词:oppressed, victimized, hounded, persecuted|
|反义词:protected, sheltered, championed|
|词性:adjective|

overpersecuted (词源)
|Prefix: over- (meaning "excessively," "too much")|
|Root: persecute (from Latin persecutus, past participle of persequi "to follow, pursue, hunt down")|
