azury:/ˈæʒəri/| adj.|天蓝色的，蔚蓝色的|Of a bright blue color like a clear sky.|The azury waters of the Aegean Sea sparkled in the sunlight. 爱琴海蔚蓝的海水在阳光下波光粼粼。|近义词：azure, cerulean, sky-blue|反义词:dark, black|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old French *azurin, from Medieval Latin *azurinus, diminutive of azurum "azure," from Arabic lāzaward "lapis lazuli."
