australes:/ɔːˈstreɪliːz/ |adj.|南方的，南部的|Relating to the south; southern.  Often used in scientific contexts, particularly in taxonomy (the science of classification of organisms).|Example: The *Eucalyptus australis* is a species of tree found in eastern Australia.  澳洲桉树是一种生长在澳大利亚东部的树种。

Example: The aurora australis is a beautiful natural phenomenon.  南极光是一种美丽的自然现象。|Synonym: southern, meridional|Antonym: boreal, northern|adjective
