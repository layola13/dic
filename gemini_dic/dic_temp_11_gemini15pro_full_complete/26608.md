caches:/kæʃɪz/| n.|缓存，高速缓存|A component that transparently stores data so that future requests for that data can be served faster. |The website uses caches to speed up loading times.  该网站使用缓存来加快加载速度。|近义词：buffers, stores|反义词:|noun


caches:/ˈkæʃɪz/| v.|缓存，隐藏|To store something in a hidden or inaccessible place. |He cached the stolen jewels in a secret compartment. 他把偷来的珠宝藏在一个秘密隔间里。|近义词：conceal, hide, stash|反义词: reveal, expose|verb
