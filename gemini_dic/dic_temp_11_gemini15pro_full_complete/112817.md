lucian:/ˈluːʃən/ | n. |  卢西恩（男子名）;  路西安（男子名）| A male given name, derived from the Roman cognomen Lucianus, meaning "light-bringer".  It is ultimately derived from the Latin word "lux" (light).|  Lucian is a popular name in Romania.  卢西恩在罗马尼亚是个很受欢迎的名字。| Lucian painted a beautiful landscape.  卢西恩画了一幅美丽的风景画。 |Synonyms:  (for the name itself, no direct synonyms, but similar names might include Lucius, Lucas) | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
