nondeductibles:/ˌnɒndɪˈdʌktɪblz/
noun
不可扣除额，不可扣除项目
Amounts that you cannot deduct from your taxable income.  These are expenses that you must pay out of pocket.
My health insurance plan has high nondeductibles, so I have to pay a lot before the insurance kicks in. 我的健康保险计划有很高的不可扣除额，所以我在保险生效前必须支付很多费用。
non-deductible expenses, unrecoverable expenses
deductibles, claimable expenses
n.
