hardcovers:/ˈhɑːrdˌkʌvərz/| n.|精装书|Books with stiff covers, typically made of cardboard covered with cloth, leather, or other durable material.  These are often considered more durable and higher-quality than paperbacks.|The bookstore had a wide selection of hardcovers.|近义词：hardback, casebound book|反义词:paperback|noun

hardcover:/ˈhɑːrdˌkʌvər/| adj.|精装的|Having a stiff cover, typically made of cardboard covered with cloth, leather, or other durable material.|The hardcover edition was more expensive but felt more substantial.|近义词：hardback, casebound|反义词:paperback|adjective
