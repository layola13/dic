legitimation:/lɪˌdʒɪtɪˈmeɪʃən/
n.|合法化，正当化|The legitimation of power is a complex process. 权力合法化是一个复杂的过程。|
legitimation:/lɪˌdʒɪtɪˈmeɪʃən/
n.|授权，批准|The government's legitimation of the new policy was met with protests. 政府对新政策的批准遭到了抗议。|
legitimation:/lɪˌdʒɪtɪˈmeɪʃən/
n.|证明，认定|The legitimation of his claim was difficult due to lack of evidence.  由于缺乏证据，他的主张难以被认定。|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *legitimatio, from Latin legitimare "to make lawful," from legitimus "lawful," from lex (genitive legis) "law."


SYNONYMS->
authorization, validation, justification, sanction, ratification, endorsement, recognition, legalization


ANTONYMS->
invalidation, disqualification, denial, rejection, repudiation, condemnation, delegitimization
