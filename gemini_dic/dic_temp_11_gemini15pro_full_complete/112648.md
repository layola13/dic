lowboy:/ˈloʊbɔɪ/| n.|矮柜，矮床，低底盘拖车|A lowboy is a piece of furniture, typically a chest of drawers, that is lower than usual. It can also refer to a type of trailer used for hauling heavy equipment.|例句：The antique lowboy in the living room was a family heirloom. 客厅里的古董矮柜是传家宝。

例句：They used a lowboy trailer to transport the bulldozer. 他们用低底盘拖车运输推土机。|近义词：dresser (for furniture), low-loader (for trailer)|反义词:highboy (for furniture)|词性:noun
