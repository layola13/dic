billings:/ˈbɪlɪŋz/| n.|账单，开单；(电影等的)宣传广告；(尤指美国西部的)城镇|the process of sending people bills, especially regularly  / advertisements for a film, play, etc. / a town, especially in the western US|例句：The company's billings increased by 20% last year.  这家公司的账单去年增加了20%。

|近义词：invoices, accounts, charges, fees, dues, statements, advertising, publicity, promotion, town, city, municipality|反义词: payments, receipts|词性:noun


billings:/ˈbɪlɪŋz/| n.|结算；开账|the total amount of money charged to customers during a particular period|例句：The law firm's billings for the month exceeded $1 million.  这家律师事务所本月的结算超过了100万美元。


|近义词：revenue, income, earnings, takings|反义词: expenses, costs|词性:noun
