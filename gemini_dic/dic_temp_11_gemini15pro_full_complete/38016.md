compline:/ˈkɒmplɪn/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmplɪn/ (US)| n.| 晚祷，睡前祷告|A service of evening prayers, traditionally the last of the day, in the Christian church, especially in monasteries and convents.|The monks gathered in the chapel for compline. 修士们聚集在小教堂里做晚祷。|Synonyms: night prayers, vespers|Antonyms: matins, lauds|noun
