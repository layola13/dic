dramming:/ˈdræmɪŋ/| n. |击鼓，敲击声|The constant drumming of the rain on the roof was soothing. 屋顶上持续不断的雨点敲击声令人舒缓。|近义词：thumping, beating, tapping|反义词:silence|词性:名词
dramming:/ˈdræmɪŋ/| v. |击鼓，敲击|He was drumming his fingers nervously on the table. 他紧张地用手指敲击着桌子。|近义词：thumping, beating, tapping|反义词:stroking, caressing|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *drummen, from Old Norse *drumma (“to drum”), of imitative origin. Cognate with Swedish drumma (“to drum”), Danish drumme (“to drum”), Icelandic drumma (“to drum, resound”).
