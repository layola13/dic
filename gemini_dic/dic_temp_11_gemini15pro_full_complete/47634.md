debilitative: /dɪˈbɪlɪteɪtɪv/
adjective
使人衰弱的；削弱的
Tending to weaken or enfeeble.
例句：The debilitating effects of the illness kept her bedridden for months. 这种疾病使她衰弱不堪，卧床数月。

例句：Stress can have a debilitating effect on both your physical and mental health. 压力会对身心健康造成削弱的影响。

近义词：weakening, enervating, incapacitating, crippling
反义词：strengthening, invigorating, empowering

词性：adjective
词源：From the verb "debilitate," which comes from the Latin word "debilitare," meaning "to weaken."
