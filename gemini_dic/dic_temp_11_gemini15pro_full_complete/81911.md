grigri: /ˈɡriɡri/ | n. | 岩石制动器；保护器 | A belay device used in rock climbing, primarily for lead climbing and top-roping. It uses assisted braking to hold a falling climber. |  Example 1: The climber dropped their grigri while packing their gear. 登山者在整理装备时掉了他们的岩石制动器。

Example 2:  Make sure your grigri is properly locked before belaying. 确保你的岩石制动器在保护之前已正确锁定。 | Synonym: belay device, assisted braking device | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps manual braking device) | noun
