Longmynd: /ˈlɒŋmaɪnd/ | n. | 隆迈恩德（地名） |  A heath and moorland plateau in Shropshire, England, forming the southwestern part of the Shropshire Hills Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty. It is the highest point in Shropshire. |  We hiked across the Longmynd and enjoyed the stunning views. 我们徒步穿越了隆迈恩德，欣赏了壮丽的景色。 | 近义词: upland, moor, plateau | 反义词: valley, lowland | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Old English *langmyned  'long mountain', from lang 'long' + *myned 'mountain, hill'. The element *myned is a back-formation from such place names as Mynedwīc (Minchinhampton, Gloucestershire) and Lygemyned (Long Mynd).
