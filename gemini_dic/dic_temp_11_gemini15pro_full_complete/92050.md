hyperpigmentation:/ˌhaɪpərˌpɪɡmɛnˈteɪʃən/| n. |色素沉着过度，色素沉积|A condition in which patches of skin become darker than the surrounding skin due to increased melanin production.|Example: Sun exposure can cause hyperpigmentation.  日晒会导致色素沉着过度。|Example:  She was treated for hyperpigmentation on her face. 她接受了面部色素沉着过度的治疗。|近义词:darkening, discoloration|反义词:hypopigmentation|词性:noun
