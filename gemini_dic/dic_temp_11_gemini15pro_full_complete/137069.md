nonstriated:/ˌnɒnˈstraɪeɪtɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈstraɪeɪtɪd/
adjective
无条纹的；平滑肌的
Not marked with striae or striations; smooth.
Smooth muscle fibers are nonstriated. 平滑肌纤维是无条纹的。
近义词: smooth, unstriped, involuntary
反义词: striated, striped, voluntary
词性: adjective

nonstriated:/ˌnɒnˈstraɪeɪtɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈstraɪeɪtɪd/
adjective
无条纹的；平滑肌的
Relating to or denoting muscle tissue that does not appear striated under the microscope; smooth, involuntary or unstriped muscle.
The walls of the intestine are composed of nonstriated muscle. 肠壁由平滑肌组成。
近义词: smooth, unstriped, involuntary
反义词: striated, striped, voluntary
词性: adjective
