criticizers:/ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzərz/
noun
批评者，评论家
People who express disapproval or find fault with something or someone.
The author's new book has attracted many criticizers.  这位作者的新书引来了许多批评家。
critics, reviewers, detractors
supporters, admirers
