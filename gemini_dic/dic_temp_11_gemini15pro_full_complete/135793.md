nonprojections:/ˌnɒnprəˈdʒɛkʃənz/ | n. | 非投影，非射影 |Things that are not projections, often referring to mathematical or geometric concepts where a projection is a mapping of points from one space to another.  Specifically, it might refer to elements of a set that are not the image of any element under a given projection. | The theorem deals with both projections and nonprojections within the vector space.  该定理处理向量空间内的投影和非投影。

The set of nonprojections formed a significant subset.  非投影的集合形成了一个重要的子集。 |Near synonyms: non-images, remainders| Antonyms: projections, images | Noun
