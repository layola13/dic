limpitude:/lɪmˈpɪtjuːd/
n.
lack of firmness, resolution, or vigor; feebleness
A state of weakness or lack of energy.
His limpitude made it difficult for him to climb the stairs. 他缺乏活力，爬楼梯都很费劲。
近义词: lassitude, languor, weakness, feebleness, inertia
反义词: energy, vigor, strength, firmness, resolve
