gastrosplenic:/ˌɡæstroʊˈsplɛnɪk/
adjective
胃脾的；胃和脾脏的
Relating to or connecting the stomach and the spleen.
The gastrosplenic ligament connects the stomach to the spleen.  胃脾韧带连接胃和脾。
The surgeon carefully dissected the gastrosplenic vessels.  外科医生仔细地解剖了胃脾血管。
Synonyms:  gastric-splenic (less common)
Antonyms:  None.
词性: adjective
