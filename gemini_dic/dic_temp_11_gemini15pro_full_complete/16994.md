bastioned: /ˈbæstʃənd/ | adj. |设有堡垒的，防御坚固的|Fortified with bastions; strongly defended. |The bastioned city walls stood firm against the enemy attack. 设有堡垒的城墙在敌人的攻击下屹立不倒。|近义词：fortified, defended, protected|反义词：vulnerable, undefended|词性:形容词

Etymology: From the French word "bastionné," past participle of "bastionner" meaning "to furnish with bastions," ultimately from Italian "bastione" (bastion).
