fore-sighted:/ˈfɔːrˌsaɪtɪd/
adjective
有远见的；深谋远虑的
able to predict what will or might happen in the future.
例句：She made a fore-sighted decision to invest in the company. 她做出了一个有远见的决定，投资了这家公司。

例句：His fore-sighted planning saved the company from bankruptcy. 他深谋远虑的计划使公司免于破产。

近义词：prescient, visionary, far-sighted, provident
反义词：short-sighted, myopic, improvident
词性: adjective
