lactamic: /lækˈtæmɪk/ | adj. | 内酰胺的；乳胺的 | Relating to or denoting a lactam.  A lactam is a cyclic amide. |  Caprolactam is a cyclic amide and a lactamic compound used in the production of Nylon 6.己内酰胺是一种环酰胺和内酰胺化合物，用于生产尼龙6。 |  Synonyms: cyclic amide (though not perfectly synonymous, as all lactams are cyclic amides, but not all cyclic amides are lactams) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, as it describes a specific chemical structure.) | adjective
