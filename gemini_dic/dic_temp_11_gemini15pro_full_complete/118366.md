meritocratic: /ˌmerɪtəˈkrætɪk/ | adj. |  精英管理的；贤能统治的；唯才是举的 | Based on the belief that power should be vested in individuals according to merit. |  Example:  A meritocratic system ensures that the most capable people rise to the top. 一个精英管理的制度确保最有能力的人升到顶层。

Example:  Some argue that a truly meritocratic society is an unattainable ideal.  有些人认为真正的精英管理社会是一个无法实现的理想。 | Synonyms:  elitist, hierarchical | Antonyms:  egalitarian, democratic | Word type: adjective
