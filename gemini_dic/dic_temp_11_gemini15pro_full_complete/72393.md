fluctuational:/ˌflʌktʃuˈeɪʃənl/
adjective
波动的；起伏的
Characterized by fluctuation; tending to fluctuate.
The fluctuational changes in the stock market made investors nervous. 股票市场的波动变化让投资者感到紧张。
The company's fluctuational performance made it difficult to predict future earnings.  公司业绩的波动使得难以预测未来的收益。
Synonyms: variable, unstable, changeable, erratic, volatile
Antonyms: stable, steady, constant, fixed, unchanging
词性: adjective
