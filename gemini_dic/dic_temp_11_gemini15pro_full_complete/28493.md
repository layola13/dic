card:/kɑːrd/ (US), /kɑːd/ (UK)| n. vt.|卡片，卡牌，明信片，纸牌，名片；发卡，记分|A card is a small rectangular piece of stiff paper, often used for writing or printing on.|She sent me a birthday card. 她寄给我一张生日贺卡。|近义词：ticket, pass, badge|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "cashless system" in some contexts) |词性: noun, verb

card:/kɑːrd/ (US), /kɑːd/ (UK)| vt.|用梳理机梳理|To card is to clean and disentangle fibres such as wool or cotton using a card, a device with wire teeth.|She carded the wool before spinning it. 她纺线前先梳理了羊毛。|近义词：comb, disentangle|反义词: tangle, mat |词性: verb
