outjinxes:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɪŋksɪz/
verb
胜过(某人)的厄运; 比(某人)更幸运
to surpass someone in the use of jinxes or spells intended to bring bad luck.  To have better luck than someone else who is trying to cause bad luck.
She outjinxed him by wearing a lucky charm. 她戴着幸运符，胜过了他的厄运。
Synonyms: outsmart, outmaneuver, surpass in luck,  be luckier than
Antonyms: be jinxed, have bad luck, be unlucky
词性: 动词(verb)
