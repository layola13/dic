antiquing:/ænˈtɪːkɪŋ/ | n.| 古董收藏；寻觅古董 | The act of shopping for antiques. |  She loves antiquing on weekends, searching for hidden treasures in small-town shops. 她喜欢在周末逛古董店，在小镇的商店里寻找隐藏的宝藏。 | synonyms: antique collecting, antique hunting | antonyms: modernizing, updating | noun

antiquing:/ænˈtɪːkɪŋ/ | v. (gerund)| 寻觅古董；使…具有古色 |  Giving something an antique appearance.  |  He was antiquing the furniture to give it a more vintage look. 他正在给家具做旧，让它看起来更复古。 | synonyms: distressing, aging | antonyms: modernizing, updating | verb (gerund)
