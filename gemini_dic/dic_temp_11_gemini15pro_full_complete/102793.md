jarble:/ˈdʒɑːrbl/ | n. |含糊不清的话，胡言乱语 |Unintelligible or distorted speech; gibberish.| The recording was just a jarble of noise. 录音只是一堆模糊不清的噪音。| 近义词: gibberish, nonsense, babble | 反义词: sense, clarity |词性:名词
