antiracial:/ˌæntiˈreɪʃəl/
adjective
反种族主义的
actively opposing racism; against racial discrimination.
例句：She is a strong antiracial activist. 她是一位坚定的反种族主义活动家。

例句：The organization promotes antiracial education in schools. 该组织在学校推广反种族主义教育。

近义词：anti-racist, nonracist
反义词：racist, discriminatory
词性：adjective
