declaim:/dɪˈkleɪm/| v. |慷慨陈词；朗诵；雄辩地谴责|to speak rhetorically; to speak formally and dramatically in public|He declaimed against the evils of war. 他慷慨激昂地谴责战争的罪恶。|近义词：oratorical, recite, pronounce, inveigh|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:动词
词源:From Latin declamare, from de- ‘fully’ + clamare ‘to cry out.’
