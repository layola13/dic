irritablest:/ˈɪrɪtəblɪst/
adjective
最易怒的，最暴躁的
Most easily annoyed or angered; displaying the most irritability.
He was the irritablest man I'd ever met, snapping at everyone for the slightest reason. 他是我见过的最易怒的人，为了一点小事就对每个人大吼大叫。
近义词: most touchy, most irascible, most cantankerous
反义词: most calm, most patient, most even-tempered
词性: adjective
词源: From irritate (from Latin irritare, "to excite, provoke") + -able (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being") + -est (suffix forming superlative adjectives).
