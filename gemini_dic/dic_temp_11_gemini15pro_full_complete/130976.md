nonastringently:/ˌnɒnəˈstrɪndʒəntli/ | adv. | 不涩地，温和地 |  In a manner that is not astringent; mildly; gently.  This describes something that lacks the harsh, puckering, or contracting quality of an astringent. | The tea was brewed nonastringently, creating a smooth and mellow flavor.  这茶泡得一点也不涩，口感顺滑醇和。 | The lotion was applied nonastringently to the sensitive skin.  这款乳液温和地涂抹在敏感的肌肤上。 | 近义词: mildly, gently, smoothly | 反义词: astringently, harshly, sharply | 词性: 副词

nonastringent:/ˌnɒnəˈstrɪndʒənt/ | adj. | 不涩的，温和的 | Not astringent; lacking the harsh, puckering, or contracting quality of an astringent. |  The wine was nonastringent and easy to drink. 这款酒不涩，很容易入口。 |  She preferred a nonastringent toner for her face. 她更喜欢用不涩的爽肤水。 | 近义词: mild, gentle, smooth | 反义词: astringent, harsh, sharp | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "Nonastringent" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adjective "astringent". "Astringent" comes from the Late Latin word "astringēns", present participle of "astringere", meaning "to bind tightly".
