actinograph:/ˈæktɪnəɡræf/ | n. | 日射强度计，光化度计 | An instrument for measuring the chemical effect of sunlight, especially for photographic purposes.  It measures the intensity of actinic rays. | The actinograph was used to determine the best exposure time for the photographs. 日射强度计被用来确定照片的最佳曝光时间。| Synonym: actinometer | Antonym: N/A | noun
