blow-dried: /ˈbloʊˌdraɪd/ (US) /ˈbləʊˌdraɪd/ (UK)| adj.|吹干的|Dried with a hairdryer.|Her hair was perfectly blow-dried. 她的头发被吹得十分完美。|My blow-dried hair looked sleek and shiny. 我吹干的头发看起来光滑亮泽。|近义词: dried, styled|反义词: wet, damp|词性:形容词

词源:  "blow-dried" is derived from the verb "blow-dry," which is a compound of "blow" and "dry."  "Blow" in this context refers to the action of air propelled by a hairdryer, and "dry" refers to the removal of moisture.
