encasing:/ɪnˈkeɪsɪŋ/
verb
包装，封装
The delicate artifact was encased in a protective layer of resin. 这件精美的文物被封装在一层保护树脂中。

verb
围住，包裹
The building was encased in scaffolding. 这座建筑被脚手架包围着。

adjective
(仅作后置定语) 包裹的，围住的
a sword encased in a leather sheath 包裹在皮鞘中的剑


Etymology: From en- (meaning "in" or "into") + case (meaning "a container or covering") + -ing (present participle suffix).  Ultimately derived from Latin "capsa" (box).

近义词: enclosing, surrounding, covering, sheathing
反义词:  uncovering, exposing, releasing

词性:  Verb, Adjective (used only after a noun)
