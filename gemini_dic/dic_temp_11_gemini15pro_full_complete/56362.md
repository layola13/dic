一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I do my homework every day. 我每天都做作业。
She does yoga in the morning. 她早上做瑜伽。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am doing my homework right now. 我现在正在做作业。
They are doing a science experiment. 他们正在做一个科学实验。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have done all my chores. 我已经完成了所有家务。
He has done a great job. 他做得很好。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been doing this project for three weeks. 我已经做了这个项目三个星期了。
She has been doing research on this topic for months. 她几个月来一直在研究这个课题。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I did my homework last night. 我昨晚做了作业。
They did their best. 他们尽力了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was doing the dishes when the phone rang.  电话响的时候我正在洗碗。
She was doing her hair when the doorbell rang. 门铃响的时候她正在做头发。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already done my homework before dinner. 我在晚饭前已经做完了作业。
They had done everything they could. 他们已经尽力了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been doing yoga for years before I injured my back.  受伤之前我练瑜伽已经很多年了。
He had been doing research on the subject for months. 他研究这个课题已经好几个月了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will do my homework tomorrow. 我明天会做作业。
They will do what they can. 他们会尽力而为。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be doing my homework this evening. 今晚我将做作业。
They will be doing a presentation next week. 下周他们将做一个演讲。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have done my homework by tomorrow evening. 明天晚上之前我将完成作业。
They will have done everything by then. 到那时他们将完成所有工作。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been doing yoga for five years. 到明年，我将练习瑜伽五年了。
By the time he retires, he will have been working here for 30 years. 到他退休时，他将在这里工作了30年。
