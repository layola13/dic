law:/lɔː/ (UK) /lɑː/ (US)| n.| 法律，法规；定律，规律；法则|A system of rules that a particular country or community recognizes as regulating the actions of its members and may enforce by the imposition of penalties.|例句：The law requires drivers to wear seatbelts.  法律要求司机系安全带。

例句：The law of gravity states that what goes up must come down. 万有引力定律表明，上升的东西最终都会落下。|近义词：rule, regulation, statute, principle|反义词：anarchy, disorder, chaos| noun
