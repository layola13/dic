overprovidently:/ˌoʊvərˈprɒvɪdəntlɪ/
adverb
过于谨慎地，过度准备地
excessively provident; providing for the future to an unnecessary degree.  Characterized by excessive or undue foresight or precaution.
He had overprovidently packed for the weekend trip, bringing enough clothes for a week.  他为了周末旅行准备得太多了，带的衣服够穿一周了。
overcautiously, excessively prudently
rashly, imprudently
词性：副词
