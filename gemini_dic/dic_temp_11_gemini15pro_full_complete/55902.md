divorcible:/dɪˈvɔːrsəbl/
adj.
可离婚的
Capable of being divorced; legally permitting the termination of a marriage.
His behavior made him utterly divorcible. 他的行为让他完全可以被离婚。
The marriage was deemed divorcible by the court. 法院认为这段婚姻可以离婚。
synonyms: separable, dissolvable
antonyms: indissoluble, inseparable
词性: 形容词
