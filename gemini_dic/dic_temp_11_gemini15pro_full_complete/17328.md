It describes something or someone that many people want or desire. | This antique vase is highly be-sought-after by collectors.  这个古董花瓶受到收藏家的高度追捧。

This restaurant's Sunday brunch is be-sought-after, so reservations are recommended. 这家餐厅的周日早午餐很受欢迎，所以建议提前预订。 | 近义词：popular, desirable, in-demand, coveted | 反义词：undesirable, unpopular, unwanted | 词性: 形容词
