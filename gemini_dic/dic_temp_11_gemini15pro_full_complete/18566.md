benet:/ˈbɛnɪt/| n.| 津贴，福利，救济金|A sum of money provided by the government or a public fund to help people in need.|She receives unemployment benefits. 她领取失业救济金。|Synonyms: allowance, grant, subsidy|Antonyms: penalty, forfeiture|noun


benet:/ˈbɛnɪt/| v.| 得益，受益|To receive an advantage or profit from something.|The company benefited from the new tax law. 这家公司从新的税法中受益。|Synonyms: profit, gain, advantage|Antonyms: suffer, lose, forfeit|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *benet* (unattested), from Latin *bene factum* "well done," neuter past participle of *benefacere* "to do good," from *bene* "well" + *facere* "to do."
