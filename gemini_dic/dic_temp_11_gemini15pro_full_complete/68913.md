fastuous:/ˈfæstʃuəs/ /ˈfæstuəs/| adj.|傲慢的；自负的；浮夸的；铺张的|Characterized by excessive pride or arrogance; ostentatious.|He gave a fastuous speech, boasting about his accomplishments. 他做了一个傲慢的演讲，吹嘘自己的成就。|The billionaire's fastuous lifestyle included a fleet of luxury cars and a private jet. 这位亿万富翁奢靡的生活方式包括一队豪车和一架私人飞机。|近义词：pompous, arrogant, haughty, ostentatious, pretentious|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fastuosus*, from *fastus* ("pride, arrogance, pomp, display").
