anthropoidea:/ˌænθrəˈpɔɪdiə/ | n. |类人猿亚目|A suborder of primates traditionally comprising monkeys, apes, and humans. Now often replaced by Simiiformes. |例句：Anthropoidea includes all primates more closely related to monkeys and apes than to prosimians. 类人猿亚目包括所有与猴子和猿类比与原猴类更密切相关的灵长类动物。

例句：The classification of Anthropoidea has been debated for decades.  类人猿亚目的分类已经争论了几十年。|近义词：Simiiformes, higher primates |反义词:Prosimii (原猴亚目，已弃用，现代分类为Strepsirrhini)|词性:名词
