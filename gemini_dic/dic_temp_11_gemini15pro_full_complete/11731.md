aromas:/əˈroʊməz/| n. |香气，芳香|A pleasant smell, often of food or spices.  The plural form of "aroma".|The aromas of freshly baked bread filled the kitchen.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味弥漫着整个厨房。|近义词：scents, fragrances, smells, perfumes|反义词：stenches, odors, stinks|Noun
