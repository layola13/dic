claimable:/ˈkleɪməbl/
adjective
可要求的；可认领的；可索取的
able to be claimed
The prize is claimable within 30 days of the draw.  奖品可在开奖后 30 天内领取。
The unclaimed baggage was claimable at the airport.  无人认领的行李可以在机场认领。
synonyms: retrievable, obtainable, collectible
antonyms: unclaimable, forfeitable
词性: adjective
