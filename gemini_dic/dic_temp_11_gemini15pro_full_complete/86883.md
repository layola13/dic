hemipteral:/ˌhɛmɪˈptərəl/
adjective
半翅目的；半翅目昆虫的
(of an insect) having two pairs of wings, the front pair being partly hardened and forming a protective covering for the hind pair when folded.  Relating to or belonging to the Hemiptera.
The stink bug is a hemipteral insect.  椿象是一种半翅目昆虫。

The cicada exhibits classic hemipteral characteristics. 蝉展现出典型的半翅目昆虫特征。

synonym: hemipterous
antonym: (No direct antonym, as it's a taxonomic classification)
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek hēmi- (half) + pteron (wing)
