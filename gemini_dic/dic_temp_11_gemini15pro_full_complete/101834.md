irrupt:/ɪˈrʌpt/ | v. | 爆发，突然闯入 | To enter suddenly and violently; to erupt or burst forth. |  Protesters irrupted into the meeting, shouting slogans. 抗议者冲进会场，高喊口号。

The volcano irrupted with tremendous force. 火山猛烈地爆发了。 | 近义词：burst, erupt, explode, break out | 反义词： recede, subside, dwindle |词性: 动词
