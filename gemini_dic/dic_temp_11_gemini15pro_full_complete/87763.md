一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are they doing outside? = 他们在外面做什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? = 你到目前为止学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? = 他整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie? = 他们在看电影之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你会完成什么？
What will have changed by the next election? = 到下一次选举会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做这件事五年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？
