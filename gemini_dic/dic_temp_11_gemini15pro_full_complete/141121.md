ontologist:/ɒnˈtɒlədʒɪst/ /ɑːnˈtɑːlədʒɪst/
n.
本体论学家；存在学家
A philosopher who studies ontology, the branch of metaphysics that deals with the nature of being.
Dr. Smith is a respected ontologist in the field of artificial intelligence. 史密斯博士是人工智能领域受人尊敬的本体论学家。
近义词：metaphysician
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ontology + -ist.  Ontology from  onto- (from Greek ὄν, ὄντος (on, ontos) - being, present participle of εἰμί (eimi) - to be) + -logy (from Greek -λογία (-logia) - study of).
