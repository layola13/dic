homozygotic:/ˌhoʊməʊzaɪˈɡɑːtɪk/| adj.|纯合的，同型结合的|Having two identical alleles of a particular gene or genes.  This means that both copies of the gene are the same.|Example 1: The plant is homozygotic for the dominant allele.|该植物就显性等位基因而言是纯合的。

Example 2:  Homozygotic twins share 100% of their DNA.|同卵双胞胎拥有100%相同的DNA。|近义词：purebred, homozygous|反义词:heterozygotic, heterozygous|词性:adjective

homozygous:/ˌhoʊməʊˈzaɪɡəs/| adj.|纯合的，同型结合的|Having two identical alleles of a particular gene or genes. This means that both copies of the gene are the same.|Example 1:  The mouse was homozygous for the recessive gene.|这只老鼠就隐性基因而言是纯合的。


Example 2:  Homozygous individuals are more likely to express recessive traits.|纯合个体更有可能表达隐性性状。|近义词：purebred, homozygotic|反义词:heterozygous, heterozygotic|词性:adjective


词源: From Greek "homo-" (same) + "zygon" (yoke) + "-ic" (forming adjectives).
