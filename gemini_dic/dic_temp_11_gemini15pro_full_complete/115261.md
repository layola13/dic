manipulator:/məˈnɪpjuleɪtər/ | n. |操纵者，控制者|A person who skillfully or deviously influences or controls others to their own advantage.|例句：He's a master manipulator, always pulling strings behind the scenes. 他是个操纵大师，总是在幕后操纵一切。

例句：The politician was accused of being a manipulator who used propaganda to sway public opinion.  这位政治家被指控是一个操纵者，利用宣传来左右公众舆论。|近义词：controller, operator, influencer, director, puppeteer|反义词：victim, pawn|词性:noun
