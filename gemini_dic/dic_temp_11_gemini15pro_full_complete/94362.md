implacableness:/ɪmˈplækəbəlnəs/
n.
无情；不屈不挠；固执
The state of being implacable; unyielding severity.
His implacablness made negotiations impossible. 他的不妥协使得谈判无法进行。
synonyms: inflexibility, relentlessness, rigidity, stubbornness,adamance
antonyms: flexibility, leniency, mercy, compassion, yielding

implacable:/ɪmˈplækəbl/
adj.
无法平息的；不妥协的；固执的
Unable to be appeased or placated.
She was implacable in her pursuit of justice. 她追求正义毫不妥协。
synonyms: unyielding, relentless, unforgiving, inexorable
antonyms: yielding, forgiving, merciful, compassionate
