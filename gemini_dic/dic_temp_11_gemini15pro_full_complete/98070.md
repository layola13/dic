inquirable:/ɪnˈkwaɪərəbl/| adj.|可询问的，可调查的|Capable of being inquired into; suitable for investigation.  Something that is inquirable is a valid subject for questions or investigation.|The matter is certainly inquirable and warrants further investigation. 这件事当然可以调查，值得进一步调查。|The origins of the universe are inquirable, though perhaps never fully knowable. 宇宙的起源是可以探究的，尽管也许永远无法完全知晓。|Synonyms: researchable, investigable, examinable|Antonyms: uninquirable, unquestionable|adjective
