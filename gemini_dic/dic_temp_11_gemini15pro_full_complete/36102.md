coenurus:/siːˈnʊərəs/| n. | 共尾蚴，多头蚴 |A larval stage of certain tapeworms (e.g., *Taenia multiceps*) characterized by a large fluid-filled bladder containing multiple scolices. It typically infects the brains of sheep and other livestock, sometimes humans.| The coenurus was surgically removed from the sheep's brain.  从绵羊的大脑中手术切除了多头蚴。 | synonym:  larva, metacestode | antonym: adult (tapeworm) | noun


coenurus:/siːˈnʊərəs/| n. | 多头蚴 |The larval stage of a tapeworm, *Multiceps multiceps*, that forms a fluid-filled cyst in the brain and spinal cord of sheep and other intermediate hosts.  |The veterinarian diagnosed the sheep with coenurosis after finding a coenurus in its brain during necropsy. 兽医在尸检过程中发现绵羊大脑中有多头蚴后，诊断其患有多头蚴病。 | synonym: bladder worm | antonym:  imago (adult tapeworm)| noun
