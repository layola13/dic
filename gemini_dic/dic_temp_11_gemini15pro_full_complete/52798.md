diligentness:/ˈdɪlɪdʒəntnəs/| n. |勤奋，勤勉|The quality of being constant and earnest in application to a task; painstaking effort.|Her diligentness in studying paid off when she got a scholarship. 她学习的勤奋在她获得奖学金时得到了回报。|近义词：assiduity, industry, perseverance|反义词：laziness, negligence, indolence|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *diligentnesse*, from Old French *diligence*, from Latin *diligentia* ("carefulness, attentiveness"), from *diligēns* ("careful, attentive"), present participle of *diligō* ("to value or esteem highly, love; to single out, select; to attend to carefully"), from *dis-* ("apart, asunder") + *legō* ("to choose, gather, select, read").
