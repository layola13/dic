eudaemonistic:/ˌjuːdiːˈmɒnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 快乐主义的；幸福主义的 |  Relating to or based on the belief that happiness or well-being is the chief good.  |  He lived a eudaemonistic life, focused on maximizing his own happiness and fulfillment.  他过着快乐主义的生活，专注于最大化自己的幸福和满足感。 |  hedonistic, eudemonistic |  ascetic | adjective

eudaemonism:/ˌjuːdiːˈmɒnɪzəm/ | n. | 快乐主义；幸福主义 | The ethical doctrine holding that the value or the goodness of actions lies in their efficacy in promoting the well-being of human beings. |  Eudaemonism is a philosophy that emphasizes living a life of virtue and happiness. 幸福主义是一种强调过着美德和幸福生活的哲学。 |  hedonism, welfarism |  asceticism | noun
