ossifiers:/ˈɒsɪfaɪərz/
noun
骨化器; 骨化者
People or things that ossify or cause ossification.  (Those that cause or undergo the process of becoming bone or bony.)
The osteoblasts are the ossifiers of the bone tissue. 成骨细胞是骨组织的骨化器。
Cartilage cells can become ossifiers as we age.  随着年龄的增长，软骨细胞会骨化。
Synonyms:calcifiers, mineralizers
Antonyms:decalcifiers, softeners
词性:n.
