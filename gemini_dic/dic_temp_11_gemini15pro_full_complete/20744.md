blanch:/blɑːntʃ/ /blæn(t)ʃ/ | v. | 焯水，使变白，使(脸色)变苍白 | To briefly immerse food, usually vegetables or nuts, in boiling water, often to remove the skin or to prepare for freezing. Also, to make something white or pale. | Example 1:  I blanched the almonds before slicing them. 我在切杏仁之前先焯水。

Example 2:  The news of the accident blanched her face. 事故的消息使她脸色苍白。 | 近义词：parboil, scald, pale, whiten | 反义词：darken, color, flush | 词性: 动词

blancher:/ˈblɑːn(t)ʃər/ /ˈblæn(t)ʃər/ | n. | 焯水器 | A kitchen utensil used for blanching food. | Example 1: Use a blancher to prepare the vegetables for freezing. 使用焯水器准备冷冻蔬菜。

Example 2: A good blancher is essential for canning certain foods.  一个好的焯水器对于罐装某些食物是必不可少的。| 近义词：colander | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Old French "blanchir," meaning "to whiten," from "blanc," meaning "white," ultimately from a Germanic source.
