coyed:/kɔɪd/| adj.|害羞的，腼腆的，忸怩作态的|Shy or pretending to be shy, often in a playful or flirtatious way.  Characterized by a deliberate and often alluring display of shyness or reluctance.|She gave him a coyed smile. 她给了他一个害羞的微笑。|近义词：bashful, shy, demure, reserved, reticent, diffident, timid, self-effacing|反义词：bold, brazen, forward, immodest, confident, assertive|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "coy," derived from Middle English *coien*, from Old French *coi* "quiet, still," ultimately from Latin *quietus*.
