coerce:/koʊˈɜːrs/ /kəʊˈɜːs/| vt.|胁迫，强制|To persuade someone forcefully to do something that they are unwilling to do.|He was coerced into signing the confession. 他被迫签署了供词。|Synonyms: force, compel, pressure, intimidate, bully|Antonyms: persuade, encourage|verb

coercer:/koʊˈɜːrsər/ /kəʊˈɜːsər/| n.|胁迫者，强迫者|A person who coerces someone.|The coercer threatened him with violence. 胁迫者用暴力威胁他。|Synonyms: bully, intimidator, oppressor|Antonyms: helper, supporter|noun
