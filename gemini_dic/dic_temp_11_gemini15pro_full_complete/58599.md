dullard: /ˈdʌlərd/ | n. |笨蛋，傻瓜| A slow-witted or stupid person. | He was considered a dullard by his classmates because he couldn't understand simple concepts. 他因为理解不了简单的概念，被同学们认为是个笨蛋。|  Synonyms: idiot, moron, imbecile, dolt, simpleton | Antonyms: genius, prodigy, intellectual, savant | noun
