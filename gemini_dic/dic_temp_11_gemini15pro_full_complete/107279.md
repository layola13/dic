laky:/ˈleɪki/| adj.|漏的；有裂缝的；渗漏的|Allowing liquid or gas to leak in or out.|The roof is laky and needs to be repaired. 屋顶漏水，需要修理。|例句：The old boat was laky and took on water quickly. 那艘旧船漏水，很快就进水了。|近义词：leaky, permeable, porous|反义词：watertight, impermeable, leakproof|词性:形容词
词源: From leak + -y.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *lekaną (“to drip, leak”).
