digamist: /ˈdɪɡəmɪst/ | n. | 重婚者；再婚者 | A person who has married twice, having been widowed or divorced. |  He was accused of being a digamist, having married two women without legally dissolving his first marriage. 他被指控重婚，在没有合法解除第一次婚姻的情况下与两名女子结婚。

The digamist kept both marriages a secret for years.  这个重婚者多年来一直对两段婚姻都保密。| 近义词: bigamist (This word implies illegality, while digamist does not necessarily) | 反义词: monogamist | 词性: noun
