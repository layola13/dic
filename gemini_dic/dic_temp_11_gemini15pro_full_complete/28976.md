carsick: /ˈkɑːrˌsɪk/ (US) /ˈkɑːˌsɪk/ (UK)| adj.|晕车的|Experiencing nausea and/or vomiting induced by the motion of a car.|I felt carsick after the long winding road. 我在蜿蜒的路上行驶后感到晕车。|近义词：travel-sick, motion-sick|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From *car* + *sick*.  *Car* ultimately from Latin *carrus* "wheeled vehicle." *Sick* from Old English *sēoc* "ill, diseased, afflicted with nausea."
