cyclorama:/ˌsaɪkləˈrɑːmə/ /ˌsaɪkləˈræmə/| n.|全景画；环形景幕|A large curtain or wall, often concave, surrounding the back of a stage set, on which a scenic view or cycloramic painting is projected or displayed. |The cyclorama depicted a vast, starlit sky. 环形景幕描绘了浩瀚的星空。|The play used a cyclorama to create the illusion of being in a forest. 这部戏用环形景幕营造出身处森林的幻觉。|Synonyms: panorama, diorama|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
