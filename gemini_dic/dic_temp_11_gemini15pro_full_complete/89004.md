holler: /ˈhɑːlər/ (US) /ˈhɒlər/ (UK)| n. v.|喊叫，叫喊；抱怨|A loud shout or cry.  To shout or cry loudly.|例句：He let out a holler when he saw the bear. 他看到熊时大叫了一声。|近义词：shout, yell, scream, cry, complain|反义词: whisper, murmur|词性: noun, verb

holler: /ˈhɑːlər/ (US) /ˈhɒlər/ (UK)| n. v.|低洼地区；山谷|A hollow or valley.|例句：They lived in a holler in the mountains. 他们住在山里的一个山谷里。|近义词：valley, hollow, ravine|反义词: hill, peak, mountain|词性: noun


词源: From the Middle English *holler*, from Old French *holer*, related to Old High German *holōn*  "to fetch, retrieve" ultimately of imitative origin.
