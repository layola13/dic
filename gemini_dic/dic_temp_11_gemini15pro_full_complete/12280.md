asceticisms: /əˈsɛtɪˌsɪzəmz/ | n. |禁欲主义，苦行主义| The practice of severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons. |  He followed a life of asceticism, abstaining from all luxuries. 他过着禁欲的生活，戒绝一切奢侈品。|  The monk's asceticism was legendary; he owned nothing but his robes and a bowl.  这位僧侣的苦行是传奇性的；他除了长袍和一个碗之外一无所有。| synonyms: austerity, self-denial, abstinence, renunciation | antonyms: indulgence, hedonism, luxury, extravagance | noun
