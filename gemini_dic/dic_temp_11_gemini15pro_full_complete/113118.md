lunges:/lʌndʒɪz/
| n. |弓步，猛冲，刺|A sudden forward movement; the act of making a lunge.  A series of lunges is often used as an exercise.|She did a series of lunges across the gym floor. 她绕着健身房地板做了一系列弓步练习。|近义词：pounce, thrust, dive|反义词: retreat, withdrawal|noun

lunges:/lʌndʒɪz/
| v. |猛冲，刺，向前冲|To make a sudden forward movement, typically with a weapon or in attack. To move forward suddenly and clumsily.|He lunged at his opponent with a sword. 他用剑向对手猛刺过去。 |近义词：thrust, pounce, charge|反义词: retreat, withdraw|verb
