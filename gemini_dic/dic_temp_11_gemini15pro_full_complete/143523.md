outgross:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊs/
verb
胜过，超过（尤指在收入方面）
to surpass in gross earnings or profit.
Example: The new movie outgrossed the previous record holder in its opening weekend. 这部新电影在首映周末的票房收入超过了之前的纪录保持者。

Example:  Her latest novel outgrossed all her previous works combined. 她最新的小说收入超过了她以前所有作品的总和。

近义词: surpass, exceed, outstrip, outperform
反义词: underperform, lag behind
词性: verb
