gallant:/ˈɡælənt/ (US) /ˈɡælənt/ (UK)| adj. n.|勇敢的，英勇的；殷勤的，献殷勤的；(男子)对女子彬彬有礼的；华丽的，堂皇的|Showing or characterized by bravery, courtesy, or nobility, especially in a dashing and showy way.  Can also refer to a fashionable and stylish man, especially one attentive to women.|He made a gallant attempt to rescue the child from the burning building. 他英勇地试图从燃烧的建筑物中救出孩子。|Synonyms: brave, courageous, chivalrous, courteous, dashing, heroic, noble, stately|Antonyms: cowardly, craven, timid, ungallant, rude|adjective
