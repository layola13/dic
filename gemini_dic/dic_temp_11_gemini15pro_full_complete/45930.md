cuttings:/ˈkʌtɪŋz/
n.|插枝，插条；剪报；(电影)剪辑|Pieces of a plant cut off for propagation; excerpts cut from newspapers, magazines, etc.; short segments of film.
|She propagated the plant from cuttings. 她用插枝繁殖这种植物。

|The journalist kept a file of news cuttings. 这位记者保存了一份新闻剪报。

|The director reviewed the film cuttings. 导演回顾了电影剪辑。
|近义词: clippings, slips, offshoots, excerpts, segments|反义词: whole, complete|词性:noun
