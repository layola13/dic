antiracketeering:/ˌæntiˌrækɪˈtɪrɪŋ/
adjective
反敲诈的，反勒索的
Relating to laws or measures designed to combat racketeering (organized crime, especially extortion).
The antiracketeering laws are designed to protect businesses from organized crime.  反敲诈法旨在保护企业免受有组织犯罪的侵害。
The government implemented new antiracketeering measures to combat corruption. 政府实施了新的反敲诈措施来打击腐败。
Synonyms: anti-corruption, anti-crime
Antonyms: pro-crime, criminal
