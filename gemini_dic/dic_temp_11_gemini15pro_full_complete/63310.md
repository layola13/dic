enticements:/ɪnˈtaɪsmənts/
|n.|诱惑，引诱，吸引物
|Something used to attract or tempt someone; an allurement.|
|The enticements of the big city drew many young people from rural areas.|大城市的诱惑吸引了众多来自农村地区的年轻人。
|Synonyms: allurements, temptations, attractions, lures, baits|
|Antonyms: deterrents, disincentives, repellents|
|noun|


enticement:/ɪnˈtaɪsmənt/
|n.|诱惑，引诱
|The act or process of enticing someone to do something.|
|The company offered various enticements to attract new customers.|公司提供了各种诱惑来吸引新客户。
|Synonyms: persuasion, inducement, coaxing, tempting, alluring|
|Antonyms: dissuasion, deterrence, repulsion|
|noun|
