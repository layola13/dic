irritants:/ˈɪrɪtənts/| n.|刺激物； раздражители|Something that causes slight pain or discomfort.|Dust and pollen are common irritants for people with allergies. 灰尘和花粉是过敏人群常见的刺激物。|synonyms: allergen, irritant, provoking agent|antonyms: soother, palliative|noun

irritant:/ˈɪrɪtənt/| adj.|刺激性的； раздражающий|Causing slight pain or discomfort.|The smoke from the fire was irritant to our eyes and throats. 火产生的烟雾刺激了我们的眼睛和喉咙。|synonyms: irritating, annoying, bothersome|antonyms: soothing, calming, pleasant|adjective
