nonpersevering:/ˌnɒnˌpɜːsəˈvɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 不坚持的，缺乏毅力的 | Not continuing in a course of action, especially despite difficulty or delay in achieving success.  Lacking perseverance. |  He was a nonpersevering student, often switching majors. 他是一个不坚持的学生，经常换专业。| The nonpersevering artist gave up on the painting after a few hours. 这位缺乏毅力的艺术家几个小时后就放弃了这幅画。| 近义词：inconstant, fickle, unsteady, erratic, vacillating | 反义词：persevering, persistent, tenacious, resolute, steadfast | 词性：形容词
