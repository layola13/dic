imbrocado:/ɪmbrəˈkɑːdoʊ/| adj.| 织锦的，锦缎的 | Woven with a raised design, often of gold or silver thread; figured. |  The queen wore an imbrocado gown to the coronation. 女王在加冕典礼上穿了一件织锦长袍。 |  The walls were hung with rich, imbrocado tapestries. 墙上挂着华丽的织锦挂毯。 | Synonyms: brocaded, embroidered, figured, ornamented | Antonyms: plain, simple, unembellished | adjective
