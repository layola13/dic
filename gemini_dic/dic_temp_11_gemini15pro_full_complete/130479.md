nonabusive:/ˌnɒnəˈbjuːsɪv/
adjective
非滥用的；不辱骂的
Not involving or characterized by abuse, especially physical or emotional maltreatment.
She grew up in a nonabusive household. 她在一个没有虐待的家庭中长大。
The company promotes a nonabusive work environment.  这家公司提倡一个没有虐待的工作环境。
近义词:respectful, kind, supportive, caring
反义词:abusive, cruel, hurtful, harmful
词性:adjective

词源:  "Non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "abusive" (from Late Latin "abusus," past participle of "abuti" - "to misuse").
