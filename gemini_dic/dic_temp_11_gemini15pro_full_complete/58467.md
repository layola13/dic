ductlike:/ˈdʌktˌlaɪk/
adjective
像管道的；管状的
Resembling a duct; tube-shaped.
The plant's stem was ductlike, transporting water and nutrients to the leaves.  这种植物的茎呈管状，将水和养分输送到叶子。
The narrow, ductlike passage led to a hidden chamber.  狭窄的管状通道通向一个隐藏的房间。
近义词: tubular, pipelike, conduit-like
反义词: solid, closed
