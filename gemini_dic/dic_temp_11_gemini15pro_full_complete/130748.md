nonamenable:/ˌnɒnəˈmiːnəbl/
|adj.|不服从的；难驾驭的；难处理的|The wild horses were nonamenable to training. 这些野马难以驯服。|Certain legal issues are nonamenable to international jurisdiction.  某些法律问题不受国际管辖。|近义词: intractable, recalcitrant, unmanageable|反义词: obedient, compliant, amenable|词性:形容词


词源:  non- (not) + amenable (willing to comply)  来自拉丁语 *aminabilis, 来自 aminare (to lead, guide), 最终来自 a- (to) + minari (to threaten).  "nonamenable" 最早出现在16世纪晚期。
