almightys:/ɔːlˈmaɪtiz/ | adj. |万能的，全能的|  An archaic or humorous form of almighty, used especially in addressing God or expressing surprise or annoyance. |  Almightys, what a mess! 天哪，真是一团糟！| 近义词: omnipotent, all-powerful | 反义词: powerless, weak | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old English *eallmihtig (formed from eall "all" + mihtig "mighty").
