burliest:/ˈbɜːrliɪst/
adjective
最高壮的；最强壮的
superlative adjective of burly: most burly
例句：He was the burliest man in the village. 他是村子里最强壮的人。

例句：The burliest of the wrestlers easily won the match.  摔跤手中最强壮的那位轻松赢得了比赛。


近义词：strongest, most muscular, heftiest, stoutest
反义词：weakest, frailest, slimmest
词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *burly, from Old English *borlic (“excellent, grand, splendid, noble”), from *bor (“noble”) +‎ *-lic. Cognate with Old Saxon burlik (“splendid, grand”), Old High German burlih (“noble”), Dutch *boerlijk (“boorish, rustic, clumsy”). More at boor. The meaning shifted to "large and strong" in the 16th century.
