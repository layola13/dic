agribusinessman:/ˈæɡrɪˌbɪznɪsmæn/| n. |农业企业家，农业商人|A person who works in or owns a business related to agriculture, typically on a large scale.  A person involved in the commercial, industrialized production of agricultural products, especially livestock, crops, and processed food.|The agribusinessman invested heavily in new technology to improve crop yields.  这位农业企业家大力投资新技术以提高作物产量。|Synonyms: agriculturalist, farmer, rancher, producer|Antonyms: consumer, laborer|noun
