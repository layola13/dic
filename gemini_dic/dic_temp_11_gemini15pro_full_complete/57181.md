dowlas:/ˈdaʊləs/| n. |粗糙的亚麻布|A coarse linen cloth, originally used for inexpensive clothing and household items.  It was typically unbleached or only lightly bleached.  |The farmer wore a simple dowlas shirt. 农民穿着一件简单的粗麻布衬衫。|The curtains were made of a cheap dowlas. 窗帘是用廉价的粗麻布做的。|近义词：linen, canvas, burlap|反义词:silk, satin|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dowlas, from Old French *doulas, from Doulas, a town in Brittany, France, where the cloth was originally made.
