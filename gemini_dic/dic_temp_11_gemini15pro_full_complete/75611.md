fugacy:/ˈfjuːɡəsi/| n. |短暂，易逝，无常|The fugacy of life reminds us to cherish every moment.  人生的短暂提醒我们要珍惜每一刻。|例句：The fugacy of youth is a common theme in literature. 青春的易逝是文学中的常见主题。|近义词：transience, ephemerality, brevity|反义词：permanence, eternity|noun

fugacity:/fjuːˈɡæsɪti/| n. |逸度；挥发性|(Chemistry) The tendency of a gas to expand or escape.  (化学) 气体膨胀或逸出的趋势。|例句：The fugacity of a gas is related to its partial pressure. 气体的逸度与其分压有关。|近义词：volatility, vapor pressure|反义词：stability, inertness|noun
