focussing:/ˈfoʊkəsɪŋ/ /ˈfɒkəsɪŋ/ | v. | 集中, 聚焦 |  The act of concentrating on something specific.  It is the present participle of the verb "focus".  Primarily used in British English; "focusing" is the more common spelling in American English. | I am focussing all my energy on finishing this project. 我正集中全部精力完成这个项目。 |  concentrating, honing, zeroing in | distracting, ignoring | verb

focussing:/ˈfoʊkəsɪŋ/ /ˈfɒkəsɪŋ/ | n. | (少见) 集中, 聚焦 | The act or process of bringing something into focus. Less common than the verb form.  | The focussing of the lens is crucial for a sharp image.  镜头的聚焦对于清晰的图像至关重要。| concentration, attention | distraction, inattention | noun
