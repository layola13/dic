noyful: /ˈnɔɪfʊl/
adjective
有害的；令人讨厌的；伤人的
causing or likely to cause annoyance, trouble, or harm.
His noyful comments irritated everyone present.  他令人讨厌的评论激怒了在场的所有人。
近义词: harmful, detrimental, annoying, irritating, vexing, obnoxious
反义词: beneficial, helpful, pleasant, agreeable
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From *noy* (annoy, harm) + *-ful* (full of).  The word "noy" is now obsolete, but its influence remains in words like "annoy" and "noyful."
