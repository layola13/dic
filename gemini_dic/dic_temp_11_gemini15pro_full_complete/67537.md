extraneous:/ɛkˈstreɪniəs/| adj.|外来的，无关的，非主要的|Coming from outside, not forming an essential or vital part, irrelevant.|例句：Extraneous information can make it harder to focus on the main points.  无关的信息会让人更难集中注意要点。|近义词：irrelevant, immaterial, external, foreign|反义词：essential, intrinsic, relevant, inherent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin extraneus "external, foreign," from Latin extra "outside of."
