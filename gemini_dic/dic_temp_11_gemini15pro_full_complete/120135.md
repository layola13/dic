minarets:/ˌmɪnəˈrɛts/ | n. |尖塔；叫拜楼 | A tall slender tower, typically part of a mosque, with a balcony from which a muezzin calls Muslims to prayer. |  The minarets of the mosque were visible from miles away.  清真寺的尖塔在几英里外都能看到。| synonyms: spire, tower, obelisk | antonyms: base, foundation | noun
