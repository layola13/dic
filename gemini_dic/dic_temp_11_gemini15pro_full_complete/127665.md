neet:/niːt/ | n. | 尼特族；啃老族 | A young person who is not in education, employment, or training. | Example: He's been a NEET for a year since dropping out of college.  他从大学辍学后已经当了一年的尼特族了。
Example: The government is trying to help NEETs find jobs. 政府正在努力帮助尼特族找到工作。 | Synonym: idler, loafer, slacker | Antonym: worker, employee | noun
