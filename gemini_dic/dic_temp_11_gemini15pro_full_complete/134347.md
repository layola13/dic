nonintelligences:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənsɪz/
|n.| 非智力因素; 非智力|Lack of intelligence or the aspects of a person's abilities that are not related to intelligence.  It often refers to qualities like emotional intelligence, social skills, or practical abilities that contribute to overall success and well-being, but aren't measured by traditional IQ tests. | Many educators believe that nurturing nonintelligences, such as creativity and empathy, is as important as developing cognitive skills. 许多教育家认为，培养非智力因素，如创造力和同理心，与发展认知技能同等重要。 |Example 2:  His success wasn't solely due to his intellect; his nonintelligences, particularly his persistence and resilience, played a crucial role. 他的成功不仅仅是因为他的智力; 他的非智力因素，尤其是他的毅力和韧性，发挥了至关重要的作用。| synonyms: non-cognitive skills, soft skills | antonyms: intelligences, cognitive abilities | noun
