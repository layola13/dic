outtinkles:/ˌaʊtˈtɪŋkəlz/| v. |发出比...更清脆的声音；胜过...的声音 |Surpasses in tinkling; makes a clearer, brighter, or more delicate tinkling sound than. |The wind chimes outtinkled the nearby brook. 风铃的声音胜过了附近小溪的流水声。|近义词：outdo, surpass, excel, outshine |反义词: lose to, be inferior to |词性:动词

词源:  "Out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "tinkle" (verb meaning to make a light ringing sound).  A relatively modern word formed by combining these elements.
