malaxation:/ˌmælækˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 揉捏；软化；按摩 | The process of softening or kneading something, especially by rubbing or working with the hands.  It can also refer to the softening or breaking down of food in the stomach. | The malaxation of the clay made it easier to mold.  黏土的揉捏使它更容易塑形。 | The stomach's malaxation of food helps with digestion. 胃对食物的揉捏有助于消化。 |  近义词: kneading, massage, softening, manipulation | 反义词: hardening, solidifying | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From French *malaxation*, from Late Latin *malaxātiō*, from Latin *malaxāre* "to knead, soften," from Greek *maláksein* "to soften."
