dilling:/ˈdɪlɪŋ/| n.|莳萝泡菜; 小丑; 傻瓜|A Scandinavian pickled dill.  A silly person, a jester, a fool.|例句：She brought out a jar of dilling. 她拿出一罐莳萝泡菜。

例句：He's such a dilling, always making jokes. 他真是个傻瓜，总是开玩笑。|近义词：pickle, fool, jester, clown|反义词:genius, sage|Noun
