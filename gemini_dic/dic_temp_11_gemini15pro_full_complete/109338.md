lenient:/ˈliːniənt/ | adj. | 宽容的，仁慈的，宽大的 | Showing tolerance or mercy; not strict. | The judge gave the defendant a lenient sentence. 法官对被告从轻判决。| synonyms: compassionate, understanding, easygoing | antonyms: unyielding, rigorous, stringent | adjective
