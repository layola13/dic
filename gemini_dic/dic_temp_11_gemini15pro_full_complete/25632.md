bunchier:/ˈbʌntʃiər/
adjective
更加成束的，更加密集的
more bunched or clustered together; more compact
The flowers were bunchier this year than last. 今年的花比去年开得更密集。
近义词: denser, thicker, more compact
反义词: sparser, thinner, less compact
词性: adjective

bunchier:/ˈbʌntʃiər/
adjective
(of clothing) more gathered or wrinkled
(指衣服)更聚集的，更多褶皱的
Her skirt was bunchier around the waist. 她的裙子腰部有很多褶皱。
近义词: gathered, wrinkled, ruffled
反义词: smooth, flat, unwrinkled
词性: adjective
