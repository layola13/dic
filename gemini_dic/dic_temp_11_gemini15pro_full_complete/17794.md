bedrench:/bɪˈdrɛntʃ/
verb
使湿透，浸透
to wet thoroughly; soak
The sudden downpour bedrenched the hikers. 突如其来的倾盆大雨把徒步旅行者淋得湿透了。
近义词: soak, drench, saturate
反义词: dry, dehydrate
词性: verb

bedrench:/bɪˈdrɛntʃ/
动词
使湿透，浸透
to wet thoroughly; soak
The lawn was bedrenched with dew. 草坪上沾满了露水。

反义词: dry, dehydrate
词性: verb
