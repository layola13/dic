bedecks:/bɪˈdɛks/
verb
装饰，修饰
to decorate or adorn something, typically with numerous small or colorful items.
The Christmas tree was bedecked with twinkling lights and colorful ornaments. 圣诞树上装饰着闪烁的灯光和彩色的装饰品。
The hall was bedecked with flowers for the wedding.  大厅里为了婚礼装饰着鲜花。

adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament, garnish
strip, denude, bare

词性:v.
