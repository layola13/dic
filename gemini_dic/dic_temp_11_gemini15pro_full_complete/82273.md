groundout:/ˈɡraʊndˌaʊt/| n.|滚地球出局|A batted ball that is hit on the ground and results in an out or outs.  A batter hitting a groundout is usually recorded as being "out" at first base, though double plays and other outcomes are possible.|The shortstop fielded the groundout and threw to first for the out. |游击手接住了滚地球，并传给一垒手出局。|近义词:ground ball, roller|反义词:fly out, home run|词性:noun

词源:  Compound of "ground" and "out."  "Ground" refers to the ball hitting the ground, and "out" refers to the resulting out in baseball.
