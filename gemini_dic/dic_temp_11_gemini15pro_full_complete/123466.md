monosperm:/ˈmɒnəʊspɜːm/ | /ˈmɑːnəspɜːrm/ | adj. n. | 单种子的，单精入卵的 | Having or producing only one seed or ovule; characterized by monospermy (the penetration of a single sperm into an egg). |

例句：
The monosperm fruits of this plant are adapted for wind dispersal.
这种植物的单种子果实适于风力传播。

This species is known for its monosperm ovaries.
这个物种以其单胚珠子房而闻名。

近义词：unispermous, single-seeded |
反义词：polyspermous, multi-seeded |
词性：adjective, noun |


词源:
From mono- (one) + sperm (seed)
