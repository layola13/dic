benchmarking:/ˈbɛntʃˌmɑːrkɪŋ/ | n. | 基准测试，标杆管理 | The process of comparing one's business processes and performance metrics to industry bests or best practices from other companies.  Typically, benchmarking involves measuring quality, time, and cost. | Example 1: The company used benchmarking to identify areas for improvement in their customer service. 这家公司使用基准测试来确定其客户服务中需要改进的领域。

Example 2: Benchmarking against competitors helps businesses stay competitive.  与竞争对手进行基准测试有助于企业保持竞争力。 | Synonyms: comparison, evaluation, assessment | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
