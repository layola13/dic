enfeoffment:/ɪnˈfiːfmənt/ | n. | 封地；授予封地；封臣身份 |The act of granting a fief, or the fief itself; the state of being enfeoffed.  | The king granted the duke an enfeoffment of vast lands. 国王将大片土地封给了公爵。| Synonyms: investiture, endowment | Antonyms: dispossession, forfeiture | noun

enfeoffment:/ɪnˈfiːfmənt/ | n. |  册封；授予（职位、权力等）| The act of investing someone with a position, power, or right. |  The enfeoffment of the new CEO with full authority was met with mixed reactions. 新任CEO被授予全权，引发了各种反应。| Synonyms:  appointment, investiture | Antonyms: dismissal, removal | noun
