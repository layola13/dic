morphological:/ˌmɔːrfəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌmɔːrfəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 形态学的，形态的 | Relating to the forms of words, in linguistics. | The word "dogs" has a morphological structure consisting of the root "dog" and the plural suffix "-s".  狗这个词的形态结构由词根“dog”和复数后缀“-s”组成。| synonyms: structural, formal | antonyms: semantic, functional | Word Property: Adjective

morphology:/mɔːrˈfɒlədʒi/ /mɔːrˈfɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 形态学，形态 | The study of the forms of things, in particular. |  The morphology of the virus was studied under a powerful microscope.  在高倍显微镜下研究了病毒的形态。 | synonyms: form, structure | antonyms: formlessness | Word Property: Noun
