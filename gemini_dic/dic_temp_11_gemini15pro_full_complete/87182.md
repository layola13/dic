herding:/ˈhɜːrdɪŋ/| n. |放牧, 群集, 驱赶|The act of bringing together and controlling a group of animals, especially livestock.  It can also refer to the act of gathering or controlling people.|The farmer spent his days herding sheep.  这位农民每天都放羊。|近义词：gathering, shepherding, mustering, corralling|反义词:scattering, dispersing|noun
herding:/ˈhɜːrdɪŋ/| adj. |放牧的|Related to or used for herding.|He has a herding dog. 他有一只牧羊犬。|近义词：pastoral, shepherding|反义词:N/A|adjective
