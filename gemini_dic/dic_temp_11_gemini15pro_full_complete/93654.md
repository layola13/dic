imbroccata:/ˌɪmbrɒˈkɑːtə/ | n. | 击剑术语，意为“突刺命中”；（比喻）一针见血的评论；精准的反驳 | A successful thrust in fencing; (figuratively) a pointed or accurate remark or rejoinder. |  He delivered an imbroccata that silenced his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中一针见血的反驳让对手哑口无言。| 近义词:riposte, thrust, parry | 反义词:miss, failure | 词性:noun

imbroccata:/ˌɪmbrɒˈkɑːtə/ | n. | 一针见血的评论；精准的反驳 | A pointed or accurate remark or rejoinder. | Her imbroccata about the company's financial situation stunned the board members. 她对公司财务状况一针见血的评论让董事会成员震惊不已。| 近义词:quip, retort, rejoinder | 反义词:compliment, praise | 词性:noun

词源: From Italian *imbroccata*, from *imbroccare* "to hit the mark," from *in-* "in" + *brocca* "point, tip of a sword."
