homophony:/hɒˈmɒfəni/ /hɑːˈmɑːfəni/| n. |同音异义词，同音异义|The words "to," "too," and "two" are homophones.|例句：The words "flower" and "flour" are homophones.  “flower”和“flour”是同音异义词。
