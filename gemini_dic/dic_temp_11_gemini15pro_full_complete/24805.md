brothiest:/ˈbrɔːθiɪst/
adjective
最像肉汤的；肉味最浓的
Having the qualities of broth to a marked degree; rich or substantial in flavor.
The brothiest stew I've ever tasted.  我尝过最浓的炖菜。
近义词: richest, heartiest, thickest
反义词: thin, watery, weak
词性: adjective

词源: From broth + -y + -est.  来自 broth（肉汤）+ -y（形容词后缀）+ -est（最高级后缀）。
