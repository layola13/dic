nonacceptances:/ˌnɒnəkˈsɛptənsɪz/
noun
不接受，拒绝接受
The nonacceptances of our offer were disappointing.  我们的提议遭到拒绝，这令人失望。
The project faced numerous nonacceptances from potential investors.  该项目遭到许多潜在投资者的拒绝。
Synonyms: rejections, refusals, declinations
Antonyms: acceptances, approvals
词性: 名词
