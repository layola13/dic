gelatinity:/ˌdʒɛləˈtɪnɪti/
n.
胶凝性，胶质状态
The state or quality of being gelatinous; resemblance to gelatin or jelly.
The gelatinity of the substance made it difficult to handle.  这种物质的胶凝性使它难以处理。
近义词：jellylike, gelatinousness, viscosity
反义词：fluidity, liquidity
词性：名词

gelatin:/ˈdʒɛlətɪn/
n.
凝胶，明胶，胶质
A translucent, colorless, brittle (when dry), flavorless foodstuff, derived from collagen obtained from various animal by-products.
The dessert was made with gelatin. 这道甜点是用明胶做的。
近义词：jelly, agar-agar
反义词：(no direct antonym, perhaps "liquid" in a culinary context)
词性：名词

-ity:/ɪti/
suffix
构成名词的后缀，表示性质，状态
A suffix that forms abstract nouns expressing state or quality.
It turns adjectives like "scarce" into nouns like "scarcity". 它把像“scarce”这样的形容词变成像“scarcity”这样的名词。
近义词：(no direct synonyms for suffixes)
反义词：(no direct antonyms for suffixes)
词性：后缀

词源:
From French gélatine + -ité, from Latin gelātus (frozen, rigid) + -inus (forming adjectives). Ultimately from gelū (frost, cold).
