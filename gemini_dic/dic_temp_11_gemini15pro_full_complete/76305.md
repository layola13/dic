gabbro:/ˈɡæbroʊ/ | n. |辉长岩|A dark, coarse-grained, intrusive igneous rock, composed mainly of plagioclase feldspar and pyroxene.|Gabbro is often used in construction as crushed stone. 辉长岩通常在建筑中用作碎石。|Gabbro forms the base of the oceanic crust. 辉长岩构成了海洋地壳的基底。|Near synonyms: dolerite, basalt (extrusive equivalents)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific rock type)|noun
