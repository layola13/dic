choke-up:/ˈtʃoʊk ʌp/ (US) /ˈtʃəʊk ʌp/ (UK)| phrasal verb | 哽咽；（因紧张或激动）说不出话来；堵塞 | to become unable to speak or breathe properly because of strong emotion, or to cause someone to be in this state |  He choked up when he talked about his wife. 他谈到妻子时哽咽了。 | synonyms:  become emotional, get choked up, well up | antonyms:  calm down, compose oneself | verb

choke-up:/ˈtʃoʊk ʌp/ (US) /ˈtʃəʊk ʌp/ (UK)| n. | 哽咽；堵塞 | the state of being unable to speak or breathe properly because of strong emotion, or a blockage | His choke-up during the speech was noticeable.  他在演讲中哽咽很明显。 | synonyms:  lump in throat, emotional outburst | antonyms:  composure, calmness | noun
