feoff:/ˈfiːf/
n.
封地;采邑
A grant of land, especially in return for service.
He held the land in feoff from the king. 他从国王那里获得这块土地的封地。
近义词:fief, benefice
反义词: (No direct antonym for a grant of land)
词性: noun

feoff:/ˈfiːf/
vt.
授予封地
To grant (land) in feoff.
The king feoffed him with a large estate. 国王授予他一大片地产作为封地。
近义词:enfeoff
反义词: (No direct antonym for granting land)
词性: verb


词源: Middle English *feffen, from Anglo-French feffer, from Old French fieffer, from Medieval Latin *feoffare, of Germanic origin; related to Old High German fihu cattle.
