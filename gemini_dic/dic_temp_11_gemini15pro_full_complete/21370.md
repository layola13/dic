blotched:/blɒtʃt/ /blɑːtʃt/| adj.|有斑点的，弄脏的|Marked with irregular spots or blots of different colors.  Having an uneven or discolored appearance.|The wall was blotched with mildew. 墙壁上满是霉斑。|近义词：spotted, stained, mottled, speckled|反义词：clean, clear, unblemished|词性:形容词

blotch:/blɒtʃ/ /blɑːtʃt/| n.|污点，斑点|A mark or stain, typically one that is irregular in shape.  A blemish or disfigurement.|There was a blotch of ink on the paper. 纸上有一块墨渍。|近义词：spot, stain, mark, blemish|反义词：cleanliness, clarity|词性:名词

blotch:/blɒtʃ/ /blɑːtʃt/| v.|弄脏，涂污|To mark or stain with irregular spots or blots.  To blemish or disfigure.|Tears blotched her makeup. 眼泪弄花了她的妆。|近义词：stain, spot, smear, smudge|反义词：clean, cleanse|词性:动词
