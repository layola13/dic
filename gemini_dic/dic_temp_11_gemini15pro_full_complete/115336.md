manny:/ˈmæni/| n. |男保姆；男护工|A male nanny or caregiver, typically for children.  A man who performs the duties traditionally associated with a female nanny.| My nephew has a manny who takes him to the park every day. 我的侄子有一个男保姆，每天带他去公园。|  Manny also helps with the kids' homework. 曼尼也帮助孩子们做作业。|Synonyms: male nanny, caregiver (male)| Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider governess as a traditionally female counterpart)| Noun
