halftones:/ˈhɑːftoʊnz/ (US), /ˈhɑːftoʊnz/ (UK)| n.|半色调|A reproduction of a continuous tone image (such as a photograph) as a pattern of dots of varying sizes.  The dots are so small that they appear to blend together to create the illusion of continuous tones when viewed from a normal distance.  Halftones are widely used in printing.|The newspaper photo was printed in halftones.  报纸上的照片是用半色调印刷的。|synonyms: screen, mesh, grid|antonyms: continuous tone


halftone:/ˈhɑːftoʊn/ (US), /ˈhɑːftoʊn/ (UK)| adj.|半色调的|Relating to or using a halftone process.|The printer used a halftone screen to reproduce the image. 打印机使用半色调网屏来复制图像。|synonyms: screened|antonyms: continuous tone
