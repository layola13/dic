immiscible:/ɪˈmɪsɪbl/| adj.|不混溶的，不能混合的|Not capable of being mixed or blended together.  Liquids that are immiscible will form separate layers when combined.|Oil and water are immiscible. 油和水不能混溶。|insoluble, non-miscible|miscible, soluble|adjective
immiscibility:/ˌɪmɪsɪˈbɪlɪti/| n.|不混溶性|The inability of two or more substances to mix together to form a homogeneous solution.|The immiscibility of oil and water is a basic scientific principle. 油和水的互不相溶性是一项基本的科学原理。|insolubility|miscibility, solubility|noun
