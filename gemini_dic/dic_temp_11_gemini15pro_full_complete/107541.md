landings:/ˈlændɪŋz/
n.
着陆，登陆，降落
The act of landing; the place where a person or thing lands.
The pilot made a smooth landing. 飞行员平稳着陆。
The troops made a successful landing on the beach. 部队成功登陆海滩。
arrival, touchdown, disembarkation
departure, takeoff, embarkation
noun
