hypertrophied: /haɪˈpɜːrtrəfid/ | adj. | 肥大的，过度增生的 | Enlarged or overgrown beyond normal size; excessively developed. |  The bodybuilder's hypertrophied muscles were impressive.  健美运动员肥大的肌肉令人印象深刻。 |  The constant stress led to a hypertrophied heart.  持续的压力导致心脏肥大。 | Synonyms: enlarged, overgrown, expanded, swollen | Antonyms: atrophied, shrunken, diminished, underdeveloped | adjective

hypertrophied: /haɪˈpɜːrtrəfid/ | v. |  (使)肥大，(使)过度增生 (This is less common as a verb and usually used in a medical/scientific context) | To become or cause to become enlarged or overgrown beyond normal size. | The doctor explained that the patient's heart had hypertrophied due to the high blood pressure. 医生解释说，由于高血压，病人的心脏肥大了。 |  Long-term steroid use can hypertrophy certain muscles. 长期使用类固醇会导致某些肌肉肥大。 | Synonyms: enlarge, overgrow, expand, swell | Antonyms: atrophy, shrink, diminish, underdevelop | verb
