arrester:/əˈrestər/| n.|逮捕者，阻止器，避雷器|A device that stops something, such as lightning or a moving vehicle.|The lightning arrester protected the building from damage. 避雷器保护建筑物免受损坏。|Synonyms: stopper, impediment, inhibitor|Antonyms: accelerator, catalyst|noun
