coachy:/ˈkoʊtʃi/| adj.|指导式的，像教练的，爱指导人的|Having or exhibiting the characteristics of a coach, especially in terms of giving advice or instruction, often in an excessive or unwanted manner.|He had a very coachy way of talking to his colleagues, always offering unsolicited advice. 他跟同事说话的方式很像教练，总是主动提供不必要的建议。|近义词：instructive, mentoring, didactic, preachy|反义词：unhelpful, unsupportive|词性:形容词

coachy:/ˈkoʊtʃi/| adj.|像教练的(指穿着)|Resembling the attire or style of a coach.|She wore a coachy outfit with a whistle around her neck. 她穿着一套像教练的衣服，脖子上还挂着个哨子。|近义词：sporty, athletic|反义词：formal, elegant|词性:形容词
