docimacy:/ˌdɒsɪˈmeɪsi/ /ˌdɑːsɪˈmeɪsi/ | n. | 检验；鉴定；化验 | The process of testing or assaying, especially of ores or drugs, to determine their composition or quality.  The art of examining the genuineness of writings. | The docimacy of the ancient coins revealed they were made of pure gold. 古钱币的鉴定表明它们是由纯金制成的。 | Near synonyms: assay, analysis, examination, testing, verification | Antonyms:  fabrication, falsification | Noun
