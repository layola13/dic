crisper:/ˈkrɪspər/ (US), /ˈkrɪspə/ (UK)| n. adj.|保鲜盒，保鲜的|A container or compartment in a refrigerator designed to keep fruit and vegetables fresh.|例句：I put the lettuce in the crisper. 我把莴苣放进了保鲜盒里。|近义词：refrigerator drawer, produce drawer|反义词: (n.) none, (adj.) wilted, stale|词性: noun, adjective

crisper:/ˈkrɪspər/ (US), /ˈkrɪspə/ (UK)| adj.|（食物）脆的，（空气）清爽的|Firm, fresh, and brittle.|例句：The celery was crisp and refreshing.  芹菜脆而清爽。|近义词： crunchy, firm, fresh|反义词: soggy, soft, limp|词性: adjective
