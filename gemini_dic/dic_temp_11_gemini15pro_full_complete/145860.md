overridden:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdn/ | adj. | 被覆盖的，被重写的 | Overridden describes something, typically a decision or setting, that has been superseded by another, more authoritative one.  It often refers to software where a default setting is changed by a user or another part of the program. | The default settings were overridden by the user's preferences. 用户的偏好覆盖了默认设置。 | The system's automatic controls were overridden by the manual input. 系统的自动控制被手动输入覆盖了。 | Synonyms: superseded, replaced, overruled, canceled | Antonyms: upheld, sustained, reinforced |  adjective
