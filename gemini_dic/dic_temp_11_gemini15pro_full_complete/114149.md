mahayanist:/ˌmɑːhɑːˈjɑːnɪst/ | n. | 大乘佛教徒 | A follower of Mahayana Buddhism, one of the two major traditions of Buddhism. |  例句：The Mahayanist monk emphasized the importance of compassion.  这位大乘佛教僧侣强调了慈悲的重要性。

例句：Many Mahayanists believe in the bodhisattva ideal. 许多大乘佛教徒相信菩萨理想。| 近义词：Bodhisattva (in certain contexts), Mahayana Buddhist | 反义词：Hinayanist, Theravada Buddhist | 词性：noun
