natureless:/ˈneɪtʃərlɪs/
adjective
无自然的；缺乏自然美的；非天然的
Lacking natural qualities or characteristics; artificial; unnatural.
The shopping mall was a natureless environment, filled with artificial light and manufactured goods.  这个购物中心是一个毫无自然气息的环境，充满了人造光和工业制品。
The plastic flowers looked natureless and stiff.  这些塑料花看起来很不自然，很僵硬。


近义词:artificial, unnatural, synthetic, man-made, inorganic
反义词:natural, organic, authentic, real, genuine
词性:adjective
