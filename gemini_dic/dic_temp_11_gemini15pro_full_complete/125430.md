multipinnate:/ˌmʌltiˈpɪneɪt/ | adj. | 多回羽状的 | Having multiple sets of pinnate structures, where the leaflets are themselves pinnately divided.  This creates a feathery or fern-like appearance. |  Example: The multipinnate leaves of the Jacaranda tree provide dappled shade. 蓝花楹树多回羽状的叶子提供了斑驳的树荫。

Example:  Ferns often have multipinnate fronds. 蕨类植物通常有多回羽状的叶子。 | Synonym:  decompound,  bipinnate (when twice pinnate) | Antonym: simple (referring to leaf structure) | word type: adjective
