overheavy:/ˌoʊvərˈhɛvi/| adj.|负担过重的|Loaded with too much responsibility or work.|He was overheavy with responsibilities and felt stressed. 他肩负的责任太重，感到压力很大。|近义词：overburdened, overloaded|反义词:unburdened|词性:形容词
