conquerors: /ˈkɒŋkərəz/ (UK) /ˈkɑŋkərərz/ (US)
noun
征服者，胜利者
People who conquer or overcome something, typically a place or people in war.
The conquerors plundered the city after the siege. 征服者在围城之后洗劫了这座城市。
victors, champions, subjugators
defeated, vanquished
