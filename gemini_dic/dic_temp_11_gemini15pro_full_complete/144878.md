over-reservedness:/ˌəʊvər rɪˈzɜːvdnəs/
|n.| 过分拘谨，过分矜持 |The state of being excessively reserved or restrained in one's behavior and interactions with others.  This often involves being shy, quiet, and hesitant to express oneself openly. |Her over-reservedness made it difficult for her to make new friends. 她过分的拘谨让她很难结交新朋友。| reserve, reticence, shyness, modesty, inhibition|outgoingness, extroversion, boldness, confidence, assertiveness|noun

over-reserved:/ˌəʊvər rɪˈzɜːvd/
|adj.| 过分拘谨的，过分矜持的|Excessively reserved or restrained in one's behavior and interactions with others.  This often involves being shy, quiet, and hesitant to express oneself openly. |He was so over-reserved that he barely spoke at the party. 他太拘谨了，在派对上几乎没说话。|reserved, reticent, shy, modest, inhibited|outgoing, extroverted, bold, confident, assertive|adjective
