monotropa:/ˌmɒnəˈtrəʊpə/ | n. |水晶蘭屬；單花錫杖花|A genus of parasitic flowering plants in the family Ericaceae, commonly known as ghost plants or Indian pipes.  These plants lack chlorophyll and obtain nutrients from mycorrhizal fungi that are associated with tree roots.|例句：Monotropa uniflora is a fascinating example of a myco-heterotrophic plant. 水晶蘭屬單花錫杖花是菌根異養植物的一個引人入勝的例子。

例句：The Monotropa genus contains several species, all characterized by their lack of chlorophyll. 水晶蘭屬包含幾個物種，它們的共同特點是缺乏葉綠素。|近義词：Indian pipe, ghost plant|反义词: (No direct antonym as it is a genus name) |词性: noun
