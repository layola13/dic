autoecism:/ˌɔːtˈiːsɪzəm/ | n. | 自居 | The condition of a parasite living on its usual host. |  The louse exhibits autoecism, infesting only humans.  这种虱子表现出自居性，只寄生在人类身上。| 近义词: monoxeny | 反义词: heteroecism | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *autos* (self) + *oikos* (house) + *-ismos* (forming abstract nouns).
