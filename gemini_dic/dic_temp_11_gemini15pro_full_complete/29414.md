catadicrotism: /ˌkætəˈdaɪkrətɪzəm/ | n. | 双重脉搏；重搏脉 |  A condition where a single heartbeat produces two palpable pulses, often due to a reflected pulse wave from the periphery.  | The patient's catadicrotism was evident in the arterial waveform displayed on the monitor. 病人的重搏脉在显示器上显示的动脉波形中很明显。 |  dicrotism, double pulse |  monocrotism (although not a perfect opposite, it refers to a single pulse per heartbeat) | noun

catadicrotic: /ˌkætəˈdaɪkrɒtɪk/ | adj. |  重搏脉的；双重脉搏的 | Relating to or characterized by catadicrotism. | The catadicrotic pulse was a sign of the underlying cardiovascular issue. 重搏脉是潜在心血管问题的征兆。| dicrotic |  monocrotic | adjective
