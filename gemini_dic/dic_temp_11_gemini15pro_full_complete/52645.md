digitalising (British English) /ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/
digitalizing (American English) /ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/
| v. |数字化，数位化|The process of converting information into a digital format.  This involves representing information as a series of numbers, typically 0s and 1s, that can be understood and processed by computers.| Many companies are digitalising their archives to save space and improve accessibility. 许多公司正在将其档案数字化，以节省空间并提高可访问性。| digitizing, computerizing | analogizing, manual | verb
