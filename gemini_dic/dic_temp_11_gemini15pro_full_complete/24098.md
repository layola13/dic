breviloquence:/ˌbrɛvɪˈləʊkwəns/
noun

The quality of speaking or writing briefly; conciseness.
His breviloquence was appreciated in the fast-paced business meeting.  他在快节奏的商务会议上简洁的表达受到了赞赏。
