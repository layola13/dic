deturb:/dɪˈtɜːrb/
verb
扰乱，使不安
To remove from a place or position; displace.  To disturb the natural arrangement of.
The construction crew deturbed the ancient burial site.  施工队扰乱了古代墓地。
The landslide deturbed the layers of rock. 山体滑坡扰乱了岩层。
displace, unsettle, disturb, disarrange
order, arrange, settle
v.
