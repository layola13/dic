mizzen:/ˈmɪzən/| n. adj.|后桅帆；后桅|The mizzen mast is the third mast from forward on a ship having three or more masts.  后桅是三桅或多桅船上从船头算起的第三根桅杆。|The ship lost its mizzen mast in the storm.  这艘船在暴风雨中失去了后桅。|近义词:aftermost sail|反义词:foremast|n.

mizzen:/ˈmɪzən/| adj.|后桅的|The mizzen mast is shorter than the main mast. 后桅比主桅短。|The mizzen sail was ripped by the strong winds. 后桅帆被强风撕裂了。|近义词:rear, back|反义词:fore, front|adj.
