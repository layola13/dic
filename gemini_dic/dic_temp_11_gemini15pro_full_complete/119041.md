methysticin: /ˌmɛθɪˈstɪsɪn/ | n. | 美乐醉素; 麦斯卡灵 |  A crystalline lactone, C₁₁H₁₄O₄, found in kava and having anesthetic and soporific properties.  It's the primary psychoactive component of kava (Piper methysticum), a plant native to the western Pacific islands. | Kava drinks containing methysticin are traditionally consumed for their relaxing and sedative effects.  美乐醉素是卡瓦胡椒中的主要精神活性成分，卡瓦酒传统上因其放松和镇静作用而被饮用。|  Methysticin is one of the main kavalactones responsible for kava's effects. 美乐醉素是卡瓦胡椒产生作用的主要卡瓦内酯之一。 | Synonyms: kavain, dihydromethysticin, yangonin | Antonyms: stimulant | noun
