crap-shot:/ˈkræpˌʃɑːt/| n.| 瞎猜，碰运气，孤注一掷|A wild guess or attempt; something done with little chance of success.|He took a crap-shot and invested all his money in the new company. 他孤注一掷，把所有的钱都投资到这家新公司。|近义词：long shot, gamble, guess|反义词: calculated risk, sure thing|词性:名词

crap-shot:/ˈkræpˌʃɑːt/| adj.| 碰运气的，孤注一掷的|Done with little chance of success; based on a wild guess.|It was a crap-shot decision, but it paid off. 这是一个碰运气的决定，但最终成功了。|近义词：risky, speculative, chancy|反义词: safe, certain, calculated|词性:形容词
