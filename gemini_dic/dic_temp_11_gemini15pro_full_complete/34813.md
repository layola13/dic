clinally:/ˈklaɪnəli/| adv.| 渐变地，呈梯度变化地|Characterized by gradual change over a geographic area, forming a cline.|The species varies clinally, with larger individuals found in the north.|近义词：gradually, progressively|反义词: abruptly, suddenly|词性:副词

Etymology: From cline + -al + -ly.  "Cline" comes from the Greek "klinein" meaning "to slope."
