groupon:/ˈɡruːpɒn/ (US) /ˈɡruːpɒn/ (UK)| n.|团购，优惠券，Groupon（公司名）|A website or app that offers deals and discounts on goods and services, often involving group purchases.|例句：I bought a Groupon for a spa day. 我在Groupon上买了一张水疗日优惠券。|例句：Groupon offers deals on everything from restaurants to travel. Groupon提供从餐厅到旅游的各种优惠。|近义词：deal, coupon, voucher, discount, offer|反义词:premium, surcharge|词性:noun

Etymology:  A portmanteau of "group" and "coupon."
