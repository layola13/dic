bicarbureted: /ˌbaɪˈkɑːrbjəreɪtɪd/ /ˌbaɪˈkɑːrbjʊˌreɪtɪd/
adjective
双碳化的；经过两次渗碳的
Combined or treated with two carbonates.  Specifically refers to a method of enriching illuminating gas with the vapor of volatile hydrocarbons, typically by passing it through naphtha.
The bicarbureted hydrogen gas provided a brighter light than ordinary coal gas. 双碳氢气比普通的煤气提供了更明亮的光。
近义词: carburetted, enriched (referring to gas)
反义词: None (specific technical term, no direct opposite)
词性: adjective

bicarbureted: /ˌbaɪˈkɑːrbjəreɪtɪd/ /ˌbaɪˈkɑːrbjʊˌreɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)
使双碳化
Having undergone a process of bicarburation.
The gas, once bicarbureted, was stored in large tanks.  一旦经过双碳化处理，这种气体就被储存在大型储罐中。
近义词: carburetted, enriched (referring to gas)
反义词: None (specific technical term, no direct opposite)
词性: verb (past participle)


Etymology:
From "bi-" (two) + "carburet" (to combine or treat with carbon) + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Carburet" itself comes from "carbon" + "-uret" (a suffix denoting a binary compound).
