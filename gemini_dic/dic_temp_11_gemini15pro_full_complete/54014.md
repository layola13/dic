discubitory:/dɪsˈkʌbɪtɔːri/
adjective
斜倚的，躺卧的
Relating to lying down or reclining.
The doctor recommended a discubitory posture to alleviate the patient's back pain. 医生建议病人采取卧姿来缓解背痛。
The Roman emperor often held discubitory feasts, reclining on couches while dining.  罗马皇帝经常举行斜倚的宴会，用餐时斜倚在长榻上。
近义词:recumbent, reclining, prostrate
反义词:upright, standing, erect
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *discubitorius, from discumbere (to recline).
