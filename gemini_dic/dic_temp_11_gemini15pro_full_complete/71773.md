fleas:/fliːz/
n.
跳蚤
small, wingless, jumping insects that feed on the blood of mammals and birds
The dog was scratching constantly because of fleas.  这只狗因为跳蚤不断地抓挠。

近义词:parasites
反义词:(无直接反义词)
词性:名词
