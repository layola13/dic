allegiant:/əˈliːdʒənt/
adjective
忠诚的，忠贞的
Loyal and committed in allegiance to a person, group, or cause.
例句：The allegiant fans cheered their team on.  忠实的粉丝为他们的球队加油。

例句：She remained allegiant to her principles despite the pressure. 尽管面临压力，她仍然忠于自己的原则。

近义词：loyal, faithful, devoted, steadfast, true
反义词：disloyal, unfaithful, treacherous, perfidious
词性：adjective
