nonresurrectional:/ˌnɒnˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃənəl/
adjective
非复活的; 不涉及复活的
Relating to or characterized by the absence of resurrection.
The philosopher presented a nonresurrectional view of the afterlife. 这位哲学家提出了一个不涉及复活的来世观。
His nonresurrectional beliefs challenged traditional religious doctrines.  他的非复活信仰挑战了传统的宗教教义。
Near Synonyms: non-resurrectionist, secular, earthly, temporal, mundane
Antonyms: resurrectional, spiritual, otherworldly, eternal, heavenly
词性: 形容词
