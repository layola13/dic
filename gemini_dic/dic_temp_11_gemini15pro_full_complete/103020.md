jejuna's:/dʒɪˈdʒuːnəz/
noun
空肠的
belonging to or relating to the jejunum (the middle part of the small intestine)
The jejuna's absorption capacity is impressive.  空肠的吸收能力令人印象深刻。
近义词:intestinal
反义词:None
词性:n. (possessive form)
