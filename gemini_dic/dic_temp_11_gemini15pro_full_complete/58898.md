durian:/ˈdʊəriən/ | n. | 榴莲 | A large, oval, spiny fruit of a tropical Asian tree, containing a creamy, strong-smelling pulp. | Example 1: The durian is known as the "king of fruits" in Southeast Asia. 东南亚的榴莲被称为“水果之王”。

Example 2:  She couldn't stand the smell of durian. 她受不了榴莲的味道。| 近义词:  (in terms of strong smell)  jackfruit | 反义词:  (No direct opposite, but could use mild fruits in terms of smell/taste) apple, banana | 词性: noun
