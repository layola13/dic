featherweight:/ˈfeðərˌweɪt/| n. adj.|羽量级；轻量级人物|A boxer or other competitor in the lightest weight class.  A person or thing that is insignificant or unimportant. |例句：He's a featherweight in the political arena. 他在政界是个无足轻重的人物。

例句：The featherweight champion knocked out his opponent in the first round. 羽量级冠军在第一回合就击倒了对手。|近义词：lightweight, insignificant, unimportant|反义词：heavyweight, significant, important|词性:名词，形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From feather + weight.  First recorded in the early 19th century, originally referring to a very light weight in boxing.
