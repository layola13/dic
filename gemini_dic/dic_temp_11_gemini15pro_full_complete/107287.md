lambasted: /læmˈbeɪstɪd/ /læmˈbæstɪd/
verb
严厉批评，痛斥
criticized harshly; attacked verbally
The coach lambasted the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉批评了球队的糟糕表现。
The film was lambasted by critics. 这部电影遭到了评论家的抨击。
synonyms:  criticize, censure, condemn, denounce, castigate, excoriate, berate
antonyms: praise, commend, laud, applaud
词性: 动词
