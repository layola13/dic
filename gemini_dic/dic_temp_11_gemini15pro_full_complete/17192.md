Bauhauses: /ˈbaʊˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
包豪斯（院校/风格）
Bauhaus refers to a German art school operational from 1919 to 1933 that combined crafts and the fine arts, and also the approach to design that it publicized and taught.
The Bauhaus building in Dessau is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 德绍的包豪斯建筑是联合国教科文组织世界遗产。
Many modern furniture designs are influenced by the Bauhaus. 许多现代家具设计都受到包豪斯的影响。

近义词: school, style, movement
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑一些与包豪斯风格对立的设计流派，例如：Art Deco, Victorian)
词性: 名词 (plural of Bauhaus)
