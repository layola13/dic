indissoluble: /ˌɪndɪˈsɒljəbl/ | adj. | 不可分解的；不可溶解的；牢固的 |  They believed in the indissoluble bond of marriage. 他们相信婚姻是牢不可破的。 | This substance is indissoluble in water. 这种物质不溶于水。 | 近义词：inseparable, permanent, stable | 反义词：dissoluble, fragile, unstable | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *indissolubilis, from in- "not" + dissolubilis "dissoluble," from dis- "apart" + solvere "to loosen, dissolve."
