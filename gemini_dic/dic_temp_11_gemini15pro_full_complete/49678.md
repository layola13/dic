demister:/diːˈmɪstər/
n.|除雾器; 去雾器|A device used to remove droplets of liquid from a stream of gas or vapor.|The demister in the bathroom mirror prevents it from fogging up. 浴室镜子里的除雾器防止它起雾。|Synonyms: dehumidifier, defogger|Antonyms: humidifier|noun

demisters:/diːˈmɪstərz/
n.|除雾器; 去雾器（复数）|Multiple devices used to remove droplets of liquid from a stream of gas or vapor.|The factory installed new demisters to improve air quality. 工厂安装了新的除雾器以改善空气质量。|Synonyms: dehumidifiers, defoggers|Antonyms: humidifiers|noun
