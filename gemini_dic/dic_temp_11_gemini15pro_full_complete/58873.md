durancy: /ˈdjʊərənsi/ (This pronunciation is constructed as the word is not standard English) | n. | 持续时间，持续; 久暂 |  The duration of something. This word is not common and considered non-standard.  Use "duration" instead.  | The *durancy* of the storm was surprisingly short. (Non-standard usage.  Prefer: The duration of the storm was surprisingly short.) | 近义词：duration, length, span, term | 反义词: brevity, shortness | 词性: noun

Etymology: Likely a back-formation from "durable" or a variant of "duration," influenced by words ending in "-ancy." However, it's not etymologically sound and not recognized by major dictionaries.
