overanalyzing: /ˌoʊvərˈænəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度分析
Thinking about or examining something in too much detail.
She's overanalyzing the situation and making herself anxious. 她过度分析情况，让自己变得焦虑。
He tends to overanalyze things, which often leads to inaction. 他倾向于过度分析事情，这常常导致 inaction（不作为）。
近义词: overthinking, scrutinizing, dissecting
反义词: simplifying, skimming, glancing
词性: verb (present participle)

overanalyze: /ˌoʊvərˈænəˌlaɪz/
verb
过度分析
To analyze something too much.
Don't overanalyze the email - it's just a friendly reminder. 不要过度分析这封邮件 - 它只是一个友好的提醒。
It's easy to overanalyze things when you're stressed. 当你压力大的时候，很容易过度分析事情。
近义词: overthink, scrutinize, dissect
反义词: simplify, skim, glance
词性: verb

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "analyze" (from French *analyser*, from Greek *analyein* "to loosen, dissolve, resolve," from *ana-* "up, throughout" + *lyein* "to loosen").
