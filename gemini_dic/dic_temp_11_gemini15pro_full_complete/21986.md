bohriums:/ˈbɔːriəmz/ | n. | 钅波 | A synthetic radioactive chemical element with the symbol Bh and atomic number 107. It is extremely radioactive; its most stable known isotope, bohrium-270, has a half-life of approximately 61 seconds. | Bohriums are very unstable and decay quickly. 钅波非常不稳定，会迅速衰变。 |  Bohrium is named after physicist Niels Bohr. 钅波是以物理学家尼尔斯·玻尔的名字命名的。 | synonyms: element 107,  transactinide | antonyms: N/A | noun
