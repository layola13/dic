grievously:/ˈɡriːvəsli/
adverb
严重地；grievously ill
seriously; causing or experiencing great suffering
He was grievously wounded in the attack. 他在袭击中受了重伤。
The company's profits have been grievously affected by the recession.  公司的利润受到了经济衰退的严重影响。
近义词: severely, seriously, badly, gravely
反义词: slightly, mildly, superficially
词性: adverb
词源: From Middle English *grevousli, from Old French griefvement, from grief (“grief, sorrow, suffering”) + -ment.
