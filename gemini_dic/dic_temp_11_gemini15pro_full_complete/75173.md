frivolousness:/frɪˈvɑːləsnəs/
n.|轻浮；无聊；琐碎|The frivolousness of the conversation annoyed her.  谈话的轻浮让她很恼火。|
frivolousness:|n.|Lack of seriousness; lightheartedness, often to an inappropriate degree.  缺乏严肃性；轻浮，通常指不恰当的程度。|He was criticized for the frivolousness of his remarks. 他因言论轻浮而受到批评。|
frivolousness:|近义词:levity, flippancy, silliness, triviality|反义词:seriousness, gravity, solemnity|名词|


frivolous:/ˈfrɪvələs/
adj.|轻浮的；无聊的；琐碎的|She spent her money on frivolous things. 她把钱花在无聊的事情上。|
frivolous:|adj.|Not having any serious purpose or value.  没有严肃的目的或价值。|A frivolous lawsuit.  一件无聊的诉讼。|
frivolous:|近义词:silly, foolish, trifling, petty|反义词:serious, important, significant|形容词|
