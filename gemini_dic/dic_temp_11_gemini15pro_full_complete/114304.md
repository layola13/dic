mainstreamer:/ˈmeɪnˌstriːmər/ | n. |主流人士；追随主流的人；迎合大众口味的人|A person who holds conventional or mainstream views, especially in cultural or political matters.  A person who follows the prevailing trends or fashions. | Example 1:  Hollywood mainstreamers tend to avoid controversial topics. 好莱坞主流人士倾向于避开有争议的话题。

Example 2:  He's not a mainstreamer; he prefers independent films and music. 他不是一个追随主流的人；他更喜欢独立电影和音乐。 | 近义词: conformist, traditionalist | 反义词: nonconformist, maverick, bohemian | 词性: noun
