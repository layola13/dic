one-piece:/ˈwʌn piːs/| adj. n.|一件式的，连体的；连体衣，连衣裙|A single garment combining a top and bottom, such as a swimsuit or a jumpsuit.|She wore a stylish one-piece swimsuit to the beach. 她穿着一件时尚的连体泳衣去了海滩。|近义词：all-in-one, single-piece|反义词:two-piece, separate|词性:形容词, 名词
one-piece:/wʌnˈpiːs/| n.|一件，整体|Something made of a single piece or part.|The sculptor carved the statue from a single one-piece block of marble. 雕塑家从一块完整的单块大理石雕刻了这座雕像。|近义词：unit, whole|反义词:multi-piece, fragmented|词性:名词
