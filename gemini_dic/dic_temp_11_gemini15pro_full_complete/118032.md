menace:/ˈmenəs/ (US), /ˈmɛnɪs/ (UK)| n. vt.|威胁，危险；恐吓，威胁|Something that is likely to cause harm; a threat or danger.  To threaten, especially in a vague or indirect way.|The rising crime rate is a menace to society.  犯罪率上升是对社会的威胁。 He menaced her with a knife. 他用刀威胁她。|近义词：threat, danger, peril, hazard, intimidation|反义词：safety, security, protection|noun, verb
