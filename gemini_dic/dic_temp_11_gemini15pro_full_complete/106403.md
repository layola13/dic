koori:/ˈkʊəri/ /ˈkɔːri/| n.|澳洲土著|An Aboriginal person, especially one from southeastern Australia.  This term is now generally considered outdated and potentially offensive, and should be used with caution.  "First Nations people" or "Indigenous Australians" are preferred.|例句：He identified as a Koori man. 他认为自己是一个库里人。

例句：Koori elders led the ceremony. 库里族长老主持了仪式。|近义词：Aboriginal, Indigenous Australian|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "non-Indigenous")|词性:noun
