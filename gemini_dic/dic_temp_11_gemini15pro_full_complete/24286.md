brigandishly:/brɪˈɡændɪʃli/
adverb
像土匪一样地；粗暴地；放荡不羁地
In a manner resembling a brigand; like a robber, outlaw, or bandit; in a rough, swaggering, or lawless way.
He grinned brigandishly, twirling his mustache. 他像土匪一样咧嘴一笑，捻着他的胡子。
近义词: roguishly, mischievously, rakishly
反义词: politely, respectfully, properly
词性: adverb

词源: From brigand + -ish + -ly.  Brigand comes from the Italian word "brigante," ultimately from a Vulgar Latin word meaning "to fight."
