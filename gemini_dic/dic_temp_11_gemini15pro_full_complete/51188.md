destabilization:/diːˌsteɪbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.| 不稳定，动摇 | The act of making something unstable or less secure. |The destabilization of the region is a major concern. 该地区的不稳定是一个主要问题。| synonyms: disruption, undermining | antonyms: stabilization, strengthening | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (meaning "reverse" or "remove") + stabilize (meaning "to make stable").  Stabilize comes from stable, which ultimately derives from the Latin word "stabilis" meaning "firm" or "steadfast."
