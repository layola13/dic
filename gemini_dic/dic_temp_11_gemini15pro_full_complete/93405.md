illiterateness:/ɪˈlɪtərətnəs/
n.
文盲; 无文化;
the state of being unable to read or write.
The high rate of illiterateness is a serious problem for the country.  这个国家的高文盲率是一个严重的问题。
illiteracy, ignorance
literacy, education
