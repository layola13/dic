acquirable:/əˈkwaɪərəbl/
adjective
可获得的；可获取的
That means that true happiness is acquirable. 这意味着真正的幸福是可以获得的。
The information is easily acquirable. 这些信息很容易获得。
近义词:obtainable, attainable, achievable
反义词:unobtainable, unattainable
词性:adjective
词源:From acquire + -able.
