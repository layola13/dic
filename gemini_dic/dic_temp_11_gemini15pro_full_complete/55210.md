disservice:/dɪˈsɜːrvɪs/
n.|损害，伤害；帮倒忙|An act that harms or hinders someone or something rather than helping them.|例句：By not telling her the truth, you did her a great disservice. 你没有告诉她真相，这对她很不利。

例句：Claiming that he was ill was a disservice to his team.  声称他生病了对他的团队是一种伤害。

|近义词：harm, detriment, injury, damage|反义词：service, benefit, help, aid|词性:名词
