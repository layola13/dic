on-the-fence:/ˌɒn ðə ˈfɛns/ /ˌɑːn ðə ˈfɛns/| adj.|犹豫不决的，观望的|Being in a state of indecision, unable to choose between two options.|例句：He's still on the fence about whether to accept the job offer. 他还在犹豫是否接受这份工作邀请。

例句：She's on the fence about which candidate to vote for. 她还在犹豫要投票给哪个候选人。

近义词：undecided, hesitant, ambivalent, uncertain|反义词:decided, resolute, committed|词性:形容词
