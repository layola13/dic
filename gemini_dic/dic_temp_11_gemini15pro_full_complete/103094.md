jeopardy:/ˈdʒepərdi/ | n. |危险，风险，危难|Exposure to or imminence of death, loss, or injury.|The hikers were in jeopardy when the storm hit the mountain.  徒步旅行者在暴风雨袭击山区时处于危险之中。|Synonyms: danger, peril, risk, hazard|Antonyms: safety, security|noun


jeopardize:/ˈdʒepərdaɪz/ | vt. |危及，损害|Put (someone or something) into a situation in which there is a danger of loss, harm, or failure.|His health was jeopardized by overwork.  过度工作损害了他的健康。|Synonyms: endanger, imperil, risk, threaten|Antonyms: protect, safeguard|verb
