imagistic:/ɪˈmædʒɪstɪk/
adjective
意象的；图像的；形象化的
pertaining to or consisting of images; characterized by imagery; vivid or graphic
Her poems are highly imagistic, filled with concrete details that create vivid mental pictures. 她的诗歌充满了意象，具体的细节创造了生动的画面。
近义词:visual, graphic, pictorial, vivid
反义词:abstract, conceptual
