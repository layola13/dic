nontemptations: /ˌnɒntempˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 无诱惑之物；非诱惑物 |  Lack of temptations; things that do not entice or allure.  It refers to a state or situation where there is an absence of things that would typically tempt or attract someone. |  Living a monastic life offered him freedom from nontemptations of the secular world.  修道院的生活让他摆脱了世俗世界的诱惑。|  The spartan environment provided a haven from nontemptations.  斯巴达式的环境提供了一个远离诱惑的避风港。 | synonyms: lack of allure, absence of enticement | antonyms: temptations, allurements | noun
