goldenly:/ˈɡoʊldənli/| adv.|金黄地，灿烂地|In a golden or splendid manner; radiantly.|The sun shone goldenly on the fields. 太阳金灿灿地照耀着田野。|近义词：brightly, radiantly, splendidly|反义词:dully, darkly|词性:副词

词源: From "golden" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Golden" comes from Old English "gylden," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*gulþinaz."
