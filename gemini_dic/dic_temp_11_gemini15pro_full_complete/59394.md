earsplitting:/ˈɪrsplɪtɪŋ/| adj.|震耳欲聋的，刺耳的|Extremely loud and harsh, as if capable of splitting the ears.|The earsplitting sound of the explosion made everyone jump. 爆炸的震耳欲聋的声音吓了所有人一跳.|近义词：deafening, piercing, loud, shrill|反义词:quiet, soft, muted|词性:形容词
Etymology:  'ear' (Old English 'ēare') + 'splitting' (present participle of 'split' - Old English 'splītan', meaning to divide forcefully).  The word literally describes a sound so loud it feels like it could split one's ears.
