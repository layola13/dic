juridically:/dʒʊˈrɪdɪkli/
adverb
在法律上；合法地
From a legal standpoint; according to law.
The contract is juridically binding.  该合同在法律上具有约束力。
The land was juridically his. 这块土地在法律上是属于他的。
Synonyms: legally, lawfully
Antonyms: illegally, unlawfully
词性: 副词
