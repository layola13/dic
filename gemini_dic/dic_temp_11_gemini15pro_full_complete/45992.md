cyanide:/ˈsaɪənaɪd/ | n. | 氰化物；氰化物毒药 | A highly toxic chemical compound containing the cyano group, CN.  It is used in various industrial processes but is also extremely poisonous. | Potassium cyanide is a potent poison. 氰化钾是一种剧毒的毒药。| synonyms: prussic acid | antonyms: antidote | noun


cyanide:/ˈsaɪənaɪd/ | adj. | 氰化物的 | Containing or relating to cyanide. | Cyanide poisoning can be fatal.  氰化物中毒可能是致命的。| synonyms: cyanic | antonyms: N/A | adjective
