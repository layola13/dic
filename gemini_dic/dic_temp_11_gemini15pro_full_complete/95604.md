incommodity:/ˌɪnkəˈmɒdəti/ | n. |不便；麻烦；累赘；商品缺乏 |Lack of convenience; something inconvenient or troublesome; scarcity of commodities. | The incommodity of the hotel's location made it difficult to find restaurants. 这家酒店位置不便，很难找到餐馆。| 近义词: inconvenience, disadvantage, hardship, scarcity | 反义词: convenience, advantage, ease, abundance | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *incommoditas, from Latin in- "not" + commoditas "convenience, suitability".
