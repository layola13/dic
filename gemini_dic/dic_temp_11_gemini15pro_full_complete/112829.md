luciferously:/luːˈsɪfərəsli/ | adv. | 恶魔般地，邪恶地，充满恶意地 |  In a manner resembling Lucifer; diabolically, wickedly, malevolently.  Characterized by an evil, malicious, or wicked nature, often with a sense of cunning or deceit. | He smiled luciferously as he revealed his plan to sabotage the competition. 他恶魔般地笑着，透露了他破坏比赛的计划。 | The villain luciferously plotted the downfall of the hero. 这个恶棍邪恶地策划了英雄的垮台。 | 近义词: diabolically, fiendishly, malevolently, wickedly | 反义词: angelically, benevolently, kindly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Lucifer + -ously. Lucifer, the name of the devil, is derived from Latin *lucifer "light-bringer," from lux "light" + ferre "to carry, bear."
