dweeb:/dwiːb/ | n. |  呆子; 书呆子; 笨蛋 |  A person who is physically awkward and socially inept, often considered to be overly intellectual. Someone who is perceived as unfashionable, boring, or overly studious. |  He was such a dweeb in high school, always carrying around a stack of textbooks. 他高中时就是个书呆子，总是抱着一堆课本。|  nerd, geek, wonk | cool, popular, hip | noun
