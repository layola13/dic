defeating:/dɪˈfiːtɪŋ/
v. |击败，战胜；使失败；阻止；挫败|
defeating someone in a game 在游戏中击败某人
The government's proposals were defeated in Parliament. 政府的提案在议会中被否决了。
近义词: conquering, overcoming, vanquishing, besting, trouncing
反义词: losing, failing, succumbing, yielding
词性: 动词的现在分词形式，也可作形容词

defeating:/dɪˈfiːtɪŋ/
adj. |令人沮丧的，使人泄气的|
It was a defeating experience. 这是一次令人沮丧的经历。
The constant setbacks were defeating.  不断的挫折令人泄气。
近义词: discouraging, disheartening, demoralizing, frustrating
反义词: encouraging, heartening, inspiring, uplifting
词性: 形容词


词源:  来自动词 defeat，源自古法语 deffaiter “undo, destroy,"  字面意思是 "un-do,"  来自 de- "un-" + faire "to do" (来自拉丁语 facere).
