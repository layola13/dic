niopo:/ˈniːoʊpoʊ/| n. |尼奥波，一种鼻烟|A hallucinogenic snuff prepared from the roasted seeds and pods of the Anadenanthera peregrina tree, used in indigenous rituals in parts of South America.|The shaman offered niopo to the participants of the ceremony.|近义词：cohoba, yopo, vilca|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of snuff)| noun
