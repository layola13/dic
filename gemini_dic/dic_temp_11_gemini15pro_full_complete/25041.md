bryozoum:/ˈbraɪəˌzoʊəm/ | n. |苔藓虫，苔藓动物|A small, aquatic invertebrate animal that forms colonies, often resembling moss.  These colonies can create large structures like reefs.| 例句：The bryozoum colony was attached to the underside of the rock.  苔藓虫群体附着在岩石的底部。

例句：Divers observed a colorful bryozoum reef. 潜水员观察到一个色彩斑斓的苔藓虫礁。|近义词：moss animal |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
Etymology: From Modern Latin *Bryozoa, from Ancient Greek βρύον (brúon, “moss”) + ζῷον (zôion, “animal”).
