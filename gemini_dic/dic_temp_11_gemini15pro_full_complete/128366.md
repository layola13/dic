neutrophil: /ˈnjuːtrəfɪl/ | n. | 中性粒细胞 | A type of white blood cell that is an important part of the immune system and helps the body fight infection by engulfing and destroying bacteria and other foreign particles.  |  A high neutrophil count often indicates an infection. 中性粒细胞计数高通常表明感染。 |  The doctor ordered a neutrophil count as part of the blood test. 医生要求进行中性粒细胞计数作为血液测试的一部分。 | synonyms: polymorphonuclear leukocyte, PMN | antonyms: lymphocyte | noun
