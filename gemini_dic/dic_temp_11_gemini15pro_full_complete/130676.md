nonaesthetic:/ˌnɒnɛsˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 非审美的，不美观的 | Lacking aesthetic qualities; not pleasing to the senses. | The nonaesthetic design of the building was criticized by many. 这栋建筑缺乏美感的设计受到了很多人的批评。 | The nonaesthetic arrangement of furniture made the room feel cluttered.  家具不美观的摆放让房间感觉很杂乱。| 近义词: unaesthetic, unappealing, unattractive, unsightly | 反义词: aesthetic, beautiful, pleasing, attractive | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "aesthetic" (from Greek "aisthētikos," meaning "perceptive, sensitive," from "aisthēta," meaning "perceptible things").
