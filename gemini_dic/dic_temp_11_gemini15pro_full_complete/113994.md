magisterialnesses:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriəlnɪsɪz/| n. |权威性，威严，专横|The quality of being magisterial; authoritative and dignified manner; sometimes, overbearing or domineering behavior.|Her magisterialnesses in handling the crisis impressed everyone. 她处理危机时的权威性给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|Synonyms: authority, dignity, grandeur, imperiousness, loftiness, pomp, stateliness|Antonyms: humility, meekness, modesty, subservience|noun

magisterial:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriəl/| adj. |权威的，威严的，专横的|Having or showing great authority; impressively dignified and weighty; sometimes, overbearing or domineering.|He delivered a magisterial performance. 他的表演十分精彩，很有大师风范。|Synonyms: authoritative, august, commanding, dignified, grand, imposing, impressive, lordly, majestic, noble, stately|Antonyms: humble, meek, modest, subservient|adjective
