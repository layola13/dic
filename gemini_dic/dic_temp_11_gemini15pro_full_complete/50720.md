dernly:/ˈdɜːrnli/| adv.|严肃地；认真地；|Used to emphasize the seriousness or importance of something.  It often expresses a sense of urgency or concern.|She dernly warned him about the dangers of driving too fast. 她严肃地警告他超速驾驶的危险。|seriously, earnestly, gravely|lightly, frivolously|副词

词源:  "Dern" is an archaic euphemism for "damn," and "dernly" is formed by adding the adverbial suffix "-ly."  It retains some of the emphatic force of the original oath while being less offensive.
