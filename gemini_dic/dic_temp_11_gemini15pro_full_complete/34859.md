clinographic: /ˌklaɪnəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
斜投影的，斜线图的
Relating to or made by clinography; representing three-dimensional objects on a plane surface by lines that are not parallel to the lines of projection but converge to vanishing points.
The architect presented a clinographic drawing of the proposed building. 建筑师展示了拟建建筑物的斜投影图。
The textbook included clinographic illustrations of various geological formations. 教材中包含各种地质构造的斜线图。
近义词: oblique, perspective
反义词: orthographic
词性: adjective
