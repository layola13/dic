diprismatic:/ˌdaɪprɪzˈmætɪk/
adjective
双棱柱的; 双棱形的
Relating to or having the form of a diprism, a geometric figure formed by two prisms joined at their bases.
A diprismatic crystal structure. 双棱柱晶体结构。
diprismatic crystal formation is rare. 双棱柱晶体结构很少见.
近义词:  prismatic
反义词:  amorphous
词性: adjective
