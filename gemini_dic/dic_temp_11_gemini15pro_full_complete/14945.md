azurn:/ˈæʒərn/| adj.|天蓝色的，蔚蓝色的|Of a bright, vivid blue color like a clear sky.|The azurn sky was dotted with fluffy white clouds. 天蓝色的天空点缀着蓬松的白云。|近义词：azure, cerulean, sky-blue|反义词：dark, black|词性:形容词

词源:  From Middle English *azuren, *asuren, from Old French azurin, asurin (“azure”), from Medieval Latin azzurinus, from Arabic لَازَوَرْد‎ (lāzaward, “lapis lazuli”), from Persian لاجورد‎ (lâjvard, “lapis lazuli”). Ultimately from Sanskrit राजवर्त (rājavarta, “king's portion”).
