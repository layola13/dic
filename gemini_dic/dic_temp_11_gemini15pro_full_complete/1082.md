accepted:/əkˈseptɪd/| adj.|已接受的，公认的|Generally agreed upon or approved of. |例句：His theory is now widely accepted. 他的理论现在已被广泛接受。|例句：This is the accepted way of doing things. 这是公认的做事方式。|近义词：approved, recognized, acknowledged|反义词：rejected, refused|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the Latin "acceptus," past participle of "accipere" (to accept), which is formed from "ad" (to) + "capere" (to take).
