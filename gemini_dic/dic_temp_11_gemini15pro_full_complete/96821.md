inescapablenesses:/ˌɪnɪˈskeɪpəbəlɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
不可避免性；无法逃避性
The quality of being inescapable; the fact of being unavoidable.
The inescapablenesses of fate weighed heavily upon him.  命运的不可避免性沉重地压在他身上。
近义词: inevitabilities, unavoidabilities
反义词: avoidabilities, escapabilities
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From inescapable + -ness.
inescapable (adj.): from in- (not) + escapable (adj.): from escape (v.) + -able.
escape (v.): from Old French escaper, from Late Latin excappare (“to slip out of one's cape and flee”), from Latin ex- (“out of”) + cappa (“cape”).
