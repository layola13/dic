misrepresent:/ˌmɪsˌreprɪˈzent/
verb
歪曲；不如实陈述；误传
To present or describe (someone or something) inaccurately or unfairly.
The lawyer accused the media of misrepresenting the facts of the case. 律师指控媒体歪曲了案件的事实。
He claims his views were misrepresented in the article. 他声称他的观点在这篇文章中被歪曲了。
近义词: distort, falsify, misstate
反义词: represent accurately, portray truthfully

词性:v.
