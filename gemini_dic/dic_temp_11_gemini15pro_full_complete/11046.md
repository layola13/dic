arch:/ɑːrtʃ/ /ɑːrtʃ/ | n. |拱门，拱形结构 |A curved structure that spans an opening and supports the weight above it.| The arch of the bridge was impressive.  桥的拱门令人印象深刻。|近义词:arc, vault|反义词:beam, lintel| noun

arch:/ɑːrtʃ/ /ɑːrtʃ/ | v. |(使)成拱形，拱起 |To form or bend into an arch.| The cat arched its back. 猫拱起了背。|近义词:curve, bend|反义词:straighten, flatten| verb

arch:/ɑːrtʃ/ /ɑːrtʃ/ | adj. |主要的，首要的，调皮的，淘气的 |chief, principal, mischievous, playful|  He's an arch-villain in the movie. 他是电影中的主要反派。 /  She gave him an arch look. 她淘气地看了他一眼。 |近义词:chief, principal, mischievous, playful|反义词:minor, subordinate, serious, solemn| adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French arche, from Latin arcus (“bow, arch”), from Proto-Italic *arku, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂erkʷós (“bow; hook”). Cognate with Greek ἁρμός (harmós, “joint, fastening”).
