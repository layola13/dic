betas:/ˈbeɪtəz/ (US), /ˈbiːtəz/ (UK)| n.|测试版，贝塔系数|A pre-release version of software distributed to a select group of users for testing and feedback before the final release; also, a measure of a stock's volatility in relation to the overall market.|例句：The company released the betas of their new operating system to a select group of testers.  该公司向一组选定的测试人员发布了新操作系统的测试版。

例句：The beta of this stock is higher than the market average, indicating higher volatility. 这只股票的贝塔系数高于市场平均水平，表明波动性更高。|近义词：trial version, preview, test version|反义词：final version, release version|词性:名词
