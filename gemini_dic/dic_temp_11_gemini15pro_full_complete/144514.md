outtravels:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəlz/
verb
超过…旅行的距离；比…旅行更多
travels further than; surpasses in traveling
例句：
His experience outtravels most people's. 他的经历比大多数人的都丰富。

Her latest novel outtravels all her previous works in terms of depth and complexity. 她最新的小说在深度和复杂性方面超过了她所有以前的作品。

近义词：surpasses, exceeds, outstrips
反义词：lags behind, falls short of
词性：动词(第三人称单数)

词源：
"out-" (prefix meaning "beyond, exceeding") + "travel" (verb)
