calvinists:/ˈkælvɪnɪsts/
noun
加尔文主义者；加尔文教徒
Followers of the theological system of John Calvin and his successors.
Calvinists believe in predestination. 加尔文主义者相信宿命论。

近义词:Reformed Protestants, Presbyterians
反义词:Arminians, Catholics
