manganesous:/ˌmæŋɡəˈniːsəs/
adjective
亚锰的，二价锰的
Containing manganese with a valence of two.  Relating to or containing manganese, especially in its +2 oxidation state.
Manganese(II) oxide is a manganesous compound. 氧化亚锰是一种亚锰化合物。
Manganous sulfate is a light pink crystalline solid. 硫酸亚锰是一种浅粉色的晶体固体。
近义词: manganese(II)
反义词: manganic
词性: adjective
