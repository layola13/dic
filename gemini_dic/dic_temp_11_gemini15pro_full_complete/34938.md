clobbering:/ˈklɒbərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈklɑːbərɪŋ/ (US)| n. |痛打，猛击，彻底击败|A sound defeat or a severe criticism.  A thorough defeat; a thrashing.  The act of hitting someone or something very hard.|例句：The home team took a real clobbering in the final. 主队在决赛中惨败。|近义词：thrashing, beating, drubbing, defeat, hammering, walloping|反义词:victory, win|词性:noun

clobber:/ˈklɒbər/ (UK) /ˈklɑːbər/ (US)| vt. |痛打，猛击，严厉批评|To hit someone or something very hard. To defeat decisively. To criticize severely.|例句：He clobbered the ball over the fence. 他把球猛击过栅栏。|近义词：bash, batter, beat, belt,  hammer, pound, pummel, thrash, wallop|反义词:praise, commend|词性:verb
