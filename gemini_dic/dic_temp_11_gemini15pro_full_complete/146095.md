overeffusively:/ˌoʊvərɪˈfjuːsɪvli/| adv.|过度热情地，过度感情外露地|Expressing feelings of gratitude, pleasure, or approval in an unrestrained or excessive way.|She greeted him overeffusively, embarrassing him in front of his colleagues. 她过于热情地迎接他，让他在同事面前很尴尬。|Example: He thanked her overeffusively for the small gift. 他对这件小礼物表示了过分的感谢。|Synonyms: excessively, immoderately, extravagantly, profusely|Antonyms: reservedly, restrainedly, subtly|Word Type: Adverb
