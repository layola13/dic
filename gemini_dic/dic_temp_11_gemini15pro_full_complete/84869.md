hanging-around:/ˈhæŋɪŋ əˈraʊnd/| phrasal verb |闲逛，无所事事|Spending time idly or without purpose.|例句：He was just hanging around waiting for his friends. 他只是在闲逛，等他的朋友。|近义词：loitering, idling, lingering|反义词: working, being busy|词性:动词短语

hanging-around:/ˈhæŋɪŋ əˈraʊnd/| n. |闲逛，无所事事|The act of spending time idly or without purpose.|例句：The police told the group of teenagers to stop their hanging around. 警察叫那群青少年不要再闲逛了。|近义词：loitering, idling, lingering|反义词: working, being busy|词性:名词

词源: "hang" (悬挂) + "around" (周围)  -  The original meaning of "hang" evolved to include the sense of "linger" or "remain." "Around" indicates a lack of specific direction or purpose.
