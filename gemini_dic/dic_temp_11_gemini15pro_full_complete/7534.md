annexations:/ˌænɛkˈseɪʃənz/
n.
兼并；吞并；附加( annexation的名词复数 )
The annexations of territory have increased the country's size.  这些领土的兼并增加了国家的面积。
Numerous annexations expanded the Roman Empire.  无数次的吞并扩张了罗马帝国。
近义词:acquisitions, appropriations, seizures, incorporations
反义词:cessions, releases
