dryth: /draɪθ/
n.
干旱，旱灾
A prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall, leading to a shortage of water.
The dryth caused widespread crop failures.  干旱造成了大面积的农作物歉收。

近义词: drought, aridity
反义词: flood, deluge
词性: 名词
