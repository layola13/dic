journeys:/ˈdʒɜːrniz/ (US), /ˈdʒɜːniz/ (UK)| n.|旅行，旅程|An act of travelling from one place to another, especially when they are far apart. |例句：She wrote a book about her journeys in Africa. 她写了一本书，讲述她在非洲的旅行。


例句：Life is a journey, not a destination.  人生是一场旅行，而不是目的地。

近义词：trips, travels, voyages, expeditions, excursions|反义词:stay, arrival|词性:名词

He journeys to work by train every day. 他每天乘火车上班。
She journeys across Europe every summer. 她每年夏天都会去欧洲旅行。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
They journeyed to the coast last summer. 去年夏天他们去了海边旅行。
She journeyed alone to find herself. 她独自旅行去寻找自我。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
We were journeying through the desert when the car broke down. 汽车抛锚时，我们正在穿越沙漠。
He was journeying to London when he heard the news. 他在去伦敦的路上听到了这个消息。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
They had journeyed for weeks before they reached their destination. 他们旅行了数周才到达目的地。
She had journeyed to India before she went to China.  在她去中国之前，她去过印度旅行。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

被动语态:  (Journeys is rarely used in the passive voice as it is an intransitive verb.)
