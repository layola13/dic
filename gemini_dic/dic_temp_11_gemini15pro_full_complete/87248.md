hereupon:/ˌhɪərəˈpɒn/ /ˌhɪrəˈpɑːn/| adv.|于是，因此，随即|Immediately after this; following from this.|例句：Hereupon he rose and left the room. 于是，他站起来离开了房间。

例句：The judge read the verdict, and hereupon the defendant fainted. 法官宣读了判决，被告随即晕倒了。


近义词：thereupon, forthwith, immediately, consequently, accordingly|反义词: beforehand, previously|词性:副词
