bigotries: /ˈbɪɡətriz/
| n. |偏见，偏执，顽固| strong, unreasonable prejudices against a person, group, or belief|  His bigotries made him intolerant of anyone who disagreed with him. 他的偏见使他无法容忍任何与他意见相左的人。 |近义词：prejudices, biases, intolerances|反义词：tolerance, open-mindedness, acceptance|词性: 名词(复数)

bigotry: /ˈbɪɡətri/
| n. |偏见，偏执，顽固| intolerance towards those who hold different opinions from oneself.| He was accused of bigotry for his remarks about immigrants. 他因对移民的言论而被指责为偏执。|近义词：prejudice, bias, intolerance|反义词：tolerance, open-mindedness, acceptance|词性: 名词

词源: From French bigoterie "sanctimoniousness, hypocrisy," from bigot "religious hypocrite," of unknown origin.
