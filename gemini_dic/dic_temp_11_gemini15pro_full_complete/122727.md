moi:/mwɑː/ (French pronunciation)| pronoun|我 (informal, chiefly French)|The French word for "me," used as a subject or object pronoun, particularly in informal contexts. It is considered more stylish or affected than the standard "je" (I) or "me" (me).|例句：Moi, je préfère le vin rouge. (Me, I prefer red wine.) 我更喜欢红酒。

例句：Il m'a donné le livre à moi. (He gave the book to me.) 他把书给了我。|近义词：je, me (French)|反义词:N/A|词性:pronoun

注意事项:
"Moi" is generally not used by itself as a subject in standard modern French.  While grammatically correct in certain older forms of French or in specific regional dialects, its standalone subjective use is often seen as archaic or affected in contemporary French.  It is much more commonly used after prepositions or for emphasis alongside "je."
