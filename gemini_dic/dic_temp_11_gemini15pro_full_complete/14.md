a-fat-lot:/ə ˌfæt ˈlɒt/ |adv.| 根本没有; 一点也不; | An informal expression used to emphasize a lack or absence of something, often sarcastically. |  He promised to help, but he did a fat lot! 他答应帮忙，但他根本没帮！| Synonyms: nothing at all, not a bit, not in the least | Antonyms: a great deal, a lot, plenty | adverb
