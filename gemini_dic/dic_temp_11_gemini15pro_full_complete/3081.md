aeronaut:/ˈɛərənɔːt/| n.|航空员，热气球驾驶员|A person who operates or travels in an airship, balloon, or other aircraft.|例句：The aeronaut skillfully navigated the hot air balloon.  热气球驾驶员熟练地驾驶着热气球。

例句：Early aeronauts faced many dangers. 早期的航空员面临着许多危险。|近义词：pilot, aviator, airman|反义词:passenger|noun
