alienability:/ˌeɪliənəˈbɪləti/
n.
可让渡性，可转让性
The quality of being able to be transferred to another.
The alienability of property is a fundamental principle of capitalism.  财产的可转让性是资本主义的一项基本原则。
transferability, assignability
inalienability, nontransferability
noun
