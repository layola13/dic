out-searching:/ˌaʊtˈsɜːrtʃɪŋ/
| adj.|胜过搜索；在搜索方面超越|Surpassing or exceeding in the act of searching; more thorough or effective in searching than others.  It implies a greater effort, wider scope, or more successful outcome in the process of searching.|The detective's out-searching efforts led him to uncover the crucial evidence.|近义词：outdoing, surpassing, excelling, outperforming|反义词: underperforming, failing, lagging|词性:形容词

out-searching:/ˌaʊtˈsɜːrtʃɪŋ/
| v.|胜过搜索；在搜索方面超越|The act of searching more thoroughly or effectively than others.|The team is out-searching their competitors for new talent.|近义词：outdoing, surpassing, excelling, outperforming|反义词: underperforming, failing, lagging|词性:动词


词源: "out-" (prefix meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "searching" (present participle of the verb "search")
