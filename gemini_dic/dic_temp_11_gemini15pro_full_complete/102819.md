jarhead:/ˈdʒɑːrˌhed/ | n. | 美国海军陆战队士兵；傻瓜 | A derogatory slang term for a U.S. Marine.  The term is believed to derive from the appearance of Marines' haircuts, which in the past were very short, making their heads look like jars. |  Example 1: "The jarheads were deployed to the Middle East."  中文：这些海军陆战队士兵被部署到了中东。

Example 2: "He's such a jarhead, he doesn't understand anything but following orders." 中文：他真是个傻瓜，除了服从命令什么都不懂。| Synonym: Marine, leatherneck (for Marine), idiot, fool (for傻瓜)| Antonym: civilian (for Marine), genius (for 傻瓜)| noun
