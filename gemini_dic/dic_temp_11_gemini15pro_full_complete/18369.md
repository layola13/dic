belly-aching:/ˈbɛliˌeɪkɪŋ/| adj.| 令人抱怨的；令人厌烦的；牢骚满腹的 | Complaining, whining, or expressing discontent, often about something trivial. |  He's always belly-aching about the food.  他总是抱怨食物。| Synonyms: complaining, whining, griping, moaning, grousing | Antonyms: content, satisfied, happy, cheerful | adjective

belly-aching:/ˈbɛliˌeɪkɪŋ/| n.| 抱怨；牢骚 | An act of complaining or whining. | There was a lot of belly-aching about the new rules.  对于新规定，大家怨声载道。| Synonyms: complaint, whine, gripe, moan, grouse | Antonyms: contentment, satisfaction, happiness, cheer | noun
