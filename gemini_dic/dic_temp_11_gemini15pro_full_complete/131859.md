nonconsultative:/ˌnɒnkənˈsʌltətɪv/
adjective
非协商性的；未经协商的；不征求意见的
Not involving consultation; made or done without consultation.
The CEO made a nonconsultative decision to close the factory, angering the workers. 首席执行官做出了关闭工厂的非协商性决定，激怒了工人。
The government imposed a nonconsultative tax increase. 政府强制增加了未经协商的税收。
Synonyms: arbitrary, unilateral, peremptory, high-handed
Antonyms: consultative, collaborative, cooperative
