fightingly:/ˈfaɪtɪŋli/| adv.|好战地，战斗地|In a fighting manner; combatively.  With a spirit of resistance. |He fightingly defended his position in the debate. 他在辩论中好战地捍卫自己的立场。|近义词：combatively, aggressively, belligerently|反义词：peacefully, passively, submissively|词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "fight" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Old English "feohtan."
