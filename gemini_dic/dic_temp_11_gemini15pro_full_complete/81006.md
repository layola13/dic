gradine:/ˈɡrædiːn/ /ɡrəˈdiːn/| n.|梯级座位;  露天剧场的石阶; (尤指古代剧场的)观众席|A raised step or series of steps forming a tier in an ancient amphitheater or theater.  A row of seats rising one behind the other. |The ancient gradine offered a spectacular view of the stage. 古代的梯级座位提供了绝佳的舞台视野。

The gradine was carved from solid rock. 梯级座位是从坚固的岩石中雕刻出来的。|近义词：tier, steps, seating, benches|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps floor, ground level)|词性:noun
