outlengthening:/ˌaʊtˈlɛŋθənɪŋ/
verb
加长，延长，超出…的长度
making something longer than something else; exceeding something in length
The new bridge outlengthens the old one by several meters. 新桥比旧桥长几米。
outstrip, surpass, exceed
fall short of, be shorter than
