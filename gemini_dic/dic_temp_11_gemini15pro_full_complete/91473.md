hylotheism:/ˌhaɪloʊˈθiːɪzəm/
n.
万物有灵论，泛灵论
The belief that God is immanent in matter, or that the world is God.  认为上帝存在于物质之中，或者世界就是上帝的信仰。
Indigenous cultures often practice a form of hylotheism, seeing the divine in nature.  土著文化通常实行一种万物有灵论，认为神灵存在于自然界中。
近义词:pantheism, animism
反义词:atheism, deism
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek *hylē* (matter, wood) + *theos* (god) + *-ism*
