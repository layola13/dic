gloam:/ɡloʊm/ | v. | 变昏暗，(天空)渐暗 |To become dark or dusky, as at twilight.  | The sky gloamed as the storm approached. 暴风雨来临，天空变得昏暗。 | synonyms: darken, dim | antonyms: brighten, lighten | verb
