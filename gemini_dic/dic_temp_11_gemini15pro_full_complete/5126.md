allegoric:/ˌæləˈɡɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 寓言的，讽喻的 |  Figurative treatment of one subject under the guise of another; symbolic. | The novel is highly allegoric, with the characters representing different virtues and vices. 这部小说寓意深刻，人物代表着不同的美德和恶习。 | The play is a richly allegoric tale of love, loss, and redemption.  这出戏是一个寓意丰富的关于爱、失去和救赎的故事。 | symbolic, metaphorical, figurative | literal, factual | adjective
