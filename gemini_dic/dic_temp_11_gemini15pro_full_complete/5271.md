allons:/ælɔ̃/ /ɑːlɔ̃/ | int. |走吧，我们走吧|An exclamation used to encourage someone to go or do something, especially used in French and adopted into English. It literally translates to "let's go" in French.|例句：Allons, children! Let's go home! 走吧，孩子们！我们回家吧！|近义词：Let's go, come on|反义词:Stay, wait|词性:Interjection

allons:/ælɔ̃/ /ɑːlɔ̃/ | n. |(罕见) 启程，出发|A noun form, rarely used, referring to the act of setting off or departing.  Derived from the French verb "aller" (to go).|例句：The allons was scheduled for dawn.  出发预定在黎明。 |近义词：Departure, setting off|反义词:Arrival, stay|词性:Noun (rarely used)
