glamour:/ˈɡlæmər/ (US) /ˈɡlæmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|魅力，诱惑力，迷人的美|A quality that makes something or someone seem attractive, exciting, or special, often connected to wealth, success, or mystery.|She was captivated by the glamour of Hollywood. 她被好莱坞的魅力所吸引。|近义词：allure, charm, charisma, magic, enchantment|反义词：drabness, dullness, plainness|noun
