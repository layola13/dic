innovationist:/ˌɪnəˈveɪʃənɪst/
n.|创新者，革新者|Someone who actively promotes or implements innovation.  A person who introduces new methods, ideas, or products.|Elon Musk is often considered an innovationist. 埃隆·马斯克通常被认为是一位创新者。|Synonyms: innovator, pioneer, reformer,  modernizer, inventor, creator|Antonyms: traditionalist, conservative, luddite|noun
