glycocoll: /ˈɡlaɪkəˌkɑːl/ /ˈɡlaɪkəʊkɒl/ | n. | 甘氨酸; 氨基乙酸 |  A simple amino acid, the smallest of the 20 standard amino acids, and the only one that is not optically active. It is a non-essential amino acid, meaning the body can synthesize it.  | Glycine is used in the synthesis of proteins. 甘氨酸用于蛋白质的合成。|  Glycocoll is a sweet-tasting crystalline solid. 甘氨酸是一种味甜的结晶固体。| synonyms: glycine, aminoacetic acid | antonyms:  (Glycocoll doesn't have a direct antonym as it's a specific amino acid.
