antivenin:/ˌæntiˈvɛnɪn/| n.|抗蛇毒血清|A serum containing antibodies against a specific venom, used to treat venomous bites or stings.|The doctor quickly administered the antivenin after the snake bite. 医生在蛇咬伤后迅速给予抗蛇毒血清。|Synonyms: antivenom, antiserum|Antonyms: venom, poison|noun
