carillon: /ˈkærɪlɒn/ (US), /kəˈrɪljən/ (UK)| n. |编钟，钟琴|A set of stationary bells hung in a tower and played from a keyboard, or the music played on such bells.|The town hall carillon played a cheerful melody every hour. 市政厅的钟琴每小时都会演奏一段欢快的旋律。|Synonyms: chimes, bells|Antonyms: silence|noun

carillon: /ˈkærɪlɒn/ (US), /kəˈrɪljən/ (UK)| v. |演奏钟琴|To play a carillon.|He carilled the wedding march for the newlyweds. 他为新婚夫妇演奏了婚礼进行曲。|Synonyms: chime, ring|Antonyms: mute, silence|verb
