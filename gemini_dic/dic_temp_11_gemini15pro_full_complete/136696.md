nonsegmentary:/nɒnˈsɛɡməntəri/
adjective
非分段的，整体的
Not divided into segments or sections; forming a unified whole, especially of a society lacking social stratification based on kinship groups.
Anthropologists often contrast segmentary societies with nonsegmentary states.  人类学家经常将分段社会与非分段国家进行对比。
The author argues that the society described in the text is nonsegmentary and based on individual achievement.  作者认为，文中描述的社会是非分段的，并且基于个人成就。
Synonyms: unified, integrated, whole, holistic
Antonyms: segmentary, segmented, divided, stratified
