earworm:/ˈɪrwɜːrm/ | n. | 耳虫; 洗脑神曲 | A catchy tune that keeps repeating in one's head. | Example: "That new song is a real earworm; I've been humming it all day."  那首新歌真是洗脑，我一整天都在哼它。 | Example: "I can't get rid of this earworm! It's been stuck in my head for days." 我无法摆脱这首洗脑神曲！它已经在我脑海里好几天了。| Synonym: catchy tune, brainworm | Antonym:  (No direct antonym) | Noun
