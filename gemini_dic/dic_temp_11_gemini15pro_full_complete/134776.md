nonmetameric:/ˌnɒnˌmɛtəˈmɛrɪk/ | adj. | 非分节的，不分节的 | Not divided into segments or metameres; unsegmented.  A nonmetameric animal, like a jellyfish, does not have a body plan with repeating segments. |  The jellyfish is a nonmetameric animal.  水母是一种不分节的动物。|  The body of a nematode is nonmetameric, unlike that of an earthworm. 线虫的身体不分节，不像蚯蚓那样。 |homonomous, unsegmented | metameric, segmented | adjective
