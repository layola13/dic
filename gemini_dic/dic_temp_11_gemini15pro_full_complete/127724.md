negligently:/ˈneɡlɪdʒəntli/| adv.|疏忽地，玩忽职守地|Failing to exercise the care that a reasonably prudent person would exercise in like circumstances.|He negligently left the gas stove on, causing a fire. 他疏忽地忘了关煤气炉，结果引发了火灾。|carelessly, recklessly, irresponsibly|carefully, diligently, responsibly|adverb
