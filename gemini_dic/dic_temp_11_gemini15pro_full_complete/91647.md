hyperborean:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɔːriən/ | adj. n. | 极北的；极寒的；(Hyperborean) (希腊神话)极北之地的居民 |  Relating to the extreme north, or to a mythical people inhabiting the extreme north, characterized by great cold. Also used figuratively to describe something very distant or remote. |  例句：They lived in a hyperborean climate. 他们生活在极寒的气候中。

例句：The explorers ventured into the hyperborean regions. 探险家们冒险进入了极北地区。 | 近义词：arctic, frigid, polar, northern | 反义词：tropical, southern | 词性: 形容词，名词

Etymology: From Greek *Huperboreioi* "people living beyond the north wind," from *huper* "beyond" + *Boreas* "north wind."
