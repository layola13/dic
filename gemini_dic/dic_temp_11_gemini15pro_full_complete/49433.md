delousing:/diːˈlaʊzɪŋ/
noun
除虱，灭虱
the process of removing lice from a person or animal's body or from clothing, bedding, etc.
The refugees were given a delousing treatment upon arrival.  难民到达后接受了除虱治疗。

verb (delouse的现在分词)
除虱，灭虱
to rid (a person or animal) of lice; to clean (clothing, bedding, etc.) of lice
The prisoners were deloused to prevent the spread of disease.  囚犯们被除虱以防止疾病传播。

近义词:disinfestation, cleaning, fumigation
反义词:infestation
