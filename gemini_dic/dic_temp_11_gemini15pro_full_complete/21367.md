blot:/blɑt/ (US) /blɒt/ (UK)| n. vt.|污点，墨渍；缺点，污点；吸墨纸|a mark or stain, typically one made by ink, paint, or dirt; a fault or imperfection in character|The ink made a blot on the paper. 墨水在纸上留下了一个污点。|synonyms: stain, spot, mark, blemish, flaw|antonyms: immaculateness, purity|noun

blot:/blɑt/ (US) /blɒt/ (UK)| vt.|吸干，涂污，弄脏|dry (ink) using absorbent paper; make marks or stains on something|She blotted her lipstick with a tissue. 她用纸巾吸干了口红。|synonyms: soak up, absorb, wipe, smudge|antonyms: smear, spread|verb
