glycosine:/ˈɡlaɪkəˌsiːn/ /ˈɡlaɪkoʊˌsiːn/
n.
糖精，甘氨酸
A crystalline glycoside obtained from ivy berries.  A sweet, crystalline glycoside, C21H24O3, obtained from ivy berries.
Ivy berries contain a glycoside called glycosine.  常春藤浆果中含有一种叫做糖精的糖苷。
Synonym:  hederin
Antonym:  None (Glycosine is a specific chemical compound, not a concept with a direct opposite.)
