bettering:/ˈbetərɪŋ/
verb
改善，改进
making something better; improving something
例句：He devoted his life to bettering the lives of the poor. 他毕生致力于改善穷人的生活。

例句：She's always striving for bettering herself. 她总是力求完善自己。
近义词：improving, enhancing, ameliorating
反义词：worsening, degrading
词性: gerund or present participle of "better"


better:/ˈbetər/
adjective
更好的
of a higher standard or quality
例句：This is a better solution than the previous one.  这是一个比前一个更好的解决方案。

例句：He has a better understanding of the situation than I do. 他对情况的了解比我更透彻。

better:/ˈbetər/
adverb
更好地
in a more excellent or effective way
例句：She sings better than anyone I know. 她比我认识的任何人都唱得好。

例句：He played the piano better than ever before. 他钢琴弹得比以往任何时候都好。


better:/ˈbetər/
verb
改善；胜过
make or become better; surpass
例句：The company hopes to better its performance next quarter.  公司希望在下个季度提高业绩。

例句：He betted his rival in the competition. 他在比赛中战胜了对手。

better:/ˈbetər/
noun
较优者；较佳者
something superior in quality or condition
例句：Of the two options, this is the better.  在这两个选项中，这个更好。

例句：When it comes to writing, she's definitely the better of the two.  说到写作，她绝对是两人中更好的那个。

词源：From Middle English *bettre, from Old English betera, bettra (“better”), comparative of bet (“good”), from Proto-Germanic *batizon, comparative of *batiz (“good”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰed- (“good”). Cognate with Scots better (“better”), West Frisian better (“better”), Dutch beter (“better”), German besser (“better”), Danish bedre (“better”), Swedish bättre (“better”), Icelandic betri (“better”).
