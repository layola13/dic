imploded:/ɪmˈploʊdɪd/
verb
内爆，骤然崩溃
collapsed violently inward; experienced a sudden and complete failure or collapse.
The submarine imploded under the immense pressure of the deep ocean.
潜水艇在深海巨大的压力下内爆了。

The dot-com bubble imploded in the early 2000s.
互联网泡沫在21世纪初破灭了。

collapse, crumble, fall apart, disintegrate
explode, burst, expand
