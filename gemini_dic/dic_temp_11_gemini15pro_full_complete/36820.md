colonially:/kəˈloʊniəli/
| adv. |殖民地式地，殖民地化地|In a manner characteristic of a colony or colonies; exhibiting the characteristics of colonialism. |The country was colonially exploited for its natural resources.  这个国家的自然资源被殖民式地剥削了。| synonym: imperially, subjectively | antonym: independently, autonomously | adverb

colonially:/kəˈloʊniəli/
| adj. |殖民的，殖民地的|Relating to, characteristic of, or occurring in a colony or colonies. |The colonially imposed government was oppressive.  殖民地强加的政府是压迫性的。| synonym: colonial, imperial | antonym: independent, autonomous | adjective
