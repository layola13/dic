microform:/ˈmaɪkrəʊfɔːrm/ (US) /ˈmaɪkrəfɔːm/ (UK)| n.|缩微胶片，缩微品|A photographic copy of a document, book, etc., greatly reduced in size for easy storage. |The library has a vast collection of newspapers on microform.  图书馆收藏了大量的缩微胶片报纸。|Synonyms: microfilm, microfiche|Antonyms: enlargement, macroform|noun
