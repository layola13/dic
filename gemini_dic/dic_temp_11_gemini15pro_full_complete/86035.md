healthful:/ˈhɛlθfəl/| adj.|有益健康的，健康的|Conducive to good health; wholesome.|例句：A healthful diet is important for a long life.  有益健康的饮食对长寿很重要。

例句：She maintains a healthful lifestyle with regular exercise and a balanced diet. 她通过规律的运动和均衡的饮食保持健康的生活方式。|近义词：healthy, wholesome, salutary, salubrious|反义词：unhealthy, unwholesome, detrimental|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *helthful, equivalent to health + -ful.
