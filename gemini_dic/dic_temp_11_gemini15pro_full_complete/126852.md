napkining:/ˈnæpkɪnɪŋ/ |n.| 用餐巾擦拭的行为; 使用餐巾 |The act of wiping with or using a napkin.| She finished her meal with a thorough napkining of her mouth and fingers. 她吃完饭后，用餐巾仔细地擦了擦嘴和手指。| 近义词: wiping, dabbing | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "leaving messy") | 词性: 名词


词源:  Derived from "napkin" + "-ing" suffix, indicating the action or process of using a napkin.  "Napkin" itself comes from Old French "naperon," meaning "small tablecloth."
