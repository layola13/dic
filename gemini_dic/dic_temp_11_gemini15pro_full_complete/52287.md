dichroite:/ˈdaɪkroʊˌaɪt/| n.|二色石，堇青石|A mineral that exhibits dichroism, meaning it displays different colors when viewed from different directions. It is also known as iolite or cordierite.|The dichroite gemstone changed from blue to violet as I rotated it. 这颗堇青石宝石当我旋转它时颜色由蓝色变成了紫色。|Synonyms: iolite, cordierite, water sapphire|Antonyms: N/A|noun

dichroism:/ˈdaɪkroʊˌɪzəm/| n.|二色性|The property of exhibiting different colors when viewed from different directions.|The dichroism of the tourmaline crystal was striking. 电气石晶体的二色性非常引人注目。|Synonyms: pleochroism (for more than two colors)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
