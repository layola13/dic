delightsome:/dɪˈlaɪtsəm/
adjective
令人愉快的；可爱的
Highly pleasing; delightful.
The delightsome aroma of freshly baked bread filled the kitchen.  厨房里充满了刚烤好的面包的诱人香味。
近义词: delightful, pleasant, enjoyable, charming
反义词: unpleasant, disagreeable, displeasing
词性: adjective

词源:
From delight + -some.  由delight(高兴) + -some(构成形容词，表示“充满...的”) 组成。
