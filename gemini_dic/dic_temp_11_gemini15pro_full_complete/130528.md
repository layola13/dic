nonaccruals:/ˌnɑːnəˈkruːəlz/
| n. |未累积收入，未应计项目|Loans or other receivables on which interest is no longer being accrued due to the debtor's delinquency or other factors indicating a high probability of default.| The bank reported a significant increase in nonaccruals this quarter, reflecting the deteriorating economic conditions. 该银行本季度报告未累积收入显著增加，反映了经济状况的恶化。|non-performing loans, delinquent loans|performing loans|noun
