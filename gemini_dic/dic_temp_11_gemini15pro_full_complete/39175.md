congealer:/kənˈdʒiːlər/ | n. | 凝固剂，凝结剂 |  A substance that causes a liquid to become solid or semisolid. |  The recipe called for a congealer to thicken the sauce.  这道菜需要一种凝固剂来使酱汁变稠。| synonyms: thickener, coagulant, setting agent | antonyms: thinner, solvent | noun

congealers:/kənˈdʒiːlərz/ | n. | 凝固剂，凝结剂（复数） | Substances that cause liquids to become solid or semisolid. |  Several different congealers were tested in the lab.  实验室里测试了几种不同的凝固剂。 | synonyms: thickeners, coagulants, setting agents | antonyms: thinners, solvents | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "congeal" which comes from Old French "congeler"  from Latin "congelare" meaning "to freeze," from "com-" (together) + "gelare" (to freeze).
