decal:/ˈdiːkæl/ (US), /ˈdiːkæl/ (UK)| n.|贴花，贴纸；转印画|A design prepared on special paper for transfer to another surface, such as glass, porcelain, or metal. |She decorated her laptop with colorful decals. 她用彩色贴纸装饰了她的笔记本电脑。|Synonyms: sticker, transfer, label|Antonyms: N/A|noun
