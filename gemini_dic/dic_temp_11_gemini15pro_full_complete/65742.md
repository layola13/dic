evergreens: /ˈɛvərˌɡriːnz/ | n. | 常青树；常绿植物；常青树枝(尤指圣诞节用) |Plants that retain green leaves throughout the year. |  Pine trees are evergreens. 松树是常青树。| synonyms: conifers, perennial plants | antonyms: deciduous trees | noun

evergreen: /ˈɛvərˌɡriːn/ | adj. | 常绿的；常青的；经久不衰的 |Remaining green throughout the year; enduring; constantly fresh. | This song is an evergreen in the music industry. 这首歌在音乐界经久不衰。| synonyms: lasting, enduring, perennial | antonyms: deciduous, ephemeral, short-lived | adjective
