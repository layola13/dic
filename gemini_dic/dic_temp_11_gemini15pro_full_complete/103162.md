jervine:/ˈdʒɜːrviːn/ | n. |杰尔文碱; 藜芦碱| A crystalline alkaloid, C16H37NO8, obtained from white hellebore. |  Jervine is a teratogen found in Veratrum californicum. 杰尔文碱是一种存在于加州藜芦中的致畸剂。|  Jervine can cause cyclopia in lambs. 杰尔文碱会导致羔羊患独眼畸形。| synonyms:veratrum alkaloid | antonyms: N/A | noun
