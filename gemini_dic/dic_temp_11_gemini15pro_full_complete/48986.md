deghosts:/diːˈɡəʊsts/
verb
去除重影，消除鬼影
Removes ghosting artifacts, typically in an image or video.
The new software deghosts the security camera footage.  新的软件去除了监控录像中的重影。
The editing software can deghosts the image, making it clearer.  这个编辑软件可以去除图像中的鬼影，使图像更清晰。
近义词:clarifies, cleans, enhances
反义词:blurs, distorts
词性:v.
