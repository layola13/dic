immersable:/ɪˈmɜːsəbl/ | adj. | 可浸入的，可沉浸的 |  Capable of being immersed; able to be submerged or sunk in a liquid.  | The equipment is completely immersable in water.  这个设备可以完全浸入水中。 |  submersible, submergible | nonsubmersible, non-immersable | adjective

immersable:/ɪˈmɜːsəbl/ | adj. | 可沉浸的，引人入胜的 | Capable of engrossing or absorbing someone's attention completely. | The new virtual reality game is truly immersable.  这款新的虚拟现实游戏真的引人入胜。 |  engrossing, captivating, absorbing |  boring, tedious, uninteresting | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *immersabilis, from Latin immergere "to plunge in," from in- "in" + mergere "to dip, to plunge."
