forex:/ˈfɔːrɛks/| n.|外汇；外汇市场|A global decentralized or over-the-counter (OTC) market for the trading of currencies. This market determines foreign exchange rates for every currency. It includes all aspects of buying, selling and exchanging currencies at current or determined prices.|The forex market is the largest and most liquid financial market in the world. 外汇市场是世界上最大、流动性最强的金融市场。| Many traders participate in the forex market, hoping to make a profit. 许多交易者参与外汇市场，希望从中获利。|近义词：foreign exchange, FX|反义词: 无|词性: 名词
