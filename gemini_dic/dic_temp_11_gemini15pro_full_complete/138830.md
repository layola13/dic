nunlike:/nʌnˈlaɪk/ | adj. |不像修女的，不像尼姑的| Not characteristic of a nun; not befitting a nun; secular. |  She wore a nunlike habit, but her demeanor was anything but pious. 她穿着一件像修女服的衣服，但她的举止一点也不虔诚。| Near Synonyms: uncharacteristic of a nun, secular, worldly, uncloistered | Antonyms: nunly, pious, devout, religious | adjective
