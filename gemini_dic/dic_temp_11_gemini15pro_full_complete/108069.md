lateral:/ˈlætərəl/ (US), /ˈlætərəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|侧面的，横向的；侧部，支线|Relating to the side or sides of something; away from the median plane of the body.  A side part of something; a branch.|

例句：
The lateral branches of the tree were heavy with fruit.
这棵树的侧枝果实累累。

The patient experienced lateral pain in his knee.
病人的膝盖侧面感到疼痛。


近义词：side, sideways, flanking|
反义词：central, medial, axial|
词性: adjective, noun
