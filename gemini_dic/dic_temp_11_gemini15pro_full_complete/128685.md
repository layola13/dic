nictitation:/ˌnɪktɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 眨眼，瞬膜瞬动 | the act of blinking or winking, especially rapidly or repeatedly; the closing and opening of a nictitating membrane (a thin, translucent third eyelid present in some animals that can be drawn across the eye for protection or moistening). | The cat's rapid nictitation suggested it was nervous. 猫快速的眨眼表明它很紧张。| 近义词：blink, wink, flicker | 反义词: stare | noun
