gairfowl:/ˈɡɛrfˌaʊl/| n.|大海雀|A large, flightless North Atlantic seabird (Pinguinus impennis) that became extinct in the mid-19th century.|The last known pair of gairfowl were killed in Iceland in 1844.  最后两只已知的大海雀于1844年在冰岛被杀死。|近义词:great auk, penguin (in older usage)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Scots *garefowl, from Old Norse geirfugl, from geirr "spear, wedge" (referring to its beak) + fugl "bird".
