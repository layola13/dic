dethronements:/diːˈθrəʊnmənts/
|n.|废黜，罢免|The act of removing a monarch or ruler from their throne.  The dethronements of several European monarchs in the 20th century marked a shift in power. 20世纪几位欧洲君主的废黜标志着权力的转移。|Example: The dethronements of the king sent shockwaves through the nation. 国王的废黜震惊了全国。 The museum exhibit showcased artifacts from various royal dethronements. 博物馆展览展示了各种皇室废黜的文物。|Synonyms: depositions, oustings, removals|Antonyms: coronations, accessions, enthronements|Noun
