nappa:/ˈnæpə/| n.|纳帕皮，软皮|A soft, full-grain leather made from the hides of sheep or lambs, noted for its suppleness and smooth texture.|例句：This jacket is made of high-quality nappa leather. 这件夹克是用优质纳帕皮制成的。

例句：Nappa leather gloves are known for their comfort and durability. 纳帕皮手套以其舒适性和耐用性而闻名。|近义词：leather, lambskin, sheepskin|反义词: (No direct antonym for a material.  Could consider "synthetic leather" as a contrasting material.) |词性:noun

From Napa, California, where it was first produced in the late 19th century by Emanuel Manasse.
