inquirent:/ɪnˈkwaɪərənt/| adj.|好问的，爱打听的|Given to inquiry; inquisitive; inquiring.|例句：The inquirent child asked her mother a million questions.  这个好奇的孩子问了她妈妈无数个问题。|例句：He had an inquirent mind and was always eager to learn new things. 他有颗求知若渴的心，总是渴望学习新事物。|近义词：inquisitive, curious, inquiring|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, uninterested|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *inquirens, *inquirentis, present participle of *inquirere, from Latin inquirere "to seek into, search for," from in- "into" + quaerere "to seek, ask."
