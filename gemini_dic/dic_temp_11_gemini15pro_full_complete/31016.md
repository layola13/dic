challenger:/ˈtʃælɪndʒər/| n.|挑战者; 竞争者; 质问者|Someone who enters a competition, or opposes another in a fight, contest, or argument.|例句：The challenger surprised everyone by winning the championship.  挑战者赢得了冠军，让所有人大吃一惊。

例句：She emerged as a strong challenger to the incumbent president. 她作为现任总统的有力挑战者出现。|近义词：competitor, rival, contestant, opponent, contender|反义词：incumbent, defender, champion|词性:noun
