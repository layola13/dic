overgesticulatively: /ˌoʊvərˌdʒɛstɪˈkjʊlətɪvli/
adverb
过度做手势地
in a way that involves making too many gestures
He spoke overgesticulatively, waving his arms around wildly. 他说话时手势过多，疯狂地挥舞着手臂。
The actor overgesticulated on stage, distracting the audience.  这个演员在舞台上做作地打手势，分散了观众的注意力。
近义词: dramatically, theatrically, excessively
反义词: subtly, restrainedly
词性: adverb
词源: From over- + gesticulate + -ive + -ly.  "Over-" indicates excess, "gesticulate" means to make gestures, "-ive" forms an adjective, and "-ly" forms an adverb.
