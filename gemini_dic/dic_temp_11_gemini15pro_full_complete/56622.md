dongas:/ˈdɒŋɡəz/ (UK), /ˈdɑːŋɡəz/ (US)| n. |工棚，临时住所|A donga is a usually small, often temporary dwelling place, typically made of corrugated iron or other lightweight materials.  Originally used in South Africa, particularly in mining or construction camps. |The miners lived in dongas near the gold mine. 矿工们住在金矿附近的工棚里。

The construction crew set up a row of dongas for the duration of the project.  施工队为项目期间搭建了一排工棚。|近义词：shack, hut, cabin, bunkhouse|反义词: house, mansion, apartment|词性:noun


dongas:/ˈdɒŋɡəz/ (UK), /ˈdɑːŋɡəz/ (US)| n. |峡谷，沟壑 (主要指南非地区的干涸河床或沟壑)|In Southern Africa, a donga can also refer to a dry riverbed or gully.  |The jeep struggled to navigate the rocky donga. 吉普车艰难地在岩石沟壑中行驶。

Heavy rains transformed the donga into a raging torrent.  暴雨将干涸的河床变成了汹涌的激流。|近义词：gully, ravine, wadi, arroyo|反义词: ridge, hill, mountain|词性:noun
