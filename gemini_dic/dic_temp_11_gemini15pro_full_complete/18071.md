beguine:/bɪˈɡiːn/| n.|贝吉纳舞，贝吉纳舞曲|A fast dance of Martinique, similar to the rumba.|例句：The band played a lively beguine. 乐队演奏了一曲欢快的贝吉纳舞曲。

例句：She danced the beguine with grace and passion. 她优雅而热情地跳着贝吉纳舞。|近义词：rumba, bolero|反义词:|noun

beguine:/bɪˈɡiːn/| n.|贝吉纳修女|A member of a Roman Catholic sisterhood founded in the 12th century in the Netherlands, who lived a life of pious devotion but did not take formal vows.|例句：The beguine dedicated her life to prayer and charitable works.  这位贝吉纳修女将自己的一生奉献给了祈祷和慈善事业。

例句：The beguines lived a simple life in a communal setting. 贝吉纳修女们在集体环境中过着简朴的生活。|近义词：nun|反义词:|noun
