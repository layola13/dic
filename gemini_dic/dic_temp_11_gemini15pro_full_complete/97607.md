inhaled:/ɪnˈheɪld/| v. |吸入；吸气|breathe in. |She inhaled deeply and blew out the candles. 她深吸一口气，吹灭了蜡烛。|The doctor told him to inhale deeply. 医生告诉他要深呼吸。|近义词：breathed in, inspired|反义词:exhaled|词性:动词

词源: From the verb "inhale," which comes from the Latin "inhalare," meaning "to breathe in."  "In-" means "in" and "halare" means "to breathe."
