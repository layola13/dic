outthinks:/ˌaʊtˈθɪŋks/
verb
以智取胜，比…更聪明
to think more cleverly and effectively than someone else
She completely outthought her opponents in the negotiation.  她在谈判中完全以智取胜了她的对手。

近义词:outsmart, outwit
反义词: be outsmarted, be outwitted
词性:v.
