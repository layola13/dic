outmantle: /ˌaʊtˈmæntl/
verb
覆盖；包住；胜过
to cover or envelop as with a mantle; to surpass or excel
The fog outmantled the city. 浓雾笼罩着城市。
Her beauty outmantled all others. 她的美貌胜过所有人。
Synonyms: cloak, envelop, shroud, surpass, exceed, excel, outdo, outshine, eclipse
Antonyms: uncover, reveal, expose
词性: 动词
