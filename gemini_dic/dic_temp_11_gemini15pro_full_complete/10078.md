apocryphal: /əˈpɒkrɪfəl/ (UK) /əˈpɑːkrɪfəl/ (US)| adj.|伪经的；可疑的；杜撰的|Of doubtful authenticity, although widely circulated as being true.|例句：Many apocryphal stories surround the life of the famous author.  许多关于这位著名作家生平的轶事都真假难辨。

例句：The museum guide dismissed the legend as apocryphal.  博物馆导游认为这个传说是假的。|近义词：spurious, fictitious, fabricated, dubious, questionable|反义词：authentic, genuine, verifiable, true|词性：形容词
