词源: From Middle English *kichen, from Old English cycene (“kitchen, cooking place”), from Proto-West Germanic *kukina, from Latin coquīna (“kitchen”), feminine of coquīnus (“of or for cooking”), from coquō (“I cook”).


注意事项:  kitchen 既可以指单数也可以指复数，kitchens一定是复数形式。
