marks:/mɑːrks/ (US) /mɑːks/ (UK)| n. v. |分数；标记；痕迹；特征|A mark is a visible sign or indication made on something.|

例句：
The student received high marks on the exam.
这个学生在考试中得了高分。

He made a mark on the wall with a pencil.
他用铅笔在墙上做了个记号。


近义词：grades, scores, signs, symbols, traces, characteristics
反义词: blemish (瑕疵，污点 -  specifically for a negative mark)


词性:n.
The teacher gave me good marks for my essay. 老师给了我的文章好分数。

词性:v.
The incident marked a turning point in his life.  这起事件标志着他人生的转折点。
