enervous:/ɪˈnɜːrvəs/| adj.|紧张不安的，神经质的|Feeling or causing nervousness; causing or characterized by anxiety or tension.  It combines the prefix "en-" (meaning "to put into" or "to cause to be") with "nervous."|She was enervous about her upcoming exam. 她对即将到来的考试感到紧张不安。|He had an enervous habit of tapping his foot. 他有个紧张不安的习惯，喜欢用脚敲打地面。|近义词：anxious, apprehensive, edgy, jittery, tense, uneasy|反义词：calm, composed, relaxed, tranquil|词性:形容词
