inflatedly:/ɪnˈfleɪtɪdli/
adv.
膨胀地，夸张地
in an inflated manner; pompously; with exaggeration.
He spoke inflatedly about his own achievements. 他夸大其词地谈论自己的成就。
近义词:pompously, pretentiously, boastfully, vaingloriously
反义词:modestly, humbly, simply

词性:副词

词源:  From inflate + -ed + -ly.  Inflate comes from Latin inflāre "to blow into, inflate," from in- "in" + flāre "to blow."
