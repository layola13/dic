overneglect: /ˌoʊvər nɪˈɡlɛkt/
verb
过度忽视，极度忽略
to neglect to an excessive degree
The city has overneglected its infrastructure, leading to numerous problems.  这座城市过度忽视了其基础设施建设，导致了诸多问题。

The farmer overneglected his crops, resulting in a poor harvest.  这个农民过度忽视了农作物，导致收成不好。

近义词: disregard, ignore, slight
反义词: cherish, nurture, attend to
词性: verb

词源:
"Over-" (prefix): from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, excessively."
"Neglect" (verb): from Latin "neglectus," past participle of "neglegere" ("to disregard"), from "nec" ("not") + "legere" ("to gather, choose, read").
