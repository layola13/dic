givens:/ˈɡɪvənz/ | n. | 给定条件，前提，已知条件 | something that is certain or already known and accepted, used as a basis for further work | The givens of the situation are that we have limited resources and a tight deadline.  这个情况的既定条件是我们资源有限，期限紧迫。| synonyms: facts, data, assumptions, premises | antonyms: unknowns, variables | noun

givens:/ˈɡɪvənz/ | n. | 规定，指定 | something that is prescribed or ordained | The givens of the competition were that each team had to build a bridge out of spaghetti. 比赛的规定是每个队必须用意大利面条建造一座桥。 | synonyms: rules, regulations, stipulations | antonyms: options, choices | noun
