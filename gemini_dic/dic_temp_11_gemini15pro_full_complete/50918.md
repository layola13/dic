deservingnesses:/dɪˈzɜːrvɪŋnɪsɪz/ | n. |  值得；应得；功绩（通常用单数 deservingness）| The quality of being deserving; merit.  It refers to the extent to which someone or something is worthy of reward or recognition. |  His deservingnesses were acknowledged with a medal. 他的功绩被一枚奖章所表彰。| 近义词：merit, worth, worthiness | 反义词：unworthiness, undeservingness | 词性: 名词

词源: From deserving + -ness.  Deserving comes from the Old French *deservir* (to deserve), from the Latin *deservire* (to serve well, to earn by service).
