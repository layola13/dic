inverts:/ɪnˈvɜːts/ /ɪnˈvɝːts/
n.|无脊椎动物|Animals without backbones, such as insects, worms, and mollusks.|Many marine inverts are sensitive to changes in water temperature. 许多海洋无脊椎动物对水温的变化很敏感。|invertebrates|vertebrates|noun


inverts:/ɪnˈvɜːts/ /ɪnˈvɝːts/
v.|使…倒置，使…反转|To turn something upside down or inside out; to reverse the position, order, or relationship of something.|He inverted the glass to drain the last drop. 他把杯子倒过来，沥干最后一滴。|reverse, overturn, flip|reinstate, upright|verb

Etymology:
From Latin *invertere*, from *in-* (in, into, on, upon, against) + *vertere* (to turn).
