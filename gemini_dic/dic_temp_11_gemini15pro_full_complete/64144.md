equalising:/ˈiːkwəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使相等，使均衡
The government is trying to equalise educational opportunities. 政府正在努力使教育机会均等。
Making adjustments to equalise the sound levels. 做调整以均衡音量。
verb
扳平比分
He equalised in the last minute of the game. 他在比赛的最后一分钟扳平了比分。
She equalised the score at 2-2. 她将比分扳平为2-2。
adjective
均衡的，相等的
The equalising effect of taxation. 税收的均衡效应。
They are working towards an equalising of resources. 他们正致力于资源的均衡化。

近义词: leveling, balancing, evening, matching
反义词: differentiating, unbalancing, varying, separating
词性: verb, adjective (present participle of equalise)
