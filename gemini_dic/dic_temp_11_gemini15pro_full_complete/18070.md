beguinage:/ˈbɛɡɪnɑːʒ/ /beɪˈɡɪnɪdʒ/ | n. |女修道院，寡妇院；（尤指佛兰德的）贞节妇女住宅区 |A beguinage is a historical housing complex, common in the Low Countries, especially in Flanders, which was built to house beguines, lay religious women who lived in community without taking vows or retiring from the world.|  The beguinage in Bruges is a UNESCO World Heritage site. 布鲁日的寡妇院是联合国教科文组织世界遗产。

The beguinage offered a peaceful retreat for women seeking spiritual solace. 这座寡妇院为寻求精神慰藉的妇女提供了一个宁静的隐居之所。| 近义词: convent, nunnery |反义词: None | 词性: Noun
