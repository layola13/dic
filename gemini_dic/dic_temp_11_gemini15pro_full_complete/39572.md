conscribe:/kənˈskraɪb/
verb
强制征召，征兵
To compel into service, typically military service.
例句：The government conscribed young men into the army. 政府强制征召年轻人入伍。

例句：During wartime, many citizens were conscribed to fight.  战时，许多公民被征召入伍作战。


近义词：draft, enlist, recruit
反义词：discharge, release
词性：verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "conscribere" (to enroll, write together), from "com-" (together) + "scribere" (to write).
