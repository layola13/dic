overloads:/ˌəʊvərˈləʊdz/ /ˌoʊvərˈloʊdz/| n. v.|超载，过载；超负荷|An overload of information can be overwhelming. 信息过载会让人不知所措。|近义词：excess, surplus, overburden|反义词：underload, shortage|词性：名词/动词

overloads:/ˌəʊvərˈləʊdz/ /ˌoʊvərˈloʊdz/| v.|使超载，使过载；超负荷|The circuit was overloaded and blew a fuse. 电路超负荷，烧断了保险丝。|近义词：overburden, strain, tax|反义词：underload, relieve|词性：动词

词源: From "over-" (excessive) + "load" (burden).
