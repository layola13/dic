cultivatability:/ˌkʌltɪveɪtəˈbɪləti/
noun
可耕性，可培养性
The quality of being cultivable; the capacity for being cultivated.
The cultivatability of the land was assessed before planting.  这块土地的可耕性在种植前进行了评估。
The cultivatability of a person's talents depends on their dedication and hard work. 一个人的才能的可培养性取决于他们的奉献精神和努力程度。
cultivability, cultivableness
incultivability, barrenness
