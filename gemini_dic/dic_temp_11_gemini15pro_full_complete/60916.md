elephantine:/ˌelɪˈfæn.taɪn/ | adj. |巨大的，笨拙的；庞大的|Resembling an elephant in size or gait; massive or clumsy.|例句：The building was elephantine in its proportions. 这座建筑的比例非常庞大。|例句：He made an elephantine attempt at a joke. 他笨拙地尝试讲了个笑话。|近义词：massive, enormous, gigantic, colossal, cumbersome, clumsy|反义词：small, tiny, minuscule, graceful, agile|词性:形容词

词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin elephantinus, from Latin elephantus ‘elephant’.
