ideogram:/ˈaɪdiəɡræm/ | n. |表意文字; 象形文字|A written symbol that represents an idea or object directly rather than a particular word or speech sound, as in Chinese characters and some other writing systems.|例句：Chinese characters are ideograms, representing whole words or concepts. 汉字是表意文字，代表整个单词或概念。

例句：The ancient Egyptians used ideograms in their hieroglyphic writing. 古埃及人在象形文字中使用表意文字。|近义词：symbol, character, glyph |反义词:phoneme, alphabet |词性:noun
