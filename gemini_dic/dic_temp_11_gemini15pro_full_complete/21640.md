blurry:/ˈblɜːri/ (US) /ˈbləːri/ (UK)| adj.|模糊的，看不清的|Lacking in clarity or sharpness of image; indistinct.|The photograph was blurry because my hand shook. 这张照片很模糊，因为我的手抖了。|近义词：hazy, fuzzy, indistinct, out of focus|反义词：clear, sharp, distinct, focused|词性:形容词

blur:/blɜːr/ (US) /bləː(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|模糊，模糊的东西；使模糊|Something that is indistinct or hazy; to make or become indistinct or hazy.|The rain blurred the view from the window. 雨水使窗外的景色变得模糊。|近义词：haze, smudge|反义词：clarify, sharpen|词性:名词，动词

词源: From blur (v.) + -y (adj. suffix).  The verb "blur" likely originated from a blend of "blear" (to make dim) and perhaps "blear-eyed."
