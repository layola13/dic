duchesses:/ˈdʌtʃɪsɪz/
n.
公爵夫人（duchess 的复数）
Plural form of duchess, referring to more than one woman holding the title of duchess.
The duchesses attended the royal wedding.  公爵夫人们参加了皇室婚礼。
The duchesses were known for their philanthropy. 这些公爵夫人以她们的慈善事业而闻名。
noblewomen, peeresses
commoners
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *duchesse, from duc "duke" + -esse (feminine suffix). Ultimately from Latin dux "leader."
