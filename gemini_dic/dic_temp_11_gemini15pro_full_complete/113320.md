lux:/lʌks/ | n. | 勒克斯（光照度单位） |  The basic unit of illuminance in the International System of Units (SI). One lux is equal to one lumen per square meter. | The lux level in the office was around 500. 办公室的勒克斯水平大约是500。| synonyms: illuminance, luminous flux density | antonyms: darkness | noun
