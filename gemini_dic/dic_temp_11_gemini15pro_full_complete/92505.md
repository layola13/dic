hypothesists:/haɪˈpɒθɪsɪsts/ | n. | 假设者，假设检验者 | A person who proposes a hypothesis; someone who engages in hypothesizing. |  Example 1: The hypothesists presented their findings at the conference.  假设者在会议上展示了他们的发现。

Example 2:  Many hypothesists believe this theory to be true. 许多假设者认为这个理论是正确的。 | 近义词：theorist, speculator, conjecturer | 反义词：empiricist, pragmatist | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hypothesis + -ist. Hypothesis comes from the Greek "hupothesis" meaning "a placing under," from "hupo" (under) + "thesis" (a placing, proposition).
