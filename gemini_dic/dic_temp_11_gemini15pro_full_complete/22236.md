bonded:/ˈbɒndɪd/ (UK), /ˈbɑːndɪd/ (US)| adj.|保税的；粘合的；结合的；有担保的；团结的|Joined securely; having formed a close relationship.  Relating to goods held in bond in a warehouse until duties are paid.|The shipment consisted of bonded goods. 这批货物是保税货物。|Synonyms: attached, connected, united, secured, guaranteed|Antonyms: separated, detached, unconnected, unsecured, unguaranteed|adjective

bonded:/ˈbɒndɪd/ (UK), /ˈbɑːndɪd/ (US)| v.|粘合；结合；(使)团结；担保 (bond的过去式和过去分词)|Past tense and past participle of "bond," meaning to join securely or to form a close relationship. Also, to provide surety for.|The bricks were bonded with mortar. 这些砖是用砂浆粘合的。|Synonyms: attached, connected, united, secured|Antonyms: separated, detached, unconnected|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
