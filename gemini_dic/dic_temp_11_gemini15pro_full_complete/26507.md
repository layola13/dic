cabaret:/ˈkæbəˌreɪ/ (US), /ˈkæbəreɪ/ (UK)| n.|歌舞表演，夜总会|A restaurant or nightclub providing entertainment, typically including music, song, and dance.|例句：We went to a cabaret show last night. 昨晚我们去看了一场歌舞表演。

例句：The cabaret featured a variety of acts, including singers, dancers, and comedians.  这家夜总会表演了各种节目，包括歌手、舞蹈演员和喜剧演员。

近义词：nightclub, club, nightspot|反义词:theater, opera|词性:noun
