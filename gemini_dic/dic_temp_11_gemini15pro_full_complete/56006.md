do-gooder:/ˈduːˌɡʊdər/
noun
行善者，做好事的人
A person who does things that they think will help other people, sometimes when other people do not want their help.  Often used with slightly negative connotations, implying that the person is interfering or self-righteous.
例句：The do-gooders are always trying to tell us how to live our lives. 这些爱管闲事的人总是试图告诉我们该如何生活。

例句：He resented the do-gooders who came to the village offering unsolicited advice. 他讨厌那些来到村里提供不请自来建议的爱管闲事的人。
近义词：altruist, philanthropist, humanitarian
反义词：malefactor, wrongdoer
