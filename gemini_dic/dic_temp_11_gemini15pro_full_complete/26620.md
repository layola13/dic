cachucha:/kəˈtʃuːtʃə/
Noun
卡丘卡舞; 一种西班牙舞; 一种安达卢西亚风格的独舞; 一种女帽
A lively Spanish solo dance, typically performed by a woman wearing a bolero jacket and a cachucha, a flat-topped hat with a brim.  Also refers to the hat itself.
She danced the cachucha with fire and grace. 她热情而优雅地跳着卡丘卡舞。
Synonyms: Spanish dance, flamenco, bolero, fandango
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a specific dance. Could use general antonyms like stillness, inactivity)

cachucha:/kəˈtʃuːtʃə/
Hat
卡丘卡帽; 一种扁平的带檐帽
A flat-topped hat with a brim, traditionally worn by women in Spain.
The cachucha shaded her eyes from the sun. 卡丘卡帽遮住了她的眼睛，挡住了阳光。
Synonyms: hat, bonnet, headwear
