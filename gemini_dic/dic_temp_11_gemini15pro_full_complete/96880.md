inexorablenesses:/ɪnˈɛksərəblənəsɪz/| n. |无情；不可阻挡；不可动摇|The quality or state of being inexorable; relentless inevitability.|The inexorablenesses of time marched on, leaving its mark on all things.  时间的无情流逝，在万物上留下痕迹。|The inexorablenesses of fate brought them together again.  命运的不可阻挡使他们再次相遇。|近义词：relentlessness, implacability, inevitability|反义词：flexibility, yielding, mercy|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From inexorable + -ness.  Inexorable from Latin inexorabilis "unyielding," from in- "not" + exorabilis "able to be moved by entreaty," from ex- "out" + orare "to pray, beg."
