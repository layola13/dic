nonpatentable:/ˌnɒnˈpeɪtəntəbəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈpeɪtn̩təbl/
adjective
不可申请专利的
Not able to be patented.  Something that cannot legally be protected by a patent.
The invention was deemed nonpatentable due to prior art.  这项发明由于现有技术而被认为不可申请专利。
The recipe, while unique, was nonpatentable.  这个食谱虽然独特，但不可申请专利。
近义词:unpatentable
反义词:patentable
词性:adjective

patentable:/ˈpeɪtəntəbəl/ /ˈpætn̩təbl/
adjective
可申请专利的
Able to be patented; meeting the legal requirements for patent protection.
A patentable invention must be novel, useful, and non-obvious. 一项可申请专利的发明必须是新颖的、有用的和非显而易见的。
The new drug formula was considered patentable.  这种新药配方被认为是可申请专利的。
近义词:inventive, novel
反义词:nonpatentable, unpatentable
词性:adjective
