entitles:/ɪnˈtaɪtlz/ | v. | 赋予权利；给予资格 | To give someone the right to have or do something. |  例句：This ticket entitles you to a free drink. 这张票可以让你免费获得一杯饮料。

例句：Being a member entitles you to discounts on purchases. 成为会员可以让你在购物时享受折扣。

近义词：authorizes, empowers, qualifies | 反义词：disqualifies, prohibits | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *entituler*, from en- "in" + title.  Ultimately from Latin titulus "title, inscription."
