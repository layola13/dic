overresponding:/ˌoʊvərɪˈspɑːndɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度反应的 | Reacting excessively or disproportionately to a stimulus. |  She tends to overrespond to criticism, taking it very personally. 她倾向于对批评过度反应，认为这是针对个人的。

The market overresponded to the news, causing a sharp drop in prices. 市场对这则消息反应过度，导致价格暴跌。 | 近义词: overreacting, hypersensitivity | 反义词: underreacting, unresponsiveness | 词性: adjective
