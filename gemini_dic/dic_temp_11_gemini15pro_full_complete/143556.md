outhector:/ˌaʊtˈhɛktər/| vt.|威吓；以大声压倒；胜过|To intimidate or silence by bluster or superior loudness; to surpass in hectoring. |He outhectored his opponents in the debate, drowning out their arguments with his booming voice. 他在辩论中以大声压倒对手，用他洪亮的声音盖过了他们的论点。|  |Synonyms: browbeat, bully, cow, intimidate, domineer | Antonyms:  cajole, coax, persuade, soothe | Verb
