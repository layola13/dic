non-judicial:/ˌnɒn dʒuːˈdɪʃəl/
adjective
非司法的；非裁判的
Not relating to, involving, or done by a court of law or a judge.
The dispute was settled through non-judicial means.  这个纠纷通过非司法途径解决了。
The organization provides non-judicial dispute resolution services. 该组织提供非司法纠纷解决服务。
近义词: extrajudicial, nonlegal, informal
反义词: judicial, legal
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + judicial.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Judicial" comes from the Latin word "iudicialis," meaning "pertaining to a judge or court."
