overrulingly:/ˌoʊvərˈruːlɪŋli/| adv.|压倒性地，不可抗拒地|In a manner that overrides or supersedes all other considerations.  It describes an action or decision made with decisive and final authority.|The judge overrulingly dismissed the objection. 法官断然驳回了异议。|近义词：decisively, conclusively, definitively, peremptorily|反义词: indecisively, hesitantly|词性:副词
词源: From "overrule" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Overrule" comes from Old French "overruler," from "over-" (above, across) and "ruler" (to rule).
