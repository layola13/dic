isopepsin:/ˌaɪsoʊˈpɛpsɪn/ | n. |异胃蛋白酶|A proteolytic enzyme found in the stomach, similar to pepsin but with a lower optimum pH. It is formed from pepsinogen under very acidic conditions.|Isopepsin is responsible for some protein digestion in the stomach. 异胃蛋白酶负责胃中的一些蛋白质消化。| Isopepsin levels can be elevated in certain conditions. 在某些情况下，异胃蛋白酶水平会升高。 |Synonym: pepsin (although not fully interchangeable due to pH difference) | Antonym: None (as it's an enzyme, it doesn't have a direct opposite) | noun
