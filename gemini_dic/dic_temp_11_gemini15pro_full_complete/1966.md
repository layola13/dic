activation:/ˌæktɪˈveɪʃən/| n.|激活，活化|The activation of the fire alarm alerted everyone in the building. 火灾警报的启动使楼里每个人都警觉起来。|近义词: stimulation, triggering, initiation|反义词: deactivation, suppression|noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *activatio, from Latin activus (“active”) + -atio. Equivalent to activate + -ion.
