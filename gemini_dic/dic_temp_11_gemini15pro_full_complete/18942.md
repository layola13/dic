besprinkler:/bɪˈsprɪŋklər/ | n. |洒水器，喷洒器 |A device that sprinkles water, typically used for irrigation or fire suppression. | The farmer installed besprinklers in his field to ensure even watering. 农民在他的田地里安装了洒水器以确保均匀浇水。

The fire besprinkler system activated quickly, preventing further damage. 消防喷淋系统迅速启动，防止了进一步的损坏。| 近义词: sprinkler, irrigator | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "dehydrator" in specific contexts) | 词性: noun
