misreforming:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɔːrmɪŋ/
verb
错误改革；改革不当
Implementing reforms in a way that produces unintended negative consequences or fails to achieve the desired positive outcomes.
The government's misreforming of the healthcare system led to increased costs and reduced access for many citizens. 政府对医疗体系的改革不当导致成本增加，许多公民的就医机会减少。
The misreforming of the education system has widened the gap between the rich and the poor. 教育体制改革失当加剧了贫富差距。
近义词: mismanaging, bungling, botching
反义词: reforming, improving, enhancing
词性: verb
