millennia:/mɪˈlɛniə/| n.|千年，数千年|A period of one thousand years.  A period of great happiness or human perfection.|例句：The Roman Empire spanned several millennia. 罗马帝国跨越了几个千年。

例句：They believed a golden millennia of peace was coming. 他们相信和平的黄金千年即将到来。|近义词：chiliad, thousand years|反义词: instant, moment|词性:noun
