mural:/ˈmjʊərəl/| n.|壁画|A painting done directly on a wall.|Example 1: The mural depicted scenes from local history.  这幅壁画描绘了当地历史的场景。

Example 2:  They commissioned a local artist to paint a mural in the children's ward. 他们委托一位当地艺术家在儿童病房画一幅壁画。|Synonym: fresco, wall painting|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "canvas painting" as a contrasting concept)|noun
