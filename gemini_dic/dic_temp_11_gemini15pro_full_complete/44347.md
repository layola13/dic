cross-aisle: /ˈkrɒs aɪl/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːs aɪl/ (US) | adj. |跨党派的，跨越党派界限的 | Involving or characterized by cooperation between opposing political parties or factions. |  The senator was known for his cross-aisle collaborations.  这位参议员以其跨党派合作而闻名。|  Bipartisan, cooperative | Partisan, divisive | adjective

Etymology:  From "cross" (meaning to go across) + "aisle" (referring to the central aisle in a legislative chamber that separates different political parties).
