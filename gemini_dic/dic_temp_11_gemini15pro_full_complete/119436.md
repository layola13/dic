microlepidoptera:/ˌmaɪkrəʊˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərə/ (US) /ˌmaɪkrəʊˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərə/ (UK)| n. |小鳞翅目昆虫|A group of insects, traditionally including all the families of moths considered more primitive than the macrolepidoptera.  These are generally small moths, often with narrow fringed wings.|Microlepidoptera are often overlooked due to their small size. 小鳞翅目昆虫由于体型小，经常被忽视。| The study of microlepidoptera can be challenging due to their diversity. 研究小鳞翅目昆虫很有挑战性，因为它们种类繁多。|近义词:micromoths|反义词:macrolepidoptera|noun
