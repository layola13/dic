gill-like:/ˈɡɪlˌlaɪk/
adjective
鳃状的；像鳃的
Resembling or suggesting gills in appearance or function.
The fungus had gill-like structures underneath its cap. 这种真菌的菌盖下面有鳃状结构。
The salamander's feathery external gills looked gill-like. 蝾螈羽毛状的外部鳃看起来像鳃。
近义词:  branchial, lamellar
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite appearance, e.g., smooth, solid)
词性: adjective
