outstormed:/aʊtˈstɔːrmd/
verb
战胜暴风雨，在暴风雨中幸存
To have endured or survived a storm.  To have weathered a storm more successfully than something or someone else.
The ship outstormed the hurricane. 这艘船在飓风中幸存下来。
The old lighthouse outstormed many a gale.  这座古老的灯塔经历了无数次大风。
近义词: weathered, survived, endured
反义词: succumbed, perished
