divertimento:/ˌdɪvɜːrtɪˈmentoʊ/ /ˌdɪvɜːtɪˈmɛntoʊ/| n. |嬉游曲；娱乐；消遣|A divertimento is a light and entertaining musical composition, typically in several movements, often for chamber ensemble.|Mozart composed several divertimenti for various instrumental combinations. 莫扎特创作了几首为各种乐器组合而作的嬉游曲。|The king enjoyed the divertimento performed by the court musicians. 国王很享受宫廷乐师演奏的嬉游曲。|synonyms: serenade, cassation, entertainment, amusement|antonyms: work, labor|noun

divertimenti:/ˌdɪvɜːrtɪˈmenti/ /ˌdɪvɜːtɪˈmɛnti/| n. |嬉游曲（divertimento的复数）|The plural form of divertimento.|The orchestra played three divertimenti by Haydn. 管弦乐队演奏了三首海顿的嬉游曲。|Several divertimenti were on the program for the evening's concert. 晚间音乐会的节目单上有几首嬉游曲。|synonyms: serenades, cassations, entertainments, amusements|antonyms: works, labors|noun
