nonpublicities:/ˌnɒnpʌbˈlɪsɪtiz/
noun
非公开；未公开的事项
Lack of publicity or advertising; the state of not being publicized.  The company chose nonpublicity for its new product launch, preferring a quiet rollout.
The company's nonpublicity surrounding the incident led to much speculation. 公司对该事件的低调处理导致了很多猜测。
near synonyms: secrecy, confidentiality, privacy, discretion
antonyms: publicity, advertising, exposure, disclosure
n.
