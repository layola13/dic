inexhaustible:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔːstəbl/ | adj. | 取之不尽的，用之不竭的 |  So large or extensive as to seem impossible to exhaust. |  The world’s supply of information seems inexhaustible. 世界上的信息似乎取之不尽，用之不竭。 |  unlimited, boundless, infinite | finite, limited | adjective

inexhaustible:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔːstəbl/ | adj. | 无穷无尽的 |  Unable to be used up because existing in abundance. |  She has an inexhaustible supply of energy. 她精力充沛，似乎永远不知疲倦。|  unending, limitless, boundless |  limited, finite, exhausted | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin inexhaustabilis, from in- ‘not’ + exhaustus (past participle of exhaurire ‘to drain out’).
