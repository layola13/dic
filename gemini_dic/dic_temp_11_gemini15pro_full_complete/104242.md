jurisprudential:/ˌdʒʊrɪsˈpruːdənʃəl/
adjective
法理的；法律学的
Relating to the science or philosophy of law.
The judge made a jurisprudential argument for his decision. 法官为他的判决提出了一个法理学上的论点。
近义词：legal, judicial, forensic
反义词：None (lacks a direct antonym)
词性: adjective
词源：From Late Latin *iurisprudentia, from Latin iūris prūdentia (“knowledge of law”), from iūs (“law”) + prūdentia (“prudence”).
