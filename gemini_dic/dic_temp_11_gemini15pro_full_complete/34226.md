clankety-clank:/ˌklæŋkɪti ˈklæŋk/ | adj. adv.|叮当作响的，发出叮当声|A reduplicative word imitating the sound of metallic objects hitting each other.|The old train moved along the tracks, clankety-clank, clankety-clank. 老火车沿着铁轨行驶，发出叮当声。| The trolley rattled along, clankety-clank, down the cobbled street. 手推车沿着鹅卵石铺成的街道发出叮当声响前行。 |Synonyms: clangorous, jangling, rattling|Antonyms: silent, quiet|Onomatopoeia
