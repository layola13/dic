biddy:/ˈbɪdi/| n.|老妇人，小鸡|An elderly woman, often considered gossipy or interfering; a young chicken.|The biddies at the bingo hall were gossiping about the new neighbors. 宾果游戏厅里的老妇人们正在谈论新邻居。|The farmer bought a dozen biddies for his coop. 农夫买了一打小鸡到他的鸡舍里。|近义词：old woman, hen (for chicken)|反义词:youngster (for chicken), young woman (for woman)|noun

biddy:/ˈbɪdi/| n.|女人，姑娘（爱尔兰英语）|A woman or girl (chiefly Irish English).|He met a lovely biddy at the pub. 他在酒吧里遇见了一位可爱的姑娘。(爱尔兰英语用法)|She’s a feisty biddy and won’t be told what to do. 她是个泼辣的女人，不喜欢别人指使她。(爱尔兰英语用法)|近义词：woman, girl, lass|反义词:man, boy|noun
