gobstopper:/ˈɡɒbstɒpə(r)/ (British), /ˈɡɑːbstɑːpər/ (American)| n. |硬糖，彩虹糖，巨型糖球|A large, hard spherical candy that changes colors and flavors as it dissolves in the mouth.|She loved gobstoppers as a child, especially the ones that lasted for hours. 她小时候很喜欢彩虹糖，尤其是那种可以吃好几个小时的。|Example: The boy clutched his gobstopper tightly, savoring the changing flavors.  男孩紧紧地攥着他的彩虹糖，细细品味着不断变化的味道。

|Synonyms: jawbreaker, everlasting gobstopper|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
