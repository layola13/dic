hot-headed:/ˈhɒtˈhedɪd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtˈhedɪd/ (US)| adj.|性急的，易怒的，鲁莽的|Easily angered; having a fiery temper.  Characterized by rash actions or decisions driven by anger.|He's so hot-headed, he always gets into fights. 他太性急了，总是跟人打架。|Synonyms: impetuous, rash, fiery, quick-tempered, irascible|Antonyms: calm, level-headed, patient, composed|adjective

hot-headed:|Etymology:  From "hot" (referring to high temperature, passionate) + "head" (referring to the mind or intellect). The combination suggests a mind easily inflamed by passion or anger.|词源：由“hot”（指高温、热情）和“head”（指头脑或智力）组成。这个组合暗示了一个容易被激情或愤怒点燃的头脑。
