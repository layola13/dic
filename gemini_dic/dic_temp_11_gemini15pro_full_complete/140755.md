omnivagant:/ˌɒmnɪˈveɪɡənt/
adjective
漫游的，四处漂泊的
Wandering everywhere; roaming over all places.
例句：The omnivagant nature of his job allowed him to experience different cultures.  他四处漂泊的工作性质让他体验了不同的文化。

例句：Her omnivagant spirit led her to explore remote corners of the world. 她漂泊不定的精神引领她探索世界的偏远角落。

近义词：wandering, roaming, nomadic, peripatetic, itinerant
反义词：settled, stationary, static
词性: adjective
