count's:/kaʊnts/ | n. |伯爵的；郡的|Something that belongs to or is associated with a count. | The count's castle was magnificent. |伯爵的城堡很宏伟。|Synonyms: earl's, nobleman's | Antonyms: commoner's, peasant's | possessive noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *conte*, from Latin *comes* ("companion"), originally a court official. The possessive form is created by adding 's.
