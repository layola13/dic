egoist: /ˈiːɡəʊɪst/ (UK) /ˈiːɡoʊɪst/ (US)| n. |利己主义者；自我主义者|A person who is excessively self-centered and concerned only with their own needs and desires, often at the expense of others. |例句：He's such an egoist; he only ever thinks about himself. 他真是个利己主义者，他总是只想着自己。

例句：The egoist cared little for the feelings of others.  这个利己主义者很少关心别人的感受。


近义词：narcissist, self-seeker, egotist |反义词: altruist, philanthropist |词性: noun
