amphichroic:/æmfɪˈkroʊɪk/
adjective
两色的，双色的
Exhibiting amphichroism; having the property of changing color when viewed from different directions.
Alexandrite is an amphichroic gem, appearing green in daylight and red under incandescent light.  亚历山大石是一种双色宝石，在日光下呈绿色，在白炽灯下呈红色。
The mineral displays amphichroic behavior, shifting from blue to green depending on the angle of light. 这种矿物表现出双色性，根据光线的角度从蓝色变为绿色。

近义词:dichroic, pleochroic
反义词:monochromatic
词性:adjective
