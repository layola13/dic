irony's:/ˈaɪrəniz/| n.|讽刺意味；反语；具有讽刺意味的事情|The irony's that he complained about the noise, but he's the one who plays loud music all night.  具有讽刺意味的是，他抱怨噪音，但他才是整晚大声播放音乐的人。|例句：The irony's that after all his hard work, he failed the exam.  具有讽刺意味的是，在他辛勤工作之后，他考试不及格。|近义词：sarcasm, satire|反义词: sincerity, seriousness|词性:名词

词源: From Middle French *ironie*, from Latin *ironia*, from Ancient Greek εἰρωνεία (eirōneía, “dissimulation, assumed ignorance”).
