missouri-river:/mɪˈzʊri ˈrɪvər/ | n. | 密苏里河 | A major river in the central United States, a tributary of the Mississippi River. | Lewis and Clark explored the Missouri River. 刘易斯和克拉克探险了密苏里河。| The Missouri River is an important waterway. 密苏里河是一条重要的水道。 |近义词: waterway, river |反义词: (no direct antonym) |词性:noun

Etymology:
From the Missouri tribe, whose name is of uncertain meaning.  The French name, "le Missoury," appears in the early 18th century.
