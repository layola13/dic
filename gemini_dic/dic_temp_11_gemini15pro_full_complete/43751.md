例句：The movie gave me the creepies. 这部电影让我毛骨悚然。|近义词：shivers, chills, goosebumps, heebie-jeebies|反义词:comfort, ease|词性:名词

Etymology:  From "creepy," an adjective meaning causing an unpleasant feeling of fear or unease. "Creepy" likely originated as a variant of "crepe" (crêpe), referring to the crinkling of skin that resembles crepe fabric, often associated with fear or disgust.
