desmogged|adj.|已消除烟雾的，已去除烟雾的|Past participle of desmog.  Means having had smog removed or reduced.  Refers to a place or a vehicle.|例句：The city center was finally desmogged after years of effort. 多年努力后，市中心终于消除了烟雾。

例句：The desmogged car passed the emissions test. 这辆除去了烟雾的汽车通过了排放测试。|近义词：clean, purified, clear|反义词：smoggy, polluted|词性:形容词
词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove") + smog + -ed (past participle suffix)
