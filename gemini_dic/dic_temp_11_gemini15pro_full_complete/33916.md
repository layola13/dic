circumvolve: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɒlv/ (US) /ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɒlv/ (UK)
verb
环绕，旋转
To cause something to revolve around something else; to rotate something around something else.
The planets circumvolve the sun.  行星绕太阳旋转。
The moon circumvolves the Earth. 月球绕地球旋转。
近义词: revolve, rotate, orbit, circle
反义词: N/A
词性: verb

Etymology:
From Latin circumvolvere, from circum- (“around”) + volvere (“to roll”).
