misallotted:/ˌmɪsəˈlɒtɪd/
verb
错误分配的，分配不当的
incorrectly allocated or distributed
The funds were misallotted, resulting in a budget shortfall. 资金分配不当，导致预算短缺。
The company misallotted resources to the wrong departments. 公司将资源错误地分配给了错误的部门。
misallocated, wrongly distributed
correctly allocated, properly distributed
