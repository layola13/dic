gumptionless:/ˈɡʌmpʃən.ləs/
adjective
缺乏进取心的；没有胆量的；没有魄力的
Lacking initiative, resourcefulness, or courage.
He was too gumptionless to complain. 他太没胆量抱怨了。
Synonyms: spineless, timid, cowardly, unenterprising
Antonyms: gutsy, spirited, enterprising, resourceful
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From gumption + -less.  Gumption is of uncertain origin, possibly related to dialectal gump 'understanding, common sense'.
