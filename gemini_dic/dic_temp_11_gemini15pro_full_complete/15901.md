ballots:/ˈbæləts/| n.|选票|A piece of paper used to record a vote, typically in an election or referendum.|The ballots were carefully counted after the election. 选举结束后，选票被仔细清点。|synonyms: votes, polls, slips|antonyms: abstentions|noun

ballot:/ˈbælət/| n.|投票|The process of voting, typically by secret ballot.|The union held a ballot to decide whether to strike. 工会举行投票决定是否罢工。|synonyms: vote, poll, election|antonyms: abstention|noun
