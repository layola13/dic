amphoric:/æmˈfɔːrɪk/ | adj. |瓮的；空洞的；像瓮的；回音的|Resembling the hollow sound of a large jar; echoing. | The singer's voice resonated with an amphoric quality, filling the entire hall. 歌手的嗓音带有瓮声瓮气的特质，回荡在整个大厅里。

The old well had an amphoric echo when you dropped a stone in it. 当你往古井里扔石头时，会发出瓮声回响。|近义词：resonant, echoing, hollow, booming |反义词：muffled, dampened, quiet |词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *amphoricus, from Latin amphora (“a two-handled jar, especially for wine or oil”), from Ancient Greek ἀμφορεύς (amphoreús). Ultimately from ἀμφί (amphí, “on both sides”) + φέρω (phérō, “I carry”).
