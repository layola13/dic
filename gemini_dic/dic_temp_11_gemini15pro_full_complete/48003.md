decipher:/dɪˈsaɪfər/ /dəˈsaɪfər/| vt. n.|破译，解密，辨认|to succeed in understanding something that is difficult or unclear|例句：I couldn't decipher his handwriting. 我无法辨认他的笔迹。

例句：The code was difficult to decipher.  这密码很难破译。

近义词：decode, interpret, solve, unravel|反义词：encode, encrypt, obscure|词性：动词,名词

decipher:/dɪˈsaɪfər/ /dəˈsaɪfər/| n.|破译，解密|the act of deciphering something|例句：The decipherment of the code took several hours.  破译密码花了几个小时。

例句：The decipherment of ancient texts is a fascinating field of study. 对古代文献的解读是一个引人入胜的研究领域。


近义词：decoding, interpretation, solution|反义词：encoding, encryption|词性：名词
