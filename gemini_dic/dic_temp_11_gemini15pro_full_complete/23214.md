bowest:/ˈbaʊɪst/| v.|你弯腰，你鞠躬 (古英语第二人称单数现在时)|This archaic form is derived from the Middle English *bowen*, from Old English *būgan*. It corresponds to the modern English "bow".  It signifies the act of bending the head or upper body as a gesture of respect, submission, or greeting.|"Thou bowest before the king." 你在国王面前鞠躬。|近义词：stoop, incline,  curtsy (especially for women)|反义词:stand erect, straighten|词性:动词
