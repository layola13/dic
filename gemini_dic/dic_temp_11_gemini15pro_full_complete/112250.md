loots:/luːts/| n. v.|战利品，掠夺物；掠夺|Stolen goods, especially after a war or riot; the act of stealing goods in this way. |The soldiers looted the city after it fell. 士兵们在城市沦陷后洗劫了这座城市。|近义词：spoils, plunder, booty, pillage|反义词:restitution, return|词性:名词/动词

loots:/luːts/| v.|掠夺，抢劫|To steal goods from (a place), typically during a war or riot.|They looted shops and homes during the riot. 他们在暴乱期间抢劫了商店和房屋。|近义词：ransack, plunder, pillage|反义词:protect, defend|词性:动词

词源: From the Hindi word लूट (lūṭ), meaning "robbery, plunder".
