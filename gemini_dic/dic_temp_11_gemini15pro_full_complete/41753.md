corruptible:/kəˈrʌptəbl/
adjective
可腐败的；易堕落的；易受贿赂的
Susceptible to corruption, bribery, or decay.
The official was corruptible and easily swayed by money.  这位官员很容易腐败，容易被金钱左右。
The system is so corruptible that honest officials have a hard time. 这个系统太腐败了，正直的官员日子不好过。

近义词:dishonest, venal, bribable, susceptible,  influenced
反义词:honest, incorruptible, unbribable, upright,  principled
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *corruptibilis, from Latin corrumpere (“to corrupt, destroy”).
