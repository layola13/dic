overfill:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪl/| v. n.|过度填充，过满|To fill something beyond its capacity or limit.  To become filled beyond capacity. |The attendant overfilled the gas tank, and some spilled out. 服务员把油箱加得太满了，有些汽油溢出来了。|近义词：overflow, glut, surcharge|反义词: underfill|verb

overfill:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪl/| n.|过度填充，过量|The act of overfilling or the state of being overfilled. |There was an overfill of applications for the job.  这个工作的申请太多了。|近义词：excess, surplus|反义词: shortage, deficit|noun
