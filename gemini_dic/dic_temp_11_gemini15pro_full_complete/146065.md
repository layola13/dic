overearly:/ˈəʊvərˈɜːli/ | adv. | 过早地 | Too early; earlier than necessary or expected. |  They arrived overearly for the meeting and had to wait. 他们到开会地点过早，不得不等候。|  The crops were harvested overearly due to the unexpected frost. 由于意外的霜冻，农作物过早收割。| 近义词: prematurely, too soon | 反义词: late, tardily | 词性: 副词

Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "early" (Old English ærlice, from ær "early" + -lice "-ly")
