antipathous:/ænˈtɪpəθəs/
adj.
互相厌恶的；不相容的
Having or showing a strong aversion or dislike; incompatible.
The two colleagues were antipathous to each other, constantly bickering and undermining each other's work.  这两个同事互相厌恶，经常争吵，并且互相破坏对方的工作。
近义词：hostile, antagonistic, averse, incompatible
反义词：sympathetic, friendly, compatible, harmonious
词性: adjective

antipathy:/ænˈtɪpəθi/
n.
憎恶，反感
A deep-seated feeling of aversion.
She felt a strong antipathy towards him from the moment they met. 她从见到他的那一刻起就对他非常反感。
近义词：aversion, hostility, animosity, antagonism
反义词：affinity, liking, sympathy, attraction
词性: noun

词源: From Ancient Greek ἀντίπαθος (antípathos, “suffering against, affected adversely by”), from ἀντί (antí, “against”) + πάθος (páthos, “suffering, feeling”).
