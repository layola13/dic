misauthorizations:/ˌmɪsˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃənz/
n.
未授权，错误授权
The act of authorizing something incorrectly or without proper authority.
Numerous misauthorizations led to a security breach. 许多错误授权导致了安全漏洞。
Misauthorizations of funds resulted in significant losses. 资金的错误授权导致了重大损失。
Synonyms: unauthorized actions, improper authorizations, wrongful permissions
Antonyms: authorizations, permissions, approvals
noun
