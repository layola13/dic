concomitantly:/kənˈkɒmɪtəntli/ /kənˈkɑːmɪtəntli/
adverb
同时地；伴随地
occurring or existing at the same time
The drug is prescribed concomitantly with other therapies.  这种药物与其他疗法同时处方。
Concomitantly, the company began to diversify its investments.  与此同时，该公司开始使其投资多样化。

synonyms: simultaneously, concurrently, alongside, in conjunction with, in parallel
antonyms: separately, independently, individually
