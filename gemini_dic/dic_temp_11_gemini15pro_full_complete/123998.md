mornings:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋz/| n.|早晨，上午|the period of time between midnight and noon, especially from sunrise until noon or from when one wakes until noon.  The plural form refers to multiple instances of this period.|I enjoy quiet mornings. 我喜欢安静的早晨。|近义词：forenoons, a.m.|反义词:evenings, nights|Noun
