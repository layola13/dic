nontolerances: /nɒnˈtɒlərənsɪz/ | n. | 不耐受，不宽容 | Lack of tolerance; inability or unwillingness to tolerate something.  It often refers to a medical condition where a person cannot tolerate a particular substance, like a food or medication, but can also be used in a broader sense of not accepting differing views or behaviors. |  Many people experience nontolerances to certain food additives. 很多人对某些食品添加剂不耐受。|  The community showed a surprising nontolerance for differing opinions. 这个社群对不同意见表现出惊人的不宽容。 | Synonyms: intolerances, sensitivities, aversion, bigotry (in broader sense) | Antonyms: tolerances, acceptance, open-mindedness | noun
