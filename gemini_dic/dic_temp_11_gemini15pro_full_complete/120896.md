miscalculates:/ˌmɪskælkjʊˈleɪts/
verb
错误地计算；估算错
Calculates something incorrectly.
He miscalculated the distance and ran out of fuel before reaching the destination. 他错误地计算了距离，在到达目的地之前就没油了。
The company miscalculated the demand for their new product and ended up with a surplus. 该公司错误估计了新产品的需求，最终导致了过剩。
misjudge, misestimate, underestimate, overestimate
calculate correctly, estimate accurately
v.
