deciding:/dɪˈsaɪdɪŋ/
| adj. | 决定性的，关键的 | Having the power or quality of deciding; conclusive. | The deciding factor in the election was the economy. 选举的决定性因素是经济。 | crucial, pivotal, critical | unimportant, insignificant | adjective

deciding:/dɪˈsaɪdɪŋ/
| v. | 决定，决心 (decide的现在分词) |  The present participle of "decide".  | They are deciding where to go for vacation. 他们正在决定去哪里度假。 | choosing, determining, resolving | hesitating, wavering | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *deciden*, from Anglo-Norman *decider*, and Old French *decider*  ("to decide, determine, resolve, judge, cut off, settle"), from Latin *dēcīdere*  ("to cut off, decide, determine"), from *de-* ("off, down, away") + *caedere* ("to cut, hew, fell, kill").
