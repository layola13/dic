muffin-top:/ˈmʌfɪn tɒp/ (UK) /ˈmʌfɪn tɑːp/ (US)| n. | 腰间赘肉，(低腰裤/裙)露出的腰部脂肪|A roll of fat that spills over the top of tight-fitting low-waist trousers or skirts, like the top of a muffin. |例句：She was self-conscious about her muffin-top, so she wore a longer shirt. 她很在意自己的腰间赘肉，所以穿了件长点的衬衫。

例句：Low-rise jeans can create an unflattering muffin-top. 低腰牛仔裤会显出难看的腰间赘肉。|近义词：spare tire, love handles, belly fat|反义词: flat stomach|词性:名词
