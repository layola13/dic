beardless:/ˈbɪədləs/| adj.|无胡须的，没有胡子的|Without a beard.  Lacking a beard. |The beardless young man looked even younger than his years.  这个没有胡子的年轻人看起来比他的实际年龄还要小。|Example: He was surprised to see the usually beardless actor sporting a full beard. 他很惊讶地看到这位通常不留胡子的演员蓄起了浓密的胡须。|Synonyms: clean-shaven, smooth-faced|Antonyms: bearded, bewhiskered|Word type: Adjective
