kitty: /ˈkɪti/ (US), /ˈkɪti/ (UK)| n.|小猫;  (游戏的)赌注;  集体基金|A young cat.  Also can refer to a pool of money for gambling or shared expenses. |例句：The little girl adored her fluffy kitty. 小女孩非常喜欢她毛茸茸的小猫。

|例句：They put £10 each into the kitty for the office Christmas party.  他们每人拿出10英镑作为办公室圣诞派对的集体基金。|近义词：kitten, cat (more general)|反义词:  (No direct opposite for a young animal) |词性:noun


词源:
Shortened form of "kitten," which comes from Middle English *kitoun*, likely derived from Old French *chitoun* or *chaton*, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *cattinus*, a diminutive of *cattus*, meaning "cat."
