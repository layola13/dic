apsides: /ˈæpsɪdiːz/
n.
拱点（复数）
The points in an orbit that are closest to and farthest from the center of attraction.
The apsides of the Moon's orbit are the points where it is closest to (perigee) and farthest from (apogee) the Earth. 月球轨道的拱点是它离地球最近(近地点)和最远(远地点)的点。
近义词:  apses
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific geometric point)
词性:  Noun
