anorthopia:/ænɔːrˈθoʊpiə/| n. |斜视|A condition in which the lines of vision of the two eyes do not meet at the object looked at; vertical deviation of the visual axes.|The patient's anorthopia was corrected with surgery.|Near synonyms:  strabismus, heterotropia, squint|Antonym: orthophoria|noun
