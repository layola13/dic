cytomegalovirus: /ˌsaɪtoʊˈmɛɡəloʊˌvaɪrəs/ | n. | 巨细胞病毒 | A herpesvirus that can cause serious complications, especially in people with weakened immune systems.  It's often asymptomatic in healthy adults but can cause mononucleosis-like symptoms. It can be particularly dangerous for pregnant women and their unborn babies, and for people with compromised immune systems. |  Cytomegalovirus infection can be dangerous for newborns. 巨细胞病毒感染对新生儿很危险。|  Cytomegalovirus can be transmitted through saliva. 巨细胞病毒可以通过唾液传播。 | synonyms: CMV, human herpesvirus 5 (HHV-5) | antonyms: None | noun
