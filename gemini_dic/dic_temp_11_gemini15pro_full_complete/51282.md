det:/dɛt/ | determiner | 限定词 | A word (such as "a," "the," "some," "any," "my," "your," "his") that comes before a noun to show which thing or things are being referred to. | The dog wagged its tail. | 近义词: (No direct synonyms for determiners as a class, synonyms depend on the specific determiner)  a/an, the, this/that, these/those, my/your/his/her/its/our/their, each/every, some/any, much/many, few/little, several, both, all, either/neither, which/whose | 反义词: (No direct antonyms for determiners as a class) | 词性: determiner

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What book are you reading? = 你在读什么书？
What color is her dress? = 她的裙子是什么颜色？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What movie are you watching now? = 你现在在看什么电影？
What song is he listening to at the moment? = 他现在在听什么歌？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What countries have you visited? = 你去过哪些国家？
What work has she completed so far? = 到目前为止她完成了哪些工作？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What project have you been working on all day? = 你一整天都在做什么项目？
What book has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么书？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did she say about the plan? = 她对这个计划说了什么？
What movie did they watch last night? = 他们昨晚看了什么电影？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么事？
What had they discussed before making the decision? = 他们在做决定之前讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习什么五年了？
What will she have been working on by the time she retires? = 到她退休的时候，她将一直在做什么？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what the answer was, I would tell you. = 如果我知道答案是什么，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我希望我知道他在想什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the conference? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the issue? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:

She doesn't understand what he's saying. = 她不明白他在说什么。
