jokier:/ˈdʒoʊkiər/| adj.|更滑稽的，更爱开玩笑的|Comparative form of "jokey": more jokey.  Used to describe someone or something that is more humorous or playful than another.|He's much jokier today than he was yesterday. 他今天比昨天更爱开玩笑了。|The new comedian's routine was jokier than the last one.  这位新喜剧演员的表演比上一个更滑稽。|Synonyms: funnier, more humorous, wittier, more playful|Antonyms: more serious, more somber, less humorous, less playful|adjective
