abiogenesis:/ˌeɪˌbaɪoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ /ˌeɪˌbaɪəˈdʒɛnəsɪs/
noun
自然发生; 无生源说
The formation of living organisms from non-living matter.  The theory of abiogenesis holds that life arose from inorganic matter through natural processes.
Abiogenesis is the natural process by which life arises from non-living matter, such as simple organic compounds. 自然发生是生命从无生命物质（例如简单的有机化合物）中产生的自然过程。
Scientists are still researching the process of abiogenesis. 科学家们仍在研究自然发生的过程。
近义词: spontaneous generation
反义词: biogenesis
词性: noun
