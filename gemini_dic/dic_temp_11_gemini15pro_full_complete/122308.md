mittenlike:/ˈmɪtnˌlaɪk/
adjective
像手套一样的；形似连指手套的
Resembling a mitten; shaped like a mitten.
The baby's tiny hands were mittenlike.  婴儿的小手像手套一样。
Similar: glovelike, mitten-shaped
Opposite: fingered, separate
词性: 形容词
