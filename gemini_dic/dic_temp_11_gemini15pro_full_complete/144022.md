outreign:/ˌaʊtˈreɪn/
verb
统治比…更久; 比…活得更长; 超越…的统治时期
to reign longer than; to live longer than; to exceed the reign of
Queen Victoria outreigned all previous British monarchs. 维多利亚女王比之前所有英国君主在位时间都长。
He outreigned all his brothers. 他比他所有的兄弟都活得长。
近义词: outlast, survive, outlive
反义词: precede
词性: verb
