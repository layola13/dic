frazzled:/ˈfræzəld/| adj.|疲惫不堪的，精疲力竭的|Exhausted and stressed, especially after a period of sustained worry or activity.|She looked frazzled after a week of working late nights.|Synonyms: exhausted, worn out, stressed, drained, burnt out|Antonyms: refreshed, relaxed, energetic|adjective

frazzled:/ˈfræzəld/| v.|使疲惫不堪，使精疲力竭 (This verb form is less common)|To make someone exhausted and stressed.|The constant demands of the job frazzled him.|Synonyms: exhaust, wear out, stress, drain|Antonyms: refresh, relax, energize|verb
