mesocuneiform:/ˌmezoʊˈkjuːnɪfɔːrm/ | adj. n. | 中楔形文字的；中楔形文字 | Relating to or denoting a style of cuneiform writing intermediate between the archaic and the later Assyrian forms, characteristic of the Old Babylonian period. Also, a character or inscription in this script. |  This tablet is written in mesocuneiform script. 这块泥板是用中楔形文字书写的。| The mesocuneiform inscription detailed the king's victory. 这段中楔形文字铭文详细描述了国王的胜利。 | Synonym: Old Babylonian cuneiform (for the script) | Antonym:  Archaic cuneiform, Late Assyrian cuneiform | adjective, noun
