over-objects:/ˈoʊvərˌɒbdʒɪkts/ |n.| 过度物化; 过度关注物质 |  Excessive focus on material possessions; prioritizing objects over experiences, relationships, or personal growth. |  Consumerism can lead to over-objects, where people value things more than experiences. 消费主义会导致过度物化，人们更重视物质而不是体验。 | materialism, consumerism |  minimalism, asceticism | noun

over-object:/ˈoʊvərˌɒbdʒɪkt/ |v.| 过度物化; 过度关注物质 (作为动词较少使用) | To excessively focus on material possessions; to prioritize objects over experiences, relationships, or personal growth. | While less common as a verb, one could say they are "over-objecting" their children by showering them with gifts instead of quality time. 虽然作为动词不太常见，但可以说他们通过送孩子礼物而不是高质量的陪伴来“过度物化”他们的孩子。 |  materialize, prioritize objects |  simplify, value experiences | verb
