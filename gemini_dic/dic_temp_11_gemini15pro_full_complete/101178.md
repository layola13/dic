invigorating:/ɪnˈvɪɡəreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
令人精神焕发的；使人有活力的
Making one feel strong, healthy, and full of energy.
A brisk walk in the crisp morning air is invigorating. 在清爽的早晨空气中轻快地散步令人精神焕发。
The cold shower was invigorating. 冷水澡令人精神振奋。
近义词: refreshing, stimulating, energizing, revitalizing
反义词: tiring, exhausting, draining, weakening
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *invigoratus, past participle of invigorare (“to give vigor to”), from in- (“in, into”) + vigor (“vigor, strength”) + -are (infinitive suffix).
