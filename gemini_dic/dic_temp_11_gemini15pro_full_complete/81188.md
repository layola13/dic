grandiloquent: /ˌɡrændɪˈləʊkwənt/ | adj. | 夸夸其谈的，辞藻华丽的 | Using pompous or extravagant language; bombastic. |  He delivered a grandiloquent speech, full of long words and elaborate metaphors. 他发表了一篇辞藻华丽的演讲，充满了长句和复杂的比喻。

| Example: The mayor's grandiloquent pronouncements impressed no one. 市长夸夸其谈的声明没有给任何人留下深刻印象。 | Synonym: pompous, bombastic, verbose, florid | Antonym: simple, plain, concise, understated | word property: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin grandiloquus (“speaking loftily”), from grandis (“grand”) + loquor (“to speak”).
