dromi:/ˈdroʊmaɪ/
noun
跑鸟，奔鸟 (尤指鹤鸵)
A dromiid crab, also called a sponge crab, decorator crab, or furry crab.
Dromia personata is a species of crab found in the North Sea, Mediterranean Sea, and connecting parts of the northeastern Atlantic Ocean. It is known as the sponge crab, decorator crab or furry crab.
The dromi was camouflaged with algae. 这只跑鸟用藻类伪装自己。
近义词:crab, crustacean
反义词:(无明显的反义词)
词性:n.
