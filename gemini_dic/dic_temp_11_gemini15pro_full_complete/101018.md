invariably:/ɪnˈveəriəbli/ | adv. |总是；不变地；一定地 | always; without exception; constantly | He invariably arrives late for meetings. 他总是开会迟到。|  She invariably wears black. 她总是穿黑色的。 | always, constantly, consistently, habitually, regularly | rarely, seldom, sometimes, occasionally

词性: 副词

词源:  From Late Latin *invariabilis, from in- "not" + variabilis "changeable," from the verb variare "to change" (from PIE root *wer- (3) "to turn, bend").
