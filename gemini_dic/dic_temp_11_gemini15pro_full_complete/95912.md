increment:/ˈɪŋkrəmənt/ (US) /ˈɪŋkrɪmənt/ (UK)| n.|增加量，增量|an amount by which something increases|The increment in salary is significant. 薪水的增量很大。|synonyms: increase, rise, gain|antonyms: decrease, reduction, loss|noun

increment:/ˈɪŋkrəmənt/ (US) /ˈɪŋkrɪmənt/ (UK)| v.|增加，增大|to increase something by a particular amount|The company decided to increment wages annually. 公司决定每年增加工资。|synonyms: increase, augment, boost|antonyms: decrease, reduce, diminish|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Latin incrementum ‘growth, increase,’ from the verb increscere, from in- ‘in, into’ + crescere ‘grow.’
