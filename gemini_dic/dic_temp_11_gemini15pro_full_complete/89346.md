homologinic:/ˌhɒməlɒˈdʒɪnɪk/ | adj. | 同源的 | Relating to, or characterized by, homology.  Specifically referring to structures or features in different organisms that share a common evolutionary origin. |  The homological study revealed surprising similarities between the bat wing and the human hand.  蝙蝠翅膀和人手之间的同源性研究揭示了惊人的相似之处。

The homloginic relationship between these two genes suggests they diverged from a common ancestor. 这两个基因的同源关系表明它们是从一个共同的祖先进化而来的。 | 近义词: homologous, cognate | 反义词: analogous | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Greek *homologos* ("agreeing, of one accord") + -ic. *Homologos* is from *homo-* ("same") + *logos* ("speech, reason, proportion").
