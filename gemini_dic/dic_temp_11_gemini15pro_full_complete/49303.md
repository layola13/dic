deliciousnesses:/dɪˈlɪʃəsnəsɪz/
|n.|美味佳肴；令人愉快的事物|The meal was a succession of deliciousnesses. 这顿饭是一连串的美味佳肴。|近义词: delicacies, treats, delights|反义词: unpleasantness, awfulness|词性:名词

Etymology: From delicious + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives) + -es (plural suffix). Ultimately from Latin *deliciosus*, from *deliciae* "pleasures, delights".
