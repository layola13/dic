electrolyte: /ɪˈlɛktrəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 电解质 | A substance that conducts electricity when dissolved in water or melted, by forming ions. |  Sports drinks contain electrolytes to replace those lost through sweat. 运动饮料含有电解质，以补充因汗液流失的电解质。| synonym: ionic conductor | antonym: non-electrolyte | noun

electrolyte: /ɪˈlɛktrəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 电解液 | A conducting medium in which the flow of current is accompanied by the movement of matter in the form of ions. | The battery's electrolyte needs to be checked regularly. 电池的电解液需要定期检查。| synonym: electrolytic solution | antonym: dielectric | noun
