bioluminescent:/ˌbaɪoʊˌluːmɪˈnɛsənt/
|adj.|生物发光的|Producing or emitting light as a result of chemical reactions within the organism.|Fireflies are bioluminescent insects. 萤火虫是生物发光的昆虫.|The deep sea is home to many bioluminescent creatures. 深海是许多生物发光生物的家园.|近义词：light-emitting, phosphorescent|反义词：non-luminescent|词性:形容词

bioluminescence:/ˌbaɪoʊˌluːmɪˈnɛsəns/
|n.|生物发光|The production and emission of light by a living organism as a result of a chemical reaction.|The bioluminescence of the jellyfish illuminated the dark water. 水母的生物发光照亮了黑暗的水域.|Scientists are studying the bioluminescence of certain fungi. 科学家正在研究某些真菌的生物发光现象。|近义词：phosphorescence|反义词：non-luminescence|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- + luminescent.  "Bio-" comes from the Greek "bios" meaning "life." "Luminescent" comes from the Latin "lumen" meaning "light."
