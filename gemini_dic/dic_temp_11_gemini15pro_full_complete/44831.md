crustacean:/krʌˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 甲壳类动物 |An arthropod of the large, mainly aquatic group Crustacea, such as a crab, lobster, shrimp, or barnacle.  They are characterized by a hard, external exoskeleton, segmented bodies, and paired, jointed appendages.| Lobsters are crustaceans. 龙虾是甲壳类动物。 | Crabs, shrimps, prawns | insect, arachnid (not direct opposites, but different arthropod groups) | noun
