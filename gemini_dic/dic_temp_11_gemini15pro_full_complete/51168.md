despumate:/diːˈspjuːmeɪt/ /dɪˈspjuːmeɪt/
verb
去除泡沫；撇去浮沫
To remove froth or foam from (a liquid).
The winemaker despumated the must before fermentation.  酿酒师在发酵前去除了葡萄汁的泡沫。
The chef despumated the stock to create a clear consommé.  厨师撇去高汤上的浮沫，做出清澈的 consommé。
synonyms: skim, clarify, purify
antonyms: aerate, froth, foam
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *despumare, from de- (away from) + spuma (foam).
