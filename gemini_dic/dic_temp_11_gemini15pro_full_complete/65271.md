eucharistically:/ˌjuːkərɪˈstɪkəli/ | adv. | 圣餐式地，在圣餐方面 |  In a manner relating to or characteristic of the Eucharist or Holy Communion. |  The bread and wine were eucharistically offered.  面包和葡萄酒以圣餐的方式被献上。| 近义词: sacramentally | 反义词: profanely | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *eucharisticus + -ly.  Late Latin *eucharisticus is from Greek εὐχαριστικός (eukharistikos, “of thanksgiving”), from εὐχαριστέω (eukharisteō, “I give thanks”), from εὖ (eu, “well”) + χάρις (kharis, “grace, favor”).
