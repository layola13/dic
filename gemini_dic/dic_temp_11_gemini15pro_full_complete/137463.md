nontinted: /ˌnɒnˈtɪntɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈtɪntɪd/
adjective
未着色的，无色的
Not having been tinted; clear or uncolored.
例句：She prefers nontinted moisturizer for a natural look. 她更喜欢无色润肤霜，以获得自然妆容。

例句：The windows of the car were nontinted, offering a clear view of the surroundings.  这辆车的车窗没有着色，可以清楚地看到周围的环境。

近义词：clear, uncolored, untinted, plain
反义词：tinted, colored, dyed
词性：adjective
