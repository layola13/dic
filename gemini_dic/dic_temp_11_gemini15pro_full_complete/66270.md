exclusivity:/ˌɛkskluːˈsɪvɪti/
n.
排他性，独占性，专有性
The state of being exclusive; limited to a particular person, group, or area.
The club offers its members exclusivity and a sense of belonging.  这个俱乐部为其会员提供专属感和归属感。

The restaurant is known for its exclusivity and high prices.  这家餐厅以其高档和昂贵的价格而闻名。

近义词:sole possession, monopoly, privateness
反义词:inclusivity, openness, accessibility
词性:noun


exclusiveness:/ˌɛksˈkluːsɪvnəs/
n.
排他性，独占性，专有性 (与 exclusivity 同义，但略微强调排外的性质)
The quality of being exclusive.
The exclusiveness of the club made it difficult to join.  这家俱乐部的排他性使其难以加入。

The brand's exclusiveness is part of its appeal.  该品牌的排他性是其吸引力的一部分。

近义词:selectiveness, eliteness
反义词:inclusiveness, democracy
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exclusivitas, from Latin exclusus, past participle of excludere "to shut out," from ex- "out" + claudere "to shut."
