mayo:/ˈmeɪoʊ/ (US), /ˈmeɪəʊ/ (UK)| n. |蛋黄酱；美乃滋|A creamy condiment made of oil, egg yolks, and vinegar or lemon juice.|例句：I like to put mayo on my sandwiches. 我喜欢在三明治里放蛋黄酱。

例句：He ordered a burger with extra mayo. 他点了一个加蛋黄酱的汉堡。|近义词：mayonnaise|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
