non-definitive:/ˌnɒn dɪˈfɪnɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非最终的，非决定性的，非限定的 | Not final or conclusive; not definitively settling a question. | The test results are non-definitive, further investigation is needed.  测试结果并非最终结果，需要进一步调查。| The doctor gave a non-definitive diagnosis, saying more tests were required. 医生给出了一个非最终的诊断，说需要做更多的检查。 | 近义词: provisional, tentative, inconclusive, preliminary | 反义词: definitive, conclusive, final, decisive | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "definitive" (from Latin "definitivus," meaning "serving to limit or define").
