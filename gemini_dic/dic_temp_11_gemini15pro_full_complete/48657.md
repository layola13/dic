defensible:/dɪˈfɛnsəbl/| adj.|可辩护的，可防御的，合理的|Capable of being defended, justified, or protected.|例句：His actions were completely defensible under the circumstances. 在这种情况下，他的行为完全可以辩护。|例句：The city built a defensible wall around its perimeter. 这座城市在其周边建造了一道可防御的城墙。|近义词：justifiable, reasonable, supportable, maintainable|反义词：indefensible, unjustifiable, unreasonable, untenable|词性:形容词
defensible:/dɪˈfɛnsəbl/| adj.|可辩护的，可防御的，合理的|From Late Middle English *defensible, from Old French defensible, from defender (to defend) + -ible (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being"). Ultimately from Latin dēfendere (to ward off, defend).|词源
