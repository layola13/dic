journalistically:/ˌdʒɜːrnəˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 以新闻工作者的方式；从新闻角度 | In a way that relates to the work of journalists or the practice of journalism. | The event was reported journalistically, focusing on facts and figures.  该事件以新闻的方式报道，重点关注事实和数据。 | The incident was documented journalistically.  这起事件以新闻工作者的方式被记录下来。 | 近义词: objectively, factually, reportorially | 反义词: subjectively, biasedly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From journalist + -ically.  Ultimately from French *journaliste*.
