emollient:/ɪˈmɒliənt/ /ɪˈmɑːliənt/| n. adj.|润肤剂，润肤的|A preparation that softens the skin.|Apply an emollient cream to soothe dry skin.  涂抹润肤霜来舒缓干燥的皮肤。|Synonyms: moisturizer, softener, balm|Antonyms: irritant, desiccant|noun

emollient:/ɪˈmɒliənt/ /ɪˈmɑːliənt/| adj.|润肤的，缓和的|Having the quality of softening or soothing the skin.|The emollient properties of aloe vera are well known.  芦荟的润肤特性是众所周知的。|Synonyms: softening, soothing, calming|Antonyms: irritating, harsh, abrasive|adjective
