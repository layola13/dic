devote:/dɪˈvoʊt/| vt.| 致力于，奉献 |to give all or a large part of one's time or resources to (a person, activity, or cause).|例句：She devoted her life to helping others. 她一生致力于帮助他人。|例句：He devoted his energies to the project. 他把精力都投入到这个项目中。|近义词：dedicate, commit, allocate|反义词：neglect, abandon|词性：动词
Etymology: From Late Latin *dēvōtāre, from Latin dēvōvēre (“to vow; dedicate, consecrate”), from dē (“down, from”) + vōvēre (“to vow”).
