biddings:/ˈbɪdɪŋz/
n.
投标，出价，吩咐，命令
the act of offering a price for something, especially at an auction; commands or instructions
The bidding was fierce at the auction for the antique vase.  古董花瓶的拍卖会上，竞价非常激烈。
synonyms: offers, tenders, proposals, commands, instructions
antonyms: requests, pleas, inquiries
