hymenophore:/haɪˈmɛnəfɔːr/
n.
子实层体，菌膜
The part of a fungal fruiting body that bears the hymenium (the spore-bearing surface).
The gills of a mushroom are a type of hymenophore.  蘑菇的菌褶是一种子实层体。
The hymenophore of this species is porous rather than gilled.  这个物种的子实层体是多孔的而不是菌褶状的。
synonym:sporophore
antonym: (No direct antonym exists as it's a specific anatomical term)
