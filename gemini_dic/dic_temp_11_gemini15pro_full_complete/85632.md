haunch:/hɔːntʃ/ | n. | 腰臀部；(四足动物的)后腿及臀部；(拱形结构的)侧翼，拱肩|The dog sat back on its haunches. 狗蹲坐在它的后腿上。|例句：The cat sat on its haunches, watching the birds. 猫蹲坐在它的后腿上，观察着鸟儿。|例句：The architect admired the haunches of the bridge. 建筑师欣赏着桥梁的拱肩。|近义词：rump, hindquarters, flank|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *hanche, from Frankish *hanka (“hip”), from Proto-Germanic *hankō (“hip, haunch”). Cognate with Middle Dutch hanke (“hip”), Old High German hanka (“hip, haunch”), German Hanke (“haunch, joint of meat”), Old English hēanca (“hip”).
