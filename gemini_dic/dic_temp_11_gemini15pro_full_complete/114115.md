magot:/ˈmægət/ | n. | 猕猴；巴巴利猕猴；守财奴 | A tailless monkey (Macaca sylvanus) of northwestern Africa, also called Barbary ape. A person who hoards money. |  The magot is found in the Atlas Mountains of Morocco and Algeria. 猕猴分布在摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚的阿特拉斯山脉。| 近义词: Barbary ape, miser, skinflint | 反义词: spendthrift | 词性: noun

magot:/ˈmægət/ | n. | 钱；财富 (古语，现已罕用) | Money; wealth (archaic). |  He had amassed a considerable magot over the years. 他多年来积攒了大量的钱财。(古语用法) | 近义词: money, wealth, fortune | 反义词: debt, poverty | 词性: noun
