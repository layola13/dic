druse:/druːs/
n.
晶簇；晶腺
A group of crystals of the same mineral, especially quartz, that project from a surface.
The geologist discovered a beautiful druse of amethyst in the cave.  地质学家在山洞里发现了一簇美丽的紫水晶晶簇。

近义词:cluster, geode
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

druse:/druːz/
n.
(尤指眼球后部的)玻璃疣
A small, hard deposit, especially of calcium salts, formed on a surface such as the back of the eyeball.
The ophthalmologist diagnosed the patient with drusen in the macula. 眼科医生诊断病人黄斑区有玻璃疣。

近义词:deposit
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
