goodfellow:/ˈɡʊdˌfeləʊ/ (UK) /ˈɡʊdˌfeloʊ/ (US)| n.| 好友，好伙伴|A convivial and sociable person; a boon companion.|He was a goodfellow and always the life of the party. 他是个好伙伴，总是聚会的活跃分子。|近义词：companion, friend, pal, chum|反义词:enemy, foe|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *godefelawe*, from *god* (“good”) + *felawe* (“fellow”).
