lectures:/ˈlɛktʃərz/ (US), /ˈlɛktʃəz/ (UK)| n.|讲座，演讲；教训，训斥|A lecture is a formal talk on a serious subject given to a group of people, especially students. Lectures can also refer to a long, angry talk in which someone criticizes another person's behaviour.|The professor's lectures on quantum physics were fascinating. 教授关于量子物理学的讲座引人入胜。|synonyms: talks, addresses, speeches, sermons, presentations|antonyms: discussions, conversations|noun
