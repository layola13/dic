bumf:/bʌmf/
n.
废纸，无用文件
useless or tedious printed documents or other papers
The drawer was full of bumf.  抽屉里塞满了废纸。
近义词:paperwork, documents, rubbish, junk
反义词:important documents, valuable information
词性:名词
