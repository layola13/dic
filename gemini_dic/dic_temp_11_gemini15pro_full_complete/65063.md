eternally:/ɪˈtɜːnəli/ /ɪˈtɝːnəli/
adverb|永远地，永恒地|In a way that lasts forever. |例句：He promised to love her eternally. 他承诺永远爱她。|近义词：forever, perpetually, always, everlastingly|反义词:temporarily, briefly, fleetingly|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *eternalliche, equivalent to eternal +‎ -ly.
