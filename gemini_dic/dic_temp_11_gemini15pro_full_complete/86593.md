heliographic:/ˌhiːliəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|日射的，日光照相的| Relating to the sun; measured or observed in relation to the sun.  Specifically, relating to the study of the sun's surface features. |The heliographic coordinates of the sunspot were carefully recorded. 太阳黑子的日面坐标被仔细记录下来了。|Near synonyms: solar, heliospheric |Antonyms: N/A | adjective

heliographic:/ˌhiːliəˈɡræfɪk/| n.|日光反射信号|A signal transmitted by reflecting sunlight with a mirror (a heliograph).|The heliograph was crucial for communication across long distances in the 19th century. 日光反射信号在19世纪对于远距离通讯至关重要。|Near synonyms:  heliogram, sun-signal |Antonyms: N/A |noun
