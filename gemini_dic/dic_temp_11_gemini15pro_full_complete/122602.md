modernization:/ˌmɒdənaɪˈzeɪʃn/ (British) /ˌmɑːdərnaɪˈzeɪʃn/ (American)| n. |现代化|The process of adapting something to modern needs or standards, typically by installing or using the latest technologies or equipment.|例句：The modernization of the factory increased productivity. 工厂的现代化提高了生产力。

例句：The modernization of the education system is a long-term project. 教育系统的现代化是一个长期项目。

近义词：development, progress, innovation, advancement, upgrade|反义词：stagnation, regression, decline, obsolescence|词性: noun
