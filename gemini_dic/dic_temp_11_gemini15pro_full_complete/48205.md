deconfliction:/ˌdiːkənˈflɪkʃən/ | n. |冲突消除，冲突避免|The process of resolving or mitigating potential conflicts or overlaps between different activities or operations, often in a military context, to ensure safety and efficiency.| The air traffic control system plays a vital role in the deconfliction of flight paths.空中交通管制系统在避免航线冲突方面起着至关重要的作用。|  Deconfliction efforts are crucial to prevent accidental clashes between friendly forces. 冲突消除工作对于防止友军之间的意外冲突至关重要。| synonyms: coordination, harmonization, integration | antonyms: conflict, clash, interference | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
"Deconfliction" is a relatively recent word, formed by adding the prefix "de-" (meaning "removal" or "reversal") to "conflict."  "Conflict" itself comes from the Latin word "conflictus," meaning "a striking together."  The use of "deconfliction" became prominent in military and aviation contexts, where preventing unintended clashes is crucial.
