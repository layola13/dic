numerological:/ˌn(j)uːmərəˈlɒdʒɪk(ə)l/ /ˌnuːmərəˈlɑːdʒɪk(ə)l/
adjective
数字命理学的
Relating to numerology, the study of the occult significance of numbers.
Numerology is an ancient practice that assigns symbolic meanings to numbers.
数字命理学是一种古老的做法，它赋予数字象征意义。
She had a numerological chart made to determine her lucky number.
她制作了一张数字命理学图表来确定她的幸运数字。
近义词:numeric, numeral
反义词:None
词性:adjective
