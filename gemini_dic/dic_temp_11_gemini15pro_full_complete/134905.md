nonmurderous:/ˌnɒnˈmɜːdəɹəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɜːrdərəs/| adj.|非谋杀的，不杀人的|Not involving or characterized by murder; not likely to murder.|The king issued a nonmurderous decree, focusing on rehabilitation instead of execution. 国王颁布了一项非谋杀的法令，重点是改造而不是处决。| The protest was nonmurderous; no one was killed or seriously injured. 抗议活动是非暴力的；没有人被杀或受重伤。|近义词：nonlethal, harmless,  peaceful|反义词：murderous, lethal, violent|词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non < PIE *ne "not"
murderous (adjective): from murder + -ous. Murder from Old English morþor "secret killing, slaying," of uncertain origin.
