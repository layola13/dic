gazebo:/ɡəˈziːboʊ/ (US), /ɡəˈziːbəʊ/ (UK)| n. |凉亭；观景亭|A freestanding, roofed, open-sided structure, often hexagonal or octagonal, providing shade and a place to relax in a garden or park.|We sat in the gazebo, enjoying the view of the lake. 我们坐在凉亭里，欣赏着湖景。|
The gazebo offered welcome shade from the midday sun. 凉亭提供了正午阳光下的荫凉。|近义词：pavilion, pergola, summerhouse|反义词: (No direct antonym exists.  One might consider enclosed structures like a shed or house as conceptually opposite.)|noun
