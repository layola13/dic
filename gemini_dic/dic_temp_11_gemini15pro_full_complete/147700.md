overprecisenesses:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈsaɪsnəsɪz/
|n.| 过于精确；过度严谨|The state or quality of being excessively precise.|His overprecisenesses in language sometimes made him sound pedantic. 他在语言上的过度严谨有时让他听起来像个书呆子。|近义词：meticulousness, fastidiousness, punctiliousness|反义词：sloppiness, carelessness|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + preciseness + -es (plural suffix).  "Preciseness" itself comes from "precise" which has its roots in the Latin "praecīsus," meaning "cut off in front," implying a sharp, clear definition.  The prefix "over-" indicates an excess or surplus.
