cosa:/ˈkoʊsə/ (US), /ˈkɔːzə/ (UK)| n.| 东西；事情；家伙|Thing; something; whatchamacallit (informal).  It's derived from Spanish and Italian.|例句：¿Qué es esa cosa? (Spanish) = What is that thing? 那是什么东西？

例句：Che cosa vuoi? (Italian) = What do you want? 你想要什么？|近义词：thing, object, item, stuff, whatchamacallit, doodad, thingy|反义词: Nothing (conceptually, not a direct opposite)|Noun
