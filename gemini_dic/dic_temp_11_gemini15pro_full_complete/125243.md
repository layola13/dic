multidialectalism:/ˌmʌltiˈdaɪəˌlɛktəlɪzəm/ | n. |多方言性，多方言使用|The presence or use of multiple dialects within a specific region or community.  The study or practice of using or promoting multiple dialects. |Her research focuses on multidialectalism in urban areas. 她的研究重点是城市地区的多方言现象。|Synonyms: multilingualism, polydialectalism, linguistic diversity|Antonyms: monolingualism, dialect leveling|noun
