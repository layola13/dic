indecisive:/ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪv/| adj.|优柔寡断的，犹豫不决的|Unable to make decisions quickly and effectively.|She was indecisive about which dress to buy. 她犹豫不决，不知道该买哪条裙子。|近义词：hesitant, wavering, ambivalent|反义词：decisive, resolute, firm|词性:形容词
Etymology: From in- (not) + decisive (from Latin decidere, to decide).
