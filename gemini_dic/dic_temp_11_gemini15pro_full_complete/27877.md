cans:/kænz/
n.|罐，罐头|A sealed metal container, usually cylindrical, holding food, drink, or other goods. |Example: I bought three cans of soup. 我买了三罐汤。|
近义词:tin, container|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) bulk, unpackaged|词性:名词

cans:/kænz/
v.|能，会 (can的第三人称单数形式)|Third person singular present tense form of "can," indicating ability or possibility. |Example: She cans peaches every summer. 她每年夏天都把桃子做成罐头。|近义词: is able to, is capable of|反义词: cannot, can't|词性:动词
