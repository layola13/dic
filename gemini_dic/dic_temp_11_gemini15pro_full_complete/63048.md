enriches:/ɪnˈrɪtʃɪz/
verb
使丰富，使富裕，充实，加料于
Makes something richer or more valuable, either literally or figuratively.
Traveling enriches the mind. 旅行使心灵丰富。
enhance, improve, augment
deplete, impoverish, diminish
词性:动词
