disaggregate:/dɪsˈæɡrɪɡeɪt/
v.|分解，分离，分散|To separate something into its constituent parts; to break up a mass into smaller parts.|The data was disaggregated to show the results for each region. 数据被分解以显示每个地区的结果。|例句：The report disaggregates the data by gender and age.  报告按性别和年龄对数据进行了分类。

例句：The company decided to disaggregate its business units to improve efficiency.  公司决定将其业务部门分开以提高效率。


近义词：separate, break down, disperse, analyze
反义词：aggregate, combine, unite
词性:动词
