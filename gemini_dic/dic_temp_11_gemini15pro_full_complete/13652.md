attorneys:/əˈtɜːr.niz/
n.
律师（尤指代表当事人出庭的律师）
A person legally appointed or empowered to act for another.  Attorneys are legally qualified professionals who represent clients in legal matters.
The attorneys for the defense presented their closing arguments.  辩方律师提出了他们的总结陈词。
近义词:lawyers, counsels, solicitors, barristers
反义词:defendant, client
词性:名词(plural)


attorney:/əˈtɜːr.ni/
n.
律师（尤指代表当事人出庭的律师）
A person legally appointed or empowered to act for another. An attorney is a legally qualified professional who represents clients in legal matters.
My attorney advised me to plead not guilty. 我的律师建议我作无罪抗辩。
近义词:lawyer, counsel, solicitor, barrister
反义词:defendant, client
词性:名词(singular)


词源:
From Old French atorne (“appointed, assigned”), past participle of atorner.  Ultimately from Latin ad- + tornare (“to turn”).
