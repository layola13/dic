amphiarthrodial:/ˌæmfiˌɑːrˈθroʊdiəl/
adjective
微动的，半关节的
Allowing slight movement, as in a cartilaginous joint.
The pubic symphysis is an amphiarthrodial joint.  耻骨联合是一个微动关节。
近义词: slightly movable
反义词: synarthrodial, diarthrodial
词性: adjective

词源:
amphi- (Greek) = both, on both sides, around
arthro- (Greek) = joint
-dial (Greek) = pertaining to
