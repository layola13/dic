anti-physical:/ˌæntiˈfɪzɪkəl/
adjective
反物质的；非物质的；精神上的
Opposed to or rejecting the physical or material.  Existing outside the realm of the physical.
例句：His interests were primarily anti-physical, focused on philosophy and spirituality.  他主要关注非物质的东西，专注于哲学和精神层面。

例句：The guru advocated for an anti-physical lifestyle, emphasizing meditation over physical exertion.  这位大师提倡一种非物质的生活方式，强调冥想胜过体力活动。

近义词：spiritual, metaphysical, immaterial, non-physical
反义词：physical, material, corporeal
