allotriophagy:/əˌlɒtriˈɒfədʒi/
n.
异食癖，吞食异物
The consumption of substances not normally eaten, such as clay, chalk, or dirt.
He exhibited allotriophagy, consuming inedible objects like paper and plastic. 他表现出异食癖，吞食纸和塑料等不可食用的物品。
