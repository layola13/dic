helianthoidea:/ˌhiːliˌænˈθɔɪdiə/ | n. | 向日葵族; 菊科向日葵族 | A large tribe of composite plants that includes sunflowers, asters, and daisies. | Sunflowers belong to the Helianthoidea tribe. 向日葵属于向日葵族。| The Helianthoidea tribe is known for its vibrant flowers. 向日葵族以其鲜艳的花朵而闻名。 | synonyms: Asteraceae (family), Compositae (family), Heliantheae (tribe - sometimes considered equivalent) | antonyms: N/A | noun
