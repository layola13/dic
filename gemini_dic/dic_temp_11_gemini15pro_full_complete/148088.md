overscepticisms:/ˌoʊvərˈskɛptɪsɪzəmz/
noun
过度怀疑；过分怀疑
The tendency to be excessively skeptical or distrustful.  It refers to an extreme or exaggerated form of skepticism, where doubt extends beyond reasonable bounds.
His overscepticisms prevented him from embracing new ideas.  他过度的怀疑使他无法接受新想法。
Near Synonyms:  hyper-skepticism, excessive doubt, cynicism, distrust
Antonyms:  gullibility, naivety, credulity
