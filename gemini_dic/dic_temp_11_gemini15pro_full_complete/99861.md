intermountain:/ˌɪntərˈmaʊntən/| adj.|山间的，群山之间的|Situated between mountains; located in a region surrounded by mountains. |The intermountain region is known for its dry climate.  这个山间地区以其干燥的气候而闻名。|  The intermountain basin is rich in minerals. 这个山间盆地矿产丰富。|近义词：mountainous, upland|反义词: lowland, coastal|词性: adjective
Etymology: inter- (Latin, meaning "between") + mountain
