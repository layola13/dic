deluge:/ˈdɛljuːdʒ/ (US), /ˈdɛljuːdʒ/ (UK)| n. vt.|洪水；暴雨；泛滥；大量；涌现|A severe flood; a heavy downpour; an overwhelming amount or number of something.|The town was deluged by floodwaters after the hurricane.  飓风过后，小镇被洪水淹没。|近义词：flood, inundation, torrent, avalanche, barrage|反义词：drought, trickle|noun
deluge:/dɪˈluːdʒ/ (US), /dɪˈluːdʒ/ (UK)| vt.|使泛滥；涌现；淹没；压倒|To flood or overwhelm with a large amount of something.|We were deluged with emails after the announcement.  公告发布后，我们的邮箱被邮件淹没了。|近义词：flood, overwhelm, inundate, swamp|反义词：drain, dry|verb
