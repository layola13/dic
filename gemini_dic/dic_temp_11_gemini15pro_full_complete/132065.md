noncoverage:/ˌnɒnˈkʌvərɪdʒ/ | n. |未覆盖，缺乏保险|The lack of insurance or protection against a particular risk or event.  A situation where something is not covered by insurance, a service, or a law.|例句：The company's noncoverage of pre-existing conditions caused problems for many employees.  这家公司对既往病不保，给许多员工带来了麻烦。

例句：There is a significant noncoverage of dental care in this basic health plan.  这个基本医疗计划明显缺乏牙科保健服务。|近义词：lack of coverage, exclusion, omission|反义词:coverage, inclusion|词性:名词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + coverage (from "cover" - to extend over)
