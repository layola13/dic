abrasiveness:/əˈbreɪsɪvnəs/
n.|粗糙; 摩擦性; 伤感情; 生硬.|The quality of being rough or harsh, causing friction or irritation, often in interpersonal interactions.|Her abrasiveness made it difficult for her to make friends. 她的粗鲁让她很难交到朋友。|近义词：harshness, roughness, rudeness, coarseness, gruffness|反义词：smoothness, gentleness, politeness, kindness, tact|词性:noun

abrasive:/əˈbreɪsɪv/
adj.|粗糙的; 摩擦的; 伤感情的; 生硬的.|Tending to cause friction or irritation; harsh or rough.|His abrasive personality made him unpopular. 他粗暴的性格使他不受欢迎。|近义词：harsh, rough, rude, coarse, gruff|反义词：smooth, gentle, polite, kind, tactful|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abrasus*, past participle of *abradere* "to scrape off," from *ab* "off, away from" + *radere* "to scrape."  The noun "abrasiveness" is derived from the adjective "abrasive."
