bam:/bæm/| interj. n.|砰，突然出现|A sudden loud noise, especially of an impact or explosion.  Also used as an exclamation to emphasize a sudden action or event.|The door slammed shut with a bam. 门砰地一声关上了。|近义词：bang, crash, wham|反义词: (no clear antonym for a sound) |词性: interjection, noun

bam:/bæm/| n.|欺骗，诡计|An act of deception or trickery. (informal)|He pulled a bam on his friends and made them think he'd won the lottery. 他骗了他的朋友们，让他们以为他中了彩票。|近义词：hoax, scam, trick|反义词: honesty, truthfulness |词性: noun
