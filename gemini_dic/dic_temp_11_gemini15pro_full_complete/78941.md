girderless:/ˈɡɜːrdərlɪs/| adj.|无梁的，无大梁的|Lacking girders; constructed without the use of girders.|The girderless concrete roof spanned the entire stadium.  无梁混凝土屋顶覆盖了整个体育场。|近义词：beam-free, unsupported (in certain contexts)|反义词: girdered|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"girder" (from Old North French "girdre" - to gird, encircle) + "-less" (Old English "lēas" - devoid of, without).  Essentially meaning "without girders".
