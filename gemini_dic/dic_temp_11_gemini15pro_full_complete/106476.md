kpg:/ˈkeɪpiːdʒiː/| abbreviation | Key Performance Goals (关键绩效目标) | Key Performance Goals are the specific targets that individuals or organizations aim to achieve within a certain timeframe to measure progress towards overall objectives. |  Example: The sales team's KPG for the quarter is to increase revenue by 15%.  该销售团队本季度的关键绩效目标是将收入提高 15%。

Example:  Management set ambitious KPGs for the new product launch. 管理层为新产品发布设定了雄心勃勃的关键绩效目标。 | 近义词: KPI (Key Performance Indicator), objective, target, goal | 反义词: N/A | noun (缩写)
