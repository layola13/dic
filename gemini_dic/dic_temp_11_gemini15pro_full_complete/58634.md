dumbed:/dʌmd/
|v. (past participle of "dumb")|使变哑；使变沉默；简化；降低…的智力水平|Simplified or made less intelligent.  This often implies a deliberate action to make something easier to understand, sometimes at the cost of accuracy or depth.|The textbook dumbed down the complex scientific concepts so that even young children could grasp them. 这本教科书简化了复杂的科学概念，以便即使是幼儿也能掌握。|simplified, watered down, oversimplified|complexified, elaborated|verb
