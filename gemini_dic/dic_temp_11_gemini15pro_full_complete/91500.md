hymning: /ˈhɪmɪŋ/
n.
赞美诗; 赞歌; 赞美
The act of singing hymns or praises.  A song or ode of praise.
The congregation joined in the hymning of "Amazing Grace."  会众一起唱起了赞美诗《奇异恩典》。

例句：
The hymning of the choir filled the cathedral with beautiful music.  唱诗班的赞歌使大教堂充满了优美的音乐。

The hymning of the birds at dawn is a joyful sound.  黎明时分鸟儿的歌唱是令人愉悦的声音。


近义词：praising, lauding, extolling, celebrating
反义词：criticizing, condemning, denouncing
词性：noun (名词)
