expenditure:/ɪkˈspendɪtʃər/ /ɪkˈspendɪtʃʊr/| n.|支出，花费|The government's expenditure on education has increased. 政府的教育支出增加了。|例句：The project required a large expenditure of time and money.  这个项目需要大量的时间和金钱的支出。

例句：The family's monthly expenditure on food is about $500.  这个家庭每月在食物上的花费大约是500美元。|近义词：spending, outlay, cost, expense|反义词:income, revenue|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: expending, from Old French expendre, from Latin expendere ‘weigh out, pay out’, from ex- ‘out’ + pendere ‘weigh’.
