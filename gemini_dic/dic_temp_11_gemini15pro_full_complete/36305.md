cohesionless:/koʊˈhiːʒən.ləs/
adjective
无凝聚力的；不粘结的
Lacking cohesion; unable to stick together.
The cohesionless soil is prone to landslides.  无凝聚力的土壤容易发生山体滑坡。
Dry sand is a cohesionless material. 干沙是一种无凝聚力的材料。
Synonyms: loose, unconsolidated, friable, non-cohesive
Antonyms: cohesive, consolidated, compact, cemented
Etymology:  From co- + hes- (variant of haer- to stick) + -ion + -less.  Cohesion from Latin cohaesionem, meaning "a sticking together."
