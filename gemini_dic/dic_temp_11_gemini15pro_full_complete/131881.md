noncontemplative:/ˌnɒnkənˈtemplətɪv/ | adj. | 非沉思的，不冥想的 | Not given to or involving contemplation or meditation; not meditative. |  She led a busy, noncontemplative life, always rushing from one task to the next. 她过着忙碌而不沉思的生活，总是匆匆忙忙地从一项任务赶到下一项任务。|  He preferred noncontemplative activities like sports and socializing. 他更喜欢运动和社交等非沉思性活动。 | 近义词：active, practical, unreflective | 反义词：contemplative, meditative, reflective | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + contemplative.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "contemplative" comes from the Late Latin "contemplativus," from "contemplari" meaning "to gaze attentively, observe, contemplate."
