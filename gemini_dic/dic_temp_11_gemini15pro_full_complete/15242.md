backfriend: /ˈbækfrɛnd/ | n. | 背后朋友；暗中支持者；损友 | A person who appears friendly but acts against one's interests behind their back; a false friend. |  He thought he was being helpful, but he turned out to be a backfriend, spreading rumors about him. 他以为他在帮忙，结果却是一个背后朋友，散布关于他的谣言。| synonyms: false friend, two-faced friend, betrayer, snake in the grass, Judas | antonyms: true friend, loyal friend, confidant | noun
