confessingly:/kənˈfɛsɪŋli/
| adv. |坦白地，承认地|Admitting something, typically something that is embarrassing or shameful.|Confessingly, I had no idea what I was doing. 坦白说，我不知道自己在做什么。|Confessingly, I ate the whole cake. 坦白说，我把整个蛋糕都吃了。|Synonym: admittedly, frankly, openly|Antonym: secretly, covertly|Word type: adverb

confess:/kənˈfɛs/
| v. |坦白，承认；忏悔|Admit or state that one has committed a crime or sin.|He confessed to stealing the money. 他承认偷了钱。|She confessed her sins to the priest. 她向神父忏悔了她的罪过。|Synonym: admit, acknowledge, own up|Antonym: deny, conceal|Word type: verb
