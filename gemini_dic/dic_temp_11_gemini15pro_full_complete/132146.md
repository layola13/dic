noncultured:/nɒnˈkʌltʃəd/ /nɑːnˈkʌltʃərd/
adjective
未受文化熏陶的，没有教养的
Lacking culture or refinement; uncultured.
例句：His noncultured table manners embarrassed his date. 他的粗鲁的餐桌礼仪让他的约会对象感到尴尬。

例句：She found his noncultured jokes offensive. 她觉得他没教养的笑话很冒犯人.

近义词：uncultured, unrefined, philistine, boorish, coarse
反义词：cultured, refined, sophisticated, polished
词性：adjective
