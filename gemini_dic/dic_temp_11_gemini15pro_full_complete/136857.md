nonsister:/ˌnɒnˈsɪstər/ | n. | 非姐妹染色单体 |  One of a pair of homologous chromosomes, not formed by DNA replication.  A nonsister chromatid is a chromatid from a homologous chromosome that is not a sister chromatid. In other words, a nonsister chromatid is either of the two chromatids of paired homologous chromosomes. | During meiosis, crossing over occurs between nonsister chromatids. 在减数分裂期间，非姐妹染色单体之间发生交叉。|  homologous chromosome | sister chromatid | noun
