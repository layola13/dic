insufficiently:/ˌɪnsəˈfɪʃəntli/
adverb
不足地，不够地
not enough; inadequately.
The wound was insufficiently cleaned, leading to an infection.  伤口清洁得不够彻底，导致感染。
The project was insufficiently funded, causing delays.  该项目资金不足，导致延误。
Synonyms: inadequately, deficiently, poorly, improperly
Antonyms: sufficiently, adequately, properly, completely
词性: 副词
