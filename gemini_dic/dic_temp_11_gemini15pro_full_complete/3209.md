affecters:/əˈfɛktərz/
| n. |影响者；感染者|Those who affect or influence something or someone.  Those who are affected or infected by something.|例句：The affecters of change in the organization were the new CEOs.  这个组织中影响变革的是新任首席执行官们。

例句：The affecters of the disease were quarantined.  这种疾病的感染者被隔离了。|近义词：influencers, agents,  patients, sufferers|反义词:unaffected (adj.)|词性:Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "affect" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Affect" comes from the Latin "afficere," meaning "to act on," "to influence."
