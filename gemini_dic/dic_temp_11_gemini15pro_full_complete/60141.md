effectualities:/ɪˌfɛktʃuˈælɪtiz/| n. |有效性；实际结果；实力；效力|The effectualities of the new policy are yet to be seen. 新政策的有效性还有待观察。|例句：The general stressed the importance of military effectualities in the field. 将军强调了战场上军事实力的重要性。

例句：We need to assess the effectualities of different marketing strategies. 我们需要评估不同营销策略的有效性。|近义词：effectiveness, efficacy, potency, strength|反义词：ineffectiveness, weakness, impotence|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin effectualitas, from effectu(al) (see effectual).
