drizzlingly:/ˈdrɪzəlɪŋli/
adverb
细雨蒙蒙地；毛毛雨般地
in a way that resembles a drizzle; lightly and steadily
The rain was drizzlingly falling, creating a mystical atmosphere. 雨细雨蒙蒙地下着，营造出一种神秘的气氛。
It was drizzlingly cold outside. 外面下着毛毛雨，很冷。
近义词: lightly, gently, softly
反义词: heavily, torrentially
词性: adverb


词源: From drizzle + -ingly.  Drizzle itself comes from Old English *drisle, frequentative of drosen ("to fall in drops").
