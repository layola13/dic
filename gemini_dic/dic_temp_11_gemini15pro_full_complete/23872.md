break-back:/ˈbreɪkˌbæk/ | adj. | 极其费力的，累断腰的 |  (of manual labor) requiring great physical exertion, exhausting. break-back:/ˈbreɪkˌbæk/ | n. | 极其费力的工作 |  Work that is extremely physically demanding. | He worked the break-back in the coal mines for years. 他在煤矿干了多年的苦力活。|近义词：toil, drudgery, grind |反义词：sinecure, easy job |词性:名词
