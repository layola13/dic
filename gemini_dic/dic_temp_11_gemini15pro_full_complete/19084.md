betrim:/bɪˈtrɪm/| vt.|装饰，修饰；使整齐；使相称|To adorn or decorate something, typically by adding something around its edges; to make something neat or suitable; to make something match or harmonize. |She betrimmed her hat with feathers. 她用羽毛装饰了她的帽子。|The tailor betrimmed the coat with velvet. 裁缝用天鹅绒装饰了外套。|近义词：adorn, decorate, ornament, embellish, garnish|反义词：strip, denude|verb

词源：
From be- + trim.  "Be-" is a prefix used to form transitive verbs, often with the sense of “all around” or “thoroughly.” "Trim" means to make neat or to decorate.
