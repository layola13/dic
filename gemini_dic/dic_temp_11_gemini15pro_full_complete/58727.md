dunder:/ˈdʌndər/| n.|双下划线|Double underscore, a shorthand term used in Python and other programming languages to refer to names that are surrounded by double underscores. These names often have special meanings within the language or framework.|Example:  The `__init__` method in Python is a dunder method that is called when an object is created. Python中的 `__init__` 方法是一个dunder方法，在创建对象时调用。|近义词: magic method (specifically in Python)|反义词: N/A|noun
