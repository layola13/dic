hyperemetic:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmɛtɪk/ | adj. |过度呕吐的；引起过度呕吐的 | Characterized by or causing excessive vomiting. |  The patient experienced hyperemetic episodes throughout her pregnancy.  病人怀孕期间经历了过度呕吐的症状。|  Hyperemesis gravidarum is a severe form of morning sickness marked by hyperemetic symptoms. 妊娠剧吐是孕吐的一种严重形式，其特征是过度呕吐症状。| 近义词: emetic, nauseating, vomitive | 反义词: antiemetic | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (Greek: 'over, excessive') + emetic (from Greek emetikos 'causing vomiting').
