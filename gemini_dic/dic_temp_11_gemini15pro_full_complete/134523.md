nonlibraries:/ˌnɒnˈlaɪbrəriːz/ | n. | 非图书馆资源/非图书馆机构 |  Resources or institutions that are not libraries.  This term usually refers to information sources or services outside of traditional library settings.  It encompasses a broad range of materials and providers, including online databases, archives, museums, research centers, and government agencies. |  Many researchers now rely heavily on nonlibraries for specialized data. 许多研究人员现在严重依赖非图书馆获取专门数据。

The internet has made a wealth of nonlibraries accessible to the public. 互联网使得大量的非图书馆资源对公众开放。 | 近义词: non-library resources, external resources | 反义词: libraries, library resources | 词性: 名词 (plural)
