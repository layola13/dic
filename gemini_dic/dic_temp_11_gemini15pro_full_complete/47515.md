dearticulate:/ˌdiːɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
verb
使不清晰；使不连贯
to make (speech) unclear or disconnected
The stroke victim's speech was dearticulated, making it difficult to understand.  中风病人的说话含糊不清，很难理解。
disjoint, slur, mumble
articulate, enunciate
verb

dearticulate:/ˌdiːɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
adjective
不清晰的；不连贯的
(of speech) unclear or disconnected
His dearticulate mumbling made it hard to follow his train of thought. 他含糊不清的咕哝声使人难以理解他的思路。
unintelligible, incoherent, mumbled
articulate, clear, distinct
adjective
