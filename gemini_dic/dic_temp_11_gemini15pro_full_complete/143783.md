outmove:/ˌaʊtˈmuːv/ |v. |胜过；以策略胜过|To surpass or defeat someone through superior strategy or maneuvering. |She outmoved her opponent in the chess match. 她在象棋比赛中以策略胜过了对手。| He outmoved his rivals in the business deal. 他在商业交易中胜过了他的竞争对手。 |outmaneuver, outwit, surpass| defeat, lose to |verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + move.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding. "Move" refers to the act of making a strategic play or maneuver.
