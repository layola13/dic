hightailing:/ˈhaɪˌteɪlɪŋ/
verb
迅速离开，逃之夭夭
leaving somewhere quickly
The robbers hightailed it out of the bank as soon as the alarm went off. 抢劫犯警铃一响就迅速逃离了银行。
hightailing it
fleeing, escaping, running away, bolting
approaching, arriving
verb
