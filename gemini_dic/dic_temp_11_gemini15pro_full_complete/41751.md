corrugent: /kəˈruːdʒənt/ | adj. |  起皱的；使起皱的；波纹状的 | Causing wrinkles or folds; having a wavy surface. | The corrugent effect of the acid on the metal was readily apparent. 酸对金属的起皱作用很明显。 |  The corrugent fabric created a unique texture for the dress.  这种起皱的面料为连衣裙创造了独特的质感。 | 近义词: wrinkled, creased, furrowed, corrugated | 反义词: smooth, flat, even | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *corrugare, from cor- "together" + rugare "to wrinkle," from ruga "wrinkle."
