bmc:/biː ɛm siː/
n.
Business Model Canvas, 商业模式画布
A strategic management and lean startup template for developing new or documenting existing business models. It is a visual chart with elements describing a firm's or product's value proposition, infrastructure, customers, and finances.
Example: The startup used a BMC to outline their business plan for investors. 这家初创公司使用商业模式画布向投资者概述了他们的商业计划。
Synonym: business plan, business model
Antonym: N/A
词性: noun


bmc:/biː ɛm siː/
n.
Base Management Controller, 基板管理控制器
A specialized service processor for computer server systems. It provides system health monitoring and alerting, power control, and remote management capabilities.
Example: The server's BMC alerted the administrator of a failing hard drive. 服务器的基板管理控制器向管理员发出了硬盘故障警报。
Synonym: service processor, management controller
Antonym: N/A
词性: noun
