fuzzy:/ˈfʌzi/ (US) /ˈfʌzi/ (UK)| adj.|模糊的，绒毛状的，失真的|covered with short, soft hairs or fibers; not clear or distinct|例句：The picture on the old TV was fuzzy.  旧电视上的图像很模糊。|例句：The kitten has fuzzy fur. 小猫的毛很绒。|近义词：blurred, indistinct, vague, downy, fluffy|反义词：clear, sharp, distinct|词性:形容词

fuzzy:/ˈfʌzi/ (US) /ˈfʌzi/ (UK)| n.|模糊的东西|something that is fuzzy|例句：I can't make out the details, it's all a fuzzy. 我看不清细节，一切都很模糊。|例句： My memories of that night are a bit fuzzy. 我对那晚的记忆有点模糊。|近义词：blur|反义词：clarity|词性:名词
