descent:/dɪˈsɛnt/
noun
下降；血统；袭击
The act of descending; lineage or ancestry; a sudden attack or raid.
The plane began its descent towards the airport. 飞机开始向机场下降。
He is of French descent. 他是法国血统。

词性:noun


词源:
