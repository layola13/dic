aund:/ɔːnt/ | n. |阿姨；姨母；姑母；伯母；舅母|A sister of one's father or mother or the wife of one's uncle or aunt.|例句：My aunt is a doctor. 我的阿姨是一位医生。|例句：I visited my aunt and uncle last weekend. 我上周末去看望了我的姨夫和姨母。|近义词：auntie|反义词: nephew, niece |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ante (Modern French tante), from Latin amita "father's sister".  The spelling with -u- appeared in the 16th century, perhaps influenced by dialectal pronunciations or by analogy with auntre "other".
