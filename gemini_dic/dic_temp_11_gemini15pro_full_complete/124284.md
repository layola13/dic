mothballs:/ˈmɔːθbɔːlz/ (US), /ˈmɒθbɔːlz/ (UK)| n.| 樟脑丸 |Small balls of a pungent-smelling substance containing naphthalene or camphor, used to deter moths and other insects from damaging stored clothes.| My grandmother always puts mothballs in her closets. 我的祖母总是在她的衣橱里放樟脑丸。| synonyms: camphor balls, naphthalene balls | antonyms: N/A | noun

mothball:/ˈmɔːθbɔːl/ (US), /ˈmɒθbɔːl/ (UK)| vt.| 封存，搁置 |To put something into storage or disuse, often temporarily.| The project was mothballed due to lack of funding.  这个项目由于缺乏资金而被搁置了。| synonyms: shelve, suspend, postpone | antonyms: reactivate, restart, recommence | verb
