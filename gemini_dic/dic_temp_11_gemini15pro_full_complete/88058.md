highhanded:/haɪˈhændɪd/| adj.|高压的，专横的，傲慢的|acting without regard for other people's feelings or opinions|例句：The government's highhanded approach to the protests drew international criticism. 政府对示威活动的高压姿态引起了国际社会的批评。

例句：He was known for his highhanded manner with his employees.  他以对员工专横跋扈的态度而闻名。|近义词：arbitrary, dictatorial, imperious, overbearing, tyrannical|反义词：considerate, democratic, humble, respectful|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "high hand," referring to the raised hand of a ruler or authority figure, symbolizing power and often used to strike subordinates.  The term evolved to describe the arrogant and oppressive actions associated with such displays of power.
