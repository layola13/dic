noising:/ˈnɔɪzɪŋ/| n. |加噪，噪声化|The process of adding noise to a signal or data, often intentionally to achieve a specific effect, such as improving robustness or privacy. |Example 1:  Noising is a common technique in image processing to reduce the impact of compression artifacts.  图像处理中，加噪是一种常用的技术，可以减少压缩伪影的影响。

Example 2:  Differential privacy relies on noising mechanisms to protect sensitive data. 差分隐私依赖于加噪机制来保护敏感数据。|近义词：adding noise, introducing noise|反义词: denoising, noise reduction|词性:noun (derived from the verb "noise")
