logic: /ˈlɒdʒɪk/ (UK) /ˈlɑːdʒɪk/ (US)| n.| 逻辑，逻辑学，推理方法| the science that studies the principles of correct reasoning |例句：The logic behind his decision is sound.  他的决定的逻辑是合理的。

例句：He explained the logic of his argument. 他解释了他的论证的逻辑。

近义词：reasoning, rationality|反义词：irrationality, absurdity|词性: noun


logic: /ˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (UK) /ˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ (US)| adj.| 逻辑的，符合逻辑的，合理的| characterized by or capable of clear, sound reasoning |例句：It's a logical conclusion given the evidence.  鉴于证据，这是一个合乎逻辑的结论。

例句：Her argument was clear and logical. 她的论证清晰且合乎逻辑。

近义词：reasonable, rational, sensible|反义词：illogical, irrational, unreasonable|词性: adjective
