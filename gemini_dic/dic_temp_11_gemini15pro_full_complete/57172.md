dowelling:/ˈdaʊəlɪŋ/| n.|榫接; 销钉; 暗销;  |A cylindrical rod, usually wood, used for joining two pieces of wood, typically by inserting it into holes drilled in both pieces.|  He used dowelling to assemble the bookcase. 他用销钉组装书柜。|近义词：peg, pin, rod|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "glue" or "adhesive" as an alternative joining method) |词性:noun

dowel:/ˈdaʊəl/| n. v.|销钉，用销钉连接|A pin, usually of wood, used for fastening two pieces together.|He dowelled the two pieces of wood. 他用销钉把两块木头连接起来。|近义词：peg, pin, rod|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun, verb
