The museum displayed artifacts related to the ancient practice of embowelment.  博物馆展出了与古代取出内脏习俗相关的文物。|近义词：evisceration, disembowelment|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Middle English *embowellen, from en- + bowel + -ment.  Ultimately from Latin *botellus (“sausage”).
