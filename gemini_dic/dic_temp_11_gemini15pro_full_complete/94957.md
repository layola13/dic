in-service:/ˈɪn ˈsɜːrvɪs/| adj.|在职的，服现役的|Relating to or provided for employees currently working in a particular job or profession.  Used to describe training or development provided to employees while they are still employed.|The teachers attended an in-service training on new teaching methods. 老师们参加了一个关于新教学方法的在职培训。|Synonyms: on-the-job, working, employed|Antonyms: retired, unemployed|adjective

in-service:/ˈɪn ˈsɜːrvɪs/| n.|在职培训|A period of training or education provided to employees while they are still employed.|The school provided an in-service on classroom management techniques. 学校提供了一个关于课堂管理技巧的在职培训。|Synonyms: professional development, training, workshop|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "in" + "service". "In" indicates a state or condition, and "service" refers to active duty or employment.
