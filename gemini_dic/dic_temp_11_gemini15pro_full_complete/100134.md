interprofessionally: /ˌɪntərprəˈfeʃənəli/
| adv. |跨专业地，跨职业地|Involving or occurring between members of different professions.  It describes actions, collaborations, or learning that takes place between professionals from different fields. |例句：The team worked interprofessionally to develop a new treatment plan.  团队跨专业合作制定新的治疗方案。|近义词：collaboratively, jointly, together|反义词：independently, separately|词性:副词
词源: inter- (between) + professional + -ly (adverbial suffix)
