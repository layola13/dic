invitationals: /ˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃənəlz/
noun
邀请赛
A competition or tournament to which athletes or teams are invited to participate.
Example: The team was invited to several prestigious invitationals this season. 这个赛季，该队被邀请参加了几场著名的邀请赛。
Example:  She's competing in the track and field invitationals next weekend.  她下周末要参加田径邀请赛。
Synonyms: tournaments, competitions, meets
Antonyms: open events, public competitions
词性: 名词
