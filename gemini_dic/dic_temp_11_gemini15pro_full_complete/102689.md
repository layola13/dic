jammed:/dʒæmd/ | v. adj. | 堵塞的，卡住的；拥挤的；干扰的 | pressed together tightly; stuck; blocked; crowded; interfered with (radio signals) | The printer is jammed. 打印机卡住了。| 近义词：blocked, stuck, clogged, packed, crowded | 反义词：clear, free, open | 词性：动词/形容词
jammed:/dʒæmd/ | v. adj. | 拥挤的；塞满的 | filled to capacity; crowded | The stadium was jammed with fans. 体育场挤满了球迷。| 近义词：packed, crowded, full | 反义词：empty | 词性：动词/形容词

词源: From the Old French "jamber," meaning "to press, squeeze."
