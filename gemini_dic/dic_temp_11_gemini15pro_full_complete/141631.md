optimal:/ˈɒptɪməl/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪməl/ (US)| adj.|最佳的，最优的|Most favorable or desirable; best.|例句：The optimal solution is often a compromise between competing interests. 最佳的解决方案通常是在相互竞争的利益之间取得妥协。

例句：We need to find the optimal balance between cost and quality. 我们需要找到成本和质量之间的最佳平衡。|近义词：best, ideal, perfect, prime, most favorable|反义词：worst, poorest, suboptimal, least favorable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *optimalis*, from optimus (“best”).  The Latin *optimus* is a superlative of *bonus* ("good").
