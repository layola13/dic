influence:/ˈɪnfluəns/ (US), /ˈɪnflʊəns/ (UK)| n. vt.|影响，影响力；势力；感化；有影响的人（或事物）|The influencer's post had a significant influence on her followers' purchasing decisions.  这位网红博主的帖子对她的粉丝的购买决定有重大影响。|例句：Social media has a huge influence on young people. 社交媒体对年轻人有巨大的影响。|近义词：impact, effect, sway, affect|反义词：insignificance, weakness|词性: 名词, 动词

influence:/ˈɪnfluəns/ (US), /ˈɪnflʊəns/ (UK)| vt.|影响，感化；左右|His childhood experiences influenced his career choice. 他童年的经历影响了他的职业选择。|例句：The weather influenced our decision to stay indoors. 天气影响了我们待在室内的决定。|近义词：affect, impact, sway, shape|反义词：leave alone, ignore|词性: 动词
