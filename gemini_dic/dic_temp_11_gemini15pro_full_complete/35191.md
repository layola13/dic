clubbish:/ˈklʌbɪʃ/| adj.|小集团的，排外的，抱团的|Suggesting or characterized by the attitudes and behavior of a restricted group of people, often with shared interests or status, and tending to exclude others.|He belonged to a rather clubbish group of lawyers who lunched together every day. 他属于一个相当抱团的律师小团体，他们每天一起吃午饭。|The club had a very clubbish atmosphere, making it difficult for newcomers to feel welcome.  这个俱乐部有一种很强的排外氛围，让新来的人很难感到受欢迎。|近义词：cliquish, exclusive, insular|反义词：inclusive, welcoming, open|词性: 形容词
