observational:/ˌɒbzəˈveɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 观察的；根据观察的 | Based on observation rather than theory or experiment. |  This study is based on observational data collected over a five-year period. 这项研究基于五年期间收集的观察数据。

The researchers conducted an observational study of the chimpanzees' behavior. 研究人员对黑猩猩的行为进行了观察研究。 | 近义词：empirical, descriptive | 反义词：theoretical, hypothetical | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From observation + -al. Observation comes from the Late Middle English word observacion,  from Old French observation, and ultimately from the Latin observationem, from observare "to watch, observe." The suffix -al forms adjectives meaning "of the kind of, pertaining to, having the form or character of."
