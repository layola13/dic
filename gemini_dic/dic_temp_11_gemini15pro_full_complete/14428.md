autumns:/ˈɔːtəmz/| n.|秋天，秋季|The season after summer and before winter, characterized by falling leaves and cooler temperatures.|例句：The autumns in New England are famous for their vibrant foliage. 新英格兰的秋天以其充满活力的树叶而闻名。

例句：We enjoy long walks in the crisp air of autumns. 我们喜欢在秋高气爽的空气中漫步。|近义词：fall (American English)|反义词:spring|词性:名词
