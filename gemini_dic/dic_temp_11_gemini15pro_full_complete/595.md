aboveboard:/əˈbʌvˌbɔːrd/ | adj. |光明正大的，坦率的 | Open and honest; without concealment or deception. |  The deal was completely aboveboard, with all terms clearly stated in the contract.  这笔交易完全是光明正大的，所有条款都在合同中明确规定。

The negotiations were conducted in an aboveboard manner, ensuring fairness for both parties. 谈判以光明正大的方式进行，确保双方公平。 | 近义词: honest, straightforward, open, transparent, legitimate | 反义词: dishonest, shady, underhanded, deceitful, clandestine | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrase "above the board," referring to card games where hands held above the table are visible to all, ensuring no cheating.  源于短语 "above the board"，指的是纸牌游戏中，手牌放在桌子上方，所有人都可以看到，确保没有作弊行为。
