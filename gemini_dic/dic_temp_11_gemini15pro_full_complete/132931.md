nonelusivenesses:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːsɪvnəsɪz/
n.
非难以捉摸性；易于理解性；清晰性
The quality or state of being nonelusive; readily understood or grasped; clarity.
The nonelusivenesses of the instructions made the task easy to complete.  指令的清晰性使得任务很容易完成。
Near synonyms: clarity, explicitness, obviousness, transparency, perspicuity
Antonyms: elusiveness, obscurity, vagueness, ambiguity, incomprehensibility
noun
