orleans:/ˈɔːrliənz/ (US), /ɔːˈliːənz/ (UK)| n. |奥尔良|A city in France, on the Loire River, known for its association with Joan of Arc. Also, a city in Louisiana, USA.|Joan of Arc lifted the siege of Orleans. 圣女贞德解除了奥尔良之围。|New Orleans is a major port city. 新奥尔良是一个主要港口城市。|synonyms: (for French Orleans) city, town; (for New Orleans) NOLA, The Big Easy|antonyms: N/A|noun
