corroder:/kəˈroʊdər/| n.|腐蚀物，腐蚀剂|Something that corrodes; a corrosive substance.  It gradually destroys or weakens something, especially by chemical action.|Acid rain is a corroder of stone buildings. 酸雨是石材建筑的腐蚀物。|近义词：corrosive, oxidant, rust, decay agent|反义词:preservative, inhibitor|noun


corroder:/kəˈroʊd/| v.|腐蚀，侵蚀|To destroy or damage something slowly by chemical action.|Rust corrodes iron and steel. 铁锈腐蚀铁和钢。|近义词：erode, eat away, wear away, oxidize, rust, decay|反义词:preserve, protect|verb
