mux:/mʌks/ | n. v. | 多路复用器，多路复用 | A device that selects one of several analog or digital input signals and forwards the selected input into a single line. |  We use a mux to combine multiple video streams into one signal. 我们使用多路复用器将多个视频流组合成一个信号。| Multiplexer, combiner, switch | Demultiplexer, splitter | n. v.

mux:/mʌks/ | v. | 多路复用 | To combine multiple signals into one. | We need to mux the audio and video signals together for transmission. 我们需要将音频和视频信号多路复用在一起进行传输。| Combine, merge, integrate | Separate, split | v.


ETYMOLOGY->
Shortening of "multiplexer," from "multiplex" + "-er." "Multiplex" comes from "multi-" (many) + "-plex" (fold, layered), ultimately from Latin "plicare" (to fold).
