betrayed:/bɪˈtreɪd/| v. |背叛，出卖，辜负，显露（无意中泄露）|To be disloyal to someone or something; to reveal something unintentionally.| He felt betrayed by his closest friend. 他觉得被他最亲密的朋友背叛了。|近义词：deceived, cheated, double-crossed, let down|反义词：loyal, faithful, true|词性:动词

betray:/bɪˈtreɪ/| v. |背叛，出卖，辜负，显露（无意中泄露）|To be disloyal to someone or something; to reveal something unintentionally.| His trembling hands betrayed his nervousness. 他颤抖的双手暴露了他的紧张。|近义词：deceive, cheat, double-cross, let down|反义词：support, protect, defend|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *betraier (Modern French trahir), from Latin tradere "to hand over, deliver, betray," from trans- "across" + dare "to give."
