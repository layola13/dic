half-begging:/ˌhɑːfˈbeɡɪŋ/
adj.
半乞求的，近乎乞求的
Appealing with a mixture of request and supplication, often in a hesitant or indirect way.
He gave her a half-begging look, hoping she'd lend him the money. 他半乞求地看了她一眼，希望她能借钱给他。
近义词: pleading, imploring, supplicating
反义词: demanding, assertive
词性: adjective

half:/hɑːf/
n./adj./adv.
一半, 半数的，半个的，一半地
One of two equal or approximately equal parts of a divisible whole.
She ate half the cake. 她吃掉了半个蛋糕。
近义词: moiety, one-half
反义词: whole, double
词性: noun, adjective, adverb

begging:/ˈbeɡɪŋ/
v.
乞求，请求
Asking for something earnestly or humbly.
He was begging for money on the street. 他在街上乞讨。
近义词: pleading, imploring, entreating
反义词: demanding, commanding
词性: verb (present participle of beg)
