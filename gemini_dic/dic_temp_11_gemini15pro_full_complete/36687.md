colligation:/ˌkɒlɪˈɡeɪʃn/| n.|搭配；组合；结合|The study of colligations helps learners understand which words typically occur together.  对搭配的研究帮助学习者理解哪些词通常一起出现。|Colligations in English can be tricky for non-native speakers.  英语中的搭配对于非母语人士来说可能很棘手。|近义词：combination, collocation, association|反义词：separation, disassociation|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Latin "colligatio"  "a binding together," from "colligare"  "to bind together," from "com-" (together) + "ligare" (to bind).
