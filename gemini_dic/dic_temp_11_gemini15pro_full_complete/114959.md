manbird:/ˈmænˌbɜːrd/| n. |人鸟，鸟人|A mythical or fictional creature that is part human and part bird.  Often depicted with the head and torso of a human and the wings and legs of a bird.|He dreamt he was a manbird, soaring through the clouds. 他梦见自己是一个人鸟，翱翔在云端。|近义词：harpy, griffin|反义词:human|词性:noun
