jottings:/ˈdʒɒtɪŋz/ (US) /ˈdʒɑːtɪŋz/| n.|简短的笔记；随笔；略记|Brief notes or short pieces of writing, usually written quickly and informally.|She made some jottings in her notebook during the meeting.  她在会议期间在笔记本上做了一些记录。

|Example:|He kept jottings of his travels in a small leather-bound book. 他把旅行的点滴记录在一个小皮面书里。


|Synonyms:|notes, memos, scribbles, annotations, observations|
|Antonyms:|treatise, dissertation, essay, formal report|
|Part of Speech:|Noun|
