homotype:/ˈhɒməʊtaɪp/ (UK), /ˈhoʊməʊtaɪp/ (US)| n.|同型，同型体|An individual, organ, or part corresponding in structure and origin to another; one of two or more corresponding parts.|The left arm is a homotype of the right arm.  左臂和右臂是同型体。|Synonyms: counterpart, homologue, equivalent|Antonyms: heterotype|noun
