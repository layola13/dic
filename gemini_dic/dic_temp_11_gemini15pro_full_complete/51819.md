deweer:/dəˈveɪr/
n.|露水；潮湿；湿气|Moisture condensed from the atmosphere, especially at night, and deposited in the form of small drops on any cool surface.|The grass was wet with deweer.|近义词:dew, moisture, dampness|反义词:dryness|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle Dutch *dauwweer, equivalent to dauw (dew) + weer (weather).  Cognate with West Frisian dowwaar (“dew weather”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dawwaz (“dew”).
