at a loss:/æt ə lɒs/ (British) /æt ə lɔːs/ (American)| adj. |不知所措，困惑，亏损|Confused and uncertain about what to do or say; also, in a state of financial loss.|例句：After her husband died, she was at a loss for words. 她丈夫去世后，她不知说什么好。|例句：The company was at a loss for the second quarter in a row.  这家公司连续第二个季度亏损。|近义词：confused, perplexed, bewildered, uncertain, baffled, nonplussed, stumped, dumbfounded, speechless, in the red |反义词：certain, sure, confident, knowing, in the black, profitable|词性:adjective
