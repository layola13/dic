claiming:/ˈkleɪmɪŋ/
verb
声称；认领；索取
present participle of "claim"  meaning stating that something is true, taking something that belongs to you, or demanding something that you believe is yours.
He is claiming victory despite the official results being delayed. 他声称自己获胜，尽管官方结果尚未公布。
The refugees are claiming asylum. 难民们正在寻求政治庇护。

近义词: asserting, alleging, demanding, requesting
反义词: denying, relinquishing, waiving
词性: v. (present participle)
