lucrativeness:/ˈluːkrətɪvnəs/
n. |获利性；有利可图；赚钱|The lucrativeness of the business attracted many investors. 这项业务的盈利能力吸引了许多投资者。|
近义词:profitability, gainfulness
反义词:unprofitability, loss
词性:名词

lucrative:/ˈluːkrətɪv/
adj.|有利可图的；赚钱的| a lucrative business  一项有利可图的生意|
近义词:profitable, rewarding, worthwhile
反义词:unprofitable, unrewarding
词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *lūcrātīvus, from Latin lūcrārī (“to gain, enrich oneself”), from lūcrum (“gain, profit”).
