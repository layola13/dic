mistaught:/ˌmɪsˈtɔːt/
verb
教错了，错误地教导
incorrectly taught; taught wrongly.
The students were mistaught the rules of grammar. 学生们被错误地教授了语法规则。
Synonyms: miseducated, wrongly taught, inaccurately taught
Antonyms: correctly taught, properly taught, accurately taught
词性: 动词 (past participle of misteach)
