emollient: /ɪˈmɒliənt/ /ɪˈmoʊliənt/
adjective

Having the quality of softening or soothing, especially the skin.
The cream has an emollient effect on dry skin.  这种乳霜对干燥的皮肤有润肤作用。

近义词: softening, soothing, calming, relaxing

词性: adjective

emollient: /ɪˈmɒliənt/ /ɪˈmoʊliənt/
noun
润肤剂；缓和剂
A substance that softens or soothes, especially the skin.
This emollient is good for sensitive skin.  这款润肤剂适合敏感肌肤。
The negotiator acted as an emollient between the two warring factions.  谈判代表在两个交战派别之间充当了缓和剂。
近义词: moisturizer, softener, balm
反义词: irritant
词性: noun


词源: From Latin *emollire, from e- + mollire "to soften," from mollis "soft."
