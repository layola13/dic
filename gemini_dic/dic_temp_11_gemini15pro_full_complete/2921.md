advises:/ədˈvaɪzɪz/
verb
建议；劝告；通知；提供意见
Third-person singular present tense form of the verb "advise."  It means to offer suggestions about the best course of action to someone.
He advises her to invest her money wisely. 他建议她明智地投资她的钱。
The doctor advises against smoking. 医生建议不要吸烟。
Synonyms: recommends, counsels, suggests, guides
Antonyms: dissuades, discourages
