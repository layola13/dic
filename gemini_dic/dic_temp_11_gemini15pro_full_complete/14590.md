avidity:/əˈvɪdəti/
n.
热情;渴望;贪婪
enthusiastic desire or eagerness
She studied with avidity. 她学习很用功。
近义词: eagerness, keenness, enthusiasm, zeal, fervor, passion
反义词: apathy, indifference, reluctance
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *aviditas, from Latin avidus (“greedy, desirous, eager”), from avere (“to crave, desire, long for”).
