dissimilarities:/ˌdɪsˌsɪmɪˈlærɪtiz/| n.|不同点，差异|The report highlighted the dissimilarities between the two markets.  这份报告强调了两个市场之间的差异。|例句：The dissimilarities between the twins are striking. 这对双胞胎之间的差异很明显。

例句：Despite their dissimilarities in background, they became close friends. 尽管他们的背景差异很大，他们还是成为了亲密的朋友。|近义词：differences, discrepancies, variations|反义词：similarities, resemblances|词性：名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French dissimilarité, from dissimilar, from dis- (expressing negation) + similar.
