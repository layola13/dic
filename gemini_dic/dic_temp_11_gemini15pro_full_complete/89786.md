horizontalism:/ˌhɔːrɪˈzɒntəlɪzəm/
n.|水平主义|A social or political theory that advocates for decentralized and non-hierarchical organizational structures, emphasizing equality and collaboration among participants.|The Occupy movement embraced horizontalism in its decision-making processes, striving for consensus among all participants. 占领运动在其决策过程中采用了水平主义，力求在所有参与者之间达成共识。|例句：The collective operates on principles of horizontalism, with all members having equal say.  这个集体按照水平主义原则运作，所有成员都有平等的发言权。

例句：Horizontalism can be challenging to implement in large organizations due to the potential for inefficiency.  在大型组织中，由于效率低下的可能性，水平主义的实施可能具有挑战性。|近义词：decentralization, grassroots democracy, participatory democracy, self-governance, anarchism|反义词：hierarchy, verticalism, authoritarianism|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From horizontal + -ism.  "horizontal" ultimately derives from the Greek "horizōn" (ὁρίζων), meaning "bounding."
