clinically:/ˈklɪnɪkli/| adv.|临床地；冷静地；客观地|In a way that relates to the examination and treatment of patients or the practice of medicine; in a detached and unemotional way.|The drug has been clinically tested.  这种药物已经过临床试验。|Synonyms: medically, scientifically, objectively, dispassionately|Antonyms: subjectively, emotionally|adverb
