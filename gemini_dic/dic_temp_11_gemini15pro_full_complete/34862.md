clinometric:/ˌklɪnəˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|测斜的，倾斜测量的|Relating to or using a clinometer, an instrument for measuring angles of elevation or inclination, especially of slopes.|Geologists used a clinometer to measure the angle of the rock strata. 地质学家使用测斜仪测量岩层的角度。|Synonyms: inclinometric,  slope-measuring|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
