demesnial:/dɪˈmiːniəl/
adjective
领地的；属于领主的
Of or relating to a lord's demesne.
The demesnial lands were held directly by the king.  国王直接掌管着这些领地。
The demesnial woods provided timber for the castle.  领地的树林为城堡提供了木材。
近义词: manorial, baronial
反义词: common, public
词性: adjective

词源:
From Old French *demeisne, demaine (“lord's land”), from Medieval Latin dominicum (“land of a lord”), neuter of dominicus (“of a lord”), from Latin dominus (“lord, master”).
