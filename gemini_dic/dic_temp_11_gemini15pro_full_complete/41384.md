cormorant:/ˈkɔːrmərənt/ (US) /ˈkɔːmərənt/ (UK)| n. |鸬鹚|A large, dark-colored waterbird with a long neck and a hooked bill, known for its diving ability and used in some parts of Asia for fishing.|例句：The cormorant dived deep into the water to catch a fish. 鸬鹚潜入水中去抓鱼。|近义词：shag, seabird|反义词: (No direct antonym for an animal)|noun
