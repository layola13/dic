laterality:/ˌlætəˈrælɪti/
n.|侧性，偏侧性|The preference for using one side of the body, such as handedness (hand preference).  It can also refer to the specialization of functions in the two hemispheres of the brain.|Handedness is a common example of laterality.  用手习惯是侧性的一种常见例子。|sidedness, asymmetry|symmetry|noun
