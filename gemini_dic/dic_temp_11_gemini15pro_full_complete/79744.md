glumella:/ɡluːˈmɛlə/ | n. |颖片，小苞片|A small bract, especially one of the two chaffy bracts enclosing the floret in grasses.|The glumella is a key characteristic used to identify different grass species. 颖片是识别不同草种的关键特征。| The glumella of this species is particularly long. 这个品种的颖片特别长。|Synonym: lemma, palea|Antonym: N/A|noun

glume:/ɡluːm/ | n. |颖片，苞片| One of the two empty bracts at the base of a grass spikelet.|The glumes protect the developing florets. 颖片保护正在发育的小花。|The glumes of this grass are hairy. 这种草的颖片有毛。|Synonym: chaff|Antonym: N/A|noun
