numismatics:/ˌnʊːmɪˈzmætɪks/ /ˌnuːmɪˈzmætɪks/| n.|钱币学|The study or collection of coins, paper money, and medals.|Numismatics is a fascinating hobby. 钱币学是一项引人入胜的爱好。|近义词：coin collecting, numismatology|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From French numismatique, from Latin numisma, numismatis (“coin, money”), from Greek νόμισμα (nómisma, “current coin, usage, custom, law”), from νομίζω (nomízō, “hold as custom, use customarily, practice, be in use, be current, be wont, hold as an opinion, believe, think, deem, consider”), from νόμος (nómos, “usage, custom, law, ordinance”).
