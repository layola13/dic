anaemic:/əˈniːmɪk/ /əˈnemɪk/| adj.|贫血的；缺乏活力的；苍白的|Lacking vigor or energy; listless; pale.  Also, medically, relating to or affected by anaemia.|例句：The anaemic child needed iron supplements.  这个贫血的孩子需要补充铁剂。

例句：The play was an anaemic performance, lacking any real passion. 这场戏的表演缺乏活力，没有任何真正的激情。|近义词：pale, wan, feeble, weak, lifeless, pallid, ashen, bloodless, colorless|反义词：robust, healthy, vigorous, energetic, vibrant, colorful, flourishing|词性:形容词
