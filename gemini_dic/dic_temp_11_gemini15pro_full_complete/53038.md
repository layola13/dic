dioeciousness:/daɪˈiːʃəsnəs/| n. |雌雄异株 |The state of having the male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals. | Holly trees exhibit dioeciousness, meaning you need a male and a female tree for berry production. 冬青树表现出雌雄异株，这意味着你需要一棵雄树和一棵雌树才能结浆果。| synonyms: gonochorism | antonyms: monoeciousness, hermaphroditism | noun

dioecious:/daɪˈiːʃəs/| adj. |雌雄异株的 |Having the male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals. |  The willow tree is dioecious. 柳树是雌雄异株的。| synonyms: gonochoristic | antonyms: monoecious, hermaphroditic | adjective
