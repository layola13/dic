grindstone:/ˈɡraɪndˌstoʊn/ (US) /ˈɡraɪndstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|磨刀石；苦差事|A thick revolving disk of stone or other abrasive material used for grinding, sharpening, or polishing metal tools.  Figuratively, it can also refer to a tedious or laborious task.|He sharpened his axe on the grindstone. 他在磨刀石上磨斧头。|Synonyms: whetstone, sharpening stone,  grind wheel |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the literal meaning.  For the figurative meaning, perhaps "leisure", "relaxation")|noun
