call on:/kɔːl ɑːn/ | phrasal verb |拜访，访问；号召，请求；要求…发言|To visit someone; to formally ask someone to speak or to do something. | I called on my grandmother last Sunday. 我上周日去看望了我的祖母。

The teacher called on me to answer the question. 老师叫我回答问题。 | visit, drop in (on), invite, summon, appeal to, request | ignore, neglect | verb phrase

call on:/kɔːl ɑːn/ | phrasal verb | 呼吁，号召 | To appeal to or urge someone to do something. | The president called on the nation to support the war effort. 总统号召全国人民支持战争。

The charity is calling on the public for donations.  慈善机构呼吁公众捐款。| appeal to, urge, exhort, request | discourage, dissuade | verb phrase


ETYMOLOGY->
"call" comes from Old Norse "kalla" (to shout, cry out) and "on" is a preposition indicating direction or position. The combination "call on" developed various meanings over time related to directing a call or request towards someone or something.
