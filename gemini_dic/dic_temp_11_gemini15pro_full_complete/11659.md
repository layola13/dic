armisonant:/ˈɑːrmɪsənənt/ | adj. | 响亮的，谐和的，声音洪亮的 |  Producing or characterized by a loud, resonant sound, often suggestive of arms or armor.  Derived from Latin "arma" (arms) and "sonant" (sounding).  This word evokes a sense of powerful, resounding noise, like the clash of weapons or a deep, ringing voice. | The armisonant clang of the blacksmith's hammer echoed through the forge.  铁匠的锤子发出响亮的铿锵声，在锻造厂里回荡。|  The orator's armisonant voice filled the hall. 演说家洪亮的声音充满了整个大厅。 | resonant, sonorous, resounding | quiet, muted | adjective
