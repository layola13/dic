disworship:/dɪsˈwɜːrʃɪp/
verb
不崇拜，不敬
To refuse to worship; to treat with disrespect or contempt.
He disworships all idols and false gods. 他不崇拜任何偶像和假神。
Near synonyms: profane, desecrate, dishonor
Antonyms: worship, venerate, revere
词性: 动词
