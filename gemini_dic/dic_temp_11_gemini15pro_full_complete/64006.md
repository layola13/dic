epistoma:/ˈɛpɪˌstoʊmə/| n. |上唇，口上片|A plate-like sclerite forming the upper part of the "face" of an arthropod, typically just above or in front of the mouth. |The epistoma of the beetle is distinctly ridged.|近义词:clypeus (though not always interchangeable)|反义词: None|noun
