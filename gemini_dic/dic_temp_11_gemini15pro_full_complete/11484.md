arhythmous:/əˈrɪðməs/
|adj.|无节奏的，不规律的|Lacking rhythm or regularity, especially in relation to the heartbeat.|His arhythmous pulse worried the doctors. 他的无规律脉搏让医生很担心。|
|近义词:irregular, erratic, unsteady|反义词:rhythmic, regular, steady|词性:形容词|
|词源:a- (not) + rhythm + -ous (adjective forming suffix)|
