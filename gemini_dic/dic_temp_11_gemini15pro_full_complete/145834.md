overdeliberations: /ˌoʊvər dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度审议；过分考虑 | Excessive deliberation or discussion, often to the point of delaying action. | The overdeliberations of the committee led to the project missing its deadline. 委员会的过度审议导致项目错过了截止日期。|  The overdeliberations stalled the decision-making process. 过度审议使得决策过程停滞不前。| 近义词：overthinking, excessive discussion, procrastination | 反义词： decisiveness, promptness | 词性: 名词

overdeliberate: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/ | v. | 过度审议；过分考虑 | To deliberate excessively or too carefully, often resulting in inaction. | The board overdeliberated the issue and failed to reach a conclusion. 董事会过度审议了这个问题，未能得出结论。 |  They overdeliberated the proposal, losing a valuable opportunity. 他们对这个提议进行了过多的考虑，错失了宝贵的机会。 | 近义词：overthink, ponder excessively | 反义词： act decisively, be prompt | 词性: 动词


词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "deliberate" (from Latin "deliberare" meaning "to weigh, consider").
