homeschooling:/ˈhoʊmskuːlɪŋ/ |n.| 家庭教育；在家教育 |The practice of educating children at home rather than in a formal school setting. |Homeschooling is becoming increasingly popular. 家庭教育越来越受欢迎。

Homeschooling allows for a flexible schedule. 家庭教育可以安排灵活的时间。 |近义词: home education, home-based education| 反义词: traditional schooling, public schooling|词性:名词
