compellers:/kəmˈpɛlərz/
noun
强制者，强迫者
People or things that compel someone to do something.
The compellers of this change were economic necessity and social pressure. 促成这一变化的强制因素是经济需要和社会压力。
synonyms: forcers, obligers, coercers
antonyms: persuaders, encouragers
n.

compel:/kəmˈpɛl/
verb
强迫，迫使
To force someone to do something.
The law compels employers to provide health insurance. 法律强制雇主提供健康保险。
synonyms: force, oblige, coerce
antonyms: persuade, encourage
v.
