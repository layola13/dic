furzy:/ˈfɜːzi/ | adj. | 模糊的，绒毛状的 | Covered with fur or fuzz; like fur or fuzz; indistinct or vague. | The photograph was old and furzy. 这张照片又旧又模糊。 | 近义词：fuzzy, downy, hairy, unclear, vague | 反义词：clear, distinct, sharp | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From *fur* (n.) + *-y* (suffix forming adjectives).
