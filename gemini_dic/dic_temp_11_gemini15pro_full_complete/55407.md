disthene:/ˈdɪsˌθiːn/ /ˈdɪsˌθɪn/| n.|蓝晶石, 二硬石|A mineral, aluminum silicate, occurring in metamorphic rocks. It is characterized by its strong pleochroism and its anisotropic hardness.  It is typically blue, gray, green, brown, or black in color.|Disthene is a common mineral in high-pressure metamorphic rocks. 蓝晶石是高压变质岩中常见的矿物。

Disthene can be used as a gemstone. 蓝晶石可以用作宝石。|近义词：kyanite|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *disthène*, from Ancient Greek *δίς* (dís, “twice, double”) + *σθένος* (sthénos, “strength”), referring to its anisotropic hardness.
