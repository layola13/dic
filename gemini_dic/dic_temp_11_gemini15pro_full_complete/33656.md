cinemograph: /ˈsɪnɪməˌɡræf/ /ˈsɪnɪməˌɡrɑːf/
n.
动态照片，局部动图
A still photograph in which a minor and repeated movement occurs, created by looping a short video sequence.
The artist created a mesmerizing cinemograph of a waterfall, with the water cascading endlessly. 艺术家创作了一幅迷人的瀑布动态照片，水流 endlessly.

The cinemograph of the flickering candle flame added a touch of magic to the scene. 闪烁的烛光动态照片为场景增添了一丝魔力。

近义词:animated GIF, living photo
反义词:still photograph, static image
词性:noun
