disciplinary:/ˈdɪsəplɪˌnɛri/ /ˈdɪsəplɪnəri/ | adj. | 纪律的；惩戒性的 | Relating to discipline; designed to correct or punish bad behavior. | The school took disciplinary action against the student for cheating. 学校对作弊的学生采取了纪律处分。 |  synonyms: corrective, punitive, penal | antonyms: lenient, permissive | adjective

disciplinary:/ˈdɪsəplɪˌnɛri/ /ˈdɪsəplɪnəri/ | n. | 纪律问题；惩戒措施 | A disciplinary measure or action taken to enforce rules and maintain order.  (This usage is less common, usually appearing in plural form "disciplinarys"). | The teacher issued several disciplinarys for disruptive behavior. 老师对扰乱课堂秩序的行为发出了几项处罚。 | synonyms: punishment, sanction, penalty | antonyms: reward, praise | noun
