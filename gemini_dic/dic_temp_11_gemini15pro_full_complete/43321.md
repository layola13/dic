crambo:/ˈkræmboʊ/| n.|押韵游戏，联句游戏|A game in which one player gives a word or line of verse, and another must find a rhyme to it.  The game continues with each player rhyming the last word given.|We played crambo at the party last night. 我们昨晚在聚会上玩了押韵游戏。|synonyms: rhyming game, capping verses|antonyms: N/A|noun


crambo:/ˈkræmboʊ/| n.|蹩脚的诗|A poem, especially one that is forced or contrived, often due to the constraints of the rhyming game.|The poet apologized for his crambo, blaming the difficult rhyme scheme. 诗人为自己蹩脚的诗歌道歉，并将其归咎于困难的押韵格式。|synonyms: doggerel, jingle|antonyms: masterpiece, classic|noun
