dupers:/ˈduːpərz/| n.|骗子；行骗者|People who deceive others for personal gain.  那些为了个人利益欺骗他人的人。|The dupers convinced the elderly woman to give them her life savings. 这些骗子说服了这位老妇人把她的毕生积蓄都给了他们。|Synonyms: swindlers, con artists, fraudsters|Antonyms: honest people, victims|noun
