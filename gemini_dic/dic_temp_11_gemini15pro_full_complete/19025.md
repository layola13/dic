beta-tester:/ˈbeɪtə ˈtestər/ | n. | Beta测试员 | A person who tests a pre-release version of a software product or service to identify bugs and provide feedback. |  Example: The beta-testers found several critical bugs before the software was officially released. Beta测试员在软件正式发布前发现了一些严重的错误。

Example:  We rely on our beta-testers to ensure a high-quality product. 我们依靠我们的Beta测试员来确保产品的高质量。| 近义词: trial user, early adopter | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
