lavishness:/ˈlævɪʃnəs/| n.| 奢侈， lavishness|The lavishness of the party was breathtaking.  派对的奢华令人叹为观止。|近义词：extravagance, luxuriance, profusion|反义词：frugality, austerity, simplicity|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From lavish + -ness. Lavish comes from Middle English lavas, lavache "profuse, prodigal," from Old French lavasse "downpour, deluge," perhaps from laver "to wash."
