bard:/bɑːrd/| n.|吟游诗人，诗人|A poet, traditionally one reciting epics and associated with a particular oral tradition.|Shakespeare is often called the Bard of Avon. 莎士比亚常被称为艾冯河的吟游诗人。|synonyms: poet, minstrel, versifier|antonyms: prosaist|noun

bard:/bɑːrd/| n.|（古代凯尔特部落的）吟唱诗人|A professional poet, traditionally one reciting epics and associated with a particular oral tradition, especially one of the ancient Celtic peoples.|The bards of Wales were highly respected. 威尔士的吟唱诗人很受尊敬。|synonyms: poet, minstrel, versifier|antonyms: prosaist|noun
