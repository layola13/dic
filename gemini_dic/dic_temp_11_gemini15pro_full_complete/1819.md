acroamatic:/ˌækrəʊəˈmætɪk/
adjective
深奥的；秘传的；只讲授给入室弟子的
Designed for or appropriate to lectures or discourses of an esoteric or highly specialized nature; abstruse; esoteric.
The professor's acroamatic lectures were only understood by a few advanced students.  教授深奥的讲座只有少数高年级学生才能理解。
近义词:esoteric, abstruse, arcane
反义词:exoteric, simple, accessible
词性:adjective


acroamatic:/ˌækrəʊəˈmætɪk/
noun
秘传哲理；深奥知识
Esoteric or highly specialized knowledge or discourse.
The acroamatics of ancient philosophy are still debated today.  古代哲学的秘传哲理至今仍在争论中。
近义词:arcana, mysteries, secrets
反义词:common knowledge, basics
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀκροαματικός (akroamatikós, “designed for hearing only”), from ἀκρόαμα (akróama, “a thing heard, a lecture”), from ἀκροάομαι (akroáomai, “I listen”).
