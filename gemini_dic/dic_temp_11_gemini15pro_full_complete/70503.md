filchers:/ˈfɪltʃərz/
noun
小偷，顺手牵羊的人
People who steal things, especially small, inexpensive items, in a secretive way.
The filchers were caught stealing candy from the store.  小偷在商店里偷糖果时被抓住了。
Synonyms: thieves, pilferers, shoplifters
Antonyms: donors, philanthropists
