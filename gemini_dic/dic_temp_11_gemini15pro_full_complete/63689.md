epigenetic:/ˌɛpɪdʒəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
表观遗传的
Relating to or arising from epigenetics.  Changes in gene expression that do not involve changes to the underlying DNA sequence.
Epigenetic modifications can be influenced by environmental factors. 表观遗传修饰会受到环境因素的影响。
Certain cancers can be caused by epigenetic changes. 某些癌症可能是由表观遗传变化引起的。
近义词: heritable, inheritable
反义词: genetic (in some contexts, as epigenetics builds upon genetics)
词性: adjective
词源:  From Greek *epi- "upon, in addition to" + genetic.
