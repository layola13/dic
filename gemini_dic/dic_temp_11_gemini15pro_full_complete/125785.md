muriated:/ˈmjʊərieɪtɪd/
adjective
氯化的；盐酸处理的
treated with or containing muriatic acid (hydrochloric acid).
Muriated tincture of iron is a traditional medicine. 氯化铁酊是一种传统药物。
近义词: chlorinated, hydrochloric
反义词: None
