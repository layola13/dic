bedazzle:/bɪˈdæzəl/
verb
使眼花缭乱，使惊叹
To impress or overwhelm with brilliance or splendor; to fascinate.
The fireworks bedazzled the children. 焰火使孩子们眼花缭乱。
Synonyms: dazzle, amaze, fascinate, enchant, captivate
Antonyms: bore, tire, disappoint, disillusion
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + dazzle.  "Dazzle" itself comes from Old English *dasol, meaning "foolish, dazed."
