infrigidate:/ɪnˈfrɪdʒɪdeɪt/
verb
使冷卻；使冷淡；使沮喪
to make something or someone cold; to discourage someone
The sudden drop in temperature infrigidated the hikers. 温度骤降使徒步旅行者感到寒冷。
His constant criticism infrigidated her enthusiasm. 他不断的批评使她的热情冷却下来。
chill, cool, discourage
warm, encourage
词性:动词
