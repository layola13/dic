coxcomical:/kɒksˈkɒmɪkəl/ | adj. | 滑稽可笑的；愚蠢的；自以为是的 | Foolishly self-important or conceited; pompous and affected. |  He strutted around in a coxcomical manner, convinced of his own brilliance. 他以滑稽可笑的方式昂首阔步，确信自己才华横溢。| synonyms: pompous, pretentious, foolish, absurd, ludicrous | antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming, sensible | adjective

coxcomb:/ˈkɒkskəʊm/ | n. | 花花公子；纨绔子弟；鸡冠 | A vain and conceited man; a dandy; the crest of a rooster. | He was a coxcomb, always preening himself and boasting about his conquests. 他是个花花公子，总是打扮自己，吹嘘自己的艳遇。| synonyms: fop, dandy, popinjay | antonyms: n/a | noun
