ectoparasite:/ˈɛktəʊˌpærəsaɪt/| n. |外寄生虫|An organism that lives on the outside of its host.|Fleas and ticks are common ectoparasites of dogs.  跳蚤和蜱虫是狗常见的体外寄生虫。|例句：The bird was infested with ectoparasites. 这只鸟身上爬满了体外寄生虫。|近义词：external parasite|反义词:endoparasite|名词
