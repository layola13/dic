glutinousness:/ˌɡluːtɪnəsnəs/
n.|黏性；胶粘性|The glutinousness of the dough made it difficult to work with.  面团的黏性使得它很难操作。|English Explanation: The quality of being sticky or gluey; viscosity.|例句：The glutinousness of the rice made it perfect for sushi.  这种米的黏性使它非常适合做寿司。|近义词：stickiness, viscosity, adhesiveness|反义词：slipperiness, dryness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From glutinous + -ness.  Glutinous comes from Late Latin glūtinōsus, from glūten (“glue”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gley- (“to stick, glue”).
