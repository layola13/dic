cable-cars:/ˈkeɪbl̩ kɑːrz/ (US) /ˈkeɪbl̩ kɑːz/ (UK)| n.|缆车|A system of passenger transportation that uses cars suspended from and pulled by a moving cable.|We rode the cable-cars up to the top of the mountain. 我们乘缆车到了山顶。|近义词：gondola lift, aerial tramway|反义词:|noun


cable-car:/ˈkeɪbl̩ kɑːr/ (US) /ˈkeɪbl̩ kɑːz/ (UK)| n.|缆车|A car used in a cable-car system.|The cable-car offered stunning views of the city. 缆车提供了令人惊叹的城市景观。|近义词：gondola, tram|反义词:|noun

词源:  "cable" comes from Old North French "cable," ultimately from Latin "capulum" (halter, rope). "Car" comes from Old North French "carre" (chariot), ultimately from Latin "carrus" (wheeled vehicle).
