comfortableness:/ˈkʌmfərtəblnəs/| n. |舒适；安逸；惬意|The state of being comfortable; ease and freedom from pain or constraint.|The soft cushions added to the comfortableness of the chair.  柔软的垫子增加了椅子的舒适度。|Synonyms: comfort, ease, coziness, relaxation|Antonyms: discomfort, unease, distress|noun

comfort:/ˈkʌmfərt/| n. v. |舒适，安慰；慰藉|n. a state of physical ease and freedom from pain or constraint. v. to ease the grief or distress of someone.|The hotel offers a high level of comfort. 这家酒店提供高水平的舒适度。 She comforted the crying child. 她安慰了哭泣的孩子。|Synonyms: solace, consolation, support|Antonyms: discomfort, distress, misery|noun, verb
