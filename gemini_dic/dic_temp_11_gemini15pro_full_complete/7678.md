anodyne:/ˈænəˌdaɪn/ /ˈænədaɪn/| n.|止痛剂|A medicine that relieves pain.|She took an anodyne to ease her back pain. 她吃了一片止痛药来缓解背痛。|近义词：painkiller, analgesic|反义词：stimulant|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anodyn* (now *anodin*), from Late Latin *anodynus*, from Ancient Greek *ἀνώδυνος (anṓdynos)*, from *ἀ- (a-)* (not) + *ὀδύνη (odúnē)* (pain).
