fraying:/ˈfreɪɪŋ/
n.|磨损，磨破，（关系、神经等）紧张，（布料边缘）散开|the process of becoming worn or tattered at the edges; a situation of tension or conflict|The fraying rope finally snapped. 磨损的绳子终于断了。|近义词:unraveling, wearing, deteriorating, straining|反义词:strengthening, mending, improving
v.|（使布料边缘）磨损，磨破，（使关系、神经等）紧张|present participle of fray|The edges of the carpet are fraying. 地毯的边缘正在磨损。|近义词:unraveling, wearing, deteriorating, straining|反义词:strengthening, mending, improving

frayed:/freɪd/
adj.|磨损的，磨破的，（神经）紧张的|worn or tattered at the edges; (of a person or their nerves) strained|The frayed edges of the flag showed its age. 旗帜磨损的边缘显示出它的年代。|近义词:worn, tattered, ragged, strained|反义词:new, pristine, intact, calm
