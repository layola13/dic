alp:/ælp/ | n. | 高山牧场 | A high mountain pasture. | The cows grazed on the lush alp. 奶牛在茂盛的高山牧场上吃草。| synonyms: pasture, meadow | antonyms: lowland | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Swiss German *Alp, from Old High German alp, meaning "high mountain pasture." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *albiz, meaning "elf," perhaps referring to supernatural beings believed to inhabit mountains.
