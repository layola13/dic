blindstory:/ˈblaɪndˌstɔːri/| n.| 虚构故事，匿名消息来源的故事 |A news story based on information provided by an unnamed source, especially in the context of political reporting, often used to disseminate information without revealing the source's identity.| The journalist published a blindstory about corruption in the government. 记者发表了一篇关于政府腐败的匿名消息来源的报道。|近义词：anonymous source story, off-the-record story|反义词: attributed story, on-the-record story|词性:名词
