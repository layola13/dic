fiduciary:/fɪˈduːʃiəri/ /fɪˈduːʃieri/
| n. | 受托人；托管人 |  A person or organization that acts on behalf of another person or persons, putting their clients' interests ahead of their own, with a duty to preserve good faith and trust. | The lawyer acted as a fiduciary for the estate.  律师担任遗产的受托人。|  trustee, guardian, custodian |  beneficiary (this refers to the person benefiting from the fiduciary relationship, not a true antonym) | noun
fiduciary:/fɪˈduːʃiəri/ /fɪˈduːʃieri/
| adj. | 信托的；受信托的 |  Involving trust, especially with regard to the relationship between a trustee and a beneficiary. |  They have a fiduciary duty to their clients.  他们对客户负有信托责任。| trustee, confidential |  treacherous | adjective
