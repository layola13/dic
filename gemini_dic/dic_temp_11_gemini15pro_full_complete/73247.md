fordry:/ˈfɔːdraɪ/ | adj. | 干透的，完全干燥的 | Completely dry; dried out. | The ground was fordry after the weeks of sunshine.  阳光照射数周后，地面干透了。 |  The towels were fordry after hanging in the sun all day. 毛巾在太阳下晒了一整天，完全干了。 | 近义词: arid, parched, bone-dry | 反义词: wet, damp, soggy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "fordry" is likely a dialectal or archaic term. It's formed from "for-" (completely, thoroughly) + "dry".  "For-" as an intensifier is found in other older words like "forlorn" and "forsake."
