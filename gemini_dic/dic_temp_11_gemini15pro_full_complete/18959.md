bestellen:/bəˈʃtɛlən/
verb
order (something); reserve (a table, tickets, etc.)
to place an order for something; to reserve something.
Ich habe ein neues Auto bestellt. I have ordered a new car.
近义词: order, reserve, book
反义词: cancel
