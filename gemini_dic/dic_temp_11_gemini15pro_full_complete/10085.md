apocynaceous: /əˌpɒsɪˈneɪʃəs/ /əˌpɑːsɪˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
夹竹桃科的
Relating to or characteristic of the Apocynaceae family of plants, which includes dogbane, milkweed, and oleander.  These plants typically have milky sap and often contain poisonous alkaloids.
The oleander is an apocynaceous shrub with beautiful but toxic flowers. 夹竹桃是一种夹竹桃科的灌木，花朵美丽但有毒。
The scientist studied the properties of several apocynaceous plants.  这位科学家研究了几种夹竹桃科植物的特性。
近义词:  dogbane-like, milkweed-like (less precise)
反义词:  (No direct antonym; one might list families contrasting in characteristics, e.g., Rosaceae - rose family)
词性:  adjective
