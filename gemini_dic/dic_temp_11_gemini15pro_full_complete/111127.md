literateness:/ˈlɪtərətnəs/
n.
文化素养；识字；读写能力
The state or quality of being literate; ability to read and write.
例句：The country has a high level of literateness. 这个国家的文化素养很高。

例句：Literateness is essential for personal and societal development. 读写能力对个人和社会发展至关重要。

近义词：literacy, education, knowledge
反义词：illiteracy, ignorance
词性：名词
