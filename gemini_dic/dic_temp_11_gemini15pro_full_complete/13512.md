attainability:/əˌteɪnəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可 attainability, 可获得性，可达到性 |The attainability of his goals was questionable.  他的目标是否能够实现是值得怀疑的。|  The attainability of higher education has increased in recent years. 近年来，高等教育的可获得性有所提高。 | 近义词:achievability, feasibility, possibility | 反义词:unattainability, impossibility | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English attaynable + -ity.  From attain + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *attangō (“I touch against, reach”), from ad (“to”) + tangō (“I touch”).
