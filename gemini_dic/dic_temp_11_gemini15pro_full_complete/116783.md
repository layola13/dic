maverick:/ˈmævərɪk (US)/ /ˈmævərɪk (UK)/
noun|特立独行的人；持不同意见的人；不守常规的人|An independent individual who does not go along with a group or party.|Elon Musk is often considered a maverick in the tech industry.  埃隆·马斯克通常被认为是科技行业的特立独行的人。|individualist, nonconformist, rebel, dissenter|conformist, follower|n.
maverick:/ˈmævərɪk (US)/ /ˈmævərɪk (UK)/
adjective|特立独行的；标新立异的；不墨守成规的|Characterized by independence and unconventionality.|She took a maverick approach to the problem. 她用一种不墨守成规的方法解决了这个问题。|independent, unconventional, unorthodox, original|conventional, orthodox, traditional|adj.
