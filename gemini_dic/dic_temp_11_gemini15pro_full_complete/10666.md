例句：The approvedness of her actions was questioned by some. 她行为的正当性受到一些人的质疑。|近义词：acceptance, approbation, approval, sanction|反义词：disapproval, rejection, condemnation|词性:名词

词源: From approve + -ed + -ness.  源自approve（批准）+ -ed（过去分词后缀）+ -ness（名词后缀）。
