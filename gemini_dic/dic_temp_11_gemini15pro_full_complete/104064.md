jumbled:/ˈdʒʌmbəld/| adj.| 混乱的，杂乱的| mixed up in a confused or disordered way | The wires were all jumbled together. 电线全都缠在一起乱成一团。|近义词：disordered, tangled, mixed-up, chaotic, messy|反义词：orderly, organized, neat, tidy|词性:形容词

词源:  From the verb "jumble," which originated in the late 16th century.  Likely of imitative origin, suggesting a confused mixture of sounds or things.
