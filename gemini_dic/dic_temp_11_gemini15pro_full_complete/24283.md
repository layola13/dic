brigandages:/ˈbrɪɡəndɪdʒɪz/
noun
抢劫行为；盗匪行径
Acts of robbery and violence committed by brigands.
The brigandages committed by the outlaw gang terrorized the countryside.  这伙歹徒的抢劫行为恐吓了整个乡村。
The history books detailed the brigandages of the pirates. 历史书籍详细记载了海盗的抢劫行为。
近义词: robberies, banditry, outlawry, pillaging, plundering
反义词: lawfulness, order, peace
词性:n.
