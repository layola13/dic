extracorporeal:/ˌɛkstrəkɔːrˈpɔːriəl/| adj.|体外的；离体的|Existing or occurring outside the body.|例句：Extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO) is a life-saving technique. 体外膜肺氧合 (ECMO) 是一种救命的技术。

例句：The surgeon performed extracorporeal shock wave lithotripsy to break up the kidney stones. 外科医生进行了体外冲击波碎石术来击碎肾结石。|近义词：ex vivo, in vitro|反义词：in vivo, intracorporeal|词性:形容词
