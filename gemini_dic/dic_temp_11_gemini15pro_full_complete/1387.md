accusants:/əˈkjuːzənts/
noun
原告;控告者;指控者
Those making an accusation; accusers.
The accusants in the case presented compelling evidence.  案件中的原告提供了令人信服的证据。
The judge listened carefully to the testimony of the accusants. 法官仔细聆听了原告的证词。
Synonyms: accusers, complainants, plaintiffs
Antonyms: defendants, accused, respondents
n.
