erythrozyme: /ɪˈrɪθrəʊzaɪm/
n.
红细胞酶
An enzyme that acts on red blood cells.
Erythrozyme plays a crucial role in red blood cell metabolism. 红细胞酶在红细胞代谢中起着至关重要的作用。
Erythrozyme deficiency can lead to various hematological disorders. 红细胞酶缺乏会导致各种血液疾病。
Synonym:  hematinase (this is a close but not perfect synonym, as it refers specifically to enzymes that break down heme)
Antonym:  None (there isn't a direct opposite for an enzyme that acts on red blood cells)
