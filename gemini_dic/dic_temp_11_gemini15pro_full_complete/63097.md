enshield:/ɛnˈʃiːld/
verb
保护，庇护
to protect or shield, as with a shield.
The knight enshielded the princess from the dragon's fiery breath. 骑士用盾牌保护公主免受火龙的火焰气息。
The heavy cloak enshielded him from the cold wind. 厚重的斗篷保护他免受寒风侵袭。
近义词: protect, shield, defend, safeguard
反义词: expose, endanger
词性: verb

词源:
From en- + shield.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "to put into or onto" or "to cause to be."  Therefore, "enshield" literally means "to put into or onto a shield" or "to cause to be shielded."
