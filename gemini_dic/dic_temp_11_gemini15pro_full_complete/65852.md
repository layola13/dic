eviternity:/iːvɪˈtɜːrnɪti/ | n. | 永恒, 无尽的时期 | An endless or seemingly endless period of time; eternity. | The universe stretches into eviternity. 宇宙延伸至永恒。|  He contemplated the eviternity of space. 他沉思着浩瀚无垠的宇宙。| 近义词：eternity, perpetuity, infinity | 反义词:temporality, finitude | 词性:名词

词源:  From Latin *aeviternitas*, from *aeviternus* ("eternal") + *-itas* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  *aeviternus* is from *aevum* (“age, lifetime, eternity”) + *-ternus* (suffix forming adjectives of time).
