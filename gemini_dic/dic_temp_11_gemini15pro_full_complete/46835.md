daphnomancy: /ˈdæfnəmænsi/ | n. | 月桂占卜 | Divination by means of the rustling of laurel leaves.  It's a form of divination where the rustling of laurel leaves, usually in response to fire or wind, is interpreted to predict the future or answer a question. |  Ancient priests practiced daphnomancy to seek guidance from the gods. 古代祭司通过月桂占卜来寻求神的指引。

Daphnomancy was a common practice in ancient Greece. 月桂占卜在古希腊是一种常见的做法。| 近义词: divination, soothsaying, augury | 反义词:  None (lack of a specific antonym for this specialized divination method) | 词性: noun
