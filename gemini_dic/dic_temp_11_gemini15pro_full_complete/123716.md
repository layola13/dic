moonlighter:/ˈmuːnˌlaɪtər/| n.|兼职者，夜间工作者|A person who holds a second job in addition to their primary employment, often working at night.|例句：He's a moonlighter – he works at a bar in the evenings to supplement his income.  他是一个兼职者——晚上在酒吧工作以补贴收入。

例句：Many teachers are moonlighters, taking on extra work to make ends meet. 许多教师都是兼职者，从事额外的工作以维持生计。|近义词：part-timer, freelancer|反义词:full-timer|词性:noun


moonlight:/ˈmuːnˌlaɪt/| v. |兼职，从事第二职业|To work at a second job in addition to one's regular employment.|例句：She moonlights as a waitress on weekends. 她周末兼职做服务员。

例句：He moonlighted as a security guard to pay for his education.  他兼职做保安来支付他的教育费用。|近义词：work part-time, freelance|反义词:work full-time|词性:verb
