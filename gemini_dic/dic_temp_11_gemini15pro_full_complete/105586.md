kindless:/ˈkaɪndləs/| adj.|无情的，冷酷的，不友善的|Lacking kindness; unkind, cruel, or unsympathetic.|He gave a kindless laugh, showing no remorse. 他冷酷地笑了笑，毫无悔意。|近义词：cruel, heartless, merciless, ruthless, unkind, unsympathetic|反义词: kind, compassionate, merciful, sympathetic|词性:形容词

词源: kind + -less.  "Kind" comes from Old English "gecynde" meaning "natural, native, innate."  The suffix "-less" is also of Old English origin and means "without."  Therefore, "kindless" literally means "without kindness."
