n.
畜牧学家；家畜饲养者
People who study or are skilled in the raising and care of livestock.
Bestiarists have played a vital role in developing improved breeds of cattle. 畜牧学家在改良牛的品种方面发挥了至关重要的作用。
The conference attracted bestiarists from all over the world. 这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的畜牧专家。
Synonyms: animal breeders, livestock farmers, stockmen, herdsmen
