adenosclerosis: /əˌdiːnoʊskləˈroʊsɪs/
n.
腺硬化；腺体硬化
Hardening of a gland, especially a lymph node.
Adenosclerosis can sometimes be a sign of underlying disease. 腺硬化有时可能是潜在疾病的征兆。
近义词:  glandular sclerosis, induration
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) glandular atrophy
词性: noun
