hypophalangy:/haɪpəʊfəˈlændʒi/| n. |指骨减少，指骨发育不全|A congenital condition characterized by fewer than the normal number of phalanges in the fingers or toes.|The child was born with hypophalangy, having only two phalanges in his little finger.|Near Synonyms: brachydactyly, oligodactyly|Antonym: polyphalangy|noun
