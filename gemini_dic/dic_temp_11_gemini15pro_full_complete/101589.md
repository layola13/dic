irradiates:/ɪˈreɪdieɪts/
verb
照射，辐射；照亮；使焕发
to shine light on something; to expose something to radiation
The sun irradiates the earth. 太阳照射着地球。
synonyms: illuminate, shine on, beam on
antonyms: darken, obscure
词性: 动词

irradiate:/ɪˈreɪdieɪt/
verb
照射，辐射；照亮；使焕发
to shine light on something; to expose something to radiation
The lamp irradiated the room. 灯照亮了房间。
synonyms: light up, brighten
antonyms: dim, shadow
词性: 动词
