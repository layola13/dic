echo's: /ˈekəʊz/ (UK) /ˈekoʊz/ (US) | n. | 回声，回响; 仿效，附和 |  A sound or series of sounds caused by the reflection of sound waves from a surface back to the listener.  A close repetition or imitation of someone else's words, actions, or opinions. | The canyon walls amplified the echo's of our voices. 峡谷的墙壁放大了我们声音的回声。 | synonyms: reverberation, resonance, repetition, imitation | antonyms: original, silence | noun

echo's: /ˈekəʊz/ (UK) /ˈekoʊz/ (US)| v. | 回响，发出回声；重复，模仿 |  To resound or produce an echo. To repeat or imitate (someone's words, actions, or opinions). | The empty hall echoed with his footsteps. 空旷的大厅回荡着他的脚步声。 | synonyms: resound, reverberate, repeat, mimic | antonyms: originate, silence | verb
