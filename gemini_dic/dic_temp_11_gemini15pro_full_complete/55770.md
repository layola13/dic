diversify:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪ/ /dɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪ/| v. |使多样化，多元化|To make or become more diverse or varied; to expand into a range of products or investments.|例句：The company is looking to diversify its product range. 这家公司正在寻求使其产品范围多样化。

例句：Farmers are being encouraged to diversify their crops. 农民们被鼓励种植多样化的作物。


近义词：vary, expand, broaden, branch out|反义词: concentrate, specialize, focus|词性:动词
