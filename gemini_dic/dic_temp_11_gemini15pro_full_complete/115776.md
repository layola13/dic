marinids:/ˈmærɪnɪdz/| n.|马里尼德王朝|A Berber Muslim dynasty that ruled Morocco from the 13th to the 15th century.|The Marinids oversaw a period of great cultural flourishing in Morocco.|近义词:Benimerin dynasty (another name for the same dynasty)|反义词:None (dynasties don't have direct opposites)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *Marinides*, from Arabic *marīnīyūn*, plural of *marīnī*, meaning "of or relating to Marin", a Berber tribe.
