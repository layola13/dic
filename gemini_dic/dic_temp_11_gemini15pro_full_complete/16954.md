basso:/ˈbæsoʊ/ (US), /ˈbæsəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|男低音；低音的|A low male voice or vocal part, or the lowest part in musical harmony.|The basso in the choir has a rich, resonant voice. 合唱团里的男低音音色浑厚而洪亮。|近义词：bass, baritone|反义词：soprano, tenor|词性: 名词，形容词

Etymology: From Italian *basso*, from Late Latin *bassus* "low, short," perhaps of imitative origin.
