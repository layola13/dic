enamoured:/ɪˈnæmərd/ | adj. | 迷恋的，倾心的 | Filled with love and admiration. | She was completely enamoured with the handsome actor. 她完全迷恋上了这位英俊的演员。| Synonyms: captivated, infatuated, charmed | Antonyms: indifferent, apathetic | adjective

enamoured of/with:/ɪˈnæmərd ɒv/ /ɪˈnæmərd wɪð/ | adj. | 迷恋的，倾心的 | Filled with love and admiration for. | He was deeply enamoured of his new car. 他深深地迷恋着他的新车。| Synonyms: captivated by, infatuated with, charmed by | Antonyms: indifferent to, apathetic towards | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *enamoré, past participle of enamer (“to inflame with love”), from en- (“in, into”) + amor (“love”), from Latin amor (“love”).
