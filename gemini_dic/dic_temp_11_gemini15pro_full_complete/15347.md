backstitch: /ˈbækˌstɪtʃ/
backstitch: n.
backstitch: 回针；倒缝
backstitch: A sewing stitch made by inserting the needle back through the material beyond the preceding stitch and then bringing it out in front of that stitch, so that the stitches overlap.
backstitch: I used a backstitch to reinforce the seam. 我用回针加固了接缝。

backstitch: v.
backstitch: 回针；倒缝
backstitch: To sew with a backstitch.
backstitch:  She backstitched the edges of the fabric to prevent fraying. 她用回针缝了织物的边缘以防止磨损。

backstitch: 近义词: oversew, overcast
backstitch: 反义词: None

词源:
From back + stitch.  "Back" referring to the direction of the needle movement.
