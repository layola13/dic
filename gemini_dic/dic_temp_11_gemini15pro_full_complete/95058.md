inadequately: /ɪnˈædɪkwɪtli/
| adv. |不足地，不充分地|Not enough; insufficiently.  It refers to something not being done or provided to the necessary or expected extent. | The issue was inadequately addressed in the report.  这份报告没有充分讨论这个问题。|  The building was inadequately heated during the winter.  这栋楼冬天供暖不足。 | insufficiently, poorly, improperly | sufficiently, adequately, competently | adverb
