houstonia:/huːˈstoʊniə/ | n. | 休斯顿草，蓝眼草 | A genus of delicate North American herbs (family Rubiaceae) with small, usually blue, flowers.  A type of small flowering plant, typically with blue flowers, native to North America. | Example:  The houstonia bloomed in early spring, carpeting the woodland floor in blue. 休斯顿草在早春开花，将林地铺成一片蓝色。

Example: She admired the delicate beauty of the houstonia growing by the stream. 她欣赏着溪边生长的休斯顿草的精致之美。 | 近义词: bluets | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: Named after William Houstoun (1695 – 1733), a Scottish surgeon and botanist who collected plants in the West Indies, Mexico, and South America.
