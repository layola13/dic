dewire:/diːˈwaɪər/
verb
拆除线路，使断开连接，戒除
To disconnect or remove the wires from something, especially a bomb or explosive device; to free oneself from an addiction or habit.
The bomb squad carefully dewired the explosive device.  拆弹小组小心翼翼地拆除了爆炸装置的线路。
He's trying to dewire himself from his caffeine addiction. 他正试图戒掉咖啡因瘾。
近义词: disconnect, dismantle, sever, wean
反义词: connect, wire, install
词性: verb

dewire:/diːˈwaɪər/
verb
去除控制，使摆脱控制
To remove the control or influence of someone or something.
The cult leader dewired his followers from their families and friends.  邪教头目切断了他的追随者与家人和朋友的联系，使他们摆脱了控制。
The therapy helped her dewire from the negative thought patterns that had been holding her back. 治疗帮助她摆脱了阻碍她的消极思维模式。
近义词: liberate, free, emancipate
反义词: control, manipulate, influence
词性: verb
