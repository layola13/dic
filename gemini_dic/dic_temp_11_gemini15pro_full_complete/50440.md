deprecation:/ˌdeprəˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 贬低；反对；轻视 |  The act of expressing disapproval of something.  The software documentation contains many deprecations, warning programmers about functions that will be removed in the future. | 近义词: disapproval, criticism, objection | 反义词: approval, praise, commendation | 词性: 名词

deprecations:/ˌdeprəˈkeɪʃnz/ | n. | 贬低；反对；轻视（deprecation 的复数形式） | Multiple instances of expressing disapproval of something. The latest release includes several deprecations of older APIs. | 近义词: criticisms, objections | 反义词: approvals, praises | 词性: 名词
