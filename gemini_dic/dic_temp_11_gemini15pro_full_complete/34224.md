clanjamfrie: /klænˈdʒæmfri/
noun
喧闹的聚会，一群人；乌合之众
A noisy gathering of people; a disorderly crowd.
The party devolved into a real clanjamfrie after midnight.  午夜过后，派对变成了一场真正的喧闹聚会。
The market was a clanjamfrie of shoppers and vendors. 市场里挤满了购物者和小贩，一片混乱。
近义词:rabble, throng, mob, crowd
反义词:order, peace, quiet
