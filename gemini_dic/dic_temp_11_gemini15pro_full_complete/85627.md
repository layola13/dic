PHRASE->
1. haul something up:  拉起某物  (Haul up the anchor! 起锚！)
2. haul something in:  拉进某物 (Haul in the fishing nets! 收网！)
3. haul something over the coals:  严厉斥责某人  (He was hauled over the coals for his mistake. 他因为犯错而受到了严厉的斥责。)
4. haul someone over:  把某人带到（通常指警察局）(The police hauled him over for speeding. 警察因为他超速行驶把他带走了。)
5. haul ass:  快速离开 (We need to haul ass if we want to catch the train. 如果我们想赶上火车，就得赶紧走。)
