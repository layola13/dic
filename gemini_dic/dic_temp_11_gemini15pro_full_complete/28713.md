carman:/ˈkɑːrmən/| n. |车辆修理工；电车司机|A person whose job is to repair or maintain railroad cars, streetcars, or other vehicles.  A person who operates a streetcar. |例句：The carman quickly diagnosed the problem with the brakes. 车辆修理工很快诊断出了刹车问题。

例句：The carman announced the next stop on the streetcar. 电车司机宣布了下一站。|近义词：mechanic, repairman, driver, operator|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps passenger if referring to a streetcar operator) |词性:noun
