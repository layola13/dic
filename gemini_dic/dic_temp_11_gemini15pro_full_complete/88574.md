hithe:/haɪð/ | n. | 码头；港湾；船坞 | A landing place or small port for boats or ships. | The fishermen returned to the hithe after a long day at sea. 渔夫们在海上度过漫长的一天后回到了码头。| 近义词: wharf, dock, pier, harbor, port | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

词源: Old English *hȳð, from Proto-Germanic *hūði-, from Proto-Indo-European *keu- (“to hide, conceal”). Cognate with Old Frisian hith (“harbor”), Middle Dutch hijte (“dam, dike”).  The original meaning was probably "a sheltered place."
