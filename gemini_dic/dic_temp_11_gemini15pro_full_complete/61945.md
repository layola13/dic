emulsifiability:/ɪˌmʌlsɪfaɪəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 乳化性；乳化能力 | The emulsifiability of a substance is its ability to form or maintain an emulsion.  | The emulsifiability of this new oil is excellent.  这种新油的乳化性非常好。 |  synonyms: emulsifying capacity, emulsion stability | antonyms: immiscibility, non-emulsifying property | noun
