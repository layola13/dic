exothecium:/ˌeksoʊˈθiːsiəm/
noun
外壁
The outer layer of a sporangium or spore case in mosses, ferns, and fungi.
The exothecium of the moss capsule is composed of thick-walled cells.  苔藓孢蒴的外壁由厚壁细胞组成。
Synonyms: outer wall, epidermal layer
Antonyms: endothecium
