outbabbles:/ˌaʊtˈbæbəlz/
|v.|胜过(某人)的讲话；说得比…多；比…说得更流利|to talk more, longer, or more persuasively than someone else|He outbabbled her in the debate. 他在辩论中说得比她多，胜过了她。|Synonyms: outtalk, outspeak|Antonyms: be quiet, listen|verb

outbabbles:/ˌaʊtˈbæbəlz/
|v.|发出比…更多的声音；发出比…更响亮的声音|To produce more sounds or louder sounds than someone or something else.|The waterfall outbabbled the nearby stream.  瀑布的声音盖过了附近的小溪。|Synonyms: drown out, overpower|Antonyms: be quiet, be silent|verb
