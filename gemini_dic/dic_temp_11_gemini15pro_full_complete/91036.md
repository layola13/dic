huskiness:/ˈhʌskɪnəs/| n. |沙哑，粗糙，干涩|A rough or hoarse quality of the voice, often caused by illness or emotion. | Her voice had a huskiness that hinted at tears. 她的声音有些沙哑，似乎快要哭出来了。|The huskiness of his voice added to his air of mystery. 他沙哑的声音更增添了他的神秘感。|近义词：hoarseness, roughness, raspiness|反义词:smoothness, clarity|词性:名词
