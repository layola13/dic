absurdities: /əbˈsɜːrdɪtiz/ /æbˈsɜːrdɪtiz/
noun
荒谬；谬论；荒诞（absurdity 的复数）
The play was full of absurdities.  这出戏充满了荒谬之处。
The idea that the earth is flat is an absurdity.  认为地球是平的想法很荒谬。
Synonyms: follies, silliness, nonsense
Antonyms: sense, logic, reason
词性: 名词


absurdity: /əbˈsɜːrdɪti/ /æbˈsɜːrdɪti/
noun
荒谬；谬论；荒诞
It’s an absurdity to think you can learn a language in a week.  认为你可以在一周内学会一门语言是很荒谬的。
The sheer absurdity of the situation made us laugh.  情况的荒唐程度让我们笑了。
Synonyms: folly, silliness, nonsense
Antonyms: sense, logic, reason
词性: 名词
