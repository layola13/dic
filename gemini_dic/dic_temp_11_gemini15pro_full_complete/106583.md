kubachi:/kuːˈbɑːtʃiː/ | n. |库巴奇（一种高加索地区的传统匕首）| Kubachi is a type of Caucasian dagger, known for its elaborate silverwork and intricate designs. |  He showed off his antique kubachi, its silver hilt gleaming in the light. 他炫耀着他的古董库巴奇匕首，银色的刀柄在灯光下闪闪发光。|  Synonym: dagger, knife | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "peace symbol" in a figurative sense) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Kubachi, a village in Dagestan, Russia, renowned for its metalworking traditions.
