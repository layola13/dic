grand-mal:/ˈɡrænd mɑːl/ (US), /ˈɡrɒn mæl/ (UK)| n. |大发作，强直阵挛性发作|A type of seizure that involves a loss of consciousness and violent muscle contractions.|He suffers from grand-mal seizures.|Synonym: tonic-clonic seizure|Antonym: absence seizure|Noun

grand-mals:/ˈɡrænd mɑːlz/ (US), /ˈɡrɒn mælz/ (UK)| n. |大发作（复数）|Plural form of grand-mal, referring to multiple instances of this type of seizure.|She has experienced several grand-mals in the past year.|Synonym: tonic-clonic seizures|Antonym: absence seizures|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French grand mal, literally "big sickness."
