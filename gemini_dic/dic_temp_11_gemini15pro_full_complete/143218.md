outculling: /ˈaʊtkʌlɪŋ/ | n. |剔除，淘汰| The process of removing inferior or undesirable individuals from a group, especially a breeding population, in order to improve the overall quality. |  Farmers practiced outculling to improve the milk production of their cows.  农民通过剔除劣质奶牛来提高牛奶产量。 |  culling, weeding out, selection |  inclusion | noun
