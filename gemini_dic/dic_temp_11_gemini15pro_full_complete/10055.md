apnoea:/æpˈniːə/ /æpˈniː.ə/ | n. |窒息，呼吸暂停|Temporary cessation of breathing, especially during sleep. |例句：The patient suffered from sleep apnoea.  病人患有睡眠呼吸暂停。

例句：Infant apnoea can be a serious condition. 婴儿窒息可能是一种严重的疾病。

近义词：asphyxia, suffocation |反义词: breathing, respiration |词性: noun
