circle: /ˈsɜːrkl/ (British) /ˈsɜːrkəl/ (American)| n. v. |圆圈；循环；圈子；集团|A round plane figure whose boundary (the circumference) consists of points equidistant from a fixed point (the center). To move in a circle, especially around something.|The children sat in a circle. 孩子们围成一圈坐着。|He circled the block twice. 他绕着街区开了两圈。|近义词：loop, ring, cycle, group, club |反义词: line, square|noun, verb
