apogeotropism:/ˌæpoʊˌdʒiːəˈtrɒpɪzəm/| n. | 背地性；负向地性 | The upward growth of plant stems is an example of negative geotropism, also called apogeotropism.  植物茎的向上生长是负向地性的一个例子，也称为背地性。|  The branches of the tree exhibit strong apogeotropism, reaching towards the sunlight.  这棵树的树枝表现出强烈的背地性，向着阳光生长。| 近义词: negative geotropism | 反义词: geotropism, positive geotropism | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *ἀπό* (apó, “away from, off”) + *γῆ* (gê, “earth”) + *τρόπος* (trópos, “a turn, way, manner”) + *-ism*.
