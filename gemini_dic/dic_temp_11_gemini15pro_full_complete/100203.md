interresponsible:/ˌɪntərɪˈspɒnsəbl/ | adj. | 共同负责的，互负责任的 | Sharing or involving mutual responsibility; accountable to one another. | Example: The team members are interresponsible for the project's success. 团队成员对项目的成功负有共同的责任。| Example:  In a truly interresponsible society, everyone feels a sense of ownership for the collective well-being. 在一个真正互负责任的社会中，每个人都对集体福祉有主人翁意识。| 近义词: mutually responsible, jointly accountable | 反义词: unaccountable, irresponsible | 词性: adjective

词源:
inter- (prefix): between, among, mutually
responsible (adjective): having an obligation to do something, or having control over or care for someone, as part of one's job or role.
