buckshot:/ˈbʌkʃɒt/ (UK) /ˈbʌkʃɑːt/ (US)| n. |鹿弹，霰弹|A type of large lead shot used in shotgun shells, designed to scatter widely upon firing.|The hunter loaded his shotgun with buckshot. 猎人往他的猎枪里装了鹿弹。|Near synonyms:  shot, pellets|Antonym: slug|noun


buckshots:/ˈbʌkʃɒts/ (UK) /ˈbʌkʃɑːts/ (US)| n. |鹿弹，霰弹（复数）|Multiple pieces of buckshot.  This refers to more than one piece of this type of ammunition.|He fired two rounds of buckshots at the target. 他朝目标发射了两发霰弹。|Near synonyms: shots, pellets|Antonym: slugs|noun
