clipboards:/ˈklɪpbɔːrdz/
n.
剪贴板
A small board with a clip at the top for holding papers.
The doctor wrote some notes on his clipboard. 医生在他的剪贴板上写了一些笔记。
synonyms: writing board, writing pad
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical object. Could consider digital note-taking devices as a functional antonym.)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "clip" (a device for holding things together) + "board" (a flat piece of wood or other material).
