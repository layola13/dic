homey:/ˈhəʊmi/ (British) /ˈhoʊmi/ (American)| adj.|舒适的，温馨的，像家一样的|Suggesting the pleasant qualities of home; cozy and comfortable.|例句：The room had a homey atmosphere, with a crackling fire in the hearth. 房间里有一种家的温馨氛围，壁炉里的火噼啪作响。|例句：She decorated the apartment to make it feel homey. 她把公寓装饰得很有家的感觉。|近义词：cozy, comfortable, familiar, inviting, warm|反义词：uncomfortable, cold, impersonal, uninviting|词性:形容词
homey:/ˈhəʊmi/ (British) /ˈhoʊmi/ (American)| n.|老乡，同乡|A person from one's hometown or region.|例句：He met a few homeys at the conference. 他在会议上遇到几个老乡。|例句：It's always nice to see a homey when you're far from home.  当你远离家乡时，见到老乡总是很高兴的。|近义词：compatriot, fellow countryman|反义词：foreigner, stranger|词性:名词
