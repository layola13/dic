mislearned:/ˌmɪsˈlɜːrnd/ | /ˌmɪsˈlɜːrnt/
adjective
错误地学习的；学错的
Learned incorrectly; acquired wrongly.
He had mislearned the formula, leading to incorrect calculations. 他学错了公式，导致计算错误。
Synonyms:  misunderstood, misconstrued, misapprehended
Antonyms: learned, mastered, understood
词性: 形容词
