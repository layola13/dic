exannulate: /ɛksˈænjʊleɪt/ | adj. | 无环的，没有环纹的 | Lacking an annulus or ring; having the rings absent or obsolete. | The exannulate fern species is distinct from its ringed counterparts.  这种无环蕨类物种与其有环纹的对应物种截然不同。|  Near synonyms: ringless, unringed | Antonyms: annulate, ringed | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exannulatus*, from ex- (out of, from) + annulātus (ringed), past participle of annulāre (to furnish with a ring), from anulus (ring).
