hubba:/ˈhʌbə/| int.|哇！; (表示惊讶、兴奋或欲望)|An exclamation expressing surprise, excitement, or desire, often related to attractiveness.  It's frequently used in combination with "hubba hubba."|例句：Hubba hubba! Look at her! 哇！看看她！

例句：Hubba! That's a cool car. 哇！那辆车真酷。

近义词：wow, whoa, hey, oh my, |反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's an exclamation)


词性:感叹词
