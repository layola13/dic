kitsch:/kɪtʃ/ | n. adj. | 庸俗的艺术；粗劣的艺术品；廉价而俗气的艺术品 | Art, objects, or design considered to be in poor taste because of excessive garishness or sentimentality, but sometimes appreciated in an ironic or knowing way. |  The souvenir shop was full of kitsch. 那家纪念品商店里摆满了庸俗的艺术品。| Synonyms: tawdry, garish, cheap, vulgar, tasteless | Antonyms: tasteful, sophisticated, refined, elegant, classic | noun, adjective

kitsch:/kɪtʃ/ | adj. | 庸俗的；粗劣的；廉价而俗气的 |  Having or displaying qualities of kitsch. | The decor was a bizarre mix of antique and kitsch.  这儿的装饰是古董和庸俗艺术品的古怪组合。| Synonyms: tawdry, garish, cheap, vulgar, tasteless | Antonyms: tasteful, sophisticated, refined, elegant, classic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Kitsch*, probably from *verkitschen* "to make cheap," perhaps related to *kitschen* "to smear, to make a mess."
