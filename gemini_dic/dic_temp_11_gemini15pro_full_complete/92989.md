idiomatic:/ˌɪdiəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 惯用的；符合语言习惯的；含有习语的 | Characteristic of or containing idioms; natural and fluent. |  She speaks idiomatic English. 她讲一口地道的英语。|  natural, fluent, colloquial, standard | stilted, unnatural, awkward | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *idiomaticus, from Ancient Greek ἰδιωματικός (idiomatikós, “peculiar, individual”), from ἴδιος (ídios, “one's own”) + -ματικός (-matikós).
