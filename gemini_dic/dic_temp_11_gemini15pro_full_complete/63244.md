enterprise: /ˈentərpraɪz/ (US) /ˈentəpraɪz/ (UK)| n.| 企业；事业；进取心；事业心|A project or undertaking, typically one that is difficult or requires effort; initiative and resourcefulness.|She showed great enterprise in setting up her own business. 她创办自己的企业展现了非凡的进取心。|近义词: venture, undertaking, initiative, ambition|反义词: apathy, inertia, laziness|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French entreprise "undertaking," from entreprendre "undertake," from entre- "between" + prendre "to take."
