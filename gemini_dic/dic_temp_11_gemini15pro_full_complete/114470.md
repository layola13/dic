makkanon: /mɑːkˈænən/ | n. | 马卡农(法式小圆饼) | A small, round, brightly colored meringue-based confection filled with buttercream, ganache, or jam. |例句：The bakery is famous for its delicious makkarons. 这家面包店以其美味的马卡龙而闻名。|例句：She gave me a box of assorted makkarons as a gift. 她送给我一盒什锦马卡龙作为礼物。| 近义词: macaron, macaroon | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a type of food) | 词性: 名词


词源: French *macaron*, from Italian *maccarone* "macaroon, dumpling," from *maccare* "to crush, beat," probably from Vulgar Latin *mattiare* "to hammer." Ultimately of Germanic origin.
