enfilade:/ˌɛn.fɪˈleɪd/ /ˌɑːn.fɪˈleɪd/| n. vt.|纵射，扫射；一览无余|A volley of gunfire directed along the length of a body of troops or a line of fortifications.  To rake with gunfire along a line.|The troops were caught in an enfilade and suffered heavy casualties. 部队遭到纵射，伤亡惨重。|近义词：rake, sweep, bombard|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "protect," "shield")|noun, verb


enfilade:/ˌɛn.fɪˈleɪd/ /ˌɑːn.fɪˈleɪd/| n. vt.|一览无余，全部看完|A clear view along the entire length of something.|From the top of the hill, we had an enfilade of the valley.  从山顶上，我们可以一览无余地看到山谷。|近义词：panorama, vista, overview|反义词: obstructed view|noun, verb
