nondeceleration:/ˌnɒndiːˌsɛləˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 非减速，不减速 | Absence or lack of deceleration; maintaining a constant speed or accelerating. | The nondeceleration of the vehicle caused the accident. 车辆没有减速导致了事故。| The nondeceleration of the economy is a good sign. 经济没有减速是一个好兆头。 | 近义词: constant speed, acceleration | 反义词: deceleration, slowing down | 词性: 名词

词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "deceleration" (from "decelerate," meaning "to reduce speed").  "Decelerate" comes from "de-" (prefix meaning "down" or "reduce") + "celerare" (Latin, meaning "to hasten").
