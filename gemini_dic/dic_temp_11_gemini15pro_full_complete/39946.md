constructionally:/kənˈstrʌkʃənəli/
adv.
根据结构地，构造上地
From a constructional standpoint, the bridge is a masterpiece. 从构造上来说，这座桥是件杰作。
English Explanation: In a way that relates to the structure or way something is built or put together.
Example: Constructionally, the building is sound.  构造上，这座建筑很坚固。
Example: The architect explained the building's features constructionally. 建筑师从结构上解释了这座建筑的特征。
Synonym: structurally, architecturally
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider using phrases like "poorly designed" or "faultily constructed" to express the opposite meaning)
Word Class: Adverb
