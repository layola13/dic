haan:/hɑːn/ | interj. | 是, 对 (Informal, chiefly South Asian) |  An informal way of saying yes, used especially in South Asian cultures.  It can also express agreement, understanding, or encouragement similar to "yeah" or "okay." | Example 1: "Haan, I understand." (是的，我明白了。)

Example 2: "Haan, you're right." (对，你是对的。) | Synonym: yes, yeah, okay, right | Antonym: no | Part of Speech: Interjection
