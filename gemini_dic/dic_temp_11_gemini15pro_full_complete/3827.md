agonising:/ˈæɡənaɪzɪŋ/ (US) /ˈæɡənaɪzɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|极度痛苦的，折磨人的|Causing great physical or mental pain.|例句：She described the agonizing process of chemotherapy. 她描述了化疗的痛苦过程。|例句：It was an agonizing decision to leave her family. 离开她的家人是一个痛苦的决定。|近义词：excruciating, tormenting, harrowing|反义词：pleasant, enjoyable|词性:形容词

agonize:/ˈæɡənaɪz/ (US) /ˈæɡənaɪz/ (UK)| v.| (为…)感到极度痛苦；苦苦思索|To worry or think about something very seriously.|例句：He agonized over whether to accept the job offer. 他苦苦思索是否要接受这份工作。|例句：She agonized for days about what to do. 她为该怎么办苦恼了好几天。|近义词：worry, fret, anguish|反义词：relax, disregard|词性:动词
