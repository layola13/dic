misproposing:/ˌmɪsprəˈpoʊzɪŋ/
verb
错误地提议；提出不当的建议
making or stating a proposal that is wrong, inappropriate, or ill-advised.
He was misproposing solutions that were completely impractical. 他错误地提出了一些完全不切实际的解决方案。
Misproposing marriage to someone you barely know is usually a bad idea. 向一个你几乎不认识的人求婚通常是个坏主意。
synonyms: misrepresenting, misstating, misconstruing
antonyms: proposing correctly, suggesting appropriately
