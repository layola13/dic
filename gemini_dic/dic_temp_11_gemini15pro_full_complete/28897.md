carrack:/ˈkærək/ | n. | 大型帆船，卡拉克帆船 | A large sailing ship of the 15th to the 17th centuries, used for trade and war, with three or four masts and a high sterncastle. | Columbus sailed to the New World in a carrack. 哥伦布乘坐一艘卡拉克帆船航行到新大陆。| The carrack was heavily armed and capable of long voyages. 这艘卡拉克帆船装备精良，能够进行长途航行。| 近义词：galleon, ship | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑：dinghy, raft) | 词性: 名词
