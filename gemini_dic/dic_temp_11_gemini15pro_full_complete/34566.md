clearances:/ˈklɪərənsɪz/
n.
许可，批准
The act of clearing or the state of being cleared.
The pilot requested clearance for takeoff.  飞行员请求起飞许可。
permission, authorization
obstruction, blockage
noun

clearance:/ˈklɪərənsɪz/
n.
清除，清理
The act of removing something.
The city organized a clearance of the debris after the storm.  暴风雨过后，市里组织清理残骸。
removal, clearing
accumulation, retention
noun


clearance:/ˈklɪərənsɪz/
n.
间隙，余隙
The space between two objects.
The low bridge has limited clearance for tall vehicles.  这座矮桥对高大车辆的通行高度有限。
gap, space
obstruction, blockage
noun


clearance:/ˈklɪərənsɪz/
n.

The sale of goods at reduced prices to clear out stock.
The store is having a clearance sale on winter clothing.  这家商店正在清仓甩卖冬装。
sale, closeout
retention
noun


clearance:/ˈklɪərənsɪz/
n.
(官方的)批准，许可
Official permission to do something.

noun
