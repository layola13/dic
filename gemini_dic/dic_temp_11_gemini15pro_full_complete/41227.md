coralligenous:/ˌkɒrəlɪˈdʒɛnəs/
adjective
珊瑚的；由珊瑚形成的；产珊瑚的
Produced by or containing coral; designating a hard calcareous deposit built up from the remains of certain red algae and forming a habitat for many invertebrates.
The coralligenous reefs of the Mediterranean Sea are biodiversity hotspots. 地中海的珊瑚礁是生物多样性的热点地区。
Divers explored the vibrant coralligenous formations. 潜水员探索了充满活力的珊瑚构造。
Synonyms: coralline, coral-forming
Antonyms: abiotic, non-coral
