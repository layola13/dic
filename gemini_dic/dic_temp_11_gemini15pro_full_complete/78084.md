geofencing:/ˈdʒiːoʊfɛnsɪŋ/
|n.|地理围栏，地理围栏技术|A virtual perimeter for a real-world geographic area. A geofence could be dynamically generated—as in a radius around a store or point location. Or a geofence can be a predefined set of boundaries, like school attendance zones or neighborhood boundaries.|The store uses geofencing to send coupons to customers' smartphones when they are nearby.|近义词:virtual perimeter, location-based service|反义词:N/A|名词
