beget:/bɪˈɡet/ /biːˈɡet/| v.|产生；引起；生育|To cause something to happen or exist; to procreate, especially to father (a child).|例句：
Poverty often begets crime.
贫穷常常滋生犯罪。

He begot three sons and two daughters.
他生了三个儿子和两个女儿。

|近义词：create, generate, produce, father, sire|反义词:prevent, stop, obstruct|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *begietan "acquire, obtain, seize; procreate," from be- + gietan "to get."  The sense of "procreate" became the main one by late 12c.
