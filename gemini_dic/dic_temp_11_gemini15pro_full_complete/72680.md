foamlike:/ˈfoʊmlaɪk/| adj.|泡沫状的，像泡沫的|Resembling foam in texture or appearance.  Having the light and airy consistency of foam.|The cake had a light, foamlike texture.  蛋糕有着轻盈的泡沫状质地。|近义词：foamy, frothy, bubbly, spongy|反义词：dense, compact, solid|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From foam + -like.  "Foam" comes from the Old English "fām," meaning "foam, froth." The suffix "-like" is used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
