mesodermic: /ˌmezəˈdɜːrmɪk/ | adj. | 中胚层的; 由中胚层发育而来的 | Relating to or derived from the mesoderm (the middle layer of cells or tissue of an embryo in early development, which gives rise to bones, muscles, connective tissue, etc.) | The mesodermic layer gives rise to the circulatory system. 中胚层会发育为循环系统。|  The heart is a mesodermic organ. 心脏是一个中胚层器官。 |  mesoblastic | ectodermic, endodermic | adjective
