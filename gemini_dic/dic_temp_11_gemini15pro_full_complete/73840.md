formlessnesses:/ˈfɔːmləsnəsɪz/
n.
无形；无定形；缺乏形式
The state or quality of having no definite shape or form; lack of organization or structure.
The artist attempted to capture the formlessnesses of the swirling mist. 艺术家试图捕捉旋转的薄雾的无形状态。
The formlessnesses of his early compositions gradually gave way to a more structured style. 他早期作品的无形逐渐被更有条理的风格所取代。
shapelessness, amorphousness, lack of structure, lack of organization
form, structure, organization, definition
