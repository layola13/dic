nuncupation:/nʌŋkjʊˈpeɪʃən/ | n. |口头遗嘱；口头指定；口头声明|The act of declaring or making something known orally, especially a will or testament.  A formal oral declaration.| He made a nuncupation of his wishes on his deathbed. 他在临终前口头宣布了他的遗愿。|近义词：declaration, pronouncement, testament |反义词:  written statement, written will | noun
