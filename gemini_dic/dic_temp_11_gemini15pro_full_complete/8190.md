anti-dissident:/ˌæntiˈdɪsɪdənt/
adjective|反异议的，压制异见的|Opposed to dissent or dissidents; used to describe actions or policies that suppress political opposition.|The government implemented anti-dissident policies to silence critics. 政府实施了反异议人士的政策以压制批评者。| The anti-dissident measures included censorship and imprisonment.  这些压制异议的措施包括审查制度和监禁。| repressive, authoritarian|liberal, tolerant
noun|反异议人士，镇压异见者|A person who opposes dissidents or actively works to suppress dissent. |He was known as a staunch anti-dissident, always defending the government's actions. 他被称为坚定的反异议人士，总是为政府的行为辩护。|The anti-dissidents used intimidation tactics to silence opposition. 这些压制异见者使用恐吓战术来压制反对意见。|repressive, authoritarian|liberal, tolerant
