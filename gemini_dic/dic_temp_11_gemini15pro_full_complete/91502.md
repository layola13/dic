hymnless:/ˈhɪmlɪs/
adjective
无圣歌的；没有赞美诗的
Without hymns; lacking hymns.
The funeral was a hymnless affair, reflecting the deceased's secular beliefs.  这场葬礼没有唱赞美诗，反映了死者世俗的信仰。
The gathering was a strangely hymnless occasion for a church group.  对于一个教会团体来说，这次聚会奇怪地没有唱赞美诗。
synonym:unmelodious, songless
antonym:hymnal, melodious
词性:adjective
