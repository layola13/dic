artiodactylous:/ɑːˌtɪəˈdæktɪləs/| adj.|偶蹄的|Of or relating to the Artiodactyla, a group of hoofed mammals that bear weight equally on two of their five toes, typically the third and fourth.  These toes are encased in hooves.|例句：Cows, pigs, and deer are all artiodactylous animals. 牛、猪和鹿都是偶蹄动物。

例句：The hippopotamus, surprisingly, is also artiodactylous.  令人惊讶的是，河马也是偶蹄动物。|近义词：cloven-hoofed, even-toed|反义词：perissodactylous|词性:adjective
