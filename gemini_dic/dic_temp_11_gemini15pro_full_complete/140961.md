one-upmanship:/ˌwʌnˈʌpmənʃɪp/ | n. | 胜人一筹；技高一筹 | The act or practice of successively outdoing a competitor or rival. | He’s always trying to gain one-upmanship over his colleagues. 他总是试图在同事面前技高一筹。 |  Trying to impress the boss by staying late is just silly one-upmanship.  为了给老板留下深刻印象而熬夜加班，这只不过是愚蠢的胜人一筹的心理。 | Synonyms: one-up, outmaneuvering, outsmarting, oneupsmanship | Antonyms: cooperation, collaboration | noun
