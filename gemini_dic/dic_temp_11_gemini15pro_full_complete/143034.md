outblessing: /ˌaʊtˈblɛsɪŋ/ | n. | 超越的祝福，更大的祝福 | An act of blessing that surpasses or exceeds another blessing in power, scope, or effect.  It can also refer to a blessing that extends beyond the usual or expected limits. |  The pastor offered an outblessing of peace and prosperity to the entire congregation. 牧师向整个会众给予了和平与繁荣的超越祝福。

| The outblessing of forgiveness he received brought him profound peace. 他所接受的宽恕的超越祝福给他带来了深深的平静。 | 近义词: greater blessing, superior blessing, surpassing blessing | 反义词: curse, malediction | 词性: 名词
