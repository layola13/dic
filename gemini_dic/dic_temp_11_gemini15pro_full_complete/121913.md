missemblance:/mɪsˈsɛmbləns/
n.
不像，相异，差异
lack of resemblance; dissimilarity
例句：The missemblance between the twins was striking. 这对双胞胎之间的差异令人吃惊。

例句：There's a marked missemblance between their political views. 他们的政治观点之间存在着明显的差异。

近义词：dissimilarity, unlikeness, difference, disparity, divergence, variation
反义词：resemblance, similarity, likeness, similitude

词性: 名词

词源:  From mis- "badly, wrongly" + semblance.  The prefix "mis-" indicates a lack or negation of the quality indicated by the root word "semblance," which means outward appearance or apparent form. Thus, "missemblance" signifies a lack of resemblance or similarity.
