fraughtage:/ˈfrɔːtɪdʒ/| n.|货物运费；运送|The fraughtage of the goods was quite expensive. 货物的运费相当昂贵。|The company paid a hefty fraughtage for shipping the containers overseas.  这家公司为海运集装箱支付了巨额运费。|Synonyms: freight, cargo, shipping cost|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fraghtage, from Old French freitage, from freter (“to freight”), from Frankish *frahton (“to load”), from Proto-Germanic *frahtōną (“to load”), from *frahtą (“load, cargo”). Cognate with Old High German frehtan (“to load, equip”), Old English frehtan (“to load, adorn”).  More at freight.
