immovable:/ɪˈmuːvəbl/ | adj. | 不可移动的；固定的；不动产的 | Not able to be moved; fixed in place; (of property) consisting of land or buildings. |  The statue seemed immovable, a solid testament to the past.  这座雕像似乎不可移动，是对过去的坚实证明。

The immovable rock stood firm against the crashing waves.  不可移动的岩石在 crashing 的波浪中屹立不倒。 | 近义词: fixed, stationary, steadfast, permanent | 反义词: movable, mobile, portable, flexible | 词性: 形容词 | 词源: From Late Latin *immovabilis, from in- 'not' + movabilis 'movable'.
