osiris: /oʊˈsaɪrɪs/ | n. | 奥西里斯 |  The Egyptian god of the underworld and judge of the dead, husband of Isis and father of Horus.  He represented resurrection and fertility. | Osiris is a central figure in Egyptian mythology. 奥西里斯是埃及神话中的核心人物。 |  The annual flooding of the Nile was seen as a symbol of Osiris's resurrection. 尼罗河的年度泛滥被视为奥西里斯复活的象征。 | synonyms: god, deity | antonyms: mortal, human | noun
