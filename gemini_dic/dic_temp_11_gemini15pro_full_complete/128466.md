newsiness:/ˈnjuːzinəs/| n.|新闻价值；新闻性|The newsiness of a story depends on several factors, including its timeliness and impact.|一个故事的新闻价值取决于几个因素，包括它的时效性和影响力。|近义词：newsworthiness, topicality|反义词:insignificance, triviality|名词

Etymology:  From "news" + "-y" (forming adjectives meaning 'having the quality of') + "-ness" (forming nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from  "new".
