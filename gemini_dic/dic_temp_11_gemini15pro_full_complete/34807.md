climbs:/klaɪmz/
verb
攀登，爬；上升，上涨
The vine climbs the wall. 藤蔓爬上墙。
He climbs mountains as a hobby. 他把爬山作为爱好。
ascend, scale, mount
descend, fall
词性:动词(第三人称单数)


climb:/klaɪm/
noun
攀登，爬；上升，上涨；山坡，斜坡
It was a steep climb to the top of the hill. 到山顶是很陡峭的攀登。
The stock market has seen a steady climb in recent weeks. 最近几周股市稳步攀升。
ascent, rise, incline
descent, fall, decline
词性:名词


climb:/klaɪm/
verb
攀登，爬；上升，上涨
The children climbed the tree. 孩子们爬上了树。
Prices are climbing steadily. 物价正在稳步上涨。
ascend, scale, mount
descend, fall
词性:动词

词源:
From Old English climban, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch klimmen and German klimmen.
