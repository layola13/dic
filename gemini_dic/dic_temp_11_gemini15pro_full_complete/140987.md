loneliness: /ˈloʊnlinəs/ | n. | 孤独，寂寞 | The state of being alone and feeling sad about it.  A feeling of sadness because one has no friends or company. |  She suffered from intense loneliness after her husband died. 她丈夫去世后，她感到非常孤独。

|  The vastness of the desert intensified his feelings of loneliness.  浩瀚的沙漠加剧了他的孤独感。 | 近义词：isolation, solitude | 反义词：companionship, togetherness | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English lonelinesse, equivalent to lonely + -ness.
