behavioral:/bɪˈheɪvjərəl/
adjective
行为的；行为方式的
Relating to or concerned with behavior.
Her behavioral problems started in early childhood. 她的行为问题始于童年早期。
behavioral therapy 行为疗法
synonyms: conduct, relating to behavior
antonyms: cognitive, emotional
词性: adjective
