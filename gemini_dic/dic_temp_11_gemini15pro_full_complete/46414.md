daftness:/ˈdæftnɪs/| n. |愚蠢，傻气|The sheer daftness of the plan was apparent to everyone. 这个计划的愚蠢之处对每个人来说都是显而易见的。|例句：His daftness cost him his job. 他的愚蠢让他丢了工作。  His proposal was met with laughter because of its sheer daftness. 他的提议因为太过愚蠢而遭到嘲笑。|近义词：foolishness, stupidity, silliness, absurdity|反义词：wisdom, sense, intelligence|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dafte, from Old Norse *daftr (“numb, weak”), from Proto-Germanic *dabraz (“slow, clumsy”). Related to Old English *deaf (“deaf”).  The original sense was of being slow-witted or mentally numb, which later developed into the meaning of foolish or silly.
