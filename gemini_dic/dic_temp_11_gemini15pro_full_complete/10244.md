apothecium: /ˌæpəˈθiːsiəm/ /ˌæpəˈθiːʃəm/
noun
子囊盘，子囊果
The apothecium is the fruiting body of certain ascomycete fungi, containing the asci, which in turn contain ascospores.
Example 1: The biologist examined the apothecium under the microscope to identify the species of fungus.  生物学家在显微镜下检查子囊盘以确定真菌的种类。
Example 2:  The bright red apothecia were clearly visible on the decaying log.  鲜红色的子囊盘在腐烂的原木上清晰可见。
Synonym:ascocarp
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific fungal structure)
