antecede:/ˌæntiˈsiːd/| v. |先于，在…之前|to be or go before something or someone in time or order|The invention of the printing press anteceded the rise of mass literacy. 印刷机的发明先于大众识字率的提高。|
antecede:/ˌæntiˈsiːd/| v. |居…之先|to be earlier in time or order|The Norman Conquest anteceded the reign of the Tudors. 诺曼征服先于都铎王朝的统治。|
近义词：precede, predate, forerun|
反义词：follow, succeed|
词性: verb
