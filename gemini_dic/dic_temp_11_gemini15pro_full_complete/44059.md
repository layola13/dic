cringley:/ˈkrɪndʒli/| adj.|尴尬的，令人难堪的|Causing feelings of embarrassment or awkwardness.|Her attempt at a British accent was incredibly cringley.|近义词:awkward, embarrassing, cringeworthy, cringe-inducing|反义词:comfortable, smooth, cool|词性:形容词
词源: From "cringe" (v.) + "-ly" (adv. suffix). "Cringe" comes from Old English "cringan" meaning "to bend, yield, fall in battle."  The modern sense of feeling embarrassed developed from the physical sense of shrinking back or recoiling.
