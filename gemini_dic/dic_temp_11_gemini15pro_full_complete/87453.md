hesperian:/hɛˈspɪəriən/| n.|西方人|A person living in the west.|例句：The hesperians welcomed the travelers from the east. 西方人欢迎来自东方的旅行者。|例句：Ancient hesperians built impressive civilizations. 古代西方人建立了令人印象深刻的文明。|近义词：westerner|反义词：easterner|词性:名词

词源: From Latin *Hesperius*, from Ancient Greek Ἑσπέριος (*Hesperios*), from ἕσπερος (*hesperos*, “evening, west”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wespero-*.
