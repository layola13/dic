nonconcern:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsɜːrn/
n.|漠不关心，不关心|Lack of concern or interest; indifference.|He showed a complete nonconcern for her feelings. 他对她的感受完全漠不关心。|近义词：indifference, apathy, unconcern|反义词：concern, interest, solicitude|词性: 名词

词源：non- (prefix meaning "not") + concern (noun meaning "anxiety or worry")
