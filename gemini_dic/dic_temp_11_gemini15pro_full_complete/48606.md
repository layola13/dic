defections:/dɪˈfɛkʃənz/| n. |背叛，变节，叛逃|The party suffered a series of defections to the opposition. 该党遭受了一系列党员叛逃到反对派的打击。|例句：The president was weakened by defections from his own party.  总统因自己党内人士的变节而被削弱。

|例句：Recent defections from the ruling party have undermined its authority.  最近执政党的叛逃事件削弱了其权威。|近义词：desertions, abandonments, apostasy|反义词:loyalty, fidelity|词性:noun (plural)

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dēfectiōn- (stem of dēfectiō), from Latin dēficiō (“I revolt, desert, fail”), from dē (“away, from”) + faciō (“I do, make”).
