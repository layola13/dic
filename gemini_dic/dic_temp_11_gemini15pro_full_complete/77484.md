gazeful: /ˈɡeɪzfʊl/ | adj. | 凝视的，注视的 | Given to gazing; looking intently. | She gave him a long, gazeful look. 她久久地凝视着他。 |  近义词: staring, watchful, observant | 反义词: unobservant, inattentive | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From gaze + -ful.  'Gaze' comes from Old North French 'gaiser', of uncertain origin. '-ful' is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by".
