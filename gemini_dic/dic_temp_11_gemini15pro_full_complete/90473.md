hubcap:/ˈhʌbkæp/ (US) /ˈhʌbkæp/ (UK)| n. |轮毂罩，轮毂盖|A decorative metal or plastic cover over the center of a vehicle's wheel.|Example:  I lost a hubcap driving on that bumpy road. 我在那条颠簸的路上开车时掉了一个轮毂罩。

Example:  He polished his hubcaps until they gleamed. 他把轮毂罩擦得闪闪发光。|Synonym: wheel cover, wheel trim|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps the exposed wheel itself) |noun
