overslung:/ˌoʊvərˈslʌŋ/| adj.|上悬式的，上置式的|Suspended from above, especially so as to hang down lower than normal.  The word is often used to describe the suspension of a bridge or part of a vehicle.|The truck has an overslung engine.  这辆卡车的发动机是上置式的。|近义词：overhead, suspended|反义词：underslung|词性:形容词

overslung:/ˌoʊvərˈslʌŋ/| n.|上悬式结构|A structure or part that is overslung.|The overslung design of the bridge allows for a wider roadway. 这座桥的上悬式设计允许更宽的车道。|近义词：overhead structure, suspended structure|反义词：underslung structure|词性:名词
