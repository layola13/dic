connectional:/kəˈnɛkʃənəl/
adjective
连接的；关联的
Of or relating to connection or connecting.  Pertaining to a system in which parts are interconnected.
The brain's connectional architecture is incredibly complex.  大脑的连接结构极其复杂。
The study focuses on the connectional properties of the network. 这项研究集中于网络的连接特性。
connected, interconnected, related, associated
unconnected, unrelated, disassociated
