nervomuscular:/ˌnɜːrvəʊˈmʌskjʊlər/ | adj. | 神经肌肉的 | Relating to both nerves and muscles; involving the nerves and muscles. |  Myasthenia gravis is a neuromuscular disorder. 重症肌无力是一种神经肌肉疾病。

The doctor tested his neuromuscular reflexes. 医生检查了他的神经肌肉反射。 |  neuromuscular, myoneural |  None | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From nervo- (combining form meaning "nerve") + muscular ("of or relating to muscle").  Nervo- comes from Latin nervus ("nerve"). Muscular comes from Latin musculus ("muscle").
