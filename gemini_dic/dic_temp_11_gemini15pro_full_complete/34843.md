clinicians:/klɪˈnɪʃənz/
n.
临床医生；临床工作者
Healthcare professionals who work directly with patients.
The clinicians are meeting to discuss the new treatment protocol.  临床医生们正在开会讨论新的治疗方案。
The experienced clinicians provided valuable insights into the patient's condition. 经验丰富的临床医生对患者的病情提供了宝贵的见解。

近义词:doctors, physicians, practitioners, therapists
反义词:researchers, administrators


词性:noun (plural)
词源:From clinic + -ian. Ultimately from the Greek klinikē (meaning "bedside").
