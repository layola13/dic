maidenlike:/ˈmeɪdnˌlaɪk/| adj.|少女般的；像处女的；端庄的|Resembling or characteristic of a maiden; modest and gentle.|She had a maidenlike blush on her cheeks. 她脸颊上泛起少女般的红晕。|近义词：demure, modest, innocent, chaste, virginal|反义词：immodest, indecent, brazen, whorish|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English maidenlike, equivalent to maiden +‎ -like.
