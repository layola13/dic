authoring:/ˈɔːθərɪŋ/| n. |创作，编写，写作|The process of creating or writing something, such as a book, article, or software program.  It encompasses all stages of development, from initial conception to final production.|例句：The authoring of the new software took several months.  新软件的编写花了几个月时间。|例句：She enjoys the authoring process and finds it very rewarding. 她很享受创作过程，并觉得很有成就感。|近义词：writing, composing, creating, producing, developing|反义词:reading, consuming|词性:noun

authoring:/ˈɔːθərɪŋ/| adj. |创作的，写作的|(Used attributively) Relating to the creation or writing of something.|例句：Authoring tools can help streamline the writing process. 写作工具可以帮助简化写作过程。|例句：He attended an authoring workshop to improve his skills. 他参加了一个写作研讨会来提高自己的技能。|近义词：writing, creative|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
