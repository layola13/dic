dismissing:/dɪsˈmɪsɪŋ/
v.
解雇；解散；驳回；不予理会
Present participle of dismiss.  It describes the action of sending someone away or rejecting something.
The manager was dismissing the employees who performed poorly.  经理正在解雇那些表现不佳的员工。
He was dismissing all thoughts of failure. 他摒弃了所有失败的想法。
synonyms: rejecting, discharging, firing, refusing, ignoring
antonyms: hiring, employing, accepting, considering, welcoming
