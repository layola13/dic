interwraps:/ˈɪntərˌræps/ | n. | 隔层纸；衬垫；包装纸 | Sheets of material placed between layers of something, especially for protection during storage or transport. | The delicate porcelain figurines were carefully packed with interwraps between each piece.  这些精致的瓷器小雕像用衬垫小心地包装着，每件之间都有隔层。| The paintings were stored with acid-free interwraps to prevent damage. 这些画作用无酸衬垫存放，以防止损坏。 | synonyms: padding, wrapping, protective layer, cushioning, interleaving, liner | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider:  exposure, bare) | noun

interwrap:/ˌɪntərˈræp/ | v. | 用隔层纸包裹；衬垫；隔开 | To wrap or cover something with interwraps. |  He carefully interwrapped the glassware before boxing it up. 他在把玻璃器皿装箱前，小心地用隔层纸包裹起来。|  She interwrapped each layer of fabric with tissue paper. 她用薄纸将每一层布料隔开。 | synonyms:  pad, wrap, protect, cushion, interleave | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: expose, uncover) | verb
