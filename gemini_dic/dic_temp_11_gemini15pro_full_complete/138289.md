notate:/ˈnoʊteɪt/ (US) /nəʊˈteɪt/ (UK)| v. |记谱，用符号表示|To write musical notes or other symbols on paper.|The composer notated the symphony quickly. 作曲家快速地为交响曲记谱。|例句：The conductor asked the musician to notate the changes to the score. 指挥家要求音乐家把乐谱的改动记下来。|近义词：transcribe, score, write down|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) erase, delete|词性: verb
