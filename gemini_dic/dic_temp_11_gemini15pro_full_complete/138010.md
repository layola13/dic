nooky's:/ˈnʊkiz/| n.|性交; 幽会; 隐蔽处|Nooky's is a slang term, primarily used in British English, referring to sexual intercourse or a secluded place for amorous activities. It's formed by adding the possessive 's to "nooky," which itself is a slang term for sex.  It can also, less commonly, refer to a hidden or cozy place.|例句：They went off to find a quiet nooky's for a bit of privacy. 他们去找个安静的隐蔽处享受一下二人世界。
