gordius:/ˈɡɔːrdiəs/| adj.|戈尔迪之结的; 极其复杂的; 难解的|Of or relating to Gordius, a legendary Phrygian king, or to the intricate knot tied by him, which, according to an oracle, was to be undone only by the person who was to rule Asia. Alexander the Great cut the knot with his sword.|The Gordian knot of the Irish question seemed impossible to unravel. 爱尔兰问题的戈尔迪之结似乎无法解开。|近义词：intricate, complex, involved, knotty|反义词：simple, straightforward|词性:形容词
