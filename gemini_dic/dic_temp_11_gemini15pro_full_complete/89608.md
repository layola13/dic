hoodwinkable:/hʊdˈwɪŋkəbl/
adjective
易受骗的，容易上当的
Easily deceived or tricked.
He was so hoodwinkable that he believed the email promising him a million dollars. 他太容易上当了，竟然相信了那封承诺给他一百万美元的电子邮件。
She's young and hoodwinkable. 她年轻容易受骗。
近义词: gullible, naive, credulous, susceptible
反义词: astute, discerning, perceptive, shrewd
词性: adjective

词源:
From hoodwink + -able.  Hoodwink itself comes from "hood" (referring to a falcon's hood used to cover its eyes) and "wink" (to close the eyes).  Therefore, to hoodwink someone is to blind them (metaphorically) to the truth.
