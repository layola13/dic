classicistic:/ˌklæsɪˈsɪstɪk/
adjective
古典主义的；古典风格的
Relating to or characteristic of classicism.
The building's facade is classicistic, featuring Doric columns and symmetrical design.  这栋建筑的正面是古典主义风格的，以多立克柱式和对称设计为特色。
The composer's style was considered classicistic, emphasizing formal structure and balance.  这位作曲家的风格被认为是古典主义的，强调形式结构和平衡。
近义词:classical, neoclassical
反义词:romantic, modern, contemporary
词性:adjective
