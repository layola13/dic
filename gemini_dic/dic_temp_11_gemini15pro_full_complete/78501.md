ghaut:/ɡɔːt/
noun
山间小路；(尤指在印度的)河岸台阶，(通往河边的)石梯
A flight of steps leading down to a river, especially in India.
We descended the ghaut to the river. 我们沿着石梯下到河边。
steps, stairway, staircase
cliff, hill
n.
