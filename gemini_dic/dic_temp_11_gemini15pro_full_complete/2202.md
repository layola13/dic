additivities:/ˌædɪˈtɪvɪtiz/
n.
可加性
the property of being additive; the extent to which something is additive.
The additivities of the ingredients in the mixture determine its final properties. 混合物中成分的可加性决定了它的最终性质。
additivity, cumulative effect
subtractivity, antagonism
noun
