outlung: /ˈaʊtˌlʌŋ/
noun
外肺; (比喻)偏远地区
a part of a country or organization that is far from its centre or main activities, often considered to be less important
例句：The branch office felt like an outlung of the company, rarely receiving communication from headquarters.  这家分公司感觉像是公司的偏远地区，很少收到总部的消息。

例句：These remote villages are the outlungs of civilization. 这些偏远村庄是文明的边缘地带。
近义词：outpost, periphery, fringe
反义词：center, core, heart
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'out-' (meaning outside or beyond) + 'lung' (referring to the organ of respiration).  The word suggests something projecting outward, like a lung extending beyond the main body.  It's a metaphorical extension of the anatomical term. 源于out-（意为外部或超越）+ lung（指呼吸器官）。该词暗示向外突出之物，就像肺部延伸到主体之外。它是解剖学术语的隐喻延伸。
