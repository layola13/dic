horus:/ˈhɔːrəs/| n.|荷鲁斯|Horus is one of the most significant ancient Egyptian deities. He was often depicted as a falcon-headed man wearing the pschent, the combined crown of Upper and Lower Egypt, or as a falcon. He was worshipped as the god of the sky, war, and hunting.|Horus was a powerful god in ancient Egypt. 荷鲁斯是古埃及强大的神。

Horus is often associated with the pharaohs. 荷鲁斯常与法老联系在一起。|近义词:Ra, god|反义词:Set|词性:noun
