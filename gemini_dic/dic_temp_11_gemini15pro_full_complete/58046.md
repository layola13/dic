drollingly:/ˈdrəʊlɪŋli/ | adv. | 流着口水地，痴迷地 | in a way that shows excessive admiration or desire, often with the suggestion of foolishness or lasciviousness | He looked at the cake drollingly. 他痴迷地看着蛋糕。| 近义词：longingly, yearningly, covetously, lasciviously | 反义词：indifferently, apathetically | 词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "drool" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Drool" comes from Middle Dutch "drolen" meaning "to be sluggish."
