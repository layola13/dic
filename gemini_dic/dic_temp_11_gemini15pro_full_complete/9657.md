antisyndicalism:/ˌæntiˈsɪndɪkəlɪzəm/
n.
反工团主义，反工会主义
Opposition to trade unionism; the belief that trade unions are harmful to society.
The government's antisyndicalism led to violent clashes with workers. 政府的反工会主义导致了与工人的暴力冲突。
Near Synonyms: anti-labor, anti-unionism
Antonyms: pro-unionism, syndicalism
noun
