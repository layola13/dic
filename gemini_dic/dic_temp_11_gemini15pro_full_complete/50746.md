derring-do:/ˈdɛrɪŋ ˈduː/
noun
勇敢的行为；大胆的举动；冒险行为
A daring action; an adventurous deed.
He was known for his derring-do in the face of danger. 他以在危险面前的英勇行为而闻名。
Synonyms: bravery, courage, audacity, boldness, valor, heroism, intrepidity
Antonyms: cowardice, timidity, fearfulness
n.

derring-do:/ˈdɛrɪŋ ˈduː/
Etymology:  From Middle Scots *derring do*, from derring (present participle of *dar* "to dare") + do ("deed").
词源：来自中古苏格兰语 *derring do*，由 derring（*dar* “敢”的现在分词）+ do（“行为”）组成。
