impend:/ɪmˈpɛnd/| v. |即将发生，逼近|Be about to happen; be imminent. |例句：A sense of doom impended. 一种厄运感逼近。

例句：The exam impends, and students are cramming. 考试临近，学生们正在拼命学习。|近义词：loom, threaten, approach|反义词: recede, pass, go away|词性:动词

Etymology: From Latin *impendēre*, present active infinitive of *impendō* (“to hang over, overhang, be imminent”), from *in* (“in, on, upon”) + *pendō* (“hang, weigh”).
