overplots:/ˌoʊvərˈplɑːts/ /ˌəʊvəˈplɒts/
verb|过度绘制;  在…上叠印| To add too many subplots or complications to a narrative, making it overly complex or confusing.  It can also refer to adding a secondary plot on top of the main plot. |The author overplotted the novel with unnecessary subplots, making it difficult to follow.  作者在小说中加入了太多不必要的次要情节，使小说难以理解。|synonyms: overcrowd, overcomplicate, overburden |antonyms: simplify, streamline|v.
overplots:/ˌoʊvərˈplɑːts/ /ˌəʊvəˈplɒts/
noun|过度绘制;  叠印|Too many subplots or complications in a narrative, making it overly complex or confusing. |The overplots in the movie made it a convoluted mess. 电影中过多的次要情节使它变得混乱不堪。|synonyms:  excess, clutter, tangle |antonyms: simplicity, clarity|n.
