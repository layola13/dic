airlifts:/ˈɛərˌlɪfts/| n. vt.|空运，空投；空运的物资|An airlift is the organized transport of supplies or people by aircraft, especially in an emergency.|例句：The Berlin Airlift was a major operation to supply West Berlin during the Soviet blockade. 柏林空运是苏联封锁期间向西柏林提供物资的主要行动。

例句：The government organized an airlift of food and medical supplies to the disaster area. 政府组织了向灾区空运食品和医疗用品。|近义词：air transportation, air supply|反义词:ground transport|n.

airlifts:/ˈɛərˌlɪfts/| vt.|空运，空投|To airlift is to transport supplies or people by aircraft, especially in an emergency.|例句：They airlifted the injured climbers to safety. 他们用飞机把受伤的登山者空运到安全的地方。

例句：The refugees were airlifted out of the war zone. 难民被空运出战区。|近义词：fly, transport by air|反义词:transport by land/sea|vt.
