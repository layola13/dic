multitrack:/ˈmʌltiˌtræk/ |n.  adj.|多轨录音，多轨的|A recording made on a multitrack recorder, or the recorder itself, which allows separate parts to be recorded at different times and then combined.|The band recorded the song using a multitrack system.|Synonyms: multichannel, multi-channel|Antonyms: monophonic, mono|noun, adjective

multitrack:/ˈmʌltiˌtræk/ |adj.|多轨的| Designed for or involving the recording of separate parts at different times, which are then combined.|They used a multitrack recorder to layer the vocals.|Synonyms: multichannel, multi-channel|Antonyms: monophonic, mono|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"multi-" (prefix meaning "many") + "track" (noun referring to a single audio or video channel).
