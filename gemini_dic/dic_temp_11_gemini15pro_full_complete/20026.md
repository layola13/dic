biomechanically:/ˌbaɪoʊməˈkænɪkli/| adv.|生物力学地|Relating to biomechanics, the study of the mechanical principles of living organisms, especially their movement and structure.|The robot's movements were biomechanically inspired by the gait of a cheetah.  这个机器人的动作灵感来自猎豹的步态。|

The athlete's performance was analyzed biomechanically to identify areas for improvement.  这位运动员的表现经过生物力学分析，以确定需要改进的地方。|近义词：physiologically, kinetically|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider non-mechanically)|词性:adverb
