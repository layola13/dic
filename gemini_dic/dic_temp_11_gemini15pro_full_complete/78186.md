geospheres:/ˈdʒiːoʊsfɪrz/
n.
地球圈层
The different interconnected zones of the Earth system, including the atmosphere, hydrosphere, lithosphere, biosphere, and sometimes the cryosphere and anthroposphere.
The interaction between the geospheres affects Earth's climate. 地球圈层之间的相互作用影响着地球的气候。
