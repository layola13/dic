moabitish:/ˈmoʊəˌbaɪtɪʃ/| adj.|摩押的，摩押人的|Relating to Moab, its people, or their language.  Descended from or pertaining to the Moabites, an ancient people who lived east of the Dead Sea.|The archaeologist discovered Moabitish pottery shards. 考古学家发现了摩押陶器的碎片。|Moabite|n/a|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Moab + -ish. Moab itself comes from Hebrew מוֹאָב (Mo'av), possibly meaning "from the father," though the exact etymology is uncertain.
