laconically:/ləˈkɒnɪkli/ | adv. | 简洁地，简略地 |  In a brief and to-the-point manner; using few words. | He answered laconically, "No." 他简洁地回答：“不”。 |  briefly, concisely, tersely | verbosely, lengthily | adverb

laconic:/ləˈkɒnɪk/ | adj. | 简洁的，简短的 | Using very few words; expressing much in few words. |  Her laconic reply suggested she wasn't interested. 她简短的回答表明她不感兴趣。 | brief, concise, terse | verbose, lengthy | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *laconique*, from Latin *Laconicus*, from Greek *Lakōnikos* "Laconian, of Laconia," the region of Sparta in ancient Greece, whose inhabitants were famous for their brevity of speech.
