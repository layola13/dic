hyperbrutal:/ˌhaɪpərˈbruːtəl/| adj.|极端残忍的，极其野蛮的|Excessively brutal; extremely violent and savage.|The video game depicted hyperbrutal violence, causing controversy among parents. 这款电子游戏描绘了极端残忍的暴力，在家长中引起了争议。|The dictator's hyperbrutal regime was responsible for countless atrocities.  这个独裁者的极端残忍的政权造成了无数暴行。|近义词：savage, barbaric, ferocious, vicious, ruthless|反义词：gentle, humane, compassionate|词性:形容词

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
brutal (adjective): from Late Latin *brutalis, from Latin brutus "brutish, irrational, insensible," figuratively, of things, "rough, unpolished, raw," related to bruma "winter solstice"
