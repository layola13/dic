outranting:/ˌaʊtˈræntɪŋ/
verb
大声叫嚷，胜过…的叫嚷
speaking or shouting louder than someone else
He outranted the other speakers at the rally. 他在集会上比其他发言者喊得更大声。

outranting:/ˌaʊtˈræntɪŋ/
adjective
大声叫嚷的
characterized by loud ranting
His outranting style alienated many voters. 他大声叫嚷的风格疏远了许多选民。


Etymology:  "out-" (exceeding) + "rant" (to speak or declaim extravagantly or violently)

Synonyms: outshouting, outclamoring, overpowering, drowning out
Antonyms: whispering, murmuring, mumbling, being quiet
