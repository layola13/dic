damn:/dæm/| v. n. adj. adv. interj.|诅咒；谴责；指责；(表示愤怒、不耐烦)该死；非常；极其|A word used to express anger, annoyance, or frustration, or to emphasize something.  It can also be used as a verb meaning to condemn to eternal punishment or to ruin.|Damn it! I forgot my keys! 该死！我忘了带钥匙了！|近义词：curse, blast, darn, bloody (UK), freaking|反义词:bless|词性:动词, 名词, 形容词, 副词,感叹词
