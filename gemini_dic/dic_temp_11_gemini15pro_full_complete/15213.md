backdown:/ˈbækˌdaʊn/| n.| 退却，让步 |A retreat or concession.|After a long standoff, the company issued a backdown from its original proposal.  经过长时间的僵持，公司撤回了最初的提案。|近义词：withdrawal, retreat, concession, climb-down|反义词：insistence, perseverance|词性: 名词

back down:/bæk ˈdaʊn/| vi.| 退却，让步，放弃 |To withdraw from a position or demand.|The union backed down from its demand for a 20% pay raise. 工会放弃了加薪20%的要求。|近义词：yield, concede, surrender, give in|反义词：persist, insist, stand firm|词性: 动词

词源： "back down" 源自航海术语。当船只逆风航行时，为了避免倾覆，有时需要“后退(back)”并“放下(down)”一些帆。这个短语逐渐被引申为在争论或对抗中“退却”或“让步”的意思。
