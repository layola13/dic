mulse:/mʌls/| n.|蜂蜜酒；蜂蜜饮料|A fermented alcoholic beverage made from honey and water. It can also refer to a non-alcoholic beverage made from honey.|例句：The ancient Greeks enjoyed mulse, a sweet drink made from fermented honey. 古希腊人喜欢喝蜂蜜酒，一种由发酵蜂蜜制成的甜饮料。

例句：She offered me a refreshing glass of mulse made with local honey. 她给了我一杯用当地蜂蜜制成的清爽蜂蜜饮料。|近义词：mead, honey wine, honey drink|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "water" or other non-alcoholic beverages) |词性: noun
