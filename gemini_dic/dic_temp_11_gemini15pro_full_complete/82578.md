guarded:/ˈɡɑːrdɪd/| adj.|谨慎的， guarded; 保护的, protected; 有保留的, reserved|Showing caution and restraint in one's speech or behavior, often due to a desire to avoid revealing too much or causing offense; protected or defended.|She gave a guarded response, not wanting to reveal too much information. 她给出了一个谨慎的答复，不想透露太多信息。|The castle was heavily guarded. 这座城堡戒备森严。|近义词：cautious, wary, careful, protected, defended|反义词：open, unguarded, careless, unprotected, undefended|词性:adjective
