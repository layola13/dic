inconvertible:/ˌɪnkənˈvɜːrtəbl/ |adj.|不可兑换的，不能变换的|Not able to be converted or changed; impossible to exchange for something else, especially money.| The currency of that country is currently inconvertible.  那个国家的货币目前是不可兑换的。|Synonyms: unchangeable, fixed, nonexchangeable, non-negotiable|Antonyms: convertible, exchangeable, changeable, fluid|adjective
