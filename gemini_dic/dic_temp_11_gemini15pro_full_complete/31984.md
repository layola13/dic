chemosmosis:/ˌkɛmoʊzˈmoʊsɪs/ /ˌkɛməʊˈzməʊsɪs/| n. |化学渗透|The movement of ions across a semipermeable membrane, down their electrochemical gradient, especially the movement of hydrogen ions across a membrane during cellular respiration or photosynthesis, generating ATP.|The production of ATP in chloroplasts and mitochondria is driven by chemosmosis. 叶绿体和线粒体中ATP的产生是由化学渗透驱动的。|近义词:chemiosmosis|反义词: (No direct antonym exists)|词性:noun
