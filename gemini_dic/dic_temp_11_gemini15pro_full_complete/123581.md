montaged:/ˈmɒntɑːʒd/ (UK) /mɑːnˈtɑːʒd/ (US)| adj.|剪辑过的，蒙太奇的|Made into or resembling a montage.  A film sequence or image composed of a series of short shots or images.|The director used a montaged sequence to show the passage of time.|近义词：collaged, assembled, combined|反义词：unedited, single, separate|词性:形容词
