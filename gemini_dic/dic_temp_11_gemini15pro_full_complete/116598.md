matriculator:/məˈtrɪkjʊleɪtər/
noun
注册员；入学者；录取者
A person who matriculates, especially a student enrolling in a university.
The matriculator processed the new student's application. 注册员处理了新生的申请。
John is a matriculator at Harvard University this year. 约翰今年是哈佛大学的新生。
Synonyms: enrollee, registrant, admit
Antonyms: graduate, alumnus
n.
