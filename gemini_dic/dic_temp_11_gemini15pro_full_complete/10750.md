apus:/ˈeɪpəs/| n. |雨燕，蜂鸟|A small, swift-flying bird with long, pointed wings and a short, forked tail.  It belongs to the family Apodidae. |例句：The apus darted across the sky. 雨燕掠过天空。

例句：Apus are known for their aerial acrobatics. 雨燕以其空中特技而闻名。|近义词：swift, martin|反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑不会飞的鸟，例如 penguin 企鹅) |词性:noun
