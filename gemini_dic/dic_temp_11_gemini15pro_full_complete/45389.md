cupbearer:/ˈkʌpˌberər/| n. |侍酒者，斟酒官|A person who served drinks, especially wine, at a royal court or in a wealthy household. |例句：The king's cupbearer was a trusted advisor. 国王的侍酒官是一位值得信赖的顾问。

例句：The cupbearer filled the goblet with wine. 侍酒官把高脚杯装满了酒。|近义词：butler, steward, sommelier |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps guest) |词性:noun
