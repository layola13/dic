limelighters:/ˈlaɪmˌlaɪtərz/
n.
聚光灯下的人; 引人注目的人;  舞台灯光师
People who are in the public eye, often performers or celebrities. Also, a person who operates stage lighting.
The band "The Limeliters" were popular folk singers in the 1960s.  "The Limeliters"乐队是20世纪60年代流行的民谣歌手。

The actress enjoyed being one of the limelighters at the awards ceremony.  这位女演员很享受在颁奖典礼上成为焦点人物之一。

The limelighters worked diligently to create the perfect ambiance for the play. 舞台灯光师们勤奋地工作，为这出戏营造了完美的氛围。
近义词:celebrities, stars, performers, notables
反义词: unknowns, nobody, nonentity
词性: 名词 (plural)
