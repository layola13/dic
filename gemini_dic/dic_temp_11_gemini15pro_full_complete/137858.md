nonviolent:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪələnt/
adjective
非暴力的
Not violent; peaceful.
例句：
They staged a nonviolent protest outside the government building.
他们在政府大楼外举行了一场非暴力抗议。

例句：
Gandhi was a proponent of nonviolent resistance.
甘地是非暴力抵抗的倡导者。

近义词：peaceful, pacifist, non-aggressive
反义词：violent, aggressive, forceful
词性：adjective

Etymology: non- (not) + violent (using or involving physical force intended to hurt, damage, or kill someone or something)
