chartered:/ˈtʃɑːrtərd/
chartered:|adj.|特许的；包租的|Having received a charter (official document) granting certain rights and privileges.|The chartered accountant reviewed the company's financials.  特许会计师审查了公司的财务状况。|chartered:|例句：They traveled on a chartered bus. 他们乘坐包租的巴士旅行。|例句：She is a chartered surveyor. 她是一位特许测量师。|近义词：licensed, authorized, hired, rented|反义词：unauthorized, unlicensed|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chartren*, from Old French *chartre*, from Latin *charta*, meaning "paper, papyrus."  The meaning evolved to refer to a document conferring rights or privileges, and then to anything authorized by such a document.
