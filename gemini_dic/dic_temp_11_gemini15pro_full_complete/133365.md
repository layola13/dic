nonextortive:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɔːrtɪv/
adjective
非勒索的，非强取的
Not involving extortion; not obtained by extortion.
The agreement was reached through nonextortive negotiations.  该协议是通过非勒索性谈判达成的。
The business practices were completely nonextortive and transparent.  这些商业行为完全是非勒索性的，透明的。
近义词:fair, just, legitimate, ethical
反义词:extortive, coercive, exploitative
词性:adjective

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "extortive" (adjective derived from "extort," from Latin "extorquere," meaning "to twist out," from "ex-" (out) + "torquere" (to twist)).
