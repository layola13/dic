kilometers:/ˈkɪləmiːtərz/ /kɪˈlɑːmɪtərz/
|n.|千米，公里|A kilometer is a unit of length in the metric system, equal to 1000 meters. |The marathon is 42.195 kilometers long. 马拉松全程42.195公里。|Synonyms: km, klicks (informal)|Antonyms: millimeters, centimeters, meters|Noun
