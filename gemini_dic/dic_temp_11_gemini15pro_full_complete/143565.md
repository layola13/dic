outhisses:/ˌaʊtˈhɪsɪz/
verb
发出比...更大的嘘声; 嘘得...比...更响亮
to hiss louder or more forcefully than someone or something else.
The crowd outhissed the unpopular speaker.  人群发出的嘘声盖过了不受欢迎的演讲者。
The booing was quickly outhissed by cheers of support.  嘘声很快就被支持的欢呼声盖过了。
synonym: outboo, drown out (with hisses)
antonym: applaud, cheer
词性: 动词
