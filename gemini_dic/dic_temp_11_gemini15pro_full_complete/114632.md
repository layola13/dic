maler:/ˈmɑːlər/ (British English), /ˈmeɪlər/ (American English)| n.|画家|A person who paints pictures, especially as a profession.  Derived from German "Maler", ultimately from Latin "mālor" (I paint), present active participle of "māllō" (I strike, paint, smear).|例句：The maler is known for his landscapes.  这位画家以他的风景画闻名。

例句：The young maler struggled to find his own style.  这位年轻的画家努力寻找自己的风格。|近义词：artist, painter, |反义词: - |词性:noun
