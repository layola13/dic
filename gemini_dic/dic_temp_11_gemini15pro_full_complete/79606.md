gloriole:/ˈɡlɔːriˌoʊl/ /ˈɡlɔːriəl/| n. |光环，荣光|A circle of light or radiance surrounding the head or figure of a person in art, often representing holiness or divinity. A halo.|The saint was depicted with a gloriole around his head.  圣徒被描绘成头部周围有一圈光环。|Synonyms: halo, aureole, nimbus, corona|Antonyms: disgrace, shame|noun
