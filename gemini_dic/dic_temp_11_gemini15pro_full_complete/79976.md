go-bush:/ɡəʊ bʊʃ/ | vi. | 逃到丛林中；躲藏起来；过原始生活；变得荒芜 | To go and live in the bush, especially in order to avoid the police or other authorities; to become wild and overgrown. | He went bush after robbing the bank. 他抢劫银行后逃到了丛林里。| 近义词：abscond, hide, escape, flee, disappear, go off the grid | 反义词: stay, remain, face the music | 词性：动词短语

go-bush:/ɡəʊ bʊʃ/ | adj. | 荒芜的，原始的 | Wild and overgrown;  uncultivated and uninhabited. | The garden had gone bush after years of neglect. 多年无人照料，花园变得荒芜了。 | 近义词：wild, overgrown, untamed, neglected | 反义词: cultivated, tamed, manicured | 词性：形容词
