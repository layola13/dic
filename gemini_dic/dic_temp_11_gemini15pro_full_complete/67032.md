explains:/ɪkˈspleɪnz/| v.| 解释，说明 |Third-person singular present tense form of the verb "explain". It signifies the act of making something clear or easy to understand by describing or giving reasons for it.| He explains the complex theory with simple examples. 他用简单的例子解释了这个复杂的理论。 |clarifies, elucidates, interprets, defines| obscures, confuses, complicates |verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *explanen*, *expleinen*, from Old French *esplaner*, *explaner* "to explain, make level, spread out," and from Latin *explanare* "to make smooth or plain, explain," from *ex- "out" + *planare* "to make plain, level," from *planus* "level, flat."
