crenated:/ˈkriːneɪtɪd/ | adj. |  圆齿状的，锯齿状的；（指细胞）钝齿状的，指动物细胞由于水分流失而收缩，边缘出现齿状缺刻 | Having a notched or scalloped edge, especially of animal cells that have lost water by osmosis and contracted. | The crenated red blood cells were a sign of dehydration. 红细胞呈圆齿状是脱水的迹象。| The edges of the leaf were crenated.  这片叶子的边缘呈锯齿状。 | synonyms: notched, scalloped, serrated, jagged | antonyms: smooth, entire | adjective
