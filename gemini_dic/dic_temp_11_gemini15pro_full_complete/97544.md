ingratiates:/ɪnˈɡreɪʃieɪts/
|v.|讨好；巴结；迎合|To behave in a way intended to gain favor or approval, often by flattering someone.|He ingratiated himself with the boss by offering to work late.|近义词：flatter, charm, wheedle, fawn, cozy up to|反义词：alienate, offend, repulse|verb
