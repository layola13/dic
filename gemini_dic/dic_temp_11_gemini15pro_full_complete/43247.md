crackpot:/ˈkrækˌpɒt/ (US) /ˈkrækˌpɔt/ (UK)| n. |怪人，疯子；不切实际的人|A person who has eccentric or unconventional ideas, especially one considered mentally unstable.|He dismissed the theory as the work of a crackpot. 他认为这个理论是一个疯子的作品。|Synonyms: eccentric, crank, nut, kook, loony|Antonyms: conformist, traditionalist|noun

crackpot:/ˈkrækˌpɒt/ (US) /ˈkrækˌpɔt/ (UK)| adj. |古怪的，不切实际的|Relating to or characteristic of a crackpot.|He had some crackpot scheme for getting rich quick. 他有一些快速致富的古怪计划。|Synonyms: bizarre, eccentric, unconventional, outlandish|Antonyms: conventional, orthodox, practical|adjective
