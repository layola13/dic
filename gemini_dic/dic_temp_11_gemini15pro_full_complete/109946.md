liberality:/ˌlɪbəˈrælɪti/
n.
慷慨，大方；宽宏大量
the quality of being liberal in giving; generosity.
He treated them with liberality and kindness. 他待他们慷慨而友善。

liberality:/ˌlɪbəˈrælɪti/
n.
心胸开阔；思想开放
open-mindedness.
Her liberality of spirit allowed her to embrace new ideas. 她思想开放，乐于接受新思想。


近义词:generosity, munificence, open-handedness, tolerance, broad-mindedness
反义词:stinginess, meanness, parsimony, intolerance, narrow-mindedness
词性:noun
