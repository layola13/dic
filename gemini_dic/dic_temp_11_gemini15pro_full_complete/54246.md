disgorgements:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒmənts/
n.
呕吐物；吐出物；被迫交出的东西
the act of disgorging something, or something that is disgorged.  Something yielded up or vomited forth.
The volcano disgorged a river of molten lava.  火山喷出一条熔岩河。
The company was forced to make disgorgements of its ill-gotten gains. 该公司被迫吐出其非法所得。
近义词: emissions, discharges, regurgitations, vomit
反义词: intakes, consumptions
