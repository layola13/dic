conferee:/ˌkɒnfəˈriː/ (US) /ˌkɑːnfəˈriː/ (UK)
n.
与会者，参加会议的人
A person who takes part in a conference or meeting.
The conferees reached an agreement after three days of intense negotiations.  与会者经过三天的紧张谈判达成了一项协议。

The hotel provided excellent facilities for the conferees.  这家酒店为与会者提供了优良的设施。

近义词: participant, attendee, delegate
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a person attending a meeting)
词性: Noun
