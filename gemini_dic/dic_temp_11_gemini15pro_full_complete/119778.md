mighty:/ˈmaɪti/| adj.|强大的，强有力的，巨大的，雄伟的|Possessing great and impressive power or strength, large and impressive.|例句：A mighty army marched across the land. 一支强大的军队横扫大地。|近义词：powerful, strong, enormous, huge, immense|反义词：weak, feeble, small, tiny|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *myghty*, from Old English *mihtig* (“mighty, powerful, strong, capable”), from Proto-Germanic *mahtīgą* (“mighty”), from *maht* (“might, power”). Cognate with German *mächtig* (“mighty”), Dutch *machtig* (“mighty, powerful”), Swedish *mäktig* (“mighty”).
