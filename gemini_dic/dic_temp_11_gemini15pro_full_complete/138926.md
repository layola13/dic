nutritions:/njuˈtrɪʃənz/ | n. |营养物质（通常用复数形式）| The process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth.  It often refers to the nutrients themselves.| These nutritions are essential for healthy plant growth. 这些营养物质对植物的健康生长至关重要。| 近义词: nutrients, nourishment | 反义词: deficiencies | 词性: 名词


词源: From Late Middle English *nutricioun, from Old French, or from Latin nūtrītiō (“a nourishing”), from nūtrītus, past participle of nūtrīre (“to nourish”).
