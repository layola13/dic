begirdle: /bɪˈɡɜːrdl/
verb
环绕，包围
To surround or encircle; to encompass.
The mountains begirdle the city. 群山环绕着这座城市。
A thick fog begirdled the castle. 浓雾笼罩着城堡。
Synonyms: encircle, surround, encompass, enclose
Antonyms: expose, uncover
词性: 动词
