multibranched:/ˌmʌltiˈbrɑːntʃt/ | adj. | 多分支的，多支的 | Having many branches; ramified.  |  The multibranched tree provided ample shade. 这棵枝繁叶茂的树提供了充足的阴凉。 | multi-limbed, many-branched, ramified | simple, unbranched | adjective

Etymology:
"multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many") + "branched" (from "branch," ultimately from Vulgar Latin *branca)
