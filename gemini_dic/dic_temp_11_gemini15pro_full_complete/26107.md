buskined:/ˈbʌskɪnd/
adjective
穿厚底靴的；悲剧风格的；夸张做作的
Wearing buskins; characteristic of tragic drama, especially in its more elevated style; artificially lofty or grandiloquent.
例句：The buskined actor strode across the stage. 穿着厚底靴的演员大步走过舞台。

例句：Her buskined pronouncements seemed out of place in the casual setting. 她那悲剧腔的声明在这种轻松的场合显得格格不入。

近义词：tragic, dramatic, theatrical, stilted, pompous
反义词：natural, simple, unaffected
词性：adjective


词源:  From buskin (a high boot worn by actors in ancient Greek tragedy) + -ed.  Ultimately from a source akin to Dutch "broek" (trousers).
