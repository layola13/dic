diathermanous:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːrmənəs/| adj.|透热的，能透过热辐射的| Allowing radiant heat to pass through readily; transparent to heat. |例句：Rock salt is diathermanous to radiant heat. 岩盐能透过辐射热。

例句：The diathermanous material allowed the infrared rays to pass through.  这种透热材料允许红外线穿过。|近义词：transcalent, transparent (to heat)|反义词：adiathermanous, opaque (to heat)|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek dia- "through" + thermos "heat" + -ous (adjective forming suffix).
