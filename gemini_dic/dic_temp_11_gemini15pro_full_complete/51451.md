detinue:/dɪˈtɪnjuː/
n.|非法扣留，侵占|The unlawful holding of another's property. |The plaintiff brought an action in detinue against the defendant.|近义词：withholding, retention, detention|反义词: release, surrender|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old French *detenue*, past participle of *detenir* "to detain," from Latin *detinere*, from *de-* "away, from" + *tenere* "to hold."
