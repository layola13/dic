cubits:/ˈkjuːbɪts/| n.|肘尺；腕尺|An ancient unit of length, approximately equal to the length of a forearm from the elbow to the tip of the middle finger, typically about 18 inches (46 centimeters).|The ark was 300 cubits long, 50 cubits wide and 30 cubits high. 方舟长300肘尺，宽50肘尺，高30肘尺。|Synonyms: forearm, ell|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
