mild-steels:/maɪld stiːlz/| n.|低碳钢|A type of steel with a low carbon content, typically less than 0.3%, making it ductile and easily formed. It's the most common type of steel.|Mild steel is often used in construction and manufacturing. 低碳钢常用于建筑和制造业。|Mild steel is commonly used for making car bodies. 低碳钢通常用于制造汽车车身。|synonyms: low-carbon steel, soft steel|antonyms: high-carbon steel, hard steel|noun
