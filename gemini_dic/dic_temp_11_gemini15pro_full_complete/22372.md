booby-trapping: /ˈbuːbiˌtræpɪŋ/ | n. | 诡雷设置；诱杀装置 | The act of setting a booby trap, a hidden device designed to cause harm to an unsuspecting person or animal. |  The soldiers discovered evidence of booby-trapping throughout the abandoned village. 士兵们在废弃的村庄里发现了到处都设有诡雷的证据。

| Example: The enemy's booby-trapping tactics made it incredibly dangerous to advance.  敌人的诡雷战术使得前进变得极其危险。 | Synonym:  mining, rigging (with explosives), setting traps | Antonym: disarming, neutralizing | Noun


booby-trap: /ˈbuːbiˌtræp/ | n. | 诡雷；诱杀装置 | A hidden device designed to cause harm to an unsuspecting person or animal. | The booby trap was cleverly concealed beneath a pile of leaves. 诡雷巧妙地隐藏在一堆树叶下面。

| Example:  The sappers carefully deactivated the booby trap.  工兵小心翼翼地拆除了诡雷。 | Synonym: trap, snare, mine | Antonym: safeguard, protection | Noun

booby-trap: /ˈbuːbiˌtræp/ | vt. | 设置诡雷；设下陷阱 | To set a booby trap. | The retreating soldiers booby-trapped the bridge to slow down the enemy's advance. 撤退的士兵在桥上设置了诡雷，以延缓敌人的前进。

| Example: They booby-trapped the door so the thief would get an unpleasant surprise. 他们在门上设了陷阱，这样小偷就会得到一个不愉快的惊喜。 | Synonym: mine, rig (with explosives), trap | Antonym: disarm, neutralize | Verb
