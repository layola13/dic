flaker:/ˈfleɪkər/| n.|薄片，剥落者，不靠谱的人|A person who is unreliable, especially one who often fails to keep appointments or fulfill promises.  Someone who flakes off or peels away in small pieces.  Something that flakes off.|He's such a flaker; he always cancels plans at the last minute. 他真是个不靠谱的人；他总是最后一刻取消计划。 The paint is flaking off the wall.  油漆正从墙上剥落。  I brushed some flakes of dandruff off my shoulder. 我从肩膀上刷掉一些头皮屑。|近义词：flake, dropout, no-show|反义词：stalwart, reliable|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From flake + -er.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *flakō ("piece, fragment").
