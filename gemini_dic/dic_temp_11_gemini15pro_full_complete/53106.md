diphthongizing:/dɪfˈθɒŋˌaɪzɪŋ/ /dɪfˈθɔŋˌaɪzɪŋ/
verb
双元音化
The process of making a single vowel sound into a diphthong (a sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves toward another).
The Great Vowel Shift in English involved the diphthongizing of several long vowels.  英语中的元音大推移涉及到几个长元音的双元音化。
Over time, the pronunciation of the word "name" has undergone diphthongizing. 随着时间的推移，“name”这个词的发音经历了双元音化。
近义词: changing, altering, modifying
反义词: monophthongizing
词性: verb (present participle)
