frapping:/ˈfræpɪŋ/ | v. | 捆扎；缠绕；用绳索扎紧 | The sailors were frapping the barrels of rum to secure them for the voyage. 水手们正在捆扎朗姆酒桶，以便在航行中固定它们。| The climber frapped the rope around a rock for safety. 攀登者为了安全起见，把绳子缠绕在一块岩石上。 |  近义词：lashing, binding, securing | 反义词：loosening, unfastening, untying | 词性: 动词

词源： From French *frapper, meaning "to strike, hit." The nautical sense of "to bind or draw tight" likely developed from the act of striking a rope tightly around something.
