impregnator:/ɪmˈprɛɡneɪtər/ | n. | 浸渍器，浸渍剂，使怀孕者 |  A person or thing that impregnates something, often with a substance like a sealant or preservative, or, less commonly, causing pregnancy. | The wood impregnator protected the fence posts from rot.  木材浸渍剂保护栅栏柱免于腐烂。 |  The impregnator applied a sealant to the concrete. 浸渍剂在混凝土上涂了一层密封剂。 | synonyms: saturator, infuser, inseminator | antonyms:  extractor, thinner | noun


impregnate:/ɪmˈprɛɡneɪt/ | v. | 使充满；使饱和；使怀孕 | To fill something completely with a substance or quality; to make pregnant. | The air was impregnated with the smell of pine. 空气中充满了松树的气味。 | She was impregnated by her husband. 她被她丈夫使怀孕了。| synonyms: saturate, permeate, infuse, inseminate | antonyms: empty, drain, extract | verb
