out-kissed: /ˌaʊtˈkɪst/
Verb
胜过亲吻; 比...更会亲吻
To have kissed someone more or better than another person has.
She out-kissed him, leaving him breathless and wanting more. 她吻技胜过他，让他 breathless and wanting more.
He out-kissed all his previous girlfriends. 他比以前所有的女朋友都更会接吻。
近义词: out-loved, surpassed in kissing
反义词:  outdone in kissing
