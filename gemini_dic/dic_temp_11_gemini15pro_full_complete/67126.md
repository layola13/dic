exponent:/ɪkˈspoʊnənt/ (US), /ɪkˈspəʊnənt/ (UK)| n.|指数；倡导者；代表人物|A symbol or number placed above and after another symbol or number to denote the power to which the latter is to be raised.|The exponent in the expression 2³ is 3. 在表达式2³中，指数是3。|synonyms: power, index, advocate, champion|antonyms: opponent, critic|noun

exponent:/ɪkˈspoʊnənt/ (US), /ɪkˈspəʊnənt/ (UK)| n.|指数；倡导者；代表人物|A person who supports and promotes a particular idea, cause, or theory.|She is a leading exponent of modern dance. 她是现代舞的领军人物。|synonyms: advocate, proponent, champion, supporter|antonyms: opponent, critic, detractor|noun
