overmournfulness:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrnfəlnəs/
| n. |过度悲伤，极度悲痛|The state of mourning excessively or for too long a period.  A feeling of profound and excessive sadness.|Her overmournfulness after the death of her goldfish was a bit much. 她对金鱼死后表现出的过度悲伤有点过了。|近义词：excessive grief, inconsolable sorrow, extreme sadness|反义词：joy, happiness, cheerfulness|词性:noun
Etymology: over- + mournfulness
over-: prefix meaning "excessive," "too much"
mournfulness: noun from mournful + -ness, meaning "the state of being sad and sorrowful"
