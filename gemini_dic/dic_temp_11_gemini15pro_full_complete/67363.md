extensionist: /ɪkˈstɛnʃənɪst/
n.|推广者，推广员，外展工作者|A person who works in agricultural extension, providing advice and education to farmers and rural communities.  |例句：The extensionist helped the farmers learn new techniques for irrigating their crops. 推广员帮助农民学习新的灌溉技术。

例句：The extensionist advised the community on sustainable farming practices.  推广员就可持续农业实践向社区提供建议。


|近义词：advisor, consultant, educator, outreach worker|反义词: opponent, critic|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "extension" + "-ist". "Extension" comes from Late Latin "extensio" meaning a stretching out.  "-ist" is a suffix forming agent nouns.
