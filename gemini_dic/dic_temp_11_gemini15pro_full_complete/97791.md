injunctively:/ɪnˈdʒʌŋktɪvli/
adv.
强制地，命令式地
in a manner that expresses a command; by way of injunction.
The teacher injunctively told the students to be quiet. 老师命令学生们安静。
Synonyms: commandingly, imperatively, authoritatively
Antonyms: permissively, optionally, suggestively
词性: 副词
