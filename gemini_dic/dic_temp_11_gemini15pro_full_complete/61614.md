emi:/ˈiːemˈaɪ/ (US), /ˈiːmˈiː/ (UK)| n.| 等额每月分期付款 (Equated Monthly Installment)| A fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each calendar month. Amortization of the loan is made by equal monthly payments. Each payment consists of the interest due plus an amount that reduces the remaining principal balance.  |例句：My car loan EMI is $300. 我的汽车贷款每月分期付款是300美元。

例句：The bank calculates the EMI based on the loan amount, interest rate, and loan tenure. 银行根据贷款金额、利率和贷款期限计算每月分期付款。|近义词：monthly payment, installment|反义词: lump sum|noun
