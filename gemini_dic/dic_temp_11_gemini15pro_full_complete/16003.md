bamming:/ˈbæmɪŋ/| n.|欺诈；诈骗|The act of defrauding someone, especially by gaining their trust and then stealing their money or property.  It often involves elaborate schemes and manipulation. |例句：The elderly woman was a victim of a bamming scheme that left her penniless.  这位老妇人是一场诈骗阴谋的受害者，结果身无分文。

例句：They were accused of bamming investors out of millions of dollars.  他们被指控诈骗投资者数百万美元。|近义词：scamming, defrauding, swindling, conning, cheating|反义词:honesty, integrity|词性:名词

Etymology:  Likely derived from "bamboozle," meaning to trick or deceive, perhaps with an added sense of force or impact.
