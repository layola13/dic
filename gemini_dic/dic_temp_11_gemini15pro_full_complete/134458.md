nonjoiners:/ˌnɒnˈdʒɔɪnəz/ | n. | 不加入者；非会员；未参加者 |  A person who does not join a group, organization, or activity, especially when expected or encouraged to do so. | Many young people are nonjoiners when it comes to political parties. 许多年轻人在政党方面都不加入。| The study focused on the reasons why some employees remained nonjoiners in the company's pension scheme.  这项研究的重点是，为什么一些员工仍然没有加入公司的养老金计划。 | synonyms: abstainers, holdouts, outsiders, recluses (in some contexts) | antonyms: members, participants, joiners | noun
