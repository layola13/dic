lancelike:/ˈlænsˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 矛状的，像矛一样的 | Resembling a lance; tapering to a point. | The plant had long, lancelike leaves.  这种植物有长长的矛状叶子。| The knight wielded a lancelike weapon. 骑士挥舞着矛状的武器。 |  similar: spearlike, pointed, tapering | opposite: blunt, rounded | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From lance + -like. Lance (n.) comes from Old French "lance," ultimately from Latin "lancea" meaning "light spear." The suffix "-like" forms adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
