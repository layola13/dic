non-reflective:/ˌnɒn rɪˈflektɪv/ | adj. | 非反射性的；不反光的；不沉思的 | Not reflecting light or other radiation; not given to or marked by reflection or contemplation. | The car had non-reflective paint, making it difficult to see at night. 这辆车的油漆不反光，夜间很难看到。

The report called for non-reflective road markings to improve visibility in wet conditions. 报告呼吁使用非反射性道路标记，以提高潮湿条件下的能见度。 | Near synonyms: matte, dull, opaque | Antonyms: reflective, shiny, glossy | Adjective
