misreferencing: /ˌmɪsrɪˈfɜːrənsɪŋ/ | n. | 错误引用；不准确引用 | The act of referring to something incorrectly or inaccurately, often by citing the wrong source or misrepresenting the original information. | He was accused of misreferencing several key studies in his dissertation.  他的论文被指控错误引用了几个关键研究。 |  misquoting, misattributing, citing incorrectly | accurate referencing, correct citation | noun
