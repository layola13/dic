leave-well-enough-alone:/liːv wɛl ɪˈnʌf əˈloʊn/| idiom | 不多此一举；适可而止；不要画蛇添足 |  To refrain from trying to improve something that is already satisfactory, as further changes might worsen the situation. |  The cake was delicious, so I decided to leave well enough alone and not add any more frosting. 蛋糕已经很美味了，所以我决定适可而止，不再添加任何糖霜了。| synonyms: let sleeping dogs lie, don't rock the boat, if it ain't broke, don't fix it | antonyms: tamper with, meddle with, interfere with | idiom

leave-well-enough-alone:/liːv wɛl ɪˈnʌf əˈloʊn/| idiom | 不画蛇添足 | To avoid interfering with something that is satisfactory or adequate. |  He was tempted to rewrite the entire essay, but decided to leave well enough alone. 他很想重写整篇文章，但决定还是不要画蛇添足了。| synonyms: let it be, keep one's hands off, refrain from meddling | antonyms: interfere, meddle, tinker | idiom
