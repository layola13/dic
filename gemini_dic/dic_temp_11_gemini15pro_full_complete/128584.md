niccolite:/ˈnɪkəlaɪt/ | n. |红砷镍矿|A silver-white to pale-copper-red mineral consisting of nickel arsenide (NiAs). It is an important ore of nickel. |Niccolite is commonly found in hydrothermal veins. 红砷镍矿常见于热液矿脉中。|  Niccolite is a valuable source of nickel. 红砷镍矿是镍的重要来源。|Synonyms: Kupfernickel |Antonyms: N/A | noun
