hygrometry:/haɪˈɡrɒmətri/ | n. | 湿度测量法；湿度测定学 | The science of measuring the humidity of air or other gases. |  Hygrometry plays a crucial role in meteorology. 湿度测量法在气象学中起着至关重要的作用。|  Hygrometry is essential for maintaining optimal conditions in museums. 湿度测量法对于维持博物馆的最佳条件至关重要。|  hygroscopy, psychrometry | N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hygrométrie*, from Ancient Greek ὑγρός (hugrós, “wet, moist”) + -μετρία (-metría, “measurement”).
