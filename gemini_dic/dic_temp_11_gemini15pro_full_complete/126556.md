nadic: /ˈneɪdɪk/ | adj. | 游牧的；流浪的 | Relating to nomads or a nomadic way of life; wandering. |  例句：The nadic tribes moved their herds across the vast plains.  游牧部落将他们的牲畜群迁移到广阔的平原上。

例句：Their nadic lifestyle allowed them to experience a variety of landscapes.  他们的游牧生活方式使他们能够体验各种各样的风景。| 近义词：nomadic, migratory, itinerant, wandering, roving | 反义词：settled, sedentary, stationary | 词性: adjective

词源： From nomad + -ic. Ultimately from Greek nomas, "roaming, wandering."
