embottle:/ɪmˈbɒtl/ /ɛmˈbɑːtl/| vt.|装瓶；抑制，压抑|To put (a liquid) into a bottle; to restrain or repress (feelings, etc.)|The factory embottles thousands of liters of juice every day.  工厂每天将数千升果汁装瓶。

He tried to embottle his anger but it eventually exploded. 他试图压抑自己的愤怒，但最终还是爆发了。|近义词：bottle, contain, suppress, repress|反义词：release, unleash|verb
