convince:/kənˈvɪns/| vt.|说服，使确信|To persuade someone to believe something or to do something.|例句：I managed to convince him to come with us. 我设法说服了他和我们一起来。|近义词：persuade, assure, satisfy|反义词：dissuade, deter|verb

convince:/kənˈvɪns/| vt.|使相信；使信服|To make someone feel certain about something.|例句：She convinced me that she was telling the truth. 她使我相信她说的是实话。|近义词：persuade, assure|反义词：deter, dissuade|verb
