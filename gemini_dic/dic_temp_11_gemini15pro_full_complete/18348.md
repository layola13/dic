bellon:/ˈbɛlɒn/ | n. | 好战;好斗 |A disposition or tendency towards war or fighting; bellicosity. |  He exhibited a bellon, always ready for a fight. 他表现出好战性，随时准备战斗。| Example: His bellon led him into many unnecessary conflicts. 他的好斗使他卷入了许多不必要的冲突。 |Synonyms: bellicosity, pugnacity, aggressiveness | Antonyms: peacefulness, pacifism | Noun

词源:
From French *bellon*, from Latin *bellō, bellāre* "to wage war".  Related to "bellicose".
