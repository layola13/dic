inflect:/ɪnˈflekt/| v. | 使(词)屈折变化; 改变(声音)的音调;  |To change the form of a word (typically a verb or noun) to express a grammatical function such as tense, mood, person, number, case, or gender; To vary the tone or pitch of (one's voice). |She inflected her voice to express her anger. 她改变了声调来表达她的愤怒。| synonyms: modulate, vary, alter | antonyms: maintain, fix, stabilize |verb


inflexion:/ɪnˈflekʃn/| n. | 屈折变化; 音调变化 |The modification of a word to express different grammatical categories such as tense, mood, voice, aspect, person, number, gender, and case;  A change in the tone or pitch of the voice. |The inflection of her voice made her sound sarcastic. 她声调的变化使她听起来很讽刺。| synonyms: modulation, variation, alteration | antonyms: monotony, stability, uniformity |noun
