compunctive:/kəmˈpʌŋktɪv/
adjective
内疚的，后悔的，良心的责备
Feeling or expressing remorse or regret, caused by feeling guilty.
He felt compunctive about leaving his family. 他对离开家人感到内疚。
近义词: remorseful, repentant, contrite, penitent
反义词: unrepentant, remorseless, shameless
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *compunctivus, from Latin compungere "to prick sharply, sting, cause remorse," from com- (intensive) + pungere "to prick."
