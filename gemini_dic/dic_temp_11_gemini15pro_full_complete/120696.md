misadvises:/ˌmɪsədˈvaɪzɪz/ | v. | 误导，错误地劝告 | To give bad or incorrect advice to someone. |  He misadvised his client, leading to a significant financial loss. 他错误地劝告了他的客户，导致了重大的经济损失。

He misadvised her about the investment, and she lost all her money. 他在投资方面误导了她，她赔光了所有的钱。

| 近义词：misguide, misdirect, mislead | 反义词：advise correctly, guide well | 词性：动词
