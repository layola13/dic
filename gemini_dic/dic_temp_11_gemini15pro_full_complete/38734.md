conditionings:/kənˈdɪʃənɪŋz/
n.
条件反射，制约，训练
The process of training or accustoming someone or something to behave in a certain way or to accept certain circumstances.
Pavlov's experiments on dogs are a classic example of classical conditioning. 巴甫洛夫的狗实验是经典条件反射的典型例子。

Repeated conditionings can lead to ingrained habits. 反复的训练可以导致根深蒂固的习惯。

近义词: training, indoctrination, habituation, acclimatization
反义词: deconditioning

词性: 名词 (plural)
