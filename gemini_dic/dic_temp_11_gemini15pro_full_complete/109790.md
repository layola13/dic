levorotatory:/ˌliːvəˈroʊtətɔːri/ /ˌliːvəˈrəʊtəri/
adjective
左旋的
Rotating the plane of polarized light to the left.
例句：Levorotatory substances rotate polarized light counterclockwise. 左旋物质使偏振光逆时针旋转。

例句：The compound was found to be levorotatory.  该化合物被发现是左旋的。

近义词：left-handed (referring to the polarization of light)
反义词：dextrorotatory, right-handed (referring to the polarization of light)
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From French *lévogyre*  and directly from  *lévo-* (from Latin *laevus* "left") + *-rotatory* (from Latin *rotatus*, past participle of *rotare* "to rotate").
