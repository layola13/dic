gas:/ɡæs/ | n. | 气体；汽油；煤气；毒气 | A substance or matter in a state in which it will expand freely to fill the whole of a container, having no fixed shape (unlike a solid) and no fixed volume (unlike a liquid). Also refers to gasoline or poisonous gas. |  Example 1: The balloon was filled with helium gas.  气球里充满了氦气。

Example 2: I need to fill up the car with gas. 我需要给车加满油。 | Synonyms: fuel, vapor, fume | Antonyms: solid, liquid | noun


gas:/ɡæs/ | vt. | 加油；用毒气攻击 | To supply with gas or to attack with poisonous gas. | Example 1: I need to gas up the car before we leave. 我们出发前我得给汽车加满油。

Example 2:  They gassed the enemy trenches during the war. 战争期间他们用毒气攻击了敌人的战壕。 | Synonyms: fuel, inflate | Antonyms: deflate, empty | verb
