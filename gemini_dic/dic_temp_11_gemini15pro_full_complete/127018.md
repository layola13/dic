nashs: /næʃz/ | n. | 纳什均衡 (通常写作 Nash equilibrium) | A concept in game theory where each player's strategy is optimal given the strategies of all other players.  | Example: The Nash equilibrium in this game is for both players to cooperate.  这个游戏中，纳什均衡是两个玩家都选择合作。| Example:  Finding the Nash equilibrium can be complex in games with many players. 在多人游戏中，找到纳什均衡可能很复杂。 | Synonym: equilibrium, solution | Antonym: disequilibrium | noun
