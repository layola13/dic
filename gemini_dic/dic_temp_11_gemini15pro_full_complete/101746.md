irresolutenesses:/ɪˌrezəˈluːtnəsɪz/
|n.|优柔寡断，犹豫不决|The state of being irresolute; inability to decide or take a firm course of action.  This chronic state of irresoluteness prevented him from achieving his goals.|His irresolutenesses cost him the opportunity. 他的犹豫不决让他错失了机会。|Synonyms: indecision, vacillation, hesitation, wavering|Antonyms: decisiveness, resolution, determination|noun

Etymology:
From irresolute + -ness.  Irresolute itself comes from the Latin prefix in- (not) + resolutus (resolved, determined), the past participle of resolvere (to loosen, dissolve, resolve).
