overimitates:/ˌoʊvərˈɪmɪteɪts/
verb
过度模仿
To copy or emulate someone or something excessively, often to the point of inappropriateness or inefficiency.
The child overimitates his older brother, even copying his mistakes.  这个孩子过度模仿他的哥哥，甚至连错误也照搬。
He tends to overimitate the actors he admires, losing his own style in the process. 他倾向于过度模仿他所欣赏的演员，在这个过程中失去了自己的风格。
synonyms:  ape, mimic, parrot, copycat
antonyms:  innovate, originate, create
词性: 动词
