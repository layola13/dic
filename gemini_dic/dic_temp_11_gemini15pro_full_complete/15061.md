babyish:/ˈbeɪbiɪʃ/| adj.|幼稚的，孩子气的|Resembling or characteristic of a baby, especially in behavior; childish.|He spoke in a babyish voice. 他用孩子气的嗓音说话。|近义词：childish, infantile, immature|反义词：mature, adult, grown-up|词性:形容词

Etymology: baby + -ish (suffix forming adjectives from nouns, meaning "like," "of the nature of," "characteristic of")
