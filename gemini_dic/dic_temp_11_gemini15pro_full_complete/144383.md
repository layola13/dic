outstreaming:/ˈaʊtˌstriːmɪŋ/
n.
流出；向外流動
The outstreaming of lava from the volcano was a terrifying sight.  火山熔岩的流出是一幅可怕的景象。
The outstreaming of refugees from the war-torn country continues.  来自饱受战争蹂躏的国家的难民不断涌出。
synonym: outflow, efflux, discharge
antonym: inflow, influx
