chemotaxis:/ˌkɛmoʊˈtæksɪs/| n. |趋化性；趋化作用|The movement of an organism or entity in response to a chemical stimulus.  The organism may move towards or away from the chemical stimulus. |例句：Bacteria exhibit chemotaxis by moving towards a source of nutrients. 细菌通过向营养来源移动来表现出趋化性。

例句：White blood cells use chemotaxis to locate and destroy invading pathogens. 白细胞利用趋化性来定位和摧毁入侵的病原体。|近义词：chemotaxy|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "random motility")|词性:noun
