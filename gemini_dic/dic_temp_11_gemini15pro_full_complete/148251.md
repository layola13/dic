overslavishly:/ˌoʊvərˈsleɪvɪʃli/
adverb
过度奴隶般地，过分奴性地
Acting or working with excessive and unnecessary obedience and devotion.
He followed the instructions overslavishly, missing the opportunity to be creative. 他过分奴隶般地遵照指示，错过了发挥创造力的机会。
Synonyms: subserviently, obsequiously, fawningly, slavishly, submissively
Antonyms: independently, rebelliously, autonomously
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + slavish + -ly.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Slavish" refers to behaving like a slave, showing excessive obedience or imitation. "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
