coscripts:/ˈkoʊˌskrɪpts/
noun
共同撰稿人; 合著者
People who write something together, such as a play, a book, or a screenplay.
Example: The coscripts of the Broadway hit worked tirelessly for years to perfect their masterpiece.  百老汇热门节目的合著者们孜孜不倦地工作了多年，以完善他们的杰作。

Example:  The two coscripts had very different writing styles, which led to some creative tension.  两位合著者的写作风格截然不同，这导致了一些创作上的紧张。
Synonyms: co-authors, collaborators, joint authors
Antonyms: sole author, single author
