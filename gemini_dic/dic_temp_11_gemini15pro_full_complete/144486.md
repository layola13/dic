outthrown:/ˌaʊtˈθroʊn/
adjective
投掷出去的; 超越投掷的
thrown out or beyond
The outthrown ball landed in the neighbor's yard. 投掷出去的球落在了邻居的院子里。
近义词: projected, hurled
反义词: withdrawn, retracted
词性: adjective


outthrown:/ˌaʊtˈθroʊn/
verb (past participle of outthrow)
超越投掷
thrown further than
He was easily outthrown by the other pitcher. 他很容易就被另一个投手超越了投掷距离。
近义词: outdistanced, surpassed
反义词:  underthrown
词性: verb (past participle)


词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning beyond or exceeding) + "thrown" (past participle of "throw")
