coextensive:/ˌkoʊɪkˈstɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 同延的，范围相同的 | Having the same spatial or temporal extent; occupying the same space or time. | The Roman Empire was coextensive with the reach of its legions. 罗马帝国的疆域与其军团的势力范围相同。

The park is coextensive with the city limits.  这个公园与城市边界范围相同。

| 近义词：commensurate, coterminous, equal | 反义词：disparate, different, unequal | 词性: adjective


Etymology:
From co- + extensive, from Latin ex(s)tendere "to stretch out, spread," from ex- "out" + tendere "to stretch."
