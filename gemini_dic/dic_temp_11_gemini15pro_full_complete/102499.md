jacobites:/ˈdʒækəˌbaɪts/| n.|雅各布派（支持詹姆斯二世及其后裔复辟的党派）|Supporters of James II of England and his descendants, especially those who engaged in rebellions in his favor in the late 17th and early 18th centuries.|The Jacobites rose up in rebellion in 1715. 雅各布派在1715年发动了叛乱。|The Jacobites attempted to restore the Stuart monarchy. 雅各布派试图恢复斯图亚特王朝。|Synonyms:  Royalists, Legitimists|Antonyms:  Whigs, Hanoverians|noun
