lawmonger:/ˈlɔːmʌŋɡər/ | n. | 讼棍；好诉讼的人；|A person who stirs up lawsuits or litigation, especially for personal gain.  A person who is excessively litigious.| He was known as a lawmonger, always looking for a reason to sue. 他被称为讼棍，总是在寻找起诉的理由。 |Synonyms: litigant, pettifogger, barrister, lawyer (though not all lawyers are lawmongers), troublemaker,  rabble-rouser (in a legal context)| Antonyms: peacemaker, mediator, arbitrator| noun
