arraign:/əˈreɪn/| vt.|传讯；控告；责难|to call or bring (someone) before a court to answer to a criminal charge.|The suspect was arraigned on charges of murder. 嫌疑犯被提审，罪名是谋杀。|Synonyms: indict, prosecute, accuse|Antonyms: exonerate, acquit|verb
