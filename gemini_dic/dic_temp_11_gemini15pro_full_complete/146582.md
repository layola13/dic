overgraze: /ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪz/ | v. | 过度放牧 | To allow animals to graze (an area) to such an extent that the vegetation is damaged and the ground becomes liable to erosion. | The farmer overgrazed his land, and now it's barren.  这个农民过度放牧了他的土地，现在土地变得贫瘠了。|  The sheep overgrazed the hillside, leading to soil erosion.  羊群过度啃食了山坡，导致水土流失。 | 近义词: overstock | 反义词: undergraze | 词性: 动词

overgraze: /ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 过度放牧 | The practice of grazing too many animals on a piece of land for too long, leading to damage of the vegetation and soil erosion. | Overgrazing is a serious environmental problem in many arid regions. 过度放牧在许多干旱地区是一个严重的环境问题。 | Overgrazing can lead to desertification. 过度放牧会导致沙漠化。 | 近义词: overstocking | 反义词: sustainable grazing | 词性: 名词
