antinaturalists:/ˌæntɪˈnætʃrəlɪsts/| n.|反自然主义者|Those who oppose naturalism, a philosophical viewpoint that emphasizes the natural world and scientific explanations.  They might believe in supernatural forces, or reject the idea that science can explain everything.|The antinaturalists argued that human morality cannot be reduced to mere biological instincts.|近义词：supernaturalists, non-naturalists|反义词:naturalists|Noun
