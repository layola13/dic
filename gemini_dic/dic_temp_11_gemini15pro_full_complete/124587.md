moused:/maʊst/ | v. |  用鼠标操作；搜索；偷偷地走动 | to use a computer mouse; to search for something carefully; to move like a mouse, quietly and cautiously |  She moused over the icon to see the tooltip. 她把鼠标移到图标上查看提示信息。 | 近义词：searched, hunted, crept | 反义词：charged, strode | 词性：动词

moused:/maʊst/ | adj. |  (通常指颜色)灰褐色的，鼠灰色的 | having a grayish brown color, like that of a mouse | The old book had moused pages. 这本旧书的纸张泛黄，呈鼠灰色。| 近义词：grayish-brown, drab | 反义词：bright, colorful | 词性：形容词
