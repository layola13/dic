hearsay:/ˈhɪrseɪ/ | n. |传闻，道听途说|Information received from other people that one cannot adequately substantiate; rumor. | The court case was dismissed because it was based on hearsay.  这起案件被驳回了，因为它基于传闻。|  Example:  The rumor of their breakup is just hearsay.  他们分手的传言只是道听途说。|Synonyms: rumor, gossip, report | Antonyms: evidence, proof, fact | noun
