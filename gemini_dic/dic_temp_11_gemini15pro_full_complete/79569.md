gloomed:/ɡluːmd/ | adj. | 忧郁的，沮丧的 | Feeling depressed and pessimistic; darkened or overcast. | The gloomy weather reflected his mood. 阴沉的天气反映了他的心情。| The room was gloomed by the heavy curtains. 厚重的窗帘使房间变得昏暗。| 近义词:depressed, dejected, despondent, glum, melancholy, sad, somber, sullen | 反义词: cheerful, happy, bright, joyful | 词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "gloom," which originated from the Middle English "gloumen," meaning "to look sullen or gloomy."  Ultimately from a Germanic base meaning "dark."
