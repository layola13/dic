cross-staff: /ˈkrɒsˌstɑːf/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsˌstæf/ (US)| n. | 十字测杆，雅各棒，古测量仪器 |A navigational instrument used to measure the altitude of the sun or stars, consisting of a graduated staff with a sliding crosspiece.|  Sailors used a cross-staff to navigate by the stars. 海员使用十字测杆根据星辰导航。| synonym: Jacob's staff, forestaff, backstaff | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern navigational instruments like sextant)| noun
