mistypes:/ˈmɪsˌtaɪps/ | n. | 打字错误，拼写错误 | Errors made while typing.  These can include transposed letters, omitted letters, or incorrect letters. | Example:  I had several mistypes in my essay that I needed to correct.  我的论文里有好几个打字错误需要修改。

Example: He apologized for the mistypes in his email. 他为他邮件中的拼写错误道歉了。 | 近义词: typos, errors, mistakes | 反义词: accurate typing, correct spelling | 词性: noun
