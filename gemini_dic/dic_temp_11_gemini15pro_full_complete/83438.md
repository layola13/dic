habitancy:/ˈhæbɪtənsi/
n.
居住; 居住权; 居住期间
The state or fact of inhabiting a place; residence.
His habitancy in the town lasted only a few months.  他在镇上只住了几个月。
近义词：residence, dwelling, inhabitation, abode, domicile
反义词：absence
