emotes:/ɪˈmoʊts/| v. |表达感情；流露感情|To express or convey (an emotion) nonverbally; to show or display (an emotion).|She emotes freely on stage, captivating the audience. 她在舞台上尽情表达情感，深深吸引了观众。|Synonyms: express, convey, display, manifest, project|Antonyms: repress, conceal, suppress, hide|verb

emotes:/ɪˈmoʊts/| n. |表情符号|A small digital image or icon used to express an idea or emotion in electronic communication.|He added a laughing emote to his message. 他在信息里加了一个大笑的表情符号。|Synonyms: emoji, emoticon|Antonyms: N/A|noun
