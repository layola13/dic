nonarousals:/nɒnəˈraʊzəlz/ | n. | 无唤起，无性唤起 |  A state characterized by the lack of physiological signs of sexual arousal.  This term is often used in the context of sleep studies, particularly in diagnosing sleep disorders like sleep apnea. | During the sleep study, the patient experienced several nonarousals despite drops in oxygen saturation.  在睡眠研究中，尽管血氧饱和度下降，病人仍经历了几次无唤起。 |  Near synonyms: non-responsiveness, unresponsiveness | Antonyms: arousals, awakenings | noun
