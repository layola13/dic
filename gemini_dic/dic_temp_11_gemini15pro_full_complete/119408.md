microfiching: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌfɪtʃɪŋ/
noun
缩微胶卷拍摄; 把…拍成缩微胶卷
The process of photographing documents and storing them on microfiche.
Libraries are increasingly using microfiching to preserve old newspapers.  图书馆越来越多地使用缩微胶卷来保存旧报纸。
近义词: microfilming
反义词: None
词性: n. (noun)

microfiche: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌfiːʃ/ | n. | 缩微胶片 | A sheet of microfilm containing microphotographs of the pages of a newspaper, catalogue, or other document. | The library has the entire newspaper archive on microfiche. 图书馆用缩微胶片保存了整份报纸的档案。 | 近义词: microfilm | 反义词: None
