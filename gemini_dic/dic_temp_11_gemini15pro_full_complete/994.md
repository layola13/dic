acaridan:/ˈækərɪdən/| n. |螨虫|A small arachnid, typically less than 1 mm long, that has four pairs of legs when adult, related to ticks and spiders. Many kinds live in the soil, leaf litter, or water, while others are parasites of plants or animals.|The house dust mite is a common acaridan that can cause allergies.|近义词：mite|反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑螨虫的天敌，例如：predatory mite 捕食螨)|词性:noun
acaridans:/ˈækərɪdənz/| n. |螨虫(复数)|Plural form of acaridan, referring to multiple small arachnids of the order Acarina.|Acaridans are a diverse group, including species that are beneficial to ecosystems and others that are harmful pests.|近义词：mites|反义词: (无明确的反义词)|词性:noun
