disrespecting:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktɪŋ/
verb
不尊重，轻视
The act of showing a lack of respect or courtesy towards someone or something.
He was disrespecting his elders by talking back. 他顶嘴，对长辈不敬。


disrespecting:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktɪŋ/
gerund or present participle
不尊重，轻视
The act of showing a lack of respect or courtesy towards someone or something.
Disrespecting your teachers is unacceptable. 对你的老师不敬是不可接受的。


Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + respect + -ing (present participle suffix).  Ultimately from Latin respectus "regard," from respicere "look back at, regard, consider," from re- "back" + specere "to look."

近义词:insulting, slighting, offending, dishonoring
反义词:respecting, honoring, revering
