foreboding:/fɔːrˈboʊdɪŋ/ /fɔːˈbəʊdɪŋ/| n. adj.|预感; 预兆; 凶兆|A feeling that something bad will happen; an omen.|例句：She had a foreboding that something terrible was going to happen. 她预感到将要发生可怕的事情。|近义词：premonition, presentiment, apprehension, omen, portent|反义词：assurance, hope, optimism|词性:名词, 形容词

foreboding:/fɔːrˈboʊdɪŋ/ /fɔːˈbəʊdɪŋ/| adj.|不祥的; 恶兆的|Suggesting that something bad is going to happen.|例句：The dark clouds were a foreboding sign. 乌云是不祥之兆。|近义词：ominous, sinister, threatening, portentous|反义词：auspicious, promising, encouraging|词性:形容词
