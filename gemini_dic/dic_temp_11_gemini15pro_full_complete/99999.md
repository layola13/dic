例句：The lost child gave her an interpealing look.  迷路的孩子恳求地看着她。

例句：The interpealing music brought tears to her eyes.  感人的音乐让她热泪盈眶。

近义词：pleading, beseeching, imploring, supplicating, entreating
反义词：demanding, commanding, assertive
