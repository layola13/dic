augitic: /ɔːˈdʒɪtɪk/ | adj. |辉石的，含辉石的| Containing or resembling augite, a common rock-forming mineral of the pyroxene group. | The geologist identified the rock as augitic basalt.  地质学家将这块岩石鉴定为辉石玄武岩。|

The soil sample contained augitic fragments. 土壤样本中含有辉石碎片。| 近义词: pyroxenic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps non-augitic) | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From augite + -ic.  Augite comes from the Greek word "augēs" meaning "brightness," referring to the luster of the mineral's cleaved surfaces.
