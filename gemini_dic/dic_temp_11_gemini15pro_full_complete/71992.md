fling:/flɪŋ/
verb
投，扔，猛冲，急行
To throw or hurl forcefully; to move oneself or a part of one's body suddenly and with force.
He flung the ball across the field. 他把球扔过田野。

She flung her arms around his neck. 她猛地搂住他的脖子。

近义词:throw, hurl, toss, cast, chuck
反义词:catch, receive

fling:/flɪŋ/
noun
一时的放纵，尽情欢乐，短暂的恋爱
A short period of unrestrained indulgence in pleasure or an activity; a brief, intense love affair.
They had a summer fling in Paris. 他们在巴黎有过一段夏日恋情。

He went on a fling and spent all his savings. 他尽情挥霍，花光了所有积蓄。

近义词:affair, romance, spree, binge
反义词:commitment
