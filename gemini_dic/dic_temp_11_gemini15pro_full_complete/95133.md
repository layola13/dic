inapplicablenesses:/ˌɪnəˈplɪkəbəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 不适用性；不适用之处 | The inapplicablenesses of the old law to modern society are obvious.  旧法律对现代社会的不适用性是显而易见的。 |  The report highlighted the inapplicablenesses of the proposed solution.  这份报告强调了提议的解决方案的不适用性。 | 近义词: irrelevancies, unsuitabilities | 反义词: applicabilities, suitabilities | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
in- (not) + applicable (capable of being applied) + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives)
