interfoliaceous:/ˌɪntərˌfoʊliˈeɪʃəs/
adjective
叶间的，生于叶间的
Growing between leaves, especially between the layers of a compound leaf.
The interfoliaceous stipules are a key characteristic of this plant species.  这种植物的叶间托叶是其关键特征。
The botanist identified the rare fern by its interfoliaceous flowers.  植物学家通过其叶间的花朵识别出了这种稀有蕨类植物。
Synonyms: interfoliar
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "terminal" or "apical" if referring to leaf position)
词性: adjective
