overclemency: /ˌoʊvərˈklɛmənsi/
n.
过度宽容，过分仁慈
Excessive leniency or mercy.
The judge was accused of overclemency in sentencing the convicted murderer to only a short prison term.  法官因只判处被定罪的杀人犯很短的监禁而被指责过度宽容。
His overclemency towards his children resulted in their lack of discipline. 他对孩子们的过度宽容导致他们缺乏纪律。
Synonyms: leniency, indulgence, forbearance
Antonyms: severity, harshness, strictness
