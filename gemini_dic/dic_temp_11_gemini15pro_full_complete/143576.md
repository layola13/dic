outhousing:/ˈaʊtˌhaʊzɪŋ/
n.
外屋；附属建筑；户外厕所
A small building such as a shed or barn, separate from but near to a main house; an outdoor toilet.
The outhouse needs a new coat of paint.  外屋需要重新粉刷一下。
synonyms: outbuilding, shed, annex, privy, latrine
antonyms: main house, main building
