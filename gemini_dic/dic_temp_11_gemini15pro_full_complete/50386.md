deports:/dɪˈpɔːrts/
verb
驱逐出境
to force (a person who is not a citizen) to leave a country
例句：The government deports illegal immigrants. 政府驱逐非法移民。

例句：He was deported for his involvement in drug trafficking. 他因参与贩毒而被驱逐出境。
近义词：expels, banishes, exiles
反义词：admits, welcomes
词性：v.


deportation:/ˌdiːpɔːrˈteɪʃn/
noun
驱逐出境
the act of deporting a foreigner from a country
例句：The deportation of illegal immigrants is a complex issue. 非法移民的驱逐出境是一个复杂的问题。

例句：He faced deportation after his visa expired. 他的签证到期后，他面临被驱逐出境。
近义词：expulsion, banishment, exile
反义词：admission, admittance
词性：n.
