columbine:/ˈkɒləmbaɪn/ (US) /ˈkɒləmbɪn/ (UK)| adj. n.|鸽子的；鸽子似的；(指植物)耧斗菜；纯洁的；天真的|Of, relating to, or resembling a dove or pigeon.  Also, a plant of the genus *Aquilegia*, having showy, spurred flowers.  Figuratively, gentle and innocent.|The columbine's delicate flowers swayed in the breeze.  耧斗菜娇嫩的花朵在微风中摇曳。|Synonyms: dove-like, gentle, innocent, pure|Antonyms: fierce, aggressive, impure|adjective, noun
