interanimate:/ˌɪntərˈænɪmeɪt/ | adj. |互相赋予生命；使有生气；使相互影响 |To give life to each other; to animate reciprocally; to make interact and influence each other. |  The interanimate relationship between the artist and her muse resulted in a series of breathtaking paintings. 艺术家和她缪斯之间相互激发的关系催生了一系列惊人的画作。

 The interanimate forces of nature, such as the sun and the rain, allow life to flourish.  像太阳和雨水这样相互作用的自然力量，让生命蓬勃发展。 | 近义词: interactive, reciprocal, symbiotic | 反义词: inanimate, lifeless | 词性: adjective

词源: From inter- + animate.  "Inter-" prefix meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally." "Animate" meaning "to give life to."
