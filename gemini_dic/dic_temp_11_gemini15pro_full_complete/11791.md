arraign:/əˈreɪn/ | v. |传讯；控告；责难|to call or bring (someone) before a court to answer to an indictment | The suspect was arraigned on charges of murder.  嫌疑犯被提审，罪名是谋杀。| synonyms: indict, prosecute, accuse | antonyms: acquit, exonerate | verb

arraign:/əˈreɪn/ | v. |责难；谴责|to accuse or criticize (someone) harshly or publicly |  He was arraigned by the media for his irresponsible behavior. 他因不负责任的行为受到媒体的谴责。| synonyms: censure, rebuke, denounce | antonyms: praise, commend | verb
