nonantigenic:/ˌnɒnˌæntiˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
非抗原性的
Not antigenic; not causing an immune response.
This modified virus is nonantigenic and thus will not trigger an immune response.  这种改良的病毒是非抗原性的，因此不会引发免疫反应。
The researchers developed a nonantigenic coating for the implant. 研究人员为植入物开发了一种非抗原性涂层。
Synonyms: non-immunogenic, non-immunizing
Antonyms: antigenic, immunogenic
