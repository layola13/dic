eyebar:/ˈaɪbɑːr/
n.
拉杆，眼杆
A metal bar or rod with a hole (eye) at each end, used for connecting parts of a structure or machine, often under tension.
The bridge's suspension system relied on a series of strong eyebars.  这座桥的悬挂系统依靠一系列坚固的眼杆。
The eyebar failed under the excessive load.  拉杆在过载下失效了。
Synonyms: tie rod, connecting rod
Antonyms: N/A
