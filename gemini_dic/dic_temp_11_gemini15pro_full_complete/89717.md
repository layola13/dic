hopefully:/ˈhoʊpfʊli/ (US), /ˈhəʊpfʊli/ (UK)| adv.|但愿；抱有希望地；可望|Used to express what you hope will happen.  It modifies the whole sentence rather than just a single word or phrase.  While common, its use as a sentence adverb is sometimes criticized.|Hopefully, the weather will be good tomorrow. 但愿明天天气好。|Synonyms: ideally, optimistically, with any luck|Antonyms: unfortunately, pessimistically, sadly|adverb
