begummed:/bɪˈɡʌmd/
adjective
涂了树胶的；粘糊糊的
covered with or smeared with gum or a sticky substance
The antique table was begummed with years of varnish. 这张古董桌子涂了多年的清漆，粘糊糊的。
sticky, gummy, gooey, viscid
clean, dry
词性: 形容词

词源：

注意事项:
