non-heuristic:/ˌnɒn hjuˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非启发式的 | Not using or involving heuristics; not based on trial and error or rules of thumb.  It implies a systematic, algorithmic, or logical approach rather than one based on intuition or educated guesses. | The program employs a non-heuristic algorithm to guarantee an optimal solution.  这个程序采用非启发式算法来保证最优解。

The non-heuristic approach to problem-solving, while sometimes slower, is more likely to yield the correct answer.  虽然有时速度较慢，但非启发式解决问题的方法更有可能产生正确答案。 | antonyms: heuristic | synonyms: algorithmic, systematic, logical | adjective
