giglot:/ˈɡɪɡlət/ | adj. n. |轻佻的女人；放荡的女人 |A woman who is sexually flirtatious or promiscuous.  A giglot is considered frivolous and lacking in moral restraint, often used in a disapproving or archaic way. |She behaved like a giglot, flirting with every man in the room. 她表现得像个轻佻的女人，和房间里的每个男人调情。|  The play portrayed the queen as a giglot, which angered many historians.  这出戏把女王描绘成一个放荡的女人，这激怒了许多历史学家。 | 近义词: flirt, coquette, wanton, promiscuous | 反义词: chaste, modest, virtuous | 词性: adjective, noun |词源: From Middle English *gigelot, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to giggle.
