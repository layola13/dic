cognates:/ˈkɒɡneɪts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡneɪts/ (US)| n. |同源词|Words in different languages that have a common ancestor.|English "father" and German "Vater" are cognates. |英语“father”和德语“Vater”是同源词。|近义词：related words, similar words|反义词:unrelated words|noun

cognate:/ˈkɒɡneɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡneɪt/ (US)| adj. |同源的|Related by descent, origin, or common ancestry.|Spanish and Portuguese are cognate languages. |西班牙语和葡萄牙语是同源语言。|近义词：related, kindred, similar|反义词:unrelated, dissimilar|adjective
