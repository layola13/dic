forefend:/fɔːrˈfɛnd/ | v. | 预防，防止，避免 | To avert or prevent (something evil or unpleasant). |  例句：May fate forefend such a tragedy. 愿命运阻止这样的悲剧发生。

例句：They built a high wall to forefend attacks from wild animals. 他们筑起高墙以防野兽袭击。| 近义词：prevent, avert, preclude, obviate | 反义词：cause, invite, precipitate | 词性：动词

forefend:/fɔːrˈfɛnd/ | v. | 保护，捍卫 | To protect or guard. |  例句：He prayed that God would forefend him from harm. 他祈祷上帝保佑他免受伤害。

例句：The amulet was believed to forefend the wearer from evil spirits.  人们相信这个护身符可以保护佩戴者免受邪灵侵扰。 | 近义词：protect, defend, safeguard, shield | 反义词：expose, endanger | 词性：动词
