chaplainries:/ˈtʃæplɪnriz/
n.
牧师职位；牧师的俸禄；牧师的管辖区
The position or office of a chaplain, often including the provision of financial support or the territory under their spiritual jurisdiction.
The king endowed several chaplainries in the new cathedral. 国王在新教堂设立了几个牧师职位。
近义词: chaplaincies, ministries
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps secular offices)
词性: noun
