backtrackers:/ˈbækˌtrækərz/
n.
回溯者；追踪者；倒退者
People or algorithms that backtrack, often in problem-solving or navigation.
The backtrackers returned to the fork in the road to try the other path.  回溯者们回到了岔路口，尝试另一条路。
The algorithm uses backtrackers to find the optimal solution.  该算法使用回溯法来找到最佳解决方案。
近义词：retreaters, searchers
反义词：advancers
词性：名词

backtrack:/ˈbæktræk/
v.
回溯；折返；撤回声明
To go back over the same route; to withdraw a statement.
We had to backtrack about a mile to find the turnoff. 我们不得不折返大约一英里才能找到岔路口。
The politician backtracked on his earlier promise. 这位政客撤回了先前的承诺。
近义词：retreat, reverse, recant
反义词：advance, proceed
词性：动词
