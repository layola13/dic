consignatory:/kənˌsaɪnəˈtɔːri/ /kənˈsaɪɡnəˌtɔːri/| n.|收货人；受托人|A person or firm to whom goods are shipped or entrusted.|The consignatory will receive the shipment next week. 收货人下周将收到货物。|The goods were shipped to the consignatory in London. 货物被运往伦敦的收货人。|Synonyms: consignee, recipient, receiver|Antonyms: consignor, shipper, sender|noun

consignatory:/kənˌsaɪnəˈtɔːri/ /kənˈsaɪɡnəˌtɔːri/| adj.|委托的|Relating to or characteristic of a consignatory.|He has consignatory rights to the property. 他拥有该财产的委托权。|The consignatory agreement outlines the terms of the shipment. 委托协议概述了货运条款。|Synonyms: consigned, entrusted|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
