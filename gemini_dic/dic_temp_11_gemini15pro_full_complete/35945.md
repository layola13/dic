coddler:/ˈkɒdlə(r)/ (US) /ˈkɑːdlər/| n.|娇惯者，溺爱者；煮蛋器|A person who treats someone with excessive care and attention; a kitchen utensil for cooking eggs.|She's such a coddler with her children, they're never allowed to do anything for themselves. 她太溺爱她的孩子们了，从来不让他们自己动手做任何事。|The eggs were perfectly cooked in the coddler. 鸡蛋在煮蛋器里煮得恰到好处。|Synonyms: pamperer, spoiler, overprotector|Antonyms: disciplinarian, strict parent|noun


coddle:/ˈkɒdl/ (US) /ˈkɑːdl/| v.|娇惯，溺爱；细心照料|Treat (someone) with excessive care and attention.|She coddles her dog like it's a baby. 她溺爱她的狗，就像对待婴儿一样。|He was coddled and protected by his family. 他被家人娇惯和保护着。|Synonyms: pamper, spoil, indulge, baby|Antonyms: neglect, discipline|verb
