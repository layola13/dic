hybridisms:/ˈhaɪbrɪdɪzəmz/
n.
混合词; 杂交词
A word or expression that is a mixture of two or more languages or dialects.
Hybridisms are common in modern English, borrowing from languages like French and Latin.  现代英语中混合词很常见，借用了像法语和拉丁语等语言的词汇。
The word "spork" (spoon + fork) is a common example of a hybridism.  “spork”（勺子+叉子）这个词是混合词的一个常见例子。
synonyms: blends, portmanteau words, mixtures
antonyms: pure words, unmixed words
noun
