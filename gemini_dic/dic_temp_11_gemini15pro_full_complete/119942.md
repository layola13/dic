A weak or effeminate man or boy.|He's such a milksop, he wouldn't even stand up for himself. 他真是个懦夫，甚至不敢为自己辩护。|Synonyms: weakling, coward, sissy, wimp|Antonyms: hero, tough guy, braveheart|noun
