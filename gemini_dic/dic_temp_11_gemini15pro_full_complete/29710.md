catholicize:/kəˈθɒlɪsaɪz/ | v. |使皈依天主教，使普遍化|To convert to Catholicism or to make something more universal or broad-minded. | The missionaries tried to catholicize the indigenous people.  传教士试图使土著居民皈依天主教。

The university is trying to catholicize its curriculum by offering more diverse courses.  大学正试图通过提供更多样化的课程来使其课程更具普遍性。| 近义词：universalize, broaden, liberalize | 反义词：restrict, narrow, sectarianize | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *catholicisāre*, from Latin catholicus (“universal, general”), from Ancient Greek καθολικός (katholikós, “universal, general”).
