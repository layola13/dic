goingest:/ˈɡoʊɪŋɪst/ | adj. | 最活跃的，最热衷的 |  A superlative form of "going," used informally and often humorously to describe someone very enthusiastic and active.  It implies being the most engaged or involved in something. |  He's the goingest guy I know, always up for anything. 他是我认识的最活跃的人，总是乐于尝试任何事情。 | She's the goingest volunteer at the animal shelter. 她是动物收容所里最热心的志愿者。 | 近义词:  energetic, enthusiastic, active, dynamic | 反义词:  lethargic, apathetic, inactive, passive | 词性: 形容词

词源: "goingest" is a non-standard superlative form of "going," formed by adding "-est" as if it were a regular adjective. While grammatically incorrect, it's used colloquially for emphasis.
