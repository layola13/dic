norian:/ˈnɔːriən/| adj. n.|诺利期,诺利阶的|Relating to or denoting the Norian stage, the uppermost stage of the Triassic period, between the Carnian and Rhaetian stages, or the system of rocks deposited during it.  It lasted from approximately 228 to 208.5 million years ago.|The Norian saw the diversification of many dinosaur groups. 诺利期见证了许多恐龙群体的多样化。| The Norian strata in this region are rich in fossils.  这个地区的诺利阶地层富含化石。|近义词：Late Triassic, Upper Triassic|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially earlier Triassic stages like Carnian or Anisian)|词性: adjective, noun
