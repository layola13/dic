adjective

The lethiferous venom of the snake killed the mouse within seconds.  这蛇的致命毒液在几秒钟内就杀死了老鼠。
The battlefield was a lethiferous landscape. 战场是一片致命的景象。
synonyms: deadly, fatal, mortal, lethal
antonyms: life-giving, life-sustaining
