daguerreotype:/dəˈɡɛrəˌtaɪp/ |n.|银版照相；银版照片|A photograph taken by an early photographic process employing an iodine-sensitized silvered plate and mercury vapor.|The museum has a fascinating collection of daguerreotypes from the 19th century.  博物馆收藏了19世纪引人入胜的银版照片。|Synonyms: photograph, image, picture|Antonyms: None (lacks a direct opposite)|noun


daguerreotypes:/dəˈɡɛrəˌtaɪps/ |n.|银版照相；银版照片（复数）|Photographs taken by an early photographic process employing an iodine-sensitized silvered plate and mercury vapor.|The daguerreotypes showed the fashions and faces of a bygone era. 这些银版照片展现了过去时代的时尚和面孔。|Synonyms: photographs, images, pictures|Antonyms: None (lacks a direct opposite)|noun
