flatteners:/ˈflæt(ə)nərz/
noun
扁平化的事物；夷平的事物；使变平的事物
Things or factors that flatten something, often referring to globalization or competitive forces.
The book "The World Is Flat" discusses various "flatteners" that have leveled the global playing field.
《世界是平的》这本书讨论了各种使全球竞争环境趋于平等的“扁平化因素”。
Globalization is one of the major flatteners of the 21st century.
全球化是21世纪主要的扁平化因素之一.
levelers, equalizers
hierarchies, barriers

flatten:/ˈflæt(ə)n/
verb
使变平；夷平；压扁；击倒
To make something flat or flatter.
He flattened the dough with a rolling pin.
他用擀面杖把面团擀平。
The boxer flattened his opponent with a single punch.
这位拳击手一拳就把对手击倒在地。
level, smooth, crush
raise, elevate, build

flat:/flæt/
adjective
平的；平坦的；扁平的
Having a smooth, level surface.
The Netherlands is a very flat country.
荷兰是一个非常平坦的国家。
The tire went flat after hitting a nail.
轮胎扎了钉子后漏气了。
level, smooth, even
uneven, bumpy, hilly

flat:/flæt/
noun
一套房间；公寓
A set of rooms for living in, typically on one floor of a building.
They live in a flat in the city center.
他们住在市中心的一套公寓里。
apartment, suite, unit
