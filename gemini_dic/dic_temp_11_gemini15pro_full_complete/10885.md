arachnology: /əˈrækˈnɒlədʒi/ (US) /əˈræɡˈnɒlədʒi/ (UK)| n. |蜘蛛学|The scientific study of spiders and related animals such as scorpions, mites, and ticks.|She's a leading expert in arachnology and has discovered several new species of spider.|近义词: None (highly specific)|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology:
From Greek ἀράχνη (aráchnē, “spider”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
