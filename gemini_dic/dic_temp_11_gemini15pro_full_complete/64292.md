equivalence:/ɪˈkwɪvələns/| n.|等价；等值；等效；相等|The state or fact of being equivalent; equality in value, meaning, force, effect, etc.|例句：There is an equivalence between the two descriptions. 这两种描述是等价的。

例句：The teacher explained the equivalence of the two formulas. 老师解释了这两个公式的等效性。|近义词：equality, parity, similarity, correspondence|反义词：inequality, disparity, difference|词性:名词

词源: Late 16th century: from French équivalence, from médiéval Latin aequivalentia, from aequivalent- ‘equivalent’ (see equivalent).
