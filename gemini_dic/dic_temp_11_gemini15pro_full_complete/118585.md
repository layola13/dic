mesothorax:/ˌmeːzəˈθɔːræks/ /ˌmiːzəˈθɔːræks/| n. |中胸|The middle of the three segments of an insect's thorax, bearing the middle legs and, in winged insects, the forewings.|The mesothorax of a dragonfly is particularly robust. 蜻蜓的中胸特别强壮。|Synonym: mid-thorax|Antonym: N/A|noun
