overdramatizing: /ˌōvərˈdräməˌtīziNG/
verb
过分戏剧化；夸张；小题大做
Exaggerating the seriousness or emotional impact of something.
She was overdramatizing the situation, it wasn't that big of a deal. 她把情况夸大了，其实没什么大不了的。
He tends to overdramatize his illnesses, making a common cold sound like pneumonia. 他总是夸大自己的病情，把普通的感冒说得像肺炎一样。
近义词: exaggerating, embellishing, amplifying, inflating
反义词: downplaying, understating, minimizing
词性: verb (present participle)

overdramatize: /ˌōvərˈdräməˌtīz/
verb
过分戏剧化；夸张；小题大做
To exaggerate the seriousness or emotional impact of something.
Stop overdramatizing! It's just a scratch. 别小题大做了！只是个划伤而已。
He has a tendency to overdramatize every minor setback. 他总是把每个小挫折都夸大。
近义词: exaggerate, embellish, amplify, inflate
反义词: downplay, understate, minimize
词性: verb

词源: over- + dramatize.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Dramatize" comes from the Greek word "drama" meaning "action, play".
