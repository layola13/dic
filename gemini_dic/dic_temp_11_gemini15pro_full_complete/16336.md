barbarically: /bɑːrˈbærɪkli/ | adv. | 野蛮地，残暴地 | in a cruel and violent way, like a barbarian |  The prisoners were barbarically tortured. 囚犯们遭到了野蛮的折磨。 | synonym: savagely, brutally, cruelly, inhumanely | antonym: humanely, kindly, compassionately | 词性: 副词

barbaric: /bɑːrˈbærɪk/ | adj. | 野蛮的，残暴的 | extremely cruel and violent |  The barbaric treatment of the prisoners was condemned by human rights organizations.  囚犯的野蛮待遇受到了人权组织的谴责。| synonym: savage, brutal, cruel, inhuman | antonym: humane, kind, compassionate, civilized | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin barbaricus, from Latin barbarus ‘foreign, barbarous’, from Greek barbaros (originally imitative of unintelligible speech).
