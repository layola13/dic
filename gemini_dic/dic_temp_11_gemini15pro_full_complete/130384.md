non-vascular:/ˌnɒnˈvæskjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 非维管的；无维管束的 | Lacking vascular tissue; not having xylem and phloem, the tissues in vascular plants that circulate water and nutrients. | Mosses are non-vascular plants. 苔藓是非维管植物。 | Non-vascular plants absorb water directly through their surfaces.  非维管植物直接通过表面吸收水分。 |近义词: avascular | 反义词: vascular | 词性: 形容词
