heliographies:/ˌhiːliˈɒɡrəfiz/ |n.|日光照片; 日光照相术; 日光照相(术)复制品|A heliography is a photograph made by the heliographic process. 日光照片是通过日光照相术制作的照片。|The museum has a collection of early heliographies.  这家博物馆收藏了早期的日光照片。|Synonyms: sun prints, photoheliographs|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, possibly consider "photograms" or "chemigrams" as alternative processes)|Noun
