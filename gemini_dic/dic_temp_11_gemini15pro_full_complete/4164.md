airbursts:/ˈɛərbɜːrsts/
n.
空中爆炸
An explosion that occurs in the air, typically of a meteor or other object entering the atmosphere, rather than impacting the ground.
The meteor created a spectacular airburst visible for miles.  这颗流星在空中爆炸，壮观的景象几英里外都能看到。
The airburst destroyed the incoming missile. 空中爆炸摧毁了来袭的导弹。

近义词: aerial explosion, atmospheric explosion
反义词: ground burst, surface explosion
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From air + burst.  "Burst" comes from the Old English *berstan, meaning "to break."
