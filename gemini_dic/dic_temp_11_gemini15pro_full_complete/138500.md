novitiate:/noʊˈvɪʃɪɪt/| n.|见习期，见习僧(尼)，新手|a period of training and instruction for a novice, especially in a religious order.  A person who is new to a field or activity; a beginner.|例句：She spent her novitiate in a remote convent. 她在一个偏远的修道院度过了她的见习期。

例句：He was still a novitiate in the world of finance. 他在金融界还是个新手。|近义词：apprentice, beginner, trainee, probationer, tyro|反义词：expert, veteran, professional|词性:noun
