overpartially:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːrʃəli/ | adv. | 过度部分地，部分过度地 |  Done more than necessary in some parts but not others, resulting in an uneven or unbalanced outcome. | The cake was overpartially baked, with some parts burnt and others still raw. 蛋糕烤得过度部分，有些地方烤焦了，有些地方还是生的。| The field was overpartially irrigated, leading to waterlogging in some areas and dryness in others. 田地过度部分灌溉，导致一些地区涝灾，另一些地区干旱。| 近义词: unevenly, disproportionately | 反义词: evenly, uniformly | 词性: 副词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much") + "partially" (adverb meaning "in part" or "to some extent").  The word combines the idea of doing something excessively with the idea of doing it only in some parts.
