/ˈælfəz/ | n. | 字母表，字母 | Letters of a language, especially the Greek alphabet. | The company's name begins with the first three alphas. 这家公司的名称以头三个字母开头。| synonyms: letters, characters | antonyms: None | noun

/ˈælfəz/ | n. | α粒子 | A positively charged particle consisting of two protons and two neutrons, identical to the nucleus of a helium atom. | Alphas are emitted during radioactive decay. α粒子在放射性衰变过程中发射出来。| synonyms: alpha particle, helium nucleus | antonyms: None | noun

/ˈælfəz/ | n. | (通常指金融领域的)阿尔法 | A measure of the active return on an investment, the performance of that investment compared with a suitable market index. | The fund manager seeks to generate alpha for investors. 基金经理力求为投资者创造阿尔法。| synonyms: excess return, active return | antonyms: beta | noun
