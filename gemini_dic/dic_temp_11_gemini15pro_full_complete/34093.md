civilizee:/ˈsɪvɪləˌziː/
noun
被开化者，被教化者
A person who is being civilized or has been civilized, especially one belonging to a non-Western culture.
The missionaries saw the islanders as civilizees who needed to be converted.  传教士将岛民视为需要皈依的被开化者。
Colonial powers often viewed the indigenous populations as civilizees in need of their guidance. 殖民列强经常将土著居民视为需要他们指导的被开化者。
近义词: convert, neophyte, pupil
反义词: barbarian, savage
词性: noun

词源:
From civilize + -ee, a suffix forming nouns denoting the person who is the object of the verb's action.  来源于civilize + -ee，一个构成名词的后缀，表示动词动作的对象。
