elaborator:/ɪˈlæbəreɪtər/
n.|详细阐述者，详细说明者|A person who elaborates; one who explains or develops something in detail.|The elaborator provided a comprehensive explanation of the complex process.|近义词：explainer, commentator, expounder, interpreter|反义词：summarizer, abbreviator|词性:noun
Etymology:  From Late Latin elaboratus, past participle of elaborare "to work out, produce by labor," from ex- "out" + laborare "to work," from labor "work, toil."
