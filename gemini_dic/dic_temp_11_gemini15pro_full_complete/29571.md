catchwater:/ˈkætʃˌwɔːtər/ | n. | 集水沟，截水沟 | A ditch or channel for intercepting surface water runoff. |  The farmer dug a catchwater above his fields to prevent erosion.  农民在他的田地上方挖了一条截水沟以防止水土流失。

| Synonyms: drain, ditch, channel, culvert | Antonyms: spillway, outlet | noun

catchwater:/ˈkætʃˌwɔːtər/ | n. | 截流的水 | Water that is intercepted and collected. | The catchwater from the roof was collected in a cistern. 屋顶截流的雨水被收集在一个蓄水池里。


| Synonyms: runoff, rainwater, collected water | Antonyms: discharged water, wastewater | noun
