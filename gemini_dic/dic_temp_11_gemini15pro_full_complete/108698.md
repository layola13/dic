leally: /ˈliːəli/ | adv. | 忠诚地，诚实地 | In a loyal manner; faithfully. | She served her country leally and bravely. 她忠诚勇敢地为国家服务。|  He leally defended his friend. 他忠诚地维护他的朋友。 | 近义词: faithfully, loyally, truly, honestly | 反义词: disloyally, treacherously, falsely, deceptively | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "leal" + "-ly".  "Leal" comes from Old French "leal,"  ultimately from Latin "legalis" meaning "legal, lawful."  The suffix "-ly" transforms adjectives into adverbs.
