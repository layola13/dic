hassler:/ˈhæslər/| n.| 骗子；强买强卖的人；敲诈勒索者|Someone who aggressively sells things or demands payment for unwanted services. | He was a real hassler, always trying to sell fake watches to tourists. 他是个十足的骗子，总是试图向游客兜售假表。|  The street hassler demanded money for "watching" my car. 街上的敲诈勒索者要求我付钱让他“看”我的车。| synonyms: scammer, con artist, extortionist, hustler| antonyms: philanthropist, benefactor | noun

hassle:/ˈhæsəl/| n. | 麻烦；困难；争论|A situation causing difficulty or trouble.| Getting a visa was a real hassle. 获得签证真是麻烦。| I had a hassle with the landlord about the broken heating. 我和房东就暖气坏了的问题发生了争论。|synonyms: trouble, difficulty, problem, inconvenience|antonyms: ease, convenience, simplicity| noun

hassle:/ˈhæsəl/| vt.|  使烦恼；找麻烦；纠缠| To annoy or bother someone persistently.|  Street vendors hassled tourists to buy their souvenirs. 街头小贩纠缠游客购买他们的纪念品。|  Don't hassle me with trivial details. 别拿琐碎的细节来烦我。| synonyms: bother, annoy, pester, harass|antonyms: assist, help, aid | verb
