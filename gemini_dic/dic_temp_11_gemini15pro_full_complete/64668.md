escalatory:/ˈɛskəˌleɪtəri/
adjective
逐步升级的；使局势恶化的
tending or likely to escalate, especially into violence
The escalatory rhetoric from both sides increased tensions. 双方逐步升级的言辞加剧了紧张局势。
近义词:inflammatory, provocative, escalating
反义词:de-escalatory, conciliatory, appeasing
词性:adjective

escalatory:/ˈɛskəˌleɪtəri/
adjective
升级的；加剧的
increasing in intensity, magnitude, or scope
The government took escalatory measures to combat the spread of the virus. 政府采取了升级措施来对抗病毒的传播。
近义词:intensifying, amplifying, heightening
反义词:diminishing, reducing, lessening
词性:adjective


词源: From escalate + -ory.  Escalate comes from the Latin "scala" meaning "ladder" or "staircase".
