janitored:/ˈdʒænɪtərd/
verb
打扫；做清洁工作；照管；
cleaned, supervised, or taken care of (a place).
The school was janitored by a team of dedicated staff.  学校由一组敬业的员工进行清洁维护。
He janitored the computer lab during the summer. 他在暑假期间负责管理计算机实验室。
近义词:cleaned, tidied, maintained, supervised
反义词:dirtied, messed, neglected
