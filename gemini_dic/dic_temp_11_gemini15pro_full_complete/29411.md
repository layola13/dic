catacombs:/ˈkætəˌkʊmz/ /ˈkætəˌkoʊmz/| n. |地下墓穴，茔窟|An underground labyrinth, especially one consisting of interconnecting subterranean passageways with recesses for tombs.|例句：The catacombs of Rome are a fascinating glimpse into the early history of Christianity. 罗马的地下墓穴为了解基督教早期历史提供了迷人的视角。

例句：We explored the dark and eerie catacombs beneath the city. 我们探索了城市下方阴森恐怖的地下墓穴。|近义词：crypts, vaults, tunnels, dungeons|反义词: surface, aboveground |词性:noun
