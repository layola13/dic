name:/neɪm/| n. |名字，名称；名声，名誉|A word or set of words by which a person, animal, place, or thing is known or referred to. |例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

|近义词：title, appellation, designation, label|反义词：anonymity|词性:n.

names:/neɪmz/| n. |名字，名称（复数）|Plural form of "name".|例句：Their names are written on the list. 他们的名字写在清单上。

|近义词：titles, appellations, designations, labels|反义词：N/A|词性:n.


词源:  From Middle English *name*, *nome*, from Old English *nama*, from Proto-Germanic *namō*, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁nómn̥*. Cognate with West Frisian *namme*, Dutch *naam*, German *Name*, Old Norse *nafn*, Gothic *𐌽𐌰𐌼𐍉* (*namo*).
