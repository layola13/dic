iridioscope:/ɪˌrɪdiəˈskoʊp/ | n. |虹膜镜；虹膜检查器|An instrument for examining the iris of the eye.|The ophthalmologist used an iridioscope to examine the patient's iris for signs of disease.|近义词: ophthalmoscope |反义词: None |词性: Noun


Etymology:
From  irido-  +‎  -scope.  Irido-  comes from Greek  īris, īridos,  meaning "iris." -scope comes from Greek  -skopion,  meaning "instrument for viewing."
