dissimile:/dɪˈsɪmɪl/ | adj. | 不同的，不相似的 | Unlike; dissimilar.  Not similar; different. | The two paintings, though by the same artist, are remarkably dissimiliar. 这两幅画，虽然是出自同一位艺术家之手，却非常不同。|  The twins are dissimiliar in temperament. 这对双胞胎性格迥异。| 近义词: different, unlike, distinct, diverse | 反义词: similar, alike, analogous | 词性: 形容词

dissimilitude:/ˌdɪsɪˈmɪlɪtjuːd/ | n. | 不同，差异 |  Lack of resemblance or similarity.  The state or quality of being dissimilar.  | The dissimilitude between the two cultures is striking.  两种文化之间的差异令人吃惊。| There is a marked dissimilitude between their writing styles. 他们的写作风格有明显的不同。 | 近义词: difference, unlikeness, disparity, divergence | 反义词: similarity, likeness, resemblance, similitude | 词性: 名词


词源: From Latin dissimilis, from dis- "not" + similis "like."
