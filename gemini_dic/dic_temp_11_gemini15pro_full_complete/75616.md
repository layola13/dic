fugato:/fuˈɡɑːtoʊ/ /fjuːˈɡɑːtəʊ/
noun
赋格风；赋格段
A short passage in a fugue style, or a movement based on such a style.
The composer incorporated a brief fugato in the development section of the sonata. 作曲家在奏鸣曲的展开部加入了一段简短的赋格风乐段。
近义词: fugue, fugal passage
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a musical term like fugato)
词性: n.


fugato:/fuˈɡɑːtoʊ/ /fjuːˈɡɑːtəʊ/
adjective
赋格风的
Resembling or characteristic of a fugue.
The fugato section of the piece was particularly complex.  这首曲子的赋格风部分特别复杂。
近义词: fugal
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a musical term like fugato)
词性: adj.
