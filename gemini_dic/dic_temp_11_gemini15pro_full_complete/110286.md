ligation:/laɪˈɡeɪʃən/ |n.|连接，捆绑，结扎|In molecular biology, ligation refers to the joining of two DNA fragments. 在分子生物学中，连接是指两个DNA片段的连接。|例句：DNA ligation is a common technique in genetic engineering. DNA连接是基因工程中常用的技术。|近义词：binding, joining, connection, fastening|反义词：separation, disconnection|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ligātiōn*, from ligātus (past participle of ligāre, "to bind"), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *leig- (“to bind”).
