geni:/ˈdʒiːnɪ/, /ˈdʒiːnaɪ/ | n. | 精灵; 天才; 守护神 | A supernatural creature, often depicted as a spirit dwelling in a lamp or bottle, that can grant wishes; a person of exceptional intelligence or creative ability; a tutelary spirit or deity. | Example 1: He rubbed the lamp and a genie appeared in a puff of smoke. 他擦了擦神灯，一个精灵在一团烟雾中出现了。

Example 2: She has a real genie for finding bargains. 她很擅长发现便宜货。 | Synonym: spirit, djinn, genius, prodigy | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a supernatural being; for the "genius" meaning: dullard, simpleton) | noun
