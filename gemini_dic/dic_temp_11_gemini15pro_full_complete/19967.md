bioethicist: /ˌbaɪoʊˈɛθɪsɪst/ | n. | 生物伦理学家 | A specialist in bioethics, a field of study concerned with the ethical implications of biological research and medical practices. |  A bioethicist might advise a hospital on the ethical considerations of genetic testing.  一位生物伦理学家可能会就基因检测的伦理问题向医院提供建议。 | Synonyms: medical ethicist, healthcare ethicist | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who disregards ethics in biological research) | noun
