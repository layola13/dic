choking:/ˈtʃoʊkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈtʃəʊkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. n.|窒息的，令人窒息的；窒息，噎住|The act of being unable to breathe because something is blocking the windpipe. Can also describe something that causes this sensation or is so overwhelming it feels like choking.  It can also be used figuratively to describe a feeling of being overwhelmed or suppressed.|

例句：
The smoke was so thick it was choking.
烟雾太浓了，令人窒息。

例句：
He saved the child from choking on a grape.
他救了那个被葡萄噎住的孩子。


近义词：suffocating, asphyxiating, gagging, strangling|
反义词:breathing, inhaling|
词性:adjective, noun
