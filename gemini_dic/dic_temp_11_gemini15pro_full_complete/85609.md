haughtier:/ˈhɔːtiər/ | adj. | 更傲慢的，更自大的 |  The comparative form of "haughty," meaning arrogantly superior and disdainful.  It describes someone who displays an exaggerated sense of self-importance and looks down on others. | She became haughtier after receiving the promotion.  她升职后变得更加傲慢了。|  He gave her a haughtier look than usual. 他给了她一个比平常更傲慢的眼神。 |  arrogant, supercilious, disdainful, pompous | humble, modest, unassuming | adjective
