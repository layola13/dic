externality:/ˌɛks.tɜːˈnæl.ɪ.ti/ | n. | 外部性，外部效应 | A side effect or consequence of an industrial or commercial activity that affects other parties without this being reflected in the cost of the goods or services involved. |  Pollution from a factory is a negative externality that affects the health of nearby residents. 工厂的污染是对附近居民健康造成影响的负外部性。

| The beautiful garden in front of your house creates a positive externality for your neighbors. 你家门前的美丽花园为你邻居创造了正外部性。
| 近义词：spillover effect, side effect, consequence | 反义词：internality | 词性：noun
