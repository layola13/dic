例句：It's flipping cold today! 今天真TM冷！|近义词：bloody, damn, freaking|反义词: wonderful, excellent|词性: adjective
Etymology->
From the verb "flip," suggesting a sudden movement or change, often used for emphasis.  Originally a euphemism for "fucking."  From Proto-Germanic *flikkōną (“to move quickly, flutter”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)pleg- (“to strike, hit, move quickly”).
