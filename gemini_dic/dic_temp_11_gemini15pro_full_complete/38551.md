conclusional:/kənˈkluːʒənəl/
adjective
结论性的，总结性的
Relating to or expressing a conclusion.
His conclusional remarks summarized the main points of the presentation. 他的总结性发言概括了演讲的要点。
The report offered a conclusional statement about the effectiveness of the new policy.  这份报告对新政策的有效性提出了一个结论性的陈述。
近义词:conclusive, final, summary
反义词:introductory, preliminary, initial
词性:adjective
