nonebullient:/ˌnɒnɪˈbʌliənt/ | adj. | 不沸腾的；不热情的；不兴奋的 | Not ebullient; lacking enthusiasm or exuberance.  | The crowd was strangely nonebullient, even after their team scored the winning goal.  观众即使在他们球队攻入制胜球后也异常地不热情。 | 近义词: apathetic, subdued, listless, impassive, phlegmatic | 反义词: ebullient, enthusiastic, exuberant, lively, animated | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "nonebullient" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adjective "ebullient."  "Ebullient" itself comes from the Latin "ebullire," meaning "to boil over," from "ex-" ("out") + "bullire" ("to bubble").
