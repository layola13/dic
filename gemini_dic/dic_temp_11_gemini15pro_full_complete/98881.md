intending:/ɪnˈtɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 打算的，有意的 |  Meaning "having the intention to do something". Describes someone who plans or aims to do something. |  She was intending to call you later. 她本来打算晚点给你打电话。| synonyms: planning, aiming, proposing | antonyms:  unintentional, accidental | adjective

intending:/ɪnˈtɛndɪŋ/ | v. | 打算 | The present participle form of the verb "intend". Expresses the act of having a plan or purpose. |  I'm intending to finish this project by Friday. 我打算在星期五之前完成这个项目。 | synonyms: planning, aiming, proposing | antonyms:  avoiding, neglecting | verb
