calculative:/ˈkælkjʊlətɪv/| adj.|精于算计的，斤斤计较的|Tending to make careful calculations, especially of profit or advantage; shrewd or cunning.|She made a calculative move in the negotiation, ensuring she got the best deal. 她在这场谈判中采取了精明的策略，确保自己获得了最佳的交易。|近义词：shrewd, cunning, calculating, scheming, wily|反义词：impulsive, reckless, ingenuous, artless|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *calculativus*, from Latin *calculatus* (past participle of *calculare* "to calculate") + *-ivus*. Ultimately from Latin *calculus* "pebble" (used in counting).
