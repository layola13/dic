bateful:/ˈbeɪtfʊl/| adj.|充满仇恨的；恶意的；险恶的|Filled with hatred; malicious; venomous.|He gave me a bateful look. 他恶狠狠地瞪了我一眼。|Synonyms: hateful, malicious, spiteful, venomous, rancorous|Antonyms: loving, kind, benevolent, charitable|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From bate (strife, contention) + -ful.  Ultimately from Old French debat (debate, strife).
