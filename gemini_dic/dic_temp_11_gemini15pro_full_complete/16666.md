barranca:/bəˈræŋkə/ (US), /bəˈræŋkɑː/ (UK)| n. |峡谷，深谷；(尤指美国西南部)干涸的河床|A steep-sided ravine or gorge, especially one in the southwestern US, that is usually dry except during periods of heavy rain.|We hiked down into the barranca to escape the desert heat. 我们徒步走到峡谷里以躲避沙漠的酷热。|The road wound its way down the barranca. 道路蜿蜒向下通往峡谷。|近义词：canyon, ravine, gorge, gully|反义词:mesa, plateau|词性:名词

词源: Spanish, from Old Spanish *barranca 'cliff, ravine', of unknown origin.
