encourage:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒ/ /ɛnˈkɝɪdʒ/| vt.|鼓励，鼓舞，促进|To give support, confidence, or hope to (someone).|My parents always encouraged me to pursue my dreams. 我的父母总是鼓励我去追求我的梦想。|The teacher encouraged the students to ask questions. 老师鼓励学生提问。|近义词：inspire, motivate, support, promote|反义词:discourage, hinder, deter|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old French *encoragier, from en- "in, into" + corage "courage, heart."
