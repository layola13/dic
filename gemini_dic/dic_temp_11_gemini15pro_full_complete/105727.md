kiondo:/kiˈɔndoʊ/
kiondo|n.|肯尼亚手工编织包；基翁多包|A handwoven bag made of sisal fibers, originating from the Kamba people of Kenya. It's often brightly colored and decorated with leather.|She carried her belongings in a beautiful kiondo. 她用一个漂亮的基翁多包带着她的 belongings。
kiondo|近义词:handbag, tote, basket|反义词:|n.
