out-humored:/ˌaʊtˈhjuːmərd/
| adj. | 被幽默打败的；被逗乐的 | Defeated by humor; made to feel cheerful or amused, especially in a way that overcomes seriousness or negativity.  |  He was completely out-humored by his witty opponent and conceded the argument with a laugh. 他被机智的对手的幽默彻底击败了，笑着承认了对方的观点。 |  Synonyms: outwitted, charmed, disarmed, amused | Antonyms: serious, unamused, gloomy | Word Class: adjective

out-humored:/ˌaʊtˈhjuːmərd/
| v. |  用幽默战胜 | To defeat or win over someone using humor. |  She out-humored him into agreeing to her proposal. 她用幽默说服了他同意她的提议。 | Synonyms: outwit, charm, disarm | Antonyms: offend, insult, antagonize | Word Class: verb
