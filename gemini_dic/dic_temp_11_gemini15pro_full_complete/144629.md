outwit:/ˌaʊtˈwɪt/ | vt. | 智胜，瞒骗 | to defeat or trick (someone) by being more intelligent or clever | She outwitted her pursuers by disguising herself as a man. 她把自己伪装成男人，智胜了追捕她的人。| The fox outwitted the hounds. 狐狸以智取胜，摆脱了猎犬。 | synonyms: outsmart, outfox, outmaneuver | antonyms: be outwitted, be outsmarted | verb
