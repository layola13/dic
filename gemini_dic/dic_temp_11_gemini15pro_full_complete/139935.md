odograph:/ˈɒdəɡrɑːf/ | n. | 里程表，路程记录器 | An instrument for automatically recording the distance travelled by a vehicle. | The odograph attached to the carriage wheel measured the distance precisely.  马车轮子上的里程表精确地测量了距离。| Example: The explorers used an odograph to map their journey across the desert. 探险家们使用里程表绘制他们穿越沙漠的旅程。| Synonyms: pedometer, hodometer | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
