endoblastic:/ˌɛndoʊˈblæstɪk/| adj.|内胚层的，由内胚层形成的|Relating to or derived from the endoblast, the inner germ layer of an embryo that develops into the lining of the digestive tract and associated structures.|The endoblastic layer gives rise to the gut. 内胚层会形成肠道。|The endoblastic cells differentiate into the lining of the digestive system. 内胚层细胞分化成消化系统的内层。|近义词：entoblastic, hypoblastic|反义词:ectoblastic, mesoblastic|词性:adjective


Etymology:
From endo- (within) + -blast (germ or bud)
