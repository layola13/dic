heathen:/ˈhiːðən/| n. adj.|异教徒，非基督教徒；粗鲁的人，不文明的人|A person who does not belong to a widely held religion (especially one who is not a Christian, Jew, or Muslim) as regarded by those who do.  Can also be used as an adjective to describe something related to heathens or their practices, or informally to describe someone who is uncivilized or irreligious.|例句：The missionaries attempted to convert the heathens. 传教士试图改变那些异教徒的信仰。
