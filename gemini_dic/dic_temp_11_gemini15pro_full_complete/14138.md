authoritarianism:/əˌθɔːrɪˈtɛːriənɪzəm/
|n.|专制主义，独裁主义|A form of government characterized by strong central power and limited political freedoms.|Individual rights are often suppressed under authoritarianism. 在专制主义统治下，个人权利经常受到压制。|The country descended into authoritarianism after the coup.  政变后，这个国家陷入了专制主义。|近义词：despotism, dictatorship, tyranny|反义词：democracy, liberalism|词性: noun

authoritarian:/əˌθɔːrɪˈtɛːriən/
|adj.|专制的，独裁的|Favoring or enforcing strict obedience to authority, especially that of the government, at the expense of personal freedom.|The authoritarian regime imposed strict censorship.  专制政权实施了严格的审查制度。|His authoritarian parenting style stifled his children's creativity. 他专制的教养方式扼杀了孩子们的创造力。|近义词：despotic, dictatorial, tyrannical|反义词：democratic, liberal|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *autoritarisme*, from *autoritaire* (authoritarian), from Latin *auctoritas* (authority).
