cubby:/ˈkʌbi/| n. |小房间，隔间，储物柜|A small enclosed space or compartment, often used for storage.|例句：Each child has their own cubby for their coats and bags. 每个孩子都有自己的储物柜来放外套和书包。

例句：He keeps his tools in a cubby in the garage. 他把工具放在车库的一个小隔间里。|近义词：compartment, locker, nook, cubicle|反义词:hall, open space|词性:noun
