antenumber:/ˈæntiˌnʌmbər/
noun
前缀码；前置号码
A number prefixed to another number, especially a telephone number, to connect to a particular service or area.
Example: Please dial the antenumber 9 before making international calls.  请在拨打国际电话前先拨前缀号码 9。
Example:  The antenumber for this conference call is 555-1212.  这次电话会议的前缀码是 555-1212。
Synonym: prefix, access code, area code
Antonym: suffix
词性: 名词
