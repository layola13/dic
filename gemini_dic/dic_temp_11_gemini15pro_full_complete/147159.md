overlove:/ˌoʊvərˈlʌv/| v. |过分地爱，溺爱|To love excessively or to an unhealthy degree.  It implies a love that is smothering, possessive, or potentially harmful to the recipient or the one giving the love.| She overloved her son, never letting him experience any hardship. 她过分地爱她的儿子，从不让他经历任何困难。|  He overloved his pet bird, keeping it caged and constantly fussing over it. 他过分地爱他的宠物鸟，把它关在笼子里，不停地瞎忙活。|近义词：smother, coddle, spoil, dote on|反义词: neglect, ignore|词性:动词

词源: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "love" (verb meaning "to have a deep affection for").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "love," suggesting an excess that goes beyond healthy affection.
