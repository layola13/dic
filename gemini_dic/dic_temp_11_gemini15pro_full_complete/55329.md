dissuade:/dɪˈsweɪd/ /dɪˈswed/| vt. |劝阻，阻止|To discourage someone from doing something, usually by persuasion. |例句：I tried to dissuade him from quitting his job. 我试图劝他不要辞职。

例句：She dissuaded her friend from making a hasty decision. 她劝她的朋友不要仓促做决定。

近义词：deter, discourage, prevent|反义词:persuade, encourage, urge|词性:动词
