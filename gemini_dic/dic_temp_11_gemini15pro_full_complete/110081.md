lichenographist: /ˌlaɪkəˈnɒɡrəfɪst/ /ˌlaɪkənˈɑːɡrəfɪst/
n.
地衣学家
A person who studies lichens.
例句：The lichenographist discovered a new species of lichen in the rainforest.  这位地衣学家在雨林中发现了一种新的地衣。

例句：The lichenographist meticulously documented the distribution of lichens in the area.  这位地衣学家一丝不苟地记录了该地区地衣的分布情况。

近义词:  botanist (植物学家), biologist (生物学家)
反义词:  N/A (没有直接的反义词)
词性:  noun (名词)


ETYMOLOGY->
lichen + -o- + -graph + -ist

* **lichen:** From Greek *leikhēn* (lichen, a kind of tetter).
* **-o-:** Connecting vowel.
* **-graph:** From Greek *-graphos* (writing, description).
* **-ist:** A suffix indicating a person who practices or is skilled in something.
