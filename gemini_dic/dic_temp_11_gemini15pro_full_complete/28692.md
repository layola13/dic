carlin:/ˈkɑːrlɪn/ | n. | 哈巴狗，狮子狗（一种短腿、短脸、皱鼻子的狗） | A small dog of a breed having a short muzzle, a pug nose, and a tightly curled tail.  It's known for its wrinkled face and distinctive pushed-in nose. |  例句：The carlin snored softly at the foot of the bed. 哈巴狗在床脚轻轻地打鼾。

例句：Carlins are known for their affectionate nature. 哈巴狗以其深情的本性而闻名。 | 近义词：pug, Dutch pug, Mop | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
