decelerating:/diːˈsɛləˌreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
减速的
reducing speed; slowing down.
The car is decelerating as it approaches the red light. 汽车在接近红灯时正在减速。
The decelerating economy is causing concern among investors. 经济减速正引起投资者担忧。
synonyms: slowing, reducing speed, braking, slowing down
antonyms: accelerating, speeding up
词性: 形容词
