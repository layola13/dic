hypophosphataemia: /ˌhaɪpəʊfɒsfəˈtiːmiə/ (UK) /ˌhaɪpoʊfɑːsfəˈtiːmiə/ (US)
n.
低磷血症
A condition in which there is an abnormally low level of phosphate in the blood.
Hypophosphataemia can cause muscle weakness, confusion, and seizures. 低磷血症会导致肌肉无力、精神错乱和癫痫发作。
Hypophosphatemia can lead to serious complications if left untreated. 如果不放任不管，低磷血症会导致严重的并发症。
近义词: low phosphate, phosphate deficiency
反义词: hyperphosphatemia
词性: noun
