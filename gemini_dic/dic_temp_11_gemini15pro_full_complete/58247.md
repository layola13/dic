drumble:/ˈdrʌmbəl/| v. |低沉地说话；咕哝；嗡嗡作响|To speak in a low, indistinct, and often monotonous tone; to make a low, rumbling sound.|The old man drumbled a complaint about the weather. 老人低声抱怨着天气。|近义词：mutter, mumble, murmur, drone|反义词：shout, yell|词性:动词
drumble:/ˈdrʌmbəl/| n. |低沉的声音；嗡嗡声|A low, rumbling sound.|The drumble of the distant traffic lulled me to sleep. 远处车辆的嗡嗡声让我昏昏欲睡。|近义词：hum, drone, murmur|反义词：shout, yell|词性:名词
