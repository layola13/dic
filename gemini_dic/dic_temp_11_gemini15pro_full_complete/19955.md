biodynamics:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/
n.|生物动力学; 生物力学|A system of farming that avoids the use of artificial fertilizers, pesticides, etc., and uses special preparations made from fermented manure and herbs to improve the soil and stimulate plant growth. It is based on the work of Rudolf Steiner and emphasizes the spiritual and cosmic influences on agriculture.|例句：The vineyard uses biodynamic farming practices. 这片葡萄园采用生物动力农业实践。|例句：Biodynamics is becoming increasingly popular among organic farmers. 生物动力法在有机农民中越来越受欢迎。|近义词：organic farming, permaculture|反义词：conventional farming|noun
