calomel:/ˈkæləˌmɛl/| n. |甘汞;氯化亚汞|A white, tasteless powder used as a purgative, fungicide, and insecticide.  It is a compound of mercury and chlorine (Hg₂Cl₂).|Calomel was once a common treatment for syphilis. 甘汞曾经是治疗梅毒的常用药物。

Calomel is still used in some electrode applications. 甘汞仍然用于某些电极应用中。|近义词:mercurous chloride|反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|noun
