beslobber: /bɪˈslɒbə(r)/ /bɪˈslɑːbər/
verb
弄脏,  用口水涂抹; 对…过分亲昵
to cover with saliva; to lavish with maudlin affection.
The baby beslobbered her mother's shoulder. 婴儿把妈妈的肩膀弄得都是口水。
The over-enthusiastic fan beslobbered the movie star with kisses.  过于热情的粉丝狂吻电影明星，弄了他一脸口水。
近义词: slobber, drool, smear
反义词: clean, wipe
词性: verb
