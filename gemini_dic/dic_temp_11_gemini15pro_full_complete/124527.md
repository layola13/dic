mountaineer:/ˌmaʊntɪˈnɪə(r)/| n. |登山者；山地居民|A person who climbs mountains, especially as a sport.|Experienced mountaineers know the importance of proper equipment.  经验丰富的登山者知道合适的装备的重要性。|Synonyms: climber, alpinist|Antonyms: lowlander, plainsman|noun

mountaineer:/ˌmaʊntɪˈnɪə(r)/| v. |登山，攀登|To climb mountains, especially as a sport.|They plan to mountaineer in the Himalayas next summer. 他们计划明年夏天去喜马拉雅山登山。|Synonyms: climb, ascend|Antonyms: descend|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From mountain + -eer (agent suffix). Ultimately from Latin *montanea (mountainous region), from mons (mountain).
