ventriloquist:/vɛnˈtrɪləkwɪst/| n. |腹语术表演者|A person who can speak or utter sounds so that they seem to come from somewhere else, especially a dummy. |The ventriloquist made the dummy appear to be talking. 腹语表演者使木偶看起来像在说话。|Synonyms: dummy speaker, puppet master|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "audience member")|noun
