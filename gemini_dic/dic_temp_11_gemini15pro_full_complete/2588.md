admonitive:/ədˈmɒnɪtɪv/
| adj.|劝诫的，警告的|Giving or conveying a warning or reprimand; serving to admonish.|The teacher's admonitive tone made the students pay attention.|近义词：cautionary, warning, reproving, admonishing|反义词：praising, encouraging|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin admonitivus, from admonit- ‘reminded,’ from the verb admonere.
