numerically:/nʊˈmɛrɪkli/| adv.|数字地，用数字表示地|Expressed or counted in numbers.  Derived from "number" with the suffix "-ically," meaning "in a... manner."  The root "number" comes from Old French "nombre," ultimately from Latin "numerus."|例句：The results were presented numerically. 结果以数字形式呈现。|例句：The data is arranged numerically in ascending order. 数据按数字升序排列。|近义词：quantitatively|反义词:qualitatively|词性:副词
