annihilating: /əˈnaɪəleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |毁灭性的，歼灭性的 | utterly destroying or defeating someone or something. |  The enemy suffered an annihilating defeat. 敌人遭到了毁灭性的失败。|  devastating, crushing, obliterating | constructive, creating | adjective

annihilate: /əˈnaɪəleɪt/ | vt. | 歼灭，毁灭；彻底击败 | to destroy utterly; obliterate. | The bomb annihilated the city. 炸弹摧毁了这座城市。| destroy, obliterate, eradicate, exterminate | create, build | verb

词源:  From Late Latin *annihilare, from Latin ad "to" + nihil "nothing".  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ne- "not".
