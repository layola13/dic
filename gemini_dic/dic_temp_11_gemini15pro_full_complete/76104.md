further-educations: /ˈfɜːrðər ɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 进修，继续教育 |  Refers to education beyond the secondary level, often vocational or technical, but can also include higher education like university.  It's generally for people who have left school and are not in compulsory education. |  Many adults choose further-educations to improve their job prospects. 许多成年人选择进修以提高他们的工作前景。|  She enrolled in a further-educations course to learn web design. 她报名参加了一个进修课程来学习网页设计。 | synonyms: continuing education, post-secondary education, higher education, vocational training | antonyms: primary education, secondary education, elementary education | noun
