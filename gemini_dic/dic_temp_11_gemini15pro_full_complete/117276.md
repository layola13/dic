mechanizations:/ˌmɛkənɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|机械化; 机械化的事物|The process of introducing machines or automatic devices into a system or process, or the state of being mechanized.  The widespread mechanizations of farming have greatly increased yields.|The mechanizations of agriculture have led to increased productivity. 农业机械化带来了生产力的提高。
|Synonyms: automation, industrialization, modernization|
|Antonyms: manual labor, hand labor|
|noun|
