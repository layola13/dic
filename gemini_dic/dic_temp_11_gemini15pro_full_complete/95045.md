inactive: /ɪnˈæktɪv/ | adj. |不活跃的，不活动的，闲置的 | Not active or in use; not working, operating, or progressing. | The volcano has been inactive for centuries. 这座火山已经几个世纪没有活动了。|  He led a relatively inactive life after retirement. 退休后，他过着相对不活跃的生活。 | 近义词:idle, dormant, inert, passive | 反义词: active, energetic, dynamic | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + active.  The word "active" comes from the Latin "activus," meaning "busy, practical."
