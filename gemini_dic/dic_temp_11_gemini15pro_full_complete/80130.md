gobsmacked:/ˌɡɒbˈsmækt/ /ˌɡɑːbˈsmækt/| adj.|目瞪口呆的，惊呆的|utterly astonished; so surprised as to be unable to speak.|例句：I was completely gobsmacked when I heard the news. 我听到这个消息完全惊呆了。

例句：She was gobsmacked by his sudden proposal. 他的突然求婚让她目瞪口呆。|近义词：astonished, amazed, stunned, speechless, dumbfounded|反义词：unfazed, unimpressed|词性：形容词
