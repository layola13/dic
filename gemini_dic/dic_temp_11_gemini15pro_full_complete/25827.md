burglar:/ˈbɜːrɡlər/ | n. |窃贼，破门盗窃者| A person who commits burglary, illegally entering a building with intent to commit a crime, especially theft. |  The burglar was caught trying to escape through the window.  窃贼试图从窗户逃跑时被抓住了。

The burglar tripped the alarm system.  窃贼触发了警报系统。

|近义词：thief, robber, housebreaker, intruder |反义词: police officer, security guard |词性: noun
