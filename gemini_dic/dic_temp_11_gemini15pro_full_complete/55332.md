dissuading:/dɪˈsweɪdɪŋ/
verb
劝退，劝阻
Trying to stop someone from doing something, by giving reasons why they should not do it.
His friends tried dissuading him from quitting his job. 他的朋友试图劝他不要辞职。

dissuading:/dɪˈsweɪdɪŋ/
present participle
劝退的，劝阻的
Acting to deter.
She gave him a dissuading look. 她给了他一个劝阻的眼神。


近义词:deterring, discouraging, advising against
反义词:encouraging, persuading, urging
词性:Verb (present participle)
