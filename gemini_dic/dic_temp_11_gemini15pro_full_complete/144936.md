overabounds:/ˌoʊvərəˈbaʊndz/
verb
过多；过剩；充满
exists or is available in large quantities; is too abundant.
例句：Fish overabound in the lake. 湖里鱼很多。

例句：Kindness overabounds in her nature.  她天性善良。

近义词：overflow, teem, abound, proliferate, superabound
反义词：lack, want, need, be deficient in
词性：v.
