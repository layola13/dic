empeach:/ɪmˈpiːtʃ/ | v. | 弹劾，控告（公职人员） | To charge (a public official) with misconduct in office before a competent tribunal. | The House of Representatives voted to impeach the president. 众议院投票弹劾总统。

The president was impeached for obstruction of justice. 总统因妨碍司法公正而被弹劾。 | 近义词: accuse, indict, charge | 反义词: acquit, exonerate | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *empeechier, from em- 'in' + pechier 'to fasten, fix'.
