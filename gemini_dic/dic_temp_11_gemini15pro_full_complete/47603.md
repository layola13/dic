debauchedly:/dɪˈbɔːtʃɪdli/
adverb
放荡地；堕落地
in a debauched manner; excessively indulging in pleasure or sensual delights.
He lived debauchedly, squandering his fortune on wine, women, and song. 他过着放荡的生活，把自己的财产挥霍在酒、女人和歌曲上。
Synonyms: dissolutely, licentiously, wantonly, corruptly, immorally
Antonyms: virtuously, chastely, morally, righteously, purely
词性: 副词
