antiempiricism:/ˌæntiɪmˈpɪrɪsɪzəm/| n.|反经验主义|The rejection of empiricism, the view that experience is the primary source of knowledge.|His antiempiricism led him to believe in innate ideas. 他对反经验主义的信仰让他相信天赋观念。|近义词：rationalism, apriorism|反义词:empiricism|noun
