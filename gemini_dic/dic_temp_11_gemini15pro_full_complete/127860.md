neocarida:/ˌniːoʊˈkærɪdə/ | n. | 新甲类 | A subclass of crustaceans that includes shrimps, lobsters, crabs, and their relatives.  It is characterized by having a carapace covering the cephalothorax. |  Neocarida are a diverse group found in both marine and freshwater habitats. 新甲类是甲壳类动物的一个亚类，包括虾、龙虾、蟹及其近亲。其特征是有一层甲壳覆盖着头胸部。新甲类是一个多样化的群体，在海洋和淡水栖息地都有发现。 | synonyms: Malacostraca (partially overlapping), Eucarida (a superorder within Neocarida) | antonyms:  N/A (as a taxonomic group, it doesn't have direct antonyms) | noun
