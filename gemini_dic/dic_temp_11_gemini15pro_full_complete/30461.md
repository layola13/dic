centrifugal force: /ˌsɛnˈtrɪfjʊɡəl ˈfɔːrs/ /ˌsɛnˈtrɪfəɡl ˈfɔːrs/ | n. | 离心力 | An apparent outward force on a rotating or revolving body. It is fictitious in the sense that it is not part of an interaction but is an effect of inertia. | The centrifugal force pushes the passengers outwards when the car turns a corner.  当汽车转弯时，离心力将乘客推向外侧。 | Synonyms: outward force, inertial force | Antonyms: centripetal force | noun

centrifugal forces: /ˌsɛnˈtrɪfjʊɡəl ˈfɔːrsɪz/ /ˌsɛnˈtrɪfəɡl ˈfɔːrsɪz/ | n. | 离心力 | Plural form of centrifugal force.  | The centrifugal forces acting on the rotating blades are immense. 作用在旋转叶片上的离心力是巨大的。 | Synonyms: outward forces, inertial forces | Antonyms: centripetal forces | noun
