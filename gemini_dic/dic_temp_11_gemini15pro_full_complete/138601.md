nubuck:/ˈnjuːbʌk/ | n. | 磨砂皮；绒面革 | A top-grain cattle leather that has been sanded or buffed on the grain side, or outside, to give a slight nap of short protein fibers, producing a velvet-like surface. |  例句：My new nubuck boots are so comfortable. 我的新磨砂皮靴子很舒服。

例句：Nubuck is more delicate than regular leather. 磨砂皮比普通皮革更娇嫩。| 近义词：suede, buckskin | 反义词: leather (in a broader sense, as nubuck is a type of leather) | 词性: noun
