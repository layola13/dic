decaled:/diːˈkeɪld/ | adj. | 贴花的；转移印花的；decaled pottery 转印贴花陶器 | Decorated with or having applied decals.  A decal is a design prepared on special paper for transfer to another surface such as glass, porcelain, or metal. |  The decated mugs featured a vintage floral design.  这些贴花马克杯以复古的花卉图案为特色。 |  decorated, adorned, embellished, ornamented | plain, undecorated | adjective
