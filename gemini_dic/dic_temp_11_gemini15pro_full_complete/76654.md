galumph:/ɡəˈlʌmpf/| vi.| 欢快地笨拙行走|To move in a clumsy, heavy-footed way, often with a sense of boisterous enjoyment. |He galumphed across the field, a wide grin on his face. 他咧着嘴笑着，笨拙地穿过田野。

The children galumphed down the stairs, eager to open their presents. 孩子们笨拙地跑下楼梯，急切地想打开他们的礼物。|近义词：lumber, stomp, clump, plod|反义词： tiptoe, glide, slink|词性：动词
