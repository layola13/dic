emplore:/ɪmˈplɔːr/ | v. |恳求，哀求 |To beg someone earnestly or desperately to do something.|She implored him to help her. 她恳求他帮助她。| I implore you to reconsider your decision. 我恳求你重新考虑你的决定。|  beseech, entreat, beg, plead |  demand, command | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *implouren*, from Old French *implorer*, from Latin *implōrāre* (“to invoke with tears, beseech”), from *in-* (“in, on, upon, at, towards”) + *plōrāre* (“to weep, cry out, wail, lament”).
