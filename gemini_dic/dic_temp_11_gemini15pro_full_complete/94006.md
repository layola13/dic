impaling:/ɪmˈpeɪlɪŋ/
|n.|刺穿，穿刺|The act of piercing something with a sharp object.|The impaling of the insect on the thorn was a gruesome sight.  昆虫被刺穿在荆棘上，景象十分可怕。|近义词：piercing, spearing, transfixing|反义词:extricating, removing|noun
impale:/ɪmˈpeɪl/
|vt.|刺穿，穿透|To pierce with a sharp stake or point.|The knight impaled the dragon with his lance. 骑士用他的长矛刺穿了龙。|近义词：pierce, spear, transfix|反义词:extricate, remove|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *empailer, from en- "in" + pailer "to pale, enclose with pales," from Latin pālus "stake."
