demisability: /ˌdɛmiˈsæbɪləti/
n.
部分残疾; 轻度残疾
A condition of having a mild or partial disability, implying a reduced but not complete loss of ability.
People with demisabilities can often live independently with appropriate support. 部分残疾人士通常可以在适当的支持下独立生活。
Demisability acknowledges that disability exists on a spectrum. 部分残疾的概念承认残疾的存在是一个谱系。

近义词: mild disability, partial disability
反义词: able-bodiedness, full functionality
