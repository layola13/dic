Judeans:/ˈdʒuːdiənz/
n.
犹太人；犹地亚人
People of Judea, an ancient region in southern Palestine.
The Judeans rebuilt the temple after returning from exile.  犹太人在从流放地返回后重建了圣殿。
The Romans conquered the Judeans in the first century. 罗马人在公元一世纪征服了犹太人。
近义词:Jews, Israelites
反义词:Gentiles
词性:noun
