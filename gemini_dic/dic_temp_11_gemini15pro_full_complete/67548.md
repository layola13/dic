extraneity:/ˌɛkstrəˈneɪɪti/
n.
外来性; 无关紧要;
The state or quality of being extraneous.  Something that is extraneous.
The extraneity of the evidence made it inadmissible in court.  证词的无关紧要性使其在法庭上不被采纳。
The book’s frequent digressions into extraneity made it difficult to follow. 这本书经常偏离主题，让人难以理解。
synonyms: irrelevance,  immateriality,  unimportance,  inappositeness,  inappropriateness
antonyms: relevance,  pertinence,  importance,  essentialness
noun
