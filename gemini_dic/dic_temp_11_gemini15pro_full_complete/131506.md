noncolonials:/ˌnɒn.kəˈləʊ.ni.əlz/
noun
非殖民地居民; 未受殖民统治的人们
People who are not from or do not live in a colony.
The conference brought together representatives from various noncolonial nations.  这次会议汇集了来自各个非殖民地国家的代表。
Many noncolonials were present at the international summit. 许多非殖民地居民出席了国际峰会。
近义词:independents, sovereign peoples
反义词:colonials, settlers
