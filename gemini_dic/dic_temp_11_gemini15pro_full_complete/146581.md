overgratitudes: /ˌoʊvərˈɡrætɪtjuːdz/
noun
过度的感激; 过分的感谢
Excessive expressions of gratitude.  This word is not commonly used and might sound awkward or insincere in many contexts.
Example: Her overgratitudes made him uncomfortable. 她的过分感谢让他感到不舒服。
Example: He showered them with overgratitudes. 他对他们表达了过分的感激。
Near Synonyms:  effusive thanks, excessive gratitude, profuse thanks
Antonyms: ingratitude, ungratefulness
词性: 名词
