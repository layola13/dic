miniaturize:/ˈmɪnɪətʃəraɪz/ | vt. | 使小型化，使微型化 | To make something much smaller than its usual size. |  Scientists are working to miniaturize electronic devices. 科学家们正在努力使电子设备小型化。

They've miniaturized the camera to fit inside a pen. 他们把相机缩小到可以装进钢笔里。 | synonyms: shrink, reduce, compress, downsize | antonyms: enlarge, expand, magnify | verb
