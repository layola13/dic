episode: /ˈɛpɪˌsoʊd/ (US) /ˈɛpɪˌsəʊd/ (UK)| n.|一段时期; 一集; 插曲; 事件|An episode is a single part of a continuing story, such as a television series or a person's life.  An episode can also refer to a particular event or period of time in a person's life or in history, often one that is memorable or significant.|

例句：
The first episode of the new series aired last night.
新剧的第一集昨晚播出了。

This episode of my life was particularly challenging.
我人生的这段时期特别具有挑战性。


近义词：incident, event, occurrence, chapter, installment
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
