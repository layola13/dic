opaque:/oʊˈpeɪk/| adj.|不透明的；难理解的|Not able to be seen through; not transparent. Also, hard to understand or explain.|The windows were opaque with steam. 窗户上满是蒸汽，变得不透明了。|The contract was written in opaque legal jargon. 这份合同是用晦涩难懂的法律术语写的。|近义词:cloudy, murky, obscure, impenetrable|反义词:transparent, clear, translucent|词性:形容词

opaquest:/oʊˈpeɪkɪst/| adj.|最不透明的；最难理解的|(superlative of opaque) Most opaque; most difficult to understand.|Of all the legal documents, this one was the opaquest. 在所有法律文件中，这一份是最难理解的。|His explanation of the theory was the opaquest I've ever heard. 他对这个理论的解释是我听过的最晦涩难懂的。|近义词:most cloudy, murkiest, most obscure, most impenetrable|反义词:most transparent, clearest, most translucent|词性:形容词
