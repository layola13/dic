grinder:/ˈɡraɪndər/ (US), /ˈɡraɪndə/ (UK)| n.|研磨机，磨床；研磨者；苦工；臼齿|A machine or tool used for grinding something.  A person who grinds something. A person who works hard, especially at tedious tasks. A molar tooth. |例句：The coffee grinder makes a loud noise in the morning. 早上咖啡研磨机发出很大的噪音。

He's a real grinder, always working late into the night. 他真是个苦干家，总是工作到深夜。|近义词：mill, crusher, pulverizer, toiler, worker|反义词:loafer, idler|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "grind" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Grind" comes from Old English "grindan," related to Old High German "grintan" (to gnash).
