fatalistic:/ˌfeɪtəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
宿命论的；听天由命的
believing that all events are predetermined and therefore inevitable.
He held a fatalistic view of life, believing there was nothing he could do to change his destiny. 他对人生抱有一种宿命论的观点，认为自己无力改变命运。

She had a fatalistic attitude towards the future. 她对未来抱有宿命论的态度。

近义词: resigned, defeatist, pessimistic
反义词: optimistic, hopeful, proactive
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *fatalisticus, from Latin fatalis (“ordained by fate”) + -isticus.
