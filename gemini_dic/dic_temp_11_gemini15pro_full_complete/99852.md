intermobility:/ˌɪntərmoʊˈbɪləti/
noun
相互移动性，不同群体间的移动性
The ability of people or things to move between different places or groups.
Intermobility between different social classes is becoming increasingly difficult. 不同社会阶层之间的相互流动变得越来越困难。
mobility, movement, transfer, exchange
immobility, stagnation, fixity
n.
