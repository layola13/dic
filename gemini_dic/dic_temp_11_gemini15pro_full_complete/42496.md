counterincentives:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈsɛntɪvz/| n. |反激励；抑制因素|Something that discourages a particular action or behavior.  It acts against the intended incentive.|The government introduced counterincentives to discourage excessive alcohol consumption.|Synonyms: deterrent, discouragement, impediment, obstacle|Antonyms: incentive, encouragement, motivation|Noun
