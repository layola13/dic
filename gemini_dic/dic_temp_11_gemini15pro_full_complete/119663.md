middleweight:/ˈmɪdlˌweɪt/| n. adj.| 中量级选手，中量级的|A boxer or other competitor in a middleweight class.  A person or thing of moderate weight or importance. | He was a natural middleweight. 他天生就是个中量级选手。|近义词：medium-weight, mid-sized|反义词: heavyweight, lightweight|词性:名词，形容词
Etymology:  mid- (meaning middle) + weight
