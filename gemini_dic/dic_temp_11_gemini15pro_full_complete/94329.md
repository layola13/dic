imphee:/ˈɪmfi/| n.|高粱的一种，甜芦粟|A variety of sorghum, originally from Africa, cultivated for its grain and syrup. It is drought-resistant and used for fodder and sugar production.|The farmer planted imphee to feed his livestock.|近义词：sorghum, sweet sorghum|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps other crops like wheat, rice)|noun
