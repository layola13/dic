Atapuerca: /ˌɑːtɑːˈpwɛrkɑː/
proper noun|阿塔普埃尔卡| Atapuerca is a municipality and town located in the province of Burgos, Castile and León, Spain. It is renowned for its important archaeological sites, including the Atapuerca Mountains, which contain some of the oldest hominin fossils found in Europe. | The Atapuerca mountains hold crucial clues to early human evolution. | 近义词: (As a place name, it has no direct synonyms.
