kotki:/ˈkɔtki/| n.|小猫 (复数)|Polish word for "kittens," the plural of "kotek."  It refers to young cats.|The kotki were playing with a ball of yarn. 小猫们正在玩毛线球。|Synonyms: kittens, young cats|Antonyms: adult cats, old cats|Noun
