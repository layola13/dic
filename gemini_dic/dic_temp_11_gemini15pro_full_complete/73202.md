forced-fed:/ˌfɔːrst ˈfed/ /ˌfɔːrsd ˈfed/ | adj. | 强制喂食的；填鸭式的 | Fed (past participle of feed) by force.  Meaning something is given to someone or something, especially in large amounts, whether they want it or not.  Often implies an overabundance or unwanted imposition.| The geese were forced-fed to produce foie gras.  这些鹅被强制喂食以生产鹅肝。| The students were forced-fed information before the exam. 学生们在考试前被填鸭式地灌输信息。|  近义词: force-fed, stuffed, crammed, overfed | 反义词: underfed, starved | 词性:形容词

forced-fed:/ˌfɔːrst ˈfed/ /ˌfɔːrsd ˈfed/ | v. | 强制喂食；填鸭式地灌输 | The act of force-feeding. |  The farmer forced-fed the geese corn. 农民强制喂鹅吃玉米。 | They force-fed the prisoners vitamins. 他们强行给囚犯喂维生素。 |  近义词: stuff, cram, overfeed | 反义词: underfeed, starve | 词性:动词
