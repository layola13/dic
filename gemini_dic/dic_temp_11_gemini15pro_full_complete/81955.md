grimly:/ˈɡrɪmli/ | adv. | 严酷地，冷酷地，可怕地 | In a stern, forbidding, or somber manner; seriously and gloomily. | He stared grimly at the news report. 他冷酷地盯着新闻报道。|  The soldier grimly faced the enemy.  士兵 grimly 面对敌人。 | sternly, seriously, gloomily, severely, fiercely | cheerfully, happily, lightly, playfully | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From grim (adjective) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The word "grim" comes from Old English "grim" meaning "fierce, cruel, severe," ultimately from a Proto-Germanic root meaning "fierce, angry."
