journal:/ˈdʒɜːrnl/ (US), /ˈdʒɜːnəl/ (UK)| n.|日记；期刊；杂志；分类账|A personal record of occurrences, experiences, and reflections kept on a regular basis; a periodical publication, especially one containing scholarly articles; a daily record of business transactions.|She kept a detailed journal of her travels. 她详细记录了她的旅行日记。|近义词：diary, periodical, magazine, record, log|反义词:None|Noun


journal:/ˈdʒɜːrnl/ (US), /ˈdʒɜːnəl/ (UK)| v.|把…记入日记；把…记入分类账|To record or enter in a journal.|He journaled his thoughts and feelings every night. 他每晚都把自己的想法和感受记在日记里。|近义词：record, log, document|反义词:None|Verb
