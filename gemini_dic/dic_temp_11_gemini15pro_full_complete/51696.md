devine:/dɪˈvaɪn/| adj.|神圣的；非凡的；极好的|Of, from, or like God or a god. Exceptionally good; wonderful.|例句：The meal was simply devine. 这顿饭简直太棒了。|近义词：holy, sacred, heavenly, wonderful, excellent, superb|反义词：profane, secular, awful, terrible|词性:形容词
divine:/dɪˈvaɪn/| v.|占卜；预测|Discover (something) by guesswork or intuition.|例句：She divined what he wanted. 她猜到了他想要什么。|近义词：predict, foretell, foresee, guess|反义词：-|词性:动词
