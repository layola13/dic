jin:/dʒɪn/| n.|杜松子酒；陷阱；困境|An alcoholic spirit distilled from grain or malt and flavored with juniper berries.  A trap or snare.|例句：He ordered a gin and tonic. 他点了一杯杜松子酒加汤力水。

例句：They felt caught in a financial jin. 他们感觉陷入了财务困境。|近义词：trap, snare, predicament, dilemma|反义词:solution, escape|词性:名词
