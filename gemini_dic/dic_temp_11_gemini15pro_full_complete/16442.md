barefacedness:/ˌbɛəˈfeɪsɪdnəs/| n. |厚颜无耻；无耻；公然|The barefacedness of the lie shocked everyone. |例句：He had the barefacedness to deny stealing the money, even though he was caught on camera. 他竟然厚颜无耻地否认偷了钱，即使被摄像头拍到了。

例句：Her barefacedness in claiming she wrote the song was astounding, given that it was a well-known classic. 她厚颜无耻地声称自己创作了这首歌，真是令人震惊，因为这是一首著名的经典歌曲。|近义词：audacity, impudence, brazenness, shamelessness|反义词：humility, modesty, shyness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From barefaced + -ness.  Barefaced (meaning shameless, impudent) dates back to the 16th century. It literally means having a bare or uncovered face, implying a lack of shame or concealment.
