nest-like:/ˈnestˌlaɪk/| adj.| 巢状的，像鸟巢的|Resembling a nest, especially in shape or structure.|The hummingbird's nest was small and nest-like, tucked away in the branches.|近义词：nest-shaped, cozy, snug|反义词:exposed, open|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "nest" (Old English *nest, from Proto-Germanic *nizdaz) + "-like" (Old English -līc).
