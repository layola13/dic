hyperphoric:/ˌhaɪpərˈfɔːrɪk/ /ˌhaɪpərˈfɔːrɪk/| adj.|欣快症的；过度兴奋的；情绪高涨的|Characterized by excessive elation or euphoria.  Relating to or marked by hyperphoria.|He was hyperphoric after winning the lottery. 他中了彩票后欣喜若狂。|Synonyms: elated, euphoric, exhilarated, jubilant, joyful, ecstatic|Antonyms: depressed, despondent, dejected, miserable, gloomy|adjective
