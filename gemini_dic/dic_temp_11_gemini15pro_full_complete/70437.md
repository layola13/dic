figuline:/ˈfɪɡjʊlaɪn/ |adj.|陶土制的；陶塑的|Made of or resembling earthenware, especially a fine kind of terracotta. |The museum boasts a remarkable collection of figuline statuettes.  博物馆收藏了大量精美的陶俑。| 近义词: earthenware, terracotta, ceramic | 反义词:  (No direct opposite, perhaps metallic, wooden) | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *figulinus*, from *figulus* "potter," related to *fingere* "to shape, mold."
