clotheshorse:/ˈkləʊðzˌhɔrs/ /ˈkloʊðzˌhɔrs/| n.|晾衣架；爱打扮的人|A frame for hanging clothes on to dry or air.  A person who spends a lot of time and effort on their clothes and appearance.|She uses a clotheshorse to dry her delicate laundry. 她用晾衣架晾晒她的精致衣物。

He's such a clotheshorse – always dressed to the nines. 他真是个衣架子，总是打扮得光鲜亮丽。|近义词：fashionista, dandy (for a person); clothes rack, drying rack (for the object)|反义词:slob (for a person)|词性:noun
