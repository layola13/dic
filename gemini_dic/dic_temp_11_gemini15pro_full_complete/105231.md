{-ful} itself is not a word but a suffix.

ful:/fʊl/ | suffix | 充满...的；具有...性质的；倾向于...的 |  A suffix that forms adjectives by indicating the presence of a quality or characteristic in abundance or to a specified degree. |  A beautiful flower. 一朵美丽的花。 | 近义词: full, abundant, replete | 反义词: less, lacking | 词性: suffix

USAGE->

The garden is full of colorful flowers.  花园里满是五颜六色的花。
She is always thoughtful and considerate. 她总是很体贴周到。

She has been very helpful with the project. 她对这个项目非常有帮助。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):

She has been acting resentful ever since the argument. 自从争吵以来，她一直表现得很愤恨。
