denominator:/dɪˈnɒmɪneɪtər/ (US) /dɪˈnɑːmɪneɪtər/ (UK)| n.|分母|The bottom number in a fraction. It shows how many equal parts the whole is divided into.|例句：The denominator in the fraction 3/4 is 4.  分数3/4中的分母是4。

|例句：A common denominator needs to be found to add these fractions. 需要找到一个公分母才能加这些分数。|近义词：divisor|反义词：numerator|词性: noun
