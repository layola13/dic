crispest:/ˈkrɪspɪst/| adj.|最脆的|Superlative form of crisp: most crisp.  Having a pleasantly firm, dry, and brittle texture.  Also, describes something concise and effective in style or expression.|The crispest lettuce in the salad was delightful. 沙拉里最脆的生菜令人愉悦。|近义词：firmest, crunchiest, brittlemost|反义词:softest, soggiest|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *crisp, from Old English *crisp, *forcrysped (“curled, wrinkled”), from Proto-Germanic *krispaz. Cognate with West Frisian krisp (“crisp”), Dutch krisp (“crisp”), German kraus (“curly, crisp”), Danish kruset (“curly”). More at crisp.
