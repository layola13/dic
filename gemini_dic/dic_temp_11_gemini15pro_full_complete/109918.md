libeled:/ˈlaɪbəld/ (US) /ˈlaɪbəld/ (UK)| v. |诽谤，中伤|To make false and damaging statements about someone. |例句：The newspaper libeled the politician with accusations of corruption. 这家报纸诽谤了这位政治家，指控他腐败。

例句：He was libeled in the press. 他在报刊上被诽谤了。

近义词：defamed, slandered, maligned, calumniated|反义词：praised, lauded|词性：动词

libel:/ˈlaɪbl/ (US) /ˈlaɪbl/ (UK)| n. |诽谤，中伤|A published false statement that is damaging to a person's reputation. |例句：The article was a vicious libel against him. 这篇文章是对他的恶意诽谤。

例句：She sued the newspaper for libel. 她以诽谤罪起诉了这家报纸。|近义词：defamation, slander, calumny|反义词：praise, commendation|词性：名词
