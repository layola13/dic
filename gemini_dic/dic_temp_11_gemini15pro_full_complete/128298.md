neurosecretory:/ˌnʊəroʊˈsiːkrɪtɔːri/
adjective|神经分泌的|Relating to or denoting nerve cells that secrete hormones.|Neurosecretory cells release hormones into the bloodstream.|近义词: endocrine, hormonal|反义词: exocrine|词性: adjective

neurosecretory:/ˌnʊəroʊˈsiːkrɪtɔːri/
noun|神经分泌细胞|A nerve cell that secretes hormones.|Neurosecretories play a crucial role in regulating various bodily functions.|近义词: endocrine cell, hormone-secreting neuron|反义词: exocrine cell|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From neuro- + secretory.  "Neuro-" comes from Greek "neuron" meaning "nerve." "Secretory" comes from Latin "secretus," past participle of "secernere" meaning "to separate."
