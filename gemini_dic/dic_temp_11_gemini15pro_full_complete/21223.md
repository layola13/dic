blog:/blɒɡ/ (British) /blɑːɡ/ (American)| n. v.|博客，网络日志|A regularly updated website or web page, typically one run by an individual or small group, that is written in an informal or conversational style.|She writes a blog about her travels around the world. 她写了一个关于她环球旅行的博客。|He blogs about his daily life. 他写博客记录他的日常生活。|近义词：weblog, online journal, diary|反义词: (None, as it's a specific form of online writing)|noun, verb
