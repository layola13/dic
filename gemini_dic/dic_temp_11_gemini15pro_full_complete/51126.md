despite:/dɪˈspaɪt/
preposition
尽管，虽然
in spite of; notwithstanding.
Despite the rain, we went for a walk. 尽管下雨了，我们还是去散步了。
Near synonyms: in spite of, notwithstanding, regardless of
Antonyms: because of, due to, owing to
词性: 介词
