aurocyanide:/ˌɔːrəʊˈsaɪənaɪd/
|n.|金氰化物|A compound containing gold and cyanide, typically used in gold extraction and electroplating.  A salt of aurocyanic acid (HAu(CN)2).|The aurocyanide solution is used in electroplating.|近义词:gold cyanide|反义词:N/A|noun
