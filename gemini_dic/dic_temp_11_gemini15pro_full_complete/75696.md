fuliginously:/ˌfjuːlɪˈdʒɪnəsli/
adjective
乌黑地；熏黑地；煤烟状地
covered with soot; sooty; smoky.
The old chimney spewed fuliginously black smoke into the air.  老烟囱向空中喷出乌黑的煤烟。
Synonyms: sooty, smoky, blackened, dark, dusky
Antonyms: clean, bright, clear, spotless
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fuliginosus, from fuligo ("soot").
