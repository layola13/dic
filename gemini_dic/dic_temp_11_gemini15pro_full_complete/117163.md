meant:/ment/ | v. | 意思是；打算；意味着 | past tense and past participle of mean |  She meant well. 她本意是好的。 | synonyms: intended, signified, implied | antonyms: unintended, accidental | verb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What does this word mean? = 这个词是什么意思？
This sign means "no parking". = 这个标志的意思是“禁止停车”。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you meaning by that? = 你那样说是什么意思？ (不太常用，通常用"What do you mean by that?")
I'm meaning to call her later. = 我打算晚点给她打电话。(不太常用，通常用"I mean to call her later.")


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have always meant to visit Paris. = 我一直想去巴黎。
She has meant a lot to me over the years. = 这些年来她对我来说意义重大。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been meaning to call you all week. = 我整个星期都想给你打电话。
He has been meaning to fix that leak for months. = 他几个月来一直想修补那个漏洞。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I meant to tell you yesterday. = 我昨天本来想告诉你的。
What did she mean by that remark? = 她那句话是什么意思？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was meaning to ask you about that. = 我正想问你那件事。
He was meaning to go to the gym, but he got caught up at work. = 他本来想去健身房，但工作耽误了。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had meant to call you, but I forgot. = 我本来想给你打电话的，但我忘了。
She had meant to finish the book before the trip, but she ran out of time. = 她本来打算在旅行前看完这本书，但时间不够了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been meaning to visit her for ages. = 我一直想去看她很久了。
He had been meaning to learn Spanish for years. = 他多年来一直想学西班牙语。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will mean to do it tomorrow. (不太常用，通常用 “I will do it tomorrow.”) = 我明天会去做。
This will mean a lot to her. = 这对她来说意义重大。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be meaning to do it tomorrow. (不自然，最好避免使用)
This will be meaning a lot to her. (不自然，最好避免使用)


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, I will have meant to call her for a month. (不自然，最好避免使用)
By then, it will have meant a lot to us. = 到那时，它对我们来说将意义重大。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been meaning to visit them for a whole year. (不自然，最好避免使用)
By then, it will have been meaning a lot to us for several years. = 到那时，它对我们来说将已经意义重大好几年了。



虚拟语气:
If I meant to hurt you, I would have apologized. = 如果我故意要伤害你，我早就道歉了。
I wish I had meant what I said. = 我希望我说的是真心话。



被动语态:
What was meant by that statement? = 那句话是什么意思？
Nothing is meant to be taken personally. = 没有什么需要针对个人。



疑问句:
What did you mean? = 你是什么意思？
What do you mean by that? = 你那样说是什么意思？



否定句:
I didn't mean to offend you. = 我不是故意冒犯你的。
She didn't mean any harm. = 她没有恶意。
