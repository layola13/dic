cooperatives:/koʊˈɑːpərətɪvz/ | n. | 合作社；合作企业 |A business or organization owned and run jointly by its members, who share the profits or benefits.| Our local farmers market is run by a cooperative of organic growers.  我们当地的农贸市场是由一个有机种植者合作社经营的。|  The cooperative provides affordable housing for its members.  合作社为其成员提供经济适用房。 |synonyms: collective, partnership, alliance, union | antonyms: corporation, conglomerate, privately owned business | noun

cooperative:/koʊˈɑːpərətɪv/ | adj. | 合作的；协作的；配合的 |Involving mutual assistance in working toward a common goal.|  The witness was very cooperative with the police.  证人非常配合警方。|  We need a more cooperative approach to solve this problem. 我们需要更合作的方式来解决这个问题。| synonyms: collaborative, helpful, accommodating, joint | antonyms: uncooperative, unhelpful, obstructive, resistant | adjective
