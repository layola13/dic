ginman: /ˈɡɪnmən/ | n. | 酒保；卖杜松子酒的人 | A person who sells or serves gin. | The ginman poured a generous measure. 酒保倒了满满一杯酒。| 近义词：bartender, barkeeper | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑teetotaler-禁酒主义者，但语境不同) | 词性: 名词

Etymology: gin + man.  "Gin" comes from the Dutch word "jenever," referring to juniper, the flavoring used in the spirit. "Man" is a common suffix denoting a person associated with a particular activity or thing.
