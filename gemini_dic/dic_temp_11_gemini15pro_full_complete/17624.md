beblot:/ˈbiːblɒt/|n.|污点，墨渍|A stain or blot of ink. |例句：There was a large beblot on the first page of the manuscript. 手稿的第一页上有一个很大的墨渍。|例句：He tried to remove the beblot with a damp cloth. 他试图用一块湿布去除墨渍。|近义词：blot, stain, smudge|反义词: cleanness, spotlessness|词性: noun
词源: From be- + blot.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "all over."  "Blot" comes from Old French "blotte," meaning a drop or spot.
