bolls:/boʊlz/| n.| 棉铃，棉桃；圆形物体 |A rounded seed capsule, especially that of the cotton plant.  The boll is the part of the cotton plant that contains the seeds and fibers.|The cotton bolls were bursting with fluffy white fibers. 棉桃里满是蓬松的白色纤维。|Near Synonyms: capsules, pods, orbs|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms like "fragments," "shards" if referring to broken bolls)|Noun
