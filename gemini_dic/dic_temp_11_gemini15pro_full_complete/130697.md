nonagglutinative:/ˌnɒnəˈɡluːtɪnətɪv/
adjective
非黏着语的
Not agglutinative; characterized by a lack of agglutination, where morphemes are combined without significant changes in their form.  A nonagglutinative language tends to rely more on word order and separate words to express grammatical relationships.
English is largely a nonagglutinative language. 英语在很大程度上是一种非黏着语。
Many isolating languages are also nonagglutinative. 许多孤立语也是非黏着语。
synonym: isolating, analytic
antonym: agglutinative, synthetic, polysynthetic
词性: adjective
