broaching:/ˈbroʊtʃɪŋ/| n. |钻孔；拉削；开孔；提出（讨论）；开始谈论|The act of making a hole or beginning a discussion.|例句：The broaching of the delicate subject was met with silence.  提出这个敏感话题时，大家都沉默了。|近义词：raising, introducing, mentioning, initiating|反义词:concealing, hiding|词性:noun

broaching:/ˈbroʊtʃɪŋ/| v. |钻孔；拉削；开孔；提出（讨论）；开始谈论|To pierce, to make a hole, or to begin a discussion.|例句：He broached the subject of a pay raise with his boss.  他向老板提出了加薪的要求。|近义词：raising, introducing, mentioning, initiating|反义词:concealing, hiding|词性:verb
