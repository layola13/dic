ankerite:/ˈæŋkəraɪt/| n.|铁白云石|A calcium, iron, and magnesium carbonate mineral, Ca(Fe,Mg,Mn)(CO3)2.  It is a member of the dolomite group.  It typically occurs in sedimentary rocks and metamorphic rocks.|Ankerite is a common mineral in hydrothermal veins. 铁白云石是热液矿脉中常见的矿物。|The rock sample contained significant amounts of ankerite. 岩石样本中含有大量的铁白云石。|Synonyms: dolomite, siderite, ferroan dolomite|Antonyms: None|noun
