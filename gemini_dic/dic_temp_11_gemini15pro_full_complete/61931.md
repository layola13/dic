emulators:/ˈɛmjʊleɪtərz/| n. |模拟器；仿真器|A device or program that imitates the behavior of another system, especially a computer or video game console.|I use an emulator to play old Nintendo games on my PC. 我用模拟器在我的电脑上玩老任天堂游戏。|Near synonyms: simulator, imitator|Antonym: original|noun
