harmonica:/hɑːrˈmɒnɪkə/ (US), /hɑːˈmɒnɪkə/ (UK)| n. |口琴|A small rectangular wind instrument with a row of metal reeds along its length, held against the lips and played by blowing or sucking air through it.|He played a melancholic tune on his harmonica. 他用口琴演奏了一首忧郁的曲子。|近义词：mouth organ|反义词: N/A|名词
