autochthonousness:/ˌɔːtɒkˈθɒnəsnəs/ | n. | 土著性；原住民性；本土性 | The state or quality of being autochthonous; indigenousness. | The archaeologist stressed the autochthonousness of the artifacts discovered in the ancient ruins. 考古学家强调了在古代遗址中发现的文物的本土性。|  indigenousness, nativeness, aboriginality | foreignness, alienness | noun

autochthonous:/ɔːˈtɒkθənəs/ | adj. | 土著的；原住民的；本土的 | Indigenous rather than descended from migrants or colonists. |  The autochthonous people of this island have a rich cultural heritage.  这个岛上的土著居民拥有丰富的文化遗产。 | indigenous, native, aboriginal | foreign, alien | adjective
