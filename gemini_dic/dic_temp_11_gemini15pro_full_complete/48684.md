defervescence:/ˌdefərˈvesəns/
noun
退烧，热度减退
The abatement of a fever.  A decrease in fever; the period when a fever subsides.
After a week, the patient experienced defervescence, and his temperature returned to normal.  一周后，病人退烧了，体温恢复正常。
synonyms: abatement, decline, remission, subsiding
antonyms: fever, pyrexia, exacerbation
