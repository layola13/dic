hillwalker:/ˈhɪlˌwɔːkər/| n. |徒步旅行者，登山爱好者|A person who walks in the hills, especially for recreation.|例句：Many hillwalkers enjoy the challenge of climbing Ben Nevis. 许多登山爱好者喜欢攀登本尼维斯山的挑战。

例句：The hillwalkers packed their rucksacks and set off for the summit. 登山者们收拾好背包，出发前往山顶。|近义词：hiker, rambler, climber|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) sedentary person, couch potato|词性:noun
