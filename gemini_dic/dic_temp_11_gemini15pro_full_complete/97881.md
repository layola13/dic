innate:/ɪˈneɪt/ /ɪˈnɪːt/| adj.|先天的，固有的，天生的|Existing from birth; inborn; native.|例句：He has an innate talent for music. 他有天生的音乐才能。|例句：Her innate sense of rhythm made her a natural dancer. 她天生的节奏感使她成为一个天生的舞者。|近义词：inborn, inherent, natural, intrinsic|反义词：acquired, learned, developed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *innatus*, from Latin in- “in” + natus “born,” past participle of nasci “to be born.”
