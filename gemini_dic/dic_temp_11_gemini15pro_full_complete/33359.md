chumpishnesses:/ˈtʃʌmpɪʃnɪsɪz/| n. |愚蠢；傻气|The state of being chumpish; stupidity or foolishness.  It refers to a collection or aggregation of instances of chumpish behavior or qualities.|His chumpishnesses were constantly getting him into trouble. 他的愚蠢行为总是让他陷入困境。|近义词：foolishness, stupidity, silliness, idiocy|反义词：wisdom, intelligence, cleverness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From chumpish + -ness. Chump is likely from a dialectal variant of chump, meaning "thick piece of wood," later applied to a person perceived as dull or stupid.
