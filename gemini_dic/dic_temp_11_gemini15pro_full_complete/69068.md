faulting:/ˈfɔːltɪŋ/| n.|断层作用；故障；毛病|The faulting of the rocks suggests a history of seismic activity. 岩石的断层作用表明了地震活动的历史。|例句：The San Andreas Fault is a major strike-slip faulting zone.  圣安地列斯断层是一个主要走滑断层带。

faulting:/ˈfɔːltɪŋ/| n.|责备；挑剔；找茬|His constant faulting of others was annoying. 他不断地责备别人，真让人讨厌。|例句：Her faulting of the project proposal was unwarranted. 她对项目提案的挑剔是没有根据的。

faulting:/ˈfɔːltɪŋ/| n.|（电子设备）故障|The faulting of the computer system caused a major disruption. 计算机系统的故障导致了重大的中断。|例句：Intermittent faulting of the electrical system made troubleshooting difficult.  电力系统间歇性的故障使得故障排除变得困难。



近义词: fracturing, cracking, blaming, criticizing, malfunction, defect
反义词: praising, commending, repairing, fixing
词性: noun (名词)

词源：From the verb "fault" (from Old French "falte," meaning "lack, want, default"), + "-ing" suffix forming nouns from verbs.
