imagism:/ˈɪmədʒɪzəm/| n.|意象主义|A movement in early 20th-century Anglo-American poetry that favored precision of imagery and clear, sharp language.|Ezra Pound was a key figure in the Imagism movement.  埃兹拉·庞德是意象主义运动中的关键人物。|
|Example 1:  Imagism emphasized the use of precise language. 意象主义强调使用精确的语言。

Example 2:  The poem was a perfect example of Imagism. 这首诗是意象主义的完美典范。|Synonyms:  modernism, symbolism|Antonyms:  romanticism, sentimentalism|noun
