drink-to:/ˈdrɪŋk tuː/ | phrasal verb | 为...干杯；祝...健康 | To raise one's glass and consume its contents as an expression of goodwill or celebration towards someone or something. |  Let's drink to the success of the new project! 让我们为新项目的成功干杯！ | synonyms: toast, salute, cheers | antonyms: N/A | verb phrase

drink-to:/ˈdrɪŋk tə/ | phrasal verb | 为...而喝；以酒消愁 | To consume alcohol in response to a particular feeling or event, often negative. | He was drinking to forget his troubles. 他借酒消愁。 | synonyms: drown (one's sorrows), console oneself with alcohol | antonyms: abstain, refrain | verb phrase
