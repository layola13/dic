misappropriated:/ˌmɪsəˈproʊprieɪtɪd/| adj.| 被盗用的，被挪用的，被滥用的 |Having been dishonestly or unfairly taken for someone's own use, especially money or property entrusted to them. | The misappropriated funds were never recovered. 被挪用的资金从未追回。| synonyms: embezzled, pilfered, purloined, misused | antonyms: allocated, returned, donated | adjective
