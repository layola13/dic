neurotypical:/ˌnʊrəˈtɪpɪkəl/ | adj. | 神经正常的，非神经多样化的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a person who does not have autism or another developmental difference. | Example: The program is designed for neurotypical children.  这个项目是为神经正常的孩子设计的。 | Example:  She explains social cues that neurotypical people understand instinctively. 她解释了神经正常的人本能地理解的社交线索。 | 近义词: normal, typical | 反义词: neurodivergent, autistic | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  neuro- (prefix meaning "nerve," from Greek "neuron") + typical (from Late Latin "typicus," meaning "of or pertaining to a type or figure").
