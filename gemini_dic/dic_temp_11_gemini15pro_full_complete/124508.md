mound:/maʊnd/| n. |土堆，堆，大量|A small hill or pile of earth or stones; a large pile or quantity of something.|The archaeologists discovered a burial mound. 考古学家发现了一个墓冢。|Synonyms: hill, heap, pile, stack, mass|Antonyms: valley, hollow|noun

mound:/maʊnd/| v. |堆积，筑堤|To form into a mound; to heap up.|She mounded the mashed potatoes on the plate. 她把土豆泥堆在盘子里。|Synonyms: heap, pile, stack|Antonyms: scatter, disperse|verb
