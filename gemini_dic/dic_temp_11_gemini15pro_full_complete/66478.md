exercising:/ˈeksərsaɪzɪŋ/
v.
锻炼；练习；运用
present participle of exercise
She's exercising her right to free speech. 她正在行使她的言论自由权。
synonyms: training, working out, practicing, using, applying
antonyms: resting, relaxing, neglecting
