commute:/kəˈmjuːt/ (US) /ˈkɒmjuːt/ (UK)| n.|通勤（路程）|The journey made regularly between one's home and place of work.|My commute takes about an hour. 我的通勤时间大约是一个小时。|近义词:journey, trip|反义词:stay, residence|词性:名词


commute:/kəˈmjuːt/ (US) /ˈkɒmjuːt/ (UK)| v.|减刑|To reduce a judicial sentence to one less severe.|His death sentence was commuted to life imprisonment. 他的死刑被减为无期徒刑。|近义词:reduce, lessen|反义词:increase, extend|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *commūtāre*, from com- (“together, with”) + mūtāre (“to change”).
