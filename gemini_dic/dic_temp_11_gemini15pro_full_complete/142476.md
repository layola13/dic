osierlike:/ˈoʊʒərˌlaɪk/| adj.|像柳条的；柔韧的|Resembling an osier, especially in being slender, flexible, and tough.|The osierlike branches swayed gently in the breeze.  像柳条一样的枝条在微风中轻轻摇曳。|The plant's stems were long and osierlike, ideal for weaving. 这种植物的茎又长又像柳条，很适合编织。|近义词：willowy, pliable, flexible, lithe, supple|反义词：rigid, stiff, inflexible|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From osier +‎ -like.  Osier comes from Middle English *osier, from Anglo-Norman oser, osyer, from Old French osier, from Frankish *ausari, from Proto-Germanic *ausō (“ear”).  -like is a suffix meaning "resembling, having the characteristics of".
