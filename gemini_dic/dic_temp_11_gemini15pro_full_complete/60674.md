electress:/ɪˈlɛktrɪs/| n.|女选帝侯，王妃|A woman who has the right to take part in the election of an emperor or other sovereign ruler, especially (formerly) a German princess entitled to vote in the election of the Holy Roman Emperor.  A wife or widow of an elector.|例句：The electress was known for her political acumen.  这位女选帝侯以其政治敏锐度而闻名。

例句：The electress cast her vote for the new emperor. 女选帝侯投票选举了新皇帝。|近义词：princess, noblewoman|反义词:commoner|词性:noun
