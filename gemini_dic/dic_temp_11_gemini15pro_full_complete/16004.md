bams:/bæmz/| n.|突然的打击，重击；(非正式)欺骗；(非正式)兴奋剂；|A sudden loud noise, especially one caused by an explosion or a heavy object hitting something.  Also (informal) a swindle or fraud. Also (informal) stimulating drugs, especially amphetamines.|The explosion went off with a tremendous bams! 爆炸发出一声巨响！|近义词：bang, wallop, thump, crash, explosion, swindle, fraud, drugs, amphetamines|反义词:whisper, silence|词性:名词

bams:/bæmz/| v.|(非正式)用力打；猛击；(非正式)欺骗；|To hit something hard. Also (informal) to cheat or swindle someone.|He bammed the door shut. 他砰地一声关上了门。|近义词：bang, slam, wallop, thump, hit, strike, cheat, swindle|反义词:caress, stroke|词性:动词
