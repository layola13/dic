bearish:/ˈbɛərɪʃ/| adj.|看跌的，悲观的|Resembling or suggesting a bear, especially in clumsiness or ill temper; (of a market) falling or expected to fall.|The stock market has been bearish for the past few months. 股市在过去几个月里一直看跌。|Synonyms: pessimistic, gloomy, downbeat, negative, depressed|Antonyms: bullish, optimistic, positive|adjective

bearish:/ˈbeərɪʃ/| adj.|笨拙的，粗暴的|Resembling or suggesting a bear, especially in clumsiness or ill temper. |He gave me a bearish glare. 他狠狠地瞪了我一眼。|Synonyms: clumsy, awkward, ungainly, lumbering|Antonyms: graceful, agile, nimble|adjective
