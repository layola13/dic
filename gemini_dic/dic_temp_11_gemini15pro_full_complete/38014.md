compliment:/ˈkɒmplɪmənt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmplɪmənt/ (US)| n. | 赞美，恭维; 敬意，问候 | An expression of praise, admiration, or congratulation.  |She received many compliments on her new dress. 她因为她的新裙子收到了很多赞美。| synonyms: praise, accolade, commendation | antonyms: criticism, insult, slight | noun
compliment:/ˈkɒmplɪmənt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmplɪmənt/ (US)| vt. | 赞美，恭维 | To express praise or admiration to someone.  |He complimented her on her excellent presentation. 他称赞了她的精彩演讲。| synonyms: praise, laud, extol | antonyms: criticize, condemn, disparage | verb
