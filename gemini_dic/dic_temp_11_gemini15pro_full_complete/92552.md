hysteranthous:/ˌhɪstərˈænθəs/
adjective
后出叶的；花先于叶开放的
(of a plant) producing flowers before leaves.
Example:  The almond tree is hysteranthous, displaying its beautiful blossoms before its leaves emerge. 杏树是后出叶的，在叶子出现之前就开出了美丽的花朵。

Example:  Hysteranthous plants often bloom in early spring before the canopy of leaves develops. 后出叶植物通常在早春树叶冠层发育之前开花。
synonym: precocious (in flowering)
antonym: serophyllous (leaves appearing before flowers)
词性: adjective
