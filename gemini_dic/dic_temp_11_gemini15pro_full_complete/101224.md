invitational:/ˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃənəl/| adj.|邀请的；邀请赛的|Relating to or being an invitational tournament or meet.|The invitational tournament attracted top players from around the world. 这场邀请赛吸引了来自世界各地的顶尖选手。|Example: The golf tournament is invitational only. 这场高尔夫锦标赛只接受邀请的选手参加。|Synonyms: selective, exclusive|Antonyms: open, public|Adjective


invitational:/ˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃənəl/| n.|邀请赛|A competition or meeting to which only specific people or teams are invited.|The annual invitational draws the best amateur golfers.  一年一度的邀请赛吸引了最好的业余高尔夫球手。|Example:  He received an invitation to the invitational. 他收到了邀请赛的邀请函。|Synonyms: tournament, competition|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
