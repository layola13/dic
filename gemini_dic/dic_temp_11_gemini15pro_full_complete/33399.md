churchgoing:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌɡəʊɪŋ/ |adj.|经常去教堂的；虔诚的|Attending church regularly; devoted to church. |She comes from a very churchgoing family. 她来自一个非常虔诚的家庭。|He's not very churchgoing these days. 他这些日子不太常去教堂了。|近义词：religious, devout, pious|反义词：irreligious, non-religious, secular|词性:形容词

Etymology:
church + going (present participle of 'go')
