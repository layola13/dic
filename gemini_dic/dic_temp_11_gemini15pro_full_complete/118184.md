mentioning:/ˈmɛnʃənɪŋ/ | v. |提及，说起|The act of referring to something or someone briefly and incidentally. | He avoided mentioning her name. 他避免提及她的名字。|  I forgot mentioning that I'll be late tomorrow. 我忘了提我明天会迟到。|近义词: referring to, alluding to, citing, quoting |反义词: omitting, ignoring, concealing |词性:动词 (gerund or present participle of "mention")
