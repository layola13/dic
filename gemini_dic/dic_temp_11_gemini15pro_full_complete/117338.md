medevacking:/ˌmɛdɪˈvækɪŋ/
noun, verb (present participle)
医疗后送，医疗撤离
The process of evacuating someone from a dangerous or remote area to a place where they can receive medical treatment, typically by aircraft.
They were medevacking the wounded soldiers to a nearby hospital. 他们正在用飞机将受伤的士兵后送到附近的医院。
Synonyms: medical evacuation, airlift, evacuation
Antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a process)
词性: noun, verb (present participle)
