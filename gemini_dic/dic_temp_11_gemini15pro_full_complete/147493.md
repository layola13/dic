overornamented:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːrnəmɛntɪd/
adjective
过度装饰的
Excessively decorated; adorned with too much ornament.  Characterized by an overabundance of decoration, often to the point of gaudiness.
The overornamented Victorian mansion felt cluttered and oppressive.  这栋过度装饰的维多利亚式豪宅让人感到凌乱和压抑。
The wedding cake was so overornamented it looked like a plastic monstrosity. 婚礼蛋糕装饰得太过火了，看起来像个塑料怪物。
synonyms: overdecorated, fussy, ostentatious, flamboyant
antonyms: simple, plain, understated, minimalist
词性: adjective
