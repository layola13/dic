cloke:/kloʊk/| n.|斗篷，披风；掩饰，幌子|A long, loose outer garment, typically sleeveless, worn over other clothes.  Something that hides or disguises something else.|He wore a heavy cloke to protect himself from the cold. 他穿了一件厚厚的斗篷来御寒。|The charity used the cloke of religion to solicit donations.  该慈善机构以宗教为幌子募集捐款。|近义词：cape, mantle, cloak|反义词: (For the literal meaning) None; (For the figurative meaning) revelation, exposure|词性: noun
