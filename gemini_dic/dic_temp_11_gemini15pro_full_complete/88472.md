histopathologies:/ˌhɪstoʊpəˈθɑːlədʒiz/
|n.|组织病理学（研究）|The study of diseased tissues at the microscopic level, typically to diagnose diseases.|The histopathologies of the tumor samples revealed cancerous cells.|近义词:histopathology, microscopic anatomy|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "gross pathology")|词性:Noun (Plural)

Etymology:
histo- (Greek histos "tissue") + pathology (Greek pathos "suffering" + -logia "study")
