horseweed:/ˈhɔːrswiːd/
n.
马草，加拿大飞蓬
A common North American weed (Conyza canadensis, family Asteraceae, also called Erigeron canadensis) with small white or pale yellow flowers, often considered invasive.  It can grow up to 2 meters tall and is commonly found in disturbed habitats.
The farmer struggled to eradicate the horseweed from his fields.  这个农民努力从他的田地里清除马草。
近义词：butterweed, fleabane, hogweed
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
