词源：
From Scots *hamesucken, from Middle English hamsocn, from Old English hamsōcn (“housebreaking, the crime of assaulting a person in their home”), from hām (“home”) + sōcn (“seeking, visiting, infesting, molesting, assault”).


注意事项：
hamesecken 是一个主要用于苏格兰法律的术语，在其他英语变体中较少使用。burglary 或 home invasion 是更常见的替代词。
