acolyte:/ˈækəˌlaɪt/ | n. |侍僧，助手，追随者 |  A person assisting a priest in religious services; a devoted follower or attendant. | He acted as the surgeon's acolyte during the operation. 他在手术期间担任外科医生的助手。|  The pop star had a devoted group of acolytes who followed her everywhere.  这位流行歌星有一群忠实的追随者，无论她走到哪里都跟着她。 |synonyms: assistant, follower, attendant, disciple, supporter | antonyms: leader, opponent, critic | noun
