animadversive:/ˌænɪmədˈvɜːsɪv/
adjective
批评的；非难的；责备的
Characterized by or expressing censure; critical or fault-finding.
The teacher made some animadversive comments about the student's messy handwriting. 老师对学生潦草的字迹提出了一些批评。
He cast an animadversive glance at the noisy children. 他责备地瞥了一眼吵闹的孩子们。
synonyms: critical, censorious, reproving, rebuking, reproving
antonyms: complimentary, praising, laudatory, approving
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin animadversus, past participle of animadvertere (“to turn one's mind to, notice, consider, remark upon”), from animus (“mind”) + advertere (“to turn to”), from ad- (“to, toward”) + vertere (“to turn”).
