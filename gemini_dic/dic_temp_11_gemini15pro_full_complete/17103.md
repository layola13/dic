batsmen:/ˈbætsmən/ (UK) /ˈbætsmən/ (US)| n. |击球手|A player who bats in cricket.|Example: The batsmen scored a century. 这位击球手得了一百分。

Example:  The opening batsmen walked onto the field.  开场击球手走进了球场。


|近义词：batter, hitter|反义词:bowler|词性:noun
