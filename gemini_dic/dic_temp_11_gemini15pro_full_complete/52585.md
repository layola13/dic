dig-up:/ˌdɪɡ ˈʌp/| phrasal verb | 挖出；发现；翻出 | To remove something from the ground by digging; to discover or unearth something, often information. |  They dug up the buried treasure. 他们挖出了埋藏的宝藏。

| unearth, discover, excavate | bury, conceal, hide | verb phrase

dig-up:/ˌdɪɡ ˈʌp/| phrasal verb | 挖出；发现；翻出 | To find something that has been hidden or forgotten. |  She dug up some old photos from her childhood. 她翻出了一些童年时的老照片。

| find, uncover, locate | lose, misplace, forget | verb phrase
