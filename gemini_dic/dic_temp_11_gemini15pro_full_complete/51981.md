diadromous:/daɪˈædrəməs/
| adj.|洄游的；溯河的；降河的|Migrating between fresh and salt water.|Salmon are diadromous fish, spending part of their lives in rivers and part in the ocean. 鲑鱼是洄游鱼类，它们一部分时间生活在河流中，一部分时间生活在海洋中。|近义词：migratory, anadromous, catadromous|反义词:non-migratory|词性:adjective

diadromy:/daɪˈædrəmi/
| n.|洄游；溯河产卵；降河产卵|The migratory behavior of fish between fresh and salt water.|The diadromy of the eel is a fascinating natural phenomenon. 鳗鱼的洄游是一种迷人的自然现象。|近义词：migration|反义词:sedentariness|词性:noun
