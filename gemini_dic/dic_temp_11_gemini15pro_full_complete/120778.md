misapprehension:/ˌmɪsæprɪˈhenʃən/
n.
误解，误会
A mistaken belief about or interpretation of something.
She was under the misapprehension that I was married. 她误以为我结婚了。
misunderstanding, misconception, misinterpretation
correct understanding, accurate perception
noun
