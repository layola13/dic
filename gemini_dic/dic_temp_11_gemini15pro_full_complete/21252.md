blonket: /ˈblæŋkɪt/ | n. |毯子; 毛毯 | A large piece of woolen or similar material used as a bed covering or other covering for warmth. |  I snuggled under the warm blanket. 我依偎在温暖的毯子下。

She knitted a beautiful blanket for the baby. 她为婴儿织了一条漂亮的毯子。 | 近义词: quilt, cover, comforter, duvet, throw | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 sheet 床单,  表示一种对比) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old North French *blankete (unattested), diminutive of blanc "white". Originally blankets were typically white.
