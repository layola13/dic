nonpertinence:/nɒnˈpɜːtɪnəns/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɜːrtnəns/ | n. | 无关紧要；不恰当；不相干 |  Lack of relevance or appropriateness to the subject at hand. |  His comments on the weather were met with stares, given their nonpertinence to the serious discussion.  他关于天气的评论与严肃的讨论毫不相干，因此引来了大家注视的目光。| irrelevance, immateriality, inappositeness | relevance, pertinence, germaneness | noun

nonpertinent:/nɒnˈpɜːtɪnənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɜːrtnənt/ | adj. | 无关紧要的；不恰当的；不相干的 | Not relevant or appropriate to the subject at hand. | The lawyer objected to the nonpertinent questions posed by the opposing counsel.  律师反对对方律师提出的不相关的问题。 | irrelevant, immaterial, extraneous | relevant, pertinent, germane | adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *non pertinent- (stem of non pertinēns), from non ‘not’ + pertinent- (stem of pertinēns) ‘pertaining’, present participle of pertinēre.  Ultimately from Latin per- ‘through’ + tenēre ‘to hold’.
