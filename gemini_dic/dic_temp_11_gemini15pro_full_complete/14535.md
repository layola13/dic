averrable:/ˈævərəbl/ | adj. | 可证实的，可断言的 |  Capable of being averred; assertable.  This means something can be stated positively and confidently, often with evidence to support the claim. | The witness provided averrable testimony, clearly stating what they had seen. 目击者提供了可证实的证词，清楚地陈述了他们所看到的。

The scientist's findings were averrable, based on meticulous research and data analysis.  这位科学家的发现是可证实的，基于细致的研究和数据分析。| 近义词: assertable, verifiable, demonstrable, provable | 反义词: unprovable, questionable, disputable, dubious | 词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "aver" (meaning to state positively and confidently), which comes from Old French "averer" and ultimately from Latin "adverare" (to make true, to verify).
