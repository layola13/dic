arithmetic: /əˈrɪθmətɪk/ (US) /əˈrɪθməˌtɪk/ (UK)| n. |算术，算法|The branch of mathematics dealing with the properties and manipulation of numbers.|She's good at arithmetic. 她擅长算术。|Calculation, computation|Advanced mathematics, higher mathematics|noun
arithmetic: /əˈrɪθmətɪk/ (US) /əˈrɪθməˌtɪk/ (UK)| adj. |算术的|Relating to arithmetic.|He made an arithmetical error. 他犯了一个算术错误。|Computational, numerical|---|---|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *arismetique*, from Latin arithmetica, from Ancient Greek ἀριθμητική (arithmētikḗ, “arithmetical (art)”), from ἀριθμός (arithmós, “number”) + -τική (-tikḗ).
