cross-fixed:/ˌkrɒsˈfɪkst/ | adj. | 交叉固定的，互相固定的 | Fixed or fastened across each other.  This describes something secured in place by crossing another element over it, creating a more stable structure. | The scaffolding was cross-fixed for added stability. 脚手架交叉固定以增加稳定性。 | The beams were cross-fixed with steel cables. 梁用钢缆交叉固定。 | 近义词:interlocked, braced, secured | 反义词: loose, unfixed, detached | 词性:形容词
