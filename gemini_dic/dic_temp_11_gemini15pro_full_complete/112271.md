lophobranchii:/ˌlɒfəˈbræŋkiˌaɪ/ /ˌloʊfəˈbræŋkiˌaɪ/
n.
海龙鱼目
An order of bony fish that includes seahorses, pipefish, and seadragons, characterized by a tube-like snout and an outer covering of bony plates.
Seahorses and pipefish belong to the order Lophobranchii. 海马和海龙都属于海龙鱼目。
Near synonyms: Syngnathidae (This is the family within Lophobranchii that contains seahorses and pipefish.  It is a more specific term.)
No real antonym exists for a taxonomic classification.
