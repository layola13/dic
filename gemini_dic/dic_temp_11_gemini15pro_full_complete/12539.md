asphyxied:/əsˈfɪksiːd/
|adj.|窒息的；闷死的|Deprived of oxygen, causing unconsciousness or death; suffocated.|The fire victims were asphyxied by the smoke.|近义词：suffocated, choked, smothered|反义词：ventilated, oxygenated|词性:形容词
asphyxiate:/əsˈfɪksieɪt/
|vt.|使窒息；闷死|To cause someone to become unconscious or die by preventing them from breathing.|The fumes from the fire asphyxiated several people.|近义词：suffocate, choke, smother|反义词：resuscitate, ventilate|词性:动词
asphyxiation:/əsˌfɪksiˈeɪʃən/
|n.|窒息|The state of being deprived of oxygen, leading to unconsciousness or death.|Asphyxiation is a common cause of death in house fires.|近义词：suffocation, choking|反义词：ventilation, oxygenation|词性:名词
