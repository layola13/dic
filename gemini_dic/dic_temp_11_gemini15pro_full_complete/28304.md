car-pooled: /ˈkɑːrˌpuːld/ | v. |拼车|To travel to and from work or school with others in a single car, thereby reducing traffic congestion and fuel consumption. |例句：We car-pooled to the conference to save on gas. 我们拼车去参加会议以节省汽油。|例句：They've been car-pooling for years to reduce their carbon footprint. 多年来，他们一直拼车以减少碳足迹。|近义词：ride-shared, shared a ride|反义词: drove alone|词性: 动词

car-pooling: /ˈkɑːrˌpuːlɪŋ/ | n. |拼车|The act of sharing a car with others for commuting. |例句：Car-pooling is a great way to save money and help the environment. 拼车是省钱和保护环境的好方法。|例句：The company encourages car-pooling by providing designated parking spaces. 公司通过提供指定的停车位来鼓励拼车。|近义词：ride-sharing, lift-sharing|反义词: driving alone|词性: 名词
