marigenous:/məˈrɪdʒɪnəs/
adjective
海洋生的，海生的
Produced in or formed by the sea.
例句：Marigenous organisms thrive in the coral reefs. 海洋生物在珊瑚礁中繁衍生息。

例句：The study focused on the impact of pollution on marigenous life. 这项研究的重点是污染对海洋生物的影响。

近义词：marine, oceanic, aquatic
反义词：terrestrial, land-based
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mari* ("sea") + *-genus*  ("produced, born").  Similar to "indigenous."
