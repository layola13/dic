interjectural:/ˌɪntərˈdʒɛktʃərəl/| adj.|插入的，感叹的|Of, relating to, or characteristic of an interjection; thrown in abruptly.|例句：He made an interjectural remark during the meeting. 他在会议期间插了一句。|近义词：parenthetical, inserted, ejaculatory|反义词：continuous, integrated|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *interjecturalis, from Latin interjectus, past participle of intericere (“to throw between”), from inter (“between”) + jacere (“to throw”).
