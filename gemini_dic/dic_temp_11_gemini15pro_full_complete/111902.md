lohock:/ˈloʊhɒk/| n.|润喉药，药糖|A medicinal confection, often in the form of a lozenge, used to soothe the throat.|She took a lohock to ease her cough. 她吃了一颗润喉糖来缓解咳嗽。|Synonyms: lozenge, pastille, cough drop|Antonyms: irritant|noun

From Middle English *lohoc*, from Medieval Latin *loch*, from Arabic لُوق *lūq*.
