illusion:/ɪˈluːʒən/ (US), /ɪˈljuːʒn/ (UK)| n. |幻觉，错觉，幻想| something that deceives by producing a false or misleading impression of reality. |例句：The magician created the illusion of disappearing. 魔术师制造了消失的幻觉。|例句：He had no illusions about his chances of winning. 他对自己的获胜机会不抱幻想。|近义词：delusion, mirage, fantasy, misconception|反义词：reality, truth, actuality|noun
