monotremes:/ˈmɒnətriːmz/ | n. | 单孔目动物 | Mammals that lay eggs instead of giving birth to live young, such as the echidna and the platypus. |  Echidnas and platypuses are the only surviving monotremes.  针鼹和鸭嘴兽是仅存的单孔目动物。 | synonyms: prototheria | antonyms: (no direct antonym, could consider placental mammals or marsupials) | noun
