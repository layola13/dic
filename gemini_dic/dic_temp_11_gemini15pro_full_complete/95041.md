inactivates:/ɪnˈæktɪveɪts/
verb
使不活跃，使失效，使失活
makes something inactive or no longer effective
The vaccine inactivates the virus.  疫苗使病毒失活。
Heating the serum inactivates the antibodies. 加热血清会使抗体失效。
synonyms: deactivates, disables, neutralizes
antonyms: activates, enables
词性: 动词
