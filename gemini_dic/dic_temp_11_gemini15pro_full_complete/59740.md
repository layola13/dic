ecocide:/ˈɛkəʊsaɪd/ | n. | 生态灭绝，生态破坏 | The destruction of the natural environment, especially when deliberate. |  The Amazon rainforest is facing ecocide due to deforestation. 亚马逊雨林由于森林砍伐正面临生态灭绝。|  environmental destruction, biocide |  environmental protection, conservation | noun

Etymology:  From eco- + -cide, from Latin caedere "to kill."  Coined in the 1970s.
