agnostical: /æɡˈnɒstɪkəl/ /æɡˈnɑːstɪkəl/
adjective
不可知论的
Relating to agnosticism; holding the view that any ultimate reality (such as God) is unknown and probably unknowable.
He has an agnostic attitude towards the existence of God. 他对上帝的存在持不可知论的态度。

agnostical: /æɡˈnɒstɪkəl/ /æɡˈnɑːstɪkəl/
noun
不可知论者
A person who believes that nothing is known or can be known of the existence or nature of God or of anything beyond material phenomena; a person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God.
She identified herself as an agnostic. 她自认为是一个不可知论者。


近义词: skeptical, doubting, unbelieving
反义词: believing, faithful, religious
词性: adjective, noun

词源: From Greek *agnōstos* "unknown, unknowable" (from *a-* "not" + *gnōstos* "known," from *gignōskein* "to know") + *-ikos* "-ic" + *-al*.
