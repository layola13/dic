image:/ˈɪmɪdʒ/ | n. |图像，形象，印象|A visual representation of something, such as a photograph or painting; a mental picture or concept; a person's public perception. |例句：The image on the screen was blurry. 屏幕上的图像很模糊。|近义词：picture, photograph, likeness, representation, impression|反义词：reality|词性：noun

image:/ɪˈmædʒɪn/ | v. |想象，设想|To form a mental picture or concept of something; to suppose or believe. |例句：I can't image life without you. 我无法想象没有你的生活。|近义词：visualize, conceive, suppose, think, believe|反义词：disbelieve|词性：verb
