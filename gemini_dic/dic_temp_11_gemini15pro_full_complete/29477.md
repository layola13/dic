cataphonic: /ˌkætəˈfɒnɪk/ /ˌkætəˈfɑːnɪk/ | adj. |紧张性精神分裂症的；紧张症的 | Relating to or characterized by catatonia, a state of apparent unresponsiveness to external stimuli in a person who is apparently awake.  |  The patient exhibited cataphonic symptoms, remaining motionless for hours.  病人表现出紧张症症状，一动不动地保持了几个小时。 |  Synonyms: catatonic, akinetic, rigid, stuporous  | Antonyms: mobile, responsive, active | adjective

cataphony: /kəˈtɑːfəni/ /kəˈtɒfəni/ | n. |紧张症 | A state of apparent unresponsiveness to external stimuli in a person who is apparently awake. | The cataphony lasted for several days before the patient began to respond to treatment.  紧张症持续了几天，病人随后才开始对治疗有反应。 | Synonyms: catatonia, akinesia | Antonyms: mobility, responsiveness | noun
