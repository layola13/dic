cuboid:/ˈkjuːbɔɪd/| n. adj.|长方体，立方形的|A solid figure with six rectangular faces.  A box-like shape.|例句：The present was wrapped in cuboid packaging.  礼物是用长方体包装包起来的。|近义词：rectangular prism, box-shaped|反义词:sphere,  irregular shape|noun
cuboid:/ˈkjuːbɔɪd/| adj.|长方体的，立方形的|Shaped like a cuboid; having six rectangular faces.|例句：The child played with brightly coloured cuboid blocks.  孩子玩着颜色鲜艳的长方体积木。|近义词：box-like, rectangular|反义词:spherical, irregular|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Late Latin *cuboīdēs*, from Latin cubus (“cube”) (from Ancient Greek κύβος (kúbos, “cube, die”)) + -oid (“-like”).
