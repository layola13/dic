defensiveness:/dɪˈfɛnsɪvnəs/| n.|防御性，守势，戒备心|the quality or state of being defensive; inclined to defend oneself or one's actions.|Her defensiveness made it difficult to have an open conversation. 她的戒备心使得很难进行坦诚的交流。|近义词：guardedness, protectiveness, wariness|反义词：openness, receptiveness|词性:名词

Etymology: From 'defensive' + '-ness'. 'Defensive' comes from the Late Latin 'defensivus', from Latin 'defendere' (to defend). '-ness' is a suffix forming nouns expressing state, quality, or condition.
