cocomposers:/ˌkoʊkəmˈpoʊzərz/| n. |合作作曲家，共同作曲者|A person who composes music with another person or other people.|例句：The cocomposers worked tirelessly to finish the symphony. 合作作曲家们不知疲倦地工作，完成了交响曲。|例句：Mozart and Salieri were sometimes cocomposers, although their relationship was complex. 莫扎特和萨列里有时是合作作曲家，尽管他们的关系很复杂。|近义词：collaborators, joint composers|反义词:solo composer|词性:noun
