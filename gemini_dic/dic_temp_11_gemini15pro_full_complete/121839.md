misremembrance:/ˌmɪsrɪˈmɛmbrəns/| n. |错误的记忆；记错；误记|An inaccurate or faulty memory of something.|She had a sudden misremembrance of her childhood, placing the event a year later than it actually occurred. 她对自己的童年突然有了错误的记忆，把这件事发生的时间记晚了一年。|近义词：false memory, paramnesia, distorted memory|反义词：accurate memory, recollection|词性: noun
