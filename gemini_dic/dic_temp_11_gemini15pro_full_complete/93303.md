ill-conceived:/ˌɪlkənˈsiːvd/ | adj. |构思拙劣的；考虑不周的；计划不当的|Not carefully planned or considered; poorly designed or thought out.|The ill-conceived plan ended in disaster. 这个构思拙劣的计划以失败告终。|Synonyms: misguided, ill-advised, foolish, unwise, flawed, impractical|Antonyms: well-conceived, well-planned, well-thought-out, sound, judicious|Etymology: From "ill" (badly) + "conceived" (past participle of "conceive," meaning to form an idea or plan).
