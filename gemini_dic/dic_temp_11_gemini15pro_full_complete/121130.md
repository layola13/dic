misdefine:/ˌmɪsdɪˈfaɪn/
verb
错误地定义；定义不当
to define incorrectly; to give a wrong or inaccurate definition
The word "love" is often misdefined.  爱这个词经常被错误地定义。
The government misdefined the criteria for eligibility. 政府错误地定义了资格标准。
Synonyms: misinterpret, misconstrue, misrepresent
Antonyms: define correctly, interpret correctly, represent accurately
词性: 动词
