chiltern:/ˈtʃɪltərnz/ | n. |奇尔特恩丘陵; 奇尔特恩百老汇剧团 |A range of hills in south central England, extending from Oxfordshire to Hertfordshire. Also, a parliamentary constituency traditionally offered to a member of Parliament who wishes to resign.  A member accepting the Chiltern Hundreds is disqualified from sitting in the House of Commons. | The Chiltern Hills are a popular destination for walkers. 奇尔特恩丘陵是远足爱好者的热门目的地。 He took the Chiltern Hundreds to resign from Parliament. 他接受了奇尔特恩的职位以便从议会辞职。 | synonyms: Chiltern Hills, Chiltern Hundreds | antonyms: N/A | noun
