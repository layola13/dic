adjacently:/əˈdʒeɪsəntli/| adv.|邻近地，毗邻地|Situated next to or very near something else.|The two houses are situated adjacently. 这两栋房子彼此相邻。|例句：The parking lot is adjacently located to the shopping mall. 停车场就在购物中心旁边。|近义词：nearby, alongside, next to, bordering|反义词:distant, far, apart|词性:副词
词源：From the adjective adjacent + -ly.  Adjacent comes from the Latin adjacēns, adjacent-, present participle of adjacēre "to lie near," from ad- "to" + jacēre "to lie."
