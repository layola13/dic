glamorizes:/ˈɡlæməˌraɪzɪz/
verb
使...更有魅力；美化；吹捧
makes (something) seem glamorous or desirable, especially spmething that is not.
The movie glamorizes violence.  这部电影美化了暴力。
The ad glamorizes smoking. 这则广告美化了吸烟。
synonyms: romanticizes, idealizes, glorifies, exalts
antonyms:  debunks, exposes, vilifies, criticizes
词性: 动词 (verb)
