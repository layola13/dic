dichroic:/daɪˈkroʊɪk/ | adj. | 二色的，双色的，二向色的 | Exhibiting dichroism; having the property of presenting two different colors, especially by exhibiting different colors by reflected and transmitted light. | The gemstone displayed a dichroic effect, appearing blue when viewed from one angle and green from another. 这颗宝石呈现出二色性效果，从一个角度看是蓝色，从另一个角度看是绿色。 | Synonyms: dichromatic, two-colored | Antonyms: monochromatic | adjective


dichroscopic:/daɪkrəˈskɒpɪk/ | adj. | 二色的，双色的 | Relating to or exhibiting dichroism; exhibiting two different colors, especially when viewed under polarized light. | The dichroscopic mineral appeared to change color as the polarizing filter was rotated.  当旋转偏振滤光片时，这种二色性矿物看起来会改变颜色。 | Synonyms: dichroic, dichromatic | Antonyms: monochromatic | adjective
