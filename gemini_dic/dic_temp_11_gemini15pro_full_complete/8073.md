anthropomorphism:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/
n.
拟人化；赋予人性
The attribution of human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object.
例句：The children's book was full of anthropomorphism, with the animals talking and wearing clothes.  这本童书充满了拟人化，动物们会说话，还穿着衣服。

例句：Anthropomorphism is common in children's stories, where animals often have human qualities. 在儿童故事中，拟人化很常见，动物通常具有人类的品质。


近义词：personification, humanization
反义词: dehumanization
词性: noun
