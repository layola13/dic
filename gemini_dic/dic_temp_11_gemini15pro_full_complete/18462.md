bemourn:/bɪˈmɔːrn/| vt.|哀悼，悲叹|To express deep sorrow or regret for the loss or death of someone or something.|She bemoaned the loss of her youth. 她哀叹自己青春的流逝。|
例句：He bemoaned the death of his friend. 他哀悼朋友的去世。
例句：The nation bemourned the loss of its leader. 全国人民都在哀悼失去的领袖。

近义词：lament, grieve, mourn, bewail|
反义词：rejoice, celebrate|
词性：动词(verb)|

词源：From Middle English *bimornen, from be- + murnen (“to mourn”), from Old English murnan (“to mourn, grieve, be anxious”), from Proto-Germanic *murnaną (“to mourn, care, be anxious”), from Proto-Indo-European *smer- (“to care, worry”). Cognate with German murren (“to grumble”).|
