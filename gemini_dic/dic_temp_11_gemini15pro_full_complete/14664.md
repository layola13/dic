avowing:/əˈvaʊɪŋ/
verb
公开宣称，承认
openly declaring or acknowledging something
He was avowing his love for her. 他公开宣称爱她。
近义词: declaring, admitting, professing, affirming
反义词: denying, concealing, hiding, disavowing
词性: verb
