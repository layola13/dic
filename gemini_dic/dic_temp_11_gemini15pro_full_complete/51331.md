detectability:/dɪˌtɛktəˈbɪləti/
n.|可探测性，可检测性|The detectability of the signal is low due to the background noise. 由于背景噪音，信号的可探测性很低。|例句：The detectability of the submarine is crucial for its survival.  这艘潜艇的隐蔽性对于它的生存至关重要。|近义词：discoverability, perceptibility, visibility|反义词：invisibility, imperceptibility, undetectability
