knuckliest:/ˈnʌkliɪst/| adj.|最顽固的，最难对付的|Having or showing the most stubborn, uncompromising, or difficult attitude or character.  This often implies a rough or tough demeanor. |He was the knuckliest old farmer I'd ever met, refusing to change his ways. 他是我遇到的最顽固的老农，拒绝改变他的生活方式。|近义词：stubbornest, toughest, most obstinate|反义词: most compliant, most agreeable|词性:形容词
knuckle: /ˈnʌkəl/ | n. v. |指关节；用指关节敲打|The bony joint of a finger; to rap with the knuckles.|He rapped his knuckles on the door. 他用指关节敲了敲门。
