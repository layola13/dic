chymist:/ˈkɪmɪst/| n.|炼金术士; 化学家|An archaic term for a chemist, especially one who practiced alchemy.  A person who studied or practiced alchemy.|例句：The chymist toiled in his laboratory, seeking the philosopher's stone.  炼金术士在他的实验室里辛勤工作，寻找着点金石。

例句：The aged chymist claimed to have discovered the elixir of life. 年迈的炼金术士声称发现了长生不老药。|近义词：alchemist, chemist|反义词: None (archaic word, no direct opposite)|词性:noun
