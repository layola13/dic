noncuttings:/nɒnˈkʌtɪŋz/
noun
非插枝；非扦插苗
Material, such as seeds or bulbs, used for propagation that is not a cutting.
They propagated the plants from noncuttings, relying on seeds and division instead.  他们用种子而不是插枝来繁殖植物。
近义词:seeds, bulbs, rhizomes
反义词:cuttings
词性:n.
