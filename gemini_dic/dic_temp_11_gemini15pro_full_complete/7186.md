angiomonospermous: /ˌændʒi.oʊˌmɒn.oʊˈspɜːr.məs/ | adj. | 被子植物单种子的 | Having or producing only one seed in each fruit, characteristic of certain angiosperms.  |  Example: The plant is angiomonospermous, producing a single seed within its fleshy fruit.  这种植物是单种子的，在肉质果实内只产生一颗种子。| Example:  Researchers studied the angiomonospermous characteristics of the newly discovered species. 研究人员研究了新发现物种的单种子特性。| Near Synonyms: monospermous, unispermous | Antonyms: polyspermous, multiseeded | Adjective
