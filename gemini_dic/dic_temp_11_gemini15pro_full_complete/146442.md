overfrailty:/ˌoʊvərˈfreɪlti/| n.|过度虚弱，极度脆弱|A state of extreme frailty, beyond what is considered normal for a particular age or condition.  It encompasses physical, cognitive, and social vulnerability.|The study focused on the **overfrailty** observed in certain isolated communities.|Synonyms: extreme frailty, severe debility, pronounced weakness|Antonyms: robustness, vigor, resilience|noun
