avenage:/ˈævɪnɪdʒ/
noun
燕麦；野燕麦；（总称）饲料燕麦
Oats or wild oats used as fodder.
The farmer planted avenage for his livestock. 农民种植燕麦作为牲畜的饲料。
Synonyms:  oats, fodder, forage
Antonyms: None (lacks a direct opposite)
词性: 名词
