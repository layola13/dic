aggravative:/əˈɡrævətɪv/
adjective
加重的，恶化的
making worse; irritating; increasing in severity
His aggressive behavior was aggravative, escalating the already tense situation. 他的侵略性行为加剧了本来就很紧张的局势。
近义词:exacerbating, worsening, irritating, provoking
反义词:alleviating, mitigating, soothing, calming
词性:adjective

aggravating:/ˈæɡrəveɪtɪŋ/
adjective
恼人的，令人气愤的
annoying, irritating
The aggravating mosquito buzzed around my head all night.  烦人的蚊子整夜在我头上嗡嗡叫。
近义词:irritating, annoying, exasperating, vexing
反义词:soothing, calming, pleasing, pleasant
词性:adjective


词源:From Late Latin aggravat- ‘made heavier’, from the verb aggravare, from ad- ‘to’ + gravare ‘to burden’.
