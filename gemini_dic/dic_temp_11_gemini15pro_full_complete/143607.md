outintriguing:/ˌaʊtɪnˈtriːɡɪŋ/
adjective
更加引人入胜的，格外迷人的
surpassing in intrigue; more intriguing than others
例句：Her story was outintriguing, filled with unexpected twists and turns. 她的故事更加引人入胜，充满了意想不到的曲折。

例句：The new exhibit was outintriguing, capturing the attention of visitors of all ages.  新的展览格外迷人，吸引了各个年龄段的游客的注意力。

近义词：fascinating, captivating, compelling, absorbing
反义词：boring, dull, uninteresting, tedious
词性：adjective
