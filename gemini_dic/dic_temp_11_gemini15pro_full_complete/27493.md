campeachy: /ˌkæmˈpiːtʃi/ | adj. n.|坎佩切的，坎佩切木，坎佩切湾| Of or relating to Campeche, a state in southeastern Mexico, or its capital city. Also refers to a type of wood, logwood, that produces a black dye, historically important to the region. Can also refer to Campeche Bay, a large bay in the southern Gulf of Mexico. | The vibrant textiles of Campeche are renowned for their intricate designs. 坎佩切充满活力的纺织品以其复杂的图案而闻名。

The campeachy wood was once a highly valued commodity. 坎佩切木曾经是一种非常珍贵的商品。

Fishing in the Campeche Bay is a major industry. 在坎佩切湾捕鱼是一项主要产业。|近义词：Mexican, Yucatecan (in some contexts)|反义词: N/A|词性: Adjective, Noun
