administratrix: /ədˈmɪnɪstreɪtrɪks/
noun
女遗产管理人
A woman who is appointed by a court to administer the estate of someone who has died without leaving a will.
The administratrix was responsible for distributing the assets of the deceased. 女遗产管理人负责分配死者的资产。

The administratrix oversaw the sale of the property.  女遗产管理人监督了房产的出售。

synonym: executrix, female administrator
antonym: executor (male), administrator (gender-neutral)
