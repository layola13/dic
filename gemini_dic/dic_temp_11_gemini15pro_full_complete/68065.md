fadable:/ˈfeɪdəbl/| adj.|可褪色的，易褪色的|Likely to fade; capable of fading. |The color of this fabric is fadable, so avoid exposing it to direct sunlight.  这种布料的颜色容易褪色，所以避免将其暴露在阳光直射下。|The printed image is fadable over time.  打印的图像会随着时间的推移而褪色。|近义词：bleachable, fading, transient, ephemeral|反义词：permanent, lasting, indelible|词性:形容词

fadable:/ˈfeɪdəbl/|词源：fade + -able|From "fade" (to lose freshness, brightness, or strength) + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").  源自“fade”（失去新鲜度、亮度或强度）+“-able”（后缀，意思是“能够”）。
