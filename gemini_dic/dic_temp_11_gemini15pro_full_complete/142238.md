n.
山系；地形；山志学
The branch of physical geography dealing with mountains and mountain systems.
例句：The orography of the region makes it difficult to farm.  该地区的地形使得耕作困难。

例句：The orography of the Himalayas affects the monsoon patterns in India. 喜马拉雅山的地形影响了印度的季风模式。

近义词：terrain, topography, relief
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "flatland" in a descriptive sense)
词性: noun
