converter:/kənˈvɜːrtər/ (US) /kənˈvɜːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |转换器，转化器|A device or machine that converts something from one form to another, such as energy, data, or signals.|This catalytic converter reduces harmful emissions from the car. 这个催化转化器减少了汽车的有害排放。|synonyms: transformer, adapter, transducer|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could be considered opposite of its function, e.g., inhibitor, blocker depending on context)|noun


converter:/kənˈvɜːrtər/ (US) /kənˈvɜːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |皈依者，改变信仰者|A person who has changed their religion or beliefs.|He was a converter to Buddhism after years of studying Eastern philosophy.  经过多年的东方哲学研究，他皈依了佛教。|synonyms: proselyte, neophyte|antonyms: apostate, heretic|noun
