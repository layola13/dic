overcorrupted:/ˌoʊvərˈkərʌptɪd/ | adj. |过度腐败的，极度腐败的 | excessively corrupted; deeply and thoroughly corrupted | The overcorrupted government finally collapsed under the weight of its own misdeeds.  过度腐败的政府最终在其自身罪行的重压下垮台了。 | The system was so overcorrupted that it was beyond repair.  这个系统腐败到无可救药的地步。 | Synonyms: extremely corrupt, utterly corrupt, thoroughly corrupt, deeply corrupt | Antonyms: honest, incorruptible, uncorrupted | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + corrupted, past participle of corrupt.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much".  "Corrupt" comes from the Latin "corruptus", past participle of "corrumpere", meaning "to break up, destroy, spoil, bribe".
