edgiest:/ˈɛdʒiɪst/
adjective
最前卫的；最尖端的；最具争议的
Being the most innovative, provocative, or unconventional.  Pushing boundaries and challenging norms.
His music is considered the edgiest in the industry. 他的音乐被认为是业内最前卫的。
The gallery showcased the edgiest contemporary art.  这家画廊展出了最前卫的当代艺术。
近义词:avant-garde, cutting-edge, daring, provocative, radical, revolutionary, unorthodox
反义词:conventional, mainstream, traditional, conservative
词性:adjective
词源:From edge (n.) + -y + -est.  Originally referring to a sharp physical edge, the meaning extended metaphorically to innovative or daring qualities.
