dispermous: /daɪˈspɜːməs/
| adj. | 二籽的；双种子的 | Having or producing two seeds. | This plant is dispermous, producing two seeds in each fruit.  这种植物是二籽的，每个果实里产生两颗种子。 | Example 2: Dispermous fruits are characteristic of this particular genus.  双种子果实是这个特定属的特征。 | Synonym: two-seeded | Antonym: monospermous, polyspermous | adjective
