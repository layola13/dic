gauchest:/ˈɡɔːtʃɪst/| adj.|最笨拙的，最不灵巧的|Most awkward or clumsy; lacking social grace or tact. |例句：He made the gauchest apology I've ever heard. 他做了我听过的最笨拙的道歉。

例句：Her dancing was gauchest, but she enjoyed it immensely. 她的舞姿很不优美，但她非常享受。

近义词：clumsiest, awkwardest, most inept|反义词: graceful, elegant, deft, adroit|词性:形容词

词源: From gauche (awkward) + -est (superlative suffix). Ultimately from Old French gauchir (to warp, turn aside).
