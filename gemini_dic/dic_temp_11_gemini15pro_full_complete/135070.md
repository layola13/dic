nonnucleated:/ˌnɑːnˈnuːkliːeɪtɪd/
adjective
无核的
Lacking a nucleus; not nucleated.  This term refers to cells that do not contain a nucleus.
Red blood cells are nonnucleated. 红细胞是无核的。
Mature mammalian red blood cells are nonnucleated to allow for more space to carry hemoglobin. 成熟的哺乳动物红细胞是无核的，以便有更多的空间携带血红蛋白。
