flytrap:/ˈflaɪˌtræp/| n. |捕蝇器，捕蝇陷阱|A plant that traps and digests insects.  A device for catching flies.|例句：The Venus flytrap is a carnivorous plant.  捕蝇草是一种食肉植物。

例句：He hung a flytrap in the kitchen. 他在厨房里挂了一个捕蝇器。|近义词：flypaper, insect trap|反义词:None|词性:noun
