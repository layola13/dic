demonomist: /ˌdiːmɒˈnɒmɪst/ | n. | 恶魔经济学家; 恶魔经济师 |  An economist whose theories or policies are perceived as harmful or destructive.  This is often a pejorative term. | Example: Some critics labelled him a demonomist for supporting austerity measures.  一些批评家因为他支持紧缩措施而给他贴上了恶魔经济学家的标签。

Example:  The demonomist's policies led to widespread unemployment. 这个恶魔经济学家的政策导致了广泛的失业。 | 近义词: none (This is a highly specific and somewhat informal term, direct synonyms are rare) | 反义词:  benevolent economist, humanitarian economist | 词性: noun
