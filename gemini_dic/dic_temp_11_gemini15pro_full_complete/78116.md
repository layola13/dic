geology:/dʒiˈɒlədʒi/ /dʒiˈɑːlədʒi/| n.|地质学；地质情况|The geology of the area is very interesting.  这个地区的地质情况非常有趣。|例句：The geology of this region suggests it was once a seabed. 这个地区的地质情况表明它曾经是海底。|近义词：earth science|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *géologie*, from Ancient Greek γῆ (gê, “earth”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
