hummocked:/ˈhʌməkt/ | adj. | 隆起的，波状的 | Covered with small mounds or hummocks; resembling a series of hummocks. | The hummocked ice made crossing the glacier treacherous.  冰面上隆起的冰丘使得穿越冰川十分危险。 | The terrain was hummocked and uneven.  地形起伏不平，呈波状。 |  Synonyms: undulating, bumpy, rolling, hilly, uneven | Antonyms: flat, level, smooth | adjective


hummock:/ˈhʌmək/ | n. | 小丘，土墩 | A small, rounded hill or mound. |  We climbed the hummock to get a better view. 我们爬上小丘以便更好地观看。 |  The tundra was dotted with hummocks of earth and ice. 苔原上点缀着泥土和冰形成的小丘。 | Synonyms: mound, knoll, hillock, dune | Antonyms: valley, depression | noun
