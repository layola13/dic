antipolar:/ˌæntiˈpəʊlə(r)/ /ˌæntiˈpoʊlər/
adjective
相反的，对映的，极地的
Relating to or situated at opposite poles or extremes; diametrically opposed.  Specifically in biology,  pertaining to opposite poles or extremities of a cell or organism, often referring to the distribution of molecules or structures during cell division.
The antipolar distribution of the proteins ensures proper cell function.  蛋白质的对映分布确保了细胞的正常功能。

The two groups hold antipolar views on the issue. 这两个团体在这个问题上持有截然相反的观点。

近义词:opposite, contrary, contrasting
反义词:similar, alike, identical
词性:adjective
