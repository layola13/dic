n.
弹珠玩家；玩弹珠的人
People who play marbles.
The marblers gathered in the park for a tournament. 弹珠玩家们聚集在公园里参加比赛。

近义词:marble players

词性: noun (plural)
词源: From "marble" (the small glass ball used in the game) + "-er" (suffix denoting a person who performs an action).  Ultimately from Old French "marbre" meaning marble (the stone).
