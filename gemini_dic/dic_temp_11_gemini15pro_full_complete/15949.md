balog:/ˈbɑːlɒɡ/ /ˈbeɪlɔːɡ/ | n. | 博客;网络日志 | A weblog, a website containing a writer's or group of writers' own experiences, observations, opinions, etc., and often having images and links to other websites. |  例句：She regularly updates her balog with travel photos and stories. 她定期用旅行照片和故事更新她的博客。

例句：He started a balog to share his expertise on gardening. 他开设了一个博客来分享他的园艺知识。 | 近义词：blog, weblog, online journal, online diary | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "offline journal" or "private diary") | 词性: noun
