overmanaging:/ˌoʊvərˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度管理
Managing too closely or excessively; interfering too much in the details of something.
He was accused of overmanaging his team, stifling their creativity. 他被指责过度管理他的团队，扼杀了他们的创造力。
Micromanaging can lead to employees feeling overmanaged and demoralized.  微观管理会导致员工感到被过度管理和士气低落。
近义词: micromanaging, meddling, interfering
反义词: delegating, empowering, trusting
词性: verb (present participle)
