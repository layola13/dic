例句：His nonconforming views often put him at odds with his colleagues. 他不墨守成规的观点经常使他与同事意见相左。

|近义词：dissident, unorthodox, rebellious, maverick, individualistic, unconventional|反义词：conforming, conventional, orthodox, compliant, obedient|词性:形容词
