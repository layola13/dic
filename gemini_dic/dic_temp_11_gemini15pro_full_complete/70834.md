finish-coats:/ˈfɪnɪʃ koʊts/
noun
面漆；罩面漆；最终涂层
The final coats of paint or other finishing material applied to a surface.  These coats provide the final appearance and protection.
The finish-coats on the car gave it a glossy sheen. 汽车的面漆使它焕发光泽。
Synonyms: topcoats, final coats
Antonyms: base coats, undercoats
n.
