crossbowmen:/ˈkrɒsbəʊmən/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsbəʊmən/ (US)| n. |弩兵；弩手|A soldier armed with a crossbow. |例句：The crossbowmen took aim at the approaching enemy. 弩兵瞄准了逼近的敌人。

例句：The king's army included a large contingent of crossbowmen. 国王的军队包括一大批弩兵。|近义词：archer, bowman|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly a swordsman or other close-combat soldier) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crosbowman*, equivalent to crossbow +‎ -man.  Crossbow itself comes from Old French *crosbow*, ultimately from Latin *crux* (cross) and *arcus* (bow).
