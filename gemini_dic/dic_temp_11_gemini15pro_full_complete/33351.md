chummy: /ˈtʃʌmi/
adjective
亲密的；友好的
Friendly and sociable.
They were very chummy with each other. 他们彼此非常友好。
The atmosphere at the party was chummy and relaxed.  派对上的气氛亲切而轻松。
近义词: friendly, amicable, sociable, close
反义词: unfriendly, hostile, distant
词性: adjective

词源:
From "chum" (close friend) + "-iness" (suffix forming nouns of state or quality)  or  "chum" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Chum" itself likely originated in the 17th century, possibly from a dialectal word meaning "chamber fellow" or "roommate."
