flutteringly:/ˈflʌtərɪŋli/
adverb|飘动地，颤抖地，扑动地|In a way that flutters; with a fluttering motion.  Characterized by quick, light movements like a bird's wings.|The butterfly wings moved flutteringly in the breeze. 蝴蝶的翅膀在微风中飘动。|近义词：lightly, airily, waveringly, tremblingly|反义词: steadily, firmly
词性: 副词
