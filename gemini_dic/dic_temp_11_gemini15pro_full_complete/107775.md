lapidus:/ˈlæpɪdəs/| adj.|多石的，坚硬的|Covered with or containing stones; stony.  Also, resembling stone; hard, inflexible.|例句：The lapidus terrain made hiking difficult. 多石的地形使徒步旅行变得困难。

例句：His lapidus expression gave nothing away. 他那毫无表情的脸让人猜不透他的心思。|近义词：rocky, stony, hard, inflexible|反义词：soft, smooth, fertile|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *lapidus*, from *lapis* ("stone").
