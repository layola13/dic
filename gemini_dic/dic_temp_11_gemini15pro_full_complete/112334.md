loreto:/ləˈrɛtoʊ/ (US) /ləˈreɪtəʊ/ (UK)
n.
洛雷托
A town or city named Loreto.  Specifically refers to Loreto, Italy, a major pilgrimage destination.
Loreto is a popular tourist destination. 洛雷托是一个受欢迎的旅游目的地。
The Basilica della Santa Casa in Loreto is a famous shrine. 洛雷托的圣家大教堂是一个著名的圣地。
Synonyms: shrine, pilgrimage site
Antonyms: (None directly applicable, potentially "secular location")
