ensign:/ˈɛnsən/ (US) /ˈɛnsɪn/ (UK)| n.|旗帜；徽章；海军少尉|A flag or banner, especially a national flag flown by a ship; a badge of office or authority; the lowest commissioned officer rank in the US Navy or Coast Guard.|The ship flew the ensign of the United States. 这艘船悬挂着美国国旗。|近义词：flag, banner, standard, pennant, insignia, lieutenant|反义词:|n.
