edulious:/ɪˈdjuːliəs/ | adj. | 可食用的；适于食用的 | Fit or suitable to be eaten. | Example 1: The berries of this plant are edulioous, but the leaves are poisonous. 这种植物的浆果可以食用，但叶子有毒。

Example 2:  After careful inspection, the mushroom was deemed edulioous.  经过仔细检查，这蘑菇被认为是可食用的。 | 近义词: edible, comestible | 反义词: inedible, poisonous, toxic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *ēdūlis, from ēdō (“I eat”).
