guarantees:/ˌɡærənˈtiːz/
n.|保证，担保（guarantee的名词复数）|A set of things that assures something.|The store guarantees satisfaction with all its products. 这家商店保证对其所有产品都感到满意。
近义词:assurances, pledges, warranties
反义词:denials, disclaimers

guarantee:/ˌɡærənˈtiː/
n.|保证，担保|Something that assures a particular outcome or condition.|The company offers a money-back guarantee. 这家公司提供退款保证。
近义词:assurance, pledge, warranty
反义词:denial, disclaimer

guarantee:/ˌɡærənˈtiː/
vt.|保证，担保|To promise that something will happen or be the case.|I guarantee you'll love this book. 我保证你会喜欢这本书的。
近义词:assure, pledge, warrant
反义词:deny, disclaim

词源：
From Old French *guarantir, from guarant “warrant, protector, defense,” of Germanic origin.
