joker:/ˈdʒoʊkər/ (US), /ˈdʒəʊkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|小丑，玩笑者，鬼牌|A joker is a playing card, typically depicting a jester or fool, which is used in some card games as a wild card or the highest-ranking card.  A joker can also refer to a person who likes to tell jokes or play pranks.|He was always the joker in the group, making everyone laugh. 他一直是群体中的开心果，逗得大家哈哈大笑。|Synonyms: clown, comedian, prankster, fool, wild card|Antonyms: serious person|noun

joker:/ˈdʒoʊkər/ (US), /ˈdʒəʊkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|令人出乎意料的因素，未知数|An unpredictable factor or element that can influence a situation.|The weather can be a real joker in a cricket match.  天气在板球比赛中真是一个变数。|Synonyms: wild card, unknown quantity, unpredictable factor|Antonyms: predictable factor|noun
