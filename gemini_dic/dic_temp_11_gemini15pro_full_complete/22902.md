bother:/ˈbɒðə(r)/ (UK) /ˈbɑːðər/ (US)|v. n.|打扰，麻烦，费心；烦恼，操心|To cause somebody trouble or to make somebody feel slightly annoyed, worried, or upset.|例句：Don't bother me now, I'm busy.  现在别打扰我，我很忙。

例句：It bothers me that he didn't call. 他没打电话让我很心烦。|近义词：annoy, disturb, trouble, worry|反义词：please, comfort, soothe|词性：动词/名词


bother:/ˈbɒðə(r)/ (UK) /ˈbɑːðər/ (US)|n.|麻烦，不便|Something that causes minor trouble or inconvenience.|例句：It's no bother at all.  一点也不麻烦。

例句：I had a lot of bother finding a parking space. 我费了好大劲才找到一个停车位。|近义词：trouble, difficulty, inconvenience|反义词：ease, convenience|词性：名词
