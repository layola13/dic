arting-up: /ˈɑːtɪŋ ʌp/
verb phrase
装饰；装扮；打扮
The process of decorating or enhancing something, often to make it more visually appealing or festive.  It can also refer to dressing up in a stylish or elaborate manner.
They were arting-up the house for the Christmas party.  他们正在为圣诞派对装饰房子。
She spent hours arting herself up for the prom. 她花了几个小时打扮自己去参加舞会。
Synonyms: decorating, embellishing, adorning, sprucing up, dressing up, glamming up
Antonyms: stripping down, simplifying, undressing
