hampering:/ˈhæmpərɪŋ/| v. |阻碍，妨碍|hindering or impeding the movement or progress of something or someone.|The heavy rain was hampering our progress. 大雨阻碍了我们的前进。|近义词: hindering, obstructing, impeding, restricting|反义词: aiding, assisting, facilitating, helping|verb

hamper:/ˈhæmpər/| n. |有盖的大篮子|a large basket with a lid, often used for carrying food or laundry.|We packed a hamper for the picnic. 我们装了一个野餐篮。|近义词: basket, container|反义词:  (no direct antonym)|noun


词源:
From the Old North French *hanapier, from hanap "drinking cup," of Germanic origin. Originally referred to a case for carrying a drinking cup, later evolving to mean a large basket.  The verb form developed from the noun, with the sense of something holding back or restricting movement, like items packed tightly in a hamper.
