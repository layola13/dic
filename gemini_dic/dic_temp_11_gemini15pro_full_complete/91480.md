hymenal:/haɪˈmiːnəl/ |adj.|处女的，处女膜的|Relating to the hymen, a membrane that may partially cover the opening of the vagina.|The hymenal ring can sometimes be torn during strenuous physical activity. |处女膜环有时会在剧烈运动中撕裂。|近义词：virgin, maiden|反义词：non-virginal|词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *hymenalis, from Latin hymen (“membrane”).
