featherlessnesses:/ˈfɛðərləsnəsɪz/
n.
无羽毛的状态; 没有羽毛
The state or condition of being without feathers.
The featherlessnesses of the baby birds made them vulnerable to the cold. 这些雏鸟没有羽毛，很容易受凉。
n.
无羽毛; 光秃秃
Lack of feathers; baldness.
The featherlessnesses of the plucked chickens were quite shocking.  拔了毛的鸡光秃秃的样子很令人震惊。


近义词:baldness, hairlessness, nakedness
反义词:featheriness, plumage

词性:noun
