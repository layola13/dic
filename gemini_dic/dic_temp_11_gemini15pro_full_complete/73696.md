forgives:/fərˈɡɪvz/ /fɔːrˈɡɪvz/
verb
原谅，饶恕
Grants pardon for (an offense); absolves.
She forgives him for his mistake. 她原谅了他的错误。
He forgave her for lying. 他原谅了她撒谎。
pardon, excuse, absolve
condemn, blame
