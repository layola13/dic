fireplaces:/ˈfaɪərpleɪsɪz/
n.
壁炉（复数）
a structure of brick or stone built into a wall to contain a fire, especially for heating a room.
The family gathered around the fireplace on Christmas Eve.  平安夜，家人们围坐在壁炉旁。
hearths, stoves
