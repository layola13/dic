nonconcerns:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɜːrnz/
noun
不关心的事情；无关紧要的事
Things that are not considered important or worrisome.
例句：He dismissed the rumors as nonconcerns. 他把这些谣言斥为无关紧要的事。

例句：Financial matters are nonconcerns for her; she inherited a fortune.  经济问题对她来说无关紧要；她继承了一大笔财产。

近义词：trivialities, irrelevancies, unimportant matters
反义词：concerns, worries, important issues
词性:n
