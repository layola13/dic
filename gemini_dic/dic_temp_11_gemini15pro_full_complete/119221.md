miaowed:/miˈaʊd/| v.|喵呜叫，学猫叫|The child miaowed, pretending to be a cat. 孩子喵喵叫，假装自己是只猫。|近义词：meowed, purred, caterwauled|反义词：(None -  lack of sound is the opposite, but there isn't a single word antonym)|verb
Etymology:  Onomatopoeic, mimicking the sound a cat makes.  Likely related to "meow."|
