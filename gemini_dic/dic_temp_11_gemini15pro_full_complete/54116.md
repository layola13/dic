disenable:/dɪsɪˈneɪbl/
verb
使残废，使丧失能力，禁用
to make someone unable to use a part of their body properly; to stop a device or system from working
The accident disenabled him from walking.  这场事故使他无法行走。
The administrator disenabled the user's account. 管理员禁用了该用户的帐户。
近义词: disable, incapacitate, cripple
反义词: enable, empower
词性: verb
