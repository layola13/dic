damning:/ˈdæmɪŋ/| adj.|定罪的， condemnatory; 非常不利的，incriminating|  (of a circumstance or piece of evidence) suggesting very strongly that someone is guilty of a crime or has made a serious mistake. | The report contained damning evidence of police corruption. 这份报告包含了警方腐败的确凿证据。| synonyms: incriminating, condemnatory, convicting, accusatory, inculpatory, implicating | antonyms: exonerating, vindicating, absolving | adjective

damning:/ˈdæmɪŋ/| adj.|极有害的，harmful| extremely harmful.  |  His silence was damning. 他的沉默极具杀伤力。 | synonyms: harmful, damaging, detrimental, destructive, ruinous, injurious | antonyms: beneficial, helpful, advantageous | adjective
