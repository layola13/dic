evangelically:/ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪkli/ | adv. | 福音地，热诚地 | In a manner resembling or characteristic of the Christian gospel, especially in being zealous or fervent.  |  He spoke evangelically about the need for social justice. 他热情地谈到社会正义的必要性。 |  She evangelically promoted her new business venture. 她热情地推销她的新企业。 | 近义词:zealously, fervently, passionately, ardently | 反义词: apathetically, indifferently, halfheartedly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *evangelicus, from Greek εὐαγγελικός (euangelikos, “of good news, of the Gospel”), from εὐάγγελος (euangelos, “messenger of good news”).
