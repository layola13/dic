as-well-as:/æz ˈwɛl æz/ | conj. | 也；以及；和...一样好 |  Used to add another item to a list, often implying that the added item is less important. It can also mean "and also" or "in addition to." | She plays the piano as well as the violin. 她会弹钢琴，也会拉小提琴。| Synonyms: and, also, too, in addition to | Antonyms: excluding, except, but not | conjunction

as-well-as:/æz ˈwɛl æz/ | prep. | 除...之外（还）；和...一样好 | Used to introduce a further item, usually one that is less important than the items already mentioned. It can also indicate equality in quality or skill. | He’s a good singer as well as a talented actor. 他是一位优秀的歌手，也是一位才华横溢的演员。 | Synonyms: in addition to, besides, alongside | Antonyms: excluding, except, instead of | preposition
