frankia:/ˈfræŋkiə/| n. |弗兰克氏菌属|A genus of nitrogen-fixing bacteria that live in symbiosis with certain non-leguminous plants.|Frankia bacteria form nodules on the roots of host plants, where they convert atmospheric nitrogen into a form usable by the plant.|Alder trees often have root nodules containing Frankia. 灌木的根瘤通常含有弗兰克氏菌。|Frankia alni infects alder roots and fixes nitrogen.  弗兰克氏菌感染桤木根并固定氮。|近义词:actinomycete (放线菌 - a broader category)|反义词: None (no direct antonym for a genus of bacteria)|词性: noun
