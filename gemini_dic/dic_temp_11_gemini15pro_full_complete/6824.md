anasarca:/ænəˈsɑːrkə/
noun
全身水肿，广泛性水肿
Generalized edema, characterized by widespread swelling of the skin due to effusion of fluid into the extracellular space.
The patient presented with severe anasarca due to congestive heart failure.  这位病人因充血性心力衰竭而出现严重全身水肿。
Anasarca can be a sign of kidney failure. 全身水肿可能是肾衰竭的征兆。
synonyms: edema, dropsy, swelling
antonyms: dehydration
