layperson:/ˈleɪpɜːrsn/ (US) /ˈleɪpɜːsən/ (UK)| n. |外行，非专业人士|A person without professional or specialized knowledge in a particular subject.|The doctor explained the diagnosis in terms a layperson could understand. 医生用外行能理解的术语解释了诊断结果。|synonyms: amateur, nonprofessional, nonspecialist|antonyms: expert, professional, specialist|noun

layman:/ˈleɪmən/ (US) /ˈleɪmən/ (UK)| n. |外行，非专业人士（尤指非神职人员）|A person without professional or specialized knowledge in a particular subject, especially someone who is not a member of the clergy.|He explained the complex theological concept to the layman. 他向这位非神职人员解释了复杂的神学概念。|synonyms: amateur, nonprofessional, nonspecialist|antonyms: expert, professional, specialist|noun
