franciscans:/frænˈsɪskənz/
n.
方济各会修士；方济各会的修女
Members of the Roman Catholic Order of Friars Minor, founded by St. Francis of Assisi in 1209.
The Franciscans are known for their vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience. 方济各会修士以其贫穷、贞洁和服从的誓言而闻名。
The Franciscans established missions throughout the world. 方济各会士在世界各地建立了传教站。
近义词:Friars Minor, Franciscan monks, Franciscan nuns
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider contrasting religious orders, e.g., Dominicans, Jesuits)
词性:noun
