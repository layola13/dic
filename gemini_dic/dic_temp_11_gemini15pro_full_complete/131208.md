nonbusily:/nɒnˈbɪzɪli/
adverb
不忙碌地，悠闲地
Not busily; in a leisurely or relaxed manner.
She strolled nonbusily through the park, enjoying the sunshine. 她悠闲地漫步在公园里，享受着阳光。
近义词:leisurely, calmly, quietly, peacefully, relaxedly
反义词:busily, actively, energetically, frantically, hurriedly
词性:adverb

词源:
"Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "busily" (adverb form of "busy")
