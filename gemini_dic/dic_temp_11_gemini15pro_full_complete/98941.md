inter-campus:/ˌɪntərˈkæmpəs/| adj.|校际的，校园间的|Existing or occurring between two or more campuses of a university or college.|例句：The inter-campus bus service is very convenient. 校际巴士服务非常便捷。|例句：There is an inter-campus rivalry between the two universities. 这两所大学之间存在着校际竞争。|近义词：intercollegiate, interuniversity|反义词：intra-campus|词性:形容词

词源:
"inter-" (prefix): from Latin, meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally."
"campus": from Latin, meaning "field," "plain."
