depleted:/dɪˈpliːtɪd/
adjective
耗尽的，枯竭的
reduced in quantity or quality; emptied
The soil was depleted of nutrients. 土壤中的营养物质已耗尽。
近义词:exhausted, drained, used up
反义词:full, replenished, abundant
词性:adjective


deplete:/dɪˈpliːt/
verb
耗尽，使枯竭
reduce the amount of something
The long drought depleted the water supply. 长时间的干旱耗尽了水资源。
近义词:exhaust, drain, empty
反义词:replenish, refill, restore
词性:verb

词源: From Late Latin *depletus, past participle of deplere "to empty out," from de- "entirely" + plere "to fill"
