browed:/braʊd/
| adj. | 阴沉的；愁眉苦脸的 | Having a gloomy or sullen expression. | The browed old man sat silently in the corner.  那位愁眉苦脸的老人默默地坐在角落里。|近义词：frowning, scowling | 反义词：smiling, cheerful | 词性：形容词
