luxuriated:/lʌɡˈʒʊərieɪtɪd/
verb
沉溺；尽情享受；纵情
indulged in luxury or pleasure, especially to an excessive degree.
She luxuriated in a long, hot bath. 她尽情享受着长时间的热水浴。
synonyms: indulged, revelled, basked, wallowed
antonyms: abstained, refrained, denied oneself
词性: 动词
