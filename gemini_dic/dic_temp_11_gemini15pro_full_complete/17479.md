bearers:/ˈberərz/| n.| 持有者，载体，抬棺者 |People who hold or carry something, or those who carry a coffin at a funeral. |The pallbearers slowly carried the coffin to the graveside. 抬棺者缓缓地将棺材抬到墓地.|Synonyms: holders, carriers, porters |Antonyms: receivers, takers| noun

bearers:/ˈberərz/| n.| 股票持有人，债券持有人 |People who own stocks or bonds. |The company informed its bond bearers of the upcoming interest payment. 公司通知其债券持有人即将支付的利息。|Synonyms: stockholders, bondholders, investors |Antonyms: issuers| noun
