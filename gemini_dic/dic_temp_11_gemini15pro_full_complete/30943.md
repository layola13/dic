chairperson:/ˈtʃɛrpɜːrsən/ (US) /ˈtʃɛːpɜːsən/ (UK)| n. |主席，主持人|A person who presides over a meeting, committee, board, etc., especially one who is neutral and does not vote except to break a tie.  This term is often used as a gender-neutral alternative to "chairman" or "chairwoman".|The chairperson called the meeting to order. 主席宣布开会。|Synonyms: chair, president, moderator, facilitator|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps participant, member)|noun
