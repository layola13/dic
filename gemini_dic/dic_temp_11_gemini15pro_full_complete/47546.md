deathtrap: /ˈdɛθˌtræp/ | n. | 死亡陷阱 | A place, structure, or situation that is very dangerous and likely to cause death. |  The old bridge is a deathtrap and should be closed. 那座旧桥是一个死亡陷阱，应该关闭。

The faulty wiring in the house made it a deathtrap. 房子里错误的线路使它成了一个死亡陷阱。 | 近义词: hazard, peril, danger | 反义词: haven, sanctuary | 词性: 名词
