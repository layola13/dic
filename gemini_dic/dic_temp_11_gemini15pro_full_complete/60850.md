electrotypic:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈtɪpɪk/
adjective
电铸的，电版的
Relating to or made by electrotyping.
The museum has a collection of electrotypic reproductions of ancient coins.  博物馆收藏了古代硬币的电铸复制品。
The printing plates were made using an electrotypic process.  印刷版是用电铸工艺制作的。
近义词:galvanoplastic
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly handcrafted, original)
词性:adjective


electrotype:/ɪˈlɛktrətaɪp/
noun
电版，电铸版
A metal printing plate made by electrotyping.
The printer used an electrotype to produce high-quality images. 印刷商使用电版来制作高质量的图像。
The book was printed from electrotypes. 这本书是用电版印刷的。
近义词:replica, duplicate, copy
反义词:original
词性:noun


electrotype:/ɪˈlɛktrətaɪp/
verb
用电铸法复制
To make a copy of something by electrotyping.
The company electrotyped the original printing plate to create several copies.  该公司用电铸法复制了原始印刷版以创建多个副本。
The sculptor electrotyped his bronze statue. 雕塑家用电铸法复制了他的青铜雕像。
近义词:duplicate, reproduce, copy
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From electro- (electricity) + -type (type, form, image)
