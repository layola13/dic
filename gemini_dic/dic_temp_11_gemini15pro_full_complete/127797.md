neighbourly:/ˈneɪbərli/| adj.|友好的，睦邻的，邻里间的|Friendly and helpful, especially towards people who live near you.|例句：She was always very neighbourly, offering help whenever she could. 她总是很友善，随时提供帮助。|例句：It's important to be neighbourly and get along with the people who live around you.  与周围的人和睦相处很重要。|近义词：friendly, amiable, amicable, cordial, kind|反义词：unfriendly, hostile, unsociable|词性:形容词

neighbourly:/ˈneɪbərli/| adv.|友善地，像邻居一样|In a friendly and helpful way, as a neighbour might.|例句：They chatted neighbourly over the fence. 他们隔着栅栏亲切地聊天。|例句：She acted very neighbourly, bringing over a casserole when I was sick.  我生病时，她表现得非常友善，送来了一份砂锅菜。|近义词：amiably, amicably, cordially, kindly|反义词：unfriendlily, hostilely, unsociably|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *neghborlich, equivalent to neighbour +‎ -ly.
