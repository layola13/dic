disburse:/dɪsˈbɜːrs/| v. |支付，支出，发放|To pay out (money) from a fund.|The company disbursed bonuses to its employees. 公司向员工发放了奖金。|近义词：pay out, distribute, expend|反义词：receive, collect|verb

disbursement:/dɪsˈbɜːrsmənt/| n. |支付，支出，支出款|The act of paying out money, especially from a fund.|The disbursement of funds was delayed due to the holiday.  由于假期，资金发放被推迟了。|近义词：payment, expenditure, outlay|反义词：receipt, income|noun
