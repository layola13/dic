malpighian: /mælˈpɪɡiən/ | adj. | 马尔皮基氏的；马氏管的 | Relating to or discovered by Marcello Malpighi, an Italian anatomist and physician, or denoting the excretory system of insects and other arthropods, consisting of fine tubules extending from the gut. |  The malpighian tubules are responsible for excreting metabolic waste in insects. 马氏管负责昆虫体内代谢废物的排泄。 |  Malpighian corpuscles are found in the kidneys of vertebrates. 马尔皮基小体存在于脊椎动物的肾脏中。| 近义词: excretory (in function) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective
