coordinated:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
协调的，配合的
arranged or organized in a way that works well together; operating in harmony
例句：The dancers' movements were perfectly coordinated.  舞者们的动作配合得天衣无缝。

例句：She coordinated the work of several different committees. 她协调了几个不同委员会的工作。

近义词：harmonized, synchronized, integrated, aligned
反义词：uncoordinated, disorganized, chaotic
词性: adjective


coordinate:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/
verb
协调，配合
to organize or arrange something so that it works well with something else
例句：We need to coordinate our efforts to achieve our goals. 我们需要协调我们的努力来实现我们的目标。

例句：He coordinated the conference call between the two offices. 他协调了两个办公室之间的电话会议。


近义词：organize, arrange, harmonize, synchronize
反义词：disorganize
词性: verb


coordinate:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnət/
noun
坐标
one of a set of numbers or letters used to show the exact position of something, especially on a map or graph
例句：The coordinates of the target were transmitted to the missile. 目标的坐标被传送到了导弹上。

例句：Please provide the GPS coordinates of your location. 请提供你所在位置的GPS坐标。

近义词：position, location
词性: noun
