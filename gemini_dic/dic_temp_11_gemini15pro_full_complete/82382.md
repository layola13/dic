grub:/ɡrʌb/ | n. | 幼虫；蛆；食物；辛苦的工作 | a soft, white, worm-shaped larva of an insect, especially a beetle. Also, informal terms for food. And hard work, especially manual labor. |  The birds were digging in the ground for grubs. 鸟儿正在地上挖幼虫。| synonyms: larva, maggot, food, victuals, chow, nosh | antonyms: gourmet food, delicacy | noun

grub:/ɡrʌb/ | v. | 挖；翻找；搜寻 | to dig or poke around in the ground, especially for food. Also, to search or rummage for something. |  He grubbed around in the dirt for worms. 他在泥土里翻找虫子。 | synonyms: dig, forage, rummage, search, hunt | antonyms: plant, bury, ignore | verb
