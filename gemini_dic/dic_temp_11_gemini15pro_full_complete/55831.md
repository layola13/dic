dividendless:/ˈdɪvɪdendˌlɛs/
adjective
无股息的；不分红的
Not paying dividends; without dividends.
This year, the company's stock was dividendless due to the economic downturn. 今年由于经济低迷，这家公司的股票没有分红。
The investors were disappointed with the dividendless performance of the stock. 投资者对这只股票不分红的表现感到失望。
synonyms: non-dividend-paying, unproductive, unprofitable
antonyms: dividend-paying, profitable, productive
