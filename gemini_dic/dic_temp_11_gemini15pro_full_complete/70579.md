fillies: /ˈfɪliz/
noun
小雌马（复数）
Young female horses, typically less than four years old.
The fillies frolicked in the paddock. 小雌马在围场里嬉戏。
The race was restricted to two-year-old fillies. 这场比赛仅限两岁的小雌马参加。
synonyms: female foals, young mares
antonyms: colts, stallions
