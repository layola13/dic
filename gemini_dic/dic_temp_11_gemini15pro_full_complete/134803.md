nonmigrating:/ˌnɒnˈmaɪɡreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非迁徙的
Not migrating; not moving from one region to another with the change of seasons.
Nonmigrating birds stay in the same area year-round. 非迁徙的鸟类一年四季都待在同一个地区。
resident, sedentary
migratory, nomadic
