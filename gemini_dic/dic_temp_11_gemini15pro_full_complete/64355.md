erastianism:/ɪˌræstiənɪzəm/
n.
伊拉斯提主义，国家统制教会主义
The doctrine that the state should have supremacy over the church in ecclesiastical matters.
The king's insistence on controlling the church was seen as Erastianism. 国王坚持控制教会被视为伊拉斯提主义。
Erastian policies were adopted by the government. 政府采取了伊拉斯提主义政策。
近义词:secularism, state control of the church
反义词:clericalism, theocracy
词性:noun
