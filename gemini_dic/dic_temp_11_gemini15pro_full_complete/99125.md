intercellular:/ˌɪntərˈsɛljʊlər/ | adj. | 细胞间的，细胞与细胞之间的 | Located or occurring between cells. | Example 1: Intercellular communication is crucial for the proper functioning of tissues.  细胞间通讯对于组织的正常运作至关重要。
