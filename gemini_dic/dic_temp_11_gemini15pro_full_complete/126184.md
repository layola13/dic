muzziest:/ˈmʌziɪst/ | adj. | 最模糊的，最困惑的 |  Superlative form of muzzy: most muzzy.  Meaning extremely confused, bewildered, or mentally unclear. | The explanation was so muzzy, it left me feeling the muzziest I'd been all day.  这解释太含糊了，让我感觉一整天都晕头转向。 | Synonyms: bewildered, confused, disoriented, foggy, hazy | Antonyms: clear, lucid, coherent, focused | adjective
