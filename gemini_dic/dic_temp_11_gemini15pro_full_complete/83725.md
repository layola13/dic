haidingerite:/ˈhaɪdɪŋəriːt/
n.
海丁格石(一种矿物)
A hydrous arsenic acid calcium mineral, Ca(AsO₃OH)·H₂O, occurring as small colorless to white crystals.  It is a secondary mineral formed by the oxidation of arsenide minerals.
Haidingerite is often found alongside other arsenic minerals. 海丁格石常与其他砷矿物一起被发现。
Haidingerite crystals are typically small and prismatic. 海丁格石晶体通常小而呈棱柱状。
synonym: pharmacolite (a similar arsenic mineral)
