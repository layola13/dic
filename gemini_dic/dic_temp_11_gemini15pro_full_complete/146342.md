overfierce: /ˈoʊvərˈfɪrs/ | adj. | 过于凶猛的，过分激烈的 | Excessively fierce; too violent or intense. | The overfierce competition led to several injuries. 过于激烈的竞争导致了几人受伤。|  The general delivered an overfierce rebuke to the soldier. 将军对士兵进行了极其严厉的斥责。 | 近义词:  excessively fierce, too intense, overly aggressive, unduly harsh,  | 反义词: gentle, mild, calm, subdued | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "fierce" (Old French fers, fiers, from Latin ferus "wild, untamed")
