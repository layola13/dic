absolutistically:/ˌæbsəluːˈtɪstɪkli/| adv.|绝对地，专制地|In a manner characterized by absolute power or authority; without any restrictions or limitations.  Holding or expressing the belief in absolutism.|The king ruled absolutistically, brooking no dissent. 国王专制统治，不容许任何异议。|The dictator held power absolutistically. 独裁者绝对地掌握着权力。|Synonym: dictatorially, tyrannically, despotically, autocratically|Antonym: democratically, constitutionally|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From absolute + -ist + -ic + -al + -ly.  "Absolute" traces back to Latin "absolutus," meaning "freed from," past participle of "absolvere," "to free from."
