auras:/ˈɔːrəz/ | n. | 光环，气氛，预兆 |A distinctive atmosphere or quality that seems to surround and be generated by a person, thing, or place. |She had an aura of confidence about her. 她身上散发着自信的光环。| synonyms: atmosphere, ambience, air, quality, feeling, mood, vibe, vibration | antonyms: - | noun

auras:/ˈɔːrəz/ | n. | （疾病发作前的）先兆，预感 |A subjective sensation or motor phenomenon that precedes and marks the onset of a paroxysmal attack, such as an epileptic seizure. |He experienced an aura before the migraine began.  在偏头痛开始前，他感到一阵先兆。| synonyms: premonition, forewarning, portent, sign, omen | antonyms: - | noun
