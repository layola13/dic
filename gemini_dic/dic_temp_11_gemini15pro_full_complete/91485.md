hymenomycetes:/haɪmɪnəˈmaɪsiːtiːz/ | n. | 傘菌綱; 擔子菌綱 |A large and diverse class of fungi characterized by the presence of a hymenium, a fertile layer where spores are produced.  This class includes mushrooms, bracket fungi, puffballs, and other familiar fungi.| The forest floor was covered with a variety of hymenomycetes, including several species of mushrooms. 森林地面上覆盖着各种各样的伞菌，包括几种蘑菇。| synonyms: Agaricomycetes (more modern term), basidiomycetes (broader group) | antonyms: None (scientific classification doesn't typically have antonyms)| noun
