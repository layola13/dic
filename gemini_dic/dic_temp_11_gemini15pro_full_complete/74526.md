fraternises:/ˈfrætərnʌɪzɪz/
verb
亲善； fraternizes (美式拼写)
associates with someone in a friendly way, especially when it is not considered socially acceptable
例句：The soldiers were warned not to fraternise with the enemy. 士兵们被警告不要与敌人 fraternizes。

例句：She was accused of fraternising with the rebels. 她被指控与叛乱分子 fraternizes。
近义词：associate, socialize, mingle
反义词：shun, avoid, ostracize
词性：verb

fraternize:/ˈfrætərnʌɪz/
verb
亲善；友善交往 (美式拼写)
To associate or mingle with others in a friendly way.
例句：The officers were told not to fraternize with the enlisted men. 军官们被告知不要与士兵们 fraternize。

例句：He fraternized with his colleagues after work. 他下班后与同事们 fraternize。
近义词：associate, socialize, mingle
反义词：shun, avoid, ostracize
词性：verb
