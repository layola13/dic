desktop:/ˈdɛskˌtɑːp/ (US) /ˈdɛsktɒp/ (UK)| n. |桌面；台式电脑|A desktop is the top of a desk. It also refers to a personal computer designed to be used at a desk. | I keep my laptop on my desktop. 我的笔记本电脑放在我的桌面上。|Synonyms: work surface, tabletop, computer, PC|Antonyms: floor, laptop, mobile device|noun

desktop:/ˈdɛskˌtɑːp/ (US) /ˈdɛsktɒp/ (UK)| adj. |台式的；桌面的|Relating to a desktop computer. | I prefer a desktop computer to a laptop. 我更喜欢台式电脑而不是笔记本电脑。|Synonyms: desktop-based|Antonyms: laptop, mobile, portable|adjective
