muscovy:/ˈmʌskəvi/| n. |莫斯科大公国；麝香鸭|Muscovy refers to the Grand Duchy of Moscow, a medieval Russian state. It can also refer to the muscovy duck.|The rulers of Muscovy eventually became the Tsars of Russia.  莫斯科大公国的统治者最终成为了俄国沙皇。|The muscovy duck is known for its distinctive red facial wattles. 麝香鸭以其独特的红色面部肉垂而闻名。|近义词：Russia (for the historical state),  (对于历史上的国家而言，近义词是俄罗斯)  musk duck (for the bird) (对于鸟类而言，近义词是麝香鸭)|反义词: N/A|名词
