hdmi:/ˈeɪtʃˌdiːɛmˈaɪ/ (US), /ˈeɪtʃˌdiːɛmˈaɪ/ (UK)| n.|高清晰度多媒体接口|High-Definition Multimedia Interface. hdmi:/ˈeɪtʃˌdiːɛmˈaɪ/ (US), /ˈeɪtʃˌdiːɛmˈaɪ/ (UK)| abbreviation.|高清晰度多媒体接口|High-Definition Multimedia Interface.
