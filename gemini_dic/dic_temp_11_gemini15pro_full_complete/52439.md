die-cast:/ˈdaɪˌkæst/ /ˈdaɪˌkɑːst/
adjective|压铸的；铸死的|Made by pouring molten metal into a mold under high pressure.|The car model is made of die-cast metal. 这辆汽车模型是用压铸金属制成的。|Synonym: cast, molded|Antonym: forged, wrought
noun|压铸件；压铸模|An object made by die-casting; a mold used in die-casting.|The factory produces die-casts for various industries. 这家工厂为各行各业生产压铸件。|Synonym: casting, molding|Antonym: forging


die-casting:/ˈdaɪˌkæstɪŋ/ /ˈdaɪˌkɑːstɪŋ/
noun|压铸；压铸法|The process of making objects by pouring molten metal into a mold under high pressure.|Die-casting is a cost-effective way to produce complex metal parts. 压铸是生产复杂金属零件的一种经济有效的方法。|Synonym: pressure casting|Antonym: forging, sand casting
