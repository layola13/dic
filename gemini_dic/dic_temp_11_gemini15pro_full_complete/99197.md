interclasp: /ˌɪntərˈklæsp/ | v. | 互相紧握，扣住 | To clasp or grasp one another; to link or intertwine. |  The lovers interclasped their hands as they walked along the beach. 恋人们沿着海滩散步时互相紧握着手。

The branches of the two trees interclasped, forming a natural arch. 两棵树的树枝互相交错，形成了一个天然的拱门。 | 近义词: intertwine, interlock, entwine | 反义词: separate, unclasp, disentangle | 词性: 动词
