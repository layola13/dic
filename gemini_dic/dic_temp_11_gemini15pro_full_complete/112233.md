looseness:/ˈluːsnəs/| n.| 松弛，宽松，不精确，不严谨|The looseness of the soil makes it easy to dig. 土壤的松弛使其易于挖掘。|近义词：slackness, laxity, vagueness, imprecision|反义词：tightness, firmness, precision, accuracy|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *losnesse*, equivalent to loose +‎ -ness. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *lausaz.
