historiographical:/ˌhɪstɔːriəˈɡræfɪk(ə)l/
adjective
史学的；史料学的
Of or relating to the writing of history; concerning the writing of history.
例句：Historiographical research is essential for understanding the past. 史学研究对于理解过去至关重要。

例句：The book offers a historiographical analysis of the American Civil War. 这本书提供了对美国内战的史料分析。

近义词：historical, historic
反义词: ahistorical
词性: adjective
