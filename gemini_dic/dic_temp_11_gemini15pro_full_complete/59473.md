easeless:/ˈiːslɪs/| adj.|不停的，不倦的，持续不断的|Characterized by unceasing activity or motion; tireless.|The ceaseless chatter of the children made it difficult to concentrate. 孩子们不停的喋 chatter 使人难以集中精力。|近义词：incessant, constant, continuous, perpetual, unrelenting|反义词：intermittent, sporadic, occasional|词性:形容词

词源:
From ease + -less.  Ease (from Old French aise "comfort, pleasure, opportunity") meaning "freedom from labor or difficulty." The suffix "-less" indicates a lack or absence of something. Thus, "easeless" literally means "without ease" or "without rest."
