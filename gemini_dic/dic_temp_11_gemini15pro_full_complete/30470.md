centrifuging: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒɪŋ/ | n. | 离心 | the process of separating substances of different densities by spinning them rapidly in a machine called a centrifuge | The blood sample was prepared for analysis by centrifuging.  血液样本通过离心进行分析前的准备。| Synonyms: spinning, whirling, rotating | Antonyms: sedimentation, precipitation | noun

centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ | v. | 离心分离 | to separate substances of different densities by spinning them rapidly in a centrifuge |  We centrifuged the mixture to separate the components. 我们对混合物进行离心分离以分离其成分。| Synonyms: spin, whirl, rotate | Antonyms: sediment, precipitate | verb
