/ˌmʌltiˌlætərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 多边化；多边主义化 | The process of involving many different countries or parties in something, especially in international agreements or decisions. |  The multilateralization of trade negotiations has led to more balanced outcomes. 贸易谈判的多边化带来了更平衡的结果。

The multilateralization of aid efforts is essential for addressing global poverty.  援助工作多边化对于解决全球贫困至关重要。| 近义词: internationalization, globalization | 反义词: unilateralism, bilateralism | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From multi- (many) + lateral (sided) + -ization (process of making).  源于 multi-（多） + lateral（边） + -ization（…化过程）。
