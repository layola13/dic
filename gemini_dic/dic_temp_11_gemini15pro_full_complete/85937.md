headlongnesses:/ˈhɛdˌlɒŋnɪsɪz/
noun
鲁莽，轻率；仓促
The state of being headlong; rashness or impetuosity.
His headlongnesses led him into trouble. 他的鲁莽给他带来了麻烦。
Near synonyms: recklessness, rashness, impetuosity, hastiness, precipitateness
Near antonyms: caution, prudence, deliberation, carefulness
