fenian:/ˈfiːniən/| n.|芬尼亚分子（19世纪支持爱尔兰独立的团体成员）|A member of a 19th-century revolutionary nationalist organization among the Irish in the US and Ireland dedicated to achieving Irish independence.|The Fenians planned a series of uprisings against British rule. 芬尼亚分子计划了一系列反抗英国统治的起义。|Synonyms: nationalist, rebel, revolutionary|Antonyms: loyalist, imperialist|noun
