nonstyptic:/ˌnɒnˈstɪptɪk/
adjective
非收敛性的，不收敛的
Not tending to check bleeding; not styptic.
This aftershave is nonstyptic, so it won't sting any cuts you might have.  这款须后水是非收敛性的，所以它不会刺痛你可能有的伤口。
The nonstyptic shaving cream was gentle on his sensitive skin.  这种非收敛性的剃须膏对他的敏感皮肤很温和。
近义词: nonastringent
反义词: styptic, astringent
词性: adjective
