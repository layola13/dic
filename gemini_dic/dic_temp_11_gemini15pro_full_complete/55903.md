The process of separating or disuniting. | They are divorcing after twenty years of marriage. 他们结婚二十年后正在离婚。|  The company is divorcing itself from its tobacco business.  这家公司正在剥离其烟草业务。 | separating, splitting, parting | reconciling, uniting | verb
