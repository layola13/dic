mistranslate:/ˌmɪs.trænsˈleɪt/ | v. | 误译，翻译错 | To translate incorrectly; to give a wrong interpretation of a text from one language to another. |  He mistranslated the sentence, leading to a misunderstanding. 他误译了这个句子，导致了误解。

|  The interpreter mistranslated the diplomat's speech, causing an international incident.  翻译员误译了外交官的讲话，引发了一起国际事件。 | 近义词: misinterpret, misconstrue | 反义词: translate correctly, interpret accurately | 词性: 动词
