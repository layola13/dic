omnipotent:/ɒmˈnɪpətənt/ /ɑːmˈnɪpətənt/| adj.|无所不能的；全能的|Having unlimited power; able to do anything.|God is often described as omnipotent. 上帝常被描述为无所不能的。|例句：An omnipotent narrator knows all the thoughts and feelings of the characters.  一个无所不能的叙述者了解所有角色的想法和感受。|近义词：all-powerful, almighty, supreme|反义词：powerless, impotent, weak|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *omnipotens*, from *omni-* (“all”) + *potens* (“powerful”).
