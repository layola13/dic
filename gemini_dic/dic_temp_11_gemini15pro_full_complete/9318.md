antipole:/ˈæntɪˌpoʊl/ | n. | 对跖点;正相反的事物 |A place on Earth diametrically opposite to another place. Something that is the exact opposite of something else. |例句：The antipole of love is hate. 爱的对跖点是恨。

例句：The North Pole and the South Pole are antipoles. 北极和南极是对跖点。|近义词：opposite, contrary, reverse |反义词：same, identical |词性: 名词
