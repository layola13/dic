apodyterium:/ˌæpədaɪˈtɪəriəm/
n.
更衣室，脱衣室
A room in ancient Greek and Roman bathhouses or gymnasiums where people undressed and dressed.
The athletes met in the apodyterium before the games. 运动员们在比赛前在更衣室集合。
The apodyterium was often decorated with mosaics and frescoes. 更衣室通常用马赛克和壁画装饰。
synonyms: dressing room, locker room, changing room
antonyms: N/A
noun


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἀποδυτήριον (apodutḗrion, “undressing room”), from ἀποδύω (apodúō, “I undress”).
