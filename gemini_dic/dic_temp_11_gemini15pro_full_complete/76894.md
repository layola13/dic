gangwayed:/ˈɡæŋweɪd/
| adj. | 有舷梯的；装有舷梯的 | Equipped with a gangway or gangways; resembling a gangway. | The gangwayed ship was ready to board passengers.  这艘装有舷梯的船准备让乘客登船。 | Example:  The ferry, gangwayed and ready, awaited the arriving passengers.  装有舷梯并准备就绪的渡船等待着到达的乘客。 | Synonyms:  accessible, approachable | Antonyms: inaccessible, unapproachable | word property: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "gangway" + "-ed."  "Gangway" originates from the Old Norse "gangvegr," meaning "going way."
