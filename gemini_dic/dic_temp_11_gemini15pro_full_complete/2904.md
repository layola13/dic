adviceful: /ədˈvaɪsfəl/
adjective
爱出主意的；好为人师的；喜欢提建议的
Inclined to give advice, especially freely and unnecessarily.
He was an adviceful old man, always telling others what to do. 他是一位爱出主意的老人，总是告诉别人该怎么做。
The adviceful neighbor offered unsolicited opinions on everything from gardening to child-rearing.  爱提建议的邻居对从园艺到育儿的所有事情都主动提供意见。
近义词:advisory, meddlesome, officious
反义词:reticent, reserved
词性:adjective
词源:  From "advice" + "-ful" (suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by").
