adjective

Lacking friends, family, and acquaintances; without companions or relations.
The kithless old man lived alone in a small cabin in the woods.  这个无亲无故的老人独自住在树林里的小木屋中。
The kithless child wandered the streets, searching for food and shelter.  这个孤苦伶仃的孩子在街上游荡，寻找食物和住所。
近义词:friendless, lonely, isolated, forlorn
反义词:close-knit, sociable, gregarious
词性:adjective
词源:From Old English *cyththe "kinship, native land" + -less.
