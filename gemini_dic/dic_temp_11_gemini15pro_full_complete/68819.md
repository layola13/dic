fascinates:/ˈfæsɪneɪts/
verb
使着迷，深深吸引
to attract or interest somebody very much
Her beauty fascinates me. 她的美深深地吸引着我。
Synonyms:captivates, enthralls, charms, enchants
Antonyms:bores, repels, disgusts
词性:动词(第三人称单数)
