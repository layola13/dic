mongrelized: /ˈmʌŋɡrɪlaɪzd/ | adj. | 混杂的，杂种的 |  Mixed or crossbred, especially of different breeds of animals; having diverse elements or origins.  Derived from "mongrel," meaning a dog of mixed breed.  Often used figuratively to describe something impure or corrupted by the mixture of different elements. | The neighborhood was becoming increasingly mongrelized, with people from all different backgrounds moving in. 这个社区变得越来越混杂，来自不同背景的人都搬了进来。 |  The architect described the building's style as a mongrelized version of neoclassical and art deco.  建筑师将建筑的风格描述为新古典主义和装饰艺术的混合版本。 | Synonyms: hybrid, mixed, crossbred, composite, heterogeneous | Antonyms: purebred, pure, homogeneous, unmixed | adjective
