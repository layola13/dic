laying-back:/ˈleɪɪŋ bæk/| adj.| 悠闲的，放松的，随和的|Relaxed and easy-going; not worrying about anything.|He has a very laying-back attitude towards life. 他对生活有一种非常悠闲的态度。|近义词：relaxed, easygoing, chill, laid-back|反义词：stressed, anxious, uptight|词性:形容词
词源: laying-back 是由动词短语 lay back 变形而来。Lay back 的意思是向后躺，引申为放松，不操心。
