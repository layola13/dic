indigeneity:/ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛnɪɪti/ | n. |土著性；原住民身份；本土性|The state of being indigenous; indigenousness.  The quality of originating or occurring naturally in a particular place or region.|例句：The government recognized the indigeneity of the local tribes. 政府承认了当地部落的土著身份。

例句：The artwork reflects the indigeneity of the region. 这件艺术品反映了该地区的本土特色。|近义词：indigenousness, aboriginality, nativeness |反义词: foreignness, alienness |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From indigenous + -ity.  Indigenous comes from Late Latin *indigenus, from Latin indigena "sprung from the land, native," from indu (old form of in) "in" + gignere "to beget."
