armour-plating:/ˈɑːmə(r) ˌpleɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɑːrmər ˌpleɪtɪŋ/ (US)| n.|装甲，装甲板，护甲|A protective layer of metal plates covering a vehicle or structure.|The tank's armour-plating protected it from enemy fire. 坦克的装甲保护它免受敌人的炮火。|Synonyms: armor plating, armor, shielding|Antonyms: vulnerability, exposure|Noun

armour-plate:/ˈɑːmə(r) pleɪt/ (UK) /ˈɑːrmər pleɪt/ (US)| vt.| 装上装甲|To cover or equip with armor plating.|They armour-plated the ship to protect it from attack. 他们给船装上装甲以防袭击。|Synonyms: armor, shield, protect|Antonyms: expose, weaken|Verb
