障碍赛跑者
People who participate in obstacle races, which involve running a course while overcoming various physical challenges.
Obstacle-racers need both strength and endurance. 障碍赛跑者需要力量和耐力。
Obstacle-racers train hard to prepare for the challenging courses. 障碍赛跑者刻苦训练以应对挑战性的赛道。
