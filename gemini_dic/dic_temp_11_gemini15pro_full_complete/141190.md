oolong:/ˈuːlɒŋ/ (UK) /ˈuːlɔːŋ/ (US)| n.|乌龙茶|A type of Chinese tea that has been partially oxidized.|I prefer oolong tea to green tea. 我更喜欢乌龙茶而不是绿茶。|Synonyms:  semi-oxidized tea|Antonyms:  green tea, black tea|noun
