ferals:/ˈfɛrəlz/ /ˈfɪrəlz/| n.|野化的动物（尤指家畜）; 野孩子; 流浪儿 |Animals, especially domestic animals, that have reverted to a wild state;  children growing up without proper care or control; street children. |例句：The island is populated by feral cats.  这个岛上生活着野猫群。

例句：Feral children are often unable to speak properly.  野孩子通常无法正常说话。

|近义词：wild, untamed, stray, homeless|反义词:domesticated, tame, trained|词性:名词
