fuze:/fjuːz/| n.|引信;导火线|A device that detonates an explosive charge, typically by means of electricity or impact.|The bomb's fuze malfunctioned, preventing it from detonating. 炸弹的引信发生故障，阻止了它爆炸。|Synonym: detonator, primer|Antonym: safety device|noun

fuzes:/fjuːzɪz/| n.|引信;导火线 (fuze的复数形式)|Plural form of fuze, referring to multiple devices that detonate explosive charges.|The soldiers carefully checked the fuzes on all the grenades. 士兵们仔细检查了所有手榴弹的引信。|Synonym: detonators, primers|Antonym: safety devices|noun
