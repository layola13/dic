antiegalitarian:/ˌæntiɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/
| adj. n.| 反平等主义的；反平等主义者 | Opposed to or hostile towards the principle of equality for all people, especially in political, social, or economic life. |  The antiegalitarian policies widened the gap between the rich and the poor. 这些反平等主义的政策加剧了贫富差距。|  The blogger wrote an antiegalitarian screed against progressive taxation.  这位博主写了一篇反对累进税制的反平等主义的檄文。 | 近义词: elitist, hierarchical, undemocratic | 反义词: egalitarian, democratic, equitable | 词性: adjective, noun
