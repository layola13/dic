esperanto:/ˌɛspəˈrɑːntoʊ/ | n. | 世界语 | An artificial international language, based on words common to the major European languages, and designed to be easy to learn. |  Example: She learned Esperanto hoping to communicate with people from different countries. 她学习世界语希望可以和来自不同国家的人交流。 | Example:  Many books have been translated into Esperanto. 许多书籍已经被翻译成世界语。 | Synonym: constructed language, planned language | Antonym: natural language | noun
