genotype:/ˈdʒiːnəˌtaɪp/ | n. |基因型 |The genetic makeup of an organism or group of organisms with reference to a single trait, set of traits, or an entire complex of traits.|Scientists compared the genotypes of the two species. 科学家比较了这两个物种的基因型。| The genotype of the plant determines its phenotype.  这种植物的基因型决定了它的表型。 |synonyms: genome, genetic constitution| antonyms: phenotype | noun
