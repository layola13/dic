outshow:/ˈaʊtʃoʊ/
verb
胜过，超越，比…更出色
to surpass or exceed in performance or appearance; to outdo
The new model outshows all its competitors in terms of fuel efficiency.  这款新车型在燃油效率方面胜过所有竞争对手。
verb
使…逊色，使…相形见绌
to cause to appear inferior by comparison
Her performance outshowed everyone else's. 她的表演使其他所有人相形见绌。


近义词: surpass, exceed, outdo, eclipse, overshadow
反义词:  underperform, fall short of, pale in comparison to
词性: verb
