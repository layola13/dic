de-stress:/ˌdiːˈstrɛs/ /ˌdiːˈstres/
verb
中文翻译：缓解压力，减轻压力，放松
英文解析：To relieve or reduce stress; to relax.  The prefix "de-" signifies removal or reversal, combined with "stress" meaning psychological tension.  Thus, "de-stress" means to remove or reduce stress.
例句：I like to de-stress by listening to music. 我喜欢听音乐来放松。

例句：She took a yoga class to de-stress after a long day at work.  在下班后她上了一节瑜伽课来缓解压力。

近义词：relax, unwind, chill out, calm down
反义词：stress, tense up, worry
词性：verb

de-stress:/ˌdiːˈstrɛs/ /ˌdiːˈstres/
noun
中文翻译：减压，放松
英文解析：The act of relieving or reducing stress; relaxation.  Used as a noun, it refers to the process or action of de-stressing.
例句：Yoga is a great form of de-stress. 瑜伽是一种很好的放松方式。

例句：Taking a vacation is a popular method of de-stress. 度假是种很受欢迎的减压方式。

近义词：relaxation, unwinding
反义词：stress, tension
词性：noun
