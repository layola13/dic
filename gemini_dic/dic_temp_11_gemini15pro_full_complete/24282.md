brigandage:/ˈbrɪɡəndɪdʒ/| n.|土匪行为；抢劫；盗匪活动|The act of robbing people, especially in the countryside, typically by an organized gang.| Travelers feared brigandage in the remote mountain passes.  旅行者害怕在偏远的山路上遇到土匪抢劫。| The area was notorious for its brigandage.  这个地区因土匪活动而臭名昭著。 |Synonyms: banditry, highway robbery, plundering, looting, pillage|Antonyms: lawfulness, order, security|noun
