nonethnologic:/ˌnɒnˌɛθnəˈlɒdʒɪk/
adjective
非民族学的
Not related to ethnology; not pertaining to the study of the characteristics of various peoples and the differences and relationships between them.
The museum's new exhibit features nonethnologic artifacts, focusing instead on ancient tools and weaponry.  博物馆的新展览以非民族学文物为特色，重点展出古代工具和武器。
The conference's theme was surprisingly nonethnologic, concentrating on universal human experiences rather than cultural variations.  会议的主题令人惊讶地是非民族学的，专注于普遍的人类经验而不是文化差异。
Near Antonyms: ethnological, anthropological
Antonyms:  (It's difficult to have a direct antonym for "nonethnologic."  Perhaps) culturally specific, ethnically focused
词性: adjective
