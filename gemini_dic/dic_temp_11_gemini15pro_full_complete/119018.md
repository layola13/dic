methodization:/ˌmɛθədaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 方法化；系统化 | The act of reducing something to method; systematic arrangement or management. | The methodization of the company's workflow increased efficiency.  公司工作流程的系统化提高了效率。 | 近义词: systematization, organization, arrangement | 反义词: disorder, chaos | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From method + -ization.  "Method" comes from the Late Latin methodus, from Greek methodos "way of teaching or going," from meta "by way of" + hodos "way, road." "-ization" is a suffix forming nouns of action or process.
