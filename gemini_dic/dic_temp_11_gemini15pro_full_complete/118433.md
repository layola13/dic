mesa's:/ˈmeɪsəz/
noun
台地，平顶山；(尤指美国西南部)
a flat-topped hill with steep sides, especially in the southwest US
The mesa's edge offered a breathtaking view of the valley.  台地的边缘提供了山谷的壮丽景色。
plateaus, tablelands
hills, valleys
