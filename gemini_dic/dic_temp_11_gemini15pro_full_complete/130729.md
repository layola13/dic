nonalphabetic:/ˌnɒnˌælfəˈbɛtɪk/ | adj. | 非字母的 | Not composed of letters; containing characters or symbols other than letters. | This password must contain at least one nonalphabetic character.  这个密码必须至少包含一个非字母字符。 |  The system only accepts nonalphabetic input for this field. 该系统此字段仅接受非字母输入。 |  近义词: non-letter, symbolic | 反义词: alphabetic, literal | 词性: adjective

词源:  The word "nonalphabetic" is derived from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and "alphabetic" (relating to the alphabet).  "Alphabetic" itself comes from "alphabet," which ultimately derives from the names of the first two letters of the Greek alphabet, "alpha" and "beta."
