hoveled:/ˈhʌvəld/ /ˈhɑːvəld/| v.|居住在简陋的棚屋里；挤在一起|To have lived or stayed in a hovel; to have crowded together.|They hoveled in a small, damp cabin during the storm.  他们在暴风雨期间挤在一个潮湿的小木屋里。|The refugees hoveled together for warmth.  难民们挤在一起取暖。|近义词：sheltered, huddled, crowded|反义词: dispersed, separated|词性:动词

hovel:/ˈhʌvəl/ /ˈhɑːvəl/| n.|简陋的棚屋；肮脏的小屋|A small, mean dwelling; a wretched hut.|The family lived in a hovel with a dirt floor. 这家人住在一间泥土地板的棚屋里。|The dilapidated building was little more than a hovel. 这栋破旧的建筑只不过是一个棚屋。|近义词：hut, shack, shanty|反义词: mansion, palace|词性:名词
