fog:/fɒɡ/ (British) /fɑːɡ/ (American)| n. vi. vt.|雾，迷雾；模糊，朦胧；使模糊，使困惑|A thick cloud of tiny water droplets suspended in the air at or near the earth's surface that obscures or restricts visibility.|The heavy fog made driving dangerous. 浓雾使驾驶变得危险。|近义词：mist, haze, smog|反义词: clarity, clearness|noun

fog:/fɒɡ/ (British) /fɑːɡ/ (American)| vi. vt.|（使）蒙上雾；（使）模糊|To become or cause to become obscured by or as if by fog.|The windows fogged up. 窗户上起了雾。|近义词：mist, cloud, obscure|反义词: clear, clarify|verb
