galvanization:/ˌɡælvənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 镀锌；激励；通电 | The process of coating iron or steel with zinc to protect it from rust. The act of stimulating or exciting someone or something. |  The bridge underwent galvanization to prevent corrosion. 这座桥进行了镀锌处理以防止腐蚀。 | synonyms: coating, plating, energizing, stimulating | antonyms:  corrosion, rusting | noun

galvanize:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/ | vt. | 镀锌；激励；通电 | To coat (iron or steel) with zinc. To stimulate or excite someone or something. | The news galvanized them into action.  这消息激励他们行动起来。The steel beams were galvanized to protect them from rust. 钢梁进行了镀锌以防止生锈。 | synonyms: coat, plate, energize, stimulate | antonyms:  weaken, discourage | verb
