come-under:/kʌm ˈʌndər/ | v. | 归入；属于；受到；遭受；被列入 |  to be included in a particular group or category; to be subjected to something, especially something unpleasant | The issue comes under the heading of "staff welfare".  这个问题属于“员工福利”范畴。 | synonyms: fall under, be classified as, be subject to | antonyms: be excluded from, be exempt from | verb

come-under:/kʌm ˈʌndər/ | v. | 受到（攻击、批评等）| to be subjected to (attack, criticism, etc.) | The government has come under fire for its handling of the economy. 政府因其处理经济的方式而受到抨击。 | synonyms: be attacked, be criticized, be under fire | antonyms: be praised, be supported | verb
