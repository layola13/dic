herbarium:/hɜːrˈbeəriəm/ /ərˈbɛriəm/| n. |植物标本馆，腊叶标本集|A collection of dried plants systematically arranged and classified for study.|例句：The university's herbarium contains over 200,000 specimens. 这所大学的植物标本馆藏有超过20万个标本。

例句：She donated her extensive collection of pressed flowers to the local herbarium. 她把她大量的压花收藏捐赠给了当地的植物标本馆。|近义词：hortus siccus|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
