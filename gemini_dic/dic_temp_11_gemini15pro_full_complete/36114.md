coercionary:/koʊˈɜːrʃəneri/
adjective
强制的，胁迫的
Relating to or involving coercion; using force or threats to persuade someone to do something.
The government imposed coercionary measures to quell the protests. 政府采取强制措施平息抗议。
The workers felt the new policies were coercionary and unfair. 工人们觉得新政策具有胁迫性，而且不公平。
synonyms:compulsory, forced, obligatory, mandatory
antonyms:voluntary, optional, persuasive
