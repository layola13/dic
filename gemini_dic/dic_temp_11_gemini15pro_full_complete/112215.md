loopy:/ˈluːpi/
adjective
古怪的；愚蠢的；疯癫的
Silly, eccentric, or irrational.
I felt a bit loopy after staying up all night. 我整晚没睡，感觉有点晕乎乎的。
Synonyms: silly, eccentric, crazy, daft, wacky
Antonyms: sensible, rational, sane
词性: 形容词

loop:/luːp/
noun
环；圈；回路
A shape produced by a curve that bends round and crosses itself.
Tie a loop in the rope. 在绳子上打个环。
Synonyms: circle, ring, hoop, coil, spiral
Antonyms: line, straight
词性: 名词

loop:/luːp/
verb
使成环；使成圈；循环
To make a loop or loops in something.
Loop the scarf around your neck. 把围巾绕在脖子上。
Synonyms: circle, ring, enclose, surround
Antonyms: unloop, straighten
词性: 动词
