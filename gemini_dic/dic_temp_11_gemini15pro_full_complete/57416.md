drabbed:/dræbd/| adj.|单调乏味的，颜色暗淡的|Dull and uninteresting; lacking brightness or color.  Characterized by or clothed in drab colors.|The drabbed walls of the prison made the inmates feel even more hopeless.  监狱里单调乏味的墙壁让囚犯们感到更加绝望。|Synonyms: dull, dreary, monotonous, somber, lackluster|Antonyms: bright, vibrant, colorful, cheerful|adjective
