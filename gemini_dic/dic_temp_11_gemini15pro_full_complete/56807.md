doric: /ˈdɔːrɪk/ /ˈdɑːrɪk/ | adj. n. | 多立克式的，多利安人，多立克柱式 | Relating to or denoting a classical order of architecture characterized by plain, sturdy columns with simple capitals and no base. Also, the dialect of ancient Greek spoken in Doris. | The temple was built in the Doric style. 这座寺庙是以多立克风格建造的。 |  The Doric dialect was spoken in ancient Greece.  多利安方言在古希腊被使用。 | 近义词: Spartan, austere, simple | 反义词: ornate, Corinthian, Ionic | 词性: adjective, noun

词源: From Latin *Dōricus, from Ancient Greek Δωρικός (Dōrikós), from Δωριεύς (Dōrieús, “Dorian”).
