choanoid:/ˈkoʊənɔɪd/| adj. n.|领鞭毛虫样的，领鞭毛细胞|Relating to or resembling a choanoflagellate, a single-celled aquatic organism having a collar of protoplasm encircling the base of a flagellum.|Choanoid cells are found in sponges and are thought to be related to the ancestors of animals. 海绵中发现了领鞭毛细胞，它们被认为与动物的祖先有关。|近义词：choanoflagellate|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun

choanocyte:/ˈkoʊənəˌsaɪt/| n.|领细胞|A flagellated cell with a collar of protoplasm at the base of the flagellum, found in sponges and other aquatic invertebrates.  It creates water currents and traps food particles.|Choanocytes play a crucial role in filter feeding. 领细胞在滤食中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：collar cell|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


词源: From Greek *khoánē*  "funnel" + -oid.
