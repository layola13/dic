nonmasculinely:/ˌnɒnˌmæsˈkjʊlɪnli/
adverb
非男性化地；不像男人的样子
In a way that is not considered typical of men.
She dressed nonmasculinely, preferring flowing skirts and bright colors. 她穿着不男性化，喜欢飘逸的裙子和鲜艳的颜色。
He spoke nonmasculinely, his voice soft and gentle. 他说话不像男人，声音温柔轻柔。
近义词: effeminately, unmanly
反义词: masculinely, manly
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来源于拉丁语 non
masculine (adjective): from Old French masculin, from Latin masculinus "male, manly," from masculus "male," diminutive of mas "male."
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives.
