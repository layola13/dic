impenetrably:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtrəbli/| adv.|不可穿透地；难以理解地|Not able to be penetrated, pierced, or entered; also, impossible to understand.|The jungle was so dense and impenetrably thick that we had to abandon our trek.|近义词:inaccessibly, imperviously, unfathomably|反义词:permeably, understandably, clearly|词性:副词

impenetrable:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtrəbl/| adj.|不可渗透的；难以理解的|Not able to be penetrated, pierced, or entered; also, impossible to understand.|His expression was impenetrably blank, giving no clue to his thoughts.|近义词:inaccessible, impervious, unfathomable|反义词:permeable, understandable, clear|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impenetrabilis, from Latin in- "not" + penetrabilis "that can be penetrated,"  from penetrare "to penetrate."
