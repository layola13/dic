branchiopoda:/bræŋkiˈɒpədə/| n. |鳃足纲动物|A class of small crustaceans, typically having a flattened, leaf-like body and numerous pairs of appendages used for swimming and respiration. They are found in freshwater and some marine habitats.|例句：Branchiopoda are a diverse group of crustaceans. 鳃足纲动物是甲壳类动物的一个多样化群体。

例句：Fairy shrimps are a well-known type of branchiopoda. 仙女虾是一种著名的鳃足纲动物。|近义词：crustacean, entomostracan|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From the Greek words "branchia" (gills) + "pous" (foot), referring to the leaf-like appendages that serve as both gills and swimming organs.
