counterdistinguish:/ˌkaʊntər.dɪˈstɪŋ.ɡwɪʃ/ | vt. | 反衬; 对比区分; | To distinguish by contrast or opposition. | Example:  He counterdistinguished the two philosophies by highlighting their core differences. 他通过强调其核心差异来对比区分这两种哲学。

Example: The artist counterdistinguished light and shadow to create a dramatic effect.  艺术家通过光影的对比来创造戏剧性的效果。| 近义词: contrast, differentiate, juxtapose | 反义词:  confuse, conflate | 词性: 动词
