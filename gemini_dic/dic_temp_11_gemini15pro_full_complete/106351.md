kokoon:/koʊˈkuːn/| n.|茧; 安乐窝; 舒适的地方|A safe, comfortable, or secluded place. |The child nestled in her mother's arms like a bird in its kokoon. 孩子依偎在母亲的怀里，就像鸟儿在它的安乐窝里一样。|近义词: cocoon, haven, refuge, sanctuary, retreat|反义词: exposure, vulnerability|词性:名词

词源: From Afrikaans "kokon," borrowed from Dutch "cocon," ultimately from French "cocon," from Provençal "coco," meaning "shell," of imitative origin.
