afforesting:/əˈfɔːrɪstɪŋ/
v.
植树造林,绿化
The act of planting trees in an area that previously had none or very few.
例句：Afforesting the barren land will help prevent soil erosion.  绿化荒地将有助于防止水土流失。

例句：The government has committed to afforesting a large area of the country. 政府已承诺在该国大面积地区植树造林。

近义词：reforesting, planting
反义词：deforesting
词性：动词 (gerund or present participle)
