nonaseptically:/nɒnˌeɪˈsɛptɪkli/ | adv. | 非无菌地 | Not aseptically; without following aseptic procedures.  This means performed in a way that is not free from contamination by harmful bacteria, viruses, or other microorganisms. | The wound was treated nonaseptically in the field.  战场上伤口未经无菌处理。| The surgeon, short on time, nonaseptically bandaged the wound. 由于时间紧迫，外科医生对伤口进行了非无菌包扎。 | Synonyms: unsterilely, uncleanly | Antonyms: aseptically, sterilely |词性: 副词
