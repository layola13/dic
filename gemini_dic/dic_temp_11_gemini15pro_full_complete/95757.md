inconspicuously:/ˌɪnkənˈspɪkjuəsli/
adverb
不显眼地，不起眼地
in a way that does not attract attention
He tried to enter the room inconspicuously. 他试图不引人注目地进入房间。
Synonyms: discreetly, subtly, unobtrusively
Antonyms: conspicuously, noticeably, obviously
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + conspicuous (easily seen) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Conspicuous comes from Latin *conspicuus, from conspicere ("to look at attentively"), from con- (together) + specere ("to look").
