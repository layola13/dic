Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
service (noun): the action of helping or doing work for someone. From Old French service, from Latin servitium ‘slavery, servitude’, from servus ‘slave’.
-able (suffix): capable of, susceptible of, fit for, tending to, given to
-ity (suffix): forms nouns denoting quality or condition
