abandonment's:/əˈbændənmənts/
n.
放弃，遗弃（的状态或行为）
The state of being abandoned or deserted.
The child suffered from a sense of abandonment after his parents' divorce.  这个孩子在父母离婚后遭受了被遗弃感。
The abandonment's impact on the local economy was devastating. 这次放弃对当地经济的影响是毁灭性的。
desertion, relinquishment, forsaking
acceptance, adoption, reclamation
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French abandonement, from abandoner "to abandon," from a bandon "at will, at one's disposal," literally "in ban," from ban "power, jurisdiction."
