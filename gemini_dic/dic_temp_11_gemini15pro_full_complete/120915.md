miscasts:/ˌmɪsˈkæsts/
noun
选角错误，角色分配不当
The act of casting an actor in a role for which they are unsuited. The result of such an action.
The play suffered from several miscasts. 这部剧的几个角色选错了人。
近义词: mismatches, wrong casting, inappropriate casting
反义词: perfect casting, appropriate casting
词性:n.


miscast:/ˌmɪsˈkæst/
verb
选错角色，错误地分配角色
To cast an actor in a role for which they are unsuited.
The director miscast the lead role. 导演选错了主角。
近义词: misallocate, wrongly cast
反义词: perfectly cast, appropriately cast
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + cast.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "amiss." "Cast" originates from Old Norse "kasta" meaning "to throw."
