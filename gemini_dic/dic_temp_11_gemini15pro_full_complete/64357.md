erasures:/ɪˈreɪʒərz/ /ɪˈreɪʃərz/
noun
擦除；消除；删除；抹去（erasure 的复数）
The act of erasing something; the removal of writing, recorded material, or data.
The teacher told the students to make no erasures on their test papers. 老师告诉学生不要把试卷上的答案擦掉。
deletions, removals, obliterations
additions, restorations, creations
