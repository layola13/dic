humbucking:/ˈhʌmbʌkɪŋ/
adjective
抵消嗡嗡声的，抗干扰的
Designed to reduce or eliminate hum (electrical interference).
This guitar has humbucking pickups. 这把吉他有抗干扰拾音器。
近义词: noise-canceling, noise-reducing
反义词: single-coil (in the context of guitar pickups)
词性: adjective

词源:  "Humbucking" comes from "hum" (the low, continuous sound of electrical interference) and "buck" (to oppose or resist).  The term describes the pickup's ability to "buck" or counteract the hum.
