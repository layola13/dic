epiloguize: /ɪˈpɪləˌɡaɪz/ /əˈpɪləˌɡaɪz/
verb
作结语；写结语；以结语结束
to add or write an epilogue to something; to conclude something with an epilogue.
The author epiloguized his novel with a reflection on the characters' fates. 作者在他的小说结尾加了一个关于人物命运的反思。
He epiloguized his speech with a call to action. 他以行动号召结束了他的演讲。
近义词: conclude, summarize, finish, end
反义词: begin, commence, start, initiate
词性: verb

epiloguize: /ɪˈpɪləˌɡaɪz/ /əˈpɪləˌɡaɪz/
Etymology: From epilogue + -ize.  Epilogue comes from the Greek epilogos, from epi- "upon, in addition" + logos "speech, word, discourse."
