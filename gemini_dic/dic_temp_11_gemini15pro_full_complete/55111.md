disrudder:/dɪsˈrʌdər/
v. |使失去方向舵，使失去控制|To deprive of a rudder; to cause to lose steering control.
|The storm disruddered the ship, leaving it at the mercy of the waves.  暴风雨使船失去了方向舵，只能任由海浪摆布。

|The sudden loss of power disruddered the aircraft. 突然的动力损失使飞机失去了控制。
|近义词：disable, cripple, incapacitate|
|反义词：enable, repair, fix|
|词性: 动词(verb)|
