disforestation:/ˌdɪsfɔːrɪˈsteɪʃn/ /ˌdɪsfɔːrəˈsteɪʃn/
n.
森林砍伐；毁林
The act or process of clearing forests; deforestation.
Disforestation is causing significant environmental problems. 森林砍伐正在造成严重的環境問題。
Disforestation leads to soil erosion and loss of biodiversity.  森林砍伐导致水土流失和生物多样性丧失。
近义词：deforestation
反义词：afforestation, reforestation
词性：名词
