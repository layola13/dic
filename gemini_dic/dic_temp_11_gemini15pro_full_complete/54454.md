disinhibit:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɪbɪt/
verb
解除抑制，使无拘束
To free from inhibition; to make uninhibited.
Alcohol can disinhibit some people, leading to impulsive behavior.  酒精会使一些人解除抑制，导致冲动行为。
Synonyms: liberate, release, unleash
Antonyms: inhibit, restrain, suppress
词性: 动词

disinhibition:/ˌdɪsɪnɪˈbɪʃən/
noun
去抑制，无拘束
The state of being disinhibited; a lack of restraint.
The disinhibition caused by the drug led to reckless driving.  药物引起的去抑制导致了鲁莽驾驶。
Synonyms: unrestraint, abandon, freedom
Antonyms: inhibition, restraint, control
词性: 名词

disinhibited:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɪbɪtɪd/
adjective
无拘束的，不受拘束的
Free from inhibition; uninhibited.
He became more disinhibited after a few drinks.  几杯酒下肚后，他变得更加无拘束了。
Synonyms: uninhibited, unrestrained, free
Antonyms: inhibited, restrained, reserved
词性: 形容词
