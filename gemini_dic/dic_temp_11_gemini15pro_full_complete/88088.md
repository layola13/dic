high resolution:/ˌhaɪ ˌrezəˈluːʃən/| adj.|高分辨率的；高清晰度的|Characterized by high resolution; of or relating to an image, display, or recording that has a high resolution.  Relating to or denoting a scientific apparatus capable of separating closely adjacent spectral lines or other signals.|The photographer used a high-resolution camera to capture the stunning details of the landscape. 摄影师使用高分辨率相机捕捉到了令人惊叹的景观细节。|Synonyms: high-definition, sharp, crisp, detailed|Antonyms: low-resolution, blurry, fuzzy, pixelated|Adjective
