deactuating:/diːˈæktʃueɪtɪŋ/
v.
使失效，解除，停止活动
releasing from action; making inactive.
The pilot deactuated the autopilot system before landing. 飞行员在着陆前解除了自动驾驶系统。
synonyms: disabling, deactivating, neutralizing
antonyms: activating, enabling, engaging
verb

deactuate:/diːˈæktʃueɪt/
v.
使失效，解除，停止活动
To release from action; make inactive.
The system deactuates automatically in case of a power failure.  如果发生电源故障，系统会自动停用。
synonyms: disable, deactivate, neutralize
antonyms: activate, enable, engage
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (indicating reversal or removal) + actuate (to put into action), from Latin actus ("act").
