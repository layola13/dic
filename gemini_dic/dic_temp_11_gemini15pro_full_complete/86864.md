hemiholohedral:/ˌhɛmihɒləˈhiːdrəl/
adjective
半全面體的
A crystal form exhibiting half the faces of the holohedral form, due to a combination of crystallographic symmetry and the specific arrangement of atoms.  It represents a lower symmetry compared to the holohedral form, showing a reduction in the number of crystal faces while still maintaining some symmetry elements.
Crystals of pyrite often display hemiholohedral forms. 黄铁矿晶体通常呈现半全面体形态。
Near Synonyms: merohedral
Antonyms: holohedral
词性: adjective
