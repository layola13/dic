inlet:/ˈɪnlɛt/ (US), /ˈɪnlɪt/ (UK)| n.|入口，进水口，小湾|A small arm of the sea, lake, or river.|例句：The boat sailed into the quiet inlet. 船驶入了平静的小湾。

例句：The factory discharged waste into the inlet. 工厂将废物排放到进水口。|近义词：bay, cove, entrance, intake|反义词：outlet, exit|词性:名词
