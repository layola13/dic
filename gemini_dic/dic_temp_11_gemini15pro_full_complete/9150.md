antinaturalisms:/ˌæntiˈnætʃrəlɪzəmz/| n.| 反自然主义（哲学流派）|  A philosophical position that rejects the idea that everything can be explained in terms of natural laws and processes, often emphasizing the role of human reason, values, or spiritual forces.|  His antinaturalisms led him to believe in a divine creator. 他的反自然主义使他相信神圣的造物主。|Synonyms: non-naturalism, supernaturalism|Antonyms: naturalism, materialism|noun
