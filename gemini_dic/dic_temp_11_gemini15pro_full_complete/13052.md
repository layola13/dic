astrocyte: /ˈæstrəsaɪt/ | n. | 星形胶质细胞 | A star-shaped glial cell in the brain and spinal cord that provides structural support to neurons, regulates the extracellular environment, and contributes to the blood-brain barrier. |  Astrocytes play a vital role in maintaining the health of neurons. 星形胶质细胞在维持神经元的健康方面起着至关重要的作用。| Synonym: glial cell | Antonym: neuron | noun
