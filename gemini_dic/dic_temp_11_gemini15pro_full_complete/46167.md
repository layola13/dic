cynorexia:/ˌsaɪnəˈrɛksiə/ | n. | 食欲倒错；厌食症；拒食症 | An abnormal aversion to food; loss of appetite.  A morbid aversion to or loathing of food. |  She suffered from cynorexia, refusing to eat even when her body desperately needed nourishment. 她患有拒食症，即使身体极度需要营养也拒绝进食。| 近义词: anorexia, inappetence | 反义词: bulimia, polyphagia | 词性: noun
