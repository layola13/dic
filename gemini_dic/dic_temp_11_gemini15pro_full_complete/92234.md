hypertragic:/ˌhaɪpərˈtrædʒɪk/
adjective
极其悲惨的，过度悲剧的
Exaggeratedly or excessively tragic; overly dramatic in a tragic way.
The play was so hypertragic that it became almost comical.  这出戏太悲惨了，几乎变得滑稽了。
His hypertragic pronouncements about the weather grew tiresome. 他关于天气的过度悲观的言论变得令人厌烦。
近义词: overdramatic, melodramatic, over-the-top
反义词: understated, subtle, comedic
词性: adjective

词源:
From hyper- + tragic.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, excessive," deriving from Greek "hyper" (over, above). "Tragic" comes from the Greek "tragikos" (related to tragedy), ultimately from "tragos" (goat), perhaps referring to the goat-song of tragedy in ancient Greek drama.
