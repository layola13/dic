manant:/ˈmænənt/| n.|居民，佃户，臣民|(Archaic) An inhabitant; a dweller, especially a peasant or tenant.  Origin: Old French, present participle of manoír 'to remain, dwell'.|The king addressed his manants.|Near synonyms: inhabitant, resident, dweller, tenant, peasant, subject (of a monarch).|Antonyms:  ruler, monarch, landlord, foreigner, alien.|noun
