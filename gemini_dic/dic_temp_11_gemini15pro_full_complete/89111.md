homarus:/ˈhoʊməˌrʌs/| n.|龙虾|A genus of large edible marine crustaceans of the family Nephropidae, having large pincers on the front pair of legs.  Specifically, the European or common lobster (Homarus gammarus) and the American lobster (Homarus americanus).|例句：The homarus is a prized delicacy in many cultures. 龙虾在很多文化中都是珍贵的美味佳肴。
