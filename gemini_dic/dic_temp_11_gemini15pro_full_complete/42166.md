coues:/ˈkuːz/| n. |库氏鼠、库氏白尾鹿|A surname and also used to specify certain subspecies of animals, named after Elliott Coues, an American ornithologist, surgeon, and historian. |Elliott Coues described this species of white-tailed deer. 艾略特·库斯描述了这种白尾鹿。| Coues' deer are smaller than other white-tailed deer. 库氏白尾鹿比其他白尾鹿小。|近义词: (For the deer) white-tailed deer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
