antisuicide:/ˌæntiˈsuːɪsaɪd/
adjective
反自杀的; 预防自杀的
Relating to or intended to prevent suicide.
例句：The hospital has an antisuicide policy.  这家医院有反自杀政策。

例句：She joined an antisuicide support group. 她加入了一个反自杀互助小组。

近义词：suicide prevention, life-affirming
反义词：pro-suicide (This term is highly sensitive and should be used with extreme caution.)
词性：adjective
