frigidarium:/frɪdʒɪˈdɛəriəm/ | n. | 冷水浴室，冷浴池 | A large cold pool in ancient Roman baths.  A room in a bath complex that contains a cold-water bath. |  The frigidarium was a welcome respite from the heat of the caldarium. 冷水浴室是从高温浴室（caldarium）的热气中得到喘息的好地方。

The frigidarium is often the most impressive room in Roman bath ruins. 冷水浴室通常是罗马浴场遗址中最令人印象深刻的房间。| 近义词: cold bath, cold plunge pool | 反义词: caldarium, tepidarium | 词性: 名词
