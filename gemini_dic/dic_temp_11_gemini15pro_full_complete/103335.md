jigsawing:/ˈdʒɪɡˌsɔːɪŋ/
n.
拼图；七巧板；类似拼图的活动
the activity of fitting pieces of a jigsaw puzzle together
She enjoys jigsawing as a relaxing pastime. 她喜欢拼图作为一种放松的消遣。

v.
拼合；像拼图一样组合
to fit together or combine like the pieces of a jigsaw puzzle
The different pieces of evidence were jigsawed together to form a complete picture. 不同的证据拼凑在一起，形成了完整的画面。
