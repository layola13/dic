lucklessly: /ˈlʌkləsli/ | adv. | 不幸地，倒霉地 |  Without luck; unfortunately.  Describes an action or event that happened due to bad luck. | He lucklessly invested all his money in a failing company. 他不幸地把所有的钱都投资到了一家倒闭的公司。|  He lucklessly tripped and fell, breaking his leg. 他不小心绊倒了，摔断了腿。| 近义词: unfortunately, unluckily, disastrously | 反义词: luckily, fortunately | 词性: 副词

词源: luck (n.) + -less (suffix) + -ly (suffix).  "Luck" comes from Middle Dutch "luc" meaning "chance, fortune." "-less" is a suffix indicating a lack of something, and "-ly" turns it into an adverb.
