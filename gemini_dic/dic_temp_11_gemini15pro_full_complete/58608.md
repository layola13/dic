dullish:/ˈdʌlɪʃ/| adj.|稍显迟钝的；有点无聊的；略微阴暗的|Somewhat dull; slightly lacking in brightness, sharpness, or interest.|The day was dullish and overcast.  这一天有点阴沉，天气阴霾。|The movie was dullish and predictable. 这部电影有点无聊，而且可以预料到剧情。|近义词：slightly dull, a bit boring, somewhat uninteresting|反义词：bright, interesting, exciting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From dull + -ish (suffix indicating a moderate degree of a quality).  由dull(迟钝的)加上-ish(表示某种性质程度适中的后缀)构成。
