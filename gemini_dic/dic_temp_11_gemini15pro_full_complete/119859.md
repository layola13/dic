miles:/maɪlz/
n.
英里；大量，许多
A unit of linear measure equal to 5,280 feet, or 1,760 yards (approximately 1.609 kilometers).  A large extent or amount or a great number.
I live five miles from the city center. 我住在离市中心五英里的地方。
He’s won miles of awards. 他赢得了很多奖项。
近义词: distance, length,  a lot, a great deal
反义词:  (For distance) short distance, nearness
