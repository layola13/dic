interliner:/ˈɪntərˌlaɪnər/| n.|衬垫，衬里；行间注释；班车|A layer of material placed between two other layers, especially in clothing or upholstery; a book with the original text and its translation printed on alternate lines; a bus or train that travels between two or more main routes.|例句：The coat had a warm interliner. 这件外套有一个温暖的衬里。

例句：He used an interliner to help him understand the ancient text. 他使用了一本行间注释的书来帮助他理解古代文本。|近义词：lining, padding, filler, shuttle|反义词:|noun

interliner:/ˈɪntərˌlaɪnər/| adj.|班车的|Operating between two or more main routes.|例句：The interliner service connects several smaller towns.  这条班车服务连接了几个小城镇。

例句：The interliner flight was cheaper than the direct flight. 这趟中转航班比直飞航班便宜。|近义词：connecting, transfer|反义词：direct|adjective
