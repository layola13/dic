delicatest:/ˈdɛlɪkətɪst/| adj.|最精巧的，最微妙的，最脆弱的|Most delicate; superlative of delicate.  It refers to something of extreme fineness, sensitivity, or fragility.|She handled the delicate porcelain with the delicatest touch. 她用最轻柔的动作拿起了易碎的瓷器。|近义词：finest, frailest, tenderest, most sensitive|反义词：coarsest, toughest, strongest, roughest|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *delicatiste, superlative of delicat (“delicate”), from Old French delicat (“delicate”), from Latin dēlicātus (“alluring, charming, delightful, dainty”), past participle of dēlicāre (“to allure, entice”), from dēliciō (“allurement, enticement, delight”), from delicere (“to allure, entice, delight”), from de- (“from, away”) + laciō (“I entice, allure”).
