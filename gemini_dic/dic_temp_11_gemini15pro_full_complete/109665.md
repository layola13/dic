leucoethiopic: /ˌluːkoʊˌiːθiˈɒpɪk/
adjective
白化病的；白化病患者的；白化病黑人
Relating to or characterized by albinism, especially in people of African descent.  A combination of "leuco-" (white) and "Ethiopic" (relating to Ethiopia or Africa).
The study focused on the genetic basis of leucoethiopic albinism. 这项研究集中在白化病黑人白化病的遗传基础上。
The anthropologist documented the social experiences of leucoethiopic individuals in several African communities.  这位人类学家记录了几个非洲社区中白化病黑人个体的社会经历。
近义词: albinotic, albinic
反义词: pigmented
词性: adjective
