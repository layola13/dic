contaminating:/kənˈtæmɪneɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)|污染，弄脏|Making something impure or polluted by adding something harmful or unwanted.|The factory is contaminating the river with toxic waste. 工厂正在用有毒废物污染河流。|polluting, tainting, adulterating, defiling|purifying, cleaning, cleansing|v.
contaminate:/kənˈtæmɪneɪt/
verb|污染，弄脏|To make something impure or polluted by adding something harmful or unwanted.|The oil spill contaminated the beach. 漏油污染了海滩。|pollute, taint, adulterate, defile|purify, clean, cleanse|v.
contamination:/kənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃən/
noun|污染，污染物|The process of making something impure or polluted, or the substance that pollutes.|The contamination of the water supply made many people sick.  供水污染使许多人生病。|pollution, taint, adulteration, defilement|purification, cleaning, cleansing|n.
