laddies:/ˈlædiz/| n.|小伙子们，孩子们（苏格兰、爱尔兰、英格兰北部方言）|A group of boys or young men, often used affectionately or playfully.  It's a chiefly Scottish, Irish, and Northern English dialect word. |例句：The laddies were playing football in the park.  孩子们在公园里踢足球。

例句：Come on, laddies, let's go for a pint!  来吧，小伙子们，我们去喝一杯！|近义词：boys, lads, fellas, guys|反义词:lassies (girls)|noun
