mis-administration:/ˌmɪs ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/
|n.| 管理不善，失政|Bad or inefficient management; mismanagement.|The mis-administration of the fund led to significant losses. 基金管理不善导致了重大损失。|The company's mis-administration resulted in bankruptcy. 该公司的管理不当导致破产。|近义词：mismanagement, maladministration, misgovernance|反义词：good governance, sound management|词性:名词

mis-:/mɪs/
|prefix.|错误的，不当的|Indicates something done wrongly or badly.|Misunderstand 误解，Misinform 误报|Mislead 误导, Misplace 放错地方|近义词：mal-, wrong-|反义词：well-, right-|词性:前缀

administration:/ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/
|n.|管理，行政，政府|The process or activity of running a business, organization, etc.|The administration of the school is responsible for student discipline. 学校的行政部门负责学生纪律。|The new administration has promised to improve the economy. 新政府承诺改善经济。|近义词：management, governance, execution|反义词：neglect|词性:名词
