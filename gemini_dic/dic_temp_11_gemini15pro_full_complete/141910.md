orders:/ˈɔːrdərz/ (US), /ˈɔːdəz/ (UK)| n. |命令，指示；订单；秩序；等级|Instructions or commands issued by someone in authority; requests for goods or services; a state of organization or arrangement; a hierarchical system.| The general issued orders to his troops. 将军向他的部队下达了命令。| synonyms: commands, directives, instructions, requests, purchases | antonyms: countermands, cancellations, chaos, disorder | noun

orders:/ˈɔːrdərz/ (US), /ˈɔːdəz/ (UK)| v. |命令，指示；订购；整理|To issue a command or instruction; to request goods or services; to arrange or organize something.| He ordered a new computer online. 他在网上订购了一台新电脑。| synonyms: commands, directs, instructs, requests, purchases | antonyms: countermands, cancels, disorganizes | verb
