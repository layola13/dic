词源:
From Middle English *Londone, *Loundone, *Londres, from Old English Lunden, Lundene, Lundenne (“London”), from Proto-Germanic *Lunduniwaz (“London”), of uncertain origin.  Possibly from Proto-Celtic *Londinion (“pool, marsh”).
