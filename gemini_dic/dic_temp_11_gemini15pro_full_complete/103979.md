jugger:/ˈdʒʌɡər/| n.|杂耍演员，玩杂耍的人|A person who juggles objects, typically balls, clubs, or rings, for entertainment.|He's a skilled jugger and can keep five balls in the air at once. 他是一位技艺娴熟的杂耍演员，可以同时让五个球在空中飞舞。|近义词：jongleur, performer|反义词:audience, spectator|词性:noun

jugger:/ˈdʒʌɡər/| n.|（尤指中世纪的）游吟诗人|A medieval itinerant minstrel.|The jugger entertained the crowd with songs and stories.  这位游吟诗人用歌曲和故事娱乐大众。|近义词：minstrel, bard|反义词:None|词性:noun
