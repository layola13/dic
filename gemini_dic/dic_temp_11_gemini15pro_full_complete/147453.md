overoffended: /ˌoʊvərəˈfɛndɪd/ | adj. | 过度 offended 的，容易被冒犯的 | Excessively or easily offended; taking offense too readily. |  He was overoffended by the comedian's jokes. 他对喜剧演员的笑话反应过度。| She's so overoffended, you can't say anything without her getting upset. 她太容易被冒犯了，你说什么都会让她不高兴。| 近义词: touchy, sensitive, thin-skinned | 反义词: thick-skinned, insensitive | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning excessively) + "offended" (past participle of "offend")
