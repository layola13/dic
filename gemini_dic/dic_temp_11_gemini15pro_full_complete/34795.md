climaxing: /ˈklaɪmæksɪŋ/ | v. |达到高潮，达到顶点|reaching the most intense or exciting point; culminating|The movie climaxed with a thrilling car chase. 这部电影以一场惊险的汽车追逐达到高潮。|Synonyms: culminating, peaking, cresting|Antonyms: beginning, starting|Verb

climax: /ˈklaɪmæks/ | n. |高潮，顶点|the most intense, exciting, or important point of something; a culmination or apex.|The climax of the symphony was breathtaking. 交响乐的高潮令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: peak, apex, culmination, zenith|Antonyms: nadir, bottom, trough|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "klimax" (ladder, staircase) -> Latin "climax" -> Old French "climax" -> Middle English "climax" -> Modern English "climax" and "climaxing"
