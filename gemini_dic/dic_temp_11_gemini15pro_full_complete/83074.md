gurn:/ɡɜːn/| n. vi.|鬼脸，做鬼脸，扮鬼脸|A twisted expression of the face, often made to cause laughter or amusement; the act of making such an expression.|He made a gurn to try and make the baby laugh. 他做了个鬼脸想逗宝宝笑。|近义词：grimace, contortion, mug|反义词:smile|词性:名词,动词

gurns:/ɡɜːnz/|n.|鬼脸（复数）|Twisted expressions of the face, often made to cause laughter or amusement.|The children pulled gurns at each other across the classroom. 孩子们隔着教室互相扮鬼脸。|近义词：grimaces, contortions|反义词:smiles|词性:名词

词源:  From the 16th century, of uncertain origin, possibly imitative.
