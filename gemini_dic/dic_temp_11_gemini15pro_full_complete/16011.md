banality:/bəˈnælɪti/ /bəˈnæləti/
n.
平庸；陈腐；老生常谈
The state or quality of being banal; unoriginality.
The banality of most political slogans is depressing. 大多数政治口号的陈词滥调令人沮丧。

banality:/bəˈnælɪti/ /bəˈnæləti/
n.
陈词滥调；老套的话
A banal remark or statement.
His speech was filled with banalities. 他的演讲充满了陈词滥调。

Etymology: French *banalité*, from *banal* "belonging to the feudal lord, common, commonplace," from Old French *ban* "proclamation, jurisdiction, district," from Frankish *ban* "order, command."

近义词:commonplace, cliché, platitude, triteness, vapidity
反义词:originality, novelty, freshness, uniqueness, profundity
词性:noun
