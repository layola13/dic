carbonised:/ˈkɑːbənaɪzd/ | adj. | 碳化的，烧焦的 |  Converted into carbon, usually by heating or burning.  The process removes water and other volatile substances, leaving mostly carbon. | The wood was completely carbonised after the fire.  木头在大火之后完全碳化了。 | The ancient scrolls were carbonised by the volcanic eruption.  古代卷轴被火山爆发烧焦了。 | 近义词: charred, burnt, scorched | 反义词: unburnt, intact | 词性: 形容词

词源: From carbon + -ise/-ize.  Ultimately from Latin "carbo" (coal).
