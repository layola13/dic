misinstruction:/ˌmɪsɪnˈstrʌkʃən/
n.
错误指示，误导
The misinstruction led to a serious accident. 错误的指示导致了一起严重的事故。
The students' confusion stemmed from the teacher's misinstruction. 学生们的困惑源于老师的错误指导。
近义词:misguidance, misinformation, misdirection
反义词:correct instruction, proper guidance
词性:名词

词源:
From mis- + instruction.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "incorrect." "Instruction" comes from the Latin "instructio," meaning "a building, construction, preparation, or education."
