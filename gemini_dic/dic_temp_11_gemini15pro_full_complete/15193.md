backcasting:/ˈbækˌkæstɪŋ/| n.|反向预测，回溯预测|The process of projecting backward from a desired future state to determine the steps needed to achieve that state.|Backcasting helped the city planners determine the necessary infrastructure investments to achieve their sustainability goals. 反向预测帮助城市规划者确定实现可持续发展目标所需的基础设施投资。|Backcasting from our desired 2050 emissions target, we can identify milestones and policy interventions needed in the intervening years.  从我们期望的2050年排放目标回溯，我们可以确定在此期间所需的里程碑和政策干预。|forecasting, predicting|hindcasting|noun
