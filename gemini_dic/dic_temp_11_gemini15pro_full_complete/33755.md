circularly:/ˈsɜːkjələrli/ | adv. | 圆形地，循环地 | In a circular manner; so as to form a circle or move in a circle. | The dancers moved circularly around the fire. 舞者绕着火堆转圈。| 近义词：roundly, cyclically | 反义词：linearly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Late Latin *circulariter, from Latin circularis (“circular”) + -iter (adverbial suffix).
