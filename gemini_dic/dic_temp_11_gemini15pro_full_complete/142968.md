outbabbled:/ˌaʊtˈbæbəld/
verb
胜过…的饶舌；比…更健谈；说得比…多
to talk more, longer, or more persuasively than someone else
He outbabbled her in the argument. 他在争论中说得比她多。
Synonyms: outtalk, outargue, outspeak
Antonyms: be quiet, listen
词性: 动词
