ovality:/oʊˈvælɪti/
n.
椭圆度，卵形度
the state or quality of being oval
The ovality of the egg was more pronounced than usual.  这个鸡蛋的椭圆度比平常更明显。

近义词:ellipticity, oblongness, roundness
反义词:angularity, sharpness

词性:noun
词源:From Latin ōvālis (“oval”) + -ity
