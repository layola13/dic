crumbier:/ˈkrʌmiər/| adj.|更易碎的；更糟的；更低劣的|More crumbly or of poorer quality.  It can also be used figuratively to mean worse or shabbier.|The cake was crumbier than I expected.  这蛋糕比我想象的更易碎。|Synonyms: inferior, worse, shabbier, flakier|Antonyms: better, superior, firmer, less crumbly|adjective

Etymology->
From crumb + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  "Crumb" comes from Old English "cruma."
