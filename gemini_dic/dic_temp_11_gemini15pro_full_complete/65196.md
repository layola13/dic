ETYMOLOGY->
From ethyl + -ate. Ethyl from ether + -yl, ultimately from Greek aithēr (upper air) + hyle (wood, matter).  -ate is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to treat, combine, or impregnate with," and nouns meaning "a salt or ester of an acid ending in -ic."
