awakening:/əˈweɪkənɪŋ/
n.|觉醒，唤醒，认识|An awakening is a time when you start to understand something clearly, or when you become interested in something.|The book describes her spiritual awakening. 这本书描述了她的精神觉醒。|
近义词:realization, awareness, discovery, enlightenment
反义词: slumber, ignorance, unconsciousness
词性:noun

awakening:/əˈweɪkənɪŋ/
adj.|觉醒的，唤醒的，警醒的|Awakening describes something that causes a realization or awareness.|The awakening experience changed his life. 这次觉醒的经历改变了他的人生。
近义词: enlightening, revealing, eye-opening
反义词: soporific, dull, boring
词性:adjective


词源: From Middle English *awakening*, present participle of *awaken*, from Old English *āwæcnan*, from *ā-* (intensifying prefix) + *wæcnan* (“to wake up, become awake”), from Proto-Germanic *waknōną (“to wake up, watch”), from Proto-Indo-European *wégʰ- (“to be lively, awake”).
