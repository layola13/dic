nonfamilial:/ˌnɑːnfəˈmɪliəl/ | /ˌnɒnfəˈmɪliəl/ | adj. | 非家族的；非家庭的 | Not relating to or occurring within a family. |  Example: Nonfamilial adenomatous polyposis is a genetic condition that increases the risk of colon cancer. 非家族性腺瘤性息肉病是一种增加患结肠癌风险的遗传性疾病。

Example:  The study focused on nonfamilial environmental risk factors for the disease. 这项研究的重点是该疾病的非家族性环境风险因素。 | Synonyms: nongenetic, nonhereditary, acquired | Antonyms: familial, genetic, hereditary | word type: adjective
