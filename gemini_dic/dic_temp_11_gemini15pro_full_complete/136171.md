nonreigning:/nɑːnˈreɪnɪŋ/
adjective
非在位的，非统治的
Not currently reigning; not holding a position of power or authority.
The nonreigning monarch attended the ceremony.  这位非在位君主出席了仪式。
The museum displayed portraits of nonreigning emperors.  博物馆展出了非在位皇帝的肖像。
Synonyms: former, ex-, retired, dethroned
Antonyms: reigning, ruling, incumbent
