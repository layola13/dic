elucidator:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪtər/
n.
阐明者；解释者
someone or something that elucidates something
The professor's clear explanations made him an excellent elucidator of complex topics.  这位教授清晰的解释使他成为复杂主题的优秀阐明者。
Synonyms: explainer, interpreter, clarifier
Antonyms: obfuscator, obscurer, confuser
noun
