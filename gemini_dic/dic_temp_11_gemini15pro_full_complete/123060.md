monetization:/ˌmʌnɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
货币化; 变现
The process of converting or establishing something into legal tender.  The act or process of earning revenue from a product, service, or other asset.
The monetization of the app through in-app purchases was successful.  通过应用内购买，该应用程序的货币化取得了成功。

The company is exploring different monetization strategies for its website.  该公司正在探索其网站的不同盈利策略。
近义词:commercialization, capitalization
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps expenditure or divestment)
词性: noun
