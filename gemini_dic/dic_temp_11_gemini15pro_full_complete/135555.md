nonpolarizable:/ˌnɒnpəʊləˈraɪzəbl/ | adj. | 不可极化的 | Not able to be polarized; not susceptible to polarization.  This means the electron distribution within the atom or molecule is not easily distorted by an external electric field. | Argon atoms are largely nonpolarizable due to their filled electron shells. 氩原子由于其充满的电子壳层，很大程度上不可极化。

The nonpolarizable electrode provided stable measurements.  不可极化电极提供了稳定的测量结果。 | 近义词: non-polarizable | 反义词: polarizable | 词性: adjective
