halalling:/həˈlɑːlɪŋ/ | v. |  进行清真屠宰 | To slaughter an animal according to Islamic law. |  The butcher is halalling a lamb for the upcoming Eid celebration.  屠夫正在为即将到来的开斋节庆祝活动屠宰一只羊羔。 | synonyms: slaughter (according to Islamic law), perform halal slaughter | antonyms: slaughter (not according to Islamic law) | verb
