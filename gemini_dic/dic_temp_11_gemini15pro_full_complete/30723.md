cerused:/ˈsɪəruːzd/ | adj. | 涂白铅的；似白铅的 | Treated with or resembling white lead.  | The cerused finish on the antique table gave it a unique, aged look.  这古董桌子上的白铅涂层使它具有独特的年代感。| 近义词: white-leaded, lead-painted | 反义词: unpainted, natural | 词性: 形容词

ceruse:/ˈsɪəruːs/ | n. | 白铅；铅白 | White lead, a basic lead carbonate formerly used as a pigment and in cosmetics. | The artist used ceruse to create the highlights in the painting.  艺术家用白铅来创作画中的高光部分。| 近义词: white lead, lead white | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle French *ceruse* (modern French *céruse*), from Latin *cerussa*, probably from *cera* "wax".
