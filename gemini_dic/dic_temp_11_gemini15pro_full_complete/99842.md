intermixable:/ˌɪntərˈmɪksəbl/ | adj. |可混合的，可掺杂的 | Capable of being mixed together without separating or reacting negatively. | The two paints are intermixable, allowing for a wide range of custom colors. 这两种颜料可以混合，可以调出各种各样的颜色。| Synonyms: blendable, combinable, compatible, miscible | Antonyms: immiscible, incompatible, unblendable | adjective

intermixable:/ˌɪntərˈmɪksəbl/ | adj. |可交织的，可交错的 |  Capable of being intermingled or interspersed. |  The intermixable strands of different cultures create a vibrant tapestry of traditions. 不同文化相互交织的脉络，创造出充满活力的传统织锦。 | Synonyms:  intertwined, interwoven, mingled | Antonyms: separate, distinct, unmixed | adjective
