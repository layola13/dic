embracement:/ɪmˈbreɪsmənt/| n. |拥抱，接受，信奉|The act of embracing someone or something; the state of being embraced.  An acceptance or adoption of something.|She greeted him with a warm embracement. 她热情地拥抱了他。|近义词：hug, acceptance, adoption, espousal|反义词：rejection, refusal, spurning|词性:noun

embracement:/ɪmˈbreɪsmənt/| n. |包含，环绕|The act of including or encompassing something.|The book's embracement of different cultures is commendable. 这本书对不同文化的包容值得称赞。|近义词：inclusion, encompassing|反义词：exclusion|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "embracier" (to clasp in the arms), from "en-" (in) + "brace" (arms).  The noun form "embracement" emerged later.
