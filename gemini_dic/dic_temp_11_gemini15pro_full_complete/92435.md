hyponymous:/ˌhaɪˈpɒnɪməs/| adj.|下义的，下位的|Of, relating to, or being a hyponym.  A word whose meaning is included in the meaning of another, more general word. For example, "dog" is a hyponym of "animal."|A poodle is hyponymous with dog.|近义词：subordinate, specific|反义词：superordinate, general|词性: adjective

词源: From Ancient Greek ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + ὄνομα (ónoma, “name”).
