deblai:/deɪˈbleɪ/ /dəˈbleɪ/ | n. | 土石方；清除物；废墟；（尤指）战场上的瓦砾 | The removal of earth, rock, etc., as in excavating or in clearing a site.  The material removed in such an operation. Debris, rubble. | The deblai from the tunnel construction blocked the road. 隧道施工的土石方堵塞了道路。 | 近义词: rubble, debris, spoil, waste | 反义词: remblai (material used to fill an excavation) | 词性: 名词
