overdemands:/ˌōvər dəˈmændz/ | n. | 过度要求，过高要求 |  Making excessive or unreasonable demands; requiring more than is reasonable or possible. |  The overdemands of the job led to burnout.  工作中的过度要求导致了倦怠。| synonyms: excessive demands, unreasonable requests, exorbitant expectations | antonyms: reasonable requests, modest demands | noun

overdemands:/ˌōvər dəˈmændz/ | v. |  对...提出过高要求 |  To place excessive or unreasonable demands on someone or something; to require more of someone or something than is reasonable or possible.  | The coach overdemanded the athletes, pushing them to their physical limits. 教练对运动员提出了过高的要求，把他们逼到了体能极限。 | synonyms: overburden, overtax, strain, push too hard | antonyms: underdemand, coddle, pamper | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over, beyond") + "demands" (from Old French demander, from Latin demandare, meaning "to entrust, commit, order")
