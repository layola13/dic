fumous:/ˈfjuːməs/| adj.|冒烟的；烟雾弥漫的|Producing or full of fumes; smoky. |The air was thick and fumous from the burning tires.  空气中弥漫着燃烧轮胎的浓烟。|The factory's fumous chimneys belched black smoke into the sky. 工厂冒烟的烟囱向天空喷出黑烟。|近义词：smoky, fuming|反义词: clear, clean|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fūmōsus, from fūmus (“smoke”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives).
