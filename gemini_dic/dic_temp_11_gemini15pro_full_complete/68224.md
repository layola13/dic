fairing-up:/ˈfɛərɪŋ ʌp/| n.|改进，好转；（尤指天气的）转晴|An act of improving or becoming better, especially of the weather. |The fairing-up of the weather allowed us to finally go sailing. 天气好转让我们终于可以去航行了。|Near synonyms: improvement, betterment, amelioration|Near antonyms: worsening, deterioration|noun
Etymology: From "fair" (pleasant, agreeable) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "up" (adverb indicating upward movement or improvement).  The "fair" part comes from Old English "fæger" meaning beautiful or fitting.
