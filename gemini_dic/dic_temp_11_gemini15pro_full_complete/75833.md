fuming:/ˈfjuːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 冒烟的；发怒的 | emitting fumes; enraged |  The fuming volcano threatened the nearby villages. 冒着烟的火山威胁着附近的村庄。 | synonyms: raging, furious, seething, indignant | antonyms: calm, peaceful, tranquil | adjective

fume:/fjuːm/ | n. | 烟；气体；愤怒 | gas, smoke, or vapor, especially when irritating or offensive; a fit of anger | The fumes from the factory were choking. 工厂排出的烟雾令人窒息。 | synonyms: smoke, vapor, gas, exhaust | antonyms:  (for anger) calmness, tranquility | noun

fume:/fjuːm/ | v. | 冒烟；发怒 | emit gas or vapor; show or feel anger | He was fuming about the unfair treatment. 他对不公平的待遇感到愤怒。 | synonyms: smolder, rage, seethe | antonyms: (for anger) relax, calm down | verb
