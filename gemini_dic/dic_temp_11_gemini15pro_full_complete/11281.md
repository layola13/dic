arctoidea:/ɑːrkˈtɔɪdiə/ | n. | 犬形亚目 | A suborder of carnivoran mammals, including bears, dogs, seals, weasels, and raccoons.  It is characterized by specific anatomical features of the skull and teeth. |  The red panda was once classified within Arctoidea, but recent genetic studies place it in its own family. 小熊猫曾经被归类于犬形亚目，但最近的基因研究将其归入其自身的科。| 近义词: Caniformia (sometimes used interchangeably but with slight differences) | 反义词: Feliformia (猫形亚目) | 词性: noun
