commit:/kəˈmɪt/ | v. | 犯罪，承诺，托付 | to perform an act, usually with a negative connotation; to pledge or bind oneself; to entrust | He committed a terrible crime. 他犯下了可怕的罪行。 | synonyms: perpetrate, pledge, entrust | antonyms: abstain, renege, withdraw | verb

commit:/kəˈmɪt/ | n. | 承诺，保证 | a pledge or undertaking | He made a commit to finish the project. 他承诺完成这个项目。| synonyms: pledge, promise, undertaking | antonyms:  renunciation, denial | noun
