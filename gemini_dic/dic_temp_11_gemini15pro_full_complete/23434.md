bracteole: /ˈbræktɪˌoʊl/ | n. | 小苞片 |A small leaf-like structure at the base of a flower stalk or pedicel, often resembling a bract but usually smaller. | Example 1: The bracteoles of this orchid are almost invisible.  这种兰花的小苞片几乎看不见。

Example 2: The botanist identified the species by the shape of its bracteoles.  植物学家通过小苞片的形状识别了该物种。 |Synonym: bractlet | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific botanical structure) | noun
