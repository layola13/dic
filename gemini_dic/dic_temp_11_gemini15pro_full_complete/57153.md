dowager:/ˈdaʊədʒər/| n.|寡妇，遗孀（尤指出身高贵或拥有财产的）；|A dowager is a woman who holds a title or property derived from her deceased husband, especially one of high rank or substantial wealth. |The dowager queen still held considerable influence in the court.  太后在宫廷中仍然拥有相当大的影响力。

The dowager countess lived in a grand mansion.  伯爵遗孀住在一座宏伟的宅邸里。|Near Synonyms: widow, matron, dame|Antonyms: bachelor, spinster|Noun
