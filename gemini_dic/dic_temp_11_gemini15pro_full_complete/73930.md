fortat:/ˈfɔːrtæt/ |  n. |  (古罗马军事)筑垒营地; 营垒 | A fortified camp, especially one of the type built by the Roman army. | The legions marched for days before establishing a fortat on the hilltop. 军团行军数日后，在山顶上建立了一个筑垒营地。 | 近义词：camp, encampment, stronghold | 反义词： (无明确反义词，可考虑 open field, unprotected area) | 词性: 名词


词源: From Latin *fortātum (unattested), neuter of fortātus (“fortified”), past participle of fortō (“I fortify”), from fortis (“strong”).
