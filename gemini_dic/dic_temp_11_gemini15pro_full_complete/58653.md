dumbo:/ˈdʌmboʊ/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A name, especially a nickname, for someone considered stupid or foolish. Derived from the name of a Disney cartoon elephant who could fly with his large ears. |Example 1:  "Don't be such a dumbo! It's easy to figure out."  别这么傻！很容易就能弄明白。

Example 2:  "He felt like a dumbo after realizing his mistake."  意识到自己的错误后，他觉得自己像个傻瓜。|近义词：idiot, fool, nitwit, simpleton|反义词:genius, intellectual|词性:名词
