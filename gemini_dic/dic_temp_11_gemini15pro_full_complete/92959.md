ideologues:/ˈaɪdiəˌlɔːɡz/ /ˈɪdiəˌlɑːɡz/
noun
意识形态拥护者；理论家；空想家
A person who strongly believes in particular principles and tries to put them into action.

例句：
The ideologues were unwilling to compromise.
这些意识形态拥护者不愿妥协。

The two ideologues debated the merits of their respective political systems.
两位理论家就各自政治体系的优缺点进行了辩论。


近义词：
theorist, doctrinaire, zealot, fanatic

反义词：
pragmatist, realist

词性
n. (noun)
