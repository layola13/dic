instructedly:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪdli/
adverb
指导地，按照指示地
in a manner that follows instructions; according to directions.
The students worked instructedly, following the teacher's guidance carefully.  学生们按照指示认真地工作，仔细遵循老师的指导。
The technician instructedly assembled the device according to the manual. 技术人员按照手册的指示组装设备。
Synonym: obediently, dutifully, compliantly, submissively
Antonym: disobediently, rebelliously, defiantly, independently
词性: 副词
