cliffside:/ˈklɪfˌsaɪd/| n.|悬崖边，峭壁边|The house perched precariously on the cliffside. 这座房子危险地坐落在悬崖边上。|例句：The cliffside offered a breathtaking view of the ocean. 悬崖边提供了令人叹为观止的海景。

例句：He carefully climbed down the cliffside. 他小心翼翼地从悬崖边爬了下去。|近义词：cliff face, precipice, bluff|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "cliff" + "side". "Cliff" comes from Old English "clif" meaning "cliff, steep rock, promontory." "Side" comes from Old English "sīde" meaning "side, flank."
