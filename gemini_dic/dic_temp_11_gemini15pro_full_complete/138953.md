nuzzler:/ˈnʌzlər/| n. |爱依偎的人；喜欢用鼻子蹭的人|A person who nuzzles; someone who likes to cuddle or rub their nose affectionately against another. |例句：My cat is a real nuzzler, always wanting to rub against my legs. 我的猫是个真正的爱依偎者，总是想蹭我的腿。

例句：The little boy was a nuzzler, burying his face in his mother's coat. 小男孩是个爱依偎的人，把脸埋在妈妈的大衣里。|近义词：cuddler, snuggler|反义词: loner|词性:noun
