conversationally:/ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃənəli/ | adv. | 会话地，口语地 | In a manner suitable for or resembling informal conversation.  It means in a way that is suitable for informal speech rather than formal writing or speaking.  It suggests a relaxed and natural style of communication. |  She spoke conversationally about her travels. 她随意地谈论了她的旅行经历。 |  informally, casually, chattily | formally, stiffly | adverb
