absent-mindedly: /ˌæbsəntˈmaɪndɪdli/ | adv. | 心不在焉地；漫不经心地 | in a way that shows you are not thinking about what you are doing | He absent-mindedly left his keys on the table. 他心不在焉地把钥匙忘在了桌子上。 | 近义词: distractedly, inattentively, thoughtlessly | 反义词: attentively, carefully, mindfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  From absent-minded + -ly.  Absent-minded is from absent + minded.  Absent from Latin absentem, present participle of abesse "to be away," from ab- "away from" + esse "to be." Minded from mind + -ed. Mind from Old English gemynd "memory, thought, purpose."
