diplex:/ˈdaɪpleks/| adj. n.|双工的，双工制；双工装置|A diplexer is a device that combines or separates two signals operating at different frequencies.|The diplexer allows two antennas to share a single feedline.|近义词：duplex, two-way|反义词：simplex|词性:形容词,名词

Etymology->
From di- "two" + -plex "fold, interwoven," from Latin plicāre "to fold."
