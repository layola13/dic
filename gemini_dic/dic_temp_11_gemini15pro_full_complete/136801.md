nonshareholder:/ˌnɒnˈʃɛəˌhoʊldər/ | n. | 非股东 |  A person or entity that does not own shares or stock in a particular company. | Example:  Nonshareholders are not entitled to vote at the company's annual meeting. 非股东没有资格在公司年会上投票。

Example: The company's decision also affects nonshareholders.  公司的决定也影响到非股东。 | Near Synonyms: non-owner, outsider | Opposite: shareholder, stockholder | Noun
