citadel: /ˈsɪtədəl/ /ˈsɪtədɛl/ | n. |堡垒；要塞；避难所|A fortress, typically one on high ground above a city, protecting it from attack. | The citadel overlooked the city, offering a strategic advantage.  堡垒俯瞰着城市，提供了战略优势。

The troops retreated to the citadel for safety.  部队撤退到城堡寻求安全。| 近义词: fortress, stronghold, bastion, fort | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly: vulnerability, weakness) | noun
