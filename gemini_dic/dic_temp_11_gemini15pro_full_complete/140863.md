on-the-march:/ˌɒn ðə ˈmɑːtʃ/ | adj. | 行进中的，前进中的 |  actively progressing or advancing; in the process of moving forward, especially in a purposeful or determined way | The army is on the march towards the border. 部队正在向边境进军。 | synonyms: advancing, progressing, moving, proceeding | antonyms: static, stationary, immobile | adjective

on-the-march:/ˌɒn ðə ˈmɑːtʃ/ | n. | 行军，前进 | the act of marching; a forward movement, often of a large group | The on-the-march of technology is unstoppable. 科技的进步势不可挡。 | synonyms: advance, progression, movement, procession | antonyms: retreat, withdrawal, stagnation | noun
