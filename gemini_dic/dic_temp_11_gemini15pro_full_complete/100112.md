interpretament:/ɪnˈtɜːrprɪtəm(ə)nt/ |n.|解释；说明；诠释；翻译|An elucidation or explanation of something; an interpretation.  The act of interpreting or explaining something. A translation or rendering of something from one language to another.|The king requested an interpretament of the ancient prophecy.|近义词: interpretation, explanation, elucidation, translation, rendering, commentary, exposition, gloss|反义词: obfuscation, confusion, mystification|词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *interpretāmentum*, from Latin interpretārī (“to explain, interpret, translate”).
