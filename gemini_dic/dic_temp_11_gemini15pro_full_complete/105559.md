kimonos:/kɪˈmoʊnoʊz/
n.
和服（kimono的复数）
A traditional Japanese garment.  A long, loose robe with wide sleeves and tied with a sash.
She wore colorful kimonos to the festival. 她穿着色彩鲜艳的和服参加节日。
The museum displayed a collection of antique kimonos. 博物馆展出了一系列古董和服。
近义词:robes, garments
反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly Western clothing)
词性:noun
