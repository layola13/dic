drearily:/ˈdrɪrɪli/| adv.|沉闷地，阴郁地，无趣地|In a dreary manner; gloomily; dismally.  Characterized by or expressive of dreariness.|The rain fell drearily all day. 雨沉闷地下了一整天。|近义词：gloomily, dismally, sadly, dejectedly|反义词：cheerfully, brightly, happily|词性:副词

dreary:/ˈdrɪri/| adj.|沉闷的，阴郁的，无趣的|Causing dejection; gloomy; dismal.  Dull and monotonous.|It was a dreary day with constant rain.  那是一个阴雨连绵的沉闷日子。|近义词：gloomy, dismal, bleak, dull|反义词：cheerful, bright, happy|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *dreri, from Old English *drēorig (“sad, sorrowful, mournful, grieving, dreary”), from Proto-Germanic *drēurijaz (“sad”).  Related to Old High German trūrag (“sad”).
