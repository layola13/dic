missionary:/ˈmɪʃənɛri/| n. adj.|传教士，传教的|A person sent on a religious mission, especially one sent to promote Christianity in a foreign country. Also, relating to or characteristic of missionaries or their work.|例句：She worked as a missionary in Africa for many years. 她多年来在非洲担任传教士。|例句：He has a missionary zeal for spreading his political beliefs. 他对传播自己的政治信仰有着传教士般的热情。|近义词：apostle, evangelist, preacher|反义词:atheist, nonbeliever|词性: noun, adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *missionarius*, from Latin *missio* "a sending, a mission."
