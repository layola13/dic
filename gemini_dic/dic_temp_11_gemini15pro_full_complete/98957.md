inter-ethnic:/ˌɪntərˈɛθnɪk/
adjective
跨种族的；种族间的
Existing or occurring between different ethnic groups.
例句：Inter-ethnic relations have improved significantly in recent years.近年来，种族间关系有了显著改善。

例句：The festival celebrates inter-ethnic harmony and understanding.这个节日庆祝种族间的和谐与理解。

近义词：interracial, multiethnic, cross-cultural
反义词：intra-ethnic
词性: adjective
