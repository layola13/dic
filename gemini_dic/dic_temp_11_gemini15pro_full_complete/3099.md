aeroscopy:/eɪˈrɒskəpi/ | n. | 气溶胶镜检，空气镜检 | The examination of air or aerosols with an aeroscope.  The study of airborne particles using microscopic techniques. |  Aeroscopy is a valuable tool for studying air pollution. 气溶胶镜检是研究空气污染的宝贵工具。|  Scientists used aeroscopy to analyze the composition of the smog. 科学家使用气溶胶镜检来分析雾霾的成分。 |  microscopy, air sampling | N/A | noun
