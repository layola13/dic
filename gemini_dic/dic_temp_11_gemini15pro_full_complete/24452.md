Britannias
不列颠尼亚（古罗马对大不列颠的称呼）;  (Britannia) 女神不列颠尼亚（常作为英国的象征）;  不列颠尼亚金银币（英国旧金币或银币）
Britannia is a Latin name for Great Britain, used especially in ancient times. Britannia is also the name of a female figure personifying Britain.  Finally, Britannias can also refer to a type of gold or silver coin formerly used in Britain.
The Roman conquest of Britannia began in 43 AD. 罗马征服不列颠始于公元43年。

Britannia is often depicted holding a trident and shield.  不列颠尼亚通常被描绘成手持三叉戟和盾牌。

He invested in Britannias as a hedge against inflation. 他投资不列颠尼亚金币以对冲通货膨胀。
近义词：Britain, Great Britain (for the place); personification of Britain (for the figure)
反义词：None (The word doesn't lend itself to antonyms)
词性：Noun
