irreparableness:/ˌɪrɪˈpærəbəlnəs/
|n.|无法弥补；不能挽回|The irreparableness of the damage caused by the fire was devastating. 火灾造成的损失无法弥补，这真是毁灭性的。|The antique vase shattered, and its irreparableness brought tears to the collector's eyes. 古董花瓶摔碎了，它无法修复的事实让收藏家泪流满面。|近义词:irrevocability, finality, permanence|反义词:reparability, mendability|词性:名词

词源: From irreparable + -ness.  Irreparable comes from Late Latin irreparabilis, from in- 'not' + reparabilis 'repairable', from reparare 'repair'.
