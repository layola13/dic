bolide:/ˈboʊlaɪd/ | n. | 火流星；陨石；非常明亮的流星 | A large meteor that explodes in the atmosphere. A brilliant meteor, especially one that explodes. |  例句：The bolide streaked across the sky, leaving a trail of fire. 火流星划过天空，留下一道火光。

例句：Scientists believe the crater was created by a bolide impact millions of years ago. 科学家认为，这个陨石坑是由数百万年前一颗陨石撞击造成的。 | 近义词：meteor, fireball, meteoroid | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bolide*, from Latin *bolis*, from Greek *βολίς* (bolis, “missile, javelin”), from *βάλλω* (ballō, “I throw, hurl”).
